Doug Brunner’s Summary

Doug has more than eight years experience in sales, business management, and software solutions. He began his career working in sales, mass merchandising, and distribution for the Frito-Lay company. After leaving Frito-Lay, Doug held various progressing titles in the newspaper and online media industry over six years, including: Advertising Account Executive, Operations Manager, Advertising Sales Manager, and Internet Development Manager; during which time he worked for well known media companies such as Thomson Newspapers, and CNHI.

Doug continued his service to the media industry by joining Cincinnati based Harvest INFO in the role of Product Manager, where he leveraged his inside knowledge of the media industry to lead development of a suite of web-based software solutions geared towards generating online advertising revenue. These solutions exist today and remain core products for Harvest INFO.
Doug later returned to the Indianapolis area and joined Humanizing Technologies, a web-based software start-up, as Product Manager. In this role, his responsibilities included product and feature definition for a variety of online technologies.

Today, Doug is a Sr. Business Analyst at Theoris, a 23 year old software developer and IT consulting services firm. Doug resides near Indianapolis with his wife and son.
