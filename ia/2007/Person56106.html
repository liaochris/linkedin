Mathijs Suidman’s Summary

My focus as a consultant is on publishing and media. Specifically on product and business innovation.
I have experience both in traditional and new ways of publishing.
I like to develop innovative publishing concepts and implement them (in existing businesses) and help organisations to change into the direction of new ways of publishing.

The last 1,5 year I worked as a consultant solely for Lulu.com in the role of international business development manager.
Lulu.com is a NC (Raleigh) based fast growing online business for creators who like to publish, print (on demand), sell and distribute their books, music and other media worldwide themselves and control all copyrights.

This summer of 2007 I am enjoying a short sabbatical (while moving to a new house). As of September 2007 I like to focus on new POD and publishing projects in (mainland) Europe and preferably The Netherlands (to enjoy my 4 kids and wife too ;-))

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry)

    June 2001 — Present (6 years 3 months)

    Ulverton is a Dutch consultancy company with a focus in on the publishing branche.

    Ulverton BV was founded in 2001 after researching the publishing market for the book "Goudmijn van Gutenberg" about future publishing.
