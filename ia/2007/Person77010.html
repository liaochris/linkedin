Scott Foval’s Summary

Scott Foval is a columnist for ChicagoPride.com and writes ScottsBigMouth blog. His column, "My Angle with Scott Foval" on ChicagoPride.com addresses politics, GLBT community issues, pop culture, and media from a GLBT point-of-view. ScottsBigMouth (http://scottsbigmouth.blogspot.com) is an independent blog where Scott gives his own unique perspective on the world.

An experienced consultant to political campaigns, not-for-profit organizations, and businessess since 1992; Scott has been a strategist and producer for events, broadcast properties, and professional services firms since 1997. He has served as an advance and political operative for Democratic campaigns since 1990; including Vice President Al Gore (D-TN), U.S. Senator Tom Harkin (D-IA), U.S. Senator Harry Reid (D-NV), The Democratic National Committee (DNC), and Iowa Democratic Party. He resides in Chicago, IL USA, with his domestic partner of 3 years.

Scott Foval’s Specialties:

Media and Event Productions, Sponsorship and Branded Entertainment, Public Relations, Crisis Management, and Strategy for new ventures, Membership and Donor Development, Communications Strategy and Image Consulting, Political Analysis and Pop Culture Analysis, Blogging and Writing, Technology Solutions Consulting (Fortune 100-500 Business, SMB, NFP, Political, Broadcast Media, Startup and Ventures) and Project Management, Security and Risk Management.
