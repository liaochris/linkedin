Victor Konijn’s Summary

I have been working in the global ICT/IT professional services industry since the 1980s in Europe, India, China and Australia.

I have seen many clients suffer from working with external experts that had their own agenda, did not match the client's business culture or otherwise did not meet their needs,
and seen many professionals suffer from being pushed in projects that didn't suit or interest them.

Then came Plutonic Zoo.

We founded the company in 2001 to give a fresh breeze to IT professional services: making sure they are aligned with the business needs and culture of our clients. Delivering with self-motivated teams that have the right expertise and the right cultural match with the client organisation.

What we not have: we have no alliances with any software or hardware provider, no professionals as employees, and no external shareholders or bosses. As a result we have no predefined deliverables or teams that need to be pushed into clients' organisations.

This allows us to be truly independent and free to add value to our clients.

We work in a subworld of trust, with people who love what they do.

Check it out on www.plutoniczoo.com or www.plutoniczoo.com.au

Victor Konijn’s Specialties:

We provide impartial advice, project outcomes and ongoing support to our clients on IT strategy, business software applications, and IT systems.

We deliver with our Zoo: a wide network of niche organisations and hundreds of associates, each experts in their own field. First we assess with the client what skills and personalities they need, then form a matching team. We manage our teams to ensure client business value. When appropriate I work hands-on with my clients.
