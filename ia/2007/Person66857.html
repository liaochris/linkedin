Paul Van Cotthem’s Summary

I am a senior marketing & sales executive with broad experience in all aspects of marketing, advertising, product development and sales channel management.

I helped drive the launch in Belgium of desktop publishing (@Xerox), personal computing (@Apple), and broadband internet, VOIP telephony and interactive digital television (@Telenet).

I received the Belgian "Marketeer of the Year" award in 2003.

I specialize in delivering fast and profitable growth in high-potential (or turnaround) companies, through a total focus on the product and service experience at all customer touch points, supported by no-nonsense efficient and effective marketing communications campaigns.

I have a deep intuition for what it takes to drive growth, profitability and customer loyalty, based on my years of experience in establishing and managing go-to-market strategies.

I rely on the "what you measure, and reward, is what you get" principle in seeking to boost the key performance indicators of my customers' business.

With regards to people, I look for attitude first, then for skill. Talent, trust and teamwork are the cornerstones of successful collaboration.
