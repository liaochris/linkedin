Kelly Reardon’s Summary

My primary areas of interest are school and workplace mental health. I am also an amateur LION who is fascinated by the breadth and depth of information here. I am a huge proponent of open information sharing, which is why I welcome your connections: mpakelly at gmail dot com .

I am interested in sharing brainpower and resources for everyone's benefit, including that elusive and abstract "greater good," not just amassing connections for the sake of having them, harvesting leads for profit, or the other commonly disliked motives. However, I think it's fascinating to find a place where professionals can harness resources in just about any field and geographic area and bring them back to those whom they serve. I realize that not everyone agrees with the concept of open networking but please bear in mind that there are many different breeds of LION.
