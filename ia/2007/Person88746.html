Vincent Dorie’s Experience

  • Sr. Software Engineer and Research Architect

    SparkIP

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    June 2006 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    SparkIP is a startup creating tools to facilitate the development and transfer of intellectual property (IP). Together with a business analyst/IP domain expert over a 6 month time frame, designed and implemented an approach to extract semantically meaningful relationships from large, human formed networks. In embodiment, we clustered and labeled the modern US patent collection with greater detail and accuracy than previous attempts in the field with goals of clarifying existing IP protection, assisting individuals in locating potential collaborators and licensees, and helping companies identify technological opportunities.

    Major tasks included:
    • Classifying malicious or malformed signals
    • Clustering the corpus around specific technologies by identifying the most informative citation patterns
    • Merging the clusters in to a hierarchy of broader concepts
    • Labeling cluster hierarchies with unique, human understandable terms
    • Adapting the above processes to an evolving data set
