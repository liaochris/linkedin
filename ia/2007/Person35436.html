Howard James’s Summary

My professional experience has always been in the people sector with a degree in Education and my early career based in the Early Childhood Education area where I was a teacher, Centre Director, educator, Government regulatory advisor to children’s services, service developer and ran a small professional development company. As part of that professional development work, I was published in this field and the main book was accepted as a text in multiple learning institutions.

I moved from there into youth employment projects where I re-met some of my pre-schoolers as they came out of the school system. Part of these programs involved close liaison, working with employers across a broad spectrum of industries which was the integral skill in my subsequent roles, including my current role and the role I held with the Australian Chamber of Commerce and Industry promoting apprenticeships and traineeships and developing employment options with Business groups, and individual companies.

My current professional goals are to continue to innovatively develop business solutions and options for my Candidates and Client companies in a global candidate poor market with dramatically changing technological base of operations. Oh and to make the odd $ :) I will continue to develop my interest and skills in Career change, transition and planning for a cross section of the emplyment market, including school leavers.
