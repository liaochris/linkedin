Eran Dror’s Summary

I am a strong believer in the power of open communication. That's why in my marketing roles I have harnessed the power of the web, blogs, e-mail newsletters, and online communities to spread my message. By understanding the way ideas spread in today's culture, I could lead multiple successful campaigns, using both the web and more traditional channels to target the sources of popular perception. It comes down to exposure and trust. Exposure means your message gets seen by the right people. Trust means they believe it. The first requires a strategic vision, the second - absolute integrity.

For the past 2.5 years I have been the chief marketing person for a small technology solution provider, and shaped the company's entire marketing program with great success. Before that, I worked as an IT magazine writer and then Editor in Chief for 5 years, and gained extensive experience creating content directed at high level decision makers. Throughout my career people of considerable talent and influence have read my words. They read them repeatedly. They believed them. They acted on them.

And they were not disappointed.
