Christopher Hall’s Summary

In every position I have ever held, I have prized customer service and the uncovering of a constituent's needs, whether they be philanthropic (in my current role), business-driven, or personal, and meeting those needs to the best of my ability. I have connected alumni back with two different universities. I have raised support for four different colleges at Kent State University. I have given consult to six national companies and their franchises on directory advertising. I have also helped colleges to improve their interactive presence through the use of web portals and guerilla marketing.

My goal is to be a leader in whatever position I hold to meet the needs of the constituents or clients that I serve--whether in advertising, marketing, or fundraising.
