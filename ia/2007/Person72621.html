Rick James!’s Summary

I'm Rick James!

More importantly, I'm your, "Jack of All Trades..."

For the past eight years I've spent my life delving into my passions, learning new skills, and melding my own ethos. My life experiences thus far have brought me high and low, to the farthest stretches of sanity, and to the most joyous times in my life. I can tell you that its been one heck of a ride and I've never wanted to get off.

I've had the opportunity to wear many hats in the past, including Tour Manager, Body Piercer, and now Business Owner. Rick James Creative is a product of the latter, my newest venture and the most fun I've had to date. As the Owner and Creative Director, I've had the ability to finally work for myself, at my own pace, with my goals, aspirations, and dreams at the center of the universe. I've been very lucky and blessed to have worked with some amazing clients so far and I wake up every morning looking forward to my next project.

By the way, did I mention that I'm a manager at an Apple Inc. store? I'm spoiled rotten.
