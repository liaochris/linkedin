Andy Yang’s Experience

Responsible for the financial modelling in most of these projects.

  • Vice President

    Bankers Trust

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; Investment Banking industry)

    February 1997 — January 1999 (2 years)

    Worked at BT Advisory Malaysia Sdn Bhd, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia - marketed and provided Risk Management Advisory services in Asia-ex-Japan. RMA assisted financial institutions to better manage their strategic risk business by quantifying their market, credit and operational risks. Marketing included contacting, visiting and presenting to insurance companies (life and general), banks and central banks at senior levels in Asia, and ensuring that BT’s Senior Bankers in the region understood RMA’s product range. Covered Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Hong Kong, Philippines and South Korea. The Asian crisis re-emphasised the importance of credit risk, and the RMA team in KL developed the Default Filter™ model (tailored for Emerging Markets) to help entities manage their credit risk and the model was patented (PCT/US99/05978 filed March 19, 1999).
