Angela Du Bois’s Summary

For the past four years I have been perfecting my two-dimensional and three-dimensional design skills while employed as a Senior Artist/Designer with the DVD design firm ‘Company Wide Shut’ in Los Angeles. While there, I have been involved with every facet of design, from concept to completion - which includes storyboarding, graphic and set design, as well as extensive photo shoots, both motion and static.

Angela Du Bois’s Specialties:

I am fluent in Photoshop, Illustrator, PageMaker, and InDesign, with a little bit of QuarkXPress, After Effects, and some basic HTML.
