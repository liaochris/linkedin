Ashu Mathura’s Summary

MADS (http://www.mads.com) is Europe’s leading mobile advertising network and marketing technology provider. MADS advertising network is an eco-system that sources ads from advertisers and delivers them through our network of mobile sites, applications and games to consumers anywhere.

MADS is dedicated to bring advertisers, agencies, mobile content publishers, mobile operators and consumers together to realize the power of mobile advertising. To this end, MADS created, operates and supports:

• the MADserver mobile ad management, targeting and delivery platform
• the MADnetwork, the Europe’s largest single source mobile advertising network
• mobile marketing consulting and campaign management services

Check out my blog: http://www.madvertisingblog.com
