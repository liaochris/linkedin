Igor Corvers’s Summary

I have been working for Cronos nv since 1999 as Account Manager for the Cronos Group. As a single point of contact to a number of large accounts for the Cronos Group I'm always in the fronline to help my customers with our broad experience in ICT projects.

Since 2005 I added 2 new challenges to my professional carreer. I also started working for 2 daughter companies of the Cronos Group: Panoptic nv (a young but leading integrator of Enterprise Content Management Solutions in Belgium) and Calibrate nv (an even younger company that develops applications on iDTV).

In March 2007 I've started with business development for e-commerce solutions within the Cronos Group. I've noticed that the Belgian market is far behind compared to our neighbouring countries and I truly believe that there is a good opportunity now to catch up! In this first phase we've selected a high end e-commerce solution called Intershop to fulfill our customers need. We will try to bring solutions with state-of-the-art technology and based on the web 2.0 principles! Check also out our Log on to Ecom event site: www.logontoecom.be!

The most important goal in my life is to make people happy and to deliver succesfull projects and/or consultants to my customer base!

Sees the day!
