Ted Horton’s Summary

I produce insanely great places to live in Northern California and Hawaii. A home defines who we are--it is the foundation of a family, an island of calm in a sea of doubt--and communicates who we would like to be. As Robert AM Stern says, "Every American house is a dream house".

Although I can improve the process at any point, I am most effective early in the process--evaluating potential sites and recruiting the team. We do whatever it takes to achieve a project's potential.

We can't do this alone--we work with designers, craftsmen, contractors and owners to design to budget, build lasting, legacy-level quality, and finish with an on-time move-in.

We have produced over $150 million of distinctive residential real property, including three homes published in Architectural Digest.

My company has invested in approximately $50 million of multifamily properties, bringing many of these same qualities to an investment portfolio that provides attractive risk-adjusted returns.

Make me your first call on your next project!
