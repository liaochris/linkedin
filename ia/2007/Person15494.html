Cynthia "CG" Anderson’s Summary

What is a New Media Provocateur? You see the future of media in all its forms, take action & make change happen. It's knowing a lot about media & related issues & a little about everything else. It's planning and strategy, with a solid foundation in research. It's art, drama, design, technology, internet, social networking, blogging, online video, 3D, gaming & stuff that hasn't even made it to prime time yet. It's merging TV, Film, Print, Radio, Guerilla & Viral Marketing, Mobile & Web into the RIGHT Marketing/Media/Product/Dev Mix. It's about getting out a great customer-focused product!

It's people & content developers-managing the flow, helping creativity and keeping everyone pumped up, happy & productive.
Most of all it's about generating excitement for the future and making that future happen and showing that the efforts and effects are real.

It's about being a leader not a follower.

It's process/project/program management (also producing/content producing & planning), account management, account planning, media planning, marketing research, design research, marketing mix planning & strategy as well as customer insight into new & old media (advertising frequency and reach included) and prediction of new trends and where to go next (and how to do it and even how to manage the process and people and keep everyone motivated and passionate about what they are building, including content creation/control, planning for what's next and other duties.)

It's taken twenty years to get to this goal of who I am now and the exciting part is that there is more to come, more ground to break, more great products & services to bring to customers AND more evolving media to add to the mix.
