Elijah Shaw’s Summary

Elijah J. Shaw is the President and CEO of Icon Services Corporation. ICON is a full service, security and investigative agency specializing in Executive and VIP Protection. On a long-term basis, his notable celebrity clients include Usher, Naomi Campbell, Michael Bolton, and until recently, Multi-platinum rap star 50 Cent. Elijah's firm also contracts business with several Fortune 500 Companies and Executives. With tours of duty that frequently take him to Europe, Japan and Australia, Mr. Shaw acts as a personal bodyguard to a select number of these individuals providing additional close protection, security consultation and staffing worldwide. Mr. Shaw’s 13 year contribution to the security industry has been in applying licensed and trained talent with a focus on professionalism and customer service, to an industry that is increasingly under the magnifying glass since the events of 9/11, and coping with current issues nationally and internationally. For more information visit our corporate website at www.Industry-Icon.com.
