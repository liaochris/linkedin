Leilani Munter’s Summary

I just became the fourth woman in history to race in the Indy Pro Series, the feeder series to IndyCar. In 2007 I was honored to be named as one of the top ten female race car drivers in the world by Sports Illustrated. I hold a Bachelor of Science in biology and I am an environmentalist. I purchase an acre of endangered rainforest for every race I run and host an Earth Watch section on my website leilanimunter.com.

Leilani Munter’s Specialties:

Television appearances include: Fox Sports, NBC Sports, ESPN, Spike TV, and National Geographic. Print includes: USA Today, Sports Illustrated, Esquire, Men's Journal, ESPN Magazine, Washington Post, and five magazine covers to date.
