Bruce Maxwell’s Summary

Bruce provides interim executive services as CEO, COO or CFO to help startups get organized and funded. He also provides troubleshooting and due diligence services to venture firms. He is a specialist in the construction of business plans and financial projections and has raised over $68 million in venture and angel funds. Bruce has run his practice since January of 1998, serving over 55 corporate clients such as Firetalk, Lineo, Sensory and Arcturus Networks and venture clients such as J&W Seligman and Outlook Ventures. Before starting his own practice, Bruce served as CEO of Positive Communications, a paging services firm. Bruce founded Positive in 1992, grew it to over $40 million in annual sales and 350 employees, and sold over a million pagers. Prior to Positive, Bruce held various positions with Voysys, AT&E and Chemical Bank. Bruce holds an MBA from Stanford University. He has also taught classes in Entrepreneurship for the MBA program at St. Mary’s College, Moraga, CA.

Bruce Maxwell’s Specialties:

Expert at constructing Financial Projections, Operating Plans, Business Plans, and PowerPoint Pitches. 20 years of experience raising private equity from Angel and VC investors. Personally raised over $68 million. Operating experience at over two dozen startups as CEO, COO and CFO.
