Ashish Joshi’s Summary

Presently employed at Oracle Corporation as a Director in CRM Product Management. I am responsible for managing Gartner-rated, CRM industry-leading Siebel Contact Center & Service Applications. Siebel Contact Center & Service Application is Siebel’s flagship product responsible for over 40% Siebel’s annual license revenue.

Before joining Oracle Corporation, I was a Sr. Principal Product Manager at Siebel Systems responsible for 'CTI & Communications Platform' as well as 'Test Automation' product lines.

Prior to joining the product management team, I was a Principal Specialist & Technical Lead within Siebel's Expert Services group. I lead the 'Call Center & Services' worldwide team, and was responsible for developing and maintaining service offerings for Communications Server, CTI, Field Service, and eMail Response products.

I have been a key contributor to some of the largest successful Siebel Call Center implementations (>5000+ call center agents) and have provided design and implementation recommendations to over 100 Siebel customers (43 of which have Siebel CTI implementations).
