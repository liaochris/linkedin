Andy Getsey’s Summary

Have collaborated with senior execs of technology companies for more than 15 years; including LinkedIn, Mint.com, Photobucket, IMAX, BitTorrent, Callidus, NetBooks, Echelon, Sigma Designs, Oracle, Fujitsu, Hitachi, Verizon, six apart and others.

Have made strategic contributions to programs for more than 18 clients acquired by major brands in recent years - Veon, Sanera Systems, 9Squared, JRG Software, OuterBay, StepUp Commerce, PreventSys, Webify, Photobucket and others.

Messaging consultant and hands-on media trainer for CEOs and other key spokespeople for brands including LinkedIn, PhotoBucket, six apart, the Electronic Frontier Foundation, Savvion, Callidus (NASDAQ: CALD), Sigma Designs (NASDAQ: SIGM), Echelon (NASDAQ: ELON), IMAX (NASDAQ: IMAX), The Asian Art Museum of San Francisco, IDOL Vodka, Vontu, FalconStor (NASDAQ: FALC) and numerous others.

Originator of Atomic ComContext™, an on–demand analytics application for communications research, PR planning and program measurement.

Previously president of Shafer, an independent technology marketing agency with clients ranging from Oracle and Quantum to Toshiba and Earthlink. Before that, global communications head for digital graphics hardware manufacturer CalComp Lockheed–Martin. Started at database marketing agency Krupp/Taylor Foote Cone Belding with technology and consumer clients ranging from Apple to United Airlines.

Bachelor's degree in psychology and an MBA from California State University.

Andy Getsey’s Specialties:

Atomic is LinkedIn's PR firm :-) Atomic Public Relations works with technology, consumer and entertainment clients to elevate their stories out of the details and into the broader dialog. Atomic’s analytic bent, creativity, social media savviness and online workflows offer a fresh alternative, and often deliver 100%+ increases in communications volume, quality and clarity. Atomic has offices in San Francisco and Los Angeles (coming soon to NYC) and employs 40 full time professionals.
