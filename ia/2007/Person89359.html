Vince LaVecchia’s Summary

I always expected to be a teacher. I was a decent student, sometimes lazy, but I took good notes and went to class. I could jam out papers with ease and get solid average marks. Out of school, I got into Action Sports and Youth Marketing through the my early career at Burton Snowboards. Coming out of Burton in early 2001, I launched Instrument, a company dedicated to marketing truth through a network of creative friends and colleagues. I began writing for clients in the world of apparel, youth marketing and sports.

In 2003, Instrument morphed into Legitify, and with two partners we grew a successful web/ marketing business in Burlington, Vermont. Legitify maintained the focus and resources of Instrument, but added the ability to design, produce and host websites and web applications for our clients. I also expanded my writing to include broader projects and a wider range of industries. One of my partners and I moved to Portland, Oregon in late 2006 and opened an office for Legitify here.

Recently, Justin Lewis and I relaunched our web and marketing company in Portland under the name Instrument, and we're now providing services for clients in diverse industries from biotech to action sports. We're building and designing custom web applications on the leading edge of programming technology, and we're building and managing websites for many long-term clients as well. I have recently completed writing projects for MasterCraft Boats, Merrell Apparel and Burton Snowboards and the projects continue to be challenging and numerous.

I realize now that I am truly a teacher, as I'd imagined. I am an educator of consumers. I am also a guide for our clients to reach and retain the most important members of their audience in order to surprise and delight them with relevance, credibility and marketing truth.
