Bryan Cox’s Summary

My first full-time job was in security, helping protect people and property. I went on to a career in law enforcement where I spent over 10 years as a major crimes investigator. Over the years, I've learned not just how to protect people and property, but I've also seen the aftermath of crime and learned why security and protection so important. My goal when working for any client, or when working with allied professionals, is to keep the big picuture in mind and deliver actual results to help prevent violent crime and control loss. I want to earn my client's trust and work with them as a true partner.
