Looking for C/C++ software engineers with interest or experience working with text to join development team in Pisa, Italy and Edison, NJ

I manage a team of in U.S., and in Pisa, Europe. See the blog and this other posting. Here you can have more information about the European R&D Center in Pisa, Italy. In 2006, I earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science and an Engineering B.S in 2003. In 2005, I had 5 papers at WWW05 conference. In 1998-2002, I was the CEO/CTO of Ideare (Tiscali group), a company which developed engines for searching & classifying hundreds of millions of Web pages, real time news, Usenet forum, audio, video, and images, all crawled from thousands of sites.
