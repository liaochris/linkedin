Seana Mulcahy’s Summary

Seana Mulcahy is president of Brand Truth. She formerly held posts on both the client and agency side of the fence. She's been creating online brands since before the first banner was sold. Her expertise includes online and traditional media planning and buying, email marketing, viral marketing, click-stream analysis, customer tracking, promotions, search engine marketing and launching brands online for both agencies and clients. Prior to Mullen, Seana was vice president of media services at Carat Interactive. She's launched and promoted brands including VW, Ocean Spray, McDonald's, British Airways, Symantec, CMGi, Four Seasons, LendingTree.com, Nextel, SmithKline Beecham, Pfizer, Titleist/FootJoy/Pinnacle, 3M, AT&T, Sprint, and Radio Shack. She's built online media services divisions for three companies and has worked with clients spanning financial, telecom, high-tech, automotive, healthcare and retail. Not surprisingly, she has taught, lectured and written about the industry for numerous trade associations and publications. She currently has a weekly online media column at <http://www.mediapost.com> and has written for numerous trades. She's a member of the American Association of Advertising Agencies, The Ad Club of Boston, MITX, and is a board member of the Boston Interactive Media Association, Dollars for Scholars, and NetElixir, a search engine marketing firm. She judges annual Ad: Tech and Massachusetts Interactive Technology Exchange events annually. She works out of Boston, Ma.and has a degree in advertising, PR, and speech.
