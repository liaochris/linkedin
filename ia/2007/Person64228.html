Noel McKinney’s Summary

The Constant Group is a professional services firm serving small & medium-sized businesses. The firm is registered with the State of Illinois Securities Department as a business broker. We provide business brokerage and related intermediary services such as sell-side advisory, succession/exit planning, new business owner education, business search, and valuation. Our management and strategy consulting services focus on business improvement and paths for growth through acquisition or marketing.

The firm offers USPAP compliant machinery and equipment certified appraisals through its Top Grade Appraisals (TM) service. We also provide machinery and equipment brokerage through a national network of brokers.

The Constant Group Inc. is a member of The River North Association and the Industrial Council of Nearwest Chicago, plus its principals are members of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), Harvard Business School Club of Chicago, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), International Business Brokers Association (IBBA), Midwest Business Brokers and Intermediaries (MBBI), Midwest US-China Association (MWCA), National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO), National Equipment and Business Brokers Institute (NEBBI), Turnaround Management Association (TMA), and the Union League Club of Chicago.

We own websites in multiple spaces including B2B, communities and retail. The firm provides information technology consulting services, plus we are undertaking some internal software development projects. Please visit our website at www.TheConstantGroup.com to learn more about us.
