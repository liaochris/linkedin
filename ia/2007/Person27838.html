Erik Zambon’s Summary

After a number of years working as a management consultant in the retail and fashion industries, I would now like to diversify my career choices by entering the Italian job market, preferably in the fashion sector, where I hope to put my international experience to good use and where I can continue the learning process by covering a variety of roles and responsibilities.

My work experience to date has been very much aligned with my own interests with a particular focus on strategic merchandising/planning and marketing. However, the varied nature of consulting has ensured that my exposure to retail remains broad and has thus provided me with a breadth of experiences stretching across the entire supply chain – from product planning through to store operations.

In addition, this has given me the opportunity to spend long periods of time abroad for study and work purposes (Luxembourg, UK, France and Japan). This has strengthened my interest in multicultural environments and my ability to operate efficiently in international contexts. A graduate from Cambridge University (BA and MA (Hons) 2001) in Management studies, visual creativity and outstanding language skills complete my profile.
