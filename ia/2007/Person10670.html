  • Founder,CEO

    Micro-80 Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry)

    May 1979 — March 1983 (3 years 11 months)

    Back in the days mid-late 70's, when Bill Gates and Paul Allen hung out at the geek stores in Seattle, I conceived the idea of finding a niche in the new personal computer industry.. Became the manufacturer of the computer industry's first series of data storage cassette tapes. We had 5, 10, 15, and 30 minute tapes. The 5 minute tape was ideal for storing software programs created at that time in Basic. We were the sole supplier of tape to Tandy Corp for its TRS-80 Model I computer (known as the Trash-80) --- As technology progressed into 5.25 inch diskette and the TRS-80 Model III computer came along, Micro-80 started supplying these under the Micro-80 label and was among the first display advertisers in "The Computer Shopper" out of Titusville FL. Oh those were the days. We had 5 people assisting me in all phases of manufacturing , marketing, salesm and shipping. Never had a single return nor complaint ever!

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Recreational Facilities and Services industry)

    1976 — 1979 (3 years)
