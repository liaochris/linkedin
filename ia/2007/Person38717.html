Jason Poss’s Summary

Independent musician with experience at the forefront of contemporary music production on high-profile projects for virtually all forms of media. Experience includes work with composer Howard Shore on "The Lord of the Rings" films as well as projects for Disney, Warner Bros., Miramax, 20th Century Fox, Blizzard Entertainment/Vivendi Games, Ubisoft, and others. Selected projects are listed under "Experience" below.

Jason Poss’s Specialties:

music composition, orchestration, arranging, and preparation
