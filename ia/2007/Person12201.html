David Armstrong’s Summary

I find I approach most things by being creative but realistic, enthusiastic but disciplined and optimistic about improvement or finding a solution to any problem.

I work everyday with people and companies in the areas of Business Development in analysis of company opportunitity, past performance, valuation and due dilligence fact finding.

I've been a technology realist for 25 years, winning programming contests in 1985 (although not a programmer) all the way up to bootstrapping my own venture two years ago and still going. I get past the "cool" to analyze and define the practical/scalable opportunities.

My experience and enjoyment comes from startups. This could be a corporate startup (which is harder), a new direction a current company wants to go, or from a whiteboard drawing and the right team. I enjoy the planning stage and execution of the plan equally.

Personal relationships are a strength as I genuinely care about the people around me, involve them in decision making, develop their skills with mine, and provide feedback to reinforce and drive improvements.

I have high expectations of myself and those around me.
