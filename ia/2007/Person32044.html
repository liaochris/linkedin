    B2B CFO / CIO LLP

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 2005 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    http://B2BCIO.com
    A Business-to-Business CIO is a CIO or CTO to companies on an as-needed basis. A company generally hires a B2B CIO for as little as a few hours a week or even a month. The B2B CIO system has worked very well for companies since the mid-1980's and is based on success through delegation, providing the most cost-effective solution. That means that a B2B CIO is utilized as a scarce resource and works with companies to prioritize and then delegate all technology and infrastructure tasks to resources that are the most cost effective to the organization.

    A B2B CIO's job is to leverage technology to enable businesses to grow through both the expeditious use of technology and the effective prioritization of projects, while reducing expense through the most efficient utilization of resources. Once requirements and issues have been established, it is very easy for a Business-to-Business CIO to work with more than a dozen clients simultaneously.
