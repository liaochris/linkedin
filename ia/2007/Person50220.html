Laurel Mathe’s Summary

Our studio, tucked away in the Sierra foothills, is proud of our word-of-mouth only clientele. We love what we do. Nothing goes out of our shop that doesn't meet our standards. We're in business to make money, but also to lead satisfying, self-determined lives. We have high standards for both ourselves and our clients. This means keeping to our business and client model as closely as possible, keeping both our clients and ourselves happy.

What makes a good client and, ultimately, a successful project? Most important is vision, but high standards, trust, and a willingness to take chances is also key. And if we both do our best to treat each other pleasantly, if not fabulously, and have a sense of adventure and humor, we should all have a pretty damn good time too! A project with those ingredients is destined for success.
