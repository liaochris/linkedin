Joaquim Jorge’s Summary

Joaquim Jorge is Associate Professor of Computer Science at Instituto Superior Técnico (IST/UTL), the School of Engineering of the Technical University of Lisboa, Portugal. Received PhD and MSc degrees in Computer Science from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY, in 1995 and a BsEE from IST/UTL in 1984.
Co-chair of EUROGRAPHICS'98, which took place in Lisboa, Portugal. Recently he co-chaired the EG SBM'04 (Grenoble, France, September 04) and EGMM04 (Nanjing, PRC, October 04) Workshops. A long time practitioner of Computer Graphics, first joined the Eurographics Association (EG) in 1986, was a founding member of the Eurographics Portuguese Chapter. Serves on the EG Promotions Board and leads the EG Working Group on Sketch-Based Interfaces and Modeling, was EG Conference Monitoring Officer for EG2001 and is elected member of EG Executive Committee since May 2000.

Participated on several research projects both at the National and European Level. Principal Investigator (PI) of SmartSketches (IST-2000-28169) project, and led the Alfa INETGAM II-0072-A network of excellence, both started in 2001. Leads the CGEMS international project, co-funded by the EC Eurographics and SIGGRAPH Associations since September 2002. Participated in European projects connected to graphics and standardization efforts as a researcher with the Portuguese Computer and Systems' Engineering Institute (INESC) CAD/CAM group from 1984 to 1989.

He was a consultant with FhG/IGD in Multimodal Interfaces (1999). Has served on the program committees of over 55 international conferences. He is a member of the Editorial Advisory Board of Computers & Graphics Journal and online editor for Computer Graphics Forum. Affiliated with ACM/SIGGRAPH since 1989, member of the Educational Commitee since 2004, IEEE Computer Society (Senior Member since 2000), IFIP TC13 (Human Computer Interaction - National Contact Person), IAPR, and is current chairman of the Eurographics Portuguese Chapter.
