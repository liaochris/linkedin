Wayne Anderson (wayne@apsg-ltd.com)’s Summary

I have over thirty years in the information technology (IT) industry. I am a senior executive with an equal blend of technical, business, and managerial skills developed during experience with several Fortune 500 companies. I have held the position of senior vice president of IT—as well as divisional CIO—for several multibilliondollar corporations.

Now, I work with CIOs and corporations to further their strategic business goals using their IT organization. I provide extensive experience in managing multimillion-dollar budgets and large professional IT staffs. In addition, I possesses the unique ability to attract, retain, and motivate outstanding management and professional personnel. I provide these consulting services to Chief Information Officers (CIO's) in any size company. I also provide coaching to non-IT senior managers on how to manage the CIO function. I frequently speak on the topic of how to be successful in the CIO position; and how to create a successful IT organization.

I am the author of the following books:
“Unwrapping the CIO: Demystifying the Chief Information Officer Position” - iUniverse Publishing – September, 2006

"Powerful People are Powerful I.T. Professionals" - (part of the "Powerful People" series) - iUniverse Publishing – January, 2007
