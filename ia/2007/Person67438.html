Peter Forsyth’s Summary

I do computer consulting for small businesses (see www.puddletowntech.com), helping them overcome technological challenges and plan for the future. I have over 15 years' experience with Macintosh, 10 with Windows, 5 with Linux.

Puddletown Tech has been in business since October 2004. I am currently working with a wide variety of clients (see http://www.puddletowntech.com/links.html .) Previously, I installed and managed all information services for the Portland Tribune, including network planning, database design, and technical support.

I also currently volunteer for Bus Project and Free Geek, primarily focusing on community and media outreach and communications. Samples of my writing may be found at http://peteforsyth.com/writing .
