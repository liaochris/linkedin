Douglas A. Funke’s Summary

BeachStreet Capital ("BeachStreet", "BSC" or the "Firm") is a boutique financial services firm. BSC's investment products invest in both public and private equity investments. The Firm also provides M&A/Strategic Advisory services such as M&A (buy or sell-side) and reorganization/recapitalization situations. When permissible, BSC co-invests in select principal investments, and BSC always invests in it's standard fund offerings.

BeachStreet manages money for public and private institutions and for select families and individuals. BSC's flagship fund invests in both public and private companies. Employing the Firm's investment experiences and expertise, we opportunistically invest in a myriad of markets and conditions. BSC's Funds can mitigate exposure prone to traditional funds such as those with a singular focus and/or expertise and if applicable, tactical investments are utilized to add alpha without increasing portfolio volatility. The Fund's targeted asset mix is monitored by an Investment Committee and an Advisory Board comprised of select clients. Because we view our clients as our Partners, we like to utilize their collective investment experiences, comments and criticisms. The added "intellectual capital" helps navigate potential pitfalls such as the investment process becoming "stale" or prone to "group-think". This insures a dynamic and robust investment process, and more importantly, enhances our already unwavering commitment to providing world class client service.

NOTE: Public equity vs. private equity.

BSC manages public equity funds with varying investor risk and return profiles. Public equity expertise includes: asset allocation, long only/core investing, specialized/tactical products, and hedge fund investing. Private equity includes distressed/deep value, special situations, venture capital, core "plus", specialty products and private equity/opportunity funds.
