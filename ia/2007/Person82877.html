Stuart Oliver’s Summary

Experienced, commercially focused, Head of Operations with strategic leadership abilities, breadth of experience and proven relationship management skills. Has been employed in shaping consultancy operations for major international financial services businesses, retail organisations & City institutions resulting in the successful delivery of solutions on high level strategic assignments.

Prior to becoming self employed in 2005, Stuart held the role of COO for an IT Services Consulting company based in Scotland. With around 50 staff, he managed the recruitment, performance and utilisation of the company's delivery capability. This role encompassed advising and delivering upon the sales strategy, delivering regular updates to the Board on key activities, issues and the company's position within a competitive market place.

Based on his experience in the financial services sector and a very strategic mind, Stuart has been able to advise corporations on the most efficient use of IT systems and future investments such that technology can play a key role in underpinning the success of the business.

Stuart has directly managed the implementation of many technology projects and has demonstrable experience managing change within business units, commercial agreements with third parties and direct interaction with senior staff and Board members from business case approval through to project delivery.

An active business networker, Stuart earns his reputation and builds trust with all those he works with. He has proven communication and relationship building skills at all levels.

Stuart is a self starter who works best when working within a high pressure environment with a strong team, all of whom are focused on delivery. He is experienced in working with third parties, internal & external staff, and is able lead the selection process of such staff or companies and the corresponding commercial negotiations and agreements.
