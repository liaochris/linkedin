Rebecca Coggan’s Summary

Rebecca Coggan is an ardent computer, technology and new-media enthusiast, rarely seen without some sort of gadget at her fingertips.

In addition to her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Cinema, Rebecca undertook minors in psychology and semiotics. She also completed extensive training in business development, enterprise, and sales techniques. Passionate about technology since her youth, Rebecca was the proud owner of one of the very first laptop computers ever to exist, which in those days was the size of a suitcase! Her curiosity with innovation manifests itself best, however, through her go-getter spirit.

Throughout her career, Rebecca has had the opportunity to familiarize herself with the various media that make up today’s market and has also learned how to maximize the methods to meet the means. More precisely, she has fine-tuned her web and mobile expertise and now, works solely on such projects for interactive agencies, multi-media producers as well as small to large businesses alike.

Rebecca is a member of the Internet Strategy Forum as well as the Wireless World Forum.

To learn more about her and the work she does, as well as client comments, please visit: www.exaltcreativity.com. Or to contact Rebecca Coggan, please email: info@exaltcreativity.com
