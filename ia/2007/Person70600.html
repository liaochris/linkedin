Ralf Schundelmeier’s Summary

More than 15 years of experience in software development and consulting. At eWork Ralf was responsible for managing the end to end provisioning of eWork’s on demand, "software as a service" solutions (SaaS). His responsibilities include Technology Strategy, Product Management, Development and ASP Operations.

Ralf has extensive experience with application integration and implementation. Prior to joining eWork, Ralf was Director Engineering Supply Chain / SAP CrossWorlds Software (acquired by IBM), where he was responsible for the development of Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) products for supply chain applications (SCM), web services enabled electronic marketplaces and eProcurement solutions integrated with SAP. Ralf led the development efforts to provide Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) solutions for leading eCommerce and supply chain applications such as ARIBA, I2 and Manugistics with the leading ERP packages like SAP, Oracle, Siebel, JDE and Peoplesoft.

Prior to CrossWorlds, Ralf held various management positions at Integration Software Consultants (acquired by Ciber), a leading systems integrator, focusing on large scale SAP implementations and custom development projects in the area of supply chain management. While at ISC Ralf worked very closely with the SAP development organization for new products in the process industry (SAP PP-PI) and supply chain management (SAP APO). He was also an instructor for the SAP Education team and the SAP Academy. Prior to ISC Ralf held various positions at IDS Scheer Inc., a prominent European software and consulting firm. In these roles, he was responsible for business process redesign (BPR) projects utilizing the ARIS methodology for designing, developing and implementing integrated supply chain solutions for Fortune 100 companies. Ralf was also part of the initial IDS team that entered the US market to spearhead efforts for business process driven SAP implementations.

Ralf Schundelmeier’s Specialties:

Over 15 years of experience in Software Development, Consulting and large scale ERP Implementations (SAP) focusing on the Supply Chain and Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) space.

Industry Experience include Pharmaceuticals, Oil & Gas, Chemicals, Consumer Packaged Goods, Healthcare, Pulp & Paper and others.

Over 5 years experience delivering enterprise software as a service over the internet (SaaS) with over 100 live customers, more than 500 suppliers and thousands of users.
