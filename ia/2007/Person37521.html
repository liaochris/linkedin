John Akalaonu’s Summary

I am currently working as a Senior Consultant for Motorola Inc.’s Upgrade Operations Division at their corporate headquarters in Schaumburg.
After graduating from Purdue University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology and History, I was commissioned as an officer in the U.S. Marine Corps. I received advanced training and valuable experience in strategic planning, organizational leadership and development, employee development, leadership training, process improvement, conflict resolution and team building. I routinely coordinate with senior executive-level military, government and corporate leaders while formulating policies, training packages, and operational plans for complex scenarios, including a crisis management plan in the immediate aftermath of Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans. During my military career, I was able to apply my leadership and organizational development skills as I led teams of up to seventy people in both peacetime and combat environments. In my various positions in the Marine Corps, I developed new team-building programs, conducted departmental re-organizations, and produced/reviewed employee performance reviews and personal improvement plans. I also gained valuable experience in leadership training and development of both front-line employees and supervisors/managers. I recently graduated from Indiana Wesleyan University with a Master of Science degree in Business Management, with a concentration in Organizational Development and Leadership, and am seeking the right opportunity to apply my knowledge in the organizational development and change management field. I am currently pursuing a Master Certification in Organizational Leadership as well as my Six Sigma Green Belt certification from Villanova University, with both certifications being completed by December of this year. In addition to these certifications, I am currently preparing to take the Project Management Professional (PMP) exam to achieve my PMP certification.
