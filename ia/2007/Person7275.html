Richard Appleyard’s Summary

Richard is a proponent of Holistic Web/IT Design with a strong focus on the user's experience. His expertise includes Web Site Management, Consumer Informatics, and Web User Experience (including Web accessibility and Section 508 compliance). He is passionate about helping companies to maximize the impact and effectiveness of their Web/IT projects.

Richard Appleyard’s Specialties:

Large Web site oversight, Web Program/Project Management, Web Security, Web Accessibility, Web Usability, User Experience Design (UxD), Human-Computer Interaction, Technology Ethics, IT Management, Systems Analysis
