Rajib Ranjan Borah’s Summary

A keen puzzle enthusiast, Rajib represented India at the 13th World Puzzle Championship at Croatia (3rd in Indian National Championship).

A computer engineering graduate, he worked at one of the most critical E-Business Suite divisions at global software giant Oracle Corporation. The Customer Data Hub product designed by his team is recognised as one of the 22 most important innovations ever at Oracle.

Apart from designing next generation software, his wide ranging responsibilities included visualizing new product features, mentoring junior colleagues, performing knowledge transfer sessions & acting as the unofficial contact for certain modules in the product bulletin board. The job involved extensive collaboration with diverse customers, product teams & colleagues from all over the world.

He has interned at three leading MNC firms in diverse geographies & diverse sectors - HCL Technologies (Software), Bloomberg LP-New York (Equity Derivatives) & Solutia Inc-Belgium (Chemicals & Automobiles).

At Solutia, he helped introduce next generation concepts into their product line by collaborating with early concept adapters (in the domains of automobiles, chemicals & software) from around the world.
