Shahar Tzafrir’s Summary

I am in the software industry for the last 16 years (and hope it never ends) with hands on experience ranging all the way from writing efficient code under demanding deadlines, to closing $$ million dollar deals, under even more demanding deadlines.

I am good in turning raw ideas into great enterprise software products. And in turning an organization evaluating that software into an organization buying that software!

I’ve led engineering groups, professional services groups, support groups, pre-sales and sales teams - and I'm proud of the accomplishments each of these groups has achieved. I worked in software organizations ranging in size from pre-seed early stage startups (read: three guys and a dream) to world class publically traded organizations.

I am passionate about great people and great software. I love defining great software, leading creating it and making it succeed in the market, especially when competition is involved.

Some of the accounts I sold to and worked with include SAP, Vodafone, BT, C&W, Visa, France Telecom, Belgacom, Telefonica, Simens, Lockheed Martin and many other Global 500 companies.
