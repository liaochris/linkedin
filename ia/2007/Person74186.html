Ronald Hermans’s Summary

During the 14 years of work experience, in all jobs focus was on being able to define goals & targets, with respect to strategic options taken (or help to take) by the company, or customer, and to derive practical tasks to accomplish and to reach those targets, with respect to cost control, quality of service, the team (right man on the right place). This required a proven analytic skill, helicopter-view and adaptation in new and/or changing environment.

Professional objective: ICT has to be a reliable supporting tool to hit business targets in line with company’s strategy, not a goal on its own. Technology has to help people to concentrate on their real tasks and responsibilities. Let ICT tools help to solve the theorem “drowning in data, but starving for knowledge”.

Professional experience & capabilities:
- Program management of large and/or complex projects
- financial management
- Business/ICT alignment
- Business Intelligence
- Project management of development & implementation of internettechnology-based applications, connected to performant, stable, secure back-end processing; with special attention towards usability towards the end-user.
