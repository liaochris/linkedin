Robin Vincent’s Summary

Co-founder of Carillon Audio Systems and Technical Director from 2000-2005 bringing the concept of the audio specific PC to the market.
Over ten years experience in building computers in the music industry and running Windows based audio/MIDI systems.
Written two books on computer music published by PC Publishing.
Written for Computer Music and Music Maker magazines.
Contributor to the Microsoft AMP Alliance.
Working experience with most PC based DAW software and hardware.
Professional video, soundtrack and theatrical soundscape experience.
Released meditative music CD's under the name Molten Meditation.
Currently running Rain Recording Ltd, providing computer solutions to the music industry across Europe, as well as acting as the global Technical Director.
