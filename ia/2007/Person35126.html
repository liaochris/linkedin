Kevin Bedard [kevin.bedard@gmail.com]’s Summary

I've been working in design since I graduated from College.I've worked in Web & Print design for great companies in Montreal such as K3-Media, CRI Agence & National Bank of Canada,while doing freelance during school, where I was employed to create and integrate design through web, with innovative ideas and the goal of creating what suited best the project, in term of graphical style and user-friendlyness.

After all those contracts and freelance I got into the videogames company, a big achievment in my life. I was hired at Electronic Arts Montreal as a Front-End Artist, pushing my creativity limits further as than I could imagine, improving my technical skills in a fast-paced environement, and getting experience, in such a big company, with the teamwork and the self-management.
