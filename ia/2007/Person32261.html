Maggie Keenan, Ed.D.’s Summary

Maggie F. Keenan, Ed.D., is an expert on strategic philanthropy for companies and foundations. Maggie is dedicated to helping her clients maximize the impact of their giving by designing strategic philanthropy giving programs that make a difference for clients, communities and the causes they care about. I make it our priority everyday to help clients make a difference in ways that count.
