Arthur Clark’s Summary

My corporate bio

Prior to a career in executive search, Arthur had gained management experience with a major bank and insurance company.

In 1988, his search practice, Arthur Clark & Associates was merged into the newly formed firm of Barrett Rose & Lee Inc. to co-found, what at the time was, a new executive search consulting practice.

His industry experience includes software, manufacturing, technology, communications, construction, transportation and real estate, as well as professional services firms.

His current focus is working with clients to build or rebuild corporate management teams and fill senior staff specialist positions in the fields of Sales, Marketing, Presales and Service Delivery for Technology Vendors and related organizations.

He has been an Officer and has served on the Board of Directors of several private and public companies.

Arthur Clark’s Specialties:

Barrett Rose & Lee recruits senior and mid-level executives in sales and marketing, finance, human resources, and technical and engineering for manufacturing. Our firm is organized in specialist teams focussed on industry categories. Specifically: software creators, electronic hardware vendors and related professional service providers, and automotive systems and parts producers - see www.barrettrose.com for details.
