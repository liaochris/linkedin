Scott Knowles’s Summary

There is an interesting business analogy in Steven Covey’s “The Seven Habits of Highly Effective People”, and again referenced in “Naked Conversations”. He metaphorically describes a jungle in which a management team is hacking its way through the dense undergrowth. A Sr. executive comes in, reviews the team, and then climbs a tree to get a broader view. He calls down with alarm: “wrong jungle!” The senior manger glares back up at him and replies, “leave us alone. We’re making great progress”
An all too common scenario in today’s fast moving business climate, most everyone can relate.

Having lived directly within the circulatory system of numerous highly successful – new-market - disruptive - innovative ventures we bring a unique perspective and track record for anyone seeking to build and or grow their business. My real passion lies in participating and assisting those wishing to change, influence, impact and lead their particular market space.
