Gary Turner’s Summary

Gary has more than 18 years continuous experience in the information technology and software applications business and has been consistently successful throughout this time. He began his career in technology with six years in new business sales roles before moving on to channel management, software product marketing and senior sales and marketing positions at Pegasus Software, a financial and accounting software vendor with over 20,000 clients in the small to mid-market sectors in the UK and Ireland.

Under his leadership the team at Pegasus successfully turned around and delivered strong and year-on-year organic revenue and profit growth, in parallel with achieving several significant service and operational performance objectives.

Gary famously demonstrates force of nature levels of passion and infectious enthusiasm for; technology, business, selling, marketing, being successful, communicating, winning, generating ideas, strategic vision, leadership and, evidently, comma separated values.

He has been cited by some of the world's leading business thinkers and writers including the business management guru, Tom Peters, and in 2005 he was cited by Computer Reseller News in the "CRN A-List" as one of the UK technology sector's most influential people.

Alongside his duties at Pegasus Software, Gary holds advisory positions with two not for profit organisations; the IT Faculty of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of England and Wales (ICAEW) and a leading representative body for the European software industry, BASDA, the Business Applications Software Developers Association.

He is also an advocate of writing about oneself in the third person.
