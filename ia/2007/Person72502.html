Richard Hoeg’s Summary

My career at Honeywell has followed the rise of the micro-computer and the world wide web. Back in the 1980's I started Honeywell's computer training program. In the 1990's I built some of Honeywell's first internal and external web sites, and now in the next century I focus upon knowledge management tools and eLearning for all of Honeywell, but with a particular focus on engineering. Some of my recent efforts include the creation of internal technical blogs, wikis, virtual libraries and global access to eLearning (both as a learner and/or an author).

Richard Hoeg’s Specialties:

I am an expert and Greenbelt certified in knowledge management. As opposed to using high priced tools, my approach is to find user friendly, practical low cost resources that may be quickly adopted by the Honeywell user community.
