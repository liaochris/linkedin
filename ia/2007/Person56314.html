Matthew Burke’s Summary

- Over ten years project management and marketing experience in the entertainment industry, specifically in the hobby game and video game fields.

- I've had the good fortune to manage the development and release of card games, role-playing games, and, most recently, online marketing programs for Atom Films and Nintendo.

- I'm passionate about producing and marketing games and, thanks to my experience at Atom Entertainment and Nintendo, I love producing Web sites that bring fresh approaches to the marketing message while immersing the visitor in an entertaining experience.
