Daniel L. Potter’s Summary

Daniel L. Potter runs Silverthreaded Information Design, a boutique design firm specializing in standards-based web design & development. His current clients include Intuitive Surgical, Inc., RITA Medical, Inc., Morpheus Software and The Conference of Delegates of California Bar Associations (CDCBA). Past clients from his time as Senior Producer at Addictive Media include Mattel Media, Walt Disney Imagineering and The Learning Company. He studied Comparative Literature at Stanford University, Cornell University, the Wilhelms-Westfälische Universität Münster and the Freie Universität Berlin. Daniel maintains a website devoted to the work of legendary French filmmaker Chris Marker, and is currently working on a theoretical book about feng shui and information design. He makes a mean espresso and listens to a lot of ambient music.
