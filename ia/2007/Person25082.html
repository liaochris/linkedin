David Rodecker’s Summary

DAVID has been involved with high-technology companies for over a decade and has a history of creating and managed technology teams. Before RelevantYellow, David built Local.com which became the fastest growing local search destination hitting 4.4MM unique visitors in 3 months. Prior to Local, David was a founding partner of Mail2World, where he created an massive email service, hosting millions of email boxes. Prior to dotcoms, David was in the financial industry with PIMCO where he establish a team that automated transaction and workforce processes.

David has formed partnerships with TargusInfo, Yahoo, Verizon, AT&T, Yellow Pages, Amacai, ICQ (AOL), iVillage, EsMas and NetIdentity.

David has appeared on CNBC and TechTV. David has spoken at conferences including: Inbox Event, ISPCon, Microsoft SQL class, and has been a guest instructor at Cal State University, Business School.

David graduated Phi Kappa Phi from in Engineering from the University of Southern California and obtained a MS from the University of California, Los Angeles.
