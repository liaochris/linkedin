Liz Tagami’s Summary

Visionary merchant with over 25 years of experience optimizing sales and profits in both direct mail and traditional store arenas. Capable business manager with comprehensive skill set, including brand development, global sourcing, product development, assortment planning, store visual merchandising, store design, and catalog layout. Adept at assessing and assembling merchandising and merchandise operations related teams of people, systems infrastructure, and financial plans in rapid growth, specialty retail environments. Proven brand builder who can turn around, grow and drive businesses.
