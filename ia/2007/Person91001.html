Will Eagle’s Summary

Starting as a web developer, I moved quickly into project management of website development and co-ordination of marketing campaigns for Virgin Management. I then moved to Virgin Mobile in Canada, taking on online as well as managing all of the below the line marketing communications.

I'm really a brand guy who loves to figure out what interactive can do, and how it can integrate with everything else - from other media in the marketing mix through to the consumers' day to day life.

After taking some time off, I found the perfect role for me at MTV Canada.
