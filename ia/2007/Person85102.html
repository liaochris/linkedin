Pamela Heywood’s Summary

Pam studied drawing and painting at State University of New York (SUNY) Oswego and continued her training at Rochester Institute of Technology, earning a Masters Degree in Fine Art. She has been a professional artist and designer for seven years
and much of her current work incorporates her love of design with her passion for, and education in fine art, using a wide array of media. This results in a unique ability to add distinctive style to each piece, giving it a unique and vibrant personality. An insatiable appetite for the visual arts and on-going her role as an educator keep Pam at the forefront of design.

Pamela Heywood’s Specialties:

Graphic art and design, branding and identity, illustration, painting, drawing, art and design education
