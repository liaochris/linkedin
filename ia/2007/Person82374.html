Steven A Bailey’s Summary

Currently the Director of Interactive Media for Homes & Land Affiliates, LLC and the driving force behind the Homes & Land Online University.

I bring an extensive 25-year career in the field of advertising sales, multimedia marketing, management and training. My experience includes introducing new products, increasing public awareness and improving customer relations.

An admitted serial entrepreneur, I started and successfully ran Creative Alternatives, “Guerrilla Marketing Consultants” for over 12 years in Phoenix, Arizona. Turned my passion for travel & adventure into a nationally distributed college magazine and website. Launched a video production company to push the boundaries of cinematic, interactive DVD sales & marketing presentations. And was on the scene with pre-bubble pioneers in the Voice-Over-IP race of the late 90’s. Prior to my current position, I served as Vice President of New Business Development for a dynamic Flash Streaming Media technology start-up under the umbrella of TSV Consulting.

Interactive Media, Digital Media, Streaming Media, Webcasting, Podcasting, Online Training, Video Production, Web 2.0, Integrated Marketing, Small Business Consulting, Public Speaking
