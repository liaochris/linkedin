Anthony de Kerf’s Summary

Founder of two technology companies in the field of Keyboard, Video and Mouse Switching. Originally started Tron International Inc. to provide product sales and services to fortune 1000 and technology organizations.

Tron has been a systems integrator since 1990 providing services to both end users and the hardware manufacturers. In 1994 we co-authored the first industry white paper on switching, and since have authored or co-authored over fifty additional technology papers and related articles.

More recently, I'm involved in development of software at start-up Global Serv Inc. This software provides a common user interface for managing data centers as much as integrating KVM, video and other types of switches used in the data center, QA departments, network operation centers, AV systems and several other market segments that have never been defined by the hardware developers themselves.

Event Driven Automated KVM Switching. KVM Push™ technology immediately pushes server control to technicians upon system failure. Global Serv software reduces server downtime closing an expensive gap in business continuity management. It provides faster recovery over today’s manual switching methods by immediately pushing server control to technicians upon system failure.

Other Global Serv solutions provide desktop switching to reduce common user errors associated with keyboard switching while reducing the effort of using the On-Screen-Display (OSD) and providing easy access to features usually hidden within the firmware of most KVM devices.

Anthony de Kerf’s Specialties:

Automating remote management by leveraging remote control technologies. Strategic KVM Switch Network designs focused on user throuput and increased security through segmented KVM switch topologies. A recognized thought leader, industry writer and analyst covering the competitive features and capabilities of KVM technology. Inventor in the field of KVM switches and maintenenace network management.
