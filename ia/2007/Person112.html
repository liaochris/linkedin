Janis Pettit’s Summary

Nineteen years of experience as an entrepreneur owning four highly successful businesses--10 years as a small business consultant and corporate trainer. Extensive experience as a motivational speaker. Articles published locally and internationally. Featured in The Business Journal for creative marketing strategy.

I focus on the key challenges that small business owners need to overcome to reach their business vision. Focus areas include marketing, systems development, sales, business strategy and planning and achieving independence and life balance.

Services include:

- CEO coaching
- Online marketing training and web design
- Tele-seminars
- Group coaching
- Live presentations and keynotes
- E-courses and E-books
- Business brokering and staging (buy and sell) with our partner, Enlign Business Brokers

o Our corporate resource center is a partnership of some of the best trainers around. We cover a number of key topics including sales, leadership and team building.
