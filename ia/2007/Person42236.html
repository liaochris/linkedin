Jodi Horner’s Summary

Claims engineering services for 837 X12 P, 837 X12 I, Proprietary formats, 835 X12, for IDX Groupcast and GE Centricity Customers /Users.

We offer back-end revenue cycle management services by assisting provider organizations with identifying payer problems, coding errors, registration errors and offering expert claim solutions.

We will collaborate with other analysts, engineers, programmers and as necessary to obtain information on project limitations and capabilities, performance requirements, interfaces, upgrades and other company initiatives.

I specifically specialize in creating new e-paper claim formats, HCFA and UB92, X12- EDI (electronic data intercahnge) by coding payer requirements using the Groupcast formatter, writing a test plan for customers for in house testing and providing post-production support of the formats. I will analyze user needs and software/ payer requirements to determine feasibility of changes within time and cost constraints

This includes compliance testing using external compliance tools, and execute external testing with insurance intermediaries and end payers when required. These services also include the delivery of new communications scripts to send electronic data and claims, print image claims and statements, and other output files to clearinghouses or end payers, using AIX, TERM, PERL,FTP scripting programs.

We are available to visit customer sites as requested by customer for support services, training services, design consulting.
