Phil Edmonds’s Summary

We don't believe in the death of the 30 sec TV commercial, but at the same time we don't call digital marketing 'new media' - it's here, it's now and for most people it's not just a nice thing to have.

At Townhouse, we look for the most appropriate solution to business problems, not just advertising - we try to understand the whole marketing mix. There's every chance that these might include sponsorship, events, distribution, affinity marketing, digital, mobile, direct, press, radio, or TV. We don't pretend to be experts in all these fields, but we know someone whose is, and we can help most clients use them together to get a good synergy.

As such, we believe marketing departments and agencies have to move beyond the confines of ATL & BTL - it’s no longer relevant. Media convergence has already happened, and as marketers we need to move on and embrace new opportunities to make ourselves relevant to consumers, however they encounter our brands.
