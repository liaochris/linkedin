Karla Porter (karla.porter@gmail.com)’s Summary

I have two distinct professional endeavors; that of Partner in a Mayan Riviera Real Estate Brokerage, First Investment Real Estate, and Recruiter in the Call Center industry with Telerx Marketing, Inc.

My fascination with computers as a means of communication began in 1980 Air Base in Germany sending messages around the world on DSTE II equipment. Each component was about the size of a large chest freezer and what was the equivalent of a PC today took up an entire room! Since then, as a user I have always tried to stay on top of new innovations and find ways to utilize them in a meaningful way. That I am able to create and grow global relationships and do business through a "notebook" never ceases to amaze me.

In my rather eclectic past I have managed a timeshare salesroom, owned a café and was the Pastry Chef, created a children's free lunch program and founded an SPCA all in Cozumel and Playa del Carmen, Mexico. I worked at a Spanish language TV affiliated of Telemundo, the now defunct Spanish language magazine El Espetacular and sold building supplies in the US. I spent a glorious year teaching English in Mallorca, Spain.

I would need many lifetimes to accomplish everything I I would like to so I must pick and choose carefully in this one since I am thinking I am only getting one shot....

I believe in open networking and invite you to join my network. Hopefully, we will do better together than just be names on each other's page. ~Karla
