Michelle Vargas’s Summary

My goals:

Concept Designer - I've been doing character concept design recreationally for seven years. Since I started at AISD my skill has only improved and even expanded into other areas I previously had no interest in. My passion has always been to develop characters. Being able to see them visually as well as mentally has made a great improvement on my written design work.

Environment Concept Artist - Over the past year I have discovered my passion for environment design. Taken from the same drive of character design, my ability to put what I see in my head onto paper has given me a satisfaction I never thought I would have for environment drawing. I have learned that they have just as much a story to tell as a character and just as much personality. They're subtle. They're bold. They're proof of life.

Writer - I have been writing creatively for 11 years and this is where my desire to draw stemmed from. My first specialty was sci-fi, then it moved into fantasy, and recently, present day reality. I have always had a great love for designing characters with compelling personalities and in-depth back stories. I enjoy taking these characters and supporting them with others so that they can each shine in their own light as I weave them through an adventurous story. The design of the world, with its cultures and life, is just as enjoyable. Because this is where the history stems from. This is where it can be destroyed and hidden so that my heroes and villains can take their goals and drives from. Storytelling for videogames [the epic RPG market especially], is something I would very much like to get into sometime during my upcoming professional career.

Michelle Vargas’s Specialties:

Creative writing, character development, environment design, world and culture design, and texturing for any environment.
