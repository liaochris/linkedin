Lan-Chi Lam’s Summary

An information designer by profession, a story-teller at heart – I believe technology is a vehicle for storytelling. My goal is to redefine “templates” to allow people to tell their stories. At the heart of this process is good design; the design philosophies I follow are clarity, communication, content, context and continuity.

Lan-Chi Lam’s Specialties:

Information & Content Design, Visual Design, User Interface, Software Prototyping, Creative Direction, Creative Producing, Team-Building, Fun !
