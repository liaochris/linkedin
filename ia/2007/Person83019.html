Sudhir Jangir’s Summary

I am Sudhir Jangir, graduated from IIT Delhi in 1998, and having an experience of over 8 years in technology domain, ex-Co-Founder of Algorism Softech Pvt Ltd, and presently Co-Founder and Director at S M Macario Software Pvt. Ltd. Before Algorism I had worked with Nagarro, Fiorano, Induslogic...

S M Macario Software is a young and vibrant startup run by a group of IIT India + Georgia Tech USA + BITS Pilani India pass outs (finest engineering institutes of the world) to specifically provide companies with a trusted partner for quality outsourcing.

Our competencies lie in setting up offshore software development teams for custom product/application development. We are always ready for our evaluation without any cost to prove ourselves.

We have been developing high end applications for our clients using the most advanced technologies like Java J2EE, Java Open Source, Data ware Solutions (Open Source), LAMP, Mobile application, GIS, Microsoft .Net 2.0, C++, Oracle, Microsoft SQL server databases, MySQL, PostGres, Reporting Services, MOSS, Documentum, Liferay, JBOSS, BEA Weblgoic etc.

Our current and past clients have been very satisfied with our services and our ability to maintain on-going communication with our partners so that there are no surprises down the road—either in terms of cost or delivery time.

We are based in Gurgaon, India and work for our clients in US, UK, & India.

We are looking for people who can work with us to grow our business further. If you feel that there is an opportunity of mutual benefit, I shall be happy to discuss the same.

We are located in the vicinity of International companies like American Express, IBM and just 10 minutes drive from the International Airport. Click here to see our office pictures-> http://www.smmacario.com/html/corporate/Infrastructure.aspx
