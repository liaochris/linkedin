Ryan J. Corey’s Summary

My experience is spread across marketing and sales. I spent the earlier part of my still growing career in B2B sales with a Fortune 500 company. I received extensive sales training from this company and began to develop some essential professional qualities (ie. mannerism and appearance, accountability, professional development, presentation etc.). I had success in this position and then left because of an opportunity that a partnered organization (my current company) provided me. My current company was seeking an individual that would thrust the company from a standstill into major growth.
