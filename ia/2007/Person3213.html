Alex Chua [coach88(at)gmail(dot)com]’s Summary

A graduate of Singapore Institute of Management, Alex Chua has a degree with distinction from RMIT University, has been recognised for scholastic achievement and excellence by Golden Key International Honour Society, and was awarded The RMIT Business Dean's Award in 2002. Alex is also the founder of Beyond Possibilities, a Training & Consultancy firm, and iROM.org, an Internet Registry of Marriage for couples.

Alex's experience is in retail sales, wholesales, corporate sales, project management, distributor sourcing, assessment & profiling, export market research & business development, export marketing & branding , and export product registration. He is especially familiar with packaged food & supplements in Singapore & Australia.

As a Speed Learning Coach, Alex can help you and your Children Learn faster & at the same time Understand & Remember more. Learning is not just for Exams. Awakening the Inner Genius triggers positive transformation at all levels & in all areas of one’s Life. Students will learn to Schedule Success, discover & unleash their Genius Potential & ultimately to lead a Meaningful life of Abundance.My students include those preparing for PSLE, ‘O’ & ‘A’ Levels, as well as those taking diplomas, degrees & certificates.

Alex has co-facilitated Accelerated Learning Programs such as Speed Reading and Mind Mapping since 2000. He has since developed several holistic Speed Learning Programs based on his proprietary BARE your HEART of SUCCESS™ Methodology. He has also facilitated Leadership (via Entrepreneur Oasis) & Financial Literacy (via EduWealth & MoneySENSE) Programs in MOE Schools in Singapore.

Popular programs include The Beyond Possibilities Speed Learning Program, Clarity Quest™ & Destiny Attunement™ etc.

You are invited to join Alex on his Mission to Create a World of Possibilities by Awakening the Genius in you and your Children.
