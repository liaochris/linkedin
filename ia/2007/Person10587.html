Bernd Fischer’s Summary

Bernd Fischer is the Manager for Electronic Design Automation at Xignal Technologies AG, Munich, Germany. He is in charge of the development and continuous enhancement of process design kits for sub mircon CMOS technologies as well as the support of Xignal's design team in doing high performance analog mixed-signal ICs. From 1998 to 2001, he was with Cadence Design Systems in Munich, as a Senior Application Engineer of the Custom IC group, responsible for technical customer support and product training classes. Prior to Cadence he was with the Siemens Semiconductor Automotive Division (now Infineon), Munich, working as a Physical Design Engineer on Smart Power ICs in Bipolar-CMOS-DMOS technologies. Bernd graduated in electrical engineering at the University of Applied Sciences Furtwangen, Furtwangen, Germany, in 1996.
