Liam Proven’s Summary

• Nineteen years working in the computer industry, including technical support, training, sales and journalism.
• Independent consultant on all aspects of personal and business computing, covering networking, hardware, software and communications.
• Experienced in all phases of business computing: specification, selection, purchase, installation, integration, user training & support, repairs and upgrades.
• Familiar with independent and team working, for resellers and end-users, including corporate environments, workshop, sales floor and in the field on customer sites.
• Specialities include configuration/optimisation/troubleshooting of operating systems, networks and communications, from planning through installation to support of live mission-critical systems.
• Expertise includes familiarity with virtually all significant operating systems and applications, enabling the support of almost any computer, as well as training of users (from novices to experts) in all aspects of computer use, including system customisation.
• Writer, presenter and trainer on all the above areas and non-computer-related subjects.
• Many articles published in most major UK IT journals; presentations to live audiences and via TV, radio and Internet.
