Richard Harrison’s Summary

A Senior Recruitment Specialist / Director with nearly 10 years' professional experience in recruiting IT & Business professionals on a contract basis for clients throughout the UK and beyond. Also former semi professional tennis player!

Providing contract professionals across varying skills spectrum / project life-cycle: Project / Programme Managers, Business Analysts, Architects, Software Developers / Programmers, DBAs, SAP/Oracle ERP Consultants, PC / Desktop Support.

Delivering recruitment services across a range of industry sectors / vertical markets: Management Consultancies, Telecoms, Engineering, Manufacturing, Retail, Travel, Web / eCommerce, Financial Services, Public Sector and Utilities providers.
