Matthieu Oliviers’s Summary

I am an Associate at McKinsey & Company’s, where I focuse on metal and mining institutions, on assignments concerning marketing, strategy, operational and organizational issues.
Included in my work experience at McKinsey, I:
- supported a mining company to develop a perspective on iron ore refining investments
- optimized the forecast of the global steel consumption using sector based modeling
- helped valuation of synergies for a merger of two long products steel-makers
- assessed potential acquisitions and competition dynamics for a regional entry strategy of a long products steel-maker
- evaluated a Chinese steel company and its specific market in the perspective of share acquisition or join venture

Before joining McKinsey, I worked for 7 years at Arcelor mainly in operation and technical management. With these responsibilities, I:
- led the definition of sales & operation strategies to increase market shares in the construction sector for Europe
- managed the day-to-day life and the technical development of production plans while attaining ambitious efficiency objectives
- conducted research and development programs for various processes of integrated coil plans

I also also worked for several years in the industrial automation industry. During this period, I worked as sales & marketing engineer at Omron electronics and as researcher in robotics at Ecole Polytechnique de Montréal, Canada and at Università tor Vergata, Roma, Italy.

October 2007
