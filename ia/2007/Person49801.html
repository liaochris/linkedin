Lance Hoffman’s Summary

Technology professional and dynamic leader with proven experience in creating effective online marketing programs, clearly defining product positioning and messaging, and building successful teams in diverse and complex environments. Great breadth of experiences in Internet ecommerce, marketing, and product management, including ten years in executive level positions with both Fortune 500 and start-up companies. Significant International experience including co-founding an online auction company in Hong Kong and having managed a team of Chinese sales and marketing professionals. Exposure to varied environments and broad spectrum of technologies, customers, partnerships and internal organizations.

Lance Hoffman’s Specialties:

Ecommerce, online marketing, International expansion, account management, product marketing, and product management.
