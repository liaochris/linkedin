Bill Vick’s Summary

By definition I'm a HeadHunter, serial entrepreneur, author, publisher, big biller and recruiting coach/trainer. I believe everybody has the ability to achieve success in life and my goal is to help others succeed. LinkedIn is one of the tools that has changed my business and my life.

After successfully creating and publishing two books (LinkedIn For Recruiting and Big Biller) I have launched a new publishing effort to help others create multimedia books.

In my early career I was involved in executive sales management with fortune 500 companies such as Revlon, Max Factor and Helene Curtis. In the mid 70's I entered the computer retail and software industries as an entrepreneur. I was involved in taking a software company public in addition to being the founder of a chain of computer stores in Dallas.

In 1986 I joined Management Recruiters International and was named their National Rookie of the year in 1987. I then ran my own retained search firm, Vick & Associates for the next 20 years and along the way created a software company that developed an ATS system for Recruitment firms which I sold in 1994.

In 1995 I founded Recruiters OnLine Network, a top 100 site which I sold in 2002.

I have been a founder and board member of the Pinnacle Society recognizing achievement in the staffing industry, a national speaker for NAPS, served on the Board of Directors of The Texas Association Staffing Services (TAPS), was an officer with the Metroplex Association of Personnel Services (MAAPS) as well as a founder of the local Dallas Independent Recruiters Group (IRG).

For a snap shot of my current activities please visit my personal home page at http://www.BillVick.com.

I welcome linking up with others.
