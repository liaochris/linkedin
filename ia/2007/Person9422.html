Barb Dickey’s Summary

Barb Dickey is President of Cre8tivision, a senior-level advertising and marketing communications consultancy that has created national and global campaigns for a wide variety clients including: United Nations, McDonalds, MTV, PNC Bank, PAC Committee for American Dental Association, Health Benefits Coalition, Asbestos Alliance, Pharmaceutical Care Management Association, AOL Time Warner, AARP, IBM Internet Security Systems, Plastics Council of America, Water Advocates, Calvin Klein, Microsoft, Mobil One, Uniroyal, Farming and Wildlife Advisory Group and NY Coalition for The Homeless to name a few.

A former Creative Director with Arnold Worldwide, Barb shepherds strategic thinking and a focused creative instinct to create the “Big Idea” that moves the masses and brands. Her career spans 15 years with such firms as DDB, Young & Rubicam, Ogilvy PR Worldwide and Porter Novelli.

Living in the heart of DC, one of her special areas of practice are social marketing and public affairs advocacy (non-partisan), serving as a strategy consultant for the President’s Aids/HIV initiatives, 2005-2007. State Children's Health Insurance Program (SCHIPS) Initiative 2006-2007. Barb’s won numerous awards for creative excellence including the Cannes Gold and Silver Lions.

She graduated Art Center College Of Design in Pasadena, Ca. and The McDonough Business School at Georgetown University, Washington DC, Graduate Program in entrepreneurship with a focus on start-ups.

While Cre8tivision.com is under construction, you can request a CD Portfolio of Barb's work, simply by contacting bdickey@cre8tivision.com.

Barb Dickey’s Specialties:

Special area of practice is social marketing and public affairs advocacy. Clients include: UNICEF Global Movement For Children Against AIDS. Water Advocates. Farming and Wildlife Advisory Group. Solidary Doctors Foundation. Foundation For Prevention of Violence Against Women.
