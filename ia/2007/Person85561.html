William Thompson’s Summary

Mr. Thompson represents more than thirty years experience in Fortune 500, real estate, consumer services, financial services, environmental services, franchise services and small-business entrepreneurial and consulting organizations. A strong, versatile leader of people and teams, he is a marketing and customer retention consultant with an excellent reputation for results, innovation, creativity, strategic vision and service. He has been a significant contributor to two large real estate service corporations’ business transformations; the acquisition of two large companies; the recruiting and training of national sales forces and he founded his own business that has grown steadily since 1991.
