Amiel Blajchman’s Summary

I have expertise in natural resource use, climate change, and environmental management. In addition, I am an experienced policy writer, moderator and presenter.

I am interested in helping natural resource companies achieve their goals of becoming sustainable. While understanding that mining, energy extraction, etc. are non-renewable activities, it is still possible for companies in these areas to move beyond simple Corporate Social Responsibility/Philanthropy, and to realize the gains achieved by integrating the three pillars of sustainable development (economy, environment, and social) into their day-to-day work.
