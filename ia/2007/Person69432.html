Kristie Prinz’s Summary

Practice:

Kristie Prinz's practice focuses exclusively on the counseling and negotiation of domestic and international intellectual property licenses and other commercial contracts. Her clients include established and emerging companies in the software, fiber optics, biotechnology, pharmaceutical, medical device, and commercial services industries.

Publications:

Writes the California Biotech Law Blog at www.californiabiotechlaw.com.

Featured as a writer on IP issues for Lawyers.com's weekly column "Ask a Lawyer."

Co-author of "When a Business Begins a Blog: It's Easy But Is it Safe?" published in the Business Law Today, January-February, 2007.

Co-author of "Ruling Brings to Light DMCA's Broad Scope" published in the New York Law Journal on June 9, 2003.

Author of "Unlocking the Secrets to Executive Coaching" published in the Women Lawyers Journal in Summer, 2005.

Recent Speaking Engagements:

"What You Need to Know About CAN-SPAM," Santa Clara Bar Cyberspace Committee, June 8, 2004;

"The Art of Telling Your Story Workshop, U.S. Chamber of Commerce/National Institute of Standards and Technology, Advanced Technology Program," San Jose, September 7, 2005;

"Changing Face of Telecommunication (VOIP, WiFi)," International Technology Law Association, 35th Anniversary Annual Meeting and World Conference (San Francisco), May 5, 2006;

"Who is Watching Out for Your In-House Career: How a Professional Coach Can Help You Gain the Edge," Association of Corporate Counsel–America San Francisco Bay Chapter, May 25, 2006; and

"Understanding the Acquisitions Process: How to Use the Sale and Purchase of Assets to Grow Your Business," National Association of Women Business Owners, Silicon Valley Chapter (San Jose), July 18, 2006.
