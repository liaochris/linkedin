Ron Gould’s Summary

OBJECTIVE
To lead a group of positive, like-minded, growth-oriented people in a creative environment that is inclusive, dynamic and profitable, at an organization where vision and flexibility are perfect partners in this age of a new renaissance. Oh, and to have fun doing it, of course.

SUMMARY
Highly motivated, creative and versatile executive with over thirty years experience in providing superior solutions to a wide range of marketing and communication initiatives. Especially skilled at pulling together diverse specialists to solve complex problems in a highly creative way. Excellent management, leadership and communication skills. In short, a balance of left and right brain goodness.

Ron Gould’s Experience

  • Executive Creative Director

    Ignited Minds

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1999 — 2007 (8 years)

Responsible for all creative output of Print, Broadcast and Online Advertising; Package and Collateral Design; Video and Audio Production; Web sites, Tradeshows and Events. Managed up to 80% of staff at times, with a culture that promoted unbridled creativity in an atmosphere of collaboration and camaraderie. And lived to tell about it.
