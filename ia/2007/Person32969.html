Graydon McKee - CISSP, GSEC’s Summary

Strategic Solutions Executive

Responsible for the development of Information Security Solutions for commerical and federal clients in the Eastern United States.

Director of Federal Enterprise Security Solutions and Information Security Subject Matter Expert for the Enterprise Security Group - Unisys Corporation (Federal Government Group) Advises clients on the balance of risk, business/ mission needs, and technology with regard to Information Security matters. Expert in the field of Certification and Accreditation (FISMA/NIST and DITSCAP) and Continuity of Operations Planning/Information Systems Disaster Recovery. Works with clients and across teams to conceptualize, architect, design, implement, and support integrated solutions encompassing security management, governance, and technology which support business needs and balance risk and cost issues. Adept at communicating with all levels of an organization from the technical staff up to and including executive level personnel.

Began in this position as a mid-level architect, interacting with personnel of equal or lower level skill sets and have since been elevated to lead and mentor functional teams and conduct executive-level briefings; both internally and for client-facing engagements.

Team Lead for National Level Seminar Series developing and delivering Certification and Accreditation seminars to senior agency officials (managers, directors, C-Level executives). To date the seminar series has been delivered to representatives from over 600 federal agencies and offices (both Federal Civilian and Department of Defense).
