Elisa Chan’s Summary

Passionate about the internet and travel I initially wanted to become an air hostess for Singapore Airlines (because I liked the Singapore Girl image) so at the age of 14 she Kick started her Career in Travel with Singapore Airlines as a work experience student. The week of work experience opened my eyes to the travel industry, finding herself in a different department everyday, she gained the basic knowledge of Ticketing, Reservations, Cargo Operations, Airport Operations Accounts and Marketing. Throughout the week at SIA I developed a better understanding on other categories of the Travel industry she could potentially build a career on.

Moving right along at 16 I started her first TAFE course in conjunction with her high school studies and began working for a local Chinese tour operator and travel agency on the weekends, upon graduating from High School at 17 she received her first
contract with Australia’s Largest Cruise Line Carnival Corporation – commonly known as P&O Cruises.

With some knowledge of the internet and having developed a few sites while earning some extra income as an affiliate while I was travelling, I am currently working as a specialist in Affiliate Marketing for one of the worlds most dynamic and innovative online Travel Co’s Flairview Travel by Travelport Inc whilst on my spare time I also enjoy developing my own website http://www.SheTravels.net - a Portal for female travellers with blogs about all my stories and adventures I’ve encountered along the way and around the world.
