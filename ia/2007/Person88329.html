Steve Glaiser’s Summary

Optimistic, business-savvy, and creative product quality professional in continuous pursuit of new and challenging test paradigms and organizations. Passionate about more predictive and less reactive approaches to product quality. Adjusts quickly, sees the big picture, and ensures that the teams and tools are "just right" in these times of ever changing, rapidly evolving, product definition to customer delivery processes. High energy and relentless determination to get the job done on time and under budget - with an eye to the future.

Steve Glaiser’s Specialties:

Automation Framework design; budget management ($100k to $7M); offshore test design, development, and management; networking; data security; fraud transaction monitoring; handhelds, PDAs, and mobile communications; data acquisition and playback systems; internet properties/sites; enterprise systems; embedded systems; shrink wrap software; navigation systems (Radar, Inertial, Stellar, and Satellite); desktop publishing; OEM and ODM vendor management experience.
