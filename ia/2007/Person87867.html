Tullio Siragusa’s Summary

Prior to Ansearch:

• Joined Operative with global top line responsibility and P&L responsibility for existing accounts. Have grown the company over 100% during tenure thus far, adding over 100 new clients (28 in Q4 alone), with blue chip clients like Dow Jones, CBS, PBS, NBCU, and many others. Recently doubled the company's valuation and the value of the stock 4 folds.

• One of 40 lead executives/partners with full P&L responsibility for managing Cognizant's $1.5BB business; Responsible for the banking and financial services consulting practice development via alliances with leading banking & financial services software companies that strengthened the company's domain expertise, significantly moved the organization up market and differentiates the company.

• While with Acxiom Corporation, developed a CRM BPO offering with strategic partner Accenture and led several complex mega transformational outsourcing deals for grid enabled data management solutions that drove better customer acquisition, loyalty and analytics.

• While with Computer Sciences Corporation was instrumental in leveraging partnerships to drive the very first new on-site/off-shore delivery deal in the company’s history, created best practices across the organization and bolstered the company’s commitment to developing a global shared services delivery model.

• Experienced with post M&A operations integration consulting having created the offering and served as national Director for PricewaterhouseCoopers’ transaction services, advisory and assurance practice.

• While at Softtek managed a strategic partnership and joint venture with Ernst & Young who ultimately acquired 25% of the company, and another joint venture with OAO Technologies; added over $100MM of booked business to the company.

• Moved up the ranks to global account management and business unit leadership with MCI Telecommunications, with a $90MM P&L.
