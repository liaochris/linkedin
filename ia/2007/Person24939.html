David Reynolds’s Summary

Presently working as Chief Technology Officer at Quantlab Financial LLC., the research and development firm in the Quantlab group of companies, with overall responsibility for ensuring the efficiency, scalability and diversity of the firm's trading technology.

Designed and developed the firm's trading system and market data management systems, corporate actions systems, and several process, system, portfolio and risk monitoring systems.

In prior posts, worked as a mathematician developing real-time control and optimization systems, and as a post-doctoral researcher in optimization.

Currently interested in and looking for individuals who wish to help our firm design, develop and operate bleeding-edge trading technology.

David Reynolds’s Specialties:

System architecture and optimization, trading systems, market data, databases, tech team management and organization.
