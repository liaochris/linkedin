Craig Chamberlain’s Summary

Security architect based in Boston doing work at a variety of companies around the world. Recently I've been working on projects in SIM tuning and customization, event correlation, behavioral threat detection, network vulnerability modeling and attack simulation, immunization against malicious code, phishing countermeasures, insider threat detection and prevention, service hardening, intrusion and extrusion detection / prevention, penetration testing, web application security and mandatory policy enforcement.

SIM and intrusion detection tuning expert. Experienced intrusion detection and forensic analyst. Seventeen years professional hands on experience in IT, network and security engineering, security architecture and risk assessment. Ten years experience as a consultant, professional services engineer and project manager. Experienced practice manager. Trained in security tradecraft while working at MIT. Alumnus of several SANS tracks and Bejtlich's elite TCP/IP Weapons School in Washington DC.

Published at DHS Science & Technology 2005, presented at ACSAC 2005. Publications and contact information are available at http://www.craigchamberlain.com
