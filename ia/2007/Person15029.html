Catherine Colmenares’s Summary

Cathy Colmenares has spent over 15 years in international and US Hispanic sales and marketing. She held key management positions as the National Advertising Sales Manager at el diario/LA PRENSA, New York’s oldest Spanish-language daily newspaper, and as the Senior Director of Global Client Relations at StarMedia Networks, Latin America’s first Internet portal.

Ms. Colmenares has always had a passion for community service, working as a volunteer with organizations such as Hands on Miami, The Guardian Ad Litem program, and The American Red Cross. Eventually that passion inspired her to pursue nonprofit work full time.

In 2004, Ms. Colmenares joined One Economy, an international nonprofit organization that uses technology to empower low-income families. In her role as the Miami Program Director, Colmenares promotes the resources and tools of their consumer portal, The Beehive, www.beehivemiami.org. In addition, she develops municipal policies, youth leadership programs, and community outreach initiatives. She recently took on the added role of developing marketing and public relations strategies for the East Coast and Southeast regions. Her work with One Economy has been featured in the Miami Herald, Miami Today, State Tech magazine, and on NBC and Univision.
