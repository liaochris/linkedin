Gareth Davies’s Summary

Gareth is Founder and Managing Director of Upside Outcomes Ltd and FODA Consulting Ltd.

Both companies focus on gaining understanding of top-line dynamics, providing visibility and generating foresight for senior management. Customer life-time value is enhanced by combining technology, marketing and real-world observation.

Upside-Outcomes (www.upside-outcomes.com) concentrates on software and IT Services, specialising in Salesforce.com, Web-Services and On-Demand Analytics.

FODA Consulting provides management consulting to create top-line strategies based on insights from the market and companies interaction with it. These lead to great decisions supported by observation.

Prior to studying for his MBA Gareth held various senior sales, marketing, operations and technology development positions in Schlumberger, while the company was charged with providing web solutions, portal implementations and outsourced data mangement services for oil exploration companies.

His work in the UK, Norway, Azerbaijan and Indonesia enabled him to experience negotiation in multi-cultural settings, the science of major account management and the art of deal making.
