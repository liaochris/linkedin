Gina Rubel’s Summary

Gina is the owner of Furia Rubel, a full-service women-owned certified (WBE), public relations agency advising law firms, service providers, educational institutions, and nonprofits internationally. A communications expert, attorney and publicist, Gina knows how to get media coverage that gets results.

A Philadelphia native, who practiced law for several years, Gina focuses on her passion for proactive communications. Her clients have been seen and heard in such places as Good Morning America, The Today Show, 20/20, USA Today, Family Circle, Wall Street Journal, New York Times, LA Times, Philadelphia Inquirer, Philadelphia Business Journal and many others.

Gina and her team of client advocates develop and implement communications plans with one thing in mind: reaching their clients’ corporate communications objectives. That may mean creating awareness, generating consumer or trade media coverage, managing media relations for high profile cases, providing media training, or implementing internal team building programs.

A graduate of Drexel University and Widener University School of Law, Gina is a frequent lecturer and is regularly published.

She serves as president of the Bucks County Women's Business Forum and as The Justinian Society Vice Chancellor. Gina is also a member of the Legal Marketing Association, Philadelphia and American Bar Associations, Philadelphia Public Relations Association (Past Publicity Chair), and Public Relations Society of America. She has held many leadership positions with the Young Lawyers Division of the Philadelphia Bar Association and Order Sons of Italy in America. She was elected to the Drexel University Alumni Board of Directors in 2006 and currently serves as the Communications Committee Chair.

Gina resides in Doylestown (Bucks County), Pennsylvania, with her husband and two children. In her spare time, she loves photography, travel, gardening, cooking, bird watching and nature.
