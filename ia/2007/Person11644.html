Tony Nguyen, PMP’s Summary

Project Management Professional (PMP) with over ten years of project management experience in diverse functional and business environments. Working in a management position to establish a quality assurance program for the new Enterprise Training Services organization of the U.S. Department of Energy. President of BlogPatrol.com, Inc., a web consulting services firm that operates BlogPatrol, a well-known web stats service for blogs.
