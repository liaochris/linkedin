Jim Rosen’s Summary

I am founder and Managing Director of Summit Street Group, which provides both intellectual and financial capital to technology firms. I provide consulting and advisory services to a variety of firms including, early stage tech firms, large tech firms, and venture firms. Much of my focus has remained in the areas where I have deep experience which is helping tech companies working in the security, collaboration, and internet space with marketing strategy and business development.

I bring more then 20 years of technology industry experience with a focus on marketing, business development and corporate development. I have been part of the founding management teams of two highly successful software startups: Netegrity and LANSystems, both of which achieved leading market share positions in their respective markets. In addition, I have also held leadership roles in some of the largest technology companies in the industry including Lotus Development and Genuity.

I am a member of CommonAngels and sit on the board of TimeTrade and Shambhala International as well as the advisory board of Tizor. I hold a Bachelors and Masters degree from MIT.
