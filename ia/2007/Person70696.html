Ramesh Nagappan’s Summary

RAMESH NAGAPPAN is a Technologist and Architect with extensive experience in Enterprise IT architecture, Information Security and Identity Management. Well-versed with IT architecture, design strategies, best practices and championing large and complex projects.

Avid practitioner of Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) for business applications, Information Security for Web-centric and pervasive applications, Identity Management, Access Control and Provisioning solutions for mission critical and security sensitive applications.

Technologist with broad experience in Java/J2EE/.NET based distributed computing, Enterprise Business Portals, XML Web Services, Application Integration and Interoperability solutions, Inter-enterprise/Business-to-Business Messaging Hubs, Network Topology Design, Firewall configuration, Cryptography, Application Security, Web Services Security, Identity and Access Management, Identity Provisioning, Identity Federation, Physical and logical access control using PKI, Smartcards and Biometrics.

Co-author of five technology books on topics related to J2EE, EAI, XML Web services and Security, who also contributed to open-source initiatives, XML industry standards and several articles to online journals and printed magazines.

Frequent Speaker at industry conferences and expert panels related to Java, XML Web Services and Information Security.
