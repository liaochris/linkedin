Adam Ducker’s Summary

I am a 25 year old graduate of Computer Science with a minor in Digital Graphics.

I believe that as the web continues to change with such great haste, the need for better standards and best practices on the Web will only grow larger.

I believe that some companies place more emphasis on programming code standards and not enough emphasis on standards of HTML and CSS themselves. Web standards are not cure alls, but deadlines and job pressure are not good excuses for failing to make the right choices from the beginning of a project about semantic HTML and browser safe CSS.

I also believe that if each of us working in this industry embraces such standards and sets that example in their workplace that more and more companies will fall in line as far as requiring this kind of work to be done.

I come from a tiny town in Northwest Arkansas, USA. I went to college in Little Rock, the capitol of Arkansas. My study in computer science and digital graphics gave me a strong foundation to do the work I enjoy doing.

After working for an eBusiness called Plugged-N.biz, I spent a year working for GoFish Corp. in San Francisco, CA. After getting married and moving to Newark Delaware I continued to work for GoFish from my home office (a couch).

Currently I am searching for new work, hopefully a remote/telecommuter job with a startup in the Bay Area again.

Adam Ducker’s Specialties:

Mastery of CSS and standards conscious HTML, construction of dynamic web pages using both client and server side programming.
