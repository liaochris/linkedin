Bernardo Rodriguez’s Summary

Bernardo Rodriguez has a PhD en Computer Engineering. He worked in Accenture (Andersen Consulting) for 5 years, where he was a member of the Internet and New Media Group in Palo Alto, and later was the founder and director of that group in Latin America, based in Buenos Aires. He founded ProcuraDigital in 2000, a company that was acquired by Grupo Santander in 2002. As the COO of ProcuraDigital he managed the growth to $25mm in revenues, 150 employees and operations in Latin America and Europe. Bernardo is currently the CEO of Mixsic.
