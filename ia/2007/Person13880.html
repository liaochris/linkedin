Burton Lee PhD MBA’s Summary

Dr. Burton Lee possesses 15 years senior management and technical experience in major global technology corporations, venture-backed startups, government executive service, federal science and technology agencies, leading research universities and non-profits.

His management expertise includes a diverse track record in technology startup and new venture development and operations; corporate strategy & strategic alliances; federal and commercial business development; technology and market strategy/assessment/roadmapping advisory services to the global high tech industry, investment banks, private equity and venture capital firms; and the federal science and technology policy community.

Corporate strategy and development experience includes the following areas, with a principal focus on high tech and energy industries:

• Deep strategy, change management, process improvement, supply chain management and off-shore outsourcing
• M&A strategy and post-merger integration
• New market entry, growth strategy and competitive strategy
• Strategic marketing management

Senior federal and state policy experience includes:

• Science, technology & innovation policy strategy and roadmapping for national and state governments; venture capital & private equity firms; and major corporations.
• State economic development policy & program strategy and management.

Exceptionally strong international background with over 10 years of work and education experience in Germany, Japan, UK, Switzerland, Spain, Austria, Czech Republic, Jamaica, Barbados and Guyana. Foreign languages include German, Spanish and French.

• Successful track record of complex program / project management.
