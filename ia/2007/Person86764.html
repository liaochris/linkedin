Tom Hernandez’s Summary

Tom Hernandez was appointed Vice President, Marketing & Business Development in May, 2004. Reporting to CEO Tim Vanderhook, Mr. Hernandez is responsible for leading all business development efforts for the company. In March, 2006 he has taken on a specialized role within the organization. As Vice President, Strategic Partnerships, Hernandez will focus on developing and managing key partnerships for the growing organization.

Prior to joining SpecificMEDIA, Tom was an Associate Publisher of 101communications where he managed national sales for the education technology group. While at 101communications, he successfully launched an integrated media lead-generation program, a series of custom micro-site marketing programs, and innovative e-media advertising initiatives.

Mr. Hernandez has over twenty years of online and print advertising sales experience with Flycast Comm/Engage Media, Autobytel, Inc and Ziff-Davis.
