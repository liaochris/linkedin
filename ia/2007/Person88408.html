Vadiraj Aralappanavar’s Summary

I currently leading the business development efforts for Aztecsoft in the Mobile & Wireless/Networking space, which I joined at a very early stage. Successfully built the business and the practice in the space of Mobile Marketing, Mobile Advertisement, Mobile Payment, Mobile VAS platforms and WiFi/WiMax systems with customers in US, Europe and India. Also responsible for the different technology partnerships, Technology and Service offerings.

Prior to this, I was the Founder, COO of TELiBrahma, a wireless application company in the wireless and telecom space, operating out of Bangalore during the period from beginning of 2004 to the beginning of 2006.

Before this, with nearly 9 Years of experience in technology products and services, solutions, management and business/models and markets across USA, Europe, AsiaPac and South Asia, I am exposed to managerial, technical, leadership and customer relationship functions. My experience spans across start-ups and MNC’s like HP, Nortel, Fujitsu, Wipro, Empowertel Networks, LongBoard and Cosystems. Apart from that, I was also involved in creating and evaluating business plans for companies.

The Masters in Management program at the Asian Institute of Management, Manila has further strengthened my skills to develop corporate strategies and business plans and to assume senior management and leadership role.
