Cherie Ann Turpin’s Summary

Assistant professor with substantial teaching experience at HBCUs and Division One universities. Specializes in training undergraduates for further research and teaching positions, as well as publishing critical theory on Western Literature. Substantial experience at researching and teaching World Literature.

Broad experience in administrative functions in higher education, with an emphasis on the development and management of academic policy, fund-raising and hiring new faculty. Direct experience in organizing panel discussions and developing policy on student retention and encouraging minority undergraduate and graduate recruitment and retention.

Cherie Ann Turpin’s Specialties:

Women's Studies, Autobiographical Narratives, African American Literature, Contemporary American Literature, Electronic Classrooms, Rhetoric and Composition, Film and Philosophy Studies.
