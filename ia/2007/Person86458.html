Todd Garrett’s Summary

Todd has worked in the financial services industry as a licensed securities and insurance professional for more than 16 years. Furthermore, he has worked as a Vice President for several investment banks that include: Kidder Peabody & Co., Roth Capital Partners, and US Bancorp. Additionally, he served on the board of directors at Glas-Aire Industries from 1999 through 2001, a publicly traded company. Todd holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Economics from San Diego State University. Todd is a Personal Financial Planner (PFP) through the school of management at University of California, Irvine. Todd’ s support team is even more impressive with over 30 years of investment & mortgage planning experience. For more on Todd team’s philosophy, go to www.ocmortgageplanner.com and www.GR-CAM.com.
