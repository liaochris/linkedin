Subramaniyam Pooni’s Summary

I have 14+ years of experience in the software industry developing Application (Java/J2EE/JNI/C++)/ System Software (Interface/Filter/Disk drivers over Linux/HP-UX) of which the last 10+ years were primarily focussed on the Design and Development of Storage Software. I have proven technical leadership skills involving managing, developing and motivating teams on complex software projects at the Storage Software R&D Labs (SWD) at Hewlett-Packard, Roseville. I have Lead geographically distributed teams both in India/Mexico. I hold several patents in the area of Design and Development of Mass Storage Interconnect software in particular SCSI and Fibre-Channel driver development. I have represented HP at the SNIA/SMI-S forums.

I have worked extensively on Device Provisioning and various Storage Resource Management Solutions like HP Open View Storage Area Manager which involved development of complex distributed applications over Java/J2EE and Design of Device Abstraction Layers to talk to devices, Out-of-band using JNI/C++ and In-Band using Host Based Filter Drivers at the SRM Labs at HP. At the Information Life Cycle Management (ILM) Labs at HP, I have worked extensively on Enterprise Information Management Solutions like D2D archival/retention based on HP's Integrated Archiving platform, the Reference Information Storage Solution (RISS), which involved development of Multi-tiered Application Systems based on Grid computing, Information Content Indexing, Document Taxonomy and Characterization, Compliance Archiving, Retention Management, eDiscovery, High Speed Data Transfer Protocol Design involving various Compression/Chunking methodologies etc. The Design of the server side content repository called CSFR was developed at HP Labs. The distributed applications were written over JBOSS/java/JNI/C++/Lucene.

I currently work at the Procurve Networking R&D Labs at HP in the area of Network Security. The work involves writing low-level kernel security modules in Linux

Subramaniyam Pooni’s Specialties:

Design of Network Security Blade/Appliance with integrated VPN/Firewall/IPS/IDS functionality, Routing/VLANs/clustering/HA, High Speed Data Transfer Protocol Design, Switch Zoning/VSANs, Host Based LUN masking, Device Provisioning, Design of I/O subsystems(SCSI, Fibre-Channel) and Interface/Device Drivers for various Fibre-Channel adapters(Tachyon/Tachlite/QLA-2200), Firmware Diagnostics for Fibre controllers, Development of Target Mode Drivers/ Disk & Raid Emulators/Fault Injection Systems.
