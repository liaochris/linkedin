Tracy Boyer’s Summary

I graduated from UNC Chapel Hill in May 2007, with a multimedia degree from the School of Journalism and Mass Communication. My passions lie in travel and multimedia production with a focus on audio, video and web-based graphics.

In the past, I interned with the Atlanta Journal-Constitution as well as worked for CNN.com as the UNC correspondent doing a variety of features and breaking news multimedia packages. During the summer of 2007, I participated in the Poynter Summer Fellowship.

Recently, I have been actively editing design, programming and copy for the 2007 Special Olympics World Games multimedia website. A variety of my multimedia work is also showcased on the website, www.specialolympicslive.org.

Currently, I have accepted a position at The Roanoke Times as a multimedia producer. I am excited about the possibilities that this job will provide and I look forward to garnering invaluable knowledge from the talented staff.

Thanks for visiting. I hope you take several minutes to look through my portfolio. Please feel free to contact me with any questions and/or comments that you may have. Have a great day!
