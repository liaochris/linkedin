    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

  • President and Founder

    Versus

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 1990 — October 2004 (14 years 4 months)

    Armed with a solid experience in software development and in operations management he acquired from high technology companies, he founded the Montreal based company Versus for which he was also the chief technology officer later on. His goals of architecting technological solutions that were in line with the business strategy of its clients allowed Versus to grow its clientele to include such high profile clients as Simons, CAA-Québec, Transat A.T., Pfizer, Air Canada, Cossette Communications and IATA. Versus, which specialized in IT outsourcing, which included managed hosting and managed applications services, ranked as the fastest growing and profitable Canadian IT companies. Versus earned three important awards, namely the PROFIT 100 Canada’s Fastest-Growing Companies 2004, the Champions de la croissance 2004 from l’ACTUALITÉ and the OCTAS Réussite Commerciale 2003 from the Fédération de l’informatique du Québec. Fusepoint acquired Versus in October 2004.

This client-server infrastructure was being used by more than 140 traders and allowed them to execute, each day, millions of dollars of transactions for the bank and its clients.
