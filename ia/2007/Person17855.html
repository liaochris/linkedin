Clement Galluccio’s Summary

With a knack for recognizing future power brands as opposed to mere collections of random letters, for the past two decades Clement has excelled in leadership roles for the world's leading naming and branding companies. Respected for his ability to optimize the creation and growth of consumer, healthcare and technology brands, this founder of Nomen USA and co-founder of Nomen Healthcare counts Nissan's Altima, IBM's nWays and Pfizer's Viagra as memorable moments in a career that began in 1990 with David Wood Associates.
