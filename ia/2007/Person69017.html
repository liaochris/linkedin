Cris Popenoe’s Summary

Cris is a creative executive and entrepreneur with a proven track record as a strategist, multi-platform media developer and integrator, content programmer and partner and deal maker. Her experience spans the distance from idea to consumer, and includes strategy, development, production, publishing, marketing and sales.

Both a strategist and a practical, down-to-earth, get-it-done person, her work includes strategies and implementation on entry, exit, capitalization, R.O.I., growth, positioning, pricing, distribution, risk-assessment, new market and international expansion, due diligence, and intellectual property as well as customer acquisition and retention. She excels at being part of the creation of a new initiative and growing it.

Cris has long-term relationships with well-positioned global industry leaders and talent in all facets of the entertainment business in LA and New York as well as Europe and Asia. She has the talent, contacts and the credibility to develop and execute large-scale media ventures and the strategy and tactics for the deployment and development of content and businesses across multiple devices in a manner best suited to each device.

Cris has 30 years experience in managing media businesses. An entrepreneur as well as an executive, Cris has been on the leading edge of self development and wellness from the beginning. She’s also been deeply involved in kids’ media and in business in China and Japan.

Her particular area focus is the development of interactive media with transactional and emotional hooks across multiple platforms, including mobile wireless devices, VOD, iTV, DVD and broadband.

Clients include: Comcast, Lifetime, Best Buy, Penguin Group, Flycell, Classic Media, Diversion Media, Digital Chocolate, Time Warner, Bertelsmann, Charity Folks, VisionTV, Aspen Club & Spa, Sapphire Group.

Cris Popenoe’s Specialties:

Emerging and digital media: DVD, Wireless, iTV, HDTV, VOD, DVR, MP3, Broadband, Internet, Digital Cinema, Mobile Content, eHome; home entertainment; media acquisition and production, deal making and representation, partnering and connecting,
