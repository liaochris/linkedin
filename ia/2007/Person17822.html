Clayton Schluter’s Summary

I focus in the Manufacturing, Environmental and Aviation/Aerospace arena in the Mid-Atlantic and Southeast. Disciplines being all but not included to: Operations, engineering, logistics, and supply chain.

I have the most diversified recruiting background. The uniqueness comes from working closely with my clients no matter what their industry and partnering to bring them a candidate in any division of the company they are in need. My success has been built on delivering a thoroughly unrivalled, professional, and personalized service to clients and candidates. I will initiate relationships with candidates and clients alike in order to expedite the entire Recruiting and Placement Process in a timely and CONFIDENTIAL manner.

My reputation and accomplishments have been built on this basic core philosophy: Nothing in the recruiting industry will substitute for honesty, integrity, ethics and hard work. Good matches do not happen over the Internet or on resumes. They happen when talented executive headhunters do their job well. Sourcing Candidates who are motivated by the right reasons to seriously consider a career growth opportunity, and whose expectations are as realistic as their accomplishments.
