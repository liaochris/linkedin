Joseph Tomaino’s Summary

Healthcare executive with senior management experience in hospitals and continuing care health systems, and governance responsibility for a major academic tertiary medical center and a community based home care agency. Educator experience as graduate level faculty in both clinical and management course, and held a full time assistant dean appointment at an Ivy League university. As a senior consultant, has assisted organizations with operation reviews and performance improvement initiatives, participated in compliance and due diligence reviews, and has led HIPAA privacy and security projects.

My goal is to expand my web based information and advice business to help others with healthcare management and governance responsibility, and to secure a part time position as a member of the board of directors of a private or public company.
