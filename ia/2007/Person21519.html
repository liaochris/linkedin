David Haddad’s Summary

David Haddad is editor of Online Payment Watch, a blog about Google's payment product.

He is a 22 year old internet enthusiast with a bachelor in software engineering from the University of Ottawa in Canada.

Being in the coop program David had the chance to take on various jobs.

In the summers following his first and second years David worked as a web application developer for a large IT company in the Emirates.

David spent his third year at school as a visiting student at the Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden where he had the chance to do some interesting course work with Stanford University and the Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship.

Consequent to his year in Europe David moved to New York City and did some product strategy and marketing work with a startup company that specializes in online payments.

David graduated this April magna cum laude and hopes to find a job doing product development / strategy work with an internet company.
