Josie Fraser’s Summary

Right now, I am most interested in where web 2.0 is going, policy, online communities and identities, training and development, personalisation and e-safety/e-literacy issues.

I'm happy to talk about speaking, problem solving, project design, research and writing.

You can check my recent itinerary out at http://fraser.typepad.com/blogfolio/

I currently manage the international Edublog Awards, and chair Future Learning Online, the UK association for educators using and interested in the potential of social software and user generated content.
