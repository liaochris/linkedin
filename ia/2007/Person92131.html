Clay Loges’s Summary

I'm skilled at adaptive applications of new technologies and service concepts.

Experienced entrepreneur:. Ten years of business development and operational management in Russia building a business of 800+ employees and nearly $20 million in annual revenues. Ten years of founding, developing and managing 10-minute oil change business (Speedi-Lube in WA and CarSavers in CA), 5 years of management in advertising agencies, and 5 years in the development and marketing of web-hosted/wireless applications.

In local geography, I've worked with small to medium-sized businesses in optimizing their use of wireless technologies that are cellular based. GPS tracking, mobile credit processing, front-line data capture, web-hosted data management, etc.

For the past three years I've also been developing a business concept for a community-generated audio content for distribution via the internet. The company is Yodio, and the beta version of Yodio was posted September 2006 Please visit . www.Yodio.com
