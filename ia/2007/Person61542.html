Michael Scherotter’s Summary

I work with customers, partners, and potential partners to understand how technically Mindjet MindManager could integrate with other systems.

I build prototypes, demonstrations, and proof-of-concepts to to develop compelling use-cases of MindManager integrated with other systems.

I manage the Mindjet Labs (http//labs.mindjet.com) and I am a MindManager Platform Evangelist.

Educated as an architect, Michael Scherotter brings a sense of design and creativity to his role as a Business Solutions Architect at Mindjet. Before joining Mindjet, Michael designed and developed custom CAD solutions for building product manufacturers. In his four years at Mindjet, Michael has lead the development of the first Tablet-PC version of MindManager and MindManager X5, and built the first enterprise integration for MindManager with a leading on-demand CRM provider. In his current role, he directs strategic software development in order to build partnerships. He is also responsible for managing the Mindjet Solutions Platform, demonstrating integrations with the Mindjet Labs (mindjetlabs.com), and building a Mindjet solutions ecosystem.
Michael lives in Marin County, California with his wife and two boys (1 and 4). When he can, he enjoys sketching, watercoloring, photography, videography and telling the world about it with his personal website, charette.com.
