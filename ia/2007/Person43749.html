Jon Sigler *jon@jonsigler.com*’s Summary

Jon has over 6 years of experience in the mortgage industry as a loan professional and sales manager.
At First Eastern Mortgage, Jon learned the business by working with many first-time buyers and working on challenging renovation loans. He helped countless borrowers achieve the dream of home ownership.
At GMAC Mortgage, Jon continued to grow his knowledge of the business, and earned "Top Producer" of his branch in his first year.
At Travis Mortgage, Jon began to share his knowledge with others in his role as manager of the Connecticut sales force. He successfully implemented leading-edge technology for loan origination and customer retention programs.
Jon works with all the partners during the transaction to ensure his customers a smooth process from application to closing. He creates a positive experience for the borrowers through proper education and communication during their home purchase or refinance. Through this approach, Jon has built a loyal following of happy, satisfied customers.
