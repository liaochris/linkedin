Volker Hirsch’s Summary

Volker is leading Hands-On Mobile’s efforts in the areas of corporate, business and legal affairs in EMEA, APAC and Latin America. This includes managing their licensor relationships, opening new areas of activity and striking strategic partnerships in all aspects of the business. Volker has lived in many countries allowing him a truly international look on the issues he deals with.

Volker’s background is in law where he has many years experience in crafting and executing on cross-border M&A transactions and strategic partnerships as well as in licensing and business development activities predominantly in the mobile telecoms, entertainment and media industries. He closed some early landmark deals in the mobile music space with a number of multi-platinum selling artists and has recently been responsible of closing a number of Hands-On Mobile’s games and lifestyle licenses. All his work is underpinned by solid foundation in corporate & commercial law, both with a thoroughly international perspective.

Volker is also a regular speaker on mobile telecoms & mobile entertainment and the (co-)author of various books and articles on mobile entertainment, investment, corporate and merger control affairs.

Volker is admitted to practice law in Germany. His admission as an English solicitor is currently pending.
