David Berney’s Summary

Since 2001, David develops strategies and technology enabled programs for new clients and leads customer relationships for companies such as a 5 Billion dollar Entertainment brand, Multi-Billion dollar Dubai - UAE based marketplace, US Navy/ Computer Science Corporation, CIBA Vision, Liberty Travel, Norwegian Cruise Lines, Cendant Corporation, Zyman Group, Coca Cola, BlueCross BlueShield, NetBank, and the Taj Hotels Resorts and Palaces, to name a few. David is passionate about designing solutions which are derived from strategic insight that fundamentally change the way users behave to achieve business objectives. His experience ranges from delivering global portal solutions to initiating customer research and a phased implementation plan to improving customer experiences across all channels.

Before joining Avenue A I Razorfish, David led Delta Air Lines first incubated company, MYOBTravel, and was responsible for the eBusiness initiatives related to the Air Lines interactions with small businesses. David was also a member of the senior management team working to deliver Delta’s consumer eBusiness strategy.

Prior to Delta Air Lines, David was the Chief Marketing Officer for eHatchery, a seed-stage investment company with venture funding by Credit Suisse, UPS, IdeaLab, and Cox Enterprises. David led the marketing and brand development for eHatchery and six start-up companies under the advisement of Sergio Zyman, former Chief Marketing Officer of the Coca Cola Company.

David spent ten years working in the advertising and media business for divisions of Time Warner and American Express. He attended Phillips Academy in Andover, Mass., studied International Business and Marketing at Franklin College in Lugano, Switzerland, and received his BA in Economics from Emory University.

David Berney’s Specialties:

David brings over 15 years of global marketing knowledge in related disciplines including strategic marketing, knowledge management portals, internet/intranet strategy, ecommerce, and brand creations/awareness.
