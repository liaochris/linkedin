Jason Price’s Summary

I have been a mortgage broker licensed in the state of Florida since 2001. My experience has led me to manage two correspondent lenders in the South Florida and Central Florida market.

In 2006, I opened my own company despite the cooling off of the real estate industry. Since its formation, I have had much success in building the business without much advertising and expensive marketing. I have also expanded the company to include commercial financing.

The goals of the company is to continue its growth and build out into surrounding cities and counties. We are also going to start focusing a portion of the company to fully dedicate its time to commercial lending. By the end of 2007, we have plans to be one of the top mortgage companies in Central Florida for both residential and commercial mortgages.
