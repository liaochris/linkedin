Nick Velander (TopLinked.com)’s Summary

In today’s global marketplace, most product development companies have an ongoing challenge to design their products within tight schedules and tighter budgets. More and more companies are outsourcing their engineering and design work to realize significant reductions in the product development cycle time and cost. If your company is ready to explore the advantages of outsourcing, we recommend you consider QTI Design.

It’s important to note that QTI Design is not an engineering placement service. We are a company that dedicates resources to a specific client. Our designers can work a one-week design project or a long- term development cycle. Because we have the support of our manufacturing division, we do not have to constantly push for long term contractual obligations. We are experienced in design for manufacturability.
