Jim Comeaux’s Summary

In October of 2006 RedWire was sold to VC funded Airband Communications.

In 1999, I founded VoloNet Technologies, Inc.,/dba RedWire "Broadband Solutions for Business" a regional inter-networking services provider. I came up with an idea and design for the a multi-tenant deployment model(suburban class b office) based on landline and uni-band wireless, deploying to over 500 commercial office buildings in Southern California with over 100 Wireless site and 70 backhaul links.

Deployed the 1st WISP in SoCal in early 2000.
Launched a managed firewall service in 2003
Purchased and engineered our own "Hosted" VoIP switch(Tekelec) in 2004 & rolled out service in January 2005.

Proven business leader with ability to grow businesses from idea stage to fully operational status.
