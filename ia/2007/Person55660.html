Martin Newman’s Summary

(I'm always keen to discuss potential third party promotions between Ted and relevant brands and opportunities...)

A unique blend of sales, marketing and business development experience in both senior level client side and service sector roles.
A multi channel player with real width and depth of experience of retail, e-commerce and direct mail channels. With a track record of delivering results.

End to end e-commerce experience; Platform/technology, range planning and merchandising, customer experience, content management, design/creative, marketing and analytics, payment processing, customer service, supply chain and fulfilment.

A true early adopter selling online since 1996, in the process implementing a host of platforms and solutions from entry level models such as Actinic to hosted applications from Fresca and Venda, bespoke solutions from leading agencies and top end platforms including IBM Websphere.
Deployment of leading edge third party e-commerce software solutions that enhance the customer experience and increase conversion rates and sales. One of the very first retailers to implement PayPal as a payment solution, live chat as an online sales driver, Mercado for guided navigation, search and merchandising, and specify-a-date delivery.

Solid marketing skills with both brand building and performance based marketing experience. A strong focus on recruitment, retention and reactivation and driving behaviour through CRM.

Sound strategic insight and tactical awareness.

Sales, margin and KPI driven. 100% results and ROI orientated.

Martin Newman’s Specialties:

E-commerce strategy, roadmap and implementation, multi channel integration, project management, usability and customer experience, site measurement and analytics, CRM, through-the-line marcoms and business development in a number of sectors including retail (Luxury, fashion, sports), travel, media, and professional services.
