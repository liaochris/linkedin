Kiran Jonnalagadda’s Summary

I work in the e-governance sector, helping governments extend a digital interface to citizens. I used to be in the Internet space back when "Internet" was a category by itself, building rich web applications and publishing platforms for the media, bioinformatics and NGO sectors.

I'm happiest when organising large masses of data into manageable databases with usable interfaces.

I started my career writing for a print publication that went on to become very influential in its vertical. In the process I learnt much about media, advertising, design, reputation, influence, power law, and the sheer drive required to run up that curve, learnings which continue to influence my work today.
