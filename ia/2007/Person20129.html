Daniel Ho’s Summary

With over 25 years of experience in both business management and information technology, I oversee the business operations of Matrix Meridian Corporation--a business consultancy specializing in information technology issues.

The firm specializes in managing projects for that have complex business processes and require innovative or highly technical solutions. Recent projects include complex web applications, enterprise-wide eLearning / online employee orientation solutions, and New Media initiatives.
