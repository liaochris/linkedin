Thomas P. Laur’s Summary

Strategy Consulting Executive with international experience. Core expertise centered around the design and implementation of corporate/business unit level growth strategies affecting customer facing functions (Marketing, Sales, Service).

Has advised many Fortune 500 organizations in Europe, Asia and the USA on matters of: marketing and sales effectiveness, corporate strategy, merger integration, organizational and process design, market entry strategies, competitive strategies.
