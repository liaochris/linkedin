Sarita Bahl’s Summary

It is difficult to summarize my career experiences, ranging right from the NGO sector to the Government, then the private industry, MNC and now an Indian Giant.

The varied roles and exposures have made me take risks and learn new things during each venture. In a strange way, each education, each qualification has added value to a new job though I have been in jobs for which I have had no formal professional qualification. Yet, the challenges have been accepted and tided over.

So in brief - corporate communications, internal and external communications, managing media and building, sustaining key relationships, and conceputalizing and executing events/programs has been my forte.
With in that, strategizing, setting the pre requisite policies to complement the business objectives through communications and corporate social responsible (CSR) projects has been my success.

Exposure with the NGO sector paved the way for writing, research and drafting advocacy papers and working in the publications arena.

My professional training as a counselor has always helped me reach out to people and build up long lasting relationships, which I have cherished over the years.
