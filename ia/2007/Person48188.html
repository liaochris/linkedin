Erik R. Bergling’s Summary

As a Managing Partner, Erik Bergling’s primary focus is on program management and protection services. Mr. Bergling offers a detailed understanding and commitment to the specific needs of our clients. His enthusiasm and willingness to ensure that every aspect of the risk management program is met makes him a valuable professional in the safety and security industry.

Mr. Bergling began his career in the United States Marine Corps. His assignment to the Security Forces in the Marines allowed him to determine a professional path dedicated to the preservation of life.

In the late 90’s, Mr. Bergling served as the Chief Contract Manager at a local private security company, managing the security efforts of four state buildings. Working together with the Minnesota State Police, Mr. Bergling assisted in advance protection for the Governor of the State Of Minnesota and liaised with the United States Secret Service on three Presidential visits.

Mr. Bergling’s professional reputation for providing superior executive protection is unmatched in the Twin Cities area. He has had the unique and honorable opportunity to serve such distinguished clients as U.S. Senators Paul Wellstone, Rudy Boschwitz, Rod Grams, and visiting dignitaries from the government and state of Israel.

Mr. Bergling is an active member of the community in crime prevention strategies and promoting safety and security awareness.
