Tom Horsey’s Summary

Froggie was founded in January 2001 to run marketing campaigns for value added telephony providers, our first client was IBC International and we ran the launch of their mobile content brand mi-logo, the first mobile content brand to launch in the Spanish market, as well as the market leader for over a year from launch date. In September 2002 we branched out into running multiple brands, and our portfolio now includes a large number of well known Spanish B2C mobile content brands (e.g. www.sinfindejuegos.com ) focusing on the print media market:

As a complement to our B2C marketing services we also run interactive television solutions, mobile content revenue share deals, and bulk sms provision for a large number of third party clients.

We have over five years of mobile marketing and mobile content sales experience, and four years experience in running both interactive tv solutions (in partnership with Cellcast Interactive) and bulk sms provision. In total we have been been involved in the marketing and promotion of value added telephony services for over ten years.

Our main markets are in Spain, Portugal, Mexico, South Africa and Argentina, and we have a physical presence in Spain and Argentina.
