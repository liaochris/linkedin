Daniel Schutzsmith’s Summary

Business Developer focused on instituting and evolving the corporate standards of the business world in today’s graphic design industry. Interested in motivating the evolution of Creative Principals into the world of business, especially through the proper use of project management, studio management, and client relations, to push the boundaries of what is capable within the resources of a creative company’s limits.
