    Final Year Project
    --------------------------
    I completed my final year project which titled “ Magnetic Sensor for Heading Determination ” for the fulfillment of the requirement for the degree of Bachelor of Engineering ( Aerospace ), University Putra Malaysia ( UPM ).
    This project suggested using the Honeywell HMC1022 magnetic sensor to measure the Earth’s magnetic field for determining the heading direction. The magnetic sensor senses the horizontal component of the earth’s magnetic field and then generates the output voltage for heading. It still can provide good accuracy and safety for the pilots by using this magnetic sensor to provide heading outputs for the small aircrafts.
    In this project, I need to study the characteristics of the earth's magnetic field. From the behavior of the magnetic field, design a simple circuit to determine the heading output. The simple circuit able to amplified the magnetic field's voltage signal and filter the undesired high frequencies.
