Catherine Tabor’s Summary

Catherine Tabor has practiced law in Austin,Travis County since 1986. Her experience and practice have focused on transactional, corporate, copyright/intellectual property, litigation (state court and state/federal administrative agencies), strategic communications and marketing. She has represented artists, filmmakers, screen writers, producers, authors, wineries, a broad clientele of businesses and every aspect of the health care industry. Tabor has also played a lawyer on TV, featured on the FOX TX show Power of Attorney. Tabor works with energetic and creative clients who embrace lateral thinking, prosperity, integrity and enjoy living.

Catherine Tabor’s Specialties:

Catherine Tabor offers complete business, legal and creative, client-focused solutions for entertainment, business and healthcare clients.
