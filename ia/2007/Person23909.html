Diane Velasco’s Summary

I have over 8 years experience as a graphic design professional. Most of those years I worked in the advertising industry, responsible for concepts, 3-D comps, full production and output of jobs which included but not limited to packaging, POPs, tradeshow graphics, brochures and other collateral, corporate identity, direct mail, illustrations and ads. As a freelancer I was able to expand my experience both in work and skills, and now am co-owner of a small design firm called 503LAB, Inc. My clientele has expanded from the pharmaceutical, corporate and retail markets into the fashion, music and toy industries. My team and I consult, oversee production on numerous projects, direct photoshoots, marketing and brand identity. With an ever growing team of other industry professionals and vendors, there is no limit to the projects my clients envision. As a consultant, my team also help ensure that clients reach their target markets with their products, and that they stand out amongst the competition.
