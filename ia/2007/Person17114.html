Christopher Bird’s Summary

I now specialise in Tutorial & Business Communication CD, DVD, and Online Videos. I got into this work quite by accident prior to videos I designed, implemented and maintained computer networks for business owners and independent professionals. Working for my clients I found I was increasingly being asked to teach them how their technology works.

Then one of my clients asked me to help him and his team train clinical and clerical NHS staff to use medical transcription software. Soon afterwards my client commissioned a new software application to be developed in India. He asked me to become a team member beta testing his new transcription application. This evolved remote server log on, telephone conferencing with programmers, monitoring bug fixes and reporting progress to stakeholders. This I excelled at, given my client and my own experience of software and training end users. During beta testing my client asked me to look at alternative training methods as I had suggested one-on-one or group training given expansion was becoming impractical and to costly. My research revealed video training would be a way forward. I haven't looked back.

I feel great seeing people’s frustrations melt away having studied one of my videos. Some people finally realise technology is the servant not them. I have never thought of myself as a trainer despite the fact within a few weeks of starting work at The Dorchester Hotel, Park Lane at sixteen, I was given the job to train new employees and have done so throughout my working life. I hadn't realised until I begun making videos I had a gift to help people manager their stress to make new learning a rewarding experience.
