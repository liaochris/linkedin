Scott Briskman’s Summary

Award-winning creative director, with 10 years of experience delivering for national and international brands such as: E*TRADE, British Airways, L’Oréal, Brown|Co, MasterCard International, Sony, De Beers, Maybelline, IKEA, JP Morgan Invest, A&E Television Networks, Candies, and others.

Industry leader, who’s working to move advertising beyond the 30-second spot by:
• Creating valuable consumer experiences across as many touch points as possible
• Proving by example that sometimes digital ideas can and should take the lead
• Fostering collaboration with internal and external parties to successfully produce campaigns with the right mix of “messages” and “experiences”
• Teaching our new methods to students from around the world.

Scott Briskman’s Specialties:

Specialist in interactive advertising, media and website development. Strong conceptual, written and visual design abilities. Able to deliver product, brand and marketing strategy. An advanced understanding of digital technologies. Excellent presentation skills. A business-savvy, entrepreneurial mentality.
