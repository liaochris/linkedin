Teresa Colombi’s Summary

Cognitive Ergonomics is a scientific discipline that improves Human Computer Interfaces by creating them to be more accessible, comprehensive, intuitive and attractive to users. This make the interface more likely to succeed in the marketplace.
After several years as independent consultant, cognitive ergonomics teacher and PhD student, I have founded LudoTIC, a company specialised in consultancy about HCI.
We use advanced technologies like the Eye Tracking device to study users' behaviors and render optimized interfaces through our objective, scientific approach.

LudoTIC is the first ergonomics agency to specialize in Video Games Interfaces.

Teresa Colombi’s Specialties:

Ergonomist, psychologist, usability specialist, eye movements analysis specialist
