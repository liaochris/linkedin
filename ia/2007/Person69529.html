Tanya Taylor Rubinstein’s Summary

I have devoted the last 15 years of my life to the exploration of the soul in writing and performance.

I have facilitated and produced hundreds of people in writing and performing authentic stories from their life through Project Life Stories. Some of the "group monologue" shows that I have produced have been "The Cancer Monologues", "The Soul of Peace "(monologues with Israeli and Palestinian teens), Core Witness (Veterans for Peace sharing their stories), Mothering:The Monologues (with Mothering Magazine) and many others.

The Cancer Monologue Project is a book that I co-edited based on some of the monologue participants I have worked with who have experienced cancer. The book as well as my workshops have been featured in many national publications including"O-The Oprah Magazine", Spirituality and Health and Backstage. It has also been on the CBS Early Show.

Currently, I teach classes in Santa Fe, N.M. entitled "Creating a Solo Show" and "How to Write Your Non-Fiction book in 5 Months". I also work with individual clients in person and by phone/e-mail. My specialties are supporting people in structuring their creative ideas,creative unblocking and soul based writing to unlock one's inner intelligence.

I am also available to produce workshops/fundraisers for corperations/non profits to feature the stories of their staff and clients in an authentic, deeply moving way. I have been hired by organizations as diverse as Odyssey Hospice, Gilda's Club and Mothering Magazine to facilitate and present this unique blend of storytelling and transformational theater.
