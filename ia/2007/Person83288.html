Kevin Krueger’s Summary

By the next decade SupahFans.com will become one of the Top 5 networked website resources for those celebrating the New England lifestyle. Whether our web consumers live in New England, left the region, if they are alums, current or prospective students at our hundreds of colleges, we wish to showcase the best New England offers.

Our core business is currently distribution of apparel designed by entrepreneur Kevin Krueger & graphic artist & silk screener Joe Lopes. We will always include distribution as a key component of our business.

In start-up phase for 18 months, we've bootstrapped our business designing, marketing and selling apparel (t-shirts, hats, sweatshirts and stickers) that celebrate our region's culture and lifestyle. In fact, we declare that we're not selling t-shirts, we sell passion.

We've developed many hip creative themes that capitalize on the fervent fanbase of our professional sports teams. Based near Boston, we also feature a state-wide MassPride product line that features local townline signs that are specifically only in Massachusetts and displayed as one enters each of the 351 cities and towns. We've involved over 50 towns and intend to produce all 351 cities and towns.

We've recently added Amy Coleman, a graduate of Boston College's Carroll School of Management, and current AssistantCoach of BC's Women's Rugby Team. Please look for her here in LinkedIn. We expect 2007 to find us at yet another plateau, in part by working with Rich Kneece (Linked In) on web redevelopement and marketing. This March we'll be advertising and promoting at City of Palms Park in Ft. Myers for the Red Sox spring training season. Late March will find us at The Boston Gift Show establishing new accounts with some of the region's finest retailers.
