Markus Krebsz’s Summary

Strategic management consultant with expert knowledge in Securitisation & Rating agency spaces - worked for 14+ years for major European banks and Financial institutions.

Currently working on an assignment consulting BoS Treasury's Structured Investments Credit and Counterparty Credit Sanctioning teams.

Worked until recently for RBS's Group Treasury where I was instrumental in articulating and shaping the banks future securitisation strategy.

Previously, I worked for Fitch Ratings as a rating analyst in the corporate & infrastructure (C&I) securitisation team focusing on trade receivables, leases, and inventory, as well as transport and infrastructure and other government related deals. Prior to that I was part of Fitch's European ABS performance analytics team covering the surveillance for C&I transactions and have also been involved in product development such as the Fitch Issuer Report Grades and the post-issuance reporting standards. During the time there I helped defining and setting the reporting standards for the European Commercial ABS market.

Acting in a variety of Advisory and Consultancy capacities. In addition, I am also member of various research groups where I provide insight and opinion.

Currently working on a couple of books as part of a well-known publisher’s forthcoming ‘International Capital markets’ series.
