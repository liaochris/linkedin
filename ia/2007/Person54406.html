Maree Conway’s Summary

Maree works as the General Manager, Quality, Information and Planning at Victoria University in Melbourne where she is manages the University's strategic planning, quality assurance and improvement system, and data analysis and reporting processes. She is currently working on integrating futures approaches in the University's strategy framework. She has worked in tertiary education institutions in Australia for more than 20 years, primarily in policy, planning and strategy roles.

Since 1999, Maree has facilitated internal futures and scenario planning workshops at Swinburne University of Technology and Victoria University, and has co-facilitated scenario workshops with government departments and business organisations. Maree is a regular presenter on futures topics at conferences in Australia and internationally.

Maree also runs University Futures, (http://www.universityfutures.org), a futures focused website that aims to support and provide resources for strategists and university planners who are interesting in using futures approaches in their work.
