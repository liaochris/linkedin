Bronson Ma – www.bronsonma.com’s Summary

For more than a decade, Bronson Ma has been a leading creative force locally and nationally. His work can be seen in restaurants, hotels and trade publications. It is also important to note that he created the logo that represented Dallas for the Dallas Convention & Visitors Bureau. As the CCO (Chief Creative Officer) of Bronson Ma Creative in San Antonio, Texas, Ma serves both product and service organizations with an eye toward building market credibility and consumer visibility through branding.

Prior to establishing Bronson Ma Creative, Ma was an award-winning and highly regarded Senior Designer/Art Director at GroupBaronet in Dallas, Texas (now MasonBaronet) for nearly a decade. He worked on projects ranging from packaging and brochures to websites and television spots for companies such as Motorola, Greyhound and la Madeleine Cafés.

Ma has seen much of his work published in design publications. He has also been selected by his peers to create brand identities for their own trade organizations, including the D/FW American Marketing Association and the Dallas International Association of Business Communicators (DallasIABC).
