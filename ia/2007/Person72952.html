Rich LaBarca’s Summary

I'm a software engineer and project leader who thrives at bridging business goals with technical strategy and execution. Starting my career in research with computer assisted surgery, my passion for emerging, life-changing technologies grew. I especially fell in love with mobile technology and eventually decided to jump into the world of mobile software startups.

My work in mobile has focused on middleware, but I've written sophisticated client-side applications in J2ME and Windows Mobile as well. I've written an AI platform for mobile gaming, a high-performance ad matching platform for mobile ad serving, and many smaller projects in between. I am happiest when I'm helping a company form a technology strategy and develop core technology - especially for the mobile sector. But I still like to keep my hand in emerging technologies for medicine on the side.
