Marc Bauwens’s Summary

A strong practice in different positions related to the Information Systems, as well in project teams as in user teams, as well in user enterprises as like consultant, gave to me the following competencies:

- Alignment of the IT strategy with the business strategy, elaboration and follow-up of IT-plans;
- Development and follow-up of budgets;
- Business and management consulting;
- IT project: opportunity evaluation, business case, requirements, functional analysis, realization, tests, training and roll-out;
- Project and program management;
- Methodology roll-out and management;
- Contract negotiation and supplier management
- Teams management and direction.

During those years, I learned how to be a central player between the top management, the IT users and the IT teams. My experience was mainly gained in branches of distribution, industry, banks and inter-banking. Member of the IT management for 8 years, I want today to take the entire responsibility of IT management.
