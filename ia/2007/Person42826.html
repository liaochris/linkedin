John Drake’s Summary

If you're reading this it most likely means that I've worked with you in some capacity beyond just saying "hello" in the halls. So, on behalf of where I sit now, I say, "thank you." Because if I've worked with you, and you've taken a moment to seek me out on this thing, then we've probably learned a few things together. Had some late nights. Figured something out. Had a few cocktails. Maybe even presented together. This is important to stop and appreciate because we are all a collection of each other's work styles. Personally, this is a great thing because I've been lucky... My time at Dailey, Deutsch, FourStories, WONGDOODY, es/drake and all the companies, vendors and clients that came along with those places, have interwoven me with extremely talented people.
