Brady Reed’s Summary

The goal of BioteamPSC is to bring the most valuable, best-in-class, portfolio of products and services to the life-sciences researcher. Our strategy is to deliver such solutions through in-house and strategic outsource expertise. We seek to develop long-term relationships with both our clients and vendor partners to create mutually beneficial opportunities and solutions for all concerned. Our interest is also on the long-term success of both our clients and vendor partners so that their combined efforts improve the quality of human life.

The most important factor in the survival and success any organization is leadership. Bioteam adds value not in just bringing parties together, but by providing crucial leadership and management consulting to help organizations develop lasting cultures and stay in business.

Brady Reed’s Specialties:

BioteamPSC's scientific portfolio currently includes ISO 9001:2000-certified custom peptides; antibodies, and 3D cell culture bioreactors.

Our leadership and development courses include team-building, managerial leadership, professional sales development, and DevelopmentPath - a life-long roadmap to achieving your greatest potential.
