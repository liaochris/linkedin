EYP London’s Summary

European Young Professionals (EYP) London is the first European chapter of the organisation, which promotes links between young business professionals.

EYP was founded in Asia two years ago and has, due to an immense success, now expanded to Europe with its fist organisation launched in London.

EYP is talking to all business professionals who are young at heart, rather than defined by age.

The aim of EYP London is to bring together and provide an active support network to all young professionals living and working in London.

The vision is to be innovative, supportive, diverse and to offer a unique, valuable networking experience to all members of EYP London. EYP members are spread among 45 countries; thus, it provides a unique networking platform.

European young professionals aim to provide the following activities and services:
Membership activities and events for members, including networking evenings, business forums and lunches

Provide a monthly networking event sponsored by companies to provide good value for members

To open bridges for new business relations within London, and beyond

To provide direction and support to new European arrivals in London
