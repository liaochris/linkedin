Dan Lieberman’s Summary

Professional software-security consultant specializing in mitigating operational risk of information security. One of the pioneers in the nascent extrusion prevention space – applying high-speed content interception techniques to prevent data theft from inside the organization.Companies like Fidelis Security, Sun Microsystems, BMC Software, Resolute Networks, Flash Networks, MainSoft, Diamonds.com and 013 Barak all use my services and solutions.Read my blog at: http://www.software.co.il/blog on issues related to to software security. I am a well-regarded sought-after speaker at at information security events such as Infosec and the Israel CISO forum.
