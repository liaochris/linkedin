  • Director - Corporate Systems Enablement

    Bell Canada

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; BCE; Telecommunications industry)

    April 2006 — March 2007 (1 year)

    Leads the Corporate Systems Enablement group.

    A group with a pro-active mandate to define strategy and tactical plans to unlock synergy, maximize business value, through the creative application of technology and innovation. The CSE group has 3 Core objectives

    1. Enable Agile Innovation: Re-Think systems for forward integration & future demands for agile implementation of innovation.

    2. Enable Massive Collaboration: Re-Think how we use technology and who uses them. The collective genius will surpass the individual.

    The IT areas that CSE focusses on are: Business Intelligence (BI), Enterprise Data Warehousing, SAP, ERP, Finance, HR, Supplychain.
