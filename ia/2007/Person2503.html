Andy Kuykendall’s Summary

Over twenty years diverse international and domestic combined experience in information security, IT auditing, business process analysis, IT governance, project management, risk management, system administration, network analysis/architecture, and operations.

Experienced in performing IT audits, using methodologies based on risk management principles and the control objectives from the COBIT Control Framework. Audits have included vulnerability assessments, physical and logical security assessments, and reviews of Sarbanes-Oxley controls, Disaster Recovery, SDLC, change management, software configuration management, IT project management, IT process improvement, policy and procedure implementations, Windows 2000/2003 AD, UNIX/Linux systems, infrastructure, operations, contracts, application controls, and more.

Particularly interested in IT Security Audits or Risk Assessments, using various regulatory compliance requirements such as PCI, GLBA, HIPAA, and Sarbanes Oxley.
