James Dunne’s Summary

James is a "One Man Band," who has been researching, shooting, and producing network TV stories since 2001.

He specializes in “fly-on-the-wall” style documentaries with strong verite. Entrepreneurs and business are favourite subject matters, and James has found intense business stories in unexpected places - such as a professional hockey arena in Scotland. That story, Hockey in the Highlands, won a Gold Medal from the New York Festivals. Another story, The Brandman, won the prestigious Silver Chris Award for best business program at the Columbus Festival.

James has also worked as a video journalist for the Toronto edition of Canada Now, CBC's supper hour news show, and at CBC's consumer program Marketplace. Born and raised in Edmonton, Alberta, James studied Film at Queen's University.
