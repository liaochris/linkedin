Erin Paine’s Summary

Erin holds a B.S. degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Maine Orono and specializes in the executive placement of highly qualified engineers within the power generation industry. She has been with FPC Bangor since 2006 and has successfully placed several candidates within the areas of gas and steam turbine engineering, maintenance, project engineering and management, mechanical engineering, power engineering, boiler engineering, combustion engineering, field service, and energy procurement.

Erin is dedicated to building strong partnerships with her client companies. She qualifies all aspects of the job search and promises a presentation of the most competent candidates in the field. Her role in the search process includes: networking with and qualifying potential candidates, selling the opportunity and bringing value to the client company, resume coaching and interview preparation for candidates, mediating job offer negotiations, and supporting all career transition needs.

Many of Erin's client companies and candidates are accustomed to significant domestic and international travel and are experts in the overhaul and maintenance of gas and steam turbine or boiler equipment for power generation facilities. She is actively seeking engineers who have worked on GE, Siemens-Westinghouse, Alstom, and Toshiba turbine generators or those who have boiler inspection and equipment expertise.

Erin Paine’s Specialties:

Executive placement searches and services for Engineering, Field Service, and Management positions within the power industry. Keywords include: gas turbine, steam turbine, power plant, project engineer, project manager, overhaul and maintenance, OEM, field engineer.
