Tommy Jensen’s Summary

I started working with ServiceCenter in 2000 after being a helpdesk agent for 3 years and a helpdesk teamleader for 10 months.

My first task was to upgrade SC from 2.1 to 3 and migrate SC from Unix platform to MVS platform. Later when we upgraded from 3 to 5 we migrated back to Unix.

I have been involved in a couple projects building external interfaces to SC from other tickets systems such as Clarify using iWave and to a different SC using home build application based on SCAuto api. Also I have developed several import solutions with Connect.It.

In the past year I have mainly been working on a Change and Release Management solution for a customer. The change and release processes are fully integrated with each other to ensure the correct process is followed.
