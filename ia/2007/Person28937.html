Federico Cascinelli’s Summary

My name is Federico and I’m an animator... or better, that's what I’d suppose to be and that's what I’ve been paid for in the last 6 years...

Sometimes, someone, ask me why I’m an animator and how I decided to start this career... actually I don't know... I didn't grow up thinking to be an animator or with the passion for the vfx... a day, somewhere, I found myself seating in front a pc using a 3d software and few years later an animator (a real one I mean) told I was good in animation... so I started... that was around 1999 and I was 28 years old...

I travelled around looking for something that gave me the opportunity to fill the gap i felt inside because i didn't have a traditional arts background...
That was a great moment of my life... i started looking at the world in a different way...
I went in Los Angels, London, Denmark, and France always following my needs in animation and not only...

I worked in the industry as animator in Italy and Uk helping the other to create their own projects... in 2001 I decided to express myself, my ideas and my attitude for life... so I created my own short film...
I’m now a full (almost) satisfied animator that tries to reach a new objective day by day, week by week, project by project...
