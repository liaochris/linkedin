Jayson Minard’s Summary

I am a senior resource that brings together the technology and business worlds. I have a range of experience from small software companies and Internet startups to large worldwide software and internet companies. I have a reputation for going after the tough tasks, the hard goals, and accomplishing what the business needs to thrive.

I can act as an operational manager focusing on supporting the business with real-time strategies or as a product and technical visionary determining the path for the business to pursue.
