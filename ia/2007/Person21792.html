David Pap’s Summary

I have 20 years experience as a residential and development real estate specialist in the Cambridge, Somerville and Boston area. For 15 years before that, I had a design/build/woodworking business. That gives me 35 years experience around the area housing market that I can offer to help you buy or sell a home. I provide a high level of service and experience to my buyer and seller clients alike. As well as enjoying the sale of historic homes & working with first time buyers, I work with developers, architects and builders to help them find and develop property. I have a lot of experience as well with condominium conversions. My process is to identify what a client's real estate needs and wants are and to do everything I can to help them realize their dream.
