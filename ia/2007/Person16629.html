Chris McGovern’s Summary

As president and founder of Emerging Marketing, Chris McGovern oversees all operations of the full service marketing agency. His primary responsibilities include developing new business, strategy, directing and approving creative work and serving as chief consultant for the agency.

Growing up in an entrepreneurial family, with all seven children opening their own companies, gave Chris early exposure to business ownership. Prior to founding Emerging Marketing in 1994, he worked in the marketing industry for 10 years, forging the eclectic background he relies on as president of his own company.

A track record of excellent service and measurable results keeps business growing extensively each year. Today, Emerging Marketing is among the top 75 direct marketing agencies in the nation. As well as in the top 15 agencies in Columbus, Ohio.

Chris has been recognized both locally and nationally for his outstanding accomplishments with his company, most recently cited as the 4th Fastest Emerging company in Columbus, Ohio as stated by Business First (newspaper). They were also a top ten finalist in the Small Business Administration sponsored “Small Business Person of the Year” Awards, and nationally as one of 23 Ohio businesses selected as a finalist for the “Blue Chip Enterprise Initiative” Awards. He has served as Community Relations Chair and Board of Trustee for the Nobel Peace Prize nominated International Visitors Council.

Chris was recently elected Chairmen of the Board for the first Contemporary African Art Gallery, Kiaca, founded by Artist Talle Bamazi. He is currently President of FirstLink
Chris is a strong contributor to the professional community. He has been a speaker for the American Marketing Association, Small Business Administration, Advertising Federation, Direct Marketing Association, Keynote speaker for the U.S. Postal Customer Council as well as moderator for one of only 6 national Department of State Town Meetings on West Asia.

Chris McGovern’s Specialties:

Our clients tell us we do everything from the Basics to the BIG idea. Emerging Marketing develops, designs, and delivers Accountable Marketing programs with measurable results. They help businesses generate leads, create sales, retain customers, strengthen customer relations, and increase brand awareness while providing clients with a higher return on investment (ROI).
