Ben Fisher’s Summary

A Jack of all trades. I am a Flash developer, a coder, a graphic designer, a photographer, a videographer, a technologist and an entrepreneur.

I'm interested in connecting with companies and individuals who embrace an innovative, humane approach to advertising–treating it as an art rather than a protocol–and a deep sense that people matter, both the people who do the work and the people who consume it.
