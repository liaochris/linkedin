Greg Boyd’s Summary

I have drafted and helped negotiate contracts for game industry licensing, investment, mergers, and development in the range of one million to more than fifty million dollars. I have worked on litigations in the game space with the potential for hundreds of millions of dollars in damages.

I am an author and editor of the popular industry reference book, Business and Legal Primer for Game Development. My speaking engagements include Harvard Business School, Columbia Law School, GDC, Austin Game Conference, State of Play. I have written for several publications including Game Developer magazine and Gamasutra. I also volunteer with the IGDA SIGs including helping edit and author the IGDA's first IP guide in 2003. I have been interviewed for commentary on business and law by Fortune, Forbes, CNN, and other news sources.

My goal is to keep helping people comfortably deal with the legal side of the game industry. I want to help make this "necessary evil" as painless as possible for my clients.

Greg Boyd’s Specialties:

Corporate law, IP law, international law, contract negotiation including development and licensing contracts, employment contracts, patents, trademarks, copyrights.
