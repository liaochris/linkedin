Mikael Blaisdell’s Summary

Beginning in the early days of Silicon Valley as a young management systems & procedures analyst at Atari, and continuing through tech-writer, hotline rep, trainer, channel mgmt. etc., positions with various companies, I have been a part of the high technology industry for almost 30 years. My career has included various management roles, a very wide range of consulting assignments across a spectrum of industries and organizations, presentations at countless conventions, and the publication of hundreds of white papers, feature articles and columns for key magazines. I've served on the boards of start-up technology companies, service/support professional associations and non-profits.

Besides writing and consulting, I've been the Secretary of the Board of East Brother Light Station, Inc. for many years. EBLS is a non-profit corporation responsible for the preservation of an historic lighthouse on an island in the San Francisco Bay area. (www.ebls.org) In addition to membership on the Finance, Facilities and Planning Committees of the lighthouse, I'm also the coordinator for the Wickies, the group of volunteers that keeps the light burning and maintains the facilities.
