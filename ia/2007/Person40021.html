Jeffery Yong’s Summary

A CPA by training, Jeffery has more than 10 years experience in management consulting, specialising in the areas of Supply Chain Improvement and financial analysis. His clients include HP, Nokia, Bayer Chemicals, Matsushita, Philips Medical Systems and Agilent Technologies.

Having had enough of living out of a suitcase, memorising flight schedules and saying good nights to his family via MSN Messenger, Jeffery decided to quit what some would call the glamorous high-flying consultant's life.

Currently, he's having lots of fun building multiple streams of passive income with his team while spending more time with his wife and kids.
