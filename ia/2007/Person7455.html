Ariane Benefit’s Summary

Being organized allows you to perform better at work, minimize conflict with coworkers and family members, provide better customer service, and live a life you really love!

My mission is to help people transform their lives by helping them create organized homes and offices that support them in living productive, happier lives.

My passion is helping families conquer the chaos and stress that keeps them from enjoying their life together. My background in Education, Psychology, Performance Improvement, and Group Process Facilitation, along with having been a live-in nanny for 3 years and having moved interstate several times, gives me a unique ability to not only create custom systems to make your life easier, but also to help you develop skills that enable you to stay organized.

I don't just organize stuff, I teach people how to simplify, clear clutter, clarify priorities and organize enough to live a life they love! Offering simple, creative yet practical solutions, I help people create the neater, simpler, less stressful life they dream of. I live in Bloomfield, NJ with her husband and 3 cats and can be found on the web at www.neatliving.org.

Visit my website to sign up for my monthly newsletter, check out my blog, and view my photo gallery of organizing makeovers!
