Greg Scott’s Summary

Greg is an inventor, strategist and entrepreneur. His entrepreneurial background includes the founding and building of Vision2Hire Solutions Inc. (www.vision2hire.com) where he was Founder, CEO, Senior Product Manager and Director. Vision2Hire was one of the first Internet Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS,) launching first in 1995. Vision2Hire Solutions developed and marketed it’s ATS for Human Resource practitioners. Vision2Hire software was ideal for any sized company in any business, and had received notable success in Bio/Medical / Technology, Educational, Hospitality, Industrial and Entertainment markets. Its key clients included Intrawest, Janssen-Ortho, Lafarge Canada and WestJet Airlines. Vision2Hire was sold in 2004 to Brainhunter Inc. Greg’s most recent entrepreneurial venture, previous to Jiibe, was Stealfocus, a Las Vegas theatrical production company, where he was the Co-founder, Managing Director and Creative Partner.

Business strategy, business planning, entrepreneur, marketing, business building, internet recruiting technologies, coaching.
