Kevin Moore’s Summary

Mr. Kevin Moore has six years of relevant computing experience, particularly in architecture and programming in the military domain in the public sector and manufacturing production and sales in the private sector . He has skills in object oriented architecture, work organization, team leading, relational databases and he knows he is very comfortable programming using the Microsoft .Net Framework (1.0, 1.1 and 2.0) and the C# language.

During those years, Mr. Moore has built up a solid background in organic architecture, work organization and programming, . He also managed his own company where he conducted and supervised projects related to parking facility management, election campaign management and restaurant management. He also worked as a programmer analyst for two companies specializing in ERP software design.

Mr. Moore gained some work organization and project management experience when he managed the custom development department, by structuring work methods as well as imposing rules and ensuring they were followed.
At the end of his studies, he received an honorable mention from the “Fédération des informaticiens du Québec” for his academic performance and extracurricular commitments. Mr. Moore is also familiar with the Spanish language and culture, having worked in Latin America for six months. His reliability, considerable resourcefulness, sense of responsibility, leadership and people skills make him an extremely flexible professional.
