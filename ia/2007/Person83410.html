Susan Dunn’s Summary

My goal is to help you achieve your goals - in career, parenting, relationships, life satisfaction, self development, life enrichment, emotional intelligence, resilience,and wellness. Visit me on the web at www.susandunn.cc and mailto:sdunn@susandunn.cc .

Susan trains and certifies coaches worldwide in a fast, affordable, no-residency program. She has certified coaches all over the US, Malaysia, India, UK, Ireland, Scotland, the Phillipines, Bermuda, Canada, and more.

Over 25 years working with people on their personal goals, life enrichment, wellness, and all areas of personal and professional development. Susan has been chosen Adult & Senior Development Expert for SelfGrowth, the largest self help portal on the Internet, chosen #1 by yahoo and google. She is Attract Your Dream Partner Expert for expert.com, and Ask the EQ Expert for WebProNews.

Susan has a master's degree in psychology. She is widely published on the Internet, and a leading expert in Emotional Intelligence. She coaches clients around the world, publishes 3 ezines, is founder of Club Vivo Per Lei (www.susandunn.cc.vivoperlei.htm), and serves on the Board of the Centre for Human Development.

Susan has been a frequent speaker on cruise lines and is the author of SPEAK ON A CRUISE, TRAVEL THE WORLD FOR PENNIES. She has written over 20 ebooks, including DATING SUCCESS MANUAL FOR WOMEN. Her ebooks are available here: http://www.webstrategies.cc/ebooklibrary.html .
