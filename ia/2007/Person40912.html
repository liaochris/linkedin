Jessica Ward’s Experience

  • Ph.D Candidate - Ecology, Evolution and Behaviour

    University of Toronto

    (Educational Institution; 10,001 or more employees; Higher Education industry)

    September 2003 — Present (4 years 4 months)

    I am currently completing my Ph.D in evolutionary ecology.
    I am broadly interested in the evolution of animal signalling systems and investigating the interplay between natural and sexual selection in the diversification of communication signals, as well as the role that such signals play in speciation. Currently, I am studying a piscine system (brook stickleback, Culaea inconstans) but I have previously worked on neotropical bats, crustaceans and birds.
