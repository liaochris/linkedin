Sue Brettell’s Summary

Personal Brand Design : online identity design & communication : compelling design and copy for dynamic solopreneurs and professionals.

I provide fresh, contemporary designs and authentic copy to creatives, coaches, and entrepreneurs who want to build a vibrant online presence and maximize the impact of a unique personal brand.

My creative background includes three-dimensional design, advertising and publishing. I've been a solopreneur since 1989, providing graphic, web and book design, writing, print-buying and online research services. As a part-time actor, I combine my talents as a member of, and creative consultant for, DayWalker Productions, a theatre and film company.

I am a Reach Certified Personal Brand Strategist, and have designed for Reach for four years. As a multi-disciplinary creative, and a passionate exponent of personal branding, I work with my clients collaboratively to express their unique brand attributes online through effective visual and written communication.

Sue Brettell’s Specialties:

Personal brand strategy, multi-disciplinary design (including graphics, websites, logos, corporate collateral, design for print, book jackets/layout), illustration, book production, writing, copy writing, editing, proof-reading, online copy, online research and picture research, print-buying, project management, event management, acting.
