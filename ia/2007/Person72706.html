Nicholas Ridis’s Summary

Currently the Director of AVODKA and maleMorphosis, lecturing in Marketing and Management at University and a Director of the Australian Marketing Institute.

ABOUT NICHOLAS

He has consulted to multinationals and SME's and reached the Australian national finals in 2003 and 2004, of the Boston Consulting Group Strategy Competition. The BCG competition is regarded as the world's premier strategy consulting competition. Mr Ridis has a growing public profile gained by chairing various marketing events, is on the speaker's circuit, a regular contributor to Professional Marketing, The Sydney Morning Herald and B&T Weekly.

Mr Ridis’ University qualifications are in Management, Marketing and Building, he is a Fellow of the Australian Marketing Institute, Fellow of the Australian Institute of Management, Member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors, a Certified Practising Marketer, a Certified Professional Manager and a Certified Real Estate Agent.

Nicholas is a Tertiary and industry certified manager and marketer, skilled in leading and coordinating direct reports and cross-functional teams, managing a product line from cradle to grave, justifying new product development investments, determining and documenting new product requirements, developing sales forecasts and product pricing, re-branding and launching new products to the marketplace. Proven ability to manage key account relationships and large-scale projects. Seasoned presenter to all levels and group sizes from CEO to shop floor. Proficient at corporate politics.
