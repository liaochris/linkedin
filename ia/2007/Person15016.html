Catherine Van Holder’s Summary

I would like to play a responsible role in a creative company with an innovative vision, aiming at solutions that at once be strategically correct, creative and effective.

I combine an analytic with a creative mind which feels at best in strategic problem-solving situations. Rooted in a holistic world view, I believe in the importance of a diversity of interests and backgrounds whenever innovative ideas are of importance. From that point of view I am convinced the combination of my philosophical and commercial-creative background can be of value for creative conceptual companies.

    I believe from time to time one should allow itself to take a step back and try to get another perspective at things. Since, traveling is not only about exploring new continents. A substantial amount of the voyage covers exploration of your innerscapes. Having the courage to look back and forward, but most of all be in the present in the most honest way possible. To step back and confront your strengths and weaknesses. To step back in with a mixture of acceptance and bravery.
