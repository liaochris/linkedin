Judith Germain (jude@dynamic-transitions.co.uk)’s Summary

Dynamic Transitions Ltd focuses on developing leadership talent and social intelligence. We develop leaders with our strategic management, executive leadership programmes, speaking engagements and tailored interventions with individuals and teams.

A particular speciality of the business is working with Troublesome Talent™. This is the type of person who is recognised as having huge potential and yet has many rough edges and is normally causing problems with colleagues and peers. We often get involved where there is a dilemma - 'Do I sack or do I develop?' Sacking can be a very expensive option, not least due to the disruption to the business. We find it easy to turn these people around so that they end up fulfilling their true potential.

All Dynamic Transitions Ltd consultants are recognised experts in their fields who are experienced in working with market leaders in a number of industries in particular Financial Services, Retail, Media, Defence, Quasi Public Sector and Sales.
