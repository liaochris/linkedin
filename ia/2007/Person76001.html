Sam Mikkelsen’s Summary

My Professional Experience
I have personally worked in IT Recruitment for over ten years having worked for one of the largest companies in the UK (Computer Futures) and also one of the smallest (Senitor Associates). Previously spent over five years working as a salesperson within Advertising (PSI Ltd) and Insurance (Norwich Union). In 2003 my business partner (Colin Adams) and I established and set up ess&cee partners Ltd. Colin had over ten years experience in Telecoms from a technical sales point of view, working for Cable and Wireless and Genesis Communications.

Our goal as a recruitment company was clear and simple. “Deliver high quality recruitment solutions to our customers showing true value for money every time”. This has been the essence of our business and now into our 3rd year our policy has not changed. With our Technical and Recruitment knowledge we understand highly technical requirements and are able to fill jobs quickly.

We are based in the Northwest of England, and with links to all major motorway links we are able to help organisations find locally based, highly skilled people anywhere in the UK.

Our specialty is within the IT, Telecoms and Software Engineering marketplace but with unlimited access to the UK’s largest and most popular recruitment websites and job boards, our clients benefit from the bulk discounts we receive, therefore we are ideally placed to handle almost any recruitment needs you may have.

To find out more visit our website - www.ess-ceepartners.co.uk
