Manoj Ranaweera’s Summary

I founded ebdex (http://www.ebdex.co.uk) to address customer pains in the areas of accounts payable, accounts receivable and procurement. The solutions bring significant benefits realisation to both small and large organisations improving the operational efficiencies of supply chains, customer bases and governance. Customer benefits include increasing profitability, improved cash flow, decreasing disputes, real time financial information for decision making and compliance. Visit http://www.ebdex.co.uk for further information.

I have successfully leveraged skills in IT, consultancy, engineering, rail, telecommunications and utilities sectors for the past 15 years, working for start-ups and multi-nationals representing end-clients, leading banks, bidding consortia, and contractors during various stages of project and product lifecycles in the UK, Middle East, Africa and the Asia.

I completed an Executive MBA at Manchester Business School in 2005 to strengthen my understanding and analytical skills in corporate dynamics, financials, strategic planning and operational management.

I am interested in getting involved in other start-ups, businesses that operate within the wider "spend management" industry including EIPP and providing consultancy services. I am very much interested in bringing innovation and performance/process improvements to any business.
