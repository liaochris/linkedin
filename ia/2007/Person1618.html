Adrian Hartley’s Summary

I have 17 years experience of outsourcing, both in the delivery of outsourced services having spent 10 years with Sema Group (now Atos Origin) and as a customer, involving; service delivery, best practice (ITIL, Prince2 Practitioner), ISO9000, programme management, bid management and sales, but also the procurement and management of outsourced services involving; contract negotiation, service definition, service management, IT governance, supplier management, software/hardware licence/contract and asset management.

Experience includes internal and external outsourcing in the areas of IT, procurement, facilities management and accounting. A confident decision maker and strategic thinker, skilled in meeting and dealing with people at all levels. Versatile with proven commercial, project management and organisational skills combined with high level of technical awareness.

As a manager I am a strong leader but with a collaborative style. I firmly believe in setting objectives at a team and personal level to maintain consistency, visibility and continuous improvement.

The experience I have gained working for an outsource service provider and as a Consultant I have extensive project management and communication skills. I have successfully managed large projects in politically complex environments and am able to communicate at all levels. I am capable of driving solutions as occasion demands and also to finding solutions through negotiation, influence and compromise.
