Matt Evans’s Summary

I have a broad international web development background with a human centered approach to development, information design and UE.

Currently I am looking working with the multinational company EF Education's Language group. I will provide technical project management on a global level and act as a guide to new product development for a forthcoming re-architecture that will be released into over 50 countries world wide.

My aim with any project is always to provide both the user and client an exceptional experience by leveraging creative ideas and utilizing the most appropriate, effective and cutting edge technologies.
