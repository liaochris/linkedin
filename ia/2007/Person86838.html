Tom Merilahti’s Summary

Tom Merilahti is an INBORN Lateral Thinker, Processor, Navigator, Ideator, Matchmaker, Inspirator, Motivator, Activator, Generator, and a GLOBAL Networker.

Tom is Creative: He is a Marketing Integrator & Communication Coordinator'. Has been described as a "lateral thinker, self-willed way maker and a rebellious pre-runner". More than 20 years of professional experience. Globally very well connected. One of the leading networkers in Europe. His mobile head office is located between his shoulders in Helsinki.

Tom is the founder of SICU (Synergy Integrated - Connects the Unconnected), and the SICU Synergy Solutions Group (SICU SSG) operating globally through 33 affiliated organisations and global partners representing 24 countries (28.12.2006).

SICU is a Multidimensional and Functional Management and Marketing Communication agency/organisation, which objective is to offer resources, and to create cost effectivity, added value and multiplicative effects for businesses, corporations, brands, industries, institutions, and ultimately consumers in diverse markets.

SICU can easily be described via the Finnish word and concept SISU (Sauna & Sibelius). It is an ability to get things done; describing staying power, will power and decisiveness to get things done even against impossible odds (e.g. Winter War 1939-1940). SISU is exactly what is needed to stand out in the way you think and act, and in the way you practice marketing communication in the chaotic world of today.

OBJECTIVES: To serve the customer by reducing the Coordination problems and to increase the Opportunities for Creative Cooperation, Added Value, Multiplicative Effects and Cost Effectivity.
