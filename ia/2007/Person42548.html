Joe Tucker’s Summary

Joe Tucker received a BS in Mathematics in 1980 and spent the next 15 years in sales and marketing for industrial companies. In 1995 he joined The Duval Group working with engineering positions. He became a CSAM after only 2 years and is the recipient of numerous awards for recruiting excellence. His genuine concern for the careers of candidates and the business interests of clients forges win-win results.

I believe that the time spent clearly establishing the needs and goals of my clients is time invested well. I focus on helping candidates and employers get the 'complete picture' so they can make decisions with confidence and quickly move ahead to future successes.

Joe Tucker’s Specialties:

I work with the most successful Consulting Engineering firms at all experience levels. My clients look to me to provide enthusiastic talented professionals who have more than technical ability to offer. They look for leaders and reward their results with bright and fulfilling careers.
