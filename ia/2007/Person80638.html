A Woman's Place was started by Dawn Charles as an Ecademy Club focussed on women. It grew into a Trusted Network and today there are regional AWP Clubs all around the world.
This is a forum for women where there are opportunities to network both on and off line, share experiences, resources, contacts, expertise, knowledge and learning. And to have fun!
What we would like to achieve with this Belgian AWP club, is to have online pleasant chat's and we are also very busy to arrange quarterly meetings (for starters) with female speakers who bring us interesting subjects, to enrich our own business experiences.
