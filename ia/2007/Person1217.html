Adam Kanne’s Summary

Adam Kanne is Deputy Director for Strategy, Communications and PR at Baltic Development Forum in Copenhagen, a think tank and networking organization for corporations, governments and academics working to promote growth and competitiveness of the Northern Europe. Adam has a background in international diplomacy and Policy Development. He has got a Master of Laws from Uppsala University and an MBA in International Management and Commercial Diplomacy from the Fisher Graduate School of International Business in Monterey, California. Adam is a passionate diver, sailor and cook, and is refining his taste of Californian wines.
