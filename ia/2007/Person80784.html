Soni Pitts’s Summary

For the past few years, I've been a personal and spiritual development coach specializing in helping people build sustainable, joyful and successful lives and lifestyles that work for them as well as the world around them.

I am now in the process of creating a successful copywriting business focusing on those who work in the "world saving" business, but who are having a hard time creating a marketing message that shares their vision in a compelling and effective manner. I am also taking a year sabbatical to serve with an Americorps program promoting literacy and academic success in underprivileged children.

I am also expanding my training and coaching work into the field of social capital and networking. After all, if we're going to save the world, it's a lot easier - not to mention a hell of a lot more fun - to do it together.
