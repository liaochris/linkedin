Kevin Richardson’s Summary

I started as a layout artist, animator, and overseas animation director for Hanna Barbera and special effects animator at ILM. Totalled, I have worked on 5 feature films, 3 children's TV series, 10 or so commercials (cinema and TV), and produced 25+ E rated games for CD or download, plus a few flash games-- the latest is Family Feud Online Party which you can download from www.iwin.com, miniclip.com and other sites (see links below). My goal is to run a small development organization where people are inspired to inovate, think out of the box (way out), push story telling and technology to the limit, and make the most exciting entertainment.

Kevin Richardson’s Specialties:

I was an overseas animation director, special effects animator, and illustrator for years. I have worked in dev. on TV series, commercials, raised money for speculative productions and pilot films. I also write fiction, an ocassional article, and have an upcoming piece in Casual Connect - the industry mag for the casual game industry.
