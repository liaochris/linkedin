David Ting’s Summary

I am currently working at Yahoo and am responsible for extending and optimizing an ultra scalable services platform that powers the Yahoo Music portal and the Yahoo Music Unlimited Services.

Prior to joining Yahoo in 2007, I was the Senior Director of Engineering for Entriq. I was responsible for the development of the security, billing, care and content publishing product lines at Entriq. I have helped to company to establish a new Digital Stores product line and strategy, which brought in over 70% of the company's revenue in 2005 and 2006.

Prior to joining Entriq in 2004, I served as the Director of Engineering at Movielink for over 2 years, and was responsible the development of the Movielink site, ecommerce engine, Content Management System, Content Distribution Network and the Digital Rights Management technology. I helped grow the Movielink service from inception to becoming one of the top video-on-demand services on the internet.

Previously, I was the Senior Director of Engineering for BayLogics, which was one of the leading providers of commercial real estate management software for Fortune 500 companies. I also worked as the Director of Engineering at AltaVista, where he managed the development of a popular ecommerce search and shopping network, shopping.com, which experienced over six million page views a day. Previous experience also includes IBM, WiseConnect and Zip2. During my career, I have received two Outstanding Technical Achievement Awards at IBM, and earned AltaVista’s Employee of the Year honor in 1999.
