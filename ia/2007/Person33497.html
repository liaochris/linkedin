Guido Lankhorst’s Summary

Guido manages an international e-Business team at Organon International (Organon -with shared head offices in Roseland, NJ, USA, and Oss, The Netherlands- is the human health care business unit of Akzo Nobel). His team is responsible for developing and executing interactive marketing programs that support Organon’s brand strategies. Guido has over eight years of experience within the field of eMarketing/eBusiness/Multi-channel Marketing/CRM. Prior to joining Organon, Guido has been working as management consultant. He holds a Master of Science in Business Administration from the Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University of Rotterdam in The Netherlands.
