Margaret S (Peggy) Glass, JD, MBA, CFP®’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

- Strategically-oriented JD/MBA generalist with broadly based legal, financial, executive management and leadership experience in corporate, entrepreneurial, nonprofit service and client advisory settings

- President/Owner, TurningPoints LLC, beginning 2005 - professional advisor and coach to individuals in transition, change management consultant to businesses and nonprofit organizations needing/wanting to change their prevailing culture & processes, interim executive, trained mediator & group facilitator, public speaker

- Prior background - corporate counsel (13+ yrs), corporate treasurer (7+ yrs), financial representative (6+ yrs) and nearly 20 yrs. nonprofit & community leader

- Finish writing, and then publish, several articles and books currently in the works

- Interested in projects, collaborations, interim executive assignments, positions on Boards of Directors / Trustees, possibly CEO/Executive Director position, calling for a visionary, insightful, inclusive, seasoned leader

- Personal interest in spiritual journey, Law of Attraction, feng shui, FLOW
