Warren H. Wong’s Summary

Warren Wong is a senior human resources professional with several years of progressive human resources management experience. He has a successful leadership track record in working in high growth complex matrix organizations.

As a strategic business partner, change agent, and member of senior management teams, Warren established the Canadian human resources functions for national and global internet/technology companies.

Warren has the ability to effectively contribute, support and influence organizational operations and strategic business strategies working with company CEOs, presidents and business unit heads. Warren has built a reputation for being a thoughtful and progressive human resources leader working mostly with high growth and/or emerging companies or business units. With more than twenty years of North American HR experience Warren is able to contribute to and lead and/or build a full service human resources function that transforms, enables and inspires organizations.
