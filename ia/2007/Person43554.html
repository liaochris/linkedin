Jonathan Soares’s Summary

Q Products, Inc. is a Connecticut based company committed to the rapid and strategic retail expansion of its gourmet products on a National and Global level. Q Products, Inc has approached every possible trade and distribution channel to ensure maximum market penetration. Utilizing their aggressive guerilla marketing and promotional strategies they have been able to target consumers from every possible demographic achieving optimum consumer and brand awareness.

Q was founded by Jonathan Soares a 23 year-old mastermind in the sales, marketing, brand development and pioneering forefront as a college project in 2005. Jonathan has aggressively taken his company from regional placement in the northeast to national placement and distribution in less than 2 years in operations.

Q Products, Inc. first product launch consisted of Jonathan's Q Gourmet BBQ Sauces ("You can't have BB without the Q"), with 3 mouthwatering flavors; Hickory Teriyaki BBQ, Spicy Cajun BBQ and Original Honey BBQ. But Q isn't just a BBQ sauce company. Q Products is about to unleash one of the hottest trends in the restaurant industry to the retail supermarket arena. The fusion concept has transitioned and revolutionized our typical cooking techniques and styles in the United States. Utilizing unique flavor profiles, and techniques from different countries and cultures; Q Products will revolutionize the specialty industry with their mouthwatering culinary creations.
