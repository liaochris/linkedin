Alfredo Coppola’s Summary

Over 15 years U.S. business development experience in the software (consumer Internet and enterprise) and telecom (wireless and wireline) sectors, working with global technology companies such as Corel, Macromedia, Microsoft, Nortel Networks, Paramount Pictures, PricewaterhouseCoopers and the US Market Access Center in Silicon Valley.

Before joining ChoiceBot, he was managing partner of San Francisco-based e-Storm International, where he headed up their Canadian office. There he offered Canadian technology companies a direct channel to Silicon Valley market intelligence and introductions to first-strike customers, investors and partners. Since launching their KickStart USA program in Canada last fall, Alfredo has helped to accelerate the growth of 13 emerging high tech Canadian companies.

In parallel with his duties at e-Storm, Alfredo was an authorized agent of the US Market Access Center (USMAC) in Silicon Valley, where he leveraged an extensive contact list of industry influencers, angel investors and VCs to help Canadian high-tech firms with US market expansion.

Prior to e-Storm/USMAC, he was president and co-founder of three Canadian technology companies, which he led to multimillion-dollar growth with offices in Ottawa and San Francisco, California. Alfredo has also previously held senior sales positions at marketing communications firms in Ottawa and Calgary, Canada.
