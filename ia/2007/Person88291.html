Urban Empire (info@urbanempire.tv)’s Summary

Urban Empire, which was founded in 2002, is a Hip Hop conglomerate that specializes in bringing new and innovative ideas to the San Diego/Southern California urban/hip-hop market and the rest of the country. Created by both club and street promoters as well as music producers and DJ's, we have a decided advantage in keeping up with the latest trends and keeping in touch with the streets.
