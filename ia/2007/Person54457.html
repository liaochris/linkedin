Jessica Margolin’s Summary

I think about and work on problems concerning the design of organizations, institutions, and communities by examining areas such as unacknowledged expectations, communication systems and messages, and learning filters.

I consider the financial markets key communication systems. My current work includes research in the areas of corporate social responsibility, intangible asset valuation, and other areas of financial and economic reform.

Web 2.0 is one critical delivery mechanism. I am most interested in the evolution of news and information transmission via web 2.0 mechanisms, interpersonal relationships and dynamics, and data analysis. I am interested in Prediction Markets and other forms of assimilating aggregate wisdom.

My background is in financial analysis, business development, organizational design, and marketing strategy and communications. My educational background includes an MS in Materials Science in the area of nanotechnology and an MBA. I have worked primarily in the internet and financial industries, and also have done philanthropic work in the educational, music, and environmental domains.
