Eric Wood’s Summary

Raised in Western Canada and formally trained by European Chefs, Eric Wood has enjoyed the best of both worlds when it comes to his cooking style. His style is uniquely Canadian, evoking both tradition and innovation. A chef known for his Focus and Risk taking, his award winning cuisine is sure to please even the most discriminating of palates. His innovative menus are hand crafted using only the freshest and finest ingredients available, many sourced from the local waters, farms and artisan producers of Alberta and B.C. Eric’s commitment to freshness and his penchant for innovation necessitate the creation of new menu items on an ongoing basis.
