Karen Lippe’s Summary

PR/marketing consulting company services private and public high technology companies with strategic and creative planning, program management and project implementation. Services include: company positioning/messaging, company and product launches, media/analyst tours, press kits materials and communications audits. Partial client list: Adobe (font division), Compaq (non-profit Bay Area-based outreach programs), Conxion ((managed host services), FileNet, Hyperion (CXO programs), MOLI (social networking), Oracle (division support), Software.com (company/product launch) and Texas Tea (company launch). Worked with Trainer Communications in 2006 on Accept Software, agami Systems, Itronix, and GlobalLogic.

Karen Lippe’s Experience

  • PR Manager & Market Analyst Relations

    Canon Computer Systems, Inc.

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

  • Senior Consultant

    Regis McKenna Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 1987 — September 1992 (5 years 3 months)

    RMI, a dynamic and fast-paced marketing firm, pioneered relationship marketing, a method of reaching out, interacting and communicating with all the audiences a company encounters.
