Scott Cohen’s Summary

Scott Cohen is spearheading development of a newly funded venture in interactive advertising as CEO of VideoAdGames. He is a founding Partner in the Red Tie Media Group, and the former President of Game Trust. As a co-founder and senior level executive, Cohen was critical to the success of two IPO's over the past nine years: LivePerson (NASDAQ: LPSN), and 24/7 Media (NASDAQ: TFSM).

Currently on Board of Directors of Active Response Group, LeadFusion, and Double Trump.

Scott Cohen’s Specialties:

Cohen has over 30 years experience in strategic deal making, negotiation and leadership, playing a major role in developing businesses during the growth of the Internet in the US and Europe.
