Grant Hauber’s Summary

Aequero Pte. Ltd. provides boutique financial and project development advisory services to the energy and infrastructure industries throughout Asia. Based in Singapore and Hong Kong, we specialize in mobilizing financing in the developing markets of Asia.

For private sector clients, we help to commercially structure energy and infrastructure projects/transactions and then assist those projects in raising both debt and equity capital. For public sector clients, we consult on development of new energy/infrastructure policy and assist in running tendering programs for new development. Our primary focus is on renewable energy projects, especially those that have the potential to generate Certified Emission Reduction credits (CERs) under Kyoto Clean Development Mechanism (CDM).

We are currently in the process of establishing an early stage development equity fund that focuses on applying a combination of early stage risk capital and hands-on project development skills to clean energy projects in Asia. Aequero intends to apply its experience in project development in its role as asset manager to actively manage and support its invested companies. The fund will cover renewable energy and energy efficiency projects and project clustering structures.

My experience encompasses leading roles as project developer, investment banker, strategy consultant, and financial advisor to both public and private sector clients. Combined with 12 years' experience in Asia, this perspective allows me to provide informed advice no matter which side of the negotiating table I sit. I have lead transaction experience in the electric power, natural gas, water and wastewater infrastructure segments.
