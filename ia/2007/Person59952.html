Milind Purandare’s Summary

Milind is a co-founder of Manas Solutions Pvt. Ltd. a privately held company.

Manas Solutions Pvt Ltd (Manas) is a software development company headquartered in Pune, INDIA.

Manas has strong Design, Delivery Capabilities and Quality Assurance. In addition use of Agile based development methodologies enables the client and Manas to have a predictable quality level with verifiable and visible deliverables.

Manas has a highly skilled and committed team which has extensive experience in dealing with the international markets including Japan (A wholly owned subsidiary incorporated in Japan in 2001 - Manas Solutions Japan Inc).

Manas has expertise is in the area of forming and executing Offshore Development Centers for our customers.

A successful track record is demonstrated by repeat orders from its customers which include large Consulting companies, ISV's and End User companies.
