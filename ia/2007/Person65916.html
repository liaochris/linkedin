Dennis Taylor’s Summary

Experienced management/business consultant with over 30 years of experience in customer service, organzational development, leadership, planning and implementation, and process definition/improvement. Seeking opportunities to assist governments and jurisdictions, and companies of all sizes and types that could benefit from an external perspective, a fresh set of eyes, and and extra pair of hands to get things done! We are located in Fauquier County, VA and our services are available on a global basis.

Please contact us for a summary of projects completed!

Dennis Taylor’s Specialties:

We work with Municipalities, small-to-large sized private and public companies and enterprises, and non-profits. Our services center on developing business and operating structure, organizational assessment/planning and development, business/marketing/strategic planning, coaching, and mentoring. We also provide quality/technical services such as: supplier surveillance services in China and Taiwan; Six Sigma, Root Cause/Corrective Action, Lean, and Malcolm Baldrige training programs.
