Mark Chrisman’s Summary

Mark is an eLearning Specialist for The Regence Group (Blue Cross and Blue Shield Association), where he develops enterprise web-based training.

His experience includes developing eLearning courseware, tutorials and web-based job aids for Children’s Hospital and Regional Medical Center, as well as multimedia production for The International Center for Limb Lengthening.

His career includes healthcare IT training design and development, as well as digital production for print, web and multimedia. He has a B.S. in Communications/Fine Art from Towson University, MD and a Certificate in Multimedia from the Univeristy of Washington.
