Steve Borges’s Summary

Steve set up Biglight in spring 2004. The business has since become a leading provider of ecommerce advice and support to leading UK retailers and luxury brands.

From 1999-2004 Steve was at Dixons Stores Group (DSGi) where he held a number of senior roles, before becoming Director of Advertising and Direct Businesses.

Steve began his career with Philips Electronics, where he worked in the UK and France and held a variety of roles in Finance, Marketing, PR and Customer Services.

Steve is a Chartered Management Accountant; he has an MBA from The University of Strathclyde and is an alumnus of the Windsor Leadership Trust. He is fluent in French and mediocre (but working hard to improve) in Spanish.
