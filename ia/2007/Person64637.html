Wayne Smallman’s Summary

Octane Interactive came into being on the 14th of June 1999. So I've been around for over 7 years, which in this industry means that I'm among the elder statesmen. I've witnessed a lot of change.

Octane is a new media design agency. That means I work with you to find innovative, professional, engaging and cost-effective ways of making things like websites, web-based applications, interactive tools like cd roms & dvd's and on-line presentations help sell and market what it is that you do.

I'm based in Barnsley, South Yorkshire, making Octane quite central to a number of major towns & cities, such as Sheffield, Doncaster, Huddersfield, Rotherham, Wakefield and Leeds.

I provide my services to the education, healthcare and manufacturing sectors among others, and I also partner with design agencies to help them extend the range of services they offer to their client base.

Wayne Smallman’s Specialties:

Taking complex ideas and turning them into simple products and services.

Making technology invisible, unobtrusive and simple.

The specifics of my trade include: PHP development, MySQL, SEO, SEM, accessibility & disabilities usability, web-based applications, business software, consultancy, CSS, HTML, content management systems, bespoke design, Flash development, ecommerce.
