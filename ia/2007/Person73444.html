Robert Stulle’s Summary

I am a Business Consultant with a strong background in graphic design and visual communication. I worked on projects developing, designing and realizing intranet portals, internet solutions and HR related systems for large companies.
I have specialized in developing strategies and roadmaps for a company’s web based future and in developing the functional design of web based solutions.
I also am an experienced project manager and team leader.

I started working as a Business Consultant five years ago when I switched from the design world to the IT and Business sector. As a designer I have over ten years of professional experience. I have specialized in the design for online media, and the work on large corporate design projects.

In the past three years I was a member of IBM's User Centered Design Group developing expertise in the field of usability, user experience design and user interface design.

Currently I work as an independent Consultant and Designer in Berlin, mostly for SpiekermannPartners. (http://www.spiekermannpartners.de)

I believe that the IT Business world still has a blind spot in the sense that the added value of competent visual design is underestimated or ignored altogether.
I aim to combine my knowledge about visual communication and the power of design with my skills as a Business Consultant.

I want to create business solutions that really add value and just work.
