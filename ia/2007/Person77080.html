THE SCARY GUY’s Summary

At 6ft tall, 20 stone and tattooed from head to toe ... The Scary Guy is quite possibly the most powerful Agent For Change on the planet today! To date, Scary has worked with millions of people around the world, addressing core behavioural issues, face to face. The results are immediate and lasting, drastically reducing today’s spiraling cycle of aggression in schools, communities and the workplace. His approach is unique, and uncompromising as he teaches students, parents and adults alike about what they can do on an individual level to change the world, teaching all how to take responsibility for their own behaviour in the form of their words and actions as Energy.

TEACHERS’ TV Throughout this year, Teachers’ TV has featured Scary’s work in the UK, providing viewers with an insight to his methods in High Schools. Filmed as part of their Inspirations series, a further piece, Scary Does Primary, will be broadcast in October. Scary also joined Peter Curran on the sofa of the Teachers’ TV Chat Show, The Bell, to give him a hug and discuss how to turn the tide on kids killing one another.

SCARBOROUGH PROJECT Scarborough headteacher, Hugh Bellamy, was so impressed with Scary’s work, that with sponsorship from their local Pindar Corporation, he signed Scary up to a three-year contract. Now a familiar face in the school’s corridors, Scary has clearly built up a rapport with its troubled teens. Pindar School, then amongst North Yorkshire’s lowest performing schools, is now one of the most improved, recently receiving The Diana Anti-bullying Award. Working on the philosophy that continued punishment does not stop antisocial behaviour, The Scary Guy focuses on developing solid emotional intelligence and communication skills, in place of fists and verbal assaults.
