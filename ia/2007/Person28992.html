Frederik Durant’s Summary

Frederik Durant, MBA has more than 10 years experience as a software developer, project leader and manager in the field of language, speech and Internet technology. He is a Certified VoiceXML Application Developer.

In his career, Frederik has been involved in machine translation (Siemens Nixdorf), database-driven websites (Allmansland), mass-market dictation products (L&H), speech-driven phone applications (Tellme Europe), and biomedical text mining (University of Antwerp).

Today his main ambition is to help build the local speech-enabled telephony market by designing and developing compelling self-service applications in the call center domain.

Frederik is a member of ContactCentres.be, the professional organisation of Belgian Contact Centres. He was chairman of CC.be's Speech Technology Workgroup from July 2005 to February 2007, and is still an active member.

Frederik Durant’s Specialties:

Speech-enabled phone applications, based on VXML/VoiceXML. See http://www.prompt-speechapps.com/ for more detailed information. Strong focus on usability (sine qua non for speech applications) and on controlled development processes.
