Daleyne Guay’s Summary

Please feel free to connect with me directly. Visit my blog at http://blog.fanbox.net

A graduate of Montréal’s Hautes Études commerciales (HEC) in retail and distribution management, Daleyne Guay has garnered solid expertise in the field of database relationship marketing, which he has consistently put into practice over the years. In fact, he has developed innovative relationship marketing solutions that have led to response rates as high as 45%. Mr. Guay has worked for a number of national and international companies in sectors as varied as insurance, food, tourism, financial services and technology. Among others, he has worked for: La Fédération des caisses Desjardins, Visa, MasterCard, Parmalat, Les assurances BELAIRdirect, Société des Casinos du Québec and NEC Computers (Europe).

Daleyne Guay is the recipient of numerous relationship marketing campaign awards and is an expert in the area of business start-up and re-launch.
His extensive experience has led to an enviable expertise in computer technology and customer loyalty. He currently dedicates all of his time and energy to FanBox. As a highly sought after key-note speaker, Daleyne Guay is also an active member of several Canadian and European associations which specialize in marketing or advertising, such as the Montreal Advertising Club, Montreal Marketing Association, and la Fédération de l’informatique du Québec.

Daleyne Guay is a born entrepreneur, a database marketing aficionado, overall techno-junkie, and a true workaholic. Nonetheless, he always spares a moment or two for a leisurely spin on his motorcycle.
