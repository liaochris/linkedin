Scott Adams’s Summary

Born in Albuquerque, New Mexico, where his father was serving in the United States Army, Scott Adams has lived almost all his life in Texas, attending public schools in Baytown, Austin, and Clear Lake City (Houston) and college at Lee College and the University of Houston at Clear Lake, where his major area of study was Accounting Information Systems. Scott has extensive experience in marketing and sales strategies in the Telecommunications and Computer Information Systems. From 1984 to 1994, he was a Senior Major Account Manager in Network Sales for MicroAge Computer Centers – Advanced Systems Group. From 1994 to 1997, he was a Multi-Processor Server Specialist for NEC Computer Systems Division, and from 1997-2000, for GTE Scott was a National ADSL On-Site Manager where he was chartered with deploying the first commercially available DSL network for GTE Communications. From 2000-2003 he was Regional Sales Manager for Interspeed a start up company with innovative DSLAM carrier technology. Scott has been a Channel Manager with Covad since 2005 during which time he has been part of Covad’s innovative Hosted VoIP channel initiative.

Scott Adams’s Specialties:

Experienced Channel Sales Manager in both Telecommunicatiosn and Computer Systems. Expert in LAN and WAN Architecture, VoIP, Network Operating Systems, Cisco, Nortel, Carrier Networks, MPLS, IP, FR, ATM, Network Security and Hosted Services. Skilled in soluiton selling and bringing new technologies to the commercial market.
