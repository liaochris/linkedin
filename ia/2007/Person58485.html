Michael Hoffman’s Summary

See3 Communications provides the U.S. nonprofit sector with video and multimedia production and distribution services. See3 helps nonprofit organizations navigate a new world where video and multimedia replace print as the primary means for nonprofit communication.

See3 is launching DoGooderTV, the first social network built around nonprofit media. DGTV is being built as a community for people who care about issues and organizations. Our goal is to bring this qualified audience into the work of organizations through the power of video. Once emotionally hooked by watching their videos, the audience will be able to act – to donate, sign-up or learn more.

Hoffman Media Group is a provider of web and software development outsourcing. Our primary customers are established software companies and start-ups serving clients in a range of industries. We have resources in our Chicago office with a fast-growing off-shore development center.

  • Board of Directors

    AVODAH: The Jewish Service Corp

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 2002 — Present (5 years 10 months)

    Michael Hoffman is a member of the national Board of Directors of AVODAH: The Jewish Service Corp. AVODAH is a Jewish service corps that combines front-line, anti-poverty work, Jewish learning, and community building into a one-year program for young adults in New York, Chicago, and Washington DC. Corps members work to change the lives of people in poverty by joining with them to improve their neighborhoods and increase their access to opportunity. AVODAH believes that work for social change and Judaism go hand in hand and we work to strengthen the connections between the two.
