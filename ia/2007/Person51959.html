lokesh sapre’s Summary

Have a professional experience of over 10 years. Worked as a Project Lead, Team Leader, Usability Analyst, Information Designer & Content Writer on numerous projects spanning from web-based applications, client server applications, mobile applications and websites to digital & corporate online branding projects, flash based interactive games, E-learning CD ROMS and print & electronic media. My current responsibility include deep and constant interaction with clients, requirement gathering, leading the Information Architecture, Usability, Content Development and User Experience Design process during projects and coordinating with the Strategy and Technology components to see the project through.

I am also involved in defining Usability benchmarks and validating them through user role-playing, user concern mapping, conducting evaluations, target task achievement, and rigorous navigation testing of any prototype we develop. I approach all this with a single-minded focus: that of the user. This outside-in approach helps me spot and help rectify many of the aberrations most site designs suffer from because of their inside-out logic.

User Research (Heuristic Evaluation, Contextual Inquiry, Focus Group surveys, Etnographic research, User Interviews, etc.), User Testing Activities (User testing in lab environment, prototype/application testing through various methods), Information Architecture, Wire frame Designing, Reporting Standards Development (User research & Usability Testing analysis & documentation, defining templates & data capturing methods for various UT & research activities etc.),Pre-sales, responding to RFPs,etc.
