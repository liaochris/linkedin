Tom Feary’s Summary

In his twelve year career, Tom Feary has earned achievement-based promotions into positions of executive leadership. In his current role as General Manager - Advanced Media, Tom is charged with leading product development and sales in the ever changing landscape that is emerging technologies in television advertising for Charter Communication's St. Louis media operation. In this capacity Tom is responsible for defining the advanced media roadmap to include marketing and sales of long-form advertising in OnDemand, Internet advertising, interactive television, and category verticals leveraging new advertising platforms.

Before joining the Charter team, Tom served as VP of Marketing for Adelphia Communications' media division. There Tom was responsible for defining and managing the company’s marketing and new media development plan, including interactive advertising services. Prior to joining Adelphia in 2000, Tom worked with Clear Channel Communications, executing promotional programs on behalf of multiple station clusters and their advertising clients. As a marketing assistant he supported the marketing and promotional efforts of cable affiliates across the southeast leveraging the stable of brands from MTV Networks.

Tom is a graduate of Auburn University, a husband, and father of two.
