Jay Shepherd’s Summary

CEO of Shepherd Law Group, a Boston firm that's changing the way businesses get employment-law help. Shepherd's unique approach keeps the client in charge:

(1) No hourly billing.
(2) Plain English instead of legalese.
(3) Business first, law second.

Shepherd helps companies manage their most important assets: their people. Clients are encouraged to think of us their "staff" employment counsel. Since we don't bill hourly, clients know that they can call to discuss an issue without the meter running. By calling us early, they can often avoid having their issues turn into crises.

Jay Shepherd’s Specialties:

Employee-lawsuit defense, noncompete litigation, employee-raiding litigation, discrimination and harassment defense, labor relations
