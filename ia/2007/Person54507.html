Mariam Dholkawala’s Summary

Having been in the field of desktop and mobile game development for nearly 6 years, Mariam's experience today has enabled her to pursue her long standing goals of extensive game development and community support. Her core focus has always been the Flash platform and all its supporting middle and backend languages.

She has always enjoyed learning and experimenting with new ideas and pushing a technology's capabilities to the edge. Today Mariam's portfolio includes more than 100 Flash games developed for the desktop and mobiles with 3 award winning games, a strong Community Support background through articles and conferences, and finally managing the Indian Adobe Mobiles and Devices User Group.

Currently Mariam is heading a Studio at Indiagames Ltd, which focuses on all Flash based initiatives - be it mobiles, desktop, set-top boxes or any other gaming platform. However, prior to getting on to Flash at Indiagames, Macromedia's Director was what had her enamoured. So catalogue of Flash games do share some space with very interesting Director games too.
