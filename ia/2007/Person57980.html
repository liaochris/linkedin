David Meyers’s Summary

David Meyers is a management consultant and business manager with over ten years consulting experience and a thirteen-plus year military and marine operations career. He has consulted to a wide variety of clients in the transportation, energy, logistics, infrastructure, commodities, aquaculture, manufacturing and process industries and public sector and executed a turnaround at a diagnostic imaging center. In analytical, client-facing, and case management roles, David is recognized as highly effective in efficiently driving the consultative process to accurate, insightful, and actionable deliverables presented articulately to decision-makers.
