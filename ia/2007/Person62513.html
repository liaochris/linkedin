Nate Hurwitz’s Summary

My biggest hobbies throughout my entire life were involving myself with both video production and music composition. When editing or scoring a work, the three things always come into my mind are Imagination, Rhythm and Continuity.

Since the basis of a good work of art requires the interlocking of these elements, I try to stay consistent with this notion throughout everything I create while also (as you probably think) having fun with it. Throughout my time in college, I have always had a growing interest in every aspect film/video, and in my classes, I have obtained a foundation of standards that I always begin with in each project I do. After applying them, I then take those standards and twist them with my imaginative thoughts running through my head (I promise you they will never conflict bodily harm!).

Through the years I have studied at Canisius College as a dual major while also experiencing projects both inside and outside of Canisius. Places and people I have worked for include Reality Check (Syracuse, N.Y.), The Syracuse Film company, Nottingham High School, Subcat Studios, Canisius College communications dept., and Canisius College Television, and more areas than are listed in this summary.
