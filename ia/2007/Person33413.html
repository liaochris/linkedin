Gabriel Simonet’s Summary

As CMO and Web Development Consultant in SX Networks, Gabriel Simonet specializes in helping businesses to reduce their operational costs and development expenses, by means of Outsourcing their Projects in a lower-cost but high-quality Company. On a day-to-day basis, new Joint-Ventures and Projects are pursued looking for partners interested in expanding their service offerings through SX Network's, while dropping expenditures down.

With a professional background in Advertising, Business and Technology, he focuses on Project and Personnel Management, Business Planning and Innovation, Problem Preventing and Solving (when is already too late to prevent), Traditional and Online Marketing, and Art & IT Consultancy.

Although Spanish is his mother tongue, communication in English and German is very good (written) and could be fluent (spoken), if the chances to use those languages were more common (allowing him to practice and get rid of the rust in his mouth).
