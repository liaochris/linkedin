James H. Nguyen, CIR, CDR’s Summary

Never-ending thirst for excellence, finding the best and brightest with a driving passion for helping individuals grow personally and professionally. Are you keeping your options open to new opportunities?

James H. Nguyen is an entrepreneur and recruitment expert based in Southern California who serves as Vice President of Newport Beach based recruitment firm Synergy Corporate LLC and Synergy Corporate Recruitment. He leverages his strengths in human capital acquisition, aggressive growth strategy and corporate development to spearhead and oversee day to day operations of Synergy. Mr. Nguyen has served internally and as a senior consultant assisting top Fortune 500 organizations such as; KPMG, Ernst & Young, IBM, Cisco Systems, Western Digital, J.P. Getty Trust, Lucent Technologies and has had multiple client engagements with Pre-IPO start-ups from the Silicon Valley to Orange County.

His experience as a “top gun” consultant spans multiple industries with client engagements for some of the largest names from New York to Los Angeles. Under his leadership Mr. Nguyen successfully expanded various sales organizations including Fortune 500 (Global) Fujitsu in developing a national US based sales force and has supported multiple clients in the financial field, including banking, mortgage lending and real estate. Mr. Nguyen has also played key roles in corporate development and strategic growth for various privately held companies during their early development phases, which have now gone public.
