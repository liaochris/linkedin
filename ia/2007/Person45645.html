Jim Weisfield’s Summary

I am a driven Business and Corporate Development executive with a 15 year track record of helping businesses achieve rapid growth. I am a proven leader who has developed and operated successful business units for online software, services, data, and e-commerce companies. I have a passion for strategy, technology and deal making. I am a licensed attorney with expertise in M&A and complex transaction negotiation. I am looking to leverage my background and extensive contacts in a senior Operations, Business & Corporate Development, or in-house Counsel role.

Since 2005, I served as EVP of Global Business Development for Global Market Insite, Inc. (GMI), the 11th fastest growing private company in WA. Reporting to the CEO, I helped grow GMI’s annual sales over 350% in two years. I led all M&A initiatives, partnerships, and database expansion. I vetted over 100 companies for strategic fit with GMI and helped acquire and integrate five companies around the world.

I have extensive international experience throughout Europe and Asia. I have completed over 40 trips to Greater China and built a large network in Asia. I recently helped launch GMI China, GMI Russia, GMI’s vendor base in Eastern Europe, and GMI’s services branch in India. I developed international partnerships including hundreds of channel, vendor and media partners.

Prior to GMI, I held several senior operating positions with both public and private companies. My background includes Business, Corporate and Product Development expertise; P&L responsibility for multi-million dollar sales divisions; reorganization and restructuring experience; and top leadership positions as the President and CEO of my own. In addition to serving as EVP of Business Development for an e-commerce company, I have served as General Counsel for three companies, including one public company, and am a skilled corporate attorney specializing in M&A, finance, and large strategic transactions.
