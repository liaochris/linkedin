Frank Birkenstock’s Summary

Management consultant specialized in corporate strategy, marketing and strategy based transformation for diverse industries with a particular focus on the telecommunication, internet and public sector industry.
Examples of consulting experience include Corporate Strategy, War Gaming, Market Entry Strategy, Marketing Plan, Customer Care Strategy, Business Plan, Business Redesign, Outsourcing Strategy, Cost Optimization, Device Platform Strategy and Device Portfolio Strategy for international companies with a main focus on telecommunication operators in different countries (incl. UK, Germany, Italy, Spain, Sweden, Oman, Dubai, Japan).
Responsibilities include programme management; business planning; operating model design; developing new strategies and propositions; planning, structuring and conducting analyses (incl. detailed business cases, NPV, market and competitor analysis); managing small teams; managing the client on a daily-basis; and presenting the results.
