Manuela Taboada’s Summary

Graphic designer, with a background in corporate and regional branding, image and identity; marketing; and digital interfaces. Substantial experience in lecturing, planning and organising courses. Good experience in managing design teams and projects. Research focus now on regional planning for sustainable tourism through involving local communities in a transdisciplinar and collaborative design process. Interested in academic work (research and lecturing, specially in design, tourism and collaborative planning areas) and perhaps in advisory work for local governments and communities on ways of designing / planning for more sustainable tourism activity in their places.
