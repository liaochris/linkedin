Tim Borden’s Summary

Tim Borden has more than 20 years experience in the hospitality and marketing businesses, as well as substantial advertising agency credentials.

Since 1990, Borden Enterprises, Inc. has provided marketing and development services to the hospitality industry and the general market, including complete concept development, business plans, real estate development, fund raising, music and entertainment services, sponsorship programs, marketing, advertising, promotions and public relations services.

Raising sales through innovative marketing and developing new revenue streams is the specialty of the house at Borden Enterprises. Our foundation is in the food and beverage service business but we serve all industries.

Tim Borden’s Specialties:

Expert in marketing, advertising, promotions and PR, hospitality industry development/project management, fund raising, sponsor solicitation and program execution, music and entertainment, creation of new/enhanced revenue streams including ATM/Credit Card Processing/Financial revenue optimization and cost-cutting techniques.
