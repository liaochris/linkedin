Leonardo Zangrando’s Summary

Owner/Managing Partner at Longitude, a firm providing consulting services on logistics operations and specializing in Port/Maritime and Container Terminal Acquisition, Investment and Restructuring projects.
Chartered Naval Engineer with 16 years experience in the Maritime sector, and eight years experience in business consulting.
Projects in container terminal acquisition, new project development drafting for container terminals, port terminals, short-sea-shipping services, superyacht marinas, logistics facilities, RFID applications.
Prior positions have included roles in project management and construction management in shipyards; new business development, salesforce strategy, licensing deals, in various industries.
MEng in Naval Architecture and Mechanical Engineering from the University of Trieste, Italy (1991) and MBA from IESE Business School in Barcelona, Spain (1999). Chartered Engineer since 1991 and Chartered Naval Engineer since 1992.

Project Portfolio
* Managed the acquisition and restructuring project of a port container terminal, doubling its total throughput capacity to support expansion of geographic market.
Direct responsibility on investments totalling € 8M, financing for € 4.7M and strategic decisions on equipments worth € 22M.
* Designed terminal development strategy and integrated operating model for the acquisition of a 2.5M TEU container terminal in the Mediterranean for the worldwide leader in container terminal management.
* Managed the introduction of innovative design practices in ship hull design for production cost optimisation

"Spot" and "System" projects in Logistics + China Import / Export / Investment projects. (expanded info on www.longitude-mc.com)

Available for interim positions in the Port/Maritime industry for evaluation, acquisition, restructuring of Container Terminals, Port Terminals, Yacht Shipyards, Yacht Marinas around the globe.
