Mike O'Neil (moneil@IntegratedAlliances.com)’s Summary

Feel free to connect to me (Mike O'Neil) at moneil@integratedalliances.com. I established a company entitled Integrated Alliances in 2003. It is a business networking association that puts on events and hosts LinkedIn activities.

Why I am here on LinkedIn:

• I am looking to expand our LinkedIn Hands-on Workshops and LinkedIn Live! business networking events into other geographies. To do so, I am looking for local Executive Directors to run the local operations in other cities.
• I am looking for opportunities to help other firms with their channel and/or marketing programs on a contract, project or part-time basis.
• I am also seeking opportunities to help other organizations implement CRM-type IT systems to run their organizations - membership, newsletters, registrations, events.

I developed the “IA LinkedIn Hands-on Workshops" (brand and curriculum) in early 2006 and have taught the monthly workshops to over 400 attendees. LinkedIn Corp. sponsored the first few workshops to help get them started. This is perhaps the only hands-on LinkedIn classroom education anywhere in the world.

Integrated Alliances also provides business development and channel development services via networking events that we conduct at least once per month in the greater Denver area. IA holds monthly events in Denver and Boulder CO. They are happy hour type events.

Several times a year IA also holds very large business networking events attracting as many as 500 B2B and technology professionals.

Recently, I was selected as the Technology Editor for The National NetWorker, a nationwide business networking publication.

In the past I have been a Sales Engineer (15 years) in a wide cross section of technology.

My most current areas of expertise include technical marketing, newsletter marketing, channel development, channel marketing, strategic planning, product development, speaking, training.
