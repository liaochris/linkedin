Pamela Ungvarsky’s Summary

Leading international management consultant for ePayments, eBanking and Security. Specialised in formulating and executing strategic global initiatives to increase profits, reduce operating expense, and strengthen customer relationships.

Bring to clients over 25 years of experience as an executive and consultant in Strategy, Marketing, and Business Development. Also have held a variety of instrumental interim management roles for my clients, ensuring them market leadership and profitable revenue streams worldwide.

Extensive expertise includes building and managing results oriented teams, both in large and small organisations.

Based in Paris since 1986, practice is focused on a global level. With specific concentration on both the European and North American markets, combines a world-wide view with local market expertise and execution.

Key strength is my multi-industry knowledge and unique business perspective, having held senior positions in the Technology, Financial and Retail industries.

Also bring strong consumer marketing expertise, along with a comprehensive understanding of complex business, technology, and infrastructure issues.

Clients include major international players within the payments industry, among them service providers, card branded networks, solution providers, chip card suppliers, and device manufacturers.
