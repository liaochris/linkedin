Amit Anand(esoteric.arts@gmail.com)’s Summary

I'm currently pursuing my passion - Art! I own a label by the name of
- Esoteric Arts India.

I love making paintings primarily on esoteric subjects..I paint the colors of esotericism and use practical sciences like Feng Shui, Tarot and Reiki as my prime themes.

I use mainly acrylic on canvas but a true work of art is always of a mixed medium and that is exactly what i specialise in!

I'm dwelling into other mediums like Pottery, Glass and the likes!

The whole idea of Esoteric Arts is to infuse positive, vibrant and powerful energy by use of sacred symbols in Feng Shui, Tarot, Reiki and other esoteric sciences that create a positive aura around an individual thereby bringing in, untold enlightenment and enhancement in an individual's dormant sphere of life.

It brings positivity.. in our personal and professional sphere..be it Work, Home, Family, Relationships, Love, Success, Wealth etc.. and the list goes on.

The Esoteric Arts uses art to enhance dormant sectors in your life pertaining to the above mentioned spheres of existence.

There are many off-the-shelf products available in the market today that claim to enhance these spheres in your life, however, mass production and commercialisation of these products render them lifeless and negative. They create an ennui in our lives instead of improving it.

Esoteric Arts comes to your rescue! The very concept of Esoteric Arts is to create and individual space rather than a mass.

Every painting of Esoteric Arts is hand made, sans mass production and caters to an individual's need and desire.

By use of powerful Feng Shui, Tarot and Reiki symbols that have untapped potential and power to bring about a monumental change in your life for the better.

My paintings and art objects bring about this power. All paintings are carefully researched as per an individual's chart and hence the best remedy is made available!

-Jimmie
