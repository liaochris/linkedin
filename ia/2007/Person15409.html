Charlie O'Donnell’s Summary

Charlie is currently the Director of Consumer Products for Oddcast, a unique New York City company whose avatar platform is well positioned at the center of user generated content, interactive advertising, and the personalization of the web. His role is to help bring Oddcast's technology, already successful with brands and small businesses, to consumers, by creating a compelling user experience and viable business model.

Previously an analyst for Union Square Ventures, the New York City based venture capital fund that invested in Oddcast, Charlie focused on early stage investments in technology enabled service businesses. He helped evaluate investment opportunities and was a member of the investment staff during the fund's investments in del.icio.us, Indeed.com, Feedburner, BugLabs, and Etsy, as well as Oddcast.

Prior to joining Union Square Ventures, he spent four years at the General Motors pension fund’s $6 billion private markets group, working on partnership and direct investments in venture capital, leveraged buyout and private debt. At GM, he worked extensively on venture capital partnerships focusing on emerging technology as well as private equity investments in the consumer sector.

In addition to his professional accomplishments, Charlie has been extensively involved in developing innovative new approaches to career education and development. He is on the board of NYSSA’s SEMI program, a unique educational opportunity given to local college students interested in finance. He also helped develop Fordham’s first Young Alumni Mentoring Program and recruited most of the 52 recently graduated alumni that took part in the program.

A Brooklyn native, Charlie received a B.S. in Finance from Fordham University summa cum laude. He is an active volunteer at the Downtown Boathouse and the co-Chair of the Fordham Young Alumni Committee. He can also be found blogging at www.thisisgoingtobebig.com.
