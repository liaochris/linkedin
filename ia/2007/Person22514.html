Deb Biggar’s Summary

I am a consultant available for project-based, freelance usability studies, user experience design recommendations, and low and high fidelity prototypes for ecommerce web and software applications.

I have 7 years experience in user experience design for interactive, web-based applications. I completed my Master of Science in Human Factors in Information Design in May 2006. I bring enthusiasm, dedication and clarity to project-based, contract, usability or information architecture work.

I also have experience in designing for the automotive user experience.

Deb Biggar’s Specialties:

Globalization/localization, accessibility for users with disabilities, user experience design for ecommerce/financial web applications and automotive in-dash multi-media systems.
