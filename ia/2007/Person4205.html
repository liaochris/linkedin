Amanda Patterson’s Summary

Nominated for SA Woman of the Year 2006
Nominated for Mondi awards for articles

The Power of Words is The Company's & The Foundation’s mantra.
What is not written down or recorded is lost forever.
We embrace the power of the individual, the power of thoughts and the power of words.
Communication is essential to the success of South Africa.


Who are we?

1. A company that teaches people to write anything - from a poem to a tender, to a press release to a song to a novel.
32 of our graduates have become published authors.
30 more have published articles, become columnists, written for the screen & made a career out of words.

2. A trust dedicated to promoting a positive future for every adult and child in South Africa.
A trust that understands that education is vital for the realisation of this future.
A trust dedicated to empowering South Africans through learning to write.
A trust dedicated to empowering South Africans through learning to to read.
A trust born out of the belief of the Power of One. One word, one thought, one idea, one individual.
A trust that believes in, and champions, the power of words.
