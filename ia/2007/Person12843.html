Brian Benitez’s Summary

I have a broad array of business experience at the intersection of online and traditional media. I've been an audio and video production engineer for over 16 years, and over that time have developed solid project management and cross-functional systems development skills. I've been involved in creating broadband digital media since its inception, and I am well-versed at successfully integrating complex business, editorial, and technology objectives. I excel at, and enjoy, taking projects from the research and development phase through to deployment of new technology and business processes.

Brian Benitez’s Specialties:
