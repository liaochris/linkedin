Rick Johnston, CAE’s Summary

Rick is a Senior Web Strategist for Ironworks Consulting in Richmond (Glen Allen), Virginia. He is also the Team Leader for Ironworks' association group practice. View his association Weblog at www.rjohnston.org. Rick served in an executive capacity with the American Diabetes Association from 1991 to June 2005, most recently as National Vice President, Constituent Relations with oversight of all Web and Call Center operations. With more than 25 years of management experience in nonprofit organizations, he has worked for the American Lung Association, Big Brothers Big Sisters of America and the Natural Resources Council of Maine. The American Society of Association Executives has awarded him the prestigious Certified Association Executive (CAE) certification. He has also served in volunteer leadership roles with several charitable organizations including President of the Board of Directors for the following: New England Health Education Association, Bangor-Brewer TB & Health Association, Big Brothers Big Sisters of Kennebec Valley and the Old Bridge Estates Community Association. Rick is often asked to present at conferences and most recently conducted at workshop at the 2006 DigitalNow Conference at Walt Disney World. Since joining Ironworks in July 2005, Rick has worked on Web strategy with such clients as the American Cancer Society, American Collge of Physicians, American Institute of Architects, National Association of Convenience Stores, Clearpoint Financial Solutions, Community Health Charities, Discovery Education and the Veterans Health Administration.
