Guy Laliberté’s Summary

Guy has over 16 years of experience in high-tech industries. In 2006, Guy joined Rheinmetall Canada as Program Manager for the Land Defence Systems Division. In 2004-2005, as VP of Operations at Embrase Communications, Guy supervised all projects and implemented the internal processes required for a rapid growth. At Mindready Solutions, he was Director of the Embedded Systems Division in 2003-2004 where he dedicated his efforts and expertise in providing day-to-day leadership across all customer facing functions including sales, marketing, professional services, operations and customer support. Also at Mindready, from 1999 to 2002, Guy was Director of the Project Management Office, which he implemented. From 1991 to 1999, he was project manager at ATS Aerospace and electronic engineer at Decatron. Guy earned a Bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering from the Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal, a Master's Degree in Science from the Universite de Technologie de Compiegne (France) and an MBA from HEC of Montreal. Guy is a member of the Quebec's Professional Order of Engineering (OIQ).
