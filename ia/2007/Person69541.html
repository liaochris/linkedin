Maureen McCann, Career Coach’s Summary

Maureen McCann is Owner and Chief Career Consultant for ProMotion Career Solutions. She is the Employment Coordinator at Experica, the practice firm, an employment program that enjoys an 85% success rate in moving unemployed individuals into their chosen field; as a result of the one-on-one coaching Maureen does with the participants.

Maureen has been called the "ultimate professional in all her endeavors from voluntary contributions in the community to her role as a career professional" by some of Canada's leading resume writers and career professionals.

Most recently, Maureen received the Career Professionals of Canada (CPC) Award of Excellence for her continued contribution to her clients and her profession.

Maureen McCann, Career Coach’s Specialties:

I work with individuals who have been unsuccessful in finding their ideal employment situation and employees who struggle to get promoted within their existing field.

I help them to understand what employers want and I introduce them to concepts, exercises and skills that will increase their chances for meaningful and lasting employment.

"My ideal clients are people on the move - willing to make a change in their life and use technology to their advantage."
