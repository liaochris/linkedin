Brandon Cardone’s Summary

Card One Concepts was founded in 1997 by Brandon and Michelle Cardone. With over 15 years combined experience in graphic design, web design, technology and marketing. We are a relatively small design company that enjoys the benefits that come with our size. As our client base grows from around the corner to around the world, our skills, talent, and love for what we do grows as well.

Our philosophy is simple! First impressions are everything.

Our extensive graphic and web design skills, along with our innovative marketing strategies, drive our goal to provide clients with the highest standards. Aside from eye catching graphic and web designs, we work hard to integrate key components to enhance your business success: your company's visual branding, your marketing objectives and goals, high quality designs, state of the art technology, the latest trends in search engine optimization, and most importantly - at an affordable investment to you. Because when it really comes down to it, if a customer can't find you online or notice your promotional message in a matter of seconds, all the bells and whistles and eye candy won't matter in the least.
