MARY DUFFY’s Education

  • State University of New York Empire State College

    M.L.A, Aging Baby Boomer Women, 2006 — 2009 (expected)

    This self-designed program encompasses Modern Feminism and Ageism, Advertising and its seeming allergy to the 50+ market which controls 70% of US Money, 50+ Consumerism Trends, the Popular History of the Boomers, 1945-Present, Creative Aging, Loss of Growing Older, Death, Dying and Grief, The Psychology of Happiness and a Thesis , which is a book on looking and feeling good for Boomer age women. It is a one of a kind program, intellectually stimulating and requires interpolation of research as very little has been done in this field. it is necessary to take what is there and rationally create a new body of work that stands up to scholastic interdisciplinary critical inquiry on the graduate level. I Hope to proceed to a doctorate in further women's studies of the 20th/21st Century, in order to have more knowledge with which to continue serving the emotional and psychological needs and interests of women.

    Activities and Societies:
    Member ASA, Boomer Project, and other Boomer and Aging related organizations.

  • Mount Holyoke College

    B.A,, Speech and Theatre Major, Art Minor, 1962 — 1966

    Most people thought I chose very impractical fields of studies, but I am amazed, in retrospect, how much one knows oneself at 17.
