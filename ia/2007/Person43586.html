Jon Beverley’s Summary

Jon is an entrepreneur and general manager with 12 years experience in business creation, expansion, financing, M&A and business development. Before his role at Fronde Anywhere, Jon was a successful interim senior exec for several New Zealand software start-ups.

Prior to arriving in New Zealand, Jon co-founded London-based Scan Mobile Ltd, a mobile commerce startup, in 1999, and was instrumental in securing financing of USD $10MM from idealab, Investor Growth Capital and Primedia Ventures. As CEO/COO for four years, Jon personally won GBP 1.2MM of contracts from clients such as Orange, Vodafone, Argos Retail Group and HSBC. Other achievements included overseeing investor relations with all three VCs, running the acquisition of Mobile.Channel Network SA of Switzerland, managing Scan’s international growth strategy, including direct supervision of the North American office for 18 months, and finally negotiating a successful merger with competitor Digital Rum Ltd. Prior to this, Jon built and ran his own new media consultancy, which he sold in April 1999, and wrote extensively on new media topics for Financial Times Media & Telecoms.

• Experienced entrepreneurial business leader and general manager, with skills in M&A, corporate finance, business development, project management, team building, change management and investor relations.
• Twelve years start-up experience, including four in a VC-funded mobile software company, on behalf of which I was instrumental in raising $10MM. Specialist in business-building from concept to execution.
• Sold an incubated business to a US VC, realising 480% profits on the initial outlay.
• Strong relationships in the Mobile Network Operator and related sectors globally, with a deep understanding of the industry.
• Excellent understanding of software development, mobile commerce, retail & payment/billing sectors.
• International experience including senior roles in New York, New Zealand & Geneva.
