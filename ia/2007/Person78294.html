Serge Manoukian’s Summary

I'm an award winning Creative Director with eleven years of experience in conception and design for the digital marketing industry, including six years in management and mentoring.

As well as creating world-class websites for Fortune 500 clients, I use my skills to conceive and design interactive campaigns, corporate brand identities, viral marketing solutions, rich-media content, CD-ROMs, interactive games, e-CRM and limited edition toys!
