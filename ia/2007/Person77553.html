Scott Webster’s Summary

The Fruit Company®

Started The Fruit Company® in 1999 and have grown the company to 8.5 million in sales and over 40 (off season) 160 (peak season) employees.

Started a 501c3, The Fruit Foundation, that has now implemented a 2k sq. ft. museum on the premise to showcase the history of the fruit industry in the Hood River Valley.

Instrumental in bringing the Historic Mt. Hood Railroad, a combined partnership with our business and the museum to create a tourist destination for the railroad’s 65,000 riders per year.

Successfully negotiated very large contracts with FedEx, UPS, 1800Flowers, Jones New York Apparel Group, and kind.

Endorsed by Oprah Winfrey. The Fruit Company® made the “O” list. Our products were featured on the front cover of the “O” Magazine’s December 2003 issue of her Holiday Gift Guide.
