Bill Conlon’s Summary

Bill Conlon is an accomplished engineer with experience in energy systems, electric power and cogeneration, systems optimization, real-time controls, telecommunications and software. He has broad technical competence, demonstrated by publications and patents in multiple disciplines, and is comfortable in both corporations and startups. An experienced manager of people and projects, and recognized for leadership and communication skills, Bill is a degreed Nuclear Engineer and a licensed Professional Engineer (Mechanical) in the State of California.

Bill Conlon’s Specialties:

Fluid and thermal systems engineering. Instrument & Control Systems, Nuclear Engineering, Gas Turbine Cogeneration, Water treatment, Project Management

Software architecture and engineering for embedded systems and web applications.
