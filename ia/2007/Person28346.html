Evelyn So’s Summary

I am a digital veteran who helps companies deliver products and services that customers notice, love, buy and keep. I expand customer experience by combining 5 disciplines: Human Computer Interaction/Usability, Social Psychology, Business Practices, Marketing, and Consumer Behaviours.
