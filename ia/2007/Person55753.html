Martin Neumann’s Summary

Martin has a broad range of experiences in all facets of journalism, primarily in the fields of small business, entrepreneurship, internet business and home-based business.

Today his focus is on new media, especially via the blogging platform to produce quality, community-engaging websites, with a news/opinion flavour.

He has written numerous articles, columns and blog posts over the past 10 years that have appeared in numerous media outlets.

In 1998, Martin established the Australian Home Office Alliance - an association bringing together hundreds of home-based business owners across Australia.

Martin has also vast experience in obtaining and managing high-level sponsorship working with companies such as Microsoft Australia, Verbatim, Oricom, CommSec, Quicken Australia, Konica and numerous smaller and online businesses.

He currently writes a weekly column at the popular Blog Herald and writes at his own column - The Blog Columnist.

He has recently sold the intellectual assets of his most recent venture - ePublishingDaily, which has enabled him to focus on his true love: setting up and running a successful online publishing firm

This venture - KickstartPress.com - is currently in soft launch.
