Michelle Stimpson’s Summary

Overwhelmed? Out-of-Balance? Out-of-Focus?

Working together with me, you will:

1) Quiet the distractions to get clear on "Who am I?" and "What do I want?"

2) Look objectively at what's working in your life and what's not; identify where you're getting stuck and release old habits and patterns.

3) Identify priorities and develop a purposeful and meaningful action plan with realistic goals that lead to healthy, positive change.

With coaching--stay focused, on-track, and accountable so that you finally do live the life of your dreams! Get out of the craziness and busyness of life...back to joy, and the essence of who you really are.

In addition to individual coaching, I lead "Life Balance & Time Management" workshops/presentations for business and community groups and professional associations.

Michelle Stimpson’s Specialties:
