Jack Rusher’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur and technological innovator. I've built a number of filesystems and databases, including a clustered filesystems, two from-scratch databases (one of which was a graph-structured semantic web store), and a best of breed high availability clustering application within the last seven years.

Jack Rusher’s Specialties:

An ability to use mathematics and computer science to solve practical problems.
A deep understanding of OS internals, especially UNIX-based systems, and skill at applying that knowledge to storage and network problems.
Various semantic web technologies.
Many programming languages. My favorites have historically been C (or a minimal subset of C++) and LISP; lately I'm softening to Ruby's charms.
