Guenter Leitold’s Summary

I want to use my skills to support others, create and enjoy good business relationships and source investment opportunities in the leveraged finance and distressed debt area. I look at public debt globally and private debt situations in the german speaking and CEE countries. We provide proprietary capital and advice across the full spectrum of leveraged finance products including senior debt, mezzanine, high yield bonds, PIK and equity. We make investments on a proprietary basis but also co-invest and/or share co-investment opportunities with other credit oriented and equity investors. We are active in secondary trading of high yield bonds, stressed or distressed loans and seasoned CDO's.
