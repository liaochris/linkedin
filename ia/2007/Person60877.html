Molly Stenhouse’s Summary

As a web editor at Yahoo!, Molly developed user standards and moderated content for Yahoo! Answers, a social networking site. She created web content for The Spark blog and employed metrics to illustrate which blog topics appeal to readers on which days of the week. She also applied editorial standards to Yahoo! properties such as Yahoo! Travel, Local, and Directory.

Her role as a Yahoo! Web editor honed her attention to detail and gave valuable insights into user search behavior and search technology.

After Yahoo!, Molly founded Spark Marketing. Spark has worked with small businesses to manage and develop marketing strategy, web sites, and produce unforgettable design and branding packages. Spark also produced and piloted an online video advertising program to promote Palo Alto businesses for Embarcadero Publishing (publisher of The Palo Alto Weekly and The Mountain View Voice). These unique commercials produced by Spark Marketing can be seen at Palo Alto Online starting 2/08.

Molly loves working with people, has a passion for helping solve problems, and brings creative flair to everything she produces. Her enthusiasm, web experience, and highly developed communication skills can help your business succeed.
