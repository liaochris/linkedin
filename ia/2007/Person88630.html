Vanessa Tan’s Summary

Officially a 'Senior Executive', I work more as a New Media consultant, advising internal clients of the most suitable channels for their programmes. I manage numerous projects. I write and repurpose offline content for use on the web. I can design websites and e-mail newsletters, especially in emergencies. I still provide creative direction for all design projects, bearing in mind usability together with aesthetics and with an increasing focus on an adherence to Web Standards.

Vanessa Tan’s Specialties:

New media, specifically blogging, podcasting, Web 2.0, social networking, usability design, writing for the web, accessibility, web standards, integrated marketing, online branding (based on experience working 2 years in a brand consultancy). Interested in expanding knowledge on virtual communities, public relations, media relations.
