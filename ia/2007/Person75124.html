Rusty Lindquist’s Summary

May 2005 – Present
VP Agent and Broker Products / Market, a la mode, Inc.

Responsible for overall strategic direction, development, and profitability of the Agent and Broker markets within a la mode. My primary job functions include selecting target market segments, understanding the needs of those individuals, determining profitable solutions for those needs, designing and creating the UI and functional specifications for those solutions, managing the development, QA, and project process to build those solutions, and then coordinating release efforts with marketing, sales, documentation writers, and support.

March 2003 – May 2005
Product Line Manager, Agent Products, a la mode, Inc.

Managed the introduction of this new market for the company. Determined a narrow focus for the initial product offering that our core competencies lended themselves well to, designed the features of that offering, and managed the development, release, maintenance, and support for these products.

Feb 2002 – March 2003
Product Manager, Appraiser XSites, a la mode, Inc.

Since its beginnings, a la mode, Inc. (based in Oklahoma City) has focused on desktop software development. Early in 2002 it was decided we should expand our services to include web-based software development. I was hired as a product manager to build a web-based product and development team in our new Salt Lake City office. After understanding the strategic direction and vision for this new venture, I designed and managed the development and release of our first online business software.

June 2001 – Feb 2002
Director, Rapid Prototype Team, Franklin Covey

Here I was involved with the corporate product innovation team. My specific team was responsible for building interactive prototypes of new product ideas.

May 2000 – June 2001
User Interface Designer, FranklinPlanner.com - Franklin Covey
