John Lee’s Summary

What you have before you is a marketing and business development executive with 13 years of experience in gaming, wireless and digital media.

Stints at THQ, Bethesda Softworks and Eidos - launched some of the biggest gaming brands, including Star Wars, Tomb Raider, The Elder Scrolls, WWE, SpongeBob, Super Bomberman and yes, even Hello Kitty! Believe in the power of kitty.

Also have extensive experience in wireless and digital media, including co-founding a venture which was successfully acquired by a public company in a multi-million dollar deal.

Now for the hardcore part. I created my first video game when I was in 5th grade. Was a finalist in the Sega National Collegiate Tournament when I was in college. Completed over 1000 games to date. Owned over 15 video game systems to date. And I can beat the whole Super Mario Brothers game in under 7 min and 30 sec. And I have a level 70 Hunter in World of Warcraft. Digital media is my life.
