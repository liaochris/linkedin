Paul Hood’s Summary

I'm a bridge-builder between the 'old' and 'new' ways of doing business in today's internet-enabled world.

I've worked almost exclusively with online projects since 1999, after gaining a solid foundation of the media sector through a traditional media sales background. I've gained vast experience of the fascinating internet and mobile mediums and am tremendously passionate about the compelling advertising opportunities they can offer.

As Head of Online for Emap Automotive, I have developed two successful large-scale web propositions and led the strategy for integration of the print and web sales teams.

During the last 18 months I have also provided consultancy for several of the UK's major motor manufacturers to help them adapt their businesses to the rapidly changing car-buying process.

Paul Hood’s Specialties:

Understanding the needs of today's consumer in relation to their lifestyle & associated purchasing decisions, and providing creative and effective marketing solutions to suit, across all media platforms. Experienced online brand-builder.
