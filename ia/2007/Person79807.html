ABOUT ME-

i am * Digital Sherpa

Providing consulting to global award winning agencies and brands on "Integrated Media" campaigns and events. High-end rich media + Advanced Interactive + Mobile Strategies in relation to marketing programmes and consumer trends / data acquisition.

Currently acting as Strategic Development Consultant to leading agencies and e-companies in Toronto & North America.

HISTORY - Having worked on both sides of the Atlantic as a principal evangelist from the early flash days, and being involved in interactive initiatives as far apart as cutting edge ad campaigns, web applications, mobile campaigns to Video/Audio content.

As former Executive Director & Ambassador of FITO & FITC (formerly FlashintheCan) I was instrumental in progressing FITO to become the world's largest Adobe (formerly Macromedia) user group and FITC to become a globally recognized industry event.
____

AIM - My goal is now to help other events and allow interactive media to overtake traditional media by proving its worth and versatility and by also changing the antediluvian mindset of brands / advertising agencies / media moguls despite consumers already proving they prefer digital media advertising.
