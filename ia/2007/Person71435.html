Rhonda DeMuth’s Summary

Ms. DeMuth has over twenty seven years of experience with several leading national and regional consulting firms as well as working in industry as a manager and a technologist. Her experience spans work in the manufacturing, distribution, electric utilities, banking and insurance industries. Her primary application focus is with automation of business processes across manufacturing and distribution industries.

Rhonda DeMuth’s Experience

  • CEO

    TDCI Inc

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 1997 — Present (10 years 6 months)

    Ms. DeMuth became president of TDCI in July 1997, and in 2002 she continued her leadership position as CEO and Chairman of the Board at TDCI. TDCI is a solutions provider dedicated to helping manufacturers and distributors leverage the principles and technologies of enterprise resource planning, commerce and engineering solutions.

    Ms DeMuth is responsible for refining TDCI’s vision and business strategies and positioning TDCI for continued growth. Through her leadership, annual revenues have grown from $2 Million to $12 Million, staff size has increased from 27 to 90 and number of customers has increased from 50 to over 320 over the past five years. Much of this growth is attributed to the MAC-PAC ERP Support contract and TDCI’s new BuyDesign software solution, a product configurator-based selling solution for manufacturers of make-to-order products.
