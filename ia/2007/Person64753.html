Marcio Okabe’s Summary

In 2000/2001 I was director of a digital agency, coordinating a team of webdesigners and programmers. In that time we developed projects for Credicard, OgilvyInteractive, Finasa and Grupo Schahin.

I founded Konfide in 2002 with focus in consultancy for web projects and online marketing.

In 2004/2005, working in Telefonica I have the opportunity to coordinate an corporate portal project, where Vignette was chosen as plataform. Due the complexity of its implementation I need to negotiate with many areas inside Telefonica to make it works.

In 2005, returning to Konfide, I have focused in online marketing (AdWords and Optimization), business analysis and online project management (using an online tool: www.aceproject.com).

Expert in Joomla!, one of the best CMS (content management system).

In Konfide's website there's many articles about web tendencies, site development and OpenSource solutions.

Marcio Okabe’s Specialties:
