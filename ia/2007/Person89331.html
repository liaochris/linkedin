Vinay Kumar’s Summary

Over 18 years of industry experience in the IT/Internet industry with 9 years at Microsoft Corporation in a variety of general management roles in Redmond USA with global responsibility. Educational background includes an MBA from Wharton Business School and undergrad in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, India.

Recently moved to New Delhi, India to head up Corporate Strategy Group for Times Internet Ltd, part of Times of India Group. Times Internet Ltd venture focuses on wireless and Internet products / services. Times Internet Ltd (TIL) is the digital venture of India’s largest media house – The Times of India Group. The primary businesses for TIL are online portals (indiatimes, timesofindia.com, economictimes.com), mobile value added services (e.g. music, games, mobile portal), rich media products (e.g. games, multimedia content), e-commerce and small business online solutions. Reporting to the CEO and MD as part of the Executive Management Team, I am responsible for the following.

Strategic Alliances/Investments: Driving TIL’s strategic alliances and investments across all businesses both domestically as well as globally.

Emerging Business/P&L: Incubating new businesses in the company such as the small business and education sectors with overall sales, operations and P&L responsibility.

Product Management: Responsible for overall product strategy and management for the company across all businesses.
