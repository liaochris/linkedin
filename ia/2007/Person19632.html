Dale Williams’s Summary

I am an Executive Coach and Business MacGyver* retired from corporate life and currently on sabatical actively exploring new opportunities.

My future work interests lie in three areas:
1. Support for entrepreneurial opportunities that require investment.
2. Consulting opportunities requiring an accountable person to deliver well-defined business outcomes.
3. Executive coaching.

My previous roles included heading up Standard Chartered's Retail Bank in South Africa and as an entrepreneur who with my partners sold our company at the height of the 1990's technology boom.

I have been running an Executive Coaching Practice since 2002 focused on leaders within large corporations and entreprenuers facing the challenges of their own business. I completed my (MA) in Executive Coaching through Middlesex University in 2005.

Clem Sunter [Chairman of Anglo American Chairman's Fund] noted that my thesis [see below] is "a unique application of the methodology of Scenario Planning; but the idea of looking at the future through a prism of possibilities is as relevant to an individual as to a business."

I have a strong belief that organisations are only as good as the people that they nurture. Healthy and functional relationships within an organisation are key to its financial success.

My coaching and consulting work with companies combine practical successes in this area with my academic learning.

When consulting I believe that the only way my assignment can be successful is to have direct accountability for well defined business critical deliverables. Writing a report or presenting a powerpoint doesn't cut it nor adds value to the business.

I am keen to take on challenges with
1. new businesses,
2. organisations going through change and
3. with individuals who believe they would benefit from coaching.

* Urban Dictionary Definition of MacGyver: "To use ingenuity to fix or remedy a problem using only the tools available at hand."

Dale Williams’s Specialties:

Solving tough management problems, entrepreneurial business people, leadership, technology, marketing, negotiation, coaching and mentoring.
