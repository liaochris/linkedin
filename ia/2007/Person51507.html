Liron Tocker’s Summary

I am a graphic designer with 10+ years of experience in the web design field and 4 years of experience in the print, brand, textile and identity design fields. I have experience with working and designing on all platforms (Macintosh, Windows, Linux) and am especially enthusiastic to work on multilingual projects. I am fluent in both English and Hebrew as mother tongues and have earned the "Zertifikat Deutsch" language certificate from the state of Germany for German as a foreign language (3 years of total German studies). I enjoy working in groups and thrive in teamwork.

I have experience in bringing visuals ready to print according to industry printing standards, including magazine and advertising post processing.

I pride myself in designing and coding websites which work on all standards-compliant browsers, such as Internet Explorer, Firefox and Safari.

I hope to develop and learn from design challenges and expand into different design fields. I enjoy the experience of artistic creation and love to learn new skills.
