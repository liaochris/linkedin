Rich Swier’s Summary

Rich Swier is the founder and managing partner of Startup Florida Ventures and is involved as a founder or director in numerous other ventures.

Startup Florida is the first managed angel fund and venture creation company in the State of Florida. Founded in 2002, Startup Florida has made helped launch and made investments in a diverse portfolio of companies in information technology, communications, internet/media and life sciences.

Prior to Startup Florida, Rich founded numerous successful companies. In 1994 Rich founded Sarasota Online, one of the first Internet Service Providers and Online City Guides in Florida. Sarasota Online was acquired by Comcast Cable (CMCSA) as the prelude to a national rollout of its cable-modem network. In 1997, Rich founded Backsoft Corporation, which was voted Top 100 companies to watch by Computerworld (acquired by Intech in 2001).

Rich lives in Sarasota, Florida and holds a BS from University of Florida in Mathematics and Computer Sciences. Rich currently serves on the board of Yokel, MobileSecure and FastPitch Networking.
