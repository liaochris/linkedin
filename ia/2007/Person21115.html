Dave Shebat’s Summary

Over twenty years of documented success in leading multi-disciplinary teams in the development, deployment and operation of information systems.

Recent initiatives include AOL's re-engineered web portal, various AOL client launches, Map Quest, City Guide, Moviefone, AOL Search, Local Search, Yellow Pages, AOL Video & Radio, and the many AOL Entertainment & Information channel websites. Previous efforts include NASA's Space Station Integration Support Contractor, DOD aquisitions, Intelligence Community programs (Top Secret Clearances) and Mortgage Banking application development.
