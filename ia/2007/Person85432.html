Thomas Hutton’s Summary

My initial function has been building and maintaining IT Infrastructure for a rapidly growing RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing) business in Buenos Aires. Since I began here in May, 2006 we have grown from 15 employees to more than 120, with an additional office in Montevideo, Uruguay. We are currently in the process of rapid expansion with two new offices coming online in Europe and Asia in the next three to six months. My role has grown into management of a staff of 5 and we are now implementing "enterprise class" solutions such as LDAP, and adding redundancy and fail-over capacity.

As for career goals I am thrilled with the excitement of a growing company and the challenges along the way. I have the deepest respect for, and opportunity to learn from the management team above me here. I love being a part of a growing business and having been responsible for building the underlying IT infrastructure.
