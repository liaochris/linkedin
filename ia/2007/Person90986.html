Will Burns’s Summary

I am an experienced Recruitment Consultant.

I come from a legal background, and seek to provide that same quality of service in the recruitment industry, one with an uncertain reputation.

I believe that there is a niche in the market for recruitment to be done in the right way, taking a consultative approach, so that repeat business can be a given rather than an exception.

I have a strong focus on the cards and payments industry, and I have a number of the leading names in this market as my clients. This is ever increasing due to my in-depth knowledge of the domain. I am always happy to speak to clients or candidates in this area that are interested in talking about the market or working with me.

I have a team that works for me who specialise in each area of IT in cards and payments, who can use their networks to find people speedily and efficiently. I also focus on non-IT roles for my clients, as the line between IT and business becomes ever more blurred.

Expertise: Credit card, payment industry (Swift, SwiftNet, settlements, STP, BACS, Chaps), Base24, EMV, Contactless (and other areas of mobile technologies and embedded systems), strategy (SEPA and Faster Payments), credit risk (SAS), smartcards, fraud.

I believe in networking to help all interested parties. I will add you if I see a mutual benefit.
