Anuradha Ramanathan’s Education

  • Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay

    PhD, Molecular Biology, 1996 — 2001

    My Ph. D. thesis work focused on the cloning and characterisation of arginase gene from the acidogenic fungus Aspergillus niger under the supervision of Prof. N.S. Punekar. Summary of thesis work: Aspergillus niger is an industrial fungus that produces citric acid under Mn[II]-limited growth. There is very limited genetic information available in this filamentous fungus. My work provided the genetic information on arginase and its role in the filamentous fungus.
