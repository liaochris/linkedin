Sandro Franchi’s Summary

Together with my wife we decided to create American Outland as a way (and a place) where to develop in full our abilities, exposing ourselves in a daily basis to new challenges and looking for an environment where looking for new ideas and solutions are our "routine".

Our objective as a company is to provide products -hardware and software- for the demanding users in the Latin American market, pointing to new technologies and innovative applications in order to create and expand those niches where bigger companies doesn't fit or are unable to provide the kind of support these potential customers requires.

Actually we are involved in the develpment of several GIS related products (both hardware and/or software), so our knowledge in these matters is very strong in terms of product to market analisys and implementation, having a deep knowledge of what our customers needs.

The Precision Farming field is a market where we are studying several solutions in order to have competitive and innovative products for both the local and regional market.
