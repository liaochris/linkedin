Robb Walters’s Summary

As a charter member of the Costco e-commerce business team, I have helped overcome many business challenges to help grow our business exponentially, year after year.

Other previous positions (marketing, operations, accounting, IT and design) have helped to round my professional experience, while helping to understand each business group and its challenges and contributions to our goals.

Comfortable presenting ideas one-to-one or one-to many, I have spoken at local and regional conferences and universities. Also, one of the most rewarding parts of my job is mentoring team-members and high-potentials, helping them develop and accomplish their career goals.

Also, I am an active member of industry organizations, including Shop.org (the web component of the National Retail Federation), a charter member of the Email Marketing Roundtable, the DMA and Seattle chapter of the DMA, in addition to attending national and regional conferences focused on internet retailing and marketing.
