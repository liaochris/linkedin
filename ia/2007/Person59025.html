Michelle Hayes’s Summary

In my role as a Senior Account Executive for Edelman (the world's largest independent public relations firm) I work with clients who desire to reach consumers directly through creative and strategic public relations programming. My specialty is brands that live within the home. Whether that is launching a high-end kitchen product line or social media interaction with a national gardening blogger, I provide insights into how our clients can connect brands to consumers.

In terms of a consumer segment specialty, I am extremely focused on the mom consumer segment, spotlighting the female 25-45 demographic. I provide insights on how best to reach moms through traditional and non-tradition marketing methods. For our consumer group, I follow market trends and create psychographic breakouts for this group along with up-to-date trends for creative perogramming.

Also, like any good PR professional, I have experience in connecting brands through multiple communications channels, including traditional PR outlets, blogs, podcasts, and other Word-of-Mouth marketing tactics.

Prior to joining Edelman, my career included being a television producer, marketing for an independent film featured on HBO and most recently, account manager for Atlanta's public broadcasting stations including National Public Radio and PBS.
