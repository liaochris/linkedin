Roger Rarick’s Summary

Roger brings a longstanding career of management, operations, organizational and technology development experience in business and public service. His current tenure is as a Sr Technology and Executive Recruiter and formerly a Technology Executive at the CIO and Management levels. Roger fulfilled a longstanding military career in the USAF serving in various leadership positions in Information Technology, Logistics, Aviation Engineering, Quality, and Organizational Development. He was instrumental in developing and adapting six sigma processes and taught classes in Leadership and Organizational Development.

After retiring from the military Roger held Sr. Executive Technology positions in small and Fortune 1000 companies within the manufacturing, high-tech, and chemical industries along with Technology Leadership consulting…and now is active in the Technology Consulting and full lifecycle Recruiting arena. Roger has walked many of the same miles as Technology Managers and Leaders. His prior experience gives him tremendous insight into an organizations Technology strategies, requirements and human capital needs for success, along with the ability to identify top shelf professionals. In addition he has worked with several vendor management and various applicant tracking systems.

Roger has also served as a Consultant to the National Guard Bureau, as well as on the Board of Examiners for the Secretary of the Air Force Performance Excellence Award, and as a board member for the Kansas Award for Excellence Foundation.
