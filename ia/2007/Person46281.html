Personal View

I am interested in People’s interpretation of technology, how they discover, rationalise and reuse it to suit their environments, goals and lifestyles.

I am adept at relationship building and utilising technical mediation skills for project development, scoping, specification authoring and management of multiple projects, research and investigations. These attributes enable me to provide a recognisable conduit of reliable and timely information for internal or external decision makers, developers and user interface producers. I am a highly proactive and creative team orientated worker able to multi-task and complete projects within limited time scales. In leadership, I enable team members to establish their strengths while developing support frameworks for complex tasks.
