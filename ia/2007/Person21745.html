David Mullings’s Summary

26 year-old entrepreneur who started my first company at 20 during my MBA program at the University of Miami (I graduated from college at 19 and took time off to work and play soccer semi-pro). Our first project was the creation of RealVibes.net, later renamed RealVibez.com, the leading website for reggae, dancehall and soca music videos, launched in February 2002. I led the site to 1.13 million unique visitors per month through innovative marketing and entirely funded by my immediate family (less than $10,000 to start).

We have since worked with Sean Paul, sponsored Reggae Sumfest, the largest multi-day Reggae event in the Caribbean and even worked with Telemundo's channel, Mun2. Lack of capital has forced us to pull down the site and identify new and innovative ways to continue the project, it will relaunch in April 2007.

We will leverage the power of that property similarly to how MTV has used its channel as a platform to promote other products and services. There are natural extensions of the brand that will be pursued in due course but the priority at this time is to generate meaingful cash flow.

Immediate goals:

- Raise capital in order to create the first media company focused on capitalizing on Caribbean music through digital technology as well as the first to create and oversee merchandising for these artistes

- Develop a vertical market website based on previously successful business plans applied to the general market

My long-term goals are:

- To create value for my investors by building a sustainable company that is seen as a leader in niche applications of existing technology
- To create a company that completely changes the business of Caribbean music
- To become a positive role model for young people
