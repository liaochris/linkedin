Barbara Dobrinsky Holtzman’s Summary

In working with many people, I’ve discovered that my work involves very distinct features, depending on who you are. What I’ve begun to do more and more is act as decision maker and executive, still fulfilling the triple role of consultant, counselor, and coach, but helping my client to be more proactive by providing real workable solutions, assessing risk, delineating what might seem to be the best options.

My clients are the classic “spend all my day putting out fires” type, having problems with operations and flow more than anything. Someone who dreads going into the office, then dreads going home, and is suffering from so much stress over it that he can’t do anything. Even when things go well, or otherwise happy things happen, there’s no joy in his heart...

There’s a standard information gathering and data collection period, analysis, and plan development, along with coping mechanisms and skill enhancements, all designed to help you get motivated, focused, and stay on-track.

I like to get out and see people, so I sell a variety of items and services in addition to my consulting practice, most of them as an affiliate, from a web site, but I also have several groups who sell for me as well.

However, there's no way I can tell you much of anything of substance in this little space. I've tried. So if you have questions, subscribe to my Be a Success! Newsletter, and you’ll get ebooks and reports that will help you be a success in all that you do, and my complete contact information so you can call or write and ask. I think you’ll be pleasantly surprised, whether you retain me or not - I also match up people I know and like with those who might do them some good. True networking, as I see it.
