Pankaj Manglik’s Summary

Pankaj Manglik currently serves as President and COO of Terabeam (Nasdaq: TRBM). He is also President and COO of its wholly owned subsidiary, Proxim Wireless. Proxim is a manufacturer of Wireless Mesh, WiMAX and Wi-Fi products, with a global sales footprint and annual revenues of $75M.

Prior to Proxim, Pankaj Manglik was co-founder and CEO of Aruba Wireless Networks, a worldwide leader in wireless LANs. Aruba Networks created a new market segment called Wireless LAN Switching. Aruba is funded by Matrix Partners and Sequoia Capital. At Aruba, Mr. Manglik raised two rounds of funding, built out the entire team including a worldwide sales force and took the company from inception to early revenues.

Previously, Mr. Manglik held senior management positions at Alteon WebSystems, which went public and was acquired by Nortel Networks. As Director of Product Management, he defined a new market segment called Web Switching. Prior to that, Mr. Manglik directed the marketing of Cisco’s StrataCom IGX multi-service switching platform increasing revenues to $400M per year. Previously, Mr. Manglik ran Intel’s network engineering & design organization, being promoted 3 times in 4 years.

Mr. Manglik holds an MBA from The Wharton School of Business, in addition to Bachelors and Masters Degrees in Electronics Engineering and Computer Science.

He can be reached at pankaj@manglik.com
