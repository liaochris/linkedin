Joe Turner’s Summary

I have been in the car business for over 10 years. In 1997 the GM asked me to handle leads from Autobytel. I have made the Internet side of the car business my passion ever since.

I have been an Internet guy at a single point store and an Internet Director for 2 Auto Groups. I have also been on the vendor side of the fence. I feel that my experience has brought me to TK with a unique perspective on how we can help build better websites that actually help sell cars.
