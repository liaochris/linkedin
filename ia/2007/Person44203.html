Josh Kimberg’s Summary

Josh is an award-winning entrepreneur and creator. For the past 12 years, he has executive produced, directed, written and invented projects across old and new media as the founder of four web companies.

Well known for developing animated series for both MTV and Discovery Kids, this year Josh received an Andy award and was short listed at the 2007 Cannes Lions International Advertising Festival for his work on hiobie.com an animated marketing campaign for O.B. Tampons. In previous years he earned two Emmy nominations for his role as executive producer on the Rosie O'Donnell show opens, which also notably were the first work in Flash Animation to appear on television.

As a pioneer on the web he has created interactive campaigns for Fortune 500 companies including J&J, Nike, Ford Motor Company, HBO, Fuji Film, AT&T, Sony Music, IFC, VH1 and Showtime Networks. He is most proud of his work in the world of online entertainment, creating and licensing short form animations for
dozens of original properties developed at Bullseye Art. Josh has recently launched a new online entertainment portal for the 15-35 male demo; Magic Butter features a new live action and animated variety show, a T-shirt and ring tone store and free downloads such as MP3s, desktop wallpapers, icons, contests and blogs.
