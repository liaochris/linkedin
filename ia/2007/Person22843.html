Denise Angarola Fernandez’s Summary

Ms. Fernandez is an award winning, tenured senior marketing executive with experience traversing industries including Fashion, Retail, Wireless, Interactive and now Travel.

In her current role as Vice President of Marketing for Sabre Travel Studios, Ms. Fernandez is responsible for driving diversified revenue streams and distribution reach via new online consumer branded products and services for this industry leader.

Previous to joining Sabre, she was the founder and President of Julden Group, a fully integrated marketing firm delivering on it’s philosophy of ”Live, Work, Play Marketing”. She is also the founder of the networking and talent development community, Network Ties. She speaks often on the power of personal brand, personal publicity and networking. Seminars are available for individuals and corporations.

Denise Angarola Fernandez’s Specialties:

Marketing, advertising, networking, wireless, promotions, personal branding, mentoring and personal coaching.
