Chris DeLeon’s Summary

I've been making computer games for over a decade - having finished several dozen - and I cofounded/managed a college organization (Game Creation Society) responsible for another couple dozen more. Most of my independent work has been in freeware real-time action & arcade-style titles, although I have also worked on puzzle, racing, platforming, strategy, and other genres.

I spent my past two summers in Los Angeles, working as a Game Design Intern for the Medal of Honor Airborne team at EA. See my past work section for a rough summary of objectives completed.

After college, my plans are to return to Electronic Arts Los Angeles full-time as Technical Game Designer.

Chris DeLeon’s Specialties:

Game design, project/task management, C++ programming, interface design, communication-related design (Visio diagramming, mock-up prototyping, PPT presentations).

Thanks to my indie projects, I also have an amateur level of applied experience in audio, animation, voice acting, input hardware design, 2D/3D artwork, and writing instruction manuals :-).
