Kim Singh’s Summary

Over 27 years of experience in media, wireless, ERP, e-Commerce, global IT and the management consulting business at Ernst & Young and IBM. Current CEO of PortaEnterprise Corporation.

Was awarded Entrepreneur of the Year award by Inc. magazine, Merrill Lynch and Ernst & Young LLP. Was also given a medal of honor from President Bill Clinton for work in technology and non-profit agencies. MS in Computer Science from the University of Southern California and also has an MBA in Marketing, a BS in Physics and Math and a BE in Computer Electronics and Engineering.

Extensive experience in global operations, with over 20 years of experience in planning, launching and executing company and product launches in various Asian and US markets

Kim Singh’s Specialties:

Assisting US companies with launching their operations in Asia. Helping them get funded with VCs and Angel investors in the US and in Asia. Localization of products and services for over 27 Asian markets.
