Francois Dechery’s Summary

I am looking for highly skilled J2EE engineers to join the Support organisation of Red Hat in order to expand the success of JBoss in the enterprise. With JBoss Application Server, HIbernate, JBoss Portal, jBPM, Rules, etc, JBoss had become the #1 choice for developers and for production environments, supporting critical 24x7 applications such as online reservation systems, online trade, etc. JBoss has now joined Red Hat to form the larget open source technology in the world.
