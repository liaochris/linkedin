Xiaojing Liu’s Summary

As an award-winning producer/director/editor of non-fiction programs Weekend Show and No. 1 Weekly from China's Zhejiang TV Station, I made the jump to America, where I earned my master's degree in the Television-Radio-Film department at Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications. In Syracuse, I found endless love in filmmaking through producing, directing and editing my documentaries, including Adrenaline Rush, Variations and A Chinese Girl's Diary, which built my confidence to create entertaining and thoughtful programming in America. I am currently putting the finishing touches on my first 120-minute feature film, Butterfly's Dream. With my exceptional creativity, work ethic and passion to be a new generation filmmaker, I look forward to the world of professional American media and creating programming that bridges the cultures of China and America.
