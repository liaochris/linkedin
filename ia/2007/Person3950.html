Alok Garg’s Summary

Alok Garg is an executive with proven success and entrepreneurial leadership in growing businesses for information technology and solutions in the Communication and Media Industry.

Starting December 2006 Garg has been collaborating with several ventures and Private Equity firms in India and US, as consultant and advisor to their technology investments and operations.

Previously, during his international career with Hewlett-Packard, he has held responsibilities in marketing, consulting & integration, off-shore technology development and strategy. He started his career in 1989 in India as an r&d engineer. Garg relocated to US in 1994, where he worked as Sr. Consultant for Telecom clients with Cap Gemini. He then joined Verifone as an Architect for e-commerce solutions for financial and retail customers and held business and product management roles, which included strategic alliances, technology availability on multiple platforms and profit and loss management.

After Verifone was acquired by HP in 1997, Alok held various global business management roles at HP, which included leading strategy development for enterprise business, new business and solution incubation in telecom sector, integration and leading strategic consulting practice globally and in a region.

Alok was the Chief Architect for HP's Adaptive Enterprise strategy and responsible for managing portfolio of solutions and incubating new offerings.

His latest appointment in HP was Managing Principal of Strategic Consulting Practice in Asia Pacifica and Japan.

Garg contributed to the integration of the merger between HP and Compaq, during 2003. He led the integration of all technology assets from HP and Compaq specific to the IT Infrastructure Solutions into one cohesive strategy and portfolio of offerings.

Garg is an expert in the Communication and Media industry and a thought leader in the IT Transformation domain.
