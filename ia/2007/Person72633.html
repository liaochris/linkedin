Rick Longenecker’s Summary

Work with C-level executives and business owners to bring precision to their sales execution. I help them with the following challenges:

1) Gaining Access Through Networking & Appointment Setting
2) Brining Consistency To Their Investigation Of Prospective Buyer Needs
3) Validating If Their Go-To-Market Strategy Can Be Sold By Their Sales Force
4) Facilitating Sales Strategy Alignment
5) Integrating Sales Forecasting and Sales Requirements Planning
6) Speeding Productivity of New Sales Reps

I serve executives and business owners in multiple capacities including:

1) Retained Advisor/Coach
2) Project Leader
3) Keynote Speaker
4) Facilitator
5) Analyst/Observer
6) Trainer
7) Interim Executive

Rick Longenecker’s Specialties:

Event and Conference Speaker, Process Improvement Project Leader, and Strategy Alignment Facilitation for sales practices, sales management practices and sales operations practices
