Chuck Dukas’s Summary

My years of coaching, training and mentoring traders and investors have provided me with a wealth of skills in the area of technical analysis. I have had the privilege to learn from students and colleagues the artful skill of technical analysis trend trading. My thirteen years as a group leader of the Boston technical analysis group and chairperson for the New England MTA (Market Technicians Association) has provided me added knowledge. I have lectured at MIT, Babson College, Bentley College, Bloomberg NYC, radio station from Canada to Florida and Boston Stock Exchange. Recently was I was named to “The Best and Brightest of Technical Analysis” by the prestigious magazine of our trade Stocks & Commodities. I have written numerous articles in our industry trade magazines. This coupled with the successful release of my book by John Wiley & Sons “The TRENDadvisor Guide to Breakthrough Profits A Proven System for Building Wealth in the Financial Markets” left me with one more pursuit to accomplish.

The AcuTREND Trading System Software by TRENDadvisor is a culmination of my passion, knowledge and life’s work. It bring to the trader and investor a sound methodology, trading plan and money management component for high probability trading.

Our web advisory service has had 9 years of annual profitable performance of 60% plus.
