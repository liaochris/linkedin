Johan Farid Khairuddin’s Summary

As one of Asia's youngest and most experienced strategic business development experts in "Youth Dynamics", I work around the region and travel the world for the ASTRO ALL ASIA NETWORKS plc, to consult the profitable development of youth-targeted entertainment centric cross-media platforms, projects & business interests.

Being also a weekend radio DJ on Malaysia's #1 English Radio Station - HITZ.FM, I have been an "On Air" Radio & TV personality with more than 12+ years experience in the music, youth, entertainment and broadcasting industries, my work has brought me all across Asia including the United Kingdom, Europe and North America.

I recently spoke on the high level panel for Youth & Media at the United Nations Global Youth Forum in Switzerland last September and am in the midst of preparing to speak at the "UN meets New Media" global meeting in New York this February 2008.

I also just returned from the Philippines to pick up an award for one of my most recent youth projects. The TAYO (Ten Accomplished Youth Organizations) Awards is organized by the Committee for ASEAN Youth Cooperation.

Please allow me another week or two to update my profile with my most recent achievements. To contact me, you may email johan@astro.com.my.
