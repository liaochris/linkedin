Rebecca Theim’s Summary

Results-focused professional with experience in corporate communications, financial communications, issues management and crisis communications for high-profile, fast-paced organizations. Understands and works exceptionally well with the media, drawing on previous experience and relationships developed as a reporter for several major news organizations.

“Strategic doer” who develops the plan and then rolls up her sleeves and successfully executes it.
