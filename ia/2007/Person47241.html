Ken Hayes’s Summary

Ken is a passionate mobile and technology enthusiast with an entrepreneurial spirit. Ken has 14+ years of experience working both for mobile operators and with companies that supply services to the mobile ecosystem. Ken is well versed in start up and growth stage environments.

Ken has a proven track record of large business development "wins" and successful product launches.

Ken is currently SVP of Sales at Motricity where he has responsibility for all aspects of sales, business development and account management for Motricity’s industry leading product portfolio. .

Before joining Motricity, Ken was Sr Director of Interactive Wireless Services division at InPhonic (NASDAQ:INPC), a leading provider of mobile services. InPhonic acquired GadgetSpace, a mobile application gateway provider where Ken was part of the founding team.

Ken began his mobile career when he joined Dial Call, acquired by Nextel, now Sprint (NYSE:S). While at Nextel, Ken spent 6 years in marketing and channel development roles. Ken was Marketing Manager during the most successful market launch in Nextel history. In addition, Ken led the development of and managed Nextel’s first retail channel – leading all channels across many key metrics (CPGA, net adds, churn, etc).

Ken has been recognized multiple times across all organizations where he has worked; most recently he was awarded Motricity’s Leader of the Year. Ken was recognized multiple times at Nextel - winning Nextel’s highest award, President’s Council (1995 & 1996) and numerous awards for outstanding performance.

Ken has a BBA in Finance from the University of Georgia and a MS in Marketing from Georgia State University.

Ken is a competitive endurance athlete having completed multiple marathons, ultra-marathons and triathlons of all distances. He enjoys home renovation, is an avid reader and studies architecture in his spare time.

Ken rarely passes up ice cream when offered.
