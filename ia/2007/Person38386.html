Jarrett Winter’s Summary

With an entrepreneurial spirit and career built on proven success, I approach sales and business development with an excitement and energy aimed at driving revenue and building long term rewards. Offering over seven of experience working with startups, I have experience taking companies of 5 employees and $300K in yearly revenue to 65 employees and $13.8MM.

Presently, I am the Director of Sales for a new and exciting magazine, LaTeen. LaTeen's mission is to entertain, enrich and empower America's Hispanic teenagers. With a target market ranging from 14 to 18 year olds, LaTeen is proving to be the voice of this generation.

As well,I am the Project Sales Director for 3Value Logic (3VL). 3Value Logic is a sales outsourcing firm. 3VL's specialty is our ability to identify weakness in both organization sales structure and sales approach. From here, we work with our client to identify a true sales cycle, implement a winning value proposition and close deals. In addition, we construct your company's sales infrastructure so that you are positioned for long term success. Presently, I am Project Director for two 3VL clients, TransMagic and Mont Blanc Gourmet.

My sales career started in the spring of 2000. I began with Silicon Mountain Memory (SMM). At the time I joined SMM, I was the sixth employee. I helped grow the company from 6 to 65 employees along with a exceptional revenue growth. In 2000, SMM did roughly $300K in business by the end of 2006 they have reached nearly $18MM. In my last year with SMM, I personally did $3MM in business and was far and away the number one sales representative. In 2005, SMM made the Inc's Fastest 500 list.
