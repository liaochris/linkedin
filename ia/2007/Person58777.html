Michael Aaron’s Summary

Director of Client Services at Tocquigny, a Top 50 national interactive agency. Responsible for Client Services group across all agency accounts, including Dell, WorldVision, Synopsys, and National Madison Group. Previously held senior positions at HSM Group (VP, Business Development) and Citigroup (VP, Product Manager). MBA from University of Texas (McCombs) and English Degree from University of Pennsylvania. Prior to business school, was Media Director for M2K, a leading interactive advertising agency in Austin, TX.
