George Hennessy’s Summary

Innovative, multi-discipline entrepreneur with over 15 years hands-on experience in the marketing, sales, and implementation of cutting-edge services and technology (both hardware and software). Helped small startups grow to become publicly traded companies. Experience includes key roles in three IPOs and three acquisitions.

In-depth understanding of technology and its markets with the proven ability to rapidly develop intimate knowledge of and insight into new technologies and markets. Technology experience for enterprise and public communications networks includes: optical networking, storage area networking, wireless (WiFi, WiMAX, mobile, and satellite), cable TV, IPTV, telephony, DSL, ATM & IP networking, and voice over IP.

Broad B2B marketing experience including brand development, public relations, corporate messaging, financial communications, IPO & VC funding roadshows, market segmentation & entry strategies, partner/channel marketing, analyst relations, product positioning, competitive analysis, tradeshows, business development, and sales support.
