Thilo Pfennig’s Summary

I am a self-educated computer expert. I started with an Amiga Computer in the 80s where I made my first contact with the hypertext system AmigaGuide, later as a Mac user I used HyperCard and later switched to HTML as soon as it was available. After a switch to Linux systems and Linux system administration I got interested in Wiki systems and decided to found a new company PfennigSolutions that specializes in such systems.

I think from HyperText to Wikis it is a direct link. I always link things.

I also was active in parts of the GNOME community and tried to give some new impulses to Freedesktop initiative (so both KDE and GNOME) because I think collaboration is MORE than essential. Besides Wikis I also install Linux Desktops for customers so I get interesting feedback from that.

I think not beeing an academic computer expert but self-educated gives me a real different view on many things (less structured but more interconnected).

I always like to test out new things. Currently I am involved in GNOMEs marketing efforts.

I am always unsatisfied with todays computer technology and always habe dozend of ideas in my head that want to get out...
