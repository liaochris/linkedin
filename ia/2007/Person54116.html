Marc Marot’s Summary

I have spent most of my Career working in the music industry.
I was a music publisher from 1982 to 1990, running Island Music Publishing.
From 1990 to 2000 I was the Managing Director of Island records, and following my resignation in 2000 I set up a music management company Terra Firma management. Terra Firma manages artists in the pop arena such as Yusuf Islam, Paul Oakenfold and Lemon jelly, however in recent years our buisness has evolved to include a number of composers for the world of Film, Television and Games, including veteran film composer Trevor Jones (Mississippi Burning, Notting Hill and the Last of the Mohicans). We have recently added Marc Canham and his company Nimrod who specialise in composing for the games industry (Driver, Act of War, Vietcong etc). Paul Oakenfold scores movies and composes for the Games industry too (Swordfish, Matrix etc).

Marc Marot’s Specialties:

I am an A&R expert. I find and develop talent in the Music space. I am also a music rights expert.
