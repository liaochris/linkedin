Bob Apollo’s Summary

Experienced, effective general manager with a joined-up approach to strategy, marketing, sales, business development, operations and team building and a sustained track record of success in leading high-tech companies through the transition from early sales success to a scalable, repeatable business model capable of delivering sustained revenue, profit and market share growth.

Equips companies to “cross the chasm” through the application of focused programmes that establish market leadership by reflecting the "voice of the customer". Spans UK, European and Global markets, enabling US-based companies to break out from initial European bridgeheads, and UK or European-based companies to move up to the next level of significant growth.

Helps recruit, train and develop great teams that consistently perform at the top of their game. Recognises the need for change and leads organisations through key transitions. Builds productive, effective channels and business development relationships. Confident communicator, at ease with press, analysts, partners and customers.

* Accelerates growth of high-potential companies
* Delivers results at UK, European & Global Level
* Actively involved in 7 successful equity/M&A events (2 IPOs, 2 trade sales, 3 acquisitions)
* Consistent track record of sustained revenue and profitability growth whilst controlling cost of sales
* Managed significant teams (80+ people) and revenue streams ($40m+) and high-growth environments (80%+ CAGR)
* Helped establish XcelleNet and SCO as undisputed leaders in their respective markets
