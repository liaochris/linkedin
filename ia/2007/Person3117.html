Alexandra Salomon’s Summary

Alexandra Salomon joined Yahoo Inc (Overture Services) in December 2003 to lead international strategy and business development for Global Product Management, responsible for Overture's key international product direction, new initiatives, market roll out and launch. She now is part of the Global Sales team for Yahoo, specifically focused on international sales development, product monetization, brand/search synergies and trade marketing events.

Prior to Yahoo, Alexandra was Executive Director at AOL International where she oversaw global deal alliances, international interactive marketing strategy, training and in country development. During her 7 years there, she worked with over 100 clients and agencies including eBay, Philips, Coca-Cola, Travelocity, Monster, Schwab and Toyota.

A long time veteran in international online advertising, Alexandra grew up in Europe, speaks French, Russian and Spanish, and holds a B.A. in International Affairs and an MBA in International Trade and Finance.
