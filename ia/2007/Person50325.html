Laurent Jadot’s Summary

I am the founder and main composer of Amor et Psyché. I went on to compose and produce music for documentaries, advertising, sound identities for Radio & TV.

Since 1994, I work as a radio producer for many national advertising radio campaigns.

I'm also executive producer and chief editor for programmes for belgian national TV and radio (VW Escape TV, CyberCafe on RTBF, SEAT emocion.TV and Bang Bang on Pure FM).

Laurent Jadot’s Specialties:

Radio and TV production, music composing, sound design, project and creative team management.
