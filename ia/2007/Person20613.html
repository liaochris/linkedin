Dan Wedgwood’s Summary

Press officer, web writer and content editor, with experience of round the world yacht racing and large outdoor events. PR experience includes high profile coverage of Dee Caffari's Aviva Challenge, when she became the first woman to sail solo and non-stop round the world against the prevailing winds. Writing experience includes Global Challenge round the world yacht race daily reports and features, ghost writing, Aviva Challenge website content, Discovery Channel International Balloon Fiesta website content and various articles published in sailing publications, local newspapers and online.
