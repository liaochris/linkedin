Warren Hitesh Myer’s Summary

Warren H. Myer, a serial entrepreneur has a BS in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay, India, an MS in Computer Science from the U. of Delaware and an MBA in Finance/Marketing from the U. of Chicago.

Warren originated the first Internet mortgage in 1992. In 1994, he setup the first mortgage website. He has successfully started and grown three different businesses from 1991 to 2006.

He created BestRate.com, Mortgage-Net.com and LoanApp.com - sites that won awards by Forbes and Newsweek. Warren has been featured as an expert on online mortgages on CNN and has been interviewed and quoted in numerous publications including Newsweek, Money Magazine, San Jose Mercury and Fast Company. He has published several articles in a wide range of industry magazines including Mortgage Banking, National Mortgage Broker, Mortgage Originator, Bank Marketing, Credit Union News, and has been a guest speaker at many national and state conferences.

Internet mortgage, real estate, sales, marketing, public relations, product development.
