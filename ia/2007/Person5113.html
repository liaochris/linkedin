Andrea Balboni’s Summary

Creative, conceptual, results driven design solutions meet client’s needs through original thinking. International experience includes New York City, Tokyo, and Milan giving a more “global, big picture” outlook and insight.

Cross media, cross cultural brand and identity solutions; visual storytelling and the creation of emotive experiences through photography and video; motion graphics; book, magazine and catalogue design; profound knowledge of technical printing techniques (special inks, varnishes, finishes) and support materials (natural, coated and treated papers, plastics, cloth, metals); impeccable typography; strong color sensibility; spot illustrations and excellent hand rendering skills.

Andrea Balboni’s Specialties:

Adobe CS2 (Photoshop, Image Ready, Illustrator), InDesign, Quark Express, Freehand, Streamline; Flash, Dreamweaver, Final Cut Pro; basic knowledge of After Effects, Flash Movie, ON2 Flix Pro, Sorenson Squeeze, XHTML, CSS, Javascript, ActionScript; Microsoft Office, Powerpoint; Windows and Mac OS.
