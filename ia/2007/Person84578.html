Alison Minaglia’s Summary

Alison Minaglia is a full-service, independent public relations consultant with a 20 year track record, and passion for, promoting business-to-business and technology clients to market leadership through award-winning, results-oriented media and analyst relations programs. Minaglia has created and personally executed successful PR programs for more than 50 technology clients which have captured the interest of investors, customers, partners and key influencers in each industry. She’s managed and represented the experts, products and services of companies ranging from emerging startups to divisions of Fortune 500, in a variety of IT, telecom and biotech industry segments.

Independently and through a network of established global partnerships with complementary service providers offered to clients on an as-needed basis, Alison Minaglia has met demand for measurable PR results, senior-level representation and outsourced communications support.

Prior to founding ARM Communications in 1995 (now dba TechnologyPR.com), Alison was Managing Supervisor of the New York Technology Practice at international PR conglomerate Fleishman-Hillard, where she created and led the firm’s first U.S. Industry Analyst Relations team and was positioned to clients as the office’s National Media Relations expert based on established relationships and results with the nation’s top business and technology influencers. Before that, Alison was Account Supervisor and then Senior Account Supervisor for the Technology and Healthcare divisions at Ruder-Finn. Her PR career began in 1985 with Assistant Account Executive and then Account Executive roles at HWH Enterprises in NY. Prior to working in PR, Alison was a broadcast news journalist. Alison has a Bachelors Degree in Marketing and Communications from University of Delaware.
