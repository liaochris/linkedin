Amy Miranda’s Summary

Amy is passionate about the Online space and whether it’s Technical, Creative or Conceptual she lives and breathes it. Having worked in all facets of the industry Amy offers very unique fun multidisciplinary perspective which is met with an equal degree of humor, commitment and creativity.

Amy’s passion for all things Interactive began when she was a Production Assistant at iXL (Interactive Excellence) in Atlanta in the mid-nineties, She began her fulltime career in the fledgling days of CNN.com in Atlanta.

Amy has produced, conceptualized and managed thousands of large-scale online marketing campaigns and solutions ranging from frequency programs to eCommerce sites. Current and past clients include: MINI Canada, Telus, AOL, Canadian Tire, Amp’d Mobile, Nike, WestJet, Molson-Coors, MSN, Bell, Bombardier, The Coca-Cola Company, and UPS. Amy has won multiple international awards for her work producing large scale interactive projects.

She now draws on over 10 years of Interactive and Operations experience delivering award-winning, digital marketing solutions. She’s worked as an Editor, Designer, Technology Manager, Technical Services Director, Project Manager and Interactive Consultant at companies including iXL, CNN Interactive, U8TV/Alliance Atlantis, Activate, Henderson Bas and now as Interactive Production Director at Strategy & Marketing's Agency of the Year, TAXI in Toronto.
