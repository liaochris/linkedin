Bert Hagendoorn’s Summary

With a background of more than 12 years of working experience in graphic design, multimedia, online and interaction design the position as editior-in-chief at Dzone magazine (a leading Dutch print publication on graphic design) gave me the ability to write about industry news, trends and backgounds in design and development. The passion and interest for online and interaction design was translated in the position of many jury's the last years, like SpinAwards and Nederlandse Design Prijzen. After Dzone, the position of project manager at advertising agency OER gave me the opportunity to work within the creative field again, with a focus on interactive projects and online advertising campaigns. Furthermore focussing on Flash media, the position of Ambassador at Satama FlashFabriek and Marketing Project Manager ata Adobe Systems gave me ability to build on my working experience.
