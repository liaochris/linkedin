Maya Frost’s Summary

I've traveled around the world, taught English in Japan for five years, taught ESL courses at the university level in Oregon, owned a retail store, served as a community activist, did a stint as the executive director of a social justice nonprofit, did another as the national outreach coordinator of an environmental nonprofit, became a mediator, trained clients around the world (including other trainers) in eyes-wide-open alternatives to meditation, and now I'm writing a book on savvy, no-angst ways to get an outstanding college education that doesn't cost a fortune. It's called Free Agent U: Skip the SAT, Save Thousands On Tuition, and Get An Outrageously Relevant College Education. For more on that, visit http://www.FreeAgentU.wordpress.com.

With four daughters in college at the moment (in three countries!), I've had lots of opportunities to discover all kinds of creative shortcuts and surprising loopholes!

I'm currently living with my husband and youngest daughter in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I'm interested in connecting with creativity trainers, teachers, exchange students from around the world (former and current) and others who are finding their own way to navigate internationally and wind up with a college degree that is relevant, valuable and meaningful.
