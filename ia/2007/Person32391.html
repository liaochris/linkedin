Glenn Head’s Summary

Marian and I are both Wellness Consultants. We have helped nearly 50,000 children, women, and men who were health challenged to achieve and maintain optiumn health by using a new food called glyconutrition.

I played the barnyard game in 1982. Marian (my wife) and I have been learning about the profession of Network Marketing since 1995. I have found this profession to be the most exciting opportunity for collaboration and win-win that I have ever seen. In 2002, I co-founded Networking University to build training so that everyone interested in this profession could have a source of training and support to be successful regardless of which company they represent.

We both have risen to the top leadership level of Mannatech, Inc., a nutritional research and development company located in Coppell, TX. Mannatech uses a product distribution model called multi-level marketing. This distribution strategy is BRILLIANT when the game is played with integrity and love.

Now that we receive an abundant cashflow to allow us to live our dreamlife, our goal is to grow our income and use the money to fund good works in the world.

You are welcome to join us!
