Patrik Jonsson’s Summary

Patrik Jonsson is a senior Process and Method Specialist with 15+ years experience in OO, and of processes for software engineering, such as RUP.

Over the years he has been responsible for mentoring and teaching modern software engineering to companies such as Ericsson, Prudential Life Insurance, American Express, Föreningssparbanken, Swedish Defence Administration, Telia, SJ and Apoteket.

Formerly at Rational, he has been involved in developing a predecessor of UML and of the Rational Unified Process – climaxing in a research project on systematic software reuse.

He is a co-author of Object Oriented Software Enginering: A Use Case Driven Approach and of Software Reuse: Architecture, Process and Organization for Business Success.

During 2003 and 2004 he led the development of a 5 week course in software architecture. This was carried out together with the Swedish university Högskolan Dalarna.

During 2003 and 2004 Patrik has been responsible for assessing and configuring RUP for use within the development of Network Centric Warfare in the Swedish armed forces.

During 2006 Patrik has been one of the key developers of the Essential Unified Process, and endeavor undertaken by Ivar Jacobson Consulting.

Patrik Jonsson’s Specialties:

Rational Unified Process (RUP), Essential Unified Process (EssUP), Unified Modeling Language (UML), Reuse, WayPointer
