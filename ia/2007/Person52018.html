Dana Lookadoo’s Summary

Dana Lookadoo has expertise in online marketing and e-commerce conversion, search engine optimization, technical writing, graphic design, programming, instructional design and training. Dana began a career in computing in 1984 and the Internet in 1996.

Served as Adjunct Faculty at Cal State University, Monterey Bay and has conducted multiple corporate training classes.

Provided technology curriculum training for Sun Microsystems, the Monterey Institute of International Studies, U.C. Santa Cruz Extension, General Colin Powell's Youth Peace Corp, PowerUp - America's Promise, and Steve Young’s NFL YET Center.

In 2005, Ms. Lookadoo authored “Traveling the Internet” for Sun Microsystems, a 100-page guide for how to travel safely and securely on the WWW.

Managed and operated three Web firms and currently runs Pixel Position, a Search Engine Marketing Agency specializing in eMarketing.

She has over 17 years of corporate experience, holds a B.S. (magna cum laude) in Telecommunications, Multimedia and Applied Computing with a minor in Instructional Technology, and an A.A. in Business Administration. She studied Virtual Reality Modeling Language at the Naval Postgraduate School with a focus on visual application for instructional design.
