Jaroslav Krecek’s Summary

I have studied and always worked in IT.
My education background is from Software Development for Computer Graphics applications - MSc.
During university I was employed as a software developer (part time).
After the university I set my own company and gained management, sales and other skils needed to run a software development company.
Mandatory military service in the Czech republic interupted my business-active time.
After that my skills were good enough to step into a Project Management role in the Czech Republic.
That time was, however, a very convenient time to make radical changes - I moved to the UK soon after the end of my military service duty.
As, at the time, my English was not good enough yet to step into a management role I started as a senior developer (despite being over-qualified). After the first year I moved on to a technical team leader, later to a product development leader and finally to an overall Development Manager role. I am now managing development of our company's flagship product - Portrait Foundaiton.

My software development skills are mainly from C++ and C# development, COM, XML, XSLT and graphical algorithms.

My managerial skills are from areas of software development processes, release management, configuraiton management, personal development leadership, release planing, solution proposals, client negotiations, etc.

My main goal is to be the best at what I'm doing, to look forward for the company and its product, and set processes, practices and tools for the future of the company.

I have now reached the level of the role I like. I'd like to get really good at that. I may want to move to Product Director or CTO roles in the future.
