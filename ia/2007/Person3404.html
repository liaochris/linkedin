Alex Papanastassiou’s Summary

"An ounce of practice is worth more than tons of preaching" - Mahatma Gandhi

Acting as a guide to bold business quests in our age of information, facilitating your development and celebrating your successes. That is what I am passionate about. That is my profession.

I have had the privilege to facilitate projects and guide several start-ups, several departments of established companies, a few high-growth businesses and many individuals. We've enjoyed every step of the way even those steps that were not the most pleasant.

"alex inc." is about passionate focus on the business and its core driving force: people. My way of working is based on a very developed ability to ask relevant questions (even when my customers will not enjoy those questions to start). Key values for me:
- progress & evolution
- growth
- professional service to customers & partners
- commitment & passion
- respect & harmony
- efficiency

I do not believe in quick and dirty "solutions", nor in waiting for perfect solutions. I guess what captures it is this:
"There are no shortcuts to any place worth going" - Anonymous

And now my "secret" recipe for success: the alliance of knowledge, experience and common sense. Again something that explains it fairly well is:

"Start where you are, use what you ave and do what you can" - Arthur Ashe

Few things make me prouder and happier than having satisfied customers, seeing businesses unfold successfully and helping people achieve their full potential in human organizations.
