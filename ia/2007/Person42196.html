Jo Cameron’s Summary

Social Entrepreneur and motivational expert, I first entered the public consciousness in the hit TV show The Apprentice. I now run URhired.co.uk, specialising in placing women into organisations where they are underrepresented.

I have held several senior management professions in the motor industry; something of which only a handful of women achieve. I have a Masters Degree and I am also a qualified engineer.

I has overcome many challenges in personal and business life and now present my own ten step programme to getting motivated, “Running With The Wind”. The energising audio CD went on sale in February 2007.

“Sometimes life can feel like we are running through a storm but if we keep running hard enough eventually we will come out of the other side. Then we can really understand what it is like to run with the wind”. Jo

Jo Cameron’s Specialties:

At URhired.co.uk we specialise in women friendly jobs and working with organisations to improve the position of women through recruitment, training and consultancy. We also work with women to boost career confidence.
