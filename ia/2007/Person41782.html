Jennifer LeClaire’s Summary

Jennifer is president of Revelation Media Networks, Inc., an integrated marketing communications firm, and parent company of Self Storage Promotions. Jennifer is also co-founder of Conversion Press, an independent publisher currently working with Wiley Publishing to produce “Web Analytics For Dummies.” Jennifer has worked with Universal Studios, the NBA, the New York Times, the Associated Press, and many large and small corporations in their quest for communications excellence. Growing companies turn to Jennifer's team for keen insight into what it takes to win an audience. Small businesses contract Jennifer's team to relate their message to the masses. And university presidents and politicians let Jennifer's team do the talking for them through ghostwritten articles that appear on the op-ed pages of daily newspapers.

Jennifer is a member of the Public Relations Society of America and the American Society of Authors and Journalists. She is also a guest technology analyst on CBS radio every Wednesday evening.
