Bert Boerland’s Summary

My strength is to be the bridge between customers / sales and delivery where I am responsible for making new products that are either customer business driven or operation driven. An example of the first is helping customers through the process of making agile websites, the second to optimize the service in quality (tooling) or reduction of costs needed in the delivery of our for highend e-business webhosting. I have for filled many roles within my employer from management to business development and high end consulting and currently act as an innovation manager for our media customers.

I have lots of experience with the pitfalls and pro's of opensource software and am an OSS evangelist within Getronics-PinkRoccade, helping out on strategic level for big outsourcing contracts and know enough about operations to fit it in our datacentre delivery group. Privatly I am passionated about Drupal (an opensource CMS) MythTV (an opensource PVR), my girlfriend, daughter and newborn son.
