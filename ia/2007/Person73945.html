Roger Oliver Smith’s Summary

My experience within the fashion industry is broad, ranging from high-end fashion retail to design, construction, production and sales. This means that I have a solid understanding of the fashion industry and the design process from idea to finished product in stores. My last position in London was with MC Corp working mainly with the Money Clothing brand where I developed limited edition men and women’s lines.

My approach to design is characterised by organic ‘thinking with fabric’ combined with a strong sense of technical construction that has roots in the sewing craft of tailoring. As a designer I'm very research orientated and I like to fuse different inspirations and twist non-fashion related inspiration to fit a clothing context. I take my inspiration from watching how people in public places use clothes to express themselves and from photographic books, ethnographic material, history of clothing, art and architecture.
