Sebastien Goiffon’s Summary

Business Objects security expert (BO5 or BO6 and BOE Xir2) / Migration to BOE Xi r2 specialist

Inventor and world wide reseller of 360view suite (Security Migration Pack),

360View Release 2 is a web-based user-friendly interface allowing to manage and document Xir2 Security.
sing 360View r2 allows customers to
- Be agile and efficient at defining, managing and documenting your XIr2 security
- Comply with SOX.
- Focus on BI not on admin tasks.
- Save time and money.
- Keep the deployment under control.

360View Release 1 is a 360 degree view toolset of the Business Objects 5 and 6 security allowing you to make an easier, faster, cheaper and safer migration to BOE Xir2: AUTOMATIC XIR2 SECURITY MATRICES GENERATION.
Our solution is a set of universes and reports querying the security domain. It is composed with three modules: Audit, Cleaning and Accurate Xir2 security matrices.
