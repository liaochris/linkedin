Manish Chowdhary’s Summary

Manish is a management executive with prior experience in Healthcare/ LifeSciences consulting and private equity. His clients have been companies from different industries - healthcare and life sciences, IT, retail and media. He has worked with them in different capacities - making and exiting investments, helping set up processes, re-engineering existing processes and designing systems to support them.

Analysis of investment proposals, valuation negotiation, financial modeling, financial and legal due diligence, monitoring, value addition, business development.

IT Consulting, Business Process Mapping, BPR, Requirements Analysis, Project Management, Clinical Trials, Regulatory Compliance, Sales/CRM.
