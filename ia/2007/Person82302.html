Steve Hulford’s Summary

I have worked for the last 12 years in the Technology and Media business. I have been managing FSN.ca & PoolExpert as an equity partner. We are the largest privately owned Fantasy Sports operation in North America with over 350,000 game players. In the summer of 2005 I helped launch a Web 2.0 business called Filemobile.com.

I started my career at IBM in Marketing and Sales, and moving into interactive content production with Sportsnet.ca. I spent time in San Francisco with Quokka Sports as a Broadband Producer. In October 2001, I was Vice-President of Sports at Bell Globemedia where I ran TSN.ca and RDS.ca two of Canada's largest sports websites.

In 1995 I Produced and Directed a 60 minute documentary called: REAL TRAVELS: 60 Days in Indonesia which aired on The Travel Channel and Outdoor Life Network.
