Michael Habib’s Summary

I plan to develop online communities and services that promote the idea of digital library as place. Like physical libraries, digital libraries need to be community centers, collaborative study spaces, meeting spaces, etc. In addition, I plan to train library users and staff to use new technologies and information resources.

I am currently seeking employment at either a library or a web services company. I am interested in working for an institution which understands the value of the internet as a tool for harnessing the power of collective intelligence.
