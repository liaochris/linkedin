Jitka Petrickova’s Summary

I have spent the last 13 years in advertising, media and marketing communications in San Francisco, London, Prague and Budapest.

I am currently holding position of Director of Media at Organic Inc. in San Francisco US.

As Managing Director, I led a 20 person Starcom operation in Budapest, Hungary. I overcame unsecured revenues, client dissatisfaction and low staff morale, and grew revenue by 20% by retaining existing clients (Procter & Gamble, Coca-Cola, Kraft, LG Electronics, Fiat, Ikea) and adding new clients (Bristol Myers Squibb, Western Union, Schering).

Prior to this, I was in Starcom’s London office and responsible for building a new communication plan for the British Telecom GS brand across several markets in Europe.

From 1998 to 2003 I managed Eurotel, the largest telecommunication account in the Czech Republic. From 2000 to 2003, I grew the account by 130% while simultaneously holding 3 roles within Starcom. Two of my team’s campaigns were short listed for an Effie Award in 2003 and another won a Nutcracker Creative Award in 2002. I also established Starcom IP, an interactive company inside of Starcom, which quickly became one of the top three interactive agencies in that market.

Throughout my career I have taken many business, marketing and management courses (e.g. People Management, Advanced Presentation Skills, On-line Marketing, Advanced Negotiation Skills). I also applied for and attended London Business School’s Young Professional Program, an accelerated executive MBA.
