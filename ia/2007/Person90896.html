Tony Miller’s Summary

Tony Miller is a Senior Programme Manager and Programme Director with over 35 years experience of successful leadership, planning and delivery of complex, technologically advanced and innovative development, business change, launch readiness, and implementation programmes in Telecommunications, Banking, Public Sector and Media sectors, often in business-critical or recovery circumstances

He is accustomed to achieving critical quality in technical or customer service performance within high-profile, time-critical or launch environments, and to ensuring that advanced development programmes are conducted within practical, effective and measurable plans and strategies, and with suitable management, technical and quality controls to meet business objectives without unnecessary risk

Programme initiation and governance; leadership; programme planning; budgeting and financial control; programme management; programme boards; change, issue and risk management; commercial negotiation; supplier management; rehearsals and pilots, systems integration; transition to live operation, business architecture, business processes, business change and implementation; technical, performance, customer service or functional reviews and audits; troubleshooting and problem resolution

Specialist rehearsal and launch readiness services - Integrating people, technology and processes into Raw Capabilities, and developing Capabilities into high-quality Core Competences
