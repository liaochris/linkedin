Natalia Perez’s Summary

Business Connect is an international business consulting company whose core business is to find opportunities for foreign firms and facilitate their access to the Mexican market offering market research, business contacts search, appointments setting and other related services

As a business consultant, Natalia Perez has participated in diverse activities related to international businesses and market analysis. She has worked for commercial organizations such as the American Chamber of Commerce and the Indiana and North Carolina Trade Offices in Mexico and has a wide experience offering other national and foreign institutions and companies market research, business contacts and market development, commercial missions, and other related services in diverse sectors.

Natalia was founding partner of BMT Consulting, a business, marketing and trade consulting company with clients such as the American and Canadian Embassies in Mexico and other foreign companies interested in the Mexican market. Since December 2006, Natalia founded Business Connect to continue attending foreign companies in their entry process into Mexico.

Natalia has a B.A. in Business Administration at ITESM and obtained a Diploma degree in Business Studies at Guildhall University in London and an International Business and Exports Strategies Diploma degree at ITESM.

Natalia Perez’s Specialties:

Market research: qualitative and quantitative market research, secondary investigation in diverse sectors

Business Contacts: identification and evaluation of potential clients, distributors, representatives or partners and elaboration of company profiles

Commercial Missions: elaboration of business agendas and assistance during face-to-face meetings
