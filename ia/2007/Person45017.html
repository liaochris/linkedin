Judy Ma’s Summary

Judy Ma was born in Seoul, Korea and studied industrial design at Hong Ik University (Korea). She started her career at Motorola in 1996. Judy has a broad international experience by working in Korea and Singapore prior to coming to the US in 1998. During her times at Motorola, she has developed and delivered the new line of cellular telephony and award winning product designs of the Bluetooth & wearable products. She possesses a solid record of accomplishment in leading industrial design staffs that contribute to developing successful and innovative new products and product platforms for mobile device entities.

Judy Ma’s Specialties:

Ability to develop product designs and brand identities for a wide variety of product types for consumer electronics. Possess excellent design and visualization talents, as well as strong creative problem solving and CAID skills. Extremely self-motivated, a global mindset, excellent presentation skills, and strong leadership skills.
