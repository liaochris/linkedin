Angela Rossoni’s Summary

I work as a freelance consultant specialized in technical and marketing writing, technology journalism and technical translation in the field of electronics and IT. My articles regularly appear on leading Italian publications following all the aspects of the electronics industry and of professional ICT.
I also keep a professional blog in Italian, l'"onda multimediale" (http://ondamultimediale.blogosfere.it/), which covers all aspects of multimedia technologies and applications and their impact on the society and on everyday life.

Previously I worked as a design engineer at the STMicroelectronics centre of excellence for non-volatile memories located in Agrate Brianza (near Milan).

I hold a master’s degree in electronic engineering at the Polytechnic University of Milan.

I am a member of the Italian institute of chartered engineers, and of the Italian council of journalists.

I speak fluent English, French, German and basic Russian.
