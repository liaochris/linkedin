Kristi Serrano’s Summary

Kristi Serrano has been working in the Internet and marketing industry for the past four years in multiple facets. From Ad Trafficking and Campaign Management to Email and Online Marketing, she is no stranger to the dot com marketplace. Kristi currently heads up the marketing team at Prime Visibility where she oversees and monitors the marketing tactics used to generate traffic and increase ROI for an assortment of clients. Kristi also creates detailed marketing plans for clients as well as Prime Visibility that include SEO, Email Marketing, Public Relations and Lead Generation. Kristi has been active member of 212 – New York’s Interactive Advertising Club since 2004 and speaks at several local Universities on Internet Marketing. Kristi currently heads up the newly launched site, Interactive Femme, a subsidiary of Ad Femme, a networking site for women in the interactive space. Kristi will also be teaching an Internet Marketing course at Long Island University, C.W. Post come Fall 2007. She currently has a B.F.A. in Public Relations from Long Island University, C.W. Post.
