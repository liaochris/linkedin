Paul Lomax’s Summary

I've been developing websites since 1999 and created IPC's web technology department in 2003. I brought them Open Source, conceived the in-house content management system, launched 60 websites, and developed applications that are now business critical. I've done this with literally no budget at times and with big budgets at other times. But the technology has always been a means to an ends, and it's been the business side that's really driven me - I'm an entrepreneur at heart.

I had been seeking a new challenge, something a bit different, and I've been given the perfect new opportunity at IPC. I've jumped into a Digital Publishing role and I will be working on Special Projects in the Connect (Women's Weeklies) division, where I will be reporting to the Managing Director.
