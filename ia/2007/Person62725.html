Nayla Chacra-Audi’s Summary

Worked as event planner (film festivals, discussions, conferences, wedding), media professional (video production, editing and photography), and project co-ordinator (human rights and Non-Governmental Organizations).

I am interested in working on video/photography projects in the fields of development and human rights. I have conducted research on the role of Arts and Media in Peacebuilding (my main focus was on photography and video).

I currently run cultural workshops in Morocco to help better the understanding of the Moroccan cultural as well as to develop and to exchange skills with Moroccan artists (i.e. photography, drawing, painting, music and dance).

Nayla Chacra-Audi’s Specialties:

Development: fundraising, project development, research and grant proposal writing.
Video production: budgeting, casting, location scouting, shooting (mini-dv specialist), video editing (final cut pro and avid), artistic events management, client relations and service, marketing and distribution.
Photography: travel, social and photojournalism. Interested in photo documentary.
