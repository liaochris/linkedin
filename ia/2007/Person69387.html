Paul Smith’s Summary

My specialty is making complex subjects simple and understandable for a variety of audiences. In June 2007 I got my MBA in Sustainable Management from Presidio in San Francisco, which has considerably strengthened my business skills and helped make me particularly attuned to how to do business in a way that's more profitable, well managed, and ecologically sound.

As an intern at GreenHome.com I led the revamping of a majority of the site content and helped negotiate collaborative partnerships.

In addition to this is 10 years experience running Traveling Massage, a successful private and corporate on site business. In doing so, I've interacted with 1000s of people from all walks of life, and as a result have developed keen insight on what makes people tick, and how to bring out their potential. I am also the founder of the 600+ member Bliss holistic practitioner association, which has served to create a stronger, more cohesive community since 1999.

All of the above has helped develop my skills in writing, organizing, motivating, and engaging groups of people in unified visions, activities, events, and projects.

Currently I'm transitioning to the Nevada County area in August. I look forward to working for a company or companies that are sustainability minded or would like to be more so. My goal is to help companies save money, be more profitable, and contribute to the health of the planet, all the while reaching a larger customer base. Coming soon I'll also be offering consulting services in this same area.
