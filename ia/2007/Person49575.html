Kyle Hunt | kyle@hunthosting.com’s Summary

My experience and expertise is in working for and with small businesses. The Internet is giving savvy small business owners the ability to generate a solid stream of new business. Great websites and smart local search marketing can be excellent ways to grow their business very cost effectively.

Traditional print Yellow Pages are on their way out and several other "traditional" marketing methods are losing their effectiveness as media becomes more and more fragmented.

I aim to help small businesses create an online presence that produces results. Are you apologetic or proud of your website? A great website should be the centerpiece of your marketing and a place you happily point potential and current clients.

Why else do I spend many of my waking hours helping small business owners? Because my wife and I have a beautiful baby boy…and those diapers are darn expensive...
