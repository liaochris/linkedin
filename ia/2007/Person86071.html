Tim Tuohy’s Summary

I was honorably discharged from the United States Air Force September 11, 1981. Twenty years later the most vicious and calculated attack ever accomplished on the United States was executed by Muslim Terrorists. "I guess they figured it was finally safe for them to do it."

I write books for a hobby. My readers benefit from my extensive training and education, the experience from my early days in the United States Air Force, as well my travels throughout the North America, the Caribbean, and South American continent. As these bring reality and excitement the novels and stories I write.

My technological career accomplishments include design and construction of a mobile command center for disaster and emergency response as well as many highly technical designs and installations in data centers and networks.

In 1992 I began writing a book that has become The Journey. It was in that book that I first began exploring a future of man, an Odyssey if you will, of one potential end of mankind. Later, when I wrote The Penitentiary of Lost Souls, (now titled Demons and Prophets) I discovered that many of the same threads wound through it.

I am currently a Systems Programmer on IBM zSeries Servers.
