Ivo Georgiev’s Summary

Ivo Georgiev is a Senior Consultant focusing on information security. He has over 16 years of experience in both management and technical roles working as a IT system architect, security engineer and consultant in the financial, government, health and transportation sectors.

Working for international companies on high profile projects has provided an in-depth knowledge of the complex requirements for their business and technical needs. Recent North American organizations include Vancouver Coastal Health, BC Lottery Corporation, Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Kelowna Flightcraft, GrowthWorks Capital, Canadian Western Trust, Xynyth Manufacturing, Legal Services Society of BC, Squamish Terminals, LDA+YW Architects, Calgary Board of Education, International Petroreal Oil Company, Pacific Honda, Xantrex, the cities of Port Coquitlam, Port Moody and Abbotsford.

Ivo Georgiev has worked with clients to build complete security architectures that include policies, strategies and technical solutions designed to enable their business operations. Mr. Georgiev has assisted companies in preparing for information systems audits. He has also performed privacy and security assessments of client systems.

Using his analytical and research skills, Ivo Georgiev demonstrates success in creating technical and business process strategies, developing implementation plans, managing the implementation process and performing system reviews.

Ivo communicates effectively with all levels of organization, senior executives, technical and business staff. He is objective oriented and capable of assuming responsibilities promptly and with effectiveness. He has held leadership positions in financial and consulting organizations.

Mr. Georgiev excels at recruiting, motivating and retaining quality professionals that consistently deliver. He has an entrepreneurial spirit, and has identified and communicated business opportunities and led teams to achieve successful results.

Ivo Georgiev’s Specialties:

Accreditations include the internationally recognized CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) designation awarded by (ISC)2 as well as an undergraduate degree, Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Computer Science from the University of Ljubljana, Slovenia.
