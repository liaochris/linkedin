Michiel Terlouw’s Summary

I am an information designer / user experience consultant with a broad range of responsibilies, including:
- advising on the merits of new concepts and technologies (e.g. whether to incorporate multimedia on a website, for what purpose, and in what form).
- overseeing the development of existing websites and applications (e.g. is
the e-business website reaching its marketing targets, how to improve it)
- creating the information architecture for new websites and application
- creating a detailed information design
- performing user research and usability testing

Current projects:
- Continuous improvements on www.rabobank.nl
- A new subwebsite for www.rabobank.com to sell complex financial products
- Rabobank Mobile
- Rabobank TV and video
- Interactive modules on www.rabobank.nl using rich internet technlogies
- Conversion rates improvements in the mortgages section of rabobank.nl

Michiel Terlouw’s Specialties:

Specialties:
I enjoy joining forces with online marketeers to see how we can satisfy users, while improving conversion rates and profitability. I have a solid background in usability and user experience, but I also understand the need for marketeers to push products and reach sales targets.
