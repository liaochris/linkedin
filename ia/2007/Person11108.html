Bill Bliss’s Summary

A trusted advisor with over 25 years of experience coaching and developing business owners, CEOs, Presidents, family business owners and other senior leaders. Extensive experience in pharmaceutical, retail, publishing, technology, financial services, transportation services, real estate, construction and other industries. My personal mission is to provide trusted advice to executives enabling them to maximize the use of their unique talents and gifts.

Bill Bliss’s Specialties:

Executive Coaching for CEO, President and Vice President levels focusing on enhancing leadership skills; Assimilation Coaching of executives new to an organization to ensure success; Meeting facilitation for strategic planning, family councils and team building; talent and core competency assessments; performance management, change management. I am most effective in helping organizations that are experiencing leadership dysfunction overcome the negative impacts of the dysfunction.
