Richard Robinson’s Summary

Leading world-wide authority on The HUON Solution. As CTO of The HUON Corporation was responsible for defining product strategy and communicating this to the client base at a senior level.

Responsible for designing and implementing the renewal and underwriting rule areas of The HUON Solution. Provided consultancy for a number of clients across the globe regarding renewals procedures. Worked with The Innovation Group to develop a version of renewals and rules that would work within a services environment.

Clients have included major insurance organisations throughout the world such as CSAA, ACSC, ACG, Travelers (all USA), Kemper, Guardian, Royal, AXA (all Canada), Direct Line, Privilege, Prudential, CGNU, Lloyds TSB, Halifax, Zurich, RSA (all UK), Folksam (Sweden) and NRMA (Australia).

Currently providing a variety of services to the insurance industry on both a fixed price project and hourly basis :

- Roll-out of new product, line of business or territory
- Implementation of rules and rule functions
- Review of existing implementations
- Support of architecture reviews
- Support of vendor selection process
- Guidance on tailoring and customisation
- Intermediary management

Richard Robinson’s Specialties:

Requirements gathering, solution design, optimisation of business processes, integration of systems and solutions, management of technical development, definition of application architecture, process definition
