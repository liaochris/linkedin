Simone Buralli’s Summary

Dealt for several years with the design of consumer and industrial products by using CAD/CAM technologies, starting from customer's ideas and needs, taking into account geometric constraints (such as the external shell containing all the internal parts), functional constraints (e.g. simplicity of use or ease of servicing), process and technological constraints (i.e. shrinkage, undercut, constant thickness, etc.). In other words: design for easily manufactured goods.

Simone Buralli’s Specialties:

CAD modeling, Styling, Surface modeling, CAD automation applications (master modeling), 3D CAD animation, kynematics and kyneto-dynamics simulations
