A LITTLE ABOUT ME:

I am here
I am here to help
I am here to help you

It's a really big world and we are all just one person. If you are like me, you have a really big job to do and If you can't count on the law of generosity, the likelihood of your success diminishes.

What's the law of generosity you ask?

Here's my version:

Good brings good.

The universe is aware of the good thoughts, beliefs, deeds, etc. and acts in a manner of making such actions consistent. Simply stated, as I do good by you, the universe will continue doing good by me.

The rub of course is I can't help you if you don't tell me how.

Networking has to start with an expressed need. Help me help you so we can both be helped. You can do two things for me:

Understand that my main purpose here is to help create opportunities for others.

Let me know who I can help so we can all benefit.

I am motivated by helping others succeed.

I am at once an attractor a facilitator and a connector. I attract and facilitate knowledge, partnerships, opportunities and resources. I uncover needs and connect the needy with the appropriate solution provider(s). I listen to people's thoughts, help them define their desires and connect them with the resources to see them fulfilled.
