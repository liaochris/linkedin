Bart Myers’s Summary

My experience is in building early stage technology companies, this includes:
- Defining a market need,
- Detailing the vision and product strategy,
- Building the team,
- Acquiring the ideal amount of funding,
- Execution

I am playing this role now as a co-founder in a new technology company with a small and incredibly capable team with the first release for our product planned for Q1 '07. Prior to this venture I was SVP of Product Development at GUBA.com - an online video focused site, one of the first to sell Hollywood films and television online. Prior to GUBA, I managed Professional Services for several B2B and B2C start-ups. And prior to those, I worked for five years at IBM Global Services as a technology consultant.

Key questions to ask going into any endeavor:
- What "problem" are you solving?
- Are you solving "it" in a novel, better, more interesting way than your competition?
- How will you solve "it"?
- And be lucky.

Other tidbits of note:
- I am a convert to the "agile" approach to software development
- Built a platform/tools/a prototype and let the community or your users decide if you've got it right. Do it quickly, then adjust course, quickly. Repeat.
- There's nothing quite like a good challenge if you've got a great team.
