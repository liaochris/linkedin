Alyssa Miller’s Summary

Alyssa Miller serves as an Associate Vice President, in 5W Public Relations Corporate / Consumer Group.

Having been a record label publicist for such artist luminaries as, Jamiroquai, Britney Spears, NSync, Moby, and the White Stripes, Ms. Miller currently leads, manages and executes PR campaigns within the Corporate Communication & Lifestyle division at 5W Public Relations. Working on a diverse roster, her broad knowledge of multi-tiered PR campaigns and strategy,ranges from Phillips- Van Heusen, Corp., Nice Systems, The Frick Estate and Lower Manhattan Cultural Council.

Additionally at 5W, Ms. Miller manages all the lifestyle and business publicity for Elite Model Management and Wolfgang’s Vault, the premier online store for legendary impresario, Bill Grahams’ rock and roll memorabilia. Garnering consistent media placements in a variety of top-tier outlets such as the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Reuters, Inc Magazine, Crain’s, Time Magazine, CBS Sunday Morning News, and CNBC, Ms. Miller also coordinates press conferences, photo shoots, red carpet appearances, and serves as clients' spokesperson, while also writing all press materials on behalf of clients.

Recently she procured celebrities such as Christian Slater, Katharine McPhee, Richard Belzer, Kristen Cavalleri, to name a few, to endorse PVH's Arrow Brand's national ad campaign to Save Ellis Island. She has been an integral player in coordinating such high-profile events, such as The Woodsman Film Tour sponsored by Martell Cognac, Lower Manhattan Cultural Council’s Benefit honoring Senator Hillary Clinton, Rev. Al Sharpton’s 50th Birthday Party, Discovery Channel’s Norma Vally’s, “Chix Can Fix” Book Launch sponsored by Diageo’s Godivatini’s, and Britney Spear’s US Tour in 1999.

Prior to joining 5W Public Relations, Ms. Miller worked at a boutique Manhattan-based entertainment PR firm, Sony, Jive and V2 Records, based in NYC.
