  • Artist

    SteveGosling.com (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Arts and Crafts industry)

    January 1973 — Present (35 years)

    Examples of my artwork: www.stevegosling.com

    Steve Gosling is primarily a self taught artist residing on the streets of San Diego. His artwork reflects his inner most thoughts and visions. His passion for art spawns from the ability to bring to reality a world where fantasy, surrealism and fact can fuse together and create thought provoking pieces. Ever since a young kid, Steve's parents always supported his vision of being abnormally creative.

    After spending his childhood in the smoggiest city in the United States (beautiful Glendora, California), Steve fled down the coast a couple hours to find clean air. In San Diego, he obtained a B.A. degree in Art with emphasis in Graphic Design from SDSU. After graduating in 1998, Steve currently has one foot permanently stuck in the sand and resides blocks from the beach. This would explain the fluid motion in most of his artwork.
