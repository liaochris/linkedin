Asif Premji’s Summary

Asif's academic background and credentials began in Barrie, Ontario where he graduated from the Canadian Automotive Institute in 2000 with an Automotive Marketing and Strategy degree. His academic pursuit followed with a degree in Business Administation - specialized with an Automotive and International Management focus from the Northwood University in Dallas, Texas. and completed his academic endeavours in Michigan, from The DeVos Graduate School of Management in 2003 with an Integrated Management MBA.

Asif's professional, retail and managerial experience is combined with work from: High River Honda, SynVier Inc, Ideal Honda and Xprima.com (Evolio). The experience gained from the above companies enabled Asif to work with individuals to assist in business development, retail strategies, e-commerce sales processes, brand awareness and creation and other business related tasks. Asif continuously enjoys dealing with customers, co-workers and fellow managers in a team-oriented, enthusiastic and family-oriented atmosphere while demonstrating leadership, creativity and exemplary written and oral communication skills. His in-depth problem solving skills, self-motivation and excellent bilingual communication abilities allows Asif to interact with potential customers and fellow co-workers, thereby creating fulfililng relationships based on trust, friendship and integrity.

Apart from his professional experiences, Asif has held various leadership positions in educational institutions and business enterprises including the President of Marketing, Vice-President of an MBA Senate, and Vice-President of Marketing for 2B1 Teknologies. Asif was also honoured as a guest speaker at Outstanding Business Leaders Awards Gala in 2002 where Fortune 500 company CEO's were awarded Leadership Awards. He also served on the Canadian Automotive Institute Marketing Department for the development of new advertising tactics, marketing images and enrolment of qualified individuals.
