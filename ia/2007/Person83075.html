Suhail Iqbal’s Summary

I have been involved with a number of organizational scenarios and have handled various large diverse mega-projects from all fields of activity. Had been a pioneer in introducing the concept of Digital Mapping, Geographic Information System, Data Warehousing and Data Mining in Pakistan.

Chairman of Asia Pacific Federeation of Project Management (APFPM) and Project Management Association of Pakistan (PMAP), Sponsored and Past-President of PMI Pakistan Islamabad Chapter. served for two years as PMI Component Mentor for Asia Region to include Japan, China, India, Pakistan and Taiwan. Contributor in standards development of Portfolio and Programme Management, PMBOK 4th Ed and as Quality Lead and SME Lead for OPM3 2nd Edition due to release in 2008. Vice Chair of MENSA Pakistan. Board member of Professional Risk Managers' International Association (PRMIA) Pakistan Chapter.

Graduated from Master level course of PMI Leadership Class of 2005, 4th Leadership course of PMI with highly competitive multi-cultural audience and evaluation by Center of Creative Leadership (CLC). Currently serving as Member on Leadership Institute Advisory Group (LIAG) of PMI.

Research Scholar of Strategic and Project Management at ISGI ESC Lille, France. Awarded PMI's Gary Christle Scholarship 2004-2005 for the PhD. Research focuses on various aspects of Organizational Maturity.

Actively involved in promotion and awareness of Project Management in Pakistan and surrounding Region. Representing Valense Organizational Consultants in Middle East with offices in Abu Dhabi.

Myself a Corporate Trainer and PM Consultant with PMP and Prince2 Practitioner Certifications. Only Global PMI REP in Pakistan. Working towards acquiring OPM3 Certified Assessor and Consultant Certifications.

Regularly networks with fellow professionals through teleconferences and PMI Leadership Meetings/Global Congresses.
