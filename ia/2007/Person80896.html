Danielle & Jim Corliss’s Summary

Sotheby's International Realty is proud to have Danielle and Jim Corliss as part of their organization representing Orange County, California. Danielle very much enjoys real estate and her clients - their satisfaction and comfort levels are important to her. Danielle's background includes management and insurance as well as serving 17 years on the Board of Directors of two Homeowner Associations. She teaches the contract classes for the agents in her company and has been a REALTOR for over 10 years. Further, she is a Broker which means she is able to provide added Real Estate knowledge and experience in order to benefit her clients that much more. Using her business background, combined with extensive area knowledge and marketing savvy, she is able to assist her clients with their real estate needs. Danielle uses her strong negotiating skills to get her buyers the best value for their new home. Likewise, she uses her comprehensive marketing strategy and sales skills to get sellers' homes maximum exposure through cutting edge marketing ideas and utilizing state of the art technology. Danielle is half of a husband and wife team. She and her husband, Jim, truly enjoy working together and by maximizing each of their strengths they benefit their clients greatly. Rarely will you see them apart and always you will see them respecting each other and valuing the work they do.

Danielle & Jim Corliss’s Specialties:

Jim has built 2 homes and has had plenty of first hand experience in the home construction process. He adds this "field" experience to the team which comes in handy when it comes to the physical inspection of properties.
