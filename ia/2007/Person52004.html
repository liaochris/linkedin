Dr. Earl R. Smith II’s Summary

I am pleased to announce the launch of a new International Fishing Club. The URL is www.shoalnreef.com. Shoal N Reef is dedicated to:

Helping you become a more successful fisherman

Advancing the sport of fishing - particularly saltwater gamefishing

Organizing members-only fishing trips to the greatest fishing spots on the globe

Providing its members with access to the latest information on destinations, equipment, imaging and fishing strategies

Providing a place where fishermen can meet and exchange information and fishing stories

Supporting conservation and catch-and-release tournaments

Registration is free – get immediate access to such useful information as fishing knot tutorials, fish identification, weather forecasting, the best fishing techniques and much, much more. Then visit the site regularly, we are always adding new content.

You can also join the Shoal N Reef Fishing Club and receive discounts on fishing equipment, travel, destinations and invitations to the ‘Members Only’ fishing trips to the best fishing holes in the world.

Enjoy the website. The first issue of Shoal N Reef – our monthly newsletter – will come out in November. It will let you know about new content on the site, planned events and other Shoal N Reef activities. In it we will also announce our first schedule of fishing trips. I can tell you that the very first trip will be to our adopted home port - Cabo san Lucas - in September or October of next year. The marlin will be there in force and so will Shoal N Reef!

Dr. Earl R. Smith II’s Specialties:

Dr. Smith is a proven entrepreneur, seasoned senior executive, political and social theorist, published author and speaker. A veteran of Wall Street, he has founded two non-profit and six for-profit companies.
