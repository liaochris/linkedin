Gregoris Mentzas’s Summary

Professor of Information Management at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) and Director of the Information Management Unit (IMU), a multidisciplinary research unit at the University.

Gregoris Mentzas’s Specialties:

My area of expertise is information technology management and my research concerns the integration of knowledge management, semantic web and e-service technologies.Specific application domains include corporate knowledge management and e-government.

Gregoris Mentzas’s Experience

  • Professor

    National Technical University of Athens

    (Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Research industry)

    November 1990 — Present (17 years)

    Dr Gregoris Mentzas is Professor of Information Technology Management at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the National Technical University of Athens and Director of the Information Management Unit (IMU) a multidisciplinary research unit at the Institute of Communication and Computer Systems (ICCS) of the University; see http://www.imu.iccs.gr.

    During 2006-2009 he also serves in the Board of Directors of the Institute of Communication and Computer Systems.

    His area of expertise is information technology management and his research concerns the integration of knowledge management, semantic web and e-service technologies in e-government and e-business settings.

    He has received many contracts, grants and awards from funding bodies and was/is principal investigator in more than 30 international research projects.

  • Industrial Experience

    Planet SA

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    Partner in charge of information and KM and director of research and innovation. I have led assignments, like:

    • Corporate strategy for the Public Power Corporation (with McKinsey)
    • Integrated information system for Ministry of National Economy
    • Strategic IT Plan for the Centre of Environmental Research
    • Strategic Study for the Modernisation of Hellenic Post S.A

  • Senior Consultant

    Technisystems

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    1990 — 1992 (2 years)

    Senior consultant in the project Large-Scale Correct Systems Using Formal Methods (ESPRIT, 5383). Main involvement in the design and development of an office information system that handles the business processes and information flows in a shipping company.   • IT System Administrator

    Hellenic Navy, Navy Logistics Center

    (Military industry)

  • Senior researcher
