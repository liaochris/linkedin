Laurie Geodakov’s Summary

My goal is to make the world a better place with easy-to-use, feel-good software -- using insightful and experienced usability analysis.

I started at the bottom of the user experience ladder as a technical support engineer at Microsoft. After speaking to more than 25,000 end users, I decided I would rather work on improving the software than field complaints. As a usability specialist, I enjoy finding problem areas and being able to suggest remedies long before the product hits the users.

Laurie Geodakov’s Specialties:

Contextual inquiry, target audience definition, task analysis, conducting interviews, rapid screen and navigational prototyping, heuristic analysis, usability testing, qualitative statistics, quantitative statistics, questionaire design, SPSS. A special talent for describing complex systems in easy to understand, bite-sized pieces.
