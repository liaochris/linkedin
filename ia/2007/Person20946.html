Dave Clarke’s Summary

You can contact me anytime by email on dc @ nrg-networks.com (remove spaces) or text +447768330330.

My expertise is in helping Entrepreneurs and Professionals who are uncomfortable with traditional selling to generate rapid results with relationship based businesses development.

Many professionals & business owners are not salespeople. Communicating their real value and the difference they make in compelling commercial terms can be difficult. NRG specialises in helping them do that in a fun relaxing way so that members quickly establish their route to market by building business relationships based on trust and knowledge.

Rather than look for a continuous supply of one off transactions NRG helps its members build a close trusted network of advocates to accelerate their business development.

We are looking to expand the network throughout the UK during 2007 by franchising and internationally from 2008. If you know anyone with a passion for helping others and real business development expertise then I'd like to talk to them. My contact details are above.
