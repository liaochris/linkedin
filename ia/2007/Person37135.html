Jaap Schekkerman’s Summary

Enterprise Architecture Consulting Services (EACS).

EACS is delivering consulting services based on the EA methods, frameworks, models, approaches and guides developed by the Institute For Enterprise Architecture Developments (IFEAD). See for detailed information the website of EACS. http://www.ea-consulting.com

Institute For Enterprise Architecture Developments (IFEAD).

The founder and thought leader of IFEAD & EACS Mr. Jaap Schekkerman will act as an Advisor and Opinion Leader in the areas of Business Technology Strategy and Enterprise Architecture for VKA besides his research and development activities for IFEAD.

Several articles and books related to EA are published by Jaap Schekkerman. On the website of the institute you can find a lot more information about these publications. http://www.enterprise-architecture.info or http://www.enterprise-architecture.eu

You are invited to bring new opportunities to my attention!

The EACS activities for the Netherlands are deliverd in close co-operation with Verdonck, Klooster & Associates.

Verdonck, Klooster & Associates (VKA) a professional independent consulting firm specialized in Program Management, Outsourcing, Telecom, Process Improvement, Information-Management and Enterprise Architecture is using methods and approaches from the Institute For Enterprise Architecture Developments (IFEAD) to deliver these services.

Jaap Schekkerman’s Specialties:

Jaap Schekkerman is working for more then 30 years in the Business, IT & Consultants world in several roles and responsibilities and he has more then 25 years experience in the areas of Business Technology Strategy and Enterprise Architecture.

He has coached and managed complex and large enterprise architecture programs in the Defence world, the Governmental area, Health Care environment, Travel Industry Financial Serviecs and High Tech Industry.
