Keith Winter’s Summary

Chief Operating Officer of Exponential Interactive (parent company of Tribal Fusion).

Exponential™ is a profitable, rapidly growing, technology-driven media services company that delivers innovative products and services to meet the demands of today’s digital advertisers. The Exponential™ group of businesses enable online advertisers to reach their target audiences through our Tribal Fusion, FullTango, LeadGenuity, EchoTopic, and MetroTip divisions. Our businesses represent over 1,000 of the top publishers on the web and are the partner of choice for many of the world’s leading advertising agencies and advertisers for their online campaigns. Through our proprietary ad serving technology, we generate 20 billion online ad impressions per month, enabling advertisers to reach over 70% of the US online population and 200 million consumers worldwide.
