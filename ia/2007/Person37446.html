Jagdish Joshi’s Summary

Jagdish Joshi, Ph.D.
Senior Partner,
Core Strategies, Inc.

Jagdish is a seasoned executive with 20 years industry experience in Engineering, IT Governance, and Application development for financial, technology, and media / entertainment market verticals.

His recent focus has been on Business (New Ventures) and Enterprise (Risk Management, Architecture, Governance, Security, BC/DR) issues facing large financial institution (New Century Mortgage Corp, IndyMac Bank, etc). He has consulted in aligning the operational environment with organization’s strategic priorities with agility and speed.

Jagdish holds B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay and an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Notre Dame, Indiana. In addition, he holds M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer and Systems Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), New York. He is member of Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA) and International Information Systems Security Certification Consortium, Inc. (ISC)2. Jagdish is President of Southern California IIT Bombay Chapter.
