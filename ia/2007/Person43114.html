John O'Toole’s Summary

I am a business planner and development adviser to start-ups and media companies, keeping one eye out for my next big challenge.

My Goals:

To join the leadership team of an innovative business, nonprofit or promising startup where I will play a key role achieving sustainable growth using my strategic skills, marketing and business expertise. I hope to create or join a workplace devoted equally to results and to best practices

My Profile:

Creative business leader with proven expertise and diverse experience

•Accomplished business-builder, strategist, negotiator and marketer

•Extensive hands-on management experience in key business disciplines including financial reporting, strategic planning, workplace improvement, HR, sales, marketing, editorial (content), production, customer acquisition, trademarks, events, branding, direct marketing, web, and more

•As a custom publisher, developed and nurtured high-level client relationships with big corporations, associations and government agencies

• Demonstrated success in growth and transition: turnarounds, launches, acquisitions, expansion, restructuring

• Innovative thinker with wide-ranging interests and knowledge

My Background:

Until recently, I ran Mediamerica, Inc., the magazine company behind Oregon Business, Oregon Home and Travel Oregon. I left the company in late 2006 to reinvent my career. Before moving here in 1997, I held a succession of jobs in national media including editorial roles at Backpacker and Folio: magazines, and production, operational and executive roles at larger publishing companies. I’ve worked on many magazines, including Eating Well, Harrowsmith Country Life, New England Monthly, Catalog Age, Direct, and various web projects, newsletters and custom publications (what’s a custom publication?) .
