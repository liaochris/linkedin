Raj Ramachandran’s Summary

Raj is responsible for assisting clients in conduct strategic learning needs assessments, service provider due diligence, final requirements development and service delivery for learning, performance management, and talent management. Raj is considered a subject matter expert in developing and implementing outsourced Learning & Performance solutions and has led major Learning Management Systems (LMS) technology implementations and content development initiatives.
Mr. Ramachandran has been responsible for managing the implementation of complex, large-scale learning outsourcing projects in an HR environment. Mr. Ramachandran has implemented and worked with such Learning & Performance software packages as SAP LS, SumTotal Systems (formerly known as Docent), Saba/THINQ, SuccessFactors, KnowledgePlanet, Macromedia, and Interwise. He has led strategic learning assessments and contract negotiations for Client engagements and has managed major content development efforts.
