    Swing Dynamite

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry)

    September 2006 — Present (6 months)

We train a troupe of dancers to perform dances from the 20's to 50's, including Charleston, acrobatic Lindy Hop and others. We offer dance classes, organize numerous swing dancing events, and provide live entertainment through swing dancing shows.

    SAF Entertainment Group

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    November 2001 — Present (5 years 4 months)

    SAF Entertainment focuses primarily on two areas: entertainment and dance instruction. We provide entertainment for events such as corporate functions, specializing in vintage music and dance performances inspired by the 20's to 50's (Charleston, Swing, Rock 'n Roll). Our dancers have travelled the world to teach and perform. We also offer dance instruction in dances of these same eras at our own studio and at several locations in Montreal, and we organize swing events at our studio/bar and other local establishments.
