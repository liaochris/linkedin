Teodora Hristoforova’s Summary

More than nine years of working on national and internationally funded economic development projects.
Extensive experience in Grant schemes, microfinance and local economic development.
Has worked with the Bulgarian Ministry of Economy and Energy and Ministry of Labour and Social Policy.
Expertise in developing and evaluating Phare project proposals. Has performed evaluation and selection of project proposals under different grant schemes within UNDP and Phare. Has advised Bulgarian Business Centres on preparation of project proposals under EC Phare regulation.
Significant expertise in design and implementation of training on employment promotion and business support. Trainer of ILO “Start Your Business” programme under the International Labour Office and “Start Your Tourism Business” programme. Has also provided training for Business centres’ staff on business development and job creation.
Profound knowledge of issues relating to integration and employment of ethnic minorities. Has worked on several projects supporting business development and job creation in regions with ethnic minorities.
