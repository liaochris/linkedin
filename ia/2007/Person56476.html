Matt Gill’s Summary

As Senior Vice President, I oversee the executive search division of Pile & Company. Pile & Company is a leader in advertising agency review consulting as well as executive retained search within the marketing and communications field. Prior to joining Pile and Company, I ran the Northeast division of a national recruiting firm. My experience includes successful start ups in staffing as well as other industries.
