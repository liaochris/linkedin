Collin Douma’s Summary

Collin Douma is a Social Media expert. He combines his 12+ years of internet advertising, branding, marketing, e-commerce and property building into a focused Social Media practice.

Collin offers advice, strategy and training on the applied business use of social media, as well as "hands on" assistance in tactical execution of projects and programs.

Over the years, Collin has worked with many clients including Coca-Cola, Fido, Sears, Singer, Placer Dome and Purdue amongst others. Recently, he has offered social media strategy and tactical executions for most divisions at GM Canada and now the Ford Motor Co. and Subsidiaries in the USA.

Collin is always seeking new business relationships:

• If you are a marketing, communication, broadcasting or advertising professional, and you need a grasp of this amazing emerging media, contact him for a discussion on what social media can do for you, your organization and your product.

• If you are aspiring to create, code or otherwise develop social media programs and properties, there is great interest in creating strategic partnership with you too.

You can contact and read all about Collin's Social Media discoveries and best practices on his blog at:

http://www.radicaltrust.ca
