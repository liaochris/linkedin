Curtis J. Morley’s Summary

Agilix
I am very excited to be part of Agilix. It is an amazing company with phenomenal people. The best thing about Agilix is that it is a company that will make a difference in the world.

mediaRAIN
Prior to forming mediaRAINI worked with many great companies. My goals were to place myself in an environment that I could work to learn rather than earn. Even though I received nice wages and pay increases with each company that I worked for, my objective was to be in an environment that would teach me the things necessary to running a successful business. I was able to help create business plans, negotiate $5M contracts, encounter HR, Legal, investor relations, and BusDev issues, and learn what works and what doesn't.

I gained a love a Macromedia Flash and have been fortunate enough to have chosen the right technology to base my business around. I became the first instructor in the state to teach Flash at the University Level. I also ranked 2nd on the international Brainbench Flash 5 exam and helped in the creation of the future exams. mediaRAINis known as the Flash House in Utah and the Mountain West. We are the only Macromedia Flash Alliance Partner in Utah and have all but one of the Flash Certified Developers/designers that work for us.

I am a frequent speaker and am on advisory boards and Board of Directors for many colleges, non-profits, and businesses.
