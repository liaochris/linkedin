Rupert Hollom’s Summary

Respected Technical Architect and solutions expert who has worked for a number of international organisations, government departments, the EU and UN. Instrumental in the architectural design of the UN International Transaction Log and Data Exchange Standards for international CO2 emissions trading under the Kyoto protocol. Architected solutions to integrate government systems with the Government Gateway and developed a portal to allow smaller departments to quickly and easily utilise the facilities offered by the Government Gateway.
