Matthew Bromley’s Summary

I am currently working for Kaos Studios on Frontlines: Fuel of War. I am a Professional Level Designer experienced with Unreal Engine 3.0. I have been working with this engine since May 2006.
Hobbiest level designer with Unreal Engine 2.5 (UT2004) from May 2004 to present.
Experience in creating Single & Multiplayer levels.
Experienced in creating gameplay areas from text and/or sketches.
Experienced in decorating the spaces using existing art assets.
Experienced in lighting spaces.
Experienced in scripting AI & sequences using Kismet.
Proficient in creating particle effects and materials.
Beginner in modeling/UVing/Texturing Static Meshes for level decoration using Maya

Goals: Learn and improve at everything I do.
