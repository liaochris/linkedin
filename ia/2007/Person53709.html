Manoj Parikatil’s Summary

Organizations, whatever its size or nature, requires effective people at the key points of influence, decision making and accountability. Finding the right fit is a complex challenge and I ensure that challenge is met by finding exceptional talent for clients.

As a seasoned Executive Search Consultant, I strongly believe that excellence in executive search depends on the degree of understanding & perception of the business need of clients
I focus on understanding the nature of client's business and drawing strategies to attract and develop high caliber candidates.
I have helped clients find the best match for each position, exceeding expectations not only for the successful candidate's qualifications but also for the way he/she fits into organization's culture.
The aim is to help client's achieve the highest possible return on their human capital investment through a strong combination of experience in executive search with global perspective of businesses.
I have been working with some of the most admired organizations and have successfully helped them build leadership teams for different business practices/functions.
Major clients are from Analytics, Consulting, Research, Banking, Capital Markets, Investment Banking/Management, Asset Management and High-Tech Industry.

Few of our clients include brands like McAfee,Yahoo,Deloitte,Wipro,Microsoft,Sitel,Genpact,24/7 customer and many more.....


I would be interested in partnering with organizations who are planning to set up their operations in India and help them hire the best talent, particularly the CEO/Board Level positions.
