Osbert Lancaster’s Summary

Sustainability and ethical enterprise consultant, educator and activist committed to working with individuals and organisations who want to improve their social and environmental performance.

Clients and project partners, past and present, include: the Scottish Parliament (responsible purchasing strategy); Traidcraft plc (environmental impact of supply chain); Lumison (environmental and social performance); University of Strathclyde (MSc in Human Ecology; WWF-UK (management education for sustainability).

Osbert Lancaster’s Specialties:

sustainability review, social impact assessment, economic impact assessment, ethical procurement, ecological footprinting, customer and competitor analysis, fairtrade, social enterprise, ethical enterprise, sustainability strategies
