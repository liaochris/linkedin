Nestor Lopez-Duran’s Summary

PhD level statistical and analytics consultant and practicing research scientist. I own Pink Elephant Analytics, a statistical and analytics consulting firm, and work as a research fellow in Affective Neuroscience at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center. I have extensive research and data analysis experience with expert command of SPSS, SAS, and SQL. I routinely use econometrics, general linear models, mixed modeling, cluster and factor analysis, HLM, & SEM in both my academic and consulting work.

Prior to my appointment to the University of Pittsburgh I was the Vice-President and director of development and acquisitions for Explorart Films, an adventure, science, and arts based independent film production company. I am part owner of this company and currently serve in the company's board of directors.

I am interested in networking with professionals in all fields and in providing statistical consultation services to small businesses, attorneys, executives, and market research firms.

Nestor Lopez-Duran’s Specialties:

Statistical consultation (general linear models, typical and atypical regression modeling, econometrics, survival analysis, all types of significant testing). SPSS, SAS, and SQL.

Independent film development, financing, distribution, and acquisitions.
