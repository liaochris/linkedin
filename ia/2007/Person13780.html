Bud Caddell’s Summary

Bud proudly joined the Imagination team in November 2006 as Managing Editor of PMI.org. After earning English Literature and Marketing degrees from The University of Texas at Austin, Bud decided to move to Chicago to pursue the only career that seemed a natural fit for his studies -- publishing. Working at his first creative agency at age 14, co-authoring a popular Flash textbook at age 16, leading development at a venture funded start-up at 18, and building an online asset management firm in Austin at 21 -- Bud has pursued an expansive skill-set to suit the ever-shifting development needs of the online environment. Most recently, Bud worked as Project Manager for New Media Development at Wencel/Hess in Chicago where he focused heavily on rich-media production and search engine marketing. Outside of the office, Bud can be found jotting story ideas down for his creative writing group or recording and playing music.
