Christian Unruh’s Summary

Licensed to practice law in Louisiana and Florida specializing in music, media and entertainment generally representing musicians, radio and television personalities, record labels, webdesigners, film producers et als.
Owner of China Shop Music, indie record label and music promotion company distributed by Fontana and home to Girls in Hawaii, Gisli, and many other artists. Currently licensing clients music to various companies and film/tv projects.

Seven years working on major label projects doing promotion and marketing at A.R.M.S. Division in Santa Monica.

An enjoyable stint of A/R at Food Chain Records.

Co-Founder of www.fastband.com a digital music portal and 24/7 webcaster a bit ahead of it's time that signed digital distribution deals with David Gray and many other artists who later had considerable success.

Original creator, music director and then program director of 106.1 the Zephyr, an alternative rock station in New Orleans. College radio at WTUL-New Orleans and high school radio at KOTO- Telluride.

Two time Ironman Triathlon World Championship finisher currently competing with the Miami Beach Rowing Club.

Christian Unruh’s Specialties:

Contract negotiation, Record Distribution, Music Clearance, Music Placement.
