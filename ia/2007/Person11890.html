Bob Ingram’s Summary

Seasoned executive with over 25 years of outstanding success in telecommunicatons planning, engineering, plant operations, product development and acquisition integration. Based on a solid formal education consisting of BS and MBA degrees from the University of Illinois, career growth through increasingly challenging positions led to senior management roles in four companies. These positions provide a breadth of experience with planning and implementing technological change, including the difficult challenge of instituting internal process change, while effectively managing both capital and expense budgets, and augmented with project management and system integration planning and execution experience.

Bob Ingram’s Specialties:

Accomplished team builder, engaging multi-functional resources to deal with needed changes or opportunities.

Effective capital budget manager.

Astute observer of underlying technological trends. Led initiatives in 5 companies to introduce new services base on contemporary emerging technologies: ISDN, ATM, FR, DSL and IP.

Listens well to customer needs and effectively supports product development to design projects to meet those needs.
