Neil Ross’s Summary

Currently serving as the CTO / Director of Enterprise Architecture for the Pennsylvania Department of Labor & Industry. I am responsible for the daily operation of the bureau and three IT divisions comprised of 50+ high-level technical engineers. My organization is responsibile for architectural oversight on all technology projects within the agency as well as for the development and enforcement of IT standards across the Office of Information Technology.

Have served as Application Development Manager for the Pennsylvania Office of Attorney General. Specializing in software architectureal concepts, design and implementation of web-based applications.

Long-time ColdFusion developer and owner of Codesweeper, a ColdFusion application development shop. Author of articles and Inside ColdFusion MX, ColdFusion book from New Riders. Frequent speaker at ColdFusion and Macromedia conferences, user-group meetings and other technical events.

Neil Ross’s Specialties:
