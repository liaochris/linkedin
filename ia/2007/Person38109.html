Janet Fouts’s Summary

Flash
We’ve been coding Flash since the early “it’s only good for animation” days. Flash has grown way beyond it’s promise and we’ve grown with it. From developing modules for hand-held delivery, flash based blogs, games, e-learning modules, product demos and websites, we find Flash to be one of our favorite tools.

Web development
My first website was in 1993. Back then code wassimple and to the point. We still hand-code 90% of our websites. We don’t go overboard with code that doesn’t look consistent from browser to browser because that defeats the purpose of every website–communicating your message. We’ve expanded our range to PHP, Javascript, AJAX and CSS. We have copy editors and writers available to tailor that message and award winning designers to make it stand out in a crowd.

E-learning
We design learning modules in HTML and Flash. It all depends on the needs of the client.
E-learning work ranges from cooking demonstrations and interactive manager training to a series of workplace safety courses.

Product Demos
Because we’re located in the Silicon Valley, there are a lot of tech companies needing their products to stand above the crowd. We work closely with the client to extract the best of each product and create an user interface that is easy to navigate.

Social networks and community
As founder of ONTHERAIL.com I had plenty of opportunities to experiment with a number of ways to bind users together in a viral network.

Web Strategy
We’ve been on the web for a long time. It’s no longer a matter of build a website and they will come. We can help create a marketing strategy that will help get you noticed. WE can work on Search Engine optimizations strategies, help you target your goals and create a presentation that will deliver your message and engage your users.
