Ronald Stead’s Summary

Ron is a native of the beautiful Shenandoah Valley of Virginia. Shenandoah County remains his home, where he owns several real estate investment properties of his own including a beautiful 53 acre farm. Ron has been an active Realtor for more than 5 years and has recently acquired his Associate Broker license. In trying to keep the business in the family, Ron's oldest daughter is also a licensed Realtor. Ron is also a licensed Class "A" General Contractor in Virginia, and is an remains active as a real estate investor himself. Ron's background before he began his real estate career was as a Plant Engineer & Controls Engineer in local manufacturing facilities in the valley. He continues these pursuits as a Plant Consultant on an as-needed basis for local manufacturing facilities. Ron is a veteran, having served 4 years in the U.S. Army before being Honorably Discharged. He then re-started his educational pursuits and received his Associate Degree in Applied Science from Lord Fairfax Community College in 1988 and his Bachelors of Science in Technology & Management from the University of Maryland University College in 1995.
Ron looks forward to bringing you unparalled service with his teammates at Sherry Wilson & Co., the Re/Max leader while helping you and your family with your Real Estate needs in the Shenandoah Valley and the Northern Virginia area.
