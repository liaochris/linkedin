Tom Granger’s Summary

I split my time between managing a Web Development Company - Florentine Design & Marketing (http://www.florentinedesign.com) - and owning an interest in several Internet properties (TemplateKit.com, NeoPolitics.com, EasyQuizBuilder.com).

I also have the pleasure of serving on advisory boards for several start-up companies as well as the Northshore YMCA Board of Directors.

We own or produce products for about 20 different websites, including http://www.templatekit.com & the http://www.proposalkits.com line of products which are sold through more than 4,000 resellers worldwide.

Our services group consults on PHP & MySQL development, AJAX application and Rich Media Flash development. We have offices in Bothell & Redmond and employ 8 people.
