Hudson is a division of Hudson Highland Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: HHGP), one of the world’s leading professional staffing, retained executive search and talent management providers.

Hudson Canada operates a generalist firm and has successfully served companies in most industry sectors. Our success is due to our strong client and candidate relationships and experienced professional staff. We also offer extensive industry research capabilities supported by one of the largest research teams in the Canadian selection industry.

Hudson consists of two integrated divisions covering staffing and outsourcing and talent management.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Our search service is built upon conducting thorough candidate market research to bring forward the best possible selection of potential candidates who meet the requirements you have specified. Candidate development is a multi-tiered process that relies on sourcing and identifying both organizations and people who are likely to be ideal fits for a particular position.

The rigor of our process allows us to guarantee that you will hire when utilizing our search service, so long as the position specifications and the market conditions are reasonable.

Once interested and qualified candidates are identified, thorough assessments are conducted based on specific needs. Detailed reference checks are conducted on the final candidates. The entire process is documented so that you may review the data we have compiled on the assignment at any time. Four to six weeks is the usual time frame for completing the search service.

We are a fully retained search firm and each assignment is invoiced in one-third increments.
