Mitch Liu’s Summary

Prior to Adteractive and salesforce.com, from 2002 to 2004, Mitch served as the interim head of marketing for www.xfire.com, a DFJ and NEA backed startup developing an online IM gaming platform, recently acquired by MTV Networks for $102 million in cash. Mitch currently serves as an advisor to www.searchforce.com, a search engine marketing management startup; and www.stepup.com, a local shopping service startup recently acquired by Intuit for $60 million in cash.

From 1998-2001, Mitch co-founded and served as VP Marketing at Elantix Corporation. He managed the development and launch of the ePartner sofware-as-a-service. Elantix was acquired in November 2000 by Partnerware, Inc. where he served as a subject matter expert selling to Cisco Systems, Sun Microsystems, and NEC America.

Prior to Elantix from 1993 to 1997, Mitch co-founded and served as head of marketing, products and business development at Brainstorm Technologies. He established and managed key technology OEM relationships with Gupta Corporation (1994), Borland International (1995), Hitachi (1995) and Marubeni Electronics (1996) that were instrumental in the acquisition. Brainstorm's products were acquired by Percussion Software in 1997.

Mitch Liu’s Specialties:

Over 10 years of product marketing and management experience in the Internet, online pay-for-performance direct marketing, and software-as-a-service space.
