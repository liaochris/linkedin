Kristie Wells’s Summary

I am a versatile generalist who ‘specializes’ in wearing multiple hats (project/event management, business development, partner/client relations, marketing, legal, HR, PR, etc.) and doing whatever it takes to get the job done. I am seen as a proactive leader who sees a situation, assesses it, makes a plan, takes charge, and makes it better. I am not an expert in technology, but keep myself educated on the tools that exist to ensure upmost efficiency in my day-to-day life.

I have a love for building relationships and mentoring others, with an insatiable curiosity and deep need to make a difference. I believe you can do anything you put your mind to as long as you bring a little common sense and a lot of positivity to the table. A little cliche'ish - yes. But true.

In 2005, I co-founded the non-profit organization BrainJams (http://www.brainjams.org) to provide a platform where groups can share insights and experiences to accelerate the pace of positive change and innovation in the world.

In 2006, we launched Social Media Club (http://www.socialmediaclub.org) to share best practices, establish ethics and standards, and promote media literacy around the emerging area of social media (blogs, podcasts, wikis, etc.).

Both organizations epitomize all that I believe in - if you get it, share it - and I am happy to volunteer my time to helping them grow.
