    Final Thesis: "The workflow automation system model, based on the requirements set of the Malopolska Voivodship Office in Krakow"

    Abstract:
    The purpose of this thesis is to present the issue of workflow administration and electronic circulation of documents in public administration institutions. It comprises, at least as far as its functional requirements are concerned, a complete bedrock for project documentation, on the basis of which a new system of Electronic Document Circulation will be introduced at Malopolska Voivodship Office in Krakow.
    The thesis gives an outlook for theoretical aspects of workflow system designing and presents our path of designing business processes leading from issue identification, through analysis, and finally to redesigning and modelling key business processes identified at the Malopolska Voivodship Office in Krakow.
