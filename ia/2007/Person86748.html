Tom Freeman’s Summary

IT Matters arrived on the Australian IT recruitment scene in January 2000 and utilising it's unique recruitment methodology, has grown to become one of the top IT specific recruitment companies in Australia.

In September 2004, BRW Magazine included IT Matters in it's "Fast 100" list, placing us among some of the stellar perfomers in Australian business.

January 2005 saw the launch of IT Matters Brisbane offices, which are staffed by several consultants and specialise in all IT spheres in the QLD marketplace.

November 2006 will mark the opening of our newest offices in Melbourne which are expected to be just as successful as the operation in Brisbane.

Tom Freeman’s Specialties:

My current specialities are in resourcing candidates in the Business Analyst, Project/Program Manager and Testing roles for the Sydney market.
I recently moved out of the Federal Government space, where I was recruiting for roles based in Canberra. In this space I was resourcing from developers through to Assistant Directors.
