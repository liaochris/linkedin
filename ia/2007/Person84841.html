Terry Carmichael’s Summary

Terry Carmichael has worked in the biotech industry for nearly 15 years. Starting out in a DNA laboratory, Terry quickly accelerated his career into technical sales, product management and business development. In 1997, Terry founded the GeneTree DNA Testing Center (GeneTree.com), a company providing direct-to-consumer DNA testing services. In 2001, Terry sold the GeneTree brand and assets to the multi-billion dollar Sorenson Development Corporation (SorensonCompanies.com).

Terry received his Master’s Degree in Molecular Medicine and Genetics from the Wayne State University (WSU) School of Medicine. His thesis suggested how DNA rearrangements in the promoter region of a virus were required to cause a disease.

After working in an academic laboratory for over 3 years, Terry was hired by QIAGEN, a company providing DNA purification solutions to the life sciences industry. Terry worked on launching QIAGEN’s Taq DNA Polymerase and QIAquick DNA Purification products. He assisted with training sales representatives and clients with understanding the automated and manual DNA purification applications offered at QIAGEN.

Terry completed a Marketing and Sales Certificate offered at UCLA, and in 1999, he was Product Manager for Bio-Rad’s Gene Technology Business Unit, where Terry successfully developed and launched several high-throughput and manual DNA purification products. He created protocols, product packaging and developed new programs for several automated platforms.

Today, Terry is employed as Vice President of Marketing and Sales for Sorenson Genomics, a high-throughput DNA sequencing and genotyping laboratory specializing in the development and commercialization of DNA testing services. Terry also sits on the board as a director of the Biotech Learning Institute (BiotechLearning.com), an organization providing eLearning training solutions to those working in the biotech, pharmaceutical and life sciences industries.

Terry Carmichael’s Specialties:

Selling and marketing DNA testing, biotechnology, and training products and services.
