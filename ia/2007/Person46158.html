Karen Snyder’s Summary

In addition to co-founding InnerMation, Karen was the sole force behind the start-up studio, Pure Media & Design. Her background includes retail management, independent film and video production, and design. Karen’s current goal is developing InnerMation by carving out a unique niche of edgy and alternative online games and mobile content. She is joined by a deep and diverse team of matchless talent who have each lent their skill to the likes of: Nike, The Beatles, Wild Brain, Cartoon Network.com, Klasky-Csupo, SBC, Pepsico, Warner Bros, and World Vision.

Her vision is to see InnerMation become a shimmering provider of interactive entertainment by creating surprising products with original characters and stories, all brought to life with advanced technology.

Her passion is to reach kids and tweens through life-altering experiences in games packed with comedy, music and the arts.

InnerMation is a Virtools Authorized Game Developer, an affiliate of the Serious Games Initiative, and Games For Change.
