Mike Borella’s Summary

Michael Borella is Senior Vice President of Engineering at Fastmobile, Inc., where he runs multiple development teams in the US and China. His role includes management of server and mobile handset software development, process definition, and intellectual property administration.

Previously, he was Director of R&D in the UTStarcom CDMA business unit, where he managed all R&D functions within this group and his other responsibilities include product and feature definition, standards, intellectual property development, business development, and customer interaction. In addition to UTStarcom, he has held positions at 3Com , IBM and ROLM, and has consulted to various high-tech companies and served as an expert witness.

He has taught at a number of universities, including Northwestern University in Evanston, Illinois, where he currently is an adjunct professor in the Masters of Information Technology Program (MITP). He has published numerous technical papers and been granted over 60 patents. He has been recognized numerous times within UTStarcom and 3Com for his contributions to their respective intellectual property development programs, including being named “Inventor of the Year” by 3Com in 2000.
