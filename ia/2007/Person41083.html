Jennifer Griffin Chesney’s Summary

Creative, results-driven leader with over ten years of expertise in product portfolio management of financial services software.

Leads production and execution of innovative business technology products with specialties in products for Institutional Securities clients (Investment Banking, Equities and Fixed Income), financial social community Web 2.0 products for consumer clients, and streaming collaborative portfolio management tools for retail brokers and clients.
