Pam Claughton’s Summary

After ten years with a large, Boston based search firm, I left to open Custom Search Group, Inc. and to focus on searches in the Creative, Marketing, Technology and Financial services space from mid to Sr. VP and C level.

I have an especially strong interest in e-commerce and web 2.0 focused companies and have developed a nice niche in this area.

Marketing/Communications, Project/Program Management, Product management, and Sales Engineers/Solutions Architects are my most common searches and where I have the deepest networks.

If you are thinking of engaging in a search, and would like to hear about opportunities that might match with your search objectives, please feel free to forward your resume to me in confidence.
