Jessica Herron Banias’s Summary

Jessica Banias is a Siebel 7.7 Certified Consultant with extensive experience in the Manufacturing and High Tech industries. She has more than 4 years of Siebel experience and other valuable experience in the information technology field. Jessica earned a bachelor’s degree in computer information technology from Clemson University. (Clemson, SC)

Her primary expertise includes: CRM Field Service Strategy, business analysis and process re-design, Siebel software technology and project management.

During the last three years, Jessica has been involved with each phase of a large scale Siebel 7.8 / Oracle 11.5.10 implementation from requirements gathering through deployment and post go live support. With each phase her role and responsibility increased to her most recent position of Service Team Lead.

Jessica has completed the Siebel 7.7 Core Consultant Certification and has taken all of the Siebel 7.7 Business Analyst courses.
