Bas Brinkman’s Summary

• general
I am a writer. And. I am a concept developer. My vision about what I do has over the years developed into a Method (where I use dramaturgy to explore marketing communication). This year I decided to practice what I preach. And live remote from distraction; keep a fresh mind to anything.

• concept development
My creative and strategic methods originate in the narration principles of modern film, television and theater. They evolved in the fun mind games that semiotics and dramaturgy give. And they matured in about ten years of field experience in unusual creativity (experience marketing, events, cross media marketing, television-formats).

Corporations usually hire me when they 'just don't know' and want a fresh, new, original look on things. On the left they developed the product, brand or idea. And on the right what they want to accomplish with it. And in between it is just. Well. Blank. No thoughts on media. No thoughts on strategy. No nothing. That's when I come in. What I do. Is me. It is my look at things. My mind. Nothing more. And you don't know my mind until you try my mind. Assign me. It's simple.

• writing
Oh. I almost forgot. And if you need clever funky fast pip pip hunky-dory fo' shizzle texts (from poetry to web to those really boring things that no one reads until I wrote them) just click me.
