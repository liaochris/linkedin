2005 – international MPLS network for mobilkom austria group (design, coordination of implementation, process definition)
2005 – Participation in conceptual and implementation project for new generation GGSN
2004 – Layer 2/Layer 3 infrastructure for mobilkom austria GPRS service (coordination of tender, evaluation, project management and implementation)
