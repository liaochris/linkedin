Bill Moersch’s Summary

Bill Moersch is the President of Management Recruiters National where he is a nationally recognized expert in premier, full service executive staffing and consulting. Bill specializes professional staffing processes, career coaching, resume development, outplacement and human resource consulting in both the Telecommunications and Information Technology industries.
