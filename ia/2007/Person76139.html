Sandeep Dixit’s Summary

Sandeep has over fourteen years of experience in application strategy, architecture, design, and development using Enterprise Java technologies, business process re-engineering, and project management. He has been consulting with fortune-500 companies such as BP Oil & Chemicals, OfficeMax, Nortel, NCR, IBM, Aetna, and National City. He is a co-founder and the chief architect of Ohioedge CRM+BPM Server and has been a guest speaker at JavaOne conferences.
