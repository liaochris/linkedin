Brett Adam’s Summary

I have more than 20 years experience in the software industry, with a background that includes CEO and CTO level positions in both public and private companies, software architecture and engineering, product management, marketing, sales and corporate development.

I have built profitable market focused organizations that deliver leading-edge IT solutions to solve real-world business problems. As an entrepreneurial technologist with extensive enterprise software experience, I have co-founded four companies and led multiple corporate acquisition plays.

I am expert in business process automation and declarative business rules technologies within the modern enterprise software architecture.

A highly accomplished public speaker, I enjoy evangelizing new ideas and exciting teams.

Brett Adam’s Specialties:

Corporate development, technology strategy, marketing strategy. Technology specialities include Model Driven Architecture, business process technologies, business rules technologies and the broad J2EE architectural space.
