Al Ramirez’s Summary

Began Sales career in Dallas with AT&T in 1992 with successful stints at Sprint and Brooks Fiber-MCIWorldCom

Served as Director of Corporate Sales with Flashnet and planned the development of the company's ATM based business class services in preparation of their IPO in 1999.

Served as the Public Networks Sales Manager at Samsung, gaining experience in wireless network deployments by managing the sale of Samsung's ATM core switching products.

Served as Florida Digital Networks' Agent Sales Manager and was based in Palm Beach.

Upon moving to Santa Monica, joined Public Communications Services and secured the company's contract for a new criminal surveillance phone system for the State of Montana Department of Corrections.

Most recently a top sales performer at XO Communications .
