Ian Eckert’s Summary

I am an award-winning journalist and online publisher with over 10 years experience in digital media specialising in the migration of traditional print businesses.

I currently run the online business for TSL Education, the UK’s leading educational publisher. The Times Educational Supplement, Times Higher Education Supplement and Nursery World and our magazines, websites and exhibitions cover the complete professional educational field, from early years through to higher education.

Prior to joining TSL I was a main board director CMP Information in a new digital development role designed to provide a strategic overview for the group's online activities and to champion best practice across the company. I also worked with the publishing teams across the business development of specific products and services.

Before my appointment at CMPi I spent five years as a project director and head of content and production at Abacus E-Media where he specialised in electronic publishing working for clients in the UK and US including Thomson Financial, Wilmington Group, Centaur, Incisive Media and Financial News.

This followed four years at Portsmouth Publishing and Printing where he built up the new media team responsible for The News Online - winner of the New Media Age Award for Consumer Effectiveness and the Press Gazette Best Online Service. During this time he was also appointed assistant editor of The News, the south coast's top selling daily paper.

I started my career in the regional press on the south coast before editing Early Times, the national children's newspaper, and then moving into consumer magazines spending five years in IPC's TV Weeklies division.

I am currently Visiting Fellow in Online Journalism at Trinity and All Saints, University of Leeds.
