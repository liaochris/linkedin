Peter Carlsen’s Summary

Booking Booster.com is a innovative web-based application that provides an integrated approach to hotel online marketing. Booking Booster 2.01 launches in July 2006. Its core application makes managing extranet partners fast and easy: use it to instantly upload room availability and rates, and track all bookings, online and in real time. Then compare online partners to see which offer the best revenue potential. Booking Booster’s add-on applications include a well-designed hotel website with real-time room availability, allowing clients to make direct bookings online. It’s search-engine optimised, so more potential customers visit the website. The final part of the package: an integrated Pay Per Click (PPC) web marketing campaign, with Google as partner.
