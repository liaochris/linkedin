Mark Cracknell’s Summary

At Littlelucifer we specialise in combining visually rich interactive creative with seamless technical solutions.

As the owner, I have over 9 years online experience (7 of these 'agency' side) working with leading Australian and Global brands across all major categories, on above & below the line projects and through-the-line digital campaigns.

Est. 2003, we work with advertising agencies, marketing companies, small–medium businesses and individuals in Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide, Shanghai, Hong Kong and Japan.
