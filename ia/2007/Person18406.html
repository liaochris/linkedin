Matt Hawkins’s Summary

'm Matt Hawkins, born in 1975 and owner of Connexions4London. Originally from Basingstoke, I have now moved to Bournemouth 5 years ago, because if I could choose somewhere in the UK to live, this has to be it. So this also seemed the ideal place to run my business from.

Starting from a young age I've always run businesses, I think its a family thing. Later going on to do A-Levels and a Degree in Computing and Business, its no wonder I ended up following that path. After working for a large mutual Funds company in London looking after the trading desks for 3 years I moved on to help run a growing Internet company in London. After being there a year and helping it well on its way, the urge of doing my own thing took over again and in early 2000 I started C4L. After years of steady growth and lots of hard work we are now one of the UK's top B2B Internet and Colocation providers. We have over 100,000 machines and servers relying on our network and millions of browsers everyday. We have created one of the UK's largest networks and an international network spanning into Europe and the USA. With 250% growth every year and over £3m turnover due in 2007, we are growing very quickly. The reason we have been so successful is my business model is designed to help companies save money on their current Internet and voice costs, rather than cost them more money by upgrading.
