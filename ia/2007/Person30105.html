Frans Dekker’s Summary

Mr. Frans Dekker (1970) is the founder of Daluz and partner of The Human Effect ®, a network organization of professionals who offer a range of services to facilitate individuals, teams, and organizations in developing their potential, and in becoming more fullfilled and effective in making happen what is important to them.

Throughout his career, Mr. Dekker has developed and applied his creative thinking, problem solving, leadership, consulting, training, & coaching skills in areas related to Strategy Development, Organizational Design, Organizational Change, Organizational Development, and Performance Management & Improvement.

Nowadays, he dedicates himself together with his partners amongst others to his passion for:

1. Training & Coaching teams in defining & achieving their objectives, by combining strategic planning techniques with developing leadership skills, creative thinking & problem solving skills, and team member interaction, resulting in improved team- & organizational effectivenes

2. Facilitating management-, project-, & department teams in strategic meetings, workshops, & events, and in planning & delivery of strategic projects, resulting in a more effective execution

3. Organizing Teambuilding & Company Events

4. Training & Coaching people in developing their personal and team leadership skills, self-awareness, and authenticity, resulting in an increased level of both personal fullfilment and leadership effectiveness

5. Training people in creative thinking & problem solving skills, resulting in improved competencies for innovation, strategy development, performance improvement, & strategy execution

6. Organizing conferences, seminars, & workshops

Mr. Dekker invites you to connect with him (info@daluz.com), and to have a look at www.daluz.com.
