David Gruebele’s Summary

A versatile, “hands-on” senior executive with broad expertise in international business, general management and operations. Trained as an engineer, David has guided successful startups and recoveries of financially troubled business units and entrepreneurial companies. Industry experience ranges from high technology manufacturing, software, and e-commerce to financial and professional services, including banking.

David brings significant international sales experience. His work has led to sales in excess of $1 Billion to clients across the U.S. and Pacific Rim. With fifteen years of P&L responsibility at public and private firms, he is adept at working cross-culturally with offshore divisions, partners and customers.

He provides quick response and effective solutions, developing and implementing comprehensive programs with a “no-nonsense”, profit-oriented approach to business that balances customer needs with market conditions and competitive realities. His judgment, knowledge, experience and objective decision making rapidly delivers high value results.
