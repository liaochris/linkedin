Ray . Casey’s Summary

Visit www.arestar.com for a complete BIO for Arestar (R*), LLCm and/or join me on facebook.com at ray.casey@arestar.com

I have over 16 years experience as an IT operations and strategy consultant, technical architect and enterprise infrastructure consultant in corporate and commercial environments. I have worked primarily in eCommerce enterprise projects, including Retail B2C, B2B, e-MarketPlace and e-Services since 1997 leveraging Microsoft technologies. Previous to this I was focused on Financial Services industry projects. In addition to performing in both functional and technical architecture roles, I have led operations and design teams, and designing and implementing a system based Project Management office. Additionally, I have trained technical personnel in system design and how to design develop and deploy enterprise solutions using Microsoft Solutions Framework (MSF), Software Design Discipline (SDD) methodologies.

For the past 9 years I have been involved in numerous eCommerce projects with Fortune 1000 companies. I have recently been a Lead Architect for enterprise eCommerce projects using Commerce Server 2007 for customers including Corbis and Monster.com. I have also provided extensive MS Commerce Server 2000/2002 technical architecture consulting with other firms such as Avanade and Microsoft/Microsoft Consulting Services since product release. As a lead eCommerce technical Architect I have also provided enterprise application integration (EAI) and product life-cycle development solutions involving MS BizTalk Server and MS Project Server 2003. In this capacity I was the functional and technical subject matter expert for the delivery of EAI solutions with emphasis on integration amongst disparate customer relationship management (CRM) and enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems and processes.
