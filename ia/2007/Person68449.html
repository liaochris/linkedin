Pierre Polycarpe’s Summary

I am Senior Solution Consultant for Europe and South at Taleo. I offer creative and strategic expertise in Talent Management programs and technologies to my clients.

I have a passion for HR Marketing, Employer Branding and HR Technologies.

I have over 12 years of experience in Management Consulting and Human Resources, including extensive experience in Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), Talent Management Vendor selection, System design and implementation, and HR marketing campaigns.

I have very valuable experiences in managing complex and international projects.

My specific area of expertise is in the staffing and recruiting process, employer branding and marketing, as well as the evaluation and design of ATS and e-recruiting systems for international corporate HR Directions.
