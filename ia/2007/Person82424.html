Steve Newcomb’s Summary

I just added my own blog relating to Powerset. Please take a look because that's where we will be adding new news about Powerset as we progress.

A serial entrepreneur whose primary career focus has been on operations and execution in search, natural language voice search, mobile, media and consumer entertainment companies for the last 15 years. Most recently, Mr. Newcomb was Vice President of Promptu's Mobile Division where he was responsible for product management, marketing, finance and business development. In the course of his career, he played essential roles in three successful exits totaling over $2 billion dollars, and has held positions of GM, VP Product Marketing, VP Product Management and Director of Software Development.
