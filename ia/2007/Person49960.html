Larry Logan’s Summary

Brands are my passion, and I've been fortunate on the client and agency side to have worked with many of the world's leading brands for more than 20 years.

In creating brand identities, I develop short- and long-range marketing, messaging, and communication strategies that build identity and image in high profile B2B, consumer, and national/global markets. It's all about creating Value.

Larry Logan’s Specialties:

Branding and corporate identity, integrated marketing strategy and execution, green marketing, growth champion, corporate evangelism and reputation, creativity and design, strategic partnerships and licensing, intellectual property and trademarks, public speaking, China.
