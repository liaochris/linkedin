Ron Simone’s Summary

I am "an architect and steward of possibilities".

Broadly regarded as a growth-inspired, results-driven business innovator and strategic marketing guy. I've been described by collegues as "an intuitive and unapologetic observer of the big picture", capable of "crafting business solutions that are differentiating, value-adding, and eminently executable." I get most jazzed by the challenge of growth, especially when it seems impossible.

I've developed over 20 new products in the past decade (some hits, some misses, some absolutely monster successes), and have operated a business with nearly $100 million in annual profit and a double-digit growth rate over 10 consecutive years.

My work experience has spanned the packaged goods and financial services sectors, with extensive practice in the area of cross-sell and direct marketing of financial and membership services. In addition to working most recently in Canada, I have led assignments in the Caribbean, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Taiwan, Thailand, Bermuda, the United States, and in the UK.

Ron Simone’s Specialties:

Product development, customer acquisition, direct marketing, membership services, brand development, public speaking, negotiation
