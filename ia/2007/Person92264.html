Dean C. Smith’s Summary

Working in the arena of television news as a videopher/editor involves a great deal more than just the technical ability to operate a camera. Above all it necessitates inventiveness, incorporating a creative eye for structure and storytelling. Strong, composed images can impress compelling reporting.
