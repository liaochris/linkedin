Sean Carty’s Summary

My flexographic web presses can print up to 17 inches wide and up to 16 colors, including four color process with incredible photographic image quality. The ability to add UV inks and varnishes, plus foil stamping, embossing, and overlaminating adds to our ability to produce outstanding prime labels. We host a vast library of cutting dies that enables us to find a size to fit your product requirements. Customized dies can also be created for that unique and individual look.
• Up to 8 color capability • 2 sided printing • Inline Foil Embossing
• Vast selection of dies and facestocks • Advanced slitter/rewind equipment
• UV inks and varnish • Overlaminating • Multi-part hinged labels
• Hang tags • IRC and other Added-Value labels

Sean Carty’s Specialties:

I specialize in labels for the food & beverage, health & beauty, and chemical industries. Also, specialized labels, added value labels, such as Instant Redeemable Coupons, multi-part hinged labels, and two sided labels.
