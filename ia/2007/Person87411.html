Travis Giggy’s Summary

I am passionate about business on the Internet.

I have been programming web sites for 12 years, since the inception of ASP technology.

In 1997, I started my first Internet business, called Carryout.com. It was an online food ordering service that allowed you to order food from a local restaurant right to your door. For the time, that was pretty cool! But, also for the time, nobody used it! lol - that didn't last too long... (actually, we had 200+ restaurants in 3 states online and delivered 50+ meals on some nights.)

Now, a decade later, I'm still learning what it takes to be successful in online business. The blog address below is how I record those lessons. Since I started the blog, I've learned the value of keeping a written record of my Internet business experiences. One lesson builds on the next and pretty soon some semblance of wisdom emerges. As long as I keep learning and growing, I'll keep writing about it.

I doubt I'll ever quit learning.
