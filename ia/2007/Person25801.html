Ed Callahan’s Summary

Personal Brand: A C-level consultant, with a sales pedigree, who can help you grow your top line, profitably.

What I do: I provide business advisory services, with an emphasis on sales, to the CEO or VP of Sales of companies in the $5M to $100M range. I will also work for well-funded, pre-revenue, start-ups or restarts.

How I work: I primarily work in part-time advisory engagements where I am retained for a fixed number of days per month for a flat fee. Additionally I am available to my clients on an unlimited basis by phone and email.

Here are just some of the ways I add value as your advisor:
- Evaluate your current go-to-market plans
- Assess your current sales team and help you make what corrections are necessary in skills, territories or quota assignments
- Help you align your sales compensation plans and your company goals
- Utilize my network to help you penetrate target accounts, find strategic partners, attract advisory board members, and recruit executive management

Where I work: I prefer to work in the greater Philadelphia market but am open to short term assignments almost anywhere, particularly in the Washington D.C to NYC corridor.

Experience: Managed organizations ranging in size from two to two hundred and fifty people, across all functions . . . Managed every type of sales channel: direct, OEM, VAR, systems integrator, distributors, and independent agents . . . Coached CEO's and functional senior management . . . Advised Boards of Directors, and investors. . . Conducted "Go to Market" due diligence audits which can result in product re-positioning, competitive analysis, channels optimization, sales force evaluation and restructuring.

Ed Callahan’s Specialties:

Complete P&L, Strategic Business Planning, Revenue & Profit Growth, Training & Development, Mentoring and Leadership, Change Management, New Market Development
