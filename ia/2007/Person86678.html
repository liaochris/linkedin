Thomas Carrier’s Summary

Thomas Carrier:

• advises clients on web market strategies for selling their products and services;
• consults small and medium enterprises on new business development techniques and streamlined operations;
• and is the founder of Carrier Web Services, LLC, a company specializing in innovative methods to best leverage the new global workforce.

Tom was the founder and CEO of Microsolutions Development Corporation, an early adopting Lotus Notes business partner. Tom led an 8-person team in the closing of $3.5 million in sales of a CRM call center system, Microsolutions Catrak. As the product manager of Catrak, Tom was responsible for the product strategy, sales & marketing, leads generation, client requirements analysis, and systems development. Microsolutions discontinued operations as the marketplace shifted, becoming increasingly more interested in SaaS product offerings.

Currently, Tom is advising a non-profit startup organization and is researching the marketplace for the next innovative products to bring to market.

If you know of an innovative product offering or a startup company in need of senior-level talent, please contact Tom by sending an email to linkedin@TomCarrier.com.

For more information on Tom's background, please visit TomCarrier.com.
