Ted Lelekas (ted@lelekas.com)’s Summary

I am a PR & communication strategy expert with 12 years of professional experience, during which I have been forging solid relationships with clients, journalists and other influencers across multiple geographies: the UK, the US, Greece, most European countries, the Middle East, the Balkans. I have spent the first 7 years of my career in the UK, specialising in pan-European communication campaigns for some of the most influential companies in the Technology and e-commerce sectors. Subsequently, since my return to Greece in late 2001, I have been building a generalist portfolio of clients and exciting engagements including IPOs, privatisations, international expansion campaigns, election campaigns, lobbying, as well as a key communication role in the preparation of the transport infrastructure for the 2004 Athens Olympic Games. My historical client list includes Greek and multi-national companies, major academic and financial services institutions, MPs, Ministries and other Government organizations, as well as the biggest Greek pop star. In January 2007 I joined ALBA Graduate Business School in the new role of Director of Marketing and Public Affairs. ALBA Graduate Business School is one of the leading European business schools, based in Athens, Greece.
