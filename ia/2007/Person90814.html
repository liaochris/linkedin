Wessel Koning’s Summary

The focus of my professional experience is on International Business Development and Sales & Account Management with a strong link to Strategic Marketing and launching new innovative products and solutions to the global market. I have experience in:
• International Business Development
• Key Account Management & Sales Management
• Marketing of New Products & Solutions, Go-to-Market
• Know-how and network in Regions of Asia Pacific & Europe, including the new Emerging Markets like China and India.

Career path:

I currently fulfill the role of Manager International Business Development & Sales and responsible Manager for the Asia Pacific Region. The goal is on launching the new Philips 3D TV/display technology called ‘WOWvx’ to the global market, initially the B2B market.
In this function my core activities (and competencies) are amongst others:
• Creating market awareness with references, targeted PR and press events
• Generating sales and contracting the right first customer base
• Selecting and contracting strategic partnerships and defining product-market combinations
• Managing global and regional accounts as well as a team of commercial professionals in the Region
• Evaluating various new business opportunities (technology licensing)

Earlier experience

• Key Account Manager B2B for Philips Consumer Electronics, in which I was responsible for the Public Market Segment of the Netherlands
• Product Marketing Manager - Region Europe for Philips, in which I worked at launching a completely new WiFi network productrange to the market from scratch
• B2B Marketing and Sales Manager in new business in a private electronics company.
• Entrepreneur / founder of own business
