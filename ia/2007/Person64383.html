Nicolas Perret’s Summary

I started my career in the video game business over a decade ago. I worked with several video game companies, before joining Delphine Software in 1995.

I worked on all major titles developed by Delphine Software, including Fade To Black and the Moto Racer series, which sold more than 7 millions units worldwide.

When Delphine Software closed its doors in July, 2004, I obtained the rights of the game user interface (UI) creation tool that was developed by my team in the company.

This tool, upgraded and polished, is now the key product of Omegame, the company I set up in April, 2005.

I am now Associate Managing Director and Chief Software Architect for Omegame.

Nicolas Perret’s Experience

  • Associate Managing Director - Chief Software Architect

    Omegame

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Games industry)

    April 2005 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Omegame’s Menus Master is the complete solution for easily creating cutting-edge user interfaces (UI) and head-up displays (HUD) for video games or any application necessitating high-end computing technologies.

    Menus Master let artists author most of the UI and HUD without the need for any knowledge of programming, thanks to an intuitive graphical interface. Light programming skills are only needed if the UI uses databases, and at the end of the project, when UI is integrated into the game.

    Artists can easily combine graphics, sound, 3D, animation, text, and video to quickly prototype UI. Validated prototype can then be polished until obtaining AAA user interface. UI can finally easily be exported on any platform.

    Menus Master is also delivered with all necessary tools to handle all localization issues.

    Menus Master is available for all current and next generation consoles, and PC.

    JIRAF has been created due to the current crisis in the French video game development industry. Many development studios and several small publishing companies simply plummeted.
