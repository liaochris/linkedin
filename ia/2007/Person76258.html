Sandra Toussaint’s Summary

Sandra Toussaint has over 9 years experience in project management. With a strong focus on large business projects, her expertise includes establishing project management infrastructures, strategic marketing process development and execution, as well as change management. As an experienced project manager, she has worked with numerous organizations across many industry verticals, such as government, not for profit foundations, financial services/ insurance and private and public health care.

Sandra is a developer of goal-oriented systems, people and teams. She has experience in leading and participating in multiple, simultaneous external relations and human relations projects. Her excellent interpersonal and team building skills combined with an effective balance of strategic focus and tactical experience, allows project teams to work effectively and focus on the key project deliverables. Sandra has a productive, enthusiastic, detail-oriented approach to her work, and is able to foster co-operation on her assignments to obtain results.

She received her Bachelors of Commerce from Ryerson University as well as her Masters in Project Management from the Schulich School of Business. She is a member of the Project Management Institute (PMI) and is certified Project Management Professional (PMP).
