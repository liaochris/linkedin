Stephen Gele’s Summary

Stephen's practice concentrates on casualty litigation, commercial litigation, environmental litigation, federal practice and government relations. He has tried cases in District and City Courts in the Parishes of Orleans, Jefferson, St. Bernard, St. Charles and Lafourche, and the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Louisiana. He has handled appeals in the Louisiana First, Third, Fourth and Fifth Circuit Courts of Appeal, the Louisiana Supreme Court and the United States Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals.

Stephen Gele’s Specialties:

I have litigated the following issues:
- product liability, e.g., RVs, SUVs, fire causation
- professional liability, e.g., insurance agents & adjusters
- consumer claims, TILA, FCRA, FDCA
- wrongful death
- vehicle collisions
- admiralty jurisdiction, navigability, and recreational vessel collisions
- insurance coverage
- premises liability
- subrogation, indemnity, liens
- medical causation
- Construction defect, NHWA, mold, termites
- public records requests
