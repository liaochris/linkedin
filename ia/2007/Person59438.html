Mike Currie’s Summary

10 years in IT.

I started out as a help desk technician, and moved into system administration. I attained my MCSE in Windows 2000 in October 2001.

I then started focusing on networking, and attained my CCNA in 2001.

Next was focusing on network security, and I attained my SCNP in 2002.

From 2001 until 2004, I was working in an IT Consulting capacity, focusing on Medical/Dental/Financial systems.

In late 2004, I shifted out of IT Consulting and started managing all of the enterprise application deployments for a 3+ billion dollar bank. After a year in the app trenches, I was promoted to System Engineer IV and took over more responsibilities with the Server Infrastructure. A year later, I was promoted to Section Lead of all of the System Engineers, and then promoted to Assistant Vice President in July of 2007.
