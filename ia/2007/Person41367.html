Jim Barr’s Summary

The list of adventures never gets any shorter; my decisions are made by opportunities for enlightenment. Choose wisely and live in a world filled with fascination.

I represent Northern Escape Heli-Skiing located in Terrace BC. I look forward to hooking you up with the information you'll need to get into the chopper for the world's top powder filled adventure. Check out the profile details- in the "Experience section" of Linkedin below- this gem is located within British Columbia Canada- North America's most accessible heli-ski destination (fly direct from Vancouver and your base camp is only 25 minutes from the airport).

Send me an email, I'll shoot you some epic skiing/boarding photos for your desktop jim(AT)neheliski.com

For the past 15 years I have worked in various sales and marketing capacities within the ski and snowboard industry. I was with snow bound adventure tour operator Downhill Riders Ski & Travel Co, working to source opportunities and double its business during my tenure there.

I am a travel & adventure writer/photographer. In addition to corporate writing, I contribute to the Edmonton Sun's outdoor adventure/travel section in the summer and its snow pages in the winter. I produce stories on my adventures for many North American publications as a freelance travel journalist. If you are looking to add some spark with fresh content for your company's communication channels, please let me know.

I also Co-Chair a climb for a cause supporting Big Brothers Big Sisters; 'Edmonton Airports Presents the Sun's Climb for Kids'. To date it has raised over $40k and brought in 100 new volunteers. We are gearing up our next expedition in June of '08; a strike for the summit of Europe's highest peak, Mount Elbrus- Russia...now recruiting trekkers, if you want more info drop me a line.
Thank you to The Edmonton Sun, Edmonton Airports, Campers Village and of course Berg Adventures International, one of the world's top expedition producers.
