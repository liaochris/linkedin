Rajesh Setty’s Summary

Rajesh Setty is a die-hard optimist (no eats, shoots and leaves!)

Rajesh Setty began entrepreneurial life the old fashioned way—by failing hard. Since this was right out of college fifteen years ago, the lessons he learnt he remembers well—even now. Moving on, Setty built company divisions, led teams and took on challenges with unabashed enthusiasm. His first venture in United States (co-founded in late 2000) — CIGNEX Technologies, grew 250% every year, without external funding during his first five years of leadership.

Rajesh currently serves as the president of Foresight Plus, LLC through which he partners with select business leaders to provide them and their businesses with sustainable and unfair competitive advantage.

Rajesh is involved in several other businesses in US and India in some combination of investor, founder, advisor and/or an executive. Businesses he is involved with include:
• Suggestica – Focuses on bringing trusted content to the web
• iPolipo – Web based solution for scheduling
• Compassites – Outsourced product development for startups.
• PSVillage – Networking for senior Professional Services executives

Rajesh is a member of the Band of Angels, an angel network in Silicon Valley and Vistage a worldwide networking organization for CEOs.

Rajesh has written and published seven books so far. He wrote his first novel when he was nine and managed to get it published (by a reputed publisher) by the time he was thirteen. His latest book “Beyond Code” (foreword by Tom Peters) was published in late 2005. Rajesh maintains a blog called Life Beyond Code. Rajesh speaks at conferences and companies in US and India on wide ranging topics that include Leadership, Entrepreneurship, Personal Branding and Living a Life Beyond Code.

Rajesh lives in the Silicon Valley with his wife Kavitha and their son Sumukh.
