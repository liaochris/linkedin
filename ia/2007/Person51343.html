Linda Noel’s Summary

Since I was first introduced to the Web in 1993 I have had a complete and utter fascination with its possibilities. I started developing websites in 1996 and complimented my self-training with a degree in Web Development in 2003. Since that time I have been an expert SEO consultant for other internet marketing companies as well as directly for my own clients. My fascination has resulted in two recent web-based business startups.

The first start-up, I Publish Press, answers to my voracious appetite for good fiction and gives me the opportunity to read books that might otherwise have never made it onto a store bookshelf. My partners and I are committed to providing authors with a genuine market for their works and an author-oriented publishing model.

The second, everyonesguide, is a result of years of frustration with the lack of good search results when looking for local businesses. No one has successfully handled the ball so, thanks to advancing technologies which make the endeavour manageable, I have decided to do it myself.

Most of my day-to-day work involves search marketing initiatives with a focus on organic search engine optimization and the use of Web 2.0 properties for marketing my own and clients' products and services. I find nothing more thrilling than hearing "sales have gone up!" from a client.
