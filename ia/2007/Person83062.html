Sue Scheff’s Summary

As a parent that has struggled with a teenager and was at my wits end - I know first hand what parents go through when they feel their teen is taking over the household.

When I decided to seek outside help, after exhausting all my local resources, I was completely scammed into a group of programs that misrepresented themselves - harmed my daughter and defrauded me.

I fought back - I defeated them in a jury trial in 2004 - and further defeated them in the Supreme Court of Appeals. In 2006 I won again in a jury trial and set legal precedence with a jury awarding me $11.3M in damages due to Internet Defamation and Invasion of Privacy.

I have been a victim not only people that harmed my family - but by extremists that want me silenced. I fought back and won!

Almost 7 years later, I have literally helped thousands of families by sharing my experiences and giving safe alternatives to help their teens.

My book - "At Wit's End" will be published in 2008 by the publishers that brought you Chicken Soup for the Soul book series. There is also a Major Motion Picture that is in production currently.

With all the bad that has happened - the emotional roller coaster - the stress, invasion of privacy, defamation, slander, as well as threats to me - I have prevailed and I am here for you to learn from my experiences and gain from my knowledge. It is not about textbook - it is about reality - the reality that in today's society the new generation of teenagers can be difficult and frustrating - that can leave you - At Your Wit's End!
