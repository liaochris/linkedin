Max Kalehoff’s Summary

I’m currently vice president of marketing at Nielsen BuzzMetrics, a leading measurement service for consumer-generated media. I consider myself an evangelist and student of interactive marketing and emerging media, and I help colleagues and clients understand and leverage the disruption caused by these growing trends. My intellectual passion is studying technology-driven disruption of marketing and media businesses.

I frequently lecture to industry and educational groups, such as Columbia University, the Word-of-Mouth Marketing Association, the Advertising Research Foundation, The Marketing Sciences Institute and OMMA. I’m the Friday “Online Spin” op-ed columnist for MediaPost, an advertising trade publisher, and blog about independently about marketing, media and life at AttentionMax.com. I’m often quoted in the business and advertising trade press on all the topics noted above, and as a spokesperson for my company.

My prior experience includes consulting to firms such as Hitwise and the Interactive Advertising Bureau. I led marketing and industry analysis for Media Metrix and later comScore. I began my marketing career at Y&R/Burson-Marsteller, and I graduated from Syracuse University’s S.I. Newhouse School.

A former Brooklynite, I now live in Pelham Manor, NY with my wife Laura and son Julian. I’m a proficient sailor and licensed captain. I like to cook and have worked as one in French restaurants.
