Stephan Tual’s Summary

I'm currently the Managing Director at SiteJourney, a company I co-founded in September 2006. SiteJourney is drawing on technology I developed for Terapad.com

A French citizen who spent twelve years working abroad in the USA, the UK and the Philippines, I am proficient in many application programming languages, including Ruby, Rails, Java & OOP, UML, ColdFusion, xHTML, CSS, XML, Actionscript 2.0 and JavaScript (AJAX); all of which I taught and have developed dozens of enterprise applications with. On the database site, was the first certified MySql Trainer in the UK and have also developed commercial applications using Microsoft SQL Server and Oracle.

As the Regional Master Macromedia Certified Instructor for the UK, during the past 4 years I have provided consulting services and training for over 150 prestigious companies, including BT OpenWorld, Cable & Wireless, Churchill Insurance, GMTV, the Royal Air Force and the Department of Defense.

A keen public speaker and product evangelist, I have presented Enterprise ColdFusion related-topic on many occasions, including the Allaire Dev Con 2000.
