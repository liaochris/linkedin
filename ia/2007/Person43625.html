Jon Ducker’s Summary

A forward thinking and highly motivated individual with an informed and clear understanding of all aspects of digital/interactive media. Experience in commercial digital strategy, with hands on production, including interactive and graphic design. Proven success in delivering critical research projects and evaluating emerging media formats for high profile brands, broadcasters, agencies and other clients.
