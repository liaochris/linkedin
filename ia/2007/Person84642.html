Ted Naganawa’s Summary

China 2047

China 2047 is a comprehensive analysis of political, economic, financial, regional, and international aspects of China's growth and development toward 2047, 50 years after Hong Kong’s handover in 1997. It also discusses China's relationship with Hong Kong, Taiwan, and the rest of the world, especially the United States and Japan, as well as epoch-making thoughts for China beyond 2047. (2nd ed., 2007 ISBN:978-0-6151-3872-5)
