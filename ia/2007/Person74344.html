Ronald Powell’s Summary

With more than 17 years' experience in information technology, and 9 years of experience as a technical project manager, Ron has had direct responsibility for technology decisions, architecture, and standards for companies in the financial services, pharmaceutical, and healthcare industries.

Ron has a combined three years of experience managing project managers at Investors Bank & Trust, State Street Global Advisors, and John Hancock. As Vice President and Director of Project Management at State Street Global Advisors, Ron directed a $2m cost center and a department portfolio worth $36m. His department was responsible for planning and managing the scope, strategy, requirements and implementation of web-based, enterprise-wide IT solutions.

As Vice President and Director of Development at CoreWeb, Ron directs web developers, manages offshore development, and is responsible for defining the technical project architecture for client projects. Ron holds an MBA from Boston College, where he graduated first in his class, and a PMP from the Project Management Institute (PMI). Ron is also pursuing the new Program Manager certification (PgMP) through the PMI.

In the public sector, Ron is serving his second term on the Finance Commitee for the Town of Reading, Massachusetts. Ron is also an appointed liaison to the Commission for Economic Development, the Finance Department, the Community Services Department, the Accounting Department, and the ad hoc Nurse Advocacy Task Force for the Town of Reading.
