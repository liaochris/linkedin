Paul Gossen’s Summary

Business Transformed is a new breed of business book. Funny, light and easy to read, it has a refreshing visual style that allows overloaded managers and executives to “get it” instantly. WARNING: This book contains seriously fresh business thinking. Business Transformed is loaded with simple tools and questions that managers can use to get results immediately. Visit: http://www.businesstransformed.com

Paul Gossen, The Breakthrough Coach, is a well known public speaker in Canada and Europe. His passionate stand for empowering managers and executives is reflected in his extensive following of business leaders. A business transformation evangelist, he has conducted programs for hundreds of companies. His first book in this series, Business Transformed, provides a fresh breakthrough approach to business and management.

Corporate Breakthrough Programs, Executive Coaching, Corprate Leadership Programs, Delegation and Accountability Programs.

I work with CEO's to design Corporate Breakthrough Programs and double or triple their revenue. My current clients include American Express, RCMP, City of Mississauga, Golder Associates. I have been an avid student of transformational training, NLP and business leadership for more than 20 years, and have been and executive caralyst since the year 2000.

Prior to this, as a technology entrepreneur and marketing strategist, I built a number of successful companies. Paradigm Creators developed and brought to market seven computer games. Paul Gossen Consulting Inc., a technology-consulting firm, doubled annual sales four years in a row with a staff of 15. Vertegri Research developed the first licensed Apple laptop clone, which, upon launch, generated over 700,000 website visitors.
