Davey Winder’s Summary

Once best known for my alter ego, Wavey Davey, I have now matured and mellowed into the Happy Geek phase of my life.

With 16 years experience as a freelance word monkey, I currently enjoy being:

Contributing Editor: PC Pro Magazine (www.pcpro.co.uk)

Blog Editor: DaniWeb (www.daniweb.com)

Columnist: Microsoft Business (www.microsoft.com/uk/business/security/)
Columnist: Microsoft NHS Resource Centre (www.microsoft.com/uk/nhs/)

Contributor: Information World Review (www.iwr.co.uk)
Contributor: IT Pro (www.itpro.co.uk)
Contributor: Microscope (www.computerweekly.com)
Contributor: Mobile Computer (www.mobilecomputermag.co.uk)
Contributor: PC Authority (www.pcauthority.com.au)
Contributor: PC Plus Magazine (www.pcplus.co.uk)
Contributor: Pocket Lint (www.pocket-lint.co.uk)
Contributor: Tom's Hardware (www.tomshardware.co.uk)

With my author hat on I have had 23 books published, including an Internet guide book for children, an online travel guide, and numerous reference books. I am currently writing a book in conjunction with the Science Museum in London that explores who we are in this virtual age.

Davey Winder’s Specialties:

Writer of opinion columns, features, news analysis, reviews including group and labs testing, white papers and interviews. Will cover both business and consumer markets with equal enthusiasm.
