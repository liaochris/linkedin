David McKnight’s Summary

McKnight is the CEO of teamMetrx, an international software development firm with operations and partnerships in China, Mexico and the US. The company specializes in distributed project and collaborative customer relationship management, web services and strategic consulting.

McKnight is President of the International Association for Technology Trade, a consortium of technology organizations, consultant partners, government agencies and corporate members seeking to generate successful business opportunities for trading partners -- with a key focus on China and developing countries. <http://TechnologyTrade.org>

He is the former vice chair of the international board of directors of the Association of Internet Professionals, where he also chaired the AIP Certification Accreditation Council (ACAC) and was an AIP representative to the US Congressional Internet Caucus Advisory Committee.

He was a member of the Information Assurance Certification and Accreditation Working Group, and is a frequent speaker and writer on Internet policy and technology issues.

A founding director of TechBiz Connection (one of the largest and fastest growing technology organizations in Southern California) <http://TechBizConnection.org>, McKnight is regularly tapped to moderate or participate on marketing, web services, wireless and security panels.

McKnight's experience also includes 17 years as a marketing executive at Fortune 500 Fleetwood Enterprises, where he specialized in the development of strategic partnerships with the likes of MTV, eBay, Disney, CBS, Chevrolet Motor Division, CNN, Universal Pictures, Coca-Cola, Steven Spielberg and NASCAR among others.

Key accomplishments include highly successful, measurable advertising campaigns, both for market-leading brands and with cutting edge industry cooperative programs.

He has been interviewed by a range of news organizations, including CCTV-9 (China), the Washington Post, USA Today and the Wall Street Journal.
