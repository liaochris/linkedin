Maarten Wegdam’s Summary

I have a background in service middleware/platforms, component middleware, QoS for middleware-based systems, context-aware systems and distributed systems in general. Current focus is on context-aware service middleware for mobile applications, identity federation and privacy. I am project manager of the Freeband AWARENESS project (http://awareness.freeband.nl).
