Tero Kankaanperä’s Summary

I've gained broad knowledge of the Finnish higher education system and universities in particular. I'm familiar with university administration and finance. I have also been the boss for almost ten employees and am familiar with Finnish employment laws. I have good general knowledge of the Finnish administrative law.

I've got a lot of experience and knowledge about association management. I'm self-tought in Finnish association legislation and some other laws. I have trained association boardmembers since 2000. I have good understanding of association finance and accounting.

I have very good general ICT support and maintenance skills. I'm a very efficient Microsoft Office user and have trained others in using Word, Excel and Powerpoint more efficiently. I've worked with mailinglists and have been doing web publishing. I'm self-taught in the field.

I'm a trained lecturer on the subjects of gender and sexuality for the national Sexual Equality association (SETA). I've done peer and professional lectures for youth, social and health care students, youth workers, health professionals and teachers. I've given a few dozen lectures and prepared material for general use by other lecturers. I have coordinated the lecturer group in Northern Savo area and done marketing of the lecture services.

I've coordinated the publishing of three Finnish textbooks of pharmacy for the Pharmacy Student Association Fortis at the University of Kuopio.
The books are:
Marvola M, Urtti A, Mönkkönen J: Biofarmasia ja farmakokinetiikka, Helsinki 1999
Hakkarainen T, Airaksinen M: Kuuri loppuun! - Lääkeneuvonnan opas, Kuopio 2001
Saano S (toim.), Naaranlahti T, Helin-Tamminen M, Järviluoma E: "Sairaalafarmasia", Nurmijärvi 2005
I have also consulted and tutored other volunteers doing book coordination projects for Fortis. These projects have thought me project management, basics of copyright and given some insight to working of the printing press.
