Healthy Pursuit’s Summary

Passionate about Health and Fitness since 1993. Mike and Harvey are Entrepreneurs, Business Managers, Recruiters, Trainers, and Internet Marketing Specialists. Interested in living and promoting a Healthy Lifestyle through information, education, and a wealth of Resources not readily available to the average Consumer. aHealthyPursuit was born out of a desire to help others in their pursuit of a healthy lifestyle.     Melaleuca

    (Privately Held; 10,001 or more employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    1999 — Present (8 years)

    Melaleuca, The Wellness Company, is an international manufacturer and marketer of unique health, home, and personal care products. Melaleuca was founded in 1985, and the name is taken from the Melaleuca Alternifolia plant that produces a naturally powerful essential oil. This oil is used in many of Melaleuca's health care, pharmaceutical, personal care, and home care products.

    Since Melaleuca's founding, they also have developed almost 300 other products, all using natural alternatives to grocery and drugstore brands, designed to help consumers live healthier lives. Their exceptional line of nutritional, home cleaning, and bath and body care products has enabled them to grow rapidly and be recognized as a leader in the wellness industry. Melaleuca's corporate headquarters are in Idaho Falls, Idaho, USA, yet their influence extends around the world.
