Matthew Olson’s Summary

In addition to being our principal, Matthew Olson is also one of the driving forces behind the creativity engine at Signalfire Productions. A diverse creative and business background gave him a unique outlook on providing a new level of creative business services and the means to make it successful. Seeing the need to offer businesses an option that has the creativity and media flexibility of a design studio while holding on the practical logic of everyday production, Olson launched Signalfire to bridge the gap between good design and good business.

While managing the desktop publishing department for a content management firm in Milwaukee, he focused on brand management that was more than just logo usage and colors, but also involved style guides, content and technical documentation. "Documentation is everything," says Olson, "in the world of technical manuals and document management, managing a brand involves more than just how your customer's logo appears, but how the entire identity is used." Olson also began working with both web design and online help projects as technical documents forged onto the Internet and into PDF workflows.

Olson moved into the commercial print industry after several years and found a way to tie his entire background together. "Outdoor advertising, technical documentation and commercial printing all came together," he explains. Having worked his way through computer-to-plate spot color imagesetting to four-color, color managed digital prepress, Olson felt the need to make use of his diverse knowledge. "I was working with the identities of international corporations and saw the edge it gave them in everyday business dealings," Olson says, "I saw a need for small business to have the same advantages."

Matthew Olson’s Specialties:

Signalfire Productions is a collective of skilled individuals with backgrounds in graphic design, web design, web application development, copywriting, digital prepress and technical illustration. Signalfire is a chorus of experience and skills to bring to customers regardless of size. "We're here to be a single source solution to businesses looking to make an impact in their market," Olson says, "we're looking forward to a bright future, but an even brighter one for our customers."
