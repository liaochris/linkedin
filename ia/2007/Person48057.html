Kimberly J. Scott’s Summary

I'm a theatrical designer with experience designing the "perfect" environment for every situation. Scenery, lighting, and projections imaginatively created and executed on time and under budget in a range of situations from easy, elegant and low budget to flashy, glitzy red carpet style events.

For the performing arts community a "true to the vision" designer. Honoring the text and the production team's vision. My goal is to continue to function as a imaginative designer in many mediums. Bringing the joy of surprise and grace to all events.

I have worked on many size stages and in many venues with designers and directors of many generations and many artistic styles. I love what I do and am always looking for the next challenge, be it intellectual, artistic, or architectural!
