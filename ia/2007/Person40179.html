jennifer gergen’s Experience

  • Independent Contractor

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Design industry)

    1999 — Present (8 years)

    Being an independent contractor, I have been able to culture my skill set by taking on a large variety of design projects, across many mediums, and for a diverse group of clients. Some notable projects include:

    View My Swing
    This was a comprehensive project for a website for golfers to submit videos to professional golf coaches for lessons and pointers. I was responsible for:
    - Website design & front-end development in XHTML & CSS
    - Visual design of flash video editing area
    - Browser compatibility for all Mac & Linux browsers, and IE 6 & 7 for windows
    - Complete documentation of the project, code, and style guide

    New York Times
    I was responsible for designing a set of 15 icons for the various New York Times email newsletters.

  • Visual Designer

    Behavior (www.behaviordesign.com)

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    2003 — 2007 (4 years)

    Behavior Design - Visual Designer

    I worked for Behavior as a full-time employee and also as a freelancer on many types of assignments for high profile clients. Memorable projects include:

    HBO
    During my tenure, Behavior worked with HBO producing “Behind the Scenes” featurettes for a number of their original movies and tv series, including Carnivalé, Deadwood, The Life and Death of Peter Sellers, Elizabeth, and Rome. The work was featured on the DVDs as well as the website. My responsibilities on these projects varied, but most often included my participation in the entire process from conceptualization to implementation.

    Citizen Change
    Citizen Change was P. Diddy’s “Vote or Die” Campaign for the 2004 Presidential election. We were charged with the creation of the website, and a very large-scale flash presentation.

    CNN
    I was assigned the complete design of a new micro-site for CNN’s advertising sales.

  • Web Designer / Computer Lab Manager

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; E-Learning industry)
