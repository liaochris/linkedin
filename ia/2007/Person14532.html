Carlos Castaneda’s Summary

I enjoy helping people one-on-one to understand Math, its concepts, formulas and operations. When people decide to move up in their work or to change careers they often go back to school into a graduate program or they take certification courses. To gain admission to such programs, student candidates are routinely required to take a test where their score depends heavily on their mathematical performance. Most people who find themselves in this position have had no contact with math since they finished college, or even before that, a few, several or many years ago. This is where I come in with my tutoring expertise, communication skills, mathematical knowledge and teaching experience.
I hold a Master's and a Bachelor's degree, both in Math.
I also have over 10 years experience in private tutoring for Math.
In the past I have spent over 20 years teaching Math in the classroom, mainly in college (Math and Science schools) but also in high school.
When you see a friend of yours looking for help with math, please direct them to me so I can help them pass their test and reach their goals.

Carlos Castaneda’s Specialties:

Standardized tests like GMAT, GRE, CBEST, ELM, CSET and SAT.
I also provide homework help and preparation for midterms and finals in subjects such as Algebra, Geometry, Pre-Calc, Calculus and Statistics.
Please take a look at the Testimonials page in my tutoring web site.
