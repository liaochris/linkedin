Doug Williams’s Summary

Owner of an executive search firm specializing in the Engineering and Architecture professions. 11+ years of search experience with over 9 of those years as a firm owner. Possess great recruiting acumen as well as strong management and leadership skills to nurture recruiting team.

I, like you probably are, am amazed at how many recruiters and recruiting firms are represented on LinkedIn. Many tout that they utilize some sort of "advanced search technique", or have been through some type of "expert training", or have a wealth of information at their fingertips because of an "expansive database" that sets them apart from others. Only thing is, so do I - as well as all of the other firms. Unlike others, Engionix doesn't boast to be the "best" or "premier" or "number one" recruiting firm, because measuring service is subjective, and ultimately it is you who makes that determination. Luckily for us, finding quality,skilled talent for client companies is very difficult. If it were easy, our clients wouldn't need us! We don't offer to have a magic solution to finding quality individuals for prominent companies. However, what we do offer is dedication, committment, and hard work to the task at hand. And ultimately, it is those attributes which help us stand out.
