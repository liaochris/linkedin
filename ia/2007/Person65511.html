Owen Kelly’s Summary

Artist, writer and lecturer. Particularly interested in ways of drawing technology out of machinery and into people's daily lives, in ways that free them to step into the world. Where entertainment and education meet is where I want to be. At the moment that involves exploring the possibilities inherent in immersive pedagogy.

Owen Kelly’s Specialties:

concept design; graphics (Photoshop, Freehand); project leadership; project management; scripting (actionsscript, javascript, Linden Scripting Language; php; MySQL); visions (my head hurts).
