Darren Pollard’s Summary

Darren has a track record of founding a number of successful entreprenreal ventures as well as holding a number of positions early in his career at a number of large information technology companies including GE Capital IT and IBM Global Services. Most recently, Darren was responsible for the setup and operations of LiveEvents Wireless's presence in the United States during 2005. LiveEvents is a boutique interactive mobile marketing company based in Sydney, Australia. Prior to working for LiveEvents, Darren wrote the business plan, raised seed capital, project managed development build of site, created and built relationships with all data and sports feed providers for another of his startups, SportzSMS, whose mobile technology was licensed to a number of high profile sports media organisations globally including ESPN in the UK and Eurosport in France.

Before his move to the United States in 2004, Darren was the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of Broadscope Training Services, which was Australia's largest Registered Training Organisation (RTO) during 2003, with more than 110 staff, over 8000 trainees and more than $15m in revenue. Darren was responsible for the drive and growth of the business and managed and automated the sales processes for the sales team of 25 sales professionals. Darren formulated the sales and branding strategy which increased the profile of the company within the Australian state of Victoria which drove amazing growth for the company via online, radio, television and print media campaigns.

Darren holds a Associate Diploma of Information Technology from Swinburne Institute of Technology and is a member of the American Australian Association and Advance, both professional networking groups for Australians living in the United States.
