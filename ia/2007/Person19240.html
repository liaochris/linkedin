Cédric Tabin’s Summary

I have a strong experience in Flash and Java development. I work with flash from several years (since 2003) and participated to many big and small projects. I worked as well in team as alone and I am autonomous. I acted as freelance since 2006 on little flash projects. I also gave a conference in Barcelona for the first Adobe Max event about ActionScript 3 optimization.
I currently develop an Open Source project called masapi (http://masapi.googlecode.com) that is designed to manage the Massive Loading in Flash, Flex & Air.
I touch several development technologies in Java (J2SE, Java EE and J2ME) and in .NET, principally when I was studying at HES-SO. I'm now developing regularly in Java for my own needs at home and for the JUGL (Java User Group Lausanne) presentation where I am JUG-Leader.
