  • President / Founder

    BigBoard Networks

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Wireless industry)

    2002 — 2004 (2 years)

The company was privately held and had ~30 employees upon acquisition.

    The ITEC Network

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Wireless industry)

    1998 — 2002 (4 years)

    The ITEC Network (renamed Transit Television) is a wireless media and technology company that provides real-time information and advertising to mass transit passengers in major markets in the United States. The company supplies government-required passenger information solutions to transit operators for free in exchange for long-term advertising contracts. Customers include Orlando, Milwaukee, Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, Atlanta and other Tier 1 and Tier 2 markets in the United States. Prior to acquisition, the company was privately funded and privately held.
