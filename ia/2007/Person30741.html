Anne Brent’s Summary

As Founder and Creative Director of Gallery No.9, I bring extensive and diverse experience in many areas of design, including corporate communication, brand development, print, packaging and web design. I graduated from Northwestern University with degrees in Radio/TV/Film and Art Theory and Practice and spent several years at a fantastic company called ThoughtWorks, working as a graphic designer, UI (user interface) designer and usability analyst. in 2006 I decided it was time to strike out on my own and Gallery No.9 was born.

I believe in producing innovative and intelligent graphic solutions - there is always a fresh approach to be taken when trying to solve communication problems. I am a firm believer in extensive collaboration with clients, the outcome of which is a 100% unique, intelligent, inspired design piece that instantly communicates.
