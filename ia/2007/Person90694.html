Steve Wells’s Summary

My interest is in working collaboratively with internal and external stakeholders to help leaders make more informed business choices.

The questions I pose to my clients include:

> how do you make sense of your operating environment?
> how do you engage your colleagues and customers in strategy development?
> what part in what you do and how you do it does collaborative working play?
> how do you develop products or services that deliver value to your customers?
> how do you develop organisational and individual capability in addressing these questions?

My intentions are to take an approach with clients that:

> encourages engagement across the organisation
> takes time to help people make sense of organisational challenges
> works collaboratively to exploit different perspectives
> focuses on amplifying existing good practice
> puts a learning process at the heart of what we do
> views constructive challenge as an essential ingredient of sense-making and strategy development
> integrates thinking and doing into the strategy process
> pays attention to the realities of a complex and uncertain world
> builds client capability development into assignments

I am an experienced strategic and operational planner, strategy developer and workshop facilitator having worked with clients to help them make sense of their operating environment in order that they can make informed choices about the development of strategy.

Supporting post-merge business consolidation, new approaches to environmental scanning and strategy development, partnership development and collaborative working capability development, I have worked as an internal consultant at Pfizer Limited’s UK Pharmaceutical business and have undertaken independent consulting assignments. In addition, I have presented on strategy development and horizon scanning at cross-industry conferences, business schools and NHS organisations.
