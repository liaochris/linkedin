Stu Libby’s Summary

Stu currently focuses on new business development in the agency vertical across all of the DoubleClick products.

Previously Stu was Director of Sales at the Root Exchange - an open marketplace for buying and selling consumer leads. Root acquired LeadFilter in December of 2005 where Stu ran sales and was the first employee. Before joining LeadFilter, Stu was a key member of the top performing sales group at MMT Sales, a division of COX Communications dedicated to selling network television affiliates' airtime to national advertisers. Stu was also co-founder and CEO of coupons4campus.com an online publisher of coupons for college students. Stu holds a Bachelors of Science Degree in Marketing from Quinnipiac University in Hamden Connecticut.

Stu is also an active member of the Make-A-Wish foundation with a seat on the New Leadership Council.
