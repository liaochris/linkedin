Tony Cruz’s Summary

Maximum Capacity Closets is locally owned and operated by Tony Cruz. We are an authorized ClosetMaid dealer serving the San Francisco Bay area in the three counties of Alameda, Contra Costa and Santa Clara.

We will show you how to maximize your storage space and create a place for everything. We will listen to what you want, design a storage solution just for you, and install it expertly. We will use only the highest quality materials -- MasterSuite® by ClosetMaid®. Our goal is to bring an elegant Sense of Order to your home and life with custom designed and expertly installed storage solutions for your closet, kitchen, laundry, garage, and office.

Reward yourself with the feeling that comes from having a place for all your belongings and knowing where to find them - the feeling you get with ClosetMaid's highest quality MasterSuite® custom products. Closets, pantries, laundry or garage storage will be expertly installed by Maximum Capacity Closets, your authorized ClosetMaid® dealer.

We'll meet with you in your home to discuss and analyze your storage needs and provide a custom design using our state-of-the-art, virtual rendering computer software. We can meet your budget requirements with an extensive array of MasterSuite finishes and styles. FREE in-home consultations and estimates are available throughout the week, including evenings and weekends.
