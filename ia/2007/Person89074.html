Victoria Pynchon’s Summary

My legal practice is now strictly limited to serving as a commercial litigation (or arbitration) neutral.

DISCLAIMER: All materials about me and my practice that appear here have been prepared by me or provided to me for informational purposes only. Nothing in these materials constitutes legal advice. Transmission of the information maintained here is not intended to create, and receipt does not constitute, an attorney-client relationship.

Internet subscribers and online readers should not act upon this information without seeking professional counsel. The information contained here is provided only as general information which may or may not reflect the most current legal developments.

This information is not provided in the course of an attorney-client relationship and is not intended to constitute legal advice or to substitute for obtaining legal advice from an attorney licensed in your state.

These materials and information may be considered advertising under applicable laws and ethical rules. I do not wish to represent anyone desiring representation based upon viewing these materials in a state where they fail to comply with all laws and ethical rules of that state.

In any event, I no longer offer legal services directly to clients. I am a full-time mediator and arbitrator, assisting parties who have legal counsel in the resolution of disputes. Only when I am engaged in community mediation through the Los Angeles County Bar Association Dispute Resolution Services do I assist individuals without counsel in the resolution of their disputes which I do gratis as part of my professional responsibilities to provide pro bono services to those in need.

Victoria Pynchon’s Specialties:

My historic legal specialties were insurance coverage, antitrust, unfair competition, patent, copyright and trademark litigation, as well as the occasional securities fraud and consumer class action. I have represented clients in the healthcare, manufacturing, garment, entertainment, insurance, finance, and banking industries.

  • ADR Neutral (Mediator/Arbitrator)

    Judicate West

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Alternative Dispute Resolution industry)

    July 2005 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Panelist at Judicate West
    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Legal Services industry)

    January 2005 – Present (2 years 7 months)

    Judicate West is the Southern California ADR panel on which I serve. Other than the work I continue to do with my own ADR firm, Settle It Now, I am exclusive to Judicate West. I chose to serve on Judicate West's panel exclusively because their case managers know the skill-sets, expertise and industry as well as legal experience of all of their neutrals, whether they be retired judges or former litigators. Judicate is able to recommend which of its neutrals is best for the particular case the parties are seeking to resolve. Judicate puts its fifteen-year reputation on the line everytime it recommends one of its neutrals, not all of whom are right for every job. I trust Judicate's people, its integrity and its professionalism. I recommend Judicate when I cannot recommend my own services. Check them out and choose the neutral who is right for you.

  • Pro Bono Community Mediator

    West Hollywood Dispute Resolution Services

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Alternative Dispute Resolution industry)

    January 2005 — Present (2 years 11 months)

I highly recommend to all mediators that they locate their local community, public-school-peer or victim-offender mediation programs to satisfy their professional pro bono obligation. You will receive far more than you give.

  • Attorney-Mediator

    Settle It Now Dispute Resolution Services (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Legal Services industry)

    April 2004 — Present (3 years 8 months)

    I founded Settle It Now Dispute Resolution Services as soon as I learned the mediation skills necessary to help lawyers settle their commercial litigation, litigation that I had been prosecuting, defending and trying for twenty-four years. Like too many litigators, I hated negotiating settlements and vastly preferred hand-to-hand combat in the courts. What I didn't know was how much I would enjoy and how naturally inclined I was to negotiation once I learned the basic skills, skills I didn't pick up in 24 years of on-the-job training. Though I am now a panelist with the Southern California ADR Services firm Judicate West, I continue to mediate (and sometimes to arbitrate) commercial disputes through Settle It Now. My areas of expertise include insurance coverage, general commercial, patent, copyright and trademark litigation, as well as unfair competition, antitrust and consumer class action disputes.
