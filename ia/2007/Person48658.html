Michael Kolowich’s Summary

Emmy-award-winning television news producer and reporter. Founder and/or leader of six high-tech ventures, including two public companies. Pioneer in internet content. Founding publisher of and columnist for Ziff-Davis' PC/Computing magazine. Architect and producer of Mitt TV, the first internet TV channel for a presidential campaign.

MBA with distinction from Harvard Business School, AB in Engineering and Applied Physics from Harvard College.

Michael Kolowich’s Specialties:

Video production, digital video, videography, script writing, DVD authoring, marketing consultant, communications strategy, private high school and college admissions, fundraising communications
