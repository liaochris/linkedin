Johnny Anderson’s Summary

Johnny Anderson is a seasoned technology executive and a leader in the Austin Community. Over the past three decades, He has developed, marketed and delivered some of the industry's most innovative solutions for network and systems management at industry leaders Saber Software, Novell, Inc., Excelan and Digital Equipment Corp. Prior to joining nVision in 2003, Mr. Anderson was Executive Director of the AeA (American Electronics Association) in Austin helping technology companies develop strategic partnerships, improve the economic development climate, gain access to capital and develop technology-friendly public policy.

Prior to joining the AeA, Anderson was the President and Chief Executive Officer for HotData, Inc., a leading provider of internet-based, real time customer intelligence solutions for the customer relationship management market. While at HotData, Anderson built the management team, developed the product, marketing and sales plans, closed a round of venture capital financing, successfully launched the company’s products and services, and signed strategic relationships with the industry’s leading CRM developers. In May 2001, Anderson successfully orchestrated the merger of HotData with Group 1 Software, the leading provider of data quality solutions.

Prior to coming to HotData, Anderson was the founder and Chief Executive Officer of e2 Software in Dallas, TX. While at e2, he attracted the management team, raised the initial two rounds of financing, oversaw the development of the products and launched a set of products that was recognized as the Internet World “Product of the Year” in 1997.

Mr. Anderson has also held executive and management positions with Saber Software, Novell, Inc., Excelan, and Digital Equipment Corporation.
