Parker Thomas’s Summary

I am a company founder with extensive experience with early stage companies - developing the business proposition, creating the offering and pricing, finding the first customers and growing from there. I have founded and sold two businesses. My last company broke even in 15 months and achieved 21% revenue growth every month that it operated - in the low tech business of document destruction.

I am looking for a new opportunity in the cleantech/greentech area with these criteria:
- A business with enough market potential to interest angel and venture funders.
- A business that will make a material difference in the world. I would like to change the environment and/or people's lives for the better.
- The opportunity to work with a bright, capable, energetic and enthusiastic team of equals or the opportunity to work with an experienced entrepreneur from whom I can learn.
- A business that can scale to a national or international level. It won't be limited to a specific geographical space.
- A business with clear competive differentiators.
- A business that fits my moral and ethical beliefs.
- A business that can create a material change in net worth.

If you have such a business or concept or know someone who does, I would like to talk to you.
