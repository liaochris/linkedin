Phillip Cartwright’s Summary

Senior Principal, Global Insight and Research Fellow in Marketing, Imperial College, Tanaka Business School (Formerly, Senior Research Fellow, INSEAD). Focused on marketing and e-marketing strategy, innovation and thought leadership related to network economics and strategy. Hold doctorate in economics with specialisation areas in econometrics, microeconomics and industrial organisation. Published widely in academic and business journals.

Senior Director, Research International. Principal in BearingPoint's (formerly Andersen Business Consulting)Strategy and Organisation team based in Paris through August 2003. Involved in significant projects in the Far East. Worked in the areas of convergence using advanced techniques such as agent-based modelling. Served as member of the advisory board of The Centre for Network Economy, London Business School. Formerly with Ernst & Young Global Client Consulting, ACNielsen and the University of Georgia. Objective to maximally leverage 20 years of experience in global business, marketing strategy and academics.

U.S. citizen, married, based in Paris for over 10 years.

Phillip Cartwright’s Specialties:

Market and marketing strategy and implementation, quantitative methods and applications, industrial organisation and thought leadership related to network economics and innovation.
