Marc Orchant’s Summary

Over the years, I've been active in the following industries:

- Newspaper & magazine publishing
- Advertising & PR
- Corporate Communications
- Digital graphics & imaging
- Software development & publishing
- Writing & Broadcast

I was named a Microsoft MVP for Tablet PCs in January 2006 (renewed for 2007). Currently, I am an independent consultant in the areas of personal and team productivity, leveraging new media for marketing and PR, and organizational learning. I am the US Technology Editor at blognation USA, co-host a Tablet PC podcast, and recently published The Unofficial Guide to Microsoft Outlook 2007 for Wiley & Sons (April 2007).
