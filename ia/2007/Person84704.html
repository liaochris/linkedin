Tekla Szymanski’s Summary

I am a multilingual foreign news editor/writer/content manager (English, German, Hebrew, French, Italian) with 15 years of domestic and international print, radio and new media experience; focus on Europe/Middle East, especially Germany and Israel.

I have lived on three continents and witnessed major global events firsthand (the Cold War in divided Berlin, the Lebanon War, the 1991 Gulf War in Israel, the First Intifadah, 9/11 in New York). I observe how political events are perceived abroad and cover news, views and trends that have yet to surface here.

I also specialize in American Jewry, German-Jewish-Israeli reconciliation, interfaith- and intercultural relations, Anti-Semitism and domestic/international women's issues.

I design, launch and maintain Web sites. Computer skills: all relevant writing, publishing softwares; QuarkXPress; QPS Copydesk, various CM systems; Dreamweaver; Photoshop; HTML/XHTML/XML; CSS; Javascript; PHP; MySql.
