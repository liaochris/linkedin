David Purkerson’s Summary

Sanford Rose Associates (Pensacola, Florida) is a premier executive search firm with a national practice to a broad range of executive areas in financial services, food processing, and closely aligned industries.

We have developed extensive industry contacts within those markets to aid in locating the exact person to meet our client's needs. We only conduct custom searches for executive and senior managerial and professional positions --- we never represent candidates in finding new positions.

We pride ourselves in our ability to identify the rare candidates to fit our client's specific long-term needs consistent with the client's individual style and corporate culture. Our consulting services are conducted with adherence to the highest professional and ethical standards.
