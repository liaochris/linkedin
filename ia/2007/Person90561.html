Christian Delvaux’s Summary

Over 20 years of experience in management.

Now owner of Webadev.com.

Since 2004, Webadev.com, Web Agency located in Liege (Belgium) realizes internet sites for the promotion and the development of brand awareness. Thanks to its expertise about the internet practices, Webadev.com realizes sites adapted to customer's image and needs while meeting the internet users’ expectations.
Our motto: "High Touch – High Tech".
We work with our customers to put in place a communication consistent with their image and in phase with the different publics targeted. The solutions that we offer are suitable for our customers' needs and use the latest internet technologies and norms in order to optimize their site and ensure it looks same for all visitors.
