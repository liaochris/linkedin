Evan Payne’s Summary

Evan was born and raised in Los Angeles and has gained a unique perspective on the ever shifting cultural soup that has coalesced amongst the hill and valley crowds. He discovered poetry and music at an early age, and found his true calling in telling stories using the medium of film. He considers himself to be an artist on many levels, a photographer, a composer, an editor, and a screenwriter; but his deepest passion is directing. The essence of his mindset is to use every person on set, every stylistic trick and hat tip, anything and everything involved, to enhance the story being told and to teach the audience something new.
