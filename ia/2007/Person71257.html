Ray Prince’s Summary

Have continued to pass various financial papers and hold the Advanced Financial Planning Certificate and the Diploma in Financial Planning accredited by the Chartered Insurance Institute. I am also working towards the Certified Financial Planner licence, awarded by the Institute of Financial Planning. If you don't know about this qualification and what the benefits could be of working with an adviser who has this qualification, visit the IFP's website:
http://www.financialplanning.org.uk

My passion is working with and helping clients who want an unbiased view as to how they can achieve their lifestyle and financial goals and objectives. I am very passionate about what I do and believe all financial advice/planning should be totally independent and impartial. I believe individuals should have access to advice that does not necessarily lead to them buying yet another policy or investment (which is often the last thing they need). It's important to say that I provide a financial planning service (as opposed to a policy/product retailing service) and follow a specific process that has been refined over the years. The aim is to create a plan that will enable you to see if you are on track to achieve your lifetime goals. This involves a lot of analysis to create the plan but is well worth it, see client
comments at our website http://www.medicaldentalfs.com/client-comments

My approach is to educate clients about financial matters. After all, the more informed you are about anything in life, the better your decision making should be. I suggest you join our members only free email newsletter 'Financial Tips'. We send it out twice monthly and you'll learn a lot from it. We cover one main article along with a Q&A each issue and it takes about 5 minutes to read. Simply visit our main website and follow the instructions. http://www.medicaldentalfs.com/linkedin
