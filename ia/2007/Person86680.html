Tom Carter, CFA’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    Fountain Financial Partners

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Investment Management industry)

    February 2006 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Fountain Financial Partners, LLC was formed at the beginning of 2006 to fill the gap between traditional lenders and equity financing. The current fund serves companies who have immediate or near term needs of $50,000 to $500,000 and our capital is structured in the form of lease lines of credit or sale and leasebacks. We consider any stage of company including entrepreneur backed (zero stage), angel or seed fund backed start ups, institutional or corporate venture capital backed companies, other private or publicly held companies regardless of current financial condition. Clients need not have revenues, or venture capital investment. Clients need not issue warrants, equity, or future equity investment rights. Fountain Financial Partners is positioned as a complement to, not necessarily a substitute for, traditional bank and equity capital.
