Ágúst Guðbjörnsson’s Summary

Overall I´ve worked in endless web/print design projects and custom developments that involve php (LAMP). In these projects I´m usually project manager, and with focus onto the design aspect of the applications.

All these projects have had special needs such as contracts, outlines, wireframes and even complex graphs. I think that experience of making those things is one of my main advantage on the market today. It´s one thing to do such things in school, another when you have a live client that need´s to understand and pay for what you work with. I guess it´s my way of learning and living, I need deadlines and strict environment around me all the time.

On the other-hand I´ve recently started to make in-store advertisements and take film-editing on the table if I´m asked. It´s my goal to expand Nebonmedia to be media partner instead of only focusing on the web-media aspect. I believe that most kinds of medias will combine like one someday, meaning you will be able to interact the same way with television and on the internet.

Because of this, I believe it´s for great importance to try my very best in expanding my knowledge over both motion designs and the technologies behind them. Though with focus on the design structure of the material at all times. I will always need to understand what is driving it forward, then again you cant be an expert in both developing and designing - It´s same as saying "I´m a plumber and electronican".
