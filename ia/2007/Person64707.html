Ogan Gurel, MD’s Summary

The Aesis Group is a healthcare & life sciences consultancy whose clients have included startups, large companies and investment firms. Previously I was CEO of Duravest, Inc. as well as a Director at Estracure – a Montreal-based coronary stent startup and at BMTS – an orthopedic injury device company.

My background in healthcare spans the business, scientific and clinical spectrum. As VP & Medical Director at Sg2 – a leading healthcare consultancy - I lead consulting and content teams, helped drive marketing & business development, and was the principal architect of the Impact of Change database - an industry-leading healthcare utilization forecasting system. Prior to that I was engaged in several projects with Fortune 500 healthcare and life sciences companies while at the blue-chip management consultancy, Booz Allen Hamilton.

My research in structural biology has been published in the peer-reviewed literature. I've also taught computer science, cellular & molecular biology, neuroanatomy, bioinformatics and mathematical modeling at NYU, Columbia, Roosevelt and Harvard universities and am presently Adjunct Associate Professor of Bioengineering at the University of Illinois in Chicago. I have spoken worldwide on the future of healthcare and have served as a juror for the 2007 Medical Design Excellence Awards. My particular focus has been on convergent medical technologies including medical nanotechnology and my commentaries have been published in many prominent publications, including the Wall Street Journal.
