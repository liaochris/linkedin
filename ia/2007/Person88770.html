Veerle Pieters’s Summary

Started in 1992 as a freelance graphic designer under the name of Duoh!

Started designing websites and GUI for intranet websites in 1995 together with mulitmedia development.

Founded Duoh! n.v. in 2000 together with Geert Leyseele.

Running a popular weblog called 'Veerle's blog' about topics ranging from XHTML/CSS to graphic design tips.

Veerle Pieters’s Specialties:

GUI for software, internet applications and websites.
Creation of XHTML/CSS based websites using (W3C) web standards.
Logo and housestyle design.
Illustration work.
