Mark Drasutis’s Summary

Over a decade of digital media experience defining and delivering creative, user-focused solutions to media and new media businesses. Creative vision, strategic thinking, strong leadership and an excellent technical understanding, allows me understand and communicate the ‘why, how and what’, to achieve the most compelling online experiences. Having worked with companies of all sizes and gained extensive knowledge of how they operate, I have successful completed a number of large scale internet and publishing system projects.
A key strategic thinker, with excellent communication skills, I am able to inspire and motivate the team around me, continually pushing new boundaries.
