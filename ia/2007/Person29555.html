Sean Alvarez’s Summary

After 10+ years working in the entertainment industry, in positions ranging from Production Accountant (Film) - Supervising Producer (Television), I decided to put this wide range of skill sets to work for myself.

In May 2006, my sister Sarah and I took the reigns of Floral Creations by Wayne, a floral design studio with a 25 year history of delivering exquisite floral designs to all of Los Angeles.

At Floral Creations LA, we specialize in providing exceptional floral design with personalized service for our clients for any occasion.

Weddings, Special Events, Corporate Accounts, and Just Because.
