Patrick Crane’s Summary

My experience represents 12 years of hi tech business to consumer marketing in 5 markets on 3 continents.

I have led teams, consumer product launches, strategic initiatives and brand campaigns around mobile and internet virtually my whole career, spanning Direct Marketing, TV, Radio, Product Marketing, CRM, Affinity Marketing, Bus Dev, PR, Grassroots and WOM. I have led the growth of innovations from nothing more than a glint in someone's eye to multi million $ lines of business.

This has led me most recently into the social web sphere, developing an expertise in web 2.0 marketing, social networking and community development.

I thrive in smaller working environments that embody fast-paced part-data-part-gut decision making, risk taking and innovation. I specialize in the "what-if", and work best with people who have a similar outlook.
