Per Axbom’s Summary

Senior consultant and web strategist, specializing in usability, searchability, information architecture, evaluations and user-centered design.

Experienced in working with information and design issues for websites, intranets and web-based applications for a large number of Swedish and international organizations.

I am also an experienced speaker; I give seminars and regularly write essays and articles. My motivation is to create opportunities for all people to understand and use information, regardless of their conditions.

Per Axbom’s Specialties:

Usability, accessibility, web standards, information architecture, evaluations, user-centered design, open source, portals, content management, blogs (blogging), web trends, web strategy, searchability, search engine optimization, Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI), Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).
