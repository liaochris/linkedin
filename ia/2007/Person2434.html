Fabio Akita’s Summary

Even though I realize that just sheer volume of years accounts for nothing, I've been working in the field for a decade and I've fortunately accumulated a dependable skill set.

I am specialized in diversity. Which means that I have free pass through a wide range of technologies. I am skillful Java programmer as well as I am a middle class .NET develope. And I have experience in several other platforms such as ABAP/4, PHP and even some commercially "exotic" ones as Python. I believe in good solutions, whichever platform the customer may choose.

Recently I published my first book, called "Repensando a Web com Rails", the first book about Ruby on Rails published in Brazil, dedicated to the brazilian audience.

I am most interested in researching, realizing new and interesting solutions, coaching skillful programmers and coordinating development teams. I am also a certified PMP project manager and I was held accountable of a few projects that fortunately delivered very well.

As a project leader I am also very interested in everything related to Agile Processes, understanding the paperwork of the so called "monumental processes" such as PMI, CMM and all the methodologies and tools that can help dealing with a project without too many problems.

Fabio Akita’s Specialties:

I have a very short learning curve, for just about anything. I'm also very strong in realizing solutions and breaking down big problems in small sets of manageable activities. And, of course, couching and coordinating the team to-do list to better usage of all resources available.
