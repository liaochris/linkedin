Wayne Chavez’s Summary

Prior to founding InterMundo Media, Wayne was a Partner at Soho Digital, a full-service online ad agency with over 200 clients and $30 million in annual revenues. He oversaw business development, sales, and campaign management. Wayne's clients included Monster.com, United Airlines, Chase, NBC and Avenue A. Soho Digital was acquired by an online advertising network in 2004. His earlier online experience includes founding a venture-funded online retailer in 1999.

Wayne’s fifteen year career in direct marketing began at Grupo Televisa – the largest media conglomerate in the Spanish-speaking world – where he helped launch and operate the company’s direct marketing division accross its network, cable, print, radio and outdoor advertising assets.

Wayne also successfully launched the Shredit® franchise in Argentina, the world's leading document disposal service. His clients included Microsoft, Procter & Gamble, Compaq, Citibank, and FedEx.

Wayne has lived and worked in Mexico City, Buenos Aires, and Quito, Ecuador. He holds an MBA from Harvard Business School and a BA from Stanford University.
