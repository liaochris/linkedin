Spreadshirt's mission is to be the world's leading platform for creative apparel.

Founded in Leipzig, Germany in 2002 by German graduate students Lukasz Gadowski and Matthias Spiess, Spreadshirt became the leading provider of custom, online apparel in Europe and one of it's most successful Internet start-ups. Spreadshirt.com made an immediate impact in the United States and has since grown to a community of over 150,000 partners who have created and sold over 500,000 custom products worldwide. Spreadshirt's US operations include a fulfillment and customer service center in Greensburg, PA and is headquartered in downtown Pittsburgh, PA.
