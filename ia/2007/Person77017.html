Simon Bisson’s Summary

Freelance technology writer and IT strategy consultant.

Currently available for short term IT strategy consulting roles, and technology writing (including commercial white papers and ghostwriting).

Messaging Editor for Server Management
Contributing Editor for PC Plus
Handling Server and Server OS and Networking for IT Pro with Mary Branscombe

Recent consulting projects have included work with large transport companies and e-government consulting.

On a freelance basis I'm now Messaging Editor for Server Management magazine, where I cover enterprise email and messaging technologies on a monthly basis. I'm also a contributing editor for PC Plus, where I write the Office development column. Elsewhere I write about enterprise IT for IT Pro, consumer tools for The Official Windows XP Magazine, I cover web technologies for Web Designer, developer tools and technologies for The Register and contribute to the FT's Digital Business section.

I was a regular contributor to The Guardian's Inside IT section, covering a range of enterprise IT issues, until the section was closed with a recent redesign. Until recently I also had a web and Internet development column in the sadly closed Application Development Advisor. I also ran ASP and PHP columns in the now closed Web Developer.

I have written technology white papers for many of the world's top IT companies, as well as undertaking commissioned commercial writing for corporate magazines and publications.
