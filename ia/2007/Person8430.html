Anamarija Stancic Petrovic’s Summary

With twelve years of experience in the field of intellectual property, Anamarija concentrates on handling patent and trademark matters, as well as design and domain name issues. Her responsibilities range from clearance and registration to handling IP enforcement matters. She is also in charge of the overall management of the Croatian office.

Prior to joining SD PETOSEVIC, she spent seven years at a leading Croatian law firm as a Senior Patent and Trademark Consultant. Anamarija joined SD PETOSEVIC in 2004.

Anamarija also contrubuted numerous articles and newsletters on Intellectual Property Protection in Croatia that were published in various world Intellectual Property Magazines such as International Bar Association, BNA Eastern Europe, INTA Bulletin, Trademark World. She also contrubuted to the Section on Intellectual Property Legislation in Croatia for the book "S'implanter en Croatie" prepared by Mission Economique de Zagreb and published by UBIFRANCE.

She is a member of Croatian Association for Consumers Protection (HUZP); International Trademark Association (INTA); Licensing Executive Society in Croatia (LES Croatia) and Life Science Committee (LSC) of LES International.
