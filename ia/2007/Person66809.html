Paul Silder’s Summary

I am a seasoned strategic communications professional, possessing more than 21 years experience including the first 10 years as a newspaper journalist. Since 1995, I have worked at marketing firms throughout Mid-Atlantic region, making my mark as an well-rounded strategic communications professional while distinguishing myself in the areas of communications strategies, media relations and brand development.

I have supervisory experience with staffs up to 10 people and most recently served as a Director at CorpComm, Inc., supervising nine employees who work across a number of U.S. Army and commercial accounts. In addition to my management responsibilities, I also served as the company’s proposal manager and lead quality assurance/quality control (QA/QC) efforts for all of the company’s writing and multimedia projects.

Previously, as the Vice President of a small public relations agency, I had been responsible for across-the-board client service and supervised the business development and account management staffs.
