  • COO

    Funny or Die

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    April 2007 — Present (2 months)

    Funny or Die is a Sequoia Capital-funded start up that strives to be the leading online platform for video shorts created by funny people. To differentiate ourselves from other online comedy video sites, and to ensure a steady flow of hilarious content, we've partnered with Gary Sanchez Productions (a collaboration between Will Ferrell, Adam McKay, and Chris Henchy) and Creative Artists Agency (CAA).

    We launched the site in mid April '07 with an exclusive Will Ferrell short called "The Landlord" and the response has been amazing. In our first two weeks, the site received over 7MM unique visitors and streamed over 30MM videos.

    My role with Funny or Die is to execute our vision of being the first site people think of when looking for truly funny videos without having to wade through a sea of mediocre content. If we do it right, people all over the world will be laughing a lot more.

  • CEO

    Insider Pages

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    October 2006 — April 2007 (7 months)

    Insider Pages is a local search site that enables friends and neighbors to share word-of-mouth wisdom about local businesses of all kinds. Millions of visitors come to the site every month to take advantage of one of the Web's largest collections of customer-written business reviews.

    The company was originally funded by Sequoia Capital, SoftBank Capital, and Idealab but was acquired in Febuary of 2007 by IAC.

    As CEO my role was to chart the company's strategy, manage day-to-day operations, oversee business/corporate development, make happy/productive employees, and delight users of our site. I also held a seat on the Insider Pages Board of Directors.

    During my tenure as CEO Insider Pages costs decreased by more than 60%, our traffic grew by more than 4X and our revenue grew by more than 3X.
