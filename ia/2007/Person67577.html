Peter Ekman’s Summary

I'm the President of Cosentino Wineries, a Napa Valley based company listed on the London AIM Exchange. Cosentino is a vibrant, innovative and very successful winery.

Up to recently, I have been developing a handful of entrepreneuerial initiatives within E-Commerce and consumer goods (www.organicbouquet.com, Epic Commerce Inc., NASA Inc., Proud Wolf LLC, Polar Design, FactsTalk), Software development (Stixy Inc.). and social networking startups (www.piczo.com and www.supervine.com).

Prior to these companies, I was the CEO for Wine.com, and had executive and management positions with companies such as Procter & Gamble, Mars Inc., LVMH, and Icon Media Lab.

I am an international CEO and Executive with a exceptional successful Turnaround-Growth career in the wine industry, consumer goods, and eCommerce industries. [Procter & Gamble, Mars Inc., Wine.com, LVMH, Organic Bouquet Inc.]
