Stephen O'Neill’s Summary

Strategic Human Resources Executive with extensive experience in Employee Benefits and domestic and international corporate transactions including mergers, acquisitions and divestitures. Skilled in leading complex, domestic and international benefit plan design, conversion and integration projects. Proven expertise in leading Human Resources due diligence for corporate transactions including contract drafting and negotiation, and the development of post-acquisition integration strategies and action plans. Innovative problem-solver with strong analytical and communications skills. Accomplished manager of internal teams and vendor relationships.
