Jose Machicao, PMP, MSc’s Summary

Organizational Project Management Consultant, with 10 years experience managing projects, programs and portfolios, creating and implementing methodologies for project and program management at public and private organizations. Wide experience implementing OPM3 surveys and solution planning. Lecturer at more than 5 universities in Lima, Peru. MSc in Energy Management, Certified PMP and member of several professional institutions in Peru and globally. Member of the PMI Government SIG.

I would like to be recognized as a solution provider, specially on organizational project management, skillful to manage teams to implement practical management methodologies for all kind of organizations, strongly linking strategy and tactics.

Main clients:
- Sectorial Public Entities (Ministries)
- Subnational Public Entities (Municipalities and Regional Governments)
- Governa, Public Mangement Consulting
- SUNAT, Peruvian Tax Management Office
