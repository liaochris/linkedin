Declan O'Connor’s Summary

Experienced Business Development Manager with specialist expertise in identifying new opportunities, articulating these to investors, and motivating start-up teams to bring disruptive technologies to market.

Bio: "Declan O'Connor is a veteran of the electronic payments, internet security, and embedded systems industries. With 18 years experience as a disruptive technology strategist he has led many high profile projects across Scandinavia, Europe, Australia, and the USA. Declan has worked in key roles for leading multinationals including Gtech Corporation [GTK], Tellabs Corporation [TLAB], Avocent [AVCT], and Asea Brown Boveri [ABB]. A previous winner of the UK Venture Capital Association's European Technology Entrepreneur award, he was the driving force behind a series of high tech start-ups in Sweden and Ireland including Smartcard Initiatives, Nixflu AB, Capinova AB, Control Access, Zanco Technologies, and Muni-Pay Technologies. During his tenure as COO and Head of Strategy at Global Currency Exchange, he led the successful delivery of multimillion-euro eBusiness projects for airlines such as Ryanair and British Midlands, and was selected to spearhead the company's activities in Australia. More recent assignments included an interim CEO role with MXSweep, a managed security venture offering Hosted Email Filtering services globally on outsourced basis. Declan studied Electronic Engineering at Dublin City University, Electronic Product Design at Dundalk Institute of Technology (DKIT), Microprocessor Engineering at the University of Limerick and Technology Entrepreneurship at Queens University Belfast. He is a member of the Project Management Institute."
