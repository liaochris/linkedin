Nuno Do Carmo’s Summary

IT degree as IT specialist - 3 years
Web Developper (ASP) - 1 year

Project and Shop coordinator - today's job

For the past 5 years, I've successfully changed 3 times the type of my work.

I started back in 2001 as a Web Developper for a startup, Fastnet SA.
My principal project was to maintenain and implement new solutions for a home made Content Management which was used by several sites of the first press editor in Switzerland.

I went then to Geneva, to work as a Programmer Analyst with ASP and Magic (L4G) at Delphisoft SA.
My mission was to continue and finish the implementation of a stock management for a department of Geneva's city administration.

For three years, I worked as a consultant in the helpdesk of Orange SA.
I started as simple operator, but my IT knowledge helped me to evolve to a post of Backoffice operator where I have to manage and create the domain windows accounts, mailboxes and distribution lists.
Before changing for my actual post, I was also at 50% the deputy of the IT Helpdesk Manager. The tasks were mainly the creation of Business Objects reports and team management when the Manager was absent.

After that, Orange's responsibles proposed me a job of coordination for the IT part of the Orange Shops and for our internal IT projects (Videoconferencing, Asset Tool and Printer's park).
With my actual Team Leader, we were in charge of these 4 major projects.
Major, because it was always in the scale of the whole company, swiss wide, which potential impact was 2000+ employees (internal/external), 2000+ computers (PC/Laptops) and 150+ multifunction printers.


Today, I'm still in Orange, but currently I'm looking for a new job for the same tasks I've undertaken or, as I love to learn new skills, for something totally new but still in IT.
