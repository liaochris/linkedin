Nivedita Basu’s Summary

I have over thirteen years of experience as a designer and design consultant working with Fortune 100 clients across various industries including software and web application development for technology companies, distance learning programs for universities and businesses and e-commerce applications for the retail industry.

I have experience in shipping and launching new products to market, planning and developing product strategy, bringing innovative design solutions, and leading projects from concept through successful implementation.

My professional design practice and experience involve developing design strategy, planning of new products, analysis of emerging technolgies and markets. I combine my analytical and creative design skills with in depth user research to create unique design solutions that meet specific business needs and provide solutions that are innovative, strategic, user-centric and elegant.
