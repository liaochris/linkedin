Asmo Halinen’s Summary

I'm one of the three founders and former CEO of Apaja. Nowadays I manage Apaja’s marketing and global business development activities. I have a versatile career in the Finnish internet industry since 1995. Among many other things I've been leading the creation of many internet content/media businesses in the country and abroad.

I am also a co-founder and former CEO of digital marketing communication agency Creapaja Oy, where I proved my selling skills by establishing client relationships with leading Finnish companies from various fields, e.g. Finnair, Yhtyneet Kuvalehdet, and Nokia.

Asmo Halinen’s Specialties:

I believe I possess quite a good understanding of internet, its technologies and design, as well as of the creation of online community services with business acumen. In addition, my core competencies consist of strong HR skills due to the various manager positions now and in the past.
