John Havens’s Summary

John C. Havens is currently VP, Business Development for http://www.blogtalkradio.com, where he has helped make connections to numerous Fortune 500 companies to introduce them to BTR's Social Broadcasting technology.

He is currently writing a book tentatively titled, "Media 2.Open" with new media guru Shel Holtz. Their blog: http://www.transparencybook.typepad.com

John C. Havens was the About.com Guide to Podcasting, (http://podcasting.about.com) Founder of Podcast Vision and Voice (a podcast/new media consultation/creation firm; www.pvandv.com) and Lead Organizer for PodCamp NYC (www.podcampnyc.org). JCH initially moved to NYC in 1992 to pursue a career in acting, where he was featured (principal/speaking roles) on Broadway (and off) in TV, and Film. Along the way he tried his hand at writing corporate scripts and fell into marketing, consulting, and PR. He has written five non-fiction titles, one novel, two one-person shows, plays guitar/harmonica, runs a Father’s Group, and loves the fact that the new media marketplace allows for people from varied and colorful backgrounds to speak their voice with passion and conviction.

John Havens’s Specialties:

Business Development, Podcast/New Media Consultation, Marketing, Copywriting, ghostwriting, web copy, full-length non-fiction (as marketing/PR materials for clients).
