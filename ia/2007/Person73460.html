Robert Van Horne’s Summary

Robert Van Horne is an experienced and talented pianist and composer who has delighted audiences with his friendly personality and his extensive repertoire of music. A list of selections include his unique compositions, traditional classical works and current and favorite popular tunes many of which he has updated with creative arrangements. His greatest asset is his natural ability to entertain audiences with a mixture of relaxing, romantic, and upbeat music.

Robert has performed on TV and radio, as well as in concerts for audiences both in the U.S. and Asia.

Today, he regularly performs benefit concerts for charities with his unique style of bringing humor and fun to his audiences as well as encouraging their participation during the program.

Robert's goal is to give entertaining concerts to worldwide audiences who enjoy listening to familiar and original music.

Robert Van Horne’s Experience

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Music industry)

    April 1993 — Present (14 years 2 months)

    Robert regularly performs for audiences in the Bay Area.

    In addition to giving concerts, he is responsible for the managing, the accounting, and the overall business development planning for his company, WPI Records.

    In 2006, he completed work on his first concerto for piano and orchestra entitled THE GREAT WALL CONCERTO.

    Warner Brothers Publication published his CHINA LOVE compositions in a piano book available worldwide in 1998.

    In 1994, he created WPI Records, an independent record company for production and distribution of his recordings. He has released five piano CD albums, all of which feature many popular and classical selections as well as original compositions.

    From 1985 to 1988, while living in Singapore, he recorded many of the popular Chinese songs for EMI Records.
