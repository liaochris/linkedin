We build brands. YOURS.

Paul Copcutt is the successful personal brand coach for professionals who are struggling with an action plan and the confidence to land the six figure job they deserve.

His clients become “Square Pegs” who stand out above the competition by getting noticed for the right reasons to be hired for the jobs they’ve always wanted.

A Certified Personal Brand Strategist and specialist for the leading career blog – Career Hub and also Higher Bracket.ca, Canada’s only $100k+ job board, also top global Career & Training contributor to the Knowledge Forum at Marketing Profs.com and an active member of the Career Professionals of Canada

Quoted in The Globe & Mail and EnRoute Magazine he is an invited national speaker on the subject of personal branding and has appeared on Report on Business TV.

Also responsible for delivery globally for the Certified Personal Brand Strategist and the 360 Reach Assessment Certification programs for Reach - the global leader in personal branding methodology.

He is involved in various community initiatives coupled with his constant passion for branding and networking also ensures that clients continue to be kept aware of new developments in all areas to update their personal brands.

Paul is also passionate about the future of Canada and the youth that will be taking that responsibility forward and is actively involved with the non-profit Youth in Motion, a network of dynamic community leaders dedicated to helping youth make meaningful career and life decisions, while inspiring them to be contributing Canadian citizens.
