Dan Tomlin’s Summary

Raised in Dallas, and graduated from Hillcrest HS in North Dallas. Earned a BBA from TCU, and MBA from SMU.

His career began in 1970 in the land investment and development field. Tomlin currently works for Land Advisors, LTD., which manages a portfolio valued at over $350MM.

In the fall of 1988 Tomlin was named to the board of the Real Estate center at the Wharton School of Business for six years. In March of 1988, the Federal Republic of Germany issued an appointment to Tomlin of honorary consul, and he was also honored as dean of the DFW consular corps for a period of two years in the early nineties. In 2002 Tomlin received the Order of Merit awarded by the president of the Federal Republic of Germany. In his spare time, Tomlin is a professional driver on the Mazda Star Series racing circuit.
