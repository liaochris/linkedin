Artur Swietanowski’s Summary

I am a professional (certified) IT Project Manager. Coming from a consulting background, I am used to having full responsibility for a project, throughout its complete lifecycle (from bidding to handing over the products) – including budget, scope, timeframe, relationships with senior stakeholders & suppliers and all other aspects.

I have 10+ years' experience in IT, 6 years as a Project Manager. I have been in charge of large & very complex IT projects & programmes, of project portfolios, a leader of team of PM’s. I currently hold both a PMP & a PRINCE2 certificate. Last but not least, I have experience in introducing project management methodology & practice in companies I worked for.

I have significant business experience – esp. in mobile telecoms & new media (ca. 4 years) and finance & insurance (ca. 3 years). My projects have ranged from business analyses, through satisfaction surveys and security audits, to a credit risk analysis system and a very large CRM implementation.

I have very strong analytical skills and outstanding academic record – Ph.D. in Operations Research (with applications in finance), followed by research assistantship in Vienna University. Both during my academic career, and afterwards, I have successfully taught hundreds of students in courses ranging from programming, through optimization methods, to project management and project finance. Recently I have prepared ca. 20 PM’s for PMI certification exam, which they have subsequently passed. I have excellent written and verbal communication skills, as well as ability to explain technical issues to either technical or business audiences.
