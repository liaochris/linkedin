Brooks Van Norman’s Summary

My network is about people I am in business with today or have worked with and know personally or professionally from the past. I am not about quantity, only quality. If you are a 'link builder' looking to increase your list of contacts, I'm not that interested.

However, if we've crossed paths somehow, I am pleased to help you however I can. I am a "pay it forward" networker who is interested in furthering your cause with no strings attached, because the world is small and we each need all the help we can get. I do not keep a "favour bank".

I am an energetic, empathetic leader who values execution and will support good ideas. I subscribe to the following pillars of business success:

* Speed of Innovation (This implies of course that you actually ARE innovating)
