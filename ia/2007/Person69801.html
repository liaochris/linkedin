Alden Purrington, III’s Summary

Chip is an experienced entrepreneur and investment banker with 18 years of expertise in private placements, M&A and high-tech management consulting. Chip utilizes his established network in New England, New York, and California to successfully raise capital for a diverse set of companies from Internet search to chip manufacturers, to advertising and media companies to medical devices and services to wireless security to consumer product and retail.

Chip’s prior experience includes 12 years of operating experience success. His leadership resulted in revenue growth along with marketing and product innovation, sales process innovation, and opening new markets for entrepreneurial ventures and established brands - Mattel, Disney, Quaker Oats and the Stroh Brewery Company. As an entrepreneur, he was part of the senior management team that built and sold a media and ecommerce company to CMGi for $25MM.

Chip is a General Securities Principal and a DPP rep (NASD Series 24, 62,63 and 22). He earned an MBA from the University of Michigan and a BA in Psychology from Bates College.

Chip is on the Steering Committee for the Boston Harbor Angels, the Steering and Committee for the MIT Enterprise Forum in NH (www.nhhtc.org) and is a Trustee for the Town of Newfields, NH

Private placements, M&A, and strategic planning and advisory services for growth stage companies.
