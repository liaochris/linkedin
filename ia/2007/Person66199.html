Patrick Driessen’s Summary

Patrick has over 18 years experience in sales management, marketing, consulting and leadership positions in professional services and products firms. As an entrepreneur and sales and consulting leader he has been successful in the arena of sales leadership, deal making, marketing, operations, solution & product development, business development and human resources. Patrick has been responsible for the turn-around and expansion of several companies, business units and projects. He is entrepreneurial in implementation of new business models (he successfully started and managed several new companies), and is an analytical- and independent thinker. He has a wide vision and is a go-getter, used to work within financial- and organizational constraints. Negotiated and closed international and global deals and settlements and has understanding of the commercial processes in large and small corporations. Translates business requirements into solutions and relates technology to business needs.
Patrick used to work out of Amsterdam, London & New York and now works out of Sydney.
