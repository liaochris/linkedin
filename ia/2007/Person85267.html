David Thiel’s Summary

Auragen specializes in developing brand focused web experiences which convert: leading directly to increased sales or reduced costs for our clients. My personal experience is in consumer retail brands in particular, and I've had the pleasure of working with many of the nation's leading brands on many projects.

I pride myself on basing Internet strategy on the ultimate business goals of my clients -- not on generally meaningless targets like "more traffic" or "a reason for customers to return to the site." Every engagement begins with the development of a deep and abiding understanding of the client's business and economic model.

David Thiel’s Specialties:

Experiential branding, online retailing, strategic business planning and consulting, analysis of new products/categories and startup operations, service organization operations management, creative and technical writing, information architecture/design, consultative selling.
