Ben Straley’s Summary

In the summer of 2006, a business partner and I founded Spring Creek Group. We are a marketing strategy and services firm that helps companies to accelerate customer awareness and sales online. Our proven methodologies increase sales conversion, brand equity, and positive return on investment for clients of all sizes. We achieve these results by partnering with clients early in the planning process to develop the right strategies, plans and tactics to deliver marketing programs that are tightly aligned with our clients' business objectives and deliver bottom-line results. If you are in search of assistance with marketing plan development, messaging and positioning, go-to-market programs and sales materials development, cost-effective customer acquisition and retention, improving website performance and discoverability, building brand equity, and/or expanding market reach, then SCG can help.
