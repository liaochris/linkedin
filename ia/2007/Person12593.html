Brayden Olson’s Summary

I am an unusually dedicated individual committed above all else to making a positive impact on society and culture through the applied use of technology and philosophy. I am also a hard-working, young entrepreneur in the technology industry.

At Novel Conception, we strive to use our technologies to empower people and to help people learn about themselves, international perspectives and to become more considerate, concerned and confident about the issues we face as a growing worldwide community.

I subscribe to the idea that leadership development is a lifelong journey. It is not a destination we arrive at when we receive an MBA. Throughout my life I seek to remain committed to becoming a better leader who is constantly finding ways to empower the people I work with so that they can achieve their personal and professional goals. I also obligate myself to be a leader that takes responsibility for my actions and who acts with integrity. I have fully come to believe that being an effective leader in the 21st century comes from focusing on helping others rather than focusing on how to help yourself—an idea many of our national leaders have forgotten.

I have been mentioned in BusinessWeek as a young leader, “who is ready to step onto the big stage,” and have recently been asked to be a guest speaker to a group of executive MBAs at the University of Washington.

However, I feel my greatest personal achievement has nothing to do with awards or my operational track record with Novel Conception. Bill George is a national leadership personality who has interacted with more than 125 of our nation’s greatest business and political leaders to compile his books on authentic leadership. His becoming a friend and mentor to me, as well as choosing to use me as an example in a number of different communications, is something that I am very proud of and that speaks to my having the qualities not just to launch a start-up, but to lead a large integrity-driven organization.

Brayden Olson’s Specialties:

I align, empower and inspire people with a positive mutual vision. I am also very comfortable making plans that account for factors such as future technology trends, sociology and psychology.
