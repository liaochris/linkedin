Fernando Barbella’s Summary

Fernando Barbella was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, where he studied for a Communication Science Degree at Buenos Aires University. He began his new media communications career in 1996 in a small software developing company, and he is the current Creative Director at OgilvyInteractive Latina South (Buenos Aires & Santiago de Chile).

He leads projects such as websites, hotsites, ad banner campaigns, interstitials, beyond-the-banners, viral marketing, interactive TV and mobile marketing for clients like American Express, IBM, Lenovo, Motorola, GlaxoSmithKline, Coca-Cola, Sprite, Kimberly-Clark, Dove, Kodak, Panasonic, FIAT, Santander RIO Bank, Banco Estado, Brahma Beer, Hellmann´s, Nestle, Telefonica, and SAP.

Fernando’s work has been recognised at the London International Advertising Awards, Eagle Awards México, Ojo de Iberoamérica, FIAP, Festival Mundial de Publicidad de Gramado, the International Business Awards, the Argentina Creative Circle, the Interactive Advertising Bureau and the Argentinean Direct and Interactive Marketing Association. He leads the marketing taskforce at the local chapter of the Interactive Advertising Bureau, is a member of the IADAS (International Academy
of Digital Arts and Sciences), a founder member of the Argentinean Direct Creative Circle and Ambassador of the Webby Awards in Argentina.

Fernando Barbella’s Specialties:

online advertising, mobile marketing, new consumer trends, branding and identity, interactive marketing, viral marketing
