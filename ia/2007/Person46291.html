Karmi Anna Mattson’s Summary

Karmi Anna provides public speaking, training, and consulting services through her company, Karmi Anna Mattson, LLC. One of her areas of speciality is mystery shopping, and she currently teaches Gold Certification Workshops on behalf of the Mystery Shopping Providers Association.

In her civic life, Karmi Anna is a member of the Grinnell College Alumni Council and is the Twin Cities Coordinator for the Grinnell Regional Admissions Support Program. She serves as Chairperson of the Lutheran Volunteer Corps in the Twin Cities, and is the President of her ELCA congregation, University Lutheran Church of Hope.

mystery shopping, customer service, writing and editing, leadership consulting, event planning
