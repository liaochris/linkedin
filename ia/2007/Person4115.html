    Singer/Songwriter ACE, a native of Las Vegas, NV was writing songs & performing in small shows since the age of 11. He is now writing and recording his own songs through his small production team called "SoulTree Music. He has also written for & recorded with artist among different independent labels. ACE contributes most of his singing and writing influence to legendary R&B groups and Gospel artist such as Guy, Marvin Gay, Usher, Brian McKnight, and Fred Hammond. ACE has a dynamic and diverse style, although his style is geared towards R&B, his writing can also fit into the musical realms of Neo Soul and Hip Hop/ Pop. ACE’s passion for music has driven him to record, and perform in various places along the western coast of the United States. Ace's goal is to be heard around the world for years to come.
