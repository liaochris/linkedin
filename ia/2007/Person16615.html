Chris Marini’s Summary

The founder of Network Savants, LLC in 2002, Christopher Marini had been the principal of C.W. Marini & Associates since 1998. Chris had started with a very specific direction: To provide professionalism and integrity in information technology consulting. His vision for Network Savants is to bring to you the right mix of people, experience and technologies; so that you can focus on running your business and not your computer systems.
