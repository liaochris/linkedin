Tom Vincent’s Summary

Hi I'm Tom.

I'm an award winning freelance Art Director with backgrounds rooted in Graphic Design and Photography. I'm passionate about strategic thinking, conceptual messaging, and typography.

"Know a little about a lot, rather than a lot about little." This ideology has rewarded me with an array of creative experience in numerous industries including: Advertising, Fashion Marketing, Publishing, Promotions and Photography. My range of experience makes blurring lines between communication mediums a natural part of my process.

My understanding of true communication integration, combined with passion for the craft, give me a colossal edge that's tough to match!

I'm available for freelance Art Direction, Design, and Consulting. I focus greatly on youth consumer lifestyle, ad agency, fashion, and automotive clients.

Tom Vincent’s Specialties:

Art Direction, Design, Photography, Sales, Promotions, Journalism, Marketing Strategy, Automotive Marketing
