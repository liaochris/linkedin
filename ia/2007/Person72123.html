Randy Hockin’s Summary

Randy is President and Executive Consultant of R. Hockin & Associates Inc (RHA). Randy has 29+ years of progressive Information Technology experience including over 15 years in the telecommunications and energy industries.

For the past seven years, Randy has been providing clients with advanced Architecture and Planning services.

As an Enterprise Architect, Randy has worked with client executives to identify practical IT strategies and initiatives to address their goals.

As a Solution Architect, Randy is experienced working at all levels of the organization to identify the best solution to solve the business problem or opportunity at hand. This often requires a demanding and broad range of skills in the areas of vendor management / procurement, technology and best practices, facilitation, and practical business case development skills. From Problem Definition through Solution Architecture, Randy has facilitated the successful identification, architecture, selection and procurement of dozens of high-end application software solutions.

As a manager, Randy has, on several engagements, been asked to supplement the IT management team and establish new IT practices and departments.
