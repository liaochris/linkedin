Reto Laemmler’s Summary

Reto Laemmler is the Founder and current CEO at Xcellery.com. Xcellery is an online collaboration service for Microsoft Excel. The service has been live for a year and acquired close to 10,000 subscribers.

Prior to Xcellery Mr. Laemmler was an Engineering Manager at Varian Medical Systems in Palo Alto, CA where he led design and implementation of the entire ARIA information system.

Before moving to California Mr. Laemmler worked as a Software Engineer at Singulars, a startup located in the TV personalization space. The company was located in Lausanne, a city near Geneva.

Mr. Laemmler earned his BS in Computer Science Engineering Degree from Hoschule Rapperswil (HSR) an Engineering school in the German part of Switzerland.
