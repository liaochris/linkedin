Elaine Larkin’s Summary

I am currently looking for and am available for work that involves the written word.



I have been working as a journalist since 1998 and I am an experienced editor, features writer and news reporter. I have also done subbing, proofreading and broadcasting. I have written about business, technology, education, travel, health, sport, news, and features. I have edited a number of magazines, supplements and newspaper sections. I have experience working on my own and as part of a team. There is a slight gap in my CV between February 2002 and April 2003 when I took time out to travel through South-East Asia and Australia.

Many people may wonder why I left my job. I resigned in December 2006 because of the restrictive clause in my contract which said that the company own the copyright to everything I write. As I plan to work on a novel in 2007 I had no choice but to resign as my former employer refused to change this clause for the purposes of a work of fiction.
