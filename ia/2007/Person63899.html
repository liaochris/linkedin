Nina Nygren’s Experience

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Research industry)

    June 2004 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Preparing my Ph.D. thesis in environmental policy in the Department of Regional Studies on cooperative nature conservation, with a research grant from the City of Tampere.

    The thesis is based on the material gathered during the research and development project called "Flying Squirrel and land use planning in Tampere city region" (2004-2006), involving cooperation with diverse city region actors to develop innovative, flexible but efficient ways to organise the conservation of the strictly protected flying squirrel in the context of land use planning in a rapidly growing urban area. The project was funded by the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, municipalities of the city region and Tampere city region project.
