Tobias Misera’s Summary

Tobias Misera is Head of Practice Foviance, London. Previously, he was the Head of Information Architecture (IA) at The Usability Company.

Tobias is managing a team of consultants and contractors with expertise in User Centred Design (UCD), Information Architecture, Usability and Accessibility.

Tobias has over 10 years of hands-on experience at producing and managing high quality brand experiences that balance both user and business goals, with a high degree of usability. His work centres around the continuous refinement of UCD services and methodologies.

Tobias is German and received a first class BA(HONS) degree in Industrial Design from the University of Northumbria at Newcastle.

Past projects with Foviance include work for Camelot, RBS, AOL, Guardian Media Group, Tesco, Betfair, Victor Chandler and Nokia.

Tobias Misera’s Specialties:

Tobias skill set includes: People and client management, Investigative Research, Requirements Gathering, Ideas Generation and Concept Modelling, Information Architecture, Usability Testing, and Project Management.
