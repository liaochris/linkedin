Mark Vedel’s Summary

Mr. Vedel is a veteran of the IT and communications industry with 15 years of infrastructure and telecommunications experience.

Prior to Pepperdine University, Mr. Vedel held several positions both domestic and international, including Director of Professional Services at Praxis Computing, Chief Information Officer for ESP Networks, Vice President & Chief Technology Officer for iNetNow.com and Practice Director for Cambridge Technology Partners.

Mr. Vedel's specialties include enterprise infrastructure, network engineering, storage system architecture and customer service applications. Mr. Vedel was the architect and technical lead on projects with clients such as ADP, British Telecom, The Yellow Pages, Dutch PTT/Libertel, Saudi Aramco and M&M Mars.

Mark Vedel’s Specialties:

Mr. Vedel has a proven record of achievement in business development, technology management and client services.

He has extensive experience in: Voice & Data Infrastructure, Telecommunications, Storage Management, ITIL and Client-Server Systems development. Business Process Analysis and Change Management.
