Rhett Wilson’s Summary

Professional experience is largely in doing business/employer relations for colleges/universities. Previous employers include the nation's largest community college system, Maricopa Community Colleges; Georgetown University Law Center and W. P. Carey School of Buisness/Arizona State University.
