Clete Thompson’s Summary

With an MBA in Marketing and nearly 20 years of experience, Clete excels at empowering individual business entities—in both product and services industries—to leverage the company brand, recruit and retain customers, increase sales and grow local market share.

Recently, he served as Director of Marketing with Home123 Mortgage, a New Century Financial company, where he oversaw branding activities, lead generation, research, business development, advertising, collateral production and creative services for the 150 branch network. Prior to that, he was Director of Marketing for RBC Mortgage, the Royal Bank of Canada’s U.S. mortgage subsidiary, where he was responsible for a host of revenue-driving, business-to-business marketing programs.

Before joining the Royal Bank, he led the marketing communications function for the box division of a Fortune 500 paper packaging manufacturer. He brings a balanced portfolio of sales, marketing, and corporate communications experience from a variety of companies including Video Airchecks News Retrieval Services, Ram Golf Corporation, KMOS-TV, and Loyola University of Chicago.
