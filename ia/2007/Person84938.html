Terry Foster’s Summary

Terry Foster is a nationally recognized Executive/Consultant in Leadership Training and Development, a leader in collaborative business development efforts in service delivery environments, and the winner of numerous awards for business development and retention efforts across the boards.

Terry established a very successfully network consulting business, Winning Connections, in Seattle. As Director of Consulting/CLO, Terry developed and currently maintains connections with many entrepreneurs throughout the region. She also served on the Board of Directors for the Women’s Business Exchange in Seattle, which named her Networker of the Year. She also received the coveted Community Service Builder Award from the Poulsbo Chamber of Commerce.

Terry is a true guru in sales and marketing, leadership training development, and client relations; her performance on behalf of her “clients” proves it. When not breaking sales records, Terry enjoys golf and travel as well as taking part in life-long learning courses; she has extensive training as a personal and professional coach from the Anthony Robbins Company in San Diego.
