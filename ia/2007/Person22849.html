Denise Friant’s Summary

Curriculum Developer in construction safety industry. Managing training projects and working with subject matter experts and production staff to create training workbooks and presentation materials for courses on building codes for architects, engineers, building officials and inspectors.

Visual Communications expert with extensive background in graphic design of print material. College Instructor in Computer, Information and Office Systems Department, teaching both beginning and advanced Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign, and QuarkXPress. University Instructor teaching training techniques and message design. Instructional designer with experience in financial services and performance management.

Denise Friant’s Specialties:

Designing and producing print media, script writing for video, editing, project management, instructional design.
