Katie Barber’s Summary

Apr 2007 - present, Digital Outlook, Communications Director , Buzz
My role at Digital Outlook is to define and shape the Buzz strategy and deliver innovative and market leading word of mouth marketing campaigns for a range of entertainment and media clients, including BVI, XBox, Aardman and Chorion.

Oct 2003 - Mar 2007, AOL UK Ltd, Senior Technical Product Manager
As a Senior Product Manager with AOL UK, my role was to deliver the UK products specifically around media, social media and video.

Sep ‘01 – Jul ‘03, MP3.com Europe Limited, European Project Manager
As European Project Manager I managed the launch of 6 European music portals for MP3.com Europe. MP3.com Europe closed in August 2003 due to the parent company Vivendi Universal withdrawing from the online market.

Oct ‘00 – Aug ‘01, Voxstar / Universal Music International, Producer
UMI sought to create a music portal known as Voxstar and a series of key Universal artist websites that would link to and from the portal. The artist websites were successfully launched after which came a soft-launch of the Voxstar portal.

Feb ‘99 – Sep ‘00, Abbey Road Interactive, Producer
At the interactive design and digital video studio of Abbey Road Studios – Abbey Road Interactive – I specialised in DVD, Enhanced CD and Web for the media and entertainment industry.

Jul ‘94 – Feb ‘99, Workhouse Ltd, Producer & Project Manager
I started at Workhouse in 1994 as an Assistant Production Co-ordinator on a BBC Children’s education television programme, working my way up through the ranks of production management, I worked eventually as a Producer and Project Manager where I managed some of the company’s largest projects and budgets.
My work at Workhouse Ltd covered many aspects of media production including, CD-ROM, Video, Television, Interactive TV, Web, Intranet, Print and Branding Design. The clients I worked for spanned children’s entertainment to large banking institutions.
