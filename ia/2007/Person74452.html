Rosemary Reilman’s Summary

I received my degree in Photo Illustration from Kent State University which drove my passion for good creative and design. Through an internship with a local commercial photographer, I found the door into the world of recruitment advertising. I started there doing project management, making sure our creative deadlined on-time and got where it needed to be. There my passion for online media grew when I was transfered to the Internet Department, managing our online recruitment ads. During my time at The David Group, I learned about branding and it's importance in advertising and marketing especially in the human resources departments. Something sparked and I gained passion for online recruitment media, particularly with interest in social networking sites.

Currently, I work with The Employment Guide, part of Dominion Enterprises, in the local office that services Charlotte, Columbia and Charleston. Here I work with customers in helping them utilize their purchased online products, trouble shoot and set up accounts, and post new job descriptions on EmploymentGuide.com. I also assist in producing podcasts, videos and writing articles for each of our area blogs. In my position I also aide in local marketing efforts, suggest and implement online products for local and national recruiting efforts.

I also am actively involved with Women of Charlotte, an online social networking community for women in the Charlotte area. I volunteer on the Management Team as the Web Administrator for WOC managing our site's PHP files and SQL database for online forums, design and update our website, and collaborate and communicate with sponsors and partnership organizations.
