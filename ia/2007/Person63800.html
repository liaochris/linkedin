Nikolas Contis’s Summary

18 years experience in branding and marketing.

A senior consultant at Lexicon Branding in Sausalito, California.

Specializing in brand strategy and the development of new brands. Directing positioning and broader messaging programs. Directing creative teams. Planning, managing and developing qualitative consumer research methodologies. Involved in more than 80% of Lexicon assignments, and all major branding programs for clients like Microsoft, Intel, AOL and TBS Networks. Visit our website at lexicon-branding.com to learn more about Lexicon clients, credentials and services.

Prior to Lexicon, eight years consulting experience in visual branding, consumer products packaging and corporate identity. Served as senior account manager and planner at branding and design firms in San Francisco and Los Angeles, including a 3 year stint at Maddocks & Company. Responsibilities included creative strategy development, art direction, client budget management, qualitative consumer research, and liaising between client, creative and production teams.

Ran a broad range of packaging and branding programs for clients like Sony(PlayStation), The Venetian Hotel Las Vegas, Warner Brothers, Disney Consumer Products, Columbia TriStar Television, Playboy International Television, Procter & Gamble, Philips, Giorgio Beverly Hills, Hugo Boss, Samsung, Murad, The Clorox Company, Bell Helmets, Nestlé USA, and DHL.

Honors graduate of UCLA, with a BA in English.

Completed a variety of executive business management, marketing and creative courses at UCLA, USC, ArtCenter, and Stanford.

Represented ME Design at the Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce.

Primary Lexicon representative at GABA (the German American Business Association). Lexicon is actively pursuing further business in Germany and the EU, where we have a growing client base and work with consumers.
