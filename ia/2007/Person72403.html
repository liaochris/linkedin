Richard E. Pino’s Summary

I am the Chief Financial Officer for The Relegence Corporation which is a wholly-owned subsidiary of AOL, a TimeWarner Company.

In addition, I am a Board Member of the following exceptional organizations: The Brooklyn Philharmonic, Brooklyn, NY - Formed in 1954, Trustee; Museum of Biblical Art, New York, NY - Formed in 2004, Trustee; St. Christopher’s Inc., Dobbs Ferry, NY - Formed in 1881, Vice President and Chairman of the Audit Committee; Co-Operation Ireland, New York, NY - Formed in 1979, Treasurer and Chairman of Finance & Audit Committee. All groups are non-profit and nondenominational.

Prior to Relegence, I was the CFO for Frontline Communications International, Inc., a telecom firm that specializes in wholesale international long distance. Prior to Frontline, I was the CFO for an independent record label and VP for Finance and Administration for another telecom firm. In addition, I spent four years as Controller for an investment banking firm specializing in media and communications, where I oversaw portfolio and partnership accounting for the firm’s venture capital funds.

I hold a BA in Economics from St. Francis College and an MBA in Corporate Finance from Adelphi University.
