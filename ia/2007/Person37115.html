Samuel Barona’s Summary

The IZLY Group comprises of an audio visual solutions distribution company that has three separate business units (IZLY Business, IZLY Home Cinema & IZLY Outdoor), a film production company (Movie Factory Ltd); an AV distribution company covering the African continent (IZLY South Africa) and a CGI firm (Mak Media Ltd).

IZLY Ltd has secured exclusivity over a number of technologies, which are used by retail companies, exhibitors at trade events, outdoor media companies and the home cinema market.

IZLY's products are award winning solutions that include sound projectors for exhibitions; digital signage and outdoor media; holographic screens for digital signage; floating video screens and giant video billboards for exhibitions and outdoor media; high resolution projection screens for digital signage and POS displays; and high contrast screens for use in home cinema.
