Sean Wise’s Summary

After 5 years as director with Ernst & Young's Venture Capital Group, I think its time to stop talking about venture capital and start working at a fund.

To that end, since leaving EY I have founded small consultancey focused on Mentor Capital, or, "everything but the money". Offering the value add that VCs bring and giving me a chance to move into more hands on work.

Sean Wise’s Specialties:

According to the December issue of Canadian Business magazine, Sean Wise is one of Canada¹s leading experts on raising Venture Capital. Over the last 24 months, he has lectured at more than 50 bootcamps, financing forums and industry conferences. He has trained over 500 CEOs who collectively raised more $200M from a wide variety of investment sources.
