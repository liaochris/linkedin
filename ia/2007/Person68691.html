PJ Sands Johnson’s Summary

After years of planning celebrity weddings, concerts & special events at Atlantis Paradise Island in the Bahamas - PJ Sands Johnson launched A Design For Destiny Intl Marketing Group in 2002. Specializing in destination events in the Bahamas and a supplier of fine retail items for weddings, special occasions and corporate clients. Our mission is to provide the highest quality in personalized special occasion merchandise at the lowest prices - converging quality and selection with outstanding value and customer service with a vast array of products from world renowned retailers like Carlson Craft, Regency, Birchcraft Studios, Encore Studios, Masterpiece Studios, Beverly Clark, The Knot, Lillian Rose, Krepe Craft, Cathy's Concepts, Hortense B Hewitt, Treasure Masters, Cathy's Concepts, NuArt and others.
