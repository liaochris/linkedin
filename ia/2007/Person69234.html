Prasad Modak’s Summary

Completed BTech (Civil), MTech (Enviromental Sc and Engg) from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay and later Doctor of Engg at Asian Institute of Technology, Bangkok. Joined Centre for Environmental Science and Engg at IIT, Bombay as a faculty in 1984. Between 1985-1989, managed a research programme with University of Newcastle upon Tyne, UK and Ecole Nationale des Pontes et Chaussees (ENPC), France.Took lien to work as a Consultant to the World Bank (WB) and Asian Development Bank (ADB) in 1990 and 1994 respectively. Left IIT Bombay in 1995 to set up Environmental Management Centre.

I have been fortunate to work with almost all key UN, multi-lateral and bi-lateral developmental institutions in the world. Prominent amongst these are United Nations Environment Programme , Paris; United Nations Development Programme , New York; United Nations Industrial Development Organization , Vienna; Dept for International Development; London; Deutsche Gesellschaft für Technische Zusammenarbeit and Carl Duisberg Gesellschaft , Germany; Asian Productivity Organization , Tokyo; Swedish International Development Agency , WB and ADB.

Apart from Government of India and various State Governments I have been advising Governments of Bangladesh, Egypt, Indonesia, Mauritius, Thailand and Vietnam. Have been invited by several international agencies, public bodies, professional journals and Governments to serve as a Jury and a Reviewer. Over past 25 years, I have completed more than 100 consulting reports and trained more than 3000 professionals across the World,

Since 1996, I function as a Corporate Consultant (Environment & Infrastructure) with Infrastructure Leasing and Financial Services (see www.ilfsindia.com). In addition, I work as a Short Term Consultant to WB since 1998 and am closely associated with World Bank’s India environmental programme. Currently, I work as a Consultant to ADB on Environmental Safeguards

Prasad Modak’s Specialties:

My skill sets are broad based. For the past few years, I have been focusing on Environmental Policy, Environmental Assessment and Environmental and Social Management Frameworks for institutions.

I continue to work in areas such as Cleaner Production/Green Productivity, Environmental Management Systems.My interests in training and capacity building continue. My current focus area is in the interface of Business, Governance and Sustainability.
