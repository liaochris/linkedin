  • Website Project Manager

    Zentropa Real Aps

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Media Production industry)

    March 2000 — December 2000 (10 months)

    In 2000, I was asked to participate in an internet project that was being developed by Zentropa Real, Aps. Zentropa Real is one of several media companies operating within Danish film director Lars Von Triers business infrastructure. My assignment for Zentropa Real was to lead the website development of Monkeyclass, which launched on Tvropa.com in November 2000. The goal of the site was to create an interactive environment to present and explore social issues relevant to a youth demographic 15-30 years old. My duties included commissioning a graphic designer, and participating in all phases of the interface development.

    In 2001, Monkeyclass entered into agreement with TV2 (one of the largest Danish national television stations), to produce the concept for television. Monkeyclass was subsequently renamed ‘Helligko’, debuting on TV2 (www.tv2.dk) in September 2001.
