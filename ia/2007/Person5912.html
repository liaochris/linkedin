Andy Halliday’s Summary

Andy’s strategic roles as Entertainment Group President for Edison Brothers Stores and as Co-Founder and President of Simon Brand Ventures, a division of Simon Property Group (the nation’s largest retail mall developer), honed his passion and expertise in creating innovative and memorable consumer experiences, and prepared him for his executive roles with leading technology companies, first as VP GM of Excite’s Electronic Commerce Group, then CEO of www.contact.com, and as COO of In-Q-Tel. After co-founding Open Field Software and leading business development for Spoke Software, Andy felt that technology and timing had aligned to the point where he could finally realize his vision to create innovative and compelling social media experiences.

Tribbit.com is "group social expression", where family and friends gather together to create a multimedia celebration of someone or something they love.

OurStory users collect personal and family media in a lifelong interactive timeline. At OurStory you showcase your photos and videos in multimedia stories about your life. These are organized in a timeline of events, people, places, and memories....
