Kevin Mitchell’s Summary

Currently serve as a senior consultant for the owners of National Amusements developing new entertainment properties. National Amusements is a closely held company operating under the third generation of leadership by the Redstone family. National Amusements is the parent company of both Viacom, CBS Corporation, Midway Games and a partner in Movietickets.com.

Served as a US producer for 2way Traffic (Dutch TV company), acquired global label partnership with Warner Music for "FABS..Search for a Video Model",

Currently music supervisor on a new BET Mixed Martial Arts UFC Show called Iron Ring. The show features TI, Ludacris, Juelz Santana, Jim Jones, Nelly, Floyd Mayweather and Lil Jon. It highlights each celebrity recruiting of fighters and it leads to a final live event with the teams competing for 3 different Iron Ring belts.

My accomplishments over 5 million copies sold, 4 Grammy noms, 3 MTV VMA and 2 Soul Train Award nominations, won a Billboard Award for HipHop Single of the Year in 1999 (Lord Tariq and Peter Gunz)

Movie clients: Disney, MGM, 20th Century Fox, New Line, Warner and Lion's Gate Films, placed songs in movies like Academy Award winning movie Training Day, South Park, Daredevil, Girl Next Door, Drumline, ATL and Friday After Next, assisted Academy Award winning directors the Coen Brothers and Grammy Award winning producer T Bone Burnett on the soundtrack for a Tom Hanks film entitled "LadyKillers".

Sports clients include Shaquille O'Neal, Tony Gwynn, Earl Monroe, Trevor Pryce, Eric Williams, Woodward Skateparks, Iron Ring UFC, Extreme Martial Arts Tricking All-stars

Video game clients include EA Sports Madden, NBA Live, Sony MLB, LA Rush and Midnight Club, provided consultation to the owners of Midway Games regarding new foreign acquisitions.

Brand clients include Pepsi, Nike, Kraft, Mars, Frito Lays, Nokia, Verizon, Sprite, Levi, Budweiser, Red Bull, Google, Paramount, Jack Daniels, Heineken, US Army, Sprint, Microsoft
