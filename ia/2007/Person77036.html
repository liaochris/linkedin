Sejal Patel’s Summary

Sejal Patel is the New York Associate Director of Business Development for Navigant Consulting, Inc., a $600 million, publicly traded (NYSE: NCI), consulting firm with over 1700 professionals in more than 30 offices located throughout the United States, Canada, Europe and China. NCI is one of the nation’s leading sources of consultants and experts to the legal industry, and recently was named as one of Fortune magazine’s “Fastest Growing Companies.” Ms. Patel works extensively with the partners and management of the nation’s top law firms. Her areas of concentration include Litigation & Investigations, Discovery Services, Healthcare, Product Liability & Insurance Recovery, Financial & Insurance Services, Construction, Energy, Government Contracts, Energy and Corporate Finance. Ms. Patel has 10 years experience developing relationships within the legal marketplace.

Prior to joining Navigant Consulting, Ms. Patel’s was the Director of Business Development & Strategy for a leading legal technology company. She also has worked in the Financial Services industry for companies such as Dun & Bradstreet and Merrill Lynch. Ms. Patel received her B.S. in Economics from the University of Nebraska in 1993, and her J.D. from Syracuse University in 1996.
