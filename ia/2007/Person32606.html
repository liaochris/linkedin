Yakov (Jacob) Golder’s Summary

Accomplished technologist with more than 20 years of technical leadership experience in high-traffic web sites, packaged software, and IT. I have managed significant lines of technology for established corporations and startups in the delivery of shrink-wrapped and Internet software, including social networking with photo/video sharing, auctions, workflow management, investment management, document management/imaging, customer relationship management (CRM) , and health care systems. I have often received critical acclaim for building high-performance engineering team that delivered well-designed, scalable, strategic architecture. Extensive experience with all facets of complex technical projects, a rational balance of well-honed engineering judgment, innovation, and quality. Ability to quickly and accurately determine the essence of business, identify potential areas for improvement, synthesize architecture-driven solutions that meet business objectives while maintaining quality and meeting aggressive deadlines. Flexibility, creativity and resiliency in making informed decisions quickly and coming up with alternative solutions to eliminate roadblocks. Exceptional ability to build high-performance teams via harnessing the existing talent, recruiting strong engineers, organizing and focusing efforts, developing and maintaining morale and a sense of commitment. Demonstrated experience in managing the organizational change from traditional silos to an enterprise-oriented organization, establishing effective priorities among opposing requirements.
