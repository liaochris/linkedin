Dr. Greg Wilder’s Summary

Under my direction, Steeprock Media has successfully designed and brought to market intelligent interactive media systems that provide uniquely tailored real-time solutions.

At the start of 2007, Steeprock celebrated the launch of Orpheus, the industry's first web-based music library that allows users to customize music cues to match the specific needs of their projects. Later this fall, Steeprock will unveil a ground-breaking system that promises to significantly advance the ability of computers to understand and intelligently process music.

Since earning my doctorate (Eastman, 2002), I've worked with top industry leaders as pianist, composer, producer, and theatrical sound designer, and have held teaching positions on the faculties of Dickinson College and West Chester University.
