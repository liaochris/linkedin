Teemu Ylikoski’s Summary

Ph.D. (Econ.) Teemu Ylikoski has a broad background in research, expert positions as well as in teaching and training. He has worked in marketing planning, market research, advertising, and trusteeship as Director, Project Manager, Research Manager, and Marketing Manager. Teemu Ylikoski has earned his Doctorate in Marketing (specifically, consumer online information search, see below for publications). He has extensive experience in teaching and training bothin academia and business. Teemu is also an entrepreneur, specializing in advertising copy and PR communications. Search marketing research since 1996.
