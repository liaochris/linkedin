CV Summary:
Currently I am a Strategic and Media Planner, with a particular focus on digital/interactive media.
I also study development in DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), on-demand television and interactive advertising.
Concerning the Internet - beside the already established on-line advertising, direct e-mailing, etc. - I analyze the development of blogosphere as a field where consumers not only inform but also practice opinion leading. I think that companies must start thinking about monitoring blogs in order to know opinion from customers and in order to plan strategic activity of enterprise communication/online PR inside the blogosphere.

My background is in the marketing communication field, with a Master degree in Marketing and a BA in Enterprise Communication. I practiced since 1999 on marketing & communication strategies and media planning for medium and big sized companies active in the fashion, IT, food and beverage, events and exhibitions, publishing industries; I am able to manage the whole media planning & buying process, starting with client's objectives through target analysis, strategic planning, budgeting, implementation and monitoring/reporting of campaign performance.
I am experienced both in online and offline marcom strategies and I am confident with tools for online advertising management and measurement.
During my career I also faced market research activity in the media landscape, establishing on Nielsen Media Research database.
