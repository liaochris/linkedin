Bill Ault’s Summary

An an entrepreneur Bill has grown and developed a number of recognizable (at least in the soccer world) brands including Grassroots Coach and CanadaKicks. A variety of skills learned, picked up and experienced in a variety of ways keeps him moving on to bigger and better projects - his current one being Ault Energy a Green Energy Solutions provider serving Eastern Ontario.

On the soccer side starting with local Eastern Ontario programs in the mid-eighties Bill has served on the Provincial Staff in Ontario the District Staff in Eastern Ontario, been a head coach at the Collegiate and Senior levels.

Most recently on the staff of the Ottawa Fury where he headed up their U13 Girls program in 2006 after spending the past two seasons with the U17s. Bill also served as the Fury's Youth Development Officer in 2003. During his time with the Fury the teams under Bill's guidance have qualified for the Super Y-League's Championships and this year finished third at the US Club Soccer National Cup Finals.

Along the way Bill has served in many capacities with younger players - working in the OSA Soccer School programs, serving as a Camp Director for Post-to-Post Camps in the United States, founder of CanadaKicks and as Technical Director for Coerver Coaching in Eastern Canada.

During his time with Coerver Bill was one of the authors of the Coerver Coaching Club Curriculum in use by a number of clubs in Ontario and most recently authored Coerver Coaching Canada East's Coaching Accreditation program. Bill is also Editor and Publisher of Grassroots Coach an online magazine.

Bill has held numerous "real-life" jobs over the years including (in no particular order) :

Public Relations Manager
Fitness Centre Building Manager
Web Page Developer
Turf Maintenance Technician
Project Manger
A/V Technician
Librarian
