Ruiying WANG’s Summary

I've been working on visual simulation ever since 1994, in the early years my focus was military simulation. At MiraLab, I did some work on cloth simulation. Then I came back Beijing, with a friend, started our own company. We mainly develop Virtual Reality sports game, like hunting simulation - you can hunt animal you are not allowed to hunt in the reality. Then when you saw then dead, you may feel guilty, so in the true reality, you will choose never shot any...sorry, off topic..
Currently, I run a large scale immersive VR lab on my own, that mean I take care of hardware as well as software. We have wide variety of interactive facilities, including Ascension's motion tracking system, Immersion's CyberGlove and CyberGrasp, SensAble's PHANTOM haptic stylus.
Good at programming hardware interface, configuring graphic cluster, building immersive environment with PC cluster (could be tile-display-wall or CAVE). By immersive, I mean with wide range interactive equipments, stereoscopic, wide FOV etc..
