Oscar Trelles’s Summary

Interaction Architect, Multimedia and Web Developer, with over eight years of experience working hands-on, leading multidisciplinary teams and consulting in a variety of business and user-centered multimedia applications, mostly for delivery via the world wide web and CD-ROMs. Particularly interested in designing and developing applications for the Flash Platform and other Adobe (formerly Macromedia) technologies, with focus on Usability and Information Architecture. Interested in writing and collaborating with industry publications and web portals.
