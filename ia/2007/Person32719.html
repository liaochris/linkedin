Gordon Roberts’s Summary

Edmund Burke said, 'There is a boundary to men's passions when they act from feelings; but NONE when they are under the influence of imagination.'

Imagination, creativity and a passionate desire to make a difference with unique, strategic, on brand, on message connecting experiences; whether they be product launches, face to face marketing events, meetings or press events are the hallmarks of my work.

Over twenty years proven success of working with iconic Fortune 100 and automotive brands in Europe, South Africa, South America and USA.

BIG IDEA person. Believe that 'Creativity requires the courage to let go of certainties.'

Ability to work closely and collaborate with Senior Corporate Executives from Chairman/CEO to Vice President to Heads of Departments

Fast, lively and flexible,strategic creative thinker; able to assimilate, understand and quickly define the root of client needs, message, challenges and issues:

Sense of Humor.

Ability to design, and supervise the execution of, strategic multi-audience communication plans and in building and leading creative teams. Believe it's better to have 30 imaginations working on a project, rather than one imagination telling the other 29 what to do.
