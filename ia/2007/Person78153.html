Steve Enders’s Summary

Web product and editorial professional with over 10 years experience in media including online content development and production, newspaper and magazine editorial and production, and television production. Well-rounded background with demonstrated ability in strategic planning, and implementing focused goals that balance editorial needs with business growth. Skills include developing high-profile online media products, project management, writing and editing news and features, as well as managing budgets, staff, and freelancers. Excellent eye for detail, design, and accuracy. Strong understanding of emerging technologies and web trends, and excellent ability to manage multiple projects within deadlines.

Steve Enders’s Specialties:

Web production, product development, project management, writing, editing, magazine and newspaper production, broadcast television writing and production.
