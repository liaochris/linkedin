Philippe Corrot’s Experience

  • Co-founder & CEO

    SplitGames

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Games industry)

    January 2006 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    SplitGames is an online video games community which enables users to trade pre-owned video-games using a point-based pricing system. Point pricing depends on the age of the game, supply and demand and general popularity. A subscription is required for users to take part in games trading. SplitGames also includes game reviews, tips and blogs, all of which contributes to the establishment of a community of users. The Paris-based company, launched the website in March 2006 and aims to become the European leader in its sector. In September 2006, SplitGames closed a series "A" round of private equity financing with Crédit Agricole.

  • Co-founder & CEO

    Maÿrev

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Retail industry)

    November 2001 — November 2005 (4 years 1 month)

    Maÿrev is a fashion accessories brand. Maÿrev opened within three years seven shops in the best locations in Paris (rue de Rivoli, rue du Four, rue de Passy, etc…), two shop-in-the shop in department stores such as Galeries Lafayette and we started to develop the brand in Japan, Italy and Egypt. Maÿrev had very strong brand recognition and we were even selected to sponsor the French “Star Academy Show”. In april 2005, three years after starting the business we were approached by Rand Group, a major European fashion jewellery distributor which wanted to buy Maÿrev and we sold it.

  • Co-founder & CEO

    Keyrus

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; 7607; Internet industry)

    September 1997 — June 2001 (3 years 10 months)

    Keyrus, one of the first web agency in France : Within three years the company grew from 2 to 40 people and landed some major clients such as Vivendi, France Telecom, Kookai, Van Cleef & Arpels, Chanel, Natalys,…
    Sales grew rapidly and the company was always profitable. In may 2000, Keyrus merged with Progiware Group. The Keyrus Group is listed on the Paris stock exchange since July 2000.
