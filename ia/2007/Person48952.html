A LITTLE ABOUT ME!

I'm an international author, broadcaster, speaker and business development mentor, considered by the media to be one of Europe’s leading Personal Branding and Marketing Strategists.

I guide executives of professional services companies in business development strategies to build distinctive corporate, employer and personal brands so that they realise more profit and greater success with less stress.

I am the Managing Director of Oneocean Ltd (www.oneoceangroup.com), a leading business development consultancy.

Over the last 20 years, I have lived and worked in Asia, Europe and North America (across some 30 countries)and have a breadth of commercial expertise in business strategy, transformation, brand building strategies, organisational development and employee engagement.
