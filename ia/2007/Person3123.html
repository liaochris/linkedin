Alexandre AVON’s Summary

I am a chemical engineer of the ESCOM School near from Paris. I have specialised for more than ten years in translation between French, English and German of scientific documents.
I have translated into French a few articles from different american scientific publications for the french scientific publication La Recherche.
I have also translated into French several scientific books that have been published, including "Keramische Glasuren" by Wolf Matthes (500 pages in 2 months), a german handbook particularly famous about ceramics. More recently, I have translated from french into english a technical publication from the professional syndicate of ceramics (60 pages in 3 weeks).
