Scott Gardner’s Summary

I am currently an Entreprenuer, founder and President of www.FairMarketValuations.com. We offer business valuation and appraisal services to business owners throughout the US. Our unique offering enables business owners to meeting with a professional consultant, in-person, in the convenience of their office or an off-site location. With nearly 500 trained professionals serving all major US markets, we bring business valuations to your doorstep.

Previously, I was VP of Sales & Marketing for Fortune Interactive, The Future of Search Marketing. Our proprietary, latent semantic-based technology (SEMLogic) effectively analyzed a client's website and its competitors to fully understand the 'How?' and 'Why?' to natural search engine rankings. Leveraging this cutting-edge AI technology with our staff of seasoned, professional search marketers provided the ultimate combination in a successful search marketing campaign.

Previously, I served as Director of Sales for WebSourced, Inc (now MarketSmart Interactive). I was there for 5 years and helped grow the company from 10 employees to 180+ and from $1MM to $30MM+ annual revenues during that tenure.

Prior to my entry into the Internet Marketing sector, I was involved in branch management for Central Carolina Bank (now SunTrust).
