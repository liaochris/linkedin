Christopher Williamson’s Summary

Head of Games Studio with substantial game design and cross-platform porting experience. Founded DreamQuest Software, a game development company specializing in casual card and board games for both Windows and a variety of mobile platforms. Grew revenue through the successful design and distribution of over a dozen game titles.

Most recently founded Bonzoid Studios, designing casual games for the whole family inspired by videogame classics for Xbox Live Arcade, Mac, and Windows platforms. Interested in both fellow developers and publishers interested in XBLA.
