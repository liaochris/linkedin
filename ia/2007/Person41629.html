Jacob Correia’s Summary

I have been working as a professional web media for the past 8+ years and have focused on Flash media and game development for nearly all of that time. While I have done work for companies on the side over the years, the majority of my work has been done as the lead Flash Developer for Say Design (www.saydesign.com). These projects have come from a large range of small to large companies such as Disney, MTV, iWin, Yahooligans, etc (more comprehensive list found at saydesign.com).

I started out in web design & development & even some 3D, but quickly found that my skills are strongest as a coder/developer in Flash. I still regularly use Photoshop, Dreamweaver, MS Office, & a variety of other programs to get my job done, as well as have worked with most professional media programs over the years. I seem to flourish in Flash because it happens to be my favorite.

I am accustomed to pushing the envelope surrounding online gaming, overcoming obstacles and I not only have much experience with OOP and dynamic coding, but I thoroughly enjoy it. I also heavily enjoy learning new technologies and am driven by the sheer desire to expand my skills and become better and more efficient at what I do. I have irreplaceable real-world experience am currently beta testing AS 3.0 for Adobe in order to keep my skills sharp and up to date.

I have spent a bit of time over the past 6 years networking at conferences and developer parties (IGDA, Minna Mingle, GDC, Flash Forward, etc) with a variety of industry professionals, some of whom are listed under my connections here on my profile.

I also have experience in teaching and have been invited on several occasions to speak at Flash festivals and universities. I've been invited to publish Flash books but have yet to find the time to do so with my busy schedule and hope to accomplish that if I ever get the time. I also hope to continue to expand my coding skills to other areas as we branch of into DS, Wii & Xbox 360 development.
