Ranjit Padmanabhan’s Experience

  • CEO & Founder

    DashNote

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    August 2006 — Present (9 months)

    DashNote is building a platform for "Context-Aware Resource Invocation" that will send highly qualified traffic to web businesses, while providing relevant and immediate utility to consumers.

    Combining a rich and responsive UI (AJAX, Web 2.0) with powerful server-side technology (Concept classification, Entity extraction), our platform enables unprecedented immediacy and precision in matching consumers and businesses. We enable consumers to interact directly with web pages and invoke highly relevant services and resources, ranging from utilitarian (e.g. Person Lookup, Driving Directions) to commercial (Price Comparison, Financial Services). Based on the Context of the web page, we can derive Consumer Intent, enabling us to send motivated traffic to advertisers, who in turn have the ability to finely customize and specify the nature of traffic that they wish to acquire.

    DashNote will launch in the 2nd half of 2007. An Exec Summary is available to investors.
