Tim Bass’s Summary

Tim Bass, CISSP, is a Principal Global Architect, Director, for TIBCO Software. He is currently focused on PredictiveBusiness and emerging commercial applications of event processing / complex event processing (CEP) for TIBCO; co-chairing the Event Processing Reference Architecture Working Group (EPRAWG). He also represents TIBCO as a Steering Committee member for the Event Processing Technical Society, Program Committee member for the Inaugural International Conference on Distributed Event-Based Systems (DEBS 2007), and Bellua Cyber Security Asia 2007.

Prior to joining TIBCO he was CISO/CTO of SilkRoad, Inc., a consulting practice specializing in risk management, IT security, operational concepts, network systems engineering, architecture and distributed network technologies. He has providing independent Subject Matter Expertise to both government and industry for 15 years with years of responsible positions advising on enterprise information security and architecture.. He has developed information security policies, standards and procedures and works to insures that all information systems are both functional and secure.

He is very familiar with a very wide variety of EAI and IT security concepts, practices, and procedures and he relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish organizational goals, performing a variety of complex management, budget and technical tasks depending on the requirement and situation. His work on Internet security and countermeasures for the DoD has been featured in Popular Science Magazine and Federal Computer Week. He is internationally recognized as a thought leader in numerous Internet domains and a list of his publications, detailed biography and company information is available at the web site (http://www.timbass.info)
