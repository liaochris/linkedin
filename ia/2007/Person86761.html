Tom Harrington’s Summary

I came to Mac OS X from a background in Unix, Linux, and embedded software. As a result while I like to focus on cool high-level stuff like Cocoa and WebKit, I don't hesitate to get down into the guts of the system when necessary (or just interesting). I've come to enjoy the life of an independent developer, and I'm always open to interesting contract jobs. I wouldn't rule out going back to full-time work for someone other than than myself, but it'd have to be a really cool environment, on a really interesting project.

Tom Harrington’s Specialties:

Software Development and Engineering, currently focused on Cocoa and BSD Unix
