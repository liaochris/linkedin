T. T. Mitchell’s Summary

T. T. Mitchell Consulting, Inc, is a corporation that has many divisions. The main division is in healthcare finance, revenue and receivables. The second division is in the area of manage/leadership and diversity training and seminars. The third division is in website optimization and design, under the company name "SEOXcellence". The fourth division is a web portal/directory site called Services and Stuff, different than other models because we help legitimate and researched businesses advertise their services and products.

In each of these industries, we offer quality services geared towards getting the best out of people and processes, concentrating first on giving people what they need, more than what they want. If someone wants to treat the causes, they can't do so without treating the symptoms.

We work with organizations and individuals to help each be better in some fashion. Changing Attitudes and Perceptions for Unlimited Growth; that's what we do.
