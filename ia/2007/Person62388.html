Naomi Simson’s Summary

Naomi has a 20 year background in corporate marketing with companies such as IBM, Ansett and Apple Computer, but she started her own show more than 10 year ago – Naomi is particularly passionate about pleasure. Given that the average life has only 27,610 days – she wonders how many are truly amazing. Naomi was announced Entrepreneur of the Year 2005 at the Champion of Champions Awards.

The RedBalloon Days for amazing activities was created as a hobby five years ago, yet has been recognised in both 2005 and 2006 in BRW fastest growing start-ups list and was a finalist in the Telstra Small Business Awards in 2005 & 2006. RedBalloon Days now lists more than 1,500 amazing activities across Australia and New Zealand – ranging from learning how to drive a tank to feng shui consultations.

Naomi Simson’s Specialties:

Naomi is a marketer by training, and with a strong corporate back ground. She has established a fabluous team of professionals committed to making a difference.

She is a regular speaker on Happy People, means Happy Customers means a Happy bottom line. Naomi calls on the resources of the Pleasure Survey as well as other data to demonstrate how getting back to basics is can give the greatest returns.
