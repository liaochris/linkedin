David Sheldon’s Summary

Business-driven, consultative sales manager with a "can do" attitude who leads from the front. He has both direct and indirect software and services sales experience selling into the Times Top 500 accounts and has an entrepreneurial approach to developing and winning new business. David has excellent interpersonal and communication skills and a passion for his client’s products and services. He has the gravitas to work at “C” level and works effectively within a virtual team environment adding incremental value.

Often described as a “retail guru” or “Mr. Retail”, David is steeped in the retail industry both from an operational as well as an I.T. sales and consulting perspective. His experience spans the total Retail Lifecycle from Supply Chain to BI & CRM which positions him well as an industry expert.
