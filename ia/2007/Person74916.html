Ruby Sinreich’s Summary

...progressive activist... local politico... professional organizer... compulsive blogger...

Ruby has combined her background in grassroots organizing and her interest in communication technology to become a passionate advocate of network-centric advocacy - a both new and old way of organizing for social change - which takes advantage of the decentralized nature of the Internet to empower individuals. By allowing people to form their own communities (or networks), they can build effective movements from the ground up.

Ruby is the founder and editor of OrangePolitics, a progessive multi-author weblog with an active community of users discussing local issues around her hometown of Chapel Hill, NC.
