Nicolette Beard’s Summary

Nicolette Beard, Owner and President, is a former editor, publisher and advertising executive. Currently, she applies her skills toward search engine marketing and website optimization for small and midsize companies. She believes that effective Internet Marketing, search engine optimization, and website promotion place business owners on equal footing with Fortune 1000 companies, even though they may have bigger budgets.

Her goal is to provide business owners with qualified, targeted traffic through solid competitive analysis, honest appraisal of their site's strengths and weaknesses and a tactical plan of action from which they will grow and prosper.

Her approach to search marketing is an holistic one in that she believes the sum of the parts is greater than the whole. There are many paths that lead to high rankings with the search engines. Depending on the goals of your website, you will discover the techniques necessary to succeed in the online world through her detailed consultation.
