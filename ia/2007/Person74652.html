John Rivoir’s Summary

RP Capital invests in distressed properties, foreclosures, rehabs, turnarounds, development deals, mortgage notes and other types of undervalued real estate assets. We currently manage $10 million in investor funds. We turn that figure over about 3 times per year, and are now raising an additional $10-to-$20 million through our network of investors. Annualized returns to investors have been in the high teens to low twenties.

We also arrange institutional and private financing for a broad range of real estate transactions, and maintain a strong network of contacts among the private equity, venture capital and angel investment communities.

RP Capital and its principals are also shareholders and hold operating positions in Panasteel Building Solutions Inc. (dba Panasteel LLC), a manufacturer of cold formed steel framing for residential and commercial construction. Products are sold into the U.S., Latin American and Caribbean markets.

In addition, RP Capital and its principals are major shareholders in OFF Productions, a company dedicated to creating and seeking to develop projects for the stage, as well as for small and large screen presentation. We co-produced an off-Broadway play in 2006, currently have a TV series under consideration by one of the major networks, and are working on a new Broadway musical.

In my prior professional life, I was a retained executive search consultant. I recruited CEO and officer level candidates for technology companies on a national basis while based in both Silicon Valley and New York, and the network established through that continues to serve me well.

I have lived in Asia, Latin America, and Europe, and am conversational in Japanese and Spanish.

Feel free to contact me regarding real estate deals, construction and development, commercial financing needs, film, theater and TV projects, or becoming an investor in any of the above. My email address is john@RPCapital.com and my phone is (212) 655-5781
