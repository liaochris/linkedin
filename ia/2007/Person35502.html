David Black’s Summary

With 14 years of focused labor, employment and business law experience Mr. Black provides small & medium sized businesses, executives, professionals, and other individuals high caliber labor, employment and business law advice and representation.

Three goals:

(1) help organizations to adopt a PREVENTIVE approach to human resource managment through best PRACTICES and POLICIES,

(2) serve as your business General Counsel to proactively manage and reduce business risk through strategic business organization, contracting, and state-of-the-art litigation risk management techniques,

(3) help entrepreneurs to collaborate with others to maximize synergistic business opportunities.

Mr. Black also speaks and writes frequently on labor and employment law issues.

David Black’s Specialties:

* Helping small businesses survive by fixing legal land mines.
* Providing small business owners with the tools and legal protection they need to succeed.
* Utilizing legal & business practice preventive maintenance
* Helping clients to network and grow their business
* Challenging entrepreneurs to act now.
