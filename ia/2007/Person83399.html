Susan Black’s Summary

Susan Black heads up the 6-year old strategy firm, Susan Black Associates, Inc. (The Black & Wright Group). Clients include leading online travel brands, traditional travel distributors and suppliers, and travel industry start-ups. She serves on several corporate advisory boards, and was elected to ATME's Executive Committee (Association of Travel Marketing Executives) and TIA's eCommerce Committee (Travel Industry Association of America). She recently served as CMO of CheapCaribbean.com, a top ranked website dedicated to Caribbean and Mexico vacations. Her previous posts include Executive Vice President, Marketing for $2B Liberty Travel/GOGO Worldwide Vacations; Founder, TravelCom Conference & Expo; Chairman of eTravelWorld; GM and Publisher of Thomas Publishing's Travel Group; and Assoc. Publisher of Corporate Travel Magazine.

As a consultant who now leads strategy for a well-funded healthcare organization, Susan also maintains her travel consulting practice, and accepts assignments with travel industry clients and investors.
