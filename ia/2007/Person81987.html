Stephen Collins’s Summary

My passion is for creating great web apps, teaching people about awesome technology and making a difference to the way people can use Internet-connectedness.

If you read through some of the posts on my blog (http://blog.acidlabs.org), you'll see my passions reflected there - building great web apps, innovative software development, user-centric information architecture, development using best practices including standards adherence, accessibility and usability. I'm particularly excited about the notion of thought leadership in the web apps space - taking clients and my own work to places where they are given the opportunity to be the best they can be.

I'm excited by the work companies like 37signals, Avenue A|Razorfish, Tangler and Atlassian ;) are doing - they are truly doing what they can to make their own business and the work they do for their clients something remarkable. It's Seth Godin's notion of the Purple Cow brought to reality.

I am also the author of The ACME Guide (http://www.acidlabs.org/acme).

I'm fairly involved (although less than I'd like at the moment) in the international ColdFusion development community. I presented in 2006 at Australia's largest web apps development conference, webDU.
