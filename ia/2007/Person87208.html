Tor Olav Haugen’s Summary

“In 2000 Tor Olav Haugen were ranked by Finansavisen (leading financial newspaper in Norway) to be one of the best leader profiles below the age of 30 in Norway. “

Mr. Haugen has been leading several companies operating within the media business. He is currently CEO in RightOnMedia AS/Out of Home Digital Media (- 2006) , previous; CEO in Cart AS/In-Store Media Retail (2006 – 2000), Founder and CEO of Maretto AS/Consultancy (2002 – 2000), Director Instore Media in Green Media Group ASA/In-Store Media/Malls/Airport/Retail (2000 – 1998), CEO in Hermes Media AS and Hermes Media Aps/In-Store Media Retail/Outdoor (1998 – 1996), finally Senior Key Account Manager i Fram Reklamebyrå AS/Outdoor Media (now ClearChannel Norway AS). Haugen is Qualified Media Advisor by Media Advice Institute Association in Norway.

Tor Olav Haugen has a wide record of start-ups, most current is RightOnMedia in partnership with Reitangruppen. Previous he has a start-up with Cart AS, when leaving the company in 2006 having a $US 35 mill turnover with a cashflow $US 7 mill over 5 years, an unofficial cap company value of $US 25 mill (2006), a company free of depth and strong liquidity. Start-up of Hermes Media AS (Norway), closely followed by Hermes Media Aps (Denmark) bringing the company to an M&A with Green Media Group ASA the Hermes shareholders having a $US 5,5 mill in cash profit. Founder and a start-up of BestePraksis™, a Joint Venture between Cart AS and TNS Gallup AS. President/Board Member Outdoor Association in Norway. Co-founder starting up of a charity foundation www.GiDetVidere.no (PayItforward) and are currently Vice Chairman of the Board.
