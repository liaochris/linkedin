Jennifer Uhll’s Summary

Juhll, Inc. offers concept, design & production of top-notch, online, broadcast & print advertising. Juhll specializes in rich media flash content, including banner advertisements, websites, email, kiosks, CD-ROM presentations, screen savers, product demos and games. Over the past 14 years, "Juhll Banners" have generated click-through rates up to and over 20% and lead conversion revenue averaged at $4 for every $1 spent on media.

Juhll’s founder, Jennifer Uhll was former Creative Director for LowerMybills.com, where she conceptualized LowerMyBills original innovative dancing silhouette ads and many more successful ads. (LowerMyBills owns all rights to it's ads.)

Juhll's competitive edge over all other agencies is simple:

1. Highest ROI - "Juhll Creatives" are proven to generate on average 3x's higher ROI than other agency creatives.

2. Lowest Production Cost - Juhll clients save 2/3 of the cost over other agency clients. With Juhll's extensive resource library, clients receive unique, customized creative without the cost & time of reinventing the wheel.

3. Least Production Time - Juhll's creative team delivers top quality creative in 1/3 the production time over other agencies. Most agencies take 1 month for 1 new banner. Juhll fully executes 1 new creative concept in 1 day. Juhll has delivered up to 20 new creatives/month for 1 client.

4. Best Creative Talent - Juhll has extremely unique imagination, along with proven ability to develop & choose winning creative ideas.

Juhll produces creative for top corporations in a variety of industries, including entertainment, financial, education, lead-generation and more. Honorable mention: Walt Disney Corporation, Starz Media - Anchor Bay Entertainment, Warner Bros, Honda, VantageMedia.com, Brandinginc.com, Route66la.com and many more undisclosed current clients!
