Joseph Morin’s Summary

Joseph has been the creative and driving force behind the launch of Storybids. A seasoned Internet advertising executive, Joseph has been heavily involved in the online video market and now through Storybids, brings a product placement marketplace to user generated content within online video.

Joseph is also a Partner with Boost Search Marketing, a Southern California based international marketing consulting firm,specializing in enterprise level search optimization for large scale and global web properties, Joseph has worked with many companies on their search marketing campaigns including Austin Ventures, Autobytel, Inc., Azure Capital, Business.com, Clearstone Venture Capital, Dentist.net, Education.com, Enterprise Rent-A-Car, MGM Mirage, UsedCars.com and World Publications.

Joseph served as Vice President of Strategic Marketing for UsedCars.com where as a principle, was instrumental in the sale of the company to Cobalt Automotive Group in a multi-million dollar transaction. He also worked as the in-house Strategic Marketing Director for Autobytel, Inc., the nation's largest online new car buying service for a two year period and pioneered their search marketing program.

He is a featured speaker at Internet Marketing conferences around the world having spoken at conferences in New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, MIami, Seattle, Las Vegas, Toronto, Stockholm, London and Tokyo. He is also a Moderator of the forums ‘In-House and Corporate SEM’ and 'SEM Related Organizations & Events' at Search Engine Watch Forums as well as the Conference Marketing Director for the PubCon series of Internet Marketing Conferences by WebmasterWorld.

Joseph Morin’s Specialties:

Video monetization, product placement, alternative monetization strategies to implement multi-tier revenue streams such as contextual, ad supported networks and lead generation.

Expert witness in search engine marketing methodology having testified in a Federal Grand Jury trial and deposed on several lawsuits on cases involving search engines.

Due dilligence expert working with several top tier venture capital firms focusing on investment strategies regarding the search marketplace.
