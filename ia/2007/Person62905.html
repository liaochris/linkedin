Neil Walker’s Summary

An accomplished, results-driven programme manager and project manager with over 20 years experience in driving change and delivering business benefit within high pressure, commercial environments.

An outstanding relationship builder. Able to operate credibly at executive level and effectively manage diverse stakeholder groups and third parties. With an inherent ability to manage and lead successful, cohesive teams to drive forward business transformation and deliver value.
