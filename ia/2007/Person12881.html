Brian Cavoli’s Summary

I am the Director of Marketing for TNS Media Intelligence/Cymfony. We provide analysis of social media to help companies measure the effectiveness of their PR/corporate communications and to gain greater insights into the emerging trends and influencers impacting their business.

My role is all things marketing. This includes evolving the brand messages, planning/optimizing media, generating quality sales leads, and creating educational materials, events and the tools to help prospects through the sales process.

What makes me tick: I love the exploration of what’s next. To determine how interactivity, social media and emerging technologies can improve the marketing experience and results efficiency.

I’ve been involved in:

- Marketing for one of the earliest web brands
- Planned a campaign featuring one of the first rich media banner ads in the US (thanks to Mediapost for that reference)
- Created the "Innovation Engine" at an global agency to explore marketing opportunities with emerging technologies
- Boston Interactive Media Association board member for over 5 years
- Launch team for the interactive group for a global media agency (which got us on the cover of Adweek)
- Created the online advertising categories for the annual MITX Awards
- Started a search engine marketing group for a global ad agency
- Winner of the 2004 MITX “Most Innovative Campaign”
- Winner of the 2005 MediaWeek “Media Plan of the Year – Best Use of Internet”
- Rebranded a company around the principles of social media
- Developed one of the first social media press releases (thanks to BusinessWeek for that reference)
