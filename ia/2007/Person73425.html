Mary Roberts’s Summary

My goal is to help the top executive of an operating company be more successful as a leader and with their organization. I consult with the key executives of a variety of organizations in Oregon through Roberts Collaboration Inc.
I have 30 years of executive experience in leading and cultivating profitable and nationally known organizations – highlighted by a decade-long stint as President/CEO of Rejuvenation, Inc., the Portland, Oregon manufacturer and direct marketer of authentic reproduction lighting. Under my direction, Rejuvenation grew from $6 million in sales to multiple locations, $27 million in sales, and 200 employees.
Prior to Rejuvenation, I served as COO/President of Hanna Andersson Corp., the national direct-marketer of children’s clothing. Throughout my 7 years in a variety of high-impact leadership positions, Hanna Andersson’s annual sales increased from $13 million to $45 million, multiple locations, and 350 employees.
I also volunteer on the not-for-profit boards of Artist’s Repertory Theatre and The Bicycle Transportation Alliance, as a Co-Chair of the CEO Roundtable at OEN, and as a mentor and speaker in Portland State University’s MBA program. I hold a B.S. in Business Administration, specializing in management, from PSU.

Mary Roberts’s Specialties:

Executive coaching, facilitating strategic plans and partner agreements, facilitating high stakes conversations with executives, boards, and senior staff.
