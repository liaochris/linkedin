Jason Rogers’s Summary

As a marketing manager for an Interactive Agency, my job is to drive traffic to client websites, analyze the results of those efforts, and optimize those activities to continually improve performance. I utilize a variety of strategies and tactics including online advertising, paid search, email marketing, and organic optimization.
