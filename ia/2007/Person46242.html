Karl De Abrew’s Experience

  • Founder, Director, CEO

    Debenu

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 2007 — Present (7 months)

    Karl is currently the Chief Executive Officer of Debenu Pty. Ltd. (www.debenu.com), a company he founded with Rowan Hanna in July 2007 -- responsible for Benubird, an easy-to-use personal document management system that allows you to collect, organize and archive documents.     You can find more about Rowan at http://www.linkedin.com/in/rowanhanna

  • Founder, Director, CEO

    Nitro PDF Software (formerly BinaryThing, and previously 'A Round Table Solution')

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 1997 — July 2007 (10 years 1 month)

    Karl founded BinaryThing (now 'Nitro PDF Software') with a goal of producing high quality software that achieved a more efficient workplace focused around the use of electronic paper such as Adobe's Portable Document Format (PDF). During his tenure, Karl achieved the following milestones:

    • Launch of Nitro PDF Professional (www.nitropdf.com), the first true alternative to Adobe® Acrobat®

    • ARTS PDF (www.artspdf.com) -- one of the world's leading PDF software developers for the last 8 years. Developer of award-winning and industry standard plug-ins that extend Adobe® Acrobat®.

    • The most popular website for the PDF community, Planet PDF (www.planetpdf.com) and its sister site PDF Store (www.pdfstore.com).

    Karl is a seasoned speaker, having been an expert panelist, moderator and track chair at conferences and seminars across the planet; as well as being an industry commentator and columnist writing for a variety of publications including Adobe’s website and Planet PDF.

  • Editor

    Planet PDF division of Nitro PDF Software (formerly BinaryThing)

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 1997 — July 2007 (10 years 1 month)

    Karl De Abrew's experience driving Planet PDF extends back to the very beginning of PDF. He watched the development of PDF as a standard document exchange format, and gained experience and expanded his knowledge through the day-to-day, hands on experience using the technology.

    With his vision, Planet PDF now caters to anyone and everyone with an interest in PDF - from professional Web or print publishers seeking appropriate tools, to rank beginners wondering exactly what "PDF" stands for.

  • Speaker/Moderator/Host

    Seybold Seminars

    (Computer Software industry)

    1999 — 2003 (4 years)

    Karl De Abrew acted as a PDF program track coordinator, host, moderator, and speaker for events including Seybold San Francisco, Seybold New York, Seybold Amsterdam, PDF Conference, and Adobe® Acrobat® & PDF Conference.

  • Systems Engineer

    ANZ Securities

    (Computer Software industry)

    1993 — 2000 (7 years)

    During his work with the ANZ Investment Bank as a Systems Engineer, Karl developed a PDF-based electronic workflow which replaced a system for the production of paper-based reports. Each night, daily reports on listed companies were produced for analysis by institutional and private client advisors. This was a heavily paper-oriented process which had been conducted for years and Karl had been involved in the late-night duplication and distribution of these documents for years.

    The new workflow involved professionally published documents being converted to PDF, indexed, uploaded, and stored within a database system for distribution on the Web. Whilst this may be passé now, at the time it was quite a slick system and provided much of the impetus for basing Nitro PDF Software (formerly BinaryThing) around PDF.
