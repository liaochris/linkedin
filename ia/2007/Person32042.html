Gilles M. Grosjean’s Experience

  • Corporate Finance - Mergers & Acquisitions

    CBC Banque SA

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Investment Banking industry)

    October 2006 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Within the Corporate Finance Department, I work closely with clients on corporate finance projects, such as mergers, acquisitions, IPOs (in collaboration with KBC Securities), private placement, LBOs and other corporate finance services such as investment appraisals, business plans and valuations. My particular focus is on small and mid-sized business.

    I manage the entire dossier, from the initial feasibility study to the conclusion. This includes carrying out valuations, producing an information memorandum, identifying potential interested parties and managing negotiations. I also deal directly with other key parties, both internal, including KBC Private Equity and KBC Securities, and external, including private investors, private equity funds and other corporate finance advisors.

    CBC Banque SA

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Banking industry)

    November 2005 — October 2006 (1 year)

    Within the Corporate Banking Division, I was responsible for managing and developing a small portfolio of corporate clients. I worked closely with the product specialists to ensure that clients would receive the most innovative and suitable banking solutions in corporate finance, employee benefits, cash management, trade finance, risk management and asset management.
