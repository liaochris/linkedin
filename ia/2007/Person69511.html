Ms. Treyce d'Gabriel’s Summary

I have over 20 years of experience in both Handwriting Analysis and Handwriting Formation Therapy. I have been featured on radio & television talk shows and in written media. I am an internationally recognized trainer for agencies such as the Intl Assn Law Enfmt Intel Analysts, many Correctional agencies, Great Lakes Gang Investigators Assn and more. I also offer online classes, books and training dvd's.

I am a published author & am the author of "Teenage Terror - the Personality Behind the Pen" and "The _____ Analyst" series. My background experience: work with law enforcement, correctional offcr, academy instructor, FTO, P.I., Agent with Wolverine Recovery & field supervisor for a national investigative agency. I am the handwriting expert for Streetcats Int'l, 4theKids, Lost Child Netwk, Missing/Exploited Children & Missing Kin.

I have worked on employee screening, school safety, violence potential, psychological autopsies, progression of suicide, profiles of Scott Peterson, Michael Jackson, etc. I have solved forgeries, identified authors of anonymous notes, uncovered lies, solved employee theft, and worked international South African murder and Dutch kidnapping cases. Other cases include: when I told a woman her husband could brutally murder her & a year later - he did. A case when I stated that a sheriff deputy was dangerous, very aggressive and not trustworthy - 3 years later he killed his girlfriend. Another case when I stated a Homicide Investigator was dangerous - a year later he was arrested for 2 counts of Child Battery.
