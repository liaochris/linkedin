Wendy Blythe Warner’s Summary

Currently I am with NetWinner.com as Marketing & Promotions Director. NetWinner provides a unique ad vehicle based on a new, companion ad model where video ads are a natural companion to the game experience, not a potential disruption. The companion ad model is coupled with the frequency of active roll, a marked improvement to the reach of traditional pre-roll video models.

We provide custom games with companion advertising for other publisher's sites resulting in increased time spent with your brand, increased traffic to your site and a revenue share at no risk to you. There are many options with guaranteed results.

Custom Marketing Solutions, my strategic marketing firm, is still in force.

In1996, when CMS was founded, I worked with Time Life to develop strategic promotions that entailed custom publishing for clients to help them achieve their marketing goals. Clients included: AMEX, Discover Card, Land O’Lakes, Pampered Chef, Quaker Oats, USPS & more. CMS achieved over $10 mm in sales with the most active accounts for Time Life Custom Publishing.

In 2000, CMS began consulting work for Hearst Corp in the Custom Publishing division. We published custom magazines for corporate clients. Clients included: Georgia Pacific, Lexington Home Brands, May Co. & Thomasville Furniture. With Hearst, CMS worked with these clients to develop a promotional plan inclusive of a custom mag that enabled them to reach their target demo and achieve their marketing goals. This included database marketing, web site tie-ins, in-store customer events and more. CMS achieved over $7mm in sales &, as an independent rep, brought in the highest number of clients for Hearst Custom Publishing.

CMS has continued to work with individual clients like Staples, Liz Claiborne, Philip Morris, Progress Lighting & Household bank as well as authors and publishing companies. CMS specializes in promotional and strategic consulting, new business development and promotional sales.
