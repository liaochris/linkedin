Jean-Claude MORAND’s Summary

Jean-Claude started his career as Finance Executive Director before to follow a marketing path at DIGITAL Equipment Europe as International Marketing Manager. As CEO of a 400 employees educational institution, he enjoyed to boost the profitability of this entity in less than 2 years. Between 1999 and 2005, he was Corporate Marketing Innovation Manager for STMicroelectronics (WW). As such, he contributed to the definition of new marketing approaches involving IT as well as System-to-System process definition with RosettaNet and other XML consortia. One of his focus is e-Marketing including Search Engine Marketing and 1to1 marketing. He played the role of evangelist toward advanced e-Marketing approaches. JC is the author of 4 best seller books targeting VP Marketing,CIO, DSI and other C* level executives covering various aspects of the e-Business and marketing paradigms. Jean-Claude holds a Ph.D. in Management. As a side activity he remains professional ski instructor.
