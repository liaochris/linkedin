Chris Venn’s Summary

I was a bodyguard, trained by ex-members of the British Special Forces, and learned early in my career how to create safety when people needed it the most. At the same time, I learned how to perform at my very best when it mattered most – when people’s lives were on the line. After five years of protecting some of the most important people in the country, I had the opportunity to make a change.

I shifted gears and headed into the business world. First as an owner of a business consultancy, then as a partner in a technology firm, and finally as an entrepreneur working around the world. I have applied my unique talent for helping people create amazing results, faster than they would have expected.

Now, as a speaker, an author and an entrepreneur, I focus on helping people protect themselves from themselves. Destructive patterns, recurring habits, and limiting thoughts tend to keep most people stuck at a certain level of performance. I serve those who want to bust through those limitations and live their best lives.

Chris Venn’s Specialties:

I have been trained in sales training and building world-class teams by some of the best minds in the world. I was also trained to protect the lives of people while I served on a world-class executive protection team. With experience in neuro-linguistics and a ton of presentation training, I create compelling experiences when presenting in front of a room. That, along with my ability to write, and a wide mix of real life experience, I have a lot of fun making a difference.
