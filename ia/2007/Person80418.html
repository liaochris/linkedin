Mario Costanz’s Summary

Mario Costanz is the President and CEO of SmartServ Solutions, an independent consumer financial services firm providing credit education and repair, mortgage origination (residential, commercial and hard money), tax preparation and investments for individuals and small businesses.

He has been an entrepreneur for the past decade in various businesses. His prime focus for the past five years has been helping people with credit education and mortgage lending. Currently, Mario is developing condominium properties in the Bronx and Brooklyn, NY.

Mario teaches at numerous community and investment groups throughout the country. Born and raised in the Bronx, Mario Costanz is a true New Yorker and savvy businessman.
