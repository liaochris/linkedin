Richard Titus’s Summary

Richard has founded or co-founded seven firms in his diverse entrepreneurial career; most significantly he co-founded and served as President and Vice Chairman of Schematic, a 300+ person interactive agency based in Los Angeles, New York & Atlanta, named a top 10 independent Interactive agency by Adweek for 2006. He has also worked with his filmmaker wife executive producing a variety of feature film and television projects including two Sundance selections; the seminal On_line, in 2002 and 2006’s hit eco-documentary Who Killed the Electric Car? – (top 35 grossing theatrical documentary films of all time).

Richard is currently helping his wife run her entertainment company in London and is considering a return to his entrepreneurial roots, where he seeks to leverage his vision, extensive entrepreneurial experience and extensive rolodex to build another successful internet, technology or media business.

Richard Titus’s Specialties:

Futurist, start-up CEO, international business, mergers and acquisitions, business development, strategic partnerships, distressed business turnarounds, intellectual property, broad entertainment media experience (Film, Television, Video Games and Music.), and new media (Interactive Television, set-top, console gaming, wireless, broadband, community, mobile and new platforms and devices) a deep and fundamental understanding of technology and human interaction with it.
