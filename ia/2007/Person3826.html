Allen Canning’s Summary

As a business-oriented Technical Consultant, my goals are always to design and configure system architectures to facilitate ease of maintenance. For my clients, I identify essential systems, outline methods for monitoring their status, use tools to monitor their performance, review system configurations, and recommend changes to improve system availability and reliability.
In support of rapid growth and change, I have performed system rollouts, and integrated new systems with existing services. I also have deployed new software and services in established environments, and migrated existing environments to new technologies.
One of my goals is to increase the repeatability of tasks using automation. Improve the reliability of a client’s environment by maintaining up-to-date documentation of the infrastructure. Create training materials for new staff members to facilitate a smooth transition into the environment.
