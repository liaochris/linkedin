Tim Perrett’s Summary

Having worked with a very broad range of prestigious clients and delivered solutions and platforms that have been highly effective. I have been heavily involved in the use and growth of the XMPie platform and am one of the longest running developers in Europe. Bespoke and integrated application architecture with an XMPie composition core is very much my forte.

I am a specialist in digital printing and workflow systems and have a strong base with Kodak, Creo and Xerox systems. Having architected system platforms that utilise all manner of vendor technology to work seamlessly. I pride myself on building comprehensive middleware solutions that meet every facet of a requirement.

Highly competent when working in a very wide variety of programming and development environments, including Python, Ruby, Java and Objective-C and well versed in deploying to any kind of platform, be it Unix, Linux, OSX server, or even win32.
