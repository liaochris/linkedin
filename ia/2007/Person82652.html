Steve Wetherill’s Summary

Steve has been active in the "mobile" space since 2002, and most recently shipped original "one button" mobile title "EA Air Hockey", now available on BREW and J2ME. Visit www.uztek.com for details.

His company, Uztek Games, is currently working on mobile titles for several publishers.

Steve has 20+ years of experience with video game technology and development on platforms ranging from ZX Spectrum to XBOX, from PS2 to Symbian and from J2ME to Game Boy Advance.
