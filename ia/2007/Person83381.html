Our 20 year practitioner and customer approach to building enterprise applications led us to cooperating with a ex-marketing team from Ogilvy who brought us through their marketing best practices. We modeled our applications on realworld campaigns that Ogilvy had conducted for firms ranging from the small enterprise to large Banks. This resulted in an integrated email marketing application called Spinnakerpro and SensorPro, a web and email-based survey application.

Thanks to this approach we are now proud to have earned customers such as Verizon, Pampers and Coty . In 2003 we won the TMC CRM Best Product Award.

Built from the ground up on the Microsoft .NET platform, utilizing C# and Web Services technologies, the products can be deployed as a licensed or self-service application. They are distributed worldwide and under OEM agreement by Epicor Scala and Epsilon Interactive.

Prior to Narragansett Technologies, I co-founded SAI/Redeo in 1992 which pioneered payment settlement and reconciliation solutions on the IBM AS/400 and Microsoft platforms. The products were and still are distributed worldwide by SSA Global. SAI/Redeo was acquired by Clarus Corp in May 2000. Prior to founding SAI/Redeo, I headed up the R&D operation for Software 2000/Infinium Software, now owned by SSA Global (acquired by Infor in May 05)
