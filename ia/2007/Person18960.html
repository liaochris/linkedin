Steve Svalina, Creative Wiring’s Summary

I have over 21 years of electrical experience. I relocated here from Northern Va in 2000 for a better lifestyle for my family. I worked for a local company in Raleigh doing low voltage. My position with them was as supervisor. I then saw that there was potential for me to start my business so I could offer my own knowledge and services to the public. I started my company, Creative Wiring Solutions in January 2002. We install home theater systems, structured wiring, central vacuum systems, home integration, phone, cable & networking, security, secuity monitoring. My company is licensed and insured and I also hold several certifications with OnQ, THX, and I am a member of the Triangle Builders Guild and Crave.

I also support our local Chamber of commerce and have volunteered our help with the Habitat for Humanity.

My goals for the company is to offer awesome customer service with the one on one feel. I believe that you truely need to know your system and I am hear to help you and learn your system and design what you need.
