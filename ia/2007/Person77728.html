Sean Greathead’s Summary

I have worked in Learning and Development and HR for more than 9 years, specialising in Learning and Change Management. I have worked as a consultant in South Africa, other parts of Africa and the United Kingdom for over 6 years and headed up a Learning and Development in Virgin Active South Africa since March 2006.

In heading up a Learning and Development responsible for all learning and development for the Virgin Active. Including 80 clubs, head office and five regional offices and the strategising and management of staff development of the R1.2 billion growth plans.

As a consultant, I have worked with a number of organisations including Shell, Chevron (Caltex), 20Twenty, ABSA, Old Mutual and Computer Sciences Corporation (CSC) overseeing, designing and managing learning and development projects. In addition, he has facilitated the development of Strategic Plans for community based organisations and blue chip companies, such as Amalgamated Beverage Industries (Subsidiary of SABMiller) and developed performance management systems for organisations such as DivFood (Part of Nampak). My work in Induction and On-boarding for new employees has formed part of my Masters degree, focusing on the development of learning skills and business knowledge to cope with the changing workplace.
