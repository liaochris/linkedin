Laura Young’s Summary

My first love is personal development coaching particularly for those in mid-life or other significant transitions. I find mid-life to be a fascinating and powerful time but I seem to enjoy aging far more than everyone around me. I'm known as the Dragon Slayer and much of my professional emphasis is on building courage and self confidence in my clients. Adulthood is not for sissies and many of my clients are dealing with aging parents, career questions, relationship questions...all the mid-life crisis issues on would assume. I like going right into the belly of the beast with my clients, providing a safe and confidential place for them to voice their fears and insecurities and then finding a way to overcome them. My blog is a great resource on a number of these issues.
Additionally, I very much enjoy working with entrepreneurs and solopreneurs on business concept development and branding issues. I work well with non-linear and creative types who tend to get lost in their own ideas and have trouble making actual movement toward their goals.
