Rob Sherratt’s Summary

Intellectual property (patents) specialist with technical responsibilities spanning over 4,000 patents. Much of my work involves preparation or defence of assertion cases in conjunction with Patent Lawyers.

Managed two startup companies, one as Director, one as interim Director.

Project management of full lifecycle systems development projects in telecommunications and process control. Team sizes from 2 to 200. Financial responsibility within projects from £200 k to £20 million.

Development of hardware, software and systems solutions in the fields of process control, telemetry, energy management, digital switching, routers, line interfaces.
