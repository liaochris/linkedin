Brian Bos’s Summary

Director of Emerging Technology media planning for Ford, Lincoln, Mercury, & Ford Corporate in areas such as interactive television (VOD, DVR, ITV), mobile phones, and video games. Additional responsibilities include continuing client and agency education on the changing media & marketing landscape through hands-on demonstration in the Team Detroit Medialab, a dedicated space that specializes on building awareness of shifts in consumer behavior.

Brian Bos’s Specialties:

Marketing campaigns for top brands on emerging platforms, including interactive television (ITV), VOD, DVRs, mobile phones, and video games for Automotive and QSR brands.

Brian Bos’s Experience

  • Vice President, Convergence Director

    Mindshare - Team Detroit

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; WPP; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    April 2005 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    As Director of the Emerging Technology department primary responsibilities include media planning and campaign development for automotive & QSR brand marketing across interactive television (VOD, DVR, and ITV), mobile phone, and video game marketing channels.

    Additional responsibilities include the development of marketing technology landscape presentations, campaign analysis, and consumer market research.

    In 2006, five WPP owned agencies combined under one roof, allowing for consolidation and expansion of client responsibilities. Included in this move was the opening of the Team Detroit Medialab, a client and WPP agency facility focused on continuing education of the changing media, marketing & technology landscape. Through the use of traditional presentation and hands-on demonstration, this dedicated space is used to build awareness and experience the shifts in consumer behavior first hand through interaction with mobile, gaming, PC, internet and television technologies.

  • Manager - Emerging Technology

    General Motors MediaWorks

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; IPG; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 1999 — April 2005 (6 years)

    Primary responsibilities from 1999 to 2003 included the media buying, planning, and project management of Emerging Technology media campaigns for all General Motors divisions.

    Media channels included mobile phones, Interactive Television, DVR, VOD, Rich Media, Internet Video, and console, PC & internet video games.

    Between 2003 and 2005, Emerging Technology media responsibilities were shared with GM Planworks, with the main job function being the management of media and technology partner relationships, formulation of proposals and negotiation of the media costs.

    Promoted in 2001 to Director of the GM Media Lab, a state-of-the-art facility focused on evolving communication channels, including Telematics, mobile phones, consumer media trends, and advanced television services. The GM Media Lab provided a forum for “hands on” exploration of current & future marketing channels with the purpose of keeping key decision makers informed on changes in the media landscape.
