Neil Walker’s Summary

I have worked for the past eight years on the border between New Media and Traditional Media.

During this period I have worked on many high profile media projects in the UK and Europe including Big Brother Series 1, Pop Idol, Popstars and Survivor in the commercial sector and on their new Broadband VOD service whilst at the BBC.

During 2002 I spent several months in the USA as part of a start-up. An experience, which whilst not as financially rewarding as I would have hoped, I still see it as being a unique learning opportunity. I fully intend to try again in the near future.

I have always dealt with the bleeding-edge and intend to carry on doing the same for some time yet.
