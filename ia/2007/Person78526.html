Stephen Frost’s Summary

I am a creative and proven video game professional with more than 10 years of experience looking to continue my career in development by working with other talented people to produce quality and innovative videogame titles. I am experienced in both internal and external development situations; well-versed in 1st party and ESRB product submission procedures and also have game design experience including core mechanics development, puzzle creation and balance/tuning.

I bring a well-rounded perspective and unique experience within the video game industry ranging from game producer on several high-profile titles to a well-respected videogame journalist for the industry's leading magazines and websites, all of which have proven invaluable given the knowledge, experience and contacts these roles have provided me. I pride myself on having detailed and far-reaching knowledge of hardware, console, PC titles, as well as general technology and entertainment.
