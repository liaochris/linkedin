Mark Taylor’s Summary

I have worked in retail for over 20 yrs and have covered the breadth and depth of the sector. This includes head office, regional and store based roles both nationally and internationally. I have managed a variety of functions; retail operations, e-commerce, mail order, buying, merchandising, finance, franchising, wholesale, dept store concessions, joint ventures and latterly new business developments.

I have worked for Marks & Spencer, River Island, Littlewoods Home Shopping, Adams Childrenswear and Episode, in roles throughout the UK and Europe (including residential stays in Spain, Portugal & Chile).
