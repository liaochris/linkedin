Vikas Chawla’s Summary

I have travelled across Europe, Asia, Far East, Australia, New Zealand & the UK, which has made me understand about other countries and cultures.

After I joined college (Commerce with specialization in Marketing), life sure has been challenging with me taking up lots of opportunities. I took up the MILT Leadership Course (which is based on Dale Carnegie’s Course) which changed my perspective about life. This was immediately followed by the most influential organisation in my life, AIESEC. AIESEC is the world’s largest youth run organisation and it provides an international platform for young people like me, to discover and develop our potential. It was here that I was able to meet people from 40 different countries and understand so many different cultures. It was here that I gained client management skills, HR skills, People management skills, planning and organizing skills and it was here that I actually understood what team work meant. Through my experiences in AIESEC, I was able to decide my future career, namely HR.

I pursued a management internship for 2 months with an export firm, & a part time HR internship (at DATS India in Chennai) that gave me practical experience in the field. Along with this, I also finished my part-time PG diploma in Human Resource Mgmt. (from LIBA) that gave me the required theoretical knowledge.

To specialize in the filed of Training, I completed my Certification for Trainer and Facilitator from Middle Earth Consultants, Hyderabad and Carlton Advanced Management Institute, USA. I am currently working as the Asst. manager - HR at Taj Coromandel in Chennai (where I joined in May 2006 after my AIESEC term). For 4 months I also went for my AIESEC internship to an e-learning company - Tata Interactive Systems in Germany.
