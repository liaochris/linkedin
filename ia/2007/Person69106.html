Pablo Päster, MBA’s Summary

As Business Development Partner for Eco-Leaf I am working on research and strategic planning to prepare this start-up company to hire a management team and begin soliciting investors.

As Technical Advisor for DriveNeutral I work on developing carbon footprint calculators for the DriveNeutral website and develop custom applications for clients.

As Engineer/Consultant for Natural Capitalism Solutions I developed a system for quantifying the ecological material intensity for a major energy bar company. This work involved the development of complex spreadsheets and performing factory audits around the country. I also provided engineering support for the Panjshir Provisional Reconstruction Team (PRT) in Afghanistan on efforts to use renewable energy to provide energy and drinking water to various villages.

As Manufacturing Engineer for DEL Ozone I was responsible for efficiency, quality, and engineering support on industrial and pool ozone generators. I worked on facility improvements and production line layout and developed PLC-based automated test stands. I led a matrix team for quality and decreased the number of manufacturing defects from 8 per unit to less than 0.5 per unit over six months by updating production documents such as engineering drawings, as well as maintaining tooling, and providing training. I worked closely with our engineering team on improving the manufacturability and overall design of the TrioPure product. I designed and documented the manufacturing process, performed time studies, and made engineering drawings for outsourcing wire harnesses. I worked on material specification and specification of rapid-prototyping samples. Once production began on the TrioPure line I performed production scheduling and addressed defect issues with foreign and domestic suppliers. I directly managed up to 8 production employees.
