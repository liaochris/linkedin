Anne Marie Smith’s Summary

Anne Marie Smith is an Information Management consultant and professional with broad experience across industries. She has exceptional, demonstrated skills in business requirements gathering and analysis, data governance and stewardship, data architecture, data and process modeling, strategic data management, meta data management, data warehouse design and development, project management, and methodology development and implementation. Anne Marie has extensive experience as a consulting instructor in the areas of project management, information systems analysis and design, requirements analysis, data governance and stewardship, testing and quality assurance, data management, data warehousing and business intelligence, and various systems methodologies. She has strong leadership and superior instructional skills. She is an accomplished speaker, facilitator, and experienced assistant professor of MIS at Philadelphia area universities.

Anne Marie Smith’s Specialties:

Data modeling, IS strategic planning, business requirements gathering and managment, project management, methodology development and implementation, course development and delivery, metadata management, data governance and stewardship, data warehousing development and assessment
