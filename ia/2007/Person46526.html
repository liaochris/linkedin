Kathie Thomas’s Summary

I am the founder of the Virtual Assistant industry in Australia. Originally I was just wanting to work at home and be there for our 5 daughters but somewhere along the line that changed when the Internet entered the business scene. Before I knew it I became the mentor and encourager for women around the world who wanted to work at home. Today I manage a group of 220+ VAs worldwide and am moderator for two VA forums totalling over 1,000 virtual assistants.

I help connect clients with virtual assistants and virtual assistants with clients. I write about the industry through articles and blogs and have since developed a coaching program for Virtual Assistants. I also speak at conferences and seminars to encourage women worldwide that they also can return home to work and be there for their families too. One of my favourite topics is the Proverbs 31 Woman.
