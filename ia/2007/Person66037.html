Pascal Opitz’s Summary

ILikeCode is a freshly created limited company based in London, UK.
It has been founded by Pascal Opitz, an experienced web developer who has been working for digital design agencies in UK and Germany since 2001. Having gained a deep insight into the process of developing professional web applications throughout countless projects for big as well as for small clients, Pascal decided to specialize in the technical side of web development.

Previous projects include The Economist, BP Gas Operations, Sodexho, Robert Bosch GmbH, Unicef, PriceWaterhouseCoopers, Eurohypo, Mainova and many more.

ILikeCode is offering technical web development ranging from bespoke back end systems and accessible UI development to template development and back end customization for CMS and e-commerce platforms. Also offered is consultancy ranging from pre-project recommendations and planning to post-project code reviews and migration strategy.
