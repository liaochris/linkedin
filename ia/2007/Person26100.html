Edward James, CFO MBA’s Summary

I am currently the highly-motivated and well-experienced CFO of a publicly-traded telecom company in the Atlanta area. I have a well-rounded executive finance and accounting leadership background with experiences that include: equity and debt transactions, startup management, investor relations, finance and accounting management, venture capital backed transactions and M&A activities within the wholesale/retail distribution, transportation logistics, and telecom industries in enterprises ranging from startup to over $35 billion. I believe my qualifications and experience clearly define me as the ideal finance executive. I have a broad business perspective that is the result of experience in several key areas that virtually all businesses share: Accounting, Finance, Information Technology, and Operations. I am a very hands-on executive that is responsible for multi-site international accounting processes, human resources, and information technology for our firm and I am strategic partner to our CEO.

I am interested in smaller organizations because of the hands-on, always-on, and generally wide scope of their typical finance group. I enjoy being involved with all areas of a business because I believe that the true financial steward, or financial guardian, of the organization is the CFO that understands and has confidence in all aspects of their business.
