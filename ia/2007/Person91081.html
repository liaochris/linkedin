William Sears’s Summary

I have worked in various facets of the Internet/online world for over a decade. I worked for a system integrator, where I founded a professional services group to integrate Internet-related projects for clients (mainly in the entertainment and media industries). We later spun that group off to form Digital Boardwalk, a web applications development company, specializing in e-commerce sites. After the acquisition of that company, I helped an Arizona-based web design company (Square Radius) establish a foothold in the Southern California market. I then joined a regional Internet Service Provider/ISP (DigiLink) where I managed marketing and operational activities. In 2005, I joined Perfect Market (an Idealab company) where I am Program Manager for Snap, a next generation search engine.

Most recently I manage a line of business that is focused on web traffic acquisition and monetization. I oversee a team of developers, editors, and analysts.

I have a passion for consumer electronics (gadgets), multimedia, and online services and properties. I am very interested in cognitive science, user experience, and human/machine interfaces.
