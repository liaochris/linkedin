Ed Cavazos’s Summary

I'm a Principal in Fish & Richardson's Austin office. My practice includes representing clients in general business and corporate transactions and in complex technology-related deals involving intellectual property. I routinely counsel clients regarding general corporate, licensing, and labor and employment matters, as well as a variety of related issues. I advise clients in the e-commerce, Internet, software, hardware and semiconductor industries. I also have experience in media and entertainment law. I have civil jury trial experience in both state and federal courts and have litigated disputes involving trade secrets, copyright, trademarks, breach of contract and Internet-related claims.
