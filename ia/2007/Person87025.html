Dr. Tony Azzarelli’s Summary

A senior professional with significant international experience gained in large corporations and global Telecom/IT service providers and operators, effective in working at all levels in organizations. Effective in planning and executing strategy to reduce industry barriers and on-time market access.

My expertise lie in regulatory affairs (spectrum management and licensing), market access and business development in all regions and most countries of the world. Great network of contacts with industry leaders (e.g. Etisalat, Eutelsat, Intelsat, Thuraya, Telstra, MELCO, Vodafone, France Telecom, Cable & Wireless), regulatory bodies (e.g. China, Russia, EMENA, Canada, Indonesia, NZL, Australia, Japan) and international/regional organizations (e.g. European Commission, ITU, CEPT, ETSI, ICAO, IATA, Eurocontrol).

Strategic thinker, customer and business focused, strong leadership skills and team player, strong analytical and technical skills, excellent presentation and communication skills, experienced in negotiating with governments and industry leaders and culturally sensitive.
