Lorraine Flett’s Summary

I am a multi-faceted project manager with a background in advertising, marketing, public relations and the Internet.

I was one of the first four students to be accepted into the San Francisco School of Digital Filmmaking, where I received an intensive hands-on education in digital filmmaking. Stephen Kopels, a veteran filmmaker and the school's founder and Director of Education, considers me the most creative person he's ever known, which makes me think "Yikes!" ~ that's quite a reputation to uphold. Nonetheless, I'm up for the job!

In 2006, I had the opportunity to work with my mentor James Dalessandro as Production Coordinator on his historical documentary, The Damnedest, Finest Ruins. Wearing my usual many hats, I was also responsible for marketing and distribution, film festival submissions, as well as planning and organizing a 5-state, 6-city tour of the Mid West to promote the film. The film won a Gold Medal in the Aurora Awards and Best Documentary in the Napa Film Festival.

I'm looking to utilize my seasoned project management skills in a creative, forward-thinking company.

If you're familiar with Jasper type sorting on Monster.com, I am a Type 3 Visionary. If you're more inclined towards Myers-Briggs, I'm an INTP (apparently the same as Bill Gates!).

You can read more about me at sassyandsingleinsf.blogspot.com.
