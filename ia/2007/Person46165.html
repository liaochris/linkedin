Karen Troll’s Summary

After a BA in international relations (option international economy and law) at HEI, I completed my formation with two masters (one LLM in international economics law and European law and the second one in international security and defence).

I previously worked for an NGO (Amnesty International) as well as for the UN (UN Institute for Disarmament Research) and for the Swiss government, at the international level (international affairs division of the Federal Office for Communications).

In each of these positions, I wrote analytical, legal and output reports, which made me developed a good analytical and synthesis mind. Particularly, at OFCOM, I was responsible for the content and editing of the web pages of the division, which is something I am also currently doing at SMWIPM.
When working for the deputies, I had the opportunity to prepare law projects and in my current position, I am the communication person for the NGO.

I am fluent in English as well as French (my mother tongue) and German and I do have a good IT literacy.

All my previous works helped me to develop a good sense of diplomacy, having collaborated with people coming from very different cultures, and this is an aspect I appreciate. I am also used to travel for my jobs and am very willing to do so if needed.

I am used to the independent work as well as the work in team, depending on the dossiers. Moreover, at the OFCOM, I often represented Switzerland during international conferences and negotiations. I am also doing so at SMWIPM.
