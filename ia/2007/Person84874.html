Terry Smit’s Summary

Terry Smit has 20 years of experience in the Information Technology Industry. During this time he has been responsible for various E-commerce, IT-Infrastructures and Software solutions.

Terry is now responsible for a Software Development Department of SNS Reaal and reponsible for 50+ employees and 25+ projects. He has also participated in key consultancy assignments regarding the establishment of Network Systems Management, Software Factories, Microsoft business solutions, security concepts and the design and implementation within major organizations, including Rabobank, AEGON, BNG, SNS Reaal, Department of Defense, Royal Navy, Department of RWS, Department of WVC, CJIB, Koninklijke Marechaussee (KMAR), Hooge Huys Verzekeringen, Gemeente Amsterdam, Lehnkering, BAM

As owner Terry Smit has been responsible for organizations up to 30 people and as employee / contractor been responsible for departments up to 80 people. Terry Smit is regarded a technical project manager as well as a people manager and respected colleague throughout all levels of the organization.
