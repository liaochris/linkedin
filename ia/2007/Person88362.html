Jeremy Tromans’s Summary

After spending nearly 10 years in the Royal Navy (Medical branch), I was employed within the UK Pharmaceutical Industry (Johnson & Johnson), starting out in a variety of Medical Sales roles and thereafter within Product Management.

Since 1999, I have been a self-employed businessman, initially forming a specialist pharmaceutical recruitment agency, which was quickly evolved to operate as an online recruitment portal (www.allaboutmedicalsales.com). Since this time, my interests have taken me towards the fast developing Internet sector, with a particular interest in Internet Marketing.

Alongside the above, there has also been an active interest in professional network marketing and in particular, the ongoing development of an independent distribution network in partnership with Telecom plus Plc (The Utility Warehouse). This has been a particularly successful endeavor, with significant financial rewards and will no doubt, be an ongoing project for the next few years.
