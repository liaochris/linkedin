David Munns’s Summary

With 27 years experience in silicon design, I have for the last 14 years provided a freelance consultancy service. Clients include many of the leading companies in the industry.

Having worked with leading EDA companies and with design flows from several major semiconductor companies I have an understanding of the relationship between all stages of design, implementation and verification.

For most of my projects I have worked on-site all over Europe, either independently or as part of a team.

David Munns’s Specialties:

Experience spanning the design/synthesis and layout/timing-closure/verification hand-over is particularly relevant with issues such as clocking, crossTalk, power optimisation and distribution and all aspects of physical verification.

My background in full-custom design has given me a deeper understanding of the physical and analogue aspects of complex digital devices.
