Devra Weissman’s Summary

From customer service positions to tough-as-nails production jobs, my work experience over my lifetime has run the gamut. I have developed a sense of confidence in regards to my interactions with others, and I have learned the value of paying attention to not only what people say, but what they do.

For the last several years, I have facilitated the filming of EPKs and DVD behind-the-scenes in a production management capacity. From small budget productions of a few thousand all the way to 3 plus million dollars, I have worn many hats in regards to production, all of which have been rewarding. Regardless of being in a management position, when food needs to be picked up and an assistant is unavailable, I have two feet and two hands that work perfectly well, and getting the job done, regardless of the task, is key in a well-functioning entity.

Although I enjoy my job immensely, and I have learned much from it, production is simply a job for me but not a career. So, with that said, I believe my experience in managing budgets, building projects from start to finish, and managing crews of all different sizes, temperaments and skill-sets, gives me a great foundation to move into commercial real estate.
