Yvonne Aburrow’s Summary

I am a web developer and usability consultant. I have used XML, XSLT, HTML, CSS, ColdFusion, Ruby on Rails, and SQL (both MySQL and Oracle). I have attended Information Architecture and User Testing courses at User Experience 2006; used TechSmith Morae usability testing suite; and devised and carried out usability tests. I have excellent interpersonal and communication skills, and in-depth knowledge of a wide variety of software packages, including XMLSpy, ColdFusion, Dreamweaver, OpenOffice, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint) and a variety of email and communication packages. I have also produced a wide range of teaching and training materials in relation to software and IT concepts.
