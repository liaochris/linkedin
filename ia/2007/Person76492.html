Santosh Varughese’s Summary

Most (about 95%) Technology and Mfg. companies in the US w/ revenues $5MM - $250MM are losing hundreds of thousands of dollars each year because they are not taking advantage of Federal and State R&D Tax Credits.

Since 2001, the specialists at my firm - FortisTCS - have identified and procured Millions of $s in Federal Government Tax Benefits and Credits for US companies - ranging from closely held private businesses to large publicly held corporations.

We retroactively go back 3-4 years and recover the Tax Benefits and Credits that you were entitled but did not take. It's like 'found money' and can have a significant impact on your company's bottom line!

We perform our services on a contingency basis - Therefore you have no out of pocket cost for the work we do. We get paid a % of the actual cash that you receive back from the IRS.

For example, we maximize R&D Tax Credits by evaluating spending activity from an operations approach rather than an accounting approach. Typical clients with US$5-250MM revenue recover US$100-2000K in cash refunds from previous and current tax years.

If your company is already taking advantage of R&D tax credits, we are probably going to get an additional 2-5 times more credits - because our analysis drills down to identify all activity and expenditures that are qualified for R&D tax credits -instead of just the 'R&D Department' within your company.

Additionally, our firm develops and implements strategies for permanent, ongoing tax advantages available via US gov't provisions and policies.
