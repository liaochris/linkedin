Karen Smiley’s Summary

I have over 20 years of broad and deep experience, as a engineer and as a manager, with the full software system development lifecycle. I am currently an SEI-authorized PSP Instructor and TSP Coach, a Certified ScrumMaster, and one of the first PSP Certified Developers.

As a team member in ABB Corporate Research, I coach and support ABB business units in software architecture, verification and validation, and technology. I also collaborate with university and professional colleagues on independent research and publications in the area of software architecture and processes.

In 2004 I initiated part-time PhD work in Computer Science with Dr. Laurie Williams of NCSU. This is now on hold so that I can use my free time to focus on actively building and launching fundraising initiatives for Echo Hill Campership Fund (ehcf.org) and for lung cancer research, in my late husband Dan's memory. I plan to re-evaluate my decision on resuming my academic work at the end of 2007. In the meantime, I am starting to learn Mandarin Chinese for both personal and professional reasons.

For more information about my professional interests, consulting and research, see www.agileteams.com/aboutKJS.html.
