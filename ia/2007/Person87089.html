Tony Lopykinski’s Summary

Visionary technology leader with a passion for helping organizations strategically manage their information and technology assets to drive business results and achieve competitive advantage. Considerable expertise in information management and the full lifecycle development of data warehouse, business intelligence, decision support, and financial reporting solutions with fifteen years of progressive management experience in the retail, banking, and publishing industries.
