Brian Ouellette’s Summary

Almost a decade spent as a money manager working in Chicago on the Chicago Board of Trade, on Wall Street in New York City and in Seattle, WA.

In 2000, While managing money for the Private Client Group of Oppenheimer & Co., Brian was invited to be the Money Reporter for FOX's Seattle Morning News TV Show. For 3 1/2 years, he reported daily from his Oppenheimer & Co. office in the Columbia Tower in Seattle. Credited with over +2,000 live TV appearances, he reported during the biggest boom and biggest bust in the history of the US Stock Market.

In April 2004, he left money management completely to focus on building his startup company, Shoewallet.com Active Gear. Featured in Shape Magazine, Runner's World, NY1, CNN, The Today Show and on over 200 radio stations nationwide, Shoewallet.com has produced custom branded shoewallets for such brands as Southwest Airlines, US Air Force, Department of Defense, YMCA, Jamba Juice, Skechers, Countrywide Financial, etc.

Mission is to provide a useful tool in the shoewallet for active people/travelers and increase awareness of the need to always carry ID when on the road or trail. This message driven by their Safer Roads & Trails Campaign; Be Smart. Be Safe. Carry ID.™ In addition, by working with their customers and partners, they want to help motivate all people, young and old, to live an active and healthy lifestyle.

Brian Ouellette’s Specialties:

A majority of the business (>90%) is focused on making custom branded shoewallets for corp./non-profit/military/event customers, www.shoewallet.com/customad. The other <10% is focused on selling shoewallets thru the retail channels of www.shoewallet.com and www.amazon.com.
