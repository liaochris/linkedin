Kaustubh Srikanth’s Summary

I'm a Web professional who architects, designs, and builds successful Web sites, applications and interfaces. With 6 years experience designing, developing and guiding the Web, I'm well versed in all aspects of Web design, development and production -- with an emphasis on user-centered design, Web standards and practical ROI.

As an expert in media delivery and publishing online, I have worked with several companies helping them setup the most optimized solutions to publish their audio and video content online using methods such as Internet Radio, high quality Audio/Video streaming and Podcasting.

I am also an active member of the Linux and Open-Source development community and have contributed to development of several open-source projects over the years.

I currently spearhead an outfit called RadioVeRVe (formerly Infinity Radio), an internet radio station which focuses on providing independent musicians in India a platform to promote their music. RadioVeRVe plays independently produced tunes from across India, 24/7!
