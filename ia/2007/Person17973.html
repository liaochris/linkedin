Charles Manning’s Experience

  • CEO/President

    PLAYXPERT

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    2006 — Present (1 year)

    PLAYXPERT provides in-game community management tools for gamers of PC games and Massively Multiplayer Online Games. PLAYXPERT delivers unique in-game overlay technology and combines it with an extensible widget architecture allowing gamers to stay in game while using the tools they require. Initial widgets include trillian style chat (works with other chat networks like XFire, Yahoo, AOL, and MSN), multiple content widgets allowing access to key content while in game (Armory, Thottbot, Wowhead, WoWWiki), and a full-featured clan management widget.

    PLAYXPERT is the next generation in-game platform for gamers.

  • VP; Product Management

    Managed Objects

    (Computer Software industry)

    June 2001 — 2006 (5 years)

    Manning worked at Managed Objects reporting to the CEO as the VP of Product Management. Managed Objects is an enterprise software company selling Business Service Management (BSM) solutions to Global 2000 customers. Having defined the BSM market in 2000 and continuing to innovate today, Managed Objects is the analyst proclaimed leader in BSM. Managed Objects’ innovative platform, rich features, and referencable customers are a testament to Manning’s ability to manage key relationships and position Managed Objects properly with the analyst community, identify critical applications of technology for a given market, and work with various groups internal to a software company to get things done.

    Manning was essential to Managed Objects growth from an operational process and sales perspective having been involved at some level with the close of over 90% of every deal since he joined.
