Patrick Monasterio’s Summary

People. That's what everything is all about, isn't? You can't go anywhere without the possibility of talking to someone, meeting someone, or really, making a lasting impression on someone. I've always been interested in people. How they think, why they think what they think, and how strongly do they feel on the matter. People are the builders of the future, and the foundation of what is to come and what must come to be. Because of this, I find the most joy out of positions where I can talk to people, create positive relationships, and ask the question "Why?".

I have eight years experience in the professional world, with success in sales, marketing, and customer relations. I'm very interested in gaming, entertainment, technology (IT, Internet, Construction, Software), and sports. Why? You guessed it. The people. It's what I call GETS, and it "gets" people together (copyrighted material). I'm also very analytical, which has allowed me enjoy success in my endeavors such as sales and management.
