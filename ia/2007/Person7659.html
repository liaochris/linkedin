Arnaud Fischer’s Summary

User-obsessed and Competition-paranoid! 14 years of product management, product marketing, research (qualitative, quantitative, usability, eye tracking, ...), strategic product planning experience for consumer search and search marketing, including deep expertise in social search, Internet sentiment analysis and Web analytics.

I started with Notes and Lotus dev. before the IBM acquisition, Microsoft during the good years, Altavista before the Yahoo! acquisition, Infospace when local search was hot.

Constantly on top of social search, Web 2.0 user interaction models such as Ajax-driven features and mashups, personalization, verticalization, API-driven syndication and distribution models, computational linguistic, data mining, sentiment analysis, buzz monitoring, online reputation management.
