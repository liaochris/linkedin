Jeff Clavier’s Summary

Business Angel and Venture Advisor with over 15 years of experience in the software industry.
Most recently General Partner in charge of US operations for RVC, the manager of the Reuters Greenhouse Fund, the Corporate VC arm of Reuters. Before that was involved both as an entrepreneur and senior executive in Product Management and Development, Sales and Marketing, Account Management, Business Development and Strategic Relationships with large financial services clients and partners.

Started as a developer of trading room software (in C on a Sun "RoadRunner" 386i diskless with 4 MB of RAM) in the late 80's at Effix Systems in Paris. After the acquisition of Effix by Reuters Ltd in 1993, led the development organisation for the Systems and then the Risk Management division of the Group, involving 250+ developers in Paris, London and New-York. Joined RVC in 2000 to bring operational discipline to the US portfolio, and lead the overall business development program for the Fund (82 companies, 70% in the US).

Photoblog: http://softtechvc.buzznet.com

Jeff Clavier’s Specialties:

Venture consultant supporting software startups and established vendors, on the definition and execution of critical stages of their business plan: VC financing, product and technology roadmap, business development, international expansion.
Recent focus has been on next generation search functionality, social media and interaction, and large scale web services applications.
