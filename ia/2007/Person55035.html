Mark Gilbertson FCIPD (mark.gilbertson@peaktalent.co.uk)’s Summary

A seasoned talent aquisition specialist with a strong commercial and business focus. My experience covers both roles in the Search & Selection industry as well in-house 'Head of Recruitment' roles. Background across multiple sectors - Consultancy, Legal, FS, IT/Telco, Mail distribution and Retail.

Peak Talent offers two distinct but copatable service lines:

The first being an Executive Search capability with a very strong research team/capability (made up of ex Heads of Research from some of the big players) but with a cost model that is well below that traditionally charged;

and secondly an Interim capability, across both the resourcing/talent arena but also across the generalist space.

Please feel free contact me with your next Search or Senior-level Recruitment requirement or indeed an interim opportunity – put me to the test!
