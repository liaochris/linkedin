Mike Kelley’s Summary

You never really know where one path will take you. I’ve gone from working in an entry level computer job to being swept away with the internet boom of the mid-90’s and along the way picked up advertising, budgeting and planning for large organizations. I have a unique skill set and feel very fortunately for the chances I have been given and the people I’ve met and worked with.

Officially, I am a graphic & web designer, a systems administrator, a project manager, and a musician. I’ve worked in Health Care for over a decade in both IT and Marketing roles. I’m currently working in the Insurance industry bringing all of my expertise with web design and marketing together for a very cutting-edge and forward-thinking company.

Unofficially, I’m a writer, photographer, chef, band manager and someone who’s still excited by learning something new each day. I’m an observer and you better know that if it comes out of my mouth, it’s well thought about and not just off-the-cuff…

The irony is that I’m a quick thinker and quick witted…
