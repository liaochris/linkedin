Maggie Fox’s Summary

I'm the founder of Social Media Group, Canada’s first agency devoted exclusively to helping business navigate the world of Web 2.0, and I like to say that I'm a communications and content expert that has never met a medium I didn't like. I have written and produced television and website content for companies like CHUM, WIC and Disney, survived the dot-bomb and created print and web-based custom publications for major retailers including Home Hardware and Do it Best. A longtime blogger, I'm presently putting my experience to work for companies who want to engage their internal and external audiences through emerging social media channels. I have been interviewed about social media by CTV News, The Toronto Star and Marketing Magazine, was recently included in Women Who Risk, a listing of influential women who head up Internet-based firms and am a member of The Social Media Collective, an invite-only group of “the web’s best thinkers on media, marketing and web 2.0”

Maggie Fox’s Specialties:

Content production, social media marketing, blogger relations, writing, editing, blogging, social media strategy, communications, marketing
