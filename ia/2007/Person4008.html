David O Hara’s Summary

Since leaving the Royal Navy in 2003 I have worked as a contractor in the telecoms industry, mainly in the SC market, gaining experience and learning the ropes. I set up ALPHA NET in 2004. We specialise in MOD telecoms installs, and are fast earning a reputation for our high standards and workmanship as well as our team lead and site/project management skills.

We are currently adding new services to our portfolio; including MCP/MCSE and CISCO certified engineers to assist with the planning and design of networks in this secure environment.

We aim to provide our clients with the facility to have their network designed, installed, tested, commissioned and their engineers trained to the standards required to maintain their network.The ultimate goal is to take the company to the forefront of this specalised corner of our industry on the back of some old fashioned hard work and honesty.
