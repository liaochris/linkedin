Matthew Aaron’s Summary

From my journalism and sales background , I caught the desktop publishing wave in the mid 80's and rode that into a successful business as a freelance creative director. As the internet came to prominence, I found that my skill set was optimised for that space, and I have been pursuing it ever since.

I have lived through the evolution in the digital graphics industry and eagerly pursued leading edge technologies, including early digital pre-press, digital offset printing, e-commerce auction sites, user generated content community web sites, blogs, and more. I feel very lucky to have lived through this exciting time.

Some of my career milestone projects include:

* Technical co-author of “Step One - A first look at the Internet” (Cybertree 1996)
* Designer and Director of Winetrader.com.au, a wine auction site established in the late 90’s. At the time, the largest fully e-commerce enabled wine auction site on-line, offering over 250 lots of wine for auction.
* Editor of the Sydney 2000 Olympic Blog from the New Balance Gold Club hospitality suite during the 2000 Olympic Games.
* Designer of the Tallwoods Village community web site supporting the $280 property development on the NSW Mid North Coast that combined 1000 home sites with a world class golf course, club newly incorporated township. Majority owned by the Fairfax family company Bridgestar Pty Ltd.
* Intranet designer, information architect and editor for Nestlé Oceania regional intranet.

I currently work on both a wholesale and retail basis as an experience architect and web producer.

For details visit my blog http://interactive-marketing.info

I am available for project based work for organisations that may require help defining, designing, writing specifications and project managing an on-line project.

I am open to offers from organisations looking for someone with my skills to join their team as a senior producer.
