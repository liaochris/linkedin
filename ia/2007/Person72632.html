Rick Loggins’s Summary

As Principal in my own company, my goal is to assist a few special clients in recruiting and hiring great new employees and contractors.

My background is a unique blend of 15 years in sales and recruiting; most recently as a small business owner and independent recruiter. I started my own recruiting and staffing firm in order to work with niche clients in niche industries delivering hard to find top performing candidates. My focus over the past two years has been in the Business Intelligence and Planning industry of I.T. and I've been working closely with a Cognos Partner and Cognos-related job openings. You are welcome to contact me directly at (423) 756-8822 or via email rloggins@bellsouth.net

Rick Loggins’s Specialties:

My clients expect me to deliver top candidates for their open positions. They want to see a few resumes and interview a few candidates and want the hiring process to be quick and painless. This requires a bit more effort on my part but if high-performing employees are preferred then why would you want (or do) anything else?
