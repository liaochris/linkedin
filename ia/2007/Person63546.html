Nicolas Mas’s Summary

In my company, H2I France, we are two engineers from the software world (programming and architecture). We use our unconventionnal approach, methodology and analysis to assist companies in solving their strategic problems. We help our customers identify and implement innovative strategies based on new technologies.

We have a very straightforward and opened approach. We want our customers to understand every aspects of the operations we set up. The goal is to help them prepare their own operation. We then just step in for the realisation. You can have a look at our projects NoMajors.com & ConceptSL.com.

We do not prospect, we rely essentially on word to mouth. We pick our projects carefuly. They must be really innovative, value-adding and with great potential.

We are trying to build a network of professionals from various domains related to innovation to address a maximum of problematics. Don't hesistate to contact me.
