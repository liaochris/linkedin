Bob Citelli’s Summary

Clients include SEO tools provider River Gold Associates (www.wordtracker.com); Digital Rights Management experts Macrovision Trymedia (www.trymedia.com); Indie Music pod caster TowerPod (www.towerpod.com); and SendYourFiles (www.sendyourfiles.com), a tool that sends up to 10 GB of info via normal e-mail channels.

Some recent accomplishments are our continuing biz dev and affiliate marketing efforts on behalf of the leading DRM company serving entertainment software publishers: Macrovision Trymedia and its portal Trygames.com (www.trygames.com). We joined Trymedia's team when the Company was situated in a SF, CA loft with less than ten employees. In 2005, publicly traded Macrovision, Inc. purchased Trymedia Systems for $34 million. We helped acquire IP for distribution from publishers like Disney, UbiSoft, Take 2/2K Games, Capcom and others who are using Macrovision Trymedia's ActiveMARK Technology. We increased affiliate distribution by securing programs with GameStop, AOL, Buy.com, Bigfish Games and others and have delivered millions in dollars of top line revenue to the Company since we first began representing it.

At River Gold we've secured data assets from Lycos and continue to work with other ISPs, telecoms and related businesses to increase the efficiency of the tools this organization resells to SMBs looking to maximize their SEO budgets.

Previously we completed agreements for affiliate programs for Classical Archives (www.classicalarchives.com) with Yamaha, NARAS (Grammys), a national trade organization serving music retailers, and a New England symphonic orchestra.

We also established distribution and sales programs for Gamerang (www.gamerang.com), an online videogame rental service with Target.com and a leading metropolitan newspaper a well as secured a used games program with Overstock.com. Other programs secured for Gamerang included cross promotions with consumer buff books and online positions within top game player web sites.
