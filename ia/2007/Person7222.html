Andrew Peek’s Summary

In the business world, the rearview mirror is always clearer than the windshield (Warren Buffet). Strategy, Disruption, Innovation - these are a few of my favourite things. I'm an avid reader, fiction for release and non-fiction for growth. Every day that passes, I notice more and more that the world works the opposite way that most people (including myself) have assumed.

Experience is the last remaining currency and those who are wealthy are looking to share it with those who trade in enthusiasm. I'm a social marketer which means I study behaviour for ways to drive business. I'm also a consultant (as we might all be someday). Essentially, I just like to help businesses solve problems - sometimes for money.

Among the few absolute truths I subscribe to...

1. Successful business leaders should surround themselves with people smarter than they are, challenge the notion that you should never re-invent the wheel, and will sacrifice to leave the world in better shape than when they arrived.

2. A certain degree of ambiguity presents the greatest opportunity for growth. My passion lies in discovering and developing the next wave of thinking and taking the necessary risks to turn good ideas into the standard.

3. A brand is not specific to a product or a company - it is a perception. We don't own our brands - we influence them. At most, what you own are the actions your brand can take. How they are interpreted is beyond your control. A generous person will always be described as a generous person. A selfish person who leaves a good tip is still a selfish person.
