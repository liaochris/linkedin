Luca de Marinis’s Summary

I worked in Italy and in the United Kingdom, mostly writing software. I was lucky to have been (most of the time) doing things which had social implications, like educational, messaging and groupware (rather than accounting), and having been able to pursue innovative ideas in those areas.
I recently helped the development of http://www.regione.lazio.it, a rather big public sector web site for which i wrote the RSS generator, while now I'm juggling various projects in Ruby and C# for Interact, a small and smart enterprise. I am a long time user and producer of free software, both within and outside of my day job.
Lately, I've been doing some stuff whith Ruby on Rails, which I regard as the best platform ever for web based services, outperforming the oh-so-standard java/struts by at least an order of magnitude (and I can prove it!).
