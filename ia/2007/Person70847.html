Randy Cassingham’s Summary

Invented for-profit e-mail publishing in the Dark Ages of the Internet (1994), producing the first paid-subscription e-mail publication with "This is True" -- which still exists in its original form today. A spin-off site, the True Stella Awards, resulted in an extremely successful book tie-in from Dutton (an imprint of Penguin USA).

Created one of the first successful "online-to-offline viral" products, the Get Out of Hell Free Card, which has sold more than one million units -- http://www.GOOHF.com/million
