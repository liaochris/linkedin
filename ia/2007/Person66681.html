Paul McBride’s Summary

The Theatre Company of Harrison, Arkansas has existed for many years. It is a volunteer organization associated with the Ozark Arts Council. They have grown to producing as many as 4 major Broadway musicals a year in the past five years. My experience with them has spanned the past 30 years, with me directing, performing, or conducting in every musical during that time. In addition I have provided music and sound effects for numerous straight plays and ceremonies. All of this is volunteer work. In addition to that, set building has been a large part of those productions, again volunteer work. I have served on the boards of The Theatre Company, The Ozark Arts Council, and the Northwest Arkansas Community Concert Association, as well as numerous educational boards during my educational career.

As for goals, it might be good to act as a consultant to other theater groups who are considering this kind of work.
