Marcel Ekkel’s Summary

ing. Marcel Ekkel PMP specializes in project management in the fields of manufacturing, supply chain, finance, customer services, new media and business development. He is experienced in running project management training and related workshops. Further he enjoys working with teams to enhance their performance. Industry areas that he has covered include: automotive, chemicals, distribution, food, freight forwarding, manufacturing, packaging, shipping, international trade and other logistics areas. Recently he has worked in the field of new media.

Marcel Ekkel acted as a translator and bridge between business teams and the IT-technical teams.
