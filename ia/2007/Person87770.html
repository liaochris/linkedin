Trevor Shanski’s Summary

Trevor Shanski is the founder of eWORDofMOUTH.com, ANTON Publishing Inc., and Anton Properties LLC, a California residential real estate development company. A successful executive, entrepreneur, and author, Shanski has written and published three books and lectured to various marketing associations and at the Wharton School of Business.

Anton Publishing Inc. (ANTON) was formed in 1996 as a small Canadian publisher of the best-selling trade paperback series Loving Your Long Distance Relationship, which to date has sold over 50,000 copies, an incredible achievement for a self published series of books. Soon after publishing the Loving series, ANTON established a web presence by launching its e-commerce book site. (www.sblake.com)

Over time ANTON has expanded to the U.S. and grown into a full service Internet Marketing and Business Consulting company. Based in Palm Springs, California, ANTON is the copyright and trademark holder of the proprietary web-based eWORDofMOUTH Customer Referral, Rewards, and Retention System (www.eWORDofMOUTH.com)
