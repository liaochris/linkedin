Gabe Greenberg’s Summary

After 14 years of global sales and marketing, I have proven track record of building businesses and delivering year on year revenue growth against goals in sales, marketing and operations. While the majority of my experience lies in automotive and retail channels, my current role at Vibrant Media will expand my experience into Men's and Sports channels.

I view myself as a sophisticated mason of sorts. I know what mix of mortar to use to build bricks and clicks and bricks and mortar companies and have a track record to prove it. More importantly I recognize the need for integration and convergence as the media and marketing world evolve.


In 2004, I built the Autobytel Direct brand and product suite. I developed profitable relationships with Automotive OEM’s, strategic partners, and their Advertising Agencies. To include: 34 of the top 36 auto brands as well as several leading insurance companies.

I have an in-depth knowledge of online media, direct marketing, leads, outdoor media, VOD and dynamic messaging, coupled with a rolodex of leaders in the auto and retail sectors. I enjoy making rain. I find it even more rewarding to lead a team and create thunderstorms or the perfect storm.

I have participated in pricing, negotiations, planning and buying of upfront interactive media. I have also played a key role in the development of strategic direct marketing plans, ROI analysis and executions for the client’s named above.

In developing the new direct marketing product suite at Autobytel, I negotiated and developed business plans, term sheets, product presentations, marketing materials and partnership agreements with our list partners, list brokers and data providers.

I work closely with IT, product teams, clients and executives, to develop new products that enhance our ability to maintain a leadership position, deliver a higher ROI and strengthen our overall position in the marketplace
