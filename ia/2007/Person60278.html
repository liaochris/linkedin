Mitch Pirtle’s Summary

I started my career in hardware engineering, and in the late 80s I became enamored with the challenges that occurred when you combined people with technology. As such I entered into software development and interface design, with a focus on emerging technologies - I wanted to have every possible avenue available to produce the best systems available.

I've since branched out to also cover both business and strategic areas, such as online marketing, brand strategy and product development.

However my real focus at present is bringing all of this varied experience together to promote emerging technologies to the non-technical, while still keeping my hands dirty in the implementation of said technologies.

Mitch Pirtle’s Specialties:

Web technologies, database technologies, open source (industry and technologies), information security, product development, enterprise architecture planning, technologies/tools evaluation and research.
