Michael Miles’s Summary

Extensive design management career which encompasses web, multimedia and print, in addition to early successes in scenic production for major film studios and theaters. Ardent believer in creating supportive environments to bring out the best in creative professionals while also setting the highest standards. Effective at finding design solutions which are both practical and results-oriented. Extremely deadline and budget oriented.

Michael Miles’s Specialties:

Managing, mentoring and growing creative teams. Most Adobe & Microsoft programs on both PC & MACs. Brand & identity, designing for both online & offline, b2c & b2b, marketing, advertising, agency & client side.
