Sherman Chin’s Summary

Sherman started game development in 1999 after graduating with an honors degree in Computing from the University of Portsmouth, UK and obtaining his Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer status.

Sherman's first game development venture was with Joseph Tringali at Epix Interactive Studios Inc. located in Delaware, USA. After its closure, he moved on to work for Zreality Inc. located in North Carolina, USA, as a Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game(MMORPG) consultant.

In 2002, Sherman was awarded a scholarship by the International Game Developer's Association (IGDA). Sherman's business plan writing skills also won him the 2nd-runner up place in Venture 2002, Malaysia's first nationwide business plan competition organized by McKinsey & Company. The award was presented to him by the current Prime Minister of Malaysia, Yang Amat Berhormat Datuk Seri Abdullah Badawi.

Sherman then founded his own company, Sherman3D Sdn Bhd ( www.Sherman3D.com ), which was funded by the Malaysian government Cradle Investment Programme and was accredited Multimedia Super Corridor (MSC) status. In 2004, Sherman teamed up with Joseph Tringali again and started the Malaysian branch of 5th Cell Media LLC. ( www.5thCell.com ) where he currently works as 5th Cell's Malaysian General Manager. He has contributed both directly and indirectly to all of 5th Cell's game titles from its conception.

Sherman, Tiffany Lim and Naoko Nakagawa incorporated Sherman3D Multimedia Inc. in Britsh Columbia on the 7th of July 2006. Sherman3D Multimedia Inc. acts as Sherman3D's branch in Canada. Sherman3D is partnered with Magnetar Games Corporation, a research-oriented software developer based in Vancouver working on serious games serving the military and government of Canada.

Sherman is always interested in people wanting to work with him in business or development. Please add Sherman to your Linkedin list directly by sending your invites to Info@Sherman3D.com
