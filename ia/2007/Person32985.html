Carlos L. Barrientos’s Summary

I have 7 years of experience in customer service and building business relationships with the community in the Bay Area. My objective to graduate from SJSU was completed on time with double major in business management and marketing. I have worked at Wells Fargo a growth oriented and technologically advanced bank for 2 years after graduation. I have two years of experience of hard nose sales environment where I exceeded to the max. Today, I work at Pacific Art Collective, where I continue to thrive as a business developer. For the second straight year Pacific Art Collective will be selected as part of the Best of The Bay by San Francisco Magazine. PAC is the leading art group building the live exhibit art genre, an organic form of experiencing artists and their work. Also at this time, I continue to take on more responsibility at Sunday Friends. A non-profit organization where kids develop 35 different developmental assets in order to succeed and grow out of poverty and away from being at risk. My goals are to continue to develop leadership skills and in order to help my business community and make an positive impact in the environment by reducing carbon emissions. I believe the saving our environment is perhaps the biggest opportunity of the 21st century, and it will up to each one us to make a contribution. A contribution can be starting a company that helps reduce post consumer waste, save energy, and water etc. My future near term goal is to work for a company that takes corporate social responsibility to heart, and has a recession proof strategy to help the environment in the boom times and the bad.
