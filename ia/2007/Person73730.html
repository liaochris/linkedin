Rob Witman’s Summary

RiffWare's patent pending technology makes it possible for Internet created content to be easily viewed on cell phones without changes to the content or requiring backend server support. Using this technology, RiffWare is poised to rapidly develop custom applications for clients that provide their Web content for deployment on all of the major cell phones in use today, regardless of their processing power. By deploying content on cell phones, content providers can take advantage of the increasingly large market potential represented by third party cell phone applications. On the BREW platform, responsible for ~40% of the US market, Cell phone application sales resulted in over $300M worth of revenue in the last year alone.
