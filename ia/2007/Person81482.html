Michael Wylie’s Summary

Michael Wylie founded Standing Dog LLC, an interactive marketing and media firm, in July 2005 after a year with Proceed Interactive where he served as Vice President of Travel and Hospitality. His duties included business and product development, partnerships and strategic direction.

Prior to Proceed, Wylie was the Vice President of e-commerce for Wyndham International for four years where he was responsible for all of Wyndham’s e-commerce efforts, including web development, online personalization initiatives, online branding, online marketing strategy, and direct online distribution. Under his leadership, wyndham.com grew from $6 million in annual revenue to over $100 million. Wylie was named one of the 25 Most Extraordinary Sales and Marketing Minds in Hospitality and Travel by HSMAI and served as Co-Chair of the HSMAI Internet Marketing Committee. He has over ten years of marketing experience within various industries and eight years within the e-commerce and hospitality sector. Before Wyndham, Wylie worked for Hilton Hotels Corporation as the Senior Director of e-business and as a senior consultant with the Synapse Group, an internet development firm.
