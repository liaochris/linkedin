Steffen Pelz’s Summary

Prior to joining ViaMetric, I served seven years at Fisher Investments where I developed a keen understanding of cutting-edge, innovative direct marketing strategies aimed to improve organizations’ bottom-lines, while becoming an expert on the subject of financial services marketing.

During my career at Fisher Investments, I held positions of significant and increasing responsibility, and I consider myself a key asset in Fisher Investments' growth from $2 billion to $34 billion in of assets under management during my tenure with the firm.

I developed and introduced a multitude of successful and profitable strategies, and assisted in building many Fisher Investments’ lead generation channels, re-marketing programs, and general marketing processes.

Most recently, as Head of Marketing for Fisher Investments’ UK subsidiary Fisher Wealth Management, my stewardship of the marketing program generated increased sales in less than a year from the beginning of my engagement.
I concurrently held the position of Head of Corporate Events, where I oversaw the successful planning and execution of over 100 client and prospective client events annually with 10,000+ attendees across 50+ markets in the US and UK

My greatest strength is my ability to deliver profitable, measurable results by applying customized marketing strategies designed to help organizations beat their revenue goals.

I earned my undergraduate degree in Business Administration from St. Mary’s University in San Antonio, TX.
