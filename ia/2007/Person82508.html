Steven Ramage’s Summary

An extremely successful business development professional with over 15 years’ experience in international commerce and multiple industry sectors. Key player and shareholder from successful Management Buy Out in 2003 for company that has doubled in size and profitability without any external funding. Business development lead for 3 acquisitions. Built partner channel from zero base to 30+ companies now representing 30% of company turnover. Developed new business worth in excess of £1m through new account development.

Extensive international business development experience in the 'location aware' market. Worked with satellite positioning in offshore survey market, then digital mapping with a global provider and now with an independent software vendor focussing on spatial data quality certification and spatial data re-use.

Good knowledge, understanding and practical experience of building new business divisions and new companies through product innovation, marketing and partnerships. Currently responsible for the 1Spatial Community - global partner network. Working mostly with systems integrators and multinationals.

Having lived and worked around the world I am now based in Norway, still get to use my other language skills like French and German, Norwegian coming slowly.

Steven Ramage’s Specialties:

Business development, channel management, strategic account management, market analysis and segmentation, partnership building, global marketing (new and traditional media) and product management. Remembering names and faces.

    Business development activities with existing customers and partners, as well as identifying new markets and sales opportunities. A door opening position, i.e. doing all the initial work prior to obtain a sale and then engaging the remainder of the sales, product management and technical support organisation to work on the opportunity. It also involved working on pricing models when transactional and subscription-based pricing were new for this type of software.

    Part of the global sales operation team that worked on the worldwide CRM system for Navteq. A great deal of time was spent travelling the world, attending industry events and meeting potential customers and partners.

  • Strategic Business Development Manager

    Future Electronics

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    1996 — 1998 (2 years)

    I joined Future Electronics as part of the international overseas training programme and went through the ranks from Product Specialist dealing with semiconductors for the North American market place to being responsible for the strategic business development function for Southern Europe ($75m turnover at the time). As well as managing a team of people within this high profile group, I was also accountable for the fast turnaround of responses, including pricing and market information, for the field sales force when answering customer requests for quotation for large or strategic bills of material.
