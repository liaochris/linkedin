Jason Fugate’s Summary

I have worked in the field of software engineering since graduating from college in 1998; my first job was in a tiny company doing 1 man/1 application, then a Fortune 50 company where a classic waterfall methodology was used, at Midway Games, on a 10-15 man team in a dynamic development environment, and now at EA Chicago, working on next-gen platforms. Through this variety of experience, I have developed a core set of personal best practices while maintaining the flexibility necessary in diverse situations.
