Kara Miller, MBA, PMP’s Summary

I've been in the Information Technology department at Dell for seven years. I've worked as a software test lead, environment management analyst, website developer, and project manager; and have played a part in the entire software development life cycle. Currently, I am a portfolio manager for Global Manufacturing Fulfillment and Supply Chain.

In May 2007, I completed the Texas Evening MBA (TEMBA) program at the University of Texas. The program was challenging and fun for me. I enjoyed bringing my knowledge to work each day and applying it to my job. While pursuing my MBA, I was the chair of the Events Program on the TEMBA Action Committee and planned quarterly events for our 240 students.

I am a Dell Company Captain for the March of Dimes. I work with a co-captain and our executive sponsors to develop ideas for company fund-raising and budgeting. We then organize WalkAmerica efforts at our Dell sites nation-wide. This has been an exciting experience that will continue to challenge me personally as well as professionally.
