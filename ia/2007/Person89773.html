Venkatesan Vivek Raghavan’s Summary

Vivek Raghavan has been the key architect of Visage Mobile's innovative multi tenant wireless platform.

Mr. Raghavan developed his experience in the area by helping several innovative wireless platform launches including Three(in the UK, Thailand and Italy), MobileESPN, DisneyMobile, Primus and Voce.

His experience includes driving Visage's mobile's initial platform offering, vendor choices and real-time billing/information management systems, CRM, Speech Recognition, and Service oriented Gateways.

Mr. Raghavan has received multiple U.S. patents for his wireless MVNO architecture and has extensive experience in bulding telecommunication platforms and applications.

Mr. Raghavan has worked as head of system's integration for Three (UK), leading the system's integration team focused on rolling out a 3G Wireless offering in the UK and supporting 3G platforms in 7 other countries.

Prior experience includes; co-founder, Director of profession services of start-up company eSelf, Inc.

As a research scientist at SRI International, Mr. Raghavan designed and developed Intrusion detection algorithms and video steam algorithms.
