    HISTORY: BRAZILSITE was probably Brazil's first aspiring commercial Internet provider, at a time when the telecommunications sector in the country was still a monopoly of the state. I founded this company with 2 University colleagues and financial backing from an Angel investor. After 1 year as a part-time member of the technical staff, I left to pursue a career opportunity in Switzerland. The company eventually folded late in 1996, having not had the funds to meet its financial commitments long enough to benefit from the market liberalization. In essence, we were too early.
