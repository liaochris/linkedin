Jason Ding’s Summary

At Cisco Systems (2005- ): Sr. Manager of Application Oriented Networking (AON) Business Unit. Currently managing AON Systems Architecture & Performance (ASAP) team with multiple dimensions of Team Mission: Drive AON product direction, systems architecture, product development, marketing message, and customer solutions from performance perspective.

At Ariba, Inc. (2000-2005): Sr. Manager of Ariba's engineering performance teams (in both US and India), responsible for performance and scalability of all Ariba enterprise software products. Played pivot role in architecture evaluation, product testing, pre/post-sales capacity planning, deployment performance monitoring and troubleshooting of 15+ product releases. Result oriented as well as good team player with HW/SW vendors, hosting partners, development, marketing, sales, professional services and TechSupport groups.

At Intel Corp. (1994-2000): Lead engineer on a number of PentiumPro, Pentium II/III/IV/Xeon, Itanium I/II systems design, performance evaluation, and enterprise software performance tuning projects on Intel platform. Also led multi-team effort in setting up Intel Application Solution Center in Shanghai China by delivering basic training, field project training and mentoring engineers. Coordinated strategic planning with ASC HQ and Shanghai site management and made Intel Shanghai ASC one of the most successful ASC centers in APAC region.

Jason Ding’s Specialties:

Performance of software and hardware systems; leadership in testing, troubleshooting, and tuning performance at all levels; able to dive deep technically and articulate issues with developers, customers, solution/support/marketing/sales engineers, architects/CTO, HW/SW/hosting partners and executives.
