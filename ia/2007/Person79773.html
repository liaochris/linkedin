Simona Frignani’s Summary

I can boast over ten years of experience in the Marketing and Communications sector, during which I have worked in several areas of the corporate communications profession, operating both at the national and international level.

I am currently the Marketing Manager in Eco-Way, a venture in the environmental consultancy sector, where I have been entrusted with the construction of the marketing department. I oversee commercial planning, the marketing structure, network-building and launch of the Eco-WAY brand and the trademark connected to it: No Greenhouse Effect. I manage a team of 2 people.

Amongst my past experiences I wish to highlight the fact that in RTT/InOne Italia (now part of the Deloitte Group) I built up the marketing department from practically nothing. I selected internal and external resources, formed a press office, looked after the re-branding and press launch of the company during its transformation from RTT Consulting to the InOne Italia Group, and then from the InOne Italia Group to Deloitte & Touche Technology Solutions.

I am an enthusiastic person, with a propensity for change, who is results-oriented, with entrepreneurial spirit and a customer-care focus, and who has an excellent knowledge of the English, French and Spanish languages.
