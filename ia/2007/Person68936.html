Philip Bourchier O'Ferrall’s Summary

VP DM MTVNE

Vice President DM, MTV Networks UK & Ireland. MTV1, MTV2, MTVBase, MTVHits, MTVDance, VH1, Flux, Paramount Comedy, Nickelodeon.



Previously, Exec Director of Twofour Convergent & Mobile TV, Philip headed the division which understands the convergent market place, building communities around content and the production of content specifically for this market. He brings together traditional broadcasting with the emerging technologies and is forging strong relationships with all the key businesses.

Philip had a focus on exploiting media assets and the commissioning of convergent productions and brand funded programming

Previously, Vice President at Player One a Fleming Media Company.
Responsible for the distribution of mobile media assets from many leading brands including young James Bond, Mike Tyson, Manchester United, Davis Cup etc.

Previously MD of Nokia Ventures Oplayo - one of the leading mobile video companies where Philip took Oplayo to 'top 5' in mobile video. Philip also launched the 'worlds first' mobile video portal 'mobile media club' and launched the 'worlds first' mobile music video in partnership with Sony/BMG.

Prior to joining Oplayo, Philip served as GM of Chyron Digital Media Services one of the leading Enhanced TV and Rich Media agencies based in London and New York.

Philip is a broadcast industry veteran. Previously, he has headed a sales team closing multi-million dollar contracts including the likes of BBC, NBC, CNN, Pearson, BskyB, Tvi, Channel4, Channel5, Disney, Discovery and William Hill.

Philip is an active member of BAFTA, RTS & IOD

Philip is an advisory board member of Flix Ltd a mobile video entertainment provider to mobile networks.

Also, Snax Ltd a mobile content rights and IP business.
