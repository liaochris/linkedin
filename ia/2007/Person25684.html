Eben Thurston’s Summary

Online media professional, with a MBA in marketing, and a focus on Audience Development including product development, strategic partnerships, web analytics and SEO. Past experience includes web development, online direct marketing and financial securities. Interested in all aspects of online business, including media, analytics, search, e-commerce, content syndication, advertising, blogging, social media, "web 2.0" and new technology at any size firm from start-ups to the Fortune 500.

Eben Thurston’s Specialties:

Audience development, product development, business development, strategic partnerships, social media optimization, search engine optimization (SEO), web standards development, web analytics, online marketing, RSS optimization
