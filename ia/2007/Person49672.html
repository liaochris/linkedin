Laetitia Lienart’s Summary

As project manager and writer, I have worked with the United Nations since 1998, which allowed me to gain experience in different sectors (humanitarian aid, plant protection, food aid and food security).

My main objective is to keep working for international development and cooperation, preferably with the public sector but I am also very motivated by having an experience with the private one.

I am interested in long, medium and short-term missions, including duty travel if necessary and home-based tasks such as research, writing and translation.

Note about 1/ languages: French (mother tongue), English and Italian (fluent), Spanish and Chinese (intermediate level under improvement through private lessons);
2/ software: good knowledge of Word, Excel, Internet & Power Point; basic usage of Access
