Bill Macaitis’s Summary

I have over 12 years of executive level marketing experience and have a passion for online marketing. I am an expert at exponentially growing traffic through SEO, SEM, social networking and other viral drivers.

I have worked for a variety of high profile online sites including MySpace, FoxSports, IGN, American Idol, AskMen, RottenTomatoes, GameSpy, ClubPhoto and Case's Ladder. I have a strong history of executing on marketing strategies that drive traffic, revenue and profits.

Every company I have worked for has seen incredible traffic growth and all moved to profitability. I specialize in B2C sites and am comfortable with a variety of monetization models including advertising (display, e-mail, paid search), subscription and e-commerce.

My specialty is search engine optimization (SEO) and search engine marketing (SEM). I have successfully performed SEO on over 50 sites all with phenomenal traffic growth. I have also managed multi million dollar SEM campaigns on Google, Yahoo, MSN and ASK using advanced campaign optimization, bid management and end to end conversion tracking for over 2MM keywords.
