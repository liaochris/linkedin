Larry L Najera Jr’s Summary

Larry Najera, Jr. is a multimedia communications designer, technology consultant, and web strategist based in Middle Georgia. He has won over 85 awards since 1988 for his design work in the field of broadcast video, print, signage, interactive and web design.

Larry L Najera Jr’s Specialties:

Larry is involved in all aspects of development of corporate web-sites, e-shops or shopping carts, web databases, tv commercials, outdoor signage and interactive CDs and DVDs. He participates in all stages of various projects - from communication with the client and definition of the design specifications to teamwork with everyone engaged in the project development and finishing.
