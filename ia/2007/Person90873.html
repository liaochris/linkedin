  • Digital Security Risk Manager (Interim)

    BP

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Oil & Energy industry)

    January 2006 — October 2006 (10 months)

    Brian was assigned under contract to BP as the Interim Digital Security Risk Manager for the Gas Power Renewables Supply & Trading (GRS&T) Sector. The main responsibilities of this position included:
    • Understanding, tracking and managing the digital security threats and risks that affected GRS&T
    • Acting as the Digital Security sign-off for projects within GRS&T.
    • Ensuring that requirements from GRS&T were fed into the Group Digital Security Agenda.
    • Promoting the awareness of Digital Security within the sector. Including the production and delivery of training packages.
    • Project management of a password strengthening initiative.
    • Acting as the digital security focal point for Sarbanes-Oxley assertions within the sector.

  • Global Information Security & Privacy Officer

    Merrill Lynch

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Investment Banking industry)

    April 2000 — December 2005 (5 years 9 months)

    Global Information Security & Privacy Officer for Merrill Lynch Investment Managers, responsible for the provision of advice for security related issues that arise during the lifecycle of projects across the globe. Brian's duties included:
    • Consultation and sign-off of all IT projects to ensure that security was not degraded.
    • Security threat and risk evaluations of systems and ensuring that any recommendations were implemented.
    • Design and implementation of security policies and standards in alignment with industry best practices and standards.
    • Guidance to the Business Unit Aligned IT Departments including the granting of dispensations where requests did not conform to policy.
    • Conducting information security awareness presentations and road-shows.
    • Compliance with US, UK, European and international legislation and financial regulatory requirements. Examples include Sarbanes-Oxley, HIPPA, GLBA, UK DPA 1998, 95/46/EC, FSA, BASEL II

  • Information Security Officer
