Joey Janisheck’s Summary

I have over 11 years experience in the web design and development field, spending over 5 years as the corporate webmaster and manager of a 12 person team that handled all of the web properties for a $5.5 billion company (NTT/Verio). I was charged with managing a group of developers, outlining technical strategies, and implementing plans to build applications for our internal and external clients. I have over 4 years of solid project management experience using several different project management and development methodologies (PMM and XP). I also excel at being able to take requirements from business stakeholders, translate them into technical specifications that a team of web designers and developers then take and turn into a working application.

While recruiting, hiring, assembling and managing this group of all-stars, I also had the pleasure of designing internal and external interfaces for applications that are still in use today. I prefer to be a player/coach and my many years of graphic design experience help me create workable layouts that developers can understand and implement. With the power of CSS-based design and modular development methodologies, I have been able to keep the developers coding and be able to make product feature changes throughout the dev process. Also with a solid separation of design and dev, the marketing department is able to continue to tweak GUI elements in the product while not affecting the progress by the coders.

I take pride in being able to work and be productive with both IT / Development and Marketing departments to get the job done. I have years of experience in both organizations.

I'm self-motivated and focused on getting the job done.
