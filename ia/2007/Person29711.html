Steve Pickering 1775+ (s.p.linkedin@gmail.com)’s Summary

Many professional people believe that because they have had huge financial success in their own field, they can quickly master trading. Unfortunately, it doesn't work that way. 90% fail. It takes years to become who you are today, so why do you think that trading can be learned in a few days?

I understand this, and therefore provide serious training in the area of foreign exchange (forex) training to professional people who want to do this right and supplement their current income or start a whole new career with the financial independence that successful trading brings. Success is achieved by learning the right methods, ditching all the hype that plagues this industry and becoming consistently profitable with realistic goals and an understanding of risk and probabilities.

I am ready to be your partner in doing this.

I have been involved in financial markets, especially the forex markets since 1971.

That experience has been as broker, trader and trainer. I have learned from thousands of traders, plus my own extensive experience and taught hundreds of traders. I am an expert in my field.

I specialise in training & mentoring traders: I lead my clients to financial success and independence. I develop in them a winning attitude and create maneagable goals.

I am available for one-on-one tuition/mentoring for those who wish to become independent forex (foreign exchange) traders. I also offer simulated trading courses and seminars and consultancy.

I also teach institutional traders at banks, brokerages CTA s and fund managers.

I also supply forex simulation training software and custom courses based on the trading simulation. I have recently provided the simulation and instruction for a Euromoney Financial Training course on foreign exchange markets.
