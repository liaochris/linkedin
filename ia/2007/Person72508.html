Richik Sarkar’s Summary

I have a wide ranging practice that focuses on complex litigation in the areas of commercial, product liability and public law. Over the years I have developed a recognized expertise in the areas of business torts, contracts, corporate governance, securities and finance, Uniform Commerical Code, real estate, real property taxation, and education law. I also represent financial institutions and their officers in class actions and other compliance-related litigation regarding: predatory lending, Bank Secrecy Act, anti-money laundering program compliance, truth-in-lending, Fair Debt Collection Practices Act, and other securities regulations.

You can learn more about my firm, Ulmer & Berne LLP, by going to our website (www.ulmer.com). There you will see that we have been named one of the Fortune 500 Go-To Law Firms. In addition we are part of a select group of firms in the U.S. named to BTI Consulting Group’s Client Service A Team for delivering the best legal value for the dollar. If you would like to learn more about my practice you can go directly to my practice profile by using http://www.ulmer.com/UB/Attorneys/sarkar_richik.htm.

Richik Sarkar’s Specialties:

Business litigation and public law practice focusing on representation in complex and class action litigation, administrative proceedings, and alternative dispute resolution for commercial, product liability, and public law matters, including the areas of: business torts, contracts, corporate governance, securities and finance, real estate, product liability, telecommunications, and school law.
