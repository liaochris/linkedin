Rohit Bafna’s Summary

An insightful and commercial director with experience of sales, project management, brand marketing and resource planning. A strategic thinker with proven business development, management, and negotiation skills. Respected for his integrity, leadership and focus on results. A consummate professional, with key skills in developing relationships at all levels. Delivering significant benfits to companies that are seeking material testing and quality assurance services. As a Director at TCR, Rohit is personally involved in day to day operations.

Founded in 1973, TCR Engineering Services (TCR) is India's most reputed and established, NABL and ISO 17025 accredited independent Material Testing Laboratory and Quality Assurance Company.

The services TCR provides include Mechanical Testing, Chemical Analysis, Positive Material Identification (PMI), Non Destructive Testing (NDT), Metallography, RoHS Compliance Testing, Corrosion Testing (including HIC/SSC), Failure Analysis, Third Party Inspection, Metallurgical Product Evaluation, Heat Treatment, Manpower Deployment, Training, Engineering Research and Consultancy.

TCR Engineering Services undertakes material testing as per international standards and specifications as defined by ASTM, NACE, DIN, AWS, API, BIS, IS and others. Testing can also be done as per client-supplied specifications.

TCR´s objective is to provide a quality service at a reasonable price. Till date, TCR has worked with more than 2000+ quality driven clients (from India and rest of the world) in Aerospace, Automotive, Oil Refineries, Petrochemical plants, Chemical Processing, Defense, Electronics, Nuclear Power and Capital Goods manufacturing industries to determine material properties, improve product's performance, assist to develop new and better products or materials, evaluate remaining life of an industrial equipment, determine why a component´s performance expectations were not met, or to identify why a product may have failed.
