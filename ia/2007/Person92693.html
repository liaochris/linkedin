May 1998 – Present (8 years 9 months)

Howdy!

Zeke's Gallery is a fiercely independent art gallery here in Montreal. Besides the visual arts I also program musical events, literary events, and other less intimidating cultural things in order to get people in the door. All of the events (including the visual arts) are recorded, and I have recently started to videotape them as well.

The mandate of the gallery is 'First Solo Show.' Part and parcel with it is my desire to completely change the contemporary art world. Back in the 1950's Vincent Price was the pitchman in Sears' catalogs for contemporary art. In the 1960's Andy Warhol was a spokesman for Braniff airlines. Or more bluntly, back then the art world was not about exclusivity, nor was it exclusionary. Zeke's wants to get back there.
