Marie Halkjær’s Summary

Through my company Book Invention I work as a consultant for publishing companies. I provide ideas for new books and projects, for companies who do not have the ressources to free an employee to follow trends and do long-term planning.
Also I am just about to launch my own publishing company, which will focus on lifestyle books. See more on www.bookinvention.dk.
