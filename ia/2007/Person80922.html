Steve Cerilli’s Summary

Visit www.sound-fx-design.com for more information on current services offered.

Freelance post production audio engineer / sound designer. Responsible for voice over recording, voice actor casting, ISDN recording, sound effects creation, audio mix to picture, audio sweetening and sound design


Sound designer and independent producer for three sound effects collections published worldwide. Involved in all aspects of sound effects creation, editing, naming conventions, data base development and composing of production music. Created "Radioactive" sound effects collection released by Sound-Ideas of Canada

Station manager, content programmer, traffic manager, creative director and co-founder of broadcast station WXHQ 105.9 LPFM- Radio Newport. Responsible for all content development including audio production, station ids, artist intros, underwriting statements, underwriting sales, public service announcements, FCC filings, performance licensing and music selection.

Voice over artist – The voice of Radio Newport, have recorded numerous radio spots and corporate instructional videos.

Professional keyboardist – performing regularly in “The Ravers” , a seven piece reggae band and in “The Swamp Angels of Marlborough” a instrumental funky jazz trio.

Steve Cerilli’s Specialties:

All aspects of audio production and sound development. Custom sound design and sound effects creation for the gaming industry, film and broadcast.
