Bruce Judson’s Experience

  • Founder

    Search Free Apps (www.SearchFreeApps.com)

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    September 2007 — Present (2 months)

    Search Free Apps (www.SearchFreeApps.com) is an innovative vertical search engine for small businesses, nonprofits, home offices, freelancers, and entrepreneurs. The engine searches over 500 hand-picked services that provide high quality free products and services across the range of Internet activities. The service provides an easy way to instantly find sophisticated free services, Users are, in general, surprised at the high quality of the services included in Search Free Apps, and the range of sophisticated services that can be found in the engine.

  • Founder & Editor

    Ventures Without Capital.com

    (Online Media industry)

    January 2007 — Present (10 months)

    Ventures Without Capital is a new blog (http://www.VenturesWithoutCapital.com) that looks to identify and recommend the best, low-cost services for entrepreneurs, start-ups and small groups that leverage their capabilities, automate their businesses and allow them to focus on what they do best. This new blog is an extension of the ideas incorporated in my book "Go It Alone" (HarperBusiness; Nov. 2004) which discussed how new Internet services were increasingly making it possible for individuals and small groups to create big businesses without employees or cash.
