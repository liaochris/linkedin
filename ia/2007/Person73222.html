Robert Torres’s Summary

Robert David Torres is a recognized and award-winning creative leader with over 14 years of experience in e-commerce, marketing, and graphic design. He offers a rare combination of aesthetic pedigree, internet expertise and strategic business thinking, with an emphasis on direct marketing.

As owner of Directly Creative LLC, Mr. Torres helps his clients in all aspects of online marketing creative and e-commerce strategies to achieve bottom line success. A partial list of success includes having built a cohesive corporate identity and family of web sites for Balboa Travel and achieved higher conversions and qualified lead generation for companies like RealAge and Find Your Customers.

As Web Producer for Intuit's ProTax division, Mr. Torres ensures the creative, technical, usability and experience success for Intuit's professional software web sites, www.proseries.com and www.lacertesoftware.com. Leveraging his wide array of e-commerce experience, he was brought on to lead various new initiatives for the company and make the web the preferred channel for their customers.

Recently, Mr. Torres held the position of Creative Director for Anonymizer Inc. He was responsible for web site marketing, content, overall creative direction, web site conversion metrics, corporate identity/branding, and product marketing/positioning. He increased web site conversion by nearly 50% in key areas after joining the company utilizing proven direct marketing, merchandising and creative solutions and increased web site average order value 30% with new creative and strategic cross/up-selling initiatives.

Prior to joining Anonymizer Mr. Torres was at Provide Commerce (Proflowers) and was the creative force behind the Proflowers web site for nearly four years. He was responsible for the creative direction and execution of the corporate identity and brand in all media, with a focus on increasing web site conversion and revenue across all channels.
