Marc Williams’s Summary

Bio:

Business Development Strategist

Marc has 20+ years of experience of executive management in technology marketing, direct marketing, merchandising, &e-commerce. As founder of 6 Degrees, was a part of a corporate turnaround w/ Audible. Prior to 6 degrees he raised the funding for Digital Tempest, managed the development of the company identity & marketing plan. He co-founded Virtual Spin & led it’s acquisition. He held the GM position at Real Networks where he drove their e-commerce efforts. Prior to Real Networks, he was VP of Merchandising, Advertising, Strategic Marketing & Business Development at Multiple Zones Intl. & engineered a company turn around, leading to a successful IPO. Earlier in his career, he held executive positions at both Micro Warehouse & CompuServe.

Marc Williams’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur in E-commerce and catalog development; specializing in partner marketing, merchandising, advertising and sales.
