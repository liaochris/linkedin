Juergen Harter’s Summary

Experienced Chemistry Analyst, Information manager, and Project Manager in a variety of pharmaceutical knowledge management and business workflow applications. Current focus on automatic building, creation and visualisation of knowledge maps, ontologies and taxonomies. Particularly interested in knowledge modelling, knowledge capture and semantic web technologies, and any techniques and methods to handle and make sense of the ever growing "information mountain". Always interested in challenging IT integration projects and knowledge-driven business development opportunities, particularly in the pharmaceutical and biotech industries.
