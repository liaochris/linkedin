Brian O'Neill’s Summary

I am a skilled user-experience specialist and web designer with a strong technical and business background. For the last several years, I have been designing interfaces and experiences at financial services companies including Fidelity, ETrade, BrownCo, Quote.com. Most recently, I directed the design group for Eons.com, a new "boomer-generation" startup led by Monster.com's Jeff Taylor . I'm also a strong advocate for "web standards," designing with XHTML, CSS and accessibility-awareness.

My goals:
* Lead the design of simple and attractive web interfaces that facilitate a user's experience with a website.
* Help businesses achieve their objectives online by helping them to define their goals clearly.
* Apply user-centered design (UCD) and Web Standards methodologies to my work.
