Lawrence Sinclair’s Summary

As CEO, I have built offshore software development companies in India and Vietnam, and have conceived, designed, and managed the development and strategy for innovative Internet applications.

As a consultant, I have worked with advanced business analytics groups to create cutting edge customer data mining, statistical forecasting, analysis and tracking systems with a focus on profitability and marketing. Clients have included Morgan Stanley, Bank of America, Wells Fargo Bank, Intuit, Clorox, and eToys.

Lawrence Sinclair’s Specialties:

(1) Internet application development: Ruby-on-Rails and Extreme Programming, Facebook and Social Network systems.

(2) Sales performance analysis. Wal-Mart & Target POS/scanner data processing, analysis and forecasting. Price, marketing and merchandising effectiveness analysis. Online customer analysis. Clickstream web log analysis. Data mining: neural networks, decision trees, regressions. Profitability modeling. Datawarehousing. Tools: SAS, DB2, Oracle, Teradata.
