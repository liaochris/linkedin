Academic & Professional Summary
-------------------------------------------------

May 2007 - present : Quality test engineering for Google UK via Kelly Services (UK) Ltd on a temporary contract basis. I live in Oxford, England.

Sep. 2004 - Aug. 2006 : Master of Science degree in Logic & Theoretical Computer Science at the Universiteit van Amsterdam, The Netherlands. Will graduate in June 2007. Master's thesis concerned new group-theoretic methods for studying the algebraic complexity of matrix multiplication, as measured by the exponent of matrix multiplication. Thesis supervised by Prof. Tom Koornwinder of the KdV Institute for Mathematics, and Prof. Peter van Emde Boas of the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC), and will be published in June 2007 under the ILLC Master of Logic Master thesis series.

Mar. 1996 - Dec. 2000 : informatics & computer science at the University of Wollongong, Australia, and graduated with Honours in July, 2001. Honours thesis was an econometric/statistical study of the relationship between IT investment and service sector productivity in the Australian economy between 1993 and 1999. It was supervised by Dr. Michael Lawrence Slater, formerly of the University of Melbourne and the University of Wollongong.

Dec. 1997 and Dec. 1998 : 3 month internships at Telpacific Co. and Railcorp Co., respectively, in Sydney, Australia.

Feb. 2000 - May 2000 : Analyst/Programmer with Australian Mutual Provident (AMP) Co. in Sydney, while finishing my Honours thesis.

Jun. 2000 - Jan. 2001 : Java software engineer for @WWW Digital Co. in Sydney.

Jul. 2001 - Feb. 2004 : Independent financial trader, Sydney. Electronic trading of stocks, derivatives and foreign exchange, using the trading platforms of Comsec in Sydney, and FXCM in London, and Thomson Financial's IFR ForexWatch service.
