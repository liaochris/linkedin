Thomas Hopper’s Summary

Extensive experience with invention and innovation of new technologies and products, particularly in Nickel Metal Hydride and Alkaline Fuel Cells. Strong interest in "end-to-end" innovation, from earliest technology concept through to production of manufactured product, and sustainable solutions to social problems.

A systems thinker who prefers to develop systemic solutions to technological, business and social problems. Dedicated to data-based decision making (e.g. evidenced-based management) using the best available analytical tools.
