Ivan Pellegrin’s Summary

Ivan has dedicated his career to translate the opportunities and constraints of information technology into relevant and cost-effective solutions.

His main areas of expertise include Data Management, Data Quality, the Value of Data, Business Intelligence, Data Warehousing, Master Data Management and Data Hubs.

After working as an independent consultant from 1987 until 2004, he joined LogicaCMG as their Principal Consultant.

Ivan conveys a degree of confidence and charisma. He often speaks at conferences and seminars throughout Europe and North America and he has been featured on the BBC and RAI. For more information about his recent and forthcoming seminars and presentations please visit http://www.ivanpellegrin.net.

Ivan liaises regularly with a wide international network at LogicaCMG, Oracle, IBM, Telelogic, Dun & Bradstreet and other companies and organisations, as well as independent authors, speakers and technologists.

By living in Italy, the United States, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands and finally Belgium, Ivan has developed a degree of savoir-faire when communicating with people coming from a different background. He is bi-lingual in English and Italian and displays a set of skills in five more languages.

Capable of an infectious enthusiasm, Ivan applies Optimal Thinking and Metta Bhavana to all aspects of professional and private life.
