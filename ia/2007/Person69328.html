Prakash Nambiar’s Summary

Life is a dream and I am living it. (Not without the intermittent nightmares though!) In the world of reality, a touch of fantasy and belief in miracles can take you out of your ordinary life. I live in a world that creates fantasies for people to believe in. To make children and the children in everyone, believe, that miracles can happen. Animation is the medium that i use as my professional endeavor, but also extend it to other mediums like live action films and books, both for children and grown-ups in my free time. I strive on challenges and love being in the midst of madness, laughter and critical deadlines!

After my architectural education, i found that my creativity in that field was limited by Municipal norms/ Bye-laws/ Government officials and was constantly surrounded by carpenters, surveyors, contractors, plumbers etc. etc. With no offence to these professions, I missed the positively charged atmosphere of my college with charged creative individuals. Animation is the industry i bumped into and have been there ever since.

I joined Purple Image where i was exposed to different technologies in the media and was a superviser for web interactives and also doubled up as a graphic designer and a programmer.

Later,in 2001, I founded Kathaa Animations out of my house and later operated out of my father's workshop doing multimedia graphics and animation. In 2004, we bagged the project of HUM TUM and successfully executed it, bagging us the Best Specal Effects for the film.

In late 2006, we joined hands with Animation Dimensions to develop Indian and International Intellectual properties, and currently we are working on a couple of them which shall hit the markets and the screens very soon!

My goal? Ten years down the line, I would like to have bought happiness into peoples lives through the collabrative work that i do with my fantastically charged and motivated team, and then move on to a newer challenge - Agriculture. Yes.... you read it right!!!
