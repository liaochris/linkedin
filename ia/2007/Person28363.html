About Wimax EU Ltd.
Wimax EU Ltd. is rolling out a pan-European WISP (Wireless Internet Service Provider) and hotspot network. The purpose of this network will be to provide wireless broadband access to government, business & private users. Our network will consist of a combination of WiFi and Wimax making it futureproof while at the same time availing of the millions of WiFi client devices already in use. We are rolling our WISP networks in rural areas as well as urban areas and intend to install an extensive network of wireless hotspots in areas with a high visitor footfall. Think of large shoppingmalls, sports stadiums, conference centers, leisure facilities etc. We will also be building large “hotzones” that will cover city centers and similar areas. Another area of interest for us is so-called “municipal networks”. These are city wide wireless networks owned by the local government but built and operated by a WISP. These types of networks have already become a major development & investment area in the US and all indicators show that something similar will happen in Europe. Our network topography combined with our authentication methods makes us a prime candidate for these types of networks.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Wireless industry)

    October 2005 — Present (2 years)

    Wimax EU intends to establish a pan-European WISP network. We've identified the lack of affordable high quality broadband access in the majority of European countries and intend to fill this gap.
    The rollout of broadband across most countries is hampered by the lack of necessary infrastructure, the sometimes bad condition of the existing infrastructure or the monopolization of the infrastructure by a Telecoms co. with no real interest in local loop unbundling.
    Wimax EU will bypass these infrastructural problems by implementing a wireless network for the so-called “last mile”. We will use wireless point-to-point connections to bring broadband connectivity to areas where it is currently unavailable. In these areas we will establish nodes from where we can provide broadband access to customers in the area.
