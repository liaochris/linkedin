Alistair Lamb’s Summary

Extensive marketing experience working for a number of major IT services and software companies in a range of roles from product manager through channel manager to marketing director.

Marketing is all about achieving measurable results designed to support an organization's business goals and reputation. Experienced team player and manager who always gets the best out of people (whether direct reports or other), by using effective coaching skills, while not losing sight of the ultimate end goal.

Alistair Lamb’s Specialties:

Skilled in the whole marketing mix: product and service definition; pricing and sales goal setting; channel selection and management; product and service promotion and business development.
