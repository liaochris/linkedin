Donald Tiffin’s Summary

My goals are to Serve Customers by Providing Solutions to Issues using Analytical and Microscopy Techniques

I have Extensive experience in Establishing and Managing Process Analytical Laboratories in support of the Fabs of Major Semiconductor Companies; experience in all major analytical techniques, strong management, technical and people skills. Understanding and experience with all major analytical and microscopy techniques. Experience in writing and development of business plans for small and larger “spin-off” companies. Marketing experience and experience starting-up and running small technical companies. Experience with budgets, capital and expense and financial management of small companies and departments in large corporations. Strong experience in solving people and team issues with departments. Direct experience in operations of analytical instrumentation. Strong analytical abilities and problem solving skills. Experience working to root cause in Semiconductor Fab Issues and heading Teams. Experience maintaining and servicing instruments. Strong vacuum experience. Strong experience in training laboratory engineers and technicians in analytical and microscopy techniques and their application to the solution of semiconductor materials, device and process issues. Hold 11 patents in analytical and semiconductor areas.
