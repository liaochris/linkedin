Enrico Noseda’s Summary

Enrico has got an extensive experience in strategy, marketing, and business development in the Digital Media sector (Telecom, Media and Internet markets).

Enrico Joined Skype at the beginning of 2006 with the role of Country Manager, with the objective of raising awareness, develop and manage customer base and revenues in the local markets (Italy and Turkey).

He moved to London at the beginning of 2007 to become Head of Business Development Europe coordinating and managing Skype partners in Europe.

Before Skype, with the role of Sales and Marketing Director, Enrico contributed to the start-up of Parla.it, a VoIP venture of France Telecom in Italy.

Prior to that, at the beginning of the year 2000 Enrico was a top manager at NTS, an Italian Start-Up (listed at Milan stock exchange) focussed on contents and services for Interactive Tv and Broadband Internet.

He started his career in 1993 covering various marketing roles in top FMCG multinational companies (Henkel and Chicco Artsana).
