Tom Haak’s Summary

Tom Haak studied psychology at the State University of Groningen in The Netherlands. He graduated (masters degree) in 1981. After his studies Tom was employed by Philips Electronics and worked in various national and international personnel and management development positions in two divisions: Defence and Control Systems and Consumer Electronics. In 1994 he started to work for KPMG in The Netherlands as their human resource development manager. He was involved in the development and implementation of competency based HRM. He also played an important role in the introduction of a new performance management system using the balanced business and the balanced individual scorecard. He was also involved in the establishment of a new consultancy group of KPMG, focusing at helping companies to develop and improve their management development architecture. In 1998 Tom Haak was appointed Director Human Resources & Communication of Aon Risk Services International. In this role he supported the Executive Board in relation to senior executive employment and remuneration, executive career development, training, knowledge management and communication. His area of responsibility was Europe, Asia/Pacific and Africa/Middle East. Tom led a small team of professionals (based in Rotterdam) in the areas of compensation & benefits, training & development, communication and knowledge management. In 2005 Tom was appointed in a new global position in Aon Corporation. Assignment: create a global Talent Management organisation that drives the improvement of talent management in Aon. Per 1 October 2006 Tom was appointed Corporate Director Human Resources at ARCADIS. Tom is married, has three children (16, 14 and 11 years old) and lives in Zeist in the middle of The Netherlands.

Tom Haak’s Specialties:

Recruitment, Executive development, management development, talent management, International HR strategies, knowledge management, internal communication, executive compensation, performance management
