Sean Mulholland’s Summary

Before joining JWT, I worked in online marketing at Trymedia Systems, a PC game security startup. At Trymedia I developed innovative ways to help market the games sold by Trymedia, including developing a comprehensive search marketing strategy for Trygames.com and its affiliate partners, managing file seeding into all major P2P networks at the time, as well as creating one of the first profitable file distribution programs on the BitTorrent peer-to-peer network.

From Trymedia I moved into search marketing full time with JWT SC, working with blue-chip clients in almost every industry represented by the agency. During this time I continued to look forward and develop extensive knowledge of new media trends such as blogging, social networking, social media, and anything else Web 2.0, which took me into emerging media full time in 2007.

When not in the office, I continue my obsession with new media at home by managing a popular urban streetwear site with my fiance (Grooveeffect.com). When I don't have a computer screen in front of me, you can find me dodging traffic on the streets of San Francisco while atop my trusty road bike.
