Paul James Brennan’s Summary

I am seeking business partners and strategic projects to help make Helsinki more fun for everyone, ie directly affect the 1 million or so inhabitants. We will work with many types of organisations & projects from government, tourism, media coverage, sponsors, alchohol importers, health organisations, sports related business and now your starting to see the tip of the iceberg...

The company I lead, Unfair Advantage, is primarily a new media marketing company. We create all types of media and offer new channels to get the message across more effectively, more economically and with less competition.

The company's secondary objective is to gather funds to implement our plans to encourage people to play with each other through sport and fun activities to chase a similar dream as the objective of the Olympic Games. Our vision is to make fun easier to reach than the remote control!

Prior to my career change I built up ten years experience in various roles with the marketing and sales of complex products to difficult markets, primarily to the largest financial services institutions, globally - I have worked on location with projects in Australia, South Africa, Saudi Arabia most of Western Europe and the USA.

My greatest achievement in that time was the pioneering of one of Europe’s biggest (8 figures) and unusual product deals with British Petroleum as relationship manager .

Pioneering to make a significant contribution to improve the world we live in by carefully mixing exchange and gift ecomony paradigms is the long term objective of my company.
