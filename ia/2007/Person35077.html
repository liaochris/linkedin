Matthew Hilchie-Pye’s Summary

With over ten years of professional consulting experience, Mr. Hilchie-Pye has demonstrated leadership in Object Oriented Analysis and Design, while maintaining an in-depth understanding in systems architecture. Mr. Hilchie-Pye has applied his expertise in the development of large 3-tier systems, shrink-wrap software and real-time systems. Mr. Hilchie-Pye's exposure to aerospace, financial services, healthcare and online retail markets demonstrates his ability to quickly adapt to a variety of technical environments, each with unique challenges.

By remaining unbiased toward specific technologies, Mr. Hilchie-Pye focuses on the explicit needs of his clients. His proficiency on Windows and Unix-based platforms, combined with his experience in most main-stream programming languages, allows Mr. Hilchie-Pye to architect a solution from a large pool of technologies.

Mr. Hilchie-Pye has performed as a Team Leader and Systems Analyst on many software engineering projects. He also has first-hand exposure to Tier-1, Tier-2 and Tier-3 levels of software and systems support, giving him a broad consideration on the post-deployment support challenges.

Finally, Mr. Hilchie-Pye is a Canadian citizen born in the province of Nova Scotia. He does not have any imposed travel restrictions, and welcomes the opportunity to travel abroad to be onsite with his clients.
