Marc LeBrun’s Summary

I am a concrete, results-oriented technology leader with a passion for all aspects of the design, development and deployment of software products, components and web services for enterprises through end users

I’ve managed and mentored individuals as well as directed teams with 30+ members and larger organizations (including distributed & international development). I like the challenge of working with the most competent and creative “wild talents” and industry professionals.

I enjoy interacting with customers, marketing and executives to capture requirements and deliver them as winning solutions. I plan and contribute to strategic business objectives, and have successfully worked in both start-ups and Fortune 500 environments.

I value and respond to what’s special and unique in each person, organization and culture. I seek challenges where I can apply my deep experience and increase my scope, contribution and expand my personal envelope.

While I am effective in leading productive teams, I’ve also written many thousands of lines of reliable shipping production Java and C++ using modern IDEs such as Eclipse and Visual Studio. I am also comfortable in more exotic languages such as Lisp, assembly and microcode, as well as XML and declarative tools.

My personal experience also includes developing high-performance combined hardware and software systems (chips, boards, racks and complete studio installations) as well as a console video game. I’m effective in many application areas, from Enterprise Business Process Management through 3D Graphics, Optimization, Artificial Intelligence, etc. I apply Object-Oriented, Model-Based and Domain-Driven Design Patterns and techniques. I research and create new and effective algorithms and technologies.

The bottom-line is that the true measure of leadership is the value of the results it delivers—I am ultimately motivated by that challenge.
