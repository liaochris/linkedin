Kristina Ross’s Summary

I specialize in UE/UI design for desktop software, PocketPC, and the web. I have broad experience collaboratively designing software for a wide variety of complex business processes, consumer-facing applications, and specialty, expert systems.

I am especially interested in opportunities to design outstanding software for:

* Connecting people with information and with each other

* Social networking, collaboration

* Research applications, collaborative tools, learning tools or data visualization for the earth sciences, or other social, educational or environmental causes

Kristina Ross’s Specialties:

Interface and interaction design, user-focused design processes, and user research. I bring over a decade of experience and fluent mastery of tools and principles for creating:

* Information architecture, wire frame schematics, flow diagrams

* User task and work flow analysis, user profiles, ethnographic research

* User-focused design process development, team-building

* Graphic design/illustration for icons, program executables

* Prototyping and site development using DHTML
