Joe Ganley’s Summary

Software architect, developer, and project manager with over a decade-long track record of successfully delivered software. I am an expert C++ programmer, with experience in many other languages and technologies as well.

All of my professional experience, plus my doctoral work, is in software for electronic design automation (i.e. the software that chip designers use to do their work). Specifically, my specialty is physical design, which is the conversion of an electronic design from a symbolic form such as a schematic to a physical layout on a chip. In addition to the usual challenges in applications design, this area is replete with problems that are computationally difficult or that are challenging to translate into an intuitive user interface.
