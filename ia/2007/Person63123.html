Neil Neveras’s Summary

I'm a human capital manager specializing in organizational change and transformation.

OK, so what the HECK does that mean?

Basically, organizations are changing all the time -- structurally, technologically, launching new products/services, etc. -- sometimes because they want to, often because they have to. But we often forget that organizations are a collection of PEOPLE. If the people don't sign on to the change, the change will fail.

My job is to help organizations get through this by helping them to answer some pretty basic questions, like:

Who benefits from the change (and who "loses out")?
How will we most effectively communicate the change?
How will we measure the effectiveness of the change?

It seems like intuitive stuff, but it's actually pretty hard to do. Fortunately, we can apply some cool tools borrowed from behavioral and social sciences that can help. And, in typical Deloitte fashion, we aim to link any change management results to shareholder value.
