    Siemens Medical

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; Computer Software industry)

    June 2004 — January 2007 (2 years 8 months)

    Manage the Soarian Financials Architecture team, which consists of Full-time employees, consultants and Co-ops/Interns located in Malvern, PA and Kolkata, India

    Soarian Financials is one component of the Soarian Hospital Information system. Soarian is a next generation IT solution, designed with a workflow orientation that manages not just transactions and data but also the process of healthcare and the interactions between members of the healthcare community.

    The Soarian Financials Architecture team is focused on the evolution of the architecture (i.e. migration to Service Oriented Architecture) and to provide service layer support for new application features. The application itself is browser-based and developed in Java. Development is performed using Agile Methodologies: Test Driven Design, Continuous Integration, Scrum. The Soarian Financials Architecture team was the first team in Soarian Financials to adopt this development methodology.
