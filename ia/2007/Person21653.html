David T. Leboe’s Summary

Leveraging fifteen years experience with Price Waterhouse and Enron Corp. as a headhunter with Mackey & Tanner, LLC focused on accounting and finance roles with an emphasis on those in the energy sector.

Mackey & Tanner, LLC is a premier Houston-based, niche recruiting firm serving candidates and clients in the areas of accounting and finance. Our approach is simple – with our people, we establish solid relationships that produce the desired results for both our client companies and candidates. Our Motto, “People. Relationships. Results.” speaks for itself. We boast a team of 18 professionals bringing you over twelve decades of collective recruiting experience building relationships and delivering results.

We have a broad, deep network of clients who are seeking the best candidates in the country. With respect to our current client base, we have relationships with large public companies, privately held companies and start-up companies. Our client base includes over 45 of the Chronicle 100 employers, 25 of the Fortune 500 companies, and the vast majority of the energy trading and asset-based companies in Houston. Our current candidates find that they are able to effectively leverage their career possibilities through a single, reliable, reputable firm. In addition to our recruiting efforts for permanent accounting and finance roles, we have a Project Resource Staffing group focused on temporary, project staffing and longer-term staff augmentation assignments. Finally, we have an Information Technology group focused on both contract and permanent staffing solutions.

In October 2007, Mackey & Tanner was recognized as the 75th fastest growing Aggie-owned business as part of the annual Aggie 100 program delivering a 39.82% CAGR. We look forward to passing along lessons learned to future Aggie entrepreneurs.

For candidates interested in joining our network and tapping some of the best relationships in Houston, contact dleboe@mackeytanner.com.
