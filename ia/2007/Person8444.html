Janet Asteroff’s Summary

Janet works in a variety of areas for program and product development, marketing and management. Her expertise includes enterprise and consumer information systems, organizational strategy, communication systems, e-commerce, writing, teaching, editing and research.

A client-focused executive, Janet is a former Gartner Group analyst in the areas of internet development and enterprise deployment. As a Market Intelligence Analyst for IBM, she developed global channel strategies including the many competitive, regulatory and economic issues which span the SMB and Large Enterprise marketspace for direct channels. She also interpreted the IBM-PC-Lenovo sale and its relation to channel management. As a Vice President for The Concours Group, she managed professional services programs in the US and Europe. She has designed electronic services for Citibank's consumer division, and created marketing campaigns and interactive training systems for the Treasury Group.

As a consultant and writer, her clients include Merrill Lynch, Citibank, The Conference Board and Saba Software, among others. Janet co-wrote Merrill Lynch's enterprise RFP for electronic mail outsourcing. For the Conference Board, she introduced web conferencing to client research groups and authored reports on current trends in information systems.
