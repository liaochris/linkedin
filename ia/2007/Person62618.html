Nathan Williams’s Summary

Nathan is currently Director of Experience Design at MRM Worldwide London (McCann / IPG) where he is responsible for designing the digital customer experience for some of the worlds leading brands including Intel and Microsoft. Other clients for which he has worked over the last ten years include Mazda, Mercedes-Benz, AirMiles, Cahoot (Abbey National), London 2012, 3 (H3G), BT and The Carphone Warehouse.
