Glenn Nicholas’s Summary

My focus is startups in the online marketing services field. PublicityShip is a publicity and online marketing services company for small business in Australia. OM4Travel provides online marketing services for tourism & travel operators. I am working on various other ventures including educational services and change management ventures.

I'm interested in joint ventures where technology and online marketing capabilities are needed. I operate on both a fee-for-service and equity share basis.

My background has been in technology services, where I have worked for multinational companies in development, delivery, and sales/marketing roles. 2005 was the year I bit the bullet and decided to stop consulting and put my own ideas and strategies into practice. Smaller companies can go global now, and the implications of that are only just starting to be seen.

The new rules of marketing are being written day by day. I translate the new rules into services available online. I also develop marketing strategies and implement them.

I’ve reacquainted myself with technology while learning online marketing. My work with Wordpress and Wordpress MU has helped with the practicalities of online marketing. Virtual servers, open source software (particularly LAMP - Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP) and a dramatically expanded addressable market. These are ingredients for major change.

Outside my corporate career, project consulting (project management, change management, business modeling and business case development) have continued to be important. Not only have these skills provided my startup capital, they now help me bootstrap my companies.
