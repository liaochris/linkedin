Tom Maszerowski’s Summary

I work with the corporate export office as an intermediary between engineering and marketing groups to ensure US export regulations are correctly followed and to advise on the use of controlled technologies, such as encrypytion, in products. I review contracts with outside companies for compliance with regulations. I act as the primary interface to the Department of Commerce's Bureau of Industry and Security on encryption technology exports.

I have worked extensively in technology evaluation and as an in-house consultant for both software and hardware programs. I prefer working with companies that have a committment to creating products and services rather than purchasing them from others. My wide range of experience (product devlopment, R&D, corporate) allows me to take on any challenge.
