Devesh Agarwal’s Summary

Entrepreneurially oriented, result driven, globally astute, senior executive with 21 years’ experience leading business strategy, operations, supply-chain, technical and sales teams, in IT infrastructure, PCB, semiconductors, technology products, manufacturing, distribution, retailing, and software testing companies, with full P&L accountability. Expert in the design, development, and delivery of advanced technology, yet, pragmatic and cost effective, solutions, to meet business, financial, competitive and customer needs.
