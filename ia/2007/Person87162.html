Toon Ketels’s Summary

My first big experience in organizing was about 5 years ago. At that time, a friend and I decided we wanted to set up a student marketing association at my college KHLim. We found out it didn't exist anymore so we took the challenge to reestablish it. I found out I loved it!

After changing to the Vlekho Business School in Brussels I came in contact with Aiesec and saw the opportunities within. I became an active member rapidly, searching for all possibilities that this organization could provide.

Next term I became president and was responsible for the recruitment of new member, which we did successfully. My network started to expand, I gained manager skills, faced though problems, learned how to stay motivated, organized events, and learned how to manage myself in all different activities...

Some more challenges in Aiesec further, 6 months studying in Mexico and a semester at a French speaking university in Brussels, I'm about to finish my Master in International Business. While finishing writing my thesis, I started a web design course in Genk.

Being very excited about this new direction I'm taking with web design, I believe my past experience results in having a different and more business-like perspective on designing websites.

I'm committed to continuously increase my knowledge-base and skills level in order to add more value to the people's life’s and organizations I interact with.
