James Miles Hobbs’s Summary

James Miles-Hobbs, aged 46, has practised as a Rural Business Consultant for over 20 years. He initially specialised in strategic financial planning for farmers and landowners. Latterly, he has focused on rural diversification and, in particular, business analysis, marketing analysis and grant acquisition. James is Chairman of the Land & Farming Discussion Group in Salisbury and sits on the Wiltshire Branch Committee of the CLA. He is also a Director of Pucknall Energy Generation Limited and Farming Wise Limited.

James Miles Hobbs’s Specialties:

James provides the following consultancy services:

• Strategic advice and financial planning
• Farm and estate management
• Detailed on-farm advice on the impact of changes to the CAP
• Farm Assurance & Risk Management Assessments
• Contract Farming Agreements
• Farm taxation and finance
• Farm Diversification and Added Value Enterprises
• UK and European grant funding
• Farm and Estate visitor schemes
• Biomass energy projects
