Jan Blanchard’s Summary

I am the founder and MD of TouristR, which provides an online traveler's map to the world allowing users to personalise and share their travel information.

TouristR was selected as one of the top 48 Europeans start ups to watch in 2007 at the Web 3 conference in Paris. The webiste is undergoing final testing and will be launched soon.

Our objective is to allow visitors to the site to discover a region through their personal interests and people's experiences.
