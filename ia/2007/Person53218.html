SGI STUDIO GALLI INGEGNERIA SPA:

SGI is an Italian leading consultancy specialised in the sectors of water, environment, energy, civil engineering and transportation. Since its establishment in 1920, SGI has expanded into a joint stock company that currently employs over 120 staff and has 8 offices throughout Italy. In order to support the implementation of major projects in the Middle and Far East and in North Africa. Committed to providing the highest standards to its clients, SGI has kept abreast with technological development while maintaining tradition. Nowadays SGI is leader in the application of innovative technologies to ensure the accomplishment of environmentally sustainable solutions while optimising the design, operation and management of infrastructure. Since the 1990s SGI ’s international activity has experienced a significant growth. Through its involvement in projects funded by international and governmental institutions (e.g. the European Commission, the World Bank, UNEP, UNESCO, the Italian Ministries for the Environment and Foreign Affairs) SGI is undertaking assignments in 20 countries. SGI is currently implementing two of the world most challenging water-related projects: the restoration of the Mesopotamian marshlands between the Tigris and e Euphrates rivers and the water diversion project from the Blue to the Yellow river in China. SGI is certified according to ISO 9001 2000 quality assurance system. SGI’s annual turnover is about 12 MEUR.
