After a twenty year career building a successful reconigition and motivational business, Mr. Ordway began his recruiting career in 1997 specializing in IT Recruiting.

Roger Ordway founded FIT Recruiting to address his sincere passion in helping individuals and companies create harmony and joy in work environments.

“I truly believe everyone is seeking passion in their work and companies who match the cultural traits of their jobs to the individual traits of candidates will drastically reduce the costly and emotional decaying effects of employee turnover.”

FIT Recruiting Solutions LLC is an official business partner with Global Behavior, www.globalbehavior.com
a national behavioral assessment company committed to presenting the finest and simplest behavioral measurement tools within a management system structure in the areas of hiring, managing and developing productive relationships for organizations.
