C. Sven Johnson’s Summary

Over 15 years combined management, engineering and both industrial and virtual design experience including overseas product development and virtual world consulting for major media.

The goal of reBang is to both facilitate the development of and provide services for transreality development; meaning developing real products and then using the resulting manufacturing data and marketing materials as part of an integrated online sales and marketing effort through the use of interactive 3D. While currently bleeding edge, there has been some movement by manufacturers (e.g. Whirlpool) to leverage this opportunity, and reBang has been gaining a reputation for expertise in the emerging field.
