Jose Arocha’s Summary

I enjoy inventing and teaming up with good people to innovate and create new internet products that empower markets and communities. I recently joined LaborFair, Inc. I am very much looking forward to contributing positively to the temporary labor marketplace through our innovations. See my LaborFair description below.

I just finished the development of GlobalHealthEdu.org, a new online space for students, faculty and practitioners to learn, participate and collaborate in global health education.

During the academic years 2004-06, I worked as research scholar at Stanford University researching, conceiving and prototyping digital, social and portable media solutions for underserved communities in emerging markets in Latin America.

My experience spans the areas of semantic indexing and search, product design and innovation, international R&D consulting, technological competitive intelligence, knowledge mining, business development and entrepreneurship in Latin America and digital, social media.
