Ray Pierce’s Summary

I started my career at Metrowerks as one of the Pascal Compiler Quality Assurance team working Joe Hayden. I then transitioned into the Software Release area (or more affectionately known as Burnmeister) within Metrowerks working for Alex Harper. I released untold amounts of CodeWarrior for . I thought at one point we would ship CodeWarrior for Toasters. We were then aquired by Motorola.

I left Metrowerks, a Motorola Company to pursue an opportunity with Palm, Inc in the Development Tools Groups under Phil Shoemaker. We developed the Palm Development Tools (compilers, linkers, and debuggers) as well as producing the Software Development Kits and Conduit Development Kits for the Palm OS. We were spun out into our own subsidiary named PalmSource, Inc.

Due to the outsourcing of my position, I was left to ponder career moves while on unemployment. I found a few contracts to pass the time with Virgin Pulse, LLC, and LifeSize Communications doing various Quality Assurance, Build Management, and Installer Development. Both very cool companies, and cool people to work with, but I was not in their long term plans.

I then took a position with Metavante Image Solutions (formerly Advanced Financial Solutions) in Norman, OK working for Onur Civi in the Software Release Management group, and was responsible for the majority of the installer development within the company. The primary software is image capture software for checks (Check 21). They have a wide array of other banking related software covering most aspects of the banking industry.

I most recently took a position as a Build Engineer / SCM with Compass Learning in Austin, TX. I am responsible for the source code control system using SubVersion, build and deployment process and procedures, as well as installer development.
