Paul Hardy’s Summary

I have helped many companies fulfil their business aspirations by understanding the challenges associated within complex processes in single or multiple projects. Over the years I have used many different methodologies to drive business change and achieve maximum ROI within Project and Product life cycles from cradle to grave.

In my roles I’ve solely been responsible for ensuring overall quality and success of projects. The roles have allowed me to work closely with all departments, individuals and stakeholders to ensure the best short, medium and long-term solutions have been conceived and realised.

My background has given me the ability to communicate both risk analysis and solutions to a variety of business and project teams; also this experience has given me the opportunity during each projects critical path to find unique solutions to project gates.

During 2006 I added SCRUM MASTER to my portfolio of methodologies that includes PRINCE2, RUP and DSDM. I'm constantly refining my experiences as a business focused project manager.
