Arne Gasch’s Summary

"Above all else, guard your heart, for it is the wellspring of life..."
(The Bible - Proverbs 4:23)

The most important treasure of each human is his own heart. “Heart” does in this place not only mean the centre of feeling, but what constitutes the human being in his innermost and as a whole. With many people this treasure has been plundered. They long for wholeness, healing of their soul. Some try to make a virtue from their misery… They fill their lives with many good things. But their hearts remain nevertheless empty.

At the very least they would expect that the God of the Bible is able to fill up this hole. Nevertheless people in all Christian denominations have experienced the reality of the bible verse above; so that Augustines' statement is still being quoted gladly even today, where he speaks with God: "Restless is our heart, until it rests in you."

Jesus Christ says: “If anyone thirsts, let him come to Me and drink. He who believes in Me, as the Scripture has said, out of his heart will flow rivers of living water."

It is my passion to bring people in contact with this great God who can be experienced today and is interested in our well-being.

Find out more at my website: http://arne.gasch.googlepages.com/arnegasch2 .

Thank you, that you took your time to look in here.

God bless you,

Arne Gasch
