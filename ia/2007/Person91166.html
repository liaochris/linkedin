William Mackey’s Summary

I'm interested in business networking and would like to link up with members of the business, academic research, venture capital, financial, legal communities to explore new ventures. My career background includes adhesives and sealants, computer software, aerospace manufacturing, and Big Four management consulting. Consulting engagements focused on Just-In-Time, Activity Based Costing, and Total Quality Management. Business experience includes Business Startups, Business Development, Marketing, Customer Service, Finance, and Operations. Among my primary interests is the evolving impact of Web 2.0 — the changing ways in which software developers and end-users use the web as a platform — more particularly in seeking ways to exploit such features in developing and transacting business around the world.
