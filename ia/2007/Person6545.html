Ann Douglas’s Summary

Author/creator, The Mother of All® books series. Titles include The Mother of All Pregnancy Books, The Mother of All Baby Books, The Mother of All Toddler Books, The Mother of All Parenting Books, and The Mother of All Pregnancy Organizers (Wiley Publishing Inc.)

Author of numerous other books on pregnancy and parenting. Total of 27 published books.

Columnist for numerous magazines, including Conceive Magazine and Glow Magazine.

Freelance copywriter specializing in mom-to-mom communications.

Instructor, The Ella Centre for Pregnancy and Parenting.

Instructor, WebMD University (pregnancy/parenting).

Instructor, Trent University Continuing Education Department: "Publish and Prosper" course for freelance writers.

Frequently interviewed by/featured in major parenting media in Canada, the US, and the UK. Appearances/interviews have included Parenting, Parents, Working Mother, American Baby, Baby Talk, Fit Pregnancy, Pregnancy, ePregnancy, Junior, Today's Parent, Good Housekeeping, Better Homes and Gardens, Woman's World, BabyCenter.com, Parents.com, Parenting.com, SheKnows.com, WebMD.com, iVillage.com, CanadianParents.com, MochaSofa.ca, Planet Parent, Yummy Mummy, The Parent Report, Parent Journal.

You can find out more about me by visiting my website at http://www.having-a-baby.com.
