Ivan Aleksic’s Summary

Started working at Civil Engineering Company in Belgrade where I gained a lot of experience in client relations. Showing that my main interest is design, I let my self into web design and basic flash animation for company based out in California.

After experience with clients from LA, I was employed by a newspaper company in Belgrade, a really big organization with a lot employee. I met interesting people and had a really good time working in a great team.

I was recruited for military training by my country, so I had to quit job and answer the call. I was away from competitors and design industry for 8 months. Right after I wanted to work something. So I accepted invitation to experience working in an OEM company. Fortunately I managed pretty well, learned basics of accounting, sales and improved my knowledge in hardware.

Naturally I moved on to a job that is more related to the design. I got contracted by two interactive media companies based out in Michigan. I've done from basic web layout designs to GIU for software's, from flash animations to full software presentations.

Knowing that there is no prosperity in working as a contractor from project to project, I had to move on and put my goals. My wish was to experience west part of the world personally and see how people think and work there.

Contracted by a heliskiing company I went to Canada to design and develop a few web projects. Beside successful job I had a great time, learned and experienced many new things.

After Canada, I came back and employed by online media company based out in Dubai with an office in Belgrade where I started as a web designer. Shortly after, I was promoted to a Lead Designer and Project Manager.

I had a good team and we delivered great projects but I received an invitation to help in establishing one brand new company on really high standards. I took opportunity and started as a Creative Director, giving my best to establish a good company reputation.
