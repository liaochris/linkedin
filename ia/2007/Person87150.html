Tony Wanless’s Summary

Tony is a former journo who brings an eclectic background and a wide knowledge bandwidth to management consulting. Believing that skills collaboration is the business model of the 21st Century, he pursues all projects as a strategic partner instead of an executor.

His company, Knowpreneur advises and coaches entrepreneurial knowledge-based and member-based organizations on collaboration, creative and critical thinking, marketing/communications, financing, and decision making/planning.
A subsidiary company, Sentare Information Services provides communications support to marketers and complex marketing and educational material.

A piercing business analyst, Tony possesses a powerful, creative ability to cut through mental and organizational clutter to reveal relevant issues and problems. He then reorders information to serve a valuable purpose in problem solving, innovation, marketing, or communication.

Tony Wanless’s Specialties:

-- Hard-nosed business planner for associations and knowledge-based organizations
-- Coach executives and organizations at inflection -- or growth -- points
-- Guide science and technology organizations across commercialization Valley of Death
-- Facilitate creativity, product and service innovation, and collaborative thinking
-- Back up marketing and communications professionals with strategies and material

Tony Wanless’s Education

  • Canadian Association of Management Consultants

    CMC, Essentials of Management Consulting, 2005 — 2005

    At the risk of ruining my own business, I'd say this program should be a requirement for anyone in Canada and the US, which has a similar program, who's even thinking of running a business, much less being a consultant.
