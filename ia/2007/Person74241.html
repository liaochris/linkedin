Ron Coleman’s Summary

• An attorney, entrepreneur and executive with more than 15 years of progressive experience as a trusted counselor to senior executives at global corporations on intellectual property, internet business development and general corporate issues, including maximization of intellectual property assets.

• Highly skilled in business development, strategic planning and risk assessment, particularly in areas involving intellectual property, the Internet and “new media.”

• General counsel of the Media Bloggers Association and an active and internationally recognized figure in the “blogosphere”; launched and operate a successful legal/IP blog, LikelihoodofConfusion.com, a leading trademark, copyright and First Amendment weblog.

• An accomplished litigator; AV-rated by Martindale-Hubbell while still just an associate; Adjunct Professor at Seton Hall University School of Law.

• Frequent and well-regarded presenter at professional association and continuing legal education seminars on intellectual property, Internet business strategies, data management and other topics.

• Oft-published author of legal and business articles on technology, intellectual property and business issues in numerous trade publications; award-winning magazine feature writer.
