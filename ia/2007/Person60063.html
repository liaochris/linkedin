Minh LE’s Summary

Minh has more than 10 years experience in one of the most dynamic industry: mobile phones.

As Sales Director, Minh has built international sales network in more than 40 countries, managed and grow multi sites sales team and implemented large scale field marketing activities and promotional campaigns in various areas of the world. He developped from scratch a business amounting to 150 millions € turnover, and more than 7 millions € fixed and communication expense.

He used all his field experience when he moved to Product Marketing/Strategy to promote the end user as the center of the company. In a complex, time pressured, multi site projects, he succeeded by federating energies and enthusiasm around clear concepts and sharing his market vision.

Lately he determined TCL & Alcatel product strategy, related product roadmap and P&L, managed innovation focus with R&D, purchasing and 3rd parties. He also dealt with operator demand and implement necessary organisation to manage it.

This extensive experience makes him fit for new complex IT / multimedia related industries, requiring strong decisional skills, adaptability, anticipation and vision.
