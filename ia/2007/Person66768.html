Paul Russel’s Summary

In my short time on this rock I've worn many hats, illustrator, graphic designer, storyboard artist, 3d artist, compositing, photoshopper and prankster. I've dabbled in most of the fine arts at one time or another, painting, acting, singing, studying photography, film, animation and have proven to be quite mediocre at everything. Though others may disagree, you know what P.T. Barnum said about suckers. Which is exactly how I got to where I am today, a respected environment artist at Bungie, making the popular xbox game Halo interesting to look at for fourteen year old boys around the world. Hooray for pop-culture!

Paul Russel’s Specialties:

Speed, attention to detail and imagination in the field of creating virtual environments for video games. I must be insane, because I love the long hours and hard work.

  • Graphic Designer

    Ravinia Festival

    (Graphic Design industry)

    January 1994 — September 1994 (9 months)

    This was one of the more interesting and rewarding jobs I've had and a bit of a turning point for me, professionally, where I had major responsibilities for Chicago's premier outdoor music festival. For those who aren't familiar with Ravinia, it is, to most Chicagoans, simply magic. Ravinia has been around for nearly one hundred years now and boasts three music venues including what most believe to be the finest outdoor stage and grounds in the world. It's the summer home for the Chicago Symphony Orchestra and runs the entire musical gamut. Bring or rent a lawn chair, food, drink and sprawl out on the great lawn and enjoy the music. If you're ever in Chicago during the summer, this is a must do, without question.

    I was responsible for the program books, calendar and Steans institute material during the 1994 season working under the tutelage of the great David Pretiz.
