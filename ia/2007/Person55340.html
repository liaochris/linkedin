Mark Storey’s Summary

As a CCMC (Certified Career Management Coach) I work with people who were laid off, downsized, right-sized, fired, feel under-employed, not respected at work, and want to invigorate new life into their current careers. With Head and Heart Career Services coaching, clients discover:
Who they are
What they want to do
What they are best suited to do
Their personal path to a worthwhile job and a fulfilling career.

I also am a Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW), and provide very high quality, results oriented resumes, cover letters, and other job and career marketing materials.

Mark Storey’s Specialties:

Career coaching, resume writing, interview skills, management and leadership coaching.
