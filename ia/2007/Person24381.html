Donald Berube’s Summary

Over 20 years of film, television and multimedia production experience. Successful Freelance Lighting Cameraman, Editor, Field Producer/ Director, Audio Technician/ Sound Recordist in the Boston area, US and abroad for – capable of wide variety of HDTV and SDTV production styles including commercial, corporate, documentary, live, podcasting and ENG. Production workflow includes Final Cut Studio 2, AJA KONA, Blackmagic Design, PhotoShop, Compressor, Sorenson Squeeze, iDVD, DVD Studio Pro, SmartSound Sonicfire Pro, GarageBand, Soundtrack Pro, Keynote, PowerPoint. Excellent technical aptitude and project management skills. Ability to interface and partner with client from beginning of project to completion with a strong focus on client satisfaction. Also provide Technical Consulting, Technical Writing and Training Services for national digital video equipment manufacturers including Bogen Imaging, Canon USA, Convergent-Design, Focus Enhancements, JVC Japan, SmartSound and Tiffen.

Donald has traveled around the World several times as part of a PBS Documentary crew for "The Visionaries", which has aired nationwide on PBS. Donald has also been a facet of MIT Video Productions at the Massachusetts Institute Of Technology, working in a multitude of video production styles in the field and in the studio. Currently, Donald can be found traveling around the country providing production services and training for various companies such as Canon USA, Focus Enhancements and Bogen Imaging. Donald often times collaborates with his brother Daniel on multiple digital video projects for noisybrain. Productions. Founded in 1999, noisybrain. Productions, LLC is a collaborative, full-service production through completion partner that implements the use of Apple’s Professional Video applications with tools for Digital Cinema.

Donald Berube’s Specialties:

Shooting (both SDTV and HDTV), Lighting, Editing (Final Cut Studio 2), Audio Tech & Sound Recording, Grip, Field Producing/ Directing, Pre-Production, Production, Post-Production. Also provide Technical Consulting.
