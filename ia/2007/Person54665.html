Marina Hoffmann’s Summary

Marina is a Senior Vice President in MWW Group’s NYC office. Specializing in design, fashion and luxury public relations, she is highly skilled at developing comprehensive, strategic, creative programs on behalf of her clients. She currently oversees the consumer division in the NY office and manages some of the agency's flagship accounts, such as Samsung and The Related Companies.

Before joining MWW Group, Marina was the Managing Director, Senior Vice President, Chief of Staff for Rubenstein Public Relations, Inc. There she managed a staff of 40 as second in command, and oversaw account work with clients in real estate, luxury consumer brands, hospitality, publishing, business and entertainment industries, such as Trump real estate properties, 15 Central Park West, YOO USA, 823 Park Ave, Scalamandré, Helmsley Hotel, Trump Vodka, CITY Magazine, Miami Art Central, City Lights Media Group, Guernsey’s, and GlobalOptions Group. She conceptualized and evaluated brand-building strategies as well as analyzed media pitches and ensured the agency’s quality of work.

As a Vice President at LaForce + Stevens, Marina supervised the agency's design & hospitality division. Among the clients she represented were Federated Department Stores, IMAN Cosmetics, Mercedes Benz, Moet-Hennessey, Swiss Army, Target, design & real estate company YOO with Philippe Starck and Jade Jagger for YOO, H&M, Travel + Leisure, ELLE, ELLE Decor, Bernhardt Design, Thomas O'Brien/Aero Studios, architects/designers Yabu Pushelberg, lifestyle gurus Colin Cowie and Chris Madden, The Container Store, JCPenney, Dream Hotel, Indochine, Kittichai at 60 Thompson hotel, Clearly First, among others.

Prior to her tenure at Laforce + Stevens, she was with public relations agencies Veeder + Perman and Loving + Weintraub, and also served as a teacher of Italian and French to students in the 9th through 11th grades. She began her career as a public relations coordinator with LIFE magazine at TIME Inc.
