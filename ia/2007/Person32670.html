Gopal Ramasammy-Cook’s Summary

Gopal has worked in the IT industry as a software developer, analyst, and trainer/lecturer since 1989. He is currently a partner and software developer/analyst at Psybergate (PTY) LTD (www.psybergate.com / www.psybergate.co.za ), a software development company serving the higher education, financial, and government sectors.

Gopal has the following academic qualifications: B.Sc (Rhodes), HED (UNISA), B.Sc. Honours (Rhodes), MSc.(WITS). He has also attended several non-university courses and workshops in software development, project management and various aspects of business management. He has more than 16 years of extensive experience in commercial software development, systems analysis and design, the use of software development life-cycle methodologies and training and developing computer related courses. Recent experience includes the successful development of Internet-based systems using the Java language and the use of UML and an object orientated methodology. He also likes Ruby and Rails.

Gopal's current personal interests are in reading and writing both fiction and non-fiction, viewing and creating visual art, the use of storytelling and art to accomplish organizational objectives, philosophy, and personal development. Some of his creative endeavors can be seen at www.gopalrc.co.za. His interests in personal development and creativity, coupled with his belief that engaging strongly with one's creativity can enhance not only productivity and success in the workplace, but can also bring more meaning and happiness to life in general, has lead him to investigate Creativity Coaching and Life Coaching as ways of enhancing his own life and that of others. To this end, he is currently taking courses with the objective of obtaining certification in both these areas.
