Dev Bhatia’s Summary

Dev Bhatia is a startup and growth-stage executive with a track record of success in mobile web and internet advertising, application services, content and analytic software. He has grown three different start-ups to multi-million dollar revenues through aggressive sales and business development. He has raised more than $30 million in venture funding for his startups, and investors in his companies have more than tripled their investments. Among his previous successes are Screaming Media, where he wrote the business plan and helped raise initial funding for a company which later went public. He was VP, Brand Management at Yoyodyne, a web advertising pioneer, where he was responsible for products which represented more than 70% of company revenues prior to that company’s sale to Yahoo! in 1998. He later founded HotSocket, a web marketing/sales firm whose clients included AT&T, Chase, Discover, DoubleDay, Earthlink, FirstUSA, Verizon and many others and whose strategic partners included Acxiom, Experian, VNU/Claritas and Excite/MatchLogic. Investors in Bhatia’s firms have included: Bessemer, Chase, Flatiron, Highland, Goldman Sachs, Softbank and Zero Stage Capital.

Dev Bhatia’s Specialties:

Bhatia’s expertise includes strategic sales & marketing management, company development, product messaging and positioning, strategic partnering, team building, financial controls, and management of software development. His passion is to build great companies which are change agents in their industries. He's now in love with mobile marketing, because he thinks it's the next proving ground for great marketers, and the next big thing!
