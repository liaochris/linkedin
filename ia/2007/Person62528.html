Nate Warren’s Summary

• 15 years of copywriting experience with The Denver Post and the Denver Newspaper Agency in the Creative Services department, a high-volume operation supporting the sales, circulation and marketing teams with a staggering variety of work: rate sheets, marketing features, special sections, promotional retail campaigns, filler ads, sales sheets, brochures, and on-site collateral. I was responsible for coordinating, editing and writing special sections (including layout when needed), developing a wide range of ad copy (display and promotional campaigns, sales materials), and seeing materials all the way to production.

• Co-editor of BIAS — a new Gen X/Y-targeted media and marketing brand conceived by myself and a co-editor and incubated at the Denver Newspaper Agency. BIAS was a creative, alternative brand aimed at the Denver metro area's 21-34s. BIAS provided a magazine, website and an event sponsorship vehicle that integrated a reverse publishing/membership model with imaginative cross-platform promotions and marketing campaigns.
With my team, I performed editing, writing and layout of a bi-weekly magazine, produced a website, e-mail and text message marketing, marketing strategy and execution of all creative, event marketing, assisting sponsorship sales and fulfillment, distribution, sales development and team leadership.
My co-editor and I went on sales calls, coached and managed a freelance stable, designed integrated editorial/event themes and developed custom proposals for each, built formal marketing partnerships, oversaw street teams, wrote and performed promotional podcasts and even wrote and acted in our own promotional DVD.

• I am available for a wide array of freelance work, but my eyes are sharpest for the client, partner or employer with the opportunity that really turns my crank: getting to fuse my writing ability with daring marketing and content exercises on a team committed to a realistic plan for success.
