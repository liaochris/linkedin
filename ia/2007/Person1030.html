Rodger Williams’s Summary

A Clean Impression Commercial Cleaning Contractors is a Stockton, California based company providing commercial and industrial janitorial services to Stockton and surrounding communities in San Joaquin County. We are a California State licensed, bonded and insured family operated business. We are members of the Stockton Chamber of Commerce and the African American Chamber of Commerce of San Joaquin County. Certifications held include the Small Business and DVBE Certification.

Services provided to commercial customers include; professional janitorial, carpet and upholstery cleaning, tile floor stripping, waxing and buffing, window cleaning, pressure washing services and parking lot cleaning.

Our motto is "When your environment needs to be a showcase for your clients and employees, let A Clean Impression be their first impression."

Under industrial service offerings, we provide our customers with final construction cleanup for the commercial and retail office environments.

Recently we extended our professional carpet cleaning services to include residential customers.

A Clean Impression provides contracted janitorial service for small to medium sized companies. A potential contract customer of A Clean Impression would consist of a company containing between 10 to 500 employees or 2,000 to 100,000 square foot of office space. Buildings of this size typically require cleaning frequencies of two to seven days per week.
