Eliot Jacobsen’s Summary

A leading corporate strategist, CEO and twice voted one of the top 100 entrepreneurs in Utah, in studies by vSpring Capital, Eliot has 20 years experience driving corporate strategy and leading venture-funded technology companies.

-Head of Corporate Strategy at PayPal, Eliot was a member of the executive staff and led a team that touched all aspects of this billion dollar online payments leader with growth strategy, strategic partnerships, mergers & acquisitions, new products, best practices, competitive intelligence, and positioning.

- Lumiport, a light therapy company who's first product, the DermaStyle, reduces the redness and size of blemishes associated with acne, has been featured on CNN and NBC news as a promising new treatment.

- Omniture: Inc. 500 member and leader in strategic web analytics for the enterprise. Omniture customers include eBay, Microsoft, AOL, Walmart, Hewlett-Packard, General Motors, CBS Sportsline, ESPN, and Warner Music.

- Freeport: Loyalty management web services. Built this new venture from 2 to 150 employees and from $0 to $10mm in revenue run-rate.

- Viewpoint Digital : (Nasdaq: VWPT) Eliot raised several rounds of financing from Kleiner Perkins, Intel, Motorola and Sumitomo and ultimately helped sell the company to Computer Associates.

Mr. Jacobsen was a strategy consultant at Bain & Company, where he specialized in technology and competitive strategy, M&A, re-engineering and process improvement for FORTUNE 500 companies in the technology, banking, manufacturing, defense, retail and entertainment industries. Clients included Dell Computer, Lockheed/Martin Aerospace, Maryland National Bank, Dun & Bradstreet, and GTE. Eliot was also an analyst at Bain Capital where he helped analyze and complete large leveraged private equity transactions.

Eliot has a B.S. degree with Honors in Business Management, from Brigham Young University and an MBA from the Harvard Business School.
