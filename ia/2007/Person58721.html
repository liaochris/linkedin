Michael Tucker’s Summary

I am a Management Professional with 15 years of experience in the software and web industries. My career began before I graduated college with a web-based content driven company that I created. With that company, we realized success in our niche, and we later were acquired by a larger company in the industry. Since that beginning, I have been involved in several start-up situations.

As an executive and manager, I have authored business plans and developed business processes that drive success at growing companies. I have conducted research and analysis, managed the development of products, and managed the marketing and business development of these companies. At various times in my career, my teams have been international. From the product marketing standpoint, I have been responsible for business development, project management, and marketing for the company, and our products and services.
