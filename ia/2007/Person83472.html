Susan D. Strayer, SPHR’s Summary

OVERVIEW: Susan D. Strayer, SPHR is a Senior HR, recruiting and performance strategy professional. She has worked as a Director in both internal Human Resources and external Human Resources consulting with dozens of companies including Arthur Andersen, The Home Depot, Corporate Executive Board (CEB), Johns Hopkins University Deloitte Consulting, and The Ritz-Carlton. Susan holds an MBA, an MA and a BA.

DETAILS: Currently, as the founder of University and Career Decisions Susan works with individuals, companies and universities in career management and HR strategy. She is the author of The Right Job, Right Now (St. Martin's, 2007)

Susan’s experience also includes working in Staffiing for The Home Depot, and as a Project Manager for GWSolutions where she managed customized learning projects for both public and private sector clients. Susan also served as a Director for CEB serving Fortune 1000 executives in research programs focusing on corporate human resources, benefits, recruiting and training and learning. Prior to working for CEB, Susan worked in corporate human resources for Arthur Andersen.

Susan has an MBA with a focus in HR Management from Vanderbilt, an MA in Human Resource Development with a focus in learning and development from The George Washington University and a BA in Communication from Virginia Tech.

She is certified as a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR), and as an administrator of the Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI). She’s a member of the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM), 85 Broads and Net Impact.

Susan has presented at numerous conferences and events including Kennedy Information's Recruiting Conference, the MBA Career Services Council, the University of California, Deloitte, Harvard University and Oprah and Friends radio and

Her expertise has been quoted in both print and online publications and serves as a blogger on several online forums and has written a syndicated career advice column.

Susan D. Strayer, SPHR’s Specialties:

Individual coaching and consulting: Career Transition, Job Search Strategies, Career Growth and Development
