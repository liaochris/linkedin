Dennis Tevlin’s Summary

Dennis Tevlin is a seasoned executive with over 20 years experience building successful technology businesses. Tevlin was most recently Vice President of Marketing and Business Development at Trumba Corp. a company he co-founded in 2003. Prior to Trumba, Tevlin was President and Chief Executive Officer of Fullplay Media Systems, Inc., a Bellevue, Washington provider of digital media technology. From 1992 until 2000, Tevlin held several senior positions at Microsoft including General Manager of MSN and Director of Marketing for Microsoft Office. Prior to this, he held various marketing and business development positions with ITT Information Systems and Alcatel NV. Tevlin holds an MBA from Cornell University, an International Business Diploma from Nyenrode University in The Netherlands, and a BA/BS in economics from the University of Oregon.
