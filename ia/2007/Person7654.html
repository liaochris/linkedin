Arnaud Collery’s Summary

His early education was in the field of international finance and banking and so were his first few jobs around the world

Arnaud’s first true love was international Affairs. He studied international law at the American U of Paris and spent years in Tokyo working as a conference speaker on international cultural issues. He is fluent in Japanese.

As part of his military service to the French gov, he worked for the military intelligence school in Strastbourg and was hired to be an aid to Judge Jean Pierre, EU Congressman and leading personnality in Europe. Arnaud’s grandfather was the senator for the Champagne region.

In his mid twenties he switched to acting. After studying at the Moliere school of acting in Paris, working as a tv host in Paris for many tvs and at different studios in NYC, doing a number of commercials in Japan (e.g. Shiseido, Fuji TV, Panasonic) he started working full time in Hollywood in 2004.

In May 06, after two years in Hollywood he has directed 24 local tv shows, 2 shorts movies and has appeared over 100 times on stage doing stand up comedy. He is now seen on a regular basis at the comedy store and the improv in Los Angeles.
He is the F1 driver for the National Mercedez Commercial shot by Tony Kaye.

He is now working on 12 projects including an action comedy film where he plays with the new rising action star Emilio Estivi and Frenchie goes to Hollywood, his tale in Hollywood.
