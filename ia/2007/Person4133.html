Alyson McNutt English’s Summary

I am a freelance writer and photographer based in the southeast U.S. Some of my main areas of interest include parenting and family topics, women's health and fitness, safety and health, child development and psychology, and first-person essays. I also write a monthly product review column for a regional parenting publication.

My national publication credits include SELF (August 2007), Kiwi (July/August 2007, Sept./Oct. 2007, Nov./Dec. 2007), MSNBC.com and HGTV.com (links to all these are on my website). My work will also be appearing soon in upcoming issues of Kiwi (Jan./Feb. 2008), Pregnancy (Jan. 2008), Wondertime (TBD), Parenting (TBD), Organize (Spring 2008), Relish (Spring 2008) and BabyCenter.com.

I am also a blogger at BabyCenter.com's "Momformation" blog.
