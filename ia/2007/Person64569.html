Scott Levinson’s Summary

During a career spanning more than two decades, I have managed counsel on an international basis for a major corporation, litigated lawsuits for private law firms, served as Senior Trial Attorney for a large insurance carrier, and developed and operated a successful multi-state law practice. I have handled cases involving automobile accidents, premises (slip/trip and falls), construction and products liability, insurance defense, healthcare and property subrogation, and real estate and commercial matters. Several of my cases have been cited in professional legal journals.

Licensed to practice law in both New York and New Jersey, I have represented a wide variety of clients at every level of litigation, from intake up to and including trials and appeals. In addition to the State Courts of New York and New Jersey, I am admitted to and have practiced in the United States District Court for the Eastern, Southern and Northern Districts of New York and the United States District Court for the District of New Jersey.

Scott Levinson’s Specialties:

Civil litigation, commercial litigation, subrogation, personal injury, insurance defense, contract negotiation, trials, appeals.
