Jon Merz - jon@jonfmerz.com’s Summary

I'm an accomplished, published professional storyteller & entertainer. What I write brings intense action, crackling dialogue, and amazing characters to the audience. If you're searching for excellent content for new media platforms, if you want to license some truly exciting material for film, TV, and beyond, or if you just want to work with a writer who can create on-the-fly and deliver compelling and fulfilling entertainment, please drop me a line. I love what I do and it comes through in everything I create.
