Joelle Reeder’s Summary

Moxie Design Studios is an established design team specializing in full-service couture blog and website design, including boutique commercial and small business websites. Custom websites designed to your specifications have been our bag since 2003 and we take pride in offering service with style. At Moxie Design Studios, we believe in quality design and competitive rates, but it’s our attention to detail that really makes our clients swoon!

The Moxie Girls, Joelle Reeder and Kathy Scoleri, have spoken at events such as SXSW Interactive and are authors of "The IT Girl's Guide to Blogging with Moxie" (Wiley, 2007). For media inquiries, please contact pr@themoxiegirls.com.
