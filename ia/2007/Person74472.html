Roshan Joseph’s Summary

I am current working as the Principal Consultant for Adaptive Consulting, a company I founded and which is based in Melbourne, Australia. I specialise in Business Technology, Software Development, Project Management, IT Management, Support Management and working with Small-Medium enterprises gain maximum benefits from their Investments in IT. I especially enjoy "bringing back to life" projects that have hit brick-walls or technical challenges.

In May 2007, I founded www.worldsportevents.com which aims to collect and distribute sporting news and schedules easier to anyone with any interest in sports.

I previously worked for EMQ Pty Ltd & Custom 161 Pty Ltd as the IT Manager and Business Development Manager respectively, responsible for Software Development, Project Management, General IT Management and Developing the Business.

Before that, I was the Solutions Architect for Patrick Autocare, the largest vehicle logistics company in Australia.
