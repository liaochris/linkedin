Brian Cavoli’s Summary

Brian is Director of Marketing at TNS Media Intelligence/Cymfony leading the company’s brand development and lead generation efforts. TNS Media Intelligence/ Cymfony integrates the analysis of social media with traditional media sources to gain consumer insights that develop stronger bonds with influencers.

Prior to Cymfony, Brian started the search engine marketing group in the US for MPG Media Contacts with clients including Vonage, Fidelity Investments and Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines.

Brian was part of the launch team for the interactive media group at Carat. As VP of Innovations, Brian created the emerging technologies initiative to advance the agency's media strategies in wireless, rich media and interactive television. Also at Carat, Brian was an Interactive Media Director leading the interactive media strategies for clients including Radio Shack and Pfizer.

Brian has also held marketing positions at Bluestreak, Monster.com and Instant Information and is a former board member of BIMA, the Boston Interactive Media Association.

- Winner 2005 MediaWeek Media Plan of the Year for “Best Use of Internet”
- Winner 2004 MITX Award “Innovative Marketing Technology”
