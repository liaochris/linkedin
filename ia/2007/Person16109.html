Chiara Mulas’s Summary

A creative industrial design engineer, with conceptual thinking skills.

Chiara Mulas has recently obtained the Master of Science degree in Integrated Product Design, at the Delft University of Technology (the Netherlands). The graduation project has been carried out in Philips Lighting, Eindhoven, on the subject of finding interesting applications for new LED lighting technologies.

Chiara's aim is to represent, as an industrial designer, the link between marketing and technology, helping companies to develop successful and meaningful products.

Chiara Mulas’s Specialties:

Product design, industrial design, conceptual thinker, strategic product design, new ideas developer
