Sirish KK’s Summary

A Training professional with 6+yrs diversified experience in Training, Recruitments and Marketing.

Currently working as Sr. Trainer – Soft Skills for a property information services company (an IT / ITES), and over the last 10 months trained more than 1400 employees from junior & middle level management.

Experience in training needs analysis, preparing training schedules and calendar. According to the business needs, modules are designed & developed from time to time. Conduct pre & post training assessment and hence provide developmental plans. Periodic assessment is based on a particular business requirement, and implemented successfully.

Training is largely on soft skills, together with attitudinal and behavioural training across domains on various skills like Corporate Etiquette, Effective Communication, Presentation, Work Ethics, Stress Management, Leadership, Team Work, Time Management, etc.

Various interventions included experiential learning through on-the-floor observation, hand-holding exercises, mock role plays customised to business need, exercises and activities best suited to adult learning method. Also, has experience in out-bound training programs like team building, team work, team energizing, etc.

Have meticulous understanding of Learning & Development cycle, is geared up to accept managerial challenges in any industry. Have experience in HR function and has thorough understanding of its activities.
