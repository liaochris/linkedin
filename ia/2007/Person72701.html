Ric Shreves’s Summary

I work in two very different industries: Open source software and luxury travel. Water & Stone and the Mambo Foundation are concerned with web application development and Open Source systems. The Elite Havens Group is Bali's leading luxury travel firm and a growing player in the property and development market.

In addition to writing for ComputerWorld magazine, in July of 2006 I published the Mambo Visual Blueprint with Wiley & Co. The book is an administrator's manual for the Mambo Open Source Content Management System.

As a result of my writing activities I often speak at industry events - typically on Open Source and CMS technologies.

Ric Shreves’s Specialties:
