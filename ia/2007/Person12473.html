Michael Hemenway’s Summary

With a lifetime spent as a serial entrepreneur, Mr. Hemenway has over 30 years of business, finance, advertising, marketing and franchise experience. He developed, operated and sold many businesses on his path to success; he brings with him a wealth of experience and hands-on business knowledge. He played a major role in establishing and developing an internet network of franchise business opportunities and concept consulting services worldwide. Mr. Hemenway is the Co-Founder, Publisher and Chief Executive Officer of brandEXPANSION, Ltd., and oversees the overall direction of the company and future projects.
