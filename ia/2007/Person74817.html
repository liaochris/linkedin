Rob Svetlik’s Summary

Currently a Global Strategist at Samsung in Seoul, Korea, which works with the highest levels of the Samsung Group on highly strategic globalization issues, Rob is a former design engineer who complemented his technical background with an MBA in five majors (Marketing, Strategy, Finance, Entrepreneurship, and Technology) from the Kellogg School of Management. As an engineer he designed five award winning mobile phones for Motorola, three ground breaking inkjet printers for HP, a coffee tumbler for Starbucks, office products for ACCO Brands, a circular saw for Skil Power Tools, and an air freshener for Yankee Candle - while sourcing and training partners from around the planet. He also worked briefly at a boutique design consultancy (similar to IDEO) as well as in notebook product planning for Dell. In the past ten years, he has lived and worked in Honolulu, Chicago, London, Portland (OR), Singapore, Austin (TX), and Seoul and has traveled to 41 countries.

Rob Svetlik’s Specialties:

Telecom equipment (wireless handsets), computer equipment (printers and notebook computers), consumer products, product planning, globalization - sourcing, logistics, manufacturing, leadership, entry strategy, and alliances.
