Ingrid Alvarez’s Summary

I've been working for RE/MAX Mutual Realty in Ballard for about two and a half years now and had great opportunity for growth in my career path. Previously, I was attending college at the University of Washington and working for local organizations such as the Seattle Art Museum and the Seattle Symphony. At Faerland Terrace, an assisted living home on First Hill, I managed the AR, billing and eventually moved into marketing.

My goals as an employee of RE/MAX Mutual are to drive as much traffic to the office as possible, whether it's on the internet or in person. Since the company and the building is so unique, it draws business professionals who have a strong sense of identity and embody entrepreneurial values. I am very interested in raising the standards of communications in business by helping people tend to their brand identity and become comfortable with social media.

My personal goals are to continue growing my business as an artist and independent professional, taking everything to New York this Fall. By fusing my talents, skills and experience, I hope to attract opportunities in helping others by creating art. I enjoy facilitating events, promoting and utilizing social media for independent business.

With a focus on art, I have developed a body of work during the past seven years that includes drawings and paintings in watercolors, oils and acrylics. By using effective composition and great attention to detail, I've become skilled in graphic design. This year, I've been learning about multimedia authoring for the web, which has tremendously expanded the opportunities to practice non-traditional marketing.

Ingrid Alvarez’s Specialties:

Marketing with social media, blogging, creating unique graphic design and writing content for branding, advertising, developing curriculum for teaching classes. As an artist I specialize in painting and printmaking.
