Dan Snyder’s Summary

The Association of Private Lenders is the first international association of individuals who lend funds on secured assets.

Private lending is when you lend funds to someone and they put up valuable assets as collateral for your loan. When we say valuable assets, 99 percent of the time we mean real estate.

Many people stumble across private lending and find that they can easily make incredible returns on their savings instead of leaving it in a CD or money market fund. However, many solo private lenders do not follow a prescribed system. They tend to wing it and only hold loosely to the principles that safeguard private lending transactions. You do not have to follow in their footsteps because now you've found the Association of Private Lenders!

The Association of Private Lenders publishes information on how individuals can lend money directly to others with a high degree of safety and still make a fabulous return on their money. People who need higher returns on their assets without taking extreme risks appreciate the security of private lending.

The easiest way to find out more about the benefits of private lending is to sign up for the free newsletter: the Do Not Lose Money Newsletter. You can subscribe on the first page of our website. This free information is another way to learn more about us and how we help individual investors.
