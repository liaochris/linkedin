Bill Lowe’s Summary

Computers and networking have been my hobby and job for over twenty years and I have always been fascinated by the ways technology can be used to create a strategic advantage or increase efficiency. I started on the technical side but grew to enjoy analyzing business processes and systems.

Formal education provides frameworks that act as a foundation on which to build. I currently hold an MS degree in Computer Networking and am pursuing an MBA in Finance. Education is only a beginning. Steadfast vigilance is required to keep learning on your own. Consistently building your knowledge base is what leads to excellence. I am absolutely fascinated by the point where technology and business meet. That is where I focus my efforts.
