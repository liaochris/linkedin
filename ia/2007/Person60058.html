Steve Ming Yeow Ng’s Summary

If I am to give myself an analogy, I would compare myself to China -- very raw, but very driven and with lots of potential. I love building teams, and being part of great ones. Took my first real step into leadership with The Digital Movement, an initiative I started with a few friends to build communities in the region for upcoming technology trends (think Web 2.0., blogosphere, virtual worlds, etc). Created some great results with Nexus 2007, and now looking to take this intiative to the next big step up.

Since I came back from the states, I have been a strong advocate of Software as a Service (SAAS) and Web 2.0, taking part in competitions where I evangelized SAAS, as well as organizing and moderating successful events centered on building communities, Web 2.0 and Social Media.

In particular, I am very excited about the potential of collaborative web based applications to enhance the productivity and effectiveness of teams within and outside of organizations. Web apps like Attensa, Google Apps and Dabbledb hints at a sea change in how people collaborate, and I want to be at the center of it all.

I am a big fan of candor, straight talk, and irrelevance at work. After all, work is life, and life should be fun!
