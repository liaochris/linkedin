Jennifer Hanson’s Summary

As Founder and Chief Experience Officer of Realsight, Jen utilizes many years of innovation experience to pioneer truly new innovation strategies and solutions. Tired of seeing the same opportunities from existing research techniques, Jen left “corporate” America in 2006 to found Realsight. Expertly trained in analytics and consumer observation, Jen has led the innovation strategies and solutions behind many successful new products such as PepsiCo’s Fuelosophy and Ben & Jerry’s Milkshakes, Kraft’s Capri Sun Raging Waters and South Beach Diet line as well as the strategy behind licensing, joint ventures and acquisitions.

Before starting Realsight, Jen worked as a member of New Platforms Innovation at PepsiCo Inc., working to develop new product categories for its beverage portfolio. Prior to Pepsi, Jen was part of the Innovation team at Diageo, the largest multinational beer, wine and spirits company in the world. Jen also worked in several innovation research management positions at Kraft Foods, Inc. She started her career as a statistical consultant for analytics companies.

Jennifer Hanson’s Specialties:

Finding growth opportunities through quantitative analysis of actual consumer behavior: products (competition), people (lifestyles), positioning (messaging), purchasing (in-store), promotion (media consumption).
