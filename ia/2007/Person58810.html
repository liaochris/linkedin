Mike Walsh’s Summary

Mike Walsh is a leading authority on new media. Through his company ‘Tomorrow’ - he advises the CEOs and management teams of some of the world's leading brands and corporations on the new digital landscape.

Some of Tomorrow’s clients have included News Limited, Foxtel, the ABC, Star TV, nineMSN and Fuji Film. Mike’s newsletter, 'The Fourth Estate' is read by thousands of media professionals around the world.

Mike previously launched Jupiter Research and internet.com in Australia. He is currently writing a book on the future of entertainment media and files a regular column on digital innovation for Australian Anthill magazine.

Mike has a commercial law degree, and has worked for a range of leading professional services firms including KPMG, Bain International, and Freehill Hollingdale & Page.

During the nineties, Mike was cited by the Sydney Morning Herald as one of the top 30 entrepreneurs under 30. While at University, he was ranked 5th best speaker in the world at the World Debating Championships. Mike currently serves on the Director's Working Circle at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Sydney.

Mike Walsh’s Specialties:

Commercial Strategy, Online Media, Digital Media, Future Trends, Branding, Search Engine Marketing, Negotiation, Business Development, RSS, Web 2.0, Mergers and Acquisitions.
