James Curran’s Summary

My broad background in business includes the ability to identify and capitalize on opportunities to enhance profitability, particularly in the face of a competitive economy and stiff competition. I can also bring value by encouraging the integration of ideas to meet market needs.

My career has been a series of positions of increasing responsibility. Several achievements are as follows:
• Increased Agency Sales from $1.3 million at time of purchase to $2 million currently by managing agency staff, developing and implementing a coherent marketing plan.
• Grew the total agency policies in force from approximately 1300 to 2000 over the last 6 years.
• Increased agency multiline (that is clients with more than one type of policy, i.e. home & auto, or home & life) from 40% to 45% over time by marketing directly to existing clients while maintaining overall growth.

As a creative, enthusiastic leader who integrates bottom-line priorities with a genuine team-building style, my goal is to join a company where I can provide the value critical to taking the organization to its next level.
