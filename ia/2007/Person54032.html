Marcin Gosiewski’s Summary

Currently running own consulting company "Marcin Gosiewski Doradztwo i Zarzadzanie", and working as a technical director and CTO of World IT Systems.

In the years 1991-2006 founder, shareholder and President of a privately held polish IT company BiG Vent S.A. which operates on Polish market since 1992. Specialises in Data Storage Systems but also operated on automotive software market (through daughter company Motosoft) and internet (through daughter company Netlandia / BiG Vent Okablowanie). Company sold in 2004 to a listed company CSS.
