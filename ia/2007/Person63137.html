Greg Grimer’s Summary

Voices in Business are a new media firm that help b2b brands establish their thought-leadership and build real credibility within a niche business sector.

We research, arrange, produce and host a series of high-quality business interviews in audio and video podcast format with the movers and shakers of any business sector. Your domain-expert joins us in those interviews, to contribute your voice.

We believe strongly that credible value contributers to a business niche, whether they are end-users, consultants or vendors, should be able to make their voices heard loud and clear.
