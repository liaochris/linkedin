Richard Cushing’s Summary

I have been assisting organizations with PC-based technology design, development, and implementations for more than 20 years with an emphasis on best-of-breed ERP solutions for
* Distribution,
* Manufacturing,
* Project Management, and
* Project Accounting.

While I have been involved with the implementation of more than a dozen different accounting and ERP solutions over the years, I have spent the last nine years focused on MAS 500 ERP (a.k.a., Acuity Financials, Best Enterprise Suite, Sage Enterprise Suite). I have been working with that product since version 1.6.

Many of my clients over the past 20 years have been related to the construction industry: General contractors, specialty contractors, engineers and architects, manufacturers and suppliers to the construction trades. I am a former member and state committee chairman for the Associated General Contractors (AGC) and former member and local education chairman for the Construction Specifications Institute (CSI).

I am a current member of APICS - The Association for Operations Management with experience in manufacturing, distribution, logistics, business process analysis, and more.
