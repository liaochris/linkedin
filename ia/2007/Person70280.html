Rahul Gaitonde’s Summary

Rahul Gaitonde is a technologist, writer and strategist operating chiefly out of Mumbai, India.

An early adopter of technology since the late 90s, Rahul is best known for his ability to connect business with technology - to convert ideas into products and products into businesses. His opinions and writings have helped individuals and small businesses understand and leverage the power of the Internet, mobile devices and Open Source.

Areas of work span venture capital in technology, the mobile data opportunity in India, Web 2.0 business models, affordable computing, and economic reform in India.

Rahul writes extensively on his web log "Rahul Gaitonde on Technology and Strategy (www.rahulgaitonde.org/blog)". He has also written columns - both print and online - for business and technical journals. Apart from his writing, he is also a frequent speaker at various technology/business events.

Currently pursuing his MBA at the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode, he holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Mumbai.
