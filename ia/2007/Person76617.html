Sarah Leary’s Experience

  • Vice President, Product and Marketing

    Shopping.com (an eBay Company)

    (Internet industry)

    May 2003 — January 2006 (2 years 9 months)

    As the Vice President of Marketing, led the repositioning and relaunch of the company as Shopping.com in September 2003. Shopping.com is the result of the April 2003 merger of Epinions and DealTime.

    Following the launch of Shopping.com in September 2003, moved into the role of Vice President, Product and lead the product plans and strategy for Shopping.com through its integration in eBay, Inc after eBay acquired the company in August 2005.

  • Vice President, Marketing and Product Management

    Epinions.com

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    August 1999 — May 2003 (3 years 10 months)

    As the Vice President of Product Management, led the team of product managers, designers, and product marketers that redesigned, and rebuilt Epinions.com in time to raise a third round of funding in January 2001.

    As the Vice President of Marketing, led a cross functional team to reposition the company as a full service comparison shopping service. The result of this effort was a September 2001 launch of integrated price comparison functionality and the first month of profitability in December 2001.

    Previously held position as Director, Content and led the 20 person category management team that built out the content and communities that covered more than 40 categories on Epinions.

    Epinions (www.epinions.com), the leading consumer reviews and rating service was started in May 1999 and was acquired by DealTime in April 2003. The combined company was renamed and launched as Shopping.com in September 2003 and later acquired by eBay in August 2005.
