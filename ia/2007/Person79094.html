Shawn Steiner’s Summary

I am on my 10th year at Autodesk's Media & Entertainment division which was formally called Discreet and before the acquisition in 1999 we were a separate company called Discreet Logic based in Montreal. Autodesk last year acquired Alias, who was based in Toronto, and has added their software products to our software product offering. I have moved into a new position at Autodesk this year to work with clients who have not owned our Systems products previously. Last year I worked on a launching new enterprise compositing production tool called Toxik which I am still selling. More information available on our web site at http://www.autodesk.com/toxik. I started my career back in 1983 as a Software Engineer for a little known company called Truevision. I was also one of the founders of AT&T Graphics Software Labs, and then I moved on to help start several companies in this industry: Electric Image, MetaCreations and Denim Software (which was acquired by Discreet Logic in 1997 and the product is now called combustion). Toxik is revolutionizing the professional compositing / paint / roto marketplace so please drop me an e-mail and let’s setup a meeting and demonstration on how your facility can vastly improve your film production workflow.

Autodesk (ADSK) is one of the largest software companies in the world at $1.5 billion in annual revenue.
