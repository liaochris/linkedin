Jason Wright’s Summary

I have recently graduated from one of the top cosmetology schools in South Carolina. I also have successfully obtained my Cosmetology License from the State board of South Carolina. My work experience began when I was so fortunately able to start attending Betty Steven’s Cosmetology Institute. I now have a year of work place experience in a salon environment. The training and education I have received has gone far beyond just the study of hair, skin and nails. It prepared me in so many ways for this next step in my career. I feel fortunate that I was exposed to so many different products and manufacturers, as well as the applications and usage of these products, while in school. On our shampoo bar and in our dispensary I was able to work with products by Aveda, CHI, Framesi, ISO, Joico, Mizani, NIOXIN, Pureology, Redkin, Rusk, Scruples, Sexy Hair, and Tressa, just to name a few. I feel that this variety has helped me to see just how different yet similar product lines are, and allowed me to learn to be flexible.

Jason Wright’s Specialties:

Purchasing, merchandizing, product knowledge and promotion, building strong client relations, working as a team, off set production hair stylist. Plus much more!
