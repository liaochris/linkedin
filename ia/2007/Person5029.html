Anders Bang’s Summary

Nordea Private Equity manages approx. €400m in Private Equity fund of funds commitments. The first fund was raised in 2001 with successor funds raised in 2004. Since early 2002 I have been responsible for the operational side, designing and implementing administration systems, risk management tools, monitoring routines and reporting.

I have served on Private Equity Partnerships' Investment Committes and acted as advisor to the Oversight Committees which approves all investment decisions.

Other work areas: Private Placement Memorandums (PPMs), product development, designing Limited Partnership Agreements, spin out to North Sea Capital A/S and fund raising.

Anders Bang’s Specialties:

Private Equity, financial statement analysis, valuation, risk management and product development.
