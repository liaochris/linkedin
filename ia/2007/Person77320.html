Scott Ryan’s Summary

I am an Open Source evangelist with experience in Java and J2EE. I implement commercial packages where appropriate and leverage open source to increase the capability of commercial packages such as BEA Weblogic and IBM Websphere. I focus on business value and ROI as well as process improvement and automation. I am a BEA Technical Director and actively participate in multiple Open source projects including Apache Beehive, Apache Maven and Cargo. I am a featured speaker as User's Groups in the area. I keep active with hands on development at several clients using Open Source technologies including Spring, Hibernate, JPA, Liferay, Struts, Tapestry, Wicket, Maven, Cargo, Beehive, Metadot, Subversion and Eclipse. I also am involved in Application Server, Portal and Web Services implementations using BEA Weblogic and Aqualogic, IBM Websphere, ATG Dynamo, JBoss. My company performs custom consulting in the Java/J2EE, Portal and Web Services areas. We also offer an open source solution to automate software delivery including source code management, developer communication and release production, deployment and tracking. I have expertise in High end graphics development and complex data management solutions.

I have a degree in Geophysical Engineering and have over 20 years of hands on leading edge technology implementation expertise. I have developed hardware and software solutions. I have been president of several Standards Organizations. I have worked on both the consulting and the IT side of the business. I have experience working for Vendors such as IBM, Continuum and Halliburton as well as IT implementers such as First Bank, Echostar, Zions Bank, Wells Fargo. I have also worked for small and large consulting firms such as IBM, Meritage Technologies, Soaring Eagle , TekCommand, Landmark Graphics. I have hands on development experience and have managed business and technical experts ranging from groups of 10 up to a group of 300.
