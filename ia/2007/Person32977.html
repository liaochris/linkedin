Gray Gleason’s Summary

Having worked as a writer, producer, audio designer and voice talent for most of the world's top advertising agencies - like O&M, BBDO,McCann, Leo Bernett, Backer Spielvogle, Dentsu, Saachi and Saachi, and others (many before all the mergers), I have a good handle on what constitutes good, creative, and effective advertising.

As a voice talent, while in the States, I was in demand even to the extent of teaching voice-over for the Screen Actor's Guild in Hawaii. Now that I am in Asia, my voice is currently heard all over the world. In Hawaii, on Maui's Paradise TV, in Bangkok as the voice of Bangkok's 98.5, Breeze FM - and as the "signature voice" for such companies as Cadillac, Mitsubishi, San Yang Motorcycles (SYM), President Milk, Ford Focus, Yamaha, Toyota Prius, and many others.

As a "radio Guy" I am interested in radio syndication in Asia. I think this is a wide open area.

I have also found that I-pod (Pod-cast) production is becoming more and more important for various companies who want to carry their "website" experience beyond the computer. Podcasts can be downloaded (even subscribed to) and played anywhere - freeing the user from their computer, and even their radio - allowing them to listen on the subway, the train, the bus - in elevators - anywhere. For companies and Government agencies, this means that their message can go anywhere, as well!

Gray Gleason’s Specialties:

As a writer/producer/ and voice talent, I can help with audio for Video, and with straight audio projects, like I-pod/podcast downloads. I have my own recording studio, and I can bring a project in quickly on a very tight budget. If your company would like to have a professional sounding audio portion dimension for your web-site - which may be streamable, or downloadable - please contact me.
