Summarizing my professional experience...My sole goal in business everyday is to do nothing short of winning, lead our team by example, be the best at what we do, know our business-our clients business-and our competitions business to the degree that we are trusted advertising consultants to our clients, truly caring about your clients and what they're trying to accomplish, and having integrity in everything we say and do. My experience in sales has been terrific and successful! I attribute my success to my summary above and to an outstanding mentor my father has been to me for as long as I can remember, as well as my General Manager Tom Cummings who took me under his wing in the transit advertising business and taught me the ropes and more.
