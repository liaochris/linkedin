Louis D'Esposito’s Summary

I am a highly experienced communications designer and marketing strategist. Over the course of my 20 plus year career I have shown an uncanny ability to understand client needs and develop creative solutions that surpass their expectations. I provide customized solutions and assemble dedicated teams of professionals for the development and implementation of creative strategies. Having worked at the corporate level, in various agencies and as an independent consultant I offer my clients a well-rounded business like approach to the design process. In March 2001 I founded Listen&Look, a design and marketing consulting firm located in Tarzana, CA.

Louis D'Esposito’s Specialties:

Annual Report Design and Production, Branding Campaigns, Brand Identity Design & Implementation, College Search and Admissions Materials, Marketing Strategy & Implementation, Sales & Collateral Materials, Trade Exhibits, and Web Site Design & Development. Distributor of ABEX Trade Exhibits and Displays.
