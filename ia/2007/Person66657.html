Paul Laughlin’s Summary

As the culmination of a varied and successful career at Lloyds TSB, I lead a high performance customer insight team (over 30 analysts & database marketing experts). Having created this function 4 years ago, I have developed it into a critical capability that adds over £10 million of profit to the bottom line year on year.

My career started in IT. Over 10 years I delivered many innovations & major programmes (1st PC-based DIP & Worklow system in Europe, Internet Banking, AI models to improve credit scorecards et al). I then moved across to the business to be the author of change rather than a slave to centralised processes.

My career within the General Insurance business has included Risk (actuarial & underwriting) and Marketing. I love the challenge of mastering and understanding new areas - especially those requiring innovation and intellectual challenge. It has been highly enjoyable to succeed in understanding reinsurance sufficiently to be the key recommender on a £20m+ budget. It has been even more enjoyable to create from scratch a class-leading customer insight capability. This has included the joy of recruiting and developing good people to become very capable and successful in their careers as well as the pleasure of mastering this capability. I have been able to lead the business in the changes needed and prove the significant profit such a change can add to the P&L.

My focus at present is on consumer segmentation, where I am again having significant success in achieving a level of understanding that is rarely seen, together with an actionability that will make segmented propositions developed on this understanding a highly competitive advantage. I would be interested to talking with other experts in the field.
