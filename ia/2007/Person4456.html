Iri Amirav’s Summary

Resourceful online marketing professional with International skills and knowledge. Currently President, CMO and founder of iMedix, a community powered health search engine, enabling consumers to easily find, contribute and share information
with other people. Formerly, established and led Metacafe's online marketing activities to position it as one of the top 60 largest English language sites in the world (Alexa). Prior to that, supervised the creative content and business partnerships department for Pelephone communications Ltd, Israel’s pioneer mobile operator. Formerly, managed the marketing and business development for the A.F.D group, a Washington DC based private investment company of entertainment ventures. Prior to that, lead various cross-platform marketing activities for NYC Time Warner Cable and directed new advertising applications projects for McDonald’s, Pepsi and Sony on behalf of MVT Inc. Served 3 years in the Israeli Defense Forces as a combat soldier in the Golani commando Reconnaissance Platoon and later on as a budget officer. Received his BBA with honors from Baruch College, City University of New York.
