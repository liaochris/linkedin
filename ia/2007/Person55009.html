Mark Johnson’s Summary

My early career in retail included GM roles for very large retail operations with headcount responsibility exceeding 40 in 2 levels of management. I then spent 5+ years managing HR and Operations for a growing retail chain in California as their VP of Operations, which included retail and operations headcount exceeding 115 in 3 levels of management. Realizing that retail had little more to offer I returned to school to finish a degree combining Business Management with an HR/OD core. After graduating with distinction I spent a year + in S. Korea creating & delivering Sr. Management training products for some of Korea's largest corporations (Hyundai, Kia, LG, etc.). While we loved Korea, we returned to the US. I transitioned in to high-tech w/ Tektronix (http://www.tektronix.com ) in a Staffing role. I grew that position as far as possible, which included management of up five direct headcount, numerous enterprise-level projects, and recruiting to the VP-level. I was recruited to The Standard (http://www.standard.com) in October of 2004 to develop and deliver staffing services to a field sales team of ~500 in 40+ offices nation-wide. Over my career with Standard I managed multiple enterprise projects, including our first ATS implementation/integration and was promoted to manage staffing (including headcount and resources) for ~60% of the business. I was recruited to WebTrends (http://www.webtrends.com) in May of 2007 to manage Staffing, and provide day-to-day management of HRIS/Comp/HR Services (policies/programs/processes) etc.

Mark Johnson’s Specialties:

Primary professional focus is Corporate Staffing and HR Operations, including ~10 years in Fortune-500 class corporate HR. I have managed direct head count of up to 115 employees in a 3-tier hierarchy, including teams of up to 5 in high-tech corporate staffing. I am proven strategic manager and contributor with ample hands-on experience.
