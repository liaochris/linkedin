Jon Silverman’s Summary

I have over 20 years of industrial & high tech field sales, sales management, business strategy, product development and marketing experience. I worked for leading engineering services companies involved in the power generation business, fluid power and process controls business in Boston & Silicon Valley. Most recently, I took on the position of VP of Sales & Marketing with Plastics Etc. which provides industrial process control solutions for large engineering firms involved with municipal water treatment and medical and school laboratory construction projects.

I completed an executive MBA in international business from CSU Haywards's TEMBA (Transnational Executive MBA) program and studied and consulted in Brazil and Singapore. While in Brazil, I completed a successful U.S. market entry strategy project for a leading Brazilian children’s shoe company wishing to establish their brand in the highly competitive U.S. market. I previously worked as an Account Manager for SMC Corporation of America (SMCUSA.com) selling factory automation motion control productivity solutions to OEM and end users in the; CD/DVD, disc drive, food packaging equipment, telecomm, speciality machinery, medical equipment, device and semiconductor equipment companies in Silicon Valley. SMC is worldwide leader in the manufacture of industrial automation components and electronic controls based out of Tokyo, Japan. Previously, I held similar positions at Siemens (formerly Westinghouse) in the Power Generation Business Unit and at Controls for Automation in New England. I completed my BA in Psychology from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst.
