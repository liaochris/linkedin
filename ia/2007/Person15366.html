Celine Roche’s Summary

I received a PhD in organic chemistry and a master degree from the Ecole Nationale Supérieure de Chimie de Paris (ENSCP). I am currently working as a Post Doc in medicinal chemistry in England.

I have several years of experience in research. I got familiar with the pharmaceutical industry with two internships (Aventis, France then Boehringer Ingelheim, Germany) which encouraged me to do a thesis with a fellowship from Sanofi-Aventis. My work about peptide synthesis has been protected with patents. Over the last year I confirmed my interest for medicinal chemistry with my post-doctoral position, where I am taking part in the design and synthesis of anticancer drugs. These experiences have provided me with scientific skills, and also given me the opportunity to work closely with academic as well as industrial staff, to supervise trainees and PhD students, and to develop the required skills to manage a research project with autonomy in an international context.

I particularly appreciate the multidisciplinar aspect of medicinal chemistry. I am looking for a position in R&D.
