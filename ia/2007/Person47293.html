Kenneth Chia’s Summary

I joined AMD in Jan 2005 as a fresh graduate. My primary role as system software developer supporting a manufacturing facility requires me to develop, maintain and provide bring-up support for all manufacturing grade platform BIOS for a wide variety of AMD processor products and internal manufacturing platforms.

I have worked on platform BIOS projects for the AMD K7, AMD Athlon 64, AMD Opteron, AMD Turion and AMD Sempron range of single/dual core products. Additionally I am also involved in platform BIOS development projects for AMD's next generation processor products such as the Quad-core Barcelona processors.

Exposure to a wide range of platform BIOS projects has given me practical development experience and knowledge in register level chipset and processor programming on both AMI Core 8, Phoenix Technologies and Award based BIOS. Working in this field itself has provided me with good working knowledge of general x86 based system architectures and related technologies such as PCI, PCI Express, Hypertransport, SMPs etc.

My ultimate career goal is to be a technical leader and manager in this field.
