PAOLA FIORE’s Summary

Paola Fiore is an Italy-based professional freelance. Indipendent Environmental Consultant & Business Communication Project Manager since June 2005, she started her parallel career in professional photography and journalism in June 2003, after graduating from the Newport School of Art, Media and Design (http://artschool.newport.ac.uk/welcome.html) of the University of Wales College Newport (www3.newport.ac.uk) in Great Britain,
with a honours degree in the BA (Hons) Documentary Photography Course, established by Magnum photographer David Hurn in the late '70s.

In November 2003 she won the first prize in the section 'Best Project' of the Italian Edition of the Canon Young Photographers Competition, and in May 2004 she was among the finalists selected by the Jury of the European Award for Women Photographers.

In September 2004 she was awarded a Scholarship by Piedmont Region and TPW Toscana Photographic Workshop to take part in the three-year photographic project 'Focus on Monferrato'.

Paola Fiore is particularly committed to work on environmental communication projects, which document and report about social changes and environmental issues. She is available for cultural commissions and editorial documentary projects on every continent to Private Corporations, Public Institutions and Non Governmental Organizations.
