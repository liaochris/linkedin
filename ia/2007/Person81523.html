Heather McDougall’s Summary

I am an expert recruiter with extensive human resource experience and knowledge. I have developed a solid reputation for targeting passive and hard to reach candidates and am skilled at presenting them to hiring managers at all organizational levels. My gift lies in relationship building which gives me that 6th sense about what type of person an organization needs to grow their company - and where to find that person.
