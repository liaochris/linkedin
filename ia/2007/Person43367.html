Jomo Starke’s Summary

Jomo “Joe” Starke has over 18 years of experience in the sales, delivery and project management of enterprise consulting and software engagements (onshore and offshore) to the healthcare industries. He has worked throughout the U.S. and abroad, but maintains his closest professional relationships with C-Suite executives in the Southwest U.S. markets (California, Texas, Nevada, Arizona, New Mexico). Mr. Starke maintains domain expertise in business intelligence and analytics, and has been involved with business intelligence software and services delivery for ten years. Further, he has deep domain expertise in healthcare, primarily in the payer and provider mid-market space (MSOs, IDNs, IPAs, Medical Groups), as well as government business with Medicare, Medicare Advantage and Medicaid.

Mr. Starke has managed technology alliances for large, global technology firms and for startups, and has relationships with most of the top-tier enterprise software and consulting firms. Further, Mr. Starke has extensive experience with healthcare technology, and spent the first half of his career doing financial modeling and strategic business planning for a number of provider organizations. Finally, he has recently been party to the successful sale/divestiture of three technology firms (InAlysys, Extreme Logic, OAO Healthcare Solutions); he maintains relationships with several prominent investment banks.

Mr. Starke currently is Senior Account Executive and Healthcare Industry Lead for speakTECH, an Microsoft Gold Certified partner focused on delivering solutions for demanding enterprise environments and growing small businesses. speakTECH helps companies reduce risk, reduce cost, increase revenues and increase profitability by building specialized solutions based on Microsoft products.
