Brian Danzis’s Summary

In this ever changing broadband world, my goal, as Director of Advertising Sales for Joost, is to pioneer new marketing solutions for clients and generate meaningful revenue for my company.

We are at a watershed moment where technology, media and marketing are converging and thus changing the way people connect with and consume entertainment and advertising messages. CMOs are increasingly holding their agencies more accountable for more ideas and deeper innovation...with measurable results. Similarly, users are tuning out commercials that clutter, intrude and annoy while content owners struggle with how to give these audiences access to more and more content, whenever and wherever.

By incubating an ecosystem where content owners, advertisers and users can interact and have relevant conversations, each constituency will thrive and gain valuable new insights into another. In developing and deploying a sophisticated advertising platform that facilitates these emotional connections, we can achieve a harmonious synergy that truly unleashes a new kind of television experience.
