Julie Shilane’s Summary

I earned a B.S. in Chemistry and Mathematics from Moravian College in Bethlehem, PA. As an undergraduate, I participated in several research projects that included statistical analysis of experimental data and mathematical modeling of enzyme reactions. Since then, I have been working on my Ph.D. in Chemistry at Princeton University. My thesis project is the design and synthesis of a novel cancer therapeutic. I am interested in a career that utilizes my experience with data analysis and my interest in the pharmaceutical industry, thus I am looking for a pharmaceutical analyst position.
