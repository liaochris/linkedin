Stacey Stockman, is the Founder and President of Synergistic Solutions. Her entry into the Recruiting industry in 1999, included having spent her formative business years working as an Applications Developer, Business Analyst and ultimately as an Operations Manager for a Leo Burnett owned web development firm.

"We understand you. Our Teams offer direct experience within the areas they support. Real simply, this helps us truly understand what you do since chances are we have been there."

Ms. Stockman incorporates her hands on business and technical skills, with nearly a decade of highly specialized recruitment training, including her work as a Division Director with Robert Half International. Synergistic Solutions has represented a variety of clients, both big and small, supporting the efforts of Travelocity, EMC, and PriceWaterhouseCoopers as well as many other Fortune 500 companies.

"Today we are happy to say that 90% of our business is through referral."

Ms. Stockman has a B.A. in Psychology and Philosophy from Shimer College including advanced studies at Oxford University, Balliol College. She volunteers at St. Mary's Hospital in West Palm Beach and has been an active member of both the National Association of Woman Business Owners and the National Association of Female Executives since 1994. Stacey's personal life consists of meditation, yoga, surfing, travel and enjoying the antics of her dachshund puppy Zoey.
