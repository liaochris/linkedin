Sev K-H Keil’s Summary

Sev is an expert in e-business with a background in marketing, quantitative methods, and information technology. Sev has served as a director of the Center on Global Brand Management at Columbia Business School, the University of Munich, and CEIBS Shanghai; as a Visiting Scholar at Yale University’s School of Management; and as a visiting professor at various EMBA programs. He has consulted and taught extensively on e-business, customer relationship management, customer satisfaction, and strategy in Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and the United States. He is a member of numerous Boards of Directors in the US and Europe. For more information, please visit www.truechoicesolutions.com

Sev K-H Keil’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy, CRM, customer focus systems, econometric analysis, preference measurement, TQM, Venture Capital

Sev K-H Keil’s Experience

  • Chairman & CEO

    TrueChoice Solutions, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    October 2006 — Present (9 months)

    Headquartered in New York, TrueChoice Solutions is a leading on-demand service provider that generates unique insights into 1:1 customer preferences --- all in real-time and for instant, measurable sales / ROI impact. Used by a growing number of FORTUNE 500 clients, the TrueChoice® Preference Technology Suite is based on patented econometric technologies that for the first time allow companies to precisely quantify the 'how' and 'why' individual customers and employees make decisions. TrueChoice Solutions, Inc. has offices in New York, Houston, San Francisco, Amsterdam, Duesseldorf and Sydney.

  • Managing Partner

    PL Venture Management GmbH

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

  • Chief Executive Officer

    Blue Flame Data, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry)

    April 2000 — September 2006 (6 years 6 months)

    Blue Flame Data is an information infrastructure firm that provides unique insights into customer preferences. Blue Flame is the commercial realization of more than 15 years of research in the areas of preference measurement and marketing science conducted by Prof. Dick R.Wittink and Sev K-H Keil at Stanford, Cornell and Yale.

    Blue Flame’s TrueChoice™ Preference Technology solutions are based on novel econometrics that for the first time allow companies to precisely quantify the preference structures of individual customers and employees. Blue Flame’s game-style interface gathers real-time data that is fed into sales, CRM, HR, or BI systems, where it is instantaneously analyzed and used for bottom-line impact. As a result, clients achieve immediate, measurable increases in profitability. Blue Flame is particularly effective in fields with complex, explanation-intensive products/services such as auto, telco, finance, hospitality, HR, and pharma.
