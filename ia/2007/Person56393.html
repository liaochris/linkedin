Matthew Loew’s Summary

I currently lead application engineering, technical marketing and customer service activities for Comet Solutions, a software development company that provides solutions that accelerate innovation by enabling product simulation and simulation driven design. In joining Comet, I bring to my role over 10 years direct customer experience in multidisciplinary engineering analysis and design.

I joined Comet Solutions from Stewart & Stevenson Vehicle Services, Inc. (now part of Armor Holdings), where I led modeling and simulation-based projects and drove efforts to initiate a systems engineering approach for the design of future vehicle platforms to aggressive performance specifications. My engineering career has had me responsible for project planning and execution for engineering-based CAD models, vehicle dynamics simulations, advanced powertrain simulations, and strategic supplier relationships. I was previously employed in the automotive industry by a Tier 1 chassis component manufacturer as both an engineering analyst and a project engineer and by a small cutting-edge research and development company focused on advanced, light-weight structures and components as a project engineer.

I am a frequent presenter at PTC/User (5 years straight!) and serve on their Simulation and Behavioral Modeling Technical Committee. I also frequent the Eng-Tips engineering forum (www.eng-tips.com) and MCADCentral (www.mcadcentral.com). I do a lot of snooping through my network on this site as well.

One of my life's goals is to participate in the Dakar Rally. This is a grueling 3-week event that starts in Europe, treks across North East Africa, into the Sahara, and ultimately finishes in Dakar, Senegal's capital. I have been a fan since I was a child.

I am always trying to connect with others that have similar goals and experiences. Feel free to send me an invitation (matthewloew @ gmail dot com).
