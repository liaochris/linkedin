Dennis Shaffner’s Summary

I am an arts advocate regardless of the industry in which I may work. Human education innately comes from and is motivated by the arts, all of the creative arts. By including creative people in every work force the employer always benefits and should tap the creative thinking we offer.

To be globally competitive one must have a broad perspective on today's culture of speaking an international language, the arts. The arts instantly communicate,often the spoken word does not; however, the dialog produced through a visual stimuli can open a universal market.

I take this point of view to anywhere I work. There are unlimited possibilities using a global perspective in business today.
