Carl Lens’s Summary

I specialize in the ways that organizations can involve consumers in the Innovation Process. I am researching this subject on a scientific basis (MscBA, E-Business), as well as on a commercial (CreativeCrowds.Com) level. You can read more about my findings on www.crowdsourcingdirectory.com.

CreativeCrowds is a venture that teaches organizations to embrace the ideas of consumers. One of our projects is CreativeCapitals.Org a foundation that aims to boost the free flow of ideas between the most creative cities in Europe.
