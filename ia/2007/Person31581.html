George Toscano MBA gtoscano2005@yahoo.com’s Summary

A seasoned pharmaceutical professional with over twelve years experience in the pharmaceutical industry in various capacities ranging from product development, product management to regulatory compliance matters and quality assurance. Involved in early stage product development from product conception, formulation development to Invitro/Invivo correlation studies to generate bioavailability (dissolution/release rate) profile in support of prospective products. Particular expertise in generic drug applications (ANDA) in bioequivalence studies for generic drug applications. Well versed in accelerated stability data requirements to ultimately support ANDA applications.

My experience ranges full cycle from ANDA's to full NDA's involving Phase I, II, III and IV clinical studies for supporting drug safety and efficay. Considerable experience in the ANDA process and paragraph IV filings for generic drugs and preparation of CMC sections to support ANDA and NDA filings for Transdermal and Solid oral dose pharamceuticals. Additional experience in the OTC market for liquid dose pharmaceuticals. Experience also includes 505(b) 2 filings.
