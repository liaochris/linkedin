Rick Mahn’s Summary

Rick is a 20 year Information Technology professional who has learned the art of Personal Brand through his own career. Now a writer as well as an IT Infrastructure Architect, Rick is bringing New Media ideas to traditional IT consulting. Currently Rick owns his own technology consulting firm, Avatar Consultants, where he works with small to medium sized businesses to prepare infrastructure for continuous growth. A self-described technology evangelist, Rick also writes about Personal Branding, Corporate Blogging, and Mobile Lifestyle on a number of websites. Recognized as a thought leader in Personal Branding, Rick has been written about in several articles, and contributes articles to Personal Branding Magazine.
