Carl "Ned" Flottman’s Summary

In a twenty eight year professional supply chain career I have implemented and managed corporate P-Card systems and purchasing systems, hired and trained staff, negotiated and managed contracts for a variety of hardware, software, and services; managed both on and off-site inventories; and improved systems resulting in operating efficiencies and cost reductions. My experience includes companies ranging from multi-national to small start ups.

My goal is to build a purchasing department that is a strategic advantage in a company that values honesty and teamwork.

Carl "Ned" Flottman’s Specialties:

My areas of expertise include supplier selection, supplier development, supplier rationalization, negotiations, inventory planning and management, RFx creation, inventory planning, sourcing, and contract creation and evaluation. I am proficient in Microsoft Word, Access, Excel, Word, and Powerpoint, as well as Lotus Notes, Approach, and 123, and have used Oracle, SAP, and PeopleSoft enterprise systems.
