Mitch Joel’s Summary

Marketing, Communications, Sales and Interactive expert, Mitch loves being an entrepreneur. To this day, he’s an avid tech junkie, public speaker, Blogger and believer in doing the impossible.

Mitch owned one of the first publications to go online in 1994. Mitch’s online marketing skills helped build one of the Internet’s largest web properties. Mitch was also the Director of Marketing for a world-leader of wireless entertainment. Mitch then proceeded to become an Account Director for a Public Relations agency. Now, as a President of Twist Image, Mitch develops brand strategies and digital marketing campaigns for global companies.

Mitch is an active volunteer with many not-for-profit organizations. In 2004, Mitch was awarded The MS Leadership Award, presented by the Multiple Sclerosis Society of Canada (Quebec Division) to people whose professional and social achievements have contributed to the growth of their businesses and the development of Quebec society.

Mitch is a public speaker for The Power Within where he has shared the stage with Dr. Phil, Mark Burnett, Po Bronson, Herb Cohen, Loretta La Roche and many more. He is a guest lecturer at McGill University, Concordia University and Youth Employment Services.

Mitch is the founder/moderator of the Montreal Business Book Club and also a certified self-defense coach. Mitch’s company, Twist Image, was included in Bull Market, marketing master Seth Godin’s directory/e-book identifying the top 500 creative/marketing firms in the world. Mitch is Vice Chair Quebec, IAB Canada (Interactive Advertising Bureau) and sits on the eMarketing Council for the CMA (Canadian Marketing Association) as well as on the Quebec Board for NABS (National Advertising Benevolent Society). He has been featured in Marketing Magazine, InfoPresse, The Montreal Gazette, La Presse, National Post, Globe & Mail and more.

In 2006, Mitch was identified as one of the most influential authorities on Blog Marketing in the world.
