Brooks Pettus’s Summary

Ten years + in the electronic dance music space. Produced DJ centric events around the world. Best of: the Spundae Labor Day event in 1999 at Ten15 in San Francisco, the Delta Heavy event in Denver, The Chems Live / Sasha & Digweed / Oakenfold at the Cow Palace, the PlayStation DualPlay Tours, the Tiesto in Concert series in the United States in 2005.

Joined Beatport in 2006. Beatport is the leading digital distribution channel for dance music.

In my old life, I was a currency trader and occasional motorcycling journalist.
