Keith Moore’s Summary

An Executive at LendingTree, LLC responsible for its Lending Exchange, which is currently made up of over 400+ unique lenders nationwide, using the LendingTree and GetSmart brands to generate both Long Form and Short Form (also called LendingTree QuickMatch) consumer qualification forms for Mortgage Loans, Personal Loans, Auto Loans, Commercial Loans, Student Loans, and more.

Since joining LendingTree, the company has ranked as the #1 media advertiser online (according to TNS media intelligence - http://www.clickz.com/3626867). Major initiatives have included a complete redesign of the GetSmart.com website and consumer forms, implementing Lead Quality programs that have significantly increased contact ratios and closing ratios across all marketing channels (w/ TargusInfo, QA processes, & RapidResponse Lender program), and launching the new LendingTree QuickMatch product to be the mortgage industry leader for Short Form leads.

Previously an SVP of Marketing in the Mortgage and Insurance industry, responsible for building profitable, direct response marketing operations that ranked as one of the top overall media advertisers online, ahead of well-known / branded competitors with less than a fourth of their marketing budget.

Prior to this role, I was fully responsible for building and marketing the Internet's first homeowners insurance instant quotes (w/ full online underwriting and policy binding), while running the supporting operations for internal and external agents to easily write and bind policies over the Internet, through our company owned call center and through remote independent/local agents.
