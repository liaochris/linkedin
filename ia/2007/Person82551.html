Steven Ericsson-Zenith’s Summary

Dear Reader,

After many years in industry I have returned to research and development, to science. I continue to apply the results of that effort to problems I know well in high technology.

I am focused on the explanation of experience in nature, the foundations of logic and apprehension, and the development of advanced technology that exploits successful theories - solving the recognition problems and identifying new solutions to sensory differentiation and motility.

I am finishing an academic book on this subject entitled "Explaining Experience in Nature." I am also beginning to assemble a popular science series called "What It All Means" on the associated historical narrative that documents the development of epistemology.

I am fundraising and recruiting for IASE. Our goal is to establish a world-class international institute of scholarship, science and engineering focused on the explanation of experience in nature. Our scientific mission is to integrate a theory of sentience with other theories of nature and to develop technologies based on successful theories.

With respect,
Steven
