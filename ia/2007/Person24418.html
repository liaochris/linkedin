Don Bahash’s Summary

The IBM Business Value Assessment team's focus is to work with our customers to develop solid business cases for IBM Software Group (SWG) Industry Solutions with foundations built on information warehousing and entity analytic solutions.

Don has over twenty five years of experience in information systems, data warehousing, and applications development, and has in-depth knowledge in the application of database technologies to support clients' operational and analytical customer centric initiatives. This expertise was developed working over a wide range of industries including telecommunications, utilities & energy services, media, insurance, finance, health, travel, transportation, retail, state government, and automotive.

Don joined IBM in 1984 and has been working with relational databases and business intelligence solutions to solve clients' business problems across multiple industry verticals. Since joining IBM, Don has held various positions including large systems and database technical sales support, certified IT Architect, Consultant, and Principal in the IBM Consulting Group, now IBM Global Services, competitive database sales, and business intelligence solutions manager. Don joined the WW SWG Industry Solutions team in January 2006.

Don holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science.
