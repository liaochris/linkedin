Andrea Brogi’s Summary

Andrea Brogi is an architect that receveid his Honor Degree at The Faculty of Architecture in Florence, Italy.
Since his training studies he experimented, at a very personal level, the potentiality of digital techiniques and 3D computer graphics to plan the architecture of urban landscape.
Actually he is Contractor Professor at the Polytechnic of Milan with a course of 3D Animation and at the University of Milan with a course of Virtual Reality.
Aside the traditional work as architect he has been working with the most important italian institution on Information and Communication Technologies for cultural heritage as digital architect to develop interactive system of Virtual Reality (ISTI-CNR; CRIBECU Lab. of Advanced Normal Schooll; PERCRO Lab. of Sant’Anna School of Advanced Study; SIBA Lab. of University of Lecce).
He is also subcontractor in two European project: ViHAP3D – Virtual Heritage: High Quality 3D Acquisition and Presentation and DHX – Digital Ecological and Artistic Heritage Exchange.
He direct the production of several 3D graphic video for advertising, music and institution demoreel and developed some pilot project for 3D cartoon tv series one of wich commisioned by Rai, the italian national broadcasting service.
Using MoCap system he plan and develope 3D Real Time digital events and interactive installations with digital characters and he is developing a theatrical performance in collaboration with Academy of Dramatic Art of Rome and the company FORTEBRACCIO TEATRO using multiscreen projection scene and the interaction between the actress and the digital scene.
