H. Martin Calle III’s Summary

Martin Calle is the marketing mind behind the development of such famous brands as Chunky Soup, Tic Tac Mints, NyQuil, Baked Lays Potato Chips, Pampers Disposable Diapers, Cold-filtered Miller Genuine Draft and Tylenol Gelcaps. Fueling turnarounds at companies such as Procter & Gamble, Johnson & Johnson and General Motors, Martin Calle has been dubbed a "heavy hitter" by consumer product industry leaders for more than 35 years. The Chairman of his own international strategic marketing organization, Calle & Company, Martin's practice envisions cutting-edge innovation processes that deliver rich, robust and proprietary consumer insights to big companies, ad agency executives, consultants and top business schools. As a public speaker, Martin shares humor, motivation, leadership development, strategic secrets, tips and tricks to students and open minded marketing, sales and advertising professionals on-their-way-up. No charge. Just ask.
