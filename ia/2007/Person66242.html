Patrick Lor’s Summary

An entrepreneurial business professional with expertise in the areas of strategy, operations management and marketing, Patrick has coached many companies to success in his career to date. He has a special interest in helping e-commerce businesses tackle critical issues including distribution, product development, branding, customer experience, and data mining.

His latest venture was iStockphoto.com, where he led the company in the areas of strategy, finance, IT, marketing, and business development in his role as EVP and director. He joined iStockphoto pre-revenue, and upon his departure, the company was selling over 12 million images annually. iStockphoto became the world’s most successful web 2.0 stock photography company, and was sold to Getty Images, a NYSE company, for US$50,000,000 in February of 2006.
