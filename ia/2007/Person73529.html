Rob Hubert’s Summary

Rob Hubert, Founder and Chief Talent Officer/Director of Technical Recruiting for Elite IT Services, has a strong background in IT Career and Leadership Development. He is presently the author of two e-books, “How to Find the Ideal Information Technology Job” and “Twenty One Secrets to Advancing your IT Career”. Rob has developed Elite’s Talent Management Process that includes the careful selection of the best IT Professionals and matches them with the best Career Opportunity. Much of Rob’s success is attributed to his intuitive listening skills and career coaching skills. Elite IT Services is one of the only companies that provides an Open Personal Coaching Program for IT Professionals.

Rob also remains current on technical innovations. He has a unique background and level of expertise in middleware, application integration technologies and enterprise information technology architecture. Rob’s technical expertise is attributed to his prior 12-year tenure at A.G. Edwards, Inc., where he implemented many new technologies and managed a team of highly skilled technical resources responsible for creating infrastructure and applications to support the firm’s business operations. As his career progressed at AGE, he became the Manager of the Integration Services team responsible for Enterprise Application Integration and Middleware Technologies across all platforms.

Rob started his IT career in 1986 at May Department stores as a Computer Operator. He worked his way through the IT Enterprise, from Application Developer to System Programmer, onto Manager of the Integration Services team at A.G. Edwards. His hard work and technical expertise paid off as he was promoted to Associate Vice President in 1996 and continually held positions of increasing responsibility. Rob left AGE., to pursue his entrepreneurial interests as founder of Elite IT Services, an Information Technology Consulting firm specializing in Project Staffing and Consulting.
