Jeremy Sisson’s Summary

I am an internet developer with experience in solving problems through the use of online technologies and applications. I enjoy exploring the many ways of using these online technologies to improve communication, usually via social media or community platforms.

In February 2000 I joined Hotship Inc. as Lead Developer, and worked there until it closed during the dot com bust. Shortly after, I joined the design company Area 50 Inc. in Burlington and worked with companies like Whirlpool Canada and Moneris to develop online applications and content management systems.

In February 2006 I moved to BorderWare Technologies Inc. to help develop the new embedded interface for the Firewall and Mail Firewall products.

I have also been working with Interscope to provide sales, marketing, and customer experience management services to a network of lawyers in Canada.
