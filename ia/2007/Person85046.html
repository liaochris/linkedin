Derrick Daye’s Summary

Behold the power of trust. With it relationships are started - and kept, purchase decisions are made, sales multiply and brands flourish. At The Blake Project our sole focus is helping organizations create brands that build and sustain trust.

With renowned brand strategist Brad Vanauken (Author of Brand Aid) we help large and small growth oriented companies design, manage and build brands that drive profitable revenue growth through differentiated customer experiences.

Located in Rochester, New York, Tampa, Florida and Frankfurt, Germany we currently serve clients in North America, Europe and Asia.
