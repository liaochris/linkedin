Sabine Lengger’s Summary

During my professional education and experience in the fields of chemistry and biotechnology I could deepen my knowledge in the fields of biocatalysis / enzymology and surface chemistry. In addition to that I have a strong interest in languages and am fluent in German, English and Spanish, proficient in French and I have good skills in Japanese.

Thanks to several grants I got the chances to work and study in Spain, Svalbard and Japan. As the approaches to teaching and research differ greatly, I could experience all aspects of studying and research during these years.
