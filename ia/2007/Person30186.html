Frederick Faulkner IV’s Summary

Over the last ten years I've seen the Internet go from crazy animated graphics to high-end customizable interfaces. It's been amazing! The good news is that it is only going to get better for the end-user. I believe in connecting a company with their target market for mutual benefit. I also believe in not making foolish mistakes by attempting to implement an idea that is not well thought out.

I've worked for companies doing product development where margins and market share is on the line to a law firm where justice is served. Most recently I've worked my way through a non-profit that has allowed me to get my hands into a variety of projects and share my opinions on how we can make the organization better. I find myself currently in a position to help my organization maximize its efforts online. I do this by educating management and colleagues on the ins and outs of the Web and how our members are using it.

As I move from company to company, my personal goal is to leave it a better place than when I got there. Contributions are not only made in improving a bottom line, but the attitude you bring in making changes. There is always room for improvement and efficiency in an organization. Finding solutions to problems where everyone benefits is what I believe I do best.
