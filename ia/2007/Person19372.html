Chris Vertonghen’s Summary

Chris brings more than 20 years of software development and engineering operations experience in roles as diverse as CEO, CTO, general manager, project manager, systems architect/designer, software engineer, EDP-manager, teacher, journalist, in a wide variety of industries including banking, insurance, copyrights, news media, internet, transport & shipping, metals & mining, networked printing, color reprography, design, DTP, imaging and datawarehousing.

Prior to joining Pajamanation in February 2007, Chris was the founder and manager of several software companies, where he has been directly responsible for the development and integration of large software solutions for banking, finance, e-commerce, large-volume data-processing, reporting, records- and document management, library-, archiving- and scanning, and learning- and productivity enhancement.

Chris has traveled and worked across Europe, Asia and North America. He lives in Belgium with his wife and two daughters.
