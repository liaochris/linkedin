Julien Penard’s Summary

Konaxis Quality Control Department : The qualitative and quantitative guarantee of your goods before shipment from CHINA.

You wish to do some kind of business with China but have doubts as for the identity and the level of qualification of your supplier and are concerned by bad surprises : non-existent company or not qualified in the production of your products (unqualified personnel, obsolete machine, nonadequate infrastructure…) so let us proceed to a factory audit in order to ensure you of the qualification, competence, capacity, size and reactivity of your supplier.

Also, to limit the risks to receive goods non in conformity with what was initially envisaged in the terms of the contract and the schedule of conditions, our inspectors proceed on your behalf to quality controls before the departure of your goods from China. Therefore, You will be able to identify any problem before shipment and consequently take some corrective measures.

If you have any questions about it, please feel free to contact me.
