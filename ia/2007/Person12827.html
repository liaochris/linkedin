BRIAN ANDERSON’s Summary

If your organization needs an executive with extensive experience in the selection and recruitment of personnel, hiring achievers is indicative of his accomplishments. Selection, interviewing, recruiting and matching talent to the position has been a passion and vocation for over two decades for Brian Anderson. Brian understands that a true win- win is when your natural talents align, match and seamlessly fit with the work and skill set needed by an organization. Brian understands organizations can’t improve unless its people do.

Brian Anderson is a registered corporate coach, certified selection expert who also provides a suite of other services to his clients which include but are not limited to executive coaching, career coaching and retail consulting. He has developed sales training programs, implemented mystery-shopping programs and built visual management tools to enhance brand awareness. Brian is a skilled facilitator, a thought provoking speaker and has several published online articles. Before launching his own practice, Brian spent over two decades leading, coaching and building high performance teams.

Continuous improvement – "this key offers some of the best insurance for both your career and your organization. Kaizen (pronounced ky’ zen) is the relentless quest for a better way, for higher quality". That philosophy is a foundation I practice myself and build on with my clients.
