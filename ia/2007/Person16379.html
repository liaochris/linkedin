Christopher Cottle (chris.cottle@trafficmp.com)’s Summary

I've been working in the online marketing space, for a little over 3 years now. I have experience in everything from Search or (PPC) campaigns to geo targeted and local campaigns.

I've also worked within the organic search space, doing search optimization for businesses to increase their natural listings online, and creating content that works for their business needs and goals.

I now work for Traffic Market Place. We are a network of several thousand top tier publishers. We our also a publisher and own several thousand domains. We have roughly 100 sites up and running live, which we operate and promote our selves. We spend a little over 50 million a year on self promotion for these sites alone.

We currently have two vertically focused sites that we just launched in Q3 of 2007. www.medicinematch.com and www.homerenovationtoday.com. Both of these sites are meant to connect the consumer with the service or provider, that best suites their needs.

I specialize in the Health, Entertainment and Retail Verticals. I deal with Cost Per Lead (CPL), Cost Per Action (CPA), Cost Per Click (CPC) Cost Per Thousand Views (CPM), Display, Rich Media, Email, Co-Registration, Promotional Sites, Online Branding and Awareness Campaigns, Lead Generation Campaigns, Customer Generation Campaigns, Data Generation, Geo-Targeted Campaigns, Demo/Psycho-Targeting Campaigns, Contextual-Targeting Campaigns, Behavioral-Targeting Campaigns. I'm basically your one stop shop for your next customer or lead.
