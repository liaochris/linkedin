Ozzie Monge’s Summary

I have over 15 years of experience in business development, almost all of which was in the defense embedded electronics industry, both domestically and internationally. Yes, I am a reformed international arms dealer!

Presently, I am serving as the Vice President of Business Development for DFC Intelligence. It's a very refreshing change from my previous life, and I am thankful I have been given the opportunity to further develop and grow professionally in an entirely new market space.

Prior to joining DFC Intelligence, I maintained a business development consulting firm supporting small businesses seeking to work with large, prime contractors and the Federal Government.

During my days at Titan, I worked initially with shipboard combat systems. This was directly related to my Navy days when I was the Electronic Materials Officer onboard a guided missile cruiser. Through the years and through acquisitions, I earned additional responsibilities - I even inherited a whole division (which is what led to my promotion to Vice President), which was later folded into my parent division. Of all the "stuff" I worked with, my favorite had to be the real-time image processing hardware. It was dropped into my lap after an aquisition and I found a home for it in the then-emerging unmanned vehicle industry. I worked with ground control stations for all of the unmanned vehicles you see on CNN, as well as their associated motion imagery exploitation systems. I'd love to yell you more, but... I also served on the board of directors of the local chapter of the Association of Unmanned Vehicle Systems International.

I have a bit of a Quixotic streak. I have invested some of my time doing volunteer work for a variety of non-profits, and I have also served on the board of directors of a small, local arts-oriented non-profit.

I am a veteran of Operation Desert Shield/Storm. If you're foolish enough to ask about my sea/war stories, I'll tell them to you!
