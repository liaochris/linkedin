Randy Foliente’s Summary

15 years of experience in marketing, with a focus on brand-driven planning, management and warfare. Not a guru; just a student who enjoys the game. A glutton for emerging markets and disruptive technologies. At times, a cruel advisor to starry-eyed entrepreneurs and senior management. Proud to have been thrown out of some of the best boardrooms, basements, and garages on the planet (sometimes they even ask me back). Experience includes a diversity of b2b and consumer markets. Career story includes hard-won victories, remarkable failures and life lessons. Above all, it's been about great mentors, partners and colleagues.

Moved from Chicago to San Juan Capistrano, CA in 2001. Completed as chief marketing officer of giantLEAP, a wireless, software services company in Sept. '06. Back to part-time consulting for now (nutrigenomics industry), while chasing an ultralight, line-class fishing record. Dreaming of the next great American fishing lure; writing the definitive saltwater fishing book; and competing in pro tournaments so I can convince my wife we need a boat (short of agreeing to have children, I have exhausted all other possibilities).
