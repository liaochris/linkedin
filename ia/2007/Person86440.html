Todd Defren’s Summary

PR executive since 1992, working primarily with clients of all sizes in arenas such as online marketing & social media, enterprise software, security, wireless, and consumer tech:

Novell, BzzAgent, WebTrends, Shimano Corp., RSA Conference, UpShot Corp., Kaspersky Labs, Hitachi, Claria Corp., Cybertrust, Netezza, Quantum, FrontBridge, Plumtree Software, Wayport, Oakley Sunglasses, UGOBE, Cymfony, etc.

Creator of the first Social Media News Release (May 2006). This template was released openly to the PR/marcomm community, and was subsequently covered in BusinessWeek ("Teaching the Press Release a New Trick," July 2006). Within 6 months the template had been downloaded over 50,000 times.

For thoughts & strategies on "PR 2.0" and marketing measurement, please visit www.pr-squared.com (blog) or the agency's website, www.shiftcomm.com. Additionally, a running log of industry reaction to SHIFT's new-media PR approaches, as well as other PR 2.0 best practices, is online at del.icio.us/shift.communications.
