    legal marketing italia is a network for professionals involved in the marketing of law and accounting firms in Italy. It was founded in 2004 by Silvia Hodges with the idea that the individuals working to market legal and accounting services needed a forum to share and exchange ideas with each other.

    By providing a networking platform as well as continuing education, legal marketing italia supports its members in their professional development. Since 2004 we have been organising Legal Marketing Luncheons three times a year in Milan, in order to educate, promote and maintain a high standard of professionalism in the industry.

    Through its international affiliations with legal and professional service marketing associations,legal marketing italia members immediately gain access to a wealth of international resources.
