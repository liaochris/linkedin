Brian Giese’s Summary

Inspired. Inspirational. I suppose those two words sum it up. I've spent over 20 years working in every aspect of the sales and marketing industry—a career about which I am passionate.

I'm a seasoned veteran known for vision, accelerating profitability, and increasing client satisfaction. I foster a culture of excellence within the walls of my clients and am expert on the how-to’s of building executive relationships. And as a result, my clients read like a who’s who in the technology marketing world. I've served as a marketing and sales performance consultant for Novell, Cisco, Sun, Oracle, NEC, Agilent Technologies, D&B, Harte-Hanks, Salesforce.com and The Corporate Executive Board among others.
