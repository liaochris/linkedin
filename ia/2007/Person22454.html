Dean Prelazzi’s Summary

Sales, Marketing, Business Development and Product Management leadership experience within the telecommunications, computing, and Internet software industries on both coasts of Canada (Vancouver and Ottawa) and the United States (Washington DC and Silicon Valley).

Veteran of the technology start-up environment;

Featured speaker at numerous technology trade events and associations including The TeleManagment Forum, The ASP Consortium, The Software Industry Information Association, and The National Investment Company Services Association.

MBA from University of Ottawa

Dean Prelazzi’s Specialties:

Certified in the 'Pragmatic Marketing' product management framework, consultative/strategic selling specialist, large account management, business plans, market analysis, market entry, new product introduction, strategic partnerships, corporate strategy transformations and change management.
