Alexis Antonelli’s Summary

Alexis has worked in the field of user interface design for 10 years. She's created solutions and deliverables for clients including Toyota, Verizon, T-Mobile, Yahoo! and Logitech while working with a variety of product types including web sites, software, hardware, CD-ROM, and on-line games. Passionate about true user-centered design, Alexis has designed and conducted usability testing and other user research studies to guide conceptual modeling and inform critical design decisions.

Alexis has focused her academic training on both business and human factors. She is skilled at negotiating conflicting project needs while educating about UX best practices. Her goal is always to help create desirable, useful and easy to use products that make good business sense.

Contact her at Alexis_Antonelli@yahoo.. with freelance, contract and business venture opportunities.
