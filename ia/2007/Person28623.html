Fabio Troni’s Summary

Fabio Troni has a strong academic background in corporate finance, financial modeling, accounting and equity valuation. He has demonstrated a strong interest for investment analysis by passing the fist two levels of the CFA examination at the first attempt and by maintaining "A" grade level in each class at NYU (Financial Analysis Program).

He has analyzed a wide range of companies in the Apparel Retail and Manufacturing industries producing equity research reports and applying Discounted Cash Flows and Market Multiples valuation methodologies.

While in Europe, Fabio had the opportunity to intern within the Financial Planning Department at SCM Group, the world leader in the woodworking machinery and systems industry, operating in 120 countries and ½ billion euros revenues yearly. Fabio learned a great deal about accounting, reporting procedures, data collection and analysis.

Fabio is currently working at Goldman Sachs in the Global Control Department and is preparing for the upcoming CFA Level 3 exam and Series 7 examination.
