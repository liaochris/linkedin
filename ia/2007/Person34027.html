Hanna Stolt’s Summary

I am currently writing my thesis for the Swedisch School of Economics and am likely finish my studies of Entrepreneurship & Leadership by autumn 2008. I finished my Candidate degree in 2005 and did a 9-month project for Sanofi-Aventis as the final paper.

I have been working with a number of companies but for the last year, my main focus has been in building a solid network for Robus Design. In 2007 I made a hard decision to give up my company and continue and focus on my career at Accenture. The challenging work, possibilities for personal development and great working environment and colleagues were in the end more lucrative than working with a small-scale design office.

My work experience has been strongly project work based; cooperating and working with a large number of people in a multitude of different projects. I have a special interest in working on an international scale and I'm always up for a change of scenery when the work (or leisure) so requires. Challenges do not scare me, they inspire me!
