Michael Marshall [ mmarshall@nimbus-consultants.com ]’s Summary

I have in-depth knowledge of the movers and shakers in the Outsourcing, Management Consulting & Advisory markets. My contacts range from very senior global managing partners to personal assistants. I am open and transparent in my approach.

I have spent the last six years honing my executive search techniques. I can find that very niche skill set that you have been told 'does not exist'. I do not rely on just one approach and believe I offer an exceptional means of identifying, targeting and finding potential candidates for my client base.
