Charles Nouÿrit’s Summary

Consultant in strategy and digital business development since 1999, I work for French or international companies which seek to include/understand and seize emerging opportunities in the electronic media, Internet, remote services, electronic trade, Web 2.0, blogs and community.

Before devoting myself to consulting, in 1994, tired with my studies and after a broken knee which put a term to career as a sailor, I decide to put a term at my 3rd year of Economic Sciences to ASSAS. Well motivated by launching my career of Contractor rather than to choose the way of wage-earning. I take part, at 22 years, to the creation of Tone Company!, a telecoms company providing Callback service and prepaid telephone cards, transformed into an SA in the beginning of 97, to become Connect France. After this profitable experiment in the telecoms, we decide, my associates and I, to turn us towards Internet, by creating at the end of 97, the company Net ², which diffused online real-estate advertisements for more than 500 French agencies and was the largest networks in France. After a short passage at Machination, I decide to create my own consulting company in 1999.

Since this date, I was brought to closely collaborate or by far with several well-known companies, such as Coca-Cola, Bouygues 6sens, Henkel, Hello-Restaurant, Cogedim, Motorola, Coframi, Inforama, SIECA, ALogic, Rosebud, AligaStore, Musée Louis Vuitton, Ralph Lauren World, Christian Dior, Camif, Thomas Pink, Théma SA, Givenchy Parfums, Veuve Clicquot, Château of Yquem, Hennessy, Prodotto Dell' Anno, the Club of Vigilant, Rouge, Wonderbra, Samsung, Philips, Vespa, Unilever, Web 2.0 Stratgy Consultant at Groupe Reflect,...
