Bennett Zucker’s Summary

Digital/interactive media and marketing pioneer and new media technology evangelist. Led electronic publishing ventures, networks and startups since 1990. Joined Real Media in 1998 as VP sales and marketing, advancing later to managing director for Latin America, responsible for sales of online advertising and digital marketing technology from offices in Miami, Mexico City and Sao Paolo.

Instrumental in evangelizing and creating new markets for interactive advertising technology, behavioral marketing and online media exchange platforms.

Have been publisher, marketer, ad director, and editor of magazines and books, as well as electronic and custom publications, with such companies as McGraw-Hill, Rodale, and Phillips.

Find me at the intersection of Code, Commerce and Content.
