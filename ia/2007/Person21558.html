David Hua’s Summary

Hua is an entrepreneur, marketer, and catalyst.

Currently, he is the VP of Marketing at Genesis Interactive (GI), a company that he helped found in 2006. In addition, he serves as the manager for an indie band called Downtown Harvest.

GI is launching GotGame, a matchmaking utility for online gamers. "Win More."

GI has raised $600,000 in seed capital through private financing and is currently securing our series A round through various angel networks.

Prior to working at GI, Hua, worked in the Latin American Equity Research Sales Department and was a salesman on the Institutional Foreign Exchange desk at Citigroup.

Hua is passionate about business and curious about many aspects of life. He hopes to inspire and learn from those he is connected with while spreading his positive attitude to make things happen.
