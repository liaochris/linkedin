Michael Dunn’s Summary

Michael Dunn

D U N N
888 224-0304

www.dunnbrand.com

An internationally regarded brand-focused business consultantancy and evolving ideas boutique, D U N N is committed to providing innovative insights, wise brand counsel and cutting-edge creative work to a diverse roster of American and International clients.

Michael has been contributing remarkable insights, provocative ideas and deep experience on both the client and agency sides of the business for 15+ years. He has been Vice President of Marketing and Merchandising in the fashion industry, Director of Account Planning at a national direct marketing firm, as well as Founder and Executive Creative Director of a successful full-service, brand-focused advertising agency in San Francisco. A creative thinker who knows the importance of a sound strategic foundation, Dunn has built his reputation by working with a diverse roster of leading advertising agencies, Fortune 1000 companies and innovative smaller enterprises. He has helped companies from the newest to the most established successfully assert their brand and product positions in an increasingly competitive and noisy global marketplace. Always the curious and perceptive observer, Michael is able to draw from his many interests and abilities to generate the sort of surprising, innovative ideas that are simply a necessity for breaking through the clutter of today’s crowded media landscape. He is also an acclaimed speaker, business writer and opinionist in the popular press.

Having traveled extensively throughout the United States and Europe, he knows a bit about other cultures, too. Michael volunteers his time as a mentor, tries to keep up with his never-ending pursuit of knowledge and aspires to be a philosopher-poet.
