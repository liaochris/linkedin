Conrad Saam’s Summary

Avvo is a new Seattle-based Internet company that is revolutionizing the way consumers navigate the highly confusing legal industry. The company is led by three of Seattle's most successful Internet executives and funded by Benchmark Capital and Ignition Partners. We are aggressively hiring a world class team.

Mr. Saam has served as a consultant to major consumer brands, assisting them to adapt their online marketing strategy to meet the changing market, technology, and regulatory environment of the internet. His experience includes numerous awarding-winning interactive projects for clients including Disney, Nickelodeon, Lego, MTV, Ford, Macromedia, Microsoft, Kraft Foods, AOL, McDonalds, Mattel and Napster. He has been intimately involved in the progression of Flash, the deployment of web analytics, and statistical testing methodologies for online marketing optimization. Mr. Saam holds an MBA from the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan. He is a sought-after speaker on interactive and service marketing and has been featured in USA Today, the New York Times and Strategy Magazine.
