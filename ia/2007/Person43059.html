John Hill’s Summary

After a shortish time in the military I took what I had learnt about leadership and people into the world of suits and ties. Rank Xerox trained me as a salesman and the computer industry allowed me to get the bug of selling more and more complex (especially CRM) systems.
Job hunting gave me the arrogance to believe I could do better than 90% of the recruiters I met - I have since discovered how difficult it is - and while I believe that I add value I have sympathy for those others who don't.
One day I read Rich Dad Poor Dad and gained the courage to put what I had always believed into practice.

Now every day I remind myself that self employed is still employed and look for the goal of passive income - (that's not Network Marketing)

Apart from that my life is balanced by being Director of Rugby at the University of Reading in England and coaching a fantastic bunch of motivated players. To help me with this I am currently working towards my Level 3 coaching award and hold the (level 3) SAQ International diploma.
