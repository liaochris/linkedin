Wayne Porter’s Summary

Co-founder of ReveNews.com one of the top three Alexa ranked collaborative blogs where I serve on the Board of Directors. Former CEO and co-founder of XBlock Systems, a specialized research firm on malware and spyware acquired by Facetime Communications in 2005.

Other projects include the SpywareGuide.com property and the X-RayPC Process Analyzer. Recent work includes working with the Federal Trade Commission to help shape spyware policy, moderating the New York Marketing Summit to develop Code of Conduct guidelines and rules of engagement for adverting based software. Development includes the co-creation of two patent-pending technologies to contain unwanted software on corporate networks.

I am a frequent speaker at e-commerce business events. I currently work for FaceTime Security Labs, a division of Facetime Communications, Inc, the world's leading IM security company, as the Senior Director of Greynets Research.
