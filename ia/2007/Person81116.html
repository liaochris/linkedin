Samuel Prasad, Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Prasad is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) who specializes in maximizing the "Return On Investment" (ROI) of technology projects by optimizing software usability and compressing the time it takes to go from concept to production using an agile function-driven project management methodology in concert with a combination of stateside and offshore development resources.
