William Wilson’s Summary

In practice since October of 1991 concentrating in civil litigation. Licensed in Indiana, I am also admitted to practice before the Supreme Court of the United States, the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit, and the U.S. District Courts in Indiana and Chicago. I have worked on cases at every level, from small claims court to the Supreme Court of the U.S.

My goal is to deliver cost-effective service to help clients achieve their goals.

In addition to practicing and teaching law, I teach wine appreciation at Indiana University South Bend. I'd be happy to expand this teaching to restaurants, businesses, or other groups who would like to learn about wine.

William Wilson’s Specialties:

Family law; copyright and trademark law; entertainment law; bankruptcy and creditors' rights law; construction law, adoptions. Wine education.
