Kord Campbell’s Summary

Associates call me a serial entrepreneur, but I swear I've never killed any of my investors.

I currently wrangle geeks at Zoto, Inc., a photo hosting and sharing site that uses bleeding edge AJAX technology to let you manage your photos online. Think iPhoto on the Web.

I also started and sold a search engine related company called Grub to LookSmart in 2003. Grub was a software ahead of its time, as is evident by Wikia's new efforts with community search.

If you need someone to understand the technology you are about to wield, I'm your guy. I know how to do everything from writing business plans to installing transactional databases. I can compose your revenue models and sniff your network for intruders in the same afternoon, and make all of it fit into a budget.

I’m a meat and potatoes guy - smart and simple is my philosophy. If I don’t know an answer to a question, I’ll admit as much and go find someone who does know, pick their brain, and figure out how that relates to the things I do know. If I know the answer, I’ll teach you what I know, and do it without hesitation.

For me, this is my dream - to create software people love to use. I love my job!

Kord Campbell’s Specialties:

Venture fund raising, business planning and development, revenue models, marketing, branding, acquisition, project planning and management, technology assessment, clustered server architectures, scalable web infrastructures, network implementation and design, load balancing, firewalls, routing, AJAX/Flash interface technologies, UI design, server administration, distributed databases, asynchronous application servers, distributed applications and various search engine technologies.
