Raphaël Fétique’s Education

  • HEC School of Management

    Master, Digital Business Management, 2006 — 2007 (expected)

    The Specialised Master’s Degree in Digital Business Management is the product of an alliance between HEC and ENST (Ecole Nationale Supérieure des Télécommunications). Each of these institutions is a landmark in its field. Both schools have decided to unite their strengths to offer a management training programme geared towards innovation management and enhancing companies’ integration of information technology.
    The purpose of the HEC-ENST programme is to train managers able to face this challenge through a twofold competency based on their:
    - Mastery in management issues, enabling them to work in a primary corporate function, such as Strategy, Project management, Marketing, Sales, Purchasing, Finance, or Human Resources.
    - Knowledge of information technology issues, potential and tools, which they can then integrate into organisational policies so as to develop a portfolio of modern management practices.
