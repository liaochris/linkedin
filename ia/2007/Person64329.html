Norrie Craig’s Summary

With over 20 years experience leading and directing the IT function, I have a track record in developing and implementing IT Strategies and innovation that deliver real business benefit. I am very comfortable operating at Director and Executive levels, with experience spanning a broad range of Business sectors (Telecoms, Airline, Public Sector, Dot Com, Financial Services, Software House, Manufacturing) and, commercially aware, have very well developed business planning and budget skills.

I have a very strong Customer and Delivery focus, and am an expert in envisioning, evolving and championing the "Big Picture" - with passion, and high levels of energy, commitment and determination. Seeking an edge, I challenge the accepted and am fully prepared to take (and manage) risks where Customers and the Business will benefit most.

My Man Management experience is extensive, having managed multi disciplined, multi site teams in excess of 200 staff, built entire departments from scratch (including start-ups), restructured, managed framework (external) suppliers, been heavily involved in change and reorganisation programs, and managed mergers.

I have built up considerable experience in Interim Management, and am adept at very quickly identifying opportunities and exploiting them, and addressing issues, concerns and problems, and rectifying them.

I am committed to playing a key a pivotal role at a senior level on a permanent, long term basis with a fast moving and/or start-up company who are both customer focused and place a very high value on their staff. I am fully prepared to commute or re-locate, even abroad, for the right opportunity.
