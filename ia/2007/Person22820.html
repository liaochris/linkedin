Antoine Denaiffe’s Summary

Corporate governance expert focused on shareholder value creation with broad experience in:

i) providing Corporate Governance Advisory services to issuers:
Functional audit of Corporate Governance practices, Implementation of “best in class” Governance practices, Assistance to management in their relationships with “activist” shareholders/raiders, Optimising/coordinating communication and lobbying strategies, Stabilising shareholding structure
Shareholder value creation/re-rating strategy, Acting as a sparing partner
Anticipating market and stakeholders reactions on key issues;

ii) assisting investment funds, and private shareholders in the monitoring of their minority stakes in listed companies, in the framework of: Public offers and M&A related transaction, Maximising conditions offered, Protecting their interest,
Screening/analysing « weak Governance » practices,Advising on Governance improvement strategy.
