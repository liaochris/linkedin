Mauricio "Maltron" Leal’s Summary

I have over five years experience in corporate mobile software development using J2ME and J2EE, including hands-on development and project coordination. My projects include implementations based on Nextel (iDEN) and Motorola technologies with Public and Cargo Transportation companies.

Additionally, I have considerable experience delivering presentations and speeches to large audiences on behalf of Sun Microsystems and SouJava User Group conferences. Presentations include topics such as JavaTV, J2ME, JXTA, Jini, Swing and Profiling.

My experience includes multiple international engagements. I delivered the first web based applications used by SKF and Deutsch Telekom in Germany. Also, I helped Denmark based Responsfabrikken expand into the Brazilian market.
