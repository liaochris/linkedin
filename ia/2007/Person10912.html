Ramneek Bhasin’s Summary

Ramneek Bhasin founded Mobio Networks, Inc. -- a venture backed company focused on the next generation of consumer wireless services -- in 2005.

Most recently, Ramneek was Entrepreneur-in-Residence at Storm Ventures, focused on communications, wireless and internet software.

Previously, Ramneek was CEO and co-founder of Vialto Corp. and successfully led Vialto’s efforts in its acquisition by Cisco Systems Inc.

In 1994, Ramneek cofounded WSB Technologies, a leading SI and Technology Management Consulting firm.

Prior to WSB, Ramneek was at Schlumberger VLSI Test Division as Director of Software Strategy. Ramneek has also held various positions at Digital Equipment (acquired by Compaq/HP) and Synercom.

Ramneek has an MS from LSU and a B.Tech from IIT Kanpur, India. He co-authored US Patent 6,891,932 - "system and methodology for voice activated access to multiple data sources and voice repositories in a single session."
