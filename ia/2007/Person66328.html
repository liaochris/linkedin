Patrik Eriksson’s Summary

Swedish citizen in Stockholm. Sales development manager in the online recruitment and staffing service industry at Jobb24 since May 1 2007.

Jobb24 is an online service for the recruitment and staffing industry in Sweden and has the ambition to become the premium brand and top provider of online recruitment and display. Jobb24 is a daughter company to Schibsted corporation where other sister companies e.g. are Svenska Dagblandet, Tasteline, Aftenposten, Hitta.se, Aftonbladet, Verdens gang.


I was previously a part of the start-up of Komet which is a staffing and recruitment firm for university graduates where I've during one year worked as an account manager and where I also was responsible for Komet's expansion and business development. Komet is part of Constellation Group where Nova100 and Agentum are members. Which in turn is part of Universum Communications where parts e.g. are Campus, Shortcut, Studentkortet and Chili.

Field of studies at LUSEM (Sweden) and ESC Rouen (France): business development, organizational behavior, HRM, recruitment and competence development.

Additional internship experience as e.g. logistic assistant (France) warden at federal prison (Sweden) and vice president of faculty student association (Sweden).
Studies in the U.S., France and Belgium.

I am outgoing with a long-term perspective on every part of my life and I am also process oriented and altruistic as a person. I always strive to develop myself in questioning and asking why at all times.

Mother tongue: Swedish. Fluent in English. Very well in spoken and written French. Good understanding in Norwegian and Danish.

More information at: http://www.jobb24.se

Sales, university student communications, recruitment, staffing.
