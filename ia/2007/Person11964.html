Bob Thordarson’s Summary

Bob Thordarson brings 15 years of experience to marketing, evangelizing and positioning innovative new hardware and software applications with Telecommunications Carriers worldwide. Specialized in the delivery of new products and productizing revenue models with carriers, Bob has worked closely with both large and small wireless and wireline carriers throughout North America.

Co-Founder, Cequint, Inc. The Company develops and markets mobile device software applications to wireless carriers worldwide. The company’s product CityID is a patented Caller ID application that automatically displays the City & State from where a caller's phone number is based. City ID is designed for incorporation into products ranging from entry-level cellular handsets to advanced mobile Smartphone devices.

Partner, Ben Elliott & Associates. Through this partnership I represent a variety of hardware and software applications companies in building relationships with the wireless and wireline telecommunications carrier channel.

Board member, GPS Flight, Inc. a pioneer and leading provider of micro-sized long range GPS-integrated wireless Telemetry solutions.

Co-Founder and CEO of Consumerware, Inc. (1995-2005) The Company designs, develops and markets consumer CPE products to the major wireline carriers in North America. Ranked #4 and #8 respectively in 2000 and 2001 as the fastest growing technology company in the state of Washington. Shipped over 4 million Caller ID devices to the major RBOC's from 1998-2004 generating combined sales of $52 million over that period. Sold a portion of the company in 2004 to ParkerVision (PRKR).

I'm interested in establishing relationships and dialog with people actively involved with the development and marketing of hardware and software products and applications to telecommunications carriers worldwide.

Member Seattle YEO/EO Chapter since 1999.
