Tricia McGarry’s Summary

Tricia McGarry has been engaged in the management and ownership of technology and growth businesses over the past 20 years. Her work includes the direction of accelerated growth in marketing divisions in direct response fulfillment operations. In addition Tricia managed marketing and distribution with strategic national accounts for technology products. Tricia negotiated and managed relationships with strategic partners that include Microsoft, Apple, Adobe, Symantec, Compaq, Novell, and HP as well as several hundred other software and hardware related computer-industry firms.

As a successful business owner, Tricia has directed two separate companies over the past 10 years with growth rates exceeding 1000%. In this capacity she initiated, developed and managed relationships with clients including Disney, Melaleuca, NuSkin, Avon, Icon Health and Fitness, Novel, Citibank and Franklin Covey.

Tricia currently owns Cobalt Communications Group, a creative services firm, is a Vice President of Business Development for mediaFORGE and manages marketing for Mercato Partners, a developing growth equity venture fund. Tricia also co-managed development of the Utah Student Venture Fund and is one of four Managing Directors developing a new venture firm with a vertical business focus and anticipated funding of approximately $250 million dollars.
