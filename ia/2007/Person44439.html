Joyce Ling’s Summary

With over 12 yrs of experience in the professional makeup art and cosmetics training industry in both the US and China market. I started with Sephora US, a professional cosmetics retailer under the group of LVMH 7 yrs ago, and later moved to Shanghai, to help Sephora start in China. Currently serving at Christian Dior Perfumes and Cosmetics in China, also a LVMH group company, as a National Training Manager specialize in Makeup and Fragrance. In order to fully contribute, I also lead a national makeup artist team of 20 members.

I look forward to further educate cosmetics consumers in the greater China market, and bringing creative ideas around cosmetics products and techniques, as well as to re-introduce professional artistry into China.

Certified with Honor by The International Fragrance Foundation
