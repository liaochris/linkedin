Karen Rands’s Summary

Karen, spent 13 years at IBM developing sales strategies and marketing programs for PC reseller channel; customer incentive programs for Large PC hardware and software companies; worked on product launch and market deveopment for industry specific niche solutions: pen-based, wireless, and handheld and wireless integration services solutions. Worked with start up and transition companies during the dot.com craze to develop business strategies and integrated IT solutions so they could attract venture capital funding and then spend it with IBM...Called the Complex Opportunity Business Manager or more commonly known within the trenches at IBM as the Deal Maker.

Karen left IBM in 2001 to ride the wave of a terrific start up and began her real education on the venture capital formation market. Launched Kugarand Holdings in August of 2001 initially as a finder for Venture Capital firms. Kugarand Holdings evolved into a full service provider for start up and early stage companies seeking venture capital that may come in the way of debt or equity.

Our goals are to help 100 companies by year 2013 build assets of value that provide return to their share holders and operate efficiently and effectively in the marketplace. This will primarily will be achieved through the infrastructure built with the Launch Funding Network, Inc.(www.LAUNCHfn.com karen@launchfn.com) and the establishment of chapters for the Network of Business Angels and Investors (www.nbai.net karen@nbai.net).

The holding company that owns both of these entity is Kugarand Holdings, LLC. Karen continues to speak and write about the venture capital and angel investor market through this company.
