Greg Brown’s Education

  • Computeach College

    Webmaster Development Certificate, Web site design and maintenance, 1997 — 1997

    Over the past few years I have done a number of Web sites for a variety of organizations and companies in the GTA. Sometimes people ask me how I got started - so here's your answer.

    It began in the spring of 1997 when I enrolled in a government-sponsored Web design course at Computeach College (then located in an office complex in Mississauga - Dixie Road just north of the 401). This six month intensive program was my start in the exciting and fast-paced world of Web site design.

    Since then, I have enhanced those skills and tried to keep up to date with the latest trends (which can change rapidly). If you're looking for someone to help you with your existing Web site, or to design a new one - feel free to contact me at any time.

    Champlain Regional College is a CEGEP. For those of you reading this who didn't go through the Quebec educational system, CEGEP is a two year program following high school similar to the community college system in Ontario and in other places.
