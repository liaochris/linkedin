Jeff Orr’s Summary

My high technology career started in 1987 with most of the roles utilizing my love for product management, product marketing, and market intelligence. To date, I have managed more than $1 billion in technology product revenues.

I joined WiMAX Forum in 2005 as the industry association's first employee where I built the marketing organization, including membership, events, web marketing, and external communications.

Prior to WiMAX Forum, I spent 7 years at Proxim, first as a Product Manager to lead the company's entry into the Wireless Home Networking market. We had early success in that market with 60% worldwide marketshare. While at Proxim, I led the introduction of several award-winning wireless brands and product solutions in both wireless LAN and broadband wireless access. My role there broadened from product management to product marketing to managing the product marketing team to setting the market strategy for the corporation. I also spent a couple years as Proxim's board member representative to industry association WiMAX Forum.

Prior to joining Proxim, I was at Diamond Multimedia where I held several management positions and received over 200 product-related awards. Joining the start-up as the 8th employee, I created a number of marketing and software advances that are now standard in high-tech industries and PC operating systems. Products that I managed had top 3 marketshare (most were #1) and established the second most-recognized PC peripheral brand in the US. When I left Diamond, we had completed one of the largest IPO's and had revenues over $800 million.

I enjoy public speaking and have appeared on numerous TV and radio talk shows, and as a subject matter expert speaker for industry trade shows and seminars on Wireless Home Networking, Last Mile Internet Access, Cellular Backhaul, and Security/Surveillance.
