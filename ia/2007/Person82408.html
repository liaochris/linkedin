Steve Curran’s Summary

Steve Curran is a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE) with experience of designing, implementing and administering Microsoft technologies in business enterprise environments. This includes the management of Active Directory, Group Policy, SQL Server, Exchange Server and ISA Server in multiple domain and site organizations. He has demonstrated the ability to adapt to fast paced and highly versatile environments and can develop new skills quickly and efficiently as needed. Steve is highly motivated with excellent interpersonal and client facing skills and is experienced working both as an individual and as part of a team. He has outstanding troubleshooting and problem solving skills and is qualified to degree level with further technical certifications to which he is always looking to add to in order to allow him to gain greater experiences and progress in his career.
