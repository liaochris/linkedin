Dan Steeves’s Summary

With Predictix I am focused on retail consulting and custom software development.

I gained a wide variety of experience at Retek (now Oracle Retail), Best Buy, and Accenture before joining Predictix. On Retek's predictive consulting team I implemented forecasting at Gander Mtn, Lids and Payless Cashways; and particpated at others. I created the requirements and design for Retek Allocation, in a close partnership with Family Dollar and Truworths. As an orginal member of Retek's enterprise architecture team, I worked with the Projekt202 consultancy to redesign the Retek user experience for new products.

At Best Buy I led two software development teams in Extreme Programming; creating a custom in-store selling and ordering system. Then I managed the Best Buy For Business electronic commerce system built on the Comergent package.
