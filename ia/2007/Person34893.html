Henry Drury’s Summary

I have been working in the automotive industry since 1992.
I have held the responsibility of a general technician up to a senior manager. I have worked for several automotive shops in the Raleigh area. Several of these facilities have expressed great appreciation to me for helping them turn there business in to something more profitable and productive. Through the years of working in this business I've built many relationships with clients inside and out of this industry. My reputation as a knowledgeable, honest, and well respected individual in the automotive industry is well known. I have received many awards and certifications from independent automotive and management training companies. I have also received factory training from Bosch, Volkswagen, and BMW. I have fulfilled the retail store manager training requirements for the Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company and have managed a profitable corporate owned Goodyear service department. I have also held the responsibility of general manager at a Goodyear Franchise in Cary, NC. Recently I went to the Midas Corp. It was a 6 month contract that I managed to fulfill in the determined amount of time. I am now currently selling new and pre-owned BMW automobiles at Performance BMW in Chapel Hill NC. It's basicly geared for the every day auto owner that may have questions or concerns. I answer questions and offer advice on various topics. Please check it out@!
