Jamie Ashe’s Summary

Jamie graduated from high school at the age of 15. Recieiving numerous awards and scholarships including the prestigious 'National Merit Honor Scholar'. Quickly educated. Jamie earned her B. S. in Computer Science, a B.A. In Journalism and then her MBA by the age of 21.

She then went on to receive her Ph.D just three years later at the age of 25.She the led the technology industry as Vice President of Sales for Top Speed Corporation and later Sales Manager for EurekaGGN during the dot com boom. It was in 2001 she decided to join the media giant AOL Time Warner. During her tenure at AOL, she was the number one sales and advertising executive. Recognized for outstanding sales achievements, she was award 'Top Sales Person of the Year for 2001 and 2002'. She also won the prized 'Managers Over Achiever Award' for both years, and in her last year, Jamie was nominated for AOL Time Warner's "Samurai of the Year Award" for overall contributions to the company.

In 2003 she was recruited by Microsoft Corporation and relocated to Los Angeles. Industry competition has never been a factor. In her position as Senior Advertising Executive, Ashe once again became the Top Sales Person in her division within just the first 6 months of employment. Determined to be an industry leader and pioneer she continued to contribute to the worlds most prestigious advertising companies such as ValueClick and Vendare Media.

In 2005 she joined forces with Medium21, an advertising and marketing agency in which she is the current Vice President of Sales. With an additional office in Palm Beach she is recognized as one of the most powerful female executives in the advertising industry with her strength and abilities to ink lucrative celebrity endorsement deals as well as award winning marketing campaigns for her clients.

Jamie Ashe’s Specialties:

Jamie takes pride in leading advertising campaigns for not only the top companies in existence today. She is also known for closing million dollar deals with Fortune 500 companies such as MTV, Disney, Fox and NBC Television. Jamie is always available to her clients like a sports agent is to their players. Developing a sense of trust and a mentality that the proof is indeed in the pudding.
