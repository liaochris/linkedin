Akhil Sahai’s Summary

An accomplished and well-respected world-class researcher and technologist in the area of Systems and IT Management, Utility Computing and Next Generation Internet based services. Have published about 70 research papers, authored a book on Web Service (related to next generation Internet), contributed 4 book chapters and have 17 pending patents. Was one of the initial members of the HP E-speak project that gave birth to the paradigm of web services. Worked in the area of web services management has led to the creation of a new HP division, and creation of a web service management standard for Distributed Management (WSDM) managed by standards body (OASIS). Has extensive experience in the following areas:

• Management and Leadership: Over 12+ years of work experience with creating and leading teams involved in designing, developing and shipping world-class, high-tech products and services with customer inputs. Strategic thinker, decisive and able to manage in a dynamic environment using sound analytical, technical, financial and operations experience.
• World Class Technology and Research Expertise: Career began with a strong software engineering foundation. Contributed to the intrinsic advancement of various technology areas such as Next generation Internet, Network Management and web services and sought out for expert input. Regarded as an authority by peers and have been invited to chair numerous world-class conferences and have been invited for keynotes around the world.
• Management of Complicated Projects and Programs: Repeatedly selected to guide critical projects/programs. Skilled at melding technological and business expertise to resolve critical issues, create and ship world-class products, and executing high-tech projects from conception to final delivery. Have been consistently ranked at the top of the ladder during various internal rankings, and have been called upon by my management at HP to execute and lead mission critical projects.
