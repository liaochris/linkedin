Tammy Lenski, Ed.D.’s Summary

I’m a dialogue jump-starter for organizations. I help people sort out their differences and build stronger, more dynamic workplace teams. I've helped individuals, work teams and entire organizations make their peace with conflict for almost two decades as an organizational leader, college professor, workplace mediator and conflict coach.

Primary client arenas include higher education, eco-business, project managers and design firms. Typical gigs include the effective launch of major change initiatives, building dialogue across workplace constituencies, and strengthening the effectiveness of workplace teams in conflict.

Tammy Lenski, Ed.D.’s Specialties:

* Building dialogue, transforming conflict into opportunity and creating dynamic workplace teams (mediation and organizational consulting)

* Teaching people how to change their behavior in conflict and negotiations (conflict coaching)

* Teaching workshops that people love (conflict management and mediation training)

* Entertaining speaking engagements (guest speaking)
