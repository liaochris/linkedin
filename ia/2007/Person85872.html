Tim Jones’s Summary

I am an Arts Producer with expertise in professional and participative arts practice across multiple art form disciplines. I offer an ability to sensitively support the development of networks and infrastructures for creative and cultural production, an enthusiasm for emerging creative practice, combined with a mature business sense and significant experience of working in socially engaged contexts.

Tim Jones’s Specialties:

Action research, artist/tour booking/liaison, consultancy/strategic planning, contracts, evaluation, event production, event booking/programming for festivals and venues, fundraising, network development, producing, project management.

Tim Jones’s Experience

  • Director

    Solar Associates

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Arts and Crafts industry)

    September 2007 — Present (4 months)

    Solar Associates is the creative production consultancy I have founded which offers collaborative expertise to artists, businesses and communities who wish to engage with evolving artforms, and transform our culture, in the era of participatory media. Clients include:
    - the Albany, with whom we are developing 'Future Audience', a project allowing the venue's audience to determine its artistic content
    - Burntprogress, for whom we act as booking agents, fundraisers and consultants.
    - artist Roshini Kempadoo, for whom we are producing an interactive digital media arts installation.
    We're also developing 'Dramacommons', an online resource for drama practitioners of all kinds - an open platform to share games and exercises, content and discussion across a very broad field of creative practice.
