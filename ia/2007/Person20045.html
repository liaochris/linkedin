Daniel De Luca’s Summary

I’ve been involved in different Internet/Intranet projects as developer and/or project manager.
I have been responsible to analyze, design, implement, deliver and maintain complete IT infrastructure. This experience can be shown from the lowest physical network layers up to the configuration of applications according to specific requirements.
I've been involved in different Research and Development projects, funded by the European Commission (European Union), in the Software Simulation (ESPRIT project) field.
For some of these projects I’ve been relocated in France (1 year) and in Russia (3 months).

I worked in the Agent Group of Riverland Research department (STARLAB), being involved in different projects in the field of Intelligent Autonomous Agent Technologies (European Commission ACTS projects) applied to Electronic Commerce. I also worked in collaboration with the Agent-Group of Pattie Maes at the MIT MediaLab (USA) in this field.

I was involved in a major S1 Corporation R&D/Architecture projet defining the next generation of financial software architecture of S1 Corporation Internet based products using technologies like Enterprise JavaBeans and Java 2 Enterprise Edition (J2EE), data/object mapping, transactions, distributed components architectures, connectivity to back-end/B2B systems, etc..
Additionally to that I was investigating the potential use of Agent Technology in financial applications.
I’ve been relocated (in 2000) from the S1 Corporation European headquarters (in Belgium) to the new research and technology group in S1 Corporation worldwide headquarters in Atlanta GA (USA).
I was involved in the design/architecture and the development of the Clear2Pay products using the latest enhancements of the J2EE specifications, Web Services and Service Oriented Architecture.
I'm now offering my competences as contractor.

French, Italian, English, Dutch and a little of Spanish.
