Kim M. Bayne’s Summary

Kim's award-winning background includes both hands-on and project management experience in nearly every aspect of Internet and marketing communications. As founder of the first listserv for high tech marketing (HTMARCOM), Kim has been a pioneer in online communications and community engagement. She spoke at one of the world’s first Internet marketing seminar series in 1994 and her book "The Internet Marketing Plan," reached #1 in 1999 on Amazon.com's Best Seller List for Web Marketing. Her internationally syndicated public radio program "The Cyber Media Show" reached over two million listeners per week in thirty markets.
