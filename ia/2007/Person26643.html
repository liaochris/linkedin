Ellen Leanse’s Summary

Ellen Leanse is an experienced brand and marketing professional seeking consulting or project relationships with technology, Internet and consumer product enterprises.

Ellen brings more than 25 years of leadership and diverse corporate and entrepreneurial experience to her work as a brand and marketing strategist. Her focus on customer loyalty, user experience and brand strategy positions her to work effectively with established or start-up companies in identifying and acheiving key business goals.

Oracle, MCI, Apple, HP, Acer, NeXT and diverse start-ups have called on Ellen's creativity and execution to strengthen customer response and deliver measurable, meaningful results. With dozens of successful marketing communications campaigns, product announcements, brand and identity systems, web implementations, and market assessments in her portfolio, Ellen provides a wide array of marketing services. Her results focus on understanding the full opportunity and making the most of budget, timeframe, and competitive environment to position her clients for success.

Prior to establishing her consulting practice, Ellen spent nine years at Apple, where she helped drive Apple's rapid growth in international markets and established Apple's first outreach to the installed base, consultants and "ambassadors" of the Apple brand. As founder and Chief Evangelist of Apple's User Group Connection, she received widespread industry and media acclaim and is credited with the establishment of Apple's first loyalist and online communities.

Ellen tutors in English and Spanish at local public schools, has worked in the U.S. and Kenya with childrens' and entrepreneurial organizations, and has run a small business "on the side" for nearly 35 years. She is a published author, was featured in Inc. Magazine's cover story on successful multitasking entrepreneur and is an internationally-ranked competitive distance rower. She and her three sons live in Menlo Park, California.
