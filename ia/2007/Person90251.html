Walter Cristelli’s Summary

Started in IBM (NYSE, IBM) as System Engineer I have developed managerial experience in these business processes: manufacturing, information systems, plans & control and human resources. In 1999 I moved to another multinational Company Bristol-Myers Squibb (NYSE, BMY) where I started as Human Resources Director responsible for sales and marketing organization in Italy and Central Eastern Europe & Israel. In addition I was also responsible for Information Management processes. My regional and international responsibilities changed and I became responsible for South East Europe: Greece, Turkey and Israel and finally Eastern Europe, Middle East, Turkey and Africa.
After a transition period I joined Bialetti Industrie (BIA, MTA Borsa Italiana) as Group Human Resources Director and during 2006 CEO of Girmi (small household appliances).
My core competencies are Human Resources and Information Management. I develop my HR leadership in multinational company at national (Italy) level and international level covering about 100 countries.

Walter Cristelli’s Specialties:

Global Human Resources Planning and Strategy. In Country and Global Policy Making. Internal Business Partner: Business, Organizational and Cultural Alignment. Cross-Functional HR Leadership: Executive Benefits and Compensation, Talent and Knowledge Management, Organizational Change and People/Program Improvement. Leveraging Technology
