Prasad Karnik’s Summary

More than 12 years of experience in the semiconductor industry defining ASICs and software for cellular applications. First contribution was to make the first cell phone chipset at PrairieComm. I was also the Receiver Architect for the first UMTS chipset in PrairieComm. This chipset was part of the Samsung prototype that made the first voice call in an IOT at Nortel. In between cell phone chipsets in Skyworks and Broadcom, I co-founded ActiveVoice and developed an innovative voice recognition engine.

My goal is to add value to the world. I have taken my first steps towards this goal by joining the Marketing group at Broadcom.
