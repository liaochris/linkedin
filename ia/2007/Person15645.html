Sebastien Chalmeton’s Summary

8+ Years of Experience in Management Consulting and Business Development. Focus on Media & Entertainment. Extensive International Experience. Yale MBA, Finance & Strategy, MFA in Motion Picture Producing.

Blending my passion for films and business development, I joined in 2006 the Strategic Planning & Business Development division of Lionsgate Entertainment in Santa Monica. Prior to my MBA at the Yale School of Management, I produced a short-film in conjunction with the French-German cultural broadcaster Arte, while pursuing an MFA in Motion Picture Producing in France and Germany.

Prior to that, I enjoyed a successful career in Europe as a strategic management consultant, which gave me deep experience in managing cross-functional teams to develop and execute strategic initiatives in the new media and traditional consumer products space.

I began my career in diplomacy in Australia and then in North America, promoting and developing the European entertainment scene at the French Consulate in Canada.

(1) Corporate, product and business strategy.
(2) Business development (deal/partner negotiations).

(4) Marketing, branding, user experience and internationalization/localization in the digital media space.
(5) Extensive project and team management in a cross-functional and international environment.
(6) Strong understanding of the home entertainment, motion picture, Internet and social media landscapes.
