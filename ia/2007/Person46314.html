Daniel Karpantschof’s Summary

After being partner in and project manager for Damage Incorporated I/S, through nearly three years, Daniel Karpantschof has chosen to found the Ministry of Innovative Storytelling.

Karpantschof is former vicechair in The Organization of Primary and Lower Secondary School Students (Danmarks Elev Organisation) and member of the Council of Primary Educational Affairs (Folkeskolerådet), under Margrethe Vestager, member of The Council of European Relations (Rådet for Europæisk Politik), under Niels Helveg Petersen, former member of The Governing Board of The Danish Youth Council (Danske UngdomsFællesråd) and has been attending many UNESCO and UN summits as representative of The Danish State.

Among others, World Youth Parliament (which he chaired), The UN Millenium Conference and UNESCO Youth Forums.

Karpantschof is educated in media productions, production coordination, journalistic and dramaturgic storytelling, project management and political sciences.

Currently writing a book on Political Management (www.ForlagsKompagniet.dk).
