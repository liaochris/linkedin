Michael A. Morell’s Summary

Mike has over 12 years of experience in recruiting and technology sales. Prior to joining Riviera Partners, he managed a small boutique firm, focused on senior level hardware and software engineers for early stage companies in the Silicon Valley.

Mike began his business career in sales representing Altera, Fujitsu, LSI Logic and Lucent selling standard and custom integrated circuits.
