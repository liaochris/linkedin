Bob McKee’s Summary

I started my professional career by spending over 5 years as a credit analyst, which included obtaining a Diploma in Credit Analysis from New York University in 2001. After meeting with enough headhunters during my 5 years, I decided to switch to the executive recruitment side of things myself because I felt that my personality matched with my finance and banking background would make an ideal match.

Just over one year later (as of April 2006), I am happy to say that I have made the transition successfully. I specialize in positions I understand best, which include credit analyst and senior accouting positions at banks, finance companies, and hedge funds. Both clients and candidates appreciate my understanding of the positions, ability to make efficient matches, and honesty in feedback.
