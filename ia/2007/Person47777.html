Kevin Stirtz’s Summary

I have spent most of my career growing businesses for myself and for others. Along the way I have become an entrepreneur, a writer, a trainer, a speaker and a mentor.

In addition to managing a non-profit organization, I also have a training and development practice. I work with franchises, independent small businesses and professional service firms to help them increase revenue and profits by improving their marketing and service. I have developed a unique and effective system for doing this.

My first book (Marketing for Smart People) is done and is available at Amazon.com. I am working on my second book which I plan to publish this June.

When I'm not working, you'll often find me (and my wife, Debbie) walking our two basset hounds through the streets of Burnsville, Minnesota, where I grew up.

One of my favorite quotes is:

"We make a living by what we get, we make a life by what we give."

-Winston Churchill

The most important secret to success in business (and in life) I've learned is to be of service to others by using your talents and skills the best you can.

You can reach me at 1-952-212-4681 or go to my website at: www.KevinStirtz.com
