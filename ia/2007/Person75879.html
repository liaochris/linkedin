Luca Sambucci’s Summary

I use computers since 1984 and I have proven work experience in the IT Security field since 1993.

This experience includes a broad range of positions and activities:

PRIVATE SECTOR: I'm currently the Communications Manager at Future Time, the Italian distributor of NOD32 antivirus and Outpost Firewall. Previous experiences include the start-up of an ISP in Perugia in 1996 and of an Italian-Russian consulting company in Moscow in 2004. I also worked as an IT consultant for several companies (eg. Telespazio, Finmeccanica group).

PUBLIC SECTOR: I've been a personal advisor to the Italian Minister of Communications for more than three years. I've also been a member of several governmental workshops and committees, including the 'Internet e Minori' committee, for the protection of children on-line.

JOURNALISM: I wrote seventy articles for various computer magazines, of which about forty for the main Italian computer magazine (PC Professionale). I currently publish Italy's most accessed IT Security information site at www.SicurezzaInformatica.it

AWARENESS RAISING: On some occasions I hold conferences on computer security, focusing on hacker activities and how to deploy effective countermeasures against them. During my work as a Governmental advisor I also took part, as a speaker, to some awareness-raising conferences about on-line protection of children.

RESEARCH: In the beginning of the nineties I conducted several independent anti-virus tests (you can read a comment on that research in the following paper located at the NIST site http://csrc.nist.gov/nissc/1996/papers/NISSC96/paper019/final.PDF ). I'm also a reporter for the WildList Organization (www.wildlist.org) since 1995.

I speak four languages: Italian (mother tongue), English (fluent), German (fluent), French (good).
I'm currently studying Russian.

In a nutshell, I possess a distinguished and proven experience in the IT field, and I constantly look for contacts in the IT Security area.
