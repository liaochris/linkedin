Ian Rehmert’s Summary

Ian has succeeded in making an impact within a number of different organizations across the financial, defense, internet, and transportation industries. Over the past few years he has focused on building and directing a first-class internal consulting team that now serves internal clients at all levels within the Servicing Operations of HSBC Consumer and Mortgage Lending. His team draws on sophisticated analytic skills and operational knowledge to deliver pragmatic solutions to real business problems within financial operations. These solutions drive enhanced fact-based decision making in all facets of the business from the executive management team to the line level employee.
