John Grace’s Summary

I see myself a business person that brings a strong financial technical background to the table. While most of my experience has been in the energy business, I'm now working in credit cards because business analysis frameworks travel across industries.

I get my greatest enjoyment from work by seeing impacts in results of the business I support. The jobs I've enjoyed the most have all be aligned with being able to see results. I like to be able to "keep score" and see results.

What I passionate about is business strategy and learning how companies succeed and fail. As a customer I'm always looking at how people are running their business and why. My wife will tell you that while we’re on vacation I’ll ask to stop and see a company's operations I’ve read about. Over the years I’ve piled up a lot of these nuggets and I’m famous for saying, “the problem here looks a lot like the one ABC Company faced, they did XYZ to solve it and I think that might work here” Some people collect stamps, I collect business models.
