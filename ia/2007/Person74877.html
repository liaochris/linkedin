Ruben Brache’s Summary

> Following a 25-year career in information technology, media and Internet marketing, Ruben Brache pioneered global theatrical online investment as founder of eBroadwayPlays.com, a community of theatre aficionados, including commercial and nonprofit theatrical producers, performers, patrons, benefactors, theatre professionals and investors.

He founded eBroadwayPlays.com to usher in a new age of theatrical investment and philanthropy by allowing qualified investors and theatre patrons worldwide to invest in commercial productions, and to subscribe or donate to nonprofit theatre organizations, on the Internet.

His current projects are Syncopation, a play by Allan Knee, targeted for a Broadway opening in 2008, and Zorro the Musical in Spring 2008 in London's West End.

He founded eBroadwayPlays.com:

1. To infuse the theatre industry with investment capital and charitable contributions from previously untapped sources by using the Internet

2. To publish information on developing theatrical productions in order to pioneer online investment and to spur charitable contributions to nonprofit theatres

3. To develop turnkey online application solutions for the theatre industry's sustained long term profitability and growth

4. To create an interactive marketing consultancy by providing both tactical and strategic interactive solutions for theatrical productions on Broadway and the West End
