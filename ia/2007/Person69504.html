Prof. Ajay Parasrampuria’s Summary

Visionary, Helmsman, Change Agent and one of the pioneering force of the Project Management movement in India.

A B.E., M.S. and an Academic Associate at the Goldratt Institute, USA, Ajay started and evangelized the Center for Project Management at S. P. Jain Institute of Management Research, Mumbai. Involved with international institutions and enterprises implementing and driving changes in improving practices as well as teaching the next generation of PM’s in MBA and EMBA programs.

Currently heads i2m management services pvt. ltd. and is involved with leading organization in implementing PM and PMO practices.

A regular at PM Conferences and has published papers on the subject. Have developed and launched a PM learning solutions designed on the principles of Artificial Intelligence and Neural Networking.

Prof. Ajay Parasrampuria’s Specialties:

PMO and Enterprise Project Management, Theory of Constraints, Risk Management, Organization Change Management
