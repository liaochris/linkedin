Peter Horsten’s Summary

Being happy in life is my first goal. As a professional I want to deliver high quality work. My professional mission is to support people and organisations with devoloping themselves.

My experience is diverse. I started my carreer supporting a reorganisation at KPN Telecom. Almost directly I was asked to become a manager in this department. Here I developed my (people and business management skills). I had the opportunity to become an accountmanager (sales representative). This is where I devoloped my consultative and commercial skills. At VKA I became a real professional as the counterpart of board members at several clients. I was able to develop a lot of skills, among others: consultative, commercial, business management, people management. Next to this I became a real IT & T specialist, mainly focussing on telecommunications. Thanks to the oppurtinity at VKA I could also get more knowledge of IT, Outsourcing and HRM (strategy and operations).

Currently I am working on my personal mission by developing my own company: GOYELLO. We are the IT outsourcing provider for the SME markets. We focus both on challenging corporate and community portals and customized application development.
