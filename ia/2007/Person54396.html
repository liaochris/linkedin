Marcy Phelps’s Summary

I’m an information expert, which means that I have extensive training and experience in finding, analyzing, and packaging information. I love to do research, and I love teaching others how to do research. After working in various professional positions related to information and education, I started Phelps Research in 2000. Phelps Research offers both research services for companies that hire outside researchers for their needs, and research training for those companies that assign in-house staff to handle research functions. I also frequently speak and write about a variety of topics, from business and networking skills to finding and evaluating information on the Web.
