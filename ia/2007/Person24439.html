Don Fornes’s Summary

I am currently having a great time starting my first business, an Internet lead generation service for software companies. The most rewarding part of this process has been managing every aspect of the business and realizing that with a little determination, I can take on any project in any function. My company's lead generation business model builds on my operating roles at Epiphany, which were marketing focused. I also have a strong background in finance, having started my career as an investment banker, managed corporate development at Epiphany and spent about a year working to acquire niche software companies through a leveraged buyout. Throughout my career, my roles have been highly analytical, strategic and demanding. My near-term goal is to continue to grow my company and build it into a substantial enterprise. Long-term, I'd like to build more companies or simply build upon my current platform.

  • Principal

    Willow Brook Partners

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    September 2004 — November 2005 (1 year 3 months)

We evaluated over 300 small software companies across many industries, coming very close to completing two deals. Ultimately, we remained very conservative amidst a rising valuation environment and had not yet completed a deal as of the time I decided to start my own business.
