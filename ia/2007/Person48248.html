Kirk Hockinson’s Summary

I have always been a story driven detailed artist. Starting as a scultptor at a very young age I knew I was bound to be an artist when I memorized most animation and FX movies I viewed. I didn't realized I could be an animator until I was attending Film School at CU Boulder CO, where I got to do my first Stop-motion animation. Since then I have worked as a 3D artist working on numerous games most notably the Sim City and The Sims game series from Maxis/EA. I have more recently concentrated on Character Animation which is my real passion.

Ultimately my goal is to animate characters in film and game production and one day create a film with a main character of my own.

Kirk Hockinson’s Specialties:

I have an eye for motion and character acting. I love to really get into an acting role, and then have my 3D character mimic this enthusiasm. I also have a talent for creatively brainstorming story elements and feel I would be excellent on a story team.
