Ron Rutherford’s Summary

Independent Music and Recording Artist
Independent Label: Rarestar Records
ASCAP Writer Member
ASCAP Publisher Member: Rarestar Music
Catalog: 300+ Songs and counting
While living in New York City from 1975, I played and sang around Manhattan and the Northeast; did some television and jingle work; was written up in the New York Times for my performances with The Rutherford & Steward Band; and wrote and performed briefly with legendary rock pianist Nicky Hopkins.
Since relocating to LA in 2004, I exec. produced and co-produced my debut album, Lone Wolf, on my independent label, Rarestar Records. The record was released on CD Baby on 13 September 2006.
Bill Wence Promotions handled the national radio campaign from October 2006 through February 2007.
In the fall of 2007, my album was well received by the Podcast community with much airplay. Internet radio also embraced the album.
My goals:
1. Independently produce and release a timeless, classic album every year.
2. Flourish and prosper as an Independent Music Artist.
3. Help and support independent music and those who support it.
4. Keep my freak flag flyin' and rock 2008!
