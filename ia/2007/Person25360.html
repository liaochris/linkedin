Duncan Work’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur with experience in business development, marketing strategy, development of effective business models, and software development. I'm a designer and inventor of social and collaborative software. Much of my previous work has involved design of social networking functionality that enables more intelligent brokering of relationships and user-controlled access to private information. In addition to managing many aspects of starting companies, I also have extensive experience in consulting with software developers and end users; and I have experience in international trade consulting and hands-on trade.

I am particularly interested in the social effects of software and technology, especially those that can help individuals think, plan and collaborate more effectively in groups and organizations. I have a background in anthropology, urban affairs and sociology; and strong interests in international development, social and business transformation, and the study of intelligence, including intelligent agent technologies and applications of networked intelligence in groups.
