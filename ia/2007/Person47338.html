Kenny Fogarty’s Summary

IBM mainframe consultant with a number of years of experience in the travel, banking, insurance, automotive and leisure industries.

Particularly interested in all facets of DB2 running on IBM mainframes (OS/390 & z/OS).

Direct experience with application installations on IBM mainframes using SMP/E and CustomPac. Installation of DB2 and associated products, e.g. QMF, Data Propagator, BMC Administration Products, BMC DB2 Utilities, BMC DB2 Recovery Products.

Direct experience of applying maintenance to IBM mainframe applications using SMP/E on various IBM mainframe operating systems, from MVS through OS/390 to z/OS.

Always interested in migration projects and have actively participated in migrating DB2 from Version 4 through to Version 7, migration of ISPFLMF to SCLM, migration to Language Environment, migration from CSP to VisualAge COBOL, migration to Enterprise COBOL V3.4.
