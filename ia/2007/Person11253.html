Bill Sequeira’s Summary

Dr. Bill Sequeira is currently the Chief Operating Officer of ACD Systems (http://www.acdsystems.com/), the leader in digital imaging and technical illustration.

Dr. Sequeira has more than 19 years of executive-level business and technical experience working with innovative firms such as Sony, Sybase, Organic Online and AT&T Bell Labs.

As Managing Director, Technology Strategy, for Sony Corp, he effected multimillion-dollar investments in Silicon Valley leading technology companies such as Transmeta, EmpowerTel, Teralogic, Sandcraft, Time Domain, and Luxcore, and has served on the Boards of several companies.

He has served in a number of C-level and technical roles, and has been the architect of technology products and services from Sybase (in partnership with HP, SUN, DEC), AT&T, Medsn, Organic Online and Americast (a consortium of Disney, SNET, SBC, Ameritech, GTE and BellSouth). As consultant and advisor he has supported Fortune 500 companies as well as startup ventures.

Dr. Sequeira has been awarded 10 patents in the protocol, broadband, application, internet and content management areas. He holds Ph.D. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from the University of Kansas, and a B.Sc. degree in Informatics from the Universidad de Costa Rica.
