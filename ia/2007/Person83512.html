Bonnie Yelverton’s Summary

I specialize in making text readable to the users. This involves rewriting text written by subject matter experts or working with a product and writing about it myself.

I try to write in short informative paragraphs (chunked) with headings that can be used to scan the text, or read in thorough tables of contents where necessary.

I work hard with formatting of the text with appropriate numbered or bulleted lists and illustrations such as screen shots or diagrams where useful, but not if the text would be cluttered with unnecessary visuals.

I work in a number of different formats and editors - Robohelp for online help type projects, Framemaker for well-designed user guide text, InDesign (and sometimes Publisher) for newsletters, chorale programs, etc. where the presentation is important, as well as html/css (often hand-coded.) For projects that I hand over to others for upkeep, I find myself using Microsoft products, but I also try to set them up with styles, to keep the formatting consistent, and I have been known to write a "user guide to updating" when I feel it would help.

At the same time, I am now working to help people get solar panels on their roofs through an innovative start-up solar panel provider. I have also been writing training materials and other documents for this company.

I prefer short contracts, in particular working mostly from home.
