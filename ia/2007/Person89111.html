Victor van der Put’s Summary

Technical oriented webdesigner with a perfect sense for functional graphic- and interaction design. In-depth knowledge of several graphical and technical software packages, including but not limited to, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, the standards for this specific industry.

Knowledge of all facets of the profession, a very detailed worker, highly motivated, always trying to learn new things each day, but also faithfull to industry standards and methodologies.

Specialised in handcoding websites, knowledge of latest developments within the area of internettechnology, experienced in the organisation and management of both projects as well as groups of people.

- Both a selfcontained worker and group-player
- Good communicational skills, both in writing and speaking
- Flexible, involved, enthousiastic and initiator
- No 9 to 5 mentality (sometimes even longer)

I have build more then 140 individual sites since I started my webdesigning carreer back in 1999, both in functional als technical design. Through sent in designs by several advertising agencies as well as my own designs. Customers were always happy with the work I delivered, but also about the specific personal way I helped them in the development of their projects and/or products.

As a studiomanager, I've initiated several groundbreaking (re)organisations which resulted in a better working environment for all involved. This mostly entailed a better workflow within a company resulting in better overall business. I've also been involved in several administrative functions, including but not limited to, projectmangagment, development, archiving and other jobs with each studio.
