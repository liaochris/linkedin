Steven Harper’s Summary

Steven Harper is a Certified Sales Coach, and President of Sharper Performance, Sales Performance Consultants in Hollis, New Hampshire. He works with clients to build sales organizations that drive new business, build deeper relationships with their existing clients, and execute on plan. His background includes EchoMail where he was Worldwide Vice President of Sales and Business Development, Exodus Communications, where he was Regional Vice President of Sales, and Vice President of Exodus Canada, and BBN Planet / Genuity where he was the Sr. Web Hosting Specialist. He also ran North and South American sales for IME Systems, where in 1994; he was a speaker at the "opening" of the Internet in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Steven Harper’s Specialties:

Teaching skills/mindsets for Relationship Building for both career/revenue growth, Sales skills, Partner and referral relationships. Consulting on compensation and behavior of sales teams, new markets, selling higher in organizations. Screen and hire high performing sales people. Selling technology without, bits, bytes, feeds and speeds. Some more interesting projects have included helping non-US sales people sell in the US market, overcoming cultural and communication issues.
