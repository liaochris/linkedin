Felix Donkers’s Summary

In random order: strong analytical skills, multi-disciplinary technical skills and experience, inquisitive, creative, good mix of structured and improvising working style, willing to pave new roads, good interpersonal and leadership skills, friendly, open communication, honest, team player, pragmatic, no eight-to-five mentality, pro-active, flexible, market and customer oriented, committed to project goals, success driven.

But most of all a "project catalyst": always aiming for doing things that I believe in, doing things that make a difference, doing things that require top quality and doing it with people who have great fun in making it happen. Being a leader is much more fun than being a manager.

I'm willing to accept (frequent) international / intercontinental traveling.
