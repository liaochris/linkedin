John Poeta’s Summary

I began my professional career as an attorney in 1989. For 11 years I represented commercial banks and financial institutions in the documentation of new loans and the workout of troubled loans and small business owners in the purchase and sale of businesses. In 2000, I transitioned to investment banking and was fortunate to handle some challenging and complex transations out of the gate. I have been active in and have lead the formation and licensing of two investment banks. I have ventured out on my own before and recently did so again. I am presently pursuing the development of Clear Vision Capital.

John Poeta’s Specialties:

Raising Debt, Subordinated Debt and Equity Capital; M&A Advisory Services
