Thomas Isberg’s Summary

With several years of experience from working in the metal and rubber industry, I had my mind set when I started my university studies. I was looking for a way to get to do the things I loved, and get paid for it. I was hooked on Flash from the very beginning, not so much for the cool "flashy" websites, but because of the flexible logic it provided. My first post school position at Framfab forced me to grow as a solo developer, and my goal was always to do my best and to help others do their best at all times. I gave it all, and worked many rough hours late at night. Today I try to use a different approach to the same goal. The real challenge for me these days is to balance work I can be proud of with spare time I need to stay in good health. Still there's a lot to learn when it comes to the infinite possibilities of Flash, and I want to know it all! I'd also love to master After Effects and perhaps 3D Studio Max.

Thomas Isberg’s Specialties:

Object oriented programming with Actionscript in Flash.
Flash design, meaning enhancing the user experience from given design - animations, transitions, sound, flow.
Understanding of server side scripts such as PHP.
