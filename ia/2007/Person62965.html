Neil Rhodes’s Summary

I am currently the Operations Manager for ShermansTravel Media, LLC. In this role, I am responsible for all operations, human resources, and special projects for the online business as well as for Sherman's Travel Magazine.

ShermansTravel Media is a leading publisher of travel deals and destination advice. Founded by James Sherman in 2002, ShermansTravel.com has built a loyal audience among travelers who share a passion for travel bargains and values.

Through strong word-of-mouth, its weekly Sherman's Top 25 travel deals e-newsletter has amassed 3.7 million subscribers. ShermansTravel.com is also a partner with USAToday.com , and its editorial features are syndicated to major sites like MSNBC.com and MSN.com. Building on its trusted online brand, ShermansTravel Media launched Sherman's Travel , the first magazine for travelers seeking smart smart-luxury values, in October 2006.

ShermansTravel Media delivers valuable, up-to-the-minute travel deals and destination content through a variety of products, including its weekly e-newsletter, the website, email bulletins, and QuickSearch, a travel search tool.
