Tom Chance’s Summary

Communications and IT Officer specialising in environmental policy for BioRegional Development Group interested in green politics and free culture/software.

Green Party England & Wales Speaker on Intellectual Property and Free Software.

Web designer with eight years of experience developing web sites and online communities. Familiar with graphic design, content management, programming (XHTML/CSS/PHP/Perl/Python), system administration (MySQL, Apache, Linux, email).

Activist focused on intellectual property and environment issues . Substantial experience managing and leading teams of up to 20 volunteers, with considerable success including lobbying for and subsequently implementing a green electricity policy for The University of Reading. Currently doing policy work on local production and One Planet Living (tm).

Political philosopher with a keen interest in research in areas of intellectual property and green politics.
