Josh Slimmer’s Summary

Josh Slimmer (josh@jerichohr.com)

Experience: Over twenty-five years of Human Resources experience, both within corporate and as a consultant. Former Human Resources Director for ATX Technologies, Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA, NYCTA), Munich American Reinsurance. Former Senior Human Resources Consultant with Touche Ross & Co. (Tohmatsu Awoki)

Philosophy: "That which does not kill you, only serves to make you stronger."

Primary focus: Bringing the best people to the best companies. And let's have fun while we're at it.

Josh is an advisor to several state and national human resource and non-profit organizations such as the Human Capital Institute (HCI) and various business boards. Josh has served on committee's and SIG's of the Dallas Human Resource Management Assoc., the Human Resources Association of NY, the American Payroll Association and the NY and DFW Japan America Societies. He also has served on the Board of the Human Resource Planning Society. Josh is also a writer and frequent speaker on human resource and Recruiting practices.

Josh has also been quoted in several publications such as SmartMoney, Human Resources Executive, Dallas Morning News, Workforce Magazine, Wall Street Journal, WIRED.
