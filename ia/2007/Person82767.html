Shawn Torkelson’s Summary

Shawn Torkelson founded Synapse SE in early 2002 and is the Business Manager of the firm. In this position he is responsible for driving the operational management and internal growth of the firm while overseeing all client and practice-related activities. Shawn is responsible for identifying, working with, and cultivating, many of the firm's client accounts and project resources for its various service lines.

Services provided by Synapse SE encompass Identity Management, Systems Management, Enterprise Business Applications, Directory Services Management, and Technology Staffing. The firm repeatedly delivers quality customer results by leveraging close relationships with market-leading technology vendors while maintaining a product-agnostic approach.

Prior to founding Synapse SE, Shawn held senior-level technical positions in the areas of enterprise applications and data management with Analysts International, Achieve Healthcare Technologies, and Seagate Technology.
