Cathryn Hrudicka’s Summary

At Creative Sage™, we help organizations, project teams and individuals innovate to create outstanding management and marketing strategies, communications, solutions, services and products.

We design custom-tailored, modular creativity and innovation programs, and we consult with, train, mentor and coach people in every level of the workplace who are currently employed in corporations, on project teams, in their own businesses, nonprofit organizations, or in the arts. We also partner businesses and nonprofit organizations for projects that make the world a better place.

Additionally, we coach and mentor people who want to revolutionize "retirement" and create more meaningful lives. We design modular programs for executives and all employees who are approaching retirement and want to re-engineer their lives for greatness after they leave their current workplace.

We bring your inner innovator out of hiding. We help you innovate to be great!

Creative Sage™ grew as a new branch from my flagship company, Cathryn Hrudicka & Associates, where we help you get the word out and get new clients through our marketing communications, public relations, business development and strategy mentoring and consulting programs.

Our team of talented associates implements marketing, public relations, print and online publishing campaigns. We write first-rate copy, manage projects and edit publications. We create online marketing campaigns, e-letters, blogs, podcasts and AV/multimedia productions.

We offer nonprofit organizations consulting, coaching and mentoring in the areas of marketing, communications, public relations, fundraising and strategic planning, and event production.

We bring your message to the world, and the world to you.
