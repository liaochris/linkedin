Jon Tando’s Summary

I'm one of the "old guard" game artists, in that I don't specialize in one area of expertize. I can model, texture, animate, script some, dabble in rigging and high poly sculpting, and try to cram as much functionality and detail into an asset as I can without breaking the system.

Kids today are spoiled, and I love it.
