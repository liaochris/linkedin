Glen Hamilton’s Summary

Glen Hamilton is an Internet retailing pro with years of marquee online merchandising, marketing and product development experience.

At Mercent Glen is the senior manager responsible for product strategy, product definition and product management helping to grow Mercent into one of the leading online marketing platforms for marquee retailers including REI, PetSmart, The Body Shop, Ex Officio, Cutter & Buck, Woolrich and many others. Glen is also responsible for the development and day to day management of Mercent Performance. Mercent Performance is Mercent's online performance marketing agency practice, which engages our retail clients and provides online retail marketing services.

Glen joined Mercent from Car Toys, Inc. where he served as the Director of Ecommerce. In the three years that Glen drove their online business, the Car Toys team grew their ecommerce revenue by accelerating triple digit percentages each year turning an unprofitable department into a business that contributes a material percentage of the retailer’s net profits.

Prior to Car Toys, Glen was the Vice President of Business and Product Development for Egghead.com. During Glen’s six year tenure at Egghead the software retailer went through the metamorphosis from a 300+ store ‘brick and mortar’ retailer to an online only e-tail and auction pioneer with over $500 million in annual online sales. Egghead.com sold to Amazon.com in 2001.

Glen has done consulting for retail, publishing and Internet marketing companies and brings a strong merchant focus to the Mercent product road map. Glen’s graduate and undergraduate work was done at Whitworth. He completed undergraduate majors in Business Management, Economics and Accounting. His graduate work was in International Management.

Glen Hamilton’s Specialties:

Online Marketing, Ecommerce merchandising, Internet Retailing, Business Development and Web Application software product management.
