Doug Petch’s Summary

My passion is helping individuals and organizations recognize and develop the knowledge, skills and habits that will encourage success. My goal is to involve audiences in the process of learning, accepting and adopting practices that will result in increased personal effectiveness. And through increased personal effectiveness to help them enjoy the pleasures of a life well lived.

When I'm not speaking or writing you’ll likely find me either in a roadster looking for new twisty roads to conquer or on a tractor around the small Kentucky farm I share with my wife and a houseful of retired racing greyhounds.
