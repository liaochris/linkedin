Craig Killick’s Summary

I set up The Escape in 1998 as a Design Agency with my business partner. He did Account Managament and Sales, I learned the rest of the business. The web really took hold of me in 2003 when we bought another company and expanded our offering to web.

I now work with some talented guys and we have developed in the areas of online marketing - SEM, SEO, SMM, etc. I am also experimenting with a couple of new businesses selling online with an e-commerce solution we developed last year. These are proving to me that there are ways to make good money on the web selling products - if the proposition is right.

The Escape is a growing web agency and we are looking to grow our position as a leading web agency in the UK. We are doing things right so far and it's a never ending drive to stay on top and move forward in a competitive market but that's the fun of it. Great news is we have a great team, some great ideas and a real understanding of business on the web.
