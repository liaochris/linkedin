Bill Schiel’s Summary

I'm a Pre-Sales Solution Consultant. My experience in this role ranges from major, well established enterprise sales organizations to my most recent situation, an early stage start-up. I was a software engineer, solution architect, and project manager for the first half of my career so I have tremendous credibility working with prospects, system integrators, partner companies, and technical people within my own organization.

My goal is to continue working with people, helping them to understand how technology can solve persistent challenges in their organization. I am passionate about doing this and judge success by not only sales wins and revenue, but on how many friends I make along the way, how much I learn about new businesses and products, and how much fun I have working on a team of professionals.

Bill Schiel’s Specialties:

My consulting spans systems used in all aspects of manufacturing in process and discrete industries. This includes deep experience in semiconductor, electronics assembly, pharmaceutical, bio tech, specialty chemical, food and beverage, consumer packaged goods, and medical device.

I've sold RFID, Manufacturing Executions Systems (MES), Enterprise Manufacturing Intelligence (EMI), Modular Batch (S88), Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA), and Enterprise Application Integration (EAI)
