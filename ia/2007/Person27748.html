Erik Johansson’s Summary

Three areas of particular skills and experiences are
1) Assessment of Enterprise Information Security.
A credible and efficient assessment method has been developed that evaluates the degree of “compliance” towards “best-practice“, international standards, and/or a specific framework (e.g. SOX or a company policy.) He his familiar with several international standards applicable for the management of information security (e.g. ISO/IEC 17799, NIST SP 800 series, OCTAVE, ISF, SSE-CMM.)
2) Cyber Security.
Familiar with the critical infrastructure and SCADA-systems development and performance. Recognize appropriate research, standards, and regulations (e.g. ISA-SP99 and NERC CIP.) Participate in creating the new standard NIST SP 800-82 "Guide to Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) and Industrial Control System Security".
3) Enterprise Architecture (e.g. Zachman Framework, DoDAF, TOGAF) and IT Governance (e.g. COBIT and ITIL.)

Erik Johansson’s Specialties:

Enterprise Information Security Assessments, Best Practises on ISO/IEC 27000-series (17799), OCTAVE, and ISF.
Develops and implements information security policies in organisations, performs auditing, and lecturing. Invited speaker on Enterprise Architecture and Information Security issues.
