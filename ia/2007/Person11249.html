Bill Schwartz’s Summary

I founded EBIZ Machine to help organizations achieve the full potential of the Internet. Its mission is to support its clients' E-Commmerce initiatives through strategic consultation on web site design, search engine optimization and Internet marketing strategy.

I am a certified Internet technology professional (Microsoft MSP, Java J2EE) in my second decade of software engineering and web site design, development, consulting and management. After consulting on web-based application development projects for Berger & Co., Idea Integration, IXL Enterprises and Qwest Communications, I founded Information Technology Machine (IT Machine) in order to help organizations achieve the full potential of the Internet. I have helped a large number of businesses establish their Internet identity and deploy dynamic, data-driven applications to the Web.

My expertise includes object-oriented design and development of multi-tier web, client/server and relational database applications in both the Windows and UNIX environments. My Internet strategy expertise includes Internet marketing (Google Adwords and Yahoo Search Marketing) and search engine optimization consulting.

I am both a certified Java programmer and a certified Microsoft Solutions Provider. I am a certified Intuit QuickBooks Retail Point of Sale ProAdvisor and a Miva Merchant Certified Business Partner. My programming expertise includes PHP, VB.NET, ASP.NET, HTML, Java, JavaScript, Miva Merchant and SQL. I have extensive training and experience in application development project management.

In 2006, I transitioned IT Machine to EBIZ Machine in order to reflect what had become business reality - my clients looked to me for Internet strategy and Internet Marketing consultation as well as the "nuts and bolts" of web-based application development. I feel that "EBIZ" or "eBusiness" better reflects the broad range of Internet strategic services that my company provides.
