Michael Barrett’s Summary

I'm a proven sales and marketing leader in fast growing companies with complex selling cycles. As a 20 year veteran inside business to business sales organizations, I've helped build international sales teams and go to market strategies that bring results.

My Strengths Include:

1. Tackling complex business challenges by breaking them down into measured, acheivable goals
2. Recruiting and motivating professional sales teams
3. Constructing large account management programs
4. An undying love for winning "the deal" - then keeping the customer delighted with ongoing results.

Current and Past Customers Include: LP Corporation, CCLI, DishTV, Tektronix, Xerox, Port of Portland, Les Schwab Tire Centers, TransUnion, T-Mobile, FEI Company, and Port of Portland.

Defensible Expertise in:
- Offshore and Onsite IT Program Development
- Offshore Application Development, Support, & Maintenance
- Global Delivery of QA/Test Programs

Avocation: Action Sports Author and Speaker

I wrote "The Danger Habit" (Random House 2007 - available on Amazon.com) and I serve as the Board President for Christian Surfers United States (www.christiansurfers.com), a non-profit benevolent organization that reaches out to surfers and surf communities around the world.
