Tal Dehtiar’s Summary

MBAs Without Borders (MWB) was founded in Canada in 2004. In 2006, MWB sent 10 MBAs and worked in 11 countries. To put that into perspective, we sent only 1 MBA in all of 2005.

Our mission is to contribute to the business and social development of upcoming nations through work rotations of MBA professionals.

Moving forward, we are very excited for 2007 as we expect to send 20 to 25 MBAs from Canada, USA, Europe and Australia to over 15 new countries on projects that will bring light to new communities in India, bring better health care to locals in Pakistan, Zimbabwe and South Africa, build up fair trade markets in Rwanda and Ethiopia and our MBAs will continue to help build up micro finance institutions across Africa…all this and more as we continuing to…develop people and nations!

Visit MWB at: www.mbaswithoutborders.org
