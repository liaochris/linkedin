Refuse Media’s Summary

Refuse Media is a media group catering specifically to the entertainment industry. Our services include but are not limited to: web design, flash development, music promotions, artist management, tour booking, film promotions, artist development, online marketing campaigns, and more. We are the creators of the ever popular www.punchapunk.com, an online musician promotion service. Refuse Media was founded in October 2003 by Los Angeles native, Eric D. Smith. Mr. Smith, has over ten years experience in the music and film industry. Our company has worked with and continues to develop strong, long-lasting relationships with many independent and major record labels, artists, film production companies, and talent agencies world wide.

Refuse Media’s Specialties:

Strategic Online Marketing and advertising services catering specifically to the entertainment industry. We also offer artist management, film, DVD, image consulting, tour booking, and online promotion services as well.
