Michael Sick’s Summary

Senior level marketer and business strategist has grown many well known brands including Arby's, Peale Vision, Jack In The Box, Burger King, Pizza Hut and others.

My strong financial analysis abilities combined with a knack for developing creaive alternatives has enabled me to provide measureable results for not only large companies but also smaller companies that I have consulted with in the software, pet supply, financial services, motion picture and publishing industries.
