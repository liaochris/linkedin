Rodrigo Madriz’s Summary

Rodrigo is the Founder & CEO of Textalive Media Technologies, Inc. (www.textalive.com) a Toronto based company developing solutions for next generation ad-supported digital content distribution.

His most recent appointments were at Bell Canada (www.bell.ca), at iMotions – Emotion Technology A/S (www.imotions.dk), and at Justlanded S.L (www.justlanded.com). At Bell Canada he architected the strategy of the company’s $6B IPTV deployment, at iMotions he helped the founders select key entry markets ensuring the quickest global commercialization of its novel and now award winning technology, and at Justlanded, he launched the Canadian portal of the world’s largest online multilingual destination for expatriates.

Rodrigo’s career started in the process automation and manufacturing industries where he held positions as a project engineer, product manager, and sales manager of highly engineered products. He graduated in the top 3 of his Mechanical Engineering class (Central University of Venezuela), has a Master of Engineering Management (University of Colorado), research studies in Strategic Management at the J.L. Rotman School of Management (University of Toronto) and has an MBA from the Schulich School of Business (York University) / SDA Bocconi.

He has lived, studied and worked in the USA, Canada, Italy, Spain, Denmark & Venezuela and is fluent in Spanish, Italian and English.
