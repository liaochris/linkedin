Ramon Thomas [ramon@ramonthomas.com]’s Summary

Ramon Thomas is an online entrepreneur with an uncanny knack for spotting global online trends and translating them into meaningful insights in South Africa. My company's mantra is "Understanding Online Opportunities." I can add value to you in the following ways:

1.Dangers – reducing or removing any dangers
2.Opportunities – helping you capture your opportunities
3.Strengths – helping you reinforce your strengths

Please listen to my interview on Monday, 29 January 7pm on Radio 2000.

My current focus is to oversee ongoing research into online trends in South Africa. This research forms the basis of specialised training programs I have developed to help business people get the most from their online experience. More specifically I this year I'm launching two new courses:

1.Social Networking – how to become a social maestro using LinkedIn, MySpace
2.Blogging for Business – How to use blogs for communications with clients, partners, staff, marketing, publicity and ongoing research

After the success of my first ebook, Parents' Guide to MXit, I am embarking on a speaking tour across South Africa to raise awareness of the dangers of chat rooms, online child pornography, identity theft, online addiction, cyber-bullying at schools. These talks is primarily aimed at parents and teachers. This will be done with the support of Childline.

In Dec 2006 I was profiled in Financial Mail, the leading business magazine in South Africa. My research has been widely reported in the media on television programs like Carte Blanche, SEX etc, 3Talk , eTV; radio stations like SAFM, 702, Cape Talk, RSG, Goodhope FM, Channel Islam International; magazines like True Love, Vroekeur, Men's Health; newspapers like Business Day , Die Burger, Sunday Independent, Sunday Times, Weekend Argus, Mail & Guardian
