Tom McCarthy’s Summary

I am a consultant with extensive background in instructional design, media development and technical writing. My company, Performance Systems Group, Inc. develops innovative performance enhancement solutions for corporate and government clients, such as web and computer-based, video, print, and audio mediated instruction.

My abilities include technical writing and editing - instructional design - video scripting - art direction - graphic user interface design. My greatest strength is my ability to render complex subjects in their simplest and most easily understood form.

I know that my success depends on increasing audience comprehension of, and agreement with my client's message.

Tom McCarthy’s Specialties:

Instructional design - technical documentation - web, multimedia and video production - print production. Became familiar with Sarbanes-Oxley requirements while working at Fannie Mae.
