Timothy McGuckin’s Summary

I have over 7 years of experience developing, designing, and producing printed, broadcast and interactive marketing materials for various audiences. Throughout my carreer i have acquired human-computer interaction design principles, ethnographic research techniques, rapid prototyping and user testing. I also have experience writing W3C standards such as HTML, XHTML, XML, CSS, PHP and Actionscript.

Currently, as Senior Interactive Designer in the Corporate Communications division, I conduct ethnographic user research and analysis, as well as, design and develop advanced, interactive web sites and web applications, multimedia CD-ROMs and DVDs, exhibit kiosks and printed collateral that support the global branding efforts for CDM. I have also designed award-winning marketing materials for the Design-Build Institute of America and the American Academy of Environmental Engineers.

Timothy McGuckin’s Specialties:

Branding and identity, user centered design, ethnographic research, managing interface implementation, managing production of marketing collateral, as well as Actionscript, CSS, HTML and XML.
