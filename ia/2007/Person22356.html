Darryll DeCoster’s Summary

Starting around 1995 I began designing and developing websites for local area businesses while attending college at the Pacific NW College of Art in Portland Oregon earning my BFA in Photography. During the 4.5 years I attended the school, I worked in the school computer lab, thus solidifying my desire of creating a career in the digital arena.

Slowly building my portfolio and expanding my skill set, I began working with other computer based applications for creating what is now known as Rich Internet Applications. After creating an Interactive Portfolio CD during my Senior year in school, I bought a copy of Flash 3 and quickly began to learn what soon became my passion and a staple of my web design. Since then, I have designed and developed more than a dozen Flash based web sites and countless others that included Flash, as well as interactive CD titles, tutorials and Marketing/Promotion materials for electronic distribution.

In the 10 plus years I have been designing and developing Interactive Applications, I have created static websites, database driven Flash applications, tutorials for software applications, promotional videos, streaming Flash videos, and computer-based learning applications.

Most recently, moving towards Standards Oriented design techniques - I have discovered another tool set enabling me to broaden the scope of the projects available as well as enhance the palette of tools at my disposal to create an immersive product for projects of all sizes.

Darryll DeCoster’s Specialties:

Flash, ActionScript, CSS, ASP.NET, video editing, large format photography.

Extensive, well thought out designs - giving even the smallest of details consideration.
