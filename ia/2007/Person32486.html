Gaurav Malaviya’s Summary

Update: May 2007: I've moved back to Dubai, UAE as Marketing Manager & Head of Purchasing for Plug-Ins Electronics (an Al Futtaim company).

I started my career as a Product Executive with Archies group (India) in 1994. I moved to Jumbo Electronics, Dubai in 1998 and was made responsible for sales & marketing of Casio products. I was pulled into the Sony Division in August 2000 and became the Marketing Manager & Category Head for Sony handling the Television category in UAE. I was awarded the 'Plaque of Achievement' by the MD of Sony Gulf and the CEO of Jumbo Electronics for turning around the television category and was awarded for 'Exceptional contribution towards Category Growth' at the Annual Sony Conference. I was also made the 'Team Leader' for the launch of Sony Wega Theater in UAE.

I migrated to Australia in 2004 and joined Nikon (Maxwell Opticals Pty Ltd), now Nikon Australia, as the State Manager for NSW & ACT. I was instrumental in introducing extensive sales management techniques and team building.

I started my own company in 2004 - Inmarg Pty Ltd. The company has grown within three years and created a niche for itself as an importer and online marketer of sports & fitness goods in Australia. Inmarg markets the brand 'InGym' across Australia.

I hold an MBA (Marketing) from Monash Business School, Australia and also holds a PG Diploma from FORE in Delhi where I specialized in International Business.
