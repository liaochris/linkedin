David Steed’s Summary

David is a consultant with a wide range of experience in Enterprise Content Management specialising in Stellent / Oracle products. He is also very experienced in producing, customising, and maintaining Internet, Extranet, and Intranet websites. David has been involved in projects at both architectural and implementation level. He is very adaptable to changing situations whilst working with a wide variety of clients, and is prepared to expand his existing skill base and develop new techniques.

David Steed’s Experience

  • Consultant

    Oracle

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 2006 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    David joined Oracle in 2006 and has continued his consultancy role in many other projects. He was involved for over a year in the planning and development of the Club project consisting of DirectGov, the Department of Health, and the Department for Education and Skills. He created the technical design with the customer and undertook substantial parts of the development as well as technical supervision of a number of other consulting developers. The websites were developed using Stellent Site Studio, and he produced numerous heavy customisations including ‘Link Bank’ and ‘multi-language’ functionality. His knowledge of HTML, JavaScript, Java, AJAX, XML, HTML, SQL, IDOC script, and the Stellent products aided in the successful website launches in 2007. Whilst being at Oracle, David has also worked on projects in various sectors including medical, insurance, engineering, and the public sector.
