Jay Taylor’s Summary

With twenty years of business, technology, management, sales, and product development experience, I have enjoyed working with complex technological concepts and being able to communicate them simply and affectively to decision makers so that teams could better take advantage of opportunities and resources. I love helping others come to informed decisions that have positive effects in revenue, clarity of message, and job satisfaction. In my heart I am an educator and mentor.

After writing my first book, I developed a new media distribution process that puts the power of the internet to work in a new way to serve the interest of writers and performers. The majority of all royalties go to the creative talent, versus the publishing company. Using the internet we can explode distribution through a new channel and realize higher returns then ever before.

My primary goal is to mentor people in Divine Economy and expand the influence of One2Another.net™ worldwide.

I have been responsible for technical validation of processes and proposed marketing strategies for rapid increases of revenue. Responsible for day-to-day operations and coordination of information and resources. I also created a process that would replace 9 years of traditional 3d modeling in less than two years. As a regional sales manager, I went from $125,000 annual revenues to $1.25M in five years.
