Jaime Chambron’s Summary

Jaime Chambron is the General Manager of Systems Integration Services for SunGard Higher Education. Jaime has broad system integration, SOA and data synchronization experience, from leading complex service engagements for clients like Hotwire.com, Key Energy, PepsiCo and Boots Well Being, to turning around the delivery of a revamped online admission system for Darden School of Business. From a product perspective, Jaime led the development team that built a first generation data synchronization product using web services and secure communications over the internet for the supply chain Global Data Synchronization movement, ensuring interoperability across vendor products. This product was key to bTrade's acquisition by Click Commerce, who recently received a CoDie award for its Enterprise Service Bus (ESB).

In light of growing up solving math problems competitively and coding her first program at age 8, Jaime obtained a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science from Harvard University where she studied artificial intelligence, internet technologies and the application of technology for education. Jaime is active in improving education outside of the office, as she encourages girls and women to pursue careers in technology via an organization she is a founding leader of and National Director for, the Alliance of Technology and Women (ATWInternational.org).
