Justin Perkins’s Summary

Prior to joining Care2, I was involved in several socially entrepreneurial startups, including a small human rights nonprofit in Congo http://www.afriqueprofonde.org, a consulting firm for democratic businesses, http://www.worldblu.com, and a wilderness solo guiding service http://www.inmomenta.com. One of my favorite projects was helping record an album for these kids from a Township near Cape Town, SA: http://www.abavuki.com. I also co-authored a book published by the National Defense University on leveraging networks to shorten the time-information gap in high stakes and high-pressure situations. In addition to several years in water resource management for the State of Colorado, and a stint as an organic farmer and musician, I have a background in international development, as well as an MBA from the University of Colorado with a focus on marketing and organizational development.
