Steven Silvers’s Summary

Steven Silvers is an accomplished corporate affairs and strategic communications expert with a 25-year track record in helping companies address the complex challenges of business growth, crisis response and organizational change. He is a principal at GBSM, Inc., a management consulting, strategic communications and public affairs firm based in Denver, Colorado.

Before joining GBSM, Steve was founding president of ClarusPR, a leading Denver firm with an extensive track record in new ventures, and previously served as a vice president with international public relations giant Hill & Knowlton. He began his communications career as an award-winning military journalist, rising to become an editor of the Pentagon’s official Army News Service during the transformation from a draft to volunteer military.

He later joined the internal communications department of Texas Instruments, where he helped lead several initiatives to support its repositioning as a market-driven company. He began his agency work in Dallas at Ogilvy & Mather Public Relations, where he helped introduce a newly patented technology called “voice mail.” He later managed the public relations groups of two Dallas-based integrated marketing communications firms.

Steve studied journalism and photojournalism at Syracuse University’s Newhouse School of Public Communication and is an accredited (APR) member of the Public Relations Society of America Counselors Academy. He is active in the business and civic communities.
