Stuart Manley’s Summary

In 2001 I started as a cross-border HR consulting and solutions firm. We helped primarily emerging and established Canadian technology companies, some with as few as 1 U.S. employee who were expanding their senior level sales, business development and operations into the U.S. market primarily with highly compensated multi-state employees, contract staff an independent contractors.

I am very successful now in helping both our Canadian and U.S. cross-border clients implement high value HR and compliance related solutions including HR administration, payroll, benefits, employment compliance and workers compensation coverage to resolve both their current and potential long term HR and multi-state/province benefits related challenges.

With my assistance, our Canadian and U.S. clients are better positioned to compete with larger Canadian and U.S. competitors to attract and retain talented key people. They are also easily able to comply with and resolve complex and unfamiliar Canadian or U.S. local, state/provincial and federal legislative requirements including HR employment compliance. We help them more accurately predict, budget and control their future costs.

Our Clients are established North American companies, multi-nationals, corporate spin-offs, and emerging businesses with Canadian, US and International workforces.

We work closely with business owners, CEO's, senior Finance and HR management providing them with best in class solutions for HR, Payroll, Pooled Benefits and Electronic Payments & Tracking. We provide needs analysis, recommendations, demonstrations, proposals, negotiating contracts, solutions implementation.

Our goal is to continue to expand our domestic and cross-border North American client base directly and through our trusted referral partners networks.
