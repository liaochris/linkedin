Thomas Hawk’s Summary

THC focuses its project and long-term services upon helping implement strategic business management systems, aimed at bridging the gap between integrating strategy with effectively managing daily operations. In the same sector, THC coaches business leaders, helping post-startup companies transition from the hectic pace of those startup activities to a smooth-running operation, through operations process engineering, planning, and execution. Part-and-parcel with this transition to implementing management systems is ensuring prior and ongoing strategic planning objectives are measured and renewed.

THC also specializes in building winning business plans, both for the purpose of guiding the organization toward achieving its growth-oriented business goals and of providing the business with a dynamic presentation of itself to potential private equity investors. These plans frequently preceed, or are done in conjunction with, building information memorandums, which are published to prospective investors or for use at venture capital conferences and workshops. THC works with company leadership to ensure its capital funding requirements are realized.

Geared toward the internationally focused organization, which is looking to grow in both established and emerging world markets, THC has worked with organizations in the US, the UK, and mainland Europe. THC's international focus provides associations within the worldwide telecommunications, energy, and technology industries, aimed at helping businesses achieve financing for growth and further development.
