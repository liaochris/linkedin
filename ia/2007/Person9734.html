Rogier van Staveren’s Summary

As a devoted freelance Java/J2EE developer I am focused on short to medium term projects in the field of Java/J2EE application development and system integration. I have strong UNIX skills and know all the ins and outs of the Linux Operating System.

I started my career at Dutch Vision Systems (DVS), one of the major players in the Dutch machine vision industry.

One year later I joined IBM, the beginning of an instructive era had come. I contributed to different customer based projects including, but not limited to, VNU, Shell and ING. During the seven years at IBM I gained a lot of knowledge and experiences and in September 2005 I decided to resign and continue as a freelance developer and consultant.

- I am not looking for an employment on a permanent basis.
- Projects in greater Amsterdam are preferred, the Randstad is no problem, beyond can be considered. Projects abroad have my special attention.
- Contact me for information about my hourly wage.
- I am available as of Q3 2008.
