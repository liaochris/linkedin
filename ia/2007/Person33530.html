Guillaume Brunet’s Summary

A recipient of a Master's degree in E-Commerce at HEC Montréal, I have been working as an Internet marketing manager for the last five years. Always eager for a challenge and inspired by an entrepreneurial spirit, I founded Adviso Consulting (Internet Strategy) in 2002 with four other associates before joining TD Meloche Monnex, Canada's leader in online insurance and direct market, as the Interactive Marketing Project Leader - E-Insurance.

In my current position, I am responsible for planning and overseeing national interactive campaigns, developing strategies and opportunities to increase website traffic, co-ordinating the production and launch of different campaigns, and measuring the impact of various interactive marketing projects.

Over the years, I have built a solid business network by becoming involved in numerous groups related to my specialty, namely, Association marketing de Montréal - Publicité Club de Montréal (AMM-PCM) and Interactive Advertising Bureau of Canada (IAB). These experiences have allowed me to put my leadership qualities to good use, and have helped me build a reputation in the media as a specialist in the field.
