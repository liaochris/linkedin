Prior to joining Columbia Credit Union, Mr. Marciante has worked mainly in public companies: including a $6.6 billion indirect lending subsidiary (Barnett Dealer Financial Services) as Chief Financial Officer, a $46 billion holding company (Barnett Banks, Inc) as Investor Relations Manager, and a $750 million banking affiliate (Barnett Bank of Martin County) as Chief Financial Officer. Mr. Marciante has also worked in a Joint Venture Partnership for a captive finance company (Dell Financial Services) as Corporate Controller. His prior credit union experience came from serving for two years as Chief Financial Officer of Power 1 Credit Union, located in South Florida.

Mr. Marciante holds a MBA in Finance and Business Management from Lake Forest Graduate School (Chicago). He earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting from Illinois State University.
