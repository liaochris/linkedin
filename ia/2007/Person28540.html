Fabiana Houghtaling’s Summary

I approach recruiting from a strategic perspective, which I developed while working previously for McKinsey Co., Monitor Co. and EMC Computer Systems. When working for McKinsey Co I was responsible for hiring the MBA graduates from the top 10 universities in the United States. It was during this time that I began to create and develop a strong network among the best professionals in the country. This early recruiting connection has continued to create a strong network of the market leaders in the United States, Europe and Latin America.

I have a decade of experience working in the areas of high technology, engineering, finance and business administration. I have had several roles as both a consultant and as an internal recruiter working for corporate headquarters. I’m known for my expertise in south, central and North America and Europe. My background includes several years of hands-on involvement in Human Resources management and the development and creation of strategic talent acquisition projects.

My international experiences is invaluable, and have allowed me to develop a unique interpersonal style that is accentuated by the cultural skills that I have acquired through studying, working and living in Europe, USA and South America (Brazil, Argentina and Chile). My professional background and education have allowed me to effectively perform the process of talent acquisition. I’m fluent in Portuguese, English and Spanish.

I work with self-assurance and strength. I interact with my client candidates and client customers using a consultative business style, solid technical competence, and strong leadership. I’m a highly focus professional who achieve good results and create long term relationships.
