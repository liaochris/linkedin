Paal Leveraas’s Summary

I am certified professional co-active coach (CPCC) and relationship/team coach (ORSC), based in Oslo, Norway. I'm also a writer and freelance journalist. I have experience from publishing, writing, editing and public relations. I started the company (that used to be) behind digi.no (http://www.digi.no), one of Europe's first internet-only newspapers.

These days I mainly work as a coach, one on one or with groups, teams or business or private relationships.
