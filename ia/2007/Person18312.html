Comedy Cocktail Live Sketch Comedy’s Specialties:

The Comedy Cocktail is produced by Jay Leggett & Amber J. Lawson. Both met in Chicago over 10 years ago. Jay fired Amber from her first acting gig and then promptly rehired her on his next directing gig. After intensive therapy the two began a long friendship always talking about finding a show to produce together. A decade later the idea for Comedy Cocktail was born. Leggett & Lawson are developing Comedy Cocktail as a network TV project. Hosted by: AmberJ Lawson, Jay Leggett & Lolita O'Connor
