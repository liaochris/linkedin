Jason Corsello’s Summary

Blog: www.humancapitalist.com

Jason Corsello is a research director for the Business and IT Decision Service. He focuses on a range of enterprise software and services including human capital management (HCM), workforce transformation and business process outsourcing (BPO). His current areas of research concentration include workforce performance management, talent management, learning management, workforce management, and human resource outsourcing (HRO).

Prior to joining Yankee Group, Corsello was the director of marketing at Commendo Software, a leading virtual infrastructure software company. At Commendo, Corsello developed go-to market plans, product road map methodologies and strategic partnerships with leading software companies, including Microsoft. Prior to Commendo, Corsello was a product manager at WorldChain, a supply chain software company acquired by Click Commerce. At WorldChain, Corsello developed and launched enterprise-class supply chain solutions that have been successfully deployed at leading Fortune 1000 companies, such as Dell Computer and Network Appliance. Corsello’s background also includes more than 6 years of industry experience at Solectron Corporation, a Fortune 200 company, where he worked with Global 2000 clients in developing their outsourced manufacturing and supply chain strategies.
