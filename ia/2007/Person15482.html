Cindy Kraft’s Summary

Certified Career Management Coach and Personal Brand Strategist for Senior Finance and Banking Executives. More than 12 years' experience preparing clients to outperform the competition with value-oriented marketing documents, a multi-faceted job search plan, and solid interviewing strategies.

Here's what some of my clients say:

"Cindy helped me develop a top-notch resume that led to an interview and subsequent position as the Director of Finance with a leading worldwide manufacturer and marketer of PC components and networking equipment."

"I just started a new job as President and CEO of First National Bank of Florida. I truly believe your assistance made it possible."

In addition to being a Certified Reach Personal Brand Strategist, Cindy is a Certified Career Management Coach (CCMC), Credentialed Career Master (CCM), Job and Career Transition Coach (JCTC), and Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW).

She is nationally published in 30+ career books including Martin Yate's "Knock 'em Dead Resumes" and "Knock 'em Dead Cover Letters," the "Expert Resumes" series, David Noble's "Professional Resumes for Tax and Accounting Occupations," and the "Executive's Pocket Guide to ROI Resumes and Job Search." Cindy is also a preferred provider for Knock ‘em Dead, Netshare, and Finance Ladder.
