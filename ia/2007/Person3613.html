Alice Lopez’s Summary

Crocs is a Colorado based shoes maker that has developed and marketed an innovative type of footwear called Crocs™ shoes. Originally intended as a boating/outdoor shoe because of its slip-resistant, non-marking sole, Crocs has become by 2003 a real phenomenon, sold all over the world for its comfort as well as its fashionable appeal.

From 2003-2004 Crocs has been expanding its product line, while always sticking to its core values: making a lightweight, comfortable, slip-resistant, fashionable and functional shoe that can be produced quickly and at an affordable price. Today Crocs are sold in every continent and carry on its fast growing expansion. You must have seen those colorful "plastic-like" clogs with holes on the top, which are actually in an innovative and licenced material called "Coslite", whose properties makes Crocs shoes so special.

My job in Crocs Japan is to Market and Communicate about the products and theirs properties, to enlarge our market and tackle emerging copies that depreciate our image because of quality's difference. I started in the company by end 2006 and since then I have been working on branding trough PR Networking, Image Catalog, Events, various sponsorship plans.
