Ben Barren’s Summary

Co-Founder Feedcorp Pty Ltd, Owner of gnoos.com.au, The Aussie (and global) Blog (and media) Search Engine launched June 1st, 2006 to favourable coverage in Australian Financial Review, Sydney Morning Herald, The Age, ZD-Net and over 300 blogs globally.

Feedcorp in 2006 conceptualised and licensed nook.com.au for News Ltd' Leader Community Newspaper division. The company has developed a platform which facilitates local web 2.0 solutions incl structured blogging, vertical blog syndication from 100k blogs, online community and video. Stay tuned for more partner launches in 2007. If you work at a publisher : Contact me if you are interested in such a solution : especially in the finance, travel, entertainment, classifieds, real estate, employment and FMCG space.

The BB Bio : Ben Barren has dedicated the past 13 years contributing to the successful building of leading Australian online media and search businesses – He started his online career in 1995 as New Media Development Manager at DDB Advertising. In 1997 he then became a Founding Product Planner for ninemsn, the joint venture which localised MSN in Australia (#1 Australian network). Ben was responsible for launching Carpoint.com.au – a top 3 car site in Australia.

At Virtual Communities Ltd, which raised $50M+ during the dot-com boom, Ben was the second employee where he was in charge of online. He then consulted to Sensis’ MediaSmart, reaching 70% of Australian Internet users. He also consulted (in 2004) to unoduo.com, Morgan Banks Investment – www.linkme.com.au and media entities (Trader Classifieds owned by PBL Media) plus Melbourne Football Club.

Read Ben's blog at benbarren.blogspot.com. Check gnoos.com.au. Interact with nook.com.au and ping him at redbarren at gmail dot com. He is also co-founder of 2WEB.com.au : Australia's Leading Network of 2.0 Entrepreneurs, who blog and podcast : Who you can read at http://blogs.theage.com.au/startupstories/
