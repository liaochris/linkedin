Tim Long’s Summary

Tim is a professional in the software development/deployment industry. In each of his positions for the last 20 years, he has been successful in the area of Account Management, Up-Selling, and Sales Growth for the company with whom he was representing. His best asset is his ability to interact with Customers, Managers, Peers, and Subordinates. Tim’s trademark is his positive “can-do” attitude he carries with him in every endeavor. He is a company man with innovation and ideas of an entrepreneur.

Tim has created hardware and software solutions for Fortune 500 Companies in the Aerospace, Telecommunications, Retail, Manufacturing, Distribution, Legal, and Pharmaceutical industries. Tim’s desire is to bring value to his company utilizing his selling skills and exceed the company’s goals and objectives.
