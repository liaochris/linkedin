Sang Min Lee’s Summary

I want to refer to myself as "Always Learner". In this regard, this period when I am studing abroad in the states is one of the most significant moments of my learning journey. I believe the most important thing during college years is to learn as many as possible in practical aspect as well as in theoretical one. And if I add one more, practicing what we have learned, while making many mistakes.

While in Yonsei University, I committed myself to Yonsei Chunchu, weekly newspaper of the university. The reasons for the commitment were, first, I were interested in journalism, in which still I am despite my major(Business), and secondly, I wanted to do something that I could do with my passion and practical learning experience, not just by focusing on getting good grade during college years. Eventually, the commitment was totally enough to satisfy both purposes.

Now in the states, I am learning how to live in global and diversified society. In addition, I have personally tasted how the U.S., so called great power, works. Of course, improving English is a primary factor.
