Ronny Louis’s Summary

More than 14 years of experience within the ICT profession. Started my carreer as 2nd line support for the complete datanetworks within Belgacom. Because of the various assignments and duties I've performed since then, my profile has become a very strong profile towards system management and datanetworking.
