Anupendra Sharma’s Summary

Anupendra is currently Investment Partner at Siemens Venture Capital (SVC), where he is focused on investing in Medtech devices and Molecular Diagnostics. He is focused on investing in the US, China, Israel and India. SVC is one of the world's largest Corporate VC firms with $1 billion under management.

Anupendra is a Board Member at China Diagnostics Medical Corp, Board Observer at Cylex Inc, and involved with SVC investments at Sequenom and U-Systems.

Prior to joining SVC, he was Director of M&A for Medical businesses of Siemens Corporation in New York. Prior to joining Siemens, he worked at JPMorgan and Salomon Smith Barney. Anupendra has over eight years of experience in M&A and Investment Banking, and has completed over $3 billion worth of mergers, acquisitions and divestiture transactions.

Anupendra is a Founder of Mobile Medics Healthcare LLC, which was named the Most Outstanding Social Venture of the Year at the won the Global Social Venture Competition in 2006.. In 2005, he won's Columbia Business School's Lorne Weil Outrageous Business Plan Competition with his idea to bring inexpensive healthcare to rural India. He co-founded the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership at BITS Pilani, named one of the top 5 centers of entrepreneurship in India.

Anupendra has worked in Strategic Consulting at McKinsey and Product Development Finance at Ford Motor Company. Anupendra was part of the core team that lauched Ford Motor's operations in both China and India.

Anupendra holds an MBA from Cornell University (1998), Masters in Accounting & Finance from Manchester Business School (1993), and MSc (Hons) Economics & BE (Hons) Instrumentation Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS Pilani) (1992).
