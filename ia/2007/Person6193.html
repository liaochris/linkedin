Alon Angert’s Experience

  • Postdoctoral Researcher

    University of California - Berkeley

    (Educational Institution; Research industry)

    August 2002 — March 2005 (2 years 8 months)

    Improved the understanding of interaction between Global Warming and the biosphere:
    • Analyzed satellite measurements database, and correlated it with atmospheric CO2 observations.
    • Diagnosed the biosphere response to volcanic eruption through computer-modeling and observations cross-check.
    • Initiated and lead a collaboration project that modeled and predicted the usefulness of a new analytical tool for water-cycle research.
    • Lead a project that utilized an existing meteorological dataset to estimate vegetation activity.
