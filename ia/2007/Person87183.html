Torben Bay’s Summary

The combination of being trained in a practical, as well as an academic design process, allows me to access the product on any level. My goal is to create outstanding products with a strong concept and a clear purpose.

Creating is not about duplicating!

A design should differentiate itself clearly, not only by neither color nor an insignificant detail. I have great respect for manufacturers that truly dare to be different.
To me, designing is all about personal expressions, searching new grounds and being able to implement this into a single, no-nonsense product.
