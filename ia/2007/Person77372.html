Scott James’s Summary

I wrote my personal mission statement when I was 21; it has not changed yet. It is simply:

"Help others."

I've been told many times that I am a "geek with people skills". That's an accurate description, but note that it still classifies me as a geek. Consequently, most of my projects revolve around technology to some degree - and leveraging that technology to help others.

I'm currently experimenting with open source technologies. The goal of my main gig day is to generate significant amounts of after-tax profits (think: ultra-low overhead) and pass those dollars on to Room to Read for international children's libraries and the Boys & Girls Club of America.

Scott James’s Specialties:

LOHAS-style marketing, connecting folks, brand-building, and using capitalism to fight extreme poverty around the world.
