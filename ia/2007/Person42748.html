John Burgreen’s Summary

Publication Art Director & Senior Graphic Artist working for MCCS Marketing an advertising agency, broadcast facility, and publication enterprise for a $104 million dollar community services organization serving 8 military bases in Okinawa and Fuji, Japan. I serve a large group of commercial sponsors and internal clients as their advertising designer. In addition to this, I am the art director for the award-winning magazine Okinawa Living, as well as Okinawa Outdoors Magazine and serve as a contributing graphic artist for 4 other publications that this department publishes.

I am a highly driven performer with over ten years as a design professional. As a publication art director and graphic layout/advertising designer in a fast-paced department, I have become a highly recognized individual by my peers as well as my employer.

I am currently working towards building my photography skills, specializing in the capture of HDR images.
