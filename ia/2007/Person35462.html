Thomas Howe’s Summary

Independent consultant to service providers, enterprises and equipment vendors with over nineteen years of experience in the design and development of next generation communications equipment. Recognized expert in Voice Over IP, member of several technical advisory boards and frequent speaker at industry events. Seasoned manager with experience in leading executive, development and cross functional teams. Currently doing basic research in integration of real time communications technologies into web services architectures.

Next generation telephony, technology and marketing strategy, large scale software development, VoIP, Web 2.0, cross functional management, international business
