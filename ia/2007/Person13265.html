Bridget Clapham’s Summary

Bridget Clapham BSc. Hons. (Psychology), Dip Ed, RGN, MPNLP




Following an early career in clinical nursing, Bridget chose to specialise in Education and worked with Healthcare Professionals.

After working as a Senior Lecturer in Helathcare and Psychology, Bridget moved successfully into corporate and public sector training and coaching, setting up her own company, Synergy Consulting in 1998.
Areas include:-

• Developing the Habits of Success
• Stress management,
• Enhancing the quality of effective communication within organisations
• NLP in Education and at work


Bridget is a Certified Master Practitioner of NLP and regularly works as a member of the course team assisting Dr Richard Bandler (co founder of NLP) Paul Mckenna and Michael Neill, (Master Trainers of NLP) on a range of Society of NLP seminars. She regularly runs programmes in NLP and its application for clients in business, healthcare and education sectors

In Education today,with THe Coach Yourself Company: Bridget runs regular programmes for teenagers and for teachers and parents on various aspects of personal effectiveness.

With a remarkable talent for helping clients to clarify the direction to enhance their potential for excellence and success, Bridget designs training and coaching interventions that involve working closely with clients to achieve desired outcomes. This enables individuals and teams to enjoy the process of moving forward through change with curiosity, enthusiasm and confidence.

Bridget’s corporate and public sector clients have included:-

Nokia, Glaxo-Smith Kline, Zurich, Nationwide, Natwest, The Magistrates Service, Boehringer-Ingelheim, Dupont, Volvo, AXA Financial Services, Birmingham Midshire’s Building Society, Government Beacon and Leading Edge Schools, The National Health Service, GP Registrar Programmes, Acuity Doctor Ltd. Royal Bank of Scotland, Paul Mckenna Training, Cancer Research UK and HM Government The Cabinet Office.

Bridget Clapham’s Specialties:

Working with children, parents and teachers in education to enable them to develop the Habits of Exam Success.

Enabling people to develop the Habits of Success in any field.
Managing Pressure for Peak Performance in Education and in Corporate and Public Sector.
