Rebecca Newton’s Summary

Rebecca Newton is the Manager for Safety,Moderation and Player Support (Customer Services), in the User Experience Unit, at Sulake.com. Her responsibilities include: overseeing global online safety for Sulake.com, overseeing global Moderation efforts for the Sulake corporation, developing and implementing online moderation and safety policies and procedures, recruitment, training and professional development for Sulake's global Community and Customer Service staff.

After 16 years in survey research, statistics and computer application and design at the Research Triangle Institute in N.C., she began her online career with America Online in 1994, where she was the Program Manager for Recruitment, Orientation and Education for AOL's Community Leader Program. At AOL, Rebecca was responsible for centralizing, developing and managing all AOL Community Leader recruitment, orientation and online education efforts. During her tenure at AOL, Rebecca and her team built the program from 5,000 to 16,000 active Community Leaders.

She has served as a consultant for several years to a broad range of clients, including: The Scottish Parliament via Communitypeople.net, HowStuffWorks.com, RealAge.com, I-Chat.com, The Johns Hopkins University, the University of Pennsylvania, American University, and national and regional Volunteer Administration Organizations in the US. Rebecca served as a contributing editor to the eGov Monitor Weekly in Great Britain.

Rebecca is the Deputy Chair of e-mint.org. She has an affinity for fine food, art, architectural art history and almost anything on film. She also serves as a volunteer for Airedale Rescue.
