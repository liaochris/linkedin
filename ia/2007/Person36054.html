Cathy Stucker’s Summary

As the IdeaLady, Cathy Stucker helps entrepreneurs, authors and professionals attract customers and make themselves famous with techniques that make marketing easy, inexpensive and even fun.

Cathy has appeared on many radio and television programs, and she has been featured in The New York Times, The Houston Chronicle, The Houston Business Journal, Black Enterprise, Startup Journal (Wall Street Journal), Woman's Day, Woman's World and many other newspapers and magazines. She is the marketing columnist for Daily Court Review, and her column appears each Tuesday.

In her spare time, Cathy is a mystery shopper, providing customer service feedback to businesses of all types. She is the author of The Mystery Shopper's Manual, the only book for mystery shoppers endorsed by the Mystery Shopping Providers Association.

Cathy Stucker is president of Special Interests Publishing. She is a member of the Publishers Marketing Association (PMA) and the Small Press Association of North America (SPAN).

Cathy has many articles available for free use in your print or online publication. See http://www.IdeaLady.com/content.htm
