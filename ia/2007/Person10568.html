Bernard Moon’s Summary

Dual interest in entrepreneurism and politics... U.S. and Asia.

You can read my blog at http://bernardmoon.blogspot.com (Business & Technology, Politics, and Random Thoughts).

Bernard is currently involved in a new social media platform for companies and organizations called GoingOn Networks. He also serves as an advisor to Innotive, an interactive multimedia platform, and Protomobl, a new mobile social-networking platform.

Prior to his current activities, he was Director of Strategic Planning & Sales at Innotive Corp. Prior to this, Bernard was a Director at iRG, a leading boutique investment bank in Asia, where he focused on Telecommunications- Media-Technology companies and provided such services as M&A advisory and capital raising.

Prior to iRG, Bernard was Vice President of Business Development & Marketing at HeyAnita Korea, Inc., a leading voice portal and solutions provider. At HeyAnita Korea, Bernard was responsible for establishing strategic and content partnerships and helping to build the company from its conceptual stage to a 54-person operation. Additionally, he assisted in the closing of the company’s two rounds of financing totaling $14.5 million. Prior to HeyAnita, Bernard was Co-Founder and Vice President of Strategic Development for View Plus, Inc., a video-on-demand company where he helped build the company from its business plan and up.

Additionally, he gained experience in the strategic planning unit of Digital City Chicago, a partnership with the Tribune Co. and AOL. He also served as a legislative liaison to Governor Jim Edgar of Illinois.

Bernard completed a post-graduate fellowship with the Coro Foundation. He received his MPA in Telecommunications and New Media Policy from Columbia University and a BA in English and Psychology from the University of Wisconsin - Madison.
