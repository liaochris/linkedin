Dan Grabham’s Summary

Dan Grabham graduated in July 2000 and immediately began his journalistic career on Internet Investor at Future Publishing in Bath. He soon joined market leading .net magazine as Online Editorial Assistant, responsible for content on the hugely popular netmag.co.uk while contributing to the magazine. Dan then moved to London to work for Haymarket Publishing at The Net Magazine as Technical Editor.

Following the closure of this title and a spell in educational book publishing, he returned to .net and Bath as Staff Writer, contributing Web reviews, features and hardware round ups for over a year before migrating to Laptop Magazine. There he was promoted to Reviews Editor. He then moved to Future’s flagship leisure computing magazine, PC Format, as Technical Editor. There he was responsible for hardware and software reviews. In early 2005 he moved to work on a new well-received launch, Computer Upgrade.

In 2006 he moved to a new development project - Future's technology website launch Tech.co.uk where he reports on the latest technology news.

He also contributes to many of Future Publishing’s other titles including PC Plus, PC Answers, WhatLaptop, MacFormat, Microsoft Windows Vista: The Official Magazine, Your Family Tree, The Official Jetix Magazine and Disney's BigTime as well as writing customer guides for Tesco.
