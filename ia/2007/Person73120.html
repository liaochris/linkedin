Robb Myers, ACIR robb@ca.ibm.com’s Summary

I have been working to bring companies and individuals together since 1992. I began my career as a 3rd party recruiter in the GTA, working on behalf of companies like ISG Technologies, KL Group, Sybase, and Algortihmics.

In 1999 I joined Cedara Software as their Corporate Technical Recruiter. I introduced online recruiting to Cedara while growing the company from 220 to 560 individuals over a 2 year period. I was a mentor to other recruiters and a coach to hiring managers and senior executives, increasing their knowledge and abilities in identifying and qualifying higher performing employees. While there I also acted as project manager on the ATS selection and implementation and redesigned the careers section of the website.

I was on my own for a while until I joined Express Professional to help them get a foothold in the IT market in Kitchener-Waterloo. I left there and joined IBM to assist with some technical hiring at the Markham Software Lab and joined Big Blue full time in April of '07.

My goal in working with companies is to clearly understand their business goals and to develop and implement effective staffing strategies with well-defined deliverables and metrics to enable them to attain those goals. Recruitment, Onboarding, and Retention are the 3 areas where I add the most value to an organization.

I partner with companies that are looking to develop and implement proactive people strategies resulting in increased workforce productivity, higher retention of top performers and an increase to their bottom line.

I am very passionate about ensuring companies put the right person in the right role at the right time.
