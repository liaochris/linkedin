Todd Sampson’s Summary

Professional entrepreneur and technology strategist with more than 12 years experience in the online sector. Through cloudspace - a website and online application development company he co-founded in 1996 - and related ventures, Todd has helped hundreds of companies and thousands of individuals deliver outstanding returns on their online projects.

Today, Todd is CEO & Managing Director of cloudspace where he is working to simplify the planning, development and management of websites & next generation online applications. He is also an active partner in MyBlogLog and The DJ List.

Todd lives in Orlando, Florida with his wife Elisabeth and son Finn.
