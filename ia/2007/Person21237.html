David Berkowitz’s Summary

Seasoned software developer with (gasp) almost twenty five years experience, rising from the IBM ranks (programmer, architect, team leader) to become a successful manager/director at Philips and now Palm.

Shipped at least one product a year since 1983 on Windows, OS/2, UNIX, Mac, and embedded platforms — typically cutting-edge, complex, high-risk products, on accelerated schedules, under less than ideal circumstances.
