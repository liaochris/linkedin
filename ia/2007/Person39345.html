Jay Cuthrell’s Summary

William J Cuthrell (Jay) is Chief Technology Officer for NeoNova Network Services. NeoNova provides wholesale turnkey ISP to rural ILECs. As CTO, Mr. Cuthrell manages development and delivery of advanced Internet and telecommunications services for NeoNova Affiliates. Prior to his role as NeoNova's CTO, Mr. Cuthrell spent three years consulting at Scient (formerly iXL) as Manager of Software and Systems Engineering then later as Infrastructure Architect. While at Scient, he worked both domestically and internationally for Fortune 500 clients. Prior to his work at Scient, Mr. Cuthrell was Head of Internet Application Development at Nortel Networks within the Nortel Information Networks group. Mr. Cuthrell holds a B.S. in Materials Science and Engineering from North Carolina State University and lives in Raleigh, North Carolina.
