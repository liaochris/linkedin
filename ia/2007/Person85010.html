Karl Dawson’s Summary

Karl Dawson is in full-time employment with UK local government and specialises in web standards and accessibility issues.

He is a member of the Guild of Accessible Web Designers, a world-wide association of organisations and accessible web designers and developers - designed to both promote and protect accessible design standards. Karl is also a member of the Web Standards Group, again for web designers & developers who are interested in web standards and best practices (accessible sites using valid and semantically correct code).

web standards, accessibility, semantic markup, XHTML, HTML, CSS, web design, microformats, Microsoft Content Management System, WordPress, educator
