Fernando Olsina’s Summary

PhD in power economics and Researcher of the Argentinenan Research Council (CONICET) at Institute of Electrical Energy (IEE) with broad expertise in stochastic methods for power system & power markets simulation. Reviewer for a number of leading scholar journals. Solid experience in international consulting and technology transfer for power companies, utilities and government agencies.
