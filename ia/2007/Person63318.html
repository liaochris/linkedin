Nick Vaughn’s Summary

Nick played collegiate golf at Coffeyville Community College and then majored in Golf Course Management at Kansas State University. He worked in the golf industry for three years before his position with AFG Capital Management for two years and is now back in the golf industry. Nick lead AFG's "Circle of Friends" golf networking program as well as helping AFG find, play, and sponsor local golf tournaments to benefit the community and charitable events. He was also responsible for AFG's own charity golf tournament that helped raise funds to benefit the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society of Kansas City.

Currently Nick is working as a Teaching Professional at the GolfTEC Learning Center of Overland Park, KS.

GolfTEC utilizes the latest swing analysis and ball flight technology to provide the most accurate and fact-based swing development program possible. Our patented SEVA Pro teaching system combines video and motion measurement technology in one simple, easy-to-use software program. Our instructors are able to measure your swing to within 1/10th of a degree at any point during the motion. We then compare the movements that you make to those of our Tour Averages. GolfTEC has tested the swings of over 150 tour professionals with our motion measurement system and compiled the data into our Tour Averages, giving us the ideal range of motion throughout the swing. Basically, we wanted to understand what it is that the best players in the world do in the swing that allows them to hit the ball so far and so consistently. Now that we know those facts, we can better help our clients develop swing movements that are efficient, simple, consistent, and powerful! We use video and motion analysis to back up everything that we teach. 90% of our customers show improvement in their game and half come through referrals!!
