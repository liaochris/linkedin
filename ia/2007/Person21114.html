David Shearon’s Summary

My goal is to help lawyers and K-12 educators, leaders, and policymakers apply the findings of positive psychology to the challenges of systemic, organizational change and personal, meaningful engagement. I get high evaluations for speaking in both continuing legal education programs for lawyers and professional development opportunities for K-12 educators-leaders and policymakers. I am especially effective helping organizations find systemic change directions and mechanisms that "fit" with current structure, leadership, and strategic imperatives.

David Shearon’s Specialties:

Speaking, consulting, appreciative inquiry approaches to change -- all founded on a background in law, education policy, and positive psychology.
