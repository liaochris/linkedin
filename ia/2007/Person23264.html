Dev Tandon’s Summary

I develop insightful brand strategies and produce innovative marketing campaigns for world-class media, technology and consumer product brands.

I specialize in building creative processes and institutions and managing large teams. I have a proven track record of success managing strategy, creative and technical teams on complex content and marketing projects.

I’ve worked at premier advertising agencies including BBDO, Saatchi and Carmichael Lynch. I ran the New York office of management consultancy Kindred Keziah. I was the Vice President of Digital Strategy at the Hallmark Channel International.

Currently, I am President of Tangerine Tribe, a brand strategy firm I founded 3 years ago.

Highlights from my experience:

- Produced one of AOL's first ad splash pages for Pentax Camera at Carmichael Lynch in 1993

- Produced Apple Computer's entire digital marketing partnership with Mission:Impossible film starring Tom Cruise at BBDO in 1995

- Produced Disney Consumer Product's first worldwide extranet at Cow Interactive in 1996

- Produced the first consumer websites for Kahlua and Skyy Vodka at Swirl Advertisng in 1997

- Produced digital marketing campaign for C|Net's launch of computers.com at Saatchi & Saatchi in 1998.

- Produced 'Personalized Jeans' web-enabled retail kiosks for Levi Strauss at Swirl Advertising in 1999

- Developed strategy for Hallmark Channel's Virtual Channel delivered over the web and set-top box in 2001

- Developed innovative Instant Messaging for Active Traders interface for Charles Schwab at Kindred Keziah in 2003

- Designed, developed and produced entire web platform for Trunkt, a trade organization for art and design entrepreneurs in 2004 and continue to lead strategy and development today.

- Produced web interface for Kodak Picture Maker Kiosk deployed in major retailers including Wal-Mart, CVS and Walgreen's in 2006.
