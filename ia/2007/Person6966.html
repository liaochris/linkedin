Antonio Leonforte’s Summary

Software architect with 15+ years of professional experience (2 years spent abroad in UK and NL) on a wide range of application domains ranging from military real-time systems to large distributed information systems.

Contract professor in “Software Engineering” at the University of Perugia, Faculty of Computer Science. Member of the industrial committee of the Faculty of Computer Science at the University of Rome “TRE”.

Entrepreneur, co-founder of two companies in the software development and in the document management fields currently employing over 20 professionals. Now involved full-time in the LiveBase startup project.

In-depth technical knowledge, curious and business-savvy approach to research & development, excellent team-building and team-leading attitude.

Past jobs include:

* Chief Software Architect leading the design, development and deployment of SETA, the largest and most advanced Italian reservation and ticketing system. Owned by Lottomatica S.p.A., SETA is a large distributed information system based on CORBA and Oracle with a dedicated web portal and several hundreds dedicated terminals now selling tickets for more than 15 millions USD per month.

* Chief Software Architect and technology specialist in the development of the GAT, the data-analysis subsystem of the international HORIZON Naval Combat Management System. Developed for Datamat S.p.A., the system features an innovative visually programmable logic and a military-strength, distributed architecture based on a real-time implementation of the CORBA standard. The system is currently in operation on board the italian and french HORIZON fregades.

* Chief Software Architect of GEDAP, a large distributed information system. Developed for the “Presidenza del Consiglio dei Ministri”, the GEDAP client has been deployed in 10.000+ public offices all over the country, and has been in use for ten years before being replaced by the current web-based system.

Antonio Leonforte’s Specialties:

Management of large software projects, conceptual data modeling & database design, design & development of information systems and command/control systems with distributed object-oriented architecture, CORBA distributed-objects architectures, model-driven architectures and model-based code generation.
