Rimy Oberoi’s Summary

I am a Human Resources Professional with over 8 years of experience in companies such as HLL and ADOBE SYSTEMS

Career Skilling has been an area that i have keenly observed during my years as a HR professional. It is only thru career skilling that the gap between personal capability and organisational needs can be bridged. This gap is most acute at the entry level roles.

Oyster Learning is a Professional Learning Services company which provides Career Learning services targeted at entry level professionals. Services include career skill assessment, skill enhancement and placement.

Our vision is to become a change agent - creating employment of choice to India's youth.
