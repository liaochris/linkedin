Marc Cenedella’s Summary

It's always free to fill your $100K+ jobs at TheLadders.com!

We're the publisher of high-end jobs website TheLadders.com, and its daughter websites SalesLadder, MktgLadder, FinanceLadder, TechnologyLadder, LawLadder, HRLadder, OpsLadder, and UpLadder (all other $100K+ jobs).

Because we don't charge employers or recruiters to post or fill their $100K+ jobs, we see more jobs than any other resource on the planet -- about 30,000 new ones each month.

I founded TheLadders.com in 2003. And we've grown to over 850,000 subscribers since then. Subscribers pay $30 / month or $180 / year for our premium version.

I previously was the SVP, Finance & Operations at HotJobs.com, and led our company's sale to Yahoo! for $436 mm in 2002.

Prior to that, I was an investment professional at The Riverside Company, the leading LBO firm for the smaller end of the middle market. And after college, I founded, grew, and sold Forbes Pacifcia, a trading firm specialized in the export of US-made pet food to Japan.
