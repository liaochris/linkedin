Darren Rowse’s Summary

Blogging was a medium that Darren Rowse stumbled upon by accident in 2002 as he was researching a new project - little did he know that just 18 months later he'd be one of the world's first professional bloggers making a living from it.

Out of writing a popular personal blog that covered everything from politics, to religion, to cultural observations Darren launched Digital Photography Blog in 2003. This quickly became a commercial success and the launching pad for 20 other entrepreneurial blogs ranging in topic from gadget (printers, PDAs, camera phones etc) through to celebrity blogs, health blogs and more recently a blog about blogging professionally - ProBlogger.net

Darren is the owner of the 'LivingRoom Network' of Blogs, co-founder of the 'Breaking News Blog Network', co-founder of 'b5media blog network'co-founder of sixfigureblogging.com and the owner of ProBlogger.net.

He is currently working on a number of other blogging and non blogging related projects including a book about professional blogging. He also consults world wide with bloggers and companies interested in the medium, speaks on the topic of blogging and new media and is regularly featured in articles and interviews in off-line media.
