Jonathan Richardson’s Summary

A native of Houston, TX, Jonathan Richardson is a writer, public speaker and all around communicator. With experience in traditional public relations tactics and a passion for new media and conversation sparking tools, Jonathan is a believer in focusing less on one-way communications and more on developing conversations to communicate your success.

The University of West Florida has recognized Jonathan for his crisis communication and recovery assistance after Hurricane Ivan and he was most recently awarded an Outstanding Bureau Award by the Council of Better Business Bureaus for excellence in overall communications efforts. The Independent News even selected him as one of Pensacola's most lust worthy residents in a fun “Lust for the Arts” competition to raise money for arts programming in Pensacola.

Out of the office, Jonathan enjoys kayaking, surfing and crashing the local dog park with his precocious pug.
