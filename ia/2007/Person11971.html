Bob Walker’s Summary

I have a diverse background in both classical and technical marketing; strong skills in developing and managing marketing operations; and extensive experience in delivering demand generation results.

My goal is to fill an organization with selling opportunities so time is spent closing, rather than searching for business. I consider this to be marketing’s primary obligation to revenue growth and profitability.

Additional strengths include:

- the proven ability to build pro-active strategic marketing plans and strategies from corporate objectives,

- a solid background in globally marketing technical products and services,

- accomplished in all lead generation vehicles and Channel Sales development,

- developing metrics to forecast sales from marketing activity and the application of reporting processes to bring accountability to each marketing program,

- extensive experience in building branding programs and establishing value propositions,

- highly experienced in public relations, media/analyst relations and communications,

- a strong blend of hands-on leadership, project management, creativity and communications skills to guide cross-functional teams and external resources,

- expertise in development of revenue generating eCommerce websites, search engine optimization (SEO) and pay-per-click (PPC) strategies,

- and perhaps most important is my knowledge and expertise in Customer Relationship Management Programs (CRM), Incentive and Referral Programs, Alliance/Channel Partner Programs and Customer Retention Programs that play a significant role in today’s business environment.
