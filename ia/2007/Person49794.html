Lana Sherrard’s Summary

ABOUT ME:
I have worked within the UK Recruitment to Recruitment industry for 5 years on the HR, Marketing, Finance and Management functions as well as secured a couple of successful placements and ongoing relations with clients. Recently I have moved onto the Consultancy side as a Rec2Rec Consultant working predominately on placing Commercial, Construction, IT or Finance recruiters within the London & Home Country regions.

ABOUT MY COMPANY:
Big Red Seven is a highly successful, independent Recruitment to Recruitment consultancy.

Since the beginning of 2006, our aim has been to simply help like minded sales and recruitment professionals find their ideal recruitment roles without the associated hassle within Rec2Rec. Along the way we have forged highly respected relations with a selection of high profile recruitment consultancies of all sizes across the UK and Middle Eastern markets.

With the drive and determination our specialist consultants have to becoming the best, we aim to deliver a consultative, honest and reliable services that we feel is a refreshingly different, and tailored to meet the specific individual needs of both our clients and candidates.

Big Red Seven placing candidates with all levels of recruitment, including:

- Resourcer / Trainee Consultant
- Headhunter / Executive Search Consultant
- Recruitment Consultant / Senior Consultant
- Team Leader / Branch Manager
- Regional Manager / Director

If you would like to find our more about our services or our live vacancies visit our website on www.bigredseven.co.uk or contact one of our staff for a friendly chat on 0207 632 5920 or email jobs@bigredseven.co.uk
