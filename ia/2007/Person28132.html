Eswar Lingam’s Summary

I'm currently focused on building a highly efficient contingency staffing company working primarily with startups but also with bigger technology focused companies. Our background in software development, integration, we are better placed to understand the technical requirements and screen the candidates effectively thus improving the hit rate for our clients. I'm also responsible for strategic alliances with early stage startups where we develop the technology solutions for them, negotiating partnerships/contracts.

  • Project Lead, Atlas Core platform

    Diagnostic X-Ray Engineering, GE Medical Systems

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; GE; Medical Devices industry)

    September 1999 — April 2001 (1 year 8 months)

Service applications were delivered on a web platform and required Java and C++ interfaces.
