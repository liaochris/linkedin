Derek Organ’s Summary

We specialize in the delivery of professional ebusiness solutions. Specifically custom-built web application development, online communities, content management systems(CMS) ecommerce sites and intranet and extranet development.

Our initial focus is on development in the Irish, UK and United States markets with the main development centre here in Ireland. We also provide reseller rates to consultancy, design and marketing companies who want to provide web services as part of their product range. Jeebers effectively becomes a development center for these companies who find out-sourcing their web services more cost effective than in-house development.

Our services are designed to provide an integrated approach to business strategy with respect to the use of web technology. With a strong understanding of business combined with the flexibility of a number of platforms, network technologies and programming software, we are in a solid position to design and implement a comprehensive solution to meet your needs.

Ultimately Jeebers provides excellent value and our quality of work is used as our best advertisement.

Some of our work includes www.1time.ie and C-ads digital signage sofware.
