Steve Saxon’s Summary

At Neudesic, I help our clients design, develop and deploy complex solutions using Microsoft techologies. My specialization is largely in the ASP.NET/WinForms space, but I get involved in discussions going all the way back into the Enterprise.

As Practice Manager for Custom Application Development in our Western Region, I am also responsible for driving our internal product development strategy, staff training, watching the horizon for the Next Big Thing and for the overall success of all Neudesic's Custom Application Development projects.

Prior to Neudesic, I designed and led development of the www.dell.com web application and the globalization/UI framework underneath it since 1998. This system was initially developed around a custom script engine written in C++, then moved to C#/ASP.NET at the end of 2000, then used to rewrite all of the Dell.com online applications (cart/checkout, My Account, Premier, Support etc.)

Steve Saxon’s Specialties:

Complex enterprise application design, developing highly modular rich clients, object oriented design, software globalization, writing very high performance dynamic websites, writing secure code
