Pete Hornby’s Summary

I am a bright, personable engineer with an enormously broad skill set and a proven willingness to embrace new challenges. I am persistent, hard-working and strongly customer-focused.

I have over 30 years of experience at Unisys Corporation, including 13 years in customer support in the UK and Europe, and over 17 years in software development in the engineering facility in Mission Viejo, California.

My work in software development has been characterised by increasing levels of responsibility and a proven track record in delivering quality products on time. I have worked in projects relating to system and application security, TCP/IP networking, systems management, real-time messaging and business continuity.

My technical interests are wide-ranging. My primary focus is the Microsoft environment, but I also follow more general areas, such as networking, security and language development.

I'm very interested in the issues facing enterprise customers. I attended the Microsoft Management Summit (MMS) in 2006, 2006 and 2007, and have been involved with Microsoft's attempts to bring a modeling discipline to the enterprise environment, centered on what is now called the Service Modeling Language (SML). I have also worked extensively in the areas of Windows-LINUX interoperability and virtualization, especially VMware and Microsoft Virtual Server.

I'm an experienced speaker, and am very comfortable presenting to large and small audiences. I have presented to many user conferences, to customers in sales situations, and to Unisys employees in training and tutorial situations.

I'm an accomplished technical writer and editor, accustomed to producing high quality work to deadline.

Pete Hornby’s Specialties:

I'm an expert programmer in C++ and C#, using Microsoft's Visual Studio. I am very experienced in the use of COM and the .Net framework. I have some experience in Java development, using Eclipse.
