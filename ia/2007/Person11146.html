Bill Dwyer’s Summary

Consultant & advisor for increasing and realizing the value of mid-size business services firms.

Consultant on strategy, marketing, M&A, sales training, customer service to hardware, software and services companies.

CEO, COO and CFO for professional services companies. Good skills and experience in strategy, operations management, margin improvement. CEO of TAOS - The Sys Admin Company from '92 - '01. Took company from $3 million to $90 million annual revenue with exceptional margins. CFO for Sterling Federal Systems. Authored over 100 federal and commercial proposals, primary player in two ground breaking contract award protests both of which resulted in a directed award to my firm.
