Tom Hughes’s Summary

A former attorney with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission, I specialize in advertising and consumer law. I monitor false advertising and consumer law developments at the Federal Trade Commission, National Advertising Division, state Attorneys General, and in civil lawsuits filed under the Lanham Act, and distills those developments into useful solutions. I also prosecute and defend suits under the Lanham Act, challenges at the National Advertising Division, and defend matters before the Federal Trade Commission and state Attorneys General.
