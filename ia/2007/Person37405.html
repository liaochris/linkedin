Jonathan Adams’s Summary

As VP, Group Director with Digitas NY (formerly Modem Media CT), Jonathan carries media management responsibilities for several accounts, including Delta Air Lines, as well as certain duties within the senior leadership of the NY and CT Media Services department.

Mr. Adams is recognized as an industry leader having served as Chairman of the AAAA Digital Marketing Committee (2003 - 2006), selected as a "Digital Player" by Advertising Age in 2003, and also selected as one of the "100 People to Know in Media" by Media Magazine (traditional edition) in 2004.

Mr. Adams, most recently held the title of Senior Partner and Group Media Director of WPP's OgilvyOne (and mOne), the interactive and direct response media arm of Ogilvy Worldwide and Mindshare at the time.

While at OgilvyOne, Adams founded the Digital Media Consultancy, an in-house consulting practice devoted to creating and nurturing deep partnerships/alliances both between clients and with leading digital brands. Prior to his seven years with mOne, Jonathan acquired much of his experience at traditional agencies including Lowe & Partners and The Media Edge.

During his tenure, he has managed a number of clients including Bristol-Myers Squibb, Motorola, American Express, Sears.com, IBM, Kimberly-Clark, Procter & Gamble, Marriott Hotels and Unilever.

He currently holds advisory board posts with iMedia, the Advertising Research Foundation (ARF), the Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB), The Weather Channel and Google.

A native of Miami, Florida, Jonathan joined the New York advertising community in 1991. He graduated from the University of Florida with a B.S. in advertising and management, and lives in Stamford, Connecticut, with his wife, Karen, and twin boys.
