Angela Ciccotti’s Summary

Angela Ciccotti is an Innovative and Entrepreneurial executive who has held key leadership positions in several successful start-up ventures. Angela has had a successful 30 year career of broad senior leadership experience including domestic and international experience with Clear Channel Communications and Time Warner Divisions. Angela has significant multi-industry experience in senior management, marketing and business development for both product and service companies within large established and mid-sized companies, start-ups and turnarounds. Angela has had consistent success with strategy development, turnarounds and leading groups of staff. Angela is the Executive Vice President, Chief Marketing Officer for Safeplace USA and is responsible for marketing and advertising operations.
