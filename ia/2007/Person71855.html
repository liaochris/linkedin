René Dechamps Otamendi’s Summary

Been addicted to the Internet since he got his first home internet connexion back in 1995, René has a real passion for the Internet Industry. René has worked in the 'agency side' of the Internet since 2001 first at Oniros and then by creating his own agency OX2 in 2003. Since 2003 OX2 has specialized in Web Analytics and the level of expertise of this field is now recognised internationaly, OX2 being considered by Industry membersas one of the European leaders in the field of Web Analytics.
Since October 2007, Eric T. Peterson (most renowned Web Analytics Guru) has joined OX2 as Director and shareholder which brings a new perspective and a great Web Analytics mind to René's Company.

René's objective is thus to continue to maintain the level of expertise while managing and pursuing the growth of our activities on a panEuropean level.
