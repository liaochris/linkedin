Letter of recommendation of my HR manager from Philips"

"As a Personnel Manager of IPLC CFL-I within Philips Lighting Poland S.A. it is with great pleasure that I respond to Marcin Holub request for a letter of recommendation.

Marcin Holub has worked for Philips Lighting since October 1999, initially as a development engineer. In April 2000 he was introduced as a Product Manager within Supply Group Compact Fluorescent Lamps (CFL-I) Europe, responsible for Professional products and Private Labels. Main areas of responsibilities of Marcin included portfolio management, defining specification with customers, coordination of Market Introduction Process for the product category, packaging artwork release. In this period he as a project leader for CFL-I successfully managed Professional Portfolio Repositioning Project, which included range definitions, packaging preparation and managing phase in phase out process. Cooperation within Philips included customers, namely Country Sales Organizations, Marketing Groups, Regions as well as Philips Global Technical Development and factories.

In April 2002 he was given an opportunity to make his next step in his career and was nominated a manager of Product Management Department. This new challenge included additionally:
· coordination of department activities
· becoming a member of management team
· taking a responsibility for Customer Results criterion of Philips Business Excellence world class quality management system
· carrying Customer Satisfaction Survey of SG CFL-I
· taking part in defining and realizing product strategy by participating in European Category Team.
