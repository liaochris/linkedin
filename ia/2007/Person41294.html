Jill Walker Rettberg’s Summary

I research the ways we tell stories online, and have studied hypertext fiction, weblogs, World of Warcraft, MOOs, electronic literature and spam fiction. I'm currently writing a book about blogging for Polity Press and co-editing an anthology of critical essays about World of Warcraft for MIT press. I frequently give talks about blogs, electronic literature and the web.

Jill Walker Rettberg’s Specialties:

Blog research, new media research, narratology, teaching with blogs, lectures on blogs and new media.
