Dan Cave’s Summary

Initially I trained as a programmer which was my primary interest but after discovering Unix at College I quickly realised that my potential was more focused around that. Being introduced to the internet in 1995 I became absorbed in that and the related technologies. Since then, I've worked with three internet providers, several blue chip companies and Unix vendors. Each company that I have worked at have allowed me to gain very valuable skills and experience of what works for each industry. I constantly strive to learn new techniques and technologies to allow my current and future employers to get the best from me, that I may get the best from myself.
