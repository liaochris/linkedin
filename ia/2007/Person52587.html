Luis Ahumada’s Summary

I am interested in entrepreneurial opportunities ~ coming in both as a provider of capital & managerial support. Please let me know if you have any interesting ideas or projects in mind.

As of now, I have been working for the last 6 months in my new startup - Kiobo (www.kiobo.com). Kiobo is a visual tool that will enable people to surf people's (friends / family / strangers) social-graph and attention-data / clickstream. Thus far, my partner and I, have bootstrapped the company investing our own capital to hire a full-time team of 5 developers to design and develop our idea. It is 95% done. We are expecting to launch an alpha release of the site in the next 3-4 weeks. We are also in dire need of someone with a technical background to join the team at the founder level (we are currently 2 founders). The individual we are looking for should have extensive web-development experience, knowledge in ruby (our platform) and should have a solid educational background in Computer Science or related technical field from a top school. Please contact me if you are interested!
