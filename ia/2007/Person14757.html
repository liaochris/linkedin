Carolyn Hayden’s Summary

I currently provide strategic consulting services to senior executives and mangers responsible for major change initiatives in major companies, foundations, and nonprofits. I provide leadership and support for project teams to ensure goals are well defined and that results are achieved in a timely manner. Consulting clients include Fannie Mae, Catholic Charities, the Annie E. Casey, Ford, and Charles Stewart Mott Foundations, and the National Economic Development and Law Center.

In my previous senior management roles in national nonprofit organizations, I have manged internal operations, led national initiatives in the fields of asset building and community strengthening, created sustainabile social enterprise organizations, developed national programs, and created national partnerships with entities such as HUD and Fannie Mae.

I am an Annie E. Casey Foundation's Children and Family Fellow, with a focus on the study and implementation of effective public-private partnerships designed to improve outcomes for low-income children and families.

Prior to my work in the nonprofit sector, I was co-owner of a company that provided marketing and service support to over 20,000 American Express business and retail establishments in four Mid-western states. In addition, I was selected for Ford Motor Company’s National Minority Dealer Training Program and owned a Ford/Lincoln/Mercury automobile dealership in Texas.

I have authored and/or contributed to a case study of faith-based organizations and their efforts to support family economic success, and a guide to assist organizations desiring to start car ownership programs to benefit low-income purchasers.
