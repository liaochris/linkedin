Passakorn Hongsyok’s Summary

Passakorn went through the usual engineering corporate routine; starting from engineering field in telecommunication (telephony network), getting some overseas exposure by working in SIEMENS Singapore for 2 years, switching to product development in 1999 at a Bangkok telecom operator at TRUE, holding an Assistant Director as his last position there in 2004, in charge of sales. He then managed to get an MBA at Sasin and, at the same time, managing a company with his friend at Miraclenet Group until 2006.

He also owns Tai-Tan Court (http://www.taitancourt.com), his own venture at Phuket, a room-for-rent in Phuket city.

He then found http://www.changkhui.com, producing a few podcasts with his friends, as a hobby, but contemplating to turn this into a real business one of these days.
