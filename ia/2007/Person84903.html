David Teten’s Summary

I serve as CEO of Nitron Advisors (www.NitronAdvisors.com ), an independent research firm which provides institutional investors (mainly hedge funds, VCs, and private equity firms) and law firms with access to frontline industry experts.

The American Management Association released last year my new book, "The Virtual Handshake: Opening Doors and Closing Deals Online" (coauthored with Scott Allen). You can download the book for free at http://www.TheVirtualhandshake.com .

I speak frequently at conferences on topics such as “How to Quadruple Your Sales", "How to Raise Capital", and other topics. To see slides and other material from these speeches, visit http://www.Teten.com/speaker .
