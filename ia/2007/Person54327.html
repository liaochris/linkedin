Marc Stewart’s Summary

Highly motivated professional recruiter and manager with the ability to establish and maintain relationships. A well-organized problem solver with excellent communication and presentation skills. Extensive experience placing candidates in both technical and non-technical positions. Experience placing contractors, right-to-hire, and permanent employees. Strategic sourcing techniques included cold calling, reference recruiting, referral prospecting, company proprietary databases, direct souring, personal candidate pipeline, Internet, and job fairs. Management experience includes mentoring, coaching, and motivating junior and senior recruiters.

Extensive experience training recruiters on sourcing techniques, performing interviews, compensation negotiations (hourly/salary rates, relocation expense, etc.), negotiating with third parties, transferring H-1B visas, interview preparation, reference checking, candidate relocations, and management of candidates throughout the “interview-to-hire” stages.
