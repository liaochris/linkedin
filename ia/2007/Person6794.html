Anthony Benedetti’s Summary

Why I am Here on Linked In:

I’m here to offer my experience solving high-level business problems to companies that seek to create or respond to disruptive change in their marketplace.

What is required by such situations is real innovation. Discovering a truly innovative solution begins with asking the right questions. If we start by asking the right questions, we’ll get the right answers.

We're all business people. We all know the formula. Assemble your team. Set goals and objectives, analyze the alternatives, select a solution and implement it. It sounds right. It feels right. Yet for some reason it just doesn’t work.

It doesn’t work because what I have just described is a decision-making process, not a problem solving process. Decision-making implies that all the alternatives are known. But when you set out to impact the marketplace in a truly innovative way, how can you say you know what the alternatives are? How can you set goals and objectives when you haven’t considered how the rules of the game have changed?

Forget about goals and objectives. When the purpose is bring about real innovation, goals and objectives become like a “You Are Here” dot on a very large, undefined map. What you need is to discover how the Business Truth for your company has changed. Then re-architect your business in alignment with that truth.

This is the essence of what I do. I call it Convergence Problem Solving, and I am an expert in facilitating this process. I have applied it across a wide range of industries including financial services, wireless, internet, entertainment, retail, health, construction, and freight.

In the course of my career I’ve played a variety of roles, including strategy consultant, project manager, product developer, team leader, marketing strategist, and researcher. In every instance, I have used Convergence Problem Solving to facilitate and forge innovative solutions. It is what’s unique about me and what defines everything I do.
