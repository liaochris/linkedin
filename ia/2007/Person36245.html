Ilise Benun’s Summary

I am the founder of Marketing Mentor, a one-on-one coaching program for small business owners, independent professionals and the "creatively self-employed."

During the 6-month program, we will teach you everything you need to know about self promotion and marketing. We’ll help you set realistic goals, show you how to reach them and we’ll keep you on track (and accountable) with a weekly phone call.

We'll teach you how to integrate into your workday the most essential (and relatively inexpensive) marketing tools, including networking, cold calling, email marketing, web site marketing, public relations and more.

With your Marketing Mentor by your side, you’ll get the work you want to grow your business.

Ilise Benun’s Specialties:

We specialize in working with creative professionals (designers, writers, video producers, photographers) as well as professional service providers (lawyers, consultants, speaking coaches, personal trainers, computer experts and even other marketing consultants) -- anyone offering a B2B service.
