About me:
I am an innovative entrepreneur, international career coach, writer, and founder of Global Relocation. I have 16 years of experience working with high technology and biotech companies in divergent cultural environments and markets ( U.S. , Israel & Canada ). I have a Masters degree with excellence in Social Work (M.S.W) and a B.A. in Social Science from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel. Over the years I have produced HR and career knowledge.
My passion is to enhance professionals & companies which face rapid changes & challenges in the local and international markets. With my vast experience over the years I have learned that nothing can stop you from achieving your desires & goals.

Past experience

Previously, I was the vice president of a practice group within JobPlex in New York and I provided HR solutions to high technology and biotech companies. JobPlex is part of the DHR group of Companies which is ranked the fifth largest search firm in North America with 40 offices throughout the U.S. and Canada.
During a five year period I established Dyokan which focused and specialized in hi-tech and bio-tech recruitments in Tel Aviv, Israel. Dyokan specialized in the search and recruitment of superior talents for their clients. Prior to starting Dyokan I was a manager at one of the largest national recruitment companies in Israel – ORS. Prior to this , I was a project manager at the Government Renewal Project in Tel Aviv, Israel.
