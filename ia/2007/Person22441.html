Deanna Cross’s Summary

Automotive retail and finance upper management and training experience for over 25 years.

Created Web properties to help women consumers, car dealers, automakers, career seekers to help change the way we buy and sell cars in today's world.

85% of car buying decision makers and 50% of car buyers are women. Only 2% of women are employed in the auto retail and finance field.

WomensAutomotiveNetwork.com online tutorial that utilizes auto related affiliate web sites to teach women how their credit works, how to obtain loans, trade-ins, warranties, negotiating, accessories, various job positions, etc.

WomensAutomotiveNetwork.TV launched Nov '06 as an extension site. First auto industry connected VIDEO web site.

Sections include:
Three .TV shows with how to and DIY advice on auto related topics on buying, selling, ownership issues. Curb Side Chat, Make That Savvy Car Deal and CARNUZ with news that matters most to women on safety, styles, economy, ecology, etc.

New Car Models: Approved by each automaker to present their news and info in an entertaining and educational manner. We covered the LA auto show. Photo and video gallery.

Your Videos: Social networking to share auto related experiences and topics that empower women.

Your Car 4 Sale: New for consumers to sell cars on video classifieds.

Dealer Directory: New for dealers to introduce themselves over a video presentation so consumers or job seekers can have a virtual tour of the dealership and personel before actually going there to visit.

Who's Online: links to affiliate web sites. Promotes women businesses and organizations.

Accessories, Books and Gifts: shopping mall where viewers can purchase auto accessories and electronics, books, DVD's, etc. Reviews of certain items and services of interest.

We have received attention from CBS Radio, NBC, automakers, auto dealer magazines, top womens organizations, Google, MSN, Yahoo, AOL and more.
