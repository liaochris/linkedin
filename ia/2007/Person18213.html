Colin Lippitt’s Summary

Having worked on two major commercial TV start-up companies in the UK: Channel 4 and Carlton Television. The latter as Head of Operations.

As well as a varied catalogue of companies I have worked for my early career has seen work as a Broadcast Engineer for both the BBC and ITV Sport, working on communication logistics for World Cup Football and Olympic events. I also have spent time working in Hollywood for a large post-production company involved with leading edge film and computer graphic systems.

My background in electronics and project management skills were beneficial for the work I undertook designs for Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) in the UK and was prominently involved with the winning project team for British Digital Broadcasting, that later became ITV Digital. This led onto designing the worlds first commercial playout centre to provide the first licensed DTT programmes anywhere in the world.

I am very active in the research and development of file exchange formats and closely involved with third party companies in schemes that involve ‘intelligent’ asset management. Consequently, I am a member of and actively involved with various European pressure groups that are seeking worldwide standards for the broadcast industry.

Combined with these background skills I have focused on the media industry led initiatives that demand sea-change working practices and cost efficient solutions for the entire business end-to-end model.

Recently, to emphasize this aspect of business needs I have successfully launched an outsourcing project on behalf of BBC Worldwide, in their attempt to enter the digital archive market by delivering a digital asset management and archive strategy for the benefit of the BBC Audiobooks division. Another world first for me and the BBC’s commercial arm!

Colin Lippitt’s Specialties:

Very good at understanding technology, cost values and resource capabilities. I can efficiently run multiple task projects and ensure delivery.

Vast experience at negotiating ITT/RFP processes, Service Level Agreements and legal compliance issues.
