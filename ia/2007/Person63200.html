Natalie Glance’s Summary

Natalie S. Glance is a senior research scientist at Nielsen BuzzMetrics. A physicist by training, her research interests have landed her somewhere in the intersection of collaborative information systems, information extraction and discovering global behavior from local actions. In the past few years, she has focused on automatic means for discovering trends in the weblog community and for visualizing social networks in on-line message boards.
