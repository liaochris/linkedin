Grant Halverson’s Summary

Some folks call me a jack-of-all-trades, but I prefer the term Renaissance man. I can organize a photo shoot, make the images, edit the perfect frame, design the publication it goes in, get the pages ready to go on the press, and post the content to the web. I also create multimedia content combining audio, video and still images into a compelling online experience.

My experience ranges from pet-of-the-week to Game 7 of the Stanley Cup finals. My full-time job is as the Director of Photography and Multimedia at The Cary News in the Triangle area of North Carolina, where I am now leading the charge into online journalism for a total of four community newspapers. I am also a contract photographer with Getty Images, where I cover local and national sporting events. I previously worked as a contract photographer with the Associated Press for 10 years, covering state and national news and sports events.

Grant Halverson’s Specialties:

Photography, photo editing, layout and graphic design, prepress/production, audio/video editing, multimedia design. Software skills include the Adobe Creative Suite (PhotoShop, InDesign, Illustrator), Quark XPress and Final Cut Pro.
