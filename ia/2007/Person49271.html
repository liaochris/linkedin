Geertje Borgsteijn’s Summary

KsiRon Creative is a division of KsiRon International bvba/sprl and committed to rendering expert services to the corporate community. By adding Virtual Assistant (VA) services to its range, KsiRon International answers the increasing demand for ad-hoc quality admin services, desktop publishing, website building, copywriting, editing, and translation needs.

Geertje Borgsteijn’s Specialties:

Our Virtual Assistants (VAs) are independent entrepreneurs providing administrative,creative and/or technical services on a temporary,contractual basis. Highly advanced technological tools ensure flawless communication,data transmission and project preparation and delivery. Our VAs are not dependent on office hours and accommodate ad-hoc project needs in the fields of Admin Services, Translations; Desktop Publishing; Website Building; Editing & Copywriting; Event Organization
