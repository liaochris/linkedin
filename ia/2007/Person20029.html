Daniel Cheah’s Summary

Daniel has 16 years of IT Sales and Technical experience and 3 years of IT recruitment experience in Hong Kong and China. His recruitment is focused on mid-level to senior-level technology roles covering Asia Pacific.

He has recently recruited General Manager for a IT equipment vendor, Regional Sales Director (North Asia) for a US-based Systems Integration firm, Presales Engineer for a US-based CRM software firm, Training Manager for a CISCO Training Centre and Regional Presales for an EAI/SOA software vendor from USA.

Before joining the Executive Search business, Daniel has 16 years work experience in the IT & Telecom industry with more recent focus in Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Enterprise Resource Planning systems (ERP), Business Intelligence (BI), Marketing Campaign Solutions and Call Centre Industry.

Daniel's previous working experience includes being a Sales Director for a CRM Systems Integration firm with telecoms and financial services industry (FSI) clients in Hong Kong and China. He is responsible for business development, partner alliances and Channel Sales for a global CRM software firm. He was involved in project management and e-commerce consulting for a listed Swedish-based Internet consulting firm.

He was in the founding Executive Committee (EXCO) of the Hong Kong Call Centre Association (HKCCA) from 1999-2001. HKCCA was formed by a group of call centre users to help local organisations improve their customer service by deploying the best management practices (Process Improvement, People Management and Technology).
