Dieter Skiba [LION]’s Summary

"A Global Board - Being globally Connected" - Center for "New Work" Munich
I´ve got inspired to that by "I Have A Dream" from Martin Luther King.

Globalization has transformed the way how we work and coooperate.
Many companies have to reduce their staff and people feel left alone
with the consequences of that process.

There is a great need for "New Work", New Business Models and Ideas.
There is a great potential in each of us, if we establish global friendships
with other creative entrepreneurs with a great heart and great dreams.
Social Software opens new ways to cooperate with high motivated persons
to create together a different business culture and a new spirit.

Global Teams of Social Entrepreneurs being globally connected
with likeminded visionary honest people in other organisations.
Global solidarity, a global community, a real world society
with no ethnical, cultural or mental borders.
Using the true potential of social software to connect hearts,
to promote innovate ideas and concepts worldwide
from many different nations, building bridges from heart to heart,
mobilizing the true potential in each of us for a common purpose.

There are many talented people, but their true potential is not recognized.
They are not in a right position to realize all their dreams and visions.
And thera are many others working on similar projects and ideas
but they don´t know about the existence of other groups with similar goals.
There are many entrepreneurs, initiatives and groups in other social networks
but they don´t cooperate effectively to make a difference.

My project is to build up such a network of coaches, trainers and institutions
to work with "New Work" of Frithjof Bergmann - to do "what you really want".

More information about the Center for "New Work" in Munich
Ein Zentrum für "Neue Arbeit - Neue Kultur" im Raum München
