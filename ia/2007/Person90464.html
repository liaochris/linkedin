William Azaroff’s Summary

I am William Azaroff, an interactive experience & marketing strategist based in Vancouver, BC with a strong background in online community building focused on extending brand loyalty and increasing engagement.

I'll be speaking about how Financial Institutions can use the social web to engage their customers and prospects at Net.Finance in Scottsdale, Arizona in April 2007.

I'm very proud of my work spearheading ChangeEverything.ca, an online social network for people interested in change in Vancouver, Victoria and the Lower Mainland. It was created by Vancity, Canada's largest credit union, where I work as Interactive Marketing & Channel Consultant.

I have developed and managed web projects in several verticals, including Financial Services, Entertainment & Media, Telecommunications and Automotive. Prior to Vancity, I worked at new media companies and digital agencies in Seattle, Los Angeles and Vancouver. My client list has included Disney, Honda, 20th Century Fox, TELUS, Nike Jordan, the Government of British Columbia, Focus Features, Intuit Canada, Universal Pictures, the Vancouver Port Authority, The WB, Nestlé and AtomFilms.

William Azaroff’s Specialties:

Creative strategy, interactive marketing, web user experience, the social web, online community building, project management, team leadership, interactive channel planning, metrics & analytics, public speaking.
