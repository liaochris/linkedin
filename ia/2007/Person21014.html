David Allen Ibsen’s Summary

David is a senior marketing executive with a passion for discovering the solutions to successfully market a company and its products. David has spent over 20 years working in corporate and entrepreneurial settings with some of the top consumer and technology brands. David develops innovative marketing programs that deliver business results.

For the last 8 years David has been consulting at both emerging companies (30+ clients) and with established brands. Under his business name "5 Meetings Before Lunch" (www.5meetings.com) David works with leaders of companies and marketing departments who want to advance great ideas and catalyze change in their marketing efforts.

Each consulting engagement is unique and custom-tailored to client needs. That said, assignments often include the development and delivery of:

-Market leadership strategies and communications messaging
-Communication campaigns (advertising, design, public relations, and more)
-Sales tools
-New brand experiences
-Building and training programs for marketing organizations
-Serving as "virtual" VP of Marketing or VP of Client Services, building these departments and eventually hiring full-time successors

Engagements range from hourly or project-based assignments, to long term full time staff-surrogate arrangements.

He is currently looking for his next assignment.
