Mark Bean’s Summary

I have worked in the document management and imaging industry for over 16 years now.

My goal is to make Altien the number one 'premium' client for document management.

Altien is the only ECM client that works with all the major ECM platforms and is specifically designed for knowledge workers. Think of it as Google meets Windows Explorer meets ECM.

ECM vendors spend much of their time working on the back end server side of things - and making sure information is available at the speed of light is no small feat.

All we do at Altien is the UI - making document management finally a compelling thing for the end user. It's easy to learn and efficient for frequent daily use.

I run a blog http://office20.com - Office 2.0 keeps busy professionals up to date with all the latest trends in Enterprise Content Management and Office productivity tools.
