Mark Wilkinson’s Summary

Having spent almost my entire career at Intel, I have adopted Intel's standard career pattern of spending 2-3 years in a group, learning different parts of Intel's business.

I started out in marketing, and through various job changes, have gained experience in everything from high-stakes certification testing and program management (for the Intel Certification Program, Intel's technical certification), to cooperative marketing program management and customer account experience (helping train and influence Intel's large OEM customers to participate effectively in the world's largest cooperative marketing program).

Through it all, I have developed my strategic thinking skills, but have kept firmly grounded in actual on-time, under budget deliverables. A significant part of my time at Intel has been spent working with customers that buy thrrough Intel's distributers, customers we call "the channel" at Intel, as well as time working with Intel's multi-national computer manufacturer customers.
