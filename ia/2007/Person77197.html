Bob Schwartz’s Summary

Bob joined Clearstone in April 2006 as an Entrepreneur in Residence, focused on identifying and developing the next wave of great consumer Internet, eCommerce & broadband media companies. His web experience emphasizes eCommerce 2.0 (Social Media + eCommerce) and companies with a platform of empowerment to individuals and "mini-preneurs".

Bob has established himself as a visionary business builder of consumer-facing internet companies with a successful track record building multiple companies delivering over $1Billion+ in revenue, achieving top web site rankings, and leadership in diverse areas such as fashion, shoes, paid content and personal computers.

Bob's career includes: President of USSearch.com (SRCH), where he turned around this public consumer Internet company and grew it into a leading on-line content site (consumer reunite, locate, background) with over 6 million unique visitors per month and merged it to form First Advantage (FADV); Managing Director of eColony, Internet Capital Groups (ICGE) early stage incubator; Founder and GM/EVP of Nordstrom.com where he built the leading fashion apparel Internet company and led the venture funded spin-off from Nordstrom Inc; and he was the youngest Vice President for leading consumer PC manufacturer Packard Bell Computers where he built two consumer direct divisions.

He also serves as an Board member and interim executive to multiple fast growth consumer Internet and eCommerce companies leveraging his consumer technology experience to guide companies into their growth stage by crafting growth strategies, product development innovations, establishing key partnerships, driving customer acquisition and securing financing. Most recent companies focus on Media/Technology, Social Shopping and Platforms of Empowerment:
- Portero.com, Kevo.com, MediaShift.com, Entertainment Media Works, StarStyle.com, KickApps, MobileTattoo, Fireclick.com, IntelliFit, LiveBid.com

www.SchwartzGroup.com or www.Clearstone.com
