Michael Tortolano’s Summary

Technology consultant to the digital home entertainment industry, natural software architect and experienced business entrepreneur.

Current role at Codevio focuses on providing consultancy and software development services to our customers who primarily operate in the PC digital entertainment, consumer electronics, corporate media distribution, hospitality and home networking sectors.

One of the founders of Codevio and a director. Previously one of the founders and visionaries behind Home Media Networks, where we grew this startup company into a successful business culminating in a trade sale after 6 years.

HMN was the developer of the highly influential ShowShifter product line, the first PC based Media Centre product. ShowShifter pioneered the 10ft UI on the PC platform combined with Digital Video Recording, DVB Digital TV, Audio Jukebox and Picture viewing functionality.

ShowShifter is widely acknowledged to have inspired and defined the PC digital media centre market place and its influence can easily be seen today in products such as Windows Media Center.
