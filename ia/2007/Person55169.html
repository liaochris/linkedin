Mark Middlebrook’s Summary

Mark Middlebrook is senior vice president and manager of
City National Bank's eCommerce Group. With 18 years of experience in the banking industry, including 11 years specializing in eCommerce, he directs the online strategy to support the company's corporate goals as well as generate revenue.

Mr. Middlebrook is responsible for the bank's public Web sites, cnb.com and a private extranet site, as well as the intranet, the firm's primary employee communication portal that offers daily news, more than 30 dedicated departmental sites and an external intranet for offsite workers. In addition, he is responsible for launching and supporting RSS and podcast feeds, making the bank one of the first financial firms to use this technology to offer content developed by its own experts.

Mr. Middlebrook also provides expertise to technology-based promotions targeted to clients and prospects, directs the interactive marketing program offering state-of-the-art graphics and animation to clients and prospects.

Before joining City National, Mr. Middlebrook was assistant vice president and AOL channel manager at Union Bank of California, where he was responsible for managing all products on the bank's AOL Web site, BankNOW™ and strategic alliances. Before that, Mr. Middlebrook worked at Home Savings of America, where he managed and launched Internet funds transfer and bill pay services.

Mr. Middlebrook earned an MBA from the University of LaVerne, with an emphasis on information technology, and a bachelor's in marketing management from California State Polytechnic University of Pomona. He also earned a certificate in Successfully Managing a Technology and Product Strategy from MIT Sloan.

Active in the community, Mr. Middlebrook serves on the University of La Verne's Business Advisory Board and was founder and the first president of the University’s Business Alumni Association.
