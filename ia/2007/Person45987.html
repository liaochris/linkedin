Kanchan Nayak’s Summary

I have been working as a writer for over 5 years now. My core competency is to build a story around a brand and make it appealing to my target audience. It isn't as easy as it seems as I need to get into the psychographics of my audience before i weave communication that targets them. In short that means, understanding their likes/dislikes, interests, goals, their pattern of living, spending habits amongst other things. It is only when i have an in-dept understanding do i venture into strategising for the brand. Every word that i pick is suited for the target
audience. I write to connect with the audience. Selling, isn't about merely profits. It's about building relationships. After having spent the years that i have, i cannot take my audience for granted. I try and do justice to each and every brief that i get.

Kanchan Nayak’s Specialties:

I am partial to ambient media when i suggest communication solutions to clients. I also try and use a lot of below-the-line media as i feel it's more in-the-face than a couple of releases. I specialise in print medium. I take pride in working with the servicing department on strategies. Soon, i intend on providing effective film solutions to clients.
