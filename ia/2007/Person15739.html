Charlene McArthur, CPA’s Summary

Currently overseeing the planning and analysis functions for Foss Maritime and subsidiaries with a bit of special project and acquisition work peppered in for taste.

A diversified, extensive proven-track record is the result of 15 years of financial leadership. I have worked in manufacturing, hospitality, high tech distribution, marine transportation, education, non-profit, consulting and have no problem crossing the lines that divide the industries. Although the lingo changes, the people and the challenges managers face know no boundaries. People are people, whether they are professional musicians, web designers, barristas or tug boat captains.

I strive to achieve goals by planning, managing, executing, evaluating, revising and re-evaluating. I am an excellent change manager and am not threatened by change or evolution.
