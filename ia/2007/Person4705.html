Alexander Muse’s Summary

Alexander is a serial entrepreneur with more than a decade of startup experience. Prior to becoming CEO of Big in Japan, Alexander served as CEO of Architel, a provider of outsourced information technology services. Previously, he was founder and CEO of LayerOne, a telecommunications infrastructure company he started in the late 1990’s.

Alexander member of various organizations including the Entrepreneurs Foundation of North Texas (EFTN), the Young Entrepreneurs Organization (YEO), the Association for Corporate Growth (ACG) and NATPE (television industry group).
