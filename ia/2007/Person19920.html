Dan Geiger’s Summary

Dan Geiger, Executive Director of the U.S. Green Building Council - Northern California Chapter, and also Principal of Geiger Consulting, has extensive executive and consulting experience, specializing in business and strategic planning, fund raising, nonprofit technology and internet strategy, and organizational development. Recent clients include GoLightly.com, DemocracyInAction.org, Rockridge Institute, Green Media Toolshed, Moving Ideas Network, Santa Cruz Barrios Unidos, Women’s Funding Network, and CompassPoint. Previously, he was the Executive Director of Groundspring.org, an organization providing Internet services to nonprofits, and Co-Founder and CEO of OpNet, a nationally recognized program that prepares low-income people for the digital economy. He was also Director of Development of the Vanguard Public Foundation, and Marketing Manager and Account Executive for Wang Laboratories, Inc. Mr. Geiger holds an MBA from the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley.

Dan Geiger’s Specialties:

Nonprofit management, technology, strategic and business planning.
