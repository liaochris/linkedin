Eric Weaver’s Summary

HI! WANNA GET MARRIED?

After meeting an attractive stranger at a party, would you talk a lot about how great you are, ask a couple of cursory questions, then propose marriage? No? Many companies use a similar approach to revenue generation: bombard the customer with monologue, push hard for a close, and then move on to the next lead...opting for a short-term transaction rather than a long-term relationship. Sure, you might get hitched, but it'll take a lot of extra effort, and there's probably a very public divorce in the near future.

In an era where trust is required for a sale, and customers' voices are now as loud as your own, I help companies build a BRAND DIALOGUE: creating an authentic offering, generating awareness, finding relevance, and guiding the customer perception and dialogue regarding your value.

As both an agency exec and internal marketer, I've been living branding, positioning, social media and online marketing since 1992. I've provided high-level strategic counsel and ground-breaking marcom production to numerous clients, including Brita, BMW, Clorox, Ford, Kraft Foods, Lincoln, Mattel, the Mayo Clinic, Procter & Gamble, the Clinton Administration, and many others. Over the years, I've had fantastic opportunities: webcasting Ford CEO Jac Nasser, successfully pitching CEOs like Mattel's Jill Barad, Yahoo!'s Jerry Yang, and Taco Bell's Peter Waller, and having my work reviewed by Bill Gates, Paul Allen, and Jack Welch.

Traditional marketing techniques are dying, and pull marketing, high relevance and social media are the future of your revenue. Let me help you establish your brand and create a dialogue - and a relationship - that is sustainable, meaningful and built to last. You'll find me easy to work with, along with other clients who have lauded me on my dedication, extra effort and honest counsel.
