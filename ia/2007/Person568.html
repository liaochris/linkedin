    (Entertainment industry)

    May 2002 — January 2003 (9 months)

    (Entertainment industry)

    January 1997 — January 2001 (4 years 1 month)

  • Hit List

    YTV

    (Entertainment industry)

  • Eye Voltage

    YTV

    (Entertainment industry)
