James Landrith’s Summary

After eighteen years in government and the U.S. Marine Corps (active duty and reserve service), health care, health insurance and financial services trade associations, I have acquired substantial knowledge of government relations in Washington, D.C. and the legislative process. In 2005, I returned to health care first in the hospital industry at BestPractice, Inc. and moved on in 2007 to the BurgessGroup, a Medicare reimbursement software and consulting firm, as their Business Development Manager.

In addition, I have participated in several national and international civil liberties coalitions on a variety of issues over the last decade. Since 1997, I have published two internationally read and cited online publications. I am also a syndicated blogger and commentary writer for several publications.

I have been interviewed for, consulted on or mentioned in several books and stories by national and international media such as ABCNews.com, ABC Evening News, About.com, Associated Press, The Boston Globe, Community College Times, Die Zeit, Entertainment Weekly, Gannett News Service, IntellectualCapital, The Kansas City Star, The Los Angeles Times, NBC Nightly News, National Journal, Newhouse News Service, The New York Times, NewsMax.com, Newsweek, Pensacola News Journal, Rodale Press, The State and Wired.

In addition, I have also appeared on C-SPAN, Columbia University Radio News, On Point with Armstrong Williams, BET News, Free Speech TV, National Public Radio, Fast Band Global Cast, XM Radio, Sat.1 (Germany), and YLE (Finland).
