Edgar Auslander’s Summary

Edgar Auslander is VP at VantagePoint Venture Partners where he focuses on investments in the Mobile Ecosystem and Semiconductors. He brings more than 16 years of operational experience in new ventures, acquisitions, alliances, business development, marketing and strategy in the wireless ecosystem and semiconductors.

Previously, he was one of the founders of TI's Wireless Business Unit and served as its General Manager, Worldwide Strategy and Corporate Development, helping bring it from "start-up" to global market share leadership and over $5B in annualized revenue. During his tenure, he was responsible for cellular chipsets marketing and business development, driving design wins in excess of $500M at customers including Nokia, Motorola, Panasonic, joint programs at Microsoft, Google, ODMs and major mobile operators worldwide. Mr. Auslander also identified and led all the acquisitions of the Business Unit, giving him good insight as to why, when, and how large companies acquire and integrate start-ups.

He also served as SVP Marketing at IXI Mobile Inc., a Silicon Valley start-up, supplying major mobile operators with mobile devices and server-side software, and phone manufacturers with integrated client-side software solutions. He led all the marketing activities of the company, helped launch the company and raise $38M in venture capital. He was also on the Board of Advisors of several start-ups and a member of the Council of Advisors at GLG.

A Member of the TiE Wireless SIG Board, the VC Task Force Wireless SIG Board, and a Senior Member of IEEE, Mr. Auslander is co-author of "The Applications of Programmable DSPs in Mobile Communications" (Wiley). He has taught at the EDHEC Business School in France, published numerous articles, and has spoken at industry conferences worldwide. He holds an MBA from Columbia University, an MEEng from Cornell University, and a Diplome d'Ingenieur from ESME in Paris, France.
