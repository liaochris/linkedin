Dave Titus’s Summary

I've been designing and producing websites since 1994 when I helped build Netscape's premiere site and created their official mascot, Mozilla. Since then I've been the lead designer/consultant on many sites for large organizations including Network Appliance, Mammoth Mountain, and The GRAMMY Foundation. My illustration credits include Better Homes and Gardens New Junior Cookbook and the upcoming Snack Attack!, Kirkland Signature Children's Chewable Vitamins, General Mills Cereal, Macmillan's Bounce series of English Learning Books, and editorial work for GRAMMY Magazine, Wall Street Journal, Sesame Workshop, and Disney Adventures Magazine. I intend to continue illustrating and designing websites. I love the way these two disciplines compliment each other.
