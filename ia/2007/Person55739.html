Martin Maslo’s Summary

As Founder & CEO of Resultics, a Search Engine Marketing company, Martin Maslo is putting al of his experience in the online industry, to deliver performance based online marketing services to companies in USA and Latin America which are aiming to reach the US Hispanic and Latin American market.

With more than 9 years of experience in the internet industry in Argentina and Latin America, Martin Maslo has headed the business development division at Prima, the largest Internet company in Argentina.

Mr Maslo has been responsible many high-margin businesses within the comany, including mobile content business (operates Tonos Ubbi which is the largest mobile aggregator in Argentina), Google commercial representation in Argentina, online photography printing networks, and many other.

Mr maslo has been invited as well several times as professor at the Master in Telecommunications at the Law School of the Universidad de Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Mr. Maslo has recently created and issued Latinteractivo. A report about online advertising and marketing trends in Latin America, with big success. He's also the responsible editor for the Latinteractivo Blog http://www.latinteractivo.la, which gathers opinions and best practices of internet experts in the spanish speaking online arena.

Martin Maslo’s Specialties:

Search Engine Marketing, specialized in the spanish speaking Latin America. Online Marketing and advertisement. Web 2.0. Social marketing and performance marketing.
