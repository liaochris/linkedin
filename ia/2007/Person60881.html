Molly Wood’s Summary

Editor and online video personality for CNET.com, producing, creating, and contributing original video content to the newly launched CNET TV (www.cnettv.com). Co-host the daily Buzz Out Loud technology news podcast, as well as the Gadgettes podcast; star of the weekly Buzz Report video podcast; author of the Buzz Report column and frequent blogger. Appear regularly on local and national media (television, print, radio) for commentary on technology industry news, trends, and products.
