Simon Foster’s Summary

My current role involves the development and implementation of an enterprise architecture to help join together the business vision and project delivery. The primary focus has been on correct alignment of business vision to a framework of capabilities/expertise that can then further align to a standard set of business processes.

Between 2002 and early 2007 I was working on a project to implement Oracle Retail (Retek) merchandising system and Advanced Inventory Planning (AIP) for a large retailer. Experienced in business systems modelling to prove replenishment benefits, forecasting methods and also includes introduction of new forecast disaggregation tool for store level forecasts.

My main area of interest/expertise is in shaping/presenting the overall architecture of a solution involving multiple applications/platforms/integration. I have experience of implementation of a data warehouse and federated data mart reporting solution.

Passionate about using architecture to understand how processes and IT solutions can be developed to meet business visions and goals. Strongly believe in componentised based architecture using best-of-breed software solutions. Experienced at presenting technical issues/solutions to senior board members
