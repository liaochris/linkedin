  • Columnist

    Colorado Music Buzz Magazine

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Publishing industry)

    September 2006 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    The D-List: delving into ways that bands can more easily connect with their fans, and vice-versa, by means of technology, business strategy, and an old-fashioned, nose-to-the-grindstone hard work ethic.

    The goal and main theme of this column is to get off of the 'D List' and onto something a little better. We talk about empowering startup bands to adopt a strong work ethic and do-it-yourself mentality, enabling them to better connect with their fans, expand their fan base, and build a buzz without needing to have a huge budget to spend on management and P.R. firms. We also talk about ways that local music fans can better connect with the local bands, and learn more about what the Colorado music scene has to offer.

    Some of the topics we cover are about smarter uses of technology, multimedia syndication, grassroots DIY promotion techniques, booking economics, and business management strategies - as well as your off-the-cuff, miscellaneous topics every now and then.
