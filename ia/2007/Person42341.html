Joe Harris’s Summary

Highly accomplished Senior Marketing and Product Marketing executive with over 20 years experience - primary focus on market development/implementation of consumer electronics & services,digital media solutions, and launching these in market. History of success with Apple, @Home Networks, TiVo, TV Guide On Screen, NEC, Orb Networks and others.

• TiVo - Launched world’s first Digital Video Recorder, on time & on budget
• @Home Network - with Comcast (partner) launched 1st broadband cable system; achieved first year subscriber goals.
• Apple Computer - Achieved $1 billion in revenue for entry level Macintosh line, with 750K units sold for a 60% growth rate.
• Orb Networks - Introduced a leading digital streaming technology - company and market launch delivered over 40 partnerships and prospects (included Sprint DSL, Dell, Pinnacle, NetGear, and Creative Labs).
• NEC Computers - Restructured into NEC Mobile Solutions and tripled gross margin in initial quarter.
• Telocity - Launched a high speed DSL service - developed a groundbreaking target market segmentation model; exceeded forecasted subscriber goals.

Especially effective in developing messaging, positioning, advertising, PR/AR, branding, events, and sales and marketing tools. Additionally, I have managed product planning, budgets and roadmap development, market research and analysis, and program launch and delivery. My experience includes both Fortune 500 companies as well as start-ups and re-starts. I have consistently focused on maximizing marketing value, whether there is a very limited startup budget, or national advertising campaigns with annual budgets utilizing million of dollars. In each instance I have managed teams comprised of both direct and outsourced resources.
