Leon Buijs’s Summary

Graphic designer, started as DTP'er with a solide, classical graphic education. Evolved to a HTML developer and from there to Flash developer. I worked for lithographer NEROC as Photoshop'er, as DTP'er / designer for magazines like Voetbal International, Marie-Claire and Avant-Garde, at packaging design agency DuBois Ording Design, for Claessens Erdmann Architects & Designers, and since 1998 mostly as webdesigner for 'regular' multi-disciplinairy advertising agencies like Leo Burnett, Result DDB Tribal and TBWA\.
