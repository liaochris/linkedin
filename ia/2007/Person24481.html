Donna Iucolano’s Summary

At the forefront of innovation, e-business and business transformation since 1994. Today, I am applying my national, award-winning reputation and 20 years of experience as an e-commerce pioneer, a digital media executive, and a teacher through Spinach Candy, LLC. Spinach Candy is the strategic digital business consultancy and development company I founded in 2006 to work with early stage, growing and well-established companies. Clients include: 1-800-FLOWERS.COM, Blue Tulip, Cranium, Fortunoff, Marc Ecko Enterprises, and Memorystone Publishing.

My entrepreneurial spirit, passion for people and ideas, and ability to engage others have been responsible for transforming three New York City area companies: national floral and gift retailer, 1-800-FLOWERS.COM, the world’s largest children’s book publisher and global media company, Scholastic Corporation and a global specialty publisher and direct marketer, IMP/International Masters Publishers. My 11 years with them generated combined cumulative revenues of $750 million through new initiatives, most of which continue to grow, and serve as a testament to strong foundation-building skills. have the distinction of having founded and led the Internet-based e-commerce efforts of two companies -- 1-800-FLOWERS.COM and Scholastic – both repeatedly ranked in the “Top 50” by Internet Retailer.

Donna Iucolano’s Specialties:

Strategic advisor; strategy formation and planning; interim or acting senior executive; partnerships and alliances; market positioning / re-positioning; e-commerce platform reviews and selection; development activities (M&A support, interactive products, new customers, new channels, and new concepts); organizing for e-success (organizational structure, operating framework, coaching and mentoring); writer and speaker.
