Aaron Nicholson’s Summary

I utilize multiple mediums and a metaphoric approach to bring companies' essence to the internet. Often this means brand development, content development and writing, art direction, design and software development, which culminate in Flash-based interactive websites that draw the viewer into an experience of the company for which I've created the site. Rather than simply finding out 'about' the company, viewers are invited into the ethos and spirit of the company and come away with a personal experience that bonds them to the company.

A background in theatre and music together with extensive training in communications theory and application gives me a unique approach to the task of bringing companies to life online.

My goal is to help push the medium into the next stage of communication that it should be: a trancendence of film and television vis-a-vis its interactive capabilities.
