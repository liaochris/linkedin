Marcus Stockman’s Summary

Marc Stockman is current President and co-founder of UltraWellness, an integrated health and wellness company in partnership with Mark Hyman, M.D., a thought leader in the emerging science of functional medicine and systems biology.

Prior to this, he consulted for the top financial publishers in the world, including Phillips Publishing and The Motley Fool. His direct response, product development and business development expertise in the publishing area was honed in his first publishing venture, SmartPortfolio, which he co-founded in 1999 and subsequently sold to TheStreet.com in 2001.

Marc's diverse background in marketing, business development, product development, technology, health, finance and investing, consumer phsychology and publishing allows him to bring a uniquely holistic perspective to any new opportunity to make the most of it.
