Jerry Pico’s Summary

Jerry Pico has a record of over 20 years of achievements in the disciplines of talent acquisition and development, organizational effectiveness, human resources, and business management – including 7 years in his own consulting practice, People & Knowledge Works Consulting. He uses his business savvy and leadership abilities to create and implement results-oriented strategies that support sustainable growth in organizations.

Offering a unique blend of analytical and soft skills, Jerry has worked with executives, managers and work groups in Wells Fargo, Charles Schwab & Company, Ernst & Young, Korn/Ferry International and Genomic Health, Inc. to acquire top and develop top talent, streamline processes, create and launch innovative programs, and implement best practices. His interventions have resulted in dramatic improvements in productivity and satisfaction within work groups, and increasing profitability by also eliminating redundancies and reducing costly overhead.

Having worked in sales and management, he has a very strong business orientation, which brings a higher level of understanding and accountability to his recruiting process. He also has a background in organization development, which gives him the ability to understand and align recruiting goals and process to organizational structure, both formal and informal. The combination of his experience in different disciplines allows him to offer very customized and holistic talent acquisitions solutions.

He is extremely adept at working very closely with very bright individuals with high expectations and little time to recruit for hard-to-fill positions at a variety of levels up to executive, e.g., he has successfully recruited Risk Management Managers for Wells Fargo, Merger & Acquisition Consultants for Ernst & Young, and Online Sales & Operations Leaders for Google, Inc.

Jerry Pico’s Specialties:

Very strong staffing process management skills and experience, able to bring recruiting structure to groups lacking that capability.

Specializes in developing high performing individuals and leadership teams through coaching and group facilitation.

Functional Specialization: Human Resources, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Accounting, Risk Management, Compliance, Corporate Administration, Life Sciences and Legal
