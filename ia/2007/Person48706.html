Koos Jan Schouten’s Summary

Webfactory Bulgaria was founded in 1998 by Koos Jan Schouten of Amsterdam, Holland and is staffed by some of the finest designers and developers available in Bulgaria. Bulgaria was not only chosen because of the lower labor costs, but mainly due to the ample availability of cutting edge IT talent. Our team has experience of over 250 websites and internet related projects and has grown to a total of 25 people.

Most of our staff were educated at at the world famous Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski and the Technical University of Sofia.

The employment of young multi-lingual students and give them an opportunity to grow in a dynamic environment such as ours, turned out to be a successful strategy for our company.

As a result our employees are among the most flexible and capable specialists available to the IT industry in Bulgaria.

Such is our specialisation that we now have 3 totally dedicated teams to Telecom, Travel and Real Estate. These teams include Account Manager, Designer and Developers with in-debt know-how of these industries.

In addition to our technical competence, we place strong emphasis on individual designs. We create interactive and multimedia solutions for the Internet – design and development of web sites, multimedia and database engines. Webfactory Bulgaria also provides Web Marketing and Promotion and SEO Search Engine Optimisation, Content Management, Data Processing and Multi-Lingual Content Management Services.

The backbone of all our websites is our own AdminTool™ system which is the central admin area. AdminTool™ enables our clients to control most areas of their website.

Koos Jan Schouten’s Specialties:

We make websites for most industries, but we are specialists in Telecom, Real Estate, Travel and Hospitality and Public Information websites.
