Thomas Weikop’s Summary

Organizing and structuring media and technology in profitable businesses.

14 years of managerial experience within technology and media. Established Neo Ideo A/S an Internet consulting business in 1995 and worked as CEO until it was sold to Leo Burnett in 2001. Continued as member of the executive management board at Leo Burnett.

Is currently working as partner and CEO for GoViral. The company is No. 1 in its class globally, specialized in User driven marketing. Clients are global advertisers and media networks.
