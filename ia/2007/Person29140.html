Corey Irwin (http://ferlanti.com)’s Summary

CEO and designer of Ferlanti Couture Jewelry & Luxury Goods, a full-service design firm specializing in high-end European & international design. Personalized client services include custom jewelry design, fashion consulting, and personal shopping services.

The designer's works are currently sold online via the company website, http://ferlanti.com, in select retail stores and art galleries in the United States, at regular scheduled trunk shows, and by private appointment.

From the inception of its very first collections, the Ferlanti brand has rapidly gained a loyal following amongst discerning clientele. The designer's works are inspired by her diverse international travels and her love of high fashion and the fine arts.

Works from the Ferlanti Collections are a unique cross-polination of multiple cultural influences, and reflect the designer's distinctive, sophisticated style.

Retailers and galleries interested in carrying the Ferlanti line and/or hosting in-store trunk shows should contact info@ferlanti.com. For more details on upcoming events, please visit http://ferlanti.com.
