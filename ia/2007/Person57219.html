Brian McGarvey’s Summary

Brian McGarvey is responsible for creating and cultivating relationships with a wide range of business partners, including brands, distribution channels, and carriers. Brian brings 20 years experience in sales, marketing, and business development for Internet and mobile companies. Most recently he was Vice President, Americas for Vivendi Games Mobile where he built and launched their North and South American mobile business. Prior that he was Senior VP of Sales at Airborne Entertainment, a mobile content provider for major entertainment properties. As Director of Marketing for Cingular/AT&T Wireless, Brian was responsible for customer acquisition and content partnerships for mMode, their ground breaking consumer data offering. Brian was also the CEO and co-founder of mylackey.com, where he built a loyal customer following around his innovative system for ordering and receiving services. Early in Brian’s career he also collaborated with industry pioneers to develop the first generation of products and services that now form the core of business online, including the world’s first home Internet access product and first commercial web browser at SPRY/CompuServe.

Brian McGarvey’s Specialties:

strategy development, business planning, direct marketing, consumer brand management, start-up, sales force management, building teams, product launch/go to market.
