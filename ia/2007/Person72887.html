Robin-Jan De Lange’s Summary

Specializing in the protection of sensitive data and critical functionality in todays ERP environments. Enjoys public speaking engagements on ERP conference about topics like Sarbanes Oxley security remediation, security design methodology ensuring data privacy and risk based approaches to 404 audits.

Currently the Chief Technology Officer at D2C Solutions, responsible for technical architecture of the D2C Sarbanes Oxley ComplianceNow product suite.

D2C Solutions is the market leader in the Next Generation Compliance (TM) solutions. Founded in 2000, D2C provides solutions that strengthen financial controls and reduce the risk of fraud. The company's patent pending methodology and software provides real-time monitoring of sensitive transaction usage and continuous monitoring capability of mitigating controls and role usage. D2C differentiates its capabilities by providing more comprehensive solutions in a significantly shorter period of time to help its clients reduce the cost of compliance. For more information visit www.d2c.net
