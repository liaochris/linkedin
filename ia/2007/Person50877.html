Leo Cunha’s Summary

Over 20 years experience designing and developing mission critical software systems. Most recently, serving as President and CTO and chief architect of the Bidchaser.com / QShopNow.com electronic commerce sites.

Responsible for network, database and application design of this high-capacity Web property, and selected all architectural elements, hardware, software and the system's development environments.
