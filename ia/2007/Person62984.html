Neil Wolstenholme’s Summary

With nearly 20 years experience in senior line financial management and financial management consulting roles I bring breadth and depth of insight into how modern Finance functions need to operate in order to best serve the ever-increasing demands of the wider business and external stakeholders.

Throughout my career I have consistently delivered thought leadership and insight, underpinned by pragmatic, practical and hands-on assistance to help Finance functions. In particular, I bring substantial expertise and real-world know-how to assist Finance to build upon the solid foundations of the traditional 'scorekeeper' role to also develop, and proactively deliver, outstanding business performance and risk management capabilities across their organisations.

I have delivered high-impact change projects across large, complex multi-national blue chip organisations in both line and consulting roles. My track record includes successful work with household names in sectors including FMCG/consumer, pharmaceuticals, oil & gas, business services and transport/logistics.

My technical and operational expertise is reinforced with strong commercial skills, developed initially in business partner roles within industry and subsequently in consulting, where I continue to play an active business development role.

Neil Wolstenholme’s Specialties:

Senior line financial management and management consulting professional: Managing multi-national, multi-location teams; developing business; project and change management; business performance management; Finance functional transformation, business performance improvement;M&A experience; line management accountability for Finance, IT, HR and administrative functions.
