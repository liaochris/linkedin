Tracy Thompson’s Summary

While studying Sports Medicine at Cleveland State University, Tracy Thompson looked to Art and Art History classes as a diversion from the science of human anatomy. She quickly discovered she has absolutely no artistic talent worth mentioning. This minor fact did not deter her from her love and appreciation of art, architecture, and antiques.

She soon found herself living and working in the suburbs of Washington D.C. in management for a major hotel chain. This permitted her to hone her computer skills and eventually accept the fact that she was a computer geek at heart. This revelation led her to developing a sales and customer relationship management program for an international building supply company.

Her computer skills, experience in the building industry, and a very supportive spouse led her to finally pursue a career as a REALTOR. Making her home in sunny Phoenix, Arizona has provided her with a wealth of opportunity in the residential and investment property sectors. Tracy has leveraged her experience and knowledge to become one of the leaders in Internet Real Estate marketing.

Tracy has recently accepted the position of Director of Career Development and Training with Rite-Way Realty. She is excited to use her background in corporate training and customer relationship management to assist new associates on their real estate career path.
