Hal Jespersen’s Summary

Cofounder, vice president, and Chief Technology Officer of Open IT Works, a privately held company in Belmont, California, that promotes and facilitates collaboration between enterprise CIOs on "Open IT." (Open IT is a concept similar to Open Source, but consists of focused collaboration between enterprises that is not limited to code development. It includes the sharing of solutions: intellectual property, methodologies, best practices, processes, and resources.) Responsible for all technology and engineering activities of the company, and collaboration and facilitation processes for the project working groups formed by member CIOs.
