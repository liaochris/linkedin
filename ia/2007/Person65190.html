Carey Sherrell’s Summary

ABOUT MY COMPANY:
We are marketing. We are a non traditional group, but we uphold traditional values. We’re a big city firm with a small town feel. We are about doing great work for great clients. We’re a passionate, innovative, exceptional, and talented group here to launch, grow, and build your brand from conception to execution.

There are hundreds of event marketing firms out there, but none that do what we do. We are unique in every sense of the word. Our ideas would never be labeled “board room” ideas, instead envision a place where we think of the world as our playground. Where there is no Sandcastle too big and no slides too high. Imagine if you will a place where there are no limits, no boundaries, nothing to hold an idea back. A place where ideas become real. Our ideas are bold, creative, new, and different. They stem from our four sectors of work which are: events, promotions, sports, and sponsorship marketing.

Here at The Onyx Marketing Group our mission is to provide a creative and innovative marketing program that not only builds our clients’ brand but fuses a personal connection with the brand and the consumers. Our ideas become solutions helping to incite brand equity, create demand for the brand, thus driving revenue. We take a special interest in getting to know your brand and it’s consumers and we fall in love with them both. From conception to execution, we will not stop until we hear the words “ Fusion Complete. Mission Accomplished”.
