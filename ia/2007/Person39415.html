JD Harvill’s Summary

Graduated with a BA from Penn State. Began selling computers, then managing the sales team, then supporting computers. Took the opportunity to live in Germany for two years, with Swan Technologies, then as a landscaper for the last several months.

Returned to the US as a recruiter, then took several PC and software support positions. I was moved to development and quality systems with several of my positions and was trained in both ISO 9000 and Six Sigma methodologies.

I did some IT work as a reporting tool and database developer, then went back to customer service and documentation with AT&T.

I'm now working again with my father as a recruiter and consultant for manufacturing and logistics client companies around Atlanta, GA.
