Monique Riviere’s Summary

V2K Window Décor & More is a woman-owned and operated, window covering business proudly serving the Washington, DC metro area. We offer one-stop shopping for your home or business with an abundant selection of products to enhance any room. We offer the following types of custom window coverings for style, privacy, or sunlight control:

• Blinds
• Draperies and Curtains
• Shades
• Shutters
• Remote-controlled motorization available

Prepare to be wowed by our complete line of custom bed coverings, throws, pillows, and beautiful fabrics for upholstery, etc.

Our design consultant will meet you at your home or office with product samples so that you can select choices to suit your color scheme, lighting, and any physical needs. The consultant records your windows’ measurements in our advanced 3D software to show you how your windows will look with your new window treatments and provides you an itemized estimate of your project. Upon delivery of your order, a certified expert installer will install your product, demonstrate how it works, and address any questions to ensure your comfort and satisfaction.
