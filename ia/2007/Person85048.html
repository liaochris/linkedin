Casey Brewer’s Summary

I grew up in a hippy commune, or at least that's how I remember it. I like to relentlessly catalog vinyl records. Not because I'm OCD, but for the opportunity to listen to treats that I may have forgotten about. I like poorly produced late 60's and early 70's soul music, where the drums sound like a whiffle ball bat on concrete. I enjoy reading authors that introduce new words to my vocabulary, words that I'll later mispronounce in conversation. My dog is my partner in crime, and I'm horrified but fascinated by Sharks.

I'm an experienced writer in traditional and interactive creative advertising. I aspire to be a Super Beast.
