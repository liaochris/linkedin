Bob McDonald’s Summary

Experienced and educated pertaining to career development and adult education. Since January 2006, I have, in collaboration with my colleagues, established a career services program at TechSkills Cleveland, including development of career workshops, establishment of externship program and devising a process to guide students into job placement. I now head up the collaborative efforts of all TechSkills Directors of Career Services in sharing best practices by presiding over the monthly conference call and moderating the DOCS e-mail list.

As always, I am working to expand our organizational relationships to create more externship sponsors and, more importantly, employment opportunities for our vocational students. Featured as a Guest Expert on the Job Lounge blog (http://www.joblounge.blogspot.com/).

Most recently, I have worked to re-establish my home-based business, TOG Solutions, in an effort to provide basic Web design and desktop publishing needs for small businesses. With my career services experience, I have expanded my company's offerings to include resume-writing and other career-development services to individuals.

I also have worked in real estate, both in commercial and residential. I am a licensed real estate salesperson in the state of Ohio and have strong marketing experience in this regard.

Any people interested in connecting with me for the purposes of learning more of what I have to offer are free to contact me.
