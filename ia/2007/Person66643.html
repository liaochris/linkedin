Paul Knegten’s Summary

I would sum up my main talents with the term "problem solving." I live to analyze, strategize, and implement solutions to problems. Now, that's pretty general -- but I'd say that its a recurring theme in all my professional successes and if I had to define an element that existed across all my different roles and positions, it would be that of my unique and creative approach to fulfilling needs.

Creativity is another facet of my personality that is impossible to ignore. Creating is my hobby, and I believe it's developed my character in ways that have significant effects on the way I think and the way I work. I believe that compellng people to change their behavior (often the goal of any business) requires thought that will inspire them, and I have a talent for channeling my creativity to move people.

Finally, adaptability: I've moved halfway across the world in my life for the sheer adventure of it. Things worked out well, but through the roadblocks and rough terrain I've encountered in this experience I've built perseverance and strength that continue to push me.
