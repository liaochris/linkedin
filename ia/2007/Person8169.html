Ashish Jain’s Summary

Ashish Jain currently works as a Principal Architect with Ping Identity Corporation, a leading provider of solutions for identity federation.

Prior to joining Ping Identity, he worked with BEA Systems where his role was to assist BEA customers in designing and implementing their e-business strategies using solutions based on J2EE.

He holds several industry certifications from SUN and BEA and is also the President for the Denver BEA User group.

He is the co-author of the J2EE 1.4 Bible (ISBN:0764539663) and Enterprise SOA (ISBN:140203704X). He can be reached at ajain@pingidentity.com.
