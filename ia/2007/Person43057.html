John Messner’s Summary

John Messner brings more than 20 years experience in Internet and general direct marketing to the table for his clients. He has been responsible for Customer Relationship Management, Retention and Customer Service, Database Marketing, Email Marketing and Sales.

John worked at Experian, where he led the sales and business development efforts of the company's Z24 and CircBase co-op marketing databases. Under his leadership, he more than doubled the division's sales volume.

Messner also has a strong entrepreneurial and international direct marketing expertise holding positions at Hallmark Cards, Unicover Corporation, EDS (NeoData), Populardemand.com, Cendant, CareerTrack Seminars and Video Professor, Inc.where he was most recently Director of Customer Retention and Loyalty.

Messner's goal is to continue his career in Internet and Direct Marketing working with his clients to optimize web and off line marketing efforts by increasing sales, conversion and increase profitability.
