Work Experience - Executive Summary

Subodh has a strong Generalist H.R. experience in diverse industries, across South East Asia & Middle East. Subodh has worked in both Operational as well as strategic H.R. areas where he has frequently interacted with Sr. Executives, business units heads & CEO in his role as Change Enabler & Senior H.R. Professional. During his professional career Subodh has worked for some large MNC’s like General Electric (G.E.) – U.S.A, Tibbett & Britten Group – U.K., Exel Logistics – U.K.

Subodh has more than 14 years experience in various HR roles, across varied industries and diverse work force specialising in Leadership Development & Training, People processes management, SAP HRIS implementation, Change management and End to End HR process mapping and consulting. Subodh demonstrated accomplishment and credibility in executing both strategic and operational business initiatives in senior HR business role in Logistics supply chain industry in Dubai. Currently Subodh is implementing SAP HR solutions to optimise delivery of HR services in various organizations’ in Australia.

Subodh has strong relationship building skills and experience in partnering with line managers to provide first class people related solutions.

Subodh is keen to engage & partner with H.R. focused organisation by empowering business leaders, in defining & driving, H.R. productivity.
