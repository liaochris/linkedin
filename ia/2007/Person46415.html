Kasper Haagensen’s Summary

I have been working in Mobile and Internet sector as a technical, business development, sales and executive capacity for the last 14 years.

My career has primarily been in a start-up environment, where I have been founder and co-founder of several enterprises. I am currently CEO for mobile digital invention as, a Danish mobile integrator. Our solutions make it possible for our users to send and receive text messages on the go – send directly from desktop/server clients like Outlook, Notes, or Navision.

I am equally strong working on the business side as on the technical side. My technical skills allow me to question and improve our software development, where I am able to code the work myself. I am a proven and effective communicator to large and small gatherings, and have the ability to think outside the box.

I have also co-authored a book on website strategies: ”Websites – beslutningstagernes guide til virksomhedens Internet og intranet-strategi”, and been part of the media world through volunteer work on community radio stations.
