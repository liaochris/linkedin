Diego Rumiany’s Summary

Staff Member at the United Nations Headquarters in New York, currently working in the Department of Economic and Social Affairs (UNDESA) on several programs on social statistics and development. Previous experience includes managerial positions in software companies serving the financial industry in Latin America, and Senior Consultant and Project Manager in Oracle Corp.

Master Degree in International Political Economy and Development in Fordham University. Undergraduate degree in Business Administration and Information Systems at the School of Economics at the University of Buenos Aires. Did further specialization in Information Technology Management at Columbia University (NY). Associate Professor for the School of Economics at the University of Buenos Aires. Teaching, thesis advisor and research coordinator on ICT for Development and IT Management.

Diego Rumiany’s Specialties:

Mainly interested in the role of Information Technology in developing countries in the following areas: e-government, digital divide, knowledge management, FDI, outsourcing and software industry development.
