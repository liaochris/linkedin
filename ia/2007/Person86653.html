Tom Watson’s Summary

Driven by a love of invention and innovation, and possessing a passion and reputation for customer advocacy, I take a consultative role in working with management and marketing partners. I strive to find better ways to get the message across and serve the customer’s need by researching customer issues regarding products, services and information. The results are increased effectiveness, sales volume, sales force efficiency - and most importantly - customer satisfaction. My broad experience stretches from marketing communications to management and includes many aspects of marketing and communications - all in a worldwide context. I am a strategic and tactical thinker, but have plenty of hands-on experience – a project-focused problem solver and an enthusiastic team builder. Desiring to branch into new areas, I have become interested and gained experience in Fund Development work for non-profit organizations.
