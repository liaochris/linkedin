Lisa Bari’s Summary

I would like to encourage the major players in the comparison shopping industry to offer more transparent and flexible pricing structures that would allow merchants to list their entire product catalogs, thereby improving vertical shopping search for all. I also still believe in Search Engine Marketing as a potentially great equalizer for businesses small and large in the realm of online advertising. I've also always associated Search Engine Marketing with the larger topics of Search Engine Optimization - the best Search Marketing campaign can't make up for fundamental flaws in site design, both for the end user and for search engine algorithms.

I recently had the honor of being a panelist at Channel Advisor's Catalyst conference in Pinehurst, NC on a panel entitled "The Future of Comparison Shopping."

http://www.channeladvisor.com/catalyst/general/future_cse.html
