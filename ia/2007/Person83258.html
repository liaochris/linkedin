Sunil Sharan’s Summary

In-depth understanding of the dynamics of business creation and marketing, and able to quickly leverage experience to craft value propositions, business models, and monetize product/services in new industries. Seven-year exposure to market trends and developments in the energy sector in the areas of advanced metering and demand response. Skilled in selling to and negotiating complex multi-million contracts with major utilities, system integrators and VAR’s worldwide. Comprehensive Rolodex of business people, regulators, and government officials in the energy sector. Solid background in the development of country-specific marketing collateral and strong technical product knowledge that allows for positioning of complex technologies. Worked in three continents – North America, Europe, and Asia; enjoy working with people of diverse backgrounds. Exceptional interpersonal, relationship building and cross-cultural verbal/written communication skills – multi-lingual in English, French, German, and Hindi. Thrive in fast-paced environments and strong proponent of a “roll up the sleeves” attitude, energy, and style. Entrepreneurial spirit and edge for success.
