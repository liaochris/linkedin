Neville Hobson’s Summary

Neville Hobson, ABC, is a communicator, blogger and podcaster, one of the leading European early adopters and influencers in social media communication for business.

He blogs at NevilleHobson.com (www.nevillehobson.com) with commentary and opinion on business communication and technology, and co-presents ‘For Immediate Release: The Hobson & Holtz Report’ (www.forimmediaterelease.biz), a twice-weekly business podcast at the intersection of online communication, business and technology started in January 2005.

He is a frequent speaker at conferences, seminars, other public professional development events, as well as privately to organizations, on the subject of social media communication and its effective use in organizational communication

More: http://www.nevillehobson.com/about/

Neville Hobson’s Specialties:

Strategic communication planning and execution, social media, communication technology, Web 2.0, weblogs, podcasting, employee communication, corporate identity and branding, communication skills coaching, public speaking, social networks, virtual communities.
