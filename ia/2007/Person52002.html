Rain Longson’s Summary

I presently live in North Delta, BC with my husband, John and our modest collection of four legged kids (Two Bernese Mountain dogs and two Sphynx cats, they have their own web site - www.PlayTime.com).

I spend most of my working hours designing GUI's (Graphical User Interfaces) and web sites. My skills and experience include animation, computer consulting, instructing, and over 20 years of print, fine arts and online design. I am always following the industry developments and update my skills to keep on top of the ever changing face of design.

I was born in Montreal, and grew up mostly on the East coast - Canada/US. I earned my first degree with a scholarship at Parson's School of Design in Manhattan for fine arts. I hold several certificates, BA's, Minors and Masters Degrees in the design field. I have since gone on to win several awards for both print and web design along with several solo exhibitions for my sculptures and water colors.

I have taught Web Design, Interactive Media and Computer Graphics for the Vancouver School Board, private schools and tutoring on a one to one basis. I am a fine artist, designer and a nerd/geek, but in the past, I have also been an animator, computer consultant, instructor, and lecturer. I have authored a manual on Adobe Photoshop and HTML, I spend substantial time exploring Photoshop as a fine art tool along with Painter.

I can be reached at my office for projects on contract or employment basis.
