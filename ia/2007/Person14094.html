Caitlin Jewell’s Summary

Caitlin Jewell
Principal and Creative Director

Caitlin brings more than a decade of brand marketing communications and entrepreneurial experience to Silverscape. As co-founder and Principal, her primary responsibility is brand positioning and launch strategy for Silverscape clients. She’s highly skilled at finding the core differentiators, big ideas, warmth and clarity to position companies for success. Caitlin entered the industry working for a Disney book publisher as a paste-up assistant (No one really remembers this much anymore.) But, being the computer whipper snapper in her early career led to a glamorous advertising position where she worked in design, strategy and business development teams producing famous work you might have seen or been influenced by.

“When asked what I do, I rarely say brand developer, marketing executive or creative director. Essentially I’m a story teller. Every company, every product has a story to be told. It’s my goal to find the most appropriate and beautiful way to communicate this to our client’s audiences in the way they want to be communicated with.”

Caitlin earned a BFA degree in Communication Design from the Massachusetts College of Art. She routinely participates in leading industry events, conferences and presentations as well as being selected for panels assembled in major award categories to judge national submissions. Her passions include brewing and enjoying craft beer, her beagle, oil painting and the rare bit of travel.
