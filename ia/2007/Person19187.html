Chris Shoulet’s Summary

Top Dog Recruiting is focused on the Technology Industry. We specialize in placing mid, senior & executive level professionals in the areas of professional services, management consulting, technology, R&D, technical sales support, sales, marketing, legal, finance, & administration.
.
Our clients include venture capital firms, start-ups, software manufacturers, resellers, integrators, & professional services organizations. We focus on both the commercial & federal market places, placing candidates with Security Clearances. We work with both small & large companies as well as privately & publicly held companies.

Top Dog has representation across North America and has experience helping companies move into the North American market-place.

Our edge is our understanding of the technology industry. We take the time to do our research. We learn who the leaders are in each space and spend time qualifying our candidates as well as our clients. Many of our recruiters have come directly out of the technology industry and have hands-on experience in development, implementing, and managing in the technical areas we recruit for.

Top Dog also provides seminars and individual and corporate training in career advancement, career transition, one-on-one career counseling, and resume writing workshops. We also provide training for in-house corporate recruiters as well as agency recruiters.
