Eric Piraux’s Summary

When you look at my professional experiences, you understand that I’ve a wide culture and knowledge in the IT world. This is due to my extreme curiosity, always interested in what’s new and wondering how it could help me in my work. I’m always self-educating on the new technologies, trying to find out the best compromise between the novelty and the reality; I’m pragmatic.
I’m structured and organized and try to match my creativity to the real-world constrains, this leads to high quality and stable deliverables. I like to coach and share my knowledge to juniors, and therefore bring my attention to the quality of the documentation.
As I’m convinced that the main goal of the Information Technologies is to help the users to better do their job, I pay a lot my attention to their needs and put the complexity into my hands to deliver user-friendly solutions.
