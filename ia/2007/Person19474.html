Cynthia Wunsch’s Summary

I have 19 years' experience in teaching and performing, a Master's degree in Music with a specialty in Baroque and pre-Baroque music, and 7 years' experience in Europe as a teacher and performer. In piano and voice, I teach exclusively classical music and my teaching is limited to 16 hours per week, so that I can give each of my students my personal attention and have time to do research to correct technical deficiencies and develop repertoire. Instruction also includes attention to music history and theory, in order that my students become well-rounded performers with a thorough grounding in the field.
