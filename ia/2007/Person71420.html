Rodrigo Dauster’s Summary

Rationally intuitive VP-level executive with extensive team building, project delivery and business development experience; team player quick to spot the best opportunities and rigorous in making sure they are real and come to fruition.

15 years professional experience from Booz Allen Hamilton, a strategy consulting firm and interactive agencies (Viant, Metrius (a KPMG company) and AGENCY.COM) and independent senior advisory consultancy.

MBA from INSEAD, in France; Masters from Brown University, in the US, in artificial intelligence.

Strong international background – lived in and worked across Europe, in Brazil and the US; speak fluently English, French, Portuguese and Spanish.

Rodrigo Dauster’s Specialties:

Expert knowledge of media (esp. print, TV, social media) and mobile telecoms industries; informed about fixed and wireless broadband services, and PSTN. Functional expertise in business strategy, product development, sales, marketing, branding and web design. Deep understanding of current and emerging Internet-related technologies and views on the benefits and risks they present to businesses.
