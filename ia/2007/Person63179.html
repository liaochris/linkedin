Stephanie Cockerl’s Summary

Stephanie Cockerl went from designing and planning to build buildings to building developing and marketing websites. Stephanie helps organizations define and implement goals for their websites. She has over 10 years of experience as a web designer, developer, programmer and online marketer. She has also served as an adjunct professor at a college where she taught undergraduate and graduate courses in using the Internet, the development of websites and e-commerce. Her work has been recognized as a Bronze Award Winner for a Public Service Website on Portfolios.com and more recently, Stephanie Cockerl's work has also been named a Finalist for Company Website of the Year for the 2004 Stevie Awards for Women Entrepreneurs. She was then selected to be a preliminary round judge in the 2005 Stevie Awards for Women Entrepreneurs.

**I am not currently available for full-time employment due to elder care responsibilities. Otherwise, if you have telecommuting and/or consulting opportunities that do not exceed 20 hours/week, please do not hesitate to contact me.**
