Rob Robson’s Summary

A Chartered Psychologist with nine years of consulting in business (IT, Business Change, OD) in sport (Sport Psychology), I joined Apter International in January 2007 and in April 2007 took up the role of Head of Employee Profiling and Research. Apter International is a niche firm with three 'businesses': Organisational and Leadership Developmen; Employee Profiling and Research; Consumer and Social Advocacy Research. My goal is simple: to make the profiling business profitable after several years of investment while maintaining my contribution to consulting revenues. Simple!

I am also involved in the development of a community, networking and business development website for the sport and exercise industry (iStadia.com) on a part-time basis.

Rob Robson’s Specialties:

Change management, profiling, psychometrics, employee research, employee engagement, motivation, emotion, Reversal Theory, performance, sport psychology, coaching, leadership, training, organisational development
