Michael Rowan’s Summary

Software executive with a long history of creating and operating businesses in the high availability and storage sectors. I am currently consulting for Incipient, prior to that I was the founder and CTO of Revivio (acquired by Symantec), StorageCom (acquired by Vyant Technologies, then Mendocino Software), and a parter at CLaM Associates (acquired by Lakeview Technologies).

StorageCom and Revivo were the pioneers of the Continuous Data Protection space. Cool stuff.
