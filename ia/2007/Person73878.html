Roel Hoekstra’s Summary

Borrow against the value of your publicly traded securities without the risk of a margin call.

We can lend up to 90% of the value of the stock, all our loans are non-recourse loans, and we can lend against the stock of almost any publicly traded company - trading on all markets globally.

You get to use the value tied up in your stocks without selling, keep the upside stock appreciation and minimize the downside risk.

Loans from $50,000 to $100,000,000.

To learn more visit us at www.globalstocklending.com or send me an email at Roel@globalstocklending.com

Roel Hoekstra’s Specialties:

Asset based lending solutions; specifically lending against publically traded stocks (including restricted stocks). Learn how to use stock lending as a financial planning tool.

Employee benefits design and strategy (retirement plans, health and welfare programs, executive compenstaion programs and outsourcing solutions) - global client team management, merger and acquisition strategy and integration suppport.

Sales channel development and integration
