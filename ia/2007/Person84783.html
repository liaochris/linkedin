Teresa Caviness’s Summary

Teresa Caviness is an experienced direct marketing professional who has worked with the leaders in their respective industries since 1984. In 2003, she brought her experience and passion for the business, along with her client relationships to re-form Metis/America Marketing (metisamerica.com) into a leader in consumer and business-to-business direct marketing.

As President of Metis/America, Teresa is proud to have maintained her former client relationships and helped to foster new ones. In early 2007, she and Jim Lane purchased the remaining shares of the company and now retain sole ownership. As the agency continues to grow, she is committed to maintaining the same level of personal contact and strategic support that help her build her former company, Siacomm, Inc.

While the agency’s primary focus is the direct marketing arena, the Metis team continues to support clients with strategic planning, research and analysis, multi-channel initiatives. Each day brings a new challenge, and the Metis team appreciates the opportunity to work with a variety of clients across the country in the areas of financial services, insurance, manufacturing, retail, consumer services, non-profit, entertainment and more.

Teresa Caviness’s Specialties:

Our multi-industry experience coupled with our disciplined approach to studying market trends arms us with the knowledge needed to deliver insights, not just observations.
