Marc Lefton’s Summary

I'm a unique blend of creativity and technology. I've succeeded working for major advertising agencies developing creative for major brands. Now I work to pioneer what's next, and what's after what's next.

At Snap Marketing, I do everything from finding new media platforms, to concepting experiential marketing campaigns to helping execute our ideas for our Fortune 100 clients.

The latest project I'm working on is "blogworking" which is a powerful combination of social networking and blogging. With this platform, I've created Adholes.com, the leading network for advertising and marketing professionals.

I'm looking to meet entrepreneurs starting new media properties, as well as anyone looking to leverage the potential of Adholes.com and its properties.
