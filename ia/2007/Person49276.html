Kelley-Sue LeBlanc’s Summary

In the last several years I have been with Compuware in Merrimack NH, leading the development for an award winning web application vulnerability detection tool. Previously I worked as Head of Development for a Fundraising Software company in Manchester, NH. My strengths include business development and analysis, process definition, SDLC methodologies, project management, and implementation specialization, though above all I am a team builder and coach. It is through my work as a business coach that I have come to be involved in my current venture, Aleuromedia. I am so excited about this work, as I am truly inspired by the energy being exchanged in bringing this creative and dynamic service offering to market.

In the simplest light I am a creative business person who thrives on identifying and filling gaps, both comprehensive and communicative. This desire to bring common understanding, and belief in community, directly correlate to my success as a team developer and leader. I am boldly honest and aggressively driven, with an entrepreneurial sense of responsibility for managing risk and project scope. Passionate about bringing creative solutions to market, my talents for relating business needs to technical initiative, improving business processes, and providing strong leadership and direction, are the foundation of my professional successes.
