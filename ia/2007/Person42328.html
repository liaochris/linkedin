Joe Gormley (joe.gormley@atx.com)’s Summary

For most of the last 10 years I've worked in communications. The majority of that time was spent with carriers however I also have experience in local area networks, server maintenance and application development and equipment.

Today I spend most of my time working with companies who want to connect multiple locations with a WAN to distribute various applications to remote offices. My experience has helped me to understand these applications and develop the best strategies for my customers.

As a member of the Association of Contingency Planners I continue to build on my network experience to help customers with redundancy, data backup and disaster recovery planning as it relates to telecommunications. Since 9/11 this has been a very hot topic in our field.

I have customers that have been with me since I started in this business in 1998. If you are interested in having me evaluate your company's communications strategy please call me at 856-813-5316.
