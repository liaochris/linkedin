Ken Standfield 1800+’s Summary

Contacts: 1800+

I help organisations:
- See where they can improve top-line and bottom-line results by scientifically measuring the financial benefits of increased engagement, productivity, and culture change in a way that employees support, not undermine by becoming goodwill champions
- Identify new areas of performance improvement and revenue re-capture which are overlooked by conventional management methods and practices through intangible intelligence
- Financially value and financially report the performance improvement value of how improvements in knowledge (strategy), collaboration (synergy), and leverage (systems) impacts productivity, cost effectiveness, revenue, earnings, market value, and share price so as to form more compelling business cases and ROI through intangible accounting
- Do all the above to help organizations out-compete their rivals so as to increase margins, decrease competitive pressure, and better attract and retain key staff.

Through Intangible Intelligence Diagnostics, international intangible standards are applied to create an "unfair competitive advantage" by assisting organizations identify sources of untapped value extraction related to:

1. Cost effectiveness: Spending less to achieve more
2. Revenue effectiveness: Gaining more revenue from the same customer base
3. Wage effectiveness: Creating goodwill champions to enhance productivity
4. Time effectiveness: Getting to market faster and enhancing service
5. Productivity valuation: Financially valuing productivity across the organization

Ken is a recognised world-leader in the financial valuation/reporting of core value driver effectiveness through international intangible standards. He is an accomplished, award winning, international author, sought after international keynote speaker, and mentor to C-level executives.
