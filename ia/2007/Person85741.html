Tim Ash’s Summary

Experienced Internet marketing thought-leader and entrepreneur. Founder of several Internet start-up companies. President of Epic Sky and its SiteTuners division. Frequent speaker at industry conferences. Author of Landing Page Optimization: The Definitive Guide to Testing and Tuning for Conversions (John Wiley Press 2007).

SiteTuners improves the conversion rates of websites. Proprietary TuningEngine technology used to run huge landing page optimization tests (1MM+ unique "recipes"). Official Google Website Optimizer Authorized Consultant - use it for smaller A-B split testing and Multivariate testing. Offer a choice of payment options including fixed-price to pure-performance based. We are the only major landing page testing company which is willing to get paid strictly on the extra profits that we create.

At Epic Sky we manage large-scale Yahoo Search and Google AdWords pay-per-click (PPC) campaigns. We also drive PPC traffic as a super affiliate.
