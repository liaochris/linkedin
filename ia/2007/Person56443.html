Matthew Roberts’s Summary

THP recruit solely within the converged communication markets in the UK and EMEA. We have a proven track record of delivering value add talent acquisition solutions to our clients discreetly, cost effectively and quickly. We also partner with several other recruitment providers to offer best of breed recruitment solutions within the contact centre arena and broad lines sales sector.

Our goals are to continue serving our exisiting clients in the nature they are acustomed whilst driving new business with clients within this ever expanding market.
