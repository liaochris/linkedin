Kevin Goodman’s Summary

I am a two time recipient of the SBN Pillar Award for Philanthropy with companies I work for. I am contributor to BlueBridge Networks success in winning awards such as The COSE 10 Under 10 and SBN Rising Star in Innovation Awards and the NEOSA 2007 Tech Awards-Best Emerging Company Finalist. I function in the sales, marketing and business development role at BlueBridge Networks, looking to make BlueBridge the best in breed in data center operations.
I am involved in the Business Development and Entrepreneurial arena for over 20 years; growing companies in both the Technology and Construction Industries.
I continue to play a role in one residential construction company, where I have been both a local and national design award winner.
I am interested in emerging e-commerce businesses.
I enjoy serving on committees and boards. I am the Chairman of the Red Cross (BEPA) Business Emergency Planning Association, Strategic Advisory Committee.
