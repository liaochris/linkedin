Christy Suerth, SPHR (cdsuerth@comcast.net)’s Summary

Christy Suerth is the founder of The Work Shop, a coaching and consulting practice dedicated to building strong careers and businesses. www.theworkshoponline.com

Valued by her clients for her strong technical competence, insight into complex issues, warmth, energy and integrity, Christy partners with business leaders to achieve their organization's mission. With a wealth of knowledge and practical experience in Human Resources Management and Organization Development, Christy is known to add value and bring results to every workplace culture -- Fortune 500, small business, Non-Profit, and International.

Christy brings 20 years of experience to the table. This experience encompasses leadership development and coaching, strategic talent management, employee development and training, workplace culture enhancement, performance management programming, as well as compensation. She is certified to use, and is skilled in using, online assessment tools like the Myers Briggs Type Indicator, FIRO B / Leadership Report, as well as 360 degree assessment. She develops excellent custom workshops to increase the effectiveness of leaders, teams and high potential staff.

Christy has a Masters degree from Loyola University of Chicago in HR. She attained the lifetime designation of "Senior Professional in HR" and recently acquired her certification in professional coaching.
