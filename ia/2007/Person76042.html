Sam Richter’s Summary

Visionary, passionate leader with extensive experience building companies, business units, and brands. Business strategist and planner. Creative thinker with wide-ranging experience in product/service development and bringing programs to market. "Rainmaker” able to identify and secure new business and generate strategic partners. Award-winning traditional, direct, and online marketing executive and practitioner. Nationally renowned speaker and author on sales improvement techniques, online information technologies, marketing, and leadership development.

Sam Richter’s Experience

  • President

    James J. Hill Group / Hill Reference Library, (srichter@jjhill.org)

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Market Research industry)

    August 2001 — Present (6 years 4 months)

    Lead and transform an 85+ year-old organization into a national business resource. Create new revenue divisions including:

    - HillSearch (www.hillsearch.org): Considered the most powerful business research engine available.

    - BizToolkit Free and Premium (www.biztoolkit.org): An online portal of business resources and tools. Premium version includes live help, and BizRewards.

    - BizToolkit for Organizations: A custom-branded version of BizToolkit Premium for organizations that serve small business.

    - HillResearch (www.hillreserach.org): A professional research service.

    - HillSource: Vertical specific information centers. Examples include an ethics portal (www.einsight.org), and a U.S. Hispanic market portal (www.hispansource.org).

    - Content Management: An ASP custom online library program. Examples include VolunteerResource (www.volunteerresource.org) and the Biz Info Library, (www.bizinfolibrary.org).

    - Client satisfaction consistently exceeds 95%.
