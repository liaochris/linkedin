Jessica Brown’s Summary

I'm passionate about design and architecture, both aesthetics and technically speaking. I graduated with Honours from the Interior Design program at Georgian College and gained a 3.45 GPA from the Architectural Technician program at George Brown. My post secondary studies have given me a wide variety of versatile skills. I am a hard working individual and detail oriented. I have a good technical understanding of the way spaces are planned and constructed and express this in my work. My full time position with my current employer has allowed me to be involved in all aspects of the projects in the corporate office design sector. The projects include spaces in both commercial and industrial buildings. A typical project involves my manager and I meeting with clients to discuss their requirements for preliminary design purposes, and drafting of preliminary design concepts and layouts for my manager’s comments and the client’s review. Following preliminary drawings I prepare construction drawings and review with my supervisor for revisions. During the tender stage, I am involved in contract administration where I am responsible for issuing the appropriate contract documents to the bidding contractors, once a contractor has been awarded and construction begins, I continue to issue the appropriate contract documents to the contractor for changes/issues per client requests and site conditions. My part-time position in the retail sector has allowed me to use AutoCAD extensively for preliminary retail layouts and preparation of construction drawings for retails establishments in malls and shopping centers. I work well under pressure, which is evident through my ability to have the drawings done on time to meet strict deadlines. I am interesting in working with a firm that is team oriented and that will provide challenges to both its team members and myself.
