Chris Forbes’s Summary

Chris is the founder of MinistryMarketingCoach.com a coaching and consulting practice for faith-based nonprofits that also seeks to train marketing coaches. Chris has experience in marketing communications worldwide through working as an international missionary, strategy leader, media strategist, and marketplace researcher.

He speaks nationally on the subject of ministry marketing and has developed marketing strategies for churches and ministries of all sizes and shapes, including, Purpose Driven Ministries. His articles are regularly featured on Pastors.com, ChurchCentral.com, and ChurchBusiness.com

Chris is a graduate of Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary (M. Divbl.) and the University of Central Oklahoma (BS).

Chris is a certified Guerrilla Marketing Coach with an interest in applying the low-cost marketing principles of Guerrilla Marketing to the nonprofit context. He is a featured coach in the Guerrilla Marketing Association Coaching forum.
