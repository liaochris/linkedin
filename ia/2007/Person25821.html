Eddie de Wet’s Summary

Project Manager with extensive experience in both the Hospitality Industry and Information Technology.

I spent the early part of my working career learning all disciplines within Hospitatlity including opening of new restaurants for a restaurant franchise, refurbishments, front of house training, management training, cost control and wages and staff management.

I then diverted into IT by Project Managing the installation of computerised Point of Sale and Stock Control Systems in Hotels, Clubs, Pubs and Restaurants.

From there it was an industry shift to Corporate IT, doing desktop and server builds, networking, troubleshooting and support.

Interested in Consulting at all levels to the Hospitalty industry including operations, customer focus and technology.
