Karen Northup’s Summary

Karen Northup has served in executive roles at a variety of successful venture-backed technology companies. In addition to founding and serving as CEO of Corefino, Karen sits on the Board of Directors of Document Command.

Before founding her company, Corefino, Karen held CFO positions with Scout Media, Equitex, Telesuite, Rivals.com and Telocity (now directv). Karen has also held management and executive roles in a variety of companies, including GlobalCenter (now Global Crossing) and PWC. Karen currently serves on the boards of several non-profit organizations.

  • CEO & Founder

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Accounting industry)

    March 2002 — Present (5 years)

    née: VFO Company, Inc., Subfocal Solutions

    Corefino provides FAO (finance and accounting outsourcing) services for early-stage companies. Unlike a bookkeeping service, Corefino provides the technology, procedures and staffing that a company needs to meet its reporting needs. Just as importantly, Corefino can grow with a company from its earliest stages through the later stages of growth - adding information systemss and staffing as needed for each stage.

    Corefino is able to leverage economies of scale to implement technologies and processes that result in a cost effective solution for our clients. So at costs comparable to what a company would be paying to deal with accounting internally, Corefino’s clients have access to higher quality information systems and accounting services. Similarly, the technology platform enables Corefino to increase its efficiencies and reduce staff loading with each increase in client count.
