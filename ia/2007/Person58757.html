Michael Sikorsky’s Summary

A seasoned veteran of the information technology industry. Attended the University of Alberta in Edmonton, graduating with a Computer Engineering degree, and holds a software patent related to innovative rendering techniques. Previously served as Vice President of ThoughtWorks Inc. after their acquisition of Servidium Inc. Currently Chairman of BrightSpot Consulting and CEO of Cambrian House Inc, and on Board of Directors for RenoWorks Inc.
