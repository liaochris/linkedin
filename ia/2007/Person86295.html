Ted Marcus’s Summary

Currently, I am PM on an infrastructure integration project for Mercury Interactive.

Prior to this, I consulted to the Hadassah Medical Organization in Jerusalem on a document management project.

I am interested either in finding full time employment in Israel, or (if a juicy project comes up) continuing to consult.

For the ~9 years prior to my return to Israel I was in the US (Baltimore), where I worked in IT Project Management, with a focus on business process improvment and new technology implementations, for a large (10,000+ staff) hospital group. Projects I've worked on include a BlackBerry rollout, a multi-site identification management system rollout, digitizing Medical Record implementation, and some high value BPI efforts. I have deep knowledge and experience with healthcare operations and services, as well as all aspects of IT - from SW implementations to managing LAN/WAN staff and programmers.

I've also published and given presentations on project management and other topics. A recent article appeared in PM Boulevard, and can be accessed from here: http://www.pmboulevard.com/Default.aspx?page=View%20Content&cid=1896&parent=92

I lived and worked in Israel for 15 years, with primary experience in medical diagnostic sales and marketing for Israeli companies to Europe. I am very comfortable in a start up environment, having worked as part of the core team with start-ups in printing and bio-tech industries.

In August, 2005 I moved my family back to Israel, where we lived prior to coming to Baltimore, in 1996.
