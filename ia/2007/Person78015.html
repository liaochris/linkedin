Michael Santarcangelo’s Summary

I work with organizations committed to improving the way they think about and practice information security through the creation and delivery of exceptional experiences. How can I help you?

Here are three of our exceptional experiences

*Avoiding the Breach*
this is the companion two-day experience to our forth-coming book Into the Breach. We work to help introduce new patterns of thinking and protecting information - more than training for less than a standard training cost.

*Security Awareness Transformation*
Think awareness doesn't work, or stalled on where to start? We've developed a program based on our speaking and training that actually connects with users and helps you build your own awareness program; designed for results at a mind-blowing low cost.

*Speaking About Security*
Two days of hands-on learning how to take the brilliance in your head and relate it to others in a way they understand and take action. This is a must for anyone in security or technology today.

Want more? Here is the more standard verbiage....

Michael has advised a range of global clients (from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies) in different industries on the entire spectrum of information security issues and opportunities. Michael is a recognized security expert and served as a top Global Lead Instructor for the (ISC2) Common Body of Knowledge CISSP review seminar, helping prepare hundreds of security experts to become certified professionals each year.

His unique style of humor, optimism and energy combined with his passion for security and keen insights gained “in the trenches” touches audiences and inspires success!

Michael offers a refreshingly candid approach that demystifies the technologies and concepts while debunking common misconceptions, allowing participants to better understand the role information security can play in protecting and differentiating their business.

Michael Santarcangelo’s Specialties:

I think differently and blend concepts and successes from multiple areas of study to help companies protect information through speaking, training, awareness and addressing how to protect information to reduce breaches and gain compliance.
