Bev Holden’s Summary

Bev is a Thinking Consultant and co-founder of The Clear Thinking Partnership. She and her colleague Kate Hargreaves provide bespoke development and implementation coaching for leaders in business.

Bev has extensive experience in people, team and organisational development and an excellent track record of achievement in the retail sector over 18 years as both a Manager (J Sainsbury, the Burton Group, Adams Childrenswear and The Walt Disney Company), and as a Human Resources professional (The Walt Disney Company and Argos).

Working with your business Bev brings an ability to seize on new ideas and give them life. She has sharp insights and a flare for creative thinking, coupled with an analytical approach to problem solving.

Bev is outgoing and straightforward and has a capacity for seeing and understanding the wider context of a situation or challenge. She is energetic, spontaneous and responsive and has proven herself to be an effective communicator.

Bev generates enthusiasm in others and enables them to perceive things in new ways, so they feel a sense of urgency to make things happen. Bev also has the capacity to manage many tasks at one time to make speedy progress towards her goals.
