Javier Cañada’s Summary

¿Is there beauty in interaction design?

I have been designing interactive products for 10 years now. Most of the time I worked at IT firms and web agencies. Now I have my own studio. It's called Programa Vostok. I chose that name to honour those first cosmonauts who saw the earth from the outside in their quest for beauty.

Besides interaction design I am an active member of my professional community. I founded CADIUS, the Spanish-speaking community of interaction design professionals. I also try to share what I learn writing in my blog since year 2000.
