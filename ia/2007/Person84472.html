Tim Chambers’s Summary

Co-Founded, with Rob Sebastian, the new company Media 50 Group. We are working to bridge the new media arena -- and especially mobile media -- with the political sphere.

At Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, Chambers leads a team of forward-thinking specialists and engineers who focus on creating and studying key technologies and building out new technology prototypes, working closely with various teams from Sony's Japanese technology groups.

Before his appointment with SPDE, Chambers established and headed the Internet production infrastructure of Columbia TriStar Interactive.

Chambers' experience has made him a leader in entertainment technology and he has been a respected addition to a number of key groups within Sony including the Sony Global Content and Technology Committee and the chair of the Device Platform Group of Sony's US E-platforms efforts.

Prior to his tenure with Sony, Chambers worked in interactive gaming with such digital entertainment companies as Activision, Disney Software and And Interactive. He holds a B.A. in Communication Arts from Southern Methodist University.
