Brian Allan’s Summary

Mr. Allan has been involved in cutting-edge technology for over twenty years. Throughout his career Mr. Allan has been a proven business builder and market rainmaker in the education and government, business services, and application technology space. He has held executive roles including General Manager, Chief Marketing Officer, VP Sales, and President at several top technology companies. Mr. Allan has deep domain knowledge of on-demand/SaaS business models including web-based marketing, closed-loop systems, and ‘on-demand’ organizational structures.
