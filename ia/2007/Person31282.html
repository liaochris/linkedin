George Ikonomou’s Summary

Technical Manager, PMP-certified Project Manager, Chemical Engineer (PhD) with 17 years industrial experience in Oil and Gas, Chemical, Specialty Chemical and Paper industries. Activity spans a variety of areas: project management; business unit management of process control and advanced process control (APC); implementation of APC, industrial database and process control solutions; process control plant experience; R&D in the paper industry and fundamental research (academic research publications in Phase Equilibria and a patent in de-inking of laser printed paper). Projects executed in North America, most industrial countries in Europe, Middle East and India.

My primary interest is in industrial Operations; primary goal is to enhance my experience in technical management, project management and technical implementation. As a generalist with multifaceted and multicultural industrial experience, I am interested in a role of global view and responsibility in industrial operations where people skills, organizational skills, focus on results as well as technical knowledge are the keys to success.
