Luc BEAL (beal@prim-time.biz)’s Summary

Prim'Time Technology SA (http://en.prim-time.fr/) is a French company specialized on child safety.monitoring concepts, based on radiofrequency-based proprietary technologies. Based in the port-city of La Rochelle. Our main product is a '''bluetooth watch''' aimed at children between 3 and 9 years old : the parents can monitor the child's whereabouts from their mobile phone. Depending on the environment (at home, in a crowded supermarket or on the beach), the 'acceptable' range for keeping the child within 'safe' range varies.

Attractively designed, our ID-Watch is the platform to a wide array of monitoring innovations : monitoring of the child's health, access point to elearning applications (at school or at home), domotics and multimedia content access (Mp3, IPTV...).


We are interested in meeting other ventures in the areas of radiofrequencies/NFC (RFID...) for safety, health and education applications development, and child-related markets (safety, delivery of cultural services through the internet...), as well as specialists of child-related markets (safety, multimedia, TV, health...).
