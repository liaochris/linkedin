Thomas Krafft’s Summary

Thomas Krafft, founder of Acquiel, Inc. has been working on the "World Wide Web" ever since it first became accessible to the general public over a decade ago. He has contributed his experience to many companies – from technology and small business startups, to larger Fortune500 companies including Intuit, Microsoft, Sun, Cisco, Oracle, IBM, Apple, AOL, Adobe and more.

His background and experience include web, graphical and brand design, user-interface and information-architecture, code and business development, search engine optimization and marketing, lead generation, and more than a decade of design, web production, usability, QA, and editorial team and project management.

Thomas has a proven track record in making the connection between companies and customers via the Web. His work has been emulated by the likes of Amazon.com, and recognized by several sources including Jakob Nielsen's usability lab at Sun, David Siegel (author of "Creating Killer Web Sites") and CNET Builder.com's "Critique of the Week" where he received the top, 5-star rating for usability.

With more than a decade of experience with internet technologies, providing software and technology solutions for business, in addition to providing various types of support and investment to small business startups in the bay area, Thomas contributes his business knowledge, management skills and internet expertise to companies every day!

Acquiel, Inc.'s offices are located in Burlingame and Palo Alto, California, and Thomas can be reached at 1-888-267-ACQUIEL (2278).

Thomas Krafft’s Specialties:

Web site design, development, usability (UI) consulting, search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), online lead generation, logo/brand design, online business services, web hosting, online commerce systems, and more!
