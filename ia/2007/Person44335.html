Joshua Steimle’s Summary

Since 1999 I've owned and managed MWI, a firm providing web development, web design, and search engine optimization services from offices in Salt Lake City, Utah to clients in California, New York, Virginia, Boston, Reno, Utah, and Idaho.

In addition to custom web development and design services, MWI has three product offerings; a content management system for updating content on websites quickly and easily that can be used for both public websites as well as intranets, an online registration system for associations and event management, and an email newsletter system that allows clients to send out attractive, HTML emails to a subscriber database and get tracking reports back.

Clients include 3Com, Altiris, Bank of American Fork, Brighton Ski Resort, Connect Magazine, Extra Space Storage, FranklinCovey, Garage Technology Ventures, Hubble Homes, Sony, Sorenson Capital, the law firm of Strong & Hanni, Wasatch Venture Fund and Zions Bank.

I'm originally from Arcadia, California, near Los Angeles.
