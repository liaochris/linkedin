Adam Sussman’s Summary

I got my feet wet in the internet world back in 1995 working for AT&T WorldNet on their new “internet dialup service”.

The early years of the Internet I have held positions with Disney Interactive, AT&T Wireless Corporate, Weyerhaeuser, Apple and IBM Global Services. My positions varied from Field / Systems Engineer to LAN / WAN Administrators.

Before the Internet became what we used today, my focus was on Microsoft server and desktop infrastructure.

In the late 90’s I left my corporate position with IBM to assist a small Internet Service Provider in Los Angeles. Since that time my focus has been primarily entrepreneurial / internet related ranging from infrastructure / server collocation to building out multi million dollar online portals.

I am currently the Vice President of Business Development for WebGuerrilla, LLC and our focus is on Search Engine Marketing and Analytics.
