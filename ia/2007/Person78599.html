Shad Smith’s Summary

After many gallons of midnight oil since the earliest times of the Internet, I have finally acknowledged that what I really like about technology is the gritty, dirty early stages. When products become easy and clean, I happily pass them on to people who can use them to better their lives or just get more work done. I have a morbid fascination with breaking stuff and then fixing it.
