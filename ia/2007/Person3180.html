Alex Barrios’s Summary

I am a Senior Field Representative for State Assemblymember Dave Jones. In my line of work I spend most of my time coordinating outreach projects in the district. Our district covers most of the City of Sacramento and some of the surrounding areas. Projects I've coordinated include the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) Regional Outreach Effort with the Sacramento Coalition for Working Families, Cal Grant (Cash for College Campaign), and a series of town hall meetings to identify what the community priorities are in Sacramento.

Alex Barrios’s Specialties:

Community organizing, outreach, facilitation, communications, public relations, public speaking, and legislative policy
