Melissa Thompson Currier’s Summary

Melissa has more than fifteen years of experience in marketing-communications, strategic planning, message training, media relations and political communications. In the last several years she has led workshops and written a white paper on the “serve and suggest method” of interactive communications and has been a presenter at the Campaign & Advocacy Schools at the Leadership Institute, Leadership Anne Arundel, and the Ronald H. Brown Foundation. She is also honored to serve on the Admissions & Marketing Committee of the Board of Trustees for Tilton School and as a member of the Women's Sailing Championship Committee of the US Sailing Board of Directors.
