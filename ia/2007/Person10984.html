Bhooshan Pandya’s Summary

It's been wonderful working for a corporate giant like TCS. My current job provides me enough independence to work and take critical decisions. My ultimate aim is to institutionalize Usability within the organization. This will enable the company to make user-friendly GUIs and improve upon the user's experience.

I also spend time in understanding the role of communication design for emarketing and the role that technology can play here. As more and more organizations compete on-line to sell their ideas, there is a definite need to translate the leadership vision and values through deft and crisply designed documents. The proper use of technology ensures the unobtrusive delivery of the message to the end-user. However, I personally believe it can only be achieved if the user-experience exceeds the user's expectation from the website. Excessive use of eye-candy technology ultimately blinds the user from his task.

Going forward, I would like to initiate research and analysis in the field of Human Computer Interaction (HCI), a field where I have really associated myself thoroughly over the past years. HCI is also closely knit to user experience design which is the prime motivator for on-line transactions and dissemination of information. At the same time, I am also interested in studying brand communication strategy for on-line marketing and how usability principles can help in achieving the results seamlessly without compromising on the least on the overall user-experience.
