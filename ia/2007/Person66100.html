Pat McGraw’s Summary

Over his 25+ years experience, Pat McGraw has been helping innovative firms differentiate themselves from the competition in the hearts and minds of the target audience.

The end result - increased revenue, profits and marketshare.

Pat's efforts have helped start-ups, small and medium-sized enterprises as well as leading Fortune 100 corporations across multiple industries. Employers and clients include IBM, Microsoft, Sears, Kmart and ExxonMobil - industries include retail, high tech, telecommunications, tourism, hospitality, education, manufacturing, business services and financial services.

Pat is currently responsible for global marketing at Laureate Online Education Inc. (www.laureate-inc.com). With a presence in 15 countries and serving more than 240,000 students worldwide, Laureate is the most profitable, rapidly growing provider of post-secondary education on an international scale.

Prior to joining Laureate, Pat's experience includes senior-level positions with University of Maryland University College (www.umuc.edu), Blau Direct Marketing (currently Euro RSCG 4D, a division of Havas) and Frequency Marketing, a division of Alliance Data. Pat has also launched and operated a highly successful consulting and services firm, McGraw Marketing (www.mcgrawmarketing.com) and Cirino McGraw Inc., a Phoenix-based direct marketing firm.

Pat earned his B.S. in business administration from Arizona State University and his M.S. in marketing from The Johns Hopkins University. He is an avid writer and frequent speaker at conferences addressing topics such as "How to Develop a Successful Marketing Plan", "Loyalty Marketing", "Online Customer Acquisition" and more.
