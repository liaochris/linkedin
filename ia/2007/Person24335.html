Dominic Ryan’s Summary

Premier Global Recruitment is an established and well known recruitment and executive search company based in Bucharest and also incorporates separate property and management consultancy services. It is British owned and managed and provides staffing solutions to some of the country's largest international organisations as well as to a broad cross section of local companies in Bucharest ranging from the hotel and catering industry to finance and banking, construction, administration and management, skilled and semi-skilled professions and senior positions. Since taking over PGR I have restructured it in such a way as to now provide staff from within the domestic market to satisfy the demand from overseas for construction, hotel and catering, oil, gas and mining, engineering and the cruise and casino industries in particular those in the middle and Far East, east Africa and Northern America. PGR also provides property and land investment consultation and advice: My partners and I, all of whom have lived and worked in Romania for many years and who fully understand the ways in which business can be implemented in this country, are ideally located to assist local and foreign investors wishing to take advantage of the excellent opportunities that exist in Romania. Currently the percentage annual return on property and land investment is the highest in Europe, a trend that will continue for many years to come. To complement the recruitment and property consultancy services offered by Premier Global and its subsidiaries, we provide a full management service to people and businesses investigating the Romanian market or wishing to build or expand their enterprise here. Premier Global therefore is poised to assist the entire international business community and welcomes communication especially from members of LinkedIn who would like our assistance. For further details either please access our website on www.pgrom.com or contact me directly on dominic.ryan@pgrom.com

Dominic Ryan’s Specialties:
