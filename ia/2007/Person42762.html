  • General Manager

    Westhaven Worldwide Logistics

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    August 2002 — Present (5 years 4 months)

    Back in 2002 I decided to take the plunge and start out on my own. I had the backing of some reliable potential customers. It soon became evident that with a projected turnover of £500,000 in the first year, and a negative cash flow (airlines and shipping lines rarely give credit) this was not going to be possible without assistance.

    Westhaven Worldwide Logistics (a trading division of E H Smith (Westhaven) Ltd) was formed. With a group turnover in excess of £100 million in 2003, and a history dating back to the 1920's I had a reliable and solid base on which to grow the business, and the group aquired a new trading division.

    The business has grown year on year and we operate B2B freight services in the following areas:

    UK to/from:
    East & Western Europe (French, Spanish and German spoken fluently in our office)
    North America
    India
    China
    Malaysia
    South Africa
    Australia
    & New Zealand

    We also run a UK Warehousing and Distribution facility in Solihull, West Midlands.
