Sarah Doody’s Summary

Sarah Doody is a marketing and creative strategist who specializes in the intersection of business, design, and experience. She is known for her broad understanding of culture, communication, and technology and their collective influencing power on society.

Sarah's unique balance of creativity, curiosity, and culture help her uncover clients' intimate ideas and communicate them in a way that connects with the hearts and minds of consumers.

In 2004, Sarah Doody won Silver in the redesign of Boxes and Arrows - the Information Architecture industry's leading web based magazine on the practice, innovation, and discussion of design.

Over the last 8 years, Sarah has worked with Fortune 500 companies, successful start ups, and world-class non-profit organizations to help them use design as a means for creating unique brand identities, engaging consumer experiences, and compelling product and service offerings - all of which are rooted in a commitment to innovative ideas, powerful creativity, and emotionally connective experiences.

Sarah is currently the Director of Marketing Experience for Saber Seven and a Partner in the TLM Agency.

Sarah is passionate about transforming advertising from a series of static touchpoints with a brand to a dynamic network of thoughtfully designed interactions that are tailored to, and seamlessly blend with, people's lifestyles - creating a strong brand connection - a lifelong marriage with the brand.

Sarah currently resides in New York City.
