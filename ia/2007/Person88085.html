Udal Degli Uberti’s Summary

In 1990 Udal started working in the IT market for Olivetti Systems & Networks in Ivrea and Milan, then in Wordperfect and Novell for selected customers in NW Italy. In this period he frequently contributed with Mondadori Informatica, translating and writing cover stories for PC Magazine, PC Week, ZeroUno. Then, as Country Marketing Manager in Attachmate in Milan, worked in his first EMEA team. After this experience he decided to move to Rome with CA, taking care of the Banking Industry. In April 1999 was hired by Hewlett-Packard as Enterprise Account Manager for the Financial Sector in Southern & Central Italy. In August 2004 went back to Milan as EMEA Software Services Account Manager to manage and grow the Software Services on the major HP OpenView customers in Italy, France, Spain and Portugal. Since 2007 he's the Software Services Account Manager at HP Italiana.

His main objective is to constantly excel and grow in a stimulating and challenging international environment.
