Don Casey’s Summary

Don has nearly 30 years of experience in the education market. He has formed a consulting company called Education Sales Solutions (ESS) and is currently enjoying working with many nationally recognized education publishers. ESS is dedicated to providing our clients with professional guidance to build sales infrastructure that will help their direct education sales organization be more productive and stay focused on sales related activities focused. The result, increased sales and improved performance by sales professionals.

In 1981, he began his career as a 3rd grade classroom teacher. He sold educational software to schools from 1983-1985 and received a Master of Education degree in Computer-Based Education from Arizona State University in 1987. He then became the Western Regional Sales Manager for Mindscape Software, a Division of Scott Foresman school textbook company. By 1989, he was elevated to the National Sales Manager at Mindscape and was recruited to be the first Education Sales Manager for Broderbund Software in 1990. After a very rewarding experience at Broderbund, he joined a Mindscape again in late 1994 as the Vice President of the Education Division where he increased education division sales to nearly $10 million. In March 1998, Mindscape and several other key educational technology companies were acquired by The Learning Company, a wholly owned subsidiary of Mattel, Inc. At TLC, Don served as Vice President of Education Sales.

Classroom Connect recruited Don as their Vice President of Sales in late 1999. This internet delivered professional development service grew from $8 million to over $13.5 million under Don’s direction. In late 2001, when this company was acquired by Harcourt School Publishing, a division of Reed Elsevier, Don formed his consulting business and worked with Headsprout and IMSI before joining IMSI as the Vice President of Education Sales in 2003. In the fall of 2005, Don and his family moved to Austin, Texas.

Don Casey’s Specialties:

Sales management, infrastructure and training for companies focused on the public education market.
