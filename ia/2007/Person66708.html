Paul Niederer’s Summary

Specialises in restructuring, licensing, systemitising, developing, managing and growing businesses. This is achieved through hands on management, facilitating solutions through project management and harnessing the power of the internet.

He is particularly adept at getting organisations that are in difficulty back on their feet through the implementation of more efficient business processes.

He has developed, built, restructured and managed businesses and markets in Australasia, Asia, the Middle East and Europe.
This includes building and managing sales organisations in Asia and the Middle East, and Accounting Software distribution business in Europe and obtaining the allegiance of Accountants for the Quicken Professional Partners Program in Australia.

For the past ten years Paul has consulted to Australian businesses in business and market development, management, information system selection and implementation, internet orientation and marketing. The most recent project with Pearl Healthcare.
