Roman Karaszewski’s Summary

I started my professional career by cooperating with the Hewlett Packard and later moved on to gather experience as a support team member in a polygraph company called Studio Q. My adventure with internet advertising started in 2000 by being employed in the CR Media. In the beginning I worked in teams defining campaigns, to later move on to the post of the AdNetwork Administrator, where I as in charge of a team responsible for the technical support of all campaigns broadcast by the AdNet advertising network and commissioned by CR Media. I was also responsible for implementing the DoubleClick Dart system into the advertising activities of the AdNet and CR Media networks. As a freelancer I created internet services and supports and conducted seminars on the subject of Internet utilization by small and medium enterprises. My experience in mobile marketing comes from writing scenarios and coordinating teams responsible for mobile applications’ creation in Mobile Media Consulting, and also from being employed on the position of Product Manager in Axel Springer Kontakt. My duties in iloggo include the coordination of marketing activities.
