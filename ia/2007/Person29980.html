Frank Béraud’s Summary

Frank has more than 16 years of experience in the life sciences industry both in Quebec and Europe. Prior to joining Groupe Créa, he was the director of marketing and business development (1999-2003) at SignalGene, Inc., a public biotechnology company whose mission was anti-angiogenic drugs discovery and development; he was involved in license negotiation projects, material transfer agreements, service contracts, the trade of research assets, and the supervision of corporate communications.

From 1998 to 1999, Frank was director of marketing at bioMérieux Canada, the Canadian subsidiary of a major international company specialized in the field of in vitro clinical diagnostics. He successfully achieved the launch of new product lines in the microbiology and immunology markets.

Frank has also worked with great success in the marketing and sales departments of industry-leading companies in France as well as in Canada (Applied Biosystems, Roche Diagnostics, Kodak Diagnostics).
