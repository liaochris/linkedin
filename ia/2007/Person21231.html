David Bausola’s Summary

Senior Account Planner for Imagination, primarily looking after digital strategies for global brands.

Specialist in designing collaborative social media communications using distruptive technologies and business models.

Managed and delivered the first online user-generated video documentary website [www.channel4.com/fourdocs], enabling Channel4 to make relationships with a broad range of documentary makers whilst delivering both its first major online video service and open licence user-generated content platform. Awarded the ‘Best Offering from a Broadcaster 2005’ by Revolution and a BAFTA in 2007.

Re-engineered and delivered Channel 4’s education video distribution business [www.channel4.com/clipbank], reviving a commercial model that was restrained by the UK’s Local Educational Authority.

Devised and delivered the software for the largest public collaboration TV show [www.channel4.com/bigdig], bringing together over 10,000 participators who produced the UK’s largest online public archaeological database, which delivered a week of prime time live television to a daily audience of 3.5million.

Launched a non-commercial open-software collective to develop innovative interfaces. ixi-software.net were positioned successfully to operate worldwide, delivering lectures, presentations, performances and workshops which finance their on going research and development into cutting-edge and award-winning digital interface design.

Co-designed and delivered the UK’s first Broadband CPD system, which pioneered full screen interactive video-on-demand with integrated personal development records.

Planned, managed and delivered the first Playstation2 web-convergence platform for SCEE in 2001, giving gamers the opportunity to build online leagues and communities based upon their gaming performances whilst SCEE explored the meaning of console-web convergence in the UK.
