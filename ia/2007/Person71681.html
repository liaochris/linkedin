Regina Baker’s Summary

Regina Baker is a woman after God's own heart. Based in Houston, Texas, Regina has a background in corporate America in the fields of Information Technology and Procurement. More importantly, Regina has a strong entrepreneurial spirit that has led her to more fulfilling roles as a Motivational Speaker, Internet Marketer [with a focus on ecommerce & affiliate marketing], Trainer and Certified Business Coach.

Her strong passion for networking – both online and in the offline world – and her love for creating relationships have taken many forms in the years that she has been focused on online businesses.

Regina is the COO of Wahmcart.com, a web based ecommerce solution for small business owners and Founder of ShoppingCartFaq.com, a frequently asked questions and resource database of ecommerce solutions (shopping carts and affiliate marketing).

She was the driving force behind the launch of Christian Business Talk Radio, an internet radio show and podcast that provides a ready online resource to the Christian business owner’s community. Regina continues to serve as Executive Producer. Regina facilitates rich discussions and helps other business owners connect with each other and important resources for their business growth. You can learn more about this by visiting BusinessByGrace.com.

Even through the passing of her husband Ruben to cancer in 2002, Regina has learned to rely and trust in the wisdom and will of God. God's grace and mercy has allowed her to share with men and women who are going through tough challenges.

Regina is a passionate and engaging speaker. Her speaking topics include having the right attitude in business, personal growth, direct sales, the network marketing industry, networking, biblical principles, ecommerce solutions and internet tips and resources for newbies.
