Russ Mooney’s Summary

Old School Animation Grunt. Directing, producing and supervising all aspects of traditional 2D, 3D / CGI / Flash & Feature Film, DTV & Television animation production including pre-production and post production supervision. Developer and producer of independent animation projects. Producer / director: 20+ years. Overseas animation supervisor / producer / creative director, Disney and others: ( Japan, Taiwan, S. Korea, Philippines ) 8+ years in Asia. Working commercial director / animator / storyboard artist / layout artist: 9 years. Animation apprentice: 5 years. Some of these positions overlapping. 33+ years total continuous employment in the animation industry, working from the bottom up. Goal: Continue working in the animation craft, as I have always found it to be fulfilling and challenging.

Russ Mooney’s Specialties:

Traditional 2D, 3D / CGI & Flash animation producer, director, overseas animation supervisor / creative director as well as animation concept and content creator. 25+ years working in the Hollywood animation industry as well as 8+ years working in Asia as a creative director, overseas animation producer and overseas animation supervisor for Disney and others.
