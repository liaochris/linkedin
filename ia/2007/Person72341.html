Richard Jones’s Summary

I develop innovative ideas through to successful corporate ventures or standalone companies - including taking one to Nasdaq (post-acquisition).

I have helped, created and/or led successful companies in telecoms, software and electronics, created products in telecoms, healthcare, computing, electronics and software – used by Universal Studios, BT, BBC etc.

My first technology management book, Project Management Survival, is published by Kogan-Page and available at Amazon etc.

Companies I have helped develop and execute complex technologies include Shell, BT and St. Gobain and these days I:

• help build businesses for others - taking start ups all the way through to success or working on spinouts for multi-nationals

• develop new businesses with my partners

• solve complex business problems - rescuing projects and programmes, carrying out major change initiatives, developing business strategies and models

Experience

Director of electronics company from startup (peak 25-times increase in share value from first round to a NASDAQ float - post trade sale).

Interim CEO of companies in Internet service provision and 3-d animation software.

Helped startups from launch leading to a $20 million valuation (75x return to date)

Worked for major telco in evaluating and implementing all aspects of new ventures from concept to launch.

Project and programme manager turning around a number of technology development and implementation projects and companies of up to £200 million investment.

Lectured for the R&D Society on Incubation and Portfolio Management and at IMD in Lausanne on New Business Development.

Led the design and implementation (in French) of a 2 year long diagnostic and change programme for a French-owned, multi-national (turnover ~ $1 billion). This involved integrating development programmes incorporating 450 people in France, Germany and the US and led to the successful creation of an internal incubation operation.

Richard Jones’s Specialties:

Managing complex teams and developments, project management, developing ventures, creating exciting product roadmaps, facilitation, managing major change.
