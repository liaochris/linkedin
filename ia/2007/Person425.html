Aaron Davis’s Summary

Over the last five years, I have been working as a Counselor, Coach and Facilitator for people struggling with multiple barriers to success. My life experiences, education and training enable me to develop collaborative client centered relationships with empathy, tolerance and the provision of a safe and supportive environment. I can foster learning and guidance in areas such as employment readiness, job club, conflict resolution, anger awareness, interpersonal relationships, communication, goal setting, substance misuse (12 Step and Harm Reduction) and other life skills based topics.
