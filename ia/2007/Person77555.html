Scott Wellwood’s Summary

Scott Wellwood is currently employed as a Senior Business Development Manager for the Dynamic Media organization at Adobe Systems headquarted in San Jose, CA. This role has overall responsibility for managing a team that develops and sustains all strategic partnerships for Adobe that interact with the Dynamic Media business unit. This encapsulates Adobe's rich media products from creation to consumption such as Adobe's desktop professional CS3 Production Premium suite, Flash Media Server, rich media DRM and Adobe Media Player.

Scott is charged with identifying, developing and sustaining long term relationships with various 3rd party partners that compliment Adobe's video strategy and ensuring both he and his team have broad knowledge across all desktop and video distribution products and solutions within the business unit they support. He represents Adobe on a constant basis to outside organizations and develops deep relationships with the senior level business counterparts he interacts with representing the partners working with Adobe, typically Sr Director and higher.

Scott has spent his entire 14 year professional career in the desktop creative software industry. He joined Adobe in 1998 and has held various roles in product management and business develpment supporting the professional, consumer and mobile imaging in addition to professional video products at Adobe. Prior to Adobe, Scott worked for a small SOHO focused software company in Palo Alto.

Scott holds a bachelors degree in Business Administration at San Jose State University.
