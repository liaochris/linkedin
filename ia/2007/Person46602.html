Katie Campbell’s Summary

Katie Campbell, a native of Marietta, Ga., joined The Haystack Group as an account executive in July 2007, where she is responsible for media relations, strategic planning and internal communications.

Prior to joining Haystack, Katie served as an associate account executive at McNeely Pigott & Fox Public Relations, one of the largest independent public relations firms in the Southeast. While at MP&F, Katie supported a variety of clients, including the U.S. Department of Labor’s Job Corps program, West Tennessee Healthcare, Colonial Pipeline and the Nashville Chamber Orchestra.

As part of the Job Corps “Blitz Build," Katie led media relations efforts in Lafayette, La., for the Coordinator for Gulf Coast Rebuilding, appointed by President Bush. Campbell also worked with the Girl Scouts of Cumberland Valley, where she organized and planned an event for more than 3,000 local Girl Scouts and volunteers to celebrate the 95th anniversary of Girl Scouting.

Prior to MP&F, Katie interned with the Country Music Television (CMT) news department, Girl Scouts of Northeast Georgia, WNNX radio station in Atlanta and Community Bank of the South in Smyrna, Ga. While at the Girl Scouts, she analyzed national research and formulated a strategic marketing outreach plan, culminating in the organization of a girl-led Girl Scout marketing team.

Katie graduated cum laude and with honors from the University of Georgia with bachelor’s degrees in broadcast news and marketing. She is an 18-year veteran of the Girl Scouts of America, where she serves on the Gold Award Alumni committee. In her free time, Katie also enjoys playing the violin, lacrosse and soccer, and volunteering.
