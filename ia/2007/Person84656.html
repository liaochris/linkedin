Ted Wham’s Summary

I have a twenty-year background as a database marketer with a heavy bent towards the technical and analytic side of the profession. Since 1997 I have focused exclusively on email marketing and Internet privacy applications, with increasing levels of responsibility in these areas leading to my current general management role for the largest email marketing service provider in the world, Epsilon Interactive.
