Josh Sawyer’s Summary

My introduction to the game industry was initially through Interplay's web design department, though I have been heavily involved in tabletop and computer role-playing games from a young age. I designed the Planescape: Torment website and worked closely with the Black Isle development team to transition over into a junior design position on Icewind Dale.

I worked at Black Isle from 1999 to 2003 and was involved in the following projects:

Icewind Dale (Junior Designer)
Icewind Dale: Heart of Winter (Designer)
Icewind Dale 2 (Lead Designer)
Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance (Additional Design)
The Black Hound (cancelled, Lead Designer)
Fallout 3 (cancelled, Co-Lead Designer)

I spent a year and a half at Midway working on Gauntlet: Seven Sorrows before returning to my fellow BIS co-workers at Obsidian Entertainment. I was one of two Lead Designers on Neverwinter Nights 2 and am currently the Lead Designer on an Aliens RPG for SEGA.

I have also done a small amount of freelance tabletop RPG design, including a co-writing credit on Goodman Games' Power Gamer's 3.5 Warrior Strategy Guide.

Josh Sawyer’s Specialties:

RPG System Design, Combat Design, Design Management.
