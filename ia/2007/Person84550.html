Robert Dempsey’s Summary

Robert Dempsey
Atlantic Dominion Solutions - http://www.techcfl.com
Rails For All - http://www.railsforall.org

Beginning a career in IT more than 8 years ago, Robert Dempsey transitioned to web application development in 2001, teaching himself PHP and VB.NET. In 2005, during one of his classes at Rollins College, a classmate told Robert to “check out” Ruby on Rails. He joined the Orlando Ruby Users Group and immediately dove into learning both Ruby and Ruby on Rails. What he found caused him to change the focus of his company, Atlantic Dominion Solutions, from a PHP shop, to a full-service Ruby on Rails development firm. For a year and a half, Robert Dempsey and ADS have been creating dynamic and flexible Ruby on Rails applications for a variety of clients, for both internal and public use.

In March of 2007, Robert launched his not-for-profit Rails For All, Inc., with the purpose of giving back to the community that had given him so much. Rails For All, soon to be a 501(c)(3) company, strives to bridge the gap between the development community and the companies it serves by bringing savvy businesses intent on maximizing their opportunities on the Web together with experienced, passionate, and innovative Rails
developers.

In addition to Rails For All ( railsforall.org), Robert gives frequent talks at the Orlando Ruby Users Group, teaches designers and others the basic of Ruby on Rails, publishes articles for Amazon Web Services Developer Connection, and mentors new freelance Rails developers on the basics of Ruby on Rails, and the business of being a freelancer.

Robert is now a published author with his most recent article on Amazon.com: http://developer.amazonwebservices.com/connect/entry.jspa?categoryID=112&externalID=846
