Heather Hartmann’s Summary

Heather Hartmann, LLC is an extension of your human resources or recruiting department. Or, if you are a smaller organization and don't have an HR or Recruiting team, we can do that for you. Our model is very flexible, and able to meet any of your Recruiting and HR needs. We've done work for a variety of industries, and all sizes of organizations, both locally and nationally.

Prior to starting Heather Hartmann, LLC, Heather was the Executive Director of the Human Capital Metrics Consortium, an independent nonprofit organization. HCMC was founded to establish standard, universally comparable human capital metrics. The Consortium also conducts standard benchmark surveys and publishes associated reports (see www.staffing.org for more info on the HCMC)

Heather has over seventeen years of recruiting experience including key positions at Peabody Energy, Accenture, Cap Gemini Ernst and Young, The Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis and EdwardJones. She has been an Advisor to Staffing.org, and has served as a Region Chair and Area Vice President of the Employment Management Association. Within the past year, Heather partnered with AAIM Management Association to build their Talent Acquisition Solutions practice.
