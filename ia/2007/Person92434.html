Yves Mulkers’s Summary

Yves is Belgian, a native Dutch speaker, graduated as a chemist but he followed his passion for automation, software and computers and turned to the IT sector.

He evolved to a data integration specialist due to self study and persistence.

Working as a software developer in several branches, he gained a great expertise in
object oriented thinking and development.
Doing the full cycle of software development from analysis, implementation, support and project management in combination with a strong empathy he positioned himself as a technical expert on mainly Microsoft technologies bridging into the needs of the business and the users.

Working on several projects as a database specialist he focused on SQL server and gained expertise on all levels in this environment, like DTS, Database tuning, linked servers and replication.
Since the beginning of 2007 Yves started already in supporting Alpha’s team on data integration issues on their projects and as of February this will be his full time job.
Yves is a quick learner, with strong analytical and interpersonal skills, who keeps quality high on his list and stands for working and usable solutions.
