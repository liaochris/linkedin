Chris Russell’s Summary

Chris Russell is a senior executive from the motion picture industry with cross-industry experience in consumer electronics and web development. He has a successful track record within entrepreneurial startups and large corporations alike, and in-depth experience across a wide range of disciplines, including new product development, engineering, business operations, product management, corporate organization, strategy, and corporate development.

Motion picture projects include Stuart Little, Hollow Man, Godzilla, Starship Troopers, Contact, Down Periscope, Sudden Death, Power Rangers, Tall Tale, Dolores Claiborne, Clear & Present Danger, Outbreak, I Love Trouble, Time Cop, and Demolition Man, amongst others.

Chris Russell’s Specialties:

Senior management, corporate strategy, operations, product management, product development, software engineering, business development, corporate development, corporate organization, content security, computer graphics, visual effects, & post-production.
