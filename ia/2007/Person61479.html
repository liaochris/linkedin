Kate Coe’s Summary

I’ve produced, directed and written television reality-based programming, documentaries, behind-the-scenes and “making of” feature films.
I have a strong love of archival film as well as stills and artwork, and know all the ins and outs of film research, clip clearance, and securing of rights and have worked closely with the legal affairs departments at all major studios and networks..
As a producer of “value-added” packages for DVD releases of classic films, I’m responsible for researching and writing scripts, booking and directing interviews and making-of documentaries, creating “Easter-egg” features, and numerous other details that go into making a successful viewing experience.
