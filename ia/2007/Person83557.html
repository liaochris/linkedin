Suzi Edwards’s Summary

As an innovative content strategist, I bring a versatile mix of expertise to business communications. My experience spans from book writing to event management. In the business world, I've written and produced high-return campaigns that synchronized email, print, online and event marketing channels.

Through my company, WallFly Marketing, I specialize in brand development and marketing communications. My comprehensive content strategies will add strength and character to your company's online and offline presence. From formulating a new brand to polishing website content, I believe that clear, audience-focused messaging is at the core of effective communications.

Examples of consultative projects and writing specialties include:

Brandswarm Session – This hands-on workshop will pinpoint the essence of your brand and new channels for customer attention and retention. Through the fly’s eye view, we connect the dots of your brand’s goals and aspirations, your clients’ perspective and your competitors’ positioning.

Competitor Buzzdown – You know your competitors best. Do you know what their next moves are? Use WallFly to go undercover and investigate.

Field Feeler Study – Focus groups and surveys can deliver misleading data and are time consuming for your audience. Get the real fly-on-the-wall view by gathering stories directly from happy customers and unsatisfied ex-customers.

Content – From press releases to confirmation emails, all content counts. WallFly will create clear, concise messaging for all channels.

Marketing Campaigns – Use WallFly to create and manage your next campaign. Together, we will construct a comprehensive strategy to include cost-effective search engine marketing, offline events and targeted promotion strategies.

Ad Creatives – Effective advertising requires the right placement and a concept that grabs your audience in under two seconds. WallFly can help you connect the dots between copy and design for immediate results.
