Brent Otterlei’s Summary

UI/Web/Visual designer with experience in a wide variety of projects both online and off. Coming primarily from a design background, but working and training in the web industry for a number of years, I have developed the ability to both write code and design.

Brent Otterlei’s Specialties:

CSS, HTML, DHTML, XHTML, XML, XSL, XSLT, JavaScript, PHP, Open Laszlo, Dojo JavaScript ToolKit, and Graphic Design.

Brent Otterlei’s Experience

  • UI Designer/Web Developer/Visual Designer

    Teqlo Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 2006 — November 2007 (1 year 5 months)

    In charge of designing, creating and maintaining the user interface and user experience for the Teqlo runtime, Teqlo Builder, and the family of QuickWork web apps that were built on top of the Teqlo runtime technology.
