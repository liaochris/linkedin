Nadim Saad’s Summary

I am a business angel and a successful entrepreneur with a 10-year track record in international business development and strategic expansion in Europe, South America and the Middle East. I was a founding member of four startups/early-stage companies in the Telecoms, Internet and Retail sectors.

I developed an expertise in international expansion and company growth and strong skills in strategic alliances, contract negotiations and getting things done. I am very well connected and I am a keen communicator with strong passion. These ingredients make me an excellent evangeliser for innovative and disruptive technologies.
