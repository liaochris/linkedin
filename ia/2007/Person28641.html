Fabrice Florin’s Summary

Fabrice is Executive Director of NewsTrust, a social news network. This non-profit venture brings together concerned citizens and experienced journalists to rate news sources and their stories according to rigorous editorial standards - leveraging online technology to identify high-quality journalism, or "news you can trust."

Previously, Fabrice founded Handtap, a best-of-breed content provider for mobile phones. GoComics, our joint venture with AndrewsMcMeal Universal, now manages our wireless product lines. Our comics, photos and animations are distributed by top carriers like Cingular, Sprint, Verizon and other market leaders.

As Macromedia’s VP of online entertainment, Fabrice launched shockwave.com, a popular web site featuring games, cartoons, music and greetings. Before joining Macromedia, Fabrice was president of Zenda, an award-winning multiplayer game developer for Compaq, Disney, Electronic Arts, Microsoft, Philips and Sony.

Prior to Zenda, Fabrice was executive producer at Apple Computer, where he created groundbreaking CD-ROM and interactive TV products with partners like Lucasfilm and National Geographic. Before joining Apple, Fabrice was president of Videowest, an innovative TV production studio, where he created a new genre of video journalism on ABC, MTV and public TV.

Fabrice Florin’s Specialties:

Development executive with 25-year track record in new media and technology. Expertise in product development, from game design to web publishing and TV production. Launched a wide range of successful products in entertainment, telecommunications and education. Extensive experience with business development, content licensing and product marketing. Innovator with entrepreneurial spirit. Passion for digital media. Strong leadership, team building and communication skills.
