Edmund Zaloga’s Summary

EZD is independent design, existing to support great ideas & free thought, through graphic media. I believe that creative visual solutions, applied thoughtfully, have the ability to make a powerful impact on our world & time. Feel free to visit my website to learn more about what I do, see current article postings, and view some of my work.

Edmund Zaloga’s Specialties:

My specialty is focusing on creating visual design that provokes thought. My experience includes designing brand identities, packaging, as well as print and web publishing. I enjoy logo deveopment and implementing an identity system across all verticals of visual design and media.
