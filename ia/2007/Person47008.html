Keith Watson’s Summary

Engineer focused on a wide range of information security and assurance issues. Primarily interested in practical security and software architectures as well as intrusion detection, prevention, and especially mitigation. Experienced with technical security education of undergraduate and graduate students. Information security consultant to a federally-funded biosecurity network. Always interested in the failure of technology and the resulting impacts. Have non-security interests in social issues related to use and availability of pervasive technology and ubiquitous networking. Former researcher, security consultant, and product marketing manager at a large computer manufacturer.
