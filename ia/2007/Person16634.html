Chris Meager (chris@smrgroup.com)’s Summary

I manage the EMEA operation for the Security Management Resources (SMR) Group, the largest specialist security management search firm globally.

Formed in 1998 SMR are the only firm specialising in this sector to have consultants based both in the Americas and Europe. My colleagues within SMR all have significant senior level management experience leading corporate security programs across a wide range of industries for some of the world's leading organizations. This ensures clients will have a subject matter expert with “real world” experience directly involved in their project.

I have over eight years practical experience recruiting security management personnel and have successfully completed projects in over forty countries. In addition to this I have two years experience within the security industry and a Post-Graduate Diploma in Security Management from the renowned Scarman Centre at the University of Leicester.

Projects I have successfully completed include roles with titles such as:
Chief Security Officer
Director, Security
Regional Security Manager, EMEA
Regional Director, Brand Protection, Asia Pacific
Regional Director, Corporate Security & Business Continuity, EMEA
Regional Security Director, Americas
Regional Head of Security & Fraud Risk, Middle East
Associate Director, Group Security, Africa
Regional Head of Fraud, EMEA

Companies I have successfully placed staff with include:
Amgen, HSBC, Deutsche Bank, Shell, Baker Hughes, WorleyParsons, Imperial Tobacco, JT International, Philip Morris International, Nestle, Barclays Capital, Kraft Foods, BG Group, Pfizer, De La Rue, British American Tobacco, Aventis, Wyeth, Barclays Group, Nike, AS Watson & Co, Metro.

Chris Meager (chris@smrgroup.com)’s Specialties:
