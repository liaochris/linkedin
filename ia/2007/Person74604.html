Roy de Souza’s Summary

ZEDO is an ad serving company. We focus on highly scalable ad serving for large internet companies, both publishers and advertisers. ZEDO is based in San Francisco and has offices in London, East Coast and India.

We also have an exciting new product in the eCommerce space.

In particular we lead the market in ad serving for Direct Marketers and Lead Generation companies. We provide all the detailed reporting and optimization that companies that buy huge volumes of ads require.

We are also now focused on Video Ads which is a high growth field.
