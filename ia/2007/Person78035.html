Seema Kumar’s Summary

Versatile and meticulous apparel Merchandiser and Product Developer with over six years of experience in apparel buying houses, as well as export houses. Backed by professional qualification of P.G. Diploma in 'Knitwear Design and Technology' from the 'National Institute of Fashion Technology'.

Work experience in the field of product design and development, sampling, merchandising, costing, T & A calendar planning and management. Have worked with various US and European customers and handled varied product categories like sweaters, fine knits, denim, wovens and also a brief experience in Leather.
