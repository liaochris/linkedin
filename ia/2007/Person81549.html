Joel Libava’s Summary

About Joel Libava/Franchise Selection Specialists Inc.

Since 1990, The Libava family has been helping future entrepreneurs with their dreams of business ownership.

We will lower your risk with our personality assessment tools, our knowledge of the Northern Ohio area, and our years of real world franchise industry experience. If you decide to work with us, know that you will be treated professionally, and will not be sold a thing. We will however, present you with some dynamic opportunities in franchise/business ownership, and give you the tools to research them..safely, so you can make a fact based decision.

Joel Libava, Franchise Selection Specialists Inc. 2nd generation President, brings real world franchise industry experience to his clients, coming from a franchise management background in the automobile and restaurant/hospitality industries.

Joel uses personality profiling tools, along with his innate intuitive skills to match his clients to appropriate franchise/business opportunities that can help his clients build equity in their own businesses.

Joel is frequently called on for his expertise by leading local and national media outlets, including The Wall Street Journal,Entrepreneur Magazine,and others.
he is an increasingly popular franchise industry blogger. Check out The Franchise King Blog!
