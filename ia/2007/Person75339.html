Ryan Lum’s Summary

I have been writing music since I first convinced my parents to buy me a guitar at the tender age of 8. In 1992, Projekt Records released my first album, which was an instant underground success. This was followed up by 3 more 10,000+ selling albums. Working with vocalist Anji Bee, Lovespirals began in 1999 and have since created 3 full length CDs and several EPs. Our music has been featured on MTV, VH1, E!TV, ABC, the WB, the EW Network, including Dawson's Creek and the Oprah Winfrey Show.

In addition, I develop our various web sites including www.lovespirals.com, www.lovespiralsdownwards.com, www.anjibee.com, and www.ryanlum.net. So I'm quite experienced with XHTML, CSS, RSS, PHP, ColdFusion, MySQL, Wordpress, DNS, SEO, New media, and e-commerce. Outside consulting of same nature.
