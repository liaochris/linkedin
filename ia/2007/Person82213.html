Steve Cummings’s Summary

As an Information Technology consultant, I offer consolidated skills associated with compliance and the reduction of cost associated with becoming compliant with Regulatory and Statutes obligations. HIPAA, Gramm-Leach Bliley Act, U.S. Patriot Act, and SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act) are many of the issues business must contend with in their Privacy and Security management. My work includes information security assessments, systems monitoring, risk management and security and contingency planning for facilities and systems, communication systems design and development and management of business systems. Systems monitoring involves the necessary production of audit trails, reporting and investigations of intrusion attempts, and message authentication. My expertise is in business impact analysis, gap analysis, risk assessment and risk mitigation. By assessing the infrastructure of facilities and institutions, I determine if procedure and practices are properly implemented and policies are supported by the documented procedures and operational practices. In that capacity, I am often responsible for compliance management of vendors’ implementation and vendor compliance validation. I have also been called upon to manage the implementation of specialized systems and to ensure compliance to specifications and contractual requirements. I act as a trusted advisor and vendor manager for many Fortune 500 customers. I have implemented multiple systems and standards for financial and healthcare institutions as well as power and industry clients. Through years of extensive involvement and experience in this field, I have developed privacy and security questionnaires to streamline what could otherwise be a lengthy process. The questionnaires are currently being used by many institutions including law firms and security companies to evaluate the conditions of compliance and risk they might face pertaining to import/export of data, privacy and security monitoring and reporting.
