Derrick Cho’s Summary

Mr. Cho has over sixteen years of marketing and business development experience in online advertising, software, Internet, and wireless technologies. A launch, and branding specialist, Mr. Cho has created marketing strategies and programs on behalf of The Globe and Mail, Mediconsult, FloNetwork, Classwave Wireless, and Wysdom.

While at FloNetwork, Derrick led the development of marketing communications programs that drove a 587% increase in sales revenues, earned FloNetwork a spot on the Deloitte & Touche Fast 500, and ultimately led to the acquisition of FloNetwork by DoubleClick (NASDAQ: DLCK) in 2001. At the Globe and Mail (Canada’s National Newspaper), Derrick was hired as the company’s very first New Media employee, where over a three year period, he spearheaded the introduction of Internet-based advertising programs, led the redesign of several of the newspaper’s Web sites, and launched PointCast into the Canadian marketplace. In addition to his technology background, Derrick has worked at several of Canada’s Top 10 advertising agencies, including BBDO, TAXI Advertising / Design, Enterprise, and DRAFT/FCB Canada. Derrick most recently led the Toronto office of RMG Connect, the direct and digital marketing arm of JWT Canada. Derrick’s Client work has garnered multiple Marketing Awards and his clients have included: Lexus Canada, IBM, Abbott Laboratories, Tim Hortons, Sick Kids Foundation, Diageo, and Novartis.

As a consultant for Clang! Clang! Derrick has provided online marketing/marketing communications services for a number of clients across a wide variety of verticals. Clients include: technology companies (internet & wireless), advertising agencies, entertainment/media companies.
