Anthony Stonefield’s Summary

Thought leader, executive strategist and evangelizer in interactive media industry.

Creation of the $1 billion US ringtone market and popularization of the $4.6 billion Western World ringtone market (2000-2005)

Industry leading skills and experience in founding and ramping start up new media ventures, including 14 years of multi-disciplinary experience in Product and Market Development and Strategy Execution on an international level; Product Innovation and Evangelism; Executive and Operational Management; Product Marketing; and Capital Raising

Identifying and implementing product and market development strategies and solutions in the convergence of interactive media, communications and technology that result in substantive market penetration, sustainable competitive advantage and blue-sky value and revenue potentials

Evolutionary, multi-dimensional understanding of emerging interactive media solutions for telcos, media companies, consumer brands, device manufacturers and the consumer market

Anthony Stonefield’s Specialties:

Futuristic innovation, development, consumer marketing and envangelizing new products, businessses and markets.

    Developper and provider of a "Reactive Social Multimedia Networking" platform that brings interactive entertainment and personalization products to the Unified Communications (wireless and fixed-base Internet) industry.

  • Co-founder & CEO

    USA Wireless LLC

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Wireless industry)

    April 2005 — November 2006 (1 year 8 months)

    Identification and scoping of an opportunity to establish interactive text messaging and media download services as product extensions for a major audio-telephony entertainment company. Corporate management, product design and consumer marketing.

  • Chief Strategy Officer

    InfoSpace Mobile

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; INSP; Wireless industry)

    October 1993 — March 2005 (11 years 6 months)

    Responsible for strategic guidance for business development and new technology sourcing, product innovation, and M&A. Ran the Los Angeles "content" group responsible for generating revenues of over $90M in 2004 and $150M in 2005.

  • Chief Strategy Officer

    Moviso LLC

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; VU; Wireless industry)

    January 2002 — October 2003 (1 year 10 months)

    Responsible for the ongoing development of the wireless content and services company as a subsidiary of Vivendi Universal Networks. Role included strategic business and product development, product innovation, international expansion and overall management. Co-managed the sale of the subsidiary to InfoSpace, Inc.

  • Chief Executive Officer / Founder

    YourMobile (aka Premium Wireless Services, GMO) / Moviso

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Wireless industry)

    October 1993 — January 2002 (8 years 4 months)

    Founded a pioneering New Media company around the concept of digital distribution of content via public networks. Pioneered digital downloading of songs (collaboration with AT&T Labs) in 1995, followed by numerous interactive media marketing and delivery solutions for the entertainment industry.

    Pioneered mass distribution of wireless content and established the ringtone and wallpaper markets in the Western World in 2000. Developed the first ringtone publishing platform for US carriers and the first ringtone client application (Ringster on BREW) in 2001.

    Sold the company to Vivendi Universal Networks in January 2002.
