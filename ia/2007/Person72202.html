Ricardo Vargas (ricardo@macrosolutions.com.br)’s Summary

Ricardo Vargas is a project management specialist. He was responsible, in the past ten years, for more than 5 billion US dollars in areas as telecom, IT, finance and energy.

He is a partner of Macrosolutions, a consulting company specialized in project, portfolio and risk management.

Ricardo is the author of eight books and one DVD. He has been published in Brazil, USA, UK and other coutries, with more than 75,000 books sold.

In 2005, Vargas was honored with the PMI Distinguished Award and also the PMI Product of the Year Award for his PMDome Workshop, considered the best solution for project management learning.

He is a PMI certified Project Management Professional (PMP), International Project Management Association Certified as IPMA-B. Vargas has a bachelor degree in Chemical Engineering and a Master degree in Production Engineering from UFMG. He has also a Master certificate in Project Management from George Washington University.

He was the first professional worldwide to be a Microsoft Certified Office User Specialist in MS Project 2002 and is an acknowledged reviser of the most important reference in the world concerning project management, the PMBOK Guide.

He is a professor in many MBA and graduation courses in Brazilian and American schools. He has presented several technical papers in this area, in Brazil, in the USA, in Europe and in Asia.

He is a member of the Project Management Institute (PMI) Board of Directors, the most important world organization focused on project management.
He is also member of the Association for Advancement of Cost Engineering (AACE), of the American Management Association (AMA), of the International Project Management Association (IPMA), of Global Association for Risk Professionals (GARP), of the Intitute for Global Ethics (IGE) and the Professional Risk Management International Association (PRMIA).
