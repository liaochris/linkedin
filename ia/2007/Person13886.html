Peter Hall’s Summary

Key challenges I am seeking include:
- Working with companies that are seeking to achieve value growth beyond their current capability.
- Joining Boards and management teams actively working to achieve substantial capital value creation.
- Guiding rapidly growing firms through the practical issues of survival, strategy and planning.

Key personal attributes include:
- A strong ability to rapidly understand complex technology, technical markets and their imperatives;
- A keen understanding of the importance of optimal interaction of markets, human capital and resources;
- A high regard for the role of leadership, recruitment, people development and people management in meeting business and investor objectives;
- An inclusive and outcomes focussed style that is calm, considered and rational;
- A high level of understanding of government and business interaction, commercial opportunities and assistance programs.
- A good understanding of corporate governance and challenges for small companies.

Major Strengths:

- Strong leadership and team building skills;
- A clear focus towards improving shareholder returns and value creation.
- A participative attitude towards strategic planning and corporate development and change activities
- Sound written and verbal communication skills; and
- An ability to get on with people at all levels and a strong lateral thinker.
- An ability to quickly understand technical concepts and issues.

Personal industry experience:

- Raised investment in successful new ventures from angels and VCs;
- Grown small businesses into corporations with 20 years in front line experience in senior management;
- 12 years in corporate business advisory, corporate development and change management in IT (hardware and software), furniture, food, fishing and aquaculture sectors in national and global markets.
