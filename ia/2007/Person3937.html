Michael Isaac Almond’s Summary

I have worked as a Web professional for the last nine years in both full time positions and as an independent freelancer. I have a particular focus on User Experience and User-Centered Design principles and specialize in front-end disciplines, including visual design, UI, site structure, site content and front-end development.

The diversity of my clients and projects ranges from eCommerce Web sites and Web applications to non-profit advocacy and education sites. My professional skills draw on ten years of previous experience as an Art Director, Graphic Designer, and Campaign Organizer at a leading Social Marketing media agency. I have management experience as well and have lead creative teams, worked directly with clients, funders and advisory panels. I am also a Contributing Writer to Digital Web Magazine.
