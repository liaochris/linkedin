Peter Bragg’s Summary

Peter has had a successful and progressive career to a senior level in IBM, Remedy and more recently with Ovidium. He has experience of managing some of IBM’s largest accounts where he consistently delivered sales performance above target.

At Mindjet he turned a small UK subsidiary business into a highly profitable, dynamic and expanding company. This was achieved by focussing on Enterprise sales through dedicated VAR Channels.

Owned by its employees, Ovidium is committed to providing best in class products and services to its clients thus helping them increase customer, employee and stakeholder value.
