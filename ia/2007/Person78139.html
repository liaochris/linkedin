Semmy Stumpp’s Summary

Started in a publishing company having a full training on the job for 3 years and became a fully accredited publisher in 1988. After working as inhouse ad copy writer and then later as independent ad copy writer I started as a software consultant. I did coach over 8,000 people in live seminars from 1990-1999 with office software like Word and associated products with the focus on helping people being more productive. I did coach clients in Europe in English and German and the special focus project management lead to my first published hardcover book about MS Project in 1998. During the same time I discovered the Internet and went online as early as 1991 with different mediums including the mailbox network pre-public-Internet. I was one of the first users on CompuServe as well AOL and all other first-to-be services (don't remind me what kind of fortune I spent to be first in all those areas!). I stopped teaching seminars end of the 90s to focus more on developing strategies for myself to be financial independent using information publishing and the Internet. Due to the fact that I lived a few years in the US and some years in Holland I speak Dutch and English besides my native German. I moved 2004 to the Canary Islands and enjoy the good climate and sunny weather here, gaining on my ability to speak Spanish.
