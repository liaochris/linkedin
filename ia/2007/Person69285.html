Laurie Bell’s Summary

I have been interested in Public Relations for some time, and am actively seeking a PR Associate position. The most stimulating part of my current work in Human Resources has been the focus on internal communications and employee relations to carefully communicate sensitive, often unpopular information in a palatable, credible fashion.

Furthermore, my Organizational Psychology and Foreign Languages background has benefited me greatly in the Silicon Valley and Europe, where doing a successful job requires a global perspective. While at the Infineon Technologies, A.G., Munich, I learned quickly that processing a simple office invoice took an understanding of three languages and cultures.

Additionally, I find in my current work a great need for understanding and detecting where cultural differences interact with daily business processes, and how to leverage those as much as possible.
