René de Jong’s Summary

In September 2005 I started to work for LogicaCMG as a Test Consultant after finishing my graduation project at LogicaCMG. In collaboration with another graduator I realized a stream suite for the creation of MPEG2 DVB transport streams.

To improve my theoretical knowledge concerning testing I successfully completed the LogicaCMG Test Masterclass in October 2005. Afterwards I brought this knowledge into practice at companies such as Wolters Kluwer, VIR e-Care Solutions, Philips Medical Systems, Nederlandse Spoorwegen (NS) and Philips Intellectual Properties & Standards. Presently I fulfill the role of Test Coordinator at SIDN.

Hands-on mentalities, social, communicative, motivated and undertaking are core words that describe me. During my professional education study Humans & Informatics I learned a lot about designing software and defining what the users, costumers and project managers want. I like to provide a ‘bridge’ between the user or customer and the software product that’s eventually realized.

My personal goal is to become a good Team Manager. The tasks, responsibilities and skills that relate to such jobs really appeal to me.
