Dean Thompson’s Summary

Here is my standard brief bio:

An expert in transaction and distributed computing systems, Mr. Thompson has a long history as a founder and leader of successful technology companies. These include: Transarc (now IBM Pittsburgh Labs); e-Transport (acquired by Descartes); Premier Health Exchange (acquired by Medibuy); and Medebiz (merged with Moai Technologies). Mr. Thompson has a B.S. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University.

Dean Thompson’s Specialties:
