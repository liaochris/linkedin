Paul Rouleau’s Summary

Quality driven Software Architect, operating under the company name of INSight Software.

I've been an independent software consultant since 1992 working primarily in the commercial software arena. I'm a high-tech guy and I like to work on cutting edge technology.

I enjoy mentoring others. I feel that a strong team is a necessity when trying to get a product out the door. I relish the opportunity to help a peer understand a concept, so that he/she is more productive and beneficial to the project.

I'm a firm believer in starting with a good design. "If you don't have time to do it right the first time, when will you ever have time to do it over?"
