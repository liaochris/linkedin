Eran Yarkoni’s Summary

An experienced high-tech executive and entrepreneur, and founder of high-tech startups.

CEO of CyOptics Inc., have led the company since its inception in 1999 and positioned it as a leading provider of high-speed active components for optical networks. Laid the technological, business and financial foundation of the company. Led the company’s three financing rounds, raising in total over $70M from leading strategic & financial investors.

An 18-year veteran of Intel Corporation, served in numerous management, engineering, marketing and sales roles. Managed Intel’s Pentium® MMX Processor launch, with >$10B in sales, having full responsibility for product development engineering, marketing, manufacturing, quality & reliability. Served as a program manager at Intel's headquarters in Santa Clara, CA, and as a marketing manager for Intel's East Mediterranean area. Managed multiple highly successful product developments & introductions in the microprocessor and data communications areas. At Intel I gained extensive hands-on experience in all phases of product life-cycle: product definition, development, marketing, manufacturing & cost reduction.

Served as Venture Partner in Israel Seed Partners, a $250M early stage Israeli Venture Capital fund. Participated in the fund’s semiconductor & communications deal flow, and in several investments in semiconductor startups, held Board positions worked closely with some of the fund’s portfolio companies.

I have extensive international exposure (USA, Europe, APAC and Israel).
