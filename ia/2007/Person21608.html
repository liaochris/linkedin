Dave Kendall’s Summary

I've put in 20+ years as a content specialist: writing, editing, producing, hosting and project-managing for TV, radio and Internet properties. But it really boils down to creating and packaging something compelling enough to make people keep watching, listening, reading or clicking.

A couple of years ago I moved to Chiang Mai, Thailand with an eye on early retirement. Hasn't quite worked out that way - aside from teaching and writing the odd documentary via telecommute, I am developing two online language schools (ESL and Thai), I give financial services advice, and I spend a lot of my time working for the highly effective HIV-related charity, www.rejoicethailand.org - check it out.

Dave Kendall’s Specialties:

Writing for print, Internet, radio and TV; producing, field-producing and story-editing TV documentaries, reality and entertainment shows; project-managing website maintenance and construction, and website information architecture; business development; business plan writing; online and offline teaching; offshore financial services.
