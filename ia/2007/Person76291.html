David Sandusky david@yourbrandplan.com’s Summary

David Sandusky built and operates Your Brand, LLC to help one build and manage their brand promise and achieve their greatest goals. David's direct and supportive leadership style has been captured in the company, its tools and events. David is fortunate to have passionate and focused partners contributing detailed attention to the Personal Board of Advisors product.

David held a variety of professional positions including Director of research, and Operations VP. He advises entrepreneurial ventures and start-up organizations.
