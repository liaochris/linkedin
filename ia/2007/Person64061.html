bojan maraz’s Summary

I am a designer and CEO of Krea, Italy, a creative design house with more than 20 years of design experience on international market.

Our main offices are in Italy/Gorizia, Bangkok /Thailand and Shanghai /China.

Our job is to offer to our clients integrated design solutions combining together design areas as, visual communication product design and corporate interior design.

I personally beleive in common sense design and simplicity use as a tool which can reach wider customer acceptance no matter what the product might be. Creativity in combination with clever ideas wraped in to appropriate technology and carefully targeted market position is alawys a good formula to make a winning product.

Not always we can as a design company touch issues which I consider as vital and they should be integrated in our working process.There are social issues besides design which are absolutelly to be considered and eccology risks to to be calculated.
