Michael Brucker’s Summary

My background includes over 10 years of online marketing experience across consumer, financial, and business-to-business products and services under some of the Internet's top brands.

In 2004, I joined WebEx Communications with the responsibility of defining and implementing online marketing strategies for WebEx's entry and growth in the SMB market.

Prior to joining WebEx, I served as the Manager of Affiliate Marketing at Yahoo where I launched Yahoo's first in-house affiliate programs and set up the framework for an affiliate network reaching across all Yahoo properties.
