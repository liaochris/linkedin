Fern Lebo’s Summary

Canada's leading communications consultant, Fern is internationally respected as a gifted author, compelling speaker and dynamic trainer. She decimates the rules and reinvents communication, delighting her audience with a whole new system for building business relationships — in person and on paper.

An internationally published author, Fern has written five books, an assortment of sales materials, technical documents, web copy, manuals, and scores of articles. She is a frequent keynote speaker, a guest lecturer at Stony Brook University, and as an adjunct faculty member of Auburn University, she teaches an accredited, online Business Writing workshop.

Professionally trained as a psychiatric Occupational Therapist, Fern brings the psychology of a positive response to the table. She is that rare individual in whom solid academic grounding and hands-on experience create the ideal blend. Her strength lies in her professionalism, enthusiasm and unfailing talent in helping her clients close more sales.

Her passion: presentations that win; proposals that connect; conversations that mean business.
