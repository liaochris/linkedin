Fiona Knight’s Summary

A brief introduction to Fiona Knight: I have operated a special events and project management firm in Toronto since 1993 and I have worked as a consultant to many clients in the not for profit and corporate sectors in Canada, the US and internationally to finance many diverse new projects: most recently I am working part time on a Masters in Policy and Public Admnistration at Ryerson University in Toronto. I have a passion for growing healthy young Canadians with the proper nutrition and clean air and water, having co- founded the Canadian Coalition for Student Nutrition in 1991 to provide a healthy school meal for all Canadian children and youth.

Fiona Knight’s Specialties:

Through five years and eight projects with the Ontario Public Health Association my knowledge and development of public and community health programming was honed through coordinating eight successful projects over five years: all of which included aspects of special events: a media launch, conference, workshops, focus groups, implementation, evaluation and budget management.
