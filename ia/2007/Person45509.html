Justin Maxwell’s Summary

I am a multidisciplinary ui designer/programmer whose interests and skills cover all types of media, spanning interactive prototypes to intranet/wikis to wireframes to creative programming. My expertise draws from years of experience shaping apple.com's user experience through programming and design, creating application prototypes for Apple's Pro Applications team, creating destination sites for major motion pictures, and much more.
