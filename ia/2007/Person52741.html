Luke Newton’s Summary

I have been developing websites since I was a sophomore in college and have developed over 30 websites since 2000. Upon graduation I migrated into an internet marketing position from an internship at an economic development consulting company where I learned how to manage marketing campaigns under the guidance of Jill Snyder, who was acting VP of Marketing. I joined Jill Snyder's marketing team shortly after she left to join ExactTarget where I continued to learn under her mentorship - implementing marketing campaigns, testing and analyzing them as well as performing many marketing technology tasks.

I left to join RCI after 2 years at ExactTarget to take a roll as Manager of E-commerce operations - leading the product development and marketing for myRCI, an RSS based application to help timeshsare owners get the most out of their membership.

I have since joined Troy Burk in helping him start up his own company - Right On Interactive where I serve as the Director of Professional Services. I work with multiple clients on making the most out of their marketing efforts and develop strategies to help them increae thier ROI. We are dedicated database marketers, focused entirely on left brained marketing.
