Ariel Poler’s Summary

Since 1994 I have started and ran three Internet companies (I/PRO, Topica & TextMarks) and I have helped (as an active board member) start and run several others (Kana Software, LinkExchange, Silicon Investor).

My current focus is TextMarks, where I am CEO and Co-Founder. I am also helping the founders of StumbleUpon, Papilia & Bills.com, to successfully grow their companies.

I make ocassional angel investments. Recent investments include RockYou, Flixster, AdMob, Kongregate, Brightroll and Voicestar.
