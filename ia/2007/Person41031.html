Jean-François Lintanff (Viadeo 3800 + ) TopLinked.com’s Summary

From French Army commissioned officer to logistics & transport business development manager,
From the French Ministry of Defence to the private sector...
From Light Armoured Reconnaissance Cavalry to aerospace, international transport and logistics... and administration of Viadeo Hubs!

In April 2006, I become Business development manager within the Geodis Corporate Sales and Marketing department (www.geodis.com) Previously, I had been holding a position as logistics project manager within the Engineering department. Meanwhile, I had started a Msc in Transport & Logistics at the Ecole Supérieure des Transports – Paris (www.ecole-est.com) I now consider joining the Dauphine Executive MBA for 2007-2009.

Previously. .

I spent seven years in the French army cavalry regiments, as platoon leader, then as staff officer responsible for training courses in varied fields. This experience helped me to develop my organisation skills and team spirit. In addition to my professional experience, I have attended a training course at the CEFELOG, (European Center of training of the logisticians – Toulouse Chamber of Commerce).

Then, I have been recruited by one of the trainers, Danzas civil aerospace department director. I could take part in the growth of the department into division, dedicated to civil aerospace industries in Europe.
I then took part in the development of Sernam as logistics project leader, in particular for the Euro logistics launch in Banque de France branches. In September 2002, I integrated the air operations division at Air France HO as Dangerous goods air transport expert until April 2005.
