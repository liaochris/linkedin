Jerry Luk’s Summary

I grew up in Hong Kong and graduated from Salesian English School. I was the Department Director of Hong Kong Joint School Electronics and Computer Society and I participated in organizing the first I.T. Festival Expo in 1999. Then I attended college in the USA.

My focus areas at UC Berkeley were Artificial Intelligence and Computer Graphics. During my junior and senior years, I did research and published a paper with Professor Ruzena Bajacy on wireless sensor networks. I also worked on footskate cleanup for motion synthesis in Professor James O'Brien's research group.

I am currently on deferral for my PhD. program from Cornell and working at LinkedIn. LinkedIn provides me opportunities to make use of my creativity to create exciting tools such as LinkedIn Companion and Outlook toolbar.

My strength is able to learn new things quickly. While I knew nothing about Javascript before working in LinkedIn, I picked up AJAX quickly and finished the LinkedIn Signature Creator Engine as well as the LinkedIn Firefox Extension in 2 months.

Other than a good learner, I also have great problem solving and management skills, as well as understandings of how to effectively communicate to different people. I believe social networking is important for success and that's why I am using LinkedIn everyday.
