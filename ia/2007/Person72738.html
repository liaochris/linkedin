Rikki Arundel’s Summary

I used to have a formal profile here because actually I was pretty formal and stuffy for 50 years as an insurance company sales and marketing executive and technology guru to the financial services industry. Then in 2001 I dramatically announced that I was changing gender. It's not the kind of thing that happens every day. I expected to be shunned - I was not - and so started a new and exciting journey in life.

As a trans women I see things differently and I discovered this is really important in a world where 80% of buying decisions are made by women. There is a huge global gendershift happening as women increasingly assume positions of power and start (shock - horror) doing things differently.

I took a couple of years sabbatical to do an MSc in Gender Research and reconnect with being a poor student, but that's nearly complete now and I am gearing up to do what it seems the Great Architect of the Universe has decided I am here to do in this life.

I help people in business to communicate more effectively and make more sales.

My challenges: I have a rich and growing international network, but I don't like the fact that I am not keeping in touch with everyone. One solution has been to develop a new Speaking and Marketing Tips newsletter. Another is to promote a Link Exchange programme between my web site and everyone in my network so that we can all grow together more effectively. Also as a professional speaker I need to provide greater permanence to my material so what I have learned and teach is being posted on my new information rich web site, full of easy to apply Small Business Marketing Strategies.(Plug)

Would like 25 great ideas on "How to Get Customers Queuing up to Buy" - Go to http://www.SpeakingandMarketingTips.com and claim your free copy of my new eBook. (What's the catch? You have to sign up for the newsletter to get it.)

Hey, come along and visit anyway and I look forward to seeing you there.
