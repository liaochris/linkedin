ABOUT GLUE

Since our inception in 1999 : Glue: has become the fastest growing UK professional services company supplying independent advisory consultancy and programme services to CIOs and senior IT executives. We focus on Integration, Architecture, Process and Data, and have a wide range of services to support IT & Business Management functions realise a range of 'enterprise integration', 'enterprise information visibility' and technology-enabled business objectives.

We focus on four key areas of IT management :

Enterprise Architecture Management
Business Process Management
Service Oriented Architecture Change Management
Integration Programmes

Some of our key services include:

Advisory Consultancy - around Integration & Architecture such as Technology Selection, Strategy, Methods, Tools, Frameworks, SOA (service orientated architecture), ESB (enterprise service bus), EAI (enterprise application integration), ETL (extract transact load) & BPM (business process management).

Project Delivery Assistance - such as planning, mobilisation, analysis, design, configuration, re-use, service orientation, use of patterns and other accelerators, leadership (teams or projects), reviews, specific technology expertise, etc.

Recruitment Services - resourcing of projects with skilled Integration and Architecture Specialists on a contract and permanent basis, at various levels of expertise. This includes specialists with skills such as IBM WebSphere, SAP NetWeaver/XI, Microsoft BizTalk, Tibco, SeeBeyond, Oracle Fusion, webMethods etc.
