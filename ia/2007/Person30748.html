Ryan Galloway’s Summary

I am skilled in design and marketing in addition to my extensive knowledge of technology. My degree from Seton Hill College is a B.A. in Corporate and Instructional Communications. I am highly trained in advanced marketing and public relations, and have put in motion effective marketing plans and organizational practices to reach my goals.

Currently employed with Educate-Me.Net, I am not only a part of the sales force, I also create, maintain and deploy their marketing materials; flyers, mailings, e-mail campaigns, signs, website information, online newsletter, trade show material/presence, as well as our internal training material.

As a volunteer I have assisted the Center for Complimentary Medicine at Saint Clare’s Hospital in Dover by helping in the development of presentations for Aromatherapy workshops and a Business-to-Business promotional flyer for the center.

In the past I have utilized these skills as a volunteer with the Our Lady of Fatima Antioch Ministries, and served as an adult advisor to teenagers (ages 16 to 18). The experience as an adult advisor kept me on my toes, and allowed me to use my social skills in conjunction with my creative thinking and design skills in ways that I did not predict. I often found myself in the role of listener, leader, and an organizer. While working to help develop young minds I was developing effective techniques to promote events and fundraisers.

I have been in the educational sales field for 5 years now creating and implementing many promotional campaigns and sales material. From my experience in this field I have come to the understanding that I am prepared for any situation, and use my determination to learn quickly and succeed.
