Andrew Wilson (awd@andrewwilsondesign.com)’s Summary

Competent creative professional whose background ranges across the spectrum of disciplines. This work is enhanced by a versatile and dynamic work portfolio, which includes every step of the creative process including sketching, wireframing, UI, styleguide, interface and visual design. Duties performed have include illustration, Web & Flash design, prototyping, branding, online marketing campaigns, manual design, game animation and print advertising.

Client work includes:
AOL, Deloitte & Touche, Disney, eFax, Fox Interactive, Intel, Microsoft, Nestea, Toshiba, Toyota, Zenith Insurance

Currently working as Art Director at Experience Prototype Lab, the R+D Design division of AOL.

Portfolio and CV at:
http://www.andrewwilsondesign.com
