  • Volunteer

    Emmaüs Youth Camp, Lescar-Pau

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    June 2004 — 2007 (3 years)

    Emmaüs-Pau is a working camp and a part of the Emmüs International movement founded by Abbé Pierre. Since 2004, I have frequently visited the working camp in Pau (south of France) to participate in the voluntary work that benefits the African farming organisation L'AIDMR (l'aide pour le développement rural). L'AIDMR works in West Africa to improve the ways of farming in the poor African région. In 2006, I was selected to participate in a three-week farming work camp in Burkina Faso and thereby I worked in the Burkinese villages in cooperation with the Africans of l'AIDMR as well as the local Burkinese people. I still work for Emmaüs now and then, mainly with collecting items for people in the countryside, and furthermore, I work as an interpreter at the meetings in the Emmaüs youth camp.
