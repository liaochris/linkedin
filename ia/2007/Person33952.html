Howard Halpern, MA (Psych), CPRW, Member OACCPP’s Summary

Profile updated Feb. 8, 2007

I'm not going to waste this valuable space writing about my experience. I know people with 50 years' experience, none of it having the slightest value. As for achievements, most providers have them; and within each industry, most achievements resemble each other. My focus is on how I have distinguished myself from my colleagues and competitors.

I distinguished myself as a:

• Resume writer, publishing significantly both inside and outside the field; creating the world's largest web collection by a certified writer; and inventing the Business-Card Resume.

• Marketer, letting people receive detailed information by calling A Real Live Person Twenty-Four Hours Seven Days®; successfully prosecuting applications for six trade-mark registrations — one in the US — without hiring a trade-mark agent.

• Web developer, being the first in North America to create websites on the fly and host them free of charge under a short URL; creating dynamic, graphic effects, yet allowing sites to be readable within four seconds on a slow modem.

• Therapeutic counsellor, saving clients hundreds of hours and thousands of dollars by being result-driven instead of process-oriented; building a network of distinguished professionals each of whom I have known at least eight years; and incorporating eastern wisdom gleaned from over 30 years of yoga practice.

I have established and maintained relationships with people from an extremely wide range of ethnocultural backgrounds. This diversity is reflected in my LinkedIn® network (click on "Connections" tab, above).
