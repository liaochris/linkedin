Raghu Raman’s Summary

Raghu Raman is the Manager, Business Analytics in Spark Networks Inc, a leading provider of social networking and online dating websites. Raghu Raman helps company take business decisions of marketing spend and business trends based on business intelligence.

Raghu Raman is proficient and comfortable on all aspects of business and financial analytics like measurement of ARPU,ASP,LTV,ROI and Churn rate.
