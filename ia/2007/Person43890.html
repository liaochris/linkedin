Julie O'Reilly’s Summary

Clients are seeking solutions...customized, targeted, measurable and scalable, to reaching an increasingly fragmented market. It is not accpeptable to interupt your target--they must be a part of the experience.

Niche-marketing, social networking, advergaming-targeting the audience with relevant messaging where they choose to be. 30 years ago an advertiser could reach 85% of the market through the 3 television networks. Today a media plan and message development must account for audiences fragmented media habits and interaction with it.

My career started in the SF Bay Area when traditional advertising solutions prevailed. In 1997 I joined Bernard Hodes and Career Mosaic was hot. Double-Click was providing reporting and metrics on advertising efforts that were unprecedented. I was hooked. In 1999, I became immersed in online solutions as media habits continued to change. Brick & mortar clients (b2b and EC) became online destinations...recruitment, banking, education, travel, etc.

The opportunity to move to the east coast arrived, as did an opportunity with Johnson & Johnson's new recruitment branding agency to develop an online recruitment marketing tool kit. This 6-month assignment was an awesome experience-fast, furious and successful.

Fast-forward to June of '05. I provided a go-to-market strategy for a social networking and game destination targeting 18-24 year-old males that received investor support/funding. What followed was 18 months of doing what was necessary, from presenting to advertisers, writing web site content, and developing player communications and promotions. The site successfully launched in September '06 and continues to enjoy a steady membership.

In November of 07 I met with TMP/Atlanta and soon joined the company. A pretty decent group of folks.

I live and breathe media, am keen at identifying target audience habits and planning interactive, integrated methods of engagement.
