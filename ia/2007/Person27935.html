Ernie Pitochelli’s Summary

I’m a professional recruiter specializing in the Financial Services Arena with a unique ability to uncover Top Talent to help companies increase revenues and drive growth. This ability stems from personal hands-on experience in the industry. With over 17 years of direct experience in the financial services marketplace & six plus years in financial services Executive Search, I am well qualified to be your talent aquisition partner.

In an effort to bring even more value to my client partners and top performing candidates, I have joined forces with NEBC (New England Brokerage Corp). NEBC is a boutique Investment Banking and M & A firm that has been in operation since 1984 and brings over 20 plus years of industry knowledge, experience, success and industry contacts to our group.

We are looking for true client “partners” that have difficult to fill needs and want exceptional talent to help them grow. We feel that we are well positioned to have a positive impact on a company’s revenue & bottom line. If you are a client, looking to partner, an executive interested in a growth opportunity, a broker dealer, RIA or company looking for divestiture or acquisition or interested in building your network, let us know.

“Business is built on Relationships”….I welcome linking up with others.
