Brett Johnson’s Summary

The role client facing strategist who can not only offer 'the big idea', and close the deal is extremely important to new Product Development. It takes a keen awareness of the state of technologies and consumer trends plus the ingenuity to connect the dots and offer something unique, exciting and compelling. What it also requires a strong understanding of the limitations of time and budget to accomplish deliverables.

In my most recent position as New Media Director at Zeal Media Group, I engaged clients, created online strategies, and delivered application outlines to the creative and development groups. Typically I would act as a touchstone between creative and development to ensure the product maintained the working integrity as initially intended.

As the Founder and Technology Advisor for two category leading podcasts, FormulaPod and PodClimber, I led the launch of several podcasting firsts, including interactive advertising placed adjacent to audio content, syndicated content via widgets, and group mobile activities. FormulaPod and PodClimber maintain an #1 ranking by continual advancement in utilization of social networking, blog and email promotions.

These podcasts (audio/video) continue to receive a higher CPM than any other I have heard reported. This is due to the strong presentation of statistical research in extremely vertical markets. During my term at PodClimber and FormulaPod I closed numerous advertising and strategic partnerships with companies such as Mountain Hardwear, Patagonia, Multinational Underwriters, Climbing Magazine and Grand Prix Tours.

My ability to see potential in the future is uncanny. Typically it is my enthusiasm for a product or campaign that starts the fire with employees and coworkers, but it is the strong understanding of what I am asking, how to ask, and my respect for the unique skills and personalities of individuals that makes my management and leadership skills shine.
