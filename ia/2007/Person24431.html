Don Dalrymple’s Summary

I help people integrate technology and create business solutions that scale. I work with people wanting to start a business or small business owners that want to grow their business. I am currently President and CEO of AscendWorks, a business coaching company for professionals who desire to win in business and grow themselves. I also do speaking presentations to groups on such topics as Working in the New Economy, Productivity, Technology Solutions to Grow Your Business, Sales - Winning Fans, Branding in the New Economy, Leadership and Understanding Your Strengths. My clients include sales and marketing professionals, entrepreneurs, real estate agents, financial professionals and small business owners.

I am also a partner and President of JPayne Mortgage, an innovative mortgage company focused on creating a remarkable experience through the lending process. The JPayne Mortgage business model introduces an entirely new approach to the mortgage industry based on systems that scale and create predictable results for customers.
