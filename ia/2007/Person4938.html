Anand Sanwal’s Summary

Finance and strategy specialist with significant expertise in corporate portfolio management (also referred to as enterprise portfolio management, product portfolio management, resource allocation, investment optimization).

I'm the holder of a corporate portfolio management patent for American Express' Investment Optimization discipline. American Express Investment Optimization efforts have been recognized by the CFO Executive Board, Beyond Budgeting Roundtable and Enterprise Portfolio Management Council and also has been recognized by Baseline Magazine with 2 prestigious Baseline ROI Awards as well as by InfoWorld.

I'm the author of an upcoming book entitled "Optimizing Corporate Portfolio Management" which is being released in May 2007 by Wiley Publishing. Am a frequent speaker on Corporate Portfolio Management speaking to organizations like the CFO Executive Board, Gartner, Beyond Budgeting Roundtable, Enterprise Portfolio Management Council and to many corporate management teams.
