Steve Gall’s Summary

Perspective

Just as large-scale software applications integration was the key driver in the previous phase of business innovation, I believe that better management and coordination of business processes that integrate systems development is where organizations are now finding the greatest cost savings and strategic advantage.

When it comes to business process engineering, what I like most about my profession is when the managers and business users I work with begin to realize the new insights they gain about how their business actually functions. They suddenly realize that they can have much more accurate conversations about how they do business both internally and with clients.
