Angel Gambino’s Summary

Angel is responsible for helping artists and labels to harness the power of Bebo’s base of 31 million users worldwide, enabling them to extend their reach and form one-to-one relationships with their audience. She also has responsibility for devising innovative partnerships between artists and brands and retailers.

Angel is Vice President, Commercial, Strategy & Digital Media for Viacom UK. She is responsible for Viacom's business strategy across the 11- channel portfolio of MTV, VH1, VH2, TMF, Nickelodeon and Paramount Comedy brands, initially with particular focus on expanding MTVN's position in digital media and activities in internet, IPTV, VOD, mobile, gaming and other on-demand and interactive areas.

Angel is responsible for managing the digital P&L and all non-advertising revenue streams including new ventures, affiliate relationships and other partnerships as well as collaborating with the Viacom Brand Solutions group on below-the-line commercial alliances. She and the UK business are responsible for the most significant P&L outside the US.

Prior to Viacom, Angel was at the BBC where she held the position of Controller, Business Development & Emerging Platforms reporting to the Executive Board. She was responsible for extending the BBC's four television and nine radio channels to mobile, broadband and on-demand. She drove multi-platform strategy for mobile and broadband as well as commissioning, licensing and launching of the BBC's first mobile, broadband and on-demand products and services.

Before the BBC, Angel was responsible for online multiplayer gaming, community and games on-demand for Gameplay, as Director of Broadband, having previously consulted to a number of Fortune 500 companies as they expanded into other markets and in the area of online transactions. For much of Angel's earlier career she served as a Director of Legal and Business Affairs for NGOS.
