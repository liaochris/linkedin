Dave Jenkins’s Summary

Orb Optronix is the Pacific Northwest leader in optical-electrical system design and integration. Orb Optronix offers multi-discipline design engineering services in the areas of Electrical, Software, Optical and Opto-Mechanical Engineering.

Orb Optronix also operates a LED characterization and test lab and has several products under development for the LED measurement and photonics industries.

Mr. Jenkins has over 20 years experience in optical sciences, systems engineering, product development and business development. Prior to his founding of Orb Optronix, Inc. in 2006, Mr. Jenkins was Vice President of Sales and Marketing at Radiant Imaging, Inc., where he created and managed market channels for new products, and generated approximately 600% revenue growth over 5 years. He was particularly successful at developing OEM and supplier relationships in Asia for display metrology equipment. Prior to his role as VP of Sales and Marketing, Mr. Jenkins worked as Sr. Staff Optical Engineer for Radiant Imaging, providing optical and illumination engineering consulting services to external customers ranging from the small start-ups to fortune 500 companies. He was responsible for inventing the first 16-bit imaging display metrology system that allowed the collection of 2-D distributions of colorimetry and photometry data at a rate of millions of data points in seconds. He was a forerunner in non-sequential ray-tracing simulation of non-imaging optical systems in his frequent use of global optimization algorithms for computer-aided design of illumination systems. Mr. Jenkins has been awarded 5 US patents with 2 pending and is the author of 7 technical publications including: "Mura Defects: Moving Beyond Display Industry Standards", Information Display, December 2003, and "Flat Panel Display Metrology Finds More than Meets the Eye: Camera-based systems for defect detection reduce production costs and improve quality," Photonics Spectra, August 2003.
