Paolo Zanenga’s Summary

Founding partner of TWG Consulting - Third Wave Group, a Management Consulting Company operating in the fields of Knowledge Strategy and Business Innovation. TWG is connected to a network of academics, companies engaged in advanced transformation projects, and developers in I&CT.

President of PDMA Southern Europe - a not for profit association, has been found on December 2nd, 2005, and is affiliated to PDMA-Product Development and Management Association, which is the premier global advocate for product development and management professionals. Its mission is to improve the effectiveness of individuals and organizations in product development and management. This is accomplished by providing resources for professional development, information, collaboration and promotion of new product development and management.

Previous Experience:

1990-1998 As the president of ABM-I, he promoted and supported the application of Activity Based Management in Italy and Europe, then developing original approaches to Business Process Management, experimented in leading companies and spreaded through articles, books, conferences and seminars.

1988-1992 Contributed research on Cost Management and Business Process Management by participating to the CMS program of CAM-I (a research consortium based in Arlington, Texas, participated by a large number of leading companies worldwide). Worked with the Product Management group on issues as Product Diversity and Target Cost.

1981-1991 Consulted to the manufacturing and service industry on technology innovation and related economic impact. Other projects in engineering, logistics, and maintenance.

1983-1987 Provided management for technology transfer projects, specially toward African Countries, evaluating feasibility and redesigning the business in different environments

1979-1981 Angst+Pfister (Zurich, Switzerland): product management for hi-tech products.
