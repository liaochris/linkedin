Massimiliano Turci’s Summary

I have been working in accountancy and management, Swiss and international. Since two years owner of an arts and crafts "gallery". Working on events' oganization (small and big ones).
Good skills in organization, projects development, find new ideas and solutions.
A big lesson I have learnd: nothing is impossible, we just have to find a solution for every problem. Clients and business partners are not asking for miracles, just solutions for their problems.

I think I am working in a wonderful place and that I have around me beauty. I believe that if more people would do the same, they would be happier. It is difficult to live, we have lot of stress and it is not easy to always have the job we would love to. In the end of the day, when you go home, you should find THE place where you can relax and be happy. I can not believe that this is possible when you have medicrioty around you. When you are home and you have some painting, glass work, or ceramics, that you feel wonderful: there because you FEEL.
There: feelings. This is what Art is: feelings. NEVER say to me: I understand nothing about art, because this means that YOU HAVE NO FEELINGS and we know this is not right. What ever you think looking at an art work, you are feeling something. Some people can express it just telling: "I do not love it" - or more hard: "this is s...". Well I have big news for you. When you are telling those things you are expressing your self, your feelings. You do not love? Ask your self: "Why". You see? It is not true you do not understand art. You really do, but just ignore it.
Some people try to give a cerebral explanation about art. Forget it. There is nothing to understand, unless your feelings.
Enjoy your life, let art enter it. You will never be sad about it. Because when you chose it, you do it with your art.
Never let a someone sell you an art work you do not feel good for you. Chose with YOUR heart.
