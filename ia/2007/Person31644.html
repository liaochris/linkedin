Gerard Corcoran’s Summary

Project Manager Billing for Meteor.
Meteor is the third mobile operator in Ireland and one of the fastest growing mobile operators in Europe with over 800K subscribers.
Completed, vendor selection, systems architecture, functional specification, billing team recruitment, design specification and project management.

Principal consultant in the Communications, Entertainment & Media industry sector. In this position, responsible for developing industry-specific service offerings and value propositions, with special emphasis on enterprise mobility, broadband and wireless operaters.

Over 25 years experience in the Information Technology, Communications and Media industries including nearly 20 years with EDS and A.T.Kearney. Travelled extensively and completed projects for leading operators in Europe, US and Asia Pacific. My professional experience has been characterized by advanced technology planning and integration projects which mirrored the development of the ICT industry. Subject matter expert in Business Support Systems (BSS) and Operating Support Systems (OSS) including CRM, ERP, Billing, Mediation and Provisioning Systems.

I joined EDS in 1985 and contributed to EDS rapid growth initially at international headquarters in London and subsequently in France, Southern Europe, Eastern Europe, Asia, EDS Management Consulting Group and A.T Kearney.

Prior to EDS, I was a product and project manager at Cap Gemini Sogeti, where I was instrumental the development of the worlds first large scale online system (Minitel) and home banking systems.

I have presented papers at communications industry forums, and have several articles and papers published in the trade press. I have served on industry committees including government committees on telecommunications deregulation in France and Ireland.
