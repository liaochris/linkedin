Melanie Stone Perry’s Summary

Facilities Management CADD Coordinator, specializing in CADD Standards creation and enforcement.
AutoCAD and related products selection, installation and support.
MEPFP drawing and data management.
Hardware selection, purchase, installation and maintenance (large-format scanner, plotters and copiers).
Work closely with Engineers, Skilled Maintenance Personnel, outside contractors, and Project Managers.
I also train our shop employees in the use of DWG viewing software and the basics of plotting; and support other departments/entities in the use of AutoCAD and LT (this includes the creation of many tutorials as well as telephone support).

Melanie Stone Perry’s Specialties:

Most of my expertise deals with the software I use; AutoCAD, Autodesk MAP 3d, Bentley Viewer, Autodesk Design Review, Autodesk FMDesktop Suite, Microsoft Access, Word, Excel, Powerpoint and Adobe Acrobat Writer. The Autodesk software includes the use of many customized routines (primarily LISP/LSP).

I have some rudimentary command of SQL Server Express, PhotoShop, Microsoft Publisher, Autodesk Building Systems (now AutoCAD MEP) and basic HTML.
