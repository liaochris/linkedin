Mike Fish’s Summary

By combining Board, executive management and investment skills to build business value, Mike has achieved success with companies of all sizes. Whether as chairman, vice-president, interim manager or consultant, his skills, international experience and network of contacts enable him to quickly add value in any situation.

Few executives can also claim private equity experience, but Mike has achieved success on both sides of the investment table. As director at Interregnum plc, a London quoted technology venture capital firm, he was responsible for several successful fund raisings and direct investments in portfolio companies. As chairman of grid software company Scali he helped secure a $7.5m investment led by Atlas Venture. And in an executive role he was one of the 6 man founding team at ECsoft, a $50m venture investment which subsequently floated on Nasdaq and London Stock Exchange.

He has in-depth European experience (has set up and run subsidiaries in UK, France and Germany, multilingual, dual nationality). He has built an excellent network of contacts throughout Europe and USA, including Fortune 500 decision makers, IT industry executives, resellers, talented managers and entrepreneurs, VCs and professional advisers.
