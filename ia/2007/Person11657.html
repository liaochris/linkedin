Bruno Lowagie’s Summary

I work for the ICT department of Ghent University. I used to work for the Student Administration Office as a software developer. At the same time I was active in the world of Free / Open Source Software. Because of the growing success of iText, a very popular Java-PDF library (used by Adobe, IBM, Google,...), I was offered a book contract in 2005. The book was released in december 2006, and as a result I am now working on a University R&D project for an American multinational.

Bruno Lowagie’s Specialties:

I'm a PDF guru. I'm specialized in document solutions (automating the production of documents with 10,000+ pages).
