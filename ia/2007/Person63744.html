    Google.com Engineering Team
    • Executing global recruitment for Google.com Engineering teams based in CA, NY, WA, Dublin, Zurich, and Sydney. Google.com Engineering team is also known as SRE.

    SRE is short for Site Reliability Engineering. Site reliability engineers (SREs) are both software engineers (SWEs) and systems administrators (SAs), responsible for Google's production services from end-to-end. These are the individuals who "own" Google services like Search, Gmail, Ads, as well as internal services like GFS, BigTable, Chubby, and DNS. SREs write software and interact with applications, service infrastructure, and platforms to ensure we have reliable and available services which scale to Google production size. They participate in software design, capacity planning, performance analysis, and incident response focused on automation and software engineering to prevent problem recurrence.
