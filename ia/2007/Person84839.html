Terence Boyle’s Summary

I have been working in the software industry for over 20 years and have extensive experience with all aspects of the product development lifecycle.

I have had the pleasure of working with a lot of talented individuals on a variety of product types including: games, education, children's, business and enterprise systems, information, community, entertainment, international, and mobile products.

I am currently focused on product development systems, organizational culture and structuring, change management, and UX processes.

Terence Boyle’s Specialties:
