C. Michael Reyher’s Summary

A rich business background that includes a strong track record of team building and building business value. Prior to joining DataStarUSA Mike co-founded and served as Vice President of Product Development for Stick Networks in Dallas pioneering next generation wireless products and experiences.

Before co-founding Stick Networks, Mike was the managing partner for Consulting Services at WorkFlow Designs where he lead the company's transition to eBusiness consulting in 1995 and later to successful acquisition in 1999.

Mike's background also includes eight years with CompUSA where he held several senior management positions including Vice President of Product and Technology, and General Manager of CompUSA's private label hardware division.

Mike has been involved with implementing, and marketing collaborative technologies for major companies for over 10 years.

Mike studied Information Systems at Kansas State University. He loves eBusiness almost as much as mountain biking (and has the scars from both to prove it!)
