Jamal Panhwar’s Summary

Started in 1989 as a Tour Guide in Karachi and sticked to the same Industry since then. Joined Sitara Travel Consultants (Pvt.) Ltd and worked there for 9 years.

After working in Sitara felt a bit monotoneous work and thought of opening my own company Travel & Culture Services with help & Guidance of Mr. Shiraz Poonja MD Sitara.

The Company worked well till 2001 but after 9/11 thr tourism in Pakistan (Already very low) went into a dead slow mode and there was literally no business.

Contacted friend and mate in US Mr. Rehan Ahmed for a position and started working for him as his remote office in Pakistan which turned out to be batter then average and we started growing form one person to over a 100 people call center.

2003 Left Super technologies due to having health problems due to working in the nights and not getting enough rest in the day time, and started back to handle the company Travel & Culture which is still my job and my bread & butter but tourism situation is not as good as it should be.
