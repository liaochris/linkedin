Brian Glicklich’s Summary

Premiere Radio Networks, Inc., a subsidiary of Clear Channel Communications (NYSE:CCU), syndicates 70 radio programs and services to more than 5,000 radio affiliations and reaches over 190 million listeners weekly. Premiere Radio is the number one radio network in the country and features the following personalities: Rush Limbaugh, Jim Rome, Casey Kasem, Ryan Seacrest, Glenn Beck, Bob (Kevoian) & Tom (Griswold), Delilah, Steve Harvey, Whoopi Goldberg, Blair Garner, George Noory, Maria Bartiromo, Ty Pennington, John Boy and Billy, Matt Drudge, Art Bell, Donald Trump, Big Tigger, and others.

The Digital Media Division translates the equities of these radio programs to the Internet, to satellite, to cel phones, and to other distribution platforms, employing subscription, sponsorship/entitlement and advertising models to encourage millions of listeners to stay in touch with their favorite programming 24/7.

We are entrepreneurially driven, goal focused, and take pride in creating a work environment that sets aside much of the politics and personalities that often dominate large companies. We are lightly sprung, fast moving, and successful in meeting or exceeding the financial metrics of the core businesses of our organization.

Brian Glicklich’s Specialties:

Development and execution of subscription online environments for the entertainment and broadcasting industries, and the celebrities they showcase.

Deal development/negotiation for digital rights attached to broadcast properties.

General P&L responsibility for all digital operations in an eight figure business unit.

Development and integration of editorial, technical and customer support teams in a variety of digital spaces.
