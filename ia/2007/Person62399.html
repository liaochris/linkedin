Narasimha Bhat’s Summary

I'm trying to do two things --
(a) set up an offshore development center for interested clients: I built a remote R&D site at Manipal for Synopsys from ground up over the last 6 years, and I'm trying to repeat my own performance, but as an independent contractor. I worked for 9+ years at Synopsys, starting as an engineer and ending as Director R&D, so I'm used to the US ways of doing things. At the same time I understand very well the strengths and challenges of the Indian engineers. My goal is provide an avenue for the rich engineering talent here to work on state of the art technology. So I'm hoping to sell myself as a reliable and experienced "point of contact" and a "one stop shop" to US clients interested in doing remote engineering work in India. I've just recently finished all the government paperwork, and now have the legal framework in place. I'm now scouting for interested clients.
(b) be an incubation center for young entrepreneurs: undergrads and grads in EE and CS in the nearby engineering colleges (NITK, MAHE) are bubbling with ideas and enthusiasm, and I'm trying to help shape these into concrete products/ideas that could be patented and/or published into technical papers, and help them start out on their own by giving them all the necessary guidance and whatever support necessary. This may or may not end up being profitable, but my main intention is to try and fill the void -- there's lot of great ideas and great engineers: all they need is a bit of guidance and encouragement.
