Yuri Ivanov’s Summary

Research in High Level computer vision, in particular - motion based techniques. My primary expertise lies in the area of vision, action recognition and on-line learning methods, with occasional ventures into audio. I enjoy theory, but keep my hands dirty trying to connect it to real problems. I try to build things that tick on their own, learning and adapting to the environment as time goes by. Most of all I like working on new problems that haven't quite made it to the mainstream yet.
