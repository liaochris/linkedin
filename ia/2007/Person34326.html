Chris Hartwell’s Summary

I started Independent Layout & Website Design (http://inlayout.net) in 2002 while in graduate school at Tulane. Since my company's inception, I have completed many web site design jobs and search engine optimization jobs for small businesses and non profit organizations. I have given numerous small, local businesses a worldwide presence in the seach engines.

My work experience involves sales, customer relations, web site design and concept development, web site marketing in print, radio, and television, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, pay per click campaign management, project completion and website maintenance, as well as ongoing customer relations.

In 2003, after having great success early on with SEO, I started "The Family & Marriage Counseling Directory" as a project of my own. The directory quickly became one of the top ranked sites in Yahoo! and Google for popular search phrases like "marriage counseling," and now has over 300 advertisers. Since then I have started numerous smaller niche sites that are now the focus of Independent Layout & Website Design, and I manage the projects in my free time.

In 2006, I was recruited by Compucast Web, Inc, as Director of Operations and Creative Director, where we manage websites, search engine and internet marketing for more than 80 local hotels and restaurants. We manage local travel and hospitality portals for the City of New Orleans that are in the top of the "natural" search results. We also manage SEM pay-per-click campaigns for those portals, as well as individual campaigns for our many clients in the hospitality industry. We are a full service web design company, and as Director of Operations, I manage projects from start to finish.

My goal is to advance my knowledge and skills in search engine marketing by working in different business sectors and continuing my education by staying up to date with current marketing reasearch. I am currently open to new employment opportunities.
