Sandie Cheng’s Summary

Global industrial designer with experience in working in the States, Europe and Asia. I enjoy the entire design process, from research to conceptualization to the final mass production stage. Currently I have relocated to Barcelona, Spain and am looking forward to new and exciting design challenges here.
