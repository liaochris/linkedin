Alan Johnson’s Summary

My most recent occupation has been running a Web hosting business under the name Solutions by DATDEC which is something I first started doing professionally while in college. Before getting back into that business, I founded a fixed-wireless Internet Service Provider, FINOWEN, which I grew to about 250 broadband customers before moving on. Right out of college, I started doing software development on a consulting basis, first as an employee of a consulting company, and then as an independent while I transitioned into the wireless data communications business that evolved into FINOWEN.

In am interested in just about anything geeky, but I am really driven by technologies that improve quality of life, especially any technology that enables people to improve their own quality of life. This is why I started FINOWEN: once you have practically instant access to the virtually limitless information that is the Internet, your quality of life is limited only by your desire to change it.

The following is a list of skills and technologies for which I have at least some experience and interest.

BUSINESS MANAGEMENT: Human capital management; Financial modeling; QuickBooks; Billing practices and automation; Customer support and feedback management; Bootstrapping; Financing / fund raising; Sales and marketing

SYSTEM AND SOFTWARE ENGINEERING: eXtreme Programming; Object-oriented software development; Component based and functional design; Data structures and associated algorithms; Classifier systems; MVC; J2SE; RMI; JDBC; JavaMail; SQL; Shell scripting

WWW AND INTERNET: HTML; JSP; Servlets; EJB; CGI; SSI; JavaScript

SYSTEMS AND SERVERS: Internet networks; Ethernet LANs; PC hardware; Red Hat Linux; MySQL; Microsoft Windows 98, NT4, 2000, and XP; Apache Tomcat and HTTP Server

WIRELESS NETWORKING: Wireless LANs and WANs; IEEE 802.11 standards; General knowledge of hundreds of products, technologies, and standards
