Matt Peskett’s Summary

I'm an online marketing and advertising consultant who has been employed mainly in the publishing sector, as well as owning my own emarketing consultancy (Firetop). I have expertise in online campaign planning & analysis, web development, design & usability planning and server admin / maintenance.

I currently head up Online Advertising Operations at nature.com; managing a global team comprising of ad traffickers, design, project and workflow support staff. In addition to managing the Operations team I am involved in online ad strategy, vendor selection and campaign / site metrics. Previous roles at NPG since 2000 have seen me employed as Online Marketing Executive, Web Development Co-ordinator and Marketing Technology Manager.

I ran Firetop Ltd full time for 18 months from 2004; a crash course in business management, accounting, VAT, employment law and business development. Service offerings focused mainly on SEM but inevitably I got involved in design, branding, web builds and other emarketing solutions - developing a base of ten freelancers with whom to manage projects. More well known clients included Greenpeace, Nature, Wiley, IEE, Centaur and Dow Jones.

In early 2001 I spent a short time at William Reed publishing, managing DM and online campaigns. Between 1998 and 2000 I worked for the Spearhead Training Group as Marketing Manager - DM campaign planning and implementation / lots of negotiation with the business trade press. It was here I developed my first website and an understanding of emarketing.

I started my career as a trainee manager at Barratts shoes - this involved lots of travelling, store merchandising, and occasional new store launches in the UK and Northern Ireland. I liked it so much I never went to Uni, instead choosing to study a part-time HNC in business and marketing whilst moving to a store management position with a stationery chain. I also spent two years working in a call centre until my studies were completed.
