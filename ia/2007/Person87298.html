Tracey Herrick’s Summary

My personal motto can be summed up in the Zig Ziglar quote: "You can get everything you want in life if you help enough other people get what they want."

Since my beginnings in retail where I learned to love the hiring and recruiting process and the thrill of finding the right person for my team I have dedicated myself to the staffing industry. I spent 4 years at one of the top executive search firms in the midwest and then moved to helping companies create an employment brand via their employment advertising. I understand the process of hiring top talent and I work with my clients to enhance their recruitment strategies to bring the best of the best to their organizations.

My goal is to take the friction out of the hiring process so companies can spend more time developing talent and less time looking!
