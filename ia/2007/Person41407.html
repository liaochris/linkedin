Jim Jonassen’s Summary

Jim Jonassen & Associates Venture Search, based in Santa Monica and Palo Alto provides search and recruitment services for venture-backed tech. & Internet companies, specializing in executing CEO and CFO searches and building world-class Sales and Business Development teams. Jim has led >500 searches while also having founded and built two successful software companies.

HEADHUNTER:
- Jim co-founded and led Riviera Partners (www.rivierapartners.com) a Palo Alto based search firm from ‘01 through ‘06
- ‘91 to ‘97, founded and led Larkin Associates - a new media search firm with offices in Santa Monica & San Francisco
- A founding Talent Scout for AOL’s Greenhouse incubator and VC fund
- Jim started his search career in ‘83 doing technical and sales recruiting

SOFTWARE ENTREPRENEUR:
- From ‘86 to ‘93 he was Founder & CEO of Micro J Systems (www.microj.com), a developer of software for the staffing industry. Micro J has >2,000 firms as customers and Jim continues as Chairman
- Co-founded and was CEO of PeopleMover (www.peoplemover.com ), a leading VC-backed software company delivering Prof. Services Automation to enterprises including CSC, Robert Half Intl, Modis and BEA. PeopleMover merged with Opus360 and went public in April 2000
- Named the ‘99 Software Entrepreneur of the Year by the Software Council of So. Calif. and named three times to the "Top 25 People in High Technology" by the L.A. Business Journal;
- Co-founded the Digital Coast Roundtable (www.digitalcoast.org) along with L.A. mayor Dick Riordan and launched LAwNMoweR - the Los Angeles New Media Roundtable (www.lawnmoweronline.com)

EDUCATION:
- ‘81 grad of University of Illinois at Champaign/Urbana; also attended the Universitat d' Barcelona
- Jim is a guest lecturer for the Entrepreneur Program at the Marshall School of Business at USC and UCLA’s InfoTech program
- A Big Brother since ‘92 and member of the Board of the Santa Monica Boys & Girls Club
