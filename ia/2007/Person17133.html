Christopher Campbell’s Summary

• Why am I on LinkedIn? Because I’m looking for the venue where I can continue to make a difference as I bring a 26-year military career to a close.

• Principal strengths: I can learn anything and teach it to others. I excel at multi-tasking, innovating, matching resources with needs, and mentoring new officers and academics.

• What I’ve been: I am the personification of the “soldier-scholar.” On paper, what I look like depends on who’s looking. If you’re an operator, you’ll see a fellow operator with experience in the air, on the ground, and in theater with two different sister service headquarters, but with more education & degrees than you’re used to seeing. If you’re an academic, you’ll see a PhD’ed, published professor with 8 yrs teaching in and administering a college English department, but with some pretty odd, high-adrenaline hobbies and “sabbaticals.”

• Who I am: A lot more Indiana Jones than Harold Bloom. What I do next will depend on what adventure presents itself. I don’t have to leave the Air Force for two more years, but under current Force Shaping Initiatives, I could leave in as little as two weeks.

• SPECIALTIES, MILITARY: Familiar with all aspects of Air Mobility Contingency Operations, Drop Zone Survey & Control, Airfield Assessment, GDSS, JOPES, JA/ATT & Global Reach Laydown. Multi-service-lingual; Air Force Senior Navigator/Instructor/Evaluator with 2000+ flying hours; 2 yrs on Marine HQ staff & Gulf deployment; 2 yrs on Army HQ staff & Afghanistan deployment. Speaks AF Blue, Hooah, Oohrah, and can translate. Static Line/HALO qualified; 800+ jumps, Accelerated Freefall Instructor, USPA PRO-rated.

• SPECIALTIES, ACADEMIC: Extensive experience in academic administration, personnel, course design, faculty development & assessment, conflict resolution. Teaching experience in classroom, boardroom, cockpit, forward operating location and freefall.
