Craig Dalton’s Summary

Mr. Dalton has 14 years’ experience in sales and marketing management as well as extensive experience building profitable business strategies. As I-play’s Vice President of Business Development, his responsibilities include formulating market strategies, developing and managing strategic relationships with partners and clients, and charting the company’s course into the mobile video space. Recently Mr. Dalton has been focused on the development of I-play’s newest entertainment offerings: Golfspan; the leading interactive instructional golf video magazine for mobile and Hollywood Movie Minutes; the first interactive mobile application with access to Universal Studios’ vast film library. The scalable Java and BREW technology that underlies these applications is the cornerstone for future offerings from the company’s content partners. Prior to joining I-play, Mr. Dalton managed business development for interactive television and mobile pioneer, Proteus, having completed the first TV to SMS campaign in the US with Super Bowl XXXVI in 2002. As an early member of the ATV Forum and mentor in the AFI enhanced television workshop, Mr. Dalton has a passion for interactive television and is excited to be part of the merger of mobile and video. Mr. Dalton holds an MBA from the Graduate School of Management at the University of California, Irvine and a BSBA in Business Administration from The American University.
