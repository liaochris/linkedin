    Development of data transformation solutions in SQL Server DTS and Sagent ETL between over fifteen disparate database systems and multiple data marts using OLAP modeling.

    Responsibilities included a discovery process to help determine hardware and software platform, installation and configuration of all components, and managed support and maintenance of the hardware, operating systems, and software. Our customers ranged from small businesses to Global 2000 corporations, with notable ones being Verizon Wireless, Hewlett Packard, PeoplePC, and Columbia Capital.
