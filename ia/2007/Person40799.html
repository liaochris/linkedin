Jesse Lau’s Summary

More than 18 years of working experience in brand marketing management of consumer goods (electronic, alcoholic beverage and tobacco industry) and in advertising agency. I have also experienced in tobacco industry whereby rapid changed from a full fledge and conventional ATL advertising/marketing to ‘through the line’ advertising in an ever challenging dark market whereby total ATL is banned.

Highly capable in initiating projects, developed and implemented marketing strategies and ideas/concept, and provide brand directions -strategic plan development to build brand equity, awareness, lifestyle affinity, trial and conversion for brand focus in the intense competitive and restricted market environment.

Inspired Brand Managers and Account Managers (agency) to develop and implement marketing programs, including packaging and merchandising as well as given support to Trade Marketing/Sales distribution over the course of these years. Overall, had made positive changes within department and led subordinates to achieve personal and company objectives.

Personally, I am pleased to have contributed to the success of the respective brand achievements in each organization as mentioned above and led many key projects including CRM, Packaging and Market/Consumer Insight Research with satisfactory. I am glad to have changed the strategic approach in Brand Plans for the respective brand including encouraging subordinates and external parties ( other agencies & suppliers) to deliver effective and detail programs for further implementation. Constantly thinking other than the conventional ways of deliverables through "outside of the box" in modern marketing.

Career Objective: To apply diversified marketing, brand management skills, advertising and techniques acquired through past learnings and working experience to contribute values towards the organization’s growth. Further, acquiring knowledge and experience to allow for personal development.

Jesse Lau’s Specialties:

Expertise not only encompassed in brand marketing, business management, advertising & promotion (including BTL/Activation), event road shows, trade marketing, R&D and packaging, CRM (1-2-1), sales forecast but organizing, staffing and coaching/training.
