Mike Jackson - LION’s Summary

An experienced manager specialising in Programme and Project management at an international level, Michael has over twenty-five years of experience in IT, the last fifteen of which have been in various management roles.

He has managed all areas of the Project Life Cycle, with a particular bias to the more challenging later phases of the life cycle, when budgets and schedules are being threatened.

Mike has excellent communication skills coupled with a strong pro-active style and a proven history of delivering under pressure.

He is always on the look out for new leadership and management opportunities and is keen to raise the level of the quality of management within the IT industry.

"Planning Prevents Poor Performance", "Let's get it right the first time" and "Perform Better!" are all favourites.
