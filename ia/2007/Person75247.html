Ryan Boyle’s Summary

Working as a freelance storyboard and concept artist has allowed me to explore and improve upon my creative and artistic skills. It also gave me the chance to improve my communications and self-management skills. I started out doing storyboards for indie film makers and have worked my way up to doing storyboards for professionals.

As an Instructor for the compositing class at Full Sail Real World Education I have gained a wealth of knowledge of Shake and compositing techniques. I have exceeded my standard duties by hosting extra educational workshops and presentations to train our staff. While working as a Lab Specialist a brief time with the Demo Reel Creation course I learned a range of tools involving editing, composition, design, and motion graphics.

I have been trained and certified in Apple’s Shake, Final Cut Pro, and DVD Studio Pro while working for Apple Computers as a Creative Genius. As a Creative Genius for Apple retail stores I have trained professionals and hobbyist in the Final Cut Studio Package, web design, graphics, operating system, and other misc software concerns.
