Nik Palmer’s Summary

I have been living online since before there was a web. I started out in 1984 wardialing for BBS sites and ended up moving to Silicon Valley as the Tech companies were Bubbling.

I went to work for an amazing company called WESTECH CAREER EXPO on their new online job site: Virtual Job Fair. Westech was great and amazing and I learned all about the tech industry, hiring, and the unsavory aspects of mergers.

I worked in sales and product management, eventually managing several remote sales groups.

I road that wave, not knowing a Stock Options from Livestock and bolted to New Hampshire before the great .COMpromise.

During the bust, I found that I had a talent for being a corporate axe and shut down 4 offices of the company, consolodating and bringing their business opportunities back to the corporate fold. I always knew that one day the axe would turn on me.

When the axe finally fell, it was good timing. I had moved to rural New Hampshire near Dartmouth College and focused on family and personal enrichment.

I went to work for AIRS (Another Amazing Company) as a regional Sales Director working on Recruitment training and tools, eventually moving from the Sales group to our specialty services group.

Now I monitor, and QA our premier product line AIRS SOURCE, known commercially as AIRS SPO. I manage several distinct back end systems, observe trends, and make the system run better. I also manage our most prominent outsourced services.

I don't Code, but I speak tech and nerd.
