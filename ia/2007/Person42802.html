John Currie’s Summary

ITERATE creates value for technology ventures, owners, and their investors by developing strategic partnerships and taking their technologies into new markets. Principal has a proven track record of getting early stage technology companies through the "Capital Gap". Entrepreneurs need key partners as their early customers and early investors - difficult deals to get done. Large co.'s have under-utilized assets that could be monetized.

Working in early stage ventures since 1994, the principal has co-founded five technology ventures and closed over 20 strategic partnerships. While at Sarnoff Corp., the principal created over $250 million in value from $45M in seed funding raised.

Select clients and current engagements include:

o Sarnoff Corp. - the venerable R&D firm has many assets that enables "better, faster, cheaper" products in many segments. (www.sarnoff.com)

o Lap Belt Cinch - innovative automotive consumer product which securely fastens child seats into autos and makes driving safer. (www.cg-lock.com)

o SevOne - unique performance monitoring software for I.T. networks. Helped place CEO and assisting in marketing plans. (www.sevone.com)

o Predictive - innovative health web service which offers an early warning system for infections. Input 2 vital signs and Predictive will tell you the probablility of getting an infection (up to 2 days).

o SafeStart Systems - Patented electric safety shut-off circuit that solves a major hazard that injjures thousands of people per year.

o Molecular Heat Eater - Innovative process from Sweden to make "green" organic flame retardent. Entering the US to take advantage of new CA laws banning polyurethane foam in bedding.

o ClassicWines.com - creating the ultimate web experience for wine afficianados and building the 'Amazon marektplace' site for wine. (www.classicwines.com)
