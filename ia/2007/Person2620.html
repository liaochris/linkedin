Alan Knitowski’s Summary

Have founded, built or monetized 5 private companies in the past 8 years. 3 were acquired by public companies and 2 currently trade publicly themselves. Have also assisted structuring, financing and building 5 other private companies and co-manage 2 funds.

Vovida Networks, a communications networking software company, was sold to Cisco Systems (NASDAQ: CSCO) in November 2000 (Co-Founder, President & CEO).

vCIS, an anti-virus and security software company, was sold to Internet Security Systems (NASDAQ: ISSX) in October 2002, and subsequently acquired by IBM (NYSE: IBM) in August 2006 (Investor & Director).

Telverse Communications, a wholesale communications ASP, was sold to Level 3 Communications (NASDAQ: LVLT) in July 2003 (Co-Founder, Investor & Director).

Caneum (OTC BB: CANM), a business process and IT outsourcing company, began trading publicly in July 2004 (Investor & Chairman).

Trycera Financial (OTC BB: TRYF), a prepaid debit card company, began trading publicly in August 2006 (Investor & Chairman).

Trymetris Capital Management, the "Manager" for Trymetris Capital Fund I, provides a diversified investment vehicle for accredited investors interested in "public venture capital".

Curo Capital, the "Manager" for Curo Capital Appreciation Fund I, provides a diversified investment vehicle for accredited investors interested in highly liquid small cap, mid cap and large cap value and growth opportunities both domestically and abroad.

Ecewa Capital Group, a holding company, acts as a corporate advisor and investment vehicle for companies large and small.

Edgewater Networks, a private communications networking equipment company, closed a Series C with Palomar Ventures and El Dorado Ventures in August 2005 (Investor & Advisor).

Vootage.com, a private high school sports webcasting and community network, is ramping its business (Director & Investor).

Alan Knitowski’s Specialties:

Business creation (idea conception / structuring / financing / monetizing), hypergrowth management, merger and acquisition management (structuring / integrating), investment banking (fund raising / IPO), investment fund management, corporate governance.
