Matthew Park’s Summary

I am an equity research analyst whose strengths include an extensive experience in following financial services companies and capital markets, a critical understanding of the political and regulatory impact on the financial services industry and a global perspective on finance and economics.

Originally trained as an academic economist, I began my Wall Street career at Sanford C. Bernstein in 1993 and have covered a variety of financial companies. Most recently at Prudential Equity Group, my research universe included a) card payment, b) education finance, c) housing GSE, d) mortgage finance / insurance and e) specialty finance.
