Ahmad Goheer’s Summary

[ From ML Fact Sheet ]

Career Highlights

Ahmad has more than 12 years of very diverse experience that concentrates around Corporate Finance and Private Equity space. In the past, he has not only managed investment portfolios on behalf of UHNW individuals/family offices but has also worked with various institutions. He is experienced at handling cross-border investments in advisory role as well as possesses direct senior-level mgmt experience. In the recent past, apart from spearheading the foreign expansion of country's largest investment bank in the Middle East, he also built an advisory unit there. Prior to which, he was a Private Equity fund manager based out of London

Academic Background

Decorated with several accolades, he has a very rich academic background. He is currently a PhD candidate in Corp Finance from the LBS. He also holds an EMBA in Financial Mgmt from London, UK and a Masters in IT from Sydney, Australia. Earlier than this he had graduated with honors in Computer Science. On top of various scholarships that he has received throughout academia, he also holds a graduation Gold Medal and an all time record in the history of universities in the New South Wales region (highest total no of Dist & High Dist)

At Merrill Lynch

Leading the corporate finance advisory practice for Middle East out of firm's Dubai office, he not only specializes in various capital market transactions but is regularly involved in financial planning, modeling, corp advisory and project valuations. He is a strong advocate of taking bold risks; creating and then seizing opportunities by focused and targeted investments in various global markets. He has a unique combination of understanding and experience from a regional investment perspective of the Middle East and is considered an authority on identifying emerging (global) opportunities and understanding the ever changing market dynamics. He frequents as an industry expert at several well respected int’l forums

Ahmad Goheer’s Specialties:

Corporate Finance - Private Equity - Investment Banking - Financial Advisory Srvices - Organizational Transformation (Corporate Restructuring & Change Management)
