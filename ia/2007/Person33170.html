Gregor Rohrig’s Summary

I am a New Media graduate form Rhodes University (South Africa) having specialized in multimedia story telling, web development, content management system administration, photography and flash production.

I am currently the co-founder, developer and managing editor of a newly established website called Jo's Toolkit (http://jostoolkit.ru.ac.za) which has recently won the annual Highway Africa Innovative use of New Media Award. The site aims to educate, train, and empower student and grassroot journalists.

My plans are to work in the online media industry, if possible multimedia innovation, publishing and producing. I am also highly interested in developmental journalism and media development regarding CC licensing.
