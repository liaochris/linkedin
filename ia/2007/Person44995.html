Judith Currin’s Summary

In the 1970's anything seemed possible and I started a glamourous career as a bricklayer's apprentice in 1976. For the next eight years I laid bricks in the summer in Virginia and I spent my winters in south Florida bartending. I hurt my back in 1984 and eventually moved to NYC in 1988 and began my NY career at Ogilvy & Mather. My work history in NY includes Public Relations positions at both O&M and A&E Television Networks, TV Production experience at A&E as a Programming Coordinator, extensive Interactive Advertising Awards show experience and expertise as Awards Coordinator for OgilvyOne worldwide, and Interactive Project Management experience at OgilvyInteractive, DIGITAS, R/GA and FCBi (now Draftfcb). I am also a Licensed Massage Therapist in NY and a former Stand-Up Comic with Comedy Central, A&E, and Starz Comedy Channel credits.

I'm a person who solves problems and gets things done - a 'finisher'!

I'd like to be part of a team which shares common goals and a vision for the future. In advertising the work is predictable, but the people you work with determine whether you have a life you can live with every day. I like to have fun while I get people to do things they don't necessarily want to do. I get straight to the heart of the matter, and I fight against the evil empire on the side of creative. I make people laugh - it's what I like about life.
