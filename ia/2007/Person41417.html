Jim Kukral’s Summary

I'm an award-winning blogger and online marketing veteran. Visit www.jimkukral.com for a list of all of my current projects and learn more about my consulting services.

I specialize in helping businesses make money online through the use of creative online marketing ideas. Give me a call, let's see if I can turn your web project around into something that actually profits.

I am the former Publisher for www.ReveNews.com, an online "blog" focusing on the billion dollar industry of online revenue, and I'm founder of a blog monetization network called BlogKits.

I've worked as a writer for ClickZ.com and Builder.com. I've also been published in Revenue Magazine and on MarketingProfs.com.

What most people don't realize is that I've been blogging since 2001, giving me that ultra-cool early adopter status. Not really, it just means that I'm an Internet geek who likes to write so naturally I was hooked on blogging from the first day I started.

It wasn't the technology that hooked me, or the thrill of instant and ongoing conversations either. It was the act of instant publishing that got my attention. I can write html by hand, and I was a web designer, so I could easily put up a website on my own. I've built thousands of websites. But building a static website, however, was not as satisfying as having a voice.

Blogging to me was/is a way to be heard. A way to instantly express yourself. That was/is what made it so powerful to me. Before blogging, there was no real way to get your voice heard. Back then, editors, both online and offline, stood as gatekeepers deciding who reads what and how, while the would-be writers of the world sat rejected with the fact that they would probably never be read because they didn't have any professional experience.

Blogging changed that, and look at us now, we have a hit on our hands. Blogging has turned the Internet into a living, breathing entity like nothing has before. We are becoming the true pulse of it all.
