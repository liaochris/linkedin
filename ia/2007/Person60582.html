Matthew Mascord’s Summary

I work within the Oxford University Computing Services where I manage the JISC funded e-Infrastructure Use Cases and Service Usage Models (eIUS) project.

Between July 2005 to March 2007 I was the project manager of the Integrative Biology VRE project and co-investigator on the EPSRC/AHRC-funded Virtual Workspace for the Study of Ancient Documents' project led by the Humanities Division at Oxford University.

I have over eight years experience working in the IT industry, in a variety of sectors and roles.

Matthew Mascord’s Specialties:

e-Infrastructure, e-Research, Virtual Research Environments, Requirements Analysis, Business Analysis, Enterprise Systems.
