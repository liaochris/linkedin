Jim Scripps’s Summary

I started as a writer, covering the business beat for a community newspaper, and writing columns and auto reviews. As a newspaper editor, I worked with other writers, photographers and artists, developing daily news budgets, and creating new products, including printed (magazines and specialty publications) as well as Web (podcasting, video, blogging, etc.). As senior editor for a 24-hour news Web site, I work on the site's content, physically designing pages, selecting and adjusting photography and working with other editors and programmers to develop the site.
My background, training and passion is writing (and I continue freelance writing for magazines), but I have become interested in all aspects of Web 2.0 content management, from journalism, to marketing and social media.

Jim Scripps’s Specialties:

I have a sound background in production software: Adobe p-shop and InDesign, Quark, Baseview, etc. I am also fluent in a number of Web content management systems, blogging tools, Google tools, MySpace, etc.
I have often been called on to represent my organization at public meetings, speaking to service groups, hosting community forums, etc.
As a manager, I believe first in developing a team of self-sufficient, self-motivated, curious, creative people.
