Sanjay Kadaveru’s Summary

Educational and Professional Background

A graduate of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) at Madras and the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University, Sanjay has over ten years of international experience in business strategy, strategic marketing and outsourcing advisory services. Sanjay is currently involved with several India focused projects in the Venture Capital, Private Equity, and Hedge Fund domains.

Sanjay has worked extensively with Fortune 500 companies as well as technology startups in the Telecom, Information Technology, Healthcare and Financial Services sectors. Sanjay’s work experience includes stints with Lucent Technologies, JP Mobile, The McKenna Group, iGATE, and American Express. His work has covered issues ranging from technology assessment and competitive differentiation to marketing strategy and business development.

Other Data

Sanjay is passionate about community building activities and is actively involved with several organizations.

Sanjay served as a Co-Chair for the Business Forum at TANA 2007 in Washington DC, an event that attracted nearly 15,000 attendees and featured a keynote address by the former US President, Bill Clinton. At the Pan IIT 2006 conference in Mumbai, Sanjay was invited to participate in a panel on “Bharat Nirman: Nation Building using Technology” (the panel included current and former Directors of the IITs). Sanjay is a Charter Member at The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE) and served as a Co-Chair for “TiECON East 2006: Innovating in a Flat World” (in Boston), one of the largest innovation focused conferences in the US. Sanjay was also the founder of AP Technology Leadership Council, a non-profit networking organization created to provide pro-bono business consulting services to civic and governmental causes in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India. In an earlier role, Sanjay served as the Chairman of the Editorial Board of IIT Maanas, the alumni magazine of IIT Madras.
