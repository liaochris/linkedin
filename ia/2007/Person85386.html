Thomas B. E. Chock’s Summary

"Man is made or unmade by himself. In the armory of thought he forges the weapons by which he destroys himself. He also fashions the tools with which he builds for himself heavenly mansions of joy and strength and peace. By the right choice and true application of thought, man ascends to the divine perfection. By the abuse and wrong application of thought he descends below the level of the beast. Between these two extremes are all the grades of character, and man is their maker and master." -- James Allen, "As a Man Thinketh"

What do you believe about the power of your thoughts to shape your reality? If you can believe, even for just a moment, that your thoughts determine your reality for better or worse, how useful would it be to:

1) Know which habits of thought you have that create success?
2) Know which habits of thought you have that sabotage your efforts at success?

As a 6 Advisors Coach, I can clearly and accurately, using a scientifically developed assessment tool, help you recognize YOUR thought patterns and work with you to unleash the greatness already inside YOU. In truth, the only things keeping you from being your best self are your habits of thought.

Beyond individual and group coaching & training, as a 6 Advisors Coach I can also provide the following services:

- Needs assessments: What if I could tell you how your organization, as a whole, thinks?

- Candidate assessments: What if you could really know how that new employee was going to perform after the "honeymoon" as a new hire was over?

Success truly is a conscious choice. Having someone else's map to success will not help you as much as having a clear idea of the specific obstacles that stand in your way in terms of your being able to create the success you desire by mastering your thoughts.
