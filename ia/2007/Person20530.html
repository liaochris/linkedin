Dan Perry’s Summary

Dan has been managing process improvement projects since 2002 and has amassed considerable skill in delivering increases to business performance and process efficiency.

Dan fuses his passion for high quality service delivery with a thorough knowledge of techniques such as Six Sigma, Lean and Systems Thinking to provide inspiration and coaching to leaders, managers and teams in the drive to continuously improve levels of customer and client service.

Dan is also responsible for overseeing the design, build and implementation of the measures and tools necessary to sustain long-term customer-focussed cultural transformation

Prior to this work, Dan spent 7 years in the pension industry. He began his career at the Automobile Association and continued it with Vickers Engineering and Rolls-Royce following his re-location to London. Throughout this period, Dan's technical ability, organisational skills ability to motivate others led to his position as team leader.

He briefly continued this work with Aon on a larger portfolio of clients before becoming involved in managing projects and operational improvement.
