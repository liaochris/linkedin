Alexander Williams’s Summary

I have been in marketing since 1999. I started working as an independent graphic designer and web developer in Washington DC, before moving to Seattle. I have developed more websites than I can count, including those for Mackie Designs, eMagin, Lumera, KV2 Audio, and Sanewave.
