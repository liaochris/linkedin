Chris Farmer’s Summary

I focus primarily on digital media/entertainment and consumer facing wireless applications on a global basis.

Current areas of interest:

Digital Media: IPTV, Music, Hybrid Online/Offline plays, Ad Networks, UGC, DRM, Virtual Worlds, Sites with strong network effects, Traditional Media that can be digitally distributed, Out-of-Home.

Mobile: Video, Payments, LBS, Advertising & Content Management, Social Networking Apps, MVNOs, VoIP, Retail (Online & Offline), Search, Advertising, Gaming/Betting, Personalization and IMS.
