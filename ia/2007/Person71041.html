Rasmus Højengaard’s Summary

I started out in the entertainment industry, by creating 3d content in a post production company. It was mostly TV ads, TV shows and Scandinavian movies. I did this for more than 2 years.

Together with an associate, I started a privately held company "Perspective", at which we produced various content for the internet, printed media and corporate identities.

I then relocated and started working for the danish game deveopment company IO Interactive A/S, which at that point housed about 25 people. I started medio 2000.

Three of the 6 years I was there, I worked as a level designer, after which I worked 3 years as as game director on the newly realeased AAA title, Hitman Blood Money.

Since the 1st of Nov. 2006, I have been working at C4-world as Director of Design, where my primary task is designing a platform to further entrepreneurship in African contries via a digital community, networking and investment interface.
