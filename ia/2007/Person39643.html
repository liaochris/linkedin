Nimble (www.nimbleit.com) provides a unique and valuable service to local businesses using an extensive marketing and distribution platform that extends their reach and increases revenue in ways that have not been possible before. Nimbleit.com is an online marketplace where businesses can maximizie their revenue by selling their excess capacity in real-time. Listing businesses remain in total control of their business, set their prices, limits, offers, and restrictions. Nimbleit.com manages the transaction and deposits the funds directly into the sellers checking account, often before they deliver the services. No redemption, no mailing, no waiting. The business remains in total control. (Think overstock.com for services.)

Nimbleit.com enables consumers to purchase unused capacity from any local businesses at a discount. Consumers purchase online using a credit card and can use their purchase immediately.
