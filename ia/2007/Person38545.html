Jason Drakeford’s Summary

I am currently working full time as the Art Director for Super Deluxe, a Turner Broadcasting venture.

I am also a short film/music video director in my time off.

In September my girlfriend and I also opened an art gallery called Hushpuppy Gallery. We offer great gifts, funky t-shirts, and an array of hand crafted items. The gallery will also be hosting special events such as wine tasting's and local acoustic musicians. To find out more, check out our website at hushpuppygallery.com!
