Paul Trowe’s Summary

20+ years in the interactive entertainment industry. Held key positions at Sierra On-Line, Activision, Gremlin Interactive, Infogrames/Atari, and Greenleaf Technologies.

In June 2003 formed Pulse Interactive and ran a "one man company" from nothing to over $1 million in revenue in three years. Sold off the game business to SkyZone Entertainment in August 2006 and now consults for them as well as others in the video game space. Currently dabbling in mobile gambling, force feedback, and XBLA but experience has taught me that no matter what platform we're working on at the time, it takes a special eye to spot a great game and construct creative, win/win deals for all mutually interested parties.

Oh yeah, have a freakin' blast while you do it too (and stay away from money hungry thieves)!!!!
