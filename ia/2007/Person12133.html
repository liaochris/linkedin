Boris Epstein’s Summary

I am the Founder and Search Manager of BINC - a Professional Search Firm specializing in the placement of software professionals throughout the US (www.bincsearch.com).

It is my daily goal to help great companies connect with great people. I work with the most reputable and successful software companies in the US - I also represent the top professionals in the software marketplace. I represent opportunities across all divisions of a software company - professional services, engineering, sales consulting, sales, marketing, operations.

I am growing my practice with the notion that people do not retire with their current employers. At one point or another, everybody has an interest in looking into the market. Whenever you are ready, call me. I represent a ton of great opportunities and I will always do everything in my power to help find your ideal fit.


BINC (www.bincsearch.com) is committed to delivering the highest quality candidates at every level to clients in the Enterprise Software Industry. We believe our knowledge of the Enterprise Software Marketplace is unparallel amongst the recruiting ranks. We have established close working relationships with top-ranking hiring authorities and our search consultants have earned reputations as industry experts. As a result of our focus in the marketplace, we are able to keep a pulse on the trends, changes and transitions in the Enterprise Software market.

As a collective, the BINC Search Team speaks with 200 candidates per day and 20 company executives per day. The BINC Search team works on 1000+ unique positions annually and finds work for 100+ Professionals annually. The BINC Search team does all this exclusively within the Enterprise Software Marketplace.
