Jeff Fisher’s Summary

In addition to my work as a counsellor at Columbia Health Centre and my work as a counsellor in private practice I am the developer of CounsellingBC.com. CounsellingBC.com was initially developed in 1999 as a mental health resource website in BC at the time that similar sites were rare. The site included a small directory of counsellors that has since expanded to include over 150 counsellors and psychologists in BC. Gradually features have been added including an interactive database that lists professionals in a number of ways.

The new website was only activated when all features of the site were functioning according plan. One of the challenges was to make sure that an individual category would appear on the site only when there was at least one therapist associated with it. Other directories frequently lead consumers to "dead links" that state something like "Sorry there are no counsellors in this area" or "Sorry there are no professionals in this category". This is frustrating to consumers who are looking for assistance and who are often already frustrated by the challenges in their lives by the time they seek assistance. This inconvenience has been essentially eliminated in the design of CounsellingBC.com.

Other features of the directory that have been well received include Google maps to therapists' offices; a schedule of each counsellor's office hours (optional); email forms for each counsellor to prevent SPAM; photos of counsellors (if desired); and a user interface that allows counselling professionals to update their profiles.

All in all the site has been very well received by the public and health care professionals. It is also extremely easy to locate due to it's high search engine positions for a wide range of search terms.
