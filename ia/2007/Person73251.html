Robert Fischer’s Summary

My background is in application design and development in a variety of technologies and methodologies. Recently, I have become a bit of a Java specialist, and I am actively seeking to gain more technical .Net and Ruby experience.

The most valuable experiences I have to offer, however -- and the part of business I am most interested in -- relate to technical team leadership. Many problems encountered by software development teams seem to be either communication-based, or based on the impact of the business reality (budgets, etc.) onto the solution. The question of how to improve communication and respect the business reality on these teams is interesting to me -- if solving these problems is important to your business, we should talk.

Robert Fischer’s Specialties:

Technical Team Mentoring and Leadership, Technology Training, Agile Adoption (particularly Scrum and emergent/business-customized processes), Software Architecture and Development (Web and batch applications in Java, C#, F#/OCaml), Continuous Integration Environments (Cruise Control and Bamboo)
