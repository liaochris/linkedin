Danilo Arao’s Summary

DANILO ARAÑA ARAO is an assistant professor of the Department of Journalism at the University of the Philippines College of Mass Communication (UP CMC) in Diliman, Quezon City where he is concurrent director of the Office of Research and Publication (ORP).

He is a member of the Board of Editors of Bulatlat, a weekly online news magazine and writes a column for Pinoy Weekly, a tabloid in Filipino. He is also a co-host of the radio program Usapang Media on DWAD 1098 khz which airs every Monday from 3:30 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Prof. Arao is managing editor of the Philippine Journalism Review - the only refereed journal on journalism in Southeast Asia - published by the Center for Media Freedom and Responsibility (CMFR). He also serves as associate editor of the UP-based refereed journal Social Science Diliman. He is part of the Editorial Review Board of the Scientific Journals International which publishes peer-reviewed online journals for all disciplines.

Prior to teaching fulltime at UP, he was editor-in-chief (1994-1999) and head of research (2000-2001) of IBON Foundation, an independent research think-tank. He also taught Political Science part-time at the De La Salle University (DLSU) from 1993 to 1995.

Prof. Arao earned a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Communication (major in Journalism), cum laude, from UP Diliman and was adjudged Most Outstanding Journalism Student upon his graduation. He finished his Master of Arts (MA) in Philippine Studies, with High Distinction, at DLSU and was awarded a gold medal for academic excellence.

For more information about Prof. Arao, please visit his website at http://www.dannyarao.com.
