Jim Lillig’s Summary

Innovative marketing solutions require practical know how when it comes time for implementation. My professional background has always been based in services marketing for both the consumer and business client. As an entrepreneur and as a corporate team player I have been privy to a unique time in Internet marketing history and can bring a fresh revitalized perspective to a clients online marketing. Diversity in both my web venture experiences and Synergy InterMedia's client base (my own e-consulting company) lead to solutions that capitalize on core competencies intermingled with new media technologies. As VP Networks for Top Niche Networks, I expanded the opportunity to synergize my years of experience and contacts into actionable and profitable outcomes. My philosophy has always been to offer a terrific product or service that is a value for the end user and build an honest array of connections for consumers and B2B clients to find them.
