Ertan Onur’s Summary

Ertan Onur received the B.S. degree in computer engineering from Ege University, Izmir, Turkey in 1997, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer engineering from Bogazici University, Istanbul, Turkey in 2001 and 2007, respectively. Dr. Onur is a BAL'93 graduate. After the B.S. degree, he worked for LMS Durability Technologies GmbH, Kaiserslautern, Germany and Global Bilgi, Istanbul, Turkey. During the Ph.D degree, he worked as a R&D project manager at Argela Technologies, Istanbul and holds a US patent. Presently, he is a researcher at Bogazici University supported by TUBITAK. Dr. Onur's research interests are in the area of telecommunications, wireless and sensor networks. He is a member of IEEE and Netlab at Bogazici University
http://netlab.boun.edu.tr/~onure/
