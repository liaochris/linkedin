Jenny May’s Summary

Professional singer/songwriter and musician who has performed with bands in the US and the UK in the studio, at festivals, clubs, pubs and parties with music appearing in the movie Philadelphia. Currently using Apple’s GarageBand software to record music and podcasts and has demoed GarageBand for the Oxford Mac Group at Oxford University. Jenny May's podcast Band Weblogs Podcast Show is currently being aired on CBS Radio's KYOURadio.com and 1550 AM in San Francisco, California.

Created BandWeblogs.com in January 2005 which was the first website directory dedicated to promoting band and music blogs. The website has since grown and now includes music related entries written by Jenny May, Guest Music Bloggers, online press distribution, Band Weblogs Podcast Show and more. Articles on Band Weblogs have appeared on websites such as Reuters, Austin American Statesman, Virgin Radio and NBC11.com. Bands, musicians, publicists, management and record companies also promote their bands and music for free in a hybrid web 2.0 setting by contributing Guest Blogger entries. Entries include band press, music reviews, music stories and links to music, podcasts and videos which are first reviewed before being published.
