Frank Zweers’s Summary

Currently I am working with Strawberries as an Information analist and Functional designer.

In the past 7 years I have acquired broad knowledge in the Internet industry, conceiving websites for companies, e.g. KPN, Unilever, Rabobank, Bruynzeel and Eurolines.

Previously I worked as a producer and director for the dutch national radio. I was educated as photographer and director of audiovisuals.
