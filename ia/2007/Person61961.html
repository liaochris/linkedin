“What’s needed is ‘disruptive technologies’ like unified electronic medical records, which can eliminate the grossly inefficient paper bureaucracy.” Andy Grove (quoted in Time, 10/2/06, p.57).

CNO PARTNERS IS A SPONSOR OF MRN ("The Meta Relationship Networking Project") -- an N3P initiative to integrate everything networkers need into a single agent-commerce platform. Networkers will use the platform to develop disruptive applications which they and their companies will access via five independent ultra-secure portals.

These portals are disruptive because they offer huge advantages compared to current networking sites. For starters, they won’t depend on the web. We’ll access them mostly on our cell phones and Internet-enabled devices. Second, they will automate practically everything we do, freeing us to concentrate on human interactions. Third, they will let the developers among us create agent-commerce applications to replace today's silo enterprise software. Last, but not least, they will provide agent commerce. We'll be able to buy and sell our products and services, create virtual enterprises and leverage a host of super-advanced features.

To learn more, please see the MRN project on our CNO Partners website shown above.
