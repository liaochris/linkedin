Phil White’s Summary

Founder and CEO of PSFEI Limited.
http://www.psfei.com

PSFEI is a consultancy and services based company, specialising in online gaming industry (5 years), project management (15 years) and general technology (15 years) - please view our website for more information.

Consultancy
Need some independent advice, due diligence on a project, market advice or strategy planning, perhaps I can help!

Community
A good community is not just about adding a forum to the website, it means much more and needs to be an integrated piece of the communication and retention planning.

My experience of creating online communities now spans over 5 years and has been rewarded by leading gaming websites like IGN where the communities and teams I have built and run have previously won the Best Online Community award for 2 years in a row.

Customer Support
Ensuring the game has the tools and information needed to help the support teams, setting up the team structure, hours of coverage and policies and procedures (both for the game and what happens when the local police contact you …. And yes they will!) is all part of this.

This also includes what is the best support model for the game, balanced against expected subscribers, growth rates, concurrency and the needs of the business.

I have a presentation as an overview of this which I call the Tiered Support Triangle which I have used to train and implement CS for many companies.

Architecture / Infrastructure
Releasing 4 online games has given me a great insight into server architecture, hosting providers and bandwidth models and how best a deal can be structured to scale (both up and down!) as user demand changes.

Revenue Models / Planning
Is the game F2P or P2P? Should the game have advertising? Which payment options and plans should you have? Do you really need game time cards in the stores? Are micro-payment viable or needed? Mobile phone support? What other revenue generating opportunities are there?
