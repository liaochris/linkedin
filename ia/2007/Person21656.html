David Leconte’s Summary

I have started to build my DW and BI experience at Business Objects for 4 years before joining the EMEA BI and DW Practice at Siebel Systems in September 2004. Re-joining Oracle after the Siebel Systems merge has empowered my EMEA BI & DW Senior Principal Consultant current position.

My experience goes from scoping, workload estimates, ressources profiling, business requirements gathering to all the technical areas of Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence projects as Data modeling, ETL, Metadata and Application Development in all their stages from design to roll-out.

I have worked in many types of project configurations: Expertise (Total, Neopost, Italgaz, SimCorp), "all project alone"(MCI, Moët-Hennessy, FNB), Fixed price projects driven by the software vendor (L'Oréal) , mentoring and driving partners (Standard Bank) , project management (Ecureuil Gestion - Caisse National des Caisses d'Epargne for Business Objects, http://www.france.businessobjects.com/evenements/archives/2004.asp), and Technical lead (Telefonica). I am also contributing to answer to RFPs.

By joining Siebel and Oracle, I have also added an International flavour to my work: After some projects’contribution to Norway, Denmark, Spain and Italy, I was working in South-Africa on two projects, one for the First National Bank (FNB) and the other one for Standard Bank during 14 months. Since, January 2007, I am Technical Lead for Télefonica, in Czech Républic in charge of the Oracle Business Intelligence Application implementation on top of Siebel Sales and PRM (former Siebel Analytics Application).

During my 9 years of consultancy experience with the last 7 years in DW and BI mainly for banks and telco companies , I have been really keen on leveraging my business backgrounds acquired through my 6 years experience as a financial controller for international brokerage compagnies and my 2 years one as ERP Consultant – Business Analyst for Oracle France before moving to the DW and BI space.
