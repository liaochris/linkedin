Peter Curnow-Ford’s Summary

Interim Director working to add shareholder value to technology focused organisations in the Telecoms/Wireless space. Working with founders to build management team, business plans, go to market strategy, investor ready company, funding and business development.

Focused on the Cellular/Mobile, Wireless/WiFi/WiMAX and Wireless Sensor markets primarily in the UK, EU, US and Asia. Large involvement with China, Taiwan and Hong Kong based technical sources and OEM/ODM manufacturing.

Founder of Viatec Associates a venturing vehicle focused on Cellular & Wireless markets and holds Interim roles in the following:-

Eisar Ltd (CEO), lead a senior international team responsible for the development, sales, marketing and branding of mobile/cellular handset products with leading Chinese/Taiwanese handset manufacturers.

Bluenowhere Ltd (Chairman), a Wireless Broadband provider (WISP) bringing WiMAX/WiFi to market.

Instruvention Ltd, a Wireless Sensor developer focused on Fire & Safety markets.

Many years experience building European operations for EU and US organisations in the cellular device, infrastructure and wireless markets. Created and managed the EMEA subsidiary for Repeater Technologies, Inc. and developed their WCDMA 3G strategy and business for products with cellular operators.

European and North American experience, with direct exposure/experience of China/Taiwan and other Asian markets.

Encourages and leads new startup companies in the Telecommunications (esp. Cellular/Mobile/Wireless) sector. General background comprising management, consultancy, sales, marketing and governance.
