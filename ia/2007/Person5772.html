Andrew Walmsley’s Summary

A founding member of Work Shares, (www.workshares.co.uk), specialising in Microsoft's Collaborative platform tools of SharePoint, Exchange and Live Communications Server based solutions, delivered via a hosted service (aka Software as a Service or SaaS) model.

As an experienced Consultant\Project Manager, having worked for ‘Big 5’ consultancy in London prior to a Microsoft Gold Partner on the south coast, I worked for several other large scale Systems Integrators both here and abroad. Over the last 7 years, I have specialised in Microsoft Collaboration and Real Time Communication platform. I have over recent years gained 'grass roots' level experience in the design, build, delivery and support of Microsoft based solutions to public/local government, education, not for profits, telecom and financial services sectors.

I am looking to utilise the skills and experience I have gained thus far in my career, to develop a successful and profitable business.
