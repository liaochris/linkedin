Des Walsh’s Summary

I'm both introspective and gregarious. I'm a story-teller. My various career moves over the years have given me a variety of roles in which I can be at times each of those - the introspective strategic thinker, innovator and mentor-coach, the gregarious networker, the story-telling school teacher and consultant.

For contemporary tags, I am an author, speaker, emerging podcaster, and coach.

My passion is to help people connect with their own best self, to find their own inner wellspring of greatness and to make their mark. And I enjoy helping business people understand how online technologies can enhance their businesses.

I am currently very focused on developing, with my colleague Bill Vick, the Big Biller Books enterprise we have initiated with our Big Biller Audiobook - see www.bigbiller.org

I'm a very experienced speaker and presenter, for small and large groups. I relish opportunities to work with like minded people internationally, across industry sectors and geographical and cultural barriers.

Some details about how I got to here from there:

* High school teacher of English and History, Australia, Germany, UK

* Taxi driver from time to time, between other jobs

* Worked my way up from base grad public service clerk to executive positions with responsibility for multi-million dollar national and state level government sponsored programs, providing policy advice and managing implementation of innovative and unprecendented programs - mainly in cultural development, educational policy and programming and major events planning

* Seventeen years ago, wanting independence and a new challenge, established my own consulting, training and coaching business.

I have an Honours degree in History from the University of Sydney and a Train the Trainer Certificate from the University of Technology, Sydney.

I'm a foundation member of the International Association of Coaching - http://www.certifiedcoach.com
