Susan E Caldwell, PhD’s Summary

After several years of working in laboratory research, I shifted my career focus to medical and scientific writing. Since then, I have managed medical writing groups at 5 biotechnology-related companies. My writing teams have drafted and edited regulatory submissions, clinical protocols, amendments, study reports, investigator brochures, CMC documents, manuscripts, and many other document types. In addition to supervising writers, I have done much of the writing, editing, reviewing, and document formatting for my departments’ projects.

My educational background includes a PhD in medical microbiology and immunology, with an emphasis in medical virology and protein biochemistry, and 4 years of postdoctoral research in infectious diseases. I have a strong publication record in the peer reviewed biomedical literature, and have presented my research at numerous national and international medical conferences. Clinical areas about which I have written include oncology, cardiovascular, pediatric and adult neurology, infectious diseases, ophthalmology, and many others.

Susan E Caldwell, PhD’s Specialties:

Expert medical writer for biotech, pharma, and medical device firms. Deep experience with submissions, including eCTDs, BLAs, NDAs, PMAs, and INDs; manuscripts; GXP documents and audit reports; SOPs, templates, and style guides; and formatting for publication (including hyperlinks, bookmarks, captions, and cross-references). Other experience: press releases, web content, technical writing. Also offer experienced copyediting, which elevates document quality and reduces writing project costs.
