Sandi Hubert’s Summary

Sandi Hubert became President and CEO of Elite IT Services in January of 2006, following a prestigious corporate career spanning over 15 years in Project Management, IT Financial Management, and Business Process Analysis. Sandra is an energetic results-oriented leader in the Information Technology field. She has a deep understanding of quality, service, and professionalism, based on her experience in IT for Fortune 500 corporations. Her desire to “raise the standards” in the caliber of IT professionals provided to clients, was the inspiration behind the slogan for Elite IT Services. The slogan became a tri-fold purpose for Elite IT Services; Raising the standards in the quality of professionalism provided to clients, Raising the standards of IT Professionals through the development of leadership skills, and Raising our standards as an organization to provide premier service in a more cost-effective manner. Sandra holds an undergraduate degree in System and Data Processing from Washington University in St. Louis.

During Mrs. Hubert’s career she assisted her prior employers in managing a two-year firm priority program that consisted of 102 projects to upgrade 6,800 Financial Consultant Workstations with new hardware, software, networking, and cabling to 670 branch offices, during which time she obtained the Series 7 Accreditation. She advanced to developing, implementing and chairing the first Director-level committee to review all Projects for resource capacity. She also single-handedly researched, developed and implemented Total Cost of Ownership and Return on Investment financial models used throughout the IT organization and still widely used today by that organization. Sandi has also personally consulted for clients by mentoring project managers, creating business processes, and conducting resource analysis.
