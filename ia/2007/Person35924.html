Ian Wright’s Summary

My experience primarily focuses on the area of Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery from the disk mirroring and automation side of things. I have experience in planning solutions for zOS (OS/390) as well as open systems on various forms of Unix, Linux, and Windows operating systems.

I have frequently traveled to present solutions and technology directly to customers as well as having used my experience and knowledge to help sales teams better understand what works and what will work for their customer.

Additionally, I have proven skill in the area of written communication. I've taken on writing assignments in two separate IBM Redbook residencies (www.redbooks.ibm.com) where I have authored books in my area of specialty. These projects involved working with teammates from other areas of the US and other countries (Germany, China, Australia).

My intention is to remain in a technical sales support sort of role where I'm able to travel and work with a wide variety of customers. Eventually, however, I'd be interested in a development design role, dealing with hardware and software technology for business continuity, based on my personal experience with customer requirements.

Ian Wright’s Specialties:
