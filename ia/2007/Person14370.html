Michael Carbajal’s Summary

Currently working as an Interactive/Multimedia Developer at NASA, I have been working in the creative industry for over 10 years.

I've been fortunate enough to have worked with a wide variety of clients, having created multimedia and websites for UFC fighter Frank Shamrock, Steve Smith (NFL), R&B singer Chris Brown, Senator Robert Pittenger and TV star Kendra Wilkinson. If you would like to add me as a connection, my email is mdcarbajal@gmail.com

Michael Carbajal’s Experience

  • Interactive/Multimedia Developer

    National Aeronautics & Space Administration (NASA)

    (Government Agency; 5001-10,000 employees; Media Production industry)

    March 2007 — Present (9 months)

    My first project at NASA was the International Space Station: Interactive Reference Guide, which became the winner of the Adobe MAX 2007 Award in Chicago, as well as Adobe's Site of the Day Award for 8/6/07. It was also featured live on CNN.

    For this project, I worked with astronaut and Expedition 18 Commander Mike Fincke for chroma-key videos at the Johnson Space Center in Houston. I also worked with photographers to take pictures of 9 different Space Station modules that were used to create 360 degree virtual tours for the presentation.

    Our latest release is the 50th Anniversary of the Space Age, which is a highly engaging and interactive flash presentation featuring rare archival footage, 6 biographies, 3D spacecrafts and a photo gallery. It also won Adobe's Site of the Day Award for 10/24/07.

    Stay tuned to www.nasa.gov for the latest developments, as well as the exciting work we will soon start producing for NASA's 50th Anniversary.
