Kenneth W. Weinberg’s Summary

Pioneers in the field of recruitment process outsourcing (RPO). We manage recruitment departments for Fortune 500 organizations world-wide. Our efforts are concentrated on candidate identification, full behavioral/labor historical screening and profiling. As the economy continues to improve, organizations are looking for alternative ways to ramp-up talent, while managing recruitment costs and maintaining flexibility. Leveraging 70 years of workforce expertise, Pierpoint® offers scalable, measurable recruitment solutions that generate a competitive advantage for our customers.

Pierpoint's Recruitment Process Outsourcing solution applies to salaried, exempt hiring, as well as to hourly, non-exempt hiring.

As your recruitment process outsourcing partner, Pierpoint assumes ownership and accountability for the recruitment process, from requisition or posting approval through on boarding of talent. We manage each step of the process and people responsible for talent acquisition, delivering the following operational benefits.

* Reduction in overall recruitment costs
* Improved quality of service delivery
* Standardization of internal and external best practices
* Improved compliance with diversity goals
* Enhanced reporting for better decision making
* Retention of strategic role of HR
* Ability to accelerate organizational change
* Improved stakeholder satisfaction and productivity, including senior management, Human Resources function, hiring managers, and applicants

Depth of experience, scalable capabilities, technology proficiency and proven methodologies make Pierpoint the recruitment partner of choice for leading organizations nationwide. We’ve saved Pierpoint customers on average 25% in talent acquisition costs. Pierpoint has more than 1,500 recruiters with access to nearly 3.0 million resumes in its candidate database.

Looking for a scalable, measurable recruitment solutions that generate a competitive advantage? Contact us today.
