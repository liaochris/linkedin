Richard Craig’s Summary

Richard's passion is in helping people to empower their lives, and blow out limiting beliefs, and then creating a compelling future. He believes in playing hard, and in pushing the boundaries. Life is an experience, and the memories we take with us are precious. Richard has travelled the world in his quest for learning and connecting with people.

As a mentor Richard is able to contribute at a higher level. It is truly rewarding to work with the youth of today, in the knowledge that they are the leaders of tomorrow.

His focus in life is to inspire, encourage, empower, and instruct people from all walks of life to enable them to see the greater possibilities for their own lives and to take action.

Richard conducts events on the psychology of success.

Richard Craig’s Specialties:

Richard combines his skills as a personal empowerment coach and a martial arts instructor, with his qualifications as a Master Practitioner of NLP, Neurostrategies, Neuro Associative Conditioning, Hypnotherapy, Time Line Therapy, Temporal Dynamics, and EFT. Richard uses his knowledge of anatomy and physiology, nutrition, essential oils, and Qigong to work with people for health related issues. Richard is a registered member of the NHS directory for complimentary and alternative practitioners.
