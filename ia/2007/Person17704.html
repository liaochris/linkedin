Karen Clark’s Summary

I am a highly organized, productive project manager with experience in the online virtual space. I advocate the use of Agile methodologies including Extreme Programming and Scrum, and have completed Certified Scrum Master training.

GarageGames, an independent game developer, publisher and maker of the Torque game engine, has joined forces with IAC to bring the excitement of full-scale action gaming to the internet. I currently serve as Director of Customer Experience on InstantAction.com and other GarageGames web properties. My goal is to provide the highest quality customer experience for users of that site.

As Development Program Manager at Linden Lab, I managed the build and release process for a year, then moved on to serve as the Product Owner/Customer Representative for one of our Development Studios. During my tenure there, I watched Second Life grow from 40,000 total users to over 7 million. It was a remarkable experience and I learned a lot about working at a fast pace in an ever-changing environment.

As a volunteer for Women in Games International, I built the Mentor Program from the ground up. My goal is to aid women of all backgrounds to succeed in the electronic entertainment industry through mentoring connections that last a lifetime.

Iterative software development management, Agile methodologies (Extreme Programming and Scrum), project management, SVN and CVS SCM systems, UNIX, Linux, Mac OS X, and Windows system administration, MySQL 3 and 4 administration, Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Access), Atlassian JIRA project management software, Viewtier Parabuild automated build system, MindManager visualization software, Japanese language.
