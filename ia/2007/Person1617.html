Adrian Hall’s Summary

I have extensive experience in coordinating the VIP delegate campaigns for very high level, international business summits. I am an excellent communicator and I have strong analytical skills which enable me to identify appropriate delegates. I play an integral part in the development of the programme of the event through an analysis of delegate preferences and the market place. As our events are annual, I believe strongly in building lasting relationships and aim to create a 'business community' feel to our events.

Adrian Hall’s Specialties:

Our events hold a consistent theme of assessing Next Generation Convergence in the ICT industry and how that is driving positive social and econmoic change. This theme is borne through many years of coordinating ICT related events and recognising the overriding desire of attendees to take a more holistic view of the development of the industry.
