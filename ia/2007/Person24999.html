Drew Olanoff’s Summary

Drew and the internet go way back. He has been consulting in various forms of media on the internet since 1996 and has a love for content generated by people. He is one of the hosts of the “Best Damn Tech Show, Period” podcast, one half of the team behind the show “scriggity”, and is known best for such projects as Gmail4Troops and BlogAbroad. His success has come with his understanding of social networking and getting the word out about his projects via blog, podcast, and video via “grassroots” means. From a short internship at Digital City Philadelphia (AOL) to working with colleges and universities (EDU), Drew’s enthusiasm about the internet is his driving force.
