Sven Schroeter’s Summary

Architectural design professional with experience in all phases of project development and a broad range of project types, including small- and large-scale residential, highrise commercial and mixed-use, institutional, and educational.

Particularly interested in the integration of architecture with the arts, new technologies, landscape and urban design issues - especially in the context of difficult sites and unusual programs.
