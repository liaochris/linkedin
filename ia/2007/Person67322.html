Marcel has worked in Project Management for the last twelve years and in Performance Management in the last five years. He designed and managed performance measurement plans; created data collection tools (balanced scorecards, questionnaires, and surveys) and led data collection; designed and implemented nation wide surveys; processed and analyzed data; designed Management Information System to manage and interpret data into an efficient manner.
Marcel supervised collective decisions making process, motivated people, and trained staff to plan and implement result-driven activities.
He worked in various fields: Project Manager or Monitoring and Evaluation Manager in International funded projects (USAID, World Bank, EU), Independent Management Consultant, Consultant (business development, revolving funds creation and management, local economic development, labor market restructuring), Export and Marketing Manager in a manufacturing company, Executive Manager in a retail company.
