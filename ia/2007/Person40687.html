Jerry Argyriou’s Summary

Jerry has had a diverse corporate advisory career at senior levels in Australia, South Africa and USA.

He has multi-industry hands-on experience ranging from Internet/ Media, Financial Services, Healthcare, FMCG and Telecommunications in entrepreneurial and corporate environments.

From March 2006, Jerry has done corporate strategy and advisory work with world leading Australian companies like BlueReef and CorProfit on accelerating their global growth.

In 2000 as Group Internet Consultant for Johannesburg Stock Exchange listed MBT (+R1bn market cap), Jerry worked with the CEO and CFO conducting due diligence on technology investments, writing business plans (one got funded at a valuation of US$60m, working with DLJ in New York), positioning and repackaged technology assets in the USA and South Africa and acting as director, advisor and consultant to group companies. Jerry was based in San Francisco for a year, also getting operationally involved in group companies.

In 1999 Jerry co-founded and served as Strategy Director of e-MD, a technology enabled secure B2B community transmitting electronic claims between doctors and medical insurers. E-MD provided outsourced accounting function services, interaction and collaboration between pharmaceutical distributors, manufacturers, pathologists, radiologists, credit collectors, leasing companies and service providers. With over 1100 doctors/clinic customers, it was the largest IBM/Domino Virtual Private Network in South Africa. Jerry joined MBT as part of e-MD's funding agreement, and returned to e-MD as CEO in 2002.

Jerry has served as head of e-commerce strategy for Investec IMS, as Business Development Director for Nielsen//Netratings and Attix5, and has advised dozens of companies on business strategy over the years.

Between 1992 and 1995 Jerry consulted to NYSE listed Catalina Marketing (market cap +$1bn) about entering South African marketplace, where Jerry was to serve as CEO.
