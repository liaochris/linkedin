Tom Foremski’s Summary

I have been reporting on Silicon Valley for US and global newspapers and magazines since 1984. I also published a local newspaper in San Francisco. I worked full time for the Financial Times from 1999 to 2004 and contributed articles from 1985 onwards..

I am currently publishing Silicon Valley Watcher - reporting on the business and culture of Silicon Valley. It is a news/blog site also featuring other reporters and editors. My goal is to explore emerging online business models with this, and other media ventures, through my company New Rules Media Inc.

I am also involved in several new media/communications projects including changing the format of the press release so that it more accurately reflects our multi-layered media world.
