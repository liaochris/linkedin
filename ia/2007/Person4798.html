Amy Polumbo’s Summary

Amy Polumbo, the reigning Miss New Jersey, is a young woman dedicated to protecting community values and perpetuating the rich tradition of the fine arts. A drama major at Wagner College, Amy Polumbo has starred in many collegiate productions, such as Swing! and Chess. She has dedicated herself to professional theatre as well, starring as Ariel in Walt Disney Entertainment’s Voyage of the Little Mermaid, and also held the role of Maria in Cabaret for Life’s landmark production of The Sound of Music. Amy Polumbo’s talent in the context of the dramatic arts has not gone unnoticed; she was awarded the “Most Outstanding Accomplishments in Acting” honor by the Fine and Performing Arts Center, and her rendition of Little Women captured the hearts and imaginations of the judges at the Miss New Jersey pageant.

Amy Polumbo has gone on to lecture college students on the topic at her own college, as well as at Rider University.

Amy Polumbo’s Specialties:
