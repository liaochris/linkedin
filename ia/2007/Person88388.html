Bob Waun’s Summary

Bob is CEO of Vacation Finance, www.vacation-finance.com, America's First Secon Home Lender. Specializing in residential, commercial and wholesale mortgage originations in the condo hotel, fractional, mezzanine, resort development, timeshare receivable and second home areas.

Bob has 18 years experience in mortgage banking, he holds an MI real estate broker license , and a Master’s degree in finance/economics and BBA in finance from Walsh College. He has personally lent over $700 million in residential loans, and overseen operations lending $1+billion. He has been a professional guest speaker and taught numerous courses and seminars on real estate finance. Since 2002, Bob has worked with condo hotel developers and lenders to improve the market for condo hotel financing. He has reviewed over 75+ projects and has been nationally recognized as an expert in vacation ownership finance.

Bob Waun’s Specialties:

condo hotel, fractional, resort and cottage financing. commercial, mezzanine, timeshare financing.
