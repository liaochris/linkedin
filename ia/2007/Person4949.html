Anand Chhatpar’s Summary

Anand Chhatpar started his first company, Pyxoft Infotech Pvt Ltd., even before coming to study in the United States. In just his second year in college, Anand was involved in his second start-up, OZ Innovations and went on to sell the product internationally in a retail chain of over 72 stores. Anand graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a stellar academic record supplemented by his research in various fields within Computer Engineering. He holds 5 issued U.S. utility patents and has 2 patents pending. Anand has interned twice at Pitney Bowes Advanced Concepts and Technology Division.

Anand is currently the CEO of BrainReactions LLC, a company that utilizes the most creative college students to brainstorm new ideas for Fortune 1000 companies. Anand is fond of meeting exciting new people and believes that opportunities can be created in any field. His connections in India and in the U.S. cover a wide spectrum of leaders in technology and business.

Anand has recently launched Innovation Trip http://www.innovationtrip.com Innovation Trip in U.S.A. is a program arranged for GLOBAL leaders to help them spark innovation and creativity in their organizations, revolutionizing work culture and reducing attrition. Tour includes workshops and places to explore the US innovation and research industry inside out. Innovation Trip will help leaders effectively execute and tap high-end work.

In October 2005, Anand was named as one of the "Top 5 Entrepreneurs Under 25" by Business Week Online:
http://images.businessweek.com/ss/05/12/25entrepreneurs/index_01.htm

In January 2006, Anand was featured on CNBC TV's "Young Turks".
