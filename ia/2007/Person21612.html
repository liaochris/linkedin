Professional Bio:

David Kinnear is co-founder, President and CEO of DDC HRO - a member of the DDC Group of Companies. DDC is one of the world’s leading providers and enablers of onshore/offshore data processing and human capital BPO solutions.

David's primary focus is C-level dialog and business development for the full range of services that the Group deploys – requiring cross-sector knowledge and perspective on how BPO services plug into client corporate infrastructure.

David is a follower and advocate of the BPO sector in the Philippines and China – and enjoys a valuable network of contacts in each and throughout the region.

David is Head of the BPO Council, an initiative of the Philippine-American Chamber of Commerce in New York.

Philanthopic Bio:

David is a Board member of The Grace Children's Foundation, which is dedicated to providing critical medical, educational and humanitarian assistance to the children of China who are awaiting adoption. Further information can be found at: www.gracechildren.org

David's personal interests extend to the success of the Rona Trust / London Sailing Project, through which David competed in his first Tall Ships' Race in 1986 and the Trans-Atlantic Tall Ships' Race in 1992. Further information can be found at www.ronatrust.com
