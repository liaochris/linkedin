Michael Lavoie’s Summary

Michael Lavoie joins CollabNet, Monday, July 11th as our new Director of Support. Michael has over 15 years experience managing technical support, customer service teams and comes to us most recently from Meyers where he served as Manager of Customer Services. Prior, Michael has been VP of Customer Service for Rockcliff Systems and also served as Sr. Director of Technical Support at Commtouch Systems, an early ASP for web-based enterprise email systems. Michael has managed several international teams (UK, Israel, & India) that have provided customer service and technical support of several ASP / ISP products.
