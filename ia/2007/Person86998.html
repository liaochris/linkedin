Toni DasGupta’s Summary

Toni is currently President and CEO of BizOrigin, a business formation service company, which helps new companies start up and grow. Toni is a member of Tech Coast Angels and on the board of advisers of a venture fund. She is involved in matching technology companies with venture and angel funding. Toni is on the board of organizers for a business bank.

A serial entrepreneur, Toni has started 3 companies. She founded WN Distribution, a distributor of specialized test hardware and software solutions to the mid market R&D and manufacturing industry. WN Distribution was sold in 2004. Toni remains active on the board of the company.

She also co-founded and headed the operations for ZennComm Communications, a satellite company based in Mountain View, California, which was formed in a collaboration with Boeing and Hughes Electronics. She was also part of a venture fund in Northern California, where she invested in and advised 5 start up companies.

Prior to that, Toni was with Schlumberger Oilfield Services, for 9 years (in Houston) There she managed cross functional product teams and directed product technology development from concept, through engineering to commercialization and field deployment.

Toni has also consulted extensively on technology and business strategy matters, and has managed several web properties.

Toni has a Ph.D. in Physics and owns 3 patents in Oilfield Technology.

Toni has a business blog profiling startup companies is at www.bizorigin.com
