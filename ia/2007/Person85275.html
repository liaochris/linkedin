Thierry Decocq’s Summary

YQ Purchasing differentiates itself from traditional purchasing consultancy companies in two ways:

YQ Purchasing does not only give strategical and tactical consultancy on how to operate, but is actually also implementing the purchasing activities.
Especially for Marketing, NPD, HRM and Operation departments,
YQ Purchasing does not only look to cost cutting approaches, but also to value creating purchasing activities within the set budgets.


YQ Purchasing has purchasing expertise in raw materials, finished goods and services on a worldwide level:

For production departments: wide range of raw materials and services: e.g. petrochemicals and juice ingredients, cleaning services, other chemicals;
For marketing departments: e.g. website building, media, market research, printing management and promotional products;
For HRM departments: e.g. mobile contracts, car leasing contracts;
