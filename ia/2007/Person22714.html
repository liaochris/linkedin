Deirdre Ryan’s Summary

I have just re-vamped http://www.deirdreryan.com. I also added a new site feature that allows me to upload my clients proofs, password protected, and from there, the client can then order their prints and other items directly and they go straight to them from the lab.
http://deirdreryanphotography.PhotoReflect.com

My goal is for my family, to create a work environment that allows me to grow my business and be with them. Being a family oriented person, I believe makes me understand the needs of certain clients, deadlines and responsibilities.

I also want to create more awareness to certain causes that are very near and dear to my heart on many levels and to donate my time to these. I was a team captain for the Los Angeles Area MS Walk 2007. Not only did I raise money and walk, but I also volunteered my services as a photographer for the event.

Deirdre Ryan’s Specialties:

My specialties are: Fashion, Product, Commercial, Portraiture(Children, Family, Couples), Stills(for movie and t.v.), Journalism, Fine Art, Nature/Urban, and certain events.

I do not do headshots and at this time I do not do weddings. I also offer digital retouching and restoration on images such as family heirloom photos.
