Bob Becker’s Summary

Strategic Agility and Product Development consultant with executive experience including engineering/product development, production, supply chain, customer support, and IT.

The Advantage Group helps in 2 areas:
* Product Development Advantage enables advantage with improved speed, quality and costs. 25 years developing market-leading high-tech products has led to the creation of effective tools and methods to figure out what the gates are. 15 years as an executive built change management expertise. Improvement efforts often focus only on process. But gating challenges are at the intersection of people and processes which can only be diagnosed and improved with the right methods and expertise. Our 'WhoWhatHow' methodology takes systems thinking principles and focuses them to identify the key leverage points.

* Strategic Agility Advantage creates sustainable advantage by leveraging opportunities in a turbulent marketplace. Trends continue to shorten the time for any competitive advantage. To stay successful, it's essential to have the capability to find the next set of competitive advantages while executing on the changes needed to win in the market - time and time again. Even when you know what to shoot for, it's hard to drive the changes to make them happen - unless this kind of change becomes something that your firm excels at. Strategic Agility is: “The ability to sense and take advantage of opportunities by planning and executing far-reaching business changes swiftly and effectively as needed”. Companies die by doing what used to be the right thing, for too long. Becoming more Strategically Agile is the cure!

Services include training, executive coaching and management consulting to help clients meet their full potential. Have also been certified as a coach and facilitator for The Alternative Board (TAB) which provides peer advisory services for small business owners helping them align their businesses to make their personal dreams a reality.
