John McGarry’s Summary

Check out Enfora's new website at www.enfora.com!

M2M (machine-to-machine) is one of the fastest growing industries. Basically, M2M is about connecting machines - cars, factory equipment, HVAC, energy production, pumps, compressors, etc. - to the same Internet each of us uses every day.

What's more, smart machine management or Machine Relationship Management, (MRM) results in higher cost savings and improves profits.

Enfora (www.enfora.com) designs, develops and manufacturers GSM-based wireless modems and terminals. Enfora modems, with a GPRS subscriber account, connect using standard TCP/IP protocols to web-based applications. With millions of devices sold, Enfora has the technology and experience to address any M2M application need.

As Director of M2M Sales at Enfora, I bring together the pieces of a M2M application which will help your company grow and prosper in the 21st century.

My background includes both hardware and application development. Systems and network management, deployment and development. I have a deep understanding of Internet and wireless integration.

If your company is searching for a partner with extensive experience with mobile tracking, telemetry, remote monitoring and control, please contact me.
