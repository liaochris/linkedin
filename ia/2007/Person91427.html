Roger Cullman’s Summary

Freelance photojournalist with writing, editing and computer consulting background.

Graduated from Ryerson University with a bachelor degree in journalism (1996).
In graduating year from Loyalist College of Applied Arts and Technology (2008).

Previously interned with (now defunct) Shift Magazine, worked in Public/Media Relations for the Canadian Automobile Association and Bank of Montreal. Web-based content developer for for charity.ca and sympatico.ca. Worked on national marketing campaigns for Apple Computer.

Regular contributor to Toronto-based web site blogTO.
