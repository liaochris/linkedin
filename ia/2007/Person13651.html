Bryce Ready’s Summary

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering. Current research in computer vision/robotics fields. My work involves vision-assisted pose estimation for mini UAVs. Specifically, I am working on making mini UAVs fly reliably without the use of GPS sensors.

I have prior experience in video mosaicking and super-resolution image estimation, and some prior experience in processor design, computer architecture, and compiler optimization techniques.

Bryce Ready’s Specialties:

Vision-Assisted Pose Estimation for micro air vehicles (MAVs), GPS denied MAV navigation, Monocular Visual (bearing-only) SLAM, Linear and nonlinear filtering (Extended & Unscented Kalman Filter, Particle Filters), Image Super-resolution and Mosaicking, Optimization techniques, Inverse Problems, Monte Carlo Analysis & Simulation, Languages of choice are MATLAB, C/C++, and Python
