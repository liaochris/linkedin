Mary Papantoniou’s Summary

Fast Property buys a number of properties in Australia each day from people that just want the problem to go away.

Fast Property understands how stressful and expensive it can be to try to sell a home the traditional way.

Real Estate Agents may provide a high valuation in the beginning, but then condition you to drop your price every month. Have you heard the common line “the market has spoken, and it is saying we need to reduce the price”. Six months later your house is still for sale, and you are no closer to finding a buyer. In the mean time, you are making repayments each month and losing money both ways.

Imagine knowing exactly what amount of money you will get for your house. Imagine knowing when you can move out, and be free of the debt. Fast Property can make the process seamless, and stress free. We can save you thousands of dollars as we do not charge commission fees or expensive marketing costs. This is because we are not Real Estate Agents or Mortgage Brokers.

Fast Property is an investment company that buys residential properties including houses, apartments, duplexes and town houses directly from sellers. We can make you an offer for your property on the spot, regardless of the age, condition or location of the house. We can settle with you quickly, so that you get your money FAST.

To provide immediate debt relief, Fast Property can look after the mortgage payments, insurances, body corporate fees, and rates. We will buy your house in its present condition, which is a welcoming relief for those that are time poor and can not renovate to attract a buyer.

Once Fast Property decides to purchase your house, you can simply leave the key in the front door on the way out, and we will look after everything else.

If we cannot help you directly we can work with you to find a qualified buyer for your home.

Whether we buy your property, or help you sell it, Fast Property can assist you.

Visit us at www.fastproperty.com.au
