Mike Symonowicz’s Summary

As a Human Resource professional I have held positions at all levels for a number of major Fortune 100 and 500 corporations, Federal and State Government(s), and world-class institutions. My in-depth experiences as a Human Resource Professional include: National HR Project Director, Managing Partner, Director of Human Resources, Director of Placement, Director of Marketing and Recruitment Planning, Division Staffing Manager, Employee Relations Manager, and National Staffing Manager. Within these various capacities, I have been involved in planning, implementing and participating in domestic and overseas employment programs, developing Human Resource publications, designing and administrating uniform company-wide policies and procedures, and workforce planning programs. In addition, I have been responsible for the administration of employee out-placement centers and have provided one-on-one placement counseling. Responsible for the development, implementation and the day-to-day administration of Human Resource programs ranging in scope from, program/project management, employment, advertising, employee relations, compensation, to training on both corporation-wide and division-wide scale. Additionally I am considered to be one of the leading employment professionals in the CyberStaffing® arena.

Along with numerous performance awards, I bring proven expertise in Human Resource management, and automated Human Resource information systems arena. My working knowledge of the Human Resource function, coupled with his technical expertise, enables me to effectively assist client companies in choosing the right programs and people.
