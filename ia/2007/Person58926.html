Michela Perin-Bert’s Summary

I have been an Architect since 1997 and I have spent the last 10 years improving my specialisation in construction matters connected to sport, turistic and civil structures (project management, design, bulding site, fire prevention, building site safety and authorisations). I worked in paralel as employee in big companies and as freelance Architect.
In the last 4 years I have been working for large Events Cometees (Torino 2007 Winter Universiade, Torino 2006 Winter Olympic Games and Athens 2004 Summer Olympic Games) improving specific knowledge about Olympic Games, big events (concerts, exhibitions, fairs) and complex organizations.
