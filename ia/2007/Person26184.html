Sherese Duncan’s Summary

Here is my brief bio:

Do not confuse Duncan with being a business coach. Instead Duncan does much more than provide entrepreneurs someone to listen and give business advice.

What is a “strategic partner?” According to Duncan, the Entrepreneur’s Strategic Partner, it’s a “trusted resource,” that will give you advice, tools, and resources that will drive execution that bring about results. Effició’s tagline says it best…

“getting to the CORE of business”

Duncan founded Effició, Inc. in 2001 to help entrepreneurs build wealth through business ownership. Signature company programs such as CORE™, SWIFT™, Lunch & Profit™, or the award-winning e-zine The Street Perspective provides the tools small businesses need to create perpetual profitable businesses. Latin for effect, Effició does just what the name implies, effect entrepreneur’s lives across the globe - from the recruiting firm that landed a $76,000 contract after six weeks of developing infrastructure with Duncan’s expertise to the IT firm, whose revenues increased from $60,000 to $2 million.

Giving advice, analyzing issues and solving problems for others always came naturally to Duncan, always defying the odds, elevating platforms and claiming the unexpected, this natural born leader is not your average thinker. Instead, she always carries with her the energy and mental power to take herself, and others, to the next level. Often heard saying, “Stop thinking outside the box, just think!”™, Duncan was able to create a business that would offer advice and solutions to entrepreneurs and position them for self-actualization.

Her unparalleled ability to lead, motivate, and give entrepreneurs the ‘kick in the pants’ they need Duncan has established herself as one of the leading and most comprehensive small business experts today that can ‘mobilize’ entrepreneurs to exceed their definition of success.

For more information about Duncan and Effició, Inc., go to www.Effició.biz.
