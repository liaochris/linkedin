Art Chua (art.chua@yahoo.com)’s Summary

Art Chua offers expertise in Asian outsourcing, product development and deployment, engineering, operational management, manufacturing process, supply chain, system design and development. His background spans multiple industries, including medical deices, video surveillance, and beverage packaging companies, supporting the success of companies with revenues of $260 million to $6 billion. Through these experiences, he developed strong working knowledge of FDA/CE Standards, GMP, ISO9001/13485 and quality/testing procedure for medical device industry.

Much of Art Chua’s business success arises from his reputation as a passionate, respectful communicator. Fluent in English, Mandarin and Cantonese, Art Chua also speaks Malay and 3 other Chinese dialects. Dedicated to building bridges between countries around the world, he has formed many solid relationships with key suppliers and business partners throughout China and Pacific Rim.

Art Chua is also a proven Project Manager with ability to recognize strategic goals and implement them through customer-driven and timeline-driven process. With over fifteen years experience in corporate America, he has executed complex organizational operations tying together business development, marketing, engineering, logistic and legal affairs in support of new product sourcing, with special focus on Greater China.

Born in Malaysia, Art Chua came to United States as an exchange student when he was 19 and eventually became an U.S. citizen. He is an honors graduate with a B.S. in Industrial Engineering and an M.B.A. from California State University, Fresno.
