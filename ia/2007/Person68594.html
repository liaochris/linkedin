Piotr Skulski’s Summary

In 1996 I created Centrum Rejestracji Oprogramowania (http://cro.skulski.pl) - first Polish online software selling company. It is still working pretty well being the national leader in shareware distribution. We are the distributor of many popular products, including WinRAR, Total Commander, Dameware Network Utilities and many more. We are continually looking for new products and developing new activities to fulfill market's needs.

In 2006 I started new venture: "Rio Bravo" and "Fort Rio Bravo" (http://www.riobravo.pl). It is a project in the area of entertrainment - Wild West styled airgun shooting range. We experienced great success during the summer of 2006 so we naturally plan to expand and upgrade Rio Bravo for 2007. Provided we find an investor, I expect Rio Bravo to be present in Poland and in Germany during all major western related events in 2007. If you are interested in our offer or simply want to invest - I will gladly answer any question.

I am also looking for new opportunities related to:
- United Kingdom
- New Zealand
- alternative payment systems (like e-gold)

I am also preparing new businesses, so Business Angels' requests welcome.

BTW - every of my businesses may be for sale, provided the appropriate price is offered. In general, for a stable business I consider an equivalent of last 12-months' turnover to be fairly appropriate.
