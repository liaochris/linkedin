Dave Morin’s Summary

Dave is an entrepreneur, technologist, marketer, and young visionary. Currently, he is the Senior Platform Manager at the internet’s leading social utility, Facebook. In addition, he serves on the advisory boards of various stealth startups in Silicon Valley.

Previously, Dave held numerous positions at Apple including Manager, Creation & Collaboration and Student Marketing Manager. And, founded two technology companies; Viadi, a web application development and innovative marketing company which is the evolution of DM Design Studios, a technology and design consultancy.

His focus on innovation, vision and technology strategy has involved extensive interaction with the global market at large. Dave received a B.S. in Economics and Business Administration from the University of Colorado at Boulder. Originally hailing from the big sky state of Montana, he currently lives in San Francisco’s legendary North Beach district.

Else:

While he’s not on the road with his day job, he writes and consults about business, marketing, technology, product development, and innovative thinking. You’ll generally find him bouncing from one city to the next, observing culture, enjoying people, laughing generously, and infusing colleagues with ideas. Occasionally, he masquerades as a professional photographer. Locally, he can also be found wandering the city with a camera, dining in the amazing restaurants of San Francisco, exploring the outdoors of California, or enjoying a handful of sports that are dear to his heart (read: skiing and a newfound love for gliders). Other than that, what you see here takes up a small percentage of his “free” time.

“Live life well.”
