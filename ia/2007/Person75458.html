Ryan Walker’s Summary

Objective:
To lead the development and implementation of effective e-business/web strategies and initiatives to substantially contribute to the organization’s success.

* One of Cincinnati’s early web pioneers, I have twelve years experience developing web applications, serving clients from the Fortune 50 to mid- and small-sized businesses across a wide range of industries.

* Early adopter of database-to-web technologies, PHP in 1996, ASP in 1997, and .NET in 2001, and Ruby on Rails in 2005.

* A thought leader in the Interactive space, having delivered speeches and articles on the importance of user experience.

* Experience with business development, management, strategy, web programming (Ruby on Rails, ASP.NET, ASP, SQL, XML, Javascript, CSS, XHTML), and web creative (Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator).
