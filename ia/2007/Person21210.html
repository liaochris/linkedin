David Thompson’s Summary

Been building computers since 1975. Developing software since 1976 (Basic & Mainframe). Telecommunications since 1984. Network Management since 1990. Netcool certified since 2001.

Mr. Thompson has over twenty seven years of experience as a senior manager, development/support manager, development/support team leader and systems programmer/analyst.

David has worked in all facets of the telecommunications industry during his 16 years with MCI Telecommunications. While in MCI, David has performed and managed software development in all areas of the telecommunications industry including, order entry, order processing, network provisioning, call processing, invoicing, billing, collections, fraud, network interfacing and network management.

David has a variety of experiences in systems development from Mainframe to LAN/WAN to PC connectivity and data communications. His experience in LAN/WAN design, development, deployment and data communications problem resolution extends to the NSFNET (Internet) in the mid-1980’s.
