Tony Moreira’s Summary

Tony holds a Fine Arts degree in Acting from Salem State College (Salem, MA), and has featured credits in film, television, and theatre. Recent projects include Fox 2000 Pictures' 27 Dresses, Walt Disney Pictures' Underdog, and ESPN's The Bronx is Burning. He also performs in various fringe stage productions in the New England area, where such works as Molasses Tank's "An Evening of Havel: Audience", and Blue City's "The Blue Room" have gained him positive review and personal recognition in media outlets including The Boston Globe.

Tony has also had a long career in the technology field and is a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT). He was once employed (1996-2002) by the very successful entertainment software publisher Hasbro Interactive (a now defunct division of the Hasbro Toy company which eventually came to be known as Atari through acquisition) as the company’s Information Technology Manager from its conception through its demise. During his time in the video game industry, he earned published technical credit for support on dozens of software entertainment titles such as NASCAR Heat (where he has his own car in the game: #118), Rollercoaster Tycoon, Monopoly Star Wars Edition, and Frogger.

After the fall of Hasbro Interactive, Tony took up acting and has become a New England personality for Radio Disney's Providence, Rhode Island (AM 550 WDDZ) & Boston, MA (AM 1260 WMKI) stations.

Tony continues his professional interest in technology by teaching Microsoft technologies as adjunct faculty at Johnson & Wales University, and as a visiting instructor in higher education technology programs at Roger Williams University, Boston University, and University of Connecticut.
