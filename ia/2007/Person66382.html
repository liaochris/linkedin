Paul Aitken’s Summary

Paul is the CEO of Editopia, a company that enables businesses to easily brand, customize, manage, store & deliver video on their websites. The business backed by Benchmark Capital and 3i. Prior to this, Paul founded and was the Managing Director of Movota Ltd, a provider of unique mobile phone based services to leading broadcasters including BBC, SBS & RTL. Movota was sold to Bertelsmann in 2005. In 2006 Paul led the merger of Movota with arvato mobile and served as Managing Director and Board Member of arvato mobile. He is experienced in providing M&A and Strategy advisory services to companies in a broad range of sectors including Media, Energy Trading, Electricty Services, Software and Property. In addition to this, Paul has extensive senior operations, marketing, & strategy experience in the Retail, Media and IT sectors. Paul received his MBA from the International Institute of Management Development (IMD) in Lausanne, Switzerland, and his Bachelor of Arts with honors from the University of Nottingham, UK.
