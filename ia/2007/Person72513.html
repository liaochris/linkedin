Richard Johnson’s Summary

Richard never misses an online advertising event. That could be why his clients describe him as “the consummate interactive advertising sales professional, with deep industry knowledge, the companies within it, and how all the competitive sets intertwine.” Richard is a leader in online advertising, sales and marketing, working with clients to help them achieve their marketing and brand awareness goals.

Richard has worked for top brands such as Bank of America and Tiffany & Co. and online publishers MyFamily.com, ValueClick Media and WeatherBug. At Bank of America, Richard was involved in the early stages of rich media, building out interactive video and B-Box tests with @Home and on the Snap.com High Speed Portal. At MyFamily.com, Richard was in charge of the online marketing group for both MyFamily and Ancestry.com before taking over as the Western Director of Advertising Sales. Richard held leadership positions at both ValueClick Media and WeatherBug where he worked with executive management to develop core sales strategies to drive company goals and revenue. With 15 years of combined work experience, Richard is considered a top sales professional in the media industry. Currently Richard sits on the board of LVIMA and BIG. Richard attended Boston University and Franklin Pierce College and has a B.A. in Polotical Philosophy.
