Sébastien Adgnot’s Summary

With 7 years of experience as a Web Developer, I worked on an e-commerce and community web site for a startup company in Paris, France (video game exchange platform) for a year.
Projects I have worked on include human resources web applications, content management systems, time tracking and management systems, and mutual fund purchasing platforms. Past clients include Schlumberger, Clarins, the French trademark office, Ogilvy One, Crédit Agricole Asset Management, etc.
In keeping with the fast changing technologies of the web, I have utilized Coldfusion MX, PHP 4 and 5, XHTML, CSS, Javascript, Flash MX, Ruby on Rails and database technologies in the course of my career.
The innovative use of web technologies interests me a great deal; I therefore constantly stay on top of new developments.
I would like to use my solid technical background and my international business experience to contribute to the strategic development of web applications.
