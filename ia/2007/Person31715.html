  • Founder, President & CEO

    HomeYeah Real Estate Technologies, LLC

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 1999 — June 2003 (3 years 11 months)

    HomeYeah.com was an on-demand real estate exchange independent of the Realtor® controlled, locally-based Multiple Listing Service. By creating a separate marketplace for home listings, and managing the transaction process via the internet and phone, HomeYeah was able to offer real estate services at fees that were significantly lower than traditional Realtors®. The Company facilitated more than $350,000,000 in real estate transactions over a 4-year period, saving consumers more than $23,000,000 in transaction fees. Company acquired by American Pacific Financial Corporation (parent company of Help-U-Sell Real Estate).
