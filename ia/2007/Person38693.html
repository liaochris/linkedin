Jason Nazar (jason@venature.com)’s Summary

Jason Nazar is the founder of Docstoc.com, a free online document exchange database and social networking site that allows users to store, search, and share virtually any type of document.

Jason is a also partner at Venature LLC, a consulting group focused on accelerating the growth of new ventures. There he has worked with dozens of companies to improve their strategy, sales, and prepare them for financing.

He previously worked in start ups as both a founder and head of sales and marketing. He holds an MBA from Pepperdine University and a B.A. from the University of California at Santa Barbara where he served as the student body president of both universities. In addition, Jason is completing his JD at Pepperdine University in May 2007.
