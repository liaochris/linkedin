Troy Minaldi’s Summary

A corporate marketing and advertising guru with 15 years of experience, I manage the entire marketing process from strategy, brand messaging, and market assessment through creative execution and media selection. I have P&L and budgeting experience as well as several years spent as marketing department head.

I am a true student of branding and I understand the importance of cross-functional cooperation in making sure that the brand image permeates the entire organization. No, I don’t beat people over the heads with it. Instead, I try to make sure everyone understands the “method behind the marketing madness” and can then at least be on the same page as to why we do the crazy things we marketers do! Basically I have always been the champion for marketing in the organizations for which I have worked!

I have extensive experience managing both internal and external advertising and PR agencies and departments through the strategic planning, campaign development, creative production, and implementation process.

My career has taken me into a wide range of industries including, CPG, entertainment, staffing, and retail with a special niche in the field of multi-unit retail and franchise marketing.

A consummate team-player, mentor, and leader, I enjoy building and motivating teams of marketing and advertising pros.
