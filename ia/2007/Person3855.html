David Allensworth’s Summary

Having been in charge of a wide variety of projects over the last ten years in television, for various companies including Impulse Media, Rainbow Media, VH1, and Golden Gut Entertainment, I have developed relationships with all major agencies, as well as production companies based in NY, LA, and around the world.

Recently forming Shady Tree Films, I intend to combine my television executive experience and my abilities as a show producer with my training as a screenwriter (I am a graduate of both Film & Television from Cal State Northridge in LA, and the Professional Screenwriting Program at UCLA) to find, create, develop, and produce projects for both television and film.

David Allensworth’s Specialties:

From running a production staff, to creating show ideas and budgets, to solid contacts at various networks, agencies, and corporations, to my storytelling abilities and vision, I have always found and opened new doors in the Entertainment Industry. I am terminally both “left and right-brained” which lets me balance a high-end creative vibe with the ability to manage several projects at once.
