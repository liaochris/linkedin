Matt Liss’s Summary

Matt Liss, broker/owner of Mark Allen Realty, has been in the real estate and development business for 5 years. During that time he has bought and sold countless properties and has developed both single-family homes and condo conversions. This includes not only rehabbing existing structures but also designing and overseeing new construction. Matt is a licensed general contractor in Chicago and is well versed in the city’s building codes, permits, and applications. He also owns several rental properties where he proves to be a very evenhanded and accommodating manager. In 2006, he closed over seven million dollars, earning him recognition from The Chicago Association of Realtors as one of the city’s top producers. Matt has clearly proven to be quite knowledgeable and successful in Chicago’s real estate market. He enjoys working with many different people, whether they are searching for their dream home, first condo, or an investment property.

Matt was born in Australia, but spent most of his life in the northern suburbs. He now calls Logan Square home, chosen for its diversity, exceptional restaurants, historic buildings, and picturesque boulevards. Matt takes great pride in his vintage 2 flat greystone, which, although many renovations have been made, maintains the integrity it was born with in the early 1900s. In addition to Matt’s passion for his job, he loves to spend time with his wife Amy, son Caden, and daughter Tahlia. Matt always finds time for a trip to the park, zoo, or anywhere else he believes will provide joy and entertainment for his family.

Matt is an exceptional businessman and maintains high ethical standards both professionally and personally. He would be an asset to anyone looking for guidance in the real estate market.
