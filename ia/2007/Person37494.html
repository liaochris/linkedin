Jaime Syjuco’s Summary

Jaime Syjuco is a strategic business development expert focused on building new businesses, creating partnerships and bringing their products to market.

He has over 10 years of global high tech industry experience with Enterprise, Startup and Turnaround companies.

Over his career, Jaime has led business development initiatives for over 20 companies in the U.S. and Asia, several of which have become market leaders and were acquired by the likes of HP, Oracle and Lexis-Nexis. Most recently, Jaime is responsible for an Asian conglomerate's market entry, venture investments and subsequent leadership position in the global IP Telephony, BPO and IT outsourcing industries. Prior to this, Jaime was a strategy consultant for Accenture serving high-tech clients and their venture capital investors. He is also founding principal in several highly successful not-for-profit projects that have spanned several continents, attracted human-rights leaders and saved over a thousand lives. Jaime holds an MBA from the London Business School.

Jaime Syjuco’s Specialties:

Rapid and effective implementation of the strategic business plan, bringing to market new businesses and products through marketing best practices and partnerships.
