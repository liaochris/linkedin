Pieter Wessels’s Summary

Initially as Financial and later Management accountant at SAB, I was responsible for a budget of R1.6 billion between 1995 and 2000.
In 2001 I developed the compensation philosophy and mechanism for SAB Ltd in respect of third party distribution contractors which remains in place (as at 2007)
Between 2003 and 2004 I conceived of and developed a franchising business model for beer distribution. The franchise operates under the name HoneyBEE.
Between 2004 and 2007 I consulted on distribution strategy, with emphasis on compensation, to Miller Brewing Company in the US, Grupo Emprasarial Bavaria in Colombia and to CCN in Ecuador.
I was part of SAB's management negotiating team in wage negotiations with the Union in 2001, 2002, 2003, 2005 and 2006.
I have been secretary of the governing body of a public school since 2006.
