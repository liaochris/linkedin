Oscar Ciornei’s Summary

Oscar A. Ciornei, senior partner and founder of The OAC Group, has lead a distinguished thirty-year career as a visionary and proven leader in the consumer electronics, professional audio, music and recording industries. Oscar has served in nearly every executive capacity and sector of those industries, including CEO, distributor, music producer, sales representative, consultant, dealmaker, board member and interim executive leader. A former singer & brass player and an audiophile and music lover, Oscar’s lifelong love of music and sound technology feeds the tremendous passion that has been the cornerstone of his personal and professional success. He and his wife are both avid music lovers & home theater enthusiasts who have made their home a technology showcase.

Oscar and his OAC team Advise & Consult manufacturers on innovative projects. Oscar prefers advisory board roles working with entrepreneurs with goals to gaining market share as well as launching ultra-successful products and businesses. Limited-time contracts will be considered, especially if they can be handled via tele-commuting with some light travel. Knowing more about your management style and structure would help address how we could structure a working relationship. Let’s arrange a time to get acquainted and discuss your needs under a mutual NDA.

Some of the companies most notably involved with: A-BUS, Atlantic Technology, BASF, Boston Acoustics, DTS, GRP Recordings, IBEAM, Konica, Qsonix, Russound, Sheffield Lab, SpeakerCraft, Sprint, and Telarc Digital.
