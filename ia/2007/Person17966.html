Chris Malek’s Summary

Experienced software engineer (Python and web applications; C and scientific applications) with over fifteen years of work in a professional environment. Familiar with C, SQL, Python, Perl, Tcl/Tk, HTML, CSS,
Bourne and C shell scripting.

Project and technical manager of a team of systems administrators and programmers that supports 7000 users. Uses both agile and traditional software development methodologies for software projects.

Ph.D. student in the School of Information Systems and Technology at Claremont Graduate University, doing work in data mining, knowledge management, human-computer interaction and information visualization in the context of social learning software.

Skilled systems administrator with twelve years of experience in the planning, deployment, and maintenance of fault tolerant 20-200 host UNIX LANs in an academic setting. Strong emphases on fault tolerant service deployment, unattended installation systems, automation of configuration management, knowledge management, and the analysis and visualization of performance and reliability data for proactive maintenance and tuning of servers.
