Justine Lam’s Summary

Justine Lam is currently the eCampaign Director for the Ron Paul 2008 Presidential Campaign. As the eCampaign Director, she is managing the change from the Exploratory Committee website to the Campaign website which will feature more usability, findability and functionality. She is also managing the campaign's strategy in utilizing social networking platforms and other technologies to inform potential voters about the candidate, to facilitate the mobilization of supporters, and to raise funds for the campaign.

Previously, as Operations Manager, she managed a fast-growing and internet-based campaign. She created administrative and HR systems for staff located through the United States utilizing her skills in streamlining business processes with web-based tools.

Prior to the campaign, Justine demonstrated her management, organizational and budgeting skills in a change-filled environment, when she expanded a highly-competitive DC internship program by 20 percent and saved the organization $15K in costs.

Her goals are to work for a company with a team-oriented environment, a clear mission, and opportunities for growth. The ideal position would involve a combination of business process management, logistical management, one-on-one interpersonal communications with clients/partners, and IT (with a focus on the "I") and webdesign skills. Perhaps, she'll be a CIO one day...

While she is working full-time for the campaign, she is also exploring opportunities in information architecture, recruitment, program management, marketing, and web design. The industries or areas that she is most interested in being involved in are web applications, transportation/logistics, private sector development and information science.
