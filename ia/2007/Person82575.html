Steve Perryman’s Summary

University Teaching | PhD Research Scholar | Public Policy and Management|

Steve Perryman has taught Public Policy and Management units at the University of Melbourne and Deakin University in Victoria, Australia in undergraduate and postgraduate Public Policy courses on a sessional basis over the last six years.

His recent teaching experience also includes leading seminars on public policy and management for visiting Vietamese and Chinese public servants here in Australia on government sponsored study tours.

His work experience spans both the public and private sectors - this includes working as an advisor to state and federal government ministers and managerial roles in business.
