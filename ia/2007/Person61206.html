Tim Hawkins’s Summary

Tim is an international speaker and presenter with 30 years of experience. He is one of the most experienced youth workers in Australia. He has trained hundreds of young leaders, and developed many in the art of public speaking. He has a passion for raising up leadership and helping people to fulfil their potential. He is the author of many books and in great demand for businesses and conventions, as well as schools, community groups and churches.
