Nir Ofir’s Summary

VP of product and content at blogTV.com . I started my Internet days back in 1997 by founding my own internet production house. Prior to blogTV, I was the editor in chief of TAPUZ.co.il. I Joined Tapuz in 2000 and was responsible for innovating and developing leading services and products. My expertise is in planning and executing community oriented products, services and spaces that enable people to create their own content. Since 2000 I oversaw the creation of dozens of internet projects in Tapuz and with the partnerships of major Israeli media companies. In 2005, I Initiated “BlogDay” , an international day for blogs and bloggers. I holds a bachelor in communications and behavioral science from Israel’s College of Management and Masters of Entrepreneurship and Innovation (MEI) from Swinburne University of Technology in Australia.
