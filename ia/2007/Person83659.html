Robert Swanwick [linkedin@swanwick.com]’s Summary

I am interested in the ways that data, information, and knowledge can improve our lives. In the business world that means enhanced productivity, better access to ideas and people, and better decision making.

I seek to help clients improve their workforce through the use of e-learning solutions. Ideas from the best minds are woven into a product that helps employees determine how their company can improve.

Some of my past clients as a consultant include: NYC Dept of Education, Broward County Public Schools, ExxonMobil [XOM], Metlife, Westinghouse, Thomson, Aflac, ITT Industries, Defense Financial Accounting Services (DFAS), Marriott, British Telecom, ABN AMRO, Renault, The Advisory Board, Merant, Best Buy, Stanley Works, Jensen Tools, Tech Data, Compaq, St. Paul Reinsurance, Hospital at the University of Pennsylvania (HUP)

I am interested in discussing e-learning and/or KM in general with anyone who would like to "Linkin". KM may be struggling as an independent discipline, but the principles are being incorporated into our business and personal lives at an extraordinary rate.
