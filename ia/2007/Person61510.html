Maneesh Sagar’s Summary

Director Investments focused on making successful investments in technology startups in the Tri-State area.

Focusing on IT, enterprise software, mobile ecosystem, security space; in charge of all facets of the VC lifecycle, from sourcing, analyzing and evaluating; syndicating, negotiating deals and active portfolio management

Extensive operating experience across the high tech and manufacturing industries including positions in enterprise sales, business development, partnerships, product marketing and executive management

Established network of entrepreneurs, early and expansion stage VCs, and CXOs across the software and technology industries in the US, Europe, and India

First hand understanding of dynamics of managing growth (HR, Product and Portfolio management, Finance, Sales Management, Marketing, and Legal)
