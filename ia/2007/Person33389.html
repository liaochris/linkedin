George Sarmonikas’s Summary

George is a Chartered Engineer professional with experience in mobile communication technologies. He specialises on radio network planning & optimisation of WCDMA, HSDPA/HSUPA and WiMAX. Other areas of specialisation include Wireless LAN and Broadband Wireless Access technologies like Flash-OFDM, IPWireless TDD and Mobile TV as well as strategic analysis of the wireless broadband industry. George has conducted numerous projects and techno-economic studies in Europe, Middle East and Asia for cellular operators, broadband ISPs and analyst firms. Companies for which he has conducted consultancy and technical training include Orange, Vodafone, OTE, Mobilink, Nokia Siemens Networks, Mobinil, Telenor, ExcelComindo, Pacific Internet, SRT, SingTel, Siemens Italy, Malaysia Telecom, Digi, Maxis, Dhiraagu, Mobily, Etisalat, ARCchart, Infineon, Olympus and many more.

George has considerable experience on WiMAX network design and deployment and he is heavily involved on WiMAX consultancy, training and product marketing in many parts of the world some of which are based in Europe, USA, Middle East and South East Asia.

George has graduated from the University of Bristol, UK with a degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering and he holds a Master in Mobile Communication Systems. He is currently pursuing a part-time MBA from the Edinburgh Business School. He is a member of both IEE and IEEE, a member of the Technical Chamber of Greece and candidate for the Eur.Ing title.
