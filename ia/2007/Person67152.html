Mark Peden’s Summary

Mark is not a typical sales and business development professional. Mark is a revenue accelerator, brand evangelist, and customer relationship driver with specific knowledge of the advanced IP communications market. For Mark, the brand and the customer relationship are intertwined drivers of revenue and profit.

Mark is actively seeking a professional opportunity where he may utilize his 14+ years of dedicated telecom expertise in a mid-senior management role adding significant value in the areas of business development, strategic partnerships, technology evangelism and product marketing.

Mark's passion is launching technology companies, products and services in North America, Europe and Asia. His leadership in wireless and wireline telecoms, next generation mobile networks and specific domain expertise in IPTV, Fixed to Mobile Convergence (FMC), have been highly recognized. While leading Intel's broadband initiative within Intel Architecture Labs, Mark provided due diligence for Intel Capital investments in broadband. This resulted in joining and spearheading the launch of Intel-funded start-up NorthPoint Communications.

After a strong IPO and subsequent merger announcement between NorthPoint and Verizon, joined Montreal start-up Simpler Networks. Following the successful launch of the company in the carrier market, contracted by AT&T Wireless (now Cingular) to lead the re-introduction of their data service offering (mMode).

In 2004 selected as one of the 40 most successful in the State of Oregon under the age of 40 by the Oregon Business Journal. Having lived and worked overseas, Mark has been instrumental in bringing two companies public and three international.

Mark is an adjunct faculty member of Portland State University in Portland, Oregon and Clark College in Vancouver, Washington. He is a member emeritus of the Board of Directors for the DSL Forum and the W3C Mobile Web Initiative.

Mark Peden’s Specialties:

Sales, business development, strategic partnerships, project management, international launch, contract negotiation, field marketing, product and company launch, product marketing, IP networks, IP-based voice/video products & services, wireless (802.1x), WiFi, WiMAX, VoIP, softswitching, advanced IP voice/video telecom services, , broadband access, xDSL, SONET/SDH, cable, DLC/remotes. Direct work experience in US, Canada, Europe and Asia.
