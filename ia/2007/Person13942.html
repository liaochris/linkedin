Dr Babu B V’s Summary

Dr B V Babu is Professor of Chemical Engineering and Dean of Educational Hardware Division (EHD) at Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani.

Obtained PhD from IIT-Bombay.

Biography is included in 2005, 2006 & 2007 editions of Marquis Whos Who in the World, in Dictionary of International Biography 2006, in 2000 Outstanding Intellectuals of the 21st Century 2006, and in Marquis Whos Who in Asia 2007.

Coordinator for PETROTECH Society at BITS-Pilani.

On various academic and administrative committees at BITS Pilani. Member of project planning & implementation committees of BITS-Pilani Dubai Campus, BITS-Pilani Goa Campus, and BITS-Pilani Hyderabad Campus.

22 years of Teaching, Research, Consultancy, and Administrative experience.

Guided 3 PhD students, 28 ME Dissertation students and 24 Thesis students and around 170 Project students. Currently guiding 6 PhD candidates, 2 Dissertation students and 9 Project students and has 4 research projects from UGC, DST & KKB Academy.

Published around 140 research papers, 5 books and wrote several chapters in books and lecture notes.

Invited Chief Guest and delivered Keynote addresses at many international & national conferences. Organized numerous Seminars & Conferences at BITS-Pilani. Chaired 10 Technical Sessions at various International & National Conferences. Delivered 23 invited lectures at various IITs and Univeristies.

Editorial Board Member of three International Journals and expert reviewer for 19 International Journals.

Reviewed four books. PhD Examiner for four candidates.

Organizing Secretary for “National Conference on Environmental Conservation (NCEC-2006)”, BITS-Pilani, September 1-3, 2006.

Dr Babu B V’s Specialties:

Evolutionary Computation, Biomass Gasification, Environmental Engineering, Biofiltration, Modeling and Simulation, Optimization, Artificial Neural Networks, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Multiphase Reactors, Transport Phenomena, Expert Systems, Computer Aided Design, Process Control, Energy Integration Analysis, Fuel Cells.
