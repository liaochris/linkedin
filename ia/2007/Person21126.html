Dave Taylor’s Summary

Strategic management consultant focused on 21st century communications mechanisms, I am very involved with business blogging (also called business weblogs) and run three busy Weblogs of my own. I am a prolific writer with sixteen published books and three in process for 2005 on both business and technical topics.

I am interested in helping corporations of any size realize the potential of reimagining their business, reinventing their company, and embracing the potential of Internet-based Web, weblog, and other forms of two way communication to establish themselves as thought leaders and experts in their industry.

Dave Taylor’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in technical and management issues, ranging from academic experience - with an MS Education and an MBA - to startup work - I've founded, grown and sold three startups - to writing and public speaking. I have a wide range of talents and can work equally well with a small group of top-level executives in a strategic planning meeting or running a technical or marcom workshop for 500 people. I am an excellent communicator, top-notch speaker and great writer.
