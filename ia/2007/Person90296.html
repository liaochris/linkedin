Wanda Ross’s Summary

Wanda manages recruiting and hiring of executives (Vice Presidents and above) for Intuit worldwide. Intuit (TurboTax, QuickBooks and Quicken) has been recognized by Fortune© Magazine as "America's Most Admired Software Company" and is in the top third of the "100 Best Companies to Work For.” Wanda attracted world renowned talent to the executive team and saved the company $1.8M in search fees in the first year.

Prior to Intuit, Wanda was a Senior Recruiter at Google for over a year. She staffed their internal consulting firm plus their Human Resources Department hiring 64 people for 32 different jobs.

Previously, Wanda was a retained Executive Search Consultant noted for delivering high quality professionalism, innovative strategy and a sense of urgency that produced successful executive search results. Searches she has conducted include positions as CEO, Board Member, COO, CFO, and Vice Presidents across the functions in large and emerging companies largely in high technology. Wanda was Director of the High Technology Practice for Rusher, Loscavio & LoPresto then later co-founded ExecutiveQuest.

Also an accomplished Career Transition Consultant, Wanda worked with Right Management Consultants and Spherion delivering dynamic workshops and coaching individuals for successful career transition.

Wanda's earlier careers have been as a University Professor and Academic Administrator in Europe and the U.S. and as an HR Director.
