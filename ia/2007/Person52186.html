Louis Delage’s Summary

A marketing communications specialist
Over 25 years of experience (both agency and client sides) developing marketing communications plans and implementing strategies using various communication vehicles (advertising, public relations, digital communications, relational marketing, graphic communications, etc.) to achieve the desired results.

A Background focused on planification
A background (particularly on the agency side) that has enabled him to develop an approach based on reflection prior to action. In accordance with the desired objectives, this has allowed him to concentrate on the selection of the best strategies, to identify the most effective means, and to determine the optimal evaluation measures.

A passion for the Web and social media and their impact on the practice of marketing communications
Since 1995, he has shown an avid interest in new Web-based media and their communications potential. This passion has drawn his attention to organizations that promote the use of these media, such as the Interactive Advertising Bureau of Montréal and the Montréal Marketing Association’s Social Media Group.

A seasoned manager interested in the impact of the convergence of new technologies
Communication is principally a team affair. While working with Cossette, he has learned to manage and mobilize matrix teams working on a variety of different projects. In 1995, he became associate manager of a Cossette subsidiary. After leaving that agency, he continued to manage and contribute to the development of various teams, including Loto-Québec’s digital communications group. Having accumulated close to 10 years of managerial experience, he has developed a particular interest in the convergence of new technologies and their impact on society.
