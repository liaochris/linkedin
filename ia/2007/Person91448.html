Suhit Anantula’s Summary

Suhit is a "Environmental Project Officer" a the Department for Families and Communities in the Government of South Australia. He is recent MBA graduate from the International Graduate School of Business in Australia.

Suhit is a contributing author on Micro-Finance to the book WorldChanging.

Suhit has co-founded www.bpodigest.com, India's first interactive vortal on the outsourcing industry which was launched by the CM of AP, YS Raja Shekara Reddy. It caters exclusively to the outsourcing community providing news, information, knowledge, blogs, forums and jobs.

Suhit’s recent assignment as a Project Manager with Deeshaa Ventures (www.deeshaa.com) involved working with the RISC core team members in helping formulate the business strategies and evangelizing the idea and has also created a community of interested members (~350) though an online community.

Suhit has been part of the core group behind the Tsunami Help website (htt://tsunamihelp.blogspot.com) which provided critical information in the Dec, 2004 Tsunami and was covered by major newspapers magazines all around the world.

Prior to joining Deeshaa he worked as a Senior Analyst at ADP India, a company that focuses on providing mission-critical software and Business Process Outsourcing services to Investment Banks. His work at ADP involved managing and migrating data sensitive projects from the US to India supporting the big Investment banks in the US. He has led teams of up to 15 people and managed projects worth $500,000.

Suhit has a very strong interest in economics, technology and development and maintains a weblog chronicling this interest at www.worldisgreen.com. The weblog was nominated as the “Best IndiBlog of the Year” for 2004.

Suhit is a featured writer for Oh! My News International where he writes on various topics.

He lives with his wife, Bindu Madhavi, in Adelaide, Australia.
