David P. Martin’s Summary

Presently, David is contributing pro bono as the managing editor of PULSE Magazine which is published by GMAD (Gay Men of African Descent, gmad.org), a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization. PULSE disseminates informative content for free to address pertinent issues within Black Gay America, in an effort to educate, heal, enlighten, and build a stronger understanding of and among this community.

Formerly, managed LaDuca Shoes (laducashoes.com), a dance and fashion footwear manufacturer, retailer, and supplier to stage and film productions as well as performing artists.

David was the Director of Membership for In The Life Media (itltv.org), a nonprofit educational corporation and producer of In the Life, the award-winning LGBT newsmagazine broadcast. He also held other positions in fundraising, communications & public affairs, web development, community relations, and volunteer management at In The Life Media. He remains a consultant on special projects.

Before ITLM, he was the assistant to the fiscal director, acting as a development associate and general manager of productions at the National Black Theatre, Inc. (nationalblacktheatre.org) in Harlem, New York.

An extensive theater arts background has provided him opportunities to work with for-profit and non-profit organizations.As a producer, David continues to make contributions to the theater/fine arts community through organizations such as Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS (broadwaycares.org), The Actor’s Fund of America (actorsfund.org), Rejoicensemble (rejoicensemble.org), the Brooklyn Museum (brooklynmuseum.org), and American Film & Stage, to name a few.

David continues work in development, management, and consulting on special events and projects. He resides in the greater New York Metropolitan area.
