Dale Rockell’s Summary

Every child matters! That’s why I developed the unique Stress-Free-Kids™ coaching programme. This provides students with invaluable knowledge, tools and skills to proactively support a lifetime of personal success, achievement and positive contribution to society.

Stress-Free-Kids™ is as a weekly coaching programme that provides students with insights into the importance of personal responsibility, understanding and coping with stress, learning from role models and understanding change. It was successfully piloted during the Summer Term of 2007 in partnership with Waltham Holy Cross Junior School, Waltham Abbey. Teaching staff also find the sessions of great benefit and the programme continues to generate significant interest.

“How many [students] do you know that can boast their own life coach?”
Diane Stygal, Headmistress, Waltham Holy Cross Junior School

Stress-Free-Kids™ perfectly supports the ever increasing demands upon teaching staff to address “emotional intelligence” and “the secrets of happiness*”, fully complements the “Every Child Matters” Agenda, PSHE, Citizenship, SEAL initiatives and the Ofsted Agenda for “Evaluating ECM Outcomes”

“With the development of personal resilience currently at the top of the educational agenda, the Stress-Free-Kids™ coaching programme is timely support for teaching staff, providing young people with essential knowledge and tools for a lifetime of personal success and achievement in an increasingly demanding world.”
Anon, Head Teacher
