Wayland Quintero’s Summary

Twenty years experience with eighteen years in New York City in the theater arts as a creative and performing artist touring across the USA and overseas, director/choreographer, writer, administrator, and manager of my own performance and touring company. Reviewed in the New York Times, Village Voice, and other publications. In addition to my arts and administrative experience I am part of the interfaith program at the New Seminary, NYC, as part of a process of integrating my arts background and experience with the ongoing journey of spiritual inquiry and practice. I am interested in networking and collaborating with artists, entrepeneurs, filmmakers and those interested in inclusivism rather than dogmatic exclusivism and ideological extremism. As a soon-to-be ordained interfaith minister I seek to serve those transitioning with end-of-life issues, as well as those who want to have memorable and meaningful ceremonies for themselves from one's own inspirations, individual preferences, and if desired- fusing traditional elements into the whole whether it is a wedding or other life transition.

Wayland Quintero’s Specialties:

Creating and directing theatrical presentations, collaborating with as well as leading and managing groups, managing short-term production projects, assisting with administering non-profit organizations, performing (acting, dance, music), public speaking, conference multimedia presentations, researching and writing on cultural issues, creating a wedding ceremony, certified volunteer with Continuum Hospice Care.
