Maia Duerr’s Summary

With over a decade of experience in nonprofit organizations, I mix a unique set of ingredients into my professional 'stew.' I have academic and field training as a cultural anthropologist, and I can offer perceptive insights into organizational culture and change strategies. I've conducted research on companies that use contemplative practices as a vehicle for cultivating meaningful work and sustainable work environments, and have published several journal articles on this topic.

I have a love for language and for helping groups and individuals discern their mission and message, and developing strategies to get their word out.

And finally, I have a passion for making the world a better place, and am committed to help others become agents of change in both nonprofit and corporate settings.

Maia Duerr’s Specialties:

Qualitative research and analysis; strategic communications and message development for non-profit organizations and others; fundraising and development; bringing mindfulness and other reflective practices into the work setting.
