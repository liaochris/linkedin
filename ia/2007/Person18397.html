Don Leahy’s Summary

Don Leahy has been working on web based projects for almost 10 years and is now an acknowledged Irish expert in 'Web 2.0' site, social networking & online communities.

Despite winning the Irish Internet Association's award in November 2005 for 'Internet Marketer of the Year 2005', Don has continued to passionately keep up to date with the fast changing eWorld.

Through his alter-ego CONMAN who resides on CONMAN.tv, Don has quietly built profiles and expertise on sites such as MySpace.com, Bebo.com, YouTube.com, Flickr.com, LinkedIn.com, Del.icio.us, Digg.com, wikipedia.org & stumbleupon.com.

Don also has e-commrce experience selling T-shirts on sites like CafePress.com, eBay.com & using PayPal.com.

As a creative entrepreneurial eMarketer, Don is also adept at identifying the all important marketing & business opportunites that this knowledge of the onlinescape provides.
