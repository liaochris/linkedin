Prakash Muralidharan(prakash_murali @ yahoo.com)’s Summary

Prakash has almost nine years of software delivery experience with increasing levels of responsibility spanning client management, project and program management, business analysis and bid management. He is passionate about new technology, outsourcing and emerging trends and it's impact on the software services business.
