Todd Albertson’s Summary

I am an author, speaker, and entrepreneur. What that means, I'm not entirely sure, but at the end of the day I love start-up and empowering people to make a difference.

I am the author of two books: THE GODS OF BUSINESS: THE INTERSECTION OF FAITH AND THE MARKETPLACE and the forthcoming DOING BUSINESS THE CHRISTIAN WAY.
