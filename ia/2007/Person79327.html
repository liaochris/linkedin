Shiloh Hagen’s Summary

Joined The Cox Team in May of 2002. I add to the level of professionalism and excellence of The Cox Team with my technological savvy, attention to detail & top-notch work ethic. I enjoy working with Marketing Director, Cameron Cox, to create the most effective, eye-catching and cutting-edge digital/print media.

Constantly pouring through the listing inventory as well as previewing homes in person gives me an advantage both in determining value for new listings and in narrowing down possible matches for buyers who do not have the time to look at every home in the valley.

I grew up in San Diego where I frequented the beach and developed my love for the ocean. After graduating from UCLA with a degree in Business Economics, I pursued a call to youth ministry. In 1998 I moved to the San Francisco peninsula and served as High School/Junior High Youth Pastor for Western Hills Church in San Mateo where I spent a few years investing in the leaders of tomorrow. When that call was complete my wife and I moved to the East Bay where we purchased our first home in Dublin.

When I have free time I enjoy working on my car, playing basketball, running, snowboarding and riding waves. Most of all, I enjoy spending time with my wife and college sweetheart (and co-worker), Wendy.
