Kim DeCoste’s Summary

I have launched my own Executive and Technical Search Firm, DeCoste & Associates. The company has a back-to-basics and highly personalized approach to recruitment and search. We are focused on industries such as distance education, training and healthcare, where technology is transforming the way work is done. If you have skills in these areas, please get in touch with us to see how we can help you move your career or your business forward.

Previously I worked for Jones Knowledge as a creator and the implementor of the JIU Total Professional, the University's Career Service offerings. I have a solid understanding of how technology can be leveraged for highly individualized professional development.I also earned my MBA at JIU in 2005 in e-Commerce while working in student recruitment.

Previously I was a Senior Recruiter in Information Technology in Denver and in the Los Angeles area.

My long-term professional interests remain focused on career and employment development, professional services, strategic management and professional networking.
