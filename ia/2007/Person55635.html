Martin Burns’s Summary

Accomplished recruiter with proven experience across a range of disciplines. Particular strength in understanding which people match which cultures, and making these connections. Placements that last, and add both immediate and long-term value.

My clients have ranged from Fortune 100's to small start-ups. Industries I have serviced inlude: software, pharma, biotech, financial services, employee benefits, and entertainment.

Current role: Recruiting Manager at ZoomInfo, which is a fairly cool thing (at least in geeky recruiting circles). We're hiring, btw.

If you're interested in work here, or advice or career search in general, I'd love to help. I'm especially interested in people with raw intellectual horsepower that know how to communicate an idea, as well as how to implement it.
