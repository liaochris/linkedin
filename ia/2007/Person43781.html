Jon Williams’s Summary

Jon is the CTO at Kaplan Test Prep and Admissions, a division of Kaplan, a Washington Post Company (NYSE: WPO).

Jon's past experience as a technology executive include Time Warner, Scholastic, Oxygen Media, News Corporation, Grey Healthcare, CDnow and 24/7 Media.

Jon was born in Australia, and moved permanently to the USA in 1986. He is a member of InfoWorld magazine's CTO advisory council, and is the co-founder of the New York CTO club, a four-year-old group of CTOs who meet and collaborate on a regular basis.

Jon Williams’s Specialties:

Software development, technology architecture, project/process management, e-commerce, business process automation, technology strategies. Extensive in both Unix/Java and Windows platforms.
