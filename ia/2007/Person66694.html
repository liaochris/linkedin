Paul Meyers’s Summary

Acme Mobile is one of Asia's largest B2B distributors of world-class mobile entertainment products and services.

Acme offers games, images, music products and Java Karaokes to carriers, aggregators and portals. Our reach extends to over 25 countries in Asia, Europe and the Middle East, covering over 300 million mobile subscribers.

Acme also provides Asian-focused consulting and market entry strategies to content, payment and technology companies.
