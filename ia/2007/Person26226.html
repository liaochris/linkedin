Eric Poulin’s Summary

Current Systems Administrator at Bergmeyer Associates, Inc. with a Bachelor's of Architecture from Roger Williams University in Rhode Island. After graduating in 2001 I started working for Revit, Inc. (before they were bought by Autodesk, this was version 3.X to 4.X) as a Client Support Architect helping new firms get up and running in the software and also serving as tech support to existing clients.

After Revit, I started at Koetter Kim & Associates as the sole IT professional and also helping with projects on an as needed basis. When I started at KKA, most of the workstations and the server were older Macintosh computers. When I left KKA, the server had been transitioned to a new Dell PowerEdge machine and 90% of the workstations were Windows based Dell Precision workstations with AutoCAD and Vectorworks.

At Bergmeyer, I am serving as the Systems Administrator in a team of three IT staff (including the Director of Information Technology). My responsibilities include daily operation and maintenance of all the server systems, the network, desktop workstations, and providing tech support for both IT issues and jointly for issues arising with all software applications including architectural software. Additionally, I provide training to new users and for the office as a whole on many of the applications that we use in the office including the Adobe Creative Suite, Deltek Vision, Phone System, Microsoft Office & Windows.

I am also about to begin the "Masters of Science in Information Assurance" (MSIA) program from Norwich University. As I proceed in my career, I am looking forward to moving up into a management position where I will be responsible for the IT direction in a medium sized company and also directly managing and mentoring other IT staff members.
