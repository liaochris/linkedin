Andrew Inman’s Summary

* 20+ years experience in the IT industry across a range of managerial, sales and marketing, technical and systems architecture roles.

* Founding director of Vision 4 (Gemini Affinitas) and the original architect of their "Gemini" service management / CRM software.

* Co-architect of the successfully patented "MediKey" medical records concept.

* Entrepreneur and visionary, positive and lateral thinker.

I commenced and rapidly progressed through a career in PC systems distribution/VAR in the mid to late 80s, successfully founded and ran a software/consulting house in the 90s and gradually became freelance from around 2000 onwards. I grew my company Vision 4 (Gemini Affinitas) from zero to around 15 people (1990-1998) before bringing in an expert management team who would take the company through VC input to flotation. Well, that didn't happen - this was 1999-2001 - the dotcom crash and 9/11 - Gemini Affinitas survived but was eventually sold during 2003-4 for a relatively small sum (around half a million pounds.)

I'm currently interested in senior positions, probably with software developers, either on a consulting or permanent basis. I am particularly interested in SMEs (including startups.) I have a range of skills and qualities, especially creativity and foresight. I would probably pitch myself at Managing or Commercial Director in a smaller company, and I can handle other functional areas. I understand sales, software development and customer support, and have a good general appreciation for finance and operations.

Andrew Inman’s Specialties:

CRM, VAR and distribution, SME, startup, software development, sales and marketing.

    Vision 4 was one of the pioneers of Customer Service / CRM software in the UK during the 90s. Founded in 1990, they were renamed Gemini Affinitas in 1999 shortly after I had invited Mike Ward to take over as MD. The company was sold during 2003-4 - see: http://www.crmandcontactcentre247.com/Call_Centre_Consolidation/Article402436.aspx

The product became acclaimed by users for its flexibility, easy of use, ease of implementation and short term ROI.

    During my time with the company I set up the sales, support and development teams and grew the company from zero to around fifteen employees. I disinvolved myself during 1998-9 to explore new opportunities.
