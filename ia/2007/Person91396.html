Marko Wolf-Pany’s Summary

Systems / Software Engineer
Professional Engineer (P.Eng.), Ontario

Specializing in Software Engineering / Information Technology (IT) Program, Project, and Process Improvement Management, (i.e. Re-Engineering the Software / Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) Management) in order to deliver reliable, maintainable, and portable software systems, in scope, on time and within budget, based on the:
