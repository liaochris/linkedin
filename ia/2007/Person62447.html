Natacha Quester-Semeon’s Summary

Journalist, author, producer and hypermedia project manager, she works in the press, TV, and the Internet since 1994 (popular press and Web : Sciences et Avenir, Nouvel Observateur group, Télérama, Le Monde...). Senior partner in the company I-Marginal for creation of contents and Web sites, specialized in scientific and technical culture (Cite des Sciences, CNRS, France Télévision...). She takes an active part in citizens initiatives. Co-administrator of the humanist associative network "The Associated Humans" (Les Humains Associés), through a virtual community since 1995 (three Web sites, forum, mailing list, blog, etc.).
Co-founder of the Parisian blog "Mémoire Vive" created in 2003 (blog-podcast-phoneblog-photoblog, vidblog…)

Co-inventor of the movement "wifi pique-nique" (wifipicnic.com), surfers meetings in a ephemeral local wifi network (wifi bubble), she is interested — to make a long story short — with what is close to human and social networks.
