Why would someone with this background and experience operate a fishing lodge and act as a consultant? I'm glad you asked. Let me tell you a bit about these interests and you can answer the question yourself...

ILLAHE LODGE

The Illahe Lodge is my family's third generation business. The lodge was built by my great-grandparents to be their retirement home. Then, they discovered just how many friends they had... Grandpa Ernie and Grandma Florence had to either stop letting friends stay with them or start charging their friends. And, a business was born.

Today, guests come to the Illahe Lodge to relax, build relationships, and enjoy one of the most beautiful, rugged, and remote parts of the United States. The Illahe Lodge is located just outside the Wild Section of the Rogue National Wild and Scenic River. Guests can come here by road, boat, trail, or air (our front lawn has served as a helipad). While here, they enjoy the benefits of public power and landline access. Guests can participate in a variety of activities including walking, backpacking, running, rafting, fishing, boatriding, birdwatching, watching wildlife, and sightseeing excursions.

CONSULTING

On the consulting side, I help people negotiate contracts, build relationships with customers and suppliers, and strengthen their practical business skills.
