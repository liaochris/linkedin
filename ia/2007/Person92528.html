Zachary Lynde’s Summary

• Chief Financial Officer/General Partner for TriFusion, LP – a technology services company offering managed deployment to its clients who are purchasing and managing large technology initiatives.
• Principal and CEO for The Zachary Group – a technology consulting firm in Austin, Texas specializing in early stage and growth companies launching new initiatives or acquisitions. Our specialty is figuring out if companies are getting rich quickly or going broke slowly because the only difference is in scaling startup costs to return over recovered fixed costs, better known as profit.
• Chief Financial Officer for American YouthWorks, an Austin based education company, with $6.5 million in annual revenues and $9 million in assets. Improved EBITDA by over 25% and reduced debt by 45%. Established detail budgets for 12 divisions, trained managers, and customized new reports for distributed budgeting.
• Chief Operating Officer for Logical Approach, a V.A.R. of accounting software (Protrax) for Architects, Engineers and Design/Build firms with offices in Denver, Albuquerque, and Chicago. Designed a 500-point methodology for system conversion and minimal consultant interface to maximize revenues and reduce re-work costs to contain expenses and reduce variances in all projects by using systems. Within one year, revenues improved by over 50% and EBITDA improved by 75%.

• Management Consultant with KPMG, working for 5 years to develop, sell and implement accounting systems for large state and federal agencies, i.e. NASA. Projects ranged from $8 to $100 million and 3 to 16 years in duration.
• Auditor for the State Auditor’s Office of Texas performing financial and performance audits. Wrote the first video audit report with the GAO.
