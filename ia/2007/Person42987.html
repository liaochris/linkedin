John Kingston’s Summary

"The one who succeeds in resolving issues is the one who resolves them before they occur." (Sun Tzu around 23 centuries ago)

Beta Testing is a modern mechanism allowing effective issue resolution.

Effective Beta Test Management means providing customer information as early as possible in the development process, field simulation provides a solution to this challenge.

Some good Key Performance Indicators are:

- % of simulated field features tested.
- Beta Testers' Responsiveness Level in % of questions answered
- Amount of Time To Market saved
- Product Transportation savings (simulation can help this)
- Engineering & Marketing acknowledgement ratio in % of feedback acted upon.

I explore techniques & processes to continuously improve all above indicators.

I currently have performed Beta Testing with Austrian, Belgium, Dutch, English, Finnish, French, German, Greek, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Korean, North American, Norwegian, Portuguese, Swedish & Swiss Beta Testers.

I have hands-down experience with the following test "field vehicles" : 4 wheel vehicles, bikes, boats, snowmobiles, haversacks as well as the more common PC.

I've also managed tests in different climate and ground conditions from the standard business office to marine, mountain and desert locations.
