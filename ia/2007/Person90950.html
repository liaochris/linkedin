Wilco van Bragt’s Summary

Consultant with primary focus on the Server Based Computing technologies with as specialization design and implementations.

Besides the traditional SBC products from Microsoft and Citrix I'm also interesed in other full SBC products like Provision Networks, Ericom, Jetro and more.

Also additional third party products like Appsense, Powerfuse, Profile and Print products are in my field of interest and experiences.

Besides my traditional work I'm also founder and author of VanBragt.Net SBC Centre (http://sbc.vanbragt.net) where I publish reviews of SBC related products and SBC related articles. I also presented at the BriForum 2007 US in Chicago.

Articles and reviews written by me are also published on www.brianmadden.com, www.msterminalservices.org and www.dabcc.com.
