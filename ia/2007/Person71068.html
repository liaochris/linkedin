Jim Rathe’s Summary

As President of Business Development Advisors, Inc. and Business Development Advisor for ThomasNet.com, Mr. Rathe is responsible for the overall strategic direction of more than 65 manufacturers, wholesale distributors, and industrial service companies all over the globe.

These local, national, and global B to B firms range in size from 1 to 750 Million in gross sales.

Prior to the most recent role, Mr. Rathe was responsible for a 40% growth in sales and doubling the profit margin of one of the largest package boiler suppliers’ accessory sales division. Clients include Ford Motor Company, Lockheed Martin, Shell Oil Company, Huntsman Petrochemical, Weyerhaeuser, among others across 12 different countries.

Mr. Rathe moved into the post secondary private education sector to assist adults making career changes to ignite their calling. He led Universal Technical Institute / Motorcycle Mechanics Institute admissions teams for 3 years by assisting nearly 900 adults to get into a career with companies like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, AUDI, PORSCHE, VOLVO, VW, NASCAR, Ford Motor Company, among others.

Mr. Rathe then became partner of a global Merger & Acquisition firm, Empire Business Brokers, serving the small and medium size closely held private firms. He successfully transitioned ownership of 18 privately held firms in a 2 year time frame.

Recognizing that many of these firms didn’t need to sell, but could thrive with strategic direction and a business development strategy, Mr. Rathe founded Business Development Advisors, Inc. to strategically drive their business to increased profitable new business. Many of Mr. Rathe’s clients have experienced significant measurable gains through the successful implementation of custom tailored strategies developed in conjunction with Mr. Rathe’s expertise, strategic alliance partners, and understanding the needs of each unique business leader.
