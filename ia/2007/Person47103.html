Kelly Maccioli’s Summary

I graduated from Colby-Sawyer College in 2003 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design. Following graduation I worked for Michael Quinn Productions doing paste-up for ad books. And since leaving there in 2003 I have been working for Classic Graphx (formerly Classic Copy & Printing) as the sole designer on the Harvard University account, and keeping busy with freelance work on the side. Currently I am working on building a larger customer base for my freelance work, hoping to someday be working for my own small business Kelly Macc Graphic Design.

Kelly Maccioli’s Specialties:

Pre-press, Invitations and logo design
