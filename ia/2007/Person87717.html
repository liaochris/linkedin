Todd C. Roberts’s Summary

Producer, consultant and creative director for various innovative entertainment projects over the last 15 years, I have proven track record as a successful taste-maker and trend-setter. Since 1990, I have managed several medium-sized creative and business units, consulted for many large companies and developed a reputation for developing quality products and successful campaigns. Past clients have included Def Jam Mobile, CIM Real Estate Group, Signatures Network, Verve Records, Res Media Group and Napster.

Currently, we represent various artist properties to create unique 'collaborative license' opportunities for brands to communicate with their target market. Truant Media works closely with new & established entertainment artists — bands, filmmakers, designers — to create innovative products carefully tailored to each artist and brand association.
