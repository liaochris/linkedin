George Arriola’s Summary

Leveraging over a decade of experience and expertise in brand and user-centered product design, George works within research and development on emerging graphics technologies and interactive experiences, enabling holistic solutions for consumer portable electronic devices, HD video, mobile, web, graphic, environmental / architectural design disciplines.

Prior to Apple, George spent the past 7 years with Adobe, where he was one of the lead Adobe Creative Suite architects within product management enabling mobile and Web customer workflow's across Adobe's diverse product portfolio.

George has also worked at WIRED Magazine (Japan), and Macromedia. While with Macromedia, George was one of the first Japanese Product Managers and an original founding member of Macromedia K.K. (Japan).

Living in SoMa/Potrero Hill, George keeps involved in the local and global design community through active participation in AIGA events and through conference and seminar speaking engagements about design, user experience, convergence and the future of content.
