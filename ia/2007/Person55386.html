Markus Räipiö’s Summary

From www.arnora.com Management page:

Räipiö, Markus - Senior Partner
Arnora Management Team Member since 2006

Born: 1973
Education: B.Sc. Tech. (Electrical Automation Engineering: telecommunications and programming)
Responsibilities: Asian markets, internationalization, sparring

Mr. Räipiö has brought to Arnora over 10 years of experience from technical and business positions within MNC-sized telecommunications and networking companies as well as from SME-sized security software companies. He is skilled in selling software and service solutions to large corporations. He has augmented his business development and corporate strategy planning competencies to executive level through both field experiences and management training. He has also taken part to a strategic merge of companies and been a member of board and adviser for multiple companies.

Markus Räipiö’s Specialties:

Mr. Räipiö has personally managed a start-up from writing a business plan (including technology strategy) to fully working company and raised investor and governmental funding. Mr. Räipiö is an experienced business negotiator and presentation performer. Successful management of technology partner networks is very familiar to him. He has also worked as an investment technology consultant for multiple venture capital companies in Europe.
