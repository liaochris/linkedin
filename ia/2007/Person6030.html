Andy Waroma’s Summary

I have worked more than 10 years with SAP applications and SAP's customers out of which last 7 years at the regional SAP Asia-Pacific headquarters in Singapore. My current role is Director - Global Ecosystem and Partner Group Asia Pacific Japan. In my previous role I was heading the SAP SRM Business Development team in Asia-Pacific and prior to that I worked as a consultant with SAP's procurement & sourcing applications and marketplaces. I started my SAP career with Cap Gemini in Finland as an ERP consultant. I've gained experience in working in almost all the Asia-Pacific countries and prior to that in Europe. As part of my tasks I've been working with multi-cultural teams, leading projects and business development teams involving various countries (India, China, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Philippines, Hong Kong, China, Taiwan, Korea, Australia, New Zealand and Japan) and industries (Telecom, Oil and Gas, Automotive, High Tech, Fast Moving Consumer Goods, Aerospace and Defense, Utilities, Retail and Public Sector). I see myself as a result oriented individual who thrives on a challenge and lives by the customer satisfaction. I welcome new contacts through LinkedIn or otherwise.
