Trude Johansson’s Summary

Extensive experience in political work, both nationally in Norway, and internationally. Have been working with different political aspects of conflict situations as well as been extensively involved in the development of the political arena in the Eastern European Countries since 1994. Have worked with democracy-building and analysis for more than ten years, as well as with report, analyzis and assessment writing, relating to several countries, among them all of the Balkans, Belarus and Sri Lanka. Work with development of larger trend analysis reports, presentations to the international community, coordination of reporting systems, development of complaints databases and statistical systems of organizations and coordinating security plan development. Work with conflict resolution and negotiation, and give courses in the same, as well as in organizational management, democratic and civil society development, public speaking, speech writing and presentation. Have extensive experience in public speaking, giving presentations on both current and historical aspects of political situations and development, and in working with child and gender policy in developing countries and countries in conflict.
