Mike Briercliffe’s Summary

I help IT related businesses - sometimes to exist in the first place - and always to focus and grow.

I base my work on long term vision, market understanding, personal connections, coaching, mentoring and where appropriate, (increasingly rarely!) executive intervention. Based in Leeds with active involvements throughout the UK and Europe, with occasional projects in USA and Canada.

The market knows me best as a specialist advisor for the UK ICT Industry, in particular for marketing & channels to market, where I have had the privilege of working with the Vendor and VAR community for the past 30 years or so.

I love to see people starting their own businesses & have helped scores of companies to start up during my career. Some of them have reached stardom & a goodly proportion of them are simply alive and well. I fundamentally believe in social networking as a platform for business growth.

I am a frequent speaker at and organiser of conferences in ICT and related fields. I specialise in facilitating expert panel sessions on a wide range of subjects in my specialist fields. These include mentoring & start-ups, trade press relations, channels to market, social networking and the marketing of emerging technologies.

I'm a very keen skier and a not-so-keen moderate golfer. I have a recent love of sailing big boats. In each of these sports my goal is to justify a single figure handicap rating. Skiing is my nearest to be achieved and sustained.

PET HATE:
I can't abide those who use LinkedIn to generate pseudo connections. e.g. entering "Technology" for somewhere you used to work and claiming to be a past associate of mine demeans the principle of this excellent medium. (This is a practice often used by out-sourcers from the Indian sub-continent). Please don't do it!

If however you have sought me out because of a particular matter (or conversely I have done that with you) that's within the reasonable tolerances of the spirit of what this is about.
