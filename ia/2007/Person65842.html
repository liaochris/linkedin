Paolo Benzan’s Summary

I have spent the past six years in senior management roles, most recently as the Vice President of Product Marketing for Tickets.com.
Principal responsibilities included:

• Alignment of our customer needs across our products and services.
• Working with internal product managers and product development leads to ensure that marketing requirements were accurately captured and relayed to product teams.
• Prioritizing marketing requirements and aligning with product roadmaps and product development schedules.
• Planning and executing regional focus groups across the United Sates.
• Conducting on-site visits with premier clients spanning the spectrum of the verticals we served. This included the St. Louis Cardinals, San Francisco Giants, and Carnegie Hall.

In my tenure with Resolute Solutions Corporation, a Microsoft consulting company focused on the upper mid market segment, I held several management roles including General Manager for the Pacific Northwest, Principal Consultant for CRM solutions, and Managing Director of Sports & Entertainment. In these roles I supervised a staff of 40+ consultants and worked with many organizations on complex data integrations for key business systems. I have extensive experience with enterprise and mid-market CRM and business solutions with 4+ years of consulting in this space.

My career started in application development where I spent 7+ years in product development roles as a software engineer developing Microsoft Windows applications using MFC and Visual C++. I transitioned into an integration and middleware specialist with the advent of Microsoft BizTalk Server and worked extensively with Microsoft SQL server on complex multi-system integrations. I have worked in software engineering and consulting roles across many verticals and have worked in small, medium, and large companies.
