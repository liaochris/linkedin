Dave Blackwell’s Summary

A 10 year consulting career that has seen exposure to more than 45 companies in Europe, North America, Australia & Southeast Asia, Dave has worked at all levels of management & is experienced & flexible enough to be as credible in the Board Room as he is with front-line Supervisors.

With typical engagement in Process Change, Business Processing Re-engineering or other Productivity-related Projects, Dave has been as comfortable in the analysis role as he has been in delivering operational changes.

He is an extremely capable trainer and can deliver seminars, workshops & individual coaching at all management levels. He has also designed & copywritten several very unique team-building events that are both effective & memorible.

He is an active CIPD Member & has just completed an assignment as Treasurer for the UKs branch of the American Society of Training & Development (ASTD)

Dave Blackwell’s Specialties:

Team Building Events, Executive Team Alignment, Competency Delivery, Customer Service Development, Keynote Speaking, Belbin Accredited, Action Learning Programs, Cultural Change, Behaviour Change, Training Interventions, Communications Skills, Coaching On The Floor & Follow Up
