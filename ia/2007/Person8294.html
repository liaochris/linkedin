Ashu Joshi’s Summary

16+ years of experience in technology leadership, management and development. Cross platform skills in Engineering Management, Business Development & Product Management. Extensive experience in customer-facing roles - technical marketing, strategic selling/positioning, and support. Managed and played pivotal roles in acquiring customers with total revenues in excess of $350m over the span of my career.
Proven ability to translate highly complex engineering and technical concepts into succinct customer value propositions and execution plans. Developed market & customer collateral as a part of design-win strategy and requirements for more than 20 projects with time & cost ranging from a 2 months ($50k) to 2 years ($10m). Significant expertise in competitive analysis, cost & solution analysis to build customer acquisition tool-kit.
Well experienced in financial valuation, due diligence for venture investments and M&A: hands-on experience on more than 3 deals. Directly responsible for raising venture funding in excess of $50m from 2001 through 2004 while at iVivity, Inc.
Proven expertise in building and running teams locally & globally. Successfully built, managed and led teams’ sizes ranging from 5 to 125+ in global locations such as Norcross (GA) & Fremont (CA) in USA; Chennai & Bangalore, India; Taipei, Taiwan and Hong Kong, China. Demonstrated ability to work with and tap into the diverse expertise of the organization.
