Dan Leyland’s Summary

Daniel started his professional career in an entrepreneurial manner, starting a sports consultancy firm whilst at University and servicing clients including many of the FTSE 100. A Virgin branded company then approached Daniel to undertake a turnaround project for their consumer leisure business. He more than doubled the expected results, and in half of the time specified before devising and project managing the company’s growth strategy, and after recruiting and training a successor, Daniel moved on to take up a role for a multi-national consumer business, where he was responsible for leading commercialisation of a new product.

By using a consistent process, Daniel has delivered great results in every role by recognising that the consumer or customer should be the driving force behind strategy, and by exploiting the opportunities that arise by delivering what the market wants and creating an “unfair competitive advantage”
