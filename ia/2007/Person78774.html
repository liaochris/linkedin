Shane Lundy’s Summary

I was recently promoted to the Executive team as the VP of Product Strategy. My previous role was the Vice President of Business Development for AWS Convergence Technologies Inc (WeatherBug), an Internet technology, sensor networking and media company that serves the public and private sectors. I am also the Co-founder of Artemis Development Corporation, a real estate development company in Costa Rica focusing on raw land and urbanization projects. Prior to AWS I was the VP of Bus Dev for a small Internet startup, Net2itive, a Redmond Wa company that did not survive the bubble burst. Prior to jumping into the Internet, I founded Lundy’s Tennis Services and ran a profitable business for 15 years in the tennis industry that focused on facility management, retail, repair services, professional placement, travel, lessons and camps.
