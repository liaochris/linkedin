Chris McCoy’s Summary

I'm 24. I started my business 5 years ago in dorm rooms and coffehouses at the Univ. of Washington in Seattle. I now work in a garage with 35 others. We're funded, have true competitive advantage, and are gaining momentum with Project 209 daily. I love what I do. And believe my team and I can do it.

I think sports can change the world. I'm doing everything I can, with all my effort and with the best people and technology I know, to bring this vision to life.
