Michael Mayes’s Summary

Michael Mayes is the Head of European Council Operations with Gerson Lehrman Group (GLG), a private information and business services organization, focused on technologies and services enabling global expert networks. Michael is responsible for the growth and management of the European GLG Councils.

Previously, Michael was a Senior Product Manager with Gerson Lehrman Group, responsible for product design of the GLG Councils website and oversight of the recruiting and retention operations for the 150,000+ member expert network. Additionally, he created Circaviso, a design consultancy focused on delivering modern and innovative brand, product design, and architecture creations.

Before GLG, Michael was a Senior Manager of Marketing Operations for TIAA-CREF, where he designed and built a project management office and developed and managed the Process Improvement strategy for all of TIAA-CREF marketing.

Prior to that, Michael served as Senior Manager, Solicitation Design, Capital One Financial, in Richmond, VA. Mayes held numerous management and business development positions with Capital One from 1999 – 2004, where he helped reduce speed-to-market, implemented creative effectiveness tracking, and redesigned multiple customer acquisition and retention procedures. He has training in Six Sigma, Process Redesign and Project Management. Michael received his BS in Architecture from the University of Virginia and studied abroad with the Art Institute of Florence, Italy.
