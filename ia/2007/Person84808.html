Teri Robnett’s Summary

I'm a natural connector. I enjoy bringing people together, introducing friends, clients and colleagues, developing strategic partnerships, and watching the magic happen.

The Internet holds enormous power to introduce people who might not be connected otherwise, a power I have seen and believed in from the beginning. I remember a lengthy conversation in 1999 with a professor at Regis University about how excited I was to be involved in the Internet because of its power to connect, a power so strong I could feel it. This is what gets me jazzed; this is what feeds me; this is why I love working with the Internet as Cerebral Cortex & CEO of WholeBrainTech.com.

My alter-ego manages a business in a developing industry - www.MileHighPedicabs.com and www.BigTreePedicab.com - and provides consulting and training on operating a successful pedicab (rickshaw, bike cab) business. I have tons of fun woking in this trendy industry that's exploding around the country.

My newest endeavor combines both advertising and pedicabs - www.SmartAdsMedia.com. Businesses are looking for new ways to get their message out, and advertising on a pedicab can be exactly the vehicle to take. Often called "ambush marketing," pedicabs can reach the target market when no other means can. With the relationships I've built with pedicab companies around the world, I can get your message on a pedicab in nearly any major city you might consider.

Educating and consulting play a big part in all my business endeavors. I enjoy teaching people about what I do, and exploring ways to make their business better. You can find some of my tips and ideas on my blogs at wholebraintech.blogspot.com and pedicabblog.blogspot.com.

e-Strategy and online marketing, web design and development, Constant Contact email marketing, writing, project planning, public speaking, networking, pedicabs. I'm great at consulting and educating people interested in these areas.
