Anand Jayaraman, PhD , CAIA’s Summary

After 6 years of experience in academic research in nonlinear dynamics, chaos theory, fluid mechanics and mathematical physics, I have started focussing on using mathematical methods in financial forecasting. I have developed (and currently trade) quantitative strategies that make exploit short-term market inefficiencies. The aim of the strategies is to be general enough to be equally profitable in all 4 major sectors (equities, commodities, currencies and interest rates).

Profile:
- PhD in Physics
- CAIA charter holder
- 6 years of experience in Nonlinear dynamics, computational physics, fluid dynamics leading to publication of several peer reviewed research papers

- Volunteered time at Association of India's Development - a non-profit organization that focusses on all-round development in Indian villages
- Volunteered time to teach science to 3rd graders on a weekly basis
