Joel Abraham’s Summary

Reach me at joela@wisechoiceit.com or check out our website www.wisechoiceit.com

CHECK OUT MY NEW BLOG WWW.TALENTMINING.COM It contains Talent Acquisition advice/info and art projects that I have been working on.

Joel Abraham jabraham1822@charter.net has over 15 years of experience in talent acquisitions & utilization in the High Tech Industry. His expertise lies in Business Development, Recruiting, Human Capital Analysis and Candidate Pipeline & Cultivation Development.

In 1999 Joel was a cofounder of the WI Technical Recruiters Network, and he served as President for 2000-02.

In 2000, Joel started a Midwest division of Duran HCP, a silicon valley based company, which was the first Technical Recruiting Consulting Company focused on Life Sciences, IT and Engineering. Duran HCP provides Recruiting & HR consulting services at a higher level for a fraction of the cost.

In 2001, Joel was a founding board member of the Global Technical Recruiters Network. There are 17 other Technical Recruiting chapters in the US and Canada.

In 2003, Joel was recruited to Time Warner Cable Milwaukee division to turnaround the human capital process. With in his first six months Joel reduce turnover cost ½ and saved several million dollars. As a result in 2004 the Milwaukee Division was awarded the “Most Improved Recruitment Practices” out of 31 divisions in 27 states.

Jan 2005 Joel was Recruited to Adecco Technical & Scientific as a Regional Vice President where he is responsible for managine 8 states, 29 branches and staff of 85.

In March 2006 Joel went to GMR Marketing as Director of QuickStrike where he now works with companies to help staff live marketing events. www.gmrlive.com

In May 2007 Joel moved to Wisechoice IT an Enterprise Staff Augmentation business focused on Enterprise Application Integration, Business Intelligence, Security and Search.
