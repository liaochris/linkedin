Roy Price’s Summary

Media ecommerce: developed and managing Amazon Unbox.

Television exec. As head of development at Disney TV Animation (1995-2000), developed Kim Possible, Teacher's Pet (won the Emmy for best series (twice)), Fillmore, Teamo Supremo, Buzz Lightyear, Tarzan and many other series.
