Allen Crane’s Summary

Bio:

Allen has more than 12 years experience in eBusiness, web analytics, profitability analysis, data mining, process improvement, customer service, and international program management. After co-developing one of the world’s first web mining platforms at IBM, Allen broadened his technology career at Sabre, Inc., where his work led to numerous profitability improvements for customers including American Airlines and US Airways. His work in data mining progressed at Dell, Inc., where he led the first of several web analytics consulting teams, focused on providing analytical intelligence to drive growth and profitability across multiple online business units. He developed the first-ever business process improvement program for pricing, marketing, and reverse-logistics teams and has spoken at several conferences on the subject. Allen also led program management teams for Dell International Services and was responsible for driving improvements in customer service, operating expenses, and margins. Allen then expanded his analytical reach as Principal Consultant with Visual Sciences, the world's leading data visualization platform. With expertise in developing controlled experimentation strategies, his client list included Fortune 50 and start-up companies such as Wells Fargo, AT&T, Dell, and Louis Vuitton. He currently serves as Executive Director of eBusiness for USAA, a leading financial services company that was voted Business Week's "#1 Customer Service Champ".

Allen has authored articles and research papers on web analytics and optimizing online marketing investments. He has been a guest lecturer at MIT Sloan School of Business, the University of Texas at Austin, and he is a sought-after speaker on Six Sigma. Allen holds a M.S. in Operations Research from Southern Methodist University, and a B.S. in Information Systems from the University of Texas at Arlington.
