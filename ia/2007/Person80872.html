Søren Jonas Bruun’s Summary

Executive, Entrepreneur and International Business Developer since the age of 23. I get my kick from growing companies and shareholder value.

The goal of 1st Corporate Technologies Ltd. is to reinvent business development. We aim to make a difference to every client by mixing best-in-class industry experts and hard work with the best from the worlds of Management Consulting, Corporate Finance and Venture Capital.

My background spans across commercial banking, industry and technology. The bulk of my expertise is within general management, corporate strategy, business development. I enjoy being involved in selected non-profit organisations.

Originally Danish, I am based outside London with my family.

Søren Jonas Bruun’s Specialties:

Board experience. Highly involved chairmanship, membership of +25 boards. Predominantly in IT and services industries. Several external board positions.

Hands-on Business Development, Strategic Corporate Finance and Venture Capital. Successful exits of +10 IT companies. Home grown and third party companies.
