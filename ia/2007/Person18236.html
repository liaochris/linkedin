Colin Taylor’s Summary

Contact Center consultancy and advisory services. Offices in North America and Australia. Guaranteed ROI.

30+ years of call center/ contact center experience in North America and globally. Recieved more than 30 awards for excellence in call/contact center operational management.

Past chair of Canadian Call Center Council, past Director CMA, past Judge and Senior Judge Canadian Call Centre Awards. Developed first Do Not Call registry in Canada, Developed Code of Ethics for call centers in Canada.
