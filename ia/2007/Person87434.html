Travis Savo’s Summary

Travis Savo is an expert technologist and serial entrepreneur with more than 20 years of hands-on experience.

He is fluent in many modern programming languages and best-practice development approaches for large scale networked systems, video games and modern video game engines, desktops applications, mobile and embedded devices, and multimedia platforms. His passion lies in the application of technology and intelligence to solve problems in groundbreaking ways.

He's presently the CEO of Verse Studios, an independent game studio, and an adviser and executive consultant to many technology based startups.

Prior to that, he was the Lead Technical Engineer for the MTV Networks owned IFilm, a premier video portal on the web.

He has been a leader of many successful teams on many profitable multi-million dollar enterprise projects, as well as volunteering for many open-source projects and games. His background in computer science and electrical engineering, coupled with his work in the entertainment and social networking industries allows him to provide a unique perspective on the near-future possibilities of technology-based entertainment.

Many of his contributions can be found in modern games and game engines, large scale enterprise web sites, and free/open source enterprise-level projects.
