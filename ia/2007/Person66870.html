Paul Walsh’s Summary

Evangelist and specialist in Web and mobile standards compliance. Specialist in content classification and content labelling, helping to enable trust on the Web.

Co-founder and CEO of Segala, a specialist in Web accessibility and mobile content standards compliance and content classification.

Was an executive at Eqos, a pioneer in the development of Web technologies for the B2B retail industry. In 1995 I became one of the first employees for AOL in Europe. Was a key member of the team developing AOL's UK presence and assisted with the launch of other AOL European territories.

Segala's W3C advisory committee representative, a member of the W3C Mobile Web Initiative Steering Council and Sponsor of the W3C's first incubator activity initiative to develop content labelling standards for the Web.

Chair of BIMA (British Interactive Media Association), a trade association for the digital industry.
