Ed Torres’s Summary

Established in 1998, Torres Enterprises is an independent agent for USANA Health Sciences. We are specialized in high quality nutritional supplements, antioxidants, non-toxic and self-preserving skin care and a weight managment program.
USANA Health Sciences is one of the few companies in its industry to manufacture its own products. To ensure that the formulas developed in its laboratories are produced consistently for customers, USANA voluntarily meets Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) for pharmaceutical-grade products.

Net sales for USANA Health Sciences in the year 2005 were approximately US $327.7 million.
USANA Health Sciences has subsidiaries in four continents including United States/Caribbean/Puerto Rico, Canada, Mexico, Australia, New Zealand, Hong Kong, Japan, Malaysia, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, Netherlands and United Kingdom.

The corporate headquarters are located in Salt Lake City, Utah.

As an independent agent we are seeking partners from the markets above for a profitable and professional business relationship.
