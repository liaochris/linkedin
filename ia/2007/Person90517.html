Steve Jackson’s Summary

Steve is a senior consultant specialising with web analytics working for Satama Interactive. His clients include Nokia, Vaisala, KONE, MTV3, Vodafone Netherlands and a number of smaller clients across Finland and the Nordic areas.

In 2003 he was CEO and co-owner of a company called Aboavista which was then acquired by Satama Interactive in April 2006 where he currently still works. He is also the editor and contributing writer for the Conversion Chronicles http://www.conversionchronicles.com/ website and is International co-chair for the Web Analytics Association.

Steve has spoken at a number of conferences educating the market about a variety of web analytics topics in the Nordic region and the UK. These include The Internet Marketing Conference (Stockholm), The Search Engine strategies (Stockholm), the IAB Finland (Helsinki), The eMetrics Summit (London, Munich, Stockholm) Divia Finland (Helsinki) and many smaller events. Email address is advertised to connect, so please do so.
