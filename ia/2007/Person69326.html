Praveen Yalamanchi’s Summary

Praveen Yalamanchi aka “Prav”, 34, is a Silicon Valley refugee with a colorful background. He was the founder and CEO of TextbookOutlet.com which sold textbooks and telecommunications services to college students. Prior to that he served as Controller at a web software firm which was acquired by OnStream Media. Prav has worked for various Silicon Valley startups in engineering and sales capacities. Later, he was able to grow a small 5 figure personal investment portfolio into a 7 figure portfolio over three years by trading stocks, options, and commodities.

Prav graduated from Univ. of Miami, Magna Cum Laude, with Finance and English; and Univ. of Florida with Computer Engineering. He holds many certifications and licenses in the both the IT and finance worlds. In IT, Prav is a Sun Certified Java Programmer, Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer and Database Administrator, Cisco Certified Network Associate, CompTIA computer repair and networking specialist, and BICSI certified data cabling installer. In Finance, he is a CFA candidate and also holds the distinction of achieving one of the highest ever scores on the Series 7 securities examination. Prav serves on the Board of Directors for a Univ. of Miami alumni association. His primary philanthropic activity is Habitat for Humanity and he in fact attended a construction trade school to be able to better assist Habitat for Humanity.
