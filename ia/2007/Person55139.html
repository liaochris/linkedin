Mark L. Olson’s Summary

I am a solid strategist, creative tactician, and respected manager. I am looking for a senior-level Marketing, Business Development and/or Operations position where I can put my considerable expertise and talent to work growing or rebuilding a company, organization or cause.

Typically I am brought in to reinvent or reposition companies, fix or enhance programs that are not achieving their full potential, or lead efforts to win market share.

My experience reaches across the three primary areas of marketing services – Corporate, Agency, and Media. I have held senior positions at companies as large as IBM to as small as “back-of-the-napkin” startups. I’ve run my programs at agencies with a staff as small as just myself up to 35 people in eight countries, and done so with budgets in the tens of thousands of dollars up to $10 million. And I spent years in newsrooms researching and writing about business.

I’ve successfully marketed credit cards; books; furniture; electrocardiogram and blood cleaning equipment; computer hardware, software and peripherals; lawsuits; real estate investments; and, Las Vegas nightlife and entertainment. I have demonstrated I can master and market anything.

My accomplishments include a role in the launch of the first IBM PC, the first Internet Service Provider (ISP), two of the most successful Initial Public Offerings of the 1990s, the creation of the data warehousing market, and victory in one of the highest profile intellectual property disputes in the mainframe software industry.

I will relocate for a great opportunity.
