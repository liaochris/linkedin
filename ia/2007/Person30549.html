Fabio FZero Neves’s Summary

Multidisciplinary profile, with bachelor in communication arts and extensive tech experience, including multimedia, internet and mobile.

Four years of experience in the brazilian mobile market, developing products for the three main GSM carriers in Brazil - Oi, Telecom Italia Mobile (TIM) and Brasil Telecom.

Extensive tech background, including programming (Python, Ruby, PHP, SQL and some C) and web-development (major graphical apps, LAMP platforms, Ruby on Rails, W3C compliant X/HTML and client-side scripting).

Ten years of experience in multimedia content creation with emphasis in music production, audio editing and mobile platforms. Deep knowledge of audio and video content production tools, compression codecs and DRM.

Accomplished music producer and performer with extensive portfolio on theatrical and TV soundtracks.
