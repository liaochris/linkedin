Kathy Simmons’s Summary

Kathy Simmons is President and CEO of NETSHARE, Inc.a membership-based organization providing executives and professionals across all disciplines and industries, with quality $100K plus job listings, networking opportunities, and a community of peers for the exchange of strategic information related to job search, professional development and best practices.

Kathy is responsible for strategic vision and financial health of the company, she was the original architect and developer of the Web site and online services, growing the business to several thousand members. Prior to NETSHARE, Simmons spent 15 years in various customer service and technology management positions. Her eclectic background includes starting up a word processing center in Tehran, Iran.

A longtime Bay Area resident, Simmons is an original member of the COO Forum, the premier professional development organization expressly for high impact Chief Operating Officers and is a participating member of the International Association of Corporate and Professional Recruiters. She is also on the Board of Advisors for Pacific Artisans - a Community where artisan and artist, and those who appreciate their creations can meet, learn, communicate and provide feedback to each other.

A graduate of the University of Phoenix, her educational background also includes San Francisco State University and Stanford University. She credits her ability to successfully engage a broad constituency due to her upbringing in Asia and her extensive travel as a business professional.
