Ross Perkins’s Summary

About China 2 West

C2W undertakes a diverse range of projects. We carry out a lot of relatively simple sourcing projects where the focus is on finding the right product and timely delivery. Whenever possible, we work with factories and suppliers in the Guangdong region of China. This allows us to keep things close to 'home' and ensure our customers get exactly what they are after at excellent prices.

Our core capabilities lie in delivering Chinese manufactured products involving new or significantly re-developed products for the Western market. As a sideline, we have also helped UK and US companies to move production to China with the minimum of disruption to their businesses. Projects of this nature are almost impossible to conduct without a presence in China. The language barrier, the difficlty Chinese factories have in understanding the Western market, and their tendency to regard deadlines and contract specifications as optional all mean that constant in-country supervision and communication are the only means of ensuring delivery to time, budget and quality.

Other activities we are currently undertaking include operating a distribution facility where Chinese made products are delivered, stripped of identifying documentation, repackaged and shipped around the world for a UK company. We also run a secure assembly facility on behalf of an American company with serious and genuine IPR concerns.

We rarely say no to a new challenge. But, if we really can't solve your problem we will almost certainly know someone who can - and we will put you in touch. We think this approach is simply good, honest business.

Our set up in China is Brit managed but all our product experts and project managers are Chinese. The team all have tons of experience in their sectors and, being Chinese, add value by negotiating better deals, spotting emerging problems and generally keeping a much better 'eye' on your project than is possible by even the most experienced Westerner.
