Jennifer Jeffrey’s Summary

I am a passionate writer and editor. Over the past ten years, I have written extensively for both print and the web, including books, articles, web site copy, press releases, brochures and business plans.

One of my greatest strengths is the ability to fluidly switch tones, voices and perspectives in order to effectively communicate to diverse audiences. I have held a number of roles during my career, including positions within executive management accountable to company investors, and so I write to achieve results.

How can I help you?

Jennifer Jeffrey’s Specialties:

I excel at writing persuasive materials for potential investors, shareholders and board members, including annual reports, grant proposals & business plans. I also write brochure copy, press releases and fact sheets. In short: compelling copy in whatever format you need and want.
