Jim Lawn’s Summary

Jim is co-founder of Polecat Ltd (www.polecatting.com), a not-just-for-profit company providing innovation acceleration support and services for not-just-for-profit start ups focussed on online innovation for social good. Jim is also co-founder of a Not-for-Profit business called Meerkat. Meerkat is an organisation which brings together many voices to assess the internet's impact on humanity. Currently, the Meerkat online presence is in closed beta under the working title of ‘Social Media Consensus’ - essentially a portal of aggregated content regarding the social impact of social media drawing from academic study, policy, technical expertise, charitable knowledge, corporate responsibility expertise, and social commentary via journalists and other media people. The site also has a social networking function to discuss pertinent topics and add to the global knowledge base.


Until recently, Jim worked for Microsoft as Head of Innovation in the UK, working with the software development industry, entrepreneurs, investors, academia and Government to help Microsoft build its role as a key enabler for innovation in the UK. Prior to this Jim was responsible for the Public Sector Consultancy Services Business for Microsoft in the UK, focussed on working with key customers within UK Government on the delivery of innovative solutions and services. Jim holds a Masters Degree in Information Systems and a Bachelors Degree in Optoelectronics from University College London.
