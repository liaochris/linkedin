Robert Cipriano’s Summary

IllumAlliance International Group, Limited is an international humanitarian project development firm recognized for our creative approach to business, government and humanitarian issues facing our world today.

The work we do is often groundbreaking.

With approximately 100 associates throughout the past 20 years, and a worldwide network of partners, IllumAlliance creates partnerships with our clients on a wide range of matters.

Our approach to humanitarian project development is collaborative, with partner's and associate's functioning as integrated teams to bring the most appropriate response to client needs.

At IllumAlliance International Group, Ltd. we foster a culture where our partners take great pride in their fundamental skills, where all of our associate's are expected to have consummate expertise in their particular discipline. We strive to provide an environment where our associates can fully apply their wide-ranging experiences and skills and excel in the substantive areas of their particular practice.

We pride ourselves in delivering project development and finance services efficiently, assigning partners and staff that are appropriate to the task, not to the perceived bottom line, but in saving or improving lifes.

We adhere to the highest ethical standards. We know that our reputation for high quality services and integrity are our most precious assets. We maintain an approach to our practice that enhances our ability to identify ethical issues as they arise, and we pride ourselves in having the fortitude to do what's right.

This heritage creates an unusual sense of stewardship in our firm, and ensures that partners and staff alike look at generations past and consider future generations when making decisions about our practice.

Since 1985, the leadership team at IllumAlliance has been highly regarded for our integrity, our work ethic, the quality of our project advice, and our people.
