Jeffrey Posluns’s Summary

I have over a decade of experience in management of security and technology companies, with expertise in risk management, security tools and techniques, anti-spam technologies, intrusion detection and prevention, and incident response. Having founded, co-founded, and invested in several e-commerce and security initiatives, I have served in the capacity of President/CEO, CTO, and CIO.

Risk Management, Security Management, Compliance, Management Consulting, Information Security & Privacy Services, Technology Analysis.
