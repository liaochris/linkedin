Jason Sprott’s Summary

One of Verilab's cofounders in 2000. Responsible for the general management of Verilab Ltd, and the development and quality of the company's service offerings. Manages all aspects of the Company’s growth in the UK, into new markets in Europe, and the US. Defines the recruitment standards, training requirements, and delivery models for all Verilab consultants. Also responsible for Verilab's strategic partnerships.

Worked extensively in all aspects of VLSI verification, with a strong background in ASIC, FPGA, and embedded systems design. Active in architecting HVL solutions, with a particular passion for introducing software design principles and practices, into hardware functional verification.

Prior to Verilab, Mr. Sprott held engineering positions at BAE Systems (Scotland) and Densepac Microsystems (CA. USA) before setting up his own independent consulting company in Scotland.

Interested in early stage start-ups and companies on the move. Always on the look out for excellent technical, and executive, potential hires.
