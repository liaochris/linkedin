Marcelo Di Franco’s Summary

For the past eighteen years I have been working in Advertising in Latin America with some of the most important and creative Agencies, for mayor local and international clients; in the Creative and in the Strategic Planning areas.

As a Creative Director working in Agencies with no Strategic Planning Department, my first contact with this field was by need. Suddenly, as I got more and more involved in creating strategies, I was promoted to a new possition in the Agency: Creative & Planning Director. It was only a matter of time for that need to become something else: passion! A passion that seduced me in such a way, that motivated me to definitely shift my career to this field, by accepting the Associate Planning Director position at JWT Puerto Rico.

During my career, I have been trained in developing effective marketing and creativity strategies, with some of the best International Agencies Networks; and now I hope to bring this experience to my new clients and planning team at JWT Puerto Rico.

Goals:
• Keep growing my expertise in Advertising Strategic Planning.
• Develop new business related skills.
• Enjoy working in advertising as much as I did the very first day.
• Find in every client's new challenge an opportunity for self-improvement.
