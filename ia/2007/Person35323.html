John Holt’s Experience

CEO

Sonar6

(Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

February 2006 – Present (1 year)

Sonar6 is an exciting new online talent management and business planning tool, which is capable of providing a dynamic perspective of an organisation’s people and their contribution at any given time.

The product simplifies the assessment and placement of people at any level of an organisation by tracking an individual’s contribution as part of the whole. That contribution can then be assessed according to a wide range of criteria including their potential.

Sonar6 can assess budget management, sales, productivity, quality and team contributions, or any other measure that an organisation’s CEO and managers identify as critical to the success of the business.

2001 – 2003 (2 years)

ANZ is New Zealand's largest bank with over 10,000 employees across New Zealand and over 15,000 in the parent company, ANZ Australia.

In late 2000, the bank launched an innovative reinvention strategy which involved segmenting the retail banking operation into 19 autonomous business units each with a CEO. Out of 700 applicants 19 CEO's were selected - 16 had previous banking experience and 3 were from other industries.

Reporting directly to the New Zealand Managing Director, each CEO had full P&L responsibility for their region and was charged with developing a strategy and business plan to drive growth through local market initiatives and innovation with full support from all central functions and resources.
