Marcelo Lewin’s Summary

I have been designing; developing and managing interactive media projects since 1995 and have been producing, shooting and editing corporate videos (marketing, training) and podcasts since 2005.

I am currently in charge of managing multiple teams of designers, developers, producers, multi-media experts and vendors to deliver world class web experiences including video and podcast projects on time and on budget.
