  • Author

    Konsultförlaget (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    May 1994 — December 1994 (8 months)

    During the summer/autumn 1994 I wrote a book together with Per Florén and Jörgen Jedbratt; "Internet - en vägvisare till morgondagens affärer" ("Internet - a way to tomorrows business").

    It was the first book in Swedish that explained Internet from a business point of view, a practical book aimed at executives, market development people and other non-technical people involved in business development.

    The book described Internet and how it could help a company to be more competitive and contribute to visions and goals. It also gave examples and ideas for possible uses of the Internet.

    The book sold over 4.000 copies in Sweden and Norway.

  • Chalmers tekniska högskola

In the analysis I used the American PDS-method (Problem Detection Study), later used in several large customer-projects at GD.
