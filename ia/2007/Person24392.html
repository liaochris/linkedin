Donald Schier’s Summary

Donald J. Schier has several years of varying experience in many industries.

After starting his career as a web developer, developing an innovative solution to civil transactions for SAP America, Donald moved into interface & communications development at Cendant Mortgage Company, where he played a significant role in redesigning an origination system that the company had out-grown.

From there, Donald began his management consulting profession with KPMG, which shortly thereafter became KPMG Consulting, and then BearingPoint. In that role, he provided leadership on an FCC compliance project for a telecommunications giant.

With years of experience in hand, Donald then decided to go into business for himself. He started to take on contracts as in independent contractor doing process design and business analysis work, which led into a Vice President position at JPMorgan Chase, where he provided consultative services for internal groups, while securing large relationships with Federal and State Governments, and the Private Sector, through targeted RFP responses and innovative thinking.

After serving the bank for two years, Donald refocused his attention on independent consulting.
In February of 2006, Donald incorporated his company, Donald J Schier Consulting, and has been making significant progress in his goal to be a leading consulting agency for large corporate and middle market clients.

Donald's goals for his company are to provide quality service, and guaranteed results.
