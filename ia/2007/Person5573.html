Andrew Gallup’s Summary

Andy is a proven leader with over 16 years of diverse information systems experience. He is currently a Vice President – Internal Audit at TD Banknorth, one of 30 largest commercial banking organizations in the United States. Prior to joining TD Banknorth, Andy had several years of experience at other publicly held companies such as United Technologies Corporation, Aetna Life & Casualty, and State Street Bank; privately held corporations such as Fidelity Investments, and Security Capital Group; and external auditing firms such as PricewaterhouseCoopers and Control Solutions, where he held a number of IT auditing and management positions.

Andy has presented at the MIS Training Institute, Institute of Internal Audit (IIA) and Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA) international conferences and provides IT leadership, management, and internal controls expertise. Andy has a Bachelor of Science degree from the United States Air Force Academy and is currently pursuing his Master’s degree in Business Administration and Global Management. Andy has a Certified Information Systems Audit certification and is a member of the IIA and ISACA chapters.
