Artie Isaac’s Summary

A career marketer and entrepreneur, I work at Young Isaac, a creative services agency providing advertising, identity (naming and logo development), marketing strategy and ideation (1) for values-driven clients and (2) for clients who need their customers to make momentous decisions.

Goals: To help clients answer the marketing questions they face so they can grow their businesses. To provide fulfilling employment to our staff and vendors.

Artie Isaac’s Specialties:

Public speaking and teaching on topics of creativity management, advertising, idea generation, consumer behavior, crisis management, values-based (or values-driven) marketing, generational marketing, and the ethical use of speech.
Experienced board member for entrepreneurial companies and not-for-profit organizations.
