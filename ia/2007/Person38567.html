Jason Fitzgerald, MBA’s Summary

As a Management Consultant, I help owners of SMEs plan for and work through the implementation of new systems, policies and procedures required to streamline operations or restructure for growth. I work with clients in a variety of industries as my specialty is to hone in on areas of operational weakness and make effective actionable recommendations.

Typically, an engagement has me working directly onsite as interim project manager. Where, I work hand in hand with end users, managers and senior management during the implementation. Effectively, becoming the one point of contact for all related issues. Thus, I am held accountable not only for the quality of my recommendations but their implementation as well.

Please, feel to contact me directly if you feel I may be of assistance to you, I am always interested in new challenges and meeting new people.
