Stephen Keaveny (Joi Koi)’s Summary

A visionary, futurist living in Ireland. I am interested in developing virtual worlds to meet social, educational and business needs.

I first experienced moos, mush's and other text based virtual worlds in the early 90's, since then virtual worlds have grown into graphically rich, immersive, environments. The Metaverse is evolving. I joined Second Life in January 2006.

Second Life has got a lot of media coverage lately but it is not the only virtual world. New virtual worlds are appearing weekly. If you need guidance in exploring the business or social possibilities of Second Life or any of these these virtual worlds, feel free to connect with me. stephen@keaveny.org

In Second Life, Kaneva or various locations around the Metaverse you can IM Joi Koi.
