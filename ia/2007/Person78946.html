Sharon Haver’s Summary

Complete bio: http://www.focusonstyle.com/profile.htm

Sharon is dedicated to empowering every woman with the know-how to look as fantastic as she can within the framework of her own figure, budget, and lifestyle. If you want a spirited "best friend" in the fashion business who solves all your style dilemmas, look no further than Sharon Haver.

Sharon has recently been seen in starring as herself in her own Macy's national television commercials as the fashion guru and fashion spokesperson in the store's fall advertising and marketing campaign. Sharon Haver’s Experience

  • Fashion Expert: Online Biography

    Sharon Haver

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    1998 — Present (10 years)

    Online biography available at : http://www.focusonstyle.com/profile.htm

    Online press kit available at: http://www.focusonstyle.com/pressclips/pressclips.htm
    Fashion expert Sharon Haver is everywoman's fashion, style, and beauty guru.

    With just about 20 years experience in the glamour industry as a New York-based photography fashion stylist, syndicated columnist, contributing editor, lecturer, on-air television/radio fashion expert, and founder/ style director of FocusOnStyle.com, Sharon Haver knows what the modern-thinking woman wants-- easygoing style that suits her individual needs, spiced with just the right amount of flip and wit.

    Anyone, anywhere can effortlessly relate to the down-to-earth way Sharon parlays the latest fashions and beauty tricks into realistic, wearable looks. Her adeptness to scope out trends and convey style--from inexpensive to simply luxurious-- makes Sharon an informative and accessible source of fashion guidance.

  • Owner

    FocusOnStyle.com

    (Online Media industry)

    January 1998 — Present (10 years 1 month)

    Insider secrets are revealed through her successful advice column, "Sharon Haver's FOCUS ON STYLE" which grew from a newswire column distributed by Scripps Howard News Service to FocusOnStyle.com, an online fashion, beauty, and style magazine.

    The column remains the anchor for the FocusOnStyle.com which Sharon is the Founder and Editor in Chief. As the resident fashion expert, Sharon also writes StyleThing!, a heads-up on the latest style buzz and QuickFix, makeover style solutions . Satellite sites include FrontRowFashion.com- runway shows, StyleMaze.com- style shopping malls, Style-Pack .com, trend shopping, and Fashion-Bulletin.com- fashion and beauty forum.

    Sharon has recently been seen in starring as herself in her own Macy's national television commercials as the fashion guru and fashion spokesperson in the store's fall advertising campaign. She has most recently been heard on the airwaves as the Beauty Spokesperson- Syneron reFirme national Radio Media Tour.
