Mike Tunnicliffe’s Summary

Summary:

Mike Tunnicliffe has spent the last 15 years as a senior advertising executive at CEO / EVP level . He has extensive global experience and a great track record in change management, building businesses and developing new services for brand marketers.

He has developed and grown various marketing services businesses and has worked across the globe with Fortune 500 and FTSE100 client companies advising on brand communication strategies. He was nominated by M&M Global Magazine as one of the top 100 people in global advertising and marketing and by leading British advertising magazine Campaign to their A list of most influential “Brits” in advertising

He has become increasingly active in Music and Entertainment through his New York based Management Company; Tuna Music LLC, and his Management Consultancy; Strategic Action Station Inc, advising European and North American music and brand related businesses and artists on brand-music integration and business strategies. He is also an advisor to a well-known multi million selling UK based songwriter , a multi-platinum selling Urban star and a select handful of other high profile artists

He writes a regular column for leading advertising magazine Ad Age and is increasingly a media commentator on Brand-Music partnerships and new business models in the music business
