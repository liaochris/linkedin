John Jackson’s Summary

A senior executive of information technology services and consulting organizations, both domestically and internationally, with experience in conducting several business startups, managing and growing profitable operations, conducting a business turnaround, and managing through severely adverse business conditions. Built and maintained an extremely loyal and dedicated staff and customer following.

Acknowledged worldwide as an industry expert in Business Continuity, Disaster Recovery and Information Protection. Significant expertise includes both domestic and international assignments, and includes complete Profit & Loss responsibility for all functional aspects of several ($600 and $300 million revenue) high technology businesses.

A nationally recognized speaker, in command of an excellent written and oral communications style, both internally and externally, with all levels of staff, media, industry, and customer personnel.

Bio online at www.JAJackson.com or www.FusionRiskMgmt.com

John Jackson’s Specialties:

All aspects of IT Risk Management, including availability, resiliency, continuity and recovery; new business startups; acquisitions and divestitures; consulting business management; general business management - domestic and international
