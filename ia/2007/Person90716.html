Wendy Brittain’s Summary

I am a communications professional with nearly ten years experience creating and implementing internal and external marketing and public relations programs for the likes of Yahoo!, Apple Computers, e.piphany and Bank of America, among other companies. I've worked on marketing and human resources teams, and understand the important role that internal marketing plays in employee retention as well as ensuring all employees are aware of company goals, vision and values.

I have worked on the PR side for Niehaus Ryan Wong and at another PR agency, doing media relations for high-tech consumer and B2B clients including Apple Computers, Yahoo!, Intel, Sybase, lastminutetravel.com, Homestead.com and others. As a PR practitioner, I handled all aspects of public relations, including writing effective marketing materials aimed to gain attention from journalists and editors, and ultimately drive sales for my clients. Before I became a PR maven, I was a senior marketing associate for Bank of America, where I wrote marketing collateral, sales presentations and customer newsletters as well as employee newsletter articles and content for Bank of America's intranet. I was also my division's project manager in charge of writing and managing copy for Bank of America's initial website.

Most recently, I've recruited PR professionals for Porter Novelli Public Relations, Burson-Marsteller Public Relations and NRW. In these positions, I focused on full-cycle recruiting as well as employee communications.

As of March 2005, I work as the HR Manager for LEWIS PR's US offices. A global public relations consultancy specializing in technology and telecom, the agency has over 150 staff across 15 offices worldwide. LEWIS is one of the fastest growing tech agencies in the US, according to PRWEEK, and the largest independent IT PR agency in Europe. We're currently hiring great PR people, so please check us out at www.lewispr.com.
