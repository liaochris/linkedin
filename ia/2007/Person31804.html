Greg Gibson’s Summary

I am a new media technologist that has founded and/or served on the management team of companies in the areas of user-generated publishing, collaborative filtering, e-commerce, and online advertising.

After more than eleven years creating and marketing web products, I have collected a broad range of battle scars from the wild West of SEO to the quiet hum of data centers.

I am particularly interested in "knowledge commerce" where communities of people exchange information in a way that drives a solid revenue model. Helium, the company I founded recently, is an example of many knowledge sellers meeting many knowledge buyers - with a pricing mechanism (in this case, peer review) to make the market efficient. Sort of an eBay for knowledge.

I am also spending a lot of time experimenting with ways to engage a community to better organize information by leveraging competitive forces and group dynamics.

Greg Gibson’s Specialties:
