Martin Miller’s Summary

Martin Miller combines 20 years of technology experience with practical operational and management skills. He is able to create and drive a technical vision as well as contribute to the business strategy as part of an executive team. He is equally comfortable mentoring engineering teams and presenting to customers and investors.

Martin's product development experience is in a range of technology: Systems, Operational Support Systems, Web/Media Infrastructure and enterprise application integration, and Intelligent Device Management (M2M, IDM). He has delivered embedded consumer electronic product devices such as Cyclometers for bicyclists and an altimeter wrist watch for hikers and skiers, and a GPS based navigation system. In addition, Martin has applied core competency in system solutions for telephony and networking clients including global Fortune 500 companies Ascend, Aspect, Lucent Technologies, Phillips and Xerox.

Martin has served as Vice President of Engineering of Cacheon, GPware, Livebooks, and Tranz-Send. Martin completed his Bachelor of Science - Electrical Engineering (BSEE) at the University of the Pacific and coursework for his MSEE.

A few other startup companies Martin has consulted with: Aspect (Merged with Concerto), Ascend, Avocet, C-Cube, Divicom, Freegate (acquired by Tut Systems), FutureTel, iBeam, liveBooks, Livescribe, Promatory (acquired by Nortel), GoBeam (Acquired by Covad), Aicon-America, Livebooks, Livescribe, Liveworks, Meebo, Onecast, Questra, Sunol Systems, ShoreTel (IPO, scheduled), Tut Systems (IPO, acquired by Motorola).
