Paul Battye’s Summary

I am search & selection specialist focused on recruiting Executive, Non Executive and Senior Management positions for the Pensions, Human Capital, Compensation & Benefits, Actuarial, and Financial Services sectors.

I have previously worked with a number of high profile Plc’s and specialist pensions, actuarial and investment organisations. Recently I have recruited for the following types of positions;

Chief Operating Officer
Pensions Operations Director
Pensions Director
Executive Compensation Consultant
International Compensation & Benefits Manager
Compensation & Benefits Director
Head of Pensions

Senior Manager Pensions Audit Accounting
Senior Manager Financial Services Accounting
Pensions Fund Accountant
Treasury and Pension Fund Manager

The service offered to clients is truly unique. All research is conducted in-house and by the consultant handling the assignment. This ensures a thorough understanding of the brief and ongoing assignment and market intelligence. As a result, the process is quick, concise and closely managed with information immediately assessed and filtered. This is coupled with a minimum off limits policy. Because I work within a boutique executive search firm my client list is manageable, enabling me to devote our resources to our clients needs at all times. We value our client relationships and do not treat any client “as just another client”. This is demonstrated by the high level of repeat business we conduct.

For further information on how we can add value to your senior management resourcing or help you develop and advance your career, please do not hesitate to contact Paul Battye for a confidential discussion
