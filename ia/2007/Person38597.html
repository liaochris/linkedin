Jason Heltzer’s Summary

Jason Heltzer is a Principal with OCA Ventures and has been working at OCA since 2001. Jason has responsibilities for five investments: Cleversafe, Selero, Alert Logic, Univa and TradeKing. He is also an advisor to Embrace Pet Insurance.

Before OCA, Jason spent three and a half years as a management consultant and technical architect for Deloitte Consulting. He also led engineering and business teams at 1stUp, a CMGI company that provided technology to 130 web properties.

Jason holds an MBA in finance and entrepreneurship, with high honors, from Chicago GSB, where he was selected as a Siebel Scholar. As an alumnus, Jason continues his involvement at the GSB as a guest lecturer in the Private Equity Lab and as a founder and director of the Chicago Private Equity Network (CPEN).

Jason also holds a BS in computer science, with highest distinction, from the University of Michigan, where he founded a fraternity, was elected to Phi Beta Kappa and was selected as the University of Michigan Fraternity President of the Year.
