Jeff Marks’s Summary

After 9 years in the video and television production business, I co-founded BrightRED Pictures because I saw a growing demand for original web video content, and the need for a professional production company with the ability to produce creative content that is effective in the online viewing environment. Since our inception, BrightRED has become a leader in the industry, producing the hit daily web show WALLSTRIP and working with clients in the entertainment and web industries.

In May of 2007, WALLSTRIP was aqcuired by CBS Corporation. As part of the purchase, I was signed to a two year deal as Executive Producer for CBS Interactive. At CBS, I am developing new original web video content, while continuing to produce WALLSTRIP.

Prior to the establishment of BrightRED Pictures, I worked in the New York documentary industry as a freelance Field Producer. I also produced and directed independent documentary and commercial projects with my partner Adam Elend through our company, E.M. Productions, including the 2005 feature FIGHTING FOR LIFE IN THE DEATH-BELT.

I am an honorably discharged US Army veteran, serving 3 years in the 82nd Airborne Division from 1991 - 1994.

I also received a Bachelors of Fine Arts from the School of Motion Picture, Television and Recording Arts at Florida State University in December of 1998. While in school I served as the Assistant Post Production Manager and interned with Academy Award winning sound mixer Richard Portman.

Jeff Marks’s Specialties:

Expert in web video production. Pioneering BrightRED web distribution strategy, innovative original content creation, production strategy design and implementation, story development, writing, directing, editorial.
