Tony Allen’s Experience

  • President and CEO

    Sonic Golf LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Sporting Goods industry)

    December 2006 — Present (7 months)

    Primary responsibility for all aspects of business development, sales, marketing, financial performance, and administration.

    Sonic Golf’s mission is to be the industry leader in the application of science and technology in the golf equipment industry, bringing to market products and concepts that will define the modern golfing experience. Sonic Golf technology is based on research conducted in the laboratory of Yale University Physics Professor Robert Grober.

    Sonic Golf’s first product uses patented technology, including high-tech sensors, microelectronics, and a wireless data link installed in the shaft of a golf club, to convert the motion of the golf club into music. This transforms the golf swing into a sound-space, enabling real-time, auditory biofeedback on the golf swing. While swinging the club, you can literally hear those factors that really matter for success - tempo, rhythm, timing, repeatability - and, more importantly, hear how to improve your very next swing.
