Karen Katz’s Summary

Through the Career Acceleration Network (CAN) and on behalf of REA Careers, I offer a track record of success with professionals and executives who are in career transition. Services are customized to maximize client success and are offered through a collaborative arrangement. Clients become comfortable navigating the career transition and job search process.

Primary services include assistance with career exploration and assessment, resume revision/writing, design of job search correspondence, and planning and targeting job search campaigns.

My ability to respond to the needs of clients in diverse fields stems from my Human Resources background with two Fortune 200 companies (Kenworth Truck Co. & Sunshine Biscuits), Training and Operations Management experience with corporate and academic employers, and entrepreneurial experience as Sales Manager/ Educational Consultant. I've worked in the career management field for 10+ years, serving as an executive outplacement counselor, career transition instructor, and career counselor/coach.

The Career Acceleration Network (CAN) is only as successful as our clients. Together with CAN is more than a tagline; it describes CAN's mission. We want to be the "go to" organization for professionals and executives who are seeking career change.
