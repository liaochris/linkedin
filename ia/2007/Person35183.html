Pål Hivand’s Summary

Pål Hivand is a Special Communications Advisor at Bærum Municipality (Bærum kommune) since September 2007. My main task is to develop better information, together with local and national awareness, around Bærum Municipalitys many building and construction projects, mainly through the use of the Internet.

I am a former Senior Advisor, Webmaster and Head of Communicaions at The Sámi Parliament) since 1999. Educated and experienced freelance writer and journalist, and have for several years been writing about Whistleblowing, Freedom of Speech, Media and Communication. Also an Editorial Staff Writer (columnist) at Depesjer. (www.depesjer.no).

Experienced Internet and Intranett Webmaster and Editor, and qualified in many publishing and design tools. Implementet several digital tools, such as fully digital archiving systems and different publishing platforms. Developed several websites, such as www.sametinget.no, www.diggi.no, www.spesialbiblioteket.no, www.advarsel.no.

Highly experienced Communications Advisor with expertice in both strategic planning and operative communication.

Skilled reporter. Freelance reporter between 1994 and 1999, with several post in different medias.

Pål Hivand’s Specialties:

Software and Web usability, Journalism, Presentation, Project Management, Press Contact, strategic and operative communications, Indigenous Peoples, policy making, government contact.
