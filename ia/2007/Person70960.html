Raphael Lacoste’s Summary

I have a Master of art in Computer Graphics and Fine Arts degree in Art and Media, Photography.

I started to work as set designer and photographer for a theatre company before working into CG, it gaves me good basics for image composition and lighting.

later, I have been Art director on AAA videogames and CG Cinematics for a few years, My goal is to focus now on new challenges in Motion Picture / Film as Concept Artist and Matte Painter, spend less time in meetings and more to draw!
