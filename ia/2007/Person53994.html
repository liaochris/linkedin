Marc Gabriels’s Summary

Marc Gabriels has graduated in 1996 on the subject of Information Management & Technology at Tilburg University. He started his career at Exact Software in Delft as a functional software developer of the logistic Exact ERP software.

In 1998 he started working for Atos Origin as a certified logistic SD & MM consultant. His combination of functional and technical knowledge of SAP R/3 software makes him an all-round consultant that can participate in different roles within a SAP project team. Marc is certified by APICS for the CPIM degree in 2000.

Because of his fascination for application integration problems and solutions he extended his knowledge and experience in 2000 in the area of SAP interfacing. He participated as a SAP interface consultant in various SAP EAI projects.

In January 2001 he decided to establish MGIT consultancy to be able to satisfy his spirit of undertaking by delivering professional consultancy and implementation services in the area of Sales and Distribution, Materials Management and Enterprise Application Integration.

June 2007 Marc has setup EXXAP Cooperation together with Rob Maas, Arthur Viool & Jean Paul Verweijen to establish a synergy impact for working as SAP consultant in the SAP market.
