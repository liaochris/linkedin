Tammy Graham’s Summary

Tammy Graham is the co-founder of True Genius Apparel Company in partnership with her sister, Deborah Graham. The company was born out of the concept that all of us posses our own unique levels of genius that are inherent to our personality - something we like to call true genius.

Through the agency of parents and society our children must be inspired and motivated to discover their true genius through experimentation, hard work and play.

Building an awesome sand castle could lead to becoming an award winning architect. Jumping off rocks could lead to becoming a champion snowboarder. Becoming really great at science could lead to the discovery of a cure for cancer.

True Genius supports this concept in its unique line of children's clothing. Every True Genius product features fun, stylish and positive designs that help express each wearer’s quest toward discovering their true genius.

Prior to founding True Genius Apparel Company, Tammy worked in the marketing communications arena focusing on the development and marketing of various programs, including development and implementation of marketing training curriculum and organizational plans; development and management of policies and procedures; training of staff on marketing and communications; strategic fundraising initiatives: research, grant writing, goal setting, prospecting and cultivating various community relationships; ongoing lecture series for neurological residency program; and continuing education symposiums of neurosurgeons.

In developing and managing such high-level programs, she has experience in designing and dissemination of high-level marketing collateral along with budget construction and management, and reporting of results. She exemplifies strategic thinking with strong oral and written communication skills.
