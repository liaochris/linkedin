Pamela Hendrickson’s Summary

Pamela Hendrickson, a magna cum laude graduate of Brown University, has been the executive VP of Content Development at the Anthony Robbins Companies (ARC) for the past ten years. In this position, she collaborates with renowned author, strategist and speaker Anthony Robbins in creating and developing all content related to human development for new products, live events and other distribution mediums.

Pam’s diverse and comprehensive skills of doing research, writing, editing, producing and organizing content along with her ability to manage multi-faceted projects, provide direction to her team and produce measurable results were instrumental in quadrupling the size and effectiveness of the creative department at ARC.

Pam also worked directly with CEOs, celebrities and entrepreneurs as well as experts in varied fields such as finance, relationships, spirituality, health, business, time management, and psychotherapy. Evidence of her ability to capture the essential information and principles set forth by such experts is found in the award-winning infomercials she helped produce and the best-selling media products created under her direction.

Prior to accepting her executive position, Pam created and implemented training programs for the Robbins’ international franchise division, providing education, support and communications to an international group of almost 100 franchisees.

Pam’s initial job experience conducting scientific studies in sleep research at Bradley Hospital in Providence, RI was an outgrowth of her university honors program in psychology. Grounded in academic discipline, her training in critical thinking, scientific protocol and meticulous attention to detail serve to enhance the integrity of her current work. With collegiate minors in economics and music, Pam also excels at new business promotions by infusing artistic and emotional elements into content, thereby attracting and motivating potential customers to take positive action.
