Travis Wright’s Summary

Travis Wright is the CEO of mediaThinkLab, LLC., Blogger at CultivateGreatness.com and Techasaur.us, and teaches Web 2.0 at Centriq Foss in Kansas City.

Travis has been a serial entrepreneur since 1996. He was a stand-up comedian and needed to create a website to promote his career. He got online in a computer lab at the School of Journalism at the University of Kansas in July 1996, and had built his first website within the next couple days. This internet thing intrigued Travis, as he continued to explore the realms of worldwide infinite possibilities.

In the past 10 years, Travis went on to co-develop a site dedicated to independent musicians, artists, filmmakers and designers called MethodLaboratories and also developed a customized t-shirt and apparel franchise in a local Kansas City mall, called DNA Customs.

Travis Wright then set up a new business entity called MediaThinkLab LLC. MediaThinkLab is a web development company that has created websites for several Fortune 1000 companies.

Through this venture, they created VoteCapture Technologies, which aids political candidates in the process of organizing their campaigns more efficiently and effectively. They also created Techasaur.us which is an encyclopedia of tech news that showcases historic technology.

Travis then created CultivateGreatness.com, which is a Technorati Top 12,000 blog and co-developed SalesMotivation.net with the inspirational, Dr. Gary Jones. He isn’t really a Phd. doctor, but to me, he is “the Docta”. Gary has been a successful sales professional, at the top of his game since I was but a wee-lad. Check out the site, he has done amazing things there.

Travis is also a technical trainer at Centriq Foss in Kansas City. The company contacted him and inquired if he would be interested in teaching students real world web development. He is now teaching his students the wonderful world of Web 2.0 and continues developing sites for his many clients.
