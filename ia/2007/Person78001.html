Sebastian Moeritz’s Summary

Entrepreneur with an extensive business experience in a number of international markets. After studies in social sciences and economics, previously been specialising in property and investment.

Since 1996 involved in a number of information technology related ventures and joined dicas, a developer of ISO MPEG-4 real-time video coding solutions based in Germany, as its CEO in the beginning of 2001.

dicas is an MPEG-focused video coding specialist and a leading developer of MPEG-4 AVC/H.264, MPEG-4 and 3GPP compliant real-time video coding solutions for a variety of Broadcast, IPTV, Mobile TV, Internet Streaming, Multimedia, Enterprise and CCTV/Security applications. For more information, please visit: www.dicas.de

Since 2004, President of the MPEG Industry Forum (MPEGIF); 2002-2004 Board Member and Treasurer. MPEGIF is the world's largest advocacy not-for-profit organization promoting the adoption of open MPEG standards -- such as MPEG-4 (including MPEG-4 AVC/H.264 and MPEG-4 HE AAC), MPEG-7 and MPEG-21 -- representing around 75 international companies and institutes from diverse industries. For many years, MPEGIF also produces full day conferences during NAB and IBC. For more information, please visit: www.mpegif.org

Regular chairman, speaker and panelist at industry events and conferences accross Europe, North America and Asia; written several articles and opinions for various industry and trade publications.

Director of various private and listed companies in the UK.

Listed in "Cable and Satellite Europe's Euro 50 for 2004", which features the leaders in Broadband and Pay-TV across Europe, Middle East and Africa.

Co-author of the book "Understanding MPEG-4 - Technology and Business Insights" (with Prof. Dr. Klaus Diepold of the Technical University of Munich, Germany) published by Focal Press. For more information, please visit: http://books.elsevier.com/us/focalbooks/us/subindex.asp?isbn=0240805941


Languages: German, English, Russian
