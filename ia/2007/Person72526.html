Rich Miller’s Summary

An experienced executive in data network and IT infrastructure, with considerable attention to middleware, security and wireless data. The second half of my career has been focused on marketing, business development and corporate strategy.

My background as a technologist includes design and implementation in messaging (e-mail, EDI) and group communication. After ten years running a venture consultancy, assisting in the creation of many successful network technology, IT and service companies, I co-founded Univa Corporation for which I served as COO until mid-2006.

Rich Miller’s Specialties:

Commercial and technical strategy for networked applications and services with emphasis on marketing, business development and professional services; IT infrastructure and middleware; network and data security; wireless data (WWAN, WMAN and WLAN/WiFi); telematics; messaging (e-mail, EDI) and collaborative systems; RFID, EPC, Auto-ID; web services and grid services; directory and identity management technologies.
