Wladek Majewski’s Summary

Since 1977 I was a leading promoter of information technology in Poland. In seventies and eighties I won several awards for achievements in popularization of science. Later I created two extremely successful popular computer magazines and contributed to establishment of three others. in 1984 I started teaching computer technology in high school. I wrote the first curriculum for computer technology adopted for polish schools. In 1987 I established a first FIDO network node in Poland.
1989-1994 I run a private owned computer equipment trading company NEXT Sp z o.o. (no connection with similarly named Mr. Jobs' enterprise) which for 3 years was listed by ComputerWorld PL among the 20 biggest computer-related companies in POland.
1995-1999 I worked at UT Southwestern Medical Center at Dallas.
1999-2002 I was head of Internet Application Department at Proszynski i S-ka SA where I created services www.pilot.pl ( now known as http://mapy.szukacz.pl ) and participated in concept phase of http://merlin.pl
