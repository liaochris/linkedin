Hans Mampaey’s Summary

Hans Mampaey is an internet marketing specialist with an expert background in search engine marketing (SEO and PPC) and internet business best practices.

Hans is currently Sales Manager Belgium at QueroMedia, a leading interactive agency based in Brussels North. Hans helps QueroMedia's clients develop search engine marketing strategies, including organic Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Pay Per Click (PPC) advertising campaigns. He also leverages his strong analytical skills to generate unique insights into customer behaviour and identify new internet marketing opportunities for clients.

Search Engine Marketing is an important component of the comprehensive set of strategic, creative, and technical marketing services that Queromedia offers to help its prestigious list of clients, which include Securex, Continental Tires, Eurorent, BizLaunch, The Ring Ring Company, Helvoet Pharma, Overtoom, Vandenborre, Robert Half Int'l,....

He has a broad experience in all aspects of search engine marketing and strategic online planning.

Hans has a well-developed skill set in multiple disciplines including sales and sales leadership and sales effectiveness. He is organized and focused which allows him to plan and execute well-defined plans and initiatives. Hans is driven to positive outcomes and results and reaches that goals by consistently following seminars and perfecting his skills.

Sells services through a consultative sales approach to small, medium, large and enterprise accounts in Belgium and The Netherlands. Successfully direct the development and delivery of several professional service suites to the market in record time.

Consistently exceed quota each of every month he works.

Hans lives in Antwerp South and is always interested in taking on new and challenging online marketing projects and sales opportunities.
