Thomas Leonard’s Summary

As a spokesperson, I was always known to be dependable, flexible, and extremely knowledgeable. The management of both Nintendo's Corporate Communication department and our PR firm, Golin/Harris, knew that I could handle any situation that arose. I was the first internal member to serve as the on-site manager of Nintendo's consumer National Media Van Tours, an event in which a tricked-out RV toured the country visiting consumer media. I also managed the interior overhaul of the RV to be updated with flatscreen HDTVs, improved lighting, and other upgraded media electronics.

As a Project/Event Coordinator, I improved upon the logistics of Nintendo Merchandising's annual National Meeting with only a three-month lead time. To help reduce costs, I streamlined both shipping and tool needs by working with other departments and combining projects when possible.
