About Marie-Chantale Turgeon:
Art director, designer, painter and founder of meïdia, she’s also well known as an active blogger and podcaster, promoting the best around technology, pop culture and indie music on her bilingual Vu d’ici – Seen from here blog.

As an extremely creative person, ideas seem to get out of her head at a speed anyone would find hard to follow. She is known to be a trend observer in the development of the ever expanding medium that is the internet, not without her artistic point of view, which makes her a part of this new indie subculture.

She’s lately been assigned to blog the latest production from Le Cirque du Soleil along with blogging Berlin’s Transmediale 06 for the Canadian Broadcast Corporation.
