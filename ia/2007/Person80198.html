Ian Davies (ian.davies@skiltrek.com)’s Summary

With over 14 years of implementation experience Ian is the Director of Professional Services and a senior consultant for Skiltrek (HP Partner - formerly Peregrine Parner), a company that provides consulting/implementation services and staff augmentation for a range of EIAM/CRM/ERP applications throughout the USA. He leads a highly skilled consulting team located with a track record of only success for the company and clients.

Over the course of Ian's career he has consulted to companies/entities such as GE, Pfizer, Bank of America, Manulife Financial, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Dept Homeland Security, IRS, Dept of Labor - NY, DFS (Singapore), Maquarie Bank (UK & Australia), EDS (Australia), Peregrine Systems (Australia), Royal Australia Navy (Australia), Rail Access Corp (Australia), Bankers Trust (Australia), AGL (Australia), KPMG (Australia).

Skiltrek also offers contract and full-time recruitment services to both retained recruiters and corporate clients for many industries.
