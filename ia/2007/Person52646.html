Luis Sarre’s Summary

Insightful and results-driven project design engineering professional with more than twelve (12) years of work experience in the automotive, consumer products and power tools industries; designing, developing and launching products from concept to regular production throughout large and complex processes; as part of experienced teams where considerable amount of knowledge, creativity, initiative, independent judgment, leadership and high level of responsibility are required to maintain design intent, quality integrity and prescribed performance under tight timeframes.
