Adesh Singh’s Summary

I am an Interaction Designer at Oracle Bangalore and working on Oracle Applications for past 3 years. I started my career with PeopleSoft as a Usability Engineer and later on became part of Oracle’s Application’s User Experience Group in the capacity of an Interaction Designer. Primarily, I have worked upon various domains such as CRM, SCM, Manufacturing, Oracle Fusion Integration etc. and provided Interaction design support for various Enterprise Solutions.

I am also involved in efforts to bring HCI community together through organizing conferences, connecting with academic institutions and student community from various Design Schools in India. I am an active member of ACM SIGCHI, UPA, IxDA and Computer Society of India and visited various HCI conferences including CHI 2006 at Montreal Canada.

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur with Master’s degree in design. My research was focused on interaction over touch screen interfaces and he developed a touch screen based navigation system for IIT Kanpur’s Academic Campus. I also holds a Bachelors degree in Computer Science & Engineering with major in Software Engineering and Communication Systems.
