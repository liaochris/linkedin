Brian Fenerty’s Summary

I am currently managing the Shanghai office for AdMark China, www.admarkasia.com, an Asian focused recruiting firm. With offices in Tokyo, Shanghai, Hong Kong, Manila, and Honolulu- and plans to expand further in Asia- AdMark can effectively serve you anywhere in the region. AdMark will successfully assist job seekers and our valued clients in the following disciplines: Marketing, Sales, Finance, Supply Chain Management, Retail, HR, Planning, all Advertising specialties, and General Management. Our China focus includes Corporate Finance, Marketing, Supply Chain, and HR. We specialize in hard to find candidates in these disciplines.


I started headhunting in 1994 at the ripe-old-age of 24 yrs old. I worked in Tokyo; worked jobs in Korea and sourced talent from Singapore. I moved to the Southern Cone of South America for a year and opened an office in Santiago, Chile; worked jobs in Argentina and Peru. I later returned to Tokyo to headhunt at my old firm, East West for another year.

In 1997 I left recruiting for telecommunications company. I was in operations and worked in the Islands of Micronesia; Guam and Saipan, till 2003. I returned to the US at this time to watch Pitt Football games and take care of some pressing family affairs. I returned to headhunting in 2005, the same year I joined Peopleflex.

Peopleflex is one of the best firms I have had the pleasure of working with. Because our sister company, Lordi Consulting, is so well thought of in the Audit sector in Philadelphia. I concentrated in corporatee finance positions in the Fortune 1000 - audit, SEC reporting, and SOX. I gained a great deal of experience in recruiting for both corporate and public accounting concerns here and recommend their services. (Unless, of course you are in China!)
