Mark Green’s Summary

According to IBM’s 2006 Global CEO Study, only 20% of the 750 top CEOs and business leaders interviewed graded themselves as having been “highly successful” at executing strategic change. If you agree with us that change is inevitable, what is your plan to beat those odds?

Performance Dynamics Group is a management consulting firm that works with organizations of all sizes to close the gap between strategy and performance. We serve as catalysts, coaches, and advisors as we facilitate the development of new processes, thought patterns and behaviors that lead to desired results. Our approach helps business leaders beat the odds by successfully planning and executing strategic change in a practical, cost effective, and sustainable manner.

We find that our clients absolutely do not want yet another "flavor of the month" or "flavor of the year" initiative. They know from experience that these initiatives are difficult to sustain and often fall by the wayside. Even worse, when this happens, it tends to teach employees not to take change initiatives seriously. Our processes provide a practical and effective approach to drive real and sustainable change in a way that people readily embrace.

Our starting assumption is that the answers to most, if not all, of your challenges already reside within your people. By putting processes in place to make everyone more results-focused, we help identify, clarify, align, and streamline the resources, wisdom, and know-how that already exist within your organization. This combination of events taps into more of the potential in your organization while also providing precision, clarity, and focus to what people should be doing and why that’s important.

The outcome is more positive, predictable, and sustainable organizational results.

We look forward to the possibility of meeting you, answering your questions, and discussing potential solutions for your organization in light of the particular challenges you face.

Mark Green’s Experience

  • Owner

    Performance Dynamics Group LLC

    (Partnership; Management Consulting industry)

    July 2003 — Present (4 years 5 months)

    Since he founded Performance Dynamics Group LLC in 2003, Mark has consulted with businesses of all sizes to help them improve revenues, enhance profitability, and manage change in a practical, cost effective and sustainable manner. The role he assumes for his clients is a hybrid of catalyst, coach, and business advisor, and his work is fundamentally about developing changes in both thinking and behavior that lead to desired outcomes.

    Mark is an active keynote speaker, and has appeared on New Jersey and nationally-syndicated radio. He published an audio CD in 2004 on self-leadership and a 3-volume DVD series in 2005 entitled "The Leadership Series" which highlights creating a vision, achieving peak performance, and implementing change. His corporate clients include Pfizer, AIG, Discovery Networks, and Rheem Manufacturing Corporation.

    Mark earned his Bachelor of Arts degree from Washington University, with a major in Psychology and a minor in Business Administration.
