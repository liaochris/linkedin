Angie Jones’s Summary

I am available for work and am currently looking at new opportunities as of January 2006. Check out http://www.spicycricket to see the new website and re-design!

Animation Supervisor, Angie Jones, has worked in the animation industry for more than 12 years with a wide ranging experience in film, commercial, television and video games. She specializes in character and creature animation and enjoys moving back and forth between the "sweet and endearing" to the "evil and deadly." Angie's strengths include pre-visualization, early development of motion and character personalities, leadership and mentoring of animation teams, pipeline consulting and organization of shows both film and commercial. Currently, she is available for off-site or on-site production.
