Colin Goldberg’s Summary

Experienced, results-oriented Manager/Architect, passionate about Internet technology, provides strong architectural, design, communication skills to develop innovative, 'software-on-demand' solutions.

I provide consulting services in the area of web application/service strategy and architecture. I am particularly interested in the new web initiatives (typically related to Web 2.0 - the 'semantic web'), including intelligent web applications, loosely coupled web service architectures, business process automation, communication and collaboration, contextual interaction, relationship, and social networking models.

I have created an Internet product - BizConnector (www.bizconnector.com) - a communication automation tool that facilitates contextual interaction with customers, and automates business processes, by handling email-embedded forms, surveys, and followup automatically. Intended for non-technical people, it is available as a "Software as a Service" tool.

Most recently, I have integrated a BizConnector component - Lead Follow-Up Manager - into Salesforce.com's AppExchange.

I am an adjunct professor at Long Island University MBA Program, Westchester Graduate Campus. Topic: Management Information Systems.

You can see my bio at http://www.macmicro.com
