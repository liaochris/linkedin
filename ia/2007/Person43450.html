Jonathan Crabtree’s Summary

Technology is too often a solution in search of a problem. I work the other way around and demonstrate an ability to out-think not outspend competitors.

I'm also passionate about getting a simple message out... Feeding 12 more a day ends starvation in 5.7 years!

Regarding my career, I have a track record of success in the internet, marketing, strategy & product development. I’m currently developing online sales and marketing strategies for a not for profit company in the health and finance sector with an annual revenue of $100+ million.

The internet (both wired and unwired) has been a passion of mine for more than a decade, so I'm looking forward to more challenges and success online.

Perhaps more important than work, is my goal to create positive change in the world through technology. Ten years ago I wrote to Bill Gates and the response from his lawyers was that I had to hand over all IP rights to Microsoft before they would consider my ideas to help end starvation. Bizarre!

I believe enabling communities unlocks brand equity and loyalty. Brands are controlled by customers and they should be the heart of your strategy...
