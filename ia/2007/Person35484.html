H Raven Rose’s Summary

I received my Bachelor of Liberal Studies in Screenwriting from Lesley University, Cambridge, MA, and am a graduate Film School of The University of Melbourne, Australia. I co-wrote, co-produced and co-edited the short romantic comedy "Speed Dating: In 60 Seconds or Less." That film short was produced on DVD and screened in Australia. I studied documentary filmmaking at Melbourne University and Duke University's Center for Documentary Studies. My play "Dark Eros," was produced as a staged readings in Los Angeles (one of which starred Jessica Biels).

I have written multiple screenplays, plays, articles, and short works of fiction and my book, "The Writer's Guide to Writing FLOW: 30 Days to Break Through Writer's Block," is my current work-in-progress and my feature film script "The TransGeniX" is being revised and readied for submission to a couple of LA-based productions companies.

My self-development trainings include certification as a CJEA (Creative Journal Expressive Arts) facilitator and in Hypnosis and NLP. CJEA training includes use of Expressive Arts as medicine, Inner Child/Inner Family work originated by Dr. Lucia Capacchione, multi-media exploration in movement, sound, music, drama, Voice Dialogue, visual art, sculpture, poetry, collage, meditation and spiritual practice techniques and Visioning® to acheive life goals and desires. Coaching training includes training and certification as a Covey Coach and training as a CTI Coach through the Coaches Training Institute. Through M.A. in Spiritual Psychology studies I trained in Gestalt, Psychosynthesis, Rogerian (Person Centered), and Reality therapeutic facilitation techniques. Other studies include Inner Consciousness work, Kabbalah, Meditation Techniques, Native American and Celtic Shamanism.

At this time I am based in Los Angeles, California and traveling for writing and filmmaking. I look forward to the time when I can spend part of each year in Melbourne, Australia.

H Raven Rose’s Specialties:

My combined loves are Writing, Art, Self-Development, Psychology, and Spirituality. I am a Screenwriter, Writer, Coach and Speaker. I divide my time between pitching and selling my own work, ghostwriting, collaborating or editing, coaching others and teaching workshops.
