Richard Kramer’s Summary

I am a senior level Graphic Designer with over 10 years of experience that spans the design spectrum. Print design, web design, client interface design, interactive media, interactive TV, motion graphics, 3D animation, illustration, marketing communications and interactive marketing. I have created exceptional, well designed, user centered, products for world class companies including AOL, Netscape, Yahoo, Disney Online, Hewlett Packard, The Special Olympics, E-LOAN, World Savings and CafePress. I am an energetic, creative, dedicated, extremely organized, and an excellent communicator both verbally and written. I can talk about my ideas and my process to instill confidence in my designs. When it comes to handling multiple projects on tight deadlines I can deliver. I have worked in cross functional teams with engineers, UI designers and usability experts and have a solid grasp of these concepts. Whether you are doing rapid conceptual prototyping or polished final deliverable assets I can do it all. I am flexible, professional, responsible and mature in my working relationships with my clients and peers. I am fluent in all flavors of creative software for both print and interactive media. I can write HTML and I am familiar with uses, best practices and limitations of web standards such as Flash, CSS, DHTML, XML and Java Script. I am comfortable on both the Mac and PC platforms and have limited Unix experience. Please take the time to review my resume for a list of my skills and past experience and feel free to call me if you have any questions.
