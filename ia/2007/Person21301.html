David Caviness’s Summary

Investors today are presented with an abundance of information and choices when it comes to managing and investing their money. Caviness Financial Advisors was created to help those tired and frustrated with the constant barrage of confusing and conflicting investment information, hit-and-miss approaches and exaggerated return claims.

The reality is, most investors spend more time trying to decipher and plan their investments than they spend planning their next vacation. Our approach is to provide you with the relevant information and education necessary to make timely, informed decisions regarding your investments. We are with you every step of the way.

Like you, many investors share similar goals: Protecting assets and watching them grow, quality education for a child or grandchild, a high quality of life during your working years and a retirement that lets you enjoy yourself without financial worry. As simple as those goals appear, they are not simple to attain. They must be planned and managed.

Successful investment and financial management has never been easy.

It is our belief that your financial success comes from having our undivided attention. That is why we stick to what we do best, comprehensive wealth management. We are not crossover insurance agents, CPA’s, or attorneys. We work collectively as a team to bring you the experience and expertise necessary to provide you with personalized, comprehensive wealth management.

We offer a service designed to reflect your personal objectives.

At Caviness Financial Advisors, it is our job to do the research and analysis in order to provide you, our client, with conclusive recommendations. In return, you make the executive decisions.
