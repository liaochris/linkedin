Nikolaus Reinelt’s Summary

The media industry is changing fundamentally. Therefore new frameworks for designing media and mediated communication become necessary. A need arises to combine classic dramaturgic means with the rules of interactive and dialogue-driven end-user communication. Remedying this need through a suitable format will provide a substantial contribution to success for future media. Personally, I believe the development and deployment of such content is the biggest challenge for the media and entertainment sector in the coming years. Underlying principles have to be derived to establish customised forms of work and new markets. This generates a demand for systematic, out of the box thinking generalists that possess specialised know how in relevant segments of the IT, media and communication industries.
My overall goal is to play a sustained and dedicated part in this fascinating process. Furthermore I aim to to broaden my professional and personal development within a dynamic and lively network.

Please visit also http://blog.eyetag.de for further information!
