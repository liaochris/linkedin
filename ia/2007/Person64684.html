Ofer Ben-Shachar’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur Ofer Ben-Shachar founded and lead several successful Internet companies, including NetDynamics in 1994-98 and Noosh (currently NewlineNoosh in 1998). Currently Ofer helps and invests in new promising companies such as: cpacket see http://cpacket.com/.

Ofer Ben-Shachar has been Chairman since inception and Chief Executive Officer for the first 4 years of NewlineNoosh's history. Under his leadership NewlineNoosh created a proven cross-enterprise collaboration technology that was deployed by some of the largest and most prominent companies in the world. He also successfully orchestrated the acquisition of digiGroups and forged several key business alliances and raised over $100 M of Venture Capital.

Ofer Ben-Shachar founded NetDynamics, which was the leader in the application server market before NetDymaics was aquired by Sun Microsystems in 1998. NetDynamics introduced the first Java-based application server product in 1996, and started an industry currently exceeding $6 billion in annual revenues.

Under Ben-Shachar leadership NetDynamics became an industry leader, with 1998 revenues of more than $20 million and 600 plus customers, including more than 100 of the Fortune 500. NetDynamics won some of the industry's most prestigious awards, including: PC Magazine "Editor's Choice," PC Week Labs "Analyst's Choice" and Ziff-Davis Labs "NetBest."

In addition, Ben-Shachar has several patents, is a frequent lecturer at Internet and software conferences, and has published numerous articles on these topics.

Ben-Shachar holds a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science from Hebrew University in Jerusalem and an M.S. in Computer Science from Washington State University.
