Lionel FAUCHER’s Summary

I created Akamedia as a content procurement solution for broadcast professionals:
- Producers can save costs in the distribution of their audiovisual production worldwide and increase their licensing revenues,
- Broadcasters can search, find and license on demand new premium content from a fast growing group of world class producers.

Lionel FAUCHER’s Specialties:

Content Distribution: Copyright Monetisation, Licensing & Delivery;
Media Asset Management: Indexing, Sorting, Tagging;
Digital Rights Management & Security: Watermarking, Fingerprinting and Tracking.
