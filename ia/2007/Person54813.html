Mark Ace’s Summary

My interest and expertise is in developing the printed and online communications that launch, enhance and support technical products, and in being a partner to product companies to help them thrive.

I have 15-plus years building and running businesses in business and technical communications. My current company, InfoPros, merged with Ace Communications in 2005, a company I founded in 1992. The resulting company has offices in four US time zones. We offer outsourced technical documentation, training development and marketing communication services to high-tech and manufacturing companies nationally. InfoPros is one of the nation's larger technical documentation firms.
