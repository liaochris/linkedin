Scott Nilsson’s Summary

Scott has 25-plus years of management, organizational and operations experience in nonprofit senior services and real estate management - with strong additional background in performing arts, entertainment and athletic organizations - at all levels from front line to Board positions.

In early 2005, Scott retired from managing a senior ministry. After focusing on design and construction projects in Atlanta and Columbus, Ohio, he now consults on a pro-bono basis for select nonprofit and charitable organizations in several areas of business operations while getting ready for the next phase. Areas of expertise include operations systems analysis and improvement; risk management; information technology; web site content and efficacy; marketing and public relations; special event development, organization and execution; staff and organizational development; creative problem solving; and, strong business and creative writing skills.

This year, Scott and his wife co-authored a coming-of-age comedy that is currently in preparation with industry editors for promotion to production companies and they are developing additional projects.

After co-organizing Athens-to-Atlanta (http:a2a.net), the international road skating ultra marathon, for several years and founding the nonprofit Georgia International Road Skating Association (http://girsa.org) in 2002, Scott now volunteers to support A2A event and maintains web pages at http://myspace.com/athenstoatlanta with the A2A Group at http://groups.myspace.com/athenstoatlanta.

Scott and his wife, Kristy, maintain a website at http://2nilssons.com, a profile on Myspace at http://myspace.com/2nilssons, and Scott is also online at Zaadz at http://scottnilsson.zaadz.com.
