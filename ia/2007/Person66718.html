Paulo Cury’s Summary

Condere is a consulting firm focused on helping its clients to achieve better results through strategy and business development projects.

From October 2003 to May 2006 partner at ACG, a consulting firm specialized in strategic planning. Paulo has conducted projects focused in strategy, channel management and pricing for financial institutions and service companies.

From January 2002 to September 2003 director of RBS Group, responsible for the creation and implementation of its start-up focused in database marketing and CRM.

From September 1999 to December 2001 worked as management consultant developing projects for companies as Santander/ Banespa, TAM, Net e Globopar.

From October 1997 to September 1999 worked as general manager responsible for the creation and implementation of the Brazilian retirement operation of Salomon Smith Barney.

From March 1994 to August 1997 worked as manager of Amil Group responsible for the creation and implementation of Amil´s indirect sales channel and of Amil Retirement Business Unit.

Paulo has a bachelor degree in Economics and an MBA from Fuqua Business School/ Duke University
