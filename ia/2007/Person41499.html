Jim Risner’s Summary

Co-founder of the popular online video & photo contest site, www.votigo.com.

Award-winning marketing executive with over 10 years of experience designing and developing world-class websites and software user interfaces for public companies and startups. Over 8 years experience managing designers and developers both in the US and oversees. Expert in online and software user experience and usability. Very strong entrepreneurial spirit.
