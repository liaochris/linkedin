Pat Calvert’s Summary

I have 30 years global sales, engineering and management experience in the Telecommunications and IT industry, working up to board level in multinational accounts for managed service providers of all sizes. My previous roles have included three years as Business Solution Consultant for Verizon responsible for complex managed solutions, 4 years as a strategic consultant and three years as Sales Manager for Telewest responsible for managed advanced data services. At various times in these roles I have held responsibility for up to $70m pa of revenues and 30 staff. Having been with Verizon Business since January 2005, I am now looking for a new challenge within my chosen field and career.

I have the ability to see through the technical fog, pulling together multiple disparate strands of internal & external offerings (be these products, service or solutions) into one coherent offering with a structured management wrap around, whilst presenting it in a seamless manner to the client.
