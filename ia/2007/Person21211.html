David Aughenbaugh’s Summary

David has expertise is in the areas of compositing, motion
graphics, and digital visual effects.

David started in the late 1980s as a 2D animator for cartoons and
computer games, interested in both art and technology in the developing field
of computer graphics. He helped to develop the first fully digital
pipeline for broadcast 2D cartoon animation. Through the 1990s he
continued as both an artist and in software development, with credits
on several animated cartoons, over 50 PC game titles and a regular
column in TV Technology Magazine.

In the dot-com era, he was involved in developing a network of
solar-powered, remote-controlled video cameras, feeding live video
streams of wild animals to clients like NationalGeographic.com and
Discovery.com. This company is still in business and can be found at
SeeMoreWildlife.com.

He then moved into large-screen video settings, creating 3D animation
and motion graphics for professional sports teams across the country.
Clients included the Jets, Giants, Bengals, Mavericks, Buccaneers and
others, as well as Super Bowls XXXV and XXXVI.

He currently works as a compositor and motion graphics artist for
commercials, corporate clients and feature films.
