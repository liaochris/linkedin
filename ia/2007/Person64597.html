Olivier Bouillaud’s Summary

-I have been the Group Treasurer of Antalis, largest European group in the distribution of communication supports (mainly paper), for more than four years.
Antalis is fully-owned by Séquana Capital, listed on the Premier Marche of the Paris Stock Exchange and part of the SBF 250 index and CAC MID100 index.

-Last October 2007, Antalis obtained a EUR 650 million syndicated loan via MLAs & bookrunners BNP Paribas, Crédit Mutuel - CIC, HSBC & Natixis. This multi currency loan term loan & revolving facility has a 5 year tenor and five other banks joined during syndication (ABN AMRO, Fortis, Nordea, Société Générale and bank Austria).
After Map Merchant Group acquisition (a EUR 382 million Cash&Debt free transaction), Antalis will have a EUR 4 billion turnover, 8400 employees, present in 44 countries (30 in Europe).

-I have worked for 15 years in the corporate Treasury field for international companies.
Prior to this position, I was head of Treasury for Alcatel CIT for three years and held several treasury positions within CarnaudMetalbox & Compagnie des Signaux (ex-CSEE).

-I have a Banking degree (Magistère Banque Finance), a Master's degree in Finance majoring in Treasury and a postgraduate diploma (DEA) in Economics – from Paris University.
I have also a CEDEP degree from INSEAD (Antalis International Management Program).
