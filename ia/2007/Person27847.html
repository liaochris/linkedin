Erin Bell’s Summary

Versatile writer and editor with broad experience in all aspects of magazine editorial, including issue planning, article assigning, proof-reading, copy-editing, photo research, and writing everything from feature-length articles to small briefs and product reviews. As part of small in-house editorial staff at Piccolo Publishing, worked on magazines including Total Gamer (Canada's only videogame publication), HUB: Digital Living (computer publication) and hub:ce (glossy consumer electronics lifestyle publication).
