Ton Meeuwissen’s Summary

My companies slogan is "Result by using common sense" or in Dutch "Resultaat door gezond verstand".

I know to draw out the potential in people. My customers have the needed specific knowledge and it is my challenge to help them making the best decision based on that knowlegde and insight. By using lateral thinking techniques I teach them how to gain new and fresh insights.

I like to deal with complex matters or projects and my method of working includes the following steps:
- analysing the situation
- Reducing the complexity by cutting it into chuncks that can be handled.
- Setting priorities
- Making a plan
- Carry it out (often as a project) to the desired resullt!

In all these stages I make use of electronic MindMapping to facilitate the process.

Besides advising in complex information management issues I am also a professional trainer (mind mapping, learning to learn, lateral thinking)
