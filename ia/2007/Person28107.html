Esther M. De Frutos’s Summary

A native of Spain, Esther is a seasoned business development professional with extensive experience in accounts acquisition and retention, customer service, sales as well as online / offline marketing strategies for industries such as Entertainment, Software, Travel and Hospitality in the US, LATIN AMERICA and EUROPE markets.

As a VP Sales and Marketing for the Red and White Fleet, Esther is responsible for product launch, sales, partnerships, alliances and marketing initiatives including branding and online-offline marketing campaigns for two tour cruises brands, including the upscale Ferry Building Line.

Prior to that, Esther held positions at INTERCONTINENTAL HOTELS and Resorts where she was responsible for selling five hotels in San Francisco and at START-UP ONLINE TRAVEL company where she was Director of Business Development responsible for developing sales and strategic partnerships in the West Coast.

While pursuing a Master of Science in Travel Industry Management with a specialization in E-COMMERCE and ONLINE MARKETING at the University of Hawaii, she held internships positions at the consulting firm KPMG – Hospitality practice as well as at STARWOOD HOTELS & Resort – Interactive Marketing Dpt where she helped with the overall online and e-commerce strategies for Starwood’s 5 hotel brands and 17 properties in Hawaii.

Prior to her graduate work, Esther worked for SOFTWARE companies in Atlanta & Houston where she was responsible for sales in the Latin American market.

Her earlier career included positions in sales, A&R and copyrights for multinational Entertainment companies such as BERTELSMANN ENTERTAINMENT Inc. (BMG) - now Sony-BMG, and EMI Records in New York and Madrid, Spain.
