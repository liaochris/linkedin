Steve Russell’s Summary

I've never really been an employee. I have been: hippie entrepreneur, traveller, R.E. broker, winegrower, student, R.E. investor, print publisher, and web developer.

Taking my 35 years' experience of street smarts, formal education, property development, print publishing and marketing, I am now developing a network of websites under one brand. It is my goal to create a brand that evokes a rich, positive, proactive and informative level of involvement and experience for the end user.

I firmly believe that investing in domain names and developing them as fully functioning websites is as important for the future vis-á-vis the internet as acquiring prime real estate was 30 to 50 years ago. Moreover, it would seem that my 35 years' experience in the real estate and publishing/advertising industries has been a solid preparation for this next step in my career.

It's very exciting, and daunting, to be at the thin leading edge of discovery. It's full of risk and uncertainty, and I readily admit I have bitten off more than I can chew--which is precisely why I am enjoying it so much!

I'd very much like to connect with like minded folks from all aspects of the Web 2.0 lifestyle and community to share ideas.
