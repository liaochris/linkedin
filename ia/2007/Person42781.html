John Conroy’s Summary

Results-driven executive manager experienced in leading and delivering successful multi-million dollar e-business and data warehousing projects. From a professional debut as one of the first DB2 Database Administrators in the world, to current head of a program management office, my career has steadily and rapidly progressed in the strategic, tactical and technical spaces. Career building roles have included, commercial software product management, lead data architect for a major retail brokerage, and on an individual contributor level, many roles in the strategic data and enterprise architecture spaces.

Under direct tutelage from the brightest luminaries in the information space (Chris Gane, Charlie Bachman, and Dr. Tedd Codd), I have the background, training and experience to tackle any opportunity.

Of primary note is my unique ability to articulate and effectively communicate complicated technology concepts and solutions to executive level audiences. Throughout my career, I have been described time and time again as a proven leader, strategic thinker, an invaluable mentor, and a results-oriented manager.

With this wide depth and breadth of experience spanning a plethora of industries, my talents would be a perfect fit for a strategic executive leadership position.
