Chuck Bruce’s Summary

With fifteen years as a professional and sixteen more years as an IBM executive, my business career was successful, satisfying and varied. My early retirement offered me a chance to make it on my own. After a few consulting engagments, my priorities focused on doing what was fun, satisfying and under my own control.

With thirty years of amateur Nikon photography and professional computer experience, I decided to put my interest and experience together in a business where my wife and I could be partners. So we started KC Bruce Photography as a commercial extension of my photography and computer background. My years of photographing my family's team sports and providing season highlights on CD's provided a base for the current offerings that delight our clients. My wife's career as an RN and a mom help keep the personal touch in our business.

Our offerings are professional in a world that is playing catch up to digital photography and computer capability. Our strategy is to offer higher quality at lower prices by leveraging our experience and low overhead. Our goal is to capture and enhance a family's most important memories in a way that is uniquely delightful and truly unforgettable.
