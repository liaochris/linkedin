Jared Slosberg’s Summary

Mr. Slosberg is President of ProfitFuel, responsible for its day-to-day operations. Prior to ProfitFuel, Mr. Slosberg was CFO of HomeCity, where he still serves on the Board of Directors. Prior to joining HomeCity, Mr. Slosberg served as Vice President of Business Development for FindLaw, the largest legal-related portal on the Web. Mr. Slosberg was a Founding Director of Round Zero, a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping young technology entrepreneurs. Mr. Slosberg graduated Phi Beta Kappa with a B.A. from Brandeis University and holds a J.D. from the University of California at Berkeley - Boalt Hall.

Jared Slosberg’s Specialties:

Startup formation and growth, both full-time as an owner and part-time as a member of the board of directors or board of advisors.
