Kedar Anil Gadgil’s Summary

I see myself as a leader-evangelist for new ideas, as a partner & mentor for people with new ideas and as a facilitator for executors of new ideas as also for those who want to try out new approaches to old ideas. In this job, I have to concentrate on people & ideas, on trying to guess the future & manage the present. I have a lot of fun creating a diverse basket of offerings that will pay off at different intervals, creating a machine that would allow me to do the same again, over & over again, with new people & new ideas.

I also preach (& practice) aggressively a certain 'attention to detail' that is necessary for fulfilment of any job, which, in its absence, would always remain incomplete. I am constantly at work towards the final vision of IndoGlobal (to touch as many lives in as many positive ways in as many areas as possible) while trying to keep asking myself, and getting every other IndoGlobalite to ask him/herself, 'Was today worth it?" at the end of each day, thereby making sure that everyone, including me, is always challenged and never bored even while doing everyday jobs.

I also try to instil a constantly questioning attitude to thought & action, creating (or attempting to create, in any case) an organism (The IndoGlobal Group) that shall be everlasting as it would build in itself, a capacity (and a propensity) to adapt, change and keep up with whatever it takes to remain relevant in all times.

This job is the most exciting & fulfilling job I have ever held. This is the ONLY job I have ever held, since I have never had to do any job that does not excite me, EVER. I maintain that apart from every other quality one needs in today's world to survive & grow, the one that is missed out the most is the courage to refuse anything that one would not do even if money (or any material gain) were no object. It is part of my job to make sure that every IndoGlobalite feels this way. As I said, this is an amazing job. I would not trade it for anything else!
