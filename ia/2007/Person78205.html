Michael Black’s Summary

In the world of SEO, I'm an old man. People are called SEO Gurus after about 5 years in the industry... I've been at it since 1997. I guess that makes me an SEO Uber-Guru! I've led online marketing campaigns for almost 400 websites. Some notable clients are:

Wal-Mart - I led a team of optimizers focused on optimizing Wal-Marts public relations site. We were involved in development of site technology, integration with search engine optimization best practices, and in the launch of the site.

Thrifty Car Rental - I led a team of optimizers that optimized Thrifty.com. We focused on a national US market, a local US market including thousands of Thrifty franchisee locations, and launched Thrifty's international campaign by developing methods for optimization focused on the Caribbean, Mexico, and the rest of Latin America.

Dollar Car Rental - Dollar is Thrifty's sister company. My team and I performed the same duties for Dollar as Thrifty.

Hallmark - A current project of IMR is positioning a new line of business for Hallmark Greeting Cards. This new line of buseinss focuses on customized, corporate greeting cards.

Farmland Foods - My team and I worked hard to capture the "online recipe" demographic for Farmland Foods. Our efforts paid off especially well around the holidays for them.

The University of Pennsylvania - I optimized the UPA site for a focused on their Management of Information Technology Master's degrees.

Interest.com - I worked with Interest.com to position their site to compete with Bankrate.com who later bought Interest.com

MonsterCommerce - I've delivered expert advice on how to improve their site's optimization.

StoreFront.net - I was the Internet Marketing Manager at StoreFront.net. I led a team focused on optimizing and maintaining over 75 websites.

SPIDERtel - I was the Internet Marketing Manager for SPIDERtel where I developed all of the internet marketing and reporting techniques.
