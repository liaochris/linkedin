Mukul Agarwal’s Summary

Boloh Ltd is a telecom service provider offering solutions to telecommunication organisations - International Carriers, MVNOs, Mobile telephony operators, Fixed line & VoIP service providers besides roaming solutions for mobile operators (whether voice, data, messaging, or all). On the other hand Boloh also provides retail services - calling cards, VoIP minutes, Broadband Telephony, hosted IPBX, Corporate hosted voice based solutions, virtual office, Asterisk solutions, call center billing and dialers, etc.

Suryansh Trans Services is rolling out a metro wide wireless infrastructure at a pan-India level in a phased manner for delivery of data (Internet), voice (broadband telephony) and video (IPTV & video-on-demand) - triple play. The target market is the second tier cities and rural India in the initial phases, addressing the needs of a bandwidth hungry nation with less than the world average of per capita connectivity.
