Pamela Ueckerman (Jones)’s Summary

Pamela is a web designer and developer with a particular interest in Web Standards, usability and accessibility.

After completing a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science and Management Skills in 1998, Pamela became a consultant for Cambridge Technology Partners and, later on, Thoughtworks - highly regarded in the industry and known for their pioneering work implementing Agile methodologies. She has worked on many high-profile projects for clients, both in Australia and internationally, such as Telstra, Pacific Access (Sensis), ANZ and Nationale Nederlanden as a web developer and Java programmer. During this time she worked closely with usability experts and graphic designers to develop web front-ends. At Thoughtworks, Pamela worked with some of the most intelligent minds in the industry both on back- and front-end development. As one of few in the company with web design and usability skills, this became her speciality.

In 2004, Pamela completed a 10 month contract with ING in Amsterdam as a developer as well as training colleagues in continuous integration, design patterns and testing best practices. This was followed by a brief contract creating software for the fashion industry.

On returning to Australia in 2005, Pamela decided to shift her focus to web design and development based on Web Standards and started her own business – Spiral Designs. Since then, she has created and collaborated on numerous sites for various small and medium-sized businesses in Melbourne and Sydney as well as completing freelance web development work for a number of start-up Internet companies and at Sensis.

Pamela thrives on building websites that are aesthetically pleasing, user-friendly and accessible. She also takes pleasure in advising business how to make the most of their website and attract more visitors.
