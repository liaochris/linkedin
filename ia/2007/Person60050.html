Mingles Tsoi’s Summary

With both accounting degrees from the Chinese University of Hong Kong and the University of Southern Queensland (Australia), I worked as financial manager for 9 years since 1988. I started my own executive search firm since 1997 and began the career as consultant.

My specialist is in business development, strategic planning and execution. I always look for challenges in deals making, having them done in various creative ways.

I have developed series of training modules about entrepreneurship, such as: skills of business plan writing, how to start a business with HK$100,000 and venture capital funding perspective. I also act as voluntary speaker for several training seminars to the Youth Business of Hong Kong. Sooner this month I will help JAHK mentoring a group of Form 6 students for a 18-week business start-up program.
