Mike Johnson’s Summary

Mike Johnson has been in sales and consulting for many years, and has now joined Pac Banc Mortgage in the capacity of a Senior Loan Consultant. He comes from a major national lending institution where he honed his skills as a loan originator.

Mike is a proud father of 2 boys and works from his home in Portland, OR, originating Mortgage and Commercial loans in all 50 states, including the District of Columbia.

He has a passion for helping others achieve their dream of home ownership and improving the financial position of others. Mike knows that long-term financial security begins with the ownership of a home.

Mike’s core belief system centers on personal accountability for getting things done and ensuring success in an ethical manner with unwavering integrity. Borrowers enjoy working with Mike because he is down to earth, a great communicator, and listens openly for their thoughts and needs.

Mike is looking forward to contributing to the community and improving the lives of others. He has already been a proven asset to the team and has contributed to policy and procedure improvements to benefit internal and external customers.
