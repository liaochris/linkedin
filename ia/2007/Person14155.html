Robert Calvert’s Summary

Robert Calvert has over 13 years of strategic business experience in the design and development of next generation carrier and enterprise networks. Technologies include telephony and IP based networks, unified communications, fixed mobile convergence, cable, IPTV, and WiMAX.

Motivated by challenge, and the ability to have an effect on or improve business performance. Robert possesses strong business knowledge, excellent communication skills (verbal, written and presentation) and the ability to think strategically.

Robert Calvert’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning…Business Development…Sales Engineering…Network Architecture and Design…IP and SIP Based Networks…Wireless, Wireline, WiMAX, and Cable Architectures
