Alisdair Faulkner’s Summary

Alisdair is a professional with strong operational experience building and acquiring international companies and technology. As a founder of Foursticks and Product Director he grew the company from 3 to 30 people to a valuation of $20million in 4 years. Responsible for products and marketing he expanded into the US, Asia and Europe with customers including VW Bank and KBR Halliburton. In 2005 he participated in the restructure and acquisition of Foursticks IP and Assets into NetPriva, managing IBM as a strategic partner and shareholder.

Prior to founding Foursticks Alisdair was a Senior Consultant with Accenture responsible for sourcing and managing deal-flow opportunities with venture-backed and spinout companies.

Alisdair is a graduate of the Venture Capital Executive Program of the HAAS School of Business and holds a Graduate Diploma in Applied Finance and Investment, a Masters of Engineering Science, a US Network Technology Patent and is a Graduate Member of the Institute of Company Directors.
