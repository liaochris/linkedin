    Sheryl Wolbrum-Schwam is an accomplished and innovative entrepreneur. She credits her intuitive understanding of people and technology for all of her many successful business ventures.
    In 1984, she founded The Protocol Connection, with its focus on permanent positions in New York City’s most elite financial institutions. .

    In 1995, founded her second company, TCP Inc. , with a client base that now includes , HSBC/, Swiss-Re, Bloomberg L.P, Morgan Stanley, E-Trade, Goldman Sachs, and A&E Networks.


    In 2003, Sheryl launched Call Sheryl L.L.C., . This year, both New York State and New York City distinguished CallSheryl/TCP /Protocol by certifying it as a Woman-Owned Business Enterprise (WBE).

    Sheryl is a graduate of NYU Business School, a competitive swimmer, an avid landscape photographer, a philanthropist and member of The Brain Tumor Foundation who also does service for many Non Profit Groups in her beloved City of Manhattan.
