Steve Gerencser’s Summary

I've come full circle back to design, this time on a smaller, more personal scale. Since 2002 I have been designing jewelry for Images Jewelers. During that time I also became a bench jeweler to get a better understanding of the structural aspect of jewelry design.

My short term goal is to start wining the design competitions I've been entering rather than just placing, and my long term goals revolve around developing a new and unique jewelry line.
