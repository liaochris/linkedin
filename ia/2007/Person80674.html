Solaiman Rahim’s Summary

Solaiman has 7+ years of experience in the EDA industry in the areas of Software Engineering, Software Architecture and Technical management.

Currently at Atrenta, Solaiman is the director of the advanced technology group. In this position, he is leading the architecture and development of new formal techniques used in several products related to timing exception verification and generation and clock domain crossing. He is also responsible of critical evaluation, software deployment and technical management of R&D. Prior to this, he has held positions at Atrenta as software architect, at Synplicity as senior formal verification engineer and Softknot as software engineer. He was also a computer sciences teacher at University of Montpellier.

His objective is to utilize proven skills on software architecture, software development and deployment, people, product and technical management as part of product management and technology improvement of an EDA company especially in a multi-site environment or in the domain of micro-projects created to fight poverty in countries under development.
