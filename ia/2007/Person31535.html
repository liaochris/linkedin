  • Director

    Ad Hoc Organizing Committee

    (Political Organization industry)

    August 1971 — February 1973 (1 year 7 months)

    This was an organization formed at the time of the constrution of the Alaska Oil Pipeline with the goal to insure the natual resources and social infrastructure of the State of Alaska and its citizens was protected from the boom and bust that had characterized previous economic cycles. We accomplished this goal by a combination of (1) successfully running candidates for office at all levels of village, city and state goverment, (2) taking control the state Democrative party, (3) authoring legislation and (4) tri-level taxation of the oil companies with the proceeds invested into a permanant fund -- inaccessable to the politicians -- which had the effect of eliminating state income taxes and instead, providing payment to residents which increases with every year of residency.
