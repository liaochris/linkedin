Suw Charman’s Summary

From the very basic 'What's a blog?' through to complex adoption strategies, long-term comprehensive mentoring or 'future of...' reports, I help businesses understand the main technologies, attitudes and ethics that comprise social software/Web 2.0. I explain how best to use blogs, wikis, IM, chat, RSS, aggregators, tagging, social bookmarking and any other interesting application or service available, for either internal or external use.

I have worked with a wide variety of businesses, from small PR companies like Jackie Cooper PR, to multinational investment banks like Dresdner Kleinwort Wasserstein, either on-site or remotely.

I am happy to do one-off engagements such as presentations to staff or board members, short-term focused projects such as getting staff trained and ready to blog, or longer term strategy/adoption projects. I don't do any off-the-peg training, nor do I have standard presentations, because there is no 'standard' when it comes to social software - everything has to be tailored to the people involved, otherwise the training/presentation misses its mark.
