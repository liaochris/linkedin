Mark Middleton’s Summary

Archivist and software entrepreneur with substantial internet and web experience. Specialising in the collection and preservation of born-digital content, web archiving, digital repositories, digital libraries and digital publishing.

Founded Hanzo and implemented web archiving tools and services for individuals, corporations and heritage institutions.

Mark Middleton’s Specialties:

Web archiving, digital repositories, digital libraries, software development, commoditisation of complex processes, preservation of emergent digital culture, intellectual property law and entrepreneurism.
