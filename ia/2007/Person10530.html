Berin Szoka’s Summary

My lifelong interests in innovation and public policy led me to law school, where they converged at the study of how the law co-evolves with technology. Today, I am a practicing lawyer with experience advising clients on how to navigate and/or change the laws and regulations governing the Internet, telecommunications and commercial space industries.

I am currently looking to:
• Expand the policy aspects of my practice, especially in the legislative process
• Advise clients on the cutting edge of the Web 2.0 revolution
• Gain experience in venture capital and technology transfer
• Help the nascent entrepreneurial "New Space" industry develop its own regulatory environment.

I am a member of the DC and California bars, and am conversant in French.

Advocates are the core of the Foundation's membership and are elected for their work to advance the cause of space settlement.
