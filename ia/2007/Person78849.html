Shannon Manning’s Summary

I have always sought to support the arts, and connect artists to one another and to resources and audiences. I've tried to master the tools of each media revolution since the mid-eighties but mostly in the service of art and creative communication. I always get out before the money gets too good.

I continue working on my own projects with wonderful people and other projects with other wonderful people.
