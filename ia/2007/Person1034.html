Anne-Catherine Lorrain’s Summary

Anne-Catherine is currently writing her PhD paper on the online exploitation of music (contractual practice of rightholders for legal online music offers, international law). As a member of CERDI, a research center specialized in IP law and new technologies at Universities Paris I Sorbonne and Paris Sud (directed by Professor Pierre Sirinelli), she is part of several research programs.

She is also publishing in scientific reviews and is regularly invited to participate to seminars and conferences.

Anne-Catherine has a past professional experience in the music industry, as a lawyer at IFPI (International Federation of the Phonographic Industry) French group (SNEP) for 3 years and as a trainee at IFPI Brussels Office.

Anne-Catherine Lorrain’s Specialties:

Intellectual property law, music business, negociation of rights (contracts), international law
