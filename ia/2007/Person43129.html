John Payne’s Summary

History of success in building or turning around early stage venture funded software and technology companies.

Former CEO of two public Internet software companies including Stamps.com (NASDAQ: STMP) and Day Software (SWX: DAYN)

Founder/Co-Founder of 4 Internet Software companies (2 were financially successful for founders and investors, the 3rd was critically acclaimed but financially a non-profit and the 4th recently raised a Series A financing following a seed funding provided by a dozen industry leaders.)

Jumpstart/turnaround CEO for an additional 4 startups where he was "the asshole brought in by the VC's" at an early point in the company's life. These exits included one highly successful NASDAQ IPO (NASDAQ: STMP), one turnaround of a still public enterprise software company (SWX: DAYN), one sale to a public company (NYSE: McAFEE) and one successful operating company in the application security space which recently raised a Series B Financing.

He is an experienced fundraiser who has raised over $550 million in public and private capital from the professional investment community, including over $150 million in venture capital from well known early stage venture firms and strategic investors.

John Payne’s Specialties:

John has a specialty in providing jumpstart or turnaround CEO level management to early stage or troubled technology companies in cooperation with their institutional investors. He is focused on pre-revenue through $30 million revenue companies in high potential situations where significant upside can be aggressively exploited through jumpstarting rapid revenue growth or company turnaround.
