Kris Nair’s Summary

Kris Nair is a connector -- introducing like minded friends, helping entrepreneurs find supporters, or just trying to make sense of disruptive ideas and the appropriate opportunities to create change. He is interested in technologies that improve connection and empower firms which have a meaningful role to play.

He is available for consulting projects for startups and large companies alike in the social media & marketing/Web 2.0, Outsourcing space, particularly around business strategy, marketing and financing or venture investment.

He's currently working with Satellier LLC, world leader in Architecture & Engineering Services Outsourcing. Since Dec 2005, Kris Nair's having a wonderful stint with Satellier doing a lot of strategically important assignments.

Previously in his role as Lead Analyst for Outsourcing, Internet and New Media, Kris Nair focused on early stage developments in information technology enabled service businesses such as BPO, HRO, F&AO, PO, Engineering Service Outsourcing and Media Process Outsourcing. He published several iconic reports on various BPO concepts as well as best practices. Internet Industry has been his pet since school days and currently analyzing best practices and money flow.

Prior to that, he spent 3 years at the world of Entrepreneurship, where he worked extensively on venture capital partnerships focusing on emerging technology as well as private equity investments in the consumer and software sector.

Kris Nair can also be found blogging at http://globalteamwork.blogspot.com

He lives in New Delhi; India, a city he has come to love for no particular reason.
