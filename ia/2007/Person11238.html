Bill Pursche’s Summary

Bill Pursche is the founder of First Call Advisors, a business advisory firm which provides on call consulting services and individual advisors on call to senior executives to help resolve strategic and tactical problems. The firm's approach is to provide a dedicated "Advisor On Call" -- someone you can call anytime for an honest and independent perspective, unfiltered by internal politics, organizational silos, and personalities.

Bill is widely regarded as one of most knowledgeable experts in the world in the area of Post Merger Integration (PMI). He has been involved in over 300 M&A and PMI efforts in a variety of industries and geographies, including cross border deals, and has authored numerous articles on M&A and PMI. His special emphasis is on the PMI process, as well as on the identification and quick capture of profit improvement opportunities. He has directly led teams in dozens of acquisitions, including deal structuring, negotiating, valuation, due diligence, and management of the post merger process. He has extensive experience advising senior executives on merger strategy, deal structuring, and all phases of the post merger process, and has worked closely with outside advisors such as investment bankers as part of the M&A process

Previously, he was a Partner at McKinsey & Company, Inc. where he led McKinsey’s worldwide Post Merger Management Group. While at McKinsey he also founded and led its Mergers and Acquisitions Group (M&A), and its Turnaround (Operational Profit Improvement) Group.

He is currently working on two books: "Synergies: The Art and Science of Making 2+2=5" about the reality of syergy capture, and "Permission to Be" (with Alaina Love) about how companies and employees can transform themselves by harnessing the power of purpose.
