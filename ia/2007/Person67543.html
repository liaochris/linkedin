Peter Dahl’s Summary

My educational experience is in Architectural Engineering, therefore I have a strong background in the design, installation and operation of building systems including mechanical, electrical, lighting and structures. I chose to pursue the Construction Management option of my undergraduate program as my interests focus on the communication, coordination and execution of building design and construction.

I completed my Masters degree as part of an integrated program. This allowed me to take graduate level courses in Sustainable Construction, Production Management, Project Delivery Methods and Legal Aspects of Construction.

I am currently working on my Doctorate to evaluate and improve sustainable strategies used in renovation and new construction projects. I see sustainable building strategies as key to our economic and social success in the future. In completing my research, I hope to further the building industry's knowledge and abilities to plan, design and construct facilities that promote a strong economy, a productive workforce, and a healthy environment.

As graduation approaches, I am looking forward to joining the construction industry as a practitioner. Sustainable business operations are becoming increasingly important due to pressures from the environment, society and the economy. I look forward to working with corporations, contractors and designers to plan, design and deliver superior facilities that support the bottom line.
