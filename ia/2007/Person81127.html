Simon Stickley’s Summary

I am an entrepreneurial, adept and experienced Senior Manager/Consultant with a reputation for innovative thinking. I take great personal satisfaction from the delivery of excellent business, technical and service solutions to customers. My present global role challenges me with Capture / Engagement Management responsibility for the specification, sale and implementation of multi-million dollar technology solutions and Managed / “Smart-Source” / Outsource service contracts for and on behalf of Siemens customers. Working with Multi-Sector Global Enterprise Customers and Tier One Alliance Partners, this requires the coupling of innovative, tailored, best-practice based service and leading-edge technology to deliver solutions that have real business impact usually addressing maximised efficiency and cost saving. As part of my management development in Siemens and as one of Siemens’ most senior and experienced Service Consultants I have also managed the construction and global implementation of customer strategies and value-based Marketing processes.

Since entering the IT and Communications Industry, prior to moving to Siemens in 1999, I have enjoyed roles in Corporate & Regional Account Management, Network and Communications Pre-Sales Technical/Service Consultancy and Senior IT, Communications & Service Consultancy roles. Other related experience includes project management, staff management and communication skill at every level.
