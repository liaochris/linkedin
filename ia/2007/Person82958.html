Subin Bhatia’s Summary

Subin has extensive experience in managing the design, development and implementation of IT solutions at various institutions across multiple industries. He is currently the CEO and co-founder of Suvitech Co. Ltd. which is a Thai IT BOI certified company and which is certified consultants with Ministry of Finance. At Suvitech, Subin has provided exemplary leadership skills, technical know how and business knowledge. He led successful initiatives for acquiring, retaining and enhancing the client base as well as successful execution of IT projects. Suvitech has had more than 250% annual revenue growth since inception.

Subin is also a Director with Weston Digital Technologies (AsiaPac), a BOI certified company delivering voice recording solutions designed to meet today's increasingly diverse market requirements. AudioNet™ is in use around the world, recording millions of telephone calls and vast quantities of radio traffic twenty four hours a day.

Subin started his career with Citicorp Information Technology Industries Ltd., and spent a major part of his career working with PricewaterhouseCoopers (formerly Price Waterhouse), during which he worked in various positions with their Management Consulting Services (MCS) and Financial Advisory Services (FAS).

Subin is a Thai national of Indian origin and has extensive international experience with strong domain knowledge of financial & telecom industries. He speaks four languages - English, Punjabi, Thai and Hindi.
