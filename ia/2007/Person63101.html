Vicki Kolovou’s Summary

After working for over 16years for a Greek technical Wood Industry magazine- changing between many positions in the company- and following the UK Open University IT bachelor degree, I decided to embark on my own adventure and start my own business.

The field that interested me always was advertisement and all that it included on a technical basis like graphic and web design.

My goal is to create a company that specialises in web design, SEO, internet promotion solutions combined with all around graphical solutions for our customers, who usually need someone they can trust in for advise and solutions for all their needs. By combining experts from many fields I believe we are accomplishing our goal.
