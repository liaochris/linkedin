Robin Blesch’s Summary

Motivated Multi-Award Winning Texas Filmmaker experienced in virtually every aspect of independent filmmaking. Skilled Producer able to organize small or large projects. Innovative thinker able to problem solve, form strategies, create safety nets, and accomplish goals on a deadline and budget. Strong leader able to manage small or large crews, experience working with a 10-50 person film crew with an average size of 30; Ability to keep workers motivated, working together as a team, and working toward common goals. Business person of high integrity, with a good work ethic, a healthy competitive nature, and a strong drive for success. Creator and organizer of the Wolfpack Film Group in Austin, TX (http://wpfgstudios.com/wolfpack.html); one of the major hubs in the indie film world. A driving force behind many recently produced projects in different genres, ranging from narrative films and documentaries to music videos and concept trailers for features seeking investment.

Robin's Major professional goals include: making large budget feature films independent from the influence of the major studios, establishing a full "all in house," film production studio, and creating a nationwide Wolfpack Film Group network of semi-pro/professional filmmakers who desire to work and operate under the same principles, and with the same goals as the Wolfpack.

Robin Blesch’s Specialties:

Driven and motivated professional, competent communicator, strong and proficient negotiator, and talented leader with strong organizational skills, "outside the box" creative thinking, rapid problem solving abilities, great financial management, tactical and strategic planning skills, the ability to read people with a high level of accuracy, and the ability to evaluate past project’s weak points and learn from mistakes in an attempt to increase efficiency and effectiveness of future projects.
