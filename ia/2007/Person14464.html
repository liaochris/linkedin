Fredrik Carlegren’s Summary

I am an Offering Marketing Manager in the IBM Retail Store Solutions group, with responsibility for IBM Self Checkout product requirements and go-to-market strategy. In this role, I work closely with retail customers and Business Partners to define points of alignment between their direction and IBM’s retail strategy for self checkout. Previously at IBM, I was Marketing Manager with responsibility for communications activities surrounding the IT infrastructure technologies that enable on demand business. I started at IBM as Brand Communications Strategist for IBM’s WebSphere software.

Prior to joining IBM, I graduated with an MBA in International Management from Thunderbird, The Garvin School of International Management in Phoenix, Arizona where I focused on International Marketing. During this time I was active in several leadership positions, including co-President of the e2v Club, President of ThunderVikings Scandinavian Club, and Vice President of Marketing Club.

Prior work experience included a 6 month project for Ericsson Mobile Communications in Stockholm, Sweden. Here I had responsibility for the development of new sales and marketing initiatives that would assist in the regional sales of the R380 smartphone and future products entering the portfolio. Previously, I was a business analyst at Hewitt Associates working on client implementations of new 401(k) plans and IT systems.
