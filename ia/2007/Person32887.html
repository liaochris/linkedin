Graham Hogarth’s Summary

My main expertise is in online traffic generation and measurement, where my background in statistics and applied mathematics has ben invaluable. From account management to product management, along with some pre-sales support (at WSPS, now thesearchworks) I've a wealth of experience with client facing positions.

I've been ideally placed to introduce new technologies and working practices to a number of agencies and clients, invariably focusing on increasing value, typically by analysing past successes (and failures) and building upon historical work, identifying areas which can be profitably expanded, as well as those requiring more focus to improve the quality of traffic, and re-allocating budgets as applicable.

Currently working on a contract basis with several mid-sized companies, all eager to train their staff (in terms of strategy and planning as well as technical ability) to be able to bring their online marketing in-house, or better understand and utilise external agencies' input.
