Seth Gersch’s Summary

Currently Chief Financial Officer at Fox Paine & Company, LLC a Private Equity firm, founded in 1997, that manages more than $2.7 billion. Fox Paine specializes in providing solutions and capital for management buyouts, public-to-private transactions and growth capital investments. The firm participates exclusively in friendly, cooperative transactions with the aim of creating tangible value for all parties involved.

Previously was Chief Operating Officer and Executive Committee member at ThinkEquity Partners LLC a San Francisco based boutique Investment Bank focused on the growth sectors of the economy. In that capacity was responsible for implementation of ThinkWealth Management, the division that provides fee-based wealth management services on an advisory basis. Responsible for completely rebuilding operating infrastructure including real estate in
four cities, technology architecture, the application software used throughout the firm.

Prior to that founded and served as CEO of the Broker Dealer Services division of Banc of America Securities. This division provided Correspondent Clearing Services to smaller securities brokerage firms throughout the country. Started do-novo in 1995 and grown to a recurring revenue business of $70 Million revenue with pre-tax profits in excess of 30%.

Earlier while at Montgomery Securities, the predessor firm to Banc of America Securities was responsible for of the day-to-day operating functions of Montgomery Securities including private client services, administration, real estate, finance, compliance, and operations.

Before that was at Morgan Stanley & Co., Inc. as the Co-Head of Individual Investor Services Dept. Was responsible for the overall management of the private client business of Morgan Stanley. .

Seth Gersch’s Specialties:

Also experienced in Transaction Evaluation & Negotiation, and oversight of Operations, Legal, Compliance & H.R. Management, Technology Infrastructure, P&L, Budget / Cost Improvement, Securities Clearance & Execution.
