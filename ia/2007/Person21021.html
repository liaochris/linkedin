Dave Johnson’s Summary

Senior-level Graphic Designer and Implementation specialist. Create concepts for top-tier consumer products, refine designs to client specifications, and transition designs to implementation stages. Also supervise junior designers and production artist's work to prescribed specifications.

LPK Career Achievement Highlights:
• 2005 – Winner LPK Lightning Bolt Award for team leadership.
• 2005-06 – Led successful design delineation of Swiffer brand in Western Europe. LPK awarded additional $1 million of design work for Mr. Clean & Flash brands in the region.
• 2006 – Winner Procter & Gamble Fusion Award awarded to LPK for Swiffer Sweeper product re-stage.

Dave Johnson’s Specialties:

Concepts for online focus testing (many times concepts are created on very tight timelines). Expertise in digital illustration, retouching, and story-boarding for concept work. Expert character cartoonist.
