Timothy Gray’s Summary

I am a quality-oriented, classically-trained designer with over eight years of hands-on and management experience in the fields of print design, branding, web interface design and application development. I am a dependable, self-motivated, well-organized creative thinker with an strong work ethic and an innate understanding of composition, color theory, typography and usability. I have a passion for good design and an insatiable fascination with technology. I keep up with the latest trends in design and development and offer consultation to clients developing or extending their online brand.

I am currently looking for opportunities and partnerships that would allow me to not only share my strengths, but also stretch myself as a designer and developer of cutting edge media and technology through research, experimentation and interaction with clients and colleagues.
