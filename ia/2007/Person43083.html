John Nemanic’s Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur, visionary and investor seeking new opportunities to leverage my extensive experience in starting, growing, managing and exiting firms. Having recently exited from a successful venture, I am now seeking other opportunities.

I believe very strongly in partnerships, since the vast majority of my success is directly due to working with honest, honorable, hardworking and intelligent co-founders and investors. I may be reached at john@geeksforlessDOTcom.

My experience is primarily as a start up entrepreneur, having funded and co-founded 3 businesses doing more than $25,000,000 USD in sales. Our latest venture, Geekforless.com, enjoys sales in the millions of dollars. I have also founded numerous other businesses, some successful, some not.

I have just spent over 3 years travelling extensively to Eastern Europe, specifically Southern Ukraine. My partners and I are premier experts in outsourcing software and business processes, especially to the Ukraine, noted for its very well educated and very high quality labour force.

I live in Panama, but do not manage any Panamanian based businesses. I am, however, a member of EO Panama and do have a list of extensive contacts within Panama. I am also a former member of EO Toronto (joined 1995) and YPO Toronto (joined 1999). I am also an investor in Panamanian Real Estate. My Spanish is basic, having picked up a few words from the Barrio. I am also a member of www.toplinked.com
