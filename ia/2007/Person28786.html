Fanny Awaliana’s Summary

The current project I'm coordinating is a combination between a Thai-based private-sector, Ethiopian design/arts and development project in Ethiopia. In fact, it is a full-cycle (therefore sustainable) income generating project benefiting the Ethiopian grass-root level artists, apart from the profit institution. I set up and maintain the organisation of the project, and conduct public relation and marketing activities.

Currently, about 15% of my time is dedicated to develop strategy and organise aid provision for displaced Karen population in Thai-Burma border.

As a background, I have a strong interest in and experience as a humanitarian (4 year with the International Committee of the Red Cross and a masters in Humanitarian Action from University College Dublin, Ireland). My expertises in this area are capacity building, inter-institutions coordination and management of aid.

I have done project implementation for more than 6 years now. Strategy & policy development, administration and public relations are my strengths.
