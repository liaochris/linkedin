Liz Ryan’s Summary

I was VP for HR at datacomm mfr U.S. Robotics and then co-founded the home networking startup Ucentric Systems, then launched the online discussion community for women called WorldWIT, a connected set of moderated list-servs around the world with 40,000+ members who are immensely smart, well-educated, accomplished, networked and resourceful women. You are welcome to come and join the free, very useful conversation (yes, men too) at www.worldwit.org.

NEW (2/07): We have opened registration for our annual women's leadership conference, Camp WorldWIT: Gaining Altitude, taking place May 3-6 in Estes Park, Colorado. Please join us! More info at http://www.worldwit.org/camp.aspx

Liz Ryan’s Specialties:

Workplace columnist for Business Week online, on-air workplace commentator for Denver's NBC affiliate 9news and for CNN, CNBC, MSNBC et al. Expertise in online networking, for women in particular; women & entrepreneurism, and women in corporate leadership. Writer and speaker on post-millennium workplace issues and work/family conflict.
