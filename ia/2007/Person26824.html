Emi Gal’s Summary

I started my first “business” at the age of 10, creating business cards for the teachers in my school. I used the money I earned to buy sweets. At the age of 16 I found my passion for programming, and started building websites for different friends and non-profit organizations. Won different prizes for web sites / web applications I created.

I worked as a web application developer for some companies in Romania and design companies in United States. By the age of 18 I had my own clients so I felt the need to go one step further. That’s when I started working on the business plan, strategy and vision for Brainient. Three years have passed and I feel that starting my own company was the best decision I made until now.
