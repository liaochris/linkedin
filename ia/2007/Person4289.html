    Aleksey Matiychenko is a Vice President in Risk Management & Quantitative Research group for Ivy Asset Management (a leading US fund of hedge funds). Within Ivy's risk group, his focus is on evaluating hedge funds within credit and fixed income oriented strategies (i.e. credit long/short, structured credit, distressed). In addition Matiychenko is responsible for risk management and quantitative analysis of Ivy's structured finance business which includes structured credit business (Ivy has issued two CDO squared deals) and structured products related to hedge funds. He has led the effort to develop quantitative models (loan level default models, water fall models, etc) for Ivy’s structured credit business and later adopted some of these tools for modeling risks of Collateral Fund Obligations (CFOs). Aleksey has MBA degree from New York University Leonard Stern School of Business. Aleksey is also a certfied Financial Risk Manager by GARP and is CFA and CAIA charter holder.
