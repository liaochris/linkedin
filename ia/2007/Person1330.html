Adam S. Tracy, Esq.’s Summary

A former competitive rugby player and licensed attorney, Adam S. Tracy currently serves as the Executive Vice President of Ecology Coatings, nanotechnology research pioneer and the world leader in the development of environmentally safe, nano-engineered industrial coatings. Ecology is set to become publicly traded through an APO transaction in March, 2007.

Tracy's diverse background allows him to operate in an number of capacities for Ecology. On the financial side, Tracy concentrates on new business development through the negotiation of joint venture agreements as well as the strategic licensing of Ecology’s proprietary technology. Additionally, Tracy manages Ecology’s investor relations function and is actively involved in the capital formation process. On the legal side, Tracy’s responsibilities include the management of Ecology’s risk management programs, including its Sarbanes-Oxley and environmental compliance initiatives.

Tracy’s past work experience traverses a broad range of industries, markets and disciplines. Tracy began his career as a trader in British Petroleum’s energy risk management practice where his responsibilities included providing forward market analysis and market intelligence for the Southeastern United States energy markets. While in private law practice, Tracy gained extensive experience representing issuers and underwriters in connection with public and private securities offerings. He also represented a wide variety of commercial clients in connection with bankruptcies, reorganizations and restructurings. Other notable work experience includes engagements with Accenture, Philip Morris and the United States Department of Justice.

Tracy holds bachelor’s degrees in finance and computer applications development from the University of Notre Dame, in Notre Dame, Indiana. He holds his masters in business administration and juris doctorate from DePaul University, in Chicago Illinois.
