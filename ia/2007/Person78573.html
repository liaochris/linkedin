Sandrine Guétin’s Summary

I have been working on the development of the distribution channel strategy in Europe for Xerox and now at France Printemps as recruitment manager and responsible for HR marketing.

At France Printemps I am responsible to re-design the recruitment stratgy and to deploy an employer brand since France Printemps is not any more in the PPR group but re-positionning as a leading luxury fashion retail shop.

At Xerox I have been responsible for a program called "Distribution Capacity" which aims at leveraging the business with a pan-European HR strategy.

- Development of an employer brand, supported by an HR intranet & a recruitment website,

- Development of a competitive sourcing strategy & pan-European media partnerships,

- Development of an e-recruitment strategy & implementation of an ATS (Application Tracking System),

- Development of a selection & assessment strategy supported by an on-line platform.

- Attrition & Dévelopment Management plans deployment with countries' operations

This experience gave me the opportunity not only to lead a European projects that tackles HR, e-business, marketing & financials stakes, but also to manage international programs with a European team and to deal with change-management across different cultures & countries.

Besides, I have now a good knowledge and understanding of how HR, supported by "e" technologies, can be a competitive and differentiation stake for the companies which will succeed in attracting, recruiting, selecting, developing and retaining tomorrow's talents.

I am particularly interested to focus now on talents selection and management, as this is a major concern for many companies.

I am also very keen on working in an organisation where HR become transversal and international but still continues to meet local expectations and to address people on a one to one basis.
