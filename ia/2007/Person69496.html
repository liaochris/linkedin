Ryan Sawchuk’s Summary

Denizen of the Net since 1997: Co-founded Regional ISP, Dream Communications located in Boston Massachusetts and Serving Southern New England and New Hampshire. Currently managing Product Marketing efforts at Cendant Travel Services, (Orbitz.com & Cheaptickets.com where I lead a team of Product Managers and Analysts focused on Customer Product Experience and Marketing.
