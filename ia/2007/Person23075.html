DEREMIAH, *CPE's BIO:
From Humble beginnings out of the dark Chicago Public Housing Projects, through the divorce of his parents and welfare lines, Deremiah has emerged as a Champion.

This International award-winning speaker received the coveted Acres of Diamonds award from Nightingale-Conant, the world’s authority on professional development programs that includes such speakers as Brian Tracy, Les Brown, Dennis Waitley, Jay Abraham and Stephen Covey. The previous recipient of the award was TV producer Drew Carey.

Deremiah, *CPE, has been mentioned in the Chicago Sun Times, Tribune, Dallas Morning News and over 169 other major newspapers across the country and on-line. His endorsements from major professional speakers and authors like Nido Qubein, Mark Victor Hansen, Dr. Myles Munroe and Don Mitchell make him stand out. Deremiah, *CPE's work with the Boys & Girls Clubs, Marwen Foundation, AWANA and his speaking engagements for the KIWANIS International organization, KiDS CLUB, and The Junior Leagues has helped him to have greater impact for the cause of children.

Partnering effectively with corporate giants has put Deremiah on the road to Raising $100 Million for Children at-risk. You see the children do matter because they’re the customers of this generation and the next. While most motivational speakers just speak Deremiah, *CPE is a doer as he walks the talk by mentoring nearly 50 children every year.
