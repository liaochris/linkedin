Ethan Giffin’s Summary

Groove Commerce helps small to medium sized company’s bridge the gap between marketing and technology. By becoming an extension of your organization we act as an 'outsourced eCommerce department' to provide an end to end suite of services.

We help to design, develop, manage and market your web site. We also have a very extensive network of partners that allows us to consult and manage any extra services needed to take your business to the next level.

Our eCommerce consulting services include:

* eCommerce Site Design and Development
* Managed eCommerce Consulting Services
* Organic Search Engine Optimization
* Pay-Per-Click Search Engine Marketing
* Shopping Feed Optimization
* Web Analytics Consulting
* MySpace/Social Media Marketing

I am eager to find new clients nationwide and would love referrals of any type. Feel free to send canned goods as well – as I may go hungry ;).

Our leadership
Groove Commerce is the brainchild of CEO Ethan Giffin, an industry expert in e-commerce, e-marketing and the dynamic technologies behind it. He designed, launched and managed Thingamajob.com, a portal for some 18,000+ continuously updated positions with a database of over four million members. For years, Ethan has lived and breathed conversion rates, search engine visibility and web analytics. His goal is for Groove Commerce to bring that expertise to clients who want to successfully conduct eCommerce – without becoming experts on eCommerce technology.

Ethan’s industry expertise has led to him speaking at many of the industry’s top conferences including:
• Search Engine Strategies
• Ad:Tech
• Frost & Sullivan

Among Ethan’s entrepreneurial and eclectic background have been stints as on air radio talent, a nightclub owner and a producer of a weekly music television magazine.
