    Teknologisk Institut

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Research industry)

    September 2005 — September 2007 (2 years 1 month)

    Did a complete system for monitoring the temperature and humidity inside concrete walls and floors. The system included HW design, PCB, Embedded SW, Drivers and porting of FreeRTOS. The system consistes of a number of humidity sensors based on the MCU/RF from Nordic VLSI called nRF9E5. A transceiver collectes data from the sensors and buffers it up and controls the behaviour of the sensors. The sensors and the transceiver communicates on RF at 433MHz. The collected data and alarms etc can be uploaded to a server via GPRS or to the user via email and SMS. The transceiver is based on ATMega1281 from ATmel and a GSM module from round solutions. A PC program controls the behaviour of the transceiver.
    Other ongoing projects including RF communication, SAW sensors, oxygen sensors.
