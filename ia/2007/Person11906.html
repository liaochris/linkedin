Bob Louden’s Summary

Bob is a Consulting IT Specialist on the Tivoli Sales Enablement team responsible for training and supporting worldwide sales professionals on Tivoli Business Automation solutions. Bob has worked with IBM for more than twenty-five years in roles including product development, sales, sales support, and consulting. Bob’s experience includes teaching, speaking at conferences, authoring six IBM Redbooks and eight technical papers, and consulting on information technology applications, infrastructure, and services. His technical expertise includes IBM mainframe environments, networking, and systems management. Together, his experience and expertise enable him to help organizations apply technology solutions to their business issues and opportunities.
