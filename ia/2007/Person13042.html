Brian Lambert, CRSP’s Summary

I am passionate about the Sales Profession.

I am looking for others to collaborate with who share my passion for sales excellence, sales professionalism, and sales strategy as a competitive advantage.

If you are a great salesperson (you know who you are!), or if you know of a great salesperson (you know who they are!) PLEASE connect to me.

Selling is the world's most complicated profession. To help define it and advance it, I founded the United Professional Sales Association and sit on the Board of Directors for the organization in 1999.

I use a wide range of skills to help sales people and sales organizations succeed:

* I am uniquely poised to help company bridge any gap between marketing strategy and sales tactics.

* I network and provide value to sales and marketing executives for Fortune 1000 companies and emerging high-growth companies.

* UPSA standards that I authored have been downloaded in 19 countries by over 4000 people. These standards are used by companies and inviduals to build a common language for sales and marketing as well as create a solid foundation for revenue generation.

* As a top producing sales professional for Dun & Bradstreet, the world's leader in business-to-business marketing and sales information, I have a proven ability to drive sales revenue in an existing portfolio as well as selling new business opportunities. I was the number one rep in the southeast, and number 2 in the country (out of 350 people).
