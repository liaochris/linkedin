Rob Boudon’s Summary

Rob has always had a mind for business (taught by his father). At 14 years old he started his own comic book stand. He learned very quickly about supply and demand, how to make a profit and when to cut losses. By 16 he was a manager and a buyer in the family run business... Video Rays (a video rental store).

Rob Boudon graduated from Kent State University in 2000 with a bachelor's degree in Advertising. After working in advertising and inside sales for the local paper (Cleveland Plain Dealer) he made a brake for New York City.

Once Rob arrived in the Big Apple he developed a passion for photography, videography and design. He was tapped for his resources by various production companies, musicians, models, actors and composers around the city as well as by websites such as Textamerica.com.

These days he spends most of his free time blogging with Flickr, videoblogging with a few popular video sites and brainstorming for podcast ideas. He wholeheartedly favors "new media" and is excited to see the changes they bring.

Rob's thoughts on our future... "The internet is enabling all of us to become better educated about each other and the world we live in. This is happening much faster than we could have imagined 10 years ago. Educating each other is the key to success as a people. I can only be optimistic about the future."
