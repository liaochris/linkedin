Ifor Ffowcs-Williams’s Summary

Cluster development: Recognised globally as a lead expert in the training of economic development professionals; the start-up and review of cluster programmes; establishing local cluster development initiatives.

Global client base includes World Bank, IFC, OECD, UNIDO, Commonwealth Secretariat, and government agencies in Australia, New Zealand, Canada, USA, Sweden, Finland, Iceland, Norway, Czech Republic, Estonia, Poland, Georgia, Scotland, Wales, Ireland, England, South Africa, Bangladesh, Malaysia, Trinidad, Tanzania, Uganda, Kenya, Mozambique, Cambodia...

Frequent presenter at economic development conferences.
