Rey Marques’s Summary

Entrepreneur in Web Startups - Currently, my partner Reuben Fine and I are creating Pipeline Producer (www.pipelineproducer.com), a website for real estate agents that automatically creates 1) Flyers 2) Door Hangers 3) Postcards. An agent simply uploads the listing information and photos, chooses a design and Pipeline Producer creates everything. You can then print yourself or forward to a print shop for printing. The key value that our system brings to the market is speed, ease of use, and printing convenience.

Rey Marques’s Specialties:

I specialize in simplifying web applications so that an average user can understand it, navigate through it, and fully utilize it. My strengths lie in branding, usuability, and networking.
