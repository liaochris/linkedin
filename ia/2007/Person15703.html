Channy Yun’s Summary

Welcome. This is Seokchan (Channy) Yun lived in Jeju, South Korea. I had 10 year experiences of internet business especially online music, e-commerce, e-payment and internet security based on world-wide web. I made such own business models and developed them in my first internet venture company. I learned account, marketing and management as well technology as a member of board. I had an experience strategic partnership with various companies such as internet portals, recording and telecommunication company. Also I had opportunities to work with VeriSign and Thawte together and I became to be good at global business through these experiences.

In Daum, I involved processes of technical strategy and support of software engineers over 300. My job forwards to technical evangelism to connect between my company and 3rd party developers.

I'm also very interested in open source. I has engaged Mozilla project since 2002, and I'm korean L10N module owner for Firefox and Thunderbird. I'm a leader of korean Mozilla community too.

My second interest is Web Standards by activities of early web community in 1995 and Mozilla's standard evangelism. I'm leader of korean Web Standards (http://webstandard.or.kr). Finally, Web 2.0 is my third interesting things. Because my job is to search trends and plan strategies on the web, naturally I do that. I gave several presentation for Web 2.0 technology in many korean conferences. You can track korean Web 2.0 in http://koreacrunch.com of my english blog.

My technical writings are continued in my Channy's Blog(http://channy.creation.net/blog). Especially I have written for ZDNet Korea about various technical subjects on the web since 2004.
