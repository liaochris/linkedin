Dan Light (dan@danlightdirect.com)’s Summary

I am Dan Light Consulting, a one man LLC with a world wide network of like-minded and similarly skilled Affiliates. I’m usually brought in by companies and individuals dissatisfied with some aspect of their lives to produce immediate results in increasing customers, reduce expenses, increase profit, balance quality of life and set achievable goals.

I help remove the barriers to growth and transforming potential into exceptional performance. About 80% of my clients double their business in 4-8 weeks.
