Trond Johannessen’s Summary

I am currently in the second year of being an entrepreneur, running my own start-up firms in Italy and the Netherlands as the PHIMARK Group. The business is focused on sale of services and equipment to operators of wireless services delivery networks world wide. BLACKWOLF, a PHIMARK Group company, is currently pursuing a leveraged buy-out (LBO) with a profitable firm in the wireless services industry, and working on the organization of several CDMA and WiMAX projects in EMEA and Asia.

In the period 1994 – 2005 I worked as a finance broker, consultant, analyst, and executive for hire with start-up and young firms in the wireless industry. I placed a few private equity deals; rounded up a lot of spectrum for wireless broadband and so pioneering this sector in Europe; built numerous business plans and investor materials for clients, and coached some CEOs in the process. From 2000 I have been doing consulting business as the Fort Corbenic project.

After graduation from Cornell University in 1980 through 1993 I worked for large companies Chase, IBM and Telenor in business development, sales, marketing, customer financing product management, finance, accounting and auditing.
