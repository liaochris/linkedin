.Keith Sawka’s Summary

We are the leader for online flash video and interactive media for the hospitality industry. We specialize in high end customized sales tools that can be used online, email marketing, rich media banner ads, digital kiosks, CD/DVD, and in-room hotel TV channels. These solutions can fit into any budget. Our clients range from Nextel, Seattle Seahawks, InterContinental Hotel Group, Hilton Hotels, Radisson Hotels, America Online, Hasbro, Pemco Insurance, Costco, US Bank, and many more. We have recently launched our online interactive guided tours for hundreds of IHG hotels around the world. We are the primary vendor for these hotels. Our goal is to provide the ultimate online rich media experience for hotel customers.

We also provide interactive kiosks for hotel lobbies and off site installations. These can be used for interactive floor plans, wireless mobile printing, flight check-in, way finding directories, and virtual concierge solutions.

If you are interested in talking about an online sales & marketing tool or an interactive kiosk solution, please contact me today. 206.229.8075 | keith@advancedmethod.com
