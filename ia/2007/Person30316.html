Michael Lemm’s Summary

Briefly, my company assists businesses in finding the best solution for their dedicated voice/data network needs...and our services are NO cost.

We have a dedicated technical staff research and negotiate all dedicated voice/data requirements....for single or multi-location projects....saving the headaches & frustration of doing it yourself. This covers T1, DS3, OC3 thru OC192, GigE connectivity, enterprise VoIP, & more. Whatever the project need is, finding the best combination of capability, quality, and cost effectiveness is critical to every business strategy. It's also our mission to make that happen for our clients. We do it all, making this time and effort consuming evolution easier for the client.

Essentially....a company leverages our time and effort to maximize theirs at NO cost to them.

Simply enter your detailed requirements in our webportal & automatically receive instant real time rate quote info via email (1st cut assessment)....comparing multiple providers available for the specific location designated (sorry, US locations only). We'll follow that up with more detailed research based on your comments.

http://DS3-Bandwidth.com/coverage.cfm

Once a client selects a solution that interests them, a technical advisor will be in immediate contact to discuss the details, confirm the best pricing, and assist with the acquisition process. We'll negotiate on their behalf, do the paperwork, monitor provisioning and installation, and even run interference for them with the chosen vendor for the life of the contract. All at NO cost to them.

Also....if you sell or install LAN or WAN networks, phone systems, telecommunication equipment, router, switches, servers, business VoIP equipment, IP PBX, or point of sale hardware, please consider joining our VAR (Value Added Reseller) Program. In basic terms, if you help us find T1, DS3, & OCx service customers, we will help find you equipment and service leads!
