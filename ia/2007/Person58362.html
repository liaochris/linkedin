Michael Thomas’s Summary

Michael Thomas is a former corporate investigator in the Washington, DC area. He was a co-founder of Caveat Research, LLC., a business-oriented private investigative firm providing sophisticated corporate research and investigative services to corporate boards, law firms, consultancies and investment banks.

Michael has also done time with expert services firm, Round Table Group; consumer safety consultancy, Safetyforum.com; investigative firm, the James Mintz Group as well as the National Archives. He currently works for Capterra, Inc., a vertical search portal for enterprise software selection.

Michael Thomas’s Specialties:

In addition to my marketing and business management duties with Capterra, I am also the primary contributor to The Daily Caveat, a blog covering the business, legal and research communities from an investigator's perspective.
