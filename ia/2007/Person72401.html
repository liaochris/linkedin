WHY YOU SHOULD WORK WITH ME:

1) Customer service
Respect, responsiveness plus "old-fashioned good manners"

2) Focus on Quality
When I do a search, I start with the technical qualifications, and then I look for people that are hard-working, productive, motivated, bright, dedicated, articulate, responsible, respectful, friendly, mature and have a good sense of humor.

3) High Quality match = reduced turnover
My approach directly impacts the bottom line, as high quality matches between company and candidate greatly reduce turnover rates.

4) No Pressure
My pressure-free approach gives the company and the candidate breathing room so each party can see if the 'chemistry' is right.

5) Competitive fees
I charge a fraction of what all the other search firms charge. My fees are targeted at small firms who can't or won't pay standard search firm fees. My fees are so competitive that it puts my services within range of even the smallest companies.
