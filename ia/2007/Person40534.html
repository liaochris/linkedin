  • General Manager

    MyFamilyLaw.com

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Legal Services industry)

    May 2007 — Present (9 months)

    Launched in 2007, MyFamilyLaw.com is a start-up "information portal" that provides relevant articles and commentary on current Family Law topics like divorce, custody and paternity. The site serves as a source of information and resources for the general public, and an advertising platform for lawyers and other professionals targeting the MyFamilyLaw audience.

  • President
