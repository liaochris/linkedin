Boban Alempijevic’s Summary

Ending phase of my masters thesis in Organizational Sociology.

During my research period within the masters levels at the University of Helsinki I have developed an apatite for organizational processes and changes on all levels within organizations, small, medium and large. Organizational culture as driving force for sustain and change, process changes from outside in and from inside (top-down or down-top related) are all areas that are of special interest for me. Except the pure academical interest within these areas my personal so far unsatisfied thirst for Information Technology is driving me towards my goal of working within the IT sphere when I finally finish my thesis this December (2007).

Management or any type of HR position within the IT sphere is what I will aim for when my research time is done at the University.
