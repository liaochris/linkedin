Dwayne Conyers’s Summary

Mr. Conyers is a graduate of the Warner Bros. Comedy Writers Workshop and studied screenwriting with Luigi DiFonzo at Harvard and with Michael Hague and the late Carl Sautter at the American Film Institute.

Dwayne’s professional writing career started in the 1980’s when he was assigned an episode of the late ABC series “Living Dolls” (starring Oscar® winner Halle Berry and "King of Queens" star Leah Remini). His portfolio also includes substantial multimedia writing, including training video for the White House and interactive video programming for the government of the Republic of Singapore.

His expertise extends beyond writing into producing. He is Executive Producer of independent film “High Point,” raising the majority of the production budget and arranging foreign and domestic distribution opportunities. Dwacon currently has other feature projects in pre-production.

Dwayne has published over a dozen books under his Dwacon Publications and Powerhouse Publications banners. He is author of many of the titles.

Dwayne's right-brain/left-brain ambidexterity includes software engineering. Dwayne is a practicing member of the Worldwide Institute of Software Architects and holds certifications with Microsoft, Lotus and Brainbench.

Dwayne holds the title of Tennessee Squire (with ownership of property in Moore County, TN), granted by the Jack Daniels Distillery. He is also a member of the Striding Man Society.

Dwacon, Inc. is a Keiretsu corporation with a rapidly expanding international profile. Corporate headquarters are in Beverly Hills, California. The company also has operations in Texas and the Washington DC area.

Dwacon Asia is a partnership with Ricco Donna Investments, based in New Zealand. Dwacon has also brokered a trade agreement with the Singapore trade agency.
