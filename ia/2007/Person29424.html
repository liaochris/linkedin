Tom Bomar (tbomar@fitlaunch.com)’s Summary

Our company, FIT Launch, ultimately hopes to keep ourselves, our partners, and staff busy providing business, education, and leisure services to professionals within the fitness industry. Our systems provide excellent resources to the fitness pro's while at the same time allowing for unique avenues in reaching consumers in need of our professional's services.

We also utilize our pre-screened database of wellness professionals for work exchange programs with all inclusive resorts. These programs provide group fitness instructors, yoga instructors, personal trainers, masseuse, medical staff, hair & nail pro's, squash pro's and tennis pro's on short term stay exchanges.

I hold a Strength & Conditioning Specialist certification with education and experience in business development for the long term care industry. Locally, providing personal training, athletic conditioning, and yoga programs for personal and corporate settings.

Tom Bomar (tbomar@fitlaunch.com)’s Specialties:

As a company we are Regional Master Trainers for the Resist-A-Ball organization and Faculty providers for the American Council on Exercise. We also partner with Fit Bodies, Inc., a Kentucky fitness company, to provide the Fit Beach Conference held in Negril Jamaica each year.

We specialize in continuing education, all-inclusive working vacations, business development & consulting, and retreat programming for the health & fitness industry.
