  • Owner, CEO

    Seismart Technologies Ltd

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Oil & Energy industry)

    2005 — Present (2 years)

    Campaigns for collecting seismic data cost millions of dollars, typically varying from tens to several hundreds of dollars per linear kilometer. Unfortunately not all surveys give satisfactory results. The reason can be acquisition defeats, bad weather, unexpected geological complexity, unsatisfactory survey design quality, and so on. However, a better approach to the processing can radically change the commercial potential of such data.

    Seismart can reprocess old and new seismic data that were previously discarded, assuming that the quality was too poor or the data itself too critical to generate data images of reasonable quality. Seismart solves this problem by using my own unique technology able to separate the various components of the signal and remove unwanted parts of the signal.

    Other applications under development based on the same core concept will be available in the near future to optimize the velocity field by a special type of PSDM and to get cleaner final images.
