Mary Pope-Handy’s Summary

I am a full-time, second generation REALTOR® since early 1993 (my Mother, Pat Pope, was a local REALTOR® from 1956 until her death in 1996)

I have an extensive real state education - including eight college level courses in real estate. Additionally I hold 6 real estate designations and one certification: ABR (Accredited Buyer's Representative), CRS (Certified Residential Specialist), SRES (Seniors Real Estate Specialist), ePro (Internet Marketing Certification), RECS (Real Estate Cybersace Specialist), CNHS (Certified New Homes Specialist), and ASP (Accredited Staging Professional).

Each year I attend conferences, workshops and courses to better my professional skills and knowledge.


In Dec 2004 I completed a book, Get The Best Deal When Selling Your Home In Silicon Valley, which can be found in local bookstores and online via Amazon.

Together with my mentor, Frances Flynn Thorsen, we excelled throughout a 14 week realty blogging competition and were able to assign the $5000 winnings to the charity of our choice, CARE. Being dedicated to cutting edge resources in real estate and having a half dozen websites, blogging looked like "the next great thing" in realty marketing - so I jumped in with both feet. Mary Pope-Handy’s Specialties:
