    What I Speak is What I Breathe

    (Partnership; Writing and Editing industry)

    July 2006 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    "What I Speak is What Breathe," is the tall-short story of a Tamil who, for the first time travels outside Tamil Nadu and is hit by...THE WORLD.

    Senthil is a common man — one of those proud, patriotic, common men who exist in every place, every country; the only differences being their respective geographic influences.

    What I Speak is What I Breathe is Senthil's journey, in more ways than one. Exploring through individual identities and related jingoism in this shrinking globe, it is an experiment in connecting languages, religion, spirituality, humanity and of course — logic, which tries so hard to bring them all together.

    The book is published by Frog Books and released on September 14, 2007.

    I am also working on a collection of short stories and a non-fiction book.
