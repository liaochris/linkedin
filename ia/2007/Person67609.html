Peter Goedtkindt’s Summary

Peter is currently working in a complex role inside a single-family-office*, involving a lot of R&D on IT and financial subjects.
He was trained as an experimental physicist, after an international career at different large-budget laboratories throughout Europe (CERN, AMOLF, ESRF,...), he moved to Switzerland in early 1999 and started a second carreer in IT. He participated in different start-ups originating out of the Federal Polytechnic Institute of Lausanne (EPFL).
Later, he joined Orange Communications where he was recognized as the technical reference for the operational and analytical Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Billing domains, including document management, document design, campaign management, retention, direct sales.
Peter is always looking for new challenges, to use his creativity, technical know-how and people management skills.
