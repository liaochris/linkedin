Jack Schwartz’s Summary

A Senior Technologist, with with over 25 years of experience in architecting, designing, implementing, managing, innovating, teaching and evaluating software/hardware systems tools, applications, methodologies and architectures. Serving as a technology innovator, strategist, architect, manager, educator, and principal software developer specializing in the design and development of, Thin-client Web and wireless architectures, tools and products, Intelligent wireless and Web remote presentation architectures, tools and products, Interactive forms and database management tools and runtimes, Web and wireless enterprise development architectures and environments, Publish-and-subscribe and Peer-to-peer messaging architectures and products, Web-based automated data acquisition architectures and products, Workflow and process flow system automation technology, Computer automated software engineering methodologies and products

As a hands-on software product development leader, using an incremental product release approach to life-cycle management, loosely-coupled asynchronous approach to architecture, lead and motivated numerous teams to deliver innovative, adaptable, scalable, and efficient solutions on time and within budget.

As a recognized and published expert in enterprise wireless and Web Internet software technology, have worked with major industry analysts to develop technology white papers and have served as a speaker on technology conference panels.
