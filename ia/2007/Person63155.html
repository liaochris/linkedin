Newman Huh’s Summary

I am an experienced designer and illustrator with nearly 17 years of experience in the design and visual communications industries. My primary focus is information design: the art of merging graphics, illustrations, photos and messages to effectively translate complex processes -- or to simply communicate ideas in a graphical fashion.
