Larry Kettler’s Summary

Larry Kettler brings over 23 years of software and hardware experience working both nationally and internationally, helping build technology companies from the ground up with his leadership and expertise. As President & CEO of Linspire, Inc., Kettler is responsible for guiding and managing the company's overall business strategy. An accomplished corporate executive, Kettler was Linspire's Vice President of Worldwide Sales & Marketing prior to becoming President & CEO.

Anticipating technological trends is Kettler's specialty, in every executive role held Kettler has been ahead of the product adoption tipping point discovering high growth potential in companies and quickly leading them to rapid expansion and market success. On the international front, Kettler has provoked multiple strategic partnerships with top-tier companies and opened offices in countries worldwide, including Latin America, Japan, and Europe.

Before his work at Linspire, Kettler served as Vice President of Channel Sales & Business Development at Raindance Communications [NASDAQ: RNDC], a leading provider of Web and phone conferencing and collaboration services. His innovative work there positioned the company for rapid sales growth and numerous successful mergers and acquisitions. Prior to leading the sales efforts at Raindance, Kettler served as Director of Business Development at Contigo Software, where he facilitated a partnership between the company and Raindance, leading to a $90 million acquisition. A technology industry veteran, Kettler also served as Director of Worldwide Sales and Marketing for KONEXX, Inc., a manufacturer of computer telephony solutions.

Kettler holds a bachelor's of science degree in business marketing from San Diego State University.
