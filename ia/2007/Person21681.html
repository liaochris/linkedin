David Lorenzo - dlorenzo@dlorenzo.com’s Summary

David V. Lorenzo is the Managing Partner of The Gallup Organization’s Executive Development and Organizational Effectiveness Group. In this role he consults with the leadership of Fortune 500 companies on Executive Performance Management, Succession Planning, Learning and Development and Integrated Marketing Communications.

Lorenzo joined The Gallup Organization’s Partner Group in early 2002 to lead the start-up of Gallup’s Manhattan Division. Under his direction, that business unit became Gallup’s most successful office. Lorenzo’s team has helped several Fortune 500 businesses drive Organizational Transformation initiatives. High-profile companies such as Pfizer, MetLife, Saks Fifth Avenue, Marriott and ADP have turned to Lorenzo’s team for strategic guidance and direction.

Lorenzo is also Chairman of a think-tank that coaches entrepreneurs and independent professionals on Business Strategy, Sales, Marketing and Work-Life Balance. In this role Lorenzo works with business owners to unlock their hidden talent and use their natural ability to make more money, have more fun and enjoy the freedom that should accompany business ownership.

As a respected Author, Columnist and Speechwriter, Lorenzo has been published in various business and trade journals. His work has been featured by such respected publications and associations as Investors Business Daily, Human Resources Executive Magazine and the American Management Association. Lorenzo also writes for MaxBizAdvantage.com where he is featured as “The Business Guru”. His first book, titled Career Intensity: Business Strategy for Workplace Warriors and Entrepreneurs, sold out in book stores in less than 90 days.

Lorenzo is member of the Wharton Fellows of the University of Pennsylvania. He received his MBA from the Lubin School of Business at Pace University, and a Masters of Science Degree in Strategic Communications from Columbia University in New York City.
