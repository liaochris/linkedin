Marco Calvo’s Summary

My background includes a total of over 13 years in the IT industry with positions ranging from college Co-ops to Project Management, Systems Engineering, and System Analyst. During this time I have developed an expertise with most flavors of Microsoft based OS, and worked with other OS types including UNIX, MAC OS and Novell. I have learned different scripting/programing languages including VBScript, WMI, HTML, Java Script, and Java. My experience has been from basic trouble shooting to project management and deployments; from end-user support to detailed design and deployment of infrastructure servers and systems analysis.

Since most of my experience is as a part of a team of technical professionals, I have developed leadership and teamwork skills. I've also learned to interact diplomatically with a diverse range of personnel including anyone from average end-users to high ranking executives.

Going forward my goal is to find a challenging position utilizing my technical skills and expanding on my Business Systems Analyst expertize.
