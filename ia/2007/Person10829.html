Bob Gale’s Summary

After nearly 30 years in Advertising and Marketing, I am most grateful to those Industry pros who took their personal time to mentor and coach me through the good, bad and off strategy. To all of those whose gift of personal time was freely given, I dedicate my practice to you. You’know who you are and I recognize the high value of your time and mentorship. I hope to repay the favor through my practice.But I would not be in business today without the mentorship of my career-long partner, Pat Lipton.

Now Pat Lipton Van Gorp, she is living the good life after an amazing era as the best of class recruiter. She humbles me with her rolodex of wisdom - there will never be an equal. Her honesty, integrity and passion gave a jump-start to help build thousands of careers.

At Gale Executive Recruiting we follow a simple mission; give back to those who value the gift as much as you do. This is not a "job shop" it is a career coaching "safe haven"; we are here to serve the long-term career needs of our candidates first. Our Clients appreciate our high level of service and dedication to our top talent. Each client wants the best, those who work with us understand what great lengths we go to find the perfect match. Perfection lies only in ataining the top talent, shared values & respect for individuality and a balanced lifestyle.
.
As an exclusive firm, you must have a direct relationship with us or a client candidate. We will take the time to know you and your talent indepth before we ever talk about specific opportunities. Every relationship we have is our long term bond, we guide and advance along with you...
