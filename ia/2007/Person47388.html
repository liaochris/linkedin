Kent Cunningham’s Summary

Proven history of working directly with customers to enable the “winning solution”, through designing an offer that maximizes customer value through the contributions of people, processes, technology and services to address their unique business challenges.

Strong background in unified communications, branch office optimization, core switching and directory services. Security expertise includes endpoint compliance, wireless networking, secure multimedia, and active response remediation systems using intrusion detection with information management.

Three years involvement as a strategy and vision team member for a global telecommunications firm.

Network infrastructure experience includes systems design and implementation of multi-service, highly-available converged network environments. Specific technology strengths include IP routing, collaborative multimedia/voice solutions through SIP/XML/H.323, public/private wireless solutions, and the implementation of infrastructure security mechanisms for edge, core and endpoint inspection.

Refined ability to negotiate with senior executives regarding technology and business development, while leveraging experienced analytical skills for working creatively and quantitatively to produce strategic and integrative solutions.

Accomplished background of conducting and analyzing market research with competitive analysis

Routinely selected for numerous public speaking engagements around the world including senior executive briefings, corporate technology planning sessions, North American universities, and numerous industry trade shows.
