Eric Meger’s Summary

Senior general manager with core strengths in a combination of strategy, marketing, technology and international experience. Extensive experience with strategic alliances and growth through M&A. After an early carreer in physics and radar systems engineering, obtained an MBA and came up through a strategic marketing path with heavy emphasis on products and technology. Success in creating new products and driving them to market success. Having had large company experience, much of my career has been with small to mid-size growing companies.
