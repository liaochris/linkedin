Lou and Robin Bonaventura’s Summary

We are members of Success University and have founded the Success Network Group! We help other people in personal development, business development and career growth. We are always looking for people who are, or have the potential to be, good, strong leaders to lead and train new teams.

Success University was formed by the collaboration of works from more than 50 of the world's top authors, speakers, and educators from the discipline of personal achievement including Brian Tracey, Jay Abraham, Cynthia Kersey, Jim Cathcart, Les Brown, Jeffery Combs, Robert Allen, Jim Rohn, Zig Ziglar, Tony Alessandra and many others who have collectively helped millions of people achieve success.Our mission is to create an international learning environment that teaches the time-tested laws of success.

We (personally and our team) will be here to support you throughout your journey of discovery and learning on your road to success! For less than a cup of coffee a day you'll learn the secrets to skyrocketing your success in life, love and finances from over 50 of the world's greatest experts! The curriculum you'll enjoy as a student of Success University has been designed with one thing in mind - to teach you the right kind of knowledge that will raise the level of success in every area of your life.

As the first and only Success University, we firmly believe that knowledge alone won't make you wealthy ... We go to school for 12 to 20 years of our lives but are simply never taught the right kind of knowledge to be successful in life. In today's world, you need to THINK in a different way to achieve real success.

Success University now brings you the most advanced online courses on SUCCESS ever assembled. You will discover the secret attitudes, techniques, methods, and strategies that, when you learn, will skyrocket your success in virtually every area of your life!
