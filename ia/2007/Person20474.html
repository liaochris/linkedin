Danny Frietman (danny@frietman.nl)’s Summary

Strategic and creative consultant capable of bridging the gap between customer business needs and technical possibities and solutions.

Background
After finishing his masters in business administration at the Erasmus University, Danny joined IBM. After working there for several years as Marketing Manager he was asked to join Exact Software in there quest to grow in Europe. He switched to TCC, a full service advertising agency, as an account director to help customers in executing there marketing and communication strategy. In 2001 he started his path as an entrepeneur and started Senza Fili, a mobile system integrator. His new step (2006) is as Managing Consultant at Marqit Advisory (www.marqit.nl) where he will take his advisory skills to the next level. Marqit Advisory is an independent advisory firm who facilitates and supports end users when choosing a mobile, software(tooling) and sourcing solution. His other role is as analist in the mobile area.
