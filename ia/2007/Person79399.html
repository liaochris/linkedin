Shiva Venkatraman’s Summary

I am a change agent with 14 years of combined products and services experience in SaaS (Software as a Service), SmartPhones, Bluetooth, RFID, Wi-Fi, GSM/3G, enterprise software (CRM/ERP), on-demand solutions, IT Utilities like hosting/telecom/wireless and associated services. I can figure out the strategies required for change and then specify products and services to meet the strategic needs. Through my exceptional execution and leadership capability, I can launch and market products and services to create new markets.

I am proud to have a track record of delivering to strategic initiatives within software product and services businesses on or ahead of schedule. Using my leadership ability, “entrepreneurial attitude”, full life cycle knowledge, program management skills, domain knowledge in technology and expertise in cross-functional areas like business strategy, marketing, client relationship management, sales support and operational management, I have influenced positive change in both early stage startups and global services firms. Having worked in 5 continents, I bring multi-cultural, multi-lingual and global leadership capability to the workplace. I have managed teams of upto 60 people in a matrix and direct fashion.

I have vertical industry knowledge in Financial Services, Telecom, Manufacturing/High-Tech, Retail and Transportation industries.

I just graduated with an MBA from MIT Sloan where I was an Alfred P. Sloan Fellow ( GPA: 5.0/5.0). In addition I have an M.S. in Industrial Engineering (a highly quantitative/computationally intensive field of engineering) from Purdue, where I graduated with distinction and a B.Tech in Engineering from IIT Bombay, where I graduated top of my stream.
