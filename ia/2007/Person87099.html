Anthony Meluskey, Sr’s Summary

Mr. Meluskey has specialized skills in Project Management, Rational Unified Process (RUP), Software Engineering, Process Improvement, Estimating, System Life Cycle Development Methodologies, Change Management, and SEI CMM/CMMI and Capability Assessments. He has led organizations to successful project completions within budget and on schedule. He is experienced in SEI CMM Process Improvement, Software Capability Evaluations and ISO audits. He is a trained Six Sigma Champion.
Mr. Meluskey was a Project Manager and Senior Consultant for eleven years in the Martin Marietta Corp. Management and Data Systems (formerly GE Aerospace) where he was a Software Development Manager, Process Improvement Manager, and Consultant in Object Oriented Technology.
Mr. Meluskey is currently the Business Excellence Manger for Unisys’ Communications group. He is managing the roll-out of the corporate standard engineering process (Unisys - Rational Unified Process (RUP), the group’s journey to CMMI Level-3, the ISO compliance, and quality improvement initiatives.)
Mr. Meluskey was with CSC for 10 years in functional and project capacities as Proposal Manager, Project Manager and Process Improvement Manager. He led or contributed to CMM and CMMI-based process improvement programs in three organizations. Prior to joining Unisys Mr. Meluskey was a Senior Manager at CSC’s IRS PRIME Modernization Program.
