Todd Herschberg’s Summary

I am a seasoned entrepreneur and marketing executive with a heavy emphasis on search engine technologies. Prior to my position at WildAttire, I was involved with two consulting firms: TakeONE Marketing (VP ClickSMART Partner Technology) and Quantimark (Founder and CTO). I also founded and managed a number of start-up tech firms where I was instrumental in the development and refinement of several search engine technologies and marketing programs. I was also one of the founders of Albert's Ambry (alberts.com) one of the internet's first specialty software search engines, where I helped to develop both our search algorithms and our fraud control filtering systems. I have additionally been involved with several other major search engines over the years, ranging in capacities from beta tester for Digital's AltaVista search systems to senior second-tier editor for the NewHoo project (now DMOZ). I also had creative input into Australia's WebWombat search engine.
