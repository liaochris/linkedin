Babette E. Bensoussan’s Summary

Babette Bensoussan is the Managing Director of The MindShifts Group, which specialises in competitive intelligence, strategic planning and strategic marketing projects in the Australasian region.

Since 1990, she has undertaken major studies and consulted to Australian and Global Fortune 500 companies in strategic business and marketing planning, competitive intelligence, and strategic analysis. Babette has carried out over 300 intelligence and strategic projects, over 15 years, in a wide range of industries and markets including aerospace, information technology/computers, insurance, transport, financial services, waste services, pharmaceuticals, utilities, mining and manufacturing operations – to just name a few.

Babette was a Founder and Vice President for the Society of Competitive Intelligence Professionals in Australia (SCIPAust), and was awarded a Fellow of SCIP in the USA in 1996. She is the recipient of the 2006 SCIP Meritorious Award – the highest award in her field.

Babette has been an Adjunct Lecturer at the Sydney Graduate School of Management, University of Western Sydney, and at Bond University, Queensland, where she has taught Competitive Intelligence in the undergraduate business and MBA programs.

She has had published numerous articles on strategic planning, competitive intelligence, and strategic marketing and is an invited speaker and guest lecturer both domestically and internationally. She is the co-author of “Strategic and Competitive Analysis”, published by Prentice Hall and released in April 2003. Currently the book is in the top 1% of best selling books at Amazon.com and is ranked the number one best selling CI book. It has been translated into five languages. She and her co-author have written a second book "Business and Competitive Analysis" for Wharton School Publishing which is due to be released in March 2007.
