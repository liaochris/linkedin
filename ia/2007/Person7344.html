Karim Arafat’s Summary

- Online media entrepreneur. Co-founder of ikbis.com, the first photo and video sharing service in the Arab World, and itoot.net, a blog aggregator of the best blogs relating to the Arab World.

- ICT Legal Consultant. Worked closely with the MoICT in Jordan in areas of deregulation, e-government, courier services and procurement.

- Corporate Legal Consultant. Worked at Legal Allies, a prominent law firm in Jordan. Areas of practice included company law, contracts, labour law, intellectual property rights, taxation and due diligence.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    September 2005 — Present (2 years 3 months)
