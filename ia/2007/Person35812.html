Ian Bell’s Summary

I hate terms like "visionary". Though I'm well-aware of what's going on at the bleeding edge, I would prefer to think that I see just far enough ahead to be practical. Therefore it could be said that as something of a technology pragmatist, my primary skill lies in spotting and monetizing emerging technologies at just the right time.

I have extensive experience creating and marketing technology brands and services. I like to build products that shake things up, and I have a hands-on technical background in IP Communications technologies, leveraged against repeated success in conceiving & launching innovative and disruptive products and services for the consumer, enterprise, and service provider markets.
