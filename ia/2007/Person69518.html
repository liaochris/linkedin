Geno Iorio (geno@profitsolutions.org)’s Summary

In a nutshell, we utilize a proprietary analysis to help determine an entreprenuer's business financial needs.

Whether a client needs a business expansion loan, SBA loan, start-up capital, a commercial real estate mortgage or Accounts Receiveable leveraging, we may be able to help.

We are advisors, strategists, and advocates for the client. We will help the client decide what type of financing or combination of financing vehicles will be most advantageous for the client.

As business consultants,we assist in preparing a financial package and getting it ready for a prospective investor to review. We fit the financial solution and product to the unique needs of the business owner. We remain available to advise the client throughout the process of securing their unique funding needs.We become advocates for our clients, not just facilitators.

We are able to provide summary business valuations on small businesses’ with one location and less than 50 employees. During this valuation process, we often find “unproductive” monies and methodologies being utilized that can address how a business owner may be able to keep more of his profits in his pocket. We go above and beyond just the accounting aspects of a business entity's worth.

If a business owner / entrepreneur is currently receiving a future payment stream,(Factoring, PO Funding, Commercial Lease notes, etc.),we can help speed up the cash flow process by providing cash now for their ongoing future payments.

If an entrepreneur is interested in buying or selling a business,we help make the deal succeed through a variety of seller-financed scenarios.

If business owners do not know their financial options, they have none.

We are an available resource to help entreprenuers with their peace of mind to allow them to more efficiently run the day to day operations of their unique business venture.
