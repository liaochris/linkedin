Ryan Shwayder’s Summary

Ryan Shwayder, also known as Blackguard, is a Game Designer/Community Relations Manager at Green Monster Games. His focus in the world of online gaming is all about design and community. He has a blog at Nerfbat.com in which he primarily discusses design, aspects of game development, and the industry in general.

Ryan got his start in the game industry in 2001 as an intern for the little-known MMORTS Fighting Legends by Maximum Charisma Studios. After that, he moved on to write for the online gaming media until early 2004, when he joined Sony Online Entertainment as a Quality Assurance Analyst. He moved on to become a Community Relations Representative before the launch of EQII, an Apprentice Designer, and then the Community Relations Manager for EverQuest II in mid 2005.

In late 2006, Shwayder joined Green Monster Games to work alongside Curt Schilling, R.A. Salvatore, Todd McFarlane, and MMO industry veterans. He is currently working with his new family on an unannounced project.
