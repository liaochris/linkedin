Nicola Asuni’s Summary

I am a very skilled software designer and programmer with several years of coding experience in C/C++, Java, PHP, and deep knowledge of the most recent HTML standards.

Main focus of my software production being user interfaces and solutions to improve accessibility and usability of server-based software, I am also an entrepreneur, founder and president of Tecnick.com S.r.l. [http://www.tecnick.com], leading provider of award-winning Open Source software solutions for the web, and founder and owner of Technick.net [http://www.technick.net], since 1998 the largest connector and cable pinout archive on the web.
