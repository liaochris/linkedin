David Hsu’s Summary

Experienced adviser with more than 10 years of experiences in IT, specialising in web-based solutions across a variety of products and services, including e-business, intranet and online media sites. Has a proven record in working closely with the business to deliver successful projects and solutions. Highlights include solution for financial services compliance and operational risk (Lawlex Compliance & Operational Risk Toolkit) and online directories (YellowPages & WhitePages Online).

Broad experiences of online business models including content-driven advertising and hosted, service-based product and solutions.
