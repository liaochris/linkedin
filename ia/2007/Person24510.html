Don Rae’s Summary

20+ years experience in software industry covering trade development, marketing, e-business, website development/admin, analysis, project management, research, training, systems documentation. Experienced public speaker.

Current position International Market Manager, South/South East Asia at New Zealand Trade & Enterprise (NZTE).

Previously ICT Sector Manager, NZTE Christchurch office. Focus on South East Asia, ICT in Transport & Logistics and Health ICT.

Prior to NZTE, track record includes Tait Electronics (2002-2005) Internet Development Manager and Jade (1994-2002) multiple roles.

Also involved with New Zealand Hi-Tech Awards since 1997 as judge and more recently as member of organising committees (2004-5 & 2007). Judge at Australian Consensus Software Awards 2007. I have a particular interest in growing networks through technology award programmes.

My main goal is to work with NZ companies to facilitate growth in South/South East Asia. Medium-term aim is to take limited number of directorships and generate significant international business networks.

Don Rae’s Specialties:

Trade development. E-business development, project management, requirements analysis, business development, governance. Facilitation of business collaboration. Accomplished public speaker and writer. Significant experience in organising and judging technology industry awards. Event management and production

Don Rae’s Experience

  • International Market Manager, South/South EastAsia

    New Zealand Trade & Enterprise (NZTE)

    (Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; Government Relations industry)

    May 2007 — Present (9 months)

    My goal is to elevate the profile of the South/South East Asia region (India, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore and Indonesia) both within NZTE and in the external New Zealand business community with a view to growing New Zealand's export trade in region.

    To do this, I manage four broad areas:

    1. Work with NZTE's Regional Director on regional business plan and strategy
    2. Work with inbound governmental and business delegations to optimise outcomes of New Zealand visits
    3. Work with New Zealand exporters in region to ensure NZTE works appropriately with those companies
    4. Ensure effective communication between onshore NZTE sector teams and offshore NZTE trade offices

    As part of the role, I am an ex-officio member of the ASEAN New Zealand Combined Business Council (ANZCBC) and the India New Zealand Business Council.

    I am particularly interested in fostering collaboration between New Zealand business and partners in the South/South East Asia region.
