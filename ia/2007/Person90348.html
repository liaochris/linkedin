Warren Marland’s Summary

As an experienced business development and marketing manager, I partner with exciting Israeli companies who are looking to grow. Working with a team of analysts in the fields of software, pharmaceuticals, homeland security and environmental technology, I am able to provide a wide range of expert services to penetrate markets and expand services in a number of regions around the world.

I also selectively assist in raising capital for startup or first stage financing.
