Keith D. Taylor’s Summary

Currently I am CFO and SVP North American Operations for Corefino, a leading provider of value–added Finance Outsourcing solutions, primarily serving the needs of venture-backed, emerging-growth companies. I am also on the Board of Crestpoint Solutions, Inc. (www.crestp.com, a Telecomm Software Solutions provider), ThunderRoad Investments, LLC (high tech investment group), and Voltix, Inc. (internet peer-to-peer search technology), as well as CFO and Board Member for the San Francisco Shakespeare Festival.
Previous positions include CFO for Encover, Inc., (Service Contract Revenue Management solutions, funded by Baird, Doll Capital, and Sigma Partners), CFO for Topio (Sequoia Capital/Sigma Partners portfolio company, acquired by Network Appliance), Page Systems (funded by Bear Gold and later acquired by HonHai/Taiwan), BrassRing Inc., (funded by Washington Post, Chicago Tribune, Gannett News, and Accel Partners), and CareerCentral (Questmark, Softbank, Arthur Rock, TCV, acquired by Spencer Stuart).
Earlier in my career I spent 5 years as VP Finance for Adecco (world's largest staffing/HR Consulting company - $15B worldwide), almost 10 years at Apple Computer, and started my career at Ernst & Young LLP, earning my CPA and CISA designations. I have my MBA for San Jose State University, and my BSBA from Cal Poly State University, San Luis Obispo.

I have had extensive experience in all aspects of financial operations, accounting and business development, leading teams from two to 150+. My experience includes pre-IPO start-up funding (assisting startups raise over $60M), mergers and acquisitions, and all facets of operations.
