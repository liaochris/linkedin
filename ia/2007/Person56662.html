Matt Regnier’s Summary

Matt Regnier graduated from Purdue and received a grant to fund a position as a Technical Animator for the Nuclear Pharmacy Certificate program. While there he created training videos that utilized animations of interactive models. When funding for the project was dropped he started work on an independent game mod called Ultima V: Lazarus to keep up with his growing interest in game development.

Moving to the East Coast and taking a job with Turbine, Inc. he started making contact with members of the art and design teams and helped out doing whatever work he could. Model degrades, fixing bugs, and taking on small tasks allowed him to get familiar with their pipelines, departments, and tools that would eventually lead to transitioning teams. Since the move, he has been working to develop the technical workflows and evaluting the software their teams will utilize for the next big project.
