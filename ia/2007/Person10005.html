Michael Beddows’s Summary

As a collaboration consultant with international business experience in eight countries, I have been developing collaboration and knowledge sharing products and services for over ten years. I have created solutions for clients in consumer packaged goods, legal and financial services, healthcare, dot-com publishing, and telecommunications industries.

My recent consulting engagements include Coca-Cola Enterprises, Miller Brewing, and Washington Mutual. I have been working closely with these information-intensive organizations to evolve their collaboration strategies.
