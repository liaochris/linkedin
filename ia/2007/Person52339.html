Lee Semel’s Summary

I’m interested in technology, entrepreneurship, design, and creating great online experiences.

I built up a consulting business, Innofinity, developing high-end consumer and enterprise web applications, for startups as well as established companies in media, entertainment, finance, e-commerce, and nonprofits. I designed an enterprise content management system built on the LAMP platform which is in production at major clients. I enjoy working with startups and meeting entrepreneurs, especially those looking for input on technology.

As a hobby, I develop online games. My all-time favorite was a first person 3D shooting game coded in a mere 5K of javascript, which you can see at http://www.wolf5k.com

I’m always interested in meeting new people. To contact me, and for more information about my work, see my web page, http://www.leesemel.com
