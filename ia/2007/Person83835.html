Don Synstelien’s Summary

I dream.

I tilt at windmills.

I run the gauntlet.

I've been working in the interactive space since 1992 in one form or another. Most of my time has been spent trying to create the rainbow that leads to the pot of gold. During my journey I've become published, won awards and submitted patents for inventions. I've worked with C level execs and burned the midnight oil with some really great developers.

I've had quite a bit of experience in new business development, strategy and acquiring talented teams, both creative and technical. I've successfully launched several products, including the DirecTV Gametracker for Fox Sports and Desktop Weather Platinum for The Weather Channel. Most recently, My team and I invented and launched SpringWidgets, the worlds first desktop and browser widget platform and the first in-house produced product released by Fox Interactive Media.

Currently I'm working under contract at Fox Interactive Media in the Labs division spending my time inventing things that you don't realize that you need yet. While I cannot at this time entertain job offers, I’m certainly willing to make connections, so please feel free to contact me if you have questions.
