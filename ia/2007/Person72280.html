Richard Cutting’s Summary

Love the internet. Love the boundaries its bringing down, raising human consciousness along the way.

The last 10 years have been in IT sales management. Specifically within the managed service providers and content management arenas.

I have built many successful sales team for those sectors.

Started Over Achievers Ltd. The main aim to recruit quality sales, pre-sales and technical personnel into those sectors above.

Prior to IT I spent 18 months as the Public Relations Manager of the largest generic pharmaceutical company in central Europe, Zentiva. I headed up the Internet Steering committee and implemented their first online PR strategy when only 10 staff out of 1500 had access to the internet.

Before pharmaceuticals I was Director of Studies at an American Business School lecturing in business communications. I taught MBA students and managed a department of 10 teachers.

My goals are now to build a first class recruitment company. I have dealt with many recruitment companies over the years and have recruited literally 100's of people for organisations. Most recruitment companies have left me cold.

Over Achievers are staffed by people who have been in the industries they are recruiting for. They understand what separates the good from the great and pledge to put forward only the brightest in the market.
