Ayan Islam’s Summary

We're an audio-visual consulting firm specializing in creative video content for a variety of businesses, individuals, and websites. Some videos could be viral, some could simply be informational, while others could be educational/training related.

At AIV Consulting, we pride ourselves in listening to your needs, and going the extra mile for your project. We provide innovative, and cost-effective means for your message being delivered effectively and creatively, in an interactive and entertaining manner.

For example, what if I told you, I could take your 30, 60, 90 or even 120 second elevator speech and turn it into a visual marketing tool for your website. Also, it’s at a cost that your advertising budget won’t cringe at!

It’s possible with AIV Consulting.

With today’s use of multimedia outlets, such as YouTube, MySpace, iTunes & more, you have the opportunity to enhance your website by taking your message to the next level. We take your message and turn it into a creative, compelling video at an affordable price.

Your video is custom tailored to your marketing needs, and can be saved in various media formats. These videos can then be placed on your website, for easy, streamlined viewing.

At AIV Consulting, we deliver creative commercials and informercials for your communication needs. Whether you’re looking to create a public announcement, would like to showcase your business, or are simply looking to expand your marketing outreach efforts.

For any of your web video/marketing needs, or literature on our various preset video packages, you can contact us directly at 202-361-5245.

Look forward to helping your business grow!
