Richard Rosen’s Summary

Richard Rosen is a top-producing business development leader with track record of driving revenue growth and expense reduction in a consultative, team-oriented sales environment. Richard has extensive contacts with executives in Internet search, publishing, media, advertising and technology and is an industry expert in call measurement and pay-per-call. Rosen is a creative, strategic thinker with strong technical knowledge.

As Vice President of Business Development and General Manager of the CallSource Publisher / Agency division, Richard launched a significant new publisher and agency division for the company in early 2003. His division sells to print and online clients including yellow pages, newspapers, specialty publishers, IYPs, online directories, advertising agencies and large advertisers. Under Richard’s leadership, CallSource now manages over 150,000 local advertising campaigns and 5 million telephone leads monthly.

Richard was previously Director of Business Development at Homestore.com and he was Vice President of Business Development for FirstLook.com, a Kleiner-Perkins, idealab!-backed company. He was also co-founder of OpenSpace, a Seattle-based Internet infrastructure provider.
