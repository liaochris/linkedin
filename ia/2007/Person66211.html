Patrick Gannon’s Summary

I am focused on delivering innovative products and services to consumers using advanced technology and common-sense design principles. I have a great track record of strategy and delivery in finance and technology.

MBA, Finance and Strategic Management, The Wharton School

Lending Club (http://www.lendingclub.com) is an online lending community. Members can borrow money more easily and at a better rate than they would from a bank, or lend money to a portfolio of many borrowers at high rates (earning from 7 to over 17% per year). Lenders have complete privacy and an easy, secure online environment that makes lending money as simple as possible. Our platform leverages industry-leading service providers to assure smooth originations and monthly servicing, and should anything happen to the company, we have a failsafe contract in place to continue the servicing of the loans.

We have developed proprietary technology called LendingMatch(TM) that matches lenders with borrowers using online and offline social connections and recommends diversified portfolios to lenders based on risk tolerance. This affiliation-based approach lowers the risk of borrower default and makes the platform more attractive to lenders who want to help those in their communities. Lending Club is a regulated entity and we provide an easy, safe, and convenient way to borrow and lend money. We store all sensitive financial data such as Social Security numbers and bank accounts in a highly secure environment hosted by a PCI-compliant payment solution provider.
