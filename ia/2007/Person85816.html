We provide commercial animation production services for websites, music companies, television networks, new productions, studios, etc. Please give us a call for a free consultation and demo material (510) 433-0776.

Time Piglets is family-oriented entertainment company that creates feature motion pictures and literary/visual arts properties. Our first feature film property, the Time Piglets trilogy, is anchored by star actor, Danny Glover, and based on an original storyline which is being developed and produced by an award-winning creative team.

The Production: By taking advantage of the rapid advances in animation technology and boom in outsourcing, we have driven down the production costs to produce full-length animation features and content for television broadcasting.

Proven Team: Our creative team comes for award-winning studios such as: Pixar, DreamWorks, Disney, Fox, and ILM.

Family Animation: Time Piglets is capturing the fastest growing market in the most lucrative film genre. G-rated movies have a considerably higher return on investment compared to PG, PG13, and R-rated films.

The project will generate additional revenue streams from second run screen markets, overseas pre-sales, DVD and merchandising sales, video games, television syndications, and other ventures.

The Ask: We are seeking $19 million to produce Time Piglets and, based on the performance of similarly situated ventures, anticipate it will gross $60 million at the box office, and $120 million worldwide (see business plan for details). We are also seeking investment into the company with its growing portfolio of ventures.

Development Budget: In order to further develop Time Piglets the movie, we are seeking a $775K convertible loan with a repayment option at the end of 9-months, or opportunity to convert the loan into a Series A preferred investment.
