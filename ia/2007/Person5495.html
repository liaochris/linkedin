Andrew Calvin’s Summary

Since 1986 I have practice in workers' compensation, general litigation, criminal law, construction and engineering law, and technology and intellectual property law.

Applying my legal and technology experience I am responsible for matter support, such as litigation support, for DLA Phillips Fox. I have project managed the technology and document issues for many matters, including the Thredbo Coroner's Inquiry and massive insurance and commercial litigation involving over a million documents.

Andrew Calvin’s Specialties:

Understanding technical issues and applying them to the law, casual .NET programming, SQL, intellectual property, technology contracts, commercial litigation, project management.
