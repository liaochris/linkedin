Ajay Mahajan’s Summary

http://www.thecreativetechnologist.com

Am a TECHNOLOGY Professional who is also CREATIVE. I Believe that the two are very finely intertwined. Technology requires Creativity to Innovate. And Creativity requires Technology to efficiently survive and grow!

In the past seven years have had the good fortune to have personally led efforts that have seen Smart Cards being used for Public Distribution Services (Ration cards), Social Security benefits, Dairy Cooperatives, Crop Management, Fishermens Identification, Retail & Distribution, Healthcare, Distant Education, Ticketing, the Driving Licenses & Vehicle registration Certificates, National ID of India, etc. - in the process have issued more than 10 Million Cards in the country!

Besides this, have also been instrumental in setting up a country-wide network of alliances and project operations offices for various projects and have to my credit successful architecting and deployment of more than 40 e-governance related and other technology and microfinance projects in the country.

Have been a part of the core technology team constituted by the Registrar General of India (RGI) for defining the smart card technology solutions for the National ID card. Was also a part of the core technology team constituted by NIC for defining the SCOSTA Operating System specifications.

Have also been involved in the Telecom segment - from SIM Cards. to VAS applications.
