Jame Healy’s Summary

Having been a senior solutions architect with Visiphor (formerly Form Consulting, Global e-biz, then Sunaptic Solutions) since 1996, Jame is an expert in the area of Business Process and Performance Management. Starting with the Financial Services industry and later broadening to healthcare, public sector, transport/logistics and manufacturing, Jame has led a number of initiatives ranging from $50,000 to $23M in size, each delivering proven business value to the enterprise.

Jame has published numerous articles and spoken at various trade and business events on Business Process and Performance Management as well as on the Microsoft integrated suite of products including BizTalk Server, SQL Server, Office System (including InfoPath) and the Windows Server System.
