Sarah Reiter’s Summary

I graduated from Arizona State University with a B.S. in Communications. Since then, I've established myself as an ethics checked Certified Real Estate Support Specialist (CRESS) with the International Virtual Assistants Association (IVAA), a Professional Real Estate Virtual Assistant (PREVA) with the Real Estate Virtual Assistant (REVA) Network, a CyberStarVA member, as well as a Certified Virtual Assistant (CVA) with the Settlement Room ™.

My association affiliations consist of the Southeast Valley Regional Association of Realtors (SEVRAR) and Scottsdale Association of Realtors in Arizona (SAAR) where I sponsor renewal hour classes, new realtor orientations, industry Expos, and Realtor designation courses. I also am a proud member of the Arizona Small Business Association (ASBA).
