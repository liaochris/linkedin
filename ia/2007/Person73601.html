Robin Wilson’s Summary

Robin Wilson Home is a privately held company, the brand revamp of WSG Consulting LLC. Our firm maintains a focus on designing concepts for residential spaces for both homeowners and developers, including Concept Homes/Space Design.

From renovation planning and on-site project management, to product design and selection, we take your space from concept to reality.

We also manage contractors and other service providers using web-based project management software to maintain efficient communication throughout a project for each client.

Let us be responsible for planning and coordinating your construction projects - to help you complete projects on time and within budget.

    Robin Wilson launched her eponymous firm, Robin Wilson Home, in 2000 with a focus on interior design, residential renovations and project management.
