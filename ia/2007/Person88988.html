Vibhav Kant’s Summary

Vibhav Kant Upadhyay is the Founder Chairman of ‘India Center Foundation’, which is working towards India’s nation building process as a catalyst. He is an emerging leader and a well-known contributor in India’s ‘Look East’ policy.

Vibhav is known for his statesmanship, macro-vision on the nation-building process and a holistic approach on development. His unique work towards Indo-Japan Global Partnership is being supported and appreciated by top policy makers, business and opinion leaders not only from these two countries but also by many around the world
