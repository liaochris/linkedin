Ronna Porter’s Summary

In-depth experience in integrated, strategic marketing and communications for some of the world's leading technology and telecommunications brands. Both in-house, and as a consultant for Report International, Hill & Knowlton, and Citigate Corporate, I've directed cross-border programmes for Compaq Computer, Oracle, Motorola, Shell Services International, and BT.

I returned to the UK in Sping 2006 after seven years living in Germany and, following some time out to raising my family, I am now involved in two start-up agencies: Sound Strategies Ltd, and Ronna Porter Public Relations Ltd.

Ronna Porter’s Specialties:

Merging my sector and discipline expertise, I am passionate about the challenges and opportunities that technological development brings to successful corporate and marketing communications, and its impact on continuous brand development - with particular interest in applied music and sound. I hold an MSc in Public Relations from the University of Stirling, plus a BA (Hons) in Commerce (with Marketing) from Napier University of Edinburgh.
