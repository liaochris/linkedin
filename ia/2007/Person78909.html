Craig Weisz’s Summary

Craig has over twenty years of experience as a motion picture producer, director, writer, and production manager. He has prepared both independent and large-scale movies for production with companies including Curb Entertainment, HBO, New Regency, Phoenix Pictures, Spelling Entertainment, and TNT. An “on-location” specialist, Craig has worked all over California and in many foreign countries. Adept at new technologies, Craig has produced new media projects including one of the first interactive movies, Fool’s Gold, for Ninth House Networks. Craig also serves regularly as a consultant to other independent producers in matters of physical production, strategic planning, and distribution.

As a freelance television commercial producer, Craig has credits in over fifty television commercial productions for national and international clients including Amgen, Citreon, Clorox, Legal Rights Defenders, Mercedes-Benz, MET-Rx, Nabisco, Procter & Gamble, Pro Sieben, SSM Healthcare, and Reebok. In 2003 his spring campaign for Los Defensores won the Best Commercial Campaign award by the Latin Marketing Association. His recent feature film credits include Co-Producer on the MGM/Summertime Films’ movie Her Best Move, Associate Producer on the Sony Pictures film, Soul Assassin, starring Skeet Ulrich and Kristy Swanson, and Co-Producer/Editor on the AIM Group movie Secret Sins, starring Nicole Eggert and Tony Sirico.

A member of the Directors Guild of America since 1997, Craig received his Master’s degree in film production from the prestigious USC School of Cinema-Television, where he was a Mary Pickford/Cinema Circulus scholarship award winner. His company ShareWonder Films has several feature projects in development based on Craig’s own creative work as well as partnerships with other producers.
