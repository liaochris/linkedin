Peymon Maskan’s Summary

Music Supervisor Peymon Maskan's credits include Cannes 2007 critic's week closer Expired (Samantha Morton, Jason Patrick), Gardens of the Night (John Malkovich, Tom Hanks), indie gem Humboldt County (Fairuza Balk, Peter Bogdanovich, Frances Conroy), ABC Family mini series "Fallen"(co Supervising with Allison Clark), indie thriller Alone With Her (Colin Hanks), and Adidas' breakthrough campaign Adicolor "Black". He is currently Supervising noir thriller Footsteps (Brendan Sexton III), and Dance of the Dead - a Rock and Roll Zombie film. He also works as a consultant for Universal Pictures Marketing, working on the campaigns for Knocked Up, Children of Men, Bourne Supremacy, and The Kingdom. 

Peymon's first job was film runner on 15 Minutes (DeNiro) after graduating from USC's Cinema Television program in 1999. While being quickly promoted to assist the director John Herzfeld, his first exposure to Music Supervision was with the film's Supervisor Dana Sano. When Dana founded her new company Zenden in 2001, opportunity called, and Peymon made the moved over to music.  Eventually starting his company Little Mountain in 2006, Please check out Peymon's podcast at www.ltlmtn.com and his Venice based theater collective "Apartment A" where he directs and serves as Music Director.
