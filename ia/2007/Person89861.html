Vladimir Campos’s Summary

Though I graduated in Economics Science, my interests in technology started since very young with a Apple II computer. But only in 1999 when I got involved with Palm devices I started a company to develop mobile solutions and applications and also keep a website about Palm platform with tutorials, software download, tips, articles and a huge amount of information for users. A partnership with Zip.Net (acquired by UOL.com.br) brought me some revenue from advertisement and the website popularity growth helped selling more and more PDA applications and solution for the corporate market.

Since I was the responsible for writing all the website content I also started writing for several American and Brazilian PDA specialized magazines like PocketPCMag.com, ComputingUnplugged.com, Webinsider.com.br and others. The website next step was covering not only Palm devices, but also Windows Mobile and Symbian devices. And after that we started also covering and developing corporate solutions for mobile phones.

With the increase of the demand for mobile corporate solutions, I became Director of Business Development in Brazil for LatinGo, an AvantGo partner for Latin America. This move helped my company develop better solutions and also reach the market of bigger companies.

Imagining the mobile phone market would grow fester then the PDA market I started working at the sales department at Claro, a Brazilian GSM carrier. Because of my skills and knowledge I was promoted and became Consultant for Products & Services, the same position I latter accepted in Brasil Telecom GSM, a GSM start up. In both companies I worked with product development, sales consulting and sales force training. I became a specialist for corporate telecom solutions and nowadays I work at GVT another Braziliam telecom company well known for its innovative VoIP products.
