Abed Farhan’s Summary

My background includes 12 years in the Telecom and electronic messaging industry and I have held business development and sales positions at New Edge Networks (an EarthLink company), Telemedia International and Computown Inc.

Currently I am the Senior Business Development Manager for New Edge Networks- EarthLink, broadband carrier with the largest DSL network in the U.S. As a member of the business development team, I have achieved key metrics that developed a new distribution channel for New Edge Networks in order to support our revenue growth objectives. Also, I have analyzed market opportunities and developed and managed solutions creation, go-to-market and sales programs for SMB customers and selling partners. In my role I developed and leveraged strong relationships with C level execs at top payment processing and POS companies including ChasePaymentech, AMEX, Verifone, First Data, Micros and Fifth third Bank.

In my roles, I was able to form strategic alliances, executed agreements and manage executive relationships with some of leading international telecommunication and sofware companies in the world. These companies include Lucent Technologies, Cable & Wireless, Siemens, Virgin and Deutsche Telekom.
