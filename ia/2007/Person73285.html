Robert Hope’s Summary

I have over 11 years of experience designing, documenting, and developing web- and windows-based applications from the UI level down to the database level. These applications have been high-volume and high-performance, including my last position at LoopNet.Com, a website that serves more than 2 million page hits per day to a user base of more than 1 million active users. My roles at LoopNet have included Technical Consultant, Senior Web Developer, and Systems Architect. In my current position I own a small consulting firm delivering advanced Microsoft.NET based web technologies. I have a demonstrated and proven ability to solve problems through motivated and determined effort and a knack for creative solutions. In addition, my ability to adapt quickly, my ability to learn and apply new knowledge immediately, and my ability to mentor and train other members of my team make me a valuable resource and leader for any company.

Robert Hope’s Specialties:

Microsoft.Net, SQL Database Administration, SQL Stored Procedures, Active Server Pages, XML, Web Services, Software and Database Architecture, Performance enhancements, design and implementation strategies, integration and migration, in particular on high-volume, high-complexity inter- and intra-net systems.
