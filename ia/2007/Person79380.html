Shiu Heng Chin’s Summary

As the Assistant General Manager at Multi Universal Instrument, grew the scientific instruments and equipment business to SGD$2m within 4 years. Automated the operations with an online ERP system to reduce time-to-quote and improve work flow.

At River Delta, a US start-up company, created a marketing strategy and implemented the plan with the objective to position River Delta as an attractive company to invest in and to interest prospective buyers by expanding the Asia Pacific market. Achieved media coverage including a live interview on CNBC Squawk Box. Penetrated 4 Cable TV companies with trials for the products.

At Motorola, created and implemented an end-user marketing programme for cable TV operators to roll out to attract subscribers and to retail cable modems. Programme includes events funding, educational materials such as a specially commissioned comic book that explains cable broadband in layman terms, point-of-purchase materials and a rewards program that promotes subscriber loyalty. Known as MBox, the programme was rolled out in Australia, South Korea and China; and won a Best Marketing Support From a Vendor award from Thrunet, a cable TV operator in Korea.

As the Relationship Marketing Manager at Compaq Asia Pacific, successfully
started a direct purchase division that enabled consumers to buy PCs and laptops
directly from Compaq by fax and phone. Convinced major banks in Singapore,
Malaysia, Korea and Taiwan to promote Compaq PCs to credit card holders.

Successfully managed the life cycle for 8 consumer PC roll-outs at Compaq Asia
Pacific as the Asia Pacific Home Computers Product Marketing Manager.

As the Product Marketing Manager for Compaq Singapore, created a user club for
Home PC owners to promote loyalty and cross-sell computer related products and
accessories.
