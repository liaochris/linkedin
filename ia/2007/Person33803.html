Geoff Young’s Summary

Graduated from University of San Diego with a B.A. in English and a Philosophy minor. Worked in academic publishing (Academic Press/Elsevier) for several years as a proofreader, copy editor, and development editor before embarking on a career as a freelance editor. Later moved into web development, first with Elsevier, then with SAIC. Currently writing full time about baseball at Ducksnorts.com and KnuckleCurve.com, with occasional appearances at HardballTimes.com and other outlets. Wrote and self-published my first book in March 2007; a second is scheduled for March 2008.

Geoff Young’s Specialties:

Writing, copy editing, proofreading. Also some technical skills: HTML, XHTML, CSS, WordPress, Microsoft Office Suite, Allaire Home Site, Jasc Paint Shop Pro, Adobe Acrobat.
