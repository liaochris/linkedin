Chris Selland’s Summary

CHRIS SELLAND
Chris Selland joined SoundBite Communications as Vice President of Business Development in September 2005. He develops and manages SoundBite's key strategic alliances and corporate development initiatives.

Prior to SoundBite, Mr. Selland founded Reservoir Partners L.P., a CRM/enterprise software advisory & consulting firm. Reservoir Partners was formed while an Entrepreneur-in-Residence at YankeeTek Ventures. He merged Reservoir Partners' consulting and research assets with Aberdeen Group and served as Vice President of Sell-Side Research at Aberdeen. Mr. Selland also served as an M&A/market advisor for Covington Associates, an Investment Bank specializing in Mergers & Acquisitions in the enterprise software and services industries.

Earlier in his career, Mr. Selland served as Vice President of Marketing at eSupportNow, a venture-funded eCRM service provider. Prior to eSupportNow, he was Vice President of e-Business Strategies at the Yankee Group, a technology research and consulting firm and subsidiary of Reuters, where he became a leading market authority in the areas of CRM and customer-facing e-Business strategies. Mr. Selland has also held senior sales and marketing positions at Primix Solutions, ON Technology, Computer Associates and Ingersoll-Rand.

Mr. Selland is past President of the Customer Relationship Management Association (CRMA). In addition, he has been frequently quoted in major business and industry publications, and is a past member of the CRM Magazine Editorial Advisory Board. He is an accomplished public speaker and presenter, having chaired and spoken at numerous leading industry conferences, including the DCI CRM Leadership Summit & Linkage's Sales & Marketing Leadership Summit.

Chris Selland’s Specialties:

Strategic Alliance Development. Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) and Capital Raising. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) services & strategies.
