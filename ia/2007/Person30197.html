Frederic Nuyts’s Summary

Specialist in Governement Tendering in Belgium, France and on European level. Main skills : marketing and communication, consultancy, IT, webdesign, sales, managing business development.

Graduated in marketing at EPHEC, Frederic worked 3 years in the distribution sector of as Key Account Manager, where he could acquire a solid experience in sales. He changed companies and became Account Manager in a consultancy firm specialised in the public sector. He was successively promoted there with the positions of Team Leader and Marketing Manager, where he could acquire all the experience necessary to completely manage a consultancy firm in the public markets. He was also simultaneous external consultant in the IT sector where he specialised himself especially in hardware, software, networks and web design. At the beginning of 2006, Frederic launched with others associates his own company of consultance regarding public tenders, Government Exchange (Govex). www.govex.eu

He also recently created the first forum and information centre for european public tenders : www.publicmarkets.eu
