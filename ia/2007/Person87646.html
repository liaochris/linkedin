Jan Pieter Tromp’s Summary

A versatile geologist, seismic interpreter and sub-surface team & project leader with 16 Years exploration, appraisal and field development experience with Shell Group companies in the United Kingdom, Albania, Brunei Darussalam and Syria.

Jan Pieter Tromp’s Specialties:

Seismic interpretation, mapping, geological modeling and opportunity evaluation (risk & uncertainy analysis, economic screening) in a variety of depositional and structural settings. A solid academic background in fieldwork based structural geology and stratigraphy.
