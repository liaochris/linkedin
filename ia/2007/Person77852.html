Sean Tierney’s Summary

My current focus is building a company that removes the pain associated with running complex software applications in small businesses. The JumpBox appliance meshes the convenience benefits of software-as-a-service (SaaS) with the privacy advantages of running software in-house by delivering pre-configured applications via an appliance.

Grid7 has been a pioneer in the structured blogging initiative creating tools that simplify the authoring of structured content. Future pursuits include the development of a tool to enable corporations to conduct internal prediction markets. Grid7's mission is to incubate innovative technology ideas by applying the cooperative model to software development. We are currently seeking a sales maestro to establish VAR network, manage the sales pipeline and close some early deals for JumpBox. The right candidate will be extended a significant equity position in an early-stage, high growth startup.
