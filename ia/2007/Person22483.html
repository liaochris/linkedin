Xavier Debanne’s Summary

Xavier Debanne has been a senior ICT manager based on 16 years of experience in Marketing and Comunication as well as in Olivetti from 1990 to 1998 and in Siemens Informatica from 1998 to 2006. As an expert of Internet and its use within the Catholic Church, he has been a professor of Religious Interactivity in Internet at the Gregorian University (Rome) from 2002 to 2004.
Now Xavier is an ICT consultant with a strong focus on Information Society, sustainable development, digital divide, Internet oriented projects, etc... He is member of the board of the Italian Catholic Webmaster Association. In particular he assists the Organizations in writing, editing and revising material for publication in the Web in order to increase their visibility.
Finally he has a strong experience as professional trainer on ICT topics.
