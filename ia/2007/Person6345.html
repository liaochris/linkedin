Anita Prins’s Summary

The depth of experience in human factors through work, research and lifestyle supports her skill that interprets and improves life quality in a designed world. Anita Prins discerns gender, age and cultural differences through her experience to educate, adapt, and create for cognitive and physical needs. By integrating healthcare, product design and business you will gain an understanding of the range of client issues that Anita has resolved. Collaborative efforts with each client rewards design ideas, in an interior layout, design and product solutions. Each specific detail originates from the values, physical and sensual needs of the owner.

Anita also comprehends lifestyle demands by way of personal understanding, independence and interdependence. An extensive study of biological factors, past and present, offers Anita additional insight to behavior patterns. She respects that individuals choose their personal symmetry of lifestyle and design. In recognizing today’s demands, your personal design criteria will define and restore time by solutions from your lifestyle not a mass market study. Restoring time to your day to share it with those you love is Anita’s passion.
