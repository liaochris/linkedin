Thomas Kühnapfel’s Summary

In the past 12 years working for Celerant Consulting, I have managed projects in: Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia, Gulf of Mexico, USA, Finland and United Kingdom. Experience in Chemical, Oil and Gas, Automotive and Energy sector. Organizations that I have worked with include BP, Shell, Holcim, Lhoist, Allied Signal, General Motors, Siemens, Sanofi Aventis, Akzo Nobel, Huntsmann, and Aspocomp.
Main focus has always been the increase of effectiveness by systematic elimination of lost time, redesigning processes and systems in the Production, Sales and Maintenance area. Through training of core methodologies like e.g. lean manufacturing, root cause analysis, data and fact based decision making … and coaching the client to apply those we enabled them to live a continuous improvement process after the project end.

Thomas Kühnapfel’s Specialties:

Main implementation areas were: Company wide Management Control & Reporting System, process optimization, maintenance and production planning, contractor management, new product introduction, sales and supply chain, project management.
