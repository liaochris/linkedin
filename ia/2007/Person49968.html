Larry Popelka’s Summary

I specialize in identifying and bringing to market "game changing" new consumer products. I have a new business and marketing model, based on best practices from successful start-ups - used by products like OxiClean, Proactiv Solution, Glaceau Vitamin Water, Burt's Bees and Method Cleaners. If you are a big company, I can take your new product program to a whole new level, and save you money. If you are a start-up, I can help you learn the latest best practices that will turn your new product into a blockbuster,

I have 20+ years of experience in consumer packaged goods. I have been a senior executive and strategist for big companies and an investor/ advsor for start-ups. I have invested $50 million in over 20 different consumer start-ups. I have seen or experienced most of the common pitfalls and success formulas. Launching a successful new brand is no longer all about spending money. I can help you create an impactful "pay-as-you-go" launch plan that will beat the mass market brands.

The consumer products world is changing due to the internet, media fragmentation and social networking. The industry leaders have not yet adapted, and as a result they are all struggling for growth. This is creating tremendous opportunities for entrepreneurs with breakthrough products and/or big companies that are willing to accept the new model.

Call me at 510-521-7230 for a free consultation.
