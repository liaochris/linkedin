Ethan Hall-Beyer’s Summary

C/C++/CORBA software developer for the vertical travel & transportation self-service industry, which includes self-check-in kiosks for airports, airlines and hotels. Development roles encompass low-level device control, development and implementation of industry standards, DCS and PMS systems interaction, and kiosk client application development.

Expert placement of the industry includes standards body participation, onsite customer support and development, technical writing and consulting.
