R. Darrel Davis’s Summary

- Since 1999, over 2000 people have received Christ through Foundation Ministries. In addition, hundreds of others have rededicated their lives and made commitments to reach the unchurched through this ministry
- Successfully coordinated volunteer resources for a three county-wide evangelism effort caller Crossover Triad in conjunction with the Southern Baptist Convention annual meeting
- 11 international mission trips in the following countries, Romania, the Philippines, Mexico, Madagascar, Colombia (2 times), Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, El Salvador and Malawi
- Ministry in 12 states through revivals, crusades, school assemblies, street evangelism, prison ministry, missions, evangelism conferences, and evangelism training
- Led 6 churches in northeastern North Carolina through the interim process
- Served 3 years as Evangelism Director in the West Chowan Baptist Association. Led churches to organize a Celebrate Jesus 2000 crusade and provided training for pastors and laity in evangelism
