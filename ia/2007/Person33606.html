Gunter Wessels’s Summary

The International Group, Inc. and its affiliates, provide sales, marketing, and financial development services to major investor-owned companies. Many of the companies that we serve are faced with maturing markets and a sales/marketing process that have become transactional, and commoditized.

We help our clients refocus their business, sales, and communication processes to enhance a value-solutions orientation. Our services focus on increasing clients’ sensitivity to their customers’ business goals, needs, and objectives.

TIGI delivers performance improvement programs to our clients in North America, Europe, and Asia.

Personal Selling and Sales Management, Marketing Metrics, Sales Effectiveness Solutions, Persuasion and Interpersonal Influence Tactics, Marketing Strategy.
