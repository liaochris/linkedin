Rebecca Rivera (rr@rebeccarivera.com)’s Summary

I've been copywriting and building brands for more than 20 years.

13+ of those years, I was on staff at ad agencies such as: RPA, Team One, Anderson Lembke (aka McCann Erickson), Hal Riney & Associates, DDB Los Angeles and PJA.

I have also freelanced on the client side and at many agencies. Some of the ad agencies I have freelanced at include: TBWA\Chiat, Deutsch LA, AKQA, Y & R/Wunderman, Saatchi & Saatchi, Team One, DDB LA, Hamon-Associates, Direct Partners, OIC, miller huber, Goldberg Moser O'Neill, the Designory, Roxburgh, Fattal & Collins (aka Houston Helm).

In addition to my background in general advertising, design and direct mail, I gained extensive interactive experience at the epicenter of the dot com era as the Creative Director of egreetings.com — an online greeting card company which became a division of American Greetings.

Just a few of the accounts I have worked on include: Ameriquest Mortgage Company, Princess Cruises, Cunard, Bud Light, Sun Microsystems, Isuzu, Schiff Nutritionals, Emode, Hyundai, Lincoln-Mercury, Kia, Toyota, Hooked on Phonics, Lexus, Jafra, Concadia, Cisco, Kia, GMC, Infiniti, K-Swiss, Oracle, Beringer, Charles Schwab, Microsoft, Saturn, GMEV, Sprint, Peet's Coffee & Tea, Yonex Tennis & Golf, Heal the Bay, Earth Communications Office, Honda, Disney Channel, DS Waters, Southern California Edison, the California Lottery, Parisian Bread, Yokohama Tires, Fred Hayman.

For more information, and to view online samples of my work, go to rebeccarivera.com.
