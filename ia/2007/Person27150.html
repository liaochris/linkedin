Pier Paolo Mucelli [ linkedin@eoffice.net ]’s Summary

eOffice takes advantage of a gap in the market for beautifully designed and high tech office environments for small businesses and start ups.
The first eOffice opened in London in 2002, the first open plan business centre in Europe. It offers virtual offices, hot desking options (with wireless access), flexible full time office solutions and a wide range of meeting facilities and videoconferencing. At eOffice we bring people together and organize regular networking events called Net Thursday. eOffice now serves over a thousand clients, ranging from start ups who require a flexible office space, consultants, SMEs, and even large companies searching for creative meeting spaces.
eOffice featured in CNN, BBC, and several international newpapers and magazines, including the FT, The Times, The Independent, Time Out and many more. We were Finalist at FX Design Award for Offices in 2003 and 2006 and among 10 National Finalists at HSBC Start-Up Star Awards 2004.
eOffice has now several locations across the main cities in the UK and plans its international expansion.
