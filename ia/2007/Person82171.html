Steve Bell’s Summary

Technical business adviser assisting tech startup & corporate business unit management teams, boards, and investors. Business development and product strategy pro with experience as a successful serial entrepreneur, executive, and industry c-level consultant. Often work cross function, up and down the organizational ladder to identify, analyze, and develop creative solutions for tough technical marketing & business development problems.

Technical expertise in next-generation IP networking, client-server computing, web technologies, enterprise software, storage systems, carrier & enterprise wireless, telecommunications, and related issues in the public and private equity financial markets.

Steve Bell’s Specialties:

Often work cross-function on issues that overlap engineering, marketing, sales, and the product market. Business/functional skills in technical marketing, business development, product marketing, competitive and product positioning analysis, business case/plan development, strategic partnering, financial analysis, merger and acquisition diligence, venture capital financing process and due diligence.

  • Founder/CTO/GM Wi-Fi & Networking Center

    Agilent Technologies/Silicon Valley Networking

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; A; Telecommunications industry)

    Founded Silicon Valley Networking; built staff / operations / 8,000 sq ft facility w/250+ ports Gigabit Ethernet; servers routers etc. Built high-profile with the trade press, developed certification programs with networking industry groups and alliances. Conducted multi-vendor interoperability events for HPNA Alliance, Fast Ethernet Alliance, Gigabit over copper Ethernet Alliance, web accelerator firms, network adapter firms, LAN switching and routing firms, software vendors etc. Working with the Wi-Fi Alliance during it's formation (at the time called "WECA"), to define and implement the exclusive interoperability certification program for Wi-Fi products; went on to certify over 3,500 Wi-Fi products. Drove sales, setting up additional centers worldwide, and establishing the first DSL interoperability suite working with Alcatel, Cisco, and the DSL Forum.
