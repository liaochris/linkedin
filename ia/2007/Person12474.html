Barbara Kwasnicki’s Summary

As an expert in design and communication, Barbara Kwasnicki (a.k.a. kwas, a.k.a Brand Girl) takes a stand for pushing messages and strategies to the next level. She is both a master collaborator and a one-woman army. Her cross-pollinated skills of creative director, strategist, forecaster, designer, copywriter, and networker enable her to envision solutions that attract both attention and audiences. She intuitively conjures up messaging that’s clear, creative and consistent. And she’ll improve your brand in the name of furthering her cause: helping companies and individuals to cultivate and communicate their greatness.

Visit www.kwasinc.com to read more about Kwas and her philosophy. And visit www.brand-girl.com to learn more about what Kwas loves in branding today.

  • Principal, Creative Director

    Kwas Inc

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 1994 — September 2005 (11 years 1 month)

    Sept 1994 - 2005 | Principal, Creative Director / Kwas Inc
    After a decade as a traditional designer and art director in all levels of advertising, Kwas felt the impetus to launch a creative strategy and brand development house dedicated to identifying and building brands with pinpoint strategy. Kwas created her agency with the goal of arming clients with the muscles needed to stay on message and to resonate appropriately with their target markets: serve, reach and keep. Moving away from traditional ad-models, Kwas Inc operated as a holistic full-service agency located in her Manhattan loft, a space that also served as a motivational think-tank for client meetings. The space itself was recognized for its exploration of creative new ideas about living/working and public/private space, and was published in James Trulove’s book, Smart Loft.
