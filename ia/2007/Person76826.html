Satish Kota’s Summary

Consultant who bridges the gap between organizations who are in need of processes such as Back office processing or call centers to be outsourced to Asian countries and organizations who are in search of outsourced work in asia.

We Plan, Procure, Analyze, Execute and Manage projects as entrusted to us by our clients in the western countries who would want to take advantage of the Outsourcing to send their low productive, mundane back office jobs to lesser and cost effective organizations in Asia.

We Pick up projects in 3 segments

1. Business Process Outsourcing such as Finance, Insurance, Banking, Medical Education, etc., also known as Non-Voice processes
2. Knowledge Process Outsourcing such as Basic accounting, Medical, Legal, Content, etc.,
3. Call center Processes also known as Voice processes for Inbound customer service help desk, Upselling and Outbound market sales.
4. Provide software services and software product development services to organizations who are in need of these services.

We also provide support for Mergers and Acquisitions for organizations in the Western countries looking forward towards companies in India.
