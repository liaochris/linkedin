Vijay Goel, M.D.’s Summary

Entrepreneur developing new models to empower consumers by helping them to shop for healthcare.

Independent consultant helping insurers, providers, and employers understand consumerism and wellness in healthcare and how to benefit from the movement. Also, strategy consultant available for general management consulting with focus on organizational alignment, growth opportunities, product development, sales/marketing, and lean operations.

Previously management consultant at McKinsey & Co. with M.D. background. Work at McKinsey concentrated on emerging healthcare consumerism space including financing, sales/ marketing, distribution, product development, and customer support. Most recent work focused on implications of pricing transparency on payors and providers.
