Dhiraj Kumar’s Summary

Media and Telecom professional with over eight years of experience in media strategy, telecom and high-tech product development. I am currently an MBA student at Columbia Business School majoring in Media and Marketing. I am also the Chief Operating Officer at Hopstop.com, a fast growing interactive local media company based in New York City. Previously I worked at Deloitte Consulting where I advised a major US telecom provider in wireless strategy. I also worked as a Business Development Manager at a media startup Oddcast Inc in New York where I was responsible for affiliate marketing and business development for their Sitepal product line. Prior to business school I was a Product Development Manager at UTStarcom where I worked with the senior management to manage growth initiatives, implement cost reduction and manage key vendor relationships. I hold a Masters with honors in Electrical Engineering from University of Minnesota and and a Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology.
