Ken Schafer’s Summary

During the past thirteen years, Ken has engineered Sony Music Canada's first online initiatives, co-founded AIMS (Canada's original and largest Internet Marketing association) and played an instrumental role in drafting the CMA's groundbreaking Privacy Code for the Internet. He started and is currently Contributing Editor of One Degree, Canada's leading source of Internet Marketing opinions.

He has consulted and developed strategies for a diverse group of marketing, entertainment and technology companies, bringing his hype-busting, business-centric approach to every project.

Currently, he is VP, Marketing for Tucows Inc, one of Canada's largest and most successful Internet companies with over 6,000 customers around the world.
