Antonio Buehler’s Summary

Former military officer with extensive leadership, turnaround and life experiences. I want to buy troubled/distressed companies and make them successful through structured turnarounds, or buy healthy companies and help them grow. I am particularly interested in the private equity world with a focus on turnarounds or growth equity. I would love to meet like minded individuals, finance professionals in the restructuring or private equity fields, operational turnaround professionals, entrepreneurs looking to sell their companies and those who would like to personally invest in buyouts.
