• Worked with Mother Teresa and her Sisters in Calcutta 1996

• Finalist in the Better Business Bureau Small Business of the Year 2006 competition.
• Developed HealthE Goods Natural Health Store in Jan 2005 with a daily average of 900 unique visitors per day. The website ranks 3rd out of 110 million websites for keyword, ‘natural health products.’ Subscriber base of 3000 which receive our Natural Health Tips monthly via email.
• Researched the applications and efficacy of balneotherapy. Attended the Medical Hydrology conference in Istanbul, Turkey, June 2006.

• Co-Authored the Balneotherapy Chapter of Dr Leon Chaitow’s recent work, Naturopathic Physical Medicine.

Goals:
To create a large scale non-profit environmental medicine clinical research facility.

To educate the public regarding effective, inexpensive natural health solutions in order to lessen chronic disease.

To integrate and educate standardized medicine with the ever-strengthening CAM.

To balance my passions of natural health education and spending quality family time.
