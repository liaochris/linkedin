Eric Jackson’s Summary

Jackson Leadership was founded in 1989. Our global practice advises CEOs, Top Teams, and Directors at our clients on how to build "SMART Organizations" with strong leadership, aligned strategy, and clear process.

Our clients have addressed their succession needs by ensuring there is a suitable amount of "leadership talent" being developed at multiple levels. They have thoroughly vetted their strategy by questioning key assumptions and gaining alignment from the rest of the organization. Their cultures are also characterized by accountability and a "talent mindset."

We help build "SMART Organizations," based on scientific research and through our proprietary and proven techniques.
