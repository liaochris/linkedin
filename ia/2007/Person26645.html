Ellen Levy’s Summary

Each of my roles has in one way or the other involved being the "interface" between groups thinking about the future of technology: the technical and non-technical communities; the VC and the entrepreneur; the industry and academia. My current interest in pursuing technology and innovation-related issues on a global scale comes from a wide range of experiences: while working in startups, large companies, venture capital, research labs and universities.

Ellen Levy’s Specialties:

New technology research, strategy, partnering, M&A, early business plan development, converting basic research ideas into business innovation opportunities. My current area of focus includes evaluating, assessing, and developing new models for innovation within and between organizations.
