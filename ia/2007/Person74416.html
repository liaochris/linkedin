Rory Wilmer’s Summary

I’m an accomplished designer and multimedia artist with over 8 years working experience. I am self taught in software engineering and hardware operating systems. I like making images, creative process and Febo.

I lived most of my teenage life on Merseyside, UK where my family still live. I studied Media Production at Blacon Arts College (Chester UK) and then went to Ravensbourne College of Design & Communication (London UK) to study Interaction Design. After taking some time out in between due to life circumstances I returned to university to combine my Design & Media studies with Fine Art (Liverpool UK). I’m currently based in Prague, Czech Republic where I live with my beloved Kristina.

I am a founder member of the Creation Project and also the BogArt movement. Two distinct art movements which strive to enhance art and our viewing/enjoyment of it. I also make short films as part of the BogArt collaborative.

I’m also a programmer for ZFK (Zizkov Film Klub) which has been running free screenings in Prague since Jan 2007.
