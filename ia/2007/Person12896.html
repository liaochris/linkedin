Brian Cole’s Summary

Currently, I am starting a business, Innovative Sports Strategies. We will provide professional and collegiate sports organizations with unique solutions to meet their business needs. Our core product is a financial management software that has been well received throughout the NCAA, NFL, NBA, and other areas of sport.

If you are interested in learning more about the business as a potential client, employee, or investor...we will be experiencing significant growth in all of those areas in the near future and I would be happy to speak with you. Also, see iss-biz.com for more information.

My professional experience in the sports industry includes a Sports Business MBA through the Warsaw Sports Marketing Center completed in June 2007. I spent the summer of 2006 working for the Boston Celtics as the Corporate Sales/Business Development Intern. I also developed a financial modeling tool for the University of Oregon Athletic Department.

Prior professional experience includes three years as a consulting actuary valuing pension plans and other employee benefits for Fortune 500 firms. I spent all three of these years working for client teams of the Towers Perrin offices in Milwaukee and Chicago.

During my undergraduate work, I had an internship at Northwestern Mutual where I worked in the Long Term Care Insurance Department.

My professional passions/skills are quantitative analysis, attention to detail, process efficiency, and an entrepreneurial spirit.

Brian Cole’s Specialties:

Focus on business operations of professional sports teams with an emphasis on applying a vast array of analytical skills. Have experience building a dynamic budgeting tool for the Univ of Oregon Athletic Department.
