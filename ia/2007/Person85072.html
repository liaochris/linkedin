Domenico Tassone’s Summary

Mr. Tassone has been innovating in the online industry since the 1993 launch of the seminal, Planet StarChild, a multimedia BBS; PSC featured pix, music clips and news from indie musicians/labels and a platform for fans to try before they buy. The following year, he co-founded one of the earliest Web agencies, Streams Online Media Development in Chicago.

Overview: Focused high-energy business professional that has worked extensively with Internet technologies/online media. MBA in Marketing; BS in Finance; developed/taught college-level course, Marketing with Digital Media.

Domenico Tassone’s Specialties:

Start-up expert. Functional expertise: business development(alliances), marketing, entrepreneurial finance, product management, contracts/negotiations, sales mgmt. Experience in software, media, retail & real estate industries.

Expertise with: ad serving, behavioral targeting/content personalization; broadband video(ITV/IPTV/VOD), hypervideo, cable television, streaming/Webcasting, podcasting/RSS, wireless/PDA media. Conversational Italian. Interests: game theory, memetics/viral marketing.
