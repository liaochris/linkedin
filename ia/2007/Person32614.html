Mark Goldwich’s Summary

I am a 20-year property insurance claim professional, a claim expert. Most of my experience and training comes from over 17 years with one of the largest insurers of homes and autos in the country.

I am now using my vast knowledge and experience to help insurance consumers maximize their claim results, renegotiate past claim settlements, or give general property insurance consultations.

My goals are to educate the public in addition to effectively representing them in the handling of their property insurance claims.

I truly enjoy knowing I can level the playing field when a multi-billion dollar insurance giant attempts to stomp on an unsuspecting consumer who mistakenly believes the insurance company is looking out for the consumer's best interests.

Mark Goldwich’s Specialties:

I provide assistance in all types of property insurance claims - fire, water, theft, hurricane, tornado, sinkhole, etc. - and in all claim phases - initial inspections, reinspections, mediation, appraisal/arbitration, and litigation.
