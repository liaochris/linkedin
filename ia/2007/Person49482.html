Kurt Venkatraman’s Summary

With extensive experience in Marketing, Operations, Startups and Disaster Recovery (of companies and projects), my current focus is on Media as a whole. The Digital Signage (narrowcasting) business is seeing the same growth / potential as what we saw in the online business in the late 90s'. My current focus is on creating sustainable Digital Signage businesses.

Given my experience in almost all areas of a company - Marketing, Finance, Operations and Human Resources combined with the experience in the Media, IT and ITES business, I am in a position to make substantial contributions to Industry specific industry organizations, new businesses and established businesses that are making a transition.

At Network Mediagroup, we are constantly on the look out for interesting acquisition candidates from Europe (East and West), partners from the USA (industrial / financial) and partners from Asia (marketing and financial) to expand our network and geographical presence, particularly in digital signage / narrowcasting.

Given my business experience in US, Europe and India, NMG has also set up a subsidiary in India for software development, graphic design and back office functions.

In outsourcing, I have personally been involved in setting up about 5 wholly owned subsidiaries in India for European and American companies.
