Terry Moshenberg’s Summary

Terry is founder and visionary behind the League Of Rock, an International music-based team building event co. partnering with prof. musicians and famous rock stars.

Terry built a dream team to deliver Music-Based Team Building Events Internationally. Core management comprises famous musicians, executives from the music and prof. sports industries, event planning, marketing and finance industries.

This International, well-sponsored organization gives non-professional musicians the opportunity to jam, record and perform with famous musicians and rock stars.

Programs Include:
1. Team Building & Corp. Retreats
League Of Rock creates team building events with famous rock stars. Events occur over a compressed 3 to 5 days. Established preselected goals and well-defined metrics measure results. Employees are charged with building a band, writing songs and recording over a 3 to 5 day period. The emotion-packed process is captured on film and presented live on stage with a rock star hosting and providing a blow by blow description. The Company Band performs and everyone enjoys life changing experiences.

2. JAB Sessions (Join A Band)
Show up, plug in, jam, and go home. League management takes care of all gear, rehearal space, rent-a-musicians if you need one... if you're solo and you want to meet others to create a band situation, this is where you'll do it. White glove service and attention to detail gives all members a fabulous experience.

3. Full Day Intensive Workshops
Intermediate to advanced musicians are invited to workshop with famous musicians in a hands-on full day event. Workshops are limited to 30 people and the events are intense and packed with fun.
