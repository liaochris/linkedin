Mark Kuznicki’s Summary

TorCamp (BarCamp Toronto) gadfly and Connector Mark Kuznicki is a researcher, writer and strategy consultant. Mark works at the intersection of community, innovation and strategy, with a particular interest in social media and the economics of peer production.

The best way to get to know me or contact me on ideas and issues of shared interest is through my blog, http://remarkk.com. But here's the quick summary.

Mark’s work is best known for the ability to take on complex, challenging strategic problems and the ability to synthesize very different areas of expertise into a coherent decision-making framework. Mark’s work crosses several domains:

* Culture, creativity and innovation theory
* Technology and business model innovation
* Economics of regional innovation clusters
* Social media
* Public policy
* Organizational strategy

Mark welcomes opportunities to work on interesting and innovative projects with:

* Communities of practice
* Networks
* Technology startups
* Governments
* Private enterprise
* Nonprofits

Mark Kuznicki’s Specialties:
