Jeremy Chatfield’s Summary

Internet marketing strategy and implementation since 1994, with a technical entrepreneurial background. Ex-academic turned software R&D manager, my focus is web marketing and technology for marketing. Particular expertise is automated use of AdWords via the API and web analytics, especially when dealing with large catalogs and fast changing pricing, keywords, messages or landing pages.

Management and executive experience in UK and USA, international/localised products in B2B and B2C. Strong focus on consumer interaction with the business and with products. More than 20 years experience in early stage startups, both self financed and VC funded.

Jeremy Chatfield’s Specialties:

Paid search automation (bidding, advertising) and paid search management. Web site design using quantitative techniques and rapid iterative development. Web analytics, especially online and offline conversion measurement. SEO, and design of SEO-friendly web Content Management Systems. New software product research and development. Staff management (performance improvement, team hiring & development).
