Stefan Schiffer’s Summary

Stefan Schiffer received the german Diploma degree in 2005 at RWTH Aachen University in Germany. His diploma thesis featured the development of a qualitative world model for soccer playing robots. Within his time as a student worker he acquired expertise in artificial intelligence, knowledge-based systems, and mobile robotics. After graduating, he received a scholarship from the graduate school 'Software for mobile communication systems' to work as PhD student. Currently he is a research assistant at the RWTH Aachen University. His research focus is on mobile autonomous robots, multi-agent systems, and human-robot interaction.

Stefan Schiffer’s Specialties:

artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems, mobile robotics, human-robot interaction
