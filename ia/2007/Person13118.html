Brian Pate’s Summary

I am in the real estate business and have been for 13 years. I am the business coach at Fonville Morisey in Raleigh, North Carolina (a Long and Foster Company).
In addition to being an instructor for pre-license, broker and continuing education classes, I am also a trainer for the company. I also have a hand in the development of new company tools and training the agents how to use them.
You can visit my web site at www.brianpateonline.com or you can visit my blog at www.brianpate.blogspot.com.

I was also in the music business for 20 years as an owner/operator of a mobile DJ company/sound company.

Brian Pate’s Specialties:

My expertise is in the development of tools for use by real estate agents.
