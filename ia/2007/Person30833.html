Gareth John’s Summary

Electronic Engineer with a 1st class masters degree in Electronic & Electrical Engineering and four years experience in the ASIC industry as a System-on-Chip design and verification engineer. A good communicator and planner with strong interpersonal skills facilitating effective teamwork and leadership. Possesses excellent self-motivation allowing them to work off their own initiative. Natural numerical and analytical skills enable effective problem solving.

Now living in Doha, Qatar looking to learn new and utilise existing skills in a role within the electronics/IT industry or further a field.

Gareth John’s Specialties:

Regular user of industry standard tools from Synopsys, Cadence and Mentor including Astro, Star-RCXT, Primetime, CeltIC, Voltagestorm and Calibre, as well as Modelsim, NC-Verilog and VCS simulators. Proficient at writing PERL scripts and makefiles. Experience of Verilog/VHDL, TCL, Tetramax, ANSI C, Assembler, SPICE, & MatLab.
