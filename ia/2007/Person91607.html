Wynn Hausser’s Summary

Passion. Creativity. Intelligence. Strategic Vision. These are the attributes I bring to the organizations with which I work.

My professional background includes a dozen years in communication, marketing, and change management roles, or positions managing those functions; ten years as a documentary and educational filmmaker; and two degrees in communication. My experience as a producer and project manager cuts across these jobs. I've run projects, teams and workgroups, and am very comfortable in leadership positions. I'm adept at using the spoken and written word as well as print, visual and electronic media.

My approach to communication planning and product development is audience-based and user-centric. I'm a firm believer in pre-testing messages and materials, and in continually improving processes. I believe in the old adage "If you fail to plan, you plan to fail." I pride myself in being forward thinking and proactive. That means I'd rather make an aggressive mistake than a passive one.

I am a horizontal and systems thinker that sees connections when others are stuck in silos. I thrive in positions at the nexus where content meets technology, disciplines overlap, and differing perspectives need bridging. I strive to seek common ground whenever possible, but am willing to ask the "go-to-hell" questions that are often left unanswered but can keep initiatives from succeeding.

I love helping people solve their problems, and serving as a mentor and coach. I'm excited by the challenge of coming up with new ideas, not afraid of ambiguity, and willing to take risks. I'm a quick study who can translate complex concepts to lay audiences. I enjoy creating stories that spark people into new understandings. All this and I'm humble too…

Most of all, I'm driven to help make the world a better place and am drawn to people and organizations that share that common goal.
