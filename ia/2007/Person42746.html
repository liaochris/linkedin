John Bryan’s Summary

Approaching 20-years of domestic and international traditional & new media / future media business development experience ("making things happen") primarily for entities involved in the distribution of professionally produced content across digitally enabled platforms.

Recent career experience has focused on evangelizing pre-IPO & recent IPO companies to the leading mixed media content producers (networks, studios and publishers).

Incredibly deep (and current) contact database covering all facets of the content, technology and distribution industries as they relate to the entertainment industry.
