Eric Brown’s Summary

As a creative learning developer and consultant, Eric Brown has a proven record as an entrepreneur and multi-talented professional. On any given job he brings to the table such diverse skills as project management, creative direction, writing, QA testing, directing, and more. As Vice President of Training Outreach for In Touch Ministries, he used his leadership skills to direct his division toward the creation of a comprehensive training model. Mr. Brown’s background includes 20 years of experience in art and design, 11 years of experience in interactive media, and 19 years of experience in leadership and training. He earned a BFA from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville in 1988.

Mr. Brown was a member of the In Touch team for almost 17 years. He was a founding member of the Marketing department where he designed collateral for the entire organization. His creative expertise and leadership allowed him to move to Art Director, and later to Creative Director. After six years in graphic design, he started the online outreach for In Touch.

As Director of the Charles Stanley Institute and Interactive Media Dept., Mr. Brown initiated and developed the online brand, coordinated multiple Webcasts, started the youth outreach, brought the ministry into electronic Relationship Management, and developed the ministry’s eLearning strategic initiative. His leadership and interpersonal skills earned him a promotion as the youngest VP, where he led the International, Institute, and Product Development departments and served as a member of the Executive Leadership Team. His goal for the Training Outreach division was training people globally through involvement in creative/relevant blended-learning experiences.

Prior to joining In Touch, Mr. Brown led WeirdGuy® from identity design to creative learning and brand consulting. Some of his clients include Hilton Hotels, Sea Ray, Fortis Family, Chick-fil-A, UTV, and Lucent Technologies.
