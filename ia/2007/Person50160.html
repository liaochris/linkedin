Laura Levitan’s Summary

The Keller Fay Group is the first full-service market research company focused exclusively on word of mouth (WOM) marketing, using a full suite of best-in-class research techniques. We measure WOM trends and campaign effectiveness; test concepts, ads, and messages to maximize their WOM potential; and offer a complete array of custom research and advisory services related to WOM.

In April 2006, we launched TalkTrackTM, a service that monitors all marketing-relevant conversations in America. The firm was founded by Ed Keller, former CEO of RoperASW and NOP World Consumer and co-author with Jon Berry of The Influentials: One American in Ten Tells the other Nine How to Vote, Where to Eat, and What to Buy, and Brad Fay, former managing director at Roper and NOP World.

Ed Keller, Fay’s CEO, has been called the “godfather of word of mouth marketing research.” And the publication of his book has been called a seminal moment in the development of word of mouth. The Washington Post said, "The Influentials ... deserves a place on the shelf of anyone in business or politics." The Influentials was selected as one of five finalists for the 2004 Berry-AMA Book Prize for Best Book in Marketing, an honor that recognizes books whose innovative ideas have had significant impact on marketing and related fields." Ed is a founding member and President of the Word of Mouth Marketing Association (WOMMA), and co-chair of WOMMA’s Research and Metrics Council. Prior to founding Keller Fay, Ed was CEO of RoperASW, one of the world’s most prominent market research firms.

One of Keller Fay’s leading points of differentiation is our syndicated research, focusing on ALL word of mouth. While some other research firms have chosen to focus exclusively on blogs and chat rooms, our research has proven that at least 90% of word of mouth takes place offline. And we want to help marketers to hear those voices, as well.
