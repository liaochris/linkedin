Tuan Bui’s Summary

tuan.a.bui@gmail.com

Tuan Bui has over 12 years of experience as an IT professional covering diverse platforms and security issues. He possesses a wide range of skills and experiences that enable him to perform in multiple roles.

Tuan has recently joined Trustwave as a Security Consultant based in Dallas, TX. He is Qualified Data Security Professional. His role as a Security Consultant will be in the payment card industry, authentication, network & vulnerability assessment, security architecture, and commercial & government compliant area.

Tuan has finished working on a Fortune 1 engagement for Cybertrust Inc. He has been working onsite at Wal-Mart Corporation in Bentonville, AR since January 2006 on various projects, including Payment Card Industry, Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act, and Gramm-Leach Bliley Act. He has helped Wal-Mart to become government and commercial complaints by identify vulnerabilities in their data repository and work with Wal-Mart internal teams to correct the problems.

Prior coming aboard to Cybertrust Inc., Tuan was the Senior Security Engineer for Rampant Solutions in Arlington, VA. He worked with Smart Cards technologies, include FIPS 201 and IDM.

Tuan has helped Capital One to integrate an Identity Management Solutions on corporate enterprise network in Richmond, VA. His role is to provide business requirements to technical engineers and vendors. He performed "Proof of Concepts" and worked with architecture engineer to best integrate the IDM with minimal time & cost.

Working on projects at the United Stated Department of Agriculture in Washington, DC, he helped document Single Sign-On integration and Point of Contact between government employees and vendors..

Tuan was one of the first staff at Bank of America in the Public Key Infrastructure department. His role was to help BOA with digital certificates on both software and hardware. He worked on "Proof of Concepts" on Verisign products.
