Nigel Balchin’s Summary

A TOGAF certified Enterprise Architect with broad understanding of industry and commerce.

Task-oriented, organised and adept at communicating across business and technical lines using skills acquired from practical experience in operations, development and strategic planning.

Motivated to deliver quality results in many roles including:
• Enterprise architect of a blue chip, multi-national organisation.
• Programme manager of European-wide software deployments.
• Director of IT strategy, planning and infrastructure services
• Project manager of customer-facing application development.
• Application software developer for customer delivery Web services.
• Freelance technical consultant.
• Instructor of professional training courses.
• Author of published technical articles.

Able to lead and motivate teams and individuals remotely (e.g., many of the architecture team were based in the U.S.A.) and off-shore (e.g., much development was outsourced to India).

Excellent oral and written communication skills (e.g., guest panellist at Gartner conference) backed up with proficient use of Microsoft tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio, Project, etc.).

Active in business and process transformation through collaboration with senior leaders and external consultants (including McKinsey, Deloitte). Changes included divestitures, acquisitions, outsourcing, off-shoring and the migration from direct foreign investment (international subsidiaries) to a B2B trading network of strategic partners.
