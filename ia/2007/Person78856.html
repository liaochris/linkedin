Shannon Seery Gude’s Summary

My experience includes interactive recruitment marketing & employment branding, search engine marketing, social media optimization, blogs and wikis, and web site design.

I consider myself an Internet original - my education and career developed in parallel with this industry. The combination of technology and how it affects people is the root of my fascination - there's nothing more exciting than helping people tap in to that; whether it's through an online recruitment strategy, integrated online campaign, or the tools we use to work with our medium.

After graduating from Colgate University, I jumped into the developing Internet world, managing biz dev for a web dev start-up. In addition to increasing our client base, I learned HTML and the intricacies of SEO.

I later joined the Internet team at Cox Newspapers. We redesigned the newspaper classified products online, launch a DART ad serving solution, and bridged the gap between established media and the Internet. My ability to effectively communicate the vision and impact of Internet advertising served to convince many traditional advertisers to take their first leap and shift advertising dollars online. This role soon led me to another media giant - Knight Ridder, where I joined their Internet arm - Knight Ridder Digital (now McClatchy Interactive). I rose quickly from my role in career channel sales, to leading the entire local online sales team, before being again promoted to Regional Manager for the Careers Channel / CareerBuilder sales team.

I later joined The David Group, a boutique Recruitment Advertising Agency that was looking to expand their online recruitment marketing expertise.

Today, I am an avid blogger and a member of the Interactive Solutions team at Bernard HODES Group, a leading provider of integrated talent solutions. Talk to me about the Internet - and my passion for this business quickly rises to the surface with the passion of a true industry evangelist.
