Karie Bible’s Summary

I run a website called FilmRadar.com which is all about specialty film going in Los Angeles. It covers indie films, foreign films, silents, classic Hollywood and other specialty fare. It has a very loyal and highly targeted audience of people who are very passionate about film!

The site has been written up in LA City Beat, the LA Times, Variety, Fade In Magazine and the Toronto Star.

I am currently working to expand FilmRadar to San Francisco and New York. I eventually hoping to committ myselt entirely to this full time.

I also love film history with a passion and have done freelance work proofreading books and doing a historic tour of the Hollywood Forever Cemetery. I was recently featured doing this cemetery tour on Turner Classic Movies.

I've juggled all of this in addition to my professional full time work which has included working for HBO Films, Innovative Artists, Miramax and IGN/Rotten Tomatoes.com
