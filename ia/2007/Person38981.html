Jay Gould’s Summary

I am currently the President of Bolt Media (Bolt.com) in NYC. Since December of 2005 I have advanced from VP Marketing, VP Product, to President upon my partner Lou Kerner's depature in July of 2006.

Previous to becoming a partner at Bolt Media, I had founded and managed a number of the Internet's most trafficked online properties.

Within three years of launch, my online properties were ranked in the top 200 U.S. properties per ComScore Media Metrix (3.36 million unique monthly visitors).

In December of 2005 Bolt Media completed their largest acquisition in the company's 10 year history acquring the company I had previously founded, InterMedia Inc.

From 2001-2003 I worked in sales for several dot coms including dot com flameout Micro Warehouse (later acquired by CDW Corporation) and PC Mall. While working at Micro Warehouse I authored several books on Internet marketing.
