Sara Selis’s Summary

Writer, editor and journalist specializing in healthcare/medicine, with 12+ years of experience. My background is in traditional print journalism, but I appreciate the amazing capabilities of the Web and the advantages of presenting and sharing information online.

In October 2006 I begin a new position as Online Associate Editor for CMP Media’s healthcare division, CMP Medica. This newly created position promises to be exciting, as I help CMP Medica overhaul its various Web sites with useful content for practicing physicians.

In previous positions, I have covered healthcare from a business, research and policy perspective, as a writer/editor for publications including a weekly business journal, a community newspaper, a monthly business-strategies magazine, and at Stanford Medical Center.

I believe in the beauty of good writing and the power of language when it expresses well-thought-out ideas. I also believe we should provide universal health coverage to all Americans -- not through a government-run, single-payer system (which would never be accepted in the United States) but through a hybrid model that combines public financing with elements of free-market competition and consumer choice.
