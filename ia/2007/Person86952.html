Tom Thornton’s Summary

Tom Thornton is currently working as a Realtor for Coldwell Banker United specializing in Central Austin residential real estate. His previous position was vice president of Traveltown, a holding company with assets in RV manufacturing, RV sales, real estate, and aviation charters. Before taking that assignment, he traveled to 15 countries while on sabbatical in fall and winter 2005. From 2000 to 2005, Tom worked in advertising, most recently as Vice President and Director of Marketing at Young and Rubicam's Knowledge Group in New York, which provided brand management and marketing strategy services to multiple Fortune 500 clients. Tom's previous experience also includes three years on WPP Group plc's MBA Fellowship program, which provided experience across multiple marketing disciplines. He graduated from London Business School's MBA program in Summer 2000.

Tom lives in Austin, TX, and is a contributing music and food writer for city blog Austinist.com. He has interviewed bands such as Snow Patrol, The Decemberists, Crowded House, and Bloc Party. Tom has been interviewed about Austinist's music coverage and SXSW by MTV.com, KUT-FM, KROX-FM, and News 8 Austin. He is a former contributor to Gothamist.com and has also written about Sundance for the Austin Film Society.
