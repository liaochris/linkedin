Marina Bajszár (Lago)’s Summary

I have over seven years of UI development experience. I specialize in CSS based layouts, JSP, JSTL, Tiles, within the Struts framework focusing on very reusable and manageable coding standards. Most recently I'm working with ASP's and looking to learn more about open source .Net applications. I have experience with internationalization/localization, co-branding, cross browser issues as well as web accessibility. Most recently, I've been diving head first into AJAX and really enjoy working with it and am familiar with some of the existing libraries. I also have a background working with other front end technologies: XML/XSLT, a little bit of .Net and Cold Fusion, always focusing on the front end architecture of web based applications I've been working on.

I'm always excited to keep learning! I know I'm successful when I can say that I learned something new today. Solving problems keeps me highly motivated and hungry for more.
