Christian Wernberg-Tougaard’s Summary

I believe in networking – and that networking will be a necessity in transforming the old knowledge-society into the future digital service-society. The Digital Service Society is the world connected – as you and I are LinkedIn. Through interoperability and globalization – together with IPlization and GRID-technology – will the way we use IT-solutions and IT-resources change dramatically. We will live in an interconnected world where IT-services are produced globally – and where our IT-systems and IT-use will be contingent on availability – that the infrastructure is hardened – bullet-proof. I will fight to make the IT-infrastructure more robust, while retaining a subtle balance between personal privacy and law enforcement including deflecting cyber terrorism.
