Alfred Huger’s Summary

(Current company bio) Alfred Huger is a well-known fixture in the Internet security industry. A two-time veteran of launching successful start-up companies, Alfred sold SecurityFocus, a leading provider of enterprise threat management systems, to Symantec in August 2002. After the acquisition, Huger joined Symantec Security Response leading Symantec’s efforts in Early Warning Products, Security Response & Managed Security Services and Software as a Service engineering efforts.

Alfred is frequently called upon to speak to security issues in the media with common coverage in such outlets as The Globe and Mail, Washington Post, New York Times, CNN and many others.

    April 1999 — August 2002 (3 years 5 months)

    SecurityFocus Inc. was acquired by Symantec in August 2002.

    As a co-founder of SecurityFocus Inc., a Security Intelligence Product company, Mr. Huger’s responsibilities covered a broad range.
