Randall McAdory’s Summary

"When consumers use blogs, forums, or other forms of “social media” to complain about a product or a service, they probably think they have the ear of only a handful of other disgruntled shoppers. But DaimlerChrysler's Randall McAdory, for one, is listening...McAdory was a keynote speaker at BlogOn 2005 Social Media Summit on Oct. 17, where he shared tips on how brands should handle the blogosphere. He sat down last week with CHIEF MARKETER's Tim Parry..."

The article above represents just one form of harnessing web-based analytics to address key questions concerning brands. With 70% of new vehicle buyers using the internet during their purchase process, there are many opportunities to utilize web-based consumer information for real-time analytics throughout a brands's lifecycle. As the manager of web-based consumer insights at DaimlerChrysler, Randall has established a unique web-based process for understanding consumer behavior and attitudes that address automotive purchase funnel questions as well as provide "cradle-to-grave" analytics for a campaign and vehicle lifecycle analysis.

Randall has an MBA from The University of Michigan Graduate School of Business and a Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineering degree from Kettering University (formerly General Motors Institute).

Randall has over 20 years of automotive experience in many diverse fields such as Marketing (including vehicle brand management), Advertising (including Dodge Advertising Sr. Manager), Manufacturing, Engineering, and Quality.
