Austin Kleon’s Summary

I'm a writer, artist, and technically adept designer looking for a position as an information designer. In the words of Saul Steinberg, I'm "a writer who draws," interested in the ways pictures, words, and design can work together for maximum user benefit, in print or online.

My wife and I are moving from Cleveland to Austin this summer so that she can persue a master's degree in sustainable design at UT's School of Architecture.

You can see my work at www.austinkleon.com.
