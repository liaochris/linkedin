Marcin Talarek’s Experience

  • Creative

    Max Weber

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 2006 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Co-creator of www.telewizjanowejgeneracji.pl weblog for GRUPA ITI which helped in the launch of “n” - a new HDTV satellite television brand offering services such as VOD and PVR. It served both as an credible information source about this new technologies for people who were unfamiliar with them and as an official teaser before the launch of “n”. Core idea of this project was to create a place where an independent journalist would write not only about “n” but also about the offer of competitive platforms in a honest and transparent way and start a constant dialogue with and between potential customers. The project, developed in cooperation with Starcom, was awarded at the EFFIE awards 2007.

  • Junior Creative

    Max Weber

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2005 — June 2006 (1 year 5 months)

    Co-author, as a part of Max Weber creative team, of: www.messageintospace.com concept for GRUPA ZYWIEC (project which allowed internet users to send their messages into space from the second biggest radio-telescope in the world awarded at Eurobest 2005 and shortlisted at Epica), www.saniteckolo.com/reflex idea for SANITEC KOLO (simulation of water droplet movement using Flash 8 environment, awarded at Golden Drum 2005 and One Show Interactive 2006) and www.knorrnudle.pl advergames for UNILEVER (50% increase in Knorr Nudle sales during the running of “Namiotobranie 2006”).
