Jason Kristofer’s Summary

http://www.newstoday.com
http://www.jasonkristofer.com

Jason Kristofer grew up in Brooklyn, New York where he studied and majored in architecture. He moved to Los Angeles, California and studied acting while working under the direction of Sean Penn, James Foley and David Fincher and had the great opportunity of acting along side of Mark Wahlberg, Jack Nicholson, John Travolta, Harry Belafonte and Michael Douglas. It was at this time where he began to reevaluate "Acting" and equated his acting experience to "self branding" and effective marketing.

He has since returned to design with a continued and strengthened focus on both brand and marketing. In 2000 he accepted the great challenge of managing over 75 creatives within Wall Street's Agency.com launching projects and campaigns for GUCCI, Donna Karan, Sony, Coca-Cola, etc.

During his time in New York, Jason Kristofer has co-created and currently co-operates, Newstoday.com. Today he continues to push Newstoday at a rapid rate positioning the Newstoday/QBN brands as the number one destination for creatives online. Newstoday has attracted sponsors and advertising partners such as, Nike, Diesel, DSL55, Rockstar games, American Apparel, DC Shoe Co, ID Magazine, Adobe, HOW, Macromedia and RES.

Jason Kristofer’s Specialties:

Branding & Identity, Strategy, Consulting, Director of Creative, Curator, Speaker and Assembler.
