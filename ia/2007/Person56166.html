Matt Bateman’s Summary

Having only a year's experience in the video games industry, I like to think of myself as a pair of young, fresh eyes. Being a keen gamer since I was a child I can tell instantly what I like or dislike about any given product.
I come from over five years in the hospitality trade, running restaurants and teams of young people. I have the ability to creatively solve multiple problems, in a friendly and efficient manner.

I am a confident, outgoing and altogether professional member of any team.
I can work diligently on my own, though I possess great skills when working as part of -or leading- a team.

I long to be part of a tight, creative team. I have a lot of skills to lend any team, from great interpersonal connection to strong, confident leadership.
My long term goal is to be in a position of creative management. I would love to directly control the final results of a game, to personally sculpt a game from beginning to end.
In the short term, I wish to create a wide range of creative assets for video games, from environments to characters. This I can do to a high, professional standard.

Matt Bateman’s Specialties:

I am a talented artist with a passion for Video Games. I am an accomplished 3D sculptor, and can use 3D Studio Max to a high standard. I am interested in low-polygon artwork, and I love working to a challenging poly-limit.
I also have excellent skills in Photoshop, and can quickly, and with great ease, produce high-quality textures of any size.
