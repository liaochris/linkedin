David MacGregor’s Summary

I have been a marketing communications professional since the early 1980s and have been involved in both international and local brands. My work has included creative direction for one of the world's largest advertising agency networks and helping individuals create and manage their own personal and life brands.

I created and launched one of New Zealand's most successful media properties in the consumer health sector and, in doing so created a viable model and business system that I later sold to my business partners and which is now a multi-million dollar venture and one of the largest advertsers in New Zealand.

I explored the world of interactive communications (brands, loyalty, experiences) as Creative Director for an IT/emarketing business owned by one of the world's most succcessful brewing giants - working to assist in the development of strategies that would intimately connect many of the world's most famous beer brands with their most loyal customers.

I currently consult to an array of clients in New Zealand and around the world and am in the process of developing a range of multi-media and networking tools in the health and creativity sectors www.wellspring.co.nz.

I co-founded Idealog magazine HB Media. Idealog (idealog.co.nz) is the world's only magazine dedicated to the creative economy. It is New Zealand's largest buisness magazine and won the Magazine Publishers Association awards 2007 for best business magazine and magazine launch of the year.

I have taught at Massey University School of Design in Auckland (Marketing, Advertising and Design Research) since 2004 and am preparing a masters thesis on branding.

David MacGregor’s Specialties:

Creative marketing communications strategies, creative direction, creative coaching, campaign design and implementation, publishing, product concept development - prototyping and implememtation.
