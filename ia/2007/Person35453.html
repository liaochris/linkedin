Howard Wright’s Summary

Howard has authoring a new book Ten Steps to Innovation Heaven (www.stickyinnovation.com) which was published on the 14th of March - the book focusses on His 12 years of experience in running Innovation and Foresight teams. His second book is currently being written - titled 'Understanding 2Morrow' which looks at Foresight - what is it, how do you do it - the book also contains material from interviews with 30 full time foresight practitioners.

Insight2foresight Ltd. is a succesful micro-business helping organisations and indiviuals understand and respond to the future.

Howard Wright was Head of Technology Foresight and Innovation for Royal Mail Group plc and has been involved in the Foresight and Innovation Community within the company for over 12 years. The focus of his work has been developing an Innovation and Open culture within a large national ‘state owned’ company; although his roots are Technology based he has found he has a natural ability to bridge the culture between the business and technology communities.

He is passionate about organisational dynamics with a focus on a holistic view of company development. Howard designed and developed two Innovation Labs within the company aimed at changing the ways ‘organisations think about thinking’. This work has taken him around the world working with other companies in the postal sector and also blue chip companies from the private sector

Howard is seen very much as a thought leader in the field of Innovation, Creative thinking, Foresight, Scenario Planning and Strategic Storytelling and has over 12 years hands-on experience as well as teaching these areas both internally and externally around the world. He is a regular speaker on the International Conference circuit and has had a number of papers published through the Henley Management Centre.
