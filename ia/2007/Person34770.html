Hema Kannan’s Summary

Hema is a marketing and CRM professional with 10+ years of experience in leading customer-centric marketing programs. She is currently a Program Director at AOL and leads the broadband activation and engagement program. Hema has overall P&L responsibilities for broadband activation including improving customer engagement and retention. Her responsibilities include marketing strategy and planning, developing online and offline marketing programs in collaboration with partners, campaign management, leading targeted segmentation and insight generation efforts. Hema also spearheaded proactive retention and migration programs to increase customer lifetime value through targeted segmentation and response modeling.

Prior to AOL, Hema worked for Bearing Point Inc. (formerly KPMG Consulting) where she consulted for Fortune 500 companies in the Internet, hi-tech and telecom industries managing overall project revenues of over $8 Million. Prior to that Hema worked in the financial services industry for Franklin Templeton, India (formerly Pioneer Funds).

She has a Masters in Integrated Marketing and Communications from Northwestern University’s Medill School and an under graduate degree in Business. Hema is an active member of Earth Sangha, an environmental non-profit group.
