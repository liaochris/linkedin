Gustav Erlank’s Summary

I am a founder and manager of several businesses, for-profit and not-for-profit with experience in Africa, Europe and the US. I've worked in intermediate means of transport [consulting] to the World Bank, National Department of Transport South Africa and USAID in addition to the renewable energy sector [solar], online media, communications & marketing. I continue to broker and structure various multi-national stakeholder partnerships, including partnerships with the UNDP and various multinational corporations in my current position as founder and CEO of Ecomiles Ltd, a company dedicated to raise awareness and funding for inspiring development initiatives around the Globe.

The Ecomiles Web portal lets members make charitable contributions for everything they buy online. Members simply log on to Ecomiles's e-commerce portal, which links up with Barnes & Noble (Charts), eBay (Charts), Hotwire, and more than 900 other sites that award ecomiles for purchases. The miles are redeemed for dollars, which members can contribute to inspiring development initiatives spanning the globe.
