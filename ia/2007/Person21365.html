David de Brito’s Summary

PROFILE
I am motivated and hard working individual used to taking on responsibilities. I have spent the majority of my professional career in the City of London, in the Financial Services industry at both Consultancy and management level (Sales). I am used to working in within a team environment and managing staff and resources within budgetary and/or profit & loss constraints. I have been fully authorised by the UK’s Financial Services Authority (FSA) to provide Independent Financial Advice and am familiar with Offshore Companies, Trusts, Tax Mitigation & Deferment Schemes. I have been involved in establishing and running offshore companies for the purposes of tax efficient superyacht Purchase/ Leasebacks and have been fully authorised in the conduct of Fiduciary business by the Guernsey Financial Services Commission (GFSC). I bring the experience of a career in intermediary and client facing selling at the HNW and UHNW end of the market, coupled with the ability to run a P&L to profit within a regulated environment.

I have taken part in both in and offshore racing in UK and EU waters. As a commercial Skipper I have navigated the North Sea/ Norwegian to Lat 70° Arctic Circle as well as numerous mixed deliveries within the. I am conversant with ships administration from a Captain’s/ Owners perspective, EU VAT regulation as applied to the superyachts and am well practised at managing Crew under commercial conditions.
