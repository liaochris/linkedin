CounterCulture Productions partners with community on a neighborhood to global scale. From providing interns real production experience and opportunities to work with other companies to providing low/no cost services such as VPR's, web content and PSA's to non profits and nurturing socially relevent content in both documentaries and in narrative, we work to be make our choices and business practices conscious.


We host The Independent Filmmakers' Resource blog, ad free, that contains everything from script requests and screenwritng tips to free tutorials on Boris FX and news and resources for the changing world of independent financing and distribution.


We strive to be religious/ethnic/racial/gender/sexual orientation/cultural/and age bias free.
