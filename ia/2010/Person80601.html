Michael Deitsch Sr.’s Summary

I am a Medical/Health Care Recruiter.

I specialize in these specialties:

1. PHYSICIANS

2. PHARMACIST (CLINICAL & RETAIL)

2. NURSES: RN/ BSN, TRAVEL AND SPECIAL NEEDS

3. PT, OT, SLP AND OCCASSONAL ASSISTANTS

4. SALES & MARKETING

5. RETAINED EXECUTING SEARCH

I have over 30 years experience in consumer products marketing. For ten years my family owned The Deitsch Design Group where I focused on new business development. After a successful business career I now devoted all my energy to management recruiting services for my clients and candidates.

I am always looking for passive, highly qualified candidates who want to work for the best medial facilities in the world.

After building my own dreams for years, its fun to give back and be involved in helping others achieve their hopes and dreams. Though hard work, strong networks and life long connections I try to open doors for my candidates and treat our clients with humble appreciation and respect.

My personal motto is to always promise less and deliver more!
