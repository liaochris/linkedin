Katie Gailes’s Summary

Helping businesses and professionals to grow and reach their lifestyle goals is fun for me. I work within the full spectrum of business development disciplines to remove their roadblocks to success. The network of professionals that I can also bring to bear for my clients include attorneys, CPAs, graphic designers, professional photographers, printers and virtual assistants. I show my clients how to use key tools that enhance their business image and their effectiveness (many of them free), like:

* Living Business Plans
* Electronic Newsletters
* Teleconference Services
* Voicemail Services
* Web-based fax services
* Web-based event registration and management
* Social networking sites and tools

If my clients need something that I don't already know about, I go out and find it.
