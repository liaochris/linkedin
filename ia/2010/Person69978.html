Fiona McEntee’s Summary

The McEntee Law Group provides immigration options & representation for a variety of individuals & employers including:

o Professionals (H-1B visas)
o Investor Visas & Green Cards
o Intracompany Transferees (L visas)
o Athletes
o Artists & Entertainers
o Individuals of Extraordinary Ability in Business & Science
o Employment-Based Green Cards
o Family-Based Green Cards
o Fiancé(e) Visas
o Citizenship Applications
o Green Card Lottery Applications

Please feel free to contact me directly at info@mcenteelaw.com

Prior to establishing the McEntee Law Group, I spent several years working for a Chicago-based full-service immigration law firm where I advised the small/medium-sized firms to multinational Fortune 500 companies in Technology, Pharmaceuticals, & Engineering. I provided counsel on all employment-based temporary categories & green card applications.

I also represented professional sports teams in Chicago on the visa options for foreign-born players.

Additionally, I advised clients in family-based green card & U.S. citizenship applications.

MEMBERSHIP:
* Member, American Immigration Lawyers Association
* Member, Chicago Bar Association
* Member, IL State Bar Association

I am very passionate about my involvement with community-based organizations in Chicago. I serve as Director of the Chicago Irish Immigrant Support Group (CIIS), a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the immigration, social & economic needs of immigrants in Chicago. CIIS is part of an umbrella group of immigrant organizations, the Illinois Coalition for Immigrants & Refugee Rights & a core member of the Coalition of Irish Immigration Centers.

I am also a Board Member for the American Ireland Fund (AIF), Young Leaders Program. The AIF is a philanthropic organization dedicated to raising funds to support programs of peace & reconciliation, arts & culture, education & community development throughout Ireland.

Fiona McEntee’s Specialties:

Employment-based immigration, family-based immigration, investor visas, entertainer visas, professional athlete visas, citizenship/naturalization, green cards, temporary visa.

***Please note the Supreme Court of Illinois does not recognize certifications of specialties in the practice of law.***
