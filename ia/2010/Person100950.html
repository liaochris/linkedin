Joel T. Jordan’s Experience

  • CEO

    SynchTank

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    April 2008 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    SynchTank is a new company created to provide useful, innovative online and mobile solutions to media companies of all sizes, including music publishers, record labels, recording and film studios, music libraries, production facilities, advertising agencies, and more.

    Our flagship product, the result of over two years of research and development, is an advanced full featured proprietary software and online platform for marketing, licensing, and delivering broadcast ready sound files directly to music supervisors, editors, producers, and directors for film, commercials, video games, web applications, rich media, or any outlet where licensed music is needed.

    Featuring unique and powerful tools designed specifically for the synch licensing industry, SynchTank is the one-stop solution for anyone with the need to organize and exploit music, video and art assets to their greatest potential.

    SynchTank is a privately held firm with offices in Brooklyn, NY and Noho, London.

  • Co-founder

    The Savant Guard

    (Music industry)

    April 2006 — Present (4 years 4 months)

    The Savant Guard is an independent 12" and digital-only label based in Brooklyn, NY and run with great ardor by NYC's favorite multimedia punks, the earthprogram ltd.™ known for their massive underground raves in the 90's, highly sought-after design aesthetic, releases on their other labels, house imprint Warmth, and drum n bass label Sound Gizmo, and their art book on the history of American rave flyers, Searching For The Perfect Beat.

    Our highly collectible 12" releases feature custom artwork on beautiful color jackets, heavy euro vinyl limited to the first and only pressing. The coinciding digital release is bundled with bonus tracks and additional remixes and available from all finer DJ outlets including Beatport, Traxsource, Halcyon, Turntable Lab, Dancetracks Digital, Juno, Stompy, Dance Music Hub, Track It Down, Wasabeat, and more.

    Artists include Pink Skull, Toro y Moi and Cubic Zirconia.

  • Co-founder, Designer

  • Author, Searching For The Perfect Beat

    Earthprogram Ltd.

    (Public Company; AMZN; Internet industry)

    2000 — 2000 (less than a year)

    Published in 2000 by Watson-Guptill/Billboard Books and currently in its third printing, Searching for the Perfect Beat is a concise graphic history of the American underground dance music scene and its influence on popular contemporary graphic design trends. Searching draws distinct lines between the past and present, illustrates the evolution of this design style, borrowing trends, regional variations, and gives a nod to those that created a design style entirely bred below the surface level of popular influence. Searching is unrivaled in documenting the distinctive visual style of the American dance music scene and looks at how the underground aesthetic has been coopted by major commercial enterprises in an attempt to attract a larger share of today’s youth market. Includes an introduction by well-known New York Times music critic Neil Strauss.
