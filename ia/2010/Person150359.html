Capt. Muthu Krishnan Iyyappan’s Summary

Capt. Muthu Krishnan Iyyappan (Muthu) has over 14 years of experience in operations and service delivery, Transition and business process consultation, sales, supply chain management, after sales service, training, start-up organizational modeling and execution, process re-engineering, business transformations, change management, transitions, business process outsourcing and the military.

Some of the professional assignment of Muthu includes:

– Subject Matter Expert (SME) in Operations Research & Quantitative Techniques for Indian Army.
– Service delivery re-engineering for BPL Mobile in India and Mobitel in Sri Lanka.
– Process modeling, mapping and execution for Kinpo group, a leading electronics & Software corporate in Taiwan.
– Developed original concepts in customer loyalty program for Shoppers Stop, India’s first departmental store chain.
– Developed original concepts for merchandising / retail space management for ITC, the largest cigarette manufacturer in India.
– Process re-engineering for After Sales service operations for Reliance Communications.
– Business process transformation at Avotus Corp.

A thought leader in process re-engineering using structured creativity, Muthu has been invited by organizations to deliver original thoughts on Branding (Indian Institute of Management – Bangalore, 1998), Churn control modeling (Kuala Lumpur, 2005) and Customer Advocacy (Stamford University- Thailand, 2006).

Muthu is an avid adventurer, traveller and a good cook. He has hitch hiked all over Europe and most of South East Asian countries. He has biked all over India - and have covered the entire strech of Himalayas from the Kashir in north west to Arunachal Pradesh in the north east. He is also an expert in exotic vegetarian cooking.

Muthu is a graduate in Physics with specialisation in digital electronics and has completed master’s course in Business Administration from the Bharathidasan University.

Capt. Muthu Krishnan Iyyappan’s Specialties:

Customer Service Delivery and Operations, Process Re-engineering and Consultation, Customer Advocacy, Customer Retention, Customer Lifecycle Management, Structured and Adhoc Creativity, Business Transformations, Telecom Management, FMCG Management
