Eric Monderer’s Summary

Eric is a top-level sales executive with 25 years of experience building world class, revenue based, OEM, licensing and business development sales teams. He has a strong track record of success closing deals with consumer, enterprise, device and wireless carrier customers and furthermore brings a mix of both entrepreneurial and big company experience.

He has the ability to analyze market trends and develop innovative sales strategies that have resulted in strong revenue growth. He is a self-starter, enthusiastic and able and willing to "roll up his sleeves" to pitch for and to close deals. Furthermore, he leads from the front to achieve early wins and positive momentum.

For the last 9 years, as the Vice President at Nokia / Intellisync he closed over 150 deals worth in excess of $250M, which helped drive the stock price from $2 to $200 in a span of three years. Eric has closed key partnerships with IBM, salesforce.com, Sybase, Intuit, RIM, Oracle, Yahoo, Microsoft, SAP, Amdocs and many wireless carriers worldwide.

Furthermore, Eric was key in Nokia's acquisition of Intellisync in 2006 for $440M.

Previously, he was the Vice President, Sales at Delrina and helped drive the revenue of the company from $5M to over $100M in a span of 4 years. Delrina was acquired by Symantec for $450M in 1995.

Eric also held senior management roles at ROLM/IBM and 3Com.

Eric was a board member of GlooLabs and involved in its acquisition by Cisco in 2008.
