A little about me:
Ph.D., Brandeis University; M.M., University of Hartford; B.A., Colgate University; graduated from the Berklee College of Music Guitar Summer Performance Program; taught numerous history, theory, and musicianship courses over the years at Emmanuel College, the University of Hartford, Brandeis University, and Eckerd College to a diverse group of students; presented papers at chapter meetings of the American Musicological Society on the songs of Liszt and Schubert, including "Liszt's 'Wanderer Dream' and the Act of Transcription," which won Schaefer Memorial Award for Outstanding Graduate Student Paper; co-author of the forthcoming Historical Dictionary of Rock and Pop (Scarecrow Press, Inc.), articles and blogs on popular music for Creative Loafing, the Sarasota Herald-Tribune, and the Hartford Advocate; former arts/social studies curriculum designer for Simsbury High School (CT).

Green building & retrofit technologies, arts education, grant proposal writing, curriculum development, freelance grant consultation, organizing successful fundraisers & concert events, journalism, music performance.
