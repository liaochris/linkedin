David Watson’s Summary

Accomplished senior executive and business leader with over 20 years of experience in Sales, Marketing and General Management in consumer products, and 18 years in fortune 200 companies. Management background includes Sales, Consumer & Customer Marketing and General Management leadership roles in both the domestic, as well as international markets. Significant experience in the food industry, i.e. Knorr culinary ingredients, Knorr/Lipton dehydrated soups, Hellmann’s/Bestfoods dressings, Mazola cooking oils, AdeS soy beverages, and processed meats. Strategically focused, proven team leader and motivator, effective change agent, with a record of outstanding results. Tri Lingual, English, Spanish and Portuguese, with experience in leading multi-cultural initiatives.
