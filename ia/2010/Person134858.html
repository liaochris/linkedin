Mark Werrell’s Summary

VP, Consulting Services with CoreMatrix Systems, a leading provider of consulting services related to the strategy, planning, design, implementation, adoption and support of Cloud Computing solutions for mid-sized to Fortune 500 companies. CoreMatrix is dedicated to helping customers extract value from their technology investment, enabling them to rapidly increase productivity and realize a faster return on investment.

Prior to CoreMatrix, held the position of Regional Director for the East coast, with Endeca. Endeca is a dynamic, high growth Cambridge MA based software firm, recognized as a pioneer and leader in the emerging Information Access Platform (IAP) space. IAP is a convergance of the Search, Web Navigation and Business Intelligence spaces.

Prior to Endeca, started GlassHouse's New York region, as General Manager, in January 2004. This was a completely green field environment, developing the region hand-in-hand with a Sales Director - literally starting from "two guys and a pencil". Achieved $1.4M/qtr run-rate within 18 months and regional profitability within 9 months.

Prior to joining GlassHouse, spent 8 years with Cambridge Technology Partners (CTP) in the UK, Ireland, France and the US. As the Services Director for the Northeast region, grew revenues from $12M to more than $24M in less than 18 months.

Prior to joining Cambridge, held management positions with Cap Gemini and Oracle Corporation (consulting) in the United Kingdom.

Co-author of "Executives Guide to Web Services" (see www.amazon.com).

Mark Werrell’s Specialties:

Thrive in dynamic / high growth software and service firms, building teams, creating collaborative team environments, rapid change, driving execution and operational excellence, developing new business opportuinities, and building strong client relationships. Excel delivering client services, with focus on time to value and return on investment, while also ensuring high quality deliverables and client satisfaction.
