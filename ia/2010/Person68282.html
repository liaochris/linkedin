Alicia Kirschenheiter’s Summary

I'm the owner of Evolution Total Wellness Inc. located in New York. My vast array of experience in the wellness and fitness industry covers many different areas. My experience includes being an American Council on Exercise Certified Advanced Health & Fitness Expert, an in-demand personal trainer, a fitness coach (for individuals and companies) and a certified weight & lifestyle management consultant.

In addition to above professional activity, I'm also a published wellness/fitness writer for a variety of publications as well as being the author of the children's book "Oscar and Otis - Fat Fighters".

My company (Evolution Total Wellness) mirrors my wellness philosophy of a "whole" fitness solution for today's healthy lifestyle challenges. "Whole fitness" focuses on a fit body, a holistic nourishing diet and inner peace for the spirit.

Wellness and fitness is not as complicated as one may think. You can achieve both if you want - and give me a shout if you would like some help! My passion is to help you!
