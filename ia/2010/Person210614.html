Scott Abel’s Summary

I am a passionate & motivated Graphic Designer working in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am a huge believer in passion, drive & work speaking for itself & hope that I will receive the opportunity to share & learn with you.

I am no longer available for design work through Variant Creative as I am fully devoted to utilizing my expertise in graphic design to Simply Hired now. However, I am always up for making new connections & helping others find what they are truly looking for. So please send me a line anytime & I'd be happy to chat.

As for my Abel 'n Kane Graphic Clothing Co. launched Oct, 2009, it isn't my side business. It is more or less an art & social project. Kind of like a Musician who's also an Accountant or a Fine Artist who's also an Engineer. Abel 'n Kane is a project of mine meant to bring fellow artists together to produce their art in the form of fashion: www.abelnkane.com

Here's a brief biography of me as it relates to me & my Graphic Design career: My area of expertise is Graphic Design, having been exposed to numerous projects ranging from advertising & marketing to information graphics, full graphic systems, new media & visual design. I’ve been designing professionally since 2002 after being hired to assist a well respected company (Murdoch's Ranch & Home in Bozeman, MT) with a name & brand platform change after my BFA. After achieving success with their new name & design, I decided to pursue an MFA in Graphic Design from a very challenging & rewarding program at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco. Since graduating in Dec, 2007, I interned at an esteemed firm called Pentagram Design for a full year & continued expanding my horizons within the design realm by contracting & freelancing at some great firms in the Bay Area. In Oct, 2009, I landed a full-time in-house Graphic Design position at Simply Hired... pretty much my dream job, as Simply Hired's values, visions & mindset is near perfect in my perception.
