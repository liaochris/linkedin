Gabriel White’s Summary

Gabriel White is a seasoned interaction designer, design strategist and speaker.

His deep experience creating next generation device experiences includes work at leading agencies and technology innovators including Punchcut, frog design, Motorola and Microsoft Research.

Gabriel White’s Specialties:

Interaction design (IxD), User Interface Design (UI), Mobile, Cellphones, Handheld, Tablet, Embedded Devices, Ubiquitous Computing (Ubicomp), User Centered Design, Emerging Markets, Consulting, Design strategy, Ethnography
