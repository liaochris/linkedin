Julie Ward’s Summary

Julie Ward joined Style Advertising in September 2008 as Public Relations Director. She has public relations experience in agency, corporate and non-profit environments. Originally from Virginia, Julie graduated with honors from the University of Mississippi with a bachelor’s degree in Journalism, emphasis in Public Relations and minor in Spanish.

Julie serves as the current chair of the 25-member Junior Board of Magic Moments, an Alabama-based wish granting organization. In this role, she helped launch the annual Kick'n Chick'n Wing Fest in 2009, which raises funds and awareness for the organization. She also serves as a Magic Maker, granting wishes for Alabama children whose childhoods have been interrupted by life-threatening and life-altering medical conditions.

In addition to her work with Magic Moments, Julie serves as the Communications Director of the Rotaract Club of Birmingham, which is the largest club of its kind in the world. Through her leadership, the Club launched a new social media plan, a message board and e-newsletter. She also manages media coverage and assists with the Club's annual report.

In 2008, the Public Relations Association of Mississippi recognized Julie’s work for the Oxford-Lafayette County Chamber of Commerce with a PRism Award, the highest honor awarded to public relations professionals in Mississippi. She was also a contributing writer to The Oxford Eagle, Invitation Oxford, The Daily Mississippian and The Ole Miss.

Julie Ward’s Specialties:

Julie’s responsibilities as Public Relations Director include writing press releases, media advisories and pitches. She also crafts copy for collateral materials, websites and ads. Julie has established strong relationships with the Birmingham media and is experienced in making clients visible in print, online and on-air. She also has experience in managing sponsorships, working with in-house marketing teams and consulting her clients on special event planning.
