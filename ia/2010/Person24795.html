Blake Vogt’s Summary

I am a motivated business professional with a focus and passion for leading people and impacting an organization’s bottom line. I have successfully held several positions of increasing responsibility within a Fortune 500 Company's Global Supply Chain Organization including, Supply Chain Analyst, eSourcing Specialist, Commodity Manager, and most recently, Worldwide eSourcing Team Leader.

Current responsibilities include:
Leading a team of strategic sourcing specialists located in North America, Latin America, Europe, and Asia. Manage, develop, maintain, and train on internal Supply Chain applications and processes. Ensure all sourcing events are conducted in a fair and ethical manner across Corporation. Manage the development of an annual eSourcing project plan. In FY10, this plan was approximately $1.5B in spend and consisted of nearly 2,000 projects. Work closely with our Helpdesk to ensure all global users are receiving the proper answers to their systems related questions in a timely manner and utilize feedback to further develop the systems and processes. Develop, monitor, and improve key performance indicators related to eSourcing. One of our most significant accomplishments in the past two years was the successful deployment of the new eSourcing software Ariba On-Demand across the entire Corporation. Effectively led the eSourcing Team in configuring the new system to meet the requirements of all 65+ Divisions and then coordinated the training of nearly 1,000 users around the world. The new eSourcing software was rolled out to the Corporation in approximately ½ the time as the previous system while training nearly 2 x the number of users.
