Robin Haynes’s Summary

Robin Haynes is one of the most sought after real estate specialist in the St. Louis marketplace. Throughout her career Robin has been an in demand leader serving in various real estate capacities such as President of Jefferson County Board of Realtors, President of WCR Jefferson County, CEO & Team Leader for Keller Williams Southwest and many more.

Today Robin is focused on what she does best! That’s working with real estate clients and coaching real estate professionals & brokers.

In an uncertain real estate market, selecting the right team of professionals is one of the most important decisions you will make in achieving your home buying/selling goals. Whether you are a first time home buyer or a seasoned home seller having the right agent committed to your best interests is critical. Our team will assist you in determining an appropriate market valuation, negotiate contracts and manage all details to achieve a successful home closing. Superior research, home positioning and marketing plans are the keys to our success.

We focus 100% of our time & energy on helping our clients meet their real estate goals. By providing outstanding customer service we earn the right to work with our clients as well as their families, friends & colleagues. We are proud of the fact that 80 percent of our business in generated by referrals from satisfied past clients.
