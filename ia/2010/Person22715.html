Beth Cole’s Summary

Beth Cole is a web consultant and coach specializing in startup marketing for consultants, coaches and growing companies. She uses coaching techniques and the latest in web tools help her clients prosper online.

Beth is passionate about start ups and online marketing. She is a Certified Reach Online Identity Strategist and a member of the International Webmaster's Association, the International Coaches Federation (ICF), Heartland ICF, the Center for the Advancement of Christian Coaching, and the Christian Coaches Network. She has served as the Director of the Nebraska Home Business Association, and as a board member for the Nebraska MicroEnterprise Partnership Fund.

One of her clients said, "Working through the Revvv! process with Beth helped me create a real way to move forward with my business. Beth's ability to coach and collaborate with me helped us co-create a vibrant, responsive website of which I'm very proud. Beth helped me find the key to using the web to articulate what I want my business to accomplish in the world, what I do, and why it's of value -- just what my clients need to know. Beth also introduced me to some new ideas and tools to make my website more interactive and useful. I'm thrilled with the design. Beth's service is unbeatable and the sense of support I get from working with her is even better."
--Joan Resnick, CEO The Real Life Training Group.
