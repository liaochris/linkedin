Dawn Van Zant’s Summary

I am the President and founder of www.InvestorIdeas.com, a global investor and stock research portal specializing in sector investing. I have over 30 years experience in the financial markets, sitting in many chairs- from trader to broker, to IR, PR, and entrepreneur and business owner.

Investorideas.com follows the money in leading sectors as the market shifts sentiment.

Investorideas.com is a hub that also contains other investing domains within it, including www.water-stocks.com, www.renewableenergystocks.com and www.gold-miningstocks.com.

Investorideas.com was one of the first investor portals for stock research on renewable energy, water and Homeland Security. We attract leading experts in the sectors to provide commentary and insight for investors. We currently have experts in energy, water and solar featured on the site.

Other key investing sectors we cover include biotech, mining, energy, tech and nanotech. We feature global stock directories, news and research for each sector we cover. Our investor ideas members get access to premium content including 11 stock directories by login including the water stocks directory and renewable energy stocks directory.

We have set a very high goal for 2010 - One Million Members –encouraging investors to invest in sectors that reflect their values and beliefs.
http://www.investorideas.com/membership/

I created Investorideas.com as a meeting place for investors, public companies, industry leaders, traders and business start-ups to come alive and thrive and find great investor ideas!

Our site attracts everyone from a small retail investor to fund managers, to fortune 500 companies, Government and media.

We are always looking to expand and grow our global network of industry experts, research analysts, newsletter writers, individual and accredited investors, public companies and funding sources within the sectors we cover.
