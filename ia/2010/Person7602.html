Alison Gilbert’s Summary

I have been self-employed and an entrepreneur since 1983. I also come from a family with a history of business ownership. I have many interests and thrive on the opportunity to build businesses from those interests.

I had a business in the health food industry that involved nutritional counseling, teaching healthy cooking and eating, created marketed and sold an allergy-free muffin product.

I had another business in the art field. It included decorative painting on re-used furniture belonging to clients as well as painting small household objects called Recycled with Love Collectables. I also invented a Five Step Decorative Painting System.

Another business I had was in the holistic health field. I represented practitioners, educated the public and advocated for holistic modalities to become part of the mainstream of medicine and healing.

I presently own a corporation that started as a graphic design firm and has grown to include marketing consulting. This involves working with clients on the development, promotion and projects evaluation of their businesses. I also specialize in their Branding 'from the inside out' as well as designing their visual identity and collateral materials, overseeing their web design and social media.
