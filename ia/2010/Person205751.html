Tomas Carlsson’s Summary

I have worked with Computers & Networks for more than 20 years, with companies and customers from many industries and sizes. I have worked in many verticals within the IT industry. These include Distributor's, Manufacturer's, VAR's, Systems Integrator's, Carrier's and ISP's.

During my career I have worked in many different roles. Developer, MIS, support, presales, system manager and consultant. I have worked in infrastructure and support in complex environments. So I have become a specialist in being able to understanding complex systems quickly and being able to provide support and help customers with those systems.

The last 5+ years I have worked with pre- and post sales support and consultancy in Security Systems Integration companies with infrastructure related products. In addition, for the last 3+ years I have designed and implemented a complete support function (contracts, inventory, call handling etc) at a startup Security Systems Integrator.

I have done some sales and presales and also some training/workshops on Security Products.
I am currently a self employed contractor.
