David Bradford’s Summary

I am Chief Executive Officer of Fusion-io. It could well be the next great Information Technology company on the planet. I joined the Company full-time when Steve Wozniak, inventor of the Apple Computer, joined the Advisory Board.

See www.fusionio.com. Fusion-io has developed a truly disruptive technology called the ioDrive. The Fusion-io Solid State Disk Drive is a high-density, extremely fast memory extension or drive replacement based on flash memory technology.

Fusion-io has major deals with HP and IBM; Dell is one of the company's top investors. The carbon footprint of this technology is only 1% of current technology. Think of the millions of $'s that this drive will help save in terms of Data Center operational costs to say nothing of the 100X performance improvement over traditional Disk drives. Fusion-io puts the "power of the SAN in the palm of your hand."


I am also the Founder/CEO or Advisor to a number of Web-based companies including currently Linking Universe, FundingUniverse.com, Turbulin.com, Terravado.com, and Greenwala.com. Linking Universe is a top business application on Facebook. Funding Universe is a website that connects Entrepreneurs with Angel Investors. Greenwala is a new social network for everything "green." Turbulin.com – is a web-based portal offering on-demand call center services. Terravado.com is a CRM app. for major corporate enterprises that want to reduce their carbon footprint - Is Your Business Carbon Neutral? Website: www.terravado.com

I am also heavily involved in private equity. My current passion is HG Equity a new buyout fund involving Jon Huntsman, Sr, Bob Gay, and Steve Young.

Prior to my work on the Internet and in private equity, I served as General Legal Counsel to a number of entities. For 15 years I was General Counsel to Novell, Inc. I also served in a similar capacity to Dynix Corporation, Prime Computer and for Governor elect of Utah, Jon Huntsman, Jr.
