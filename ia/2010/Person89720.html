Jan-Willem Derksen’s Summary

In 2007 I obtained my bachelor of Public Management at the University Applied Sciences Utrecht. My internship was on Aruba where I worked as a Security Manager at the Radisson Aruba Resort & Casino. Some of my responsibilities entailed renewing and updating the emergency plans.

After my graduation I went to the HAN University of Applied Sciences where I took up Economics. I combined this study with a part-time job to gain working experience.

During my studies I gained valuable working experience at several companies. In July 2009 I got the opportunity to start working fulltime as an Account manager at Aaldering ICT. My responsibilities are:
- Generating new business in a B2B-setting for region Arnhem
- Expanding business relationships
- Solution Selling
- Managing ICT projects
- After Sales
- Customer Relations

My goals for the next few years are to improve my leadership and project skills.
