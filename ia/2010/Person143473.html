Michael Sato’s Summary

Seasoned HR/Learning Development Executive specializing in:

Working with Executives and their teams on organizational alignment, business goals, roles and responsibilities to cascade throughout the Company for maximum organizational and employee alignment to Corporate Goals.

Providing Executive Coaching and integration of new executive assignments (The First 90 Days) including accelerating team alignment and productivity.

Developing training and development action learning programs for a variety of executives and management.

Designing and facilitating Management and Team business planning offsites.

Providing generalist HR support for programs, practices and policies within the broader human resources arena.

Working with non-profit and educational Governing Boards, Executives/Superintendents and their teams to set vision, mission and goals for organizational - programs alignment.
