Bruce Hurwitz’s Summary

At HSS we offer executive recruiting services to our non-profit and corporate clients. Our mission is, whenever possible, to promote the hiring of honorably discharged veterans of the US Armed Forces and Merchant Marines.

For our international clients, in addition to meeting their staffing needs, we will assist them with establishing a presence in New York City. Services include finding managers and administrative support staff, office space, and working with attorneys to obtain all necessary licenses and permits.

Bruce Hurwitz’s Specialties:

We offer executive recruiting services for domestic clients, and additionally, for international clients, we will assist them in setting up their operations in New York City.
