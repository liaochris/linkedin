Jay Engelmayer’s Summary

My short-term goal is to make my company, Optimax Ltd. the foremost retailer of prescription eyeglasses and vision care products on the web. I believe that the technological barriers are minuscule compared to the psychological barriers the average customer has to buying glasses online. This is where marketing and site design become ever important. Thinking outside the box is crucial - my long-term goal is to change the way people buy eyeglasses and by doing so, change the nature and structure of the optical industry as a whole.

My professional experience in the office environment has, for the most part, been in the online marketing and business development arenas. I have a strong understanding of the metrics behind a campaign and an overall general sense of what consumers want. I have strong writing skills and have spent much of my professional and personal life advancing that strength. I believe in a strong affiliate program as the core to any successful campaign and believe affiliate partners should be compensated as true partners for their efforts.

I have an innate understanding of Marcom strategies and how to create ads and site designs that work (they are not always the ads and site designs that look the best). The flow of a website and the written and aesthetic components within them are my specialty. I have broad connections in the media and public relations field. I understand the need for press-releases even when there is nothing to truly announce and I advocate regular op-ed submissions as a part of any PR strategy.

Stay tuned to see how it all turns out.

Jay Engelmayer’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy, writing, building creative and marketing materials, working with the design team. Business development via networking and closing the deals. Turning lofty ideas into reality.
