•Designed, built and installed the world’s first illuminated bus advertisement for New York’s MTA as well as generated an installation guide complete with images and graphics for VIACOM/CBS Outdoor.

•Organized and directed the sponsorship of Cerwin Vega’s custom Ford Mustang featured in StreetTrendz Magazine utilizing LEC Technology as the primary lighting accessory.

•Fabricated interior cabin of 2005 Pontiac GTO demo vehicle utilizing aluminum, urethane plastics, silicone molds, fiberglass structures and electronic motorization.
