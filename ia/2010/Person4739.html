Allison Tucker Keisman’s Summary

As the founder of Customer Finders, I have 14 years of online and offline direct marketing, retailing and ecommerce experience. I provide strategic and consumer marketing leadership to various retailers and publishers including Saks Fifth Avenue, NetGrocer, Spa Finder, Mothernature, The New York Times, Home Décor Products (Homeclick.com), Time Inc.s’ This Old House Ventures, ClubMom, Takkle.com, Appetizerstogo.com, Portero.com, and many others.

I began my career at Saks Fifth Avenue in Corporate Marketing where I was responsible for acquiring new customers for the Saks retail stores and launching Saks’ first ecommerce website in 1996. As one of the co-founders of Shop.org, which is now owned by the National Retail Federation, I helped plan the industry’s first e-retailing conference for Shop.org in 1997. I have enjoyed being a visionary and trend spotter since the industry’s inception.

I have held various marketing positions and consulted to companies in order to build brands, grow a customer or member base through integrated direct marketing programs, generate millions in revenue, and develop strategic partnerships. I have also been published as an expert in search engine marketing (SEM) with over eight years experience and a strong knowledge-base on trends in the search industry.

As the former VP Marketing for Homeclick.com, my biggest accomplishment was contributing to the success of turning this near bankrupt retailer profitable in 15 months through judicious and creative marketing practices.
