Bruce Kratofil’s Summary

At The Plain Dealer Market Research department, I support advertising sales, editorial, and circulation. I use: GIS tools such as PrimeLocation and MapInfo; consumer opinion tools Scarborough Prime Next, Media Audit and RAM; web analytics tool Omniture Site Catalyst and statistical analysis with SPSS.

BJK Research specializes in Website design and technical writing.

Web design - specializing in economic and financial sites; Webmaster for the National Association for Business Economcs (www.nabe.com) and the National Economists Club (www.national-economists.org); Podcast editor. Use Adobe Creative Suite products for design.

Technical Writing- co-author, Windows 2000 Secrets and the Windows 95 BugBook. Former Editor of BugNet, and writer of The BugBlog (www.bugblog.com)- where I write about things that go wrong with your computer.
