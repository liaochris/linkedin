Adam Chronister’s Summary

I started developing websites and graphics independently in 2003. Once I got my feet wet in web and graphic design, I began to take on bigger projects, both personal and professional. In 2005, I decided to take the next leap and attend college to pursue my passion for web design and software development. My studies were focused around object oriented programming languages and design theory. I have since worked in many realms of the technology industry from networking to software development to my most recent role as Marketing Director / Project manager at West Coast based software development firm Gravity Jack Software. There I manages a large array of projects and task including overseeing search engine optimization, social network marketing as well as other related task.
