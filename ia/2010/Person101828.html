John Cunha’s Summary

John Cunha is a lawyer and senior associate with ASAR Attorneys in Kuwait. John is a dual qualified attorney and a member of both the law societies of England & Wales and of South Africa. He received his law degree from The University of the Free State in South Africa and thereafter obtained his Masters degree in Business Administration (MBA) at The University of the Free State, South Africa in collaboration with De Paul University, Chicago, USA. John also holds a Masters degree in International Trade Law (LLM) from the University of Stellenbosch, South Africa. John was admitted as a South African attorney in May 2000 and practiced as such in Johannesburg, South Africa, where he primarily focused on corporate/commercial transactions as well as commercial litigation matters. He joined ASAR Attorneys in April 2006 and currently practices in the areas of Banking & Finance, Mergers & Acquisitions and Capital Markets. John is a dual citizen of both Portugal and South Africa and is fluent in English, Portuguese and Afrikaans.

Corporate Law, Banking and Finance, Merger's & Acquisitions and Capital Markets Law.
