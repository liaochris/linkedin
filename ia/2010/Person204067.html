Tim Norton’s Summary

I'm a Web and Social Entrepreneur who builds online communities that make action all over the world. I see decentralizing power as the number 1 key for the human race to truly evolve and get in sync with the larger natural world, so we live sustainable lives.

To achieve this, I focus on making the small strong. Whether this be individuals in a mass, small businesses in a corporate controlled marketplace, or a small country in a super power centric global economy - I build communities that unite the small into powerful sustainable movements.

A big part of the first stage in this journey for me is helping make it easy for entrepreneurs to grow their small businesses and run them across the world on the web. I have founded, self funded and co-invested in the development of several online business tools and online communities in the areas of business, health and sustainable transport and run a successful web and consultancy business for many years.

I am currently an owner and co-founder or partner in the following ventures:
* Online Personal Finance - Heaps! http://www.heaps.co.nz
* Online Community Design/Dev - Social Capital http://www.socialcapital.co.nz
* New Zealand's Global Business Community: Made From New Zealand - http://www.madefromnewzealand.com
* Living Offices - Airspace http://www.livingairspace.com
* Online Business Plan Tool: PlanHQ - http://www.planhq.com
* Global Cycling Community - Challenge for Change - http://www.challengeforchange.co.uk
* Online health Community: The Naked Trainer - http://www.thenakedtrainer.net


You can find more about me here: http://www.timnorton.co.nz

  • Green Office Designer & Founder...

    Airspace

    (Privately Held; Commercial Real Estate industry)

    September 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Office buildings and workplaces haven't been built for healthy living, so with Airspace we're changing them into living, breathing self sustaining spaces, with fresh open air, living walls, solar, wind and bicycle energy, grass areas, vege gardens and more.

    Why 1 Floor at a time?
    Many office buildings are now old, and the cost and time needed to upgrade the whole building to be environmentally friendly is often not affordable and it doesn't engage the tenants in any actual daily life change. The solution is converting each floor from offices into living air spaces, that the people who work in them, help convert and maintain.

    Our 1st Active Mission - Courtney Quarter, Wellington
    The first space mission is converting a 450 square metre floor at level 3 Symes De Silva House, 97-99 Courtney Place in Wellington New Zealand. Conversion started on 9 September. You can track progress by following our video documentary at http://livingairspace.com
