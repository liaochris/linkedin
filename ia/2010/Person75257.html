Gigi Kizhakkechethipuzha’s Summary

Senior level technology executive with 20 years of experience in increasing organizational value and reducing operational costs using innovation, technology, entrepreneurship and off-shoring. A true catalyst for positive changes in organizations as well as people.

Prior joining ARC Group Associates in 2006, where I currently work as the Chief Information Officer, I have worked in multiple industries in both US and India for organizations of varying sizes as an employee and a consultant. In my previous role, I worked as an application architect for Comcast Corporation for 4 years. Some of my former employers include Comcast Corporation, Lockheed Martin, Pfizer, University of Pennsylvania and VisAlign. Additionally, I had played vital roles in more than one start ups which includes Courtport, LLC (http://courtport.com) where I serve as the CTO and partner. Recently I founded http://FreeCourtDockets.com, the only web site where you can get a case docket free. I am also experienced in setting up off-shore development centers and have managed and delivered BPO solutions with high rate of success. My project management skills include managing and mentoring both on-shore and off-shore IT \BPO teams. My technical skills include hands-on enterprise level development on dotnet platform including web applications, DBA level SQL Server expertise and excellent data-modeling skills.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, Physics and Statistics from M.G University, India and another Bachelor of Science in Information Science from Drexel University, graduated with Summa Cum Laude.
