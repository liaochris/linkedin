Ron Brant’s Summary

Brant Creative is a husband and wife collaborative. Whether working with a client, or supporting the design and advertising community, our purpose remains: Create business success and iconic brands through a continuum of interactions and experiences, from advertising to architecture, identity to public relations, packaging to philanthropy.

  • Design Director

    (Privately Held; Graphic Design industry)

    April 2007 — February 2009 (1 year 11 months)

    Build brands and business success for every client, regardless of size, caché, or budget. Understand the context and business environment and apply them to invent, evolve, and build identities, visual systems and corporate communications – the company face and voice that will halo experiences, tell compelling stories and propel brands.

    Serving both Minneapolis and San Francisco offices and clients, Ron was responsible for the concept and implementation of identity and visual systems, advertising and channel engagement, new product introductions, and event communications.

    Every day Ron strives to invent what’s meaningful, what’s engaging, and what’s important to people. But most of all, he uses the power of design and creativity to humanize the world.

    Clients: Applied Materials, Brocade Systems, Boston Scientific, Invisalign, Mayo Clinic, MTS, NEP Financial, Starkey Labs, Charles Schwab, and Wausau Paper

  • Group Creative Director | Brand Architect

    Carlson Marketing Group

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1998 — 2006 (8 years )

    After leading teams with clients like Northwest Airlines, Merrill Lynch, US Bank, and Visa, Marilyn Carlson Nelson engaged Ron's insights and talent to evolve the culture and identity of Carlson's global brand. Through a variety of media, including broadcast, print, interactive, events, and environmental design, Ron transformed what was to be a short-term Six Sigma initiative into a distinct brand personality across the enterprise.

    During the 8+ years with CMG, Ron received two Carlson “Golden Rose” Awards for his work on greatwork, the initiative that created more than $200M in enterprise efficiencies.

    In 2006, Marilyn Carlson Nelson awarded Ron Brant with a "Carlson Fellows Award" in the category of Creativity. From nearly 200,000 global employees, only eight are given out annually. Ron has the distinction to be the first person from any creative department within Carlson to win this award.

  • Vice President, Associate Creative Director

    Lindsay Stone + Briggs Advertising

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1989 — 1998 (9 years )

    Beyond partnering, Ron's consistent goal was to be a co-conspirator to clients. Every project, campaign or initiative, regardless of size or scope was an opportunity to explore and create compelling, story-telling communications that consumers and customers fall in love with, act upon, and talk about.

    Ron was an integral part of strategic planning, art direction, design, production and presentation of print and broadcast advertising, collateral and sales promotion materials, and new product development.

    Ron received numerous national and international awards for creativity, design and effectiveness.

    Clients: Caradco Windows, Norlight, First United Methodist Church, Humane Society, Kimberly Clark, Klarbrunn, Oscar Mayer Foods, Research Products, Unity Health Plans, SieMatic Kitchens, Steven's Point Brewery, UW Hospital & Clinics, Valley Bank, Weyerhaeuser
