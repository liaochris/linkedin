Dan Rasay’s Summary

Dan is a technologist with 15 years of experience with companies ranging from interactive agencies (ISITE Design), management consulting (Stockamp & Associates), software (Supertracks, Infinity Softworks), direct to consumer (The Fruit Company) and multi-channel retailer (lucy activewear).

As an active advocate for education and the arts he currently serves on the Board of Directors @ Snowdays Foundation and Friends of the Oregon Symphony.
