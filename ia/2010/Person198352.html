Suzi Edwards’s Summary

Strategic Recruitment Manager/People Lead/Change Lead with a special interest in diversity.

I have worked for ThoughtWorks since 2002, firstly in the UK, then Australia, Canada and Brazil and now Chicago. Some might say I am institutionalized.

A recruiting SME by trade, moved into a more generalist People Lead role for ThoughtWorks Calgary in 2008, also taking responsibility for resource management and people support. While this was my day job, I also helped with our expansion into Brazil. Turns out I can work out if there's enough bandwidth to set up a development centre, hire a dozen people and set up a benefits package in about a month.

I recently moved to Chicago to lead a strategic change programme in the people space.

I'm still a hands on recruiter with lots of experience assessing technical and cultural fit across all sorts of roles. I am comfortable working at management and board level to create strategic recruiting plans that fit with overall strategic corporate objectives. I work a lot around encouraging women to join and stay in the IT industry, and am also passionate about other diversity dimensions. I have also been involved in both recruitment and corporate marketing.

I like to think I write pretty well, but the above doesn't really show that.

My aim is quite simple: do interesting and challenging work and be inspired by the people around me.
