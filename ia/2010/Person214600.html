Wendy Corry’s Summary

My lifelong passions have always been traveling abroad and immersing myself in foreign cultures and the people of those cultures. I have been fortunate to have my passion be the focus of my career. Working as a Public Relations expert for various global companies, I’ve gained my experience firsthand, in many regions of the world. This combination of experience and passion has naturally led me to Cross-Cultural Advisory and Coaching.

The uniqueness of my International Public Relations, Communications, Marketing and Individual Cultural Coaching Services combine to form a truly rare and beneficial commodity for companies doing business overseas.
