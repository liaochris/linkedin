Kristi Smith, MBA kristismith.HR@gmail.com’s Summary

I love the feeling of helping others (and if I can earn a living along the way - even better). I firmly believe that every small to medium size business should take a look at this service to see if it may be right for you.

Oasis Outsourcing was founded by The Wackenhut Corporation and is the nations largest privately held PEO. Oasis’ program allows employers to focus on their core business, reduce costs, become an employer of choice, and maintain peace of mind through a comprehensive human resources outsourcing solution.

Some of the key areas are:

• Control Workers' Compensation Costs: Coverage under A+ rated Zurich.
• Affordable Health Insurance Plans: Multiple plans at Oasis' preferred group rates.
• Payroll: Eliminate the cost, liability and burden of processing payroll.
• Unemployment Taxes: Reduce cost & liability for unemployment claims.
• 401(k) and Other Employee Benefits Plans with no set up or maintenance costs.
• Human Resources and Compliance Department: Reduce exposure to liabilities.
• Protection from Employment Liabilities: Coverage under Oasis' EPLI policy.
