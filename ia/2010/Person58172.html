Don James, CPA/PFS, CFP’s Summary

I provide tax and wealth advisory services for high net worth individuals, closely held corporations and partnerships, family groups, community associations, and corporate executives in need of complex financial & multi-generational planning. I enjoy participating in the qualitative aspects as well as the quantitative aspects of the planning process.

Niche Business Segments:

COLLEGE CONSULTING – I help parents with a college-bound high school student, who won’t qualify for need-based financial aid, reduce their college expenses by thousands of dollars. In many cases our customized planning results in a family knocking off a full year’s cost of college.

ESTATE & TRUST RETURN PREPARATION – I work with several probate attorneys providing the tax compliance aspects of winding up an estate, post-death planning strategies, and on-going trust return preparation.

ESTATE & TRUST PLANNING– I assist clients and their estate attorneys in all aspects of developing and managing a personalized estate plan designed to ensure the continued growth and preservation of their family's hard earned assets. We are then involved on an on-going basis assisting with the financial management and tax compliance for Family Limited Partnerships and various Trusts making sure there are no missteps leading to adverse tax consequences.

RETIREMENT PROJECTIONS & PLANNING – Our Retirement Planning Projection Tool provides the detailed strategic planning you need to be able to rely on consistent and effective guidance that will offer the best solutions to connect you with your financial goals. It answers the questions “How much do I need?” and "How much can I spend?" It is a “what-if” tool for ongoing financial decision making. It also serves as a benchmarking tool for annually monitoring the growth of your investment net worth providing, if needed, the details needed to determine where things have gone awry.
