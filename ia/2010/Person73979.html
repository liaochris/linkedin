Genevieve Bos’s Summary

Co-Founder and Publisher of PINK magazine - launched in June 2005. Think of the editorial as "Fortune meets Oprah". Over 15 years in the software and technology industry. One of the founders of ZapMedia - an Atlanta based technology company.

On the Atlanta EO board - eonetwork.org; WITI board Atlanta - Women in Technology International, BDN - Board of Directors Network - whose goal is to get more women on public company boards; Advisory Board member of PADV, Partnership Against Domestic Violence.
