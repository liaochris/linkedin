Dave Haas’s Summary

I am currently on sabbatical. Among the many plans I have for my time off: biking, kayaking, hiking, traveling, yoga, playing music, reading and writing.

Prior to the start of my sabbatical, I worked at PopCap Games. During my 6+ year tenure at PopCap, I held a number of senior executive roles, including:

+ EVP Worldwide Game Studios, heading up the company's North American and European game development studios.

+ Chief Operating Officer, during which time I variously headed up PopCap’s mobile games studio, Localization, IT, Facilities, PopCap.com and Customer Service departments.

+ Chairman of the Board of PopCap Games International, the company's first international subsidiary, based in Dublin, Ireland.

Previous to PopCap, I served as Technology Archon for Sabertooth Games, where I managed IT and network operations. Prior to that I was founder and CTO of Seer Logistics, a logistics and technology startup. I've also held senior IT management positions at BootlegTV/BootlegNetworks and at Definitive Stock/Visual Communications Group. I was also the owner and founder of ICS Consulting, a computer and network consulting firm.
