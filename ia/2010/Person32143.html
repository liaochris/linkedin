Cari Draft’s Summary

EcoTrek Fitness is group workouts, all outdoors, in all kinds of weather, in a different outdoor location every time! (It never gets boring!) The sessions integrate strength training, cardio, balance & flexibility - and every session is different.

Pre-paid sessions can be used between all NINE West Michigan Series -- Lakeshore area, Coopersville area, North Muskegon area, Holland area, SW Grand Rapids, NE Grand Rapids, SE Grand Rapids, North Kent County Series and the Portland, Michigan Series opened in May. Also in May we opened our first out-of-Michigan series in Morgantown, West Virginia!

EcoTrek Fitness is growing across Michigan - check out www.ecotrekfitness.com for details on a Series near you!

Cari Draft’s Specialties:

Being an ACE-certified Personal Fitness Trainer, I've studied practical training, applied sciences, client assessment, program design, implementation & adjustment. ACE is the American Council on Exercise; ACE's programs have been accredited by the National Commission for Certifying Agencies (NCCA) and ACE trainers are known in the fitness industry as the best-of-the-best!
