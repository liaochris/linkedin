Sandy Spadaro’s Summary

I'm an author and speaker on business & marketing communication and professional growth with a passion for my niche area of stealth communication habit building for women. As a business owner of a marketing and public relations boutique firm, SS Marketing Solutions, I teach and utilize marketing & communication skills-sets to build relationships and power my upcoming projects. Published works include regional and national print & online publications while I continue to enjoy demand for my first international book project, “Overcoming the Superwoman Syndrome” (which has just been translated into Spanish and Polish). My second project "The Women's Handbook for Empowerment" has just been released and I'm currently seeking a publisher for the next professional women's communication resource, "Assertive Doesn't Mean Bitchy".

Sandy Spadaro’s Specialties:

Niche target markets of building & real estate comprise many of my SS Marketing Solutions client connections, while the much larger area of Women In Business and Women's Communication are specialty topics that I actively speak and write about.
