Edna Rendon’s Summary

Industrial Psychologist with a successful career in specialized recruiting for the manufacturing industry in Mexico and the United States. Supported the start up of operations for several companies, establishing procedures, creating manuals with guidelines on recruiting methods and techniques, defining job profiles, creating and implementing technical and psychometric evaluations for aspiring candidates, collaborating in salary and compensation surveys. Mentor to many up and coming Recruiters. Excellent relationship with people at all levels of the organization, embracing a profound sense of commitment and responsibility to both client and candidate, accustomed to work upon established goals and highly demanding standards of Quality and Excellence.
