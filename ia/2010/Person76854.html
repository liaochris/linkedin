Ryan Steinolfson’s Summary

I am a internet marketing and real estate expert specializing in social media marketing, online video marketing, and online business centralization and automation.

Mission Statement- We Accelerate Our Client's Brand Recognition: Maximizing the Potential for Increased Sales and Exponential Growth!

Vision- Our vision is to assist 1,000 organizations increase their sales within the next 5 years. We will constantly strive to increase our clients potential for increased sales and growth by providing our clients with best of class online marketing tools, new ways to create automated online income streams, and by teaching our clients how to centralize and automate their online business.

Company Values: We will treat each employee, stakeholder, partner and client with Fairness, Honor and Integrity.

I own and manage 25 web sites that produce a positive cash flow. Through the management of these sites I have learned how to effectively use article marketing, affiliate marketing, social bookmarking, press releases, social media marketing, online video marketing and pay per click advertising etc. I have had success with my web sites because of the effective integration of all of the above mentioned techniques.

I am currently joint venturing with a partner on 5 web sites that were purchased through my retirement account. Each of these 5 web sites produces a return on the initial investment of between 20% to 80% per year. What that means is that if I invest $10,000 in a web site I get about about $2,000 to $8,000 of my money back from that investment in one year. Now that is leverage!

My goal is to joint venture with 10 business owners by the end of 2010 and to implement what I have learned about internet marketing in their business so that they can increase their sales and decrease their expenses and spend more time with their families.
