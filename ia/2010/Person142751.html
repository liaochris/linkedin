Michael Ivanovich’s Summary

After having been a chief editor since 1996, most recently with Consulting-Specifying Engineer and earlier with HPAC Engineering, I've decided to start The Ivanovich Group, LLC, to seek new challenges, develop new solutions, and work with a wider variety of professionals in the buildings industry.

Before having a career in publishing, I was a research scientist in the fields of building science and computer technology working for Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, Minnesota Department of Health, Camroden Associates, and NOAA Aeronomy Laboratory (through Univ. of Colororado at Boulder).

I am married to a warm and talented professor of Education, Dr. Amy Smith, and we have a dog, Bogart, who keeps the house from ever getting too serious.

Communications and business development strategies; context and content development; networking; event development; custom media; B2B media.
