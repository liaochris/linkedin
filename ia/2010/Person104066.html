Jonathan D. Sherrill’s Summary

Avid sportsman with core competencies in business development, marketing, and sales processes specifically concerning the development and enhancement of field sport venues and specialty real estate. A born entrepreneur, he co-founded, with Dennis Doyle, Sporting Heritage Group in 2006 and the two collectively developed a partnership with Beretta.

Jonathan D. Sherrill’s Specialties:

Quality assurance; research & development; marketing; branding and identity; sales; sales management; hospitality; customer relationship management; work flow management; Six Sigma process development; business development; technology solutions; feasibility studies; club structures; recreational real estate; and mergers & acquisitions
