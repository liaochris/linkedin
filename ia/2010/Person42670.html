Michael Collins’s Summary

Michael Collins is an innovative, hands-on business leader specializing in moving people – clients, prospects, partners and employees – to action. Whether it’s launching a new product or service, creating lead generation programs, updating a brand, enabling sales teams or taking a company public, his philosophy is simple: “Marketing should build sales overnight and brand over time.”

Michael develops and executes award-winning marketing campaigns that have delivered profitable sales growth for companies with revenue ranging from $20 million to nearly $2 billion. He has created marketing programs to bring technology and service solutions to consumers, SMB and enterprise markets through both direct and indirect sales channels. Supporting sales teams with sales tools and lead generation programs has been a key part of his success.

As Chief Marketing Officer and SVP Sales/Alliances for the technology-enabled services division of wealth management firm Rockefeller & Company, Michael launched the Rockit Solutions brand and the firm’s first integrated marketing, product management and sales teams.

Prior to Rockit, he ran worldwide marketing at Iomega Corporation where the company transformed from a sluggish business-to-business technology supplier to a revolutionary new consumer brand. As VP of Corporate Marketing at SAGA Software, Inc. Michael helped reposition the brand and take the company public. At Mercator Software, as part of the company’s turnaround strategy he and his team repositioned the company around industry solutions and developed an indirect sales channel. And as CMO for Gevity HR, he was a key leader driving the transformation of the company’s business model and brand.
