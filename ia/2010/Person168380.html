Ralph Pombo’s Summary

After being raised on a farm/ranch in the California Central Valley, Ralph Pombo began his career as a Mechanical Technician at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory working on several Strategic Defense Initiative ( "Star Wars" ) projects during the 1980's. He assisted in the assembly, and operated, history's largest and most powerful particle beam accelerator, known as ATA (Advanced Test Accelerator). In the 1990's, Ralph was a key player in the design and development of what are now silicon chip manufacturing processes. He is published both as an author and a coauthor of many publications detailing physical vapor deposition used in the manufacturing of xRay mirrors. Nearing the end of the 90's decade, he earned a Computer Science degree from Cal State Hayward (East Bay). After 24 years of wide ranging experiences, Ralph is now running his own software firm. Sticky Web Productions(SWP) develops LAMP process web based applications and web development. His specialties include Joomla websites, Joomla extensions, PHP, mySql, Ajax, and Javascript based projects.
