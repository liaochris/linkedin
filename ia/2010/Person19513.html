Bruce Kaalund’s Summary

I am responsible for leading a team of cyber security professionals that evaluates voice, data, and video products and product services to ensure they are robust and resiliant to internal and external threats, while balancing security needs with business requirements, ensuring that timelines are met. My team is responsible for aggressively advocating for security methods and systems needed to protect content, data, new products and services, with a growing focus on application security. We also represent Comcast at industry meetings focused on cyber security.
