Tom Williams’s Summary

I founded InnoGage, LLC after sensing a tectonic shift in traditional marketing with the onset of Web2.0 and created InnoGage to address that shift. The core of InnoGage is to help companies understand and effectively utilize Social Media for Search (SEO) marketing, branding and internal communication flow.

InnoBlogs: Business Class Blogging is quite simply the Best Blogging Software for Business. InnoBlogs optimizes online search and drives increased web traffic to your site. We take all the headaches out of running a blog program and our SEO optimized software and processes deliver a powerful ROI.

InnoComm is our unique internal communications optimizer application. If your Intranet is the "library", InnoComm is the "newspaper + magazine + blog". Built on top of our InnoBlogs technology, InnoComm is a closed information network for your employees to share and obtain critical information. We have found the areas of customer service call centers and sales to be the most active users of this technology.

Social Media Footprint. Have no idea where your company or brand stands in the social media sphere? Maybe you should find out! Let us run our intensive diagnostic Social Media Footprint analysis to tell you where you are (and aren't) AND where your competitors are and their weaknesses. Social Media takes effort. Get your information up front for decision making!

Social Media Consulting. In the Social Media world, you know where you are and where you're not...but where SHOULD you be? Our systematic consulting approach starts with your strategy and your goals. Using our knowledge of industries, competition and trends, we put together a comprehensive social media strategy which can be executed and results measured.
