Alon Toker’s Summary

I established Mega Builders in 1988 and incorporated it in 1991. The company completed our first high-end custom home in 1991 which went on to win awards and was featured in the Los Angeles Times Home Tour of that year. Our work was featured in local and national media multiple times, and I was the 'resident construction expert' for the Los Angeles Times Remodeling Blog from 2006-2008.

I am proud of my company's inclusion in Remodeling Magazine's Big-50 list (highlighting the top remodeling companies in the nation) and in the Power 500 list (to name but a few). I also wrote the cover story for the Journal of Light Construction's (JLC) December 2008 issue ("Adding to an Existing Slab Foundation").

I have concern for the environment, and have a 'Certified Green Professional' (CGP) designation from the NAHB (National Association of Home Builders) as well as a Solar Photovoltaic Installation certificate.

I am also an expert in dealing with insurance companies with regards to insurance losses (water, fire, etc.) and have helped secure settlements of claims for the full value of the loss for hundreds of losses in the past two decades.
