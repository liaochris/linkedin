John Tomasso’s Summary

Senior level Attorney and CPA with diverse in-house legal and corporate tax experience, including nine years as General Counsel providing legal and business counsel to the executive team and the operational units for the principal multi-billion dollar subsidiary of the largest distributor of electrical and datacom products in the U.S.

John Tomasso’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include: mergers and acquisitions, contracts transactional matters and commercial leases, human resources and employment matters, litigation management, corporate governance, and corporate tax and ERISA benefit plan reporting. Earlier career includes Big 4 Tax Manager and tax department experience.
