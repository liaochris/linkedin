Joyce Weston’s Experience

  • Sole Proprietor

    Joyce C Weston Design

    (Graphic Design industry)

    October 1984 — Present (26 years 1 month)

    I have been a freelance book designer since 1984. My clients include major book publishers such as Houghton Mifflin Company, Pearson Education, and MacMillan. I also design books for many university presses and small independent publishers.
    Just recently a book I designed, Facing Death by Linda Watson, was awarded the Independent Book Publishers Association Benjamin Franklin Award for best gift book.This book has also been awarded 2009 National Best Books award in the category Spirituality: Journals & Quotes.
    This is just one of many awards I've gotten for my work over the years. Please take a look at my portfolio on my website.

  • Book Designer

    Various Manhattan Publishers

    (Publishing industry)

    1972 — 1981 (9 years )

    After moving to NYC to become a famous painter, I found a job to pay the rent at a small — and wonderful — publishing company, The Two Continents Publishing Company. There I discovered something that fit my skills well: book design. The rest is history! I studied book design under some of the best tutors, moved on to magazines for a while, and then returned to books as a freelance designer.
