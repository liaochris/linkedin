Marie-Alice Dibon’s Summary

Pharmacist with an extensive experience in cosmetic, OTC, medical devices product development, manufacturing and controls, and as a second life, innovation sourcing, scientific communication, network building and business development for the lifescience industries (biotech, pharma and cosmetics).
My focus is on the lifescience industry with a particular interest in innovative technologies, biotechs start-ups and their specific needs in business development as well as the potential for business development for these companies in the consumer products arena.
Also very interested in working on the future of the industry and its impact on society through networks and public policy.

Scientific communication for marketiing and internal communications purposes
