Jeff Franson’s Summary

I started Window Film Depot (WFD) after graduating from Boston College with honors. Since then, my company has tackled some of the most impressive and difficult architectural film installations ever completed in every US state and beyond.
We are experts at listening and then recommending the right applied film solution for buildings and storefronts and delivering on promises made.
Our project management capabilities are truly unique in our indistry. With offices in major markets across the US, we rely on our well trained employees and skilled installers to service our clients installation needs.
Our value proposition for our clients is simple: Why pay the third party mark-up from a 'facilitator' or trust a 'network of subs' when you can source a turnkey solution directly with WFD?
When you want the job done right and at a fair price, call the experienced pro's from WFD. We don't dissapoint.
Our goal is to be the only architectural film installation company you ever need - or want - to call.
