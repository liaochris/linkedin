Matt Gist’s Summary

I am an Experience and Interaction Designer who thrives on figuring out purpose, workflow, layout and the creation of the best web-experiences meshing business objectives with accomplishing user goals.

The goal is to make the internet make sense, and that's what Matt does. There are many ways to create solutions, he promotes exploring new techniques, having a plan, experimentation with process and maintaining an ability to remain versatile - only good things can come of it.

As a kid he liked drawing comic books. In high school he enjoyed art, creative writing and painting. During college he grew to love psychology, anthropology and graphic design. Now he's a UI/UE Designer - It all makes sense right?

Matt Gist’s Specialties:

Valid HTML/CSS, Adobe Creative Suite, Web-Site Usability Design, Rich Internet Applications. Thinking first, and not talking too fast.
