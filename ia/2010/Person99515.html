J. Matthew Becker, M.Ed., ACC’s Summary

I have worked in the field of Human Resource Development for 10 years, I am an Associate Certified Coach through the International Coaching Federation, and own a career and life coaching business, Authentic Excellence LLC. I am passionate about helping clients clarify their values and natural strengths in order to design a fulfilling career and/or life. It is my ability to listen intently, ask powerful questions, and see the possibilities others may not have thought of that makes me a successful coach. Are you ready to design the authentic life you have always dreamed about? If you are, then contact me today and let the journey begin!
