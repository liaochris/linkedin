Christopher Harris’s Summary

General Overseer Christopher J. Harris, I, a native of Palatka, FL, currently lives in Greensboro, NC with his wife, Carmen J. Harris and children, Christina, Christopher, II, & Christian Jonathan. Overseer Harris is currently serving as Pastor of Youth & College Ministries @ Cedar Grove Tabernacle of Praise of Greensboro, where Dr. James A. Johnson is Senior Pastor. Further, Overseer Harris is also currently serving as International General Overseer of the Children & Youth Division for Full Gospel Baptist Church Fellowship International, under the leadership of Presiding Bishop Paul S. Morton. He is also the Sr. Partner of the Inspired Ideas Groups

Overseer Harris is an alumnus of The Florida State University and the University of South Carolina-Columbia holding a Bachelor of Science and Master of Education. He is currently completing requirements on his Doctorate in Education at Nova Southeastern University. Previously, he has served and been a member of New Ebenezer BC (SC), Mt. Tabor 1st BC (FL), and Calvary BC (FL).

He is an initiate of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., of which in 2000, he was selected as National Brother of the Year. He currently serves on the Board of Directors for Win-Win Resolutions, serves as a Chaplain with Guilford County Juvenile Center, and as one of the leaders of the Triad Youth Leaders Network.

In 2000, Overseer Harris was inducted into the FSU Hall of Fame, and has received over 400 other awards, honors, and special recognitions. Harris receives as his standard of excellence the Apostle Paul’s instruction to Timothy (1 Tim. 4: 12), “...be an example in speech, in life, in love, in faith, and in purity”.

Christopher Harris’s Specialties:

Although in full-time ministry, He has served as a bi-vocational minister. Harris has previously served as Site Dir. for Communities In Schools of High Point, @ TW Andrews High and also as a Student Affairs Officer & Administrator in higher education on multiple college campuses.

Specialties:
Teaching, Preaching, Administration, Ministry Consulting, Event Planning, Ministry Training, Educational Consulting, Educational Training
