David Girolami’s Summary

I have been involved in the digital media and design technology industries since 1995.

Recent experience includes working at digital marketing and advertising companies such as TBWA Toronto and BensimonByrne (Mighty Digital) as producer and account director. Development director for FITC Design and Technology Events. Founder, producer, and curator for TDAA/Pixel Gallery overseeing 15+ technology art exhibits over 8 years.

In addition, I was a part-time Faculty instructor for 2 years at the Art institute of Toronto instructing the finer issues of Law in the Graphic Arts and Professional Practices.

An active advocate and advisor on development across all digital channels to advance the creative and business capacities and business goals for individuals and organizations alike.
