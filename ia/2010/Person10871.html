Andrea Applegate’s Summary

The Columbus Chamber leads and supports economic growth and development in the Columbus Region. Through the Chamber's Attract & Retain Talent initiative, local employers are assured access to a workforce that meets their needs now and in the future.

-> ColumbusInternships.com helps link local employers to college students seeking internships in the Columbus Region.

At ColumbusInternships.com, employers can find information on best practices for internships, links to funding sources for wage subsidies, and one-on-one assistance to help figure out how internships can be a meaningful part of their business strategy.

When college students understand the employment opportunities that are available to them, they will be more likely to stay in the Region after graduation.

-> The Chamber's suite of "Recruiter Solutions," including liveworkplayColumbus.com assists employers when they are recruiting candidates from outside the Columbus Region.

Employers have no trouble selling the position or the company, but may find it challenging to convince a prospect (and trailing spouse/family) to move to Columbus.

By being able to investigate Columbus objectively and on their own terms, liveworkplayColumbus.com helps prospective employees better understand how they can replicate, if not advance, their current lifestyle with a move to Columbus.

-> Young Professionals are integral to the competitiveness of the Columbus Region. Because the population of the generation following the Baby Boomers is significantly smaller, it is imperative that the Columbus community actively attract and retain younger workers to meet the needs of local employers.
