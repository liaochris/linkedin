Kate Hobbie’s Summary

Dedicated, passionate, and reliable online media and marketing specialist with over 20 years experience in sales and marketing. In depth knowledge of social media, lead generation programs and web publishing.

Industry/vertical areas of expertise include: technology hardware and software, computer and video games, as well as experience marketing specific technology topics such as web 2.0, SOA, virtualization, SaaS and cloud computing. Also experienced in working with small business and local markets.

Strong copy writing skills from ten years working in the direct marketing industry, capable and organized campaign manager with financial skills that include: business modeling, budgeting and forecasting.

Created some of the first advertising driven podcast programs in 2005, developed white label social networks, and produced nearly 100 webcasts. Possess both creative and analytical skills to help clients understand how their business is performing and where it should be going from a marketing perspective.

Will develop strategies to drive leads, improve a client’s brand messaging and increase traffic to a product or service’s web site.

Kate Hobbie’s Specialties:

* Integrated print, live events and online media plans - direct, plan, negotiate and analyze results
* Web publishing – increase traffic and improve navigation
* Brand messaging and positioning – develop creative, consistent and tightly focused messaging
* Sales development strategy – create collateral and sales support systems

Tireless, energetic and committed to driving results to help businesses overcome obstacles and achieve success!
