John Jacobsen’s Summary

Feature film director with an expertise in working on new material and with actors. Director and host of the TV show, Artist Toolbox. Strong theatre background, as well as commercial background. Has worked on Broadway and off, in regional theatre, and now predominately in Los Angeles and Seattle writing and/or rewriting feature films. Screenwriter and published author. Leading acting teacher and writing coach. Founding partner with Tom Skeritt of TheFilmSchool, one of this country's best places to come learn story. Former Vice President of the ACT Theatre, one of this country's best regional theatres, current Trustee.

John Jacobsen’s Specialties:

Directing film and theatre. Writing for film. Working with actors, writers, and directors. Producing. Negotiating. Teaching. Administering school programs and courses.
