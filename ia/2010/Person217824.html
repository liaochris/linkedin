Ellen Thomas’s Summary

My name is Ellen Thomas and I’ve recently moved back to the US (Southern Pines, NC) after three years living in Europe.

My undergraduate degree is from Ithaca College, where I majored in Spanish and minored in French, Russian and Italian. I attended graduate school at the University of Georgia and earned my MBA in International Marketing and am also a Certified Coach Practitioner. Earlier in my career I worked mainly in sports marketing, involved with projects ranging from Olympics to motor sports.

I have had many experiences that have allowed me to see both sides of marketing, on the company side and the agency side, as well as many facets of HR. As president of my own company for several years, I have conducted business internationally in the United States, Canada, Japan, the United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand and Germany.

My expertise has expanded into the field of transformational coaching where I work with clients to help them assess how to get from where they are currently to where they would like to be. After the analysis, I develop a personalized roadmap to help them transform their lives. All of my experiences and education have led me to develop unique systems and tools using synchronicity, The Law of Attraction, principals of marketing and more, which allow my clients to effortlessly make the transformation from their present state to a happy, healthy and successful future. Over the past few years I have developed and taught several teleclasses and seminars including the popular Infinite Warm Market and Cold Market classes as well as coached individuals and groups.
