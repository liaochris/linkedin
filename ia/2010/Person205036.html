Todd Meador’s Summary

One Pulse Artist Group was founded in 2010 in Austin Texas. Our goal is to help artists develop long-term careers in a manner that is consistent with their creative philosophy and vision. We value our relationships with the artists and their business associates and consider our creative workload as one body working towards a common goal, with one heartbeat. Our desire to maintain a forward thinking approach in an ever changing industry will allow our artists the tools and connections they need to progress.
With a background of 20 years in the music industry in performance, production, booking and management, our leadership team is equipped with the knowledge and connections to help our artists grow in their craft.
One Pulse AG is currently representing Miranda Dodson, Jason Poe, Jordan Whitmore, Brittany Shane, Ten Thousand Years, The Romeria Lights, Renter & Apodiform.
__________________________________________________

One Pulse Music & Sound Design believes in the power of original music. It is with this intention that we strive to create the most engaging, most memorable soundtrack for your project. Whether it's setting the tone for a feature film or drawing people to your brand, One Pulse Music is there to work with you on attaining your vision. Our eclectic group of producers and composers work together to keep your project's finger on the pulse.
One Pulse MSD's past clients include Heineken, McDonalds, Showtime, Navy, Chevrolet & Cadillac.

One Pulse Design Group is currently designing for all of the One Pulse Artists, One Pulse Music, Music For The City, RISE Austin, Metropolitan Gallery, Lucy Skyrocket, CommuniCard, Harlan Realty, The Law Office of Abigail Klamert, Covera, Red Wheelbarrow, My Healthy Life, Keep Moving Austin & Amazing Auto.
