Mary-Beth Cooper’s Summary

After 20+ years in the medical staffing and executive recruiting industry, I took my networks seriously and branched out to utilize my talents in project management, event production and team cultivation in community and large scale projects. I have worked and volunteered in many projects ranging from local to federal campaigns, world wide winter games for Special Olympics, Ironman Event production, to a new traditional law school in Boise, Idaho.

Previous to these endeavors, I have recruited over 55 medical providers to the Treasure Valley area in Idaho, held a management position for a professional staff of 75 medical providers in an acute care hospital for 6 years while volunteering in my community for various projects in the entertainment industry.
