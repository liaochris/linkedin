Michelle Clarke’s Summary

Based in Cape Town, South Africa, Michelle Clarke is an executive and business coach who works with already-successful, high-achieving individuals, coaching her clients to increase their value proposition – not only professionally, but also holistically. Trained as an Integral Coach with The Coaching Centre in South Africa, Michelle also holds a Master Coach Certification with The Behavioral Coaching Institute of Europe. She is also a certified Tele-Class Leader (USA) and trained in the Nancy Kline Time To Think™ Methodology and the Action Learning Methodology (AWOL). Michelle also oversees the training of business coaches in South Africa through an alliance with the South African College of Applied Psychology and gives numerous public workshops and Master Classes on Personal Branding and Personal Marketing.

An entrepreneur by passion, Michelle started her career in the IT industry, as a sales engineer, later a brand manager, sales manager and branch executive. In her late-twenties, she founded, grew, managed and later sold her own IT business before migrating into the corporate arena to head up the entrepreneurial department of a multi-national, where she held a seat on the divisional board. She founded Michelle Clarke Coaching in November 2005 and secured her first multi-national coaching contract in the same year.

Born of British and Dutch heritage, Michelle is a first generation South African. She has travelled extensively in the USA as a former conference guest speaker. She travelled to the east to write a book and then went on to the UK to market it to publishers. She works in alliance with an Indian-based executive coach to provide laser-coaching sessions to middle managers in Fortune 500 companies in New Delhi and Chennai. Her local client base is wonderfully diverse, including Zulu, Tswana and Xhosa nationals as well as clients from DRC, Angola and Egypt.

Michelle Clarke’s Specialties:

Michelle has an affiliation for coaching people who are already performing to a high level. The core content of her coaching satisfies the self-assured high-achievers developmental and feedback needs. Her expertise lies in the positive reinforcement of skills, knowledge and competencies, while still focusing on integral personal growth.
