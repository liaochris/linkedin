Liz Link’s Summary

As Marketing Manager for TicketCity, I play an integral role in developing and distributing communications both internally through company initiatives and externally through various marketing channels.

From researching, negotiating and placing media buys to designing the creative for all print, email and banner campaigns, I also organize events such as the TC Golf Invitational, UT tailgates and TicketCity participation in the Texas Gridiron Kickoff.
