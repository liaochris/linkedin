Theo van den Berg’s Summary

I am currently part of MIH's (Naspers) M&A team for the Africa and Middle East region, where I participate in deal making from conception through to deal closure. I assist in the identification, due diligence, financial modeling, valuation and execution of business development and M&A related opportunities for the company. Furthermore, I prepare strategic analyses and recommendations to help develop long-term divisional strategy and evaluate new business initiatives.

My previous experience includes managing the development and growth of MKX, an enterprise social network for the Naspers group and performing industry research on the Internet and mobile space at SWAT (a global think tank and execution arm of the Internet division within Naspers).

After finishing my MBA, I consulted to a media and entertainment company, doing business research, writing a business plan, financial modeling and strategy formulation for their online venture looking to launch in the BRIC countries. Reporting directly to the two co-CEOs, I also delivered high-level reports and recommendations for use in the business plan and strategy document.

I also have three years experience in the semiconductor industry, working in Project, Pre-Sales and Junior Design Engineer roles. This included coordinating and managing projects from the project brief stage to the final production of the product, as well as liaising between clients and design engineers to ensure the successful development of products.
