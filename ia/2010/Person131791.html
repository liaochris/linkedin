Marco Valerio Abbati’s Summary

I've completed an intense and interesting, professional experience in corporate travel industry. I have had the opportunity and luck of growing and holding managing positions, being able to manage business relations with reference suppliers, studying and looking after the needs of some of the main corporate clients like the European Space Agency, where I covered the role of account manager for Italy, acquiring skills, service standards and procedures of Travel Management Companies’ partners at European level.

After a short period, still very young, during which I covered the role of “country manager” for FCm Travel Solutions, I came back to Italy with a perspective and knowledge of the European business travel market much richer and more in-depth.

During the year 2007 I started the project under the brand name VacanzeGay.it : tour operator fully dedicated to LGBT people and luxury segment

In 2009 I've also replaced the role of business development manager at one of the best online travel company in Italy.
