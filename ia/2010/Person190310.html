Sissy Siero’s Summary

After returning from a national tour with Joan Osborne, Sissy Siero established a full service audio company offering corporate & media voice over/narration.
By delivering consistent, quality narration with speed and accuracy, and being highly responsive to project timelines - all while offering competitive rates - Sissy Siero’s unique audio company is a standout.
The ability to be flexible to the evolving needs of the industry has made our services indispensable to clients in the prestigious fortune 1000.

What we do like no one else :
We can do as much or as little as our client’s important projects dictate.
Some of our most requested services include:
* Casting the perfect voice talent- we’ll offer the best, yet affordable talent
to brand your corporate and media audio with the mark of professionalism
your company deserves.
* Session direction
* Post production - editing, sound design, music composition
and remote recording
* In 2009 we branched out again to offer our clients superior copy writing,
translation services, and design.
Our goal is to become the 'go-to' company for all of our client's audio projects.

On the artistic front Sissy has finished her first feature length screen play - "Across the Divide", and is now working on book exploring the theme of personal empowerment from a down to earth, and very humorous perspective. Sissy is also writing and producing a cutting edge song catalog targeting the commercial licensing market with online partner Sir Groovy.

With collaborator and producer Hilary Six, Six Sense productions, Los Angeles. Sissy co-wrote a six episode webseries entitied "Recovering Religiholics" - explores the funny side of self-help, compulsions and religion. As an actor, Sissy has also been cast as a guest star on "Friday Night Lights", has a national commercial for Merry Maids on network TV, and wrapped on her first feature film lead in the indie "Anything for Now", due for release in spring of 2010.

Sissy Siero’s Specialties:

Distinctive, accurate, consistent voice acting; full audio services; translation; competitive pricing.
Dynamic vocal range and versatility as a writer and performer.
