Chris Lowe’s Summary

A successful and innovative recruiter specializing in the creative and marketing categories as well as start-up environments. Over 10 years experience in finding not just people with the right skills but also the right personality for a given organization. I work proactively to help companies define how they want to be perceived among potential employees and combine recruiting with marketing to attract the most dynamic and innovative candidates.

Chris Lowe’s Specialties:

Recruiting top level talent for a wide range of roles. OK, every recruiter says that but when was the last time you talked to a recruiter who's hired sherpas? Extensive full-life cycle staffing experience including reviewing portfolios/resumes, passive sourcing, workforce planning, salary/rate negotiations, reference checks, extending offers, and on-boarding new hires. Additional experience in managing internship programs, vendor management, and new business development.
