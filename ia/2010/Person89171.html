Janelle Weeden’s Summary

Catering to your senses, Janelle Weeden of The Flour Shop, specializes in creating spectacular custom three-dimensional cakes and elaborate wedding cakes for life's most memorable occasions. With an artist's flair and the freshest ingredients, Janelle transforms your ideas and dreams into reality.

A lifelong artist, Janelle's passion for creativity is well ensconced in everything from the traditional to the most abstract. With more than 10 years of experience in cake design, Janelle has done it all… weddings, 50th anniversaries, 40th birthdays, baby showers, and corporate events. With an emphasis on three-dimensions, Janelle has created many fun cakes like laptops, cell phones, beer bottles, topsy-turvy whimsical cakes to realistic life-size edible guitars. Even her most elaborate and traditional wedding cakes create a picture-perfect moment, prior to being devoured by hungry guests.

Get to know Janelle one-to-one by becoming a fan on her official Facebook page… http://www.facebook.com/TheFlourShop. There you will find many more photos, blogs, conversations and more! You can follow her at http://www.twitter.com/TheFlourShop as well.

Looking for more photos? Check out Janelle's blog at http://TheFlourShop.blogspot.com. <-- (Copy-and-paste this address into your browser.)

Although Janelle works out of a commercial kitchen, her hours at the store are by appointment only. Give her a call at (321) 55-SUGAR and she will gladly consult with you to help you have the cake of your dreams. You can message Janelle directly on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/JanelleWeeden or message directly here on LinkedIn.
