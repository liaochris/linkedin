Blair Gibson’s Summary

Twenty years of pharma marketing and strategy-development experience. Have worked in four of the top-ten pharmaceutical companies and have employed (and developed some of) the best practices in just about all elements of brand marketing and strategic planning. There are two key drivers in my business thinking – the customer and innovation. Understanding and getting closer to the customer makes the innovation part a lot easier!
Spent my career on the following three areas of marketing and strategic planning:

New Products Management
• Collaborated across all therapeutic areas to lead the commercial assessment, valuation prioritization and optimization the late-stage portfolio (including in-licensing opportunities) of clinical studies.
• Led the early-commercial development of strategies, forecasts, market analyses and valuation of a variety of specialty and primary-care compounds in the phase IIb and beyond space. (CV, heart failure, thrombosis, diabetes, anti-infectives, oncology)

Product Launches:
• Launched two blockbuster drugs where I led development of the global messaging, value proposition, patient & physician [customer] mapping and the promotion campaign.
• Both products are still leaders their respective therapeutic classes.

Strategic Planning & Portfolio Management:
• Developed innovative techniques in portfolio analysis and strategic planning to offer options to my company's senior management -- options that would not be apparent with normal P&L analysis or market research.
• Combining the financial data and market dynamics in unique metrics to deliver a broad set of tradeoff choices for any investment or spend decision (in R&D & in the in-line portfolio).
• Characterize and clarify risk, growth and return to make the tradeoff as straightforward as possible. My work significantly improves ROI, in some cases by 400%
