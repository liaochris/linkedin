Sarah Lindsay’s Summary

I offer a diverse range of day-to-day public relations support to several Tierney account teams, including Independence Blue Cross, Keystone Foods, Foxwoods Casino Philadelphia, Prudential Fox & Roach, REALTORS’, The Salvation Army of Greater Philadelphia, Sun Life Financial and most recently Bayer Environmental Science.

Working with me, you get a range of skills and expertise that meets at the point of strategic public relations and creativity. My nearly four-year experience at Tierney includes strategic planning, events, data analysis, online communications and crisis and issues management.

In addition to daily support, I'm the go-to guru for all things social media. Whether it's strategic message placement, counsel on various Internet tools, optimizing your press releases or need for out-of-the-box thinking, I understand and utilize new media to enhance a client’s public relations campaign.
