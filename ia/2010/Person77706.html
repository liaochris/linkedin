Gregg Stocker’s Summary

International operations executive working on consulting projects throughout the world in a variety of areas, including lean, strategic planning, leadership development, team building, and continual improvement. A team-oriented executive who achieves results by improving teamwork, focus, and communication throughout the organization.

Previous experience includes:

Director of European Operations for ICO Polymers, Inc., a polymer products and services company (revenues of $170 million+) based in The Netherlands.

Managing Director of ICO Holland, B.V., a business unit of ICO Polymers serving Northern Europe. Position included P&L responsibility for an 85 person operation. Led business to dramatic improvement in profitability and ROIC.

Authored the book, "Avoiding the Corporate Death Spiral: Identifying & Eliminating the Signs of Decline" (Quality Press, 2006). Frequent speaker and recognized expert in business and performance improvement having been interviewed on television, radio, and in a number of newspaper and magazine articles (e.g., The Washington Post, The New York Times, BusinessWeek, and InformationWeek).

Gregg Stocker’s Specialties:

International experience, teambulding, leadership development, succession planning, strategic planning, ERP system development/implementation, lean manufacturing, continual improvement, and quality systems in a variety of industries, including plastics processing, compounding, valve assembly, and instrumentation.
