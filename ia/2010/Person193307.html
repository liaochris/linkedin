Stan Gottfredson’s Experience

  • Principal

    Gott Consulting Services

    (Legal Services industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Gott Consulting Services provides objective analysis, reporting and implementation of internal and external factors related to firm branding and business efficiency. Most firms understand the need to establish an image and practice based on confidence, competence, credibility and trust. We help firms focus first on infrastructure and effecient tracking modes that begin at the point of intake. Our philosophy is best described as an inside out approach.

    We provide modern solutions that enable firms to manage, delegate, refer and categorize all intakes at point of entry in real time. This gives firms complete control and accountability of all leads through the process of determining case relevancy. Once the lead is deemed a case all information including form letters, communication history and medical records can be downloaded seamlessly to your case management system.

  • Executive Director

    Ceatus Media Group LLC

    (Internet industry)

    November 2004 — Present (5 years 6 months)

    Ceatus Media Group is the leading health media company on the Internet providing specialized health information related to ophthalmology, plastic surgery and cosmetic dentistry. Ceatus Media Group is responsible for the educational portal sites AllAboutVision.com, YourPlasticSurgeryGuide.com and YourDentistryGuide.com. Over 10 million consumers a year visit these family of sites looking for information from Lasik and premium IOL’s to breast augmentation to dental veneers. Ceatus Media Group has a reputation of working with the top Board Certified surgeons and Cosmetic Dentists in the country.

    Ceatus Media Group is a leader in all components related to Internet marketing including web site development, Search Engine Optimization, Pay Per Click advertising , call tracking services, competition analysis, social media implementation and website tracking and conversion.
