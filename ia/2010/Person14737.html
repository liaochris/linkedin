Anthony Bontrager’s Experience

  • President

    Onecast, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    1CAST is the world’s first desktop and mobile broadcast news and information service to put users in control of what, when and how they consume video news content. 1CAST’s unique video search and newscast platform delivers near real-time video clips of the latest headlines in business, politics, sports, technology and entertainment. Viewers can also create micro-casts around the topics and people they are most interested in and access these personal channels across any broadband enabled or wireless device. News networks worldwide use 1CAST to reach new audiences, ensure their current audience can engage with their content in a uniquely relevant manner and to further monetize their daily news programming.

    • Led company through initial development and concepting to commercial service launch;
    • Developed company’s business strategy around the industry’s first truly personalized news service distributed across the web, mobile and iTV platforms;
    • Created company’s financial package, including income statements, balance sheets, statement of cash flows, revenue models, depreciation schedules, etc.;
    • Identified and hired executive management team;
    • Conceived the company’s go-to-market strategy covering content acquisition, channel partnerships and advertising sales;
    • Personally responsible for all content partnerships, including deals with NBC, FOX, BBC Worldwide, Bloomberg, Agence France-Presse, Thomson Reuters, E! Entertainment, Style, Dow Jones Interactive, and others;
    • Secured first charter advertising partnership with Infiniti Motors, achieving significant CPM growth over other online video platforms;
    • Achieved 59% sequential month-over-month organic viewership growth;
    • Established 1CAST as having one of the highest engaged online news viewing audiences on the web and smart-phone platforms;
    • Secured channel distribution partnerships for direct and private label opportunities with companies such as Clearwire, Yahoo! and Phoenix Technologies.
