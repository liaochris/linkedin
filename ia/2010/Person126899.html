Lindsey Jarrell, FACHE’s Summary

Lindsey is an accomplished healthcare leader with over 15 years of experience and an exceptional record of achievement in consulting, clinical transformation, strategic planning, contract negotiation and CIO leadership in a large integrated delivery network.
Lindsey has served on the executive committees of fast growing technology companies and large integrated healthcare providers with 17,000 employees. He currently serves on the Board of Directors for First Services Administrators Inc based in Lakeland, FL.
Lindsey recently Co-founded Source88, a full service healthcare IT consulting firm, with Scott Kizer. Source88 serves hospitals and health systems throughout the United States.
Keep an eye out for Lindsey's latest book titled "The Future of Healthcare in America: How Consumer Behavior is Changing Medicine" coming out in September 2010.
