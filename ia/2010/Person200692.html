Ted Martin’s Experience

  • Director Sales and Training

    Vorsight

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    December 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Vorsight teaches the techniques of cold calling and getting in the door via interactive training and development workshops. Our clients are B2B lead generation, field sales, channel sales, and inside sales organizations that sell software, technology, telecom, research, consulting, IT services, and business services to senior executives. We also support clients with our outsourced appointment setting business. Our associates schedule hundreds of qualified meetings every month with senior executives at Fortune 1000 companies. Both sides of the business work side-by-side; we utilize our internal demand generation team to update the tips and tricks we teach in our prospecting workshops. Both offerings work with you to fill the top of your sales funnel with more qualified introductory sales meetings increasing your overall sales performance.

  • Business Development Associate

    Vorsight

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    June 2008 — December 2009 (1 year 7 months)

    In this role I worked with over ten clients conducting business research to determine specific industries and companies within the Fortune 1000 that would have a demand for their product or service. I identified specific corporate-level executives to target for marketing initiatives using networking and other sourcing tools. Then I promoted the service to executive prospects via phone or email, and scheduled over 500 introductory sales meetings between the client salesperson and the corporate executive prospect, coaching clients as needed. I executed sales opportunity assessments, and if appropriate, generated demand for the clients' product or service. I worked with management to establish our internal coaching program. Internally, I lead the coaching effort for others on my team, and also utilized my communication skills training sales representatives working for outside companies.
