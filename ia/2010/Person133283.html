Mark Astarita’s Summary

For 25 years I have been involved in representation of brokerage firms, investment advisory firms and individual financial professionals in all aspect of their regulatory, compliance and litigation matters.

My clients include wirehouses, regional and small broker-dealers, shops, financial web sites, trader sites, investment advisers, individual brokers and investors in every conceivable type of litigation, as well as in regulatory and compliance matters with the SEC, FINRA/NASD and the NYSE. I have represented parties in investigations and enforcement actions by the SEC, FINRA and the NYSE, as well as in over 500 securities arbitrations before the NYSE, the NASD, FINRA and the NYSE.

In litigation matters, I have represented parties in hundreds of disputes between investors and brokers, as well as countless disputes between firms and brokers regarding promissory notes, restrictive covenants, U-4 and U-5 issues, and just about every other issue that arises in that relationship.

I continue to provide these services in a manner that combines efficiency with quality, and use alternative fee arrangements that best suit my clients' needs and goals.
