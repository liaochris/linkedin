Wills Sweney’s Summary

Wills has extensive experience in the market research, design, and environmental management fields. He is currently a Senior Project Manager for Pandora USA, a highly specialized consultancy focused on print and paper commodities. Prior to Pandora, he was a Market Analyst with EnerNOC, a global energy management firm, where he focused on US energy markets from the end-user perspective. Prior to EnerNOC, he co-founded renewable energy company, Ecovolve, that develops innovative biomass gasification technologies for distributed generation. He has also worked in a research position for the Office for Metropolitan Architecture (OMA) in The Netherlands, researching intersections between sustainability and architecture.

Wills graduated from Princeton University with a degree, cum laude, in Classics with a certificate in Environmental Studies. He completed the urban design program at the Harvard Graduate School of Design which focused his interests in design and research methodologies. In addition to this design education, he has also complete substantial coursework in supply chain management, life-cycle assessment, and industrial ecology at UC Santa Barbara's Bren School of Environmental Science and Management.
