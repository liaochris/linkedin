Moh Houshangi - PMP®’s Summary

With over 20 years of progressive experience in IT project and team, Moh is an expert in project management, IT team leadership and building relationship between IT and Business partners. He has managed several multimillion Dollar Infrastructure and application development projects, from forecast stage till full delivery phase. Moh has worked with numerous high profile clients across diverse industries such as American Express, Bank of Montreal Financial Group, TD Bank Financial Group , Solcorp/EDS/HP, Transamerica and Siemens-Nixdorf and has exercised his wealth of knowledge in project management, and IT team leadership launching major projects and initiatives in these companies. Moh has his PMP, Six Sigma Certification, and Masters Certificate of Project Management, and is an active member of the Project Management Institute, IEEE and senior member of American Society of Quality.
