Tim Bailey’s Summary

I am an experienced IT Manager having run full service IT departments within small and medium sized organisations, working for central government, higher education, small private businesses and in the not-for-profit sector.

For the last two years I have been an accredited PRINCE2 project manager and have managed the complete development life cycle of a number of complex web applications - from system conceptualisation to production management.

I also have person and organisation management expertise and have previously undertaken organisational change consultancy work with SME clients.

Always looking for new adventures and happy to hear from people and organisations who can offer me a challenge.
