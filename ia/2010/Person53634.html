Deborah Smith’s Summary

I am a social media marketing consultant and coach to small and medium sized businesses. In addition to private consulting and company based workshops, I also conduct monthly general workshops and virtual coaching sessions. 10 years ago I launched a very successful ecommerce business which operates a network of websites serving the Nanny Industry. I began employing email groups, chat rooms and online message boards as marketing and networking tools well before the term "Social Media" was ever conceived. In 2007, I launched a food blog called Jersey Bites which now welcomes over 8000 visitors per month and has a Google page rank of 5. In April of 2008, I launched a private Social Network to serve the Nanny Industry called NannyIsland.com which now has over 700 members and is the most successful networking platform in the Nanny industry. In December 2008, I spearheaded the Blogging Out Hunger campaign for the Community FoodBank of New Jersey and enlisted over 120 bloggers from across the state to raise money and awareness on a one day "Blogburst."

Follow me on Twitter @JerseyBites
Fan My Page of Social Media Marketing on Facebook: http://www.Facebook.com/SocialMedia4Business
