    Arista's mission is to deliver networking products that address emerging IT architectures, including:

    • Server virtualization
    • Cloud computing
    • Network-attached storage (NAS) and iSCSI clusters
    • High Performance Computing (HPC)

    Arista has developed a new network switch architecture and network design principles to address these new environments within a framework we term cloud networking. The highlights are:

    • Cost/performance breakthrough enabling cost-effective non-blocking 10 Gbps Ethernet infrastructures to handle mobile, bursty workloads with predictable throughput and very low latency
    • Robustness with Arista's Extensible Operating System (EOS), which permits In-Service Software Upgrades (ISSU) and self-healing operation via Stateful Fault Repair (SFR)
    • Operational simplicity delivered through a single software image with consistent features across our entire range of products, a consistent management interface across virtual and physical networks, and 10GBASE-T technology which enables the use of standard CAT-5 and CAT-6 cables

    As testament to the real customer problems that we address, close to 200 customers have chosen to install and benefit from Arista's award-winning switches in just our first year of product shipments.

    Arista partners with leading companies in the IT sector who take advantage of our cloud networking platform, including Citrix, Dell, Greenplum, NetApp and VMware.
