Daniel Perlaky’s Summary

SUMMARY:

Dániel Perlaky is a creative director, non-traditional marketer, producer, and entrepreneur based in Austin, TX.

City On Fire is an award-winning visual communications studio producing client-unique works in multiple media including editorial, identity, and promotional design, editorial and commercial photography, packaging, experience design, web design and coding as well as consulting and execution regarding branding, media planning, identity cohesiveness and refinement, and overall identity brand management... and really long sentences.

The Dark Agency is a non-traditional marketing company organically integrating brands into media and experiences through a variety of ever-evolving methods that authentically resonate with a discerning audience of 21-35 tastemakers.

Reversal Films is a motion picture development house focused on feature length narrative and documentary films and new media content.

Other projects include Indierect Records, Art Disaster, BleachTV, Fashion Leak, Echotone, Home, and others.

Logo, branding, and identity work for a wide range of clients, especially in the entertainment and arts industries.
