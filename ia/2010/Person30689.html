Budi Hartono’s Summary

Engineer, researcher, and lecturer: systems thinker.
The above keywords reflect Budi's world of professional activities.

Budi Hartono is currently pursuing a PhD degree in the Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, NUS. The study is conducted in the area of construction project management.

In short, Budi is investigating 'the risk of doing risk management'. In particular, the research focuses on the identification of systemic and systematic human judgmental errors in the context of senior managers determining competitive bid mark-up prices.

Hence in this study, Budi needs to integrate various concepts of diverse academic domains, including: project management, risk management, decision theory, cognitive psychology, and systems thinking while maintaining a reasonable depth of study.

Budi holds a position as a permanent academic staff in the Mechanical & Industrial Engineering Department, Universitas Gadjah Mada (UGM). He is currently on leave. He used to be the head of Simulation & Computation Laboratory. During his stint in UGM, he lectured, conducted research, and provided consultancy services and professional trainings for industry practitioners mainly in the area of project & risk management.

Budi is a Mechanical Engineer by training from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) Indonesia and has a master degree in Project Management from the University of Queensland.

In addition to the strong academic background, Budi has extensive hand-on experience in the Engineering & Construction field. His past roles centered on the best utilization of an advanced 3D CAD/CAE system for multi-disciplinary, concurrent engineering plant design projects.

His past exposures in industrial projects and the academic experience convinced him to earn a living and to contribute to the society in a slightly different way compared to his colleagues.

He would stay on the current course of becoming a good engineer, researcher, and lecturer: a systems thinker.
