Pankaj Kedia’s Summary

Senior global business strategist, product marketer and business development executive with unparalleled achievements – launching industry-changing products, driving business growth, and leading I2A2 (investment, incubation, acquisition, alliance) efforts for Intel Corp. across mobile, handheld, Internet, and wireless segments.
