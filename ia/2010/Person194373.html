VP OF SALES
In 1997 Stephen began his mortgage career with Ameriquest Mortgage. His career quickly grew from branch manager to Vice President of Sales and Call Center Operations. Stephen managed production objectives in order to develop and achieve company goals and oversaw the centralized sales call center with a staff of over 500 people. Mr. Fontenot was able to maintain production volume levels at an average of $500M per month with annual revenues exceeding $90 million for his division with an increased net return of 10%.

Soon after his promotion to Vice President of Sales & Call Center Operations, Stephen was given the task to reorganize; ensuring ethical practices and quality assurance were in place. Stephen had begun his tenure with 112 employees seeing it through to a staff of over 500. In his new position with Dunmore Capital Inc., Stephen was responsible for researching, identifying, and contacting appropriate major institutional investors and introducing them to the Dunmore Capital Fund I, a $100 million dollar commercial real estate private offering. Stephen has dealt directly with real estate acquisition decision makers for institutional investors throughout the United States and abroad. Stephen’s secondary role in the company was to research, locate and contact suitable Joint Venture Partners for Dunmore Capital to purchase distressed properties. He also negotiated the terms and conditions of each potential Joint Venture Partner.

Most recently, Stephen is the owner of a net branch that conducts Debt Settlements. He is seeking alliances with the institutions, banks, and servicers to assist in this very tumultuous environment. Stephen is a participant of the Harvard Business School Executive Course. For the past decade, Stephen has organized over 12 philanthropic events supporting communities and charitable organizations.
