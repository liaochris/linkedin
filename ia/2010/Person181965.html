WHY TRAIN WITH RIZK CLINICAL RESEARCH GROUP?
The global need for properly trained clinical researchers is growing rapidly. While there will always be a demand for research in traditional academic settings, there is an increasing need for clinical research, including post marketing surveillance, to be conducted under actual clinical conditions. Pharmaceutical, biotech, government and private foundations all sponsor clinical studies, and each has a growing demand to identify private practice clinicians who have training in the fundamental principles and practices of clinical research.

For over ten years, RIZK has been creating a standard of excellence in clinical research through training and education throughout the world. Our aspiration has always been to develop high-quality training programs for new and experienced clinical research team members. Our goals also reflect an educational partnership between sponsors, contract research organizations, site management organizations, universities, and sites to develop clinical research excellence in research Globally.
