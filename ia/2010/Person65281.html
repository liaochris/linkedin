Evan Powell’s Summary

Successful entrepreneur.

Currently focused on Nexenta Systems while also advising a couple of other promising start-ups.

Now recruiting top storage software 'solutions consultants'; if you are the right person, you probably know the profile -- solution oriented, comfortable with sales and support, motivated by helping to create a new industry by solving customer problems dramatically better than alternatives. Please contact me if you feel you might be interested or if you know someone who would be a great fit.

Previously I founded Clarus Systems, the leading provider of software to enterprises for the management of their next generation phone systems. We count many of the largest firms in the IT industry as partners and customers, including IBM, HP, JPMC, Lehman Brothers, and Cisco.

Before founding Clarus Systems I was an early employee at ThinkLink, which pioneered the delivery of applications over VoIP. At ThinkLink my responsibilities included business development and network strategy.

Prior to ThinkLink I spent a number of years helping to grow Working Assets, the leading progressive communication services company. At Working Assets my responsibilities centered on determining current and future customer values and recommending and implementing changes, such as marketing efforts and customer segmentation, based on my findings.

Earlier in my career I started a company that provided 'fair trade' textiles and other crafts from Guatemala and also coordinated a non-profit, the Berkshire Food Project, that provided food and counseling to community members of North Adams, MA. Like many entrepreneurs I started my first 'business' in grade school.

I am an enthusiastic advisor to a number of start-ups.

I am a devoted Dad and Husband and Georgia football fan.
