Steve Fretzin’s Summary

Mr. Fretzin is the Founder and President of Sales Results, Inc. and as such, provides overall strategic direction and leadership to the firm.

Prior to establishing Sales Results, Inc. in 2005, Mr. Fretzin specialized in sales coaching, marketing management, and business operations in the franchise industry. In 2005, Mr. Fretzin began SALES RESULTS, which is an elite provider of sales coaching, training and business networking. Sales Results clients know that effective coaching is the best investment they can make in their careers, and Sales Results strives to maximize that ROI. Sales Results works with entrepreneurs, sales teams, and business professionals who aspire to the highest standards and practices of business development in their chosen industries.

In addition to his duties at Sales Results, Inc., Mr. Fretzin oversees the operations of three other organizations. He is Founder and Partner of The Executives Profit, LLC, Founder and Managing Partner of Team Discovery, LLC, Founder and Managing Partner of Networking Monkey, LLC. and Co-Founder of Tekworking.

Mr. Fretzin received his B.S. from Illinois State University in 1993. He has authored numerous articles on selling, networking, and business development. He was recently featured in Entrepreneur.com and on the Bob Sirott show on WGN Radio.
