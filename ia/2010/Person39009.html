Christina Geyer’s Summary

Experienced health research and clinical trial statistician, with additional statistical interests in a wide variety of business applications, including risk analysis and operations research. Particularly interested in Bayesian methods.

Founder of MamasWorldwide.com blog and the author of AmiExpat.com blog.

Christina Geyer’s Specialties:

General skills in statistics and computing. Specific expertise and interests in:
- Statistics: clinical trial planning and analysis; Bayesian methods
- Computing platforms: UNIX, Windows
- Programming languages: C and HTML; a little COBOL, FORTRAN, and LISP
- Statistical software: S-Plus, SAS, BUGS (Bayesian inference Using Gibbs Sampling)
- Text formatting and office computing: LaTeX, Emacs, Word, Powerpoint, Excel, Frontpage
- Spoken languages: Fluent in English and German
