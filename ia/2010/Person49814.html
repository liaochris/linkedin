Dave Ratner’s Summary

As a mortgage specialist for the past 15+years, I have a unique understanding of the mortgage industry, how it functions and how to get things done. I specialize in self-employed individuals and executives who may have an unusual situation that needs a creative solution. The Mortgage market is in flux these days, while the credit markets and institutions are drying up and money is harder to find, it's more important than ever to work with someone who has the experience, expertise and understanding of this new and complicated mortgage industry.

Dave Ratner’s Specialties:

I specialize in all grades of loans from perfect credit to not so great credit. I also handle all forms of housing from primary residences to condos and investment properties. I have a particular specialty when it comes to no-income verification loans. We are also licensed to handle Reverse Mortgages, the newest rage for seniors 62+ who are house rich and cash poor. If you need financing, I am definitely the person to call.
