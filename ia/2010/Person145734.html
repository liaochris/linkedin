Mike McNally’s Summary

As a maritime officer, handling crew, cargo and navigation responsibilities for 30,000 ton Oil & Petro Chemical Tankers, I had a good foundation to build on.

Control Data Corp provided a more formal business education and exposed me to a broad variety of disciplines. I designed and sold solutions for processs industry optimization, marketing analysis systems and Electronic Data Interchange (EDI).

Marine Management Systems combined my Sales, IT Systems and Marine background. I built the global distribution for the organization in 6 years. I was noticed by Globe Wireless who hired me away in 1997.

Globe Wireless has been an incredible experience over the past 12 years. We have built the customer base from 100 ships to over 9,000 while drastically increasing the revenue per ship. I was responsible for developing the European sales operation, and supporting the global infrastructure. I have been able to turn that over to one of my early hires.
My focus is now directing the Satellite Services buildup for the company. Developing and working with our suppliers to fulfill the needs of our customers, while supporting the sales forces with pricing and reinforcing the company's strategic direction.

Looking forward to the next big challenge.
