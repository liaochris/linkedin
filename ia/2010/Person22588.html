Bessy Nikolaou’s Summary

Originally hailing from Halifax, I recently joined the Time + Space team after a 9 year layover in Toronto. I would describe myself as extremely passionate about internet technology and online media, where it is today and where it is headed tomorrow. Immersed in Social Media and Web 2.0, long before the terms were mainstream, I've been a part of the online conversation for 10 years+.

I spent my time in Toronto planning and implementing the online media and marketing strategies for Tucows Inc., and most recently Torstar Digital (including brands such as toronto.com, TheStar.com, Ourfaves.com, et al). I was a member of the flickr, ourfaves and numerous other start-up beta communities, worked on the usability of blogware before blogging became a verb and still geek out over SEO.

Traditionally working client side in the digital space, I'm now eager to share my knowledge of online and social media marketing with both my team and our clients.

Bessy Nikolaou’s Specialties:

Search engine optimization, search engine marketing, Web 2.0, passionate about social media + marketing, developing online and new media engagement strategies et al
