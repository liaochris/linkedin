Natacha Quester-Semeon’s Summary

Natacha Quester-Séméon is co-founder and senior partner in the company I-Marginal dedicated to the creation of contents and Web sites, specialized in education, scientific and technical culture for New medias (Cite des Sciences, CNRS, France Télévisions...).

Journalist, author, producer and hypermedia project manager, she works in the press, TV, and the Internet since 1994 (popular press and Web : Sciences et Avenir, Nouvel Observateur group, Télérama, Le Monde...).

She takes an active part in citizens initiatives. She is general secretary of the humanist associative network "The Associated Humans" (Les Humains Associés), through a virtual community since 1995 (three Web sites, forum, mailing list, blog, etc.).

Co-founder and animator of the Parisian blog "Memoire Vive.tv" created in 2003 (videoblog…). She is also co-inventor of the movement "wifi pique-nique" (wifipicnic.com), surfers meetings in a ephemeral local wifi network (wifi bubble), she is interested — to make a long story short — with what is close to human and social networks.

She also made and co-lead the network Girl power 3.0, with women interested in Internet, business and society.

In Second Life, as Natacha Salomon, she is Community Architect, Project manager, Avatar customization, Blogs & in-world Community Specialist, Social Networking animation.
