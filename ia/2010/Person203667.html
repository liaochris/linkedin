Tim Baran’s Summary

Over 15 years of experience in the legal industry including the federal court, law school and law firms, serving from 1999-2008 as the director of library services and the continuing legal education program at Anderson Kill & Olick, a law firm headquartered in New York City with offices in Newark, NJ, Philadelphia, PA, Washington, DC, and Chicago, IL.

A passion for efficiency, technology, innovation and productivity served as impetus for the vision and development of uMCLE (formerly CLE Post) to address the evolving needs of the profession and the legal community.

I have several other ideas in development or percolating but will be on low fire, since the first two endeavors are already quite consuming.
