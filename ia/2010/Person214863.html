Wesley Lindquist’s Summary

Results driven professional with 10 years of experience and expertise in senior financial, accounting and operations management in multiple industries. Expertise in managing organizations by numbers and quantitative measurements. Top-level expert in using advanced Excel to model business performance and strategy. Thrives in a fast paced environment, highly enthusiastic. Adds significant value through a broad range of business acumen, combined with an exceptionally deep level of expertise in finance, accounting, project/program planning and project cost control.

Directly responsible for accuracy of P&L, balance sheet, cash flow statement, debt covenant management, all forecasts, financial analysis, overseeing journal entries, GAAP compliance, revenue recognition, cost accounting, project accounting, annual budget and cash flow management at multiple mid size, well run, established firms.

Significant experience in the energy industry, including as a Corporate Controller of a 300-employee biofuels, petroleum distribution and natural gas trading firm, and as Asst CFO of a 500-employee firm that provides solar installation to Fortune 100 clients.

Extensive interdependent work with division and executive level management and Boards of Directors, including as a Board member and Treasurer at a 60-employee organization.
Very advanced Excel skills and very strong software skills across a wide variety of programs, including Great Plains/FRx, Hyperion Essbase, Monarch, Lawson, SQL query writing, ODBC queries, Crystal Reports, database design, everything Microsoft and much more. Bachelor's degree in Accounting Information Systems.

Formal training and experience in large project management, monitoring and controlling, based on principles of the Project Management Institute (PMI). Pursuing Certified Associate Project Associate designation. Responsible for cost control and financial oversight of hundreds of $100k+ projects.
