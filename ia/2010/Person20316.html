Barry Weiss’s Summary

With an $8.6 Billion track record of box office success, Barry has worked on some of the most profitable, highly acclaimed and award winning projects of the last fifteen years. As an animation executive, producer, and collaborator with Sony and Turner he has achieved an unparalleled track record of success. Barry was instrumental in the successful creation of two animation companies and the animated characters for the “Stuart Little” and “Spiderman” franchises, as well characters for over 35 feature films such as “Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone”, “Superman Returns” and Academy Award nominee “Surf’s Up”. He is credited on 58 Feature films and shorts which garnered two Academy Award’s and eight additional nominations. Barry is recognized as an industry leader in the evolution of animation into all forms of feature films as well as an exceptional budget and resource manager who is known for extraordinary skills in talent development.

Currently looking for a leadership opportunity that involves a high degree of interaction with the creative team and the business or production management of a project or a company.
