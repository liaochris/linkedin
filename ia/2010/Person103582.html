Joanne Mason’s Summary

About Joanne Mason - I have enjoyed an unusually broad range of experience and accomplishments in a rewarding and productive career in teaching and educational leadership. In recent years, combining teaching and organizational management experience with creative work in production, technology, and photography, I have fashioned a second career in media production, management, and business development. I've been told that I'm an inspiring, driven leader, a skilled manager, teacher, and producer who inspires commitment, loyalty, and creativity, who understands organizations and gets things done. More than anything else, I like to work with teams helping people and organizations be more effective and successful.

Joanne Mason’s Specialties:

Management, marketing, business development, media production, production planning and coordination, internet development and marketing, team leadership and staff development, strategic planning and analysis, branding and identity, organization development, teaching, mathematics, educational administration, photography, photography and video editing and authoring.
