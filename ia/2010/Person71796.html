Emmanuel Frenehard’s Summary

Senior IT executive with rich international experience and expertise. Strong understanding of technology’s capabilities to enhance business models and create new revenue opportunities.

Manage and motivate successful IT professionals in multi-lingual, multi-cultural and distributed environments to deliver rich and flexible technology services at reasonable rates. Focused on evolving the enterprise IT services into a consumer experience which is more adapted to the needs of the employees of the 21st century.

Experience in managing very large global initiatives with significant budgets across multiple companies over the past 8 years including ERP implementations (Oracle Applications), Shared Services implementations and worldwide infrastructure rollouts. Last project span 43 countries across Asia, Europe, the Middle-East, Africa and Latin America.

Interested in evolving towards a Chief Operations Officer (COO) or Chief Technology Officer (CTO) role in a media or entertainment company.

Digital Convergence is forcing media companies to focus on technology when developing products. Technology (IT) is now mainstream as it makes its way to the center of the home (Wi-Fi, broadband, VoIP, IPTV...). Technology has become a weapon in the hand of successful consumer product enterprises who have their genesis in the IT world (Apple IPod, Microsoft xBox360,...).

A business school education, coupled with international experience and technology strategic vision are key strengths for a senior executive role. Four spoken languages (French, Spanish, English and Portuguese) and a background as a resident in different countries.
