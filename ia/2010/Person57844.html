Dominic Jacquesson’s Summary

I am a hands-on executive with plenty of start-up experience and deep expertise in subscriptions, digital marketing, CRM and data-driven customer acquisition. Also very strong in operations, finance, and IR. Seeking a role as a CEO, CMO or COO in an exciting venture-backed business - or a highly visible executive role in a larger corporate that is looking for change.

I set up Ink On Dead Trees this year as a consultancy-vehicle specialising in digital marketing, CRM, and the transition from print-to-digital media.

From 2001 to 2009, Chief Operating Officer of Electric Word plc, an AIM-listed specialized information publisher serving the education and sports sectors (B2B & B2C) through a range of media - websites, magazines, newsletters, books/reports, conferences/exhibitions, e-commerce. In 2001 revenues were £600k with £1m losses. FY2008 results were revenues of £17m and pre-tax profit of £1.8m. Equal contributions from organic and acquisition based growth. Also on the Board were Peter Rigby (CEO of Informa plc) and Stephen Routledge (founder of Trillium Partners).

Previously COO for the US operations of Euromoney Institutional Investor plc, based in New York, with revenues of $80m. This followed roles with Euromoney working for David Levin (now CEO of United Business Media plc) in London project managing the design and build of a global data warehouse, and acting as a group trouble-shooter.

Started my career as a strategy consultant with Gemini Consulting (now Capgemini), helping to establish the healthcare & pharmaceutical practice, and also working in financial services and supply chain assignments.
