  • Vice President of Business Development

    iEnterprises, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    January 2006 — August 2009 (3 years 8 months)

    iEnterprises is a mobile CRM solutions company, where Phil Sheehy grew revenue over 200% year over year, taking these revenues from zero to over $3 million in less than 2 years. During his tenure, Phil successfully guided product development and was responsible for marketing and sales for the company’s wireless CRM solutions based on the iEnterprises MobileEdge software platform. These solutions wirelessly extended popular CRM platforms such as Siebel, Microsoft Dynamics CRM, Sugar CRM, NetSuite and Lotus Notes to BlackBerry, Windows Mobile and iPhone devices.

    Phil pioneered wireless Software as a Service (SaaS) CRM solutions by launching products such as Mobile Edge for NetSuite and Microsoft Dynamics On-Demand. He also pioneered wireless CRM for Open Source with Mobile Edge for SugarCRM and Concursive Corporation. Phil’s vision that powerful CRM tools should be available anytime, anywhere, and his delivery of solutions based on this vision has helped lead the CRM and mobile CRM markets.
