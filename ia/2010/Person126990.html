Lance LeTellier, PE MS & MBA’s Summary

Engineering assistance for residential and commercial building and real estate owners with primary emphasis on structural or damage investigations, building inspections and Property Condition Assessments or plan constructability reviews. Services also include Phase I Environmental Site Assessments, community association and capital reserve studies, project development assistance, expert testimony, construction quality assurance, and specialized facility engineering support including low impact repair and design concepts.
