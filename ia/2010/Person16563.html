Arnon Kohavi’s Summary

People need to connect to the Internet everywhere and anytime. Mobile devices and technologies will make that happen.

I started WeFi to connect everyone to WiFi - free and high bandwidth compared to 3G.
Before, I was more established: an executive (EVP) at DSPC, a wireless chip maker, that brought the wireless revolution to Japanese consumer electronic companies; and EVP at Vyyo, a wireless broadband system company.

I was a fellow at the Reuters Digital Vision program at Stanford bringing wireless communications and the Internet to people in rural areas in the developing world.

The combination of the Internet and wireless communications can make a difference in People's lives. I want to be part of it.
