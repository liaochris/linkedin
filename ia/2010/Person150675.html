Mona Yousry’s Summary

Dr. Yousry is a remarkable leader who was a Senior-level executive for multiple corporations in high-tech, Telecommunications and the Energy markets. She worked directly with the Board of Directors, Shareholders and Senior Executives. She has excelled in general management with P&L responsibility, strategic marketing, directing and controlling the structuring of new businesses and product development. She was successful in identifying market opportunities and implementing appropriate teams and business strategies; especially for mass markets.

In 1993, Dr. Yousry managed a 40 million residential customer base for AT&T Communication Services. This business unit had a budget in excess of $200 million and a $2.1 Billion revenue stream. In 1994, Dr. Yousry was a senior member of the AT&T Consumer Division; the only AT&T Business unit to win the prestigious Baldrige Award.
In 1995, Mona worked for a telecom division in the midwest. She was a vice president in charge of product manag.ement. In 1996, Mona became a Senior Vice President at Sempra Energy in San Diego

Dr. Yousry received her Ph.D. in Mathematics/Statistics from the Ohio State University. She joined Bell Laboratories as a Member of the Research Staff working directly on engineering projects, dealing with Telecom networks and manufacturing issues. This position requires high academic performance and working directly with researchers at universities and technology transfer centers. In 1991, Dr. Yousry was invited by the Egyptian President of RITSEC to lecture to Egypt’s top industries, Universities and Government agencies regarding improving Egypt’s plans for privatization and protection of Western intellectual property. Dr. Yousry was chosen in 1991 to serve on the AT&T Management Committee staff supporting the Office of SVP Corporate Strategy and Development.
