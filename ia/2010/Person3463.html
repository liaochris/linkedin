Adrienne Dyer’s Summary

I am a self-employed print and web site designer with a focus on brand marketing. I am constantly exploring the web for new tools and trends to push my creative limits. Always trying to keep things fresh, I am lucky to have a strong base of clients who desire a vast range of services.
