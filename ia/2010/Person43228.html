Bill Corbett Jr.’s Summary

William J. Corbett, Jr., is the President and CEO of Corbett Public Relations, Inc., a leading Long Island public relations, social media marketing, event promotion and government affairs firm based in Floral Park, NY. The firm, provides media relations, social media marketing and personal branding services to small, medium and large business professional firms, not-for-profit organizations and individuals in the New York metropolitan area and across the nation.

Mr. Corbett has developed and managed hundreds of public relations programs, special events and publicity campaigns for his clients. His public relations savvy has resulted in numerous stories about his clients in dozens of major publications including Entrepreneur Magazine, Inc. Magazine, Newsday, News The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, Crain’s New York Business, Long Island Business News and Long Island Magazine.

In 2002 Long Island Business News named him one of the Top 40 Business Professionals under the age of 40. Newsday recognized him as one of the "Five People to Watch" in the public relations profession. For his leadership in the Long Island Public Relations field he was named the salutatorian of the LIBN 40 Under 40 Class of 2002 in February 2009.

Mr. Corbett is a graduate of Marist College and earned a Master’s Degree in Environmental Management from Long Island University. He is a member of the Board of Directors of The Marty Lyons Foundation and the Core Committee of the Long Island Fight For Charity.

Long Island public relations, social media marketing, on-line marketing, business development consulting, personal branding, environmental PR for businesses, real estate, large special events including festivals and fairs, unions, law firms, professional firms and resturants
