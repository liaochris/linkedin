  • Senior Scientist

    Camber

    As Senior Scientist at the FAA’s Satellite Navigation Program Office, I was responsible for performing software independent validation and verification on the Wide Area Augmentation System (WAAS) in all stages. I also developed algorithms to correct satellite Selective Availability for clock filtering on both satellites and receivers and identify the UDRE bounds. Other activities including participation in design reviews to ensure contractor compliance with DOD-STD-2167A and in safety related issues.
