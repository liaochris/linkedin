Andrew Bass’s Summary

Straight Design is visual solutions made simple + direct providing smart, high-impact visual solutions for small to mid-size businesses, not-for-profits and individuals. To achieve this my studio believes in being straight forward—with the focus on your message—and not being distracted by trends or cool tricks. Using this process, I’ve created award-winning logos, brochures, annual reports, magazines and much more for my clients.

Most recently, I was an adjunct instructor at New York City College of Technology teaching typographic & graphic design.

Andrew Bass’s Experience

  • Chief Idea Maker

    Straight Design LLC

    (Graphic Design industry)

    September 2005 — Present (4 years 10 months)

    Straight Design is the brainchild of Andrew Bass. Motivated by the ineffective design programs used by many small companies, he wanted to present a studio that focused on creating design that thinks about its message and not just be a pretty face. Straight Design is visual solutions made simple and direct.

    That is where our expertise comes in. Following our KNOW YOUR FACE™ principles, we make powerful creative that’s straight to the point. With these principles, we’ve created winning logos, brochures, annual reports, magazines and much more for many of our clients.

  • Art Director

    VNU Business Media

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; VNU; Publishing industry)

    September 2001 — August 2005 (4 years )

    Lead editorial design direction of award-winning monthly trade title (circulation 65,000), Sales & Marketing Management, with an annual special mid-year edition, Suryey of Buying Power. Spearheaded a complete redesign in fall 2002 with a launch issue of May 2003 which focused on a new cover directive, new grids for interior pages, new section layouts, reimagined cover logotype and initiated new visual direction in photography and illustraton that garnered a “Best Redesign” 2004 Gold Award from the American Society of Publication Editors.
