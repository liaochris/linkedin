Evrim Kuran’s Summary

She was born in the capital on a summer day.
She studied at Gazi Anatolian High School, Hacettepe University English Language and Literature, Marmara University HR Management and Executive MBA, Sabancı University. She has trained people in various institutions such as Yeditepe University, Naval High School Command, and many others. Despite having the title of "filologist", she has fallen in love with the idea of becoming a salesperson. Her sales career is full of success and failure stories of many products from chemical fertilizer to blue jeans. She co-founded Dinamo Training & Consulting in 2005. She likes working on Gen Y, Key Account Management, CRM, Developing Effective Behavioural Patterns of Salespeople, Organizational Commitment, Peak Performance, Multiple Intelligence and Quantum Thought. She is a "differentialist" and more than that, she is against collective mediocrity.

She is also a professional coach and a member of ICF.

She keeps on observing, thinking, producing and learning.

She is married and she is Ali's supermom.
