Jon Dupree | Internet Marketing Manager’s Summary

The heart of any successful sales team is its ability to determine why a customer choses to buy, how to over-come objections and present the best marketing campaign of products and services in a "what does it do for you" manner.

Companies are facing challenges and opportunities that rapidly change and need guidance on how to use the latest marketing technologies. The Internet has changed how companies communicate their stories; transparency and validation is being forced into the marketing message to qualify the unique value proposition; and the customer is now in control of the company branding. Search Engines have allowed any company in the world to be our competitor, and every customer in a "zone of indifference" now has a global megaphone to express themselves. By combining direct sales experience, over 15 years of creating marketing messages and an in-depth knoweldge of the technical aspects of the internet, I help companies put their best marketing onto the internet.

My specialization is in the consultant method to build a company's expertise position in their existing markets and develop new business markets for established products. Utilizing deep technical knowledge for implementing and training of Internet Marketing Products, I assist my clients to grow and prosper with solutions such as Modular Websites utilizing Content Management Solutions that enable relevant and timely information to be on the web, Search Engine Optimization / Marketing so they can be found on the web and Conversion-centered marketing campaigns to make sure visitors become prospects and clients.

Most recently technology changes in Social Media Marketing have added to a sales persons networking arsenal and also provides in-depth knowledge and feedback for customers and prospects like never before. Social media lets a company know if the customer experience lived up to their expectations, promote successes and fix disappointments in record time.
