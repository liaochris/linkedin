Laura Posey’s Summary

Founder of Dancing Elephants Achievement Group, a Richmond, VA-based consulting and training firm focused on Sales Magnetism(TM)

Referral marketing and client attraction strategies, national speaker and trainer, author of Six Secrets Of Sales Magnets, and a thought leader on creating dynamic sales cultures.

Extensive experience training entrepreneurs and sales teams in referral and relationship marketing and sales.

Previous experience managing independent contractors at Nationwide Insurance as well as training sales teams and owners at Proforma, Allegra Print and Imaging, Chem-Dry, and Apple Marketing Services Committee.

An active member of National Speakers Association (NSA) and National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO).

Active member of the Rotary Club of Innsbrook, former board member of Executive Women's Golf Association (EWGA), River City Express Network (RCEN), Fargo Business Network International (BNI) Chapter, National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO); passionate about saving homeless animals.

Named 2005 American Business Women's Association (ABWA) Top Ten, 2007 Canon Businesswoman of the Year Top Ten Finalist, 2005 American Business Women's Association (ABWA) Tri-Cities Woman of the Year.

Featured in Innsbrook Today Magazine, American Business Women's Association Magazine.

A native of Southern Maryland, Laura earned an MBA from Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) and her BA from State University at New York (SUNY) Buffalo. She has recently been adjunct faculty at the University of Richmond. Laura has also lived in Germany and speaks fluent German.

Laura Posey’s Specialties:

Referral marketing, sales team creation and development, hiring and managing salespeople, entrepreneur development, profitable revenue growth, small business growth and management, emotional intelligence assessment and development.

Dancing Elephants Achievement Group specializes in Referral Marketing Planning and Implementation with its Sales Magnetism training program. We design, develop and deploy referral marketing processes for entrepreneurial companies and salespeople.
