Résumé de Melanie Ellingsen

I was born an entrepreneur, making money cutting grass at the age of 7. When I got to college, I started a quite successful one-woman show, excited about the market value of good tutoring in a wealthy suburb.

I aspired to have my own business as long as I can remember… hoping that brilliant, unique idea or skill would somehow come to me.

I graduated with the desire to be in corporate financial analysis. At this “dream job”, I realized I wasn’t quite satisfied - entrepreneurs rarely are. I took my constant hunger to learn about myself and the world, and returned to school to get a Master’s in Education.

When I came to understand that I was drawn to running a classroom because it was much like running a business, but with very little pay, I decided it was time to pursue my dream and work for myself. By that time, I had acquired a service to provide, and along the way, learned of my passion for marketing. I learned a lot, and began helping other small businesses start up. I get so excited about the wealth of opportunity and abundance of need if you get in the front of the right audience. I later realized that social media marketing embodied so many of my values because it is really centered around abundance and about people lifting each other up.

Meanwhile, my hubby was pursuing his dream, offering the by-product of his years of developing a uniquely deep level of software architecture skills. Finally, one day, we realized how synergistic we could be by combining our skills! And we created a powerful, unique offering via our company, Ecommerce Architects.

These days, I am busy writing proposals, beginning exciting new projects, speaking to groups about social media… and carving a path for growth, aligning my work with vision and values.
