Aldonna R. Ambler, CMC, CSP’s Experience

  • President

    AMBLER Growth Strategy Consultants, Inc

    (Management Consulting industry)

    May 1971 — Present (39 years 5 months)

    Aldonna R. Ambler has earned the right to be called The Growth Strategist tm. She has won over 2 dozen national and statewide "entrepreneur of the year" awards for the resilient growth of her own international busineses across 4 recessions...but more importantly, her clients get on, and then stay on, the published lists of the fastest growing privately held companies. A certified management consultant (CMC) and a certified professional speaker (CSP) she brings fresh ideas, insights, and energy about Achieving Accelerated Growth With Sustained Profitability®. As a strategic advisor and speaker, Ambler has earned 92% repeat business from over 800 midsized service, technology and distribution client companies. A positive force for economic development, Ambler Chaired the NJ delegation to the White House Conference on Small Business, has provided expert testimony at over 30 legislative hearings, and conferred with 4 Presidents in the Oval Office/White House about how to help midsized companies continue to grow. She is in her 5th year hosting a peer-to-peer Internet radio show, The Growth Strategist™, where the host, guests, and listeners (180,000+) are Presidents/CEOs of midsized companies (typically $20-200Mil/yr) and discuss the growth strategy of the week.
