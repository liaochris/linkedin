Cynthia Sanko’s Summary

Healthcare communications and marketing talent with extensive creative management and diverse industry experience in corporate, agency and design firm environments. A combination of sophisticated art direction/design talent to execute the client’s creative vision and marketing strategy and an outstanding aptitude for initiating and coordinating collaborative marketing efforts to drive key brand messages, has been instrumental in maximizing ROI for the many corporations I have partnered with. Using an approach to design for marketing that holds back from developing a strong personal design style of stylistic obtrusiveness, I have freed up my ability to handle many variations of print, new media and broadcast projects that have allowed for the successful combination of a market-oriented blend of esthetics, communication and salesmanship. I have demonstrated high proficiency in major print graphics applications, office software and extensive knowledge of current new media, pre-press and printing processes. I have developed a strong educational background with specialized expertise in the advertising, marketing and communication design areas.
