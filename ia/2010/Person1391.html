Abdul Qabiz’s Summary

I am a passionate self-taught computer programmer and have many years' experience in software development, specifically Adobe Flash Platform (flash/flex/air/flashlite) and web-technologies.

Allies Interactive Services: I am trying to use all my experience and potential to build a great team, culture, process, practices and products. I see myself working with a team which full of passion and innovation.
--

Yahoo!: I had an opportunity to work on some platform components and Yahoo! Avatars, it was great experience to be among the best developers and to work on some of cool products/services.
--

Mixercast: I had an opportunity and freedom to learn and contribute in different ways: platform-architecture, implementation of best flash-platform practices, startup-culture, venture-capital, building syndication engine based on standards like RSS(MediaRSS), suggesting/implementing new ideas and lot more.

I would say, working in a startup or in a startup like culture is a great experience, I would love to do it again and again.
--

Macromedia/Adobe: It was a dream company for any passionate flash developer, I was no different. I ended up working here among the initial team for Bangalore centre.

I contributed in Flex SDK 1.5 and 2.0 releases. I contributed in two releases of Flex: 1.5 and 2.0.

I made very good friends and learned many professional and personal things, which help me everyday now. Totally great experience.
--

In my role as full time entrepreneur, I am still a developer from heart but my experience over the years helps me learn new things and deal with challenges everyday.

As a programmer, I always believed - A good programmer always writes good code by making right choices and decisions. I want to solve complex problem with simplicity and work on applications, which provide great experience to users. To do that, I am up for doing whatever it takes.
