J.D. LeaSure’s Summary

Professional provider of Counter Espionage, Technical Surveillance Countermeasures (TSCM) - bug sweep services to business professionals, government offices, celebrities, consulting firms, P.I. firms, government contractors and Law firms, etc.

Implementation of counter espionage protocols to help your organization maintain the integrity of its security posture against Corporate, Economic and Industrial Electronic Espionage Threats.

J.D. LeaSure, Counter Surveillance Specialist is the President/CEO of ComSec LLC. He founded the Virginia Beach-based firm in 2007 filling the need for an experienced and knowledgeable Counterespionage, Countersurveillance and Technical Surveillance Countermeasures TSCM service provider in the greater Virginia/DC Metro area of the United States.

J.D. has been active in Counterespionage and Technical Surveillance Countermeasures TSCM activities since 1984 and has extensive training, knowledge and experience of eavesdropping devices, detection methods and the tactics most often employed by those seeking to steal valuable information.

J.D. started the Spy vs Spy Blog to educate the public about the law, legislature, emerging technologies, new detection devices and espionage tactics. The blog also provides the public with a source for information should they become the victim of corporate, economic or industrial espionage.
