Toon Kerssemakers’s Summary

Companies and other organisations including government are never alone. To make a more professional and a better world they have to build cooperation with partners to serve the client better.
When building up cooperation goes wrong, you can bring severe damage to economy and social society.
A very important part of my professional experience has to do with this creating of cooperation.Many years by leading different organisations, in the Netherlands and abroad.
Connecting people, open networking, giving advice and being a consultant are the essentials in this job: not to forget an optimistic nature and humour
