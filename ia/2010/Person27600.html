Brennan Burkhart’s Summary

Brennan is Redkite’s Founder & Managing Principal, responsible for all aspects of client delivery.

Brennan has been working in Financial Services for over 10 years, and has unique expertise in delivering on-demand solutions to enterprise financial services clients, having led many of the world’s largest and most complex implementations of salesforce.com.

Prior to founding Redkite, Brennan was a co-founder of Fujitsu Consulting’s SaaS Practice (formerly OKERE) where he ran OKERE’s Professional Services. Additionally, as an engagement lead and solution architect, Brennan led the design and delivery of more than 30 Salesforce implementations.

Brennan has led engagements with clients including: Citi, ThomsonReuters, Barclays, TD Ameritrade, Merrill Lynch, MetLife, and Morgan Stanley. He has presented at industry events, and has provided insight on emerging cloud trends in the enterprise client space to research analysts from Garnter, Forrester, and IDC.

Brennan was previously a Vice President of JPMorgan’s Investment Bank. He has also worked for Ernst & Young’s Financial Services Consulting Practice.

Brennan Burkhart’s Specialties:

SaaS, Salesforce.com, CRM Strategy and Cloud Solution Architecture, CRM Vendor Evaluation and Package Selection, Implementation Methodology, Professional Services Management, Business Development and Global Account Management, Financial Services Technology
