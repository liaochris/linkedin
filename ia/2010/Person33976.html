Catherine Byers Breet’s Summary

Catherine Byers Breet is turning job hunting on its head. In fact, she is obsessed with helping people change their stripes to do what they love for a living.

After 13 years of professional recruiting (helping companies hire right the first time), she got tired of watching so many job seekers struggle to get jobs. The result? zCoach™ ... and the Job Hunt Coaching System. A revolutionary new approach to job hunt coaching, harnessing the power of VIDEO, AUDIO and the WEB.

After years of building simple, results-driven recruiting processes for companies, she's done the same for job seekers. And it's working. Through grass-roots community outreach and partnerships with large organizations, over 3000 job seekers agree: "You bring something none of the others do! Years of experience actually hiring people - and it shows."

Catherine is available for SPEAKING & CONSULTING (recruitment consulting, layoff relief, career development). Through ARBEZ, she offers workshops, webinars and customized online / video courses. She has spoken onsite at colleges & universities, state agencies, associations and over 29 faith communities. She's been quoted by the Associated Press, Star Tribune, Campus Career Counselor and others ... and has been a guest on radio and Fox 9 Morning News.
