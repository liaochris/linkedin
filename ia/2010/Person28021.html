Brian Pereira’s Summary

I have served the Indian Publishing industry (in the capacity of technology journalist) since September 1994 (15 years). Activities include reporting assignments in India and abroad, feature writing, online journalism, and custom publishing.
It has been a long and exciting journey from junior reporter to Editor.

Most recent accomplishment: Team lead for the launch of InformationWeek India (online) at www.informationweek.in.

Most cherished project: Computer easy, for Vogel Burda Communications, Munich. I set up and lead a team for Content Outsourcing.

Crossing new frontiers: Public speaking engagements and panel discussions. Editorial marketing. Microsoft Office certifications (Microsoft, USA).

Track record:
I've worked at companies like Infomedia 18 Ltd (CHIP), Indian Express Newspapers (Express Computer), Times of India (Times Computing), Jasubhai Digital Media (CHIP) and Aptech (a technology portal).

Before my journalism career I did a 5-year stint as a computer instructor and helped a student set up his own computer institute at Dadar (W). I also did projects such as a data-entry system for the CKP Bank at Dadar and trained employees at the Central Bank of India and PIL.

My passion for technology and journalism led me to set up my own website (now offline) called "Technology Journalist @ Work" around 1999 - 2000. It's just been relaunched: http://www.tjatwork.com

My goal in life:
To achieve perfection in everything I do, and do it with passion and integrity.

Other passions:
Social Work, Personality Development, Personal Organization & Time Management, Personal Finance, Career Development, Civil Aviation, Music, Traveling.
