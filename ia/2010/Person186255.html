Damon Abramson’s Summary

Harmony Technologies is a versatile Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Holistic Internet Marketing company with 18 years of expertise in technical sales and web application design. We also have 10 years of internet marketing, SEO friendly web design, SEO and Pay-Per-Click experience. As a specialty, we focus on the nexus between e-commerce platform integration and SEO, the accompanying transitional linking strategies along with the whole of Enterprise Search. Our consultants have produced a wide range of e-commerce applications and solutions.

We have proven technical expertise, capability, and experience in designing and conducting interactive marketing presentations to an audience of IT and non-IT professionals; particularly "C" level personnel. So we make the perfect partners if you're an agency, perhaps a traditional agency, looking to get more involved in the interactive space.

Our extensive Internet Marketing, SEO (Search Engine Optimization), SEM (Search Engine Marketing), keyword placement, SDLC, agile, and scrum Project Management skills and experience makes us excellent lead generating and revenue generating consultants. We can handle the demand generation side of the equation, as well as the demand harvesting side. And bring efficiencies to both.

Due to our years of program and project management experience, we have extensive budgeting skills, communication skills, meeting facilitation, organizational management, and technical presentation skills in house as well.

An often overlooked niche in e-commerce is how the products for sale will rank in the search engines after a re-platforming is completed. Harmony Technologies has extensive real SEO experience in bridging these thought and activity gaps and can bring proven strategies to bear.
