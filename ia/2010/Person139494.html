Maarten den Braber’s Summary

Familiar with the innovative scene in mobile and tech, I am focused on applying these ideas in healthcare and other social sectors. I particularly enjoy connecting people online and offline!

I am doing this by organizing events (on mobile, tech and healthcare), speaking and writing on subjects like Health2.0 and mHealth (with Nexthealth) and creating new products and services for healthcare.

Furthermore I am employed as freelance Event Engineer providing technology infrastructure, management and advice for events and other temporary setups.
