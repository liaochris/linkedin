About ENIGMAI:

ENIGMAI delivers SaaS (Software as a Service) business management solutions that are easy to use and has a low price tag.

Enigmai Business Suite (EBS) is a web based application designed for Small to Medium Businesses, to easily manage all their employee related information and activities in a dynamic workforce environment.

The first available major module of the solution is a shift management application. The application provides tools for creating and managing employees' work shifts, continuously updating and distributing the shift schedules and communicating the changes to all relevant employees and managers.

The application was designed with unique business intelligence to handle most tasks automatically without the need for intervention by managers and team leaders, thus dramatically reducing the time spent for scheduling, conflict resolutions and updating,
