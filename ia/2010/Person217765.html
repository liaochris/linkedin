Jacob Young’s Summary

Jacob Young, President and CEO of Young Enterprises LLC, is way out of the box and hopes to take everyone with him. Soon to embark on a nationwide tour aptly named, “There Is No Box”, Jacob Young hopes to change the way the world views business. "Just thinking outside the box is not enough {and that] it’s about believing that there are no boundaries, that opens the door to a whole new world."

Young believes, that “giving back” is the key component to his innovative business model, creating a synergy between business and community and Young Social Media, his media consulting and development firm specializing in viral and organic optimization is doing just that. Fifty percent of all net profits from Young Social Media projects are to be donated to worthy causes and charities.

Jacob Young brings over a decade of experience in cutting edge web development, branding, marketing, promoting and business consulting. Jacob has two books scheduled for release this fall, discussing the not so new, yet still emerging field of “Social Media Optimization,” and the dangers of email distribution lists.

Young recently became a registered author at the Writers Guild of America (WGA) and is a Certified Expert in multiple disciplines including non profit marketing, internet marketing, and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) by Expert Rating, an ISO 9001:2008 Company.
