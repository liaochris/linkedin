Florent Genoux’s Summary

I graduated from CERAM Business School in Sophia-Antipolis in 2003, with a focus on Innovating companies. I first worked for a high-tech start-up before joining France Telecom as a Project Officer to work and test new innovating services for mobile telephony. I also worked on technology and competitive watch studies, to strengthen my expertise in the telecommunications field. Then I joined Sigma Orionis in 2003, and have worked on various projects in the field of the Connected Home (through Sigma’s Homega Research division) and ICT at large.

I now act as a Project Manager with a focus on EU projects, including namely
- Coordination of marketing & dissemination activities within research projects
- Competitive and technological watch in the ICT field
- Production of market research and industry reports
- Organisation of business events
- Writing of proposals in response to the EC call for projects
