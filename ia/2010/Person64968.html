Dalene Stuteville’s Summary

Dalene was born in Australia in 1972, as a U.S. Citizen born abroad. Her father a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine worked with the United States Government and the British Crown as an inspector for the Department of Agriculture. At times they lived in the metropolitan city centers, like Melbourne, Perth, Darwin, Bendigo, and Queensland and others were in the outback near Alice Springs with the Aborigines where they got their first taste of what it's like to live in a surprisingly different culture. They returned to the U.S & each year followed the Veterinary Seminar Circuit as much an excuse to travel and see some corner of the country as to continue her father's education.

Today, she and her husband live in the Midwest with their 4 beautiful and creative children, Cayla 14, Grace 10, Jackson, 9 and Nicole (born on Valentine's Day) is an independent 5. Dalene's true passion is the theater. Writing and directing compelling drama with a greater purpose is why she feels blessed to be a Writer / Director for Granger Community Church in Granger, Indiana.

Encore began in 2006 as a Real Estate Investment Group supporting economic and political change. In 2007, nominated by Senator Tom Cole to serve as Honorary Chairman of the Business Advisory Council for the NRCC (National Republican Congressional Committee) Dalene was honored to received the National Leadership Award for contribution to Business Tax Reform and Fiscal Responsibility. In June, 2008 she received a Congressional Medal of Distinction for Outstanding Meritorious Service to the Republican party. Committed to change, her belief is that investors should become a greater part of the global solution in our economy, one person at a time.
