Cameron Toth’s Summary

Cameron Toth has fourteen years of experience in bringing people together to be educated in a fun, exciting, and entertaining environment. Immediately after High School, Cameron, started working at SBI College and Career Preparatory Institute (CCPI), which afforded him the opportunity to develop his skills as a meeting coordinator. He worked with the director of the program to create educational workshops and meetings, which included multi-day seminars that would culminate in elaborate productions and performances.

Subsequently, Cameron shifted his career path to the Food Service Industry where he was quickly promoted to a management position. As a manager, he gained more experience in creating marketing plans, as well as event design and implementation.

For four years, Cameron worked in the field of Medical Education Meeting Planning through The Scienomics Group but has left Medical Education to return to where it all started at CCPI. Now with close to fifteen years of events experience under his belt Cameron returns full time to the SBI College and Career Preparatory Institute program at A. Philip Randolph Campus High School in Harlem.

With a continued dedication to volunteering and programs for the social good Cameron has jumped right into the Program Manager role helping to grow and develop the organization with smart computer assisted tracking of students and sophisticated social media fund raising.

Cameron’s strengths are his strong work ethic and his attention to detail. He is looking forward to new challenges that will sharpen his skills in managing staff and meeting planning.

Over the last year Cameron Toth has taken his talent for social media and education to the stage. He spoke at BizBash in the "Twitter Lounge" November 2009. He also presented for MPI WestField Chapter in December 2009 that was webcasted courtesy of Sonic Foundry Media Site.
