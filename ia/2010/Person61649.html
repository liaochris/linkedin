Ed Augustine’s Summary

I am the Founder and Managing Partner of The Pathfinder Group. My primary work is to generate revenue for cities by developing marketing partnerships and sponsorships with corporations and to identify major untapped revenue streams and public/private partnership opportunities.

Since 1997, I have been directly involved in generating over $30 million in revenue for cities around the U.S. I designed the first and most successful Corporate Partnership Program in the country for the City of San Diego.

I also have considerable experience working on environmental and sustainability issues. I was an environmental attorney, General Counsel for the Georgia Conservancy, a large environmental organization, Environmental Coordinator for the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Director of Environmental Programs for the National Football League for ten Super Bowls (1994-2003) and part of a team of environmental planners for the 1994 Winter Olympics in Lillehammer, Norway.

I also served as Special Assistant to Atlanta Mayor Andrew Young and held other positions in Atlanta City Government (1980-1982).

I live in Atlanta with my wife, Judith, and our Golden Retriever, Lulu. Our son, Jed, is a third year student and the University of Chicago. He is spending the 2008-2009 academic year studyng in Paris.

My website is www.pathgrp.com
My e-mail is ea@pathgrp.com
