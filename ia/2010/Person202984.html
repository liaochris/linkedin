Thomas J. West’s Summary

I have taught in grades 3-12 in public schools in both New Jersey and Pennsylvania. I have taught band, choral, strings, and general music in all of these settings. My primary focus is secondary instrumental. I have designed, written, and instructed marching bands for over 15 years.

I have also taught and performed in jazz bands, choral groups, and contemporary a cappella groups. I have several compositions for concert band available and have successfully published one title with more to come. I am also an active adjudicator with USSBA and Cavalcade of Bands as well as an active private instructor.

I maintain an active website featuring music education articles, blogs, resources, and practice tips. In these articles, I combine traditional music teaching techniques with the latest research on neurology and mind mapping.

I have used technology throughout my career and am currently finding new applications in music teaching for Finale, Sonar, Smart Music, and VOIP applications.

My current CV is available at http://bit.ly/1cX4i

My goals are to share my music instructional knowledge with as wide an audience as possible
