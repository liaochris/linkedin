Doug Fitzgerald’s Summary

President: Boomer-Living.com and Key Life Benefits, LLC

Boomer-Living.com is a leading internet resource destination for baby boomers. BLC, using the collective wisdom of top experts in the country, offers tips, tools, life-long learning and reliable solutions to the challenges associated with living and aging in the 21st Century. Focus areas include Career Management, Financial planning, Health and Fitness, Travel, Arts and Entertainment, Sustainable Living and Baby Boomers who are “Making a Difference”.

Key Life Benefits, LLC is a comprehensive supplemental benefits company serving the needs of the entire community. With over 90 products and services, KLB custom designs affordable benefit packages for individuals, businesses, groups and associations.
