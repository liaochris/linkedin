Sheila Brown’s Summary

Contract Recruiter specializing in Entertainment and Digital Media recruiting. I have over 14 years of corporate, executive and contract recruiting experience in the Entertainment, Interactive/Internet Media, Video Game, Advertising, Hospitality, Retail, Home Entertainment, E-Commerce and Consumer Products industries.

I have experience with developing staffing plans, employee relations, succession planning, legal compliance, Unions, employee training & development, diversity awareness, team building, administering performance appraisals, compensation, ADC payroll, worker’s compensation and benefits administration.
