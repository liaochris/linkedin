Scott Case’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, adventuresome, multi-talented finance and operational executive with an eye for best-practices and efficiency. I've spent most of the past decade in a variety of "CFO+" operating roles for Technology companies -- strategic roles that include M&A work, significant capital raising, and simplifying operational complexity. I am currently CFO of First Virtual, Tom Siebel's family investment office and related foundations.

Prior to these executive operating roles, I was a Partner with technology venture firms, focused on semiconductor, communications, and infrastructure companies. At GE Equity I also opened & ran their San Francisco office. (I expect to live in the SF Bay area for the remainder of my career...) My investment returns for all my venture investments were good: 3.7x on $53M invested, 143% IRR.

My initial professional training was with McKinsey & Co and JP Morgan, both great firms with terrific cultures, filled with exceptional people.

Scott Case’s Specialties:

Significant international experience in emerging markets: established sales & engineering offices in China, helped manage a large (80+ person) engineering office in India, worked extensively in Tel Aviv Israel, and lived and worked in Mexico City for a year.
