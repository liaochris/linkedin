Timothy Bachman’s Summary

Tim provides the vision and strategic overview for implementing integrated communications and merchandising programs into "prepared" retail and workplace environments. He helps clients visualize ideas, create process, and inspire change within their organizations.

His 30 years of experience in design thinking, strategic planning, marketing, and visual communications includes designs for knowledge-rich retail environments and workplaces, corporate brand programs, graphic/interactive multimedia design, and merchandising displays. Tim has worked with financial services, manufacturing, specialty retail and healthcare clients.

As President of Bachman ID, Timothy Bachman’s company is a nationally recognized leader in creating innovative design solutions that have been widely adopted in numerous retail and corporate industries.

Prior to founding Bachman ID, Tim established and operated the graphics and communications divisions of Nexus America and Retail Planning Associates, both international retail design and planning firms.

He earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Visual Communication Design from The Ohio State University. Tim participated in the young professional Rotary Exchange Program to Australia. As an active member of Design Management Institute, he has served on the Advisory Council for 18 years. Tim is on the Board of Trustees for the Ohio Arts League, former Vice President of the Columbus Society of Communicating Arts and past Commodore of the Hoover Sailing Club.

Tim's goals are to create experience-rich environments and communication that inspire users and visitors. His goal is to position design thinking as a strategic activity in his client's culture. Building a culture of design allows innovation to flourish. Innovation is a necessary activity for any major enterprise that wishes to remain relevant and profitable.
