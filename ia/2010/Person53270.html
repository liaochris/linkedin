Dean Tanella’s Summary

Dean Tanella's professional goals involve taking full advantage of the skills that God has given him, and focusing those skills on providing for clients products and services of only the highest integrity. It is Dean Tanella's sincere intention that with the products and services that he delivers, Dean Tanella's clients can go about their investment planning with assurance and peace of mind.

Since 1983, Dean Tanella has held the title of registered professional in the securities business. Dean Tanella has held a seat on the board of numerous public and private companies, and currently holds the post of President and General partner of Safe Harbor Capital Management and of HarborLight Capital Management, LLC. Concurrently, Dean Tanella has served as EVP of Capital Markets at GunnAllen Financial in Tampa, Florida since December 2004.

Dean Tanella grew up in a close-knit rural community in northwestern New Jersey, where he first began his dedication to competitive sports. Through his athleticism Dean Tanella won scholarships, awards, and a taste for competition. Dean Tanella's competitiveness led him to achieve great things in business, putting himself through Princeton by working four jobs, where he graduated with honors. Dean Tanella ran the Boston office for S.G. Warburg four months before his graduation from Harvard Business School. Dean Tanella went on after graduation to start his own fund-of-hedge funds business in 1999, and has recently accepted a post at GunnAllen Financial in Tampa, Florida. Dean Tanella's non-work-related passions in life are his wife, his kids, his church, fitness, and travel.
