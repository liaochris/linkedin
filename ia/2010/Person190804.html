David P. Lindquist II’s Summary

I am looking for new and exciting opportunities in Broadcasting, Arts, and Entertainment in the field of Production and/or Management. I have an extensive background in Live Television and Theatre, including Location Production, Lighting, Staging, Power, Videography, and, most recently nine years of Studio and Personnel Management.

I am also interested in expanding my horizons by working for corporations and companies on the cutting edge of technology, where I can offer my creativity in a number of capacities; including problem solving, think tanks, and team leadership.

I have a proven track record of being a supportive team leader, self motivated and a quick learner, and the ability to offer creative problem solutions while executing workloads at a consistently high level.

As a Studio Floor Manager of QVC's 24-hour live broadcast, I led a team of Backstage Studio Assistants and support personnel to execute the on-air selling strategies put in place by the Producers and Sales Managers, while meeting the needs of external customers, product vendors and on-air guests. While managing the studio operations of one of the largest television studios on the East Coast I directed talent and staff, operated camera and lighting equipment, offered quick solutions to unexpected issues, and maintained a safe and productive environment.

Cosmic Theatre Works is my studio for the independent projects for which I have worked on over the last 10 yrs. I am continuously looking for opportunites to express myself creatively and to collaborate with other like-minded artistic individuals and companies.

In the meanwhile, I am pursuing a possible missed calling in Teaching. I am passionate about providing a positive and thorough experience in education and parenting to my children and want to share this with as many children as I can reach.
