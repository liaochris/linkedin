Kim Ficera’s Summary

I am an award-winning writer, editor, Web content manager and social media specialist. I’ve authored one book, contributed to three others, and have written over 1,000 articles, columns, celebrity interviews and blog posts on everything from pop culture to significant socio-political and media issues.
