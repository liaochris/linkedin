Jonathan S Cook’s Summary

I am a third year doctoral candidate studying physiology at the Penn State College of Medicine. I examine how melatonin, a hormone that signals day vs. night, alters orthostatic reflexes and vascular blood flow in humans. My goal is to expand upon my analytical, leadership, and communication skills obtained during my PhD program by entering the business field.
