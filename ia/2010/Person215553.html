Bill Ward’s Summary

I've been recruiting for over 10 years and have worked in both contingency and retained search practices. I excel at helping clients understand what it takes to make successful, long-term hires. I enjoy working with candidates that desire a collaborative, consultative relationship that is based on honesty and focused on long term career success.

I am a senior member the Accounting and Finance practice for GMR, a retainer based executive search practice that has been in business for over 33 years. I am responsible for business development and full cycle recruiting.
