    ESRI Ireland

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2002 — Present (8 years 3 months)

    ESRI Ireland, with offices in both Dublin & Belfast, is the exclusive local partner of ESRI Inc, the global leader in Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

    In collaboration with its customers, the company is responsible for designing and building some of the largest and most advanced GIS systems in Ireland.

    Employing nearly 30 professionals, the company provides solutions for business, society and the environment with clients from Government departments, Government agencies, Local Government, Utilities, Transport, Distribution and Logistics and the GIS Professional Community.

    Established in March 2002, ESRI Ireland is the largest GIS company in Ireland and Northern Ireland as well as the only Authorised source of ESRI software and the only Accredited provider of ESRI Training.

    It is a member of the Irish Organisation for Geographic Information (IRLOGI) and the Association of Geographic Information, Northern Ireland.

    ESRI Ireland Supports ESRI Inc’s mission to support users in the successful implementation of GIS, to provide the most robust GIS software programs in the world and continuous development of technology and to conduct meaningful GIS application projects that push technology and demonstrate the innovative use of GIS.
