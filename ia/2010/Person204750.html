Tish Troutman - LION’s Summary

Tish Troutman is making a living through giving. She is the Founder of Parents for Safe Technology. She is Managing Director for eWomenNetwork, Dallas, Texas.

She has extensive background in Entrepreneurial and business sales. She is also a former Executive Assistant to the CEO, North America Pharmaceuticals, for Hoechst Marion Roussel, now Sanofi-Aventis.

Troutman’s career with HMR and its predecessors companies spanned more than 17 years. While attending college at Oklahoma State University, she pursued her degree in Human Resources. During that time, she worked for The CIT Group, a finance company, as a Human Resources Assistant. She was involved with all aspects of HR, as well as, the Chamber of Commerce, United Way, and the OKC blood institute to help provide grief management during the OKC Bombing.

In 1996, Troutman took a position at Grafton Staffing where she ultimately became a Sales Recruiter. She had clients such as; Applebee’s; Anheuser-Busch, Bank One, Cigna, Countrywide, Doubletree, Farmers Insurance, MCI, Sprint, State Farm, and Toys R Us, just to name a few.

She has been involved with several fundraising events. Including the 60 mile 3-day for Breast Cancer where she was captain of an 11 person team that raised nearly $25,000. She also participates in Volleyfest which raised $125,000 for Breast Cancer Research. She has been the Captain of her Tennis Teams and Director of neighborhood groups.

Troutman is a volunteer for her Children’s activities, as well as a school fashion show fundraiser where she was a “Model for Charity”.

She has been the Treasurer for The Wilmington Court Homeowners Association, BNI Leadership Team, a Rockenbaugh School Volunteer and a member of the Southlake Tennis Center. She is very active in her community.
