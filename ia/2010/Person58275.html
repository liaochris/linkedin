Donna Gilliland’s Summary

DONNA GILLILAND: Entrepreneur | Webcast Specialist| Social Media Trainer | Consultant & Coach| Microsoft Office Specialist

My biggest passion: HELPING PEOPLE LEARN to use technology.

I opened my company over 5 years ago with a focus on corporate productivity training in desktop applications and presentation skills training.

MOSTraining continues to add courses to help companies and individuals meet the challenges presented in this changing world of technology

THE SOCIAL MEDIA CHALLENGE: Social media is changing how we do business.

Linkedin, Facebook, Twitter are among the more popular that people want to master. Each of these is a powerful tool that has many features. It takes training to learn the many features and then develop a strategy to maximize these social media tools to grow business.

THE ONLINE PRESENTER: Have you decided to expand your reach by conducting meetings and training online? The learning curve is two-fold; 1) learning the platform 2) the techniques to effectively present online.

It is really an exciting time for all of us. You can overcome the learning curve with the right training.

I am an award winning instructor who has been empowering people with knowledge since computers used DOS based operating systems.

You can learn how to maximize the power of technology and have fun doing so.
