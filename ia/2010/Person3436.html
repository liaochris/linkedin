Adria Richards’s Summary

Adria is an Organic Technology Consultant who helps business owners generate more revenue in less time.

She works with individuals and organizations to help them master technology.

Adria offers technical support, website development and training services to clients around the world. She holds MCSA, MCDST and A+ certifications and is currently pursing her Comptia CTT (Certified Technical Trainer).

Combining 10 years of experience with a passion for empowering people, Adria turns complex topics into everyday examples that make technology fun.

In June 2009, she launched http://freshworkshops.com to provide live, online training which focuses on provide hands-on exercises rather than having people sit in a room and watch a Powerpoint presentation.
