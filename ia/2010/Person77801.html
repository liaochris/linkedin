Greg Linch’s Summary

I’m a storyteller who uses several platforms. With a background in writing and reporting, I’m as comfortable composing a 1,000-word in-depth story for print as I am shooting and editing a three-minute video for the Web. Or a 140-character update on Twitter.

I also have experience with audio, photography, Web design, online production, online community management and copyediting.

When reporting I don’t think, “How can I write a great story?” but instead, “How can I tell a great story?”

Greg Linch’s Specialties:

Writing, reporting and editing / Community management and engagement / Multimedia reporting (video, audio and photo) and editing (Final Cut Pro, iMovie, Soundtrack Pro, Audacity, Photoshop) / Blogging / Video livestreaming / Podcasting / Web production / Community management / Project management / AP style / (X)HTML and CSS (hand-coded) / Basic JavaScript / Basic Flash
