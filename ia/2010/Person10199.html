Amy Laine’s Summary

Skilled brand marketing professional with proven experience in creating and executing revenue-generating integrated marketing solutions for media companies, major marketers, independent business owners and non-profit organizations. Opened the marketing consultancy hyperlaine (www.hyperlaine.com) to work with small business owners and startups in NYC, along with media clients. End goal is to help independent business owners succeed, and to make sure neighborhoods stay vibrant and alive with choice and opportunity.

Partial client list: Peter Hurley Photography, Atlantic Media Company, Daily Candy, Chrysanthemum Partners, CHERP.

Amy Laine’s Specialties:

NYC hyperlocal marketing, hyperlocal media, integrated marketing and promotions strategy and execution, new business development, social media, events, coaching, mentoring, copywriting
