Tibor Tallos’s Summary

Tibor is a well known thought leader in Intellectual Property consulting & monetization. With over 20 years of progressive leadership experience in the technology industry, combining knowledge of strategy and intellectual property (IP). Tibor has counseled a wide variety of emerging growth, private and publicly held companies on intellectual property management and patent licensing matters. He has assisted IP professionals in reducing the cost involved in any kind of patent research and analysis.

As a CEO/President at E-data Corp (Freeny Patent fame), the Company successfully obtained patent licenses with major European and US companies. All positions included duties related to licensing; analyses and assessment of new technologies/patents, strategic marketing of licensing, patent mining, technology assessment and analysis for patenting or licensing purposes. E-data Corp existed for the sole purpose of licensing its intellectual property holdings.

Tibor also has extensive experience in detailed analyses of patents and/or potentially patentable technologies; formulation of opinion as to long and/or short term commercialization viability; addressing the potential infringement and freedom to operate issues; providing a list of possible partnership targets. Also, analyzed and predicted potential patent litigation outcomes/probabilities for money market managers/funds holding shares in publicly trade companies involved in patent litigation.

Tibor also understands the creation of the patent sales activity from the ground up, generating a new revenue stream from underutilized corporate intellectual property.

He also has a proven track record of creating profitable growth opportunities in emerging and established markets through strong partnerships, and delivering innovations that produce economic value for customers and investors.

Tibor Tallos’s Specialties:

Managing aggressive global PATENT ASSERTION campaigns. All aspects of licensing; commercialization, negotiation & drafting licenses and related agreements, monitoring compliance/royalty payments, patent/technology valuation, initiating & closing deals; identifying and licensing infringers, managing legal process. Expert analysis of intellectual property litigation 'potential outcomes': (ongoing, pre-trial) for money market managers and private investors/groups, institutions.
