  • Environmental Programs Coordinator

    City of Thousand Oaks

    (Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; Government Administration industry)

    May 1990 — June 1999 (9 years 2 months)

    I was originally hired by the City to implement its recycling, trash and hazardous waste program. The program grew into an award winning model program that excelled at all levels and was broadened into other environmental programs and policies. Over the years, I implemented an electric vehicle program (until the industry took all the cars back), wrote and managed over a $1million in city grants in numerous fields and implemented many high profile and successful projects. I serving the City in both roles, I was ask to take on more emergency management tasks, including the establishment of the City's formal program.

  • Community Services Coordinator

    City of Simi Valley

    (Government Agency; 201-500 employees; Government Administration industry)

    June 1986 — May 1990 (4 years )

    I was first hired to manage a federally funded employment training and placement program for the City. After exceeding program goals, I was promoted to manage both the Youth Council Program and Neigborhood Council Program. I served as the City's liasion to the general public and private sector on a wide variety of issues that required interfacing with the City Manager, City Council and all City Departments. The program included five adult boards and one youth advisory board that each met once per month. I served as a parliamentarian for all the board meetings, prepared the agendas, minutes, reports and learned alot about how local government operates.
