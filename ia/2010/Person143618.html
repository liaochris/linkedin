Michael Szabo II’s Summary

I am currently the Director of Programming for the Undergraduate Student Government at Kent State University. This is an elected position as voted on by the undergraduate student body at Kent State University. I was the first Director of Programming under the newly created Undergraduate Student Government (formerly the Undergraduate Student Senate). I am currently in my second consecutive term. I am responsible for overseeing the duties of the USG Programming Board which is composed of 5 members. My overall duty is to plan, coordinate, and promote up to (5) University-wide events throughout the school year. I work with a budget of around $300,000 to promote these events. I report directly to the Student Organization Advisor in the Center for Student Involvement. Tasks associated with the job include but are not limited to; searching for talent, contacting talent agents, reserving venues for events, contract and rider negotiations, marketing events, promoting events, requesting sponsorships, working with tour managers and artist managers, overseeing production of lights/sound/staging, day-of-show management, hospitality management, and other tasks involved in the promotion and production of a concert.
