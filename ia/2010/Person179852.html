Ryan Pulliam’s Summary

I am a branded entertainment expert and digital media guru, focused mostly on new/interactive media and strategic marketing roles.

My expertise in creative development, media production, brand management, and strategic marketing have led to an extensive and diverse clientele list, including Lionsgate Digital, ESPN College Football, Popchips, Arista Networks, award-winning photographer, adventurer, and environmental advocate, Sebastian Copeland, singer/songwriter/producer Jem, singer/songwriter/actor Joseph George, rock band 'The Gracious Few,' Aspen Fashion Week, Fashion Los Angeles, The Global Summit, and The Maybach Foundation among others.

As a gatekeeper to large networks in the entertainment, technology, and new media sectors, I consistently seek out unique niche opportunities for small to mid-size personal and professional brands and their entry into online media and television via cable networks and digital/social media.

Additionally, I advise nonprofit organizations on effective social media and viral marketing strategies, as well as corporate sustainability and environmental initiatives. I am also a contributing editor for various online travel publications, and a ghost writer for customer case-studies in the technology sector.

I have a well established network across the board and actively participate in many conversations to be a good source for finding new or interesting business opportunities. I network the deals and find the synergy.

Prior to my consulting endeavors, I worked in broadcast television as an associate producer for ESPN/ABC College Football and Altitude Sports & Entertainment. I traveled with the #1 football crew throughout the U.S., and worked with on-air talents Bob Griese, Brad Nessler, and Lynn Swann. While working with Altitude Sports & Entertainment, I co-produced an emmy award winning one-hour special on the Starz International Film Festival, including a red-carpet interview for the premiere event of the movie Ray.
