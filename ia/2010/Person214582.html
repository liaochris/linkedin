Wendy Brache’s Summary

As co-creator of the Sales Force Branding Program, I help telecom and high-tech corporations differentiate their sales executives from the competition.

As a corporate B2B Social Media Specialist, I help small to medium-sized telecom and high-tech companies migrate from traditional marketing methods to Web 2.0 and Social Media strategy.

My agency's customers have specific budgets and time frames. They need a consultant with excellent project management skills, clear and concise coaching and training methods, and a deep understanding of priorities in order to get the biggest bang for their buck.

That's where Access Marketing Company fits in.

The ultimate goal is to generate, track and increase the number of leads our clients receive from online marketing activities. Everything we do is with this goal in mind, and everything we do must have a proven ROI. Each client is different, but some methods have proven most popular and successful, including:

Sales Force Branding.

Marketing Automation Programs.

Corporate Reference Programs.

LinkedIn Profile Building, Messaging, and Training.

Press Release Programs.

Corporate and CEO Blogging.

Newsletter and Email Campaigns.

PPC/Google AdWords Campaigns.

Contact us to discuss how we can help you and your company at www.getmoreaccess.com.
