Ken Grammer’s Summary

We are opening an indoor electric karting center in Danville, VA. The center will be located 20 minutes from Virginia International Raceway and about an hour each from Martinsville and South Boston speedways.

Member of the Motorsports Industrial Advisory Board (IAB) at Old Dominion University.
