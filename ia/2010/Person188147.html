Shawn DuBravac, CFA’s Summary

Provides economic analysis to association and technology industry leaders regarding future economic activity and the relative health of the consumer electronics (CE) industry. Also contributes research into technology trends that underpin the industry. Widely published author and frequent speaker on topics related to finance, economics and technology.

Active member of the National Association of Business Economists (NABE) and the National Business Economic Issues Council (NBEIC) and currently serves as President of the CFA Society of Washington, DC.
