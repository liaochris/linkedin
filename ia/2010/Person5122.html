Alan J. Gotcher’s Summary

Dr. Gotcher is pursuing a senior management position in a private or public company that is product based, has manufacturing operations and intellectual property with key elements of the position including: a major influencing role in strategic vision and direction, managing innovation, growth and change agendas and providing significant organizational leadership.

Dr. Gotcher has 20+ years of leadership experience as a senior operating officer; “C” level executive and Board member in multi-national public and private companies having annual revenues from $10 million to over $3.3 billion. In the past four years, Dr. Gotcher has raised over $90 million from public and private equity markets, testified twice before the U.S. Senate on advanced emerging technologies and provided expert witness testimony in intellectual property litigations.

Dr. Gotcher serves on the Board of Directors for Clean Tech companies with product, business development, manufacturing and IP management needs.

Email contact: agotcher@agotcher.com

Dr. Gotcher was previously President, Chief Executive Officer & Director for Altair Nanotechnologies Inc. (Nasdaq: ALTI) a nano-material based Clean Tech company developing unique power & energy storage products, where he focused on company vision and strategy, business development and forging partnerships with Fortune 500 companies. Dr. Gotcher co-founded and co-managed IdeaSpring, a venture capital investment firm.

Dr. Gotcher worked for two Fortune 500 companies. He served as Senior Vice President, Manufacturing & Technology and Chief Technology Officer for Avery Dennison Corporation (NYSE: AVY). Before joining Avery Dennison, Dr. Gotcher held a variety of R&D and management positions at Raychem Corporation prior to Raychem’s acquisition by Tyco (NYSE: TYC).

Alan is a graduate of the University of California, Irvine, with Ph.D. and B.A. degrees in Chemistry. Dr. Gotcher serves on the Board of Directors of Clean Energy Inc.
