Mike Shipman’s Summary

I'm a former wildlife biologist now full time freelance photographer and writer (but I also freelance wildlife work - fieldwork, writing and researching ESAs, BAs, forest management plans, etc.).

My photography focus is commercial (annual reports, corporate portraits, interior/exterior, products, industrial), editorial (environmental, agriculture, travel, general interest), education (photography classes and workshops locally and throughout the western U.S.), and fine art.

I do shoot commissioned portrait work and model/actor portfolios.

I can travel anywhere in the world. I love what I do and enjoy working/collaborating with clients.

If you would like to work with someone who will give your project undivided attention and the quality it deserves, drop me a line and let's get together and start something.

I'm also available for group presentations.
