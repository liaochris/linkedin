John Paul Stephens’s Summary

Instructor and researcher. Experience in psychological laboratory experimentation, field experimentation, large organizational survey design and delivery, as well as ethnographic research. Focus on how interdependence and relationality are performed by organizational actors.

John Paul Stephens’s Specialties:

Teaching at the undergraduate and graduate level
Research laboratory management
Statistical experience using SPSS and LISREL
