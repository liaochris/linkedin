Nick Cartmell’s Summary

Cartmell Design is a vibrant and enthusiastic design practice, with specifically focused resources in the areas of Packaging, Brand Design, Corporate and Tourism projects. A key area of focus for the practice is with the Food & Beverage industry. We provide creative & consulting services to businesses around the globe, with clients in England, Southeast Asia, South Pacific, USA and the Middle East. We offer a very professional, personal and very affordable service suited to these leaner economic times.

Nick Cartmell’s Specialties:

Having worked with a variety of industries, Nick brings a wealth of experience and know-how to his clients. The business maintains strong ties with the South Pacific, where Nick spent many years working with different design businesses and tourism development projects.
