Fine Edge Landscape Design’s Summary

Fine Edge Landscape Design was founded in 1992 as a basic landscape design company in the Charlotte, NC market. As our knowledge and client base grew from year to year, we began adding paver walkways, patios and limited stonework. Those jobs were still very basic and while they all looked great, there were still many other items we could be designing into the grand scheme.
Low voltage lighting design / build and some minimal water feature installations were one of the next steps for Fine Edge Design. Although we don't receive many inquiries for elaborate water features, we make sure to give all clients an optional lighting plan for every design.
The patios and outdoor areas started getting bigger and we realized exactly where our company was headed. We looked for competent, professional sub-contractors in the irrigation, electrical, concrete, fencing & pool construction industries.
Having more than 8 years in the construction business, I knew all of the basics for these fields but there was just not enough time to handle eveything in-house.
We now offer complete landscape design & build capabilities that center on custom outdoor living areas. This includes everything you may see when you walk out your door such as:
* Raised patios (stone, brick, concrete ).
* Outdoor fireplaces ( gas or woodburning ).
* Outdoor kitchens.
* Retaining walls, stone walls, columns.
* Custom woodworking ( pergolas, arbors ).
* Complete drainage solutions - Very crucial in the design stages.
* All shrub, tree, perennial planting using only quality organic soil and fertilizers.
* Low voltage landscape lighting.
* Irrigation services.
* Decorative concrete.

Fine Edge Landscape Design’s Specialties:

Custom landscape design, outdoor fireplaces, outdoor kitchen areas, landscape lighting, new patios and patio extensions.
