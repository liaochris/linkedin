Jonnie Jensen’s Summary

I'm an internet marketing consultant. I help business owners who know they need to improve their internet marketing activities but don't know where to start.

I provide practical consulting and do the work. I work as your digital marketing manager and put the internet marketing strategy in place. Often these skills are missing from businesses. I solve that problem. I help businesses ensure that their internet marketing is in line with their business objectives and working hard to find them new customers, increase awareness and drive sales.

You need to go beyond your website today to achieve success. I'll reach out using email, social media (like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube and others), SEO, a blog, online PR and other techniques. This will improve your search rankings and will also put you in front of many other people in targeted communities across the web.

I help make internet marketing simple and I help you succeed.
