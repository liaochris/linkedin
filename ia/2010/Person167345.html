Rachel Melia’s Summary

I am a marketing consultant/contractor specializing in digital marketing - including online advertising, social media marketing, and mobile marketing. I do strategy development as well as implementation and management of marketing programs to help companies achieve their business objectives.

I have over ten years experience in online marketing and four years in mobile marketing. I worked as the media lead at San Francisco advertising agencies including Anderson Lembke and Carat Interactive on clients including Microsoft, Electronic Arts, Kodak Mobile, Palm, Tivo, and Siebel. I have been consulting for the past four years for a variety of consumer and b-to-b clients.

Services include:
1. Online advertising: media planning, creative development management, campaign implementation and management, analysis
2. Social media: strategy, implementation, management of Facebook, blog, YouTube programs
3. Website development management
4. Email/newsletter programs
5. Mobile marketing: mobile advertising, user activity and retention
6. Other: offline advertising (print, outdoor, street teams, direct mail), PR agency management
