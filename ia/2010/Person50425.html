David Coughlin’s Summary

My goal is to build a leading UK property investment business over the next 5 years. I intend to thoroughly enjoy living as I do this, and I look forward to retiring into the sun as early as possible!

I have over 15 years' varied business experience working within 'Blue Chip' companies such as United Biscuits and Mars/Masterfoods. I had a fantastic time and gained bags of experience working alongside truly exceptional people on amazing projects. As absolutely great as it was, I could not wait for the opportunity to start and run my own business!

I left Mars in 2005 to focus full time on building my property portfolio. Business and opportunities subsequently completely mushroomed! I now have a portfolio in excess of 100 properties and I am managing director of my own property investment businesses - www.hbfinvestments.co.uk and www.homesboughtfast.co.uk.

I am also an active investor in several other businesses. I thoroughly enjoy investing and working with great people who I known for some time and who offer exciting business opportunities. Working with talented and successful people, who really get on with each other, and who are totally motivated about their businesses, generates the most amazing buzz. The spin-off rewards and opportunities are simply endless!

If you would like to learn about successful property investing, and if I can help you and/or your business in any way, please do not hesitate to contact me.
