Barbara Toorens’s Summary

Barbara has worked in the technology marketing space since 2005. Before starting her own consulting company, she was an event planner and registration coordinator with CRG Events, managing projects related to partner involvement and event expos. Currently, she works with Microsoft, managing field relations and coordinating strategic marketing projects related to Microsoft's Platform and Developer products including Visual Studio and Expression.

She has a strong customer service background in non-profit and corporate sectors - with proven track records of problem solving and mediation at the Better Business Bureau (BBB) and active participation in the planning, coordination and implementation of events for the BBB and Microsoft and a high degree of fiscal responsibility, budgeting and management of revenue streams.

Barbara is holds a Bachelors Degree in Human Services from Western Washington University and is currently completing her Masters of Arts in Cross Cultural and Sustainable Business Management at American University of Paris. Upon graduation, she looks to take her skills in marketing and consulting to work in sustainable development.
