Joy Davidson Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Joy Davidson brings insightful viewpoints to provocative issues in her work as a psychologist, columnist, speaker and author, reaching millions of people worldwide through her presence on the internet and in the media.

Dr. Davidson has been a sought-after guest on hundreds of national TV and radio shows, including Oprah, Entertainment Tonight, Montel, Maury,and Bill O'Reilly. She was the host of the Playboy Channel’s "Secret Confessions and Fantasies," the co-creator a of the award-winning home video series, "Secrets of Making Love to the Same Person Forever," and the host of the groundbreaking "Guides to Sexual Pleasure" for couples.

As an expert commentator on sexual issues in popular culture, Dr. Davidson wrote "The Psychology of Joss Whedon"-- one of Benbella Books' acclaimed Smart Pop series. She also contributed to four other Smart Pop books, including: "Finding Serenity: Anti-heroes, Lost Shepards and Space Hookers in Joss Whedon's Firefly," and "Five Seasons Of Angel: Science Fiction, Fantasy and Romance Authors Discuss Their Favorite Vampire."

Dr. Davidson has been the monthly sexuality advice columnist for Playgirl and Men’s Fitness magazines, Msn’s Underwire, and Msn's Women Central. Currently, her new columns can be found on the Love & Health channel at LoveandHealth.info. Her feature articles have also appeared in Cosmopolitan, New Woman, Family Circle, Men’s Fitness, and First for Women. She is frequently interviewed by national magazines and newspapers including Cosmopolitan, Marie Claire, Redbook, Men’s Health, Prevention, Maxim, and Esquire.

Dr. Davidson is the author of "Fearless Sex" and "The Agony of it All: The Drive for Drama and Excitement in Women's Lives. She holds a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology and a Master’s Degree in Counseling Psychology. She is a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist and an AASECT certified Sex Therapist. She resides happily in New York City.
