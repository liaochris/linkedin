Pamela Currie’s Summary

Leveraging 20 years of combined experience in sales, franchising, and professional training & development.

Pamela provides consulting and insider franchise knowledge to help individuals select a franchise that aligns with their professional goals, finances, and lifestyle ambitions. She also works with Franchisors by becoming an extension of their organization and assisting with franchise sales and development of operational training and support programs.

Pamela's objectives as a franchise consultant are to assist you with 4 key goals of franchise ownership:
1) Help you achieve your annual income goals and maintain a quality of living.
2) Build equity in a business that you can eventually sell. (Buy, Build, Sell)
3) Find a business that fits your lifestyle.
4) Contribute to the development of a retirement nest egg.

In addition to business matchmaking; Pamela provides professional training and development services. Her expertise is in sales, facilitating professional development courses and contributing to the design of effective training programs to acheive improved performance.
