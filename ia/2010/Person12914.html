Angela Barry’s Summary

Angela Barry is the Director of Marketing for Engineering Technologies, Inc. (ETI). Her main objectives are to direct client relations and recognize opportunities for client development. She maximizes the impact of numerous business relationships while also developing public relations programs and managing the corporate brand. Most recently, Angela directed the development of ETI's strategic plan and new corporate identity.

Angela is a graduate of the University of Nebraska - Lincoln where she earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Advertising with an emphasis in Marketing and Graphic Design. She has worked as the marketing specialist for the Nebraska Hospital Association where she was responsible for developing the associations new brand identity. Angela also developed the associations web site creating new opportunities for communication between members and business partners.

During her position as Marketing Representative at Sandhills Publishing, Angela recognized the need for a training program to establish consistency in policies and procedures. She developed and implemented a new training program and Sandhills Publishing awarded Angela a new position as Training Analyst. She was responsible for the development of training standards and presentations to the marketing staff, interviewing new empolyees, managing training schedules and training manuals. In 1999, Sandhills Publishing established a Web design department which allowed Angela to enhance her graphic design and Web development skills.

Currently, Angela serves on the International Association of Business Communicators - Lincoln Board of Directors as the Membership Chair where they are considered one of the fastest growing chapters internationally. She is also active in the American Marketing Association, the Society of Marketing Professional Services and serves as the Co-Coordinator of Maxey Elementary School's largest fundraising event for the Parent Teacher Organization (PTO).
