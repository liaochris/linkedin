Rick Skogg’s Summary

I started my real estate career in 1980 at Roy Long Realty in Tucson while majoring in real estate/finance at the University of Arizona. I then joined my Father's firm in Green Bay in 1982. I went out on my own and started a Realty Executives franchise in Green Bay in 1991. In April 2009, I sold the firm to Coldwell Banker, The Real Estate Group out of Appleton, WI. I stayed with Coldwell Banker and I am currently actively involved as a broker/associate with them.
