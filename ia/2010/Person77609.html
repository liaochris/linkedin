Greg Ellis’s Summary

Senior sales and business development manager with more than 14 years of executive-level experience driving market share, revenue, and profit growth for wireless, broadband, telecom, and consumer electronics companies. Adept at defining and executing business models for new product lines, business units, and companies. A deal closer with particular expertise in launch sales for new products, product lines and companies. Expertise in creating and monetizing Intellectual Property through licensing programs.

Hands-on experience creating, selling, and marketing cellular, wireless, cable TV, consumer electronics, and satellite communications products and applications.
