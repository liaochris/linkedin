Cathy Aitchison’s Summary

I am a London-based media freelancer/consultant. I specialise in participatory (community) media and online communications, focusing on:
~ project management, training and facilitation
~ production and reporting for radio and new media
~ research
~ website design and management

My published work (print and audio) covers a range of topics including the media, education, travel, migrants, public transport, libraries and women's issues.

As a researcher, I am experienced in survey research techniques (qualitative and quantitative), data analysis and information research.

Cathy Aitchison’s Specialties:

Project management, radio production and training (speech radio), using media for empowerment, managing volunteers.
Website design (html), managing information websites.
Features journalism for print and audio.
