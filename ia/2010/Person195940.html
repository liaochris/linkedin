Steve Porcaro, ACC’s Summary

As the Surgical Sales Coach, Steve is extraordinarily passionate about inspiring Medical Device Sales Professionals to create the life and business they deserve. Using the 1•4•All Coaching Approach, Steve will help you sell more products, services, and ideas.

Besides being a Certified Professional Executive and Business coach, Steve’s background includes medical device sales, leadership development, small business operations, training, and clinical surgical operations.

Steve works with Medical Device Sales Professionals, including clinical sales/support, managers, executives and distributors. By uncovering and harnessing your passion, focusing your strengths, and sharpening your competitive edge, you will reach new levels of performance and effectiveness.

Steve will soon be releasing, 6 Simple Steps to Building a Better Sales Team, a new book co-authored with Michael Stone of the Stone Group. Designed for sales managers and business owners, Steve shares six methods of leveraging the diversity and strengths of your organization to create a winning sales team.

As a keynote speaker and facilitator, Steve is recognized for his dynamic and engaging presentations on selling, leadership, and motivation. He understands the real-life challenges of today’s workplace and shares innovative and practical solutions.

> Increase sales and commissions
> Manage your territory strategically
> Sharpen your "Unique Selling Abilities"
> Set and reach your sales vision/goals
> Balance your professional and personal life
> Create a powerful game plan to grow your business
> Build solid business relationships
> Learn new sales and self-marketing skills
> Stay focused and on target
> Prioritize your time
