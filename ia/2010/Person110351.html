Alex Karakozoff’s Summary

My personal passion is "turning vision into reality." When all is said and done, I am a B2B sales and marketing (haven't seen that term in a while) guy. I established my professional reputation as a sales executive at SGI, Oracle & Peoplesoft in the 90's and startups in the new millennium. Now I lead an itinerant existence as a venture financier (patient debt and equity), start up/interim Executive, and coach.

I live in Washington, DC and focus on companies that distribute to corporate and government entities and are located in the under served markets of greater Washington, DC and Montreal, QC.
