Anil Punyapu’s Summary

Anil Punyapu is Vice President of Enterprise Sales at Cvent. He is one of Cvent's earliest employees and is currently stationed in Cvent's Los Angeles office. Anil manages Cvent's Enterprise Sales Team which focuses on selling enterprise meetings management solutions to large organizations. This includes shaping Cvent's enterprise meetings solutions, marketing initiatives, and sales training and process. In addition, Anil has been instrumental in the development and management of Cvent's relationship with its strategic partners – including meeting management companies, travel management companies and meeting industry associations such as MPI, AMA, IABC and HSMAI. Previously, Anil started and built Cvent's Chapter Sales Division, which focused on chapter-based associations.

Prior to Cvent, Anil co-founded Entertainment Ventures, a hedge fund of funds, based in the Washington DC area, and was instrumental in raising the initial investment into the entity. Prior to this, Anil was a Senior Associate at Friedman Billings Ramsey (FBR), a full-services investment firm based in the Washington DC area. During his time at FBR, Anil assisted in the development, fundraising and management of alternative investment vehicles such as hedge funds, venture funds, etc.
