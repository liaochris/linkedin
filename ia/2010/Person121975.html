Laura Neff’s Summary

After more than a dozen years climbing the corporate ladder in internal communications, training, facilitation, executive communications and associate/leadership development, I leapt from the corporate workplace and, after much soul searching, found my true vocation in Life Leadership Coaching. Now, I help individuals discover how they can develop and magnify their own innate (and learned) leadership attributes in every part of their lives!

Laura Neff’s Specialties:

Coaching, communications, leadership, goal setting, strategizing, measurement, self-discovery, visioning

Laura Neff’s Experience

  • Life Coach

    "More In You" Life Leadership Coaching

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    2006 — Present (4 years )

    Greetings! As a Life Leadership coach, I know there is more in all of us and that we each have the potential to be powerful leaders of our own lives instead of living at the mercy of the world and systems around us. In my work, I partner with women and men who know--at least with an inkling--where they want to be, only they haven’t been able to get there on their own. They crave the clarity, courage, confidence, and empowering choices they need to actively create a life that enlivens rather than depletes, fulfills instead of drains...a life that joy-fully reflects on the outside who they truly, deeply are on the inside. And together, we get them there!

    This year, I am honored to hold the title of 2009 President of the International Coach Federation, Charlotte Area Chapter, a group of 45+ outstanding coaches from the greater Charlotte area.

    For more information about "More In You" Life Leadership Coaching, go to http://www.moreinyou.com. To learn more about the International Coach Federation-Charlotte Area Chapter, go to http://www.icfcharlotte.org.
