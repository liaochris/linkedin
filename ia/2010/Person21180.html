Robert J. Bee’s Summary

As a consultant, I help clients dig deeper into their marketing goals and challenges and help them see the power of brands and the emotional connections they can create with consumers.
In the past 20 years I have worked in the US, Europe and India for top national and international brands, big and small, in refining, developing and implementing creative marketing strategies and generating award-winning campaigns that proved highly successful in the market place.
I consider it my responsibility to demonstrate to my clients that today’s brands are built on lifestyle connection, a commodity that is desired and enjoyed by its user and not just bought on price. Success in the market is built on a business’ ability to create passion, interest and desire in those they wish to sell to.
Purchase decisions spring from emotional judgments. It is my job to find the keys that unlock those connections.
This means consumer insight research is as vital to designing successful marketing relations as finding a unique positioning and creating outstanding creative concepts.
My clients get great creative and ideas, but they also receive help in mapping and planning their brand & marketing strategy, new product and market analysis.
