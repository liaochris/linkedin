Allison Bucchere’s Summary

Award-winning creative executive leading design teams to create world-class, interactive brand experiences. Extensive background in brand management and direct marketing for online advertising generating $500 million annually.

Allison is a featured speaker on Creativity and a board member of AIGA, where she orchestrates a monthly series of intimate roundtable design discussions to inspire creative professionals.
