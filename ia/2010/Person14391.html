Ann Grain’s Summary

I have over 10 years experience working on communication campaigns, strategic planning and issues management and a strong track record of delivering results. I have directed and advised on all aspects of communication – external and internal - and provided strategic support to senior management to ensure effective engagement with key stakeholders.

I have worked in the public, private and voluntary sectors with a number of major ‘brands’.

I am a specialist in strategic national and local media relations, campaign development and delivery. I also have extensive experience in the production of publications/editorial services, having been a journalist for 15 years.

I am a fluent Spanish speaker and have a good command of Portuguese and German.

My expertise spans across:
•Strategic communications advice and implementation
•Management of issues and crisis
•Press relations/communications
•Day-to-day running of a communications team, press office/publications department
•Writing and production for a wide variety of internal and external publications
•Managing communications budgets
•Working for agencies on a freelance basis
•Event planning and management
•Healthcare, retail, food and drink, website design, sport, home improvement and legal affairs

Goal: be the best I can be!
