Jeff Johnson’s Summary

I am a senior business and technology executive with broad business and technology management experience in the Energy, Utility, Technology Consulting and Financial Services industries. As the VP & Deputy CIO at Constellation Energy I have succeeded in driving signfiicant strategic business value through a re-engineering of IT service delivery organizations to leverage standard processes and enterprise architecture platforms. I commissioned a recent Gartner benchmarking study to measure tangible strategic progress. This study empirically showed that we had achieved below average IT costs along with high customer satisfaction measures in the top 15% of the distribution.
