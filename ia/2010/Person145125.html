Mike Brunkow’s Summary

Experienced hotel industry leadership known for opening, operating and successfully repositioning union and non union hotels. Served as general manager at 11 hotel locations includes opening a 345-room international resort located at Queensland, Austraila, a 337 room corporate hotel, a 222 room boutique hotel and directing five major hotel renovations.

Managed properties that were consistently at the top of brand quality assurance and customer service rankings. Provided team members for interim general manager, rooms and food & beverage task force for sister properties needing assistance.

Recognized for consistent financial success with outstanding customer service at multi-faceted hotels. An active community leader.
