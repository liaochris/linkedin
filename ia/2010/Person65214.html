Eoin Stewart, FSA Scot’s Summary

Eòin Òg MacIain Stewart, Production Director and Consultant began his career in printing over 20 years ago. Although Eòin chose a path in Production Management, he planned for, and enhanced, his professional skill sets by working as a prepress operator/preflighter (prior to preflight applications) in early digital workflows, a Production Coordinator, a CSR for a midsized commercial printer, and a print Production Manager. In 1998, Eòin took the position of Production Director for the Periodicals group at Wizards of the Coast. One initiative included optimization of all production processes (end-to-end) both in-house and at various print partners. Working with Quad Graphics and RR Donnelley, Eòin planned for and implemented the in-depth systems necessary to ensure accurate and consistent CTP production. In November 2001, after 8 months of planning, Eòin implemented the world’s first PDF/X1a workflow.

In 2003 Eòin joined the Microsoft Global Procurement Group team via xpedx, to develop end-to-end agency and printer production processes. Eòin designed and implemented workflow enhancements, as well as assessing and advising with regard to all aspects of end-to-end print production workflow. Eòin’s most recent successes were launching the largest PDF/X3 initiative in the history of Commercial Printing and a unique color management workflow for both content producers and printers. Eòin also developed the "Process vs. Product©" production methodology and the "Production Based Costing©" model.

Eòin is a published author and a public speaker. He serves with the GRACoL and SWOP Committees and authored portions of GRACoL 6 and 7. Eòin is also a member of the IDEAlliance Print Properties and Colorimetrics Working Group. Eòin belongs to several professional organizations including IDEAlliance, PIA, and the DMA.

Eòin is a former active duty United States Marine and lives in Seattle Washington with his wife and two of his three children.
