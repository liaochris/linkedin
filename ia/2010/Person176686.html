Roger Walker’s Summary

Specialize in Boulder/Denver/Front Range area residential real estate sales and remodeling consultation.

I work with a range of buyers and sellers from first timers to experienced investors; from condos and townhomes to million dollar estates; from student housing to investment properties. I enjoy working with families and people relocating to enjoy the Colorado lifestyle.

My background in advertising, marketing and Internet business helps me to effectively market and sell homes faster saving my clients time and money.

I have completed several "fix and flip" projects and I know what specific improvements you can make to your home to add value before you get ready to sell.

Please keep me in mind if you know of anyone buying, selling or investing or who are considering a move to the greater Boulder/Denver/Front Range area.

Thank you to my friends and clients for years of great referrals and for posting your recommendations below!
