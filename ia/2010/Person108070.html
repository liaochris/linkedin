Julia Mee’s Summary

Brand marketing professional with twenty years of experience managing multi-million dollar programs for leading brands including Yahoo!, Hewlett-Packard, Electronic Arts (EA), Nintendo, and Pillsbury. Strengths include global mindset, strong creative instincts, proven skill at building brands that connect with consumers, and collaborative and positive approach.
