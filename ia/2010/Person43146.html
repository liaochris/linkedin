Suzen O'Rourke’s Summary

I founded Cooking by the Book over twenty years ago with this goal: to have people come together in the kitchen by cooking, communicating and learning. IOver time, I have learned, too, and my programs have evolved into a world-class business.

At first, I offered monthly cooking parties where friends and newcomers would come together to prepare a meal. In just a few months, I saw the potential for this to be a real, full-time business.

For several years, I focused on public events. By word of mounth, the demand for classes increased and I kept expanding the program. Soon, some of my clients requested events for their firms. And those requests triggered a wonderful growth path.

Today, most of my events are corporate ones. My clients range from Fortune 500 firms to startups. We have huge repeat business with loyal clients having several events each year. I have a strong presence in several vertical markets: law, financial, insurance, pharmaceuticals, PR and advertising, and luxury goods. My events from cooking a meal together to a full day offsite program.I am, of course, constantly looking to expand our list of clients.

We provide events in my Tribeca loft or offsite at event spaces in the city or in the tri-state area.

My business approach has been successful because my entire team takes great care to meet the indivdual needs of each client. We offer menus that clients rave about. With our focus on teambuilding through culinary education, our staff of culinary professionals have decades of experience. I have worked with the staff to hone every aspect of an event. From the time clients enter the door until they leave after their meal, we ensure that clients relax, learn, and enjoy their time with us.

To see more about me and my business, please see my corporate website at cookingbythebook.com. Or, you can visit my blog to see information about classes, terrific recipes, culinary news, or cookbook reviews that will make you smile.
