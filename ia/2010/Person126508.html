Lisette Muntslag’s Summary

Victory for Humanity. Dress to Express.

Victory for Humanity is a thought provoking contemporary brand that strives to create quality apparel that transcends time, race and culture. It is comprised of those best attributes that make people unique, inquisitive, caring and passionate. It is all about Style, Fashion, Art and Freedom of Expression but most importantly, it focuses on individuality and social responsibility and what it means to be human—resilience of the spirit.

Dress to Express. Express to Progress.

LIMITED EDITION ART & Culture focused Tees and Contemporary Fashion for Men & Women.

Lisette Muntslag’s Specialties:

With a background in the world of glamour and make believe, Lisette Muntslag has taken her passion and creativity and applied them to developing limited edition T-shirts that will each tell their own special story. The special art work created for these high quality Tees will celebrate people, places and the things that make life worth living. Fashion, Art, Style and Freedom of Expression: Avant-garde Funky-Chic.
