Robert E. Conrad’s Summary

An experienced Lawson Procurement and Technical Consultant with extensive global procurement and extended supply chain operations experience managing materials in Europe, Asia and North America.

Numerous implementations on Lawson ERP S3 versions 8.0.3, 8.1, 9.0 and 9.0.1 Applications, including e-Procurement, Requisition Self-Service, Process Flow (BPM) and Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) 810, 850, 856 transactions.

Solution Architect who has designed and implemented custom Advanced Planning Supply Chain Management Applications for semiconductor distribution.

Lawson M3 (Movex) System Administrator who installs, upgrades and maintains internal CIBER M3 (Movex) systems to support EAM, FAB and DIS Quickstep projects.
