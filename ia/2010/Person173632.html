Rita Dixon’s Summary

I have over 20 years recruitment experience in both blue chip organisations and running my own business. I am a mother of a young primary school aged child and understand the frustrations of wanting it all that many parents face - how to have time to be with their children and still have a career with flexibility.

At Recruitment Admin, we are able to offer Employers a Total Recruitment Service providing a personalised service tailored to meet your needs. Our Recruitment Service is designed to assist in the recruitment process for any medium sized or small business. We are a recruitment pathways and strategic marketing specialist. We help you focus the dollars you’re already spending on advertising and recruitment into a more time efficient pathway to ensure that your money and effort get you the right person for the job. Importantly, you retain complete control over the whole process

Whether you are finding your way through the recruitment process for the first time, or expanding your business we provide an affordable alternative to recruitment agencies. We bring job seekers and companies together. We attract and screen candidates for you without charging per person or a percentage of salary.

If you are in need of additional services such as personal support conducting the interviews, psychometric assessments, background checks, employment contracts, video interviews, HR systems audit or workplace evaluations, we can tailor a package that’s right for your business.

We guarantee will save you time and money by providing you with pre qualified highly talented professionals. We commit to a 7 working day turnaround time from end of advertising and are able to recruit across all industries.

For further information please go to our website www.recruitmentadmin.com.au or send me an email!

    Up until now, if you wanted to recruit somebody you either had to do it yourself or go through a recruitment agency. Doing it yourself is time-consuming, costly, and usually ineffective. Using a recruitment agency costs you 15 – 25% on top of the candidate’s salary, you only get a limited number of candidates to choose from, and you lose all control. There must be a better way…

    At Recruitment Administration Support, we can help you find the candidates you want without the hassle of doing it yourself, or the expense of a recruitment company. Instead, you get the best of both worlds. We attract and screen candidates for you without charging per person or a percentage of salary.

    • We do not recruit on a per-candidate basis
    • We recruit across all industries

    In this buoyant job market, attracting people can be hard. That’s why we concentrate on making the application process as easy as possible. Candidates don’t have to sign up or register before applying and, because they answer a number of tailored questions in the first round, they don’t need a resume. Perfect for passive job seekers!

    You don’t have to tell people who you are: If you’d prefer, your advertisements can remain completely anonymous, so your existing staff, applicants and competitors won’t know your plans.

    Recruitment Administration Support makes is easy for you to accept applications from potential job applicants; we also provide a reliable and confidential way that applicants can submit their personal information.
