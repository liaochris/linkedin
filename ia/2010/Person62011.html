Ed Thomas’s Summary

I am Director of UtilityExchange.org, an affiliate of the non-profit Electric & Gas Industries Association (EGIA). UtilityExchange.org produces Home Energy Makeover Contests as well as in-person and web-based activities where utilities, energy-related organizations, and contractors can learn and network regarding energy efficiency, renewable energy, customer service, marketing, sales, and enhanced business operations. Most recently, Ed helped the Electric Cooperatives of South Carolina and Energy Trust of Oregon implement statewide Home Energy Makeover Contests. In 2009, Ed has produced the New York Building Performance Contractor Exchange, 3rd Colorado Utility Efficiency Exchange, “Ramping up Efficiency Program” series of Utility Web Exchanges, and an article on Contractor Marketing Tactics for Home Energy magazine.
