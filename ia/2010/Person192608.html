    (Sports industry)

    December 2008 — June 2009 (7 months)

    The mission of the Replicast Challenge is to provide young riders that lack sufficient resources an opportunity to develop their skills in motorcycle road racing and to channel them towards advanced motorsports careers. By creating a cost-controlled youth development motorcycle road racing series, the Replicast Challenge will minimize expenses while maximizing rider growth in the motorcycle road racing industry. The series is designed to help young riders learn and improve their racing skills while stressing the importance of teamwork and communication in bike setup and race strategy execution. The emphasis is on learning and camaraderie via motorsports competition, with restrictions on motorcycle modifications to create a cost-controlled series that is economically feasible for developing riders and their families. The select motorcycle for the Replicast Challenge is the 2009 Aprilia RS 125 repli-racer, which delivers excellent performance and value for young riders.
