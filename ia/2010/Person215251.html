Wilco Leenslag’s Summary

Wilco is working as an advisor at KPMG IT Advisory Netherlands. He is engaged in various advisory assignments varying from IT strategy, IT governance to risk management. He is a member of the IT Strategy & Performance service line within KPMG IT Advisory. His interests lie in the field of the topics IT governance and IT performance.

To achieve the most appropriate IT strategy for organizations during assignments Wilco uses in cooperation with his colleagues a workshop approach called TransForum. This approach, preceded by a factual analysis of the (IT) organization, focuses on an intensive working conference environment with an organizations stakeholders and employees to achieve its ambition, its necessary steps and (re) establishment of the IT organization. The goal of the approach is to let people (of an organization) determine the required change and associated concrete steps to achieve its goal.

Wilco has an educational background in accounting, business informatics and IT auditing. During his bachelor and masters he focused on IT management and the business IT alignment within organizations with a high dependency on IT. He has written several bachelor and master thesis on the subject of IT governance, IT management and alignment of IT. Next to his studies Wilco has been an active member of the Young professional committee of the Dutch association for IT auditors, NOREA. The committee is a platform for the new generation of IT auditors.
