Scott Meyer’s Summary

Scott Meyer is President of Breville North America, part of Breville Group, an Australian company that markets the highest quality small kitchen electrics available.

Prior to Breville, Meyer was General Manager of the Faucets business at American Standard Brands, a $1 billion plumbing fixtures and fittings company focused on the US, Canada and Mexico, that is a portfolio company of Sun Capital Partners and Bain Capital. He initially joined the company as Chief Marketing Officer.

Before joining American Standard, Meyer was president and founder of Meyer Group, LLC, a retained strategic management consulting practice advising private equity and venture capital portfolio companies in consumer products. Meyer Group specialized in due diligence of consumer products from consumer and retailer perspectives, providing a "real world" dimension to traditional financial analysis of both distressed and high growth potential businesses. For existing portfolio companies, Meyer Group provided Fortune 100 best practices in strategic sales, marketing and new product development planning as well as financial processes and controls to improve economic value creation.

Prior to starting Meyer Group, LLC, Meyer was President/CEO of T-Fal/WearEver, a marketer of cookware and small domestic appliances and a division of Groupe SEB, a French corporation that also owns Krups, Rowenta, and All-Clad. His background is classical consumer package goods brand management and sales, having held positions of increasing responsibility at Gillette and Johnson & Johnson for a combined total of 20 years on brands such as Tylenol, Splenda, Oral-B, and Braun in the US, Germany, and globally.

Scott Meyer’s Specialties:

Private equity consumer products portfolio company due diligence, turnaround management, new product development, consumer products branding, Fortune 100 best practices implementation into small to mid cap companies
