    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    December 2009 — Present (11 months)

    ILRU is dedicated to improving the lives of people with disabilities by providing resources to government agencies, industry and centers for independent living. Lex Frieden, Director, served as chairperson of the National Council on Disability, president of Rehabilitation International, and chairperson of the American Association of People with Disabilities. He is credited with defining and pioneering the concept of "independent living" in the early 1970's.

    After Hurricane Ike, ILRU mobilized staff to provide emergency assistance to people with disabilities. They provided assistance with medication, food, clothing, furnture, etc. As a result of this effort, ILRU has received grant funds to continue assisting people with disabilities in the 23 counties affected by Ike, with special funding for Harris, Galveston and Brazoria counties. With their unique experience with disability, ILRU provides case management, peer counseling and resources for those still recovering from Hurricane Ike.
