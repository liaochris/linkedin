Eytan Shander’s Summary

I have spent the last 5 years working in Sports and News/Talk radio...I have had the good fortune to work in different parts of the country, from Philly to Nashville. Radio is my greatest passion, covering sports teams and interviewing players are a major part of it. I have produced shows, hosted & co-hosted, ran on-site remote broadcasts, booked guests, blogged, interviewed players from locker rooms, and anchored sports reports. I finally found a job where I can be paid for having opinions and also be a little obnoxious. Working in talk radio has allowed me to get insight into politics and the workings of our government, something I am thankful to have experienced. I'm now working for CBS Radio here in Philly, and looking to use any of my work/life skills to expand in the local media.
