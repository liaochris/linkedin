Rebecca Taub’s Summary

Bachelor of Arts in Spanish and Second Language Education from University of Iowa and is certified to teach Spanish as a foreign language in grades 5 - 12 and holds a TESOL certification. Master of Arts in Communication and Education from the University of Barcelona, Spain.

While living abroad for 5 years, I taught English as a Foreign Language to native Spanish speakers, provided consultancy services for developing pedagogy and educational content for Mizar Multimedia, an independent educational multimedia company specializing in the creation and development of educational technology products. While completing my Masters at the UAB, I obtained scholarship funds to work in the Department of Communication and Education as a researcher. My research focused on how to utilize communication, education, and new media to empower society and innovate learning.

I am an Educational Program Manager with extensive experience in Content Development, Teaching and Training in diverse environments, and Bilingual Communications. Background in classroom teaching and online instruction with K-12, higher education, and adult training throughout USA and Europe. Deep knowledge of enhancing educational environments by promoting digital literacy and creating tools that foster active community participation, civic engagement, and lifelong learning.

I am deeply committed to sharing, and capitalizing on, my knowledge of best-practices in education, superior language skills, and use of instructional technology to work with organizations that aim to promote, provide and/or develop programs and products for education and training.
