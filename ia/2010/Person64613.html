Emily Kinskey’s Summary

Creative writer, graphic designer and interactive marketing strategist with a passion for small business, world travel, sustainability and interior design. At heart I am a strategist, and I enjoy finding creative ways to communicate brand presence across a variety of traditional and interactive mediums. With a background in graphic design, a passion for writing, a head for strategy and an entrepreneurial spirit, I am constantly straddling the line between "account" and "creative" - which is exactly where I like to stand on a project - up to my elbows in every aspect.

At Amble Resorts, I enjoy working on a small team and contributing to every element of marketing, public relations and advertising a unique sustainable resort community on a stunning Pacific island in Panama. While at McGowan Crain I enjoyed working on a set of Corona billboards and visual concepts for a solar powered winery. As an undergrad, I gained valuable leadership experience as Creative Director for both SLU Ad Club and the 2009 National Student Advertising Competition while creating and managing a blog called TheAbbrevetionary with co-creator Christine Henry. As a freelancer, I enjoy working with purposeful individuals and small business owners to craft a meaningful brand image and marketing strategies that the client can employ themselves. I have experience designing for the music industry, non-profit organizations, bloggers, fashion, photographers and other artists. I am available for freelance writing, photography, graphic and blog design projects, and I have considerable experience in branding, marketing planning, CSS/HTML and SEO.
