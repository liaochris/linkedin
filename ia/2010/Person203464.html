Tiffany Quayle’s Summary

I have an extensive background in IT Sales & Marketing. I've actually been in the industry since I first interned (as a Marketing Coordinator) for MicroAge, Inc. while I was going to ASU. Things have come full circle now that I'm managing another reseller, Insight Enterprises, for CA - a company I've worked for on and off for the past 10 years!

Now that I'm in sales, I know it's where I'm going to stay.
