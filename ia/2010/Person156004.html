Noelan Brewington-Janssen’s Summary

I am currently pursuing my degree in International Business with focus in Finance from the University of San Francisco. I have a passion for learning that is extended beyond the class room.

Amid school and work I find time to pursue my hobbies of reading, playing music, exploring San Francisco, and eating at new restaurants. One of my greatest hobbies is learning about other cultures. I enjoy talking with people from other countries about their experience in the USA, what they think of my culture, how their culture is different from mine, and most importantly ways in which our cultures are similar.

In the future I know that I would like to pursue a carrier in international business. However, I am unsure, at the moment, what specifically related to. I am always looking to learn as much as I can about international, finance, marketing, trade and business in general.
