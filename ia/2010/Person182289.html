Sanyee Yuan’s Summary

Sanyee Yuan is an aspiring on-air entertainment news correspondent, host, and producer.

Currently producing three shows for the campus Harvard Undergraduate Television Network, Sanyee has worked on filming, writing, hosting, and teaching herself how to edit with Final Cut Pro.

She is the executive producer of Love@Harvard, the campus reality dating show, which aims to help undergraduates ignite more romance in their lives, in a shorter version of "The Dating Game."

She also produces the travel segment, "Explore Boston!" with her co-producer at Boston University, going on location to report on and film performances and festivals in the greater Boston Area. Past events that Sanyee has covered include The Moth Mainstage at Boston, the 23rd 2009 Annual Boston Christmas Festival, and the 10th Annual East Coast Elements Hip-Hop Dance Competition.

Her third show, which has enabled her to prepare for her future talk show aspirations, is the campus talk show, "Talk Harvard." In short 10-15-minute segments, Sanyee interviews visiting prominent figures, who are coming to Harvard to accept awards, give talks, or perform. She has interviewed actor Blair Underwood and political correspondent Chris Matthews. Sanyee has also interviewed athletes--including Olympic swimmer Dara Torres and renowned figure skaters Peggy Fleming, Dick Button, Paul Wylie, and Dorothy Hamill. Additionally, she has also had actor / performer John Lithgow, in the studio.

Along with producing these three shows, Sanyee has had experience working in a newsroom environment. She interned in the Public Relations / Communications department at the San Francisco local CBS news affiliate, also working as a production assistant for the local community affairs show, Bay Sunday. Having observed the live and animated dynamic between the host and different studio guests during the weekly tapings of Bay Sunday, Sanyee is inspired to have her own talk show and interact with new innovators every day.
