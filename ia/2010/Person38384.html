Chris Potts’s Summary

What can i say... the lovely Network Marketing arena hasen't always been the friendliest. I have definitely had my fare share of growing pains, however as strange as this may sound i am actually glad i went through what i did.

There is a favorite quote i like to reference, it says

" The Prizes Of Life Are At The End Of Each Journey, Not Near The Beginning "

This quote has stuck with me everyday a reminded me especially when i would get frustrated to "stay the coarse" and keep pushing forward.

I'm not going to sugar-coat this industry and act like it's easy, with that being said i will tell you that it is extremely possible to have enormous success if you find a (Mentor) who will be in your corner every step of the way. This alone will put you light years ahead of everyone else right from the beginning.

Although there are a record amount of people today looking to start a business from there home, nine out of ten of them have no clue as how to run a legitimate, profitable organization.

This is the major reason why i started my own training facility MLMTakeOver.com as a way to help newcomers in the industry as well as experienced professionals and teach them step by step the exact way to create an empire online from the comfort of there own home.
