Alan Bustamante, PMP, CSP’s Summary

Alan has over 9 years in IT management and services. His project work has spanned the globe, encompassing countries such as Brazil, Canada, China, India, Malaysia, Pakistan, Philippines, and Romania. He has worked extensively in the Project Management, Requirements Management, and Test disciplines for multiple verticals, including Energy, Environmental Services, Insurance, IT, Manufacturing, Non-Profit, and Telecom. Alan possesses a unique blend of certifications including Certified Scrum Master (CSM), Project Management Professional (PMP), and Certified Solution Designer in Rational Unified Process (RUP) v7.0. This powerful combination makes Alan a trusted advisor to his clients, in that his impartial no-nonsense advice comes from years of well rounded experience.

Alan is also a founding member and a chapter leader of the Agile Project Leadership Network (APLN) Houston Chapter. Among other things, Alan's contributions have helped grow the organization from a nascent membership of around 20 to over 600 in a little over a year.

An accomplished speaker a major conferences, such as SD West (www.sdexpo.com), Alan's passion is to continue to give back to the software development community and push the profession forward through speaking engagements, writing, and his work with non-profits such as APLN (www.aplnhouston.org).
