Michael Nolan’s Summary

I am the Director of the Small Business Development Center in South Central Minnesota - serving the Region 9 area.

The SBDC helps small businesses (Using the Fed’s definition of small - under 500 employees!) expand and excel. We provide one-to-one counceling, educational programs and financial analysis services to businesses small and large.

I also serve on the Board of Directors of Yovia - creating strategic marketing assessments to develop and execute marketing tactics that utilize the most effective social media channels available.

Yovia creates and manages online communities, cultivating exceptional user generated content for brand advertisers. We connect thousands of real people using all manners of Social Media to drive a far greater ROI than traditional interruption or inbound marketing.

BIO

A former broadcaster, Nolan sold his radio stations in 2000 to focus attention on the automotive online and vertical publishing company he started in 1996. After growing to a network of 450 automobile dealerships and partnerships with hundreds of newspapers across the country, he sold the company in 2006 to CarTruckTrader.com.

He has nurtured several ventures, including providing Angel funding and leadership to a music industry start up, seeing it through to acquisition by a publicly traded company. Mr. Nolan led the series A funding effort for a green manufacturing firm, and has consulted for automotive, jewelry, manufacturing and health care companies including staff development, leadership training, and leading e-commerce and traditional marketing campaigns.

Mr. Nolan recently earned an MBA from the Melbourne Business School in Australia in 2007. His studies included research and lectures at the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University in Illinois in the USA, WHU in Koblenz Germany, Ekonomická Univerzita V Bratislave - Slovakia, and Shanghai China.
