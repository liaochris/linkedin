Gary Golden’s Summary

Gary Golden; Garrett Golden; Garrett K. Golden

Gary Golden is a broadly experienced senior executive and consultant who has held CFO, Corporate Development, Strategy, HR, International, Corporate Services and Legal leadership roles. He has a strong background in M&A and Corporate Finance, having closed over 100 transactions. Gary develops and executes changes in business plans and processes that build sustainable organizations. He is recognized as an effective communicator at all organizational and governance levels and as a creative problem solver, skilled negotiator, and strong team builder.

Gary Golden’s Specialties:

Corporate Development, Strategy and Finance. Planning, Building and Repositioning of Businesses.
