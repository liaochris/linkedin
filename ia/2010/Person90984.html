I enjoy new challenges and am well respected within the community (the last time I checked…or was it the last time I ‘wrote a check’). Personal:
I have to say, one of my most successful accomplishments in life is becoming a parent. Being a father enriches my life. My kids mean the world to me and I to them. It is a true testimony of who you are when challenged with the task of being a good parent. It, like many other things in life can be stressful, uncomfortable and confusing, but I wouldn’t trade if for anything in the world. My motto in parenting is this: Parenting is an extreme sport. You have to be mentally prepared and ready to “grip-it and rip-it” on a daily basis, no matter what the circumstances are. Give all you can to your children and it will return to you ten-fold. I owe much gratitude, humility and love to those who are a part of my life. Without my loving family and friends, I would not be here today.

Life is an occasion…Rise to it. – Jason Soto
