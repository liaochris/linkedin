Stan Kitson’s Summary

We merge our background in sales and marketing (30 years) with our experience delivering web technology solutions (10 years) to solve problems faced by many small and medium businesses as they incorporate web systems into their sales and marketing strategies. We work specifically in the areas of:

Increase Revenue
- Increasing customer satisfaction
- Improving business efficiency in delivering products and services
- Projecting sales forecasts/trends
- Improving communication within the business
- Creating more efficient customer/prospect communication
- Reducing information duplication

We provide strategy, training and products for web systems that include:
- CRM - Customer Relationship Marketing, PRM - Partner Relationship Marketing. (Marketing is a more dynamic word than managment)
- Association management (member database management, online registration, member directory)
- Web site content management systems for business including websites, online catalogs/stores, blogs, discussion forums, surveys and forms.
