Rick Froehlich’s Summary

A driven senior executive and change agent skilled at growing businesses globally.

Rick has grown the Global OEM business at Dell from $250M to over $1.6B in revenue over the last 6 years. This global P/L includes all hardware and services sold to customers that integrate Dell product into their solutions. Rick has a 350 person dedicated engineering, operations, finance, HR, Sales and Marketing team reporting to him. Previous Dell experience included running Dell's Powerconnect Network Switching business in Product Group and launching and running the Dell|EMC partnership for 3 years.

Prior to Dell, Rick spent six years in management consulting working with AT Kearney doing strategy and operations engagements in consumer products, medical, retail, and manufacturing. Rick left AT Kearney as a Principal.

Rick started his career in the finance development program at Procter & Gamble. Rick spent 6 years at P&G managing Tide and other brands in the Laundry category.

P/L management, sales leadership, strategy development and execution, negotiations, purchasing, computer hardware and services, Financial analysis, people management, product development
