Joost Allard’s Summary

85% of failing partnership deals are due to controllable factors.
Why is the failure rate for partnerships still hovering around 50%?
The latest ASAP survey indicates that those companies deploying best practices see their success rate increase to 85%! Allinnova offers tools that show you how to align your teams around best practices for partnering. We build success profiles for you that visualize what improvements to prioritize on to achieve dramatic improvement!

In the past, I have shared my expertise and excitement about the potential of Alliances and Partnerships to help find answers to:
* Ensuring that corporate strategic goals and metrics are translated and executed in a comprehensive alliance program
* Partnerships are performing effectively so that a team can demonstrate its value to the corporation.
* Partners are true team members and are enabled to perform at the best of their ability while enjoying the ROI on their partnership investment.

I have had Alliance, consulting business leadership and IT assignments in financial, medical equipment, consulting, non-profit and other industries. I have managed software development programs at various levels of responsibility. More recently, I was a principal with Client Account (P&L) and Project Management responsibility in business software and management consulting settings.
