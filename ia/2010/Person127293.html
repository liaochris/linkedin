Lolita Carrico’s Summary

Marketing, business development and social media expert specializing in creating relationships for brands with moms.

Founded Modern Mom Inc. in 2001, publisher of ModernMom.com. Co-Founder of Urge Public Relations, leading boutique marketing and PR firm which launched high profile brands including Shopzilla.com and Fiji Water and orchestrated the re-launch of the Puma sportswear brand.

Spokesperson for high profile brands including Procter + Gamble's Swiffer and Bounty brands, Baskin-Robbins, and Fresh & Easy Markets, among others. Regularly featured on radio and television programs as a mom expert.

Contributor to LA Moms Blog, ClubMom / CafeMom, Disney's Family.com and MediaPost's Engage: Moms publication.

Lolita Carrico’s Specialties:

marketing, marketing to moms, social networking, promotions / partnerships, business development
