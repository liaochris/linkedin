Gunar Penikis’s Summary

Gunar Penikis is an established industry expert in metadata technologies and digital supply chain optimization strategies.

Previously as the Senior Product Manager for Adobe XMP, and Extensibility Technologies at Adobe Systems Incorporated he was responsible for defining platforms and products that empower the creative community to better manage media assets and workflow, He has collaborated with international standards organizations within the creative and publishing communities to build awareness and drive adoption of disruptive metadata technologies.

Early on at Adobe, Gunar held the position of Product Manager for Adobe Bridge CS3, collaborating across internal products teams and engaging with customers to define critical media management requirements. Before that, he help launch Adobe's first online collaboration and review service - Adobe Studio. He originally came to Adobe to help program manage Adobe's PhotoDeluxe consumer software.

Prior to Adobe, Gunar applied his software engineering skills at a Creative Wonders - a children's learning software company. He has also worked in Japan for over 2 years teaching English for the Japanese Ministry of Education.

Gunar holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Engineering Physics from Queen's University, Canada, and a Masters degree in International Business Administration from York University, Canada.

Gunar Penikis’s Specialties:

Metadata related technologies, digital supply chain optimization, media lifecycle optimization, platform technology management, evangelism, business development, partner relationship management, cross-functional collaboration, strategic planning, public speaking
