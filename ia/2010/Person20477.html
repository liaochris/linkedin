Uri Baruchin’s Summary

About: http://www.baruchin.com
Blog: http://www.marketingbabylon.com

Uri Baruchin is a multi-channel brand strategy, narrative marketing and customer experience expert with significant international experience. He worked in the UK, Europe and the Middle East with London international brand consultancies Siegel & Gale, Brandinstinct, Figtree and Landor Associates as well as a busy independent consultant. Previously, substantial new-media client-side experience in product innovation and strategic planning management. Uri’s innovative work combines business savvy, pattern recognition and cultural sensitivity. In concert with facilitation skills, he brings clarity, insight and empathy to projects; promotes client collaboration; improves relationships; pushes ground-breaking design through and repeatedly brings in more work.
