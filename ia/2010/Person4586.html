As Global Client Director, AJ develops, implements, and manages the strategic go-to-market initiatives with the Microsoft Corporation and Avanade, Inc. He has cross-departmental responsibilities that include Sales, Marketing, Business Development, Corporate Development, and Professional Services.

AJ has over 14 years in the Infrastructure Technology industry. Prior to joining SUN Microsystems, AJ held various roles in Sales as well as Channel & Alliance Management with companies such as IBM and Internap Network Services.
