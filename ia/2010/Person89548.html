Jankees van Woezik’s Summary

As an experienced freelance Flash developer/software engineer I am specialized in ActionScript 3.0 for websites and web applications. My strength as a developer is my allround programming knowledge including some backend languages like PHP. I am not afraid to do some HTML and CSS as well. I also have an interest in design, user interaction design and music.

My focus is on what is happening around me, and I love open source. I spend hours reading RSS feeds and surfing the web to find the latest developments.

My passion is about creating new innovative and creative cross-media concepts, following on-the-edge technology. I am always interested in new challenges, so feel free to connect!

I am currently developing my own Flash framework that will be open sourced later this year.

Jankees van Woezik’s Specialties:

Flash, ActionScript 3.0 (OOP), touchscreen applications, PHP, XML, HTML, CSS, Javascript, AJAX, CakePHP and interaction design.
