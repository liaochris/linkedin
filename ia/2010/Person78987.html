Gustavo Javier Wrobel, Ph.D.’s Summary

Gustavo Wrobel is the Corporate Director of Communications for Latin America in Motorola. He is responsible for developing and implementing the communications and PR strategies, as well as for managing analyst’s relations. It involves external and internal communications, issues & crisis, CSR, and communication strategies for the business units.

He joined the company by the end of 1999 as Communications Manager for Argentina, Chile and Uruguay. Subsequently, his responsibilities extended to include the Latin American region and, in early 2005, he was promoted to Communications Director for all Latin America - Mobile Devices Business. In 2006 he expanded his responsibilities assuming the same position for the B2B area, currently Broadband Mobility Solutions, and in 2007 he was promoted to his current position for the entire region.

Before joining Motorola, Gustavo Wrobel worked as Public Relations Manager of Mastellone Hnos. S.A. - La Serenísima -a leading dairy company of Argentina. Previously, he was Executive Director of the Foundation of the University of Buenos Aires School of Agronomy, and a journalist for twelve years in some of the Argentine leading media groups, such as El Cronista (business newspaper), Apertura and Siete Días (magazines).

Gustavo received a PhD in Communications – the first to obtain such degree in Latin America – and a Bachelor’s degree in Journalism in Universidad del Salvador. Moreover, he obtained a postgraduate training in Leadership from CEIDA.

In the academic field, he has regularly served as Jury on doctoral communications dissertation committees at Universidad del Salvador. He has lectured at said University, at the University of Buenos Aires, at the Universidad San Andres and at other relevant educational institutions. Additionally, he is a founding member of Círculo DIRCOM Argentina (comprised of the top communications executives in the country), and member of the jury of PR Awards, such as the Eikon Awards.
