Freda Mooncotch’s Summary

Freda Mooncotch is The Wine Wench™ – The Wench Knows Her Vine.™ Simply put, I am the girl next door concierge to interesting wine, food and travel experiences. Although I may not be a sommelier–but am pursuing my certification– I am completely in love with everything wine, travel, food, adventure and people. A wine aficionado. This is my zeal for life and I love to share my adventures and experiences with everyone! To that end I live.

I love domestic and international travel, old and new world wines, especially red wines and the people who make and drink them. Think of me as Your Wine Concierge! I represent YOU! I’m not getting paid to do this so all of my recommendations are born from pure love! Since there are a ton of excellent sites that already pair wine with food and describe them so much better than I can, I am writing from a different angle…one that will tell you of the stories and adventures behind finding these wines and the people I met while drinking them. I support mom and pop wineries and boutique vineyards. I also love olives, fish, cheese, charcuterie and chocolate. They say do what you love and you’ll never work a day in your life! This is true. Lucky is the person that does what they love.

I am currently pursuing a WSET Certification (London-based Wine & Spirit Education Trust) through American Wine School and my Sommelier Certification through The Court of Master Sommeliers.
