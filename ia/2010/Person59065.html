Douglas Q. Davis’s Summary

I've had various levels of responsibility in my 10 years of experience at ad agencies and digital shops. My category experience includes Entertainment, Financial services / Insurance, QSR, Publishing, Beauty, Email Marketing and Pharmaceuticals. Areas in my specialty include multi-channel concept and design, digital marketing, branding, acquisition, retention, reactivation strategies, creative strategy, team building and design direction. My approach combines design and business strategy to produce creative solutions for business problems. I've lectured on this approach as a guest lecturer at NYU, SCPS, New Marketer's Bootcamp: Integrated Marketing Communications and within the Advertising, Design and Graphic Arts Department within CUNY.
