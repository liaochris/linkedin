Craig Cunningham’s Summary

In summary, I have practiced law for fifteen years. I have worked in corporate large firms, such as Sonnenschein, Nath, and Rosenthal, Adler, Murphy, Mquillen. I have worked in government offices, such as the State's Attorney's Office of DuPage County. I now own a firm that operates in more two offices. I work with attorneys and staff in my firm to bring clients the best legal representation. In short, we solve problems. We make sure that our clients are protected. We make sure that our clients problems are solved.I have participated in major pro bono activities with Kane County, and carry pro bono cases with DuPage County Legal Aid. I provide services to clients, and provide them with solutions.
