Sherry Lu’s Experience

  • President

    Lula Apparel, Inc.

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    January 2001 — Present (9 years 4 months)

    Lula Apparel, Inc. was founded by Sherry Lu in 2001 in Los Angeles. Sherry Lu had experience in retail and wholesale distribution before forming Lula Apparel. We chose to specialize in the Asian market because we understood the market and the desire of the Asian market for American brands.

    Lula Apparel is the exclusive distributor of the contemporary streetwear companies such as STUSSY, OBEY, UPPER PLAYGROUND, REBEL8 and MISHKA for Taiwan.

    Sherry has traveled extensively throughout Asia and the United States, and is fluent in multiple languages. Sherry uses not only the relationships developed in the industry, but also the experience and understanding she has from both sides of the industry having dealt with the perspective of a brand and that of a retailer.
