Rich Redman’s Summary

Marketing communications professional with strategic and operational experience in marketing, customer communications, brand management, and public relations.

Rich Redman’s Specialties:

Expertise includes developing and executing market-based communications strategy, managing corporate brand and identity, writing and producing marketing/promotional collateral, Web site concepting and writing, direct mail strategy and writing, database marketing and 1:1 customer communication, advertising, media relations, market research, project/people management, plus hands-on experience with social media/Web 2.0.
