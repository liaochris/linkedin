Dan Valentine’s Summary

Real Estate agent for 32 years and A Broker for 30 Years. 32 years professionally representing buying, selling and investing clients, always putting their needs above mine. 90% of my business today is from past clients or referrals.

I am a blessed man today as I have 5 grown children whom I am proud of, 9 grandchildren who are all beautiful, a beautiful wife of 32 years, an awesome profession making others money and A Great God that Favors me more than I deserve.

I am walking with a limp today, having been hit hard by the market changes forcing the closing of some of the family businesses. But better times are on the horizon. I am uniquely qualified, having been in the top 10% of producing agents in AZ much of my 16 years with Realty Executives, marketing homes in Phoenix for 25 years, having rehabbed/repaired over 160 homes over the past 5 years, managing near 400 homes as CEO of our family management firm (sold 2 years ago), selling over 400 homes to investors for rental portfolios and having been involved in over 25 fix n flips.

Always looking for hard money, paying 18% for 80% 1st position on a wholesale purchase. 1 yr note.
