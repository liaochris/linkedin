Steve Tinsley, CPA’s Summary

Steve is a certified public accountant providing senior-level accounting and finance services to those companies who need to supplement their projects and interim staffing needs. Steve also conducts internal investigations for fraud, employee dishonesty, and any other unethical acts.

Steve has been a chief financial officer for a multi-office national consulting firm, a legal administrator for an eighteen-attorney law firm, and spent his initial career in public accounting. Steve has also provided forensic and investigative consulting services on a wide variety of insurance claims and litigation situations.
