Claudia Kim Nichols’s Summary

Pilot Health Advocates, Inc. serves health consumers in California who want help with navigating the health care system. We provide out-patient and in-hospital advocacy, assist with complex medical case management, help with referrals and assessments, coordinate communications with medical providers, organize and build comprehensive medical case files, review medical records for accuracy and completeness, research relevant clinical trials, facilitate health care planning services, and develop resources for informed, personalized health care decisions, within the framework of medical ethics and the individual's cultural milieu.

>> Encourage health care planning for the future to maximize options and resources.

    Private Piano Studio

    (Internet industry)

    2008 — 2010 (2 years )

    I provide lessons to beginning- through intermediate-level students of all ages. My studio's mission is to inculcate an appreciation of classical music that is wedded to the individual's creative faculties. The syllabus is crafted to the individual's pianistic goals; the studio does not assume each student aspires to be a concert artist. Musical theory is taught within the context of my students' reasons for playing the piano; emphasis is on musical expression within the framework of various developmental approaches. These approaches include, but are not limited to, the Kodaly Method, the Orff Approach, the Dalcroze and Suzuki instructional methodologies, Gordon Musical Theory, and the Carabo-Cone Method. The curriculum is rigorous but its overarching philosophy is to protect, develop and project the joy of music. I emphasize injury-free technique, provide rehabilitative training, and stress music as a language which facilitates emotional, physical and intellectual interconnectedness.
