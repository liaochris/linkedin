Raj Ghosh, P.E.’s Summary

Well Spoken, highly computer literate, self-motivated, goal-oriented, team player committed to pursuing a long-term career in Electrical Engineering/Project Management.

Offer 13-year track record (10+years in USA) as “Professional Electrical Engineer” demonstrating excellent leadership, technical, managerial, analytical, written & verbal communication skills, computer proficiency, and ability to follow through with projects from inception to completion. Presently working on different Facility Design task orders arising out of various ID/IQ contracts from the City, US Government, Military and other Private Companies and with various Energy/Power Utility companies on multiple Material Handling system projects all over USA.

Familiar with NEC (National Electrical Code), IEC, NESC (IEEE C2-2007), NFPA 70E, NFPA 101 (Life Safety Code), NFPA 780 (Lightning Protection), LPI-175, IBC, IESNA, NEMA, NECA, UL, IEEE, OSHA, ASHRAE 90.1, and LEED (Version 2.2). Has in-depth experience in electrical design for various Municipal, State, Federal, Institutional, Industrial, Commercial, and Energy/Material-Handling Projects; Cost Estimating, Project Coordination, and Construction Management.
