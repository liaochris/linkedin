Jack Doyle MBA’s Summary

With 25 years experience in Real Estate and Mortgage Banking, I have seen the industry from all sides. My career began as a loan originator in New Jersey in 1975 and progressed into branch, regional, divisional, and national management roles in the same bank holding company over 13 years. I then spent almost 5 years as the President of a yacht mortgage banking subsidiary of a major savings & loan. In 1992 I relocated to Florida and established a mortgage banking and real estate brokerage business. In 2003 World Savings wholesale mortgage division recruited me for a position of senior wholesale account sales executive. I was promoted to the Palm Beach Mortgage Banking Leader in 2005. In 2008, I established a real estate brokerage business, Marquis Real Estate that tapped my experience in finance, real estate and mortgage banking and coordinate the company's affiliated businesses: mortgage, title, insurance, settlement services as well as our concierge business. I am the Broker /Owner/Realtor and Chief Exeutive Officer of these companies, including the core real estate company. I bring to this position a vision of a broader real estate model that integrates other businesses more fully into the company's core real estate services. Marquis Real Estate will continue to focus on meeting the ever changing expectations of consumers through the development of the Southeast's most professional and knowledgable real estate and mortgage professionals.
