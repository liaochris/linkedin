Jan Libby’s Summary

Jan created the popular indie Alternate Reality Games - Sammeeeees & Wrath of Johnson (Sam II). After Sammeeeees, she spent nearly a year working with LG15 Studios (on the Lonelygirl15 Series and the Jumper ARG) as a Writer and Interactive Designer. Jan then partnered with Brian Clark & GMD Studios on Book 3 of the horror/sci-fi Eldritch Errors. She has been a speaker and panelist for DIY DAYS, ARGfests and the NATPE LATVfest. She also works as a New Media/Digital consultant to Media Companies and Agencies. Having just wrapped on Levi's "GO IV" Game/Experience, Jan is now wondering... what's next?
