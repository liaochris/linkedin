Brian Fitzgerald’s Summary

My goal is to help companies and individuals tell their stories through images in a way that captures their personality, their uniqueness and their "brand".

Thirteen years' experience in the newspaper industry as a content producer (photography, stories, multimedia) and as an editor have shaped the way I approach each assignment. I am relentless in my desire to provide effective visual solutions that communicate the brand experience and provide ongoing value.

As a technology expert, I consult for media organizations looking to develop a strategic approach to building a lasting and effective online presence.

Brian Fitzgerald’s Specialties:

On-location and studio photography, visual journalism, management, coaching, systems building, people and product management, Imaging and content workflow.
