John LaRocca’s Summary

John LaRocca has earned his reputation in the world of credit card processing at Sage Bankcard through years of dedication and perseverance. A self-starter in the business, John made the transition to credit card processing after more than 2 decades as a well-respected, successful entertainment industry figure. He has amassed the knowledge, the contacts and the personal “hands-on” experiences that characterize triumph in any field. Born in Stamford, Connecticut, John’s early life was infused with principles and values that reinforced an earnest approach to life. The multi-lingual business Pace University graduate subsequently accepted a position with Xerox Corporation, rapidly rising to the top national ranks as a sales executive, and receiving an award for #1 salesman of the year in 1978. At the same time, John’s interests expanded to include the film and television industry. Pioneering into new territory, John brought the insights and strengths he gained from his continuing work in sales and opened the “LaRocca Talent Group”. He was extremely successful in this transition, and ultimately became known for his ability to single-handedly launch the careers of an impressive list of stars. The commitment previously demonstrated to artists, producers and other entertainment industry professionals, has translated to his current clients and associates in the credit card processing area. He is committed to providing his clients with the highest level of service possible. John and his partner Rick Garboski have made a pledge to meet all of your credit card processing needs and to build lifetime clients. His business is built solely on referrals. Referrals are what determine his success … one friend at a time … one client at a time … one deal at a time.
