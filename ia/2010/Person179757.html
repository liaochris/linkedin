Ryan Mickley’s Summary

FURTHERED EDUCATION: I was accepted in the College of Business at the University of Florida in '02. I eventually changed my major to Recreation, Parks & Tourism (specialization in Hospitality Mgmt) through the College of Health & Human Performance at UF. While attending the University of Florida, I was an active brother of the Phi Kappa Tau Fraternity. Here I took on such responsibilities as Executive at Large, New Member Orientation Officer and Sergeant at Arms.

PROFESSIONAL LIFE: In 2007 I began my professional life as an intern at Quail Valley Golf Club & River Club where I conducted day-to-day operations in every department throughout the club. After completion of the internship, I was offered a position (Club Activities Coordinator & Riverhouse Manager) and accepted with delight. When I first started at Quail Valley, they presented me with a project that was on the To Do List for quite some time and just needed the right guy to complete it. They needed me to redesign the club's website and take over all email marketing campaigns and content management for the club's events and general happenings. It was at this point that I found my passion for Internet Marketing. I found the FSO IMBS program and decided to pursue my passion by changing career paths.

CHARITABLE EXPERIENCES: The two types of non-profit organizations that mean the most to me are charities that assist in helping overweight teens and pre-teens and charities that assist in helping high school and college graduates find themselves in the world. While at Quail Valley, I was a chairman on the executive committee for the Quail Valley Charity Cup. This weeklong event consisted of Golf, Tennis, 5K Walk/Run, Gourmet Wine Dinner, Bridge and Live/Silent Auction events that raised money for local non-profits that supported children and education. I was responsible for the Duplicate Bridge Tournament.
