Paul Cronin’s Summary

I help companies grow, faster.

I offer a fresh perspective on how to make your company grow and be more profitable. I have generated millions in revenues and have led teams to generate tens of millions.

Over the course of my career, I have helped businesses in the fields of consulting, marketing, printing, publishing and software to grow faster. I have sold products to the full array of retailers: giants such as Wal-Mart, Target, Amazon.com, Best Buy, Borders Books and Music, TJX Corp., American Greetings, Hallmark, Talbot’s, Nordstrom, Macy's, as well as smaller companies such as Learning Express and thousands of independent retailers.
