Joel Carson’s Summary

A motivational leader with the proven ability to inspire real estate agents to become team-oriented, award winning sales associates formally recognized at local, state and national levels in the real estate industry. Eighteen years of proven leadership, increasing sales, profit and developing high-impact real estate agents and employees.

Founder and President Prudential Utah Real Estate
• Merged Impression Group Real Estate with two of the strongest real estate companies in the state to form Prudential Utah Real Estate by acquiring Prudential Prestige Real Estate and Prudential Coleman Real Estate
• Company started with 266 fulltime Realtors in five offices. Grew company to more than 700 fulltime Realtors in 16 office locations.
• Under Carson's leadership Prudential Utah Real Estate became Utah’s number one real estate company in total volume topping 3.2 billion in sales serving all Northern Utah markets, received national recognition
• Prudential Utah Real Estate named among Prudential Real Estate’s “Top 10 in the franchise,” year after year
• Ranked 7th in the nation in the Prudential Real Estate network in 2008
• Company ranked in top 50 in the US out of all real estate companies two consecutive years

Recognized as one of the “Top Seven Companies in America” for business management by the National Association of Realtors with “Real Estate All-Star Award”
