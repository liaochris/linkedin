Brooke Austin’s Summary

Accomplished marketing and creative design director and brand specialist offering extensive experience in agency, client and consulting roles. A skilled professional with a proven track record of success leading cross-functional teams to integrate every business and product touch-points to: brand and market high-end products, develop creative concepts, and distinctive designs for business to consumer (B2C) and business to business (B2B). These brand and product successes are accomplished through insightful brand values, brand promise, brand voice, brand strategy, brand positioning and development, creative concept development and innovative brand design. The positive effects of a successful brand builds, product recognition, brand strength, brand equity and lasting value to increase sales across market segments and connects the consumer with the brand on an emotional level.

Brooke Austin’s Experience

  • Creative Director and Brand Specialists

    Austin West

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2002 — Present (8 years )

    As the principal of Austin West we work collaboratively with clients on concept development, creative solutions, integrated brand strategies, brand and branding development, brand values, brand essence and brand positioning, naming projects, brand identity and logo marks. AW gather's key stakeholder input and conduct competitor research to clarify core goals, values, vision and positioning opportunities, culminating in strategic creative and brand briefs. AW directs and manages accounts, projects, budgets and clients. We can work with your marketing team in providing a piece of the branding effort or lead your branding project.

    At Austin West we make use of numerous trusted creative and production partners while keeping the core business small and flexible. We begin each project with only one point person, myself. We hire assistance and expertise only as needed to meet the unique requirements of your project. This way, we can offer most of the services of a larger agency while maintaining a lower overhead. This allows us to be cost-effective even for our smaller clients. This approach of flexible, tactical staffing has been a big hit with AW's current clients. We can put together larger teams for broader projects which may include designers, production artists, programmers, writers, photographers, illustrators and manufacturing vendors.

    At Austin West Design we have a commitment to make the world a better place through the use of branding, marketing, design and creative thinking. We look to partner with like minded, mission-driven clients; people who share the same business objectives of driving economic success through positive contributions to customers and society at large.

    To view my portfolio and client list please see my website at: www.austinwestdesign.com
