Elmer Gulle’s Summary

Since 1985 when I started my career as an IT faculty for a university, then doing various technical work in various industries, and until now that i was able to build a career in IT Sales, Consulting and Business Development, the total experience molded me to become a well-rounded, results-oriented yet practical business executive that I'am today. The past 23 years of my career was a period of growth and learning. In the next 20 years or so, i would like to see opportunities for me to help the younger techies grow and learn from my experience, and for me to learn from them too.

I'm a business development person with an acute eye for business opportunities as well as partnering. This is what I do best!

Professionally, my interest is to further hone my other skills and talent for business process enhancement, and enterprise architecture. If I cannot be successful in planning and designing signature buildings for people, i certainly would like to see myself help companies realize their own business architecture, linking business strategies with IT implementations, and ensure long-term success through continuous business process improvements.
