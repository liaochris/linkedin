Mauricio Samayoa’s Summary

I am a unique breed of an MBA PR type who has evolved into a full blown Social Media type out of a genuine passion for all things *social* - i.e. Social Media and Social Business Design.

My career blends together practical knowledge of making business decisions based on hard numbers, experience in the creation and roll out of traditional PR campaigns integrating tech-based communication tools, business strategy theory acquired during a full-time MBA program at a top-ranked business school (IE Business School, Madrid - Spain), and on-the-ground experience working the first formal activities of a high-profile brand within the social web.

My passion for all things *social* stems from both my professional and personal life. On the professional front I not only embrace the fact that the *social* factor redefines the way we communicate, influence each other, and pass on word of mouth, but also advocate the fact that the *social* factor produces a fundamental disruption in today's mainstream business models and approach to business strategy. On the personal front I have adopted *social* tools as a way of life. I am a natural networker who has relied heavily upon online social tools to build, maintain, and cultivate personal networks from my experiences growing up in Latin America, and adult life living in top-tier cities of the world including Washington D.C., New York City, Miami, Madrid, and now London.

Most recently I completed the role of Social Media Account Manager of the Magners UK brand on the social web. This served as a means of earning real-world, on-the-ground experience of bridging the gap between Social Media strategy and Social Media execution. In addition, it served as a unique one-time opportunity to set up, build, and activate the brand's communities on major social platforms - Twitter (http://twitter.com/MagnersUK) and Facebook (http://facebook.com/MagnersUK) - from scratch.

Currently, I work as an Account Manager at We Are Social.

Mauricio Samayoa’s Specialties:

Hands on knowledge of the social web, administration and engagement on branded Twitter profiles and Facebook fan pages, Social Media strategy, industry analysis, adoption and incorporation of social media tactics into traditional PR campaigns, creation of design concepts, Spanish and English (both as native language).
