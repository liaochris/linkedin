Anthony Belasco’s Summary

Brand-Armor, LLC is a Brand Protection & Document Security Solutions Company.

We act as a liaison between technology suppliers and the brand owner or the original document provider. Our experience in overt and covert layered applications and multiple anti-counterfeiting technologies help determine the right solution to secure your packaging and documents. Various layers of technology can be applied in the printing, adhesion, embossing, stamping and etching process to yield a strong and effective deterrent from duplication, forgery and diversion.

Please contact us with your application and project requirements.

If you are looking for ID card printing equipment and consumables or anti-copy/scan security paper stocks, please send us your specifications.
