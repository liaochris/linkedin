Pier Scotti’s Summary

I start working on music since 1999 as a DJ and remixer. Then I became Computer Music and ProStudio Project Manager into a big italian Music Store.
I continued with my Studio/producer activity, based on Apple platforms and different softwares, whilst I studied for degrees in both Audio and Apple application. Then Oneway Srl called me to work, organize and manage (as a store manager) its Apple shop in Legnano, not far from Milan. From March 2008 I became the Sales Manager, Audio Project Manager, Shop Supervisor into the newest Apple Premium Reseller in Como (Italy). After 6 months of very hard work, I decided to change. Now I'm a Sales Account at Irpe Spa, focused on Apple Solutions, Audio and Video, iPhone software development.
