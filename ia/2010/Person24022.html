Bill Roth’s Summary

Bill Roth writes nationally on best practices of actual companies succeeding on growing green revenues.

He is also the author of The Secret Green Sauce that profiles the best practices of actual companies succeeding at growing green revenues.

As President of NCCT he leads a consulting practice on the commercialization of clean tech and the adoption of sustainable business practices.

He is also founder of Earth 2017, a website that posts tweets and blogs on companies and technologies that are pioneering how to grow green revenues.

Bill Roth’s Specialties:

Bill has uniquely developed a national network of pioneering green businesses and entrepreneurs through his past role as the Green Business Coach for Entrepreneur.com. Through his consulting practice he provides insights and advice on pricing, branding and marketing strategies that are proven to grow green revenues. He has also developed the Green Entrepreneur Masters Certification Program that trains on the best practices being used by actual companies to grow green revenues.
