Tom Tapp’s Summary

Highly successful senior content and communications innovator with over a dozen years experience in the development, operations and monetization of digital and print businesses.

Expert at leading both large teams and small teams in content production, product development and audience-specific communications.

Highly collaborative and creative. Excels at seeing the big picture and communicating across diverse disciplines including content, production, technology, marketing and management. Proven track record in executing multiple projects on time and on budget. Excellent communication and people skills.

Notable projects include:
- Launch of Tina Brown's The Daily Beast.com
- Creation, editing of and eventual sale of Hollywood WireTap.com
- Creating and running Variety's glossy lifestyle publication, V Life
- Founding Operation Amped, a service organization for wounded veterans
- Rebranding and relaunch of the new Hollywood.com
- Relaunch of Los Angeles Times' The Envelope
- Development of online video property Dog and Pony for Deca
- Launch of E! Online.com

Tom Tapp’s Specialties:

Content innovation, business development, editorial vision, team leadership and management, brand creation, competitive analysis, managing multiple communication streams, CMS, light HTML.
