Brian Wilcher’s Summary

I am a highly skilled and extremely versatile copywriter, copy editor and proofreader. I have written, edited and proofread web and print content for a wide variety of clients, from a major advertising agency and several online businesses, to an educational company and a published Civil War historian. This includes writing website copy, online articles, email blasts, blog posts, and print brochures; editing eBooks, essays and manuscripts; and proofreading manuscripts, print and web advertisements, educational products, and accounting reports.

My exceptional written and verbal communication skills give me the ability to create written materials that are clear, concise, and persuasive, as well as edit material to make it more readable and engaging. Moreover, my attention to detail and knowledge of grammar conventions make me an excellent proofreader.

I am currently attending classes at The Bookshop with the goal of having finishing my portfolio by the first of next year so I can begin my next career as an advertising copywriter.

Before I began my career as a copywriter/editor/proofreader, I spent a decade as an English, reading and journalism teacher.
