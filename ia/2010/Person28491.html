Brian Holmes’s Summary

Hi, my name is Brian Holmes. I'm an advertising/graphic and web design/business marketing specialist. Here is a little about me...

I was born and raised in Seminole, Florida. The creativity ran in my family beginning with my late grandfather who was an accomplished painter and illustrator and was passed down to my father, uncle, sister, and myself. My drawing talents were noticed as early as age 5 and continued well into high school. Embracing my creative talent and exploratory personality I went on to the International Academy of Design only 6 weeks out of high school and began working towards a degree in Interactive Media. This was the fusion of graphic design, web design, multimedia, audio, and video. In a matter of months I quickly abandoned drawing and developed a thirst for media design for it's aesthetic beauty, yet functional purpose in business advertising.

Unlike most right-brained creative people, I'm also very left brained as well, and find great joy in organizing, structuring, and managing. I'm good at paying attention to the small details while maintaining focus on the big picture. Feeling that this side of my personality wasn't challenged enough with graphic design, I decided to balance my design skills by getting more into business, advertising, and marketing. This combination of traits later led me towards finding my real passion—the fusion of all of my talents—and led me towards starting my own company, Effective Advertising Solutions, in 2006. This was perfect because I could not only run and market my own business, but also other businesses as well. Late 2008, I officially took the plunge and went full-time.

I get great satisfaction from working for myself, and dedicating my life to helping others succeed. I think my client's success is my success. As an entrepreneur at heart I truly feel that this is my calling. I hope that you will put your trust in me and let me help your company reach its potential.
