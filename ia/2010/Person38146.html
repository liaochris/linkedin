Chris Lucerne’s Summary

I enjoy coaching inspirational leaders who take the high road in business and leadership endeavors. They are men and women interested in going beyond just getting extraordinary results. They want their influence to be felt... to truly make a difference in the world, and to know that their life and work deeply matter. They want to to fluently use a multi-facteted leadership toolkit - knowing what tool to use with whom, and in what situation... while emerging deep wells of potential, integrity, and authenticity.

With 25 years experience in the field of human development, including executive positions in the corporate world, I offer mastery in inspirational leadership, mindful relationship (with self and others), emotional literacy, and whole-brain manifestation.

Fortunate to have focused on my own evolution for over two decades, I've experienced some of the top transformational work taught by some truly great thought leaders of our day.

Being an idealist, I envision a day when integrity is fully restored in our world and our leaders inspire us to take the high road to be the best we can be. It is my pleasure to coach inspirational leaders, groups, individuals, and couples to follow this most satisfying path, and in doing so to ripple out their influence to the people in their direct path and beyond.

I am also the co-creator of the transformational program Imagination Made Real, A Path to Reliable and Sustainable Manifestation. My creative partners are Gayle Knight-Colman, CFP, Gay Hendricks, Ph.D., and Kathlyn Hendricks, Ph.D. (www.imaginationmadereal.com).

Chris Lucerne’s Specialties:

(1) Leaders who want to cultivate an environment where their team naturally follows with enthusiasm and commitment. (2) Groups with a shared vision working together in a harmonious and energizing way. (3) Individuals interested in being their own authority, embracing their worth, and aligning with a deep sense of integrity while creating a life of purpose and passion. (4) Couples who want to create greater companionship, love, commitment, and equality.
