David Bascom’s Summary

Degreed professional / discipline leader with international experience in Environmental Health, Safety & Security field. Certified in Environmental and Safety with Security expertise. Champion & stimulate EHS&S efforts company-wide with a proven record of sucessful incident reduction and full compliance to regulatory requirements.
