Claire Chapman’s Summary

I'm a Social Media Coach and Specialist and International Coach Federation ACC Accredited Coach and Coach Supervisor.

What you need to know about me: I love helping individuals and businesses connect and communicate more effectively. When we stop to listen and then communicate with thought and care we build effective action that gets results.

What I do and how I can help you:

Social Media Specialist: UKSocialMedia Coach and for Intendance Ltd.

Independent: www.uksocialmediacoach.co.uk

I use the skills I have gained in corporate life, running my own Coaching Business and as a Career Coach to help businesses and individuals get more value from social media as a tool. I offer coaching and training on how to set up your strategy, how and where to listen into the social web, and specific training on individual platforms like Twitter and LinkedIn.

Intendance Ltd: www.intendance.co.uk

I am social media specialist for Intendance and work with a variety of organisations to help them leverage social media as part of their marketing and engagement plans.


Owner, The Fabulous Coaching Company

The Fabulous Coaching Company offers a range of services to coaches and small businesses including:
- Coaching and Mentoring for coaches
- Get Clients Now Marketing Support
- Coaching Supervision
- Career Coaching and Job Search

You'll get value from working with me if you want to be a more effective coach, build your belief in yourself and your business, and want the additional support and accountability of working with a coach.

I am available for training, speaking, and workshops as well as individual and group coaching, social media support and coaching supervision.

I'm looking to connect with individuals and businesses who want support with their social media plans and implementation, and coaches ready to step up their coaching
