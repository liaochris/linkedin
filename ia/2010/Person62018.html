Edmund White’s Summary

Edmund is a Solutions Engineer in the SharePoint Practice at Hubbard One (a Thomson Reuters Company). He has a background in engineering and IT consulting and has worked with over 60 clients in nearly five years. Most recently, Edmund served as a consultant for SWC Technology Partners where he designed and completed SharePoint farm infrastructure migrations and upgrades. He also has experience as the head engineer for multi-site enterprise software implementations across international and domestic locations. Today, Edmund can be found configuring custom OneView implementations for many Hubbard One's clients. Edmund graduated from Northwestern University with a Bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering. A Boston native, when he's not working at his computer, Edmund paints abstract artwork in acrylic and is active in the Chicago arts and culture scene.
