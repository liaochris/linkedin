Gerhard Schwandt’s Summary

5 Star Executive Chef:
I came to the USA in 1973 as an Executive Chef trained and apprenticed in Germany and achieved a Five Star Rating & a Michelin Star within the first 2 years. Without having a very high level of efficiency it is not possible to reach that level.

Sales Leader:
When I quit working as a Chef I started to work for a sales and promotion company (East Coast Promotions), broke every sales record and then bought the company and expanded it.

CEO & Business Owner:
Simultaneously to owning and running East Coast Promotions I started an import business - Norma Set, Inc - (bringing disposable cloth [felt] chef hats from Germany into the USA) and expanded that business to where we were selling 1000 hat a day. I then sold both companies and worked as the Executive Chef for Majestic Cruise Lines for 2 1/2 years.

Consultant:
Also, during these years I continued education in the Hubbard Administrative Management Technology procedures and their delivery and became a Certified Consultant.

Personal Efficiency Expert-Plus and Coach:
In 2002 I trained on and then further helped to develop the specific Efficiency Management System coaching procedures in Germany together with my partner, Markus Vogel. There are over 1000 satisfied clients in Germany alone and you can see a whole lot of testimonials from our US clients on our US website at www.efficiencyexperts.us.

Our GOAL
is to establish a much higher level of efficiency in the leaders of the US business and organizational structures, thereby increasing the ability of these business and organization to flourish and prosper at a whole new level and so reach and maintain a higher level of competitiveness.
A CEMSG (Certified Efficiency Management System Graduate) is very quickly becoming a desired, wanted and looked for designation.

Gerhard Schwandt’s Specialties:

Our step by step approach enables you, the business owner & executive, to take a fresh new look at your existing systems and procedures and then helps you to design and implement the most effective and productive systems to process your work flows. These systems make it possible for you to stay on top even in times of change and to stay focused on the growth of your business and organization.
