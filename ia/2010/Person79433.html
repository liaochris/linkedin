Hal Collins’s Summary

I am one of the managing partners for a company called AcrobatAnt. We are made up of old friends and co-workers who have been working together for a decade. We have survived through four different companies and we are still going strong.

I am an advertising and marketing executive with 20 years experience in Internet Presence Management (IPM), social and digital media, SEM / SEO, advertising, mobile marketing, traditional marketing and web technology. I try to combine a strong strategic, creative and analytic skill set with excellent communication and team building capabilities to help small to large companies tackle complex marketing problems.

I am a strategic thinker and driven professional that will utilize excellent skills and a passion for agency operations, account management, strategic planning, marketing, sales and marketing strategy for customer acquisition / retention.

I am currently in a position that is focusing on growing the agency. Responsible for maintaining a portfolio of business while targeting and selling new business opportunities. Creating the necessary structure for the company to meet aggressive growth goals.

I have been responsible for client marketing strategies and agency services, including but not limited to:

• Integrated marketing
• Internet and intranet development
• E-business development
• Brand strategy
• Identity development
• Multimedia, broadcast, video
• Relationship marketing
• B to B marketing
• Direct to consumer marketing
• Offline marketing
• Print
• Direct Mail
• Outdoor
• In-store / POP

In the past, I have been responsible for client advertising and marketing strategies for many organizations such as Abbott Laboratories, ARAMARK, CITGO, ConAgra Foods, Franklin Covey, GE, Pentair Corp., RE/MAX, Sporting News and Enterprise Car Rental.

Prior to AcrobatAnt, I managed the creative group in the advertising and marketing agency within Fair Isaac Corp.

Hal Collins’s Specialties:

Advertising / Marketing operations and strategies, Internet Presence Management (IPM), e-business, brand strategy, customer experience and online/offline marketing.
