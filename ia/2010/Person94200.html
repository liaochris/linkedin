Jeff Zugale’s Summary

I'm a concept artist with many years of experience and more shipped games than most people who've been in the business twice as long. I want to be challenged to create better and better art for ambitious high-quality game, tv, and film projects.

Jeff Zugale’s Specialties:

My strengths are environment and hardware prop/vehicle design. I especially enjoy "real world" scenarios and sci-fi action/adventure.
