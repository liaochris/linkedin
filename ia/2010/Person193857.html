Stéphane Guérin’s Summary

I have been working for 10 years in Internet related environment. This has been from tech support to e-commerce analyst while being a database manager and web site developer.

I have founded many web sites and some of them have become huge success like dating web sites: 4adate.net, SeducZone.com and online webmasters community WebdOnline.com. Those web sites have been sold. Two of them during the Internet boom making me one of the few people from Quebec who profited from this boom.

Since then, I completed an IT degree and I recently obtained a MBA with a e-business specialization. I also founded few companies. Even though it didn't always bring the expected results, this brings me to great jobs opportunities or a lot of business contacts.

My main project is Percute Analytique. This is an online web mining & Web analytics application helping e-business managers better understand their visitors' behaviour in order to make the right strategic decision.

Beside, Percute Technologies offer e-business services such as web traffic analysis and e-marketing strategy.
