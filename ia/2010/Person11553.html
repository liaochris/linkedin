Andrew Bennett’s Summary

Experienced Multi-Discipline Team Manager with 16 years technical managerial experience, including 7 years as CE Product Lead and 9 years Video Game Production.

Extensive experience of taking Hardware, Firmware and Software (shrink-wrapped and Web) projects from initial thought to final retail product, including cost-reduction and China-based software development and factory bring-up (4 months in China in 2005) – working with teams of up to 50 local and remote staff.

Widespread knowledge of all computer industry-related trades including project management; programming; art; design; localization and hiring as well as Marketing and PR.

Andrew Bennett’s Specialties:

Project management of large, 50 person, cross-discipline teams; design and manufacturing of consumer products; Purchasing and Manufacturing in China.
