Marilín Gonzalo’s Summary

Online journalist, with broad experience in many areas of journalism and tech culture internet oriented.

I am the Director of Editorial Content at Hipertextual, one of the most important blogs' network at the Spanish language speaking internet. We produce and create blogs and we help companies to understand this new media.

In Argentina I have worked as producer of radio and tv programs. I have also done research on national and international news, political and media discurse semiotic analysis.

Since I came to live to Madrid, I have been writing articles for magazines, and I've also been very connected with online media. I have worked as content provider for some websites. I had been exploring new possibilities in journalism and microblogging.

During 4 months I worked full time as Editor in Chief for an online press agency that produced video content for journalistic media on the internet, focusing on tech and internet news. Reporting directly to the director, I led the team and surveilled the quality of the content produced, in both technical and narrative aspects.

After this, I started being requested for some internet companies to guide them over social networks and other internet related projects. I currently also work as Internet Consultant for some clients.

In June 2009, I started working as Director of Editorial Content for Hipertextual. Part of my duties consists in the coordination of a team of +65 editors and 16 blogs, and giving my insight over the projects of the company.

I am fluent in English, Spanish and Dutch, I also speak a bit of French.
