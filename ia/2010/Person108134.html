Julian Kulkarni’s Summary

Julian Kulkarni

I am an International Marketeer and Entrepreneur, having set up and launched several businesses across Europe and globally. I have also helped other companies to establish themselves and grow in Europe. What I enjoy most is driving or supporting European and International market execution.

I currently run my own marketing consultancy, Market Execution, and am advising easyFairs as Chief Marketing Officer. easyFairs is an innovative company which is revolutionising the trade show and business to business event industry - providing more focus and return for all participants - to find out more justvisit www.easyfairs.com and www.marketexecution.com

Before this, I was at Ariba (Formerly FreeMarkets) which I joined in 2002 and was responsible for building the company’s marketing efforts (including direct marketing, communications, PR, analyst relations and events) in Europe.

Prior to joining FreeMarkets, I co-founded a software company, JobPartners, where I was Marketing Director. So I am a dot com survivor!! In this capacity I managed the company’s lead generation across Europe and created the company’s brand identity. We also raised over $25M of private equity

I spent 4 years as a European Marketing Manager with SAS Institute, the world’s largest business intelligence software company. I have also worked at Barclays Bank, the BBC, Ideal Loisirs a Travel Agency and a Management consultancy.

When I was 18 I thought it was a good idea to get a bachelor’s degree in European Business Administration from Middlesex University, London, UK and a European Management Degree from Reims Management School, France so I did - which was a great fun.

I am interested to hear from people to share ideas and opportunities partnering, business development, investment opportunities, services, software and technology startups or businesses looking for transformation, from anywhere around the world.
