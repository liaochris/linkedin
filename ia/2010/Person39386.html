Christine Sneeringer’s Summary

Christine Sneeringer is the Vice President of Marketing And Advertising for Prudential Homesale Services Group, one of the nation’s largest and most successful real estate firms. She is an expert in leveraging media to create consumer relationships and leads for real estate agents and brokers. Christine earned her Bachelor of Communications degree from Penn State, her MBA from Eastern University, is a Certified Integrity Coach, and will attend and graduate from Leadership Breakthrough One at Rapport Leadership International in Las Vegas, Nevada.
