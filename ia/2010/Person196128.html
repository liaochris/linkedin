Steve Urquhart’s Summary

Steve Urquhart is the founder of T21 Solutions, a niche consulting firm based in Florida, with an emphasis on corporate communication, training, human resources strategy and business development.

Steve was most recently vice president of workforce performance for Workforce Florida, the state's public-private partnership that oversees and monitors the administration of the state’s workforce system.

Steve previously led the corporate communications and investor relations functions for Enterprise Florida, the public-private partnership responsible for leading Florida's statewide economic development efforts, and served as interim director of the Florida Defense Alliance.

Prior to Enterprise Florida, Steve worked for Siemens AG's Power Generation group in Germany as the head of employee and leadership communications worldwide. He has held a number of leadership roles in corporate communications and human resources, with companies in Orlando, Las Vegas and Boston.

Additionally, Steve had a 14-year career in the US Army, including active duty tours with the 82nd Airborne Division during Desert Shield/Desert Storm and the 1st Infantry Division in Bosnia, and six years with the 143rd Transportation Command headquarters in Orlando, Florida.

Steve is a graduate of Rollins College and serves a variety of roles in the Central Florida community, with organizations such as Habitat for Humanity, Rollins Philanthropy Leadership Center, the Rollins College President's Leadership Council, and Heart of Florida United Way. He is a member of the International Association of Business Communicators, the Small Business Resource Network (SBRN), the Greater Orlando Organizational Development (GOOD) Network, and serves as a Trustee of the Florida Chamber Foundation.
