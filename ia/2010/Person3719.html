Amol Kelkar’s Summary

Amol Kelkar has more than a decade of technology and executive leadership experience building web services and desktop software such as VideoReadr, aflatune.com, The Game Net, Windows Live, Internet Explorer and InfoPath. He is passionate about all things Web, machine intelligence and music.

He has been an active inventor and has 8 patents filed for diverse inventions such as electronic forms digital signatures, unified localization platform for web services and extensions to the CSS language. He holds a Master of Science degree in Computer Sciences from the University of Texas at Austin and has multiple publications in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning research. An active musician, he is the founding member of 2 music bands and has performed at many live concerts.

Amol Kelkar’s Specialties:

Consumer facing web applications, Machine learning, Executive leadership, Business development

    (E-Learning industry)

    February 2010 — Present (9 months)

    VideoReadr is an online video transcription service. With VideoReadr, you can write video-synchronized text transcript for any YouTube video. The resulting VideoReadr can be embedded in your web sites and the transcript text can be searched. Transcripts are not limited to plain text, they can contain rich text and images too. Viewers can share links to specific paragraphs in a video. VideoReadr can make video content (especially instructional video content) more accessible, searchable and usable. A couple of links to demo interesting features -

    * Jump directly to a specific paragraph in a video: Push! - http://videoreadr.com/read/richard-st-john-secrets-of-success-in-8-words-3-minutes?time=119.22
    * Search transcripts across videos - http://videoreadr.com/read?search=privilege
    * Xtreme SEO for videos! Each paragraph in the transcript gets its own page with contents from the paragraph as its title and description. Check out page title and description for this page - http://videoreadr.com/read/steve-jobs-2005-stanford-commencement-address?time=549.26

    VideoReadr will allow users to purchase transcripts - they will specify a video and we will get transcript written for them. Pricing will be based on video length, for example it would cost $45 to transcribe a 1 hr video. This feature will be available soon.

    VideoReadr.com is based on the timelined web applications platform ("Infusion" platform) that we have created. In fact, it is the simplest application of the platform. Infusion can create more involved and domain specific applications by adding more UI elements like table of contents and interaction types like pauses and loops. For example, VideoReadr shows video and transcript, and by directly accessing Infusion platform's capabilities, synchronized slideshows or table of contents can be added easily.
