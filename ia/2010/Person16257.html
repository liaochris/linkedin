Ari Patrick’s Summary

I am an independent game developer working on my Master's in Game Production. Having worked on a number of mods and game development projects, I've had the opportunity to meet and work with many talented individuals, both in the independent and professional game development communities. My goal, after completing my Master's, is to make the switch from independent to professional developer, and begin my career as a Producer.

I consider game development to be an evolving art form, growing as rapidly as the technology and people that power it. I feel it is my duty as a Producer to enrich the people I work with, helping them realize both their individual and collaborative potentials, to facilitate the development of better processes and products.

I have experience in all of the following:

Languages: C/C++, C#, Lua, Javascript

Development Environments: Microsoft Visual Studio 2005/2008, Unity Engine, Adobe Flash CS3/4

Production Tools: Adobe InDesign, Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Visio, Project, Pivotal Labs' Pivotal Tracker, Subversion (SVN), Avid Alienbrain, TestTrack Pro

Ari Patrick’s Specialties:

Institutional Review Board (IRB) Certified; Strong understanding of many Traditional & Agile methodologies; Working with diverse teams, in terms of ability and culture; Maintaining professionalism in high-stress situations
