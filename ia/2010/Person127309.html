Art Secor’s Summary

Founded the Southgroup Companies in the Spring of 2001 including Southgroup Realty, Southgroup Real Estate Investments, Southgroup Mortgage, Southgroup Development and Southgroup Marketing.

Principal in Coastal Venture Group, a residential land development company specializing in waterfront and mountain gated communities.

Working in the real estate business for 14 years including investments, both commercial and residential. A big empasis on raw land, marketing, horizontal development and pre-development.

Personally invested in Real Estate since 1996. This includes commercial, residential and land.

Southgroup implements comprehensive develoment and marketing strategies, land sales, builder relations and financing consulting.

Founded London Wilson Capital (LWC) in 2007. LWC services borrowers for a variety of capital needs. Whether it requires a partnership, joint ventures, credit enhancements or straight capital. Capital includes either bank, institutional, commercial, corporate, and/or private money.
