Randy J. Mohr’s Summary

As a career real estate professional (since 1975), I have performed as an award winning salesperson, managing broker, recruiter, trainer, business consultant, franchise sales director and regional services director. During that time, the only constant in our industry has been change...some of it better than others! My unique path has led me to my current role as Broker of Record for Great American Real Estate in Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky & Ohio as well as a Senior Regional Sales Manager for North American Apartment Association, a division of Kinetoscope Media. Inc.
