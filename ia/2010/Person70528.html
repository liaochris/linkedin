Forest Williams’s Summary

I have been in technology sales and new business development for over 25 years. After a great career in the semconductor industry I founded a GPS tracking company (VLST) which helped lauch the world's first portable GPS tracking device, the TrimTrac. I am now working with two different companies in the emerging Electric Vehicle market. Electric Motors Corporation is introducing the first Plug-In Hybrid Electric pickup truck, and Liberty PlugIns, which is developing pay station enabled EV charging stations for the commercial parking industry.

Forward Sales Concepts is developing new ways for companies to extend their marketing efforts through innovative partnerships with government programs intended to stimulate the economy and generate new "green" jobs.
