Victor Babbitt’s Summary

Victor Babbitt is an entrepreneur and manager who has started and helped innovative companies create new service offerings, analyze and strategize new markets, develop key alliances, execute on aggressive business plans, find funding and find and execute acquisitions.

Victor’s value to an organization lies in his unique blend of technical, managerial and entrepreneurial skills that has allowed him to consistently invent, initiate, develop and grow business opportunities from a blank slate.

Victor has been involved in large-scale battery systems and the energy industry since 1980, and he founded and later sold what became the largest industrial battery sales and service company in the Western United States. Victor is an expert in the quickly moving technology for grid-scale batteries to supplant carbon-based generation, supply ancillary energy services, and support renewable energy projects.

In addition, Victor has 4 technical patents, managed the Department of Defense internet voting project, wrote and managed development of graphics software in the Microsoft Operating Systems, and founded a built a health-related website network sold in December of 2008 for 7 figures to a public company.
