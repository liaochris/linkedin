MOHAMED ELBANNA (CAMS, CFE & CIFE)’s Summary

Mohamed Elbanna is currently the Chief Executive Officer of International Compliance Expert which provides unique services to US banking training providers. He trains the CEOs and Top executives to get familiar with Middle East culture and provide special consultancy on Public Relations and creating a professional alliance with the main Middle EAST counterparts to increase their market share in the highly Competitive Middle East Markets.


Mohamed Elbanna is also head of Risk Management and Chief Compliance Committee Officer for Galaxia International Service - a Money Services Business serving the Middle East Region. Mohamed has more than twenty years of experience in the Financial Services Industry in compliance, accounting and operations management with his current focus being Corporate Governance , Auditing , Compliance and Anti Money Laundering. Prior to this position, Mohamed was the Senior Accountant at Galaxia International Service where he worked closely with the management team on strategic and operational cash management issues. Before joining Galaxia, Mohamed spent ten years as a Director for the Indo-Arab Granite Corporation.

Mohamed is a Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS), Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE), Certified Islamic Finance Executive (CIFE) candidate for the Certified Sarbanes-Oxley Act (CSOX).

Mohamed was the first ever-Arab descendant to graduate from American Banker Association - Compliance School (ABA). Mohamed is known to be expert in all US regulations related to banking operation, deposit and lending compliance.
