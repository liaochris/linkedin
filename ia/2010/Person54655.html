Dennis Foley’s Summary

I am in the business of helping people who are frustrated with trying to get a call back from their contractors, upset that their contractors made a mess and left trash behind, worried that they have to find another contractor to paint and repair areas that the plumber had to get access to, and angry that the jobs never get finished.
I have been self employed for 30 years, and it's amazing to me how much I still have to learn! The industry has changed a lot the last few years, and we have been changing with it. There is a distinct lack of professionals in the trades, and we are always trying to cultivate a professional approach to what we do. Because of the prevailing practices that people accept from tradesmen, it is sometimes an uphill climb.
Our goal is to have clients that rely on us for all the repairs in their houses, not just plumbing and heating, but remodeling, electrical work, even painting. If we don't do it, we can bring in an experienced, competent professional someone whose work we trust and guarantee. A professional that we would use in our own homes.
I have been remodeling houses all my adult life, and not just as a plumber, but as a contractor and carpenter. We have the experience and knowledge to do renovations that will last for many years.
