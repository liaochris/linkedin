Beverly May’s Summary

Founder & President of Oxford Technology Ventures, LLC http://www.OxfordTechnologyVentures.com

Oxford Technology Ventures, LLC offers expert strategy, user experience/ usability and new product development consulting services for the Internet, Mobile and Software.

We help businesses succeed with their technology initiatives by merging the conceptual realms of business strategy and management consulting with the applied disciplines of technology product development, usability and user experience.

Specifically, our services help businesses to:
1. Generate significantly increased revenue,
2. Foster greater user loyalty, satisfaction and engagement,
3. Attract and retain new visitors and consumers, and
4. Streamline internal processes to deliver significant operational efficiencies and cost savings.

Our services include:
-- Product & Business Strategy & Conceptualization
-- User Experience/ Usability/ Information Architecture
-- Business & Systems Analysis: Creation of Business, Functional & Technical Requirements

We also work with other specialist vendors in Graphic Design, Web Development and Software Development to provide the best boutique end-end technology conceptualization, design and development services.
