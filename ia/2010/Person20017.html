Barb Miller’s Summary

Developing Leaders - Building Collaborative Teams

Seasoned (salt, pepper and spicy sauce, plus 30 years of experience) consultant assisting executives to optimize organization and personal performance by enhancing leadership competency, building collaborative teams and improving virtual work effectiveness.

I coach leaders to achieve their personal best. I not only help individuals improve their leadership effectiveness but I also work with them to improve the entire organization. By coaching an executive team, I am able to then work with them to improve both their team effectiveness as well as the organization's effectiveness. I take coaching to a systems level. Most coaches work only 1-1. I work to engage individuals and the organization in continuous improvement. I use a suite of 360 Assessment tools that are targeted to the private, non-profit or public sectors. Also, customize 360's if an organization has it's own leadership competency model or wants to develop one to support their culture.

I also have special expertise in multi- generational issues. I help managers and teams work across the four generations: Seniors, Boomers, Gen X and Millennials.

Authored: ReInventing Work: Relinking Live and Livelihood to Benefit Business and Staff. Co-developed Virtual@Work: a six module, web-based, interactive tool to teach managers and teams how to work virtually.

Facilitate leadership development and team building. Conduct virtual offsites and teach managers and teams how to improve their virtual leadership and team effectiveness.

Using the 5 Dynamics Assessment, coach managers and teams to improve their performance and productivity.

See more about our products and services at www.virtualconnection.biz and www.artemismanagement.com

Our recent clients include: Hewlett-Packard, Intel, Accenture, Merck, Juniper Network, AMD, The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, HSBC, Charles Schwab, UC Berkeley, Stanford University, and the Ford Foundation.

Barb Miller’s Specialties:

leadership development, executive and managerial coaching, team building, virtual leadership and team effectiveness, managing change, women in leadership, managing conflict, strategic planning, performance management, empowerment, behavioral interviewing, work-life balance, leading the multi generation workforce.
