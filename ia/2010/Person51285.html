David Leopold’s Summary

For the last 35 years, I have been a Small Business Owner, interacting with more than 35,000 Small Business Owners. In the 90's, in addition to maintaining a low profile with a Small Business venture, I maintained a high profile with an International Organization, MATRIXX Marketing/Convergys, sharing my expertise in the Insurance Direct Marketing(place).
Today we are focused on distributing information via LIVE Internet Broadcasts and "the book route", either through the manufacturing process or online technology.
I can work with other Small Business Owners to leverage their "Intellectual Property" and market it to the 30-50MM Small Businesses in this country.
With our "untraditional" publishing expertise, the Small Business Entrepreneur will realize more money for their efforts.
