Iyabo Asani’s Summary

As a Life Strategies Coach, I help professionals who feel stuck in their corporate jobs to define and create their life work. From there, I help them create successful businesses around that life work.

I practiced law for 20 years. Personally, I met every standard of “success.” I had graduated from a top notch law school, Georgetown University, and for 14 years I owned a small law practice with three lawyers and ten support staff.

Yet, I was burnt out and not fulfilled. I also experienced a lot of guilt because I felt I “should” be happy. I came to the realization that practicing law was no longer in alignment for the vision that I held of my professional life. I left the practice of law and moved into coaching where I was able to create my own personalized definition of success.

I discovered that I had a gift for seeing people as whole and complete and helping them express that in their business. I naturally “read” clients through the clogs in their money and business energy.

I attended coaching school and focused on entrepreneurial coaching and business strategy consulting. As I mentor and coach for small business owners, I am in awe of the capacity of the entrepreneurial spirit to create powerful growth in the marketplace.

I invite you to receive your own free copy of the report and audio that I prepared called, "How To Charge What You Are Worth" at http://www.coachiyabo.com/linkedin
