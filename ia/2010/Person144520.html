    For example, you need to reach a "Franchise Seeker." Your campaign can look like this: 500,000 banner ad impressions targeted to franchise seekers, $150k+, males. With behaviorally targeted banners ads you can reach them across a network of 14,000 websites. So no matter where they go: Usatoday.com, CareerBuilder.com, Eharmony.com as long as they are within the network of 14,000 websites they will see your ads. Then you can "retarget" them with additional banner ads. If they go to your website but leave, we can drop a pixel on your website so your potential customers can then be followed wherever they go with an additional message to entice them back!
