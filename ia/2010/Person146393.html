Milisa Burns, Certified Professional Coach’s Summary

As a certified professional coach, I specialize in working with professional women in high stress work environments. I help people focus, and then act on, what's important to them in their lives and to really connect with their inner wisdom. I love to help people grow and evolve in a proactive, strategic way that will help them reach their full potential and thus find more meaning and fulfillment in their lives.

I am a Associate Certified Coach (ACC) with the International Coach Federation and an Adler Certified Professional Coach (ACPC) with Adler International Learning Inc./OISE University of Toronto. I also have a BA from McGill and Law degree from the University of Toronto.
