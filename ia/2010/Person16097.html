Ari B. Adler’s Summary

I'm a former journalist who is now putting those skills to use on the other side of the industry as a media-relations professional.

By day I'm the Communications Administrator for Delta Dental of Michigan, Ohio and Indiana; by night I'm an adjunct instructor at Michigan State University.

I serve as a professional adviser for the MSU chapter of the Public Relations Student Society of America. I'm also available as a speaker, trainer and consultant. More info here: http://aribadler.wordpress.com/speaking-and-training/

Ari B. Adler’s Specialties:

Media relations, new media/social networking, message development, communications, strategic planning, issues management, freelance writing & editing.
