Carl Gerber’s Summary

IT Executive with over 25 years experience. Proven leadership ability to develop and execute strategic information technology plans aligned with corporate business goals with Domestic and International success in Fortune 500 companies. Track record of achieving business value, IT transformation, cultural change to adopt analytics in decision-making, leveraging data assets across complex environments, building cohesive teams and turning around failing projects.

Carl Gerber is a frequent presenter on Leadership, Master Data Management and Enterprise Architecture topics. He is co-founder of the Columbus Business Intelligence Roundtable and past President of the Southwest Ohio Data Management Association.

Carl Gerber’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning, IT Transformation, IT Governance, Application Rationalization, Taming Data Chaos in Large, Complex Landscapes, Enterprise Architecture, Business Intelligence, Data Warehousing, Master Data Management, Electronic Commerce, Information Security, Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX), Outsourcing, Mergers & Acquisitions, International Business
