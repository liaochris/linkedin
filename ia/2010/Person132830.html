Marina Goodman’s Summary

Markets are always shifting, and life is never static. Our goals and circumstances change, and we need our financial plans to change with them. High net worth clients that want to protect, grow, and ultimately transfer their wealth need advisors that truly understand their personal and financial objectives, family and financial goals.

Brinton Eaton provides a full suite of integrated financial services, a client-centered philosophy and a personalized approach to help clients safeguard their lifestyles, now and in the future. At Brinton Eaton, we believe our financial, tax and investment services are best provided within an integrated framework by a single trusted advisory firm that can best meet individualized needs.

As a specialist in investment analysis and portfolio management, I was drawn to Brinton Eaton because of its commitment to getting to know clients personally, in order to understand their true financial picture and their goals. At the same time, Brinton Eaton’s approach is scientific and analytical: We are diligent about seeking the best approaches and tools to not only grow our clients’ wealth, but also protect it when markets do not cooperate. A significant part of our research and client service involves asset allocation modeling, rebalancing and portfolio protection.

I particularly enjoy helping people prepare for unforeseen life events, as well as adapting to change as circumstances require it. Examples include providing for a special needs child, ensuring families are protected in the event of a sudden death, preparing for long-term elder care, and ensuring that a family’s legacy is transferred in the most financially efficient way possible.
