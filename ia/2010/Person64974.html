Aaron Endelman’s Summary

I'm a Principal Software Engineer in Silicon Valley with over 25 years' experience writing, designing, and architecting software. Specialties include design and development of object-oriented software, GUIs, software tools; C++, C, Java, Lisp, scripting languages; Windows, Unix, Linux; EDA; ATE.

I enjoy doing state-of-the-art software development in well-managed companies, be they startups or long established. I like contributing to all aspects of product development, from architecture through implementation, and see myself spending more time architecting as my career progresses.

I'm particularly interested in exploring opportunities with scientific and medical instrumentation companies.
