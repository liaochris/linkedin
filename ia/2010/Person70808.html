Franchise Speakers’s Summary

SPEAK! was launched in 2008 by Katrina Mitchell. It is a customer-focused speakers bureau that pairs top-notch speakers and trainers with franchise companies to create compelling, highly charged events for annual conferences, regional meetings and webinar training. We consistently deliver clear, action-oriented content coupled with the right personality for your organization and the theme of your event.

SPEAK! is committed to building long-lasting personal relationships with our customers based on trust, honesty and our willingness to exceed expectations to ensure an outstanding event.

    Speak!

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    2008 — 2010 (2 years )

    We are always interested in adding new speakers to our database. In order to offer the very best possible service to our clients we carefully screen all possible candidates.

    1. Biography
    2. Video/CD – Demo video and if available, a recent full length recording of an actual keynote, presentation or seminar.
    3. References – 3 references from clients who have hosted you in the last year. Please include name, phone number, email, date of presentation, name of organization and the topic you presented.
    4. Photo – A polished, professional headshot – 5 x 7 vertical
    5. Topic or topics -Outlines or PDF’s of your 3-5 most requested presentations (no need to send if these are included on your One Sheet)
    6. List of venues where you have spoken in the past 18 months
    7. One Sheet(s) or professional Press Kit which includes your biography, topics, program descriptions, testimonials and a professional photo
    8. Travel requirements
    9. AV & room set-up requirements
    10. Fee Structure
    11. Testimonials (if not included on your One Sheet)
    12. Any additional material –such as books, articles, audio bundles etc. – you feel would represent your abilities

    Applications are reviewed the last week of each month. If we think you might be a good fit for our team, we will request a date to come and watch you present.
