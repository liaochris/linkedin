    ABOUT RAZORWEST

    Razorwest improves small-to-mid-market companies by delivering a progressive business education and a set of performance improvement services that work together to re-ignite the innovative spirit, increase knowledge bases, build internal skills and capabilities, and move the entire business forward.

    Individual members and small business teams can improve skills and performance with the Razorwest Learning Assistant, an on-demand, intelligent learning system that delivers a business-centered education, tools and techniques, and expert-level guidance right to the workplace.

    Companies can deploy a set of performance improvement services that work together to substantially lower the barriers and costs associated with creating a smarter, more effective and stable workforce.

    Business executives looking to deliver a continuous business learning experience - and also want to encourage innovative thinking, attract and retain high-caliber talent, increase retention rates of existing talent, and create work environments that support happier, motivated, and engaged people - should consider Razorwest.

    Razorwest was founded in 2004 and provides service in North America, Japan, Canada, and in the U.K.
