Michael Kurland M.Ed’s Summary

Michael Kurland, M. Ed., is currently looking for a full time instructional or curriculum design position in education.

Michael Kurland is an academic adviser with the University of Southern California in Los Angeles CA. He has led and executed advisement presentations and trainings throughout his almost four years at USC. He has partnered with committees across campus to further develop software, training, and organization management to help benefit academic advisors.

Michael Kurland is a free-lance instructional and curriculum designer with experience in relationship management, Cognitive Task Analysis (CTA) and Learning Management Systems (LMS). Michael Kurland is also a free-lance marketing consultant with experience in copy-writing and commercial creation to create direct ad to lead generation.

Michael Kurland leverages his creative, analytical and strategic skills to produce innovative marketing and instructional solutions for organizations that aim to grow and improve locally or internationally.
