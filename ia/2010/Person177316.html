Ron Jacobs’s Experience

  • Sponsor

    Jacobs & Stone Multichannel Marketing Communications Certificate Program at DePaul Universtiy

    (Education Management industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    The Ron Jacobs & Bob Stone certificate program is designed to help marketing & communications managers the new marketing environment, where brands can no longer rely upon pushing predetermined messages to mass audiences of consumers, but must instead reach many markets of one. This program teaches the latest strategies, tools, and techniques in direct, interactive, digital, and database marketing including planning, execution, and the right KPI's (Key Performance Indicators) to demonstrate success. Outstanding professionals will teach you through their experience, case studies, and real-world examples how to think like today's consumers and be successful in this new marketplace. And you will learn how to describe and sell these ideas to your CEO, President, CFO, and CMO, with new ways to track the performance of the marketing communications expenditures to reach true accountable communications.

  • Co-Author

    Successful Direct Marketing Methods

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1996 — Present (14 years )

    Ron is co-author with Bob Stone, of the Seventh Edition of Successful Direct Marketing Methods (SDMM) and the updated Eighth Edition which will be released in Fall 2007.

    SDMM is the best selling text and trade book on the tools and techniques of multi-channel direct marketing, selling more than 250,000 copies worldwide. The current edition has been translated into Russian, Romanian and Spanish.
