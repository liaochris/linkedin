I can multi-task on many levels within an organization. If it needs to be done, I can do it. I am a skilled writer who can deliver a multitude of results. I over deliver and under promise. I can clearly communicate to audiences of any type.

Working closely with the Executive Director and Board of Directors, I created the language that established the organization’s authentic style and communicated its mission to donors and the community, writing grant proposals, marketing brochures, web site content and general correspondence.
