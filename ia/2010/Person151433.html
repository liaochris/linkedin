Naneinee (Nan) Ross’s Summary

Nan Ross provides marketing communication and design services to a diverse number of businesses. But, her career turn has finally landed on new media marketing with a twist of style. She is a social butterfly with the passion for computer technology. Nan love to inspire and teach entrepreneurs how to leverage the power of web technology to attract their customers and to gain online visibility. While, she has found the best of both worlds. She believes that solo business owners wear every imaginable hat. That's why she found her freelance practice in 2002, to reduce the hat tricks and support her clients' goals to help grow their entity to its fullest potential. This venture had lead her to a career in marketing consultancy and a burning desire to help companies succeed.

The understanding of an entrepreneur struggles allows Nan to be a motivator who keeps entrepreneurs focus on the goals rather than themselves. Nan is an inclusive leader who believes in the value of branding and building long-term relationships. 14+ years in computer technology should make Nan a geek, but her sense of humor and her vibrant personality belie that stereotype. In the eyes of some, her time and energy as a social media marketing coach, makes her a creative thinker and an adamant researcher but her tenure as a graphic designer offsets that as well. She is a consultant with eyes that see innovation and creativity. Nan will challenge you, involve you and give you the guidance to ease your journey to achieving your marketing goals.
