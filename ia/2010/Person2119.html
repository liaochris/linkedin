Adam Alter’s Summary

Late 90's and early 2000 not only were there no schools you could attend to learn internet marketing practices such as SEM, SEO, and PPC, but there were only a handful of people who had a clue. I played around with SEO on my first personal websites in mid to late 90's, but had to become an expert quickly with the start of my first business.

I've remained obsessed with keeping up with best practices and studies in this area. While having great success with my own businesses, I've learned what a drain they can be and take you away from what you enjoy. Now I'm focusing on using my skills as part of a team to take All Web Leads, Inc. into limitless success.
