Heather Reid’s Summary

A highly successful online publishing, e-learning and library industry professional with broad ranging experience in the for-profit, education and non-profit sectors. Extensive experience in the development, management and aggregation of full-text content, bibliographic and rights metadata. Working knowledge of a broad suite of standards relating to publishing, library systems, rights management and license encoding. Have served as key player in several international standards development initiatives. In-depth knowledge of both U.S. and international copyright issues and practices. Proven success in the areas of product support, content development and information technology management. Stellar track record in developing and delivering new content-based products and services on-time and on-budget.

Many of my positions have involved building new organizations from scratch or re-energizing and re-vamping existing organizations to meet new challenges and changed landscapes. I am an exceptionally strong and effective people manager, with extensive experience building new organizations and implementing organizational change and growth. Fast learner; excellent communicator;always able to get things done. Engaging public speaker. Published writer.
