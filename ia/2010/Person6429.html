Alex Engles’s Summary

SUMMARY

Senior risk management professional with over 14 years experience building risk management capabilities in trading, asset portfolio management, and enterprise wide risk management. Broad knowledge of commodity markets and risk management across a wide range of complex businesses, markets, and risk systems in North America, Asia, and Europe. Specific experience with both proprietary trading and industry hedge books across power, gas, crude, metals, and freight. Excellent communication, team building and leadership skills. Focused on pragmatic application of risk management to safeguard the organization while enabling the commercial enterprise. Value driven from pro-active analysis that results in actionable insight.
