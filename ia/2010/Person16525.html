Arnie Herz’s Summary

Arnie Herz is a nationally recognized lawyer, mediator, speaker and consultant known for his practical and inspired approach to conflict resolution, client counseling and helping lawyers optimize their careers.

Arnie presents engaging and powerful programs on business development, work-life synergy, client relations and professional service delivery to law firms and bar and business associations across the country.

Arnie's work has been featured in a number of distinguished publications, including the Harvard Negotiation Law Review and the New York Law Journal. An article in the ABA Journal characterizes Arnie as "remarkably skilled at helping clients get past the emotions that distort their sense of what they really need." In his popular blog, Legal Sanity, Arnie shares commentary, insights and information on bettering the business of law.

Arnie is a graduate of the University of Michigan (B.A. 1984) and the Fordham University School of Law (J.D. 1991). He began his career as a litigator with the preeminent law firm, Weil Gotshal & Manges LLP. He then became a partner in a thriving Wall Street practice. In 2000, he started his own law firm and currently handles a range of matters for a diverse group of clients and industries.

Arnie is also very active as a private and court-appointed mediator.

For more information about Arnie Herz, please call 516.767.0800, email arnie@arnieherz.com or visit www.arnieherz.com.
