Lorraine Marks’s Summary

I create images in human form to express what existence is like in our skin, our brief experience on this swirling orb. In a gesture I capture an emotion, a feeling we have all had, a sense that we are not alone, not the first, not the last.

The soul voice speaks through my hands. I am compelled beyond my will to articulate through clay what my spirit knows. Often I am not sure what it is I have learned, or what I am ordained to teach, until the clay shows me.

The naked male figure is any man, the female nude is every woman. There is no reference to time and place, socio-economic status, just us, naked and exposed.

The wrapping, our flesh and bones, the exterior, is merely the perch from which we view the world. It is the conveyance which moves us through our experiences, transporting us into and out of our next or last great adventure. The figure accompanies our soul on its journey, delivers us to our emotional, spiritual and physical destination. Our eyes are the portals for inner and outer sight. A gesture is the embodiment of self-expression.

  • Sculptor

    Gallerie LaReine

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    1981 — Present (29 years )

    I sculpt nudes. A naked man is any man, a naked woman is every woman. We are all born naked, and without clothes there is no frame of reference related to a date and time, or country, or socio-economic status. I try to capture the essence of our common experience as humans on this planet. The gesture indicates an emotional state, feeling, or situation.

    'The Hanging Man' is a man at the end of his rope, trying to hold on to his possessions, relationships, etc. The end of the rope is slipping through his fingers. Everyone has felt that way at one time or another.

    'The Climbing Man' has let go of his "stuff" and is trying to pull himself up, grab on with both hands, away from the edge.

    Experts and amateurs alike, agree that my work is not erotic. It is something other...human, real, and contains significant emotional impact. Not all of it is beautiful. I do not sculpt beauty for beauty sake.
