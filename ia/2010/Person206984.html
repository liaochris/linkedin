Tony Mclachlan’s Summary

Workplace Connections aim to provide SME's across UK with Independent advice
We can save you time and money by optimising your existing office space, and in turn improve staff retention and productivity.
With over 20 years experience in Facilities Management, Workplace Connections can ensure your project is delivered on time and within budget.
By ensuring your project is stress and hassle free, we enable you to focus on your core business and maximise your bottom line.
Adding value not cost.

Workplace Connections are not connected to any other company or supplier and in turn can offer unbiased assistance and advice; ensuring the right people first time.

Not relocating or in the market for new office furniture? Let Workplace Connections help improve your existing office space with minimal cost with our “Office Doctor” service.

Independence. Working for YOU; saving you time and money more importantly focusing in on your two main investments, your staff and property.
Let us worry about your worries.
