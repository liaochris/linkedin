Ralph Modica’s Summary

At Phase 1 Digital we are passionate about speaking through visual media. For us, video is a story telling vehicle that has the power to engage, and stir a multitude of emotions. We treat editing as an art form, using images, video clips, graphics and text as the medium with which to paint an unfolding story. It is a continually exciting process for us to help our clients convey their message in the most effective way, in their own unique voices. Every story expresses a different mood, and like all good movie making, our approach is intertwined with storyline. Video is our expertise, and it is our joy and passion to help our clients fine hone their message and express it visually. We maintain that in these increasingly visual times, video is one of the most powerful ways to communicate a message.

After 15 years of working in cable network and news graphics, Ralph Modica formed Phase 1 Digital in 2005 in the wake of a changing media industry and landscape. With the arrival of Apple’s Final Cut Pro in the early 2000’s, professional video editing was no longer an unreachable service for corporate businesses and entrepreneurs. Modica witnessed the big ‘giants’ of the NY post-production companies dwindle. Places like National Video, Post Perfect and The Image Group eventually closed as smaller boutique editing houses sprouted up, offering comparable service at a fraction of the cost. He saw an opportunity to bring these services to businesses that needed to upgrade their media exposure and presentation at an affordable rate.
