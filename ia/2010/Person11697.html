Andrew Doan [adoan@medrounds.org]’s Summary

I practice general ophthalmology and perform cataract and anterior segment surgeries. I specialize in eye cancers as an ocular pathologist. My research interests focus on improving the training of medical students and physicians, as well as medical informatics and information technologies.
