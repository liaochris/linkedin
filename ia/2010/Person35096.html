Cézanne Charlebois’s Summary

Cézanne is the owner of Charlebois Associates, a firm that focuses on workplace injuries and disability management. Charlebois Associates is composed of a team of former WSIB adjudicators and CAD-7 specialists who together provide exceptional value and assistance to employers. Cézanne's practices is almost exclusively in the area of management side WSIB claims management for employers and occupational health and safety. Her work in management side WSIB law regularly saves individual companies hundreds of thousands of dollars in compensation costs while helping them to manage their risks in employment law matters and reduce their liabilities in occupational health and safety.

Service to the Community

Cézanne is committed to assisting vulnerable individuals in achieving access to justice. To that end, she will gladly receive calls from organizations who assist women, children and seniors and also vulnerable workers who may be subjected to exploitation and abuse. She has many years experience in the field of violence prevention and is currently working on a book called "Hard Targets" which aims to protect children, not from strangers, but from people they know.
