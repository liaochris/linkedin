Chris Tacy’s Summary

At my core a business and digital strategist - I have spent much of my life in the entrepreneurial community. For a large part of my career I focused on the intersection of Business, Brand, Internet Technology, Professional Services and Venture with a particular interest in the strategy and tactics of successful companies.

I spent years developing expertise and ability in Corporate and Strategic Development and applying it to multiple markets and sectors. I combine this with my experience in sectors as diverse as Venture Capital, Consumer Electronics, Industrial Design, High Technology, Marketing, Publishing, Packaged Goods, Building Products, Automotive, Financial Services, Media and Entertainment, Food Service, Advertising, Non Profits and Specialty Coffee to bring a rather unique and wide perspective to all ventures. Some people call me an odd shaped peg but I prefer to think of myself as unique generalist.

I have more than a decade's track-record of business development success - fifteen years of experience in professional services - and have started and run three companies (with two successful exits).
