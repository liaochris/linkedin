John Thornton’s Summary

Seasoned business executive with proven track record of developing new products and driving them to market. 20 years business development and management experience with responsibilities including program management of complex projects, new product development, manufacturing and supply chain development, lean manufacturing, and strategic sourcing

Demonstrated ability to take complex problems and far-reaching goals,then simplifying into a way that can be translated into solutions and action plans.

Strategic and tactical direction for operations, design and development engineering, sourcing and supply chain during product development. Broad experience to bridge the continuum from product concept through design review phases, product development (including engineering, testing and validation), sourcing and supplier qualification through ultimate release to manufacturing.

Experience as an entrepreneur in new ventures and also as an intrapreneur in global corporations, to take a new idea and create a profitable product.
