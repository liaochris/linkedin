Deirdre Baker’s Summary

Deirdre Baker is the Business and Marketing Manager at Brosnan Risk Consultants as well as an internal investigator.

Brosnan Risk Consultants, LTD is a full service Private Investigation and Security Company with corporate offices located in New York. Brosnan offers a wide range of investigative and security services to corporations, government agencies, and private citizens. Brosnan is fully bonded and insured and is licensed in ten states.

Brosnans security division is comprised of former law-enforcement officers and professionally trained and licensed Security Guards. Our armed services include executive protection, home security, and retail site protection. Our unarmed guard services include commercial real estate security, retail stores, and construction sites.

Brosnan employs a diverse team of professional investigators with backgrounds in law enforcement, private security, litigation, accounting, computer forensics, and military intelligence.

Brosnan Risk Consultants, LTD was founded by Patrick J Brosnan in 1996. Mr. Brosnan is a contributor to Fox News Channel, CNN, MSNBC, and Court TV and has been featured in the Wall Street Journal and the New York Times.
