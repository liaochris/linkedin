Randy Malinoff’s Summary

An accomplished digital leader in entertainment, media, business development, social media and e-commerce revenue generation with demonstrated ability to expand an organizations online vision from needs assessment to execution. A collaborative strategic thinking digital executive with a proven track record of success in digital media and emerging online markets for lifestyle brands.

In 2007, Randy started Thats It! Media Group and developed the first of kind "shoppable" technology that identifies merchandise for an e-commerce transaction directly on digital images and video. Thats It! was used on several Conde Nast Style.com online promotions with P&G and a media promotions featuring Kim Kardashian and Adrianne Curry.

In 2008, Randy formed a joint venture with B1 Media Group and developed and marketed a new shoppable format for BD Live. This new format was rolled out with Disney's Hannah Montana The Movie Blu-ray in August of 2009.

Prior to founding Thats It, Randy was Vice President of Online Marketing for Universal Studios Home Entertainment for 7 years. During that period, Randy helped shape the online marketing of some of the first online marketing campaigns such as Erin Brockovich, Jurassic Park 3, Meet the Parents and DVD promotion anniversary editions of Blues Brothers, Animal House, Back to the Future and Schindler's List. Randy received acclaim from Steven Spielberg on his work on www.schindlerslist.com. Randy led the online marketing in international markets and the product and experience team and all of the digital product marketing efforts across multiple partner retail channels.

Prior to Universal Studios, Randy was the EVP GM of Ktel Records and was responsible for the online launch of Ktel Records in 1997, being one of the first record labels to have their entire music catalog online.

Randy began his Internet career in 1995 with Castnet.com which was the first online casting company to use the Internet for casting movies and TV shows.
