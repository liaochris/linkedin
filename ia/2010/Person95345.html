Jennifer Smith’s Summary

Jennifer Smith is the owner of Driftwood PR and a seasoned event producer and marketer with over 10 years experience working in the entertainment industry.

Driftwood PR, specializes in event production, publicity and entertainment marketing.

Jennifer started at Paramount Pictures, where she coordinated press interviews, junkets, and screenings.   After Paramount, she worked at PMK-HBH Public Relations, with celebrity clients. At Focus Features, she worked on the Award campaigns and planned the Academy Award® and Golden Globe® parties.

While working at JDate.com the leading Jewish singles website as National Event Manager, Jennifer produced over 50 Jewish singles events in major cities (New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Boston, etc) and launched JDate.com’s Travel Program, taking singles on trips to the Bahamas, Vermont, and Maui.

At Variety Magazine as Director of Event Marketing, she oversaw event marketing and publicity activities at major film festivals (Sundance, Cannes, Toronto) producing over 100 large and small-scale events, domestically and internationally. She started the Variety Screening Series, a bi-coastal screening series and Variety’s Cannes film festival operation, which included setting up editorial and office space during the festival, planning events, overseeing branding and sponsorship integration for the publication during festival.

After Variety, she gained sales experience at Medialink Worldwide and then oversaw PR for Bold New World, an interactive agency.

In 2008, she worked in New York City for IFP as Producer of the Gotham Independent Film Awards.

Having planned events all over the world with world-class chefs (Wolfgang Puck, Jonathan Waxman, Jamie Oliver and Charlie Trotter), Jennifer, a self described foodie with a passion for food and cooking, attended The New School of Cooking and also tried out for "The Next Food Network Star".
