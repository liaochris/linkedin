Gene Chamson’s Summary

I'm an idea guy. Whether it's a new business or product idea, or coming up with creative terms to clinch a deal with a strategic partner, nothing excites me more than the spark of a great idea.

As a seasoned marketing professional, I have been able to apply my creative imagination and strategic thinking to produce a 20 year track record of accomplishment, with proven skills in business development, entrepreneurship, new product development, product management and sales.

I've started businesses and worked in start-ups and Fortune 100 companies. Whatever the assignment, my pathway to success has been to see possibilities and opportunities where others just saw more of the same.

I’m currently seeking business opportunities or a career position at a start-up or growing company where I can contribute my proven skills as part of a dynamic team, working toward meaningful objectives. My goal is to find a situation where a good idea can go a long way.
