  • Artist

    Ricardo McRae.com

    (Public Company; Myself Only; Fine Art industry)

    July 1969 — Present (41 years 3 months)

    Ricardo is a Toronto based Artist His artwork is abstract, expressive and raw. The content of his work reflects the perspective of people of colour within a western mosaic, and western society's perceptions and assumptions about them. He weaves text into his paintings and drawings using colours that range from earthy ochre's and raw sienna, to brilliant cadmium yellow and blues.
    His accomplishments include solo and group exhibits in Toronto, Detroit, Windsor and Oshawa. Articles featuring his work have been published in Now Magazine, The Windsor Star and Oshawa Times. Ricardo's is also a sculptor, his work "Of the same Rock" was chosen for the inside covor of the book "Art Symbolism and Multiculturalism". He has received many awards, medals, and certificates of recognition for his work. Ricardo's art work is held in several private collections, most notably the University of Windsor has 16 pieces of his work on display as part of their permanent collection. See www.ricardomcrae.com
