Emily McFarlan’s Summary

Perhaps you've heard: Print is dead.

I don't believe that, actually.

Print is evolving.

The demand for news is higher than it's ever been. It's just the way people are getting that news that's changed. And I've got a few ideas about that. I'm experienced across a variety of media platforms (Web, radio, cable television news, network television news, magazine and -- of course -- newspaper), passionate about journalism and optimistic about its future.

So, as we say at the Sun-Times, "Let's get into it."

What's a blog? What's a "tweet?" What's going to happen to newspapers after the Internet? I'm fluent in "Web speak," and I'd love to talk to you. Please don't hesitate to contact me at emilymcfarlan@gmail.com.
