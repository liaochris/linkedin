Mike Johansson’s Summary

As a lifelong learner I have found great satisfaction in helping others learn new skills. This has led me down a career path that now finds me at the perfect nexus of knowledge and interest.

As a Visiting Professor in higher education I get to both teach and learn from students about the intersection of old media skills such as public relations, advertising and news writing and the new media skills needed today in the increasingly social world.

As a Social Media Strategist with Brand Cool Marketing I can help brands choose, prepare for and get up and running in Social Media in a way that engages customers, builds trust and helps the bottom line.

As a Social Media Consultant for my own company Fixitology I help businesses understand the benefits of the social web, guide them through a plan for engagement and then connect them with service providers to establish meaningful presences. I can also coach and train existing staffers on best practices and continuing engagement.

As a founder of the Social Media Speakers Bureau I organize and help run successful symposia and conferences around Social Media for Business.

In everything I do I bring creative (and some say, insanely different) thinking to the table and I aim to make the whole process from learning to implementation to practice a great experience.

If you would like access to my Connections I am happy to open them up to people I know very well (I generally keep them closed so that I don't become a conduit for unethical LinkedIn users).

Feel free to contact me at mikej@fixitology.com or (585) 746-0965.

You can also follow me on Twitter - @mikefixs - or find me on Facebook - www.facebook.com/mikejny/. Or you can read some of my thoughts on my blog: http://forfreeblog.blogspot.com/
