John Digles’s Summary

JOHN DIGLES is a category-creating marketing and communications officer with nearly two-decades of corporate executive management experience, agency leadership and board service.

As XanGo’s Chief Marketing Officer, Mr. Digles played a key role in transforming a young company into a recognized global brand with a million distributors in 30 markets and a billion in sales in 5 years. John negotiated XanGo’s historic jersey-front sponsorship with Major League Soccer and Real Salt Lake -- the first deal of its kind in North American pro sports. Creator of XanGo.TV, he led introduction of the industry's first brand-centric video sharing and social networking site, sparking new digital and social media platforms. His innovations earned John an invitation to address the WFDSA World Congress XIII.

Leading brands, government agencies, trade associations and new growth ventures relied on John's counsel during his service as SVP and Midwest GM with MWW Group, one of the industry’s top PR and marketing firms. Mr. Digles gained national recognition for creating the "BlackBerry Thumb" campaign and won industry awards for his programs in consumer marketing, health care, issues management, public affairs and energy.

Architect of the premier agency offering for nutraceuticals and functional foods, John earned a reputation for reinvigorating brands, successfully launching new product categories and bridging sales gaps. His creation of ScienceDialogue established a strategic methodology to promote scientific research through marketing.

Founder of Traveller Jones Productions, Mr. Digles is an award-winning filmmaker whose work has gained critical notice, including the Sundance Film Festival. Elected to the board of the Illinois Production Alliance, John helped gain legislative approval of Illinois’ first film production tax incentive which generated a record-setting increase in revenues and new jobs.

John Digles’s Specialties:

Category-creating initiatives, marketing, corporate communications, strategy development, P&L management, digital innovation, crisis planning and management, strategic counsel, brand and product launches, entertainment integration, executive leadership
