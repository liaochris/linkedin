Andy MacIntyre’s Summary

For 8 years, I worked primarily in the music entertainment industry for concert promoters and Los Angeles based artist managers and talent agencies. In 1999, I came to my senses and moved back to the midwest and began working for GMR Marketing. In my current role as Director of Entertainment Marketing, I still work in the music business AND get to use my Marketing and Advertising degree (finally!) in developing and executing entertainment and sponsorship programs for Fortune 100 clients.

Andy MacIntyre’s Specialties:

Sponsorship evaluation, business development, contracting, negotiation, market research, public relations, advertising, brand strategy, event planning, ROI, artist relations, brand partnerships, on-site execution, Sarbanes/Oxley, creative approvals, etc.
