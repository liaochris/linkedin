Thomas Bartel’s Summary

Results oriented with strong interpersonal, communication, problem solving and relationship building skills. Have over thirty-five years experience as a Program Manager and Director of Regulatory Compliance in the Environmental Health and Safety fields at Honeywell, Data General, United Technologies, Storage Technology Corporation and Unisys Corporation. I have established and implemented both Environmental and Quality Management Systems Programs, including training, auditing as well as inplementation of ISO 14000 Certification programs on a worldwide basis. Also held positions as Account Executive, marketing semiconductor test equipment solutions to Motorola. I have in recent years held Customer Service and Sales positions with a number of Fortune 100 companies in the Pharmaceutical, ESH Consulting and Information Technology sectors, including positions as an ASR for Hewlett Packard and Account Executive at Insight Enterprises.
