Cameron Chell’s Summary

Most of my experience is based around working with and or leading within teams to develop and finance new projects, technologies, companies or industries. The most notable of these would be the founding of the Application Service Provider industry, www.aspindustry.org, as well as one of the world's first Application Service Providers, FutureLink, (google: Futurelink, asp). The industry is now often referred to as Software as a Service, (SaaS). This industry is also the underpinning of Cloud Computing a cornerstone of the internet tech space.

I spend 35% of my time working with a very talented professional team building Podium Participation Funds www.podiumfunds.com.

I spend 35% of my time working directly with Business Instincts Group partners and their projects to determine what is most important and specifically how to achieve it.

I spend 15% of my time with the executives of the Tarco Engineering group of companies, www.tarco.com. More specifically I help enable the organization meet the demands of it opportunities and growth. This specifically includes Strategic Planning, Tactical Measurement, Executive Development, Corporate Structure and Culture.

I spend 15% of my time reading, researching and/or adding to my knowledge and/or tool set as it relates directly to Business Instincts Group clients and projects.
