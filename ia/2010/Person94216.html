Jehangir Mehta’s Summary

I am the executive chef and owner of New York City restaurant Graffiti Food and Wine Bar, and author of the cookbook "Mantra: The Rules of Indulgence."

I also offer a cooking class for children 4-14 at my restaurant called "Candy Camp" designed to an build interest in preparing and enjoying more diverse foods, and host exclusive in-home dinner parties through my event planning company.

Most recently, I competed against Morimoto on “Iron Chef America” on Food Network in August 2009, and will be a contestant on the “Next Iron Chef” beginning in October 2009.
