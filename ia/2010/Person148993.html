  • Member of the Executive Committee

    Americans for a Safe Israel / AFSI, Philadelphia Chapter

    (Think Tanks industry)

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Please see www.phillyafsi.com.

    Americans For a Safe Israel (AFSI) was founded in 1971 by concerned Americans convinced a peaceful settlement of the Arab-Israel conflict would only occur when the Arabs realized once and for all that Israel could not be defeated militarily or in any other way. The founders were convinced that Israel must retain possession and control of Judea, Samaria, Gaza and the Golan. To abandon territory won in wars forced on it would make it vulnerable once again to renewed subversion and attack, not only by hostile Arabs in Judea and Samaria but unstable Arab regimes.

    Most importantly, the pioneering efforts of the residents in these areas have the potential of rejuvenating a dormant Zionism and igniting a second Zionist revolution.

    The Philadelphia area chapter of Americans for a Safe Israel was founded in 1986.
