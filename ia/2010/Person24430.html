Bob Foster’s Summary

I have read many highly touted business and management books over the years, and found most of them to be, basically…useless. Academics, wannabes, single-industry experts, and pundits alike all have something to say about how we should be running our businesses—without ever having run a business like ours.

In light of all this information being bestowed upon us, we need to consider a couple of facts:

1. There will be over six (6) million new full-time businesses started this year. (Kauffman Index of Entrepreneurial Activity.)

2. There will be over five (5) million businesses disappear from existence this year. (Working Research Paper by Bob Foster.)

Considering all the hype, as well as the above facts, it is my intention to try and cut through all the misinformation being promoted in the business world and bring a straightforward and honest approach to business, that is based on actual experience of real-life business situations. I want to use my decades of broad experience to eliminate the common myths, and help small business owners with their "real" problems.

To start this process, you can check out phase 1 of my new web site: http://www.business-solutions-and-resources.com .

In the event your business is currently in serious trouble, you may want to read my book: "Be Your Own Turnaround Manager: A Common Sense Guide to Managing a Business Crisis." It is available at Amazon.com, and barnesandnoble.com.

For current events affecting small business, you can visit my blog at http://www.bizmaverickblog.com .
