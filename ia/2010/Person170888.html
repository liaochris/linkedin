Morten Reitoft’s Summary

Through my career I have always been interested in creating results - though people, with people and based on high ethics and a great knowledge about the products and services I have represented.

In the past years I have had an increasing interest in Mega Trends - trends that are generic to many industries and which is a tremendous management issue for future leaders.

In general - and probably for most markets - the trends can be summarized to:

- Volume batches decreases
- The turnover for each transaction decreases
- Value for each transaction increases
- Time-to-market decreases
- Quality requirements become an increasing issue

and many more issues.

All are very important issues to deal with, since it dramatically changes our mass-production culture and will put the individual in center of all developments etc.

I have a great interest in these issues, but I find that many old-school leaders, don't pay attention to the changes and therefor will become obsolete and not able to sustain and/or grow their business.
