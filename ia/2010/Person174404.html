Robert A. Green, CPA’s Summary

I've been a founder and CEO of several businesses, the main and longest-lasting one being Green & Company CPAs, which consists of three distinct divisions: GreenTraderTax.com (for traders), GreenTraderFunds.com (for investment managers) and GreenEnergyActiveInvestors.com (for green energy tax-advantaged businesses).

My goal is to generate interest in our new business ideas and non-profit missions on LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter.

You can become a part of these efforts by joining the GreenTraderTax Traders Association for online and self-employed small business traders. We continue to fight off the dreaded “financial-transaction tax,” which, if enacted, could put traders out of business. We defend and improve our unique tax breaks, such as trader tax status, Section 475 MTM tax-loss insurance, lower 60/40 tax rates on futures and forex, increases in the capital loss limitation, and more. We assist in obtaining health insurance, mortgages, business loans and more. Every new member gives us greater power!

Using our “Green Active Investors” tax breaks, Green & Company focuses on tax-advantaged private-equity investments in small businesses and new alternative energy markets. It’s a home run solution in this difficult economy. The idea is to raise money from friends and family (the active investors) with limited paperwork and very few strings attached. You’ll compensate the investors in the early years primarily with tax losses, rather than cash or interest income. Plus, your active investors may be able to provide your business with special assistance, knowledge, work, contacts and influence so you can be more successful sooner rather than later.
