Steven May’s Summary

With over 25 years of experience, May is a seasoned veteran of the retail loss prevention industry. Prior to founding LP Innovations in 1998, May was the First Senior Vice President, Director of Loss Prevention and Auditing with J. Baker Inc., a national retailer with over 2,000 stores and over 1 billion in annual sales. During his tenure, May oversaw all corporate facilities, distribution centers and retail stores.

Steven May’s Specialties:

May is a Certified Forensic Investigator (CFI) in Advanced Interview and Interrogation Techniques from WZ Associates. May is an Advisory Board member for CFI and the RILA Loss Prevention Steering Committee. He is a Member of both the National Retail Federation (NRF) and the Retail Industry Leaders Association (RILA). May completed certification programs for Paralegal studies and Computer Crime Forensics through Northeastern’s University School of Professional and Continuing Studies (SPCS).
