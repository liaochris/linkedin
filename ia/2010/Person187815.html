Sharon Killion, MLA’s Summary

Sharon is an American living on the Indian Ocean in South Africa and loves it. She is a multi award winning Smart Marketing & Soft Selling Mentor and Certified Money, Marketing and Soul Coach. Sharon is passionate about helping High-Achieving Entrepreneurs Worldwide learn Smart Marketing & Soft Selling so they experience clients, cash and abundance.

Sharon loves mentoring high-achieving entrepreneurs worldwide grow their businesses in an authentic and rich way that allows them to be who they are, do what they love and prosper. She has over 22+ years of extremely successful marketing, sales and entrepreneurial experience in two continents--the USA and South Africa.

Sharon is known for selling over 50 condos (flats) in less than 10 months in South Africa after only living there for 9 months.

Sharon mentors clients through her popular Brilliant Success Gold, Gold Plus and Platinum Coaching Programs. For Sharon it's an honor to mentor passionate, high-achieving entrepreneurs on the Smart Marketing & Soft Selling tools and business success mindset they need to quickly and easily gain more clients, make more money and experience abundance.
