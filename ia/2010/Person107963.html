Juli Bridgers-Schatz’s Summary

Founder of Author! Author! Chicagoland Book & Author Marketing Network.

More than 20 years experience as a copywriter, PR specialist, marketer and market researcher, helping businesses take full advantage of the myriad opportunities to promote their products and services through PR.

When every marketing dollar MUST count and bring results, publicity and public relations delivers the greatest ROI.

Beyond PR, marketing messages delivered via other media and format -- from your printed business cards and brochures to website content -- must also speak volumes to ensure your continued business growth and greater success.

Especially in today's challenging economy, you MUST find new and effective means to keep your current customers engaged and informed, and to continue to attract new customers.

Juli Bridgers-Schatz’s Specialties:

Author! Author! Chiucagoland Book & Author Marketing Network, bringing together authors (published and soon-to-be-published) to share ideas and experiences about promoting authors and their books/work.

"Do It Yourself Marketing" for small businesses, a 1/2-day workshop for helping small businesses take advantage of easy-to-use, no- and low-cost marketing and publicity opportunities. To date, more than 200 businesses have participated in the workshop and reported successful results
