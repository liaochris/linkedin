Starsky J. Robinson’s Summary

Starsky J. Robinson
New Media Marketing Consultant

Starsky Robinson is a seasoned interactive new media marketing professional with a concentration in event production. Using a multi-platform approach, he is experienced in integrating live activities and events with new media channels from gaming, to web, and mobile campaigns. As the Marketing Manager for JuiceCaster.com powered by Juice Wireless Inc., Starsky Robinson devoted his unique skills in new media, business relations and events to enhance JuiceCaster.com branding and consumer awareness. He successfully created and leveraged digital word of mouth and cross-channel marketing, organically growing strong user base acquisition through digital buzz and live events. His efforts cut overhead marketing expenses by 40%.

Starsky believes that the world is evolving beyond traditional marketing tactics to interactive new media marketing. The evolution is heavily reliant on digital word of mouth (D.W.O.M.) which, in turn, triggers an invaluable viral buzz. Through his experience with JuiceCaster, Nike Brand Jordan and independent consults he is well-versed in creating and harnessing this buzz.

Currently a freelance consultant, Starsky Robinson focuses on enhancing companies online and new media brand awareness and has worked with Feed Co, JuiceCaster.com, Metaphor Entertainment, Onpoint for College Organization, Magnate Music, and Baltimore Maryland artist Duece Tre Duece.

Born in Syracuse, New York, Starsky J. Robinson is a graduate of University at Albany, State University of New York with a Bachelor's Degree in Communication and Marketing.
