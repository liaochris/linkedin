Mitchell Feldman’s Summary

Mitchell Feldman has been publicizing, promoting and marketing jazz, classical and world music since 1979. MFA – Mitchell Feldman Associates is an independent arts marketing communications and media relations consultancy founded in 1996. MFA provides strategic planning, publicity and radio promotion services to jazz artists and ensembles, record labels, organizations and institutions, festivals and other special events on a retainer or project basis. In addition to coordinating all aspects of publicity and promotion for events or organizations in local markets, he conducts national and international media campaigns for tours, recordings and other creative projects and provides a variety of editorial and creative media services including the conception and production of program notes and biographies, press kits, brochures, fliers and other promotional material as well as the production and placement of print and radio advertisements.

Mitchell Feldman’s Specialties:

30 years professional experience in music industry & international jazz community. Have cultivated contacts in North America and Europe. Counsels and raised profiles of emerging/regional artists and independent labels. MFA provides access to the editors, staff writers and freelancers that cover jazz in the US and the program or music directors and the DJs or on-air hosts that broadcast it. Some of Feldman's media, record label, producer, artist and management relationships date back decades.
