Dave Chaffey’s Summary

Dave Chaffey is a specialist in using Digital Marketing to improve company performance. My main roles are as a bestselling author, consultant, speaker and commentator.

Dave has written four best-selling books on E-marketing including ‘Internet Marketing: Strategy, Implementation and Practice’, ‘E-marketing Excellence’ and ‘Total E-mail Marketing’. He has delivered over 100 Internet marketing training workshops for the CIM. Recent in-company training and consulting assignments include 3M, HSBC, Siebel Systems and Tektronix.

Dave has been recognised by the CIM as one of 50 marketing ‘gurus’ worldwide who have shaped the future of Marketing.

He is also proud to have been recognized by the Department of Trade and Industry as one of the leading individuals who have provided input and influence on the development and growth of E-commerce and the Internet in the UK over the last 10 years.

He is the managing director of Marketing Insights Limited and also lectures in e-business at leading business schools such as the Universities of Cranfield, Leeds and Warwick.

Find out more at http://www.davechaffey.com/blog.

Internet marketing training, E-marketing courses, Digital marketing consulting. Specialist in: Internet marketing planning & Strategy, Web analytics, Search engine marketing, E-mail marketing and persuasion marketing.
