Cari Kraft (ckraft@jacobsmgt.com)’s Summary

Jacobs Management Group, Inc. offers you 20 years of hiring success in the Medical, Pharmaceutical, Biotech, and Technologies fields. We have a team of recruiters, candidate developers and researchers who specialize in meeting our client's specific needs with a supporting database of over 250,000 of the top professionals in their fields. We place C-Level, Vice Presidents, National Sales Managers, Regional and District Managers, National Accounts, Marketing Directors and Managers, Product Managers, Sales Associates, and Managers in areas such as Regulatory, Reimbursement, Engineering, and Quality.

Cari Kraft is the President of Jacobs Management Group, Inc. Ms. Kraft has a rare combination of experience in healthcare recruiting and the IT industry. Ms. Kraft has held positions in both Sales and Marketing as a Senior Sales Executive, Director of Business Development and Director of Marketing over a 20 year period.

Prior to her association with JMG Inc., Ms. Kraft has held positions Omicron, Packard Press and the Strategic Management Group where she taught business leaders Strategy, Sales, Marketing and Finance based on the Wharton Business Simulation Game.

Ms. Kraft holds a Bachelor of Science degree with a concentration in Decision Science from the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School with a concurrent Bachelor of Arts Degree in Economics.

Ms. Kraft sits on several boards including the Society for Information Management Professionals (SIM), The LaSalle Computer Science Advisory Board, and the Abington Arts Center. She has been recognized as an expert in the Sales and Marketing and IT fields and has been a guest lecturer on Sales and Marketing for Penn State University, as well as spoken on technology and its use in the Marketing as Sales arena at Client/Server World, NWCT, SIM and for numerous corporations. Ms. Kraft is also credited with the article “Executive ERP – the Real Deal for Mid-Sized Enterprise”.
