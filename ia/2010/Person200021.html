Binod Taterway’s Summary

Binod Taterway is a recognized innovator in the business of technology. He is a serial entrepreneur who has gone through the complete lifecycle of starting a company and then successfully exiting it. At Taterway Group, he owns a portfolio of companies that are focused on growing traditional and online businesses.

Prior to starting Taterway Group, Binod co-founded Blue Canopy, an IT startup, in 2002 with three other partners and was CEO of the company from 2004-2006. He grew the company to $12MM in five years – the last two of those years, his company was awarded Inc. 500 honor for being the fast growing company in the USA. In 2006, he successfully sold the company to a private investor. Before Blue Canopy, he was Vice President at Sapient and Managing Director at Proxicom where he ran their global Automotive practices. Binod began his career at Xerox and helped grow many startup Internet consulting firms during the dot-com days. He possesses 20 years of consulting and entrepreneurial experience in the field of information technology management.

Throughout his career, Binod has been a consultant to large and small organizations alike. He has designed and delivered solutions that have guided such organizations as Comcast, Marriott, US Department of Commerce, America Online, Hyatt, General Motors, Toyota, Nike, The Gap, Gartner, and Duke Energy to increased performance and effectiveness, while improving revenue and business value related to their IT enterprises.

Binod is also a professional speaker on industry and technology topics alike. He was a panelist at Travelcom and has spoken twice at ESTO, a Travel industry event. He was a speaker at Gartner conference in 2004 and has been recognized by the media as an expert on IT outsourcing. He has also spoken over a dozen times at IT infrastructure and technology conferences over the past decade.
