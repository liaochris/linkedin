Giulio Coraggio’s Summary

Giulio is a Senior Associate in the Technology Media and Telecommunications and Commercial department of Lovells, one of the largest international law firms, with over three thousand people operating from 28 offices in Europe, Asia, the Middle East and the United States.

Giulio was awarded with a law degree at University Federico II of Naples. Before joining Lovells, he worked in London, focusing on privacy and data protection issues, where he received an LL.M. in Intellectual Property, Information Technology, Internet and Telecommunications law at the Queen Mary College, University of London.

Giulio has a strong expertise in advising clients on gaming and betting law issues (both online and offline) as well as on information technology and e-commerce matters. Also he advises companies on telecommunications and data protection issues under Italian and European law.

Likewise, he has reached a considerable experience in drafting and negotiating international commercial contracts, including sponsorship agreements and product and service supply and distribution agreements, also dealing with competition issues arising out of vertical agreements.

Giulio is frequently a speaker at conferences on gaming and betting law topics and is the editor of the blog www.gamingtechlaw.com on gaming and technology law.
