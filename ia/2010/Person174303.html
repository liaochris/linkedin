Robert E. "Robby" Dunn III, CAM, CRM, CIC’s Summary

Principal in 247 residential units in the Houston area. I buy, rehab, and sell residential real estate for a profit. These projects are either to buy and hold as rentals or to sell as flips.
I am proud to pay private investors above average returns for short and mid-term loans to finance my projects. Each loan is secured by a formal mortgage filed with the county clerk and closed through a title company.
To learn about Private Lending go to www.HoustonDirectedCapital.com. Be sure to ask for the free Special Report.
