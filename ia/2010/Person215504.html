William Grand’s Summary

Distinguished by his eye for design and attention to detail, he has successfully completed projects for various reputable companies and organizations. William excels most at programming front-end applications. His work includes visual effects, university campus maps, xml-driven sites, Section 508 compliance, and he is multiple-award winner. His ability to communicate with people equals his development skills. He has excelled in writing user's guides, presenting to clients, and has demonstrated a competent ability to interact with clients. Lastly, he has a sense of humor and is a pleasure to work with.
