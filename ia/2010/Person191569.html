Sol Fischler’s Summary

I’m proud to say I started out editing on 35mm film, and I’ve got the Moviola scars to prove it.

At Dennis Hayes Editorial in 1988, we were the New York beta-test shop for Avid Technology, and I began working exclusively on AVID.
In 2009, I added Final Cut Pro to my repertoire.

But enough about technology.

I’ve been lucky enough to cut my teeth with the most talented editors of New York’s advertising heyday – Dennis Hayes, Steve Schreiber, Jay Gold, and Harvey Eckert. That background served me well, and helped me win awards for both short-form promos and longer-format programming.
