Mariah Andrews’s Summary

My ultimate goal is to have fun while I work. I love to collaborate and play very nicely with others, particularly with those on multi-disciplinary teams. In terms of my skill set, I offer five professionals in one: 1) I'm an award-winning copywriter with a wide breadth of experience in a range of media. 2) I have 10 years of experience in user experience design and information architecture. 3) I'm a strategist at heart and have lead multiple accounts on strategy/research/plannning for integrated marketing. 4) I have training and experience in search engine optimization. 5) I'm a social media fiend (Twitter, Facebook, blogging, etc.) and am working with the PR director at my current firm to develop social media marketing campaigns for our clients.

In the end, I want to create fun, engaging, interactive experiences for my clients' customers (mainly advertisers) that produce results ... in sales, traffic, engagement, brand loyalty, etc. Beyond all of that, I'm an optimist and an idealist who wants to save the world by collaborating on some great web app, literature, film, poetry or some other such creative endeavor ... but all of that is for another website and another discussion entirely.
