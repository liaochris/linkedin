Debi Silber, MS, RD, WHC "The Mojo Coach"’s Summary

Debi Silber is a Registered Dietitian with a Master’s degree in Nutrition, a Personal Trainer, Whole Health Coach, Lifestyle Expert, speaker, President of Lifestyle Fitness, Inc. and author of The Lifestyle Fitness Program: A Six Part Plan So Every Mom Can Look, Feel and Live Her Best (recommended by Parenting Magazine). Debi’s branded “The Mojo Coach” because after working with unfit, overweight and overwhelmed women for nearly 20 years, she's discovered the six things every woman needs to look, feel, live her best and “get her mojo back”.

Debi’s a featured expert on numerous websites, providing information, articles and insight to thousands of struggling moms. She’s a popular radio guest, is quoted regularly in print and online magazines, was recently featured as a successful “career-at-home-mom” in three new books and serves on the Advisory Board for the National Institute of Whole Health and the National Association of Home Based Business Moms.

Debi also holds two certifications in pre/post natal fitness, with specialty recognition in weight loss and weight maintenance. She’s the New York Ambassador for W.O.M.E.N. e.o (Women Own Mentor Empower Nurture Each Other. Debi’s been honored for her unique charity program (2008), is a Madison Who’s Who (2007), Notable American Woman (1992, 2000) and Outstanding Young Woman of America (1992, 1998).

Debi understands that women need a program which supports their goals, values their time, meets their specific challenges...and creates lasting results! She’s a role model any woman can appreciate and relate to as she tends to her business, home, husband, four children, four dogs and her own self care!

In a warm, fun and “info-taining” way, Debi can encourage anyone to find the balance between motherhood and womanhood…because it doesn’t have to be a choice.

Debi Silber, MS, RD, WHC "The Mojo Coach"’s Specialties:

Weight loss, fitness, stress control, emotional health, relationship wellness, lifestyle change, weight maintenance support and coaching for women.
