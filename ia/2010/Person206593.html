Tom Woodhead’s Summary

I specialize in:

- B2B Internet and e-Mail Marketing
- Organic Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
- Copywriting -- Web copy, white papers, case studies
- Actionable Web analytics implementation and analysis

In 2002, after a lengthy career as a technical writer, I turned my attention to B2B Internet Marketing. I now help companies achieve better visibility through SEO, better lead generation through Internet and e-mail marketing, and better prospect and customer education through collateral such as white papers and case studies.

The Internet is first and foremost an information medium. People use it to research products and services so that they can make informed buying decisions. If you don't give them what they want, they'll move on quickly.

To use the Internet effectively as a sales & marketing medium, companies must become data-driven, using actionable Web analytics to build a culture of continuous improvement.
