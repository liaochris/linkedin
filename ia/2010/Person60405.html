I am seeking a significant leadership position, contract, or consulting opportunity with an organization that wants to increase employee engagement to drive improvements in:

And Ultimately…Revenue!

Twenty-five years of management experience in manufacturing, fleet/logistics/distribution, and Lean consulting, delivering turnaround performance and changing cultures, has molded me into a strategic, influential leader.

My ability to function effectively at the executive level while interacting, influencing, and gaining respect at the floor level has allowed me to develop strong relationships and loyalty among my employees...yielding significant results.
