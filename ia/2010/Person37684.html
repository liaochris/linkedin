Chris Eddie’s Experience

  • Owner, Publisher

    Smiley Pete Publishing

    (Public Company; Publishing industry)

    October 1997 — Present (12 years 7 months)

    With over a decade of experience in magazine publishing, Lexington-based Smiley Pete Publishing specializes in content that is locally focused and locally generated to serve reader needs and interests for its growing family of publications.

    Launched in October of 1997, Smiley Pete’s Chevy Chaser Magazine is the longest-running neighborhood-based publication currently distributed in Lexington, reaching an estimated 15,000 households in select areas.

    Chevy Chaser’s sister publication, Southsider, was started four years later to serve an additional 15,000 homes in growing communities in southern Fayette County.

    Business Lexington, a news journal focused on the Central Kentucky business community, is distributed every other week to 18,000 business leaders in a seven-county region in the Bluegrass.
