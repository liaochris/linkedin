Successfully engaged senior business and technology leaders in strategic initiative planning to prioritize the high impact technology investments. Recognized as an expert in formulating and managing to key performance indicators. Proven track record of instilling confidence at all levels of an organization through excellent communication, relationships, planning and execution.
