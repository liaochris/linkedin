Alicia Moura’s Summary

Since graduating with my BA in Journalism and Marketing, I have been all over the place writing, advertising and marketing for both US and international companies from Mexico, to the Netherlands, Spain, Italy, Iceland, Venezuela and Turkey.

Social media marketing, public relations and search engine optimization really represent industries that will always be fresh and in constant change, an industry that allows me to integrate my deep-seated love for the written word and community, as well as integrated marketing strategy and a fascination with new people and places.

It is my goal to help the amazing companies that I work with maintain and grow their positions as integrated online marketing firms as well as into brands that are constantly able to push the envelope of discovery and find new ways to please our clients and help their businesses grow.

And marketing in all its guises is not the end. We are, at heart, humanitarian addicts. It is our current and continuing intention to use our skills, services and profits to help fund various non-profit projects worldwide.
