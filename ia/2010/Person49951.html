David Sapiro’s Summary

Hi,

Open Source is more than just technology for me. Not being an "Ayatollah", I like to work in fine, efficient and ethical environments. FLOSS and Open Source are the values that bring me satisfaction in my work and business.

Pilot Systems is a company I founded, specialized in OSS in general, with a main focus on Open Source solutions integration, especially the ones based on Python for the technology side and CMS / DMS on the solution side.

We're one of the main specialists of Zope and Plone in Europe (no kidding ;) but the market is real small, don't worry, not another world leader claim here).

I have many non-profit activities, especially in the PLOSS cluster in Paris and the CNLL professional organization.

As an evangelist, at least trying to act as one, I lead the Open Source workgroup in the Guide Share France organization, where I seat at the board. This is the place wheere key IBM accounts meet, the criteria being : do I use mainly IBM for HW, SW or Service.

Beside these activities, Pilot Systems is launching a new offer for an emailing SaaS solution called Cockpit Mailing. More in French here:
http://cockpit-mailing.com/

And the 2010 focus will be Immersive Worlds. Especially using the Vast Park Open Source technology. More to come on the VastPark / Pilot Systems collaboration soon.

All these activities are related to our strong infrastructure background. We host 20K+ free Plone web sites, our managed hosting facility is 100% virtualized using Xen technology and we aim at deploying private clouds on our infrastructure and at customers.
