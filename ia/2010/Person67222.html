Ernesto Casas’s Summary

Ernesto Casas, a seasoned senior executive with more than 30 years experience in the IT and Telecom industry, with a graduate degree in Computer Science (Dean Honor Roster – Cum Laude) from Universidad Simon Bolivar – Venezuela, specialized in Computer Design, Information technologies and Operations Research, and extensive courses in business and finance from IESA (Instituto de Estudios Superiores de Administracion), associated professor in several IT related subjects in Universidad Humbold and Politecnico de Las Fuerzas Armadas de Venezuela.

Ernesto has occupied very important position in the last 20 years, including Business Development Manager for Voice products at 3Com Corporation Latin America, Marketing & Product Development Director at CANTV (Verizon Subsidiary), Executive VP and CTO for SuperCable International, CTO at Click Interconnect Inc., Marketing Director and Service Creation Manager at Vox 2 Vox Communications (A group Cisneros company) among others.

Currently holds the position of Regional Director for Latin America and the Caribbean for Patton Electronics Company, the manufacturer of the leading VoIP product line SmartNode, besides more than 500 other telecommunication products.

He has been involved in several consulting projects in many areas directly related with technology, IT and Telecom, including Engineering and construction fo the first Fiber Optic Computer Loop network at the second largest Oil Refinery in Latin America, Due diligence and assessment for Centenial/Infochannel to recruit and buy cable operations in Jamaica; develop a marketing strategy to introduce Disposable Phones in China; traffic simulation model to diminish car traffic in Caracas during the underground Metro construction.

During these years he has developed a unique combination of in depth technology knowledge, strong business sense and understanding on financial impact of technology application.
