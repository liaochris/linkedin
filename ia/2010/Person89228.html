Janet Ann Smith’s Summary

Janet Smith has provided corporate communications and marketing services since 1985, and offers for-profit, not-for-profit and government organizations a priceless storehouse of marketing insight, ideas, knowledge, and skills. Whether serving as a strategist, project manager, content developer, creative director, fundraiser or grant-writer, Janet is committed to strengthening your organization’s bottom line.

Janet has a strong performance record across many industries, including high tech/information technology, multimedia, medicine, science, pharmaceuticals, education, financial services, travel services, and more. She co-authored Customer Bonding: Pathways to Customer Loyalty (NTC Business Books, 1994), and has written many articles on marketing technologies, strategies, and techniques. She also served as editor for two Consumer Reports publications, the Consumer Reports Travel Guide of 1992 and 1993. She earned her MBA in marketing from Babson College in 1980, and a BA with Honors in language studies from Brandeis University.

Janet Ann Smith’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy and execution, Web-based marketing, branding, PR, advertising, Information Technology integration into business processes, change management, custom publishing, content creation and deployment.
