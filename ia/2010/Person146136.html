Michael Walker’s Summary

Working with sellers to formulate and execute the proper price, marketing plan, and negotiation techniques to result in the fastest sale at the most monetary return possible.

I use the most advanced tools to ensure that every property that I represent is presented to the most buyers. You will always be updated on your properties status and know what is going on with your sale. I communicate at least once a week with you throughout the process.

Buyers: you have similar benefits as well. You'll be connected with a local lender to get pre-approved, I will set you up on a search of the entire MLS and only put properties that match YOUR specific needs. You will have your own website available to view, edit, track, and share the listings with others. It's specific to your needs and like an online manila folder holding every property you've seen online and/or off line. When a new list comes on the market ... you're notified immediately allowing you to be in the know at all times.

If you or anyone you know has any questions ... I'm always available to answer those and of course will take extreme care to ensure any referrals you may provide me are taken care of with the utmost of care!
