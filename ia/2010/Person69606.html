Fernando Martin Santana’s Summary

Open-minded technologist, with a very strong business inclination. I have worked in Europe and Asia for a broad range of companies - from one of the most advanced research and development laboratories in the world, to the biggest corporations in the Finance and Telecom industries.

In my first year of University I founded and presided a start-up, and yet still managed to graduate 1st of my Business Administration class. I later went on to further my education with a MSc in Computer Science, and a Master in Business Intelligence and Knowledge Management.

I was greatly honored to be awarded with the Leonardo da Vinci scholarship from the European Commission, as well as the Vulcanus scholarship from the EU-Japan Centre for Industrial Cooperation.

Following my passion, I became President of the largest Spanish Entrepreneur Organization, and joined several European entrepreneur associations as an advisory member to coach young companies in business and technical areas.

On a personal note, my interests range from being an early adopter in new technologies, to the occasional disc jockeying of electronic music, and sports such as snowboarding, sailing, futsal, Judo and Jiu-Jitsu
