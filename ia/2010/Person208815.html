Tim Cook’s Summary

I take homes that need a lot of work and fix them up well beyond the standards of most Rehab professionals. I want my homes to go from being the worst to the best on the block. In this way, I provide a needed service to the community. I help end buyers get into a beautiful house, at a price below fair market value, so they have instant equity. I also help increase the value of homes nearby, and thus the entire community benefits.

That is why I call myself a Residential Re-Developer.

Please contact me if I can assist you in any way.
