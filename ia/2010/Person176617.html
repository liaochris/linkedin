Roger E. Reyna’s Summary

Bilingual energy industry professional with extensive experience directing the Credit capability and as an individual contributor in the management and control of counterparty credit risk. A proven decision maker and leader in consulting senior management in organization design, staff selection, enterprise-wide credit systems deployment, and risk mitigation. Strengths in "know-your-customer" financial analysis and credit risk assessment, domestic and international trade finance, and credit structuring of term transactions and financings, credit process assessment, re-engineering & improvement. Extensive skills in Excel modeling and in negotiation of energy commodity contracting documentation including 1st Lien inter-creditor derivative structures, review of bank credit agreements and review of long term municipal prepay supply and derivative structured transactions.

Roger E. Reyna’s Specialties:

Assessment and limit model development; Credit facility management; Term deal credit structuring; LC draw presentation; Trading, Cargo, Pipeline movements, and Rack transport load transactions; Natural Gas, LNG, Power, Crude Oil, Refined Products, and Petrochemicals; ISDA contract negotiation; SAP; HotTap
