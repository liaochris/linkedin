PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
In my position as Marketing Manager of Walsh College, I'm able to combine my love of marketing with my enthusiasm for professional development as I help to promote our College's undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs, as well as support our students and alumni. I love my job and feel blessed to have the opportunity to blend my outside interests into my career.

Walsh College is the only all-business college in Metro Detroit. Our mission is to deliver a business education that integrates application and theory to prepare graduates for successful careers. If you're considering a business degree, let me know and I'd be happy to connect you with an Advisor.
