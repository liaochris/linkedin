Carolyn Bligh’s Summary

Branding and marketing communications expert specializing in the design and construction industry. I am closely integrated and submersed into a company's business goals and marketing strategy. My passion is to leverage and develop valuable company assets that build distinction and create opportunities. Specializing in the building industry for over 20 years, I understand and connect with architects, engineers, contractors, subcontractors, facility owners/managers, and real estate developers. As a trusted advisor on branding and image, I have spoken to building industry groups on a local and national level. Speaking topics: branding, web site design, and email marketing. I am the author and designer of an award winning eNewsletter, Brand Crumbs, as well as articles of interest for industry organizations.

If I am in my office, I am having a great day. If I am out of the office at a client or board meeting, networking or seminar event, I am having a great day. I thoroughly enjoy my work and relationships, and a day doesn't pass that I am not grateful for my relationships, and people believing in me.
