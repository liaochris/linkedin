Laura Manz’s Summary

SENIOR EXECUTIVE/ENERGY INDUSTRY: A nationally recognized leader in the energy industry, especially in bulk power, electric transmission, wholesale restructuring and demand response. Demonstrated ability to solve complex issues, create winning teams and implement initiatives. Designed and implemented power exchanges for Mid-Atlantic (PJM) and California wholesale electricity markets. Implemented innovative transmission planning processes and retail choice programs

Transmission Planning and Interconnections: Implemented Order 890-compliant transmission planning for a 50,000 MW power grid and played a key role in the Renewable Energy Transmission Initiative and ensuing collaborative among California transmission owners to develop coordinated planning processes that satisfy reliability, economic, and environmental policy mandates. Executive over complex regulatory contract negotiations and regulatory policy, including generator and transmission interconnection agreements, and regulatory policy.

Wholesale Market Design: Served a key role in start-up of wholesale nodal electricity markets for the Mid-Atlantic Region (PJM) and, ultimately, California international best practices.

Reliability Compliance: Launched formalized reliability compliance programs to align with mandatory standards of Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC), North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), Western Electric Coordinating Council (WECC), and California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC), including infrastructure planning and transmission asset maintenance requirements. Served as industry sector representative to NERC’s Board of Trustees.

Regulatory and Business Strategy: Developed long-term strategies and business objectives of the Company with frequent interaction with the Board of Governors.

Demand Response and Smart Grid: Executive sponsor of Demand Response, Smart Grid, and long-term renewable initiatives.
