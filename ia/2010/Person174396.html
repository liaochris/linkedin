Roberta Czaplewski’s Summary

As a Business Consultant, I work with a team of training and development consultants that provide consulting, program development and coordination, assessment and evaluation services and facilitation services to businesses, industry partners, State and Federal agencies and business and industry associates. Our focus is in helping these client organizations grow through the provision of education and training services.

I’m a certified DACUM facilitator and work with organizations to conduct job/occupational analysis using the DACUM process.

I’m currently working closely with Riverland Community College in several capacities. I’m a member of Riverland’s Supervisory Management Team responsible for educating people about our supervisory training that prepares first time supervisors with the skills they need to succeed in the corporate world. As Program Coordinator for Riverland’s Community Leadership Program, I work to enable candidates to learn about their respective community and facilitate the process of identifying and solving important community issues. I also work in the capacity of Business Consultant for the training and development team. We focus mainly on offering online and traditional college level workforce education and training to adult learners in the areas of computer applications and information technology, management and supervision, leadership, and business.
