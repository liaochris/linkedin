Tod Gerardo’s Summary

I’ve been a career coach and performance consultant working with professionals and individuals since 1981.

I offer a long term, passionate interest in providing talent management coaching and counseling for individuals in the areas of careers and work performance. I’ve developed and currently deliver highly customizable programs directed to career management, performance coaching, succession planning, leadership development, executive coaching, career decision-making, and job/career transition.

I have coached and counseled extensively with individuals and small groups who share the commitment and focus of effectively integrating business goals and human performance.

Whether I am coaching and counseling within organizations or working with individuals who come to me privately, it is my ongoing commitment and privilege to use my skills and competencies to match people to their jobs and careers so that they experience being more:
• Effective and happy in their career and work
• Secure and certain about their present/future
• Confident in taking on new challenges
• Comfortable/in control in a job/career change
• Fulfilled in their ability to make contributions
• Connected with their work and associates
• Rewarded for their contributions/performance

Tod Gerardo’s Specialties:

The Accord Key Person Program – From Good to Excellent ... targets the talent of smaller to mid-sized organizations that have similar technology, markets and business plans as their competitors. This program measurably increases the performance of the specific, revenue-generating and/or mission-critical "key people" and the business and profit goals for which they are responsible.
