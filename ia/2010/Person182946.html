Sarah Yancey Pratt’s Summary

Ms. Pratt is a results-driven, senior level executive with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the University of Arkansas. She is accomplished in management, technical sales and operations.

Ms. Pratt has over fourteen years of high-tech industry experience, including telecommunications, software development, sales management and company operations. She began her career as an engineer on the F-16 with Boeing in St. Louis. She then moved into management with JDSU (formerly Acterna). At JDSU, she was named the top sales manager and eventually moved into major sales team management. Then, Ms. Pratt became a Vice President for Ayoka, a software services company. Currently, Ms. Pratt is a National Account Manager for EMC and is responsible for managing the strategic direction and executive relationships for several of EMC's Global partners.

Ms. Pratt offers valuable consulting services with a background of strong technical and business knowledge. With her services, she has helped organizations streamline their processes and increase their revenue. She also has a proven ability to lead operations, teams, and establishing long lasting customer relationships.

Ms. Pratt has been a resident in Texas for the past thirteen years. She is actively involved in the community, multiple non-profit organizations and with her two children. She was honored as nominee for Women of the Year for the City of Richardson, sits on the Executive board of the YMCA and has won many business and sales awards.
