Adam Josephs’s Summary

Adam Josephs has worked with enterprise and start-up companies to accelerate their performance for over 20 years. He is currently the Managing Partner at Celerity Consulting Group LLC (www.celerityconsulting.com) which trains and coaches organizations all over the world. He works in a range of industries including software, semiconductor, material sciences and investment banking. Celerity coaches organizations to modify culture and team behavior in simple, effective ways so that they achieve product development and commercial objectives faster and more reliably.

The core of Celerity’s approach is to rigorously upgrade the transparency, accountability and commitment of teams. There is a focus on the clear articulation of goals and the early identification and mitigation of risk. We work with executive teams as well as product development, operational and customer facing cross-functional teams. Our hands-on model effectively creates lasting changes in behavior and results within our client companies.

Adam began his career in technology at companies at the forefront of innovation. At Apple computer, he led projects in cross-platform and joint venture product development. Adam then developed Internet products at Microsoft.

For the last 10 years, Adam has taught at numerous conferences including ProjectWorld, Management Roundtable, and Project Management Institute. He has served on the faculty of New York University’s School of Professional Studies and The Silicon Valley Extension of University of California, Berkley.

Adam is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and makes his home in New York City where he is working on his second book. Adam serves on the Board of Directors of the New York Musical Theater Festival and is an active Rugby player. He is a graduate of Stanford University and currently studies at the Saïd Business School at Oxford.
