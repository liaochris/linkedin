Chris Bangs’s Summary

Chris Bangs has served as a top sales/marketing educator and motivator to the retail, financial, and real estate industries. Through his classroom curriculum, seminars, webinars, and products, he has trained literally thousands of salespersons all over the United States. In 2003 and 2004 Mr. Bangs was contracted as the Director of Internet Marketing and Technology for All Fund Mortgage. In 2004 Mr. Bangs participated in training a sales force of over 2000 sales persons in marketing and internet sales transactions leading to $1.4 billion in volume and $69.7 million in revenue in 2005 and 2006. During this time All Fund was an Inc. 500 Company and listed as one of the nation’s fastest growing companies. After Mr. Bangs left LenderHost, All Fund was purchased by Carlile Investments
Mr Bangs also worked several years within a successful software development and marketing firm where he was responsible for launching, ramping, and managing several cutting edge technology products, which grew our business by $4.8 million in sales in the first year of launch.
