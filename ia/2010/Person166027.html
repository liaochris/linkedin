Bryan Bloom’s Summary

Priority Moving is a large, award winning San Diego moving company that performs over 3,500 moves per year. Services include local San Diego moves, long distance moves, international moving and secure on-site storage.

Our logistics division handles white glove, in-house delivery of fine furniture throughout Southern California and the world in addition to large hospitality renovations.

Priority Moving has been featured on/in Fox 6 News, San Diego Magazine, The San Diego Union Tribune, The San Diego Daily Transcript, and the Mark of Excellence (SDCAA).

Agent for Wheaton worldwide moving.

Bryan Bloom’s Specialties:

San Diego mover providing local San Diego moves, long distance and international moving. Agent for Wheaton worldwide van lines. We also provide logistics and delivery of fine furniture and large hospitality renovations.
