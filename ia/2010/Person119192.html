Kristina Alyssa Smith’s Summary

I chose to emphasize China in my Business Degree because I knew China would play a crucial role in the world market. In 2002 I decided to study abroad for one year in China and soon upon graduation in California, I immediately found a position with a JV in the High-Tech zone of Shanghai.

After the JV fell through, I moved to Mexico for an internship in order to evaluate potential investments of Siemens Manufacturing facilities in Mexico or China. After this Project Economist position, I was offered to take the responsibility as a Financial Analyst in Siemens Mexico corporate department.

After three years of working for Siemens in Mexico I decided to learn more about the country of origin of my company, Germany. I applied for a contract position as a Project Controller at NXP Semiconductors in order to prepare for a Masters program in Germany. Fate has it that I did not attend the Masters program in Germany but instead in Edinburgh, Scotland where I currently study.
