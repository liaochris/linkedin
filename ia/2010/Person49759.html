David Murray’s Summary

Entrepreneur and artist full immersed in the social web. The process of mixing new media methodologies with established marketing practices is something I truly enjoy. This results in successful business solutions that “push the norm” while maintaining the integrity and goals of the assigned objectives.

A member of iDetroit, I founded the Social Media Club chapter of Detroit, and I serve as one of the co-chairs for FutureMidwest, the region’s largest technology and knowledge conference. I've also been quoted in Wall Street Journal Online, selected to be member of Detroit Chevy 2010 South by Southwest Team, and have been featured in David Meerman Scott’s blog, Web Ink Now; and his book, The New Rules of Marketing and PR.

I carry extensive experience in online community management, social media, product development, and projects that require creative out of the box solutions.

Sharing what I have learned is my true passion and working by the mantra, “leave no regrets”, I am constantly motivated to learn new technologies and to push my creative energy in any project I am involved with.
