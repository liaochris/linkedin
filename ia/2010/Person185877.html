Maria Ortega’s Summary

First NYS Division of Criminal Justice Security Guard School, approved and operated by a Hispanic female. Ms. Ortega has over twenty years in Law Enforcement and the private sector Ms. Ortega was approved by New York State as a Security guard trainer in 1993, and a New York State approved School Director since 1994, In 1995 she was part of the undercover team for Mayor Rudolph Giuliani at the event held at Yeshiva University in New York City. In 1999 Ms. Ortega became President/CEO of Security Works Inc., providing training & placement as a sub-contractor to Curtis & Associates Inc., with the New York City Human Resources Administration W2W & SAJP contract, she was able to place 90% of all participants trained. Ms. Ortega was recognized by The Strathmore’s “Who’s Who” distinguished achievement in major professions throughout the United States in 1998 and 1999. She was recognized with the Rising Star Award from New York State Department of Labor, Latino Job Service Employer Committee for excellence, growth and assisting the community 2000. In 2001 Ms Ortega, was elected as the Chairperson for the New York State Department of Labor, Latino Job Service Employer Committee.

Maria Ortega’s Specialties:

Over 20 years human resources & workforce experience in diversified business services. In-depth understanding of compliance auditing,HR management, employee relations, recruiting, incentive compensation, leadership development, and training.
