Derek Falk’s Summary

In case this is your first time visiting Falk Custom Adventures and you’re wondering what all the commotion’s about, first let me welcome you, (Hello), but also let me tell you a little bit about ourselves.

Falk Custom Adventures was created by Derek Falk, a guy who really enjoys meeting new people and loves all types of adventure, but most of all just wants to have a good time.

Derek worked many years as a firefighter and paramedic, and then one day decided to follow his passion for travel and adventure and, “Voila!,” he started this amazing little company that we have today.

Traveling, having a good time, meeting lots of great people, immersing ourselves in different cultures and experiencing as many new things as possible is our passion. We use all of those elements to design amazing adventure itineraries to some of the most exotic destinations found anywhere in the world for small groups and individuals.

If you want to go on an authentic safari in Africa, we'll do it. If you want to trek tigers across India by elephant, we've got you covered. If you would rather relax on a private island lost at sea for a while, we can do that, too.

We really have a great time doing all this, but we definitely couldn't do this without all you good people! We look forward to having you join us on any one of our trips or just be a part of our extended adventure family.
