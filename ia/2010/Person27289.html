Branndon D. Kelley’s Summary

Branndon brings a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information System, a Master of Business Administration (MBA) and over eleven years of experience managing, directing, governing and implementing Infrastructure programs and projects. His professional experience bridges many industries, including financial services, healthcare, retail, consulting, industrial, government, energy, employer services, and education. Branndon is a seasoned professional with exceptional references. He has helped organizations realize business value through the implementation of Infrastructure technology solutions. Those technology solutions range in size, complexity and scope in the following areas:

• Virtualization
• Collaboration
• Messaging
• Communication
• Deployment Services
• Data Center Design, Management and Migration
• Application and system hosting
• Environment Design
• Hardware maintenance and support
• Hardware purchasing and procurement
• Small and Medium Business Managed Services.

Branndon received his Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems in 2002. In 2005, Branndon received his Master of Business Administration (MBA) with a focus on technology and finance. Most recently Branndon enrolled in Capella University and began work on his PhD focusing on Organizational Leadership. He spends his free time with his wife Shannon, his son Jackson and their Jack Russell, Mya. He is also a member of the general education department as an adjunct professor at a local college.
