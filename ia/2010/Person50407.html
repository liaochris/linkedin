David Cohne’s Summary

David specializes in Tactical Allocation Strategies, Quantitative Fundamental Portfolio Strategies (Long Only Value and Long-Short) and Manager Research. He is an investment strategist/portfolio manager with experience researching investment securities (ETFs, funds & Stocks) and managers, in addition to managing quantitative & fundamental portfolios.

He has worked in the capacity of investment management, investment publishing and investment consulting. In addition to his research and portfolio management experience, David was also a writer for an investment column and weekly market update as well as a manager for a research team.

David is always looking for new ways to analyze the market as well as economic conditions. Some new interests include demographic economic forecasting and technical/behavioral forecasting methods.

  • Executive Vice President & Managing Director of Research

    The Rankings Service

    (Financial Services industry)

    August 2003 — April 2009 (5 years 9 months)

    David Cohne was Executive Vice President & Managing Director of Research at The Rankings Service, an investment research and consulting firm. David Cohne oversaw the development and implementation of investment research and strategy for the firm. He started with TRS as an Analyst in 2003 and was instrumental in the creation and expansion of the proprietary manager database. Cohne had the day to day responsibility of managing the Quantitative TRS Equity Indices and Fund of Fund portfolios, supervising manager research and editing TRS publications. David was in charge of the firm's quantitative back tests of stocks, ETFs and Funds. David also provided manager analysis for various consulting projects such as fund company transactions (M&A, buy outs, & manager lift outs), and customized manager reviews. In addition David wrote the TRSreports Manager Spotlight, a weekly investment column that analyzed top fund managers.
