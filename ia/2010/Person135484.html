Martin Häntzschel’s Summary

Martin has over four years of experience in the Software industry, three years and counting in project management and leadership roles. His professional career began as a QC Analyst on an auditing tool project, later he wrote its documentation and after that he was encouraged to manage a project which consisted on building a software identification library for the same business vertical: Software Asset Management

Martin's passion is leading a team, trying to get the best out of everyone involved and enhance the way everyone works by institutionalizing processes and communicating openly the client's needs, as he believes the team should be aware of everything in the project. That helps to achieve consensus and reach richer team-driven decisions.

Early in his career Martin worked in T&M (time and material) projects, but now he's specializing in fixed price projects. Managing teams with a fixed deadline and budget while trying to get the best yield for the company and the best value for the client (change management process) is a challenge he's deeply enjoying.

Martin is also a Radio Host & DJ in a weekly radio show, an avid reader, taekwondoist and amateur runner.

Martin Häntzschel’s Specialties:

Martin is currently working in Gaming projects, but he used to work mostly in SAM (Software Asset Management) and LC (License Compliance) projects, CRM, Online Service Business projects and lately with Audit, Tax, Consulting, and Financial Advisory Services projects.
