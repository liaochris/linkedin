James Glazebrook’s Summary

For the last five years, I have been writing and editing for the web, contributing to music and lifestyle websites, personal blogs and “business and pleasure” Twitter accounts.

I am currently working as the voice of a major brand within social media, writing appropriate, search engine-friendly copy across a network of sites including Facebook, YouTube, forums and discussion boards, and creating insightful presentations to demonstrate the results of these actions.

This work builds on eight years’ experience of business writing, during which I have written reports to help corporate clients understand, explain and justify their media relations activity, edited reports produced by others, and ensured delivery to strict deadlines.
