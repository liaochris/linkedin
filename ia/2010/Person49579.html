Dave Hendricks’s Summary

In addition to domain expertise in Interactive Marketing, Social Media and email, I have extensive experience in building, re-tooling and top-grading all types of business operations, including client services, operations and sales. Implementing and customizing Salesforce.com for the particular needs of organization - not just sales - is a passion of mine and I have worked with their product since 2000. Building and managing collaborative organizations is something that I find challenging and rewarding.

I like people more than machines.

At Datran my teams brought on clients like Sony, BELO and Media Newsgroup. At ARGI, I worked with the CEO and BOD to raise capital and make strategic acquisitions. While with Donnelley Marketing, I successfully completed a re-staffing of two regions from top to bottom and created and staffed two new business development functions. At CheetahMail I reengineered the sales and marketing function and built the pricing model and strategy that has led it to the top of the email marketing industry.

I have extensive re-engineering, strategy design and balanced scorecard implementation skills. I am a metrics-driven leader that enjoys getting into the fray, but letting people do their jobs.

Dave Hendricks’s Specialties:

eMail marketing, Tech/Operations, Client Services, Strategy Design, Online Ad Sales, Acquisition Strategy, Sales & Business Development, Design of Customer Acquisition and Retention campaigns, Recruiting/Developing teams, Negotiation of Contracts, implementation of sales force automation solutions and sale process management. Loyalty programs, Public Speaking and Presentation. Can recruit and develop staff.
