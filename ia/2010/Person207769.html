Travis Baldwin’s Summary

With 10 years of award winning experience in both corporations and consultancies, my skills combine industrial design, brand management, and production design. This facilitates focused & successful product development in every part of the process, including the role of championing good design for good business. Creative challenges are truly my passion and I am searching for a position to bring amazing products into the world.

Currently, I am researching brand consultancy tools that could be used for 3d design, in order to make more informed strategic decisions during product development.

Travis Baldwin’s Specialties:

Industrial Design, Product Development, Brand Management, Strategy, Project Management, Design Research, Human Factors
