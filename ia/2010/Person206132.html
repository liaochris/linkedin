Tom Jonas, Ph.D.’s Summary

Outstanding facilitator of ideations and strategy sessions with multi-department teams for both B2C and B2B businesses . Leverages stakeholders’ interests to advance alignment – all with an eye on the bottom line.

Highly skilled at clarifying business decisions, specifying corresponding decision criteria, and then defining research objectives. 20+ years designing and fielding studies, reporting and interpreting results. Focused on making recommendations and drawing implications with business impact.

Experienced with qualitative and quantitative market research methods including positioning, creative and advertising testing; concept testing, tracking, trends, A&U and more.

Approachable, enthusiastic style with a roll-up-your-sleeves attitude.
