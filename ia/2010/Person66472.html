Eric S. Young’s Summary

Founded in 2003, VISIONSHOCK ENTERTAINMENT within the last 5 years has become THE premier Asian American Promotions and Events production company on the West Coast. Our goal from the start has always been to continually envision and implement fresh and exciting concepts to the Asian club scene. We cater to an upscale clientele, consisting of elite and affluent young business professionals and college grads all over the greater Los Angeles and surrounding cities as well as SF and SD.

We believe that being "cutting edge" and "new" are important when it comes to nightclub entertainment and events. We were one of the first promotional companies to bring the Asian club scene to the Sunset Strip and Hollywood and now in '08, Visionshock has changed the way Asian Americans party in California. We have set the industry standard on how events are produced and promoted with all others following our lead. Visionshock Entertainment has set the precedence and knocked down the doors that were previously closed and announced to this town that, "Asian" is no longer just a model minority...

We will constantly bring you highest quality in events as well as the best clubs and venues that LA, SF, SD and beyond has to offer. With a regular residence and rotation of the hottest and best venues, Visionshock will be constantly on the forefront of what's HOT...

We, at Visionshock Entertainment are determined to continually produce new and exciting nightlife events and this year we will once again change the landscape of the club scene on the West Coast. Stay tuned...
