Raghu Lakshminarayanan’s Summary

Pendula IT is now two years old and we have made enviable achievements as a startup company. Enlisting new and large size clients, moving to larger space, setting up security networking – conforming to the most stringent standards set so far, achieving ISO 9002:2008 certification from TUV plus we are on our way to achieve CMMi 3 by the mid of 2010.
Focus area and our core strength remain with Data Warehousing (DWH) and Business Process Management (BPM)

Enlisting new customers was no mean an achievement. Like I had mentioned in the last summary write up, Pendula IT, which I have co-founded, the achievement is entirely because of constant quest for identifying the right solutions to our clients what will not just serve the business through the value addition but also edge the competition in terms of efficiency, cost management, effective utilization of human resource, etc. This is how we impressed the World’s largest bank (in terms of number of branches). The result – they had to remove the project from a billion dollar software Development Company to Pendula IT.
