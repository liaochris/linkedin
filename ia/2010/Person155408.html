Nina Piccini, Dinner Bella Personal Chef and Catering’s Summary

After 9 years as a recruiter, and with a longtime interest in cooking, I made a major career change and decided to indulge my passion. I became a licensed Personal Chef, and "Dinner Bella" was born! Now I am able to cook and write about my cooking (thedinnerbella.wordpress.com). Making others happy with my meals, makes me happy, which creates a win-win! My license plate reads, "Do what you love, love what you do" and I’m doing what I love, and motivating and meeting wonderful people in the process.

    Dinner Bella

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    July 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Dinner Bella is a personal chef and catering service that offers a customized catering with a personal touch. Here's what we do:
    *In-Home Meal Preparation: Dinner Bella will stock your fridge with dinner for a week, two weeks or even a month.

    *Dinner Parties: we can accommodate small and large dinner parties from plated dinners to buffets. We can make it fancy and formal or casual- whatever you prefer!

    *Cocktail Parties: we can prepare a delectable and delicious spread of appetizers to satisfy your guests as they sip away on their favorite drinks.

    *Romantic Dinner for Two: one of the Dinner Bella chefs will arrive at your home to prepare a three or four course dinner for you and your sweetie to enjoy in the privacy of your own home. After the meal is prepared, your chef for the evening takes off leaving the two of you to enjoy your meal.

    *Dinner and Massage: Oh yeah, you read it right. Dinner Bella is collaborating with GirlsSpaParties to provide this fabulous experience. Licensed massage therapists and your chef arrives at your house. While you and your significant other get a couples massage, your chef is in the kitchen cooking up a three or four course dinner for you to enjoy after your massage. Once dinner is served, everyone leaves the two of you alone to enjoy the rest of your night.

    *Cooking Classes, Cooking Parties and Cooking Lessons: need some help in the kitchen? One of Dinner Bella's Chefs will guide you through the endless mysteries to be found in your kitchen. Or why not try an interactive cooking party for your next get together. After all, there is nothing better to sitting down to a meal that you helped to prepare. And who knows, you might learn something too.

    *Catering: Dinner Bella is here for any and all of you catering needs. No party is too small.

    *Gift Certificates are available for any of our services.

    Check out the website for menus, pricing and additional information. www.dinnerbella.com
