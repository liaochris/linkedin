Van Minh Nguyen’s Summary

Five years of experience in telecommunications industry with activities in development, research, and work in different research consortiums. In-depth understanding of cellular technologies and solid business-management knowledge.

Specialized in cellular technologies including 2G, 3G, LTE, LTE-Advanced, and WiMAX radio access networks with competence in network optimization and design. Theoretical research includes wireless network modelling using Stochastic Geometry, Extreme value theorey, Graph theorey, and Applied probability
