Jason Lampa, MBA, CAIA’s Summary

I'm the founder of Armana Capital Partners, a 3rd-party investment marketing organization that provides Sales & Marketing services for boutique investment managers. Investment catagories we represent, currently, include:

* Mutual Funds
* Separately Managed Accounts
* Hedge Funds
* Real Estate
* Project Financing for Private Firms
* Life Settlement Funds
* Unified Managed Managed Accounts

In conjunction with our investment manager partners, we work with a select group of independent investment firms and their advisors to help identify opportunities to increase their assets under management, increase their income and make their job easier.

If I had to pinpoint one thing that differentiates our firm from our peers, is that we actively source new prospects to the advisors who are loyal to our firm.

Prior to the establishment of Armana Capital, I raised capital and provided strategic marketing services for financial advisors across all distribution channels. My efforts in conjunction with my team of investment marketing professionals have raised over $3 billion during the course of this decade. Vehicles to raise this money include mutual funds, hedge funds, separately managed accounts and alternative investment structures.

Armana Capital Partners is actively seeking to add investment strategists in our distribution efforts. Please feel free to contact me to discuss possible opportunities to work together.
