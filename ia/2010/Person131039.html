Prof Manzoor Iqbal Awan, Col (Retd)’s Summary

Initial career in the Army; held variety of jobs including 11 years of teaching assignments in various institutes including Command & Staff College, Pakistan Military Academy & King Abdul Aziz Military academy Riyadh Saudi Arabia. After early retirement from the military, was General Manager in food storage related corporation for 3 years, followed by 8 years as Head of Human Resources in a national oil & gas company. Subsequently worked in Turner Construction International LLC, a US company working as Project Mangers for UAE based Emaar's development in 14 countries in the Middle East region; heading the support team.

Currently engaged in providing Human Resources consultancy either as free lance or through Dual Matrix Corporation/Management Development Institute. Concurrently teaching Human Resources, Leadership, Conflict Management, Creativity, Innovation & Entrepreneurship, International/ Comparative Management, Research Methods, Business Communication and Corporate Governance to graduate (BBA) and post graduate (MBA) classes at various universities of international repute as a visiting faculty member.
