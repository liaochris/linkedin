David Segel’s Summary

David Segel is an active investor in financial services and media assets.
In 1992 he moved from the US to London, England, where he lived for 16 years with his wife Tina. The Segels have three children, ages 16, 13 and 12. The Segel family returned to the US in 2008 and settled in Los Angeles. Mr. Segel is a committed Christian and has been an active member of the Church of England. The Segels currently attend Bel Air Presbyterian Church.
Mr. Segel is an options market maker and open outcry trader by training.
In 1999 Mr. Segel launched a London based derivatives trading firm, the Mako Group (www.makoglobal.com) which has become one of Europe’s leaders in options market-making.
In 2007 Mr. Segel set up The Pelagus Fund, a fixed income relative value hedge fund, which was awarded the 2008 European Fixed Income Hedge Fund of the Year by EuroHedge.
Mr. Segel currently operates Arrabon Management Services (AMS) as a family office to serve the various investments of the Segel’s portfolio.
Media Businesses
Mr. Segel is active in growing media interests. In 2007 he founded Mpower Pictures (www.mpowerpictures.com) with Steve McEveety and a leadership team.
Mr. Segel is credited as Co-Executive Producer on the The Stoning of Soraya M. (2008), and Producer of the film Snowmen (2009).
In 2007 Mr. Segel also launched Mpower Media (www.mpower.net), a cable television and internet content management technology company that is committed to empowering homes to manage their intake of media by filtering out unwanted content and finding appropriate content for easier viewing.
Philanthropy
Mr. Segel is committed to philanthropy and exercises this through the Arrabon Promise Trust, managed through National Christian Foundation. APT is committed to supporting social and cultural causes. APT also invests in evangelism and other Christian causes.
