FPMSI *’s Summary

Fulfillment, Print & Mail Solutions, Inc. (FPMSI) was founded in 1993 by Laura Harper. It began in the basement of her home simply with an attitude of ”doing whatever it takes” to help her clients with their marketing efforts. 14 years later we have grown to 50 employees and have a 75,000 square foot facility, however, our objective has remained the same—“doing whatever it takes” to assist our clients! It is this attitude of going above and beyond that has driven FPMSI to our success and we will always continue to have this as the core of our business philosophy!
