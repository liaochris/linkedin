James Patrick’s Summary

My name is James Patrick and although I am originally from Chicago I have called Tucson home for nearly two decades.

I began my career as a journalist working as a staff writer and editor for several local newspapers. Then in college I developed a strong passion for photography (pun intended). In 2006 I graduated from the University of Arizona with a Bachelors in Journalism.

Focusing onto editorial and commercial photography, I have had my work appear in a variety of publications and commercial projects.

Currently I work are a freelance photographer, professional marketer, public speaker, and journalist.
