Raouf Shabayek’s Summary

I have worked for many technical magazines in Dubai, Emirates, writing in both Arabic and English language, garnering a vast experience in technical writing, varying from electronic gadgets, mobile phones, to videogames.

I also run an Arabic Entrepreneurial Blog (blog.shabayek.com) to talk about: Startups, e/Marketing for the SMB, Sales, Advertising techniques. I also interview entrepreneurs, review books, and write motivational essays. My blog is considered among the best Arabic blogs on the internet.

To date, I’ve published five books in Arabic:
1- The Newer Translation for The Art of War,
2- 25 Success Stories,
3- Best Success Quotes,
4- Publish Your Book Yourself with Lulu.com
5- Marketing for All
Moreover, I can help you publish your book(s) on Lulu.com, send me the text, and I'll take care of the rest, for a little premium.
