Vickie Dellaquila’s Summary

Enjoy helping residential organizing clients who are very overwhelmed due to a loss of a loved one, divorce, or illness. Also enjoy with working with the chronically disorganized and hoarders. Love to work with seniors who are going through the emotional and physically overwhelming process of downsizing and relocating.
Give presentions on organizing, frugality, and senior downsizing and relocation based on my book, Don't Toss My Memories in the Trash.

Vickie Dellaquila’s Specialties:

CRTS (Certifed Relocation Transition Specialist), CPO (Certified Professional Organizer) member of NAPO, NSGCD, and NASMM. Attend conferences and educational seminars to further my education as a CPO and CRTS on a regular basis.
