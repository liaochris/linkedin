Chris Hultner’s Summary

1988 to current - Professional photographer, specializing in Action photography and Lifestyle photography, starting as the staff photographer at Hi Torque Publications, featuring Dirt Bike, Motocross Action, Mountain Bike Action, BMX Plus & more.

1992 to current - Features Editor & Photographer for nationally distributed magazines such as Motocross Action, Dirt Bike, Racer X Illustrated and Amateur MX Magazine

1998 - Publisher/Editor/Photographer Hultner Publications - Motocross West (longest running & most popular motocross regional in the country), Amateur MX Magazine (first and longest running amateur motocross magazine distributed on newsstands), Impound MX Magazine (first all digital motocross magazine in the US)

2009 - Publisher/Editor/Photographer Edmond Active Magazine

Chris Hultner’s Specialties:

22 years as an Action Sports Photographer, 18 years of editorial experience, 11 years successful publishing experience

  • Owner/Editor/Moto Photographer/Layout/Design

    (Publishing industry)

    June 2003 — Present (6 years 11 months)

    After many requests from racers, their families and moto industry, I wrapped what I had done with Motocross West Newspaper into a national monthly glossy mag. I took my Western Amateur Rankings and turned it to a National Amateur Rider Ranking. Ranking the top 500 riders in the country after 8 of the top national amateur motocross races, this is one of the most followed content items in the amateur race scene.

    Amateur MX Magazine is currently the most distributed and followed amateur mx based publication on the market. It is the only monthly amateur mx magazine that is distributed nationally. Amateur MX Magazine is also the only amateur motocross magazine on the market in the world with a digital magazine available on the internet.

    It's website - www.AmateurMX.com is currently the most visited website on the internet with amateur motocross coverage or content.
