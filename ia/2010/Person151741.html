Natalie Slater’s Summary

I have been a published writer since grade school when I helped to start St. Mary's Immaculate's first student newspaper. I went on edit my high school paper and to become the editor-in-chief of my junior college newspaper. In college I was the Food and DIY editor of our school magazine as well as a successful blogger and freelance writer. Currently I work for an eco-friendly company in Chicago, where I run all aspects of our social networking outreach including our Twitter account, Facebook fan page, corporate blog and our Ambassadors Program.
