Summary
I am a results-focused professional with passion and experience in discovering and implementing technology-based solutions to users and workgroups.

I have excellent communications skills, with a special talent of articulating complex technical concepts to users of varying technical understanding and experience.

I am experienced in incorporating both legacy and state-of-the-shelf applications into workflows. My creativity at finding practical and elegant solutions has been especially useful.

To help companies build teams using the best people possible. I believe teams should be built by finding people with the right skills and availability, regardless of their physical locations, and I will fnd solutions for these distributed teams to work well together.

I have performed the role of Project Manager for many medium and large projects, from department-level portals through smaller corporate development efforts. I have also managed other Project Managers for very large projects, such as replacing a corporate intranet with a secure extranet.

I have excellent communication and people skills, including management, and I am also creative and innovative.
