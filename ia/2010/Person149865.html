MSP University’s Summary

MSP University provides I.T. business operations, sales and marketing and technical service delivery improvement education, training, fulfillment and consulting services to membership organizations, vendor channels, franchises and independent I.T. service organizations worldwide. MSP University is the ultimate resource for I.T. service providers either preparing to transition to an annuity-based Managed Services delivery model, or who are already delivering Managed Services, and wish to increase their knowledge of Managed Services vendors, services, solutions and business, technical and sales and marketing best practices.

We assist our Manufacturer, Vendor, Distributor and Franchise Organization clients better understand the needs of their channels and partners, and develop internal as well as partner-focused training programs designed to help their partners become more successful. We also create content in the form of Best Practices Guides, White Papers and recorded and live marketing and training Webinars, and provide marketing, benchmarking and peer group advisory services, in addition to product and service consulting.

Our industry experience differentiates MSP University from any other organization of its kind anywhere – after all, we wrote the book on Managed Services…
