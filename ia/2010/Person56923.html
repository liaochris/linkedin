Dirk Robinson’s Summary

My strength comes from working 20+ years in some of the world's leading advertising communications companies in the USA and Europe. 16 of those years were at Wieden + Kennedy (first in the head office in Portland, Oregon 1986-1996; in Philadelphia to close down that office; in Amsterdam 1996-2000; and finally in London 2000-2002 when I left to begin consulting). Job-wise, I have done it all--from basic accounting to managing large finance departments; from simultaneously wearing the HR hat while assisting with IT and Office Management issues while being the Finance Manager/Director. I have helped establish foreign offices, manage fast-growing head-offices and also closed down an office. I have assisted clients in the boardrooms of major corporations (Sara Lee, Nike, Royal Bank of Scotland, Adidas) to help negotiate client-agency service agreements and revenue retainers. I enjoy helping fast-moving offices develop policy, create procedure and implement documentation to 'manage the chaos'. Writing policy, dealing with expatriate issues, setting up budgets, negotiating with suppliers, re-designing inefficient processes, creating templates, assisting head management 'tame' their clients to protect revenues, searching for relevant software systems--anything needed to help a business change for the better and grow profitably is my interest.
