Felicia Gray’s Summary

Felicia Gray has a ten-year track record working in international, domestic and regional marketing/ public events, with multimedia, interactive, print, television and sports companies. Her experience includes working in major corporations, including Channel 4 TV (UK), Time Inc. and the National Football League. Felicia's staff positions have included marketing coordinator, special events, client and project management. She's been independently responsible for planning and managing event logistics including housing, transportation, PR, temporary staffing, food & beverage, VIP’s and budget management. Felicia's public events and project management experience includes four Super Bowl's (XXXIV, XXXV, XXXVI & XXXVI), as a liaison to international television partners to produce game programming for international markets; event management for the 50th Royal Film Performance, in the presence of the Queen and the Duke of Edinburgh; and promotional activities for Channel 4 Television. Her international and domestic experience working with diverse markets and communities would bring added value to any company.

Felicia is a decisive, results-oriented professional. She understands the importance of connecting and being connected with people to meet deliverable goals. Felicia has been successful in implementing time sensitive projects adhering to budget requirements. In addition to having demonstrated a track record of achieving consumer marketing, public events and corporate branding deliverables on schedule.

Felicia's experience and core competencies: Entrepreneurial Drive, Logistics Management, Consumer Marketing and Team Management support her in achieving great success for the companies she works for to inspire their audiences and create brand loyalty.
