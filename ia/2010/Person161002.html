Paul Rossi’s Summary

I am the communications, advertising and general fundraising contact for Southern Tier Hospice and Palliative Care. We represent patients and families in Chemung, Schuyler and Steuben Counties of Upstate New York. I enjoy planning our many fundraising events and bringing in the much needed funds for our organization to support those in our care.

I am very active in our local community and continue to look for ways to build a better environment for my children in this ever changing and more challanging world. This works well with my very active political core.

Most of all, I am a proud father and husband - my two favorite jobs in the world.
