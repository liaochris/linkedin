Jesus Bibian, Jr.’s Summary

Freelance 3d Artist specializing in the creation of digital models, both character and non-character work, used for print, multi-media, film, animation, and 3D. Particularly interested in creating digital content for broadcast media, print advertising, and film.

Jesus Bibian, Jr.’s Specialties:

·3D modeling of both organic and hard-surface objects using polygons and subdivision surfaces.
·3D sculpting using Mudbox.
·Lighting and rendering using mental ray.
·Retopology of high-resolution objects to 'cleaner' more efficient meshes.
·UV mapping.
·Texture painting.
·Basic to intermediate animation.
·Traditional drawing.
