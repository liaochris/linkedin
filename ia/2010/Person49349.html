Dave Bahls’s Summary

Telecom consulting sounds complicated, but what we do is to simplify telephone and data network services for companies and save them a lot of money in the process. Best of all, our services are totally free. Companies can check on the health of their telephone and data network without worrying about the cost.

Here is what we do:
• Analyze phone bills to find billing errors. 80% of all telephone bills have errors.
• Suggest solutions to phone and data network transmission problems
• Get multiple quotes on telephone and data network services and systems
• Coordinate presentations by telecom hardware vendors
• Implement telephone and data network improvements
• Integrate cellular services with land lines
• Monitor telephone bills for accuracy. 85% of all companies pay phone bills without even checking to see if they are over-paying.
• We partner with every top-name technology provider and carrier in the industry so we always provide a state-of-the art solution.

Companies who worked with Symplicity Communications last year saved an average of $16,536 or $1,378 per month.

I really enjoy saving that kind of money for local businesses. I would like the opportunity to show you how I can save your company a lot of money also.
