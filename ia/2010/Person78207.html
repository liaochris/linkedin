Greg Stuart’s Summary

Proven digital media General Manager – Has built consumer digital media & Internet Ad Technology businesses since 1993 with successful acquisitions & IPO.

Board Member - 3 exits of companies achieved liquidity events last 24 months over $750M & not less than 10x multiple revenues.

Recognized thought-leader in digital media & advertising & identified by Ad Age as 1 of “10 Who Made Their Mark” in '06.

Former CEO of Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB), serving AOL, CBS/CNET, Google, Disney, Yahoo! & others. Led the industry from $6 to $17 billion annually, while growing IABs revenues +500% (37% CAGR) in 4 years.

Ad Age identified his book, “What Sticks: Why Most Advertising Fails and How to Guarantee Yours Succeeds,” as the “#1 book you should have read".

Additional Highlights:
* VP Biz Dev/Ad Sales & Marketing at Cars.com, drove site to #1 six mos after launch
* VP BD at Flycast Networks, which IPO'd & was sold to CMGI/Engage
* Former Venture Partner/EIR & CEO of Ad Technology companies
* Award winning campaign for Sony Online Ventures' social media site - The Station
* Founded & built Interactive division at world’s largest direct marketing agency Y&R's Wunderman
* Extensive consumer Brand ad background

Current BOD member of Zimbio, backed by August and Menlo
Former BOD of Rapt in SF, sold to Microsoft
Former BOD of Allyes in China, sold to Focus Media

Served on Advisory Boards of venture-backed companies backed by Softbank, Conway, TimeWarner Ventures, M. Eisner, Greylock, Spark, Intel, DFJ, USVP, Greycroft, Union Square Ventures, First Round, Oak, Canaan, Velocity & more.

Member of National Speakers Association & global speaker on issues facing advertising & digital media - Istanbul, Israel, Germany, Mexico City, Jakarta, Sao Paulo, Switzerland, Zurich, Shanghai, Sydney, Barcelona, Tokyo, Buenos Aires & others.

Lives happily near New York City in Bridgehampton, NY with wife Pamela, twin daughters & son.
