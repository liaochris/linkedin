Marc Dooley’s Summary

Light-hearted BIO

I am half-Korean and half-Irish so I have heard it all. My business background is as diverse as the different looks of my three daughters which range from blonde headed and blue eyed to black hair and brown eyes. Growing up in the South, I cannot remember ever having a bad peach cobbler or a summer that was not spent on a river or lake. I grew up a son of an Army General so discipline and leadership comes second nature to me. I am passionate about helping people grow their business.

I started my career with an open heart and drive for success. Despite my charming personality, I was recruited as a Naval Officer in the Elite Search and Rescue and Special Operations Group. Basically, I could go anywhere, by any means, save you and give you a hug afterwards. I then moved into corporate America where I quickly climbed the ranks earning a reputation for being a change agent and team builder. I combined my strengths as a big picture visionary and strategic leader to grow sales and market share by double digit averages at every company I was employed. You won't find me sitting behind a desk, but leading the charge and setting the example. I have owned my own companies, worked for Fortune 500, and made small companies little giants.

Over time, I realized that my true passion was not only solving big corporate challenges, but also helping people succeed on a more personal level. If you asked me what was my biggest business accomplishment, I will recall the time that I took minimum wage employees and groomed them into six figure income producers as well as landing a Global Account with $108M in revenues per year for an Interactive company. Today, I am looking for my next career and want to make a difference. If you can’t reach me during business hours……………try the golf course.
