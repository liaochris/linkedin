Chad Weinman’s Summary

I have multiple years of experience with IT auditing & security consulting within the “Big 4” professional services firms. My experience with both domestic and international clients includes internal/external audits, security pre/post implementations, compliance benchmarking and readiness, and technical security configuration projects.

Additionally, I have experience with the deployment and configuration of application tools to assist in audit controls and business processes; whether it be security logging, assessing segregation of duties, provisioning of users, and application integrity.

I add value to my clients through my deep knowledge of financial and IT security risks, my auditing experience across multiple industries, and through leveraging my prior experience as a software engineer designing & developing application solutions.
