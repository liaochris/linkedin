Scott Hull’s Summary

SCOTT HULL defines the gap between integrated strategy and identity expression where visual branding is important.

As a designer-turned-creative-entrepreneur, I am well aware of the demands of creativity in the corporate world. Early on, I learned the ins and outs of the business, and realized I enjoyed assembling the right team to pull off a project as much as the hands-on designing part. This realization led to a new creative alliance serving other artists, art directors and designers. For 29+ years, I’ve looked beyond the traditional representational model, choosing artists not only for their distinctive styles but for their professional demeanor. All of the artists on my team exude professionalism. And in an art world of prima-donna’s, it’s a refreshing experience to work with artists who understand the concepts, produce on time, and genuinely love their work.

As a visual branding leader, SCOTT HULL ASSOCIATES has served over 8,000 global customers delivering artistic results to the buying needs of global market leaders, Fortune 500 companies, respected agencies and design firms, large and small. We match art with concept to deliver creative solutions for clients in industries from consumer goods to healthcare, publishing to technical.

Clients range from small start-ups to bigwigs like Target, Microsoft, Scholastic and Disney, catering to national needs like those of Leo Burnett or Starbucks and local regional needs like Whole Foods or The Tampa Bay Museum of History.
