Peter A. Wachtel’s Summary

A “KID” at heart... I live & breathe the market place of toys & gaming every day, and have been a designer/ creative- art director, inventor & instructor in the Toy and Entertainment design industry for over 15+ years while having fun (I’m Left & Right Brained)... Have worked on licensed and non-licensed properties in many categories: preschool, girls toys, boys toys, premiums, vehicles, action figures, plush, sports, product design, educational toys, and theme park design…

"I Play, therefore I am"...

*Designed more than 500 products for the Toy, Entertainment & Video Game industries- Featured in: ID Magazine, Playthings, The Toy Book, Licensing Book, Kids Screen & The Smithsonian...
