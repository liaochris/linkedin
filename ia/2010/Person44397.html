Joe Crawford’s Summary

I started a company in my early twenties, a commercial construction firm in Michigan. Built from scratch this company grew to over 2.5 million and over 30 employees in under 8 years. Wow! That's a short summary for an awsome experience.

This was THE experience that taught be about what it means to succeed in business. From creativity to competition and from picking a good market and knowing when to quit.

For most of the years I was building this business I felt like there was the big "Thing" going on and I wanted a piece of it. Wanted to be a part of it. That "thing" was the Interenet and I'm happy to say 5 years later I'm right in the thick of things.

I became quite profficient at Search Engine Optimization and SEM...Search Engine Marketing. I offer consulting on these matters and an SEO website solution that rocks. I use this solution myself and get over 40k pageviews a month. We offer a no strings attached platform to get more clients, customers and sales in any industry. Zero ongoing fees, automated social networking and the SEO is out of this world. It is the best software for natural search and ease of use, I've tried them all and have 3 years of proof on my own sites.

My goal in life is to be engaged in pursuits bigger than myself and in service to others. I've learned I feel good when this occurs, I like to feel good, so that is my goal. I have a passion for making your business better whether that means more clients, customer and sales or creating more free time through helping to stream line systems.

I am an entrepreneur to the core. One of my favorite definitions of "entrepreneurial" is moving something from a place of low value to a place of high value.

The Internet solution my company offers does just that, if you have something worth offering to clients, I can help you do it.
