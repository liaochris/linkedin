Key Compton’s Summary

Co-Founder of a new start-up in the online advertising industry working to pioneer socially targeted advertising.

Co-Founder of SoundSpectrum, Inc. the leading provider of "music visualization" software for the online music industry (licensed to Apple, Microsoft, and Yahoo). Paying customer base in the hundreds of thousands and registered database of users in the millions.

Co-founder of Mimeo.com, Inc. which provides online document printing services. Mimeo is based in New York with printing facilities in Memphis, TN, Newark NJ and Oakland, CA. The company has won numerous awards, including the Inc 500 List of Fastest-Growing Companies, Deloitte's Technology Fast 50, AlwaysOn 100, and Red Herring 100 North America.

Founder of Solbright, Inc. providing revenue management and billing software systems for the online advertising industry with customers such as Yahoo!, Lycos, The Weather Channel, MTV Networks, The New York Times, USA Today, The Washington Post, and CBS SportsLine.

Hobbies include skiing, mountaineering, rock climbing, running, photography and spending time with my family.

Key Compton’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur, manager and investor in multiple software companies operating in the entertainment, advertising and financial industries. Expertise includes sales & marketing, product design & development, finance and legal.
