Bart Bierman’s Summary

Herakles Pharma & Research is a SME focusing at outsourcing of interim/freelance clinical research personnel, like sr. CRA's, CPL's and MA's. The company has been incorporated in 2008 and employs 7 dedicated professionals, all detached/contracted to wellknown CRO's and pharmaceutical companies.

Bierman Pharma Biomedical is a Dutch consultancy company in the pharmaceutical industry and life sciences biotech. Core business is consultancy “in and around” clinical development of drugs (phase II-IV clinical studies), medical affairs, (medical) marketing, medical dpt. mgt.and small company general management. BPB offers services in NL, Belgium, and the UK; but also in other Western European markets. Via BPB Bart Bierman works as self-employed professional (freelance contractor) and will, further to pharmaceutical, healthcare clients or CRO’s, also take projects from executive search agencies, network colleagues.

Bart Bierman’s Specialties:

Pharmac. Physician with clinical/medical/marketing experience in most therapeutical areas, at several levels of seniority, in most NL pharma affiliates - Bart is somebody who can be assigned in any phase of pharmaceutical life cycle mgt. to support your companies, product or project succes. Bart's specialty is being a generalist in the true sense of the word. No nonsense transition from science to market. Modesty. Forget those zillion marketing and managing models. Do your work.
