Mike Timmons’s Summary

I have over 21 years of experience in the automotive industry; I started out washing cars for a large auto dealership at age 14 and since then have built a reputation for design, outstanding sales expertise and customer service. From washing cars to owing my own retail accessory shop, I’ve worked just about every angle of the industry. In 2005 Lanphere Enterprises and I joined forces to open the Northwest's leading accessory store in Beaverton, Oregon; BTL Auto Sports & Off-Road. During this time I was able to focus on building award winning one of a kind show vehicle. In 2008 I was recruited by Unichip of North America for the North American Sales Director position where my primary goal was to educate end users about custom tuning and increase sales through all outlets of distribution. With the down turn in the economy Unichip was forced to let me go, fortunately for me SS Sales & Marketing asked me to be there representative for Washington, Idaho, Montana, Alaska and Western Canada. I represented manufactures such as; K&N, Magnaflow, B&M, Hurst, SSBC, Daystar and many more. I worked close with WD to build sales through a tough economy and was the communication liaison for the manufactures. I’m currently working for Wes-Coast Marketing as one of their Northwest Manufacture Representative. I’m very excited about this new venture and selling lines like MBRP, Diablo Sport, Bedrug, Convert-A-Ball, CST Suspension, Eaton, Extang, Goodridge, Heatshield Products, Husky Liners, Keystone Restyling, Master Craft Seats and Safety, Mc Guard, Pop-N-Lock, Pacesetter, Percy’s High Performance, Ramsey winches, Street Scene and truXedo.
