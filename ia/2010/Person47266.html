Daniel Hindin’s Summary

Having gathered an extremely wide variety of professional and volunteer experiences during my nine-year career, I've decided to focus my efforts toward combining my passions of marketing and media by returning to school at Northwestern University's Medill School. I'm currently pursuing a master's degree in Integrated Marketing Communication with a concentration in Media Management, which is a crossover program with the Kellogg School of Management.

When I graduate in December 2010, I'll have built upon my natural communications skills and background in social media to be able to build and grow marketing programs that resonate with the consumer AND measure the results effectively.

Within a month of entering Medill, I was named Managing Director of Vitamin IMC, the school's student-run blog. With 20 people on staff, we're focused on becoming one of the top marketing blogs in the nation. Check us out at www.vitaminimc.com.

Daniel Hindin’s Specialties:

My strength lies in my diversity of left- and right-brained skills. While I’ve spent most of my career building connections between companies and consumers using the written and spoken word, my graduate studies have allowed me to explore my natural talent in making sense out of complicated data. My understanding of data analytics, my passion for social technologies and my talent for communicating make me a well-rounded asset who can harness the power of the full range of marketing processes.
