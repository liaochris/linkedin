Miguel A. Moran’s Summary

Miguel A. Moran is the President and CEO at Moran Consulting, an independently owned consulting firm located in the States of Florida and New York specializing in the ever expanding industry of Correctional Services within all levels of the government and the private sector. We bring professional expertise in all areas of correctional operations and management with great attention to detail to all factors governing such operations.

Miguel A. Moran, a twenty plus year veteran of the New York City Department of Correction has served both as an Decorated Officer and Union Official with the Correction Officers' Benevolent Association, Inc. in the City of New York. Mr. Moran is also a veteran of the United States Marine Corps, having served his country between the years of 1980 to 1986. Miguel has worked in many areas of corrections and has forged professional relationships with many of this industry's leaders and managers who make up the extremely experienced and capable network which the Moran Consulting firm offers to their client base.
