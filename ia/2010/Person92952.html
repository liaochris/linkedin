Jeff Beals’s Summary

As author of the award-winning book, "Self Marketing Power: Branding Yourself as a Business of One," I travel the world delivering keynote speeches and in-depth seminars for companies, associations, colleges and other organizations. The presentation and book help professionals develop widely known and respected personal brands and then use the power of their brands to attract more clients, go further in their careers and make a bigger difference in their communities. As of May, 20, 2009, I have spoken in 44 cities and 20 states.
