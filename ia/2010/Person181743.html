Sandra DeWitt, RN, MSN, ACC’s Summary

Healthcare today demands that Physician leaders demonstrate exceptional leadership skills. I offer 28 years of experience as a healthcare clinician, coach and trainer and mentor for motivated leaders. These inspired Leaders work with me to take action to move their knowlege and skills to the next level of leadership to become Exceptional Leaders.

I am grateful to have partnered with over a hundred inspired, growing Physician and healthcare leaders by building an environment of trust and support for them to explore their ideas, expand their possibilites and to take action to unleash their potential.

    We utilize the 360/Individualized Conflict Dymanmics Profile and DISC assessments with leaders to maximize their insight and knowledge of their specific habits, patterns and areas of growth.
