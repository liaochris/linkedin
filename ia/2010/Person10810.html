Anders Östlund’s Summary

Anders Östlund holds an MSc from Stockholm School of Economics. While a student at the SSE Anders was also involved in the establishment of the Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship. Anders became inspired while studying and working at SSES and chose to become an entrepreneur rather than pursuing a career on the corporate ladder.

Anders Östlund's entrepreneurial projects include an e-learning platform, a health clinic and a convenience retail chain. In May 2006 Anders co-founded Ticket2.com, an internet market place for event ticket trading. And, since its launch, Ticket2.com has grown rapidly throughout the Nordic countries.

In spring 2007 Anders was listed as one of Sweden’s hottest IT-entrepreneurs by Internet World.

In April 2009 he moved to Kiev in Ukraine where he started the professional development network Stella100, which cooperates with companies and universities in the Ukraine to further recruitment, employer branding, career development and professional training.
