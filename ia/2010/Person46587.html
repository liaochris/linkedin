Daniel Constantine Gregory’s Summary

I have developed a passion for building organizations around engineering challenges. I have worked on power system projects throughout the world and I cannot imagine anything more exhilarating. I am proud of my contributions to building our modern power system, but I am humbled by the challenges that we now face.

"Challenges are the renewable fuels that we need to feed our imaginations and power our future"

Daniel Constantine Gregory’s Specialties:

I have assembled and led teams in all phases of business development for Smart Grid, SCADA, substation automation and integration, Intelligent Electronic Devices, and solar generation. I consulted with EPRI for five-years helping define, develop, and pilot the Utility Communication Architecture (UCA). UCA ultimately formed the foundation for IEC61850, the new world-wide standard for the design of "Smart Grid" and electric substation automation.
