Priscilla Grim’s Summary

I am foremost, a fundraiser, and I place myself into the mind of a front line development officer when I am investigating individuals. During the discovery process of prospects I make sure to exhaust every avenue of information both online and terrestrial. When I construct the profile, I constantly think about the many ways in which and why the prospect could and should be introduced and cultivated into the organization.

I embrace and I am passionate about the intersections in the fields of Prospect Research and online technology. As the world becomes an even further transparent business environment, the opportunities for information cultivation will increase exponentially. For the NGO industry this will continue to improve the ability to find and cultivate the best major gift prospects so the only challenge remaining will exist in “making the ask.”

Priscilla Grim’s Specialties:

Prospect Research, Project Management, Information Management, Implementation of Web Based Solutions, Database Management using the following systems: Rasier’s Edge, ResearchPoint, Filemaker Pro, Microsoft Enterprise, and Pledgemaker
