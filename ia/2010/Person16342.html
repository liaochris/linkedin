Arjun Roychowdhury is a seasoned technology and business executive who has served many roles in the past. His core focus is to transform emerging technologies into profitable businesses for a company.

He currently serves as the Head of Mobile Terminals and Applications at Hughes Systique Corp (HSC). HSC is a HUGHES company, an industry leader in IP and communications technology. In his role, Arjun leads the company's efforts in the fast-paced mobile terminals and applications market. HSC works with leading ISVs, OEMs and Operators and helps them build innovative applications for a wide variety of platforms and networks that span from messaging to social networking to location based applications.

Under his leadership, HSC has become a strong player in mobile applications and has helped several large and small customers roll out cutting edge solutions on emerging platforms such as Iphone, Android, Palm Pre as well as well established platforms like Symbian and Windows Mobile.
