Alexandra Suazo’s Summary

A graduate of CUNY The City College’s Media and Communications program, Alexandra Suazo came to Shiraz Events as a PR/Marketing assistant to president, Shai Tertner. In the last year, she has worked in generating brand awareness on all levels of communication from social media marketing (Facebook, Twitter, blog, website, industry websites) to PR (photo shoots, industry collaborations, sponsorships, celebrity management, press placements) for both Shiraz Events and Shai Tertner brand identities. Alexandra has been successful in landing placements for both brands in BizBash.com, Manhattan Magazine, Brides Magazine, Best Events, Catering Magazine, The Knot, in addition to supporting Shiraz’s finalist position in the BizBash Awards and JuliB Style Awards.

Alexandra assists in the PR/Marketing elements of production events, as well as marketing and business development for press events, primarily with The Holiday Lookbook (a biannual media-only event produced by Shiraz Events for short and long lead placements for new and exciting products/brands). Alexandra has shown successful relationship development with PR firms, publicists, media and brands, and has helped Shiraz continue developing and growing its presence in the industry.
