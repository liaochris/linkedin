Jason Roberts’s Groups:

In 1995, Jason took that experience and started his own investment company, JKR Investment Group Inc. He is considered one of the foremost experts in the real estate industry investing primarily in distressed foreclosure properties. As one of the first investors to control the process from lead generation to resale, Jason has spoken in front of several investment groups all over the Midwest. At the peak of the market in 2005, his company was rehabbing five properties at a time.

In 2002, he created his Realtor company, JKR & Associates, Inc, primarily to sell his investment property.

In October 2007, Merida Marketing Co Inc launched the new website, SellMyHouse.com. Currently, the website is one of the fastest growing real estate companies in the country and manages a national FSBO, Realtor, and Investor network. As one of the first real web 3.0 companies, SellMyHouse.com operates a customer service call center.
