    A DISTINCTIVE STYLE MAGAZINE

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    A Distinctive Style is an eMAG for today’s readers who are interested in living a greener, healthier life without sacrificing comfort or style. ADS is an interactive magazine that provides an original presentation you won't see anywhere else. Our one of a kind publication will entertain you with a variety of eco merchandise, living green tips, recycled products, inspiring interviews, unbelievable artwork from around the world, award-winning graphic design and topnotch journalism. We cater to an earth conscious, art loving, community by inspiring everyone to live their very best life. We support a socially responsible, environmentally conscious lifestyle and strive to help those less fortunate by bringing awareness through inspirational stories and videos.

    Our Mission is Simple: To Bring Awareness, To Entertain, to Lift and Inspire You to Create a Better Life Now and for the Future.
