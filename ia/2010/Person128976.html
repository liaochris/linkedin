Luke Wellock’s Summary

I am currently finishing my undergraduate degree at the University of Leeds Business School. I study BA management and from spending a year in Copenhagen I now have a wide array of international experience upon which to draw from. My most recent professional experience has been with *RMM London, a social media company which helps clients understand the opportunities social media offers. My role was the plan, implement and nurture campaigns into success.

My professional goals are to continually further my knowledge of CSR and general sustainability to be ultimately situated in a profession where I can mix my business mind with my passion for change to further positive and rational changes to the world we live in.

    (Online Media industry)

    June 2009 — August 2009 (3 months)

Working for a charitable cause gave me a feel for CSR issues from a young age which in many respects I can hold accountable for my interest in the topic today.
