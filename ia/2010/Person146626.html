Mindy Thomas, MS, CPRW, CFJST, CJTC’s Summary

As a career strategist, I specialize in résumé writing, career coaching and career counseling. Having dedicated my career to assisting in the professional development of students and adults, I am committed to helping professionals brand or re-brand themselves, so they can compete and win in the "war for talent."

As a Certified Professional Résumé Writer, I have written nearly 1000+ résumés over the years. Having developed professional recognition in the greater Philadelphia area, I have also been a guest on NPR, interviewed by the Wall Street Journal and have been recognized as a Comcast Newsmaker of the Week.

I have extensive experience across all industries and have helped many candidates to succeed in this competitive market including mid-to-senior level leaders within the legal, finance, law enforcement, engineering and IT sectors. Armed with a flexible skill-set, I am also in equal demand by university grads hungry to land their first professional role, and by underemployed women.

In the past, I have held director level positions in admissions, career planning and placement and still continue to teach college courses in life assessment, adult development and Human Resources.

I possess a unique, diverse and global perspective with a "no option to fail" mentality. This outlook probably stems from being reared as the daughter of a Marine Corps fighter pilot, and living across the U.S. as a youngster and living and studying in Verona, Italy as a teen during my high school years.
