Nathan Kievman’s Summary

Nathan Kievman is Founder & CEO of DemingHill, a Leading Corporate Social Media Consulting & Marketing Firm. DemingHill is a consortium of the top thought leaders in each of the core disciplines of Social Media. Companies hire DemingHill to solve one or more of these problems:

- To find a BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) option for Social Media that is credible, on cue with Strategy, and can fully execute Strategy

- To recover from a failed or stalled Social Media initiatives

- To recapture 20 to 40 hours a month of lost productivity from any member in their Organization spending 30 minutes or more a day on Social Media for business

Our experienced team has consulted with Fortune 500 companies, INC 500 companies, well known business leaders, athletes, and many small to mid sized businesses. Some of the larger companies include:

• JP Morgan Chase
• Sears/Kmart
• 1st USA Bank
• Bank One
• Forsythe
• Northern Trust
• Leo Burnette (ARC Worldwide)
• Federal Reserve, and
• Many more

Mr. Kievman is also host of Inside the Mind of a Master’s Interview series, which features top world business and thought leaders such as Bill Bartmann a self made Billionaire, Michael Gerber author of E-Myth, and many other Business Executives, Owners and Entrepreneurs from companies such as Coca Cola, AOL, and Google.

Additionally, Mr. Kievman is considered one of the leading authorities on LinkedIn Strategy, having taught over 20,000 LinkedIn members and owner of the #1 LinkedIn Strategies group. He is the author of LinkedintoMarketing, which teaches businesses and LinkedIn users how to achieve their business or professional goals using the #1 professional networking site in the world.

Mr. Kievman holds two masters degrees in Business and in Sports Administration from the highly acclaimed sports program at Ohio University and is married to his beautiful wife, Leah and has three vibrant children. He currently resides in Cleveland/Akron area of Ohio
