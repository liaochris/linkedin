Craig Owens’s Summary

Responsible for Global Product Marketing in the EA Games Label managing the EALA Games Studio business including Medal of Honor, Command & Conquer, and The Lord of the Rings RTS franchises.

Joined EA in 2002 starting at EA Pacific to launch Command & Conquer Generals. Merged with EALA to continue managment of EA's RTS business with the launch of The Lord of the Rings, The Battle for Middle-earth as well as expansions to C&C Generals. Expanded responsibility for the entire portfolio of the EALA business and marketing department with the launch of Red Alert 3, Command & Conquer 3, Medal of Honor Heroes and Medal of Honor Airborne.

Previous to EA drove product marketing for Crave Entertainment, a computer gaming company, including the Ultimate Fighting Championship franchise. Started my career at Activision in 1993 followed by a stint with Interplay Productions as well as managing my own boutique creative shop, Redbox Design Group.
