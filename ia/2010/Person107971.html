Juliet U. Okafor’s Summary

I've successfully worked with clients from different industries, each one has tapped my services to design creative sales promotions, web-savvy marketing plans and secure deceptively simple event gigs. Each project reaffirms my philosophy that marketing success is a result of clear goal-setting, practical application and a passionate commitment to the end result.

Savvy Marketing = Passion + Persistence + Performance

Marketing should also tell a story about a company. When done effectively, it often blurs the line between the product and the company. Ever heard of a company called Google? Yes? Well, who hasn't?

Savvy Marketing should:

Tell a Story
Reflect the brand
Promote the service, talent or product
Excites one's inner passion, and
Align with your overall business goals.


Those who commit to their long-term goals early, develop a marketing plan and exhibit a true passion to succeed, can experience great marketing results.

I look forward to helping you make your marketing savvy!
