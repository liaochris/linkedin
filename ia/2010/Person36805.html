Cheryl Lasse’s Summary

Helping companies achieve their sales and employee potential. I believe learning (about products or processes or anything!) should be fun and "edutaining". Life is too short to waste time on things that are not engaging. I still remember everything I learned in Schoolhouse Rock, don't you?

The company I co-founded with Stuart Rogers focuses on e-learning, sales training, e-marketing and staffing of in-house training departments.

As a content provider, we are rich media specialists. We blend marketing and training concepts, so that marketing is more effective and training is more enjoyable and better retained.

Everything we do is built on the concept of true reusability, so content can be used for sales tools, marketing AND training, and can be used in emails, presentations, and complete animated modules and delivered on devices of all types.

My background in consulting, marketing and sales shaped my desire to continually make things better and easier for my business partners.

Sales Training: Existing and custom courses.
