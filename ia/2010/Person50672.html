David Fedler’s Summary

Entrepreneur and Company Executive. Co owner of Cadpo, a PLM consultancy with operations in the US, UK, Ireland, Singapore, and Japan. Cadpo was acquired by INCAT International Ltd (London) in June 2005 and subsequently merged into the TATA group in October 2005. Served as President of Tata Technologies iKS and Chief Knowledge Officer of Tata Technologies until 2010.

Founder of Summit of Everest Group LLC in 2008, a entrepreneurial and management consultancy focused on business incubation, transformation, and brand platform development.
