Alder Yarrow’s Summary

Alder Yarrow is the founder and principal of HYDRANT, a consulting firm that focuses on designing and building superior customer experiences that span multiple channels (web, tv, retail, catalog, phone, etc). With more than 12 years experience in the Internet consulting industry, Alder has focused his career on helping clients leverage the possibilities of interactive technologies to create value and cost savings for their businesses.

Alder has led mission critical engagements for clients such as Hewlett-Packard, Fidelity Investments, Nordstrom, Williams-Sonoma, The Home Depot, Sun Microsystems, 3Com, Wal-Mart, Caterpillar, Virgin Entertainment, Rand McNally, Mitsubishi Motors, Verizon Wireless, Gymboree, Motorola, and OfficeMax. His project background includes experience with business strategy and digital brand strategy consulting, large scale corporate web deployments, intranet and extranet development, CRM automation, and business process automation, and especially e-commerce design and development.

In addition to providing leadership and general management oversight at HYDRANT, Alder also has deep subject matter expertise in the domains of interaction design and information architecture. He facilitated the creation of the industry's first and most complete information architecture methodology, and in the course of his professional career he has hired, trained and mentored more than forty information architects.

Prior to founding HYDRANT, Alder spent nearly 5 years at Sapient Corporation leading the User Experience practice for the Technology and Communications industry unit. Alder holds dual bachelors degrees in Photography and Communication from Stanford University and has also spent time studying at Oxford University. Outside of work, Alder enjoys improvisational theater, rock climbing, and drinking wine.
