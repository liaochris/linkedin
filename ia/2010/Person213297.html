Geri Reilly’s Summary

I've been selling real estate for over 27 years. I became a Realtor® shortly after graduating from the University of Vermont in 1979 and quickly became a top producer. I've been a top residential real estate agent since 1983 as well as the top overall company salesperson from 1986 to 2006 and as Partner with Coburn and Feeley Real Estate, Inc. from 1990 to 1997. I've continued to maintain my high sales performance today as the number one residential real estate producer with Coldwell Banker Hickok & Boardman Realty and the number one Coldwell Banker agent in Vermont since 2000. I consider myself a dedicated professional selling over 125 homes a year, specializing in listing homes, buyer brokering and consulting throughout Vermont's Chittenden County. I'm told I am well known for my personalized service and attention to detail. It is my consistent, professional service that allows me to be one of the few in Vermont who is able to make the statement, “Thousands of Homes Sold.”
