Andy Collinson’s Summary

I have spent 20 years working in Wales as an on screen journalist and programme maker before moving into management. Formerly, chief reporter and features editor, ITV Wales News. Also former National Executive Council member of the NUJ for Wales, a trained senior lay negotiator and founding chair of the union's Wales Council. Past Series Editor of Wales This Week and launch Producer/Director of Fishlock's Wales. Now responsible for the running of the ITV Wales newsroom on a day-to-day basis.

I have a keen and long standing interest in on-line journalism. I am diversity champion for ITV Wales and helped to set up a panel bringing together ITV Wales staff and senior representatives of the diverse communities of Wales. I have recently become an ITV group-wide Talent Champion covering issues of sexuality. I am a former trustee of one of the ITV group pension schemes.

Outside work, I am a board member of an internationally recognized arts company. I am also working on new ways of linking diverse communities with the media of Wales

I aim to continue developing my leadership skills and to use my many years experience in journalism and broadcasting to prepare my team for the challenges of the future.
