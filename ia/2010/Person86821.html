Jack Steiner’s Summary

Accomplished Professional Engineer with experience leading and overseeing large and small electrical and fire protection projects in the power and petrochemical industries. Superior analytical skills and attention to detail. Proven track record of delivering projects on time and within budget. Recognized for strong technical skills, ability to complete challenging assignments, and commitment to excellence.

    • Led engineering effort to identify and replace degraded portions (approximately 50%) of the main underground fire protection loop to meet regulatory commitments to the Nuclear Regulatory Commission.
    • Led the preparation and issuance of two major amendments to the station Fire Protection Report.
    • Converted the Fire Protection Report to an electronic file for improved access and utilization.
