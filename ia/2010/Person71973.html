Michael Gannon’s Summary

Having been involved in the pond and watergarden industry for the last 15 years on a daily basis I have come to aquire a level of familiarity with these types of systems that is very hard to come by under any other type of circumstances. Not only is my career my "work" it is also my personal passion. I have created a company that is full service, professional, and truly the best at what we do. Our installation work is not only functional to the end user, but it is also truly a unique work of art. I work with natural ponds as well remediating environmental problems and beautification. I have an intimate and deep understanding of fishkeeping whether koi or tropicals, in an aquarium or in a pond setting. Rainwater harvesting is one of our services offered. We install simple rain barrels and full blown rainwater harvesting systems. Rain garden design and installation is available as well. My desire for Full Service Aquatics is simply to be the best at what we do, work hard, and create happy satisfied customers with their expectations exceeded!

Michael Gannon’s Specialties:

Pond design, waterfall design, installations of water features, rainwater harvesting, rain gardens, koi expert, tropical fish expert, aquatic plant expert, service provider. I also have a specialty in holiday lighting, the installation fo, and creating amazingly memorable lighting displays.
