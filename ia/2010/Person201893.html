The Event Angels Ltd’s Summary

Founded in 2006, The Event Angels is a full service public relations and event management company, specialising in creating PR and event campaigns that communicate your company objectives and achievements to clients and media.

We’re young, innovative, and energetic with a wealth of experience between us. We are passionate about creating communication that matters and believe in going the extra mile focusing our energy, drive and conviction behind everything we do to make things happen for our clients.

Laura Barry MPRII, Co-founder and Director of The Event Angels Limited has over 15 years experience dealing with the public. A graduate of Public Relations Institute of Ireland and currently studying a Bachelor of Science degree in Trinity College Dublin, along with her qualifications in web design, Laura is a skilled communicator with several years of experience providing PR and event planning services to small to large size companies and select individuals.

Laura’s visionary and creative management coupled with her desire to go the extra mile to achieve results has attributed to the overall sensation that is ‘The Event Angels’.

Darragh Hayes is Co-founder and Director of The Event Angels with over 12 years experience in marketing, promotion and media. Darragh has gained experience across the broad range of business and event management from professional football and a property portfolio in Spain and the USA, to evolving into one of Irelands leading male models. His impressive contact list would make a fascinating read!

Darragh’s innovative leadership has been the driving force to the success for The Event Angels to date and is constantly reinventing his company’s services using the most up to date technology and marketing tools to sustain his clients’ competitive edge.

The Event Angels Ltd’s Specialties:

Founders and Directors, Laura Barry and Darragh Hayes have the know-how and the extensive contact list to take any individual or company to the next level. They have a knack for making The Event Angels clients stand out from the pack and taking them to the very top, as evidenced by the strong media coverage secured in VIP Magazine, Evening Herald, The Star, The Irish Times, KFM, SPIN 103.8, Dublin’s 98, to name a few.
