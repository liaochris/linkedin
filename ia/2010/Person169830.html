Rob Bernshteyn’s Summary

Enterprise software executive with over 15 years of experience in the software industry. Most recently served as part of the executive team at SuccessFactors, running product marketing & management for the software-as-a-service leader in Performance & Talent Management. Additional relevant experiences include product management at Siebel Systems, management consulting at McKinsey, and systems integration consulting at Accenture. Qualifications include an MBA from Harvard Business School and a Bachelor of Science Degree in Information Systems from the University of New York at Albany.
