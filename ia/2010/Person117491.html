    KLR Literary Management is paving a new path in publishing. KLRLM was established to give writers a place where all they do is write. With diminishing marketing budgets at publishing houses and the ubiquity of self-publishing, authors need support to get their books noticed.


    KLRLM works with authors of all types of fiction and non-fiction developing customized integrated marketing and sales/distribution plans to help you reach your maximum potential audience.


    Publishers and their authors collaborate with KLR Literary Management to leverage all resources to not only get their seasonal lists noticed, but individual titles, as well. KLRLM researches how a book’s audience consumes news and targets that community, engaging them with creative content, sleek promotional videos, and communicative blog responses.


    Self-published authors have the freedom from traditional publishing obstacles, but add the burden of handling the entire publishing process - from production to publicity - on themselves. KLR Literary Management offers guidance and expertise in handling your publishing project. We advise you through the self-publishing process, offering strategy for marketing and promoting your book and authorial identity.


    KLR Literary Management has nearly two decades marketing experience. From working closely with bestselling authors to navigating the terrain of major Hollywood studios,

    KLR Literary Management brings multifaceted creativity to the table.


    We believe in the personal touch and endeavor to form lasting, successful relationships with all of our clients. Beyond marketing a single title, we promote a writer's career as a whole.
