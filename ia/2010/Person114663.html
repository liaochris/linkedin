Kenneth Strong’s Summary

Kenneth Strong has over 30 years of senior management experience. His experience started in an upscale Boston area campus that included:

• Life Care Continuing Care Retirement Community
• A 72-bed Skilled Nursing Facility
• Certified Home Health Agency
• Wellness Center

Mr. Strong successfully directed the creation of a state of the art Wellness Center, Corporate Education Center (North Hill Academy), Information Technology Department, Certified Home Health Agency and Human Resources Department. He was directly responsible for developing and monitoring a $20.5 million budget, consistently operating within two percent of budget.

His experience also provided him the opportunity to supervise 11 department managers who oversaw 300 employees. He also initiated:

• Long range strategic planning
• Team leadership concept
• Employee training
• Benefits
• Recognition and reward program

These efforts helped him attract and retain well-trained and highly motivated staff at all levels. Has demonstrated strong financial leadership, interpersonal skills and has a record of consistently low employee turnover, Mr. Strong is now the Executive Director of Glenwood Retirement Community in Marietta Ohio.
