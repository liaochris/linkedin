Jim Bacon’s Summary

My goal is a position that satisfies my needs for professional diversity. Ideally, that would involve something that does not limit me to purely hardware or software -- something that needs a bit of both would be ideal.

Professionally, I started out in hardware (field service), migrated to engineering (bringing new product from engineering to manufacturing). That naturally led to learning software at all different levels (embedded, product test, corporate networking).

I'm addicted to all the above, resulting from experience in medical, telephony, and laser based systems.
