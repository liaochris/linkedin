Danna DuBois’s Summary

As I enter into my final year of college I have one thing on my mind: internship. As I prepare myself for my internship search I realize how much I have learned in the past few years and how rapidly I have acquired this knowledge. It's incredible. I think the most significant experience I can talk about would be my opportunity I had to study abroad. For four months I was fortunate enough to live in Leiden, the Netherlands (about 30 minutes south of Amsterdam). And it was the most incredible time of my life. I met fabulous people and traveled through ten different and wonderful countries. It was then I realized my true passion for travel and culture. Although I only have the experience from what I have learned in the classroom and my recent job with Webster, I feel prepared for anything that can be thrown my way because I have arrived to a country with no hostel booked and no map, missed flights and trains, and taken chances and mistakes and they don't scare me. I want to be challenged and I want to work my hardest everyday. I cannot wait for my internship this semester because I want to see what this is really about.
