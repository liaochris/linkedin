John Haake’s Summary

John Haake is the Head of Marketing for Turn, Inc., the smart platform for digital advertising. Through its advanced platform, Turn delivers exceptional performance, precision audiences and powerful insights to global agencies and premium advertisers.

John is an innovative and award-winning marketer who is an expert in channel integration. Having cut his teeth in advertising agencies in the 1990’s while working on fashion brands including Stetson and Democracy Jeans, he has spent the new millennium crusading for a unified path to conversion.

A staunch advocate of the “Promise of Digital,” John is known to fly into an inarticulate rage when discussing the chasm that exists between digital media consumption and its share of the advertising dollar. He is currently amassing an army of like minded coconspirators to deliver advertising from the traditional.

John’s background presents seasoned experience harnessing imagination and innovation to deliver truly inspiring and effective marketing solutions for a wide array of products, brands and industries. His mix of agency, vendor and brand experience makes him uniquely qualified to create and manage campaigns of any size and profile. John’s successes include: innovative marketing communications, SEM, online advertising, online visual merchandising, online direct marketing, CRM, breakthrough go-to-market programs, as well as traditional marketing strategies and tactics.

He matches this experience with the ability to toggle between the big picture and crucial details. Indeed, John has the hands-on experience transforming strategy into results that your brands deserve.
