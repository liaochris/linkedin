Glen Hartman’s Summary

Glen Hartman is a Partner and Senior Executive in Accenture’s Digital Practice (recently renamed: Accenture Interactive). In this role, he defines Accenture’s digital marketing offerings and go-to-market strategy. Glen also leverages his 21 years of experience in digital marketing, CRM and multichannel marketing to help Accenture clients develop winning online and multichannel strategies and build lasting brands.

Prior to Accenture Glen held the position of Managing Director, Digital Practice at Harte-Hanks where he set the vision for the company’s digital marketing offerings and provided thought leadership in digital and multichannel marketing innovation to Harte-Hanks clients. A recognized expert on Web 2.0 and emerging digital marketing technologies and methods, Glen authored Digital Optimization™, on how best to optimize customer relationships on line, a topic he speaks on frequently at industry events and for publications.

Prior to Harte-Hanks, Glen held the position of EVP/Managing Director of Horn Group where he helped to launch its Digital Marketing practice. Previously Glen was President & COO of KSciences, a technology consulting and systems integration firm specializing in CRM, business intelligence and digital solutions. Under his leadership KSciences was named as a finalist for Ernst & Young’s Entrepreneur of the Year Award in 2002.

Past positions include: Vice President of Marketing and Strategy at Identify Software, a Deloitte & Touche Fast 50 Company (acquired by BMC) and Chief Marketing Officer at Giga Information Group (now part of Forrester Research).

Glen’s career began in B-to-C direct marketing and advertising where he led award-winning campaigns for Fortune 500 clients and set the foundation for relationship-marketing for which he’s best known.

Glen received a B.A. from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and completed the graduate program in music and film composition from the Grove School of Music.

Glen Hartman’s Specialties:

Digital and Multichannel Strategy, Digital Presence (web sites, web 2.0, social networking, user experience design), Digital Media (online advertising, SEO, SEM), Digital Interaction (email, mobile) and Digital Insight (web analytics).
