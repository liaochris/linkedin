Michael Hyatt’s Summary

You need a designer, writer, editor, or proofreader. I do those things. Curiously, I do each of them well. I've designed, edited, proofread, or otherwise contributed to 90+ published books. I'm seeking a staff position or freelance assignments in print or online publishing, advertising, or marketing.

Primarily a copy editor by trade and predilection, I'll buff your author's prose to a luminous sheen while ensuring perfect grammar, spelling, punctuation, and all that, and ferreting out those rogue typos and goofs. At the same time, I'll adhere to the words sacred to both physicians and copy editors — "First, do no harm" — and never change meaning inadvertently. I'll offer suggestions and bring significant issues to your attention. I'll smooth, polish, rejigger, reword, fiddle, tweak, improve.

I can cite the Chicago Manual of Style chapter and verse, and also know and use AP style. If you prefer, I'll use APA, AMA, or your style guide of choice.

Did I mention I'm a fanatical proofreader? A former manager was fond of saying that I could spot a wrongly italicized period. And I'm some kind of walking thesaurus.

That's just the half of it. I play Adobe InDesign like a guitar hero, designing and laying out books, newsletters, ads, and marketing of all kinds. I specialize in book interior design, with more than 25 published titles to my credit. If your project is long and technical, chances are I'll do it in FrameMaker. If an insanely complex table or chart is involved, I'll use Illustrator. And Photoshop is my middle name.

Recent and ongoing freelance assignments include using CMS in editing a medical text; designing and editing a book; editing several other books, a Web site, and marketing materials; and writing a marketing course. A Web site under construction will soon showcase some of my work. In the meantime, I'll be glad to provide samples.

So let's get busy and make that crazy deadline. I'm at 951-536-4833 or artful-editor@charter.net.
