David Basile’s Summary

Perfumer with more than 31 years experience and many creative successes in varied product types with a strong concentration in both Personal Care and Household (Air-Care & Laundry). Specialties include the creation and development of Traditional, Natural and Organic Fragrances.

My professional career started in 1978 with Lever Brothers as a Senior Research Technician / Perfumery. From 1982 to 1988 I was a Perfumer with Unilever Research. Thereafter, I spent a year as a Perfumer/Research Scientist with Colgate Palmolive, which I left to become a Senior Perfumer at Flavor-Scents, Inc. Following Flavor Scents, held a Senior Perfumer position at Belmay, supporting their creative centers East & West Coast for over 14 years, servicing Global Accounts including P&G, Dial, Henkel and Kik. Successes at Charabot US(Robertet) have been fragrance wins at USA Detergents, AAN Services (Lauren Arpel), SCJ (Fruits & Passion). At Trilogy Fragrances, my main focus was to re-establish the creative Perfumery Laboratory, while some of my resulting successes included Burts Bees (Clorox), Fruits & Passion (SCJ), Hain Celestial (Jason), Toms of Maine (Colgate-Palmolive), Aubrey Organics and Dayna Decker, for which one of my fragrances was nominated for the 2010 FiFi Award.

Throughout my career in the fragrance industry, I have created and developed successful fragrances in both U.S. and Global, Personal-Care and Household product markets. I have been an active member of the American Society of Perfumers for over 28 years.

David Basile’s Specialties:

Creation & Development of Traditional, Natural & Organic Fragrances. Competent as Perfumery & IT liaison. Proficient using Aroma-Cis Perfumer's Software. Served in advisory capacity for over 20 years to other Senior Perfumers to assist with matters relating to Creative Perfumery, technical recommendations and evaluations.
