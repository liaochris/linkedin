James J. Sullivan’s Summary

Professional Technical & Management Recruiter and Career Counselor for 10+ years. Industry focus is chemical based consumer products, foods, nutrition, and personal care products. Primarily, we recruit Mid-Senior level Engineers, Managers, and Supply Chain professionals across the country.

Our primary goal at Ethical Search is to find top notch talent for those companies seeking assistance in locating, recruiting, interviewing, and hiring professionals. I've been very fortunate to work with, and bring together, great people and great companies!

Work very hard for my clients, but also do my best to get to know each candidate's motivators and passions. I'm interested in building long term relationships whenever possible. Always interested in networking and staying in touch. Love getting referrals because good people know good people!

James J. Sullivan’s Specialties:

Technical/Engineering, Management, Planning, and Supply Chain talent for our clients. We work with the plants, R&D, and HQ's locations.
