Laurent Jadot’s Summary

Since 1992, I'm an audiovisual producer specialised in communication films, commercials, art direction and post-production.

My highly varied career path has led me to work for various television channels and communications agencies as an executive producer, senior editor or artistic director.

I have been responsible for the creation of audiovisual identities, as well as being the executive producer for television programmes, communications films, documentaries and advertising films.

I'm the founder of DoubleDouble, a diversified video production company based in Brussels that creates institutional films, corporates, commercials and TV programs.

I also own and manage Amor et Psyché productions, an audio service provider in Brussels (radio commercials, sound design, original music,...)

Laurent Jadot’s Specialties:

TV & Films production and post-production, executive production, project and creative team management, audio services provider (music, voices & sound design).
