Richard Kallabat’s Summary

Knowing that all sophisticated clients interested in any aspect of real estate will want to work with a team, I launched Kallabat Consultants in 2003. The future of real estate is servicing clients by a team of professionals who specialize in defined areas. We have an extensive worldwide referral network which we can introduce to you.

In 2008, I relocated to Dubai to pursue new and exciting opportunities. I joined Bravo Real Estate as Sales Director. Under my supervision, Bravo has grown to become a well respected and top Brokerage in Dubai. I recruited, trained, and managed a team of Senior Consultants who specialize in defined areas in order to service clients with the greatest amount of accuracy.

Although I have moved back to the USA, I continue to work closely with Bravo as an Operations Consultant. We are always looking to add to our talented staff and wish to grow our team while adhering to our mission, vision, and values.

Some professional skills which have helped me take Bravo from inception(paper) to reality(profits) include:

* Highly articulate, demonstrating excellent interpersonal skills across all levels including management, employees, and external agencies. Genuine team player; committed to organizational success.
* Combines passion for business development with commitment to contributing to an organization's bottom line. Excellent negotiating, closing and communication skills amongst all types of people.
* Aligned company’s strategy with vision and mission of organization and led, managed business development and operations functions within a matrix system.
* Strengths in communicating complex information in non-technical terms, and inherent enthusiasm for analyzing and resolving challenging problems.
