George Dearsley’s Summary

• I have helped scores of blue chip companies and organisations to communicate better in print, on radio and television, through training and role play since 1990.

• I have my own clients and also work for top training firms like Bladonmore, TRT, Electric Airwaves, One to One Communications and Media Friendly.

• I am currently delivering a series of courses on crisis news management and internal communications for a major international training company based in Dubai.

• I am also an award-winning journalist with 30 years of experience in national and regional papers and magazines and on SKY TV and a copywriter for a range of top design companies and magazines.

• In media training I have trained, among others, the CEO and main board of the Co-operative Bank, the CEO and main board of Swinton Insurance, the CEO of the NEC, two CEOs of Earl's Court & Olympia and football manager Gary Megson.

• I have guest lectured at Manchester Metropolitan University, Manchester University and the University of Central Lancashire.

George Dearsley’s Specialties:

How to use the media with impact and authority. Key message development. Moderating or chairing meetings and events. Media training & media interviews training. Crisis news management training. Presentation skills training. Writing clear and interesting copy. Brainstorming sessions. Recording interviews on audio or video. Guest lectures on the media. Writing ingenious advertising copy. Effectıve communication skills. Confidence in public speaking. The media landscape. Feature writing.
