Laurie Lumenti Garty’s Summary

As a Managing Director in the Entrepreneur Services Group at SVB Capital, I actively manage the company’s relationships with universities, incubators, angels and a number of seed stage investors in the Bay Area.

Prior to joining SVB Capital, I was Manager of Silicon Valley Bank’s Emerging Technologies Practice, where I led a team that focuses on early stage companies that are bootstrapped or backed by angel investors. Before joining Silicon Valley Bank, I was a lender and a Director in the Venture Capital Group at Comerica Bank. Prior to Comerica, I held positions at two Bay Area startup companies; I was the Director of Strategic Alliances for Ditto.com and Business Relationship Manager for iOwn.

With nearly two decades of experience in financial services, working for the FDIC, Capital Crossing Bank and Bank of America, I enjoy leveraging the financial and operational experience I have to helping the clients of SVB. I have been fortunate to have developed an extensive network of angel investors, venture capitalists, entrepreneurs and CXOs, and I am involved in many civic and industry organizations, serving on the board of directors and advisory boards of many. Some of the organizations I am currently involved with include: The Forum for Women Entrepreneurs & Executives, The Enterprise Network (TEN), Astia, Technology Ventures Corporation, National Center for Women in Information Technology, and the Angel Breakfast Club.

I am a frequent speaker and panelist on the subjects of pitching, fundraising and angel investing and I enjoy meeting aspiring entrepreneurs.

Laurie Lumenti Garty’s Specialties:

Fundraising Strategies for Startups; Helping Make Connections to Angels and Early Stage Investors, Connecting Angels and Early Stage Investors to Each Other and to Great Startups.
