Jesse Freeman’s Summary

Over the past few years, I have been on the cutting edge of Interactive Development with a focus on the Flash Platform. All of work tries to push the envelope of creativity, technology and self-expression.

After receiving a BA, in Studio Art from FSU, I returned to New York and worked for some of the city’s most prestigious companies doing web design. As an independent Flash Consultant, I created interactive works for the Tommy Hilfiger, VW, Heavy.com, MLB, New York Jets, HBO, Fox, Arista Records, BBDO, AKQA, and many more. In 2006 I graduated from the School of Visual Arts in Manhattan, with a Masters in Computer Arts.

Jesse Freeman’s Specialties:

My specialties are Flash AS 3/Flex Development, RIAs, Photography, Technical Directing, Video Editing, and Design.
