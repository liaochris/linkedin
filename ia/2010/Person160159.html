Patrick Vrana, LEED AP’s Summary

Patrick has both creative/design vision as well as the ability to develop on the strategic level. He worked on the account side at BBDO managing and developing strategies for various clients, most notably for Cingular Wireless during their brand launch. He was responsible for over $70MM of Cingular's annual advertising budget while at BBDO. For the past several years he has been an independent marketing and web consultant with clients largely in the high-end interiors and architecture realm.

A marketing/ad guy by trade Patrick also has an entrepreneurial spirit and a true interest in the environment. Achieving the goal of becoming a LEED AP, he started a side business building houses with a heavy green element (super high-efficiency HVAC, low VOC paint, dual-flush toilets, tankless water heaters and Energy Star appliances). He did all of the space planning and design work, purchasing, financing and management of the builds.

Certified as a LEED AP by the USGBC his interests lie heavily within the green space. In late April he and his team at Local Planet will launch it's full blown site localplanet.com linking socially conscious and environmentally sustainable businesses to like-minded consumers. With both offers and content, Local Planet will launch first in Atlanta and then in three additional cities.
