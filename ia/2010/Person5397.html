Albert Caballero’s Summary

Albert Caballero is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional with expert level training, experience and education in both security management and implementation of security platforms and technologies. He has spent the past several years designing and implementing enterprise security solutions that address needs in security information and event management, incident handling and security operations management.

In addition to a CISSP, he possesses a Bachelor’s Degree in MIS Security from Florida Atlantic University and holds the SANS GCIA, GSEC Gold and SSP-MPA certifications, as well as CEH, LPIC, CCNA and others. Authored and co-authored numerous technical documents and book chapters on Security Management, secure remote access, host-based IPS/IDS and Sarbanes-Oxley compliance which have been published in the ISSA Journal, the Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance Journal and the Computer and Information Security Handbook.
