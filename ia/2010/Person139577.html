Marlon Doles’s Summary

My professional experience has primarily been focused in Human Resources spending significant time in Diversity and Diversity Recruitment, Training and Development, and Talent Acquisition. I also have extensive experience in program management having developed and managed a number of management and leadership development programs.

My strengths are building effective relationships at all levels of the organization in order to enhance organizational capability, strategy development and implementation, human capital and succession planning, training and development, talent acquisition from entry to executive levels, building and developing college relations and leadership development programs, etc.

I've also led a number of cross-functional teams to include HR professionals, line managers, senior leaders and executives at different organizations in various industries.
