Kelly Riggs’s Summary

A two-time national salesperson of the year, former Director of Sales Development, former National Sales Manager, and former owner of three different companies. My passion, and my talent, is developing people-focused leaders and high-performance salespeople.

I am a Registered Corporate Coach (World Association of Business Coaches) and currently serve as the leadership and sales & marketing trainer for the Associated General Contractors of Oklahoma. I recently appeared as a private sector expert for an Oklahoma House of Representatives subcommittee to address factors that impact employee engagement, turnover, and retention.

My first book (1-on-1 Management™: What Every Great Manager Knows That You Don't) was released in January 2008, and my second book (1-on-1 Selling™: How to Win More Sales, Defend Your Margins, & Build Your Brand) will be published in Summer 2010.
