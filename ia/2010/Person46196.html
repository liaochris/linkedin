Damien Smith’s Summary

I spent the early part of my career in a production setting, working my way up from production color-matcher to manufacturing manager reporting directly to the CEO. During this time, I developed a sense of what it takes to meet the strict demands of large-scale production in the fast paced automotive industry. I directly managed manufacturing, process maintenance, product quality, and cost-savings efforts while supervising and training production and support employees. I spent over 5 years working in Mexico at an automotive start-up facility, honing my skills and learning a second language.

While working for Stahl USA, I held the functions of Senior Technical Sales Representative, Automotive Interiors Applications Specialist and during during my final year and a half at Stahl, I was the Market Manager for Synthetic Coatings covering the entire NAFTA region (54% of all Stahl USA sales). In addition to being the key customer contact and account manager, I was responsible for developing business strategy and rationalizing new products to increase our market share. During this time I traveled extensively, meeting with presidents, VPs, and directors of both automotive and non-automotive customers. My focus was on building and maintaining relationships and forming partnerships that would mutually benefit our companies. I was instrumental in increasing sales at my key accounts, even during tough economic times.

While now working for United Paint, which is a very diverse and unique coatings company servicing the automotive industry, I have the ability to help design, sell and strategically place a wide range of performance coatings used in the automotive interior marketplace.

Unlike many, I thrive under pressure and have found the demands of the automotive industry exciting and challenging. I look forward to bringing my expertise and experience to the markets in which I have worked in or even new markets where there will be new challenges.
