  • Business Development - Middle East

    Jumpstart-up

    (Non-Profit; Philanthropy industry)

    September 2009 — Present (5 months)

    High impact entrepreneurs change the world. We help them do so.

    They pioneer financially sustainable ventures whose innovations shift or create whole industries. They address some of the toughest challenges facing humanity, often to do with basic human needs such as food, health, environment, education, clean water, and energy.

    Support and funding for early stage high impact ventures is frequently missing. Risks are poorly understood. Stakeholders tend to enter the market at a later, safer, stage e.g. venture capital firms.

    Ideas often come from poor parts of the world with few role models and no informal seed funding from friends and family.

    We search the world for such people and provide a catalyst to turn their ideas into ventures that change the world.

    Above all, they get validation and access to our international network of high impact entrepreneurs, mentors, and seed level investors.

    In short, we provide an ecosystem for seed level high impact ventures.
