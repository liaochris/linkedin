Bill Dollins’s Summary

I have been working in the geospatial industry since the early 1990s. I currently lead the geospatial line of business at Zekiah Technologies. I am interested in the integration of geospatial technologies with traditional enterprise systems. My background is software engineering and database development and I am proficient with most modern development languages.
