Bio:

Greg Erickson has over 14 years experience in Information Technology with 6 in management roles. These include government, financial, legal and the Health Care industries. Prior to joining Kaiser Permanente, Greg was a Senior Citrix Consultant with a Platinum Citrix Consulting firm in the Bay Area (Ryno Technology). During this period Greg designed and installed systems for US Air Force, Veterans Administration, North Bay Health Care, Russell Investments, and Gilead Sciences. Greg's primary responsibilities were the end-to-end system design, installation, documentation and hand-off to the firms for production usage. Prior to Ryno Greg was an Officer in the US Coast Guard managing the West Coast migration to Windows NT and development of the citrix architecture for all USCG small station installations. Greg retired from the US Coast Guard after 20 years of service with a BS in Information Technology and MS in Project Systems Management.
