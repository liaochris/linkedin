Leslie Thompson’s Summary

Top-notch marketing writer and journalist with extensive experience creating print and Web content across a range of industries. Highly-focused self-starter accustomed to a fast pace, tight deadlines and multiple assignments. Exceptional portfolio includes feature articles for trade and consumer publications, marketing collateral, video scripts, press releases, and online content.

Leslie Thompson’s Specialties:
