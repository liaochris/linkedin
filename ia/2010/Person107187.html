Jon Shamah’s Summary

A graduate of Aeronautics & Astronautics. Roles at Hitachi, McDonnell Information Systems, Thales, CoreStreet, and BBS, spanning over 10 years involvement in Identity Management and Transaction Security. Specialising in the business exploitation of technologies related to Public Key Infrastructures and IAM, especially in critical national infrastructures and large scale programs across Europe.

Vice Chairman of EEMA (www.eema.org).

Winner of 2009 EEMA Fellowship Award for Services to European Identity

Jon Shamah’s Specialties:

• Expert knowledge of national ID programs, eborder initiatives and ePassports
• Experienced Pan-European Business Development Director providing high level technical/commercial consulting on large identity projects for both operators and vendors.
• Proven track record of success and specific expertise of identity management via alliances with large System Integrators.
• Results orientated. - hands-on.
• Self reliant and enjoys green-field challenges.
