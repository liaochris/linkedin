Karen Adams’s Summary

A seasoned financial professional with more than 30 years experience growing companies in a fast paced environment and improving financial performance. Currently President of K J A Associates, LLC, a firm established in 1993 to provide consultation and support in the areas of accounting, finance and operations to early stage companies or companies in a high growth or transitional mode. Areas of expertise include design and implementation of accounting systems, budgets, forecasts and management reporting tools.

In the past sixteen years Karen has worked with over 25 companies, primarily privately-held, venture backed businesses in the areas of technology or life sciences,

Engagements can run for years or months, generally dictated by the growth trajectory of the company.
