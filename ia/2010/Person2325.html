Adam G. Lewis, CMFC®’s Summary

Adam is a dynamic financial services executive with approximately 20 years of successful sales, management and marketing experience. Adam is currently a Vice President - Financial Consultant with Charles Schwab in the San Francisco Bay Area. Adam was most recently Regional Vice President, Northern California and Northern Nevada servicing Banks and Planners, for Dreyfus and BNYMellon Asset Management. His distinguished career highlights include multiple sales honors, Dalbar Award winning team member and a decade of providing service and value for a Global Financial Leader. Adam has held various other roles in financial services distribution covering separately managed accounts, mutual funds, variable annuities and off shore solutions. He was also a Senior Account Executive at A.T. Brod & Co., a NYSE member firm.

Adam is a graduate of Nassau Community College where he earned an Associates Degree in Arts with a 3.46 GPA. Presently, coursework at Cal State - East Bay. Acquired the CMFC® designation, Chartered Mutual Fund Counselor, education program at the College for Financial Planning. Adam is currently studying for the ChFC® designation.
