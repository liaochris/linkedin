Bill Collins is an industry analyst, consultant and consumer research professional who focuses his work on the field of networked Digital Signage and Digital Out-of-Home media.

Digital Signage and Digital Out-of-Home media are networked digital media that consumers view and/or interact with on flat panel screens in retail stores and other out-of-home venues..

 Co-authored the world’s first syndicated research report on Digital Signage.

 Established the Narrowcasting News from Bill Collins newsletter, the world’s first publication to cover the Digital Signage market.

 Co-founded and edited AKA.TV – http://www.aka.tv -- the world’s first international publication to provide news and analysis on the global market for Digital Signage and Digital Out-of-Home media.

 Organized, convened and presided at the founding meeting of the Out-of-Home Video Advertising Bureau (OVAB) in January 2006 in Las Vegas at the Venetian Hotel.

 Served clients such as Best Buy, 3M, Philips Design, Instrumental Media Group, C-nario, Barco, the Out-of-Home Video Advertising Bureau (OVAB), LocaModa, Christie Digital Systems, Symon Communications, TracyLocke, Foot Traffic Media Group, ActiveLight, Benjamin Moore & Company, the Digital Signage Group and Reflect Systems.
