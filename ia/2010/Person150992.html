Implements cost accounting procedures and methods. Preparation and analysis of cost reports and costing audits. Examines and review unusual cost records and ensure cost data is allocated correctly. Participates in the month-end closing process.

Document inventory accounting procedures and generate month-end cost, inventory reports and analyses as requested by management.
Ensure that journal entries are prepared, as needed, to accurately report raw material, work in process and finished good inventory as well as rework expenses, product returns and manufacturing variances.
Reconcile inventory valuation reports to the Company’s GL ledger. Monitor physical inventories movements and cycle count procedures are being performed by performing monthly test counts to verify accuracy of inventory balances, investigate significant adjustments.
Partner with Materials Management to analyze inventory obsolescence, slow moving inventory and recommend inventory dispositions or adjustments to reserves as necessary.
Partner with the Purchasing Department to analyze the purchase price variance account and also perform year-end or mid-year standard price cost updates as necessary.
Perform standard cost updates at year-end or as necessary during the year.
Review the budgeted overhead calculation and work with manufacturing to update labor standards per product.
Assist with estimating costs for new or revisions to products.
Analyze gross margins and cost of goods sold by product.
