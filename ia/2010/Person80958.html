Heather Stone’s Summary

Well, it's June, summer's in full bloom and hazy heat, and there are all sorts of projects both on the horizon and on the back-burner. I'm doing some print reporting just to keep my skills in that area sharp. It's a somewhat stabilizing experience; a little more grounding. Blogging, however, is still quite enthralling, and with each post I consider it more of an art...the gathering of facts and links, the SEO tweaked just right, the whole philosophy is a totally different head.

I had to clear the boards a bit in order to make room for a pet project, a new diabetes-interest/advocacy blog and non-profit called DiabetesTypeMe.org, which is currently under development.

Jeffrey Slott, a good friend of mine that has the disease is working with me, along with a few other contributors. We're looking at blog architecture and logo design, and getting ready to incorporate as a 501c3 non-profit.

This is going to be a labor of love, so I'm taking it slowly.

I've also been working diligently to center my efforts around building up PostRanger.com and Lehigh Valley Source. Lehigh Valley Source is under metamorphosis from being primarily an online newspaper to becoming the number one news, arts, and entertainment aggregator for the Lehigh Valley.

I'm constantly on the hunt for new blogging and social media opportunities, so to all my contacts, please feel free to refer prospective clients to my Linkedin and Facebook pages!
