Kipp Krukowski’s Summary

Having been involved in the successful sale of over 85 businesses, my personal mission is to help business owners successfully transfer ownership of their privately-owned business while maintaining confidentiality.

As a business broker, or business intermediary, I work with business owners to help them position their business for sale opportunity to maximize value and minimize risk. I also assist individuals and companies that are looking to acquire a business. Many buyers and sellers are going through the process for the first time and I can provide valuable insight on the transaction process to ensure success.

As a certified machinery and equipment appraiser, I can also assist business owners, lenders, accountants, and attorneys in completing USPAP compliant machinery and equipment appraisals.

Being an owner of two businesses, Confidential Business Sale and Equipment Appraisal Services, I understand the issues that entrepreneurs and business owners face as their companies evolve. I enjoy working with existing business owners as they look to exit their businesses as well as future entrepreneurs that look to pursue their dreams of owning their own business.

    Value Link Advisors, a division of Confidential Business Sale, Inc., specializes in business exit planning services. We have developed a model to better prepare business owners to maximize the value of their business and provide them options for the transition / succession planning and wealth preservation. All of these are important issues that business owners will eventually have to face. Our mission is to have a positive effect on our client’s lives by guiding them through the complex, high stakes, and emotional process of transitioning out of their business. We have also learned that transitioning a business is a process, not an event. The process takes time, expertise, coordination and commitment by many parties. That’s why we collaborate with other professionals such as Attorneys, CPA’s, Estate Planners, Financial Advisors and Insurance Specialists in order to deliver superior results to our clients. The earlier this process can begin the more effective the results will be.
