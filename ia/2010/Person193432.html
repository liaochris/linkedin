Regina Saxton, RD, LD’s Summary

Regina Saxton is a registered and licensed dietitian and owner/operator of Starting Point Wellness, LLC. She has over a decade of experience providing nutrition counseling services to individuals of all ages from birth to seniors, for-profit companies, government agencies, and charitable organizations. She works closely with personal trainers, Naturopathic Doctors, Chiropractors and physicians to offer and promote a complete healthcare approach. She provides individualized medical nutrition therapy specializing in weight management, gastrointestinal and eating disorders, cancer, diabetes, and many other disease states. She also provides athletic performance education with vitamin and mineral supplement recommendations and focuses on lifestyle and behavioral change coaching and strategies for overall health and wellness. Whether your goals include weight loss, peak physical fitness, disease management, or general health education, Starting Point Wellness can get you on the right track for success! Starting Point Wellness is located in Georgia about 30 miles northeast of the Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport. Regina is available for national corporate nutrition and wellness presentations, group education, and individual consulting services via telephone, email, webcam, or in person throughout the metro Atlanta area. Please visit our services page for information on the program or plan right for you or your company and call today for your free consultation: 678-372-2346.
