Ryan Bell’s Summary

I have grown with the web marketing industry from the early days of HTML pages and META search engines through to social bookmarking and server-side languages. I have experience with enterprise-level search engine optimization for Inc 500 companies, as well as localized optimization for privately-owned firms. I have also created and maintained wildly successful PPC campaigns for the same.

Through this evolution I have gained valuable experience in outsourcing/offshoring, web analytics implementation and monitoring, split/multivariate testing, creation of KPIs for sales and marketing, as well as management of specialized project teams.

Looking to the future, I see the arena of web marketing expanding far beyond the connections between PCs, into cell phones, digital signage and navigation systems. This new world of open-source communication and advertising will require marketing strategists who understand and use the technology and who can invent effective campaigns across these mediums.
