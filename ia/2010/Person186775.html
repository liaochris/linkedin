Robert Weiss’s Summary

I founded The Sexual Recovery Institute in 1995 in Los Angeles after working with Dr. Patrick Carnes for several years. I am a national speaker and author on the subject of compulsive, addictive and offending sexual behaviors and wrote two books, Cruise Control:Understanding Sex Additction in Gay Men and Untangling the Web: Sex, Porn and Fantasy Addiction in the Internet Age. In the past few years I have appeared on Oprah, Larry King, Anderson Cooper 360, To Catch a Predator, The Discovery Channel and a host of media. Quoted in the NY Times, Reuters etc. I see select clients for treatment, but mostly travel providing professional training and program development for addiction programs and mental health centers.
