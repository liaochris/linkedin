Steve Spotts’s Summary

At present, I am finishing a massive restructuring assignment with Sperian Protection's Gloves US Division by transferring operations to China and consolidating division management at the Company’s headquarters in Paris, France. After 12+ years with the company, Sperian has asked me to relocate to another US site but I have declined. With the full knowledge of Sperian, I am actively searching for a new company that can utilize my skill set and keep me in Western Pennsylvania. My goal is to land in a new and challenging position before my Sperian assignment ends in the coming months.

I am a fair minded, well balanced manager and a people oriented leader. I possess a high level of integrity and I am an excellent planner. I am persistent, reliable and self motivated. Additionally, I am an effective implementer who can focus on the tasks necessary to achieve the goal.

I am looking for a position where I could utilize my leadership skills to run a division or a line of business. I could be responsible to develop and manage the P&L, manage teams and interact with vendors. I could develop and implement plans, drive out costs and develop schedules. I could also communicate the vision both verbally and in writing to ensue that everyone understands what needs to be done. Additionally, I could be involved with the development of new products.

To be more specific, I could see myself in of the following positions:
1. CEO/President
2. General Manager
3. Managing Director
4. VP of Business Unit
5. Head of Global Operations
6. VP of Product Management or Strategic Marketing
