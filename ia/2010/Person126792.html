Liz Kearns’s Summary

Did you know, how much MONEY you make in your business is directly related to how much you VALUE yourself? Over and over I see gifted business women struggle because of one thing: they just don't feel that good about themselves or how they look. So they hold themselves back from taking the actions they know would make a big difference to the bottom line of their business.

I struggled for years with my own self-image issues, and I've turned my life completely around. It's my passion to support others to do the same.
