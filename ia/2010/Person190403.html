Stephen Jenkins’s Summary

I've spent time in the technology and financial sectors of industry, and also in the world of higher education.

Whomever the client or employer, I have always brought a creative and innovate approach to problem-solving, specifically in the area of technology.

We are living in time where almost every process, method, and practice seen in business, industry, and education are being changed by the transformative nature of the Internet.
