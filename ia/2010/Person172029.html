Richard George’s Summary

After starting my career working for a leading regional advertising agency, I moved into public relations in 2005.

I'm experienced in the management of international public relations campaigns, coordination of global product launches and corporate reputation management for international clients.

While working with two of London’s leading PR agencies I gained client experience including LinkedIn, Visa, Qualcomm, Fox Mobile, Yahoo!, HTC, Glu Mobile, KCOM, Shazam, Amobee Media Systems, Russian mobile operator MTS and the GSMA.

In August 2009 I joined LinkedIn as European PR manager where I look after media and influencer relations across the region with particular focus on the UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain and the Netherlands.
