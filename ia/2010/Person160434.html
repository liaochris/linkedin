Paula Montes’s Summary

● I am a passionate architect, creative, thinker and tenacious. I strive to create architecture that touch the emotions and speak to the soul.

● I work as a Project Designer + Manager at Scholz Architects; architectural firm in Utah Valley. Prior to this, I gained six years of professional experience in Mexico working as a Project Designer for one of the best architectural firms.

● I run a blog called “Paula Montes Sketchbook”, which focuses on notes and thoughts of architecture and showcases my design work and portfolio.

● I am here to connect with architects and people that are passionate about what they do as I’m always looking for interesting opportunities in life.

● My professional goals: Gain an architect’s license in the state of Utah in order to set up my own studio with full architectural services and establish a branch in Mexico. Yes, I want to work in both countries.

● But more important: I love my profession, I have high expectations and nothing is impossible for me.
