Adams Hudson’s Summary

Adams Hudson is the president of Hudson, Ink Corp. His firm focuses on helping the in-home service contractor market more effectively with turn-key customer newsletters, custom copywriting, ad design and marketing strategy.

Adams has authored numerous marketing manuals for contractors (including the Marketing PowerPack series, Competitive Intelligence, Customer Retention Goldmine, and Yellow Page Lead Builder) and recently published his twelfth book, Contractor Marketing Secrets Your Competition Doesn’t Want You to Know. (First printing sold out; second printing doubled.)

Hudson Ink is the National Marketing Partner for several associations, including ACCA, PHCC, QSC, and Adams is a columnist for The News, P&M Magazine, Contractor Excellence plus undreds of other published articles and national appearances. He was recently offered a new automobile for winning The National Sales Letter Contest open to all 110,000 members, sponsored by Dan Kennedy, perhaps the top copywriter living today. (Hudson chose consulting/teaching from Mr. Kennedy instead of the automobile.)

Adams Hudson’s Specialties:

Adams' specialty is direct response advertising and copywriting. His ads and marketing materials help produce an astounding $330,000,000 in sales for contractors in one year. Although his fees are the most expensive in the contracting industry, it is this skill that keeps him most busy and has launched other marketing initiatives in contracting.
