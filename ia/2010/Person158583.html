Chris Lloyd’s Summary

Currently residing in Atlanta, but coming from the "great" city of Murfreesboro, Tennessee, there is one major field of work there: Restaurants. It is a field in which I have worked since 1998. My experience in that field ranges from serving tables, to working in the kitchen and back of house, to even running and managing. I pride myself on being a people person, and this industry seemed like a great way to use those skills in a professional field. I understand it is far from a more "professional" office job, but the food industry is still one of the largest fields because everyone eats, and not everyone eats at home any more!

As for now, I am focusing on the IT world, with a communications company. It is a great outlet for someone with my technical skill set, and look forward to furthering myself within it.
