Axel Roselius’s Summary

As a converging media pioneer, I’ve created innovative projects in interactive media for the last 15 years. My main expertise is in digital entertainment distribution with an emphasis on the youth and pop culture market. I conceived of and produced the first fully interactive television station, “Piazza Virtuale”, launched MTV’s first digital channel M2 Europe, and have developed pioneering interactive TV and 3D online worlds. I have managed large creative and business units, and have taught academic courses on the promises and pitfalls of new media strategies. My client list includes Universal Music Group, UCLA, MTV Europe, IDEO, Quincy Jones, Deutsche Telekom, NHK (Japan), UPN, PremiereWorld (PayTV Germany), the International Olympic Committee and Thomas Dolby’s Beatnik.
