David Levine’s Summary

David was Director of Marketing for AllSector Technology Group Inc., an I.T. company, with deep experience in delivering a wide range of information technology and application services to many companies, not-for-profit organizations, and government agencies. David’s responsibilities include Marketing, Sales Management, Strategic Alliance, Vendor Relations, and an executive management role.

Prior to AllSector, David was a principal advisor, consultant and certified business coach in his own firm representing ActionCOACH. In addition, David was an award-winning adjunct-Professor at Rutgers University School of Communications. He is also a managing partner of ShuaLife LLC, whose mission is to help children and adults create and maintain healthy, strong bodies and minds through fun fitness games, exercises and programs.

Previously, David was Director of Global Marketing & Communications for Silverline Technologies (NYSE:SLT) with responsibilities for corporate marketing activities, which included Advertising, PR, Strategic Alliances, and IR. Silverline provided IT software consulting and solutions in e-Business, CRM, Legacy Transformation, and Application Maintenance.

David also held Director of Marketing and Communications positions for VocalTec (Israeli company who invented and provided VoIP Solutions), Comcast Cellular One (a former cellphone company), and Micro Central (a former computer hardware and software distributor).

David also spent 11 years in the advertising industry in New York City. Prior to his career in advertising David spent 5 years in the financial arena.

David holds a BS (Banking/Finance & Marketing) and MBA (Marketing & Finance) from NYU. He has been an officer/board member of various industry, educational, and community service organizations. And has been involved in several new business startups, where he worked on business plans and strategic planning, as well as capital raises. David is a US Patent Holder and published author.
