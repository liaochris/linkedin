Jaya Gibson’s Summary

I head up 3 Lotus Media in London, where I combine my expertise in media campaigns with my passions film production and human rights advocacy. I have also directed sensitive political lobbying throughout the world and within the UN on China human rights issues. During the course of this work I have met privately with world leaders such as His Holiness the Dalai Lama to advise on advocacy campaigns.
After submitting a report to the UNHRC in Geneva, alleging political influence of the Chinese Communist Party on the Singaporean judiciary, my advocacy work credentials were verified when I was detained by the Singaporean government upon my return.

For the past 4 years I have been investigating the medical exploitation of Chinese prisoners. More recently I spent 3 months in Dharamsala, India, working directly with the Tibetan Government in exile’s security department interviewing ex-prisoner refugees.

I have been co-producer of three documentaries and films on Chinese human rights, including the multi award-winning feature “Sandstorm” and award-winning documentary “Plum Blossom In Snow.”

Previously, I combined my passion for film with my passion for extreme sports in New Zealand, forming my first production company to make 3 snowboard feature films; “Gifted”, “The Dream” and “Early Days”. I went on to produce and direct a 13-part documentary series on mountain-biking for National Geographic. At this time my company also developed a clothing fashion label and marketing services agency, producing youth market and freestyle culture campaigns for clients such as Vodafone, Export Gold and Boost Mobile. In the nineties, I cut my teeth in the London film industry creating key art and marketing campaigns for the likes of Artificial Eye, Merchant Ivory and the BBC as well as heading up major branding campaigns for Budweiser, Holsten Pils and Camel Trophy.

Throughout my career my goal has always been to work on creative projects that benefit and improve society.

Jaya Gibson’s Specialties:

Documentary production/project management, investigative journalism, video editing, animation/graphic design, branding strategy and identity creation, cross/social media promotions, marketing strategy.
