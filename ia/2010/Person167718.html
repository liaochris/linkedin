Hayley Raffill’s Summary

Hayley Raffill is an Account Manager working in the healthcare practice at Porter Novelli.

Hayley has worked in public relations for over seven years and in that time developed in depth experience of the healthcare and consumer affairs sectors.

Hayley has implemented and delivered of a wide portfolio of UK, Global and Pan-European PR & communications campaigns for ethical and consumer health clients, including Pfizer, Roche Pharmaceuticals, Roche Diagnostics, Reckitt Benckiser and Bausch & Lomb.

Hayley has implemented activities in various healthcare fields, such as diabetes, vaccines, cancer immunology, hepatitis C and renal anemia. On both a global and local level Hayley has worked with patient groups and key opinion leaders to implement, enhance and create disease awareness campaigns.

Hayley is based in London in the UK and has a Bachelor of Arts degree in European Business.

Hayley Raffill’s Specialties:

Awareness campaigns: professional relations; educational initiatives; press office management and developing relationships with national, regional and consumer press journalists; event management; and third party relations (charities and professional bodies).
