Mark Dollan’s Summary

An eagle-eye for design, branding & skeet...

Veteran artist Mark Dollan has been shooting aces for years. He’s got aim, attention, and experience. With an individualized approach and focus on communication, Mark has ensured that hundreds of businesses hit their targets.

Sure, his business has gotten a bunch of awards. But it’s not the awards he treasures – it’s the rewards. His true reward? Creating kick-a$$, impactful branding and communications for his clients.

When Mark isn’t shooting for the stars for his clients, he’s shooting trap and skeet, aiming high on the golf course, wrestling trout, and capturing the intense beauty of nature with digital photography.

Mark Dollan’s Specialties:

Creativity, design, websites, marketing, branding, photoretouching, innovation.
