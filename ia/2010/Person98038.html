Jill Konrath’s Summary

I'm the Chief Sales Officer & CEO of Selling to Big Companies. We help salespeople, entrepreneurs and business professionals sell to today's crazy busy buyers. We teach them how to crack into corporate accounts, speed up their sales cycle & win bigger contracts.

I'm a change agent. My job is to provide a wake-up call to sellers and sales organizations. I believe that new skills, strategies and tools are essential for success in this business environment.

I'm passionate about impacting as many people as I can. That's why I ...

WRITE — My 1st book, Selling to Big Companies, is a Fortune magazine "must read," Amazon Top 25 sales book for 4+ years & Gold Medalist at 2008 Sales Book Awards. SNAP Selling, my new book, will be out in May, 2010. I write a bi-monthly newsletter & have a popular blog.

SPEAK/TRAIN — I'm a frequent speaker at annual sales meetings, kick-off events, and professional conferences. You'll never hear me spouting the same old sales techniques that your salespeople have heard for years. Instead, I'll show them new ways to be successful. I do lots of webinars & teleseminars too. This enables my clients to train their salespeople without all the travel costs.

CREATE SALES TOOLS — To spread my expertise to those who need it, I create sales training programs that include audios, CDs, ebooks & coaching.

To bring me in to speak at your sales meeting or to learn more, email jill@sellingtobigcompanies.com
