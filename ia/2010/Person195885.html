Steven Zolman’s Summary

As the Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer of NET(net), Inc., Mr. Zolman is involved in every aspect of the business and is responsible for the overall operations of the company while maintaining a specific focus on building and maintaining executive level relationships with NET(net)'s clients and key technology supplier executives.

As the Founder of NET(net), Inc., Mr. Zolman pioneered the concept of IT Investment Optimization as a service after seeing the vast inequities in the commercial markets first hand. Mr. Zolman is a 15 year industry veteran and combines solid industry vision with a strong technology background to deliver exceptional leadership. Mr. Zolman has detailed work and management experience in some of the world's largest technology firms and brings that contract negotiations experience to the benefit of NET(net) clientele.

Mr. Zolman's personal efforts have helped create an organization that is focused on delivering incremental value to its clients at every level and exceeding expectations in every way. Mr. Zolman has been instrumental in acquiring and maintaining many of NET(net)'s key clients, as well as instilling a set of business values that allows NET(net) to enjoy outstanding client satisfaction ratings. Mr. Zolman's leadership and passion for excellence have helped drive and maintain NET(net)'s impressive growth and has earned NET(net) recognition as an "Industry Leader".

Prior to NET(net), Mr. Zolman's experience included executive management, consulting, corporate contract negotiations, and specialized programs focused on business development and strategic partnerships. Mr. Zolman brings a positive, candid and contagious attitude that, combined with business savvy, offers NET(net) clients an opportunity to leverage high-level supplier relationships and proprietary negotiating tactics and strategies.

Steven Zolman’s Specialties:

IT Investment Optimization Services, IT Supplier Lifecycle Management and Optimization Services. Complex Contract Negotiations with key technology suppliers. Software Licensing and Asset Management Optimization.
