Holly Hardwick’s Summary

As a designer who has been able to pursue her passions, work with some of the most incredible people and travel to some of the best places, I truly feel lucky! Those high flying and hard working days in LA and NY have allowed me the indulgence to find and settle my business at home near my family. The 21st century of e-commerce has assisted with that, too. My new goal is to reinvent Holly Hardwick, Queen of Crowns in the social media arena. Link me in!

Holly Hardwick’s Specialties:

Our exclusive online store specializes in crowns, crown jewelry and accessories. My forte is custom crown design and Production for events and individuals.
