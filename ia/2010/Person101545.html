John Argo’s Summary

Renewable energy and energy efficiency professional with 21 years industry experience. Photovoltaic expert with 11 patents pending. Track record of operations excellence. Experienced consultant and project manager with 17 years managing complex projects and infrastructure for two Fortune 100 companies. International experience. Founded three successful startups, two in the energy space. Highly creative with excellent analytical and communications skills. Holds MBA and PMP credential.
