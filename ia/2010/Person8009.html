Allen Peterson, CMC’s Summary

General profile: Certified Management Consultant (CMC), Certified Strategic Planning Facilitator, sales trainer, and advisor.

My consulting consists of strategic planning and marketing/sales assistance to professional/financial services and high tech firms. I am also expert in applied neuromarketing.

Founded founded Peterson Associates and joined Keiretsu Forum (now world's largest angel investment group) in 2002. Previously was president of Kewill ERP—a software company with 6,000 manufacturing customers. Career includes high tech and financial services industries with eleven years of general management, and experience in multiple start-ups, start-overs, and established large companies. Started my career at IBM Data Processing Division and held systems engineer, sales, and management positions during my eleven years there.
