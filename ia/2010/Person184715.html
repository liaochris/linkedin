Scott Pollov’s Summary

Scott Pollov is a results oriented professional with more than fifteen years of experience in planning, developing, growing, marketing, and financing entrepreneurial ventures, middle market companies, and commercial real estate and development projects as a consultant, capital advisor, board member, and investor. Primary focus is on bringing together a strong strategic business plan, with the most appropriate capital, connections, and resources to help companies catalyze growth and profitability.

Scott Pollov’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    BizPlanIt

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    November 1996 — Present (13 years 5 months)

    I am the Managing Partner of BizPlanIt, a management consulting and capital advisory firm founded in 1997. BizPlanIt assists clients through the process of planning, launching, financing, growing, and sustaining their business, from start-up to an exit or liquidity event. We work independently, as well as in partnership with leading management consulting and real estate services firms, to assist clients in:

    • Developing comprehensive business plans and financial projections for small, emerging, and mid-sized businesses to help them achieve their financing, strategic and growth objectives;

    • Preparing business plans, investment summaries and financial/cash flow projections for real estate investors, developers and owners seeking capital for new or existing projects, properties, or funds;

    • Facilitating introduction of venture and real estate clients to appropriate debt and equity funding sources, and assist throughout due diligence process;

    • Serving as executive or C-Level management on an interim basis for start-up and emerging growth companies.

    I am responsible for overseeing BizPlanIt's pre-transaction preparation and positioning services, including business planning services, strategic and growth planning, financial analysis and projections, market and competitive research, business model and value proposition development, investor presentation materials and coaching, advisory board recruitment and selection, as well as the identification and development of strategic, marketing, and technology partners. I have directly managed or supervised several hundred engagements for companies in nearly every industry and stage of development, with a particular emphasis on assisting small businesses, lower middle market companies, as well as firms throughout the real estate industry.
