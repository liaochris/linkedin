Christian Culpepper’s Summary

Tax preparation - 1040 (personal taxes), 1120 (corporations), 1120S (S-corporations), 1065 partnerships), 1041 (fiduciary & trusts).

Enjoy helping clients minimize taxes by meeting with clients before the tax year ends and preparing a tax return to determine tax liability and then by implementing tax-saving strategies (again before the tax year ends). This has resulted in tax savings as high as $75,000.

Also skilled in tax resolution as follows:
1040 clients who haven't filed past due taxes
1040 clients who need to negotiate amounts owed with the IRS
Business clients who have not paid delinquent 941 payroll taxes
Largest successful case yet - client owed $2.5million in back taxes and taxes were settled at $250,000 (or 10 cents on the dollar)
