    (Computer Software industry)

    With universal file and disc support, metadata, and a self-contained streaming architecture, Cinemo sports a modular design providing extensibility and platform specific optimization, Video and audio renderers are highly customised to take full advantage of the available hardware acceleration features of an embedded device.

    Cinemo’s media engine will represent the best in its class of embedded Atom™ multimedia when it's made available to OEM’s during the first half of 2009.
