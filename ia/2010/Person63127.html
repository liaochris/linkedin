Elena Ciccotelli’s Summary

An emerging entrepreneur with a BFA in dance and a national fitness training certification who launched TrainerDiva, Inc., an in-home personal training company in January 2009 in order to empower individuals with fitness training. Dedicated to bringing health and wellness to the forefront of both men's and women's lives by providing exceptional guidance, support and motivation. TrainerDiva, Inc. will continue to be the pioneer in in-home group and individual training, designed to efficiently instruct fitness programs in the comfort and privacy of the client's home.

TrainerDiva, Inc. continues to be actively involved with the American Diabetes Association through its participation in various educational workshops and awareness events. These events include World Diabetes Day Philadelphia held at Temple School of Podiatric Medicine and the Healthy Campus Initiative held at Temple University. During these events, TrainerDiva, Inc.'s primary function was to educate and promote physical fitness as a means to improve quality of life for those living with diabetes.

Through the development and practical implementation of social media marketing strategies for TrainerDiva, Inc., I have attained an extensive social media marketing background and knowledge base. Currently I am using this expertise to consult, strategize, and instruct other businesses to leverage major social networking platforms in order to increase sales and enhance client relationships. Specialties include customized analysis, formulation and implementation of a social media marketing strategy. This encompasses, but is not limited to the creation and maintenance of an effective Facebook and Twitter presence, optimal content creation and administration for blogs and websites, online reputation management, email marketing, social media convergence, and complete customization of relevant platforms such as YouTube and LinkedIn in order to achieve organic yet powerful online visibility.
