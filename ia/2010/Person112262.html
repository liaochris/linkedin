Katherine Mancuso’s Summary

I am an action researcher specializing in anthropology, disability studies, and technology. In the academic and nonprofit world, I specialize in translating research into real world results by writing grants to fund it, working in teams to create larger projects, using social media to publish results immediately and communicate it to a large audience, and conferences and other traditional media to disseminate it through traditional channels. In the for-profit world, my specialty is online community management & moderation, user experience, and social media strategy, particularly focusing on methods of engaging traditionally ignored populations such as people with disabilities.

Katherine Mancuso’s Specialties:

online community management, web content management, documentation writing, technical support, grassroots activism, media production, qualitative methods, anthropology, disability, virtual worlds, mixed reality, non-profit technology.
