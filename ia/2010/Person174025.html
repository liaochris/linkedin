Rebecca MacKinnon’s Summary

I have just moved to Washington DC where I will be a Schwartz Senior Fellow at the New America Foundation beginning on September 1st. I am working on a book about the future of freedom in the Internet age.

I am co-founder of Global Voices Online (http://www.globalvoicesonline.org), a global citizen media project. I continue to be involved with running GVO, and am a member of the Board of Directors.

In my twenties and early thirties I worked for CNN for about 12 years, based in China and Japan. I was Beijing Bureau Chief & Correspondent from 1998-2001 and Tokyo Bureau Chief & Correspondent from 2001-2003.

To find out more about what I'm thinking and working on see my personal weblog at www.Rconversation.com. A more formal bio, with full resume is there on the "About" page.

All of us had research projects and were expected to produce a research paper by the end of the semester. My project examined the future of journalism as it is being redefined by weblogs and other forms of internet-based, participatory journalism.
