Dirk Eschenbacher’s Summary

Dirk is an advertising creative director, designer and digital marketing expert based in Beijing, China. In his most recent job Dirk lead the creative product of Tribal DDB’s 16 offices across Asia Pacific, winning many high profile accounts and creating award-winning advertising campaigns across all media channels, for many top local and international brands in China and throughout Asia. With his help Tribal DDB was awarded Digital Agency of the Year 2009 in Asia Pacific.

Now Dirk embarked on his new mission, a PhD program at the Central Academy of Fine Arts in Beijing. Over the next three years he will work on China’s top creative school researching Chinese creativity, design and visual communications.

Dirk is a frequent speaker at industry events, judges many advertising and creative award shows, is partner in China’s leading venue for live music Yugong Yishan (www.yugongyishan.com), and rides his motorbikes whenever the Beijing air quality permits.
