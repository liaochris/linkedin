Jessica Killenberg Muzik, APR’s Summary

As vice president – account services, Jessica oversees a number of the firm’s accounts, as well as contributing to new business development efforts.

She is dedicated to helping clients build their brands, increase awareness, enhance return on investment (ROI) of their promotional dollars and ultimately enhance their revenue.

Jessica has managed several award-winning media relations campaigns during her 10+ years with the firm.
