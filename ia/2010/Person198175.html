Susan Young’s Summary

I work with businesses and nonprofits who want to increase their visibility, credibility and revenues by getting publicity.

I also work with professionals and college students who want to improve their communciation and self-confidence. These two traits are the cornerstones to all success.

I've been in the news, communications and PR fields for 25 years. I'm an award-winning radio news director, reporter and on-air anchor with ten years of experience in newsrooms in New Jersey & New York. I also served as Deputy Director of NJ Governor Christie Whitman's Office of Radio & TV, was a PR Director for a statewide nonprofit and now own two companies...Susan Young Media Relations, Inc. and Get in Front Communications.

I'm the author of Communicating with Confidence: Tips & Techniques for Powerful Business Communication. I also publish a monthly e-zine, The PR Buzz.

People hire me as their ghostwriter for wedding toasts, speeches and eulogies too!
