Halim Trujillo’s Summary

I am a marketing expert and owner of Creación Marketing Communications LLC. My advertising agency specializes in Hispanic marketing strategies and brand architecture. Creación comes to life to satisfy the industry’s need for true Hispanic expertise in the areas of Integrated Marketing Planning, Consumer & Prospect Definition and Creative Architecture. Creación is in charge of developing the necessary foundation for a flawless Hispanic marketing execution.

Through 15+ years of experience working in marketing and advertising with more than 50 global brands at Fortune 500 companies, I have been able to build a wealth of knowledge regarding Mexican consumers and Hispanic consumers in the U.S. that is unrivaled.

I have worked on the client side most of my career at global companies including Nestlé, Kellogg, Parmalat S.p.A and Domino's Pizza. On the agency side, I have worked for WPP and Omnicom agencies on clients such as Unilever, Kimberly-Clark, Wrigley, Mercedes-Benz, Sprint, BP, Kmart and Lowe’s, amongst others.

I have created breakthrough brand and media strategies across a wide range of product categories and global brands including the creation of a fully branded TV show in Mexico named “Está de Kellogg’s”.

In addition, in 2005 I was awarded with the AHAMP’s (Association of Hispanic Agency and Media Professionals) ‘2005 Media Director Best of the Best Award’ and beginning in 2004, I have been nominated for five consecutive years for the AHAA/Hispanicad.com’s ‘Media Planning Executive of the Year Award’. In 2007 I was one of the nominees for the AdColor Innovation Awards.

Halim Trujillo’s Specialties:

P&L Management, Brand Management, Corporate Negotiation, Acculturation and Language Preference, Market Intelligence, Consumer Insights, Account Planning, Media Planning/Buying and Content Creation.

Multicultural expertise including Hispanic, African American and Asian segments.

Multi-category experience including: CPG, Retail, Automotive, Telecomm, Financial Services, Liquor, Entertainment and Pharmaceutical.
