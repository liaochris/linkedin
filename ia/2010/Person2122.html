CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER (COO) & CHIEF ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER (CAO)
Orange County, CA and Greater LA Area

Seasoned, hands-on senior manager able to step into any company (including start-up or transitional companies) to provide strategic vision and clear-cut operational management. I have a proven track record of creating successful businesses by developing winning strategies, while managing and motivating individual contributors and entire companies. I have a strong belief in "roll-up-your-sleeves" involvement with employees and the daily happenings within the business.

I have over twenty years of experience in a wide variety of areas. I cut my teeth as a senior executive at well-known technology and retail companies, where I had the opportunity to learn from some of the best people in business.

• Back Office Functions including Finance (AP/AR/GL/Treasury), IT, HR, legal & PMO.
• Supply Chain, Production & Inventory Control Processes, MRP Tools & Analytics.
• Brand Equity Development through a variety of Customer Service & Warranty Support Programs.
• Relationships with US & Overseas Vendors, including Parts Suppliers, Manufacturers, Logistics Providers, Field & Technical Support Companies, & Technical Call Center Partners.

In addition to core business competencies, I also offer hard-earned relationships and contacts, including U.S. retailers, Asian manufacturers and key component suppliers, and outsource partners such as call centers, distribution centers, logistics providers, service centers, rebate houses, liquidators, etc.
