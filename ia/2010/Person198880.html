A Drop of Syrup .’s Summary

A Drop of Syrup is a creative design studio, based in Manchester UK. Taking a straight forward approach to visual communication, we specialise in graphic design, web design and development and marketing.

Our knowledge and passion for brand and print design, combined with our ability to develop unique ideas is becoming well known among the people we work with.
We are also adept at designing highly effective, modern and user friendly websites.

The work we produce is exciting and captivating, and our rates are extremely competitive. No matter what size of project you have, our aim is to turn your marketing budget into profit for your company.

Why Choose A Drop of Syrup?
We stick to the project. We are only happy to say a project is completed when our client is 100% satisfied with the final design and it achieves all of their goals.

A Drop of Syrup .’s Experience

  • Owner

    A Drop of Syrup

    (Graphic Design industry)

    June 2008 — Present (2 years )

    We are Spencer Turner and Jenny Woodworth, the co - founders of Manchester based graphic and web design studio, A Drop of Syrup. Together we formed A Drop of Syrup in the summer of 2008, after both graduating from university with a BA (Hons) in Graphic Design.

    The passion we share for design and the aesthetics of visual communication is the driving force behind Syrup. With backgrounds in fine art and graphic design we enjoy being able to bring our unique designs to life on paper and screen and watch brands develop.

    Meeting clients from around the world we've worked on a variety of projects ranging from brand identity and marketing to web site design and development.
