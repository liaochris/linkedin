Yuri Tello’s Summary

Professional manager (MBA) with over 15 years of international experience in the areas of finance, sales and training management in a large multinational company.

Fluent in English and Spanish, has lead training project implementations involving up to 12 countries located in South America, Africa and the Middle East.

Managed the government and utility sales team in Canada with sales of 8,000 vehicles annually and revenue of $240 million, exceeding the annual sales target by over 10% and becoming the top automaker in the segment for the first time in history.

Managed the financial services area of the Fleet Sales department including departmental budget preparation, forecast reporting, transaction based activities initiated by dealers, as well as business case development for marketing initiatives.
During this assignment implemented a policy change that reduced costs in the rental sales business by over $1 million a year for which was awarded an Ideas For Excellence Award by the President of GM Canada.

Has also participated in manufacturing multi-site system implementations in Canada, parts warehouse inventories and numerous special projects.
