Brent Harris’s Summary

I am a CPC, Certified Personnel Consultant, in my fifteenth year of staffing and recruiting experience. As a member of the GAPS Million Dollar Hall of Fame I have had proven success working with clients to help them find not just the right skills, but also the right person for their organization.

Accountants One has been a leader in Atlanta since 1973!

Accountants One, Inc. has been in business for 37 years – Focusing on matching the goals of our clients with those of our candidates. We are a true recruiting firm, primarily focusing on only the top 10% players in the Accounting / Finance industry.

I specialize in financial and accounting placement with a focus on CFO, Controller, Accounting Manager, and tax positions ranging from Director to Staff. I have extensive experience in helping build an entire accounting department from scratch to include accounts payable clerks through CFO. We are able to help with any temporary/contract, temp-to-hire or direct-hire needs you may have.

We have detailed job descriptions at www.accountantsone.com if you would like to see if we can be a resource to you or someone you know.
