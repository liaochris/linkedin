Brian Schuster’s Summary

My name is Brian Schuster and I am a Small Business Consultant. I run a business known as Clever Web Technologies, a consulting firm located in Chapel Hill, NC, specializing in social media marketing. I have been working with social media for the last six years, as well as starting five blogs. In addition, I have been working with small businesses all my life, starting with my father’s small business when I was 14. Since then, I have worked with executive recruiters, franchise consultants, individuals in the food industry, and private practice owners.

My staff and I made it our mission to understand the world and constraints of the small business owner and how social media can better connect them with their future clients.
