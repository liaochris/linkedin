Courtney Banks’s Summary

I am currently an undergraduate student at The Ohio State University studying Strategic Communications. I am learning about a million and one things as an Intern for Young University.

Why Do I Want to Work with Interns:

After my junior year I felt that I had an amazing resume and was ready to get an equally amazing internship. I got to the second round of interviews with MTV's Santa Monica office however did not receive an internship. I believe that if I had the right connections or opportunity to meet with the company in person I would have had a better chance as the majority of my competition were students in CA. From this experience I would love to help college students connect with the companies to further their chances of getting to the next level. Bottom line: Internship experience, good or bad, is invaluable for interns and businesses and I would like to show both students and companies how to maximize this value.

What Do I Want To Do After College?

I am currently learning as much as I can about entrepreneurship in hopes to own my own business in the near future. I am combining my passion for event planning with my love of developing the skills and talents of college students. I want to help businesses understand the value of interns and how to get the most out of their interns. On the flip side I would like to work with college students to develop their skills and become a valuable asset to any company. My goal is to write a book within the next year on the subject.
