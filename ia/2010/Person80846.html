Heather Loftiss’s Summary

Award-winning brand designer, marketing professional and communications expert.
• 2009 AMA Crystal Award winner for Branding/Rebranding; 2009 AMA Crystal Award finalist for Website; 2008 ADDY winner for Advertising for Arts and Sciences; 2008 ADDY winner for Out of Home Advertising.
• 2004 Featured by Seth Godin as one of the top companies that help make businesses remarkable in his book "Bull Market"
• Branding "Czar" for American Advertising Federation Houston

Visionary strategist known for bringing wacky ideas and crazy marketing strategies that yield results to corporate, B2B and consumer clients.
• Placement as lead designer on problematic annual report for a prestigious client was designed, approved and produced early and under budget. The client raved about how smooth the process was.
• Creative pitches secured new business accounts for clients resulting in >$500K in increased billings.
• Development of the Segway HT America branding campaign, Kids R Kids branding campaign, Clearpoint re-branding campaign, and Oxyde Chemicals re-branding campaign propelled each of these companies to number #1 in their market.

B2B clients include Hewlett-Packard, CB Richard Ellis, Segway HT America, CallWave, Scalable Software, Holliday Fenoglio Fowler, Mentor Corporation, and Baylor College of Medicine.

Consumer clients include Radiance Medspa, Kids R Kids, Glow Girl Cosmetics, One 30 Fitness, The Woodlands Children's Museum, and Salud Winery.

Projects include brand and marketing strategy, annual reports, corporate identity, brand books, sales guides, advertisements, publications, brochures, whitepapers, data sheets, sales presentations, packaging, direct mail, website design, and case studies.
