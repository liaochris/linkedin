David Snell’s Summary

Career focused entirely in high technology with an entrepreneurial management and sales and marketing vertical market focus. Mr. Snell began his career in systems engineering with IBM and evolved into solutions selling, sales and marketing team building, channel development, strategic and tactical planning and then executive management. His progression and development of skills and experience has included technology startups and Fortune 100 companies in situations from extraordinary expansion, to major re-engineering and rightsizing.

Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) from Queens University and active in the high technology industry having been asked to present at Conferences and Industry Association meetings such as:
- AIA Business Conference 2009 (San Diego);
- International Robots Vision & Motion Control Conference 2009 (Chicago);
- Acetech CEO Conference, and;
- BCTIA IMPACT Series.

A Provincially ranked squash player (played in the Canadian Nationals), family skier and avid boater. Expertise in multimedia communications, social and consumer networking and is an amateur videographer.

David Snell’s Specialties:

Executive management; change agent; strategic selling and training; target market, territory and sales development and management; channel and strategic partner relationships; online marketing. Operational experience includes: founding, expansion and sale of a vertical market software solutions company; leading Regional, National and International sales, services and marketing; and, the building and sale of two small high technology companies into worldwide leaders in their markets.
