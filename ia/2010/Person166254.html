Dax Kennedy’s Summary

PROforma Main Group is a full-service promotional product and commercial print agency with offices in Houston TX.

PROforma is North America's largest promotional product (ad specialty) distributor and the country's single largest commercial print broker,as well as one of the 5000 fastest growing private companies in the US in 2008.

PROforma is committed to bringing ideas, inspiration, strategy, and tools to its customers to help them grow their business and achieve success. Companies of all types and sizes rely on PROforma for professional solutions. From stationary, business cards, direct mail, collateral materials, signs and banners… to logo apparel, tradeshow giveaways, gifts and awards, PROforma offers its clients a one-stop shop for all of their marketing needs.
