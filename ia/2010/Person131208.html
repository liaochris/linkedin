Marc A. Dole’s Summary

Founder, CEO, and a bunch of creative stuff at Hatchling Studios, a multi award winning New England based Animation and Interactive studio serving corporate and entertainment clients since 1999.

Since starting in the industry, I have always pushed the merger of Computers, video, film, and interactivity. Hatchling is a convergence of these skills and of talented artists and technicians that bring the projects to life.

Producer - "The Toll" - CGI Animated short mockumentary interview with a bridge troll
Winner of 9 Animation awards and 1 Comedy Award

Producer - "Endurance Challenge" - Webisode series starring voice actor Billy West

Marc A. Dole’s Specialties:

Producing, Animation, Visual Effects, Digital video conversions, Film and TV budgeting.
Final Cut Pro, After Effects, Flash, Premiere, Digital Fusion, Softimage XSI, Lightwave 3D. Final Draft, Word, Excel and Tetris
