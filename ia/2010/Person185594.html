Sean P. Stiver’s Summary

I graduated from Texas Tech University School of Law and began a career with the prestigious law firm of Haynes and Boone, LLP. While with Haynes and Boone, I provided representation and drafted various documents for companies in regard to mergers and stock/asset acquisitions. I also collaborated with multiple companies while conducting venture capital financing, mergers and acquisitions and advised organizations on initial and subsequent rounds of venture capital financing, conversion of promissory notes, and corporate financing. I also authored and filed Regulation D private placement notification filings and provided coordination with state securities regulatory agencies. My background is wide-ranging with a record of increasing responsibility, innovation and accomplishment in providing efficient and effective solutions. My exceptional communication skills lead to excellence in building and cultivating strong client relationships. I also bring extensive skills in researching complicated issues, performing efficient due diligence, problem solving and financial statement analysis. I am a compassionate, entrepreneurial individual with great networking ability. Upon leaving Haynes and Boone, I opened my own firm, The Stiver Law Firm, and specialize in general corporate law, family law, and estate planning (wills and trust).
