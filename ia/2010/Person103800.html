Jonathan Foxx’s Summary

Over 25 years in the financial sector, experienced in all areas of mortgage banking, mortgage and lending compliance, bank residential lending compliance, statutory licensing, federal and state regulatory compliance, and guidance in credit policy.

Developed the CORE Compliance Matrix, the industry standard and well-respected risk management profile that evaluates company's Compliance Program, Organizational Structure, Regulatory Risk, and Enforcement Strategies.

Previous positions include: Chief Compliance Officer; EVP/Compliance; President; CEO; Executive VP; Director/Government Lending; Director/Mortgage Risk Management; Director/Operations & Underwriting.

Jonathan Foxx’s Specialties:

Mortgage compliance services to the financial services industry.
