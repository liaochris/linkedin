    Brinson Patrick Securities Corporation is an investment banking boutique serving the equity capital raising needs of publicly traded companies, from small cap to Fortune 500 for over a decade.
    Leveraging our DOCS® (Dynamic Offering of Common Stock) financing facility, we have worked with companies of various sizes and stages of growth to raise capital when and how they need it. We are a broker-dealer, registered with FINRA and the SEC.
    The DOCS® financing facility low-profile equity offering is appropriate for publicly traded companies across multiple industries. It has been successfully utilized by companies in the energy, life science, technology, real estate, financial services and hospitality industries.
    Through our DOCS® financing facility, Brinson Patrick enables issuers to efficiently raise capital over time as they need it, with little or no negative market impact, whether larger or smaller amounts of capital need to be raised.
