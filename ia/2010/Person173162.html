Rick Koh’s Summary

We reinvented ourselves in October of 2008, just in time for the economic downturn. We closed our Central Avenue location after 40 years and merged with our White Plains location at 50 Main Street where we have operated Copy Stop for over 20 years.

With a larger space we reinvested in new and more efficient equipment and expanded our capabilities. Being in the same location as my pre-press computer guru and boyhood friend Ken Kaufman is an added bonus both for myself and our customers.

We are now operating more efficiently than ever.
Business has been growing due to our exceptional customer service, reasonable prices, attention to quality and most of all customer loyalty.

I am a graduate of Syracuse University's Newhouse School of Communications with a BA in Public Relations and the Maxwell School of Citizenship with a BS in Political Science. I have worked for the family business since I was 15 years old.

I am currently interested in reacquainting with old friends and customers. Of course we would always appreciate additional clients to help fill our expanded productivity.

Thank you to the many clients who have written recommendations and sent referrals.

Rick Koh’s Specialties:

In business since 1960, are experts in all forms of 1 to 4 color offset printing and high speed copying with a specialty in litigation copying.
However, our true specialty is working closely with corporate or non-profit organizations to make sure you are efficiently spending your printing dollars.
We also have an amazing resource in Ken Kaufman who can professionally help with any pre-press or file issues which is the most important part of a successful print project.
