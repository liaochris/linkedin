Candace Kaller’s Experience

  • Owner/Curator

    Kaller Fine Arts

    (Fine Art industry)

    January 2006 — Present (4 years 10 months)

    Kaller Fine Arts is a private gallery and art consulting service dedicated to providing affordable, high quality artwork to individuals and businesses. Candace Kaller brings to KFA over ten years of experience as a museum educator at the National Gallery of Art and the National Museum for Women in the Arts, as well as prior ownership of the Galerie de Tours in San Fransisco. Kaller Fine Arts' philosophy is that strong art enhances all environments, providing inspiration and visual interest to our surroundings.
