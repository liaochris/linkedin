    BASIS for ALL

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2010 — Present (5 months)

    We believe in Change! BASIS can change this country.We are committed to establish Digital or e- Bangladesh.

    We need to implement following Initiatives:
    E-Government Initiative:The Impact of E-Government or Digital Bangladesh Initiatives will help us to Reduction of Poverty and it will also help us to achieve Millennium Development Goals(MDG).In this link,Government should allocate 5%(Five) percentage of annual budget for computerization of government and semi-government offices.

    Domestic Market Initiative: It provides the platform to link the non IT sector with the IT industry and to develop a vision for sustainable growth of the domestic IT market.

    Innovation Initiative: It seeks to address the specific needs of the large, medium and small companies and create a conducive ecosystem in the country.

    Education Initiative: Under this initiative, BASIS aims to enhance graduate employability through appropriate skill enrichment and act as bridge between industry, academia and government.
    Women in Leadership-IT Initiative: Government & BASIS have to launched the Women in Leadership-IT Initiative to enhance participation of women into the workforce and create more women leaders in the IT-BPO industry.

    Security Initiative: Aim towards creating an enabling environment in the country for information security and compliance through awareness and conducive legal framework.
