Cara Stewart’s Summary

Remarx Media helps the companies and people driving the Innovation Economy make their mark. Rabble-rousers. Disruptors. Believers. Truth-tellers. Our clients are pioneering radical changes to products, processes and services. Their innovations improve the global economy, make supply chains run faster, amp up technology applications and create government efficiencies.

They share one reason for being: Propulsion. They have an unyielding, sometimes unspoken, motivation to solve a problem (and the cheeky audacity to believe they have the solution).

Remarx Media rings true for theses bellwethers of the Innovation Economy. We help turn entrepreneurs into titans, risk-takers into winners and perception-changers into policy-makers. We call it Propulsion PR, or simply PRopulusion™. Fit for the Innovation Economy, we solve communication problems.

Cara Stewart’s Specialties:

Our portfolio includes go-to-market strategic plans, competitive assessments, brand messaging, financial communications, channel and sales communications, media relations, enterprise PR, social media, analyst relations and communications training for emerging-through-midmarket business-to-business and technology companies.
