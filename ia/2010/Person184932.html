Scott Van Kerkhove’s Summary

Scott Van Kerkhove is the Founder and President of The Energy Group, LLC and Co-Founder of PowerMyCampus™.

The Energy Group, a leading systems integrator in sustainable, renewable & energy efficiency solutions, is accelerating Conscience Capitalism through the deployment of low-carbon Integrated Energy Solutions.

One of The Energy Group's primary programs, PowerMyCampus™ (www.PowerMyCampus), works with not-for-profit organizations across the nation to deliver the benefits of clean, low-cost energy without out-of-pocket capital costs, technical risks or ongoing maintenance responsibilities.

By helping clients to prioritize, monetize, fund, and implement a broad range of energy projects that reduce dependency on fossil fuels, The Energy Group promotes Conscience Capitalism to insure Triple-Bottom-Line outcomes for its’ clients, supporting People-Planet-Profits.

At The Energy Group, Mr. Van Kerkhove utilizes his construction experience, a background in building science, and his passion for energy efficiency/sustainability to service his clients effectively. He has been involved with the design and construction of over 300 sustainable building projects.

Mr. Van Kerkhove has served in the construction industry since 2004, where he founded and led a multi-trade contracting firm specializing in insulation and drywall services across the Gulf Coast of the U.S. He was instrumental in providing post-Katrina construction services on over 250 projects during his tenure.

In 2004, Mr. Van Kerkhove ended a 19-year career managing and consulting in the health care field. While he served as a CEO in operations that included hospitals, surgery centers, and public health programs.

His proudest accomplishments in health care include his work with state agencies in support of the disabled community. As a Veteran of the U.S. Navy and the Gulf War, Mr. Van Kerkhove proudly served his country and was widely recognized for his leadership and team-based approach.
