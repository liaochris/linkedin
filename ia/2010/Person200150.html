Taylor Dodson, EI’s Summary

Having spent over 5 years in the Power Substation industry, I have supported over 50 different Air Insulated Substation (AIS) and Gas Insulated Substation (GIS) projects. Additionally, I have worked on many and recently led engineering proposal efforts. My primary goal has been to develop task automation, design tools, and templates to speed up the process/aid others. In addition to developing SCADA, RTU, and protection relay schematics, physical layout and elevation drawings, I have designed, engineered, programmed, and tested an enterprise software solution to aid in the marketing/sales of turn-key substations. Additionally I have Microsoft Project experience including: engineering hours estimation, schedule creation, and schedule modifications. I perform QA/QC evaluation of project deliverables and help ensure tasks are completed on time.
