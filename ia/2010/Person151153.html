Nancy Alvarez-Hernandez’s Summary

I am a Business Development Specialist working for the U.S. Small Business Administration, North Florida District Office in Jacksonville, Florida.

I has been serving the small business communities of 43 counties in North Florida since my transfer to the SBA in March 1997. I am responsible for providing financial, management and technical assistance.

I have the responsibility for the administration of the 8(a) Business Development Program to over 50 participating firms in Duval, Nassau and Clay Counties. I ensures and enforces 8(a) program compliance and provides information, counseling and training in all aspects of the 8(a) Program including assistance in marketing and development and implementation of strategic techniques for contracting with the Federal government and prime contractors.

I started my Federal career in 1990 under Department of Defense, where I held several clerical and administrative positions at Human Resources, Guantanamo Gay, Cuba; Family Practice, Naval Air Station Hospital, Jacksonville; and Personnel Support Detachment, Mayport Naval Station while pursuing part-time a higher education.

I graduated from Jacksonville University with a Bachelor degree in Business Administration (Magna Cum Laude). I am a member of Phi Beta Kappa Honor Society and serve on the Mayor’s Hispanic American Advisory Board and the Beaver Street Enterprise Board. I also served on the Mayor's Small & Emerging Business Advisory Council.

My long-term goals is to complete a doctorate degree in Business Administration with a minor in Acquisition Management.
