Stephen D. Hall, PMP { LION }’s Summary

How may I serve you?

My passion is helping others. This includes individuals as well as organizations through personal development, quality improvement and problem solving, most often in high tech environments. I'm a published author and enjoy public speaking. I've given a motivational speech on live television.

I've been blessed with skills in Project Management and Software Quality Assurance, and have an extensive technical background, strong leadership abilities, and superior team management skills. With experience managing multiple, international projects from eLearning to Manufacturing, I am sensitive to cultural differences, and can provide quality improvement for clients through testing, proven project management and quality management techniques. Let's challenge the status quo to improve processes and customer service. Experienced in numerous industries, and adapting easily to the language and culture of the work environment, I produce quality results through consulting, training, coaching, public speaking, and writing.

I'm an avid follower of the success industry. My role models include Jim Rohn, Brian Tracy, Tony Robbins, Chris Widener, Bob Proctor, Connie Podesta, Denis Waitley, and Og Mandino.

My favorite quote is from Zig Ziglar, "If you can dream it, then you can achieve it. You will get all you want in life if you help enough other people get what they want."
