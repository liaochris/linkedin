Adriel Hampton’s Summary

Adriel Hampton is a journalist, Gov 2.0 and new media strategist, public servant, and licensed private investigator. Charismatic, driven leader with an eye for detail and the insight to envision and shape the future. Cool under pressure, a tenacious and effective problem solver. Excels at building organizationally beneficial relationships at all levels. Results-oriented analyst, Cal Berkeley grad, award-winning journalist and news commentator. 2009 Democratic primary Congressional Candidate in California's 10th District.
Daly City Councilman David Canepa, former aide to State Senate Asst. President Pro Tem Leland Yee, says, “His knowledge of the inner workings of ... government are of tremendous value to any organization.” Steve Ressler, founder of GovLoop, the “Facebook for Government,” calls him a “genius govie.”

Adriel Hampton’s Specialties:

White-collar investigation, legal analysis and research, community building, Web 2.0 and social media publishing, traditional and new media strategy, and Gov 2.0.
