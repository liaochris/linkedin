Mike Paradis’s Summary

Overview:
My career has been dedicated to creating the models, tools and best practices leadership teams need to confidently manage revenue and growth. By facilitating the deployment of customer-driven growth strategies through capability based process, organizational change management and efficient CRM technology, my organizations provide leadership teams the structure and clarity they need to dominate in their respective markets.

Intelectual Property:
During the past 10 years, my team and I have designed, tested and patented the Customer Alignment Maturity Model and Index (CAMMI). In addition, we developed and patented the online diagnostic environment which dynamically captures and analyzes an organization's comprehensive capability maturity within hours online, not weeks of subjective interviews. To date, over 100 organizations have leveraged the model.

Background:
I left GE Capital/PSINet in 2001 to found BenNevis with members of the Cargill founding family. The late, W. Duncan Macmillan believed in the CAMMI model early, recognizing how it would become a proven standard for leadership teams to measure and manage their growth strategies. After Duncan passed away in 2006, we made the decision to transform BenNevis into the organizations you see today.

In 2006, I launched Demand Chain Systems Inc, leveraging existing resources and expertise, to provide additional focus to the enabling CRM technology organizations need for growth.

Finally, in 2009, with the support of key investors, founded CAMMI Logic LLC to deliver the full value of the CAMMI Model and Framework. The vision of the organization is to facilitate a systematic approach to managing revenue and growth, similar to how budgeting and costs are managed today.
