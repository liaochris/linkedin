Anders Bolin’s Summary

Documentary film producer, freelancer in media content production and publishing. Co-founded independent a Swedish label for documentary expressions and urban artforms in 2005, UZI.

As a producer and publisher, I have since 2005 released and distributed three feature documentaries for release on DVD, as well as minor research projects in the mediasphere, often in an international context.

As a freelancer, I have co-produced, assisted and edited informational film projects, music videos, authored DVD productions, trailers and more.

I am looking to work in projects where it is possible to develop and push the limits within the documentary narrative.

Anders Bolin’s Specialties:
