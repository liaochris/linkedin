Grayson Daughters’s Summary

Do you want your your company in "the media?" Do you want your crisis communications to work in times of disaster?

Or how about creating timely, interesting content that IS the media now?

Are you aware of the emerging real-time web? (Just ask me!)

Keep in mind "the media," and our definition of it, are changing before our very eyes. You can be part of that change.

Whether in traditional media or otherwise, I can make it happen - with a combination of inside-the-industry experience, networking, ability to target, and the most cutting-edge online communications and social network savvy.

As I say... I know what to with media nowadays.

There simply isn't another Atlanta-based media consulting company out there with a stronger combination of old and new media expertise.

Grayson Daughters’s Specialties:

I am a journalist/content developer turned media consultant. I specialize in real-time crisis communications for disaster relief. I bring with me over 20-years of network and corporate experience developing video, print, broadcast and Internet communications.

I am HTML and techno-literate, and I am an early-adopter of most tools, apps and platforms that further new communications trends. I shoot DV and edit.
