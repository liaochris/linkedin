Ingo Elfering’s Summary

I combine the experience of managing a variety of IT Capabilities in a global and cross-functional customer environment within a Fortune 150 Company with the business experience of founding and running a successful entrepreneurial business.

I'm an Innovator and Change Agent with broad and deep IT Knowledge paired with an understanding of the Pharma and broader Healthcare Industry globally, having created and executed both Business and IT Service Innovations.
