Mark Mueller’s Summary

Mark Mueller is a Global Executive Director and Certified Health Coach with Take Shape For Life a division of Medifast (NYSE "MED"). The company's mission is to create Optimal Health for every person in America. Sound's like a pretty big undertaking, doesn't it? Well it is...but we have the Company, the Products and the People to do it.

At Take Shape For Life we have the opportunity to create the life you have always imagined. We provide free personalized health coaching and support, to help our clients quickly and safely reach their ideal healthy weight and keep it off for life. Along the way, we teach them the Habits of Health to maintain a healthy weight and lifestyle to lead a more fulfilling and productive life with optimal health. We help others achieve health as health coaches and mentors.

Our company supports it's growing team of health coaches empowering them to have a life of personal and financial freedom built from helping others find health in their lives.

We teach our clients and health coaches the Trilogy of Optimal Health - Healthy Body, Healthy Mind & Healthy Finances.

    (Public Company; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    September 2006 — Present (4 years 2 months)

    There are few arenas in America today where it is possible to make a greater, more meaningful impact than in the health and wellness of our country. Obesity has reached epidemic proportions in the United States. 2/3 of adult Americans are now overweight or obese. Excess weight is a direct contributor to diseases including diabetes, heart disease, arthritis, liver disease and even certain types of cancers. Obesity leads to symptoms of metabolic syndrome including elevated blood pressure, high cholesterol, insulin resistance, inflammation, blood fat disorders and arterial plaque. The principal role of the current medical system is to treat diseases rather prevent them, promoting costly "sick care" vs. "health care", relying on medications, surgeries and expensive procedures to cure symptoms. The solution to our national health crisis is prevention, education and personal empowerment...that has become our mission and passion.

    We offer health coaching and mentoring to our clients, which include medical practices, corporate wellness managers, fitness centers, wellness providers as well as private individuals seeking to reach healthy weight and lifestyle goals for themselves, their patients and their employees. We utilizing a very simple, cost neutral and effective meal replacement program, coupled with close coaching support, to allow them to reach their ideal weight safely and rapidly and then teach them how to maintain that healthy weight and develop new habits of health for a longer life. We have created an opportunity to make a significant, positive impact on the health care system in this country and state of health in America.

    We also sponsor others into our business as Health Coaches and have created a bio-network of professional "triliologists" who teach and coach the ideas of a Health Body, a Health Mind and Healthy Finances.
