Roque Rodon’s Summary

To inspire, motivate and create the highest quality designs and media is my goal. My expertise in communications spans 13 years. I have been Art Director at numerous companies including Creative Marketing Impact and Students of America, all in Palm Beach County, Florida. Currently, I run my own design firm for companies in need of web work, online presence or any other artistic need.

I have worked on a wide range of jobs such as children book illustrations by authors like the well-known Peggy Marshall, have created billboards and album art for music bands such as Korn and the Wu-Tang Clan, and have been recognized on the Oprah Winfrey show for my interior mural work. In 1995, I gained national recognition as one of the only artists, in Florida, to score a perfect 5 on my panel judged, Scholastic portfolio.

With all honesty, my goal is to treat every job as I would treat something I am creating for myself; With pride and integrity. I strive to make sure every client's project is treated with total care, craftsmanship and respect. Making sure it reaches its intended audience with sufficient impact and presence.

Roque Rodon’s Specialties:

Web/Graphic design, Flash media, architectural renderings, photo doctoring, fine art & murals, character conceptualization, cartoons & practically anything artistic.

Branding and brand development, marketing, pricing, task management, corporate identities & creative consultation.
