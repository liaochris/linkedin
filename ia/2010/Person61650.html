David Curiel’s Summary

Currently doing a stint as a translator and writer, I am a sales professional with ten years of sales experience—including six years in multiple segments of the wine industry, an MBA in small-business marketing and strategy from a top 20 program, management experience and a passion for wine and doing good.

I am looking to sell wine again while envisioning ways to change the world. Or to raise funds for world changers while sipping an occasional glass. Those who value the full nourishment — physical and emotional — that a good meal made from sustainable whole ingredients fairly gotten can to bring a person and a community will always find a partner and a friend in me.

I have vastly traveled the world and am enjoying life immensely. I hope to leave the world a better place than I found it—for my children and for those of others.
