Spencer Spellman’s Summary

Committed continually to challenging myself and exceeding my expectations, my experience has varied over the years. After graduating from Western Carolina University with a bachelor's degree in Philosophy, I began freelance writing. My work has included writing articles for travel publications, current affair magazines, entertainment magazines, tutorial websites and blogs. As a copywriter, my experience has involved writing SEO content for retailers, realtors and major hotel chains, as well as doing everything from doing the keyword and niche research to writing the content to recoding pages and uploading the content.

As the Interactive Content Editor for 10Best, my work caused me to wear many different hats. It included writing and editing content for hotel websites and writing travel guide content and articles for http://www.10Best.com. I helped pioneer 10Best's social media and online presence, by overseeing the development of mobile and Facebook applications and creating and maintaining 10Best's social media presence, using Facebook, Twitter and Stumbleupon.

I've since moved on from 10Best, with a focus on travel journalism. I contribute to multiple print and digital travel publications, while you can regularly see my work on Uptake.com and NileGuide.com. I recently created a website for the Carolinas, called Carolina Nomad (http://www.carolinanomad.com), which offers insider tips, industry news and travel reviews on North Carolina and South Carolina.

Feel free to contact me about questions or to inquire about business opportunities. I look forward to connecting.
