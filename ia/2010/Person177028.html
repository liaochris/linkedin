Ron Borland’s Summary

I am a process mangement consultant, specializing in internal communications as well as a motivational speaker and storyteller. I have earned Advanced Communicator Silver and Advanced Leader Bronze designations from Toastmasters International, and have presented at several international symposia. I now serve as president of my home Toastmasters club and am Area Governor for District 40, Central Division, Area 12. I am a certified Project Management Professional, with a background in information technology, specifically in the fields of data design, data quality, and metadata management.
