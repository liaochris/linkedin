Emilia Doerr’s Summary

Emilia Doerr is a Client Treatment Specialist at Web Marketing Therapy, an online educational resource that helps businesses and individuals restore their marketing health to achieve maximum wealth from the web. Emilia is passionate about helping individuals, companies and organizations hone their own unique narratives so that they can tell and sell their compelling stories on the web.

As an account manager with Lorrie Thomas Web Marketing, a California-based agency that helps make small to mid-size businesses big with the web, she provides advisory, management and execution for results-driven strategies that marry that art and science of web marketing.

With a background in journalism, Ms. Doerr is passionate about using web marketing channels to help businesses tell and sell compelling stories. Her expertise includes online public relations, blog strategy, web site optimization, and social media marketing.

A graduate of UCLA, Ms. Doerr has worked in performing arts and health care marketing, as well as in editorial departments at Entertainment Tonight Online and various national consumer magazines including The Robb Report and People.
