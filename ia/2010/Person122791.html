Michael Pines’s Summary

Since 1992, I have been working to help people recover the maximum financial compensation for their personal injuries. The legal process can be very confusing, but with my experience, your claim will be made as easily and with as little hassles as possible.

I have worked for the insurance companies before, so I know how they think. You shouldn't have to worry about fighting insurance companies or even recovering what you lost after your injury.
