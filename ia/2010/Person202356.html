Theresa Geyer CRS, GRI, AHWD, SFR, NRBA’s Summary

My mission is to provide Buyers and Sellers with all of my real estate knowledge to allow you to make the most logical decision about your largest investment. I stand behind my promise of "Not just selling homes....selling SATISFACTION!" This promise means that I will provide as a Top Producing agent a superior level of educated, informed and professional real estate services to Buyers and Sellers. More information can always be obtained through my website: www.BuyingDaytonaBeach.com

Sellers - Planning to sell your home in the near future? I offer a complimentary service to help you determine your home's current market value. Each home is unique and must be approached with different techniques which have proven to be successful for selling homes in different price ranges. A different marketing approach is required for each home and my personalized approach works!

Buyers - I know you're busy...with better things to do than look at dozens and dozens of houses...most of which are of no interest to you at all. That's why I am personally familiar with every fine home on the market...so that I can quickly match your needs and desires to houses that are available. I can keep you informed everyday with a personalized email of all new listings that meet your criteria through a search of the local Multiple Listing Service.
