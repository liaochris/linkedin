Nam Huynh’s Summary

While working toward my BS in Economics degree at the University of Houston, I was employed part-time as a Campus Marketing Rep for Apple Computers. I increased awareness about Apple products at this position on campus via face-to-face contact with students and faculty.

After graduating in the fall of 2006, I was employed by Epic Merchant Energy as a Virtual Trading Analyst. I quickly trained to become a very successful and profitable trader in the Midwest ISO power market dealing with the day ahead products (virtuals). At Epic, I was trained in the analysis of weather, forward electricity load forecasting, and congestion. Using this information, I was successful in employing profitable strategies and trades in virtuals, FTR and ICE trading.

I wish to expand my knowledge of the energy markets by bringing my economics background, analytical skill set, and my thirst for new information. I am willing to take on new responsibilities and have a strong desire to tackle any new challenges in the work environment.

I am currently enrolled at Texas Southern University in the Executive MBA program with an energy finance focus. The expected graduation date is in the summer of 2011.
