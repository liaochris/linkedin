    (Sole Proprietorship; Graphic Design industry)

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 7 months)

    Rick Osborn is a Lifestyle, Wellness & Solutions Coach who specializes in issues specifically pertinent to men and women in today’s culture – particularly to executives who are in midlife. Having worked with countless people over the years, he brings his experience, spiritual insight and practical wisdom to the table with a simple and informal, yet effective and empathetic coaching style.

    Much of Rick’s wellness coaching deals with issues such as: weight loss, weight gain, proper nutrition, exercise, stress management, self-awareness, self discipine, motivation, accountability, lifestyle management, boundaries, and spirituality.

    Rick holds a liberal arts degree (B.A) in Spanish and English from Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia and has a Master of Divinity (M.Div.) from Southeastern Seminary in Wake Forest, North Carolina.

    Paralleled with his coaching, ministry and self-education, Rick has a background in art and design, language, music, ministry and writing. Furthermore, he is a published writer and has contributed to books and programs on nutrition, weight loss and fitness. He is an accomplished singer and musician and spends much of his free time doing itinerate music ministry and speaking. The remainder of his time consists of consulting in areas of blogging, freelance marketing, exercise, graphic design, web design, podcasting and recording projects.

    His mission as a lifestyle and wellness coach is to reach out to individuals and help them understand and achieve proper life balance and management through self-awareness, motivation, accountability and inspiration for a healthy, successful, happy and fulfilling existence. He hopes that all who read his blogs, articles and ebooks will find peace, wisdom, inspiration, and resolution for the difficulties that arise in the course of one’s life.
