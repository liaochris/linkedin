Margaret Ryan’s Summary

I connect consumers and retail brands through traditional and digital marketing and online influencer programs. Over my 15 year career I’ve helped companies like America Online, BabyCenter, LimeLife, Olson Ogden Wines, Polyvore, ShopStyle, Tickle, MyShape and Zappos build their brands, increase web traffic, drive sales and engage the audiences critical to their success.

I was part of AOL's first communications team. I managed marketing and public relations programs that were instrumental in introducing AOL to the media and public as one of the world's most recognized brands and helped grow the company from 300,000 to 30 million members in five years.
