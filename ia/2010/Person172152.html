Richard Wilson’s Summary

I am a hedge fund consultant working within the areas of capital raising, prime brokerage and speaking/writing.

I run the Hedge Fund Group (HFG), HedgeFundBlogger.com and CHP Designation Program, a hedge fund training and certification program. You may learn more about my business at http://HedgeFundConsultingGroup.com.
