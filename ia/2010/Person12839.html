Andy Wiebe’s Summary

My goal is to be creative, serve my client well, provide for my family, and be self-employed. As a filmmaker I believe the power of story and emotion best drive effective video communications.

For almost a decade I have served a variety of corporate and non-profit clients nationwide in producing broadcast, web, and DVD based videos. My daily tasks in serving my clients include: contract writing, scripting, scheduling, crew/talent management, directing, shooting, post-production supervision, mastering and final delivery.
