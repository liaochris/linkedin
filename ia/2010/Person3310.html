Adrian Constantin’s Summary

Adrian is a businessman/entrepreneur with over 4 years of experience in internet/online businesses. Over 4 million people use the services created and owned by Adrian’s companies each month. Almost 100 million people have used these services in the past.


In 2010 Adrian created AdVance NGO, the non-profit arm of his activities in Romania. The purpose of AdVance is to implement social projects aimed at two at-risk categories: children and elders.

In 2009, Adrian established InteraktMedia, a B2C company that currently offers internet-services ranging from business intelligence analytics to high end entertainment.

From 2007 to 2008, Adrian was a partner at ADVMG LLC. He was responsible with the operations of both the affiliate marketing arm of the company as well as product development. The company offered high tech IPTV/webTV services, computer learning software and online lessons teaching users vital computer skills.

In 2006, Adrian has founded FP Entertainment, a web entertainment/information network. Over 50 million people have used services that involve legal information, news and commentary and digital content applications.

In 2005, Adrian acted as Director of Public Relations for RResearch, a Hawaii registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated at helping people with inflammatory conditions.

Adrian holds a Bachelor of Science degree from the prestigious Polytechnic University of Bucharest in Electronics, Telecommunications and IT with a specialty in Computers and Information Technology. He also currently studies Law. He is a Certified Cisco Engineer and a member of the IEEE.

During high school, Adrian ranked in different competitions among the top 10 programmers in Romania. He also holds various awards in mathematics and physics. He has received awards for merit from different IT organizations, including from Romania’s minister of Education. He is fluent in Romanian and English and has basic knowledge of French, Italian and Spanish.
