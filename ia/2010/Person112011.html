Kathleen Rhodes’s Summary

For over 14 years I have worked successfully in the direct sales/network marketing industry. Although I enjoy building my own business(es), I specialize in working with other home based entrepreneurs, empowering them to succeed, too. I am a big believer in the creative use of innovative tools, especially online ways to connect with customers, team members, and potential clients.

I focus on three areas:

1. Promoting innovative, "level playing field" network marketing companies and practices.

2. Using an easy, effective and inexpensive way to stay in touch with people who matter to me (you can do it too): send real greeting cards through the mail - http://www.ConnectionCards.com - let someone else print, address, stamp and mail your cards and postcards for you! It's a great way to send birthday and anniversary cards to those you love, but it's also a fantastic tool for attracting and retaining customers! This is an excellent way to do "offline" marketing to complement your online activity.

3. Promoting products and services for others who have a home based business. I write my "musings" and evaluate products and services that can assist home business entrepreneurs. Visit my blog at http://www.APerfectHomeBiz.com . Personally, I have learned a great deal about online marketing from http://www.renegade.marketingmerge.com - although this site is targeted to network marketers, it is a phenomenal source for ANYONE who wants to learn how to market online!
