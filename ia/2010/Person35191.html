Chris Fuller’s Summary

Performance Writing and Public Relations serves the communication needs of businesses.

We develop marketing & sales materials, such as white papers, brochures, product manuals, case studies, web site content, newsletters, media campaigns, and more. We also provide public relations counsel in areas including media relations, crisis management, and communications planning.

My experience in a number of jargon-heavy industries, including information technology, health care, and public education, has given me the ability to make complex or arcane concepts understandable for those who aren't immersed in them every day.
