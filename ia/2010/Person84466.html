Ilene Moskowitz’s Summary

Marketing and communications leader with extensive background in building brands, relationships and revenue with Fortune 500, middle market and high net worth clients, as marketing & sales leader of Grant Thornton’s northeastern U.S. region, marketing leader of PricewaterhouseCoopers’ Financial Services practice in the United States, and head of marketing & strategic planning in North America and South America at Bank Leumi le-Israel’s largest subsidiary.

Expert in brand strategy, sales strategy, key message development, product positioning, building eminence, and blending tangible and emotional benefits into client value propositions. Skilled in leading teams across business, functional and geographic lines. Aligning marketing strategy to business priorities is a key collateral strength.

Accomplished advisor to senior management on initiatives leveraging clients’ key issues, relationship marketing, new business development, and refocusing brands during business transitions. Respected as keen marketing strategist and leader by example who has led business strategy development as well.
