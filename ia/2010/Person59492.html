Bob Turner’s Summary

I am a senior human resources executive experienced in companies ranging from small start-up operations to publicly traded billion dollar corporations in diverse industries. I have been responsible for organizations ranging from 600 employees to more than 25,000 union and non-union employees. I have designed, recruited and managed start-up HR organizations, building responsive and supportive HR functions that provided superior transactional support and strategic partnerships with key departments.

I participated in several acquisitions and also closed several facilities so I am skilled in M and A that showcased my superior skills in cultural integration, policy harmonization and employee relations with an emphasis on uniform and consistent interpretation and application of policies and programs across the full spectrum of remote sites. I have two advanced degrees in OD and have championed numerous OD initiatives in a variety of industries.

Some of the things I have accomplished are:

· Implemented manpower planning to fill more than 1500 open positions saving approximately $8 million.

· Designed and implemented HR partnership with Nursing and Operations to design a joint retention sstrategy that increased retention by more that 10% in the first year.

· Designed and implemented program that identified millions of dollars in questionable pay practices; this brought more $10 million of savings to the bottom line in one year.

· Overhauled field human resources operations by improving accountability and customer service. Upgraded 60% of managerial positions that improved results by 17%.

· Redesigned a benefits plan and negotiated a reduction of nationwide insurance costs by 15%.

· Negotiated vendor cost-sharing programs, resulting in more than $1 million in savings.
