Anthony Jones’s Summary

Currently I work for TBWA\ Manchester as the Senior digital Art Director & creative head of a 16-strong digital department. This role encompasses team management, operations, creative direction, strategy, art direction, and I still chip in with the production when I have the luxury.

I cut my creative teeth practicing graphic design for a period of 7 years before I decided to specialize in interactive design. Since 2002 I have been responsible for overseeing the creative output of interactive and web teams and employ a very hands-on approach, encouraging constant evaluation of the relationship between design principles, usability and creativity.
Through-the-line thinking, planning, strategy, departmental management, art direction and creativity form the core of my current roles.

I believe that to it is fundamental to understand the technicalities of the medium to create effective communication through design & creativity. As a result, I maintain a high technical ability, comfortably creating web applications to AAA W3C standards using the latest XHTML, CSS and AJAX techniques as well as rich, immersive Flash pieces.

I also enjoy an active life out of work hours practicing martial arts, Scuba diving & photography, although rarely at the same time.

I enjoy playing with my twin son & daughter and my myriad of games consoles. When I find time away from these vices, I also play guitar and write music.
