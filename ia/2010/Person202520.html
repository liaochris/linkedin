Lee Johnson’s Summary

Hello!

The Sweep Doctor is an expert in the facilities management industry providing “green” solutions for construction and property management companies who want a healthy alternative for final clean services and janitorial services.

We have over 20 years experience servicing the commercial marketplace. There are poisons, gases, and toxins in our everyday cleaning products. The Sweep Doctor was motivated to make some changes. Why you ask? We were lugging in bins of products, we were concerned about the health and well-being of our clients and employees. What are they breathing in? Dust + toxic chemicals is a bad mix.

We care; so as a company we wanted to be responsible. We were so shocked at the level of indoor air quality in most businesses. Through our research and professional affiliations we discovered sustainability which led us to focus on indoor air quality.

"Going Green" is not a trend for us. It is the way we operate,it is a lifestyle choice and a way to partner with our community. The Sweep Doctor has formulated plant-based biodegradable green cleaning solutions. They contains No Acid No Ammonia No Bleach No Phosphates No Solvents and No Toxins No VOC's. They're eco-friendly and will not contaminate the soil, poison the water supply, or have negative effects on indoor air quality because it they are free of chemicals. We believe that by bringing these products to the forefront we can all "breathe easy" .

As parents we just began to wonder what was happening to our children. There is a lot of research out there, however we commisioned our own. We found that parents that have children with asthma are in need of some relief. We understand how important it is to control your children's environment and the overall health of your family!


Best Regards,

The Sweep Doctor
