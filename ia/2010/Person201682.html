Thea Few’s Summary

After two years of planning and work, we have launched our new site, Arttowngifts.com. The new site combines both shopping and tourism into one online destination by selling the works of art and fine crafts created by artists from featured art towns across the United States and Canada. The site offers a broad collection of unique gifts, regional art, and other items originating from the featured communities in a website format that replicates a visit to a small art town. Arttowngifts will also sell other products originating in the towns such as regional food and spa products.

Towns were selected for inclusion from actual visits to the towns and a variety of articles and books written on the subject of the best towns in North American. A travel guide for the featured towns highlights each community’s recreation, tourist attractions, activities and art scene. Artists’ Studios include a narrative about each artist and specifics regarding their art work. An extensive menu system makes it convenient for viewers to find the information or works of art they desire. Residents, tourists and others can recommend artists and also post photos of the town and surrounding areas and relate their experiences about towns on the site. They can also recommend towns for inclusion.

Local artists and fine crafters are asked to submit their artwork for acceptance. The company is also selling sponsorships for the website’s popular Articles Section, Glossary and Town & Travel Guides. As part of the Town and Travel Sponsorship offering Arttowngifts provides exclusive sponsorships to only one real estate firm in each featured town offering the firm all of the lead originating from the Arttowngifts.com site. Those interested and potential qualified investors should visit the website at www.arttowngifts.com or call 877 278-8696.
