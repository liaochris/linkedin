Our Mission


The so called “health care” becomes less affordable each year for the majority of Americans. “Sickness care” is a more appropriate term.
More and more people use medical help only in crisis situations, when the health problem becomes an emergency. There are more sick people in this country than in any other developed country in Europe and elsewhere.
So people are looking for the alternatives, alternatives they can trust and afford.

The future of health care is becoming increasingly clear – an emphases on health and wellness that stresses the prevention rather than the mere treatment of disease.
There is no magical remedy. There are many ways, however, to naturally improve your health, to keep you healthy and happy.
There are many aspects on the way to healthy life and wellness.
Lifestyle: habits, work, exercise, sleep, food, natural healing products, cloth we ware, simple natural remedies.

We use many or the products ourselves and we offer them to our relatives and friends.
