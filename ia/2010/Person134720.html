Mark Tosczak’s Summary

I am an account supervisor at RLF Communications, a public relations and marketing agency in Greensboro, N.C., where I have provided counsel to for-profit and nonprofit clients in a variety of industries. I manage client account teams providing public relations, advertising and social media marketing. I spent about a decade working in journalism as an award-winning writer and editor. And I have been blogging off and on since 2000.
