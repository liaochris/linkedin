Art Fernandez’s Summary

Online Marketing professional with a proven track record of delivering impactful online marketing and communication programs for a Fortune 500 company. Over the last 10 years, I have successfully executed a broad range of product management and online marketing roles and have proven my ability to assemble and partner with global, multi-disciplinary teams. For the last two years, I have lead the development of global online strategy and programs leveraging the use of emerging technologies including web 2.0, best practices for the use of social networks, mobile communication, and online marketing to Generation Y.

I possess a deep understanding for the use of emerging technology within the enterprise to deliver pragmatic and measurable solutions that maximizes return on investment. I am passionate about the use of web 2.0 technologies and their potential to deliver turn-key solutions that build online communities, enable group collaboration and drive the distribution and sharing of information online that provides marketing differentiation. I offer deep insights into the challenges and solutions required to develop and execute global web-based solutions for Fortune 500 companies
