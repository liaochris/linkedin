David Hicks’s Summary

Consistently rated as among California's top 5% of lawyers, David Hicks has been described by judges in Contra Costa and Alameda Counties as having the most interesting law practice in the Bay Area. David has counted many in the legal profession and judiciary as clients, and himself been asked to serve as a Judge Pro Tem in 4 Bay Area Counties, doing jury trials and other community service judicial functions. David has maintained offices on both the east and west coast, taught law, and accepted referrals of very complex cases. In law school some of his professors referred to him as the renaissance man from his interest and activities across many disciplines. He is proud of having clients who have stayed with his firm for decades.

This past year, he served as a Court Appointed Receiver to run battling parties' insurance brokerages in 4 cities. David's paralegal's 27 years in banking and David's Business Law background worked to quickly bring their the business back to profitability in just a few months.

Currently, David is serving as a binding arbitrator on various matters including medical malpractice and insurance.

"Combining experience and creativity, we work to solve our clients problems as quickly as possible. What other goal does an attorney need?"

See www.DavidHicksLaw.com.
