Bruce Lund’s Summary

A doctoral candidate in Leadership and Organizational Management, Bruce takes lower level resources and personally brands them for success. Bruce unlocks the super-hero we all have inside of us to live a life of meaning and purpose.

The first step is do identify what a person is interested in, what they are naturally talented at, and marries them with a niche in the world for for which only they can fulfill. His four core success principles center on creating an effective gameplan (vision), using adversity as a way of progress (emotional intelligence), articulate this message and value to the world (personal branding), and to be a great #2 in order to become a great #1 by anticipating the needs of your mentor or boss at all times (Leadership).

The NEXTPERT concept requires hard work and a realization that there are no short-cuts to success. No job is beneath a young professional starting out. Become the person and professional that exists within you by engaging in a consistent set of behaviors that allows you to do something tomorrow that you simply cannot do today.

Bruce developed and is now sharing what a NEXTPERT is after experiencing a shift in his own life. Once he realized his "unique ability" (Dan Sullivan) then he began working towards that DOMINANT FOCUS.

Previous experience includes over two years as a journalist with the Grand Rapids Press (second largest newspaper in Michigan), a high school baseball and basketball head coach and teacher, speaking to thousands of children while working community relations with the Nashville Sounds, and now many of his methodolgies have been formed working as the Public Relations Director for Coach Micheal Burt.

Bruce hopes to lead a LURE Y movement for Generation Y to stand out in this age of wisdom and technology and to become the BEST of the NEXT Generation. This is the first generation to inherit a declining economy, compete against four generations for jobs with the lowest job placement rate ever.
