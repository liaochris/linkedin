Sable Mi’s Summary

Insightful marketing professional who is well-rounded in strategic thinking and creativity, possesses a proven track record of maintaining high quality, timeliness, budget control, as well as the ability to develop multi-channel campaigns to improve both client satisfaction and the client-agency relationship.

Strength as a marketing strategist facilitates clients’ market segmentation and product positioning is supported by experience in launching multi-million-dollar product campaigns. Proficiency in user-experience and customer-centric approach increases market share, loyalty and profits. Builds brand awareness and preference with traditional, digital channels and word-of-mouth via ambassador programs and social media. Expertise in consumer products and higher education targeting women and moms.

Advanced degrees in Communications and Business Administration as well as fluency in Mandarin Chinese and English aid my ability to convey written and verbal messages effectively in international environments. Stays in touch with members of our society via my work as an active community volunteer.
