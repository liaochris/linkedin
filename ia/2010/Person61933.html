John King’s Summary

John is an accomplished, zealous technologist, and proven technical pre-sales leader and mentor with over ten years of solid solutions engineering, deployment, pre-sales, sales, system and network administration, customer service, and technical support experience within the electronic document (e-Discovery)/legal services, wireless, data center, and other technical fields.

He is adept at emphasizing the added value to clients’ specific business and technical needs by highlighting beneficial and unique attributes of proposed solution and by clearly defining the advantages over a competitor’s potential solution. John is a professional who delivers compelling and impactful demonstrations and POCs that grab the attention of decision makers and compresses sales cycles..
