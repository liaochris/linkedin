  • Founder & Board Member

    LaunchMemphis

    (Government Administration industry)

    2006 — Present (4 years )

    LaunchMemphis was established by a group of Memphis area leaders to grow the city’s entrepreneurial community.

    Through events designed to highlight Memphis’ entrepreneurial activities, we hope to welcome and nurture new technology startup businesses.

    Past events have included networking events and investor forums, and even SocialCamp and Startup Weekend. SocialCamp is designed to get attendees a leg up on how to use social media in their lives and in their businesses, while putting them in the driver’s seat. Startup Weekend is a project to start a new business in just 52 hours. Find out more about past events, as well as upcoming events on our schedule.

    It is our hope that everyone in the Memphis area who is interested in growing or participating in a new technology-based business will join us. Together, we can achieve more in and for the Memphis entrepreneurial community.

    In that spirit, we’d be remiss if we didn’t mention the generosity of our sponsors. We wouldn’t be able to support the business community as we do if it wasn’t for them.
