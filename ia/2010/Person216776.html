Xander Davis’s Summary

Xander Davis is a creative media artist and game designer based in California, going on nine years of professional experience.

He recently completed UI Artist work at High Moon Studios (Activision) on Transformers: War for Cybertron for Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, and PC, rated 9.0 "Outstanding" by IGN.

Additionally, he has been designing independent game projects for five years, and produces original electronic music as Force Effect, available on iTunes. Xander's writing has also recently been published on the official website of Chuck Palahniuk (author of Fight Club).

Xander is a Graphic Design and Business Administration graduate of Wright State University, with a refined all-encompassing digital skill-set for premium-quality intellectual-property development and production.
