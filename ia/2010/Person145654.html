Mike Little’s Summary

I am the co-founding developer of the WordPress project. I started it in 2003 with Matt Mullenweg (now of Automattic Inc.) and have been developing with and using it ever since.

I recently worked on a number of government WordPress sites, most notably, I developed a Law Commission consultation site and now develop and support a site for a large Cabinet Office client. I am also producing a custom WordPress site for DWP. My background is in software development and I have more than 25 years under my belt, many at the most senior levels. I am a published author and a member of the Usability Professionals Association.
