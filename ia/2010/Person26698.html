Brad Lucas is founder of Beacon Hill, a New York based technology solutions provider to financial firms. An expert in enterprise system development, Brad has recently worked on projects for a number of New York financial firms.

Before working in New York, Brad was an architect for a number of well received systems for Fidelity Investments, including the companies internal research publishing system.

Early in Brad's professional career he designed, marketed and sold a popular screen capture program for Windows called Snapshot.
