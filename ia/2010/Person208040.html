Trevor Levine’s Summary

Levine is called the “secret weapon” behind real estate gurus like Dave Lindahl, Alan Cowgill, Larry Goins, and Terry Paranych -- who sell out their high-priced Boot Camps using Levine’s sales letters.

He recently wrote an email campaign for Robert Allen, author of the #1 best seller "Nothing Down". And before that, Business Credit Services hired him to write an ebook and three sales letters, as well as the emails leading up to a product launch.

Being a real estate investor himself, Levine is uniquely qualified to write copy for real estate offers.

“My last Boot Camp generated 125 people who paid $2000 each to attend. I sent out 19,800 sales letter that cost $6900 to produce and $8300 to mail. The revenue generated from the Boot Camp was over $250,000 in 2 days. We already have a waiting list for the next event!”
--- Terry Paranych

From 2000 to 2003, Levine wrote all of the sales letters for The Internet Marketing Center (IMC), founded by legendary Web pioneer Corey Rudl. One of his letters generated over $800,000 the first time it was announced to IMC’s house list.

Levine has also beaten control letters for prominent Internet marketers like Jonathan Mizel and Shawn Casey -- both talented copywriters in their own right.

“For our Online Marketing Power Pack, we had a sales letter that pulled well for three years, but started to lose its punch. After Levine made several changes and added a different headline, VOILA! We now get double the response before his revisions!”
--- Jonathan Mizel

In the early 90's, Levine was a salesman on commission. Because his income varied, he incurred a great deal of credit card debt. So he understands the financial stress of the prospects he writes for.

This firsthand experience is why prospects respond to his sales copy. After all, people are more motivated by pain than by benefits. Levine agitates that pain and lights a fire under prospects that compels them buy.
