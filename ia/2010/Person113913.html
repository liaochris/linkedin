Kelly Johnson’s Summary

Kelly Johnson manages the User-Centered Design team for BB&T Creative and Delivery Services. The UCD team includes User Experience, Web Design, Copy, and Metadata and Taxonomy.

Kelly has experience conducting usability analysis, information architecture, search engine optimization, competitive intelligence, and interface design projects for both Fortune 500 companies and startups.

Kelly founded Converta Design, Inc., a website design and internet marketing consulting firm located in Raleigh, NC. She combines information architecture, usability, search engine optimization, and internet marketing strategies with website design and development to increase online conversions or sales. Conversion Design is the process of implementing usability and search engine optimization techniques at the design level and integrating websites with business and marketing goals to increase online conversions.
