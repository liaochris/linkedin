Joyce Miller’s Summary

J. Miller & Associates is a public accounting firm that provides audit, accounting, tax and consulting services exclusively to nonprofit organizations and related entities. The Firm began operations as of May 1, 2010 after having provided professional services in varying capacities for almost thirty years. The last twenty years have been spent providing services primarily to nonprofit organizations of all sizes and sectors. The desire to serve this industry exclusively was the genesis to forming J. Miller & Associates.

J. Miller and Associates operates under the following core principles:

 To provide audit, accounting, tax and consulting services in a manner that honors the mission of the organizations we serve.
 To provide services rooted in technical expertise and delivered in a high quality manner.
 To communicate openly with our clients, even (especially) when the issues are difficult.
 To educate our clients (Boards of Directors or Trustees, management, accounting personnel) in accounting and financial matters that are critical to their organization.
 To employ persons who have a desire to use their expertise to support the nonprofit industry.
 To be a part of positive change in the organizations we serve.

Joyce Miller’s Specialties:

Community development organizations, social service agencies, children & youth organizations, including educational institutions (charter schools, independent schools), advocacy groups, arts organizations, religious institutions and housinig for low-income families and senior citizens. Experienced with requirements of federal (including ARRA), state and local government funding, tax exempt organization applications, Form 990 and charitable registration, benchmarking and more.
