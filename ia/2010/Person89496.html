Janine Harrison’s Summary

At Hallam Medical, we specialise entirely in placing Advanced Practitioners into assignments throughout the UK.

If you are an ANP (Advanced Nurse Practitioner), ECP (Emergency Care Practitioner) or an FCP (First Care Practitioner) and you would be keen to top up your hours, or gain further CPD by increasing the variety of your activities, we have hours across many major Primary and some Secondary care entities across the UK. Examples include, Walk in Centres, Urgent Care Centres, Out of Hours Services and GP Practice - our rates of pay are very competitive.

Hallam Medical also offer training courses, (1/2 and 1 day Minor Injury, along with foundations of Advanced Practice).

We have a dedicated, helpful team. Two of the three Hallam Medical Directors are Advanced Practitioners. (One of whom was the Operations Manager of 40 ECP’s within an Ambulance Service.)
They contributed to the national development of the Competence and Curriculum Framework for ECP’S with Skills for Health.

Please feel free to link with me or call me on 07821298236 for any further info.
