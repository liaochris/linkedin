Chris Baker’s Summary

Current Info: Chris Baker has over 500 international news stories illustrating his effective and influential use of social media channels and his public relations contacts.

Chris Baker Public Relation’s (CBPR) clients include a diverse group of talented artists, business persons, companies, and organizations who have expanded and enriched their brand, reputations, networks, business, and image.

In 1992, Chris Baker incorporated Excel Promotions, Inc. Excel was the 1st company to provide entertainment & merchandising for “Spring Break” holiday markets on location in the U.S., Mexico, and Australia. Under his management, Excel Promotions successfully completed 50 special-event merchandising and entertainment ventures at coastal resorts. 1996, while the Olympic Games were being held in Atlanta, Baker worked directly with the former Mayor of the City of Atlanta and current President of the Buckhead Coalition, Sam Massell. Together they promoted Buckhead,Georgia as the "Beverly Hills of the East" to help attract new residents, visitors, and business to the city. --In 1997, Baker launched Flavourset Travel, a specialized travel and entertainment company for “Gen-X” young, cosmopolitan jet setters. The concept was named, “Best New Entertainment Concept in the Music Industry” at the 12th Annual Miami Winter Music Conference’s 1999 award ceremonies. Flavourset Travel went on to promote their Ibiza holiday package directly to their target markets in events in 20 metropolitan cities with DJ Paul Oakenfold as their sponsor and headline entertainer. Flavourset Travel was later hired to produce and execute tourism programs for the Ibiza Consulate and government of Spain. Baker co-produced, E! Entertainment Television's “Wild on Ibiza” program. Wild on Ibiza was the first of the massively successful, “Wild On” series which was broadcast in 25 countries, and seen by over 400 million viewers. Flavourset Travel’s promotional efforts helped increase tourism to Ibiza by 25%.

Chris Baker’s Specialties:

--In 1999, Flavourset Productions was formed to produce themed multi-media events presenting emerging fine artists, fashion designers, fusion chefs, & restaurants with celebrated electronic music acts, performance artists, V.J.s, and D.J.s. The events drew 1,000 to 3,500 guests to different venues in Atlanta each month. Notable venues hosting Flavourset events included The Atl Contemporary Arts Center, The Tabernacle, and The 4 Seasons Hotel. See Box Files for Complete Bio.
