Barbara Weaver Smith’s Summary

I am a female entrepreneur and founder of The Whale Hunters. The Whale Hunters is a strategic sales coaching company. We help small businesses achieve explosive growth by landing bigger deals with bigger customers. We help you create a repeatable, team-based system that drives lasting performance improvements for your business. Does that sound complicated? We walk you through every step of the process, and the first step begins at http://www.thewhalehunters.com. Before launching The Whale Hunters, I was a professor, a university dean and the president of a statewide nonprofit organization.
