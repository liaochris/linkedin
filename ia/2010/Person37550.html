Chris Miles’s Summary

I have been in the financial services industry for more than 7 yrs and focus primarily on helping clients and business groups create wealth, happiness, and financial freedom now rather than in 30-40 yrs by living and serving with a clear purpose in their life. I teach the opposite of the traditional industry leading to more wealth and cash flow with more certainty. More importantly, my clients learn to live a higher quality of life rather than just having quantity of things. Furthermore, I coach other financial professionals in mortgage, insurance, and financial advising industries in building their businesses and providing more value for their clients. Also have several years experience trading stocks and options. In addition, I was a volunteer missionary and taught English in Japan for 2 years and am relatively still fluent in Japanese. Began teaching ballroom and social dance again in 2008.
