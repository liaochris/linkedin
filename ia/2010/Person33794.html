  • Teacher

    Summer at Barstow

    (Primary/Secondary Education industry)

    June 2006 — August 2009 (3 years 3 months)

    I have worked for Summer at Barstow for the past three summers and have absolutely loved my experiences. For the first summer, I was a Teacher's Assistant in various classrooms including: 5th Grade Readiness, a preparatory class for 5th grade math and reading, where I designed and implemented math lessons; Science Wild, a class focusing on fun science experiments for 1st through 3rd graders; and Math Funatics, a class showing 1st through 6th graders the fun side of mathematics. The second summer was my first summer teaching classes in addition to my Teacher's Assistant duties. Classes I taught that summer included: Math Puzzlers, demonstrating the intriguing side of mathematics by spotlighting on its omnipresence; MathFunatics for 4th-6th graders, focusing on mathematics learning through games and applications; and aiding in 7th/8th Grade Readiness Math. This past summer I taught the entire session... and what an incredible experience! Not only was I able to teach previously taught classes in order to enhance them, but I was also able to incorporate completely new class concepts! Some of the classes I led were: MathFunatics for 4th-6th graders, exploring mathematics through games, strategy builders and projects; Math Puzzler for 4th-6th graders, investigating and building popular puzzles, such as the SOMA cube, while exploring math in our environment; Extraordinary Engineering for 4th-6th graders, examining the basic principles of engineering through hands-on design projects, experiments and class discussion-- my husband (a civil engineer) even visited the class to talk about the real life of an engineer and led a class activity; and Readiness 7/8 Math, a preparatory class that enhanced algebra skills by examining patterns, functions, solving equations and graphing through a technology infused classroom experience.
