steven zuback’s Summary

zuback crc is an international executive and business coaching practice that provides creative and resourceful executive and business coaching to CEOs and other "C Suite" executives, Presidents, entrepreneurs, business owners, senior executives, emerging leaders, key contributors, and leadership groups/teams.
zuback crc works to create highly effective and focused leaders, executives, successors, and businesses.

Prior to founding zuback crc, Steve was Senior Vice President/ Senior Consultant and executive coach for Lee Hecht Harrison, an international executive coaching and career management consulting firm.

To Lee Hecht Harrison, Steve brought over 20 years of progressive and diverse experience as a senior human resources executive in large complex multi-site, multi-SBU, health care delivery organizations, medical universities, ranging in size from 1,500 to 13,000 employees with revenues from $150 million to $1.0 billion. He is effective in union and non-union environments. Steve has created and delivered multiple custom executive and leadership development initiatives, organizational development interventions and other human resources development endeavors.

For seven years, as the principal of zuback consulting group (zpg), his own bi-coastal human resources and organizational development consulting firm. Steve consulted and coached the leadership of large, mid-sized, and growth organizations with diverse employee populations ranging in size of 8-89,000 with revenues of $1.5m to $300m on cultural integration, corporate re-structuring, leadership and executive development, intra-preneurship, strategic planning, employee and management development, executive selection and placement, as well as labor-management relations.
