Elizabeth Nolley Tillman’s Summary

Creative. Results-oriented. Customer-focused. Experienced B2B and B2C Services marketer. M.A. in Corporate Communication and over 15 years public relations, marketing communications and brand management experience on the client- and agency-sides.

Currently, I serve as Vice President, Marketing Communications at GfK Custom Research North America where I am responsible for public relations, external communications and marketing strategy supporting GfK Group divisions and brands in the US and Canada.

Additionally, I have developed and implemented a broad range of public relations and marketing strategies for such organizations as Allstate Insurance Company, UBS Financial Services and others in support of sales and revenue goals.

Elizabeth Nolley Tillman’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include social media marketing, brand management, internal communications, event marketing, strategy development, change management, rebranding, public relations, direct marketing, collateral development, corporate communications, program measurement and on-line strategies.
