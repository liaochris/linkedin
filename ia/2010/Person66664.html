Erika Stetson’s Summary

A creative professional and manager with 13 years of journalism and public relations experience. Employers have included Foster's, a daily newspaper covering several dozen communities in New Hampshire and Maine, as well as The Associated Press. Journalism experience includes work as a newspaper editor and print reporter; also familiar with broadcast copy writing. Public Relations experience includes acting as a media liaison, conducting crisis response planning and the planning, marketing and management of small- and large-scale events.

Also a speaker on issues such as alternative lifestyles, gender diversity, sexual diversity, and sexual health and safety. Is transgendered, male to female. Founded and runs the Boston Toy Party, organized under the umbrella of the nonprofit New England Leather Alliance. NELA, based in Boston, works to enhance safety for and the understanding of individuals in alternative lifestyles through outreach, advocacy and sex-positive education. The Boston Toy Party offers a variety of safety classes, supports local artisans and vendors, forges community partnerships and provides a forum for positive discussion.

Was a speaker and panelist at NELA's 2010 convention in Providence, R.I. Is slated to speak at an upcoming convention in Portsmouth, N.H., in October 2010. Also has spoken in various smaller settings on issues relating to gender identity and expression.
