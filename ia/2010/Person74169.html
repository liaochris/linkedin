Geoffrey Rhine’s Summary

GLT Consulting, LLC is focused on helping mid-cap manufacturing in the Greater Philadelphia Metro Area and mid-Atlantic region to improve performance through focus on manufacturing process optimization, reduction of G&A costs, addressing customer profitability, and cash flow / debt management.

Business Process Optimization, LLC is focused at providing process improvement expertise for small to medium sized enterprises to reduce the costs, delays, and inefficiencies which get in the way of delighting your customers.

We will help you find the biggest obstacles, reduce wasted effort, and refocus resources to increase sales and profitability. We can bring you the tools from Fortune 500 training and help you to shine because you are more nimble and closer to your customers.

We bring expertise in process analysis/design, organizational development, business development, international financial analysis and tax planning, root cause analysis, and SixSigma DMAIC and DMADV. Our staff are backed by solid credentials that include degrees in engineering and business finance, with certifications in process improvement, quality and validation.
