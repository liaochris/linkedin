Gerd Naschenweng’s Summary

Gerd Naschenweng joined bidorbuy in March 2010 and serves as Chief Technology Officer overseeing architecture, technical design, strategy and technical product development.

He worked as a freelance Internet consultant and contractor prior to joining bidorbuy and provided IT consulting and architecture guideance for companies such as Vodacom South Africa, First National Bank, BG Phoenics GmbH, CEDA Botswana, VHV Hannover, DBV Winterthur, Daimlyer-Chrysler/Smart and Standard Bank.

Naschenweng began his career in 1992 at Uniquare (formerly known as Genesis Software Consulting) where he worked as a Lead Programmer on the development of financial branch-banking solutions and successfully implemented the then largest IBM OS/2 branch solution. After consulting for more than 5 years at Standard Bank, he joined smart.com in 2000 to manage the IT-franchise setup for the new market launches in UK, Japan and Taiwan.

During his four-year engagement in Germany he consulted as the technical lead architect in the implementation of several insurance internet portals and architected an unique single-signon solution for the major insurers in Germany.

Upon his return to South Africa in June 2004, Naschenweng joined First National Bank's branch-banking division as Chief Architect and headed up the division's architecture migration to a thin-client, web-based transactional system supporting 700 outlets and 14,000 users.

In 2006 he consulted at Vodacom South Africa as JEE architect and in 2007 assumed the role of Acting Manager Systems Design of the Vodacom Commercial System Group, responsible for technical solutions across 12 call-centres, 550 retail franchises and 15,000 users and was instrumental in the migration from old WebSphere stack, the implementation of open-source solutions, compliance with PCI and SOX and acted as an evangelist for open source technologies and portal technologies.

He holds diplomas in Business Enterprise Studies and Marketing Management.
