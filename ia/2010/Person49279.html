Kenny Jacobson’s Summary

10 years application and database development. Datawin Consulting specializes in Business Intelligence with an emphasis on SQL Server, .NET, Analysis Server, Flex (Flash), Silverlight, and DotNetNuke software development technologies.

Datawin is currently developing "OODStore", a very light Framework for Object Oriented Asynchronous design and programming using the Google's App Engine for object persistance (read: data store). Optimized for use with Silverlight, with OODStore you create your Business Logic Layer Classes, inherit the OODStore Base Class, set a couple parameters, and voila...you instantly have "Persist()", "Retrieve()" and "Remove()" for the for your business classes. No more database creation/administration; no more ORM mapping code. Plus, you will be taking advantage of Google's infrastructure: high availability, high scalability, and data backup. Essentially, you are getting a free Data Layer, and Data Acess Layer, and since .NET 3.5 allows you to bind objects to UI controls, your UI Layer is almost trivial, allowing you to contentrate your time on the Business Logic layer. If you design your Business Logic Layer well, future changes become much, much easier.
