Mario Gastaldi’s Summary

Organizational Change Facilitator and Consultant, (bilingual, Italian and English), with over 20 years of experience, with different roles, in diverse organizations across different geographies, industries and cultures.
Specializes in Employee Engagement, Motivation, Change Management, Organization design and Strategic Planning using solid approaches that make change happen fast.
Mario engages with diverse, complex organizational systems, interacting closely with people and facilitating collective learning and self-renewing processes that lead key players and stakeholders to discover their path, and define actions that are consistent, coherent, synergic, based on enthusiastic support and strong motivation.

Mario is an expert as to ways to foster collaboration, and the overall business effectiveness, through Enterprise 2.0 technologies, and approaches.

An intense experience working closely with business leaders generated Mario’s peculiar business acumen and a meaningful sensitivity to multiple bottom lines.
Mario designs and delivers interventions that successfully impact both the business side and the human side of organizations enhancing their mutual relation and improving both in ways that are especially valuable.
