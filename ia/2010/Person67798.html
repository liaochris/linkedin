Eugene Aronsky’s Summary

While in graduate school I became disillusioned with the ever growing cost of textbooks, which every year make up a bigger percent of every students budget. I decided to work to solve this problem by increasing transparency in the textbook buying process through the creation of a book comparison site that would enable students to compare prices on any book and always find the lowest possible price. Although many such sites already existed, my research/personal experience demonstrated they were very inefficient, and often ineffective at finding the actual lowest price of any given book. With this goal, and the help of friends and business partners I founded We Compare Books.
