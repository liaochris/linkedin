Marco Tosi’s Summary

Dealing with internet and new media since 1995, I've passed through all the phases of the digital business sector. I have not left this sector because I really believe in the potential of interactive communication, and I think we have used just a small fraction of this potential. I think my main contribution to my company is to have created and manteined a place that is not only innovative for what we do, but mostly for how we do. Innovation is in my DNA. I love uncertain future and challenging tasks, and I hope to have the pleasure to continue to work dealing with them for a long time.
