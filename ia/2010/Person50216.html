David Boardman’s Summary

Focused on the intersection of urban environment, social/cultural complexity and technologies, David is a designer engaged in envisioning radical solutions to create connections between people, information and physical spaces. To sustain his approach, he actively takes part along a design process with contributions in terms of creative thinking, design strategy, user experience and prototyping techniques. He has presented his design and media arts projects in a number of international exhibitions and conferences. David is a distinguished graduate of Domus Academy / Interaction Design Institute and he holds an undergraduate degree in Media & Communication Studies. He is currently a Research Scholar at the MIT Design Laboratory (Mobile Experience Lab), a research lab operating within the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

David Boardman’s Specialties:

Design research, Strategic design, Creative thinking, Interaction design, User experience, Fore-casting, Ethnographic & field studies, Prototyping.
