Jordan Finger’s Summary

Jordan Finger, is CEO and founder of Ardis Health.

Ardis Health is an Internet Marketing company whose cutting-edge, proprietary three-tier marketing, media and technology platform allows it to develop and distribute health and beauty products in the nutraceutical and skin care categories.

Since its inception, Ardis Health has been a leader in creating and marketing branded health and beauty products sold directly to consumers via the Internet. We have been able to leverage our marketing, media and technology platform to create consumer awareness, drive traffic to our product sites and provide strong customer experience and support, as well as process, fulfill and distribute millions of customer orders in the United States and throughout Europe.

Mr. Finger is a board member of the Entrepreneurs' Organization New York Chapter.

Mr. Finger holds a Business in Science and Management Degree in Marketing from the A.B. Freeman School of Business at Tulane University in New Orleans.
