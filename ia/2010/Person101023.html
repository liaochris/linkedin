Joe Mediate’s Summary

A dynamic and innovative executive with a mature marketing and sales background in early stage, growth and start-up organizations. He is a diverse strategic and tactical thinker and is an expert in marketing strategy and business positioning using internet marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), and social media as a vehicle for success.

Joe is also an expert at bringing new products to market successfully, and building brands through online, and traditional marketing campaigns and at creating client loyalty and retention programs.

He has helped companies ranging from high tech, startups to e-commerce companies increase profits through company positioning, branding, strategic alliances, and innovative sales tools. Joe's 20 years in sales and marketing includes extensive experience in B2B marketing and has marketed a $500K enterprise software solution to major corporations. He draws on his many years of experience leading sales and marketing teams in the software and internet industries.

He is also very adept at B2C, having successfully marketed to consumers targeting Mom’s in the upscale market.
