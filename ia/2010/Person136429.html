    The Security Department of the International Observatory on Cards and Payment is a national and international point of reference for citizens, companies and administrations for the description, analysis, guidance and dissemination of the security culture related to the payment system, with a particular focus on those payments executed via means of a credit card, whether present or not present, and the confidence in the Payment System’s Value Chain.

    Activities of the Security Department

    The Observatory will conduct its activity via different means and in partnership with other entities in order to fulfil its goals. Activities that will be performed can be among these listed:
    - Conduct researches and analysis related to payment systems security throughout the payment value chain
    - Analyse, identify and propose solutions regarding payment systems security
    - Develop a certification scheme for products to be used throughout the payment chain
    - Identify, analyse, test and certify products and security products to be used in payment chain
    - Awareness towards the public, the authorities, the merchants and the financial system towards the benefits of security, also by defining an evaluation scheme for security adoption
    - Identify and perform trainings on payment system security
    - Sponsor, endorse, organise or participate in payment security related seminars, conferences, workshops, trainings
    - Define and deploy workgroups regarding specific topics like advanced payment technologies (NFC, mobile payments, …), advanced security techniques (biometrics, new devices,) …
