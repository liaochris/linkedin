Robert Godden {Executive Talent Broker-LI.O.N.}’s Summary

I've spent much of the last two decades helping organisations find the best executive talent.

I believe in using every method available to turn over every stone - because in order to see real success, every organisation needs the right person at the right time for both parties.

I like challenging work offering flexible solutions to difficult questions - hard-to-find candidates, talent broking for unusual skillsets and often taking on assignments that have been tried by others to no avail.

I've been working in the research and operations aspects of recruitment since 2001.I have expertise in salary benchmarking and consulting. I have often appeared as an expert witness in various Courts on salary and employment issues.

Prior to joining the recruitment industry, I owned a small business for seven years, specialising in market research, general research, information publications and database systems analysis and programming.

I have also taught general research skills, market research, marketing and business legal issues to Small Business Management Trainees as part of a Federal Government Programme.

I am the author of "1001 Nights in The Trans-Arabian Corporation's Boardroom", a book which uses fiction to spark creativity in business operations and career management. I have also written two books on Employment Skills.

I also co-host an internet TV show. Invite me to connect at robert.godden@gmail.com .
