Beatriz Infante’s Summary

Beatriz Infante’s career as public and private company CEO and Director has spanned multiple high-growth business areas sharing the common characteristic of highly scalable systems: data center infrastructure; database/distributed systems; and communications software and appliances. She is a customer-centric executive with extensive experience in creating and driving revenue growth, expanding businesses internationally, and optimizing operating metrics. Infante has raised funding in public and private markets and led multiple successful exits, mergers and acquisitions to deliver exceptional shareholder value.

Beatriz is currently CEO of BusinessExcelleration, the business transformation experts, and is Executive-in-Residence at USVP. Most recently, she was CEO and Director of VoiceObjects, transforming this German provider of self-service systems into a global company and successfully completing sale of the company to Voxeo.

From 1998 to 2003, Beatriz served as Chairman, President, and CEO of Aspect Communications, a leading global provider of contact center solutions. Under her leadership, Aspect grew to record revenues of $600M and operating margin improvement from -19% to +15%. Prior to Aspect, Infante held multiple senior executive positions at Oracle, where she led the Open Systems Division to well over $1B in revenue, led the $100M acquisition of Rdb, and created and evangelized the Application Server category which became a billion-dollar business for Oracle. She has also served as CEO and Director and successfully led the sale of Sychron, a policy-based desktop virtualization company.

She received a BSE in EECS from Princeton University, and holds an MS from CalTech. She currently serves on the Advisory Committee to the Princeton School of Engineering, is Director at Sonus, RNA Networks, and Skybox Security, and is an investor and advisor toTandem Entrepeneurs. Prior board roles include Netli and Silicon Valley Leadership Group.
