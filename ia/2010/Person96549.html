Jerry T Brown’s Summary

In an era where progress thieves on the back of technology and the glories of economic growth are explicitly linked to the leaps and bounds of its advancement, it seems only natural that business and technology function synergistically as two parts of a greater whole.

But, the trends in software development are painting a far more fractured picture, where technical experts forego simplicity and build without consideration for business need, while businesses retain little hope that technical experts will make any attempt at understanding what these needs are.

From this fragmentation, though, Win-Web has envisioned the future and intends on leading the path to new heights. Built at the intersection of business need and technical expertise, Win-Web is reintroducing the fundamental quest for simplicity through an unrivaled process designed specifically to leverage technological value only as it advances the businesses it serves. It starts with discovery – discovery for an answer to questions never before met with strategic consideration from the business’s perspective.
