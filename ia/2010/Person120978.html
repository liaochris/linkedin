  • personal health coach

    LJR NoCrisco

    (Alternative Medicine industry)

    August 1994 — Present (16 years 1 month)

    Wanna be slim, sleep deeper, and slide through life more vigorously?

    Let's discover a mix of smart food habits and activity that you truly enjoy. I encourage a very flexible and individually tailored plan that features partnered exercise along with phone and email consultations.

    In my opinion, the best exercise or diet routine eventually should feel a natural part of your life. It's the positive way to view the command to 'make it a habit'. Activity keeps us physically strong, but there is also evidence that truly voluntary exercise optimizes brain health.

    Exercise is one of many things that will stimulate adrenaline. Voluntary adrenaline is received by the brain in a positive way. Torture is received in a negative way. So make exercise fun and it will become a naturally pleasant habit.

    A story: After many years unsuccessfully battling exercise plans a client discovered the joy of regular workouts when she joined a rowing team. She was surprised to learn that the she had driven by the clubhouse for a decade without noticing it. Aren't surprises in your own neighborhood nice? Expanding our options and listening to our natural preferences can make proactive steps toward lifelong health easier.
