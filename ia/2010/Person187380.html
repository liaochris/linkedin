Shannah Whithaus’s Summary

Since beginning my college teaching career in 2006 (I teach technical, science, and business writing), I have been very interested in e-Learning, distance education, and course/learning management software as it relates to student learning and student writing. I enjoy helping others integrate e-Learning tools into their classrooms, as well as developing and teaching online courses.

I am also energized by new ventures, and love working with start-up companies in high tech industries. I like making connections between and among businesses, and building teams for specific situations. If I have a passion for a technology, I make an amazing evangelist!

My experience includes editing manuscripts on subjects ranging from physics to biology to sociology as well as personal memoirs and fiction. I love to edit and shape a manuscript so it is the best it can be.

I am currently looking for a position that will allow me to continue to experiment with and deploy educational technology, work with adults on achieving learning objectives (either remotely or on-site), or work on publications that create learning opportunities for internal or external audiences. I am also open to opportunities to drive business through partnerships or team development.
