Mónica Díaz de Peralta’s Summary

I have been training with THE HUMAN ELEMENT approach since 1987. My experience includes a wide variety of consulting/training/coaching experiences in Mexico and abroad. In 2003 I began the development of a coaching model that would fill our expectations and take advantage of the profound work we were accustomed to through THE. The product of such work is now the Q Coaching Model, in use in Mexico, Latin America and other parts of the world. I am currently certifying coaches in the model, actively coaching and speaking internationally on my book OTHERESTEEM. I continue to advance on my Aspirations Theory and the derived measures for it. Through Quidam Quidam Global, we strive to assist people in placing a renewed value in their work and in their lives, to make them tanscendent, wise and thouroughly enjoyable, as they become more productive.
