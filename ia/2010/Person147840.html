Susan Oslin’s Summary

Susan Oslin has proven competency as an advanced UI Developer in HTML/CSS and ActionScript 2.0. She continues to advance her skill set in object oriented programming with current works in progress both in ActionScript 3.0 and Ajax. To fully round out her capabilities as a UI Developer she is also working with PHP/MySQL. Committed to using best practices, Susan utilizes design patterns and is studying MVC architecture and UML diagramming.

With 16+ years in digital media arts, Susan was trained as a coder within the context of a hybrid fine arts / computer science program at the University of Illinois’ Electronic Visualization Lab, a forerunner in the advent of computer graphics. Susan began her career in digital visual effects and spent 8 years working for animation giants such as Walt Disney and Warner Brother’s Feature Animation studios programming particle effects such as water, fire, and ‘pixie’ dust. 8 years ago, Susan founded MMM Creative, a marketing communications business with a focus in interactive. She continues to serve her clients on a part time basis but is interested in returning to the challenge of the cutting edge technology and high profile projects she had been accustomed to in visual effects.

She is pursuing studies in User Experience design and building better, more dynamic user experiences utilizing technologies such PHP/MySQL, ActionScript and Ajax at the UCLA Extension’s Advanced Web and Interactive Design program. She stays active in the UX community with the ultimate intention of integrating all of her experience into a role as a User Experience Architect.
