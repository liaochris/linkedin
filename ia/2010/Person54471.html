Denise Marron’s Summary

I am a consummate Administration and Training Professional with 24 years experience in the Education & Training and Community Services & Health sectors, predominantly working in School Support roles and Not-for-Profit organisations. My qualifications include a Certificate IV in Business (Human Resources), Certificate III in Community Services Work (Specialising in Mental Health Work - Non-Clinical) and I am an Accredited Workplace Trainer.

Now, as a Virtual Assistant and Owner/Director of FastFingers Typing Service...Your Virtual Admin Assistant, I aim to provide proficient and cost-effective online Administrative Support and Social Media Assistance remotely, to not-for-profit organisations and training consultants, small businesses, entrepreneurs and individuals, at very competitive rates in order to facilitate need and contribute to the principles of social inclusion.

I have a PASSION for Learning and Development and assisting people to achieve their full potential! To this end, I am currently studying the Certificate IV in Training and Assessment in an endeavour to upgrade my skills and knowledge, with plans to progress to the Certificate IV in Community Development in Semester 2 of 2010.

My ultimate professional goal is to branch out from my current Virtual Assistant business and into my own Virtual Training Consultancy business where I can fulfill my desire to assist people to achieve their full potential in work and life.
