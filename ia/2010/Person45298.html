(Curtis) R. Curtis’s Summary

MY PROFESSIONAL BIO:
17+-year veteran of the Internet and co-founder of one of the first service providers (ISPs). A recognized leader in the Internet, Hosting, Ecommerce and Search Marketing industry; speaking regularly at such conferences as Pubcon, HostingCon, SMX Advanced, AffiliateCon and other industry related conferences on these areas of expertise.

Since the early 1990s, Curtis has been involved in the evolution of the Internet, first consulting with Fed Gov’t in 1992-93, then moving on to the startup of one of the first ISPs. Since selling the company, he has founded several Internet startups & online access providers, most recently, Sr. VP with Superb Internet, a fully managed hosting company. Currently he is CSMO with RankSense SEO software & Chief Rainmaker for Sharksfly Marketing which provides online & offline marketing solutions such as SEO, social media, lead generation and partnerships.

When Curtis is not working, he is fulfilling his passion of outdoor sports. He is an extremely active skier, snowboarder, windsurfer, cyclist and Hobie Cat sailor. When not aggressively pursuing one of these, he can be found relaxing on the golf course or at the gym. He is married to his beautiful Bulgarian architect wife, Dafina and has 2 wonderful sons, Stefan & LG.

Curtis has a Bachelor of Business in Marketing & Economics from the University of Alberta and did his MBA in Marketing & Business Law at the University of Calgary.
