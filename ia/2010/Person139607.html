Melina Druga’s Summary

Dynamic president, co-owner and operator of One of a Kind Baskets, INC.

Custom gift baskets are truly our speciality. They're our best seller! Why? Because we work with your budget and style. If we don't have what you're looking for, we'll gladly find it for you. In addition to orders for individuals, we also do orders for gift shops, other stores, corporations and charities. We're a family-owned and operated business located in Ohio. However, we ship anywhere using UPS.

We are committed to providing high quality products while offering the opportunity to design a gift as unique as the recipient. We are a family owned and operated business located in Ohio that prides itself in doing quality work while building relationships with customers one basket at a time.
