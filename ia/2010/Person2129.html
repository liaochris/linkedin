Adam Aston’s Summary

An experienced writer, editor and analyst, specializing in global trends in green business, renewable energy and other clean technologies. A quick study, able to identify innovations, synthesize technical issues and clearly articulate complex trends. With strong graphical and design abilities, has produced national magazines, books, market atlases, blue-chip consulting reports, boutique newsletters, websites and blogs. A decade of international experience writing about corporate, political and economic issues in Asia. Experienced in managing cross functional teams, with a track record for using technology to streamline work flows. For live links to my clips please see the PDF of my resume in the Box.net link, below.
