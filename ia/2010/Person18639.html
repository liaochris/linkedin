    The Freedom Skater LLC

    (Partnership; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2008 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    I’m Austin, and I will change the way you think about relationships, business, passion, purpose, and life in general. I am a speaker, but I no longer call myself a “motivational speaker.” The profession has become so tainted by old jargon and cookie-cutter solutions to complicated relationship, business, and life problems as to be rendered useless.   • Engineering Cooperative Intern

    Henry Ford Health System

    (Non-Profit; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    January 2007 — October 2009 (2 years 10 months)

    This position was in partial fulfillment of the graduation requirements for my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Kettering University (formerly GMI). It provided diverse technical experience in biomechanics research. My experiences at Henry Ford included:

    • Designing a new loading fixture for compressive mechanical testing
    • Designing and conducting a validation study for compressive test fixture
    • Collaborating with supervisors, colleagues, and external personnel on a design project
    • Designing and machining a testing fixture for four point destructive testing of human tibiae instrumented with external fixators
    • Collecting, analyzing, and reporting motion data
    • Harvesting and instrumenting human tibiae for mechanical analysis of a new internal fixator
