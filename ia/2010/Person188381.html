Dan Sheehy’s Summary

Internet executive with 15 years of experience with online businesses including general management, product development, operations, business development sales and marketing.

Currently President and Founder of Freelanthropy.com, LLC, a company that provides fundraising, branding and communication tools for nonprofit organizations in the United states and Canada.

Prior to Freelanthropy, was CEO of New.net. As a leading distributor of paid and algorithmic search results for Yahoo and Ask.com, New.net pioneered innovative software technologies and amassed one of the world’s largest domain portfolios. Initially leading New.net’s business development efforts as the company’s first employee, rapidly took on increasing responsibility before being named CEO.

Previously served in key roles during more than seven years with The Walt Disney Company, including Vice President Strategic Partnerships for the Disney Internet Group securing distribution, licensing and sponsorship relationships.

Dan Sheehy’s Specialties:

General management, business development, strategy, team building, product development, creative problem solving.
