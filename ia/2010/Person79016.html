Guy Stephens’s Summary

Champion of the use of social media for customer service. I am a Senior Consultant at Foviance where I help brands to understand how they might use social media to engage with customers in an increasingly fragmented cross-channel customer service environment. Founder of the LinkedIn group – where social media meets customer service. I was previously the Customer Knowledge Manager at The Carphone Warehouse, where I helped set up their use of social media to provide customer service through the use of Twitter, Facebook, YouTube and a help blog. Prior to this I was the Global Online Marketing & eCRM Manager at Mars Drinks.

I have over twelve years online experience in customer experience, strategy, marketing, eCRM, SEO and project management. Conference speaker and writer for various blogs.
