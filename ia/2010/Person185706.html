Interactive Internet Marketer, Pilot, Ultra Marathon Runner, MA in Social Anthropology, Wine Enthusiast, and Goofball. I am an independent contractor that offers short and long term Social Media tutorials and campaign maintenance. I also host and/or organize wine tweetups and wine and food events in the Seattle area. Please see my latest Social Media campaign @SeattleWineGal, which I set up to illustrate some of my capabilities of engagement within Social Media.
