Lanae Paaverud’s Summary

I'm a Mompreneur, and proud of it!

I stumbled upon eBay in 2000, and was hooked. It allowed me the freedom to be a stay-at-home Mom, and still do something for the household, and for me. Fast forward....I also now sell on Amazon. While it is a different crown of buyers, it is also very exciting.

The best of all is my own website...Old World Limited, LLC(www.OldWorldLimited.com)!
By setting my own rules, and giving even more personal attention to my customers, while still supplying quality product is terrific! It is my niche :-)

We have a variety of areas:

The Trendy Tie (www.TheTrendyTie.com) -- Jerry Garcia Ties, both regular and extra long lengths. We also carry Claiborne, BCBG, Jacobson and more.

Military TShirt Store (www.MilitaryTShirtStore.com) -- t-shirts from all branches, including Support our Troops, and Vietnam Veterans.

Bath Soaps and Suds (www.BathSoapsandSuds) -- A little something extra to throw in with your purchase. European soaps made with Shea Butter and herbs will delight your senses while leaving your skin soft.

Sun N Shades (www.SunNShades.com) -- Designer Inspired sunglasses at deep discounted / cheap prices.

The Spoken Shirt (www.TheSpokenShirt.com) -- Graphic t-shirts in a variety of themes.

Dogs Ruff Life (www.DogsRuffLife.com) -- Ruffwear dog gear, for the outdoor dog on the go. We also carry By Breed items...cubicle/office signs and memo boards, car signs and more.
