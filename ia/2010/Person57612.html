Sarah Adelmann’s Summary

I first started out in the STD testing and DNA industry almost 2 years ago. I was very intrigued by the DNA side of it. With so many different types of DNA testing available, I found myself eager to learn more. I was lucky enough to join a team of amazing individuals who allowed me to help them build up the DNA side of their company.
AMTC, Inc is an internet based company and we set up Legal and Non-Legal DNA testing local and nationwide. Being on the receiving end of the phone call you can only imagine the types of stories I hear. We specialize in all types of DNA testing such as Paternity Testing, Relationship Testing, Immigration, Infidelity, Ancestry, Adoption Reunion & Forensics Testing. Locally in St. Louis, I am legally certified to collect DNA for Paternity and Relationship Testing.
An individual will contact our company and I speak with them to find out which type of DNA test is needed for their situation. We set up the individuals being tested at one of our facilities that is closest to where they reside if they need a Legal DNA test. If a Non Legal DNA test is needed, I simply put a DNA kit together and mail it to them and they return it to our laboratory. The resulting part is where it gets interesting. When it comes to DNA Paternity and DNA Relationship testing, I am either providing these people with the best news of their lives or the worst news ever. It is understood that the individuals setting up testing are most likely going through an emotional phase at the moment, therefore each case is treated with respect and care.
