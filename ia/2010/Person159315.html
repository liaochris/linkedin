  • Founder and CEO

    Evenio Media

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    April 2009 — November 2009 (8 months)

    Evenio Media is a dynamic media company that is revolutionizing location-based advertising. Evenio Media® specializes in creating private Visual Media Network (VMN) solutions for enterprise businesses. We provide the conduit for you to communicate directly with your customers.

    Our proprietary technology is called Media Ecocast® Platform (“MEP”). MEP enables any entity or organization or company to create, manage and disseminate advertising and content to high definition displays installed in any of its facilities located anywhere in the world.

    MEP now gives you the power to communicate directly with your customers on your terms. You decide what content you want to deliver to what location, at what time, for how long, and how often…. You can promote and cross-promote products, services, and offers directly to your customers. The flexibility of MEP also allows you to segment your customer markets by region, demographics, language, location….etc. Consider the impact of delivering your message in Mandarin to a location whose demographics is mostly Chinese - the connection with your customer is now even more personal and relevant. Our solution is known to increase customer awareness by up to 30%.

    Incredibly, the entire network of displays can be easily quickly controlled by you from a single central location and any content on your private VMN can be updated at a moment’s notice. Now, you can keep your customers up-to-date, improve their experience, and be more informed while at your facility.

    • Increased product and service adoption
    • Improved customer experience
    • Heightened brand awareness
    • 100% control of the visual experience
    • Direct connection with the customer – at the right time and the right place
    • Customized advertising by market segment, geographical locations & demographics
    • Centralized control of your global VMN and all content delivery
    • On-the-fly updates to any existing advertisement or content

  • Microsoft Canada Development Center, Managing Director

    Microsoft

    (Public Company; MSFT; Computer Software industry)

    September 2007 — April 2009 (1 year 8 months)

    The Microsoft Canada Development Centre (MCDC) was established with the hopes of becoming one of the premier centers for software development for Microsoft. Located in Richmond, British Columbia, the MCDC develops components of 50% of the products and services offered by Microsoft that are destined for global markets.

    As the Managing Director of the MCDC I was responsible for all aspects of the centre’s operation, including its overall profit/loss, budget management, policy implementation, facilities, IT, immigration, and external partnerships. I was also responsible for developing a long-term growth and sustainability strategy which the centre is successfully currently executing. I represented Microsoft’s interests with federal, state and local governments in Canada and the US. I partnered, as an industry advisor, with government agencies in establishing business policies involving immigration, foreign credential validation, and corporate taxation.

    The center experienced tremendous success, growing from inception to more than 325 employees in its first year of operation. I managed, in a highly matrixed environment, a technical staff of engineers, software developers, testers, systems architects, and program managers.

    The center is now recognized as one of the most productive and culturally diverse centres within the Microsoft and as such was awarded the third best employer to work for in British Columbia in 2009 by an independent third party source - BC Business Magazine.
