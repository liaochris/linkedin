Tim Stafford’s Summary

I am a Hightech recruiter with 12 years of experience in the High-tech industry. My experience spans Riverbed, Isilon, Google, BEA and Intuit to name a few companies. My main specialty is storage/security systems and protocols to include SAN, NAS, NDMP, SSL, enterprise storage, SNMP, TCP/IP, Infiniband, Python, C/C++, UNIX, and Linux. Good mix of cloud computing and Web 2.0 and 3.0: Ruby on Rails, Java, JavaScript, Perl, AJAX, PHP, DOJO, LAMP, ASP.Net, and C# recruiting. Systems and Network searches all the way up to CCIE level. I also have Nonprofit recruitment and HR experience. I possess in-depth knowledge of recruitment strategies, metrics, hiring plans, and behavioral interview training as well as affirmative action plan development, succession planning, compensation and benefits, laws and regulations, organization change, performance measurements and reviews, and career counseling. Currently working and pursuing doctorate degree in Public Administration at Golden Gate University.
