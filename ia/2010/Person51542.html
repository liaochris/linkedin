David M. Miller’s Summary

After many years as a consultant developing UNIX and Windows software, I began searching for a permanent position in 2002, through which I might build a more durable legacy than that possible working independently. By 2004, this search began to bear fruit, leading me first to Lava Trading (now part of Citigroup), and subsequently to Direct Edge ECN (part of Knight Capital Group), neither of which, for various reasons, turned out to be ideal.

Now at Tradeweb, I am responsible for the Post Trade feed, a principal component of Straight Thru Processing (STP), which notifies participants of their trades and other activities.

Facile with many technologies, I also remain involved in a number of freelance ventures in my spare time, including but not limited to independent development projects, as well as systems support for small businesses and individuals.

My consulting experience has afforded me a wide perspective across the financial services industry, providing a broad range of domain knowledge spanning front, middle and back office environments:

- Fixed Income: Trading and Analytics
- Equities: ECN, Market Data and OMS
- FX: Trading and Portfolio Management
- Asset Management
- Trade Clearing, Aggregation and Reconciliation
