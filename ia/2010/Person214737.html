Wendy Taylor’s Summary

Its amazing how quickly your goals and experience change. Learning is an every day thing for me and my goals change on a regular basis but always remain focussed on one thing - helping others to succeed.

My business is Taylored Assessments, a management consultancy and business diagnostic firm that specialises in helping others identify their "performance" and "opportunity gaps". We have two ranges of business diagnostics that are specifically designed to enable others to build their businesses on solid foundations.

Our first range is the range that I started Taylored with and these are designed to build awareness around a certain subject, such as marketing (a personal passion of mine). The second is our new "Business Insights Range" which is based on research and enables others to accurately identify what they do well and what they can do to make improvements which positively effect their bottom line.

I am an avid networker and love getting to know new people, in my spare time I sail and ski and enjoy bringing up my little boy. People play a big part in my life and I practice Reiki and Shiatsu.

My goals for this year are to re-brand Taylored (this is underway), develop more relationships with other consultants as we share the work that we generate from our diagnostics and get a proper work life balance.

Wendy Taylor’s Specialties:
