Krista Canfield’s Summary

Krista joined LinkedIn in January 2008. As LinkedIn's Senior PR Manager, Krista chats daily with professionals and the press about how you can network globally online. She advises professionals about how they can land jobs at the hottest companies, get that next promotion, find experts, achieve career goals, revamp their resume, meet new clients, build their businesses, manage their brand online and receive career advice from LinkedIn's more than 50 million members.

She's helped ambitious twenty-something job seekers who are looking to get that first 'real' job as well as moms that are looking for creative ways to re-enter the workforce after having kids. Krista has helped small business owners (like wedding photographers, realtors, event planners, etc.) take their companies to the next level and also talks to 'big' businesses and executives about how they can maintain deep relationships with their customers by networking online. Her work at LinkedIn has permitted her to speak with professionals across the globe. By speaking with a wide range of professionals (from entrepreneurs in Canada to business owners in Germany and MBA students in India), Krista has had the unique opportunity of networking with workers from all over the world.

Krista has been quoted in local, national and international print publications and also has appeared on both live and taped radio and TV segments.

She's spoken at numerous events as both a panelist and an opening keynote speaker. Krista is a graduate of Syracuse University and began her career in broadcast television.

...and she's just getting warmed up. :)
