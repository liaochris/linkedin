Debra Murphy’s Summary

As a marketing coach, I empower independent professionals, business owners and entrepreneurs to take control of their marketing to get better results. I help you change how you think about your business and put activities in motion that attract your ideal client.

Vista Consulting is a successful marketing agency that works with high technology companies and service businesses to help them market their products and services more effectively.

Masterful Marketing® LLC is a coaching practice that offers both coaching services and information products to business owners, independent professionals and entrepreneurs who want to take control of their marketing.

Information products currently include:

Masterful Marketing - 8 Steps to Marketing Success: a marketing system offered as a series of 8 teleclinics that helps professionals develop and execute effective marketing strategies.

Get Connected, Get Found - A practical guide to marketing using social media: a teleclinic providing practical advice on how to select and utilize the right social media tools to market your business. Attendees get a LinkedIn e-book and the recordings. This teleclinic can also be given as a workshop.

I blog regularly on Masterful Marketing® and have written articles on marketing that can be found on my Vista Consulting Web site. My published work includes "Creating a Simplified, Integrated Practice Marketing Plan" for "The Business of Healthcare" three volume set edited by Kenneth H. Cohn, M.D. and Douglas E. Hough, PH.D. and "Cultivate Market Perception" for the co-authored work "Create the Business Breakthrough You Want: Secrets and Strategies from the World's Greatest Mentors" featuring business giants Brian Tracy, Robert G. Allen, Bob Proctor and Mark Victor Hansen (Chicken Soup for The Soul) and endorsed by Dr. Stephen Covey and Ken Blanchard.
