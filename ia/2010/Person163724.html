Philip Hesketh’s Summary

phil@heskethmediation.com

I am an independent professional mediator. I help individuals and businesses work out their own solutions to disputes. This means they do not have a decision imposed upon them by an external person or agency.

I also help companies and organisations resolve internal conflicts between individuals or departments.

I offer a range of services in addition to "traditional" face to face mediation meetings. Give me a call (0845 056 36250) or visit my website.
