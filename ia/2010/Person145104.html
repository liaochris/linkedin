Mike Boyd’s Summary

My name is Mike Boyd and I’m an entrepreneur.

I live in Brisbane, Queensland Australia.

I am a freelance Social Media Strategist and Personal Brand Consultant that advises businesses, organisations, and individuals on how to best use Social Media and social networks to build real relationships, connect with their current and potential client base and derive profit opportunities.

I understand the complexity and sheer pace of the Social Web and how businesses can best engage their audiences in meaningful, transparent and open conversations online. I help bridge the gap between the online and offline worlds and am a strong advocate of transparent, value-adding communication. In other words, I hate spam too!

If you need help unraveling the tangle that is Facebook, Twitter, MySpace, Ning, StumbleUpon, YouTube, Flickr, Delicious, Last.fm, Seesmic, Vimeo, SlideShare, Wordpress, Blogging, Digg, Email Marketing or any other online communication tool that could greatly benefit you personally or your business; please drop me a line - I’m available for hire, contract or partnership opportunities.
