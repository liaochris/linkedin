Derek Moore’s Summary

A creative and entrepreneurial Marketing Executive who excels in driving innovation across multiple channels to build brands, engage consumers/customers and develop multi-disciplinary marketing teams that deliver highly creative and effective results for clients and agencies alike.

Key strengths:

Strategic Vision:
Expert at getting into the head, heart and gut of the consumer/customer while analyzing market conditions, the competitive landscape and the place where the brand connects with the consumer/customer. Developed successful, completely integrated customer engagements and experiences across all touch-points.

Creative Leadership:
Excels at identifying creative solutions that cut through the clutter, connect with consumers and deliver results. Recruited and built high performance, creative teams and developed creative professionals that exceeded expectations. Successfully integrated Strategic Planning, Account Management and Creative Departments to form collaborative teams bent on delivering success.

Innovation:
Expertise in adding/developing new capabilities to advertising agencies and marketing services companies. Pioneered/led development of integrated, direct, data-driven and interactive marketing capabilities at Leo Burnett, FCB Advertising and The Marketing Store.

Financial Management:
Drove all aspects of business’ P&L to deliver on revenue and profit goals. Developed work-flow processes and applied financial systems & technology to optimize resources. Successfully started, managed and grew a virtual integrated agency during the difficult post 911 economy.

Business Development:
Conceived and implemented new programs and initiatives that grew revenue streams and created new ones. Acquired new and retained clients at multiple agencies.
