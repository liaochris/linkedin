June Armstead’s Summary

A licensed Cosmetologist and a pre-certified Trichologist with over 12 years of experience in styling, training and management.
My primary concentration is Trichology, which is the study, examination and treatment of hair breakage, hair loss and other hair and scalp maladies. As a salon owner, I have successfully developed and implemented a customized client tracking system which has enabled my salon to track detailed client service history over a minimum of 10 years. My creative sales promotions and specialized services have been featured in local and national publications and radio broadcasts. I am currently pursuing my Board Certification in Trichology and PhD as a Holistic Health Practitioner.

June Armstead’s Specialties:

As the Owner/Stylist, my responsibilities include hair cutting, coloring, perming, weaves and styling. Chemical hair styling: relaxing, straightening, permanent waving. Thermal Hair styling, Hair Analysis and Trichology Consultations. As the business owner, my activities include initiating and monitoring business development, purchasing, inventory, payroll, sales promotions to cultivating youth career apprenticeship and training programs.
