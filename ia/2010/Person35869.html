Charlie Robbins’s Summary

My practice is focused on commercial real estate. I have represented clients in a wide variety of commercial real estate matters, including the purchase, sale, leasing, development and financing of all types of commercial properties.

Charlie Robbins’s Specialties:

I have extensive experience in commercial real estate leasing, having negotiated leases with/for national retail, restaurant & grocery tenants, retail & mixed-use center landlords, & office & industrial tenants & landlords. I have also assisted clients in the development of numerous mixed-use and condominium projects in Tennessee, including the development of a proposed 70-story, mixed-use building and a 93-acre development including office, retail, restaurant, hotel and residential components.
