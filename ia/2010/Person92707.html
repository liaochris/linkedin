Jeanne Quereau, MA, LPC, CPC’s Summary

As an experienced career counselor and coach, I help people evaluate both their talents AND their interests. Then I help them learn how to be a savvy career changer/job hunter so that they can find or create the job/career their hearts desires.

I also offer what I call "career therapy,” a hybrid of career counseling and psychotherapy, to clear the inner beliefs and emotional baggage that specifically impact career choices and success. I help people learn to access and follow their own inner guidance, so they can fully "step into" who they were meant to be.

As a life coach, I offer practical guidance and proven strategies for getting one's life on track, experiencing more fulfillment in various arenas of your life, and the great joy that comes from being in alignment with your true self and your unique life's purpose.

My work as a Licensed Professional Counselor combines transformational spiritual techniques with pro-active psychotherapeutic methods to bring about deeper, more profound changes in people's inner states and their outer relationships.

I help people recover from old wounds and traumas and open themselves to new levels of self-acceptance, inner peace and confidence, and the ability to give and receive love more completely.
