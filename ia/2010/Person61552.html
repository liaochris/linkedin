Boryana Kolf’s Summary

My name is Boryana Kolf, the creator of Eclipse Line. As a child living in Bulgaria, I always had a fascination with beautiful jewelry… so much so that it inspired my career as an artist. I have had the delight of experimenting with blown and fused glass. I have felt the warmth of creating pieces from natural stones, carved bones and unique, lustrous pearls.

My jewelry is a reflection of my world view. Every day, we must experience unique beauty. My collections are of limited quantity and are made from exotic components. My inspiration is the natural beauty and texture of each piece and the historical use of these components through the ages.
