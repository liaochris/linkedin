Ray Crawford’s Summary

Throughout my career, I've made things happen. From a technical aspect, I was the person that was called when things went wrong or when seemingly insurmountable technical problems presented themselves. That said, I am not a typical "gear head". I have a voracious desire to improve the bottom-line and to seek solutions to the problems which inhibit business opportunities.

In my current role as a business owner and consultant, my goal is continuous innovation through entrepreneurship for myself and my clients with one end in mind -- to build revenues and improve profitability. I deliver against this goal through listening and learning about my clients' businesses and then helping them seek market opportunities both online and off.

My specific flair is for online marketing but many of my clients and personal startups need end-to-end marketing assistance. In these situations, I bring the tools and resources to the table which address the business need despite the "medium".

My goal, simply put, is to generate revenue by making IT happen.
