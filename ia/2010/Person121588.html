Latoya J. Williams’s Summary

Latoya J. Williams is a consultant who has worked with various non-profit, corporate, government and small-business clients for the past 11 years. She has developed a comprehensive management perspective and skill set from performing organizational development, data management, business and systems analysis, web development, and training responsibilities. She works well as a consultant or functioning in an organizational/business management and administration role that requires a broad range of knowledge and aptitude.

Latoya has developed her own coaching practice specializing in helping individuals and groups enhance their sense of wellbeing through career and professional development and personalized self-care practices including "intuitive eating". She has completed a comprehensive 120-hour training to become certified as a Global Career Development Facilitator (GCDF). She will also complete advanced Intuitive Eating practitioner training and become certified as a holistic health coach.

Latoya demonstrates her commitment to community development and social improvement through ongoing volunteer projects and activities. For the past decade, she has contributed in her local community as a tutor/mentor, a volunteer teaching instructor, and a group home counselor for at-risk youth. Latoya has a great ability to adapt and work well with diverse individuals and groups, due to living in several different regions of the United States and the resultant understanding of a multicultural perspective and its vital importance.

Latoya is also extremely passionate about caring for the Earth and improving environmental sustainability and helped coordinate and complete planning and implementation tasks for the largest Earth Day Fair in the Piedmont Triad area in North Carolina in 2009.

Latoya J. Williams’s Specialties:

Latoya works with clients to analyze and identify evolving business needs, create effective solutions, and develop new skills to more effectively and joyfully administer their organizations and enjoy their lives.
