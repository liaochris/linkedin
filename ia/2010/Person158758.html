Pam Durkin, ASID, LEED AP’s Summary

Goal: To bring energy, fun and style to all my projects. I'm currently fascinated with adding fashion, art (graffiti, tatoo, etc.), and musical influences to my designs. Starting to explore product design.

Professional interior designer with 17+ years experience (I know, I look too young for that!)

Commercial Interiors: Single office to 1,000 person moves, restacks, etc.

Residential Interiors: Single space to 10,000 sf homes.

I can appreciate all styles as long as it is a true reflection of the client and not a trend follower.

Pam Durkin, ASID, LEED AP’s Specialties:

LEED AP, GREEN AP. Custom product and pattern development, branding in interiors. Environmental and people friendly interiors.
