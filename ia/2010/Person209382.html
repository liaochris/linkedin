Uros Djekic, PhD’s Summary

During my training I have obtained a specialized knowledge of virology through work on HIV-1, earning a Ph.D. in Cell Biology. Following completion of the 2-year FDA Commissioner’s Fellowship Program, I will stay at the FDA where I will continue to develop FDA policy and guidance documents both on the division and CBER level. Concurrently, I will continue to provide expertise in regulatory review of CBER regulated product applications including INDs, IDEs, PMAs, BLAs, 510(k)s).
