Résumé de Max Blumberg

Welcome.

Background: I worked as a management consultant at Accenture in South Africa and the US before founding and successfully exiting a technology start-up followed by a PhD at the University of London.

Today, I hold three key roles: Founder of the Blumberg Partnership employee insight consultancy, Research Fellow at Goldsmiths, University of London, and Chair Elect of the British Psychological Society SGCP.

The Blumberg Partnership delivers the following solutions to global and national enterprises:

Analytical Talent Management: We have developed a unique recruitment process for helping companies accurately identify high potential candidates while maximizing engagement and retention. The process is based on deep statistical analysis of the organization’s existing high performers, values and competencies to maximize fit. Clients using our processes typically report performance improvements of 20% - 30% and include the Rentokil Initial Group, the BBC, Brit Insurance, the British Army and Friends Provident.

Executive Coaching: As leaders in coaching psychology, we deliver individual and group programmes for developing full potential, improving work performance, career development and personal branding. We achieve this using cutting-edge psychometrics to identify the psychological drivers of our clients who include the MOD, Barclays Bank, the John Lewis Partnership, Covidien, and Novo Nordisk.

Market Research: We blend traditional market research with modern data mining to deliver deep customer insights, market segmentation, and maximize lifetime value. Clients include GN, United Media and AXA UK.
