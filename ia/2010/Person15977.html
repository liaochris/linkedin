Arden Moore’s Summary

As America's Pet Edu-tainer, my goal is to help alleviate frustration and guilt in pet lovers and bring out the best in dogs and cats so that everyone wins -- two and four-leggers! I have appeared on more than 300 radio and television shows as a pet expert, including Martha Stewart, CNN, Fox and NPR.

I am fortunate to wear many "collars" in getting the people-pet bond message out. I am editor of Catnip, the award-winning national monthly published with Tufts Veterinary School; editor-at-large of Fido Friendly, a national bimonthly about traveling with pets; host of Oh Behave! on Pet Life Radio.com and weekly newspaper columnist for Today's Local News with my, "Paws Up for Pets" column appearing every Sunday.

Arden Moore’s Specialties:

I am a professional speaker, editor, author who has conducted national satellite media tours for pet companies as well as completed a successful 6-week national book tour.
