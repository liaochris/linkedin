Jason R. Thomas’s Summary

Skeleton Key offers IT Consulting and Custom Software Development. What makes us different? We focus on service not products, in fact, we have NEVER sold a product. We make your needs our problems and solve them. Simple.

Our IT team makes Mac and PC play nice together better than anyone. You know the commercial with Mac (the hip young guy) and PC (the balding middle aged guy) talking. It doesn't look like they will ever understand each other. We make sure they shake and share the ice cream.

The Skeleton Key software development team is passionate about FileMaker. We design the software that your business needs, not more and certainly not less. All the utility you need and none of the fluff that slows you down.
