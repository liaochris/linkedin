Elizabeth Doty’s Summary

Management consultant and coach with extensive experience helping leaders and teams fulfill their value propositions and achieve their goals in the midst of chaotic change. Specific interest in learning and improvement strategies, collaboration among professionals, and personal engagement. Recent clients include Camp, Dresser & McKee, Intuit, Skillsoft, Archstone Smith, Hewlett Packard, UC Davis, Stanford University, and CTB/McGraw-Hill.

Elizabeth Doty’s Specialties:

Systems thinking, organizational learning, change, learning communities, on-line community tools, knowledge harvesting, knowledge sharing, story, experience mining, simulations, instructional design, ROI, metrics, followership, collaboration, personal engagement, conflict management, playing a bigger game, saying no.
