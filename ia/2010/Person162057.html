Peggy English’s Summary

With my sister Patty Nalley; we launched Organic Bug in 2007.

We grew up in Louisville, Kentucky in the 50’s & 60’s in a typical working class neighborhood where friends gathered to sit on their porches on warm summer evenings sipping iced tea, while children played hide & seek in the shadows of tall trees. Our mom was a single parent and although dad provided what financial help he could, she worked full time and had the full-on responsibility of maintaining our home and caring for three children. No easy task! Big thanks, mom!

Our mom had grown up on a small farm; so although we lived in the city we always had a garden and lots of animals in and around our tiny house. Fresh creamy milk was delivered to our door twice weekly and who could forget the Strawberry Man yelling “Strawww-berries” as he walked the streets of our neighborhood peddling his freshly grown sweet fruit harvest! We watched our dog give birth to several litters of puppies and helped mom nurse injured squirrels & birds back to health. She respected everyone no matter what their lot in life and always showed us the beauty found in nature and the many creatures that inhabit the earth.

Fast forward to 2005, after going our separate ways and with our own families now grown, Patty & I found ourselves together again in neighboring sleepy Florida beach towns. Our shared childhood memories, appreciation of the natural world and the four grandchildren we currently have between us led us to create Organic Bug. We are excited to celebrate and support all of the amazing individuals and companies whose products we offer and love being a part of the movement to bring about awareness and change for social justice and a sustainable future.

Peggy English’s Experience

    OrganicBug.com

    (Retail industry)

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    OrganicBug.com is an online retail of products that fall into one or more of the key categories of Fair Trade, Handmade, Made in the USA, Natural, Organic, Recycled and Sustainable.

    Our Mission

    Through the offering of a wide variety of quality contemporary eco-friendly, fair trade and health-oriented merchandise, we invite individuals to use their purchasing power as a vote for social and environmental change, while delivering an incredibly interesting and informative one-stop shopping experience for today's busy consumer.

    Our Philosophy

    Organic Bug was born to nurture the relationship we all have with the Earth, each other and our own inner beings. The spirit of Organic Bug is to build a successful business with a focus on the environment, social justice, personal development and sustainable living. Organic Bug is a destination where one discovers a diverse assortment of goods produced to create a positive and lasting effect. Building a company to promote a better world, we pledge quality products, excellent customer service and an ongoing commitment to provide the newest, most innovative merchandise that contributes to personal enjoyment, relaxation and renewal. We believe that it is in everyone's best interest to care about each other and the planet. If we empower ourselves, we empower others. We can make a difference, one purchase at a time.

    Our Vision:

    We envision a compelling future where people collectively embrace the idea of living in harmony with Mother Nature while respecting life in all its forms, further effecting dramatic change for the common good beginning with the simple practice of conscious consumerism.
