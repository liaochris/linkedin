Lynda Ward Pierce’s Summary

Ms. Pierce is an active member of the San Francisco human resources community. She is currently the Vice President of Human Resources for MobiTV. Ms. Pierce manages all of MobiTV's HR programs. She previously served as Head of Human Resources for SVB Financial Group, where she managed all of SVB's human resources, facilities and security programs. Before SVB, she was Organic, Inc.’s Vice President, Human Resources from July 1999 until October 2004. Organic is a leading provider of e-commerce consulting and solutions.

Ms. Pierce joined Organic from Navigant Consulting/The Metzler Group, Inc., a provider of consulting services, where she was the Global Director of Human Resources. As Director, Ms. Pierce implemented and developed all human resources programs, policies, and processes for a high growth professional services and consulting company with sixty offices in fifteen countries. She also worked as Director of Human Resources for LECG, a provider of litigation support and management consulting. While at LECG Ms. Pierce managed the company's HR needs during its IPO and global expansion.

Ms. Pierce is currently on the faculty of San Francisco State University where she has taught a variety of classes in the Human Resources Management Program.

As a subject matter expert, Ms. Pierce is a frequent speaker at human resources seminars and professional gatherings. In addition, she occasionally does consulting that helps to guide organizational success by providing human resource solutions to small, mid size, and large organizations. Her clients range from small entrepreneurial start-ups to Fortune 1000 industry leaders.

Ms. Pierce's extensive HR experience includes twenty years as a human resources professional in a wide range of industries including:
- Internet/Technology
- Professional Services/Consulting
- Retail
- Government
- Academia
