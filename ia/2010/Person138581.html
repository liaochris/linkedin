Maximilian Court’s Summary

I am currently studying an MA in Contemporary Chinese Studies at the University of Nottingham's Chinese campus (Ningbo). I am specialising my knowledge of economics so as to understand how a modernising economy can overcome developmental challenges as well as China is. I hope to use my knowledge in an international forum that deals with UK-Sino economic relations.

My bachelor is in International Economics (BA, Hons - 2:1 class degree). I think it is quite impossible to have such a degree without knowing anything about China's economics and that encouraged me to do my MA.

I am organised and capable of time management both of which were required in my management role at Epsom Downs Racecourse. I have applied my economics knowledge in the work place at GlaxoSmithKline in Shanghai (China).

I am motivated, enthusiastic and insist on doing my most and best. I especially enjoy travel and the feeling of having overcome a challenge.
