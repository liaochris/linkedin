Liliana Uruburo’s Summary

While technology is a big part of my experience, my success is attributed to the way in which I am able to make technology palatable to people. This has been called user-friendly design or user interface design. As technology becomes more social, the group or community interfaces with technology and each other. This adds another level of complexity that can best be addressed through understanding sociological and cultural concepts around networks or social groups. This intersection between human networks and technological networks is the focus of my current and future endeavors.
