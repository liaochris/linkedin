Denise Brosseau’s Summary

Denise Brosseau is the CEO of the Brosseau Group, a executive talent agency working with executives to accelerate their career success. Combining the services of personal branding strategists and personal talent scouts - executive agents help you increase your connections, raise your personal profile and identify (and take) the right next steps in your career. We open our Rolodex to only a few select clients a year. Contact us if we can help you achieve your personal and professional goals this year. (www.brosseaugroup.com)

Since 2002, Denise has also led Brosseau & Associates, a management consulting and business planning company. Her clients include PG&E, KPMG, and HP.

Prior to founding The Brosseau Group, Denise was the the co-founder of Invent Your Future Enterprises (www.inventyourfuture.com), a professional development accelerator building the next generation of women leaders through programs, consulting, tools and publications for individuals and global companies. She continues to serve as an advisor and will speak at their annual conference in March.

From 1998 until 2002, Brosseau was the CEO of the Forum for Women Entrepreneurs (FWE), an organization she co-founded in 1993. Denise made FWE (now FWE&E) the country’s leading organization for women-led startups. (www.fweande.org)

In 1999, she co-founded and continues to be an advisor of the powerful venture conference series for women entrepreneurs, Springboard Venture Forums. The Forums have led to over $3B in funding for women entrepreneurs. (www.springboardenterprises.org)

Denise is a frequent public speaker and serves as the Chair of the Business Leadership Council of Wellesley College and the Housing and Human Concerns Committee in Redwood City. Recently, she was selected to participate in Leadership California's - California Issues and Trends program (www.leadershipcalifornia.org), a unique program, designed to develop and mobilize female leaders.
