Wilson Grabill’s Summary

Wilson Grabill is a global PR executive and writer with extensive corporate communications experience in the pharmaceutical and biotech sectors. He works with teams to create value by harnessing the power of strategic communications to open markets, engage stakeholders, build organizational alignment and drive change.

Wilson focuses on communications strategy and messaging, media relations, policy communications and corporate communications. He launched his consulting business in San Diego in 2008, and has partnered with clients at Amgen, Vertex Pharmaceuticals and Illumina. Currently he is working full time with the marketing group at Illumina, a leading genetic analysis biotech, as the company moves into consumer genomics and diagnostics.

Prior to relocating to southern California, Wilson headed worldwide corporate communications at Bristol-Myers Squibb, a global pharmaceutical company based in New York. He worked directly with three board chairmen and CEOs on key communications, including speechwriting, investor and earnings presentations, employee engagement and policy statements.

A global strategist, Wilson has developed and managed successful leadership communications initiatives in the US, Africa, Asia-Pacific, Europe and Latin America. He also has significant experience leading and managing communications in crisis situations, including director and senior leadership changes, investigations, financial restatements and product recalls.

Prior to BMS, Wilson held communications and policy positions in publishing, research and government organizations in the US and Europe, including the US State Department. He has written high-profile speeches for business leaders and government officials, including the President of the United States. He holds degrees in international relations from Yale University and George Washington University, and speaks French and German in addition to his native English.

Contact info: wilson@ceo-voice.com
