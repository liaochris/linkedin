Ed Rowland’s Summary

Ed is a senior executive with domestic and international experience in running businesses in the OTC, consumer packaged goods, and food service categories. His 25 year track record includes industry powerhouses Pfizer, Kraft, PepsiCo, Mars, and McDonald’s. Ed has expertise in P&L management ($10MM-$150MM), Brand and Category growth, Innovation, M&A negotiations, opening new markets, and business turnarounds. Effective team leader. Multi-lingual including Spanish. Ed specializes in bringing strategic direction to complex businesses.
