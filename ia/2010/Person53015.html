Daniel Donahoo’s Summary

Daniel Donahoo is the author of "Idolising Children" (2007) and co-author of "Adproofing Your Kids" (2009). He has established his new company Project Synthesis to allow him to continue to work with great ideas and help passionate and engaged people and organisations bring them to life.

He has worked across public and private sectors in public policy development across industries including early childhood, disability, aged care, youth, media, parenting and infrastructure.

Daniel's aim is to contribute new ideas and support organisations and policy makers to think about how they will work and operate in the medium to long term. His aim is to engage people in thinking that challenges them to change for the better.

Creatively, Daniel continues to write through GeekDad.com and collaborates on new technology work such as http://www.inbflat.net
