Ronald T. Meaux’s Summary

Ronald Meaux is the former Director of CRM Online for Microsoft North America. He built and led the on-demand sales and partner organization for this start-up business within Microsoft. His responsibilities included field and inside sales, business operations, channel partners and strategic alliances.

Prior to Microsoft Meaux held senior positions with industry leaders like Ariba, Lawson Software, HealthSouth, and Maxim Healthcare. Meaux received his Bachelors of Science degree in Business Administration from the University of Central Florida. He currently lives in Seattle with his wife and two children.
