Dorsi Diaz’s Summary

As a business owner for over 14 years, I have learned alot about business and people.I love working with people, helping them achieve their goals and dreams.
I am currently a reporter/examiner with the SF Examiner on the subject of climate change. I also work part-time teaching art to children through a drawing program called Kidzart.
A prolific writer, I have affiliated myself with Google, Kontera, EBay and Amazon over at my writers platform called HubPages. I have wriiten over a 100 articles there on subjects as diverse as "How to get the skunk smell out of your dog" to controversial issues such as Barack Obama's birth certificate. Never one to avoid political controversy, I like to write about topics such as climate change that engage readers to think out of the box.
My mission this year is to hook back up with lots of old friends whom I miss very much since closing our business at Signs Now.
