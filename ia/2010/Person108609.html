Julie Ott (@julieaott)’s Summary

Julie’s illustrious career began with a BFA in Photography from Arizona State University. While waiting for Life and National Geographic to call, she landed every shutterbug’s dream job: high school prom portrait photographer. Tragically, her career was cut short by a freak corsage accident. So she turned to advertising and has been cranking out award winning print ads, television spots, logos, brochures and web sites ever since.

Over the years, Julie rose through the ranks and became the Associate Creative Director at a darn good little ad agency you’ve probably never heard of because it’s in Arizona. But you’ve definitely heard of her clients: Nike, Fujitsu, The Boys & Girls Clubs, American Funds, Best Western Hotels and many more. And now she is off on her own creative adventures in San Francisco.
