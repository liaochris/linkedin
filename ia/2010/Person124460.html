Levin Nock, PMP’s Summary

As a project manager and engineer, I build collaborative teams to transform ideas into products. At Siemens and at multiple startups, I have managed several R&D projects including a successful multimillion dollar federal contract; and helped implement an ISO-14001 Environmental Management System. My skills include:

+ Focus on customer-oriented results and business objectives.

+ Manage stakeholders e.g. government, corporate, academic, healthcare, international.

+Synthesize diverse information into coherence, finding holistic solutions to address multiple challenges efficiently.

+ Translate conversations into clear, useful documentation: patents, proposals, presentations, budgets, requirements, reports, schedules, specifications, use cases, etc.

+ Write Matlab programs and build prototype devices spanning multiple engineering disciplines.
