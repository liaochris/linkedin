Günther Schmalz’s Summary

European patent, trademark and design attorney, licensed to represent third parties before the Monaco Patent and Trademark Office, the European Patent Office, the European Trademark Office (OHIM), the World Intellectual Property Office (WIPO) and the German Patent & Trademark Office.

Günther Schmalz’s Specialties:

Handles patent and trade mark applications / oppositions; infringement / counterfeiting litigation; media law and copyright; negotiation for performing artists, writers, painters, film producers, sports celebrities, acrobats, sculptures, photographers, architects license agreements; assessment and evaluation of intellectual property portfolios; due dilligence, implementation of effective IP asset holding structures; effective transfer pricing structures; litigation cost dispute management.
