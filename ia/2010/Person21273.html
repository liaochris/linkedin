Belinda Ang’s Summary

A child of the dot-com generation, Belinda is a digital native who has built a reputable online influence in Singapore. Her notable presence can easily be observed across the different social platforms.

She is currently Digital Specialist with MS&L Group Singapore (member of Publicis Groupe), also lending support and counsel to the regional office and clients. Aside to this, Belinda is also a personal social media consultant for world-renown C-level business and brand strategist, Mr. Martin Roll and adviser for Greater China to local social media start-up, PepperConn.

She spearheaded @SGbrands, a Twitter account to help promote brands and businesses in Singapore. She was also invited as a guest writer to pen marketing related topics for Lianhe Zaobao in 2009.

Prior to this, Belinda was originally a film major trained in Temasek Polytechnic, Singapore and Beijing Film Academy, China. She started her own food & beverage start-up upon graduation, taking charge of the multiple business functions in her 2 outlets. The business was sold after 5 years as a career move to pursue her interest in event marketing.

She then joined premium event companies, A Group of People Pte Ltd as Event Director and Festival Square Circle as Senior Account Manager, managing key accounts like South Australia Tourism Board, General Motors, Fuji Xerox, Singapore Tourism Board, WDA, Marina Bay Sands and Singapore International Water Week among others.

Belinda's creative fundamentals, diverse experience and exposure to cross marketing disciplines puts her in a good position to take on versatile roles in organisations that motivate changes at the forefront.
