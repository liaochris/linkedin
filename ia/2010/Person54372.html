Deni Kasrel’s Summary

I am an energetic and insightful communications leader with a background that includes strategic web and print communications, marketing, public relations, journalism and graphic arts production. Together these experiences enable me to take a holistic creative approach to marketing and communications.

- Operate both independently and as a team player
