Taylor Moulton’s Summary

Nuclear engineering graduate with a master's degree and over a year in relevant internships. Much emphasis on systems thinking and computational methods.

Aided as teaching assistant in Sloan's System Dynamics for Business Policy course for a year and after taking other management and policy courses related to the energy industry, became interested in better understanding deregulated energy markets.

Currently working as an energy trader for DC Energy and absolutely loving it. My team is the greatest and I especially appreciate that my work continuously evolves as we adapt to changing market conditions. I hope to stay at DC Energy while I am learning and challenged to solve individual investment problems and as long as I am increasingly asked for input in firm-wide development.
