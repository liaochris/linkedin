Charles Ebeling’s Summary

Creative communications PR-kitect. Designed and built strong PR-kitecture and public relationships for McDonald's, Baxter, Toyota and Allstate. 18+ years heading corporate communications for Dow 30 component McDonald's and Fortune 500's Baxter International. Creative executive with major public relations agencies -- Burson Marsteller and Golin/Harris Communications. Award-winning military public affairs officer. Now a semi-retired public relationship consultant. Current and former board leadership with not-for-profit public service organizations in the health care, land conservation, and hunger relief fields. Speaker on communications issues before universities and professional organizations. Creator of the copyrighted Ebeling PR-ize at two universities, for team excellence in cause-related communications.

Charles Ebeling’s Specialties:

Designing and building PR-kitecture and public relationships: creative reputation management; crisis communications; PR agency selection and reviews; news media relationship strategies and execution; product publicity strategies; CEO positioning with target publics; communications strategies for not-for-profits; global corporate communications strategies and coordination; communications speeches before professional and educational organizations; travel writing and photography. Land conservation.
