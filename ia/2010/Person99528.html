Jean-Marie Bonthous’s Summary

TribeStrategist, an inbound marketing consultancy, helps client organizations build brand passion and improve new product development, sales, and customer loyalty.

SocialMarketingSpotlight, a project of TribeStrategist, is a free online resource for social marketing professionals, with a focus on influence marketing and social media monitoring.

I’ve been a trusted advisor to business leaders for 30 years, headed strategic planning initiatives for many Fortune 500 companies (expertise in strategy maps, balanced scorecard & scenario planning). I’ve led business transformation efforts for large-scale ERP implementations in the $50M – $100M range, and led countless performance improvement consulting projects.
I’ve directed many strategic communication consulting projects, managed reputational crises for several global organizations, and conducted brand strategy workshops for Coca-Cola brand managers on 3 continents.

- My team won the Silver Anvil, the highest PR distinction and won a Gold Medal and the Grand Award, the highest distinction, at the Int. Film and TV Festival of New York, for PR films.

- While a member of the board of directors of the Society of Competitive Intelligence Professionals, I wrote 2 books on business intelligence. They were published commercially and sold well for 10 years.

- In 2007, while running my management consultancy, I co-founded CREOPoint.com, the leading online forum for commercial real estate leaders worldwide, and served for 2 years as part-time CMO,

- I still provide “traditional” consulting services in business strategy and marketing under the umbrella of BrightMagnet, my other consulting practice.

- I hold a PhD in strategic management from the University of Paris, France, have taught business intelligence, competitive strategies, scenario planning and culture change at the Stern School of Business at New York University, and have addressed more industry conferences on these topics than I can remember.
