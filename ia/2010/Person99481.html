Jason Mallas’s Summary

I typed my high school papers in PageMaker, drew graphs in Illustrator 88, and printed them all out on a laser printer. The rest, as they say, is history...

I eat, sleep, and breathe design, production, printing, marketing & advertising. It's in my blood. (Pantone 186)

My career has truly "run the gamut" of positions in the industry. I've swept the shop floor, fed saddle-stichers, done "blood & guts" print production, managed a team of graphic artists, developed & maintained client relationships, and even trained current staff & prospective clients on implementing production solutions that directly impacted the bottom line. (In a GOOD way!)

Jason Mallas’s Specialties:

Internal & external software support, coaching & mentoring staff, training, graphics production & production management, Adobe Creative Suite applications, QuarkXPress, LEAN.
