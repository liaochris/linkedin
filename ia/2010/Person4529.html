AJ Egerton’s Summary

An established hard working retail professional with over 15 years in retail (10 of those spent in Consumer Electronics). I have made my journey through commissioned sales, softlines management, multiple Department Managers, Operations Manager, Regional Trainer for the Elite Team, Store Director (all previous positions with Circuit City) and currently a General Manager for hhgregg, Inc. "Big box" retailing is my passion, having worked and managed two $20+ million facilities. However, I am anxious to conquer my new position back with the consumer electronics industry. At my current leadership position I want to be able to share my story... educating my associates and managers about my experiences, my challenges, and of course my wins.
