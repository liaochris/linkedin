Candida Toccia Seasock’s Summary

Founder and President of CTS Partners, Ms. Seasock is a seasoned executive with technology and management consulting experience. The firm specializes in growth of technology companies. Several of her clients received Fastest-Growing Company awards of Philadelphia and South New Jersey. Based on Ms. Seasock’s experience as a strategic advisor, the Growth Path to Success is continuous business development, 360 marketing, strong client relationships and agile operational processes. She focuses on building value for mid-size companies through generation of business opportunities, identification of revenue streams, development of marketing cycles and sound management principles. She believes strongly in fostering long-term client relationships versus a transactional mentality. Ms Seasock works closely with her clients’ executive leadership teams to plan the identification, positioning, and pursuit of continuous growth. Her experience spans multiple disciplines from Fortune 500 corporations to emerging growth companies.
