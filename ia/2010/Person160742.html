Paul Ekon’s Summary

Paul Ekon, a private businessman, was with Management Technologies Inc. from 1995. Management Technologies Inc. is a company that develops, installs, and then supports software used in international banking. Its two main products are the OpenTrade family, including TradeWizard, and Abraxsys. While working at Management Technologies Inc, Paul Ekon promoted the notion of maintaining an experienced and lean board of directors, all of whom had hands on experience in the field.

Education

Paul Ekon was born in 1959 in Johannesburg, South Africa. After the death of his mother, he was sent to St. Stithians College to continue his studies. Upon graduation in 1976, he signed up for the South African Air Force. When he had completed two years of military service, he took a job with Paratus, where he worked as a photographer for the defense magazine.

Professional Photographer

As Paul Ekon’s love and knowledge of photography grew, he left South Africa for the United States and settled in Los Angeles, California. He took advantage of the movie studios, famous models, actors and actresses and the world of high fashion to learn and gain valuable photographic experience. After this period as a professional photographer, he returned to his native South Africa.

Business Interests

In South Africa Paul Ekon’s professional interests took a different direction. This time he delved into the food and beverage industry and the entertainment field. A fast learner, he quickly put his knowledge to use and opened several eating establishments in Johannesburg. Hot Tin Roof, Walnut Grove and Harpers are a few of the popular restaurants he opened in the city.

Continuing to build his business portfolio, Paul Ekon opened a company that sold door knobs and handles. He ran this business successfully until the arrival of cheaper products from China. He sold the business in 1995 and invested in Management Technologies Inc.
