Lori Denny’s Summary

I am a president and co-owner of a computer support company that has been in business in the Sarasota/Bradenton area since 1996. We assist customers with planning, installation, monitoring and maintenance (managed services) , and repairs of their computer and network systems. We are well known for our personalized and professional customer service, and have received most of our business from customer referrals. We make sure customers have the technology tools they need to run their businesses with maximum efficiency and reliability.

Our Chief Technology Officer and co-owner, Earl Denny, has 30+ years of experience in the fields of technical support, corporate network planning, computer repair, and training. I have technical and management experience in the technology field; my organizational and analytical skills are valuable in promoting and implementing new programs. Our goal is to attain steady company growth without compromising our high level of customer service.

On the personal side, I love the outdoors, animals, water, and my family. I would rather be in the country than the city, and I prefer seeing the moon and stars than the lights of Broadway. I was born in Bangor, ME and retain the down-to-earth attitudes, empathy and compassion of my New England family line. I enjoy meeting people who are open, honest and friendly, and do not have time for those with hurtful intentions or ulterior motives. I believe I am a good person, and I look forward to meeting other good people!

Lori Denny’s Specialties:

Our company is designated a Managed Services Provider (MSP) providing proactive monitoring and maintenance services for customer computer networks. These managed services allow us to monitor customer computers, servers, and networks 24/7 and give us the ability to anticipate problems occurring and fix them before they actually happen, which saves the customer valuable time and money. We provide computer solutions that make sense for our customers!
