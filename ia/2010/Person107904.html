Judy Taylor’s Summary

I have been a successful business person in a large staffing company for almost 25 years. I have now embarked on a new venture to involve my passion for pets. In my corporate life, I had occassion to travel for businesss and for pleasure and finding care for my beloved pets has always been a major priority. I have utilized in home pet care so my animals could stay as close to a normal routine as possible and have been intrigued by the business for some time.

I have formed Shasta's Shepherds to provide quality in home care to pets in the Ahwatukee area of Phoenix.

I am proud to conduct business predicated on one primary rule: focus my business every day in a manner that will glorify our Lord and Savior.. . . Be honest, treat people as I want to be treated, do the right thing.
