Peter Wung’s Summary

Research and development engineer in electric machinery design and analysis. Emphasis on creative and innovative designs incorporating finite element analysis and other computer aided designs of electromechanical energy conversion devices. Doctorate in Electrical Engineering with emphasis in electromagnetics, electric machinery design and analysis, and modern control techniques.

Peter Wung’s Specialties:

Induction motor design and analysis, PM motor design and analysis, Switched Reluctance motor design and analysis. FEA and SPEED software experience.
