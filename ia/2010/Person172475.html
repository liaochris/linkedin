Rick Haas’s Summary

Turn-Around Experience – Ford South American Operations
- Six years of executive engineering experience in Ford South American Operations (FSAO) as the Chief Body & Electrical Engineer, managing a team of 150 engineers.
- One of 6 Ford Product Development executives sent to Brasil to lead the $1.6 billion FSAO turn-around plan, reversing a string of annual losses to emerge as a leading regional profit center.
- FSAO gained 5 points of market share and posted record profits in the South American region during this period through the design, development and launch of a family of class-leading, value-oriented B-Class vehicles, manufactured in an all-new manufacturing facility in Camacari, Brasil.
- Directed the creation and implementation of a full-service engineering organization and B-car "Center of Excellence" to sustain this performance in the region.

- Supplier management
- Launch and post launch activities
- Senior management reviews and progress updates
