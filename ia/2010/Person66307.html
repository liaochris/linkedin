Eric Pratum’s Summary

Currently, I focus on client social media and analytics, socialization of client content (eg. I help get community buy in on your latest campaign, mission, etc), and big picture strategies for online client marketing campaigns. I work with Grizzard clients to not only understand social media and carry out successful campaigns, but also to improve their brand awareness, cultivation tracks, and opportunities for two-way communication between the donor and the nonprofit.

Previously, I was the Analytics Program Manager for Microsoft’s Windows, Internet Explorer, Office, Bing, and Zune while at Seattle's Spring Creek Group. As well, I was the lead social media analyst for our Razorfish partnership and have worked with companies small and large from the founder of Starbucks (and coincidentally another of Seattle's powerful social media agencies) to the local coffee shop as an independent consultant. Prior to this experience, I managed e-commerce, web analytics, and two websites for Hilton. I began my career as a startup researcher in the angel investing world, while also studying for my MBA and masters in German.

I have significant work experience in:
– Google Analytics, Omniture, and many other web analytics tools
– AdWords, AdSense, and other PPC
– TubeMogul, YouTube, & the rest of the online video worls
– Visible Technologies, Radian6, SM2, & the whole social media monitoring toolset
– Twitter, Facebook, Forums, & Blogging
– Social media management and outreach

I blog professionally for Grizzard.com and personally at EricPratum.com. If you email me, I will respond. Work: eric.pratum@grizzard.com. Personal: contact@ericpratum.com.
