Barbara Steinberg’s Summary

Currently, I am the Site Moderator at a fantastic professional B2B community of doctors and other health care advocates called Integrative Practitioner.

For five and a half years before that, I was involved in the teen MMOG world, with my primary issue being safety both with Moshi Monsters and Habbo Hotels all around the English-speaking world.

In June 2004, I was hired as a moderator at Sulake Labs Habbo Hotel, where I educated members about safety, trained other moderators, and contributed to a radio station project, HabboHut Radio, which fulfilled the promise of my masters thesis, Radio Free Monterey. On July 1, 2007, I was promoted to Community Assistant for Safety and Education at Sulake. I was a member of a 4-person elite Community Management team that reported to Finland Headquarters. On May 1, 2008, I was moved to be involved in in-game campaigns, moderation projects, and player support to Sulake North America to broaden my experience with the company. In February, 2009, I marked my 4-year, 9-month anniversary with Sulake.

I have been building communities for 15 years in both entrepreneurial and corporate projects. My masters thesis for the Interactive Telecommunications Program at NYU, was the first audio/visual chat community on the Internet, Radio Free Monterey. I then had the privilege of working with some of the best CIOs in the business at Computerworld Executive Suite.

I am also writing a book about my online life called "What Have You Seen Outside Your Window?"

You can find me in Laredo, TX, with my 5 pets: Harry (red-lored amazon parrot), Grace, Lucy, Alice, and Sarah (cats).
