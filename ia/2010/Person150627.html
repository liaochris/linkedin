Myles Halsband’s Summary

Here I am in 1955 at 18 Marion Road, Marblehead, MA with my buzz cut from Dick’s Barber Shop on Humphrey St in Swampscott, MA. . Dick was a short Italian guy. His three sons, all much taller, worked with him. I believe it’s still there.

I graduated Marblehead High in 1963 and went on to UC, Berkeley, growing long hair, smoking pot and marching in picket lines behind Joan Baez and Bob Dylan.

I came back east, taught English in a inner-city school, then fourth grade, preschool, started an alternative school in Marblehead, MA and then taught in another one in Natick, MA. In the midst of all this I worked at the toy department at the Harvard Coop, produced cable television children’s shows, sold pagers, PCs and Novell networks.

I started my own cable television company, founded a technology which delivered on-site advertising to retail stores, and built three different ERP consulting practices, two of which were sold to other companies.

I’ve done door-door sales, cold calls, marketing campaigns, database and ERP consulting, and whatever else it took to get a job done.

I’d welcome a chance to meet, talk buzz cuts from Dick the Barber, and tell you what I mean by "more than just the buzz".
