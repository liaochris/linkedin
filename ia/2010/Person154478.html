Nicolas Roberge’s Summary

Nicolas Roberge is a professional software developer of 11 years of experience during which he diversified his expertise by working with different technologies. He specialized most of his career in web development by working on multiples projects of all sizes. He is now specialized to integrate seamlessly cloud SaaS Internet applications to existing software infrastructures.
