Pam Claughton’s Summary

I have over 15 years of recruiting experience in the greater Boston area. My firm, Custom Search Group, Inc. is headquartered at 18 Main St. Ext. in Plymouth, MA and we also have an office in Boston's financial district, at 101 Federal St. We offer direct hire and contract/temporary staffing for technical, marketing/sales and administrative positions.

We have a team of senior level recruiters, all with over ten years of local experience. This means that we have a vast network to tap into for our searches and if you are candidate looking for your next career move, we have many long-standing and exclusive relationships with top companies.

We are also technology and research focused, meaning we leverage the newest technologies including social media to broaden our reach. In addition, we specialize in targeted competitive research and cold-calling into competitors to direct recruit and generate quality referrals of passive candidates.

Our clients range from exciting, VC funded start-ups to established market leaders. Niche areas that we focus in and have a strong passion for, include e-commerce, virtualization, internet security and cloud computing.

If you are thinking of engaging in a search, and would like to hear about opportunities that might match with your search objectives, please feel free to forward your resume to me in confidence.
