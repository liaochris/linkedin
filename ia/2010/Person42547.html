Colleen Carrington’s Summary

Highlights from references:
• "one of the brightest minds in marketing and business I've met in many years,” (2009)
• “highly dynamic speaker who is both experienced and knowledgeable,”
• “technology leader.”

Colleen has more than 10 years of experience in marketing, communications, and project management. She is a customer-focused, results-driven leader who has a proven track record of successfully managing multi-million dollar projects to drive and increase ROI. Colleen has outstanding analytical, strategic planning, and execution skills. She is especially interested in voice of the customer initiatives, online marketing, and building brand communities through social media.

Colleen served on the Board of Directors of the American Marketing Association, and has worked as a Marketing Consultant, the Director of Marketing for the QCBC, an Account Supervisor at BBDO Worldwide, and in various roles in client services, media and production. She also has expertise as an educator which strengthens her ability to ensure messaging is meaningful and relevant to the target audience. Colleen is an energetic, dynamic, creative individual who is able to manage and motivate others.

Throughout her career, she has been passionate about the pursuit of excellence. Colleen recently finished the 'Marketing Management Extension Program' at UW and attended the 'Online Marketing Summit' in Seattle where she sharpened her skills in strategic planning, digital marketing and social media.

During her free time, Colleen enjoys spending time with family and friends, travel, photography, cycling, reading, cooking and volunteering in the community. (Status: Permanent US Resident)

Colleen Carrington’s Specialties:

Marketing management, digital marketing, integrated marketing communications, social media, event planning and project management.
