David Tawil’s Summary

Currently, principal of ETG Capital Advisors, the investment manager to a family of investment funds focused on making deep value-oriented investments in distressed and high-yield assets, including bank-debt, bonds and trade credit.

An entrepreneur in high-risk trade credit. Trained and practiced as a bankruptcy and restructuring attorney.

Co-product founder and head and of trade-receivables hedging product; skills include credit evaluation, sales and marketing, trading and hedging, documentation and closing.

David Tawil’s Specialties:

    ETG Capital

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    October 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    I specialize in investing in high-risk and distressed situations, with a particular focus on trade debt. With Steven Azarbad, I have founded ETG Capital.

    ETG Capital is a provider of creative credit solutions to companies dealing with customers of high-risk. ETG is primarily focused on protecting vendors to customers experiencing the greatest distress. ETG professionals have over 20 years of experience in corporate bankruptcy, restructuring and credit underwriting.

    ETG takes a conservative approach to capitalization, underwriting and capital preservation, emphasizing diversification and substantial capital reserves (50% or greater). With minimal organizational constraints, ETG has the flexibility to underwrite some of the most difficult accounts and broaden the scope of the A/R put option market.
