Geert Overdam’s Summary

For the last more than 30 years I've been working in the field of art-education and art-management. I started my professional carreer in 1971 as cultural officer of the city of Vlissingen in the south-west of the Netherlands. From 1980 - 1990 I was working in theatre management in the Province of Zeeland in the Netherlands. From 1990-1992, I was working as general director of Cultural Centre the Marienburg in Arnhem, after the Marienburg I was in charge of the department of art-education with the LOKV, the national institute for art education. I worked there from 1992- 1996.
My next job was at the Fund of Performing Arts, I worked there from 1996-2001. From 2001-2003 I was working as a consultant in art and art-education and from 2003 till now I'm general and artistic director of Theaterfestival Boulevard and general director of Stichting Bosse Nova, both at 's-Hertogenbosch

Geert Overdam’s Experience

  • Artistic and general director

    Theaterfestival Boulevard

    (Entertainment industry)

    March 2003 — Present (7 years 3 months)

    Every summer Theaterfestival Boulevard treats lovers of theatre, music and dance to a 10 day journey of discovery through the world of the arts. The festival has grown into one of the biggest art festivals in the Netherlands. Annually some 135.000 visitors make their way to the festival. The atmospheric heart of the festival lies at the foot of St. Jan’s Cathedral in the historic centre of ‘s-Hertogenbosch, but the productions, concerts and projects take place all over the city.

    The festival has developed a new profile in which the focus is on outstanding artists and innovative projects from North Brabant, the rest of the Netherlands and Dutch-speaking Belgium/Flanders and also from all over the world. The festival likes to make an artistic contribution to the artistic climate and to the national and international exposure of the city. Just as important is the festival’s support in helping talented artists reach the biggest possible audience.
