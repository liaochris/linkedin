Ken Faulkenberry, MBA - Investments’s Summary

** I never DNK and invite connections with those having an interest in investments or portfolio management. My company is dedicated to helping Investors manage their own money!**

I believe in individual freedom and responsibility, free markets, and peace through strength. America is the best place on earth to take advantage of the vast opportunities granted to us by God. Our system allows us the opportunity to properly plan and achieve financial independence.

I graduated from the University of California (USC) Marshall School of Business, MBA program in 1986, with an emphasis in Investments.From 1986 - 2007 I worked as the General Partner of a Limited Partnership investing in real estate and equities in which I sold our holdings near the top of the California Real Estate Boom.

During this period I helped family and friends with their investment planning. Because they experienced success, these people encouraged me to develop a service to help others! I spent many years developing and honing the Arbor Asset Allocation Model Portfolio (AAAMP) for the individual investor who wants to manage their own investments.

After many years of building an outstanding record and implementing changes to make the service investor friendly I began marketing the Arbor Investment Planner. The service t advocates sound conservative principles, discipline, and diversification through proper asset allocation.

More Information available at: www.ArborInvestmentPlanner.com
