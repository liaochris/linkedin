Martin Lowenthal’s Summary

I was one of the youngest ever members of the Johannesburg Stock Exchange at 23 and one of the founding members of the equity options , financial futures and commodity exchanges in South Africa.

I spent 10 years as a partner at a boutique investment bank where I oversaw the nationwide trading desk that serviced institutional investors and high net worth individuals. I advised and executed equity and derivative strategies based on both fundamental and technical analysis. I also ran one the largest proprietary trading desks at the same firm.

The firm also helped small to medium size enterprises access capital with mezzanine finance, IPO and asset securitization and general financial advisory. I was also actively involved in helping these companies with corporate strategy, structure, M&A and other key managerial issues. The firm listed the first internet company in SA as well as sponsoring the first asset securitization.

I was a regular markets commentator on national radio, Bloomberg and Reuters.

I established the Boston office for a nationwide broker dealer that specialized in sophisticated electronic equity trading platforms and sales trading service to institutional investors. Firm was sold to RBC.

I now help people change their lives and make them financially secure through holistic financial advisory.

I have a Bachelor of Commerce from the University of Cape Town as well as an MS in Investment Management from Boston University. I am an active angel investor with a special focus on internet and technology. I am also a mentor for the Babson MBA EIT program.

I have FINRA 7,24,55 and 63 licenses. Life, accident and health insurance.
