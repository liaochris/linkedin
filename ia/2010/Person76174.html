Glen Wilson’s Summary

I have over ten years of experience in the professional services industry, primarily within the Information Technology domain, working with both large and small businesses. During this time I have worked in various capacities ranging from IT consulting, business development and human resources/recruiting. My hands-on technical experience has allowed me to effectively recruit all levels of technology professionals within infrastructure and development environments. In addition, my general business experience has enabled me to successfully recruit for non-technical and management positions (e.g., finance, operations, and sales).

I enjoy continually building new relationships. It is from these relationships that I have been able to stay on top of the latest developments in technology as well as the best practices in business. If you are currently searching for a new opportunity, whether it be a career or a discussion on the latest trends in technology/business, feel free to drop me an email at glen.wilson [at] aquilent [dot] com. I look forward to hearing from you!
