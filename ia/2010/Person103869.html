Jonathan Hughes’s Summary

Jonathan is the founder and strategic business partner for Abray Learning, consulting to Fortune 500 companies for over fifteen years. He specializes in awakening teams to reach their full potential; creating extraordinary results through strategic planning, leadership development, team performance improvement and individual coaching.

Jonathan is unique in bringing both business results and organizational effectiveness perspectives to the organizations with which he works.

Jonathan brings to his consultancy an unparalleled combination of business experience and leadership & team development expertise.

Jonathan Hughes’s Specialties:

Business experience includes production management, supply chain, product management, customer support, information technology, solution sales and project management.

Leadership & team expertise include development of strategic charter and planning programs, qualification to facilitate self-awareness and team collaboration interventions, an MBA with a specialisation in leadership, certification in adult training & development, and an ongoing commitment to personal life-long learning.
