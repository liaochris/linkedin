Robin Hensley - Business Development Coach’s Summary

rhensley[at]raisingthebar[com] All invitations to connect are welcome, no IDK.

Robin Hensley is a business development coach specializing in coaching attorneys and CPAs that are at the top of their game to maximize their rainmaking skills.

Robin was with Ernst & Young for five years serving on both the audit and consulting staffs and the Atlanta law firms of Kilpatrick Stockton and Swift Currie in both marketing and business development roles for more than six years.

Robin’s distinguished board service includes serving on the Northside Hospital board for 10 years. She currently sits on the board of directors of public company, Superior Uniform Group where she chairs the audit committee and serves on the compensation committee of this NASDAQ company.

Named by Atlanta Business Chronicle as one of Atlanta's Top 20 Self-Made Women, Robin’s record as a business development coach has been featured in numerous publications including the Atlanta Business Chronicle, The Atlanta Journal-Constitution and Daily Report. She is also the Exclusive Executive Coach for Atlanta’s Business to Business magazine.

Before starting her coaching practice, Robin held the following positions:

Professional Services Marketer and Coach: Robin served for seven years in professional services marketing and business development coaching roles for two widely recognized law firms, Kilpatrick & Stockton and Swift and Currie, McGhee & Hiers.

Corporate Vice President and Principal: Robin was one of the few women internationally to serve as a Vice President and Principal of Marketing and Business Development for a major industrial and commercial general contractor.

Entrepreneur: Robin founded and grew a highly regarded business services company, achieving 20% annual growth in sales each of the eight years she was president.

CPA and Consultant: Robin was with Ernst & Young for five years serving on both the audit and consulting staffs and earning her CPA certificate.
