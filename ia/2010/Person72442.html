Gail Holmberg’s Summary

A consultant and senior executive who drives change to create business value out of messy and/or ambiguous situations across a wide variety of industries. Gail Holmberg is President of Blue Morpho Consulting. She specializes in back office/headquarters improvement projects across HR, Finance, Call Centers, Management Reporting and Information Technology. These projects include business process outsourcing, business process optimization, cost reduction assessment, acquisition integration, divestiture separation management, creation and consolidation of shared services, systems implementation and integration, and infrastructure cost reduction.

Former CIO, divisional CIO, shared services manager, systems development manager and financial analyst with experience in consumer manufacturing, consumer retail, consumer services, B2B sales and distribution. professional services, government and not-for-profit industries.

A believer in creating networking forums to assist professionals in sharing information and experience. Gives back to the profession through leadership in professional and networking organizations. Gives back to the community through service on not-for-profit boards and committees.

Gail Holmberg’s Specialties:

Business transformation, technology transformation, CIO advisory services, technology strategy development, business process optimization and outsourcing, technology insight, ERP project turnarounds, risk assessments, technology cost reduction, corporate reorganization, mergers, acquisitions, divestitures and spin offs, SOX regulatory compliance, PCI regulatory compliance and Chapter 11.
