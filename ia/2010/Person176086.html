Rob Woollen’s Summary

I am an experienced wellness consultant working with organisations of all sizes from small IT training and design companies to universities and government departments employing thousands of staff. I write extensively on the subject of corporate wellness and have been published in the likes of Occupational Health, Personnel Today and HR Zone. I also wrote and delivered the first corporate wellness training course for personal trainers to be endorsed by Skillsactive (the sector skills council for health and fitness).

I was proud to be a finalist in the 2009 Manchester Entrepreneur of the Year awards. I sit on the advisory board of Manchester Metropolitan University Business School, and am a member of the RSPH, CIPD, ITOL and the North West Health and Physical Activity Forum.


Professionally, my mission is to make wellness a norm in the workplace. There are so many ways in which we can do things with wellness in mind- often at little or no cost. This way we can build a productive and motivated workforce to take us round the corner and into the upturn after recession.

The first personal trainer in Manchester to be recognised at level 4 by Skillsactive (REPS), I continue to update my knowledge of personal training and maintain an small, exclusive client base. I completed my first Ironman triathlon in 2009 and will be back to beat 13.5 hours this year.
