Xavier Keeling’s Summary

Having worked in the Design and New media industry for over ten years, I am now enjoying running a small design consultancy specialising in moving image and animation and cutting edge online interactive Brand Experiences. We have extensive skills and knowledge and are becoming increasingly popular with ad agencies to provide extra creative flare and polish to projects.
My skills cover much of the digital production suites and have extensive experience of directing interactive projects for many big names.
I have a passion for animation and it is my dream to develop long term animation projects for the web and television.
Bristol has been a fantastic place to work and I am keen to see it firmly on the interactive media map.
