Tim Perry’s Summary

“If at first, the idea is not absurd, then there is no hope for it.”
--Albert Einstein

I believe that success comes to those who look outside their comfort areas for the disruption in technology, business models, and markets. Any successful business must either cultivate a climate for such innovation or be able to buy their way into those innovations. It is more fun to pioneer and create them!

In my 20 years of experience I have relished those roles and cultures that reward innovative, broad-thinking business executives. In those years, I have developed a proven track record of understanding emerging technology and business disruptions, relying on partnerships inside and outside, with some of the following results:

•Phased infrastructure integration for $12 Billion merger to produce delivery of services while complying with outstanding contractual obligations through unique application of technology and business processes,
•Avoided hundreds of millions of dollars in stent inventory expiration through mobile inventory management with emerging vendors,
•Planning and performance for 600+ servers around the world to create a seamless experience for enterprise business users & FDA qualification,
•Introduction of emerging technologies to enterprise (e.g., Blackberry, Instant Messaging, Social Networking),
•Infrastructure Architecture practice thru industry standard frameworks & benchmarking with leading companies
•Design For Six Sigma, including redesign of entire enterprise email system to enhance end user satisfaction, ensure stable and scalable performance, and reduce costs.
•Assembled, cultivated, and managed teams of 4 direct and 100+ indirect/matrixed reports around the world
•Budget / profit & loss responsibility for products in shared services for the enterprise (10's of millions of dollars for expense and capital).
