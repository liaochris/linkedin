Gary S. Barnhart’s Summary

The GSB Design Group, is a Landscape Architectural Planning and Design Firm offering a wide range of Interior and Exterior Design/Development Consulting Services of both public and private clients. These clients consist primarily of developers, cities and municipalities, architects, other landscape architectural firms, engineers, school districts, homeowners, and contractors.

To date, we have accrued over 35 years experience in the profession of Landscape Architecture and have been involved in a variety of projects primarily in the areas of large scale residential development, streetscape and median design, accessible recreation/park and open space planning, urban planning and design, housing and high end residential planning and design, school campus planning, master planning, regional shopping centers, botanic gardens, commercial office buildings, signage and site graphics, detailed site design and construction documents, specifications, and construction management. We also have written as contributing editors for several national trade publications. In addition, periodically we are invited speakers and seminar leaders for many national trade shows and professional conventions.

We remain committed to the concept of producing the most functional, aesthetic and cost effective design solutions, within a client's set budget and time frame.

Our projects continually involve a team approach consisting of the client, other consultants and agencies, contractors, and ourselves. Communication, coordination and a knowledge of all project elements between each of the team 'players' inevitably leads to the production and implementation of many successful projects.

Registered/Licensed Landscape Architect in Colorado and Kansas via the U.N.E. (Uniform National Exam) and C.L.A.R.B. file (Council of Landscape Architectural Registration Board)

Gary S. Barnhart’s Specialties:

General landscape architectural design & site planning, outdoor recreation planning, interior landscape design, plant material tagging & aquisition, construction documents and specifications, project budgeting and detailed cost estimates, construction administration & observation, project management.
