Don Tinney’s Summary

Don has a 30+ year history of helping entrepreneurial organizations grow. He has functioned as an owner, leader and manager in three successful companies and is now a partner with Gino Wickman in growing EOS Worldwide, an abundance-minded organization that exists to help business consultants, coaches, trainers and leaders to succeed in strengthening entrepreneurial leadership teams and organizations. They do that using a train the trainer model, supplying a complete business system with a comprehensive set of simple tools. (visit www.eosworldwide.com to learn more)

Don Tinney’s Specialties:

Don has invested most of his life into drawing growth-oriented people into environments of collaboration. He is particularly gifted at building teams, understands how to make businesses work, and is a skilled teacher, facilitator and coach.

Don Tinney’s Experience

  • Owner, VP

    EOS Worldwide, LLC

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    November 2005 — Present (5 years 1 month)

    EOS Worldwide is a growing organization of successful entrepreneurial business coaches, consultants, trainers and business leaders, collaborating to help other entrepreneurs succeed.

    EOS, the Entrepreneurial Operating System, is a complete, thoroughly proven system that strengthens the Six Key Components™ of entrepreneurial organizations, harnessing all the moving parts of a business and focusing all energy and resources in one meaningful direction. Clarity replaces chaos, simplicity replaces complexity, everything becomes more controlled, profit and cash flow improve, and the organization advances as a cohesive team with tremendous traction.

    EOS Worldwide employs a train the trainer model, working with business consultants, coaches, trainers and leaders who want to teach, facilitate and coach leadership teams of small to medium size businesses.

    To learn more, visit www.eosworldwide.com. If you are an entrepreneurial business coach, trainer or consultant using EOS, consider joining the EOS Traction Group here in LinkedIn.

  • CEO/Corporate Manager

    Preferred Carlson Realtors

    (Real Estate industry)

    July 1995 — June 2003 (8 years )

    Preferred Carlson Realtors was a residential and commercial real estate company, with mortgage and title services, located in Kalamazoo, Michigan. As the Corporate Manager/CEO and business partner, Don was responsible for overseeing the marketing, sales, operations, IT and HR functions of the company. In 8 years, Don led the campaign that grew the company from 65 agents to 235 agents, 1 location to 6 locations, 1 manager to a leadership team of 8 and $154 million in sales to over $500 million in sales. Don purchased this company with a partner in 2003 and merged it with another company to create Prudential Preferred, REALTORS, the 4th largest real esate company in Michigan, exceeding $900 million in sales.
