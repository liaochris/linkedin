David Helgason’s Summary

I have served as CEO of the game technology company Unity Technologies since co-founding it in 2003. The vision is to democratize game development, and develop technology for the next generation of the industry. From in-browser MMO, through mobile, to social, casual, serious, and beyond. The most amazing thing is that we're pulling it off: Unity is used by dozens of big game publishes and media companies, hundreds of smaller studios, and thousands of independent professionals, hobbyists, students, and 14 year old boys.

My background is in software, and an assortment of university dabbling. I created and participated in several startups during and around the .com's, in areas such as early experiments with news & community integration, music business content distribution, and IT-consulting for a variety of local and global companies. I currently serve on the boards of serious game studio Serious Games Interactive, and fashion-technology startup World On A Hanger.

David Helgason’s Specialties:

Game industry entrepreneur and technologist with a passion for figuring out stuff, and making it work (like, building businesses and applying new and old business models to a changing industry).
