Liang-Jie (LJ) Zhang’s Summary

I love BEST (Business Evolution and Services Transformation)! I use this network to maintain professional connections with friends, colleagues and students.

I am the founding chair of the IEEE and IBM Reserach's Services Computing community, which addresses how IT and computing technology help perform business services more efficiently and effectively. I was the Chief Architect of Industry Standards at IBM Software Group and the Technical Assistant to the VP and CTO of IBM Global Industrial Sector for integrating technology strategy and milestones for individual industries (Automotive Industry, PLM, and Electronics Industry) and across industries to support business goals.
