John H. Valenti’s Summary

Although much of my recent work has been in live event production, I like to dip the quill in ink as often as possible. I've written for television, new media, magazines, newspapers, blogs, and regular online publications. I also currently have several screenplays in various stages of development. I'm currently writing (and living) a novel.

So, Linkedin people... Linkers, if I may, I would love to share with you samples of my writing, which has alternately and simultaneously been described as: "Provoking," Funny," "Brilliant," "Vindictive," "Smelly," and "Mostly Readable."

The "smelly" was given by a second-grader, whom I consider my most valuable critic.
