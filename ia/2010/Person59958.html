Kathleen King’s Summary

Prolific, educational expert recognized for her unique expertise in combining her knowledge of adult education with technology, online learning, digital media and publications for faculty and staff development, and educational innovations.

Dr. King helps professionals transform their careers and lives. Many conference planning groups and faculty are interested in her topics of Engaging the 21st Century Learner, Developing Distance Education Courses and Programs, Facilitating Online Learning, Bringing Transformative Learning to Life, Podcasting for Teaching and Learning, and Faculty Tenure Success.

Kathy says, "I enjoy empowering professionals to realize the many opportunities for new professional inspiration, discoveries and lifelong learning."

Dr. Kathy King is a professor at a major research university and president of a consulting firm. She has worked across corporate and non-profit organizations and settings as well as all grades and most content levels k-12, adult education, training, higher education. Transformation Education, LLC is as a dynamic vehicle for her message of empowerment and success.

King often says,
"From coaching, keynoting, program consulting to training- we are the "Go To" people for embracing professional success.
Help us, help you; call us today to discuss your goals and challenges."
