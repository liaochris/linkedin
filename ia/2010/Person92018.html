Burton Lee’s Summary

Lee Biosolutions has an incredible dedicated and talented team of scientists that work closely with our partners in the diagnostic industry and clinical researchers around the world helping them with thier efforts with earlier detection of clinical diseases and conditions.

Specialize in the ability to purify rare proteins for the IVD Medical and Pharmaceutical Industry. Production and purification of enzyme(s), antigen(s) and related protein(s) from animal and human source by classical chromatography.

Burton Lee’s Specialties:

Cardiovascular Disease: Purification of protein marker creatine kinase MB, LD isoenzmes,Troponin's,C-reactive Protein and MHC,MLC

Isolation of Neutrophil proteins : Myeloperoxidase isoforms, elastase, PR3, EPO, ECP.

cGMP production of crystalline lactic acid as an excipient for the pharmaceuticals

Biological collections from a large donor base for semen, vaginal secretions, urine and saliva

Animal and human tissue, blood and plasma Protein purification by classical chromatography
