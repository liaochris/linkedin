Gary Ranftle’s Summary

Gary’s 20+ year career in the financial services arena has afforded him with a vast spectrum of experience in sales, management, and executive capacities. He began his Financial Services Career with Connecticut General and CIGNA Financial Advisors and started directing his talents towards management and agency growth in the mid and late 1990’s. While affiliated with Lincoln Financial Advisors (the successor to CIGNA Financial Advisors) he was the Managing Principal of the New York / New Jersey agencies and orchestrated their growth and development. During his tenure, NY/NJ was consistently one of the top 3 agencies in the Lincoln network.

Today, Gary has taken on the challenge of leading and developing the quintessential fee-based Financial Planning organization whose focus is to foster an environment dedicated to the growth and development of exceptional financial planners and providing the client with comprehensive financial services needed to help them take control of their financial situation.

Gary insists on a high level of service and professionalism. Gary has lectured at numerous conferences and seminars on topics such as the use of technology in your business and incorporating Financial Planning fees into your practice.

Gary is an active member of the General Agents Association, the Financial Planning Association, the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors and is currently enrolled in the Certified Financial Planning Program at the College of Financial Planning. Gary holds a BA from Potsdam State University.

Gary P. Ranftle is a registered representative of and offers securities, investment advisory and financial planning services through MML Investors Services, Inc. Member SIPC http://www.sipc.org/
