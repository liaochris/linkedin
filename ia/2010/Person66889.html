Erik Vonk’s Summary

Founder of BOTH or 'Back Of The House'.
BOTH provides every support function that any self-employed professional, freelancer or solo practitioner needs to be successful.
The integrated set of services permanently empowers self-employed professionals to work for themselves, not by themselves; BOTH fortifies each client with a hosted and supported IT environment, internet banking, health and retirement benefits and a Personal Advisor who handles all operational and administrative hassles that commonly challenge anyone who works solo.
BOTH simply and cost effectively transforms self-employment into a fully supported and professionally configured 'Business of One'.
