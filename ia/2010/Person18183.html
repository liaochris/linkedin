Adam Ailion’s Summary

After growing up in the real estate industry and seeing his father run a Brokerage Company, Adam decided to join forces with his father creating the Ailion Team. It was his familiarity of the industry that allowed Adam to acquire his real estate license at age 19. He continues to learn about the real estate industry on a daily basis by attending seminars to better his skills, dealing with clients, and observing the market’s many ups and downs. Adam specializes primarily with home buyers, lease terminations of tenants, real estate investors, and foreclosed properties. He also is an aspiring real estate investor with two rental units in his own portfolio.


* Adam graduated Kennesaw State University Cum Laude with a Bachelor Degree of Business Administration concentrating in Marketing.
* Adam plans to attend UGA or Georgia State University to receive his Masters Degree in Real Estate and Urban Affairs.
* Adam is a perfectionist always paying attention to detail while improving his knowledge and efficiency.
* Adam is dedicated and determined to completing tasks in a proficient and punctual manner.
* Adam possesses an outgoing, friendly, trustworthy, go-getter winning attitude with the willingness to “do whatever it takes”.
* Adam is a continual learner with a work ethic to always improve his work.
* Adam was born and raised in Marietta, Georgia, attended Walton High School in East Cobb, and knows the Cobb County market and Atlanta area very well.
