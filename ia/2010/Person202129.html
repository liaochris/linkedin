Michelle Price’s Summary

We assess your existing social connectivity, and develop strategies that increase your influence within your target market online using the principles of online thought leadership to distribute your ideas, integrating the best tools of social media to accelerate the process.

I have a special affinity for women authors who operate service businesses (consultants, coaches, speakers, CPA, chiropractors, etc.) with expertise in niche business or personal growth topics and have published a book to establish credibility and want a "quantum leap" in internet visibility and generate higher quality leads and prospects for services, programs and products.

We integrate best practices in social marketing to establish an expert platform and execute a laser-focused market strategy that attracts targeted leads that turn into profits more quickly.

We also take existing content and turn into online passive income streams.
