Karen Grace’s Summary

I am a Residential Interior Designer and a Commercial Office Interior Designer. I enjoy working with entrepreneurs to create offices that are professional and express who this person is in business. My designs touch the senses and create an experience of emotion through color, shape,textures and patterns. My specialty is working with people who have gone through life changing experiences move on with their lives through the designs I create for them. I do this by working with individuals in their home or by designing or redecorating Senior care facilities. My commercial clients are entrepreneurs such as attorneys, CPA's, the financial services industry, the medical profession, the natural healing profession and psychotherapists.

Karen Grace’s Specialties:

Space and furniture planning, window treatments, flooring, accessorizing a room, remodeling kitchens and bathrooms and color consultations.
