Randy Bias’s Summary

The Cloudscaling team is led by me, Randy Bias, a Cloud computing pioneer. I built one of the world's first cloud computing management systems (CloudScale Networks) and most recently was VP Technology Strategy at GoGrid, one of the top three U.S. clouds.

Cloudscaling focuses on delivering strategy, architecture, and implementation solely for cloud computing systems. Given the scope of cloud computing, this means we have a full bench of cloud experts with both breadth and depth of expertise from infrastructure to applications.

CURRENTLY: Providing architecture and cloud strategy consulting for a number of cloud computing pioneers:

* GoGrid / ServePath (#3 in U.S. for cloud infrastructure)
* VMware (#1 in U.S. for virtualization & cloud infrastructure software)
* Kaiser Permanente (The largest managed health care organization in the U.S.)
* EngineYard (#1 in U.S. for Ruby on Rails platform infrastructure)
* CloudCentral (#2 in Australia for cloud infrastructure)

CONTACT US if you have an interest in engaging our cloud strategy services. More can be found on our website:

* http://cloudscaling.com

Randy Bias’s Specialties:

Exceptionally deep knowledge of cloud computing and all of it's facets. Recognized thought leader, widely read blog, and cloud twitterer. Provide exceptional value in a short period of time.
