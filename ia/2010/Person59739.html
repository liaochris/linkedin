Drew Mouton’s Summary

Drew Mouton is a serial entrepreneur, strategy consultant, investment manager and private equity investor. In recent years he has provided start-up management and strategic growth consulting for businesses in the financial services, Internet, science and technology, real estate and retail sectors, while acting as managing director for a closely-held private equity firm. He has been involved as a founder and c-level manager or acquirer of over a dozen companies, most recently in the technology, defense, construction and manufacturing industries, and has provided transaction oriented consulting on many more. He has been personally responsible for the acquisition of tens of millions of dollars in contracts with clients ranging from large enterprise to the Federal government and United States military. Currently a Managing Director or active Board member of several growing companies, he was recognized by CitiBusiness Magazine as a Power Generation Award recipient, by K∑ with a national Leadership award, and is a graduate in both the liberal arts and sciences from Tulane University.
