Tim Nichols’s Summary

My most recent background is working on HP-UX and Linux backends for Customer Service CTI and IVR and applications.

These days I do a lot of work with the Genesys IVR suite, including their T-Server, ERS, Framework, and VCS systems. I am currently heavily involved in a large multi-site call center implementation.

I also regularly do operations support of HP-UX systems, and Oracle database systems.

I have strong software development background, working primarily in the scripting arena, and some dabbling in Java and C/C++. I have experience producing some rather large scripted products which were designed to streamline software development and system administration tasks. Some projects include a software development and deployment process automation; in-depth application monitoring and SLA reporting tools; application transaction performance monitoring and reporting; data aggregation and reporting; etc.

I spent a couple years managing mail infrastructure, directory services, and IPSec remote access for a large internet retailer in Seattle (is there more than one? :) ). There was a lot of custom coding and sendmail config design work involved.

I spent several years working in the medical records department of the WSU College of Veterinary Medicine. During my time there I played a key role in implementing a 4GL medical records database.
