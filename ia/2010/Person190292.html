Jerry Pratt’s Summary

Jerry Pratt has 20+ years of producing, shooting and editing experience which has taken him around the world. Being a collaborator, he has served a great variety of people & entities – from world class directors like Gus Van Sant & Jim Blashfield to Fortune 500 companies like Nike, Intel & Starbucks. The bulk of his experience has been amassed at nationally renown PBS affiliates like WGBH in Boston, Arizona Public Media & Oregon Public Broadcasting. This past year he served the New York Director, Ekwa Msangi-Omari, in Nairobi, Kenya for a dramatic cable television series (M-Net.)

As a savvy producer, cinematographer, editor & motion graphic designer (22 countries), Jerry began with Super 8, 16mm & 35mm film projects and advanced confidently – applying his technical aptitude to mastering many nonlinear editing systems like Steenbeck, Lightworks, D-Vision, Media 100, Discreet Edit, Adobe Premiere, Avid & Final Cut Pro. Photoshop, Illustrator & AfterEffects followed as well.

Over time film, DV & Digibeta cameras gave out to HDV, HD & RedOne technologies, however, the importance of great storytelling has only become even more important under the editor's watch. Jerry rejoiced – first as a musician, then writer, film & video editor, director, cinematographer, motion graphic designer, storyteller and along the way as a world traveler.

Serving on site or remotely from the other side of the world, Jerry has prided himself on doing everything possible to fulfill the mission of each project – always trying to exceed his client's standards. This lies at the center of his personal philosophy. Regardless of the role, whether he is serving as the creative "offline" editor, technical "online" editor, colorist, motion graphic designer, DVD authoring designer, post-production supervisor or even consulting the entire production team as he did recently in Nairobi, Kenya, he has looked forward asking the ultimate questions and with it earning his client's respect.

Jerry Pratt’s Specialties:

In addition to his artistic background, Jerry has sought to become proficient on every N.L.E. platform within his grasp: Avid (all flavors), Final Cut Studio, Lightworks, Media 100, Adobe Premiere, Discreet Edit, After Effects, Photoshop, & Illustrator. Jerry has also written, produced & directed award-winning productions from start-to-finish.
