Russell Baruffi’s Summary

Through my joint Masters in Business Administration (MBA) and Masters of Science (MS) in Energy and Environmental Policy, I have developed expertise in marketing and business strategy, with a focus on energy and the economics of low-carbon infrastructure. I have experience in clean energy project development, marketing and business development with clients such as Pacific Gas & Electric, DTE Energy, Sony Pictures Entertainment and Apex Wind Power.
