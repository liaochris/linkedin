Mark Snyir’s Summary

Communication is important to a successful move.

TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® Customer Service Representatives are always happy to answer your questions about moving, packing, costs and scheduling. We want both you and our staff members to be prepared on moving day so your move goes exactly as planned.

We have an excellent reputation. More than 92% of our customers refer us to family and friends.

Our movers are professionally trained and work in uniforms.
Locations sell packing supplies and boxes including customized boxes like dish packs and wardrobe boxes.

Staff members are courteous, hard-working and conscientious.

Our team will pack and move as little or as much as you like, even just moving the big screen TV from one end of the house to the other.

Movers can disassemble and reassemble furniture if you like, although they are unable to disconnect or reconnect any hard wired items.

The cost of your move will be based on actual time spent on your move and drive time.

We offer free padding and stretch wrapping. By doing this, not only does it protect your furniture from scratches or chipping, but allows you to leave your clothes in the dressers.

We are bonded and insured.

TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® does not use day labor or temporary workers. All of our employees are full-time.
