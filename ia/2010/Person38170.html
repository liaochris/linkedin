Chris Maniates, PMP’s Summary

Free your people do what they do best: design, program, sell, strategize… Their brilliant talents are wasted unless there is a proven structure to implement towards positive deliverables. I create efficient plans with detailed processes to safeguard scope, schedule, cost and quality.

My style of project management removes roadblocks to enable your team to be their best. This involves a wide array of solutions that include mentoring, initiating better processes, securing hardware or simply opening communication. It all begins with my concise, understandable analysis.

For over ten years, I’ve created project plans and led teams in fast paced environments to successfully deliver projects for global Fortune 500 organizations through local start-ups.

I am interested about your goals, projects and initiatives that we can complete together successfully. If you are interested in solving project issues, start by requesting my management case studies. Thanks!
