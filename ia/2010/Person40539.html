Cindy Fletcher, President/Founder

cindy@strategicsearches.com

Cindy has personally built relationships over the past 18 years with clients hiring throughout the United States. Together with her team at Strategic Search Solutions, they handle some of the hottest, fastest growing companies in such markets as Business Intelligence, On-Demand, e-Commerce, e-Marketing, Infrastructure Management, Web Services, Analytical Applications, etc.

Within these specialty markets they cover the full “sales” spectrum from Account Executive, System Engineers, Business Development Management, and Sales Management level searches.

Strategic Search Solutions’ pursuit in staffing is in the satisfying of Clients and Candidates alike and they have committed to a standard of excellence that transcends the way many search firms conduct business today. It is their intent to establish a lasting and trusting relationship with their Clients and Candidates. This is accomplished by developing and maintaining a dialog that treats Clients and Candidates as the professionals they are. Every effort is made to understand their business needs and listen to their requests and requirements.

As a result of those efforts, top tier techology companies look to Cindy and her firm to round out their sales teams with the best performers in the candidate pool. This is why they have earned a reputation in the industry as one of the most professional and effective "go-to" recruiters for candidates and clients alike.
