  • Author

    May 2008 — May 2008 (1 month)

    I published an article on brandchannel.com, titled "People Buy Brands Like They Make Friends."

It offers the simple principle that building brands is less about love and more about friendship. The article calls out types of friendship models that would be useful for brands to adopt and articulates 5 key questions brand managers need to ask themselves to ensure their brands are building friendships with consumers.
