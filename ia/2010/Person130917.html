Matt Mansfield’s Summary

My Passion

I have a passion for introducing and coaching small businesses and startups in how to use the tools and opportunities available online to take their business farther while spending less time and money.

I feed that passion with both the day-to-day running of my business, Biggest Possible Future, LLC and by constantly adding to my blog and resource site, www.leverage-the-web.com.

About Me - Matt Mansfield

My eclectic career experience spans over 20 years of technology support and administration, software design and development, product and project management, marketing and PR and training for both groups and individuals. For more details, check out my LinkedIn profile below.

Since 2000, I have been immersed in the World Wide Web - planning, designing, building and managing Web sites. For me, the Web satisfies both the creative and gear-head sides of my brain and I am a strong advocate of the Web's ability to communicate, connect, sell and provide an platform on which starting and running a business can be done both more quickly and with much less complexity.

I have also served as a member of the School Town team. In that role, I designed the functionality and look-and-feel for a unique and powerful student learning environment.

I live in the suburbs North of Chicago with my wife, four kids, three fish and many, many books.
