M. Ahmad Shahzad (PMP)’s Summary

Ahmad Shahzad is a senior technologist and professional in the field of data warehousing and project management, with over thirteen years of experience. His strengths include managing large projects; scope management, resource management, time and cost management. He demonstrates command in analytical and creative problem solving with the ability to rapidly establish effective working relationships with clients, coworkers and external vendors. His typical responsibilities include: analyst, application architect, data modeler, re-engineer, and project management.
