Jon Williamson’s Summary

Jon Williamson is a barter professional dedicated to helping small and medium businesses monetize unused inventory.

In today’s increasingly uncertain economy, barter is enjoying a renewed and explosive growth.

As the owner of Smart Barter USA, Jon works directly with members both to market their goods and to locate the items they need to operate their business. By using this two prong approach, Jon is able to increase sales and decrease costs simultaneously.

Jon is currently accepting new members for Smart Barter USA. The network has over 50,000 members and is doing over $18 million in monthly trading.

Jon is an open networker who accepts all invitations to connect. Contact Jon to schedule a complimentary consultation to learn how barter will create value for your company. You will also receive a custom report of products and services available in your area.
