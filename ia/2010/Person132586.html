Maribel Lackey’s Summary

I help brands build brand awareness and increase their market reach by connecting with people, and I love it. After many years of working with traditional advertising, I really enjoy what social media and other new media have brought to marketing. They have put the human factor back into companies large and small, and have allowed companies to build stronger relationships with their customers.

I began my career in advertising after graduating from California Long Beach State University with a degree in Communication Arts and an emphasis in Organizational Communications. Since then I have had the privilege of working with successful brands such as IKEA, Los Cabos Tourism Board, Aeroméxico Airlines and See's Candies. I have enjoyed building lasting and meaningful relationships with our clients and appreciate the opportunity to be a part of their success. As a bilingual professional with a Hispanic background, I have focused on Hispanic marketing and look forward to helping organizations better reach the growing, and increasingly powerful multi-cultural consumer.
