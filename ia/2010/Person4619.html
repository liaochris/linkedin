Angela Jones’s Summary

Angela Jones is an award winning, Certified Professional Resume Writer, Certified Employment Interview Consultant, and an active member of The National Resume Writers Association, Career Directors International, and Career Management Alliance. She also serves on PARW's Certification Committee and CDI's Membership Committee.

Other notable credentials held by Angela include her status as one of the few professional resume writers to have received the coveted TORI Award (Toast of the Resume Industry); a prestigious international competition recognizing the “best of the best” by industry peers and an accolade that few experience. Angela has also had samples of her work accepted for inclusion in recently published books.

Angela recently had the opportunity to study under master resume writer, Wendy Enelow. Additional Continuing Education training has taken place at NRWA conferences each of the last 3 years.

As a globally recognized professional resume writer, her resume service, Haute Resume & Career Services LLC has clients in every region of the U.S., as well as Canada, Germany, Costa Rica, Argentina, Iraq, and Afghanistan.

Angela's own background includes 15 years of banking, 6 years of office management and administration, 7 years of Information Technology, and experience in Human Resources. Surprisingly, the most valuable experience that she has gained was not in HR but while working in the trenches. Angela knows first hand the skills and experience that hiring managers and recruiters seek.

Through the years of managing a resume service and working with clients in the U.S. and around the world, Angela’s client survey found that approximately 90% have attempted to write their own resumes before seeking the expertise she provides. With a 100% satisfaction rate clients were glad they did!

Angela Jones’s Specialties:

All types of resumes including: Executive, Military Transition, Information Technology, and Pharmaceutical Sales.
