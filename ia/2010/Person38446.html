Chris Roberson’s Summary

Chris Roberson is a Consultant in PwC’s Performance Improvement practice. His six years of focused technology experience translate to an in-depth understanding of both the technical and business requirements of information technology and risk analysis. He previously worked for the largest IBM training provider on the west coast in a technical lead role overseeing the setup and configuration for the entire IBM curriculum across five geographically dispersed locations. While enrolled at San Francisco State University, Chris completed an internship at a healthcare practice management startup and also a semester long independent study. These opportunities were centered on leveraging open source web based java technologies as a competitive advantage to expensive proprietary solutions. Prior to his work at San Francisco State University Chris was employed as a Linux Systems Administrator for the physics and astronomy department at Arizona State University.
