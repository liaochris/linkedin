Anthony Reeves’s Summary

My name is Anthony Reeves, Esq. and I am dedicated to representing the Disabled and Discriminated through a unique virtual law practice with marketing through contemporary and traditional means.

As a Navy veteran and former public employee, I have dedicated a good portion of my professional career working for those individuals who have been injured physically or professionally.

However, my services are more than just providing legal services. In the last 3 year, I have spent time as an online internet radio show host, hosting two shows.

"IN THE KNOW with Tony Reeves" is committed to introducing the world to good people doing great things. With topics that range from foreclosure and finances to employment and the arts, "IN THE KNOW with Tony Reeves" has been dedicated to providing positive, productive, and personal resources to the average listener. Whatever you NEED to KNOW, I will keep you "IN THE KNOW".

"LegalBEAT in the MORNING" is quite frankly, the BEST DAMN morning show with a Legal Twist. Every Monday morning from 8:00am to 8:30am EST, 'LegalBEAT in the MORNING' is designed to get your week started with Informative Legal tips, Inspiration Motivational thoughts, and Positive professional commentary. Don't waste your time looking for the right place. The HOTTEST space is with me every Monday morning.

The goal is to provide a vehicle where people are introduced to the vast resources that are at their finger tips.
