Mark Mason’s Summary

Mark is the Founder & CEO of Mubaloo.

Mubaloo is the UK's largest B2B & B2C smartphone application development agency based in London, Bristol and New York, employing 37 people.

Mark founded Mubaloo with CTO, Ben Trewhella, in late 2008 following the sale of Mason Zimbler Digital Marketing to the US marketing services company, Harte-Hanks, in early 2008.

Mubaloo builds smartphone applications for companies who want to take advantage of the massive growth in this market. Clients currently include The AA, AXA Insurance, RBS, Institute of Physics, Heineken, Roche, King of Shaves, Samsung, Johnnie Walker, Nike, Sophos, William Hill, Experian and Brighton & Hove City Council.

Working closely with our clients we will create, develop, test, host and market apps to be used on the iPhone, iPad, Android, Blackberry, Windows Phone, Samsung Bada, Palm, .mobi or Facebook platforms.

Our development process guarantees that your applications will be completed on time and to budget.
