Gordon Gus’s Summary

I am a passionate technologist obsessed with making new media work for you. I am convinced that rich media can help any business on the Web. I have been a believer from the start and have seen what works, what could work, and what just doesn’t. A self-confessed media junkie and hacker, I understand both the traditional and online media landscape and dynamics. I am focused on finding better ways to apply media technology to deal with the challenges that face businesses today including content piracy, convergence, hyper-competition and day-late regulation. My experience includes integration of digital media applications, multi-tiered e-commerce site design, performance optimization, broadcast media operations, IPTV, conditional access systems and content delivery infrastructures. I have helped many companies find new ways to profit from online audio and video.
