John Elmer’s Summary

John Elmer is CEO of Gelb, a strategic marketing firm based in Houston, Texas. John is also CEO of G2M Partners, a new Gelb venture that provides a complete Go to Market solution for generating revenue to emerging growth companies in the oilfield service, energy and technology industries.

Prior to starting G2M Partners, John managed revenue generation as Chief Marketing Officer for Ingrain, a rapidly-growing technology startup serving the oil and gas industry. Ingrain is commercializing digital rock physics technology developed by Dr. Amos Nur of Stanford University. For more information about Ingrain visit the Ingrain website at www.ingrainrocks.com.

John joined Gelb Consulting in 1984. He is a recognized expert in marketing strategy and new product development and has deep experience in the oilfield service industry. In 1999, John acquired Gelb Consulting Group from its founder, Gabriel M. Gelb, who started the firm in 1965. John has received professional certification as a management consultant (CMC) and in new product development (NPDP). John earned his M.B.A. in marketing from the University of Houston in 1984, and received a B.A. with honors in psychology from the University of Texas in 1980. John was an adjunct faculty member at the University of Houston College of Business Administration from 1989 to 1993.
