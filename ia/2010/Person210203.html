Valerie Curl’s Summary

As the social media-community manager/webmaster for Placer Nature Center in Auburn, CA, I maintain the social networks, including writing blogs and growing the Facebook page. PNC has experienced over 200% growth in both stats and event participation since I began in Sept, 2009.

I’m also busy building up a small business clientele in Placer and Nevada Counties, developing marketing plans and implementing them. From designing websites and brochures to writing copy and buying printing and mailing. In addition, I handle the SEO side: insert codes and validating the websites for search engines, creating and inserting search engine sitemap coding, and analyzing the data.

Prior to moving to Colfax, CA, to be close to my family, I worked in large corporate marketing departments and numerous advertising/marketing agencies in LA, SF and Chicago. My specialty was project management and print production including web site development, copyediting and graphic design. As a former journalist, I enjoy writing stories that highlight the accomplishments of PNC as well as their mission. We have only one planet - one environment - and taking care of it is the goal at Placer Nature Center.

Among my greatest accomplishment was supervising the design and execution of several of VISA’s best, award winning marketing pieces. My other major accomplishment was expanding the role of my graphics production department, which created a 50% increase in revenue for the agency and became the model adopted by the numerous other company offices.

In 2001, I started my first community forum for SF Peninsula professional women. It grew to over a hundred members in the six months before I moved to take a job in Chicago as a direct mail Account and Production Manager. Revenues grew, on my accounts, to well over 50%, and I was asked by a second division of one of my clients to take over their work.

For more information on freelance and contract work, contact me.
