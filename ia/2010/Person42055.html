Christine Epres, JD/MPA’s Summary

Born and raised in Silicon Valley, Christine attended law school and graduate school in New York. She then moved to Washington, DC for a stint in policy/legislative analysis and community advocacy. Christine currently resides in the SoMa (South of Market) neighborhood of San Francisco and works in the Financial District.
