Danielle Douglas’s Summary

Danielle Douglas is the President of Inspire Enterprise, Inc., teaching physicians in private practice how to be more entrepreneurial, how to become CEOs of their organization and life.

We deliver leading edge coaching, training and educational services focused on developing high performance individuals and teams.

Additionally, Inspire Enterprise works with organizations by providing strategic planning workshops, to gain clarity on goals that will take their staff and departments to new levels of efficiency, effectiveness, and profitability.

For approximately twenty years, Danielle sold successfully over $4 million dollars in one product and almost $2 million dollars in another for Fortune 100 companies such as XEROX Corporation and Pfizer Inc. She achieved great success, winning numerous sales awards, due to the strategic planning she created and implemented during her sales career. Her level of expertise ranges from lead generation to client fulfillment.

I would like to connect with entrepreneurs, organizations focused on business development, and professional organizations that can benefit from my services. Additionally, I would like to connect with Human Resources Professionals in Training and Development, who can use my sales training expertise for training sales managers and teams.
