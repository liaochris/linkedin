Kate Stewart’s Summary

In her roles as OD consultant, coach, mediator, conflict resolution system designer, and trainer, Kate has assisted many individuals, as well as organizations of various sizes in diverse industries. A partial list of organizations she has served or provided training to or through includes Banco Nacional de Mexico, City of Austin, Centerpulse, Coca-Cola Enterprises, U.S. Dept. of Defense programs, Freescale and Motorola SPS, governmental and nonprofit organizations, Halliburton/KBR, Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, private medical practices, Rentway, Society for Human Resource Management, St. David's Medical Center, Symbol Technologies, Texas Hospital Association, Texas Medical Association, Texas Nurses Association, The University of Texas at Austin Option II MBA Program, The University of Texas - Pan American, and USAA Insurance.

Author, "Storied Conflict Talk: Narrative Construction in Mediation", John Benjamins Publishing, 2010 (co-authored with M. Maxwell).
Author of forthcoming book "Scaffold for Success: Use Skillpower, Not Willpower, to Achieve Any Goal".
