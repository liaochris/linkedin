Seth Goldstein’s Summary

Since 1998, Seth Goldstein has designed Websites for a variety of different sized companies. Having worked hard to be extremely proficient with the design tools needed to be successful in the Web design industry (ie. Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Flash, etc.), Seth strives for excellence in every project he takes on.

In 2007 after many years working as a contract designer, Seth opened his own firm, Goldstein Media, to serve the Web design needs of small and mid-sized businesses.

As the Internet continues to evolve, so do Seth's goals for Goldstein Media. In addition to Web Design, Goldstein Media has developed specialties in Search Engine Optimization, Social Media and Internet Marketing.

Though these are ventures into the various aspects of Internet marketing, our goal for Goldstein Media remains simple -- to provide the complete package for our clients at the highest possible quality.

With help from Goldstein Media, we hope our clients will be able to get the most out of their investment and time on the Internet.

Currently the Goldstein Media Website and Blog rank on the first page of Google for our target keywords.

Seth prides himself on the work that he produces and the contacts he makes.
-------
As side project, A2SM (Addicted to Social Media) started in April 2009. A2SM incorporates Seth's other love, journalism and writing, into the mix. Having been a journalist for more than 4 years, prior to going into Web design full time, Seth has the know-how to build and nurture an online publication. A2SM is about Social Media and societies growing addiction to the emerging technologies and platforms.

Along with a business partner, Seth manages, edits and writes for the publication. There are more than 10 active writers for the site.

Seth's goals for A2SM are as far reaching as those he has for Goldstein Media. He would like to see it catch on and succeed as both a resource and as a business. You can check out A2SM at http://www.a2sm.com
