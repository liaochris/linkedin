Robert McCray’s Summary

Specialize in design and construction of large Life Support Areas (LSA) and other base life support requirements in a contingency environment. These projects include requirements for ROWPU (water procurement), waste water treatement, DFAC (dining facilities), solid waste disposal, public works, roads and grounds, and other projects required in a contingency or forward operating base.

Supervise large groups of self performed or subcontracted workers, provide project estimating, scheduling, cost accounting, and overall management for large projects.
