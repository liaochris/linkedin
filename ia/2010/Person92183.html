Jose Carlos Marco’s Summary

Having acquired a broad vision of the business world and a sound knowledge of Information Technologies, I have decided to become an entrepreneur and have started a venture called Croma Technologies. Our aim at Croma Technologies is to apply IT and Internet to the entertainment industry by helping companies make the most out of new IT tools and customer relationship marketing, while creating for customers an enjoyable experience based on social and media networks, the Internet and mobile phones.

While studying Telecommunications Engineering (Master's degree) at the Polytechnic University of Valencia, Spain, I started several IT businesses and pioneered the creation of one of the first Internet Service Providers in Spain in 1994. It was a valuable experience that enabled me not only to complete the final university project with honors, but also to engage for good in the Internet sector.

I then started working at IBM Global Services, which gave me a unique vision of the organization of work teams, methodology and project management. I gained experience in government-related projects, outsourcing in commercial banking and Internet portal projects, among others. At IBM, I progressively held the positions of Team Leader, Project Leader, Technical Manager and Project Manager.

Later on, I was able to apply both that technological knowledge and business experience in Securitas Direct, an international leader in the consumer security sector. As a CIO, I gathered an IT team of more than 50 professionals and with their support I lead the domestic expansion of the company which grew to reach five times its original size and customer base.

Owing to the positions I have held, I have been able to work in international environments interacting with companies in Europe, the USA and Asia.

I completed my education with a Senior Management Program at IESE Business School, I am a member of the IEEE and my name appears in Who's Who in the World since 2007.
