Rick Ferguson’s Summary

As Client Manager for Barney & Barney Retirement Services, I'm responsible for managing the client database, marketing efforts, and process & systems innovation & integration. I also manage & execute coordinating proposals, plan reviews, RFP consolidation, investment research, and vendor benchmarking,

Prior to joining Barney & Barney, I worked as a Pension Specialist at Guardian Life Insurance Company/Park Avenue Securities and I started my financial career as a stockbroker with TDAmeritrade. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree from Western Illinois University and Series 7, 63, 66, and 24 licenses through the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) and am an Accredited Investment Fiduciary (AIF) and a Retirement Plan Associate (RPA).
