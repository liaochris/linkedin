Rob Luchsinger’s Summary

Dedicated professional with over sixteen years of design, drafting and project management experience with an emphasis in mechanical and piping design. Diversified, knowledgeable and highly experienced in designing, documenting and detailing individual components as well as large complex systems in the 2D and 3D environments, as well as cross platform file conversions.
Experienced in several industries and professional disciplines:
Mechanical, Electro-mechanical, Process Piping, Oil, Gas and Petrochemical Processing, Refining, Off Shore Drilling Platforms, Power Generation, Nuclear, Robotics, Pressure Vessels, Hydraulics, Structural Steel, Commercial and Residential construction, HVAC, and Plumbing.
Proficient using the latest CAD software which includes:
AutoCAD, Mechanical Desktop, Inventor, 3D Studio, CADWorx, and Solidworks as well as experienced in using Micro-Station, Pro-Engineer, and AutoCAD Electrical. Streamlined in generating and managing BOM’s, P&ID’s, ISO’s, CNC Program Sets, MTO’s, ECO’s, ECN’s, and Gantt Charts.
Familiar with industry standards and codes:
ASTM, ANSI, ASME, ISO, AWS, API, NPS, NEC, NEMA, IEEE, DOD and MIL-SPEC.
Extensive knowledge in the specifications for workstations, networking, network administration, windows administration, software and hardware installation, security permissions, password administration and IT administration for intranet and remote management. Proficient at an above average level in Window OS, Microsoft Suites, Adobe Suites, file extension conversions, photo software, and FEA Software.
Pride in being a quick study learning new design and analysis software required in the engineering professions.
