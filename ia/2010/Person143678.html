Michael Trust, MPA, SPHR-CA, CCC, CECC’s Summary

I am a Certified Executive Career Coach, Certified Career Coach, and Certified Senior Professional in Human Resources, and an International Coach Federation (ICF) member career strategist who believes in helping clients realize their full potential and achieve clarity about work. I have helped many people reevaluate their career and professional goals, and then helped them put plans in place to achieve those goals, helping them to exceed their own expectations. I have an extensive background in Human Resources, as well as a background in Real Estate and in coaching, and having successfully worked for others and successfully run my own businesses, I bring a different perspective to the conversation. I am an action-oriented coach who derives great satisfaction from seeing clients achieve at their full potential. I also have assisted many people with resume development and interview preparation that has resulted in a high percentage of interviews.

My career strategy and coaching practice takes my 20+ years of human resources and hiring experience, along with my coaching experience, and puts them to work for you. I give you the tools through our coaching sessions help you discover your true passion relative to your career, good and bad cultural and position fits, and indicators for success. I then help you tailor your career search, resume, and cover letter to find work you are passionate about. This may include things like assessments, how to find jobs in the hidden job market, how to effectively network, the use of social media in the job search, how to negotiate overall compensation, and helping you to develop your own marketing and branding plan. I use the Authentic Vocation™ and Quantum Shift™ methodology for career exploration. I abide by the International Coaching Federation’s Standards of Ethics®.

I offer career coaching to all professions. I additionally offer business coaching to Human Resource professionals and to Real Estate Agents and Brokers.
