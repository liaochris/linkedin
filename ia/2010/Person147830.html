Michael Miller’s Summary

I am a graduate of Wilmington University with a B.S. in Sports Management('03). I have spent the last 5 years working as an experienced sports professional in minor league sports as well as at the collegiate level.

My first position was with the Richmond Riverdogs ('03-'04) as their Director of Group Sales and Game Day Operations. From there I was hired by Delaware State University in fall of 2004 to help form and implement their box office operations. In 2008 I began my current position with the Wilmington Blue Rocks as the Ticket Manager.
