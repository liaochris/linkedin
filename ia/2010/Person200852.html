Dan Baldwin’s Summary

Dan has been a sales consultant in the telecom & data network service industry since 1990.

He specializes in working with the owners and IT managers of multi-location businesses to help them set up or optimize their data, Internet, phone and wireless networks.

He also helps his customers maximize sales to existing customers and prospects by creating and maintaining his client’s online marketing elements including interactive blogs, social media and e-mail marketing.

Dan is also the Founder of Telecom Association, a membership organization of 3,500 independent distributors and vendors of business telecom, data & Internet products and solutions.

Dan Baldwin’s Specialties:

Invoice & technology review; phone and data system network design; IP-PBX & hosted VoIP phone systems; audio, web & desk-top video conferencing solutions.

Marketing of channel distribution programs; technical writing for end-users and distibutors; creation and maintenance of telecom agent programs; creation and maintenance of online marketing elements such as interactive blogs, social media and email marketing.
