Stephanie Gunning’s Summary

Stephanie Gunning is a bestselling author, nonfiction editor, and publishing strategist with 22 years of experience in the book business. According to Jill Kramer, editorial director of Hay House, “I have rarely worked with an editor who is so adept.” Her A-list clientele includes New York Times and national bestselling authors (Hale Dwoskin, Gregg Braden, Baron Baptiste, Ernest Chu, Ruby Payne), major publishing firms, top caliber literary agencies, and innovative self-publishers.

Gunning is co-creator of The Great American Think Out, a national campaign to promote presence and meditation with major live events taking place across the United States on October 2, 2008, following a five-month rollout on the 30-day interactive website www.stopthinkingnow.com. The program is being used by people in 84 countries around the world. Her spiritual/balanced lifestyle blog "Stop Thinking Now" launched in fall 2008 on the new web network Intent.com.

A former senior editor at Bantam Doubleday Dell and editor at HarperCollins Publishers, she has coauthored and ghostwritten 17 books for mainstream publishers (Perigee, Health Communications, Tarcher, McGraw-Hill, Jossey-Bass, Three Rivers, Applause, Broadway, New Page, and Jodere) and independents (Sedona Press, aha! Process, and Quimby Press). Publicity guru Arielle Ford calls her “a writing Goddess.” She has mastered the art of transforming powerful ideas into highly marketable books.

After graduating with a B.A. from Amherst College in 1984, Gunning launched her publishing career in New York City, rapidly rising through the editorial ranks and then ultimately establishing an independent business, Stephanie Gunning Enterprises LLC, in 1996. Currently, she resides in Manhattan, from where she teaches teleseminars, lectures, and leads workshops on nonfiction publishing and book marketing. To find out more about her books, audio products, and services, visit: www.StephanieGunning.com.
