Craig Elimeliah’s Summary

The past 13 years have been an amazing journey and it only gets better by the day. My success in technology, design, social media and advertising is a blessing that I am appreciative for each and every day. I am lucky that I am able to do what I love.

I represent a new breed of digital advertising professional who is helping to usher in a new age of communications through the use of creativity, social media and cutting edge technology.

I work closely with world class brands and top advertising agencies by applying my keen understanding and expertise in helping my clients understand how to better strategize and integrate new forms of digital and social media techniques into larger campaign initiatives and to help close the loop between traditional and digital advertising .

I am the consummate team player.

My abilities include extreme focus on objectives and goals, managing expectations, allocating roles and identifying strengths and weaknesses on any team I am a part of.

I lead by example and always employ a positive and motivational leadership methodology. I encourage my team members to stay informed on design, development and industry trends as well as staying ahead of our competition through experimentation and always pushing the bar of excellence.

I have earned major industry awards and accolades as well as contributed through community involvement, speaking engagements and have written many articles for publications such as The FWA, AIGA, OMMA, and other industry publications.

My passion extends itself to constantly striving to deliver solutions that cater to the goals and objectives of my clients rather than my own personal recognition. My client's success is my top priority.

Past and current clients include: Microsoft, Intel, ZUNE, Fila, AXE, Mini, CITIBANK, Sean John, USA Network, HP, Herman Miller, Toyota, Daffy's, Coke, Sony Music, NBC Universal, Aprica, Perdue, John Varvatos, NYDJ and SyFy.
