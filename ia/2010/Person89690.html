Janus Boye’s Summary

Despite not having gone through academic education, I have built up quite an experience working with online professionals. I enjoy learning from past mistakes and sharing with almost everybody who wants to listen.

Have worked with digital projects and specifically web content management since the late 90's in various roles throughout Europe. Have worked for vendors and thus had the "inside perspective". I have subsequently helped with vendor evaluations and acted as "fire fighter" on troubled projects.

I also enjoy taking occasional speaking engagements and have spoken at events in Asia, Europe and America.

My mission is to help mature a young marketplace and build a global community of online professionals while increasing the professional confidence of our members.
