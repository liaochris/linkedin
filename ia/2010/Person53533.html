Deborah O'Brien’s Summary

Driving sales, acquiring key accounts, and establishing new revenue streams for national publications have been the keys to my successful career in advertising sales. Throughout my tenures with major publishers including American Express (Food & Wine Magazine), Hearst Corporation (Weekend & Veranda Magazines), and A&E Television Network (Biography Magazine), I have enjoyed top rankings in key sales categories including new account development and new market development.

My greatest asset is the ability to strengthen the depth of a publication by establishing accounts in non-endemic categories. I have consistently penetrated key market segments by leveraging my extensive network of contacts and establishing strong alliances with senior-level decision-makers.

In each of my positions, I have worked collaboratively with my counterparts to design integrated, multimedia campaigns to maximize value, reach, and effectiveness for our advertisers. I continuously monitor my clients’ advertising programs to ensure alignment with their ever-changing objectives. This has resulted in consistent increases in account volume and an exceptionally high rate of client retention.

In addition to my sales achievements, I have also served in leadership positions including my role as an interim Associate Publisher with American Express for their Executive Travel and Sky Guide magazines. In my career, I have built top-performing sales teams and provided support to close major accounts and achieve sales goals.

As our industry continues to evolve, I maintain an understanding of new media and technology. I strive to provide advertisers with the latest tools and vehicles to communicate their message and achieve their marketing objectives.
