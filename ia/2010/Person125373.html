Lindsay Abbott’s Summary

Primarily I am a full-time international voice over artist based in Cambridgeshire, UK, with my own voice recording studio, providing voiced and edited mp3’s/wavs/aifs etc directly to your inbox, to suit your requirements.

People often ask what does a voice over artist do?
Well, the range of voice over services I provide for clients include:

radio and television commercials
film and documentary narration
audio books - narration and character work
corporate video
website video
e-learning / educational media
cartoon and animation
computer games
telephone on-hold messages
radio imaging

If you're a company or an organisation who has been thinking about adding some audio/visuals to either your website, YouTube or DVD and you're interested in exploring this further using a voice over artist, I also work in partnership with a number of video and/or media companies who can help to guide you through this process.

CORPORATE & MEDICAL ROLEPLAY / SINGER
In addition to this, I am also a corporate and medical roleplay actor, plus a singer with my own jazz and swing band. Details under Career description at foot of page.

BUSINESS BACKGROUND
You will see from my profile that I also have a business background in Human Resources, which has added invaluable experience to all of the work that I do, as has my training as an actor and singer, with an honours degree in Drama & Theatre Arts and a postgraduate diploma in acting from Guildford School of Acting.
