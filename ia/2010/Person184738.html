Scott Cleere’s Summary

Scott is a registered patent attorney with several years of experience practicing intellectual property law including patent prosecution, trademark prosecution, and patent licensing. Scott's practice focuses on advising clients on developing their intellectual capital and building strong intellectual property portfolios as part of their business strategy. Scott's clients range from start ups and small businesses to multinational corporations.

Scott Cleere’s Specialties:

Patent prosecution; trademark prosecution; trade secrets; preparing patent opinions related to patentability, invalidity, and infringement; and copyrights.
