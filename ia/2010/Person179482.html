Ryan Eichler’s Summary

A recognized leader in the marketing communications field with nearly ten years of digital communications, media relations, corporate communications and event management experience, Ryan has managed teams that told the world about the new Chevrolet Volt, driven social media strategy and content development for top companies like Mars and Unilever, and executed ground-breaking product launches for Chevrolet, Cadillac and Harley-Davidson.

Ryan’s personality has transcended his account role on teams and become the content driver for both M&M’S Racing and GM Racing, where “Right Turn Ryan” and “Racemaster Ryan” have created social and traditional media content and created sustainable social networking relationships with consumers and press.

Ryan has held Account Supervisor and Senior Account Executive positions at CKPR-Chicago and was a Senior Project Manager in the GM North Central Region Public Relations department. Previously, Ryan was Senior Account Executive at Weber Shandwick Worldwide, working on key accounts including the Riddell Football Helmets, Unilever’s Degree deodorant brand and Campbell’s Chunky Soup NFL program. Ryan was also an integral member of the Harley-Davidson account as one of the lead staff members for media relations during the Harley-Davidson 100th Anniversary.

Prior to joining Weber Shandwick, Ryan worked for Harley-Davidson Corporate Communications in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, as a project coordinator, assisting in the launch of the VRSCA V-Rod and Buell Firebolt XB9R motorcycles. He also was an associate editor to Harley-Davidson’s Enthusiast magazine, the oldest continually published motorcycle magazine in the world.

A certified “gearhead,” when not in the office Ryan can be found at the racetrack, cheering on his favorite NASCAR and IRL teams. Ryan has run his own detailing business, and was the Art Director for the Vintage Snowmobile Club of America’s quarterly magazine, Vintage Snowmobile.
