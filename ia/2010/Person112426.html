Katie Hollar’s Summary

Katie is currently a product marketing specialist at Cvent, the market leader in event managment, web survey, and site selection software. She is specifically focused on marketing the Cvent Supplier Network (CSN)- a database of over 100,000 event venues and event service providers across the globe. The CSN allows meeeting planners to search for meeting venues and send electronic requests for proposals to get bids back from suppliers. Katie's responsibilities at Cvent include collaborating with Cvent's marketing partners, blogging at http://blog.cvent.com, and helping with public relations & social media outreach.

Prior to working at Cvent, Katie was an Account Executive at BoldMouth, a social media agency based in Charlottesville, Virginia. She began her position at BoldMouth after graduating from the McIntire School of Commerce at the University of Virginia in May of 2008. At McIntire, Katie received a Bachelor of Science in Commerce, with a double concentration in Marketing and Management.

Previously, Katie worked as the Public Relations Coordinator for Hollar and Co. Residential Real Estate. At Hollar and Co., she planned events for prospective clients at various community venues in Northern Virginia and also assisted with the design of print marketing materials. Over holidays while in school, Katie worked as a sales associate at the Nordstrom department store in Tyson's Corner.

Katie was a student member of the American Advertising Federation in college. The Virginia Advertising team competed in the National Student Advertising Competition in 2008, in which they won first place regionally and third place nationally for a campaign for AOL Instant Messenger. She also was an active member in the Greek community, serving as the Public Relations Vice President and Inter-Sorority Council Representative for her sorority, Gamma Phi Beta.
