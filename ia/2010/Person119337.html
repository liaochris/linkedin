Kristin Martin’s Summary

Kristin Martin is a recruiter for WPP, a global marketing and communications company with approximately 100,000 employees in over 100 Group companies in more than 106 countries. Kristin works across WPP’s operating companies in North America. Her focus is on strategic hires in direct marketing.

After being a stay-at-home mom for a few years, Kristin worked as an independent recruiter working mostly in the advertising and fashion retail industries.

Prior to starting her own business, Kristin spent six years with Heidrick & Struggles’ New York Office. While at Heidrick & Struggles, Kristin worked across a variety of industries and functional specialties. The majority of her search experience is in the consumer, retail and media industries. Kristin also served as the Associate Director acting as a liaison between senior management and the associate group.

Kristin has worked on searches for a variety of clients, ranging from entrepreneurial start-ups to prominent multi-national corporations. These clients represented a spectrum of industries. Some of the clients Kristin has worked for include WPP, TBWA\Chiat Day, John Wiley & Sons, Columbia University, FedEx, Whirlpool, United Airlines, Levi Strauss & Company and The Gap.

Prior to Heidrick & Struggles, Kristin spent several years with SpencerStaurt’s New York Office where she was a generalist.
