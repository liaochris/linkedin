Steve King’s Summary

"Steve was one of the more effective CEO's in our portfolio companies. He is a great relationship builder and sales guy. He responded to the dying IT services market by turning Endymion into a software product company that created value for dozens of Fortune 500 companies and made it an attractive acquisition. I would not hesitate to fund him again."

- John Grillos, Chairman and CEO, MVC Capital

Steve has over 30 years of computer industry experience in the delivery of information technology services, software engineering and product development. Extensive experience with Security, eBusiness Software, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, Content Management, Web Services, Data Center Services, Systems Development and Integration (SAP, Oracle, PeopleSoft, JD Edwards, Lawson and Siebel), Network Engineering and Web application development for both Java and MS platforms. Raised over $25m in Venture Capital.

Key Accomplishments:

~ Successfully ran a $36m Software and IT Services company
~ Built and trained sales teams that sold over $80m in software/services
~ Led software engineering for successful Web-based content management product
~ Created market-leading imaging products for business process automation
~ Managed product development for BI and Data Warehouse software
~ Managed over 450 professionals and 7 large data centers throughout the US
~ Served as CIO for 2 companies in the Manufacturing and Health Care sectors
~ Founded 3 start-ups and raised over $25m in venture capital
~ Published author of business book on Retail Web-marketing Strategy

Steve King’s Specialties:

VC development, professional services and consulting management, software engineering management, business development, product development and management, IT management, Web marketing, product marketing.

  • CMO

    ConnectAndSell

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    2005 — 2009 (4 years )

    Chief Marketing Officer for this SaaS start-up in the Sales Performance space. The Connectandsell solution is delivered through a combination of patented telecommunications technology and a team of people who are assigned to each customer sales rep. The call and information about the call are delivered simultaneously in less than ¼ of a second so there is zero latency and the people doing the dialing never speak to a prospect. The call appears to have been made by the sales rep themselves. We guarantee 5 connects per hour or the customer pays nothing. We generally deliver between 8 and 12 calls per hour. The industry average is 1 call per hour. We grew revenue from $2m to $6m in 2008 and 2009.

Endymion was named one of the “100 companies that matter in Knowledge Management” in 2003. Raised a $15m ‘A’ round of Venture Capital. The company was sold to Soluziona USA in 2004, and ManagedPay has been recapitalized as a seperate software company.
