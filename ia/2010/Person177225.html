Ron Elkins’s Summary

Mr. Elkins is a seasoned technology leader with 18 years experience across several industries including energy, software and online services, government, healthcare, and consulting.

During this time he has been the leader of major initiatives for Fortune 50 and other international and government clients, including creation of custom enterprise IT solutions, development and release of commercial software products, managing corporate relocations, and coordinating the IT side of mergers and divestitures.

Over the years, Mr. Elkins has also been involved in several technology startups, one of which became an early industry leader in its market and went on to enjoy a successful IPO. He is currently involved in a new venture, ThoughtPlace Technologies which will bring innovative IT solutions to the growing Renewable Energy market.

Some of his achievements include:

• Winner of 2 national awards in 2006 & 2007 for development of market leading B2B and B2C service portals
• Recognized twice for leading top performing teams in a 13,000 employee organization.
• Responsible for new strategies and technologies that have resulted in dramatic improvements for
organizations. Recent examples include :
o 300% increase in eCommerce revenues over 2½ years to $70 Million in 2007.
o 27% reduction in customer service calls requiring assistance of human representatives due to the addition of new IVR self-service and PhonePay features for a major regional utility providing services to over 2 million customers
o 18% increase in the use of the online customer self-service system supporting over 400,000 accounts
