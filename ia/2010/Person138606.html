Rob Reed’s Summary

Entrepreneurial leader adept at implementing high-profile marketing programs, developing new business, and coordinating across multiple departments for maximum results. More than 15 years of results-driven leadership, team building, and startup experience. Served as corporate spokesperson for public and private companies. Unique ability to integrate technology and innovation with product marketing to maximize company profitability and growth.

Rob Reed’s Specialties:

Startup companies. Business strategy and development. Integrated marketing, branding and communications campaigns. Government relations and public affairs. SEO/SMO. Building companies.

Rob Reed’s Experience

  • VP of Marketing and Government Relations

    Zumbox

    (Internet industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Developed Zumbox positioning as the first paperless postal system and "paperless mail" as a new product and product category. Authored key messaging and value propositions for consumer, business, and government audiences. Executing B2C, B2B, and B2G marcom strategies.

    Designed PR strategy to support key business objectives. Managing multiple outside PR agents. Serving as company spokesperson for all media. Conceived, developed, and launched PaperlessPlease.org online community on the Drupal open-source platform as a cause-driven campaign centering on Zumbox's key "paperless" value proposition. Designed and authored company blog and monthly newsletter for Zumbox users.

    Developed and lead the company's highly successful government (B2G) strategy. Launched Zumbox in in the Cities of San Francisco, Newark, New York, and Minneapolis with key support and participation from Mayors Newsom, Booker, Bloomberg, and Rybak respectively. Working with city departments to send paperless mail and transition residents to Zumbox.

    The Zumbox web-based platform powers the world’s first paperless postal system. For every U.S. street address, there is a corresponding Zumbox – a digital mailbox – which enables postal mail to be sent as digital files and received online with no paper and no scanning. This new category – paperless mail – represents the first practical alternative to traditional postal systems.

    Rob also initiated the popular #EcoMonday Twitter meme and is ranked among Cision's "Top 100 Social Media & Internet Marketing Bloggers."

    Max Gladwell is Rob's professional platform, which is built on his reputation as a thought leader in web strategy and sustainability. It includes a network of relationships with fellow bloggers, thought leaders, entrepreneurs, and influencers.
