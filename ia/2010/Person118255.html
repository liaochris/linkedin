Konrad M. Rzasa’s Summary

Konrad Rzasa currently holds a Director position for the internet search engine marketing & web design company Vayu Media. His role includes the oversight of Vayu Media's North American operations and business strategy consulting.

Konrad Rzasa also holds a consulting position at ProSys Information Systems (VP Finance). ProSys offers its clients assistance with professional services, IT infrastructure services and e-Commerce application development. ProSys is a division of Bell Microproducts (BELM) and has annual sales of $480M.

Konrad Rzasa is developing the finance function at Prosys and managing the transition of Prosys from a privately held company to a subsidiary of publicly held Bell Microproducts. The company has completed its restatement of prior period financials and is current with its SEC filing requirements.

During his career, he has directed the integration of portfolio acquisitions, business acquisitions, new portfolio start-ups and new product launches into the accounting center of GE Money Americas as Business Integration Manager, as well as, managed accounting teams in transition as a district controller for Allied Waste ($95+MM) and Advanced Disposal ($50MM). Konrad Rzasa has led efforts in market development, pricing strategy, compensation plans, waste volume internalization, waste swap, profitability and ROI analysis, while improving the financial close cycle, implementing SOX and strengthening purchase controls in his tenure as controller. He also held finance/accounting positions of increasing responsibility at Raytheon Aircraft where he provided financial modeling analysis for a $500MM aircraft debt sale, supported debt reduction efforts and analyzed the profitability of the commuter aircraft product line.

Konrad Rzasa holds a Master of Arts in Economics and a Bachelor of Business Administration with an emphasis in Finance. He speaks fluently German, Polish & English.
