Julie Spokus, MCT, CTT+’s Summary

I am a skilled Technical Trainer with more than 10 years in the Information Technology field. Many trainees have remarked on my ability to take the complex and make it simple. I have held various positions in the training and development field as well as in the IT field. Currently, I am a a Senior Training Specialist at CareFirst BlueCross BlueShield.

I have numerous certifications including Certified Technical Trainer (CTT+), Synchronous Facilitation and Design, and Microsoft Office Specialist Master Instructor (Office 2003).

I have been an active board member of ASTD Maryland for five years serving as Technical Director, Finance Director, and Professional Development Director. I am currently President of ASTD Maryland. So far this year, ASTD Maryland has received two Sharing Our Success (SOS) awards as well as being named Chapter of the Month for November 2009.

I have a Bachelor’s degree in Global Studies and a Master’s degree in Teaching English as a Second Language. I am working on a second Master's - an MS in Information Technology with an emphasis in Educational Technology.
