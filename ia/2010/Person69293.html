Fernando Pinto’s Summary

Fernando Pinto has been developing is activity in e-Learning and Human Capital Management and have already developed several projects in these areas in Portugal and to Angola. In the last years acted as a consultant in Portugal where he was involved in the process of selling and implementation of various solutions such as Learn eXact, Plateau, Questionmark, iLinc and IntraLearn. He is graduated in by the University of Minho, Portugal on Mathematics and Computer Science with a Master in Management and Business Consulting by the Lisbon University.
At this moment is based in the USA working in Giuntilabs North America implementing Learning Content Management Solutions in some of the major companies in the world.
