berry burgess’s Summary

I started as a junior artist at the same time 10cc and the Eagles were popular, in the advertising land of B.C. Before Computers. Times have changed since those days and after 30 years as a Creative Director with North East Ad agencies, it was time to practice what I always preached. ARMADILLO CREATIVE LIMITED is full service marketing agency founded by myself and fellow designer Lee Skinner in 2005. Armadillo offers a variety of strategic communication solutions to a wide variety of clients both large and small both regionally and nationally. We understand that it is not just great ideas that are important, but great communication that delivers effective result for our clients, large and small, time and time again. Across the creative spectrum we help our clients engage people, communicate their business effectively, and most importantly get a return on their investment, from website, e-marketing, SEO or a press campaign - It's not what you spend but what you get that counts.
