Eric Facas’s Summary

Eric Facas, is a digital media strategist and Internet entrepreneur with over ten years of experience at leading Internet companies and start-ups. Eric is a founding partner at Tallwave, a digital consultancy that helps growth focused organizations realize their potential through a mix of business planning, internet strategy and online marketing services.

Prior to Tallwave, Eric spent five years at Google where he played a significant role in important program launches, built new teams from the ground up, and developed sales strategies across platforms ranging from Search Engine Advertising to Social Media. His accomplishments included successfully running Google’s SEM and Agency Councils, growing strategic partnerships, and participating in the creation of the multi-billion dollar AdWords API program. Eric received an exclusive EMG/Founder’s award from Google’s Executive Management Group for his work on this particular product in September of 2008.

Before joining Google in 2004, he spent several years with America Online. While with the AOL division of Time Warner, Eric was responsible for managing the sales programs for their largest content partners such as eBay, CBS, and Monster.com. Eric also successfully integrated Time Warner's online properties including People.com, CNN.com, Money.com and Fortune.com into AOL's interactive media offerings. Eric holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from UC Santa Barbara.

Eric also sits on the Board of Advisors for several Internet Start-ups.
