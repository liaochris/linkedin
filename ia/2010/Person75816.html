Giuseppe Di Fazio’s Summary

Giuseppe is a recent MBA graduate from the UCLA Anderson School of Management with an emphasis on strategy. Already a Dean’s Scholar after his first year, Giuseppe has been indicted into the Anderson Honor Society shortly before graduating. While at school, Giuseppe also worked as a teaching assistant for the Executive MBA Financial Accounting course.

Prior to business school, Giuseppe worked in Italy as a strategy consultant in the management consulting industry. His main focus areas were the tourism industry and the public sector. The most prominent projects have involved the framework and design of a new tourism portal launched in occasion of the Turin 2006 Winter Olympic Games, and the creation of a new strategic development plan for the Pollino Park – Italy’s largest national park. With a strong accounting background, Giuseppe has also co-authored a published study on financial performances and trends of the eight major hotel chains in Italy.

Subsequently, Giuseppe moved into a partner position and was actively involved in the development of an internal project related to a public web portal. He managed the operations and strategic direction, assessing how to create a viable and profitable service while still offering enticing web-based services for its advertisers.
