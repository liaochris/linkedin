About Me:
Hi, I am Marvin Wilson. I do freelance resume writing and job search coaching.

Talents alone, however, do not make for an effective resume. Skills alone, however, do not make for an effective resume. Accomplishments alone, however, do not make for an effective resume.

Resume Writing:
I write resumes and help you find jobs. I also show you how to use your resume to sell or market your skills, talents, work experiences, and career successes.

Sales Professional:
I’m skilled at combining more than 15 years of selling with more than 13 years of writing resumes and job search coaching. I blend sales and job search tactics and strategies to create the most effective resume and job search possible.

Social Media:
I also blend social media tactics and strategies with job search tactics and strategies. While I’m not an expert in social media (or social networking), I do have a passion for social media that just can’t be quenched!

So, you should connect with me. Go ahead. It’ll be my pleasure. And you’ll be glad you did!
