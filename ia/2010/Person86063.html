Yitzchak (Itzak) Ehrlich’s Summary

I have over two decades experience in software development, architecture and management.

My company, Dynamic Ventures, has been at the forefront of innovative software development since 1986, with offices in the Silicon Valley, Romania, and Vietnam.

We have completed hundreds of software development projects for many companies, from small start-ups to large corporations such as Cisco Systems, Volkswagen, eBay and GE Health Care.

We handle ambiguity and come-up with the right intuitive and easy to use solutions, taking on projects that require high skill levels and strong technical expertise.

Some of my most interesting recent projects include computer imaging application to measure the changes of morphing objects from videos, and specialized 3D scanning application.
