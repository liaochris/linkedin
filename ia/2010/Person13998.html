Anna Sponer’s Summary

Italian Canadian Artist | Anna Sponer

Self taught Italian Canadian artist, born & currently living in Canada. My parents immigrated to Canada from the province of Agrigento, Sicily - only daughter and youngest in a family of six children.

My motivation for creating this body of artwork (Expression's of Life - www.annasponer.com) is to bring to visual fruition my unyielding sensitivity to critical moments in the lives of people. The manner with which the human form and general composition reflect and react to these moments, shape my work and move me to exploit my emotional and artistic sensitivity. I am naturally predisposed to tell stories through portraiture and figurative painting.

I aim to engage the viewer’s interest in both the aesthetic and soulful aspect of my paintings as it reflects life’s challenging realities. I make every effort to insure that the composition of each piece, its colour palate and the infinite body of detail is replete with narrative.
