Nick Kizirnis’s Summary

I am dedicated to providing a positive user-centered web experience for my customers, partners and stakeholders.

This online experience is realized through the development of a custom web strategy based on customer and stakeholder research, information architecture, usability testing and rapid prototyping techniques.

I help business and organizational leaders in higher education as well as the legal, automotive, entertainment and finance industries to establish their online goals, understand their customers, prototype concepts, and manage the teams who execute on their vision. I also assist small businesses and start-ups to realize their potential online.
