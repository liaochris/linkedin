Patrick Driessen’s Summary

Dutch born fast-paced visionary, passionate, forward-thinking, entrepreneurial and intuitive business leader, entrepreneur, executive coach, business mentor, author and agent of change with a proven track record of achievement in a career that spans over 20 years in sales management, deal making, strategic consulting, operations, innovation management, finance, marketing and hands-on leadership positions.

Extensive experience in building companies, business units and brands. Patrick has started, managed & rapidly developed high-growth companies including technology start-ups and has extensive experience with new venture finance, business development and operations, across IT&T, FMCG, new media and financial services. He has held senior leadership positions with responsibility for the USA, Europe, Middle East, and the Asia Pacific region, including P&L responsibility for up to US$350M and 675 people.

Proven and tested leadership in managing high performance leadership, sales, solution development, finance and delivery teams in various regions around the world. Reputation for being highly energetic with excellent organizational, time management, leadership, interpersonal and problem solving skills. A "Rainmaker” who's able to continuously create demand, secure more new business and build valuable strategic partnerships.

He has been instrumental in over 20 start-ups, 4 mergers & acquisitions as well as numerous fund raising activities. He now uses his network powerbase, skills and experience to help fellow technology entrepreneurs and their ventures to succeed faster & better and he and his team help large enterprises to enhance their level of leadership, entrepreneurship and innovation.

Used to work out of Amsterdam, London & New York and now works out of Sydney & Singapore.
You can contact me via: pdriessen@email.com
