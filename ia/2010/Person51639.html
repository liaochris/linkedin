David Officer, SPHR’s Summary

Over 18 years of diversified HR leadership and multi-industry experience with a major emphasis on strategic HR. I'm currently leading the HR team at Vesta Corporation, an international e-commerce payment company with over 1,000 employees. Prior to Vesta, I spent 5.5 years at T-Mobile in a number of key HR leadership roles, including leading the Recruiting function of T-Mobile's Customer Service Division with 40 plus recruiters and over 5,500 hires per year in 26 call centers. Prior to T-Mobile, I led a HR team in Oregon for two manufacturing facilities. Previous HR roles include generalist work for Swedish Medical Center, Holland America Cruise Lines, and Airborne Express(DHL).

David Officer, SPHR’s Specialties:

HR Leadership. Being a strategic partner with CEO and key company executives to help drive the companies continued fast growth and high performance. Providing added value and reduced costs through quality hiring, benefits, compensation, employee relations, training, organizational design/change, and strategic planning. HR partnership with key customers to ensure HR is a key contributor in moving the company forward through HR metrics, HR business solutions, and culture development.
