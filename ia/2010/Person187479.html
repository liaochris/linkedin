Shannon Kelly’s Summary

Passionate about understanding consumer culture, I founded In Your Head consultancy to transform my knowledge of marketing, innovation and merchandising into strategies for retail, food & lifestyle industries. By combining bespoke services with good old fashion listening, I customize each project plan with insights and visual elements that pinpoint the values for clients, agencies and innovators alike.

Prior to delving into the heads of my clients, I developed marketing strategies, brand concepts and merchandise assortments for a number of retailers, coffee giants, and lifestyle products and services.

Shannon Kelly’s Specialties:

Trend Research (trendscaping™, retail anthropology, market identification, innovation & cool-finding), Market Exploration (customized category research and analysis to validate projects & assist in launch credibility), Brand Strategies (building brands, brand extensions and sub-brands, positioning & channel strategies), Merchandising (product development, procurement & implementation), Creative development (theme origination, website development, copywriting), Marketing & Promotional Planning.
