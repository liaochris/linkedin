Peter Pugh’s Summary

I am seeking a new assignment -- preferably in the USA. I already have a Green Card, hence require no sponsorship.

I have made Information Technology my career for over 25 years, with Human Capital Management Systems being my primary focus. I support the Human Resource, Talent and Performance Management, Training Management, Payroll, and Time Management functions for Private and Public Sector organizations worldwide.

I am UK based, where I live in Cheshire, having visited from Australia in 1993 and thence meeting my wife – I still prefer the weather in Australia, although England on a sunny late spring day is just about the most beautiful country I have ever seen. I am legally entitled to work in the European Union, United States, and Australasia, having a US Green Card along with dual Australian and British nationality.

I am well versed in all aspects of Oracle Human Capital Management including HRMS, Payroll, Self-Service, Absence Management, Talent and Performance Management, Time & Labor, Learning Management, etc. I also support Oracle Financials (GL, AP, AR, FA, etc.). Additionally, I am very familiar with the Oracle HCM application schema, and very comfortable working with SQL, PL/SQL and Discoverer for the purposes reporting, data analysis and migration.

Over the past 18 years, my clients have included various global organizations such as Bahrain Telecom, Banque Nationale de Belgique, Earth Tech Inc., Lloyds-TSB, Oracle Corporation, Saudi Telecom, Teva Pharmaceuticals, Tioxide, UPC, and Vodafone.
