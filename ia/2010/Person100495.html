Joe Cole’s Summary

Highly accomplished technology executive with proven experience building world class products, and increasing revenue through innovation. Seasoned CTO, CIO, and VP Product Development with a BSEE from SMU. Experience ranges from Lead Architect at Perot Systems, to co-founder of a search engine technology start-up, to serving presently as CIO at an investor-backed software company in the health care sector. Unique blend of hands-on technical expertise and executive management chops. Proven ability to reshape departments, with practical experience in all aspects of product development. Solid reputation for implementing strategies that transform companies, boost product quality and drive bottom line profit.

Joe Cole’s Experience

  • CIO

    Symbio Solutions

    (Privately Held; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    Drove a team of 28 and managed a $2.5 million annual IT budget. Reorganized departments and delegated authority to product managers and directors of project management, quality assurance and IT. Established an agile, flexible, roadmap-driven project management methodology, defined all the necessary checkpoints and milestones, determined branching and merging best practices, and defined promotion paths for both roadmap and defect deployments.

    • Achieved transparency through score cards and release notes, and enjoyed a 100% satisfaction rating concerning the communication between development and the rest of the company.
    • Eliminated the need for downtime through the implementation of automated differential builds. Enabled production deployments to occur with no interruption in service to clients.
    • Reduced hardware costs by 40% through virtualization. Implemented SAN storage and VMware host machines, providing a redundant, automatic failover cluster for mission critical servers.
    • Launched a beta version of the company’s third product, Time & Attendance.

  • VP of Products

    Symbio Solutions

    (Privately Held; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    February 2007 — January 2008 (1 year )

    Performed a sweeping bottom-up transformation of nearly everything development- and IT-related. Installed, configured and trained personnel to use Microsoft Team Foundation Server, ReSharper, Sharepoint 2.0 and WhatsUp Gold. Migrated and reorganized entire code base to standardize solution- and project-level organization. Developed a core architecture framework, defined best practices and implemented a template that was used as a go-forward example for both. Customized work item layout and workflow to enforce code reviews and ensure QA testing. Corrected inadequacies in the production environment, including the creation of a web farm, a SQL cluster, a standby database server and a fully redundant remote disaster recovery site.

    • Increased developer productivity by more than 50% while limiting payroll growth to only 25%.
    • Reduced defects by 75% and improved quality by establishing a QA department.
    • Cut the time-to-market for new features by more than 50% by pursuing an iterative design process as opposed to iterative implementation.
    • Enhanced company sales by creating standalone sales laptops that leveraged virtualization to enable product demos without the need for internet connectivity.
    • Launched the company’s second product and helped secure the first 100+ hospital client.
    • Received company’s “Most Valuable Player” award for 2007.
