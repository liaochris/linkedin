Stephen Moulton SPHR’s Summary

Author of the book "The CEO’s Advantage – 7 Keys for Hiring Extraordinary Leaders."

Stephen Moulton is the President and Chief Insight Officer of Action Insight, author, software inventor, and competency guru. Using these skills, he has helped clients reduce turnover by as much as 69% and increased the quality and fit of employees at the same time.

Many organizations struggle with getting the right people in the right seats at the table. Stephen has innovated the combination of competency interviewing and behavioral base interviewing to increase selection reliability. In addition, he has created a paradigm changing approach to competency based performance management coaching.

In addition, Stephen has developed an HR Management Software that includes a competency dictionary for each level of healthcare leadership.

With over 20 years of corporate experience Stephen found that if you don’t make it easy for leaders to accomplish their objectives they won’t do it.

Stephen has worked for such companies as Lockheed and McDonnell Douglas, and some hospitals and hospital systems including: Lee Memorial, Shands Healthcare, Columbus Regional, and Avera Sacred Heart.

His educational background includes a Masters Degree in Management from the University of Redlands, California, and two professional designations from UCLA, one in Industrial Relations and the other in Organization and Human Resource Development.
