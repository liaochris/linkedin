Stefan Vittori’s Summary

Tangram 3DS is a visualization & design solutions company providing customized services in design visualization/animation for architectural, industrial, millwork and furniture design; 3D library creation; creation of architectural presentations in print and digital media; multimedia presentations, DVD video and motion graphics; and graphic design consulting, as well as the most contemporary photo-simulation techniques and technologies available today.
