David Deziel’s Summary

Consider me and my company your "outsourced marketing department" — a resource totally dedicated to your success in the marketplace. Or engage my services to leverage the creativity and effectiveness of your internal marketing staff. Either way, I bring a high-level of marketing know-how to your business and a proven approach to:

1. Define clear and compelling marketing strategies and core messages.
2. Build a results-oriented marketing plan.
3. Implement creative, effective marketing and promotional solutions with excellent project management skills.

At Nebesek, we develop a strong understanding of your business and work within your budget to deliver the high quality and marketing results you need to succeed.

Importantly, we're not locked into a single marketing solution. We'll recommend what's truly best for your business and get it accomplished - from web sites, online marketing, and printed sales support materials, to social media, direct mail, and traditional advertising. Our bottom line? To make your bottom line grow and thrive. In short, we'll think and act like your V.P. of Marketing.

I draw from almost 25 years of marketing success across a variety of industries including professional and business services, financial services, health care, retail, nonprofits, specialty manufacturing, technology and higher education. And I'm learning something new everyday!

PS: If you are wondering, Nebesek is an Abenaki (Native American New England) word meaning “at the lake.”
