Adam Creighton’s Summary

Short Version: I'm a guy who makes sure things get done.

Longer Version: I make games, build teams, grow market share, and increase revenue -- and I like to be creative and motivating about it. I believe you can be passionate, ethical, fun, and professional while making big things happen for yourself and people around you.

I'm a business development and technology leader who has built teams, launched & improved products, and grown business & revenue across multiple companies and markets (B2B and B2C).

Currently, I'm a producer at Junction Point (a Disney Interactive Studio), working on the Epic Mickey video game for Nintendo's Wii platform.

Prior to disney, I was the Product Manager for Emergent Game Technologies, a game engine and technology provider. I was responsible for the features and market relevance of our products (Gamebryo and Gamebryo LightSpeed), which included building, maintaining, and maximizing those products in the market, working directly with licensees and prospects, and translating between business, marketing, & technical groups.

Life before Emergent included several years at Visa, the world's largest payments company. I was responsible for business development and managing large-budget local and worldwide software programs and engineering teams.

I moved from financial services to gaming because I'm passionate about interactive story-telling, and working in gaming lets me leverage my professional enterprise background and my independent creative skills.

I'm also a professional actor in voice, film, commercial, animation, video game, and other projects.
