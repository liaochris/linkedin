Peter Ekman (peter@peterekman.com)’s Summary

My partner (Jim DeBonis) and I raised enough capital from private equity firms and as a team we secured an equity and distribution partnership with W.J. Deutsch (the 4th largest wine company in the US) to buy 8 brands and 5 wineries from Constellation Brands June 10, 2008. Geyser Peak, Buena Vista Carneros, Gary Farrell, Atlas Peak, Columbia Winery, Covey Run, XYZin and Ste. Chapelle.

Up to recently, I have been the President for Cosnetino Signature Wineries, been developing a handful of entrepreneuerial initiatives within E-Commerce, Wine and Consumer Goods (www.organicbouquet.com, Epic Commerce Inc., Chance Fine Wines LLC), and a social networking startup (www.piczo.com).

Prior to these companies, I was the CEO for Wine.com, and had executive and management positions with companies such as Procter & Gamble, Mars Inc., LVMH, and Icon Media Lab.

I am an international CEO and Executive with a exceptional successful Turnaround-Growth career in the wine industry, consumer goods, and eCommerce industries. [Procter & Gamble, Mars Inc., Wine.com, LVMH, Organic Bouquet Inc.]
