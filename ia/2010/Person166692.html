Paulina Friedel’s Summary

GRAPHIC DESIGN is my LIFE'S PASSION.

I was born in Poland but have spent my life travelling around the world and this is what makes my projects a unique mix of european style and world-wide influences.

I'm an experienced graphic designer with a unique, modern, cool and classy taste, unfailing positive energy, passionate about my work, flexible, easy to work with and still affordable ;)

I have owned my company ArtNet and the popular music website TEKSTY.pl since 2002 in Poland.
I currently live in Hollywood California and I've recently launched PUNIAF.Productions. We are proud to be a GREEN Graphic and Web Design Studio!


I have enjoyed working with well known corporations as well as small businesses and music artists.

Feel free to check my websites and if you like my style, let me know what we can help you with!
