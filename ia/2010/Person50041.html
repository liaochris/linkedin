David Albornoz’s Summary

I am an educator, consultant, and activist for social change, a multicultural advocate for equity, and a believer in civic engagement. I hold extensive national and international experience in cross-cultural public relations, outreach to diverse communities, fund raising, marketing and development, as well as business, non-profit, education, and philanthropy management. I advocate for the advance of the communities of color in the USA through equal access to education, empowerment, and community participation.
