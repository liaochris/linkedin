Professional Profile:

Duke T. Khuu, MD, FAAD is a Board Certified Dermatologist by the American Board of Dermatology. He specializes in medical, surgical and non-invasive cosmetic dermatology. Dr. Khuu received his Doctor of Medicine from Stanford University School of Medicine. Prior to medical school at Stanford, he received his Bachelor degree in Biochemistry from University of California at Berkeley. He completed his internship in Internal Medicine at St. Vincent's Hospital in Manhattan, New York. Dr. Khuu then went on to complete his specialty training in dermatology at State University of New York in Stony Brook, where he served as Chief Resident in Dermatology. During his dermatology residency, Dr. Khuu had in-depth training in skin cancer diagnoses and surgical treatments at Memorial Sloan Kettering Skin Cancer Center.

Dr. Khuu also has published extensively and continues to stay at the forefront of the field. He volunteers his time to attend Dermatology Grand Rounds at Stanford University to discuss challenging cases facing community dermatologists. Dr. Khuu see both children and adults for their dermatological needs. Dr. Khuu is an active member of the AAD, SCCMA, CMA. He has hospital privileges at El Camino Hospital and Good Samaritan Hospital.

Dr. Khuu enjoys figure skating, traveling and spending time with his family.
