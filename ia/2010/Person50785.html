David Ginsberg’s Summary

Results-oriented executive manager experienced in creation-to-customer supply chain architecture, execution, and control. Has extensive expertise in the design and reengineering of business processes to become scalable, Lean and controlled. Most importantly, develops the winning teams and creative solutions that enable the supply chain to delight customers… and CFOs.

Proactive and risk-adverse in managing cash, inventory and liability. An excellent negotiator and problem solver with an eye for least total cost solutions. Tenacious in identifying the root-cause of problems, achieving resolution and maintaining accountability across internal functions and external supply chain partners. Loves team building, collaboration and building a shared vision.

Holds an MBA, CFPIM from APICS, Lean Six Sigma certification and has filled the role of business systems analyst (BSA) in parallel to operations responsibilities for more than a decade. Background includes industry presentations, published papers, professional society leadership & service. Technical advisor for supply chain integration providers.
