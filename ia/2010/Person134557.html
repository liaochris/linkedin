Mark Schoneveld’s Summary

I am the Senior Community Manager at XLNTads, parent company to Poptent.net. We are actively connecting our community of 13,000+ independent commercial videographers with major brands such as Nestle, M&M Mars, Unilever, Miller Brewing, Anheuser-Busch, and Callaway Golf for commercial media development. I am active in many areas of the business, from project management, content development, UI design to social media marketing and strategy.

My personal company, Poverty Jet Set Productions, is an award-winning video production and social media company. We created one of the first fully-sponsored web series in Philadelphia, The Illadates Show on uwishunu.com which won a 2008 Bronze Anvil Award from the Public Relations Society of America. We create and consult companies on cutting-edge, web-based new media: video podcasts, blogs, music videos, live music performance videos and documentaries.

I also run an active and growing music blog (http://yvynyl.tumblr.com) that covers music, art and new music marketing strategies using the web.
