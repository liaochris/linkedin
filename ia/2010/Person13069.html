Angela Palmer’s Summary

I am a graduate of the AFI Conservatory (American Film Institute) with an MFA degree in Screenwriting. I spent five years prior to attending AFI in my native New York working as a Production Assistant/Coordinator, 2nd AD and Office PA/Intern. I have worked in production on everything from features and shorts to commercials and music videos. I worked as an intern briefly for Merchant Ivory Productions, where I had the pleasure of working directly with producer Ismail Merchant before he passed away. I enjoy working on a set like no writer does; it's criminal.

My professional goal is to continue writing and get paid for it. I enjoy writing feature films, but am also attracted to television writing. Writing screenplays is a both a challenge and a pleasure on which I hope to base my livelihood in the long-term.

I have been successful in getting my 90's pop culture blog, Damn The Man, Save The Empire! up and running. Please check it out at http://co-optingthe90s.blogspot.com/. If you like what you see, please leave comments and join Damn The Man's Yahoo Group at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/damnthemansavetheempire/. Thanks!

I am also looking for freelance writing work, including blogging to sites other than mine, copywriting, proofreading, etc. I also am available as a Marketing/Publicity consultant if you have the need.

Angela Palmer’s Specialties:

When it comes to Screenwriting, I enjoy writing drama, especially those involving socio-political themes, horror, and (dry, sarcastic, dark) comedy. In general writing, I am inclined toward pop culture, including music and film.
