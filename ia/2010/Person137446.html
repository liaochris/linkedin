Matthew Mesgleski’s Summary

Experienced, energetic and focused corporate executive with over 10 yrs. in healthcare, employee benefits, consulting & service administration. I am committed to delivering innovative, efficient, results-driven voluntary benefits consulting services along with our enrollment solutions.

I have proactively served and partnered with a variety of corporations across the US with multi-state and international locations. My experience and committed service delivery model focuses on an array of enrollment, communication and outsourced administrative services custom designed to suit the unique needs of each of my clients with a cost neutral solution that will provide your group an intuitive and interactive benefit program.

My enrollment solutions have enabled companies to consolidate the communication, enrollment and administration of all benefit programs, group and voluntary, with a single vendor.

My management style is collaborative in nature focusing on consultative strategies and “outside the box” approach. My early training in the US Military has been paramount for my customer service delivery and passion. I take pride in my proactive customer service, high level standards and business ethics. I have successfully built many long term relationships with senior executives and government professionals across the country.

I am currently involved with Knights of Setonia, several Chambers of Commerce and donate my time to local charity events. I value relationships and work hard to build and maintain them.
