Jeanne Schad’s Summary

Jeanne Schad, President and CEO of Internal Relations, is a passionate believer in the process of coaching for business results.

In July of 2007, she formed Internal Relations, combining her resources in coaching and insider's knowledge of corporate environments. Internal Relations is a single source for all business coaching needs. Jeanne has built a network of over 150 coaches worldwide who share her understanding of corporate culture and are skilled in helping people work through human challenges that come up on the job. These coaches are carefully matched to corporate environments to ensure a good fit and quick results.

To keep her coaching skills fresh, Jeanne enjoys personally coaching Gen X women who are going through professional transitions: exploring new careers, working through changes in business or stepping into leadership roles.

Jeanne is also active in the Los Angeles coaching community, serving as the VP/Marketing for the LA Chapter of the Professional Coaches and Mentors Association, coach advisor for the mentorship program for Step Up Women's Network, founding member of coaching program for residents of the Santa Monica Homeless Shelter and an active member of the International Coach Federation and OD in LA organization.

She resides in Venice, CA with her husband, John.
