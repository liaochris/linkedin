Joe Brown’s Summary

Joe Brown, Slope Resources’ Founder and Principal, possesses 20 years of management consulting experience.

Slope Resources believes that a nonprofit organization, in order to fulfill its mission, requires as high a degree of organizational effectiveness as any other endeavor — if not higher. We also believe that the key to that effectiveness lies in the success of the organization’s people.

To support effectiveness at the organizational and individual levels — and thus mission fulfillment — Slope Resources provides nonprofit organizations of all types and sizes with affordable access a range of human resources and organization management consulting services. Our unique strength lies in creatively integrating multiple disciplines to deliver on-target solutions.

We partner with our clients to develop innovative, effective, and affordable solutions tailored to the unique needs of each organization and its people. We take great pride in the longevity of many of our client relationships. Our history of repeat engagements and referrals serves as a testament to our success and the value we provide.

Founded in 1998, Slope Resources is located in the New York City metropolitan area, and provides services to organizations in the Northeast and beyond.
