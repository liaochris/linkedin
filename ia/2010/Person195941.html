Steve Potestio’s Summary

Creative industry Human Resources, Talent Management and Operations Management expert dedicated to building people positive cultures to help businesses grow.

Hired and placed over 1300 people in over 50 Portland creative service firms and with corporate clients including: Nike, adidas, Columbia Sportswear, Providence, Knowledge Learning Corporation, Legacy Health Systems, Portland State University, Webtrends, Wacom, Xerox, Hollywood Entertainment, Intel, OHSU and many more in the last 13 years locally.

Executive management experience at some of the fastest growing private companies in Oregon making the top 100 in 2004, 2005, 2008 and 2009.

Portland's most connected creative and marketing talent management leader.

Skilled in Employer/Employee relationship dynamics.

12 years of creative agency experience in executive management in human resources and operations, copywriting, graphic design, traffic management, and account services.

17 years of experience in creative staffing and as creative recruiter, marketing recruiter, technical recruiter, administrative recruiter and management recruiter for creative agencies and internal marketing communications departments.

Career coaching and development specializing in the creative and marketing industries.

Has spoken/presented at Portland State University, University of Oregon, Pacific Northwest College of Art, The Art Institute, American Institute of Graphic Arts, American Institute of Architects, Public Relations Society of America, Entrepreneurs' Organization Accelerator and Federal Department of Corrections.

Managing Director and Owner of 52 Limited, Portland's only locally owned and operated full-service creative staffing agency, placement and consulting business servicing the creative and marketing communications industries in Portland, Seattle and the West Coast.

Director of Operations and Human Resources for Via Training, one of Portland's largest interactive agencies.

Steve Potestio’s Specialties:

Creative staffing and creative recruiting, talent management, corporate and creative agency human resources and operations, career coaching and development, advertising graphic design, writing and account services, human resources consulting, company development and culture.
