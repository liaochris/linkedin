Jennifer Walker’s Summary

Jennifer Walker specializes in corporate identity systems, website, publication and interactive design. Since 1999, this Madison native has assisted ad agencies, television stations, and marketing departments with graphics, animation, web design and social media branding. She particularly enjoys helping new companies establish their first public image. That initial image is critical. It can make or break a new venture. She serves approximately 100 clients each year. She works with companies as diverse as the University of Wisconsin to HR Effect - a one person human resources company . One of her recent award-winning designs is the logo for the DVD In the Red Zone: The History of Camp Randall. Walker believes that no matter what size a company is, consistent application of a corporate identity is absolutely essential for success. “Anything less than consistency spells confusion,” says Walker. “All else being equal, a company with a strong, well-applied graphic identity will beat less well-identified competitors every time.”
