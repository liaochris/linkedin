Chuck Harold’s Summary

Celebrating 134 years in business, McRoberts is oldest and 26th largest security agency in America, providing approximately two million hours of uniformed guard service annually to clients in fourteen geographic markets and ten vertical markets, including special events such as the Academy Awards.

Our offices are located in the heart of Hollywood on the Sunset Gower Studios. We specialize in all aspects of entertainment security including studio protection, content protection and stalker/threat management.

McRoberts has been certified as a Women Business Enterprise (WBE) by the Women Presidents Educational Organization (WPEO), a regional partner of the Women’s Business Enterprise National Council (WBENC).
