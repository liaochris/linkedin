Christy Stanley’s Summary

Christy specializes in Information Technology (IT) process design, documentation, and IT security and controls integration. She focuses on assessing and redesigning processes and controls around logical security, change and release management, and separation of duties within the IT environment. Christy also works with her clients to assess and improve testing procedures for new technology implementations and to develop metrics frameworks and specific measurements to monitor processes on an ongoing basis. These metrics focus on the efficiency and effectiveness of processes, compliance with regulations, and overall quality control to aid continual process improvement. Christy also aids her clients in determining reasonable targets and thresholds for these metrics.

Additionally, Christy has helped clients develop control frameworks based upon COBIT, ISO 27000, HIPAA, PCI, and other industry standards or guidelines. She has assisted clients in streamlining their IT SOX controls and in developing a process for managing compliance within the IT organization. She has also developed several trainings around IT business processes, IT Change Management, IT controls, data and identity theft, and compliance management.

Recently, Christy helped a large financial client charter, design, and implement a new department, which was responsible for monitoring compliance with new regulation-driven processes and enforcing quality throughout the processes. She performed in a project management and business analysis role and developed extensive documentation for the department, including processes, procedures, tools, training, and metrics. Christy also has helped a large manufacturing client redesign its IT Change Management processes to include greater efficiencies and proper controls integration and form tactical remediation plans around ongoing process improvement.

Christy Stanley’s Specialties:

Christy specializes in Information Technology process design and documentation and in the analysis and assessment of IT security and controls. Christy has accumulated over 4 years of experience assessing ITGC and SDLC controls in addition to over 10 years of advanced training and experience in business writing. In addition, Christy has obtained certification in ITIL (IT Service Management Practitioner: Release & Control).
