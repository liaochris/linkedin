Chris Bressi, Senior eDiscovery Director’s Summary

Christopher Bressi is a seasoned eDiscovery and Instructional Design expert who currently serves as a Senior Level eDiscovery consultant for Aspect Consulting, Inc.

He also serves as an Instructional Design and Portfolio Professor at various colleges and career schools.

Chris has over eleven years of senior level educational management experience serving as a Law and Justice Department Chair, Trainer, Security Professor, Dean, Director of Education and Site Director.

He is very proficient in various emerging areas of Instructional Design that revolve around Social Networking.

Electronic discovery (or e-discovery) refers to discovery in civil litigation which deals with information in electronic format also referred to as Electronically Stored Information(ESI). Electronic information is different from paper information because of its intangible form, volume, transience and persistence.

Also, electronic information is usually accompanied by metadata, which is not present in paper documents. However, paper documents can be scanned into electronic format and then manually coded with metadata. The preservation of metadata from electronic documents creates special challenges to prevent spoliation.
