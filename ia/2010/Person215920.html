Mary Wilson’s Summary

Results oriented career coach, trainer, consultant and business communicator with extensive experience in both public and private sector organizations. Proven skills in coaching people in transition, writing stand-out resumes, developing leaders, designing and delivering training, building teams, and resolving conflict. Specialized expertise in Federal government career transition. Draw on broad experience as a human resources professional, business publications editor and coach to help individuals discover and market their unique strengths and overcome roadblocks to success. Consistently delight clients with services that enhance individual performance and organizational effectiveness. Named as one of “101 Best Job Search/Career Experts” on Twitter.

Inscape Publishing Authorized Distributor (DiSC and other profiles).

Independent distributor of the ENGAGE Card System, a simple and effective tool for leaders to encourage conversation with employees, strengthen connections and increase engagement.

Provided career transition services for public and private sector organizations. Coached, wrote resumes, assisted with online job searches, conducted skills workshops.

Coached senior managers, including debrief of 360 feedback.

Designed/facilitated strategic planning and teambuilding process for senior management team that enabled them to move forward as one.

Coached cross-functional team charged with development and implementation of innovative approach to employee development in start-up company.
