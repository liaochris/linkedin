Heidi Cool’s Summary

I have over 15 years experience in Web Development with a background in marketing that includes advertising, direct mail, copywriting, design, photography, product marketing and Internet marketing.

As a marketer and Web strategist I develop Web sites and social media plans that are meant to serve the needs of both my clients and their target audiences. Useful content, whether delivered via Web site, blog or other channels attracts visitors. If that content is then presented in a way that is easy-to-find, users are more likely to explore our sites, act on our calls to action, link to our resources and return in the future for more.

When building Web sites I apply World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) standards, semantic HTML mark-up, and search engine optimization techniques to ensure a stable browsing experience--and increased "findability"--on a variety of platforms. In packaging and arranging the content I pay close attention to navigation so that visitors can easily find whatever information they are seeking.

I apply similar concepts to social media. In developing social media plans I find it is important to establish measurable goals, research where one's audience spends time online, then meet them there on their terms.

Web development and social media are constantly evolving. For clients who want to learn more, or wish help to get started with the planning process, I also offer consulting services. Whether you want to understand how Twitter works, wonder if blogging will serve your needs or want to learn more about measuring social media, I can teach you how to get started. I can meet one-to-one in person (or via Skype) as well as with small groups.

To learn more about my Internet philosophy, visit http://www.heidicool.com where you will find my blog (which includes My Planning Your Web Site Tutorial), my portfolio and other resources.
