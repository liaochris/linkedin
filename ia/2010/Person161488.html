Paul Thomsen’s Summary

Since 1995 I've specialized in Microsoft Systems Management Server (SMS), now called System Center Configuration Management (ConfigMgr or SCCM). I've served Microsoft, SMS customers, and the community in a variety of roles, including engineering, administration, technical writing, presentations, and training.

Prior to that I was an OpenVMS (as in VAX/VMS) system manager and a programmer (Basic and MUMPS, primarily for line-of-business applications). I have also supported a variety of other technologies, including TCP/IP, PATHWORKS, and IBM System/34's.
