UPDATE January 2011: Please contact me re. job opportunities in SoCal as I peddle between NY and LA regularly.
_____
If you don't treat the brain first you are only managing the symptoms of the disease!

Do you know that diabetes type-2 is due to a deficiency in dopamine in your brain? That hypertension and anxiety is due to a deficiency in GABA? And that the reason why you crave carbs at night is because you are deficient in serotonin!

I specialize in brain chemistry and nutrigenomics: how nutrition activates our genes. My work is centered around brain science due to the simple fact that our brain controls our body.

Every imbalance - be it physically or mentally - is linked to specific neurotransmitters in our brain being out of whack. Neurotransmitters act like communicators to your body and are built up by specific amino acids and cofactor nutrients. Once you understand how your eating changes your brain, you can prevent genetic disposition from becoming genetic determination. It all starts in the brain. Simple, really.

Eating right for you depends on how your brain is wired - that is why people respond differently to the same diet. You cannot cure yourself with medication, simply because it does not build up your brain, but only manages the symptoms.

When you change your brain, you change your body. Weight loss has nothing to do with willpower, but all about brainpower. If you are deficient in the communicator in your brain responsible for burning fat (dopamine), you will find that you are constantly struggling with your weight, even though you hit the gym and follow a ridiculously low-cal diet. It's time to understand the science behind METABOLIC OPTIMIZATION!

Starving and restricting is so yesterday - brain science is the NEW BLACK! And it's here to stay! Welcome to the health care specialists of the future!
