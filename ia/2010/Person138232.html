Matt Vittal’s Summary

I began my professional career working for Canon Business Solutions selling office equipment and software. At Canon, I had the opportunity to hone my sales skills and develop a strong understanding of business, sales, and marketing.

While working at Canon I started working on a web-based business. When it came time to choose a web developer, I found that most companies were very difficult to work with. Most of the companies seemed to focus on the wrong things. I was starting a business, so I was interested in a website that created a great experience for the end user and made it very easy for the prospect to become a paying customer. The firms I spoke with seemed more concerned about adding animation, excessive video, and other extras than creating a website that focused on converting customers.

This experience showed me that there was a real need for an Internet marketing and website design firm that understood business and how the Internet could be used to add to a company’s bottom line. Most business owners don’t have the time it takes to keep up with the constant changes happening with the Internet. They need a company they can trust to handle all aspects of their online presence, a true business partner that is as interested in their company’s success as they are.

When my business partner and I decided to start MetaLOGIC Design, we knew that successful businesses wove customer service into every aspect of the business. That is why we have made it our mission to provide the very highest level of customer service in our industry. We never want one of our customers to have the same poor experience that we had with those other Internet marketing /website design firms.

We are not designing websites to be works of art, we are creating business tools. That is why every aspect of our client’s website and marketing plan is tracked. We focus on getting results and the only way to do that is to test, track, and make continuous improvements.
