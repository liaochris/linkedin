Frank Ableson’s Summary

I run a 10 person technology services firm in Northern New Jersey. Our firm prides itself on delivering value to our customers with the right technology, in the right doses, and at the right time. We are not a "technology for technology's sake" shop.

Our firm has the following offerings:

Network Services: This is infrastructure hardware and services including Servers, Workstations, Switches, Routers, Firewalls, Security. Wireless, Email, Web Hosting & Design. Think Cisco and Microsoft.

Software Development:

1. Custom website design including ecommerce and Content Management Systems for businesses, organizations and local government.

2. Custom mobile and wireless solutions for iPhone, Android, BlackBerry, WebOS and Windows Mobile.

Training & Testing: Private Vocational School approved by the State of New Jersey and State of Pennsylvania. Courses include the basics of Windows, comprehensive Microsoft Office with Certification as well as Quick Books accounting software.

Frank Ableson’s Specialties:

My personal area of interest and specialization is in the wireless application space. I write a column for Linux Magazine where I share what I am working on from week to week.
