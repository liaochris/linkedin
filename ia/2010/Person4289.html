AgravatInternational Medical & Dental Academy’s Summary

Mission
AIMDA provides a variety of programs that will increase the knowledge of our participants. By using the appropriate methods, training, materials, innovative and evidence-based educational activities, we develop, provide and evaluate learning opportunities and resources and as a result, our CDE participants develop technically and diagnostically skilled, scientifically based professionals who are committed to continued professional growth and development.
Vision
Health care is a constantly evolving field with many exciting changes and breakthroughs. As a busy clinician and practitioner, you may find it hard to keep up with these advances and may also end up feeling overwhelmed with the amount you need to learn.
AIMDA is global. Our faculties are regular speakers at National and International conferences and are much sought after for their knowledge and skills. Each of our programs are carefully put together by clinicians and surgeons who have years of experience and are themselves into full time practice. As a participant, you attend not just lectures but also get the opportunity to view live clinical and surgical demos, hands-on practice, special discounts on equipment and materials, and much more!
We will serve as a benchmark for effective community and professional collaborations that promote innovations in curriculum, evidence-based clinical education, technology, and research.
