Fian Arroyo’s Summary

Illustrator Fian Arroyo has been creating award-winning illustration and character design for over 20 years. He is an idea person with a creative mind and a quick-draw, with the proven ability to conceptualize and execute fun, creative solutions with proficiency and integrity within the deadlines provided. Besides creating high quality artwork in a professional and dependable manner, Fian is very personable and a blast to work with and it shows in his work.

Fian Arroyo serves advertising, editorial, corporate, publishing and toy and game markets clients with customized digitally created illustrations, in bit-map or vector formats, to fit his particular clients needs.

Fian Arroyo’s Specialties:

Use of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Corel Painter, Adobe Flash and the Wacom Intuos drawing tablet to create unique commercial artwork.
