Dean Selleseth’s Summary

I’m a research scientist with over 23 years of pharmaceutical research experience at GlaxoSmithKline, GlaxoWellcome, Burroughs Wellcome, and Pfizer specializing in virology and metabolic diseases. I have experience working with HSV, HPV, HBV, HCVcc, HCV replicons, HCMV, PHIV, RMLV, and FIV. I have several years of experience doing research and running assays under BSL-3 containment conditions. Over 15 years of experience testing new antiviral compounds in small animal models of viral disease. Self motivated and enthusiastic with a strong work ethic, plus excellent multitasking, troubleshooting, and problem-solving skills. I have a strong ability to work both independently and in a matrix team environment to achieve goals. Extensive experience with high- and medium-throughput screening, data analysis and compound triage to progress lead identification and optimization. I also have experience in the robotics and automation, plus the development and optimization of cell-based assays. I possess excellent communication, organizational and interpersonal skills with critical attention to detail. My research has supported several compounds that are now marketed drugs, such as Zovirax, Valtrex, Retrovir, Ziagen, Epivir, and Combivir. I am also a board-certified Medical Technologist and Clinical Laboratory Scientist with clinical laboratory experience at Duke University Medical Center and Abbott-Northwestern Hospital.
