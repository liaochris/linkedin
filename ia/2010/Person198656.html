Scott Weiner’s Summary

I have extensive jury trial experience. In my first year I completed 35 Jury Trials to verdict. Subsequently I was sought out and hired by competing firms to do trial work which led me to start my own firm. From there we started doing pure Employment Discrimination cases in Federal Court. Most of these cases resulted in Mediation either by an independent third party or a Magistrate Judge. In the six years I ran the firm we settled 90% of our cases prior to trial.

I have done well over 200 Arbitrations, and a large number of Mediations.

I've participated in well over 300 Depositions.
