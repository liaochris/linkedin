Bryan Mull’s Summary

BMull Consulting is a team of designers, web developers, and internet marketing professionals that provide web design and internet marketing consulting. Our diverse team has years of experience working with Fortune 500 companies and we offer the same technology and techniques used by large corporations to the business owners of the Greater Reading, PA area.

We keep a very open mind when it comes to website development. Many web developers want to build customized solutions for everything. We choose not to “reinvent the wheel” and often use pre-existing technology or template based solutions to meet clients needs. More often that not this allows us to produce websites that are within budget and still completely meet the needs of our clients.

A major focus of BMull Consulting is internet marketing. Great looking websites mean nothing if they are hard to use or can’t be found in keyword searches. Our web development process is based around search engine optimization and every website we produce Every website we produce keeps in mind basic search optimization strategies to give our clients a leg up on their competition.
