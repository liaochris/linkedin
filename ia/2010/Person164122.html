Phil Strauss’s Summary

Business executive, Duke Law/Haas MBA graduate with 17 years of experience, including strategy/business development (H5, Xerox), VP/GC and Corporate Development (M&A) for Nasdaq-listed enterprise software companies (Brio, Actuate), large-company strategic licensing/partnerships/M&A (Adobe), venture-backed market research technology company VP/GC (MarketTools, Inc), Wall Street corporate law (Shearman & Sterling), and commercial litigation (Jones Day).

Past and present Board member/advisor for established companies (DataBeacon, Lance-Kashian & Co.) and promising start-ups (WeStayGreen, ACV). Past-President of the San Francisco Bay Area Chapter of the Association of Corporate Counsel.

Adjunct professor (business law, Haas School of Business), and aspiring screenwriter in spare time.

Phil Strauss’s Specialties:

Extensive experience with
* eDiscovery and litigation management
* commercializing new technologies
* applying technology to internal operations
* all legal aspects of business, including technology licensing, corporate law, SEC/M&A/compliance (including Sarbanes-Oxley), HR/employment, international matters and mentoring/teaching/training.
