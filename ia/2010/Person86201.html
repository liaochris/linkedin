Ivan Pecel’s Summary

When Ivan Pecel does a show, you get high energy and intense comedy. Able to entertain any size crowd, Ivan blends world class juggling, audience participation, and clean humor into a show that is sure to please any audience.
Have you ever seen a man juggle ping-pong balls using only his mouth? Ivan does that… Have you ever seen a man balance a flaming torch on his forehead, drop it back behind himself and blindly kick it back up over his head? Of course you haven’t… don’t be ridiculous… Ivan does that… EVERY TIME!!! Ivan’s show is overflowing with his original comedy/juggling routines combining everything from rolling a single ball around his face to juggling a human being from the audience to some of the most intense technical juggling routines ever assembled. This is what makes him a favorite among Fortune 500 Companies, Colleges, Cruise Ships, and Comedy Clubs.
One of the main factors that differs Ivan from other performers is the fact that most jugglers are either very technically skilled, but don’t have any other production value to their show… or they aren’t very skilled, but are very funny. Ivan's show is both. You will see world class juggling as well as a clean, funny, and entertaining show. He brings a new sense of style and charisma to each one of his performances that most people have not seen before. He is the new generation of performers and is one of the best at what he does. He is a 9-time world champion juggler, winning gold, silver, and bronze medals all over the world since 2000. He has appeared on television on MTV, ESPN, CBS’s King of Queens, and most recently on NBC’s America’s Got Talent. Leave EVERY notion of what you thought a juggler was at the door. Ivan is guaranteed to change your mind about juggling. If you didn’t love this art form before his show, you will afterwards… Or at least tolerate it…
