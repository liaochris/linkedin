Richard Weltman’s Summary

I am passionate about resolving client business and bankruptcy challenges across diverse industries through outside-the-box thinking.

Unlike many colleagues I genuinely enjoy what I do. Except for my family, my clients come first. I make myself accessible and compulsively reply to emails and return telephone calls promptly.

I bring to Weltman & Moskowitz, LLP my commitment to do the right things in the right order with the right people. On target and on time.

Client service starts with active listening. Early on, I learned listening to clients enabled me to create effective advertising and public relations programs for national food and apparel accounts and regional hospitality companies and nonprofits.

Solid writing and editing skills now empower me to present winning legal arguments persuasively and creatively without ignoring the bottom line.

Our firm intensively leverages its resources and connections to serve lenders, credit unions, real estate developers, owners and management, entrepreneurs, investors, middle-market business owners, contractors, professional practice groups, and individuals, in court rooms and board rooms throughout New York, New Jersey, and elsewhere.

Richard Weltman’s Specialties:

Complex litigation; insolvency, bankruptcy, and debtor-creditor law, including workouts, reorganizations, preference recovery and trustee litigation, and loan modifications; asset purchases and sales; entity formation, governance, control,dissolution, business divorce; website legal audits; web content creation and clearance; intellectual property licensing, copyright and trademark protection; arbitration and mediation; and local counsel representation in all NY and NJ federal and state courts.

    The Executives' Association remains the gold standard business referral organization.   • Founding partner

    Weltman & Moskowitz, LLP

    (Privately Held; Law Practice industry)

    August 1987 — Present (23 years 3 months)

    Our law firm represents clients with confidence, experience and expertise borne from our concentrated focus on skillfully resolving business and bankruptcy problems--including contract disputes--both in and out of court.

    Our commitment is to help clients achieve the best possible results whether facing complex litigation, business transition, crisis management, or other legal challenges.

    In order to manage or minimize client disruption and remain efficient, the firm's attorneys pay close attention to detail and client objectives surrounding document and contract review, preparation, negotiation, and enforcement. Whenever possible, mediation, arbitration or out-of-court alternatives are pursued to end disputes.

    A particular strength is our thorough understanding of debtor-creditor law, including bankruptcy chapters 7, 11, and 13, reorganization, liquidation, stay relief, reaffirmation, lease assumption, assignment and rejection, secured and unsecured claim priority, claim objection and transfer, judgment, lien, and tax relief and enforcement, reclamation, consignment, UCC Article 9 security interests, preference, fraudulent conveyance, and other trustee litigation, commercial loan modification, and forbearance.

    Other practice strengths include aggressively representing clients on a full range of contracts--including construction, asset purchase and sale--and counseling early-stage technology (and other) ventures regarding choice of entity, LLC or corporate formation, governance, operation, control, partner and shareholder disputes, dissolution, and corporate divorce; creating appropriate user and privacy policies, and advising website owners and developers on disclaimers and content protection; guiding online owners, sellers, marketers, developers, producers, and publishers with marketing, distribution, and licensing issues; and prosecuting and defending copyright and trademark infringement actions in New York and New Jersey courts.
