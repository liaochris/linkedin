Diana Evans’s Summary

At present I am the owner of an Illustration and Design firm that specializes in the children's market, packaging and surface design. Creating wonderful art that will be shared with many generations is a dream come true for me....I have extensive business and technology experience that gives me a greater edge in working my art career as a business....

I understand quality client service and the need for great detail in any and every project. I come from 15 years of experience in Technology and Business. I have several published pieces and my latest picture book is available in several bookstores.
