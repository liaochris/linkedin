Dermot Foley - Safecon’s Summary

Entrepreneurial by his very nature, Dermot Foley is the founder and Managing Director of Vision Safety Ltd, trading as Safecon Training & Consultancy.

Dermot first began his foray into health and safety in 2001, having worked previously in the pharmaceutical sector in the mid-nineties, before turning to Software Engineering in 1998.
It was through employment with a software development company, Novara that he developed a range of Health and Safety software systems with the company.

Over the last number of years, experience and further educational qualification in Project Management, Quality and a Health and Safety Diploma, he now provides a range of risk management and safety services to the healthcare, manufacturing, financial and pharmaceutical enterprises.

Today, Safecon Training & Consultancy currently employs 9 and uses over 60 subcontractors with an average turnover of € 750k over the past 8 years.

His current role is to oversee the development of the company via interaction with clients and prospective customers from a Project Manager’s point of view.
These duties consist of;

• Planning and successfully delivering large, visible projects with high levels of risk, complexity and change management.

• Providing mentorship and leadership to team leads in project execution

• Working with external clients in a structured PMO framework to budget, schedule and drive health and safety management projects to successful completion

• Manage risk, budget and resources for all Safecon client activity.

• Assessment and continuous improvement of current project management tools and techniques
