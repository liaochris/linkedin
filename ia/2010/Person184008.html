Scott Stewart’s Summary

10+ years experience in system-level embedded hardware/software design environments acting as System Designer, Technical Lead, Principal Engineer, Board Designer, ASIC/FPGA Designer and HW Manager. Released into production numerous PCBs such as backplanes, line cards, switch fabrics, and system controllers using off-the-shelf components or in-house designed ASIC/FPGA’s.

Early-on employee and key contributor at over 5 VC backed start-ups.

Have designed and released to production many high-speed SERDES backplane and midplane designs. Designed and released to production many packet processing, storage and Data Comm Interface cards.

Daily tool usage 2008-2009 includes:
Stack-up Design - Polar Si9000, Ansoft Q3D
Transient Analysis - HSPICE, Agilent ADS, Ansoft Designer
3D Electromagnetic - Ansoft HFSS, SIWave
Statistical Signal Analysis - StatEye and in-house SSA tools.
PCB: Cadence Allegro with full constraint management

Other 2008 Designs completed with:
Mentor HyperLynx - SI analysis. DDR2 SI & timing analysis.
Mentor DxDesigner and Expedition w/CES.
Full PCB Conversion from Mentor to Cadence

Hardware FPGA/ASIC: Verilog/VHDL with Xilinx ISE, Modelsim and embedded processor in FPGA

Test and measurement using Agilent 13GHz real time sampling oscilloscope, TDR's and VNA's
