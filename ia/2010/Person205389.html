Todd Gable’s Summary

My undergraduate degree is in Psychology. After I graduated, I realized that I wanted to better utilize what I'd learned in the business arena, so I completed an MBA with an emphasis on the healthcare industry.

Professionally, I've worked on both the clinical and business side of hospital operations. I feel that has given me a unique perspective on how the hospital and all its parts function. I understand that the business world is a constantly changing arena, particularly in healthcare, so I try and seek out opportunities that will help me to grow and garner a deeper understanding of my industry and the factors that influence it.

I currently work for one of the largest healthcare providers in the country on the reimbursement side of the operation. I work directly with our largest commercial insurance payer to keep the accounts receivable low, which is in the best interest of both sides. I also supervise 11 direct report employees who specialize in collecting payments from the insurance industry. During my tenure, we have also been recognized as an integral piece of a "best practice" model by BlueCross of Florida for our efforts.
