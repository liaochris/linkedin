Stuart McLean’s Summary

A results focused strategic manager and project director, with an innate ability to create and motivate high performance teams that deliver exceptional business outcomes. Highly relationship driven and team oriented, having worked across businesses of all scales, in the Finance and IT industries, in Australia and Europe.

My business experience ranges from small firms of 200 to large international corporations such as Intel and JP Morgan - bringing discipline and order to smaller companies, while driving change in large enterprises.

Specifically this includes IPO preparation, mergers & acquisitions, large scale infrastructure implementations, wholesale business process redesign, and aligning structure to those processes and with the business’ vision.

Financially focused, I am a highly motivated professional with the necessary strategic, operational, and general management competencies to lead and implement wholesale change resulting in sustainable team oriented structures, best of breed customer experience, and superior financial outcomes.
