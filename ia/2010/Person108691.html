Julie Taraska’s Summary

I have spent the last decade assigning, producing, writing, line-editing, and packaging news items and stories on architecture, products, interiors, travel, technology, restaurants, collecting, and cultural trends; my personal passions are the American and European markets, emerging talent, and sustainable design.

Currently I am the writer and editor at Gilt Home, which is part of the e-retailer Gilt Groupe. I oversee all copy for the division, which sells everything from furniture, decor, and rugs to art, food, and wine. I also interview designers and use social media to promote sales efforts.

I am a contributing editor to Metropolis and Interiors, and freelance for numerous publications, including the NY Times, Wired, Fast Company, Wallpaper*, Interior Design, Elle, Azure, and Architectural Record.

In addition I am the cofounder and editorial director of Product Placement, an educational series in which designers from a range of disciplines discuss the influences and processes behind one of their products. The event is held in front of a live audience.

Julie Taraska’s Specialties:

My specialties are European, American, and sustainable design; emerging talent; interiors; and products. I'm equally adept at writing as editing, and can translate the European market to American publications (or vice-versa).
