Lisa Darden’s Education

  • College of Marin

    TV/Film Production -Communications 1989 — 1993

    Lisa Darden's love of communications began in Las Vegas when she completed her first college course in Television Production. Moving to Los Angeles, she had her first experience in film production at the prestigious American Film Institute (AFI) where she worked as an Assistant to the Director in the Directing Workshop for Women (DWW).

    AFI’s Directing Workshop for Women has been a major force in training women in narrative filmmaking since 1974.

    After L.A. came San Francisco, her home for the next 12 years. She attended the College of Marin in San Anselmo, California and continued the gain experience in visual media by working as a professional camerawoman, assistant director, floor director, technical director, and also as a director on several projects. From behind-the-scenes directing of commercials, corporate and music videos to on-camera work and an actress and voice-over artist, Lisa has immersed herself in the communications industry.
