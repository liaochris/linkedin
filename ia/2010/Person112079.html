  • Owner

    Fletcher Thompson Chartered Accountants

    (Accounting industry)

    April 1998 — Present (12 years 4 months)

    Fletcher Thompson was established in 1998 by David Thompson and myself to provide accountancy, advisory and support service to owner managed businesses, alongside the traditional services provided by accountancy firms. The Practice is now run solely by me.

    Our client base consists of owner-managed businesses (sole traders, partnerships and limited companies) and individual taxpayers who want help and advice in dealing with their own tax affairs.

    Our aim is to provide an approachable, friendly but professional service to clients who want more than just a rubber stamp to their year end accounts and tax returns from their accountant.

    Although we mainly cover local areas from Peterborough, Cambridge, Ely, Newmarket, Bury St Edmunds and surrounding areas, the use of email and other electronic technology allows us to keep in touch and exchange information with clients across the country.

    The firm is regulated by the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales.
