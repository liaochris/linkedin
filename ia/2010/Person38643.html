Christer Østergaard’s Summary

Experience:
* 10 years of development experience on the Microsoft platform in full time work.

* Participated in analyzing and developing an extension to www.jobnet.dk, a web site owned by the Danish national labor market board, used to give more than 130.000 users access to unemployment self service online.

* Participated in conceptualizing, analyzing and developing a large system called “The Digital Backpack” used to distribute documents and applications to more than 1400 locomotive drivers at Danish State Railways (DSB).

* Participated in conceptualizing, analyzing and developing a nationwide announcement system for Danish Rails (BaneDanmark) used to deliver announcements at more than 50 train stations in Denmark.

* Team leader for a team of 4 members through a 5 month project. By continually quantifying the status of the project by using Work Breakdown Structures and Task Burndown, we delivered the project before deadline, at the quality the customer asked for.

* Had my own consultancy business for 5,5 years (2003-2007 and 1998-1999).

* Experience of how to lead a team and how to get the best effort and energy from the team.
