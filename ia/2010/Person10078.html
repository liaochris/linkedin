Amy Elizabeth Stewart’s Summary

For nearly 20 years, Amy Stewart has represented corporations in complex business and commercial litigation, including insurance coverage and bad faith litigation, contract disputes, business torts, fraud and misrepresentation, deceptive trade practice claims; construction litigation; healthcare litigation; ERISA litigation; and professional liability litigation.

In addition, Amy represents clients as insurance coverage counsel for claims arising under CGL and specialty liability policies (D&O, E&O, fiduciary, professional liability, EPL). As coverage counsel, Amy advises her clients regarding the existence and scope of coverage, issues of policy interpretation, claims handling matters and claim resolution, primarily with respect to litigation against directors and officers (including securities fraud litigation under state and federal laws, ERISA litigation, shareholder derivative claims and governmental investigations).

Amy Elizabeth Stewart’s Specialties:

Complex insurance coverage litigation, insurance bad faith litigation, complex contract disputes, breach of contract litigation, business litigation.
