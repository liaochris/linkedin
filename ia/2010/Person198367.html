  • Chief strategic planner and key buzz maker

    Garden Media Group/IMPACT Marketing & PR, Inc. -- Your reputation is our mission

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    September 1988 — Present (21 years 6 months)

    Your reputation is your most important asset. At the Garden Media Group, your reputation is our number one priority! We are the leading PR firm in the lawn and garden business. We help shape brand character and build trust and loyalty with customers. Our clients enjoy tremendous coverage in traditional national and regional print media, on mainline broadcast outlets, on the internet and within key social networks and extensively in the gardening trade and consumer media. IMPACT Marketing & PR, Inc. is the parent company of Garden Media Group. Over the years we have represented such diverse giants as Urban Outfitters, Burpee, The Philadelphia Academy of Natural Science, The Philadelphia Flower Show, Trammell Crow, Ball Horticultural Company, The Conard-Pyle Co., Costa Farms, Hines Horticulture, Jones Motor Group, National Penn Bank and the American Mushroom Instititue. Our firm specializes in building profits by building a great reputation.
