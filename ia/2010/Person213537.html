Luke Walling’s Summary

Building things fascinates me. Especially companies.

Under my leadership Walling Data grew from from a home based business to a nationally known software distribution and support company that was ultimately acquired by the fourth largest security company in the world (AVG Technologies).

My goal today is to apply what I know made Walling Data truely great, to a much larger business; A strong focus on customer care, reasonable operation costs, and providing great value to our real bosses (customers). I have been told that I am obsessed with customer service, and I'd agree. It's absolutely true, and that is also one of the primary drivers behind the success of both myself, and my team.

  • President and Founder

    Walling Data Systems, Inc.

    (Computer & Network Security industry)

    August 1994 — June 2010 (15 years 11 months)

    AVG Technologies, USA, Inc. acquired Walling Data Systems on June 9, 2010.

    Walling Data began in 1994 as a young teenagers dream, in the basement of his parents house with no startup funding. Of course that teenager was me. I started the business very young around supplying tech. repair and implementation services, as well as sales – such as white box computer systems. All products were delivered and in many cases setup free, all services were provided onsite at no additional charge – from the very beginning, the business was built around value adds that the competition did not or simply could not offer.

    Today, 14 years later, Walling Data represents a group of divisions that offer tech. distribution and support services to a base of over 75,000 end user and reseller customers throughout North America, break/fix repair services from our two office locations in North Carolina, as well as Virtual IT outsourcing via our GuaranteedIT program. All under the same ownership that started it way back when, in a basement, with no investors or outside funding.
