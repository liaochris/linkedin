Rick Lopatin’s Summary

Senior financial executive with proven leadership, management and technical skills in service, distribution, research and manufacturing organizations. Strong domestic and international background in accounting, financial reporting and analysis, strategic and financial planning, business development, risk management, tax strategies, profit and loss management and treasury activities. Experienced in healthcare organizations including pharmaceuticals, home health, medical products, and software whose ownership ranges from private equity to public to not-for-profits. Proven record of managing organizations through rapid growth and expansion and in financially troubled environments requiring aggressive cash and working capital management and identification and implementation of cost reduction opportunities.
