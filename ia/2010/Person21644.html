Ben Ipema’s Summary

Mr. Ipema is an owner and officer of several small businesses in Michigan, Indiana, and Illinois. President of QuikWall, offering a patented panel system to Exhibit Designers and Builders for use in their convention exhibit design and construction services.

Mr. Ipema has served many charitable and civic organizations in Kalamazoo including Young Life Kalamazoo, Southern Heights Church Council, Kalamazoo Christian Schools, and various organizations dedicated to education, poverty and housing.
