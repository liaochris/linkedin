Marc Hafner’s Summary

Marc Hafner is an accomplished Senior Executive and CEO with over 20 years of successful leadership in companies ranging from a high profile market leading public company to a Fortune 1000 global company from early stage start-ups to "turn around" situations. He has competed effectively against as well as partner with Tier 1 companies such as HP, Dell, EMC, IBM, Alcatel, Cisco, Microsoft, Oracle, Intel with multiple business plans including hardware, software, or solutions in highly competitive industries: IT equipment and solutions, Enterprise Software solutions (both SaaS and deployed), and Unified Communications & Telecommunications for global organizations.
• CEO/GM with demonstrated track record in developing overall vision and leading strategy implementation using leadership skills in achieving success in high growth entrepreneurial environments as well as in the Board room with the ability to build and develop a highly effective team.
• Senior executive level experience includes balance sheet and P&L responsibility, strategic planning and execution, operations, corporate and business development and sales for global operations managing organizations ranging from 50 to 800+ employees.
• Developed relationships with many premier venture funds as well as blue-chip Wall Street firms with proven fund raising experience ranging from IPOs to VC funding having raised almost $200MM.
• Successful track record in M&As, spin-offs, IPO’s, financing and corporate restructuring. Largest acquisition process managed was $400M - largest divestiture process managed was $20M.
• Proven relationship builder as demonstrated by creating strategic alliances with Fortune 1000 global companies thus strengthening operations globally.
• Polished evangelist able to articulate the company’s products, strategies and mission with employees, analysts and the press and customers.
