Curtis L. Luckett III’s Summary

Secure Options, LLC, a Columbus, Ohio based company, provides a suite of services from Business Development Funding (Startup & Working Capital), Comprehensive Identity Theft Shield with Complete Restoration Services, and Affordable Legal Services through Pre-Paid Legal Services, Inc, a New York Stock Exchange Company that is stable (see PPD ticker), growing, and is unmatched in the industry of services provided. Secure Options consults and markets these services on a business to business, family, or individual platform. We are reaching out to educate and empower individuals, families, and organizations, simply because we live in challenging and tough economic times surrounded by an era of complex legal battles and growing identity theft crimes.

Curtis L. Luckett III, President & Owner, began his career with the United States Army, serving honorably as a SATCOM Supervisor in the Nuclear Command & Control Division from 1994-2002. After the military, he returned to college, earning a scholarship his first year as a walk-on football athlete, and eventually all four years competing and then graduating with a degree in Business Management from Benedictine College in Kansas (2006). Post college he competed as a Semi-Pro football athlete for two years in Kansas, while managing a successful Fine Jewelry Retail outfit with Fred Meyer Jewelers. There he earned National & Regional honors (2007 & 2008). In 2009, he returned to his home in Columbus, Ohio and sought a career with Yellowbook USA. In just one year he was one of Ohio's top Account Executives at Yellowbook in several sales categories, and was a specialist in New Media Marketing, Direct Response Marketing, and Leads Generation. Curtis produces performance results, not just promises.
