Jessica Manna’s Summary

As VP & Chief Marketing Officer at Residential Finance, Jessica Manna oversee's the corporate marketing communications, public relations, and brand management strategies for the national mortgage lender.

Prior to joining RFC, Jessica was VP of Business Development at Intellidyn where her team was awarded the 2008 “Gold Consumer” Award by MarketingSherpa for a customized e-mail marketing solution developed for GMAC Mortgage.

In 2008, DM News named Jessica their #2 for their ”Top 30 Under 30 Award“ listing the nation’s top marketers under 30 years old.

In 2009, the National Center for Database Marketing awarded Residential Finance the award for Analytics and Modeling excellence.

Jessica attended Florida Atlantic University for Marketing and Management and is currently completing Six Sigma Blackbelt Certification at The Ohio State University.

Jessica is on LendingTree's Lender Advisory Council and is a member of the American Marketing Association, Public Relations Society of America, Mortgage Bankers Association, American Bankers Association, and previously served on the Financial Services Council of the Direct Marketing Association.

Trainings and certifications include: MarketingSherpa Email Marketing Certified Professional; Mortgage Bankers Association FHA Product Training; ProActive Training & Consulting Selling as a Consultant; Dale Vermillion's Mortgage Champions Mortgage Sales & Leadership Training; and National Center for Database Marketing Database Marketing Certificate.

In 2009, Jessica has spoken at the Direct Marketing Association's DM Days Conference in NY and at Net.Finance's Multi-Channel Marketing Conference in Vegas.

When not working Jessica enjoys cooking and taking cooking classes, reading, gardening, traveling, and mentoring with Big Brothers Big Sisters where she's been a "Big" for 2 years.
