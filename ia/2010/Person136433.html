Massimo Galarza’s Summary

Massimo Galarza provides vision and innovation through brand identity and exclusive product design as well as product development through global outsourcing.

“Innovation is improving and reinventing great ideas – thinking outside of the box while still understanding market demands - and leveraging design integrity to maintain a brand image that is timeless.”

Over the past 15 years, Massimo has provided major licensed name brands, retailers, specialty stores and design firms with unique and exclusive gift product and apparel designs. His reputation in the industry reflects his commitment to excellence, fast turn-around-times, sustained high output levels, quality workmanship and attention to detail.

Massimo’s years of experience in orchestrating product development through overseas resources is a major asset to any project. His experience and expertise includes:

• Product ideation driven by market and consumer analysis in the U.S.
• Product design to specification
• Creative product design
• Integrated product-brand design development
• Understanding of Merchandising strategy and product line development
• In-store merchandising and visual display strategies
• Product line presentations
• Package Design
• Management of outsource engineering and manufacturing to meet design specifications
• Directing and managing Asian sourcing teams to assure design integrity and attention to details from manufacturing sources

Massimo was retained as the Gift Product Design & Development Consultant to Things Remembered, a specialty retailer, with 600 US stores. He was responsible for many of their high dollar volume items and worked in conjunction with merchandising teams and executives to develop new product concepts and strategies for growth.

Prior to Things Remembered, Massimo was a product design and development firm serving the several large licensing companies, retailers and wholesalers.
