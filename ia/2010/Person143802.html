Michael Wyrick’s Summary

Goal: Provide clients with mature and cost effective best practice solutions.

Hands on service provider with experience and expertise. When additional resources or technical knowledge are required I only use people that have worked with me or for me to staff projects. SATISFACTION GUARANTEED!

Satisfied clients include: J&J, Amgen, Boston Scientific, CIBA Vision, Eisai, Eli Lilly, Genentech, Insmed, Novartis, Otsuka, Pfizer, Schering-Plough, Sepracor, Sanofi Pastuer, Stiefel Labs, Watson, and Wyeth.

Industry thought leader having lead PhRMA and PDA Computer Validation committees which created industry standards for: validation, requirements/design, security, change control, testing, supplier management, auditing, training, and Part 11 compliance.

Member of: IVT Editorial Advisory Board, FDANews 21 CFR Part 11 Advisory Board, and Horwood Publications Editorial Advisory Board.

Former member of: GAMP Americas Steering Committee (7yrs) and Pharmaceutical Technology Editorial Advisory Board (10 yrs)

Thirty years of pharmaceutical industry experience working for an operating company in a variety of Information Technology and Quality Assurance roles. Six years experience leading Life Sciences Global Validation Consulting Service Organizations (KMI, Inc. a division of PAREXEL, Washington Group International, and IT&E International).

Owner of successful consulting company for the last three years using no advertising, all work coming from client requests.

Michael Wyrick’s Specialties:

Author and instructor for 20 years.

PDA certified Technical Report 32 Auditor.

Globally recognized subject matter expert in the fields of: Project Management, computer validation (including ERP, DMS, and LIMS), IT Infrastructure qualification, 21 CFR Part 11 compliance, Quality Management Systems, internal auditing, and supplier management.
