Brandi Farra’s Summary

PERSONAL:

Brandi Farra, Senior Marketing Associate and Graphic Designer, has over ten years of success providing quality marketing, graphic design, advertising, publishing, promotions and public relations services. As the lead marketer in Cardno WRG’s Nevada, Phoenix and Idaho offices, she strives to create and design marketing materials that maintain the ethics and integrity that Cardno WRG is known for. She also provides graphic and marketing assistance to Cardno WRG’s Portland, Sacramento and Corporate offices.

As the winner of SMPS’s 2009 Member of the Year, Brandi has received a reputation as a positive team member with a “get it done” attitude. This positive attitude paired with creative solutions helps motivate her team of dedicated professionals so that their finished products and services stand out above the rest. Brandi prides herself on being extremely organized and able to easily manage project teams under short deadlines.

CARDNO WRG:

At Cardno WRG, we think beyond the needs our clients have today. We invest in their future by providing them a talented group of experts in land development and design. The land development process is the way we plan for tomorrow, so from concept to completion, our team is focused on developing land with an emphasis on livability while always incorporating and preserving the natural and irreplaceable surrounding environment. We offer solutions allowing our clients to gain more than they ever imagined from their development.

Cardno WRG has full-service offices in Portland, Las Vegas, Phoenix, Sacramento and Boise and has a team of professionals with solid technical and managerial abilities that pride themselves on their quality of work, responsiveness and their ability to focus on the intricacies of process and follow-through.
