Jason Kiefer’s Summary

Jason Kiefer is the Founder of Pictage, Inc., the industry leading ecommerce and fulfillment solution for professional wedding and portrait photographers. Over the course of ten years at the helm of Pictage Jason grew the company from an idea to profitable company with over $40 million in revenue.

Combining a world class custom built Web 2.0 platform, outsourced vendors in multiple countries and the nation’s first 100% digital professional color lab, Jason’s experience at Pictage has given him a in-depth understanding of what it takes to run a successful start-up in the internet age.

In March 2006, Jason completed the sale of a majority stake in Pictage to global private equity firm Apax partners for $29 million resulting in an average cash return for Pictage investors of 3X. Jason continued on as CEO after the Apax transaction more than doubling revenue over the next three years. In March 2009, Jason completed the final stage in the life of a successful start-up and allowed Pictage to stand on its own by hiring Jim Collins as Pictage’s new CEO. Jason remains involved as the company’s Chairman and passionate fan.

Shortly after hiring Jim, Jason, his wife Jennifer and their three sons (4, 7 and 9) fulfilled a dream by setting out on what turned out to be a nine month adventure that spanned 27 countries on five continents. Living in hotels, b&bs, apartments, cottages, and even boats while homeschooling along the way the “wandering five” gained an incredible appreciation for what the world has to offer.

Jason began his career in the Mergers & Acquisitions department at Chase Securities (now J.P. Morgan) in New York where, among other things, he developed an expertise in diving deep into a company’s financials and creating detailed projection and models that forecast the company’s future.

Jason and Jennifer have recently relocated to their childhood home of Boulder, Colorado.
