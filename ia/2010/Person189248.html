Roshan Stanton’s Summary

I started promoting teen parties around 14 years old and was deejaying my first nightclub before I could even drink at 16. I ran through the Philadelphia nightlife scene as a DJ, promoter, music producer, and nightclub manager. I eventually out grew the area and trekked out WEST and ended up striking gold in Nevada and California doing some big things. I was GM of Nightlife Operations at Caesars Casino - Lake Tahoe and later went on to work with Dennis Hof over at The Bunny Ranch/HBO Cathouse running his nightclub NV50. After 6 years out west I came back to Philly and took over Nightlife Promotions for RJ Reynolds and ran their Camel Nightlife Program at Media Star Promotions. Finally in 2010 after a eye-opening vacation to Costa Rica I decided I wanted to switch gears and do something different. So I resigned from my comfy job in one of the worst economies ever and decided to travel the world. I know crazy right :)

Since then I have become a photographer, blogger, and went back to my DJ roots spinning at various clubs around the globe. After settling in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil and becoming a Brazil geek I started one of the fastest growing online sites for travel to Brazil called BrazilGeeks.com. It's been a fun, exciting adventure and life is great. I'm living my dream.
