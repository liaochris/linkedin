Chad Trutt’s Summary

I am a graphic/web designer with more than eight years of experience in the industry. During my time in the business, I’ve worked in a variety of settings, ranging from small independent companies to national firms including Our Town, Inc., Market Street Mortgage and internationally MET Studio Design Ltd. in London England.

I have experience designing environmental graphics, way-finding, directional signage, icons, branding, brochures, invitations, fliers, garment graphics, vector packs, editing video, developing presentations from credentials to final design, and designing and maintaining web sites.

I am seeking a position that will challenge me as well as allow me growth.
