Athena Navarro Hinkhouse’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur. Primarily, a management consultant with a diversified approach in doing business, including consulting, relationship building, and coaching utilizing core values, intuition, and Androgynous Semantic Realignment (A.S.R.).

My career started at a boutique contingency firm placing people right out of public accounting into private industry. After a few years, I went to a large national firm to build an accounting and finance practice there doing a combination of retained and contingency search. In 1999, I started The Elitist Group. The name was chosen because it had become my teams nickname having developed a niche early on in placing people from the top consulting firms, who had graduated from top schools. The majority of my candidates, and now clients, have worked for McKinsey, Bain, Deloitte, Anderson, Price Waterhouse Coopers and/or attended Stanford, Cornell, Harvard, Duke, Tufts, etc...

As my client and candidate relationships have evolved through the years, I've become more of a generalist, working accross industries, and levels of talent. I have over 12 years of experience in management consulting, executive retained search, full cycle recruiting, contract recruiting, mass hire, personal and corporate coaching.

I really enjoy working people and organizations, helping them to grow and develop. I find relationships fascinating, be it personal or professonal. I’m an avid reader of personal development and motivational business literature. I enjoy attending development seminars. I completed Byron Katie’s 10-day “School” in Cologne Germany in 2006, and am recently finished The Want Institute ASR Coaching Certification.

I'm happy to discuss your goals or how we can work together.
