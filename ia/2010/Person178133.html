Roy Hsu’s Summary

Award winning Art Director/ACD with experience in a wide variety of Fortune 500 companies ranging from fashion, financial, consumer packaged goods to pharmaceuticals. Primarily focused in Interactive/Digital with experience in print.

Also a photographer and served as 2007 President of the Stock Artists Alliance (SAA). As president , I lead the international trade organization in dialogue and negotiations with major international distributors, helped create and edit PR, represented and spoke at various public events and panels and and helped maintain SAA's website. In addition, I have served on the steering committee of ASMPNY, and developing programs and panel discussions.
