Phil Caruso’s Summary

I'm a recent graduate from Marquette University. I am currently starting a public relations consulting group, Caruso Communications LLC, a boutique which tailors each project to our clients needs.

As a student I had the privilege to serve as Editor-in-Chief of the Marquette Tribune. Before then I was the Managing Editor, Assistant News Editor and Crime Reporter.

I have a Bachelor's of Arts degree in journalism with a minor in criminology and law studies.

I was exposed to the power of communication at an early age by my family. By age 12 I was handling press conferences and media advisories for Holy Family Church in Chicago. Around this age I was also inducted into world of special events with assisting in planning and executing community runs, walks, balls and professional bike races.

It was only fitting for me that I spend my summers in college with Special Events Management. I can safely say I have extensive experience with planning, executing and profiting with special events.

In my spare time I moonlight as freelance photographer.
