My experience in the advertising, marketing and publishing industry is extensive and, forms much of my professional & creative value.

I have worked in advertising agencies, design agencies and publishing houses and have gained a thorough understanding of how each operates. My overriding expertise is a creative one. I have a proven ability to produce conceptual thinking at a consistent level and quality and rely on this to deliver appropriate solutions to my employers, and their clients. The variety of roles I have occupied has broadened my understanding of market sectors and enables me to quickly determine the needs of each, without time-wasting personal research.

I also have many craft skills which support my thinking ability and can craft creative work from concept to production, in any media. My typographic skills allow me to develop a visual style for a given project with surety and confidence. My innate design skills are reliable and are at a very high level (please see my work examples).

I am an advanced user of all the major Mac OSX graphics and office software packages and use them on a daily basis.

My inter-personal and client-facing skills are consistent with my open and positive personality, and I am, discreet, and generous.

My goals are to continue to enjoy offering my skills to my industry and will strive to improve, progress, and gain more useful knowledge.
