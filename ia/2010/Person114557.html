  • Founding Partner

    Sustain Brands

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2006 — Present (4 years 1 month)

    Sustain is a brand that is adapted to regional brands across the country. Because it is only applied to regional and local products of a certain area, Sustain signifies regional... wherever you are.

    Regional brands that adopt Sustain Brand are automatically given the name and consumer recognition they need to survive in a mass market driven world.

    Our mission is to elevate the role that regional, local and place-based brands play in their respective categories by identifying and leveraging the unique benefits these brands offer to consumers.

    We support and sustain regional brands because strong regional brands lead to strong regional economies, as well as more fresh and healthy food choices for the consumer.

    The Sustain Brand is a creation of the Regional Brand Builders at Kennedy Creative, a Triple Bottom Line Company.
