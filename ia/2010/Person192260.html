Tim Spencer’s Summary

Experienced Computer Games Designer with formal Prince2 Project Management training as well as team management and production experience.

Experience working on a wide variety of genres: 1st Person, 3rd Person, RPG, Strategy, Platform, Action/Adventure and Racing games for both single and multiplayer design (core mechanic and level design).
