Jennifer Reynolds’s Summary

I started my company, Sauce Goddess, because I just loved cooking for others and sharing good food. It has always been a dream of mine to take that to the next level and make unique and tasty food available to more than just my immediate circle of friends and family. Sauce Goddess Gourmet sauces and spices let me do just that.

Sauce Goddess is growing like crazy right now. We are expanding into Northern California, the Pacific Northwest and the Rocky Mountain area.

Down the road I am writing a cookbook, teaching some cooking classes and doing a lot of showing off via what I call the "Sauce Goddess World Tour".
