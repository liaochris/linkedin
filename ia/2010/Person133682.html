Melissa Cartagena-Ulrich’s Summary

Melissa is an industry thought leader focused on creating customer value by increasing conversions and decreasing acquisition costs. A senior professional with expertise in high level consulting for all types of Search Engine Marketing Campaigns.

Currently Melissa is focused on consulting with businesses regarding their marketing needs as they relate to the Internet. Melissa was the Director of eCommerce with Marine Accessories Corporation, as well as the Director of Paid Search with PCSecurityShield, prior to these roles Melissa was the Manager of Professional Services in the Online Marketing Division at Hostway Corporation, one of the largest Web Hosting Companies with over $150 million in annual revenues. In that role, Melissa developed, built, and led a team of 11 Online Marketing Specialists who managed and consulted on Paid Search and Natural Search strategies for small to mid-sized businesses. In addition to managing the team she also played a key role in developing strategies for the online marketing business unit overall working closely with Product Management, Executive Management, and Channel Partner clients with Hostway.

Previous to Hostway, Melissa worked with a couple of Search Marketing firms one worth mentioning was Morevisibility, one of the most experienced Search Engine Marketing and Optimization firms, in Boca Raton, FL, where as the Senior Client Relations Manager she played a strategic role in consulting with many of the firms top clients on Paid and Natural Search Strategies.

Melissa received her MBA/eBusiness in June 2007 from the University of Phoenix in the hopes to continue her efforts of educating small to mid-sized businesses regarding how to become successful online today. Her entrepreneurial spirit and need for continued knowledge in the search space has pushed her to become a true leader and mentor to many.
