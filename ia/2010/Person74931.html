Mike Read’s Summary

Currently Director of MarComm at Ixia

Previously:
Senior Manager of Marcomm at Spirent Communications
Active functional title: Sr. Market Manager for Wireline and IMS Architectures

Mr. Read joined Spirent Communications of Ottawa, Ltd. in March 2000 in the position of Product Manager. One year later, he was promoted to Sr. Manager of Marketing Communications, responsible for the Broadband Business Unit in Canada. In this role, Mr. Read exercised independent discretion and authority to manage this essential function at Spirent.
Mr. Read held this position unitl January 1, 2005 when he was promoted to the position of Sr. Manager of Market Development for the Global Broadband Test Group. In this capacity, Mr. Read was the senior level person responsible for directing marketing activity related to Spirent’s integrated performance analysis solutions for next-generation network technologies for the Broadband business Unit.

Within the Global Shared Services Corporate Communications Group
In July 2006, Mr Read was promoted to Sr. Market Manager for Wireline and IMS Architectures within the Corporate Communications Group for all Spirent’s business units globally.
This area includes: Triple Play Testing, IP Telephony, VoIP and Voice Testing,
Video and IPTV Testing, Content Aware Testing, L 4-7 Security Testing, Application Testing, IMS testing, L 2-3 Security Testing, Switch Testing Router Testing and Broadband Testing.

Mr. Read continues to direct and develop messaging strategies and continues to exercise independent authority and discretion over all marketing activities, including initiating market research studies and analyzing their results, assisting in product development, and directing and evaluating our marketing communication strategies, including the design of product launches and implementation of “high impact” marketing campaigns, with the overall goal of maximizing the companies revenues and customer mindshare.
