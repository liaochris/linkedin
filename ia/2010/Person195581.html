Steven Stark’s Summary

Software project/product development or Quality Assurance position for an organization seeking an increased improvement in product development and deployment through applications of Project Management skills and Quality Assurance methods.

After receiving over 300 corrective action reports during a previous customer review prepared and executed a process validation plan that reduced the number of customer reported corrective action requests by 78%

Successfully orchestrated the QA efforts for two key product releases which secure continued business. The first released resulted in a $2M project. The second release containing several key features secured a $1M project.

Planned and successful implemented a new testing strategy. The new strategy focused on identifying the individual features of the product and focusing the tests to verify each feature. This allowed us to simplify and speed our individual tests while ensuring all of the product features are verified. The simplified tests and feature focus created greater efficiency in the product release cycle, decreasing the amount of time for existing test by 20% and increasing the amount of coverage by 20%.

Implemented automated testing tool with the QA team taking advantage of one the company’s other products. Effectively doing the same or more QA with less tester assigned to the team and making the testing more predictable and repeatable.

Awarded the designation of Certified Tester - Foundation Level (CTFL) by the American Software Testing Qualification Board, Inc.

Managed and coordinated project requirement development, implementation, testing and customer acceptance which resulted in customer acceptance one month a head of schedule and customer ROI in three months after start of operation, nine months ahead of plan.

Designed and Developed a modular object-based software product that reduced the field setup and integration time by 40% and was used in over 100 installations.
