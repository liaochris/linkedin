Arie Jongejan’s Summary

Recent MBA / MS dual degree graduate from the University of Michigan. Proven deal generator and closer involving transactions with multiple stakeholders. Published in smart grid, electricity markets, demand response and dynamic pricing, and electric vehicle to grid interface. Creative problem solver who is empathetic to customer issues, is relationship oriented and is intrinsically motivated to close deals. Genuinely committed to building clean tech growth companies that address significant environmental issues.
