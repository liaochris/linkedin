Paul Cecala’s Summary

A Certified Career Coach with a decade of experience in helping people get started in new careers, transition to new fields, and re-inventing themselves to advance their current career.

A public speaker/trainer on topics related to job search techniques, resume writing, interview skills and related areas. Recent speeches given to ChemPharma Professional Association (NJ Chapter), NJ Dept of LWD, Out Professionals of NYC, several area Libraries, County College of Morris and GAAMC.

Seeking to help others in finding their passion in life and making money doing it.
