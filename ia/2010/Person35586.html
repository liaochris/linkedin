Tim O'Gorman’s Summary

Tim has held the senior executive and marketing/sales positions in both the computer and investment banking industries. After fifteen years of successful sales and sales management in the computer industry, Tim refocused his efforts in the financial service arena, primarily in equipment leasing.

In 1976 Tim started The Form Financial Corporation. Where he served as president ultimately merging his company with the largest regional investment-banking firm in Chicago, Illinois. As president of ChiCorp Financial Services, a Sub of The Chicago Corporation, he was a member of the board of directors and served on the credit and executive committees. After sixteen successful years, The Chicago Corporation was sold to ABNAMRO Inc., the seventh largest bank in the world. While there Tim served as the Executive Vice President for three years.

In 2000, Tim retired and relocated to Bonita Springs Florida to start his new venture as the owner and president of Champion Cleaners.

Tim O'Gorman’s Specialties:

Sales and sales management; equipment leasing; Series 7, 24 and 63 securities licenses.
