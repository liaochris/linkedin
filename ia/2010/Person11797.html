    FormaLogix was founded in 2003 and has since built a revolutionary system that enables a person to virtually try on branded footwear to find the proper size and fit using its patented VFit™ technology.

    FormaLogix VFit™ system starts with retail based kiosks that are built around patented technology using digital imagers and software. The Technology takes a 3-Dimensional image of a person’s feet and then compares that to a 3-D image of the inside of a shoe. Images are saved in the FormaLogix data center for future fittings and direct purchases online.

    VFit - the “breakout” technology that will fundamentally change the way people buy shoes in stores and on the Internet.
