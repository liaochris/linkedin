    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2004 — Present (6 years 7 months)

    Integer Leadership Consulting (ILC) focuses on developing leaders (trustees, corporate boards, senior executive staffs) and teams within organizations. The objective of ILC is to help established and emerging leaders become more effective by understanding the practices of healthy and transformative leadership. ILC emphasizes not only the critical competencies of leadership but also advances the practice of authenticity. As leaders match leadership knowledge with leadership practice integrated with authenticity and wholeness, their impact and effectiveness is significantly increased. ILC approaches leadership development from an organizational platform. ILC helps businesses, both for-profit and not-for-profit, design and build processes that identify leaders and cultivate core leadership competencies, including resilience and hardiness, that align with the vision, mission, and core values of the organization.
