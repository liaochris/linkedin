Michael Duley’s Summary

Michael H. Duley, Senior Director, St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, Digital Internet Group Strategy, leads an International online business delivering 10 Million pages of content per month across the portfolio with revenues exceeding $35 million annually. With a staff of 40 plus, the Digital Internet Group (DIG) is comprised of online product development, e-product management, and e-product marketing responsible for the "go-to-market" online, mobile, and social, acquisition and cultivation strategy, and donor experience for stjude.org and the portfolio of web properties.

Our team shares a vision that stjude.org is the front door to the world and we believe that someday all fundraising will be done this way. Our job will be complete when you “feel” the same way from visiting stjude.org as you do from touring the hospital.

Prior to St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, Michael made significant contributions in B2B and B2C models at AT&T, Lucent Technologies, and Nortel Networks. The latter in a lead e-business marketing role for the enterprise and small and medium business units working with Bellsouth, Sprint, Bell Canada and thousands of smaller businesses to get their products online.

Michael has achieved academically an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering (EE) and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Vanderbilt University, Owen Graduate School of Management. Michael 's passion is building high performance teams that use the Internet in new ways to change the world.
