Richard Hartley’s Summary

I consider myself to be a highly-motivated, hard-working and capable manager, with the unique position of experience across Digital, Buying and Marketing. I can offer commitment, enthusiasm and creativity - along with a strong commercial awareness and a passion for brands, products, retail and customers.

As part of Mirfield Agricultural Show I have overhauled the publicity, advertising, promotions, programme and website which have helped the show to grow every year to be a key event in the local events calendar.

At OYYY.co.uk I have led the Marketing team that has introduced and delivered a proactive promotional calendar, targeted direct marketing and dynamic e-mails, quarterly catalogues, online optimisation, google adwords and affiliate activity and maximised funding from our suppliers – resulting in growth of over 250%.

At Staples I led the team, and re-structured and re-marketed the poorest performing, over-stocked, loss-making category into a customer-focused, simple-to-shop from, high margined growth area – with profit growth over twice the company average. Promoted via catalogues, leaflets and online activity.

At Asda I worked in conjunction with Customers, Buyers, Suppliers, Technical and Marketing to design, develop and deliver two new own brand electrical ranges worth over £60m per year.

At Grattan I was responsible for the research and catalogue communication of the new Children’s range which significantly increased sales, and opened new opportunities in to the under achieving teenage market.

I managed the team who developed and delivered the Mamas & Papas Promotional Calendar which successfully; increased sales by £16m per year; and launched new ranges at the bi-annual trade show.

Professionally I enjoy the feeling from seeing something you have driven be a success. At home, I enjoy being involved with community projects and spending time with my family.
