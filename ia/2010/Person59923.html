Dr JOY Madden’s Summary

Initially a Bio-Medical Engineer, then Consultant Clinician and Research Professor, I have over three decades of experience in Health, Academia and Commerce, instigating and co-ordinating innovative multi-disciplinary projects across the diverse fields of Medicine, Engineering and Music.....but with a touch of passion to learn what really makes people and the universe tick !

In bringing together people from all walks of life to work on National and International projects, I have spent time with thousands of people, getting down to the real issues that concern patients, their families, students, professionals, etc. This led me to my interest in 'Happiness'.

'TRUE Happiness' is defined and experienced in many different ways across the globe. On a Happiness-scale of 1 to 10, most people rate themselves around 3-6. Few people live at 10/10 and even fewer know how to stay at that level whatever life hits them with.

It is said that Happiness correlates with reaching your full potential but does the latter actually exist? Do you know when you are there? Does the journey there give birth to even greater 'potential' you never knew you had? Does it ever stop? Can everyone become cleverer and happier with time?

The study of Happiness crosses the boundaries of work, culture, geography, religion, etc. It is a multi-disciplinary subject that has a direct impact on Emotional Health and Wealth. This is what makes it so fascinating.

If you are seeking 'TRUE happiness' and a way to achieve the SUCCESS you want:

* Professional (whatever your field)
* Business (however big your company is)
* Personal (social, attaining a childhood dream, etc)

DO email/phone me direct via my website at the top of this page. I will always reply to you personally and in total confidence.

As Self Development Editor I also write articles on a wide variety of topics in the field. For my 'FREE Weekly Newsletter' and to view (or join!) my lively Forum, do visit my website above.

Dr JOY Madden’s Specialties:

The running thread through my work has been my interest in the effect of 'Sound' on people from Medical, Scientific and Commercial perspectives. ('Sound' here includes music, noise, pure tones, complex chords, conversation/speech, vibrations....and silence!)

I also give professional advice to homeowners and the commercial sector on ways to reduce Maintenance costs and Health risks of their Conservatories, Living and Work spaces with bespoke Window and Roof blinds/coverings.
