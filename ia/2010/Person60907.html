Don Crane’s Summary

BIO
Don Crane
Advertising/Branding/Marketing/Sales Promotion Programs:
Manager, Strategist, Creative Director, Copywriter & Broadcast/Video Producer

Don's primary area of concentration has been the development of advertising, branding, direct marketing, sales promotion, communications and social media programs for companies such as: American Express, AT&T, CBS Television, Nuance Communications, The Providence Journal-Bulletin, State Street Bank, Stride Rite Children’s Shoes, The North Face, Westinghouse/Group W and Viacom.

His work has been recognized by the Ad Clubs of New York and Boston, the American Advertising Federation, the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences, the Art Director’s Club of New York, Graphic Design USA, The New York Times, and other industry groups for its "creative excellence" and "sales effectiveness."

Don also conducts "Profiting From Your Creativity" seminars for business and professional audiences and has facilitated brainstorming sessions businesses and nonprofit organizations including 3Com, ABC Television, Boston College, Genesis ElderCare, Harvard Business School Publishing, Sun Life of Canada and Tufts Health Plan.

He has been a co-host of the Podtini.com Podcast available on the World Wide Web.

Don was featured in a “Chronicle” (WCVB-TV/Boston) broadcast that examined the roles of creativity and innovation in the arts, business and personal growth. He is a past member of the Creative Education Foundation; an organization dedicated to fostering innovative approaches in the growth and development of business, education and industry.

Don has been a member of the American Advertising Federation, the Advertising Club of Greater Boston and he has served on the Board of Directors of the Massachusetts Chapter of the American Society for Training & Development. He has also served as the Alumni Representative for the Emerson College Varsity Athletics Council and as a South Shore YMCA Camp Committee member.

Don Crane’s Specialties:

CATEGORY EXPERIENCE: Development of advertising, branding, communications, marketing and lead generation programs for B2B and B2C audiences of companies from banking, broadcasting, financial services, healthcare, hospitality, insurance, publishing, real estate and technology.

RESPONSIBILITIES & SKILLS: Project Management. Plans & Budgets. Strategies & Tactics. Creative Concepts. Creative Direction. Art Direction, Design & Copy. Broadcast/Video Production. Print Production Supervision.
