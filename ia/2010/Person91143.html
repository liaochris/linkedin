Jasper Klapwijk’s Summary

Trained as a Political Scientist, I took the opportunity to further develop my skills in a management trainee programme with Woonzorg Nederland. As a marketeer for Woonzorg Nederland, I was involved in the implementation of products and services in Real Estate for the senior segment.
This has led me to write and implement a business plan for personal services in the senior segment of the real estate market.
After the successful implementation of six personal services for seniors I was asked to lead the Communications department as a Communications Manager.
I am very happy to work with seven outstanding people on the improvement of marketing, PR, and internal (customer-driven) communication of Woonzorg.
I am the spokesperson for Woonzorg Nederland and advisor to the boards of Woonzorg Nederland and Espria, a merger of Philadelphia and Evean Group, affiliated with Woonzorg Nederland.
In my spare time, I am reading an MBA @ TiasNimbas. A challenging task for a father of two quite lovely daughters.
