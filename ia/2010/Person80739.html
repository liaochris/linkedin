Heather Dougherty’s Summary

Heather Dougherty is a spirited interactive professional who has been delivering high-quality client solutions in the digital space since 1999. She's worked in both start-up, corporate and agency environments servicing both B2B and B2C clients. A business-minded, strategic thinker, Heather skillfully merges client and audience goals and marketing best practices to develop effective and dynamic digital marketing solutions for her clients. She is a proven leader, with a demonstrated ability for managing people and processes, as well as establishing solid cross-departmental and client relationships.

Heather Dougherty’s Specialties:

360-degree online strategy – website, email marketing, online advertising (display ads/paid search), social media, etc – for a powerful and profitable online presence. Advocating for user-centric, standards compliant, accessible design & development methodologies. Helping integrate offline and online media strategies.
