Profile
I am a marketing and project management professional with over 28 years experience with IBM. My most recent experience is as Marketing Communications Professional and Project Manager, where I led project teams to strategize, plan, develop, execute and measure marketing communications projects to generate leads for IBM products. I am an innovative, self-directed and results oriented individual. As a leader, I am skilled in motivating teams to perform at their best.
