Wayne Maceyka’s Summary

I am a marketing professional with 15 years of customer-facing experience in marketing support, direct sales, and sales management. I have an emerging interest in social media and its role in the b2b space. I graduated from the Bainbridge Graduate Institute's sustainable MBA program in June of 2009, seeking to integrate environmental & socially responsible practices into business activities. I have a broad range of experience in commercial, industrial, renewable energy, and consumer markets in small start-ups and multi-national corporations. I enjoy working across functions, departments, and stakeholder groups in an evolving and changing environment to develop cohesive business programs.

Some things I think about:

How might we reach and engage customers & stakeholders most effectively in the industrial markets we serve?

How might we create strong human/nature-scaled economies that nourish the development and preservation of natural, financial, and human capital?

How might we create working environments that foster self-determination and individual alignment with Purpose to achieve personal and organizational development?

What does a completely regenerative ecological economy look like?
