Sanjay Mathur CISA, CISSP’s Summary

I am an accomplished internal auditor with a strong grasp of technology and information security controls and having working experience with big four, Fortune 1000 and global companies. I have a track-record of adapting to new technologies and a wide breadth of expertise from developing custom database applications to auditing IT infrastructure, processes and large system development projects. I am known for my ability to build effective cross-disciplinary teams across international boundaries.

Since 2006, I have been working with Visa as Director/ Business Leader Internal Audit. I managed IT and process audits. initiated annual risk analysis that identified high risk areas, audit priority and opportunities to improve the internal key-controls compliance. Planned, designed and executed systems development and IT audits using an in-house audit framework based on COSO, COBIT and Project management Book of Knowledge (PMBOK). Developed audit issues, findings and action plans using electronic work-paper module (EWP) of Teammate Audit management System. Managed relationship with the auditees and co-sourced audit staff. Participated in post-audit reviews and follow-up of the unresolved audit issues with auditees.

Prior to it, I worked as a contractor for Inovant, a division of Visa for around three years, where I led the development of a business risk analysis framework for Inovant that is based on OCTAVE and ISO 17799 controls. I have extensive risk assessment and audit experiences from my previous employment with KPMG, and system development and security architecture expertise through working with several multinationals and local start-ups.

During my long and accomplished consulting experience, I have had served global clients, such as, Inovant, AT&T Wireless, Sun Microsystems, Karna Technologies, Sybase, Bank of Boston, Amdahl, HP, University of Berkeley Extension, and PMI Insurance, BHP Steel, to name a few.
