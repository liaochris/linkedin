Cory Hill’s Summary

PhD in Applied Physics from Caltech, 2001. Currently a researcher in design, crystal growth and characterization of III-V semiconductor optoelectronic devices (infrared detector arrays, cascade lasers, solar cells) at JPL.

Pursuing an MBA at the UCLA Anderson School of Management (expected early 2010). Emphasis on finance.

Cory Hill’s Specialties:

Developed high-temperature mid infrared lasers for NASA space missions as well as high resolution infrared imaging arrays based on III-V quantum wells, quantum dots, and superlattices.
Also providing custom optoelectronic device design, fabrication and characterization through Infravision Systems, LLC.
