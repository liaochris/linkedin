Javier Tordable’s Summary

I am Senior Software Engineer working at Google, in the Kirkland office. Currently I am the Tech Lead of the Webmaster Tools backend team. Before that I worked at Microsoft, doing search relevance algorithms in Live Search (currently Bing). Previously I worked in my own startup, in Spain. I also stayed for a short time at McKinsey, as a Business Analyst intern.

At the same time I am working towards my Ph.D. in Mathematics by the University of Valladolid. I collaborated with the DAVAP research group on topics related to Photogrammetry and Bundle Adjustment. After completing my final examination in September 2009 I started working in a doctoral thesis under the supervision of Antonio Campillo.

On the side of social service, I volunteered at Executive Services Corps, in the Washington branch. ESC is a nonprofit that does consulting work for other nonprofits. The projects range from strategy and finance to marketing and human resources.

I am interested in a wide variety of topics within Computer Science, Mathematics, Technology, Finance and Artificial Intelligence. I also enjoy classical literature, movies, flamenco music and videogames.
