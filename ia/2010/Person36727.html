Cher Wada Koenig’s Summary

To view my Professional Bio and to learn more about my Professional Background please visit my website at: www.wadaconsulting.webs.com

I have been working in the ‘people’ industry since I started into the work force at an early age. I finally started recruiting, among other things, for a financial advisor, some years later and many years ago. It was said that I was solely responsible for growing his business exponentially.

That is when I got the recruiting bug, and decided to start recruiting full-time for a national recruiting firm; well known to many in the industry. This was a great position for me as I was able to help many people throughout many areas, but couldn’t ever really accept how the candidate’s were viewed as a pay check and not as humans trying to sustain their families as a whole. So I started learning everything I could and making as many contacts as possible in hopes of changing, not only the way candidates were treated, but how recruiters were viewed by candidates as well.

I started Infinity Career Group, LLC in 2007 with the same concepts that I had gained by my previous experiences with candidates. I ran this as an on-line service with a small local team, and it exploded in popularity. Candidates from all over the US responded to the new way of thinking better than anyone could have expected, and the growth of work quickly engulfed the days.

It was then that I had to rethink how everything was running, and exactly what our job functions were, in order to restructure our business into what it has become today.

Since my main facet with the candidates had already been consulting on a personal and professional level, the consulting came easy. It was through many confidential and personal phone calls from clients that the rest just fell into place; and thus Wada Consulting was born.
