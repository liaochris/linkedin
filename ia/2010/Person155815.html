Nathan Williams’s Summary

Nathan is currently an Inside Solution Sales Professional for Microsoft helping clients lower costs and secure datacenters through Microsoft's security and management solutions. He is a team player and innovator and has developed and propagated tools across the team to accelerate the sales motion.

Nathan was a 2008 MACH hire from the MIT Sloan School of Management (MBA) and came into the inside sales org in Issaquah, WA. He has a deep technical background with a Master’s in Computer Science and 5 years of experience as a software engineer.

Nathan earned his MBA from the MIT Sloan School of Management with a focus on entrepreneurship and sales. While at Sloan, he was President of the MIT Sloan Sales Club where he founded the International MBA Sales Competition and led the team of volunteers that provided practical sales training and education to students and the local sales community.

Fluent in English, conversant in Portuguese (Brazilian)
