Subodh Kumar, IFMA Fellow, CFM’s Summary

Subodh Kumar, IFMA Fellow, CFM, President of the Chartered Facility Management Group Inc, is a globally recognized strategy and management professional, who has served as a Vice-Chairman of IFMA, been featured as a “Visionary”, and “Industry Leader - Making a Difference” and published and presented, internationally. He developed the long-range Technology Master plan for the largest Community College District and is now developing an Implementation Plan.

He excels in strategy, process and quality enhancements for all aspects of "non core" aspects of an organization's business with a life-cycle perspective, for clients like RAND, AMGEN, Los Angeles Community College District, Symantec, SCAN, Nissan, Honda, Citi, TCW, ActiVision, Omnitrans, and Sacramento County.

His graduate education includes Facility Planning/ Management at Cornell University, Construction Engineering/ Management at the University of Michigan, and Business at UCLA; in addition to an undergraduate in architecture. An adjunct faculty of the UCI and UCLA facilities programs, he initiated the first FM degree program in California, at Woodbury University.
