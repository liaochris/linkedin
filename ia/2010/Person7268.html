Alice Rene’s Summary

Published author whose education, a Masters in Social Welfare, gave insight into growth and development and inter-personal relationships. Two decades in the travel business, packaging and escorting groups to exotic destinations world wide added a knowledge of intercultural issues that come to play in understanding personalities. Serious writing in the last decade has produced several short stories and a published book, "Becoming Alice, A Memoir."

Alice Rene’s Specialties:

Literary non-fiction and short stories.
