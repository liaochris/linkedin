Résumé de Aziz Boxwala

Several years of experience in medical informatics in product development and R&D. Specific expertise in clinical solutions design, decision support, business rules engines, content for EMR systems and CPOE systems, medical vocabularies, and knowledge representation.

Compétences de Aziz Boxwala

Healthcare information technology, patient safety, clinical vocabularies, clinical content.
