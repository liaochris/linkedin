    Eurocontrol

    (Government Agency; Airlines/Aviation industry)

  • Real Time Simulation Project Manager

    Eurocontrol

    (Government Agency; Airlines/Aviation industry)

    August 2007 — August 2008 (1 year 1 month)

    Large scale Real Time Simulation Management.

    EUROCONTROL has, at the EEC premises, developed over years a unique real-simulation facility and has gained an important experience and a recognized neutrality in the conduct of such large scale experimentations.
    In this context EUROCONTROL has been requested to set up a large scale real-time simulation experiment to validate several operational airspace reconfiguration scenarios.
    The airspace covers an area controlled by LvNL, DFS and MUAC, including both civil and military operations.
    The project is called AMRUFRA (Amsterdam, Ruhr, Frankfurt).
    The simulation has involved 25 controllers and 20 pilots working for three weeks starting on 07/04/08, to validate the proposed solutions.

    Main Tasks:
    - Team management : (6 experts)
    - Planning and Risk management.
    - Contracts and budgets.
    - Workshop organisation.
    - A lot of missions, for coordination with the working group (Amsterdam, Brussels, Frankfurt, Maastricht, Budapest)

  • Controller Working Position HMI Work package Leader

    Eurocontrol

    (Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Airlines/Aviation industry)

    January 1999 — June 2004 (5 years 6 months)

    Due to the Human Factor Group needs, it was necessary to implement a new Controller Working Position, with a high level of flexibility, documentation and traceability.

    To do so, few tasks were conducted:

    - Definition of a new Generic Software Architecture.
    - Complete UML Modelisation of this CWP (technical and operational).
    - MS Word existing specification migration in the UML model.
    - Full traceability management, from the requirements to the software modules.
    - Generic testing implementation, based on the full UML model.
    - Automatic documentation generation; technical, operational, testing plan, etc, using the MSWord or WinHelp formats.
    - Promotion of this new CWP production method:
    - In the DASC conference (Florid).
    - In the European ATC Exhibit (Maastricht).
    - In the INRET Conference (Paris).
    - In the Air Traffic International magazine.
