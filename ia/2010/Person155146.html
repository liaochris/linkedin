I have extensive experience implementing cutting edge GIS solutions in local government. I offer you excellent problem solving skills, technical acumen, and strong organizational ability.
As the GIS Coordinator for the City of Cupertino, my accomplishments began with the creation of a GIS Needs Assessment and Implementation Plan, followed by the creation of a strategic GIS Master Plan document. The GIS Master Plan is now integrated into the City's five-year Master Technology Plan, and was instrumental in my development and definition of the City's initial five-year GIS budget forecast and annual budget requests; GIS data and application development priorities; hardware and software requirements; GIS database and graphical design standards; accuracy standards; data usage agreements; data maintenance, policies and procedures; organization/training needs; and GIS cost recovery options. I succeeded in establishing the first cooperative data sharing agreement between the City of Cupertino and the County of Santa Clara to help foster inter-agency data sharing and to improve the quality and timeliness of GIS basemap and assessor data updates. In order to keep the GIS Master Plan moving forward, I routinely plan, supervise and evaluate the work of GIS interns and consultants hired by the City.

The accompanying resume and results can give you an overview of my potential to make a worthwhile contribution to your company.
