Stephen Kann’s Summary

Over 20 years experience as entrepreneur and capital markets professional. Founded, financed, advised dozens of small companies in diverse industries and varying stages of development, giving me unique perspective on issues affecting clients. Expertise in business development, sales training, management and compensation plans, as well as telesales operations. Authored part of the sales and sales management curriculum for Professional Sales Institute (now 2Logical).

$300 million+ in investment banking transactions, including private placments, IPOs, PIPEs and M&A. Large network in venture capital, private equity, hedge funds and family offices. Active in Federal IT (mergers, acquistions, biz dev, IP licensing).

Research analyst. Founder/Editor of Bull Market MicroCap Review, a subscription-based investment newsletter providing commentary and analysis on microcap stocks. One of the best-performing stock newsletters in the US in '03-04.

Developed several business models that leveraged digital media, including Document Planet, a provider of online pre-press and print management; Online Research Partners, a corporate advisory firm; New Media Partners, a reseller of online marketing technologies; Virtual Films, an online entertainment concept. Consulting clients in industries as diverse as recycling, antique art, and mortgage lending. Sales management assignment in 2006 for one of the largest sub-prime mortgage lenders in the U.S.

Created program for America Online called AOL Select, which developed an alternative marketing channel for AOL's service and e-commerce partners. Bought out by AOL.

Exec. Producer on feature film "Moving Target" which gained international distribution and has played on various cable networks. Continue to contribute to entertainment projects, and recently served as an advisor on the feature film, "Checkout."

Top producing salesman and sales manager at multiple companies.

NASD Series 62, 63 and 24 licenses.

Stephen Kann’s Specialties:

Rainmaker: strengths are in business development (connecting clients to customers/partners through large Rolodex), deal origination, out-of-the-box strategic thinking, high-level negotiations, capital raises, sales, sales compensation plans, and sales management.
