  • Speaker

    Extreme Entrepreneurship Education Corp.

    (Education Management industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    The Extreme Entrepreneurship Tour(EET) brings the country's top young entrepreneurs to college campuses to spread the entrepreneurial mindset. Started in fall 2006, the tour has visited over 50 schools nation-wide and has received the Innovation Award from the National Association of Development Organizations and the Program of the Year award from Northern Michigan University. You can bring it to your college/university or region by emailing liv@extremetour.org or calling 800.930.8021

    Morgan is a keynote speaker on the tour who talks about starting a company right out of college at the age of 21. She highlights "How your weakness and differences can be your strengths" encouraging entrepreneurs to build a strong team of people who can overcome their weaknesses and highlighting how differences can be what make you stand out from the crowd instead of hold you back.

  • Owner/Publisher

    1st Publications MAP BOSTON

    (Publishing industry)

    January 2006 — April 2009 (3 years 4 months)

    1st Publications is a travel oriented publishing company that creates indispensable products to help consumers spice up their daily routines and put their lives in motion. MAP BOSTON, a weekly agenda and adventure guide to Boston was the first product from the 1st Publications and is now in its third edition. You can find the MAP BOSTON 09 edition in bookstores all over Boston from small independents to large chains like Barnes and Noble.

    As Publisher Morgan overseas the creation and organization of all the content in addition to handling the distribution, marketing and promotion of the book.
