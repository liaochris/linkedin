Haren Samarasekera (Haren Sam)’s Summary

As senior regional marketing & sales leader with 14+ years of experience in APAC and Mid East, Haren has been leading the charge to grow the regional business for Enterprise Solutions and Business Services organizations (i.e., ERP Software and Services).

Adept at managing cross-regional teams of sales leaders and sellers, Haren has successfully developed heterogeneous channels (direct and indirect teams across regions) and launched businesses into emerging markets. Ability to effectively execute strategic initiatives, ability to integrate team members from multiple cultural backgrounds, a metrics driven management orientation and a coaching mindset helps Haren to build and drive teams to achieve high performance.
