Tiffanie Tillman’s Summary

In an impressive and eclectic career that spans more than 15 years, Tiffanie Tillman is known worldwide as a trail-blazing and respected figure in the entertainment and broadcasting industries. Tiffanie has a fiery passion for three things: entrepreneurship, journalism and people. Tiffanie took full advantage of the growing market by building an award-winning online virtual assistant staffing company (The Virtual Professionals), and a reality-based talk show, called Gripping Conversations.

Gripping Conversations, a talk show that showcases regular people discussing real life issues involving relationships, politics, culture, social injustices and breaking news. “I like to call it ‘purpose-driven’ TV,” says Tiffanie of her weekly show. Tiffanie created the show because she felt mainstream media was not discussing topics in ways everyday people wanted to hear them. So she decided to fill the void by airing real issues in an authentic way.”

“Inspiring others, laughter and moving people into their divine purpose in life is a method with which I am truly comfortable,” says Tiffanie. The messages of her show follow Tiffanie to her live speaking engagements. “The show leaks into the presentations,” admits Tiffanie. Both in the way she aims to connect to people from the stage and in the way she gets people to openly discuss their views on various topics.

Tiffanie Tillman has explored & succeeded in all of these facets of the business & entertainment industry. Its obvious Tiffanie has a drive for versatility.

    Gripping Conversations, the first completely viewer interactive Radio/ TV/ Internet talk show. Each week, your host, Tiffanie, will voice thoughts and opinions from real people around the world. This show is about YOU!

    The show is full of genuine comments and opinions – nothing pretentious or staged, with lots of humor and earnestness. “We appeal to people who want to have a voice in society’s role regarding world issues that matter the most to us; whether it’s to be a better spouse or how to get rid of a slimy politician.”

    Some of the topics: They’re Still Married, But Promise to Get a Divorce! (Would you date someone who is still married, but says they’re not happy and wants to get a divorce?); and, Teachers Protest Merit-Based Pay! The latter is a clear example of how Tiffanie and her ‘purpose-driven’ approach about life in general can drive home the points of what is really important in our lives.

    So, join us each week as Tiffanie grips the conversations that are on the foremost people's minds.
