Gary Breitbart’s Summary

CEO/COO/CMO with ability to improve marketing & operations to drive top line and EBITDA growth

25+ years P&L and marketing leadership under-pinning with Fortune 200 B2B and B2C firms:

- Head of strategy & marketing for Prudential's employee benefit division; set plan that grew revenue from $ 4 to $ 6 B. Recognized for putting Prudential back on map in employee benefits (group life & disability, retirement, relocation) space after sale of its dominant healthcare division.

- Re-vamped product lines & set structure for NatWest’s U.S. turnaround; led to deposit and loan growth of over 25%

- Group sales executive at AT&T with responsibility for growing revenue & share w/ top insurance and brokerage accts

Experience with start-ups and middle market turnarounds

- First COO for family owned off-price retailer, Daffy's. Turnaround resulted in moving same store comp from -3% to +3% in one yr; record EBITDA results

- CEO of workplace marketing start-up. Reduced burn rate by 33% & grew revenue and customer accounts by > 50%

- Led start-up & growth of Human Resource strategy and information technology practice at Aon to over $ 20 M in revenue in 18 months

E-commerce/internet expert

- President of Interactive division for Charming Shoppes, drove revenue growth of 35% while improving technology platform & business processes to boost profitability

- Led takeout of $ 200+ M in operations cost by creating first web portals for Prudential and development of self-service capabilities and on-line account management

- Established PCF Consulting – focused on improving go-to-market strategy and tactics for multi-channel retailers and business service providers

Deal experience and strong negotiator

- Led sale of Prudential Service Bureau to generate gain of over $ 30 M after improving EBITDA by 25% after years of decline

- Led private equity investment in RewardsPlus of America and served on its board of directors
