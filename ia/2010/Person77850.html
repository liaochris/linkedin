Greg Meadows’s Summary

To all: please click FOLLOW on my Twitter link above.

As a 100% Native American-owned Small Disadvantaged Business located in Washington, DC, Red Cloud Services Inc. provides highly talented systems engineers and operations personnel, skilled at the design, development, acquisition, implementation, and integration of the Federal Aviation Administration's Next Generation Air Transportation System programs/projects. To guide the evolution of the National Airspace Systems (NAS), the FAA requires systems engineering firms with a thorough understanding of user needs, the services provided by NextGen and the interrelationships among the NAS that provide these mission-critical services.

Red Cloud Services respond to the Human Factors research, acquisition and analysis needs of NextGen stakeholders for the full human factors life cycle, from literature review to final report development including:

• Human-in-the-loop simulation expertise
• Conduct research on current and future Air Traffic Control concepts, interfaces
and automation tools
• Validation of existing concepts critical to NextGen
• Analyze the impact of automation across air traffic domains
• Perform studies on human performance, situation awareness, workloads, etc.
• Recruitment, training and staffing competency analysis associated with NextGen

Our mission is focused on providing qualified Human Factors professionals in the field of cognitive psychology, modeling & simulation and related disciplines for NextGen development and acquisition.

Red Cloud Services Inc. represents a strategic partnership with the FAA in the NextGen development process. It's CEO/President, Greg Meadows has over 23 years experience in the private and government sector supporting the Federal Aviation Administration, Environmental Protection Agency and Small Business Administration with management and operation of customer programs, information systems, strategic planning and corporate business development.

Greg Meadows’s Specialties:

FAA, Aviation, NextGen, Human Factors, Air Traffic, Strategic Planning, Knowledge Management, Software Development, Program Management, Enterprise Architecture. Excellent customer skills and a proven ability to implement workable solutions that support critical business objectives.
