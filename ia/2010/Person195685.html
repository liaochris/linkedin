Steven Nagle’s Summary

I have over 10 years experience in the field of MEMS and Nanotechnology development, including business development, technology strategy, micro- and nano-fabrication, device design, system implementation, modeling, and testing.

Most recently I developed 35 products over 3 years and contributed $3 million to the bottom line. In particular, my SNL(tm) probe product has shown 10x better performance compared to a similar product while costing half as much. My intuition, strong technical background, and communication skills made success possible.

Steven Nagle’s Specialties:

Microsensors, MEMS, Nanofabrication, Development of systems utilizing microsensors, Management, Business Case Development, Project Management.
