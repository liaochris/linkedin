Matthew A. Krueger’s Summary

Matt has joined Greenman Alliance to direct the Water Technology and Services effort. Through his creativity, resourcefulness and dedication to understanding his client's visions, Matt strives to employ consultative solutions aimed at achieving more with less, while meeting his client's strategic staffing initiatives. He is a member of The Milwaukee Water Council, the Urban Ecology Center, and the Milwaukee River Keepers, among other local and national groups.

Matt has over a decade of experience delivering collaborative talent acquisition solutions. As a recruiter, business developer and staff-augmentation consultant, Matt has served business partners ranging from innovative start-ups to Fortune 100 industry leaders in science and technology.

Matt holds a degree in Journalism and a degree in American History, both from the University of Wisconsin at Madison. He currently resides in Milwaukee, WI, within only a few minute's walk to the beautiful Lake Michigan parks and waterfront.
