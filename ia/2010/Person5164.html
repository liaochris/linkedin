Alan Kerzner’s Summary

A proven business-builder in firms ranging from private start-ups to public multi-billion dollar corporations. A change agent committed to creating and implementing strategies that provide short-term growth and long-term competitive advantage. Special focus on building world-class organizations based on personal integrity, open communications and dedication to the team.

Alan Kerzner’s Specialties:

Record of developing concepts and transforming them into successful commercial businesses; repeated success turning around declining brands such as Oil of Olay, Johnson's Baby and Hartz Pet Care.

Extensive experience with acquisitions, licensing agreements and private equity-funded businesses; expertise with holistic, natural and wellness-focused enterprises; considerable involvement managing social media efforts to enhance consumer engagement.
