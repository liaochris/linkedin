Erica Becks’s Summary

As a mediator and facilitator, I help resolve issues involving: family, and workplace disputes. As a conflict resolution trainer, I teach service professionals the necessary skills to effectively communicate and resolve conflicts in the workplace and with clients.

I have met the California Dispute Resolution Council's Standards for qualifications as a Mediator and have also completed advanced training in family law, elder, divorce, workplace, small claims and guardianship mediation.

In addition to my professional endeavors, I also volunteer my time as a Community Mediator and Mediation Trainer at Seeds Community Mediation Center, a volunteer Guardianship Mediator for Contra Costa County Probate Court, and a Community Mediator and Trainer for Conflict Resolutions Programs. I recently joined the San Francisco Superior Court's Self Represented Litigant Mediation Panel, which provides free mediation services to Small Claims disputants.

I am a member of the Association of Conflict Resolution and the Alternative Dispute Resolution of Northern California organization.

My goal is to help empower my clients to resolve their disputes in a timely, cost effective and non-adversarial manner. I also hope to educate the greater public about the benefits of using conflict resolution skills to improve their professional work and personal lives.
