Laurie Baird’s Summary

Ms Baird is Director Technology Partnerships for Turner Broadcasting System Inc. She is responsible for identifying global research and development partnership activities in new media, covering the areas of advanced TV, wireless, broadband, data analytics and emerging advertising technologies. She supports all of Turner’s brands including CNN, Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, TNT, TBS, TCM, truTV and Turner Sports by seeking technologies that will expand Turner’s business. She also launched Turner’s university research program, investing in top US and international universities.
