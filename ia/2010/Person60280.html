Jeffry Martini’s Summary

I am a specialist in sourcing. locating, authenticating and purchasing/selling high value collectibles. These can range from automobiles, to rare books, to any Playboy item, to consumer electronics, to music, to tv, to movies, to exotic travel locales, to great and obscure restaurants, to great wine (at any price).....to anything you want or need! I like to think that I am a specialist in helping people reach and obtain their dreams and as a result, I have made many good friends along the way. My background includes stints at Paramount Studios, Rolling Stone Magazine, Associate Publisher of the L.A.Weekly, Publisher of Soundwave Magazine, Director of Sponsored Programs at CBS Radio and a Director at Superfriends Marketing. I have written for Rolling Stone, Playboy, Esquire and the L. A. Weekly. My endeavors have resulted in helping people find and do their Dream. I have been fortunate to be named to this year's Who's Who, been made a Kentucky Colonel, am an Eagle Scout and member of the Order of the Arrow, a winner of a Cine Golden Eagle and an Emmy for my TV work. I do enjoy life to the fullest!

Jeffry Martini’s Specialties:

Knowledge of all areas of Collecting with ties to collectors, dealers & auction houses, worldwide. I know how to source out unique and rare items and purchase them for a fraction of their true value. Example: I recently purchased at a public library bookstore, a pristine copy of Ernest Hemingway's "The Old Man and the Sea" for $5.00. It was a First Edition with the rare bluish tint back cover on the dust jacket, making it worth over $1500!!
