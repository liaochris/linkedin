Leif Jason’s Summary

Leif Jason has been in the web design industry for 13+ years, as webmaster, web designer, creative director and project manager, technical product, to principal of his own online marketing firm. Leif's thorough understanding of all elements of online design, development and marketing make him uniquely suited to assist any small to mid-sized business in their online marketing objectives.

Through Mastermynde, Leif Jason provides strategic marketing, website architecture, and production services for approximately 60 ongoing clients, ranging from SOHO to enterprise companies, in industries as diverse as renewable energy, steam punk clothing, recruiting, real estate and adventure travel.

Leif is passionate and dedicated, with a focus on achieving specific measurable goals. He is friendly and no-nonsense, and enjoys what he does. He is currently the top recommended Graphic/Web Designer on LinkedIn.
