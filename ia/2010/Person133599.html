Mark Benson’s Summary

Mark E. Benson presently serves as Managing Director, Chief Marketing Officer for Double Rock Corporation, a leading cash management and technology company providing the industry’s most innovative cash management and cash-related solutions to the broker-dealer, bank, qualified plan, and retail direct markets. Mr. Benson is directly responsible for the development and management of Double Rock’s strategic global integrated marketing initiative and each of its companies’ integrated marketing programs.

Mr. Benson recently served as President of Mark E. Benson & Associates, a full-service integrated marketing firm with clients that included Wells Fargo & Company and previously was Chief Marketing Officer for Key Bank USA, National Association's Champion Mortgage — a division of KeyCorp (Key).

During his tenure at Key, Mr. Benson rebranded a twenty-year-old company in 6 months, realized a 70% sales increase in 1 year with a 20% reduced marketing budget and guided the company from a regional to a national company with $2 billion in annual sales.

As President of Mark E. Benson & Associates, Mr. Benson strategically developed and tactically executed Wells Fargo & Company's first marketing audit, specifically, aligned brand marketing and direct marketing to improve the efficacy of lead acquisition and client retention through an integrated marketing program for Wells Fargo & Company’s Consumer Credit Group (CCG).

Mr. Benson's other senior-level positions include Executive Vice President, Marketing for the Economic Development Corporation in New York City and National Director of Marketing and Communications for the global financial services company DCI. In addition, Mr. Benson serves on the Advisory Board of The Advanced Marketing & Media Group, LLC.

Mark Benson’s Specialties:

Senior-level strategic, results-oriented marketing executive. Hired, trained, motivated and developed highly successful marketing teams.
