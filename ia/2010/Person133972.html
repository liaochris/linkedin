Mark Clifford’s Summary

A strong results-oriented professional specializing in Engineering Management and Product Commercialization. Held positions with increasing responsibilities including Chief Engineer, Director of Electrical Engineering, Technical Program Manager and Engineering Manager. Masterminded both long-term strategic and short-term tactical product and technology plans. Directed technology, product development, support and critical problem solving. Broad multi-disciplinary experience for automotive, aerospace, industrial, medical and process control instrumentation companies. Products have produced more than $1B in company revenues.
