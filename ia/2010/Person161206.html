Paul Nezandonyi’s Summary

Paul is a qualified public relations professional with a range of communications and stakeholder engagement experience in the social housing sector. He is a full member of the Chartered Institute of Public Relations and he is working towards Accredited Practitioner status.

Since June 2008 Paul has been Communications Manager at Lewisham Homes. He manages a team which has responsibility for a full spectrum of public relations activity including internal communications, media management, online and digital marketing, corporate communications and event planning as well as supporting consultation and community engagement.

He is Chair of a local refugee charity in Lewisham where he line manages the manager and works with the board of trustees to provide strategic direction and support. Also, as well as providing communications advise on public relations, fundraising, campaigning and forming local partnerships, he has a hands-on role to apply for funding and develop communications tools.

Paul is also a steering group member of Ignite - a network to drive forward the benefits of cultural diversity in PR.
