    "About the Babson College Fund

    The Babson College Fund is an academic program in which specially selected students manage a portion of the Babson College endowment.

    The program builds students’ investment research and portfolio management skills, and offers practical experience. Students conduct intensive fundamental and quantitative research to identify stocks that are good values at their current market prices. Selected stocks are then overweighted within a diversified portfolio.

    Managers monitor the fund’s overall risk and the sources of risk, aiming to keep risk and tracking error within allowable bounds. Managers budget the allowable risk to the active positions expected to add the most value.

    The portfolio is entirely equity, comprising of five sectors:

    Basic Materials & Energy
    Healthcare
    Consumer Goods & Services
    Telecom & Utilities and Technology
    Technology
