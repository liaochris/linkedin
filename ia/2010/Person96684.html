Jesse Davis’s Summary

Jesse Davis has over 12 years of experience in the wholesale and retail side of insurance. A graduate from the University of Central Florida, Jesse has worked his way up the career ladder in the insurance industry, working for Fortune 100 & 500 insurance companies and two other insurance companies with assets well over $500 million.

Jesse has been an active member of the community, involved in many aspects of leadership including the National Association of Health Underwriters, Florida Association of Health Underwriters, Christian Chamber of Commerce, Orlando Chamber of Commerce, Orlando Chamber of Commerce Small Business Board Member, Leadership Orlando, MyRegion.org, BusinessForce, Seminole County Chamber of Commerce, Board Member for the Central Florida Society of Human Resources Management, Member of the Jacksonville Society for Human Resource Management, Board Member for the Jacksonville Society of Human Resource Mamnagement, Advisory Board Member for Breakfast Club America, UCF Alumni Association, Heart of Florida United Way and is the founder of the Central Florida Executive Seminar Series which brings education and business opportunities to the small and mid size business community in Central Florida and Co-Founder of the Central Florida Economic Cooperative (www.economiccooperative.com) which is designed to bring business development, external and internal business education and community involvement to top level executives and their companies in the Central Florida business community. In 2007 & 2008 Jesse was named in the top 40 “Most Influential Men” by the Orlando Business Journal. In 2009 Jesse Davis was appointed to the board of the Baker County Planning & Zoning Agency. He also serves as the Chairman for the School Advisory Council for the Baker County Pre-K/Kindergarten Center.
