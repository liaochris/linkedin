Thomas Hebert’s Summary

My main career focus has been visual arts, advertising and multimedia design, in the past 9 years I have been focused on work flow analysis, usability, human factoring and UI design. I have been in the web, graphic, and print design field since 1984. I have worked in companies ranging from large corp. like AT&T, Hughes Aircraft through small start-ups like Webb interactive services, and my most recent job was for 3t systems, a small consulting company. Currently I'm an independent consulting.

My duties have included managing web-design and graphic design teams, creating print and online marketing and advertisement campaigns, creating design and UI specs, storyboarding websites, coding websites, optimizing websites using SEO techniques, localizing websites and applications, optimizing UI design, usability and human factor enhancements, optimizing and embedding video for websites, editing and encoding video for seminars and demonstration and maintaining content management websites, using Microsoft Sharepoint 2007, Silverlight to one off PHP CMS systems.
