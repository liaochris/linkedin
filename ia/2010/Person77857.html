Greg Meyers’s Summary

Greg has been developing software for more than 15 years, working on everything from 3D training simulations, to video games, to internet applications and web sites. Greg is President and Co-Owner of ForgeFX, a privately held company in San Francisco that specializes in the development of 3D training simulations, serious games, and simulator software.

After graduating from the University at Albany in New York where he studied computer animation, Greg moved to Silicon Valley in California to develop console and PC-based video games like Broderbund's The Last Express and Sega's Football Pro and Virtua Fighter game series. Holding positions like Director of Technology and Technical Lead, Greg has worked for a number of top digital media companies including Sega of America, Red Sky Interactive, Presage Software, Human Code, and Sapient. As President and Co-Owner of ForgeFX, Greg brings 15-plus years of software development experience to the table, helping the ForgeFX team develop quality 3D simulation products for their clients.

With a long list of successfully completed titles for high profile clients including Heifer International, LeapFrog, Intel, Global Ground Support, Pearson Education, Bandai, EA, Adobe, Pfizer, McDonalds, Chrysler, and Nissan, Greg is always ready to help clients develop high quality software products. A natural born puzzle and problem solver, Greg enjoys every challenge that a software development project brings.

Greg Meyers’s Specialties:
