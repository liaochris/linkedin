Mark Bower’s Summary

An accomplished, customer-facing technical leader whose strategic thinking and strong communication skills put him at the forefront of the software industry.

Connectegrity Chief Technology Officer and SharePoint specialist, Mark Bower has a proven track record in designing and developing IT solutions for business that deliver enormous efficiency and financial benefits to give companies the scope to grow incrementally and in a more clearly focussed way.

In his previous role at Microsoft, Mark was one of just a handful of SharePoint experts in the UK and worked on a wide range of programmes for customers such as the BBC, HBOS, DEFRA, Nationwide Building Society and KPMG. He also played a key role in the launch of SharePoint 2007, a business collaboration platform with integrated search functionality that allows colleagues and project partners to work together securely online. His exceptional communication skills encompass both business and technical concepts, from board level to developers, and combine detailed technical knowledge with astute business and commercial awareness.

Mark is a strong believer that the ongoing commoditisation and consumerisation of technology brings solutions that were once the preserve of FTSE-100 companies within reach of mid-size businesses, enabling them to think big without the burden of blue-chip overheads. As CTO of Connectegrity, he brings tangible competitive advantage to clients by identifying opportunities, defining a vision and designing a strategy to deliver IT solutions that minimise risk whilst maximising ROI.

Why not contact Mark to find out how his business-focussed creativity can take your business on to a higher level and open the doors to growth and efficiency?
