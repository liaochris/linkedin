Ronald Smit’s Summary

My goal: Responsible and sustainable resource development, mineral, institutional and human.

More than 25 years of experience in exploration and mining sector development, including:

* Leadership of and participation in donor-funded mining sector development projects:
- EU-funded National Environmental Impact Assessment and Strategic Environmental Assessment Project, of the Mining Sector Support Programme (MSSP), Ghana, 2005-2008;
- NDF-funded Mining Cadastre Design and Registry Project of the Sustainable Management of Mineral Resources Project (SMMRP), Uganda, 2009-2010;
- EU-funded Integration of DAP servers into the IMS and Incorporation of Geophysical and Geological Data project, of the MSSP, Ghana, 2009-2010;
- NDF-funded Establishment of GIS and Internet/Intranet Network for a Geological and Mineral Information System (GMIS) project, of the SMMRP, Uganda, 2010.

* Country assessments

* Multi-disciplinary mining project assessments

* Exploration company management in developing countries

* Gold and base metal exploration in various geological settings and different African countries

Ronald Smit’s Specialties:

- Leadership of teams with varied composition i.t.o. qualifications, nationalities, etc.

- Communication, negotiation, liaison and cooperation with government institutions, public and private sector organisations, mining and exploration companies

- Recognition of the big picture, the long view
