Dannelle Walker, Esq.’s Summary

Dannelle F. Walker is licensed to practice law by the Supreme Court of Tennessee. One of her goals is to be one of the leading policy consultants in the field of education law. While most of her work experience comes from the education field, she also has experience in probate work, labor/employment law, and working with underprivileged populations. Dannelle also completed the 40-hour Mediation Certification Training in the State of Arkansas. In addition, she also has an interest in voter education and protection having volunteered for Barack Obama's voter protection initiative.

Ms. Walker also hopes to be a successful philanthropist with the development of her first non-profit organization along with her brother, David Jon Walker, to help children whose parents have been diagnosed with HIV/AIDS. More to come on that endeavor.
