  • Unit Manager for Parks and Grounds Division (First woman to hold this position in the City)

    City of Oklahoma City

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    1979 — 1989 (10 years )

    Managed crew for the greenhouses for the city of OKC, plant production for the municipal buildings inside and out. We produced over 100,000 bedding plants per year for the medians, airports, civic buildings and parks. We used to produce every single plant from seed, cuttings or division there in the greenhouses. Maintenance of the Eldon Lyon Conservatories. I managed crews for the grounds to maintain 28 acres including the 4000 roses in the rose garden, the arboretum and the many other display gardens. Worked with the neighborhood associations and garden clubs to maintain relations and volunteers throughout the city. Maintained display flowering plants and tropicals to decorate downtown buildings for special events. Building, vehicle and equipment used to be maintained by our crews. None of this is done any longer. All work is done by private bids and purchased. I am amazed at what we did by hand and with no outside help. We did have a crew of 30 during the summer months.
