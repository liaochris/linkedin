Robyn Young’s Summary

Robyn Young is a passionate multi-talented artist born and raised in New England. She received her B.A. from Dean College and has also studied at UNLV and The Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater. In addition she has studied and performed across the country with master teachers who share her passion for art while inspiring many to make their own dreams come true.

Last summer Robyn became part of the FLIP FEST family teaching dance at one of the top gymnastic camps in the country. Working alongside with past and present Olympians John Macready and John Roethlisberger, Nastia Luikin, Carly Patterson and Alisha Sacramone to name a few, Robyn taught over 180 gymnists a week about dance, life, passion, and that with good work habits no goal is beyond your reach. For one month she choreographed and directed the weekly dance show finishing with a powerful tribute to Micheal Jackson.

Robyn has been a work-study, assistant, intern and volunteer with many companies and performing arts organizations across the country. As a member of the Brooklyn Arts Councel she has performed at many public schools teaching children and adults about the history of theater and dance from around the world. For the past two summers she has been working as an assistant choreographer for the ATDF Tap City Youth Program and has a work-study student since 2005. She's also had the priviledge of being on the staff at the L.A. Tap Fest., D.C. Tap Festival, and the Chicago Human Rhythmn Project Summer Dance Festival as a work-study.

Whats new with Robyn...

You can see Robyn dancing in SuperStarShun's new music video which will be airing in all Footlockers, Against all Odds, Mandees and more in malls across the United States. Keep up the Good Work Robyn : )
