Erik Magraken’s Summary

I am a Personal Injury and ICBC Claims Lawyer practicing in British Columbia, Canada.

My practice is restricted exclusively to plaintiff-only Personal Injury and Class Action Claims with a particular emphasis on claims involving orthopaedic injuries, complex soft tissue injuries, spinal cord injuries and brain injuries.

With over 20 MacIsaac Group branch offices I travel throughout BC representing clients involved in tort claims with the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia (ICBC).

I do take referrals and pay competitive referral fees in appropriate circumstances.

If you or your clients need help dealing with an ICBC claim please don't hesitate to contact me for a free consultation.
