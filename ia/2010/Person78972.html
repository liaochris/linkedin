Gustavo Larriera Sosa’s Summary

Gustavo Larriera (aka Gux). Gux is a SQL Server and .NET consultant, he provided consultancy services to the Microsoft South Cone's Development & Platform Evangelism team during 5 years. He has more than 15 years experience in database teaching and researching for academic institutions. He also has 5 years experience as speaker in technical events and conferences. Gux is graduated in Informatics Engineering and System Analysis, and also holds a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer certification. Born in Montevideo (Uruguay), he lives in Buenos Aires, Argentina, with his wife and little daughter.
