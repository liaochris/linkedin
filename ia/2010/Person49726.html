Dave McClure’s Summary

I'm a geek, startup investor, former software developer and entrepreneur, tech blogger, marketing nerd, and overall fan of entrepreneurship. I've lived in Silicon Valley for 20 years and loved every minute of it.

I run a seed-stage investment program for Founders Fund called FF Angel which makes $50K-250K investments in startups raising seed or series A capital. As of Dec 2009 FF Angel has made investments in 18 startups. I also ran the fbFund REV 2009 Incubator program which made 22 investments in Facebook & Facebook Connect-related startups.

Under the umbrella of various projects, I advise and invest in geeks and entrepreneurs creating new consumer & small business internet startups. My specialty is helping startups improve product and marketing efforts by emphasizing customer development, an iterative approach, and the collection and measurement of simple, actionable metrics.
