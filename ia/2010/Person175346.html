    (Higher Education industry)

    March 2007 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Custom executive education development and consulting for clients in higher education, financial services and public sector. Develop program for Carnegie Mellon University, "Post Merger Integration: Mastering M&A Intangibles," about how to fix the leading causes of underperforming or failed mergers. Surprisingly, the key drivers of merger failure are not connected to the "hard numbers" of the deal, but by communications, teamwork, sales force/channel integration and the typical organizational problems associated with cost reduction.

The technology underlying data, data warehousing and analytics has become more sophisticated, but our ability to harness advanced technologies and achieve business results that show up on a P&L hasn't. The key is deploying "just-in-time" insight in a language that everyday people can understand and use. The language gap between people who develop insight and those who use has grown proportionate to changes in technology.
