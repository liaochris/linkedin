Emily Peterson’s Experience

  • Founder and Director

    Select Law Pty Ltd

    (Legal Services industry)

    July 2008 — April 2010 (1 year 10 months)

    Select Law Pty Ltd was an online legal tendering service which helped clients to find a suitable lawyer. Law firms were invited to tender online for suitable matters and clients were able to log into their secure Select Law portal and compare cost estimates, skills, experience, size, history etc.

    The company launched in Victoria in June 2009 and given the success in this State, expanded to NSW the following month.The business achieved widespread publicity (eg in BRW, Lawyers Weekly, Sky’s Law TV and ALB Magazine) and formed an alliance with the Australian Corporate Lawyers Association.

  • Commercial litigator

    Arnold Bloch Leibler

    (Partnership; Law Practice industry)

    March 2006 — July 2008 (2 years 5 months)

    I completed articles at Arnold Bloch Leibler (“ABL”) in the Litigation Department in March 2007. Thereafter I was admitted to the Supreme Court of Victoria and remained in the Litigation Department at ABL until July 2008. During my two and a half years at ABL I worked solely in commercial litigation acting for a range of clients from high net worth individuals to listed companies. I had the day to day carriage of some matters which involved negotiating settlements, liaising with clients as principal contact and instructing counsel. I was supervised by Leonie Thompson, Leon Zwier and Alex King.
