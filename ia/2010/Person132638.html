Marie-Josée Gagnon, ARP’s Summary

I founded CASACOM in Montreal in 2001. It is a house of communications known for its highly creative, results-driven team, determined to build enduring and profitable relationships with its clients. Moreover, CASACOM has 100 partner firms all over the world thanks to its membership in the Worldcom Group one of the largest partnership in PR in the world.

Marie-Josée Gagnon, ARP’s Specialties:

Specializing in corporate communications, public affairs, and marketing communications, CASACOM offers strategic planning, branding, crisis management, media relations, social media strategy, internal communications, and event planning services.
