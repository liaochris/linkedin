Colin Wright’s Summary

Intellectual Property Counsel with Citrix Systems located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA. As an in-house attorney, I assist in the evaluation of new technologies, manage the procurement of trademarks and patents, provide internal consulting with respect to IP matters, and assist with diligence when pursuing acquisitions. Previously, I assisted law firm clients in the fields of software, telecommunications, semiconductors, medical devices, and computer hardware with the procurement of patents, trademarks and copyrights. Prior to law school, I worked in the semiconductor, energy and software industries for 7 years as both a sales & marketing engineer and a software developer. I am licensed to practice law in Georgia, the District of Columbia and before the US Patent & Trademark Office.
