Ron Ratliff’s Summary

Ron is a teacher, mentor and coach. He listens, counsels, encourages and challenges clients to excel in managing their finances, both business and personal.

As a Certified Public Accountant with 25 years of experience in both corporate and public accounting, Ron has developed a unique set of skills to help clients identify and quantify their financial goals and, accordingly, to develop an appropriate action plan that sets out to achieve those goals.

Ron focuses on developing relationships with clients as their “coach and mentor” with the goal of becoming their "Trusted Financial Guide". Ron has been recognized for having “the heart of a teacher”.

Ron is a member of the Principa Alliance, the Texas Society of CPAs, the AICPA and is a Dave Ramsey Certified Financial Counselor.

Ron Ratliff’s Specialties:

Strategic Advisor/Financial Counselor; Change Management; Management Reporting; Budgetary and Internal Controls; Financial Literacy in the Workplace; Financial Coach/Counselor; Tax Compliance -- Business and Personal.
