John Parker’s Summary

John Parker has been involved in the music business since 1985. His career began when he joined Kingsborough Community College's radio station, B91 (NY's only 24/7 Dance Radio Station at the time). John held the positions of Publicity Director, Music Director and then Program Director for B91 during his stay.

In 1989, Parker made the jump from Radio to Records, joining independent label, Profile Records. There he worked with Run-DMC, DJ Quik, Judy Torres and many others. John was in charge of Retail and Dance Promotion.

After a 5 year stay at Profile, he went to work for Select Records. There he worked with acts like The Jerky Boys & Chubb Rock.

In late 1995, John was given the opportunity to join a start-up label that was going to be opened by Cory Robbins. John holds the position of VP, A&R/Dance Promotion. He has worked with Cascada, D.H.T., DJ Sammy, Lasgo, Ian Van Dahl and Rockell to name a few.

Parker is also one half of the Mix team known as the Happy Boys. With over 1.5 Million Mix CD's Sold, the Happy Boys are amongst the Biggest Selling Mixers of all time.

He has also Produced over 30 Compilations to date on Robbins including the their best selling Trance Party & Dance Party series.

At the 2003 & 2004 International Dance Music Awards during the Winter Music Conference in Miami, John was named "Best National Record Promoter, In House".

John was also named the #1 Dance A&R Person in the World by HitQuarters.com for 2007. He also wound up on the list of overall A&R people in America at #14 and on the World Chart at #20.

In addition, John was the on-air celebrity judge for WNEW-FM's "NY Morning Mix Idol" in 2006. He was also a founding board member of the Dance Music Hall of Fame, has been a part of the DanceStar Awards nominating committee and is a voting member of NARAS.

He's also spoken at a number of music seminars, including the Winter Music Conference and DJ Times Expo, about issues from A&R to Marketing and Promotion.

John Parker’s Specialties:

Producing compilations, DJ mixing, promotion and ability to identify "hit" records.
