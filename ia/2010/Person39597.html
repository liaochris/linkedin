Chris Reed’s Summary

I have over 20 years of B2B and B2G sales experience. This includes account and relationship management and customer service. My experience has been honed through the wireless industry starting in the paging business then through the cellular business. Currently I am the the owner of CREED Improvements a general contracting firm that helps home owners turn their houses into their dream home. You can check out our website at www.creedimprovements.com and email me at chris@creedimprovements.com.
Also I am co-owner of IndyDodgeBall.com we host public or private dodgeball events for fun and team building. No long term commitments is the key to our offering. Just sign up through the website and come out and play. We put you on a team and you have fun.
I am also a mentor of young adults at options charter school for a group called CFI. You can learn more at www.go-cfi.org. Last but not least I am a trainer that trains anyone how to use certain technologies such as LinkedIN especially how to use the tool to market your business. Ask me how you can attend one of my classes.
