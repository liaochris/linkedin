Claire Palmer, Career & Coach Development, PCC’s Summary

A Professional Certified Coach (PCC) with the International Coach Federation (ICF) and a qualified coach supervisor with extensive coach training and experience together with a corporate business background.

I work over the phone and face to face and different time zones are not a problem. I provide;

~Leadership Coaching; if you are already successful in a business role yet see that ‘fine-tuning’ leadership skills or career planning or being truly authentic could make a huge difference. Focus areas areas; handling and leading change, making successful transitions, greater impact as a leader, career planning.

~Career Development Coaching; enabling you to get a clear picture of the career or next role that is absolutely right for you. Including raising your own self-awareness; clarifying your own strengths, those activities you love, being clear of your individual requirements from your next business or organisation. Practical sounding board and support.

~Coach Development (Supervision, Mentoring); taking your coaching to a new level of mastery. Reflective practise. ICF accreditation mentoring. Skills Development. 1 to 1 and Group.

Please contact me direct, either email or phone, if you'd like an exploratory conversation or more information.
