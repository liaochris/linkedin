Justin Cordes’s Summary

I've been a television editor for over 10 years. I got my start at Grey Advertising in 1997. I started in the dubbing room and quickly moved up to editor. It was an exciting time and it was only a few years since the introduction of the computerized "non-linear" editing. I went on from Grey to work at other ad agencies until about 2002 when I started working in news and info-tainment. For the last 5 years I've been perma-lance at ABC's Good Morning America Weekend Edition with some moonlighting on the weekday show. I've also started shooting and this past summer I directed, shot and edited a web commercial for the book Prince Harming Syndrome from best selling author Karen Salmansohn.
