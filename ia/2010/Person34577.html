Cyndi Craven’s Summary

I'm a web design pro who has been helping business and professionals harness the power of the Web since 1996. I specialize in "all things WordPress," using WordPress as a content management system to design and build highly customized web sites 1) that you can control, manage and maintain on your own--in an extremely simple yet powerful manner, and 2) that will grow with your business as it helps to grow your business!

I also specialize in designing custom Online Media Packages that combine a beautiful, custom web site–that you can update yourself–together with an integrated blog, and custom graphic treatment and customized setup of your Twitter and Facebook profiles. I then integrate the function of your online accounts and profiles (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn), web site and blog, so you can make the most of the power of cross-posting your content… while reducing the time, and money, you spend doing so.

Visit me at CCravenDesign.com to find out more!
