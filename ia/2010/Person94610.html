Jennifer Chamberlain’s Summary

Pixel Pop Designs is an Atlanta-based design studio specializing in website design and online marketing. We design user-focused websites, micro-sites, online marketing programs and product branding that drives engaging user experiences and achieves your business goals.

Pixel Pop Designs was started in 2008 by Jennifer Chamberlain. Chamberlain has more than 15 years of design experience. Before starting Pixel Pop, Chamberlain led WebMD's in-house design studio working on online marketing programs for big brand consumer packaged goods, prescription drugs and medical device companies such as Tylenol, Kellogg, Pfizer and Stryker.

Prior to WebMD, Chamberlain served as lead designer and user experience guru for ECS, the in-house interactive design studio for German giant Bertelsmann AG. Her design career began in New York City in the late nineties working in magazine print design for a variety of prominent publications including Food & Wine Magazine, Conde Nast Traveler and YM Magazine.

Much of Pixel Pop Design’s work is confidential new business development that cannot be shared. Given that, please enjoy the examples below.
