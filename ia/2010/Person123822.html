    – Brand Strategy Measurement
    – Visual Identity and Creative Expression

  • Manager, Business Planning Office

    Toyota Motor Europe

    (Privately Held; Automotive industry)

  • Project Manager, Long Term Product Development Team

    Toyota Motor Europe

    (Privately Held; Automotive industry)

    2001 — 2005 (4 years )

    In addition, I was involved with new business development projects for Toyota in Russia.
