Prantik Mazumdar’s Summary

I have had the golden opportunity to serve the Singapore government for three years in its efforts in industry development and assisting its local enterprises in internationalizing. This has provided me with the necessary exposure and opportunity in interacting with scores of Singapore based entities and understand the challenges and critical success factors that are quintessential when companies globalize. I have had the blessing to work and partner some of the brightest in the Singapore Civil Service to learn more about, understand and contribute to the best practices the proactive government implements to boost the strength of its local economy.

Having moved to the private sector, it has been an interesting experience to actually live and breathe through the challenges that private businesses face in developing business, especially in an international context.

At StrategiCom I had the unique opportunity to work directly with the CEO to learn and hone my consulting (brand management) and international marketing skills. In the recent past I also have had the opportunity to publish in local Singapore and Indian newspaper and business peridiodicals in the domain of B2B brand management.

Currently at Pinstorm, the world's leading 'Pay-for-Performance' digital marketing firm, I have the unique opportunity to evangelize the use of the technology across the Internet and social media in particular to implement marketing/advertising campaigns that generate leads and sales for our esteemed clients.

Its a fascinating experience to witness the rapid evolution of the digital domain and to be able to recommend and implement revenue generating campaigns for clients.
