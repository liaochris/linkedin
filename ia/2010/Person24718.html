Scott Blair’s Summary

I am senior regional editor for McGraw-Hill Construction, where I edit and produce Southwest Contractor Magazine, a bi-monthly construction publication covering architecture, engineering and contracting in Arizona, Nevada and New Mexico, and Northwest Construction, covering Washington, Oregon and Alaska. These publications cover the people and companies working on large and small projects that make a difference in local communities and the trends that impact the industry.

I also report for Engineering News-Record as the Southwest and Northwest bureaus, and contribute to Architectural Record and GreenSource.

I edit the websites for SW and NW and write a blog covering the Southwest market. Visit at www.southwest.construction.com and www.northwest.construction.com

Scott Blair’s Specialties:

I have worked in media and publishing for over 20 years, including radio, film, television, newspapers and periodicals. I've gained valuable industry experience from multiple perspectives: as an editor, producer, advertising sales executive, music director, general manager, freelance writer and circulation specialist.
