Marinella Len’s Summary

Marinella Len is an award-winning marketing strategist and graphic designer with over 12 years professional design experience with an emphasis on brand development and art direction. Marinella worked for KB Home Corporation and Leisure Publications, Inc., as an art director before establishing Verve Network, in Long Beach, California serving client that include: ad agencies, non-profits, corporations, and large and small businesses.
