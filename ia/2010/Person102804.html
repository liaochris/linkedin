JOHN WENZEL’s Summary

Reporter, editor and designer at newspapers, magazines and tabloids in Wisconsin, Pennsylvania and Florida. Oklahoma State Pot-Limit Hold'em Champion. Author of three books on poker: The Everything Poker Strategy Book, Everything Hold'em and The Only Poker Book You'll Ever Need.

Only poker publication editor who is both a longtime, world-class high-stakes player and an experienced professional journalist. Also gives lessons and seminars on poker strategy and tactics.

JOHN WENZEL’s Specialties:

Writing, poker, overseeing magazines
