Lauren Buchholz’s Summary

I am a highly-skilled writer and photographer seeking work involving researching and documenting environmental issues of various scales. I have a strong educational and professional background in environmental studies and communications, and have several years of experience working with diverse state- and national-level environmental organizations. As an avid outdoor explorer, researcher, and naturalist, I hope to dedicate my life to understanding and investigating pressing environmental issues and presenting these to others through my work. Examples of my previous and current articles and photographs are available as links under "Websites" and "Honors and Awards."

Lauren Buchholz’s Specialties:

Online and print journalism, photography, creative and technical writing, communications, publications, editing, environmental education and recreation, graphics and website design, French.
