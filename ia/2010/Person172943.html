Rich Whalen’s Summary

Rich Whalen is currently the SVP, Head of Delivery / Group Executive Producer at RAPP, where he is responsible for all Delivery for the NY office. Responsible for both print and digital, Rich brings a deep history in project management, strategic planning, and agency operations over the past 13 years.

Rich Whalen’s extensive program management experience is centered in web-based solutions, supporting business acquisition initiatives and building client relationships. He is adept at working with multiple departments creating efficiencies and optimizing profit margins for the agency. Rich has spearheaded strategic development, managed full site builds, redesigns and ongoing relationship marketing campaigns for Merck, J&J, FoodTV, British Airways, XM Radio, New York Knicks, Novartis, Pfizer and various other clients.

Prior to joining RAPP, Rich Whalen worked as an Executive Producer for Wealthhound.com, a financial portal that features online stock trading, online mortgage and up-to-the-minute financial and internet news. There he won a Webby Award for excellence in financial services and an Adtech Award for best financial services site. Prior to that, he was a Senior Project Manager at AGENCY.COM, a leading interactive agency that develops strategy, branding technology and marketing services to help grow interactive initiatives for Fortune 500 clients. He started his career at R.R. Bowker Publishing Co. where he delivered web-based solutions for clients like Barnes and Noble, Borders, Yahoo and the New York Public Library dating back to 1996.

Mr. Whalen is proficient in web-based application development and campaign management. Rich also works with the Scott Hazelcorn Children's Foundation serving as Board Member and is a graduate of West Virginia University.
