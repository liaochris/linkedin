Sarah Mueller’s Summary

Sarah melds passion, intuition and creativity with over fifteen years of insider hospital operational and institutional knowledge, focusing primarily on logistics, planning and design. Her overall outlook on projects is driven by a dedicated curiosity as to how facilities navigate their inner workings, from the most basic daily tasks to the most complicated issues. At the very beginning of her career she decided that truly successful medical institutions work much like a well-oiled machine, all the parts and pieces functioning together to form a unit that is efficient and capable of meeting expectations across the board. Her ultimate focus has always been to adhere to institutional and design goals while not compromising the patient/family experience.
Sarah has touched all aspects of healthcare infrastructure and design. Her scope of knowledge within the built environment, allow her to work not only with architects and builders, but also interior designers and major medical equipment/furniture manufacturers. She has an innate ability to collaborate and build partnerships with a variety of industry experts which makes her an efficient and dedicated consultant on any healthcare related project.
AGVS (Automated Guided Vehicle Systems) are a unique part of streamlining materials handling and logistics for hospital operations. This system removes the need for people to be pushing and pulling heavy carts throughout large medical campuses as well as improving customer service with timely deliveries and creating a more efficient and reliable system. AGVS can be integrated both horizontally and vertically into a project or existing campus. This is a technology that has been in manufacturing for years and will continue to be on the forefront of healthcare design and operations.
