Taunya Zilkie’s Summary

Taunya Zilkie, CEO of Zilk Inc. has worked in various aspects of entertainment, fashion, PR and technology for over twenty years.

She has been privileged to grow and learn from some of the most prominent and successful individuals that have shaped the creative and media industries.

As a former figure skater, actress and singer, Taunya Zilkie has evolved her artistic talents and combined her business experience in the Entertainment Industry to form a company that focuses on business management & development, branding and public relations using a variety of cutting edge techniques. Ms. Zilkie has developed a unique approach and comprehensive set of skills to serve an ever-changing world. As the internet, entertainment and legal industries collide, Ms. Zilkie has developed a company equipped to handle a wide variety of personal or professional challenges and goals. From the creation of a basic website... to the launch of a brand.... to the management of public perception of a client or entity, Ms. Zilkie customizes dynamic solutions.

Her prevailing aspiration, and the aspiration of ZILK INC., in all facets, is to provide clients with artistry and business solutions of the highest caliber and a professional experience based in integrity, positivity and productivity.

---Since 2004, Ms. Zilkie’s Website Design & Online Services Company has been creating dynamic websites primarily for small businesses as well as artists and celebrities such as Michael Jackson. Zilk Inc. is a boutique ‘creativity’ company with large company capabilities and services located in Los Angeles, California. The services we offer include website development and design, ecommerce, hosting, maintenance, SEO marketing, analysis and multimedia production. Free Consultations.
