David Carpenter’s Summary

"The old advertising & marketing hound doesn't hunt like it once did. Time for a new dog!" David continues to push the envelope of digital and emerging media by solving tough business problems for great clients with new and innovative solutions.

David has demonstrated this expertise with recent / current clients like Microsoft, Cisco, eBay, Motorola, ADP and Nortel. David led a team that created and launched the CMO Thought Leadership Series.

Prior to joining Alt. David provided professional services and strategic direction on acquisition, retention and loyalty programs for clients including Xerox, Microsoft, Toshiba, Avery Dennison, Qualcomm, VISA, World Vision, AAA, Kawasaki, RSA Security, H&R Block and Option One Mortgage.

Digital Go-To-Market Strategy, Online Marketing Optimization (OMO), Digital Marketing, Inbound Marketing, Search Engine Marketing (SEO and SEM), Social Media, Word of Mouth (WOM), Mobile and Direct Marketing, Acquisiton, Retention, Loyalty and Lead Generation.
