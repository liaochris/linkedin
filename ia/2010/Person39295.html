Christine Lindquist’s Experience

  • Program Manager

    Western Washington Area Health Education Center

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    July 2004 — Present (5 years 9 months)

    I currently run two Department of Health contracted programs through the Western Washington Area Health Education Center in Seattle:

    -The Volunteer/Retired Providers (VRP) Program, which supports volunteer health care professionals and clinics providing care to underserved patient populations across Washington State. The VRP Program is a health care safety net to Washington's under- and uninsured patients, providing medical, dental and mental health care to over 70,000 people annually.

    -Project H.O.P.E. (Health Occupations Preparatory Experience), a summer internship program for high school students from medically underserved communities. Students are competitively selected to participate in a paid, six week, health care internship within their home community. Support is also provided to assist students in pursuing their educational goals. Over 94% of the students who participate in Project H.O.P.E. end up in a health career. A full 100% graduate from high school.
