Stephanie D. Burroughs’s Experience

  • President

    StephanieSpeaking LLC

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    2009 — Present (2 years )

    Welcome to StephanieSpeaking LLC a public speaking, training, business coaching and consulting firm.

    Stephanie Burroughs is an informative, inspiring and engaging national keynote speaker, trainer, facilitator and small business coach and consultant. She provides both audiences and clients with practical insights and success strategies for business growth in the government procurement process; government procurement preference programs and government and private certification programs.

    We empower small business owners to confidently develop strategies and action plans to transition with greater ease through the government procurement process. We will help you demystify the various government procurement and certification programs by providing you with clarity, structure and growth strategies.

    Our services include keynote presentations, training programs, business consulting, group and one-on-one coaching and consulting.

    Market: small-midsize businesses; entrepreneurs; would be entrepreneurs; christian women's organizations, faith based EDCs; business associations, business technical assistance organizations, women's groups; business networking organizations and professional associations.

  • Director, Procurement Programs

    NJ Small Business Development Centers

    (Program Development industry)

    2004 — Present (7 years )

    Currently contracted by the NJSBDC to develop and produce training programs; workshops; expos;and conferences for the business to business and business to government small business community. Provides small business counseling; advises and assists business owners with successfully surfing and identifying appropriate contract opportunities.

  • Project Director

    NJ Transit Corp.

    (Government Agency; Program Development industry)

    2000 — 2004 (4 years )

    Project Director for Contract Compliance, Inc. on the NJ Transit contract for the Hudson Bergen Light Rail Project. Managed this contract over a period of 4 years totalling approximately $4.5 million with a staff of 9 personnel. Provided the following services: DBE certification auditing and recommendation services, contract compliance monitoring and reporting, outreach, workshops, event planning for small business fairs, workshops and seminars; and small business client counseling.

  • Business Development Representative

    The Port Authority of NY & NJ

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    1987 — 1995 (8 years )

    Created the first of its kind Expo in 1991 for minority, women and small contractors The Construction Contractors Clearinghouse provided S/M/WBE contracting firms the opportunity to meet with and learn from major prime contractors, large construction management firms and public agency personnel.
    Performed onsite contract compliance monitoring of numerous Port Authority of NY & NJ facilities for the M/WBE/DBE programs. Audited and made certification determinations for M/W/S/DBE programs. Organized and produced numerous outreach events, seminars, workshops, and business fairs and worked closely with minority, women and small business owners and with PANYNJ construction, engineering and architecture department personnel.
