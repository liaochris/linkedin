Jon Horn’s Summary

Jon Horn is the President of JMH Consulting and a leading expert on the use of technology in adult education. He is a frequent presenter at conferences and has been a dynamic presence in the continuing education field for more than ten years as an administrator and educator. Jon spent several years as a technology and web consultant before specializing in consulting for continuing and professional education.
