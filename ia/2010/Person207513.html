Tracy Brodd’s Summary

I'm Tracy Brodd, Founder and President of Brodd Promotions, a full service promotional products distributorship. For many years I've been told by colleagues and friends to start my own business so the time has finally come.

As an accomplished, Account Executive, Mentor, Networker, Sales Manager and Production Manager and now Entrepreneur I strive to excel at everything that has my name on it. From hiring a receptionist to approving an annual marketing plan it must meet my specifications because now it truly is my signature of approval.

This is an easy industry to poke fun at -- it is viewed by so many as simply salespeople pushing product...bagavon's let's say. Walking into your office with a bagful of chatchkeys which is almost poured on your desk, "OK, which one?".

I vow to do it differently. I see the value in approaching a customer in a consultative way to learn about their business and goals. What is their message, who is their customer, how can they be reached? Once reached, how can we track the ROI? What is their goal, driving attendance to their trade show booth? Increasing clicks on their website? Getting new customers? Incentivising the sales force. Showing appreciation on Customer Service Day to your team? There are so many goals that the strategic, use of creative promotional products can impact.

Call me for a free consultation or mention this bio and receive 30% off your first order!
