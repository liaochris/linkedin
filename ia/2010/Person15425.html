Anuraag Sunder, CPCU’s Summary

I am an insurance professional with 10 years of relevant cross functional, multi- geography focused experience.

Am presently working with PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC). As a member of Business Advisory, I am supporting Life, General and Health insurers plus financial services intermediaries across functional and business aspects including new JVs, partner search, market entry, strategy consulting, operational & transformation consulting, distribution consulting and related areas. Looking forward to working on IFRS, M&As and other exciting areas !

Prior, I was a member of Watson Wyatt Worldwide's Insurance Practice .Then, I was focused towards providing consulting support to life, general and health insurers around strategy, market entry (insurers, brokers etc), bancassurance, distribution, training structures etc. For nearly an year, was dedicated only to Health Insurance Consulting

Prior, I was a member of GE Capital/ GE Insurance Solutions for 3 years in the Insurance Analytics team for Global Market Research & Strategy. Provided key strategic, marketing research inputs to customers & leadership team of GE Insurance Solutions (and post buy out, to Swiss Re.)

My first role, immediately after MBA, was with Tokio Marine & Fire Insurance in year 2000. As a member of Tokio’s start-up JV team for IFFCO-Tokio General Insurance in India, I was involved in a range of functions including Corporate Planning, Financial Modelling, Conducting economic or feasibility studies, Project Management, Strategic Alliances, Bancassurance, Marketing, Business Development, Underwriting (Personal lines & Commercial lines), Operations and Accounts.

Am also a member of

•American Institute of Chartered Property Casualty Underwriters (AICPCU) and Insurance Institute of America
•Insurance Institute of India.
