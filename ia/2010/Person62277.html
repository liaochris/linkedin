Ed Garcia’s Summary

Ed Garcia is Director of Implementation Services at TSYS, one of the world's largest companies for outsourced payments services, based in Columbus, Georgia, U.S.A. In his role, Ed is reponsible for bringing new South American clients onto TSYS' processing platforms.

After joining TSYS in 2006 to focus on Latin American business strategy and growth, Ed moved to marketing in 2008. There he assumed his product marketing responsibilities and began overseeing the marketing efforts for TSYS' entire product line. In his role in Implementation Services, Ed utilizes his significant Latin American experinece to successfully board new clients throughout South America.

Ed brings more than 15 years of payments experience to TSYS. Before joining the company, he spent his career in the international cards market, including eight years with Citigroup International as the Cards and Risk Technology Head for Latin America. Prior to his contribution at Citigroup, Ed was responsible for all software development supporting the Travel Services Group of American Express. Ed started his career in the payments industry at Visa International where he directed the introduction of chip cards into the Latin American market.

Ed is fluent in English, Spanish and Portuguese, and holds an MBA from Concordia College. He is also a graduate of the American Bankers Association School of Bank Card Management and is a certified Six Sigma Black Belt.
