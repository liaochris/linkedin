Celeste Wroblewski’s Summary

Extensive experience in relationship building and communications for nonprofit organizations.

Currently, I work as vice president of external relations -- leading fundraising, marketing communications, and policy communications -- for Donors Forum, which promotes philanthropy and a strong nonprofit sector in Illinois.

I also volunteer as a blogger for the Communications Network, a great organization for communicators in philanthropy: http://comnetwork.typepad.com.
