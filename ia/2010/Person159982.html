Patrick L. Schmidt’s Summary

Diverse experience in advising foreign companies and governments on tourism related infrastructure and telecommunications, with an emphasis on projects in Latin America and the Caribbean. Advised Caribbean Government on the development and financing of hotels and the privatization and modernization of its airport, including the issuance of airport revenue bonds. Advised foreign governments and foreign as well as domestic companies on policy matters before the U.S. Congress and Executive Branch. Advised foreign companies investing in defense related industries on restrictions on investment in the United States, including the Exon-Florio Statute.
