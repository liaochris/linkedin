Bill Schaffler’s Summary

I have an extensive background in Restaurant and Retail.
My restaurant experience includes start-up concepts, mergers and acquisitions, and turnarounds.
My passion lies in building and developing great teams and products for the users of them.
My current endeavor is People Report / Black Box Intelligence.

People Report is the leading provider of human capital metrics and benchmarking for the restaurant and retail industries. Our clients number 150 companies and almost 20,000 units in the restaurant industry.

Black Box Intelligence is our newest venture focused on benchmarking sales and operational data, and is a sister company to People Report. Black Box Intelligence launched in June 2009 and already has approx 50 member companies and approximately 10,000 units in the membership base. Contact me if you would like more information about our interactive tools.
