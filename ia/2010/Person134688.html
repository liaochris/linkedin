Ella Arps’s Summary

personal mail address: ella.arps@gmail.com.
By definition, we are an interactive agency that creates business communications and experiential marketing programs that deliver results. By reputation, we’re smart. Hard-working. Fun. We’re passionate about our work. But we don’t take ourselves too seriously. For us, “It’s in the experience.”
It’s in the experience we’ve gained working with great projects for nearly 40 years. What makes us different is that we are not a traditional agency. We’re 100% interactive, and driven by our desire to deliver creative, interactive solutions that strengthen your brand and build your business. One way to do this is through personal brainstorm sessions and mixed group workshops.The other way is to remain in the public domain jobhunting for career planners.
