Sony Mayuvi SE Ak, CPMA’s Summary

After 3.5-years study in Accounting University of Indonesia, Sony joined Unilever Indonesia in 2002. He enjoyed various positions from internal audit, sales finance, supply chain finance & marketing finance.

As an internal audit staff he was responsible for assessing risk & internal control implementation in several area such as sales branch, factory, account payable department and accounting for fixed assets. He visited branch and factory to review the internal control and prepared audit report for group internal audit manager.

In 2003, he was made Modern Trade Customer Accountant of Key Account (i.e Carrefour, Matahari,Hypermart,Hero, Giant, Indomaret & Alfamart, etc). He was successful to manage his responsibility in term of account receivable management and trade marketing investment management by improving debtor collection days and doing great promo evaluation. Therefore he got Unilever Enterprise Award in 2005, sales finance creativity award in 2006, club 25 award in 2006.

Three years later, he had an opportunity to lead the finance team in biggest factory in PT Unilever. His challenge was to ensure that finance process meet global standard and also to support the factory get TPM advance special award. He closely worked with factory general manager and in return the factory passed global assessment in 2007 and get TPM advance special award in 2008. His department was also the best department in implementing TPM ways of working among other in factory.

In 2008, he was assigned to marketing finance for hair category which is the biggest category in Unilever. He started the job by improving ways of working in Innovation Project Management. The first focus is to improve quality of business case in division (home and personal care) and then continue to improve monitoring tools of the project. In 4 months He was successful to improve Indonesia score from rank 18 to rank 4. Therefore company gave him CFO award 2009 for his contribution in 2008.
