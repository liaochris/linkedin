David Moore’s Summary

A pragmatic visionary and results-oriented leader – with 20 years’ experience and a proven record successfully building and leading global teams, business initiatives, and organizations at the regional, national and international level.

A demonstrated and recognized talent for envisioning, developing and executing technology, product, and services strategy; delivering business value and competitive advantage; increasing organizational performance; and, for driving productivity through process design, technology innovation and operational maturity. Experienced creating and managing virtual and geographically distributed organizations. A proven ability to build, develop, and motivate teams, departments and organizations of senior management, technology, user experience, quality, and e-business professionals.

David Moore’s Specialties:

Organizational Leadership; Start-ups, M&A; Technology, Product and Services Strategy; IT Strategy and IT-Business Alignment; Global Delivery and Leadership at the Regional, National and International level (U.S., N.A., U.K., E.U., India, Asia); Large Program Leadership (US$100m, multi-year); E-Business Strategy and Solutions; Web 2.0; Enterprise Architecture, EAP and EAI; Software Product Development; Systems Development Methods (OO SDLCs, proprietary SDLCs, MilStd), QA and IV&V, and CMM/CMMi.
