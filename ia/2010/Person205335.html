Todd Martin’s Summary

Todd brings over 20 years of sales and executive management experience, with 10 years exclusively within the Customer Relationship Management “CRM” industry. Todd has served in sales management roles with early stage growth companies like GoldMine, SalesLogix, Sage CRM and Nimble.

During the years of 1995 through 2001, Todd held the position as Director of Sales for GoldMine Software Corporation. During this period, Todd managed the companies sales operations, value-added reseller channel, and business development partnerships.

During the years 2001 through 2003, Todd held the position of Regional Manager for the Western United States for Interact Software. In this position, Todd was responsible for the major account sales to Strategic Accounts focused on the sale and delivery of SalesLogix.

During the years 2003 through 2005, Todd held the position of Business Development for ACCPAC. During this period, Todd helped Sage CRM successfully build a value-added reseller channel.

In January 2010, Todd joined Nimble as the Director of Sales. At Nimble, Todd is responsible for growing the company’s global customer base through the management and expansion of Nimble’s partnerships with leading technology and service providers worldwide.

Todd received his Bachelor of Science from the University of Arizona in 1990.
