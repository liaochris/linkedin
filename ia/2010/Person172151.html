Richard Cusick’s Summary

Innovative, results-oriented Executive with an entrepreneurial sprit and success in strategically planning and executing new business development within the media and technology industries. Proven success in implementing the full product life cycle from idea generation to market execution as evidenced by the creation of TV Guide Mobile and the re-launch of TV Guide Online. Unique combination of business development and financial planning experience leading to successful execution of merger, acquisitions and joint venture opportunities. Self-driven leader with fluency in French and extensive knowledge of international industry trends. Outstanding communicator who effectively collaborates with geographically diverse cross-functional teams, high-level executives and delivers product presentations to large groups.

• Directed six-month process to develop new corporate mission and strategic plan to reposition the more than fifty year old TV Guide brand.
• Successful track record of innovation and strategic thinking as evidenced by the launch of new digital media product line - TV Guide Mobile – and redesign of TV Guide Online.
• Mobile content and applications target both “What’s on TV” and “What’s on Mobile” providing TV listings, TiVo remote recording and guidance to mobile video.
• Negotiated and initiated licensing agreements will all major US mobile carriers.
• Created new Online Video Guide providing browse, search and editorial guidance for Internet video.
• Named inventor of pending mobile and interactive advertising patents.
• Increased 2006 and 2007 online revenue 27% and traffic 40+% each year by spearheading organizational turnaround; grew revenue to over $15 million and traffic to over six million Nielsen uniques.
• Saved $12 million by efficiently managing liquidation process of eBooks division; pursued asset sales, oversaw negotiations with creditors and minimized potential consumer protection liability.
