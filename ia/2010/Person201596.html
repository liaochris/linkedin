Thamir Ghaslan’s Summary

7 years of senior IT consultancy experienced in a wide variety of business applications and network hardware. Always interested in business process improvements and managerial roles.

Aramco's IT assets are gigantic, with over 50,000+ employees, 50,000+ workstations, 1000+ servers, 2000+ network switches. Keeping all these assets/services running at 24/7 with zero downtime is the department goal. Total value of assests is estimated at $ 60,000,000.

Started at the front line where work was routine. On average 4000 calls are received daily distributed over 30 agents. Then Moved into greater responsibilities within the newly established Change, Problem, and Event Management groups due to my high key performance indicators.

The direct impact of change/problem/event management group activities has created a 30% reduction in service downtime. Major outages lasting a day on average have been reduced to an hour max due to better redundancy in links/servers and better coordination and escalation.
