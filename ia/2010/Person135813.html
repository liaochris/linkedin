Marty Weil’s Summary

Marty Weil is an award-winning business journalist, copywriter, and online content producer.

He writes about the use of technology in a variety of industries--specializing in corporate case studies. With deep expertise in enterprise business technology, Marty develops strategic content to build credibility, generate demand, and accelerate the sales cycle. He has written case studies and other materials for companies such as Microsoft, 3M, EMC, Sun, Siemens, to name a few.

He is also a frequent contributor to national business publications, including Managing Automation, Technology & Learning, and Data Center Management. In 2007, Marty received an Eddie Award for excellence in magazine feature writing from Folio Magazine.

Marty is the author of more than 300 magazine features on technology-related topics. He has written hundreds of case studies, advertorials, and white papers for Fortune 1000 companies. There are more than 500,000 words in print under his byline.

Previously, Marty spent 15 years as the President of Weil Marketing Communications, a groundbreaking PR consultancy that contributed to the success of leading technology, manufacturing, and enterprise software companies.
