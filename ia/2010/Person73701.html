Greg Matthews’s Summary

After spending a career helping to build, grow and transform businesses, I'm now focused on using social media to create different kinds of interactions with consumers – with the goal of supporting a social revolution in health.

As Director of Consumer Innovation at Humana, I have two key areas of work. The first is to guide Humana in its quest to be a leader in the coming collaborative economy. The second is to leverage the power of social networks to help people live healthier and more fulfilling lives.

During my tenure at Humana, I have had responsibility for the start-up HR operations for joint venture companies like Green Ribbon Health and Sensei, and in Humana’s first European subsidiary in London. I led the design and launch of the innovation center's first web site, CrumpleItUp.com, and built my industry's first social analysis tool, myTPSreport.com.
