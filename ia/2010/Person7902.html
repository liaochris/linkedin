Allan Tsang’s Summary

As a Strategic Ideation Architect, executives and companies hire me to help them be more competitive. I achieve that by employing one or a combination of the following skill sets:

As an executive coach, I help build more effective and confident leaders.

As a business advisor, I help my clients strategize the best course of action to lead their companies in the direction they want to go and to provide access to resources.

As a talent recruiter, companies hire me to help them find executives, strategist and subject matter experts to help plan and execute their business ideas.

As a marketing consultant, I help companies create dynamic business and marketing plans, as well as vet their existing plans to drive more sales.

As a strategic outsource consultant, I help companies analyze their outsourcing strategy, provide sourcing feasibility studies, locate and coordinate vendors, and arrange for third party audits/inspections.

As an analyst, I have helped companies objectively look at their financial situation, employees, productivity, and profitability.

As an entrepreneur, I have started, grown and sold companies.
