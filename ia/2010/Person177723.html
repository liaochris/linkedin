Roselle Cronan’s Summary

Award-winning writer and editor with more than 15 years of experience with newspapers, as well as online e-learning and classroom training materials. Collaborative team member with project management training and experience including keeping projects on track, learning new ideas and maintaining cross-functional partnerships. Experienced in developing video scripts, newsletters, brochures and other marketing collateral. Strong Word, PowerPoint, Acrobat and Quark X Press skills; familiar with Excel and InDesign, WordPress blogging software, and Facebook and LinkedIn social networking software.

Roselle Cronan’s Specialties:

Academic and business writing and editing (Associated Press, Chicago, APA styles), journalism, marketing collateral (product sheets, white papers, newsletters), media relations/press releases, "ghost" blogging, strategic marketing plans, innovation/knowledge management assessments
