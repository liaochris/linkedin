Julie Kantor’s Summary

Julie Silard Kantor is a veteran in the field of youth entrepreneurship education and a recent author of I SAID YES!.

Book I Authored- I SAID YES! Real Life Stories of Teachers, Students and Leaders Saying YES! to Entrepreneurship in America's Schools (amazon.com) http//www.amazon.com/Stories-Students-Teachers-Entrepreneurship-Americas/dp/0976582368/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1255706157&sr=8-1

As National Vice President, Kantor is focused on the expansion of entrepreneurship education in America’s schools especially the Greater Washington Region and opportunity to scale NFTE’s proven ‘Mini-MBA’ programs in partnership with federal and state government.

In collaboration with the Aspen Institute, Kantor lead a dynamic policy initiative: the Youth Entrepreneurship Strategy Group (YES Group) www.aspeninstitute.org/yesg

In her previous position as the Regional Director of NFTE New England, Ms. Kantor served the most economically challenged neighborhoods. She established and personally taught NFTE classes at numerous schools and youth programs. Over 22,000 local youth from have graduated from NFTE’s ‘mini-MBA’ Program model under Kantor’s leadership and over 280,000 youth to date globally.

Ms. Kantor received the NFTE Leadership Award in 2005 and 2007 for the top operating unit in the country, was NFTE Executive of the Year in 1999, Washingtonian Magazine’s 100 people to watch under 40 . Kantor has been catalytic in expanding NFTE programs in India, Baltimore, Philadelphia, and Boston.

Born and raised in Washington, DC, Ms. Kantor started working in small business at the age of eleven and was heavily influenced by her father who escaped to America in 1956 for opportunity. She attended Sidwell Friends School and earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Communications and English from Simmons College in Boston. Ms. Kantor lives in Washington, DC with her husband, daughter and two dogs.
