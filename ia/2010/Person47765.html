Danny Ruppert’s Summary

My travels have taken me to work in 17 states through out my career. Beginning in cable and satellite communications, telecommunications, electrical and most recently renewable energy. Supply green electrical devices for consumers and businesses. Improving lighting without hiring an electrician or buying new fixtures. Reduce inductive motor loads, thus improving power factor and improving electrical efficency. Lower HID lighting energy with control devices that cut electrical consumption by an adverage of 35%.

Danny Ruppert’s Specialties:

Replace old-style inefficient lighting with new electronic energy saving retrofits using lamps that improve see ability more than 20% over the competition. Energy saving devices are installed that capture and recycle lost energy from inductive loads and provide surge protection and cost savings for whole buildings.
