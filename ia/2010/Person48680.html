David Pincus’s Summary

David A. Pincus is a partner in the law firm of Schoenfeld, Schoenfeld & Pincus, PC, a personal injury law firm. David has focused on personal injury law for over 15 years. Before that, David’s practice included a variety of general practice areas including estate matters, bankruptcy, divorce, criminal and real estate. David is a graduate of Brooklyn Law School (1982) and State University of New York at Stony Brook (1979).

In 1983, David was admitted to practice law in the State of New York and in the United States District Court for the Eastern and Southern Districts of New York. He is a member of the New York State Trial Lawyers Association and the Suffolk County Bar Association.

David has taken cases through Suffolk County Pro Bono Project, a joint project of Nassau/Suffolk Law Services Committee, Inc. and the Suffolk County Bar Association.

David Pincus has been quoted in Newsday on legal matters. He has been on the Executive Board of the Long Island chapter of the NJDC. David loves the water and is always on or off the docks; he continues to live on Long Island with his wife and 3 children.
