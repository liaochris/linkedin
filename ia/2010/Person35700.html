Channy Yun’s Summary

Seokchan (Channy) Yun had many experiences of web technology and business especially web 2.0, web standards, open source, e-commerce and digital contents from early web industry in Korea

In his first company, Nine4u Inc, he developed Nine4u.com, the first Korean online music broadcasting service in 1998 and Asiamusic.net, the largest music database from 1997 to 2004. (Both was ended in Dec.2005). It was connected to e-commerce of CD retails and engaged to develop various online CD shopping mall.

After his enginnering career, he made own technology based business models as a CTO in Nine4u from 2000. He had opportunity to work with VeriSign and Thawte as a reselling partner and online payment system of Mypay.net.

In Daum in early 2004, he involved to make technical strategy as a staff of corporate R&D center and designed educational programs for over 300 software engineers from 2004 to 2005.

His job forwarded to technical evangelism of Daum web APIs and encourages Web 2.0 mash-up applications in Daum Developers Network and Associates (http://dna.daum.net) from 2006 to 2008.

In 2009, he back to the school to study social semantic web in Ph.D course of Seoul National University.

He also has engaged "Mozilla" project since 2002 as a korean l10n module owner to make Korean version of Mozilla Firefox and Thunderbird and a leader of Korean community (http://www.mozilla.or.kr).

His second interest is web standards to improve web accessibility in Korea leading community (http://webstandards.or.kr). He is also WaSP ILG member and W3C HTML W/G invited expert.

His social activities has been active in Korean web technical community i.e. founding BarCampSeoul and FutureWeb Forum and Webappscon.

His Channy's Blog (http://channy.creation.net) has over 20,000 RSS readers with his insightful articles, especially he has written for ZDNet Korea about various technical subjects on the web since 2004.
