Jakob Steen Hansen’s Summary

With 17 years in the IT industry, I've had opportunity to deal with many challenging jobs and working though many changes. While having software creation "in my DNA", I'm super passionate about leading teams of innovative and technically strong people, creating breakthrough software to enable individuals to pursue their goals and make their business succesful. It's my strong belief that longterm success comes from being able to perform excellently today while striving for future break-throughs.

I'm repeatedly rewarded by the great experience of working with people to develop them, their careers and their businesses and help them ensure success of their life and projects.

I'm developing my insight, skills and practical experience in the area of technology, software architecture and development, engineering, leadership, strategy and running a business through broad involvement, on-the-job experience and studies/training.

Currently I'm working on the final stages of the next version of our product, Dynamics AX, ensuring a rich developer experience and powerful business intelligence, incorporating a number of new technologies while managing the impact on existing customers and developers. That is great fun and a big professional challenge. And there's no doubt that "cool continues" with this version of AX...
