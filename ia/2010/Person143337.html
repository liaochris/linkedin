Michael Johnson’s Summary

I'm currently Global Vice President of Sales for Avature. Joining the organization as one of the earliest employees, I've helped guide Avature to a leadership position in the emerging use of Web/Enterprise 2.0 software in the HCM marketplace. Prior to Avature, I spent six years at HotJobs.com/Yahoo!. While at HotJobs.com, I began my career as an Account Executive and one of the first 15 employees in the company in 1997. I was quickly promoted to Regional Sales Manager after the company went public in 1999 where I opened the Chicago office and added 30+ sales people and $30M+ in revenue. Following the sale of the company to Yahoo! in 2002 I was promoted to run all Strategic Account Sales for the western half of the US with offices in Chicago, Austin, LA, and San Francisco with a $50M revenue target.

I am a frequent guest speaker at industry trade events; most recently at the Australasian Talent Conference in Sydney, Australia and as a panel speaker at the Software Association of Oregon's "The Impact of Social Media on Talent Acquisition" event.
