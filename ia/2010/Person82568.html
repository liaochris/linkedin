Holly MacDonald’s Summary

Human Resources Strategist - over 15 years of demonstrated success in developing learning, talent management, employer brand strategies, coupled with solid project management techniques, to craft workable, scalable, business oriented solutions.

I don't sell packaged solutions or products. I am not peddling a magic bullet or piece of technology. I partner with organizations and identify the people strategy that will have the most impact on their bottom line and their brand.

I have a knack for translating strategy into action and am known for creating order out of chaos. I see the forest AND the trees.

Holly MacDonald’s Specialties:

Crafting 21st century learning strategies, creating performance support systems and partnering with organizations to develop their talent.

Writing persuasive business cases which identify return on investment for people strategies.
