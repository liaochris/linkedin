Rick Racela’s Summary

In August 2010 I joined the Comcast SportsNet team in Philadelphia. I am now tasked with helping to develop social media strategies for all of the regional sports networks, identifying new technologies and vendors, as well as formulating best practices to maximize ROI on any new or current technologies.

Prior to coming on board at Comcast, I helped change the culture of a free weekly newspaper into a 24/7 multimedia news operation, now dominating the market with breaking news, video, and mobile text alerts in my home town in Cape May County, New Jersey.

The website, www.capemaycountyherald.com, has won various national awards. Not only did I have a hand in building the application, I spearheaded a growth of an audience of 12k unique monthly visitors to 120k over the course of four years as well. I have evolved my role from Webmaster to an online producer to eventually taking over management of social media and audience development.

Simultaneous to that, I was involved in a start-up venture called the Digital Press Consortium and helped convert our online application development into a product and deployed it nationwide. I was involved with product management, installation, training, Tier I & II support as well as testing and bug identification. These products included a publishing content management system, a marketplace business directory and various other 3rd party vendor tools.

I enjoy engaging online audiences, managing and streamlining internal workflows and educating everyone, from top to bottom, inside and out. I aspire to enhance my skills as a web developer.

Many hats are on my rack. I take great pride in shaping the way my neighbors receive their local online content.
