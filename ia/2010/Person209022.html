Judy Jenner’s Summary

Twelve years' experience in German<->Spanish, English<->Spanish and English<->German translation and interpretation for businesses of all sizes, including Fortune 100 companies like Sony and IBM and smaller, regional enterprises in both Europe and the United States. Extensive copywriting experience in three languages for clients from diverse industries, including travel and tourism, fine arts, high-tech, engineering, cosmetics, general marketing, etc. We are not a translation agency, but a small, women-owned business that takes pride in providing high-quality work at adequate prices. Our clients like to think of us as the Porsche of translations.

Judy Jenner’s Specialties:

Legal texts (international contracts), hospitality (online and offline), multilingual PR texts, marketing texts, including campaigns, taglines, and online ads, technical texts, business plans, financial (annual reports, analyses), engineering, multilingual copywriting, translation, and localization. Spanish and German consecutive interpretation services available for community settings, business meetings (escort interpreting), and the health care field.
