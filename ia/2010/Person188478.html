Sheila Scarborough’s Summary

I'm a full-time freelance writer and blogger specializing in travel, tourism, the social Web and NHRA drag racing.

A certified Navy Master Training Specialist, I'm also a former Associate Professor of Naval Science at the University of Florida.

Co-founder of Tourism Currents, where we teach tourism professionals how to use social media tools for destination marketing.

Sheila Scarborough’s Specialties:

I write and blog about general travel, usually with a cultural twist, and family travel. My motorsports expertise is in NHRA drag racing because there is one winner, one loser and no whining. I show individuals and companies (particularly travel and tourism organizations) how to navigate social media and use the Web to enhance their professional and personal lives.
