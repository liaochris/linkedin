Keith Springer’s Summary

I am the founder and president of Capital Financial Advisory Services in Sacramento, CA. We have been providing professional financial advice for more than 25 years, specializing in wealth management solutions for even the most complex individual and institutional portfolios worldwide. I have developed a proprietary process known as Top-Down Tactical™ for building tax-efficient and retirement portfolios for high net worth individuals.

My career began with Merrill Lynch in my hometown of Boston, Massachusetts, where I completed my financial consultant training in 1985. In 1990, I moved to California to study advanced portfolio management and investment management studies. By 1996, I felt my clients’ interests would be best served by going independent, so I became a Registered Investment Advisor. This ultimately led to the formation of Capital Financial Advisory Services, where I continues to serve as president today.

In conjunction with my career as an advisor, I have dedicated myself to serving the community. It gives me great pleasure to help the dreams of others come true. Thus, in 1999 I organized the Springer Turkey Challenge to raise money to feed local hungry families. Through the generosity of my friends and clients, each holiday season we raise funds, then I match their donations. Collectively, we give away thousands of turkeys and other complementary items to those who would otherwise go without. I looks forward to doing more in the years to come.

I am also a member of The Society for Financial Awareness (SOFA), where I donate my time delivering free seminars and workshops for companies, civic organizations, church groups, etc. My goal is to help raise financial awareness and assist people in taking charge of their financial future.

I am a graduate of Clark University in Worcester, MA, with additional studies at Kings College, University of London.
