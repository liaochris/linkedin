Olivier Jouve’s Summary

Olivier Jouve is Vice President, Corporate Development, at SPSS Inc. His role consists in defining SPSS strategic directions, growth opportunities (through internal development, merger and acquisitions and/or tactical alliances) and go-to-market strategies.
As a pioneer in the field of data and text mining for the last 20 years, he has accomplished much including inventing the foundation of SPSS’ Text Analytics technology for analyzing customer interactions.

SPSS has sold, to date, this new technology (embedded in Text Analytics for Modeler and SPSS Text Analytics for Surveys) to most of the top 500 Fortune companies.

Jouve also managed Product Marketing for Data Mining and Text Mining at SPSS Inc.


Previously, Jouve was the COO of LexiQuest (after having managed the European Operations, including Sales and Marketing), a worldwide leading Knowledge Management company that he sold to SPSS in 2002.

Jouve is a successful serial entrepreneur. He founded Instoria (sold to LexiQuest in 2000), a start-up dedicated to knowledge management, and co-founded with Press Index (www.pressindex.fr) in 2000, Portalys; a start-up dedicated to automatic Web press monitoring (sold to PressIndex in 2007). He recently co-founded Voozici.com, a social networking site to review small and local businesses.
Jouve was also Associate Director at www.webcarcenter.com, sold to AutoReflex in Sept 07.
Prior to that (1989 – 1999) Jouve was VP of Engineering of C-S, a leading European system integrator (4000 employees) where he paticipated to the development of the Internet Services offering.
He has also acted as a consultant for companies including France Telecom, AOL, French Army just to name a few.
He has held faculty positions as an Associate Professor in Computer Sciences (1995 –2000) at Leonardo Da Vinci University in Paris.
Olivier Jouve had his works published internationally in the area of Analytical CRM, text analytics and data mining.
