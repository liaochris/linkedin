    VISTAGE (formerly TEC, est. 1957) – is a “by invitation only” organization for CEOs and business leaders. The core component of the Vistage experience is the exclusive (16-seat) “think tank” environment for CEOs and business leaders to share unbiased, agenda-free advice. Vistage members meet once a month for a full day executive group meeting that entails a candid give-and-take, where trusted peers ask each other tough, in-depth questions that get to the heart of any business issue. As part of these meetings, members receive direct and honest feedback from their peers, and derive actionable ideas they can implement immediately into their business. Group discussions are led by a professional executive facilitator, the Vistage Chair™.
    Since its founding, over 80,000 Vistage members have outperformed the competition and on average realize 3X revenue growth rates within 3 years of joining. Vistage offers its members:
    - safe, trusting peer group meetings
    - ongoing professional and personal development
    - sharing of best practices and best current thinking on business issues
    - confidential help for sensitive situations
    - 1-to-1 executive coaching
    - subject matter expert workshops
    - “member only” conferences
    - Vistage Gateway™ online best practice databases
    - a global network of >14,500 CEOs in 16 countries
    Vistage-member companies generate nearly $300 billion in annual revenue and represent more than 1.8 million employees worldwide.
