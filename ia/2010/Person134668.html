Mark Susman’s Summary

Accomplished producer, director of photography and technical director for video programs and television documentaries. I received my Bachelor of Arts degree in Television and Film from Trinity University in 1982. Areas of expertise expertise in broadcast and corporate communications including brand promos, employee communications, occupational safety training, customer relations, crisis communications, OSHA compliance. Credits include several award winning PBS documentaries.

In 1984 I founded VideoFiles Media Group, Inc., a communications consulting company specializing in video documentaries, motion graphics, still photography and video editing. VFMG provides creative services and editorial expertise in every area of the production process. VFMG has built a solid reputation working on behalf of clients both large and small in all industries, from Fortune 500 companies to the non-profit sector.

In 1996, we diversified our services to take advantage of new digital video technologies and founded a second company -- Fast Cut Films -- to support this new market. Fast Cut has become a leader in the field having produced many notable projects for corporations and charitable organizations, as well as television documentaries and music videos. We also have heavy experience in the non-profit sector, having worked with several universities, including Rice University and Prairie View A&M University, and non-profits such as the National Multiple Sclerosis Society (including MS150 Bike Race), Cystic Fibrosis, Muscular Dystrophy and Texas Foundation for the Arts. Corporate clients include Air Liquide, Cooper Industries, Diamond Offshore, HP, American General (AIG), and VALIC.

Sample reels available on request and through our Vimeo channel.
