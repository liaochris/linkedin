Nancy Hoppe’s Summary

Pharmaceutical industry expert in Congress and Convention Management, Marketing, and Advertising tactics with experience in the following therapeutic areas: oncology, hematology, vaccines, infectious disease, transplantation, gastrointestinal, pain management and consumer products.

Received Wyeth President's Achievement Award in 2009.

Member and current President of HCEA (Healthcare Convention and Exhibitors Association), and member of IPCAA (International Pharmaceutical Congress Advisory Association).

First member of HCEA to obtain CME/H (Certified Manager of Exhibits/Healthcare) in 2006.

Certificate of Excellence awarded in 2004 for Business Process Excellence (Six Sigma) project for successful consolidation of Global Congress and U.S. Conventions.
