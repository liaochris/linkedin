Samantha McGarry’s Summary

At Gomez, I manage day-to-day strategy and execution of our corporate communications, media and analyst relations programs in North America. In this role, I've created substantial and consistent media coverage for Gomez in both business and trade media. Working with the analyst community, I've helped Gomez moved from its reputation as an interesting company to being acclaimed the market leader in web experience management. I am building Gomez's social media strategy from the ground up and have already attracted over 1000 Twitter followers. I am thrilled and motivated by how social media enhances traditional PR methods.
I have almost two decades of US and international B2B PR experience, with a focus on technology. Over the years, I have represented and delivered outstanding results for public companies like HP (multiple lines of business), Cisco, Analog Devices, Iomega, Keane and MatrixOne as well as many private companies like SunGard Availability Services, Nervewire, Platform Computing and Bluestreak.
