Anthony LaGrasta’s Summary

I'm a highly energetic and entrepreneurial professional that knows how to implement strategy and drive creative. I understand the use of combining internal resources as well as outside vendors creating a winning dynamic to meet marketing objectives. I have earned a reputation as a conceptual thinker, the big-idea person. I enjoy attacking a project from new directions to ensure a lasting effect on the intended target market. I get a sense of pride from successfully relying on creativity to work within tight budgets or timelines. I’ve found that projects that at first glance seem restrictive or even impossible can, with careful planning, turn out to be rewarding both in profit and morale.

My years spent at agencies and in-house focused on new business, health care, finance and high technology while my years in publishing encompassed automotive, aeronautical and the international shipping community.

I’m currently in the market for a full-time position that will make the most of my hard-earned skills in advertising, marketing and publishing. Please be in touch for an interview!
