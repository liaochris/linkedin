Isaac Udotong’s Experience

  • Founder/CEO

    Isatong Limited, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1988 — Present (22 years 4 months)

    Isatong began its business in Nigeria in 1988 as a company devoting to Technology Consulting and Information Systems distribution. In the same year, Isatong was appointed as the sole distributor of Motorola Computers and Mannesmann Tally Printers in Nigeria and other West African Countries and successfully introduced these products to Nigeria. In 1995, Isatong started IT consulting services in the United States and export technology to Africa.

    In the past, Isatong has provided products and services in the fields of Information Infrastructure Transformation, Storage Networking, Engineering Services and Facility Maintenance. Through offering a comprehensive portfolio of information infrastructure, Isatong helps various companies and organizations to process and manage information and process management. Isatong designs build and operate IT Infrastructures for banks, oil companies and governments in Africa.

    Following the recent acquisition of certain US assets of TallyGenicom, Printronix has appointed Isatong to be distributor of TG printing products in West Africa.

    Currently, Isatong is in a growth and expansion mode to become a medium-size global company through acquisition and reinvestment in business growth. The expansion focus is driven by the need to grow it’s IT product distributorship, Establishment of several Document Management Bureau in Africa and America and storage infrastructure to support, Participation in Infrastructure (Power, Renewable Energy, Transportation, and General Maintenance) enhancement projects in Africa and North America.
