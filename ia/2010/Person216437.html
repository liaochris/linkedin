Vikram Malik’s Summary

Currently, I am the South Asia Head of A2 System, which is a South American Mobile Applications market. As part of my mandate, I am looking to partner with mobile network companies across the region, to incorporate the wide variety of mobile applications that A2 has on offer, in to the network of these companies.

I am also very majorly into English language research and content development, primarily for the Internet. This essentially involves writing articles and other forms of written pieces which are published on the Internet, for a variety of reasons, primary among which is to draw traffic, to the particular site in question. For this, I am constantly on the look-out for good English language writers, from any part of the world, as the volume of work is way beyond my personal capacity!

Translation also forms a major part of the work that I do. Currently, I am doing a huge Bengali to English translation project; I have also done translation assignments to and from English to various other Indian and foreign languages.

Prior to my current mandate, I held research, advisory and consultative positions in reputed research and consulting firms such as Datamonitor Ventures (DMV), Outsource Partners International (OPI), and Evalueserve.

I have also been in the Media Planning industry, doing the same for Wipro Consumer Care.

After my Master’s, I started my career with NIIT Knowledge Solutions which is their E-Learning division and then went on to the Public Relations industry, working with South Asia'a No. 1 Image Management company, Perfect Relations.

During and before my Master's, I had the pleasure and privilege of working with reputed companies such as Integral PR, Warner Brothers and Signature Inc. in diverse capacities.
