Leonard Scales’s Summary

// About Leonard //
Leonard Scales is a software solutions sales professional, social media marketing and social commerce strategist with more than 17 years of hands-on complex sales and brand sell-through experience, with 8 of those years focused on the Internet. Leonard has worked as Dir./VP of Sales for fast paced software startups and corporations up to $400mm in sales.

// How he got there //
Leonard has worked in sales for top rated sales organizations like Bristol Myers, Hewlett-Packard, and JDA. His career includes creating and executing sales strategies designed to produce predictable and repeatable results.

// What Leonard does //
Leonard also works with brands, both large and small, and advertising/PR agencies, to leverage the rapidly changing opportunities presented by the social web and social media. Helps brands new to social media, make sound initial decisions, and for veterans, expand their program's reach & returns.

// Last 8 years //
For the past eight years, Leonard has worked with Silicon Valley SaaS startups. He has delivered sales for some of the most promising startups in the Valley. Sales have targeted retail, ePhilanthropy, social media marketing, social commerce, financial services, manufacturing, entertainment, financial services, CPG, partner relationship management...

// Current Activities //
He is also a sales process and social media marketing blogger, leonardscales.wordpress/com. His Sales Reminder blog is intended for sales pros, who on occasion, need a sales process refresher, and for brands interested in social commerce. He is currently working with a stealth startup that will soon introduce a very exciting consumer oriented experience!

He is a strong proponent of solid sales process, tactics and strategies and advocates several Sales 2.0 techniques to support pipeline development & selling.
