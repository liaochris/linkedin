Troy Angrignon’s Summary

Troy Angrignon is a socially and environmentally aware entrepreneur, advisor, and consultant who believes that business and technology can be used to address humanity's largest challenges.

He is looking for an opportunity to join and lead an early stage cleantech company somewhere in the energy, carbon, efficiency, or materials markets, based somewhere in the west coast of North America, but probably in BC or California.

Currently he is a consultant and advisor to emerging technology companies and consultancies focussed on cleantech and Saas related ventures. He is also co-chairing a conference on cloud computing in Vancouver, BC in spring 2010. His functional strengths are in business planning, team-building, sales, strategic marketing (joint ventures, partnering, channel development).

His writings, partner deals and ecosystem map work done in concert with RightScale and Appirio have garnered press in Sandhill, eWeek, Information Week, Info World, VentureBeat, ReadWriteWeb, Deal Architect, and many other technology industry web-sites.

In 2009, he was guest co-chair of the “Under the Radar: Clarity in the Cloud” conference where he helped vet over 350 companies to find the top 24 new companies in the cloud computing space.

Prior to consulting in the cloud computing space, Troy was Part-owner, President, and VP Sales & Marketing for a multi-million dollar startup based in Sacramento CA that specialized in building private label social networks for startups, media companies, and Fortune 1000 enterprises.

Before building the startup, he was the Emerging Technology Strategist for Business Objects, where he did market analysis for the company's first Saas offering as well as wrote the company's draft web 2.0 / enterprise 2.0 strategy. Before that, he was an Enterprise Sales Account Manager who achieved greater than 300% of quota.

Prior to joining Business Objects, he owned a management consulting / system integration firm for ten years

Domains: Sustainability, cloud computing (all layers), Saas, Enterprise Software, IT
Currently studying energy (traditional and clean), carbon markets, and efficiency related industries.
