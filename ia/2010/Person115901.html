Kevin Remde
IT Pro Evangelist - Microsoft Corporation

Kevin Remde is an IT Pro Evangelist with Microsoft. A prolific blogger, Kevin shares his thoughts, ideas, and tips on his "Full of I.T." blog (http://blogs.technet.com/kevinremde). He is also a regular contributor and interviewer for TechNet Radio podcasts (http://www.microsoft.com/tnradio). He's a strong speaker and webcaster who's landed several times on Microsoft's top 10 webcast list and has delivered at least six top-scoring TechNet webcasts to date. In his past outside of Microsoft, Kevin has held positions such as software engineer, information systems professional, and information systems manager. He loves sharing helpful new solutions and technologies with his IT peers.

Kevin Remde’s Specialties:

Public Speaking, Communications, Information Technology, Microsoft Server and Security Products and Services
