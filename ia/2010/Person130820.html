Manny Berrios’s Summary

Mr. Berrios has 17 years of extensive technology leadership and entrepreneurial experience as Chief Technology Officer of several Internet Media and technology services companies.

In 2005, Mr. Berrios was founder and CTO of in2apps.com a Software as a Service (SaaS) platform providing a new delivery model for online publishers to immediately and effortlessly deploy, at no cost, powerful enterprise level Distributed Rich Internet Applications for social networking, online classifieds e-commerce and self service advertising. There by facilitating greater community interaction by actively engaging larger audiences of like-minded users, to increase content, community and commerce. In 2001, Mr. Berrios founded and served as CTO of inTRUST Technologies, a full service IT solutions provider, where he concentrated his team’s efforts on computer security, application development, e-commerce development and building network infrastructures for a variety of different industries. Prior to inTRUST, Mr. Berrios served as CTO for UGO Networks Inc. In this capacity Mr. Berrios built UGO Networks technology infrastructure, deployed and managed the data centers of UGO Technology Solutions, a service provider for hosting and managed services. In addition, Mr. Berrios developed UGO's advertising network and UGO's e-commerce initiatives. Prior to joining UGO Networks, Mr. Berrios held the CTO positions of several online gaming startups; ActionWorld, Playlink.com and Gamedealer.com. Mr. Berrios worked at ICON CMT, as a Senior Security Engineer on the Advanced Implementation Team, as part of which he consulted several Fortune 500 companies and designed, built and managed their business technology infrastructures.
