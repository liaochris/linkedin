Keith Winter’s Summary

President & CEO of EmSense Corporation

EmSense has created the first scalable physiological and brainwave measurement technology providing accurate, objective, moment-by-moment analysis of how a large audience responds emotionally and cognitively to media messaging. To date, this type of technology has been confined to a laboratory environment.

Based on rigorous EEG neuroscience applied to thousands of participants, our non-invasive tracking of involuntary brain, heart, and other body responses combined with respondent generated diagnostics provides clients with a compete, non-biased assessment of media and message content.

Currently EmSense is actively working with various clients in the video game and advertising industries. Our technology can also be applied to political messaging, television and movie content development, consumer product development and other market segments.
