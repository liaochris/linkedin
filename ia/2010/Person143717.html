Michael Garcia’s Summary

My expertise is working with clients, regardless of industry, who are looking for top talent for key management roles.

I like to work with small businesses or companies which manage themselves like small businesses. I can work directly with decision makers and can support clients by finding talent for all levels of the organization and across all functions.

I have a track record of success working for major service companies and well-known brands. In a range of management, marketing, and sales positions, I have experience in B2C and B2B markets, in client and agency environments, in domestic and international geographies, and in general and direct marketing.
