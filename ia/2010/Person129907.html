Magdalena Bogart’s Summary

Experienced Interior Designer of high-end residential interiors with European flair, clean lines, timeless elegance.

I have great passion for good design. I am very creative in finding and implementing stylish solutions to your space problems. My work tools are AutoCAD, Sketch Up and other software allowing me to be most organized and efficient.

My four-year business degree from Germany and many years of working in the corporate world trained me well in managing your project and staying within set time and budget schedules.

I travel the world to find the right pieces, fine furnishings, art and antiques for you. (Europe, US and Indonesia).

Please visit my website for more pictures and information:
www.magdalenabogartinteriors.com

Magdalena Bogart’s Specialties:

My signature style includes European timeless elegance, highest quality, best functionality and easy maintenance for your home.

My specialty is working with busy "Silicon Valley" people, whose time schedules are tight. They appreciate working with me because I work efficiently, they can always connect with me via email and phone, I strive for excellence, I am proactive and absolutely reliable and accountable.
