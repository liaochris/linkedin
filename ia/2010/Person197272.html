Summer (Terich) Widmer’s Summary

Summer Widmer, a 2001 graduate of the University of Portland has proven herself as a top notch performer in high caliber sales and creative business strategist in entrepreneurial settings. Entrepreneurial markets such as a high end contemporary clothing brand and state of the art medical device company. Summer's strong creative skills are equally balanced with her ability to promote and present well. Her highly infectious energy and attitude has attracted many for speaking engagements in the community on topics regarding Leadership, Sustainability, and Service.
Summer is a loyal and dedicated individual who works to drive success in all of her professional endeavors. Her extensive networking and relationship-building skills enable her to quickly build solid partnerships with her client base. Described as witty, extremely creative and tenacious. Out of college she designed a high performance crutch, the initial product offering for Keen Mobility and holding a patent in that design to positioning a startup clothing company in two of the top ten stores in the country within the first year of business.
Known for her highly motivating and passionate attitude in the workforce and the community she brings everyone in to be focused on a common goal for the benefit of all. Genuine interest in helping people Summer sits actively on two boards: University of Portland Board of Regents as the youngest regents and of only 10 women board of 55; Saint Child and actively involved with Self Enhancement Inc (SEI) and United Way Young Leaders. Most recently nominated University of Portland Contemporary Alumni Award and for Portland's 40 under 40 awards. Award winners are recognized as the 40 most accomplished influential and civic-minded young executives in Portland.
