Luis Cabrera’s Summary

Luis brings exceptional competencies to any company, most notably a deep expertise in marketing and a wide-ranging vision of digital technologies coupled with a strong business sense.

Most recently, Luis cofounded Leapfactor, a startup company that is revolutionizing the way people work on-the-go by providing mobile apps that can be connected to virtually any backend system, thus enabling workers to access their systems as if they were in front of their PC.

Before Leapfactor, Luis headed the Latin3 offices in Mexico City, Latin3 is a leading digital marketing agency in Latin America and the U.S. Hispanic arena. Luis successfully grew businesses in key clients like Nextel and Banamex.

Previously, he worked in Washington DC at New Media Strategies (NMS), a company acquired by Meredith Corporation (NYSE: MDP) as the architect and leader of the Hispanic practice. NMS is one of the first online media firms of the Web 2.0 era, a pioneer in online intelligence, word of mouth marketing and digital PR.

Earlier, Luis worked as Managing Director of Bravo Uno-a-Uno, the experiential/digital marketing business unit at The Bravo Group, WPP’s US Hispanic advertising agency (NYSE:WPP). At Bravo, he led the creative and strategic operations of his unit and worked closely with other non-Hispanic agencies to ensure brand consistency and maximize efficiencies.

Luis’s background consists in a formal education in media and communications, and by a solid professional development that includes being a strategist for Neoris, a global consultancy where he designed programs to leverage digital platforms to deliver operational efficiencies and elevate marketing program results.

Having lead international award winning projects, his exposure to a wide range of industries and having worked in different Latin American countries, gives Luis a unique perspective to approach business challenges and great insights and multicultural management skills.

Luis Cabrera’s Specialties:

Digital Marketing, Mobile Solutions, Interactive Marketing, US Hispanic Market, Hispanic Advertising, LATAM, Web Development, Communication, Knowledge Management, Product Management, Business Consulting, Fortune 500 experience, startup, new business development.
