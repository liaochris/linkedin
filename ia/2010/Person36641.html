Vivian Chen’s Summary

Vivian is a dedicated Financial/Asset Manager at Big Ocean Management Inc., a private investment firm based in Los Angeles. Her diverse background in business includes: business communications consulting, strategic business development, organizational change management, real estate private equity analysis, hotel investment analysis, hotel real estate asset management, hotel development, and hotel renovation.

Having come from a transpacific business family with international background, she has decided to combine her fascination of architecture with her passion for business to become a rising entrepreneur and commercial real estate investor/developer. She has analyzed a portfolio over 190 gas station investment properties throughout the U.S., contributed in acquisition due-diligence for a Mixed Use/Planned Unit Development project on 45 acres of land, and strategically advised on hotel franchise agreements and investment projects.

Prior to joining Big Ocean Management, Vivian held positions as an Acquisitions Analyst at Brentwood Capital Partners, a real estate private equity firm; and Business Communications Consultant for the President at Fukuda USA, Inc., a Leak Test Specialist division of Tokyo based Nagano Keiki Co., Ltd. In addition to her current position, she has started two business ventures of her own in hopes to become a successful entrepreneur and commercial real estate investor. Her commitment to business and real estate is shown through her education, experience, and career ambitions.
