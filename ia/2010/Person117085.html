  • Board Chair

    Delaware Humanities Forum

    (Museums and Institutions industry)

    April 2004 — April 2009 (5 years 1 month)

    The Delaware Humanities Forum is partially funded by the National Endowment for the Humanities.

    DHF is designed to bring "the thinking arts" to the people of Delaware, offering high-quality programs to clubs, schools, libraries and other non-profits. DHF is best-known for our Grants Program (est. 1973), which offers small grants and technical assistance to Delaware non-profits working on community-based public programming.

    Our Speakers Bureau (est. 1978), presentations include talks by local scholars and experts on a variety of topics, including Delaware history and folklore; social justice and world history topics such as the Holocaust, ethics in health care, and violence in American society. Our Visiting Scholars Program (est. 1986) provides free, high-calibre lectures to thousands of Delaware elementary and secondary school children, grades 1-12.

    Please visit our website at www.dhf.org.
