Rajeev Kumar’s Summary

Rajeev Kumar is an Entrepreneur, Advanced Technology & Green Technology Evangelist and Investment Banker by profession, a Social Entrepreneur, an Urban Ecologist, a Mentor and a Speaker by passion.

Rajeev along with close friends are launching the Prithvi Sustainability & Technology Fund - for Indian Ventures from Sustainability, CleanTech & GreenEnergy Sectors.

A Serial Entrepreneur, he is a Cofounder and board member of a few Companies in South India. Rajeev Kumar has been involved with several exciting ventures from diverse sectors - including IT, BioTech, Entertainment, Hospitality & Tourism, Food, Education and Social Sectors (as a Mentor, investor or co-Promoter).

A Postgraduate in IT, Business Management, Green Technology and Foreign Trade, he has about 16 years of expertise in leading different technology domains and have advised several reputed companies in South India in important verticals sectors such as FMCG, Manufacturing, Retail, Advertising, IT, Pharmaceuticals and Biotechnology, Government, Enterprise Management, Infrastructure and Education.

He is more known for his social initiatives - Project Agastya, The Sixth International Ecocity Conference (Ecocity6), the Cleaner Production initiative & the Rajeev Kumar Foundation, and is currently along with his friends launching the India Innovation Network with the objective to bring together all Stakeholders of Innovation in India together on to a single platform and work together to promote, incubate & mentor Innovation, and develop better policies, infrastructure and environment for Innovation & Entrepreneurship in India.

Rajeev is a recipient of several awards & recognitions and was a Bangalore Finalist at the "Lead India" by 'Times of India' - Bangalore, India (the world's largest English Newspaper) - an initiative to identify Young Leaders (apolitical) to lead India into her future (September 2007).
