Bruce Benson’s Summary

Bruce Benson’s Specialties:

On time and high quality delivery of software and software intensive systems. One secret to improving quality is to deliver a project on time, especially if you have a history of delivering late. Just getting to on time can result in a huge bump to quality. Getting to on time is centered on measuring your past performance with brutal honesty. Once you get to on time with quality you may come to realize that speed-to-market is not the only way to grow a business.
