Susan B. Young, FBCI, CBCP’s Summary

Experienced risk management professional with 20+ years global business continuity and technology disaster recovery PMO and program development experience in various industries: insurance, banking/brokerage, pharmaceutical, and research. Flexible in any business environment and sector. Developed business continuuity plan which was implemented during 911.

Good understanding of business practices and risk climates in the Americas, Europe, Asia Pac/South Asia, Middle East/ Mediterranean/ Africa.

Skilled in root problem analysis and in defining concise business requirements by developing and implementing simple, yet strategic, cost-effective resiliency solutions to bring value to clients. Have a track record of having the vision for emerging trends, coming up with innovative ways to solve business problems and thrive in totally new roles.
