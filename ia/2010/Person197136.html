Sujoy Kumar Chowdhury, PMP’s Summary

I am an HCI and Human Factors Masters student from a Computer Science background. I have 4+ years of industry experience across 4 multinational telecom operators in developing innovative products and services. My multi-disciplinary skill-set is complemented by credentials like Certified Usability Analyst (CUA) and Project Management Professional (PMP). This repertoire can add value to any UX team in blending product development visions with real user needs.

My career in telecom industry started when I was a third year student of engineering. I teamed up with three other classmates to create ‘3SM Systems’. Before graduation, I steered this startup to successful product launches in two telecom operators (and almost made the third). Our flagship product Bangla SMS (a local language SMS system) earned nation-wide recognition and bridged a substantial gap between industry and academia. After graduation, I discovered myself working for a fourth mobile operator, designing a wider range of services, and creating more impact over millions of customers.

My telecom experience taught me some lessons about innovation. When innovation doesn't solve the right problem, it may perish unnoticed. As a volatile differentiator, innovation has an edge only until it is copied. A product has to be simultaneously functional, usable and tuned to users' needs before it can differentiate itself from competition through its unique user experience. The winning products or services that we see around us are of this kind.

This revelation encouraged me to learn more about the ‘H’ (Human) aspect of HCI through graduate studies on Cognitive Psychology, Human Factors and Usability Testing. This academic specialization is currently grooming me with a well-rounded background in user experience research. My exploration continues through this inter-disciplinary horizon, putting HCI research into practice.

  • Project Lead, Banglalink Bangla SMS Project

    3SM Systems

    (Telecommunications industry)

Unfortunately, on the verge of agreement signup, the launch of this product got postponed for an indefinite period.     The unique features of this software included:
     First implementation of Bangla B9 input method (similar to T9 teachnology of Tegic Communications), which allows a user input Bangla at the rate of merely one keystroke per letter
     First dictionary based predictive Bangla Input prompt, the user is auto-suggested a list of words during his input.
     First handset resident content engine for creating and sending Picture SMS: if the user is not sure whether the recipient has this software installed, he can send a picture message which won’t require any software installation at the recipient side for viewing the message.
     Intelligent auto-suggestion of conjunct letters after typing of first letters
     Provision for Bangla & English multi-lingual messaging in the unified SMS
     A comprehensive set of categorized and editable message templates suitable for different moods and occasions
     Highly expressive set of emoticons
