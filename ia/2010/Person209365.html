Uday Kumar’s Summary

I have five years experience in the software services sales. My sales experience has been a good mix of hunting for new logos and farming accounts. I have focussed on leveraging the opportunities thrown open by the dynamic business and technology environment and selling solutions that deliver the promise of the same.

In this process, I have gained an excellent understanding of Offshore based sales models. In the last four years I have gained a good domain knowledge of Manufacturing, Automotive and Retail industries.

In these industry verticals I have been able to successfully sell solutions across the organization's vertical and horizontal requirements. In the product development services I have sold solutions in Embedded Software, Machine control, Process Automation and Telematics.

In terms of IT applications I have been able to build solution offerings that encompass Enterprise Portals (Commercial and Open Source), Middleware Technologies and Identity and Access management.

I enjoy traveling and currently manage a portfolio of accounts spread across the North East.
