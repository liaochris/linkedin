Merrin Pearse’s Summary

As a sustainability consultant Merrin is looking for new career opportunities to help companies enbed sustainability in their company .

He is a Project Manager whose leadership style allows projects to be realised through unleashing the full potential of staff and suppliers. His open communication engages colleagues and stakeholders across different cultural backgrounds, experiences and ages enabling company objectives to be met, on time and, crucially, within budget.

Merrin is an excellent online media communicator and actively uses social media tools.

He has a wide range of international experience working for Government Departments, SMEs, international companies and NGO’s in quality assurance, auditing, sustainability, benchmarking and community engagement roles.

Specific projects Merrin has been involved in include the, quality assurance of United Nations border missions, auditing of engineering projects, and training and facilitation in the use of new technology and processes.

Dr Merrin Pearse has a skill set which is based upon
•A Passion for the Natural Environment (yes bugs and trees) and helping people to realise that we have to look after it if we are wanting to look after ourselves.
•A background in Land Surveying, Measurement Technology and Quality Assurance
•19 years experience in Government and Private Sectors
•A PhD that researched precise positioning through use of GPS equipment, for
•Establishing local, national and international coordinate systems to support property boundary surveys and mapping applications.

Merrin is looking for a role with a forward thinking organisation that demonstrates strong company values and culture. Merrin provides focused and effective leadership, project management and communication skills.

Merrin is prepared to assist you and your team to achieve your goals, though you have to be willing and prepared to at times, Extend your Boundaries.
