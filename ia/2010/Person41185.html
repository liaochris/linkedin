Claude Johnson’s Summary

Black Fives is all about the history of dozens of African American basketball teams that played before the racial integration of the N.B.A., from 1904 through 1950. We combine this history with the lessons and experiences of those pioneers to provide meaningful insights about life that people can use today. Our slogan is “Make History Now!"

Claude Johnson’s Specialties:

Licensing, basketball history, African American history, sports marketing, merchandising, personal improvement, marketing, promotions, youth culture.
