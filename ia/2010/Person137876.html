Résumé de Matt Laurence

Through an exciting series of twists and turns, I have spent the last 15 years of my 21-year advertising and marketing communications career in the interactive and online space. During that time I have found myself at every position along the spectrum, from producer, designer, IA, business analyst or developer to online strategist, client liaison, project manager, copywriter, team leader and everything in between. It is this breadth of experience within the industry that gives me a unique insight into the vagaries and possibilities in the online marketing sphere.

My goal is to keep having fun while providing the very best creative and technical work to accomplish business goals for my clients, to find a group of people to work with that can become mutual support and family while on the job, and to play as much music as possible while being a solid and present father and husband to the most important people in my life. 'Nuf said.

Compétences de Matt Laurence

Management of freelancers and designers, project strategy and oversight, client communication and presentation, working with vendors, IA and usability, on- and off-line design/art direction, 19 years of experience in the MarComm industry, 14 years in online and interactive; significant experience with html/javascript/CSS/XML, the entire Macrodobe Suite. Flash/Actionscript, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver all specialities, solid facility in After Effects
