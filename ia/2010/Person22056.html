Benoit Freslon’s Summary

I'm a Game Designer and a Flash developer as a freelance. I'm also an indie game developer.

I have more than 3 years of experience with casual games, advergames and multiplayer games.
And 5 years of experience with Adobe Flash and ActionScript 2.0/3.0.
I worked on several projects as Game Designer, lead game designer, level designer and coder during my previous missions.

I can make my own games alone : game design (game design documents), code, graphics, level design, sound design, ....
Obviously I can also work with a team on bigger projects.

As an indie game developer, I try to create original games for casual gamers: easy to play, fun and addictive.
Most of my games was awarded and played more than 2 millions times on the web.

In this connection I'm selling licenses of my games to different websites.

All my games are playable here:
http://www.benoitfreslon.com
