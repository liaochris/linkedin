Adelaide Lancaster’s Summary

I am a co-founder of In Good Company Workplaces, a collaborative workspace that gives women entrepreneurs’ flexible access to a professional space that they can call their own as well as access to colleagues and educational programs.

Our beautiful and fresh loft-like space is not only affordable but also available through several flexible membership plans, which is helpful for accommodating various and changing work/life needs. Members can use it as a place to work, a place to meet clients, and a place to hold classes and workshops. IGC’s programs and community provide members with a strong peer network built through authentic, in-person connections and interactions. In Good Company currently supports more than 250 women business owners in NY and is in the process of expanding.

In addition to running and growing IGC, I love reading, writing and teaching about entrepreneurship, innovation, and independent small businesses. Aside from my interests in small business and entrepreneurship, I have a background in organizational development, psychological counseling, career counseling, and teaching.

Outside of my professional interests, I’m really into interior design, home décor, and barns – the kind people renovate and live in. I also love my bull dog Josephine.

Though my office is in NYC, I make my home in Philly and split my time between the two cities. I believe that this enables me to get the best of both worlds AND that commuting on Amtrak beats LIRR, NJTransit, and MetroNorth anyday.
