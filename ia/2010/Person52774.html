Danny Boice’s Summary

Danny Boice is a proven thought leader experienced in building and managing global software development and systems integration departments responsible for the conception and implementation of high-visibility, mission-critical systems for varying organizations from Fortune 500 to Small and Medium Sized Businesses. Boice has a rare blend of technology expertise and business acumen.

Engagements have included EVP of Pantheon and Director of Technology at ASCII. Both positions included managing teams responsible for large, mission critical system development. Clients during both positions included but were not limited to FedEx, Microsoft, Ingram Micro, Dell, Comcast, eBay and more.

Early in his career, Boice was employed as a software engineer at Musicmaker.com where his team was the first to utilize SOAP/XML in E-Commerce.

Last, Boice has been a featured speaker and panelist at several Technology Events and Tradeshows. He has also been featured in Baseline, CRN, Silicon Valley Journal, Business First, Yahoo Finance, Business Journals, News Forge, MIT High Tech Journal and many more.
