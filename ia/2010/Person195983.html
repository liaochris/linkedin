Steve Nolte CPPM’s Summary

My strong technology aptitude, combined with excellent oral and written communications skills, enable me to clearly define customer business requirements and translate them into technical requirements for programmers & developers. I am always focused on practical, user-friendly applications of technology to increase the customer's productivity.

Very strong aptitude for translating end user business requirements into technical specifications for development team members. Experienced in group facilitation and making technical presentations understandable to a wide range of audiences. Ability to establish professional rapport with a wide range of personnel. Desire to utilize my experience to assist business users in the design, development, testing, implementation and training of technology solutions.
