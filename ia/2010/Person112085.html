Kathryn Gorges’s Summary

I increase the visibility of small and medium sized businesses using social media and traditional marketing. My services include:
+ Development of a strategic marketing plan, including social media
+ Creation of web presence on key platforms for the business: including the most currently popular (Facebook, Twitter, YouTube)
+ Coach and help maintain an engaging social marketing presence that increases business revenue

I am available to clients in Santa Cruz, Silicon Valley, Monterey, and online.

My work enables organizations to leverage the intersection of their inspiration, talents, and value and the needs and desires of their market.

The strategic plan developed clearly articulates: 1) the contribution of the organization through a well-defined exploratory process; and 2) the marketplace experience of the product or service.

The plan translates these insights into powerful verbal and visual messages that consistently connect organizations with the markets they serve.

My work is also effective at awakening the inspiration of organizations and connecting employees to the larger mission.
