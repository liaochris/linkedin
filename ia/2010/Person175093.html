Roberto J. Tenenbaum’s Summary

More than 2 decades of experience in leading companies, nonprofits and civic institutions.

Currently Chairman & CEO of OSO LLC in Massachusetts.

Small, privately-held, international merchant bank offering advisory services for middle-market, branded companies and real estate. OSO’s advisory services are varied and include international transactions, business expansion, mergers and acquisitions, marketing, operations, and governance. The firm also focuses on developing large energy and infrastructure projects in emerging countries. OSO conducts transactions mainly between North America and small, business-friendly countries in Latin America, Central and Eastern Europe, and Israel. Our mission is also business and social in nature: To Invest in Quality of Lives.

Roberto J. Tenenbaum’s Specialties:

Leadership experience in Growth, Turnaround and Complex challenges in both Businesses & Nonprofits (Civic, Foreign Affairs, Humanitarian, National Security, and Education); Cross-Border Transactions; M&A; Governance; Marketing; Organizational Culture; Strategy; Entrepreneurship; Private/Public Partnerships; working with Elected Officials; and working with different Constituencies.
