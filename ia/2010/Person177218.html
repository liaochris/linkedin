Ron Durant’s Summary

My background includes 25 years of providing leadership in Sales, Management and Business Development within the Travel & Entertainment, Financial Services and Consumer Goods market sectors.

As president and chief "Marketing Strategist" at e-NERTIA Marketing, my focus is on helping business owners improve their ROI through the proper utilization of online marketing, including social media. Online Marketing works fast, it's economical and the results are easy to track. Properly utilized, online marketing concepts will;

• Attract and convert new customers and clients online
• Turn Search Engines into an online sales force
• Grow your client base and reduce the cost of acquisition
• Enhance business relationships and improve customer loyalty.
• Utilize Social Media to monitor the competition, manage company and executive reputations and tap into new markets

I take an active role in every project, to insure that each of my clients has the opportunity to benefit from the tremendous potential of online marketing.

At e-NERTIA, we have unique access to a network of highly skilled online marketing professionals throughout the United States. We are always ready to deliver the very best website and internet based marketing services available.
