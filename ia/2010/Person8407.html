António Lopes’s Summary

I'm currently an Assistant Professor at the Lisbon University Institute (ISCTE-IUL), in Portugal, and I currently teach the following courses of the Computer Science Engineering degree:

* Introduction to Programming (1st year-1st semester)
* Algorithms and Data Structures (1st year - 2nd semester)

I'm also a PhD student of Artificial Intelligence at ISCTE-IUL, with a PhD scholarship grant provided by FCT (Portuguese Foundation for Science and Technology). My research work focuses on Resource Coordination in Unstructured Open Agent Societies.

My main fields of research are Intelligent Agents and Multi-Agent Systems, which are part of the wider research area of Artificial Intelligence.

My research interests are related to autonomous agents and multi-agent systems, more particularly the creation of a synergy of intelligence by combining the different, yet complementary, capabilities of multiple autonomous agents.

António Lopes’s Specialties:

Development of intelligent agents and multi-agent systems on fixed and mobile environments
