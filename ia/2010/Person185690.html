Geoff Simon’s Summary

Multi-talented Marketing Executive with over 10 years of database, direct & Internet marketing experience with small Agencies like W3PR to larger ones like G2 Direct & Digital to Large Media Companies like The Walt Disney Company, Carsey-Werner Distribution and smaller boutique PR Firms with some freelance work thrown in.

I have category experience in entertainment (promotions for That 70's Show syndication that included a contest featuring a KISS "experience" along with a guest appearance on the show finale. I also have experience doing viral and micro-site development, deployment and content building for the Red Bull USA sites. Red Bull USA maintains several event related "micro-sites" such as "RedBullUSA.com", "RedBullFlugtag.com" and many others.

I am a numbers guy but respect the creative process and understand what it takes to balance the analytical with the creative sides of your brain to come at problems with a unique and successful approach. By understanding the numbers, it helps drive me creatively to think of ways to manipulate them further to ensure even more campaign success; whatever the metric may be.

I am most comfortable with SEO and organic optimization from the ground up, including wordpress CMS customization, optimization & submission, but am also well suited to help fix sites that have been neglected over the years and need a "tune up" incorporating some of the successful tools of today and tomorrow instead of yesterday and even today at the breakneck pace technology and the search industry moves. Part of this is knowing who has the good information and who is selling snake oil. Years of experience has yielded me a large number of reliable sources for Google Optimization, rumors alike.

I am an expert author for the iEntry network of publications including Web Pro News, SearchNewz and many more. These publications serve over 4 million subscribers worldwide. I have also authored pieces for About Public Relations.
