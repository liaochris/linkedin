Murtaza Kapadia’s Summary

A Senior Energy Professional with over 18 years experience in the energy industry in various engineering and senior management roles in energy efficiency, peak load management, clean energy and demand response industry.
Responsible for new market and business development for large turnkey demand response MW programs and design of DSM programs.
Extensive experience in designing demand response, energy retrofits and demand side management programs. Successfully analyzed and designed retrofits and programs for energy efficiency and demand response projects worth over 400 million dollars in hospitals, university, commercial, government, industrial, correction facilities and schools.
Roles and Responsibility
• Provide technical leadership in establishing strategic project development team objectives to achieve the team goals.
• Provide training to the sales and delivery group for execution of turnkey demand response and energy efficiency programs.
• Develop DSM proposals in competitive bidding environment while responding to RFQ/ RFP within tight time schedules.
• Develop Client – Vendor relationship, mitigate company risk and design projects to meet customer needs and regulatory requirements.
• Provide training to sales, engineering and strategic partners for developing and implementing energy efficiency projects leading to white certificates under the requirements for new state energy efficiency performance standards.
• Design and install complex Measurement and Verification equipment adhering to the requirements of International Performance Measurement and Verification Protocols for DSM measures, obtaining white certificates and developing ODR (other demand resources) projects.
