Pierre Kerbage’s Summary

Nearly 3 decades of experience in building extremely successful technology companies and taking each of them to the top of their ranks. Profitable growth has been a common denominator in all operations

Founder, and President of Total Systems, Inc. Started company from basement in the late 80s and with 0 capital, and took it to the largest privately held managed IT services company in Kansas and the 2nd fastest growing company in the State of Kansas. Sold company in November of 1999 and moved to Austin, Texas.

Owner, President and CEO of Network Logistic, Inc.(“NLI”) http://www.networklogistic.com in Austin, TX. Built the company starting year 2000 from the ground up (also with no external capital), and took it to the largest and fastest growing privately owned Managed IT Services & Interconnect company in Central Texas in 2008, as well as the inclusion in INC 5000 rating before selling it on August 2008.

Owner, President and CEO of an innovative Web Based Media company in Austin Texas(since 2005), and built Radio Streaming company: www.internetbroadcastinggroup.com and was awarded 7 American Graphic Design Awards. Company was also sold in August of 2008.

Today, Senior global Vice President at ZULTYS, INC. (out of Silicon Valley) – the most innovative and elegant Enterprise class VoIP IP PBX Phone Systems company in the world.


Specialties:
30+ years of building extremely profitable Managed IT Services companies and Telecom companies. Strong leadership, Marketing, Sales, Hiring, Training, manufacturing programs, strong knowledge in financial analysis and building companies from 0 to multimillion and making them extremely profitable in a short amount of time.
