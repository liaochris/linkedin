Ankit Maheshwari’s Summary

Listed in Asia’s most inspiring young entrepreneurs and social Changemakers by Paragaon 100 Asia; co-founder and CEO of Instablogs Network.

Instablogs which was founded three years ago today stand as one of the top citizen newspapers in the world and an active community of 30,000 members, and 80 lakh pageviews per month.

At a time when every mainstream media house is cutting down on its international coverage, Instablogs is fast expanding it.
Instablogs was conceived from the idea of news for a global citizen's newspaper.

Wanted to build a platform where through collaborative news approach the world's critical issues would be highlighted from the people who are actually affected from them.

Would love to interact with fellow (or budding) entrepreneurs, share ideas and exchange expertise in all possible areas.
