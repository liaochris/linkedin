Javier Mateache’s Summary

I am a Business man very focused on objectives as i learnt in my home from my father during my childhood.

I have worked from very small companies to huge multi-national corporations, public and private, in many different areas but mainly in the energy sector.

I like to grow business from difficulties like we did in NEO / EDP as well as from scratch. I need change i like the momentum created. And my goal always is to add value.

My profile is an experienced manager with tecnical and commercial skills among others.

From 2003 to 2007 i worked in Renewables achieving all the goals we expected in NEO Energia, what today is called EDP Renovaveis.

I incorporated Gestamp Eolica in NOV 2007 with Corporacion Gestamp and some months later my old friend and former CEO in EDP, Dionisio Fernandez Auray joined us.

Dionisio is now managing the European Business of our company as i am doing with the american. 2 years later we have wind assets in 8 countries, more than 300 Mw under operation, others in construction and we are still growing. Remarkable is our position in USA, Spain, Brazil, Belgium and Poland as well as our manufacturing side with facilities in Galicia and Pernambuco.

Now we are 50 people, a team very well balanced and efficient with capabilities to mantain and increase our growth rate. Our investments currently surpassed 700 M$.

Corporacion Gestamp and The Riberas Family are very ambitious regarding the renewable branch of Gestamp as we are or even more. Finally i have to say that we fell completely aligned with our majority partners and very happy to work for and with this fantastic private familiar group.
