Ivan Pepelnjak’s Summary

Ivan Pepelnjak, CCIE No. 1354, is a 25-year veteran of the networking industry, with 15+ years of experience in designing, installing, troubleshooting, and operating large service provider and enterprise WAN and LAN networks. He is currently chief technology advisor at NIL Data Communications, focusing on advanced IP-based networks and web technologies. His books published by Cisco Press include MPLS and VPN Architectures and EIGRP Network Design.

Ivan Pepelnjak’s Specialties:

Skills: Large-scale network design, technical consulting, training curriculum development, high-end technical training development and delivery, web 2.0 planning and deployment.

Networking technologies: IP, MPLS, VPN, BGP, QoS, Security
Web technologies: XSLT, ASP, XML, CSS, Ajax
