Mike Wells’s Summary

Originally arriving in San Francisco to pursue a music career, I found myself as a freelance computer consultant and audio engineer throughout the 90's, moving exclusively into mastering at the end of the 20th Century.

My work over the last 10 years has been working with independent acts and within the last few years working with national and international signed acts. I am a proponent of emerging technologies and their integration into the mastering process as our industry continues to evolve.

I have taken a leading role in the industry as an early adopter of ISRC and Metadata integration within the Mastering role along with offering continuing education for the artist sector through articles and seminars.

I have sat on a number of industry panels discussing future technology integration into the Mastering industry and continue the implementation of these technologies into my business to assist my customers.

Mike Wells’s Specialties:

Audio Mastering, Customer Service, Analog & Digital Audio systems, Digital Audio Metadata (including ISRC/UPC/EAN & TOC Header info), Digital Asset Encoding, Digital Asset Distribution
