John Morgan’s Summary

Morgan Pinnacle, LLC. is a rep firm that specializes in the kitchen & bath industry. Our Firm was founded in 1978 as John H. Morgan & Associates and we are well known for our professionalism and long term relationships with our suppliers and dealers. That makes us quite unique in this profession. We strive to only associate with quality companies and individuals and we work hard to build very profitable relationships for both our suppliers and dealers. If they are very profitable, we will be too.

John K. Morgan is a 1989 graduate of Loyola University with a degree in Finance. He began working in the kitchen & bath industry while in college and has achieved much success in his 20+ years in the industry.

John has earned the distinction of being one of the industry’s top selling reps in both cabinetry and technology as well as one it’s top consultants and trainers.

He is a leader in the networking and volunteer community. John has served NKBA nationally since 2004 and is currently proudly serving as the National Secretary on the NKBA Executive Committee.

He is also recognized throughout North America as the author of THE REP’S VIEW column which offers insight to the industry and has been featured in KBDN (Kitchen & Bath Design News) since 2001.
