Milind Jadhav’s Summary

Hi, I am Milind Jadhav, a Brand Consultant and Life Skills Coach. I conduct self-development programs for individuals and corporates – programs that empower people and help them deal better with challenges thrown up by life.

I’m based in Chennai, India and am an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore, India. Before taking up Life Skills Coaching, I was an Advertising professional having spent nearly 15 years in that field. During this period I have gained rich experience in people management and creative thinking (two skills critical to a successful advertising career). And these skills are what I use to create and deliver programs that deliver value to participants.

I also am a Brand Consultant to a few companies. I take up Brand Consultancy projects either as one-off projects or on a long-term basis depending on my time availability.

Stay connected with me. It will be my privilege to partner you in your journey of self-development!
