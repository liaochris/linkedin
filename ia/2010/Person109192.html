Justin Kownacki’s Summary

As an explorer (and practitioner) of social media since 2003, I use online tools to improve communication among individuals, groups and corporations.

I currently serve as the Director of Social Media for Creative Concepts, LLC, a Fairfield, CT-based messaging agency that helps direct the social media strategy for corporate clients and small businesses in the greater New York region.

I'm the co-founder of PodCamp Pittsburgh, which is western Pennsylvania's annual "un-conference." The event unites podcasters, bloggers, artists, journalists and business owners in one 2-day learning session that teaches social media in layman's terms. I've also spoken about social media at numerous conferences, including Video on the Net 2007 in San Jose, the inaugural Blogference (July 2007) in Tel Aviv, Israel, and the 2009 Mensa Annual Gathering in Pittsburgh, PA.

In addition, I'm the creator / writer / producer of the web sitcom Something to Be Desired (or STBD), the web's longest-running comedy series. I also write alumni reviews for The Art Institute of Pittsburgh's PR office, and I blog at http://justinkownacki.com/ (and formerly at http://justinkownacki.blogspot.com/)

When I'm not doing all of the above, I'm looking for ways to collaborate with interesting people across all channels of media. Distribution is merely a means to an end.
