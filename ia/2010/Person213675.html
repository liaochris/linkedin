Rick Wandrych’s Summary

ATTRACT NEW CUSTOMERS
We develop affordable, custom, attractive and functional websites for small businesses that will meet the needs of a small business today and tomorrow.

* Pay their bill
* Request your services

Since time is valuable, each website we develop not only allows for easy navigation, but is search engine friendly and search engine optimized.

KEEP EXISTING CUSTOMERS
Ever lose a customer because they couldn’t get a hold of you? Most business owners have. That’s why Email Marketing has become an important part of customer retention. With email marketing, your customers will be hearing from you consistently, with helpful information and a point of contact always included. Emails can also convey:

* new services you offer
* one-time deals
* specials to spur on business

It is not only for customer retention, it can also attract new customers when emails are forwarded on to new people.

Bare Essentials’ email marketing programs can be designed to fit your needs and budget.

Our Easy Online Payment Systems...

Whether your business is landscaping, pool service, pest control, real estate or assisted living, you do have something in your business that can be simplified using eCommerce. Bare Essential Marketing puts the pieces of the eCommerce puzzle together for you.
