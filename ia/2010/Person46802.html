Dan Giuliani’s Summary

Dan Giuliani is a professional voice talent, specializing in play-by-play broadcasting. Dan has served as the voice of the Seattle Wolves FC of the United Soccer Leagues, high school football and basketball teams in the Seattle area, Washington Huskies Women's Basketball team, and of Seattle University Baseball. In addition, Dan has filled-in as the voice of University of Washington Baseball.

As part of the Seattle Wolves media department, Dan received the USL-PDL Media Department of the Year Award in November 2009.

Dan has extensive experience in the field of sport, having played, coached, studied, or called athletics since he was a young man. He played college football at Colby College in Waterville, Maine, helping to lead the team to a 7-1 record in 2005 and was the recipient of the coveted Paul "Ginger" Frazier Award in 2004.

Dan's athletic experience includes; football, basketball, soccer, lacrosse, tennis, baseball, track and field, and crew. He has also coached football, wrestling, track and field, and strength and conditioning.

Dan is a member of the National Football Coaches Association (AFCA), the National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA), and is a certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS).

Dan will earn his Master of Sport Administration and Leadership from Seattle University in June 2010.

For more information about Dan and for samples of his work, please visit www.DanGiuliani.com.
