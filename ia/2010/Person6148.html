Alexandra B. Kelemen, MBA, CPCC, ACC’s Summary

Alexandra is your confidante. That unique person you can trust. How many people like her to do you have in your life?
If doesn't matter if you are a CEO, a celebrety or a lay person, she will keep your secrets like they are SECRET.

Alexandra is an International Executive Coach with global expertise and a unique background that brings more to the table. She holds an MBA in International Business. She is a CPCC Certified Professional Coach, and ACC Credentialed Professional Coach, with a private practice in Greenwich, CT. For the last 20 years, Alex has worked in the personal development field. Her combination of psychology training and unique cross-cultural background brings an uncommon dimension to her coaching methodology.

Alex is a specialist in HR, Organizational Change and Cross-Cultural Communication and speaks four languages. By training managers she show them how to be the best leaders they could be. She has held several international positions and served for many years as a professional in the UN before she founded Life Coach 22, LLC. She brings this rich mix of experience, techniques and passion to her work. She is an internationally recognized expert in the field of personal transformation and has developed many workshops over the years. Alexandra has being published in the Northern Trust Magazine and Greenwich Times.

Alex is an active member of the Board of the International Coach Federation ICF-CT. She is also the President of the BNI Greenwich chapter and a Board member of the Woman Graduates USA. Alex is an Integrated Energy Therapy IET® Master-Instructor and a Reiki Master. She is Trainer of Trainers of OH-cards™ by EOS.
