Joyce Dungee Proctor’s Summary

Joyce has over 20 years of business management, leadership, human resources and coaching experience. She currently holds the position of Managing Director at ICT. She has spent years inspiring thousands around the world to make positive changes in their work life that have empowered them to move beyond contemplating change to make the changes necessary to pursue their passion and fulfill their dreams. A much sought after professional keynote speaker and published author of , Take the Limits Off series one: Nine Ways to Stop Talking and Start Doing. Joyce teaches a variety of courses at ICT and is available for one-on-one or group coaching experiences.
