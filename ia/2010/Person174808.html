Robert Hoogendoorn’s Summary

During my first job at CowWorks and managed the content production. We were producing textual and visual content for websites and narrowcasting channels. Besides that we organized the World Cyber Games, ESWC and Electronic Sports League Nederland. Our focus on E-Sports also resulted in a pilot broadcast on RTL 4 for a program called Total E-Sports.

In 2006 I started working for myself. Doing freelance writing for several organizations, producing videos for the Librium TV narrowcasting channel in McDonalds, and still playing my role as editor in chief for Ownage.nl.

In september 2007 I started working at ThreeSixty Entertainment. Here I was responsible for the production of all videogame content and distribution to all our cliënts, including Ziggo, SlamFM, Bol.com and the most popular gameshow of 2009, GameRush.

My ambitition: writing a book on videogame culture, managing an international group of editors over several countries, start a digital tv-channel,

Robert Hoogendoorn’s Specialties:

branding, content providing, content analysis, targeting an audience, academic approach, television concepts, website concepts, background stories and interviews, in-depth research, project management, working hard and never exceeding deadlines. great at teamwork, working with freelancers and volunteers.
