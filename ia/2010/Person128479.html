Lucendia Halliday’s Summary

Lucendia Halliday founded Measurable Marketing Results in January 2005. She previously owned a similar business in Los Angeles, CA for eight years prior to moving back to her home state in Michigan 14 years ago.

She previously held senior positions at the former advertising agencies and both large and small corporations such as Campbell-Ewald, Marketing Advertising Services, A.C Nielsen Television Ratings, Citicorp and several hi-tech companies. When moving back to Michigan in 1993 she also worked as contractor for Ford Motor Company and General Motors.

Lucendia began her career in Clearwater, Florida in a small ad agency and was frustrated that everything was cut and paste with scissors using the Xerox machine. She knew computers were the better way to go. Later, she landed a job at A.C. Nielsen Ratings in Dunedin, Florida and began taking computer classes. She was hooked immediately and has always kept in tune with the hi-tech industry to utilize all the tools to help her be more efficient and productive.

She is a native of Michigan. Lucendia was born and raised in a family business that is still very successful.She often mentions that is where she earned her M.B.A. She earned a bachelor of arts degree in Advertising and communications from Michigan State University in 1982 and a masters degree in Human Performance Improvement from the University of Michigan in 2004. Even more importantly she continues her education daily and has studied all the marketing gurus worldwide.
