Richard David’s Summary

1. Bi-lingual Entrepreneur of 31 years experienced in the fields of international finance, import/export, business lending and real estate. Specialist in structuring and funding complex commercial and business loan transactions using equity as the secondary funding component allowing up to 100% LTV financing.

2. Created the BancoFunds Program; an integrated banking, business and insurance solutions model benefiting small to medium sized businesses, contractors and merchants. It is an innovative growth capital lending program operating on a platform of various managed controls consisting of proactive financial oversight, payroll administration, merchant processing, collateral management, and funds disbursement protocols allowing small businesses to access working capital not available through traditional lending channels.

3. Developed and currently marketing the following lending products and services under the Bancofino label.

• BancoCash - Cash advances for merchants
• BancoFactor - Purchases of small business A/R
• Banco Purchase Order Financing - Advanced funds for
businesses in need of quick working capital
• BancoBond - Performance and Payment Bonds for
contractors doing business with the government
• BancoLoan - Commercial loans up to 100% LTV
• BancoLease - Equipment leasing
• BancoSurance - Liability Insurance
• BancoPayroll - Outsourced payroll administration
• BancoMerchant - Businesses that accept credit cards
• BancoSBA - Government loans coming soon
• BancoPayDay Loans - Short term business loans
(under development)

4. Manager of the Bancofino Passive Investors Program administered through Merrill Lynch Wealth Management, a subsidiary of Bank of America Corporation.
