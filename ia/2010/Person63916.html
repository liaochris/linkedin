Ellen Goldstein’s Summary

I have been a photographer throughout my professional career. Beginning with door to door portraiture but later branching out to multi-media, shows such as the SF and NY Experience's. I worked for a film editor before working for a SF multi-media company as a photographer and producer of audio visual programs for ad agencies, corporations and non-profits.

I have experience shooting for magazines, newspapers and businesses both large and small. I love photographing people of all styles, families and pets. I have had the opportunity to work with some extraordinary people and some ordinary people. A journalist at heart, I like to cover the scene from all angels. I have a small studio for portraiture and product photography but I like to photograph people in their own surroundings or in a natural environment. In addition, I have exhibited my art and travel photography locally and through several photography sites and online publications.

I enjoy working with others in a collaborative manner and promote good business communications and well planned marketing strategies. When working with a new client we help them define themselves and target their business, with well designed business communications, PowerPoint presentations, advertising and marketing campaigns.

We started Bullseye Productions in 1984 as a multi-media and print design company. When the world wide web was developed we were one of the first bay area design companies to adapt to digital platforms.
I have worked with my partner of thirty years, Dan Reich, out of a small cottage on the rear of our San Rafael home. We have had to continually learn new technologies and be aware of market trends in order to offer clients a full range of advertising, design, and marketing communication services.

Call us to help you hit your tarkets!
