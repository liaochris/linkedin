Deana Zelenka’s Summary

Deana is Founder and CEO of Zecozi, the first of it's kind Shared Shopping marketplace for sustainable products that's set to launch in 2010. In this role, she has spent significant time building a rock star team and commissioning extensive market research after recognizing an absence of any viable solution capable of creating and sustaining significant global change in the $400B sustainable market segment. This space is of particular importance to Deana as she has been actively involved in the global green movement for over 18 years. Prior to Zecozi, she utilized her background as an executive in the chemical manufacturing industry to found Oppelle, an executive think tank founded for women CEOs, Presidents and business leaders. Her reputation as a growth leader and turn-around business leader makes her an ideal candidate to lead Zecozi to a position of market leader.

Additionally, Ms. Zelenka's philanthropic endeavors include providing business expertise to organizations aligned with her values and she currently serves on the Board of Directors for The Franklin Foundation, an organization devoted to the advancement of mathematics and technology innovation in education.

An active member of the Philadelphia and New Jersey business communities (and previously Florida), Ms. Zelenka is a frequent keynote speaker and presenter and has spoken at both local and national conferences. She currently speaks on women's leadership issues and peak performance.

Deana is highly committed to environmental causes and living "green". She works extensively in these areas to educate and promote green initiatives.

Deana, along with her daughters, lives in the Southern New Jersey.
