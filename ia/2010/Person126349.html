Lisa Rojany Buccieri’s Summary

Editorial Services of Los Angeles (ESOLA) offers editorial services ranging from detailed line editing with manuscript-specific critiques, developmental editing, and ghost-writing, to project management in which we oversee the creation of your publishing list from concept stage through manufacturing. We provide services from creative development of text and art through all the elements of book packaging to finished and delivered books with as much input as you want—or don't.

I am a publishing executive and author of over 50 credited children's books, 3 YA novels, 4 novelizations, 2 grown-up books, one a New York Times bestseller and the other, WRITING CHILDREN'S BOOKS FOR DUMMIES (Wiley, 2005), a consistent top seller on Amazon in its category. I have also ghost-written 100+ titles, and overseen the publication of hundreds of titles from concept through manufacturing. I have helped lots and lots of writers make their work the best it can be, which I find extremely gratifying.

For more about my services, please visit: www.EditorialServicesofLA.com

Lisa Rojany Buccieri’s Specialties:

Novelty books, editing, series development, in-book technologies, publishing list enhancement, developmental editing, trade fiction editing, young adult and middle-grade novels—writing and editing.
