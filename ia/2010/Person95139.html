    GAGA Sports & Entertainment Inc

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    October 2010 — Present (3 months)

    At GAGA, we provide our customers, sports leagues and organizations, the ability to create that engagement with their fans and let them “inside the velvet ropes”. Our years of experience in sports has given us a true understanding of the special place sports hold in the hearts and minds of fans, and we provide our customers a way to connect with their fans on that more intimate level. Whether it’s a fun trivia contest or poll engaging them on a daily basis, shooting video of behind-the-scenes places they have never seen, or getting to know people in the organization, we assist in building out engagement strategies with fans.

    By fans registering, for free, to view content, we create engaging sites that also allow our customers to develop unique insights into their fans. Analysis of data allows fans to receive customized, contextually relevant messaging from their teams, and allows our customers to get a deeper understanding so they can tailor specific marketing messages to segments of their fan base. Providing specific messaging to specific fans streamlines communications and optimizes efficiency.

    We know today’s and tomorrow’s fan will engage with a variety of media. They will do so 24/7/365 and on their schedule. Our engagement strategies are “portable” as content easily flows back and forth between with social media sites, mobile platforms and whatever “the next thing” may be. We work with our customers to develop personalized “playbooks”, utilizing our deep knowledge of the social media space, to most effectively and efficiently use these media platforms to drive engagement.
