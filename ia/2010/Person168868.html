Randy Bowen’s Summary

To Increase ROI on the email campaigns I manage by raising the inbox and conversion rates of delivered email. Sending email was done in the past. Delivering email is what's done today, and it requires tight control of the technical and practical sides of the industry to do it profitably.

The majority of the technical side is done prior to hitting send, and must be configured correctly before the first email can be sent. The practical is the less defined gray area of "Best Business Practices" that one must abide by to achieve inbox delivery. However, what's best for AOL may not be best with Yahoo, so knowing the difference and acting on it is key to getting that Increased that ROI stuff mentioned earlier.

Once delivered, precedence is placed on motivating the recipient to "click" to a landing page that delivers a message or facilitates a conversion. This is the marketing element and is done on a per campaign basis, so please contact me personally if you wish to discuss this in more detail.
