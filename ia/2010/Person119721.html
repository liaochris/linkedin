Karen Sieczka’s Summary

Karen Sieczka is a people developer, training professional, author, Creativity@Work expert, and founder of Growing Great Ideas.com, a workplace training resources website. Growing Great Ideas.com was recognized by ASTD as a Champion of Learning 2009!

Karen is currently seeking a workplace training position, contract training, and volunteer training projects in the Atlanta metro area.

As a workplace training professional, Karen has facilitated over 250 workshops, with over 3,000 participants trained in a variety of programs including Microsoft Office programs, soft skills training, and customized training workshops in customer service and business communications.

Karen is available to speak about Creativity@Work in the Atlanta Metro area with ideas and insights from her 2008 book, Growing Great Ideas: Unleashing Creativity at Work.

For more information visit: www.growinggreatideas.com/creativity
