Bruce Hennes’s Summary

Bruce M. Hennes is the Managing Partner of Hennes Paynter Communications, a crisis communications, media training, litigation communications and media relations consulting firm based in Cleveland serving corporations, government agencies, nonprofits and individuals who are “on trial” in the Court of Public Opinion.

Hennes has more than 30 years experience working in politics, including three years as Executive Director for The Temple on the Heights in Pepper Pike, Ohio, and three years doing government and public relations in the automotive industry. He opened his own firm in 1989.

As one of Cleveland’s best-known crisis comm specialists and media trainers, Hennes is also an in-demand speaker on the subject of crisis communications. Under the auspices of local County Emergency Management Agencies, he often speaks before public safety, education and government officials about “Extreme Crisis Communications” (i.e. how to handle mass casualty and “extreme” situations involving threats to life & limb).

Hennes currently serves on the boards of the Cleveland Metropolitan Bar Association, the Business Emergency Planning Institute, the Press Club of Cleveland, the Cleveland Leadership Center and the Judicial Candidates Rating Coalition (judge4yourself.com). He was one of the founding board members of the FBI Citizens Academy Foundation of Cleveland. He is also a Graduate Lecturer in Advanced Communications/Media Relations in the Levin College of Urban Affairs at Cleveland State University and a frequent guest lecturer at John Carroll University, Kent State, Case Western Reserve University and Ursuline College. Hennes is also president of the Leadership Cleveland Class of 2008 and a member of Contingency Planners of Ohio,.

Bruce Hennes’s Specialties:

Besides crisis communications, media training and media relations, Hennes Paynter Communications is also adept at public relations, community relations, rezoning & referendum management and litigation communications support.
