Leslie Wetzel Theodore’s Summary

Ms. Theodore has over 15 years of management experience in financial services working with individual and institutional clients, both domestically and abroad. She leads new business development, strategy, program development and innovation in emerging markets.

Over the last 13 years, Ms. Theodore's work has focused on the growth and expansion of microfinance institutions (MFIs) in Latin America, Africa and Asia -- securing financing, developing business plans and working with field personnel to implement projects. She has worked with some of the largest MFIs in Latin America and global development agencies including the World Bank, the InterAmerican Development Bank and USAID. She also has broad experience partnering with corporate and private foundations.

Prior to her non-profit work in microfinance, Ms. Theodore was a private banker specializing in Trust and Investments. She holds an MBA from Thunderbird, Global School of Management.

A US citizen born in Spain, Ms. Theodore has lived and worked in many countries, is fluent in business Spanish and brings a multi-cultural perspective to her work.
