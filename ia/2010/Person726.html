Aaron Adjemian’s Summary

Currently I am the Marketing Manager of DATAMARK, Inc., a Founding Member of the Kiwanis Young Professionals Group in Las Vegas, Nevada and Founder of the EP Young Professionals Society located in El Paso, Texas.

DATAMARK, Inc. is one of the leading global outsourcing firms based in the U.S. and is responsible for the development of several 'best-practice' outsourcing solutions used by many Fortune 500 companies today.

The El Paso Young Professionals' Society is one of the premiere, business networking groups in El Paso, Texas. In a little over 6 months our members number nearly 200 highly motivated and passionate young professionals, including Small Business Owners and Entrepreneurs. We organize THE most effective upscale networking events that both enrich member's social and cultural experiences, while enabling them to meet other young professionals in a friendly environment.

The goal of both groups is to organize motivated young professionals in the community to create an energetic and dynamic environment of learning, networking and entrepreneurship.

I graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 2007, where I majored in Management with a focus in Entrepreneurship. As a member of the Varsity Notre Dame fencing team for 4 years, I participated in several NCAA fencing competitions, including the 2005 NCAA National Championship event where my teammates and I won the NCAA National Fencing Title.
