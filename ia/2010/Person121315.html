Larry Miller II’s Summary

Larry Miller is a Staffing Consultant in the high-tech software industry, with a nationwide scope of assignments focusing on Sales related roles.

I genuinely like helping people…we are all in this thing together…and people helping people…is what makes the world go around.

In our efforts to succeed and reach our goals, we find that we all have to deal with “Numbers Problem”.

I like to think of what I do…as a part of the "Numbers Solution!"


Larry worked with Stafftopia from Q/3-03’-thru- Q/2-07’where he was the top performer with focus on Software Sales Executives (nationwide). Prior to that Larry was with was Emerson Brooks managing their Sales & Marketing division, having the highest quarterly and annual sales in that division.

Larry is excellent at developing relationships with VP and C-level executives in his field of specialty…as well as long standing relationships with his candidates he represents.

Larry works with candidates and companies located throughout the country, making placements in almost every major city in the US.

Prior to Staffing & Recruitment, Larry worked in the Food and Beverage industry for almost 20 years, this experience has helped him understanding customer satisfaction, employee relations and performing well under pressure.

Larry graduated from Boise State University with a degree in Marketing and Business (BBA) in 2000, and since then have had a successful career in technical recruiting.
