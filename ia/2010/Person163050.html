Peter Philipp, CFA, CFP®’s Summary

Peter Philipp manages the San Francisco office of Cambridge Investment Research. He has over 20 years experience in the financial services industry having held various positions at Wells Fargo and as a strategy consultant with Accenture. Peter specializes in investment management for high-net-worth clients and corporate benefit plans.

Peter is also an instructor for UC Berkeley's Financial Planning Program and helped develop the Wealth Management curriculum. For his work with the program, Peter was awarded the 2008 Golden Bear Award by UC Berkeley and the Leading through Innovation Award from the Haas School of Business.

He holds an M.B.A. from the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley and is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charterholder.
