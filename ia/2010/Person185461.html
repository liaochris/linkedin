Sean Morrow’s Summary

Sean Morrow is a senior manager and company leader with a background in online product management and Search Engine Optimization (SEO). He is well versed in B2C and B2B market research methods, target market identification and customer segmentation strategies. His teams have delivered more than 1200 media hits in the past 3 years, from the local to international level and in a variety of mediums.

Sean has positioned and represented companies and their brands for most of his professional career. His capacity to analyze business logic and implement strategies alongside business goals set him apart when delivering. He has successfully managed teams of up to 40 people in the technology and marketing arenas and has delivered projects that have returned significant venture capitalist funding.

Sean is ever the intermediary between parties; his capacity to listen, analyze and offer thought often sees him at the center of interdepartmental discussion, conflict resolution and negotiations.
