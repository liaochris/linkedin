Carol Davidson’s Summary

Carol Davidson, AICI, CIP brings fourteen years of corporate experience to her image and wardrobe consultancy, StyleWorks of Union Square Inc. As one of only 25 Certified Image Professionals (AICI, CIP) in NY, her expertise has helped thousands of professionals tackle their careers and personal life with confidence and enthusiasm.

Carol is a respected educator and teaches in the Image Consulting Certificate Program at Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT). She has managed the personal shopping team at Macy’s By Appointment in New York, where she also conducted on-site corporate seminars to educate clients about appropriate business casual wear.

Carol Davidson’s Specialties:

Color and Style Analysis, Wardroe Planning, Personal Shopping, Closet Organization, and Corporate Seminars
