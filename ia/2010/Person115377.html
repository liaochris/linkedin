Kevin C. Tofel’s Summary

* My passion is mobile technology, specifically in finding new ways to use it and then share those methods with the community at large.
* I've been podcasting on the topic for over three years and I'm also published in the New York Times, PC World and PC Magazine in this subject. Additionally, I've had television and radio appearances to share mobile technology with a larger audience.
* I'm also an effective team builder as evidenced by my role at Weblogs / AOL. I built the HDTV website and blogging team up from start-up status to nearly a half-million page views in under eight months.
* I've been blogging and writing on mobile tech since 2003 and currently write daily at jkOnTheRun.com, a GigaOM network property.
* Supplementing my current passion, I have 15 years of I.T. and project management experience at three Fortune 100 companies.

Kevin C. Tofel’s Specialties:
