JoAnn Locktov’s Summary

JoAnn Locktov, owner of Bella Figura Communications started her career in Public Relations after writing two books on contemporary mosaics. A new Venetian mosaic school needed students and a career was born. JoAnn uses her experience as a writer, development director, and imaginative strategist to determine the how to best represent her clients’ needs both economically and effectively. Her great passion for art and design has enabled Bella Figura Communications to assist creative individuals and businesses with innovative solutions to reach their goals. And that Venetian mosaic school? It is thriving with students attending from every corner of the globe.
Hai fatto bella figura……the Italian expression for making a positive impression by pleasing both the eye and the mind.

This is the essence of our Public Relations services, which function as the conduit from your business to your market through written and visual communication.

JoAnn Locktov’s Specialties:

We have developed a specialized focus in design, architecture, shelter and travel for campaigns reaching both the trade and consumer markets.

Social Media, Press Relations, Writing/Editing
