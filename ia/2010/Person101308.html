Joey Galon’s Summary

Joey began his creative career in Mrktg, Public Relations and Special Events in the fashion industry, producing high profile fashion shows which allowed him the opportunity to establish new product launches, public relations, design sets, choreograph shows and work exclusively with top couture designers and their collections. In addition to fashion show presentations, Mr. Galon contributed his creative eye doing visual displays and event planning for such renown retail companies as I.Magnin, Neiman Marcus and Armani.

Joey has a colorful and varied twenty year history in the Event Planning & Design industry. Originally working as an “independent” in the San Francisco Bay Area, he is known for contributing vast amounts of energy, creativity and talent toward the success of events such as Macy’s Passport Fashion Show, Sausilito Art Festival, and has contributed toward events for such esteemed companies as Bob Mackie Fashions, Thierry Mugler, Oscar de la Renta, Hubert de Givency(with the late Audrey Hepburn), San Francisco Opera & Ballet, the De Young and Asian Museum, I. Magnin and Gentlemen’s Quarterly Magazine (GQ). “Special Events Magazine” honored Joey with a nomination for “Best Event of the Year” when his company at the time, “Bang Bang Party Productions” produced a fantasy driven event in San Francisco, spanning a space that would be the equivalent of three football fields long entitled “Alexis in Wonderland”.

Previous Employment: Catering and Convention Sales Director/Mgr: W Hotel, Desert Inn Resort, Hard Rock Casino, General Mgr. for production show: Hotel Lotte, Pusan, Korea featuring a cast of 65 dancers and showgirls, specialty acts, exotic animals including white tigers, servals, black panthers and African Elephants showcased in the grand opening of a five star Japanese owned resort, Events & Mrktg. for Belo Medical - Dr. Vicki Belo, Manila, Philippines, Mrktg. & Investor Relations - Revitalize Wailuku, Maui, Hawaii.
