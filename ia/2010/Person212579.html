Vishnu Varma’s Summary

Vishnu Varma heads information technology at Tiger Global Mgmt.

Prior to joining Tiger, Varma led the front office technology team at Caxton Associates. In this role, he was responsible for quantitative application development supporting various trading desks and integration of trading/market data applications.

Prior to joining Caxton, Varma served as a Senior Manager at Charles River, where he was in charge of trading and compliance system implementations at premier hedge funds such as AQR, Paulson, Ramius, Atticus and Alexandra. He also managed large scale, high volume trading systems implementations at Morgan Stanley Asset Management and Mellon Investor Services.

Prior to that, Varma served as a Vice President of Banking Technology at Goldman Sachs, leading major cross divisional technology initiatives while supporting Executive Office and Investment Banking division, for six years.

He also served in various technology consulting and management roles with Infosys Technologies, a major Indian IT services company, focusing on retail and consumer industries, driving world-wide re-engineering efforts at major organizations such as Reebok, Liz Claiborne and General Electric.

Varma is an electrical engineering graduate of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University at Hyderabad, India and holds an MBA in Finance from the Stern School of Business at New York University.
