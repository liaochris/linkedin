Allie Brown’s Summary

Technology Partners, founded in 1994, is a different kind of staffing company. Our reputation as an industry leader attracts the best and brightest local and national talent in the areas of Information Technology, Accounting/Finance, Administrative/Clerical, Call Center/Customer Care, Human Resources, Professional/Legal, Technical/Engineering and more. Our unique business model provides our clients with top candidates at competitive salaries and our consultants with the best opportunities. We approach client satisfaction with a simple philosophy in mind. Unique clients have unique needs and challenges, so we better provide them with unique solutions. Technology Partners specializes in creating a staffing strategy that not only considers what you need now, but what you may need in the future. In this way, we can develop the right answer for your specific situation. Whatever the position, we have the workforce to fill it.
