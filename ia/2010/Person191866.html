Sonya Vallejo’s Summary

With 13 years of recruiting experience in both staffing and corporate recruiting, Sonya has built a solid network in numerous industries and believes “It is all in who you know.” Sonya started her recruiting career in the staffing industry and quickly realized recruiting was where she was meant to be. Enjoying great success in staffing for 4 years, she decided to make a change and move to the corporate side and was the Recruiting and Retention Manager for a mid-size medical device company, Orthofix Inc. During the 5 years at Orthofix, Sonya created and implemented the company’s recruiting processes, re-wrote the company’s employment application, implemented an ATS and was the first to recognize the importance of retention programs to retain the company’s biggest asset…the TALENT. After accomplishing everything she could accomplish at Orthofix, Sonya went back into the ever changing and challenging staffing world as a Selling Branch Manager for Ultimate Staffing and CoWorx Staffing Services. She had full responsibility for the entire operations of a full service staffing firm including selling, recruiting, P&L management and team management. Sonya has most recently joined the ReachLocal recruiting team as a management recruiter focusing on top sales management talent in the digital advertising space.

Sonya is a Woo, a Relator, an Achiever, and her decisions are Strategic. She believes that work must be meaningful and adheres to very strong core values. Throw in her 10 years of employee relations expertise, her 10 years of management background, her 5 years b2b sales experience, her strong communication and presentation skills and you have the FULL PACKAGE!
