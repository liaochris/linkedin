Geoff Fellows’s Summary

I have over a decade of experience as a marketing professional managing such high-profile clients as Verizon Wireless, DuPont Teflon, Microsoft’s Xbox Live, NHL, The SciFi Channel and AOL. This experience has taken me to Boston, Atlanta, and New York where I have had the good fortune to learn from and work with some of the best and brightest marketers, both agency and client side.

I began my career twelve years ago as a traditional designer, but soon was engaged in the potential of online marketing and consumer relationships. This passion for the interactive environment and my creative experience made for a logical transition into broader interactive strategy. I have spent the past eight years leading the development, management, execution and measurement of interactive-led programs for a variety of regional, national, and global clients with a range of business objectives. These programs included many aspects of the marketing process – from traditional broadcast and print to promotions, public relations, sponsorship and events. They also gave me the opportunity to coordinate closely with multiple marketing partners to achieve truly integrated results.

This combination of experience along with strong instincts, strategic vision, a eye for creative design and true passion for marketing provide me a unique arsenal from which to draw.
