Howard D. Richards, CFP®’s Summary

I have been addressing the unique needs of individuals and businesses through the financial planning process since 1991 and I have been a Certified Financial Planner™ practitioner since 1995. Prior to that, I spent 18 years in the banking and commercial real estate finance industry.

In addition to being a Certified Financial Planner™ professional, I am also a Certified Financial Educator, CFEd®.

As we are working together, you will experience a new approach to financial planning, based solely on trust, experience and personalization, including dedication to developing lasting relationships.

I truly believe in helping my clients create appropriate financial goals, along with flexible strategies to achieve them, without violating your risk tolerance.

I pride myself on the ability to provide clear, easily understood explanations of financial concepts and solutions with financial education. The personalized program that I provide is a roadmap to working toward a more independent financial future.

From an investment standpoint, it is well known that diversification is a critical component of investment success, but at the same time, I believe that proper planning tools must be employed to assure that the risk/reward characteristics of the portfolio are adhered to. Why take more risk than you need in order to reach your goals?

I enjoy working with all of my clients, building a holistic relationship that truly considers an individuals goals, aspirations and values.

I look forward to meeting new clients and helping them pursue their financial goals utilizing a variety of state of the art techniques and products that focus on an outcome that is not based on a product sale.

Please feel free to email me at hdr@securuswealth.com or visit my website at www.howardrichards.net.
