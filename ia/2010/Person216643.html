Steve Nye’s Summary

For over 20 years, I have enjoyed working with technology firms, senior executives and customers in executive management and leadership positions. My focus in these positions has been revenue and market growth, portfolio expansion, profitability, strategic thought leadership and executive general management for early stage to large Fortune 100 technology businesses. I have led 4 companies to successful exits in between holding executive positions in large publicly traded companies. I have a strong record of execution and results. My career has been centered on leading software businesses or software related businesses serving enterprises, service providers, Federal Government and small businesses.

Steve Nye’s Specialties:

Executive Leadership and Management, Software business, management software, business development, business turn around, investor relations, sales, change management, business model innovation, developing and attracting great talent.
