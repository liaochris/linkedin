Simon Bisson’s Summary

Freelance technology writer and journalist

With a wide background in IT and engineering, I mix deep technical knowledge with the ability to explain complex subjects in simple, easy to understand terms.

Currently available for journalism and technology writing (including commercial white papers and ghostwriting).

On a freelance basis I write about enterprise IT for ZDnet UK, IT Pro, Server Management and IT Expert, developer for PC Plus and Hardcopy, as well as covering web technologies for Web Designer. I occasionally write about consumer IT for TechRadar, Tom's Guide and Tom's Hardware. With Mary Branscombe I blog on enterprise IT (with an emphasis on Microsoft) for ZDnet UK.

I have written technology white papers for many of the world's top IT companies, as well as undertaking commissioned commercial writing for corporate magazines and publications, as well as for newspaper supplements.

Recent deliveries include a series on working with Amazon's cloud computing tools, a guide to software as a service written for the Association of Chartered Accounts for England and Wales, whitepapers for a hosted email management service, sections of the Business Link web site, articles for a supplement to The Times, and coverage of the latest tools and technologies for web designers and developers.

Simon Bisson’s Specialties:

Web services, XML, IT strategy, infrastructure, solutions architectures,service oriented architectures, messaging technologies, mobile computing, IT journalism, technology journalism, white paper authoring, article authoring, media consultancy.
