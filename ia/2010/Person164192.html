Paul Holser’s Summary

My goal is to create software that exhibits high internal and external quality, to improve the lives of its users and its maintainers. I believe that agile software development practices help my teams reach these goals. I am a test-driven development practitioner. I hope that those developers who have never known the joys of a comprehensive, fast-running automated suite of executable behavior specifications for their software project come to know them sooner than later. I want to work with multi-disciplinary teams--developers, testers, SMEs--who want quality software, rapid feedback, and a healthy work/life balance.
