Arthur Hedge’s Summary

Arthur Hedge is the President of Castle Ventures LLC. Castle Ventures is a consulting firm focused on Information Security and Infrastructure Management. We have offices in New Jersey and Connecticut. Castle Ventures is a Premier Partner of Varonis Systems.

Arthur has over 25 years of consulting experience in the Information Technology field with both privately held and publicly traded firms. Before starting Castle Ventures in 2002, he was an executive with several technology consulting firms.

Mr. Hedge is a graduate of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Arthur holds the CISSP certification and an MCP with a focus on SharePoint. He is a member of a number of professional organizations, including AIIM, Information Systems Security Association (ISSA), and the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP). He is active in standards work for AIIM. Mr. Hedge serves as a mentor for Columbia University’s Technology Management program.
