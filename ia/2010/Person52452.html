Davit Adoni’s Summary

Davit has over 14 years of SAP finance and logistics implementations experience, in the public services, retail, consumer packaged goods, chemical, gas, automotive, electronics, higher education, health care, telecommunications and the utilities & energy industries.

Davit is a Senior / Lead consultant and his deep configuration experiences include Project Systems (WBS, network, milestones, scheduling, capacity planning, resource/workforce planning, substitutions/validations, results analysis, settlement), Controlling modules i.e. CO-PA, Product Costing (discreet manufacturing, configure-to-order, engineer-to-order), Profit Center Acct, Cost Center Acct along with their Financials, PLM, CRM, SCM/SRM, HCM and BI/BW complex integration points. He has designed WRICEF (Workflows, Reports, Interfaces, Conversions, Enhancements, Forms) objects throughout his SAP experience.

He has 10 years of industry experience prior working as a consultant on a global electro-technique company. He has proven track of records leading teams of Fortune 500 companies in the global Finance and Logistics implementations, countries include Germany, Canada, Mexico, Singapore, Spain, Japan, Turkey and US. Davit led his clients and consulting team members with his excellent communication, coaching and problem solving skills in blueprint, configuration, testing, training, go-live and support phases successfully.

Davit has substantial skills in project planning, budgeting, forecasting, managing scope, change management, risk management and he is a PMP certified Project Manager.
