Kavi Grace’s Summary

Kavi Grace is an associate in the Business & Technology Sourcing and Corporate & Securities practice groups. Mr. Grace focuses his practice on the representation of public and private companies in the areas of outsourcing, software development, e-commerce and information technology transactions. Mr. Grace has experience negotiating and drafting a broad range of agreements involving software distribution, technology licensing and development, consulting services and business process and operations outsourcing.

Prior to joining Mayer Brown, Kavi Grace worked as an associate in the Technology & Sourcing and Corporate & Securities practice groups of a leading international law firm. Prior to entering the legal field, Mr. Grace founded and ran two web development companies and held a variety of technical consulting and software engineering positions in the Los Angeles and Silicon Valley dot-com industries.

Kavi Grace’s Specialties:

IT Outsourcing; Business Process Outsourcing; Commercial Contracting; Technology Licensing; Technology Transactions; General Corporate Law
