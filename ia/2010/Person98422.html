Jim Haviland’s Summary

Highly accomplished entrepreneurial executive with startup, turn-around, and merger experience and proven strengths applying marketing savvy, engineering discipline, business acumen, and project management and agile methodology to improve the quality and efficiency of projects and operations.

I am the marketing and sales Swiss Army Knife: message, brand, process, technology, execution, lead gen, development, negotiation, close.

15 years in business development and consultative sales of complex offerings to corporate and higher education clients. Product manager of profitable, enduring products and services for business and consumers in SaaS, consulting, IT services, media, and software driving annual sales >$150MM. Insightful organization builder, managing hierarchical and matrixed multi-discipline teams as large as 50. Led development of over 300 websites and IT projects for ecommerce, portals, data analysis, and entertainment for the higher-education, consumer, business, and government sectors.

Creative technologist, mentor and coach with vast start-up (30+), product launch (200+), and new market(countless) experience working with greatest impact in the innovation of products, sales, and marketing. Significant experiences in media development, eCommerce, integrated marketing, higher education, advertising, and management consulting.

I'm always looking for companies and projects that seek excellence.

I love a challenge. People call me when there's a difficult problem that they don't know how to solve. I am passionately curious. I have chosen breadth over depth, but I am always striving to be the absolute best at whatever I am doing.

I am currently working with clients on projects in educational technology, mobile technology, and educational media and I'm helping clients move to more agile operational methods.
