Geoff Baldwin’s Summary

In IT for over 40 years - when I started paper tape and punched cards were the norm!
I have a background in programming and systems design which was put to good use during 35 years selling software to Universities, Government and Business.
Having spent my whole working life in small businesses I understand and appreciate the need for technology that helps get the job done rather than technology for technology's sake.
Beaufort Networks provides a part time IT manager to organisations that cannot justify a person in a full time role.
I provide advice on appropriate IT infrastructure including e-Mail systems, anti virus systems, Backup and Disaster Recovery policies and strategies; and then provide support for the hardware, software and users.
