Michael Bishop’s Summary

Michael Bishop has been engineering award-winning recordings for more than 30 years and has received EIGHT Grammy® Awards (2008, 2007, 2006, 2004, 2002 and 1997) including FIVE for Best-Engineered Recording and one for Best Surround Album. Michael has recorded many major orchestral, jazz, blues, and pop recordings, plus notable independent projects. The former Chief Recording Engineer for Telarc Records, Michael now records and produces independent projects through Five/Four Productions, Ltd.. (www.Recording.Pro)

Having worked on pop quadraphonic mixes in the early 1970s, Michael applied that early experience, combined with his recent surround techniques, to produce some of the most notable 6.0 surround music releases to the acclaim of music fans, audiophiles, and reviewers.

Michael’s recording credits include The James Gang, Wild Cherry, Pere Ubu, Dizzy Gillespie, Ray Brown, Gerry Mulligan, Joe Williams, Ray Charles, Chucho Valdez, Monty Alexander, the Count Basie Orchestra, Hiromi Uehara, Stanley Clarke, Jim Hall, Pat Metheny, Al Di Meola, Bonnie Raitt, Isaac Hayes, the Memphis Horns, Bobby Womack, Liza Minnelli, Clark Terry, James Moody, Rosemary Clooney, Junior Wells, Carlos Santana, Oscar Peterson, Dave Brubeck, Dianna Krall, Shirley Horn, Maria Muldaur, Mel Torme, Ernest Ranglin, Andre Watt, Robert Shaw/Atlanta Symphony Orchestra and Chorus, Erich Kunzel/Cincinnati Pops Orchestra, Lang Lang, the Mormon Tabernacle Choir, Paavo Jarvi/Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra, and Robert Spano/Atlanta Symphony Orchestra and Chorus.

Michael is a member of AES, NARAS (where he serves on the Producer & Engineer Wing Advisory Council), ASCAP, AQHA, and NRHA. Michael and his wife enjoy participating in Team Penning & Sorting and Reining with their American Quarter Horses.

Michael Bishop’s Specialties:

Michael has recorded and produced music across numerous, varied genres in the 30-plus years of his career. Having worked in every professional audio recording format, Michael is adept at bringing World-Class production techniques to a Real-World production budget. His specialties include audio production, engineering, editing, mixing and mastering for LP, CD, SACD, DVD, Blu-Ray, Film Soundtrack and Commercial productions.
