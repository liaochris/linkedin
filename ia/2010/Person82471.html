Stan Holcomb’s Summary

Stan Holcomb is a Vice President with Global Business Management, Sales, Business Development, and Market Management expertise. He has led profitable businesses achieving $1.6B in sales, and led new business creation from concept to $200M of revenue.

Stan's expertise spans both Services and Products sold to Telecommunications Providers and Enterprises. He is a globally mobile executive with experience living and working in several international regions. Stan has demonstrated leadership transforming teams to develop integrated product and service solutions that fully address a customer's needs.
