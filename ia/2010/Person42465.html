Colin Loretz’s Summary

My unique background in software development, design and marketing has provided me with the equally unique opportunity to build things people actually want to use. At Lively Labs we want to show the world that business software does not have to suck. We we develop custom solutions using the Salesforce.com, WordPress and iOS platforms.

I'm the co-founder of the Reno Collective, Reno's first coworking space. By combining a shared office environment with the talent and enthusiasm of freelancers, independents, creatives and entrepreneurs, Reno Collective offers full office amenities, hosted events, and social/business opportunities for the independent and creative individuals in Reno.
