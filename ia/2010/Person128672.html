Luigi Catzola’s Summary

Luigi Catzola Dr., received the Electronic Engineering degree from the University of Naples “Federico II”, Italy, in 1977. He is specialized in Computer Science, Program Management, and Complex Dynamic Systems. Since 1978 he works on large IT projects for the markets of Defence, Space, and Telecommunication. Currently he is also an Associate Researcher at Semeion Research Center dealing with Complex Dynamic Systems and Artificial Intelligence.
