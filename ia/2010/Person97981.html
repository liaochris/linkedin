Jill Griffin’s Summary

Throughout her career, Jill has strategically chosen opportunities to round out her skills while continually challenging herself. She has garnered such expertise as digital marketing, social media, sales development, brand and ad product innovation, strategic media planning, brand strategy, cross-platform planning, website launches, mobile marketing and commerce plus social media marketing initiatives.

In 2005, she received the honor of being named one of the "25 Women to Watch" by Advertising Age.

Jill has served in senior marketing and digital strategy roles for Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia, Anomaly, a leading brand strategy and intellectual property shop, OMD and the Starcom MediaVest Group. Additionally, she has independently consulted to many large organizations, small business and holistic health practices.
