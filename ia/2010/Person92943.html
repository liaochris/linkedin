Jeffrey Barry’s Summary

We provide legal counsel both to individuals in connection with their tax and estate planning needs, as well as to businesses, beginning with the entity formation process and providing assistance with day-to-day operations, and continuing through an exit from the business through a sale or other liquidity event.

Jeffrey Barry’s Specialties:

Tax planning for businesses and individuals, business formation and transactions, mergers and acquistions, criminal record expungement, elder care and abuse prevention, estate planning
