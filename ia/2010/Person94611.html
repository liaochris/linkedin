Jennifer Ferguson-Mitchell’s Summary

Jennifer is a results-driven international communications expert with more than 15 years experience. She is the owner/president of Chanler Communications – a consulting company that provides committed strategic and tactical communications and public relations support to organizations striving for social change, in a way that drives real results.

Prior to this, Jennifer served for more than eight years as Deputy Director of Comms. and as Global Comms. Mgr. for IFAW. In these roles, she led HQ-based comms staff and oversaw all IFAW wildlife comms and media-relations activities worldwide.

She is also an accomplished video producer, having produced, written and directed documentary shorts, tv commercials, PSAs, fundraising videos, and podcasts; and carried out video assignments that have taken her around the globe from China to Cameroon.

Immediately prior to joining IFAW in 1999, Jennifer served as a regional newspaper editor in the U.S. Between 1988 and 1998, Jennifer lived in Nairobi, Kenya, where for six years she worked in the East Africa bureau of Visnews (the world’s leading broadcast news content provider at the time).

Jennifer went on to establish her own communications company in Nairobi, Chanler Marketing & Publishing. She was then hired by the UNDCP to head up their public information activities for Eastern and Southern Africa, covering 13 nations.

Jennifer holds a MA in Communications from Marist College, a BA from Wheaton College in Mass., and has also studied at Paris IV University (La Sorbonne). She is a dual national of the U.S. and France; and speaks English, French and some Kiswahili.

Jennifer, together with her former IFAW colleagues, has also won several PR industry awards including the Bulldog Gold Award for outstanding media comms activities, and the PRNews Platinum Award.

She is a member of the National Press Photographer’s Association (NPPA) and the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA).

Media relations, non-profit communications, cause branding and communications, cause related marketing, public relations, producer, writer, photographer, strategic planning -- special focus on US, European, Caribbean, and African markets.
