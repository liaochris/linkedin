Michael Perry’s Summary

Managing multi-million dollar construction projects and overseeing the quality commitment of trade workers and subcontractors, for both commercial and residential projects, are some of the many ways I have served an instrumental role in the success of past employers. With more than 14 years of experience leading and coordinating activities of multiple trades, I am now seeking the opportunity to join a company in need of a highly qualified Construction Project Manager.

A sample of my highlights while serving as Project Manager and previous roles include:

· Tracking all reporting and financial controls required for project:
Monthly Development Reports, Status Reports, & Cash Flow reports.
· Bid solicitation and contract administration for various commercial projects.
· Estimating takeoffs, bid solicitation, bid qualification, shop drawings, materials ordering, and coordination with field personnel.
· Strong communication skills, project management, contracts administration, tracking contacts, field work directives, change orders, RFI's, and submittals.
· Managing daily construction activities through procurement of materials, mobilization of manpower, project scheduling, field inspections and quality control.
· Highly organized with a great ability to multi-task, work well under pressure.
· Proficient with Prolog, Microsoft Excel, Word, Project, and Powerpoint, as well as Primavera scheduling software.
