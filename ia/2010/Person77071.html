Grady Carden’s Summary

I am a paralegal and consultant to the legal profession. I assist in all areas of legal practice - client intake, legal research, drafting and filing of legal documents, case management, use of technology to aid in management of a law office, and accounting practices and billing.

On a consulting basis, much of my time is spent developing legal theories and case development for matters involving the financial industry, corporate litigation, and personal injury. Examples of past experience include credit defamation and damages, mortgage fraud, illegal collection practices, stopping foreclosure, and bank fraud. Other areas of focus are estate planning issues that invovles trust, wills, and probate issues.

I am a law student at Thomas Goode Jones School of Law in Montgomery, Alabama. I will graduate law school in May, 2010. I plan to practice law in Montgomery and Birmingham, Alabama with a focus on corporate law and litigation, bankruptcy, and plaintiff litigation.
