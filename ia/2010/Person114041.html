Kelly Quattlebaum’s Summary

Kelly Quattlebaum is the President of Global Marcoms, an international management consulting firm that offers marketing, public relations, business development and sales services. Kelly has seven years of corporate, business-to-business experience in the Caribbean and Latin America and is well versed with the various cultures and customs of the region.

As the regional marketing manager for Intec, a global software provider of operations and business support systems, she focused on defining and implementing marketing and communication strategy that targeted telecom operators and service providers throughout the Latin American and Caribbean region. In her role she was also responsible for developing strong relationships with channel partners such as Sun, HP and eServGlobal; clients including América Móvil, Cable & Wireless, Digicel, Telefónica and Vivo; as well as various regional media outlets and local suppliers.

Kelly has years of experience in the technology and telecom industry, as well as a deep understanding of intercultural communication and appreciation for diversity. A linguist by nature, she is fluent in English (native language), Spanish and Brazilian Portuguese. Kelly graduated summa cum laude from Kennesaw State University where she received her Bachelor of Science degree in communication, a minor in chemistry, and studied computer science.
