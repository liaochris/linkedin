Ali Syme’s Summary

I specialise in business strategies over the web - e-commerce and technology projects, new market entry and consulting on markets such as bioplastics, polymers, tech start-ups, SaaS distribution, Worldwide SEO projects, cleantech ventures, "green marketing" and various other aspects of technology and internet industries.

Primarily I deal in long term marketing contracts - representing large organisations marketing rather than their sales in the UK and Europe. I'm also involved in market entry proposals and investment strategies.

Industries I've worked in focus around innovation - plastics and packaging, bio-technology (bioplastics), media networks and internet marketing platforms. I also have experience in marketing and driving sales of SaaS and niche CMS and e-com systems.
