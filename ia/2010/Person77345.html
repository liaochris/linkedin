————| Portraits & Events

————| Motion Picture Set Stills

As a partner in GrayPictures, LLC, I bring close to three decades of experience as a still photographer to client projects -- from producing images for publications, brochures, documentary editorials, as well as a comprehensive stock library of portraits, landscapes and editorial.

My professional and personal stills are heavily influenced by decades of work in the motion picture industry, with camera department experience that includes underwater filming, remote camera cranes, time-lapse, special effects and high-speed film/video production with some of the best cameramen and directors in the world.

Today, I continue my work on feature films as a Set Still Photographer, in addition to providing customized imagemaking services to corporate, individual and industrial clients.
