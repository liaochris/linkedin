Dr. Gary Donaldson’s Summary

Board Certified Chiropractic Orthopedist serving Milwaukee area since 1984. Emphasis on spinal rehabilitation and maintenance through postural correction and non-surgical disc decompression.

Dr. Gary Donaldson’s Specialties:

Treatment of herniated discs, spinal stenosis, sciatica, arm and leg nerve pain, degenerative disc disease, work and auto injuries.
