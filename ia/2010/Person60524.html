Duane Evarts, CSTE’s Summary

I am currently the Chief Engineer of the EchoStar/Dish Network Corporate Broadcast Studio. Now with over three decades of experience in video and multi-media production, broadcasting and businesses operations, I look forward to the challenge of not only maintaining, but creating the future High Definition Corporate Video Production facilities for Dish Network L.L.C. and EchoStar Satellite L.L.C., a Fortune 250 company.

Duane Evarts, CSTE’s Specialties:

I recieved my first FCC license in 1978 and have worked in the audio/video production and engineering industry for more than 20 years. I became a member of the Society of Broadcast Engineers in 2004 as a CBT (Certified Broadcast Technician) and earned my CSTE (Certified Senior Television Engineer) certification in 2009.
