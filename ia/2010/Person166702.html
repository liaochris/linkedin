Punit Soni’s Summary

Currently, I am the lead Product Manager responsible for Google's Mobile Apps effort.
I have been a Product Manager at Google for the past 2.5 years and have since launched two products in the News/Books domain, and now am building one of its most exciting products in the Mobile space.
Before that, I graduated from Wharton with an MBA in finance and entreprenuerial Management. I am starting work at a Product Manager at Google. The last job I had was as an Investment Manager Intern at Intel Capital. At ICAP, I built a telecom valuation tool, worked on a $8M broadband wireless investment and did an investment thesis in mobile services. I also conducted financial/strategic diligence on 10+ deals and created a analytical tool to assist in valuations for Broadband wireless companies.
At Wharton, I have won the VCIC competition, the Wharton Tech Case Competition and am an associate with the University Venture Fund. I also run the Wharton Technology Conference and am doing an India-focused Investment Field project.
Before school, I was an managing/developing products in Cadence Design Systems. I have years of experience in Semiconductor space and am leveraging my technology background coupled with my MBA to do something in the VC/entrepreneurship area.
I have also worked in the ground floor of a very early stage startup which used EDA algorithms in the space of taxonomical software.
