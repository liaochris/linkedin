George is an experienced executive with 20+ years in senior management with success in multiple areas including business development and strategic partnerships, marketing strategy and sales management with an emphasis on growth and expansion for small to mid-size companies as well as with leading start-ups.

His current venture is growing a large recreational series of tournaments across the United States while raising awareness for the Prevent Cancer Foundation through the Bad Beat on Cancer initiative.
