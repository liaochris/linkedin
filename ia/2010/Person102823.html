John O Gorman’s Summary

A sales trained marketer: 13 year experience of sales and business development in the information technology sector. After graduating with a bachelor of commerce business degree and a postgraduate qualification in marketing from NUI Galway, held several senior positions in Ireland and North America with multinational software companies (Compaq/Hewlett Packard, Eontec and Phoenix Technology Group) targeting the I.T. and the financial services sector in Europe and North America. Have recently completed the full-time International MBA in The Michael Smurfit Business School, UCD, Ireland.

Set-up Jog (www.jog.ie) Commercial Development Ltd in June 2006 and The ASG Group (www.theasggroup.com) in 2007. We help organisations accelerate sales growth.

Clients include: Exaxe, Rockall Technologies, The Playprint Group, Risaris, Daysha Consulting, GoldTier, PolarLake, Delphi Technologies, Finantix, Acorn Life, Ergo, Cora Systems, Visor, Enterprise Ireland.
