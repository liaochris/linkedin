Ben Stone’s Summary

I started RPO Consulting because it is my personal belief that the relationships between organizations and people are crucial for success. Since these relationships will determine your business success or failure, the amount of attention you pay to them is crucial. I offer consultation services to address these relationships in two areas: Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) & Web Strategy Consulting.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS OUTSOURCING

I focus on developing and delivering solid recruitment processes for your organization. From designing positions all the way through onboarding new employees, I will design and run your recruitment process with an eye towards hiring people that match up to your brand promise.

- Sourcing potential candidates
- Recruiting candidates
- Reference checking
- Negotiating offers
- Relocation support
- Tracking recruitment metrics


WEB STRATEGY

I can help your organization develop a strategy to make online communication more effective, efficient, and ultimately more profitable. It is no longer a question of if your business needs an online communication strategy; it's a question of how comprehensive that strategy should be. The manner in which your organization approaches online communication will increasingly determine your success or failure.

- Analyze your current Internet communication
- Develop a listening strategy to tap into meaningful online conversations
- Design and build out your web presence
- Training to get your team engaged in the online conversation
