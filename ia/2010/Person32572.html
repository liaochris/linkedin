Carlos Paz’s Summary

Multi-functional manager and pharmaceutical scientist/engineer with over 20 years pharmaceutical technology experience. Proven track record of leadership and execution coupled with strong technical background and hands-on experience. Expertise in managing and executing R&D Product & Process Development and Technology Transfer projects interfacing with Manufacturing & Quality Operations, to bring products rapidly to market. Strong oral and written communication skills and building working relationships and team facilitation.

2. Immediate & Modified Release Solid Dosage Formulations
3. Liquid & Semi-solid Dosage Forms
4. New Product/Process Development, Process Improvement, Technology Transfer & Production Troubleshooting
5. Pilot Plant Operations & CTM Manufacturing
6. Process Validation
7. cGMPs, SUPAC, QbD & Compliance
