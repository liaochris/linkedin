Stephanie Mack’s Summary

As a marketing consultant in Antigua, I have had the opportunity to work in a number of different industries, including travel and tourism, banking, wholesale supply, hotels, restaurants, and real estate sales.

With research and writing as personal passions, my favorite task is preparing comprehensive business and marketing plans, inclusive of situational, competitive and product mix analyses, as well as sales projections and financial analysis.

I particularly enjoy working with a small client base on mid to long term projects as that usually includes being involved in the implementation of plans and ensuring that clients achieve the expected results.
