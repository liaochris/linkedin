Mike Harman’s Summary

Responsible for technical support, development and sustaining engineering for high volume wafer fabrication.

Over 24 years of engineering experience driven to improve and stabilize process and product yields through the use of Statistical Process Controls (SPC) and Design of Experiments (DOE).

Improved manufacturing techniques, introduced new process equipment to production.
Continually made incremental, measurable improvements in processes.

Interfaced with production, and other engineering groups including design, product and manufacturing to ensure the most efficient processing techniques.

Solved production and equipment problems, wrote process specifications and procedures. Improved process control.

Always looked to improve work, products, functions, and processes through on-going analysis.

Communicated and shared new information, learnings, and methods with cross-functional team members to achieve organizational goals.
