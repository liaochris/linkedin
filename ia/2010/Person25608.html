Bob Oord’s Summary

Marketing, Entrepreneurship & Internet are recurring theme's throughout my career. That's why I started my own online marketing agency; Leadfrog.

Leadfrog is dedicated to realize effective online marketing campaigns with one central goal: increase the sales results of it's customers.

As a consultant I like to write about the business issues I have worked on. My online marketing articles have appeared on the Marketingdesk of Kluwer and for CostFocus I wrote a book on Cost Reduction Tactics, also published by Kluwer.
