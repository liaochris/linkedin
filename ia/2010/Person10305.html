Amy Wallace’s Summary

Since graduating from the University of Georgia with a degree in marketing, I have had a variety of sales and marketing positions. I started out with inside sales of web-based products for accountants and physician practices. Realizing that I preferred to work with people in person rather than over the phone, I switched my focus to customer service in the property management industry. After a couple of years as a leasing consultant, I am ready to find an exciting career focused strictly on the marketing of business products and services (B2B or B2C). I am particularly interested in technology industries, software and website companies. I plan to go back to school within the next five years to get a graduate degree in marketing communications or marketing research.
