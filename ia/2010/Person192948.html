Steven Swimmer’s Summary

Steven Swimmer is an innovative media professional with deep experience in the fields of Web, Digital Media and Motion Pictures. His work has spanned strategy, management, creative and technology.

Swimmer has hands-on experience in online strategy, product management, technology management, creative direction, production, analysis, content, information architecture and integration.

His digital media experience includes work on major entertainment and museum Web destinations, digital video, imaging, museum kiosks, corporate presentations and electronic gaming.

Swimmer is a filmmaker who co-produced the hit independent feature film, Gabriela. His experience includes writing, packaging, developing and marketing feature films. He has directed music videos, documentaries and short subjects. Swimmer does public speaking, most recently speaking at USC School of Cinematic Arts about careers in interactive entertainment.

Swimmer has a portfolio of premium domain names and has sold one domain for $26,000. His domain names include prime keywords and short brandable names.

In his spare time, Swimmer is a stand-up comedian, who performs at the Comedy Store, Hollywood on the main stage. Swimmer is a photographer, guitar player and an avid music fan.
