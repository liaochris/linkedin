John Christie’s Summary

I have been a principle in my firm since 2003. Our office handles all litigation matters. My practice involves small business and contruction matters, including litigating many issues in those fields. I have successfully litigated several contruction matters, including sharing in a Million dollar verdict against a private utility company for damages to my client's property.

In addition, I have defended companies and individuals. I have tried numorous cases to verdict, successfully obtaining defense verdicts on behalf of clients who were improperly sued, as well as obtaining six figure verdicts on behalf of clients. Our offices represents individuals, companies and insurance carriers, as both plaintiffs and defendants.

The goal of my practice is to find the best solution for each client, not to force clients into a solution that does not meet that client's specific needs. Sometimes this means mediation, settlement or other pre trial and pre suit resolution, while other matters need to be pursued vigorously to trial.

John Christie’s Specialties:

Negotiation, litigation, counsel & advice pre-suit and in an effort to avoid litigation, most small business matters, construction claims. Extensive actual trial experience, having been lead trial counsel for several multi million dollar clients in trial.
