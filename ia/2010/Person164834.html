Pamela Stephens’s Summary

Highly regarded, innovative leader with over twenty years’ corporate and consulting experience in all aspects of talent management and development. Led numerous large-scale, complex change management efforts that were implemented across world class, global organizations (including Fortune 100). Worked with all types of organizations (public/private, profit/non-profit, domestic/international, government, and consulting) and many different industries (Information Technology, hospitality, healthcare, education). Employs strategic approach to deliver high-impact organization and talent development programs ensuring alignment with overall business objectives. Expertise spans the areas of organization & human capital strategy, competency models, talent/learning development (including curriculum design, complete instructional systems life cycle, all delivery methods, coaching/mentoring, leadership & career development), talent management, succession planning, technology and measurement systems, change management, communications and project management.

Member of numerous professional and research organizations, serving on several advisory boards and committees (ASTD (National & Mt Diablo Chapter Board of Directors – VP Membership), SHRM Professional, Service Excellence Committee - Aging Services of California, Bersin & Associates Research Member, Toolbox for HR Founding Members Council, eLearning Guild, Elearning! Magazines' Research Advisory Panel, Federal eGovernment Working Group, HUD Section 508 Advisory Board, OPM eLearning Systems Committee).
