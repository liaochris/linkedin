Anthony Trott’s Summary

Anthony Trott is an Artist, specializing in pencil, charcoal, pastel and acrylic.

An animal lover, Anthony spends his weekends at Pets Alive Animal Sanctuary in Middletown, NY, tending to the many animals who live there.

Much of Mr. Trott's professional experience is based around Document Management, Support and Client Services.

Future goals of Anthony Trott's include starting a non-profit promoting public education and advocacy of so-called "bully breeds", as well as a non-profit for promotion of the arts and fine artists.
