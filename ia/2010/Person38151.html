Chris L Wagner’s Summary

I have done many things in my life from training Bank of America employees how to use the ATM when when the technology was new to an esthetician in a swanky sports club, and all roads in between. By far my most rewarding adventure thus far has been owning my own business.

When I decided to find a career that came from the heart, my true entrepreneurial spirit came out. In 2005 I started working for a local pet sitting service and by 2006, Waggy's Pet Sitting Service was born. I combined my life long love of dogs with a viable career choice. I now have 12 contractors working for me, and we found a wonderful niche in providing dog boarding in a private family home.

As if that wasn't enough, I decided to open an on line pet store, and Waggy's Eco Pet Products was launched in October of 2009. We offer primarily eco friendly pet products, but will expand into other areas also. I strongly believe in the entrepreneurial spirit of individuals and have decided to carry lines from smaller, upcoming companies that only manufacture products in the United States.

My goals are to become a place where people can buy high quality pet products and also be a place where they can get educated on pet related matters.

Chris L Wagner’s Specialties:

Providing loving care for animals for the animals I board, helping people with problems, great customer service.
