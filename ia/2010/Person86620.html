Jackie Cameron’s Summary

Since setting up Cameron Consulting in 2004 my focus has been on working with others to help them maximise their potential in a variety of ways including

+ coaching in using social media for business - including one to one coaching on Linkedin, Twitter and blogging.

+communication skills - public speaking training ( including with school students), speaking and listening skills for business in both open and employer supported programmes

+ individual coaching - new graduates, managers - from first time through to experienced , business owners and during the recruitment process including CV review and preparation for interview

+group coaching - young people in school on enterprise projects, students on university management development programmes


My continuing aim is to work with others to make a difference.

Jackie Cameron’s Specialties:

I have specific experience of working with young people in schools on a number of projects from which they learn about their own personal leadership and skills - all of which will be attractive to future employers.
