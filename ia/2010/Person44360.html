Jeffrey Smith’s Summary

Crash Avenue is a publicity firm fueled by a passion for good music and art. We were founded in 2004, and since then, have become a widely recognized workhorse in the evolving media and music industry. At the same time, Crash Avenue maintains strong ties to our DIY roots. We don’t just support our clients through our PR efforts and wealth of experience – we develop an artist-focused music community.

Simply put, Crash Avenue can assist you in all of your press needs, be it your forthcoming album release, upcoming US tour, or your festival event. If we feel like it’s something we can knock out of the park for you, we’re on it!

We’re more about action than a bunch of fancy jargon and unrealistic expectations. It’s tough knowing that there are over a hundred thousand albums released every year and tons of shows going on in every major market, every night of the week. With that information overload, how does your project make like Black Flag and “rise above”? This is where Crash Avenue comes in to offer professional support, so you can spend more time concentrating on what you do (whatever that is, and that’s never our business).

So, if you’d like more information regarding our history, what’s on the horizon, or how we can help your label, your event, or your band… let’s talk. We like to talk… it’s what we do for a living over here. Well, and action, too.
