====Professional Profile====
With over 13 years of experience, I am among the privilege few professionals who have managed and demonstrated true integrated digital marketing thinking.

As we see a shift in traditional media to one that is digitally & socially-driven, I lead global brands to understand better their customer and community needs, redefine their business goals, integrate their marketing practices with digital technologies to formulate and deliver successful strategic plans that will impact their businesses.

As the demands from brand owners continue to push for marketing programs to demonstrate measurable outcomes and increase in the returns of marketing investments; understanding and taking advantage of how customers think and behave have been key to achieving such marketing effectiveness. I lead and manage teams that believe in having current insights to our customers understanding their behavior and what they want, are some of the most essential components when planning any integrated marketing programs as this will drive both engagement and influence towards the brands.

The progression of web technologies today allows us to leverage on such insights that are both current and relevant where the use of data (behavioral, contextual, geographical, and demographical) in both traditional and the social web collected continually optimizes integrated digital marketing to work towards the business objectives set before us.

I always keep my door of opportunity open, to work with a dynamic and forward looking organization that will provide an environment to push holistic marketing practices that will position their brands in the forefront of their industry through a collective thought leadership.
