Haiko Meelis’s Summary

Management professional with over 10 years of experience in helping businesses to define their strategy or goals and translate it into a concrete, realistic plan that will work and help to implement it. So: action and results. Worked mainly in telecoms and internet environments. Industry experience in telecoms, media, entertainment, electronics and banking. Direct experience ranging from start-up companies to corporate environments.

Particularly interested in the definition and implementation of new concepts, products, services and processes that use today's technology to realize previously unthought-of opportunities.

Married and 2 daughters. Holding a MSc in Computer Science of the University of Leiden, a MSc in Business of the Nyenrode Business University. Currently studying Philosophy at Birkbeck College, University of London.

Knowledgeable person, professional attitude, very driven, pragmatic and entrepreneurial. Able to draw a realistic plan from imperfect or scattered information in an uncertain environment (expert intuition).

Accredited associate of the Institute for Independent Business (IIB), which gives access to an international network of over 5000 business executives in every industry imaginable, who can provide me with their specific expertise when needed.
