Gary Evans’s Summary

Gary Evans is a Certified Foot Surgeon by the American Board of Podiatric Surgeons and a successful doctor in his own podiatry practice. Dr. Evans holds an array of additional podiatry skills, including board certification in Podiatric Orthopedics and a certification in Extracorporeal Shockwave Therapy.

Dr. Evans treats so many patients with heel pain, he often refers to his practice as the ultimate Heel Treatment Center. While there are many successful treatment options, Dr. Evans has taken the time to become profecient in the newer technology to help those hard to treat patients. Extracorporeal Shockwave Therapy is one of the highly specialized podiatry treatments, and Gary Evans's expertise and certification in the rare practice puts him on a short list of medical experts who can perform it on patients. Gary Evans uses the therapy as an alternative to traditional surgical procedures, using high frequency shock waves aimed at areas of foot pain to stimulate healing within the body. The treatment is non-invasive, lasts about 30 minutes under light anesthesia, and allows Evans to remove chronic heel pain without conducting lengthy surgery.

Gary Evans earned his Doctor of Podiatric Medicine (DPM) in 1983 from the New York College of Podiatric Medicine. After working in the field for over two decades, Evans has become a specialist in sports medicine, foot orthotics, and forefoot surgery. Many of Evans' sports-related treatments involve runners. Evans himself is an avid runner and athlete, and he has coached various youth sports programs for over 10 years.

Gary Evans’s Specialties:

Most patients treated by Evans suffer from heel and arch pain, but he handles a variety of other medical problems, including bunions, which can create intense pain and stress on the foot and make it difficult to move normallyand wear normal shoes. In order to combat the bunion problem, Evans has become a specialist in the Triplane Bunionectomy, an innovative procedure that removes bunions with a thoroughness and efficiency that is unmatched by most standard procedures.
