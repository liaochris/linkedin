Bernie Rushe’s Summary

Cordatus is one of Ireland's longest established (2001) lean manufacturing, lean service, and six sigma consulting and training companies in Ireland. In addition to managing Cordatus, I specialise in working with senior management teams to develop a vision of organisation competitiveness and a practical lean six sigma transformation roadmap to deliver that vision. The Cordatus mission is to enhance the competitiveness of client organisations.

Bernie Rushe’s Specialties:

- Business development
- Team leader/people skills
- Lean manufacturing and lean service transformation - programme management in a variety of sectors, including pharmaceutical, medical device, automotive manufacturing, and financial services.
- Six sigma implementation - trained to Black Belt level.
- Supply Chain Management - first class honours master’s degree in SCM.
