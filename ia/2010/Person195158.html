Steve Kapaun’s Summary

After more than 10 year of human capital consulting experience with Accenture (Andersen Consulting at the time) and IBM, Steve Kapaun founded TrainingPros in 1997. For over 10 years, TrainingPros has been the premier provider of human capital consultants in Atlanta, Boston, Charlotte-Raleigh, Chicago, Dallas-Ft. Worth, Florida, New York/New Jersey and Washington, DC.
