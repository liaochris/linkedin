Ro Malik’s Summary

As Co-Founder and Managing Broker of Green Brick Realty, Ro Malik now heads a firm that offers a wide array of services including development, asset management, acquisition, sales and leasing. Ro Malik spent nearly 8 years in lending for one the largest privately held mortgage banking companies in the mid-west. As one of the Director’s of the Bank he oversaw a division that averaged close to $100 Million per year in closings. Ro’s decade of experience in real estate finance allowed him to transition seamlessly into real estate sales where his focus remained on creating personal client service and exceeding expectations. His client’s not only receive his experience and expertise in real estate but they also have someone who can truly guide them in the area of lending.

As a driven, results-oriented, leader with a passion for real estate Ro Malik decided to leave his previous position and start up his own Real Estate and Development Company. “I have been investing in real estate for years and I have a true passion for the business. Starting up my own business and helping clients to find their dream homes or invest in real estate was a natural transition for me. Every time I walk into a new property or I see a For Sale sign I get excited. I feel truly blessed because I have found what I love to do in life.”
Green Brick Realty started as a conversation at a local neighborhood coffee shop with his now business partner. The vision was to create a realty company that would give back to the city and communities. Ro wanted to give back a portion of every sale to Green organizations dedicated to beautifying our wonderful city of Chicago. Ro felt that although it was important to grow a successful business it was equally important to give back as the business grows.
