Jourdi de Werd’s Summary

Over 20 years of diversified financial experience including the completion of a large variety of transactions including cross-border and domestic mergers and acquisitions, initial and secondary public offerings, leveraged and management buyouts, venture and mezzanine capital private placements, merchant banking, restructuring engagements, high yield debt and equity placements.

For the 14 years prior to founding de Werd Capital Partners, LLC, was a co-founder and Managing Director of Greif & Co., a Los Angeles investment bank which had completed over $10 billion of mergers and acquisition and other corporate finance transactions in those 14 years.

Holds an MBA in Entrepreneurship from the University of Southern California Graduate School of Business Administration and was recognized as the Outstanding Entrepreneur Graduate Student of the year in 1984. Holds a BS, with highest honors, from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo.

Regularly authors articles on investment banking and corporate finance topics for industry publications and lectured in the field for the Marshall School of Business at USC, the USC Family Business Program, the Los Angeles Times Symposium for Small Business, the CalTech/MIT Venture Forum, the American Management Association, LAVA (Los Angeles Venture Association), Harvard Business School Association of Orange County, California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo and the Anderson School of Business at UCLA.

Former President of the Board of Trustees of The Family School, an independent, CAIS Accredited, elementary school located in Los Olivos, California. Currently serves on the Superintendent's Economic Advisory Committee for the Santa Ynez Valley Union High School District in Santa Ynez, California.
