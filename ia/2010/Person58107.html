Don Dougherty’s Summary

It is highly unusual to have experience in image advertising, direct response television and direct mail marketing. I do. Very few creative people cross over from doing work at brand agencies to direct marketing, let alone create award-winning campaigns in both disciplines. I have.

Ultimately this cross-over experience led me to conclude that even brand advertising could (and should) be measurable and all direct response efforts should further the brand. My current goal is to create campaigns that are fully integrated – applying my image advertising expertise, direct marketing tactics and new media marketing experience to build seamless brand-building campaigns that drive results.

I am completely channel agnostic and truly cross-channel knowledgeable. This rare experience makes me uniquely qualified to build creative strategies that suit the new results-oriented traditional/digital hybrid campaigns that will be the required norm for all advertising agencies in the very near future.
