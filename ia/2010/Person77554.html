Gregory Clark’s Summary

Gregory (Greg) Clark is a senior procurement professional with more than twenty years experience in all aspects of Supply Chain from demand management through to Customer Engagement. He has extensive background in Manufacturing, Service Industries, Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and the Broader Public Sector.

Currently Mr. Clark is engaged as management consultant to public and private sector with specific focus on organizational transformation.

Mr. Clark has held various senior roles overseeing procurement and supply side activities in both BPO and Manufacturing for global organizations and is well versed in ERP solutions, process improvement and strategic development.

Mr. Clark holds the Purchasing Management Association of Canada’s designation of C.P.P. and its US equivalent the CPM.
