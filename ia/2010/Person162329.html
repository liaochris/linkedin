Mike Robinson’s Summary

Permit Place gets permits and entitlements nationwide. Our clients include retailers, restaurants, banks, hotels, recycling centers, asset managers, private equity firms, REIT’s, brokers, law firms, architects, developers and governmental agencies.

Our core competency is project managing land use, entitlement and permit expediting for retailers, restaurants, banks, hotels, recycling centers, telecommunications and home builders. In business since 2004, with offices in Los Angeles, Seattle and San Francisco, and permit expediters all over the country, we can help you get your permits nationwide.
