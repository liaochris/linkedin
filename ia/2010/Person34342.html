Christie Barkan’s Summary

I started my career in fashion and finally had an opportunity to move to London and live my dream. At this time, I worked designing clothes, managing a staff of 40, organizing fashion week and instructing photo shoots. I soon realized that this would only take me so far in my career. After my return to the States, I found that my love for people and desire to succeed has led me to sales. The Creative Group is perfect for me as I not only work placing 'creative' professionals into freelance positions; I get the opportunity to work directly with my clients and manage a division.

The Creative Group is a Fortune 500 company and I staff the same type of organizations. I also get the pleasure to work with some of the top ad and corporations around.
