    Estonian Ecotourism Association - ESTECAS

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    September 1996 — Present (14 years )

    ESTECAS connects individuals, organisations and authorities responsible for tourism development. The main goal of ESTECAS is to promote cooperation for development of ecotourism in Estonia and unite stakeholders in order to formulate the framework and conditions for the sustainable utilisation and upkeep of the country’s natural and cultural heritage in tour products. ESTECAS contributes substantially to capacity building in local communities and to the conservation of the natural and cultural heritage of Estonia.

  • Owner

    www.soomaa.com

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    January 1994 — Present (16 years 8 months)

    SOOMAA.COM / Karuskose Ltd. is a small locally owned company that organizes tours and provides travel services inside and nearby Soomaa National Park since 1994.
    The backbone of our activities is a network of farms along the rivers and people living there. We do co-operate with many local people, accommodations, guides and service providers.

    The philosophy of SOOMAA.COM is ecotourism, which means to us promoting local economic development, supporting preservation of cultural and natural heritage and educating travelers and public.
