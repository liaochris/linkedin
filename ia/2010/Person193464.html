Juerg Staudenmann’s Summary

16 years of international experience in development cooperation, environment / natural resources management and knowledge management. - Currently working as Senior Programme Adviser / Deputy Resident Representative a.i. for the UN Development Programme in Belgrade/Serbia.

Specialized in Water governance, knowledge and information management, strategic partnership & practice network development. Promoter of “web2.0”-tools in international cooperation, knowledge codification and dissemination, and "community networking". Demonstrated skills in strategic leadership, innovation, team building and “Community of Practice” facilitation, always aiming for practicable solutions for complex situations.

Founder of WaterWiki.net, an interactive knowledge and collaboration platform for water professionals worldwide. Earlier career achievements range from the establishment of a new research department at the Zurich University of Applied Sciences, to the design and realization of a public-private funded, 1.0 m USD wastewater recycling plant in Switzerland.
