Michael Burroughs’s Summary

Experienced Executive Recruiter, Executive Coach and Organization Development consultant with a broad corporate executive background in operations, business development, program management and HR. Retired Army Reserve colonel.

Prior to becoming an executive recruiter and consultant I held a variety of executive positions with such companies as McDonnell Douglas, American Express, ARAMARK and Spectrum Healthcare Services. I have served as the top OD executive for divisions of these companies and held other positions in operations, program management, business development, HR and sales. Prior to the corporate world I served for 12 years as an army officer. I entered the Army Reserve when I joined the private sector and served for 17 more years retiring at the rank of colonel.

I left my last corporate employer in 1999 and formed my own consulting firm, Segue Consulting Group, LLC. Shortly afterward I contracted with the "top five" retained executive search firm, DHR International, and grew my search practice steadily over the years, focusing in healthcare, life sciences, industrial, aerospace/defense and the nonprofit sectors, while also maintaining my executive coaching and organization development practice. In 2003 I was asked to lead DHR's St. Louis operation as managing director and led that office in personal production for most of my years there. As a search consultant I have recruited many executives ranging from director to CEO in public and private companies, healthcare systems and hospitals, major nonprofit organizations and universities, in the US, Asia and Europe. In 2009 I ended my relationship with DHR to re-focus on my consulting business and to build a boutique executive recruiting practice, Unique to my search practice are several innovative features such as a new leader integration process that we perform at the conclusion of our searches. My executive coaching assignments remain with some of the major companies in the Fortune 500.
