Lars Kolind’s Summary

Lars Kolind is an entrepreneur, board member, professor, author and philantropist. He lives at Løndal manor (www.loendal.dk) in the western part of Denmark and he has extensive management experience from most parts of the world. He has built about one new company each year since 1998 and he has turned around companies and made them world leaders within their fields. His latest book, "The Second Cycle - Winnig the War against Bureaucracy", was published in 2006 by Wharton School Publishing. The book is coming out in a number of languages and the version in Danish became national business book of the year 2006.
Kolind is highly involved in World Scouting, serving as chairman-elect of the World Scout Foundation.

Lars Kolind’s Specialties:

Transforming mature businesses and organizations into agile, innovative and growth-oriented communities.
