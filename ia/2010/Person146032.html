We help investors earn high-yield returns including double-digit returns. You, the investor, will have the ease and comfort knowing your investment is safe and secure when we use your funds to purchase property tax liens and lend on commercial income producing properties.

    Our Tax Lien Fund offers high yields to those that do not rely on a monthly income and are looking for steady growth. We invest in county and city property tax liens earning between 14% and 36% returns. Government State law backs these liens and we have first position, even over the mortgage company.

    There can be many benefits to investing in the Commercial Equity Partners, including, but not limited to:

    • High Annual Fixed Returns 8.95% to 12.375%
    • Potential Windfall Profit-Sharing
    • No fees to investors
    • Continuous returns (no down time between investments)
    • Secured by an equity position of 70% to 90% with first position over other liens
    • Tax Liens are backed by state law and enforced by the county government and the state courts, ensuring we collect our funds or foreclose on the property
    • Easy to transfer IRA, Roth IRA and 401K Retirement Accounts


    We personally inspect every property and execute our thorough due diligence process before making a bid on any tax lien.
