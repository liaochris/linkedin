Michael Salort’s Summary

Was an award-winning journalist working for outlets such as ABC's 20/20, Dateline NBC and CNBC. In 2000, became a founding executive of the NIC Company, a SF-based tech company funded by Oracle Corp. chairman Larry Ellison.

In 2003, joined Viewpoint Corporation as head of Business Development, Marketing and Corporate Communications. Helped develop and launch company's initiatives in Internet search, 3D technology and ad-serving platforms. Negotiated and closed company-transforming search partnership with Yahoo. Spearheaded all business development, product launches and branding, and developed new strategies for corporate positioning. Was integral part of the turn-around team that:

• Achieved three of four consecutive EBITDA positive quarters in 24 months, after years of previous losses
• Raised over $12 million through four PIPE transactions
• Launched new products generating positive EBITDA in 17 months
• Quadrupled stock price within first 18 months.

After Viewpoint became Managing Partner of PersonalScreen Media, a new film production and finance company, which produces content and technology platforms for "new media." Co-wrote and co-executive produced the Emmy-nominated web series, IN MEN WE TRUST, which will premiere in 2009 on Women's Entertainment Television.

In August 2006, became Chief Operating Officer of BrainPop, Inc. producing animated movies for four million paid subscribers. During interim assignment, reorganized company's infrastructure and invigorated business development, marketing and financial operations.

In November 2007, founded Symbiotic Pictures Entertainment to leverage opportunities in traditional film, TV and "new media." Currently running film, television, publishing, online media at Bankable Enterprises.

Michael Salort’s Specialties:

Corporate and business development; organization optimization and growth; marketing and branding; brand management; fund raising; contract and partner negotiations; production management from both the creative and business perspectives.
