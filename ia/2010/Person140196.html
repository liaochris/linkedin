Megh Chakrabarti’s Summary

My professional goal is to be a social worker dealing with clients facing trauma from sexual violence. I currently work at MOCSA, providing prevention education in the greater Kansas City area to youth in order to prevent sexual violence from occurring. Part of my job also requires me to provide crisis intervention to survivors of sexual violence, primarily those who are survivors of rape. I am also the Human Trafficking point person for MOCSA.

I have direct practice experience through case management at a domestic violence shelter in Lawrence, KS. I also have macro practice experience by working directly with research participants conducting interviews and collecting data, and by helping complete a mixed methods social work research study on Parent Support Services through data collection tools such as Atlas.ti and SPSS.

I have developed great people skills through my work at jobs and volunteering, learn quickly, and understand that advocacy, empathy, and education are needed to help alleviate and eliminate domestic and sexual violence. Additionally, I have the skill of being fluent in Bengali.
