Stan Marts’s Summary

Stan Marts is President and founder of the executive search firm DES Growth Partners, Inc. Stan understands the fundamental value proposition of a search firm is to “provide better candidates faster” – so Stan invests considerable effort in expanding his reach to candidates. Stan serves on the MBA Council of Houston, is a local leader of the Chicago Booth School of Business Alumni Association, a founding member of the Houston Chapter of CEO Netweavers, and the founder and past president of the Professional Development Group of Houston, the premier business and professional development association for executives in Houston.

Prior to getting into the executive search field in 2003 Stan worked in the energy and chemicals markets (Fluor, Shell Oil) and the management consulting industry (Navigant.) Stan holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of New Mexico and an MBA from the University of Chicago.

Stan Marts’s Specialties:

Placing key Mid and Senior Level executives into energy firms. Placing expereinced consultants (individuals who have acheived a level of engagment manager or higher) into consulting firms as well as into senior positions in industry. Pre- and post employment assessment for "job fit", management and leadership development.
