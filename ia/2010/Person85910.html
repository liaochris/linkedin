What is TunkRank?
Tunkrank is an influence ranking tool that helps you locate sales leads, partners, leading influencers & do sentiment analysis on Twitter.

What is Cell Alert?
Cell Alert is a completely free email and text-messaging alert service that locates the free items you're looking for; from dressers to cars to cookbooks.

You might be looking for a laptop, a stereo, software, a couch, a stroller, French doors, garage sale giveaways, storage bins, moving boxes, or even a free car or truck (yes, many people have gotten cars!).

Cell Alert tracks them all for you and then provides you instant notification! Cell Alert is your instant alert service for finding any and all special items you're searching for in your area.

And at the very moment Cell Alert finds your item, we send an alert directly to your email address (or a text alert to your cell phone or both). This gives you the following advantages:
* Early responder advantages.
* Free, automatic alerts of your favorite free items. Instead of monitoring the Internet 24 hours per day for the free things everyone is searching for, we provide you the immediate information you need to get what you want!
