Michelle Waters’s Summary

After running the technical side of a web hosting, design and ecommerce company, I am focusing my efforts on what I love the most -- teaching and coaching.

As owner of Watersweb for almost a decade, I have helped hundreds of small business owners around the world start and grow their online ventures.

Beginning in January 2010, I am revamping my business to enable me to reach out to more business owners who need guidance in navigating the online world.

I started by changing my business name to Night Owl Marketing LLC and am developing coaching, consulting and technical programs to help your business reach its full potential.
