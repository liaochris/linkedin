Fabio Consentino’s Summary

Fabio Consentino brings over 13 years of international capital equipment and instrumentation product management and marketing experience in the metrology and inspection, automation, and photolithography industries.

As a Sr. Director of Product Line Management at Veeco as well as with previous roles at KLA-Tencor, Ultratech, and Asyst Technologies he has held positions managing product marketing and applications engineering groups that focused on delivering profitable solutions to a variety of high-technology markets including semiconductor, data storage, MEMS, flat panel displays, material science, optoelectronics, solar cell/photovoltaics and HBLEDs. He has won a “Product of the Year” award from Semiconductor International Magazine and has been quoted in several industry magazine articles.

His focus on strategically planning and executing product line business plans for market share growth and increased profitability has provided him with the instinctive skill for indentifying and creating new growth opportunities and seizing them for improving the P&L.

Mr. Consentino received his BSME from Faculdade de Engenharia Industrial in Sao Paulo, Brazil and his MBA from St. Edward’s University in Austin, TX.

Fabio Consentino’s Specialties:

P&L management w/ operational metrics (“mini GM” of product line); C-level interactions/negotiations; strategic planning and roadmap development; business plans and budgeting; market sizing (TAM/SAM) analysis and validation; MRDs; competitive research; use-case scenarios; product positioning and value propositions; PLC expert; launch plans, pricing and ROI analysis; channel training/support; regional strategies; definition of sales tools; supervision of Product Managers and Application Engineers
