Jason Dunne’s Summary

I founded London Smartphone to help publishers, brand owners and non-technical individuals turn their iPhone ideas into working apps. I also develop my own apps alongside some wonderful programmers.

I believe that mobile apps are the best thing to happen to publishing since Amazon.com. I also believe that we've only just begun. The publishing giants of tomorrow have yet to make their first app and they're probably not working in publishing today, so why not give your ideas a go?

I'd love to hear from anyone with an idea for an app that's grown out of their niche interest. It doesn't matter whether that niche is skateboarding, philosophy, caring for the deaf or baking cupcakes: the more niche the better.

From 2002-2009 I looked after the UK publishing arm of the “...For Dummies” brand, one of the world's best-known consumer information brands. During this time I was also publisher for the Capstone imprint, producing books for business, personal development and general interest markets. I published several category-top-ten titles and established a reputation for being able to uncover commercial opportunities in a very broad range of topics, helped by the experience I gained as a bookseller and sales rep in the UK book trade.

I enjoy staying on top of trends in my industry and other industries too. I enjoy business for its own sake and I adore the publishing business in particular, not least because I get to meet so many fascinating people.
