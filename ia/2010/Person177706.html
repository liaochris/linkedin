Steve Rosebaugh’s Summary

Steve has been coaching individuals for over 20 years in various business roles. Coaching has always been a passion of his. Steve has experience managing change in both technology and business and can work with business owners to drive change in their own business results.

With a strong diversity of business experience, Steve’s background includes engineering, manufacturing, quality, product management, and marketing. Steve has international experience with both small and large businesses and has handled key account management, project management, conflict resolution, and operations management roles.

Steve completed his certification in Business Performance Coaching with FocalPoint International. He coaches owners and executives in small to medium businesses in the central Texas area. The content applied by coach Steve is unparalleled in the coaching industry. The curriculum is comprehensive and can systematically uncover numerous opportunities to energize your business and deliver results faster than most business owners imagine possible.

But much more than content, Coach Steve can work with business owners to apply and drive rapid results in multiple areas, including:

Revenue and Profit Growth
Time Management and Efficiency
Employee and/or Supplier Teamwork and Responsibility
Net Worth and Exit Strategies

Corporate background is as a generalist in the semiconductor industry, having served in many different roles, international experience, and managing change in both business and technology. Effective communicator at all levels, from the factory floor to the top executives in large corporations.

Demonstrated abilities in business plan development, competitive analysis, new product introductions, product launches, product engineering, key customer engagement and management, product branding, sales training, marketing communications and events. These continue to be my passion.
