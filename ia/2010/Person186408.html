Serge Roux’s Summary

I Manage innovation projects, ranging from product design to redefining product and technical roadmaps for clients in the most various sectors of industry.
I link creavity sessions, patent and business inteligence, industrial design, Life-Cycle Analysis, and rapid prorotyping to create high end-user value products.
My everyday work involves costumer relationship, commercial proposals, sketching, CAD drawing and rendering, HMI design, supplier management and of course project management.
I am also in charge of benchmarking and inventing new tools and processes for design adn creativity, as well as directing the overall training and recruitment of fellow industrial designers.
My clients are mainly in the MedTech field but extended towards consumer goods and clean tech. In the past, I dealt with a wider range of client ranging from Automotive to Luxury, Health, Petroleum, Train interiors, Military, and Telecommunications...
My goal, and ultimate pride is, as Steeve Jobs would put it, to produce eco-designed user-oriented innovation that ships!
