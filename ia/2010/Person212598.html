Dave Roberts’s Summary

Dave has worked in management consulting for over 15 years, he has held positions in both general & line management and he has managed small businesses and start-ups. Dave has consulted in a wide range of industries including telecommunications & technology, financial services, consumer goods, retail, government & not-for-profit. He also consults on development programs in Africa, in particular in the health industry in Ethiopia.

Dave brings skills in strategy, HR and change management, sales & marketing and operations. Dave believes that experience is the greatest source of wisdom and he is dedicated to the use of experiential and holistic approaches to build his clients’ core competencies. He brings a highly analytical and creative approach with a consultative & engaging leadership style, strong problem solving & project management skills.

Dave is certified to administer level C psychometrics and is a unlicensed psychotherapist in the State of Colorado. He holds a Bachelors Degree in Psychology and a Masters Degree in Organization Development.

Dave Roberts’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning, Human Resource Management, Organization Development, Change Management, Sales & Marketing, Process design & reengineering
