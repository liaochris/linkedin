Guillaume K. Saouli’s Summary

PMP, em. DMCS, Chairman Green ID SA, Managing Partner Musketeer Investment. Winner of the President's Award from PMI Risk SIG, and "2010 Innovation Award" from the Swiss Society for Project Management & PMI Swiss Chapter

As managing partner of Musketeer Investment, Guillaume Saouli puts into practice his project management and business consulting skills, in order to support the development of innovative and alternative projects. He is also the chairman of Green iD - Green Infrastructure Development, a company he helped jumpstart from a concept on a drawing board to a fully operating company developing sustainable mobility infrastructures. For the past decade, as program manager, and program director, Guillaume Saouli supported global initiatives in various industries, ranging from Financial Services to Telcos. He has also been involved in large multiyear programs where he delivered business to technology transformation programs, or remediations for e-business solutions. As distinguished member of consulting staff for Lucent, he contributed to the development of the Swiss and EMEA operations, by amongst others proposing the foundations of a lightweight PIM to be used for all engagements. Previously Guillaume was senior consultant for a regional technology consultancy, active in telecommunication infrastructures and IT security. Before joining this company, Guillaume was the head of operations for an ISP, he co-founded in Geneva. His education is as comprehensive as his professional career, he attended the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne. In order to extend his acumen, he completed his MBA studies following a global management concentration in 2008 at Business School Lausanne.

Over the years, Guillaume has recieved numerous accolades, ranging from Global Netwokring Award in 1995, the President's Award from PMI Risk SIG, and his latest one "the 2010 Innovation Award" from the Swiss Society for Project Management & PMI Swiss Chapter.
