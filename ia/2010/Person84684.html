Kevin Xu’s Summary

Kevin is an active Freshman at Stanford University within the entrepreneurial and business world. Eager to create a startup, he has joined several entrepreneurship groups on campus and is learning as well as networking with his peers. He has already been put in charge of several projects to raise awareness about entrepreneurship and how others can get involved.

His interests do not lie in any "get rich quick" startup but a game-changer, an idea that would significantly change the market or how something in the world currently works. He foresees everything we use today going virtual and mobile in the future. Ultimate goal is to create something and be damn proud of it.

My focus right now is meeting fellow entrepreneurs and finding out how to make my ideas real. Feel free to email me at kevin (at) imkevinxu (dot) com
