Mary E. Allen’s Summary

I’m a Graphic, Web + Multimedia Designer. I was born and raised outside of Boston, Massachusetts. I’ve been drawing, painting and crafting ever since I could hold a crayon. I love all kinds of creativity, but my real passion is design.

In May of 2007, I graduated Summa Cum Laude (GPA 3.9) from the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, where I received my BFA in Visual Design/Letterform, with a minor in Business Administration.

Learning didn’t stop there. I’ve since been exploring the different avenues my degree can take me, from publishing and magazine design to advertising and brand marketing to video editing and website development. I've taken an HTML + Dreamweaver course at Boston's Massachusetts College of Art and Design (MassArt) and a web design and development class at Providence's famed Rhode Island School of Design (RISD).

Recently, I've been interested in dressing up my websites with a touch of animation. I'll be returning to RISD in January of 2010 for a Flash course.

Someday, I’ll establish my own design firm. In the meantime, I’m building a network of contacts, doing freelance jobs, and working my way up. Take a look at my website, http://www.maryeallen.com, for more information about me!
