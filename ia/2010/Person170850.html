About the Toolbox Inc/The Non-Profit Toolbox
The Non-Profit Toolbox is all about helping those who help by building a community; collaborating; educating and providing valuable resources. Take your non-profit organization to the next level!

About TRB Design, Inc.
TRB Design, Inc. was incorporated in 2002. We started out as a video production company, producing videos for organizations, school plays and graduations. Realizing that the web would be a growing place for video, we also integrated web site hosting and web design and management. Recently, because we saw a void in understanding of web marketing, both inbound and outbound, we have also included web marketing consultation into our business. Tom has recently written a booklet on the topic to educate individuals and businesses on the growth we see using the web. For more information go to www.TRBDesigns.com.

TRB Design, Inc. is active with a number of a variety of organizations: NPVA of New England (National Professional Videographer’s Association); Norwell Chamber of Commerce (Reiko is President); SSWBN Education Foundation – board member; SS YMCA – Mill Pond Board member; Norwell Women’s Club; South Shore Ad Club; Friends of the NVNA, and Boston Media Makers. We believe that involvement in local community efforts helps everyone to grow and prosper; and have contributed our web and video expertise to a number of these organizations.

About GymSmarts
GymSmarts offers athletes, parents, coaches, trainers and club owners multimedia products in both video and print that will enable students to achieve their full athletic potential. GymSmarts' instructional videos offer you the opportunity to learn the techniques and teaching methods of internationally recognized coaches of national champions, international champions and Olympians. GymSmarts' is the premiere worldwide resource for instructional gymnastics material. www.GymSmarts.com. Free gym tips at: www.youtube.com/thegymnasticminute

Reiko Beach’s Specialties:

What TRB Design brings to the table when we talk about web marketing is a successful video production company, years of marketing know-how, with personal experience in marketing products on the web through our GymSmarts line of products.
