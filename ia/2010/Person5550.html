Pat Harmony’s Summary

First you shift...Then you Bloom!!!

Harmony, fondly referred to as a modern day Alchemist, excels at holding space for people to easily release, heal and shift their limiting beliefs and negative self talk so they can live inspired and be empowered. As an innovative and respected healer and coach, she brings an extensive palette of high vibrational healing tools and modalities to all of her work.

Her clients include people looking for mind body spirit healing, are in career transition or are looking to discover meaning and purpose in their lives. As Harmony’s clients get clear and change how they see and value themselves, they make new choices that free them to live the life they were meant to live with more happiness, peace and joy.

An award winning and inspiring speaker and workshop facilitator, she has delighted and educated audiences across the Delaware Valley with her insightful and empowering messages. Harmony believes healing comes from within and as we form a deeper connection to Self and the Divine, we are able to facilitate our own healing, creating a life in harmony.
