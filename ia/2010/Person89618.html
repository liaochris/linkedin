Jann LaGoy Mirchandani’s Summary

I am the principal and creative engine behind Mirchandani Consulting, a graphic and web design firm. I am an award winner designer specializing in helping small and mid-sized firms in Westchester and outlying counties to find their voice and communicate more effectively with their target audiences.

Together, with strategic partner Kashen Communications, I founded and lead eMarketing Bootcamps. These workshops provide entrepreneurs with the critical training and personalized roadmaps needed to achieve online marketing success.

My industry expertise spans the financial sector, human resources and executive recruiting and non-profit. Through an extensive and robust network of business partners I provide big business marketing support to enterprises that are not yet fully-grown.

Most recently, my work was recognized by the Advertising Club of Westchester’s Big W Awards and Hermes Creative Awards. My affiliations include the Business Council of Westchester and the Yorktown Chamber of Commerce.

Jann LaGoy Mirchandani’s Specialties:

I specialize in working with small businesses, helping them communicate more effectively with their customers via print and online communications; flyers, brochures, website, email, etc.
