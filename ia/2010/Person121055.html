Lara DeSignor’s Summary

Lara DeSignor works with her award-winning teams to create and execute cutting edge digital solutions for the world’s top brands and agencies. Lara is always seeking out the latest in integrated solutions and technologies and fuses them into every campaign. Her approach places the content and client as hero with an end goal to break the mold and produce a win-win success story. Lara started her career in Broadcast PR and video production and with the changing industry and her passion for innovation, has evolved into the digital marketing space. Having worked in this arena side-by-side leading marketers and public relations executives, from Estee Lauder to Elizabeth Arden, Giorgio Armani, CHANEL, COTY and more, she understands how to engage, excite & bring the emotional connection to your campaign.
