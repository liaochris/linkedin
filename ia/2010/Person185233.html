Sean Campbell’s Summary

I've built two different business in the broad arena of technology marketing.

The first, 3 Leaf, was a technical consultancy which consistently worked for the some of the largest companies in the software and hardware field. We wrote a lot of code, developed a number of great solutions for our clients with the latest technology, and built a very successful business along the way that employed a great team of people.

After growing and selling 3 Leaf I moved on to found Cascade Insights a few years ago.

Our approach at Cascade Insights combines deep technical and software industry expertise with on-the-ground diligence. We tailor solutions for each client with services that include:

Competitive B2B market research that helps you better understand your position in your market through in depth interviews, focus groups, and custom research studies.

Competitive product analysis that identifies your product's advantages and disadvantages compared to competitors.

In depth and ongoing custom reports and analysis on technology and market trends.

Sales intelligence projects that enable your organization to accelerate sales.

Beyond the above I've also been the lead author of three different technical books (two on programming and one on virtualization) during my time at 3 Leaf.

I also frequently do public presentations and author articles on qualitative research and competitive intelligence related topics.

Recent presentations and articles have been hosted on SoftwareCEO, the AIPMM site, MarketingProfs, and other sites.

I also currently am the Chair of the Oregon Chapter of the Society of Competitive Intelligence Professionals (SCIP), and I'm an active participant in the Qualitative Research Consultants Association (QRCA) and the Market Research Association (MRA).

Contact me at: sean@cascadeinsights.com / Twitter: @Sean_Campbell
