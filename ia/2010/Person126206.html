Lisa Duncan’s Summary

Professional Bio

Lisa is an extraordinary business thinker with an expertise in communications planning. During her 20+ years in the business, Lisa has a record of success at building world-class brands and developing innovative marketing and media solutions across a broad range of industries.

Her breadth of experience and noted success includes gold-standard clients such as: Publix Super Markets , Southeast Toyota, Orlando Convention and Visitor’s Bureau and Outback Steakhouse. Other noted experience includes travel and tourism giants Marriott International and Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines/Celebrity Cruises. Lisa also developed some of the industry’s most cutting edge, award-winning work in the condom and cigar categories.

Lisa spent over 14 years at WestWayne, Inc. (now 22squared) fast-tracking from Media Planner to EVP, Executive Media Director overseeing media operations for all WestWayne offices. While there, her entrepreneurial spirit and drive to explore new ideas lead to the development of a proprietary media optimization model, as well as a creative ideation process. In partnership with brand planning, Lisa also worked to develop a consumer-centric planning model.

Lisa is now the Director of Marketing and Media at Flaxen, a boutique advertising agency in Central Florida. There she gets to combine her big brand strategic thinking, mixed with the scrappy resourcefulness and survival instincts of the small business atmosphere, and this translates into a significant advantage for the clients of all sizes that they serve.

Lisa is also Owner and Publisher of www.LivingFrugalTips.com, and helps others realize the importance of balance and how to create a rich life for themselves by living on less. Her writing on Living Frugal Tips led to an exciting partnership with www.WomenXchange.com. As a contributing writer she helps encourage women to relish the fully lived life through purpose living.
