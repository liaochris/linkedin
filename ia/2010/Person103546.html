Joleen Deatherage’s Summary

Founder and Writer -- D'RAGE | Writes

I help businesses and organizations increase the efficiency, persuasiveness, and professionalism of their written communications for Web and Print.

A native Angeleno, I am driven by a commitment to clear writing and creative excellence.

As a former marketing director with years of copywriting experience, I have a proven track record of communicating clearly to multicultural audiences.

I provide fully integrated marketing communications to small businesses and start-ups.

I bring creative thinking and marketing strategies together to help you communicate your brand and value to the viral marketplace.

I help clients avoid weak value propositions and grammatical errors that turn prospects away.

D'RAGE Writes is the writing arm of D'Rage Media Group, a woman-owned small business certified by the State of California and registered with the U.S. Federal Government as a supplier of professional services.
