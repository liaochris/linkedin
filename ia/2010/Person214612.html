Wendy Edsall-Kerwin’s Summary

I have a BFA from Tyler School of Art, I continue to take workshops to improve my skills and add new techniques to my portfolio. I currently sell my work at the Radius Gallery in Harrisburg, PA, Luke & Eloy Gallery in Pittsburgh, PA at Veleska Jewelry in Lancaster, PA, at various craft shows, and through local, regional, and national exhibitions.
I am looking for representation in another one to two galleries and to expand my craft show schedule. I am also always looking for new exhibitions to enter that can best showcase my work.
