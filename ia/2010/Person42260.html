Cody Johns’s Summary

Cody's passion for footwear and apparel companies brought her to Generator Group in early 2007, where she works as an executive search consultant in the consumer products practice. She has succeeded in placing 20+ executives including VP Sales, VP Operations, Development Director, Sr. Apparel Designer, GM Apparel, VP Merchandising and Marketing and more in the outdoor and action sports consumer products industry.

Before joining Generator, she spent nearly six years at the Portland Business Journal as an advertising sales executive and special projects and event manager. There she produced about 25 business events and hosted speakers such as Anne Mulcahy, CEO of Xerox; Steve Young, former quarterback for the San Francisco 49ers; and Dennis Madsen former CEO of REI. Reponsibilities included full P & L and all event logistics including marketing, ticket sales, advertising/promotions, catering, A/V, all vendors and speaker acquisition.

Cody holds a bachelor's degree in speech communication from Oregon State University. She is a member of the Outdoor Women's Coalition.

Outside of work, Cody spends her time strapped to a board, whether it's snowboarding, surfing, wakeboarding or wake surfing.
