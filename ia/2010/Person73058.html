Gary Kazmer’s Summary

Open to new opportunities. Managing turnaround or high growth companies as a President or COO is my sweet spot. I have spent the bulk of my career taking on tough opportunities and re-igniting revenue growth, creating new customer facing sales products, generating profitable bottom lines (net income / EBITDA ), right-sizing the management staff and creating technical and operational changes to increase efficiency or create a better customer experience. The early part of my career was focused on managing the fixed and variable expenses of operations dealing with large scale distribution centers, logistics and customer service or call centers. For the last 7 years I have expanded my focus to revenue growth, brand building, sourcing and the shift in business from traditional revenue streams to the web. I have successfully managed sustained growth upwards of 35% per year making transitions transparent to the customer.

Gary Kazmer’s Specialties:
