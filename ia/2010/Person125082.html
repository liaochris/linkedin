Linda Bucher’s Summary

Coach. Teacher. Pathfinder. Inspiring speaker. Gratitude advocate. And all around creative soul. My PASSION and PURPOSE is to help you find and live YOURS!

I work with people who are optimistic and eager to live life by design but who experience barriers or blind spots in the form of unsatisfying jobs, relationships or life situations. Through coaching I help you FIND YOUR WOW! and DESIGN YOUR LIFE - Inside & Out.

Yes, the grass IS greener here and you CAN get HERE from there.
