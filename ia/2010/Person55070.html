Derek S. Carter’s Summary

A graduate of Michigan State University’s Business program, Derek postponed graduate studies in Industrial Relations and chose instead to start his career at General Mills as a Financial Analyst. Following an early promotion and transfer, Derek was presented with the opportunity to become the Controller of the company’s Yoplait facility in Southern California.

Derek transferred back to Minnesota having gained substantial experience in Manufacturing Operations and Database Systems. Through several additional moves and advancements, Derek took on significant roles in both the Sales division and Diversity Marketing.

After 12 years with General Mills, Derek was recruited to Deluxe Corporation for the purpose of using his unique combination of Operations, Finance and Marketing skills as Director of their largest business segment.

During this period, Deluxe developed and financed an idea for a way into the dot.com domain and transferred Derek to this venture. Not long afterward, with the dot.com market beginning to slow, Derek opted to make use of his years of experience and distinctive background by leaving Deluxe to work in the consulting field as an Executive Recruiter.

After several years and an MBA earned in Human Resource Management, Derek began work at JB Scott Search – a retained search firm specializing in diversity at the senior level.

In 2008, Derek accepted a new role as Sr. Recruiting Manager at Cargill.

Most recently, Derek was recruited by 3M Corporation and is Responsible for their diversity recruiting strategy, executive recruiting and lead for the recruiting team supporting all corporate functions (IT, Finance, Marketing, Strategy, Legal, Sourcing/Supply Chain and HR).

.

Derek joined the Minnesota Chapter of the NAAAHR in 2004 and was Chapter President from 2007 - 2009.

Derek lives in a suburb of Minneapolis and is married with four children.
