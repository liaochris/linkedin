Wendy R Friedman’s Summary

SoHoSoleil Locations, LLC has partnered with the Mayor's Office of Film, Theatre & Broadcasting to host the “Made in NY” Mentorship program organized by the IFP.org. The day rental business, known for its diverse and unconventional group of locations, has a long tradition of supporting industry related projects. SoHoSoleil, also a sponsor of the Independent Film Project, provides discounts on shooting sites and meeting venues for IFP’s 150,000 members.

Wendy Friedman/SoHoSoleil, has also sponsored the APA - Advertising Photographers of America over the years as well as the Graphic Artist's Guild and other creative arts organizations.

Wendy R Friedman’s Specialties:

We find unusual loft spaces in mainly in SoHo, NYC and organize creative off-site meetings for Fortune 500's, celebrity interviews, film, TV, video and photo shoots.
