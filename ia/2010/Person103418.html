John Whelan’s Summary

Experienced musician, producer ,writer and publisher in the Celtic/World Music industry for the last 40 years. While always aware of my traditional roots in Irish music I am and have been a pioneer in crossing genres bringing people of the world together in music. Let's work together in that common goal to understand each other better and create a working environment and a world where we can accomplish our goals with understanding and integrity. That is what music can do for your employees in the work place or home. We can inspire people to become better workers and outstanding citizens. If you need music for a project or a producer I am the man for you. I can coordinate from beginning to end any kind of musical production whether it be a CD , Video or live performance. Give me a call and lets get it done.

John Whelan’s Specialties:

Motivational music and video projects. TV Commercials, Film scores and more. No project is too small or too big. Every project I have worked on came in under budget.
