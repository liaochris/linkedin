Barry Kolodkin’s Summary

Barry Kolodkin is a New Jersey-based Emerging Markets Consultant focused on business development, market research and strategy, communications and public affairs, economic and political analysis and foreign investment advisory services.

Prior to consulting, Barry served in international business development and project management roles for technology and professional services organizations.

Barry is seeking to expand his business by networking with potential clients, partners, or project consultants. However, he is especially interested in new challenges; namely, leadership positions with blue-chip or growth-oriented companies related to international business development/expansion, international public affairs/public policy/communications or regional management.
