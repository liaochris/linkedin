Christian Lindner’s Summary

After acquiring formal training at Rochester Institute of Technology, I wanted to expand my design experience and take on a broad range of challenges not available to me in Buffalo, NY. So, in 2005 I moved to Chicago, IL where I continued to build my portfolio and hone my design skills. Chicago also allowed me to learn how to compete—and succeed—in a fast-paced, demanding, and well-supplied market. Soon after my website was created: CmyWork.com on a shoe-string budget (so just imagine what I can do with a larger account).

Moving back to Buffalo in 2007, I've tackled a handful of projects notable to the Buffalo, NY community (as listed below). A division of Random House Inc., also credited me during that time for a novelty humor book that made it's way in all major book stores nationally. Today, I am a lead design consultant for a photography studio and am trained both in Macintosh & Windows operating systems. Although I love Buffalo, I would consider a new scenery change if the right opportunity presents its self in a full-time setting.
