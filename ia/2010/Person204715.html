M. Rachael Schafer’s Summary

I've collaborated with a wide range of companies, from corporate institutions to independent creative enterprises.

These days I prefer to work with a diverse group of companies to establish and hone the tactical role that design can fill and to achieve marked results across all media, with an increasing emphasis on the social nature of media. When done clearly and effectively, design can spark conversation, interest, and generate an elusive affection and loyalty.
