Karen Ansel, RD’s Summary

As a freelance journalist and author my feature articles, recipes and meal plans regularly appear in national women’s and health magazines such as Woman's Day, Family Circle, Prevention, Cooking Light, Fitness, Muscle & Fitness Hers, and Women’s Health. In addition, I am also the co-author of "The Baby & Toddler Cookbook" (Weldon Owen, March 2010).

As an American Dietetic Association Spokesperson, I provide the latest breaking nutrition information to the national media.
