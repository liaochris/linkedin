Malcolm Davidow’s Summary

Malcolm Davidow is a driven, results-oriented business executive with a demonstrated ability to lead. He offers a unique combination of both entrepreneurial and large corporate experience, spanning both North American and international markets. Acknowledged as an auto parts industry visionary, Davidow is frequently called on for his insights on industry imperatives and strategies. He regularly speaks at industry conferences, and writes and comments for Aftermarket Business, MotorAge, Service Tech, MEMA Focus and AAIA Aftermarket Insider.

Davidow’s inspiring leadership style presents uncommon, but significant distinctions: he can be confident yet humble, forthright but guarded, good-natured or intense, accessible or confidential, outspoken or appropriately reserved. He makes timely, effective decisions based on his attention to detail and strong analytical ability. His knowledge of business trends and strategies, in and out of the auto parts industry and across business process areas, derives from his unceasing curiosity, as he is a voracious reader and long-time student of business.
