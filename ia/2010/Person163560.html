Phil Ahern 2500+’s Summary

CONFLICT MANAGER .

COULD YOUR WORKPLACE BE MORE EFFECTIVE IN THE WAY IT DEALS WITH CONFLICT ?
WHAT IS THE REAL COST OF CONFLICT TO YOUR ORGANISATION ?

Conflict is endemic and often expresses itself in spiteful and negative ways .

Backbiting, bullying and harassment cause anxiety,depression and sometimes even lives.
It also severely cuts into profit. Absenteeism , high turnover of employees and the training of new ones and ineffectual teams cost money.
Phil Ahern, through a creative mix of professional development seminars, conflict coaching, counselling and mediation all tailored to the needs of your workplace or school assists you and your organisation to utilise conflict in more productive ways.
PERSONALLY, do you need to prepare for a possible conflict scenario or deal with one that has already exploded ? The one-to-one Conflict Coaching and Scripting session will help you.

All- inclusive seminars on conflict resolution skills are also provided on request.
All of the above is offered Australia -Wide
