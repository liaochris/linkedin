Greg Chadwell’s Summary

Global integrated marketing & channel sales expert with 15+ years of success launching innovative technology to grow revenue. Led agency and client teams to deliver award winning marketing campaigns including branding, digital, retail, direct, promotions and field events to increase sales for world class iconic brands like AT&T, Nokia, Hilton, Sprint, Qwest, Sony, Dell, CableVision, Best Buy, Cadillac & USPS.

Directly managed over $50MM in client fees, generated $17MM in new/organic revenue and delivered 20% annual profit. Recognized for building exceptional client/team relationships based on trust and achieving great results.

Accomplished integrated marketer, brand builder, savvy strategist, team collaborator, creative thinker, highly effective communicator with demonstrated success transforming brand strategy into big ideas that drive sales.

Strong expertise in B2B/B2C acquisition & loyalty marketing driving brand demand to increase conversion and grow ROI.
