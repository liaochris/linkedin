John Whiting, MPM’s Summary

What I offer:

• Organizational skills to bring people together and get them pointed in the right direction.
• User/Stakeholder service through
o Extracting, documenting and understanding customer wants and needs
o Elimination of ambiguity by verifying with users, before much work has been done, that we understand their requirements through models, prototypes or other means
• Execution of the project with schedules, resource plans, and the resources needed to complete the work.
• Assurance that
o all the stakeholders are represented, kept informed and remain OK with the project direction;
o issues and risks are identified and dealt with to minimize any detrimental affects
• Detailed adherence to the schedule and scope management
• Sense of urgency to get scheduled work done on or before expected completion, with integrity and quality
• A success-centric management environment that fosters creativity, a strong work-ethic and as roadblock-free as I can make it.

What I bring:

I bring the benefit of great educational credentials, plus skill honed by many years of successfully project planning, execution and control that allows me to manage multiple, complex, large scale projects in matrixed, on-shore/off-shore, vendor-based environments, or any combination of these. Success in both small and large organizations, to deliver, by every means available, on time (effort), on schedule (date), within budget, meeting or exceeding quality expectations.

What I need from you:

The chance to bring the same levels of success to your organization that I have done for others in the past.
