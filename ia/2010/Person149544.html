Peter Bowerman’s Summary

16-year sales/marketing career (1978-1994) followed by 16 years of professional marketing copywriting experience (www.writeinc.biz).

My company WriteInc. is an umbrella business entity engaged in several writing-related activities:

1) a professional marketing communications firm specializing in copywriting services for businesses large and small (www.writeinc.biz)

2) a publishing company with four award-winning books on copywriting and self-publishing (www.wellfedwriter.com; www.wellfedsp.com).

3) Consultancy/business coaching practice for those starting/reinvigorating writing businesses or self-publishing. Both on-on-one and group coaching programs (visit "Mentoring" link for both sites noted in #2).

4) Book titling/subtitling/back cover copywriting practice (details at www.titletailor.com)

5) Speaker, seminar/workshop leader, teleseminar presenter. Details at http://www.wellfedwriter.com/needaspeaker.shtml.

Peter Bowerman’s Specialties:
