Richard Mattson’s Summary

With more than 25 years in B2B technology marketing, and hands-on experience in nearly every marketing discipline— program strategy, marketing communications, PR and investor relations, trade shows and events, and market research—I'm broadly equipped to re-develop a company's total marketing plan, define and drive a range of campaign initiatives, or dive deeply into key problem areas that need special troubleshooting. At whatever level, I'm committed to delivering powerful and high-impact business results.

A Microsoft alumnus who joined The Partner Marketing Group in early 2008, I spent much of the last decade creating programs targeting small and midsize companies supporting Microsoft business solutions. Shortly after the acquisition of Great Plains, I moved into thru-Partner marketing and spearheaded initiatives for Microsoft Dynamics Partners that often delivered results at 125-175% of prior benchmarks.
