Marc Marroquin’s Summary

Ah, the inevitable 'Who Am I and What Have I Done' section. It is an interesting dilemma because my inclination is to write about everything I've done in great detail and what I'd like to do...to put the hard sell on about me. But who would read that in this day and age of Short Attention Span Theater? Here is a Reader's Digest version for you...hopefully you'll find it interesting enough to contact me and learn more.

My past is an interesting mix of technology, product management, marketing, and sales. It may sound random but it does tie together around one thing - selling technology. Over the last 15 years I've learned a lot about how to sell technology in different roles (technical specialist, product champion, marketing guy, and sales manager) to different people (customers, a customer's manager, sales people, my managers). And the bottom line is I love doing all of that...and more importantly I have been successful doing those things and made a good living to support my family.

So now what? My overriding goal is to find challenging new opportunities that enable me to use all of these experiences and do something great. The thrill to me is in being presented with a problem, finding a way to solve it, given the time/resources to apply that solution, and then seeing that solution blossom in to success. Simple right? So when do we get started?
