Joy M. D'Amore’s Summary

Entrepreneurial Human Resources leader with a broad and successful 15+ year track record of leading human resources, organization development and talent acquisition for H.I.G. Capital, FOLIOfn, Zaplet, Amazon.com, and Manugistics. Proven strategic as well as hands-on executive with a passion for finding innovative, effective solutions to human resource issues. Achieved results in high-growth, resource-constrained, rapidly changing and turnaround environments. Experience spans small to large size companies within Technology, Financial Services, and Management Consulting industries. Trusted, empathetic coach and advisor. Highly analytical, systemic, and divergent thinker.

Graduated cum laude from Wesleyan University with a B.A. in College of Social Studies, (High Distinction/Distinction in CSS) . Graduated from American University's Washington College of Law and was a Dean's Fellow. Completed extensive training in Organization Development through NTL Institute. Additionally, graduated cum laude from Sidwell Friends School. Admitted to the Connecticut Bar and clerked for a Federal Judge, prior to a Human Resources career.

Joy M. D'Amore’s Specialties:

Spectrum of human resources with depth in talent acquisition, talent management, executive coaching, organization development, change management, and employment law.
