  • Lead Buyer's REALTOR

    Gateway REO Properties

    (Real Estate industry)

    October 2006 — September 2009 (3 years )

    I Originally started position as an experienced Loan Officer to collect pertinent documentation, submit this documentation to underwriting and determine approval or denial status of potential financing programs for the client's lending scenario. This financial background and being bilingual in Spanish gave me a significant competitive advantage when transitioning into the Lead Buyer's Agent REALTOR position when the market was infused with the first major wave of foreclosure homes disbursed by banks REO (bank foreclosure portfolios) and managed by their asset managers. By allowing me to expedite the qualifying process by asking key preliminary questions to qualify each potential buyer, I was able to synergistically assist the buyer's loan officer because, quite often, they did not get creative and think of everyway possible to qualify the client. For instance, a lot of loan officers forgot to ask if their was a potential co-borrower for an FHA loan, most Real Estate Agent would not think to ask this, I exhausted every option and program available at the given date. I spent my time on clients and transaction that closed. This experience catapulted my overall knowledge base on the current market with submitting a variety of different purchasing scenarios and understanding how to achieve a positive outcome with each different bank and client. Thus, giving me the necessary confidence and experience to launch my own full-service brokerage, Power Real Estate Group.
