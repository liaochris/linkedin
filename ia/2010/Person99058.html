JJ DiGeronimo’s Summary

A strategic initiator in Cloud Computing, JJ DiGeronimo is bold and effective as she advises Fortune 500 companies toward next generation data centers to support IT service architectures with Cloud Computing. A multifaceted talent, she over-achieves in fast-pace and high-stakes environment. Her passion for technology enables her to quickly align business obstacles to solutions that encompass skilled people, quality technology and redefined processes.

JJ has an innate ability to reveal new revenue-streams. In 2003; she created an online business that addressed technology workforce development. Entrepreneur Magazine featured the site in January 2005. The business is currently pending sale. From this initiative, she was hired to define the technology direction and work on a principal team to assist with mergers, acquisitions or relocations of technology companies that aligned with existing verticals.

As an external voice, she educates and participates in panels and keynotes focused on emerging technologies including Cloud Computing, Virtual Desktops and Green Data Center. She has also participated as a speaker at over 150 events including Project World, WITI, EURADA, Emerging Technology Forum, Business Expo and COSE and an author of articles for the Small Business Forums. In August 2008, JJ launched Tech Savvy Women (300+ members) to bring women with technology skills together across various industries to create and foster relationships while enhancing their knowledge & network.

JJ immerses herself in emerging technologies with years of technology experience and a technical degree. She has a portfolio of customers and relationships across multiple verticals including Accenture, Inktomi Corporation, National City, Eaton Corporation, Sprint PCS, Carolinas Medical Center, BellSouth, Duke Energy and Bank of America.
