Tom Galvin’s Summary

I have 27 years of equity investment experience at premier financial services organizations such as Lehman Brothers, Smith Barney, Forstmann-Leff and UBS. I am a highly regarded Chief Investment Officer, Senior Portfolio Manager, Director of Research and Investment Analyst.

Throughout my career I have demonstrated excellent stock picking, portfolio construction and communication skills. Utilizing a top down, thematic approach combined with rigorous fundamental, quantitative and technical research I have consistently been able to identify stocks that provide above average returns.

I have combined this ability with comprehensive risk management skills to construct portfolios that have consistently produced superior excess returns. The $4 billion of long only growth oriented portfolios I managed at Forstmann-Leff achieved buy ratings at PIMCO, Merrill Lynch and Frank Russell with excess returns of more than 300 basis points. AUM’s under my leadership increased 40%.

The unlevered global long/short equity carve out portfolio I managed at Forstmann Leff produced more than twice the market returns with half the exposure. The technology and financial services sector long/short equity portfolio I managed exceeded its benchmarks by a factor of 1.3x.

While co-managing $8 billion of large cap growth portfolios at UBS I led a 400 basis point turnaround in performance.

This success has continued at Galvin Asset Management. Since 1/1/08 $100,000 invested in the S&P 500 and Russell 1000 Growth Indices would be worth $74,110 and $79,320 through 5/31/10. During this same time frame $100,000 invested in my Large Cap Growth Plus and Unlevered Global Long/Short Equity products would be worth $98,580 and $83,350.

I believe my investment and managerial experience, proven track record for superior investment returns, ability to build and motivate investment teams will lead to growth in institutional and high net worth AUM's.

Tom Galvin’s Specialties:

Investment management in long/short, growth equities, asset allocation accounts. Managing investment teams. Interfacing with clients.
