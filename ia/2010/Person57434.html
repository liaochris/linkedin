An award-winning internationally-published investigative business fraud and government malfeasance/corruption journalist, I have been an advocacy and enterprise reporter for nearly 20-years.

Recently, I found out just how outstanding a journalist I must be when I found myself battling a large-scale, aggressive press suppression campaign launched to prevent me from exposing a national crime syndicate and other criminal activities engaged in by several individuals known personally and/or professionally to me.

Thus, my primary goal is to end this nightmare and tell my story--and those of others either experiencing similar attacks or who have been victimized by these criminals. Thus, look for a series of explosive stories by me and others that expose business fraud, organized criminal activity and the military and government misappropriation engaged in by the individuals engaged in these activities to cover up their crime.
