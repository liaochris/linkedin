Sarena Harvey’s Summary

Having spent several seasons renting luxurious villas in Ibiza with International Villas Ltd, I decided to relocate to Sardinia to offer the same high standards of acommodation and services with the Sardinia Holiday Concierge and The Sardinia Villa Collection.

I feel the island of Sardinia is one of the most beautiful places on earth, but can often be inaccessible to the general public. I aim to provide a reputable, personal service with a touch of luxury to connect holiday makers with the beauty and myriad of experiences Sardinia has to offer

Sarena Harvey’s Specialties:

Everything Sardinia... villa rental, hotel bookings, agriturismi, boat hire, staff (chefs, butlers, nannies, chauffeurs etc), guided tours and itineraries, wedding services, car hire and anything and everything else you could possibly need. Try us, we make the impossible possible.
