John Stringer’s Summary

John Stringer is an Intuitive Entrepreneur serving as founder of AskRealityLove.com, co-founder of PolyPlat Records and front man of the Billboard charting, indie rock band, State of Man. His music industry experience as an independent label owner and artist provided the catalyst for PolyPlat Records Artist Development Services Division and IndieHitMaker.com - an artist development company offering low cost, innovative services such as independent venue sales reporting to SoundScan. With a rapidly growing unsigned/indie/DIY artist roster and other revolutionary music industry solutions in development, PolyPlat ADSD and IndieHitMaker are poised to play an expanding role in the success stories of emerging artists.

Goals:
1. Continue to Merge Spiritual Growth and Career
2. Provide positive CONTENT (healing, inspiring, encouraging, empowering, enlightening and entertaining music) and ACTION to the global music market
3. Effectively serve the independent artist community through artist development services
4. Give generously of my time, wealth and love to family, friends (and anyone else) in an intelligent and responsible way.
