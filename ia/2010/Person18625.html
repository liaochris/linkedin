Austin O'Brion’s Summary

With 8 solid years of experience, Austin has managed technology, operations, and marketing for Fortune 500 companies and start-ups alike.

Serving a diverse mix of industries, he has marketed and advised companies in life sciences, finance, commercial real estate, data management, and fashion. Throughout his career, Austin has managed over $39M in projects, helped raise over $250M for venture-backed startups and increased client sales leads up to 543%. Prior to Martin Ashal, he won an international design award sponsored by The Art Institutes and founded a branding firm that was acquired in 2007.

Austin earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Design Management from The Art Institutes International Minnesota and earned additional credentials from the University of Notre Dame, Mendoza College of Business. He is regularly invited to speak at college campuses sharing his experience and youthful entrepreneurship.
