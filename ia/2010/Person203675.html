Tim Baskerville’s Summary

Senior manager, entrepreneur, board member, consultant or investor in a wide range of enterprises built on intellectual property. As either a strategist or roll-up-your-sleeves P&L manager, have helped unlock value in organizations ranging from startups to the Global 1000.

Extensive exposure to these sectors: publishing in vertical markets, online and offline subscription services, Internet, media, entertainment, music, games, film, telecommunications, wireless, cable, broadcasting and video.

Best known as CEO of JupiterResearch (New York), president of Kagan Research (Monterey, Calif.), founder of multinational B2B publisher Baskerville Communications (London), and operator of Vidmar Communications (Hollywood).

Tim Baskerville’s Specialties:

Turnaround management, information product development, direct response marketing, licensing of intellectual property, editorial management, writer-columnist, TV and DVD production, due diligence, post-acquisition integration, building start-up teams and field sales organizations.
