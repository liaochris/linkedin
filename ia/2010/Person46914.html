Dan Bailey’s Summary

I have over 10 years of online marketing and web strategy experience along with supervisory experience throughout my career. I was most recently the eBusiness Market Manager

purchased the entire catalog business and online store.

Today usbank.com continues to receive tens of thousands of new visitors and improved conversion rates based on the initiatives, promotions and changes I have recommended and implemented.

I have had the pleasure of working in several driven environments full of teamwork and success. I believe I can help my next marketing team exceed their future goals.
