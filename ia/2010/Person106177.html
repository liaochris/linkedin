Josh Anderson’s Summary

I first showed signs of writing acumen way back in fourth grade when I placed No. 2 to wide-rule paper and scratched out my first short story. “Romancing the Leaf” was 2-1/2 pages of unabashed adventure, romance and spelling errors. Sure it was a rip-off of “Romancing the Stone” with a more believable plot line. But hey, I was only 10.

My spelling has gotten a lot better since then, and my ideas are my own. But instead of writing fiction, I write the real-life stories of companies using mass communications and advertising. And I've parlayed my unique grasp of the written language into a successful career. Even my wife admits that I'm a prose pro.

From advertising to marketing, news editorial to financial writing and direct mail, I've created the gamut of engaging messages that target audiences of all types. I've done work for LiLu Interiors, Securian Financial Group, Allied Solutions, Cherokee National Life Insurance Company, Pisa Design, Kai*len Love + Life Architecture and Agile Mobile Solutions, all of which have benefitted from the engaging things I do with words.

Drop me a line if you think we can hook up on a project.
