David Menzies’s Summary

David D. Menzies is an award-winning professional communicator with more than 20 years experience in PR and media relations, marketing and advertising.

Working out of the technology-rich Research Triangle region of North Carolina, he services a vibrant portfolio of domestic and international clients across a multitude of industries including:

biomedical * medical devices * health care
pharmaceutical * biotech * technology transfer
finance * law * general technology * manufacturing
product design * nonprofit * animal care

Menzies is a founding member of the NC Product Design & Prototyping Co-Op and is active with the Morrisville Chamber of Commerce (Chair, Marketing Committee). He maintains membership in the Online News Association and Society of Professional Journalists, and is Editor/Publisher of the online news publication NCTechNews. Over the past ten years, Menzies has volunteered his expertise to community organizations such as Wake Health Services, Inc., TiE Carolinas and the Apex Chamber of Commerce.

Menzies Consulting, Inc. helps clients figure out what they are trying to say, who they are trying to say it to, and develops tools and methodologies to deliver that message. In addition to servicing individual client needs, Menzies Consulting, Inc. has developed a number of enhanced online news publications to contribute to a vibrant and growing economic environment including NCTechNews and PRPowerhouse.

A former print newspaper editor and reporter, Menzies is a contributing author to several regional industry publications and publishes Strategic Messaging News You Can Use, an electronic newsletter delivering tips and advice for business professionals on how to get their message out utilizing proven public relations tools and methodologies. The newsletter is distributed monthly at no charge to over 500 readers from throughout the U.S. and overseas.
