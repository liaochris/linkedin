Steven Spencer’s Summary

Mr. Spencer is consulting for several media, advertising, and mobile companies. Specific focus is in the areas of areas of advertising, social networking, media, technology and content. He serves as an executive board advisor to Mojiva.

Mr Spencer was COO of Beezag - a video advertising company leveraging web, social media and mobile delivery.

He served as CEO of UPOC Network. He has successfully integrated the business, operations and technology of Upoc into Dada following Dada’s acquisition of Upoc in August of 2006.

He is a well known industry leader and an often requested speaker at trade events. He serves as a member of the leadership board of the CTIA WIC.

His previous role at Upoc Networks was as CTO and SVP. In this role, he directed the product development and infrastructure deployment for this leading wireless messaging and marketing company. He managed the technology infrastructure, key customer relationships, development, QA and customer service. In his tenure at Upoc, the company consistently exceeded highest SLA levels.


He was previously the EVP for Wireless Systems at Pinnacor. In this role, Mr. Spencer was responsible for the overall executive leadership of the company's wireless and alt access segments. He grew this business from its start-up, to be a significant supplier of content and wireless application services to Verizon, Vodafone, Virgin Mobile, AT&T and other wireless carriers. Mr. Spencer also served as Pinnacor's CTO and headed up their Product Management discipline. Pinnacor was acquired by CBS Marketwatch in 2004.

Mr. Spencer's previous experience spans 20 years with Bell Laboratories and Lucent Technologies.

Mr. Spencer has authored eight patents.

Mr. Spencer has a BSEE from Union College, MSEE from Cornell University and an MBA from New York University's Stern School of Business.
