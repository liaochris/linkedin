Rick Storlie CMP, MCSP, CSP’s Summary

I help home contractors maximize their sales in any economy. Specifically, I help them find niche opportunities, generate leads based on that niche and increase their sales conversions. This is done by implementing a process that can be replicated and duplicated at will.

The results- a business that operates on sound marketing principals, not one that relies on a good market to make sales. Anyone can succeed in a good market. Only the best companies thrive in a down market.

If you rely on the market to make your sales, your business is only as good as the market!

Are you ready to take control of your sales?
