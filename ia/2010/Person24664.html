Blaine Austin’s Summary

I’m a well-qualified executive who would be an excellent choice for a Sales, Marketing or Operations position in a growing company. I am also very experienced in the area of Customer Service.
The majority of my experience was attained with two companies, All Brands.com and Oreck Corporation. In the two years I spent at AllBrands.com, I oversaw growth increases of 35% and $12 million in sales. In the seven years I spent at Oreck Corporation, I oversaw growth increases of 346 new stores and $150 million in sales. Most company’s greatest challenges are recruiting good people, advertising, compliance and expansion. These are areas in which I excel. I recruited and hired numerous people for positions from store mangers to regional mangers, call center associates to warehouse personnel. I have spent the last nine years learning and practicing different means of advertising. I was able to develop different marketing programs and received buy-in from the Franchisees and field employees. Compliance is another area in which you will always be challenged. When you have a group of entrepreneurs out in the field, it is often very difficult to get them to see the company vision. Ensuring company values through relationships was something that I was able to accomplish at AllBrand.com and Oreck. As you know this is an area that takes the most work and finesse.
I am a leader who is a team player. With the company vision and my leadership, I know that I can help any company achieve all of its goals.

I'm a leader who stays abreast of the day-to-day business, who earns 100% support from the staff and the field, who is very committed to ensure success, that is very experienced in Sales, Marketing and Customer Service.
