George Bounacos’s Summary

OVERVIEW

Online marketing leader, creating profits in the B2B, B2C and B2G sectors. I hold PPC certifications from Yahoo and Google and lead online marketing efforts for dozens of organizations. At least two are always pro bono non-profits.

SKILLS

A technologist with online content, SEO/SEM and usability expertise, I can lift organic rankings, raise conversion rates and write link-worthy content. I'm also an anlytics hound who adores micro-targeting.

"You talk marketing to geeks and geek to marketers," said a colleague explaining my role.

WHAT'S A SILVER BEACON?

The silver beacon is presence and profitability. We have the creative chops to make your site sparkle, your content glow and your brand shine.

Then we apply proprietary modeling based on profit, not revenue. This isn't about revenue or visitors. This is about profit and running small business well, not playing catchup all day.

Silver Beacon excels at creating incremental profit for small businesses. Don't call us an agency; we're your partner and prove that every day.

Silver Beacon is art and science. Silver Beacon is creative and data. Ask our partners and clients.

GEORGE'S BACKGROUND

The science and data side of Silver Beacon Marketing, I've also led a groundbreaking online consumer advocacy firm, search marketing for multiple IYPs, consumer efforts for big brands and large content networks with tens of thousands of pieces of unique content.

In earlier operations roles, I managed B2B help desks, cross-functionally teamed with Marketing at a top automotive site, led an online community for an eBay affiliate and was first paid for generating online content in 1986!

Creating compelling content accessed at 300 baud while customers paid $48/hour for access may be one of life's great marketing challenges.

INDUSTRY EXPERIENCE

Online marketing, SEO/SEM, customer segmentation, keyword research, content creation, communities, public speaking.
