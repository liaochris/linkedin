Vivek Das’s Summary

I have been associated with the Internet as a marketer since 2003 with some of the leading names of the industry. My relationship with the Internet has evolved into a harmonious one over the years and I have been closely associated with its progress in India.

I have led agency and account teams with India's leading interactive agencies BC Web Wise and Webchutney amongst other assignments. The challenges have guided me to continually push the boundaries of my own experiences to enable richer consumer & customer engagements by leveraging the power of interactive marketing.
