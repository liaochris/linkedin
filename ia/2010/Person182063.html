Sanjay Kumar’s Summary

Work Aspirations ...
"Add value to life by Designing better products & applications + be good with people"

Sanjay Kumar is a user experience design and usability professional. He has worked with Fortune 500 clients like GE & CITI Bank, helping them envision and improve the experiences and usability of their web sites and applications.

Worked extensively in User Experience Management, Financial Products, User interface design and information architecture methodologies, including UCD(User-centered design), heuristic evaluations, User Interface Competitive analysis, accessibility, wire framing, prototyping, usability testing, User surveys and iterative development. His unique background bridges the gap between creative, engineering, and business goals.

Currently he is involved in User Experience Consulting for developing mobile device applications for travel & transportation industry group of Mind Tree Ltd. He is active in writing usability articles and brown papers pertaining to user experience & mobile usability. Worked on Kotak NRI banking proposals and came up with two alternate NRI home page designs. Developing new User Experience designs for applications is a passion for him; he is also involved in user experience consulting for eBay.com to come up with alternative designs to improve the overall user experience of eBay.com listing section.Conducted Remote usability testing using Morae usability testing tool. Experience in IA & card sorting.
