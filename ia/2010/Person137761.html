Mattias Karlsson’s Summary

I have a deep and true fascination regarding the mystery of good software development. How can we increase the quality of the code? How can we make the development process more predictable? How can we reduce the test effort? Perhaps the solution is within Lean Software Development? It there an Agile "Toyota model" for software development to discover?

I have experience from many different roles like: developer, architect, team leader, coach, manager, teacher and skills manager.

I lecture Test Driven Development, Java EE, Testing with mock objects and “How to manage your internal competence development”
