Craig Kempf’s Summary

Craig Kempf is the founder of Follow Me to Haiti, a professional photographer, pilot, and (in his spare time) the CEO of CK Communications, Inc.
CK Communications, Inc. (CKC) is an award-winning advertising, design and public relations company. The goal of CKC is to provide dynamic and results-focused strategic marketing to ensure effective communication across all channels. From logo design to Web design and from advertising campaigns to media relations, CKC is a one-stop resource for organizations of all sizes.

Craig Kempf’s Specialties:
