Daisy Wright’s Summary

If you are ready to move your career forward, then partner with someone who has walked in your shoe.

My career has taken many a twist and turn, probably like you...but are you enjoying what you are doing? Are you loving your job? I am!

I have been making a difference to peoples' careers since 1995 when I began writing professional resumes. In 2003, I expanded my services after earning a post-graduate certificate in career development. Below is a synopsis of my credentials and professional involvement:
