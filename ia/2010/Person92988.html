Jeff Blosel’s Summary

• An innovative financial and tax expert with significant operations background. A graduate of Penn State University and a member of the American Institute and Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants.

• Began career with Ernst & Young in Pittsburgh, becoming a Tax Partner in 1987. Taught firm-wide education programs, was a frequent speaker at business and tax seminars and specialized in start-up, closely-held, and family-owned businesses ranging in size from $10 Million to $500 Million.

• In 1994, became CFO and a member of the board of directors for a $50 Million industrial packaging manufacturer and distributor.

• In 2001, purchased a manufacturer and distributor of freight damage prevention products, tripling its size and selling the business in 2009 to a publicly-held company.

• Now providing consulting in my areas of expertise to other companies.
