Charles Henri’s Summary

«Design is the result of a concept that perfectly combines functionality, structure and aesthetics. But mostly, design is all about the unspoken stuff that ignites and inspires all communication.»

Educated at UQAM, then at the great school of GSM (my first job!) where I gained rigor and discipline - and most of all - a taste for simplicity and minimalist aesthetic.

Over the years, I have assimilated a vast experience in Web and print design. Having founded Graphic Minds Design Studio in 2002, I perfected my method of work and my ability to communicate.

Now senior designer at Kryzalid, I pour my creativity into my designs and on our blog.

Charles Henri’s Specialties:

Web design, print design, branding, illustration, animation.
