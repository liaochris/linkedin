Anji Bee’s Summary

I've been obsessed with music since day one and have explored many angles of the biz. As a youngster I created fanzines, took recording classes, and was a college DJ, then went on to sign with both a music label and a new media company. I currently host several popular podcasts and vidcasts. In addition to my own band, Lovespirals, I have collaborated with a number of bands including Chandeen and Bitstream Dream. My music has been licensed worldwide in many mediums, including TV, cable, DVDs, and video games, such as EA Games, The Oprah Winfrey Show, and multiple placings with MTV, VH1, E! TV, The WB Network, and more. Many people have asked me what "my dayjob is" and I can honestly say that it is independent music creation and podcasting.
