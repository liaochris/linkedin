Dawn Nelson’s Summary

Dawn earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Communication – and a Minor in Business – from Washington State University. Prior to entering the real estate profession, she worked for 15 years in the research and development field, holding positions of Business Manager, Leasing Operations Manager, and Facilities & Operations Manager.

Upon becoming a full-time Real Estate professional in 2007, Dawn quickly became a top producer in one of West Seattle’s premier offices.

Dawn specializes in listing and selling single family homes, condominiums and multi-unit buildings in the greater Seattle area, including Ballard, Capitol Hill, Queen Anne and West Seattle. Her attention to detail and the emphasis she places on educating her clients throughout the transaction has earned her high praise from her loyal clients and fellow associates.

Whether you are an experienced investor or a first time buyer, Dawn can help you in choosing the "right" property, making an offer, negotiating, financing, getting the best mortgage rates, moving, and everything involved in making a smart real estate decision!

A diverse background in the fields of advertising, business and facility management, remodeling, and property management all contribute to Dawn’s unique style, the hallmark of her young-but-successful real estate career.
