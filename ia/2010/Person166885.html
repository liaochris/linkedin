Sandy Rockwerk’s Summary

For the past 30 years, Sandy has stage managed more than 600 Industrials, involving over 1500 performances. The size and scope of these events run the full gamut from centennial mega-celebrations, to business meetings, to one day award/benefit events.

His combined experience in production and stage management enables him to run extremely efficient and productive events even under typically compressed show schedules with limited set up and rehearsal times. His attention to detail and ability to anticipate unforeseen circumstances enables him to provide clear-cut and precise direction that results in smooth-running and highly successful shows.

Sandy’s business is founded on numerous repeat clients. Some clients have been with him for as long as 22 years. Many others fall into the 9 - 13 year range.
