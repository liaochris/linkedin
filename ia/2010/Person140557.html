Melinda Hodell’s Summary

Extensive experience in business and management. Licensed psychologist with specialty in change leadership and culture change. Expertise in creating results in difficult environments, coaching leaders to maximize their success, creating high-performing teams, leading corporate culture shifts, organizing financial turnarounds and innovation engines. Top-of-chart marks in client satisfaction and leadership.

Throughout Dr. Hodell’s extensive career, she has been involved in all aspects of helping executives lead their organizations. In the early 1990’s, she worked for RHR International, a firm of corporate psychologists specializing in executive assessment, executive coaching, leadership team development and organizational redesign. RHR is the preeminent consulting firm in defining the discipline of executive coaching and assessment.

Melinda also worked for Kaiser Permanente, the market-leading healthcare organization in California, as the managing director of an internal consulting department called Strategy and Culture Alignment (SCA). She was a member of the senior leadership team and reported to the President. She led her SCA team in partnering with senior executives throughout Kaiser Permanente to create and sustain a new performance-oriented culture that stressed customer service, clinical quality and financial well-being.

Melinda’s expertise in executive coaching and leadership team development is a result of her business orientation, leadership abilities and psychological knowledge. She has held line management positions in customer service, sales and marketing and organizational development in publishing, retail, computer and healthcare companies. She is a licensed psychologist with a specialty in organizational psychology. She received graduate counseling training at Loyola Marymount University and obtained her Ph.D. in social psychology from Stanford University.
