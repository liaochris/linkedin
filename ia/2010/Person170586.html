Tim Hutchins’s Summary

Being in the industry for 10 years now, I have learned from a lot of great people. I've learned that it's growth that never stops. There is always so much to learn in an ever changing industry.

I grew up watching my father do great things with his clients.
They included McDonalds, Pier 1, Volkswagon (and numerous other national accounts)

I have worked virtually every facet of the business, establishing highly developed skills in broadcast, print advertising, web design, programming and collateral design. I've worked for JCPenney Company and ad agencies that include The Richards Group, Levenson And Hill and Marcus And Associates. Also, all advertising mediums for Pizza Inn, Guaranty Insurance, Furr's Cafeteria, Sprint, GE, mystockbroker.com, Rent A Center, JC Penney, Papa Johns and others.

I've been in the creative industry for over 10 years and I have very much enjoyed writing, art and creating endearing brands. I also enjoy working with other great talent because it allows me to grow conceptually and have fun at the same time.
