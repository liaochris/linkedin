Pam Usher’s Summary

The Most Important Things You Should Know About Business Coach ...Pam Usher
Pam’s coaching services are framed around
• Challenging business owners to greater success
• Providing practical support to business managers/owners, Boards and company owners
• Testing and monitoring all important growth and profit points of your business
• Partnering for Business Success, including through the transition from growth to maturity
• Coaching towards formulating balanced lifestyle goals
Pam has held a lifelong passion for growing businesses and ensuring they are sustained for the long term.
1. After 25 years as a CEO, coach and mentor of many different companies and people throughout Australia, she has developed and tested ways to ensure profitability is a key feature of a business, equal to having a team who are productive and happy, and who stay and grow with the company, particularly in this time of skills shortage. Increasing growth by 700% and profitability by 30% of an organisation in a highly competitive market was one of many successes Pam has had.
2. Having led small and medium-sized companies throughout Australia, she understands the challenges of a newly establishing organisation through to large, mature companies in both the Not-for-Profit and Commercial sectors.
3. As your coach, Pam will encourage the importance of successful team development, ensuring strong cash-flow, knowing the importance of marketing for strong growth, successful public relations and marketing, through to having profitable job costing models, and many other essential business systems which help streamline the business owner or manager’s time.....it’s your choice
4. Through coaching, Pam will provide practical support coming from a strong base of firsthand management and leadership experiences. She will partner with you; supporting progress in business growth for profitability, and helping you develop your lifestyle goals, balancing work and home life.
