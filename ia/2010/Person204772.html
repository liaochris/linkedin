Titus Capilnean’s Summary

In the past three years, I have been active in both the university and AIESEC, the largest youth driven leadership organization.

My first steps in the organization were in the finance department. I then moved on to the Training area, where we created 2 events for students - one on personal and one on professional development, me designing and implementing the promotion campaign.

During the second part of 2007 and early 2008, I became project manager of an on-line platform for which we managed to create a complex documentation. In Autumn 2007, I delivered my first trainings and facilitations along with my first interviewing experience.

In 2008 I became Vice-President Communication. Throughout 1 year and a half, I, together with my team of 20, designed and delivered 16 communication campaigns, achieving 1300 applicants in 3 recruitments and over 3000 students participating in our events. Also we gathered over 1500 media coverage in my term. 12 websites, on-line mailings and banner campaigns, usage of social media are only some of my term's achievements.

I took part in over 20 local and national conferences, 4 international ones, where in 4 of them I was a facilitator. I also developed a learning program for my team members and delivered 22 trainings on soft and hard skills. In June - September 2008 I designed and delivered AIESEC University Communication Track, manging the agenda and speakers and charing the event itself.

I also took part in events like Trust by Danone - where we acheived national 4th place, Olimpiadele Comunicarii, "Meet me in Diversity" - Maribor, Slovenia - cultural diversity and communication conference, EBBF forum and Biz Days.

For my team and my activities in 2008-2009 I achieved the Excellence Award 2008 and the External Positioning Award 2009 in the AIESEC Romania network.

I am very interested in on line communication, copyrighting and creative works, like freelance designing in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
