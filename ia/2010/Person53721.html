Debra Atlas’s Summary

I am a freelance environmental writer. I focus on sustainability, eco-innovations and as-needed topics of interest.

I write a weekly column called "Distinctly Green" for a regional newspaper (http://3.ly/qib).and am a regular contributor to the Red Ferret Journal (see http://3.ly/p5w), a well-known website dedicated to presenting the latest innovations and gadgets from around the globe

In March 2010, I launched Eco Trends (http://bit.ly/ahWih8), a radio feature that discusses the newest, innovative eco-friendly consumer products on the market. This fun, informative radio spot can be heard live (or archived) online every Tuesday and Thursday around 3:20pm Pacific.

I’ve written for 3 business journals, including feature articles for the Upstate Business Journal (www.upstatebusiness.net). I also contribute to regional and national magazines and write regularly for several websites, including www.e-how.com.

I excel in researching, interviewing and accurate reporting. I always meet deadlines on time (or early). My articles can be counted on to be thought provoking, informative and inspiring.

I have been a professional speaker since 1993. I have presented to a variety of venues - from large conferences (including Inc Magazine's "Growing the Company " conference) to workshop and seminar settings.

Debra Atlas’s Specialties:

sustainability, Innovations in environmentally-related solutions, LEED-related stories, green building, alternative energy sources, eco-tourism, other environmental issues
