James Stanford’s Summary

My passion is to grow companies, regardless of their size, to meet their true potential. My first start-up, at the young age of 16, was a retail business selling Sinclair PCs with great success in the early 80s.

My experience spans from building and running start-ups to leadership and executive management roles in top Fortune 100 companies in owning full P&L responsibilities, leading marketing, driving innovation and managing operations. I've lived and worked on four continents and have built and led global teams.

Among my latest notable accomplishments, I led and grew Logitech (Nasdaq: LOGI) Strategic oem business to $200 million/yr. shipping 300 million units, serving CE and top tier PC OEM/ODMs such as Apple, Lenovo, Dell, HP, SONY. I became one of the three General Managers at Logitech to turn-around of a retail business unit and recently served as a Chief Strategy Officer and President to an Intel Capital Portfolio Company.

Now I help a number of hi-tech companies by working with their CEO and Board Members to develop their corporate, product and go-to-market strategies. I serve on the board of two companies and I enjoy and welcome any new challenging opportunity.

Connect: James@SocialVenturesInc.com
