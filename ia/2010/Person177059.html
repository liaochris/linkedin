Ronald Van Surksum, CFP’s Summary

I am a Certified Financial Planner (TM) Practitioner and a member of the Financial Planning Association (FPA) and NAPFA (National Association of Personal Financial Advisors). I have been in the financial services business since 1992. I have been with Advanced Asset Management since its inception in 1997 and became majority owner in 2009.

Ronald Van Surksum, CFP’s Specialties:

I am a family practitioner, I review your financial position and consult on ways for you to improve your situation and work towards and achieve your financial goals.
