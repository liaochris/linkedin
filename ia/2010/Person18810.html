Avi Brown’s Summary

Experienced senior level ad operations professional with a deep understanding of online advertising. Specializing in cutting edge ad serving solutions, rich media, ad network optimization, mobile and social media ad integration. Develops and manages exciting new ad products that span far beyond traditional banner and in-video advertising -- specifically within the application and widget based platforms of today and tomorrow. Consistent track record of creating and applying ad optimization strategies and techniques, producing tremendous growth in market-share and income or drastically reducing costs. Possesses the rare skill of fluently speaking both the language of the ad business and tech talk. Excels in a fast paced environment.

Avi Brown’s Specialties:

• Multiple ad serving platforms including Doubleclick DART / DFP, Google Ad Manager and Right Media Yield Manager.

• Custom ad integration within app based mobile and social media platforms such as iPhone/iPod Touch, Facebook, MySpace, Bebo, etc.

• Ad Network Optimization - Maximizing revenue generated by remnant (unsold) inventory

• Third party rich media integration - Experience with vendors such as PointRoll, EyeWonder, EyeBlaster, DoubleClick DFA, Atlas, etc.
