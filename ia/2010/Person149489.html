Mark Moran’s Summary

Director, Business Development for Ingersoll Rand Security Technologies.

Chair of the Board of Advisors to National Center for Spectator Sports Safety and Security. Board of Advisors for the Center at the University of Southern Mississippi includes Security & Facility Directors from Major League Baseball, NBA, NHL, Major League Soccer, NFL, National Association of Collegiate Directors of Athletics, NCAA, NASCAR, Indy Racing League, USGA, LPGA, US Olympics Committee, US Open Tennis Association & officials from Department of Homeland Security, architectural firm Populous, Stadium Managers Association & Robinson Cole LLP.

Chair of the Committee of the National Laboratory for Spectator Sports Safety and Security Strategic Council.

New York Building Foundation Board of Governors

Served on the NASCAR B to B Council & sponsored the NASCAR Security & Safety Championship Awards.

Featured in Forbes Magazine, Bloomberg Television, NASCAR.com, KFAN Sports Radio, Speedway Illustrated, Street and Smith’s conference on Sports Sponsorships.

Co-produced the “Security and Safety Guideline Reference Manual” for the NASCAR Security Summit which is used as a reference in the 2008 Department of Homeland Security Publication entitled, “Protective Measures Guide for U.S. Sports Leagues.”

Subject Matter Expert (SME) for the National Risk Management Program for Spectator Sports & in the creation of the curriculum for the Sports Event Risk Management Participants Guide for a NACDA/DHS Grant.

Center for Architecture Professional Member of AIA, New York Chapter.

Member of the New York Building Congress

Awarded Ingersoll Rand’s Security Technologies Presidents Award for Operation Home Delivery launched by Habitat for Humanity International to help provide assistance and rebuilding opportunities in New Orleans and along the Gulf Coast

Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) Pepperdine Univ

Bachelor of Arts (BA) Rollins College
