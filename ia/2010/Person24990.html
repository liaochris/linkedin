Remy Arteaga’s Summary

I have very deep experience with Internet based new ventures and start-ups. I have been the CEO/COO of 5 new ventures over the last 16 years.

I graduated with an engineering degree. After several successful start-ups I obtained my MBA degree. My first exposure to business innovation was with General Motors where I was selected to be part of a very innovative think tank whose mission was to "change the way GM did business."

After GM, I was involved with a number of IT projects which eventually led to my first start-up - a digital imaging service business. That business turned its first profit after 6 months and was acquired by a printing firm. The next star-up was an Internet sports magazine, which was acquired by IGN.com for $7M. I went on to be the CEO of an Internet games portal, a software technology firm and a social media analytics firm. In these ventures, I have been primarily focused on new business development and online marketing.

Over the years I have been deeply involved in all aspects of marketing an Internet business including Search Engine Optimization, Search Engine Marketing, and Social Media Marketing. Along similar lines, I have been focused on business development and negotiating large scale deals with billion dollar Multinational firms.
