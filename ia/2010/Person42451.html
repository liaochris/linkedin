Colin Goodwin’s Summary

I am currently completing my final year of a Bachelor of Business Administration program with a major in Marketing at Acadia University in Wolfville, Nova Scotia. I am also presently the Vice President of Social Media Marketing & Promotions of SIFE (Students in Free Enterprise) Acadia, an international business society operating on 1,800 university campuses in more than 45 countries. SIFE encourages students to gain practical experience in projects managing many different people and processes. I am currently a member of their executive council.

As the Marketing & Brand Development Summer Student at ServiceOntario, I gained valuable work experience and knowledge of the marketing field. I am also currently a participant in the national "Canada's Next Top Ad Exec" student business competition, utilizing non-traditional marketing, such as Social Media, Digital, Event, Guerilla, Viral, and Mobile strategies to develop a pre-launch campaign for the 2011 Chevrolet Cruze.

Additionally, I have performed a number of volunteer roles during my time at Acadia. Most notably, these have included the titles of “New Student Ambassador”, where I showed and sold potential students on the town of Wolfville and the university itself, and “Video Recruitment Representative”, where I worked with both the university’s recruitment office to produce video podcasts that marketed Acadia University to potential students.

I am a positive contributor in a team environment and enjoy building relationships. My strengths have consistently proven to be my ability to communicate effectively, both through team and leadership roles; my energy and passion that I bring to a task; my ability to pick up new concepts quickly; my persistence for perfecting new ideas; and, my creative and intuitive nature.

I am an enthusiastic and motivated student who is looking for an opportunity to apply my academic background and work experience to a meaningful, interesting career where I can have an impact.
