I am an Independent Executive Search Consultant and Head Hunter with more than 15 years of strong experience with specialization in CEO search.

My core business is to ensure the highest standards of professionalism in the practice of Executive Search and by that to give new perspectives to my clients as well as my candidates. When companies seek to hire senior-level management from outside their organization, I make sure they hire not just any qualified person, but the most qualified and appropriate person to meet the challenges of the position and by that the future of the company. As a retained Executive Search Consultant I work under an exclusive contract with the client organisation dedicated to searches that succeed.

I have an excellent knowledge of the executive search business in CIS market. I had been successfully working with several international Executive Search Companies with their projects in Russia and Ukraine.
