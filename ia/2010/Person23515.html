Mike Browne’s Summary

Mike Browne
Artrepreneur

Mike Browne grew up with the intention to be a syndicated a cartoonist like Charles Shulz. Thousands of drawings later in combination with digital tools, Mike has created content for all four screens. The ability to character design, storyboard, animate and edit comes in handy in today's multi-platform environment. He is a creative Swiss Army knife using Flash as his main tool.

Combining his art talents, gaming experience and knowledge of social media, this holiday, LoveFinderGames.com will launch on Facebook as well as host it's own game site. TheKissingFishGame will be an online dating aquarium where people around the globe will meet playing a fun social dating game.

LoveFinderGames mottos is,

Play Games ❤ Find Love

Look for a viral Facebook Christmas Greeting featuring singing fish mid December.

On a personal note, shortstop Mike hung up the cleats years ago. No more motorcycles, football, karate, waterskiing etc. Knee operations are expensive. Today road bikes and rowing crew have been the athletic venture. Kite surfing is next, hopefully in Greece.
