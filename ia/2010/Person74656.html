Gerald Noemdoe’s Summary

At Viorum Capital, Gerald is lead advisor to financial sponsors and corporations on critical elements of their value-creating strategies. Viorum’s advisory services match the speed, efficiency, and rigor with which M&A transactions need to be executed to secure optimal results.

Viorum’s comprehensive capabilities in M&A and bringing emerging business initiatives to market have been honed over more than 20 years of professional experience with Big “4” advisory firms, investment banking firms, multi-national corporations, start-up ventures, and companies in transition. Our professionals executed close to 100 advisory engagements for financial sponsors and corporations in the US, Canada, Europe, and Africa.

Viorum’s team have led or had significant participation in a wealth of strategy, operations, M&A and financial advisory projects that focused on creating sustainable value for clients. A few select examples include:

> Led a multi-disciplinary team on a strategic review and shareholder value analysis of 12 businesses for an underperforming $800 million publicly traded industrial conglomerate, including advising management on restructuring to restore shareholder value
> Led management consulting team implementing M&A best practices at a $4 billion biotech company
> Led multi-disciplinary teams performing more than 60 due diligence reviews for corporate clients and private equity investors on acquisitions ranging in value between $20 million and $36 billion
> Formulated and advised an underperforming $100 million engineering solution company on its repositioning and acquisition strategy.
