Victoria Klein’s Summary

The infamous "creatively versatile eco-powerhouse" is still at large! Professionally, you'll find me writing, taking pictures, and providing eco-consulting services to businesses of all sizes. Born + raised in Indiana until age 9, I've also moved nearly 20 times since then. These family- and career-related moves have given me the chance to explore the delight and difficulty of the human experience, and our effect on the planet, which I share through my writing + photography projects.

While on the hunt for fresh ways to share my knowledge + experiences, I've had the pleasure of seeing my writings published in numerous magazines + websites, such as: Yoga Journal, CRAFT, ReadyMade, Boho, Life.Gaiam.com, Venus, InnerRewards.com, Yogi Times, Greenopia.com, Sprig.com, and Office Solutions.

My photographic images have been published in Mother Nature News Magazine and are available as prints in my Etsy shop.

On a personal level, my interests and pursuits expand to an even larger plateau: knitting, sewing, reading, practicing yoga, Buddhism, tattoos, cooking, interior design, gardening, bicycling ... just to name a few. Though I've put my professional modeling days behind me, I still enjoy a fun photo shoot now and then.
