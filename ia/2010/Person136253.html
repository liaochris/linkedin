Mary Pat Blake’s Summary

Senior marketing and operations executive with deep experience with Fortune 500 companies as well as new business ventures. Founder of three companies. Board Director. Expertise in business growth, including mining for new growth in existing businesses, extensive new product ideation and development, new venture development and management, and mergers & acquisitions (strategy, search, due diligence, integration of new acquisitions). Expert in brand development, advertising, marketing, sales. Executive coach. Consultant to business and law firms, including expert witness work for business lawsuits.
