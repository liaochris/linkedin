Alexandra Pao, PHR’s Summary

“Power up Companies by Powering up Their People!”

It is people that make companies great and people that drive profits. It is people that will help companies thrive in this economy. Like doctors diagnose challenges with the human body, I diagnose challenges with the Human Resource segment of a company.

JFK said in 1959 that "When written in Chinese the word crisis is composed of two characters - one represents danger and the other represents opportunity."

Another often quoted comment from Shakespeare was this…
There is a tide in the affairs of men.
Which, taken at the flood, leads on to fortune;
Omitted, all the voyage of their life
Is bound in shallows and in miseries.
On such a full sea are we now afloat,
And we must take the current when it serves,
Or lose our ventures.

This economy is an opportunity, a tide, that we must master or lose an opportunity to capture a greater share of a smaller market and the lion’s share of a growing market. Let me show you my concept of TEEAM (Test, Educate, Empower, Audit, and Motivate) and how TEEAM will help you create the most powerful team in your industry.

If you agree with JFK and Shakespeare and if you are ready to shift your business paradigm to a “Professional Sports Team”, contact me at (562) 665-8866.
