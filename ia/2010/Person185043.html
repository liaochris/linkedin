John Basile’s Summary

About Scraster Professional Screencasting:

Borrowing from years of experience in web and radio production and a natural talent for understanding and explaining, Scraster Professional Screencasting produces polished, enhanced screencasts, or scrasts, for clients needing to communicate a message with web users, customers or employees.

A screencast is a narrated video of computer screen actions. Scraster's brand of videos are post-produced with zooms, pans, and highlights, as well as eye-catching supplementary graphics to effectively convey complex information not adequately conveyed with text or still images. Please visit http://www.scraster.com for a 3-minute video explanation of how Scraster can help your organization communicate more effectively with professional screencasting. Contact info@scraster.com for more information.

professional screencasting, instructional design, online video consultation
