David Buttner’s Summary

Mr. Buttner has over 25 years experience in the management consulting, banking, and government industries. He has considerable project management experience. Dave is also a PMI-certified Project Management Professional (PMP) as well as a Six-Sigma Green Belt. He will soon complete his Six-Sigma Black Belt certification.

During his consulting career, Dave has provided operations improvement, technology design, development, and implementation support to clients in a wide range of industries. His focus for the past several years has been in food, beverage, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.

Mr. Buttner’s recent accomplishments include designing and implementing plant-wide maintenance enhancement programs for several clients, as well as providing interim project management support on technology implementation projects.

Prior to starting his own practice, Dave was with the international consulting firm of Deloitte & Touche as a senior-level consultant.

David Buttner’s Specialties:

Business Process Improvement, Continuous Improvement, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC), Project Management, Balanced Scorecard, Kaizen, Lean Six Sigma, Activity-Based Management (ABM/ABC), Reliability-Centered Maintenance (RCM), and Total Productive Maintenance (TPM).
