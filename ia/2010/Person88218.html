James Kozuch’s Summary

Mr. Kozuch has extensive experience litigating intellectual property matters and has been lead counsel in jury trials, bench trials, and hearings in cases involving patent infringement, trademark infringement, misappropriation of trade secrets, breach of non-competition agreements, and breach of technology service contracts.
He also counsels clients on all aspects of intellectual property law, prosecutes U.S. and international patent applications in the areas of mechanical engineering and business methods, and provides opinions, including patentability, infringement/non-infringement, validity, and insurance coverage opinions.
Before becoming an attorney in 1985, Mr. Kozuch worked for 10 years in engineering, strategic planning, and financial analysis for several energy-related companies, and is experienced with electric power generation, petroleum refining, co-generation, synthetic fuels, alternative energy sources, and the exploration for and mining of coal and uranium. He has been a licensed Professional Engineer in Pennsylvania since 1977 and is a member of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME).
With his experience and education in engineering (BSME) and business (MBA), Mr. Kozuch communicates well with inventors and executives developing business strategies for managing their intellectual property assets, as well as with technical experts and economic damages experts in the litigation arena.

James Kozuch’s Specialties:

James Kozuch is a trial attorney who handles patent infringement cases and other intellectual property (IP) matters. He also counsels clients on IP law issues, prosecutes U.S. and international patent applications, and provides patent opinions.
