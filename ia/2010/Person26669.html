Brad Staker’s Summary

Staker Ventures is an Industry Leader in funding; from private equity firms to angels, VC’s, public offerings and corporate endorsements. Staker can help you connect to the best kind of capital for your company and prepare you to receive the investment you are seeking. We have established a distinguished reputation by assisting many clients become investor ready and ultimately funded.

Staker prepares businesses for their first or second round of investment capital. We offer reputable backing for financial and business planning and documentation, preparation of the management team and targeting of funding sources. We allow the management team to focus on running their business while maximizing every hour spent on the time eaten up by the demands during this important period of growth.

The Advisory Council of Staker Ventures has 54 years of global experience in small and medium sized businesses across a range of touching nearly every industry, in finance and real estate funding. Staker has worked with organizations such as Digital Hollywood, World Trade Center, Atticus Capital, Development Associates, Jefferies & Company.

In our Current economy, the companies that succeed are expected to concentrate on their core competencies while leaving other tasks to expert organizations that can accomplish the same or better results faster and at less cost.
