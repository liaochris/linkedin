Jeff Shipp’s Summary

After receiving a Masters in Financial Engineering from Kent State I was a futures proprietary trader in Chicago. I then moved back to my home state of Texas where I worked with a futures trading software company Trading Technologies (TT). With TT I worked with over 100 energy traders in software training and strategy development. I then left TT to become a Quantitative Analyst for Whiteside Energy where I worked primarily with their risk team, improving and implementing faster and more efficient methods of position recon, portfolio P&L and Greeks calculation. In addition, I helped design the infrastructure and strategies to be used in a systematic/algorithmic trading portfolio.

I am currently working as a sales director for Logical Information Machines (LIM) whose products are known as the top of the line in Data Management for the Capital Markets.

Jeff Shipp’s Specialties:

Risk Management and Trade Analysis. VBA and MATLAB programming. Software Sales including public speaking for group sales presentations and individual training. Systematic/Algorithmic portfolio designing, building and implementation.
