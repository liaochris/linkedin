Kevin Thompson, MPA’s Summary

Kevin is a speaker and consultant to companies, organizations and individuals helping them to identify and embrace their talents and strengths, maximizing their productivity and improving their bottom line.

He leverages more than 25 years of leadership and knowledge to improve work performance and customer service outcomes. An author and eight time award winning professional speaker, Kevin is certified as a Six Sigma Black Belt in Process Improvement and has a Masters in Public Administration.
