    Are you a person that has filed bankruptcy?
    No worries, if you've filed bankruptcy, we can still help you avoid a foreclosure on properties that were included in the BK.

    Most Attorneys will tell you that you don't have to worry about the property after filing, but in order for the bank to take legal title to a property that was included in a bankruptcy, they must remove the property from the bankruptcy and then continue with the foreclosure process.
