Christopher Burkhart’s Summary

Currently I am a Structural Materials Representative (SMR) acting as a Quality Assurance contractor to the State of California Department of Transportation (CALTRANS) on the Self Anchoring Suspension Bridge construction project. This bridge is replacing the existing Oakland Bay Bridge span from Oakland to Yerba Buena Island. As SMR, I ensure all materials and fabrication meet the specifications outlined in California's Standard Specifications.
