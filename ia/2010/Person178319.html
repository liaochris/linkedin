Rajan Vasudevan’s Summary

Rajan Vasudevan has over 18years experience in business and software development and has held key management positions in Software Consulting.Organization specializing in software exports and 100% EOU units.

Rajan founded Safaltek with a passion to design a Software Services 2.0 organization (customer centric software company) that revolves around the customer experience. Largely credited with the Company's increased growth and earnings, Rajan has focused Safaltek's resources into developing new markets and customers. Known for leading by example, Rajan has encouraged his team to excel at listening to customers and providing them with world-class network and software solutions & services.

Rajan has worked for several years in US prior to returning to India in 1993. He has extensive experience in business development and managing offshore and offsite engineering teams. As a Technocrat, Rajan is Informed and well tuned to contemporary technology trends in business solutions and experience in key verticals, including Healthcare, Manufacturing, Mortgage Banking, Real Estate, e-Learning, Bio-informatics, Business Intelligence, GIS, CAD and EAI.

Rajan has a B.Tech from IIT Delhi with Masters degree(s) from Stanford University, USA. He is well traveled in US, Europe, Asia.

Prior to starting Safaltek Software Pvt. Ltd, Rajan has held VP Engineering & CTO and CEO positions at smart Data Enterprises (I) Ltd.; Managing Director at AMPRO Information Systems, Delhi; Technical Director at RM Software Pvt. Ltd, Delhi.
