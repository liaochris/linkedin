Pervez Mohammed’s Summary

Marketing & Business Development Professional. Provides hands on help to CPG / FMCG companies seeking to expand internationally by
- scoping opportunities,
- helping to develop entry strategy and business plans to build
profitable and long term sustainable market positions,
- finding partners, and
- facilitating business & cross cultural relationships.

Specific expertise in Emerging, Multicultural & Ethnic Markets.

Currently operates as independent market development consultant managing a leading European dairy's export interests in the North American ethnic market, and, does consulting / business development for non-competing clients, most recently in Europe, India and SE Asia. Before going independent, pursued career with two major multinationals:
