Linda Ricci’s Summary

Senior Business / Marketing Strategist with international consulting & management experience. Results driven executive with a proven track record developing consumer-insight driven strategic plans that leverage brands into tangible, creative, successful new directions.

My passion lies with developing strategies at the intersection of brand, business, creativity, culture, and technology. Insight-generating market research capabilities, strong analytical skills, creative lateral thinking abilities and a big picture outlook combine to create strategic and pragmatic solutions.

As an experienced consultant and manager with proven leadership and management skills, I enjoy working with a team to solve complex challenges and leading implementation of the results.

Proven business development and client management skills; independently won and built long-term relationships with blue chip clients across a wide range of industries and countries..
