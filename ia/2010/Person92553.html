Jeanetta Lee Findleton’s Summary

Designer / Merchandiser / Actor

Fashion, Jewelry and Graphic Designer known for an innovative and edgy aesthetic. Natural eye for color and fabric, with an eclectic understanding of garment construction and fit. Specializing in cutting edge women’s contemporary apparel, dresses, accessories and evening wear. Published in multiple trade magazines including Gen Lux and Apparel News, and two feature films.

I am a very creative, motivated and talented fashion designer and merchandiser with a keen eye for fabrics, colors and textures and I always keep up on the latest trends and fashion forecasts. My specialty is designing women’s contemporary dresses - from day to evening, and selecting fabric and trim. My vision and designs are innovative and cutting-edge and I am able to see them through from concept to completion. My drawing and flat sketching abilities allow for easy communication with the design team and overseas factories to ensure proper design and fit. I have extensive technical and construction knowledge with a thorough understanding of patterns and the woman body that will allow me to work efficiently with the patternmakers and sample makers during fittings. My detailed eye for fit and fabric can be a great asset during meetings, as I wont let the smallest flaw go unnoticed. I am extremely computer and tech savvy with many years experience designing flats and line sheets on Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as creating specs and tech packages on Excel.
