Bren Wilson’s Summary

CURRENTLY:
I am freelancing for games as well as broadcast on commercials with clients ranging from Toyota to KFC.
Additionally I am polishing up my Videography and Post Production skills.

A BRIEF HISTORY:
I was an instructor at the Art Institute of Dallas in the Audio / Video production Department while completing the Computer Animation and Multimedia Program.

Upon Graduation I accepted a position at Origin Systems as a modeler / animator on Ultima Online. Eventually I was promoted to Art Lead and managed up to 3 additional artists.

I continued with Origin for 5 years and acted as Lead for UO, UO The Second Age, UO 3rd Dawn I was also brought on to Ultima 9 for character and cinematic aniamtion.
Additionally I was the animation lead for UO2 where I managed 4 creature animators and Directed multiple motion capture shoots at EA's Vancouver studio.. This involved scheduling, Auditioning talent, pipeline development, directing performances, etc...

I then moved on to NC Soft. During my 7+ years there, I served as both Animation Lead and Senior Animator throughout the development of Tabula Rasa. After shipping Tabula Rasa, NC Soft provided the opportunity for me to expand my horizions in the field of Freelance Animation.
