BACK 2 BUSINESS training & coaching:
Back 2 Business is primarily intended for women who want to take or retake their personal and/or professional life in hand and develop it in full accordance with their values, while optimising their resources.
We also advise companies open for innovative solutions to improve the transition of their staff returning from maternity/parental leave.
Our speciality includes coaching and training for women within their company when returning to work after a break (maternity or parental leave, illness...) The objectives are multiple: improve self-confidence, motivation, efficiency, time managment...
Interested in knowing more? Join the group Active Mothers Back 2 Business or contact me via my Linkedin mailbox !
