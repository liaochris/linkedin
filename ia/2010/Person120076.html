Kush Technologies LLC’s Summary

Our focus is finding the best quality people, faster and at lower cost. We will partner with you to perform the sourcing that you do not have the time for.

Our efforts are concentrated on candidate identification, full behavioral/labor historical screening and profiling. As the economy continues to improve, organizations are looking for alternative ways to ramp-up talent, while managing recruitment costs and maintaining flexibility. Leveraging years of workforce expertise, Kush Technologies LLC offers scalable, measurable recruitment solutions that generate a competitive advantage for our customers.

Kush Technologies LLC’s Specialties:

* Reduction in overall recruitment costs
* Improved quality of service delivery
* Standardization of internal and external best practices
* Improved compliance with diversity goals
* Enhanced reporting for better decision making
* Retention of strategic role of HR
* Ability to accelerate organizational change
* Improved stakeholder satisfaction and productivity, including senior management, Human Resources function, hiring managers, and applicants
