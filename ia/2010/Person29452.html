Britt Michaelian, MA’s Summary

Britt Michaelian, M.A. is a passionate, serial entrepreneur dedicated to helping parents keep their families happy and healthy as they pursue their own personal ambitions. With a Masters in Marriage and Family Therapy and Art Therapy and as the Founder of Responsible Family, Britt Michaelian is well positioned to lead families into a new way of living in abundance. Responsible Family is a San Francisco based business that educates and empowers families about child safety and family balance through an acclaimed blog, an award winning video, a bestselling book and live events.

In early 2009, Britt joined forces with Wealthy Girl Summit creator Alicia Dunams to form See You On The Call, a teleseminar listing service that helps entrepreneurs to promote their businesses. In addition, Britt Michaelian added a new product category to the Responsible Family brand called Work Smart Mompreneurs, an innovative mindset and productivity coaching program. Designed to help guide aspiring and established mom entrepreneurs to juggle a thriving business while being healthy roles model for their children, the Work Smart Mompreneurs program includes The Millionaire Mompreneur Start Up Manual, The WSM Morning Mindset Meditation and the WSM Productivity Mastermind Group.

In the coming year, Britt Michaelian is looking to expand the media presence of Work Smart Mompreneurs through national television and radio appearances and through internet video channels. Britt is currently seeking television and spokesperson opportunities to leverage her experience both as a mom and an entrepreneur as she continues to help responsible mom entrepreneurs to raise healthy, happy families.
