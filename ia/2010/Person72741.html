Garo Hovnanian’s Summary

Having experienced both sides of the new home sales process, as a salesperson and now a marketing specialist, my goal is to enhance and engineer the sales process in a way that provides a more enjoyable experience for the buyer and leads to more sales for the company.

Using an analytical, emotional and psychographical approach, my team and I aim to understand what the prospect is thinking, what brings them to our sales office, and how to help them decide to purchase my product versus a competitor's. Understanding "why" our prospects do what they do provides the underlying blueprint for how to reach them more effectively and completely.
