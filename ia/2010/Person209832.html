Uriel Alvarado’s Summary

I work with multidisciplinary teams of experts in the communications and creative arenas. We work across time zones and continents within the Latin region (Mainly Spain, Portugal, Argentina, Colombia, USA and Mexico) constantly seeking to offer value added, profitable and scalable win-win-win solutions for Saxo Bank’s clients and partners.

I overview the framework and strategy of these innovation work teams with the aim of allowing creativity to happen and disseminate knowledge from within Saxo Bank to our clients in the most effective way. Our final goal is to offer the best trading experience while following value co-creation strategies.

Following our brand strategy, we combine IT development with, PR, SEM, SMM, traditional, online advertising and all the other means of integrated communications to reach our target groups and offer relevant and differentiated online trading experiences within the fast evolving Online Financial Industry (we offer financial products such as Forex, CFDs, Options, Futures, ETFs, etc).

Saxo Bank’s Latin Region Marketing department was awarded this year for having developed one of the top 3 best business innovations of the year in the first Linkedin - Cisco European Business Awards 2010, for our key project “Sala de Inversion” www.saladeinversion.com www.saladeinversion.es .
