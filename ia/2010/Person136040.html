MARY DUFFY’s Education

  • State University of New York Empire State College

    M.L.A , Aging Baby Boomer Women , 2006 — 2010

    This self-designed program encompasses Modern Feminism and Ageism, Advertising and its seeming allergy to the 50+ market which controls 70% of US Money, 50+ Consumerism Trends, the Popular History of the Boomers, 1945-Present, Creative Aging, Loss and Grief, The Psychology of Happiness and a Thesis , which is a book on looking and feeling good for Boomer age women.
