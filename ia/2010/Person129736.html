Michael Addyman’s Summary

I am a detail oriented problem solver with a spirit of service to customers and clients. I have 11 years experience in desktop publishing at VillageProfile.com, Inc. in Elgin, IL and, prior to that, 27 years in community access and small format video production in Chicago, suburban Northwest Indiana, suburban Dayton, Ohio and Dubuque, Iowa.
I want to produce high quality, accurate content in an organization that values easy cooperative teamwork and outstanding customer service. I have always taken pride in my work ethic and quality of output, and will always strive to continue that tradition.

Michael Addyman’s Specialties:

I have written training manuals for Public Access and digital prepress procedures. I have managed departments from 3 to 20 employees. I streamlined and organized publishing procedures at Village Profile. I have designed & operated several cable TV facilities. I have managed video production departments at three cable systems Public Access facilities. I was instrumental in the specification and design of Master Control and the tape library database at Chicago Access Corporation.
