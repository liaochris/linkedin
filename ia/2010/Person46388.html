Dana McManus’s Summary

I am an Internet Marketing and Social Media Trainer and Consultant, Personal/Life Coach and own an Online Business Management company specializing in Internet Marketing and Social Media Marketing. I offer implementation and management of online profiles, wordpress blogs, and website development.

Having studied under some of the biggest names in the Internet industry, I specialize in helping entrepreneurs integrate effective social media, social networking and Internet marketing techniques and strategies into their sales process in order to increase sales.

Having been a licensed therapist and life coach for many years, my personal development areas of specialty are Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) with an emphasis in its clinical and personal empowerment applications.

I keenly recognize that, in addition to great business techniques and strategies, it also takes a positive mind set and specific affective skills to excel in business.

As a Personal/Life Coach, I help entrepreneurs to remove their road blocks and discover their hidden personal power to create lasting and positive change within themselves for peak performance.
