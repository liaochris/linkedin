Barry Wallace’s Summary

I bring brands and the brand experiences to life. Beginning with name development, brand personality, creative development, brand experience and brand evolution I bring a unique perspective to each brand I work with. From initial logo design to environmental design and graphics to web design to electronic and new media design, I believe creating a memorable and lasting brand happens by design at every customer touch point.
My goal is to bring my 20 years of experience and my passion for branding to firms and major corporations that desire to create a lasting and memorable brand experience for their clients and customers.
