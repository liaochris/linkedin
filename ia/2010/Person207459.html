Traci Deveau’s Summary

I have over fifteen years of experience in the sales, marketing, recruitment, benefits administration, and employee relations fields. I'm passionate about results and utilize a customer centric sales approach. I'm extremely goal-oriented, known for my high standards/integrity and completely at ease presenting to the C-suite. My track record is one of accomplishments and sustained success.

Prior to my tenure in the recruitment solutions and technology industry, I was a PeopleSoft HRIS consultant interfacing with major corporations to integrate the software’s Benefits Administration and Payroll modules. This was a natural outflow of my previous role as Director of Human Resources for Fairfield Mortgage Corporation.

My vast background in the Human Resources field includes hands-on experience developing benchmark recruiting strategies, implementing all benefits programs, and administering/maintaining compliance with Federal COBRA, HIPAA, ADA, and EEO regulations. My experience encompasses all aspects of employee relations, payroll administration and high-level company communications. I have a practical understanding of the inner workings of the HR function, the ability to identify workforce issues and develop strategic positive time-proven solutions to address them.


I'm a graduate of Salem State College.
