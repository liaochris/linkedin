Stephen Arnold’s Summary

I have a successful history in the audio sector, involved in sound design, music production, label admin, publishing, marketing, media relations, PR & promotion, and currently work in the games industry as Business Development for e4e Interactive (formerly Absolute Quality).

This position involves working as part of a global team, networking with game developers and publishers worldwide, providing them with detailed information on our services and arranging contracts & partnerships. I also source clients in other multimedia sectors that require software development infrastructure assistance.

I have been an avid gamer since I was a kid, and it's a great privilege to be in a position to work with the companies that have been such a big part of my life growing up.

- - -

Under the pseudonyms 'Espion' & 'Error Response', I have music published by www.2pointzero.tv, released 1 of 3 albums & over 20 ep's on various record labels from around the world. In recent years I have played live, DJ'd and appeared on radio stations & music magazines internationally, and worked tirelessly to progress into an artist known beyond the electronic music underground. http://www.myspace.com/espion / http://www.myspace.com/errorresponse

My goal is to work in business development, marketing & PR, or development in the games industry, most likely creating original music and sound for games, films and animations, while continuing to release music worldwide.

  • Composer

    Espion

    (Music industry)

    January 1994 — Present (16 years 3 months)

    Stephen Arnold began producing electronic music in 1994. In 1997 he moved to Glasgow to study sound, audio engineering & music technology, and began to network with influencial producers & promoters from the industrial city. This fuelled the fire to produce darker, more experimental IDM. After several years as a promoter from 2001 and resident at various club nights, Espion made his first mark on the underground scene in 2005, with sell-out releases on Transparent Sounds' label Orson Records, followed by remixes on two of the best selling records on Billy Nasty's Electrix Records; Transparent Sounds' 'Cider Club ep' & 'Spot the Difference'.

    Since then, his diversity in his music has seen him progress into a well established artist, releasing with pioneering electronic artists from around the world, with frequent plays on Dave Clarke's White Noise radio show.

    Espion has brought his dark and meticulous sound to venues all over the UK, Prague, Berlin, Udine (Italy) and Shenzhen (China), while also doing sound recording, sound design, post production & music for short film.
