Cory Foy’s Summary

Being agile is more than just checking boxes on some spreadsheet. It's about team dynamics, organizational change, technical excellence and a passion for delivering great software. Whether you are looking for training, consulting or coaching, my company, Cory Foy Consulting and Training, can help!

From large (Microsoft, Wachovia) to small 5-person startups, we've tailored trainings and consulting for organizations of all sizes. So if your organization is looking to reclaim it's passion for delivering the most valuable software you can, and want to do that every week, contact us today.

We offer training and consulting in a variety of areas:

- Scrum and XP
- Kanban
- Test-Driven Development
- .NET consulting
- Ruby and Rails Consulting
- Project Rescues
- Emergency Debugging

Most importantly, we offer a "we're not happy if you aren't" policy to let you cancel at any time during the contract.

We look forward to hearing from you soon!
