Jayson Graves’s Summary

I've created a counseling ministry around the issue of sexual integrity and recovery featuring a radio and speaking component and developing the invention of the teleconference recovery group.

My mission is “to promote a message of grace and healing and construct a platform of leaders and redemptive healers in the U.S. and internationally, launching these redeemers and leaders to the nations proclaiming true sexual purity, freedom from addiction and unwanted attractions, authentic marital intimacy and proactive precepts for men, couples and youth.”

Jayson Graves’s Specialties:

Leading international recovery groups over the telephone, speaking on the subects of sexual addictions recovery and same-gender attractions, counseling, marketing and leadership.
