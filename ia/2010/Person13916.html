Annalisa San Juan’s Summary

Annalisa San Juan was born in SF. As early as her teenage years, she recognized her passion for the arts and devoted herself to a career as a recording artist and lyricist. Finding her true intentions manipulated by poor representation, she chose to develop herself professionally in the field of Sales and Marketing. She found success in the corporate environment working for companies like State Farm Insurance and Careerbuilder.com.

Despite having realized a talent for business, an appreciation for the arts still resonated at her core. Having found an opportunity to apply her professional aptitude with her love of the entertainment industry, She then moved to Las Vegas in 2003 to pursue a career in Publicity.

This role in Public Relations positioned her as a critical figure for the planning and production of high profile events in the industries of music, film, and art in NYC, Miami, Las Vegas, L.A., and in Cannes, France.

A career in publicity immersed Miss San Juan in these industries and integrated her into their associated networks. This, and her love of independent film, lead to the appointment of Miss San Juan as the VP of Sales and Marketing for ITN Distribution, Inc. in 2005. In addition, she has been asked to speak at over one hundred seminars regarding various aspects of the music and film industries, sitting on panels with the A&R representatives of Sony, Atlantic, and Capitol Records. Most recently, The Hollywood Reporter hosted an event at the Cannes Film Festival, having Miss San Juan speak about digital film distribution.

Miss San Juan, having come from a complex childhood and learning life’s hardest lessons as an adult, wanted to channel the skills, talents, and passion that were all key to her success and direct them toward a good cause. She began to formulate a plan for an organization that could use art to help women and children in need.

In April of 2009, she moved back to her hometown of SF and formed The SJ Arts Group, Inc.
