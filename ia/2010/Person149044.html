Brendan Smith brendan@motiveinteractive.com’s Summary

Internet advertising visionary and entrepreneur. Since 1996 I have been researching and exploring all aspects of Interactive Advertising. I cut my teeth in the Double Op-in email marketing industry as both a leading list owner and senior business development manager at Netcreations. I attribute much of my success to the strong regards to privacy and accountability that was the core of that business. As the CEO of Motive Interactive I call on my experience as a top tier website publisher, advertiser, and agency senior manager to represent our business with a keen eye from all sides. *Expert knowledge in Performance Advertising, Affiliate Marketing, and CPA offers.

  • Founder/President

    Tech 22 Inc

    (Online Media industry)

    April 1996 — May 1999 (3 years 2 months)

Core focuses: Email Marketing, Viral Marketing, List Management, Consumer Surveys, IT training, Travel, Sweepstakes, and Real Estate. These web properties generated a user membership of over 2 million users. We, owned, and maintained 100% of its software and data base solutions. These software and development attributes were purchased by 3rd parties and used in developing of 3rd party websites in the Travel, and Survey Industries.
