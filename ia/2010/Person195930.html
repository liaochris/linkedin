Steve Koch’s Summary

I have extensive and varied experience in many aspects of advertising, from working at small and medium sized firms to one of the world’s largest advertising agencies. My clients have ranged from Fortune 500 movie studios to public transportation projects, from historic sports teams to some of the infamous dot-coms.

During my career, I have worked in Los Angeles, Seattle and Phoenix in account management for agencies such as DDB Worldwide and WONGDOODY on many clients, including Cold Stone Creamery, Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, Wells Fargo, MGM, Clif Bar, Luna Bar and the Los Angeles Dodgers. Most recently, I was Director of Client Services at Santy Integrated in Scottsdale.

Steve Koch’s Specialties:

All aspects of advertising (print, TV, radio, outdoor, media planning & buying.) Deep understanding of digital marketing strategies and tactics (Paid search, social media, mobile, online display), Public Relations (Media relations, event marketing, influencer campaigns, media training, speakers bureau, blogs.)
