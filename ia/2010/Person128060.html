Lou Mongello’s Summary

Nationally recognized expert and historian about the Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando, Florida. A fan of the Walt Disney World Resort since it first opened in 1971, he’s since transformed himself into one of the world’s leading experts on WDW history and lore.

Featured speaker at numerous events in the U.S., including fan gatherings and corporate events with 550+ attendees.

For more than 30 years, Mongello has shared his love of the park with thousands through storytelling, trivia books, radio broadcasts and his website (www.WDWRadio.com), boasting nearly 30,000 registered subscribers, and is one of the top three active communities of Disney fans anywhere in the world.

Host of the weekly WDW Radio Show Disney Podcast (wdwradio.com), awarded Best Travel Podcast 2006, 2007, 2008, and 2009

Author of the Walt Disney World Trivia Books (Volumes I and II), the definitive resource for Walt Disney World trivia, history, secrets and fun facts.

Host, narrator and producer of the Audio Guide to Walt Disney World - a virtual audio tour of WDW

Founder of the Dream Team Project - fundraising project devoted to raising money to help send seriously ill children to Walt Disney World. Annual events and other activities have raised more than $90,000.00.

Co-Editor and Publisher of Celebrations Magazine (www.CelebrationsPress.com), a print publication for Disney fans. Readers can discover the magic of the Disney theme parks & resorts, relive the classic films, and celebrate the secrets, stories and history of the most magical place on Earth.

Lou Mongello is a member of the national, New Jersey and World Chapters of the NFFC, the national Disneyana fan club, and a graduate of the College of Disney Knowledge.

Attorney licensed in the State of New Jersey, having practiced there since 1994

Former IT Director for multi-modality medial imaging facilities and owner of IT consulting firm
