Douglas Lagame’s Summary

A highly-motivated, creative field technician and specialist with strong initiative and leadership, possessing 9+ years of industry experience, backed by an Associate degree in Electronic technologist as well as many other technical, finacial and management classes offered internally at Schlumberger.
As a Schlumberger field specialist and electronic technician, received extensive international land and ultra-deepwater offshore operational experience, as it applied to well testing characterization, evaluation, production services as well as conventional and unconventional completions, such as: Multilaterals technology.
