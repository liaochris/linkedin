    (Public Company; PUB; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 2007 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Elena Robinson – Vice President, Director of Production
    Elena is responsible for the process by which Pixel’s core capabilities are realized and work gets created, executed and deployed. She plays a key role in Pixel’s operations, leading the development and management of all of Pixel’s digital resources, projects and people. She oversees the production processes on a day-to-day basis, advising producers on project resourcing and planning from concept through to delivery. She ensures that all projects are on time and within budget. Working with, digital strategy teams, production, creative & developers, her role is pivotal to the execution of all our clients’ projects, and any subsequent updates/additions/stages to the projects. Her teams are charged with delving into the hyper expanding, ever changing world of interactive experiences seeking out both innovative applications of the technology and the appropriate digital touch points for each client.

    She is responsible for the development and management of all Digital resources. This includes the growth of Pixel’s digital resources meeting demand, the management of outsourcing, securing of a range of reliable and cost effective external supply sources in various locations and time zones and the development of Pixel’s own digital resources.

    Elena has worked across a multitude of platforms — including television advertising, radio, event marketing and interactive — for clients such as McDonald’s, Coca Cola, ESPN, Allstate, Walgreens, Verizon, Nintendo, Keebler, Bayer, Lego, Proctor & Gamble and Gatorade, to name just a few.

    Hailing from Portland, Maine, Elena graduated from Northwestern University with a degree in Performance Studies. Her passions include her family, science fiction and yoga.
