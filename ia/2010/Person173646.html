Rita Lim-Wilby’s Summary

Rita has developed new projects in small molecule therapeutics for thrombosis, cancer, malaria, HIV, and HCV. Having worked as a laboratory manager, bioinformatician and computational chemist, project leader, and principal investigator, she has the skills to effectively communicate in all media and formats with pharmaceutical and biotechnology research scientists. She holds 13 patents, including one for bocepravir, which received in 2006 the Thomas Alva Edison Award from the Research Council of New Jersey.

Adding to more than 15 years in life science research, Rita has found the most satisfaction working on business aspects of science.

business plans, strategic marketing plans, branding wheels, writing case studies, and sales cheatsheets, running user meetings, focus groups, webinars, and tradeshows.
