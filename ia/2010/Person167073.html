Richard Eng’s Summary

Highly successful real estate executive with experience in asset management, acquisitions, dispositions and development. Closed over $1.3 billion in transaction in both market rate and affordable housing in brokerage and syndications. Served as a project engineer and construction manager to over $450 million in construction projects which include institutional and public works rehabilitation and renovations to new construction of subways and light rail transit.
