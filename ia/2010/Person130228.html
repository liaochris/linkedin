maite COUREL’s Experience

  • Post-doctoral Fellow

    UCSD

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    September 2005 — Present (4 years 10 months)

    Since September 2005, I am working in the Chromaffin Cell and Hypertension research lab, located in the SSPPS building on the UCSD campus. We are studying the molecular mechanisms of regulated secretion, using the rat sympathoadrenal cell line PC12 as a model for neurosecretion. We focus our attention on the formation of dense-core secretory granules, and the role of granins proteins in that process.

    Peer-reviewed publications:
