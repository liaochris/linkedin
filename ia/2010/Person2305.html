Adam Filipowski’s Summary

Adam Filipowski is a software designer and business development manager. He is the founder of 314 Technologies, software development house (www.314t.com).

Adam and the 314Technologies team help businesses to analyze, scope, develop and maintain bespoke software solutions tailored to their unique business needs by using the latest cutting edge programming languages and tools. They design bespoke, simple and elegant web and mobile apps to help simplify and improve people's lives, whether they be a small start-up business or an industry leader.

Recently 314Technologies have released a FREE unique task management web app - TodoMapper (www.todomapper.com).
