Tony Uphoff’s Summary

Innovative leader with a unique track record of success in building, growing and leading media businesses in highly competitive markets. Executive experience in all aspects of media; digital and online, live events, print, integrated media and data and research. Experienced as well in mergers, acquisitions, transformation/restructuring, public companies, fund-raising and start-ups.

Served in key positions at Ziff-Davis Media during the build out of the company in the late 80's and early 90's. Joined CMP Media as publisher of InformationWeek leading the brand to the # 1 position and into one of the largest brands in B2B media.

Key executive during CMP's dramatic growth in the mid 90's from a $200 Million family company, to a $600 Million, publicly traded, global leader in technology media.

Founding CEO of the internet media company Beliefnet, which was sold to News Corp in 2007.

Leader on 3 of the top 10 B2B brands in history at the peak of their revenues, profits and market share; InformationWeek, The Hollywood Reporter and PC Week.

Named one of the top 100 business media executives by B2B Magazine's "Who's Who in Business Media" 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010. Named a Top Innovator in 2009 by Media Business Magazine.

Guest speaker and panelist on media, technology and entertainment issues at industry and association conferences including; The Folio Media Conferences, The Milken Global Conference, The LA RoadShow, The Association of Media and Entertainment Attorneys, Price Waterhouse Coopers "Outlook Conference", Media Post "Outlook 2008", "Inverge" and The American Business Media Conferences, SISO Conference.

Guest lecturer on "Leading Internet Businesses", "Managing High Growth Environments", "Technology and Financing in Film" and "Digital Transformation" at Columbia, Emory, NYU and USC's Annenberg Business Schools.

Currently CEO of UBM TechWeb. UBM TechWeb is a division of UBM.
