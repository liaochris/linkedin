Vivek Varshney’s Summary

Vivek graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2009 with a Masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering. As a graduate student, he was a Research Assistant in the Machine Vision Lab, working on the Precision Personnel Locator and the Mantenna Project with Professor Cyganski. As an undergraduate at WPI, he was actively involved with the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers student branch, including being President his senior year. In addition, he was a senior tutor for various courses in the ECE department.
