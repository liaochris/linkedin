Scott Hajer’s Summary

I am passionate about recruiting - helping people find new, satisfying positions and establishing a good fit between the company and the individual. Recruiting is not about 'slamming' a person with the right skills into a position, or convincing a candidate to take a job. Career growth and retention are key elements on both sides of the equation. Truth breeds trust.

I've built a college recruiting program from the ground up and I've also hired entry- to director-level professionals in a variety of roles (technical, accounting, administrative, marketing, human resources, sales).

Scott Hajer’s Specialties:

Full life cycle recruiting, sourcing, negotiating, strategy and tactics, establishing and refining processes and procedures, process mapping, mentoring, relationship building.
