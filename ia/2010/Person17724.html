Ashley Seddon’s Summary

Digital Media recruitment specialist, Director at Digital Personnel, the UK's Digital Media Talent Provider.

Digital Personnel has pioneered an approach to recruitment that fully utilises the power of Social Media. By creating and engaging with captive audiences online we are able to communicate new opportunities directly to the desired talent. We extend the reach of our clients to target candidates with the most specialist of skillsets - identifying true pioneers within the digital space. This expertise and efficiency enables our clients the peace of mind to leave us with the recruitment, while they get on with creating and implementing fantastic online campaigns.

I am the owner of the Digital Personnel Group on LinkedIn, one of the largest and active Digital Marketing groups on the LinkedIn platform, please feel free to join if you are a digital professional.
