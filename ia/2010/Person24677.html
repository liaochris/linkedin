Blaine Riley’s Summary

My goal is to help micro and small cap companies with early and later stage financing (debt and equity) by connecting them to the network of several hundred accredited investors, institutions, funds, underwriters, and lenders.

20+ years of experience in the US Capital Markets, including developing strategic trading methods on trading options for Leveraged Buyouts.

Capital advisory services to a large number of diverse micro and small cap companies. Extensive experience with healthcare and biotechnology companies, including product analysis, FDA filings and peer benchmarking.

Frequent guest-speaker at various investment banking and industry events, such as the Television & Radio Society event in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Blaine Riley’s Specialties:

I specialize in microcap and small cap stocks, strategic business development, mergers and acquisitions, licensing, including international licensing, partnering, capital raises, and investor relations.
