Joe Traverso’s Summary

Joe has over 15 years of experience in the field of information technology, and is currently the Systems Administrator for the Town of Pepperell in Massachusetts. Though only being in his current position for less than 6 months, Joe has established security for the town's network infrastructure, implemented a UTM system, and rolled out open source site-to-site VPN.

As the IT Manager for the Boston office of CurtCo Robb Media he was responsible for supporting and maintaining the office's infrastructure, servers and client computers for both office-based and remote employees.

Before moving to CurtCo, he was the Network Manager at Hitchiner Manufacturing Co., Inc., where he managed both the U.S. and Mexico locations' servers and network infrastructures. During his tenure he introduced VMware virtualization, installed a Compellent storage area network (SAN), and upgraded the network from a Windows NT domain to a Windows 2003 Active Directory structure.

In his previous position as Systems Administrator at Edgil Associates, Inc. Software and Consulting he mastered his first network operating system -- IBM's OS/2. He went on to upgrade the network from OS/2 to Windows NT 4 and migrate the messaging system from Lotus cc:Mail to Lotus Domino/Notes while supporting both the office employees and their customers.

Joe received a B.A. in English/Professional Writing from Fitchburg State College and originally intended on making his livelihood by writing. However, his hobby and interest in computers turned into his career as he continued his technical education in subjects ranging from Microsoft, Apple, UNIX, and Linux operating systems to virtualization, ethical hacking, and security.

Joe is currently pursuing an M.S. in Information Assurance from Capitol College in Laurel, Maryland. After receiving his MSIA, he ultimately intends on getting his certification as a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and working as a security consultant.
