Joshua Hebert’s Summary

Joshua Hebert, Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Joshua Hebert is Founder & CEO of Magellan Jets. Prior to founding Magellan Jets Joshua worked as a Wall Street executive at the prestigious firms of Shearson Lehman, Sands Brothers and Paine Webber where he solidified his knowledge and expertise in financial relations.

In 1997, Joshua returned to Boston and worked at The Boston Business Journal, a division of American City Business Journals, Inc., a Conde Nast company, as a Sales Manager. In this role, he increased sales by an 800% margin and earned the company its highest sales award for the next three consecutive years.

In 2000, Joshua helped build a new private jet company CharterAuction, as Director of Sales & Marketing. he led the aircraft charter sales team to award-winning sales growth, in 2001 the company grew 600% under his direction.

In 2002, Joshua was promoted to Managing Partner and Executive Vice President, where he managed all of CharterAuction’s Sales, Concierge, Aircraft Membership and Fulfillment Departments.

Joshua also oversaw all aspects of the Private Jet Membership Program and on-demand charter sales for the company. He arranged an exclusive marketing deal with the New England Patriots.

He has served on several Board of Directors including, Jets.com, The Heath Evans Foundation and Iowngreen.com. He is Chairman of the Board of Directors for Magellan Jets.
