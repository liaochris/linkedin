Emma Pearce’s Summary

Pearce Marketing enables you to add cost-effective marketing expertise to your team at the time you need it and get results. The most popular services to date include writing a marketing plan and being an outsourced marketing manager.

Pearce Marketing has a range of services that can be tailored to your business objectives and circumstances. Our services work well for large and small businesses and for both one-off and ongoing projects.

Our services include:
- Marketing Audit
- Marketing Strategy
- Researching New Markets
- Marketing Action Plan
- Outsourced Marketing Manager
- Marketing Mentoring and Training

The purpose of marketing is to grow your business profitably.

Pearce Marketing Consultants can help you to meet your business development objectives. For example, do you want to increase sales, develop a marketing plan, use online and email marketing campaigns, launch a new product, enter a new market or change your perception in the market?

A flexible outsourced marketing solution sounds like a good idea doesn't it? You can benefit from the skills of a friendly, experienced marketing professional to build your business, but without the commitment of a full time senior level employee. We have many years of commercial and practical experience of launching, managing and delivering successful business and marketing projects of all sizes. These range from developing a marketing strategy and a marketing plan to managing an email campaign.

Our marketing services are tailored to your business, so they work equally well for small and large businesses on short or long term projects. Not only are our services affordable, but your marketing consultant will always recommend the most cost-effective mix of activities and marketing campaigns for your business. We can help you target your budget effectively to maximise sales potential and keep costs to a realistic level. This will ensure you have the best return on investment.

    Emma Pearce set up Pearce Marketing Consultants in 2008 to offer businesses an affordable and flexible outsourced marketing solution. Emma is an experienced strategic and tactical marketer who has been working in commercial marketing positions since 1994. Her career has included working for small to medium sized companies and large corporates (e.g. IBM).

    She is currently working with companies ranging from manufacturers to architects and farm attractions. She has previously worked in the IT, pharmaceuticals, professional services and builders merchant sectors.

    Emma Pearce is on the Database of Preferred Suppliers of Consulting Services for MAS (Manufacturing Advisory Service) in the South East. (There is often funding for manufacturing companies for marketing services via MAS.)

    Emma spent 4 years working in Hastings, East Sussex, on key strategic business projects and launching and managing the marketing function for Stamco Timber (wholesale), Stamco Building Supplies and Karelia Wood Flooring (national wood flooring business selling to trade and retail). For 2 years prior to this Emma was the Business Broker at Ten Sixty Six Enterprise supporting local businesses on various projects.

    Emma was a Senior Management Consultant for Chorleywood Consulting for 4 years. Although she was based in London, she worked with clients in Europe and Thailand on business and marketing project in the telecoms sector (particularly in CRM and Billing). Emma has also been a marketing consultant to an IT management consultancy and a telecoms software supplier in the UK. In 1997, Emma joined IBM New Zealand as part of a global marketing team for a telecoms software product. She lived and worked in New Zealand for 18 months.

    Prior to this Emma was on the marketing team at Linklaters and Paines, a global law firm based in London, worked for an IT firm in Sydney, Australia and spent a year with Wellcome pharmaceuticals in London.
