Bennett B. (Ben) Grimmett’s Summary

Project Leadership: Develop project scopes and timelines; allocate resources; supervise budgets; coordinate scheduling and timelines; prepare financial analysis and marketing reports; build, coordinate and lead teams; select, audit and supervise vendors and subcontractors; develop, review and approve vendor and customer Requests for Proposals; manage customer relations
