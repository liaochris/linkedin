Mary Lascelles’s Summary

“Moving Links 4 You” offers ease in relocation for those who have no corporate relocation help.

Moving Links 4 You geographical focus: United States and Canada.*
*For points beyond, please contact us for direction as we have global connections.

Most people lead busy lives already without adding the demands of what a move (relocation) takes. At no added cost to you, Moving Links 4 You will pre-screen the professionals and services you will need to make the transition straightforward. Our fee is paid by those we refer to without adding to your bottom line.

What can we do for you? A Realtor to list and sell your home? Appraiser? Home stager? Household mover? Car transporter? Temporary housing? Area tour in the new town where you are moving? Temporary storage? Realtor to purchase your new home? Mortgage lender? School information? Neighborhood information? Medical community information? Utility transfers? Pet transportation? The list goes on.

We have networks and resources in place to typically have you connected within 24 hours. Why start from scratch when we are at the ready to help you?

We do the pre-screening for you to make sure the professionals we refer you to are fulltime in the business and are focused on delivering you red carpet service. For us to refer to them, they must be equipped with the tools, resources and willingness to go to bat for you if there are any issues along the way. Sometimes there are – that’s called life.

Again, this will cost you no more than if you did everything on your own. We remain a phone call or email away for any questions or concerns.

If you or someone you know would like to learn more, contact me at 530.515.6299 or email relomary@gmail.com with questions.
