Jeff Jimerson’s Summary

Information architecture: I develop user flow diagrams and architecture outlines for websites large and small, from static brochure sites to complex ecommerce and 2.0 communities.

Wireframing: I create detailed drawings of key front- and back-end pages for website applications, intranets and content management systems.

Web interface design: I design Photoshop mockups for key pages, understanding how design decisions will translate into HTML/CSS; I deliver clean, layered files with guidelines to developers; I’m obsessed with pixel-perfect detail.

Print design: I design marketing and sales collateral, outdoor advertising and direct mail campaigns; I design brochures, retail packaging, posters and billboards.

Identity design: I develop logos and wordmarks, brand guidelines, color palettes, type styles and grids; I demonstrate how the brand extends to stationery, signage and the web.

Jeff Jimerson’s Specialties:

Information architecture, wireframing, web interface design, print design, identity design, art direction.

    After 8+ years as Designer/Art Director at Edge Design, I left with a real wealth of understanding and knowledge for how to create an outstanding experience for my clients today.
