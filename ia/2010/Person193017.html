Stacey Feldman’s Summary

I am a Special Event Perfectionist. Since 1998 I have been instrumental in the planning and implementation of over 500 functions and events. My most recent success being the nationally televised Pillsbury Million Dollar Bake-Off, for which I conceived and specially designed a floral and custom creation for the 20’ fabric covered finalist’s table.

My love of the industry began as a young child, as her mother always included me in a variety of fundraisers, school functions and acting classes. Anything to do with stage, lighting and entertainment became natural to me. Life is just one big party!

Born with ‘itchy’ feet, I enjoy exploring what else the universe has to offer. A world traveler, I have visited over 14 countries, and brought back a unique cultural and visionary experience that is applied to all my events today.
Having lived and worked all over the United States and Canada, I has proudly created and produced events from as basic as a high school function to the elaborate, never-before-envisioned, eye-poppers of a wedding to the gala stylistics of a Fortune 500 company.

Today, I am known for my passion and spirit, which shows in every event. With my positive energy, talent and keen eye for details, I am able to work with my staff through every aspect of the event to produce the ‘wow factor’ experience for all my clients.
