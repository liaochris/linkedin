Steven Marder’s Summary

Dynamic leader, serial entrepreneur, executive and advisor with diverse and proven track record in digital media, social media and traditional media/entertainment. Almost 20 years executive and advisory experience in Media with major media conglomerates such as EMI Music and Tribune Company to various start-ups. Proven ability to secure financing (seed/ venture capital/debt), launch and evangelize companies and products through top analysts, journalists and blogosphere, recruit and retain top tier management teams and corporate directors, source and negotiate complex strategic partnership deals and attract and secure merger/ acquisition opportunities. Sophisticated corporate development, business development and corporate communications skills. Extensive experience in cross border management and transactions between US market, Asia Pacific and EU territories. Global personal network of top tier technology analysts, journalists and bloggers, investment bankers, media corporate development executives and institutional investors.
