Voyages Designers Consulting’s Summary

Voyages Designers Consulting Pvt. Ltd. is a Worldwide Travel Management Company based in New Delhi, INDIA. We are specialized in Taylor made travel packages, all inclusive and Luxury vacations, Transportation, Hotels and Apartments services reservations.

As a Travel Consulting Company our expertise lies in handling all travel arrangements for:
 Leisure travelers,
 Economical or Corporate tours
 Educational Travels
 Medical Tourism
 Meetings, Incentives, Conferences and Events (MICE).
In this capacity we have created a range of partners across the globe which helps us to provide to our customers the Best Product, at the Best Time and at the Best Price.

Our mission is to offer to travelers’ quality service, value, and the best collection of resorts and vacation packages available in the market.

Walt Disney used to say:” Do what you do so well that they will want to see it again and bring their friends..."
To make it happens, we take great pride in delivering the highest quality service to the Guests and assure them that the utmost care and attention will be given because great travel experiences often have a life time effect on you and your life.

Our approach:

 Long Term Partnership with our customers
 Flexibility in our Design Packages
 Clarity in reporting
 Work exactly in accordance with the agreed budget
 Attention to details
 Strategic and tactical
 Passionate about your well-being

By giving an opportunity to experts like us, you can be sure that the services we will provide will match your wishes and ensure the great holidays you have always dreamt about.
