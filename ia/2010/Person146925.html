Cristina Ackas’s Summary

I have been a part of Moxie’s Research & Participant Insights team since 2006 and currently serve as a Senior Analyst, providing perspective on participant behaviors and trends in the digital space along with developing strategy recommendations based on brand objectives. My experience at Moxie has allowed me to wear many hats including Researcher, Analyst and Strategist.

Throughout my career I have had the opportunity to work on Fortune 500 companies including CPG, Entertainment and Luxury brands. These various brands have provided me the opportunity to gather and report data in different and innovative ways.

Finding actionable and strategic insights out of raw data is a mix between art and science, and it’s what I love to do. From turning in-depth consumer segmentation reports to simple 1 page personas, I have learned that the most important aspect of my job is making sure data is presented in a manner that the audience can understand and react to, whether it’s internal creative teams or the client.
