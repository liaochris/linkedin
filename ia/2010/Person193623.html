Stefano Bernardi’s Summary

An Italo-French junior venture capitalist, with a passion for internet startups and new business venturing.

I currently blog about the european startup scene at theStartup.eu, a weblog that covers the european startup scene, focused on technology companies, web applications, social media, growth entrepreneurship and venture capital. I am always looking for new connections and new opportunities in the startups world, please do contact me.

I sit on the Board of Directors of Startup Commons, am the Italian Ambassador for Startup School and collaborate with Mind The Bridge foundation.

I also contribute to TechCrunch Europe, covering the italian startup scene.

I recently graduated in Computer and Software Engineering at Roma Tre University with a final note of 101/110, during which I founded Hosteeo LLC, a company providing hosting services and server management, and Roma3.net, the biggest online student community for any italian university.

I speak English, French, Spanish and Italian while I enjoy DJing, photography, politics and loads of sports.
