Kev Roberts’s Summary

An experienced manger and management consultant, specialising in helping business become more streamlined, efficient and profitable.
With a background in IT and business consulting, I’ve worked at senior manager level in a wide range of business across market sectors as diverse as mobile telecommunications, automotive, finance and the performing arts. In every case I’ve taken a lead role in helping that particular business move their operations up to another level of business performance.
Key roles have included household names such as:
Volkswagen Group UK - responsible for all systems, process and technology across the business
Orange - responsible for the development of mobile gaming platform
BMW / Rover - integration of the 2 companies manufacturing and production systems
Recently I’ve taken the experience and best practice from world leading companies and have been applying my skills pragmatically in the SME sector to help companies grow bigger and more profitable.
With my strong systems background and mindset, I approach every business from the process perspective and as a Business Mechanic, I help business owners and managers tune up their current business to cope with increased demand, provide better service and increased profits. A world class business is built on systems, and the people who operate the systems.
As the Business Mechanic partner in Sherpa Consulting my experience and expertise in efficiency and leverage is a powerful complement to Phil Smithers' skills and experience in Sales and Marketing - as Phil helps a organisation attract more business, I help them ramp up the efficiency to profitably service this increased demand.
When I’m not helping people grow their business I’m a keen outdoors enthusiast and can usually be found somewhere steep; walking, canoeing or mountain-biking with varying degrees of success but with a great deal of enjoyment. And like all enthusiasts I also enjoys coaching other people in these activities as well.
