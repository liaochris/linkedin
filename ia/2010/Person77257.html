Grant Cooper’s Summary

Mr. Cooper is a career services professional, resume writer, business plan writer, and Human Resource consultant with clients throughout the U.S. and overseas. Mr. Cooper's personal businesses (Strategic Resumes & Career Pro Resume Service) have helped over 15,000 business professionals, executives, entrepreneurs, and job seekers to attain their goals and dreams through creating powerfully worded, concisely written, well-researched, and attractively formatted resumes, business plans, letters, proposals, presentations, and other materials.

Strategic Resumes® has recently been ranked as the #3 online resume provider in the U.S., and is the only resume writing firm recommended by the award-winning national website, CollegeGrad.com and the book, The College Grad Job Hunter, available in all Barnes & Noble and Borders bookstores. In addition, Strategic Resumes® landed and successfully managed Kinko's Corporation's only national resume writing contract, servicing over 250 Kinko's locations with custom written resumes over a 5-year period.

Headquartered in New Orleans, Louisiana, Strategic Resumes provides a nationwide custom-written resume, career services, job search skills training, and business document writing service, representing clients in virtually all career fields, and has operated continuously since 1994. In addition to resumes, Mr. Cooper designs and writes HR manuals, business plans, proposals, presentations, marketing materials, websites, political speeches, and other communications.

Clients have included the CEO of a $2 billion company, the President of Louisiana’s #1 per capita income Parish, NFL football players, NCAA & NBA coaches, as well as physicians, bankers, police officers, scientists, professors, intelligence agents, politicians, engineers, executives, nurses, attorneys, students, pharmaceutical sales representatives, and accountants. Mr. Cooper performs extensive community service with dozens of non-profit service organizations.
