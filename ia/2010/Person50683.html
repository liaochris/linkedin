    Carbon Consulting, LLC

    (Environmental Services industry)

    July 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Carbon Consulting LLC is developing a business which establishes itself as a leading player in positioning biochar technologies and in associated systems analysis. Biochar is the stable, carbon-rich product produced by thermal decomposition when biomass is heated in an oxygen deprived environment (pyrolysis). Biochar production is a carbon negative process which has the potential to deliver annual gigatons (billions of tons) of carbon dioxide equivalent emissions offsets in the effort to reduce atmospheric concentrations.
