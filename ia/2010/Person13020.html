Angela Loucks’s Summary

I am a University of Kansas doctoral candidate in Audiology with an anticipated graduation date of August 2010. I received my Master’s from KU in 2006 and completed my Clinical Fellowship Year in Southern California. My CFY gave me experiences beyond a standard first year of practice with a case load of twelve patients each day and majority of the population I served did not have English as a first language. I currently own my own business working with people who struggle with Auditory Processing Disorder (APD) and work part time for USD 497.

Specialty interest in Auditory Processing Disorder and tinnitus
