Liquid Siding of America’s Summary

Liquid Siding® has been around for over 30 years and was developed for coating Lighthouses to help resist the rigors of the salt, sun, moisture and corrosion.

Since that time duplications have been attempted by competitors but most competitors are only a derivative of our original formula.

Our Dealer Network extends throughout the entire US and we are always interested in welcoming qualified applicants to become dealers.

It all starts with science ...
The science behind Liquid Siding® is at the forefront of technology. Many factors contribute to Liquid Siding®'s superior performance...

Liquid Siding® employs a super-thin layer of proprietary microspheres that is applied to approximately the thickness of a credit card. These extremely durable, heat-reflective particles are the key to protecting your building envelope from the extremes of temperature for years to come.

Liquid Siding® is able to stretch—up to 645%—and to recover its original shape. Buildings naturally settle and shift over time, resulting in small cracks or splits. Liquid Siding® literally stretches, ensuring such cracks remain hidden from view, and sealed from the elements.

Liquid Siding®'s unique composition resists mold mildew and dirt. All of which ensures that the beautiful finish laid down by our applicators lasts for years and years.

Liquid Siding ® is vapor permeable. Moisture that's normally trapped below the surface is allowed to pass through Liquid Siding® in the form of vapor. So Liquid Siding® limits your exposure to mold and mold spores that gradually destroy the things they grow on.

Liquid Siding® prevents damage to your home or building, saves money, and helps you to avoid potential health problems by eliminating the moisture build-up that causes mold growth.

Liquid Siding of America’s Specialties:

We also manufacture and distribute an industrial cleaner called Spray-N-Shine that our Dealers clean the building envelope with prior to applying the Liquid Siding® system. Spray-N-Shine is also used prior to applying our Weatherseal Concrete, Masonry and Paver Sealer to walks, drives and walls.

Weatherseal is a state-of-the-art-water-repellent sealer specifically designed for concrete, brick and masonry. Weatherseal works better and lasts much longer than conventional sealers
