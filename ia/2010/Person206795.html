Tony Bradshaw’s Summary

Since March 2001 I've been focused on leveraging technology to reach people on the internet and fulfill our company's mission. We focus our time and resources on teaching people how to manage their money...the common sense way. Interestingly enough, if more people listened, the country wouldn't be in the financial crisis it is today.

More recently, we've started expanding our business into business/leadership training with a product called Entreleadership. Entreleadership comes in two flavors. There is a 5 day Ultimate Business Conference and a one day event covering five crucial lessons in leadership/business. Numerous other products exist all of which have some form of web component involved. Approximately 60% of our company's revenue is in some way tied to the web directly or indirectly. 43 web employees or approximately 20% of the workforce.

My responsibilities include leading our technology, marketing and creative teams to accomplish our internet business and branding goals. Our website is visited by 800,000 people per month (www.daveramsey.com)

Read my blog at http://tonybradshaw.wordpress.com
