Gina Persson, Ph.D.’s Summary

Gina Persson, Ph.D. is a social media enthusiast, Assistant Professor of Communication, small business owner and strong believer in the power of understanding and applying theory to help solve practical problems.

She earned a Ph.D. from Rutgers University’s School of Communication & Information in New Brunswick, NJ.

Gina teaches Introduction to Public Relations, PR Writing, Message Design, Principles of Persuasion, Presentation Skills, Organizational Communication, Interpersonal Communication, and Political Campaigning. Her dissertation, "Media Mindfulness: The ability and motivation to process advertisements", emphasized Media Psychology.

Gina Persson, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Is a passionate teacher who loves what she does. Over fifteen years experience with the development, delivery and assessment of communication curriculum related to new media, advertising, civic engagement, interpersonal communication, customer service, public speaking and media literacy.
