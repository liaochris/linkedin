Laura H. Jacobs’s Summary

Laura Jacobs has worked in publications and communications since 1995, and has been with University Relations at the University of Arkansas since 1998. There, she has done a little of everything – graphic designer, manager of marketing and advertising services, development communications, director of the department and, since 2006, managing editor of Arkansas magazine.

She has been active in organizations such as Public Relations Society of America and the Junior League of Northwest Arkansas. Her publications work has garnered several awards.

Laura has a bachelor's degree in English/communication and a master's degree in higher education from the University of Arkansas.

Laura H. Jacobs’s Specialties:

Communications research, planning, execution and evaluation; publications development, writing, production; print and design management; fundraising communications and writing; team building

Membership surveys indicate that the publication is considered one of the premium benefits of membership. Readers surveys show a high satisfaction in the publication.

    The International Association of Business Communicator/Arkansas chapter has honored Arkansas magazine in 2007 and 2008 with awards of excellence. The Public Relations Society of America recognized the magazine with a 2009 Northwest Arkansas Apex award.
