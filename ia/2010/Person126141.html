Lisa Lake’s Summary

Internet Marketing and Interactive Staffing- Washington, DC

Onward Search is focused on one thing, and one thing only. We connect the most talented internet and interactive marketing talent in the country with the nation's best companies.

We know that matching the right people with the right companies can be a pretty magical thing, and that it always produces pretty amazing results. The reality though is that it is not always easy, so that's where we come in.

We have the most experienced and networked group of Internet marketing, interactive, and mobile recruiters in the industry. We attend the trade shows, read the blogs, and most importantly, stay connected on a daily basis with the thought leaders, doers, and linchpins of the internet marketing world.

If you need talent, you can be confident that we can help you find the right people for your team.

Temporary Staffing
Sometimes you need an extra hand (or two!) to push a project forward, and that's where our temporary division comes in. We have an impressive nationwide database of the experts, wizards, and gurus of online and interactive world.

Our talent is experienced across a range of disciplines to include search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC), social media, user experience, web development, interactive, and mobile application development, among others.

Executive Search - Direct / Permanent Placement

In other situations, you may be recruiting for executive or senior leadership for your team. That's the domain of our Executive Search team. They are experts in recruiting for the senior marketing leadership positions in your organization.

We have managed the placement of senior level talent for many of the nation's leading companies. Typical searches include: Chief Marketing officer (CMO), Vice President of E-Commerce, Director of Search marketing, Vice President of Social Media, Vice President of Web Analytics, and more.
