Donna McLeod’s Summary

My background is in apparel design and I have had the unique opportunity to work in various areas within the industry. I began working in a production based environment that has been the foundation of my knowledge and dedication to detail. I know the importance of detail and planning ahead; not just with “plan A” but with “plan B” and possibly “plan C”! Since the market has moved overseas time lines are imperative. I have developed and maintained time and action calendars for two of my clients and brought their lines from being late to on time with time for careful inspection before shipping. I have worked with vendors in Asia, Mexico, Canada and Europe who provided piece goods, findings and the manufacturing of salesman samples and production. I have managed design assistants and merchandising coordinators in previous positions during which time it was my responsibility to teach them proper procedures as well as review their performance. I have an extensive knowledge of Photoshop, Gerber Artworks and Colour Matters Design Software (CM32). I am, of course well versed in Microsoft Office; Word and Excel as well as PowerPoint and Publisher. I believe one of my strongest qualities is flexibility and the ability to get the job done.
