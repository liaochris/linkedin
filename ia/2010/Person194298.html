Stephen Carr’s Summary

Stephen Carr, PCC, CEC integrates strong leadership experience, communication skills, and business background with a natural talent for seeing the best in people. He is an Executive Coach, Facilitator, Workshop Leader and Speaker with over 25 years of experience in coaching, consulting and managing at sales, general and executive management levels.

Stephen works with his clients to help them transform their executive performance and presence, interpersonal relationships and organizational results by helping them to recognize and utilize their natural and unique talents and qualities with confidence.

He has worked with executives, managers and teams in technology and services, software, engineering, publishing, public relations, advertising, higher education, law and banking. His experience, enthusiasm and commitment create a positive, productive environment for learning and change.

Stephen’s leadership experience scales to teams of 1800 employees and $800M in revenue. He leverages his extensive experience as an executive general manager and brings a systems approach to his coaching that supports sustainable change in thinking, behavior and results.

Stephen has been a leader in the Boston coaching community as a board member, speaker and teacher. He is a member of the International Coach Federation (ICF), and is a past President and member of the Board of Directors of the ICF New England Chapter. Stephen is certified through the International Coach Federation, the Coach Training Institute, and the Executive Coaching Institute. He is a certified PaperRoom Coach and Facilitator and is the co-creator and co-leader of the PaperRoom System Licensing Program, an ICF accredited program. He has been a speaker and presenter at ICF New England and other New England Associations. He continues to pursue ongoing education in human and organizational development.

Stephen Carr’s Specialties:

Executive Coaching focused on Leadership and Management Development, Executive Presence and Influence, Interpersonal Communication and Team Engagement, Personal Productivity and Effectiveness, Strategic Thinking and Planning, Career Development and Transition. Skilled in facilitating new thinking and behaviors that lead to new and desired results.
