Résumé de Jennifer Paule, CRTS

A-Mrazek Corporate Transportation & Logistics / Moving and Storage Specialist:

When life moves you, we make it easier. I am Relocation Director (CRTS) with A-Mrazek Moving Systems, an agent for United Van Lines. We help individuals and corporations transition their lives and products across state lines and Internationally.

When you trust your move with A-Mrazek, you can expect that we will create solutions; exceed expectations; demonstrate care and concern for your personal possessions and property; and, advocate safety while traveling on and off our nation's highways. Our reputation depends on it.


Personal note:

I enjoy meeting new people, especially in the moving industry. I started http://www.movingandstorage101.blogspot.com because I passionately want to help others achieve their relocation goals, and I am always open to building quality relationships. It’s a fact, moving is stressful. Often, the first impression a new hire makes of his/her employer directly relates to their move experience. I take joy in the opportunity to help families transition the mosaics of their lives across state lines and in to their new homes. A smile at the end of a move is a priceless reward. If I can help you with your moving and storage needs, please let me know.
