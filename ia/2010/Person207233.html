Tori Harris’s Summary

Tori Harris: RCDD, CCDP, CCNP, MCP - is an independent consultant , owner of Three Green Integration, Inc., and Director of Operations for 3PointData, Inc. Over the past 18 years, Harris has specialized in both the design and operational aspects of of highly available systems, including web based application deployments for companies including Toyota, Microsoft, and General Motors. Recent improvements in service provider offerings have allowed these same complex deployments to transition to cloud based architectures, dramatically improving both scalability and cost effectiveness. Tori has also assisted large commercial and government clients with ITIL based performance improvement and configuration management projects. The list of successful implementations includes such diverse clients as Energizer Battery, Tetra Tech Engineering, BellSouth, the Department of Energy, and the Department of Defense. Harris was the lead consultant for the U.S. Air Force effort to establish operating system change management for its 400,000 active computer systems (recently covered in Government Computer News). Prior to his career in consulting, Harris served as a manager, director, and program manager directly responsible for IT operations / support. Harris earned his B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Missouri – Rolla.
