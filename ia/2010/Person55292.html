Desiree Jellerette’s Summary

Co-Owner and Co-Founder of Select Film Fund - raising investor capital, creating media products and servicing the Film and Television Industry. Over 15 yrs experience as Producer/Line Producer/Production Manager/Post Production Supervisor, specializing in Independent Film and Television, some titles include Julian Schnabel's "Basquiat", Night Shaymalan's "Wide Awake", Jason Alexander's "Cherry Pink", BET's "ComicView". Admin Director for Spike Lee's 40 Acres of A Mule Filmworks ("Malcolm X", "Mo' Better Blues", "Jungle Fever", "Crooklyn"). Worked on over 25 Music Videos, talent included Ice Cube, Master P, Shaq, and E40. Have a wealth of experience in preparing Film Industry Documentation - Film/TV Budgets, Cost Reports, Shooting Schedules - expert in Managing People and Projects.
