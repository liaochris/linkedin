Dr Charles Parker’s Specialties:

New brain and body functional science provides new evidence, - and that new evidence can significantly, dramatically, change people's lives. Superficially based interventions often work for the uncomplicated, simple presentations, but more often do not work for the far more common complex presentations with mind and associated body dysregulations.

My mission: to teach the public and professionals how to avoid these pitfalls in the first place.
