Tom Howard’s Summary

I've been involved in various aspects of interactive marketing for more than 14 years now. I enjoy all aspects of marketing, but interactive marketing is where my strategic skills are most honed. My development background enables me to see opportunities and possibilities more so than most marketers. I have intrinsic knowledge that is impossible to gain without hands on development experience. I use this experience to help my clients understand how to turn their business/marketing goals and objectives into interactive strategies and solutions that deliver results.

If you are looking for new, out of the box strategies, then you definitely need to contact me. I constantly look for creative strategies that may be of use to my clients. The important thing is to understand the goal of my clients and to help them utilize interactive marketing strategies to maximize their budget and support their customers. I have experience with integrated marketing, social media marketing, search engine marketing and website development.

I took over GeekMeet at the beginning of 2004. GeekMeet is a high tech networking group in Dallas-Ft Worth. I have been a GeekMeet member since its inception in 1999. We provide happy hours, executive dinners, mini tradeshows and conferences. If you want to know what's happening with technology in DFW, GeekMeet is the way to find out.
