Scott McCorkell’s Summary

Through combined knowledge and expertise, M&a aims to create practical advertising,marketing and media solutions for clients’ business. We address the needs of clients big or small, whether emergent companies or long-established multinationals. Be it the formulation of marketing and advertising strategies, media placement, event creation through management, telemarketing, advertising campaigns or PR, we never forget our two most pivotal responsibilities: to reach deadlines and get results. View M&a as a complementary marketing department, interfacing our skills and pooling our resources with clients’ to create synergy and achieve success for your business. Beginning with the hands that touch your business. M&a currently employs over 50 professional people across every key discipline in the IT&T marketing and advertising arena. M&a believes in the personal touch, which means a hands-on approach to your business,no matter how big or small the job. Employees make it their business to know your brand. Above all, your business is handled with care. As specially appointed custodians of your brand equity, we do everything in our power to nurture, protect, and grow it. With M&a’s professional touch, you can rest assured your business is in capable hands.

Scott McCorkell’s Specialties:

M&a (McCorkell and Associates) was established in 1990, and from its humble beginnings, has evolved considerably in 18 years, to embrace a broad range of communications services, including marketing, advertising, event management and public relations, with offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Singapore, Hong Kong & India.
