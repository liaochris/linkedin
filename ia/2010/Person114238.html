    At High Speed Telecom, LLC we provide, install, and service business telephone systems from several manufacturers including Nortel such as the Meridian Norstar and the VoIP system, Business Communications Manager (BCM) as well as solutions from Avaya, Toshiba, NEC, Panasonic, Samsung, and ESI. We also have certified technicians to assist in relocating your phone system.

    Many of these systems support VoIP and are perfect for offices with 4 or 5 phones up to 250 phones.

    These systems support all of the time/money saving application such as Voice Mail, Automated Attendant, Unified Messaging, Automatic Call Distribution or Call Center, Music or Message on hold, Multi-party Conference Calling, and Flexible Call routing to name a few.

    We work with both single site locations as well as multi site offices tied together via VoIP.

    High Speed Telecom, LLC can implement a complete solution including all of your voice and data cabling and video surveillance systems.
