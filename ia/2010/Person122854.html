Lawrence Touitou’s Summary

Lawrence is an accomplished online marketing professional with expertise in search marketing, strategic and tactical SEO, social media, and web analytics. He has a proven track record of driving business results through boosting organic traffic, focusing on revenue-generating initiatives, and training core business units in search methods unique to their roles. His search portfolio includes work with high-profile sites such as MySpace, IGN, Rotten Tomatoes, AskMen, Photobucket, WhatIfSports, SpringWidgets, FilePlanet, Direct2Drive and GameSpy.

Currently, Lawrence is the head of SEO at IGN Entertainment, directing SEO activities for IGN's many gaming and entertainment sites in San Francisco and assisting partner sites such as MyFOX affiliates, FOX television and PR in LA and, NYPOST and WSJ in NY. He helped successfully launch verticals across these various properties related to Celebrities, Movies, Sports, News, Horoscopes, Academy Awards not to mention MySpace Latino (Social Media en Español) which leaped from 0 to over .5M uniques/day in 6 months, as well as AskMen’s Top 99 2008 & 2009 launches which got them their highest traffic day -over 1M uniques- in history.

Lawrence’s success is owed to his ability to create key relationships within the organizations he works with, breaking through bureaucratic barriers to achieve results. This has led to many company-wide initiatives to which he is credited. He also contributes greatly to the SEO knowledge base by creating research docs and presentations on object-page best practices, on-page SEO, off-page SEO, FIM Autolink program, MySpace channels, new hire docs and the FIM WIKI. This, in addition to countless other recommendations, insights, collaborations with other SEOs and professional accomplishments makes him an invaluable player in the executive SEO space.

Lawrence was 2008 guest speaker for Search Engine Marketing Professionals (SEMPO) at SMX Santa Clara and voted SEO MVP by Natasha Robinson.
