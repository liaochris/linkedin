Frank Johnson, Digital Media Strategist’s Summary

I am a digital media strategist who specializes in helping companies and organizations effectively use the web and other digital means to increase profits, reduce expenses, improve efficiencies, or otherwise promote their cause.

My recent accomplishments include:

- Managed pay-per-click advertising program to an estimated 513% increase in revenue and an estimated 2,730% increase in profitability over a five-year period

- Directed online marketing efforts for niche ecommerce website, resulting in 453% increase in revenue over three years

- Initiated relaunch of dormant music technology website and shepherded site to ongoing profitability within 4.5 months
