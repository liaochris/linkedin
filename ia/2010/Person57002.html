Janet Powers’s Summary

Janet Powers is active in the Boston-area business scene, and is a popular speaker on topics that include creating a website, blogging, social media, and entrepreneurship.

The Diva Toolbox is a multi-media online community that inspires, empowers, educates, entertains and connects women.

The Diva Toolbox provides women the opportunity to contribute to the conversation, have their voice heard and is an outlet to publish and share their expertise, products and services.

Expressive Web Solutions is a regional leader in providing custom graphic design services for websites, promotional products, social media and other marketing collateral for a wide variety of businesses.
