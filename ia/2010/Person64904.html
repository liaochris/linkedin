Steve Davies’s Summary

With over 16 years experience in the Financial Planning Industry, both as a qualified Financial Planner (CFP status) and in a variety of Business Development roles, I have experienced many facets of the investment industry. These include; strategic financial planning advice to a wide client demographic base, being a representative of product providers (including positions with larger organisations and smaller niche groups), and establishing an AFSL, with the role of Responsible Officer and Key person.

Using logical analysis to reach conclusions I enjoy solving complex problems and seek structure within the business environment. Communication in these roles has been paramount and I perform, especially when public speaking, with energy and enthusiasm.
