Michael Shaw’s Summary

I endeavor to identify with our clients’ business needs and create mutual and insightful methods to deliver significantly differentiated results valued by the clients’ customers.

The process is grounded in the principle that authentic innovation is present at the crossroads of a developing need and an enabling technology. At Innovation Focus we provide custom programs that will uncover opportunities with your current customers, customers you don’t currently serve and potential future customers. And, we show you ways to drive those opportunities into results that impact your business.

Our client engagements cover a broad field of B2B and B2C business areas with Fortune 1000 companies across many diverse industries including; CPG, consumer products, consumer healthcare, financial services, pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, construction, agriculture, education and medical research.
