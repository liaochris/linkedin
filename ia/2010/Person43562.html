Cory Mausolf’s Summary

Career Coach  Resume Writer  Etiquette Trainer

Cory started her professional career as a Recruiter in 2001 helping small to mid-sized companies find the right candidate for Management, Sales and Engineering positions. In 2007 Cory shifted professions staying in the Career Management industry specializing in Information Technology.

She ran a very successful Career Service Department for a Microsoft I.T. Training Facility that opened the doors to a Campus Director role in a year. Due to the demand and personal success coaching her students Cory started Authentic Business and Career Coaching late 2008 which gave her the opportunity to work with more people.

Her business specializes in helping individuals understand their strengths, gain clarity in career direction and fulfillment of career goals. Certified in Career Coaching she offers a unique coaching methodology that promotes action and achievement. As a trained Resume Writer and Interview Coach she works with job seekers to improve their personal brand, marketing plan and job search that significantly increases opportunity for job offer's.

Cory also has a Certification in Etiquette Training that she uses to helps college student become business ready and Managers to properly conduct business globally. Her consulting with businesses also includes training staff to work efficiently through team building, time management and employee positioning potential that positively affects the return on investment.
