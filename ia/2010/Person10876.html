Andrea Balboni’s Summary

I have worked with the luxury + fashion, arts, corporate and media industries as a creative consultant and graphic designer.

International experience includes London, New York City, Milan, and Tokyo.

I practice a user centered design approach. User centered design considers all factors of users’ experience: cultural, social, cognitive and physical. By establishing a deeper understanding of the user, I am able to design products that answer to the needs of individuals. The result is the generation of long-term client/consumer relationships, the establishment of a strategic edge over competitors, and more efficient work processes.
