Dr. Rafiq M. Choudhry’s Summary

Dr Rafiq Muhammad Choudhry began his carrier by rendering services at Mangle Hydroelectric Power Station Extension Project Units 9 & 10 in Pakistan before proceeding for master degree abroad. After completing master’s degree, he completed two expressway projects, i.e. Royal Pinklao Elevated Road Project and Don Muang Tollway Project as Project Engineer/Deputy Project Manager and Project Coordinator in Thailand. He gained more than eight years of construction industry experience during which he completed three mega projects. Since 2000, he is a Faculty Member of National University of Sciences and Technology, Pakistan till to date. He used to teach Project Management to civil engineering students. Other courses taught to Military Engineers of the Pakistan Engineer Corps at Military Engineering Services (MES) School include Construction Planning and Scheduling, Economic Decision Analysis in Construction, and Construction Project Administration. His expertise includes project management, construction management, construction safety, risk management, claims and disputes resolution, and PM training. He rendered services in organizing an International Conference for International Council for Research and Innovation in Building and Construction (CIB) Working Commission W99 held on 28-30 June 2006, Beijing, China. He has edited the book titled “Global Unity for Safety and Health in Construction” on the conference proceedings. He has been the guest editor for a ScienceDirect journal, Safety Science, Special Issue on “Construction Safety” Volume 46, Issue 4 April 2008. He has published 30 papers in refereed journals and conferences. His experience in industry and subsequent research has demonstrated a strong interest in improving the safety and efficiency of construction projects. He is a licensed Professional Civil Engineer in Islamabad. Presently, he is Head of Construction Engineering and Management program in SCEE, NUST, H-12, Islamabad, Pakistan.

Dr. Rafiq M. Choudhry’s Specialties:

Dr Rafiq has extensive professional expertise in construation industry for managing projects and for teaching in project management, construction management, construction safety, risk management, claims and disputes resolution, and PM training. He is has good command on analysis by using computer software such as SPSS, Minitab, Microsoft Project, @Risk, Build Soft Estimating System, and Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) for writing and editing of technical papers and presentations.
