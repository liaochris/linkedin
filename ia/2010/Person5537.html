Ashley Albrecht, CMP’s Summary

Ashley Albrecht, Managing Director of Albrecht Events, LLC, brings extensive event marketing and logistical experience to corporate events. She has produced events locally in the Philadelphia region, nationally, and internationally for a variety of organizations including pharmaceutical companies, associations, and charitable organizations. Through these experiences, she has met an array of meeting and event objectives and built strong relationships. Ashley is known for juggling countless unseen details with ease; providing intelligent, thought provoking strategies; and having a thoughtful, friendly, calm, down-to-earth demeanor.

Ashley enjoys helping clients to tell their story by creating environments and experiences that allow guests to deepen relationships and explore new ideas. She has an authentic drive toward hospitality and an aim to think about routine formats creatively.

Ashley has grown her company organically one successful event at a time. She attributes this success to being fully engaged in the needs and goals of her clients; to offering flexible, prompt, understanding, and thorough responses; and to making business decisions that reflect a caring attitude for the people involved (employees, customers, vendors) and for the natural world. Ashley has made green meetings and events a priority for her company and strives to offer more and more ways to produce events with less and less environmental impact. Ashley has published several articles on the subject for the PA Chapter of Meeting Professionals International.
