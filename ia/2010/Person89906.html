Jaret Christopher’s Experience

    CareerAdvantage

    CareerAdvantage provides web-based, job-seeker tools to help candidates (from entry level to C-level executives) research potential employers and identify new relationships and "hidden" job opportunities.

    Our online Career Portal is delivered as a Software as a Services (SaaS) platform and contains fast, accurate information including in-depth information on millions of companies and key executives, a national recruiter database, hidden job opportunities obtained from thousands of web sites and news sources.

  • Founder

    SalesFuel

    (Internet industry)

    November 2007 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    SalesFuel provides on-demand sales intelligence and prospecting solutions that increase sales productivity and revenue. Sales and marketing professionals use SalesFuel to quickly identify "high-probability prospects" based on in-depth company profiles, contacts and trigger events. SalesFuel users can track real-time, sales intelligence about their clients, prospects and target companies that are more likely to purchase their products and services in the near future.

    SalesFuel aggregates sales intelligence on millions of companies and updates its business information daily through a combination of its proprietary natural language processing (SmartNLP) technology, web-crawling technology, and third-party data providers. The SalesFuel sales intelligence portal is delivered in an easy to use web-based prospecting system or can be integrated with third-party CRM providers.
