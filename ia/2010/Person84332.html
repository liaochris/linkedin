Ian Griffith’s Summary

Beverage Media Group has been in business for 70 years publishing the beverage journal for several markets. We also support a network of publishers that together account for the primary trade journal 33 states. Typically a beverage journal will list products available from wholesalers in that market and is used by retailers and restaurants to source products and review pricing. In certain markets this information is available online using our B2B ecommerce platform. Licensees in those markets can find the distributor of a product, review available pricing levels and order directly online.

We also offer ecommerce websites for retailers to sell wine direct to consumers. Each website has a custom design and takes advantage of a platform designed specifically for selling wine. We collect product updates directly from the store's inventory system, store items are matched with a database of labels and tasting notes to help support sales. In certain markets we can leverage our relationship with wholesalers to offer "virtual inventory". This allows a retailer to sell products online that are available through their wholesaler, without owning any stock.
