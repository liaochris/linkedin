Lewis Armenta’s Summary

Former Army Officer; served initially within the enlisted ranks in Military Intelligence with assignments in Fort Leonard Wood, Fort Devens, Kelley Air Force Base and most notably Berlin Germany during tumultuous times '90-'92; selected to receive an Army Green to Gold Scholarship returning to College to complete my degree and earn a Commission as an Armor Officer. Served in positions of increasingly greater responsibility beginning my career as a Platoon Leader leading 4 M1A1 Abrams Tank Crews rising through the ranks to Captain and completing a successful Company Command. Transferred branches to the Army Acquisition Corps - the Army's contracting and procurement arm. Served one year as an Assistant Program Manager with TSM Abrams, the users representative of the Abrams tank program before resigning my commission and leaving the Army in July '03 to pursue family stability and career goals in the civilian community. Since leaving the military, I have pursued several job opportunities increasing my knowledge about all aspects of owning, rehabbing, leasing, and managing residential and commercial real estate, while simultaneously furthering my education, and honing my skills in negotiating creative strategies for acquiring and controlling real property.
