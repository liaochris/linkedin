Tiffany Lynch’s Summary

As community relations manager, Tiffany oversees Time Warner Cable’s support of local community, charitable and not-for-profit organizations through in-kind and cash donations and the creation of and airing of 30-second public service announcements.

Tiffany is responsible for Time Warner Cable's community relations in its footprint in San Diego County, Coachella Valley, Morongo Basin, Banning and Barstow in Southern California.

Tiffany is also responsible for all TWC events in the communities that she works within, including the Best Moms essay contests and events in San Diego and Desert Cities, Clothes 4 a Cause and Time Warner Cable's new initiative Connect a Million Minds.

She is manages all programming carried on the TWC's San Diego On Demand channel 144, where local producers and nonprofits can share information on what they are doing in Southern California.

Tiffany Lynch’s Groups:

Tiffany serves on the board of directors of the Poway Unified School District Foundation, where she holds the position of treasurer and the San Diego Council on Literacy, where she serves as vice chair. She also a member of Women in Cable & Telecommunications (WICT) and the National Association for Multi-Ethnicity in Communications (NAMIC).

She currently is on the Girl Scouts San Diego-Imperial Council's Corporate Council and is an advisor to Madison High School's School of the Arts, as well as the Coronado High School School of the Arts.
