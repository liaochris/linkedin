Steve Tonkin’s Summary

E-mail to: steve@quantiple.com

We seek to add value to organizations by combining principles from Leadership Development and Training with principles from Operational Accounting to come alongside current leadership to increase organizational value. Whether it’s to grow profit margins and cash flow, maximize the overall value of a company, or to help an organization accomplish their social mission, Quantiple will design, implement, enhance, and monitor a strategy to get results.

The Quantiple strategy considers both staffing and training as well as process and procedures to identify root problems and develop comprehensive solutions to increase value and effectiveness.
