Dr. Alan Winder’s Summary

Feel free to add me to your network if you think we may be able to work together or help each other in any way.

Currently I'm involved in several entrepreneurial ventures as well as evaluating new investment opportunities for myself and several partners.

My professional background is diverse:

I've co-managed a hedge fund that focuses on overlooked and undervalued companies. Activist philosophy, in a nice way whenever possible, working closely with company management to unlock shareholder value.

I've also worked as a FINRA-registered investment banker at a boutique bank in NYC, focusing on raising capital, facilitating PIPEs, and providing strategic advisory services to companies on a wide variety of topics including corporate restructurings, divestitures, and M&A.

I am also a licensed Clinical Psychologist with specialization in treating stress disorders, mental health issues relating to aging, and mood disorders related to adjustment difficulties. I maintain a part-time private practice and own and direct a company providing contract mental health services to long-term care facilities. I'm interested in academic work as well, having taught as an adjunct for several semesters at a private college.

Dr. Alan Winder’s Specialties:

Finding undervalued public companies to invest in, raising ~$3-$30mm institutional capital for quality deals, connecting quality private companies with Private Equity.
