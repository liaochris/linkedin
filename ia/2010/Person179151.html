Ruth Urban’s Summary

Ruth Urban MS, Principal in The Urban Group, is a Certified Professional Facilitator by the International Association of Facilitators, and believes in problem solving by tapping the wisdom in the room.

Ruth brings 25 years of conflict resolution experience to her facilitation and organizational development practice.
