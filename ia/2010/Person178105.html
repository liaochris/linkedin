Roy Albers’s Summary

Roy Albers is a seasoned jewelry professional with extensive experience in diamonds, gemstones, and manufacturing. While senior vice president of operations of Slane & Slane, he managed explosive growth of six-times the sales volume. He was chief gemologist and vice president of gemstone acquisitions of Tiffany & Co. As a buyer of diamond and precious gems at QVC, he created the Affinity diamond brand. He also managed operations for luxury start-up Adornis and the New York operation of Swarogem. Roy travelled the world in search of the finest gemstones for Krementz Gemstones. He has served on the board of directors of the American Gem Trade Association and the American Gem Society.
