Webster Lewin’s Summary

As a thought leader with nearly 18 years of experience in the interactive media and digital space, Webster is responsible for spearheading digital thinking and strategy in MS&L's North American offices. Webster works with a variety of clients in the healthcare, consumer and corporate practices, driving home the importance of digital innovation to both clients and staff.

In 2008 Webster was elected to the Mobile Marketing Association, North America Board of Directors. He also serves as co-chairman of the Education committee of the MMA, and has been active member of the MMA Mobile Adverting committee.

Prior to joining MS&L, Webster was the director of mobile marketing at R/GA in New York, working with the Mobile & Emerging Platforms (MEP) group to promote and develop the agency's in-house mobile marketing and advertising practice. He was responsible for extending that capability across client accounts, including Nike, Johnson & Johnson, Nokia, L'Oréal, and Verizon Wireless.

Prior to joining R/GA, Webster served as supervisor of mobile marketing at VML, a WPP company, where he had a leading role in the development of mobile initiatives for Windows Mobile, Windows Live, Sprint, Burger King, and others.

Webster has worked in film, television, interactive media, and digital marketing. He was the co-founder and creative director of the DE-LUX’O interactive design studio and publishing company and led the development of interactive marketing programs for international clients like IBM, Sony, Disney, Audi, Siemens Mobile Networks, Tetra Pak, Bertelsmann Online, Citibank, Quicksilver, Lee Jeans, Nissan, Neutrogena, and others.

Webster has an MFA in film directing from Columbia University School of the Arts and a BS in communications from the University of Texas at Austin.
