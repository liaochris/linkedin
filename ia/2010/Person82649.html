Home Management Solutions’s Summary

Home Management Solutions is the answer for foreclosed, troubled or abandoned properties. Some of the nation's top banks, realtors, mortgage companies and private owners rely on HMS for every aspect of rehabilitation & maintenance associated with keeping houses and commercial buildings ready for resale.

HMS executives and contractors have more than two decades experience in landscaping, rehab, inspections, redesign, drywall, roofing, tarps, painting interiors and exteriors, pool services, installation of water systems, fencing, cabinetry, carpeting, bathrooms, electrical boxes, damaged tile, ripped up flooring, broken windows and replacement of damaged electrical and gas systems.

HMS also secures homes, commercial buildings and properties for banks, government agencies, business and private owners, including evictions, re-keys and security.

HMS has many clients choose monthly property support services, such as pool and house cleaning and lawn maintenance.

HMS is the first choice of banks such as Bank of America, Citibank, Chase and WaMu; it also is the first choice for government agencies in South Florida; including Freddie Mac, Fannie Mae, and the cities of Wilton Manors and Oakland Park.

HMS also offers contracting services. The company has more than seven years experience building and rehabilitation of south Florida homes.

Home Management Solutions’s Specialties:

Renovation, repair, preservation, rehabilitation, maintenance of bank-owned properties in and around Southeast Florida.
