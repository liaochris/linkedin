Stan Relihan’s Summary

I'm President & CEO of Expert Executive Search, a Global Search, Selection & Recruitment firm located in Sydney, Australia.

With a background in Marketing, Communications & Media, I was privileged to be involved in the genesis of Pay TV, Broadband Internet and Mobile Telecommunications in Australia.

Over the past decade, I’ve leveraged this knowledge & experience to establish an enviable track record of success in assisting start-ups, blue chip multinationals, and leading local & regional players with their Human Capital requirements.

This success has come as a result of my unique ability to understand the needs & motivations of both Client and Candidate - and then to match the appropriate Skills, Desires, Personalities & Cultures in a way that maximises potential synergies for both parties.

I enjoy meeting people - and my philosophy involves developing relationships with smart, ambitious & successful individuals who share my interests and passions - well in advance of requirement.

As a result, I’ve developed an extensive network of friendships and business alliances across the Asia Pacific and around the world.

For Employers & Managers who truly believe that People are their greatest asset, Expert Executive Search is able to discreetly run rings around any and all competitors in Precision & Speed of Response - as well as Candidate Pedigree, Motivation and Cultural Alignment.

With a network of 20,000+ LinkedIn connections, I operate on a Global basis - and invite you to put this to the test with your next Retained Search or Senior-level Recruitment requirement.
