Manuela Marcheggiani’s Summary

Manuela Marcheggiani launched Isomers Laboratories in 1988 with a blender and an idea. Frustrated with not being able to win the battle with her own skin care issues from over the counter products, Manuela set out to create something for herself that would work. Work, they did!!! The results she was getting from her inventions caught the eye of many other people who wanted to solve beauty and skin issues of their own.

Today, with her team, she is working to revolutionize the skin care industry by developing and designing skin care systems that combine product innovation, approachable science, exceptional customer care, and tangible results. Our goals are to debunk myths about the beauty industry and to empower today’s consumer to make informed choices.

With a strong commitment to Research and Development, Isomers Laboratories formulates and manufactures more than 100 products addressing skin care, cosmetics and nutritional needs. Clients aged 20 to 80 and older – who seek to look their best – purchase Isomers products to prevent and reverse aging skin conditions.

Manuela is also a lecturer, speaker, and teacher of Cosmetic Chemistry. She and her products are regularly featured in women's and beauty magazines: Women's Wear Daily, Fashion, Canadian Hairdresser, Wish, Elevate, Cosmetic World, Beauty Snob, to mention a few.

Manuela has also co-authored select research throughout her career and published them with her partner and husband. Recent examples of published works include:

"Evaluation of Synergistic Effects of Topical Application of Phosphatidylcholine and Lupinus Albus (White Lupin) Extract in Treatment of Puffy Eyes" ;
"Evaluation of Treatment of Photo-Aging Through Synergistic Application Histamine, Tryptase and Nuclear Factor Kappa B (NF- κB) Inhibitors"
"Effect of Topical Application of Creatine Monohydrate as a Function of Delivery System and Concentration on Skin Elasticity and Cellular Turnover"
