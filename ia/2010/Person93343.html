Jeff Kimball’s Summary

Jeff is a leader with extensive experience in operations, product marketing and program management. With his business and technical background, Jeff has built his career by creating solutions to business problems.

Jeff is a well-respected, experienced manager who focuses on people development and change management. He has directly managed large and small teams, geographically diverse teams, and large, matrixed teams. He is a natural coach and leader and takes much of his professional satisfaction from developing people as individuals and as business assets.

Currently, Jeff is the Chief Operating Officer at Internet Broadcasting (IB) in St. Paul, Minnesota. There he is responsible for the business success of its Web Services division, with a focus on products, operations, advertising solutions, technology, and service.

Previously, Jeff was Senior Vice President of Product Development at Vive Computing. Founded in 2007 by Jim Rutt, Vive was formed to fundamentally improve support for computers, software and peripherals. Jeff’s responsibilities included quality assurance, product marketing, and program management.

At AOL, Jeff led the Strategic Alignment team and was responsible for the communication and adoption of the US strategies in Europe. Simultaneously, Jeff managed the pan-European Product Marketing team and was accountable for product and business strategies spanning AOL Europe.

Before moving to London, Jeff was the business lead for more than ten releases of AOL including AOL 4.0 Update, AOL 5.0, AOL 6.0, AOL 7.0, AOL 8.0, and AOL 9.0. AOL 9.0 was the most successful release in AOL’s history; it was adopted by 10 million consumers in 130 days.

Jeff also managed teams that ran AOL.com (improving the advertising and content business by 50% to $150 million) and developed and popularized almost every AOL consumer service (e-mail, Search, instant messaging, AIM, Parental Controls, Pictures, Chat, etc.).
