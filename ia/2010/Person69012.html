Fareeza Fadzil’s Summary

Fareeza is driven by the hunger for knowledge, with the ability to work methodically in delivering to deadlines. A team player with a natural instinct to lead and contribute valuable and uncommon ideas by having the ability to think out of the box. She feels very strongly for the quality of deliverables thus pushes for undivided cooperation from her team members. Her humble experience provides versatility and creativity in her approach to tasks at hand.
Having passion in developing brands, coupled with her experience in creative branding, and a flair in PR, Fareeza has a combination of experience in understanding client’s needs, wants and expectation.
She is instrumental in developing the elaborated launch campaign of the South Johor Economic Region (ISKANDAR) in 2006. This includes the full extend of public relations and media. She was also responsible in the preparation of the accomplishment report presented to Khazanah Nasional following the launch to measure the success of the event.

Tourism Malaysia was her single most pride in having participated, crafted and executed the strategic development and implementation of an interactive campaign through the website ‘I Need A Break’ for Singapore Market. Her responsibilities include undertaking regular market visits to evaluate communication effectiveness as well as understanding local market needs (on behalf of agency team). The campaign over achieved expected hits and targeted sales. This site is still being used by Singaporeans in booking their holiday packages to Malaysia, and has in itself become a sub-brand for Tourism Malaysia amongst Singaporean.

Additionally, Fareeza was also involved in conducting researches as well as the brand plan development of FMCG brands such as F&N Fun Flavours, F&N Dairies, and HACO Swiss. These brand plans includes the plan’s expected effect and ROI for the fiscal year of 2008/2009.
