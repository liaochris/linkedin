Marc LaVoie, PE’s Experience

  • Manager of Operations and Project Management - Northeast Regional Office

    PM Realty Group

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    August 2009 — Present (10 months)

    Marc LaVoie is the Manager of Operations & Project Management of PM Realty Group’s Northeast Regional Office. LaVoie has worked in the commercial real estate industry since 1992 and has experience in engineering, development, construction management, operations, and property management.

    In this capacity, LaVoie is responsible for managing the engineering team for the daily operations of 9.0 million square feet of R&D, office and industrial warehouse facilities. He and his team work closely with ownership and tenants to provide repairs and maintenance, energy management, preventive maintenance, and life safety testing. He is also responsible for managing the redevelopment projects throughout the portfolio. These projects range from complete building renovations to building system upgrades, architectural improvements, site improvements and reconfiguring buildings from single tenant to multi-tenant occupancy.
