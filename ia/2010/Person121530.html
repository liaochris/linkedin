Last Minute Massage’s Summary

Last Minute Massage is a therapeutic massage website whose mission is to connect Licensed Massage Therapists with online customers looking for massages at Last Minute times. The Last Minute Massage website and social network is set up to promote Massage Therapy business and help educate people on the benefits of therapeutic massage.

Last Minute Massage started with the purpose of providing online consumers with discounted massages in their area and at their convenience. You can read the Licensed Massage Therapist reviews before booking a Massage with that business.

We believe that you deserve a great therapeutic massage when you want it, even if that’s at the Last Minute! We provide you with a licensed massage therapist that is located in a city near you.

To make each massage experience special, we do the home work for you ahead of time. By this we mean; giving definitions on the various types of massages, offering “how to” videos for you to learn techniques, and publishing reviews from other clients who had recently been given a massage by your choice of therapist. Now you can know what to expect before each massage that you book withour website, Last Minute Massage.

We also provide services for massage therapists and business owners by helping promote and market their services via a user friendly patent pending system and method for posting distressed or unfilled inventory of massage times (at the “Last Minute”). You (the therapist or business) control the prices and post the times you have available in your schedule!

At Last Minute Massage, we know scheduling in advance can be inconvenient, so we are here to help you get a relaxing, therapeutic, soothing massage at a great price with the massage therapist of your choice!
