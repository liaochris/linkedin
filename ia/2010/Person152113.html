Nathaniel Williams’s Summary

Nathaniel has more than three (3) years of professional experience in the field of structural engineering.

At Jacobs, Nathaniel is currently a Structural Engineer II at the Cypress office in California. As a structural engineer, he is responsible for the successful design of a wide range of structural applications in petroleum refineries. In particular, Nathaniel is responsible for the implementation and review of structural calculations, drawings, and details. Nathaniel is also responsible for preparing material requisitions and performing material estimates. Nathaniel’s key strengths are his abilities to meet the demands of the client in an effective, efficient, and professional manner. Nathaniel always takes the initiative to take on additional tasks that will help the project meet significant deadlines.
