Carolyn Kepcher’s Summary

Carolyn & Co is aimed at women just graduating from college, women looking to advance their careers and women juggling the challenges of careers and families. The website is www.workherwaycom. Kepcher is the author of the best-selling business book "Carolyn 101," which landed at No. 2 on the New York Times best-sellers list. She also writes a column for The New York Daily News: "Your Career," which addresses business, career and balance. .

A regular on the speaking circuit, Kepcher gives frequent seminars to various Fortune 500 companies, as well as executive women's groups and university audiences.

Kepcher has publically spoken with national role models including Hillary Rodham Clinton. She has appeared on such shows as"The Oprah Winfrey Show" and the "Today" Show and continues to contribute to business shows to share her experiences and philosophies on woman, business and balance.

Kepcher partnered with large companies including Yahoo!, Avon and Microsoft in developing programs to assist entrepreneurs.

Kepcher was Executive Vice President and Managing Partner for Trump Golf Properties. She started with the Trump Organization in 1995 and was responsible for start-up operations, sales, marketing and development of Donald Trump’s golf clubs.

At A Glance: When Donald Trump needed to select two advisers to assist him on NBC’s reality show "The Apprentice," Carolyn Kepcher was an obvious choice. Trump found her to be a "killer" businesswoman, as exemplified by promoting Kepcher to the top ranks of the Trump Organization. Her input proved to be invaluable to Trump in making his firing decisions.
