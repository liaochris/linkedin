Steven Puma, MBA in Sustainable Management’s Summary

I am a big-picture thinker, a technology nut, a writer, "green MBA", a Maven, a Connector. My favorite thing is to learn about new ideas, new people and new technologies. My second-favorite thing is to connect people with the ideas, people, resources, and technologies they need to change the world!

I am interested in electric vehicles, alternative-fuel vehicles, clean technology, cradle-to-cradle manufacturing, social media, innovation, mass collaboration, whole-systems design.

I believe that we can redesign our world to be both hyper-efficient and hyper-abundant, and that those things aren't mutually-exclusive: it's all about systems thinking and design.

I received my MBA in Sustainable Management from the Presidio School of Management in 2008. Since 2007, I have been promoting sustainability, and writing about my interests, especially electric vehicles, through blogging, and my writing regularly appears on TriplePundit.com.

Through my own consulting efforts, I have helped small startup companies communicate their message more effectively through my network of contacts, through social media, blogging, business plan and other writing, all supported by my research skills

My prior background includes over 10 years of experience in the IT field, focused on automated software deployment and standardized desktops, and a B.A. in Computer Science from Rutgers, which gives me the strong technical background necessary to understand how technology works, and communicate that effectively to laymen.

As for hobbies, I love cars, cigars, guitars, Belgian beer and mechanical watches, as well as gadgets, computers and the outdoors: hiking, biking, skiing and travel.
