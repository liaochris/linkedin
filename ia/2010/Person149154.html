Mike Gulitz’s Summary

Mike is the chief editor of the http://mpc-Blog.com, author of the Marketing Mojo eZine and Co-Founder & CEO for MPC. Since 1996, Mike has led sales and business development for technology start ups including Purchase Pro, CollegeClub.com, & MIR3. Mike Co-Founded MPC with his wife and business partner, Juliana.

Mike writes and speaks regularly on the topics of best practices for contact management including lead development, workflow automation, client appreciation and CRM technology.
