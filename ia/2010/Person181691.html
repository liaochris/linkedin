Sandi Verrecchia CMC, MBA, CPCC’s Summary

Sandi Verrecchia is a professional leadership and life coach trained with The Coaches Training Institute. She is a Certified Management Consultant, holds a Masters degree and has over 20 years experience in the financial services industry. Her diverse background of consulting, operations, marketing and sales is a wonderful compliment to her passion for coaching.

Her goal is to is to help individuals and teams explore the infinite possibilities in life choices, decisions and paths both in work life and in personal life to support living a more fulfilling life.


Sandi is an experienced presenter to both small and large groups and bring many life experiences and professional expertise to her coaching and consulting clients.
