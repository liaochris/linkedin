Becky Washington’s Summary

I love my role helping launch the careers of Community College students.

As a Certified Professional Resume Writer, I enjoy helping people look good on paper and online by helping them to make the most of their background while being honest and positive.

I am hungry for information about the labor market and employment trends. I pay attention to issues around college costs, company-paid educational opportunities, and career paths.

I teach "Scholarship Dollars for College", "Career Exploration Cooperative Education" and "Career Exploration Seminar."

My passion is anything that helps people thrive in their work. I am interested in applications of the StrenghtsFinder approach, the research into Flow by Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi and labor market economics.

My StrenghtsFinder results top 5: Ideation, Activator, Positivity, Woo & Responsibility
