Michael Oon’s Summary

Proposition:

Imagine: your property development project; the buildings are complete but the remaining 40% is unsold. There is no interest.

Imagine: In your business park development; After a heavy investment of PR & Advertising and networking, there are still no takers for any of the plots. What do you do next - Carry on with this development?

Imagine: The development project is more than half way through. All the buildings are sold and at a premium to the market. You can start preparing for the next project.

I work as a consultant to property developers and use the techniques of traditional Feng Shui. I specialise in enabling property developers to sell their buildings faster and at a premium to the market. The focus of traditional Feng Shui is to save our clients time and money, maximize revenue and efficiency, to ensure the project is completed correctly the first-time, and the harmony of the nature and built, to ensure the health and well-being for the future residents of this project.

Our clients who use our services experience this and therefore, use our services on a continual basis. This decreases their risk profile in the investment, but increases their business confidence, reputation and business growth.

How do we work?

The procedures:
Assessment of the Owner’s Goals And Needs.
Assessment of Building Site Including Site Selection.
Initial Feng Shui Design Guidelines for the Architect.
Initial Landscaping Recommendations.
Participation with Design Team and Review of Building and Landscape Plans until the Plans are Finalized.
Input into materials, Colours, Furniture and Object Placement etc.
Assist in the Sales Process.
Date Selection for Best Time to Begin Work.


We respect the confidentially of our clients and do not give references.


The size of the projects can vary from the design of a single family residence, residential or commercial estates, commercial building, hotel/resort/spa to a city.
