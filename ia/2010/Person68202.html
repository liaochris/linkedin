Candace Polk’s Summary

Events by Candace is a boutique full service event planning firm specializing in creating stylish, chic and sophisticated events.

Candace is committed to helping you create your special event.  She listens to your needs, desires, and brainstorms with you to make the day even more special.  She is skilled at inspiring creative ideas and bringing them to life while reflecting individuality, family traditions and spiritual beliefs.

Candace Polk’s Specialties:

Experience in wedding and event coordination, locating the perfect venue complimentary to clients' budget, extensive resources and contacts with vendors and venues in the Chicagoland area, attentive to detail and most importantly listening to what clients desire and giving them what they ask for.

    About Candace:

    After 15 years of working for executives in the legal field and planning special events throughout the years, as well as planning her friends and family's social parties and weddings, Candace decided it was time to start her own event planning company.  Candace believes in making your dreams come true.   She is a minority sole proprietor, but a multi-cultural operator.  She also has a B.A. Degree in Psychology from National-Louis University in Chicago.

    Candace's philosophy is simple, she is committed to putting a creative, stylish and personal touch on each and every event that she has the honor of being a part of.
