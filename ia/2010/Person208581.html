Mindy Paget’s Summary

At the Law Offices of Mindy R. Paget
WE FIGHT YOUR TICKETS!
A Full Service Trucking & Transportation Law Firm
Dedicated to Competitive Pricing & Professional Service
Protect your business and your livelihood. Look to the Law Offices of Mindy R. Paget to defend your trucking, traffic, parking and related criminal offenses in New York City. Specializing in trucking defense and transportation law, our lawyers provide superior legal representation to corporations and individual drivers at reasonable, competitive rates.

An attorney from the Law Offices of Mindy R. Paget will appear in court on your behalf (or accompany you if your appearance is required) to deal with your tickets so that you can spend your valuable time running your business and keeping your drivers on the road. While no lawyer can guarantee a specific verdict, we can guarantee that our intimate knowledge of the New York City courts and thorough understanding of the judges and their practices will monumentally improve your chances of a favorable outcome. With each case, our attorneys will set an individualized strategy based on the venue and charge(s) in question to maximize your chances for success. In most cases, we are able to avoid traffic related misdemeanor convictions and can either greatly reduce points or eliminate them entirely.

We treat all of our clients equally, big or small -- there are no fleet requirements or ticket minimums with our firm. We fight your tickets and are pleased to counsel our clients when it is in their best interest NOT to have us fight a ticket (even if it means losing our fee).

We are committed to timely communications with our clients.
Companies that use our firm can expect to have phone calls returned the same day.

You have nothing to lose and everything to gain by calling us today!

For a free consultation, please feel free to call us at (212)-673-3220
