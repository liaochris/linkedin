Greg Corwin’s Summary

Twenty plus years of experience leading global fortune 100 IT organizations. My experience spans engineering and manufacturing IT for Zenith Electronics, worldwide application development organization for Hyatt Hotels, > $100M of infrastructure services for Motorola, and running a small manufacturing company Vance Industries. The positive impacts I have made with my leadership style, character, ethics, and dedication has made lasting changes and relationships in the organizations I have supported.

My career has been focused on being a trusted advisor in the area of technology used by the business. My approach is based on obtaining a deep understanding of the businesses that the IT organization supports, staying abreast of current technologies and the trends within, fostering strong partnerships with the business leaders, and with leveraging the talents of the IT team developing a strategic plan.

I take great pride in my work, my adaptability to new environments and my ability to build highly energized and efficient IT teams.
