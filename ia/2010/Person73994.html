Genevieve Manion’s Summary

Freelance video editor and motion graphics artist with experience in creative consulting and delivery producing.

Professional Highlights Include:

Developing templates and techniques to enable producer/editors to easily and quickly create still graphics and motion graphics using Photoshop, Final Cut Pro and Motion for PBS' International news program, Worldfocus.

Developing motion graphics production techniques with one of the "fathers of motion capture," Wes Treger, for Firebrand.

Editing video and designing motion graphics for Parry Aftab, Executive Director of Wired Safety, and foremost expert on child internet safety. These videos had been sponsored by Johnson and Johnson, Disney, The Motion Picture Association of America, and international governments to protect children and inform law makers in Washington on current issues related to keeping children safe all over the world.

Genevieve Manion’s Specialties:

Video editing and 2D motion graphic design for commercial, promotional, broadcast news and narrative projects.

After Effects tool-kitting: making dynamic animations easy and quick to re-produce for other animators.

Instruction of professional video, motion graphic, and still graphic programs to both professionals and beginners.

Workflow consulting: developing efficient, time and money saving work flows for both production and post production environments.
