Jan Mulder’s Summary

Jan Mulder has an extensive and successful career in marketing, sales and information technology. He couples this with a broad, senior management level expertise, in both the B2B and B2C aspects of approaching the market.

Jan has been active as a creative entrepreneur with his companies Hug Your Customer, Travelogue and Alleman Ideas. He has adopted the roles of Director of Encounters, Creative Entrepreneur and Social Media Speaker within these organisations. Jan is also acknowledged as an internationally renowned expert in virtual networking.

He is an experienced and confident individual whose method of approach is solid, thorough and profound. He has a clear and well established approach to public speaking and is easily approached. Jan is dedicated to establishing and maintaining a network of social and business contacts and leads the way in Europe as one of the most networked people on the planet.
