* Media Architect
* Technical Architect
* Consultant
* Educator
* Public Speaker
* Artist

I set up the first website in NSW, Australia (1993) and since then have worked in several countries in a wide variety of roles, principally on digital projects. My goal is to help with the evolution of story-telling processes through interesting and successful work with talented people. For years I have balanced corporate work with award-winning innovation through my own ventures as an artist, entrepreneur and technologist (MOD Films, thequality.com, Rack&Pin).

Michela Ledwidge’s Specialties:

Creative and technical leadership, delivery of complex projects, big picture vision, bridge-building, digital production, marketing, technical architecture, digital strategy and policy, troubleshooting, interactive product and service design, invention and prototyping.
