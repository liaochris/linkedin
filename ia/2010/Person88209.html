James Kitcher’s Summary

• NSCA Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist with substantial focus on speed, strength and power development. Design specific in and off season programs for middle school and high school athletes using Sports Specific Training (SST) and CrossFit methodologies.
• Personal Trainer with experience working with a broad clientele base with varying needs and skill levels. Basics are always stressed and consistency placed before intensity. Always interested in helping people get a piece of their life back by becoming fitter as much as I am in developing elite athletes. The emphasis is always on functional fitness - on structural movements that generate the strongest adaptations - and increasing capacity -- the ability to move large loads long distances across broad time and modal domains.
• As a coach, I'm interested in imparting knowledge. Clients can always get recommended programming from me to leverage between sessions in order to optimize their training.
