Joshua Gilmer’s Summary

Joshua Gilmer is currently earning a Master's degree in entertainment business from Full Sail University.  After working for a Birmingham eLearning house, Today's Class, he opened shop to complete computer animation and web work.  As owner of Huebris he has been working with many clients in Birmingham, and has built a small team of four individuals who he manages.  He graduated with a concentration in environment and small props modeling in September 2008 from Full Sail University.  In addition to college, Josh worked various freelance contracts, and has completed work for such companies as Duck Studios in Los Angeles, California, Phenomena Studios in Birmingham, Alabama, and HighPoint Holdings in Birmingham, Alabama.

Previously, Josh attended Virginia College in his home town of Birmingham, AL.  He worked hard, both as a work/study, and as a student.  At Virginia College, his major was also computer animation.  However, when the school's program disentigrated, Josh transferred his credits to Full Sail University .  Both colleges are nationally accredited, and he spent two years at Virginia College.
