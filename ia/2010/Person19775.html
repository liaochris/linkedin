Barbara Barde’s Summary

Barde Career Solutions was founded 2007 after business owner Barbara Barde, realized her passion involved helping career professionals discover their “true” career paths and then helping them connect to the top companies who sought and needed their talents.

Before officially starting her business, Barbara worked with a number of friends, and friends of friends, in career assessing, coaching and mentoring, and rewriting resumes to help them successfully find new careers. It was providing this support and service over three years ago now when Barbara discovered her own career passion and decided to launch her own professional services and job placement business – Barde Career Solutions.

For over 17+ years, she has been involved with the growth and development of career professionals as well as companies locally, nationally, and internationally.

Barbara brings an exceptional range of skills that encompass resume writing, building personal and business relationships, managing teams and organizations, coaching and mentoring career professionals, and strategic project planning, management, and implementation.

Prior to launching her own business, Barbara was engaged as a technical recruiter, responsible for identifying, locating, attracting and recruiting top talent for a leading IT and Engineering consulting firm. Previous positions have included the development and management of recruitment, marketing, IT call center management, Human Resource Management (including hiring/firing, training, coaching, mentoring), sales and support programs for membership-based organizations, as well as serving as the coordinator for regional youth activity programs locally as well as working in multiple states and The Netherlands.

Barbara Barde’s Specialties:

Professional resume and cover letter writing and development, career coaching, career assessment, career planning, recruitment, career placement, interviewing techniques, salary negotiation, and business plan development and implementation.
