Ron Ribitzky, M.D.’s Summary

Senior executive physician with unique range of experience that brings visionary thinking, global perspectives and clinical insight together to shape strategy and drive execution in healthcare I.T., bio-medical informatics, and Health 2.0 arena.

Results oriented situational leader who drives value creation by accelerating innovation from idea to practice, charging ahead of the technology curve, and leading cross-disciplinary multi-national teams through complex projects.

Successful track record of commercial, technical, scientific, and organizational accomplishments throughout product and service lifecycle -- from envisioning through development, marketing and sales to implementation and systems integration as a CIO, software developer, scientist, inventor, consultant, and executive with world-class companies, start-ups, healthcare organizations, R&D and academia; and ten years of clinical practice as a Flight Surgeon, Primary Care Physician, and Residency in Pediatrics.

Solution oriented hands-on and scientific technical expertise includes Mobile Point of Care, Clinical Decision Support, Electronic Medical Record, Medical Vocabulary and Semantic systems; Knowledge Management, Data Warehouse, Search technology, Artificial Intelligence, Expert Systems, Real Time Location Services/RFID, and Enterprise I.T.; Service Oriented Architecture, Component Based, Object Oriented and Relational techniques; Distributed software development, Rapid Prototyping and Extreme Programming.

Inventor and sole owner of a patent on Component-based Object-Relational Database Infrastructure and User Interface.

Published and presented extensively on diverse topics relating to Healthcare I.T. and Bio-Medical Informatics around the world.
