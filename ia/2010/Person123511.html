Lee Rhodes’s Summary

I have more than 30 years experience as a developer and manager across a wide range of engineering disciplines. Technologically, I have been particularly interested in mathematical / statistical software for the real-time analysis of massive data for networking and applications / systems performance monitoring and have been issued 4 US Patents in this field. Managerially, I really enjoy developing and growing highly-skilled engineering teams that can execute quickly and get the job done.
