Erin Runtz’s Summary

I am your multi-purpose digital media maven. I have mastered the agency side of researching, planning, buying, and executing custom digital campaigns and creative. Now, I am enjoying the publisher side of the business at AOL (and Advertising.com). Once the insertion order is signed, I help clients launch and optimize their online ad campaigns. This includes ad testing and troubleshooting, research implementation, custom ad executions such as Welcome Screens, Roadblocks, and AIM, and managing the campaign so that it executes flawlessly and delivers in full. I love digging into the more technical aspects of my job duties in the examining of tag code, 1x1's, click commands, and page loads for troubleshooting and solving issues. I am currently leading cross-brand training to integrate AOL O&O with the Advertising.com Network. I am proud to be a nominee of the first-ever company-wide Blue Monster Award recognizing top achievers.
