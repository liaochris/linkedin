K.D. Hardy’s Summary

K.D. Hardy is an author, speaker and motivator who
offers practical physical and mental tools to make it through the
transition into college life. Hardy keeps it light and bright when he
explains how to:

a. Avoid the dangers of destructive decision making
b. Get through the tough times and still perform at a high level
c. And more!

K.D. speaks for schools and organizations throughout the United
States. A few of his signature programs topics are:

1. Drug Awareness
2. Overcoming Adversity
3. Change
4. Leadership Enrichment
5. Diversity
