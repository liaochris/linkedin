Magdy Hanna’s Summary

I am in the Audit and Assurance and Business Consulting Services with over 18 years of professional experience. I have gained extensive experience in all facets of accounting and auditing, and have led audits for publicy-traded and privately-held companies, local and international, in a variety of industries, including oil and gas distribution, services, construction, manufacturing, distribution, financial services, technology, apparel, real estate, entertainment and film production. I have broad experience with the Security and Exchange Commission 1933 and 1934 Act filings including initial public offering and periodic reporting requirements. I also have expertise in employee benefit plan audits. My business consulting experience includes acquisition consulting, internal control review and training.
