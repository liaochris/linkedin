Alexander Torrenegra’s Summary

I'm a serial and parallel (?) entrepreneur specialized in online marketplaces. I've founded numerous successful ventures, including Torrenegra Labs, Voice123.com, Language123.com, and LetMeGo.com, among others.

My professional career began in Bogota, Colombia, and led me through Miami, where I founded Torrenegra Internet Solutions (Torrenegra Labs’ predecessor) before moving to New York in 2000. I focus on forming talented, dedicated teams and creating leading-edge technology platforms that lead to the rapid creation and deployment of innovative businesses in a variety of industries.
