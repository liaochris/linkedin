Bernard Casey’s Summary

Servant-leader with 20 years of providing vision and tactical planning in high-tech greenfield and turnaround situations in startup companies and entrepreneurial divisions of multi-billion dollar public corporations with results that exceeded the financial and market share goals set by Board of Directors. Delivered profitable, compound annual growth in pre- and post-IPO stages through acquisitions and mergers and organic growth. Experience includes assignments as Vice President/COO and President/CEO, development and implementation of 5-year business plans in rapidly evolving and cyclical technology markets, and growing companies/divisions from inception up to $50 million in revenue.

Responsibilities have included running all facets of American, Asian, and European companies including operations, sales, marketing, finance, engineering, and technology development. Proven ability to build highly motivated world-class organizations through attracting, retaining, and inspiring top management and contributor talent. Extensive international experience in North America, Asia, and Europe includes acquiring new customers, establishing alliances, and founding sales and manufacturing operations. Intellectual property management experience involving portfolio management, royalties from licensing, technology acquisition, and proactive and defensive litigation.
