Griff Foxley’s Summary

I am a NYC-born communications guy based in Los Angeles, CA. My mission is and has been to learn and practice the various schools of communicative arts -- from entertainment to advertising, essays and blogs to high-brow poetry. All in the hopes of inspiring people, challenging the status quo, usurping tired systems, promulgating progressive ideals and encouraging the construction of sustainable systems. Lofty ideals aside, I'm also just a dude who likes sitting by campfires, shooting the sh*t or talking about the music of the spheres. A work in progress.
