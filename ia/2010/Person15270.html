Antonio Vitti’s Summary

Co-founded MobileSpinach.com in 2009 with two partners; with the goal of delivering customized offers from local merchants to people’s cell phones. From 2007 to 2008 I was the Hong Kong based head of business development for the Asia/Pacific region for a private equity subsidiary of Franklin Templeton where I was in charge of fundraising from Asian sovereign wealth funds. In 2006 I worked as a commercial real estate analyst at BlackRock in San Francisco. I previously worked for 9 years at Lehman Brothers where from 1999 to 2005, where I was a Sell-Side Equity Research Analyst in San Francisco and covered large cap US Broker and mid-cap Asset Manager stocks on a two-person research team. Prior to that, from 1996 to 1999 at Lehman Brothers, I was a Counterparty Risk Analyst working in New York where I evaluated and managed capital market, derivative, and off-balance sheet financing and trading risks with US Brokers, Asset Manager, Mortgage firms, REITS and other specialized financial counterparties as a member of the global risk management team. Prior to Lehman Brothers, I worked for 2 years (1994-1996) at Brown Brothers Harriman & Co. as an International Bank and Country Analyst.

I am an MBA graduate from the University of California at Berkeley's Walter A, Haas School of Business (May 2007), where I spent part of my MBA studies on exchange at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology during 2006. I obtained a B.S. in Finance, Cum Laude in May 1994 from Villanova University.
