Chris DeBoo’s Summary

I help Homeowners, Realtors and Property Managers get their homes ready for sale so they sell quickly and at the best possible price.

An Accredited Staging Professional (ASP®), a member of the International Association of Home Staging Professionals (IAHSP®) and President of the Greater Chicago Chapter of Home Staging Professionals,

After attending the Chicago Academy of Fine Art, spent several years in the field of graphic design. After spending over 25 years in the corporate world I am now Owner of
SIMPLYSTAGING.

A long time resident of Naperville, an active member of the Naperville Area Chamber of Commerce, Marketing Chair for the Naperville Women In Business Committee and an Affiliate member of the Women’s Council of Realtors

My goal is delivering quality service and creative Home Staging solutions for my Clients.

Chris DeBoo’s Specialties:

Home Staging, Real Estate Staging, Redesign, Color Selection serving communities in Naperville and Western Suburbs.
