Imran Qureshi’s Summary

Senior Technical Manager/Architect with 15 years of software experience specializing in taking complex projects in the early stage, building the team, designing the technical architecture and implementing the development processes to make it succeed. I have experience leading teams and projects in both big companies and startups including six years at Microsoft, six years at Hotmail and three years at Internet Startups.

In my career I've played two main roles (often at the same time): managing technical teams and architecting scalable systems. In my technical manager role at Spleak Media Network, I was able to speed up development cycles, hire great engineers and improve service uptime. In my architect role at Hotmail and Spleak Media Network I was able to design scalable architectures that handled 50M users/day and 2M users/month respectively. I am also used to doing a good amount of hands-on development on the core infrastructure and complex features.
