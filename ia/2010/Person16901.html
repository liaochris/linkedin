Lisa Powell’s Summary

Art Rent and Lease was founded in 2000; we provide fine art rentals, leasing and sales to building and facility managers, corporate, hospitality and healthcare clients in the US. We're a woman-owned small business featuring 3,000 original works of art with over 50% of our inventory utilizing "green" and sustainable products and production methods.

Our goal is twofold: 1) Help companies conserve capital by providing tax-deductible payments on fine art via art rental and art lease financing programs and 2) to place fine art from established and emerging artists into corporate, hospitality and healthcare environments - gaining income and exposure for the artist and their works...a win-win for all.

Lisa Powell’s Specialties:

Art consulting (selection, placement & budgeting), art sales, art rentals, art leasing, rotating art rental programs, corporate art placement and curatorial services, healthcare fine art, hospitality artwork and sustainable artwork from our on-line gallery of over 3,000 works of original art - always with a 100% money back satisfaction guaranty!
