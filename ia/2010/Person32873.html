Carol Carriuolo’s Summary

Carol, M.S.Ed., CPCC, has lived and worked in Maine for over thirty years. She has a deep commitment to assisting individuals, organizations, and groups realize their dreams. She has helped launch and support many programs that continue to provide services, educational opportunities and benefits to their communities. She coaches individuals and teams to lead their lives with passion and fulfillment. Her experience is broad; she has served as a director and founder of a day care center, a public school principal, a private school administrator, a teacher, a consultant, and a facilitator. Currently she consults with nonprofit leaders, small start-up projects, and schools that are requesting assistance with strategic planning, board development, leadership support, sustainability, vibrancy, and aligning with their vision.
