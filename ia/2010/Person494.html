Larry Robles’s Summary

5 Creative Group works with some of America's most popular brands. We target small and medium sized organizations and the Fortune 500's. We utilize all new media and Internet strategies in addition to "grass roots" marketing methods.

Our mission is to cultivate strategic and long term client relationships to increase our clients' brand name recognition, develop local, national and global awareness, automate business processes and to help our clients to increase sales revenue and business growth; we are committed to offering our clients the highest level of quality, service, and creativity in the industry.
