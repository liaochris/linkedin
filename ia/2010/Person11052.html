Andrea Lelli’s Summary

I'm a Creative Designer with over 10 years of experience in strategic communication, visual and web Design.

Since October 2004, I run my own web agency: Koinema. My goal is to explore the multimedia potential to communicate in the most effective and innovative way.

I've worked for clients like: Figurine Panini, Lamborghini, Dolce&Gabbana, Citroën, Marazzi, Volvo, MSN, Ras Assicurazioni, DADA, Cineteca di Bologna, Arcte, Burson-Marsteller, Vodafone, Principe di San Daniele, Dole.

My approach is full of passion, commitment, creativity, attention to details, energy.
