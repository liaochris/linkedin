  • Co-founder

    Weboostu

    (Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    Weboostu is a reputation management service whose time has come. Thanks to the ever-growing popularity of social media sites and the booming growth of blogs, more people are using the Internet to express their views and share their lives with family and friends. In the process, those lives are becoming more transparent, which creates a perplexing dilemma: How much should the world know about me?

    Unfortunately, many consumers don't consider what living a transparent life means: Anyone who wants to know more about you can find it by searching your name. And what they find may be negative, which could be the difference between success and failure when searching for a job, being vetted by potential business partners or evaluated by a love interest.

    While weboostu doesn't promise to make the indiscretions disappear, we do help create positive messages and push them higher up in a search of a name. It's cost-effective and it helps the world view the very best of you.
