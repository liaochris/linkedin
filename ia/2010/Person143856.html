  • Hostel Manager

    Hostel Service Sp.j.

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    June 2005 — August 2005 (3 months)

    As the manager of a newly opened summer hostel my responsibility was to ensure that the facility started operating smoothly and remained this way during whole summer. My team consisted of two receptionists and our reception was opened 24/7 so I assumed the role of the third one.

    This position was an exceptional experience in many aspects. First, the managerial layer was quite new for me. Second, keeping up with changing demands of almost one hundred guests speaking English with varying levels of proficiency and different accents was a very gratifying and challenging task. Third, we had to innovate as many "best practices" were still not there and we had to come up with our own. Finally, my team and I had no previous experience in this industry but this did not stop us from being one of the better performing hostels in the company.
