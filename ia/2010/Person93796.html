Jeff May, M.Ed.’s Summary

The May Development Group offers professional training development design, delivery, and advisory services, assisting organizations to look objectively and strategically at their business, its potential, and the challenging and complex recruitment, retention, and employee development issues they face. We help businesses to determine and then implement the best approach to address their training and development needs … one that fits their budget and yet still provides the positive return on investment that every training and development program should deliver.

Our goal is to act as a business partner with our clients, combining business experience and acumen with the practiced art of listening, in order to identify challenges, seize opportunities, and deliver solutions that will provide a positive return on investment. We have worked with a wide variety of organizations facing both adversity and opportunity. Our ability to provide keen insight and practical advice is not limited to any specific organizational structure, industry, or venue.

Whether your organization is seeking advice and assistance on employee development, succession planning, recruitment, or retention, you want to find solutions that make sense, are prudent, and can be executed in a timely manner. The May Development Group excels in delivering prudent advice and solutions. Jeff offers a wide breadth of skills ranging from situational employee needs analysis, to performance management, to curriculum design, to leadership and professional development initiatives.

Jeff May, M.Ed.’s Specialties:

> Designing and implementing training and development initiatives
> Managing employee development needs assessments, curriculum design, facilitator selection and preparation, professional resource selection, and return-on-investment analysis
> Mapping leadership and management competencies
> Providing consultative services for existing internal training initiatives, organizational development, and employee recruitment and selection
> Online and e-learning assessment
