Lou Leporace’s Summary

Experienced broadband executive with extensive experience in all aspects of marketing, product management, and business development. Successful record with Internet, enterprise, and consumer companies utilizing a variety of distribution strategies from direct to multi-tier channel sales. Involvement in companies at various points in their corporate development has necessitated the utilization of a variety of business and marketing approaches. Significant international responsibility and travel. Strengths in leadership and management skills, combined with solid communication skills, have resulted in a highly successful track record within each company. Significant experience raising venture capital and membership on corporate advisory boards has provided insight into successfully building an early stage company. Interested in early-stage startups as well as companies “on the bubble.”
Track record of successfully preparing and positioning companies for sale, resulting in several exits in the past ten years – Sale of Kaseya in 2001, Alopa Networks to C-COR in 2004, Convergys Cable and Broadband to Innovast in 2003, Auspice to Arris, and Coding Technologies to Dolby Laboratories in 2007

Lou Leporace’s Specialties:

Executive Management, Product Management, Marketing, Business Development, Strategic Partnering, Mergers & Acquisitions, Strategic Planning
