Kevin Flight’s Summary

As a Senior Account Manager & Social Media Strategist at Regan Communications, I conceptualize, execute and quantify public relations plans for a variety of clients in different industries utilizing both traditional and new media. Additionally, I've helped lead the development and implementation of Regan Communications’ strategies in social media both internally and for our clients.

My client base includes The Boston Celtics, Dunkin' Donuts, Entercom Boston (WEEI-AM, WRKO-AM, WAAF-FM, Mike-FM and WEEI.com) Boston magazine, New Balance, RE/MAX of New England, Franciscan Hospital for Children and The Boston Wine Expo.
