Fahad Khan’s Summary

Fahad Khan, 28, is a successful entrepreneur, driven by a set of larger-than-life ambitions and goals and an abiding desire to understand how the world works.

Most recently, Fahad was appointed President and CEO of Organic Spread Media (“OSM”) as a part of OSM’s recent acquisition of Social Shore. OSM is owned and backed by investors and entrepreneurs associated with Veronis Suhler Stevenson (VSS) - world's largest media-focused private equity firm.

Prior to his current job, Fahad Khan was at helm of Social Shore, a company he founded, grew, managed and successfully sold. Social Shore was a pure-play social media strategy and technology consulting company serving several of Fortune 500 brands and a number of innovative small to medium sized advertising agencies.

Before founding Social Shore, Fahad headed-up WiMAX Telephony Product Development Division at a $500+ million Abu Dhabi Group telecom venture. Fahad was an influential part of the core team that launched the world’s first and largest Mobile WiMAX Network (802.16e) - world's first national wide 4G, E2E IP, NGN network.

Prior to that, Fahad founded an innovation consulting company, while still a student at Chalmers, in Sweden. In his early career, he worked for a New York based telecom startup and was single handedly responsible for starting, scaling, growing and managing 80+ person multi-country BPO operations for telecom providers based in New York and Florida. He was roughly 21 years of age, at that time.

A scholarly expert in the area of International Entrepreneurship, Fahad holds Civilingenjörsexamen degree in Management and Economics of Innovation, from Chalmers University of Technology, Göteborg -- the most prestigious university in Sweden and one of the oldest & top tech universities in the world.
