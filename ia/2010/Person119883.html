Kumars Multiservice Inc’s Summary

Kumars Multiservice, Inc was established in 1997 to provide specialized services to families in the Harlem, Hamilton Heights, Washington Heights, Bronx and other NYC communities. Currently, Kumars Multiservices is expanding to offer a wide range of industry leading services which are specifically designed for urban families and communities.

Kumars Multiservices is headquartered in New York City, NY, and convientely located at 604 West 143rd Street, between Broadway and Riverside Drive. We are in the process of expanding our presence by introducing offices in all 5 boroughs of New York.
