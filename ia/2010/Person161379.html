Paul Santeler’s Summary

Seasoned Product Development executive with strong depth in Hardware and Software Engineering yet equally strong breadth from Marketing to Operations.

Direct contributor to programs accounting for $5B+ in revenue and 14 patents during 15-year Fortune 50 career with Compaq and Hewlett-Packard. Accustomed to P&L responsibility with budgets of up to $150M and organizations with 2000+ personnel.

Change agent with demonstrated talent for working outside the box to transform dysfunctional organizations, lead expansion into new markets, create new businesses and product lines, devise unique go-to-market approaches, and integrate all corporate functions into a unified mission.

Passionate leader with strong interpersonal skills at all management levels, who creates environments of mutual trust and respect, sets clear vision, and removes barriers to success

Some key career accomplishments include:

• Championing and creating the 8-processor Compaq ProLiant server product category that captured 60% market share and $1B in revenue within 18 months.

• Increasing the customer usage of HP Insight Manager from 34% to 70% and achieving a 4.5 out of 5 ranking in the Meta Group Management Tools category.

• Championing and creating the ProLiant Essentials high-margin software option business by leveraging the installed base of hardware and the “stickiness’ of the management console as an advertising and deployment vehicle resulting in over 250,000 licenses in first 18 months.

• Delivering high-visibility keynote presentations at major industry events and featuring in multiple promotional videos for Compaq Computer, Hewlett-Packard, Microsoft, Intel, and Novell.
