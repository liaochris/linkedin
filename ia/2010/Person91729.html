Jay Syzdek’s Summary

I founded One Blink Media out of my desire to truly help businesses reach their goals. I love watching a company grow because it benefits the families of everyone involved- and the feeling of being successful is a wonderfully contagious state of being.

We have a unique approach to Social Media for businesses to remove the work they don’t have time or the expertise to do. It's a lot of fun to keep up with such a fast-paced industry. It keeps us on our toes and constantly innovating!

Prior to starting One Blink Media, I started Jaymunda in 2004 and sold the company profitably in 2010. For 6 years it grew into more than just a web design business, we provided consulting, online marketing, and became a resource for many of the needs of our clients.

Before starting Jaymunda, I was the Marketing Director for Quo Productions in Philadelphia. Music has always been an instrumental part of my life, so being responsible for all of the marketing programs for a music recording studio was rewarding on both a personal and professional level.

When I am away from my computer I like to spend quality time with my wife and son. I enjoy playing jazz piano and love learning new things.
