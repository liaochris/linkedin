Eduardo Hauser’s Summary

Hauser turned to entrepreneurship after a career in senior management positions in the Internet, media and entertainment fields.

In 2005 Hauser founded DailyMe, a leader in the field of personal news discovery and distribution.

Before founding DailyMe, Hauser was AOL's Executive Vice President for Latin America. Prior to AOL, Hauser held various positions with the Cisneros Group including corporate Vice-president; Vice-president of News & Information for Venevisión; and Managing Director of the Cisneros Group's Corporate Office.

Hauser has a keen interest in media and technology. He serves on the Board of Directors of National Public Radio (NPR), the Journalism Board of the Knight Foundation and the Board of Visitors of Duke University's School of Law,

He holds a Law Degree (J.D.) from Caracas' UCAB (1991), and a Master's Degree in Law (Ll.M.) from Duke University (1993).
