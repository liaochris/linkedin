Ben Hwang’s Summary

Looking for more opportunities all the time. There's only so much time in the day to take an idea and mold it into something that the world would like to see. Been a technical person all my life, but decided that it was time for a change and approaching a new direction.

Professionally trained in telecommunications, programming, information technology and information research while having done as much civic work as time allows. One of my current fascinations is short-long term strategy planning, innovative design, and process optimization in order to control fiscal efficiency and profitability.

I am also a current contributor to 8Asians.com, Virtual to Reality, Insight Community, and other online blogs and publications. Also the Technical Advisor for both the esteemed Asian American literary journal, Kartika Review, and the fashion design firm, Taryn Zhang.
