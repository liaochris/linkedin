Chris Russell-Smith’s Summary

Chris has been working in media since 1996 when he landed a graduate job at YMG. Shortly after YMG was folded into TMD Carat, which then became Carat. During this time he planned and bought regional media - press, radio, out of home, leaflets, magazines etc for clients like Specsavers, Multiyork, British Gas, Arcadia, NTL, and LVMH.

In 2000, Chris went publisher side to work on Business Development at publisher Newsquest Media. In 2003, Chris moved internally to head up the marketing team. He stayed here until 2007, completing the CIM Postgraduate Diploma and gaining Chartered Marketer status along the way.

Chris now heads up Sales and Marketing at dealgroupmedia UK Ltd, one of the UK's leading online technology and services providers.

Chris is passionate about digital marketing, and helping online advertisers get the very best from their online investment.
