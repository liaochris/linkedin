Danielle Pelletier’s Summary

Danielle Pelletier has been working with international professionals for over ten years. International professionals working in well-known San Francisco Bay Area and Silicon Valley companies have improved their English language communication skills and developed confidence through one-on-one classes with Pelletier.

She has worked with professionals such as quality control managers, senior marketing analysts, software programmers, and senior accountants from bio-engineering, high-technology, and financial accounting companies.

Specializing in oral and written communication skills, one-on-one classes can be customized to meet the specific needs of each client. Presentation skills, meeting language skills, phone skills, email correspondence, technical reports, and pronunciation skills are a few of the popular skills that international professionals improve when they work with Pelletier.

If you or someone you know would like to increase confidence in using English with Americans professionally, academically or socially, feel free to contact Danielle Pelletier.
