Joe Calhoon’s Summary

Joe's clients consistently experience business growth, organizational transformation, and a higher quality of life.

Through keynote speaking, business growth services, and strategic consultation, Joe works to identify lasting solutions to the problems and opportunities organizations face.

For example, in one year WGK Engineers improved profits by 800 percent and Wilson Auctioneers doubled their revenues utilizing Joe’s proven business growth methodology.

As the most requested and highest rated keynote speaker with the Covey Leadership Center, Joe knows the importance of synergy, communication, and effectiveness. He brings these to every client experience. Joe has taken his message to four continents and continues to refine his approach through each client experience.

Joe is the author of two breakthrough books, Prioritize! and On the Same Page.
