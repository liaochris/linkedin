Stephen Williams -LION-’s Summary

Stephen W. Williams, has over 20 years of experience in operational, financial, and strategic management with international manufacturing firms. He developed a sales and marketing strategy for a Canadian manufacturer which increased sales volume by over 100% within a year of implementation. Mr. Williams later purchased the company and optimized the plant configuration to support JIT production. He secured international purchasing agreements, reduced inventory cash requirements by 50%, and reduced purchasing turnaround by 60%, and expense by 30%. Mr. Williams has established Joint Ventures in Australia, Belgium, Canada, France, and the U.K., and has negotiated relationships with GE, ABB, Reliance Electric, General Dynamics, Westinghouse Electric, and Eastern Electric Company. He is experienced in due diligence and M&A negotiations. His current work focuses on commercialization, operational and financial efficiency for small and middle market businesses.
