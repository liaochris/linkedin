Michael Lenczner’s Summary

Michael Lenczner is a Montreal-based entrepreneur and community organizer who works on public-interest technology projects. Michael co-founded Ile Sans Fil (a volunteer-run free wireless network with over 100,000 users) and CivicAccess.ca, serves on the board of several non-profits, and is a frequent partner in academic research projects. He consults in the areas of strategic development, partnership and online-collaboration strategies. He also speaks and writes about technology and citizen involvement.

He is currently preparing to launch a commercial web service.

Michael Lenczner’s Specialties:

developing and implementing web strategy, open source & open data strategy, community telecom, developing community-academic collaboration, online/offline team collaboration and knowledge sharing, project management
