Brian Callahan’s Summary

I represent individuals, families, sole proprietors and corporations in most aspects of their businesses and personal lives. The description of my firm is "General Practice" and that is the best explanation of what I and my firm do. We offer a wide range of legal and business consulting services providing our clients the opportunity to efficiently and economically overcome their legal hurdles. Some of the many legal services we provide are: real estate closings, wills, trusts, estates, probate, foreclosures, debtors & creditors rights, small and medium size business formations, contracts, employment, trademarks, copyrights, and all criminal & civil litigation. In addition to people and their businesses, we also help municipalities in the various aspects of public administration as well as the enforcement of their contracts and taxes.
