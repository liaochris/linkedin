Rodrigo Madriz’s Summary

10+ years of international experience in strategy, product and business development in the high technology sector across several industries (telecomunications, digital media, software, manufacturing & process automation) with large/tier-1 corporations and private-equity backed start ups.

Proven relationship builder at all levels (including the C-level and Board suites). B.Sc. (Mechanical Engineering, Class Rank 3rd out of 49), Master of Engineering Management (R&D Management track) from the Lockheed Martin Engineering Management Program at the University of Colorado at Boulder, M.B.A. (Strategy & Entrepreneurship) from York University’s Schulich School of Business. Fluent in English, Spanish and Italian. World Bank Graduate Fellowship Recipient. Canadian “Top 40 under 40” nominee.

  • Founder

    Textalive Media Technologies, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Consumer Electronics industry)

    October 2002 — October 2008 (6 years 1 month)

    Textalive started as an award winning MBA business plan intended to create a service to make digital text publications accessible to the blind (i.e. newspapers, books, magazines, etc.) as personalized audio podcasts using off the shelf text-to-speech synthesizers. Later evolved into audio/video codecs, DRM, watermarking and methodologies for monetizing content distribution.

    In late ’06 raised two rounds of angel funding, designed a platform-agnostic US patent pending methodology for direct-to-consumer and consumer-to-consumer (P2P) ad-supported distribution of content that guarantees the collection of royalty payments for content rightholders in a per-consumption-event basis, doubled the enterprise value in just 6 months, but realized that they key value prop was just too far ahead of market demands. The company provided consulting services for a number of European and North American clients including media, advertising, consumer electronic OEMs and telcos.

    www.textalive.com

  • Managing Partner - Americas

    Just Landed SL

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    April 2005 — January 2007 (1 year 10 months)

    Launched Justlanded.com in Canada.

    Justlanded.com is one of the most comprehensive & interactive expats/immigration guides for people wishing to live abroad and it is the world’s largest and fastest growing multilingual online destination for expatriates (30+ countries and counting). Visitors learn from the published guide, from advertisers and from each other about real state, housing, immigration, banking, taxes, health care, law, telecommunication services, transportation, education, culture, etc. The site is available in English, French, German, Italian, Dutch, Chinese, Spanish, etc.

    www.justlanded.com

  • Strategy & Innovation Advisor

    iMotions - Emotion Technology A/S

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    August 2004 — December 2006 (2 years 5 months)

    Having worked intensely with one of the co-founders for a number of years, I got involved with iMotions soon after it received its first round of financing as an innovation & strategy advisor. I worked with Peter Hartzbech & Jakob de Lemos to position iMotions value proposition in a vertical market where it would produce the biggest impact in terms of cash flows, valuation and PR in the shortest time. As a result, iMotions is today one of the fastest growing and globally focused companies in Denmark recognized locally and even by MIT experts. The company has filed for a series of US patents and it develops and produces emotion-sensitive software tools based on highly sophisticated facial analysis hardware and the latest neuroscience and cognitive psychology research. Leading applications include pre-tests of advertising campaigns, security, video-games self rating mechanisms, etc.

    www.imotions.dk
