Tara Mahady’s Summary

20-year nonprofit professional with substantial experience in development and marketing. Current focus is on social media. I've raised millions of dollars through sophisticated development programs at top organizations throughout New Hampshire and Vermont. I've worked with museums, arts and humanities organizations, grassroots lobbying organizations, and in public media. I took a brief detour into the for-profit world working for a fundraising software company as their Communications Director. This experience gave me a unique perspective on donor management software and how to use it effectively.
