Heather Raines’s Summary

October 2004-Present, Technisource (a division of Spherion), McLean Va.
Recruiting Manager
Spherion® provides integrated solutions to meet the evolving staffing needs and priorities of companies from administrative/clerical, light industrial and contact center talent to seasoned professionals in accounting and finance, technology, engineering and manufacturing, legal, sales and marketing and human resources.
-Technical Recruiter for one of the largest IT staffing offices within Spherion which generates nearly 40 million in revenue per year.
-Recruit for technical consultants on both a contractual and permanent basis.
-Full life cycle recruiting of candidates from searches within users groups, national job boards, and consultant referrals.
-Specialize in multiple financial and government accounts such as the International Monetary Fund, The World Bank, International Finance Corporation, The Board of Governors, and Fannie Mae.
-Currently have 50 consultants on board and am tracking 60 by fiscal year end 2007.
-Consistently place 4-5 new consultants on billing per month.
-Considered one of the branches top recruiters with the unique ability to multi-task and recruit on multiple skill sets at a time and for multiple companies. Skill sets include all technical from PC Technicians and Help Desk Analysts to Senior Software Engineers and Project Managers.
-Work with Account Executive closely and often help in the sales effort.
-Very interactive with all the clients and am given additional responsibilities from my Account Executive and Management.
