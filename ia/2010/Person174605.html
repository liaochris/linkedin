Robert Heaney’s Summary

I began working with Internet Technology in 1993, and I specialize in user interface design and development. I am currently focused on UI work for both web and mobile technologies, as part of a highly skilled team at kgb.

As a designer, I have advanced graphic design ability not normally found in developers, combining traditional with digital media - sketching web design concepts in charcoal and graphite before recreating on screen (Fireworks, Photoshop, Illustrator).

As a developer, I am highly skilled at optimizing semantic markup and using CSS to maximize accessibility and cross-browser compliance. I use current front-end technologies (XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, Web 2.0) across multiple development platforms (JSP/PHP/ASP/RoR). Also, I am a "hand-coder" writing all code via text editor without having to rely upon visual IDEs.

These skills, combined with an ability to translate between technical and business people, have allowed me to become a highly valued member of every team I’ve been on.
