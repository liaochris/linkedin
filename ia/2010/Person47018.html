Daniel Broche’s Summary

Discounteo is an e-commerce pure player. We sell high tech devices & home appliances in France & Belgium (fridges, washing machines, flat panel diplays, mp3 players...).

Discounteo is a profitable 4 years old company. Our efficient and optimized dropshipping model is very scalable and well suited to highly competitive environment. With a skilled team of 15 persons near Lyon (France) we realise an annual turnover more than 20M€ and manage a 300 000 active customers database.

Discounteo.com especially focuses on customer satisfaction and brand transparency. We are a low cost service provider but our services are reliable and relevant. We are using Internet to develop conversations between customers and our brand in order to achieve regular quality improvement in our processes.
