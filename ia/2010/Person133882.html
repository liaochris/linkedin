Mark Headd’s Summary

Information technology and its use by governments and not-for-profit organizations. The use of social media and VoIP technologies to improve the delivery of public services.

Mark Headd’s Specialties:

Over 10 years of experience working in a variety of capacities for different state and local governments.

Self taught in IVR and voice application development (VoiceXML, CCXML, etc.), web application development, computer systems administration and Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) technologies.

Strong background in research and policy development with an emphasis on technology, public finance, personal data privacy and security, and the development of social media.
