Kapil Gupta’s Summary

On May 9th, 2007, a group of passionate individuals got together and created OMLogic. We have all witnessed and experienced, first hand, the magic of Web as the great equalizer. All of the members of OMLogic team thrive when we work on planting seeds and turning them into seedlings – we enjoy building businesses. We also learned how to run businesses after going to school of hard knocks but are less than passionate about this phase – in our hearts we prefer to be serial entrepreneurs.

For us getting into the business of helping other businesses harness the power of the Web in growing their reach or taking on bigger competitors gives us the best of both worlds. We get to work on the part that is our passion, a win for us; and our clients get to realize the full potential of their business, a win for them. We get to play the role of serial entrepreneurs – virtually.
