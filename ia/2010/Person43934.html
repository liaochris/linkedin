Chris Haines’s Summary

I've been a licensed NY insurance agent and broker since 1980.

For the business client, I work on the more complex accounts and coverages for the agency. Municipalities, schools, and other public entities make up the majority of my business clients.

For the personal client, I like a more consultative approach than most agencies. Most insurance purchases by individuals are rushed by closing dates, car salesmen, and HR departments. I prefer to take the time to review all coverages (including auto, home, life, and long-term care) to make sure that all coverages are appropriate for the individual's situation.
