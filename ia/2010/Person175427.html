Robert Follis’s Summary

Founded in 1990, Robert Follis Associates (RFA) specialises in knowledge based PR for the consumer electronics industry. This means we know a lot about the products and technology we represent. We don't have to spin the press a line and we don't continually revert to the client when difficult questions are asked.

Our reach covers all print and e-media relevant to target markets

Clients include some of the industry's largest and most respected names and some of the smallest start-ups. The common thread is quality products and services.

RFA is the only specialist agency based in Central London and has fast access to all important publishing houses and all UK and Global transport links. Our central location is perfect for press entertaining and hosting client meetings.

RFA is also the only agency to have full AV demonstration facilities with an audiovisual room available to showcase clients' products. Members of the national & specialist press visit regularly for product briefings.

Services range from pure PR for large clients, to full marketing support for small ones. Some clients have been with RFA for over 20 years and most contracts are long-term. Short-term PR blasts can be discussed on an ad-hoc basis.

Robert Follis’s Specialties:

Total knowledge of the markets and technologies involved in the promotion of consumer electronics. Art and Design as applied to product development and advertising. Extensive knowledge of computing and the web.
