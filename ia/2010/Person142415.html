Michael Carter’s Summary

I am an intellectual property professional with over 7 years of in-house and law firm experience. I am registered to practice before the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) and hold a degree in Electrical Engineering. I have managed and conducted all ranges of patent services, such as patent drafting, patent prosecution including domestic, PCT, and U.S. national stage filings, patentability searches, patent drawings, and patentability analysis. I have also assisted inventors and corporations in the development of functioning inventions.

Michael Carter’s Specialties:

Patent Drafting and Patent Prosecution, Intellectual Property Law, National Stage filings of Foreign Patent Applications, Intellectual Property Portfolio Management (Domestic and International), Patent Searching and Drafting, Understanding of PCT, EPC, as well as patent laws of China, Japan, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, South Korea and Taiwan
