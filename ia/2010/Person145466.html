Mike Heah’s Summary

Dr Michael Heah (mikeheah@corporate-coach.com) is the CEO of Corporate Coach Academy and Corporate Coach and an Adjunct Professor for University Utara, Malaysia. He is the Founder and the first President of the International Coach Federation, Malaysia Chapter. A well known figure in Coaching, he is a columnist and writer in Malaysia’s leading media.

He is an ICF Credentialed Professional Certified Coach, the first in Malaysia and the very few in Asia to have this world class coaching qualification. He is also a Licensed Coach-Trainer from an internationally-recognized coach institution.

Academically, he is qualified with a Doctorate and Masters in Hospitality & Tourism, a Degree in Business Administration and a Postgraduate Diploma in Organizational Psychology from various universities in UK. Michael’s contribution to Coaching in Malaysia and in this part of the world is tremendous. Not only has he created a high Coaching presence in this region, but has also helped thousands of individuals, organizations, including universities to attain greater success. He actively coaches and certifies aspiring coaches to become professional and workplace coaches.

Michael is also well known internationally. He is an active member of a number of international coaching networks and he is perhaps, the first and only Malaysian (probably in Asia) to have coaching clients in many parts of the world, notably Europe, North America, Canada and Australia.

He is well-backed up with more than 30 years of hands-on corporate exposure in a range of portfolios covering many industries, from a shop floor worker to senior management positions.
