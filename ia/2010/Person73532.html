Gayle Rodgers’s Summary

From a junior high teacher to stay-at-home Mom to owner of a manufacturing company for 20 years, and now coach and speaker, Gayle's life has been fun, hectic, and varied. As a certified Dream Coach (R) Group/Spiritual Leader and Trained True Purpose Coach, she is working to put more joy and fulfillment back into peoples lives. Gayle loves to work with teens to give them more direction and teach them skills that will help them in the future especially on decision making.

Some topics Gayle covers in speeches are finding your passion and purpose; goal setting; how to reduce stress and stay healthy; rediscover the child within; and how to lead a more joyful life. She loves to work with teens and puts on classes about making choices, what it takes to be a friend, confidence building, and being an entrepreneur. She loves to teach teens her "LOVE" decision making model. Gayle helps people "Live Life to the Fullest."

She is helping individuals and businesses stay connected and save money using SendOutCards. She helps her friends look, feel, and perform better everyday with her Univera LifeSciences business. Both of these businesses are a way to create wealth on a part-time basis or full-time.

Gayle Rodgers’s Specialties:

Gayle teaches her Decision Making Model called "L.O.V.E. to Choose" to teens. Her DreamBook is a process she teaches teens and adults on how to live the life of your dreams. She takes adults who feel "stuck" and are ready for a change and guides them to a place of joy and fulfillment. Motivational speaker/trainer on topics relating to decision making, finding your passion and living your dream; keeping a positive attitude; and how to live a passionate life, etc.
