Bob Nikolajczyk’s Summary

~ “Hands-on” ADVISOR ~ SENIOR EXECUTIVE LEADER

Senior Executive with 30 years of the know-how, skills and subject matter expertise in Technology and Services companies. Lead business development strategies plus tactic execution across multiple business units and cross functional areas.

Identify business strategies to drive revenues, profits and an operational environment that is reliable, repeatable, and scalable.

Working directly with owners, founders and CEOs, I work as a “hands-on” Advisor and become an extension of their staff to help them get to the “next level”. Each engagement brings its own set of circumstances that define the “next level”.

Clientele value the fact that my level of expertise can be part of their TEAM within a part-time and temporary environment. They are getting the expertise and the results they need without the risks associated a new executive hire.

Engagement examples include; better ‘focus’ on building the business, leveraging past successes to grow existing and new verticals, develop new marketing strategies, business development, operational & organizational effectiveness and leading the proper preparation and interaction with the investment community. Plus some of the quantifiable results;

• Product / service delivery cycle time reduced 75% and 85% in two examples while customer satisfaction improved significantly.

• Product revenue pipeline increased 100% while reducing marketing costs by 25% in six short months which helped generate record level of new business revenue that more than tripled the previous four years.

• Reengineered customer services activities when it became clear that achieving measurable goals were not related to customer satisfaction. Redefined customer and corporate expectations, metrics, and performance criteria. Retained 100% of current customers, reduced staff by 30%, and improved customer service and exceeded customers’ expectations.
