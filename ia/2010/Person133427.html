Mark Carbone’s Summary

Mark is a business professional excelling in a variety of disciplines from sales and business development to management and business strategy, with strong business skills developed and applied in multinational corporations to several start-ups. He has been responsible for growing revenues in excess of $30 million, building and managing a sales force as well as developing business strategy for start-up companies. Mark is entrepreneurial minded with a focus on clarifying both opportunities and issues then developing strategies to maximize opportunities and minimize issues. He is an expert at separating the noise from the core concern resulting in focus and efficiency in dealing with multiple issues.
