Charlie O'Flaherty’s Summary

Charlie O'Flaherty is a proven leader with a track record for identifying new markets and business opportunities and then recruiting and leading top executive teams to execute category-dominating business plans.
His nearly 20 years of experience in Asia, UK & the US include building and running HSBC Securities USA (APAC); founding AsiAlliance, Asia’s first financial technology VC incubator, and selling it to Dublin-based Digital Channel Partners; purchasing Lonrho’s Index Air Cargo and repositioning the airline as the IFRC’s primary East African logistics partner; and restructuring ABN AMRO’s US equity derivatives business including bringing both manufacturing and distribution in-house to produce sales of over $2 billion in year one.

As a hands-on operator, Mr. O’Flaherty is Co-Founder and CEO of EverythingTree.com, where he enjoys building the company from a disruptive startup to the category-defining solution for broadcast shopping and lead capture.

Bank, broker-dealer and NBFI formation, operations and restructuring.
