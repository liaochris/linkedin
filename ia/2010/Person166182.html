Ryan Sawchuk’s Summary

Graduate of Princeton University

While still in college I co-founded Regional ISP, Dream Communications in 1996. Located in Boston Massachusetts and Serving Southern New England and New Hampshire Dreamcom was sold in 2000. Joined Amazon.com's product management team in 2000 and launched pre-order functionality for DVD/Video business and later joined Amazon's email team where I was responsible for the development and execution of over 300 email campaigns. Next I joined X10 Wireless where I was lead analyst for one of the most successful Direct Marketers in the history of the Internet -in 2002 X10 was 6th most visited Website in US. Moved to California where I helped LowerMyBills.com grow into one of the largest Internet Advertisers while also launching BillPay Plus, an online bill payment and presentment service from LowerMyBills.com. Left LowerMyBills.com it was sold to Experian for $350 million.

After LMB I went to PayPal & eBay where I managed the Marketing Program for PayPal's Consumer Credit Card, Business Debit Card, Online Bill Payment service as well as PayPal's industry leading Money Market Fund. Additionally helped develop strategy for PayPal Buyer Credit, a private label capital program focused on both on and off eBay transactions.

After PayPal I joined Orbitz, where I managed six person product marketing team responsible for product experience and travel booking engine. Personally led several multi-million dollar projects including implementation of a CRM platform, website analytics solution from Webtrends and new email service provider. Left Orbitz after $4B acquisition by the Blackstone group.

Currently Head of Digital Marketing & Social Media Strategy for Flip Video & Cisco Consumer Products, managing a team responsible for Web Analytics, SEO/SEM, Email, Affiliate, Online Banner and Social Media marketing Flip Video was acquired by Cisco Systems for $600MM in 2009. Check out www.theflip.com.

Can be contacted @ rwsawchuk@yahoo.com
