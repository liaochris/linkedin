Stefania Savluc’s Summary

I am a highly creative, skilled and open-minded professional with extensive experience in fashion and apparel retail.

I live the concept of fashion. I'm not speaking at conferences nor writing books - I'm executing it.

I worked in a variety of fields – starting with law and social field and finishing with retail, apparel and image consulting. I’m a loving wife and a mother of two beautiful kids. I believe my real expertise has come from overcoming many obstacles, dealing with a variety of people and experiencing many changes and places in my life.

I think fashion should make people happy and should emphases their qualities, help them to shine, accentuate their assets and hide their flaws. I believe in harmony, equilibrium, and the fact that esthetics and beauty is not dependent on time, trends, gender or age.

How we dress, speak, behave, eat, walk or talk on an ordinary day or at a special occasion and finally how we see the world, are all important for how we are seen by the rest of the world and will define their behavior in relation to us. This is our brand and we are judged by it. Be it an individual or an organization.
And here I can help. I can make you shine.
