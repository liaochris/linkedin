My goal for 2009-2010 and beyond:

Bravo'sWorld

A new online children's social network- where books books become games, games become a world of discovery and kids rule the world!

Designed and built by the world's leading online game developers, Bravo'sWorld is based on contemporary characters featured in hilarious stories with wild wordplay, brilliant graphics and animation. Offering kids of all ages countless multi-player and mini-games, quests, adventures, contests and more, Bravo'sWorld will touch kids' lives both online and off.

Bravo'sWorld is created for a generation that is smarter than we think...and deserves much more than "virtual pets."
