Nicola Hopkinson’s Summary

Virtuality should be on the agenda of any responsible modern business in the light of the climate change debate. There are compelling reasons, both economically and environmentally, to deploy web technology to reduce business impact on the planet for future generations and more and more businesses are embracing the concept of virtual working arrangements.

Virtual PAs will play a part in the modern business world alongside virtual banking and finance, virtual logistics, Virtual HR etc. The use of Virtual Pas can be a flexible and cost effective solution to a business’ needs.
