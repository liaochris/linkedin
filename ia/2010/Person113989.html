Kelly Hundley’s Summary

As a counselor, litigator, and trial lawyer, I work toward resolving disputes. If that means a trial, I am prepared and ready. If it means recommending a course short of a trial, I candidly tell my clients. I also help my clients lay a foundation to minimize, if not avoid, disputes.

I cut my teeth more than 20 years ago at a general litigation law firm. It was not unusual to have a matter hit your desk on a Monday and be tried in court on Tuesday. Crossing my desk were probate matters, landlord/tenant disputes, divorces, and collections cases, to name but a few.

That evolved into handling mainly commercial matters. I provided advice and representation to businesses; negotiated lease agreements and vendor contracts; represented developers in funding disputes; and defended retailers against property damage claims.

Employers -- public and private -- increasingly called for day-to-day HR advice and guidance to negotiate the alphabet soup of employment law -- ADA, FMLA, FLSA, Title VII, ADEA, NLRA, NLRB, OFCCP. That led to defending employers in administrative (e.g., EEOC and DOJ) and judicial settings.

I've appeared in state and federal courts across the country defending against such claims as sex/race/age/national origin/religious/disability discrimination and harassment. Understanding the law, I provide employers guidance to reduce the risk of disputes with employees. I prepare policies and procedures for employers and provide training and guidance on the law and implementing policies and procedures.

This continues at Henderson & Hundley. My goal is to expand our base in these service areas, particularly for businesses tired of paying the large fees to big firms. High quality guidance and representation does not have to come with exorbitant price tags.

Kelly Hundley’s Specialties:

My practice emphasizes my role as a counselor to and defender of public and private employers in disputes with employees. My desire is to help employers analyze and respond to disputes successfully and efficiently and to head off disputes before they occur.
