Laurie Miller’s Summary

Assist those in need (seniors, those with illness and/or disabilities) with everyday activities to allow those people to remain independent and in their own home for as long as possible. Apple Care can help people on an hourly or live in basis. We can work on a short term (assistance with cancer treatments) or long term basis.
Our experienced caregivers can help with light housekeeping, meal prep, meds reminders, errands, shopping, doctor appointments, personal care and companionship.
We specialize in eldercare but can assist those of any age, as well.
We strive to help the adult children, those in the boomer generation, enjoy the relationship with their parents in a stress free environment.
