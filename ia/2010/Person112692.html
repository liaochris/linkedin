  • Manager / Buyer / Marketing

    Nolita Inc.

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    May 2004 — July 2009 (5 years 3 months)

    As the buyer and manager for Nolita Inc., Portland, for five years, I orchestrated several promotions with area non-profits including the Nolita Denim Swap for Big Brothers Big Sisters, and the Nolita Bottles Butts and Boots Fundraiser with Widmer Brewing Company.

    Additionally, I was one of two founding partners of Portland's well-known PDX Collective Sales at the Ace Hotel twice a year.

    I designed over 150 targeted email newsletters for Nolita, including new arrival and sales announcements. I initiated a store blog in February 2008, which received over 2000 hits a month, effectively raising Nolita to the very top of search engines like Google and Yahoo.

    In early 2009, I designed a Facebook page for Nolita. The product updates I posted helped grow our Fan Page to 200+ and Friends of Nolita to 425+. Additionally, the store Twitter account had 150+ followers and our updates were re-tweeted regularly.

    Other job functions:
    Executed weekly sales floor merchandising plans in four departments and designed eye-catching window displays.

    Set budgets, financial goals, and pricing policies to maximize revenue.

    Compiled detailed sell-through reports and processed fill-in orders using Quick Books and Microsoft Excel.

    Managed all aspects of marketing and promotions including store website, blog, social media, email newsletters, printed collateral, and events. Garnered key media mentions from GQ and Lucky in addition to other trade magazines.

    Coordinated merchandise buys from forty denim and clothing and accessory designers including Seven for all Mankind, True Religion, Vince, Helmut Lang and John Varvatos for men and women.
