Brittanie Stuber’s Summary

While it's easy to say I enjoy working with people the truth of the matter is, I really do love meeting, greeting, talking and learning from those around me. I know this passion grew the day I walked into my first job as a Sales Associate, assisting customers with their decorating dilemmas and finding comfort in the warmth of a satisfied smile. I altered my customer service role and began brewing coffee during college. I occupied my time away from class interning and volunteering as a Guardian ad Litem for Court Appointed Special Advocates (CASA) and as a Youth Programs and Office Volunteer at the International Rescue Committee (IRC). After graduating from Boise State University, I moved to Denver, CO, and currently am the Administrative Assistant at Faction Media, an innovative digital marketing agency.

Whether I am taking an order, editing a proposal, greeting a client, or offering my optimistic attitude during a volunteer event, I enjoy learning and growing from every experience and opportunity.

What the future holds for my eager feet has yet to be determined, but I do know within the next five years, I want to make way into the non-profit sector, focusing on gender-based violence programs for women and youth refugees. I believe in the beauty of education and want to live, know and work in a world where educational opportunities and freedom from oppression/violence are no longer a distant dream, but instead a reality.

Brittanie Stuber’s Specialties:

Women's rights and educational programs, a creative mindset, project development, fundraising/event planning, case management, office manager and customer service.
