Cheryl Bame’s Summary

Former broadcast journalist and experienced public relations professional in Southern California.

I do public relations for lawyers and law firms, professional services firms and the commercial and residential real estate industries.

I am a news junkie and counsel clients on how to use traditional and social media to generate exposure and promote their brands.

Experience in public relations, media relations, social media and networking and media training, writing, development of communications strategies, community relations, and crisis communications.
