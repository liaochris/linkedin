Tsolig "Sunny" Shahinian, MS, RD’s Summary

You can reach me at sunny.shahinian@yahoo.com

Dedicated professional with a strong belief in taking a proactive and preventative approach to health through client education, disease prevention, and health and wellness promotion. Demonstrated expertise developing innovative programs streamlining the patient care process in both hospital and long-term health care facilities. Recognized to work effectively with patients and clients and analyze their progress, helping them achieve better health.

Experienced Registered Dietitian with 3+ years working in both hospital (pulmonary, cardiac), long-term health care facilities (rehabilitation, sub-acute, MDS), and wellness and health promotion. Skills include: nutrition assessment, diet education, weight monitoring, monthly performance improvement, and managed/mentored dietetic students who worked on various projects.

I am a resource to companies who are interested in lowering their cost of health insurance. I do this by helping both clients and patients achieve better health by managing a healthy weight, lowering elevated cholesterol and high blood pressure.

I am open to different areas including wellness, health promotion, weight management, public health (dept of health), non-profit, community programs, outpatient / ambulatory clinics, insurance, corporations, supermarket chains, restaurants, food industry, education, and school districts.

Even though my background is in healthcare, I am not seeking dietitian positions in hospitals and long-term care facilities (nursing homes and rehabilitation) except for outpatient, ambulatory, and clinic settings.

Interested in wellness and health promotion. Helping clients reach optimal weight and health.
