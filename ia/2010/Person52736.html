David Benjamin’s Summary

Spending the past eight years recruiting for Executive Search & Consulting firms has taught me best practices in sourcing, onboarding, and employee retention. Working with Fortune 500 companies, startups, and everything in between has given me the unique perspective on a variety of recruiting processes- do's and don'ts!

Today, companies are running leaner and expecting more from their salesforce. The best companies, those that are leaders in their industry and which all others are judged by did not achieve their success by chance.

Could your organization use another talented professional that will positively impact company revenue? If you can't find the best talent through your resources, feel free to contact me. The best companies are comprised of the best talent.
