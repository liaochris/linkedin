Joel N. Sussman’s Summary

I'm a communications professional with experience in both traditional marketing strategies and New Media marketing techniques, and have held positions as Associate Director of Public Relations for a statewide legislative lobbying organization, Communications Director of a regional small business association, Proposal Writer for an international I-T consulting firm, and News Director of a rural radio station. My freelance experience has included writing articles for Albany-area business journals and script writing for a video production company in Buffalo. For the past three years, I've been a search engine optimization (SEO) specialist for a Saratoga-based ecommerce website solutions company.
