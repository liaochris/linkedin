David Cancel’s Summary

I am the CEO & founder of Performable.

Most recently I was the co-founder & CTO of Lookery. Before starting Lookery I started Compete where I was the CTO; acquired by Taylor Nelson Sofres / WPP [LON:WPP]. Before Compete I was the CTO of BuyerZone; acquired by Reed Elsevier [NYSE: RUK]. In the late 90’s I was a part of the founding team of Bolt.com & part of Lycos.

I’m originally from New York City and now live in the Boston, MA area with my superstar wife and daughter.

I sit on the advisory boards of Visible Measures, Sonian Networks, MyPerfectGig, and Shareaholic.

My "nights and weekends" projects include:

1. Ghostery - http://www.ghostery.com/ (Acquired by Better Advertising)
2. Bzzster (Acquired by Shareaholic)
