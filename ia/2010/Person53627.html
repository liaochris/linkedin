Deborah K. Gauthier’s Summary

My background includes all aspects of textile and print design from placement and all over printing on wovens and knits, domestic and international mill direction, pigment and wet printing, burnout and embroidery design. I have traveled extensively through out Europe, the Orient, and the states for trend research and trade show attendance. I would definitely consider “trending” a major strength.

In the interest of managing designers, I am very through and organized as well as a very diplomatic communicator. I have managed both in house as well as freelance designers on an as need basis to get the projects completed on a timely basis. I fully understand the tight time frames that projects need to be completed for markets and do everything possible to make “It” happen.

I truly love overseeing the design and printing as well as managing the mill with color corrections and finishing techniques; including keeping thorough track of all solids, lab dips, and seasonal color palettes. I make sure that the artists in my studio keep clear color records for future all over prints and solid lab dips.
