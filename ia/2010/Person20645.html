Patrick Baynes’s Summary

My internet career began at LinkedIn pioneering customer operations. At the time, social media was just taking off. Now we can all agree, it's definitely here to stay.

Freesource was founded by former LinkedIn employees and includes a team of seasoned consultants from Yankee group, and Deloitte & Touche. We're the connection between what you wish social media would do for your business, and what it can do. Our services range from strategy consulting and general leg-work to sales training and corporate policy development.

Today I go one-on-one with each client and hold myself accountable for the success of their program; so far I'm doing pretty well. When I'm not working directly with Freesource clients, I also manage www.freesourcing.org - the world's directory of truly free business resources, and add value on twitter @freesourcing.

You're welcome to contact me at pbaynes@freesourceagency.com.

- Patrick
