Tom Rice’s Summary

Mr. Rice is a founding member of the firm and has served as a member of the firm's Executive Committee. Litigation has been his primary focus for the last twenty-five years. His philosophy is simple - obtain favorable results for clients as quickly as possible at a reasonable cost.

He has tried cases in numerous states for various Fortune 500 companies. He has experience coordinating national, regional and industry-wide litigation.

At Baker Sterchi Cowden & Rice, litigation encompasses comprehensive skills from negotiating a settlement when settlement offers the best solution, to Alternative Dispute Resolution when it is most appropriate, to well-prepared, articulate defenses in the courtroom. The decision regarding which approach to take is made with the client, through an early evaluation of each case during which we calculate cost-effectiveness, possible duration, and the opponent's strengths and weaknesses. Regardless, each case is pursued with the same dedication as the pursuit of trial.

Centrally located in Missouri and Kansas, we practice throughout the United States serving national, regional and local companies and professionals who receive the added advantage of economical Midwestern fee structures. Our practice covers most types of litigation, except domestic and criminal cases. We have exceptional strength in product liability, medical malpractice, commercial, aviation, toxic tort, environmental, construction, fidelity and surety, premises liability, architects' and engineers' malpractice, insurance coverage, and employment law. With approximately 50 dedicated litigators and a substantial staff of litigation paralegals, our size and the types of cases we handle rival much larger firms with a litigation "department."

Tom Rice’s Specialties:

Mr. Rice's civil litigation practice emphasizes toxic torts, product liability and employment litigation. Mr. Rice's practice also includes drug and medical device litigation, insurance coverage disputes, environmental issues, intellectual property and commercial litigation. In addition, he provides consultation for and development of electronic case management, discovery compliance and litigation support systems.
