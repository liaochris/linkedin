Kevin Fox’s Summary

I have been a management consultant for most of the past 18 years with a break for 4 years as VP of manufacturing for a division of MAG Industrial Automation. I work primarily with executive management to redefine their strategies and operations with the objective of improving profitability and their ability to compete in the global economy. Industries that I have either been employed, consulted with or both include; aerospace (OEM and Tier 1), capital equipment (OEM), materials processing, food production, contract manufacturing, high-technology (OEM and Tier 1), automotive (OEM and Tier 1) and Bio-Technology (OEM and Tier 1).

At present my clients are redefining their organizations using lean manufacturing strategies and practical implementations.

We take particular pride in our ability to work within the board room through to the shop floor. Our "roll up the shirt sleeves" mentality is a key in allowing our clients to quickly adopt change and see results.

Kevin Fox’s Specialties:

Strategic analysis, lean manufacturing, systems analysis, manufacturing education.
