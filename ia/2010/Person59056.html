Douglas Park’s Summary

Doug helps executives solve business and legal problems.

He applies deep knowledge of strategy, law, and organizational management to tackle complex challenges. Doug focuses on strategy, corporate and securities law, corporate governance, and intellectual property.

An award winning business professor, Doug also provides executive education for business leaders, directors, and attorneys. Examples of topics covered include:

Strategy Formulation and Execution
Merger and Acquisition Strategy
Strengthening Your Company's Board
Leading Innovation
Optimizing Business and Legal Risk

Doug also leads two short courses for busy professionals:

MBA for Attorneys
Business Law for Executives
