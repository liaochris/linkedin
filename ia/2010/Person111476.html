Kate Buck’s Summary

Prior to creating KBJ Online in January 2009, Kate Buck spent 3 years with Landmark Education, a global leader in training and development. As the Community Manager for LandmarkConnect, Kate was responsible for growing (from launch!), maintaining (tech support, customer service) and enriching (track statistics, research trends, propose enhancements) the online community of 30,000+ global members. Coordinating designers, developers, writers, consultants and executives, she launched a full-scale social network for this community on the Ning Platform.

Kate is passionate about helping individuals and businesses get started marketing themselves online. A student herself, Kate is facinated with the technology that connects people of like minds and interests around the world and strives to keep abreast of current thought and trends. Her mission is to bridge the gap for those that are apprehensive or overwhelmed by all the "hype". Her approach makes social marketing manageable, affordable and effective for her clients.

Since launching KBJ Online, Kate has consulted for dozens of individuals and businesses around the country. Most days, you will find Kate tweeting (@katebuckjr) and creating hashtags (one of her most favorite things! #DUH). She frequently live tweets/blogs events she attends (What? You don't remember the Adventures of #KOZi from SXSWi 2009?), as well as lead strategic social marketing campaigns for the independent films Let Him Be and EXIT (which was runner up this year for Best Film in Austin's 48 hour Film Project!).

Kate lives in Austin, Texas (the Social Media Capitol of the World!) with her husband, Chef Morris Buck, and her 2 step-kids. She enjoys watching sports, dancing (really, like ballet) and spending time out on the lake.
