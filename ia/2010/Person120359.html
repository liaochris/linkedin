Kyle McShane’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur and current M.S. student at the School of Information Studies at Syracuse University. I am using LinkedIn to connect with people that could offer me advice and help me improve my business, DreamFetcher.

While looking for a job myself, I realized many inefficiencies with the online employment searching process. After researching the matter further, my friends and I realized recruiters were facing difficulties with this process as well. We decided that something needed to change to make the job search and employee acquisition processes easier.

So, my friends and I decided to found DreamFetcher. DreamFetcher is a matching and connection website for job seekers and recruiters. Think of DreamFetcher as the e-Harmony of the job search.

Gone will be the days of recruiters vetting through tall stacks of resumes. Recruiters will only receive qualified applicants on DreamFetcher.

I am seeking advice from anyone who would like to help build DreamFetcher into the most efficient job website on the Internet. Any human resource professionals, entrepreneurs, and anyone who has any advice that could improve DreamFetcher, I would appreciate the chance to speak with you.
