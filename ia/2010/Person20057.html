Jim Cosgrove’s Summary

Jim Cosgrove is an energetic, creative thinker with solid logical and analytical problem solving skills and a successful background in sales and marketing, and the creative and performing arts. He has excellent interpersonal skills and a very strong technical understanding of programming environments and data management.

This diverse skill set allows him to easily combine technical, business, marketing and creative environments for organizations wishing to increase communication and visibility in an ever more technical marketplace.

Along with with these skills, he has assembled unique group of business-minded and creative professionals called Barefoot Media Group who are experts in the fields of programming, website and graphic design, traditional and internet advertising, electronic and broadcast media production, media negotiation and placement, copy editing, and audio/video processing.

Barefoot Media Group is located in Marion, Arkansas, in the Memphis, Tennessee metro. Jim is secretary of the Marion, Arkansas Rotary Club, serves on the board of directors of the Marion Chamber of Commerce, is Vice President of the board of EastArk Enterprises (The Sheltered Workshop) in West Memphis, Vice President of the Catholic Association of Music, is the founder and president of the Crittenden County Community Band and the music director of St. Michael's Catholic Church in West Memphis, Arkansas.

Barefoot Media Group is a member of the Marion, Arkansas Chamber of Commerce, the West Memphis, Arkansas Chamber of Commerce, and the Sales & Marketing Society of the Mid-South. Barefoot supports the work of the 8th Street Mission for Jesus Christ.

Cosgrove is an avid collector of antique church hymnals and musical instruments, and loves the outdoors.
