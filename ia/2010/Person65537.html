Erica Lockwood’s Summary

Since joining the Joseph Chris Partners Family of Companies in 2000, Erica has built a rich global network of contacts in the residential and commercial construction, engineering and real estate industries. As she continues to retain her clients in the residential and commercial construction arena, Erica made the conscious decision to combine her passion for renewable energy with the opportunity to bring top professionals with transferable skills and experience into the renewable energy sector. With over 17 years in Professional and Executive Recruitment knowledge, Erica has established credibility and built trust with countless clients by effectively attracting and identifying top industry talent. As a nationally recognized executive search professional, Erica has successfully placed candidates at the COO, President, VP and Director levels within all major skill sets. She has been a consistent award-winning top producer with stellar career achievements.

Erica attributes her success to a continual commitment to integrity, customer service, and professional ethics.
