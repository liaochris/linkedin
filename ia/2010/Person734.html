Aaron Auxier’s Summary

Aaron Auxier – “Hollywood’s Connection to Vegas”®.


With vision, Celebrity Real Estate Agent Aaron Auxier breaks the mold by practicing what he calls "Realtainment" - the combination of luxury real estate and a loving approach to life, leadership, and people.

During his career, with gratitude, Aaron has befriended some of the world's most respected dignitaries, comedians, CEOs, athletes, actors/actresses, and inspirational leaders.

In addition to protecting the best interests of clients when buying/selling extraordinary properties in the Las Vegas arena, Aaron acts as the private consultant to several billionaire high-rise condominium and casino/hotel developers. Aaron's life is dedicated to helping, empowering, and teaching others.


“In the world of sports agents, Leigh Steinberg is one of the country’s most famous, serving as the inspiration for Tom Cruise’s character in Jerry Maguire. But when they make a movie based on the real-estate equivalent of Steinberg, they just might look to Aaron Auxier.”

VEGAS Magazine


Aaron has been featured in/on NBC News, Entertainment Tonight, Wall Street Journal, ABC News, Real TV, New York Times, VEGAS Magazine, Wealth TV, FOX News, CNBC, The Insider, Travel Channel, Los Angeles Times, 944, CBS News, and Fortune Magazine.

Aaron's areas of expertise include high-rise condo projects and guard-gated golf course communities such as The Ridges, Red Rock Country Club, Green Valley, Palms Place, CityCenter, Southern Highlands, Sky Las Vegas, Roma Hills, Veer Towers, Mandarin Oriental, Panorama Towers, Seven Hills, and Summerlin.

While lighting up the new world, as a focused business leader on a mission to challenge the status quo, Aaron Auxier appoints his people with knowledge, experience, and power.
