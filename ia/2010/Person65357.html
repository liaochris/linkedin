Emanuel Carpenter’s Summary

Visit Emanuel at www.cold-call-selling.com.

Emanuel Carpenter is the author of three books. He is currently submitting proposals for his latest "Six-Figure Cold Calling." It is written from the unique perspective of a former gatekeeper and active cold caller who still makes up to 80 dials per day. He also runs the LinkedIn Group called Six-Figure Cold Callers.

Emanuel's work has been published by Little, Brown and Company, Forest Wade Press, The Midwest Book Review, Salesgravy.com, and AALBC.com.

Emanuel's experience includes operating and managing a lead generation company, account management for a software company, and several years of selling to the high tech industry and logistics world. He has provided lead generation services for some of the best known brands in the world, including SAP, Salesforce.com, and Freightliner.

Carpenter is a cum laude graduate of Capital University and veteran of the US Air Force. He lives in the Cleveland area with his family.
