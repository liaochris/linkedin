Allison Erdeljac’s Summary

I am a Creative Director with15 years of professional design experience in North America and Europe. My experience in multiple disciplines includes Corporate Communications, Branding, Identity, Environmental Graphics,Packaging, Multimedia, Annual Reports, and Print Literature.

I studied at The Ontario College of Art & Design and graduated top of my class with a degree in Environmental Design. I began my career as an Environmental Designer, but quickly moved into the world of Graphic Design. I worked in Toronto for 10 years, starting as the head of the in-house Graphic Design Department for a prestigious Architect firm, moving quickly into the editorial world as the Art Director for the marketing department at Macleans Magazine. Just before leaving Toronto for Italy in 2004, I was working for Maclaren Momentum (part of McCann Erickson) as their Senior Creative in their 2D Department.

In Milan, Italy, I worked in the fashion industry for a design agency as their Art Director, working for clients such as Vanity Fair, Vogue, Italia, GQ and Mariella Burani.

In London, England I worked as a Creative Director for various agencies whose clients included, Motorola, Shell, 3GSM, Thomson Reuters, Royal Bank of Scotland, IBM, HSBC and Cisco Systems.
