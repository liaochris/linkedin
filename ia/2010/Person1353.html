Amarendra Bhushan Dhiraj’s Summary

Amarendra Bhushan Dhiraj is a journalist, syndicated columnist, technology consultant and serial entrepreneur. He is most widely known as founder of the CEOWORLD Magazine and Lucentbyte Media Technologies. He currently lives in Athens, Greece.

He is the Chief Executive Officer and Editor of CEOWORLD Magazine, the only magazine written strictly for CEOs and their peers. He is also serving as CEO of Lucentbyte Media Technologies, a leading business management, information technology consulting and outsourcing firm in the world.

He is on the boards of Journalist Association of Europe (JAE), the Association to benefit media professionals and advocates on behalf of journalists worldwide.

Amarendra is a life-long geek who loves to write about business, social media and technology. He is always testing web services, software and hardware that figure into an online lifestyle. You can catch up with him on Twitter @ceoworld
