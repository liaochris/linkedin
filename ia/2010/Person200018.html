Tata Peryoga’s Summary

Fifteen years experiences in geotechnical, civil and mining engineering consultancy, lecturer, education, research, training, quality assurances and quality control assessment, contractual assessment, materials engineering and laboratory testing in Kalimantan, Sumatera, Java and Papua. Has been involved with training, design, supervision, evaluation, testing, investigation, analysis, assessment, project management and construction of geotechnical and civil projects such as road, bridge, airstrip, embankment, dam, levee, infra structures facilities, earthworks, slope stability analysis and foundation materials. Also having an interest in safety standards development and any regional and international issues
