Kari Elam’s Summary

I am a recent graduate of American University’s School of Communication: Class of 2009. Well-versed in media, I have hands-on experience from strategy building, to execution, and content creation.

As a project manager and VaynerMedia I consult and build strategies for companies to best enhance their digital presence. I work with clients to amplify their voice and engage with their audience.

As a Media Planner I have built media buying strategies for clients that provide optimum results in the most cost-effective manner, and benefit both the advertiser/purchaser and the media provider. As a Social Media Strategist I also as create custom social media strategies that expand brands into the digital space, and balance traditional promotion with direct interaction.

I have a collection of work experience spanning social media, public affairs, television production, government, global communication, public relations, campaign building, strategic communication, advertising, writing, media buying, pop culture/current events, grassroots/non-profit, children’s retail, and the arts.

The insight I’ve gained from the breadth of work between each position was fundamental to my integrated approach to communications and work.

I am dedicated to building integrated media campaigns, fully utilizing advertising and social media. My strongest passions are culture and society. My best work comes when the focus is on people, the impact and engagement — the motives, behaviors, trends, and effects. Everyone has a story, and the beautiful thing about Communications its ability to give everyone a voice and a soapbox.
