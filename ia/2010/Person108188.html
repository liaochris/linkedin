Julian Wilkins’s Summary

Trained as an Architect, Qualifying in 1972 in the UK, I worked for a design and construct firm in the UK ( now AMEC) and from 1980 I specialised in Pharmaceutical facilities and from 1990 in the protection of Product, Patients, People and the Environment from highly potent pharmaceutical compounds.

I have been responsible for many innovations in this area and continue to develop more user friendly and cost effective solution that can handle potent compounds down to 0.001 mcg/m3.

Julian Wilkins’s Specialties:

Understanding of all aspects of Risk, Hazard and Exposure of People and Patients in the Pharmaceutical Industry, with particular emphasis on Exposure Control measures.

Deeply involved in the ever changing regulatory aspects. I am a founding member of a team of world class experts attempting to bring a science and risk based approach to the manufacture of pharmaceutical compounds.
