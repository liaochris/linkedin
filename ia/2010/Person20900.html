Brian D. Smith’s Summary

Brian assists as a consultant to the Call Center and Direct Selling Industries mentoring businesses and industries in effective management principles within his areas of expertise. Brian previously held management and executive positions at InnerLight Worldwide Inc., The STOPZ Corporation and USANA Health Sciences Inc. where he was responsible for sales & marketing, Operations, numerous projects, departments and international objectives. At USANA, he lead the customer service and call center operations as well as the management of USANA’s European operations and supply chain logistics. Prior to USANA, Brian assisted Holiday Inn Worldwide of Utah in one of their largest customer service & reservation centers worldwide. During the time Brian was at Holiday Inn Worldwide, the call center was recognized as a leader in the industry; often visited by competition and other call center management for insight into successful principles and business practices. Prior to Holiday Inn Worldwide, Brian also worked in several call centers for companies like Packard Bell and Sears Telecatalog as well as Wirthlin Group/Decima Research. Brian was an instructor of Spanish and Latin culture specific to Mexico at the renowned Language Training Center located in Provo, Utah. Brian has traveled extensively throughout Mexico and has worked in many international countries.
