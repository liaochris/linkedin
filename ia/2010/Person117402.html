Kirk Harnack’s Summary

Professional experience includes responsible technical positions with two radio station groups, radio automation playout company, and a premier development and manufacturing company in the broadcast audio market. I've always found sales and marketing of the systems and products I work with to be enjoyable, as I'm a real fan of the companies and technologies I've been involved with.

My career goals include reaching all broadcast engineers with the knowledge of using IP technology to improve their efficiency and provide greater results to their facilities.

My interest in technology extends into the physical/natural world in the form of meteorology and hydrology. I enjoy occasional work as a TV weather caster and news reader.
