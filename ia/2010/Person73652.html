Gary Cohen’s Summary

Gary Cohen is currently a faculty member for the Robert H. Smith School of Business at the University of Maryland. He is also a professionally trained and certified coach, specializing in executive/leadership, business and career coaching.

He has held several senior management positions in his 30-year corporate career. He has worked in several disciplines, including international business and logistics, product development, marketing and sales. He has served as Executive Vice President of Nuway Distributors, President of Majestic Tool Corporation, President and COO of Bestway Dealer Warehouse, and most recently as Executive Vice President and a member of the executive management group for West Chester Holdings, Inc. Cohen has also served on corporate advisory boards and on the Board of Trustees for a non-profit organization. He is a principal of Forward Action Coaching, LLC, a firm specializing in helping businesses and organizations in the private and public sectors realize their full potential in successfully executing sound business strategies and achieving overall objectives.

Gary Cohen has a bachelor's degree in business from the University of Maryland, an M.B.A. from Regis University and an advanced M.B.A. certification from the Freeman School of Business at Tulane University. He received his certification and professional coach training from the International Coach Academy, accredited by the International Coach Federation (ICF).

He is also an active member of the International Coach Federation (ICF), Academy of International Business (AIB), American Society of Training and Development (ASTD) and the American Marketing Association (AMA). Gary has also been named in Who’s Who of American Executives and Who’s Who of Young Executives.

Gary Cohen’s Specialties:

Career, executive & corporate coaching, and consulting for family businesses. Academic specializations are in multiple disciplines in International Business and supply chain management.
