  • VP of Marketing

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    May 2005 — March 2008 (2 years 11 months)

    - Drove product management process that combined proactive market research with feedback from customers, early adopters and prospects.

    - Launched a new product into a new category, positioning the company as a leader in high performance computing.
    - Built lead generation engine to deliver and track over 15,000 quality leads from a broad range of sources including direct marketing, partner marketing, web search, PR and tradeshows.
    - Built extensive suite of tools to support every step of sales process, including self-guided pre-sales presentations, multi-media videos and demos, white papers, case studies, data sheets, self-qualification materials, and software evaluations.
    - Developed CRM infrastructure in Salesforce.com to manage prospect pipeline, partners, and customers, distribute leads to reseller channel, and automate workflow.
    - Led development and roll-out of educational materials to get prospects and customers productive within days, using a mix of live hands-on tutorials, regular web-based seminars, and self-guided materials.

  • VP of Marketing

    June 2000 — February 2005 (4 years 9 months)

    SolidWorks was a $200M enterprise software vendor (3D mechanical design software).

    - Led SolidWorks' global marketing efforts, including product management, product definition, field and technical marketing, partner marketing, marketing communications, CRM, business alliances, and OEM program.
    - As company evolved from a single- to multi-product company through acquisition, led integration of disparate marketing teams, product lines, and corporate positioning. Connected the 75 field and corporate marketing team members around the world and managed $15M budgets to achieve corporate goals.
    - During my tenure the company grew at >25% annually to reach $200M in revenue; and customer base grew 300%, with 750,000 users at 80,000 organizations worldwide using our software.
    - Extensive participation worldwide in presentations to press, on panels, and at conferences. Consistently achieved company plans by cultivating an exciting, fast-paced environment with emphasis on high standards, business results, and camaraderie.
    - Fostered a culture of analytical rigor, with a “dashboard” and actionable analysis to measure and improve every aspect of marketing including lead generation, advertising, sales tool usage, market opportunity analyses, product definition, VAR performance, customer demographics.
    - Drove the localization and adaptation of strategies and resources to support worldwide sales channel with new materials, products, lead generation offers, local marketing programs, and prospecting tools. Increased leads (>100%) and reduced cost/lead (>50%).
    - Responsible for content, logistics, and delivery of worldwide product roll-outs, sales and marketing training, reseller conferences, and sales meetings. Reached thousands of channel sales and marketing professionals, tens of thousands of customers, and hundreds of press members.
    - Leveraged solution partner program to significantly increase marketing “footprint” and lead generation.
