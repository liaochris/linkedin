Craig Handley’s Summary

Founded in 1999, Revenue Enhancement Consultants, Inc. is a full service Direct Response marketing agency specializing in developing and implementing solutions to increase lost revenue within existing media buys (online and offline). REC's direct response experience has evolved from consulting for major DRTV Radio and TV marketers at the call center level for campaign design, call center scripting, and call center training that now encompass an array of services that support the overall success in leveraging today's multi-media platforms including inbound and outbound call centers, internet and affiliate marketing, mobile marketing, and leveraging third party loyalty programs.
We show our clients that creatively merging media platforms for your non buyers and buyers on a performance basis allows average campaigns to generate a roll out status and established successful campaigns to accumulate lost revenue never before realized.
