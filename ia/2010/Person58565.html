Steve Anderson’s Summary

Doovle is a technology company that wholly owns runs and manages its proprietary video content delivery platform. The Platform enables video rights holders to monetise their content on its multi-channel billing and payment platform, for delivery to the end user online and on mobiles worldwide. We believe we have developed an outstanding product which provides clients and individuals alike with a cutting edge video platform which can be adapted and engineered to not only build new revenue streams but provide publishers and companies with the productivity and marketing advantages over their competitors by leveraging the very real advantages of Video content delivery and engagement. Doovle's high definition video content-hosting and management platform gives its users complete control over how, when and to whom their video content is distributed and viewed.
The platform enable users worldwide to capture, communicate, record, and enhance digital video content from their computer screens so they can share information and knowledge in compelling multimedia formats, and allow others to observe, analyze and learn from their experiences.
