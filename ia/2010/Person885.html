Aaron Hoos, MBA’s Summary

I provide writing that builds better businesses.

I was once a sales manager, journalist, stockbroker, and entrepreneur. Today I'm a BUSINESS WRITER and STRATEGIST. My work includes sales and marketing content, business and marketing plans, ebooks and reports, blogs, articles, training content, technical writing, and more. My work also includes coaching on communication, content, and marketing strategy as well as assisting or managing communication and marketing plan implementation.

As the creator of the Business Diamond Framework(TM) I develop strategies to help organizations achieve insight, develop innovative strategies, and then implement those strategies.

Along with business coach Heather Villa, I've co-founded the BusinessLunchClub on Twitter, a way for professionals to get the benefits of a business lunch even if they happen to be stuck at their desk. Visit BusinessLunchClub.com for more information and add #businesslunchclub, #bizlunchclub, or #blc to your tweets.
