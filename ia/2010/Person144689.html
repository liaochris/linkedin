Paul Velgos’s Summary

I'm a certified Microsoft Dynamics CRM consultant based in the Chicago area. I’ve been involved with Microsoft Dynamics CRM since its initial launch in 2002 including versions 1.0 through 4.0. For over 11 years I’ve helped companies improve business performance through designing and implementing Customer Relationship Management applications. I share some of my personal CRM thoughts and experience on my blog at http://www.crmdynamics.net.

Currently I’m a Sr. Microsoft Dynamics CRM Consultant at Hitachi Consulting, a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner for Microsoft Dynamics AX and Microsoft Dynamics CRM. Hitachi Consulting offers the agility of a smaller firm plus the stability of larger firms. Hitachi participates in the Partner Advantage Plan for Microsoft Dynamics, ensuring fast support response times, streamlined implementations, and excellent customer satisfaction. Microsoft recognized Hitachi Consulting with the Microsoft Partner of the Year Award in 2005 and 2006 and as a finalist in 2007 and 2008.
