Jo Jordan’s Summary

We are work & organizational psychologists. Our mission is to pursue happiness. Yes, it is!

We work with individuals, businesses, organizations and communities to connect people online and offline. Social media, the new internet, has multiplied the ways we reach each other and work together. At Rooi, we keep an eye on developments, help you find the easiest and quickest way to use internet technologies, and stick with you till you get new software going.

We do work on big projects - provided we are going to see results. Because we like results, we look for projects where the basic work can be done in a few days and we can see results in one to two months.

We do some work 'off our own bat'. Some businesses ask us to work for fees. And best of all, some people come in with us on joint ventures.

We look forward to talking to you if you would like to explore the possibilities of social media in your enterprise, no matter how big, or how small!
