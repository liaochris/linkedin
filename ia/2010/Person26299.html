Barrett Parkman’s Summary

Barrett is passionate about helping Chinese companies expand into foreign markets and helping international companies access China’s market, and is excited about using his current project, The Great Wall Club to do that in the mobile internet industry. He recently graduated from the Tsinghua University and MIT Sloan MBA program in Beijing. During the MBA program, he worked on consulting projects with companies such as Wal-Mart and Facebook and also, worked for Plus Eight Star, a mobile and internet strategy consultancy in Beijing. Before moving to China, Barrett led a team to market Dell enterprise level server equipment in over 30 countries. Barrett holds a degree in economics from the University of Georgia. He is especially skilled at helping companies utilize online tools to develop influence, increase sales and improve efficiency.
