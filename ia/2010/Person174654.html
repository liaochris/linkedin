Robert Elrod’s Summary

Illustrator and graphic designer with over 23 years of print and interactive design experience. My focus is mainly on flash projects and illustration. I also run www.monsterportraits.com. My illustration portfolio focuses primarily on horror, science fiction, and fantasy subjects. My work has been accepted into several small galleries to date, on cd covers and in print publications.

Robert Elrod’s Specialties:

Adobe Photoshop and Flash, digital painting and interactive business-to-business as well as business-to-consumer projects
