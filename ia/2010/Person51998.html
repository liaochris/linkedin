David Harper’s Summary

Many years of experience as an intellectual property attorney in all phases of U.S. and foreign patent and trademark prosecution, litigation, client counseling, agreement work, due diligence, and opinion work in areas including pharmaceuticals, biochemistry, cell biology, immunology, molecular biology, medical devices, diagnostic and detection systems, nanotechnology, chemistry, biofuels, polymers, thin films, semiconductor technology, image processing, informatics, flexible displays, and mechanical devices, representing organizations of all sizes with a particular focus on start-up and early phase companies and the companies that finance them, as well as university clients.
