Mike Rose’s Summary

Eighteen years of marketing experience on both the agency and client side. Leader in implementing multi-channel marketing programs for both business-to-business and consumer direct marketing clients. Possess a combination of front-line strategy experience as well as implementation and execution history. Consistent track record of revenue growth and client development. Experience in working on a global scale with start ups and established brands. Chameleon in managing product launches and turnarounds across various industries within the sports, entertainment and consumer goods world.
