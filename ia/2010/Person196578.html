Stuart Dorf’s Summary

Stuart M. Dorf, Esq. is Executive Vice President of RIISnet. His duties include all RIISnet’s business and investment strategies and serving as primary liaison to financial institutions, special servicers and the investment banking industry.

Prior to joining RIISnet, Stuart practiced banking and finance law with prominent firms in Chicago and Detroit where he counseled financial institutions on matters of commercial loan origination and workout strategies. Stuart has demonstrated expertise in high-intensity marketing campaigns and accelerated sales programs on behalf of Fortune 100 and 500 companies.

Stuart is an original founder of InStadium, LLC, the nation’s leading provider of game day advertising to professional sporting stadiums. InStadium’s network spans over 425 sports venues in 181 DMAs and has long-term relationships with the MLB, NCAA, USL, IFL, AF2, AFL, ECHL and NBADL.
