Jinsoo An’s Summary

Jinsoo’s interest in media and technology began at age six, when he created his first interactive game with thousands of lines of code. Since those early days, Jinsoo has received numerous recognitions in art, design, technology, and engineering in both the U.S. and Korea. With his unique blend of interests, Jinsoo studied Informatics and E-Business at Cal Poly Pomona, and has worked in over fifteen different industries for a wide variety of clients. He is an organizer and a member of the Interaction Design Association (IxDA) and is active in the design and technology community in the Los Angeles area. Jinsoo specializes in interaction design, ethnographic research, infographics, rapid prototyping, and usability testing.
