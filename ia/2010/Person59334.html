Dave Proffitt’s Summary

Dave Proffitt is a business coach with The Growth Coach®, a business coaching firm dedicated to assisting business leaders in developing a mindset of a business owner. His passion is to help his clients gain greater focus and leverage so they can work less, earn more, and enjoy richer lives. The motto of The Growth Coach is, “Driving Success and Balancing Life.” Dave has over 20 years of business and leadership experience through business ownership, corporate experience, and the military.
