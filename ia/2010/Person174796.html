Robert Herr’s Summary

If you live in the St. Louis Metro East and are asked, “Who is Bob Herr,” a few answers may come to mind. Mr. Undertaker…Mr. Funeral Home…Mr. Collinsville…Bob who? Regardless of your answer, Bob is simply a man that puts the “fun” in funeral home…

As a 1963 graduate of Collinsville High School in the heart of the Midwest, he followed his father’s and his father’s father’s footsteps by earning a degree in Mortuary Science from Southern Illinois University in 1966, and taking over the Herr Funeral Home family business soon after.

Born and raised in Illinois, Bob has become an avid fan of the state’s own Abraham Lincoln, collecting priceless mementos and memorabilia that even the Smithsonian would love to get their fingers on. And every piece of history has a story, and usually a very interesting one.

If you want to save a trip to the Lincoln museum in the Illinois state’s capital, visit one of Bob’s funeral homes. Seriously. This guy owns things that would make any museum or history buff jealous. From a Sayers & Scoville hearse dating back to 1918 to a collection of hair wreaths, there is a lot to see throughout the Herr Funeral Home trifecta.

This is one of the reasons why Herr Funeral Homes and Sunset Hill Funeral Home has dedicated a large area of their facilities to the preservation of funeral tradition.

Robert Herr’s Specialties:

Robert has been a licensed Funeral Director/Embalmer since 1966 and is also a licensed insurance producer in the state of Illinois.
