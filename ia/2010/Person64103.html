Ed Mayuga (@elmayuga & @stl_pr)’s Summary

Public Relations Consulting:

I co-founded AMM Communications, public relations consultants of Greater St. Louis, in 2008, with my wife, Ann Marie Mayuga. We are the St. Louis-based public relations firm that specializes in business development, marketing, and social media consulting. Our clients are small- to mid-sized financial institutions, professional service firms and manufacturing/distribution companies nationwide.

Public Speaking:

I have speaking engagements with a wide variety of organizations, such as the Kaufmann Foundation FastTrac for Entrepreneurs, the National Sales Network (Interview and Networking Skils), Enterprise University (Social Media Marketing), the Business Bank (Social Media for Business Owners), SLAREP (Social Media Marketing for Real Estate Professionals), Society of Financial Service Professionals and the GO! Network St. Louis.

Columnist and Blogger:

I write "Public Relations Consultants of Greater St. Louis" blog and I am also a columnist for the St. Charles County Business Record, Life & Health Advisor, and Small Business Monthly.

Civic Involvement:

I serve on the Catholic Charities Archdiocese of Saint Louis Board of Directors, the Public Relations Society of Americe (PRSA) St. Louis chapter board, and the Saint Louis University John Cook School of Business Alumni Board.

I am also a member of the Hispanic Chamber of Commerce Saint Louis.
