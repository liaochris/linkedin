Jonathan Brownfield’s Summary

Jonathan Brownfield aka Johnnybond86 is Sports Photographer, Technology Blogger, and Thinker (For On the Ball). He is best known for his photography work and technology blog. At the age of 17, he was the youngest photographer published in Sports Illustrated Magazine. His work is regularly seen in ESPN Magazine, USA Today, and many other publications. Jonathan is based out of Miami Beach Florida.
