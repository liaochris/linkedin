Dr. Lani Simpson, Women's Health Consultant’s Summary

Lani is a professional speaker, author and women's health care consultant. She has been teaching, The Heat is On seminar for health care professionals for over 15 years. She has studied Western and Eastern approaches to health care. In her seminars she offers a balanced view, utilizing the best of integrative healing options.

Dr. Simpson’s 2010 courses include;
The Bone Age, Diagnosis and treatment

➢ Women's Health Mentoring Program for health professionals.

➢ CEUs are available for chiropractors, acupuncturists, nurses, psychotherapists and naturopaths.

➢ Free Webinars on Wednesday evenings – topics include, Osteoporosis, Hypothyroidism, Digestive disorders and Vitamin D. These webinars are for lay people and health care professionals.

The Bone Age book - will be published in 2010.
