Gregg Stewart (stewart@optonline.net)’s Summary

My current responsibilities and goals include working with clients to leverage and optimize Directional Marketing opportunities across their Interactive and Local Search programs. 15 Miles, TMP DM's Interactive/Mobile division is focused and committed to answering the issues on every advertiser's mind; how to best manage the evolution of consumer usage across print and online media.

In addition to my responsibilities at 15 Miles, I also write on the topic of Local/MobileSearch as the "SEW Expert" on Searchenginewatch.com & Clickz.com.

I pride myself in the ability to assemble high performance teams that produce astounding results. Truly a case of the the "whole is greater than the sum of the individual parts", better known as 'synergy'.

A frequent speaker/presenter at industry events; I am a consummate student of Interactive Marketing and how the online and mobile spaces are transforming online sales and lead generation for major national brands.
