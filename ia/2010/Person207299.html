Laura Hunt’s Summary

I'm a web and graphic designer, copywriter, and branding consultant with a track record for helping clients move up a couple notches in the food chain we call the marketplace.

I believe that smart branding requires more than a great logo--it also requires that, together, we discover the real uniqueness of your business, and craft a marketing message that turns heads. I strive for a perfect blend of artistic thinking and farm girl practicality.

I create distinctive logos and powerful marketing tools, turnkey, that elevate your organization to the next level.

And if you need to achieve some group ownership of the branding process, I can lead your key executives in workshop settings to focus on and create a marketing and brand strategy. I work with clients to ensure they are making the best use of their marketing dollars in online and traditional media.

If you visit my web site or blog, you'll see a farming theme as part of my branding. Besides farming being a great metaphor for marketing, it's also part of my personal story. Don't expect me to have pigtails, overalls and a straw in my mouth. Do expect a down-to-earth, friendly attitude and a lot of common sense.

A little about my life outside of work: I'm a huge Lyle Lovett fan. I'm learning organic gardening. I play a little guitar. I love fruits and veggies, and experimenting with recipes that make the most of their wonderful flavors. And I'm a woman of faith. inside and outside of work.
