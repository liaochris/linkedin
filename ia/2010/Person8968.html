Amanda Meadows-Mathis’s Summary

As a lifelong resident of the North Georgia area, Amanda has a passion for the town she grew up in. Amanda has been married since 2006 and has one child.

The last five years of her career have been spent in the residential construction world working closely with builders and other trade professionals. For the past two years she has managed the Education Department at the Greater Atlanta HBA. Amanda got her real estate license in July 2007 and she obtained her Certified New Home Sales Professional (CSP) designation from the National Home Builders Association in 2008.

Amanda has an extensive background in residential real estate as well as finance, which proves to be a perfect balance to help her clients. Call her today for more information about buying, selling or leasing property in the North Georgia area!
