Dr. Bolden Harris’s Summary

I graduated from Martin High School in Arlington, TX and attended Wartburg College, in Waverly, IA where I graduated with a degree in Biology. Soon after, I earned my Doctorate in Chiropractic from Palmer College in Davenport, IA with honors as Valedictorian and Summa Cum Laude. I am board certified in the state of Texas and by the National Board of Chiropractic Examiners. I have lectured at colleges across the country, Camp LaDainian Tomlinson and served as team doctor at the 2006 National Wrestling Coaches Association tournament and UTA club football team. I specialize in the Gonstead Technique with an emphasis in pediatrics.

Prior to working along side Dr. Ross Coccimiglio at Four Points Family Chiropractic, I was the founder of Harris Chiropractic in Arlington, TX for three years. I have provided thousands of adjustments to people, both adults and children, to help them live a more healthy, balanced life.

I have also had some amazing professional and clinical experiences. My travels have taken me around the world to Hong Kong, Brazil and Nicaragua where I have taken care of people with everything from polio to malaria, dengue fever, strokes, amputees, osteoporosis, sickle cell, diabetes, cancer, herniated discs...the list goes on and on. I have also taken care of infants, children, pregnant women, elderly, athletes and families. My passion is working with children, especially children with Cerebral Palsy, ADD, bedwetting problems...etc. and people looking for general and overall improved quality of life.

We also offer corporate wellness programs for businesses through the US Wellness Chamber of Commerce. We assess your employees' level of health and provide options to reduce their physical, mental, legal and financial risk. We can even help restructure the benefits package for your company.
