Hadji Williams’s Summary

Hadji Williams has been a top advertising copywriter and brand consultant for over 17 years at US agencies, great and small. Hadji has developed successful campaigns across every major category and medium.

His work for clients has ranged from: Absolut, Aleve, Alli, Allied Domecq, AT+T, and Bailey’s Cream to Cleveland Clinic, Cingular Wireless, Dominick’s Finer Foods, Ford Motors, Gillette, Glaxo-Smith Kline, Mercedes Benz, Nationwide Insurance, Nissan Motors, Sprite, Radio Shack, Rock & Roll Hall of Fame + Museum, Visa and Wm. Wrigley, Jr. Co., to name a few.
HIGHLIGHTS INCLUDE:

• Developed creative to successfully pitch and retain over $150 million in billings for ad agencies.
• Developed work that successfully bridges urban/multicultural
and mainstream audiences across demographics.
• Helped launch such brands as: Aleve, Ford Focus, Rock &
Roll Hall Of Fame, and Cingular Wireless.
• Wrote/produced over 1,500 radio & TV commercials.
• Paired Absolut + RedBull, creating one of the most popular
(and lucrative) drinks of the 2000s.
• Adjunct college advertising professor, having taught over 55
introductory and advanced ad courses at Columbia College
Chicago.
• Emerging digital media strategist and web writer.

In 2006, I launched my own web-based publishing house—ProdigalPen Publishing, Inc., which is dedicated to the rich voices and stories of America’s burgeoning multicultural communities.

I'm currently a brand consultant for ad agencies, start-ups and mid-level corps. I'm also an in-demand writer and public speaker on topics of social media, cultural diversity, branding and marketing issues.
