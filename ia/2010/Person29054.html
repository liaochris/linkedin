Brian Cummings’s Summary

With 30-plus years of experience in both corporate and agency public relations, Brian Cummings is one of the most respected public relations professionals in the United States. He has served as general manager, CEO or owner of several public relations entities — the Pittsburgh office of Burson-Marsteller, Publicis Public Relations (formerly Bloom Public Relations) in Dallas and Cummings McGlone & Associates.

Before founding Cummings & Company in 2007, he served as Chief Operating Officer for Michael A. Burns & Associates in Dallas. He joined the agency in 2001 when it acquired Cummings McGlone & Associates (CMA). CMA was named one of the five fastest growing technology agencies in the United States in 1999 by the trade publication, PR News.

He joined QMobius as a senior counselor in October of 2008.

He has significant experience in consumer products in the food and healthcare industries including award-winning work for a wide variety of consumer products and services including Tavist, Iron Kids Bread, Thompson's Water Seal and TGI Friday's restaurants.

More recently, his clients have included Legacy Heart Center, a leading cardiology practice in the Southwest; Personal MD, a medical services company that provides low-cost access to primary medical care; and Our Attic Storage, a new company with an innovative solution for personal and business storage problems.

His interest in food and sustainable agriculture led to the formation in 2007 of Eat Green LLC which operates an on-line farmers market at www.eatgreendfw.com. The site specializes in locally grown meat, poultry and vegetables along with an eclectic mix of artisanal products.

Brian is a past chairman and member of the board of directors of the American Heart Association Dallas Division. While at Bloom/Publicis, he also served as treasurer and later chairman of The Worldcom Public Relations Group. He holds a B.A. in Journalism from Duquesne University.
