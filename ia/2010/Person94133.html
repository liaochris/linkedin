Jeff Webber’s Summary

With a focus on storytelling, and interactive user experiences, I have years of experience in developing and managing creative teams and interactive projects. I recently joined the IDW Publishing team as Director of ePublishing. Past creative leadership roles have included VP - Product Management at uclick, the digital division of Andrew McMeel Universal, Senior Producer for Macromedia’s shockwave.com, and Studio Manager for Hallmark Cards, Inc., Vice President, Interactive at public relations firm Fleishman-Hillard, and Account Manager at pharmaceutical Web development firm Intouch Solutions.

Jeff Webber’s Specialties:

Mobile technology and creative content, Web development, creative direction for the comics industry, digital publishing, animation and design direction, public relations, pharmaceutical, digital pre-press, licensing, and humor product development.
