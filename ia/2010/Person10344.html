Amy Reed’s Summary

An innovative marketing communications pro with a knack for crafting compelling copy that motivates, inspires and targets the right messages to the right audiences. With over 15 years of experience in a variety of industries, I can effectively communicate complex business issues, particularly related to emerging B to B technologies and today‘s leading business concern — sustainability. A problem solver, I can develop integrated marketing communications strategies, training materials and internal cross-functional teams to marry innovation with the desire for corporations to implement environmentally-friendly document management solutions. At Ricoh, my sales enablement tools, consumer education materials and programmatic support helped to demystify environmental issues for account reps, channel partners and customers and promote a greater understanding of the cost-saving benefits and marketing advantages of going green. As a result, sales reps realized greater customer retention, competitive advantages, lead generation and closing of multi-million dollar contracts. IT center managers and purchasing agents were better able to understand ROI factors, and justify change to their internal audiences. I am currently available for open positions and consulting. Rest assured that my skills are transferable to any B to B, consumer-focused industry or non-profit as I have the ability to quickly ramp up, research topics and gain the relevant knowledge to be an effective marketer.
