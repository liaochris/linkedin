Ralf Jacob’s Summary

As Vice President of Operations, Ralf brings more than 17 years of technical and executive management experience to his organization. Ralf is accountable for extending Blink’s business from creative and authoring services supporting DVD and Blu-Ray products to fully encompass interactive, networked, digital consumer entertainment experiences. He has been involved in key roles through the conceptual development and later execution of many of these projects with a track record of success.
Ralf came to Ascent from Move Networks where he served as SVP of Engineering & Operations. He assumed responsibility over R&D, deployment and management of their adaptive streaming technology. Move’s media player has been installed by more than 100mil users and was deployed to sites such as ABC.com, FOX.com, CWTV, ESPN360, Prosieben, Televisa to name a few. He was in charge of Comcast’s “TV Everywhere”
initiative that rolled out to 24mil Comcast subscribers Dec ‘09. Prior to his tenure at Move Networks he served as VP of Engineering at Knowledge Universe and VP of Engineering at iXL.
Ralf is an expert in adaptive streaming, web engineering and application server technologies. Prior to Move Networks, he was Vice President of Engineering at Knowledge Universe in charge of architecting a content management system that synchronized powerpoint presentations alongside streaming media assets. He oversaw all software projects from inception through acceptance. Ralf formerly served as Vice President of Engineering at iXL Inc. from 1996 to 2001 and as a Sr. Software engineer for BoxTop Interactive (acquired by iXL in 1996) from 1994 – 1996. Ralf earned B.A. degrees in Marketing and Computer Science from Fachhochschule Wiesbaden, Germany. He was a member of the German Olympic team in diving.
