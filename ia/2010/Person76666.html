Drew Goodmanson’s Summary

Drew Goodmanson currently serves as CEO of Monk Development and co-founder/pastor at Kaleo Church. Actively serve on the following Boards: The Center for Church Communication, Tentmaker Group and the GCM Collective.

Drew uses his 10-years+ of expertise in the Internet Technology field to help organizations use web technologies to fulfill their mission. Drew is a recognized expert in this field, he writes on non-profit, ministry & church web technology for publications like Christianity Today and CLA’s Outcomes magazine. He currently writes the ‘Church Web Strategy’ column for Christian Computing Magazine. He wrote a chapter in Voices of the Virtual World: Participative Technology and the Ecclesial Revolution. Drew's blog is recognized as one of the Top Church blogs. He is a featured speaker for national conferences such as the Christian Leadership Alliance, the Ministry Internet & Technology Summit, NRB's Advanced Research Symposium, National Outreach Convention, Christian Web Conference, National New Church Conference, Story, Cultivate Conference, GCA & Acts 29 church planters conferences and Total Church conferences.
