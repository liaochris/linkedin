Bruno Tedeschi’s Summary

I can help you seemlessly integrate third-party applications, such as Constant Contact, Campaign Monitor, Fundraising by Net, Aristotle Direct Credit Card Processing, Click & Pledge's Trio into your web presence. Oh, and I can write and edit, too!
