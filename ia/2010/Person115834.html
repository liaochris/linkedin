Kevin O'Brien’s Summary

Kevin O’Brien…
…has generated over $125 million in advertising revenue and strategic marketing programs, to the benefit of media groups and local businesses in the United States and abroad. Kevin facilitates Broadcast AdVentures' signature PartnersProgram campaigns, and conducts focused sales and marketing training and development programs for clients.

Kevin’s career has included successful posts in business management and consulting, and in media as General Manager, Director of Sales, General Sales Manager, Account Executive, Program Director, Air Personality, and Sportscaster.

Kevin has earned a variety of awards and honors personally and for clients for leadership, sales performance, and advertising campaign creative work. He has been a featured speaker several years at the Radio Advertising Bureau’s annual Sales, Management, and Leadership Conference, has taught classes in Advertising and Marketing, as well as in Broadcast History and Programming at Regis University in Denver, Colorado, and is a past winner of the National Sportscasters and Sportswriters award as Sportscaster of the Year for his state.

Kevin is a Cum Laude graduate of California State University, Long Beach with a degree in Radio-TV and Film and an emphasis in Business. He completed the National Association of Broadcaster’s Executive Development program at Georgetown University’s McDonough School of Business and the Media Buying Academy’s certification program to better serve clients.
