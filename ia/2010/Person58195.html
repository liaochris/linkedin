Don Hawley’s Summary

• In 1998 as Founder, Chairman & CEO of NetsWork, Inc. created the business plan, orchestrated the funding of $12 million in first round venture capital and negotiated approximately $40 million in total funding to build a company to $100 million in sales over a 3 year period. Through over 750 employees the company deigned, installed and maintained large scale voice and data systems for entities such as Wells Fargo Bank, Intel, Hewlett Packard, municipalities and universities.
• In 1987 as Founder, President and CEO of Homestar International raised in excess of $10 million in venture capital funding. Introduced over a dozen new products and secured distribution in department stores, specialty stores and warehouse clubs resulting in first year sales approaching $10 million.
• In 1984 as Founder, Executive Vice President, Chief Financial Officer of Worlds of Wonder introduced two of the fastest growing toys in the history of the industry. As CFO was responsible for original financing of $15 million of Angel capital and subordinated debt. Concluded stint as CFO with a successful public offering raising in excess of $80 million in equity from U.S. and European investors. The company grew from a start up to over $300 million in revenue and almost $20 million in profit in two years.
• In 1975 as Director of Finance and Administration of Sunset Designs had management responsibility for a number of middle managers, supervisors and 125 employees. The company grew from $6 million to $30 million in the manufacture and distribution of needlecraft products. The company was successfully sold to a large British company
