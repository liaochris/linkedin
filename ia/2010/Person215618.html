William Myrhang’s Summary

Product Manager with over 10 years demonstrated marketing expertise working in world wide high tech companies including National Semiconductor, AMD, and Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories. A proven liaison recognized for communicating effectively with all levels and meeting the needs of customers and the internal engineering team. Reputation for excellence in innovative product marketing and business development creating strategic plans that contributes to the bottom line.

At Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories (SEL), William specialized in embedded automation controllers targeted at the high growth electrical utility and smart grid markets. SEL’s focus is to “Make Electric Power Safer, More Reliable, and More Economical”

Before coming to SEL, William worked for AMD and National Semiconductor as a product and marketing manager focused on low-power x86 processors targeted at the single board computer, thin client, pos, education, and mobile pad/tablet market. His product marketing job has taken him to various cities in China, Taiwan, South Korea, & Europe supporting leading embedded OEMs and ODMs device manufactures.

With a technical background, William has successfully taken numerous high tech products through various NPD processes at consumer electronic, medical, semiconductor, and electrical utility companies. Additionally, he planned, organized, and set up more than 25+ national based trade shows and executive events which included technology demonstrations shown to President George W. Bush, former Governor of Maine Angus King, Empower America, National Semiconductor board of directors, and at George Lucas’s Skywalker Ranch on the benefits of low-power embedded computing devices.

William Myrhang’s Specialties:

Product lifecycle management, product positioning, strategic business plans, market and product requirement documents (MRD & PRD), roadmap development, pricing and forecasting, evangelism, marketing research, competitive analysis, branding, product launch plans, marketing collateral and sales tools creation, partner management, presentations, tradeshows, customer engagement, plus liaison to engineering & sales team
