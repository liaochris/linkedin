Miguel Mira da Silva’s Summary

Leader of the "IT Governance" and "Information Systems Development" groups at INOV since 2007. Professor of Information Systems in the Technical University of Lisbon (Portugal) since 1998. Sabbatical at the London Business School (UK) in 2004-2005. Founded a software start-up in supply chain integration in 1998. PhD in Computing Science from the University of Glasgow (UK) in 1996.
