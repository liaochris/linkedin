Erich Buerger’s Summary

Erich Buerger is the President of RipeVine, Inc. He has held several key positions in several e-Commerce engagements. Mr. Buerger was the Chief Developer at Garg Data International. Specifically, he was one of the project managers and lead developers of e-Commerce Solutions. His expertise includes e-commerce development, relational database design as well as programming in C++, Java, J2EE, SOA, SOAP, EJB, JSP, JDBC, Net.Data, JavaScript and SQL. Mr. Buerger is a Certified Solutions Expert in IBM's Net.Commerce/Websphere Commerce Suite. He is also experienced in all aspects of development including full cycle development; systems and applications design, project management, analysis, coding, testing, implementation and documentation.

Some of the e-Business clients he has implemented for include: Toshiba, Borders.com, RitzCamera.com, David's Bridal, FannieMayChocolates.com, Sanyo.com, Avery Dennison, ePuck.com, BoatersWorld.com, StoryBids.com, RazorReef.com, Kozmo.com, Entepreneur.com(SmallBizShopper.com).

Mr. Buerger was an e-Commerce Instructor at California State University, Fullerton and holds a B.S. in Information and Computer Science from University of California, Irvine.

Erich Buerger’s Specialties:

RipeVine, Inc. is a corporation founded by e-Commerce gurus. Our development team consists of IBM e-Commerce Certified developers who work in high-transaction volume and enterprise level architectures. Our team has deployed high-availability sites and has custom configured systems to exceed in excess of 400 transactions per minute.
