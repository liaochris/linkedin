Kristen McClarty’s Summary

Kristen McClarty has ten-years experience researching, editing and producing award-winning covers and packages for top media outlets. As a freelance Art Buyer, Kristen works hard to locate original imagery and specializes in the hard-to-find, way-past-the-deadline images.

Kristen successfully worked with National Geographic Adventure, Newsweek and Black Star Photo Agency prior to going freelance in 2004. Kristen has also bid and produced advertising campaigns with her former boutique representation company, M3 Reps. Her freelance clients include, but are in no way limited to: Outside, Men’s Journal, GQ, Time Inc. Content Solutions, Fortune Small Business, HP, The Fairmont, Syngenta, Orlando Convention Bureau, Upstate University Health System, Jansport, Anheuser-Busch, Velocity Sports.

She continues to enjoy working intimately with photographers furthering their career goals and establishing new client relationships, providing image consultation, reinventing their portfolios and marketing strategies. She is available to help estimate and negotiate commercial bids.

Kristen McClarty’s Specialties:

Photo editing, researching, art buying, print production, budgeting, bidding, negotiating, marketing, branding, styling, talent and location scouting.
