Sabin Ephrem’s Summary

20 years of management consulting background working with the Big 4 leading major projects and development centers for the Oracle ERP and Siebel CRM service lines. Previous experience includes technical leadership in the custom development practice at Oracle Corporation (Consulting Services) and additionally has worked in leadership roles leading large enterprise IT Strategy and Transformation, IT project turnarounds, Sales & Marketing automation (CRM) implementations and other executive consulting roles.

Presently Founder/President and CEO of Horizontal Integration, Inc a boutique consulting firm bringing to market the unique fusion of strategy, user experience design, technology delivery and strategic staff augmentation capabilities that has proven to be a winning combination for our clients. This unique business model has enabled the firm to deliver increased business agility to its customers and higher value and efficiencies across multiple service disciplines from one vendor.

At Horizontal Integration our vision is to build a global consulting firm that focuses on the real-world goals of getting and keeping customers, generating revenue, and building competitive advantage for its clients and help them make an immediate, cost-effective impact, by offering enduring strategic solutions that anticipate rather than merely react.

Sabin's core areas of consulting expertise includes the following disciplines:
1. Interactive Marketing Solutions - Digital Strategy, User Experience Design, Usability, Creative Services, and Web Development.

2. Enterprise Business Solutions - IT Strategy & Transformation, Enterprise Architecture, CRM Solutions, E-Commerce, Custom Application Development, EAI, Database Development, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, Content Management.

3. Strategic Staff Augmentation - PM's, BA's, Architects, Developers, DBA's, QA's, CRM specialists, and other IT specialists

Sabin Ephrem’s Specialties:

Business Strategy, Information Technology Strategy & Architecture, CRM Strategy and Solution Execution, Interactive Marketing strategy and execution, Sales and Marketing Automation, General Management, Finance
