Ronald Mannak’s Summary

I love to create gadgets and electronic toys that make the world a (slightly) more fun place.

I created Amazon's #1 best selling music toys that received the perfect 5/5 average user rating. I also created the innovative iPhone beatbox app that Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak demonstrated on YouTube.

I founded several successful companies and had to close one (after I was unable to raise a new round of financing from Dutch VC's).

I am very passionate about entrepreneurship, innovation and technology, and I am always happy to help other startups to succeed. I am a guest lecturer at several universities in Asia and Europe.

Ronald Mannak’s Specialties:

I have a cross-disciplinary background and the ability and persistence to:

1) Identify early and unconventional product and market opportunities.

2) Successfully transform the opportunities into real products with rich user experience.

3) Build great interdisciplinary teams of 'A players' to develop and commercialise the product.
