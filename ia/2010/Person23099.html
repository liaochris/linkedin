Aurore Henze’s Summary

My passion as a writer and natural health advocate evolved slowly over a period of 15 years. I graduated in 1996 from the Carnegie Medical Institute and went on to receive top honors at Baker College in the nursing program. Helping my son to recover from autism, I spent the following 12 years studying Wilhelm Reich's work on orgonomy and different alternative therapies. I earned my bachelors of natural health and am ending my practicuum in the doctoral program of eastern medicine: specifically Ayurveda, TCM and yoga.

Based on the research I have accumulated from working with my own son and now other children, I have formed a new approach to healing-which is truly whole-istic. Specializing in reattaching by working along with parents to get to the core issue concerning their behavior and or illness has been a primary goal and focus in my practice. I am also honored to be sole creator of the revolutionary AMHC music therapy program for children, available through my website.

Aurore Henze’s Specialties:

Color, sound, aromatherapy, constitutional homeopathy and a Reichian perspective (orgonomy) = naturopathic psychotherapy. Dr. Aurore networks with Orgonomic doctors around the country.
