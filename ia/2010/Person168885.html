Randy Cooper’s Summary

A seasoned executive with over 20 years of unparalleled experience in the Business Services sector and Commercial Software sector including Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) with emphasis and experience in Payroll, Benefits, HCM, Finance/Accounting, Business Analytics, and Human Resources Outsourcing.

Has held various executive level positions in companies ranging from start-up phase to Fortune 500 and has served as a Management Consultant to numerous Fortune 500 companies helping those companies to apply technology, business process, and innovation to drive sales and operational excellence.

Randy Cooper’s Specialties:

Well versed in all aspects of company build-out including finance, branding & positioning, service delivery, sales, marketing, M&A, technology infrastructure and development, Mr. Cooper provides advisory services as an industry expert to various private equity groups as well as provides SWOT analysis and strategy consulting to enterprises seeking to align sales growth, operational capabilities, and technology infrastructure in support of the corporate objectives.
