    (E-Learning industry)

    September 2006 — November 2009 (3 years 3 months)

    Taught online Spanish courses using Skype. These included:

    Beginner level: Covered simple conversation and vocabulary to allow students to express themselves in the present tense and conjugate regular and irregular verbs. Near the end of the course basic past tense and reflexive verbs were introduced.

    Intermediate level: Indirect and direct object pronouns, imperfect tense, present perfect tense and the future tense.     Advanced level: Covered the use of the present subjunctive mood and advanced grammar. The introduction of past subjunctive, conditional tense and relative pronouns occurred in later classes. The course included various exercises in reading, writing and conversation

    All online classes were designed with the active participation of the individual student.
