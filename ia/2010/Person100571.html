Joe Fisher’s Summary

Early in my engineering career, I discovered that doing design work in an office was not very appealing to me. Thus, my venture into a professional sales career began….

As Peter Drucker once said “Nothing happens until a sale is made.” Everyone in an organization from accounting to shipping relies on the sales force to drive revenues and profits through discovering new business and opening doors to new markets. I find it challenging and exciting to be on the “front-line” and this is where I excel.

Throughout my career, I have been blessed to gain exposure on a wide range of responsibilities including: estimating civil infrastructure projects; field engineering on a $60 million Cogeneration project; developing innovative concrete forming solutions on high rises, parking garages and bridges; and product management for a cutting-edge hydronic temporary heating system. These experiences, coupled with my ability to interact with many different people, enable me to understand the sales cycle in my industry from many angles.

Through my entrepreneurial approach to managing a sales territory, I have documented success growing existing business as well as creating new business and markets where none previously existed. This was accomplished by practicing a consultative selling approach, building relationships and conducting value-based presentations to high-level decision makers. I have found that people not only buy from someone they like, but most importantly, someone they trust. I build this trust by being honest, following through on my promises and being available whenever my customer’s need me.

Joe Fisher’s Specialties:

Conducting technical presentations, strategic account development, managing sales blitzes, prospecting and developing new business, product management, sales engineering, interpret construction plans and specifications, negotiating contract terms, creating detailed sales proposals, account management, territory management, new product launches and territory business plan development.
