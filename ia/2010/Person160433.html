Paula Jablonski-Hansen’s Summary

Superior Medical Services, Ltd. is an Illinois-based medical recruitment firm. We specialize in the recruitment of all types of physicians and advanced practice nurses across the United States. Our firm is managed and staffed by a team of professionals from the healthcare industry. Over the past 19 years we have developed strong relationships with numerous medical facilities, group practices and surgery centers to help them with their healthcare staffing needs.

Paula Jablonski-Hansen’s Specialties:

SMS is also a licensed anesthesia staffing agency, providing both locum tenens and permanent staffing of anesthesia professionals throughout the country. You can choose coverage for just one case, one shift, several months or for a permanent employee. If you are an Anesthesiologist or a CRNA seeking a new opportunity or a practice in need of coverage, we can help.
