Peter Clayton’s Summary

Founder; writer/producer/director of the first Internet radio show focused on career management: Total Picture Radio. Every week, I record interviews with senior executives, best selling authors, change agents and leading experts in career development, human resources, recruiting, executive search, networking skills, and employment trends. Sponsorship opportunities are available

Through Total Picture Media, I produce and direct executive conferences, webinars, corporate events, and podcasts for American Express, American Management Association, Coca-Cola, JPMorgan Chase, IBM, Citibank, AT&T, Mead Johnson Nutritionals, Procter & Gamble, Xerox, and other global brands.

Producer/host of a new Web 2.0 marketing service for recruiters and executive search firms, RecruitingPods.com
