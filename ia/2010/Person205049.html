Tim Haynes’s Summary

Tim is a pioneer in digital branding, marketing, and execution with over 11 years of experience. Tim is currently General Manager, Starcom IP China, which is the digital marketing practice of Starcom MediaVest Group. Tim was previously the Chief Digital Officer for SinoTech, a leading independant Chinese digital marketing agency.

Skilled in both strategic and tactical issues, Tim has provided digital expertise and services to businesses in China, Hong Kong, Singapore, United Kingdom, Australia, and New Zealand.

Tim, who lectures frequently and publishes articles on the Internet culture, virtual communities and technology and society, was most recently on UGC’s panel at ad:tech Shanghai last year. Tim has also been invited to sit on the Advisory Board for the Hive Awards, an industry ceremony to recognize the ‘unsung heroes of the Internet”.

Formerly trained as a sociologist, Tim, who has a broad knowledge of digital technologies, audience culture, and experience creating and executing innovative strategies, has been living in Shanghai for the past three and a half years and has traveled regularly throughout Asia on assignments.
