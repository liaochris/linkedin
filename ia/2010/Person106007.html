Joshua A. Boren’s Summary

I am responsible for leading and coordinating business development opportunities for Aedas Los Angeles and Aedas Sport, a design studio focusing primarily on sports and entertainment facilities as well as commercial, mixed-use, and transportation-oriented projects. Having received my collegiate degree from the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania, I bring a real estate, finance, and entrepreneurial background to Aedas with previous employment including banking and consulting with an emphasis on market and financial analysis for various real estate product types. I am also a die hard sports fanatic, responsible for leading the firm's direction in new international business opportunities related to the sports and entertainment industry and establishment of the Aedas Sport sub-brand.
