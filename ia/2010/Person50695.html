David Flash’s Summary

I love helping my clients grow sales and profits through effective coupon advertising. I have worked in advertising since 1996, specializing in coupons since 1998. It has been my pleasure to help hundreds of businesses create and execute profitable coupon, direct response and promotional advertising strategies. My favorite thing about working in coupon advertising is that my clients' ads are trackable...so they KNOW their ads are working.
