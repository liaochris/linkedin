Benjamin Manes’s Summary

Strong software engineer specialized in developing core infrastructure services and scaling on-demand platforms, specifically those based on Service-Oriented Architectures.

I am interested in lock-free concurrency, distributed systems, and workflow engines. For example, to explore concurrent data structures I have implemented a high-performance cache. It avoids lock contention by using recency queues to store a memento of an update to the LRU data structure and amortizes the penalty of draining the buffers across threads.

I also enjoy dabbling in many different areas. In my free time I have worked on an iPhone application, enjoy playing with different languages, or have fun digging into other people's code.

Due to my hardware background, I tend to take a holistic view towards a system. I focus on the interactions, data behavior and characteristics, and other scaling attributes. I enjoy helping to define and push forward the architecture, as well as contributing towards building a strong engineering culture.
