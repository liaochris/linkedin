Taron Boney, PMP’s Summary

Taron Boney is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and Senior Program/Project Manager Consultant with extensive global business and technology project management experience. She has 25+ years experience in portfolio, program and project management across all life cycles, processes and knowledge areas. Ms. Boney offers a successful record of achieving strategic benefits and portfolio / program / project objectives across a number of industries, including: investment banking, brokerage and financial services; airline; education; information technology; government; manufacturing; public utilities; retail and transportation. She has a keen ability to translate business results tied to technology changes through effective strategic planning and tactical implementation on initiatives ranging from large, complex, multi-million dollar program/portfolio initiatives to small, stand-alone projects.
