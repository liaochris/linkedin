Audarshia Townsend’s Summary

An obsession with Chicago's vibrant nightlife and dining scenes drove me to first write for respected publications like the Chicago Tribune, Essence, UR Chicago, Chicago Preferred and Playboy.com. Then I co-penned two tomes, Cheap Chow Chicago: Third Edition and BlackBook Chicago Guide 2008. I still write nightlife, dining and lifestyle stories for the Chicago Tribune Sunday Magazine, Playboy.com, UR Chicago, Concierge Preferred and Chicago's Restaurant Guide, but the fast pace of what's really going on behind the scenes drove me to create 312 Dining Diva, a daily blog keeping you up to date on everything you need to know. From the most glamorous "in" spots to those hidden 'hood gems, you'll get it all from the 312 Dining Diva.
