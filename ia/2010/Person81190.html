    Dept. of Health & Human Services (DHHS), National Cancer Institute (NCI)

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    July 2010 — Present (5 months)

    Senior technical writer for the National Cancer Institute. As one of NIH's leading institutions, NCI provides four billion dollars annually in grants for cancer research. The systems that manage this complex process have been fully automated, except for a few select groups of grantees. The application that will support these groups will soon be implemented by ATSC (a large govt. contractor), in conjunction with Oracle. In support of this project, I wrote a 100-page training manual, whch will also be used for their online help content and classroom handouts. I helped design PowerPoint presentations. The manual contained screenshots and diagrams, and made use of Unified Modeling Language (UML) use cases organized and managed as requirements for the software system implementation. The manual also defined the change process required by the NCI staff, to learn and utilize the new system’s workflow.
