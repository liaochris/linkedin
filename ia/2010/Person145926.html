Mike Romano’s Summary

Entreprenuer of 3 tech start-ups in the past 12 years.

If your company has utilized streaming audio & video advertising, mobile marketing, mobile advertising or social media, then you've probably met, spoken to, or heard Mike speak.

As the EVP Sales at SmartReply he's led the sales growth with over 200 clients, including 50 Fortune 500, in retail, grocery, CPG, healthcare, insurance, financial services, sports / entertainment and direct marketing agencies.

Prior to SmartReply, Mike was a member of the team that introduced the world to internet radio and streaming audio advertising at industry pioneer WWW.COM, later branded OnAir.com.

Mike's relationships at the C-level, along with his building high-achieving sales teams and strategic partnership channels, has jump-started the revenue growth of three successful start-ups.

Mike's first real job was as a professional baseball player for the Cincinnati Reds organization.

Mike Romano’s Specialties:

Enterprise software, database technologies, services, ASP, API & SaaS. Multi-channel marketing including Mobile, ecommerce, email, print, direct mail. AdMob, Google, Yahoo, Digital media, Social media, Twitter, Facebook, mobile applications
