Greg Kostello’s Summary

I have worked in technology and media for my entire career.

I really enjoy working with organizations to develop groundbreaking products. I thrive on creating a fast paced environment where brilliant, creative people can produce amazing work. My philosophy is try to predict trends and develop products ahead of the curve. I have worked at Apple, Netscape, MP3.com and now VMIX. All these companies have in someway shaped the future and brought great value to their customers.

Because the world of media and technology is ever evolving, there will always be something new and exciting to work on.

Greg Kostello’s Specialties:

Participated as a individual contributor, manager or executive at seven start ups and early stage organizations. I have worked for Apple (Claris), Netscape, AOL, MP3.com, Vivendi/Universal and Kintera. I have also consulted with a number of media, Internet and non-profit organizatons on media, information security and internet strategy.
