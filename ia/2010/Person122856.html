    (Privately Held; Automotive industry)

    January 1999 — Present (11 years 6 months)

    Responsibility includes accelerating automobile manufacturer and insurer adoption of an emergency contact locator system providing emergency responders, primarily law enforcement, anywhere, anytime access to electronic data they need to optimize the care and safety of individuals following a motor vehicle crash.
