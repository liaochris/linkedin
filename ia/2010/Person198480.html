Sandra Vogel, PhD’s Summary

Dr. Sandra Vogel is an international trainer, speaker and facilitator. She is interested in speaking and training opportunities that require interactive speaking/training techniques with participants.

Sandra has multiple training certifications including the following:
-DDI
-DISC
-Achieve Global
-Kepner Tregoe Analysis
-G.E. Essential Leadership Curriculum

Her management experience includes supervision of front line operations and the creation and management of a Training & Education Department for a Fortune 500 company.

Over a twelve year period she progressed in the organization and was selected as the "Most Motivational Employee" in the People’s Choice Awards, “High Performance Manager” and nominated for “Circle of Excellence” for Managing the Sales & Customer Education Departments.

Sandra has received multiple awards including:

- Ohio-Small Business Administation's - Women in Business Champion-2008
- Cincinnati's Leading Women-2005
- 100 "Wise Women"- Cincinnati 2007
- "Innovator of the Year" -International Conference of eWomenNetwork -2006
- Cincinnati’s "LEADING WOMEN" -Entrepreneurship- 2006
- The West Chester Chamber of Commerce "Woman of Excellence" -2005
- Cincy Biz- Athena Award Finalist- 2005
- Miami University Faculty award for "Women Breaking Barriers"- 2005

Sandra is also a graduate of “The Center for Creative Leadership" in North Carolina and was selected as a trainer for "The White House Project" Go Run Ohio.

Sandra Vogel, PhD’s Specialties:

Training & workshops for all organizational levels from administrative professionals, executives, assembly line, engineers and managers.

Live- interactive training workshops, key notes, Personal & executive coaching, learning solutions, training multimedia & seminars, retreats for women & executives.
