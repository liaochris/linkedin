Mark de Kock’s Summary

Since 1995 part of the upcomming wave called internet. I studied at the Utrecht School of the Arts (Interaction Design). In my career i have worked in different roles from allround Interaction designer to E-marketeer and have a special interest in Integrated marketing(combining all mediachannels if necessary to produce the best possible marketing solutions).

Currently focussed on the development of all connected devices(mobile but there is more to connectivity).

I have helped develop sites and campaigns for companies like T-Mobile, ABN-AMRO, Postbank among others. Have been responsible for E-marketing at a dutch insurance and banking company called Delta Lloyd and am currently employed by Sara Lee as int. E-Marketing manager Global Brands C&T / H&BC.

This role mainly focusses on developments across Europe for brands such as Douwe Egberts, Sanex, Senseo, Ambi Pur, Zwitsal etc.

Nominated for the title online mediaman of the year 2007 by Adlink, IAB, Marketingfacts and IPAN.

Part of the 2010 Spinawards jury (cross media category)
