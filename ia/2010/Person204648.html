Tina Pugh’s Summary

Centriq Training is Kansas City’s largest and most experienced IT training provider - having trained over 3,000 IT professionals from over 400 companies in the last year. Whether its corporate IT training, training individuals for a new IT career, or as a source for hiring IT staff, Centriq has been Kansas City’s trusted partner for all its technology training needs for over 20 years.

Centriq has a staff of over twenty instructors and operates seventeen classrooms from two convenient locations in the Kansas City area (Leawood and Topeka). This combination enables Centriq to offer 70+ courses for corporate IT professionals that are not offered anywhere else in Kansas City and to offer the most comprehensive technology program for individuals seeking a career change.

Locally owned and operated, Centriq Training is the area's only training provider with all of the following authorizations:

• Microsoft Gold Partner (CPLS)
• Citrix Authorized Learning Center (CALC)
• Novell Authorized Education Center (NAEC)
• Cisco Sponsored Learning Organization
• Adobe Authorized Leaning Center
• EC Council Partner

Centriq’s class calendar covers all areas of technology including network administration and programming. Centriq Training offers training on vendors and platforms such as Microsoft, Cisco, Citrix, Novell, Crystal, Oracle, Linux, Project Management, Java, ITIL, Security, WebSphere and Web development.

The offerings also include training tracks for vendor certifications such as Microsoft (MCSA, MCSE, MCTS, MCITP, MCPD, MCDBA) CCNP (CCENT, CCNA, CCNP), CITRIX (CCA/CCEA), Novell (CNE), Linux (CLP) and many others.

See more on the Centriq website at www.centriq.com.
