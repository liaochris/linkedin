Michael Fury’s Summary

Materials and process development executive with a broad background in introducing new production technologies and equipment into mainstream electronics manufacturing. Specific expertise areas include CMP slurries, pads, equipment and metrology; post-CMP cleaning chemistries, brushes, equipment and metrology; high purity wet process chemicals, equipment and metrology for general wafer cleaning, photoresist stripping and post-etch residue removal; low-k dielectric materials, processes and equipment; CVD and plasma etch equipment; inks, enzyme systems and electron mediators for biosensors; inks for printed electronics and related applications.

Goals include new business development around nascent materials and process technologies including categories now identified as "nanotechnology" (which those of us in semiconductors have been doing for years already...). Novel applications of these technologies hold a host of opportunities for innovation and wealth creation.

Michael Fury’s Specialties:

Semiconductor process development; electronic materials & process development; inks for printed electronics, biosensors,gas sensors, fuel cells & solar.
