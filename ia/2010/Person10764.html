    Women On Board

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    February 2010 — Present (2 months)

    Both public and private companies in Belgium currently have a very poor balance between genders at senior executive level and the evolution over the past 10-15 years shows very little progress. Different reasons are invoked to justify that fact, from lack of interest from women, lack of qualified women, lack of women networking, ..
    In view of the above factors, Women On Board will regroup qualified women, both French and Dutch native speakers, with the aim of promoting diversity in boards of directors and committees and it will give Belgian corporations access to a pool of qualified female board candidates.

  • Member Advisory Board

    Job-brokers

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    February 2010 — Present (2 months)

    Job Broker's cost per click software is a performance based software. Their unique approach is designed to eliminate upfront and long term posting fees and to bring job seekers directly to your front website. Companies that use Job Brokers to attract candidates to their postings, typically set a monthly budget that is related to the number of postings available. If more traffic is desired (e.g. more open vacancies) in a certain month, companies can increase this monthly fee.
    The job advertising CostPerClick software is unique, and the first of its kind in Europe. It differs both from typical pay-per-click keyword advertising systems form general search engines like Google and Yahoo, and from the conventional pay-per-listing classified advertising model. The software is simpler to use than search engine keyword advertising; advertisers may sponsor their jobs without having to pick keywords or write advertising copy.
    Instead of having to pay a bundle for a 60- or 30-day listing - typical for job boards -, job advertisers can sponsor all their jobs and only pay per click-through. Recruiters and advertisers can fine-tune their job advertising campaigns by specifying a daily or monthly budget and by altering the price-per-click to make their jobs more or less visible.
