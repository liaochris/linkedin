Teo Ortiz’s Summary

I was first introduced to network marketing in 2007.

I thought right away it was an awesome way to make money.

I did start talking to a lot of people right away friends and family and who ever I could talk to but, soon enough my warm market was gone and so was my business, I said to my self it has to be another way.

If you are a network marketer or sales person you should know about rejection I had a lot of that and people making fun of me by saying something like are you making 20 or 30K a month I was not but, I told them I will.

Still, deep down inside I knew it was a better way to make money and not by accepting a JOB (Just Over Broke)

I knew there has to be a better ways to succeed in this industry than running around chasing people… but just didn’t know how exactly.

Until…

Few months ago, I found a person on the internet who was pulling in multiple 5 figures a month in his network marketing business. Coincidently, he had the same struggles I had before he cracked the code.

Soon enough I was hands on using the power of the internet to market online, making friends and socializing not just with people from my town but with people from all over the world.

My Mission:

I am very fortunate to be able to grasp and take advantage of this incredible tool call internet. But my passion had always been helping and inspiring others to reach their goals and desires.

My mission is to help a select group of students to reach 6-figure income working from home (or anywhere) within the next 9-12 months. I believe life is a lot more than just being imprisoned in a job to make ends meet. Each one of us are so powerful and have the potential to greatly contribute to the world in a way that’s never been seen.

If you have what it takes and have the ambition I described above, I invite you to take the next step http://www.teortiz.com
