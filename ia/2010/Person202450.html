Sascha Stone Guttfreund’s Summary

I throw shows. I began doing club promotion at the age of 15, when I was involved in the start up of Cali Visions, and event company out of LA that was started by Derek Glasser and Jason Deutchman (the Dolce Group). I moved to Austin to go to The University of Texas. I began working with a hip hop artist by the name of Kevin Jack, on artist development which turned into a management role. There was an article written in a magazine about the two of us working together and the steam that we had gained, which started to create a buzz. As the buzz grew, i was contacted by Paul Magee (Global Groove). I was working with someone with experience.. someone who I could learn from. He contacted me because he was in a tight squeeze. He had booked Shwayze for the MONDAY night after ACL. For those of you who don't know, Austin City Limits is one of the nation's biggest festivals.. it consists of people partying nonstop friday through sunday from 12pm to 10 pm.. He booked it the following day... As you can imagine, that's a tough pitch, after people have spent over $200 at ACL and are completely fried from baking in 100 degree+ weather.. Well.. Global Groove had sold only 3 tickets for the event, and it was only 3 weeks away. We sold 900 tickets. The rest is history. Began running club promotion for 12 different night clubs simultaneously, and have been blessed enough to meet some of the best people in the entertainment world in town... it's gotten to a point where I am now well respected as the new kid on the block taking over with the up and coming college market.. but what's my passion? - THE MUSIC. So, I got hired by a music venue to be their director of events (Aces Lounge on 6th street). The Score More team has a home on 6th street, a BEAUTIFUL music venue they can call their home... and the result has been beautiful, the madness has just begun.
