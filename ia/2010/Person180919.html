Samantha Moore’s Summary

Lead Placement Manager (aka Recruiter)

COMSYS IT Partners, Inc. (NASDAQ: CITP) is a leading information technology services company with 47 offices across the U.S. (including Puerto Rico) and offices in Canada and the U.K. We currently serve more than 30% of the Fortune 500 and 60% of the Fortune 100 companies. With approximately 5,000 consultants on assignment in the U.S, Canada and the U.K., we support client staffing and project needs on an international, regional and local basis.

Our recruiting expertise and client-service culture enable us to provide scalable teams of IT workers to tackle the challenges of recruitment, staffing, management and/or administration of IT human capital.

Our integrated project methodology, along with our PMI® and ISO 9001:2000-based project management process, allow COMSYS to deliver turn-key technology and business process solutions, ranging from applications to the infrastructure. COMSYS' Managed Solutions group can provide full deliverable based project services, ranging from discrete development and integration activities, to full functional outsourcing. Through our network of development centers across the U.S. and our global resources, COMSYS can deliver services onsite, offsite or offshore.

As a leader in the vendor management sector, COMSYS has developed a proven offering to help companies effectively manage their contingent staffing usage. With our Service Procurement Management (SPM) and Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO), COMSYS can provide the complete cycle of human resource management services.

Whether you need an individual resource, a team of resources or a complete solution, COMSYS is here to help put IT to work.

Please send your resume or connection request to smoore@comsys.com
