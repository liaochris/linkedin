Henry Eveland’s Summary

Having spent my accounting career in cost and financial management in manufacturing facilities, I have a wealth of experience in both the operations and financial areas of these types of businesses. My expertise in cost containment, inventory, and plant operations allows me an in depth understanding of their effect on the financial stability of a company as well as ability to spot trends in monthly reporting that alert me to possible areas of concern.

A pragmatist, each decision is contemplated and conceived for the good of the company. Not one to shoot from the hip, I will review my understanding of the facts prior to giving an opinion or advise on any project or opportunity.

Leading by example, I work closely with my staff, instilling in them a desire to achieve through mutual respect and an understanding of their abilities and offering them an opportunity to be part of the process rather than a footnote.

Henry Eveland’s Specialties:

Inventory and cost accounting and control, cash management, budgeting, union negotiations, a/p, a/r, general ledger, financial management, statement preparation, laison with banks, insurance companies, outside accounting (audit) firms and attorneys.
