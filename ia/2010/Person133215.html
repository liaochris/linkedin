Mark Carbone’s Summary

Mark Carbone is a business strategist who uses social technologies to implement those strategies. He’s the owner and CEO of XE Corporation, an award-winning enterprise software firm in Orlando, FL. XE Corporation began in 1999 when Mark created the first business management engine for entrepreneurs: XE-700. His company also creates social enterprise strategies and builds mobile apps for iPhones, Nexus, and Facebook for companies in a variety of industries, from movie makers to farmers. Mark also serves as CTO for Kingdom Racing, an Indy Car racing team, where his technology helps the team do more to win races while spending less money to get there.

Mark’s faith in God is first in his life along with his beautiful wife, Susan, and their first child on the way. Mark has an MBA from Rollins College, where he also founded the Rollins Business Journal, and served on the alumni board. He is also UCF graduate in Pre-Law, Lifework Leadership graduate, and a current Dream Builder. Mark also speaks to groups about the economy and how to harness technology to prosper in this new normal. Mark was also nominated for the Most Influential Businessman of 2007 by the Orlando Business Journal.
