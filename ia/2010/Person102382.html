John Karp’s Summary

A history of managing and executing innovation, morphing into managing technology driven companies, has led to interesting consulting work assisting companies in their growth and profitability.

A major factor in this work has been market studies and feasibility analysis, which has led into work with investors in due diligence.

John Karp’s Specialties:

I have extensive experience in product development as well as in construction management, having been engaged in residentail development as a sideline for 24 years and commercial development for 3 years. I am a licensed Professional Engineer, with a general certificate allowing work in many areas.
