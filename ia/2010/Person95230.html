Jennifer Olvera’s Summary

I'm a freelance writer with a decade of experience covering food, dining, travel, nightlife, entertainment, spa and lifestyle-related topics for internationally recognized magazines, newspapers, books and websites. I’m the Chicago nightlife editor for Zagat and an assistant editor for its upcoming dining Survey. I'm the Midwest editor and Chicago dining writer for Gayot.com, regularly cover stories (and test recipes) for the Food section of the Chicago Sun-Times and write about overseas destinations for the Travel sections of Chicago Tribune and Los Angeles Times as well as Frommers.com. Over the years, I’ve penned pieces on Nigella Lawson and “Top Chef” winner Stephanie Izard for Stop Smiling; spoke to author Ray Bradbury about his epic career; and written countless features for Where, CS, DiningOut, Michigan Avenue and Crain’s. I’ve also covered scenic byways for Mobil Travel Guides, served as a dining critic for Chicago magazine, developed recipes for Shojo Beat---a monthly Manga mag --- and been a reviewer for dining, spa, shopping and city events categories at Citysearch.com since 2001. I'm always in search of interesting, challenging opportunities on both the advertorial and editorial (writing) side.

Jennifer Olvera’s Specialties:

Dining critiquing, food writing, recipe testing and developing, feature writing (from financial to food to arts, trends, fashion and entertainment), travel writing, spa reviews.
