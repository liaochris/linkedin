Carmen Anneeze’s Summary

16 years of experience as a self-employed interim manager in marketing and communications. I've worked both profit as well as not for profit and B2B as well as B2C. Got a strong preference for profit because of my commercial streak and because I am sales-driven; like to work on sales-targets. Also consumer-driven; love to analyze customer insights to build the right proposition.

I am energetic, positive, flexible and getting things done! I am a builder and I like to enthuse and motivate people, teams and departments.
Goal- and result-oriented and I always stay focused on the quality. Very practical and pragmatic; got the Dutch down to earth mentality. I am serious about my profession and in my work approach, but I love humor so like to laugh a lot, at work and in private life.

I consider myself a rational person who values good argumentations but also trust my instincts and let my gut feeling lead me. And when I do, it does work out.
