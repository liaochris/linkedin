Sarah Mills’s Summary

Sarah Mills is a creative and entrepreneurial professional with a passion for achieving a sustainable environment through combining the power of individual action with progressive public policy initiatives. With a background in demand-side energy management programs, Sarah has successfully managed many municipal and state-wide environmental programs and specializes in environmental policy, program design, education, and community partnerships.

As Owner/Venture Environmentalist of Sarah Mills Consulting, Ms. Mills has provided project management services for clients such as The Energy Coalition and the City of Irvine – for whom she developed a municipal recycling campaign which received national recognition as Most Innovative Campaign, awarded by the United States Conference of Mayors in January 2008 and January 2009.

Prior to her role at Sarah Mills Consulting, Sarah was the Program Director for Community & Educational Partnerships with The Energy Coalition and managed community-based energy efficiency and demand response program activities in California, Illinois, and Sweden as the director for PEAK Student Energy Actions, the Community Energy Partnership, and PEAK Plus.

Sarah received her B. A. in Molecular, Cellular, and Developmental Biology from the University of Colorado.
