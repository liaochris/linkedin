Janet Morgan’s Summary

President of CSRwire, the Corporate Social Responsibility Newswire. www.csrwire.com.
Prior to joining CSRwire, Jan was a consultant and executive for various organizations such as the Nassau Community College Alumni Association, American Express Tax and Business Services, RSM McGladrey, Friends of the World Food Program, Media Minds Group, Zink Communications and ABC Network Television. Jan has a wide range of management and marketing expertise in both the private and nonprofit sectors.

Janet Morgan’s Specialties:

Strategic and business planning, organizational design, marketing, fundraising and philanthropy. Foundation counsel and board development. Currently serving on two boards - Antioch University Board of Governors and the READ Global Board of Trustees. She is on the board of advisors for the Katerva Challenge and is a member of the Social Venture Network, TiE Boston (to foster and support entrepreneurship), and the Association of Governing Boards of Universities and Colleges.
