Robert Eric Walker’s Summary

Accomplished worship leader with 20 years experience spearheading successful worship arts ministries. Consuming desire to lead and challenge God’s people toward the passionate pursuit of authentic worship.

Motivated toward entrepreneurial ministry development, utilizing creative initiatives to expand outreach, multiply existing resources and facilitate future growth. Known for innovative ministry design and implementation.

Enthusiastic leader, inspiring and challenging teams toward high-impact worship arts ministry locally, nationally and internationally. Gifted at discovering, developing and empowering the gifts of others. Exceptional ability to build, train and motivate teams structurally, relationally and spiritually.

Articulate Biblical communicator with engaging style, dedicated to a Christ-centered preaching model. Popular speaker for conferences, retreats and workshops. Takes 2 Timothy 2:2 to heart, practicing a lifestyle of ongoing one-on-one discipleship.

Life-long learner, always seeking to increase knowledge and skill sets. Having earned both Master of Music and Master of Divinity degrees, offers a unique blend of musical excellence and spiritual challenge.

Has enjoyed consistently outstanding performance evaluations with a solid history of results-oriented ministry. Characterized by others as hard-working, creative, relational, self-motivated and hungry for new challenges.

Robert Eric Walker’s Specialties:

Contemporary worship arts ministries, entrepreneurial ministry development, organizational leadership and administration, booking, promotion and execution of outreach events/concerts, Biblical preaching/teaching, innovative ministry design and implementation, creative evangelistic outreach, experienced vocalist, piano/keyboards, choral/instrumental conducting, music arranging/orchestration, training/mentoring of assistant leadership, certified church consulting, conferences/retreats/workshops
