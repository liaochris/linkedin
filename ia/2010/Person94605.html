Jennifer Nedeau’s Summary

Jen Nedeau is a Digital PR Manager for Time Inc. working with Time, Fortune, CNNMoney, Money and Life Magazine brands.

Previously, Jen worked as a new media consultant, writer and speaker with organizations such as Women's Media Center and America: Now and Here.

From June 2009 to January 2010, Jen served as the Director of Digital Strategy at Air America Media. At Air America, she helped transition radio, a legacy medium, into the age of the internet by launching a new website and coordinating social media marketing. From August 2008 – December 2009, Jen served as Editor of the Women’s Rights Blog for Change.org where she facilitated daily discussion about the feminist movement as it related to politics, technology and social norms. In addition to blogging, Jen worked for New Media Strategies in Washington, DC from 2007 to 2009 where she employed online campaigns for Fortune 500 companies utilizing the power of social media to promote and protect clients. On nights and weekends, Jen volunteers for New Leaders Council, a non-profit that offers skills-based training for young leaders.

She received a bachelor's degree in Journalism from The George Washington University and is originally from San Francisco, CA. You can learn more at www.jennedeau.com and follow her on Twitter @JenNedeau.
