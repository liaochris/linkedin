Shen Heng’s Summary

My multi-disciplinary roles, since 2000, at H1Studio (Founder / Managing Director), at Singapore Polytechnic (Lecturer / Module Coordinator), at a new Research & Innovation Centre – Centre for Experience Design (Centre Manager), and recently, at Procter & Gamble (Virtual Solutions Adoption Manager for Australia / Asia / India), has allowed me to work directly with co-workers from various countries, backgrounds and organisational positions.

Although I graduated as an Architect, my versatile training has allowed me to venture into other areas of interest, especially in Interactive and Digital Media Technologies, providing design consultancy and development services in 3D, Web & Interactive Digital Media to businesses & individuals, for their business, service, product. I have assisted clients by packaging a myriad of consumer experience touchpoints from corporate identity, 3D visualisation, interactive websites, to application development for online, mobile and portable devices such as PDA / iPhone.

Since 2008, I have been developing augmented reality applications for web and also for mobile platforms (Symbian, Windows Mobile, Android) - demos at http://h1studio.com/blog.

I have collaborated with ad agencies such as OgilvyOne, TribalDDB, BBDO, Proximity, Formul8 and completed projects for Heineken, VISA, Universal Music, Mercedes-Benz, Temasek Holdings, Discovery Channel and many more.

Your organisation will benefit from my wide range of capabilities covering all scopes of creative design / project management. It is important to stay in touch with the moving landscape and I can recommend the various additional possibilities in fulfilling and transgressing a project to fuse aesthetics and functional components and help the client eventuate his / her requirements.
