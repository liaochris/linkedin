Joseph Kestel, PMP’s Summary

I am a certified project manager professional (PMP) with 15 years of project management experience. I am currently serving as a Sr. Partner at Project Consulting Group, and have been a strategic consultant since its inception in 1998 and oversee the development of our project management consultants through an intensive training program I have developed for PCG.

I have recently become a sought-after project management speaker and trainer- having provided presentations to local Project Management Institute (PMI) Chapters as well as PMI Global Congresses in Europe, North America and Latin American. PMI has recently posted a video of me talking about the upcoming Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) on their web site at: http://www.pmi.org/Movies/4StandardsVideos/PMBOKonly.html

My areas of expertise include: consulting acumen, project management, program management, project office and program office installation and configuration, customized performance improvement, process reengineering, process improvement, proper implementation of estimating methodologies, web application project management, software development and customization, software testing, personnel and project management infrastructure assessments, and instructional design and development and training. I have demonstrated, consistent success through the use of: strong problem solving skills, communication and facilitation skills, commitment to excellence, technical acumen, bureaucracy busting, and team building ability. I have managed and empowered a multitude of teams to help them reach their strategic goals and provide them with performance improvement solutions in order to solve the most challenging business problems. I have strong experience working on cross-organizational projects and globally-dispersed projects where collaboration is a must between different business areas, stakeholders and cultures.

Joseph Kestel, PMP’s Specialties:

Strategy and change management specialist with a strong background and practical program, portfolio, and project management experience. Can help any organization develop creative future strategies for current business challenges.

As such, I asked to experts who had been heavily involved in the writing the Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK) to join this team and help revise the content of this section in particular.
