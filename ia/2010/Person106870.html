Jonathan Freeman’s Summary

"The co-creator of MLM Dashboard (tm) and the Archangel (tm) system, international best selling author and multi-millionaire Jonathan Freeman has over 22 years of business experience, leadership, web design, project management and internet marketing. Freeman is the most in-demand MLM consultant in America today. Freeman is also a world most recognized authority on MLM (Multi Level Marketing) companies online video reviews and viral MLM product launches.
Freeman earned his ACE Certification in 1990. Later that year he started his first company, Animationeer Studios, LLC and authored his first web site for his father's publishing company (http://www.thechristian.org) using a new protocol called Hyper Text Markup.
During the early 90's Freeman became a proficient project manager and became heavily involved in the Adobe line of products as well as a key consultant with a growing company called MetaTools and Fractal Design where he oversaw the production of their key software offerings. With associations such as famed digital artist Kai Krause and the creators of the HTTP protocols for the Macintosh Platform, Chuck Shotten and John Weiderspan Freeman began executive level consulting and overseeing web site development for Microsoft's Encarta Division, MetaTools KPT Bryce offering and more. In 2001 he earned a Master CIW Designer certification while heading up the Web Services Division of a startup .com called Gaia.com where Freeman helped established JV's and partnerships with Fortune 100 and 500 companies; Tommy Hilfiger, Estee Lauder Companies, Stella Corp, Toshiba and Microsoft.
Mr. Freeman is a member of MENSA, the MRGA, MIFNA, Forbes Entrepreneurs, eWeek Advisory Panel, Forbes Personal Technology Forum, Executive Council of Newnan, Atlanta Blue Executives, United Professional Sales Association (UPSA), Forbes Atlanta and New York City group member, The Networking Community, Republican National Committee (RNC), and other civic and professional associations.
