Chris Bailey’s Summary

I'm an experienced and innovative online marketer who understands how to use technology to cultivate meaningful relationships. In my early career as a nonprofit association membership professional, I led successful initiatives to both attract prospects and retain members. Later, working as an account manager for a leading software company serving the nonprofit sector, I provided clients with expertise to help them build interactive and action-oriented websites for their constituencies.

Today, I bring this wealth of expertise, insight, and creative ideas to clients who recognize that meaningful customer relationships lead to higher satisfaction, greater likelihood of purchases and stronger loyalty to their brand.

What makes me unique is my ability to integrate marketing experience with insights from social media, business anthropology, leadership coaching, and industrial design. I believe a multidisciplinary and holistic approach is what's needed to differentiate business from the crowd.
