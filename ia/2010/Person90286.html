Jason Clement’s Summary

Jason is the Director of Findability for Wieden+Kennedy and also does digital strategy work for its clients. Jason is a nine year internet industry veteran who specializes in search advertising, advertising measurement and social media strategies.

Since starting his career with a short stint at a pure-play search agency in 1999, Jason's found a new home helping to develop successful digital operations for some the world's best creative agencies.

Jason's currently managing a group of search and digital strategy professionals in New York and Portland and overseeing strategy for campaigns globally.

In spite of spending most of his time on conference calls and airline flights, he still claims to live with his three cats, two dogs, whip-smart son and tolerant wife in suburban New Jersey.
