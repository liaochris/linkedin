Andrea Genovese’s Summary

Electronic Engineer, Digital Entrepreneur, Multimedia Publisher, Design Professor and New Media Consultant.

Specialized in corporate communication, experience design and crossmedia publishing.

He worked for several companies in Italy: Digital Producers, TV Broadcasters, MK-COM-IT-HR Corporates, Crossmedia Publishers, like RAI, Telecom, TIM, Enel, Edison, Treccani.

He has founded a New Media Company called Map Cross Communication, which has designed and realized the first Mobile Corporate Portal in the world for TIM/TELECOM.

He also realized projects of Intranet, HR Portals, CRM, e-learning, multimedia productions, advertising campaigns and digital Marketing.

In the last few hears he started the first Free Press Corporate: 7thFLOOR. Business & Design Magazine. A crossmedia hub able to link corporate managers with innovators like designers, developers, creatives, new media expert and digital entrepreneurs.


Projects dealing with:

- IULM University, Faculty Master Social Media Marketing & Web Communication;

- Domus Academy, Director of Technologies Faculty at Master in Cultural Experience Design and Management;
