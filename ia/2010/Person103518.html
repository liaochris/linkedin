JoJo Zawawi’s Summary

JoJo Zawawi is a self-taught improvisational classical and jazz pianist and composer. She composes all her own music, and at live concerts, rather than playing music already written, she improvises all new pieces -- sometimes based on made-up melodies solicited from the audience.
