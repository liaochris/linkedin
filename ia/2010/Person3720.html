Ari Fleeman’s Summary

The vision of the COMSYS Center of Recruiting Excellence (CORE) is to transform how global customers are served by enabling the human capital supply chain to streamline their process and improve efficiency.

Focus on 3 basic principles:
out [think] the competition by having a clear view to our process and applying the latest technology and innovation
out [pace] the competition by making real-time adjustments to performance based on measurable data
out [performing] by having a culture with a maniacal focus on efficiency and performance improvement

Ultimately we want to build a unique culture which approaches recruiting as
1. an art - by preserving decades worth of recruiting best practices in building lasting candidate relationships, and
2. a science - by leveraging the latest in performance managment and improvement
