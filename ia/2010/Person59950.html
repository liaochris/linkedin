Dr. Kevin Keough’s Summary

Practiced as a self-employed licensed clinical psychologist through 12.2007 delivering comprehensive psychological services through the Marriage and Family Center of Delaware (MFCD) and the Police Psychology Consultation Center (PPCC).

The MFCD established in 4.1993 was a general practice with focus on teens, young adults, couples, men, "treatment refractory" patients, people with chronic medical conditions, prominent community members,and those with increased risk for violence.

Consulted with DuPont, MBNA, and Blue Cross, local churches, community groups, EAP employees, high schools (at my old high school-taught AP Honors Psychology course three semesters for high school seniors who earned 3 college credits), advocated for mental health parity on local, state, national levels.

Comprehensive initial evaluations, co-development of treatment plans, identification and management of medical conditions and medications that present as psychological conditions, coordinated medical and psychiatric care with family physicians and psychiatrists, maintained admitting authority to local psychiatric hospitals;

Established the Police Psychology Consultation Center in 7.1995 to provide comprehensive psychological services (pre-employment psychological evaluations, special unit assignment and fitness for duty assessments, training, consultation with police administrators, consultation and problem solving with rank and file officers, ridealongs, program development, shift work modification, etc; diverse and innovative consultation to police administrators from over a dozen departments and a range of office and patrol car-street based consultations, interventions, and education with police officers and other first responders.
