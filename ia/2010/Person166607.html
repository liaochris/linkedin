Stile Moffett’s Summary

I've been in this industry for a couple of years now and gained so much knowledge. With an ever changing industry there is no limit to what one can learn. I've had the pleasure of working with some of the best of the best; both in office and outside partnerships.

I'm constantly looking for the next best thing and how to make it even better. Working so close with affiliates during my first round here at AffiliateTraction gave me much needed insight as to the grunt work needed to fuel this industry.

After moving onto the network side of things I've had the chance to learn more about the corporate side of this industry and how the mechanics of it all work. This, along with working for a ground breaking agency such as AffiliateTraction and working with/on behalf of advertisers I have gained enough knowledge to feel confident in any task presented to me; as well as assist affiliates, publishers and networks in their quest for success.

I love to learn and I'm always looking to gain knowledge on how this industry is changing.
