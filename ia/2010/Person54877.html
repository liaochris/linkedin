Dennis Wozniak’s Summary

I have extensive expertise implementing complex projects within tight delivery windows. Such experience includes managing projects ranging in scope from individualized IT implementations to multi-functional projects to meet strategic plans as well as meet key regulatory requirements.

In my role as the Director of Enterprise Project Management, I continue to utilize my strong project management background while leading a dedicated staff of Project Manager’s to support, more broadly, the strategic directives of the Enterprise. This is done through developing and maintaining key relationships across the Enterprise to ensure that we have the appropriate visibility into the key initiatives and are appropriately staffing the most critical projects.

I believe that strong leadership begins with appropriate and consistent communication. Nothing gets accomplished in a vacuum or without the involvement of others. Therefore, I take special attention to ensure that everyone is working towards the same goal and that their voices are heard. I have had the pleasure to have worked on, and lead some terrific teams and the ones I believe were the most successful, were united in their efforts and articulate in their goals. My job as a leader is to not only create, but foster such an environment.
