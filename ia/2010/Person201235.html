Terry Carmichael’s Summary

Terry has many years of experience in interactive marketing (eMarketing), primarily within the biotech, diagnostic, and healthcare industries. Starting out in a genetics lab, Terry accelerated his career into technical support, biotech sales, product management, and marketing.

Terry’s involvement in the biotech industry started back in 1992, when he began working on a Master’s Degree in Molecular Medicine and Genetics at Wayne State University School of Medicine. After working in an academic laboratory for nearly 3 years, Terry was hired by QIAGEN, a leader in providing DNA purification. Then, in 1999, Terry completed a Professional Designation in Marketing and Sales program offered through UCLA's Extension Program, and became Product Manager for Bio-Rad’s Gene Technology business unit, where he launched new DNA purification products.

In 2000, Terry founded GeneTree, one of the first companies to provide direct-to-consumer DNA testing services on the internet. While with GeneTree, Terry placed the first human DNA testing product on the shelf in a national retail drugstore (Rite-Aid) and a regional chain of superstores (Meijer) - currently being distributed under the IDENTIGENE brand. Terry sold GeneTree to the billion dollar Sorenson Development, Inc. in 2001.

In 2008, Terry reiterated his charter as a entrepreneur when he founded the BioPharma Institute, a skilled e-Learning organization providing online corporate training solutions.

Terry is currently working as a Vice President of Marketing for the BioPharma Institute - one of the most successful companies in the US distributing eLearning training courses to the biotech, pharma, and healthcare industries.

Terry Carmichael’s Specialties:

Marketing molecular diagnostic tests, medical devices, and direct-to-consumer products; finding new distribution channels; developing communication strategies targeting specific audiences; new product development; and selling to high-level positions in pharmaceutical, biotech, life sciences, and clinical research organizations. Terry has an exceptional understanding of interactive marketing tactics and has demonstrated success in selling to all levels of organizations in several disciplines.
