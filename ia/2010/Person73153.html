Gary Hayes’s Summary

Visionary Producer, Designer and Manager with proven track record of taking world-class media organisations from the linear to the experience age. Took the BBC into emerging media futures by producing many of its ‘firsts’ across web and interactive TV from 1995-2003. With strong innovation, communication and leadership, managed new production teams and mentored world class traditional producers in convergent communication.

Now full time Directing a Laboratory for Advanced Media Production in Sydney. Helping Australia enter the divergent cross-platform age, with emphasis on social networking, contribution and deep interaction. See lamp.edu.au. He is also leading global metaverse development as Head of Virtual Worlds at MUVEDesign having just built two of the leading brands in Second Life, ABC and BigPond.

Recent work in the US includes consultancy on interactive ads and video-on-demand, projects/pilots with AFI and CBS. Co-wrote a white paper for the DTi about standard metadata enablers for mass adoption of Personal Video Recorders in the UK.

Keynote speaking engagements at SPAA Fringe, Kanz, Ad:tech, Online Networking & Business Collaboration, Cross-Media Storytelling, Digital Hollywood, Digital Days 2005 (also produced), SPAA, XMediaLab, Fast Forward Rewind, Small Screen Big Picture, AIMIA conferences, AFTRS Industry events (Mixed Reality, Games, Virtual Story) various ABC internal labs, hundred plus LAMP orientation events around Australia and many more. Chaired the Mobile Content World Australiasia 2006 conference and the IPTV 2007 coming up and presentation and consultancy into AMP, Fairfax, Channel 9 and others.

Gary runs a very popular blog on 'the digital you' at www.personalizemedia.com and a static 'resume' and producers guide site at www.garyhayes.tv

Gary Hayes’s Experience

  • Director

    Laboratory for Advanced Media Production

    (Government Agency; 201-500 employees; Media Production industry)

    June 2005 — Present (4 years 8 months)

    Directing, training and presenting on an initiative to stimulate cross-platform and cross-media growth in Australia. LAMP is managed through the Australian Film TV and Radio School (the premier post-grad media school in Australia) and is a series of seminars, workshops, production labs and commercial development.

    Groups from the Australian media industry work with world class mentors to develop cutting edge projects with a view to near to medium term deployment. Find out more on lamp.edu.au and my blog at personalizemedia.com.

    Also leading Australian development in virtual worlds being producer, designer and builder on the Telstra BigPond Second Life service, consultant and developer on the ABC TV project and involved in a range of LAMP virtual world projects in longer term development. www.muvedesign.com and www.justvirtual.com.

    LAMP is now running extended courses in business and developing projects over longer 4-6 month periods to pilot and user testing.

  • Senior Development Producer/Manager

    BBC

    (Public Company; BBC; Broadcast Media industry)

    November 1994 — September 2003 (8 years 11 months)

    “Gary Hayes is undoubtedly one of the outstanding pioneers of interactive TV, having devised the first 24/7 service for Digital Terrestrial, and the first truly interactive programming for Broadband TV, both for the BBC…” Scott Gronmark (ex Head BBC iTV)
