Melissa Kuzma’s Summary

After a long and successful career in Information Technology, I completed my MBA with honors in Business Management. My goal was to find a way to leave the corporate world behind and put my technology skills to use for something more personal. I found that calling in the senior market.

Like so many others in our age group, I found myself helping my parents transition to a Continuing Care Retirement Community. As fate would have it, my friend and soon to be business partner, Sue Thomas, was helping seniors downsize and transition to retirement communities. Sue had a special take on these services; she developed and offered Redesign for Retirement Living™.

As we worked together we realized we had very different, yet complimentary skills. In 2008, we joined forces to create RICA Enterprises, LLC. Our mission; to bring education and marketing resources to senior service providers, giving them the support they need to grow their business and enhance the quality of senior living.

Through our 3 divisions: RetireEd (www.RetireEd.com), The Retirement Living Resource Directory (www.RLRDOnline.com) and our newly launching Senior Business Builder (www.SeniorBusinessBuilder.com) we look forward to helping businesses grow and succeed in today's competitive senior market.
