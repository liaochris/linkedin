Megan Guilfoyle’s Summary

I graduated from Marquette University in May 2009 with a Bachelor of Arts in Advertising and a Minor in Marketing. I was both a Dean’s Scholar and on the Dean’s List.

At Marquette I discovered who I wanted to be like. I discovered a passion for event planning and organizing. I love detail-driven assignments, coordinating and managing promotional activities, and the thrill of a successful event. I realized how much I loved advertising, specifically account management and brand planning. I love consumer trends and release the magnetism between a brand and its target. Much of what I've experienced has lead me to where I am today.

I am an optimistic leader who is always willing to take on new challenges to improve the quality of my work.
