Christopher Johnson’s Summary

Chris Johnson is a licensed insurance agent with a background in corporate finance, business planning, technology commercialization, project management and international business. Johnson’s high-tech experience includes working as CFO for a fabless semiconductor start-up, as well as stints on the boards of directors of three technology companies in the fields of engineered materials, software development and robotics. Johnson also has four years experience as director of a contemporary fine art gallery in Chicago. During this time he was also a founding member of a neighborhood gallery association that promoted the area as a visual arts destination, and he completed a Certificate of Connoisseurship in Fine & Decorative Arts at Northwestern University.

In his insurance practice Johnson specializes in the fields of Fine and Decorative Arts, Film and Video Production, Performing Arts, Creative Businesses and Technology Businesses.

Christopher Johnson’s Specialties:

Johnson is licensed to sell property & casualty insurance in CA, FL, IL, IN, MD, MI, MN, NY, OH, TX and WI. Target clients include Art Dealers, Photographers, Framers, Graphics Designers, Architects, Musicians, Boutiques, Film Productions, Pop-up Stores, Spas & Salons, Museums, Historical Societies, Theatres, Concerts & Special Events, Private Collectors, Corporate Collections, Computer Consultants, Software & Website Development, Computer System Design, and Technology Sales Organizations.
