Erin Guttenplan’s Summary

Erin believes in unleashing the potential in adolescent girls throughout the developing world. She understands the ripple effect that one girl can have on her community, a nation, our world. She also strives to expose Americans to the world’s main development challenges so that they, too, can become agents of change. She understands that the ripple effect that one volunteer can have on their community, a nation, our world.

Erin is passionate about international service. For the last decade, she held seven different roles with EF Education, the world leader in international education, working in sales, customer service, marketing, human resources, and business development. After recently volunteering for five months in Asia, Erin saw an opportunity. She saw a movement that has been dubbed The Girl Effect. She met agents of social change ready to serve. Imagine the potential. Erin has traveled to 27 countries on five continents and hopes to conquer all seven soon. She holds a B.S. from Cornell University.
