John Lusk’s Summary

John is the author of the best-selling book, The MouseDriver Chronicles. TMC is published in 5 languages and 6 countries and is required reading in over 200 universities worldwide. Most recently, he served as the interim-CMO of Yapta.com, a travel website that helps travelers save money on flights and hotels before and after they've purchased a ticket. He is also a co-founder of Vespa Partners, a general management and strategy consulting firm focused on consumer products and services businesses in the Pacific Northwest. Before Vespa Partners, he served as the Vice President, Marketing at WhitePages, Inc., a privately held Top 50 web and mobile publisher. Prior to WhitePages, John led marketing efforts for Microsoft's Windows Live Messenger product and directed midmarket marketing activities in Microsoft's US Subsidiary. Prior to Microsoft, he co-founded Platinum Concepts, Inc. and served as VP, Sales and Marketing for the MouseDriver product line.

John holds an MIS degree from Southern Methodist University and an MBA from The Wharton School. He served on the Small Business Administration's National Advisory Council and spent three years playing Semi-Professional soccer.
