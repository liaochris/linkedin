Ronald Gordon’s Summary

I am an internal and executive corporate communications professional with 10 years’ experience supporting senior executives at IBM and senior leaders (CEO; senior partners) at Analysis Group, an economic, financial, and strategy consulting firm. For 10 years, I also served as a contributing editor to The American Lawyer, the nation’s leading news and features publication for the legal profession.

At IBM, I developed my role to encompass executive communications support for the Worldwide Sales Leader, the Worldwide Marketing & Strategy Leader, and the Americas Sales Leader of the $34 billion enterprise systems business unit. I also created and assumed the role of Field Communications Managing Editor, functioning as a team leader of senior communications professionals and overseeing all field communiqués from product line sales leaders. In addition, I was the winner of the inaugural global communications function proposal competition to develop a program to enhance the effectiveness of IBM communicators. My core business skills training program, developed jointly with Duke Corporate Education, continues to train high-potential IBM communicators from around the world.

As a senior writer and editor with Analysis Group, I proposed, wrote, and produced a series of web video profiles (available on YouTube) to showcase the thought leadership and expertise of the CEO and other senior practitioners. I transformed the firm’s biannual client news magazine from a “case wins” newsletter into a substantive forum for internal and affiliated economic and financial experts to address pressing client issues. I also created a bi-monthly email newsletter to increase the frequency of client outreach, and collaborated with senior practitioners on content for the re-launch of the firm’s website – winner of the 2009 Web Marketing Association Standard of Excellence award.
