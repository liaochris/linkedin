Mark Minevich’s Summary

Mark is considered to be a leading global sustainability change agent due to his unique character and an innovative transformative thought leadership. Mark is an international sustainable development management expert and entrepreneur, cleantech and Green investing Industry advisor, globally recognized innovation technology visionary and strategist. He is an author of highly acclaimed books including, Six Billon Minds and The CTO Handbook: The Indispensable Technology Leadership Resource (Thomson Publishing). As an analyst, Mark has written extensively on the impact of globalization on the international business community and authored numerous high profile rating reports on technology, innovation and global outsourcing. Mark currently focuses on Green investing, Smart Cities, Smart Buildings, Smart Grids and Energy Efficiency issues but also is an influential authority on technology and especially on the role of the CIO/CTO private wealth, social networking, impact investing and innovation. Through Going Global Ventures, Mark provides strategic consulting services and comprehensive smart sustainability solutions to help leading corporations, enterprises, governments, smart cities and investors achieve a greater measure and effectiveness of self-sustainability development models, promote sustainable development on large scale, improve environmental standing as well as decrease overall costs. Currently ,Mark acts as a special Advisor to EU Countries on Green investing, energy efficiency and smart City Planning. Mark is also a an Executive Partner of Global Cleantech Business Intelligence and Analytics Consortium Holding operating in India, China, Singapore, UK and US/ Mark founded and served as a Chairman of Billion Minds Foundation from 2006 to 2010, and also is a founder of private wealth community, www.markminevich.com
