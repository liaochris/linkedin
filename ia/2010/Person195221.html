Steve Lazuka’s Summary

Steve Lazuka has over 12 years of experience in the Search Engine Marketing (SEM) industry.

In 1998, he co-founded Website Results, a Los Angeles based search engine marketing firm which was later purchased in 2000 by 24/7 Media, a public, global, marketing and media company. He served as Vice President of Operations and Business Development at 24/7 Media until 2001, when he started Infosearch Media (ISHM.ob) out of an apartment in Marina Del Rey, CA. With no funding, Mr Lazuka increased revenue from zero to over $10 Million in annual sales within 4 years. As CEO and Chairman of the Board, Mr Lazuka took Infosearch Media public in 2005.

In 2007, Mr Lazuka co-founded Interact Media (www.interactmedia.com), a natural, content-based search engine optimization company and content development service.

Mr Lazuka currently resides in Chardon, OH, where he lives with his wife and 3 sons.
