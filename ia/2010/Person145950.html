Mike Rundle’s Summary

I have been designing and developing for the web since 1996 and in that time I've led projects for many types of companies: Internet startups, Fortune 500 corporations, large and small consulting firms, non-profit organizations and the U.S. Dept. of Defense. In 2004 I co-founded Business Logs, a design firm specializing in corporate blogs and communication consulting. I am also the co-founder of 9rules, an online community and social network that helps independent bloggers get more visibility. 9rules was recognized as the Best Community Site of the Year in 2006 at South By Southwest Interactive festival.

Recently I've been focused on cross-platform user experience design and development for the web, iPhone & Mac. I'm the author of Beak, a Twitter app for the Mac currently in public beta testing and am also working on a variety of iPhone projects in my spare time.

I'm originally from Upstate NY and attended Rochester Institute of Technology graduating with a BS Information Technology specializing in design and usability. I live in Raleigh, NC with my wife Eleni and our black lab Dora.

Mike Rundle’s Specialties:

User experience design, information architecture, user interface design using web standards (XHTML/CSS), user interaction design and prototyping, social media marketing and strategy, Javascript & Ajax (jQuery), Twitter APIs, Objective-C (iPhone & OS X Cocoa APIs), Photoshop, PHP, MySQL, XML, Section 508 & WAI-WCAG accessibility guidelines, Flash & ActionScript programming, XSLT, XUL
