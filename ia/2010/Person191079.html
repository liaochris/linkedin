Steve Smiley’s Summary

My career has been blessed with the many friends, and associates I have acquired over the last 30 years. My current position allows me to utilize all the skills developed to help the printing industry, and my customer improve their products and reduce cost and waste.

My current position allows me to work with pre-press, designers, photographers, CPC's, and Advertising Agencies.
Setting up colour managed work flows, with process control tools for consistent and accurate delivery of print. Acting as a Print Buyer for my customers in packaging, advertising, and publication, while optimizing file structure and format for the best print. Utilizing ISO standards to improve quality while reducing turnaround time and cost in the supply chain. Looking ahead with the requirements to multipurpose files, improved PDF-X work flows, and xml reporting there are many opportunities in our industry to improve.

Working with industry standard groups to improve process for my customers has been my focus for many years.

Currently chair of Print Properties in US, Chair of GWG Colour Committee, ICC member, ISO TC130 US Flexo expert.
