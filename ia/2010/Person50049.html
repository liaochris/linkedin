David A. Cobb’s Summary

Mr. Cobb's professional experience spans thirty-one years in the real estate industry. After graduating from the University of Texas, David was hired by General Homes Corporation, and over the next several years, progressed through its construction management program to the position of Division Manager for New Orleans by the age of 30.

After ten years with the General, Mr. Cobb owned and operated an Arthur Rutenberg franchise in Tampa, Florida, building custom homes in the award-winning communities of Tampa Palms, Hunter's Green, Theresa Arbor, Carrolwood, Belle Meade, The Lakes of Keystone, Van Dyke Farms, and Bayside.

Mr. Cobb recently concluded an eleven year career with Engle Homes as one of their Division Presidents, managing multiple divisions in Florida. Areas of expertise include P and L responsibility, land acquisition and development, single and multi-family product design, supply chain, value engineering, finance and accounting, marketing, and professional sales associate training and development. Significant achievements include the organic start-up of the Engle Homes Southwest Florida division and the development of many well-respected master-planned developments in Sarasota, Fort Myers, and Naples. These desirable communities include Gleneagles at Lakewood Ranch, Reflection Lakes of Fort Myers, Heatherstone at Rookery Pointe, Casa del Lago, Cape Royal, Reflection Key, Reflection Isles, The Shores at Naples, Marbella Lakes at Spanish Wells, Reflection Lakes Naples, and Lago Villagio at Glen Eagle.

Currently, Mr. Cobb is President of Residential Building Science, Inc., a HERS rating and Florida Green Building Coalition certifying company.

Additionally, David is a member of the 2009-2011 Corporate MBA program at Rollins College in Winter Park, Florida.
