Scott Roy’s Summary

Scott is a marketing professional with extensive technology and Agency experience. He is currently working to build a new company in South Florida. Recent endeavors include leading the Marketing Operations at the nation's 50th largest Travel Agency.

He combines a strong research and technology background with a clear marketing focus, to make strategic recommendations, and provide the actionable insight essential to online success. Mr. Roy has created websites and online marketing programs for organizations such as Unilever/Bestfoods Arvida, Global Travel International and Playtex Apparel.

Scott also spent several years with Gartner the world’s leading authority on Information Technology. Additionally, Scott has served as a Strategic Advisor, working to formulate business plans for companies such as Vermont Natural Stoneworks, a startup company formed to produce and sell finished slate products.

Scott has established himself as a branding and web analysis expert, and has been featured and quoted in publications such as Jupiter Communications/Media Metrix Reports, Headcount.com, iMarketing News, and Digitrends Magazine.
