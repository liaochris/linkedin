Jason Wilson’s Summary

He’s worked on projects for Adidas, Adobe, Apple, BEA, Charles Schwab, EA, eBay, Google, Hard Rock, HP, Intel, Janus, Microsoft, Nike, RIM, Sprint, Sun, Wells Fargo and others.
