Matt Gray’s Summary

At MyLogoSource, Inc., we exist to help you grow your business and to help you get a return on your investment in promotional ideas. We design programs that, for example, help drive traffic to your tradeshow booth, get better response to direct mail with lumpy dimensional mailings and more! We take time to find out your target client and pick promotional ideas that deliver your message to the target in memorable way. We don’t just sell you a product. We have ideas that use products to get you noticed and remembered and call the target to take action! The item is the vehicle to deliver your message. Let us help you custom design a program to effectively deliver your message to your target!
