Becky Shankle’s Summary

I specialize in single & multi home residential kitchen design using modular and sustainable materials. My design style is clean, simple, modern and efficient. I specify green finish materials and products that stand up to the wear & tear of daily use and still look great. I'm both budget & eco-conscious.

I'm an advocate of responsible urban living, which includes community & guerrilla gardening projects, promoting locavore habits, & efficient living within a city environment.

Follow me on twitter: @ecomod

Becky Shankle’s Education

  • University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill

    BA , Psychology , 1980 — 1984

    Particular Courses of Influence:

    1: Science and the Arts, Dept. of Physics: I loved the connections between hard science and free form arts examined in this class. Items of discussion included the physical shape & structure of wood cells in Stradivarius violins, architectural elements, and perception of physical surrounding.

    2: Neuropsychology and Perception, Dept. of Psychology & Neurobiology: biological phenomena and activity associated with optical illusion and visual cortex processing, and bridging the gap between psychological behavior and chemistry.

The combination of sensory perception and elements of architecture (space, light, temperature - sensory stimulation, basically) has been a lasting area of interest, mainly hobby.
