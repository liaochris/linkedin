Mark Lewis’s Summary

A seasoned marketing executive with over 20 years of leadership experience in the consumer technology sector. I have strength in building teams, managing product lifecycles, channel management, programs and promotions and building brands.

With broad experience in key areas of marketing and as a veteran of working on executive leadership teams I have been responsible for setting and driving the strategy and executing the tactics under a variety of budget scenarios. Measuring results and understanding the impact to the P&L I view as a key part of my role. My experience spans start-ups to multi-billion dollar companies as well as running my own small business and I am comfortable in any of those settings.

My global experience includes four continents and travel to various countries has given me first hand knowledge of the unique challenges to growing the business there. Managing employees in other countries has given me a broader perspective that makes me more capable as a manager.

I have been fortunate to have worked for some great companies and I am proud of how I have contributed to their continued success and what I have learned every step of the way.
