Tina K. Cooper’s Summary

Tina is a transformative leader with a passion for developing business strategy, aligning people and processes, and improving performance. She help individuals, teams, and organizations tackle complex challenges, develop innovative solutions, and realize sustainable change.

Tina K. Cooper’s Specialties:

Leadership and Business Coaching. Change Management, Organizational Design, Process Re-engineering, IT Service Management, Communication, Performance Management, Business Development, Alternative Conflict Resolution.

Tina K. Cooper’s Experience

  • Director, Cook County Foreclosure Mediation Program

    Center for Conflict Resolution

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    May 2010 — Present (7 months)

    The Center for Conflict Resolution (CCR) launched its Foreclosure Mediation Program in May 2010 as a partner of the Circuit Court of Cook County Mortgage Foreclosure Mediation Program. As the Director of the Program, Tina is responsible for:

    • Program and Process Design
    • Organizational Structure, Staffing, and Training
    • Fiscal Oversight
    • Relationship Management with Circuit Court of Cook County, Defendant's and Plaintiff's Counsel, and Housing Counseling Services.

    Cook County has over 60,000 pending foreclosure cases and anticipates over 50,000 new foreclosure filings in 2010. Mediation is a form of alternative dispute resolution that enables parties to find mutually agreeable solutions outside of Court proceedings.

    Tina K. Cooper, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    September 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Tina K. Cooper is an ICF-certified coach with more than 20 years of experience in business management, leadership and consulting, Tina has coached hundreds of executives, managers and employees in organizations ranging from 5 to 5,000 employees on myriad business management topics.

    Tina specializes in helping leaders successfully navigate business growth and change, with specific focus on:

    - Program and Process Design
