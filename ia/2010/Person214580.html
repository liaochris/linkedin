Wendy Blomseth’s Summary

* WENDY HOUSER BLOMSETH: Entrepreneur, lifestyle & portrait photographer, social media networker, speaker, photo teacher, visual branding expert using photos as a strategic tool.

Owner of InBeaute Photography with 20+ years prof'l photographic experience. Passionate about creating photographic images that enhance your personal brand with authentic images of success, joy, business, career and loving relationships.

Specializes in photo placement online at LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter as well as blogs and websites.

Speaks on (1) personal branding strategies using visual image presentation as a strategic marketing tool and (2) photography as a business and/or hobby.

Partner with husband, Karl Blomseth in Fertile Mind Foto (Learn * Look * Love > Fotography) a photography education business helping individuals take their photography to the next level. We specialize in beginners and intermediate film and digital photographers.
