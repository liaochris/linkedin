Steven Fonseca’s Summary

Dr. Fonseca is a technologist and professionally trained manager with 13 years of experience helping organizations achieve state-of-the-practice results for software development with a focus on distributed systems middleware and applications in mission critical and applied R&D contexts. He has held positions at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Hewlett Packard Labs, British Telecommunications Labs, Intel, and NASA Ames Research Center. His technical interests include service oriented architecture, ontologically-based information systems, enterprise-class middleware, the dynamics of organizational change, and R&D technology transfer. Steven completed his PhD in Computer Science from UC Santa Cruz in collaboration with HP Labs. Steven completed a MS in Computer Science from UC Santa Cruz and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from UC Davis. Steven is also a certified Enterprise Architect (FEAC) and Software Architecture Professional (CMU SEI). He is currently finishing an MBA at UCLA.

Steven Fonseca’s Specialties:

Software Architecture, Information Architecture, Enterprise Architecture, Applied Software Research, Software Engineering Best Practices, Technical Management and Organizational Change
