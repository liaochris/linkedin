Jonathan Evans’s Summary

An experienced business developer specialising in managing and developing software and services business within the Telco and Communications sector, over 10 years of deep expertise in convergence and hosting solutions environments.

Skilled in the areas of business management, market engagement, consultative sales, situation analysis, propositions creation, development of ROI cases, working with and building the appropriate teams to initiate activity that delivers to the highest standard.

My application

Infectious passion; I have a strong network, valuing people and relationships as they provide me with the deep insight you need to succeed.

Results driven; committed whatever the challenge. I have deep domain expertise in IT managed services and have an excellent track record.

Solid delivery; Blue chip foundation, focusing my career developing emerging software and services propositions. I strengthen my individual value having been accountable to investors and responsible for operating businesses.

Achievements

1. I developed and sold some of the first SaaS solutions into the Legal sector, winning top law firms and an award in San Francisco from BlackBerry for enterprise innovation, Oct 08.

2. I led the turnaround of a VC backed SaaS business, managing a team of 25, building out datacentres achieving an exit in 09.

3. In 2004, I developed the B2B SME strategy for a division of Siemens, creating propositions based on application hosting , broadband and unified communications - 50,000 new users, £6million turnover in 3 years.

4. In 2001, I launched Mobile CRM across Siemens achieving large wins in public/private sector valued at £10 million. I owned the Siemens School of Excellence for 'Mobile Business' through to 2003.

5. In 1999, I designed and delivered the largest enterprise mobile solution to one of the most prestigious investments banks in the world and was part of a team to architecture the largest VOIP solution at the time.
