Mary Modney’s Summary

Mary brings more than five years experience in the media industry: radio, new media marketing/sales, newspapers and magazines.

Mary is editor-in-chief/public relations for GreenNurture, the corporate sustainability software company. The turnkey Software-as-a-Service application, GreenNurture.com, engages employees to decrease their company’s environmental impact and increase bottom-line benefits. It educates employees, filters best practices, tracks results, offers transparency through a green report card and rewards engaged participants.

Notable achievements include implementing PR strategy for GreenNurture’s Enterprise launch at DEMO Spring 2010, the world-renowned launch platform for some of the most notable emerging technologies introduced over the past two decades. GreenNurture was the ONLY green technology at the conference and joined such DEMO alumni as Java, E*Trade, Symantec, TiVo and Google’s mobile search.
