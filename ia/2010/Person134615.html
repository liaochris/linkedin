Mark Snow’s Summary

Mark is a seasoned marketing executive with sixteen years of experience. He has extensive functional experience in marketing strategy, market planning, marketing science and leadership. His industry experience includes six years in wireless telecommunications, eight years in cable (both business and consumer markets) and two years in the advertising industry.

Mark is a proven leader who can hire and inspire a marketing group and has a track record of rebuilding and turning around teams. He is both a scientific marketer with extensive applied statistical and analytical skills and an award-winning creative marketer with extensive television, radio, print, direct mail and e-commerce advertising experience. Mark is a big picture thinker who can also drill into the detail to determine the course of action.
