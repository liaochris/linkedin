David Bryan’s Summary

David is a highly motivated and passionate individual which is supported by his career progression to date. He has a strong business and technology focus combined with a personal drive and ambition for ensuring that information technology solutions can be delivered as cost effectively as possible, to achieve real and measurable business benefits.

David’s vision is to combine initiatives such as Open Source, Software as a Service (SaaS) and Cloud Computing to make them accessible to businesses and organisations looking to reduce costs or achieve business growth on a minimal budget.

David’s career has spanned a range of sectors including Public Sector and Government, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Pharmaceutical, FMCG, Automotive, and Energy & Utilities. Over the years Dave’s experience has grown from a highly technical background in software engineering and web applications development to a more business focussed career specialising in IT strategy and business roadmap planning, enterprise architecture, architectural governance, business change management, business analysis and change control management using the ITIL framework.
