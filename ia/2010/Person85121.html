Vipul Patel’s Summary

InnFocus is a strategic consulting group which advises, finances, develops, transacts and repositions hotel real estate throughout the nation. InnFocus is headquartered in Philadelphia, the first established capital of the free world. InnFocus network includes affiliates/partners located throughout the United States who exclusively transact and consult on financing and acquisition/disposition of hotel properties.

Vipul Patel, Founder and Managing Director of InnFocus, is a well-rounded hotel advisor with significant industry experience in hospitality brokerage/finance. Past transactions include (sales closed):
-Sheraton Denver Tech Center
-Former Hilton Denver Tech Center, Current Doubletree
-Doubletree Lake Buena Vista
-Hampton Inn Wisconsin
-Holiday Inn Centennial Airport
-Comfort Suites (Various, CO)
-Clubhouse Inn Portfolio

He has brokered/representated buyers and sellers in the sale or purchase of distressed and performing hotel real estate to global and national level hotel investment companies and high net worth individuals as well as private equity funds, retailers like Best Buy and trust funds. In many cases, Vipul has played the role of a transactional broker, or for lack of better words, representing both sides in the same transaction.

As a result, Vipul is also disciplined in analyzing and providing financial analyses specifically tailored to the hospitality industry including in-depth modeling and return analyses (NPV, DCF, IRR, Cash-on-Cash) as well as provide projections or budgets for new or existing hotel properties.

Vipul Patel’s Specialties:

Mergers and acquisition, debt and equity financing, due diligence, property dispositions, negotiations, construction, development, repositioning, fund investments/acquisitions, joint venture partnerships
