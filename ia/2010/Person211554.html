Victoria Schanen’s Summary

There are three things that drive my career: learning new technology, connecting people to resources, and making complicated processes easier.

As a recruiter, I'm dedicated to helping I.T. professionals find positions that are an excellent match both technically and culturally. I strive to be a good listener and strong advocate for my candidates to ensure the best possible fit. My goals are your goals. Let me know how I can help you succeed.

I look forward to building strong relationships in 2010 and beyond.
