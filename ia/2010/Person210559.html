Michael Dean van Leeuwen’s Summary

Van Leeuwen de Witt (VLDW) has experience, personal approach and balance.

The experience of VLDW lays already in 20 years of HR. We focus on Management, Marketing and Sales positions for small - start up - and large (inter)national companies.

Personal approach means that our customer and our candidate are known by us in a professional way and in a personal way. The situation of our customer is clear and the candidate is in balance. The profile of our customer and the candidate is presented by us.

Balance will be shown in our excellent personal approach. The match for our customer and our candidate lies in a working horizon of a minimum of 3 years working relationship.

The personal interviews are essential, because of the different focus of the customer and candidate. In our experience we have to feel what’s going on in our contacts with the customer and candidates. The situation of the company of the customer and the track record of the candidate is well known.
