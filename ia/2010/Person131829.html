Marc Pitre’s Summary

Although I've worked in many industries, I mainly focus on three specific areas; Healthy Lifestyle, Entertainment/Sports and Energy Technology customers.

My agency Right Here Interactive is a reflection of my desire to help you build market share. Whether you need to complement your existing team with specialized services, or want a complete marketing program, we're right here to deliver results.

We help businesses like yours to create effective strategic plans and execute them using our vast network of professional associates. We'll track your campaigns and provide you opportunities, sales and market insight that you need to stay competitive.

Contact us today and lets talk about how your business can generate more leads and opportunities.
