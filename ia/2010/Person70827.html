Francine Taylor’s Summary

Programming is my life! From the first moment I was introduced to the concept in Business 121 my freshman year at Oregon State University, I knew that this was what I wanted to do.

I started out in C, then moved to C++, VB and now C#.NET. Winforms is what I've been doing most recently, but I can do VB(.NET) and ASP.NET. It's been a while since my mainframe days (oh, please don't ask me to do punchcards :)

My talents extend from the analysis/design area to coding to training and installation. I give great user support, and I'm one of the few programmers that you'll find who actually loves fixing bugs.

I enjoy challenges and learning new things; if I don't know it, just give me a couple of weeks to get acclimated, access to Google, and I'll be ready to rock. For my first programming job I was hired to do C, and I'd never even heard of the language before. In six months I was their senior programmer.

So if you want a versatile programmer with a great attitude who is ready to Code It Your Way, contact me!
