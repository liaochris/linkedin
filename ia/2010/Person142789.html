Michael J. Corbett’s Summary

Could I help any of your clients or customers in the Boston area as Chief Financial Officer or as a financial consultant?

For the past seven years I helped to build William A. Berry & Son, Inc. into a $500 million construction management firm with projects throughout New England, New York and Algeria.

Berry did so well, in fact, that the company was bought out by our largest competitor in order to enter the healthcare, life sciences and higher education markets that they were not able to compete in.

I would appreciate it if you would keep me in mind for opportunities in which I could assist a company in growing or reorganizing during these difficult economic times.
