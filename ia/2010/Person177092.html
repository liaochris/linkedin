Ron Thomas’s Summary

Currently ending a long term project and looking for a corporate opportunity focusing on Strategic Human Resources/Talent Management.

Author, blogger and Senior Level Human Resources strategist with strong focus on Talent Management.

Accomplished and effective senior human resources executive with a history of providing strategic HR and talent management leadership.Proven problem solver that works with a high level of integrity and has a thorough understanding of the dynamic role that HR must play in the future.Dedicated, energetic, inclusive leader and team builder with strong coaching skills and a organizational development mindset that has created and implemented numerous successful strategic initiatives. Led significant organizational changes in core client groups while maintaining a high level of excellence and a reputation for getting the job done.

Author: Co-Author of an upcoming book tentatively titled "Successful New Employee Onboarding Programs to be published by McGraw Hill in 2010

Blogger: StrategyFocusedHR. Widely followed both national and international. Blog focuses on Strategic Impact of HR.

Global: Traveled to China as part of a Senior HR Executive Exchange program sponsored by the Society of Human Resources (SHRM). Delegation met with human resource executives at GM-China & Lenovo. The delegation also met with two graduate business schools and hosted HR professional dinners in both Beijing and Shanghai, meeting with local HR executives during the 2 week stay.

Work has been featured in Workforce Management Magazine, Crain’s New York Business, Chief Learning Officer Magazine, VentureHacks.com, Entrepreneur.com and The Wall Street Journal. Frequent panelist and speaker on Strategic Human Resources and Talent Management..
