Jenn Steele’s Summary

Jenn Steele, a graduate of MIT and the Simmons School of Management, has diverse experience across Internet marketing and online media. Working as a webmaster or IT director since 2000, Jenn has seen several different sides to the industry of internet content and marketing. Jenn has also served as an adjunct professor for Boston University on how to run a law firm from a technology standpoint. She's been blogging for years and has been the face in social media for the legal technology industry as well. While she was blogging and working with her webmaster experience, she realized that she enjoyed blogging more than being a technology executive, and came to work at HubSpot as an Inbound Marketing Consultant.
