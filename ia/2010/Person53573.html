Deborah Chapman’s Summary

Deborah is a Business Development Manager for Questar Partners. Her specialty is business development, executive recruiting, training, and administrative functions. She is a proven leader in generating sales and profitability. Deborah received the Women of Influence Award 2009 from Nashville Business Journal in the "Behind the Scenes Category."

Deborah calls upon over 20 years professional experience in sales and management to achieve corporate staffing goals. Deborah has incorporated innovative ways to connect with her clients and educate them on new resources.

In 2007, Deborah founded 3Strands Networking to offer professional development and a network to encourage and support women in their spiritual, business, and personal goals. 3Strands meets the second Friday of each month at Otter Creek Church, Brentwood, TN from 7:30-9:00 am.

Deborah is a dynamic and enthusiastic communicator with a passion and interest in other people and their development. She can be reached at Questar Partners, 100 Winners Circle Ste 160, Brentwood, TN, dchapman@questarstaffing.com, 615-371-0111.
