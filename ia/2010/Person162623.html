  • Freelance Photographer and Writer

    Peter West Carey Photography

    (Photography industry)

    December 2003 — Present (6 years 11 months)

    I purchased my first 35mm camera directly after high school and have been enthralled with the media ever since. Experimenting first with black and white basics and moving on eventually to digital photography, I have always been pleased with how much there is to learn about capturing light and presenting it.

    Photography and travel have always gone hand in hand for me. I prefer grand, technical and powerful shots that help the viewer feel what it's like in a far off land or right around their corner.

    I enjoy making connections with others and getting the chance to tell a bit of their story and that's why I enjoy portrait and wedding photography as much as I do. I've been shooting weddings for over 17 years and enjoy the challenge of capturing the uniqueness that makes every wedding a very special day.

    If you are in need of a photographer for your wedding, event, portrait or product shoot, drop me a line at 206-973-8309 or email me at peter@peterwestcarey.com.
