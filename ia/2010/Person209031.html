Thomas Witter’s Summary

Senior executive with over 25 years experience in Health, Benefits, and Information Technology industries. Experienced in developing successful positioning strategies.

My goal is "Continuous Improvement" in everything I do.

Virtual Benefits Administrator is a robust, leading edge technology solution for claims processing and benefit administration. Our functionality includes medical, dental, vision, and life and disability. FSA, HRA, and HSA administration will be released soon. Essentially all aspects of benefit and plan management can be handled by VBA.

Flexibility is the hallmark of VBA. Along with our comprehensive Functionality and leading-edge Technology, VBA provides the best combination of Performance and Value in the marketplace.

VBA is also a software development shop with our own proprietary data exchange (EDI) tool called VBAXchange, which allows us to handle any format in or out very efficiently.

VBA also has a strong call center component and a complete notes section that quickly allows access to all records for a particular member, provider, or plan. We have also just released a very dynamic Care Management / Case Management module and a Process Automation feature.

Our technology is Smart Client and our adjudication is offline, which maximizes system efficiency. We're well positioned for growth in the Health Care and Health Plan management markets.
