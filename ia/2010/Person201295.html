Terry Humphris’s Summary

Terry comes from a customer focused technical background (from chip level electronics and assembler to .NET software development) giving him the skills to manage and converse with a wide range of people. He is a Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer with a Bachelor of Information Technology with majors in Project Management and Human Resources.

Terry started his career as a data technician with the New Zealand Post Office. After a promotion to Senior Technician, he spent two years as an Instructor at the Auckland Technical Training Centre teaching data communications and microprocessor theory. He was then seconded to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and worked in Asia and Europe for eight years as the Senior Technical Specialist. Returning to New Zealand, he started his own business, Kiwi Carvings. In 2002, he graduated with a Bachelor of Information Technology from the Nelson Marlborough Institute of Technology and for two years, contracted in the Nelson area building Windows, Windows Compact and Web based systems.

In 2005, Terry started a three year job with PolyMEDIA where his primary role was adding functionality to a large US Website using a range of Microsoft technologies.

After PolyMEDIA, Terry spent a year with Datacraft (NZ) in Wellington working on the technical challenges associated with splitting Telecom(NZ) into three core businesses.

In November 2008, Terry moved to Bestquote Ltd In Nelson to develop software to help the building industry. By August 2009, this was completed and he returned to PolyMEDIA (now FuseIT) as Head of Technology.
