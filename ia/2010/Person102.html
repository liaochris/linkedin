Catalina Flore’s Summary

What is the best of what you know now?
How greater would your life be if you lived each day to the best of your ability in accordance with the best that you know?
How do you find your next best?
It all has started with my personal breakthrough...I have realised that what moves my heart and mind the most is detecting and experiencing human greatness. Whether it is a sport performance, a piece of modern art, beautiful music, a child expressing her talents, somebody overcoming adversities and reaching unimaginable levels of achievement and fulfillment, or business performance they all are a manifestation of the human spirit defeating imposed limitations.
I left an extensive and successful career in international banking to pursue my purpose – to be a catalyst to greatness and great being to highly commited professionals, to inspire, challenge and guide them through their personal transformation to live by design and choice.
Since 2005, I have been coaching internationally, among my clients being executive bankers, entrepreneurs, corporate mothers to be, talents climbing the corporate ladder. I believe the leader of the next generation company lives consciously, purposely, invests in his performance, success and contributes beyond himself. He invests in his and his family’s wellbeing. She builds a mind & body set that allows her to go beyond the current limitations. She re-learns to be authentic and free.
I believe in the powerful choice to find the best way forward and deliver the results you are committed to: not reasons why you can't reach those results. Which will you choose for you? Reasons or results?
I partner with my clients to construct practical plans, which link individual needs, life and career aspirations and current performance to move them towards their TOP vision.
Based in Amsterdam. Mother of three, I have an international clientele and I am passionate about helping companies becoming more inspiring, fun, high performing environments.
