Daisy Nguyen’s Summary

I am honored everyday to be in such an exciting and vibrant industry, surrounded by some of the best in business.

Having been in the apartment industry for over 10 years, successful in many capacities, cumulating in several industry awards, I now bring my expertise, passions and talents to help others achieve excellence. RENT SODA is my firm - providing expertise in the areas of operations, marketing, and development. To read more about me: http://blog.rentsoda.com/about-daisy-nguyen/

I have over 30+ recommendations (on LinkedIn alone) from every level and aspect of the industry. From clients, to partners, to supervisors, to vendors, to collegues, as well as staff I have managed. From every aspect of the industries I have served - finance, accounting, marketing, operations, and development. I have been successful, and can assist in your pursuit of excellence.

I participate on 4 committees at Minnesota Multi-Housing Association (MHA is a local apartment association) and serve on the board of directors of a local non-profit affordable housing provider, Aeon. I write 2 industry blogs. I not only serve my clients, I am committed to the success of the multi-family apartment industry. In doing so, I stay connected from the ground level up and can better serve my clients and appointments.

Daisy Nguyen’s Specialties:

From new construction consulting-design/branding/marketing/lease-up plans. To property repositioning & rebranding for today's market. To streamlining operations & marketing-providing additional focus in areas of new/troubled apartments. To feasibility & market studies.

Well-rounded background with a thorough understanding of accounting, accounting systems, Yardi, RMO, fee-managed, development models, budgeting, marketing, branding, social media marketing, policies/procedures, training, etc.
