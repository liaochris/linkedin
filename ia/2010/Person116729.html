Kimberly Monday’s Summary

Kimberly Monday, president and founder, recognized eight years ago the need for a full-service marketing firm that can partner with clients and bring them to a higher level, helping drive new revenue. Kimberly formed Monday Group with the goal to provide client’s with a one-stop-shop solution: Marketing + Public Relations + Advertising, utilizing 21st Century technologies.

We combine an innovative approach with our team’s integrated skill set and extensive experience in Marketing, Public Relations, Advertising, and Technology by becoming an extension of the client’s company.

Monday Group thinks “outside the box,” and becomes your partner, not just a vendor.

Monday Group is an award-winning communications firm that partners with our clients to increase their revenue. We use a multi-tiered approach including: marketing, technology and communications. Our marketing blends branding, positioning and multimedia to build awareness. Using technology such as: (Search Engine Optimization, eCommerce and database tools, we help you achieve your objectives. Communications, including creative Public Relations and Strategic Advertising, expand your reach and visibility. Our unique full-service solution, together with our proven experience and creative approach, ensure your success.
