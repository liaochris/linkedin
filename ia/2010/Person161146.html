Paul McQuade’s Summary

MC2 Technical Recruitment Ltd is a specialist in Contract and Permanent Recruitment providing white collar engineering personnel to a variety of clients on a global scale.
As part of the MC2 Group, it shares facilities with MC2 Executive Recruitment (providing Executive Search to various Industry Sectors) and MC2 Training Solutions. Our Training Division provides a wide variety of courses to Industrial Clients throughout the UK.
