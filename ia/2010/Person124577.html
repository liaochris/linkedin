Ian Loew’s Summary

Lform Design prides itself on helping clients build, manage and leverage their reputations and services to help grow their organizations and businesses. We strategize with clients to create meaningful experiences. Having over 30 years of combined experience in professional graphic and web design experience has allowed us to have an in-depth knowledge of the necessary marketing needs and solutions to the ever-expanding World Wide Web. By bringing a unique focus and approach to marketing, business and organizations hit rates have increased from 25% to 300% on their web sites. Often time's web designers use difficult jargon and do not facilitate a constructive environment. We pride ourselves on taking the client from start to finish with sincere hand holding. Lform Design encourages our clients to comment throughout the design and development process.
