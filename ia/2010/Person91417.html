Jay Weber’s Summary

Dr. Weber has a broad record of technological innovation and entrepreneurism, having co-founded multiple companies, run technical conferences, earned over 20 patents, published dozens of research papers, written lots of code, and co-won two "Product of the Year" awards.

Jay is currently Co-founder and Chief Technical Officer of www.vivaty.com, a Kleiner Perkins/Mohr-Davidow funded social gaming startup. Jay was pivotal in defining the company's strategy, raising Angel funding, recruiting the CEO, and pitching and closing the Series A funding. In addition Jay built-out and ran the Engineering, QA, Release, and Operations teams that shipped a web-based multiplayer 3D social game, integrated with Facebook, AIM, and Target/Coke marketing campaigns.

Jay has served on the Boards of ScholarSafe, a web-based college-savings startup, Zeum, a non-profit San Francisco-based Media Arts museum, B3TV, and Perceptual Robotics, Inc. (acquired by divine, Inc.).
