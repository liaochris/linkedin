Kiana Franulic’s Summary

My practice assists individuals in need of affordable legal representation in the areas of debtor/collector, business and family law. As your legal representative, I am able to work around your schedule to provide you with legal services after normal business hours as well as weekends.

Additionally, the Law Offices of Kiana Franulic can assist you with forming and running your small business. I provide representation to small businesses in need of legal counsel and advice. Please feel free to contact my office by e-mail twenty-four hours a day or by phone between the hours of 8:00 - 6:30 p.m.
