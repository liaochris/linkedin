Arlene G. Pritzker’s Summary

Ms. Pritzker is a multi-disciplinary Senior CAD Designer/Drafter in the engineering and architectural fields. She is experienced in transit and rail, bridges and tunnels, environmental, energy and utilities having worked on various municipal, industrial, and military assignments.

She works well under pressure to simultaneously meet multiple project deadlines and is equally effective working in a team environment as working independently based on general direction.

With extensive experience in coordinating with engineering staff, other technicians, project managers and other team members, work that she is assigned is completed accurately and quickly.

Having shown her versatility and professionalism in roles of different levels of responsibility and authority, she is capable of assuming a number of possible roles within an organization.

Ms. Pritzker has been responsible for project scheduling and management, assisted in preparation of bids and bill of materials, participated in the field surveying of new projects and inspection of projects in progress, QAQC’d CAD drawings, interviewed job applicants and participated in hiring decisions, supervised five full-time manual drafters, trained new drafting employees, handled inventory control, created graphics for public hearings, studies, reports, and litigation, designed and created the Silicon Valley Rapid Transit Corridor (SVRTC) project team website from original HTML code and provided weekly update services from 2004 through 2006 when the website’s use was discontinued.

She possesses excellent communication and problem solving skills, is well organized, self-motivated and has the ability to learn new concepts & techniques quickly. She has proven these facts by learning both AutoCAD and ICEM Computational Fluid Dynamics Auto Hexa 4.2 on her own.

Arlene G. Pritzker’s Specialties:

AutoCAD Civil 3D 2010, Sketchup Pro and ICEM Computational Flow Dynamics Auto Hexa 4.2.2
