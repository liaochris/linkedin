Kabin Thomas’s Summary

I have been a professor of music at the University of Arkansas in Fayetteville for 10 years and it has been a joy. I have positively influenced the lives of over 8000 students here at the UA and have won several teaching awards for my creative, entertaining and educational lectures about the awesome power of Classical music. I have planned all aspects of, hosted and moderated events with actor James Earl Jones; violin virtuoso, Midori; Jazz great, Kitty Margulis; Silk Roads artist, WuMan; legendary Opera impresario Sarah Caldwell; Sony Country Music Vice President of A and R, Mark Wright, among others. I have even had the honor of entertaining several thousand children with my live Classical Music show, "Symphony Sam." (http://www.symphonysamshow.com)

As an administrator, I have been involved with every aspect of event planning, curriculum development and the analysis and creation of budgets. I am familiar with and proficient with many desktop platforms and have always been considered a blessing wherever I have been. I am also very good at the cultivation and development of resources, donors, patrons and benefactors.

Kabin Thomas’s Specialties:

An accomplished Teacher, Lecturer, Performing Arts Professional with extensive experience in managing personnel, coaching, mentoring and instructing students and working with the public as a performing artist/lecturer/teacher.
