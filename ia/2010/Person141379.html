David Shaw | 蕭宇弘’s Summary

Industrial Designer / Product Designer / Invention Developer able to contribute to all phases of product development from concept to design implementation, 3D CAD/CAID, design strategy, program management, and manufacturing liaison.

Possess broad experience in creating innovative, functional, and aesthetically appropriate products for a wide variety of industry sectors ranging from consumer, to high technology, toys, sports, scientific, and medical devices.

Serve a wide variety of clients and joint-venture partners ranging from multi-national corporations to contract manufacturers, start-up companies, design firms, and inventors.

Able to devise unique product designs slated for efficient manufacturing as well as visualize conceptual designs for compelling product idea pitches used to entice venture capital investment or to elicit meaningful feedback from focus groups.

Adept at identifying new opportunities for improvement and differentiation. Determined to contribute game-changing, market-disrupting product design solutions for joint-venture partners. Resulting designs have a proven track record of helping clients get, save, and make money while providing superior value to consumers.

Experience lecturing in Universities in USA and Asia and evangelizing benefits of industrial design to clients and training junior designers and students.

Fluent in Mandarin Chinese and English facilitating cooperation with international clients and manufacturing suppliers.

Guided by the core philosophy that good design is about being "considerate."
