Adrienne Zoble’s Summary

My favorite saying is "It's not what you spend in marketing; it's how you spend it." We put BUSINESS OWNERS AND EXECUTIVES on the paths toward GREATER REFERRALS AND ADDITIONAL BUSINESS from existing clients/customers, i.e., working smart instead of working hard.

I specialize in EFFECTIVE, YET INEXPENSIVE MARKETING for PRICE CONSCIOUS CLIENTS reluctant to spend money on marketing. We look at the entire picture, rather than home in on an isolated strategy that is not given enough time to be successful.

I speak and consult nationally, present a monthly teleseminar for the National Association of the Remodeling Industry (NARI), and present two monthly local Marketing Forums in Fort Collins, Colorado. In June 2008, I'll be speaking for the ninth consecutive year at InfoComm International (this year in Las Vegas).

On my website is our "Do-able Marketing Plan" workbook, available by itself or with four hours of COACHING AND HANDHOLDING from me. There are four e-books (including a Schmoozing Calendar) to help business owners and executives sell more in less time.
Learn how effortlessly you can GROW YOUR BUSINESS THROUGH MARKETING that requires only MINUTES A DAY.

• Prepare a "Do-able Marketing Plan."
• Decrease your marketing costs by 85%.
• Market consistently, without detracting from business as usual.
• Sell more in less time.
• Increase your numbers of referrals.
• Strengthen your average sales.
• Generate additional business from existing clients/customers.

Remember, marketing is not only about getting new client but PAYING ATTENTION TO AND MAINTAINING the clients you already have.
