Chris Turner’s Summary

Eighteen years of experience leading strategic development efforts with regards to battery and energy storage systems with hands-on experience with many battery technologies including lithium ion, lithium polymer, lithium primary, nickel metal hydride, rechargeable zinc air batteries, as well as renewable technologies such as photovoltaic, and fuel cells. Diversified experience on all sides of the battery industry from cell manufacturer to battery pack integrator to major OEM customer.

Chris Turner’s Specialties:

Various battery chemistries including lithium ion (Li-ion), lithium polymer, lithium primary, nickel metal hydride (NiMH), rechargeable Zinc Air (Zn-Air).
