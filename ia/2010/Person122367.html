Lauren Mclean’s Summary

Lauren is passionate about increasing the impact of philanthropy and working with individuals to envision their giving potential or to align their social change values and goals with their philanthropic strategies. Guided by her passion for catalytic philanthropy and informed by her background in strategic planning, social change organizing, environmental advocacy, and socially responsible investment management, Lauren works with clients to develop results oriented strategic giving plans – both short term and long-term - to realize their social change goals.
