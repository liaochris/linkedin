John Levine’s Summary

I provide real estate services for companies who need to acquire or shed space used for their business. My expertise is in negotiating leases and sales of offices, factories, warehouses, retail and investment properties. Primary geographic area is New Jersey. My company has affiliates worldwide providing expertise in commercial and industrial real estate. We give multinational and multilocation companies the ability to focus on their varied geographical needs through one company, eliminating the need to establish relationships with different brokers for each transaction.

John Levine’s Specialties:

My lease negotiation skills provide tenants represented with the knowledge of potential economic consequences of the many clauses of commercial leases for business properties. This gives my clients the opportunity to try to negotiate a better deal or at least know what the potential exposures to their economic health may be.
