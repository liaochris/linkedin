Lisa J.B. Peterson, CFP®’s Summary

Lantern Financial, LLC, conveniently located in downtown Boston, provides financial planning to help you reach your goals and fulfill your dreams. If you are seeking a financial advisor that truly has your best interest at heart, you have come to the right place. We offer comprehensive, independent financial advice and we have no hidden agenda - we are dedicated to helping you live a more satisfying and successful financial life.

Here at Lantern Financial LLC, we are dedicated to helping young professionals and families navigate their prime saving years, as well as achieve their future goals and dreams. I am excited to help my clients live more successful and intentional financial lives.

Due to the fact that our model is fee-only and that we accept no commissions of any kind, my clients know that I am not trying to sell them any products and that my advice is truly non-biased.

For years, I have been listening as friends and family have sought a financial planner that would both take them seriously and give them true advice (as opposed to a veiled sales pitch), even when they don’t have much money to invest. I am pleased to be able to offer this peace of mind that has been so elusive to so many.

Lantern Financial is truly comprehensive. I can help with everything from investments, insurance, retirement, tax and estate planning to debt management, cash flow analysis and budgeting. Above all, I am interested in building a strong, lasting relationship with my clients.

Please visit our website, www.lantern-financial.com, to learn all about the company.

If you are seeking a financial planner who is truly interested in your success, please give me a call at 617-482-2700. I will be honored to help you. Additionally, I will be grateful if you forward my information along to any friends or family members who may be interested in a brighter financial future.

I look forward to hearing from you!
