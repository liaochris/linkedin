Carolyn Dickerson’s Summary

My personal goal is to help businesses change their stumbling blocks into stepping stones by developing an organizational strategy that identifies the next steps needed to move an organization toward increased market visibility, increased profits and develop a more productive workforce.

Research, marketing, sales and customer service are not just jobs to me, they are my passion! I'm a life-long student and teacher who researches and learns emerging marketing technologies that will increase an organization's lead generation and/or sales volume via their website(s) while servicing the current client base.

I am capable of developing online marketing strategies that enhance off-line direct marketing campaigns for optimum ROI by leveraging website analytics such as Google Analytics and Omniture to determine referring search terms from search engines, content popularity, referring URLs, percent of visitors who visit the homepage and the website's bounce rates.

I enjoy leveraging my experiences and talents to integrate online marketing strategies into an organization's traditional offline marketing efforts to make their website revenue and/or lead generation channel outperform expectations and to have a lot fun while I'm working, too!
