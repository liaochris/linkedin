Stelle Smith’s Summary

Since joining Sage (when it was still Medical Manager Health Systems) my experience has been directed in the adoption and use of technology in the outpatient healthcare market. The focus of my training and expertise has shifted from the ancillary business staff, to the clinical staff to the providers of service to holistic patient care. My involvement as an early adopter of personal health record technology through WebMD along with my clinical experience in bringing context to the concept of electronic health record software has afforded me the opportunity to advise a wide variety of physicians and healthcare entities in how to navigate successfully electronic medical record implementation to maximize efficiencies and speed up return on investment, as well as clarify new goals in clinical automation.
