Peter Mandeau’s Summary

Developed, produced and managed world-class events for a wide and diverse range of organizations and celebrities for over 25 years. Currently President of PM Events and Productions, Inc.; a Boston-based sports and events management company. Licensed Personal Manager and Booking Agent.

Broad range of diverse talents and skills spanning across a number of industries from professional sports to business development to management. From 2004-2006, Chief Operating Officer and General Manager of The World TeamTennis Boston Lobsters at Harvard University (www.bostonlobsters.net); a national professional tennis league founded by Billie Jean King.

A partial list of additional groups and clients includes: Harvard University, the Pilot Pen Professional Tennis Championships, Tenacity, Olympian Greg Louganis, Professional Baseball players, professional musicians, Co-produced national and regional television broadcast for ESPN2, Comcast and The Tennis Channel, Pro-Celebrity events, the United States Tennis Association and numerous charitable organizations.

Producer and promotion of Claws for a Cause charity compilation CD (http://www.pmeventsandproductions.com/claws.htm) to benefit: Women's Sports Foundation, The Joey Fund, Tenacity and Tennis Against Breast Cancer. Founder and producer for five years of annual fundraising event for drug and alcohol education center in Connecticut. Nominated for a Presidential Point of Light Foundation Award.

Over 25 years of professional tennis coaching experience including most recently as Harvard University Associate Head Tennis Coach. Served as Special Events Director and Stadium Emcee for the Pilot Pen Tennis Championships, in New Haven, CT.

Donated extensive time advising and mentoring with youth groups and individuals over the past years including as a member of Harvard's Lowell House Senior Common Room, AFC Mentoring, Tenacity, BAGLY, Coach of Harvard Men's Tennis Team, USPTA Youth Tennis Clinics and many more.

Peter Mandeau’s Specialties:

Representing musicians and comedians for gigs. All aspects professional sports franchises, sports marketing, venue operations, sponsor development, broadcasting (producer, commentator and interviewer) and event production. Professional and NCAA Div. tennis coach. Large-scale event management/ operations, vendor operations,director and professional emcee.
