Jordan Scott’s Summary

My name is Jordan Scott and I am a visual communicator with a passion for Motion Design.

I'm drawn to all facets of design especially motion design including the physics behind how things move in space. I am obsessed with the challenge of recreating these real-world movements and exploring this in my personal and commercial work. I'm quick to adapt and extremely dedicated and passionate towards all my projects.

Aside from design, I often find myself falling in love over and over again with film, photography, animation, typography, music, and storytelling. In my free time you can most likely find me at shows, looking for inspiration online, traveling, eating and sharing my wife’s delicious food, getting tattooed, connecting with other creatives, and skateboarding. Coincidentally these are all examples of where I find inspiration for my work.
