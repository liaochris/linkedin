Kelly Meassick, PMP’s Summary

I have over 20 years of experience in the areas of program management, executive management, consulting, and training. I co-founded Charter Performance Management Group, a company that develops and implements Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS) and provides Project Management and Earned Value Management training. Before starting Charter Performance, I co-founded two successful software companies.
