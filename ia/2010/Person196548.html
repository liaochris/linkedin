Steve Tsuruda’s Summary

Tsunami Racing is a next generation motorsports business.

The team plans to have entrants in all levels of the newly formed “Road to Indy” ladder system, that was announced late in 2009.

Tsunami Racing plans to enter a full time entrant into the IZOD IndyCar Series, with a second entry as a strong possibility.

The team will be a pioneer in the Indy Racing League, as it will be the first team to have entrants in all four series of the Indy Racing League.

The team will build its program so not only drivers have the opportunity to advance to each series through scholarships and other funding opportunities but the plan on grooming engineers, mechanics and the business side of racing in marketing and media sales, client activation and hospitality.

It's not racing..its providing racing and building value..that's the business of racing.

Steve Tsuruda’s Specialties:

Our team was incubated with the Indy Racing League designed specifically to leverage the series to bring value to business.

We do not treat sponsorship as a way to pay for racers to go racing, as the majority of the teams still do, without any ROI. We value your ROI as an investment in the growth of your business.
