Greg Kellogg’s Summary

Greg turned a military intelligence career into an information technology one, spanning everything from systems analysis and test to training and software development.

He currently serves as a Senior Solutions Architect working for Open Text Corporation, the largest provider of ECM systems and solutions.

Greg has also enjoyed a long and rewarding secondary career as a producer of fantasy football content for a variety of web sites ranging from his personal site (Komments.com) to major media outlets like FOXSports.com and SportsIllustrated.com. He is currently focused on the Fantasy Sports Group family of web sites. You can see his work at http://www.FantasyU.com or http://www.FFReport.com or listen to his podcast at http://www.BlogTalkRadio.com/Fantasy-Sports-Group.
