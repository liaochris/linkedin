Sara Yule’s Summary

I am a producer for a boutique production company - Wiggle Puppy Productions. I have worked on many films in an array of producer roles as well as consulted for first and second time producers. We made one feature, Urban Ground Squirrels – a romantic cult-like comedy. It was great for a first time film and still stands up to this day. (I was ONLY 21 when we were in production), shot on 35mm, and I raised over 600K for it with my partner (film is expensive). In the end it was a great learning experience and like any first time we'll always have a love hate relationship with UGS.

Currently most of my work consists of band related videos, documentaries, and concert films/DVD’s. I am always looking to make things more logical, efficient, and organized - usually better. It's in my blood. My directing partner Mark fills in my gaps (& vice versa).

Sara Yule’s Specialties:

I am a key communicator. I am known for getting my clients the most "bang for their buck". I can work magic into a budget. Other specialties: contracts, negotiations, research & the general framework that keeps a production tight. I am very detail oriented & often hired for micro-management (although I prefer the alternative, the option to assemble the right team where each member can shine vise-a-vie their own skill-set). I ooze common sense & am hyper aware - both great Producer qualities.

    Wiggle Puppy Productions was created in 1998 by partners in crime, Mark Krieglstein and Sara Yule, as a Chicago based boutique multi-media production company. Their first major endeavor was a 35mm comedic feature entitled Urban Ground Squirrels. In 2000 they collaborated with MTV on their award winning series True Life. With a handful of indie films under their belt they branched into music cutting their teeth on Phish covering their Millennium event. Since, we have done film & video work w/ a variety of bands inc.
