Mark Buchholz, PMP’s Summary

Current Project Management Professional (PMP), recertified in 2008 for the second time. Over 25 years of professional experience, with more than 18 years managing government projects and programs with an expertise in program/project management, and contracting processes, as well as governance, scheduling, and performance based acquisition. Published author with numerous speaking engagements in various project management topics.
