Bob Haefner’s Summary

Mr. Haefner is COO/CFO and co-founder of Enlyten LLC, the exclusive marketing arm of new and exclusive absorption and delivery systems for nutraceutical and supplement technologies. Mr. Haefner owns the Oklahoma and Wichita, Kansas markets for The Entrepreneur Authority (TEA), the premier franchise advising firm and is a Business Advisor with Business Transitions, LLC. Mr. Haefner was a founder of National Hall of Records in June 2007 and currently holds the Managing Director position. He is a Certified Public Accountant and has held numerous officer and board positions with INC. 500 and other emerging companies.

Mr. Haefner joined InternetYellowPages.com in May 2005. Prior to joining InternetYellowPages.com as EVP, CFO and COO, Mr. Haefner was Executive Vice President, Chief Operating Officer and Chief Financial Officer of ShapeXpress from its inception until January 2005. He helped drive ShapeXpress from a startup company to a trend setting franchisor with 250+ franchises in less than eighteen months. Before ShapeXpress, Mr. Haefner held the CFO and COO positions for Wave3, a telecommunications marketing and licensing company and EnerGreens, Inc, a nutrition company. Mr. Haefner has also held the executive positions with SkillPath, the largest public training company in North America, and with a manufacturing software company. Mr. Haefner also had tenure with KPMG (Peat Marwick), a Big 5 accounting firm, and was a primary auditor for several Fortune 500 companies.

Mr. Haefner is currently the COO and CFO of Enlyten LLC (a marketing company), a Manager and Co-Founder of Business Transitions, LLC and is the CEO of Haefner Enterprises (both are franchise, career transition and entrepreneurial consulting companies).

Bob Haefner’s Specialties:

CPA, Franchise Center of Excellence graduate, IFA, YPA, DMA, ADP

Mr. Haefner has a considerable background in finance, marketing, sales and operations both nationally and internationally. Mr. Haefner has extensive experience in both the private and public sectors including a diverse background in franchising, licensing and emerging businesses.

Mr. Haefner has been integral in the training and development of numerous entrepreneurs, franchisees, agents, and business owners.
