Chris Marhefka’s Summary

Chris graduated from the University of Florida’s Warrington College of Business with a Bachelors of Science in Finance and a minor in Entrepreneurship. During his undergraduate time, Chris was heavily involved in student government and several student organizations while serving as Chapter President of Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity in 2006-2007.

Chris currently works in the health, wellness and fitness industry consulting clients on fitness and nutrition, personal training, selling and distributing nutritional and weight loss supplements. Chris has a goal to start a chain of fitness clubs.

Currently Chris is an active member of the University of Florida Alumni Association, and Sigma Phi Epsilon. He also regularly volunteers with the Alachua County Humane Society, Habitat for Humanity, and serves on the Relay for Life Committee for The American Cancer Society.
