Ashley Munday’s Summary

Barrett Values Centre is an international organization and consulting network in over 60 countries.

I’m a former ad agency executive who participated in Native American purification lodges on the weekends. I spent a month studying with Tibetan Buddhists in Dharmsala, India and I scour fashion magazines. I’ve read Ken Wilber books as bedtime stories and love nothing more than to hike through tree-covered mountains. I’ve been a Collaborative Communication Trainer and a Book Editor.

As part of the Network and Thought Leadership team, my primary function is to support Barrett Values Centre in becoming a global leader in the exploration of the role of values. On a practical level, I study the work of our 2500 affiliated consultants and share information through consortiums, public speaking, video interviews and case stories.

I am passionate about the integrity and power of the Barrett Seven Levels Model. Above all, I believe in caring - deeply and compassionately - for all people.

The core products of the organization are the Cultural Transformation Tools. These tools have been used in over 1000 organizations to support leaders in understanding the people and the culture, and to create new conversations that have a positive impact on employee fulfillment, customer satisfaction and overall organizational success and sustainability.
