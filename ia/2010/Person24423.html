Hector Vazquez’s Summary

At Bizilla, our mission is simple: to help connect people who want to sell a business with people who want to buy a business. We provide business owners and brokers with flexible options for listing their business online. For buyers, we offer helpful tools such as our saved listings feature and customized email alerts to make finding the right business easier.

Unlike other business for sale Web sites, our goal is to be more than just a search tool. We strive to provide our customers with the resources and tools they need to succeed. Our weekly blog goes beyond "buying and selling 101" to offer real insight into the ins and outs of being a successful entrepreneur.

Bizilla was founded by experienced entrepreneurs. Frustrated by business for sale Web sites that were cumbersome to use and cluttered with ads, we decided to apply our success as entrepreneurs and create Bizilla.

I have spent over 20 years building my business management skills. I have owned three companies throughout the course of my career and I have a broad background encompassing senior management, sales, marketing and project management across a variety of industries. For the past seven years, I have been a business broker and sole principal of TheHMIG. I also sit on the Board of Directors for the Virginia Hispanic Chamber of Commerce.
