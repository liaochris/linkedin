Darren Morris’s Summary

Darren Morris is Chief Executive Officer and Chairman of Darren Morris Industries, an international business group of seven businesses with two additional affiliations and partnerships.

Darren Morris Industries is essentially a collection of firms dedicated to serving their clients’ needs and objectives on the local, state, federal and international levels. Mr. Morris serves a wide-array of clients including corporations, associations, foreign governments, celebrity clientele and political clients.

Morris is also affiliated with one of Washington’s premier government relations firms, The Livingston Group, as a consultant providing assistance in three practice areas: Health Care & Pharmaceuticals, International Relations/Business Development, and Science/Technology & Telecom.

Morris’s close relationships with elected officials and their staffs in the halls of power around the world – combined with his skills, experience, and relationships with the people to whom they listen, key decision makers, media outlets and public opinion leaders – provide the results for clients that has led to his reputation as one of the leading public affairs strategists in the United States.

Over the past fifteen-plus years, Morris has served as a political strategist and communications consultant to six presidential campaigns, four national political conventions, and over 100 political campaigns throughout the world. Furthermore, he has represented the leading corporations in the United States and on three other continents in their goals related to business development, political strategy, legislative affairs, public relations and image management, as well as numerous parliamentary candidates and foreign political parties.
