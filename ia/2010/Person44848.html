Crystal Gurin’s Summary

Vice President of eMarketer -- publisher of eMarketer's media properties. When I'm not selling and managing ad sales, I'm handling corporate marketing and partnerships for the company.

Founded Creative Media Strategies, Inc. in 2001, following the acquisition of Cybereps by Interep Interactive. eMarketer is CMS's primary client.

Prior to Creative Media Strategies, Gurin was Co-Founder, CMO and EVP of Cybereps, Inc., which grew into one of the top 10 interactive ad sales and marketing companies. At Cybereps, co-managed a national sales force and provided the creative and managerial leadership for all corporate and client marketing efforts. Also active in high-level strategic sales and the development of integrated media programs. Created and published Totalwoman.com, turning it into one of the top women’s networks on the web within months of launch, and was instrumental in building eMarketer, IMDb.com and Andover.net into leading media brands in their categories.

In the years before Cybereps, was founding Director of Sales & Marketing at PCTV, producer of The Computer Chronicles and Internet Café television series. At PCTV, established and ran a successful ad sales organization for two weekly broadcast series and two daily cable shows on technology. Launched The Internet Café, secured international distribution for all series, developed syndicated radio programming, and spearheaded the company’s early entry to the Web.

Also held senior management and marketing positions at WHYY TV/FM, New Jersey Network (NJN), WITF/TV, Philadelphia College of Art (University of the Arts), and the Opera Company of Philadelphia. She was Co-Founder and President of PTV Reps, Inc., a company formed to work with advertisers to network the nation’s public TV stations. Gurin was also a partner in Epilog Television Productions, developing programming and managing sales activities.

Crystal Gurin’s Specialties:

Specialize in high-level strategic sales, marketing consultation and client relationship management. Extensive cross-media experience: online, radio and television.
