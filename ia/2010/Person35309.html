Chad DeBolt’s Summary

DeBolt Interactive is a full service Internet Marketing Agency for local businesses and professionals. We specialize in organic Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Online Lead Capture Systems, and Social Media Marketing.

How are we different? First of all...thanks for asking! Seriously though, we are different because we actually care about local small businesses and their success with online marketing. We want to help you in actually reaching the goals and aspirations that you had when you first created your website.

Tell us if this sounds familiar – Your business is doing well for itself and then someone tells you that you need a website for whatever reason. You hire a web design firm who tells you that they also do online marketing or SEO so you are excited! What you end up paying for is a flashy, pretty website that doesn't do a single thing for your business. We absolutely hate web design firms who say they do SEO and really don't. They are our arch enemy!

We only focus on getting real tangible results that you can actually measure! We know the Internet can be a daunting thing for business owners and we want to partner with you to make sure you are maximizing every dollar you spend on advertising your business.

What are our goals for each client?

Drastically cut their advertising expenses
Increase their profits
Automate the entire online marketing process
Get them found online (many times to the first page of Google)
