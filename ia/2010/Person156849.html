Eric Sanders’s Summary

Eric Sanders is an OD economist, a learning and organization development consultant who helps clients achieve measurable results better, faster and/or cheaper through investment in people. His key area of expertise is clearly defining learning and development initiatives in terms of business objectives, and measuring results to ensure the objectives are met.

Eric holds an MA in economics, an MBA in international business and a Graduate Certificate in organization development. He has twenty years of sales and management experience, five years as an economist, and the past several years as both an internal and an external OD practitioner. He has taught leadership, organization development and economics at several universities, and has published and presented on the return on investment for learning and OD programs at conferences for ASTD, OD Network and others. He is fluent in Spanish, and has lived and worked in Spain and Canada. He gives back to the profession as President of the Organization Development Network of Chicago, the leading OD group in the area.

Eric, his wife and son live in Elmhurst, IL. He is a runner who completed the Chicago Marathon in 2008.

He can be reached at eric.sanders@ODeconomist.com

Eric Sanders’s Specialties:

• Measurement and evaluation of learning programs and organization development initiatives (results coaching for Trainers & OD consultants)

• Organizational Transformation (quantitative & qualitative assessments & action planning)

• Large group processes (Appreciative Inquiry & Open Space Technology)

• Leadership development (assessments, coaching and training)

• Training and Team Building (design & facilitation of programs & events)
