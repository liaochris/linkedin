Nancy Worrick’s Summary

Nancy Worrick brings Five Star service to the real estate industry. Previously, Nancy worked in hotels throughout the East Coast and Midtown Manhattan. She now applies that industry’s gold standard to her career as a real estate broker, specifically: personal attention, responsiveness, sincerity, value, and appreciation.

Having studied graphic design and marketing, Nancy also worked as a marketing consultant for the Downtown Club of York, a private professionals’ club, where her marketing efforts were responsible for the best attendance the club had seen in many years. She has a sharp and creative eye that helps showcase the potential and uniqueness of every property she represents.

Nancy made her first foray into the real estate world at the age of 19, purchasing her first investment property while in college. She has personally completed many renovations to that first property, taking a hands-on approach to each project. Her experience as a landlord, investor, and agent has given her a unique perspective that allows her to negotiate win-win deals for all parties.

Originally from the Washington D.C./Baltimore metropolitan area, Nancy was drawn to the cultural diversity and vibrancy of New York, where she now works. She has a passion for travel and food. Her unique background and diverse skill set provided for her success in the real estate industry.
