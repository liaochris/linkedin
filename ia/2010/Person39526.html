Christopher Avery’s Summary

World-class expert on personal and shared responsibility...

Popular speaker, author, and mentor Christopher Avery PhD, is committed to redefining responsibility in all societies throughout the world. He supports people and organizations in realizing exponential gains by recognizing and accessing untapped resources.

Christopher broke new ground with his pragmatic and insightful research on individual and shared responsibility in the workplace. This work was readily adopted by groups in R&D and Supply Chain Management worldwide. His book "Teamwork Is An Individual Skill" describes the principles.

Christopher innovates in leadership development, organizational culture, personal growth, and coaching/teaching/counseling with two field-tested breakthroughs, the Responsibility Process™ and the Keys to Responsibility™. His intent is to accelerate performance, success, and sustainability while increasing personal freedom, choice, and power worldwide.

He earned a Doctorate in the Communication of Technology from The University of Texas at Austin. He's been president of Partnerwerks Incorporated since 1991 and a senior consultant with the Cutter Consortium's IT and Agile Project Management practices.

Christopher co-authored the Declaration of Interdependence, an integral set of values guiding agile and adaptive approaches to business. He is a co-founder of the Agile Project Leadership Network focused on supporting and developing great project leaders.

Featured or quoted in hundreds of media reports he also authors a prolific stream of articles, columns, and essays read by professionals in more than 50 countries.

Christoper is an avid cyclist and a certified shotgun instructor devoted to developing responsible youth and families through competitive clay target shooting.

Christopher Avery’s Specialties:

Exemplary team skills and leadership development for engineering and scientific professionals and their enterprises. Supporting individuals in taking personal responsibility in shared-responsibility environments.

Extensive experience developing integrative mindsets and skills for independent thinkers who must collaborate in competitive environments.

Training, Leadership Development, Leadership Coaching, Team Development, Organizational Change and Development.
