Andrew Kraft’s Summary

AMP® is the leading ad network administration, audience targeting and reach extension platform. This proven platform combines a robust ad network management and audience-targeting platform with the industry’s most comprehensive online audience analytics and reporting tool for agencies, publishers and advertisers.

Agencies and advertisers are increasingly buying audiences, not only sites. This shift creates a great opportunity for brand-name publishers to generate incremental display ad revenue through audience targeting. The AMP product suite is designed to help online publishers like you to maximize this fast growing segment of the display advertising market. The three components of the AMP product suite include:

* AMP Console - AMP Console leverages the Audience Cloud's deep user analytics, allowing you to probe audience behaviors across the entire spectrum of our network. Discover untapped "look-alike" audiences and manage your spend more wisely.

* AMP Audience Cloud - Target audiences with anonymous behavioral data across thousands of segments (including demographic, psychographic, search, shopping, and in-market behaviors) aggregated from 3rd party data providers such as NextAction, BlueKai, and Bizo. Over 165 million unique users in the United States.

* AMP Network - On-demand ad impressions on comScore 250 and similar brand-name sites. Increase your reach and frequency across any target audience on a network delivering over 10 billion ad impressions monthly.
