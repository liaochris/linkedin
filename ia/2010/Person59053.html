Douglas Ollie, MBA, PMP, MPM’s Summary

Doug is a resourceful leader in the e-retail, social media, merchandising and supply chain space. He drives highly complex, high profile, and high risk projects to achieve results for key stakeholders.

Just a few of his key projects included:

- Leading the redevelopment Sears.com & Kmart.com
- Building of high velocity B2C fulfillment centers
- Brand developer for Canyon River Blues denim
- Creation of a company wide inventory reconciliation process
- Development of "green-field" WMS solutions
- Creation of a new e-retail content management solution
- Leader for procurement initiatives

Combine this with his MBA and PMP, and you have a unique skill set that will aid your company in achieving industry leading results.
