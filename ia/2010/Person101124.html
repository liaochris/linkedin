Joe Martin’s Summary

I am a dedicated "award winning" real estate broker specializing in distressed properties including REO's (bank owned) & Short Sales, as well as traditional real estate: Residential & Commercial - Individual and bulk sales.

I have an experienced support staff backing me up to handle both Buyers & Sellers of distressed properties and traditional real estate transactions Valleywide.

I handle all aspects of REO property management, sales & disposition and also have a Short Sale division focused on helping homeowners understand their options to avoid foreclosure with in-house negotiators, Certified Distress Property Experts and SFR's (www.AzSPO.com).

I am currently looking for opportunities to further expand my network of lenders and asset management companies to work with in both of the above areas.

My infrastructure is set up to handle significant volume of BPOs, REOs, Short Sales and buyer transactions.
