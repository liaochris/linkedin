Dean Mitchell’s Summary

I have applied my personal business expertise in numerous interventions producing impressive results—improving safety performance of a managed company to an award-winning level, increasing sales in the Midwest from $500k annually to more than $5M, reducing costs in a Midwestern company over $1M annually, increasing area market share in the Northeast from less than 10% to over 35%, developing a blasting system for a specific national and international market, merging two separate companies for increased market share and reduced costs, establishing new distribution methods for international sales to produce profitability in place of previous losses, and increasing international sales by over $5 million. My explosives and blasting experience includes Midwestern limestone for aggregates and construction, Midwestern coal strips and underground, Midwestern underground salt, Western seismic, National and International trenching system and application development, Eastern limestone for aggregates and construction, and Eastern granite for aggregates and construction. I have held such executive positions as President and General Manager, Regional Manager, Technical Programs Manager, Operations Manager, Corporate Pricing Strategist, and Project Manager over the years having earned an MBA in Marketing and Finance from the University of Delaware, and a BS cum laude in Business Administration from Roanoke College in Salem, Virginia. I am ready to share my business expertise, keen analytical insights, and Customer Profitability or Blast Analysis Model with organizations desiring greater daily efficiencies, improved profitability, and more informed decisions.
