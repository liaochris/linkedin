Kr!st! Ramsey’s Summary

Kristi Ramsey is an Executive Recruiter who is passionate about building relationships while representing quality candidates to the Executive teams she works with. She has strong relationship building skills and her proactive approach to develop business through consulting has laid the ground work for a solid foundation in the relationships she has established on both the Client and Candidate side. Kristi believes that there is no greater satisfaction than the opportunity of successfully bringing together a great talent with a great client company. She is someone that you can count on as she continually strives for excellence. Her work includes working with Executives, Sales and
Operations in Supply Chain, Logistics, Transportation, Freight Forwarding and 3PL companies as well as Agriculture and the Integrated Environmental Management Industry and Engineering.
Kristi Ramsey knows the job market is tough for candidates right now. But as a top recruiter with the world’s largest recruitment organization, she also knows exactly what a candidate needs to do to get an interview and land the job.
Kristi Ramsey started with Management Recruiters of St. Charles in March 2008, and by year’s end she earned the number 4 spot on MRINetwork’s prestigious “Rookie of the Year” list. She ended the year in the top 5 percent of more than 1000 recruiters in the Midwest Region.
Management Recruiters of St. Charles is part of MRINetworkä, the largest and most successful recruitment organization in the world. Started in 1987 by Daniel Lasse, Management Recruiters of St. Charles is currently ranked in the top 10% of more than 1100 offices in 25 countries.
