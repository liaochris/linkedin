Ellie Mixter-Keller’s Summary

Using her ability to champion a person's unique qualities, Ellie is a passionate job seeker forum speaker, group facilitator and board member of 40Plus of Southeastern WI and the Lumen Christi Employment Network: coaching job candidates and people in career transition to market themselves into the job market, proactively with positivity.

Dynamic marketing professional with expertise in the creation, implementation and management of marketing programs. Unique combination of ad agency creative/production background and client-side management experience results in the development of marketing solutions that are cost-effective, strategically sound and, most of all, successful. Exceptional ability to use analytical and quantitative skills as well as new technology opportunities to increase program quality, decrease cost and generate an attractive return on investment.

Marketing strategies, corporate and product branding, advertising, strategic planning, corporate communications, dealer/distributor communications, B2B business to business, B2C business to consumer, creative solutions, process/business development, distributor/customer retention, corporate image/copy consistency police, cost effective, team builder, vendor management, voice of customer, change agent, proactive interaction with management/staff.
