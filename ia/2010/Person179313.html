Ryan Allis’s Summary

Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder of iContact, a 175 person email marketing company in Durham, North Carolina. iContact allows over 50,000 customers to easily create, send, and track email newsletters, surveys, autoresponders, and RSS feeds in one central web application.

Chairman & Founder of Virante, Inc. - Web marketing consulting firm focusing on search engine optimization, viral media, web site design, and web app development.
