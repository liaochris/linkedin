    (Education Management industry)

    June 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    As a registerd agent for Child Shield U.S.A.The Child Shield, U.S.A™ mission is twofold. Our primary goal is to reduce the alarming numbers of lost, missing, abducted, and runaway children in America. To that end, our child safety program provides parents with an array of easy-to-follow educational materials that will empower their children with important knowledge that can help prevent a tragedy.

    This proactive, educational part of the Child Shield, U.S.A™ service features our unique "Safety Seven Poster" and "Play It Safe" Coloring Book. These materials provide ongoing reinforcement and help children to retain the important safety information they've learned from our "Guide to Safer Children".

    Our program also includes a no cost, monthly subscription to "The Shield", child safety publication. "The Shield" reminds parents to talk with their children, gives them topics to talk about and keeps them updated on the "tricks" child predators use. It is the only ongoing, extensive, educational program available.

    The second goal of Child Shield, U.S.A™ is to offer victim parents and law enforcement agencies a "one stop, one call source" for immediate assistance in distributing information that is vital to the recovery effort. Whenever a Child Shield, U.S.A™ registered child becomes missing, we are prepared to act IMMEDIATELY by providing local and far-reaching assistance to the child's family and Law Enforcement agencies involved.
