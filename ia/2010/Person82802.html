Sarah Anderson’s Summary

As a diversified and talented on-air sales professional for national shopping networks, I've been presenting products on television and nationwide at trade shows for over ten years. Previously, as an actress, singer, and dancer I was a member of the Radio City Rockettes and performed in many National and International Tours of Broadway musicals.

Sarah Anderson’s Specialties:

Accredited Jewelry Professional certified by the Gemological Institute of America. Ear-prompter, tele-prompter proficient, voice-overs. National commercials, infomercials, tradeshow and television host appearances.
