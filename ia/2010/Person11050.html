Andrea Chin’s Summary

I am a USC masters student in gerontology, whose background has been in student services program development and clinical and cognitive psychology research. In the past three years, I've coordinated a peer mentoring/paraprofessional counseling program for low help seeking first year students and facilitated a weekly student seminar on social justice issues.

I'm interested in translating my experience helping students successfully transition to college life into making a difference in the lives of older adults. My broad career goals are in geriatric care management and long term care coordination, collaborating with older adults and their families to assess their care needs to develop and implement a suitable plan for their long-term care.

program development, program evaluation, peer education, paraprofessional counseling, resource referral, research grant writing, clinical literature reviewing, SPSS data analysis
