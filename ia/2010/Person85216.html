David Hoglund’s Summary

Professional with over twenty five years of multi-faceted technology, solution design, consulting, marketing in the verticals of (healthcare,and DOD) which included of all facets of wireless modalities, RFID/RFLS, WAN/PAN/WLAN,,i.e. expert knowledge of the wirelesss domain space, specific to medical devices.This includes implantable and to the WLAN specific devices. Past corporate and military experience (officer in the United States Air Force), (medical device) experience with Siemens Medical Systems,the Department of Defense, Hewlett Packard, General Electric, Symbol Technologies, Draeger Medical, and also conducted M&A for high technology ventures with Johnson Controls as well as defined agreements with Andrew Wireless Solutions. Managed complex projects, provided work flow modeling, launched major medical and wireless product releases both on a domestic and international basis, Scoped the FDA/PMA510K regulatory and ISO 90001 process for multiple medical device products, written white papers for HIMSS, IEEE, and AAMI, provided speaking engagements at numerous AAMI, HIMSS, and RFID conferences.Finally designed enterprise mobility strategies, and implemented global alliance agreements for private and public companies. Founded Integra Systems, Inc.in 1997 www.integrasystems.org. Integra Systems, Inc. which has consulted with many established partners and clients in the wireless/PAN/LAN/WAN/MAN, and RFID/RFLS space(s), the total medical device community,as well as the DAS (Distributed Antenna System) (Cell/PCS/LMR/Public Safety) marketplace. Have provided strategic business direction to enable the right marketplace and competitive positioning strategy of both medical device applications as well as strategic wireless applications and technologies. Desire strategic and/or overall technical consulting, planning, and/or design opportunities to enable the next level technical and competitive differentiation for the planned 2011 healthcare agenda
