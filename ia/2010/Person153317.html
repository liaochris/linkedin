David Albright’s Summary

New Deal Capital is a unique financing alternative for companies looking to raise capital. We present accredited investors, brokers, and financiers with superior opportunities in a variety of sectors. These opportunities are not open to non-accredited investors.

Our team sees deals flow from all over Canada and the US. These opportunities are in sectors from mining to biotech. In the past, our team has sourced funds for companies in amounts ranging from $500,000 to $5,000,000.

We look at start up situations and we can help companies prepare presentations for investors which are effective in raising capital. Experience is a key element of New Deal Capital's competitive advantage. The financing contacts in New Deal's arsenal help clients successfully raise funds in a timely manner.

This LinkedIn profile is not a solicitation to invest. The goal of this page is to network with qualified and interested investors.
