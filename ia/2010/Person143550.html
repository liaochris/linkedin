Michael Siek’s Summary

Spencer Trask develops innovative ideas into world-changing companies. We are private investors who have backed some of the most influential ventures in technology and healthcare. Today, we are pioneering the use of incentive-based collaboration to accelerate the growth and success of our missions.

Spencer Trask portfolio companies are founded on business-model and technology innovation across a broad range of sectors including semiconductors, networking (storage and communications), software (enterprise and internet), healthcare, medical devices and the emerging category of cleantech.
