AnnDrea Boe’s Summary

SparkFun Electronics enables electronic enthusiasts to explore and play with technology by giving them access to prototyping gear and resources. SparkFun strives to break down the current misconception that technology is inaccessible to the individual.

Go ahead, get your hands dirty and start exploring! Use SparkFun's free online tutorials to learn the basics of Electronic Engineering, PCB design, and even DIY through-hole and surface-mount soldering; use SparkFun's Batch PCB service to get your own PCB design fabricated; then purchase components and other gear from SparkFun's store and start creating your own electronic gear.

With a background in visual arts and psychology, it's my job to focus on developing a long-term vision for SparkFun's brand and positioning strategies. I have passion for what I do, and my reward is watching SparkFun grow by empowering its customers!
