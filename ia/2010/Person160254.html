Patti Peeples (Gustafson), PhD, RPh’s Summary

Experienced Consultant/Communications Expert/Medical writer with proven track record, focused on HEOR, with >150 publications in print. Experience in finely crafting your message into a journal submission, abstract, or white paper using data from CEA, CUI, CBA, budget impact models, patient-reported outcomes (PRO), and other value assessments. Highly experienced senior manager, people manager, researcher and writer, with proven entrepreneurial capabilities.
