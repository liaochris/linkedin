Alan L. Sklover, Esq.’s Summary

Alan L. Sklover empowers employees worldwide to stand up for themselves at work. Mr. Sklover’s legal practice is limited to assisting executives with concerns and opportunities in employment, compensation, transition and severance. Mr. Sklover’s law practice provides Executives with legal counsel, negotiation and litigation. Mr. Sklover also writes a Blog (SkloverWorkingWisdom.com) that offers career assistance and helps employees navigate and negotiate for themselves at work to gain the most for their skills, time and efforts. SkloverWorkingWisdom.com offers the trade secrets and 'uncommon common sense' of Alan L. Sklover, Esq.

Alan L. Sklover, Esq.’s Specialties:

Negotiation of Executive employment, compensation and severance. Counseling executives in career navigation.
