jim schwenke’s Summary

Having worked both on the client-side and with top agencies, I've led creative efforts for numerous industry sectors including Financial, Telecom, Tech, Hospitality, CPG, Pharma, Automotive, Retail, Gaming, and Entertainment...making every effort to provide relevant messaging at the right time, in the right place, whether it's on- or offline.

Throughout my career, I’ve made every effort to stay as close to the forefront of where things are going, and in numerous cases, ahead of the curve. For example, I did Brand/Direct advertising while at Chiat/Day; created an Interactive Brand/Direct group for Charles Schwab in 1997; and Integrated on- and offline campaigns behind the Microsoft 2000 launch that drove customers to a microsite from print and TV...a first for Microsoft.

Currently I'm working with numerous clients across all digital platforms including social, mobile, video, and experiential. In addition, I'm also involved in the development of a new online advertising platform that utilizes cloud-sourcing data (interested? send me a note).
