David Jones’s Summary

Prior to joining Kaiser Permanente, David was a founding partner of a top 50 digital marketing agency based in San Francisco, called Basement, Inc. As VP of Marketing, he manned strategic brand development and digital marketing programs for Fortune 500 clients.

With over 20 years of integrated marketing and advertising experience, David specializes in leveraging traditional strategic practices with new economy tools and thinking. His marketing acumen stems from working in competitive industries such as high-tech, health care, consumer electronics, apparel, packaged goods, banking, travel & hospitality, energy services and Internet/e-commerce.

David’s expertise lies in building strong customer relationships through meaningful brand differentiation, and setting the marketing framework for advanced online activation. His career consists of senior leadership positions both in-house and agency-side, as well as in the capacity of consultative marketing services.

His agency experience includes work for Avery Dennison, Bank of America, Four Seasons Hotels, Greenpeace, Johnson & Johnson, Murad, Mattel, MTV, Pioneer, Toshiba Computers and a host of entertainment clients. David's in-house experiences include his current position at Kaiser Permanente, Director of Marketing at AllBusiness.com, and Brand Manager at Sempra Energy.

David graduated from the University of San Diego with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration (Marketing emphasis). He studied Law at Western State University, and earned a post-graduate certificate from the American Association of Advertising Agencies for coursework at USC.

David Jones’s Specialties:

Brand development & management; communication planning; relationship marketing; loyalty & reward programs; web development and e-commerce; digital product and application development; mobile marketing; social media; revenue optimization; and managing national advertising campaigns from broadcast to new media.
