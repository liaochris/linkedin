Travis Braithwaite’s Summary

Travis Braithwaite is a proven Senior Consultant with over 13 years of Business and IT experience serving major corporations such as Enbridge, Direct Energy and TD Canada Trust. Specializing in general management, he is skilled in strategic planning, process design, team building and leadership development. He has significant experience with strategy development and implementation, financial and operational assessments and modeling, and performance measurement. Recent accomplishments include restructuring a $190 million in Sales Enbridge partnership involving 6 parties negotiating simultaneously. With Direct Energy, managing rental operations on a portfolio of 1.3 million residential water heaters with revenues over $200 million annually. He has served as a Business Manager on several projects which involved changing major operational IT components and reengineering operational processes.

With a Master of Business Administration Degree and a Bachelor of Computing Science, Travis has a solid combination of business and technical skills. He has strong negotiation and problem solving skills and a long track record of building and nurturing high performance teams. Clients and peers have consistently recognized his contributions and he has been nominated for multiple Direct Energy Platinum Awards.

His career objective is to implement performance improvement projects with a focus on process, systems, people, leadership and development. His specialties include process redesign, managing change, strategy development and implementation, financial and operational assessments and modeling, performance measurement, group facilitation, and business skill development.
