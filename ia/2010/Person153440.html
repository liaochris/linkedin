Nancy Frishberg’s Summary

I promote the user-customer point of view.

Qualitative customer and user research provides rich data about your customers and how people use your products. I help your organization design products, create processes, and communicate.

Recent engagements refreshed my knowledge of information technology, health and wellness, and higher education markets.

And, I create artifacts of lasting value. Ask me about them.

Nancy Frishberg’s Specialties:

qualitative user research, concept development, product evaluation, field research, usability research, programmatic relationships with customers, group process, coaching and team building, presentations, keynotes and graduation addresses
