    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    iNET Property, a real estate brokerage fueled by Internet marketing, was founded by Rich Barnhart in Denver, Colorado in 1997. The company currently specializes in Short Sales, Internet property marketing and buyer representation while providing cutting edge real estate education and web-based solutions for its clients.
