EMPLOYMENT:

Scott currently serves as the Director of Internet Marketing at Lands' End. His organization manages all aspects of E-mail Marketing, SEM, SEO, Affiliate Marketing, Display Advertising and Social Media.

Prior to Lands' End, Scott was the Executive Director, eBusiness at USAA. The organization Scott stood and directed was responsible for online demand generation channels including E-Mail Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, Search Engine Marketing and Online Store Retailing.

Prior to USAA, Scott served in Sr. Management roles at Dell, Inc. and Level 3 Communications. During his 15 year career, Scott has developed and directed high performance teams who've designed, deployed and operated eCommerce, CRM and ERP solutions globally.
