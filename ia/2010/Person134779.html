Markus Roznowski’s Summary

Markus Roznowski owns his own computer consulting business. Markus has a keen knack for problem solving all types computer related difficulties. He especially loves teaching those folks who admit themselves they are "computer beginners" who may not be "computer literate", no matter what age they are, or where they are at in their careers. Markus specializes in Computer Tutoring, Service, Repair, Networking, Computer Backup, and System Analysis... for Windows XP, Windows Vista, and Mac OS X computer systems.

Markus Roznowski is also a Community Organizer for a group called, Sprawl has No Future in Phoenix Arizona, which is comprised of community members who are interested in urban planning issues. Markus has earned a Bachelors of Science in Justice Studies, and a Master's in Urban and Environmental Planning.

Markus has specialized in Community and Urban Development while he was in graduate school for the Master's of Urban and Environmental Planning program. Markus is an advocate for community involvement and collaborative planning, throughout the planning and design process.
