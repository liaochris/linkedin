  • Director of Multicultural Marketing

    Dish Network

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Entertainment industry)

    2004 — Present (6 years )

    Her responsibilities include development of marketing strategy for DISH Network’s DishLATINO Hispanic segment, as well as for more than a dozen of DISH Network’s international channels within the International Business. Carolina oversees marketing plans and strategies oriented to increase subscriber acquisition and retention, identify potential markets, increase awareness of DN multicultural programming packages, and define their correspondent pricing strategies. She has led the most recent repackaging of DishLATINO; developed thorough segmentation analyses to identify potential Latino targets and the impact of acculturation on U.S. Hispanic TV viewing habits; enhanced DishLATINO brand positioning and creative, created and implemented nationwide retail programs; and has lead all DishLATINO media tactics, retention and loyalty campaigns, merchandising programs, promotional initiatives, grassroots campaigns, and joint marketing initiatives with programmers to meet growth objectives.
