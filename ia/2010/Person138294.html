Matt Youngquist’s Summary

Job hunting is a tricky business. As a Seattle-based career coach and outplacement consultant, however, my passion is to constantly study "what works" out there and provide people with practical, proven career advice that will help them jump the learning curve and gain the greatest competitive advantage in achieving their career objectives.

To date, I've had the privilege of assisting more than 6,500 individuals through the job hunting process. These individuals have ranged from new college grads to Fortune 50 executives, and by drawing upon this experience, I'm able to help individuals from all walks of life clarify their career goals, sharpen their self-marketing abilities, and navigate successfully through the many peaks and valleys of the career transition process. Additionally, since I believe each person is a unique product in the marketplace, facing unique challenges, I don't put a lot of stock in one-size-fits-all approaches to finding work -- and pride myself, instead, on ensuring each client receives a personalized game plan for approaching the employment marketplace.

For more information about my career coaching and corporate outplacement services, please don't hesitate to call me at (425) 450-4960 or visit my website at www.career-horizons.com!
