Stephen Reddell’s Summary

My slogan is “Sowing a Home, Reaping a Family”. It is from Galatians 6:7-8 and signifies to me that a home is so much more than just the wood, bricks, rocks and paint that we live in. Home is an experience. It is an experience of worship to our Creator. It is our joy at the birth of a child. It is an experience of sadness from a hurt or a loss that we no doubt will have in the house we buy. It is an experience of family or friends gathering for food and fellowship. “Home” is all of these experiences of life and more. It takes a whole village to raise a child and I believe it takes a whole village to have a “Home”. It takes us as individuals having peace of mind and enjoyment of our lives. It takes our own family units having daily life go by and sharing our experiences together, good and bad. It takes our neighbors and friends to experience life together with us. “Home” is the schools, the churches, the restaurants. It is games, concerts and plays, parties and other social events in the community. Home is the community! All of it combined.

It is also all of the above that can make it a difficult thing to need to sell a “Home” and move away. On the selling side, I understand this. I am sensitive to the stresses that some sellers have, and I do all I can to ensure a quick and painless selling experience for them, while doing everything I can to get them top-dollar for their “Home”. I am a person, a realtor, in this community who understands that buying and selling a home is a process that deals with much more than just the house. I am a Family Realtor.
