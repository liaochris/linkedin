Amy Weaver’s Summary

I am a Project Management and Process/Methodology professional with over 13 years of leadership and management in the IT and creative services industries. Accomplishments focus on PMI-based project management, process improvements, business analysis and creative design/management expertise.

I have worked in several fast-paced, corporate and small environments including West Marine, DeVry University, Hoffman-York, CNA Insurance, Business Logic Corporation and Adapt Telephony Inc., having held project management and director level positions. In 2002 I became partner in a small creative marketing services firm and in 2004 started my own company. I have been able to combine my project management, process methodology and design background for creative, marketing and web-based projects.
See www.amyweaver.net for my more creative side.
