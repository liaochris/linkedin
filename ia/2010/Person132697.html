Marie Pieniazek’s Summary

Ms. Pieniazek has held a number of upper management positions within the demand response industry, and is lead consultant for the largest privately held Demand Response provider. Ms. Pieniazek is an expert in demand response and energy efficiency fields. She gained essential knowledge in the regulatory field, at the federal and state levels, while working as Vice-President of Government & Regulatory Affairs for one of the nations largest privately held demand response providers. Prior to working in the demand response industry Ms. Pieniazek worked at the New York Independent System Operator where she was responsible for facilitation of New York’s $750,000,000 Installed Capacity market. Prior to joining the New York Independent System Operator, Ms. Pieniazek was employed by Selkirk Cogen Partners, a 345-megawatt cogeneration facility that provides power to New York’s wholesale market. Ms. Pieniazek holds a BS in Business, Economics, and Management from the State University of New York, Empire State College and an MBA from The College of Saint Rose.
