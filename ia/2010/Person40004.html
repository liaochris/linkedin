Christos J. Palios’s Summary

I endeavour to merge the uniquely enthralling elements of our world to create evoking, awesome photographic panoramas that comfortably challenge the mind while delighting the heart.

Imagine seeing everything all at once, in true color, in perfect light. Envision a globe sliced open and laid flat like a map, like a world atlas. Now journey into a sweeping vista, rich with color and texture, unlimited depth, endless detail - a world truly reimagined.

Always, I welcome the element of serendipity that frequently lies before me. One goal I strive to achieve in my work is to evoke inspiration and insightfulness; it is at that point, with solid conviction, that I wish to share the evolutionary culmination with the spectator.

Christos J. Palios’s Specialties:

Finding singular, compelling subjects to capture while enjoying the memorable spaces and cultures I traverse. I create large, photographic panoramas as edition-limited artworks that inspire and fulfill.

Fluent in Greek + Conversational French \ Landscape + Portrait Photography \ Lighting \ Graphic + Conceptual Design
