Edward McKnight’s Summary

I have nearly 20 years of experience in the industry. Having worked in both the vendor and supplier sides of the business, from bottom to top, from left to right, from down to up, I believe I have a great understanding of what clients want and how to provide top quality services for them to make sure their needs and objectives are met. I started as a telephone interviewer when I was 16 and didn't stop since that first phone call. Ah!, the good ole P&P days. Now, with much gained knowledge and experience, I offer the best I can in Client Services and Field/Project Management. I take pride in my work and the company I work for.
