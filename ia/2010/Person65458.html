Erica Conway’s Summary

My brother, Jim Conway, and I bought MacPros, Inc (dba Retso Group) in 2004, after identifing ways to improve our service to clients and talent. We've done just that and changed our name, location and attitude, becoming C2 Graphics Productivity Solutions. We've served Wisconsin as the only Adobe Authorized and Quark Recognized Training center that's locally owned and operated in Madison and Milwaukee.

Erica Conway’s Specialties:

Our core competencies: software training & talent placement to the graphic design industry. We offer Adobe/Quark Certified Instruction to professional graphic designers & production artists. Our trainers are practicing professionals who share best practices & industry-driven curriculum to our clients. We also recruit, assess & place industry professionals (graphic designers, production artists, marketing pros, copy writers, etc.) at competitive rates in Madison and Milwaukee, WI. www.c2gps.com
