Business has always been a huge passion of mine. While playing football for the Philadelphia Eagles, I attended the Wharton School of Business' Executive Education Program. Befriending Professor Peter Linneman (www.linnemanassociates.com) allowed me to spend a significant amount of time at his offices and working at his private equity fund (American Land Fund).

Utilizing the skills acquired in the classroom, on the ball field, on the mountain, and in life, I Co-Founded the fastest growing health and medical website. MDinfo.com is a social media health platform that delivers answers to health questions in real time by real health experts. In addition to our Q&A section, we have an extensive video archive and drug directory. MDi currently reaches over 12 million unique users a month.
