Andrew Dudum’s Summary

I studied Entrepreneurial Management and OPIM at the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. I am passionately involved in Palestinian-Israeli Dialogue organizations and in 2008 co-founded www.LendforPeace.org, an online portal that allows individuals the opportunity to lend directly to micro-entrepreneurs in the Middle East. I am currently the Product Manager at TokBox and enjoy working to build the simplest multi-party video chat service on the web.

  • Founder

    myGreek.org, Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    June 2008 — January 2010 (1 year 8 months)

    The one-stop organizational and social utility for fraternity and sorority chapters.

    myGreek allows all incoming freshman to connect directly with the chapters on their campus. myGreek's most exciting and innovative feature, TheWire, allows freshman and Greeks to subscribe via SMS to their favorite chapters on campus. In turn, this allows chapters to save money on fliers and operating costs, and allows students to receive text messages regarding rush events and parties on campus.
