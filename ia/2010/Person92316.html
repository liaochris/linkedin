David Rhea’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic creative professional journalist, editor and manager who enjoys challenging, imaginative and team-oriented work. The highlight of my career in the news industry is helping reimagine traditional print newspapers online. Multimedia news - the convergence of print, video, audio and online media - allows me to indulge my greatest passion, which is learning. I am enthusiastic about the future of the news industry, and I believe that multimedia is the key to survival of "traditional print" news.

During my career I have worked in just about every form of print media, including daily and weekly newspapers, local and national magazines and virtual print as a blogger. Online, I produced and performed in various daily and weekly video news and entertainment shows, produced and trained others into reporter video, produced mini-documentary videos and produced and performed a radio show on NPR.

I am also a professional freelance writer - currently a contributing writer for Outdoor Life and Blade magazines. Both are the world's leading periodicals in outdoor sports and for knife enthusiasts, respectively. I have been writing for Blade for just over six years and began my relationship with Outdoor Life in November 2008.

I received my Bachelor's degree from the University of Florida College of Journalism, where I trained in every aspect of the craft, from writing and editing to layout and design to photojournalism. My specific course focus was magazine management.

In addition to my journalism career, I was a professional musician for five years, associated with two record companies, Elektra and Wind-Up, with two rock bands, VonRay and Dust for Life, respectively. This career excursion allowed me to travel extensively and perform on stage, in recording studios, and on radio and television. I had opportunities to tour with top bands such as Creed, Nickelback and 3 Doors Down, perform on television and radio shows such as Smallville and Mancow Show.

David Rhea’s Specialties:

Writing; leadership and management; video production, including videography, on-camera performance, voiceover, and video editing/production using Apple Final Cut Pro; audio production using Digidesign ProTools; radio performance; print design using Photoshop, InDesign, Quark Xpress.
