Tim Goleman’s Summary

I have an innate curiosity and aptitude. I want to understand what makes things tick, from marketing, brands, advertising, communications, culture, people, etc to pinpoint trends and inform recommendations for greater engagement. Then to identify opportunities and value provided by use of social engagement tactics within current and future projects and marketing/communication mix. I am early adopter of social media tools, with knowledge of the social media space to develop programs where marketers can engage their audiences to increase awareness and spark advocacy This knowledge includes social media management tools: URL shorteners, Facebook, Twitter platform and blogging/podcasting.

My position at TMP taught me how to leverage traditional media. This experience gave me the background to integrate traditional and interactive. I primarily worked with print, including newspapers, trade journals and magazines. One of my responsibilities was to develop media plans for campaigns under $5,000. This let me be creative in placement and negotiate to get the best rates for my clients. My experience there was not based in just print, other mediums I worked with were radio and outdoor. Radio consisted of buying in major markets, minor script writing, and approval. This job taught me how to get the most out of a small budget and negotiate for the best rates.

I am team player who is willing to roll up one’s sleeves to get the job done. I don’t mind helping out others in my department and others in the organization.
