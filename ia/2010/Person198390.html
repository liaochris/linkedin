Serghei Vahnovan’s Summary

I'm currently Deputy General Director - Legal Affairs at Mechel Campia Turzii SA.
In my career I've accumulated experience in various fields of business law. My favorite area is Mergers & Acquisitions, but I also have a vast experience in litigation, corporate law, collection, compliance and contract drafting.
I've worked in different companies (from start-ups to publicly-held) in various fields (from telecommunication and real-estate to steel making), but even after 10 years of experience, I continue to study, being now enrolled for a PhD in Business Law.

I'm result oriented and a quick learner, being able to deliver clear advices and solve complex problems.
