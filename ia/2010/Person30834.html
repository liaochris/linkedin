Rick Burke’s Summary

My education and life experience and professional experience have all contributed to my success as a Realtor. How? While I am proud to be a 'salesman', I am first and foremost a consultant who seeks to ask all of the questions regarding what it is you really want and need in your next home. My previous career as a marketing communications consultant taught me that attention to detail and a sense of urgency are critical to superior service. Follow-up is important and I never assume anything. You are only as good as the last satisfied client is happy with your performance. My objective is to make all clients "raving fans".
