Karl Soderholm, RLA, AICP, LEED-AP BD+C’s Summary

Mr. Soderholm entered the landscape architecture profession filled with a passion to shape the world to be a better place, creating special places for people to enjoy. In his 12 years of professional practice, Mr. Soderholm has been immersed in a wide array of project types and design challenges. Beginning his career producing award winning landscape and hardscape design packages for private developers, institutional users, and governmental facilities, a strong background in the fundamentals of the practice was born. Upon joining England-Thims & Miller, Inc. approximately six years ago, Mr. Soderholm has ascended to Director of Landscape Architecture, Land Planning, and Graphic Design. His responsibilities include all aspects of the design process, from conceptual master planning through Graphic Design for signage.

Currently Mr. Soderholm is taking a leading role in the design of sustainable places and currently sits on the Florida Water Star Technical Advisory Committee involved with the development of the latest water conservation policies within the State. Professional work currently involves many large scale projects ranging from master planning of large mixed use developments to the planning and design of national cemeteries. Mr. Soderholm points to thoughtful planning of sites, sustainable design which is both aesthetic and maintainable, as well as a desire to develop special places for people to enjoy as his primary mission as a landscape architect.

Mr Soderholm is a licensed landscape architect in Florida, Georgia, and Alabama. In addition, Mr. Soderholm is a certified arborist, certified planner, as well as being a LEED Accredited Professional.
