Malin Gustavsson’s Summary

I am a third part of Ekvalita Ab (www.ekvalita.fi). In Ekvalita I work part time as a consultant in gender equality and diversity issues. We are giving a broad range of different workshops and courses for public sector, organizations and different kinds of groups and network.
The other part of the time I work with gender equality issues in a health organization, Folkhälsan. My responsibility is gender mainstreaming in education. I am educating teacher to deal with sexual harassment and gendered bulling, manly for primary schools and high schools, but also how to put up gender equality plans.
I give one to two courses a year at University of Helsinki or at University of Åbo Akademi about how to work with gender equality in ones professions. But I also give some lectures for other educational units
I am also do freelancing on a regular basis. I give lectures in gender equality for associations of Hem och skola rf, associations for parents that want to support schools.
On a regular basis I am participation on national and international conferences and seminars. It s important to me to always have one foot in the discussion of the Academia and the other one next to people being interesting in working with this issues on a very practical way in everyday life. I see the aim of my profession as the bridge between theory and practice.
