Ronny McInerney’s Summary

Experience Summary:
I have been involved with Real Estate since graduating from Quinnipiac University in 1997. My experience in the field began with the purchasing, renovating, managing, and selling of multi unit properties. A few years later I entered the Real Estate Appraisal industry. I was mentored by two highly regarded appraisers during my apprenticeship into appraising. My latest mentor recently retired to Ireland after 35 years in the industry. I acquired Domus Appraisals after working within the firm for a number of years including a 2 year term as Vice-President. Currently, I am the owner and principle of the firm. My experience in the appraisal industry has been highlighted by "high-end" residential appraisals, including the valuation of properties in excess of $40 Million. In addition to specializing in the appraisal of these multi-million dollar properties, I have represented homeowners in the grievance of their Real Estate Taxes on a continual basis. Other areas of my appraisal experience include: Estate Appraisals, Relocation Appraisals, and Matrimonial Appraisals. My major clients include many NY Law firms, a number of Private Banks, as well as, some of the larger lending institution in the Country (CitiBank, Wells Fargo, Chase, etc). I also have experience as an expert witness for proceeding involving the valuation of a variety of property types.
