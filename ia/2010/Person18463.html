Augusta Duffey’s Summary

I am an experienced online/offline creative with background in many industries including technology, automotive, financial services, pharmaceutical, & consumer packaged good. Managing teams and helping them create big ideas is where my passion lies and while I also still enjoy getting my hands dirty by creating the vision behind the ideas. I love good creative-- it is still what gets me excited about coming into work everyday. From the kerning of the headline to the big idea concept presentation-- it is all important in really great executions. And all of it excites me in a way that nothing else has.

I am looking for a position that will continue to excite me. Not only by supporting great creative, but realizing that our industry is really about coming up with great ideas. And those ideas could not only lead to a great print campaign or a content driven website, but could even help change the way we think about something that's inherent to our everyday lives. Its really about ideas. And then the execution can make them even better.
