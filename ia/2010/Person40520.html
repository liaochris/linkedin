Cindy Clark’s Summary

As a business, marketing and communications strategist, I help owners of small businesses climb out of chaos and break into freedom by creating systems, marketing solutions, and communication strategies. I enjoy working with start-up businesses as well as seasoned professionals who have that "stuck" feeling and need re-direction or a new direction.

I partner with my clients by asking the right questions and listening to them to learn what their goals are and what they are looking to achieve, to provide the desired results. I take great pride in assisting my clients achieve their success. Their success is my success. I am their biggest cheerleader and greatest ally in everything they do. I am always willing to listen and provide direction when they feel they need something more or are looking for other means of accomplishing their desired results.

I work with a great network of virtual professionals that I regularly refer to and recommend to my clients. If a small business owner is need of a Virtual Assistant, Web Designer, Graphic Designer, Social Media Expert, and/or other virtual partner, I am able to make a qualified recommendation and assist with the partnership.

Having a strong background in traditional off-line marketing, eMarketing is a natural for me. Learning all of the tools involved provides me with cutting edge technology and the ability to provide the services that my clients need. All of my packages include a custom-made marketing plan for my clients.

Cindy Clark’s Specialties:

Specialties include creating and implementing strategic marketing action plans - as well as exceptional project management skills. Looking at the big picture and being able to pull all the pieces together and make it work effectively, is one of my strong points.
