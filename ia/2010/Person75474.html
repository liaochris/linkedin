Gina Drew’s Summary

Legal Trends Network is an information resource and collaborative exchange that allows lawyers, judges, law professors, and students to share articles, news, and other resources focused on peaceful and therapeutic means of conflict resolution and dispensation of justice. Legal Trends Network covers new modalities in:

* Social Justice and the Rule of Law
* Alternative Dispute Resolution, Collaborative and Cooperative Law
* Transformative Mediation
* Problem Solving Courts & Therapeutic Jurisprudence
* Court and Law Office Technology
* Restorative Justice, Criminal and Prison Reform

Other topics covered include sections on law practice management and components centered on lawyer satisfaction and well-being. The site is organized so that each topic of discussion has its own resource page as a starting point for those individuals interested in a specific discipline.

Legal Trends Network is a free resource for legal professionals.
