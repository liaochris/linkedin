Karri Ojanen’s Summary

In philosophy, experience is defined as “the totality of the cognitions given by perception; all that is perceived, understood, and remembered.”

It takes a combination of strategic, tactical, and engineering thinking to create truly exceptional experiences. I have over a decade of experience working in digital media, advertising, and digital and social product design first as graphic designer and art director, and then as concept designer – a role very similar to a creative director, but with a stronger focus on user experience and interaction design. I’ve also worked as project manager, managing projects for clients like Nokia, the world’s largest manufacturer of mobile phones. I've gained experience in four countries on three different continents: Finland, The United Arab Emirates, Canada and the US. Currently, I work as Interaction Design Director at R/GA in New York.

Karri Ojanen’s Specialties:

Concept development; ideation and strategy; information architecture + interaction design; usability evaluation; IA/UXD team management and mentoring; process development; creative direction
