Jay Cooper’s Summary

Jay is a social media and mobile marketing expert, an entrepreneur having set up and sold communications businesses for the last 20 years.

He has pioneered the field of driving engagement through dialogue, and has pushed the boundaries in what is possible in marketing.

He also runs the influential Digital Lounge networking group, an unaffiliated association with over 3,000 members, whose aim is to take digital to a higher plane and drive growth across the industry.

Jay Cooper’s Specialties:

Social Mobile, Digital Marketing, Business Start Ups, Digital & Mobile Marketing Training
