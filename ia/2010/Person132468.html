Marianne Robben’s Summary

Marianne has a Doctoral in Communication Science from University of Nijmegen, the Netherlands. And extra curriculum in business administration, economic psychology, and leisure studies from Tilburg University. After working as a marketing research manager en marketing strategy director at TNT Post, she started working in on the agency-side. There she worked as a Strategy Director and was a shareholder at ARA Direct, part of ARA Group and the TBWA Worldwide Network for six years. Next she was Director of Strategy and in the managementteam of Proximity, part of the BBDO Worldwide Network. After already starting her own company 'LaRobben in 2007, this company is now here full-time business. It focusses on Dialog marketing strategy and concepting. Marianne was chosen Direct Marketeer of the year in 2005/2006. Is co-author of the book “Dialogical Thinking” and author of the book “In dialogue with Professors". She is a frequently asked speaker, inspirator, author and educator. She for exemple teaches at the postgraduate at the University of Groningen and is a columnist for the magazine 'MarketingResult'.
