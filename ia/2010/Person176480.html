Rogeema Kenny’s Summary

Rogeema is currently employed at Eskom Distribution (Western Region) as a Network Analyst Engineer in Network Optimisation Department. Her expertise is in power system analysis and advanced load flow studies.

She is also passionate about developing people and harnessing the potential in them that they may not be able to even see in themselves. This has led her to be a co-author in "Empowered", the latest book in the Wake Up... Live The Life You Love series. Empowered was released during 2009.
