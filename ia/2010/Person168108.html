Raj Sonak’s Summary

Fourteen years’ experience in technology strategy, consulting, product management, network architecture and development, project management, product development, business case formulation, staff management and execution of projects from “cradle to grave” in the areas of telecom, wireless and IT. Proven abilities include management and leadership of large teams, strategic planning, quantitative and qualitative analysis, mastering of new technologies, and the articulation of complex ideas at all vertical and horizontal levels. Proven versatility in multidisciplinary teams. Strong experience with customer and vendor relations. Experience in forecasting, analysis and management of large program budgets over $10 Million.

Specific technology experience in product requirements, network and systems architecture and design, software design and development, testing, field deployments, planning, modeling, analysis and evaluation of technologies and services.
