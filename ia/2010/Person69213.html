Fay MacDonald’s Summary

In a nutshell....
Founder of Diosa Media creating client communities & conversations through marketing & communications, social media & events. Enthusiast of creative business goodness.

I deliver results because I am..., resourceful, creative, energetic, skilled, determined, flexible, responsive and committed.

My passion is based on working with companies to develop their 'personality' and how to improve their business through creative communications including relationship marketing and bespoke events that really speak to their clients and prospects and set them apart from their competitors.

What can I offer?
Clever events that can cost about the same as a couple of full page adverts and have far more impact! These can be business to business, product or service launches, community initiatives or just a fantastic party.

Marketing, promotions and networking that communicate what you really want to say, to the right people.

Consultancy and advice on what media and marketing mediums will work most effectively for your business including online/offline PR and face to face networking.

Ideas, research, article and copywriting, press releases and media relations that work to complement all of the above!

Established business contacts, colleagues and associates that enables Diosa Media to offer a comprehensive service to meet your marketing and event needs.

I have over ten years experience in a range of corporate, commercial and not-for-profit environments where I've worked to increase impact within B2B and B2C target markets.

A key ability of mine is to offer innovative ideas in combination with a high level of process, meticulous organisation and delivery and measurement of results.

I am fuelled by understanding the ‘bigger picture’ (strategy) within organisations and fusing bold concepts (innovation)with achievable, targeted goals (tactical delivery).
