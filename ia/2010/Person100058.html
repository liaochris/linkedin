Joanne Situ Allen’s Summary

With over eight years of experience in conservation science and an established business philosophy of sustainability and progressive stakeholder engagement, Joanne Situ Allen brings a vast skill set to Canopy Strategies, LLC as Managing Partner. She specializes in creative solutions and sustainability programs that enhance an organization’s value while leveraging the power of corporate, industry and community stakeholders. Joanne’s ability to synthesize large amounts of information and pare it down to relevantly effectual directives produces solutions that have resulted in simple, communicable and successful agendas for myriad organizations. Her ingenuity, human understanding and aptness to build alliances have contributed to unprecedented collaboration between cities, businesses, educational institutions and non-profits. Joanne approaches all situations about which she is impassioned with empathy and a bent ear, allowing for solutions that are infused with the needs of all involved. In addition, she has always embraced her Chinese heritage, allowing her to approach a wide variety of people and situations and create solutions from a uniquely culturally diverse perspective. When not in the office, you can find her hiking, exploring the performing arts, drumming, and creating art.
