Daniel Portuga :)’s Summary

My name is Daniel Portuga. I was born on 1980, in São Paulo, Brazil. My heart is Brazilian, but my blood is Portuguese. I am currently living in London, have a European Citizenship and have been working in online advertising since 1998. I graduated with a BA in Advertising and Marketing and post-graduated in Design Management from the “Faculdade de Belas Artes” in São Paulo.

I currently work in London at The Rapp as a Senior Digital Designer. While in London, I had the opportunity to work in Profero, Saint @ RKCR Y&R and The Bio Agency. In Brazil, I have worked as a Creative Supervisor and Senior Art Director at Rapp Digital, an online Direct Marketing agency and a member of the Rapp Groups and the Omnicom Conglomerate.

During my career I have had the opportunity to work with almost 80 clients in 4 differents countries, such as Accor, Avon, Boomerang TV, Cartoon Network, Coca-Cola, Ibis and Mercure Hotel, Itau Bank, Itaucard, Lloyd's bank, Mc Donald’s, Nestle, NHS, Nokia, Petrobras, Pfizer, Philips, Sky TV, Samsung, Santander Bank, Roche, Skype, Telefonica, Volkswagen, 3M Post-it and many more.

I have won some awards for my work including 4 shortlists in Cannes, FWA, Adobe Edge, 2 shortlists in the BIMA Awards , shortlist in LIA, 2 shortlists on the MM online, gold and silver in the International Gramado Festival, bronze on the ABEMD, 2 silvers on the “Desencannes”, 2 golds in “Peixe Grande”, bronze in Ibest grand prix, 2 golds in Ibest and 3 golds in American Design CA.

I have also given seminars at the Pixel Show, one of the largest design events in Brazil and a had my work exhibited in the gallery of modern art in Slovakia. Articles and interviews over 30 magazines and blogs around the world. Besides working well in a team, I am also very committed, hard-working, able to meet tight deadlines and comptetent at prioritising my work effectively.
