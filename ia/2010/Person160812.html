Paul Gail’s Summary

Recruiting is what I love to do, and I love helping candidates by really understanding their passions and interests instead of jamming them into the closest open job. When I bring the right candidate to the right role at the right company, EVERYBODY wins.

Email me your resume at paul.gail@triadresources.com

I am a technical recruiter with experience managing the full recruiting cycle including:
Making initial candidate contacts (cold calls, active and passive candidates, referrals)
Manage ongoing candidate and client relationships
Clarify and qualify position requirements and requisitions
Qualify candidates for technical skills, culture fit, realistic expectations, availability, and potential red flags
Present opportunities to candidates and candidates to clients
Coordinate interviews (phone and face to face)
Negotiate salaries and/or contract rates
Extend job offers and close candidates
Filling jobs!

Quality Assurance (QA) and Quality Management Testers and Managers (Automated, Manual, white box, black box)

Business Intelligence Analysts and Developers (SSRS, SSAS, Business Objects, Cognos, Oracle BI suite, Spotfire)
