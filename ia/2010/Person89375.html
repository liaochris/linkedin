Jane Woolsey Business Organizing Consult. CPO®’s Summary

With over 25 years in business, I have a wealth of experience in all areas of corporate administration at a senior level and have lent my expertise to a variety of clients across diverse industry sectors. They include provincial and municipal governments, investment, insurance, marketing, advertising, education,and financial institutions including pension fundss.

Our services include a comprehensive training program, offered on an in-person or virtual basis, for managing both paper and electronic information including emails.

A related company, NADUM (www.nadum.com), offers a number of corporate training programs including Facilitation and Managing Effective Meetings. Visit the website for more course details and our client list.

I am currently the Past President of The Professional Organizers in Canada Association and am one of the first few Professional Organizers in Canada to achieve the designation of CPO® ~ Certified Professional Organizer through the Board of Certification for Professional Organizers (BCPO). I am also a nominee for the 2008 & 2009 Harold Taylor Award which recognizes an individual's contribution to the organizing industry.

My media appearances include CBC National Morning News, CTV Newsnet, CTV Noon news, Globe & Mail, Toronto Star, Rogers Cable and Reader's Digest.
