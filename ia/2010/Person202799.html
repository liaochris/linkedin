Thomas Brian Lang’s Summary

I am working towards to obtaining an administration position in the healthcare industry in marketing or operations. A marketing position would make use of my creativity skills and operations would make use of my problem solving skills.

My studies focused on qualitative and quantitative analyses of companies and the development and implementation of strategic strategies. In my spare time, I volunteer at the Saban Free Clinic in Los Angeles in the medical records department. I get to help out and learn about a facet of the administration side.
