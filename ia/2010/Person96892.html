  • Senior Designer

    Energy BBDO

    (Empresa pública; OMC; Marketing y publicidad industry)

    junio de 2006 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Jessica considers herself a visual thinker, and more importantly, a creative ‘do-er.’ Her work can be seen in the latest pages of internationally recognized Design annuals, including Graphis and How International, among others. Jessica thrives on positive energy, has a maddening love affair with typography, adores the color wheel, and over indulges in anything letterpress. She’s ever thankful for key commands, appreciates hand-lettered type, and spends countless hours getting lost in D-I-Y Blogs. She believes in designing with Reason, and that the best kind of Design starts first and foremost, with a great Idea. Jessica often finds much of her inspiration outside the office, using her creativity in a variety of artistic avenues: including designing jewelry, re-furbing vintage furniture, and baking a mean banana bread.

    Jessica has been with Energy BBDO for over four years and has worked on a variety of clients, including Jim Beam, The Art Institute of Chicago, Illinois Lottery, Bayer Diabetes Care, Wrigley Jr. Company, On the Border Mexican Restaurant & Cantina, Romano’s Macaroni Grill, and non-profit accounts such as River North Chicago Dance Company and Wild Life Trust.
