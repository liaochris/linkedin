Ryan Smith’s Summary

The Law Office of Ryan P. Smith, PLC helps individuals with a variety of issues - including litigation and arbitration - involving banks, brokerage firms, and other financial institutions and their products and services.

The Firm assists our nation’s veterans in preparing, presenting and prosecuting claims for benefits before the Department of Veterans Affairs and litigating their claims before the U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims.

Please visit www.ryanpsmith.com for more complete information about my practice and "L'awful Prose" - my blog on issues concerning the securities industry and veterans benefits.

Ryan Smith’s Specialties:

Litigating or arbitrating claims involving financial products and the brokerage industry before FINRA Dispute Resolution as well as in civil courts in Maryland, Virginia and the District of Columbia.

Representing veterans' claims for benefits before the Department of Veterans Affairs and litigating their claims before the U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims.
