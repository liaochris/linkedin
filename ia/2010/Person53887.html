Dee Adams’s Summary

Innovation, design thinking, Interaction design, visual graphic design, illustration, technical scientific/medical/botanical illustation, website design, design speaking. Also a visual artist/painter skilled in art marketing and self promotion.

I am a Senior Designer with over 11 years experience covering the roles of Design Manager, Mentor, Interaction Designer, Information Architect, Art Director, Illustrator, and Visual Designer supporting the development of large scale web sites, web applications, and enterprise software and desktop applications.

I am proud to have built the long-standing reputation as a highly respected contributing member on all of my teams across many level of business. Always an advocate for user centered design, my approach is a holistic one that examines all areas of product development that encourages other designers to mold themselves into multi-disciplinarians with a vast and senior level skill set.

I am seeking a role that leverages my many hybrid abilities and broad design knowledge-base. I am especially looking forward to working with teams who may have trouble translating mental models, broad or vague requirements, and brief design concepts into tangible products. I seek to be an advocate for user centered design, holistic design practices, and personal development within your organization or industry.
