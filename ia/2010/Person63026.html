Elan Perach’s Summary

I have over three years experience in online marketing with a focus on Google Adwords Pay Per click advertising. Over the years, I am continually amazed and astounded by the power of PPC advertising, due to its advanced and precise nature. Unlike traditional forms of marketing: TV, print and radio, that can not accurately calculate ROI, PPC is extremely accurate. It is a priority for me to keep up with the latest developments in the PPC industry, I read many blogs, participate in twitter and share information at blog.ppcproz.com

My PPC expertise includes:
• Fast and efficient work flow integration between Adwords Editor and Excel, A vital technique for creating and managing large scale accounts.
• In-depth keyword research and brainstorming to find all of the keywords relevant to the client.
• Structuring accounts according to Quality Score best practice.
• Account/Campaign/Adgroup/Keyword/Budget setup.
• A/B Ad Split Testing for optimal CTR, conversion rates and most importantly CPA/CPL.
• A/B Split Testing Landing Pages.
• Landing page reports for converting keywords/adgroups.
• Regular Search Query Reporting - adding new phrase/exact kws.
• Adding negative keywords.
• Bid Management for optimal ROI according to goal CPA (Bid = conversion rate X CPA)
• Google Analytics - tracking exact search queries, user behavior and conversion funnels to learn and help optimize PPC campaigns.
• Keyword Research/intelligence - legally observing the work of competitors using Spyfu, Google Keyword Tool, Compete, Keyword Discovery, Wordtracker and PPC Web Spy.
• Social Media Marketing - Using online social platforms such as Facebook, Myspace and Twitter to create a following and a status of "authority".
• Blog sites - Creating and designing blogs.

Elan Perach’s Specialties:

PPC, SMO, Adwords, Excel, Adwords Editor, Yahoo, Google Analytics, Conversion, Landing Pages Optimizations, Keyword Research, Ad Text copy, Advanced bid optimization.
