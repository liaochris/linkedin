Curtis Burnside’s Summary

A Florida Native, Curtis was educated at a number of the country’s top Preparatory schools in the northeast U.S. and attended Hawaii Loa College in Kailua Hawaii.

His sales career started in the family insurance business in Coral Gables Florida in the early 1980s. As the agency manager and later Regional Manager of the insurance operation, Curtis held numerous insurance industry certifications and licenses in Florida and other states over the years.

Specializing in developing and managing agent based sales organizations, most of them scratch agency operations, Curtis won a number of Presidential, Regional and District awards for sales production and agency profitability.

In 1999, Curtis embarked on a second career with BellSouth Business. Promoted numerous times during his tenure, he received training at "BellSouth University" as an e-Business and data networking specialist.

As The Telecom Broker with an ever expanding technology offering, Curtis possesses unique certified training and relationships, along with the customer centric mindset to assist businesses in implementing the right technology, at the right time at the right price.

Why use a telecom broker? As your Network Consultant, we provide the following benefits:

1. Assistance is uncovering your technology and telecom needs to provide the most cost effective and efficient holistic solution.
2. Design of the desired solution before taking it to market for pricing.
3. Independent, unbiased proposals that cannot be obtained directly from carrier representatives. You can get your own 'real time" quotes on my website at www.TheTelecomBroker.com
4. Assistance in negotiating pricing and terms, then reviewing your contract for services.
5. Project management of the desired solution on a turn key basis. Once your services have been ordered, we assure they're properly installed.
6. On-going layer of customer service not normally available if you're dealing directly with the provider's sales force.
