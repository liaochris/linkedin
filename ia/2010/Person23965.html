Bill Palladino’s Summary

Based in Traverse City, Michigan and Dallas, Texas. Business Consultant, Executive Coach, Instructional Designer, and Keynote Speaker with over 20 years of management and business ownership experience. Client list includes Fortune 100 companies, nonprofits, community groups and governmental agencies. Palladino provides high level business consulting & coaching services with specializations in change management, innovation & ideation, future scenario modeling, meeting facilitation, and the language of business.

Through his company, Krios Consulting, he partners with the world's leading business authors to deliver cutting edge training programs based on proven methodologies, (John Kotter, Joel Barker, Keith Ferrazzi, Patrick Lencioni, Mark Thompson, Doug Hall...and others.)

Palladino specializes in keynote speeches on change management and corporate, community, and cultural innovation. He provides high energy, content-rich presentations that are both memorable and practical. He leaves audiences moved and challenged to utilize the knowledge he presents for the immediate good of their organizations. Whether the topic is Change Management, Innovation, or Future Scenarios, his years of business, entertainment and journalism experience ensure an upbeat strong message to any audience.

He currently serves on the following nonprofit boards:
the Michigan Land Use Institute www.mlui.org
SEEDS www.ecoseeds.org
Interlochen Public Radio Advisory Board www.interlochen.edu/ipr

Bill Palladino’s Specialties:

Change management, meeting facilitation, strategic exploration, community conversations.

Business sectors of specific interest include: Healthcare, Education, and Economic Development.
