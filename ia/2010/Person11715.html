Andrew Dyrdahl’s Summary

A Director of Design with experience in all aspects of garment design and production, as well as design team leadership. Effective in trend and color research, incorporating global travel to fashion capitals as well as travel to garment factories. Extensive experience in quality garment construction with an emphasis on fit and attention to technical product detailing. Proven leader in building team morale and development of my support staff. Additional education at The Fashion Institute of Technology, New York City.

Andrew Dyrdahl’s Specialties:

Trend research / color direction / eye for detail / great taste!
I am also highly experienced in garment construction and pattern making -- I am a corset maker and an accomplished bridal designer.
I am skilled at fashion show production and I have experience as a photo shoot stylist.
