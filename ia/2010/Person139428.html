Tim McQuillin’s Summary

For 18 years I have helped drive growth for innovative mobile startups and high-growth divisions of leading telecom/IT corporations, primarily through marketing analytics, product marketing, brand strategy, advertising, and account development.

I am a versatile hands-on marketer with a head for strategy and analytics, and broad experience within the mobile ecosystem - service providers, smartphones, mobile OS, content, fixed-mobile convergence, mobile marketing, and mobile consumers.
