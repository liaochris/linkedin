Andy Steuer’s Summary

I am currently the CEO and Founder of MerchEngines, an online marketing company focused on traffic generation solutions combined with "post click marketing" solutions to help our clients convert their traffic efficiently. In July 2009, MerchEngines was acquired by Deluxe Coproration.

With over 15 years of leadership and entrepreneurial experience, I have been an executive manager for publicly held media companies, and have led business development and product teams for several top-10 Internet media companies including Xoom.com/NBCi, GO.com/Infoseek/Disney, and CNET structuring and executing strategic partnerships.

I am also a Managing Partner in an investment fund, called Andojo LP. I was Vice President of Product Marketing and Development for Xoom.com/NBCi where I defined and developed the Product Management process and managed a team to build products that grew it's membership base from 1 million members to over 16 million within 1 1/2 years. Prior to Xoom.com/NBCi I was instrumental with the launch of Disney's GO.com. Prior to GO.com, I was a Group Product Manager at Infoseek.

In 1993, I co-wrote and produced a documentary on the history of U.S.cable television for NHK (Japanese Public Television). I hold an MA from the Annenberg School for Communication at University of Southern California (1994), and a Certificate in Recording Arts and Sciences from University of California, Los Angeles (1992) and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from San Diego State University (1990).
