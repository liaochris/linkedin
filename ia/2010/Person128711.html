Luis Belen’s Summary

With over 10 years, managing and implementing enterprise technology projects, Luis is the President and Chief Executive Officer of VWCGlobal, a leader in strategic business development and technology solutions for small to mid-sized businesses. He plays a critical role in the continued development of the company’s strategic growth, and strategic partnerships.

He also serves as the National CIO & EVP for Latinos in Information Sciences and Technology Association (LISTA), and is responsible for the implementation of LISTA’s National Health IT initiative.
