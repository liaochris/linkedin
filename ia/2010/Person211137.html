    Myriad Entertainment

    (Media Production industry)

    2009 — Present (1 year )

    MYRIAD ENTERTAINMENT is a new, unique production financing company. Specializing in international co-productions, Myriad is the first of its kind in the U.S.

    Myriad Entertainment launched to fill a vital need in international nonfiction television production – brokering international partnerships and co-production funding. Now, production companies and broadcasters can turn to a team of television professionals to finance projects from various international sources.

    Myriad Entertainment is a new venture from co-founder and former General Manager of ABC News Productions Lisa Zeff and international production and development executive, Véronique Bernard. Together, their backgrounds and expertise comprise extensive broadcast and productions contacts on five continents and in all the major international television markets.

    Based in the U.S. Myriad finds international funding partners for U.S. film and television projects and U.S. funding partners for international projects. Focus is nonfiction programming including science, history, reality, lifestyle, specialist factual, specials and events, with an emphasis on series.

    Myriad Entertainment looks for unique projects that have wide appeal for which it can provide development, packaging, pre-sales, co-production and executive producing services.
