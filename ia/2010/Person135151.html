MARSHA MARSH’s Summary

MARSHA MARSH, CPCC, is an executive coach, expert team facilitator, conflict resolution specialist, and the Founder & President of Marsha Marsh Coaching, Inc. Marsha excels in leadership development and in the design of comprehensive programs for teams geared toward optimal performance. Her model centers on the reversal of unhealthy trends in team interaction using conflict as a platform for collaboration, innovation.

Marsha is an acute observer of the human condition. Her coaching style is a blend of nurturing, intuition, and the telling of the hard truth. She ushers executives out of their comfort zones and inspires them to work better with their organizations. Marsha is able to drill down to the core to produce transformational change. She calls individuals forth to assert their strengths and realize their leadership potential.

Marsha is also a dedicated student of team dynamics. She aligns individual strengths of each member to the overall team needs, and facilitates the necessary communication for sustainable high level performance. Marsha works through the people issues underlying conflict to get to organizational change at all levels. “When we can move conflict from being something personal and tense to merely a difference of opinion, it becomes useful,” states Marsha, “these differences managed effectively create innovation.”

Marsha facilitates small and large meetings according to organizational needs. She designs experiential exercises and delivers inspiring curriculums around diversity, communication, accountability, and trust. Her programs incorporate coaching, leadership training, and conflict resolution and resonate with all participants. The message is in creating a unified corporate vision of an exciting future. “I’m an optimist,” she says, “but I can honor and appreciate people’s difficulties because I’ve been there myself. I see purpose and I don’t see limits. Anything is possible.”
