  • Assistant Printer on the Queen Mary 2

    Cunard Line

    (Public Company; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    February 2006 — August 2006 (7 months)

    Working on the Queen Mary 2 cruise ship was a great opportunity to gain experience in the print and design industry while traveling and meeting people from all over the world. In the print shop, I ran a
    Heidelberg QM46 2-Color offset printing press and worked as a graphic designer for all printed materials the passengers received. Printed jobs included the Daily Programme, international newspapers, information on ports of call, departmental forms, and all restaurant menus.

I also designed flyers for notice boards, crew announcements and publicity materials for an on-board luxury-goods company. This contract completed my college co-op requirements. There was never a dull moment; working 7 days per week, being available for last-minute jobs and problem solving issues related to working at sea.
