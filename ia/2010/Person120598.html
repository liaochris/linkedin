Being driven by new technology and staying ahead of the industry is key. Today more than ever green is the thought when engaging in any IT project. With this in mind I have joined forces with Mushroom Networks.

Mushroom Networks’ products are based on the unique and patented Broadband Bonding® technology developed by their engineering team through extensive research and development.

Mushroom Networks was founded in 2004 as a spin-off from the University of California at San Diego (UCSD). The leadership team has over 70 years of collective cutting edge research and development experience in the networking field, and has management experiences at a wide array of companies from small and large technology enterprises to venture capital.

The first product offering of Mushroom Networks using the Broadband Bonding® technology is the TRUFFLE Broadband Bonding Network Appliance (BBNA). The TRUFFLE BBNA6401 is a standalone product designed for a small and medium sized business (SMB) that implements aggregation of multiple Internet access resources, for example, multiple DSL lines, cable modems, T1 access lines, etc. The SMB can use the BBNA to obtain faster and more reliable Internet access in a cost effective manner than is otherwise possible.

If you are tired of slow Internet speeds contact me for information on the Mushroom Network Broadband Bonding Appliance. Bonding is not just for T1's anymore.
