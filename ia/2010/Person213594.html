Walter Gibson’s Summary

CEO, General and Operational Management and Business Development of Heavy Engineering Service and Manufacturing businesses servicing the Mining, Construction, Sugar, Shipping and Agricultural industries of Qld and NSW.

Experienced national and international Networker serving as CEO of Mackay Regional Development Bureau and the Boards of Austmine ($3.6b exports) and MESCA and as a consultant/advisor for the Mining Services Export Business of Austrade.
