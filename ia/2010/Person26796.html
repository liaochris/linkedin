    JobDig

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    JobDig, Inc is an employment focused media, advertising, and technology company dedicated to serving the recruitment advertising industry since 2001. Our mission is to provide tools to improve the process of attracting, engaging, and acquiring great talent. We accomplish this through delivering quality service through our 3 brands: JobDig (job board / niche print publication), JobDigTracker (low-cost SaaS based Applicant Tracking System), and LinkUp (nationally branded job search engine).

    The JobDig product line has been around since 2001. We serve over 6,000 customers via multiple media channels – online, print, radio, & television. We provide enhanced traditional recruitment solutions at an affordable price.

    JobDigTracker, our SaaS-based Applicant Tracking System (ATS) designed for small- to mid-sized companies (under 2500 employees) entered the market in 2008. We offer a powerful, full-featured ATS at competitive price.

    Responsibilities: Strategic Planning, New Product Development / Brand Building, Process Assessment / Automation / Improvement, Marketing / Development Liaison, Business Development, Organizational Reporting and Metrics, Offshore and General Vendor Management, Contract Negotiations, Budgeting

    LinkUp Job Search Engine

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    The LinkUp Job Search Engine (www.linkup.com) was launched in 2006 as an alternative to the major job boards. Similar to how Google indexes the web, LinkUp automatically collects and indexes jobs directly from the original source -- the company website. With this strategy, LinkUp aims to be the purest database of jobs on the web – no duplicate or outdated job listings. We focus on providing performance based (PPC) advertising and technology solutions to strategic, forward-thinking companies and recruitment advertising agencies.
