K. Edward Bachrington’s Summary

A former U.S. Marine with over 13 years of experience in mergers & acquisitions, senior management and business ownership. Has owned and operated several successful businesses including a software company, a management consulting firm, an office equipment company, and a real estate investment company. Extensive business background contibutes to being a sought after advisor to middle-market businesses, private equity funds, and an expert witness for legal proceedings. Tenacious yet objective and fair in dealings with staff, investors, peers, executives and board members.

Well-rounded operations professional draws on depth of experience to bring important insights to bear on complex issues faced by clients. Years of profit and loss management, budgeting, inventory control, supply chain logistics, and expense management experience. Experience with multinational corporations and middle-market experience provide added perspective and the ability to execute successfully.

At Merity, Anderson & Associates, focus is on providing overall financial and operational leadership for the firm. Responsibility for guiding the firm in working with lower middle-market companies, business owners, executives and management teams on financial recapitalizations, enterprise valuations, mergers and acquisitions advice, and exit planning services.
