TJ Marta’s Summary

Marta on the Markets LLC is an independent financial market research and advisory firm. The timely and concise written commentary and analysis provided by the firm are designed to put global developments into context with particular emphasis on investment ramifications. Speaking engagements, whether as a keynote speaker or as part of a panel discussion, are approached with the multiple goals of offering information, provoking thought and dialogue, and doing so in an entertaining and engaging manner. The firm also provides customized consulting services, which include efforts such as overviews of the market environment for M&A, capital projects or debt issuance, and commitments to monthly or quarterly meetings for senior management.

TJ Marta is Founder and Chief Market Strategist of Marta on the Markets LLC. He is Editor and Publisher of the daily Morning Minute and a regular contributor to several notable investment publications. TJ is a respected strategist and speaker with more than 20 years of Wall Street and business experience.

Marta uses his technical knowledge, market experience and passion for history to provide not only context for global developments, but more importantly, the investment implications of those developments.

TJ is routinely sought after worldwide as an expert speaker and commentator on market and economic developments and is regularly quoted in top‐tier media outlets, including BNN, Bloomberg, Reuters, CNBC, Fox Business News, Barron’s, Forbes, Investor’s Business Daily and MarketWatch. His professional affiliations include the National Association for Business Economics and the Money Marketeers of NYU's Stern School.
