Phillip Crone’s Summary

I spent nearly 20 years in the High Tech Arena. I sold my computer supply distributor in 1997 and took the next 8.5 years off. During this time I played over 3000 rounds of golf, wrote a motivational book titled, A Little Bit Over a Long Period of time,and spent much time with my wife and 2 children.

In July of 2007 I went back to work with Marsh, the largest Insurance Brokerage in the world. I'm a Vice President in sales in charge of developing releationships and contacts to stimulate new business. My Office is in Buckhead, just north of the City of Atlanta.
