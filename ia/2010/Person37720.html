Chris Pitisci’s Summary

At Ferreri Search we are focused exclusively on providing recruitment services in the information technology and accounting & finance sectors within the Tampa Bay area to employers and individuals. Our three practices are Executive Search, Permanent Placement, and Contract/Project Staffing.

Our team has extensive experience in the field of professional recruitment and customer service within accounting, finance, and information technology. This combined experience gives us the ability to better understand your career ambitions and more importantly help you achieve these goals.

Chris Pitisci’s Specialties:

At Ferreri Search our mission is to fulfill the accounting & finance and information technology recruitment needs of our clients and support their business while exceeding expectations for service, quality and value. We will strive to out perform our competition on a consistent basis and our goal is to establish long-term loyalty by being honest and fair and “going the extra mile”. By providing exceptional personalized service, we hope to create a positive,long term business relationship.
