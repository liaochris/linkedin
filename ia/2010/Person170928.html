Tiffany Torgan’s Summary

Tiffany Torgan has been a highly successful real estate professional for 19 years and during that time, she has developed extensive knowledge and expertise in all aspects of the real estate industry.

Combining her exhaustive industry knowledge and exceptional sales skills, Tiffany was Centex's top-producing selling agent for seven consecutive years before becoming an independent realtor with RE/MAX Plano in 1997. Within one year of joining RE/MAX, she was responsible for amassing $25 million in real estate transactions, all from a successful web site-based business she established. At the time, she was considered a virtual pioneer in using the Internet to help clients relocate to Dallas from all over the world. Highland Homes and Drees Realtor of the Year In Texas

In 1999, Tiffany relocated to San Diego, CA and brought the Internet-based business with her to the San Diego market. Over the course of her career, Tiffany has closed deals on more than 1,200 homes and has worked closely with hundreds of families, diligently assisting them with every last detail of their transactions. An experienced and effective marketer, Tiffany has significantly raised the profile of Prestige Properties Realty by appearing as San Diego’s real estate expert on Home & Garden TV’s “What you get for the Money” program. She has also been featured and interviewed on local market conditions on several local news channels such as San Diego’s KUSI Channel 9, KGTV Channel 10, and KFMB Channel 8.

After obtaining her California Broker's license in July of 2002, Tiffany founded Prestige Properties Realty, Inc. She is currently the agency’s President and Broker. In 2007 she joined as the California Broker of Record, New Century Real Estate Group out of Texas, and 2009 California Broker for Luxe Real Estate Group (REO Specialist) out of Arizona.
