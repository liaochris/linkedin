Shawn Santos’s Summary

As Director of Programs & Community and TSIA’s Social Media Research Lead, Shawn focuses on social media strategy for technology companies, program portfolio management, market research, community building, word of mouth marketing strategies and producing insightful content for the technology services industry.

Previously, Shawn worked as Marketing Manager of Global Services at Agilent Technologies and has held several management roles in the wine industry, including Product Manager for leading wine consultancy Enologix and General Manager for Global Vintage Research. He has also held several notable consulting engagements in the wine, technology, and marketing agency industries as well as winemaking positions in California and France.

Shawn has a BS degree in Molecular Biology from the University of California at Santa Cruz, and currently moonlights as Managing Director for Hope Animal Network, a non-profit organization focused on advancing animal welfare in developing countries.
