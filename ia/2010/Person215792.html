William Kassebaum’s Summary

William R. Kassebaum, P.E., age 45, is co-founder, President, CEO and serves on the Board of Managers. With over 22 years working in the private and government sectors, Mr. Kassebaum has extensive engineering, executive management, and entreprenuerial experience. His pioneering work at the Naval Air Warfare Center earned him and his team the prestigious National Performance Review "Heroes Of Government Reinvention" award presented by the Secretary of Defense and the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff on behalf of Vice President Al Gore. While working with private companies such as Escient, LLC, he lead teams that developed award winning products. Mr. Kassebaum is also the co-founder of Stellarwind Development, LLC, in 1997, the incubator company for Stellarwind Bio Energy. Mr. Kassebaum holds a Master's Degree in Electrical Engineering from Purdue University and is a licensed Professional Engineer in the State of Indiana. He currently serves as a Director of the Central Indiana Section of the IEEE, Pre-College Educational Activities Coordinator for Region 4 of the IEEE, Founder and Co-Chair of the Central Indiana Engineering Consultants’ Network, and a corresponding member of the Alliance of IEEE Consultants’ Network (AICN), a committee of the IEEE-USA.
