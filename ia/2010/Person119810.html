Kelley Quain Troia’s Summary

Kelley Troia joined Peter Mayer Advertising in August 2009 as General Manager responsible for overseeing all of the agency's internal operations, including all process, procedures, and systems. Prior to Peter Mayer, Kelley spent 9 years at Walmart Stores. As Senior Director of Marketing Operations at Walmart Stores, she oversaw all Marketing portfolio/project management, systems, and sourcing. Kelley was part of the core Marketing transformation team at Walmart, working closely with executive leadership to roll out the "Save Money. Live Better." rebranding campaign. Kelley spearhead all Marketing technology needs, procuring a digital asset management system which contains all Walmart collateral as well as a workflow (MRM) management system both of which launched in early 2008. Before relocating to Arkansas, Kelley spent 5 years in San Francisco in the Walmart.com office as the 2nd member of the Marketing/Creative department - a team that eventually grew to 30. As the Sr. Producer, Kelley's role was to oversee all Walmart.com Marketing projects as well as manage the day to day operations of the team.

Kelley grew up in Northern New Jersey and began her professional career in printing as a print production manager (after a 4 year stint in college as a disc jockey), moving to the online space in the early 90's. She spent 5 years in New York City managing the development of websites for CMPnet, Music Boulevard, and CDNOW before moving to San Francisco to "follow the gold rush" in January, 2000.
