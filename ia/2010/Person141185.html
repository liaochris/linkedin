Meredith Collins’s Summary

Short:

Social Media Strategist and Professional working on recommendations, implementation, education and maintenance of Social Media strategy (Facebook Business Pages, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter and niched sites) for business. Streaming media and eLearning specialist, social media optimisation, Key Note presenter and speaker. LION - Open Networker

Long:

Video.Social.Marketing has evolved through years of experience in education, eLearning, streaming media, personal development/coaching and NLP.

Working with many small businesses and entrepreneurs, through affiliation with Loral Langemeier (The Millionaire Maker) it became clear that small businesses and entrepreneurs were clearly confused by social spaces and the application of the social spaces to their business.

We have worked to change this, demystify the space and support business people to get a handle on the paradigm shift in marketing and relationship development and PR. In working with Business, we can create optimal Facebook Business Pages, LinkedIn profiles, Twitter accounts and advise on the use of video for social networking and business growth.

We offer Social Media Health Checks to give you some feedback on your social media presence or The Blueprint to Video Social Marketing takes readers through a seven step process (with videos) to building a social media strategy for their business.

Exciting work for those who go with it - with lots of support, experts and access to help if required - educate yourself now so you can make a choice about which way you move.
