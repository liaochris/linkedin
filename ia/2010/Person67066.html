Erin Malus’s Summary

I split my time between creating and editing quality SEO-friendly Web content with Remilon, and developing my publishing company, Bare Bones Press & Productions.

As a proud ally to trans, genderqueer, and gender variant people of all types, my goals with Bare Bones include creating a safe space in my community for the other-gendered folks who may not feel they fit within the narrow confines of "gay and lesbian" groups. I aim to use my publishing background to offer professional attention, advice and advocacy to people who may not have a voice otherwise.
Though my lived experience does not include that of not identifying with the gender assigned to me at birth, I believe in advocating for gender nonconformists for more reasons than I could list here.
