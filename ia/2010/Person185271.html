Sean Dailey’s Summary

The Difficult we do right away

The Impossible takes slightly longer.

Started in the news business with two internships at KGTV-ABC and KNSD-NBC. After which I spent many years in the freelance arena doing everything from Producing to Videography to On-line Video Editing to Directing broadcast TV and corporate video productions.

In 1991 I entered into the Software multimedia production business. Became part of the cutting edge in computer generated content and digital media distribution.

In 2000, I decided to get back into live TV news with birth of Fox 6 News in San Diego. Here I am 10 years later Directing fast-paced, graphic intensive newscasts ...and I'm having a BLAST!

I am currently seeking freelance directing and producing opportunities.
