Steve Godman’s Summary

For 12 years have worked at the sharp-end of digital marketing technologies and have held sales, senior sales and senior management positions. Currently at PlayNetwork Europe, the international arm of PlayNetwork Inc, most recently voted the worlds No.1 provider of music and media solutions for brands. Extensive experience in contract negotiation, developing new revenue streams through conceiving new products and delivering business solutions that really work for my customers.
