Bob Pickell’s Summary

Over 30 years of experience in the Graphic Arts industry. I consult with my clients to maximize their marketing dollar through creative manufacturing of their printed materials. Because of having digital, sheet fed and heat-set web capabilities, along with our hi-definition printing technology and ability to offer unique coating options, we have produced an array of award-winning printed pieces, including direct mail, annual reports, product catalogs and publications.

Environmental responsibility has long been part of Intelligencer's culture with our comprehensive sustainability program. We are FSC & SFI certified, Rainforest Alliance certified, print with low or no VOC RENEW bio-based inks and utilize wind energy to power our presses.

We operate a 70,000 square foot mailing and fulfillment center.

Intelligencer is 216 years young and continually growing in capability and technology. I look forward to new and beneficially rewarding business relationships.

Let's put the POWER OF PRINT to your advantage.
