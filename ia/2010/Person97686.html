Jennifer Greenfelder’s Summary

Public relations professional with more than five years of experience in media relations, executive media training, event management, social media efforts, writing and editing.

Dedicated to helping clients build their brands, increase awareness, enhance return on investment (ROI) of their promotional dollars and ultimately enhance their revenue.

I have represented companies in a variety of sectors including automotive suppliers, trade groups and associations, materials suppliers, sports, non-profit organizations and others.

I also serve as co-chair of the automotive council for the Public Relations Society of America Detroit chapter.

Jennifer Greenfelder’s Specialties:

Media relations; PR program planning / research; writing / editing (news releases, newsletters, advisories, biographies, fact sheets, historical backgrounders, etc.); event management; media training; social media guidance and implementation
