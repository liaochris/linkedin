Ben Edwards’s Summary

I am Executive Vice President of The Economist Group and Publisher of The Economist online, where journalists, readers and guests analyse and debate the global agenda.

Ben Edwards’s Specialties:

Digital strategy and development, change management, enterprise transformation, applying agile software development methods to corporate management, certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) for agile software development

Ben Edwards’s Experience

  • Executive Vice President

    The Economist Group

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Publishing industry)

    February 2007 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    I run The Economist's online publishing business. I am responsible for setting online strategy and developing the online product. I manage a global leadership team which designs the customer experience, develops the product, builds the technology platform, acquires the customers and fulfills client orders.

  • Director, New Media Communications

    IBM

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; IBM; Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2005 — February 2007 (1 year 10 months)

    I was responsible for driving the development and adoption of a wide range of new communications technologies, from blogs and podcasts to wikis, social-networking software and interactive web tools and techniques to reach customers and for internal enterprise transformation within IBM.

  • Reporter and Editor

    The Economist

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Newspapers industry)

    January 1996 — May 2005 (9 years 5 months)

    In my nine years at The Economist, I held three different jobs:

    Finance correspondent in London, covering investment banking (1996-1998)
    Japan Correspondent and Tokyo Bureau Chief in Tokyo, covering Japanese business, finance and politics (1998-2001)
    American Business Editor in New York, covering American Business (2001-2005)

  • Reporter

    Euromoney Magazine

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Newspapers industry)

    January 1993 — December 1995 (3 years )

    I reported on investment banking and the capital markets. I researched and wrote in-depth investigative financial journalism from London, Moscow, Johannesburg, New Delhi, Bangkok, Budapest and many other cities.
