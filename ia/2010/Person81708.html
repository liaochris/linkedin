Henta Ellis’s Summary

I am now a Grammy Nominated singer/songwriter, performer and recording artist, originally from the United Kingdom and I have been writing music for over 30 years now.

I write heartfelt songs and instrumental music, whose lyrical and content range from secular, to spiritual and feature melody, rhythm with strong sensual vocals.

In June 2006 I made a life changing decision as a result of my newfound musical collaborations over the internet which subsequently brought me from Guildford in the UK to Seattle, US.

August 2009 - I have just released my new album called 'Laserium for the Soul' written especially for relaxation and chakra activation, available through my website and itunes. This has just been nominated for a Grammy in the Best New Age Album Category!

In March 2008 I released my third album ‘Daisy Dancing’ and organised a number of CD Release Parties in Seattle which helped to raise my profile here in Seattle.

I am also an active and founder member of Electronic Music Collective LLC who organises a variety of musical events, as well as a member of the Recording Academy,ASCAP and Sound Exchange.

Henta Ellis’s Specialties:

Music for healing, Surround Sound performance experience, musical collaborations, library music, piano teaching for children.
