Rob Peterson’s Summary

Rob Peterson joined Downtown Kalamazoo Inc. in 2008 in a new position for the organization, Business Recruitment and Retention Director. His responsibilities include creating programs to assist businesses that choose to locate within Kalamazoo’s DDA boundaries, such as the proposed Retail Incubator concept currently being planned. His background is in commercial real estate development, residential real estate brokerage, and mortgage banking. He holds a BA and an MBA from Western Michigan University.
