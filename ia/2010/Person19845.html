Barbara Groth’s Summary

"Delivering Marketing Excellence"

Barbara is a highly driven, self-starter, with 14 years experience executing corporate sales / marketing communication programs and business development across highly regulated industries, including medical device and financial services.


Barbara's pragmatic leadership style directly, quickly and methodically moves organizations forward and she enthusiastically welcomes challenges, solves problems, and offers organizational structure. She is known as the "go-to" person who is resourceful, adaptable, and committed to business results and customer satisfaction.


Her strengths include conceptualizing, orchestrating and administering sales and marketing strategies that effectively reinforce and build the brand image and directly impact organizational performance.

"Barbara is a resourceful, action-oriented person who is a natural manager and finds herself in command through her ability to plan and keep both long and short-term objectives clearly in mind. She is a highly practical person whose agile mind and skills combine well to generate fast results."
