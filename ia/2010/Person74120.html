Geoffrey Mccaleb’s Summary

I am a true Texan (ignoring the fact that I was born in Chicago). Have been living in London since 2001 but am spending an increasing amount of time in Silicon Valley. I love music, technology, and have an unhealthy addiction to twitter and BBQ'ing.

I started Nsyght as a side-project, experimenting with mashing a users social graph (via bookmarks) into a standard web search in order to improve relevancy. However, after realizing how deeply users were shifting their social and search patterns, and how important emerging platforms are, I evolved the idea towards including all social events (posts) as well. I spent nearly a year with my co-founder Dogacan to get the company to a place that it could become full time.

We're very excited about the months ahead.
