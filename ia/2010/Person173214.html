Rick Morris’s Summary

rick morris
creative director. designer. illustrator
based in los angeles. california
rick@nobleassassins.com

rick has been steadily on the television and film graphics scene for nearly 12 years doing titles. show opens. network packages. commercials and interactive media content for many of the heavy heavies in the biz. he’s taught and spoken on motion design at art center college. american film institute. ucla and otis college of design (where he has been faculty for the past 5 years) as well as numerous conferences.
his various works have been featured in creativity. communication arts. print. type directors club. resmag. zoo magazine. british design + art direction. lemon magazine. the n.y. festivals. lovebytes. rotterdam. resfest and oberhausen film festivals + others. clients include: hbo. mtv networks. 20th century fox. apple. abc. toyota + others. he’s inspired by almost everything around him and wears a backpack for picking up anything he may find on the way to anywhere he goes.
