Paulette Bennett ACIR, CIR, CTS’s Summary

Senior level recruiter with more than 15 years experience creating and delivering effective recruiting strategies in corporate and staffing recruitment environments.

Self-starter who excels within a dynamic, multi-tasking environment. Adept in building effective relationships with hiring managers and candidates to achieve successful closures, retention, and job satisfaction, within a spectrum of industry verticals including: retail, life sciences, engineering, IT, accounting and finance, healthcare, and non-profit.

AIRS Advanced Certified Internet Recruiter (ACIR), AIRS Certified Internet Recruiter (CIR), and Certified Temporary Staffing Specialist (CTS). Member, National Association of Personnel Services.
