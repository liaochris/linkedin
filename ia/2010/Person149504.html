Gary Jenkins’s Summary

Public Speaker, past Area International Speaking and Humorous Speech Champion (Toastmasters International UK). Conference Chair, Trainer and Coach.

Gary firmly believes that, today we live in a business culture that abuses the art and science of public speaking. We power up our computers and present poorly prepared and hideously designed presentations to audiences who want to be inspired but are left unfulfilled.

Where you bored with your last presentation? Did it motivate and change lives? Perhaps it ended a few lives. It is time to end the abuse of PowerPoint today!

It is my mission to bring my award winning style of public speaking and presentation, providing information that will inspire your audience. My background is varied, as a boy I was a cowboy in my native Arizona, I was a combat officer in Southeast Asia, I spent 32 years working as a counsellor with 24 years in addiction treatment specifically. I have been a business owner as well as a general manager of a multimillion pound environmental solutions firm. I know a thing or two about :

Disability Awareness
Leadership
Management
Motivation
NLP
Psychology to include Hypnosis and EFT (Emotional Freedom Technique)
Time Management

Gary has a M.C. (Master of Counselling) and a BA in Psychology from Arizona State University, Tempe, Arizona and also has a D.D. (Doctorate of Divinity). Gary is a Medical Hypnotherapist, Master NLP Practitioner, Master EFT Practioner, Certified Additions Therapist, Certified Sex Addictions Counsellor and many other boring professional designations.

Whenever Gary speaks the common feedback he receives is “your speech has produced results that have been measurable over time”. I don’t believe I can receive any greater compliment.
