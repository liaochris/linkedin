Scott Archibald’s Summary

Scott Archibald is a Managing Director of Bender Consulting, a specialized management consulting firm focused on critical, high-value projects for the world’s leading businesses and institutions. We help leaders make lasting, measurable improvements to their organizations. Bender Consulting consultants have delivered exceptional results at companies including AT&T, Chevron, Kellog Brown & Root, Tesoro Petroleum, Tyson, Waste Management and many others.

Scott helps teams navigate the complexities of IT & Business transformations, and brings a foundation of project and change management to his work to quickly align stakeholders, ensure commitment, and facilitate progress.

Scott's consulting skills are complemented by his nearly twenty years of industry experience at Hewlett-Packard & Intel Corp. where he served in senior leadership roles in multiple functional areas including Information Technology, HP Services, and Mergers & Acquisitions implementation.

Scott Archibald’s Specialties:

Transformational Change Implementation; Mergers & Acquisitions; Strategy & Planning; Organization Design & Capability Development; IT Infrastructure Management; Business Operations; 501c3/non-profits.
