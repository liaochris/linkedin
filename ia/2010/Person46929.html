Daniela Guido’s Summary

As Marketing Communicator, I have been involved in Customer Experience Management strategies, dealing with the full spectrum of consumer relations.

Contributing to intense attitudinal research has led to the application of data to social influence marketing and market analysis. Using tools from the field of Voice of the Customer, such as Listening Platforms, gives me access to knowledge on how to build synchronized customer and employee engagement, retention and loyalty.

A post-master’s in Public Opinion and Journalism background gives me the skills to comprehend customer behaviour and work accordingly to develop products and promote core values – internally and externally.

The understanding of different media channels, including new media, and how best to use them assist in producing effective print, broadcast and online communication, enabling me to better transmit the corporate message.

I feel responsible for the protection and enhancement of the image of the organization, leading to increased value for shareholders and employees.
