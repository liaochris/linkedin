Nancy Zablud, MBA’s Summary

During my financial career, my roles within an organization have been both tactical and strategic in nature. As an entrepreneur and business owner, my roles included leadership, business development, lead generation, financial executive, create, and implement processes and procedures, and education and training of staff members. My Controller roles included timely financial and managerial reporting and analysis, strategic cash management and budget planning, establishment of accounting internal controls, successfully reorganized the accounting department, developed and documented processes and procedures improving workflow both at the corporate and desk level, resulting in first department desk level training manuals, effectively cross-trained all team members, and greatly reduced accounting errors with desk manuals.
