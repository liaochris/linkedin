Jenni Beattie’s Experience

  • Director of Digital Consulting

    The Digital Edge

    (Market Research industry)

    July 2008 — April 2009 (10 months)

    The Digital Edge launched in October 2008 taking to market new market research products focusing on gaining richer consumer insights via the use of interactive web 2.0 inspired platforms and methodologies.

    With a background in the digital space my role was to integrate web 2.0 philosophy into the market research practice and develop relevant and innovative products for todays web 2.0 marketing environment.

    I developed the methodology, implemented and managed the bespoke Online Communities platform which helps gain rich consumer insights through a facebook style online environment - an Australian first.

    During my role I managed Online Research Communities for: Kimberly Clark, Love Communication, Kelloggs, KFC, CBA, Weight Watchers and others.

    Online Communities can be set up for npd, ad testing, comms testing, creative concepts and makes use of multimedia functionality where participants can upload photos and videos and enable 'organic' discussion to form.

    I also initiated an Online Reputation Audit product- a combination of web crawl technology with social media /pr strategy primarily used by pr agencies.

    In addition, I produced and delivered Social Media training for pr agencies. (references below)

  • Online Research Manager/ Social Media Strategist

    www.networkpr.com.au

    (Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    May 2006 — July 2008 (2 years 3 months)

    First employee of the Online PR division of Network PR.

    Bringing together my 15 years media experience and University qualifications (Bach of Applied Science -Information - UTS) my role is to research and assess the latest in new media/online techniques.

    During my role I developed the Online Engagement Cycle which became the basis for the agencies social media strategy work.
    Methodology: http://tinyurl.com/cbkgo7

    I worked on social media campaigns for Paramount Pictures, Kirrihill Wines and Tourabout.

    I also produced an internal New Media Manual for employees and initiated and developed the methodology for the Online Healthcheck -an online audit for clients.
