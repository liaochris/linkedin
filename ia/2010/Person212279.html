Vinod Paul’s Summary

Founded in 1995, Eze Castle Integration is the market leader in outsourced IT technology and services for hedge funds and other specialty investment-management firms.

Already, more than 450 hedge fund companies - managing more than $200 billion in assets - rely on Eze Castle Integration to be their "virtual CTO" and single source of IT and telecommunications infrastructure.

Eze Castle Integration develops and manages customized, powerful, premium solutions for all of their technology requirements: Startup and Relocation, Outsource Technology Support, Telecommunications, Network Infrastructure Design and Management, Business Continuity Planning and Disaster Recovery, Storage and Internet Services. Eze Castle Integration has points of presence in major financial centers with offices in Boston, New York, San Francisco, Greenwich, Conn., Los Angeles, and Minneapolis.
