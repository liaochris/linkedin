Anthony Risser’s Summary

Consulting Neuropsychology, Diagnostic Assessment, CNS, Pharmaceutical Clinical Trials, Rater Competency Services, Efficacy and Screening Measurement, Clinical Assessment of Neuropsychological Disorders (e.g., aphasia and language, executive function, attention).

Clinical Trials (CNS): Scientific Affairs consultation --- therapeutic areas include Alzheimer's, chronic pain, sleep disorders, epilepsy, stroke, ADHD, mood disorders; applications include protocol development, computerized testing, rater/CRA training, feasibility, subject recruitment/retention, investigator relations, and teamed capabilities & new-business services with Business Development.

Internet applications in psychology education and in medical/health domains. This has included social-networking communities for patients, caregivers, and professionals, as well as online teaching using educational software platforms and providing Internet content management.

Undergraduate/graduate teaching history: Biopsychology, Physiological Psychology, Assessment, Developmental Neuropsychology, Neuropsychological Rehabilitation, Learning and Behaviour.
