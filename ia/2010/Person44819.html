Crystal Blahnik’s Summary

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Business with a major in Advertising and a Certificate of Direct Marketing, and have six years of distinctive marketing and advertising experience.

Fulfilling my desire to MOVE WEST, I relocated to Reno, NV in the fall of 2007 from Detroit, MI. Back in Michigan, I worked in account management (with an amazing team!) at J. Walter Thompson on the Brand Content & Alliances / Entertainment team for our client Ford Motor Company. Prior to JWT, I started off my career in Traffic at McCann Erickson.

I miss my friends and fam back in Michigan, but now that I've made the jump across the country - I'll never look back!

I love Social Media, Internet Marketing, Branded Entertainment, running unique and exciting on-air and web-based Promotions and the art of Advertising in general.
