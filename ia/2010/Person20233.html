Barry Hurd’s Summary

I have been in the 'online' field for over a decade. My past has included multi-million dollar projects for a list of Fortune 500 clients, a variety of high-end projects that had significant impact on the world around me, and a some challenging stories along the way.

My overall experience and focus is in reaching new markets by utilizing social media. As president of 123 Social Media, I provide online marketing and advertising consulting working with search engine marketing and leveraging social media communities. With over 15 years of entrepreneurial Internet and online marketing experience- I have become an author and prolific blogger. I have reached online audiences around the world and have been involved in numerous efforts to bring forth technical innovation through online business models. Past projects have included NIKE, REI, TMP Worldwide, Monster.com, Verizon Superpages, Intuit, and RISMedia.

Barry Hurd’s Specialties:

I have a developed understanding of blogging services and online social networking trends. I also have a background in online recruiting and developing.
