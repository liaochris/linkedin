    General Compression (GC) is developing a near-isothermal compressor/expander module to create 1MW to 1,000 MW, 8 to 300 hour, compressed air energy storage projects. General Compression Advanced Energy Storage (“GCAES™”) projects enable renewable generators to output energy to almost any power curve required by a customer. GCAES™ units require no fuel to turn air into power — reducing operating, fuel, and permitting costs compared to other compressed air energy storage technologies, and expanding the number of potential project sites — and have a roundtrip electrical efficiency of over 70%.
