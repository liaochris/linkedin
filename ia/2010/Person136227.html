Maryna Riggien’s Summary

I qualified with a B.Sc. Occupational Therapy from the University of Cape Town in South Africa. I completed a Diploma in Business Management and confirmed my interest in combining my medical knowledge with business skills. I entered the Pharmaceutical Sales industry as a Medical Sales Representative and held various roles such as GP Rep, Specialist Rep, Hospital Tender Rep, Regional Sales Manager (Sandoz), Business Unit Manager Cardiovascular (Novartis), Business Manager (GSK), National Sales Manager (NBI) - spanning both firstline and second line management roles in Sales.

I obtained a Master of Science in Healthcare Management and qualified as Business Coach.

In January 2001 I established Maryna Norbury & Associates, trading as MNA Recruiting, entering the Pharmaceutical and Medical Recruitment market. The business expanded to a National level, recruiting predominantly for Sales & Marketing roles as well as supplying dedicated Contract Field Forces for both National and regional teams. I sold the business after immigrating to Australia.

I worked as Senior Recruitment Consultant with Pharmaceutical and Medical Professionals (PMP) based in Sydney. My specialty focus was Sales & Marketing Recruitment and I recruited for both Permanent and Contract Placements across Australia and New Zealand.

My experience within the Healthcare Industry spans in excess of 25 years and I have gained insights through the various roles I have held. I prioritize Service Delivery for both Candidates and Clients and enjoyed success in the Australian Healthcare Recruitment market as evidenced by achieving the top recruiter position at PMP (2009/2010).

My role as General Manager at McKenzie Consulting consolidates my Healthcare Recruitment career, in terms of significant impact in healthcare recruitment, people management and business development.

Maryna Riggien’s Specialties:

My specialty focus is Sales & Marketing Recruitment at both Executive and non-Executive levels. I have recruited for roles such as GP Sales Representatives, Specialist Representatives, Territory Manager, Device Sales Representatives, Clinical Educators, Business Analysts, Business Development Managers, District Sales Managers, Regional Sales Managers, National Sales Managers, Product Managers, Marketing Managers, Brand Managers, Business Unit Managers, Commercial Manager amongst others.
