Anne B. Freedman’s Summary

Speaking and Content Development Specialist.

Having made the transition myself from a quiet and invisible news reporter to a front-line speaker and community leader, my company is dedicated to helping others achieve success in their business and nonprofit pursuits through engaging, persuasive communication skills and strategic content development.

Why are so many people uncomfortable and often ineffective speaking in front of a group? It’s because most of us have been taught how to write for the “eye,” (in reports, emails, and proposals, for example), but we not for the “mouth.”

So to assure your messages hit the mark whenever you speak, we’ve devised a proven, individualized process that transforms your words and ideas into more powerfully spoken results. For important remarks to the Board of Directors or your team. Elevator speeches and networking. Sales and investor presentations. Speeches, media and other public speaking opportunities.

No matter what the goal or experience level, we specialize in addressing the communication challenges of executives, business owners, sales reps, civic leaders and celebrities. Privately and in Small Groups.

Speakout, Inc. Building revenues, relationships and reputation … word by word .
Since 1990.

Choose among these ways to reach your speaking or sales objectives:

1) Confidential executive coaching and message/content development.
2) Customized small group programs onsite at your organization or via Internet.
3) The Presentation Lab™, One-Day and Weekend Programs to create your Elevator Speech and more, open to individuals from different companies or offered in-house
4) Do-it-yourself, fill-in-the-blank, quick and easy-to-use E-books that guide you through all phases of writing and producing your own outstanding messages and presentations (on our website at www.speakoutinc.com).
5) Invite Anne B. Freedman to speak or facilitate at your next conference or board retreat
