Jon Quist’s Interests:

Professionally I am a REALTOR but a very unusual one. I call myself Tucson’s Buyer’s Only Agent, as I NEVER work for sellers, only the buyer. Everyone else tries to do both. Certainly legal (in Arizona) but I always wonder who is really getting taken care of best, with the constant temptation of a double commission. As I never take listings from sellers I never have to face that scenario. Personal interests include golf, walking/hiking and reading. I also follow thoroughbred horse racing and breeding.
