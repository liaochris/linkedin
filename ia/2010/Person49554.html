    (Broadcast Media industry)

    September 1988 — December 2008 (20 years 4 months)

    As director and “face” of smallest sports department in market, helped lead 6 & 11pm newscasts to #1 for first time in history.

    As producer and co-host, led local high school highlights show to #1 in its time slot.

    Performed numerous satellite and microwave live shots from national events, such as: Super Bowl, NCAA basketball Final Four, NBA playoffs, NHL Stanley Cup finals, Daytona 500, Orange Bowl, Peach Bowl, Independence Bowl and Seattle Bowl.
