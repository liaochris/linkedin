Ron Hobbs’s Summary

Dr. Ron Hobbs is a naturopathic physician and educator. For much of his career Dr. Hobbs has worked to recruit and train the next generation of naturopathic physicians. He is currently Public Programs Assistant at the Museum of Flight. Dr. Hobbs is a Solar System Ambassador for NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory. Dr. Hobbs has taught, lectured, and written on topics as diverse as herbal medicine, public health, astronomy, and mountaineering. He is a co-author of Treating Arthritis in The Natural Pharmacist Series.

I consistently met admissions goals, most notably an increase in the enrollment in the percentage of underrepresented minorities.
