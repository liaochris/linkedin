Sonali Dhulap’s Summary

I am currently working as a Subject Matter Expert in the IP Operations team at Cheers Interactive (I) Ltd.

I recently completed my post graduate diploma in Patents law, from National Law University of Hyderabad.
I am interested in Patent law cases and Patent drafting. I have cleared the certificate course exam on Intellectual Property from WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organization).
I am preparing for the national patent agent exam.

I have a bachelors degree in Microbiology and a masters in Clinical Research. I also have a post graduate diploma in Pharmaceutical Management. I am well-versed in English, Marathi, Hindi and German languages. I am currently learning French.

Apart from my academic background, I am an avid writer. I have my own blog at Wordpress. (http://sonalismrules.wordpress.com)
I have also won many awards and recognitions for my poetry.
My poem- "Change is a part of life" received the Editor's choice award in the year 2001-2002 from the International Library of poets, Maryland, USA and was later published in a book, "Miracles of Nature".
Besides that, many of poems are hosted by various websites like the voicenet and poetry-club.com.

I am also an artistic person and an amateur cartoonist. I have attended many workshops and participated in Art competitions.
