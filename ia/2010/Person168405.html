Ralph Weeks’s Summary

My mission at IPITEK is to provide extremely efficient networking solutions having unbeatable reliability, data security, and transport protection (with efficiency judged on system level CAPEX, OPEX, footprint, and power consumption). Though frequently first to market with new capabilities, we remain interoperable with all other vendor's standards-based equipment to protect our clients’ past capital expenditures.

What makes us different is our patent portfolio fed by ongoing internal R&D. This allows continuous integration of more capability into smaller and easier to manage platforms, quite often collapsing several layers of network equipment and management overhead into a single more efficient layer.

For example, our latest Carrier Ethernet product integrates 3 layers (access/switching, aggregation, and DWDM transport) with simultaneous TDM, packet, SONET, and PDH traffic into a single open-architecture/standards-based 1RU platform pumping 20 Gbits/sec while consuming only 80W. This massive carbon footprint and physical size reduction is matched by a dramatic shrinkage in CAPEX and OPEX for the overall communications infrastructure not even dreamed of a few years ago!

We actively gather input from communication service providers to focus not only our R&D into photonics but also our development of management and operations software. That’s how we got to our converged network platform (T1/E1, DS-3, OC-3, 1GigE, and 10GigE) with full capability to add, drop, drop-and-continue any point-to-point or multi-point circuits. The integrated carrier grade QOS and rate control allows SLA-compliant coexistence of these traffic types and the switched architecture ensures low end-to-end latency and jitter for real-time services (along with efficient and robust timing and synchronization, Synch-E and IEEE 1588v2).
