Colleen Zaruba’s Summary

As senior partner for Aqua Funding Group and a licensed California real estate broker, I arrange bridge and hard money loans for commercial properties from private money sources. Rates range from 5% for conventional guidelines to 10-15% for quick turnaround true hard money.

Maximum loan amounts right now to $100 million.

We are also providing discount currency exchange to import-export companies and receivables factoring, in addition to commercial real estate lending.

Just added merchant account business credit lines as well.

Currently we are seeking REO, note portfolios and CMOs.

We are also the direct representative for a private issuer of MTNs and VRDNs as well as providing access to private trading platforms in the U.S. and Europe.

We are very interested in expanding our investor and lender network at the moment, and welcome all inquiries.
