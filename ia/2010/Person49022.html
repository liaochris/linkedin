Darren Kerr’s Summary

In demand as a much sought after business communication professional, Darren has been actively engaged as a Creative Director, Executive Producer and Experiential Marketer in the creative design and delivery of innovative and engaging live communication programs, strategic marketing, brand experiences, & B2B/B2C events for a broad range of clients.

In producing events and live communication marketing programs, Darren draws on his business theatre fundamentals, showcraft technique, practical and prudent project management skills, targeted program design creation, eclectic styling senses, creative production and strategic marketing expertise.

He is well regarded for producing distinctive experiential marketing programs that effectively address key strategic communication objectives, on budget and on time, through the creation of memorable event experiences - regardless of complexity and scale.

Darren Kerr has produced events throughout Australia as well as staging quality events in Auckland, Bangkok, Siem Reap, Macau, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Bali, Shanghai, Beijing, Seoul, Fiji, and Hong Kong. He has also consulted on and contributed to the creative direction of events held in London, Paris, Las Vegas, San Francisco, and Orlando.

He has built an impressive portfolio of produced events for many of Australia’s leading organizations and in addition to valued clients drawn from USA, UK, and Asian based companies. These have included the Horace Mann Insurance Company, Qantas, NCR, Amway China and Europe, Novell, Miki Prune, Oracle, Prudential, insurance giant AIA, Microsoft, CSL, Clinton Global Initiative, and Audi. He has also worked on the delivery of signature events such as Openworld, BrainShare, TechED, Fusion, and the Presidents Challenge.

In recent years he has broadened his professional business communication offering to encompass brand personality management services and the seeding and nuturing of creative cultures within organizations.

Darren Kerr’s Specialties:

strategic and tactical design and delivery of live communications, special event management, experiential marketing, immersive environments, brand experiences, brand equity, strategic and tactical marketing, corporate communications, media relations, inspired team leadership, change management, corporate cultural re-orientation, growth enabler, presentation technique and coaching, project management, consultative/solution selling, contract negotiations, organizational creativity.
