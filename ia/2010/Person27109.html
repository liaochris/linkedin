Brandon Kerkstra’s Summary

I have worked with hundreds of companies in a wide range of industries during the past eight years as president of MSG. In addition to building the successful business, we have facilitated the implementation of ISO 17025, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, APQP, CMMI & SPICE.
