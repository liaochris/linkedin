Michael Pangas’s Summary

Please add me at mpangas@grnsouthbeach.com and/or follow me on Twitter at http://twitter.com/michaelpangas.

A former Hollywood executive, Michael Pangas is now the Managing Partner of Global Recruiters of South Beach ("GRN South Beach").

A native of Akron, Michael began his career working for the CPA firm of KPMG in Cleveland as an Audit Manager. After seven years at KPMG, he was recruited by The Walt Disney Company in Los Angeles, where he worked first in Corporate Audit, and then at Buena Vista Television & Production. While in Audit, Michael’s areas of responsibility included The Walt Disney Studios (i.e., Motion Pictures, Television & Home Entertainment), Hollywood Records, The Disney Store, and Disneyland. In 1993, he led the financial due diligence for Disney’s acquisition of Miramax Films.

After four years at Disney, Michael began a nine year executive career with Sony Pictures Entertainment (five years in Los Angeles & four years in Miami). While at Sony, he was CFO/COO for Columbia TriStar International Television. In addition, he led financial due diligence for several of Sony’s key investments and acquisitions (including Sony’s purchase of Telemundo in 1998). In 1999, Michael moved to Miami where he helped launch Sony's Miami-based regional operating headquarters for Latin American Television.

In 2004, Michael’s passion for recruiting and hiring talent, combined with his entrepreneurial spirit, lead him to Global Recruiters Network. Recognizing the need for a premier recruiting firm in the Entertainment & Media industries, he launched a GRN office in Miami.
