Bryan Thompson’s Summary

Mr. Thompson is an Oracle Consultant with over 10 years of functional and technical implementation experience within the Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, Life Science, Publishing and Education sectors. With an expertise in Oracle Order to Cash, he has participated in several global implementations of the Oracle E-Business Suite and has broad experience in other areas of Supply Chain Management (SCM). As an implementation lead, Mr. Thompson provides business process analysis, scope definition & design, module configuration, client training and go-live support services. In addition to his functional skills, Mr. Thompson has extensive technical experience with custom Oracle forms and reports development, PL/SQL programming and data analysis for the Oracle Enterprise Suite, SCT Banner and other Oracle-based systems.

Expert in Order Management, Advanced Pricing, Shipping Execution, Inventory, Receivables, TCA and iPayment (Payments) modules.
Secondary expertise in Cost, Manufacturing, Projects, Purchasing, Planning and Configurator.

Bryan Thompson’s Specialties:

Primary Expertise in Oracle R12 & 11i Consulting, ERP Consulting, Order to Cash Business Processes, Systems Integration, Systems Consolidation and Relational Database Development.

Secondary Expertise in Web Development, Database Development, eCommerce, and Web Hosting Management.
