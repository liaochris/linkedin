Robert Feigin’s Summary

I am a business lawyer solving a wide range of problems. My clients include software companies, distributors, retailers, health care providers, recruiters, designers, contractors and service providers. I also represent both businesses and individuals in employment / severance matters and civil disputes. My colleagues at Hutchings Barsamian also offer expertise in estate planning, tax law and representation of religious organizations.

Start-Up: Choice of Entity and Formation. LLC Operating Agreements. Shareholders' Agreements. Partnership Agreements. Articles of Organization and By-Laws.

Software and Internet: License Agreements. Service Agreements. Maintenance and Support Agreements. SaaS. Website Terms of Use and Privacy Policies.

General Business Matters: Promissory Notes. Security Agreements. Non-Disclosure Agreements. Vendor Agreements. Franchise Agreements. Strategic Alliance Agreements. Finders Fee and Brokerage Agreements. Representation in civil disputes and litigation.

Buying and Selling Businesses: Letters of Intent / Term Sheets. Buy-Sell Agreements. Stock Purchase Agreements. Asset Purchase Agreements. Dissolution.

Employment: Employment, Consultant, Non-Compete, Non-Solicitation and Severance Agreements. Independent Contractor Agreements. Representation of Employers, Doctors, Engineers and other Executives and Professionals.

Real Estate: Commercial and Residential Leases and Subleases. Purchases of real property. Option Agreements.
