Todd Weaver’s Summary

My mission is to help college bound students and their families become aware of and visible to the most appropriate colleges. I also assist with keeping college costs in line with each family’s financial profile and educational objectives. This is accomplished by utilizing a unique college counseling system developed and refined over the past 20 years – College Decision Management.

Disclaimer: I DO NOT sell any financial products.

    I help ease the high anxiety usually derived from the emotional decision making process of finding a college for "junior." My firm assits young men and women who seek admission to colleges and universities that match their level of intellectual curiosity, interests and abilities. Our College Decision Management (CDM) integrated counseling system is distinctly different from what you will find elsewhere because it synchronizes the college search and admissions process with the economic aspects of college. Assessing eligibility for need-based financial aid is only a small part of this effort. The colleges our clients consider and the resources they actually have or want to use to pay for college are essential elements of a customized CDM strategy from the very beginning.
