Bill Fraser’s Summary

Bill brings 20 years of training and professional services management experience with leading Internet technology and enterprise application companies to Workflow Ready. Prior to Workflow Ready, Bill co-founded Mitre Peak, a SharePoint/InfoPath consulting and training services and IT operations services company.

Before Mitre Peak, Bill was Vice President of Product Development and Training at Vianeta, a leading dictation, speech recognition & transcription software provider. Prior to Vianeta, Bill served as Vice President of Professional Services at Xdrive, an Internet infrastructure company that developed technology for content storage and syndication.

Before Xdrive, Bill managed the Professional Services group at Tumbleweed Communications, where he was responsible for application developers, partners and training programs. Bill founded and built the training departments at BroadVision, Healtheon/WebMD, and Tumbleweed, respectively.
