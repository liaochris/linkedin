Naoum Liotas’s Summary

Naoum is an organisational consultant who likes working with people in groups and individually and has artistic inclinations and skills. His current work includes engaging groups and individuals for enhancing their awareness through the use of Arts and Gestalt theory. Collective and individual creative ability and awareness can be enhanced and polished through coaching and work in groups (for example with HR departments). He currently works as a freelancer organisational consultant using arts for training and enjoys speaking about himself in the third person.

Naoum's professional experience includes significant research and project management experience gathered from working for projects funded by the European Commission and the Greek National Government under the University of Macedonia. The main focus of these projects was citizen active participation with the use of Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) - eParticipation and ICT for governance and public administration - eGovernance and eGovernment. During his project management positions, he has submitted successful bids to the European Commission for European funding, working with a diversity of partners from several different cultures. Finally, he has worked as a computer instructor and in the research field and thus has acquired a multitude of skills that are linked with these professions.
