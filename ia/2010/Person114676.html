Kenneth Warn’s Summary

I am a professional engineer with over 12 years of post-graduate work experience including approximately 9 years within state and federal government. My experience includes air quality permitting and policy planning studies, environmental impact analysis (NEPA), and related project management. In addition, I hold graduate degrees in chemical engineering and public policy.

I am ready to enter the next phase in my career by finding opportunities to apply my full range of skill sets in ways that help to build and inform the emerging Green Economy. I seek new challenges that benefit from a transdisciplinary perspective and a desire to be an active participant within the evolving fields of sustainability science.
