Paul Wilson’s Experience

  • Owner

    Varacorp

    (Market Research industry)

    1990 — Present (20 years )

    Successor company to Sights on Service working in niche markets within the market research/mystery shopping industry.

  • Owner

    Sights on Service

    (Market Research industry)

    1990 — 2008 (18 years )

    Sights on Service performs mystery shopping under the tradename Secret Shopper (secretshopper.com). The company is hired by major retailers, hospitality companies and others who want to measure their customer service and conformance with operational procedures. Trained "undercover" shoppers regularly visit the stores and report on their experiences. The data is then compiled by Secret Shopper and sent to corporate management. The company has over 300,000 independent contractor mystery shoppers across the United States in its database. Co-founded and co-owner with Brian Anderson, we sold most of the assets to another entrepreneur who now operates under this name.

  • Owner

    Happenings

    (Fund-Raising industry)

    1972 — 1989 (17 years )

    Happenings was a vertically integrated promotional advertising discount coupon book covering the restaurant, hospitality and entertainment businesses. Over the years we printed and distributed over 1 billion coupons in multiple markets. Previously a competitor with Entertainment Publications, it was acquired by and merged into Entertainment in 1989. Co-founded and co-owned with Brian Anderson.

  • Partner

    Anderson & Wilson, Attorneys

    (Law Practice industry)

    1975 — 1981 (6 years )

    Brian Anderson and I had a small business law practice for a few years, specializing in tax and estate planning. We left the practice of law to follow entrepreneurial pursuits.
