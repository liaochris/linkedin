Lisa Feldon’s Summary

In 2007, I joined McCormack & Farrow as a Partner. McCormack & Farrow is a retained executive search firm that conducts searches in all industries for corporate directors, C-level, division, group and top functional executives on a national basis. I have over twenty years of recruiting experience.

Prior to joining the firm, I held a variety of positions with a Southern California-based firm. For the last ten years of my tenure there, I was Division Director and built a successful division specializing in placement of human resources professionals in a wide variety of industries.

I am actively involved in professional organizations and have held a number of board positions including as President of the Orange County affiliate of the National Human Resources Association. Currently I am on the Board and President of the Healthcare Businesswomen's Assocation (HBA) Southern California Chapter.
