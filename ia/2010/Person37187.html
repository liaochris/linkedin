Chiru Roy’s Summary

I am a communication designer with several years of professional experience in the field of Advertising, Branding, Communication Design, Print and Packaging. I work in various mediums from designing a single logo to creating an entire branding system, a brochure to an entire magazine.

I have worked with brands such as AT&T, Carolina Herrera, Colgate Palmolive, Corbis Images, Discovery Channel, Glaxo Smith Klien, Mark Jacobs, Oxfam, Proform, Unitec, Vatika Group, Westin Resorts to name a few.

My ability to design strategically, with strong visual and typography skills, allows me to work in a fast paced environment.
