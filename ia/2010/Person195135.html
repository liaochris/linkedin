Steve J Bayer’s Summary

As a brand crafter, I select and/or structure the best approaches to expand brand recognition, strengthen consumer relations and increase consumption rates (including sales) of wares such as applications, products and services.

As a design strategist, I research and strategize the implementation of design processes in the production, distribution and marketing of applications, products and services.

As an experience/interaction designer, I design applications, products and services to have maximized rates of consumption while being desirable and easy to learn and use.

When the opportunity presents itself, I voluntarily provide my skills and expertise to open source initiatives and related initiatives which benefit events and activities that support social awareness on issues such as health and safety.

Steve J Bayer’s Specialties:

I design solutions for communication and interface/interaction related issues at consumer contact points to increase: stakeholder satisfaction, consumer loyalty and where applicable, consumption rates of applications, products and services.
