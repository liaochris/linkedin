Scott Allison’s Summary

I am motivated by the desire to build businesses, either in the trenches as chief executive, or as a non-executive board director, or advisor. I’m therefore looking for any businesses that think I may be able to help them go to the next level, or someone with a bright idea and no business experience that needs an experienced business leader to help them get started. Please get in touch, we can have a chat and see if there’s a match.

I love entrepreneurship, and am keen to assist others fulfil their potential, that’s why I am on the board of The Entrepreneurial Exchange, which aims to help entrepreneurs scale their high-growth potential business, and am also a volunteer business advisor to 18-25 year olds at the PSYBT, helping young kids who are just starting out.

I founded my first business in my spare time while at University and since then entrepreneurship and business has become part of my blood. Most recently I was the managing director of abica, a remarkable business telecoms provider. abica was an exciting place to be with a clear vision of the future, and during that time I learned how to build and manage a great team, developed the company culture and core values, repositioned and rebranded the business, and delivered a new set of products to enhance long term shareholder value and bring in new recurring revenue streams. I left at the end of 2009, coinciding with the company winning a prestigious award for Excellence in Customer Service, which recognised the quality of our offering.

Outside of work I love global travel, gadgets, and the Isle of Arran.
