Daniel Afrahim’s Summary

Daniel started working as a freelance web and graphic designer in 2002 for different design firms, such as GraphicIQ. After working on a wide range of projects and getting really passionate about design, he founded Design by Daniel in 2004 where he started to design extensive number of award-winning web sites and print designs for his clients. Daniel recently graduated from New York Institute of Technology with B.A. in Computer Graphics on top of his class. Currently he is working for Blue Fountain Media and various clients in New York City.

Visit www.DesignbyDaniel.com for more information.
