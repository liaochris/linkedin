Vicki Hess, RN, MS, CSP’s Summary

I'm an Escape Artist! I help healthcare and human resources professionals escape from Professional Prison and provide them with a passport to Professional Paradise.

I believe that deep down everyone wants to be the CPO (Chief Paradise Officer) of their job and I provide the tools to get them there. With many years of healthcare (nursing and staff development) experience and a Master's degree in Human Resource Development, I am a resource for companies that want to create highly engaged leaders and employees.

Vicki Hess, RN, MS, CSP’s Specialties:
