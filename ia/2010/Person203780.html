Tim Dixon’s Summary

Over 28 years of progressive senior level business experience including multiple Senior Executive assignments and small business ownership. Currently the Principal-Owner of Apex Consulting, LLC a business advisory service dedicated to helping small and medium-sized business. Tim has been an advisor working directly with business owners and senior executives for over four years. His clients gain assistance in growing their sales and profits, improving processes, strategic planning, sales and marketing, personnel hiring and initiatives, technology use, succession planning and behavioral coaching.

Before founding Apex Consulting, Tim executed an acquisition, turn-around and sale of a local construction company more than doubling revenues in a 12 month period. Fully responsible for all aspects of highly volatile and demanding concrete business including all Sales, Accounting, Operations, Customer Service and Marketing activities.

Past experience includes Executive-level (Vice President) functional responsibility for Product Management, Product Marketing, Corporate Marketing, Business Development and Customer Service in multi-national corporations.

Responsibility for all Product Management, Program Management, Strategic Business Development, Corporate Marketing, Technical Marketing and Segment Marketing activities working for early-stage Silicon Valley start-ups and where over $300M in funding was raised. Negotiation and acquisition of a leading edge nano-technology company to secure both vertical integration cost advantages and a competitive advantage in technology.

Full P&L responsibility for $200M divisional unit of a Fortune 500 company. Director-level Product Management, Strategic Planning and Engineering responsibility for a series of diverse and successful telecommunications and high-speed networking products including the most successful telecom product introduced to the market in the 1990’s.
