Jen Vondenbrink; Trainer and Coach’s Summary

At Life Simplified, I've taken my experiences and training as an internal corporate coach and brought them to people struggling with the chaos of life's transitions. My mantra: "Life is really very simple when we remember to breathe."

I've helped people:

Transition sanely from their "perfect job" to their next "perfect job."

Learn to manage their daily demands and become more relaxed parents.

Go within to find the strength and the courage to grow their businesses.
