John Kirkland’s Summary

Senior corporate development/M&A executive who has successfully created and executed strategic merger, acquisition and divestiture strategies to advance critical growth, market entry and repositioning goals for large and small companies in the U.S. and abroad. Demonstrated success and industry expertise in health care, pharmaceuticals, consumer goods, food, and retail sectors.

• Deal-maker – Negotiated and closed 30 transactions valued at $3.5 billion over a 15-year period.

• Development strategist – Insured alignment of corporate strategic goals with all aspects of M&A deals.

• Project leader – Repeatedly organized and led high-level, multi-disciplinary teams of corporate management and financial, tax and legal specialists to execute complex deals and subsequent integration.

• International specialist – On site for years in Europe, led strategic acquisition/divestiture target screening, due diligence and negotiations. Built strategic alliances, joint ventures, government relations.

• Diverse organizational experience – Thrived in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments, including Fortune 500 companies, family-owed holding companies, non-profits and venture capital.
