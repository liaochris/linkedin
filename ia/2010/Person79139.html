Gwendolyn Andersen’s Summary

An energy analyst with more than sixteen years of experience in renewable and conventional energy, energy efficiency, and climate change. Work has included designing new institutional means of sustainable rural electrification, developing guidelines for retrofitting diesel gensets with renewable energy technologies, training in the installation and maintenance of photovoltaic systems, and contributing to a database of renewable energy projects in order to disseminate lessons learned. My expertise is in the policy, finance, and institutional aspects of renewable energy with particular focus on solar, wind, and micro-hydro.
