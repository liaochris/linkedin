Joe Malinowski (joe@businessnetworkinglife.com)’s Summary

I have been an entrepreneur all of my life, always looking for creative and innovative ideas to create multiple passive streams of income. It all started at the age of 20 when I became a Pre Paid Legal associate and began creating a nationwide agency. Over the past few years it has been an exciting journey making connections and constantly learning.

In October of 2008 Business Networking Life was born which is a networking resource site that continues to grow and open new doors. It has been a pleasure to speak for over two dozen organizations in the past 2 years and I look forward to learn from and benefit everyone I meet.
