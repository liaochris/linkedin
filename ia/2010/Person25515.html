Robert Kraft’s Summary

Attorney and Owner -- Injury, Disability, Immigration, Bankruptcy and Consumer Law. I have been a lawyer in Dallas, Texas since 1971, representing injured and disabled clients. Kraft & Associates has six attorneys and many experienced staff members. We have helped tens of thousands of clients over the years.

We represent clients in the fields of personal injury, Social Security disability, Immigration, Bankruptcy, and Consumer Protection.
