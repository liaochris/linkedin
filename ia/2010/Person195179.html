Steve Kietz’s Summary

BUSINESS LEADER/INNOVATOR/CHANGE AGENT

Steve Kietz is an innovative leader with a unique set of experiences. He has been a highly accomplished executive at Citigroup and JP Morgan Chase, and has successfully lead a Silicon Valley-based start up firm. His management experience includes traditional retail banking, all aspects of credit card marketing, internet banking, and mobile software creation and distribution.

Steve is a founder of Woodbury Advisors, LLC. A strategic consulting firm focused on financial services industry. Steve believes that now is time for major innovation in marketing, products, technology and customer loyalty programs.
