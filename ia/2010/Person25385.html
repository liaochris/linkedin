Bob D Ward’s Summary

Are you thinking about having a post card, poster or brochure printed for your next event or promotion? Are you a small business owner and don’t have a large advertising budget? Have you investigated those online printers that provide beautifully printed pieces, but you’re confused about printing industry terminology? Do you prefer your own custom design and not the online template for your print solution? If you don’t feel confident that you know what you’re doing and don’t want to waste any more time to get your promotional pieces to print, then consider graphic artist Bob Ward of Boston with your next printing project.

Bob has a BFA in graphic design with extensive experience in the printing and advertising industries. He has worked for small nonprofits and large advertising agencies. He is skilled in prepress production for small print jobs or large volume high-res color offset printing. Bob knows exactly how to prepare accurate files with the specifications that provide the image and print quality you want for a professional looking print job. With a studio equipped with an iMac, Adobe CS 4, and an Epson high-res printer, Bob can take on projects of any size in his home studio. He can even provide small print runs in record time to meet your crazy, hectic schedule!

Or, if you prefer, Bob can come to your worksite to provide his expert print production services. He's a team player and able to manage and prioritize multiple projects in fast-paced studio environments. His emphasis is on accuracy, quality and professionalism. He also specializes in photo illustration, graphic design (with strong type sensitivity) and he is an accomplished photographer.

With the advertising world changing so rapidly, Bob is now learning and mastering Internet marketing, online video and social media, and is certified as a Social Media Strategist from Social Media Magic University, 2009.

prepress print production, graphic design, branding campaigns, photo illustration, social media/SEO, video
