jeremy hunsinger’s Summary

My career centers around using information technology to support and encourage development in learning, cultural institutions and systems of governance. I design, build, install, and use a wide variety of open source software to that end. My research centers around ethics, policy, political economy and the meanings found on the internet, its functions, and communities.
