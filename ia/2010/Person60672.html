Duncan Seay’s Summary

Two decades of results oriented, senior executive management experience at consumer product (beverage & nutriceuticals), high tech (e-commerce) and financial services (private equity) companies. Founder and seed financier of start-ups; manager and builder of teams; and operator of fast growth ventures. Actively involved in setting company strategy, as well as managing a venture’s operations, sales, marketing and finance departments. Proven ability to recruit and motivate teams while soliciting the involvement of renowned industry titans. Success has been driven by a keen ability to listen, a passion for innovating, in depth strategic analysis, a laser intuition, a results oriented philosophy, the pursuit of bottoms-up due diligence, entrepreneurial persistence, a strong competitive drive and a healthy dose of professional skepticism.

Duncan Seay’s Specialties:

Industry Expertise: beverage/consumer packaged goods, investment banking, private equity, e-commerce, toy, software and healthcare services.
