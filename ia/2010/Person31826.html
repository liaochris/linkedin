Candice Richardson’s Summary

A child of entertainment, Candice Richardson grew up watching her father performing for audiences locally and nationally all her life. Lead singer of the Seattle vocal group, The Main Attraction, Antowaine Richardson instilled in his children a love for music and the arts as well as the value of education. He also encouraged them to work hard and pursue their interests, whatever they may be. For Candice those interests were reading, writing and show business. As a junior in high school, she was able to fuse those interests together by becoming the Arts and Entertainment Editor for her school newspaper. An early dream of becoming an entertainment journalist led her to achieve a degree in Communications/Journalism at the University of Washington where she was the recipient of a full academic scholarship. During her studies at the university she interned under Shyan Selah at Brave New World, an entertainment company that handles all aspects of entertainment and media including film, records, publishing, promotions and management.The internship opened up a new world for her and expanded her career goals to include working in publicity and promotions in the entertainment industry. While maintaining a steady career as a freelance journalist after graduating college, Candice also sustained a strong relationship with Brave New World. After working for ColorsNW Magazine and writing for The Seattle Medium, her connection to Brave New World led her to join the company full time in November 2007 to head their media/promotions department where she currently serves as the Vice President of Media Relations.
