Nathaniel Pool’s Summary

Mr. Pool is a visionary, results-oriented CEO/ President / Board of Directors Member with unparalleled background in starting, building, and driving multimillion-dollar, high-tech companies to significant success.

Intimately involved in the Internet space since 1993 with over 20 years experience in the satellite communications, cable TV and mortgage banking industries.

An expert on founding, building and growing successful start-ups. A charismatic and dynamic team builder that cultivates a fun and competitive "win-win" environment, in which each team member is energized to perform at the top of their ability, while building loyalty and trust. A marketing and networking guru with a "NO LIMITS" attitude.
