Currently, I am a freelance journalist and online publisher. Writing is my passion. My articles on technology, journalism & social media, and social responsibility have been published in everything from Electronic Design News to the Washington Post.

I'm also a corporate communications consultant. My company, Dan Holden Communications, can help you build a public relations department, create a social media program or expand your existing marketing capabilities. My strong, international team can create websites, help you establish blogs, and get you online with Twitter or Facebook, or organize a customer or community relations program. Are you planning special events here or abroad? We can do that, too!

I have 24 years experience working as a journalist and PR manager covering high-tech companies through significant industry transformations. I've executed award-winning and global public relations programs at three large companies - Altera Corp., TSMC and Cadence Design Systems, building significant brand value for each.

Dan Holden’s Specialties:

My passion is writing. I enjoy translating deep technical subjects into ideas that businesspeople and consumers can understand and use. I make extensive use of social media, including Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn and YouTube as well as other platforms. I'm also a creative writer, working on several songs, plays, stories and movie scripts at any given time.
