Edu Martin Julve’s Summary

I have been working in the CG industry for almost 15 years. During that time I have been involved in all kind of projects ranging from 3D previz, animated TV series, videogames, VFX for TV commercials to Feature Animation films. I also started my own company in year 2000 called Pixel in Motion were I co-developed a couple of TV animated series and Directed a couple of worldwide awarded Short Films. Although I have been working for many years as 3D generalist I have specialized in Lighting and Compositing for animated feature films during the last few years. I currently work as Lighting Techinical Director at Pixar Animation Studios.

Edu Martin Julve’s Specialties:

Lighting, cinematics, filmaking, character modeling
