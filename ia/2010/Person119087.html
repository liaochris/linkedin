Kristi Enigl’s Summary

Career Coaching - Career Management - Career Marketing

I am a Career Coach and Job Search Consultant with 19 years of experience in career coaching, human resource management, executive recruiting and hiring. I specialize in Personal Branding and creating winning strategies for clients experiencing a career transition. I also write resumes that get results - FAST!

As a seasoned public speaker and panelist, I participate in career workshops and seminars, providing training and coaching to all levels of candidates. Last year I conducted hands-on workshops for USC, the American Institute of Architects, LA Chapter, and Dream Big Career Center; and provided resume reviews for Women for Hire.

I have interviewed and coached 1000's of candidates and hired hundreds employees in a broad spectrum of industries. I teach my clients innovative Career Management programs at affordable prices, and focus on what works today, such as personal branding and direct marketing. The majority of my clients have achieved their career goals withing 6 months.

Whether you need a renovation or a ground up career makeover, I can help you get on track!
