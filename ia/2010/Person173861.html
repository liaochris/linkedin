Robert Schwartz’s Summary

I am the Director of Recruitment at Lightspeed Research. Prior to this position as a senior staffing professional, I had been deeply involved in the field of contract recruiting for corporate clients. With over 30 years experience in professional staffing behind me, I have done all facets of corporate staffing, contract staffing and executive search, My corporate contract staffing engagements have included:

Honeywell International
Operative Media
L-3 Communications - Mobile Vision Division
McGraw Hill - Grow Network Division
Quest Diagnostics
Sony Electronics
Aventis Pharmaceuticals

My work and reputation in the field are very well established. I have had many excellent long term corporate clients with strong recommendations of my work. (Many of those endorsements are found on the LINKEDIN profile here.)

Professional corporate staffing particularly in companies where there is a sizeable technology and information systems component to their hiring needs.
