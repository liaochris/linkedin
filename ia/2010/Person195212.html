Steve Latham’s Summary

For the past 7 years, I've been immersed in the world of social media and interactive marketing. During that time I've planned and managed interactive campaigns for companies of all sizes including global leaders (FedEx Kinko's, ConocoPhillips, Continental Airlines), mid-market challengers (The Scooter Store, Lasik Vision Institute, etc.) and startups. I'm also the founder and president of the Houston Interactive Marketing Association an exec committee member of the national IMA alliance.

I'm not a designer or developer, but I am an expert in many areas of interactive including strategic planning, online media planning, measuring results and translating data into actionable insights.

I write and speak 10-15x annually and have recently published some articles and presentations that may be of interest:
-Social Media Strategy http://bit.ly/5Slboq
-Business Case for Social Media http://bit.ly/lEB4b
-Online Demand Generation: Strategy and Metrics http://bit.ly/4F7lRt

You can also find articles and insights on my blog at http://blog.spurinteractive.com

My new interest is social media strategy for brands. If you search for "social media business case" you should find me at/near the top of organic listings. I am always happy to discuss!

Steve Latham’s Specialties:
