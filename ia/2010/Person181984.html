Sandy Williams’s Summary

Strategic Marketing Innovators LLC is a strategic marketing consulting firm with a focus on strategic smart marketing.

The end goal is to empower clients to address complex business challenges with a strategic roadmap that empowers marketing organizations for success!

A strategic roadmap is about creating a unifying theme that gives coherence and direction to the actions and decisions of the marketing organization. It is an integrated, overarching concept of how the marketing organization will support the overall business in creating value for customers and shareholders.

What is the deliverable? A strategic marketing vision that is executable, measurable, and revenue generating.

Marketing Keys for Success:

Optimize -
Evaluate your marketing organization and take what is currently working and make it work to its optimum.

Innovate –
Identify breakthroughs and innovative ideas to ‘WOW’ your customers and create new opportunities for wealth.

Measure –
Determine the ROI on your marketing investments to identify wealth opportunities as well as marketing sinkholes.

Strategic Marketing Innovators marketing principle is to empower organizations to take on a growth-minded strategic approach to address customer’s needs and create a measurable revenue-generating process for your business.

Let us help you be a solutionist in 2010 by creating a marketing strategy that creates differential advantage combined with accountability processes that allow you to act on revenue and cost information faster to close sales and reduce inefficiencies.


Sincerely,
Sandy Williams
