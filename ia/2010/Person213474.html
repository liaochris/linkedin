Kim Bloom’s Summary

Kim R. Bloom combines a demonstrated background in work and project management and client needs focused disciplines with hands-on experience in arts and entertainment, television broadcasting, human resources, recruitment, information technology, sales and marketing, and executive level secretarial and administrative support. Kim has assisted managers, directors, chief executive officers, presidents, vice-presidents and senior-vice presidents from small businesses to large corporations.

A native of Washington, D.C., she earned her B.A. degree cum laude in English and a minor in Journalism from Virginia Union University and received her Graduate Virtual Assistant (GVA) certification from Virtual Assistance U, recognized globally as “The Benchmark for Virtual Assistant Training”. She received her professional training as an actor at the Charleston Stage Company in Charleston, SC and The Theater Lab in Washington, DC. Her acting credits include television commercials, movies and corporate industrial films in the Washington, DC, South Carolina and New York markets, and numerous movie and television extra roles.

She is a proud member of the following professional organizations: Virtual Assistance U (VAU), International Virtual Assistants Association (IVAA), The Theater Lab, The Actors' Center (AC) and Southern Fauquier Business Owners Association.

Kim is dedicated to empowering artists and entertainers, individuals, and small businesses by offering them the support and tools necessary to grow their businesses. Her formal education, professional training and over 15 years of broad work experience encompass disciplines necessary to help solve specialized business problems for Waivasir clients.
