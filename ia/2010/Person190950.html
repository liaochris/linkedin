Bill Marlow’s Summary

I started this company because I really believed that the business world was in need of an alternative to the same old tricks of the trade that continued to leave businesses failing, managers ill equipped to do anything about it, and employees constantly looking to move on to the next assignment. Prior to starting Smart Management 101 I had the opportunity to work in a number of industries such as hospitality, health and wellness, insurance, transportation, and direct sales.

These industries have exposed me to many different people. Business owners, managers, drivers, vendors, trainers, representatives, consultants, and literally 1000’s of employees. I have great resources to assist me in giving businesses an option to up their game.

The severe lack of experienced, well trained managers and business owners is the very reason this company exists. I intend to bridge the gap. Many times managers have not had the benefit of proper training. Many times a business owner cannot find the time to train or possibly does not know how to do it themselves.

This is where I come in. Throughout the past 20+ years I have been exposed to a great many scenarios that business owners and managers face every day. I know the challenges because I have faced them myself. I have lived through dealing with staffing problems, production quotas, facility needs, marketing challenges, customer service issues and the list goes on.

One of my strengths has always been the ability to assess a situation and develop a strategy to create momentum to move things forward. I sincerely believe that I can provide a perspective to your business that will move it in a positive direction. My expertise is not free of charge but then nothing of any real value ever is. I appreciate the opportunity to work with people to create a business or career that is true to their goals and dreams. I look forward to working with you!
