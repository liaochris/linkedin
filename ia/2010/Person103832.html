Jonathan Greechan’s Summary

Jonathan Greechan has been deeply involved in the product management, licensing, distribution, production and B2C/B2B marketing of online media and technology since 2004.

Currently, Jonathan heads up Business Development and Marketing for TheFunded.com, an online community of entrepreneurs, and The Founder Institute, an entrepreneur training program and startup incubator currently on pace to launch 500+ companies per year in over 10 cities. He also is the Lead Producer for The Founder Showcase, a quarterly start-up pitch and networking event based in Silicon Valley.

Previously, Jonathan was the Product Manager for Online Games for RealNetworks, Inc, where he oversaw the online games community/ social-networking toolset and content product strategy, roadmap, and execution. Jonathan joined RealNetworks via their 2007 acquisition of Game Trust, a leading online games community platform company, where he was integral to both the company’s growth and exit as the Director of Content & Marketing. He was also vital to the production and publishing of eight downloadable casual games; including "Shroomz," which won a Billboard award for the "Web/Downloadable Game of the Year" of 2004. Jonathan was previously the youngest member of the IGDA's Online Games SIG Executive Steering Committee, and his writing has been published in Gamasutra, Casual Connect Magazine, and the 2008/2009 IGDA Casual Games Whitepaper. He graduated with a B.A. from Villanova University and received a Graduate Certificate from New York University.
