Marsha Collier’s Summary

Author of the "for Dummies" series on eBay (see Experience below for current list), Twitter and Facebook For Seniors For Dummies, radio host and host of the Public Broadcasting program, "Making Your Fortune Online"

BusinessWeek best seller list author, passed the 1,000,000th book sold and remains the top selling author in the ecommerce field. My books for every level of online seller are published worldwide in Spanish, French, Italian, Chinese, German and special editions in the UK, Canada and Australia.

Host of KTRB 860 AM Computer and Technology Radio - also www.computerandtechnologyradio.com

Embracing social media and working with online businesses to reach their customers through best practices.

Appeared in/on The Today Show, CBS Morning Show, The View, CNN, FOX, ABC, Bloomberg, Martha Stewart Living, Reader's Digest, Fast Company, New York Times, Twit.tv and many more

Marsha Collier’s Specialties:

eBay sales and Marketing, e-commerce, social media, building online businesses, expense reduction and bottom line increases. Online customer service.
