Sohui Won’s Summary

10 years of experience in the UX field as an UI designer and visual designer. Lead overall UI/visual design of software applications and services for mobile devices, home appliances, and the web.

Personal projects and interests in developing emotional objects observed from everyday life. This includes creating interactive spaces using design and art pieces such as lighting, sculptures, furniture, etc. Participated in exhibitions in London and Korea.
