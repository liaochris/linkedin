Rachel Lavern, Personal Development Speaker’s Summary

Rachel teaches women entrepreneurs and business owners who want to earn a 6-figure or 7-figure income how to become WILDLY SUCCESSFUL at selling.

♦ INDIVIDUAL COACHING

Customized coaching programs allow you and me to work 1-on-1 together. The person at the end of the program is hardly recognizable from the person who entered the program.

♦ GROUP AND TEAM COACHING

I offer flexible coaching programs for small groups, departmental (i.e. sales staff, executive board), and large-group coaching tailored to your specific needs.

• Building personal leadership skills
• Increasing sales and profitability--exponentially
• Improving morale and employee relations
• Developing open lines of communication
• Designing dynamic mastermind teams
• Implementing effective systems
• Setting and achieving strategically-defined goals

♦ Presentations and 'Lunch and Learn'

I can give you an overview of any aspect of our work, from individual study programs to the very powerful Partnership Summits where we deliver a day long seminar for your organization bringing together your management team, your suppliers and your customers.

♦ SEMINARS

We offer a number of programs in half-day, full day and customized formats for large or small groups, in-house corporate trainings, executive and personal retreats, and more. Please view our best-selling programs from the menu at www.rachellaverne.lifesuccessconsultants.com.

♦ MASTERMIND GROUPS

A 10-week study that will change the course of your life! During the course of this free, dynamic, 10-week study, you will learn how to apply to your life the foundational principles of Napoleon Hill's number one selling book, "Think and Grow Rich!" This study will afford you the opportunity to join forces and mastermind with a unique group of like-minded individuals who are focused on taking the results, in every area of their life, to a new level.
