Nick Braak’s Summary

IN SHORT: On the hunt for prospects, projects, partnerships, opportunities. NYC and beyond.

Curious? Then email, call or just keep reading...

SKILLS: I bring to the table both the left-brained logic of the tech world, and the right-hand side's creativity and imagination. I am driven to ask questions, see patterns, build, explore, create value and strip away the layers. Conversely I am equally at home rolling up my sleeves and brainstorming web copy, writing a pithy comment, listening to a CEO's concerns or digging through mounds of data in search of the answer.

EXPERIENCE: 20+ years as an independent advisor and problem-solver. 25+ years as a self-made founder and owner of multiple technology and internet ventures in New York City, Santa Fe, NM and online.

WHAT OTHERS SAY: "Nick is a sharp guy with a fresh point-of-view, a sense of humor and a cool head. Knows his stuff. Great insight. Asks a lot of questions. Amazingly resourceful. Has an accent."

HOW DO I DO IT?: By incessantly reading, listening, learning, looking for patterns and testing ideas in macro-economics, marketing, social networks, attention, persuasion, personal development, e-commerce, privacy, innovation, collaboration, sales, psychology, broadband, cyber-crime, virtual business & everything Internet.

GIVING BACK: Sharing knowledge, resources and giving advice and feedback freely. Active participant in the discussion online and offline. Try to speak and listen in the correct 1:2 ratio.

BACKGROUND: Grew up in South Africa, emigrated to the UK in 1979. Then moved to the USA in 1981 to study. Never left. Legal citizen of both the US and New York City (not the same thing.)

EDUCATION: The most useful? My post-grad degree from the University of Life, New York City campus. Our school motto: "If you can make it here you can make it anywhere."
