Paula Zimmerman’s Summary

Video Editor for 12 years, at KBDI Public TV from 2000- 2009. I've focused on promotional spots, but also produced, directed, and edited 5 short films, co-edited a 30 minute documentary for KBDI, and more.

Most recently, I've focused on acquiring remote clients. My work has diversified to include editing of web video spots on various subjects.

Editing and graphic design are my passions, I love taking raw footage and making it into something great. I seek a company that offers a variety of editing opportunities-commercials to corporate presentations, to short and feature length narrative and documentary projects. I can also be a videographer/camera assistant, sound engineer/assistant, lighting assistant, or production assistant. I'm currently seeking to diversify into Multimedia Design.

Actually, I offer more than just editing experience. I have been called upon many times for creative input and project planning expertise. I was involved in coordinating the editing and delivery efforts of USA Talk Network, Inc., the producing entity for "The Aaron Harber Show", as well as editing shows for weekly delivery myself. It was quite a challenge and I consider it a good addition to the other skills I have built during my career. I also hope that it will be valuable to my future endeavors.

My proudest achievement comes from winning the 2008 Colorado Broadcasters' Association Award of Excellence in March 2009 for Best Membership Appeal for my "Donate Your Car" spot. I co-produced, wrote, directed, and edited the spot. If you want to see my best work, check out mystimaginedesign.com.

I design print materials, too-business cards, letterhead, flyers, and direct mail postcards.

I am currently studying other software packages to expand my skills.
