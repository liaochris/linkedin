    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 2007 — Present (3 years )

    The NJRCC Young Business Professionals (YBPs) is the leader for business networking events in Passaic County, consisting of a motivated networking group of focused, talented young professionals ranging in age from 21-40, located throughout NJ. Our lively and fun YBP events give the opportunity for Young Business Professionals to share advice, their experiences, and to help one another develop needed skills in today’s world. We also feel it is our duty to help out in the community and that is why the NJRCC YBPs partner with fantastic charitable organizations at all our events such as the American Cancer Society, Tomorrows Children's Fund, and St. Peter's Haven, to help raise awareness and money for their meaningful cause. Please join us at an upcoming event and build your business while doing something great for the community at the same time!
