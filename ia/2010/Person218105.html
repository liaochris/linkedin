Yvette Kamperin’s Summary

HireBoost is a recruiting company focused on top tier executives, senior managers, business and IT architects and developers. While we have a few very large clients, we predominantly work with startup companies, both pre-revenue as well as high growth companies that are ramping engineering, operations, product management and client services.

Our current and past clients have been within Search Engine Marketing, Digital Media Entertainment, Mobile, Social Networking, Financial Services, Content Management, Customer Analysis and Enterprise Decision Management.

If you are ready to make a career move, we can assist you with your search to target the best position that matches your qualifications and personality.
