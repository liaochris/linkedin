Paul Dooley’s Summary

I have over 15 years experience in senior talent sourcing throughout EMEA, America and the Middle East. My company is in existence since 1967 and has focussed on the knowledge worker sectors i.e. technology, financial & pharmaceutical.

Specialties
Global & local search for senior technical and non-technical candidates for projects ( Directors, Functional Managers, Principals, Sole contributors etc), Talent acquisition, headhunting and recruitment strategy and methods, expert international and local recruiter.

We really enjoy empowering individuals and putting them on projects where they excel. More and more now companies are moving away from the typical hiring model and are interested in a collaborative approach and hiring a person (SME) for a specific deliverable. Time and time again this has proven to be a win win situation for all parties in terms of quality, cost, time etc.

Examples :Set up eBay/PayPal in Ireland, did payroll for circa 200 staff and hiring over 300 FTE's Thanks Gerry, Glenda, Alex and all in PayPal!

Hired and did payroll for AOL Time Warner contract development teams for 4 years. (java, C++, web development, data warehousing etc. ) Thanks Manus!

Global team (US, Canada, South America, Asia and Europe) working in the banking and insurance sector in the data warehousing & SOA arena. Some of the best guys on the planet! Thanks to a fantastic client !

Responsible for the setting up of pilot TQM (Lean, Six Sigma etc.) team for Allied Irish Banks Plc to roll out enterprise wide change (re-engineering). Thanks Marcel and all the guys and girls in AIB.

Currently working on new projects in Saudi to help the banks reengineer their operations. Another very exciting project for "World.Inc" is helping a client set up a new research laboratory in Exascale (Parallel) Computing - machines so powerful they could save the world…!
