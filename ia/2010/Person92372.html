Jo-Ann DeLuca’s Summary

As of July 2009, I became Vice President Sales and Marketing with Explorica Canada, Inc. In this new role, I am responsible for International and Domestic Sales and Marketing in Canada as well as General Management of the Canadian Office.

Prior to my appointment at Explorica, I was Director of Sales for the Higher Education Division at McGraw-Hill Ryerson. Our division experienced its best profitability in 6 years and revenues of $75 Million during my tenure as DOS.

Prior to my appointment as Director of Sales of the Higher Education Division at McGraw-Hill Ryerson, I was the Director of Marketing. In this role, I was responsible for directing a team of marketing professionals that created and executed strategic plans designed to grow revenue in key target markets in the Higher Education space in Canada. Previous to the Director of Marketing role, I held the position of Director of Media Technology also for the McGraw-Hill Ryerson Higher Education Division. In this role I successfully transformed an IT cost center into a revenue generating services group. I was instrumental in positioning McGraw-Hill Ryerson Higher Education as the premier provider of technology based learning solutions. My group created innovative technology solutions in order to drive strategic sales opportunities.

During my career in the publishing industry, I held the following positions: Senior Acquisitions Editor for Computer Information Systems and Management Information Systems in the Greater New York City area. As well, I held the position of Executive Marketing Manager at Prentice Hall based out of Toronto.

In my role as Manager, Marketing and Communications at LGS (an IBM company), I developed and executed on strategic marketing plans to support and drive sales of 8 information technology practices.

I am originally from Montreal and earned my Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Concordia University.
