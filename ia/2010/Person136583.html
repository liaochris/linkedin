Mathieu Bastian’s Summary

Mathieu is the co-founder of the Gephi open-source project. He has a M.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Technology of Compiègne (UTC). Highly motivated technical leader with a passion for software development, visualization and open-source communities.

By developing and managing the Gephi project, Mathieu developed an expertise in graph visualization issues and in delivering user-oriented software. Proficient in Java, Swing and OpenGL he successfully created the Gephi platform, recognized for its performance, usability and extensible design.

Willingness to learn and face new challenges.

Mathieu Bastian’s Specialties:

data visualization, software design, graphics, HCI, network science
