Dennis Wells’s Summary

I am an experienced electrical engineer with a broad background ranging from assembler to principal electrical engineer including management focusing on the development and testing of RF, microwave, and millimeter wave subsystem assemblies. I have deep practical knowledge in all phases of product development with an emphasis on yield improvement, streamlining processes, and cost reduction within small start-up companies and large global corporations.

    Bandwidth Semiconductor

    (Public Company; Semiconductors industry)

  • Senior Electrical Engineer

    Tyco Electronics, M/A-COM

    (Public Company; TEL; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    June 1989 — March 2001 (11 years 10 months)

    Senior Test Engineer and Lab Manager of M/A-COM's Corporate R&D test lab.

    Performed DC and RF characterization on various active and passive circuits at the wafer, die, carrier-based die and packaged level utilizing a wide variety of S-parameter calibrations, coaxial and on-wafer, particularly at mmWave frequencies. Most characterization work was performed at mmWave frequencies including on-wafer measurements up to 90GHz.

    Co-authored “The Design of a DC-to-90GHz Resistive Load,” published in the IEEE Microwave and Guided Wave Letters, vol. 9 number 2.
