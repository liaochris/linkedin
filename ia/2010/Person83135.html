Howard Schwartz’s Experience

  • Partner

    Tatum, LLC

    2002 — Present (8 years )

    Currently serving as CFO for Brighton, Inc. Brighton is privately owned manufacturer and retailer of a line of accessories featuring handbags, belts, shoes, small leather goods, watches, jewelry, home accessories, eyewear and luggage. Brighton products are sold through a network of nearly 6,000 largely family-owned specialty stores nationwide, as well as more than 140 Brighton Collectibles stores from coast to coast.

    As a Tatum Partner, I served as Chief Financial Officer for Roger Dunn, Inc. With 42 stores in high demand markets and over $138 million in sales, Roger Dunn is one of the largest golf retail chains in the U.S.

Areas of success include accounting for proper revenue recognition; developed methodologies to account for elimination of inter-company profit and capitalization on interest under FAS 34.

    Also served as Senior Vice President & Chief Financial Officer for Bari Management, Inc., a $100+ million company that is one of the largest Burger King operators in the U. S. The company also owns the Original Roadhouse Grill, a chain of casual dining restaurants, a real estate development company and L&R Construction, Inc. Served as Chief Restructuring Officer the company’s Burger King operations and completed a restructuring and refinance of the company’s indebtedness which included the elimination of $20 million in long and short term debt and successfully emerged from bankruptcy.
