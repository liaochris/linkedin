Nigel Carr’s Summary

Nigel has nearly 30 years experience on both sides of the footwear business, both in the buying and the supplying of footwear to retail.
After a career in Buying and Merchandising, Nigel quit to form his own design and development company in 1996, followed by the launch of TMC Footwear Ltd, a design and distribution agency in 2000.

Nigel launched online shoes retailer Shoes.co.uk in 2006, to capitalise on the huge surge in footwear sales over the web. The unique domain name was secured in 1999 and the aim is make www.shoes.co.uk the UK's leading online only branded footwear store.

Shoes.co.uk is a profitable company experiencing explosive growth - the new Shoes.co.uk website launched at the end of June 2010 featuring over 2000 styles from 80 brands and added rotational photography in October 2010.
