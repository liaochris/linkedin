Melinda DiPerna’s Summary

No one sets out to be a "vacation rental manager", or to provide "interim and corporate housing", but most people who do these jobs exclusively do them because they love their work. That's the kind of work this is. We get to take care of people's favorite assets (their vacation homes) and help people have special vacations and make memories. In the case of our corporate and temporary guests, we get to make their times away from home more pleasant.

My professional goals: provide owners the income they need, protect their vacation homes, provide value to vacationers, make vacations special, and do it all while helping to provide for my family's future, and be there for their present! This is a 24/7/365 profession - but it also allows for fantastic flexibility for me to be there when my teens get out of school and to vacation with my own family. Being self employed and the owner of a company allows me to work with people I enjoy, and with people who enjoy their work. Life is too short for anything else!
