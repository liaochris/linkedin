Manjit Singh’s Summary

PMP Certified IT Manager with 17 years of professional experience in managing Enterprise Level Programs and Initiatives, delivering highest quality product on-time and on-budget with focus on developing and implementing best practices and processes. Extensive experience with PMI PMBOK and CMMI Level 3, developing standard processes, conducting Best Practice Reviews, business process analysis, business process change/reengineering and designing performance metrics.

Proven ability to manage complex high profile technology initiatives through the entire full project management lifecycle. Strong planning and organizational skills with experience as primary liaison with internal business stakeholders and sponsors, interfacing and managing external vendors, collaborating with internal teams to develop integrated plans linking various program areas and business objectives together in a cohesive, integrated execution plan.

Experience managing teams of up to 26 professional staff, in matrix and functional organizations, through exemplary leadership and empowerment leading to a highly collaborative and cohesive work environment. Strong technical background and fundamentals with hands on experience in software development, technical architecture, SDLC, ITIL, and various development methodologies like Agile, waterfall, Test-Driven Development (TDD), and iterative project life cycle. Skill set includes excellent organizational, interpersonal, verbal and writing communication, presentation and customer service experience.
