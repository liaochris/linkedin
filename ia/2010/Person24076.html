Bill Sloan’s Summary

Bill Sloan has been with Barnes-Jewish Hospital since 2006 providing direct sourcing and talent acquisition services to a variety of inpatient care areas, Goldfarb School of Nursing at Barnes-Jewish College and Barnes-Jewish Extended Care.

Mr. Sloan has sourced and recruited for both clinical and non-clinical positions including RN’s, Advanced Practice Nurses, Nursing Assistants, Rehabilitation Therapists, IT, Accounting, and Nursing Educators. In his current role as Talent Acquisition Specialist for College Recruitment, Mr. Sloan travels to local, regional and national Colleges of Nursing identifying top graduate nurse talent for Barnes-Jewish Hospital.

Bill’s primary area of interest involves the use of technology and social networking to increase passive and active candidate pools and adopting the use of Lean methodology to eliminate waste in HR processes.
