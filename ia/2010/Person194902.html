Stephen Roberts, MD’s Summary

Recent founder of Protanza Corporation - parent and research arm of DoctorStevesBanjo - a marketing-driven consumer health care company.

Banjo is a novel OTC medication that safely and rapidly eliminates the pain and inflammation associated with many chronic and acute ailments - including migraine, neuropathic pain, arthritis, neck pain, back pain, and other musculoskeletal conditions. Banjo will improve life for many millions. (www.doctorstevesbanjo.com)

Recent founder of c/it/2/believe/it (www.cit2believeit.com) - a direct-to-consumer marketing and product development company with an emphasis on infomercials. Our first project/product is on TV - a kettlebell fitness program - KettleWorx. (www.KettleWorx.com)

Previous founder, chairman and CEO of GelStat Corporation. A very early and somewhat primitive product (GelStat Migraine) placed on shelf at 24,000 stores nationwide - and there it stayed (no one purchased it.) Nuff said - live and learn - on we go.

Physician turned investment banker turned entrepreneur. Substantial experience in management, operations, corporate governance, new product development, clinical trials, consumer packaged goods, marketing, retail and finance. Inventor & creative thinker.

Substantial past involvement and continued interest in finance, especially as regards new ventures.

Open to connections (please, no spam): sroberts@protanza.com
