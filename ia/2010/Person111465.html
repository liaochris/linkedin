Kate Billing’s Summary

At Blacksmith, we’re all about transformation and chose our company name as we see a multitude of similarities between the role of the traditional blacksmith and the
work we do forging successful, sustainable businesses with engaged cultures and awesome employer brands.

My personal mission is do my bit to make the world a better [work]place. I believe there is massive untapped potential lying dormant in people and businesses all over New Zealand (and the world!) - potential that can make individuals more successful and fulfilled; businesses more successful and sustainable; and our communities richer and stronger.

My career’s been an ‘emergent’ one spanning management consulting; talent acquisition and management; employer branding; HR and performance coaching. I understand the imperative for achieving business results and know that the only real way to truly create this is through clear goals and expectations, self-aware individuals and aligned, engaged teams.

I have experience working with CEOs, business owners, senior leadership and the wider business, opening people’s hearts and minds to what powerful change can be affected in organisations and individual lives through awesome employment experiences, WOW customer moments and the creative magic of connection.

If you want different results you need to do things differently. I don't pull any punches but the challenge will always be honest, compassionate and respectful. It’s all about shaping conversations, changing mindsets and aligning behaviours with the attitude of what’s possible. My style is intelligent, collaborative, creative, passionate and, above all, fun.
