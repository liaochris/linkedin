Kenneth Lerman’s Summary

Ken has over forty years of experience as a manager, designer, developer, and architect of products ranging from compilers to graphics interfaces, from high availability networks to real-time embedded systems. He has been a consultant to Ericsson Information Systems, IBM, SNET, Nynex, MCI, and EMC, among others. Ken has the key patent for the control processor redundancy architecture for the optical networking system developed by Sirocco Systems. He also has patents pending for applications in veterinary anesthesia and electronic mail.

At Bunker-Ramo, Ken was the chief architect leading the design of a next generation five-nines availability network specified to support fifty thousand terminals in five thousand offices.

Ken has a fourth degree black belt in jujitsu and is an avid student and teacher of martial arts. In his spare time, he is a volunteer Emergency Medical Technician.

Kenneth Lerman’s Specialties:

Embedded and real-time systems, compilers/interpreters, graphics, communications, high availability networks.
