Kim Massey’s Summary

I am a dedicated professional committed to helping organizations and individuals realize and reach their full potential. With 13 years of professional experience, most recently as a corporate coach with Clear Channel University, a division of Clear Channel Radio, I have expertise in multi-level leadership development, training, executive coaching, and group facilitation. Leading over 30 sessions a year and providing extensive one on one coaching, I specialize in helping leaders create an engaging work environment and unlock potential through positive psychology.

Passionate about improving the world through leadership, my work places an emphasis on self-empowerment, inner development, and individual responsibility.

I hold my MBA with a specialization in Leadership from the University of Nebraska and a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Tennessee. Certifications include: The Gallup Organization as a certified Strengths Performance Coach and as a Human Capital Strategist from the Human Capital Institute. I have also received professional training from the Creative Education Foundation’s Creative Problem Solving Institute, Second City Communications, and the Human Performance Institute.

A life long learner, I volunteer my time and expertise both locally and internationally. I love to stretch myself (literally) through yoga, reading, engaging conversations and travel.

Kim Massey’s Specialties:

Positive Psychology, Strengths based Performance Coaching, Creative Problem Solving and Employee Development
