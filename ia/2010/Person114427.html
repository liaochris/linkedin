Ken Hayes’s Summary

Ken is a passionate mobile and technology enthusiast and is well versed in start up and growth stage environments. Ken has 17+ years working for mobile operators and with companies that supply apps and services to the mobile ecosystem. Ken has a proven track record of large business development "wins", successful product launches and leading teams in hyper growth environments.

Ken is responsible for all Sales and Business Development at PocketGear - the world's largest independent app martketpace.

Most recently, Ken was a Venture Partner at Acta Wireless where he advises companies in the mobile ecosystem. Prior to Acta, Ken was EVP at Thumbplay where he responsible for optimizing and expanding the company's business with mobile operators, OEMs and mobile OS providers.Before Thumbplay, Ken was SVP of Sales at Motricity where he had responsibility for all aspects of sales, business development and account management for Motricity’s industry leading product portfolio.

Before Motricity, Ken was Sr Director of Interactive Wireless Services division at InPhonic (NASDAQ:INPC), a leading provider of mobile services. InPhonic acquired GadgetSpace, a mobile application provider where Ken was part of the founding team.

Ken began his mobile career when he joined Dial Call, acquired by Nextel, now Sprint (NYSE:S). While at Nextel, Ken spent 6 years in marketing and channel development roles. Ken was Marketing Manager during the most successful market launch in Nextel history. In addition, Ken led the development and managed Nextel’s first retail channel – leading all channels across many key metrics (CPGA, net adds, churn, etc).

Ken has been recognized multiple times across organizations where he has worked; he was awarded Motricity’s Leader of the Year, Nextel’s highest award, President’s Council (1995 & 1996) and numerous awards for outstanding performance.

Ken is an avid runner, cyclist and triathlete. And, he rarely passes up ice cream when offered.
