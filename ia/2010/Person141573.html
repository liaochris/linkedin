Michael Gayle’s Summary

Michael Gayle has a passion for creating wellness through awareness. He doesn’t sit still and wait for life: he experiences it. Michael treats every waking moment as an opportunity to learn, contribute and take action. This passion has translated into more than 14 years of remarkable results for clients ages 8-88.   Michael gives his all to his clients, and they return the favor. Through his motivation and guidance, they experience life-changing shifts that expand far beyond the physical. In addition to his energy, his first rate credentials have propelled him to become one of the top personal trainers in Minnesota.
He believes that great physical fitness results from a deep emotional commitment to change. And through this commitment, people will fully realize their enormous potential. That is the overarching philosophy guiding how he runs his studio and inspires his clients and staff.   Michael's education, training and certifications include: former elite force U.S. Marine; BS Kinesiology and Minor in Nutrition, University of Minnesota; American College of Sports Medicine Personal Fitness Instructor; Stott Pilates CMR; Align Pilates Comprehensive Certification (Mat, Reformer, Cadillac, Chair Barrel) and personal trainer for clientele at The Marsh, Ritz Carlton and The Minnesota Vikings. 
He is extremely active in the community and has received several awards including:
1. Highland Business Association and Saint Paul Sunrise Rotary 2009 Business Person of the Year
2. Leadership Saint Paul Alumni
3. Featured speaker at the Minneapolis Women’s Expo
4. Networking groups: Toastmasters, Business Networkers International, Highland Business Association, St. Paul Area Chamber of Commerce and Midway Chamber of Commerce.
Future plans for MGayle Studio include expansion and creation fitness adventure “camps” for both kids and adults. Please contact Michael for speaking, networking, personal training, Pilates and corporate wellness opportunities.

Michael Gayle’s Specialties:

Pilates for Kids and helping those who are "stuck" in a particular area of life move forward with energy and enthusiasm.
