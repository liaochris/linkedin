David Rinker P.E.’s Summary

An accomplished, highly dedicated and analytical-minded professional with twenty-four years of experience in the application of electronic controls.

Coordinated and participated in the redesign, development and test of real time embedded microprocessor control modules.

Interests and skills include the support of customer based activities dealing with product definition, and in the creation or modification of product to meet customer requirements.
