Ben Wallace, MBA’s Summary

I am a Senior Business Consultant and Team Lead at First National Merchant Solutions who has had the privilege of working with amazing clients and colleagues over the last few years.

I believe that everyone wants to have someone they can trust to help them with their challenges. I enjoy being a trusted advisor in the many facets of my career and personal life.

I believe that no one can achieve real success on their own. The most successful people in the world have a group of advisors that they can trust to give them real perspective including advice that may differ from their own. The real value in having advisors is to help indicate whether your path is truly on track or whether you are off base and need a course correction. I try to be an honest and trustworthy advisor to my clients and my colleagues so that we all can work towards being closer to our professional and personal goals.

My life experience has taught me that I will always need more patience and that you can never be too inspired as long as you are remember the importance of human limits at the same time.

My goals are to be a successful husband and son, and to keep in mind the things that are truly important in life. Treat others with the respect and dignity that they deserve, even if sometimes you feel like they haven't earned it. To smile more, worry less, have fun, and continue to be a responsible adult at the same time.
