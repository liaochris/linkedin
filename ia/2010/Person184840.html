Scott Severson’s Summary

Scott Severson is the president of ARAnet, the leader in using brand storytelling to build brands, educate consumers and drive sales. Scott is a passionate advocate for online marketing tactics, measurement and optimization and has employed this expertise to a wide range of businesses.

Founded in 1996, Scott joined ARAnet in 2006 with the goal of rapid, sustained growth with a focus on developing the Adfusion product from an early stage product to a full national roll out.

Today, Adfusion is a game-changing online advertising platform, delivering billions of impressions monthly on the worlds most visited news sites. It employs technology-enabled custom content to drive educated and motivated traffic to a client’s Web site on a CPC basis. As a result, Adfusion generates some of the highest quality traffic on the Web and is growing at an exponential rate.

Adfusion was recently named to the Upshot Institute’s 2009 list of 50 most innovative, powerful and useful online marketing tools available today.

Adfusion grew out of ARAnet’s industry-leading ARAcontent service. ARAcontent generates brand-story placements for their clients in daily and weekly newspapers as well as SEO-optimized online placements on hundreds of media web sites. ARAcontent helps marketers and PR professionals reach and win new customers in a way that is relevant, measurable and effective.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 2006 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    At ARAnet we believe that the best advertising doesn’t look like advertising. We help our clients reach and win new customers in a way that is relevant, measurable and effective. We currently offer two great products to help our clients engage consumers: ARAcontent and Adfusion.

    ARAcontent is our print and online feature article placement solution. We specialize in writing and editing feature articles that highlight our clients’ products and services. We then partner with top print and online publishers to guarantee the articles are published in a wide range of newspapers and Web sites.

    Adfusion is our article-based cost-per-click advertising platform. With Adfusion we write engaging consumer and business to business articles that contain important information about our client’s product or service. Within each article is a direct link to our client’s landing page. Adfusion has an unmatched ability to drive qualified, educated and motivated traffic to our client’s landing pages.
