Colin Stewart’s Summary

I have spent my career at Morgan Stanley advising either companies from emerging economies and/or companies with emerging technologies on raising capital in the public and private markets. I have spent the last 9 years working in the technology space helping companies from the internet/new media, software, cleantech, semiconductor, communications equipment, hardware and it services sectors fund their expansion and growth. The US, China, India, Taiwan, HK, and UK/Europe have been among the most active geographies for me.

Colin Stewart’s Specialties:

All Aspects of Raising Capital - IPOs, Follow-ons, Convertible Bonds, Private Placements, Straight Debt; Strong Understanding of the Capital Markets - Investors, Valuations, etc.

Colin Stewart’s Experience
