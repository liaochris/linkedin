Tom Konitzer’s Summary

Tom is a Recruiting, Sales Management, Business Development and Operations Professional with more than 14 years of experience in the Healthcare Industry. For much of his career he developed and managed the provision of experienced personnel for the Home Healthcare, Hospice and Wellness industries. His clients have included Providers and Managed Care Organizations in the private healthcare sector as well as those in the Federal Government. Tom’s background in Executive Recruiting is deep and diverse, bringing immediate value to his clients.

Tom had a successful 10-year experience with a national homecare and medical staffing company during which he held several management positions at the Branch, Regional and Senior Executive levels. While Regional Manager for the State of Texas, he had oversight of nine (9) home healthcare, medical staffing and wellness branch offices with fifteen (15) direct reports and $50 million revenue responsibility. Tom was chosen from a field of 375 managers for the prestigious “Leadership Award” based on his demonstrated Leadership in guiding the direction of the company, his Character in setting an example to follow, and his Dedication to the success of his team and the company.

Tom brings energy, passion, and integrity to STAT Search. He is extremely driven and will ‘Go the Extra Mile’ to care for his clientele. He realizes that his success is determined by the appreciation of his Clients and Candidates for the professional service he provides.
