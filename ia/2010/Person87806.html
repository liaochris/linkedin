James B. McKay’s Summary

James has over 35 years of experience in the investment industry including working as an investment advisor, a stockbroker, and in the private equity investment arena. Starting at age 15, he worked for his father's investment advisory service analyzing small growth companies, primarily focusing on NASDAQ companies, for investment for family and client accounts. After his father's retirement and the closing of McKay Management & Research, Inc. he then spent a brief stint with Dean Witter as a stockbroker and then managed his own portfolio for several years. For the last 20 years, Mr. McKay has invested in, co-founded and provided research and business development for a number of start-up companies. Most recently he was Co-Founder and V.P. of Business Development & Research for EventAmerica, Inc. a luxury VIP sports tour and event portal start-up offering event management and retail software applications.

Since the end of EventAmerica in 2004 he has founded his own start-up, Wall Street Scores, which offers an investment decision support system; developed around intelligent analytical tools, for individuals and institutional investors via a web-based subscription service including smartphone applications for mobile users.

James B. McKay’s Specialties:

Providing business plan research and business model development consulting for early stage companies seeking capital. Stock analysis and portfolio management.

    Wall Street Scores, Inc., (WSS), plans to market web-based subscription services offering investment analytical tools to individuals, stockbrokers and professional investment services. The Company’s initial product is a unique stock analysis tool, called a ScoreSheet™, providing subscribers an opportunity to download and review thousands of SEC quarterly (10-Q) and annual (10-K) reports in a format that will provide investors, who do their own research and analysis, significant time savings and improvement in identifying and analyzing corporate financials thereby improving the return on their stock investments.

The Company’s solutions included a Web-based application for the marketing and driving of event information, merchandise and promotions to event attendees.
