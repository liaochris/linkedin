Rick Ruffolo’s Summary

Rick is a C-Level Executive Business Leader with 20 years of proven results driving profitable growth at market-leading specialty retail and consumer goods companies. He has the unique combination of both classic packaged goods marketing experience from Procter & Gamble, Ralston Purina, and SC Johnson and specialty retail leadership at Bath & Body Works, Yankee Candle, and Crabtree & Evelyn.

He is well-regarded as an outstanding innovator, creative problem-solver, and general manager who blends strategic thinking with great merchant instincts and the business savvy of a successful entrepreneur. He holds several patents for his innovation work, and has developed and launched three separate brands/businesses in the last decade each of which has generated over $1 Billion in revenues (and counting).

He also is founder of a leading executive consultancy to the consumer goods and specialty retail market called "R4 Innovations".
