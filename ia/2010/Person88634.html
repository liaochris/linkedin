James Wilkinson’s Summary

Running the day to day operations of a small non-profit requires a suite of skills, experience, ingenuity and creativity.

My responsibilities run the gamut from publicity, communications and marketing, to Board and member relations, web-guru, accountant, intern coordinator, vendor selector, fund raiser, and trade education resource.

Every day presents new challenges, and each year I've been at WITA we've grown in total number of members, number of sponsors, and in revenues (gross and net.)

Frankly, it's a team effort from an engaged & connected Board of Directors, a supportive family, a great group of professional members and sponsors, and quality interns. I feel privileged to be the quarterback on this winning team!
