James Girling’s Summary

Headhunter and recruitment consultant at GW Risk Ltd. (highly profitable and first subsidiary of GraingerWest Ltd.) specialising in Information Security.

I recruit for Big 4 professional practices, IT solutions and information security consultancies, banks, financial institutions, asset management, hedge funds, commercial companies, charities, regulatory bodies, and government organisations.

Although I am based in London, I work globally and my clients and candidates are spread across the UK, Europe, Asia (Japan, Singapore, India, etc.), South America, Canada and the USA.

For clients:

I provide search and selection services either on a contingency or retained basis depending on your specific requirements at the time. Our fees are negotiable depending on the commitment on offer (such as exclusivity, etc.) in order to come to an agreement that is mutually beneficial.

For candidates:

I am always happy to speak to skilled candidates and offer help and advice on matters such as CV / resume formatting, market knowledge/information, etc. Please do not hesitate to contact me or send me your CV / resume if you are considering progressing your career elsewhere.

I also rely heavily on recommendations and referrals from people in my network. As a small token of appreciation for this, a £200 cash reward is on offer for any successful referrals, so if you have any friends or colleagues that you would personally recommend please get in touch. I'm always happy to have a no-strings-attached chat.
