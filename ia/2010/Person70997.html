Fran (Holm) Hogan’s Summary

Holm Personnel Consultants - A Career Search & Job Placement firm I started in1975.

I work with manufacturers, distributors and whoesalers in the broad ranging building products industry to find the best Sales Management, Sales Professionals, Operations, Executive and Administration Management talent.

My client companies can expect the benefit of a comprehensive database developed over the past three decades. An effective, confidential resource to find key people and a long-established commitment to the highest ethical standards and a solid reputation for integrity, professionalism and effectiveness

Job Seekers can expect a confidential resource to obtain career goals.
* All inquiries, search activity and communications are handled on a strictly confidential basis.
In addition, I am happy to offer up-to-date industry news, salary guidance and relocation advice.
