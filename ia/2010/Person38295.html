Chris Nicoli’s Summary

After spending most of my life observing and assisting my father in the telecommunications industry I took over Teltek in 2006 with plans of growth while maintaining our core values. A lifetime of coaching, a passion for sales/buying psychology, along with continuous innovation has enabled me to build a solid foundation with stable brand for which I plan to continue to deliver quality products with a memorable experience for years to come.
