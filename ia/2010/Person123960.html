Leonard Chin’s Summary

At EC-Council, Leonard's role as the Director of Marketing makes him responsible for all aspects of marketing for the group. He is responsible for designing and managing strategic marketing plans and initiatives, and is the key driver of all global marketing efforts.

Prior to this appointment, he was the Director of Business Development, which requires him to develop and manage business partnerships across the globe and growing these businesses in line with EC-Council's vision. In addition to identifying global business opportunities, he is charged with leading the business development across various business units within EC-Council.

As the Conference Director for Hacker Halted 2008 & 2009, Leonard was responsible for the overall production of the Hacker Halted USA Conference series, a world-class information security conference that features internationally renowned security experts. HE was instrumental in the conceptualization and organization of EC-Council's first Hacker Halted Conference in the United States. In line with EC-Council's mission to evangelize information security awareness and skills globally, Leonard leads a conference team that brings Hacker Halted to a growing list of global markets annually.

Leonard possesses extensive field experience specializing in sales and marketing functions across industries including finance, conference, franchise, and as well as in training and education. With this experience, Leonard has successfully concluded key deals with numerous Fortune 500 companies.
