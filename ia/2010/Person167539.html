Richard Perez’s Summary

I am an ex Minor League baseball player predominatly with the Houston Astros, San Francisco Giants Chains and 4 others.... I was fortunate enough to work for Georgia Frontiere's Los Angeles Rams as Assistant to the Players and Coaches led by Chuck Knox. 2004 Voice of the Harlem Globetrotters. I have done Play by Play for over 600 Boxing matches from Friday night Fights fame. I would like to work with my craft at a Radio station and or a TV station doing play by play of sports and or entertainment shows as I have a rolodex of quality people that I can utilize my main guys are Joey Browner, Ricky Watters, Mark Clayton, Rick Upchurch and Darryl Talley. I have been fortunate enough to have worked professionally in NCAA, all 5 Major sports of the NBA, MLB, NFL, NHL. I also have a 20 year past with Harrahs/Hilton Corps and the last year with MGM/Mirage at Aria City Center as Sales Consultant.
16 years of on air broadcast experience, 10 of which have been in the Las Vegas/Honolulu Market. I currently host The Sports Legends here in Las Vegas aired in 15 states on 690am KHNR Hawaii, 1380am KTKZ Sacramento, CA and KBAD FOX Sports Las Vegas at various times and dates.. My past includes Pre game, Play by Play and Post game shows for the University of Hawaii (ESPN 1420 KKEA), Iowa State University (WB NETWORK), University of Notre Dame. (Radio/TV Pregame Shows MCCA)experienced in both Radio & Television consisting of hosting Talk Shows to Play by Play as well as management. I've covered everything from Super Bowls, NBA Finals, Pro Bowls, World Series delivering the Play by Play on many College Football games from Iowa State to Hawaii and much more!!
I have done other TV & Radio.(University of Notre Dame Campus & Area MCTV) I also have covered the Colorado Rockies, Denver Nuggets and Denver Broncos for DCTV in Denver from 1998-2000. I am also building my sports company (Nationwide Sports Group LLC) My USMC son & namesake LCpl Richard A. Perez Jr. passed away in Iraq 2/2005.
