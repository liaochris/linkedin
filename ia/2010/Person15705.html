April (Cole) Damron’s Summary

I am a dynamic and analytical marketing professional who enjoys working with people. My strengths include project management, attention to detail, communication and problem solving. With 10 years of wine industry experience, I founded Damron Marketing to focus on helping wineries improve their brand image, customer relations and strategic planning. I enjoy creating marketing materials such as print collateral and websites, while providing the ultimate consumer experience at every point of contact.
