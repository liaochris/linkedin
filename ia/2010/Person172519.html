Richard Roocroft’s Summary

I have been flourishing with an impressive digital / online publishing company for almost 5 years now, working on the sales and account management side of things which has been a very satisfying journey SO FAR :-)

Always here to give a helping hand with a majority of the new starters, my input for development ideas, polishing off existing methods of doing business and valuable feedback is always appreciated by the team - which is key to maintain our position in the market as leaders whilst continuing to grow!

The beauty of working for an online business has meant that I have been fortunate enough to deal with all types of outfits worldwide, having a portfolio of 800+ clients in 20+ countries which has generated over £2,000,000 since I started in September '05

My passion is helping other businesses grow through the use of online publishing - why don't you get in touch and let me explain how, depending on the project you have in mind.

The main goal in my life would be to start a business fired by my own creativity, but with the option on the horizon of opening another office for YUDU and managing a team of my own, it's just too tempting to pass - so I'll continue working hard to get to where I need to be!
