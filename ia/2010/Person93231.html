Jeff Guarnera, PMP’s Summary

A PMI PMP certified Project Manager with experience in information technology, banking / insurance / financial services, health-care & employee benefits. Jeff holds a BA in Communications from Seton Hall University. Jeff provided consulting, administration, implementation & conversion services for employee benefit qualified & non-qualified defined contribution & defined benefit plans. Jeff managed a technology support group for end user support, contract / vendor application & data management, ad-hoc report programming & testing. Jeff served as a Business Analyst writing functional & technical requirements & specifications, testing, training, policy, process & procedure evaluations & documentation. Jeff has been a Project Manager for 11 years, managing business, process & technology (web, mainframe & distributed) projects & programs both across the USA & for 10 other countries. He has also defined & implemented a project management methodology that achieved SEI’s CMM for Software maturity level-3 certification. Additionally, he authored the interpretation of the SEI CMM Software Subcontract Management Key Process Area, incorporating vendor selection & off-shore outsourcing components. Jeff trains, coaches & mentors on portfolio / program / project / risk / vendor / requirements management & process improvement principles & practices. Jeff has established Project Offices, Project Management Offices & Software Engineering Process Groups. Finally, Jeff served on the PMI Pittsburgh Chapter’s Board of Directors as Vice President of Communications & Publicity and VP of Programs, also as their Email Communications Coordinator.
