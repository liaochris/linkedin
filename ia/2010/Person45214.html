Lauren Rafanelli’s Summary

Cupcakes Catering is a custom order cupcake company on Long Island, baking fresh, handcrafted cupcakes and delivering them right to you! Creating custom cupcakes that add creativity and style to any event is our speciality. We design for weddings, baby showers, bridal showers, corporate events, graduations, birthday parties, fundraisers, communions, bar & bat mitzvahs, holidays, or any other occasion when you want to make people smile!

Visit us on TheKnot.com, WeddingChannel.com, PartyPop.com, Newsday - Catering For The Holidays November 1, 2009 Issue, CupcakesTakeTheCake.com, PartySpot.com, LIFamilies.com, Facebook, Twitter, Flickr.com, GettingMarriedOnLongIsland.com and WeddingWire.com!
