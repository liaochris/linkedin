Robert Henninger’s Experience

  • Director of Information Technology

    Merrick Towle Communications

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2004 — Present (7 years )

    Our agency has grown to be the largest independent advertising agency in the Washington D.C. area with over 80 employees. During this period of growth I sought out opportunities to expand my knowledge and apply my talents. In 2006 our agency moved to our new 20,000 sq. ft. facility. I viewed this as a opportunity to apply my talents to this mission by; working with the architect to ensure the floor plan fit our workflow, the design and build of our datacenter, ensuring our current and future electrical requirements were addressed, acquiring voice and data connectivity, securing the cabling contractor, layout and purchasing of our systems furniture and coordinating all needs between the general contractor and our agency enabling us to stay on schedule. Our new office was a success boosting productivity and increasing sales. It was also featured in a local architectural magazine “Washington Spaces” as a bold and bright space that communicated the growth of our agency.
