Joe Luna’s Summary

Mr. Luna is a Founder and Managing Partner of Logic Security, Inc., an Information Security consulting firm located in Irvine, California. Mr. Luna has over 10 years of Information Security experience in various technical, operational and leadership roles where he has an established track record of managing complex, enterprise-wide initiatives that satisfy regulatory and business requirements while keeping security posture at an acceptable level.

By utilizing past experiences that cover areas such as Vulnerability Assessments, Penetration Testing, Policy and Procedures, Risk Management, Audit and Compliance, Incident Response and Forensics Services, Mr. Luna is able to offer a deep understanding and clear vision of an Information Security program to an organization.

Prior to Logic Security, Mr. Luna served as Director of Information Security for one of the nation's largest Internet Service Providers where he was responsible for maintaining the confidentiality, integrity and availability of more than 50 million members in an organization where annual revenues exceeded 500 million dollars.

Mr. Luna is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Security Manager (CISM) and Certified in the Governance of Enterprise IT (CGEIT).

In 2003, Mr. Luna received an award from the FBI recognizing his efforts in playing an integral role in apprehending a highly sought after group of individuals.
