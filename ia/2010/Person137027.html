Matthew Grimm’s Summary

My professional experience began as a recording and post production engineer for various studios in Chicago including Studiomedia Recording, Post Effects and Airstream Audio. Shortly thereafter I began a career as a Sound Designer for the gaming industry. I worked for numerous companies such as Stern Pinball, Playmechanix, IT, EA and Midway. Some titles included Fightnight Round 3 (EA) Mortal Kombat Shaolin Monks (Midway), Blitz the League (Midway), Lord of the Rings Pinball (Stern) and Silverstrike Bowling (IT).

After a few years freelancing I started a full time position at Midway games working as a Senior Sound Designer on several titles including John Woo's Stranglehold and Mortal Kombat vs.DC Universe, as well as an Audio Lead on an unannounced title.

Most recently I've started as a Senior Sound Designer for Netherrealm Studios (WB Chicago.)

Matthew Grimm’s Specialties:

I'm an experienced sound designer and foley artist. I'm a Pro Tools instructor. I have experience in middleware programs such as Unreal and FMOD and Wwise.
