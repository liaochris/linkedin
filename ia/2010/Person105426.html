Joseph Limone’s Summary

Unique background encompassing both legal and business expertise in commodities having managed legal, front office, origination, credit, and operational groups. Superior commodity structure analysis, business strategy, and financing knowledge. A skilled communicator; able to synthesize and deliver complex information to diverse audiences.
