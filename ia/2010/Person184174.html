Scott D. Johnson, MBA’s Summary

A trilingual international finance and accounting professional fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese with experience ranging from a small start-up to multi-billion dollar corporations across a variety of industries. Strengthens overseas finances and operations, improves internal processes and controls, eliminates inefficiencies, and facilitates the flow of information. Successfully accomplishes objectives by building productive, working relationships with management, peers, and subordinates in the U.S. and overseas through effectively bridging the language and cultural barriers between the groups.

• Refined financial reporting tools and procedures of 7 countries to shorten the monthly reporting process from more than 4 weeks to less than 5 days while maintaining and/or improving accuracy

• Reduced accounting costs in one country more than $20,000 per month and headcount 80% by outsourcing the functions to a third-party service provider

• Led a team of internal personnel and external consultants in re-engineering the accounting software of an overseas operation, resulting in 40% lower labor costs and simultaneously aligning it with the corporate system's structure

• Shortened the annual budget process from roughly 6 weeks to 2 by migrating international offices to uniform budget templates and assisting office leaders and administrative heads with the completion and submission of budgets

• Transferred nearly $100,000 to the U.S. from a Venezuelan subsidiary despite the lack of a currency market for Venezuelan Bolivars
