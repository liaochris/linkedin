Valerie Hurst’s Summary

GALLO focuses on face-to-face event and exhibit marketing, and I am in sales, support a portable program, and work on marketing projects. I work with clients to create a comprehensive marketing strategy to communicate their message through three-dimensional marketing; including events, trade show exhibits and corporate environments. I love the tradeshow industry because it offers variety, a fast pace, and long-term customer relationships.

I am one thesis away from being a Certified Trade show Marketer (CTSM) through San Francisco University, and one of my goals is to complete this tradeshow marketing certification. I believe strongly in networking and helping others succeed. Success is built through these strong relationships, and this method of networking has proved to work well for myself and others.

Valerie Hurst’s Specialties:

Being a marketing partner and an advocate to each of my clients, and working smart to understand their trade show and marketing needs, is my specialty. I believe in strong relationships with each of my clients. Whether the need is a tradeshow portable exhibit, lobby signage, corporate event, or custom exhibit system, I look forward to working with each client to help them achieve their specific objective.
