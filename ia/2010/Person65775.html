Eric Dose’s Summary

Leader of corporate technical laboratories, most recently for advanced flavor, beverage, food, and related products. Extensive experience in analytical chemistry and related measurement sciences, including color technology and product stability. Expert in: coordinating projects spanning R&D and QA; facilitating New Product Development and Introduction; high-resolution chromatography (gas and liquid); chromatographic isolation of trace components; and anticipating future measurement needs.

Consistently identifies and hires first-rate corporate scientists at technician through Ph.D. levels, and integrates their talents into exemplary teams. Explains technical results and strategic meaning to other company leaders, as plainly and concisely as possible.

Eric Dose’s Specialties:

Set strategy for laboratories in analytical chemistry and related measurements. Assemble tasks and projects into coherent and realistic portfolios. Design and apply effective metrics of product performance.

Identify and hire superior technical talent. Ensure that they (1) get and stay on the right projects, (2) have what they need to progress rapidly, and (3) work extremely well together.
