Tim Day’s Summary

Tim’s 22 years in operational / international marketing has given him a broad and extensive understanding of the pharmaceutical industry. While at Bayer Corporation he served as Product Manager for Cipro®, where he was involved in launching several new indications and formulations and growing the product into a billion-dollar brand. His involvement with Advisory Boards at Bayer laid the foundation for his subsequent position as Vice President at a medical communications agency where he oversaw the development and operation of the GOLD, GINA and ARIA initiatives. This role provided a forum for Tim to develop his skills in identifying key-opinion leaders and fostering professional alliances between health care professionals and industry. Tim’s work with the initiatives included close collaboration with the WHO, NHLBI and the sponsoring pharmaceutical companies.

While at PAREXEL Medical Marketing Services, Tim served as Director of Creative Services for the US market for 5 years giving him insight into the agency side of the pharmaceutical industry. This extensive background in operational marketing provides clients with creative and innovative approaches to medical marketing including international marketing experience in developing branding, themed meetings and events, designing and operating exhibits, and conveying marketing concepts through various multimedia.

Most recently Tim has been working closely with the American Society for Microbiology (ASM) and is responsible for the development and execution of their HIV – Drug Resistance Initiative as well as several ASM CME-accredited satellite symposia presented at their 47th ICAAC. The HIV – Drug Resistance Initiative has been such a success that Tim is currently spearheading the design and implementation of the ASM’s Methicillin Resistant Staphylococcus Aureus Initiative (MRSA-I).
