Scott Goginsky, CFA’s Summary

Investment Professional with 15+ years experience as an Equity Analyst/PM. CFA charterholder. Value/GARP investor. Responsible for equity analysis, stock selection, portfolio construction, and client relationships. Extensive industry & sell-side contacts. Both buy- and sell-side experience, including mutual funds and long/short hedge funds. Formal credit training and risk management experience on private placement and high-yield desks. Private equity and venture capital experience. Have performed capital raises in excess of $30mm. Well-versed in economic/top-down topics and principles, as well as geopolitical issues. Great stock-picking track record.
