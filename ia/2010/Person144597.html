Michiel Van Kets’s Summary

I started my first website in 2002 to suport my design collection. Slowly I got more and more active with it, especially SEO and that's how I eventually became a professional SEO'r.

Since 2006 my company Luminous offers all kind of SEO-packages.

My goal with my company is to be able to deliver a whole set of services to other SEO-companies.

point being; we're in Thailand, so I can offer services at a low fee, but then again, it's incredible hard to find highly trained professionals. So most service we offer are the easier ones, like directory submissions.

Another element is that in the total of a well balanced SEO-campaign there's a lot of easy, yet time-consuming work, which we do, but still a lot of spefic, specilizad work just as well, so that's what the SEO-companies do themselves.

This synergy has work well for both of us; as well for my company as the SEO-companies who order from us.
