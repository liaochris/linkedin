Kim Kiefer, MSC’s Summary

I am currently the Director of Marketing for CCC Technologies, Inc. CCC Technologies helps businesses nationwide to design, implement, and manage
everything with a blinking light.

CCC on the web: www.ccctechnologies.com
CCC on Facebook: www.facebook.com/ccctechnologies
CCC on Twitter: @CCCTechnologies
Kim on Twitter: @KimKiefer

My focus and work experience have been exclusive to business telephony since 2000. All of my marketing experience is strictly B2B with a focus on lead generation, campaign development/deployment/management, collateral design/development, trade show/live events, advertising (print/web/radio), web development/design/deployment, graphic design, social media, and public relations.

My experience as web designer and developer has been exclusive to the Macromedia/Adobe platform (including Cold Fusion). I do web design/development and graphic design for friends and family in my spare time including website design and management and promotional graphic design for local bands and DJ's.

I most recently attained my MSC from the Business Marketing Institute (Nov. 2008). In addition to my marketing role I am also a project manager for our ConvergeSecure solution portfolio (Unified Threat Management/Backup & Recovery).
