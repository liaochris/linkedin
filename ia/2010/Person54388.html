Denise Angarola Fernandez’s Summary

An award winning, tenured senior marketing executive with experience traversing industries including Fashion, Retail, Wireless, Interactive and Travel.

My current role as EVP, Digital Strategy & Client Services for Luxus Inc keeps me busy with US expansion goals and exciting projects. I'd love to share the Luxus story with you!

Previous to Luxus, I headed up Marketing for Sabre Travel Studios where I launched two products for this industry leader; including TripCase, the travel app featured in the Fall 2009 iPhone television campaign.

Previous to joining Sabre, I was the founder and President of Julden Group, a fully integrated marketing firm delivering on it’s philosophy of ”Live, Work, Play Marketing”. I am also the founder of the networking and talent development community, Network Ties, and speak often on the power of personal brand, personal publicity and networking. Seminars are available for individuals and corporations.

I sit on the Board for the Girl Scouts of Northeast Texas. While the cookies are great, you should also know that this organization provides so many wonderful services to girls K-12. Girl Scouts mission: Girl Scouting builds girls of courage, confidence and character who make the world a better place.
