Chris Skinner’s Summary

I am a freelance snowboard and surf coach based in London, UK.

I have over 10 years of Sports Coaching experience from the very basic of levels to Elite Squad training. Working in locations throughout the world including, North America, Europe, Maldives and the southern hemisphere.

Recently thought I have started up a Blog about my travels and work.

The world wide web and blogging, had always been a big interest of mine so back in September 2009 I started up a small Blog.

I am now in the process of developing the site to share my knowledge and experience gained from over 10 years of travelling and working within the Tourism/Sports Industry.

The plan is to include small tutorials and how-to's in both snowboarding and surfing as well as articles relating to both the summer and winter industries.
