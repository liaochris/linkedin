Felipe Abala’s Summary

BACKGROUND SUMMARY:
A Chartered IT Professional (UK), certified in Project Management with 13+ years of utilizing information technology as an enabling tool in re-engineering business processes.

Has created (at Cricket Communications) a system that stores real-time field report from cell site construction managers, utility coordinators, vendors and "Tiger Teams" and notifies stakeholders (i.e. project managers, lead construction managers, utility managers and etc.) via email on the task status of the site as it happens on the field. This system interacts with the company-wide system (Siterra) for task forecast dates and completion dates allowing a real-time re-scheduling of tasks based on the recent field status report. This system is also capable of interfacing with other systems i.e. Oracle E-Business Suite, and data on Excel spreadsheets, for information consolidation and management reporting. The system's front-end is Excel Workbook (with VBA) with it's database back-end in MS Access Database.

Has hands-on experience on PC/OS/Packages/Devices Installation & Technical Support, LAN Administration (Network), Systems Development (Business Analysis, Systems Analysis & Design, and Database Development & Design), Web & Internet Messaging System Administration & Development, and IT Project Management.
