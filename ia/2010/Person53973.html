Deepak K Karthikeyan’s Summary

Graduated from University of South Carolina with Chemical Engineering major, currently working as a Research Scientist at Envia Systems in Bay Area, CA.

Main objective is to design a more stable, cost effective and high performance novel materials for Li ion battery electrodes for their application in Plugin Hybrids and Electric vehicles. Long term Goal is to be a Entrepreneur myself, to start a Li ion manufacturing facility in India.

Received my bachelors degree from Central Electrochemical Research Institute (CECRI) in India, which gave me a strong foundation and understanding in Electrochemistry. Apart from Batteries, some of my previous projects also includes Electrodeposition, Corrosion and Electrometullargy.

Deepak K Karthikeyan’s Specialties:

- Material Synthesis and Characterization, Process scale-up
- Parameter Estimation, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Finite element technique
- Cell fabrication, Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy
