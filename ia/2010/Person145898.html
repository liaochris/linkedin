Mike Reger’s Summary

Mike Reger has over 12 years of experience in both the digital and traditional realms, designing award winning work for such global brands as Nike, Pepsi, Discovery Channel, Kellogg's, Nissan and Audi among others. From his first design job helping rebrand MCI's consumer offerings across multiple channels (from identity to packaging to print to broadcast to web) to his most recent engagements with Pepsi's "Dear Mr. President" campaign (which included branding, video production, video-banners, a website, a branded youtube channel, mobile and even a coffee table book), Mike has always been a strong multidisciplinary thinker and designer, with a focus on emerging media and a solid foundation in traditional channels of engagement.

In Mike's 4+ years working with Nike, he has engaged with many brand teams on multiple global initiatives. With a particular interest in the lifestyle category, Mike as an art director and designer, helped develop and launch the global Nike Sportswear brand, participated in artist collaborations and content creation for Nike Dunk, helped evolve the Nike iD service, and the "Nike OS" platform across many sites, helped design the Nike+ offering, engaged in other performance brands such as Nike Soccer, Nike Running, and Nike Basketball on work that utilized his many talents including concepting, art direction, design, experience on video shoots and in post-production, Flash animation, After-Effects, photography, and even sound design. Many of his projects have won awards at AIGA, Art Director's Club, Cannes, Clios, CommArts, the One Show and more.

In his free time, Mike is a professional music producer and DJ. He has produced commercial soundtracks on a freelance basis for many global brands. He has also released original music on many labels worldwide including Eighteenth Street Lounge (ESL), Stereo Deluxe, Kickin UK, Select Cuts/Blood+Fire, Echo Beach and a few of his own imprints.
