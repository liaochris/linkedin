Claudia Putnam’s Summary

Fearless, creative, 360-degree marketing professional. Strategic? Tactical? Yes and yes. In the end, the success of any business is about the way you tell your story, from the banner on your Website to the detailed analysis in your white papers or the way you manage a crisis in the press.
And--here's the biggie these days--how do the web and social media fit into your plans? If you're out there using these new approaches, how do you think about your objectives and your results?

I love talking about these things. Feel free to start a discussion about the role of conversation and story in marketing. What's a strategy? What's a tactic? Very often, people confuse the two, or fail to see how they might best be integrated. Twenty years of experience at your service!
