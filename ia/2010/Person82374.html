Martin Calle’s Summary

I run Calle & Company, the most successful product development company in the consumer products industry. More corporations own and manage brands invented or reinvented by my firm than any other single entitity in the consumer packaged goods business. As Chief Culture Officer I move consumer culture and creativity into and back out of the corporation. Our least successful new product, Baked Lays Potato Chips sold $310 million in the first ten months. Our most successful new product, Pampers Phases Developmental Diapers sold $1.2 billion in the first year and $11 billion over 10 years. I mention these numbers because between them you have the low and the high of the most successful new products anyone has launched in the CPG industry over the last 57 years. I publish MADISON AVENUE, The Journal of Social Influence Marketing, Advertising & Branding. MADISON AVENUE is a member of the Forbes Business & Finance Network and is an Advertising Age Magazine Power 150 Marketing Blog.

Martin Calle’s Specialties:

Our turnaround on Folgers turned a $300 million mature earnings business into the category's top selling $1.6 billion brand - the CPG industry turnaround record.
