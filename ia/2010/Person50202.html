David Black’s Experience

  • Publisher

    inBiz Connection Magazine

    (Publishing industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    The first regional business-to-business magazine in Essex County, inBiz serves more than 18,000 businesses. The resource-packed quarterly helps small- and mid-sized businesses connect with local Chambers of Commerce—and with other businesses in the county. With features, news briefs, experts’ tips and more, inBiz focuses on innovative ideas and solutions to help small and mid-sized businesses grow, and to increase the vitality of Essex County’s business community.

  • Publisher

    Vicinity Magazine

    (Publishing industry)

    2005 — Present (5 years )

    Vicinity Magazine is a vibrant and informative lifestyle magazine that offers an in-depth look at local shopping, entertainment, human interest, events and more. The Calendar of Events is the most comprehensive guide in North Jersey. Mailed directly to more than 46,000 single-family homes in upscale areas of Essex, Somerset, Union and Morris counties, the sophisticated publication serves as an invaluable resource for its educated, affluent audience.

  • Publisher

    Suburban Essex Magazine

    (Publishing industry)

    2005 — Present (5 years )

    Suburban Essex is an exciting and informative lifestyle magazine that covers local shopping, dining, arts, events, human-interest, news and more. It also features the most comprehensive Calendar of Events in North Jersey. With compelling photography, strong editorial and innovative design, the magazine is ideally suited to its affluent, educated audience: it is mailed directly to 50,000 single-family homes in 10 upscale Essex County towns. Suburban Essex reaches more people in the region than any other publication, and has become a popular must-read for the entire community.

  • Founder and CEO

    Vicinity Media Group

    (Publishing industry)

    1992 — Present (18 years )

    Vicinity Media Group was started in 1992 as "The Towne Crier." We have since grown to include Suburban Essex Magazine, inBiz Connection Magazine, Vicinity Magazine and the inBiz Connection B2B network. We also own and operate multiple web properties and produce numerous events, including "The Best of Essex inBiz Expo."
