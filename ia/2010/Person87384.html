Jaime Ubilla’s Summary

UB & CO is an international law firm with presence in Greater China and Latin America that provides legal services to local and international clients, including multinational corporations, investment funds, social enterprises, environmental enterprises, NGOs, government organizations and high net worth families and individuals.

My personal focus is in the areas of M&A, private equity, tax planning and international commercial arbitration.

My work areas also include renewable energy projects and CO2 projects.

Our Latin American office based in Chile also helps Chinese, Japanese, Indian and other Asian companies in their South American market entry.

Our office in China helps Japanese companies in their China market entry.
