Thomas Lee’s Summary

I've worked in the public relations and newspaper industries for over 14 years. I am a partner at 451 Marketing in Boston, a full-service communications agency specializing in public relations, social media marketing, and search marketing. We work with consumer brands to build awareness, engage customers, create fan loyalty, and drive business through a cutting-edge integrated communications approach.

Prior to 451, I spent five years at Regan Communications in Boston, providing strategic communications for a wide range of local and national companies including Dunkin’ Donuts, New Balance Athletic Shoe Inc., RE/MAX of New England, Mix 98-5 (WBMX-FM), Boston Magazine, and Boston Herald.

Before Regan, I served briefly as Public & Media Relations Manager at The Massachusetts Hospital Association. Before that, I was the Spokesman for three years at the Massachusetts Water Resources Authority (MWRA), where I worked on major public works projects like the Deer Island Treatment Plant and Integrated Water Supply Improvement Program. I began my career as a newspaper reporter, working at weekly newspapers in New Hampshire and Massachusetts. I graduated with a BA in English from Plymouth State College in New Hampshire. At PSC, I played football and served as News Editor for The Clock student newspaper. I was born and raised in Rockaway Beach, Queens (New York City) and graduated from Xavier High School in Manhattan. I currently reside in North Andover, MA with my wife Krista and our three daughters, Faith, Madeline and Ava.
