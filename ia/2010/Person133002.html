Mario R. Tur, Jr. AIA NCARB’s Summary

Mr. Tur has over twenty one years of professional experience as an Architect and an additional ten years internship in the industry giving him an in-depth and thorough knowledge of project management, environmental design, construction documentation, and contract administration. His experiences have been honed from the ground up in the field where he learned construction and then in the office where he applied field experiences to construction documentation and managing project teams. He has provided professional services for private individuals, corporate boards, institutional, and public government agencies under a variety of contractual and employment formats giving him versatility of communication styles to draw from.

Mr. Tur’s forte lies in his project organizational, planning, directing and controlling skills that positively impacts client’s schedule and budget.
