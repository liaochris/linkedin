Adrian Henry’s Summary

Adrian is a results driven technical programme manager and business consultant specialising in large scale, multi million pound business & technology transformation/change programmes within the telecommunications and public sectors. He has a very flexible approach and has worked across all aspects of a programme management from business development, bid & supplier management through to strong delivery and client engagement skills. Adrian’s business skills are built upon a firm technical foundation with experience in various design methodologies and development languages and an in-depth understanding of PRINCE 2 – he is also a member of the PMI. His consultative approach means he is able to engage successfully with stakeholders at all levels from board executives to frontline staff. Adrian has worked on a number of high profile projects was a main player in securing and delivering the NHS contracts for BT (one of the largest oracle implementations in the world). He has also worked with the BT Global Services Executive to introduce key global technology transformational change programmes into the business. Whilst at Deloitte Consulting, Adrian secured a number of lucrative contracts for their public sector practice. Whilst at the Ministry of Justice, Adrian was responsible for the deployment of the Libra Application to a number of early adopter sites and also the national lead for training. This was a critical business & technology transformation project within HMCS and key to its future. In his role at NHS Connecting for Health, Adrian was responsible for managing the end to end implementation of the TraMS National Technology programme. Adrian is currently working with BT Global Services and is responsible for the deployment of business transformation programmes for key Health clients within London.In 2005 Adrian set up his own business Redleaf Technology and is available for contract and interim roles.
