Martijn van Balken’s Summary

Over 10 years of professional experience in Digital TV Product Development, specialised in:

- Video on Demand (VOD)
- Interactive TV
- Advanced advertising
- Next Generation Gateway / Set Top Box / Remote Control

Responsible for the Product Development & Strategy of UPC’s VOD service in 5 countries and new customer centric digital tv products involving interactive advertising, gaming and enhanced TV as well as next generation hardware platform. UPC is the 2nd largest cable operator in the world.

Previously responsible for the New Product Development of advertising initiatives, portable devices and Home Networking product proposition at leading satellite operator Sky (BSkyB) in the UK and later at UPC’s sister company Chellomedia responsible for Product Development in Interactive TV.

Successfully founded a startup company in IP Telephony and digital TV with leading Blue Chip clients like Philips and KPN Telecom.

MSc in Digital TV, Management & Production - graduated with honors - and a Bachelor in Industrial Product Design Engineering.
