Wendy Scharfman’s Summary

--------------------
Wendy Scharfman is a communication coach, teacher and speaker. She is the founder of Coaching for Effective Communication, a business she created to help her clients become dynamic, confident speakers who can inspire action by delivering a message that matters.

EXPERIENCE:
-------------------

Wendy is not your typical communication coach. Her unique background as an actress with a 25-year career in film, television, and theater distinguishes her as a coach who knows how to connect with and move an audience. She brings this rare perspective and expertise to every client.

Wendy is an expert in helping clients prepare and deliver their message with clarity, confidence and authenticity – whether they are talking to the media, giving a speech, interviewing for a job or taking part in a meeting. Her clients say she is “loyal and committed”, “savvy”, and “able to turn really hard work into fun”. She is known for helping her clients create a little magic with their audiences. In these competitive times, effective communication can definitely be the keystone to success.
