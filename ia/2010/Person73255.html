Gary Veasy’s Summary

I am a conscientious, detail-oriented, and focused architect and an effective listener. I possess excellent written and verbal communication skills, strong planning and design capabilities, and a valuable strategy development and execution background. Strengths include a solid work ethic, professional approach, and honesty in dealing with all parties. I have managed a significant development pipeline and always meet quality, budget, and timing expectations.

My longevity in retail design and development industry has provided me with a dynamic landscape that is easily transferable to healthcare, drug, grocery, restaurant, consumer services, QSR, health and beauty, commercial facilities, and pharmacy and food users.
