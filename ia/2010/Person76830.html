Beth Gorell’s Summary

Accomplished business strategist and consultant, with extensive experience in strategy development and execution, process analysis and improvement, organizational design, channel programs and change management. Over 20 years of experience in a broad range of senior-level consulting and management positions within Silicon Valley entities, including Hewlett-Packard, Cisco Systems, VMware and Stanford University.

Selected Accomplishments:

• Led training and communications planning for a significant, multi-national technology company during their launch of a major new channel partner program. Designed comprehensive plans for both internal and external audiences, secured executive sponsorship and supported implementation.

• Led multiple organizational assessment engagements for a large, multi-national technology company, resulting in progressive organizational designs to address global business needs and support collaboration goals.

• Led analysis to improve development of organizational business strategies for a prestigious, private institution of higher education, facilitating definition and implementation of targeted improvements in business planning, objective setting, metrics identification and communications.

• Designed and implemented a strategic planning and performance management system at Stanford University, fundamentally improving Alumni Association focus, integration, accountability and synergy.

• Developed breakthrough global assessment and planning process at Hewlett-Packard, providing visibility into resource utilization, and resulting in significant shifts in financial and human resource allocation.

• Secured sponsorship and executive approval for innovative Stanford University institutional ad strategy, leading to significant budget approval from the Office of the President.

• Led effort to reconceive and relaunch company brand at IPS Associates, delivering strategy-led, integrated brand implementation across the company.

Beth Gorell’s Specialties:

Experience with domestic and international business, Fortune 500 to start-up environments, private sector and non-profit.

Leadership skills in business planning, process improvement, marketing operations, globalization and communications.
