James Burchill’s Summary

Today trust is the new currency, transparency is crucial, and authenticity is an absolute must. That’s where James comes in.

"A rare combination of marketing, business and technical skills makes him a brilliant marketer"

James helps you market and sell more of whatever it is you market and sell.

And since we all buy from people “we know, like and trust” James help companies develop strategies to achieve these objectives.

James is also a certified Coach and creator of numerous web sites and other educational training materials. His unique approach to web sales and marketing (along with his personable training style) make him the preferred choice for select companies of all sizes: from ’solo-preneurs’ to large corporations including Goodyear, eBay, Esso, CAA, Rolls Royce, BMO, and DoFasco.

Prior to starting his own consulting practice in 1999, James was Vice President of IT & Consulting for an international management and recruiting firm in southern Ontario. There he lead numerous multi million dollar development projects and managed a team of more than a dozen skilled web programmers and designers.

James has lived in Europe, Africa and Australia and along with numerous credentials including public speaking, psychology, communications, automation and technology, James is a certified Neuro Linguistics practitioner and learned the craft of penmanship at England’s prestigious London School of Writing.

James enjoys public speaking, training and writing about Internet technologies (such as social media and content marketing) and why you need to embrace the Internet for both personal and professional use. Current presentations and keynotes include: Fixing Your Follow Up, Social Media Marketing, and Automating Your Marketing.
