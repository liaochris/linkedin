Ran Mullins’s Summary

Passionate business and community leader familiar with creating something out of nothing through disruptive thinking and entrepreneurial spirit. Celebrates the long view in technology and business. Provides direction, vision, new revenue models, brand development, renewal and awareness. Expert creative director, designer, usability and user experience specialist.

Ran Mullins is a consultant and adviser on branding and Web site strategy integration to corporations and community organizations. He is a major proponent and shaper of Community Experience Development™ (CeD) business philosophy. In addition to being an artist and entrepreneur, he is a business owner and community advocate in Cincinnati's historic Over-the-Rhine neighborhood.
