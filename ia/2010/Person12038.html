Andrew Meringoff’s Summary

Meringoff Wealth Management Group, LLC. (MWMG) is an independent firm providing fee-only investment and financial advisory services.

As a fee-only advisor, I do not sell any financial products or receive commissions or other compensation from third parties. My sole source of compensation is from my clients, which ensures the complete independence and objectivity of all recommendations.

Every portfolio is tailored to the individual client needs. There is no model portfolio or cookie cutter here. I am not trying to scale my business by having a one size fits all approach. I believe one size does not fit all.

Using sophisticated planning tools; I assist clients in designing properly diversified portfolios based on their respective financial goals and risk tolerance.

The Fee schedule is simple and straightforward. The two options are as follows:

1.) A flat half of one percent (.50%) of assets under management.

2.) One quarter of one percent (.25%) plus a .75% performance fee if returns are 10% or greater annually.
If my clients don't do well, why should I?

You choose the custodian/platform. For example, Charles Schwab, Fidelity or another large firm you feel comfortable and safe with. It is your money, lets keep it that way!

Please call me at 703-224-2800 or email me at Andrew@Meringoffwealth.com. My website is Meringoffwealth.com and my blog address is meringoffsmarket.blogspot.com.
