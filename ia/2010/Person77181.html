Grainne McClorey’s Summary

Currently, I am the Director of Strategic Partnerships and Marketing for Net Fusion Services headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona. We offer services on a national and soon to be international level.

In my role at NFS I maintain relationships with several strategic partners, including a managed relationship with Microsoft as a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner; a VMWare Enterprise engagement, as well as succesful partnerships with I/O Communications and Cisco Systems.

NFS has been a leading provider of IT solutions since 2002 and we offer a rich portfolio of advanced technology services to a full myriad of professional industries. Our goal is to create business value from technology investments and offer an inventory of core services that include comprehensive IT Solutions, a Managed Services program (complete with 24/7/365 Help Desk support), remote monitoring and nationally recognized Custom Software Development team. NFS is dedicated to instilling client confidence and security.

For consecutive years NFS has been recognized as an industry leader and one of Arizona’s, “Best Places to Work”. Net Fusion has also been named by the Arizona Small Business Association as one of 50 “Companies to Watch” and is one of the fastest-growing technology consulting firms in the state.

For more information, visit www.netfusionservices.com.
