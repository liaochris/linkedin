Erin Kinikin’s Summary

Twenty-two years designing, implementing, recommending, and marketing customer database and application systems. Responsible for product messaging and launch of major data warehousing and CRM applications and technologies such as bit-mapped indexing, turnkey data marts, analytic applications, customer relationship management (CRM), application integration, and customer data integration (CDI). Strong verbal and written customer communication and consultation skills, including customer data management best practice studies for leading financial services companies; product requirement design (RFP) and vendor evaluation for CRM and CDI; and message tuning and white papers for major enterprise application launches.
