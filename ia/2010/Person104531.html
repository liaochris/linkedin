Jon Ripans’s Summary

Jon Ripans, J.D., M.B.A. Finance, Esq., is a Georgia Certified General commercial real estate appraiser. As an independent fee appraiser, Jon has appraised $1.5 billion of commercial real estate from Texas to Florida to Upstate New York, and just about every state in between. His appraisal work has been in the arenas of lending, investments, mergers, acquisitions, partnership and corporate buy-ins and buy-outs, trusts & estates, property tax, collateral used for supersedeas bonds (appeal bonds), bankruptcy/foreclosure confirmations. Jon’s valuation experience includes market value, value in use, going concern value, business value & insurable value. Jon works with developers, lenders, buyers, owner/occupants, investors, accountants, attorneys, business brokers, real estate brokers, and medical doctors. Because he is both a lawyer and an appraiser, Jon is also qualified to serve as a property tax arbitrator/referee.

Jon Ripans has also coined the phrase "distressed refinancers" to describe commercial real estate owners who have to refinance their balloon in frozen credit markets. Of course, the counter argument is: The banks don't want to take the collateral back - that would be like the dog that finally caught the car.
