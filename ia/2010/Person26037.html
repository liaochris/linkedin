Justin Crosby’s Summary

Justin is a specialist in developing and implementing world-class, UK and global consumer brand-building communications programmes. From strategic development through to campaign activation, Justin has created award winning programmes for some of world's biggest consumer electronics and entertainment brands, such as Philips Electronics, BT, Activision and Microsoft.

Justin has also developed some of the UK’s most successful consumer PR campaigns in the entertainment and FMCG sectors, for example creating the CarlingLive 24 music programme, groundbreaking Xbox experiential activities for Microsoft, the relaunch of Lara Croft Tomb Raider and award winning campaigns for Entertainment Rights, Pioneer, Celador, Clear Channel Entertainment and Chorion.

Justin won the CIPR Award for Best Consumer Relations campaign in 2007

He launched Boom! a new PR and creative communications agency in Summer 2009 and current clients include Future Publishing

Justin Crosby’s Specialties:

Marcoms, creativity, team management and strategic advice for consumer & entertainment brands
