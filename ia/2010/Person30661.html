  • President, Purpose Driven Publishing

    Saddleback Church

    (Non-Profit; Religious Institutions industry)

  • Chief Executive Officer

    Pastors.com, Inc.

    (Religious Institutions industry)

    October 2000 — June 2004 (3 years 9 months)

    In addition, served as the agent-of-record for "The Purpose Driven Life" by Rick Warren (Zondervan Publishers, 2002), the best-selling hard-cover non-fiction book in publishing history. From 2002 until early 2005, supervised the acquisition, development, and promotion of 67 unique PDL products in 54 languages with 35 publishers resulting in sales in excess of 30 million units.
