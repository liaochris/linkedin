Laura Myers-Wagner’s Summary

Laura Myers-Wagner is a Human Resources professional with experience in the development of organization structures, teams and operating systems; talent acquisition and talent management; employee relations and culture development for rapidly growing and changing organizations. Laura has a proven ability to collaborate with business leaders and employees to deliver human resources and talent management programs and services to assure organizations and their people achieve business goals.

Laura has fifteen years experience in Human Resources management with generalist and specialist assignments in catastrophe insurance at ICAT Holdings, LLC and professional liability insurance at Royal & SunAlliance/DPIC Companies, food manufacturing at Basic Vegetable Products, LP and hospitality at Doubletree Hotels.

Laura holds an MBA with emphasis in Human Resource Management from Golden Gate University and a Bachelor of Science in Health Education from San Diego State University.
Laura holds the following certifications:
-Senior Human Resources Professional, SHRM
-Certified Facilitator, DDI
-Compensation and Benefits Professional, World at Work

Results oriented with a keen interest in people, Laura has been successful in building human resources teams and creating talent management programs and services that enable companies to succeed. Laura is looking for an opportunity to join an organization which values people as an integral component of their business strategy. An effective business partner, Laura will share her talent management experience to meet today’s and the future’s business challenges.
