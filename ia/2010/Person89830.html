Jared Knowlton’s Summary

Jared completed a broad array of Dale Carnegie training including “High Impact Presentations,” “Sales Advantage,” and “Leadership Training for Managers” and “Effective Communications & Human Relations.”

Jared holds a California Real Estate License & Insurance Sales Licensee

In management and sales Jared has promoted explosive growth to occupancy rates for Buckingham Property Management in Clear Lake, California, where he was responsible for managing two separate properties. Leveraging keen business acumen and an innate talent for sales, marketing, and business development, he achieved an 80% increase in occupancy across both properties, within three short months. Additionally, he drove a 300% increase in tenant satisfaction ratings, ensuring a dramatic improvement in tenant retention.

He rose from the ranks of IT Specialist to Lead in the WAMU/CHASE conversion; one of the largest retail conversions in history. Previously he was at the United States Coast Guard. Where he maximized development, delivery, and technical support of multimillion-dollar, technology-based military college campus installation, including training for a variety of operational software, applications, and technical system support to over 1500 students and faculty.

12 years previous to this he was Chief Technology Officer/Founder, of business operations & management of his own technology consulting firm, where he was accountability for integration, configuration, and maintenance of projects.
