Ryan D. Cook, SPHR’s Summary

Ryan Cook is Vice President of Energy Central’s Employment Services Division where he is responsible for creating unparalleled talent sourcing and acquisition services for the U.S. power industry. Ryan currently leads EnergyCentralJobs.com – the nation’s premier job listing, candidate sourcing, and research provider focused exclusively on the power industry.

Mr. Cook has spent the last fifteen years building cutting-edge passive candidate research programs, successful employment brands, and winning talent acquisition strategies. He holds a degree of honor in Human Communication from the University of Denver and is a certified Senior Professional of Human Resources. He is also a member of the Colorado Human Resource Association and a long standing member of the Society for Human Resource Management.
