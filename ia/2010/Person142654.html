Michael Hansen’s Experience

  • Chairman, co-owner

    Moneytainment Media Group - MMG Holding Ltd

    Some of the Brands:

    www.travnet.se/liveracing - A pool betting product focusing on Trotting

  • CEO and co-owner

    Daytime Media House AB

    (Media Production industry)

    September 2005 — May 2007 (1 year 9 months)

    A Nordic leader and pioneer in high quality analysis on sports and betting information. Business embracing TV production, print products and web sites, e.g. www.goalwire.com and www.travnet.se the number one horse racing web site in Sweden. Parts of this group were acquired by Moneytainment Media Group - MMG.

  • CEO

    Universum / Cosmos Media

    (Privately Held; Market Research industry)

    May 2000 — June 2003 (3 years 2 months)

Editorial focus was mainly on career development. Cosmos Media was the owner of Ungdomsbarometern, a leading research company in Sweden, focusing on consumer behaviors amongst 15-24 year olds.
