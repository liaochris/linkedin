Seth Hosko’s Summary

I want help brands discover their why and tell their story through every touchpoint by creating holistic brand experiences.

I have an insatiable curiosity for the why, for understanding people — their values, why they do what they do — and the process behind finding the answers to what could be.

I find value in making connections, distilling information down to what's relevant, and finding common threads between art, human behavior, design, and culture.


• I am a dreamer, planner, and project manager. I thrive from making sense out of the vague.

• I’ve organized a 6+ person office from the ground up, steered ambiguous client projects, and learned new industries and technologies when needed through direct experience.

• I’ve visited 28 countries, lived in Boston, Philadelphia, Vancouver, and Bangkok in the past year while managing client and internal projects.

• I learn through experience. During the 2010 Vancouver Olympics with no formal journalism training, I joined VANOC press operations as a reporter, interviewing Olympic athletes and coaches while working with CTV, NBC, and seasoned journalists.

• I love the study of New Urbanism and the culture of civic design, Manhattans (w/ Martini & Rossi please), theater, Appalachia, travel, and the art of mastering the uncomfortable.
