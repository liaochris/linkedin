Kate Fiandaca’s Summary

Kate is an enthusiastic relationship builder and networker. Having worked in recruiting for nearly two years, Kate prides herself in her ability to connect with individuals, providing candid career guidance, exciting opportunities, and a positive candidate experience.

Kate received her BS in Business Administration with a focus in Entrepreneurship from Boston University's School of Management.

She is excited to be working in the heart of Silicon Valley with some of the very best start-ups and technology companies in the area. On the weekends, you can find Kate hiking and exploring the Bay Area, the Sierras and beyond, or cooking up exotic recipes in her kitchen.
