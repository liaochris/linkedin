Jose Luis Marín de la Iglesia’s Summary

Multidisciplinary College education in Technology (Engineering) and Business Managament provides me a deep understanding of both technical and managing issues involved in leading projects.

Main expertise in IT consulting projects and internet software development. Broad experience leading R&D projects involving Internet related technologies. Strong communication skills and wide experience in teaching and conferencing.

Interested in every single aspect of the impact of Internet and IT technologies in our lifes, including Intellectual property, new business models and ethical concerns.

Currently undertaking PhD studies on Web languages and technologies, field in which I have leaded 7 R&D projects in the past few years.

Co-founder of the Open Economy blog dedicated to technology, business and economy challenges and transformations driven by the Internet revolution.

As an author I wrote: “Web 2.0. Una descripción muy sencilla de los cambios que estamos viviendo”. Netbiblo. (2010), ISBN-13: 978-8497454834, and contributed with the chapter "Doing business by selling free services" to the book “Web 2.0: The Business Model”. Springer. (2008), ISBN-13: 978-0387858944

I also support and participate in many initiatives and projects related to the open source software, innovation community and the spread of free and open knowledge. At the moment committed with the open data movement and the reuse of public sector information.
