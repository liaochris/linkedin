Brian Neely’s Summary

As CIO and CTO for AMERICAN SYSTEMS, Mr. Neely leads the overall vision, planning, and management of information technology and information management resources across the enterprise. As a pragmatist dealing with the realities of business, and as an evangelist fostering innovation and technology awareness, Mr. Neely partners with the organization to enable the business strategy by increasing IT efficiency and agility, and maximizing value creation. Mr. Neely is known for his strategic formulation and execution, including financial planning, policy and governance, risk management, and continuous process improvement. He began his career at AMERICAN SYSTEMS in 1996, and has been instrumental in the company’s strategic and operational plans, including acquisition, growth and sustainable development…supporting thousands of users distributed at hundreds of locations globally.

With over twenty years of hands-on experience and a background of intense formal education, Mr. Neely effectively fuses the business and the technology worlds. On the business side, Mr. Neely’s acute business sense comes from five years as a “C” level executive in a progressive technology organization, building on his knowledge and insight gained as an alumni of the Wharton School of Business (UPenn) and through executive certifications in Management & Leadership and Strategy & Innovation earned at MIT’s elite Sloan School of Management.

On the technical side, Mr. Neely has an engineering background and 15 years of real-world technical experience, staying current through active involvement in the ever-evolving technology community. This experience builds on his B.S. in Engineering from Virginia Tech, his Masters in Information Technology from Carnegie Mellon’s Heinz School of Policy and Management, his CIO Certification from the Federal CIO Council, and his Chief Information Security Officer Certification from Carnegie Mellon’s Cyber Security Lab.

Brian Neely’s Specialties:

Mr. Neely was selected as one of ExecutiveBiz’s Top 10 CTOs of 2009; is certified in Lean Six Sigma, ITIL, & CMMI, and is a practitioner of COBIT, PMP, and ISO 9001; Mr. Neely is a member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), the Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA), and Technology, Entertainment, & Design (TED).

Specialties: Technology Business-Alignment, IT Business Value Creation, and Efficiencies Through Effective Technology Use.
