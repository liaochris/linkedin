Jeff Zdilla’s Summary

Highly knowledgable in the area of lubrication. Specific formulation proficiency includes metalworking fluids, gear oils, open gear lubricants, greases, sacrificial coatings for tungsten carbide, die casting lubricants, centrifugal casting lubricants, foundry release coatings, compressor oils, quench oils, emulsification of high molecular weight polybutene, emulsification of inorganic metalic complexes, and forging lubricants. With respect to forging lubricants, my specialties included colloidal graphite and high temperature synthetic systems for strategic alloys. Well networked throughout the forging industry community. Past member of the FIA Plant Engineering and FIA Technical Committees and frequent speaker at technical seminars. In May 2008 in Orlando, FL, I was part of the team that developed the Technology Road Map for the Forging Industry. I published an article on forging lubrication in the 2009 January issue of FORGE magazine. .

My current role at Rhein Chemie has me transferring my technical knowledge, business development, and sales skills from the formulation side of the lubricant business to that of the additive supplier. My specific role focuses on the NAFTA market. Hopefully, I will have the pleasure to meet many new peers and friends within the industry at various NLGI, ILMA, and STLE functions.
