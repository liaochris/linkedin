Amanda Worthington’s Summary

As an Associate at Boyden Chicago, I am responsible for executing senior level search assignments in various industry sectors. My primary focus is candidate development and relationship-building. I source, contact, interview, and qualify every candidate who is later presented to our clients. Through a combination of knowledge from my MBA and careful listening skills when working with candidates, I have a deep understanding of the industries from which my candidates emerge.

The opportunity to work closely with clients, as well as learn from some of the best managing directors in the business is what attracted me to Boyden Chicago. My interaction with Boyden offices in over 40 countries allows me to serve both my candidates and clients at the highest possible level. We approach all of our clients and candidates with honesty and professionalism. I appreciate and respect the amount of trust our candidates put in us, and I treat the opportunity to hear both the professional and personal stories they share as a privilege. The effects of this approach are shown in the number of candidates who later become Boyden clients.

Prior to joining Boyden Chicago, I began my career in executive search at a firm specializing in insurance and financial services, where I worked to identify senior level professionals for managed care companies across the United States. I began my professional career as an undergraduate admission counselor for my alma mater, Loyola University Chicago, traveling extensively throughout the United States recruiting future Ramblers. During this time, my interest in Millennials and intergenerational joie de vivre in the workplace was born as I was responsible for attracting, interviewing, hiring, and training a team of 120 undergraduate students—a task that required both a lot of patience, as well as a lasting supply of caffeine to keep up with their energy.

Please let me know how I can help. I am open to networking.
