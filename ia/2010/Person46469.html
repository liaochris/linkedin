Dan Austin’s Summary

Dan Austin has more than 15 years of experience in building successful organizations within both the consumer and enterprise high-technology industries.

Dan has founded, led, and managed some of the most innovative product development teams of the Internet's first era. Dan was an original member of Food.com's team and served as the Director of Online Business Development and Sales. He has also held executive level positions at both startups and established companies, including internet media/entertainment pioneer Vivendi/Universal and High-Tech firm TMP Directional Marketing - Monster.com.

Dan has served in a variety of executive level and consulting positions within the interactive sales development, marketing and high-technology organizations including: TMP Directional Marketing, Monster.com, The Chicago Tribune, NewMedia Inc., Meca Communications Inc., Food.com, NBC Vivendi/Universal, and most recently Game Time Technologies.

Prior leadership roles included acquisitions, management services and other business development and strategic activities. His entrepreneurial spirit has propelled several ventures beyond incubation stages. He has won numerous awards for outstanding achievement and consistency.

In his role as Chief Revenue Officer and Co-Founder of Game Time Technologies , Dan works closely with the Game Time Technologies Board of Directors to provide strategic direction for the company and to maintain the Game Time value proposition within all departments; while also playing a critical role in driving the strategic vision and revenue of the company.

Dan Austin attended Sonoma State University. While attending that institution, he excelled on the Baseball diamond as the only two-year team captain in the school's history and was named academic All-American.

With the proliferation of hyper-local data and insights, and local targeting capabilities emerging across many media, Geomentum enables marketers to pin-point their customers and plan the most relevant and responsive media down to the zip-code and even neighborhood block level.

    Geomentum has been in development for more than two years. A next generation business unit, Geomentum combines and integrates the best of consultancy, technology and media agency expertise for a range of clients in categories such as Retail, CPG, Financial Services, Telecommunications, QSR, and Automotive. Geomentum is led by CEO Dave Walker, a former head of Marketing for Toys “R” Us, Musicland and Circuit City.

Geomentum extends and enhances the best-in-class resources and massive buying clout of Mediabrands’ current hyper-local agencies; NSA Media (Print), Wahlstrom (Local Search and IYP) and Outdoor Services (Out-of-Home). In 2009 Geomentum will plan and place more than $2B in hyper-local media, according to RECMA.
