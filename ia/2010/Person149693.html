I founded an investment club that meets regularly to play the educational game CashFlow by Robert Kiyosaki of "Rich Dad Poor Dad". The club has evolved into an investment focused online discussion group that meets in person bi-monthly to discuss money making opportunities.

The PMBA program is focused on delivering a world-class business education, without enrolling in a traditional MBA program. I participated in the PMBA : Business Crash Course and would recommend it to anyone that wants to establish a real world business knowledge base.
