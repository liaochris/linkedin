David Kennedy’s Summary

My company, Corona Insights, serves as a think tank for clients needing to make decisions on a wide variety of topics. We help clients uncover the right answers to the questions most important to them. Then we guide them on how the answers can inform their decisions and plans.

I am personally passionate about identifying a business's or organization's true needs and then working to address those needs through accurate, cost effective market research.

I have been at Corona Insights (formerly Corona Research) for over four years, in which time I have worked to help a company uncover the need for additional distribution strategies to reach their market; understand myths by the public associated with their cause which enabled more effective marketing communications; and conducted online studies for large consumer brands.

I enjoy working with clients and ensuring that they are getting what they need out of their research. I work to communicate research (and especially quantitative research) in a way that is meaningful and actionable.
