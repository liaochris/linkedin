Tess Taylor’s Summary

Tess Taylor, a music industry career strategist, is President of the National Association of Record industry Professionals (est. 1998) & of the Los Angeles Music Network (est. 1988), & author of many articles & interviews with top record executives. Tess has consulted leading businesses such as MySpace, InsideSessions (a joint venture between the Universal Music Group & Penguin Putnam, Inc.), The Walt Disney Company, BMG Entertainment (now Sony BMG), Concord Records & others. A frequent featured expert on national television & radio, her analyses have appeared in national & international business press. She speaks to colleges, professional associations & organizations around the world guiding individuals to personal & professional fulfillment. Through her work in the US & abroad Tess has helped connect countless people to jobs & opportunities, & facilitated in launching hundreds of projects & several companies. She is editor & publisher of the NARIP News, now distributed to 85,000+ record industry professionals worldwide. In May 2009, the World Championships of Performing Arts Board unanimously voted to honor Tess with its 2009 Achievement Award & induct her into its Performing Arts Hall of Fame. Past award recipients include Ann-Margret, Liza Minnelli & Garth Brooks. In its Dec 2008 edition, Music Connection magazine included Tess in its list of "50 Innovators, Iconoclasts, Groundbreakers & Guiding Lights of 2008" & calls NARIP "top-notch & brilliantly executed." The list includes Robert Plant, Trent Reznor, Irving Azoff, Brian Eno, AC/DC & others. A classically trained pianist, she studied music at the Univ. of Vienna, & is a Phi Beta Kappa, Magna Cum Laude graduate of the Univ. of Redlands' Johnston College where she received her BA in music, German & literature. Tess loves nibbling dark chocolates & drinks lots of tea. She also loves traveling the world, fountain pens, gripping crime novels, & anything ever written or recorded by Glenn Gould or Pete Townshend.
