David Novosad’s Summary

David Novosad came to Teneros from NeuStar, Inc. (NYSE: NSR) where he was Vice President of Ultra Services, a division created by the acquisition of UltraDNS Corporation by NeuStar in 2006. Serving as General Manager and Vice President, Operations at UltraDNS, David was imperative in defining the direction of the Corporation. Through numerous executive positions within UltraDNS Corporation, he was instrumental in product initiatives, strategic relations, corporate operations, global expansion, and launching of UltraDNS' East Coast office. Before joining UltraDNS, he held senior positions with several high-tech firms including Edge Connections and Logix Communications. At Edge Connections, he was responsible for the corporation’s financial plan and market strategy, as well as tracking company performance to a number of high-growth metrics. He gained significant experience in the telephony space at Logix Communications where he was responsible for revenue assurance, telephony services, and financial planning for product and business initiatives. David holds an MBA from Oklahoma State University where he graduated with the highest honors.
