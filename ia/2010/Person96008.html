Jeremy Greshin’s Summary

Jeremy Greshin has more than 18 years of executive level sales, marketing, business development, and account management experience in various industries, both domestically and internationally. His expertise lies in launching products and growing small and mid-size businesses by developing and managing relationships inside large client organizations. He identifies business opportunities, creates strategies and grows relationships that result in significant revenue generation from new and existing markets.

Jeremy Greshin’s Specialties:

Sales and channel planning and management, hiring and training sales professionals, domestic and international business development and account management.
