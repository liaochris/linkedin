Brian Turner’s Summary

Thirty year experience in developing and managing the sponsorship of major sports and entertainment projects. Currently looking for a limited number of projects that can utilize our wealth of experience and industry contacts. Based in Southern California but available to travel for projects that are of interest.
We were part of the group which started the Long Beach GP and were involved in most of the temporary street circuits of the 1980's. We worked extensively with Philip Morris in developing their motor sports program. More recently we were involved with the development of Heartland Park, Topeka, Pikes Peak International Raceway and Miller Motorsports Park.
We have many contacts in global motorsports

Our current assignments include developing sponsorship for the globally distributed TV show, Inside Grand Prix and the Dana Point Concours

Brian Turner’s Specialties:

Event and Facility development and management, Sponsorship development and activation.
