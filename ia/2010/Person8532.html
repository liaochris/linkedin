Al Thompson’s Summary

I've been a musician/songwriter for 40 years, and an IT professional working in several roles for 20 years.

I did concert sound for over 12 years for major national acts. Tasks included designing, configuring, setup and operation, troubleshooting and repair.

IT tasks have run the gamut from sales, configuration and customer support, to network administration, online administrator for several national online services, and also writing/editing for an online news service.
