Anne Cull’s Summary

Online Marketing and Business Development consultant working to generate leads for unique markets through creative branding, affiliate programs, social media/networking, and online advertising.

Founder and Managing Partner at ThinkViralKC.com, a business development organization for small to medium sized companies in the Kansas City area. We provide online social media/networking training, as well as monthly all-inclusive service packages. Month to month pricing - no long term contracts required. Easy start-up terms for tight budgets.

Our best clients are small business owners who don't have time to market themselves, don't have the budget to hire a marketing employee but need to bring awareness to their organization, product, or event.
