Kelly T. Mullaney’s Summary

Working Art Media is a multimedia and design company that more than building websites, focuses in creating online solutions for small businesses. We provide tools that empower our clients to take control of their websites, managing their resources, undertaking sales, service and marketing activities.

Our approach to projects is “beyond consulting.” Each client relationship is considered a business partnership where we provide personalized service, flexible to a client’s individual situation. From basic design to elaborate expansions, our skills have helped clients move through every stage of the website development process.

My professional background and education is both extensive and culturally diverse. With over 15 years experience ranging from content development to sales and marketing applications. Specialized in user-centered website design and multimedia development. Fluent in Spanish and English, offering bilingual services to all of Working Art Media’s clients.

I love my work, and I derive a lot of job satisfaction from a client’s success in their business due to my contributions.
