Peter Csathy’s Summary

Seasoned digital media/technology executive with an unbroken track record of company successes (including 3 for 3 successful exits) in emerging growth, growth and Fortune 500 companies. Significant international business experience.

CEO of Sorenson Media, Inc. -- the recognized leader in video solutions that empower businesses and consumers to distribute and monetize their video assets across the Internet. Former CEO of SightSpeed Inc. (acquired by Logitech Inc. in November 2008) -- the recognized leader in video and voice over IP communications, video-based content/community/social networking and commerce. And previously, President & COO of digital music pioneer Musicmatch, Inc. (company was acquired by Yahoo!); and COO of real-time media search company eNow (company was acquired by AOL). Before running digital media/Internet companies, Senior Vice President at Universal Studios and, earlier, business affairs executive at Savoy Pictures and New Line Cinema. Career began as a media/entertainment attorney representing motion picture and music clients.

Education -- Harvard Law School (J.D.) and University of Minnesota (summa cum laude)

Peter Csathy’s Specialties:

Strategy, operations, strategic international business development and partnerships, product development and marketing, M&A, and VC fund-raising across media, digital media and technology (with a vast network of senior executive, VC, press and analyst contacts)
