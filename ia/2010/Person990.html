Aaron Norris’s Summary

Born and raised in Riverside, California, Aaron returned to Southern California after living in New York City for seven years. Performing since the age of six, Aaron left for New York in 1997 where he graduated from the New School University in New York City with a BFA in theater. Aaron has performed in over 60 productions around the world.

Between "gigs" in New York City, Aaron found his second love in graphic design and marketing. Work experience in New York and LA include investment banking firm Lazard Freres, KTU Radio, MGA Entertainment, Clear Channel Entertainment, Riverside Public Utilities, the Orange County Affiliate of Susan G. Komen and several financial and law firms. Aaron completed his MBA in marketing at the University of California, Irvine in 2009.

Aaron is Vice President and marketing director of The Norris Group which specializes in real estate investor education, hard money lending, and real estate investments. Aaron's responsibilities include marketing and PR, business development, producer for TNG's award winning radio show, events, and seminars. TNG has also help raise over $170,000 for Susan G Komen for the Cure through it's award-winning series I Survived Real Estate.

Aaron currently serves on several local non profit and professional association boards and is actively involved in the community.

Aaron is also a principal at Palisoul, Norris, + Conroy, a marketing and strategy firm which produces the Cocktail Party Statement podcast and also teaches web 2.0 strategies at colleges, professional associations, and businesses all over Southern California.
