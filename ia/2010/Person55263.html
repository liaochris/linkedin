Kelly Chavez’s Summary

I am a full-time stay-at-home mom, a full-time MBA student, a part-time karate/cardio kickboxing instructor and I recently launched the website for my jewelry design business.

I am a stay-at-home mom and love nearly every minute of it. I am launched my business andI love that I can be at home with my children and still volunteer in their classrooms. I am wrapping up my MBA program in March 2010 and am currently searching for a position in the field of education.

I began my jewelry design business in 2006 ago but only did shows here and there, never really pushed it. I decided it was time to push my business into the next level and launched my website. I now work daily on my website, adding products, and gaining network contacts.

I graduated in June of 2008 with my Bachelors in Marketing Management. In the fall of 2008 I returned to school to work towards my MBA.

I designed, implemented and teach a women's only karate and cardio kickboxing class as assist in classes with various ages and ranks. I also am the substitute instructor when ever the lead instructor is unavailable.

I have a very busy schedule, but I would not have it any other way. I look forward to networking and meeting many new people and learning of new job opportunities.
