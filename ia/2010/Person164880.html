Go Green’s Summary

PlanIT Green Promotions is the earth-friendly Division of Capstone Promotions. We have been passionate about sustainability for many years and looked for opportunities within our own business to be socially responsible. What better way than to offer "eco" versions of traditional promotional products.

We have been a traditional promotional products agency serving major corporations since 1998. We specialize in fulfillment services and our customers and client base include businesses of all sizes throughout North America.

OUR MISSION: Educate ourselves so we can educate others. The “green” promotional products industry is in its infancy, but emerging rapidly with their offerings of eco-friendly products. We are committed to being leaders in this industry and will help you find the best product available for you budget that is the least harmful to the environment.
