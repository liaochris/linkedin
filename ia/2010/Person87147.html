Jacqui Sakowski’s Summary

Skilled at helping sales professionals at all levels up their game; their energy and their self-belief. Focusing on skill development; conceptual sales message development; sales strategy development and their implementation I enable individuals and teams to move forward - further and faster - to their goals. .

Since 2001 have worked with more than one hundred and fifty corporations around the United States to deliver high energy, customized training programs and one-to-one coaching that engages and empowers participants to greater accomplishments while being their best authentic selves.

Sales Professional with more than twenty five years of sales and sales management expertise gained in both Fortune 500 and privately held small business enterprises.

Served clients on five continents in service and manufacturing industries. Overcoming linguistic and cultural barriers to build highly profitable and enduring partnerships.

Built and managed highly motivated and skilled sales teams in ISO9000 environments.

I seek to work with individuals and teams who are committed to their own success, and that of the companies for which they work.
