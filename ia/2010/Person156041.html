Noel Lyons’s Summary

The founder of The Expert Success Academy and creator of the “GO-To Expert's Roadmap”. I'm 100% committed to helping you build your dream business that LASTS (with Leverage + Automation + Systems + Time Off + Scale) and that authentically represents YOU.

I encourage all experts to proactively focus on building a “real” sustainable business versus a reactionary promotional business. It can seem confusing at first as there is no one perfect business model (indeed how you do it is as much your competitive advantage as what you do) but in following my simple Good To GO guides, I guarantee you can build a buzzworthy business truly worthy of you.

Online since 2001, with Pure ProActivity, I know what to pay attention to online, who can be trusted and what’s the best return on your time. Time being THE critical component as it’s the only factor you can’t get back (plus the Internet rewards those who move fast).

My background is in health + fitness. Even today, I am one of an elite few in the fitness world with 21 yr experience at the highest level, a Master’s Degree in Exercise + Health Science (Bristol University), a bachelors degree in Physical Education (Birmingham University) and a world-class sporting background (GB Triathlon – 5 world champs). I was the original fitness manager at the world reknown Chelsea Harbour Club, UK education manager for Technogym and Quality Assurance Manager with BUPA. A regular columnist for Inside Out Health magazine, I've been featured in the Daily Telegraph, Fit Pro magazine, Andalucia Golf, Gibraltar Chronicle, Gib TV and Best Magazine.

I've also been involved in the Coaching and Mentoring world for the last 10yr. A founder member of Coachville, I was part of the r+d group of the legendary Thomas Leonard, as well as having digested virtually every personal development book over the last 30 years!
