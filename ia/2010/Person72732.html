Greg Garner’s Summary

With an innovative and entrepreneurial mindset, Greg Garner has over 14 years of high-tech leadership. He excels at transforming vision into strategies necessary to executing a successful business plan.

Greg has been a part of more than seven product launches in both enterprise and startup companies. Most recently, as one of the initial hires at Conenza, he led efforts to create and launch a social networking platform.

Currently, Greg is an independent consultant for several technology startups. As a consultant, he has completed technology due diligence for a startups resulting in technology road maps and strategic plans and served as the lead for technology projects for a national wireless carrier.
