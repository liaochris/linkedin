Catherine McQueen’s Summary

The Green Leaf Inn in Lake Geneva, Wisconsin, will be the Midwest's first purpose-built green B&B.

We will be breaking ground soon. You can follow our progress at http://www.thegreenleafinn.com.

Our goal is to create a space that succeeds both as a luxurious B&B and as a working education site for a green lifestyle. We will maximize sustainability and renewable sources and minimize environmental impact in all aspects of the development of the Inn, from power to water to building materials to landscaping to the pillows on the beds and the faucets in the bathroom, including solar power, solar thermal, wind turbine, in-ground heat pump, grey water reuse, bioswales and rain gardens.

The Inn will have 19 suites and a conference center dedicated to renewable energy. People who visit the Inn will be able to immerse themselves in the working details of green building and green living, or simply enjoy a relaxing weekend in one of the top vacation spots in the Midwest.

Please visit the site and share your thoughts with us.
