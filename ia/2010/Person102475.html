John Latham’s Summary

John is an executive consultant with expertise solving business problems in multiple industries. He helps the marketing and sales C-Suite achieve their goals in Customer Loyalty, Integrated Promotion and Social Media. His philosophy and that of his company – ePrize – is that people and ideas make things happen, not products. As a result, John has been fortunate to surround himself with other passionate, positive, dynamic and intelligent people.

John's strategic, consultative style and approach is a natural fit for marketing executives who are interested in creative, collaborative and innovative ideas that drive results. His entrepreneurial spirit lends itself both to the fast-paced world at ePrize, and to his role as a chief consultant to some of the world's best brands.
