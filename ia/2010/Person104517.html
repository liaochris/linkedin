Jon Perry’s Summary

I am an OpenLink member. Send me your invite if you would like to connect. I do NOT accept invitations from those without a profile picture.

His company Merchant Services, Inc. (formerly 888QuikRate.com Merchant Services), was the Fort Worth Star-Telegram's, 2007 Small Business of the Year. 2009 Stevie Award Finalist, partner Vanessa Lang, Best Young Entrepreneur.

Since 1995, Jon Perry has specialized in working with organizations that want to recreate their culture and make more money. His expertise has provided over $1 Billion in organic sales during the past decade.

With an extensive background in systems integration, business process reengineering and marketing, Perry has a true understanding of what the customer-centric culture organizations need to survive in the new economy. Jon advises companies on best practices used in implementing successful sales and marketing programs. He has a unique ability to envision a complete end-to-end systems process, one that will allow organizations to communicate more efficiently, more consistently and more effectively with their customers.

Prior to creating his own successful businesses, Jon served in Executive roles at Relizon, ManTech International and MCI/WorldCom. Over the past decade Jon has provided consulting for Fortune 1000 companies. He has also held various technical consultative and senior management positions in both private industry and government. Jon holds a bachelor’s degree in mathematics/education from the Southern Illinois University and a master’s of business administration degree from the Florida Institute of Technology.

COMMUNITY Jon provides Pro Bono consulting for non-profits. He is also active in other community projects including Chambers of Commerce, the Fort Worth Knowledge for Success and Eliminating Poverty in Tarrant County.
