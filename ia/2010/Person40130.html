Chris Carson, PSP, CCM, PMP’s Summary

Chris Carson is Corporate Director of Project Controls for Alpha Corporation, a professional consulting firm, managing a team of schedulers, analysts, project managers and trainers, providing full construction management services specializing in scheduling, schedule analysis, claims avoidance, and claims preparation and defense, in facilities and transportation projects. Chris has over 35 years of experience in scheduling and construction management of educational, municipal, governmental, industrial, multi-family, residential, and commercial projects, in building construction as well as infrastructure and civil/site work. He has provided seminars for dozens of scheduling professionals in CPM scheduling methodologies, and manages the in-house training program at Alpha. He is active in the PMI College of Scheduling as Managing Director of the SEI project writing Best Practices and Guidelines for Scheduling and Claims, and the AACE, serving on the Claims & Dispute Resolution and Planning & Scheduling Committees, as well as CMAA, as a team member revising the Time Management Chapter of the Construction Management Standards of Practice. Chris is certified as a Planning and Scheduling Professional (PSP) by AACE, a Certified Construction Manager (CCM) by CMAA, and a Project Management Professional (PMP) by PMI.
