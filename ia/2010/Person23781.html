William (Bill) Gates’s Summary

Goal is to expand solutions worldwide in order to reach the vision of Star Trek with Federations of Federations in other planets such as the Moon by expanding the abilities of every person on the planet in the areas of Global Education, Health and Development through exciting methods that allow for learning quickly. Allow people to make mistakes by letting them experience different ideas and reward those who make the effort although they may fail at first but don't give up so that they eventually succeed.
