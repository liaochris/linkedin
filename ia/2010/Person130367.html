Malcolm Gabriel, MBA; MA (Ind Psy)’s Summary

Malcolm is currently the Talent Manager at Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company's Gadsden, Alabama operation. Here Malcolm oversees the development and execution of the talent management strategy, implements the acquisition, development and retention of talent, ensures compliance to employment laws and regulations, drives the rigorous implementation of performance management, facilitates the implementation of organization development initiatives, and oversees leadership development.

Previously, Malcolm held leadership roles in HR and OD in telecommunications, pharmaceutical, financial services and manufacturing companies, as well as local government.

Malcolm has managed the development and implementation of executive learning programs, facilitated organization development and change management interventions, initiated and implemented knowledge management projects, and provided generalist strategic and operational HR leadership in non-union, multi-union, multi-site and corporate environments. Malcolm authored articles and presented on leadership, talent development and performance management.

He is currently the Chairperson of the Advisory Board of CCI Leadership Strategies and presents on the Leading a Change Leadership program at the University of Toronto. In the past, Malcolm served on the Applications Committee of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Ontario, served as a strategic advisor to a Hydrogen Taxi Company start-up, served as Program Director of the Toronto Organization Network, served on the Board of Directors of Progress Career Planning Institute, and on the Executive of the Cape Town Junior Chamber of Commerce.

Malcolm has a MBA and a MA (Industrial Psychology).
