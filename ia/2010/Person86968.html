Jacob Saaby Nielsen (jacob.saaby@gmail.com)’s Summary

Instead of summing up what you can already read below, I want to try to give you a better picture of... me :)

I'm a guy who take the fun and interesting aspects of life serious, and is firmly founded in my relations to family and friends. I reach out. And I reach back, when reached out to, in general. I believe in the not so obvious relations, they can often turn out to be very powerful.

My sociable nature has lead to a passion for social media, several of which I actively use on a daily basis. My passion though, is how you use social media within the company, to create stronger relations between employees, develop and persist information and decisions, and make knowledge and innovation something in which everyone participates.

The thoughts of We-Mind and The Wisdom of Crowds are strong influences to me.

I act on my thoughts when I find them interesting, or feel passionate about them. This has led to me starting to run (from not being able to, to running a half marathon on june 12th), play the guitar, make sushi, and I'm currently starting the website coollabo.net, a site on Enterprise 2.0 and related topics. As well as starting an Academy Profession in Business Communication.

My focus is, and will be in the future, on how to collaborate and communicate better and more efficiently, how to leverage social/knowledge platforms in the business, to support business processes, and on making innovation something to which every employee contributes.

As a colleague, I try to include people, contribute value and share knowledge.

I'm the owner of a strong inquisitive mind in combination with an eagerness to learn, and the ability to apply what I learn, in real life.

These are aspects of me. If you want to know more, get in touch or just plain say hi - you know what button to push.

I look forward to making your acquaintance :)
