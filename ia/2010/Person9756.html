Amit Shukla’s Summary

Driven and results-oriented cleantech expert with over 10 years experience in renewable energy and energy efficiency, sustainability, biobased chemicals, and water. Educational achievements are an MBA and B.S. degrees in both Chemical Engineering and Biochemistry. Skills include feasibility studies, development of business strategies, evaluation and selection of renewable energy technologies, business plans, market analysis, development of financial and risk strategies, due diligence, government grant proposal review and submission.

Won more than $5 million in government grants by authoring grant proposals to commercialize new biomass cogeneration processes for the biofuels and agricultural processing industries. These funds leveraged an additional $11 million from the private sector and the resulting new processes saved $3 million/year in operations costs for clients. Successfully led projects from planning to full commercial implementation while working in cross-functional teams and capitalizing on my leadership, communication, and organizational skills.
