Casey Potenzone’s Summary

Casey Potenzone is a seasoned technologist with a focus on startups and early stage companies. His experience includes hands-on and executive roles in sales and business development, application development and architecture as well as running data centers and security operations. Potenzone specializes in taking emerging products and companies to market and has served as the key intermediary between multiple startups and their acquired, fortune 500 and SMB customers.

Currently Potenzone is the VP of US Sales for eCommerce provider Avangate. Previously he served as CIO of Uniloc, VP of Operations at Relegence, Director of Technology at MTV, as well as various consulting roles within oil, banking and entertainment. Potenzone continues to serve as an advisor to several public and private operations as well as providing expert witness testimony in the area of digital media.

At any point Potenzone has at least one to two companies or products bubbling on the back burner. To date he has been awarded two patents as primary inventor, named an inventor on three others and has several additional provisional and in-process patent applications. Potenzone is a product manager and developer at heart and is constantly pushing the envelope of new technologies.

Casey Potenzone’s Specialties:

Emerging companies or products, product development, online marketing and sales, business development, web architecture and development and turning big ideas from small companies into highly successful ones.
