Steven I. Adler’s Summary

Steven Adler is the Privacy Manager for UnitedHealthcare a core business of UnitedHealth Group, a Fortune 25 leading health care company serving more than 70 million people. UnitedHealthcare provides comprehensive commercial health benefits for individuals, small- and medium-sized businesses and large national and international companies. In partnership with AARP.

As Privacy Manager, Steve is accountable for collaborating with cross matrix business owners in the identification, characterization, implementation and monitoring of federal and state regulatory requirements into UnitedHealthcare's Service Operations. Steve also is responsible for all aspects of crisis management.

With over 10 years experience Steve has a comprehensive background in such areas as insurance operations, audit, business risk management, business continuity planning and vendor sourcing arrangements. He has an integrated business and IT background with experience in claims administration, call services, member/providing contracting and regulatory compliance. Likewise, his experience includes the development and adoption of an ITIL shared service model supporting 80 critical business applications. He also has significant experience with two M&A integrations (Pacificare and Definity Health acquisitions).

Steve holds a Masters of Science in Management from Rensselaer and a Bachelor of Science in Public Administration from Western Connecticut State University.

Steve was reelected to the West Hartford Town Council (2009-2011) and is a ranking member to their Administration Technology and Community Planning committees. He is also a Legislative Liaison to the Connecticut General Assembly.
