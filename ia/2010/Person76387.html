Gus Mumby’s Summary

Planet Webfoot is a social networking firm that was founded on the simple idea of providing the best quality social networking platform on the market and building strong relationships with our clients, all while maintaining reasonable costs. Established in 2005, Planet Webfoot started out in the discipline of e-commerce, web design and web development. Since that time, we have narrowed our focus and become one of the leading social networking firms in the social networking software industry. From gifts to diving to cooking, our wide range of experience has allowed us to become a leader in our field. Our powerful social networking platform allows our clients to establish an online community that can communicate and interact with their target audience.

We at Planet Webfoot understand that your social network is going be an online representation of your ideas, and we take that very seriously. We maintain a constant commitment to research and development to ensure we maintain our reputation as a cutting edge social networking firm. We bring experience, flexibility, and customer focus to the table which gives our firm a distinct advantage over our competitors. We make sure you are putting your best (web)foot forward.
