Sue Henry’s Summary

For the past 8 years I have been helping others learn how to network effectively and for increased profitability through workshops, trainings, seminars and individual coaching. I teach people how to identify their potential clients or customers, where to find them, how to get personal introductions, and then how to meet with them and build trust quickly.

In 2009, more than 1,400 people took my classes and learned how to effectively use social media in 15 minutes a day. This includes blogs, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter. I lay out the basic foundation for monetizing your social media presence with a simple to follow, step-by-step action guide that truly gets results.

2010 GOAL: Touch the lives of 1 million people with The Tiara
Experiment.

A GOOD REFERRAL FOR ME would be the person you know who manages or owns a speakers bureau, a radio or television talk show host, or the leader of a large organization that brings in motivational/inspirational speakers.

As an area director for BNI, I have been taught directly from Ivan Misner, the Networking Guru, as well as many other BNI leaders. My vision and innovation combine common information into uncommon applications which produces great results.

Sue Henry’s Specialties:

I have delivered workshops and trainings to hundreds of business people who want to improve their networking skills and increase referrals and profitability. My audiences have included people from 18 countries and 37 states.
