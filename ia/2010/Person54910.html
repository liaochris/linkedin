Derek Johnson’s Summary

As an open networker, I gladly accept invitations.

Research – Create/Apply – Analyze – Repeat
I’m a career business development professional continuously looking for creative ways to use emerging technologies to enhance traditional business practices. LinkedIn is a great example of technology elevating the way business can be done. Our ability to connect or the reach of any messaging we decide to put forth has never been greater. I’m always interested in meeting people with new ideas or fresh and innovative approaches to old ones. Your invitations are more than welcome, they are encouraged.

I have a voracious appetite for knowledge and tirelessly attempt to stretch myself professionally as well as socially. For many years I propelled though life unafraid to tackle challenges outside the scope of my expertise in attempt to find my true calling. Somewhere along the journey I realized one of my great talents is learning and passing that knowledge on to benefit others. Some call me a consultant, some an adviser, while the youngest have called me coach. I see myself as a great communicator.

My travels have taken me from the ocean floor as a certified rescue diver to conference room strategy sessions of Silicon Valley start-ups. I’ve acquired a passion for life on the mats of martial art dojos and passed that passion on across corporate sales floors. I’ve given council to CEO’s in private chambers and learned a thing or two from 9 year old girls on the softball field.

My recipe for success and happiness is simple:

RESEARCH your passions and interests in life. Build your knowledge base. Then take that knowledge and turn it into your own CREATE-tion or APPLY it to your life to make it better. Then step back and ANALYZE the results. The best case scenario you’ve made a positive impact on the world as a whole. The worst case scenario you’ve learned more about something that was already of interest to you. Finally, you REPEAT the process again.
