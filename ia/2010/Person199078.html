Muhammad Taimoor Sabir’s Summary

Taimoor is Chartered Management Accountant (UK & Pakistan) with 11 years of core Finance experience with 7 years in Telecommunication. He is a self motivated initiator with a strong desire to excel at highest level with a unique quality of problem solving with a sound grounding in all disciplines of the finance function and the experience of playing a pivotal role in 1 telco start-up projects in West Africa Region. With 11+ years of cross functional and multi-national Finance experience, He offers an unusually wide range of attributes, combining strong ethics and Finance best practice with a pragmatic, business focussed and results driven approach. He has worked in PTCL, Wateen Telecom and then advancing from Manager Finance role in PTCL to the role of Director Finance (Acting CFO/Team Lead Finance) for the start up project in Nigeria. Presently he is working as GM Treasury & Financial Strategy at PTCL.

Muhammad Taimoor Sabir’s Specialties:

Business planning, Budgeting, Accounting & Reporting, commercial evaluations, Treasury, Financial Strategy, Mergers and Acquisitions & corporate finance and development of finance function.
