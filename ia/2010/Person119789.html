Kirsten Texler’s Summary

Public Relations & Communications: with an insatiable desire to show fantastic things and experiences to people who may have always wondered "what's that like", being the people-connector has been more than a vocation. Even better is the chance to guide people who are just about to discover how good that "next, neat" opportunity is...for example, bringing media to my home ski resort to share my passion for the area. As a true PR pro, I have a clear understanding that the lifestyle is frequently more about the "un-glamorous" side of logistics and flexibility, in order to produce the spectacular "glamorous" results. Humour and grace are the key components in this business!
Writing: After years of hearing "You really should write about that", I have finally started to take that advice, and it has led to some amazing adventures beyond the ski hill.
If you'd like this humble ski instructor's (yep, oxymoron) opinions, check out my blog at skitownjournal.com/edge and vaildaily.com/featuredblog. A must see is recently re-launched skiresorts.com--they really get it right, and I'm happy to contribute to their growing project.
Quick bio: I'm a PSIA fully certified instructor with over 18 years of experience in Colorado and Australia. The professional figure skating aspect of my life has allowed me to travel extensively and see a unique view of the world. My resume also shows my escapades as a school teacher in Alaska and Colorado and as a corporate event organizer.

Kirsten Texler’s Specialties:

Contracted advertorial writer: The Apex Group; Peak Properties; Swany International; et al. Freelance writer for print and online publications.
