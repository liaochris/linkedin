Ian R Stuart, Financial Executive / CFO’s Summary

Experienced finance executive/strategic CFO with a broad financial services background. Progressed to the Price Waterhouse partner track and then acquired extensive divisional CFO expertise during a 13 year Travelers/Citigroup career that spanned several multi-billion dollar insurance and global banking business units (the last of which took me to Dallas). Most recently led a small NASDAQ healthcare insurance agency and services company – successfully concluded a turnaround with a 2009 sale transaction that positioned the company for further growth under new management.

Currently seeking a challenging CFO role/finance position with a mid-sized organization, or an autonomous division of a large corporation. Will relocate for the appropriate opportunity.

Creative, decisive, and operationally focused. Excel at applying financial acumen and partnering with business leaders to solve performance challenges – examples of results generated:
 Reversal of the small NASDAQ company’s declining revenue trend with 20% growth in 2008 – a key element of the platform for concluding the successful turnaround with a 2009 sale transaction.
 Realization of $50 million of annualized savings at one Citigroup division in 2002.
 Key member of a Travelers team that grew income 10-times, over 7-years, to $500 million in 1998.

My financial leadership philosophy and skills encompass the following principles:
a) “Run the trains run on time” - deliver timely/accurate reporting and credible communication of results;
b) “Provide a superior dashboard” - apply analytical acumen to proactively measure business performance and align operational metrics with the corporate plan/desired risk profile; and
c) “Be a trusted lieutenant” - establish the relationships needed to be an effective “financial conscience” (i.e. a business unit partner and banker).
