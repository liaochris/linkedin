President & CEO of REBEL Travel Corporation, Fred Bean has more than 15 years experience in the hospitality technology industry. Fred previously served as Vice President of Hotel Business Development for Cendant Travel Distribution Services with direct responsibility for the management of the merchant model hotel programs of Orbitz, Cheaptickets, Lodging.com, Neat Group, and WebHotels.

Before Cendant, Fred worked for Travelweb LLC. In this role, he helped Travelweb to become the largest hotel (participating) distribution company at a record pace with more than 15,000 hotels in less than two years.

Fred has also worked at Travelocity.com where he was Senior Manager for the Merchant Model Vacations product. Prior to Travelocity.com, he spent six years in the hospitality technology and online distribution industry with WorldRes.com, Sabre, and Hyatt Hotels, holding positions in reservation sales, customer service, call center management, operations, product management, systems development, and sales engineering.

Speaking experience includes: American Lodging Investment Summit, Goldman Sachs Annual Conference, Hotel Electronic Distribution Network Association, Hospitality Financial and Techology Professionals Regional Meeting, International Hotel Conference, The Lodging Conference, Florida Hotel & Motel Association Conference, HITEC, and more.
