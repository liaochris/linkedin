C. Kim Miles csc’s Summary

My introduction to the film business took place in 1988. At the behest of my best friend, I stumbled into a job as a grip at Peter Robinson Studios in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, where I grew up.

I was hooked, and stuck it out. In 1997, I was given the opportunity to direct photography on a series of commercials with my good friend, the late Gerry McKenna. Thanks to the chance that he took on me, I've never looked back.

I now live near Vancouver, BC, and work wherever in the world this business guides me. While long-form drama is what I work on the most, I also delve in music videos, commercials and documentaries.

I am a proud member of the Canadian Society of Cinematographers, and a member of the International Photographers' Guild, IATSE Local 669.
I'm always looking for opportunities to collaborate on projects of all sizes. The more meaningful the project is to someone, the more interested I become. I'm a storyteller at heart, and this is my calling.

C. Kim Miles csc’s Specialties:

I'm proud to be familiar with all contemporary motion picture capture formats from P2HD and RED to 35mm Anamorphic. I also have some aerial and underwater experience, and have worked around the world with crews from everywhere.
