John Savageau’s Summary

I've had a lot of great jobs over the years, and continue to enjoy being close to many cutting edge companies and the visionaries that make them work. While the majority of my experience is in data communications, 34 years in the business has given me tacit experiences and knowledge in many areas of both telecommunications and general business.

I consider myself an entrepreneurial, results-oriented executive with expertise in turnaround management, start-up management, business strategy, and venture capital procurement.

With extensive international business experience, focusing on China, Japan, and developing Asian markets, I have proven ability to create new market-leading business and sales strategies while maintaining a highly profitable and sustainable business model.

However without a doubt the most enjoyable positions I’ve held allowed me to lead teams developing and delivering cutting edge technology solutions, data center designs, and participating in basic infrastructure implementations in developing countries.

I'm currently working on ICT development projects in Ramallah (Palestine), Vietnam, modeling a new implementations of cloud computing and data center design that will bring sustainable technology down to the small business and startup - as well as government agencies.

Fluent in Japanese.

John Savageau’s Specialties:

Data center design and construction. Telecommunications network design. Telecom and network operations. Technology assessment, development, and integration.

Cloud computing, business plan development, and technology due-diligence.
