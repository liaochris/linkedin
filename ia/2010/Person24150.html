Bill White, PMP’s Summary

I’m a certified Project Management Professional with expertise in planning, development, and test of a wide array of hardware/software and software products.

Something I am really proud of is the overwhelming success of new products that were developed by various teams I’ve either managed or provided technical leadership throughout my career.

I’ve held positions in test systems engineering, software engineering, and software quality engineering for defense contractors who valued mature process, metrics, and quality assurance.

I find that wherever I work, I bring three key strengths to the table, the first is my ability to translate identified work it into a detailed schedule. Secondly, is my ability to manage, lead, and accomplish goals through others by utilizing plans and strategies. Lastly, is my ability to satisfy the customer by developing quality products that meet or exceed requirements and expectations.

At this time, my future plans are to leverage my skills and strengths as an experienced Project Manager in managing the development and test of new products and services.
