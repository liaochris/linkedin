Ken Ducey’s Summary

I work with an M&A team that assists small to medium sized businesses realize their company’s full potential value. Selling or exiting a business for a premium involves more than simply finding the right buyer. Fairfield Capital will not only find the right buyer, but we will also use our experience to:

• Properly prepare the company so that the process is smooth and efficient
• Minimize any risks the transaction will not close
• Make sure the process remains confidential
• Working to make sure the clients goals are realized
• Managing the process to coordinate all legal, tax, personnel and customer issues are coordinated.

Fairfield Capital Maximizes the Value of Small to Mid-sized Business seeking to Merge or sell through a strategic acquisition by another company. Our Process has been maximized to make certain we deliver the best possible exit strategy for our clients. Each client and situation is unique, and therefore each of our engagements involves a customized process to make sure our client receives a premium value. Our M & A team has successfully helped corporations exit through management buy outs, Generational Transitions, Selling or financing from Private Equity and Hedge Funds, and Roll-up strategies. We have references available upon request.
