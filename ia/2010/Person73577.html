Gadi Binness’s Summary

I bring over twenty years of management, sales, and business development experience in the insurance, relocation and Internet industries.

In July of 2003 I established Relocation Insurance Group, LLC, the first interactive and online insurance agency to provide insurance to customers who are in the relocation process whether using a full, self moving services, mobile storage or self storage. Our network includes thousands of domestic and international moving, mobile storage and self storage companies.

In January 2008 I founded GI Marketing Group, dedicated to building and empowering online social communities. We are working on some cool projects which are due to launch soon, draw millions of users worldwide and which will add much spice to the real time web communities.

    Relocation Insurance Group, LLC was established in July of 2003 and is headquartered in Upper Montclair, NJ. Our innovative online technology is available to customers whether they utilize a full service moving company, mobile storage company, a self storage facility or operate a rental truck. Relocation Insurance Group, LLC (www.RelocationInsurance.com) owns and operates a variety of websites and each is dedicated to providing customers with an affordable, comprehensive insurance program geared to their specific relocation needs. Customers have the ability to receive a quote or purchase insurance online, 24/7. All our insurance programs are underwritten by OneBeacon Insurance Group, an A rated insurance company. We offer quick and effective claims handling through the expertise of our in-house claims management department. The Relocation Insurance Group, LLC management team brings over 100 years of experience to the moving and insurance industries.
