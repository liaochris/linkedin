Mike Knight’s Summary

In my career as a salesman, sales trainer, and business development expert, I have found most salespeople have the drive to succeed but lack the proper training to reach their goals. Time and time again I heard the same sales issues repeating themselves and so I decided to do something about it and follow my true passion at the same time,

I’m passionate about providing good sales answers to tough sales questions, and consulting with Business Owners and Managers on how to maximize their processes and results. Our clients include Fortune 500 Companies, local mortgage firms, real estate companies, and individual sales managers & salespeople from every industry imaginable,

I assist Business Owners “cross-train” current employees so they can save money, time and resources that would have been involved in the procurement of a new employee. I assist people new to an industry or even new to the workforce with the proper skills and methods they need,

People are waking up to the realization that times HAVE changed and during a “BOOMING” economy, some weaknesses they have were previously hidden, however when business shrinks, EVERY little blemish they have is exposed and they have to act. I work with you to show you HOW to act and HOW to bring out your strengths,

I've "done it all" from pounding the pavement selling a wild and at the time unheard of product: Ostrich Meat to Developing Business in the Commercial Construction Industry to Public Speaking and Seminars in venues across the US on topics ranging from Investing and Networking and Sales Practices to Building Relationships with your clients,

I’ve used these same principles to thrust me from being “one of the many” to being sought after for Speaking at Seminars stretching across the US and even culminating with interviews by CNN and ABC News.
