Guy Wilkins’s Summary

Mid Level executive with 30 years of diversified experience in areas such as bank operations/systems, Internal Audit and Fraud Investigation, Sarbanes-Oxley compliance and just recently A/P Cost Recovery.

I worked for First City National Bank, N.A. for 20 years in Operations as a VP until the bank closed in 1993. I went on to work for Looney & Co., a litigation support firm, as their Operations Manager until 1995 when I resigned and decided to "re-tool", and work part-time in order to attend graduate school at the University of Houston. I received my MBA with a concentration in Accounting/Auditing in 2000 and accepted a position at Lennar Corporation in the Internal Audit Department. Soon after arriving at Lennar I completed my CIA and CFE certifications. Lennar also provided me with the opportunity to conduct fraud investigations and gain valuable experience as a Fraud Examiner. Later SOX compliance consumed most of my time at Lennar until I left in 2004 to do contract SOX consulting for Schlumberger through Protivity, and then SOX for Baker Hughes Int'l through Independent Professional Management, LLP. I joined Sirius Solutions, LLLP in 2006 and have worked on several SOX/Audit related projects to date. The latest project involved construction cost recovery audits that resulted in $6 million in recoveries for the client.
