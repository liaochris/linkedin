Mari Kono’s Summary

I am keenly interested in using the mediums of fine art and arts education to help bring positive social change that results in young people becoming self aware, participating in one's community and finding healthy and fun ways to build self esteem. Currently as an independent artist and art teacher at Da Vinci Design School, I am able to use all of my past experience working in the non-profit and commercial arts sectors doing work I love - creating art projects with my students, guiding their creative spirit and making art amongst colleagues I respect and enjoy spending time with.

Mari Kono’s Specialties:

Art creation, specializing in digital and paper-based collage work, high-end photography with experience in the commercial arts field, and over 10 years of experience as an arts educator, working with all ages and cultures of students, and using arts education as a way to bring about positive social change.

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    August 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Da Vinci Design School is a charter High School dedicated to providing a rigorous college preparatory curriculum integrated with real-world active learning. Our school is a place where students are encouraged to form safe and strong relationships with teachers, peers, parents, mentors, and the community. Through art projects, students learn to integrate knowledge in math, science, physics, English and Humanities to create aesthetically pleasing, well constructed work. As a teacher at Da Vinci, I strive to guide students to discern and appreciate the beauty of the constructed world around them, to persevere in their artistic efforts and use creativity to find eco-conscious solutions that will benefit their community.
