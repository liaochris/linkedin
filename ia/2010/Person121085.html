Lara Shackelford’s Summary

Lara Shackelford is an accomplished marketing executive with over sixteen years proven experience driving demand, product, segment, solution and corporate marketing. Ms. Shackelford maintains a constant focus on Return on Marketing Investment (ROMI) and building long term, sustainable growth. Her hallmark is her ability to identify and resolve the root cause of issues, tackling tough problems while constantly driving for results.

As Leader of Worldwide Demand Marketing at SPSS, Ms. Shackelford is responsible for directing senior marketing professionals, driving growth by creating global standards, streamlining core processes and improving targeting and messaging.

Prior to joining SPSS, Ms. Shackelford was responsible for BearingPoint’s Global Solutions, Product and Alliance (Oracle, SAP, MSFT, IBM) marketing organization. Ms. Shackelford spent two years at Microsoft leading ERP and CRM applications marketing for the U.S. Midmarket and seven years at Oracle in various leadership positions driving marketing, sales, and channels.

Ms. Shackelford began her career in sales and marketing for Levi Strauss and Company before changing to the hi-tech industry. Originally from Kansas City, Missouri, Ms. Shackelford earned her BA in Fashion Design from Stephens College.
