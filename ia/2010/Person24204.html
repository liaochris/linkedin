Billy MacDonald’s Summary

I've helped numerous businesses in varying industries increase their leads and sales through search engine marketing.

Through search engine optimization I help clients become more visible to prospective customers as well as those merely looking for information for branding purposes. I help educate clients on the importance of proper SEO as well as the understanding that it is an ongoing process. SEO must be maintained for long-term effectiveness.

Through paid search marketing I help clients focus in on their target market in the buying cycle to quickly capture and convert on leads. I ensure clients advertising dollars are spent more efficiently and advise on landing page design to increase conversion rates.

I started Siphon Marketing because I believe it is important that both the design of a website and the type of traffic we drive to it through search engine optimization and paid search marketing work together in attaining the ultimate goal for our clients, more sales.

Lastly, I rely on website analytics to get a true understanding of clients websites are performing and make the necessary changes to its search engine optimization and design so that their website is always improving on its core function, selling the business.
