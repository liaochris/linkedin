John Roberts’s Summary

25 years of experience developing and launching software, solid state lighting, optoelectronic and machine vision products in the computing, display, semiconductor and automotive industries.

Knowledgeable in parallel computing with GPU’s and multi-core systems, applied software development, image processing, machine vision, sensors and controls, automated data collection/archival/visualization systems, process control, color and illumination sciences, photometry/colorimetry, optics, thin-film interference coatings, thermal engineering, optoelectronics, LED technology, electronic display technology and glass processing.

Adept at building and leading teams to transform ideas into robust, profitable products and processes. Extensive history organizing new businesses and negotiating win-win supply agreements, licenses, and other ventures. Widely traveled and comfortable working in many cultures and settings.

Now working in applied, massively parallel programming in CUDA and OpenCL API's for NVIDIA GPU's. Primary areas of focus are accelerated imaging, image processing, and machine vision, along with SDK and library development.

For recent sample work, see CUDA VisionWorkbench at http://openvidia.sourceforge.net/
