Jonathan Levitt’s Summary

Jonathan has spent over 15 years helping a diverse list of brands understand their audience and leverage technology and data to build better customer experiences. He has worked with brands of all sizes including leading global players such as Bank of America, Verizon, Dell, Procter & Gamble, Ford, and Reebok. Data-driven to the core, he is a recognized authority in the spheres of web analytics, voice of customer, and conversion optimization.

A prolific and sought-after speaker, blogger, and writer, his unique slant on digital marketing has been featured in several industry publications including 1to1 Magazine, ClickZ, DM News, and MediaPost.

Market Development, Research, Branding & Identity, Strategic Planning, Grass Roots / WOM, DM/CRM, Interactive and Social Media Marketing, SEO, Acquisition, Advertising, Corporate Communications / PR, Marcom, Consumer Advocacy. Go to Market. Building brand and demand.
