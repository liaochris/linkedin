Shane Darrisaw’s Summary

Shane Darrisaw is a multicultural marketing and management consultant with the bulk of his career involved in the banking, mortgage and retail finance industry. Most recently, he has worked for Suntrust Banks as First Vice President of Emerging Markets where he was responsible for implementing a multicultural marketing program that focused on growing the banks business with ethnic minorities. Prior to that, he worked for WaMu, Wells Fargo and Wells Fargo Finanacial all in the capacity of emerging markets. Wherever he has worked, he has brought sound judgement, practical real world experience and proven results. His current plans are to grow his consulting business along with his partners and his client portfolio!
