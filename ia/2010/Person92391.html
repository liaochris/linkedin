JD GERSHBEIN’s Summary

J.D. GERSHBEIN is a prominent Internet marketer, industrial psychologist, wordsmith, cartoonist, keynote speaker, video producer, broadcaster, author, blogger, humorist, and the Founder & President of OWLISH COMMUNICATIONS. J.D. is highly regarded as a global LinkedIn expert, lending a distinctive voice to LinkedIn profiles and advancing the business goals of individuals and companies through customized and focused LinkedIn training. Revered for his unique take on personal branding, razor-sharp wit, biting sense of humor, and engaging storytelling style, J.D. is widely acknowledged as an accomplished communicator and one of the most original personalities in Social Media.

J.D. is an example of a successful adaptation to the changing landscape of business. As the Social Media sites came into vogue, he transitioned away from traditional marketing practices, and focused his energy exclusively on LinkedIn. LinkedIn provided J.D. with a communications and branding vehicle that inspired an entire system of online interaction, strategic messaging, and content management. As word of J.D.'s new-found business model spread, he soon found himself in high demand as a LinkedIn profile writer, LinkedIn trainer, and consultant. Subsequently, J.D. became well-known for his enlightening and entertaining LinkedIn presentations, and is now a highly sought-after keynote speaker on LinkedIn ethics and etiquette, strategic communication, business development, and personal branding on LinkedIn.

J.D. is a true pioneer in the delivery of LinkedIn education. Through the creation of The Owlish Institute, he has popularized the LinkedIn Town Hall Meeting where those seeking knowledge can ask questions and engage in a warm information exchange in an open forum. J.D. has also developed the definitive corporate workshop training program that will unify top-level executives, managers, and salespeople with a firm’s brand and underscore the communication theory in monetizing LinkedIn.
