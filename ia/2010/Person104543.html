Jonathan Schechter’s Summary

I graduated DigiPen Institute of Technology in December (one semester early) with a Bachelor of Science in Real-Time Interactive Simulation (Computer Science) along with a double minor in Mathematics and English. Each year in school, I’ve programmed a video game from start to finish (no middleware) with a team of 4 other programmers and 2 artists. In my senior year, I was the Producer, managing a team of 5 programmers and 2 artists. I also programmed the artificial intelligence, gameplay, and sound. As Producer, I was responsible for drafting weekly status reports, developing milestone presentations, and coordinating with the artists. My team was the first at DigiPen to develop a video game for the Apple iPhone. This application along with all of my games can be found by clicking the Games tab on my website (www.jonathanschechter.com).

I spent this past summer (summer ’09) in San Francisco working as a Production Intern with Shaba Games, a game development studio for Activision Blizzard Inc. I had the opportunity to work on an unannounced title for the Wii, Xbox 360, and PS3. Alongside my production tasks, I utilized my programming background by writing multiple tools to streamline production. Throughout the summer, I worked with producers, programmers, animators, character artists, audio engineers, and testers. I also learned how to communicate and work with the publisher and other development studios. Check out the description below for more details about my internship. I really enjoyed working with Shaba and I can’t wait to take the next step in my professional career.
