Jeff Sanders’s Summary

Jeff is an experienced entrepreneur and career coach who, in addition to founding Graduated And Clueless, also writes articles for Examiner.com and works as a Career Services Advisor at High-Tech Institute. He has been employed by many Fortune 500 companies including Apple, State Farm Insurance, and Citibank, and has extensive experience interviewing, hiring, and training many candidates. Jeff is also a professional member of the National Career Development Association, the Midwest Association of Colleges and Employers, has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Theater and Psychology, and has lived everywhere including Missouri, Boston, Prague, and Nashville.
