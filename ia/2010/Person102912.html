John P. Middleton, CFA, CAIA’s Summary

I joined Brighton Financial Planning in August 2008 and assumed ownership of the firm in January 2010. I focus on investment management for individual investors, investment advisory services for individuals and institutions and financial and estate planning.

Prior to Brighton, I spent 7 years with the Invesco Quantitative Strategies Group as a Senior Director & Client Portfolio Manager. While with IQS, I was responsible for administrative portfolio management, portfolio attribution & relationship management for more than 50 clients worldwide with assets over $2 billion. I also supported the marketing efforts of the Invesco Sales & Service team including finals presentations,serving as a strategy specialist and writing white papers. Finally, I was a frequent speaker at conferences, including to the U.S. S.E.C. on 130/30 & alternative strategies.
Prior to Invesco, I was as an Officer & SVP at Mutual of America Capital Management. While with MOACM, I was responsible for sales & marketing efforts to the institutional community & pension plan consultants.

Prior to Mutual of America, I held several positions with Raymond James & Associates, Inc.. These positions included Dir. of Marketing & Client Service for Awad Asset Management, RVP for Heritage Asset Management, Inc., Dir. of Corporate New Business Development for the RJA Investment Banking Group & Marketing Associate with the Partnership Investment Banking Group. My responsibilities included marketing tax credit & equipment leasing partnerships, marketing to venture capital & professional services firms, marketing mutual funds, marketing/client service for a $1 billion small cap manager & managing a small cap growth portfolio.

I graduated from Rutgers University, with a B.A. in 1988, & from the U. of Tampa, with an M.B.A in 1993. I possess the CFA & CAIA designations & am a member of the CFA Institute, the New York Society of Security Analysts, the CAIA Association & the Financial Planning Association.
