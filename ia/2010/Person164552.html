Will Pinnell’s Summary

Will Pinnell is the principal and owner of WilliamHarrison Consulting, a company he established in late 2007. WHC is a specialty firm consulting with companies deploying social media and mobile technology. He is also a principal at Sabre Holdings, Inc., a company that provides distribution and technology solutions for the travel industry. With over 9,000 employees globally in 60 countries, Sabre’s 2006 revenues totaled $2.8 billion. At Sabre, Mr. Pinnell oversees mobile marketing strategies and research and develops wireless business pricing models for three business units including Sabre Travel Network, Sabre Airline Solutions, and Travelocity, one of the world’s largest travel companies.

Prior to Sabre, Pinnell spent nearly 10 years at Handango, most recently as the Senior Director of Content & Strategic Alliances where he owned all global content business development activities. As an early founder of Handango and industry-recognized content expert, he served in various management capacities overseeing partner management, strategic marketing and planning, international franchise expansion, social media endeavors and the deployment of subscription services. At Handango, Pinnell oversaw nearly $100M worth of revenue generated directly from his ventures.

Mr. Pinnell has also worked for the STAR Sponsorship in Fort Worth & The National Children’s Scholarship Fund, a non-profit organization based in New York. He also spent time at PR Texas, now Interstar Strategic Marketing and Communications.

Pinnell holds double Bachelor of Science degrees in marketing and management from TCU and graduated cum laude from Columbia University’s Biosphere2 Ecological Studies Program in NY and Arizona. He is a frequent speaker at international tradeshows and conferences on wireless technology and has spoken at the Neeley School of Business Speaker Series sponsored by PriceWaterHouseCoopers. He currently teaches classes at TCU on technology innovation and social media.

Will Pinnell’s Specialties:

Contract negotiation with fixed and mobile web distribution agreements, deep wireless and digitial media industry expertise and contacts, worldwide business development and partner management capabilities, e-commerce and mobile application capabilities, subscription services, and social media consultation
