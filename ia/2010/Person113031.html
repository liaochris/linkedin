Kriss Bush, CPA’s Summary

Experienced Chief Financial Officer with extensive restructuring, cost containment, IPO and turn-around experience gained as CFO of two Canadian listed global technology companies (Rand Technologies and JumpTV), as the VP of Tax for Cable & Wireless (a UK parent global telecommunications company) and as Director of Corporate Finance and Tax with ITT Europe Corporation,based in Brussels, Belgium.

Strong global banking, financing, capital markets, wealth management and M&A knowledge gained as an executive with Scotia Bank (Scotia Capital and ScotiaMcLeod) in Toronto; Lehman Brothers Investment Bank in London, UK and American Express International Bank based in New York..

Corporate finance, accounting, branch operations, regulatory compliance, securities regulations, treasury management, tax, M&A, system Integration, government relations, investor relations, H.R., Legal, Marketing, Sales Management, internal controls design and corporate governance.

Industry experience in technology, internet start-up (IPTV) software development and sales, banking, wealth management, telecom and Big 4 public accounting consulting
