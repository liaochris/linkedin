Roger Snyder’s Summary

Since 1953, our exclusive legacy service, The Belles Celebrity Answering Service ™, has offered a prestigious line of personalized telephone answering services to our elite A-list made up of composers, producers, actors, news anchors, divas, singers, authors, artists, publishers, designers, sports commentators, reporters, photographers, talent agents, book agents, writers, band members, among many other legendary stars.

Our legacy combines The Belles Legacy R.S.V.P ™ and The Belles Legacy Wake Up Call ™ to help our clients manage their special events, offer them wake-up and reminder call service, and ensure that your calls are answered 24/7, wherever you might be. Belles Legacy adds a human touch to a world of electronic voices and push-buttons.

The Belles Business Answering Service ™ will be deployed soon to offer an array of inbound/outbound sales and customer service support services.
