Kartik Ramakrishnan’s Summary

I have 15+ years experience in product strategy, planning, market development & delivery of software products & services, in startups as well as established company environments. Currently, I am leading the Algo Transition Team responsible for integrating Microsoft's algorithmic search platform with Yahoo!'s Consumer Search Products (web, image & video).

As an entrepreneur, I founded QUIQ Inc., one of the earliest SaaS companies, growing it to 70+ employees and several million in revenue, raising over $18M in venture capital.

My business management experience includes driving all Go-To-Market-Initiatives for the Oracle middleware platform in Latin America, and leading the online services initiatives for a multi-billion dollar pharmaceutical product line at Pfizer Inc..

QUIQ, the startup I co-founded (we received over $18M in funding from Interwest Partners, Fleet, Altos and Discovery Ventures), was a leader in online collaborative customer service, bringing to market the first Q&A service on the web -- we powered the Ask Jeeves Answerpoint service. QUIQConnect, our flagship Q&A product was deployed at Business Objects, Citrix, Compaq/HP, National Instruments, and Sun Microsystems. QUIQ was eventually sold to Kanisa, Inc.

My academic credentials include an MBA from Stanford University, and Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Austin, and Arizona State.

Prior to QUIQ, I drove the web services efforts at Synopsys, Inc., and earlier worked in marketing and software development roles at Bull Information Systems.
