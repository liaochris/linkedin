Jaren Angerbauer’s Summary

I am currently providing email deliverability expertise as a private consultant. I have been in the email deliverability space for almost 8 years, and have worked with companies of all sizes, from start ups to Fortune 500 level. I currently work with clients to resolve challenges with their email deliverability stemming from issues such as a bad sending reputation, technical setup, knowledge of sender best practices, blacklist and ISP blocking issues, etc.

I not only provide visibility in these areas, but also long term solutions, including a road map for ongoing success, so that these types of challenges can be avoided in the future. I have established myself as an expert in this industry, and have either direct or indirect access to almost all Internet Service Providers and email receivers.

I have a deep level understanding of mass email infrastructures, including DNS setup, email authentication, and platforms such as StrongMail, MessageSystems, and Postfix. I also have a strong background in Linux systems and database management.

Most recently, I also teach a class on email technologies at Utah Valley University. For this course, I was responsible for creating the core syllabus, as well as lesson plans and other teaching materials.
