Michael Driehorst’s Summary

Mike Driehorst is a proven public relations professional with 16 years experience in strategic communications planning, public relations and other modes of marketing communications -- including being active in social media and digital marketing since 2005.

Mike is a co-founder of Social Media Breakfast-Toledo (February 2009) and often is asked to speak on PR and social media topics.

Based near Toledo, Ohio, he has blogged at MikesPoints.com since mid 2005, and has worked with clients in social media monitoring and analysis, blogger outreach, discussion board participation, online communities (including Facebook and Twitter) and other activities.

Early in his career, Mike worked for three Northwest Ohio newspapers as a reporter (for two dailies) and editor (a Toledo weekly).
