Kathleen Glass’s Summary

Proven track record of creating, executing, and analyzing online and offline lead-generation and retention campaigns, brand positioning, and media relations including sales support tools, web content, search engine marketing, newsletter content, white papers, promotional material, user-conferences, and tradeshows.
