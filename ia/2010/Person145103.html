Mike Bowles’s Summary

Experienced with quantitative finance and fully automated quantitative trading. Formal education in signal processing and business from top universities. Solid research and development experience as entrepreneur, manager and sole contributor. Excellent communications skills.

See my recent ACM presentation using data mining to improve performance of a traditional trading system. The video and audio are on fora.tv:

http://fora.tv/2009/05/13/Michael_Bowles_Neural_Nets_and_Rule-Based_Trading_Systems#post36919

The slides for that talk are on slideshare the link is further down my profile.

Mike Bowles’s Specialties:

Research and employ modern mathematical methods – data-mining (SVM, SVD , neural nets, Bayesian, etc), SSA, regime switching, Kalman filtering etc. Current programming skills – C++, C, Python, Unix, Fortran, Perl, MS Visual Studio, GNU, R, SAS, Numerical Recipes, Excel, Matlab. PhD thesis at MIT - numerical propagation of Bayesian posterior density function to extract signal from noise. Product and business definition. General management.
