Marcelino Coll d'Arnaude’s Summary

I was born in Barcelona in 1960. After studying Civil Engineering in the Universidad Metropolitana in Caracas (Venezuela), and getting an outstanding qualification in my final project, I started my career in 1988, working in Sidercomes as a Production Management Assistant, controlling and monitoring costs, orders and works.
In 1989 I left Sidercomes to join GTD Ingeniería de Sistemas y de Software, the company where I have developed the most of my career. I started as a Project Engineer, until 1992, when I was sent to the French Guiana as the Responsible for the Delegation of the Company in that country. There I combined the delegation management with my task as the Responsible for the Quality Assurance in the European Spaceport, taking part in 11 launching campaigns of the Ariane 4 Rocket.
Afterwards, in 1995, I became Responsible for the Delegation of the Company in Brazil. I finally came back to Spain in 1996 as a Project Manager in the same Company, developing projects such as the IT Project for CERN.
My Human Resources experience started in 1997, when I became the Human Resources Manager of GTD. I held this position for almost eight years. The same year I decided to enlarge my knowledge in the Human Resources area, getting in 1999 the Human Resources Master’s Degree by the Fundació Politècnica de Catalunya.
In 2005, and after sixteen years in the same Company, I decided to leave and I joined a Selection Consultancy, Euromanager S.L., where I simultaneously was a consultant, the responsible for Human Resources and the responsible for IT Systems. Two years later I left Euromanager S.L. and started working as an Executive Search Manager in Agrupa S.L., another Selection Consultancy.
One year ago, in 2009, I left Agrupa S.L., and together with Marta Olivé, my partner, we created Consultans SCP, our own Selection Consultancy business, which we are day by day developing with very successful results.
