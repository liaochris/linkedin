Lori Ann Robinson’s Summary

Lori Ann is a Los Angeles-based image,fashion and personal branding consultant, professional speaker, and four-time Emmy-nominated costume designer. She focuses on matching personality, lifestyle, and today’s clothing to her client’s career aspirations. She coaches her clients to success by developing their personal brand and offering the latest advice and guidance on professional development including, networking, handling business meetings and body language.

Lori Ann has appeared in local and national publications, including Men’s Health, Los Angeles Times and the Chicago Tribune, and contributes to numerous on-line publications. Lori Ann is the author of “Pearls of Wisdom: The Art of Successful Dressing,” a specialty booklet designed to help people avoid serious dressing and fashion mistakes.

She has appeared on radio and national television, most prominently on “The View,” “SoapTalk,” “Pure Oxygen” and “E!,”

Her clients include: Macy’s, Morgan Stanley, AIG SunAmerica, Paramount Pictures, First Private Bank and Trust and California Association of Physicians Group. Lori Ann is a member of the Association of Image Consultants International and the Fashion Group International and is a consultant for TC Fine Shapewear, a division of Cupid Intimates.
