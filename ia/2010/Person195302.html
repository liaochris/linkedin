Steve Milford’s Summary

My passion is to advocate for products and services in a consultative sales approach in face-to-face environments from a pay-it-forward perspective.

I excel in environments where I am able to consult with customers face-to-face, finding "needs" in 'Maslov's Hierarchy of Needs', and then helping customers find appropriate solutions with the products and services available. The solutions that I advocate are profitable and build referral & word of mouth marketing ultimately driving customer count.

My style!
* I embrace an aggressive-soft-sell push-marketing style.
* I work to find the best solution by asking many questions, and actively listen to the answers.
* When I hear "no", it really means, "Not right now with this solution; a creative alternative might be suitable."
* I believe that the more times that I trial close, the more opportunities for a "Yes!"
* I believe in building relationships and marketing partnerships with a 1-to-many delivery perspective at the grassroots level to build word-of-mouth marketing and referrals.
* I have a positive disposition, and engage everyone as if they are a new friend.
* I believe that computers are tools to make our jobs easier - not just to look at.
* I believe in evaluating my immediate environment by looking at the big picture, determining what the right thing to do is - even if it is unpopular, and "paying it forward".
* I believe in work - life balance.
* I believe in forgiveness - everyone makes a mistake now and then, and deserves a fresh 2nd chance!
