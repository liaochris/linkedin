Thomas Crawford’s Summary

Tom Crawford is co-Founder & Managing Partner at OTG Partners and is a trusted executive search and strategic consultant to early-to-mid stage technology companies and venture capital. Tom specializes in the digital media, healthcare, financial services, and software industries and has placed numerous senior-level executives, including CEO, CIO, CTO and VP-level candidates into both publically traded and privately held organizations. He accepts senior-level technology assignments with an emphasis on leadership roles in engineering, marketing, product management, and operations.

Tom brings more than 20 years of operational experience in consumer software sales and business development to OTG and was an early, central figure in the launch and growth of several venture backed technology startups across Silicon Valley. His efforts were instrumental in bringing multiple technology innovations to market, including industry’s first web-based and mobile email consumer products as well as ground-breaking anti-spam and anti-phishing SaaS solutions for the enterprise. Tom’s operational career included multiple funding events and successful exits, including three acquisitions in addition to XOOM.com, which went public after only 2 years and $10M raised. XOOM’s IPO in combination with a $250M secondary offering resulted in 30x returns to investors and led to the company's acquisition by General Electric/NBC [NASDAQ: NBCI].
