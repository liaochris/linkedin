Tomas Löwstedt’s Summary

I'm a Structural Consultant and I help people and organizations create dynamic urge to reach their goals.

We in Dynamic Urge start from the basis of creating what matters to you! By consulting us you explore what matters most to you, what patterns you have and how you can build a structure that supports your goals and dynamic urges.


The approach Dynamic Urge uses is based on Structural Dynamics, an area developed and founded by Robert Fritz.
I've been trained as a Structural Consultant to manage the fundamentals of this area.

Everything has a structure, be it a person, a group of persons, a relationship or an organization. This underlying structure determines our behaviour with its patterns and gives rise to patterns that are either oscillating – you create success but the that success is eventually neutralized; or advancing – you use your success to build momentum over time to build future success yet again.
