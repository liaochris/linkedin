Scott Gingold’s Summary

Scott Gingold gets positive results for clients, and in today's challenging economic climate, isn’t that what you need?

For over three decades Scott has helped clients turn red ink into black ink. He has helped them to achieve their short and long term goals and taught them how to successfully move forward after his assignment is completed.

Prior to starting his own companies, Scott worked as a senior level executive for several Fortune 100 firms where he was directly responsible for creating and implementing turnaround strategies, as well as positioning his employers for expansion, acquisition or merger.

Scott is a pioneer in the field of online market research. He launched powerfeedback.com at a time when the Internet that we know today was an Intranet. Powerfeedback continues to be a pioneers and leader in the realm of qualitative and quantitative market research.

Thanks to Scott’s leadership, Confidential Counselor is highly sought out for business advice, mentoring, strategic planning, and growth strategies.

Scott is a frequent public speaker for private corporate and public events as well as a noted business commentator.
