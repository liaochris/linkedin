Bruce Barnet’s Summary

Financial and business executive with experience as CFO, COO, Controller and Director of Internal Audit working in various private and publically held companies. Currently CFO of McCue Corporation, a privately held international company providing damage protection products including installation, maintenance, service and design services and solutions for retailers and other applications.

Prior to joining McCue, Bruce was the COO/CFO and original founder of Supply Strategies, a privately held strategic outsourcing and procurement company assisting mid market companies in outsourcing to China.

Bruce's prior experience has been as a financial and business executive for multinational public and private companies including positions held as CFO, Global Controller and Director of Internal Audit for diverse manufacturing and high tech businesses. Bruce holds a CPA, MBA and 20+ years of experience working with diverse, multinational companies in a financial leadership capacity driving efficiencies and change.

Bruce Barnet’s Specialties:

* Expertise in financial reporting & analysis, strategic planning, forecasting, budgeting, working capital management, due diligence, acquisitions and divestitures, enhancing business practices and improving internal controls.
* Strong change agent for driving efficiencies and cost savings.
* Strong international experience including hands on experience and knowledge working in China.
