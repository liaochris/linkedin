Debbie Gilliam’s Summary

Eco-Friendly Home Improvement and Staging Expert
Reuse, Repurpose, Repair, Restore, Reinvent are what we first look to do with our staging a home re-design projects. Showing you how to save money is priority. Whether you're looking to sell, or just looking for a home make-over, I provide a $65 design consultation with documented options that you can incorporate yourself, or hire us to complete. Plans can be customized to fit any budget. Through custom paint techniques, updating existing pieces, restoring unique pieces from antique sales, auctions, and discount stores, etc, I can show you how you can have a high end designer look, without the high end price tag. I will also provide a pre-inspection inspection with a checklist, to alert you to any problems that a buyers inspector will find, resulting in reduced offers. I also offer eco-friendly tips on home weatherization, and energy saving solutions that can save you up to 40% on energy bills, and give you another advantage when selling.
I have more than 21 years of experience and a lifetime of passion for home improvement and design projects, and a desire to give back to those who wish to learn.
My experience as Full Service Mortgage Originator, with access to more than 80 lending institutions, including FHA, VA, and Reverse Mortgage products, 15 years in the credit industry, as well as my experience as a Real Estate Investor, relationships with local Realtors, and 8 hours spent learning side by side with a home inspector enables me to offer advice to my clients about the entire real estate sale and purchase process. If needed, I can provide a full overview of my clients credit situation to provide options, beyond just the mortgage. If you are looking for personalized service from a professional who cares, and is available when needed, give me a call. My services go far beyond getting your home ready for market, and I will be the last contact that you need when it comes to your largest asset.

Room Design, home staging, custom paint techniques, installing tile, laminate flooring, molding, carpeting,paneling, lighting/plumbing fixtures, patios, landscaping, light re-upholstering, furniture restoration, and more.
Credit Analysis, Mortgage advice and pre-inspection inspection are added values that you will find no where else.
