Perry Newman, CPC CSMS’s Summary

Want to increase your apply to interview rate? Then you found the perfect person to turn to for advice.

Does your resume resonate why you should be considered the front runner for a job? If not, we can collaborate on a resume that leaves no doubt why you should be interviewed and hired!

Want a coach to support, guide and educate you on the changing dynamics of a job search. Then turn to the author of “Job Hunting in the 20th Century – The New Reality.”

As an Executive Resume Writer and Career Coach I am passionate about all things related to the job search and personal career management process; from understanding what a resume is and how it is perceived, to creating bold new resume formats that standout in a field of equally qualified candidates, identifying professional values and artfully integrating them into a marketing portfolio, and most of all advocating the importance of networking with live people rather than blindly sending out resumes that are rarely viewed by human eyes. These are all topics I cover in my professional practice and webinars

With searches now averaging 26 weeks, I can help cut that down 5%-25%, so call or connect with me and follow my blogs, webinars & group discussions to learn how you can get hired in today’s economy.
