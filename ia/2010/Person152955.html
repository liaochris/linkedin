Neil Pradhan’s Summary

I am a second year Master's student in the Human Computer Interaction Program (HCI) at Georgia Institute of Technology.

This program has helped me to develop my skills of gathering the user needs and to design, implement & evaluate products based on these user needs. I successfully used these techniques in my project as a summer intern in the R&D Department at Walt Disney Imagineering. Here I worked on the project which was in the area of collaborative computing. The project aimed to increase the communication amongst different people working in the R&D department.

I am interested in the area of interaction design and to create interactive products to support people in their everyday and working lives. Hence in this semester I have taken a Project Studio under Prof. Janet Murray. Under her guidance I am working on the Project: Virtual Couch. It is helping me learn more about communicating design ideas using prototypes, wire frames and interactive mock ups.
