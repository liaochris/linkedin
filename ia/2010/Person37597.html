Christopher Curtis’s Summary

A strategic analysis & business operations professional who is a self-starter, thrives in a rapidly changing landscape, tolerates ambiguity, and demonstrates problem-solving leadership.

After being an early employee at eBay who got to touch everything from Product Marketing to Strategic Analysis to International expansion & management, I have moved on to numerous angel investor/strategic consulting relationships with a mix of both internet and 'bricks & mortar" businesses in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Most recently, I co-founded a new vertical ad network called VERTual Media. We are focusing our efforts on helping mid-tier niche website publishers with quality content optimize their advertising opportunities. We are actively recruiting publishers in the areas of Sports, Gaming, Technology and Entertainment/Lifestyle, or who otherwise have audiences that are predominantly male and skew towards the 18-34 year old age demographic. We also actively engage with ad agencies and advertisers who are looking to reach this audience in its passion points, as VERTual Media offers a cost effective and highly targeted channel for this particular demographic.
