Tim Mitchell’s Summary

I am an experienced and broadly skilled web and mobile product professional, with a focus on digital media. I have 12 years of experience at fast-paced and cutting-edge technology companies. I have held high level positions in large and small organizations, and I’ve managed several large cross-functional teams. I possess a high level of creativity backed by strong business acumen, effective management skills, and a solid quantitative and analytical ability.

Career Highlights
■ Principal/Consultant: For the past two years, I struck out on my own and operated my own consulting business and worked with great clients on compelling products. I have helped both large and small companies to launch new products, define product and marketing strategy, and build teams.

■ @ IODA: Significantly contributed to the rapid growth and success of IODA, holding 2 executive roles, including a dual executive role with a wide range of management, strategic, and P&L responsibilities. Helped grow the company from 9 employees to over 70, and helped grow revenue to a projected $14M for 2008. In 2009, the company successfully sold to Sony Music. In 2005, the company was voted one of Fortune Magazines’ “Top 25 Breakout Companies”

■ @ CNET: Re-launched the MP3.com music destination Director level position with General Management level responsibilities. Launched site from ground zero in 5 months and grew site to 2.9 million unique users a month and 100,000 registered community members.

■ Well regarded as industry expert in convergence of music, media and technology. Quoted in publications such as Red Herring, Billboard, Digital Music News, eCommerce Times, Wired.com, Villagevoice.com, and others. Continually sought for speaking engagements at various conferences.
