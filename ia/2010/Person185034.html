Michele McGraw’s Summary

For the last 13+ years, I have been helping women find support online for their hobbies and lifestyles by sharing my experience and passions. Here are some of the activities I’ve worked on:
• Served as a Director of Internet Services and board member of Mothers & More providing online support for SAHMs through their national website, forum and e-mail support groups. (http://www.mothersandmore.org)
• Overseeing the Healthy Chicks forum for The DigiChick’s online forum for digital scrapbookers interested in maintaining a healthy lifestyle (http://www.thedigichick.com)
• Currently serving as an Ambassador for the Mom Bloggers Club to provide support and information for Mom Bloggers (http://www.mombloggersclub.com)
• Working as Community Manager and Director of Social Media for a new start-up company, FormerFatChick.com to help others use the Internet for support in their healthy lifestyles
• Currently serving as a founding board member on the Mom It Forward board of directors and working to start a local chapter in the Washington D.C. area. (http://momitforward.com)
• Launching ScrapsofMyGeekLife.com to offer daily commentary, advice, reviews and shared experiences of how my real life and the digital domain connect
• Actively participating in the women bloggers community online through Twitter and Facebook
• Digital Soul Hour Tech TV Show Host on MomTV.com
• Participated in the Max Mom and EA Sports Active More Workouts campaigns
