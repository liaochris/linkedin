Jamie Brodersen’s Summary

During my career as an independent contractor and a full-time employee, I've been fortunate to gain a broad experience base. This base transcends across many platforms, including corporate branding, multimedia, visual & audio talent, character animation, printed goods, garments, video production and more. My experience has gained me valuable knowledge in many software programs, processes and environments. It has also allowed me the ability to become a better leader and manager.
