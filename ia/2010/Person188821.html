Sherri Haymond’s Summary

I am a Law and Finance Professional with significant experience as an Investment Banker, Corporate Finance/Securities Lawyer and Equity Analyst. I am also the Founder of What's Your Twenty, Inc. (@WY20), a walkie-talkie rental business.

As a Corporate Finance Associate at Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP, I gained hands-on transactional experience in a fast-paced and extremely demanding work environment. I focused my practice primarily on securities deals (public and private – debt, equity and everything in between), and also have experience working on mergers & acquisitions and restructuring transactions. Then, as Deputy General Counsel of MortgageIT Holdings, Inc. (a formerly NYSE-listed mortgage REIT), I applied my legal skills in a corporate environment. I advised on corporate governance matters and created and implemented Sarbanes-Oxley compliant policies and procedured; drafted/commented on earnings releases, earnings call scripts, investor presentations, and Board materials; managed the execution of securitization transactions; drafted/commented on commercial and employment contracts, including trading agreements and confirmations; and advised on employee benefits plans. Next, at Banc of America Securities LLC, I transferred my corporate finance skill set into the structured finance environment, building a mortgage REIT practice from scratch (generating over $3M in fees in approximately two years), while running my clients’ transactions and consulting on complex deal structures for the Bank. Later, at Breeden Capital Management LLC, I honed my analytical skills, developed my modeling skills and built upon my due diligence skills as I sourced and pitched undervalued stocks for inclusion in our long-only portfolio. Most recently, I founded a walkie-talkie rental company, What’s Your Twenty, Inc., branding the company and developing a client base that generates a significant revenue stream in under six months.
