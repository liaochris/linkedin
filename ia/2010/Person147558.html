M. SEN-SEI’s Summary

As an Event Marketing Manager, I produce event experiences that are experiential, original, dynamic, and impressive. I am sought by marketing directors, promotions managers, and public relations managers to conceptualize strategies for events that effectively add value to brand equity. I have expert-level experience in project management, budget management, team development and management, sponsorship sales, as well as stage and sound design. Events may be concerts, fashion shows, black-tie galas, golf tournaments, festivals, or multi-city road show tours. I have produced successful sell-out events that range from 500 to over 1000 attendees.

How do I do it?
It all starts with me and my team. I have worked with existing event production crews and I'm experienced with recruiting top-talent crew members. My leadership motivates my team to produce flawless "Wow!" events. Leadership and teamwork.

Over the course of my twelve year career, I have earned the honor of a well-respected reputation in the entertainment industry both as an event marketing manager and as an electronic music recording artist and musician. I leverage my contacts across a broad spectrum of industries including hotel/hospitality, film and television, music, nightlife, video game entertainment, software, Internet technology, and automotive as both inspiration and resources for event production.

My entrepreneurial spirit, results-driven attitude along with my commitment to integrity, accountability and responsibility has earned me successful collaborations with recognized brands such as Playboy Enterprises, VOOM HD (Rainbow-Media), Heineken-USA, CBS Radio, Apple Computer, GRAMMY, and Hartle Media.

My event marketing philosophy is “Continuous positive engagement leads to the creation of customer evangelists.” Customer evangelists are the most effective brand ambassadors. I produce incredible events to honor customers for their patronage and loyalty.
