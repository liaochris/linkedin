christopher dusseldorp’s Summary

Wherever possible, always searching for something new... Writing for me is fired by a driven spontaneous creative will, enhanced by a vision and guided by a set of intelligently structured parameters to achieve powerful end results. Writing professionally since 2001, has provided me with the experience of producing a varied collection of specifically targeted writing requirements. A writers’ writer, through the implementation of imagination, reading and experience. For business copywriting, I'm capable of stylising a writer’s voice and message for various company purposes.

Quick written, long and short copy, conceptual advertisements, memorable tag lines, edm, search engined optimized web copy and content and media release angles are well within my writing capabilities. A perfectionist at heart, I know the difference between something fresh and exciting and something dreary and dross. I’m keen as ever to jump in and tackle any new writing requirement, concept, brand, field of knowledge or marketing media without delay. I aim to please every time and on time.

My work on Tony Robbins’ www.empowernet.com.au, allowed me to practice a fast paced, efficient and high turnover copywriting style that retains creative marketing strategy, integrity and economy and consistently achieved outstanding results.
