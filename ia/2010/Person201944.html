Amanda Collins’s Summary

Do you ever wake up one day and say, "Wow, I'm doing what I should have always been doing—and I'm good at it!"? Well, that's what happened to me recently when I realized that I have been writing since I was 10 years old, first in volumes and volumes of journals and then with short stories, poetry, and finally into my true love: marketing.

I never really fashioned myself a writer until recently—and it was something that took time to embrace. Now, though, I am keenly aware that I am a writer, and I find myself rereading old stuff and realizing how good it is. I'm currently the editor of Jetset Magazine, a national publication, and I have a few bylines under my belt. I'm also working on a book to be completed in early 2011.

I find satisfaction in putting my talents to work on behalf of my clients, creating the strategy behind marketing communications and then implementing the writing on blogs, articles, newsletters, press releases, and bios, as well as résumés. My voice is fun, engaging, and real because I know that words have a lot of power, but only so much time to flex their muscles.
