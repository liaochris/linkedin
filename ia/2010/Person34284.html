Catherine Flowers | Private Investigator’s Summary

Cat’s Eye Private Investigations is a fully-insured professional private investigations agency licensed in North Carolina by the North Carolina Private Protective Services board and licensed in South Carolina by the South Carolina Law Enforcement Division. Since our company's inception more than 10 years ago, our goal has been to provide our clients with the highest standard of ethical, confidential and professional investigative and surveillance services.

With investigators in both North Carolina and South Carolina, we work to bring our clients the best possible services and products at reasonable prices. Our investigators have a wide variety of experience to suit our clients' needs; and our support staff works to assist our clients with any questions or concerns that they might have about their investigation.

At Cat's Eye Private Investigations, we hold ourselves to a higher standard of client service, including (but certainly not limited to): scrupulous investigation, meticulous documentation, and proficient reporting. We use efficient and well-tested intelligence techniques, including state-of-the-art technology, which allows us to offer each of our clients the high quality service they seek.
