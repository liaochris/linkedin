Robin Barker’s Summary

Just back from organising this year's Annual Convention for the Tourism Management Institute (TMI) - so tired but enthralled!
Now back to planning the South West tourism awards, developing relationships with sponsors, building up some new product ideas... also working with the Met Office, Tourism South East and on the Tourism Olympic Marketing Plan for England and marketing strategy for the Brecon Beacons.
Really enjoyed chairing the panel at this year's Tourism Information conference - what a positive attitude all round!
The Services for Tourism website, remains nearly ready for launch but still has a few bits in Latin (a small and diminishing market, despite what my teachers told me!)
Commercial expertise, marketing, experience of bridging the public/private sector divide and general 'nouse' seem to be much in demand!

Robin Barker’s Specialties:
