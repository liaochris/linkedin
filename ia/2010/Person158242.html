André Rodrigues’s Summary

I am a professional in the Online and Print creative space. Currently learning the intricacies of the motion world. A dynamic and always under reinvention to stay up to date with the latest trends. My expertise covers Illustrations, Digital Matte Painting, Magazine Redesign, Brand Identity, Print as well as Web 2.0.

I am currently the Art Editor for BBC Top Gear Magazine, helming the redesign and bringing out one great issue after an other. In charge of the conceptualisation and ideation right up to the execution of shoots, that have now escalated to become the hallmark of our magazine. Top Gear India has currently some of the best photograpy and styling among all the rest in the crowded automotive print segment.

I also write and illustrate under the guise of Ousiadroid.com. Have worked for companies like Web 18, Forbes Arabia, PC Magazine, The official PlayStation Magazine and Mans World in the past. I have also has designed the first phases of Femina.in, Mumtazz.com, 7Days.ae and done the branding for www.tech2.com, cricketnext.com, DXB7, mumtazz and indiwo.com among many others.
