Paul Garner’s Summary

Industries include bedding, metal stamping, textile manufacturing and textile outlet store retail.

System applications include: Lawson, Hyperion, Essbase, Metacube, Manbase, SAP and AS400 based Software 2000.

* Generating budgets; monitoring and providing explanations and counter measures of variances.
* Turning around departments or functions lagging in managerial expectations.
