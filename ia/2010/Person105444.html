Joseph Martinez’s Summary

Public relations professional with experience in public relations agencies, tourism and financial services. In agency setting, managed PR departments and served clients in banking, retail, consumer products, technology, pharmaceuticals, trade associations and government. As in-house counselor, experienced in public affairs, travel/tourism, financial services and internal communications. Excellent writing skills, in Spanish and English. Active in professional associations and accredited by the Public Relations Society of America.

Joseph Martinez’s Specialties:

Marketing public relations, internal communication, special events, corporate communication, editorial services.
