Riccardo Lattanzi’s Summary

Riccardo Lattanzi is a biomedical engineer, who is currently working on improving the diagnostic power of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). His research focus is the optimization of the design of radiofrequency (RF) coils for MRI applications. Previously, he had worked in the field of computer assisted orthopedic surgery and was involved in the development of a software for pre-operative planning of total hip replacement surgery.

Riccardo also collaborates as a freelance journalist with the scientific editorial board of the Italian newspaper “La Stampa” and writes for the Italian edition of the magazine “Wired”.

Riccardo Lattanzi’s Specialties:

MRI, Parallel MRI, Parallel Transmission, Electromagnetic Field Simulation, Medical Imaging, Computer Assisted Orthopedic Surgery
