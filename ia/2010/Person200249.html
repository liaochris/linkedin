Tracy Benson’s Summary

Interactive & Digital Marketing capability leader for Best Buy Marketing including consumer brand interactive marketing - digital marketing, emerging technologies, social marketing, mobile marketing, video advertising, and a host of other emerging platforms in the digital space.

Accomplished results and action-oriented senior marketing executive with 10+ years driving digital and internet channel growth in consumer, retail (big box and specialty), entrepreneurial, and agency environments. Multi-channel; Digital; Interactive experience driving profitable growth for many retail and cpg clients through innovative, customer-focused ideas and insights that produce successful results when measured against business objectives. Thorough working knowledge of interactive marketing strategy, brand positioning & management, promotions, consumer research and insights development, online and offline media planning, user experience, and creative/interactive development. Strategic innovator and visionary.

Areas of category subject matter expertise include apparel, food, packaged goods, home, consumer electronics, and consumer goods. Digital expertise includes web development, online marketing & advertising, emerging media, social computing, conversational and relationship marketing, and integrated multi-platform programs. Notre Dame Executive M.B.A. graduate.

Vertical strengths primarily include Retail (Fashion, Apparel, Home Goods, Soft Goods, Consumer Electronics, Mass Retailing to Specialized Retailing), Consumer Goods (both Direct and Brand including Food & Bev, Cosmetics), and Telecommunications (B2C, B2B).
