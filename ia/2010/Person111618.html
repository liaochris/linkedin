Kate Meyers’s Summary

CDIA+ certified, having the expertise and competence to take an entity from paper to digital.

Executive Administrator for Fortune 500 corporations.

Certified Court Reporter in the State of Maine. Excellence in freelance depositions in civil litigation and Workers’ Compensation cases.

Founder of Brown & Meyers in 1994. Brown & Meyers provides Court Reporting services in the State of Maine, Medical, Legal, Insurance, Government and Business transcription services nationwide. Providing full document scanning services, document management/e-mail archival solutions and paper document storage in our secure, state-of-the-art facility in Westbrook, ME.
At Brown & Meyers, I am primarily responsible for technology operations and strategic direction of the company.

Brown & Meyers is certified as a Women’s Business Enterprise (WBE).

I'm on the WBE certification committee in Boston, MA.

    Certification , CDIA+

    The CompTIA Certified Document Imaging Architect (CDIA+) certification identifies top imaging industry professionals.

    The CompTIA CDIA+ exam is an international, vendor-neutral certification that tests expertise in the technologies and best practices used to plan, design, and specify a document imaging management system.

    The industry recognizes CompTIA CDIA+ certification as a consistent, objective way to evaluate imaging industry professionals. Several companies that provide document imaging solutions value and support the CompTIA CDIA+ certification, including Canon, Ricoh, Konica and Kyocera.
