Doreen DeJesus’s Summary

After 23 years in Corporate America, Doreen retired in early 2009. Through out her stint in Corporate America, she had the pleasure of being employed in the hospitality, real estate, accounting and financial industries.

Doreen spent 18 years of her corporate career in the financial industry servicing different departments and companies. She has worked in legal and compliance, mergers and acquisitions as well as collections. Doreen considers herself very fortunate to have partnered with CEO’s and top management of fortune 500 companies all of whom allowed her to continue to expand her skills and grow.

Doreen's extensive training includes office management, event coordination, social media implementation and management, author support, data research, marketing, project management, multimedia presentations and much more. In order to provide her clients a diverse skill set, she has completed certification for the following; Social Marketing Specialist (2009), Internet Marketing Specialist (2009), Master Shopping Cart Specialist (2009), Professional Virtual Authors Assistant (2008), Certified Travel Planner (2008), Certified Real Estate Assistant (2007), Graduate Virtual Assistant (2004), Certified Real Estate Support Specialist (2004) and IVAA Ethics Checked (2004).

Doreen is proud to be an active member and volunteer for many online and local organizations. Some of these organizations include:

* Greater Pocono Chamber of Commerce - Member since 2009
* International Association of Online Business Managers - Member since 2009
