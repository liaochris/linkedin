Daniele Andriolo’s Summary

Experienced and high skilled professional with a background of 15 yrs in HR and Organization management and 16 yrs in Real Estate and Facilities management.
Always worked in International environments, effectively managing diversity and successfully balancing corporate and local needs. Versatile, innovative, creative, feels comfortable either in designing sophisticated processes, or in managing complex and high-tech building sites.
Proven successes in starting-up projects, leading and motivating teams, raising customer service to the highest level, interacting positively with stakeholders, driving changes, enhancing value, communicating in creative and innovative ways, controlling and reducing costs and risks, managing vendors and service providers, negotiating effectively, designing and following road-maps, delivering results in complex and challenging environments, always minding security, safety, and environmental sustainability.
