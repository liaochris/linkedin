Matthew Blaylock’s Summary

I have a passion for supporting others in achieving their goals! Whether it be for a candidate or client, I am committed to utilizing my expertise and trusted network of professionals to facilitate your success.

I have placed well over 500 professionals over the past 11 years and work primarily off of referrals of those I have placed and worked with in the past.

I differentiate by relationship building rather than creating JUST another staffing/sales organization climate. ALL of my clients have utilized my services on multiple occasions and most still to this day.

Respecting others through keeping promises, commitments and consistency is the foundation for building relationships. Building a network of good relationships is the key for a successful career, business and life.

My focus is contract staffing services in ERP, CRM, BI, Supply Chain, Web, SaaS, LAMP and related technologies - primary focus in Oracle and J2EE related technologies. Services offered Nationally. I place candidates with direct clients and channel partners/implementers and provide contract, contract-to-hire and permanent staffing.
