Fiona Bevan’s Summary

My background is in management accounting. This means my focus is on providing business people with current financial information about their business that will help them make the right decisions for them. I also help with planning for the future so they are more likely to reach their goals.

I have worked with, and for, a wide range of different businesses in an extensive range of business areas.

I am a Chartered Management Accountant (CIMA) and have 20 years experience working in business. This means you can rest assured that YOUR business is in safe hands.
