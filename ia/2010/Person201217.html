Terry Brown’s Summary

Terry Brown and his staff at Armor Bearer Protective Services are a collection of highly trained Executive Protection Specialists (Professional Bodyguards) & Investigative Consultants with strong backgrounds as former law enforcement officers, military operatives with special ops training, and private sector firms. They have undertaken assignments nationwide and in other countries throughout the world.
