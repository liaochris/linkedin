Brijesh Kumar’s Summary

Brijesh is a leading Semantic Web & XML Workflow Solutions expert who founded Digital Media Initiatives (formerly called XML Frontier) in November 2000, a software development and digital content management firm with a focus on digital publishing and learning. Web:<http://www.DigitalMediaInitiatives.com>

Launched an innovative online automated production workflow for producing DocBook v5.0 XML and EPUB format eBooks from XHTML source fileset. The platform is called ePubNow! <http://www.ePubNow.com>.

Digital Media Reserve is yet another online product which is encompassing an entire publishing ecology, including authoring, publishing, online reviews, distribution under digital rights management (DRM), and retail, completely compliant to the leading international standards.

Brijesh is also involved with education leadership at primary and secondary level education.

Brijesh defines his goal to bring books and education to all children of the world through leveraging technology.
