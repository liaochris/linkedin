I was awarded a distinguished honor of "Volunteer of the Month" by the agency who recognizes people who have made a outstanding contribution to the community.

    Isa made his transition in April of 2007 and Yolanda now is retired.

    I continue to use "Releasing" in my courses and offer private "Releasing" sessions.
