Laura Bennett’s Summary

I am one of two co-founders of Embrace Pet Insurance. We are known for our outstanding customer experience, our flexible pet insurance policy that provides coverage that pet parents expect, and the trust we have built with our Embraced pet parents.

I am the first pet insurance actuary in the US (one of two) and the Chairman of the Board of the North American Pet Health Insurance Association, that strives to educate and build confidence in the US pet insurance industry.
