Doug Boebinger, PMP [LION][Project Mgmt Guru]’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY:

* 20+ years of project manager with profit / loss responsibilities
* Founder and President of Integrated Process Developers, Inc., a project management and process re-engineering consulting and corporate training company
* December, 1994 to Present: A Project Management Institute (PMI) certified project management professional (PMP)
* June, 2001 to Present: A Project Management Institute (PMI) Registered Education Provider (REP)
* MCTS: Managing Projects with Microsoft Office Project 2007 certification from Microsoft
* Developed the MAPD Strategy of process re-engineering / project management methodology
* Developed and facilitated multiple project management training / workshop programs
* Lead project management/process development consultant to Ford Motor Company - Powertrain for 12 years. Recipient of Ford's Customer-Driven Quality Award in 1994 & 1995 for Project Management
* Host of the radio show "It's Your Business" which featured experts in the various areas of business management
* Seven years of experience in the construction industry as a assistant project manager, project manager and operations control manager
* Thirteen years of experience as a consultant and corporate trainer in automotive, information technologies and banking industries
* Undergraduate and Graduate degrees in Construction Engineering and Management from Purdue University
