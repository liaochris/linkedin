Eric Willems’s Summary

Profile Eric Willems (1953)

Eric is an experienced, results-oriented manager with around 35 years experience in the world of ICT. Over the last few decades Eric has developed the necessary skills for management consultancy, concentrating on roles where change is the rule rather than the exception.

Eric sees things through to the end and has the will and energy to overcome obstacles. He likes challenges that call upon his strength of imagination and his daring. Eric has an entrepreneurial outlook which makes him able to see new opportunities. As a generalist he can view projects from a business perspective. He feels most at home in a dynamic environment. His ability to get the job done means that he achieves success.

Working in the roles of project- and interim manager, Eric has worked on projects for various clients building up considerable experience and wide-ranging knowledge. He has also developed his own style which he describes as a participative and coaching leadership-style. Eric carries a clear vision and believes in continuous learning as a means of achieving personal growth.

Eric is client-oriented, has a positive attitude, sees the good in people and has clear personal values. Freedom, flexibility, change, personal growth and vitality are important to him.

His motto is ‘when you say you’re going to do something, you do it.’
