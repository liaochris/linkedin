The Affiliate Way Network’s Summary

Becoming an affiliate marketer is one of the easiest business propositions because you don't need a product or a website, which is why it is gaining in popularity. Businesses want to decrease their marketing expenses by having as many affiliate marketers promoting their products. For this reason, businesses make it very easy to sign up for an affiliate program. Find a program, sign up for free, and just like that you're an affiliate.

I struggled for over 3 years to make it in the industry of Affiliate Marketing and MLM/Network Marketing. I've gone from chasing family and friends, to cold calling, passing fliers out at the mall, house meeting and even buying leads.

Then I realized I was going about it the wrong way. I needed to be the hunted and not the hunter. I changed my entire attitude and my business and income started to grow rapidly. I created The Affiliate Way Network to train others because I understood its about relationships not just about the money. The more people I help, the money will follow and besides, I love what I do and I do what I love.
