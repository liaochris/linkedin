Edward Palmer’s Summary

During my time of practical experience, I have designed hardware for FPGAs, firmware for microprocessors, and other system-on-a-chip (SoC) devices for research projects and medical device products. I have also developed software and hardware tests for FDA approval of medical devices.

In the next few years, I would like to expand my knowledge of electrical engineering, especially in the medical device industry. My longer-term goals are to manage engineering projects on a corporate level and to develop technologies to be patented.

I have also served as an Expert Witness in two separate product liability cases. This role required me to perform research on product failures and engineering design standards, extract information pertinent to the cases from hundreds of pages of documents, and perform and/or observe exhaustive functional testing in order to form unbiased opinions. In each of these cases, I was deposed by the opposing counsel. My depositions helped the attorneys successfully negotiate out-of-court settlements, greatly benefiting my clients.

Edward Palmer’s Specialties:

VHDL, Verilog, DSP, FPGA, ASIC, LabVIEW, OrCAD, ModelSim, Leonardo Spectrum, Microprocessors, Assembly Language, C/C++, Hardware and Software Verification Testing.
