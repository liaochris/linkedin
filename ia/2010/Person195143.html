Steve J. Moore’s Summary

I am certified to teach English in the state of Missouri, grades 9 through 12. I am currently teaching developmental reading for freshman in the Republic R-III School District in Republic, Missouri near Springfield. I help coach the Academic Team and Chess Club.

It is my goal to become a better leader in the public school system as a teacher and, eventually, as an administrator.

My ultimate goal is always to do everything I can to increase student achievement, retention, and learning.
