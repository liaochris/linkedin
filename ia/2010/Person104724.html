Jordana Jaffe’s Summary

Live ORGANIZED was created to help small business owners, moms, and young professionals reinstate a sense of order and simplicity in their homes, offices and calendars. A native of New York City, I know and appreciate the limited space available to most New Yorkers, and the pressing need use it to its fullest potential.

We work with small business owners and young professionals to help them organize their office space and business systems so that they work can more efficiently, manage their time more effectively, and approach each business day more confidently.
We also work with moms and help them create organizing systems that work for the entire family, so that everyday living becomes significantly more manageable.
