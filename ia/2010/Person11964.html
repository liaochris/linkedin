Andrew Leiman’s Experience

  • Employment Specialist

    Personified

    (Privately Held; Human Resources industry)

    December 2009 — Present (3 months)

    Clients approach Personified with the need to fill certain positions within their company that they have not been able to find with their standard recruiting methods or postings on Career Builder. Personified then has a team work closely with the client to identify the specifics of the position they are looking to fill and then begins to design and organize a campaign that will yield the most qualified candidates. Applicants who engage with us are then screened against the qualifications the client has shared with us so that they only see people who are a good fit for the opportunity.

    We use many different mediums and channels to get the job opportunity the most exposure within the target markets. This involves using everything from passive sourcing to new and advanced methods in social media. The entire process is documented and monitored closely to insure that the management of the project is done in real time and any changes or tweaks will create immediate effects on the campaign. The client receives a final deliverable at the end of the project which details how the project was run and where their funds were most effective so they can focus their future recruiting efforts in more appropriate areas.

    Personified’s services are an efficient solution for the recruitment of a variety of different positions in the workforce today, as we can make sure that clients are not wasting resources on candidates who are not what they are looking for. We also see to it that the qualified individuals are being made aware of the opportunity with the company, thus creating a successful relationship for all parties involved in the process.
