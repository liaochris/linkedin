Richard Loos’s Summary

I am a respected managed care and medical group executive. With more than 30 years of experience as a business leader and strategist, I have guided numerous health care organizations to growth and financial success.

For over 8 years I was CEO of a regional HMO in San Francisco. Prior to this I have held significant leadership roles in health plans, organized physician groups and direct care organizations, including over 15 years as a senior planning and development executive for another regional health plan in northern CA.

I have also worked with diverse health care organizations as a management consultant serving managed care companies, medical groups and hospitals and healthcare advocacy organizations. I have also served as a board member for an established FQHC community clinic.
