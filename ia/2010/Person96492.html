Jerry Laskowski’s Summary

Twenty eight years in providing electronic design support and test engineering of complex military airborne electronic systems to very small fast RFIC package components and wafer integrated circuits used in both commercial and military applications.

I integrate well with design engineers by providing highly accurate characterization test data to be correlate to their design models, design validation and design troubleshooting. I posses a high understanding of high frequency losses with countless hours spent within the lab investigation dielectrics and signal path losses, gauging manufacturing and repeatability of process. This understanding is very important as frequency rates have increased and small minute parasitic losses need to be considered in design.

I have provided integration path of new products into production by effectively transfer accurate test instruction and operations to fellow test engineers or technicians. Working knowledge of high frequency analog, digital and optical circuits and associated testing methodologies.

Very dynamic and capable of fulfilling multiple roles in test engineering and electrical design support.
I have spent twenty some years in the search of understanding effects of small changes and to become an ultimate support personnel. I have accomplished design skills in high frequency PCB designs as well very clever in fabricating circuits for test support.
