Corey Madden’s Summary

My goals include:

to continue expanding my work as a director/writer in theatre
expanding opportunity as a writer/director in media and entertainment.
to finish my master's degree in creative writing.
continued growth of my production company L'Atelier
teaching at the college level
arts leadership opportunities


I was awarded a graduate fellowship for my second year in USC's Masters of Professional Writing program. By arrangement with the program I will also be studying in the Film School.

I was awarded a Lucas Artist Residency at Montalvo Center for the Arts this summer and a residency at Oregon Shakespeare Festival.

I have 25 years experience as a director and producer in the large scale professional theatres, including 22 years at Center Theatre Group, 3 years at Actors Theatre of Louisville and numerous regional theatre directing and co-producing gigs.

Spring -Summer 2009: As resident director of CREATURE FROM THE BLACK LAGOON I took over rehearsals during the final month of tech, training three new casts. I guided the show through a month of previews and to a successful opening with strong reviews. During the summer I have worked with executives and writers, as well as production staff to incorporate script changes, while the show continued to run multiple performances, and also maintained the performances with weekly visits and notes.

I have 10 years teaching experience at the college and graduate level in theatre at UCLA, Cal Arts, University of Washington, NCSA, UCSD and have been a guest artist at many other institutions.

I founded L'Atelier Arts in 2007 to help create supportive links between independent artists and cultural and educational institutions.

My project, ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS won a Rockefeller MAP grant in 2008 and played for six months at Childsplay in Phoenix/Tempe, Arizona beginning in January-June 2009.

My play, WORTH, was seen at Pasadena Playhouse in the Hot House series in March 2008.

Corey Madden’s Specialties:

Arts executive. Experienced Stage Director ( musicals, new plays, opera and dance-theatre, Playwright (adult and youth theatre), project producer, grant writing, arts education program development, college level professor of directing, playwrighting, producing , youth theatre, movement for actors
