Richard Burckhardt’s Summary

I am in the Palm Springs, California area, not in Los Angeles as LinkedIn insists. I'm just about as close to San Diego as L.A.

SEO in The Desert!

I started out to be a journalist, snagging my first reporting job at the Houston Chronicle around 1982, but a little something called a personal computer was introduced to the masses in the 1980s, sending me on a slightly different career path. Somehow writing about technology seemed more natural to me than writing about bank robberies or sports.

I have experience in the publishing field, both online and offline, as an Internet marketer, search engine analyst, web site developer, blogger, writer and newspaper reporter. I have designed and written software user manuals, news/feature articles and web site/blog content. I have training and teaching background, too, including one-on-one and classroom, as well as software beta testing, search engine optimization (SEO), social media, e-marketing and tourism experience.

I was raised in Houston, Texas, have lived in Dallas, Austin and Idyllwild, CA, but currently reside in Rancho Mirage in the Palm Springs Valley (technically, the Coachella Valley to locals).

I love to write and am currently writing and editing two blogs, The Eye Zone (where I am "The Eyeglasses Guy") and The Web Optimist and doing SEO (natural search) as a full-time virtual contractor.

You might call me a "Web Traffic Controller."

I offer local SEO training workshops and Internet marketing consulting to businesses in the Palm Springs area and virtually.
