Ron Marshall’s Summary

I started my company in 1997 as an editorial consulting firm named Marshall Editorial Services in Detroit, MI. In time, editorial services became an arm of a larger operation focused on providing a wide spectrum of capabilities to business in several industries.

Through our eclectic client base, The Mythic Group, as the company came to be called in October 2005, helps serve the needs of several Fortune 50 companies in a broad range of industries, including automotive, financial, food service, health care, marketing, retail, and technology to name a few, and customers in those markets.

As we march into the future, The Mythic Group continues to help businesses begin, develop, and succeed. We are continually looking for further opportunities to do so. With dedication to our mission, a knack for innovation, and our strategy of Professionalism, Partnership, Perseverance, Perfection, we look forward to a prosperous future for ourselves and our clients in the coming year and beyond!
