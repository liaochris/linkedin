Andrew Johnson, Ph.D., PMP’s Summary

Experienced life science business leader, and molecular and cell biologist with 10 years of expertise in commercializing biotechnology instrumentation, reagent kits, and software in both large (PerkinElmer) and small (Protein Forest) companies. Experienced leading cross-functional new product development teams on both national and international levels in both the genomics and proteomics marketplaces. Participated in corporate strategy by reporting directly to the CEO and meeting regularly with the Board of Directors and investor analysts. Recently led the successful commercialization of a proteomic biomarker discovery platform (instrument, reagent kits and software). This venture involved creating a Marketing function, coordinating new product introduction activities between R&D and manufacturing, training and supporting a domestic sales team, launching a CRM system and creating and directing budgets (R&D, Sales & Marketing) tied directly to corporate P&L. It also involved creating a Tech Support system, coordinating the work of outside contractors, training and managing a global distributor network, and preparing product line extension and marketing plans based on regular customer interaction, which ultimately contributed to the successful acquisition of the company.
