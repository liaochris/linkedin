Anna Cearley’s Summary

After 15 years as a newspaper reporter, I left the business to work in public relations and to dive into the evolving world of social media.

I have just completed a master's degree in Communication Management at the University of Southern California. The degree was through a specialized social media track called the Annenberg Program on Online Communities. I took classes while working full-time at USC's public relations department as a media relations representative.

My skills span traditional and new media practices. I have an eye for a good story, and use marketing techniques to bring products/ideas/content to a wider audience. I have experience with online tools used to leverage information, messages and engagement. My goal is to apply this knowledge to current and future communications challenges.

I have recently started a job with Tree.com/LendingTree as their Social Media Director.

Anna Cearley’s Specialties:

- Shot video for breaking news stories in Mexico.

- Proficient in Photoshop and adept with video editing tools such as Adobe Premiere Elements.
