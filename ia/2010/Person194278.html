Stephen Box’s Summary

•I work with Neighborhood Councils and CBO's throughout Los Angeles on communication and outreach endeavors, funding, advocacy, board retreats and campaign workshops.

•I currently write for CityWatchLA, LAist, and the Transit Coalition on alternative transportation, grass roots politics and bicycle activism.

•The tools and technology are changing as quickly as we master them but through it all, I believe that Storytelling remains the simplest and most effective mechanism for changing the world.

•I've produced Music Videos and Commercials, shooting in Miami, Detroit , Las Vegas and Los Angeles for companies such as Propaganda, A Band Apart and DNA. I've worked with artists ranging from Limp Bizkit, Sugar Ray, KORN and Staind to Melissa Etheridge, Marc Anthony, Ricky Martin and Gloria Estefan. Through it all, I've delivered - exceeding expectations and arriving on schedule and under budget.
