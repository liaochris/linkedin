A.C. Kinard’s Summary

20-year law enforcement professional with extensive law enforcement training and experience. Served in the following positions: Corrections Officer, Tactical/Special Operations Officer, Law Enforcement Trainer, Parole Agent, Parole Supervisor, Fugitive Investigator, Internal Investigations Investigator, and Criminal Investigator.

A.C. Kinard’s Specialties:

Law Enforcement/Corrections Administration and Management, budgets, purchasing, contract monitoring, development of agency policies and directives, planning and development, employee discipline, recruitment and hiring, audits and inspections, union negotiation, court testimony, arbitration, community policing, criminal law and procedure, team leader, and certified police trainer.
