Christian Linholdt’s Summary

I am an entrepreneurial & result-oriented executive with strong experience within the fields of Search/Headhunting, Business Development, Sales Strategy, Relationship Management and Networking.

Throughout my career, I have been working with human relationships in connection with sales, service and recruitment.

My knowlegde and experience comes from companies such as ISS, Siemens, Inter-Mail, Moment and SAM Headhunting, where I was responsible for personnel, sales and business development.

The past three years I have focused on three core strategic areas:

¤ Headhunting (search of specialists and executives)
¤ Career Counseling and Personal Branding
¤ Lecturing in "Strategic Networking"

I have a network oriented approach to all assignments, and I work hard to ensure the right match between clients and candidates.

I work quickly, efficiently and professionally and always strive to meet clients and candidates with a human face.

For recruitment and career advice, I combine my extensive experience in business with my recognized certifications in Personal Profile Analysis (DISA).
