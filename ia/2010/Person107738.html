Judi Knight’s Summary

I really hate lists such as the above where you get to choose from one of the primary industry categories such categories as Fishery, Think Tank or Supermarkets. I get all whacked out about it because I cannot be easily labeled.
Whats a girl to do?

Back in the day I was a clinical psychologist, got the Ph.D. and did clinical work for years. I then co-founded and ran a psychiatric medical records software company and built it up over ten years by getting ( millions of) Federal SBIR grant money back when psychiatric hospitals were just beginning to use computers.

In 2001 we were bought out and at that point, I decided to design and build buildings which is what I did for a hobby for my whole life. I love doing it and it is one of my major artistic outlets.

With the economy as it is I am taking a breather from building anything I would have to sell, and instead doing design build projects for other people both residential and commercial. I love creating unique vignettes and usable space that creates interaction and community. In addition, I have started a business, New Tricks, to do business consultation with craftspeople and other creative types helping them with their branding, web/blog and social network marketing.

Oh and my husband and I run a bed and breakfast and Event Space Rental in our huge Atlanta loft, and rent out our Florida beach house. What do I do for a living? See it is really hard to pin down.
