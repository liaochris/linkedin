Chad Porter’s Summary

My experience has encompassed both the creative world with writing, production, and problem solving as well as the "bottom line" world of accounting. This has allowed me to create out of the box, yet practical solutions.

This experience has created a perspective of me of risk/reward versus "yes or no". At some point trying things makes business sense and saying no is a cost. The question is, how much risk is acceptable and can it lead to achieving goals?

My life goals are to create value - whether it be with the company I work for, my start up tech company, or my two little girls who will eventually represent my family in life.

I get frustrated with untapped potential and with a quick "no".
