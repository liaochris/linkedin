Heather Press’s Summary

Sebring Talent Strategies has been helping companies reach their goals since 1999. It has been said that an organization's competitive edge is increasingly dependent on a highly variable asset – Human Capital. How well you attract, engage, develop, deploy and retain talent is increasingly important to the success of your business. Talent Management is about maximizing the potential of this important asset – the ongoing growth of your organization's Talent.

Sebring taps into the latest research, the best thought leaders and the most innovative, proven practices to provide creative, practical Talent Management Solutions for you. We know you have a business to run. We know you don’t have spare time. We provide the knowledge and the solutions that fit. Solutions that work. Knowledge when you need it.

Heather Press’s Specialties:

Workforce planning, recruitment, onboarding, talent assessement, talent engagement and satisfaction assessment, succession planning.
Additional specialization in the geospatial and associated industries.
