Patrina Kerr’s Summary

I am a co-founder and Director of Consulting WorX Pty Ltd, a privately owned Australian company that connects businesses with freelance consultants for short term and long term business projects across Australia.

Having spent over 17years in corporate sales, marketing & management roles across Australia and New Zealand I took a break for family commitments. When connecting with other parents and business professionals who had also chosen not to work in the more traditional corporate world, I realised the extent of unutilised intellectual capital that existed within this community and questioned as to how it could better contribute to Australian businesses while providing the lifestyle and flexibility options to the individual. Thereby driving the creation of Consulting WorX.

I am passionate about driving a better work life balance for those that want that and creating a reputable business with high standards and ethics.

Patrina Kerr’s Specialties:
