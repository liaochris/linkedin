Justin Culver’s Summary

I am working at Maven Training concentrating on marketing and business development in the Company's drive for growth in the Capability,Project and Pragramme management arenas.

Companies need to build capability to meet the challenges brought about by changing markets whether in the public or the private sector. The need to build internal capability has never been stronger; demand for efficiency and the ability to respond rapidly are becoming increasing priorities, with investment in the development of organisational and individual capability now recognised by most organisations as a business critical requirement, especially in a challenging economy.

Why should you undertake your training and CPD with Maven?

Quite simply Maven has the people, the experience, the objectivity – and the time; it’s what we do. Even when organisations have some of the required skills and experience in house they invariably do not have the capacity to undertake such an assignment and they would find it difficult to be objective. Working with Maven you will be an integral part of the development but with minimum impact on ‘business as usual’.
