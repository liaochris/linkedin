Michael Allegretto’s Summary

Performance driven leader, with a proven track-record directing event production and site management activities within the entertainment industry. Handled site development for seven seasons, for Phish's multi-day, multi-arts festivals among the top grossing and largest mass gatherings in North America.

Effective project manager with extensive experience leading cross-functional project teams. Combines meticulous attention-to-detail with exceptional organizational skills, and the ability to excel in time sensitive and high pressure environments. Possesses solid written and verbal communication skills. Willing to travel.

Michael Allegretto’s Specialties:

Music Festivals, special events, film and video production.
