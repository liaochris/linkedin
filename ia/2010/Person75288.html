Gil Alter’s Summary

Gil has developed countless relationships with senior investment professionals over the years and has a few years of experience in the Israel and U.S. venture capital markets. Gil has vast and diverse experience in online marketing, sophisticated SEO and PPC, investment banking, law, technology entrepreneurship and web related product development.

Gil Alter brought extensive leadership, management and operational expertise to Syntryx.com, a leading marketing intelligence firm based in the US.

Gil founded the company FireNetworks in 1999 and led it into a strategic merger with MicroGaming in a deal (valued at a few million dollars) to create one of the first online social networks at the time. Gil served as FireNetworks’ CEO and product manager for two years. He subsequently lead the marketing team at the ITS online gaming agency in Sydney, Australia, where he focused on early stage marketing technology adoption in high-growth online adverting markets.

Gil then went on to found Vertical Traffic, a leading team of 14 online affiliate recruiters and media buyers in the gaming vertical. Vertical Traffic manages a couple of millions of media dollars, negotiation and buying media all over the web, using cutting edge tracking and optimization techniques to achieve better returns.

Gil Alter’s Specialties:

Media buying, PPC, SEO and affiliate recruitment for the gaming and forex online industries. fund raising and business development.
