Beth Nuccio’s Summary

I am a businesswoman, mother and Republican running for Oakland County Commissioner-District 1. I believe that government must be held accountable to the people it serves and live within its means.

As a commissioner, my priorities will be to:

1. Listen and represent the people I serve;
2. Fight to eliminate government redundancy and bureaucracy;
3. Ensure taxpayer dollars are spent wisely;
4. Resource conservation and sustainable prosperity.

My fifteen years of service on the Brandon Schools Board of Education is based on traditional values, fiscal responsibility, accountability and sound decision making. My common sense leadership is rooted in my ability to listen, learn and be decisive.



Strategically-minded with exceptional student focus and leadership skills. Particularly effective at building cohesive teams, leading organizational change in competitive environments and defining/accomplishing objectives. Skilled at getting everyone on the same page and creating a shared purpose.

In-depth general management and teaching experience, with expertise in marketing, business development, innovation, and leadership developed over twenty five years in a non-profit educational environment.

Interested in utilizing technology to deliver education and in connecting students to employment opportunities.

Also building a relationship marketing business and working together with a team to encourage health, wellness and peace of mind. Mona Vie offers the world's best superfood, the acai berry, in a healthy juice that contains 19 types of wild and organic fruits.
