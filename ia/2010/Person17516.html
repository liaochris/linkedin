Ashley Kingsley’s Summary

Ashley E. Kingsley thrives on building relationships online and off. Ashley has been implementing Social Media Marketing plans and strategies for her clients since 2005.

Ashley brings years of experience in viral, guerrilla and grassroots marketing; equally savvy with business development, event planning and promotions.

Ashley has consulted with both non-profit and for-profit organizations, including: Movember.org, Rocky Mountain Singles Summit, The Entrepreneurial Standards Forum, Angel Capital Summit, Thin Air Summit, Fair Housing Council of San Diego, University of California San Diego, ColoradoBizBuzz.com, Downtown Denver News, KNRC Radio, Colorado AIDS Project, the Matthew Shepard Foundation, LoDo Music Festival, and Special Olympics Colorado.

In 2001 Ashley created and wrote ‘SoLo in LoDo’ a monthly column featured in the Downtown Denver News. The column has been recognized on WB2 News, Savvy Singles Magazine and continues, eight years later, to get published by online entities as well as an anthology published in 2006.

MAKING A DIFFERENCE

In May of 2005 Ashley founded ASHLEY’S LIST, an e-newsletter designed to encourage people to support small business. The list has grown substantially and has garnered several community partnerships, including This Week in Denver, The Colorado Connector, and more.

In 2007, Ashley founded TWICE IS NICE, a group dedicated to recycling gently used children’s clothing, toys, books and gear in effort to cut down on waste and draw the community together in sharing.

Ashley has volunteered for Coloradoan’s Working Together to Prevent HIV/AIDS, the San Diego Women’s Homeless Shelter, Special Olympics Colorado, Movember.org, and the San Diego Mediation Center.
