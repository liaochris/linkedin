Hazel Rider’s Summary

As a graphic designer for more than 14 years, I find inspiration from print to digital to the simplest handmade projects. I love researching ways to stay inspired, what the latest design trend is, and where to go for inspiration.
I do work in both print and web media. I currently work in the web department of a music company.
I post on my own blog, aptly named “D.A.M.N.” which stands for Designer, Artist, Marketing Network. I wanted to create a haven for other artists to meet, to collaborate and create.
Once a quarter, I host this free event where we meet and greet to talk shop or to share our latest projects for creative feedback.
Also, I am always finding other ways to market myself and other artists because I find it a valid and satisfying career to be able to work AND make a living from whatever your creative talents are.
