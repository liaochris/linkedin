Steak-Out Franchise’s Summary

WHY BUY A STEAK-OUT FRANCHISE?

Steak-Out does what no other food establishment has been able to do: put some fire into the world of delivery. Go ahead and check out our menu. You’ll find everything from jaw-dropping steaks to mouth-watering chicken filets, all served with a side salad, a piping hot baked potato, and a fresh-baked roll. And we’ll deliver the guest experience straight to the home or office. At Steak-Out, we have exactly what our guest is looking for, everyone at work, friends and family are craving to “Savor the Sizzle” delivered to them!

Our Business is to deliver a hot great meal experience without hassle or confusion. We will serve anywhere there is a gathering large or small to the office, church or any social catered gathering providing our guests with convenience without sacrificing quality.

* Prime Markets Available

Steak-Out Franchise’s Specialties:

At Steak-Out, we're dedicated to providing products and services that exceed our customer's expectations. Satisfying your needs is our specialty. And those aren't just words. We mean it. So try us out and savor the sizzle.

    -Franchise Prospect Criteria: Single Unit

    Liquid assets: $100,000
    Minimum Net-worth: $300,000
    Experience: Two years management experience in all business disciplines. Strong knowledge of day to day operations delivering high quality service standards. Resides in the market to be developed. Exhibits a passion for the hospitality industry and the ability to follow the franchise operating systems.

    -Franchise Prospect Criteria: Area Development / Multi-unit

    Liquid assets: $200,000
    Minimum Net-worth: $750,000
    Experience: Two years multi-unit P&L management experience. Track record of building strong operational teams. Understands trade area development and business planning. Exhibits a passion for the hospitality industry and the ability to follow the franchise operating systems.

    -Estimated Initial Investment:
    Franchise Fee: $30,000
    Royalty: 5%
    P&D Fee: 2%

    Total Investment (per Franchise Disclosure Document)
    $272,000 to $458,5000

    Site Criteria:

    -Strip Center: close to street – for optimum visibility (preferably local ownership)
    -First Choice: End Cap - Minimum Width: 20 Feet
    -Second Choice: In-line - Minimum Width: 25 Feet
    -Parking availability for delivery vehicles and customer pick-up
    -Total Square feet required 1600 to 1800 square feet
    -Location: On drive home side of street for evening traffic
    -Residential population with-in 3 mile radius: 40,000 +
    -Day time population with-in 3 mile radius: 30,000 +
    -Traffic count: 20,000 +
    -Average age: 35
    -House hold income level: $40,000 to $65,000

    Next Steps – the process is painless. Fill out the "Request for Consideration" form on our website. We stand ready to serve your business development goals.
