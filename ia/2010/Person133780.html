Mark Fryer’s Summary

Senior human resource strategist providing strategic HR solutions to organizations including executive coaching, organizational development, succession planning, and leadership development.

As a staff executive, I managed human resource functions in the manufacturing, distribution and service sectors. My real world experience includes a successful track record in executive development, HR process improvement, talent management and acquisition, union avoidance, employee and labor relations, along with succession planning, compensation and benefits and HRMS. Through it all, I’ve seen first-hand the importance of solid executive leadership skills in achieving successful outcomes.

Now I am putting my 30 plus years of experience in human resource management to work helping others develop the leadership skills necessary to succeed in today’s competitive business environment.
