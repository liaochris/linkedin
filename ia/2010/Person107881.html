Judy Paolini’s Summary

Product Design and Development for the Furniture and Gift Industries. Graphic and Packaging design for consumer products. Freelance garden, home and lifestyle writer.

Judy Paolini’s Specialties:

In addition to Product Design and Development, TPDA provides Trend Forecasting, Marketing Strategy, Branding and Packaging expertise, as well as freelance writing.

Judy Paolini’s Experience

    (Furniture industry)

    2000 — Present (10 years )

    Design Director, Patio & Office Furniture
