Kristine Co’s Summary

Kristine is a recent graduate of the University of Southern California, class of 2010. Having received a B.A. in Communication and Marketing, Kristine has not only maintained academic excellence and demonstrated campus leadership, but she has continued to refine her communication skills as a sales and marketing professional with several internships since her freshman year. As a result, she has been exposed to several industries, including -- transportation, hospitality, publishing, technology and pharmaceutical.

Kristine also co-founded TAYO Literary Magazine, the first literary magazine dedicated to Filipino-American youth. As Co-Director, she oversees all marketing, outreach, fundraising and business development efforts for the publication, based in Los Angeles.

At heart, Kristine is goal-oriented, motivated, energetic and extremely competitive, making her a strong candidate for sales positions with established companies.
