I began working as a freelance writer in 2007. By June of 2008, I was able to leave my customer service position at T-Mobile to write full-time.

I offer a variety of writing services, including but not limited to the following:

Blog posts
Articles
Academic writing, editing and research help (Ethical Requests only, please...I will not write your term paper!)

Great content is the key to attracting traffic to your website and building customer loyalty. If you provide people with the information they crave in a style that grabs them and makes them want to keep reading, you'll create a loyal following for your website or blog.

Good content can also help boost your site's search engine rank. Poorly written, keyword-stuffed text is no longer enough to bring search engines to your site-you need quality content. I can provide you with content for your website or blog that is engaging, informative and original.
