Family Business Australia’s Summary

Family Business Australia is not-for-profit, national member-based organisation for families who run a business and their professional advisers. From spouses to cousins and beginners to empires, the emphasis is on helpful information, education, training, reasearch, and discussion to ensure you or your clients' businesses prosper for generations to come.

Trust, understanding and harmony are vital for success of family business and our mission is to ensure you keep it all in the family and in business.

Our support programs are designed to build stronger families and healthier businesses. Our education programs foster entrepreneurship, innovation and family business best practice.

We provide access to specialist family, business and technical services.

We value our members' time and respect individual needs by offering a variety of inspiring events, engaging functions and insightful publications to benefit all industries and life cycles of business. Moreover, Our networking events generate opportunities for families in business to learn and grow by networking and sharing with their peers.

Family Business Australia assists members to reach their full potential. If you work with your family or plan to, JOIN FBA NOW following this link http://www.fambiz.org.au/documents/MembershipBrochureJan09.pdf and discover that you are not alone!

For more information, visit our website www.fambiz.org.au
