Gavin Mc Kiernan’s Summary

I have a diverse group of high level experiences including managing full time and volunteer staff directly and remotely on a national level. I have handled seven figure budgets, been responsible for strategic planning, working with other organizations, coordinated PR strategies and dealt with media, trained people in a variety of disciplines, and been a guest on Fox News and CNN as well had my writing on education and children’s issues printed in The Washington Post, The New York Times, and dozens of other publications.

I have worked in non-profits but am open to opportunities in the for profit world.

    Long Beach Neighborhood Foundation

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 2007 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    The Long Beach Neighborhood Foundation is a 501(c)3 not for profit organization committed to organizing and sponsoring athletic events and programs in Long Beach. These events will promote community involvement, education, tolerance, and a healthy non-violent lifestyle. All funds generated will be used to benefit at risk youth through grants to local programs and through economic development efforts in historically underserved parts of Long Beach.

  • National Grassroots & Membership Director

    Parents Television Council

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 2004 — Present (6 years 3 months)

    The Parents Television Council (PTC) is an education and advocacy group with more than 1.3 million members dedicated to protecting children from graphic sex, violence and profanity on television and in other media. As one of the PTC's national spokespeople Gavin has been a guest on Fox News and CNN and spoken to several State PTA Conventions.

    He is responsible for leading the PTC’s efforts to expand membership and activism at the national, regional and local levels. He Organizes and manages a national network of local chapters, providing training and ongoing guidance. Organizes and executes an annual National Conference and periodic Regional Conferences to support the training needs of local PTC chapters.
    Coordinates initiatives toward the goal of broadening the base of support for all PTC programs and initiatives. Provides leadership and coordination with PTC’s media relations partner to provide local news releases, Op-Eds and other support for media relations involving local chapters.
    Works with other PTC departments to optimize the role of local activists in support of key PTC campaigns and development efforts.
    Develops and maintains professional relationships with key individuals and LMOs active at the state and national level in support of parenting and healthy child development, toward the goal of broadening the base of support for national PTC efforts. Assists local chapters in building relationships with local and state legislators and local LMOs.
