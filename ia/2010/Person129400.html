Lynn Marentette’s Summary

Creative and energetic consultant with a background psychology, counseling & education. Focused on developing and promoting universally usable applications and technologies that support natural-user interaction, collaboration, cognition, and communication.

Interested in multimedia applications and games suitable for screens of all sizes, including, interactive whiteboards, hand held devices, large displays, and multi-modal interfaces.
