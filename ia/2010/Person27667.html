Brent Gohde’s Summary

Creative problem-solver with proven Account Services experience. Strategy, writing, activation, and just generally connecting the dots for Fullhouse clients which include MillerCoors, Oshkosh Fire & Emergency, and Kimberly-Clark, since 2008. Founder and Director of Cedar Block, an events-planning outfit and consultant designing audience experiences/parties for such organizations as the Milwaukee Art Museum and Milwaukee Film Festival since 2005. Occasional freelance arts & culture writer for the Milwaukee Journal-Sentinel and The Onion's AV Club - Milwaukee. For six years, served as Promotions Director at the highly-regarded Milwaukee radio institution, WMSE 91.7FM. Hoping to bring this experience to new organizations and audiences.

Brent Gohde’s Specialties:

Writing, storytelling, connecting the dots, and building campfires.
