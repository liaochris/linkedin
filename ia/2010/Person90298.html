Jason Coots’s Summary

My current focus is focused on acquiring new customers for a direct bank and mortgage company. My team is dedicated to crafting an integrated marketing plan that includes direct branded response marketing, measuring performance by channel and optimizing our marketing plans on a near real-time basis.

I am also leading the efforts to activate, grow and measure the new EverBank Field naming rights sponsorship with the Jacksonville Jaguars. This includes local promotions, national impression exposure, employee engagement, ticket allocation, and philanthropic strategic integration.

My current goal is to achieve an Executive role in an exciting, emerging brand that is dedicated to moving mountains, not pebbles from a marketing perspective.
