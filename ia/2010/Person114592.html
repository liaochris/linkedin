Kenneth Edward Piner’s Summary

Kenneth Edward Piner is a Director for FINRA, the securities firm regulator in Washington, DC where he manages broadcast and digital media producing online educational content. Before coming to FINRA, he worked on the Board staff at the Public Company Accounting Oversight Board after working as an International Corporate and Global Custody specialist at Baker & McKenzie. He holds a masters degree from the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University. While there, he reported for the Medill News Service as a Washington correspondent for TV and radio clients.
