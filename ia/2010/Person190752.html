Slavko Tasic’s Experience

  • Power Electronics Engineer

    Delphi Electronics and Safety / Advanced Power Electronics Group

    (Privately Held; Automotive industry)

  • Principal Electrical Engineer

    Basler Electric Company

    (Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    June 1996 — February 2010 (13 years 9 months)

    Designed complete hardware (analog and digital circuitry, I/O interface, power supply) for a series of complex embedded products used in power generation systems for generator excitation control, including: digital excitation control systems (DECS) for control and protection of complex synchronous machine excitation systems; module for universal phase-control of SCR/diode rectifier bridges; 2 kW regulator with integrated DECS; and 3 kW regulator with integrated DECS.

    Designed analog and power electronics products, including: high-current SCR rectifier bridges (up to 750 V @ 1,100 A DC output); low-cost, low-power analog voltage regulators (up to 100 W output power, using MOSFET or IGBT as a chopper switch, or single-SCR for phase-control); single-phase SCR bridge (up to 15 A output) with protection and interface circuitry for usage with DECS in low-cost applications; metering transducers for AC and DC voltage and current measurements (low-signal distortion, and high input/output voltage isolation - up to 4 kVac) for usage in excitation systems); AC/DC power supply for digital TV transmitters (32 V @ 500 A output, SCR- based).

    Managed interdisciplinary teams (software engineers, mechanical engineers, PCB designers, technicians) toward timely and successful completion of project deliverables. Worked with team to prepare product quotes (schedules and cost estimates).

    Identified product requirements, defined their operation and interface with other parts of the system, and wrote specifications.

    Led verification tests of prototypes, defined test methods and equipment, evaluated test results and prepared design verification test reports.

    Provided problem-solving consulting services to other projects, especially in the areas of electromagnetic compatibility and product safety. Contributed to the design and development of other products (e.g., protective relays, power conditioners, transformers) within the company.
