Jason Stapleton’s Summary

Jason is a professional trader, educator an entrepreneur with more than 10 years of experience in the financial markets. He is the CEO and head trading coach at 4x Traders Live and recently finished 4th in the 2009 Varengold Trader Challenge.

Jason’s heavy focus on trader psychology along with his in-depth knowledge of advanced technical analysis gives him the unique opportunity to work with traders of all skill levels, helping them to achieve a higher level of success and understanding in the FOREX market.

“I pride myself on being extremely approachable. My number one mission is to help traders achieve consistent profitability. To take them from unconscious incompetence to conscious competence. The most satisfying feeling for me is seeing the light bulb go on when a new trader begins to see the markets in a whole new light.”

--Jason Stapleton—
