Craig Espelien’s Summary

Experience - building, developing and executing brand strategies and tactics. From product and package design to overall brand marketing launches and campaigns - targeted at both internal associates and the consumer as a whole.

Goals - to work with companies, clients and individuals who think about the longer term future in terms of how to build a strong foundation using brand, brand image and brand strength as a base.

Additionally, I have sourced almost every product - from national brand items from the largest vendors to items like pallet wrap and dry ice. I understand negotiating tactics and strategies to help the company achieve the lowest sustainable price on an identified level of quality - and devise ways to remove costs from the system over time through behavior changes.
