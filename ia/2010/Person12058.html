Andrew Mustoe’s Summary

A dynamic and client focused individual with a strong emphasis on safety in the workplace, Andrew gained his Degree in Mechanical Engineering as a mature student at the University of Sussex in June 2000. Andrew gained experience with Laing O'Rourke in Design, Design Management, Project Engineering & Project Management roles with experience in the Design and Construction of Gas Systems Design, Sewerage Treatment Plant Design and Water Treatment Plant Design.
Andrew has also acted as Design Manager / G17 Design Approver on High Pressure Gas Pipeline Projects for National Grid, SGN and Centrica. Andrew is a registered National Grid G17 Design Approver / Appraiser for above 7 bar pipelines and AGI's and is at present acting as a Principal Engineer for Parsons Brinckerhoff. Andrew is also presently in the process of applying for chartered status with the Institute of Mechanical Engineers.

Andrew Mustoe’s Specialties:

Onshore Pipeline Design, Onshore Pipeline Construction, HAZOP, Safety Leadership, Project Management, Mechanical Engineering, Gas Engineering.
