Lawrence Dagstine’s Summary

This seasoned science fiction writer, non-fiction writer, proofreader is available for your project(s). Since 2000, I’ve been published more than 350+ times in the genre fiction paying presses (both online and in print), and about 75 non-fiction articles in small circulation prints. I specialize in generating and engineering the written word. Press clips available upon request.

Basically, I'm the man to meet your small to medium-sized journalism or writing needs. My research skills are A+.

My objective for your project(s) is to develop fresh, innovative, and compelling work that meets or exceeds your expectations. I drive constant voice, grammar, format, and diction across all text. Additionally, as part of certain collaborative processes, you may participate in the development of your documents' architecture (e.g., we can go through a series of suggestions and drafts).

When I am engineering your documents I use specific knowledge of your company (it is up to you to provide this information), your publication, subject matter, your audience and demographic, and strategically design your document so that it meets your objectives. In addition to developing new material for your business, articles and letters, or publication, updating and rewriting is a service I can provide. The same goes with research. My clients and I enjoy a close professional relationship in an effort to create well-formatted, visually engaging, professional-quality documents. I use Microsoft Word and Open Office.

During the creation of your documents I am able to employ a professional voice or take the layman's approach. My abilities include translating complex strategies and themes into clear, compelling, readable, and persuasive prose. I work well under pressure, prioritize my projects, and meet deadlines. You are invited to contact me for a FREE consultation. I respond to all inquiries.
