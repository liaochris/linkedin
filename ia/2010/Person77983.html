Gregory Miller’s Summary

For over 25 years I have worked in Health Care, serving the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device industries. With a demonstrated track record for implementing creative solutions to implement cost-effective strategies in the areas of compliance, regulatory affairs, manufacturing, and IT. Having held director-level positions at Bristol-Myers Squibb, Covance, and Schering-Plough in the areas of Regulatory Affairs and Compliance, I am recognized as an expert in the areas of compliance and regulatory affair, who has presented at numerous industry events. As a consultant I have served both domestic and international clients, with an extended engagement in Europe implementing SAP. At Schering-Plough I was brought in to, and orchestrated the compliance effort for the IT infrastructure for the consent decree.
