Susan Lynch-Smith’s Summary

As a Business Development Professional my mission is to bring individual companies together so that each may increase their own competitive advantage, period !

My mission is accomplished by having the backing of a great scientific team that can deliver expertise in formulations/manufacturing along with analytical support in accordance with cGMP practices and principles.

I have been in the pharmaceutical industry for over 20 years, and have both the background and understanding of what my clients need in order to succeed with their own objectives and milestones. Some that come to mind:
-work with professionals who have the background, expertise and understanding of one's project needs
-communicate, communicate
-don't just provide the "hands", but bring the "brains" along!
-work within the agreed upon budget, or let me know in advance
-deliver on time and within the guidelines of cGMP
-Last, but not least, don't surprise one with anything that would hinder a filing of an IND, NDA...

Developing the right outsourcing partnership is a critical first step. With that, I remain committed in bridging the "two" together by taking the time to understand what the client needs in order to "formulate" their project success. This is what I love to do, and that is why I am known as "Synergizer Sue". Enjoy the journey and always stay great !!
