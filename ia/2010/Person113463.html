Keith Messer’s Summary

Data Services, Inc. provides a full spectrum of global data processing solutions for the direct and interactive marketing industries. We were the first company in the world to offer all US, Canadian, and International data processing services under one roof and are today able to correct, verify, house, and process data using over 200 different postal databases covering nearly every country and territory throughout the world.

Our services include US, Canadian, and International Postal Address Hygiene (correction and verification), worldwide Merge/Purge (duplicate elimination), worldwide NCOA and suppression services, database development, list maintenance, online list rental fulfillment services, US Consumer List Enhancement services, US Business List Enhancement services, as well as Email broadcasting services. Our International Market-Link (IML) division offers worldwide mail distribution and product fulfillment consulting services as well as other logistical and marketing support to aid our clients’ expansion into lucrative global markets.

For over 40 years, Data Services has been providing solutions to meet the data processing needs of marketers, both on and off-line, spanning both geographic locations and industrial verticals. Whether you market across the globe or across the street, let our experience make yours better!

Data Services, Inc. is active in numerous associations across the globe including: the Direct Marketing Association (DMA), Canadian Marketing Association (CMA), the International Mailers' Action Group (IMAG), and the Federation of European Direct and Interactive Marketing (FEDMA). Our company president is a past chairman of the DMA International Council’s Operating Committee and, in addition, Data Services, Inc. proudly partners with the Universal Postal Union (UPU) in the acquisition and validity testing of postal databases from around the globe.
