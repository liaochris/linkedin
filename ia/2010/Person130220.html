Internationally published Small Business Internet Strategy articles I've written:
http://messagemedium.com/websitearticles.htm

In 1999 I founded message medium, an NYC Internet Strategy Consulting firm that helps small businesses create the highest ROI, the biggest digital footprint and the most dynamic online brand through Web site design, development, optimization and Internet marketing.

We have provided guidance for initiatives in every space of industry - editorial, politics, government, retail, non-profits, professional & alumni associations, fine art, real estate, bridal, health care, public relations, and travel & tourism.

In 2004 I began empowering small business owners with the knowledge they need to turn marketing & technology into revenue. Speaking & teaching have evolved into a passion. Some victims of my speaking passion include the NYC Dept. of Small Business Services, Project Enterprise, The Workshop in Business Opportunities, The Upper Manhattan Empowerment Zone, The Princeton Entrepreneur's Network & Columbia University.

In 2007 I published my first book "Web Site Fundamentals for Entrepreneurs" which helps small business owners unravel the world of online business (without needing a marketing or CS degree).

In 2008 I launched the weekly column "The Internet Strategist" for Inc. magazine where I share insights from my 13 years of Internet industry experience working with hundreds of small businesses + major corporations like Unilever, Save the Children, Delia*s, AOL/Time Warner & the NYC Dept of Education.

I'm also a media source, providing expert advice on how to build and market small businesses online to publications like Our Town, Harlem News, Inc., Entrepreneur, Black Enterprise, Essence, The New York Enterprise Report & The Wall St. Journal.
