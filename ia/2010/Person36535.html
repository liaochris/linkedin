Chelfyn Baxter’s Summary

I'm Creative Director of Mohawk Media, producing 3D animation and a weekly science & technology slot called 'the g33k show' on Kiwi FM.

I have many years commercial design experience from my varied career in the UK's Broadcast, Web & Games industries. I spent two years at 4:2:2 Video-graphics, specialising in natural history. My show-reel includes work on the triple BAFTA award winning Longitude, and the BMA silver medallist Body Story.

I have had extensive experience in web design and online development. I was the lead technical/creative liason between the design and coding teams on a multi-million dollar website while in Europe.

Recent examples of my work:

http://www.fluxmedia.co.nz where I built a custom-themed Drupal site with a streamlined back-end and video support for an animation company.

http://twisted.co.nz, a local radio site where I designed and built the entire site.

http://piyn.net - a wordpress site for the People In Your Neighbourhood project for the British Council.

Other examples of my artwork can be found at http://chelfyn.deviantart.com/gallery/
