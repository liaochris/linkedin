Pat Dunlap’s Summary

I founded Dixie Studios for Web Design seven years ago and the business continues to grow each year. My specialty is designing unique sites for small or new businesses and non-profits. I am very people-oriented and get to know the business in detail so I can present the organization well on the internet. Often my websites are quite colorful and artsy, depending on the desires of the client.
