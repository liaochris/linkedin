  • Founder / COO

    MojoBaby Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    June 2006 — May 2009 (3 years )

    MojoBaby, Inc. was formed in 2007 to develop new ways of connecting Mobile Phones, Social Networks, Video Games and Web Technology.

    MojoBaby.com was the first service to give users the ability instantly share pictures/video/text from mobile phones to social networks, blogs and forums. MojoBaby worked on all US carriers and many overseas carriers. More than just fun and games, people used MojoBaby to document protests, political rallies, conferences, trade shows and their day to day life.

    Our second project MojoGamer connected online games, social networks, mobile phones and the web. The MojoGamer Platform helps users interact with our clients games. Users were given powerful tools used to stay in touch with the game. From any web or mobile device they could monitor their in-game auctions, find out if their friends were online, check in-game e-mail and so much more. Building on the MojoGamer platform we were able to custom develop a site for each of our clients unique needs.
