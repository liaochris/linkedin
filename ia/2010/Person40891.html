Chris Connolly’s Summary

Christopher Connolly has over 20 years of experience in the ever changing technology industry, and has held several senior level positions. He began his career in a hands-on technical role and has progressed from an architect into a high level management position. He has worked for a number of large international firms including Digital Equipment Corporation (DEC), Compaq and HP. Chris is currently a Division Director at Robert Half Technology managing a large territory of Recruiting Managers and Account Executives. His knowledge and expertise in the ever changing job market has allowed him to not only build the trust of fortune 10 to fortune 1000 customers but also provide CIO’s to Line Managers an outlook of the employment arena that clients use for strategic planning.
