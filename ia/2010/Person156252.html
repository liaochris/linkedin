Norman Nolasco’s Summary

Entrepreneur specializing in software consulting, related business processes, and practical application of technology for multiple industry verticals:

- Founded Advarion Incorporated in 1997: A software consulting firm specializing in high-profile projects with aggressive timelines, unique limitations, and mission critical specifications.

- Broad background in software production, cutting-edge Internet technologies, security consulting, medical research, political and government applications, retail customer management, and energy-related online applications.

- Directed projects with Compaq Computer Corporation (now HP), Ford Motor Company, Wellpoint/Blue Cross of Califonia, Saturn Corporation, the Houston Airport System, and the United States Navy. Responsible for development projects ranging from $50,000 to $5 million.

- Advarion clients include over 60 political campaigns, 8 non-profit organizations, public corporations, and...

- Currently a principal of SSL Corporation (CTO) involved with development of business strategy and technology planning. Our primary priority is to transition the company into a WebTrust Certified Certificate Authority while scaling it into a multi-million dollar business.
