Izzy Neis’s Summary

My goal is to create safe, entertaining environments for youth, both online and in the real world, where they can build self esteem, enjoy their limitless imagination, gain empowerment, and enjoy childhood.

* Panel Moderator, "Virtual Worlds", YPulse Tween Mashup 2007
* Speaker, "Edutainment in Virtual Worlds", ADEC Conference, June 2008
* Panelist, "It's the Activity, Stupid!", Engage! Expo March, 2009
* Speaker, "The Future of Virtual Worlds", Annual Kid Power Conference, June 2009
* Speaker, "Safety in Online Worlds: How the Federal Trade Commission Sees It", Engage! Expo Feb, 2010
* Panel Member, FTC COPPA Review Roundtable: COPPA's Exceptions to Parent Consent, June 2010

I am a published Author with 15+ years working with children of all ages, and 10 years participating in social networking communities. I have extensive knowledge of online media, social networking, and pop culture. My expertise is in youth media, especially in the 8 - 14 age groups.
