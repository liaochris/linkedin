Anthony Carrabino’s Summary

With a passion to design, create and market innovative technologies that enrich people's lives, Anthony's professional experience is rooted in launching and managing start-ups focused on developing commercial software products.

Highlights include working at Microsoft as Sr. Product Manager where he was the global Web and Digital Marketing Lead for the Microsoft Enterprise Application Platform. He was responsible for driving digital marketing efforts for SQL Server and BizTalk, which generate $4B in revenue. His efforts impacted millions of visitors to these websites each year.

Highlights before joining Microsoft include founding Vista Software and serving as President/CEO to manage the development and marketing efforts of two award winning database products called VistaDB and Apollo. Both products enjoyed great success in the marketplace and are used by over 100K customers worldwide. During this period Anthony also served as VP of Marketing for CyberSage, where he led the company to a successful acquisition by Macromedia (now Adobe).
