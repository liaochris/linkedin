David Lutman’s Summary

My career is a bit unusual and unique. Unlike most marketing professionals whose career focuses on one or two areas of expertise, my experience encompasses many areas such as copywriting, account management, strategy development, relationship building and special event production (in short, I'm a one-stop shop marketing professional). But within all of these varied positions, however, there lies a common thread - creativity. Whether it's assessing a client's business objectives and determining the best approach to maximizing marketing dollars or developing various communication materials, including copy and design, being creative is what I enjoy.

And now because of my return to Chicago from Paris and Munich where I've lived, worked (and commuted back and forth to the Windy City) for almost four years, I'm in pursuit of a creative marketing opportunity (either permanent or freelance) that would allow me to continue exploring and utilizing those creative skills I’ve applied to companies such as:

- Big brands like McDonald’s for 15+ years
- Big partners such as ExxonMobil and State Farm
- Big entertainment properties including Lucasfilm

Because it’s all about finding the right fit for everyone and everything involved – the company, my interests and overall personal happiness. Life is too short not to enjoy your work. So, that’s my goal.
