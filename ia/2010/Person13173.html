Angelika Blendstrup’s Summary

Angelika Blendstrup holds a Ph.D. in Bilingual, Bicultural Education from Stanford University. She is a business communication coach who helps foreign-born executives understand the differences and nuances of American business culture and the US English language to communicate well and understandably in their jobs.

She is the founder and principal of Blendstrup & Associates, whose focus is on coaching these foreign-born professionals in accent reduction, presentation skills and how to work successfully in the US business culture.

Blendstrup & Associates also works with clients on personal branding and interviewing skills in order to make them more competitive in the job market and able to stand out better.

Angelika has traveled extensively throughout Europe and Latin America and speaks Spanish and Portuguese as well as French and German. She has taught accent reduction, cross cultural and US communications skills to international executives both privately as well as in companies in the Silicon Valley such as DreamWorks, Sun Microsystems, HP, Tibco and Roche.

She teaches classes at Stanford’s Continuing Studies Program:
1. Branding Yourself Strategically
2. Communications for International Business
3. Riding the Rough Waters of Managing Virtual Teams
4. Interviewing for Success

In addition, she gives workshops at HAAS Business School, UCLA's Anderson School of Business and San Francisco State University's College of Business on interviewing skills and US business communications.
