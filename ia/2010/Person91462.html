Jay Fulcher’s Summary

JAY FULCHER is a proven CEO with an impressive track record of driving revenue and market share growth for public and private technology companies for more than 20 years. In 2009, Jay was appointed President & CEO at Ooyala.

At Ooyala, Jay is focused on building the industry's best and most comprehensive online video platform company. Content and online video is strategic for media companies, large enterprises and SMBs. Ooyala has 500+ customers globally, made up of leading media and web marketer companies including ABC/Disney, Alloy Media, Arsenal, Caracol, Cerner, Dell, EA, Endemol, General Mills, Johnson & Johnson, Martha Stewart, NTT, NY Daily News, Ogilvy, Sungard, REI, Telegraph Media Group, TV Azteca, US News & World Report, Wenner Media, and Warner Bros.

Before joining Ooyala, Jay was President & CEO of Agile Software, a publicly held enterprise software company, which was acquired by Oracle in 2007. During his tenure, Agile became the fastest growing PLM company in the industry, establishing itself as the market leader with over 11,000 customers, and a tremendous reputation for customer success.

Previously, Jay spent 5 years at PeopleSoft where he led the transformation of the $1+B global services organization, and before that was President of PeopleSoft's $400M Products Division - the catalyst organization that helped the company transition from an HR and financials applications company to a full-blown strategic ERP company.

Jay is a proven entrepreneur and dynamic leader who has started or backed many successful companies over the years. He has recently advised a variety of companies in diverse industries including Life Sciences, Clean Tech, Information Technology and Sports Media.

Jay is a graduate of San Jose State University, where he currently serves on the Advisory Board for the Lucas Graduate School and the College of Business. Jay is active in a variety of industry, investment, education and leadership groups.
