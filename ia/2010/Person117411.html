Kirk Laughlin’s Summary

A graduate of the Seattle University Master of Not-For-Profit Leadership program with 15 years of fundraising, grant writing and public affairs experience. Currently oversee strategic fund development and donor relation strategies for The Children's Museum, Seattle. I have personally performed and managed direct mail fundraising, corporate and foundation relations, government affairs, public/press relations and marketing.

Other interests and points of experience include business development, script writing and film/video production.
