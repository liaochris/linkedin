Lisa Birnesser’s Summary

Why Transformations? Quality, continuity and value. Our licensed therapists have dedicated their professional lives to improving your health and relieving your pain. Our small-business setting fosters long-term relationships, so that when you return, your therapist will already understand your massage needs. And our commitment to high-quality care ensures you will receive the best massage experience possible.

Situated in Knoxville’s Bearden area at 18 Forest Court, Transformations Therapeutics gives you an out-of-the-way feeling in an easy-to-reach location.

Thanks for stopping by our website. Feel free to book massages, read informative articles and get to know our staff all from the convenience of your computer. Or give Lisa a call and talk to her personally..
