A forward looking executive with a history that includes growth through identifying and developing future leaders, engaging and retaining top people who contribute to long-term success and profitability while achieving consistently high sales results.

Skilled at identifying and monitoring factors that impact daily and long-range plans and operations; recognizing and analyzing trends and initiating the appropriate action. Proven ability to build rapport between senior executives and create productive business environments across multiple channels and to establish synergy among diverse groups. Outstanding communication, negotiation and mediation abilities at all organizational levels.
