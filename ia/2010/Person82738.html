Hope Hills’s Summary

Since 1991, Hope has coached CEOs and C-suite executives to leverage their leadership approach and style using strategic self awareness to create more effective business results and personal impact. Each coaching and consulting engagement is unique, but in every case, she takes a pragmatic, practical and yet insightful approach that helps leaders consider new and more flexible strategies for better results.

Her clients describe Hope as a coach who "works with her clients to clearly understand and commit to the coaching goals that will lead to both personal and organizational success” and that she “has the ability to help the client discover the solution that really works for their unique situation and challenges. Her methods enhance self-reliance and courage to face old problems and patterns as well as new challenges.” She is “honest, concise, and provides understandable insight into how to improve leadership.”

Hope has a special interest and extensive experience working with CEOs, Senior executives, High Potential Leaders and C-suite succession candidates. She has a depth of experience in OD consulting and supporting teams to become more highly performing as well as helping individuals and teams to successfully transition through major changes.

As a licensed psychologist, she brings a depth of understanding to helping organizations more effectively handle their human system, the key to organizational success.

Hope Hills’s Specialties:

Licensed psychologist in NC; OD Consultation; Executive Coaching; Professional use of the MBTI, FIRO/B, PSI and other key assessment instruments; Team Development; Leadership Training.
