Angela Apon’s Summary

In the summer of 2006, on the beautiful coast of the state of Maine, Belle Pearl was created. Searching for a way to pursue her passions, yet keeping in mind she wanted to work close to home (with her 5 children), Angela found the creation of Belle Pearl to be the perfect answer. Belle Pearl has become a continually growing brand name that exemplifies quality jewelry and exceptional customer service.
The name Belle Pearl comes from two inspirations, Belle, from the French word meaning “Beautiful” and the pearl which has been known as the “queen of gems”. For Angela, it is all about feeling beautiful. Whether you are giving the gift of Belle Pearl jewelry or wearing it yourself, Belle Pearl captures the essence of styles that make every woman feel confident, stylish, and of course, beautiful. Belle Pearl jewelry offers beautiful pearls for beautiful girls of all ages. Initially, the jewelry line was exclusively a fresh-water pearl collection, but as the business expanded, the designs now include semi-precious stones such as peridot, chalcedony, citrine, amethyst, among others.
As a natural „people person‟, Angela continues to lead Belle Pearl as a business that prides itself on extraordinary service, allowing each customer to feel appreciated. It is this special customer care that has allowed Angela to continue to expand her business through additional sales representatives, participation in trade shows, and by promoting Belle Pearl to a greater number of boutiques and buyers nationwide. Belle Pearl has received recognition in several national publications including Bridal Guide, Premier Bride, Life & Style Weekly, OK!, Pregnancy & Newborn and Teen. In 2008, Belle Pearl was awarded the prestigious PTPA Media Award and also the "Reader's Favorite" Children's jewelry in the Baby & Children's Product News Trade Magazine. Belle Pearl has been gifted to celebrities such as Suri Cruise, Brooke Burke, Robin Roberts, & Allison Sweeney.
