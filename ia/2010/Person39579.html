Christopher Clary’s Summary

Christopher Clary is an ambitious young professional, who strives to meet his customer's needs in a timely and efficient manner. He has nearly four years of human resource and insurance related sales experience, and is extremely organized and focused on meeting his company's and personal objectives. For over three years he has been mentored by some of the leading names in the PEO industry and he uses this knowledge effectively to grow as an individual. Driven is the word that can be used to effectively describe Christopher Clary - driven to meet his sale's goals, driven to exceed his customer's expectations, and driven to succeed as a young entrepreneur.

Currently, Christopher Clary is excited at the opportunity to help get a new Segway touring venture off the ground in Austin, TX. Mr. Clary's partners will be Jason Stoneberg and Amy Stanley, co-owners of Keystaff, Inc. Please visit www.SegwayNationInc.com for more information.
