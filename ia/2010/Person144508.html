michelle snow’s Summary

Michelle Snow is a driven entrepreneur, inspirational professional WNBA athlete, and a passionate community advocate.

Her purpose is to help others through her positive impact and influence that she uses to ignite the fire in others.

After losing her mother to Lupus in 2004 a fire was ignited deep within Michelle. She became determined to show others how to live "in the moment" so that we all would take advantage of every opportunity and blessing that life sends our way just as her mother had for 14 long years.

Michelle will not be deferred in educating others on a national and global platform.
