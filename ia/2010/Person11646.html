Andrew Cooke’s Summary

Infrastructure Architect / Manager with 12 years experience in delivering infrastructure solutions and operational support from the desktop through to the data centre; covering design, team management, project management, security and procurement.

Having spent the last 4 years as a principal consultant, I have gained significant field experience delivering a variety of cost effective solutions, by integrating technologies such as server virtualisation using VMware and Hyper-V, VDI and dynamic desktop strategies to a range of financial, local government, retail and legal organisations.

My focus has always been on improving IT efficiently and driving down support costs. My most successful projects have all had either server, application or desktop virtualisation at their core.

Andrew Cooke’s Specialties:

• Server Virtualisation (VMware / SRM, LabManager, Hyper-V / SCCM)
• Application Virtualisation (App-V, ThinApp)
• Profile Management (Appsense, RES Powerfuse)
• Desktop Virtualisation (XenApp, XenDesktop, vWorkspace)
• SAN Design (HDS, NetApp, Equallogic, EMC, HP)
• Team Leadership & Project Management
