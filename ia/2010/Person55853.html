Dave Hill’s Summary

Experienced engineering executive and entrepreneur with over 20 years of innovative, diverse, hands-on engineering leadership. Project experience ranges from shrink-wrap application software to operating system design to custom real-time embedded systems to N-tier web sites to chip verification to interactive television.
