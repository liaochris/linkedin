Lauren LoFrisco’s Summary

My advertising and brand building skills in the consumer marketing arena have ranged from launching new businesses to branding, product marketing, advertising, ecommerce, digital marketing, customer acquisition and retention in the residential phone, video, broadband and wireless services arena.
