Michael Hackmer’s Summary

Over the last 10 years my experience has been in marketing, sales, public relations, grassroots organization and government affairs. I have successfully managed marketing campaigns for some of the world's leading technology companies, as well as developed public relations and government affairs strategies for the nation's leading retail corporations. I have experience in reaching public and private sector audiences, B2B and B2C.

Currently I am using social media and traditional marketing for divisions within BIA/Kelsey. My work at BIA/Kelsey includes using social networking for marketing and lead generation, developing new product branding, building websites and blogs, and other marketing roles. I work successfully with contractors in marketing, graphics / design, website development and public relations.

Apart from my work at BIA/Kelsey, I have a marketing consulting firm that I manage in my spare time. The goal is to help companies and political campaigns identify ways in which they can use social media in their strategic marketing and business development plans. Where possible, I help build teams to manage social media activities.

In November of 2008 I started a weekly radio program, which covers topics in media, new media, technology and marketing. The show is called Social Web Radio. More information on the show can be found on my blog: http://hackmer.com/hackblog.

My goals include learning more about Social Media, networking and technology - especially in how they are changing the way we communicate with one another, process information, and make purchasing decisions.

Please follow me on Twitter at: http://twitter.com/hackmer
