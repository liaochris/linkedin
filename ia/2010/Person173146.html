Rick Jaworski’s Summary

After leaving the Cable TV Industry a few years ago I decided to reinvent myself in the Internet business. My wife Stephanie had developed a popular website, Joyofbaking.com and I decided to take this site and develop it into a business.

Since then Joyofbaking.com has become a very profitable business and one of the top food sites on the net. In December 2009 prior to Christmas Joyofbaking.com was ranked 890 of all websites in the US, a ranking higher then most large newspapers, magazines, cable networks and e-commerce sites. In the 30 day period prior to Christmas the site beat one of the net's top food sites, Epicurious.com with 1.7M US visitors vs. Epicurious's 1.6M. The site served 11.8 million page views and 3.8 million visitors during the 30 days prior to Christmas 2009.

Joyofbaking.com's business model is to rank at the top of the search engine listings for all baking related key words. For example searching on keyword "baking" on Google we rank number 1 and number 2. This drives large amounts of traffic to the site which is monetized by advertising. A wide range of advertisers advertise on the site from large national brands to small niche based companies. This has proven to be a highly profitable business model.

In August 2010 Joyofbaking.com became a part of iFood Media LLC of which I'm part owner and CEO

My goals are to leverage on the expertise I have learned in Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and monetizing websites.
