Matteo Deninno’s Summary

A software and telecommunications engineer, Matteo Deninno moves his first steps 20 years ago, at first developing custom-made Office Automation solutions then moving to Multimedia design and development.

From 1989 to 1996 he has designed, developed and contribute to market several Telecommunications Value Added Services solutions: Videotext, Audiotext, Audio&Video-On-Demand, contributing also to the launch of Stream TV (now SKY TV) the first Cable, Satellite & Interactive TV in Italy.

In January 1997 he started his first company, DEI, focusing on Interactive, Multimedia and Web applications development with which he won several major assignments in the Country including the complete outsourcing of the Web services offering of Wind, now the 3rd Telecom Operator in Italy.

During 2000 he was also the founder of eVision, the first Satellite and Web Digital TV channel dedicated to the Information Technology Industry, who took the attention of BPCI Bank as a minority shareholder.

Then he moved his focus on Marketing Services with the intention to create Value-Added-Solutions for the industry. This is why he created a Marketing Agency who matches software solutions to marketing campaigns, events and communications.

Since 2006 he is developing a global network of marketing agencies under the brand Keyword Group International, Corp. which is headquartered in Manhattan - NY.
The Keyword Group International network now includes several leading US and EU agencies.

During 2007 he acquired the majority interest in Gruppo Input, a leading group of companies with focus on Corporate Events and Conventions, Incentive Travels and Multimedia Contents. Recently, after gaining full ownership of Input, he acquired Promoconvention, another 30 year - multiple award winning event agency in Milan.

Matteo Deninno, 42 years old, lives both in Rome and New York with his wife Francesca and two daughters Sara and Eva.
