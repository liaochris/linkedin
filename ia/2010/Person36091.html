Ryan Miller’s Summary

I have built my entire professional career around internet marketing and website content creation, with a primary focus of turning increased traffic volumes into lead conversions and sales.

In my present position at LexisNexis Martindale-Hubbell I am a SEO Specialist, working one-on-one with lawyers and law firms to gain visibility for their website in the major search engines including Google and Yahoo through natural white hat SEO, and gaining greater exposure for the firm across the internet.

Previously, I managed an affiliate marketing program with a website advertising company, where I worked to drive traffic to specific landing pages and convert click-thrus to email sign-up offers and sales.

I got my start and found my passion for online sales and traffic generation in eCommerce, maintaining an online DVD retailer. I oversaw the day-to-day operations of the business including website content maintenance, stock quantities, order processing and shipping.

In my free time I enjoy writing for host a websites, including freelance work reviewing films and DVDs, and maintaining a site dedicated to celebrities and their legal issues.
