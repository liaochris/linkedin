Gregg Witt’s Summary

Gregg Witt is a niche-marketing adventurist who specializes in building positive connections between brands with youth. In 1991 at the age of 16 he built Goodtimes skateboards and apparel into a global brand and in 1999 co-founded Premise, a full service youth marketing services agency that is celebrating it’s 10th year. In 2007, Gregg was honored with the San Diego Metropolitan 40 under 40 award for his achievements in advertising and marketing that positively impact young consumer groups. As Premise partner, he is responsible for overseeing strategic campaign development and agency marketing efforts. Gregg helps contribute to the success of many Premise client projects for leading national brands, some of them include: Skatewave by Landscape Structures, The Partnership for a Drug-Free America, U.S. Air Force, ISIS (Internet Sexuality Information Services), NMFA’s Operation Purple and Six Flags Theme Parks.

Gregg Witt’s Specialties:

Immersive marketing across all media channels. Core areas of expertise include; building brand relationships with tween, teen and young adult demographics, word of mouth marketing, promotional campaigns, development of youth market strategy, providing brand-relevant cultural insights, digital engagement strategies, educational programs, brand creation and development, experiential marketing, guerilla promotional stunts and anything action sports related.
