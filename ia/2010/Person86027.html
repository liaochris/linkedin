Crystal Crawford’s Summary

Crystal has more than 15 years’ successful leadership experience in Hospitality and Tourism, from the Trainer levels up through Management. Known for being creative, energetic, and results-oriented, Crystal has defined organizational vision and strategy, executing new ideas, being professional, dedicated and bringing a customer service performance impact in every position throughout her career.

Crystal has built a company called 'It's All About Tours' which is a Professional Tour Guide Company Servicing ALL Theme Parks and Local Attractions in Southern California Cities. There are many variations of tours that are offered through Crystal's Services. Crystal is also expanding her knowledge of travel and tourism with her services as a Travel Assistant and Travel Planner.
