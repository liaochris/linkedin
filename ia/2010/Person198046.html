Susan Tibbs’s Summary

Susan Tibbs-Smith, CEO, and founder of Sojourner Truth In Home Health Care Service, LLC in 2006. She has a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling. In 1999, She graduated from the Unversity of Detroit Mercy with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Human Service. She also graduated from Wayne County Community College in 1989 with an Associate of Science Degree in Pre-nursing.

Susan began her research on the gerontology population in 1988, in which her compassion is to help senior maintain their dignity and intergity. Susan worked for more than 15 years in healthcare. She became the caregiver of multiple loved ones in her family.

Susan has a passion to service individual with mental health challenges. She implement therapeutic services to meet the wholistic need of individuals that include mental, spiritual, and physical. Comprised integrated services, physical, psychological, and social services interventions.

Susan treatment approach provides support and is highly individualized to allow the client to make practical decision by embracing a balance lifestyle. Counseling is a reciprocal relationship that promotes empathy, investigation, questions, education, and insight. Respect for the client uniqueness is valued. Supporrtive and sensitive with the demonstrated ability to teach and guide others; recognized for inspiring the confidence and trust of clients. Have strong practical and theoretical foundation in various therapeutic and intervention models. Demonstrate excellent skills in assessing client's needs with commitments to enhance personal growth.

Registered with the National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES).
