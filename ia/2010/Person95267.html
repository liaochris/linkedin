Jennifer Rai’s Summary

Jennifer Rai, President and Founder of Today’s Admin, started the company in 2005, when she realized that a business owner's outsourcing needs do not stop with IT services, printing and marketing.

Outsourcing administrative services is an ever-present necessity in today’s business world and Jennifer understood and saw first-hand that business owner's of both large and small companies could benefit from using an outsourced or “virtual” administrative assistant. The Virtual Assistants would provide daily support or work on special projects, in blocks of time, thus eliminating the need for business owners to compensate a full-time employee in addition to paying out benefits and overhead expenses.

Starting with a strong local base of clients, Today’s Admin and its team of Virtual Assistants have grown quickly by word-of-mouth referrals. Jennifer’s sense of humor and innate leadership ability has fostered a company environment that is positive, hard-working and dedicated. Jennifer’s insight, understanding, and genuine interest in her clients’ requirements enable her to make the perfect match between Virtual Assistant and business professional. This perfect match is the partnership and support a business owner needs to develop their business and increase revenue.
