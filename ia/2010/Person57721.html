Clark Dodsworth’s Summary

I do product strategy, management, development, & experience design for digital devices and online applications, including smartphone Augmented Reality / context-aware / Location-Based Services. Also technology assessment and intellectual property strategy.

Over 17 years experience with clients that include Apple, Philips Electronics, Fujitsu, Evans & Sutherland, Gensler, Compaq, SimEx-Iwerks, Chimera Design, Gyroscope and startups. Real solutions to innovative problems and vice versa. I work with senior management, strategic partners, marketing, engineering, and creative.
