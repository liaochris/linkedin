Salem Honey’s Summary

My name is Salem Honey and I am currently working as a Technical Recruiter for Parallel Hr Solutions, with an emphasis on mid and senior level designers and developers. I have a BS in Business Management from the University of Utah and have been involved with multiple small businesses and industries. I have a keen interest in longevity, vision and business processes.

I began my career with College Works Painting, successfully building a residential paining franchise to become the Manager of the Year, holding the record for the largest branch in Utah for 2005. Following a promotion to District Manager, I successfully trained branch managers in sales, marketing, employee management and production. My division focused on quality and customer satisfaction eventually becoming one of the most efficient teams in the country with sales in the top twenty teams of teams in the country. Our team revenues were $385,000 for the year of 2006.

I then had an opportunity to work as the Vice President of Marketing with Design My Idea, LLC., a small invention design firm geared towards individuals and small businesses. I had carte blanche to increase lead generation and sales, and succeeded with effective Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and Search Engine Optimization (SEM), gaining first-page Google organic-search placement, and increasing weekly lead generation by 200%.

In 2007 and 2008 I was involved in the financial analysis and marketing strategy design for a 32 unit $11.02 million PRD. Sadly the ROI for the project dropped dramatically with the economic downturn right after city planning approval. I was slated to also manage the development contractor during a two year construction time period due to my operational background.

I am excited to create a career path with Parallel HR Solutions, as I have a great understanding of business needs, opportunities, and have the ability to place to right person with the right position.
