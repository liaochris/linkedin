Anton Konikoff’s Summary

Digital media expert/speaker. Keyword-driven marketing & usability, semantic web analytics, SEO/SEM. CEO, Acronym Media + ROI Labs. Web entrepreneur since 1995.

--

I am proud to be the Founder and CEO of Acronym Media (www.acronym.com), an independent, global search and Keyword-Driven Marketing agency, headquartered in New York's landmark Empire State Building, with offices across the U.S., in Toronto, London, Singapore, and other locations around the world.

My experience in the search engine marketing and online marketing industy goes back to 1995, with a consistent focus on search marketing and other Keyword-Driven Marketing disciplines, as well as web analytics and usability.

Consistently rated as a "Top 10 Search Marketing Agency" by Advertising Age, my firm, Acronym, offers our Clients 15 years of search marketing excellence and a distinct approach to enterprise-wide keyword optimization. Our award-winning Keyword-Driven Marketing™ framework places the topmost importance on the language and keywords customers use and understand, to help Clients build effective marketing programs.

Guided by the real-time, granular keyword intelligence, Acronym's experts drive cross-media optimization and craft enterprise-level, large scale, multilingual SEO, paid search, contextual, and other digital keyword marketing programs, supported by in-depth expertise in web/campaign analytics and user experience optimization.

We have done and continue to do brilliant work for the global industry leaders such as Four Seasons Hotels, SAP, BMW, Priceline.com, Sirius Satellite Radio, Nokia, Johnson & Johnson, The Wharton School of Business, Nokia, HP, Scholastic, Tribune Company, Boehringer Ingelheim, Humana, among many.

I am also a regular speaker at Search Engine Strategies, Search Marketing Expo, Search Insider Summit, Online Marketing Summit, AD:TECH, eComXpo, DMA, and others.
