Reid Mihalko’s Summary

Reid Mihalko is a coach, writer and educator with a knack for creating a profound sense of safety for clients upon meeting or speaking with them. His coaching focuses on opening up new space for self-expression within oneself and in relationships, romantic or otherwise. Through the use of role modeling, honest communication, and a deeper, pragmatic understanding of how relationships and emotions work Reid teaches people have to make their relationships real.

As a creator and facilitator of numerous workshops held all over the world, Reid is well-known for presenting information in a comedic and knowledgeable manner. Discussion and Humor find their way into almost every workshop and participants often leave an event wanting more! Known worldwide for creating the cultural phenomenon, Cuddle Party, Reid is currently getting ready to launch Speed-Flirting, the workshop and social event that is sure to sweep the nation (and world).

Reid has worked with thousands of individuals from myriad walks of life, orientations, relationship styles, countries and religions. Known for his charisma, humor and emphasis on integrity, Reid travels the country teaching and coaching. He has been a writer and producer on a number of film and television projects about sex and relationships, and appears regularly in the media.

Reid has been a featured speaker at dozens of conferences on relationships and sexuality. He has also appeared on the Emmy award-winning talk show Montel, Fox News, VH1’s Scott Baio is 45 and Single, Showtime’s Penn & Teller’s Bulls**t!, Canada’s The Sex Files and SexTV, and Life & Style, on NPR, Sirius’ Maxim Radio and Cosmo Radio, and in Marie Claire, GQ, People, Newsweek and The National Enquirer, as well as media across the globe in thirteen countries and at least seven languages.
