Gerald Blakley’s Summary

Experienced software developer with particular expertise in Java, C/C++ and Perl. Major projects I have developed for include:
* large simulation models of cutting-edge, Enterprise servers, in Java, as part of the hardware architecture team at Sun
* a simulation environment for routers and switches for developing complex software prior to arrival of hardware, in C and Tcl/Tk, at Bay Networks
* embedded software on a SONET system, including a hardware abstraction layer interfacing hardware and software, and an abstraction layer interfacing platform software with applications developed in ObjecTime, in C++, at Applied Digital Access
* compilers, assemblers, debugger, and tools, in C, at Bay Networks

I have developed GUI Visualization engines in Java Swing and a JNI interface to weave together programs into a larger software system.

Throughout my career I have excelled at solving unique and difficult problems, often outside of the core competence of the project. I have demonstrated the capacity to manage and tame large systems, including large build systems.

I have lived abroad extensively, and am proficient in English, French, German, and Nepali, and conversant in Hindi, Greek, and Italian.
