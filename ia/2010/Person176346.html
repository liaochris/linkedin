Rodney Gagnon’s Summary

Rod Gagnon is president of Professional Support Services, Inc. (PSS) located in the historic mill district of Manchester, New Hampshire. PSS offers cutting-edge pre-employment and tenant screening solutions to companies nationwide.

He is also the owner of Gagnon & Wendt, a NH private detective agency (Agency #DA-597) which specializes in asset searches, corporate due-diligence investigations, the purchasing and recovery of Court awarded judicial judgments, and the locating of missing heirs.

As a private investigator he has been active in his state's PI association, the New Hampshire League of Investigators (www.nhli.net) as a member, Board Director and currently as the Treasurer. He is also active with a newly formed non-profit; The Investigative Center for Missing Children and Cold Cases that is located in Concord, NH.

From 1998 to 2009 he was the owner of NH Background Investigations, LLC in Nashua, NH. NHBI is a public-record research company that searches criminal and civil records in every Massachusetts and New Hampshire court. At the peak in the economy NHBI was processing as many as 18,000 criminal record searches a month, with clients from many of the largest national screening companies. In March of 2009 NHBI was merged with County House Research, Inc. of Philadelphia.

Rod's investigative history started in the mid-1980's, when he joined forces with friend and fellow investigator Marcus Wendt and built Phoenix Collections & Investigations into New England's first contingency repossession agency. Clients included all the major finance companies (GMAC, Ford Motor Credit, Toyota Motor Credit, etc.) as well as every bank in the state.

Rodney Gagnon’s Specialties:

Pre-employment & tenant screening, asset searches, judgment enforcement, unclaimed funds recovery and missing heir searches.
