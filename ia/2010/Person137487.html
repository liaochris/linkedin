Matthew Paulson’s Summary

I was born in Gettysburg, SD, raised in Mitchell, SD and currently live in Sioux Falls, SD.

I attended Dakota State University and have a B.S. in Computer Science and a M.S. in Information Systems/E-Commerce. I am currently pursuing a M.A. in Christian Leadership from Sioux Falls Seminary on a part-time basis.

American Consumer News, LLC is a small content-company that I own and manage. The company publishes various consumer and financial websites.

Factor 360, Inc is my day job. In this role, I'm the lead developer for the company's web content management system (360 WebCMS) that is used to manage the company's client websites. I also assist with developing client-proposals and handle any online marketing work that's needed.

I am also an adjunct instructor for the College of Business and Information Systems at DSU.

My professional interests include e-commerce, web development, search engine optimization, online marketing and computer security.
