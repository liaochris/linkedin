John Brian Hennessy’s Summary

I have enjoyed hands-on, multi-hat wearing labor within 12 start-ups across 10 distinct industries. This is one of the things I do naturally - help give birth to Start-Ups and assist early stage companies with growing pains.

I have worked as a teammate on companies that became significant financial successes, some simply successful, and a couple of eviscerating-learning failures. I am batting at a 750 success avg.

My "Life-Work" (est 1980) concerns the process-modeling, representation, education of, and (probable) predicability of innate, gift-centric individual and human-systems evolution.

From 1980 to 1985 I had the absolute pleasure of working in the Stanford University Neuropsychology Laboratories and under the tutelage of Professor Karl H. Pribram.

Between 1980-1990 my work focused on the neuropsychological and quantum physics framework and basis for human learning, performance and meta-cognitive, cognitive and behavioral modeling, which evolved into a focused on natural, gift-based human and human systems evolution and the actionable idea of an "Ecology-of-Mind."

In that time I invented a number of technologies the most important of which is a little known "human and human systems evolutionary-ontology" and corresponding taxonomy.

In 1990-91 this human evolutionary-ontology became "pop characterized" (cartoon characterized) so that it could be more effectively consumed by consumers and children. I tested it. Kids like it. The result? It produces personal awareness about one's natural, innate, genuine, gifted-driven & YOUnique Self.

Along with graduate school I was one of five start-up team members of SyberVision Systems, Inc. and in 1982 I founded Psykon Achievement Systems, Inc. in Palo Alto, Ca. both being truly "Kelvin-Kewl" adventures.
