Maureen O'Connell’s Summary

I have spent a lifetime in the Motion Picture Industry, beginning as a technician in motion picture & television film laboratories, moving on to stock film library apprentice, apprentice editor, special effects assistant editor, assistant film editor, Motion Picture Film Editor, and finally head of a major studio sound effects library. I've also worked for the Special Department of the IATSE, and for the IATSE's Local 700, MPEG, the latter as a labor organizer.

My goal, since taking early retirement from my Guild, is to find gainful employment working for a nonprofit, not-for-profit or nongovernmental organization, with a focus on human rights, social justice, health, affordable housing or issues related to civil liberties and/or vigilance in the maintenance of the separation of Church and State.

Maureen O'Connell’s Specialties:

Negotiation, persuasion, sympathetic listening, passionate advocacy, research (print and internet), arbitration, written communication.
