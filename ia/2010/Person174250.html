Robbie Banks’s Summary

The Columbus Chamber leads and supports economic growth and development in the Columbus Region. Through the Chamber's Attract & Retain Talent initiative, local employers are assured access to a workforce that meets their needs now and in the future.

The Attract & Retain Talent Initiative is comprised of 3 areas of focus:

ColumbusInternships.com helps link local employers to college students seeking internships in the Columbus Region.

The Chamber's suite of "Recruiter Solutions," including liveworkplayColumbus.com assists employers when they are recruiting candidates from outside the Columbus Region.

Young Professionals (YPs) are integral to the competitiveness of the Columbus Region. Because the population of the generation following the Baby Boomers is significantly smaller, it is imperative that the Columbus community actively attract and retain younger workers to meet the needs of local employers.

Robbie Banks’s Specialties:

Attraction and retention of young professionals, including presentations to various audiences; employees (getting involved as YPs), college students (how to effectively network) and organizations; volunteer management and recruitment; event planning and execution; meeting facilitation; member/customer relations; connecting people to people, businesses/organizations, information and resources; Columbus facts, people, places and things; social media (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, blog and videos).
