Salim Virani’s Summary

An entreprenerd, consultant and Lean Startup advocate.

I'm the creator of Leancamp, an event format which brings multiple industries together around entrepreneurial and design methods to help businesses find unmet needs and get to market faster.

My background is in direct & integrated marketing and Internet technology. The businesses I've founded range from technical service providers to consumer e-commerce sites, and include a digital media department for a major advertising agency.

I help business founders and strategists move quickly from vision to market traction. I help my clients understand how to systematically find unmet needs, align research with business impact, and reduce early-stage risks with digital platforms and design process.

I also offer direct support with marketing & technical strategy, product launches, and developing new revenue streams.
