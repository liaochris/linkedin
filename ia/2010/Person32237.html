Carl Allen’s Summary

Systems engineer specializing in analog and digital communication, COMINT, and SIGINT systems . In-depth experience providing systems engineering support in the design, development, verification, test, and delivery of advanced systems. Experience includes modeling and simulation of satellite and airborne communications, SIGINT and COMINT systems from the system level to the individual RF or digital units. Also, I have extensive experience in the field of Military Intelligence.

Carl Allen’s Specialties:

Systems engineering, modeling and simulation, specification development, integration and test, requirements sell-off.
Communication systems, SIGINT, COMINT,

Expert in communication link analysis and SATCOM network loading analysis. Expert user of Satellite Evaluation Tool (SET) and a certified Satellite Tool Kit (STK) expert. Proficient with Satellite Orbital Analysis Program (SOAP), System Effectiveness Analysis Simulation (SEAS), MATLAB, and SIMULINK.
