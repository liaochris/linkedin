David Yun’s Summary

Since 2000, I have worked in a variety of roles in Development, Product Management, and Product Marketing. Starting as a developer at a small start up called iHarvest that was acquired by Interwoven, I eventually became the Sr. Product Manager on TeamSite, Interwoven's Flagship Content Management platform. In 2003, I joined Macromedia to work on the Breeze (now Adobe Connect) product staying on through the Adobe acquisition. While taking a break to get my MFA degree from San Francisco Art Institute, I consulted for Adobe in the role of Community Manager working primarily on the community site ConnectUsers.com, building up the user group program, and developing training and resources for customers. I recently joined ConnectSolutions, a leading provider of secure and scalable managed services for real-time collaboration and web conferencing to lead a team building a suite of new real-time collaboration products for government and enterprise.

Outside of my work in high tech, I am an accomplished artist. My work spans a variety of mediums including film/video, photography, social practice, and installation and has been shown around the world at venues such as the Museum of the African Diaspora (MoAD), International Documentary Festival Amsterdam, European Media Arts Festival, International Asian American Film Festival (New York), Sydney Underground Film Festival, Seoul International Film Festival, and Documenta Madrid. I recently participated in the Present Tense Biennial, an international survey focusing on Chinese and Chinese American art. See my full artist resume here: http://www.davidmyun.com/about/cv.html
