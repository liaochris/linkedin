Ben Jordan’s Summary

Hello, my name is Ben Jordan. I am a hybrid designer/developer/director with skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, XHTML/CSS, PHP, Coldfusion, and .NET. I have over 10 years of experience in print and interactive design/development, as well as extensive experience in business process development. I believe a business can only truly be as successful as the processes that run it. These processes give people opportunities to be successful by focusing on the product and not all the drama that can distract from the task at hand. To me, processes are king.

I have had the opportunity to work with some great clients including: American Airlines, Southwest Airlines, the Dallas Stars, the Ritz Carlton, Haynes and Boone, Brinker Restaurants, and many others. My design work has been featured in several publications and has won numerous awards.

Ben Jordan’s Specialties:

I am proficient in HTML / CSS / XML and have experience with ASP / PHP / JAVASCRIPT / RUBY / COLDFUSION / ACTIONSCRIPT.
