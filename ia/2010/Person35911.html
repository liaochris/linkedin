Charles Fuller’s Summary

I've been involved in media and publishing for over 25 years, starting out as an associate editor for Entrepreneur magazine and later spearheading the expansion of the magazine into the online space in 1992 with the development of Entrepreneur's community on CompuServe.

In 1996, I launched Entrepreneur's web site and followed that with the development of SmallBizBooks.com, SoyEntrepreneur.com, BizStartups.com and HomeOfficeMag.com, all of which were rolled up into Entrepreneur.com.

My experience includes:

• Business development: Formation of strategic partnerships focusing on revenue production through the development of new revenue streams and the optimization of existing revenue opportunities. This includes the identification of new potential partners through market observation and analysis to assure the alignment of the opportunity with the current goals, negotiation of terms and generation of initial contracts, evaluation of new product concepts and creation of strategic business plans.
• Seach engine optimization: Extensive experience building SEO programs through the development of site keyword plans, optimized page and site structure, content interlinking and external backlinking programs and guidelines.
• Interactive ad operations: Expert in the development of interactive ad trafficking systems and operations. Have implemented and optimized ad serving systems such AdForce, DART, Open AdStream and Atlas.
• Email marketing: Created initial list program for Entrepreneur and developed outbound marketing programs. Experience includes list database schema, ESP selection and management based on list management, email deployment and projected ROI goals

Charles Fuller’s Specialties:

Strategic partnership development, web site development, new product development, editorial development, online marketing, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, strategic management & planning, project planning & management, client relationship management, online advertising sales, contract negotiations, P&L management and and team building & leaderhsip.
