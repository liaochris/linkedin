Marty Cagan’s Summary

Held executive product positions at eBay, AOL, Netscape, Continuus and HP; start-ups and Fortune 500. Responsible for business strategy, product strategy, product management, product design, user experience and product development process for leading Internet companies.

Author of the book: "Inspired: How To Create Products Customers Love" published in 2008.

Marty Cagan’s Specialties:

Specialize in defining successful products, building effective product teams, and improving product development processes. Publishes bi-weekly newsletter and blog to the product community. Clients include Yahoo!, eBay, TiVo, the BBC, PROTRADE Sports, and Internet startups in emerging markets worldwide.
