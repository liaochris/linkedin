David Gruder, PhD, DCEP’s Summary

Dr. David Gruder, PhD, DCEP, is a clinical & organizational psychologist, as well as an ordained interfaith clergy, who was hailed by Radio & Television Interview Reports as "America's Integrity Expert.

His latest book, "The New IQ: How Integrity Intelligence Serves You, Your Relationships and Our World," is a road map for addressing the worldwide crisis of lack of personal, relationship, corporate, governmental, and leadership integrity. It has won six unusually diverse book awards in the areas of Current Events in Politics & Society, Social Change, Conscious Business & Leadership, Health & Wellness, Self-Help, and Meta-Psychology.

Dr. Gruder is Executive Director of Integrity Revolution in Del Mar, California. An international speaker, trainer and consultant, he has for thirty years taught the skills necessary for creating sustainable happiness, health and prosperity without sacrificing integrity, ethics or social responsibility.

The author of two major print books, four e-books, over 70 CD sets and more than a dozen training manuals, his first print book, "Sensible Self-Help," won two book awards, including Colliers Mental Health Book of the Year. In addition to having been the co-founder and first president of the Association for Comprehensive Energy Psychology, Dr. Gruder has taught graduate courses in Archetypes, Human Development and Energy Psychology at the California Institute for Human Science in Encinitas, California.

Dr. Gruder is also active in the ManKind Project, an international non-profit organization helping men deal with the blocks interfering with their integrity and leadership so that they can fully embody their personal, relationship, professional and service missions.
