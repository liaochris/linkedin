Amit Peleg’s Summary

I am a “people” person and always interested in how people think and behave.

This fact naturally lead me into marketing, which later was fine tuned into web marketing. I am fascinated by the ability to generate more quality traffic to a web site, more leads and more sales, only by operating successful methodical SEM, SEO, SMO and web analytics processes.

I started my way in this “marketing world” at “Globes” with basic one on one sale sessions.

After I learned how to handle “sale objections” for over a year, I moved into the real marketing environment and become a brand manager at a cellular content company. Although, I was responsible for a few marketing channels (including print, cellular, TV & internet) I was particularly enthusiastic about the web marketing channel. Completing 2 years at “Tippcom” made me realize that web marketing is what I wanted to do.

In the last 3 years, I specialized in operating integrated web marketing tactics, working with leading brands from miscellaneous industries including travel, financial, Hi-Tech, communications, media and more. As a team leader at "Compucall” and later as VP, Client Services, I have the privilege of developing a leading web marketing agency to become better and to generate more clients, more projects and dramatically improve the knowledge of our team members.

I have specialized in:
* Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
* Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
* Pay-per-Click (PPC) and other Online advertising campaigns
* Web Analytics mainly with Google Analytics
