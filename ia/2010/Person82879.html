Howard Collens’s Summary

Trusted counselor and adivsor to individuals and their families. Goal-based planning with concentration on development of estate plans using wills and trusts for large to small estates. Detailed powers of attorney, designations of patient advocates, medical directives and living wills. Contested and uncontested probate and trust administration in the Detroit metro area, primarily Wayne County, Oakland County, and Macomb County. Elder law attorney including at long term care issues at home or in nursing home, Medicaid planning, Medicare and Social Security.

Estate planning, Probate, Trust Administration, Elder Law
