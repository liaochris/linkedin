Candice Clark’s Summary

Working under electrical lights during the day? Contact me for a solution in turning them off.

Over five years with a Manufacturers Representative Firm, supporting Engineers and Designers led me on a path working for an amazing Manufacturer- Solatube International, Inc. Solatube International produces a top down Daylighting system, piping natural light through a building, with an effective use of daylight for a majority of the work day. Our leading industry technology allows this product to be a suitable solution for multi floor Daylighting in interior zones of office spaces, educational buildings, full illumination of distribution center floors, warehouses, and hotels, etc. My Clients are Architects, Engineers, Designers, Design Build Contractors, Building & Business Owners, Developers and Sustainable/Green Consultants.

Solatube Daylighting Systems are in-line with today’s demands for energy saving and carbon emission reduction initiatives.

I would like to meet people in your network who can benefit from the advantages of daylight. Can you introduce me?

My territory is the South Central US; CO,WY,NM, KS, MO,TX, IL (south), LA, AR, and OK. Outside of my territory? I will pass your info on.

In my free time, my passion and volunteering time is spent in the festival and event industry. A prior-position on the Board for CFEA- Colorado Festival and Events, keeps me linked to the art, diversity, and culture of celebration.
