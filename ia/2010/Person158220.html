Barry Otterholt’s Summary

Project management expert for small (<$5m) to medium (<$50m) scale projects, with CMC and PMP credentials and 30+ years experience.

Consultant, providing subject matter expertise from project inception through closure, as well as the recovery of troubled projects. Background includes information technology and organizational development projects across private and state level government sectors.

Coach, coming along-side internal management with hands-on situational mentoring, tools and techniques to address urgent needs, and transfer skills to project and customer workforce.

Instructor, with ready-built curriculum across most facets of project management from entry/foundation level to advanced/masters level. Experience in both graded and non-graded environments, as well as just-in-time education for urgent project workforce training needs.

Barry Otterholt’s Specialties:
