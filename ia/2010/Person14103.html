Anne Donker’s Summary

I am executive director San Francisco at Hubtech21. Hubtech21, with offices in Paris, Boston and San Francisco, specializes in helping European technology companies establish a presence in the United States market, providing a range of corporate development services including market research, business development and funding.

Building on years of roaming the globe in search of innovative technology ventures for Accenture’s R&D and investment groups, and working closely venture capitalists, I help Hubtech's clients maximize their success on US markets as well as helping them understand how Silicon Valley operates.

In addition, I serve as a Program Chair of the Silicon Valley Startup Entrepreneurs (SVASE) Startup University, a program which has helped 40,000 entrepreneurs understand how to build businesses. This allows me to tap into a vast, global rolodex I can leverage for deal flow as well as for the benefit of my clients and investor contacts.
