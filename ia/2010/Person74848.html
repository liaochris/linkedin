Gerrit Reinders’s Summary

Self described Tree-Hugging Capitalist!

My areas of passion, expertise and responsibility include developing lighting solutions that are budget neutral and help building owners grow top-line sales, reduce operating expenses, reduce environmental impact and improve the quality of life of people for those who work and shop indoors.

I've had a lot of experience in sales, marketing, business development and creating strategic and corporate plans - particularly for Sustainability, High Performance Green Buildings, Lighting, Energy and Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Emission Management.

I love to be an impact player and do so by developing and deploying sustainability strategies and programs that create superior Economic, Environmental and Social value. I've been lucky enough to have done so frequently with commercial, industrial, government, healthcare and institutional clients.

I am honored to serve as Secretary on the board of directors of National Association of Energy Services Companies (www.naesco.org) and until recently was quite active on the board of the Energy Services Coalition (www.energyservicescoalition.org).

Over the years I've been an official spokesperson - currently for Energy Focus and before for Johnson Controls. I'm a frequent speaker presenting at various national and international venues as an expert in lighting, high performance (green) buildings, energy management, and GHG emissions management. I've also been fortunate to have published multiple articles on energy management, green buildings, GHG emissions management and sustainability reporting.

Back in 1983 I earned a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Marquette University, then earned an M.B.A. from Keller Graduate School of Management in 1991.

I was born in Holland to Dutch parents and lived in Latin America for almost 15 years. I'm considered to be fluent in English, Spanish and Dutch with conversational skills in a few other languages.
