Jill Johnson’s Summary

Jill Johnson is an award winning management consultant who has impacted over $2 billion worth of business decisions. Jill has a proven track record of dealing with complex issues & getting results. Jill's consulting work focuses on developing effective market-based business strategies that are grounded in reality. She is also an accomplished speaker.

Johnson Consulting Services works with a wide range of industries, from corporate clients to entrepreneurial ventures. Our clients are typically success-oriented and ambitious leaders. They share our passion for results and are prepared to act boldly to attain them. Our clients frequently make significant decisions based upon our work.

Jill’s clients are located throughout the United States, as well as in Europe and Asia. Our entrepreneurial clients include privately held and family owned businesses, as well as some of the most successful and highly regarded entrepreneurs and corporate directors. We advise senior executives of sophisticated non-profit organizations and government agencies. Our corporate clients include leaders who are CEOs, Fortune 500 senior executives and board members.

Our clients typically cite the comprehensiveness of our research, along with our analytical ability, candor, integrity, and objectivity as being instrumental to their organizational and financial success.

Jill J. Johnson is known for her ability to effectively present complex and challenging information to her consulting clients. Jill is also an accomplished speaker who has given hundreds of speeches to corporations, associations, as well as to business and professional organizations. Whether she’s speaking to senior executives or addressing a group of young adults, Jill brings her extensive professional experience and unique voice to deliver presentations that offer substance with style.
