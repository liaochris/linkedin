Maurice Gilbert, CPA’s Summary

My objective is to continue expanding our Conselium "executive search" business in Europe, Latin America and China.

Another goal is to further the development of Corporate Compliance Insights (CCI). CCI is an on-line knowledge sharing forum devoted to corporate compliance issues. Our audience include Chief Compliance Officers, Chief Audit Executives, Chief Financial Officers and General Counsel who routinely touch "compliance" as part of their core professional responsibilities.
