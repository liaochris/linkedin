Raymond Green’s Summary

Experienced professional in regulated industries including Defense, Health Care, Real Estate and Oil and Gas.

Extensive defense experience includes: Earned Value Management system implementation and maintenance; proposals and estimating in competitive and sole-source environments; negotiating development, production, engineering services, supply and maintenance contracts; evaluating, pricing, negotiating and implementing contracts in foreign currency; evaluating, monitoring and reporting risks and opportunities in fixed-price and cost-plus situations; development, monitoring and reporting performance against 5-Yr forecast, annual operating plans and estimates-at-completion; development and negotiation of Forward Pricing Rate Agreements; evaluation of accounting practices against GAAP, FAS's, SOPs and Sarbannes-Oxley. Thorough understanding of Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR), Defense Supplement to Federal Acquisition Regulations (DFARS) and Cost Accounting Standards (CAS.)

Experience in financial/operational analysis and evaluation of accounting methodologies, process, policies and procedures. Experience in identifying, designing and implementing process improvements (6 Sigma Certified.) Experienced in evaluation of internal controls and establishing a strong internal control environment.
