Art Brosius’s Summary

My business life started with larger companies. Owning my own or being an integral part of a smaller one became a goal.
In 1982-3 I became an owner of a small financial planning firm.
I was able to retire from that and started to pursue a latent passion for the game. My fondest memories since childhood were the opportunitiies I had had to play a number of the worlds best venues.
Creating " Exceptional experiences in Golf" now drives my company.
