Stu Libby’s Summary

AdCopy is on a mission to make online advertising better for users, advertisers and publishers - to learn more about how we're going to make it happen - please reach out to me directly.

Prior to joining AdCopy I spent time at Google working on new business development across the DoubleClick platform. I joined the DoubleClick team before the Google acquisition to start & run their Ad Agency New Business Development.

Before joining DoubleClick I was the Director of Sales at Root Markets - an open marketplace for buying and selling consumer leads. Root Markets acquired LeadFilter where I ran sales, was a member of the executive committee and the first hire. Before joining the LeadFilter team I was the co-founder and CEO of Coupons4Campus.com - a leading online publisher of coupons for college students. I graduated from Quinnipiac University in Hamden, CT and graduated with a BS in Marketing.
