Jim Bollenbacher’s Summary

"For most people, it's not what they are that holds them back, it's what they think they are not." This, a quote by one of Jim's early sales mentors and Best Selling Author John Maxwell, helps propel Jim in his business efforts daily. Whether it is finding the next impact medicinal chemist for an organization on the move, or supporting a proven player in considering options outside of their current organization, Jim will "out care" his competition every day.

A 2005 Graduate of The Ohio State University's Fisher College of Business, Jim began his sales career in mortgage lending, where he was part of a team recognized in the Top 5 of almost 80 with a National Lending Institution. In just his second full month of originating loans, Jim led his region in Monthly Sales. Referred to us through a mutual acquaintance and based in California at the time, our opportunity was enough to convince him to move back to the Midwest. We couldn't be happier with that decision and are confident you too will recognize his competence in a single conversation.
