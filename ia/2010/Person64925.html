Emre Ersahin’s Summary

Looking back at what I have done, I can simply say for almost 20 years I have been trying to make ideas happen and succeeding in most of them.

Starting at the strategy dept of an int’l ad agency I have watched how ideas are formed and cultivated. Later at a multinational FMCG company, I learned telling apart which ideas are worth to run after. Later actively participated in making a big idea happen: The 1st internet advertising network in Turkey. Achieved good market share back then with a behavioral analysis tool.

Then, instead of participating in others' ideas, together with a partner we decided to take up our own idea to establish the 1st youth marketing agency in the Eurasia Region. In over 5 years we made many pioneering ideas happen.

After that entrepreneurial episode, lured myself back to corporate to participate in a great team making many first time ideas for Turkcell to happen. To name a few, first iPhone applications, first operator social music website and award winning first time 3G services.

Keen to make ideas happen on an international level, I recently joined a locally successful web company, to help make the idea of a globally successful Turkish web company real.
