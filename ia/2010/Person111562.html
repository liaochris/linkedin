Kate Holgate’s Summary

Are you a professional speaker or author who finds that you are spending too much time working on running your business and not enough time developing your presentation or writing your book or just enjoying your life?

Maybe you've put off applying for your Certified Speaking Professional designation because the process is too daunting or you need assistance with new business development, event and client management or general clerical duties,

I am a Professional Staff Member of the National Speaker's Association, an Affiliate Member of the NSA-Central Florida Chapter, a member of the American Society of Administrative Professionals, National Bookkeepers Association and I LOVE working with speakers! Edith Wharton said it perfectly: "There are two ways of spreading light: To be the candle or the mirror that reflects it". Let's talk about how Alliance for Success, LLC can be your mirror!

Kate Holgate’s Specialties:

Complete CSP application processing. Event management and client support. Maintaining a web-based database of your events, clients and bureaus. New business development ranging from identification and making contact with new leads to contacting your former clients for re-booking and referrals. We also offer accounting services, graphic and website design, electronic and print marketing campaigns, publishing and book roll-out services and general clerical services.
