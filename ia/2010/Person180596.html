Sal Becerra’s Summary

I have designed and directed hundreds of market and user research projects, both qualitative and quantitative, for corporate, political, non-profit, and governmental clients. Research projects include:

• Surveys (online, SMS, telephone, intercept, mail, fax)

• Usability / Lab Sessions (online, mobile, kiosk, software applications)

• Ethnography / Contextual Inquiries / Field Studies

• Wants & Needs Analysis

• Prototype / Concept Testing

• Card Sorting

• Participatory Design

• Rapid Iterative Testing & Evaluation (RITE)

• Focus Groups

• Panel Management

• In-Depth Interviews (IDI's) 1:1


Leading corporate clients include: eBay, Honda, Intuit, Yahoo!, the North Face, Acura, Nissan, and LinkedIn.


Experience in marketing and user research studies includes methodology design (of both sample and instrument), usability and focus group moderation, collection and analysis of research results, as well as presentation and implementation of findings.

I've worked with qualitative and quantitative research studies in a variety of roles including: consultant, project manager, analyst, quality assurance specialist, and field director.
