Leo Polovets’s Summary

Mid-level software engineer with a strong math/problem-solving background and over 5 years of experience (including at LinkedIn!). So far, much of my career has focused on building the frameworks and infrastructure that everyone else on the team uses and eventually takes for granted. I am passionate about creating software that is robust, readable, reusable, and as efficient as possible. I focus on what is practical, and once I learn the tools I need for a project, I write a lot of clean code and I write it quickly. While my professional experience is mostly on the back-end, I am very interested in all aspects of software development, from user experience design to algorithm implementation to database normalization. It's rewarding to do something I am good at, but just as rewarding to become good at something unfamiliar.

Leo Polovets’s Specialties:
