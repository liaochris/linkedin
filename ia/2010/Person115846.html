Kevin Oskow’s Summary

Kevin is the Director of Business development at Lynch Interactive, managing the ad firms marketing department and over 100 clients, including health, wellness, educational and informational sites. He has over 13 years of design, development, marketing (print and online), management and leadership experience.

As a director for the last 5 years, Kevin has developed a keen sense for project management. His experience has been with projects as small as 300k/yr to over 8m/year.

Kevin has a real passion for design and technology. He has built dozens of web, database, small business and email servers, beta tested many operating systems, and produced creative materials for hundreds of companies.

Kevin enjoys the direction he is currently in, and is looking forward to helping companies achieve their goals through business development, project management and stellar marketing.
