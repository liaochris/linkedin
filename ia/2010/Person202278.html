Natalie Gahrmann’s Summary

Natalie Gahrmann, an international expert, empowers professional women to ignite their passion, demonstrate personal leadership and exude greater confidence. Her background in business acumen and leadership development is instinctively applied through 1-1 coaching, workshops and keynote addresses

Natalie helps people at every level of an organization address a wide range of work, family, personal and professional issues. For 25+ years, Natalie has helped leaders and aspiring leaders identify and constructively address work and life concerns, resulting in improved job performance, a stronger bottom-line, and a more dedicated and loyal workforce.

Natalie is the author of the award-winning book, "Succeeding as a Super Busy Parent". She has an MA degree in Administration & Supervision, BS in Marketing Education and is certified by the International Coach Federation.

Natalie is the life coach for Parenting magazine and work/life expert for BlueSuitMom. She has appeared in magazines, newspapers, on-line resources, and a variety of programs on national & cable TV and radio. She has worked with employees from companies such as Genentech, Daichi Sankyo, American Standard, Schlumberger, GlaxoSmithKline, Novartis, and AT&T.

Natalie seeks to make a positive difference in the lives of super busy people and the companies they work for by helping individuals decrease distractions while increasing focus on the task-at-hand and improving bottom-line results.

Natalie Gahrmann’s Specialties:

Focus on employee productivity & performance, personal effectiveness, work/life balance and skill development through presentations, workshops, coaching, articles and her BLOGs.
