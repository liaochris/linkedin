Prasad Muppirala’s Summary

Leadership, Entreprenuership, Business Development, Marketing & Technnology Strategy, Software Architecture, Product Development & Operations

Prasad is a native Indian hailing from Nellore in the state of Andhra, where he received his Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. After working in India as a R&D engineer for BULL Power Systems for nearly three years, he relocated to the USA to pursue and complete his Masters Degree in Computer Science from Minnesota State University in Mankato, MN. While he was working on his Masters, he began to work for BORN Information Services, where he served in many roles, both technical and management, for about seven years. He left BORN to co-found and serve as President for RAJEN Technology Services, a niche technical architecture consulting firm. He successfully managed that business for five years.

Upon experiencing a cardiac related event at the age of 38, Prasad was successfully treated and, driven by that personal experience, he became very interested in the impact of lifestyle factors on health. To that end, he joined RedBrick Health, a USA consumer-directed financing and health care advocacy company in early 2007, where he served as Director of Technical Architecture & Platform Engineering. There he worked on multiple health and lifestyle related initiatives. While at RedBrick, Prasad also enrolled at the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University in Chicago, IL.in the MBA program.

Prasad left RedBrick Health in July 2008 and started consulting at Express Scripts as an interim engagement while wrapping up his MBA.

Upon completion of MBA recently in Dec 2009, Prasad has started pursuing opportunities within the healthcare space in Product Management and health care technology areas.
