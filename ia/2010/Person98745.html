Jim Reilly’s Summary

At APUS my responsibilities include management of 7 programs in the School including Math, Space Science, Environmental Science, IT, and the development of future programs. APUS is one of the fastest growing and most highly ranked online university systems in the country with active student enrollment over 60,000. The system offers over 70 degree plans in undergraduate and graduate programs via the American Military University serving active duty military personnel anywhere in the world, and the American Public University offering courses to government and civilian students.

Jim Reilly’s Specialties:

Education management, team development, technical evaluation and program management operations, requirements development, and project/program management.

Jim Reilly’s Education

    PhD , Geosciences , 1991 — 1995

    Research conducted on the distribution of chemosynthetic organisms around hydrocarbon seeps and mthane hydrate deposits in the deep water regions of the US continental slope, Gulf of Mexico. Dissertation titled: "The Structural and Stratigraphic Controls on the Distribution of Chemosynthetic Communities in the Gulf of Mexico".
