Steve Riley’s Summary

Senior System Engineer with an 18+ year proven track record of performance and outstanding business results in fast-paced, demanding and rapidly-changing environments. Solely responsible for managing all aspects of 100+ servers, both physical and virtual. Experienced in the design and support of Wintel and VMWare ESX infrastructures including leading a 60+ server ESX consolidation, an Exchange 5.0 to 2003 migration and a migration from an NT 4.0 domain to a parent 2003 AD enterprise domain.
