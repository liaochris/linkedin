Harry Elkin’s Summary

EXECUTIVE PROFILE

Harry Elkin has extensive senior operations management and entrepreneurial experience in a diverse number of Fortune 500 and privately held businesses, and a record of success in improving sales and profitability, turning around underperforming businesses, and increasing shareholder value.

His most recent experience has been as the President of a very profitable boutique capital equipment trading company, where he led his team to deliver sales that generated a 46% gross profit and 30% net income during the past ten years.

Harry is equally qualified and capable of managing the M&A process from sourcing to due diligence to final negotiations. He was responsible for sourcing and evaluating acquisition candidates, managing the due diligence team, and negotiating transactions forr a $100 million multinational industrial manufacturing company.

His experience includes P&L management, mergers and acquisitions, business turnarounds, developing global strategic alliances and partnerships, profit/productivity improvement, operations management, industrial engineering, subcontract manufacturing and internal auditing in industries that include: snack food, semiconductor capital equipment, TV and stereo manufacturing, diesel fuel injection systems, point of sale displays, foundries, paper mills and packaging operations.
