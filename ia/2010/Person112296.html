Kathy Rapp’s Summary

Human Resources Executive with leadership experience at companies such as Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts, the Redstone Companies, Morgan Stanley and First Data Corporation. Additionally has a unique blend of start-up experiences within the technology, entertainment and healthcare industries and has also worked internationally in Asia Pacific.

Kathy is currently expanding hrQ in Texas. The company's focus is exclusively on identifying HR talent via national search, HR contract staffing and leading HR consulting projects.

See www.hrQinc.com for more info on the team, our professional development event calendar and current projects.
