Jerry Rocco’s Summary

Jerry Rocco, Vice President of Sales and Marketing, is co-responsible for overseeing all aspects of the developing, marketing, and configuration of iMar Solutions. He is dedicated to making a difference in the internet world through innovative marketing and entrepreneurial leadership.

Jerry was born and raised in East Rockaway, New York and has now been residing in San Diego, California for the last nine years. He enjoys spending time with his family, surfing, snowboarding, and traveling, just to name a few. Previously, he worked as a Chef, Wine Sommelier, and then as a Real Estate/Mortgage Marketer.

After being involved in the volatile Real Estate market in 2002 – 2006, Jerry ventured out seeking a business that was not subject to the whims of a questionable economy. After meeting with a group of dedicated Internet Entrepreneurs he knew that without a doubt an Internet Business was the Definitive 21st Century Business Model and that was where he needed to focus. Shortly thereafter he enrolled to work under the wing of an internationally renowned and grossly successful Internet Marketer.

After only one year in the industry in October of 2008, Jerry was recognized and given, The Master Marketer Award, in Dallas, Texas by his primary Internet Marketing Company. Shortly after, he formed Ascendant Enterprises, LLC and then teamed up with his business partner, Steve Schwartz; together they formed ThinkiMar (iMar Solutions, LLC). Jerry keeps his finger on the pulse of this fast changing market by attending several internet marketing conferences every year and frequently meets with other leaders in the industry to mastermind new ideas and internet strategies.

Jerry continues to help others achieve financial independence by introducing them to business models and solutions that are traditionally considered “outside the box” and not commonly available… yet.

Jerry Rocco’s Specialties:

Dominating Google and creating systems (Internet sales funnels) that do most of the heavy lifting and selling for you. The better you are at marketing the less you have to sell.
