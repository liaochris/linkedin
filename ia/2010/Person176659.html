Roger Warner’s Summary

Founder of Social Media and Online PR agency Content & Motion (est 2008), an Online PR and Social Media Agency (see below).

C&M works with progressive brands such as Hackett, IBM, Continental and Rightmove, helping them to do smarter customer acquisition and retention via content programs and Social channels.

15+ years experience in web and PR-related communications practices – including a lengthy stint as Director of PR for IBM Europe, and running agency-side campaigns for the likes of HP, Toshiba and other household names.

Prior to C&M, five years senior management experience helping web and tech-related service agencies to move from zeros to heroes. For example…. responsible for kick starting the fortunes of world-beating open source software developers, Squiz and their market-leading open source CMS, MySource Matrix. Also recently helped Velocity to become the funkiest B2B tech marketing consultancy on the block.

Many moons ago, was also a budding young centre half for Charlton Athletic. Unfortunately shown the door at the age of 18 after two years of boot cleaning, chasing centre forwards (slowly) and praying for the offside trap to work.

I live in Lewes with my wife Gerry, my son Lucas and an ever-growing menagerie of dogs, cats and bunnies.

Roger Warner’s Specialties:

Building *winning services-based agencies* - and the kind of teams that move them; Creating long term *client relationships* via insanely great service levels; Devising and delivering effective, *long term communications programs*; Measuring all of the above and always proving it, proving it, *proving it*
