Kathleen Eckelt’s Summary

I have over 30 years of diverse clinical experience, primarily in critical care areas of ICU, CCU, Neuro ICU, and Burn Unit, followed by ten years as a case manager in Home health Care. During that time, I acted as liason between patients and physicians, provided nursing care, and participated in peer chart reviews.

In 2001, I started in an ABA Paralegal program, then continued on, taking Criminalistics and Criminology courses, finally obtaining certification as a Forensic Nurse Examiner in 2003.

Since then, I have received training as a Medico-Legal Death investigator, attended medium level & advanced FNE training as well as continuous CEU's; and became an Associate member in the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners, specializing in Health Care Fraud.
