Ben Wright’s Summary

I've done a variety of things that have evolved around one thing - a passion for cities and for discovering new things. My company, Atlas Advertising, has grown from a two-man operation, run out of my house in 2007, to what it is today—serving clients in over twenty-six different states and four countries.

I know enough to be dangerous about design and technology, but what I really know is how human beings use modern communications to look at places - places they visit, places they are considering buying real estate in, places they are thinking about locating a business in, and places they want to work in.

Ben Wright’s Specialties:

Branding, website development, and research and creative services. Atlas was founded in 2001 and the company has worked for over 70 different real estate developments, cities, states, and regions, all with one goal – to bring vitality to those communities. Our approach includes branding, website design, search engine marketing, email marketing, website content, and lead generation.
