Federico Fasce’s Summary

I'm a game and interaction designer, mainly focused on the development of meaningful game content and on play strategy.

I design games able to convey messages for advertising, information, education and plain entertainment.

I also use my experience as a game designer to design Internet applications and to analyze digital media from a different point of view.

Web design with XHTML, CSS and PHP

Skilled in vector, bitmap and 3D graphics (PS, AI, 3DMax)
