Jodi Peterson’s Experience

  • Managing Editor

    High Country News

    (Non-Profit; Publishing industry)

    May 2010 — Present (5 months)

    High Country News, published every other week in Paonia, Colo., is an award-winning nonprofit newsmagazine and Web site "for people who care about the West" -- grassroots activists, public land managers, tribal officials, government policymakers, educators, students and interested citizens. We cover environmental, natural resources, political and cultural topics.

    Jodi oversees the editorial staff and the content of each issue of High Country News and of hcn.org. She edits news stories, book reviews, and features; recruits freelancers; and very rarely finds time to write her own stories (favorite subjects: environmental policy and wildlife). She also serves on the organization's Diversity Council.

    She's always looking for talented journalists to freelance for HCN.
