Paul Kirch, PRC’s Summary

I have been blessed to have found an industry which I have grown to love. I have a passion for entrepreneurship, sales and sales coaching. I have a strong proven background and track record, which I'm proud to say I have earned. I am President & CEO of my own sales management consulting firm, ActusMR, Inc., specializing in the marketing research industry. Actus is a Latin word meaning "perfection, determination and compliment." I believe these words describe our overall philosophy. We are determined to succeed, we strive for perfection and I believe we provide the perfect compliment to our clients. In addition to my role within ActusMR, I am an active volunteer within many industry organizations, including the Marketing Research Association (MRA), where I am President of the NorthWest MRA chapter. Thank you for visiting my profile.
