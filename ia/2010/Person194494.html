Stephen McDonald’s Summary

I have over a decade of web development and system administration experience spanning a wide variety of platforms and technologies, primarily as a technical lead in a digital agency environment providing mentoring and leadership to junior and senior developers alike.

As someone who is passionate about keeping abreast of trends in technology I am also heavily focused on research and development, and in this regard I am a firm advocate of both using and contributing back to the open source software community.

I also have a strong background in product development with a proven track record of successfully shipping software in the areas of content management, e-commerce and analytics targeted at business users and software developers.

Throughout my career I have gained considerable experience with the following technologies:

Python, Django, PHP, Haskell, JavaScript, jQuery, SQL, C#, ASP/ASP.Net, PyS60, ncurses, VB/VBScript, ActionScript, LSL, HTML, XML, CSS, XSLT, SVN, Mercurial, Git, Linux, Apache, IIS, memcached, nginx, gunicorn, Fabric, PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server, wxWidgets, GTK, Amazon S3/EC2, Pinax, Satchmo, VMWare

Stephen McDonald’s Specialties:

Web application security, database design and optimisation, network programming, object oriented programming and API design, usability and information architecture, rich internet applications, cloud computing and scalability, test driven development
