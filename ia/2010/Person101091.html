Joe Perez’s Summary

Joe Perez, CPRW, CJSS, is Principal of Writing Wolf, an Editorial Services firm, and has helped hundreds of clients in developing and implementing plans for work/life transitions. His expertise in career management and personal branding is grounded in an appreciation of the common truths of the world's wisdom traditions.

Joe was once featured in a BeliefNet column as an “Angel in Real Life.” In the Foreword to Joe’s spiritual autobiography, philosopher Ken Wilber said Joe is “given to telling the unvarnished, brutal, searing truth… the truths that speak to you deeply, authentically, radiantly.”

His day jobs for 17 years have been in communications and coaching. He holds dual certifications as a professional résumé writer and job search strategist. In previous careers, he led an Internet advertising startup and was a technical writer at Microsoft, where he once worked down the hall from Bill Gates.

Despite a B.A. from Harvard University in The Comparative Study of Religion and Philosophy, he is not through with school yet. He is studying Integral Leadership at Pacific Integral and preparing to complete the M.A. in Divinity from The University of Chicago. He aspires to lose the label “divinity school dropout,” which he has worn for a decade.

Blogs

Joe is currently blogging daily at SeattleJobCoach.com and is preparing for the re-launch of Joe-Perez.com.

Books

Joe is currently working on a book on spirituality, an epic poem, a screenplay, and career books/websites.

"Soulfully Gay" (Integral Books/Shambhala, 2007) is a memoir of Joe's discovery of Emerging Spirituality. "Rising Up" (Lulu.com, 2006) is a work of cultural criticism.

Coaching / Facilitation

Joe has facilitated experiential groups and offered one-on-one guidance for job search support, men’s shadow work, and Integral Life Practice (ILP). Forthcoming later in 2010... additional service offerings in this career area.
