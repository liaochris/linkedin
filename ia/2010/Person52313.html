David Whitney’s Summary

David enjoys writing and architechting well designed systems using modern methodologies and practices.

From a background featuring a mixture of Microsoft cannon technologies and OSS solutions, David currently specialises in the .NET platform and Object Oriented design and implementation.

Making use of the most modern technologies in the field (.Net 3.5, WCF, WPF, ASP.NET MVC, O/R Mapping, Agile methodologies / test driven development and n-tier architecture, Inversion of control using the Castle framework, nHibernate), David has a wide range of experiance producing both highly available windows applications, web applications (using both C#-Asp.net and PHP), and distributed systems.

David has a strong emphasis on patterns and standards driven development and endevours to maintain an exceptionally high quality of work by following these principles.

Previously David has worked as a systems administrator on a manner of platforms ranging from Windows NT 4.0 to Linux and has an extensive knowledge of Windows platform administration and virtualisation.
