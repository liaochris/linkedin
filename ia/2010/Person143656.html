Michael Levine’s Summary

I'm a dedicated Executive Recruiter with over 20 years of successful search experience placing mid-senior level executives in the retail, wholesale, fashion and consumer product industries.

I possess a track record of developing and cultivating long-term relationships with client companies and candidates through positive results, high rate of candidate placement tenure, service, communication and strong follow up.

I pride myself on the reputation I've earned for quality, professionalism, partnership and developing client and candidate trust.
