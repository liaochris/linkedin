Wendy Van Parys’s Summary

Integrated marketing communications consultant for more than two decades, providing results-driven strategy and execution to regional B2B, B2C, professional service firms, political campaigns, civic organizations and not-for profits. Trusted advisor to senior management, works effectively as a team player with staff at all levels to achieve client objectives.

Wendy Van Parys’s Experience

  • President

    Edwina A. Van Parys, LLC

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    1989 — Present (21 years )

    Wendy Van Parys Marketing Communications (Edwina A. Van Parys, LLC) celebrated its twentieth anniversary in 2009. The firm uses a wide variety of communications tools to build bridges between companies and the people they want to reach. Clients have included area market leaders in technology, financial and professional services, automobiles, commercial and residential real estate, building materials and construction, design, landscaping, travel and food, as well as political campaigns, non-profit and civic organizations. The firm has handled both business-to-business and business-to-consumer assignments.
