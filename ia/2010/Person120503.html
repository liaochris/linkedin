Kevin Zicherman’s Summary

A passionate and business savvy marketing, Internet and sales professional, Kevin brings a wealth of cross-industry experience. With Marketing Strategy and Business Management Honour Degrees from York University, along with helping establish multiple companies over the past decade, Kevin has worked in corporate and entrepreneurial strategic marketing capacities in industries such as Electronic Payments, Consumer Packaged Goods, Environmental and Entertainment. His results oriented and ROI approach to value-added offerings and strategic insights help unearth customer-focused opportunities for all his clients. Kevin has most recently been instrumental in developing online success with high profile clients such as Maytag, Fuji Film, LG Electronics and The Rexall Family of Pharmacies.
