Carol Fitzpatrick’s Summary

Founder of Living the Miracle Center for New Paradigm Leadership and Global Network.

Serve as an advocate for personal and global change by speaking on topics of building new paradigm community. Provide the tools for teaching people how to trust what is beyond the five senses.

Facilitate transformational processes with groups and teams that are working to bring about radical change to the face of our human family. Provide the knowledge and the transformational tools for creating the shift from old to new in a safe, supportive environment.

Advise, coach and mentor new paradigm leaders, individuals that have chosen the joy of inner peace as a way to shift global consciousness.

Collaborate with husband Mark Torgeson, musician and sound healer. Together, we facilitate workshops, retreats and presentations that involve energetic and sound healing, meditation and movement.

Author of two books: A Call to Remember: Follow Your Heart, Change the World and Fear Not My Child.

Background: The opening for my work began in 1989 when I experienced a spiritual transformation that took me deep into the mystical realms of the divine. During that time in my life, I owned and operated an advertising design firm that had grown into a regional publishing company. I went on to serve at the forefront of the national community policing movement in a lead marketing role. In 2000, I started a grassroots community building organization before transitioning into my life's work.

Carol Fitzpatrick’s Specialties:

Inspirational speaker, facilitator, activist, organizer, healer, teacher.
