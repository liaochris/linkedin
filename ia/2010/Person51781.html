David Rachlin’s Summary

David globalized Ben & Jerry’s ice cream and revived the Boursin cheese business in North America and Europe, in 16 years at Unilever. Since then, he has served as CEO for early-stage companies marketing environmentally sustainable foods, beverages & housewares.

A Senior Marketing Executive who creates value by growing brand equity, revenue and profit nationally and internationally, David is expert in Classical Brand & Business Development Strategy and Execution for CPG and he is now learning best practices of Social Media Marketing and Social CRM, to keep his marketing skills at the leading-edge.

Pragmatic go-to-market strategy and swift, sure execution are David’s strengths. He leads teammates with strategic vision, enthusiasm and collaborative rapport, that energizes people to stretch beyond their comfort-zones.

David's teams have succeeded at the most challenging, higher-risk assignments, including pioneering new markets and turning-around newly acquired and strategically-impaired businesses. He speaks English, French and Spanish.
