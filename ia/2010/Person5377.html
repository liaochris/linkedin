    Clancy Real Estate

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    January 1994 — Present (16 years 10 months)

    Kevin Clancy has been providing real estate brokerage and appraisal services to the Capital District for over 16 years. He is a New York State licensed real estate broker, Insurance broker, and general certified appraiser. Kevin is a HUD approved broker and started his career specializing in the sale of HUD homes. Kevin's appraisal experience ranges from vacant land and single family homes to multi-tenant office buildings and retail centers. A graduate of Siena College with a degree in marketing, Kevin obtained an MBA from the University at Albany in the Spring of 2005. During his career Kevin spent several years evaluating residential and commercial real estate throughout the northeast. This includes more then an estimated 10,000 residential homes in the Capital District. Services include full service and discount brokerage, appraisals, property management, and general consultation services.
