Scott Downey’s Summary

I have been active in the Software Industry for more than 18 years, holding positions at nearly every level of organizations, and in a wide variety of organization sizes.

I have focused the last half of my career on organizational process optimization and have worked with a variety of methodologies, including formal waterfall, plan-driven development, TDD, RUP, XP and, of course, Scrum. In my current role as the Head Agile Coach for MySpace.com, I am headquartered in Beverly Hills but have multi-site responsibilities throughout the organization. I am a Certified Scrum Practitioner, a member of both the Scrum Alliance and the Agile Alliance, and work with Dr. Jeff Sutherland each quarter to arrange and conduct Scrum Master Certification courses in Beverly Hills.
