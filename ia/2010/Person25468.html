Robert Hockensmith’s Summary

Robert Hockensmith is the President and Chief Financial Officer of Robert F Hockensmith, PC, a certified public accounting, certified financial planning, certified legal document preparation, and consulting firm. He prepares financial statements, offers payroll services, corporate and individual tax returns, legal document preparation, and gives financial advice to the public. He presently teaches at University of Phoenix, Phoenix, Arizona, in graduate (MBA and Masters in Accountancy) and undergraduate accounting classes, and has also taught for Glendale Community College, Glendale, Arizona, in undergraduate accounting classes. He has been a newscaster for NBC in Phoenix, since 1995, broadcasting financial and tax topics to the general public, on weekly basis. He is also a Public Affairs Specialist at the Federal Emergency Management Agency. Previously, he was a Consultant and Accountant with Arthur Andersen, CPA's, and Ernst & Young, CPA's. He served as a Colonel with the US Army, retiring from military service after 36 years in 2008.
