Jeanne Tsan’s Summary

An HR Professional who is passionate about leveraging Human Resources functions that will contribute to the success of companies. A strong believer in continuous improvement by evaluating and questioning practices to keep up with the dynamic business environments.

Jeanne Tsan’s Specialties:

Jeanne Tsan’s Experience

  • Director, Human Resources
