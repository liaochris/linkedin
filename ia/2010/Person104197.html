Jon Burr’s Summary

Jon is a bandleader/bassist/composer/lyricist/producer with 30 years in the business.

Jon's most recent project is the new vocal/bass duo: Jon and Lynn, with artist/curator/vocalist Lynn Stein.

Jon produced 27 master recordings of his original songs in 2007 for jbQ Media LLC, a partnership with his brother Bill Burr, and is also the sole proprietor of jbQ, a music production studio.

Jon has recorded and performed with an extensive roster of names, including Tony Bennett, Stephane Grappelli, Mark O'Connor's Hot Swing Trio, Eartha Kitt, Rita Moreno, Chet Baker, Stan Getz, and many others.

jbQ does music production, video editing and package design for music releases, and produces recordings and demos of songs and musicians, serving at every step of the way, from transcription to notation to arranging to midi to ProTools recording to editing, mixing, mastering and replication. We also serve other producers by delivering bass tracks via FTP or email. We are AutoTune pitch correction specialists. We do graphic design and layout, specializing in CD's and onesheets for physical product releases.
