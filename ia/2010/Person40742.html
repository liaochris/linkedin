Guglielmo Parasporo’s Summary

After graduations in the beginning it was tv and cinema productions. Then came the idea of a film commission, from there of incoming services and got gradually focused into websites for vacation rentals. I enjoy the idea of slowly developing a sustainable business satisfactory for all. I've been running vacation rental websites for well over a decade now, so I can start considering myself an experienced professional.
