Leah Moon’s Summary

Leah Moon graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism from Ohio University's E.W. Scripps School of Journalism in March 2008. She has completed internships at Clary Communications and Quinn & Co. Public Relations.

For the past year, Leah was a digital strategist at Spectrum in Washington, DC where she worked on various accounts in the health, medical and science fields on their digital initiatives.

Most recently, Leah has accepted a position with Universal 1 Credit Union where she will be their new social media specialist.
