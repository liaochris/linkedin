Joe Angeline’s Summary

An accomplished, results driven Senior Technology Manager with demonstrated success in the design and of large-scale global systems infrastructures that seamlessly manage high volumes of data and digital media. Strong combination of technology and management skill, capable of performing “hands-on” engineering and managing multi-million dollar initiatives. Expertise in Systems Infrastructure Design, Data Center Design, LAN/WAN Network Engineering, Storage Area Networks (SAN), Enterprise Systems, Telecom/Telephony, Satellite Networks and Video Acquisition Systems. Extensive experience in project management, systems operation management, strategic technology planning, process re-engineering, IT budgeting, technology sales, venture capital negotiation and technical documentation. Track record for managing projects that included bleeding edge emerging technologies to develop fully redundant, highly available systems that meet requirements for some of the most challenging infrastructures in the world.
