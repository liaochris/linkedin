Melissa A Benson’s Summary

My name is best known in the gaming industry for creating powerful and imaginative illustrations for many of the top collectible card game (CCG) publishers. I worked with Wizards of the Coast before they became a corporate giant producing Magic: The Gathering in 1993. I created several iconic character designs for that game and did illustrations for many subsequent expansions as well.

I have also worked for White Wolf Publishing when they started Vampire: the Masquerade, for Iron Crown Entertainment's Middle Earth game, for the Dragon Storm game published by Black Dragon Press, the Shadowfist game and Camelot Legends both published by Z-Man Games among others.

I would like be a concept creature designer for characters in the movie industry as well as produce cover art for fantasy novels. I have a couple of themed Tarot decks in the works as well as a couple of fantasy themed books.

Melissa A Benson’s Education

  • Paier College of Art

    BA , Illustration , 1986 — 1989

    A terrific college where most of the instructors are still active in the field which gives you a very relevant education.

    Probably the best known instructor there was Rudy Zallinger who painted the famous dinosaur mural in the Peabody Museum in New Haven CT. Another well known instructor was Ken Davies, a noted Trompe-l'oeil artist.

    His portfolio class was conducted one-on-one where he took on the role of "Art Director" behind the desk and as the "Critic" of your work.
