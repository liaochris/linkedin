Karen Talavera’s Summary

Karen Talavera, owner of Synchronicity Marketing, is a nationally-known digital marketing expert, speaker, trainer, consultant and author of the Enlightened Emarketing blog.

Karen's mission is to help marketers get started with, improve and *leverage* digital channels like email, social media, blogs and mobile. Her philosophy is that when used in conjuction with each other and a solid content marketing strategy, emarketing avenues generate viral momentum, sustained online visibility and increased sales.

She has taught digital/email marketing strategy and best practices to thousands of marketers, business owners and solo-entrepreneurs through the Online Marketing Institute (OMI), the Direct Marketing Association (DMA), the Email Experience Council (EEC), MarketingProfs, the Association of National Advertisers (ANA) and more. Karen is a certified expert with Solo-Entrepreneur (Solo-E) and a member of the invitation-only Only Influencers (formerly Inbox Insiders) group.

Karen provides custom digital/email marketing coaching, seminars, training and workshops for all types of organizations ranging from major corporations like ServiceMaster, Kaiser Permanente, AAA, Applied Materials, and Texas Instruments to trade associations like the Craft & Hobby Association, to private small business owners and entrepreneurs.

If you or your company needs help building, sustaining or expanding customer relationships through online conversation marketing, Karen would like to work with you. She not only helps companies learn how to make their digital marketing perform better, but teaches them how to strategically connect online channels together for maximum emarketing leverage.

It can be done, are you ready to try?

http://twitter.com/SyncMarketing for daily digital marketing insights, research and new tactics.
