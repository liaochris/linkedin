Sandra Picart-Thompson’s Summary

Homefront Diabetic Services has been providing quality diabetic testing supplies to patients nationwide for the past 13 years. What makes us different from any other supplier is our unbeatable "Customer Service". We truly care about our customers. We carry most brands of Blood Glucose Monitors, Test Strips, Lancets.Lancing Device, Control Solutions, and Impotence Device. We offer a wide range of "FREE" meters with very Low Cost Test Strip Prices for those who do not have insurance.
