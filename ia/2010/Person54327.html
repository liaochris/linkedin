Dena A. Lorenzi’s Summary

Dena A. Lorenzi graduated magna cum laude in business from Marquette University. Hard work in a variety of summer and part-time jobs helped finance her education. After graduation, she earned increasingly challenging assignments in financial and marketing positions in five years with Kimberly-Clark, progressing from cost analyst to brand manager with this $16 billion revenue, Fortune 200 global consumer products firm. Moving to Acxiom, America's leading data mining and management firm, headquartered in Arkansas, as marketing team leader for the firm's largest business unit, the Diversified Services Group, she led marketing and development efforts, managing sales support, marketing and promotion for a rapid-developing line of customer intelligence and information products. Dena uses her financial experience to analyze and understand markets. She thrives on creative endeavors while maintaining a focus on the bottom line, building brand, and adding value. While learning in a corporate environment, she has developed and maintained a strong small-business. Dena is passionate about cultivating long-term relationships with her clients by providing them with strong market insights and analytics. Dena's significant portfolio of interpersonal skills and professional experience would be an asset to the creative function of any growing firm.
