About me:

I am an open networker and enjoy connecting people to the right opportunities. LinkedIn is critical to my business. I am an early adopter of technology and web 2.0 applications. I make presentations to job networking groups and individuals interested in understanding how to leverage web 2.0 applications to do the heavy lifting in their job search.

I am passionate about education, particularly K-8 and am an elected official (School Board Secretary) for District 45 in Villa Park, Illinois.

In late 2006, I created a Yahoo Group that helps facilitate career networking amongst Human Resources professionals interested in the Chicago metropolitan HR job market. I am the site owner and moderator of the Chicagoland HR Transition Group. The group consists of close to 1,400 HR professionals.

Membership is free and open to experienced HR professionals (working or in transition). Sorry, no third party search firms or providers of HR services are afforded membership.

If you are an HR professional and would like to become a member of the Chicagoland HR Transition Group, please visit the Chicagoland HR Transition Group website (the link is listed in my profile above).

If you would like to post an HR position to the group, please see my contact information in MY FILES below.
