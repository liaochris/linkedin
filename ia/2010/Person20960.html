Beate Chelette’s Summary

Accomplished executive with domestic and international experience in the creative industry. Decisive, results-oriented leader with entrepreneurial spirit and proven success in creating and launching brands. Has excellent track record of increasing sales and growing bottom line during volatile global market conditions. Excels in new ventures and start-ups.

Consults and develops projects for all spirited business. Assesses the 'is' and maps out the road to the 'need to be'. Well-connected with most major players in the photographic industry; excellent track record; good business sense. Possesses ability to generate cash. Ability to make tough decisions; stays the course. Wrote a business plan and obtained funding backed by the Small Business Administration.

Built a stock photography company that was acquired in 2006 by Corbis, a company privately held by Bill Gates.

Author of "Women Who Want It All, and Get It, Too," introducing ego-RHYTHM. Ego-RHYTHM is a breakthrough method based on the notion that everything happens at the right time. Ego-RHYTHM teaches how to identify which rhythm a person is in, and how to focus on one main event while the rest of one's life ‘happens.’ This knowledge and understanding—that your life’s focus will continue to change according to your own ego-RHYTHM—allows you to overcome daily worries and live a happier life in the now.
