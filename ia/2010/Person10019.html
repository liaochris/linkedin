    Amy B. Cesario arrived at a real estate career from the unusual direction of professional sports management. In 1990 she went to work for the Denver Nuggets durring years when the team was being rebuilt into an NBA powerhouse. The Nuggets applied her energy in two pursuits: Marketing, where she helped coordinate special events, & Recruiting, where she assisted in tracking college talent, then in working to resettle newly recruited players to the Denver area. Cesario found she enjoyed the personal dimension of working with families in transition, as well as orienting clients to the Denver area.

    In 2000, Cesario made her move into real estate. In 2005 she met with Prestige’s management team who recognized her leadership ability, and put her to work building the Cherry Creek office. She is now a broker-owner in the company, manages 50 agents, while finding time to build her own book of business. Cesario is a single mother of a 17-year-old son who attends Bishop Machebeuf High School.
