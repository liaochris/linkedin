James Tierney’s Summary

Jim is the Director of Business Development for Philly.com - online home for The Inquirer, Daily News, "I" Magazine, Phrequency, Student Union 34 and numerous blogs and video series produced by the over 400 journalists who call Philadelphia Media Holdings (PMH) their home. He reports to the VP of Integrated Media Sales, who in turn reports to the CEO and Publisher of PMH.

Jim has a background in financial sales but in September of 2007 joined the local ownership team of PMH as a sales professional for the National Advertising team. He later moved on to manage the department.

In November of 2008, Jim took the helm of the Inquirer's Real Estate Advertising Sales team for a restructuring period. He also assumed the role of Associate Managing Director for the launch and first year and a half of "I" Magazine, The Inquirer's luxury lifestyle publication.

In March 2010 he moved into his current role. He lives in Philadelphia with his wife Anna, a physician, and son James.

Jim's professional interests are currently centered around developing strategies to increase the effectiveness of media content and advertising in their existing online forms and more importantly their mobile future.
