Aaron Schwartz’s Summary

Biography:

Aaron serves as the founder of The Schwartz Consulting Group and is a top contractor for the retail and fashion practices of Karabus Management and PricewaterhouseCoopers. As an industry veteran of retail, marketing, merchandising and sales in the fashion footwear, handbags and accessories sectors, Aaron’s career has included positions as President of Polo Ralph Lauren Footwear, President and Chief Creative Officer for the Bruno Magli Group based in Bologna Italy, and VP/DMM for Women’s Shoes at Federated Department Stores. Earlier in his career he was at Ann Taylor Stores and Macy’s West in various merchandising and executive assignments. Aaron is also a lifetime member of the Two/Ten Footwear Foundation Aaron has lived and worked in Europe as well as the US. He has also been profiled and featured in several consumer magazines and trade publications globally.
