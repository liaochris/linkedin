Toby Marshall’s Summary

Do you provide a great service and add a lot of value to your clients?

Just need more prospects to know about what you do, what you're good at and to seek you out?

Small business marketing is my passion—delivering good sales leads using the power of the internet & harnessing the new goldmine, social networking.

[BTW: I am NOT an ‘open’ networker. Every connection is either someone I have met or they have attended one of my workshops. So my 2nd level connections are good.]

What makes our business very different is an interesting story ...

A story about how we change the lives of young people. And transform companies in the process.

They say you can’t teach old dogs new tricks, but I learnt a beauty when the GFC hit … We were in the boardroom, bit depressed as our clients were in the hardest hit industry, financial services.

Tough times, marketing budgets slashed.

One question then changed our world: “We’ve had lots of work experience students come from overseas, could we find interns from Oz?” Long pause … then, “Why not?”

“We’ve hired 100s of part time uni students for our clients; let's apply the same rigorous hiring process but start them as unpaid interns.”

So we now have a large team, and many of them are now paid staff. The energy of these talented part timers keeps old guys feeling young!

We’ve spent 18 months creating training processes & systems to make them productive. And it’s GREAT for their careers--gaining real experience, finding great jobs.

Importantly, each intern specialises in a narrow field—e.g, one only does Twitter. It's not just for twits: it's a gold mine when used strategically, carefully.

LinkedIn: the biggest goldmine of all, and 2 interns specialise there.

Businesses are sick of marketing consultants who only tell you what to do. With our interns, we implement it, we affordably make it happen.

My new book: "Small Business Internet Marketing", selling well on Amazon.
