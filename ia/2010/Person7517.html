Alex Lirtsman’s Summary

For over 10 years, I’ve worked to make good ideas better. Having experience in leading high-level marketing efforts for national brands and now owning a digital agency, Ready Set Rocket, I have built a career that focuses on innovation, strategy, and measurable results.

My clients value the high-level strategy I bring to the table; helping bring their traditional marketing efforts into a digital world. As brands are connecting more-and-more online, it is more important than ever that their brand engagement is consistent, measurable and adaptive. My strength is in developing and executing digital strategies that result in conversions, growth and substance. My mission is simple: drive profitable, targeted traffic to your brand – then segment, analytize and continually lift conversion rates of that traffic.
