David Brownstein’s Summary

DAVID BROWNSTEIN, PCC, CPCC, is the president and founder of Hollywood Coaching and The Leadership Studio which provide career coaching, executive coaching and custom leadership training for the entertainment industry and other creative businesses.

David was named one of the Top 100 Thought Leaders by Leadership Excellence Magazine, and is an author, certified life coach, workshop leader and business consultant. He was featured in the NY TIMES article on Life-Coaching in Hollywood has been interviewed and featured in Movie Maker, Men's Health, CNN.COM, Japanese Vogue, Fitness, Awareness, Creative Screenwriting and Investors Business Daily. He was interviewed live on “The Coaching Show” and was humorously mentioned on KCRW’s “The Business.”

He has coached creatives, executives, leaders, producers, directors, managers and employees at ABC-TV, NBC-TV, CBS-TV, Sony Pictures, Touchstone Television, Warner Brothers Television, The Walt Disney Company, HBO, E! Entertainment, Bravo, MTV Networks, ICM, William Morris, BBC-TV, British Telecom, Network Rail, Waddel + Reed, IGT, Creative Light Entertainment, Valley Community Clinic, Challenge Day Associates, Chabot College, The Santa Rosa Symphony, and the Environmental Protection Agency.

He is the author of “The Inner Game of Hollywood: How To Stay Inspired and Productive in The Industry That Rocks The World" and is a popular presenter on such topics as "War and Peace in the Writer's Room," "The Essentials of Leadership" and “A Course In Hollywood Miracles.”

David Brownstein’s Specialties:

Executive Coaching, Leadership Coaching and Training. Group and Team Coaching. Facilitation. Workshops. Retreats. Advances. Keynotes.
