Christian Cipriani’s Summary

I am a seasoned journalist and copywriter, and in recent years developed these skills into creative leadership at a number of ad agencies. In 2009 I formed my own company, The Agency Collective, which redesigned the website for the Miami Parking Authority, among other projects. Currently I am servicing a handful of private clients and lending writing and creative support to Nobox Marketing Group.
