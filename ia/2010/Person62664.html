Emily Lux’s Summary

I help pet businesses come up with just-the-right words, marketing tactics and promotional strategies that motivate consumers to try, buy…and buy again. Messaging that’s head turning and click generating. Fresh thinking that’ll rock your ROI.

I'm a strategist who knows how to think creatively – and an imaginative thinker who never loses sight of the strategy. I've been fortunate to work on out-of-the-box, leading-edge, outside-the-lines (choose your metaphor!) programs and campaigns for some of the most iconic brands in the world.

I believe in giving back. I serve on the Board of Directors for the Kerry Blue Terrier Foundation where I oversee fundraising and grant writing for rescue initiatives. I also volunteer my services for numerous animal rescues and nonprofits around the country...and around the world.

Emily Lux’s Specialties:

Promotion idea development, Ads & brochures, Catalogs, Direct response, Product name/tagline generation, Branding/identity development, Product launches, Packaging, Sell sheets, In-store signage & displays (POP, POS), Outdoor media (billboard, etc.), Free-standing inserts, Product descriptions, Proposal/presentation creation, DVD/video/radio scripting, Web, Social media, editing/proofreading
