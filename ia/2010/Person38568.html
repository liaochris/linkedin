Chrissy Blackwell, CPS/CAP, MCAS’s Summary

I have been a member of the staff of the Tuscarawas County, Ohio Convention & Visitors Bureau (CVB) since 2000. My main focus is the methods of interaction the CVB has with group and leisure travelers to the area and local area tourism businesses.

I oversee many of the CVB's communication streams including our website and blog as well as publications the CVB produces: the Tuscarawas County Travel Guide, seasonal Adventures Calendar of Events, the Tuscarawas County Meeting Guide, Tuscarawas Tidbits E-Newsletter, and the bi-monthly Travel Talk Newsletter for local businesses. I have also worked to develop social media as a marketing tool by developing Facebook and Twitter profiles.

In addition to creating positive visitor response, I also assist in the internal office management of the CVB's Tuscarawas County Welcome Center located in Downtown New Philadelphia.
