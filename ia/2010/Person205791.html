Tomasz Ciamulski’s Summary

Multidisciplinary, mixed industrial and research engineering experience from both hardware and software perspective. Experienced different working cultures. Technology consultant in electronics, electromagnetism and computing. Looking for adaptation of modern electronics technologies in different industries.

Special interest in electromagnetic (EM) analysis and high-frequency (HF) phenomena to utilize EM waves and energy (propagation, reflection, energy dissipation) in sensors, heating, drying, medicine etc.

Tomasz Ciamulski’s Specialties:

Expert in EMC/EMI problems, signal integrity, couplings and crosstalks, radiation, resonances, high-speed signal transmission, interconnections, packaging etc.

Specialist in advanced programming (C, C++, assemblers), embedded systems with real-time processing and firmware.

Experienced generalist in RF and microwave techniques, analogue and digital electronics, parallel processing, communication systems (wire and wireless), cryptography.
