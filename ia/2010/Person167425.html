Rachel Stivers’s Summary

Stivers Consulting
I help entrepreneurs and business owners realize their dreams by designing and helping them implement systems and strategies that are simple, effective, and easy to implement and run. For people that get skeeved out by the word “strategies” and other businessy language, basically I help people get stuff done consistently by using simple things that work.

In my business, I use all my finance, business, and people knowledge, along with the other lessons I learned through my 5 years of experience at the worlds best professional services firm (no bias here) and my own personal school of business ownership .

Connect with me on here, check out my website, find me on twitter - and we will figure out if I can help yo too!

DC Divas
I play pro-tackle football for the DC Divas. www.dcdivas.com. We are a premier team in the IWFL (www.iwflsports.com). Go to our website for season and ticket info.

WBIS
I am the Membership Director for WBIS, a business education and networking group in Northern Virgina for women (www.wbis.biz). Come check us out!

Deloitte
I spent my first five post college years at Deloitte. I spent most of my time with D&T on financial services clients (broker-dealers, banks, insurance companies, mortgage companies, etc) including 2.5 years working as a key senior on the “most complex audit in history” according to the New York Times. That statement means nothing to most people, and I might have lost a few cool points by being such a nerd. But to nerdy accountant-types, its pretty cool.

Keller Williams
I decided to “be an entrepreneur,” so I left Deloitte and I went into real estate because I love it and thought I would make millions. I learned a lot then too, including the fact that I don’t want to be a Realtor. If you are a Realtor and reading this, Realtors are awesome, we need them; but I have a different mission. I want to help businesses and brands be as awesome as possible (including Realtors).
