Jan Stittleburg’s Summary

My main focus is interior and exterior architectural photography for residential and commercial designers, builders and architects. I also enjoy product photography for industries as diverse as dog clothing, jewelry, cleaning products, machine tools and electronics.

My graphic design experience in magazine layout and ads, brochures, catalogs, and postcards helps your small business gain market attention.

I am available for tutoring in Photoshop and beginning photography techniques.
