Garett Bugda’s Summary

My story, I’m afraid, is probably one you have heard before. Its about a boy, a military brat who grows up all over the world. He attends 4 high schools in three countries, joins the Marine Corps and ends up meeting the girl of his dreams while working at the Pentagon. The boy and girl fall madly in love and move to Fort Lauderdale. The boy, anxious to explore civilian life, learns of a marketing job and starts as an intern while attending college to become a broadcast journalist.

Flash forward 15 years. The boy, now a father of three who still gets giddy holding hands with the girl of his dreams, is the creative leader for AgencyNet-a full-service digital marketing agency with offices in Fort Lauderdale, New York, Los Angeles and Mexico. His interests have grown to now encompass writing children’s poetry, illustrating, running, singing karaoke and desperately trying to stay one step ahead of his children, but his devotion is to the craft of cultivating consumer-centric creative concepts.

Along side some of the most amazingly talented and intelligent people he has ever known, the boy has once again fallen in love. This time, it is with what is just over the horizon. Stay tuned for the next chapter-who knows, you might just be written into it.
