Steve Rosenbaum’s Summary

Steve Rosenbaum helps companies large and small integrate a profitable sales, marketing and social media networking strategy. Current clients include restaurants, builders, authors, financial companies, stocks and options, manufacturers, realtors, attorneys, webmasters, advertising agencies, radio stations, television stations and consumer products companies.

Steve has been featured in Wall Street Journal, Austin Business Journal and countless websites, newsletters, and ezines. In addition, Steve was one of 6 finalists in the nationwide “Better Your Best” Internet Marketers competition.

Do you have an upcoming speaking engagement?

Steve has presented to groups as large as 500 and regularly conducts online and in-person training and webinars for companies of all sizes. Steve is a proven sales professional and consults with companies and individuals looking to improve their sales and marketing skills both online and offline.

In these challenging times, it is essential that companies go to market with a purpose and with a system. Steve can help you, by utilizing state of the art technology, CRM’s, and automated sales programs to make sure that every lead is followed and nothing falls through the cracks.

Steve can be heard weekly on ESPN Austin radio.
