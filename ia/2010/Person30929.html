Kristin Thayer’s Summary

I worked in the equipment leasing industry for a number of years before venturing out on my own.

During my time working for others, I always found more enjoyment in working with the business owners who were tougher to get approved, including start-up companies.

The vendors I worked with appreciated my passion, personality & communication skills, which allowed me to quickly expand my portfolio to thousands of businesses nation-wide.

When I started my own company, I temporarily left the leasing industry to focus more on working capital needs with an interest in helping restaurants, franchised concepts, medical practices & retail businesses, who may not have the credit or the time in business needed to qualify for a lease or business loan. I finally decided to add equipment leasing to my own company as I realized I just couldn't stay away from it. :-)

Our working capital programs have also been used to purchase equipment, expand, advertise & debt consolidate, just to name a few.
