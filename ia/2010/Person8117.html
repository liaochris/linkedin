Glen Allison’s Summary

Writer and speaker. Author of STILL STANDING TALL, the biography of The Williams Brothers singing group, and the Forte suspense novel series at www.torturedhero.com. He has written for Guideposts, Memphis Magazine, Mississippi Magazine and others. His short story, “The Christmas Monkey,” was chosen for the book CHRISTMAS STORIES FROM THE SOUTH'S BEST WRITERS..
