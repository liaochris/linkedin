Adam Kallish’s Summary

Senior creative director,e-brand strategist & business leader with skills in graphic design, defining user-centric needs development, information architecture, cross-system integration, brand and organizational design. Award winning creative design solutions through focused concepting,integration of content/imagery and media.Particular interest in visualizing information and concepts using new technologies,cross channel media integration in two and three dimensional environments.

An accomplished project manager collaborating with multi-disciplinary design, technology and business strategy. Tested underpinnings with large and small project life cycles (problem definition,concepting/prototyping, deployment and feedback). Developed creative solutions to business consulting with some of the largest and best known digital consultancies.
Active management of distributed delivery teams, program management and integrated delivery linking vision to require- ments to results.Demonstrated entrepreneurial abilities with deep involvement with client development, organization building, and capability development.

Recognized as articulate,proactive practitioner with entrepreneurial abilities that effectively build client relationships with delivery teams by raising the caliber of thinking and final outcomes.
