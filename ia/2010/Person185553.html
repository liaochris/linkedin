Sean Cunningham’s Summary

I am a co-founder of Logic20/20 and serve as its President. I am an experienced CIO, consultant, small business coach and inventor.

My passion is helping firms to become exponentially more efficient and productive through the use of technology. Although technology offers businesses great promise, tech projects are too often unnecessarily complex and difficult to manage.

I enjoy working in all areas of business, including operations (finance, legal & logistics) and technology. I have particular expertise in designing and implementing technology solutions that simplifies company operations and provide transparency. I especially enjoy building and optimizing software development teams to drive much higher levels of success and predictability.

My industry experience includes financial services, mortgages, sales and marketing, manufacturing and general custom development.

The value I offer is a clear vision of how to simplify technology into manageable component parts; professional and clear communication; discipline and focus on each client's issues, and over 16 years of experience at delivering successful projects.

I have brought multiple commercially-successful software products to market, as well as led troubled product line turnarounds.

Our work isn't intrinsically magical, but the net effect of having a well-managed team following a clear methodology where every team member understands expectations and deliverables has produced magical results. It has been used by numerous local, off-shore and hybrid teams to produce great products on predictable timelines.
