Simon Kuo’s Summary

Simon Kuo is a senior executive who is passionate about creating vision, strategy & products for small to multi-billion dollar businesses and who has a demonstrated ability to achieve results, provide superior leadership and apply insight & critical thinking to business problems.

Experienced in strategy, marketing, product management & development, application development, corporate training and startups, he has been directly responsible for developing and managing products that have conservatively generated over $1.5B in revenues; has led the strategy, business planning and competitive intelligence functions for companies and business divisions of companies from $6B to $30B+ in size; has led all web development for a Fortune 65 company and has negotiated agreements worth hundreds of millions to billions of dollars in size. More recently he has been an active board member and has been successful in founding and operating a number of innovative startup companies in diverse areas of business.

He has worked with many well-known brands in these roles including McKinsey & Co, Boston Consulting Group, Alcatel-Lucent, Nokia-Siemens Networks, Accenture, EDS, IBM, Microsoft, Deloitte, John Deere, Altman Vilandrie, PRTM & IDEO.
