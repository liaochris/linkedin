Kenji Yamaguchi’s Summary

My name is Kenji Yamaguchi and I'm a Recruiter for the Windows & Windows Live division here at Microsoft. My groups are responsible for a variety of products & services which really serve to enhance the Windows user experience and keep track of all the online "stuff" you care about. If you've ever used products like Hotmail, Messenger, SkyDrive, or Photo Gallery - you've sampled the effort and hard work my team is passionate about delivering.

As a recruiter - I hire for a variety of different positions such as Software Developers, Developers in Test (Our testers code as much as devs!), and Program Manager's. To do this, I search for candidate strong in computer science fundamentals & problem solving, as well as those with a deep passion for technology and improving the user experience. Social networking is the 'hot-spot' in the market right now and if you want to contribute and help Microsoft make an impact - I'd recommend checking us out!

I'm an open networker and believe there's value in every contact. If you'd like to connect, feel free to send an invite to my email at kenjiy (at) microsoft.com.

Also - I'm on Twitter! //Folllow me @snoobic :)
