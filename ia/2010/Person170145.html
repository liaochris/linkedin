Jessica Hosfeld’s Summary

I worked for The Timken Company as a International Marketing Intern in Colmar, France and Canton, Ohio during college at The University of Akron. During college I studied International Business, Marketing and French. After graduation I worked for The Little Tikes Company as an Associate Product Marketing Manager for the Juvenile Furniture category ($30MM). After the birth of my son in 2005, I took 1 year off and received my real estate license in 2006. Since then I've worked in the REO/Foreclosure market as an Investment Specialist as well as working with first time buyers and listing residential homes. I sold over $500K my first 6 months and $1M my second year in real estate. I currently work for Keller Williams Realty Tri-County Properties. My husband and I personally invest in real estate. I also have my Group Exercise, Boot Camp and Cycling license and teach at my local YMCA.
