Kimberly Hudak Brown’s Summary

Client*Focused I Candidate* Driven I Cost*Effective

c3source is a women-owned clinical staffing firm with over 22 years combined clincial experience. Founded in April 2009, the firm remains committed to the three principles in stellar staffing. Our process is seamless. We are an extension of our clients staffing teams.

We provide top clinical talent on a national as well as international basis. Our industry knowledge coupled with our breadth of experience offers clients the best staffing experience in a timely and professional manner.

Our Clinical Management Team has over 22 years of combined experience in this highly competitive and emerging market. We understand the clinical trials process and deliver professional talent with a sense of knowledge, urgency and commitment.

We are the 'source' for clinical staffing. We are the 'source' for finding the best full-time and contract employees. We take ownership of each and every candidate to deliver the best staffing relationship for our clients.

c3source is experienced in many therapeutic areas to include: Oncology, Respiratory, Obesity, Cardiovascular, Vaccines, Central Nervous System, Diabetes, Immunology, Infectious Diseases, Gastroenterology and Medical Devices.
