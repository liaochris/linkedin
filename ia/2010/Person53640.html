Deborah Lockridge’s Summary

Writer and editor with 24 years of experience in trade publishing/B-to-B media, including trucking, construction, farming and medical industries. Currently Editor for Newport Communications' Heavy Duty Trucking and Heavy Duty Aftermarket Journal magazines, as well as Truckinginfo.com.

Local web site and blog, www.bhamdining.com, offers listings, reviews and commentary on locally owned restaurants, food and wine in the Birmingham, Alabama, area.
