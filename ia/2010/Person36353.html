David Chartier’s Summary

I have over 17 years experience telling stories, selling products and evolving brands, with both small and large teams for advertising, network branding, interactive, web and big video game titles. I am hands-on but also very comfortable managing teams and I thoroughly enjoy a healthy challenge in whatever I am doing.

As a hands-on Designer & Director, I have strong experience with most relevant software (ex. CS4, 3DS Max, Maya, Motionbuilder, FCP, Flash, Dreamweaver, et al.), plus years of experience doing written + visual treatments, style guides, as well as casting & directing actors and stunt performances for shoots and motion capture, all with an eye for the 'bottom line'.

I am looking to work with good people, inspired teams, juicy projects, maybe have some fun & learn something new along the way.
