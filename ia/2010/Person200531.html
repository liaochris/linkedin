Andrew Hermanson’s Summary

From 1978 beggining as a warehouse supervisor moving to inside customer service and telesales to purchasing and inventory control eventually moving on to outside selling then negotiating with international suppliers. Been in the packaging business my entire life. If it relates to commodity packaging supplies, Ive handled it, bought it, counted it sold it and now I eat and breath it.

Andrew Hermanson’s Specialties:

Sales and marketing of carton sealing tapes, stretch films, polyethylene and polypropylene films and bags, foam, PP and polyesther strapping, can liners and a myriad of other packaging items. Overseeing importing and exporting of all of the same from Eastern Europe, Taiwan and mainland China as well as South America.
