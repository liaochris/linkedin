Debra Simpson’s Summary

I wanted to start a business I could run from my laptop anywhere I was in the world. Syndicating Your Content is for digital product creation. North San Diego Business is an online community in North San Diego County.

My programming experience offers me the opportunity to create a web presence for a small business owner that is search engine friendly and user friendly. I pride myself on offering great value at reasonable small business prices. I employ guerrilla marketing strategies, helping small business owners leverage their businesses over the internet.
