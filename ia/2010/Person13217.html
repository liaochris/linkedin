Angel Lorente, MBA’s Summary

FindAFitnessPro.com matches our network of fitness professionals with prospective clients that have registered on our website looking to hire a fitness trainer. All client leads are prequalified by our team before we assign them to you.

What we look for in a fitness pro?
We are looking for U.S. fitness professionals that are serious about helping their clients achieve their fitness goals.

To join our network you need to have experience in multiple fitness training specialties, certified by one or more certification agency, fully insured, and MUST pass a background check.

We want to take the guesswork out of finding the right fitness pro for the client so that they can focus on getting in the best shape of their life.

About Angel Lorente:
Angel Lorente is the founder and owner of FindAFitnessPro.com. In May 2007, Angel suffered a medical scare that required him to lose weight and get in shape. Initially, he struggled to find a fitness professional that would give him the advice, direction, and motivation he needed to achieve his fitness goals. Based on the challenges he faced to get fit, Angel created and launched FindAFitnessPro.com to help people find and hire prescreened, insured, and certified fitness professionals that are serious about helping clients achieve their fitness goals.
