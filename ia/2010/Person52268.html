David Wasserman’s Summary

Beverage caterer interested in impressing clients and guests as our company brings artisan drink preparation to an area where such quality is often overlooked. Our drinks taste even better than they look, and all of our ingredients and business dealing is forward thinking: using organically grown coffee, tea, and espresso, post consumer recycled content paper products, PLA (plastic) cold cups made from corn, and much, much more.

Our staff is professional, engaging, and fun to work with. Joes on the Nose impresses clients, giving something to enjoy and remember at events. We encourage all to improve on a "standard" coffee service and deliver something new and different. Our baristas pay attention to every drink they make and take care of every guest.

Our Organic Espresso Bar is a great addition to any event- weddings, corporate meetings, trade shows, hospitality suites, office visit, convention, bar & bat mitzvahs, we love it all.
