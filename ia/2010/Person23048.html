Beverly Cornell (LION)’s Summary

I am a globally minded & passionate person, who believes that we are citizens of a very diverse world. I look for ways to connect with people & build relationships. These are fundamental human activities that reach across borders, cultures & languages.

I am so excited about my career, job & company. I think I am one of the lucky few who have figured out how to make a personal passion generate revenue. My experiences living in both Alicante, Spain and Brasilia, Brasil have helped me to understand what is is like to not know a foreign language or culture but to be embraced by the local people. I have many fond memories of the familes I lived with & the people that I met from these experiences. I am blessed to still call them my families all these years later.

Working in the online foreign language learning industry is always interesting. We specialize in a complete foreign language program that teaches you vocabulary, grammar and culture all in a fun and easy program. Our linguists and teachers are located around the world and specialize in many different languages my day is never the same from one to the next. I am proud to be a part of a young and fun tech company who helps people communicate in foreign languages.

I am an avid user of Social Media & Web 2.0 technologies. As a person born on the cusp of Generation X & Y - I am fortunate enough to understand & embrace this new frontier. I see many advantages for global businesses or those that want to take their companies across borders to use the newer technologies of Skype, Blogging, Podcasting, Social Networking & many others to integrate their content & marketing to create a VERY powerful message and conversation with their clients, customers, employees & partners.
