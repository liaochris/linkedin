Joe Kovacs’s Summary

I have more than 10 years of public relations and marketing experience in the for-, nonprofit and public sectors. My core strengths include writing and editing, strategy, media relations, and program management. In my current role as grants and communications specialist at Adams 14, my goal is to maximize fundraising support for the District's educational programming and facilitate strong relations between the District, the local community of Commerce City, CO and other stakeholders in the success of our students.

Joe Kovacs’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, public relations, integrated marketing communications
