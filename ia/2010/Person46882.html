Dan Humphrey (danh@richgroupusa.com)’s Summary

The Richmond Group USA
Helping People and Companies Manage Change

Led by Dan Humphrey, The Construction Equipment Recruiting Division has been successfully serving as a trusted partner to both companies and the individuals who work within the Construction Equipment Industry.

Built on a foundation of caring about the people and the success of the companies he works with, Dan’s division has assisted in the growth of organizations such as Foley Equipment, Flint Equipment Co., and Linder Industrial Machinery by adding impact players that can drive bottom line numbers. His philosophy of “being a strategic advisor, not a vendor” has created strong open lines of communication. Defining an assignment by the "top three skills" on a job description is not an option instead focusing on what talent is required to position the company to accomplish their goals and get results. His team is committed to facilitating win/win relationships that drive continued success of the CE industry.

Dan's recruiting career began in 2005 although he has a foundation in helping people. Starting in nursing and nurse administration in the Navy, he transferred his desire to make people’s lives better into a successful sales and sales management career. As a result, Dan was named “Rookie of the Year” for TRG in 2005. His passionate and energetic team is driven to make a difference. TRG’s 40+ years as a global leader and recognized expert in recruiting, has given the CE Division its’ path to continued success. All conversations are held in the strictest confidence and he welcomes the opportunity to serve as your strategic advisor and partner with clients and candidates.

“Digging up the Talent that Powers Success”
