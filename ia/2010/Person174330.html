Rob Clinton’s Summary

Rob Clinton is the founder of 180 Career Coaching. He is a motivated visionary that specializes in guiding people who have a desire or need to move towards that next level in their life and career. He has a deep heart for inspiring people to fully live their lives. He’s all about the self discovery and helping people to draw forth that inner self, or at least set them on a path for finding their own unique calling and purpose.

He believes that everyone has some sort of desire and purpose that is driven by an all pervading Source, and knows this Source on a very personal and passionate relationship to be God.


Rob’s ultimate dream is to make a great contribution to the harmonic unity of eventually peace on earth, and feels that his entry point for making this happen is by connecting others with their own unique God given gifts and talents to keep advancing the progress of humanity into perfect freedom.


Rob is a member of the 48Days Coaching Connection at www.48days.net, and is endorsed by Dan Miller – author of 48 Days to the Work You Love – and believes whole heartedly in the 48 days process. Along with Rob’s message for finding purposeful work he also incorporates the 48 days teachings with his message. Rob is also a member of the Free Agent Academy, a group of highly motivated entrepreneurs who desire only but to be masters of their own destiny.
