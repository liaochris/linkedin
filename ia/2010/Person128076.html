Lou Sagar’s Summary

Social Web/Brand Strategist working with clients to apply social networking platforms for greater e-commerce performance, and deeper engagement with customers. Particularly interested in real-time hyperlocal news delivery, store-based retailing, and emergence of social retailing e-commerce models online. Highlights include recent assignments with Zappos, Etsy, and Dean&Deluca. Previously, built world class home furnishings and lifestyle specialty retailer operating under the brand name of "Zona" with nine stores and wholesale operations around the world. Flagship store was based in Soho, New York, representing innovative and highly influential approach to lifestyle merchandising, and product development. Between 1998, and 2007, built boutique consultative practice for licensing, product development, and use of e-commerce and web-based technologies for brand extensions.
