Nadia Brannon’s Summary

Nadia Brannon specializes in the areas of damages quantification, complex data and statistical analysis, large-scale electronic data discovery, data conversion, database development and data warehousing. As a litigation consultant with over 15 years of experience, Ms. Brannon advises counsel in a variety of class actions, complex litigation matters and financial investigations including consumer class actions, wage and hour disputes, breach of contract, employment discrimination matters, personal injury and wrongful termination matters, insurance claims, fraud and forensic accounting investigations, etc.

Ms. Brannon led teams of data analysts and programmers in a number cases involving class certification issues, liability and damages assessment that required analysis of large amounts of electronic data, sampling and statistical analysis, as well as the development and design of specialized databases and applications. Ms. Brannon is a certified SAS programmer.

Nadia Brannon’s Specialties:

Commercial damages, data analytics, statistical analysis, liability and class certification, labor and employment, wage and hour, labor damages analysis, lost profits and unjust enrichment, tort damages, business interruption claims
