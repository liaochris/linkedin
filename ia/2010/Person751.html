Aaron Berndt’s Summary

I have 6+ years of experience working with venture backed high growth companies in both technical engineering and operational project management roles. I joined the startup firm Adam Aircraft when the company’s total head count was 85 and was a part of the core team responsible for its expansion to over 900 employees.

I came to Haas to build a solid general management skill set as well as transition into energy and cleantech. To date I have completed projects with Serious Materials, Tesla Motors, and Silver Spring Networks. I have strong interest in energy management systems, smart grid technologies, and solar companies.

As an engineer I have a strong passion for understanding how things work and utilize my technical skills to solve problems. As a leader I am very skilled at managing complex cross-functional projects in high growth environments.

I am looking for a big, exciting project to join.

I hold a BME, Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering from the University of Minnesota and I am currently pursuing an M.B.A from the Haas School of Business at U.C. Berkeley, where I am a Mayfield Fellow.
