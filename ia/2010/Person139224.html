Dwayne McDorman’s Summary

My team and I provide for FREE, an Educational Money Management Seminar to Companies or Organizations and Churches who want to benefit their communities, help people better plan their giving, highlight common responsibilities to be fiscally fit, and find solutions to money problems. We can accomplish the implementation phase of tactical, and strategical programs like Dave Ramsey, and Crown Ministry by providing a customized, COMPLEMENTARY Financial Needs Analysis for each person upon request.
Some of the questions this seminar helps answer:
* Do I owe too much?
* What is the most efficient way to get rid of my debt?
* How can I free up money to save?
* Do I have enough - or the right kind of - life insurance?
* How much should I save to meet my children's educational expenses?

MY PHILOSOPHY:
Most people today dream of 2 things: to be financially secure and to have a career or business they love. These 2 dreams can be achieved together or independently, yet many people today find themselves financially overextended and stuck in a job they hate. I specialize in giving people an opportunity to go into business for themselves, without all the risks of going at it alone; To attain those seemingly "impossible" dreams - to own their own business, and to take control of their financial destiny. As a man of faith, I realize God's principals of money and possessions touch the lives of everyone. I'm looking for people who want something better for their lives, people who have a desire to make a difference. I’m looking for people who want to practice God's principals of finances and who need to capture the vision for their family. People who want to control their own destiny by God's grace, because what we do today has a major impact on eternity. The daily choices we make regarding money and possessions are of eternal consequence. A.W. Tozer said it best, “Listen to no man who has not listened to God.”
