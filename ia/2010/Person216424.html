Michelle Maria Nicholas’s Summary

 Since 1991, I have worked in some capacity with for profit and or nonprofit corporations providing middle to upper level office administration and management support for departments and their Executives, Directors, Vice Presidents, department heads and Managers. I fulfilled my job function in the capacity of an Executive Secretary, Administrative Assistant, Administrative Secretary, and or Office Manager/Office Administrator.

 My work experience in corporate administration & management included having responsibility for office operations logistics, screening telephone calls and being the gate keeper for walk in visitors; membership database administration; conference call coordination, office supplies and equipment inventory management; human resource administration including staff benefit, personnel record management, recruitment and selection, new hire reporting, employee orientation, payroll processing and paycheck distribution, accounts payable, accounts receivable, and event planning.

 In my capacity as Executive Secretary, Administrative Assistant, Administrative Secretary, and or Office Manager/Office Administrator, I also was responsible for coordinating travel arrangements, calendar management and appointment setting as well as expense reimbursement and various other adhoc administrative tasks.

Professionally, I am a possibilities thinker, a problem solver and a solutions provider with a passion for technology and business automation. I am out going, result driven, people and detail oriented, proactive, organized, systematic and an articulate individual who enjoys working in an environment that encourages teamwork.

I also possess computer skills and a working knowledge of MS Office suite software, QuickBooks Pro, as well as other computer software applications. I am passionate about knowledge and I am constantly researching ways to develop my skills. I consider myself to be a "Possibilities Thinker"
