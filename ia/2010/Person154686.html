Nicole Marie Richardson’s Summary

Nicole Marie Richardson is a seasoned writer and editor, working in the journalism industry for more than nine years. She is currently the senior editor for special projects at Inc.com, the small business resource for entrepreneurs.

Some recent projects include:

- The Business Owner's Social Media Toolkit: A complete resource on how to use social media, including blogging, social networking, videos, podcasts, mobile marketing, and online reviews, to reinvent and drive your business.
http://bit.ly/68aqDO

- The 2009 Inc. 500|5000: Inc.'s exclusive listing of the 5,000 fastest-growing, privately-owned businesses in America, including fascinating features on how they did it, profiles on each company, and statistics on this unique demographic.
http://bit.ly/17P8dQ

Previously she was the news and entertainment editor at Black Enterprise magazine. There she was responsible for developing, writing, and editing Newspoints, the section of the magazine that covers business news and politics. Richardson contributed regularly to the magazine’s cover stories with topics ranging from pastors as CEOs to interviews with Sen. Barack Obama and actor Will Smith.

Richardson is a graduate of Rutgers University where she double-majored in biology and journalism and minored in African Studies. She received her master’s degree in journalism, with a concentration in political reporting and new media, from the University of Maryland, College Park.

She also freelances for Black Entertainment Television (BET), Forbes, Jungle Media Group, Essence and Mediabistro.com. She resides in New York City.

Please contact me for freelance opportunities that meet or challenge my skill set. I look forward to hearing from you!
