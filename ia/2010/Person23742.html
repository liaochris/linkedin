Bill Erickson’s Summary

In 2003, I combined my passion for graphic design and business by starting my own web design company. Since then I've been an entrepreneur with a focus on the tech industry.

WordPress Consulting has been my main focus since 2006, along with a few startups I've been involved with.

In 2007, I gathered a group of local entrepreneurs together and we started The Creative Space, a coworking office in Downtown Bryan. It was the first coworking space in Texas.

I am also one of the founders of the BIL Conference, a free unconference run in conjunction with TED.
