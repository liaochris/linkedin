Karen Rager-Takeda’s Summary

Results-oriented professional with over fifteen year’s experience in recruitment consulting, providing business solutions and customer relationship management.

Rager Resources Group, LLC is an international executive search firm. We specialize in placing top professionals in the areas of accounting, finance and IT in direct hire and on a special project basis. Based in Orange County, we take a consultative approach with our clients and candidates.

We focus our business in directly recruiting for candidates and developing relationships to continually build a network of experienced professionals with connections inside the top employers to work for. Our client business partners would much rather work with people already successful in their industry.
