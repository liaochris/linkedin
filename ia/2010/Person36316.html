Charly Lammers van Toorenburg’s Summary

PR and PR 2.0 consultant with over 20 years of experience in communications. Since 1994 self employed. Now owning PR agencies in Bunnik (Utrecht), Brussels and Amsterdam, employing around 40 people. Over the years, we worked for market leaders, start-ups, multi-nationals and local companies.

Charly Lammers van Toorenburg’s Specialties:

Lammers van Toorenburg Benelux PR is a leading Benelux based privately owned PR and PR 2.0 company. LVTPR is specialised in the fields of ICT (the largest specialist in the Benelux), Consumer products, luxury and utilities (our fastest growing division), Retail and financial services (servicing market leading companies) and professional services and logistics. (www.lvtpr.com).
