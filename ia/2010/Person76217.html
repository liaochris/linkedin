Edward Johnson’s Summary

Global Furniture Installation Inc. company serving Georgia and the greater South Eastern United States. We work primarily through office furniture dealers and with end users who use us again and again. Our staff is the best in the business and are driven by solid field and operations management group including project management services. All installations are not complete until they pass a post-installation punch list. This includes a fit and function check with a thorough cleaning of the installation and work area. We value our repeat customers as if they were new customers. Since conception, we have been establishing a solid foundation upon which to build a future for continued growth. Today, our customers are expecting higher levels of quality at competitive price.
