Joe Wiggins’s Summary

I founded Perfect Circle PR in 2010 to help financial services companies enhance and maintain their reputations via traditional and digital media.

I have worked in financial services public relations for many years, both agency and client-side. My previous roles for Legal & General, Santander and Nationwide Building Society involved managing relationships with key media, acting as a spokesperson and managing strategic, proactive PR campaigns.

One of my key achievements has been to successfully kick-start Legal & General's activity in social media, including establishing an audit, monitoring facilities and proactive outreach. I have also been instrumental in establishing support from Compliance and the commencement of social media 'guidelines' for staff.

I have experience of PR and communications in most areas of financial services, but particularly mortgages, banking, insurance and protection.

In 2009, my PR campaigns achieved more news coverage for Legal & General in key trade magazines than any other protection provider. This included the highest proportion of favourable coverage amongst key competitors. (Source: Precise Media). I was soley responsible for this PR coverage.

Joe Wiggins’s Specialties:

Media relations, being a media spokesperson, social/ digital media, social networking, copywriting, generating news coverage, events and awards, strategic planning, integrated campaigns, news release writing and placement, bylined articles, enhancing positive PR and managing reputation risks.
