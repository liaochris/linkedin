Itzik Ben-Bassat’s Summary

An online and gaming industry veteran and entrepreneur, Itzik Ben-Bassat is the chairman and co-founder of GameGround (gameground.com). Throughout a career typified by innovation, Ben-Bassat architected some of the first Games-on-Demand services and was a key member of the executive team that transformed Blizzard Entertainment (World of Warcraft, Starcraft & Diablo) from a game development studio to a global leader in online entertainment. At Blizzard, Itzik was responsible for a wide range of global planning and business activities, representing over $500MM in revenue. He spearheaded the company’s international presence, establishing and managing offices in Europe, Korea, China, & Taiwan.

Today, in addition to his work with GameGround Itzik is an adviser to Insight Venture Partners, on current asset management and new investments/acquisitions in the gaming and web 2.0 spaces. He is also an active board member at Wix.com where he has helped the consumer-based web development company to achieve fast growth and adopt a successful premium subscription model.

Ben-Bassat co-founded GameGround (www.gameground.com) with the goal of giving gamers a better way to play and progress in their games, cross-platform. GameGround offers its users a single, consolidated space to track and be rewarded for their gaming no matter where they play. The company is backed by Sequoia Capital and additional unannounced leading investors. Currently in a closed beta, GameGround looks to launch its open beta later this year
