Michael J. LeBlanc’s Summary

Group LeBlanc began as MJL Resources in 1991 when Michael J. LeBlanc (Mike) started "connecting people".

Mike owned restaurants in the 1990s, then worked for the Intertek Group for 14 years, where he retired in July of 2009 to focus on Group LeBlanc.

In his corporate role, Mike was General Director of Intertek in Mexico where he put a solid team together of over 1,000 employees and contractors. Mike and his team increased profit by millions of dollars. Mike was also a member and a leader of various global teams in the organization over the years.

Since the age of 21, Mike has enjoyed networking and business consulting. His consulting business was only advertised by word of mouth until 2009, when Mike won 2 large contracts.

Mike has been fortunate enough to build trust and friendship with hundreds of top executives and global leaders over the years. A network that reaches inner circles including: CEOs for some of the world's largest corporations, some of the world's wealthiest individuals and Presidents of various countries - including many Senators, Representatives and Governors in the United States.

Today, Mike is now focusing on his company, Group LeBlanc, which is the parent company to various other companies in the following fields: Business Brokering, Business Consulting, Mergers and Acquisitions, Venture Capital Investments, Construction, Total HomeCare Services, Real Estate Investments and Rentals, Energy Reduction, Personal and Corporate Investment and Financial Planning and Personal Growth/Motivational Speaking.

Mike and his team at Group LeBlanc have recently received an honorable award from an association of North American business and government leaders. The presentation of this award will be announced soon.

With the press covering this historic event at Times Square in New York City, Group LeBlanc will use this date as their official public launch date.
