Karen Langston Nutritionist Holistic Nutritionist’s Summary

Karen Langston, Nutritionist, Functional Medicine Specialist and a Life Style Educator,is also a Nutrition coach, author and creator of many different nutrition, health, wellness and emotional well being programs.
Karen developed the most incredible Wellness program and from this came the Ultimate Corporate health and wellness program.

Karen also developed and implemented, along with Dr. Phil Walsh in Toronto, and Lance Ware in Florida, an unique approach to releasing unwanted old emotions that hold one back from obtaining great achievements, and replace with positive, useful techniques that puts one back in control and on the path to greatness
Karen is a founding partner in The Ultimate Health and Wealth Network, promoting a functional health and wellness program for small and mid-sized businesses and corporations.
As a Nutritionist, naturally, food is her my primary focus.
Educating and inspiring is her passion, and believes in empowering others to take responsibility for their own health by providing the tools and resources to make informed decisions.
Karen is a public speaker dividing her time between presentations for a specific audiences, health, wellness and corporate venues. She also has an exclusive private practice working one-on-one with those seeking a healthier, more satisfying lifestyle.

Karen Langston Nutritionist is available for all media venues, public speaking, group and individual consultation.
