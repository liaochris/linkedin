Greg Fegley’s Summary

Operations Executive / General Manager

Generates VALUE by improving operating performance throughout an organization. Identifies key strategic and tactical initiatives and creates a structure and culture of accountability. His passion is leading, developing and empowering people to achieve results.

Greg has a broad management background with significant accomplishments delivering revenue, managing projects, and supporting clients. He is effective with both internal and external clients at all levels and delivering results through cross-functional teams.

He has also led restructuring and investment activities to fund growth and reduce costs, increasing profit by optimizing multi-company integrations. Greg has managed both P&L and operating functions for domestic and international divisions where they delivered high quality mission critical software, hardware and services to Fortune 500 companies globally.

Greg Fegley’s Specialties:

Finding hidden revenue and cost reduction opportunities. Getting the best out of people and team building. Organizing and managing processes and people to achieve results. Enterprise-wide management from sales forecasting through delivery fulfillment including customer support. Effective dealing with customers, internally and externally, and suppliers at all levels. Merger & Acquisition due diligence and consolidation of operations. Contract negotiations from buying and selling perspective.
