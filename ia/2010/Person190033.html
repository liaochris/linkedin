Simon Melaniphy’s Summary

Passion and skill are the two most important attributes to have in your work, and mine are business and digital.

Since 2003 I have been building Refreshed Media into one of the key digital marketing agencies in the South West, and as MD have been instrumental in taking the business from 2 to 15 staff, turning over £2m, being part of a £40m group and boasting a client list including Comet, Cunard and RM.

I have very strong communication skills and the ability to read people well – useful traits when pitching and speaking, such as at Business Link events. I have also played a significant part in product development, managing and recruiting staff, finance, legal and acquisition.

Prior to starting Refreshed Media I have setup and run previous businesses including a DJ agency and a web design agency.

My BSc Hons 2:1 degree was in Computer Science/Software Engineering which has given me a solid understanding in how business ideas can be implemented through web technologies.

In previous years my passion for music led me through a number of valuable experiences including event management and promotion, A&R and Licensing for a Sydney based record label, as well as a very successful club and radio DJ career with audiences of up to 100,000 on-air in Australia.
