John Lefferts’s Summary

John Lefferts is an award winning senior executive with a proven track record of leading and developing people while delivering exceptional results in the financial services industry. He has a unique ability to create and instill vision in an organization and is best known for leading transformational change. Look to John for leading growth, identifying/building talent and leading organizational change and transformation. Summary career highlights include:

•Crisis Manager: Cut $50 million as President/CEO of AXA Advisors, navigating the post 9/11 market downturn while simultaneously increasing 7,000 advisor field force productivity to all time highs

•Leader: Personally recruited, trained and developed the leading advisors and managers in the industry and is sought after domestically and internationally to teach and train on his systems for success.

•Transformational: Successfully led a project to transform sales force into fee based advisors changing licensing, training, products, distribution processes, compensation and recognition

•Integrator: Led team to integrate MONY sales force with AXA sales force after acquisition

•Student & Teacher: Obtained CFP, CLU and ChFC professional designations in early career while writing articles, giving speeches and consulting within the industry later in career

•Winner: As District Manager (#1 out of 600), Branch Manager (#1 out of 90), Regional President (#1 out of 18) and President, led each organization to the leading position in the business.

With new economics in the business, a shift in consumer behavior and financial re-regulation, few executves are as qualified and prepared to lead transformational change than John.

John Lefferts’s Specialties:

Organzational turnarounds, Strategic Planning, Sales, Recruiting, Marketing, Team Building. Adept at leading/collaborating with cost control, compliance, HR, legal, training and sales support. Holds FINRA Series 7, 24, 65 and insurance licences. Earned CFP, CHFC and CLU professional degrees.
