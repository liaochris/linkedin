Coree Francisco’s Summary

In the past 9 years, Coree's dedication of support into the interactive media industry in Manitoba and Nationally has recently been honored as 2009 Manitoba Woman Entrepreneur of the Year Award, for her Contribution to Community.

Through involvement in associations, education and with interactive companies including her own company Girlone Interactive, Coree has been responsible for the project management of over $5.1 million in interactive media budgets.

From a solid reputation as a hard working entrepreneur ready to take on new challenges while training new comers, Coree is consistently is asked as a guest to many galas including 2009 Woman of Distinction Awards sitting with the Honorable Jim Rondeau Minister.

Coree Francisco’s Specialties:

Strategic thinker, entrepreneur, outstanding project manager and excellent trainer, teacher and advisor.
