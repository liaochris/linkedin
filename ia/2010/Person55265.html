Currently looking for a strong producer and manager to join our team as we showcase our new screenplay - a series that fills the niche for a Mad Men-type deficiency at any network. If you are looking for your own high-end Mad Men series with the humor and pace of Entourage, please contact me for a synopsis and script or download it here: http://designshrine.net/LitLife_synopsis.pdf.

Contact me at ty.wenzel@designshrine.net or at the telephone number above if you would like me to send you the pilot script.

Written by myself and Kurt Wenzel - we have four published books (Random House, Little Brown & Co, St. Martin's Press).

Fashion Quest (Producer, Host), Behind Bars (Producer, Writer), Behind Bars: The Straight-Up Tales of a Big City Bartender (Author, St. Martin's Books). Written several screenplays.

Motivated professional writer and producer with multiple levels of experience that ranges from published author of books, television producer, magazine editor and article writer.

Flexible and versatile – able to maintain a sense of humor under pressure. Poised with demonstrated ability to easily transcend cultural differences. Thrive in deadline-driven atmospheres. Second novel in the can.

Formerly a theater actress with a bent towards Shakespeare. Co-Founder of the Zzyzx Studio Theater Group, NYC.
