John Krech’s Summary

My name is John Krech and I am the founder of ePhiphony Incorporated and the inventor of our patent pending products Phitch and our new SaaS product RightOn Inventory Order Management..

Having the right amount of inventory is a big headache. Fact, the bottom 30% performing businesses have NINE times more inventory than their top 20% performing peers. Furthermore, they are SEVEN times more likely to be in a stockout situation. In other words, businesses often have too much of what they do not need and too little of what they do need. This is because today’s accounting and ERP platforms make it difficult to order the right amount of inventory at the right time. That is why we developed RightOn Inventory Order Management.

We provide online inventory order management services to make your small business inventory management easy. You can create purchase orders online in minutes. Our built-in calculator instantly evaluates over one dozen key variables to determine how much to order.

RightOn Inventory Order Management automatically sets reorder points. Just click to set your reorder points electronically so that you do not run out of stock..

Our online order management service provides the advantages of inventory management software, with none of the hassles. No inventory software to install. No guess work and no outdated business models that were developed in 1913 that no longer fit the world of business today.

Additional features include our email alerts to automate your order management schedule, reports and charts to track how much you have saved, and our anytime, anywhere access that lets you do order management online while on the road, from any computer.

Phitch has been featured in Finance & Commerce, AOL Money & Finance, PC Magazine, The Progressive Accountant, SBDCNet, and Enterprise Minnesota.
