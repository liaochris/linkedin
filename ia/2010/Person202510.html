Diana Foster’s Summary

For me, life is a picture. I know, down to my very core, that every picture has a story. I see stories everywhere I go, and take great pleasure in greeting others (through my art and my sassy self) with snapshots of beauty, and on a really good day, wisdom. I have been expressing myself through art since birth, (you should see my Self-portrait in Diaper!)

I have been a teacher and mother for most of my adult life, and I can cut, double-stick tape, or photograph my way out any problem, and into your heart. Not only am I a published artist, card maker, and photographer, but I have a passion and knack for making everything—every thing—beautiful.
