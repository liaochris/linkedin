Lael Hazan’s Summary

I am the contributing editor of Saveur.com. In addition I assist my husband Giuliano Hazan in his ventures and have a wonderful time teaching people of all ages at our cooking school in Verona Italy, I currently blog for Sarasota Magazine, educatedpalate.net/blog; and write for Edible Sarasota magazine.

I am also a programmer on WSLR, 96.5 fm, and host FOCUS ON FABULOUS FOOD. Listen every other Wednesday at 10:00 am. We stream live www.wslr.org
