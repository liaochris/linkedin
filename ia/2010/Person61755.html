Eddie Neiman’s Summary

28 years in the mortgage banking industry

Eddie Neiman, Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist with Professional Financial Solutions/Guild Mortgage has over 25 years experience in the mortgage banking industry as a Financial Planner specializing in mortgage debt management. His focus is to integrate his client’s mortgage plans with their overall financial plans and have those plans be in concert with the client’s life plan. Eddie takes the “Client for Life” approach to his business. Hundreds of satisfied clients are using or reusing his services for the second, third and even fourth time. Client referrals account for over 85% of his business, while the remaining 15% is a result of referrals from his strategic partnerships with key Realtors, Financial Planners, CPAs, Attorneys and other related professionals. Eddie’s goals are simple: To make you a “Client for Life” by providing you with professional, high-quality service and valuable financial planning advice focused on mortgage debt management, for now and in the future.

Eddie Neiman’s Specialties:

Mortgage Originator that takes a financial planning approach to managing mortgage debt; integrating the mortgage plan with the financial plan making sure they are in concert with the life plan.
