Lawrence Freese’s Summary

Zenovation's mission is to provide high-quality home renovation. Service is our hallmark, and effective project management is the key to our success.

We take away the stress of a remodel by developing a clear plan for your project, then managing it to completion. Before a hammer hits a nail, we provide you with a comprehensive Estimate detailing all costs, as well as a project schedule.

By partnering with skilled and creative designers, architects and tradespeople, Zenovation is able to provide the depth of experience and knowledge found at large design/build companies at a competitive price.

Our clients tell us time and again that they appreciate our professionalism, level of quality, and clear communication about costs and project duration.

Service is what makes working with Zenovation such an enlightening experience.

Zenovation was created in 2006 as a partnership with Brian Crawford (now of Dwelling Renovation). In 2007 the partners decided to pursue different business strategies, and Lawrence Freese became the principal of Zenovation.

Prior to Zenovation, Lawrence ran Circe Services, Inc., a professional services company (2001-2006) with his wife, Rebecca Hoffenberg, then Barka Lounge Playcare, a doggy daycare (2004-2006) in Portland, OR.

In 2006, Barka Lounge Playcare was sold and Circe Services, Inc. concluded business to allow Lawrence to focus on home remodeling.

In his spare time, Lawrence enjoys cycling, hiking and cooking with his wife and two sons, Paolo and Mateo.

Lawrence Freese’s Specialties:
