Barak Paz Tal’s Summary

Barak has over 11 years experience in the Internet and Mobile Phone industries in Mainland China and Israel. He Co-founded and managed two internet start-ups in China: www.meijob.com in 2006, now one of the leading job search engines in China and www.jipingmi.com in 2007, now one of the leading property search engine in China, and serves on the Board of Directors of both companies. Barak's main expertise is General Management, Marketing, Investor Relations, and Online Product Management.

Prior to Meijob, Barak was Customer Marketing Manager at Orange Israel (PTNR). Barak led the company to reduce its customer churn rate from 16% to 7% and increased its mobile internet revenues from 2M$ to 23M$ within 2 years. Barak holds an MBA in Marketing and Finance and BA in Economics and Management from Tel Aviv University.
