Yoshifumi Yamamoto’s Summary

Yoshifumi Yamamoto is an earthquake engineer with a solid background in strong motion seismology, structural analysis, mathematics, and statistics. He focuses on artificial ground motions as input ground motions for strucutural analysis, especially time-frequency characteristics of artificial ground motions.
His programming skills are very strong in Matlab, R, Fortran, Java, C#, VB, vba, GMT, PHP, Perl, and xml.
He has industry experience in erathquake evaluation, structrual analysis, soil and structure interaction, high performance computing, and grid computing.

Yoshifumi Yamamoto’s Specialties:

Earthquake modeling, earthquake engineering, time-frequency analysis, wavelet analysis, probability and statistics
