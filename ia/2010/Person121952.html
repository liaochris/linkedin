Laura Neidert’s Summary

I am a career development professional in the business of storytellling, helping others shape their stories, helping students make sense of college experience and connecting their interests to experience outside of the classroom.
Some of my main responsibilities are:
-Internship prospecting and management.
-Managing and customizing Symplicity CSM system.
-Leading technology initiatives, and website maintenance.
-Providing career counseling for students and alumni.
-Creating and executing outreach programming on various topics.
-Developing and implementing an assessment program with FOCUS.
-Directing the application and interview process for a $14,000 internship stipend fund.

I have recently begun to:
-Serve as an academic advisor to 9 undecided freshmen, providing resources, information and guidance on a successful transition to college. Supervise a peer mentor.
-Serve on college-wide Intercultural Affairs Committee.

I have built a national scholarship advising program from the ground up in conjunction with a faculty member. I have planned recruitment events with the President of the College, partnered with offices and programs across campus to raise awareness of national scholarships, developed standards and methods of identifying targeted students, and networked with scholarship representatives and colleagues to host informational sessions on campus.


Presented "Finding a Pot of Gold for Internship Students" at the 2009 ACPA Convention. Presented a webinar called "School's Out-How Do I Keep My Interns Happy?" for Intern Bridge, Inc. in February 2009.
