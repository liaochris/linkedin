James Huddleston james@riedelglobalexperts.com’s Summary

Riedel Global Experts (RGE) is a network of industry executives, specialists and other professionals in emerging and frontier markets who consult directly with our institutional investment clients through confidential phone consultations.

Our unique service approach is designed to deliver local insights to facilitate the most informed investment decisions in emerging markets. We do the global legwork to connect our clients with the most insightful experts to answer specific questions about markets, trends, competitive landscape and individual companies. Furthermore, we provide transcriptions of the calls as well as live interpretation to expand the universe of accessible experts. The service is a subscription based model which allows our clients to participate in unlimited expert calls. Our high level of service and total focus on Emerging Markets makes RGE very unique. 

"On the Ground" advantage has been the benchmark for Riedel Research Group over the last five years by providing locally generate investment coverage for the companies that matter in emerging markets. The Group is comprised of over 40 analysts in 15 countries, providing investment coverage for over 330 companies. www.riedelresearch.com.

Through our expansive network of experts and regional partnerships we are able to capitalize on great resources of information that directly impact investment decisions in Asia, Brazil, China, Emerging Europe, India, Latin America, MENA and Russia.
