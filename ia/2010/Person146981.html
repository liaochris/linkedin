Michael Oronde Wright’s Summary

Michael Oronde Wright, a.k.a "Big Shot", is the C.E.O. and President of Big Shot Music Group, Inc. and Owner of Next Wright Marketing & Promotions, LLC. Big Shot started his music label in April of 2002 in California. Big Shot Music Group was then incorporated in 2003 in Florida. Big Shot Music's in-house lead producer, Andre J. Clarke, a.k.a. Big Dre "The Decepticon", best known for music scored on 50 Cents M.T.V. reality show "The Money and the Power", Yo Momma, and Katt Williams "Pimp Chronicles Part 1", convinced Big Shot to start the label to release a project they agreed to joint venture on. Once their project was complete, Big Shot started his own marketing and promotions campaign by setting up performances in clubs, car shows and street fairs. Big Shot took his promotion even further by working the project with MixShow radio and DJ's nationwide as well as on-line. Due to this type of successful promotions and following the advise of a seasoned record executive, Big Shot started his own marketing and promotion division called Next Wright Marketing & Promotions (.N.W.M.P.) in 2004. Since then, Big Shot Music Group has been responsible for such acts as producing music for Lingerie Bowl 2 and 3 (Pay-Per-View), Missy Elliot "Road To Stardom" (UPN), and coordinated several independent events at venues such as the House Of Blues (CA and Las Vegas), Hard Rock Cafe, and most venues in the Hollywood area. Due to all the hype surrounding Big Shot Music Group, Big Shot managed to land his first distribution deal with Innovative Distribution Network (I.D.N.). Today, Big Shot Music Group relocated to Florida and is currently distributed by RED distribution, also known as SonyRED, a Sony Entertainment owned company, through the Thompkins Media Group and MRI Associated Labels.
