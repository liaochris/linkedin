Aurelia Ammour’s Summary

Aurélia Ammour is the co-founder and co-CEO of iVentures Consulting, a stragegy consulting boutique based in Paris and San Francisco, focused on the Internet activities in the Media, Entertainment, Travel, Luxury and High tech sectors. She advises VCs, Fortune 500 corporations and start ups.

Prior to creating iVentures Consulting, Aurelia spent five years in venture capital at Europatweb and Global Technology as an Incubation Project Director and Senior Associate specialized in Internet, Entertainment, Media and Mobile services.

In 1995, Aurelia took part in the creation of one of the first interactive agencies in France, Lolita, later sold to Havas Advertising Group to become ConnectWorld. Within Lolita/ConnectWorld, Aurelia has been until 2000 Project Director for international multimedia and internet projects for prestigious brands such as Vivendi, Canal Satellite, Dior, Chanel, Givenchy, Bon Marche, Hennessy, Chandon, M6 and RFI.

Aurelia holds a Master in Entrepreneurship & Innovation (Major) from ESCP Europe and a Master in Multimedia (Major) from Novocom (Institut d’étude et de recherches sur les nouvelles communications).
