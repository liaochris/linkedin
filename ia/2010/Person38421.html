Chris Reichard’s Summary

My entrepreneurial spirit, energy and appetite for risk has driven me to launch or work at several startup companies through the years. The latest being L&T Interests, an internet and mobile technologies incubator firm where I actively manage and participate in the planning, strategy and development of early state internet technologies. This level of participation allows me to create and analyze company business models, develop strategic plans and utilize my web platform programming experience.

In 2004 I developed the initial business plan and launched Biomatix Systems, which engineers, manufactures and markets automation systems for large scale aquatic environments. Prior to this I worked as a robotics and automation engineer in the demanding and precise space flight industry at NASA's Johnson Space Center. I then moved on to a biotech startup company where I was in charge of the engineering and development of state-of-the-art genetics research automation. It was this experience that developed my management skills and ability to lead colleagues toward a common goal of success. I then employed my talents at the headquarters of an international biotech company where I was in charge of worldwide engineering operations and was instrumental in the technological advancement of the company’s proprietary automation systems, thus providing a significant competitive business advantage to the corporation as a whole.

Reichard Motorsports was founded in an effort to build knowledge and experience in the professional auto racing industry. As the Managing Director my objective of the organization is to build mutually beneficial business relationships in the motorsports industry. We are actively discussing opportunities for team partnerships, investor involvement, and sponsorship. Interested parties are welcome to contact me at creichard@reichardmotorsports.com
