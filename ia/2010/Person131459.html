Marcia Xenitelis’s Experience

  • Managing Director

    Communication At Work

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 5 months)

    Marcia Xenitelis is the Managing Director of Communication at Work, a specialist business in the area of strategic employee communication and employee engagement. Marcia has extensive experience in this field including organizations such as Medibank Private, Esanda Finance, Telstra MobileNet and Exxon Mobil.

    Marcia is an author of manuals on the topic of employee communication and engaging employees in the process of change. The texts Marcia has written have been sold worldwide to Fortune 500 companies, universities and thousands of companies.

    She is a past president of the International Association of Business Communicators (IABC) Victorian chapter, and former IABC Asia/Pacific board member. An international professional speaker on the topic of employee communication, Marcia has spoken at conferences in the USA, Canada, China, New Zealand and Australia. Visit the website www.communicationatwork.com for free resources and valuable information.

Marcia Xenitelis’s Interests:

Communication At Work conducts Change Implementation Reviews (CIR) on all technology and business focussed change. These are conducted in much the same way as a Project Implementation Review (PIR) only the focus is solely on how successfully stakeholders including employees have been engaged with the business reasons for the IT changes. Marcia also is a professional speaker in the area of change management and employee communication. She can be booked for a keynote presentation, half day workshop or one day masterclass. Her main topics are Employee Engagement - The Secrets of Success, and Change Management - Strategies that Inspire Employees. Marcia is the author of manuals on the topic of employee engagement, change management and strategic employee communication. The texts Marcia has written have been sold worldwide to fortune 500 companies, universities and over a thousand companies in Australia alone.
