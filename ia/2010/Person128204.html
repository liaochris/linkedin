Lori Riley’s Summary

Lori Riley is a licensed Cosmetologist, CMT and a Business Major that has been working in the beauty industry for approximately eight years. Although Lori is very interested in all the disciplines within the beauty industry she has devoted the last four years of her life to makeup artistry.

Lori has traveled throughout Northern California working as part of the Lancôme Pro Team where she has had the privilege to be formally trained in the discipline of applying makeup by Ross Burton the National Artistic Director for Lancôme. After her education with Lancôme she worked on their Pro Team at special events with Ross and other well known makeup artist to the stars in fine department stores such as Saks Fifth Avenue, Neman Marcus, Nordstrom’s, Macy’s, and Gottschalk’s.

She has also been formally trained by makeup artist to the stars, Creator and Artistic Director Sheila McKenna and Roque Cozzette of Kett Cosmetics in the application of airbrush makeup.

Recently throughout her travels as a Mirabella educator and freelance makeup artist Lori has been lucky enough to work photo shoots and runway shows through out California as well as been featured makeup artist on the Wake Up News Show an affiliate of NBC and CBS, in Chico California. She has been published in an issue of Mirabella’s news letter “Beauty Know – It – All” and she is soon to be published as one of the makeup artist whose work will be featured in the Fall 2008 issue of the national publication Nirvana Woman. She also is in the process of opening two new adjacent businesses called “eleMENts by Beaute’ ” which well be a Unisex Dayspa, Wellness Center, Wine Bar and Coffee Shop combination, and a Barber Shop / Man Center that will be named “eleMENts.”

Until recently her primary business as a freelance makeup artist for her little company Beaute’ has been doing makeup on brides. Lori’s passion for makeup and skin care drives her to find and learn the newest technology happening in the industry.

Lori Riley’s Specialties:

Skin care, make up application, massage therapy, hair, nails, waxing, eyelash extensions and barber services.
