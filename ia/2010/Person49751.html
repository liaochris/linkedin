Dave Morehouse’s Summary

I am a digital marketing specialist, with 10 years’ experience in site and product management roles at Microsoft.

My forte is in developing and delivering content of all shapes and sizes through online media, from traditional websites of yore to the new-fangled, social network-infused properties on today’s Web. Particularly structured in my thinking, I can put order in most any disorder, especially where content, design, and Web application may struggle to deliver the needed clarity of message, aesthetic appeal, and user engagement. And within and around such structures, my talents in delivering relationship marketing strategies and programs can draw tighter the connections between people and the brands they embrace.

Dave Morehouse’s Specialties:

Analysis and synthesis of business requirements; communication and presentation of vision, business value, objectives, etc.; distillation of complex ideas and information into simple and clear messages; project management; online community technologies, theory, and praxis.
