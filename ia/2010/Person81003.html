Heath Reed’s Summary

My wife, Nicole and I have 18 years combined experience teaching bodywork and massage therapy. We've taught at the Utah College of Massage Therapy and Arizona School of Massage Therapy, and have provided Nationally Approved Continuing Education Credits to spas, massage schools, and yoga studios throughout the country. We have also worked in spa, medical, resort, and private practice settings.

Our intention in every seminar is to provide a fun learning atmosphere emphasizing new techniques that feel good to give and good to receive!

Heath is approved by the NCBTMB (#330232) and the Florida Board of Massage Therapy (#50-8962) as a continuing education Approved Provider.

Heath Reed’s Specialties:

We specialize in Thai Yoga Massage and Cranial Sacral Therapy.
