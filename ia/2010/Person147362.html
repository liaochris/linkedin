Michael Johnson’s Summary

I have extensive experience writing and reporting on sports at the high school, collegiate and professional levels. I've covered colleiate programs in all three divisions and have also done extensive freelance work covering the Green Bay Packers. Through over four years in the sports writing industry, I've developed an extensive network of contacts and the experience necessary to report on all different sports at all different levels.

Publications I've written for include the Kenosha News, the Manitowoc (Wis.) Herald Times Reporter, the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel, the Philadephia Inquirer, the Janesville (Wis.) Gazette and the Capital Times (Wis.).

In addition, I've done freelance press release writing for GS Marketing in Kenosha, Wisconsin, and currently I'm working part-time as a content writer for the University of Wisconsin-Parkside Web site.

My goal is to make myself available for newpapers and other companies searching for freelance sports reporters, though I'm also available to report on other topics and to do freelance PR work, as well.
