Isabella Franceschi’s Summary

I’m a Senior Creative Copywriter with over twenty years of experience in agency-based advertising and direct marketing disciplines for the Hispanic market.
I’m currently a Sr. Copywriter for The Vidal Partnership, a prestigious Hispanic Advertising firm. I develop creative concepts and copy for existing clients and new business proposals, including direct mail, print ads, direct response TV and radio spots, websites and online banners, promotion and fulfillment materials.

Previously, I spent 4 years as freelance Copywriter and Translator.

Prior to this, I was a Senior Creative Copywriter for RK Hispánica Direct, a Direct Marketing company based in NYC. I developed campaign strategies, creative concepts and copy for existing clients and new business proposals, including direct mail, print ads, DR radio and TV, and fulfillment materials. My campaigns helped Club Música Latina to increase their membership, Rodale Press to increase their subscriber base for the Spanish language version of Prevention Magazine, and Time Life-Bertelsmann Publishing to create a successful niche in the Hispanic market.

I also spent 14 years increasing my advertising expertise and levels of responsibility at Venezuela’s local and international advertising agencies.
