Everett Larsen’s Summary

Technical communications generalist with wide experience in information products for manufacturing, academic research, healthcare, pharmaceuticals and microelectronics. I have prepared and produced user manuals, marketing materials, on-line help, policies and procedures. In addition, have produced bids, proposals and reports for US government contracting activities and multi-national development banks (World Bank, Asian Development Bank).

As a qualified technical trainer, I develop and present coursework in procedure authoring, content management and other enterprise software system training.

Open for part-time opportunities in these or related sectors in the Montreal area, or remote work opportunities from any country or region.
