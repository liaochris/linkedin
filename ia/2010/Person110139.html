    The Golden Fur Pin Award is one of Denmark’s most prestigious design awards, where young designers are given the unique opportunity to present their ideas on the contemporary design possibilities of fur.

    Design students from the leading fashion, textile and industrial design schools in Denmark have been invited to participate in the competition.

    The styles were then presented at a large-scale show that has become an industry gathering.
