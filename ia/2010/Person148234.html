Molly Fulton’s Summary

The beauty of working in small business is the opportunity to gain a wide range of experience – you wear a lot of hats. Vmeals is a web-based platform for catering procurement - a technology solution to an age old dilemma: a drawer full of menus and an office full of Sallys (remember When Harry Met Sally? Very specific food requirements!).

I came to Vmeals as the Business Manager – the door that was open - which gave me a strong foundation in all aspects of operations and financials. I inserted myself into communications and marketing projects, and made sure there was always enough coffee and Diet Coke in the kitchen. I patiently primed the pump for a full time sales manager, which turned out to be me.

While I still make coffee and sort recycling when necessary, it has been awesome to contribute to the company’s growth by expanding and developing our sales force and ushering in some process and technology improvements along the way.

Before Vmeals I was the director of a corporate child care center. Again, as the wearer of many hats, I had full operational and P&L responsibility. I managed the business successfully, increasing enrollment, revenue and margins consistently year after year. My focus has always been on operational efficiency, exceptional customer service, and a passion for staff development. I believe strongly in the power to communicate a vision, solicit buy-in, and then provide the tools and the training to make the vision a reality.
