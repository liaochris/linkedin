Wendi R.W. McGowan’s Summary

Wendi R W McGowan has over 18 years experience in marketing strategy, building successful brands, innovation leadership, and executive-level management with 10 of those years in digital marketing.

- Consulting to Fortune 100 firms, medium companies, and over seventy-five small businesses.

- Published author and sought-after panelist on branding and interactive marketing topics.

- Thinks at the highest strategic levels and yet is not above the day-to-day work that gets things done.

- Clients have included: Texas Instruments, La Quinta, Gold’s Gym International, and TXU Energy.

- Currently consulting at Verizon, http://smallbusiness.verizon.com

Running against the CMOs at Neiman Marcus, Wyndham International, Cadbury Schweppes and Rent-A-Car, Wendi was a Dallas Ad League Finalist Nominee in 2006 for their “Best Brand Manager” award. She is an active member of the National Association of Professional Women, Social Media Club of Dallas, the Dallas Chapter of American Marketing Association, and the Junior League of Dallas.

Wendi says: "My day job now is consulting in Digital Marketing and Strategy to a Fortune 15 telecom company. While it is an amazing industry and I completely enjoy my work, my passion lies in the art world.

You see, I have always wanted to be a Gallerina...
Gallerina: The waif-like girls in opaque tights who rule the art galleries in Chelsea and other art districts. Like ballerinas, they are generally delicate-looking, coiffed, and can come off as cold.

Therefore, Wendistry.com is a place that focuses on "Art Appreciation for Amateurs"... featuring Dallas artists, sharing what specific aspects of a piece viewers should be appreciating when they look at particular works, and basically simplifying down and warming up what can be an intimidating topic.
