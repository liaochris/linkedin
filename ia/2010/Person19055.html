Per Axbom’s Summary

Senior consultant and web strategist with extensive experience in designing people-oriented and value-driven websites and applications.

Polished background in usability, interaction design and user experience design; exhibiting an aptitude for identifying, addressing and solving complex user interface and information design challenges.

Proficient in navigating company politics, pinpointing and mediating the resolution of seemingly intricate issues; a benefit of having worked with intranets and web-based applications for a large number of Swedish and international organizations of all shapes and sizes.

A team player committed to sharing and interacting with colleagues and peers for the benefits of creativity, competence growth, the sheer passion and personal delight of doing great work together.

An accomplished public speaker; regularly presenting at conferences as well as writing essays and articles, blogging or podcasting as per assignment – or just for the fun of doing something that hits a chord with the community and generates that sweet buzz.

Per Axbom’s Specialties:
