Rick Garner’s Summary

I have 20 years experience in multimedia production with several years containing management roles. Whether audio, video, or interactive production, all projects have the potential to tell a story. I enjoy finding these stories in every project and showcasing them so all can experience.

In the interactive field, my emphasis is on programming as my skills are sharper in the backend and coding of websites rather than developing user interfaces.

For management, I believe in open communication and expressing expectations. Understanding the personalities of your staff allows for honest feedback and supportive work flows.

Becoming a certified Agile Scrum Master in 2008 strengthened my management skills and assists me in empowering others to function at their creative best.
