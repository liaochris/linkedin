Nigel Temple’s Summary

Nigel Temple is a marketing consultant, trainer, speaker and author. He is the founder of The Marketing Compass, the marketing mastermind group for small business owners. (www.themarketingcompass.com)

Nigel helps businesses to find more clients / customers. He teaches a seven-stage system for generating a river of enquiries. Once implemented, this low budget system will work for years to come.

His website, nigeltemple.com, is packed with (free to read) marketing articles. The website is currently offering a free, five part, Marketing Toolkit.

Nigel can help you to:
* Stand out from the crowd
* Create an effective promotional mix
* Generate more website sales leads
* Produce written words which get a response
* Attract a steady stream of referrals
* Get more repeat sales
