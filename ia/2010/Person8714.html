Aly Walansky’s Summary

I have a decade of experience in editing from both a structural and content perspective. For the last four years, I have concentrated on the lifestyles realm and my fashion and beauty advice is coveted across the Internet. Having shared my expertise as shopping expert and budget shopping columnist for iVillage and Beauty and Style editor for SheKnows.com, I currently have six daily blogs and am quoted in countless publications on a weekly basis. I am also excited to share I was recently rated as one of the top four beauty bloggers currently found in cyberspace by New Beauty magazine!
I currently reside in New York City, where I am focusing on the travel, beauty/spa, and fashion writing realm but am always excited for new opportunities and challenges.
