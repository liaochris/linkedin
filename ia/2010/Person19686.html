Banka Magazine’s Summary

For seventeen years Banka has been the leading financial-business magazine in Croatia. It holds this prominent place among the Croatian media thanks to a direct and professional writing style and recognizable identity characterized by impartiality, relevance, expertise, excellent design and a broad variety of topics.

We follow all sectors of Croatian and world economy, offering insight, expertise and analyses. Our topics are supplemented with articles on science, culture, information technologies and a lifestyle addition, called After 5.

Through its special financial publications and its internet site (www.banka.hr) Banka's mission is to give fast, accurate and useful information to businessmen, helping them to face the daily market challenges in the financial, banking and economic sectors.

Banka's readership is around twenty thousand people, a hand picked audience that consists of bankers, high and middle level managers, government officials, entrepreneurs and investors. In a nutshell, people who direct and control the financial flows in the Croatian economy, as well as doctors, lawyers, architects…

Banka već sedamnaest godina ima poziciju vodećeg financijsko-poslovnog časopisa u Hrvatskoj, a to važno mjesto u našem medijskom prostoru ostvaruje direktnim i profesionalnim stilom pisanja te prepoznatljivim identitetom koji karakterizira objektivnost, aktualnost, stručnost, vrhunski dizajn i široki spektar tema.

Promišljeno i analitički pratimo sve segmente gospodarstva u Hrvatskoj i svijetu, a teme upotpunjuju i članci iz svijeta znanosti, kulture, informatike te lifestyle prilog Poslije pet.

Cilj je časopisa Banka da svojim specijalnim financijskim izdanjima i portalom www.banka.hr omogući pravodobne, cjelovite i korisne informacije poslovnim ljudima u njihovom svakodnevnom suočavanju s tržišnim izazovima na području financija, bankarstva i ekonomije.
