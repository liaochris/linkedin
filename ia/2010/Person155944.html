Noah Ring’s Summary

Corporate Brokers, LLC offers an array of services designed to add immediate value to our clients' human capital needs. With a focus on recruitment and retention, we offer scalable, best-practice solutions for both Permanent and Contract Placement Services.

Our solutions include: Temporary Placement Services, Permanent Placement Services, Retained Search Retention and Payroll Services.

Corporate Brokers' success comes from the recruitment of today’s most talented professionals. Whether you are a manager looking for outstanding talent, or you are an outstanding talent looking for a challenge, we will exceed your expectations. From total outsourcing of talent acquisition strategies to interim contract recruiting, we can meet your needs.

For each client, Corporate Brokers considers the short and long term impacts of our solutions, not only on the staffing function but on the business as a whole. We pay special attention to our clients' core competencies and corporate culture, so that our solutions are beneficial in each stage of corporate growth.

Our Mission:

"At Corporate Brokers, our success will be measured by the growth of our clients, and the quality of the candidates that we provide. We will hold ourselves accountable for meeting our clients’ varied staffing challenges and vow to put their needs first in order to generate strong, long-term partnerships. We also embrace the belief that our staff is our strongest asset, and by supporting our people in the same manner as we show our best client, we will elevate the level of candidates we provide, ultimately resulting in a superior service and client workforce"
