Maya Albanese’s Summary

I have a passion for global sustainable development and business with a specific interest in ecological design, which includes topics such as energy-efficiency, minimization of waste, reduction of costs, and use of organic materials. I am a business-minded individual who thrives at writing, public speaking, and marketing. These personal skills combine with my life-long commitment to environmentalism. I have taken coursework in renewable energy technology, LEED green building criteria, green interior design, environmental studies, international socio-economic policy, and digital design applications.

I just recently completed six months abroad of language study, cultural exchange, freelance travel writing, and environmental volunteering. I bring a lot of world and language experience to the table, and I like to use this international knowledge of development and the supply chain in my work.

I am dedicated to the implementation of energy, environmental or other sustainability programs on behalf of constituents of public, non-profit, and corporate agencies. I offer outstanding written and oral communciation skills, leadership abilites, and passion to make a positive difference in the world. My future goal is to start a global sustainability consulting practice that will help public and private entities become more environmentally and socially responsible on a global scale, so that we may preserve the natural resources we all rely on for generations to come.
