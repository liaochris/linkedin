Kellie Schroeder’s Summary

I am the Wünderwoman/President of Fuelhaus Brand Strategy & Design in San Diego, California.

Fuelhaus is passionate about bringing innovation to brands via print, web and package design. We specialize in product development, naming, and brand identity for mid-to-large scale companies.

Our commitment to brand building makes everything we do better. We look at the bottom line, the desired outcome and aim beyond that to create something crazy, cool, professional and thought provoking...and we have fun doing it.

Our eighteen years of experience include work with Rhino Linings, Jack in the Box, Stewart Title, Qualcomm, Wildwood Casino, Marriott, Hilton, Procter & Gamble, Coors, Dirt Devil and other great consumer brands. What can we do for you?

Kellie Schroeder’s Specialties:

Systems, systems and more systems, big picture thinking and detailed execution, innovation, brand strategy, marketing, package design, print design, web development, web design and web programming, interactive design, store environments and POP displays.
