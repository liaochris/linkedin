Shawna Culp’s Summary

Media Producer

Shawna Culp, an online video producer, editor, and independent TalkFusion licensee. She specializes in developing video email marketing and communication campaigns. The use of video marketing opens up a fresh and more personal element in the marketing process and offers live visuals to each campaign.

The unique part of Shawna’s work is her ability to customize each message and campaign. It’s a valuable and efficient way to directly speak to customers and increase exposure and customer retention. With vast knowledge and experience in video production and editing, Shawna skillfully develops videos that enhance email and marketing campaigns, websites, blogs and social media communications.

Shawna is well known around the East Valley and Phoenix metro areas for her personable networking abilities, where she develops long-standing relationships in the business world as well as great friendships in the process.

She is a native of Indiana and lives in Chandler, AZ with her husband and two sons.
