Stéfane Fermigier’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur in the IT industry.

My current expertise lies in open source development and business models, and in the enterprise content management (ECM) application domain.

I believe that to succeed as a software vendor, you must be "business oriented" as well as "technology oriented", and build a community and an ecosystem around your products.

    About Nuxeo:

    Nuxeo is the pioneer and leader of open source enterprise content management (also known as ECM). Our software is used by large companies throughout the world to manage all kinds of documents and digital contents.

    About my job at Nuxeo:

    As the founder of the company, I've been involved with, and at some point, fully in charge of, every side of the business: strategy, marketing, technology, sales, production, human resources, finance:

    - Coming up with (and later, improving) the vision for the technology and business model
    - Evangelizing this vision to create a community and an ecosystem
    - Managing online marketing and lead generation
    - Selling to our first customers
    - Recruiting top-notch employees able to share our vision and execute our strategy
    - Ensuring productive development and business environment and processes are in place
    - Managing budget and cash flow, including seeking financing our research and development
    - Setting up foreign subsidiaries
