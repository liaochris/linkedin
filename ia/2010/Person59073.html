Douglas Sanford’s Summary

I have been in the film and video industry for over 25 years, primarily as a producer/director, and I have spent the last 12 years at Directions managing the Production Department. In 2009 I took on the role as the Director of Business Development. My goal is to gain greater exposure of Direction's creativity and capabilities to the meeting and events market.

Douglas Sanford’s Specialties:

Directions is a collaborative partner with many companies and associations, developing themes and concepts, coaching speakers, working with presentations, and developing video and graphic elements that support the theme. We stage the event with energy and showmanship, and work with clients to repurpose their meeting content for the web.
