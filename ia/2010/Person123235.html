Ron Pereira’s Summary

I am the managing partner of Gemba Academy LLC and also write over at LSS Academy, a weblog dedicated to spreading the gospel that is lean and six sigma.

I have more than 15 years experience working in operations, engineering, supply chain, and continuous improvement (Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma).

In 2002, during my corporate days, I was the first American to win the Nokia Global Six Sigma and Overall Quality Award.

I've also personally trained and mentored thousands of individuals across the world in both the lean and six sigma methodologies.
