Belinda Waggoner’s Summary

Belinda owns hr-haven, inc., a full service human resource outsourcing company. Based in Kansas City, focusing on the Kansas City, St. Louis and nationwide markets, our sole purpose is providing skilled human resources services to small to mid-sized businesses and professional service organizations; at a fraction of the cost of hiring your own, skilled, in-house HR Department.

Your single biggest asset are the people you work with and employ, and the talent they bring to your organization.

Our biggest asset is our industry experience, knowledge of best practices and our human resources expertise; guiding you through all aspects of the HR needs of your enterprise.

Contact us today to see how your business could benefit from the services we provide. belindawaggoner@hr-haven.com or 913.940.5391.

Belinda Waggoner’s Specialties:

We specialize in assisting small to mid-sized businesses and professional service companies with all aspects of their human resources function, acting as their HR Department.
