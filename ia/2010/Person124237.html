Leslie Diamond’s Summary

Leslie’s extensive broadcast and acting experience give her a unique sound imprint – a rock-solid talent you can trust on every level, no matter whether its commercials, narrations, education and training, telephony, internet and more. Her vocal range runs the gamut from outright excitement to quiet desperation and has been described as sparkling, sizzling, high energy, magical, mischievous, seasoned, authoritative, focused, professional, real, urgent, varied, friendly, warm, sexy, upbeat, bright, smooth, happy, articulate, and conversational.

My Goals: Voice acting is my full-time profession. Be warned – I build long term relationships with my clients. You can expect me to be your trustworthy team member on every vocal project. I love what I do! I love giving 110% on each project I voice, and meeting or surpassing your expectations. You can expect affordable rates and full production of your projects in a timely manner, too. What could be better!
