Anthony M Turner’s Summary

My passion is helping small business owners and individuals 'achieve their best' in life and business.

My career began in the corporate arena where I worked through a variety of Sales, Management, Marketing and General Management roles, often with State, National and/or International responsibilities.

I came to realize the many similarities between running a successful business and having a successful life whilst experiencing firsthand how the effective use of key business and management tools combined with life skills learnt through many years of personal development enabled me to transform my life after personal trauma that resulted in suicidal depression.

In 2003 I created my own life skills and business development consultancy (Transform your Business) which specializes in helping individuals and businesses achieve their best through a series of easy to learn, simple to use and highly cost effective ethical business/marketing tools drawn from my own ‘real life’ experience and from the training I received in Personal Development, General Management, Marketing, Strategic Planning and OH&S.

I have now coached over 500 independent small businesses and spent the last 12 months working as part of the business recovery team assisting business affected by the Black Saturday Bushfires.

I hold qualifications in Management, Training & Assessment, am a Mentor with SBMS Inc, Author of Dream to Reality...connecting heart & business, a registered life/business coach with CoachNetwork, a ROQUE facilitator and currently studying Counselling to further enhance my range of services.
