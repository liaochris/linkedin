Drew Graham’s Summary

Drew Graham
European Director Marketing and Business Development
Kelkoo / Yahoo! Marketplace

Drew Graham is a leader in strategy and execution of Marketing and Business Development for Yahoo! Europe and Kelkoo Europe.

Drew GRAHAM led the European Marketing and Business Development teams for the Commerce properties of Yahoo! Europe and Kelkoo from 2004 to 2007 including Kelkoo Shopping, Kelkoo Travel and Kelkoo Cars as well as Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! Travel and Yahoo! Cars. As a seasoned expert in Performance marketing driven by ROI measurement, Drew has developed and managed teams with expertise in SEM (Search Engine Marketing), SEO (Search Engine Optimisation), E-Commerce Leads generation, Sponsored links, Affiliation/Syndication and Email marketing.

Before coming to Kelkoo in 2003, Drew managed Excite@Home's European Business Development in London. Previously, Drew founded a venture management company in California working on business development mandates with clients such as Looksmart and Ask Jeeves. Drew began his career at Arthur Andersen within the Economic and Financial Consulting Group. Drew completed a post graduate immersion program in French at Middlebury College after obtaining his degree B.S. in Business Administration with a Finance emphasis from the University of Southern California (USC) in Los Angeles.

Drew served as an Advisory board member of the European CMO Council.
http://www.cmocouncil.org/europe/index.html

Kelkoo is Europe's largest e-commerce website after Amazon and Ebay and the largest e-commerce advertising platform both in Europe. Kelkoo operates in 10 European countries. Kelkoo is shopping and price engine allowing internet users to find, research and buy products online with confidence. It was purchased for 475 million EUR by Yahoo in 2004. Yahoo! is the leading Internet brand globally and the most trafficked Internet destination worldwide.

www.kelkoo.com
