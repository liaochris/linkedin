Chris Tang’s Summary

I help people in the legal profession to advance their careers, their practice or their in-house legal team. How? By placing lawyers and legal staff in law firms and in-house teams in Hong Kong, and helping teams grow.

As a qualified lawyer, I am able to understand the needs of a law firm or in-house legal department, and introduce them to the right candidate to help achieve their aims. As well as law firms in Hong Kong, we act for a wide range of industries on in-house assignments, including financial services institutions, listed companies, large private companies and multi-nationals. With my past experience as a lawyer, I am committed to delivering higher standards in the recruitment industry with a practical, results-led approach, working closer with clients and candidates alike.

Separately, I also provide strategy planning advice to companies, whether assisting with business plans, sales or investment strategies or deals search, particularly those with a cross-border element between China, Hong Kong or the UK.
