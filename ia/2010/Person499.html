Ron Miller’s Summary

Masters degreed senior executive, homeland security manager, strategic intelligence analyst, and information technology professional with over twenty-four years of increasingly responsible multi-disciplinary experience in the application of sound analysis and practical solutions to enterprise-level challenges. Breadth of experience includes planning, management and execution of homeland security, military intelligence, information management, and information technology programs. Experienced strategic planner, leader and manager, and operations analyst with a unique blend of exceptional leadership, management and communications skills, business acumen, and technology savvy. Possesses an innate ability to communicate goals and build consensus within an organization. Eligible for a Top Secret/SCI security clearance.

  • Executive Director

    Regular Folks United

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Regular Folks United is a non profit 501(c)3 organization dedicated to providing condensed information on the foundational principles of liberty to educate the general public. We provide concise issues papers and host educational events.


    Our issues papers do not offer the details of current policies, although some topics include brief discussions on specific legislation to help better explain the underlying principle in question. They are designed to describe and infuse within us the principles our founders embraced in the establishment of this great nation.


    Our primary purpose is to bring "regular folks" - everyday Americans - to a greater understanding of liberty so that, together, we will embrace it and defend it against "all enemies foreign and domestic."

    Ultimately, the end result of our educational mission is the preservation of liberty for ourselves and future generations.
