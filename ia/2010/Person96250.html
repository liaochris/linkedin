Jeri Ekdahl’s Summary

My long-term career goal is to obtain a position with the U.S. Department of State, and I am specifically interested in working on projects that are related to the intersection of technology, diplomacy and development.

As a member of Women in International Security, I also plan to conduct future research projects related to the complex relationship between climate change and U.S. national security. I am bilingual in Spanish and conversational in French, and I have traveled to Central America, Western Europe and Northeast Asia. I am currently working on my master's thesis, which will examine the national security implications of China's sovereign wealth fund activity.
