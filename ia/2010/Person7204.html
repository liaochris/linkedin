Alexander Liberman’s Summary

I provide in depth financial services to select families, companies and occasionally individuals. By starting with a comprehensive analysis, I am able to direct funds through the planning, accumulation, growth and distribution stages, assuring efficiency at every step.

My job is to keep an eye on the big picture, evaluate strategy, check progress and make recommendations for changes where applicable. From lending to asset accumulation, growth to distribution, each step has to be carefully woven into the overall tapestry of the financial picture.

By keeping up with the rapid evolution of a family's needs and providing custom tailored solutions, I effectively become the CFO of the company that is today's modern family.

Alexander Liberman’s Specialties:

Lending, retirement planning, comprehensive financial analysis, portfolio analysis, liquidity management, education planning, succession planning, charitable donations, tax free income and cash flow management.

Commercial lending, equipment loans, cashflow management, installation of retirement plans, generating returns on inert funds (inerest on accounts with check writing privileges, etc.), mergers, acquisition and expansion planning and implementation.
