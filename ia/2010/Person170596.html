REDNews Texas Commercial Real Estate Magazine’s Summary

REDNews (based in Houston, Tx) has been the #1 commercial real estate marketing source for over 15 years. Our objective is to provide the commercial real estate community with effective marketing/branding services via print and digital advertising, email blasts, flyer/brochure printing/design services and website design. REDNews has a very targeted audience of commercial real estate brokers, tenant reps, leasing agents, investors, property managers, developers, mortgage companies, title companies and banks. Through direct mail and public distribution points, REDNews reaches over 47,000 individual readers per month with a total monthly readership of approximately 76,000 readers nationwide. For more information contact REDNews at info@rednews.com and reference Linkedin in the subject line. Toll Free: 800.836.2191

REDNews Texas Commercial Real Estate Magazine’s Specialties:

Advertising and Marketing commercial real estate properties in the Greater Texas area. REDNews advertises commercial properties through its print edition, digital form, email blasts, etc.
