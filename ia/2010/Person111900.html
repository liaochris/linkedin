Kathi Gaston’s Summary

As a member of the Sagicor team Kathi Gaston specializes in insurance programs for life, annuities, long-term care and disability. She places strong emphasis on educating every client and matching products to fit their specific needs. Since joining Sagicor, Kathi has combined her past experience as a cost analysis manager with her carrier services knowledge in helping clients achieve personal objectives that manage risks associated with health and long-term care. "I have a passion for my work and for helping people plan well for their future," she says. Kathi has been a Sarasota resident since 1981 and a volunteer religious education teacher for the past 5 years. She is a graduate of the University of South Florida with a BA in Marketing.
