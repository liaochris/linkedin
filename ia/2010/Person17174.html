Alberto Sapoznik’s Summary

Alberto specializes in Office / Warehouse & Retail Tenant Representation. Mr. Sapoznik brings a dedicated and aggressive approach to the brokerage process. His goal is to assist his client(s) in making the most advantageous decision. Mr. Sapoznik is a Cuban-born, bilingual Broker, who is sensitive to the needs of his clients. He began his career in Commercial Real Estate in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida with Intercontinental Commercial Brokerage Corporation. After relocating to Atlanta, he went with Prudential Georgia Realty, Commercial Division (3 years), and then served as a V.P. with Ackerman & Co. for over 6 years. Awarded the 2005 President's Award for the Atlanta Commercial Board of Realtors. Also is proud to mention that he was awarded the 2001 Rookie of the Year award for Ackerman & Co.
He has recently opened his own firm: 1st Global Realty providing a personalized approach for your Commercial Real Estate needs.

Alberto Sapoznik’s Specialties:

Landlord and Tenant Representation with over 20 years experience, specializing in Office, Office / Warehouse and Retail.
