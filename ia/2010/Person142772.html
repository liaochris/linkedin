ABOUT MJRCO

Michael James Reid & Company continues the values of inclusive diversity and high quality that Michael Reid, the founder of Montgomery West Executive Search, brought to the search industry more than two decades ago.

Michael James Reid & Company is minority owned and managed and is committed to:

Povide high quality and inclusive diversity in every candidate pool.

Provide the "Dual Sourcing Process" of sourcing both in discipline specialties and in minority professional communities simultaneously.

We provide behavioral and competency based evaluations and referencing.

Michael James Reid & Company's ideals are best stated in the words of Nicholas Imparato in his book, "Jumping the Curve".

"In today's brain-based global economy, the ability to attract and retain talented people becomes the most important competitive edge a company can have...Therefore, it seems obvious that leaders concerned about prudent, forward-looking management should waste no time in actively pursuing top talent from among women, minorities and immigrants."

Uniquely, Michael James Reid & Company walks its talk by hiring women, minorities and immigrants in its search and consulting practices.
