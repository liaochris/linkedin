Ben Cotton’s Summary

I'm currently working for Spook Media who are part of Edelman Digital in the UK.

I'm also an Associate of the Centre for Public Relations Studies at Leeds Metropolitan University. The Centre is a PR think-tank that undertakes research, training and consultancy.

I my spare time undertake pro-bono work as Digital PR Advisor to the Family Holiday Association. In this role i'm responsible for keeping the FHA briefed on latest social media trends and implementing much of their online PR strategy.

Previously I have worked in a busy press office, London-based consultancy and a famous sports club. In addition to this wide ranging PR experience I also have a 2:1 BA (Hons) Public Relations from Leeds Metropolitan University.

I am interested in social media, word of mouth marketing and how different generations communicate. In order to further this interest I offer pro-bono consultancy to third sector organisations.

I have aspirations to study for a Masters qualification in Corporate Communications.

Ben Cotton’s Specialties:

I have worked in-house, agency and for a PR research centre, so have a wide range of PR experience encompassing sport, education, food, cultural events and B2B.

I'm interested in how the Y Generation communicate through social media and how this will impact upon the PR industry.

I'm a keen blogger (www.socialwebthing.com) and have a personal website (www.ben-cotton.com/). The blog was nominated for the Euprera Social Media Awards.
