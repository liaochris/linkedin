Adrian Rus’s Summary

I have started as a graphic designer and I've worked as a freelancer for some companies in my town, Cluj Napoca.

In 2007 I've decided to start my own web agency. ( in 2009 I rebranded it. Gook Koof is the new brand. )

In 2008 we become an important web development agency in Cluj Napoca and we work with companies on the Romanian market as well as foreign ones.

We have a list of friends, collaborators and partners we meet often, we develop various campaigns and with whom we like to work.

We work for EVW Holding, International Brand Consultants, Artis3 - Surgery Center, Medstar Medical Center, Romsound, etc.

The whole Goof Koof team studied at Babes Bolyai University, at the Computer Science Faculty in Cluj-Napoca, place where we torture ourselves with C++, Java, Linux, Artificial intelligence and many other technologies.

I love design, travelling, cars and my beautiful home town Cluj-Napoca.
