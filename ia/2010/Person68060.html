Evan Sage’s Summary

I am now working in my fifth year in the Toronto Real Estate market and I am enjoying the different challenges that seem to present themselves each day. I have been very fortunate in my career with some great people putting faith in my ability to market, negotiate and service the sale of their homes.

I have found particular success working in Central Toronto and the Factory Loft market. This expertise ranges from Hoggs Hollow to Summerhill and from Leslieville to Parkdale. I have spent a lot of my time working in the Summerhill and Rosedale area.

Johnston and Daniel honored me by giving me the 'Award of Excellence' for 2006, 2007 and 2008

Evan Sage’s Specialties:

I specialize in extensively marketing homes, negotiating them for top dollar and providing fun, friendly service throughout. I do a lot of work selling homes in Summerhill, Rosedale, Lytton Park, Hoggs Hollow, Leslieville, Parkdale and Factory Lofts throughout the city.
