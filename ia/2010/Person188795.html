Casey Sherman’s Summary

Casey Sherman is the communications coordinator at Madison Square Garden for the New York Liberty, a charter member of the Women’s National Basketball Association (WNBA). She manages the team's day-to-day public and community relations, and new media.

Prior to joining the Liberty front office in 2007, she spent three years working as a publicist with Rubenstein Associates, followed by Four Corners Communications in New York City. Casey's client experience in the sports and entertainment fields ranges from magazines (Sporting News, Us Weekly, Men's Journal) to events (New Year’s Eve ball drop in Times Square, Real Simple's "Get Organized New York" in Central Park, Grand Gourmet) and celebrities (Al Roker, Rocco DiSpirito).

Throughout her professional career, Casey has secured media placements in national trade publications MediaWeek, BrandWeek and Advertising Age, and locally in the New York Daily News, New York Post & Bergen Record. She has booked clients as guests on national television spots The Today Show: Weekend Edition, The Best Damn Sports Show Period, CNN Headline News, Fox and Friends, The Morning Show with Mike & Juliet, The O’Reilly Factor and MSNBC Live.

She completed media relations internships with the Boston Celtics, Women's Sports Foundation and New York's bid committee for the 2012 Olympic Games; where she was a frequent contributor to each organization's website.

Casey graduated from Daniel Webster College in 2002 with a bachelor of science degree in sport management. While at Daniel Webster, she was a member of the women’s basketball team, a new student orientation leader, a tutor at the student writing center and participated in the school’s first two student-theater productions.

She currently resides in (and loves) Manhattan's neighborhood of Hell's Kitchen.
