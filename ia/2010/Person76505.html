Gökhan Toka’s Summary

Gokhan Toka drives Yonja’s product and community management in the company’s Istanbul office. In this capacity, Gokhan is responsible from managing and improving the product and enhancing user experience. Prior to joining Yonja Media, Gokhan spent several years managing operations and business development in European online and print yellow pages services leader SEAT Pagine Gialle’s Turkish investment, Katalog A.S. Most notably, Gokhan managed the company’s online yellow pages brands, Altin Sayfalar and bravoo! In Turkey

Earlier, Gokhan was a consultant in Internet projects, search engine optimization and e-commerce. Gokhan started his career in Business Intelligence (BI) providing BI implementation consulting for Business Objects and Hyperion product lines in Turkey. Gokhan has a B.A. in Business Administration from Bogazici University, Istanbul.
