Atul Phadnis’s Summary

Earlier in Advertising (Rediffusion-DYR, Mindshare) before moving to a Strategic Planning and Consulting role (at Starcom Worldwide). My last assignment at TAM India was a mix of TV Ratings, Audience Research Analytics and Media Monitoring.

Currently, in an entrepreneurial venture with a senior team of media professionals to create an Indian TV Guidance and EPG powerhouse in a swiftly transforming TV industry environment.

My company launched India's first consumer TV Guidance channel in Dec'09 on Cable and DTH. Before this channels launch, in 2007-08, we launched an industry wide EPG solution for all cable, IPTV and DTH providers.

Atul Phadnis’s Specialties:

Atul Phadnis’s Experience

  • Founder, Chief Executive

    What's-On-India

    (Privately Held; Broadcast Media industry)

    2005 — Present (5 years )

    What's-On-India (formerly called MediaE2E) is India's largest TV Guidance and EPG (Electronic Program Guide) company.

    Our TV Channel, WHAT'S ON INDIA, is India's first and only consumer TV Guidance Channel. The channel is available on Cable and DTH and helps millions of viewers in choosing what to watch on TV.

    Our industry-wide EPG (Electronic Program Guide) solution aggregates forward programming schedules for 300+ Indian TV channels to power millions of set-top-box EPG systems across DTH, Cable and IPTV.

    We believe our advantage besides being TV domain experts is that our venture came about at the inflection point of an Analog TV industry going Digital. In creating this unique business model, we are fortunate to enjoy the support of a host of TV Networks, TV distribution companies and our esteemed financial investors - Sequoia Capital and Nexus India Capital.
