Todd Tretsky’s Summary

Todd Tretsky has 20 years of expertise in the mortgage and real estate industry. Over the years, Mr Tretsky as been a sought after speaker on the ever changing mortgage and real estate markets. He has been a trusted advisor to many real estate companies in how to navigate all aspects of Distressed Debt.

As a Founding Principal of Financial Funding Corporation, he has transacted more than $50mm of foreclosed real estate in 42 states, giving him an exceptionally broad view of national trends in the market for foreclosures in the U.S. As the Managing Partner of First Guarantee Mortgage Corporation, he led a division that included production, sales, marketing, profit and loss, pricing, underwriting, loan processing, due diligence, compliance, whole loan sales and trades. Mr. Tretsky has secured financing from major institutional lenders,brokers and banks for bulk mortgage, REO, and short sale transaction. His extensive industry contacts with leading public and private mortgage institutions have proven to be an invaluable asset to to any firm in the sourcing of foreclosed bulk-REO product. Mr Tretsky has been involved in over 100 successful short sale negotiations which have resulted in the banks discounting the properties for many homeowners.
