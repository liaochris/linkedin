Marc Coleman’s Summary

Marc is co-founder and Director of the Pan European HR Network. He is responsible primarily for member networking, product development and business development.

A Master’s graduate of the National University of Ireland, Galway in Biochemistry & Microbiology. A seasoned professional with cross-country and cross-functional expertise that includes, regional management roles in Asia Pacific and Europe for both large market enterprises and start-up operations. He began his career at Microsoft HQ in Redmond – Seattle, and has held senior management & project leadership experience in roles ranging from selling & marketing sports sponsorship to producing global leadership forums & leadership reports.

Marc currently lives in Budapest, Hungary with his wife and son.

A West-Waterford native of the "sunny south" Ireland.
