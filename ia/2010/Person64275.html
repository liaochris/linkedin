Emanuela Zaccone’s Summary

I began studying journalism in order to become a film critic. Later I specialized in Cinema, Television and Multimedia Production at the University of Bologna. During this experience I went on writing articles about movies, but I also get interested in new media, marketing and promotion.
At the moment, I attend a Ph.D. in Cinema Studies and I'm interested in fandom phenomena connected with social network experience and non-conventional marketing strategies.
I'd be interested in working for Internet social networks' web sites and marketing companies in order to promote movies or television programmes/series.
Alternatively, I'd like to work in a press office, especially for the organization of cinematographic events (festivals, etc.).
I've experience of journalism about cinema and press office in cinema festivals (Venice Film Festival).
