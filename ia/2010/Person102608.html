CEO Drive ERP

John McGrann describes himself as an 'ERP Leader' and has a practical and professional approach with well developed business management, financial and applications skills, gained through working on a range of projects and support assignments for UK public sector and international private sector organisations.

His functional applications skills and technical knowledge enables him to lead effectively implementation teams; and his wide business management experience and communication skills quickly facilitate relationships with business managers at CEO, CFO and CIO level on the client side. John comes from a general management and finance background and assignments are always business-driven.

Based in Dubai, his mission now is to maximise value for money for his clients from their investment in ERP systems such as Oracle, SAP, PeopleSoft, Baan etc.

He achieves this by delivering short term on-site and remote consultancy, webinars, seminars and interim management. On a fee or retainer basis.
