Keith Snow’s Summary

Responsible for the day to day operations of the business and development of the key product and service offerings of the company. Increased gross billings 300% the second year of operations, 40% the third year and another 40% the forth year. Key business relationships have been forged to allow 360° marketing solutions.

• Worked with Marsh and McLennan Companies in the development of their database marketing strategies and best practice methodologies. These best practices earned several DMA ECHO awards due to outstanding campaign response rates and return on investment.

• Developed a database and Internet marketing program for Huxley Communications that allows them to cross-sell and up-sell their current customers in a more cost and time effective manner. Huxley Communications is now more prepared to react to the ever changing telecommunications industry.

• Worked through negotiations to become a preferred vendor of AG Edwards & Sons (now Wachovia Securities) out of St. Louis, MO. B2E Direct Marketing now provides qualified leads for their 12,000 financial consultants nationwide.

• Contributing to the success of GuideOne Insurance by providing on-going direct and database marketing solutions. B2E has become an integral part of the GuideOne Marketing team for marketing database development, list research and fulfillment, territory mapping and marketing consultation.

• Provide ongoing direct, database and Internet marketing support to Strategic America and their national clients. Key clients that receive B2E Direct Marketing support include Lennox Industries, Clifton Gunderson, Service Experts and BioGuard.
