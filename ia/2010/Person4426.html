Ajai Singh’s Summary

A result oriented professional with 4+ years of exposure in Client Servicing, Team Handling, Domain consulting, Business Analysis and Project Management.

I started my career as a Project Engineer just after the completion of my Bachelor degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Though a having a sound knowledge of Electronics and communication, I had a flair for Business Development and Administration. After gaining invaluable experience for a stint of one year and seven months, of not only the Corporates India but also the most convoluted sector to deal with the Government sector, I moved to pursue MBA from ICFAI Business School, Hyderabad in Marketing and IT. After completion of my MBA, I worked in Financial Sector at various responsible positions, gaining experience and insight in different domains. My entrepreneurial spirit and my enthusiasm to make foray in IT Sector stimulated me to leave the job and resulted in transition from Financial Sector to Information Technology Sector. Presently I am working as V.P (Sales & marketing) with Insync Tech-Fin Solutions Ltd. and continuously strive to make Insync as one of the leading IT Company in India
