Josh Rangel’s Summary

I'm currently a Senior Account Executive in the consumer brands group for the Chicago office of GolinHarris. In my four years with the agency, I have worked on several top-tier accounts, including Coors Brewing Company, McDonald's, Unilever ice cream brands, Purina and General Mills.

Currently, I'm responsible for establishing strong working relationships with key social and consumer media contacts. I also assist with the development and implementation of all social media elements for my clients.

The past two years I've supported a highly successful national and local media campaigns for the “What would you do for a Klondike bar?” national video contest and "What would you do for a Klondike bar NOW?" microbytes initiative.

Most recently I assisted with the planning and execution of the 2010 McDonald's High School Basketball All American Games. Currently, I'm managing social media efforts for the Popsicle brand and Breyers, as well as playing a key role in the planning and execution of the upcoming Klondike campaign.
