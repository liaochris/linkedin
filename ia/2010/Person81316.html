Helen James’s Summary

A multi-skilled, talented, commercially adept promotional filmmaker who formed Helen James Productions Ltd - a highly creative and innovative video production company - after 20 years working in radio and television (BBC/ITV).

An exceptional communicator who is self-motivated, full of ideas, accustomed to working to tight deadlines under pressure and a fantastic problem-solver, whose ‘can do’ attitude and capacity for sheer hard work means she always delivers a top quality product on time and within budget.

• Highly regarded TV presenter (live from studio and location “Helen James is a presenter with a relaxed style, clarity of purpose and a warm regard for her audience" – RTS citation.)
• Accomplished producer and experienced director with substantial technical skills, including editing (AVID/Final Cut Pro).
• First class senior news reporter, who spent 14 years also presenting ITV’s award-winning live news magazine programmes, Meridian Tonight, Thames Valley Tonight and Calendar.
• Knowledgeable and skilled interviewer with a warm friendly manner, who is as confident with senior politicians as Hollywood celebrities.
• Carved own niche as arts and entertainment specialist with excellent contacts in the film industry and world of theatre.
• Presenter, producer and director of high gloss entertainment features, regularly bringing engaging interviews with some of the world’s finest actors to ITV regional news shows throughout the UK (Meryl Streep, Sir Michael Caine, Kate Winslet and Daniel Craig).
• Forged unprecedented links between ITV’s news and drama departments, successfully promoting programmes such as Lewis, Marple, Midsomer Murders and Primeval.
• Produced and directed half hour factual programmes for ITV.
