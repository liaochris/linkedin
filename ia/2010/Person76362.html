Gregory Miller’s Summary

Greg is an acoustics consultant with an expertise in creating connections between people--performer & audience, teacher & student, co-workers, worshippers, and patrons--using sound. That expertise is matched with a passion for good design and the ability to collaborate with architects, engineers, and other consultants to develop designs for high-performance spaces.

With many years of experience, Greg also has the eye and ear to quickly evaluate existing spaces and find effective means of improving their performance.

Gregory Miller’s Specialties:

Acoustics of assembly spaces, including drama theatres, concert halls, opera houses, conference centers, classrooms, worship spaces; analysis of HVAC noise; computer modeling; multi-channel auralization
