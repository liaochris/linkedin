I sit between the backend and front line, managing the product team, dealing with customers daily and presenting at industry events to consumers and partners. Helm was the first ever off-the-shelf, multi-server control system, and the latest version (Version 4) is also the first class 3 system to also support a modular architecture, as well as being architected to become the first ever class 4 system in the world. The system was architected, designed, developed and released within a project span of just 2 years including embeded modular server management systems, provisioning systems, billing systems, domain registration systems and licensing/distribution systems with a development team of just 4 very talented guys.

    The company and product was aquired by SWSoft Inc in December 2007.
