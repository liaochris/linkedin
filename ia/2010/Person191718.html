Dr. Tim Smith’s Summary

Tim the Educator: Tim holds a Bachelor in Music Education from Northwest Nazarene University. He did his post-graduate studies in secondary music education at Western Oregon University and received his Masters of Divinity and Doctor of Ministry from Moriah Institute of Christian Studies in North Carolina.

Tim has taught orchestra, guitar and choirs in public and private schools for 27 years. He oversaw the Church Music Department of City Bible Church and taught for 13 years at Portland Bible College (Music Theory, Composition, Arranging, Conducting, Church Music Ministry, Instrumental techniques, private lessons) . While there, he developed a thriving Church Music Ministry Degree Program and raised up many worship teams to minister the word and inspire worship around the world. More than 25 nations have now been touched by these teams. Many of Tim's former students are now ministering as worship leaders and pastors in churches worldwide.

Tim the Pastor: For 12 years, Tim served as a staff pastor at Living Hope Fellowship (Pastor Jess Strickland) in Aloha, Oregon where he not only led the worship ministry of the church, but also functioned pastorally. His role extended beyond the Sunday Worship services to include worshiping with recovery ministry, senior saints and children's ministry as well as leading prayer meetings, counseling, preaching; basically influencing the life of the church on an everyday basis.

Tim the Composer/Musician: Tim is a professional cellist as well as an accomplished pianist and guitarist. His love for working with musicians naturally integrates with his skills as a choral director, songwriter, arranger and worship leader. Tim has written hundreds of songs, many of which are translated into other languages and sung around the world. He is a published composer with Integrity Music and was instrumental in birthing CCLI, an international copyright licensing organization for the church.
