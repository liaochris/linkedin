Andy Rosenthal’s Summary

Andy Rosenthal serves as Co-Founder and Creative Principal at GRAPHITE, a full-service brand strategy and design agency located in Los Angeles.

GRAPHITE was formed on the premise that strategy and design are not mutually exclusive; that brands should be championed from the inside out; and that good design should permeate every aspect of business.

We believe it is a brand’s ability to deliver a more human experience that separates a good brand from a great brand. Good brands deliver their promise in everything they do while great brands make meaningful contributions to our lives. Our experience tells us that brands who wish to be culturally vibrant must think and act as living companions. Those we have helped in this process have unlocked the power to transform their business, engage consumers and create fans.
