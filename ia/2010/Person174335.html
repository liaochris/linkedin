Rob Cote’s Summary

Fifteen plus years of experience in the field of Construction Management specializing in the fields of Quality Assurance, Structural Steel Inspection and Nondestructive Testing. For the last six years subcontracting as an independent consultant to various large reputable Construction Management, and independent testing laboratories in the Greater Boston area. Has overseen quality assurance programs, and managed the structural steel inspection programs of several large construction projects in the Boston, Massachusetts and greater New England area. Resident quality manager on projects such as the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Stata Center, a 450 million dollar 10 story educational facility, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Cognitive Brain and Science building, a 300 million dollar 9 story educational facility, and the Logan Airport Central Parking Expansion Project, a 290 million dollar project.

Served as Quality Manager on several large power generation projects such as the Bellingham Power Station, a 450 million dollar co-generation project, and the Kendall Station Re-powering Project, a 175 million dollar rehab project.

Served as a consultant to Wal-Mart as a construction inspector during construction and rehab of approximately 15 Wal-Mart Centers in the New England area. During this time, answered directly to the Wal-Mart Vice President of Construction and to Wal-Mart’s engineer of record.

Served as an NDT technician and CWI for many of the major inspection and testing laboratories in the Greater Boston and Rhode Island area. Managed and was responsible for performing UT, MT, PT, & VT inspections, scheduling work assignments, receipt inspection of materials, accessing conformance to applicable codes (i.e. AWS, ASME, API, AISC, etc.) and standards, reviewing welding procedures and welder qualifications on many diverse projects.

Rob Cote’s Specialties:

Quality Assurance / Quality Control Professional. 15 years QA/QC experience in diverse projects including Steel Structures (erection and fabrication), Power Plants, Commercial and Industrial High Rise Installations, Marine Inspections and special service installations such as high pressure piping systems. Certified Welding Inspector (CWI), Certified Level II NDT Inspector, Certified Underwater Instructor, U.S.C.G. Master Ocean Operator License (Marine), Private Pilot License (FAA-161).
