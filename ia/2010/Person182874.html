  • President and CEO

    Maps.com

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    April 2010 — Present (7 months)

    Maps.com is a leading provider of mapping products and solutions to business, education and consumer markets. Founded in 1991 as Magellan Geographix, Maps.com is privately held with four primary business divisions:

    Business/Consumer Web Site
    As industry leaders in the online marketplace, Maps.com has earned status of The Largest Online Map Store (Alexa ranking February 2006) with 10,000 products available, including Wall Maps, Globes, Atlases, Online Mapping Tools and much more. As a premiere online destination, Maps.com continually provides products and information to millions of visitors.

    Mapping Services
    Maps.com's Mapping Services provides Custom Map Creation, Digital Content Licensing, and Publishing for a broad range of businesses including educational publishing, telecommunications, retail/restaurant chains, cruise line, real estate, ticket broker industries, major map and travel guide publishers, and general businesses requiring sales territory maps, marketing and strategic planning maps, and other digital and printed presentation and display maps.

    Maps101 Online Education Program
    Developed in 1998, maps101 is a web based interactive database for K-12 schools, comprised of teacher lesson plans, geography tools, online learning games, dynamic interactive map modules, and over 4,000 viewable and printable maps, currently used by over 4,500 K-12 schools in the US.

    Classified Concepts
    Classified Concepts provides revenue-generating Locator Map Programs for newspaper companies throughout the US & Canada.We provide print, online and mobile solutions to help connect readers and advertisers and drive new revenue for newspapers.
