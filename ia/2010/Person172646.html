Richard Webb’s Summary

Over 30 years of high growth global business experience as CEO in numerous publicly-listed and privately-held companies. A results oriented and driven business leader with extensive international experience and over 75 company board directorships in 21 countries. Key strengths include driving shareholder returns through building high growth results oriented sales processes, establishing new businesses, turning around under-performing businesses, consolidating industry verticals through organic growth and M&A activity, and finding creative exit opportunities for investors, by developing highly effective and successful teams of people.
