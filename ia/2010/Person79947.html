Hari Joshi, PMP’s Summary

Commercial Sales Process Leader with the Smart Grid SWAT team. Master Black Belt & Operational Excellence Leader with over 10 years of global process improvement experience. Leadership experience in Aviation businesses. Technical experience in Lean, TPS, and Change Acceptance methodologies. Process improvement experience includes Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Engineering, Finance, Information Technology, Human Resources, Legal and Sourcing.
-Led over 80 multi-team Kaizen improvement events including suppliers throughout the Americas
- GE Certified in Manufacturing & Transactional Lean processes.
-GE Crotonville Certified Instructor of Change Acceleration Process (CAP), Facilitation & Workout.
- GE Certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt.
- Trained in "Toyota Production System" from Toyota Motor Manufacturing Kentucky.

Leader of high performance teams. Thrive on executing on continuous improvement with a wide range of experience in process improvements, project management and problem solving techniques. Ability to work both independently and within a team structure with proven leadership skills involving managing, developing and motivating teams to achieve their objectives. Excellent analytical, technical, design and problem solving skills.
