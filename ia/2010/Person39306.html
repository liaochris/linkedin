Christine Marie’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur, author, producer, marketer, blogger. I created a few educational and motivational systems for families, and have also been a marketing consultant for clients ranging from TV stations to media companies to artists to technology geniuses. I published 24 books (many self-published and for children, so don't be impressed by the numbers) and have written a few articles and columns in the areas of sales, training, education and marketing. I am currently working on developing family media for Internet technology, mobile phones and television...and am an Independent Contractor with en2go, currently developing business channels and operations.

Christine Marie’s Specialties:

My specialties include IPTV, marketing, new media, and technology for women, mothers and children. I also teach parenting classes such as "Baby Care" and "Nurturing" a couple hours a week.
