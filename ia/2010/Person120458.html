Kym Gully’s Summary

As the Chief Technology Officer, Kym is a member of the FIS executive management team and is responsible for:
1) Responsible for the scientific and technical issues within FIS organization namely the transformation of ALIS application in furtherance of the company's objectives and ensure the ALIS L&P application remains competitive in the market segment.
2) Liaise with executive level officers (CIO, CTO, CEO, Architects, etc) both FIS client and potential clients within the L&P domain to understand the preferred IT strategy individually and ensure the ALIS application roadmap development path is aligned including the necessary stake holder management
3) Executive management of the FIS’s global ICT department
4) Executive Management of the FIS Business Solutions team for Enterprise Architectural expertise on behalf of FIS to Principle Architects
5) Executive Management of FIS’s UK development arm
6) Support the sales management team and bid management processes from initial contact, building & presenting the proposition to project handover

Regarded as an expert in his field Kym brings extensive experience gained from a practical experience in both design and implementation of business systems architectures. He works directly with clients and prospects at the executive management level, providing both advice and guidance in all matters relating to the deployment of financial services applications, more recently ALIS within FIS. This includes defining partner relationships for global services and offshore development capabilities with appropriate project governance and implementation. Kym was personally responsible for a successful ISO9000 certification and a key player in a FIS’s CMMI certification. Repetitively throughout his IT career Kym has been instrumental in working to define product content and design to ensure market capture and application longevity with great success.
