Its marketing promotions agency, Active Marketing Group, helps brands connect with active-minded individuals, and through the company’s numerous online communities, The Active Network provides consumer services and resources that support participation in healthy lifestyles.

    In addition to the endurance and team sports market, The Active Network provides technology for governments, parks and recreation departments, schools, campgrounds and major business events to name a few.
