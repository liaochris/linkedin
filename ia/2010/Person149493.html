Matt Spangler’s Summary

Experienced digital marketing entrepreneur and strategic thinker with recent experience growing a brand management and marketing agency from $0 to 3 million (2 to 16 people) in revenue in less then 4 years.

Strong focus on curating and connecting talent and generating strategic insights that help elevate the role that design and brand play in effective communications. Proven ability to direct account strategy and content deliverables for clients, while managing team members in the development, planning and oversight of new opportunities with existing and potential clients through to final execution.

Entrepreneurial environments and strategic thinking have been the backbone of his career to date, most recently as a founding partner at thehappycorp global and prior to that being a part of small start-up teams that demanded considerable depth and diversity in ability. This includes being a member of the early five person team at the Comedy Central digital group, running the branded content group at a ground-breaking Beverage industry digital media startup and experiential marketing consulting for entertainment properties including the 1st Tribeca Film Festival, IFP/New York and USA Networks.

Matt Spangler’s Specialties:

Strong expertise in digital marketing, business development, strategic media planning, brand strategy, naming, website launches, viral initiatives, client services and account management, financial/legal management, media planning and buying, seo, public relations, email, mobile and experiential marketing. Exceptional analytical and communication skills (verbal/presentation and written), sound judgment, business acumen, organization, diplomacy, professionalism, and a great sense of humor.
