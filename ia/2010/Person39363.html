Christine Rooney-Browne’s Summary

My name is Christine Rooney-Browne and I am a PhD researcher based within the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at the University of Strathclyde in Scotland. I am funded by the Arts & Humanities Research Council.

My research interests encompass public library evaluation, social value, social auditing, and the nature of public library services in the twenty-first century.

Over the last 11 years I have gained extensive professional experience working in public libraries, the book trade; and corporate marketing for media organisations and newspaper publishers.

Christine Rooney-Browne’s Specialties:

Public libraries, marketing, communications, evaluations, social auditing, web 2.0, library 2.0, virtual libraries, library theory & research
