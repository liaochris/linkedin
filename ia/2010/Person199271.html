Tamara Patterson’s Summary

Benefits Selling magazine is the leading resource for brokers, agents and consultants selling group and/or voluntary benefits. Our editorial content focuses on the latest trends, techniques and tools benefits professionals need to create a lucrative practice and increase sales.

In addition, Benefits Selling hosts the annual Benefits Selling Expo. The Expo is the biggest event in the benefits market that leverages powerful industry speakers, a sold-out exhibit floor, extensive content topics and multiple networking events.

It is our goal to provide the most practical sales-focused information for benefits professionals to boost their business and improve the industry as a whole.

We strive to be the leading resource for brokers and consultants in the benefits market by delivering exclusive real world sale tactics, news and information to the industry’s top producers through our magazine and bring brokers together with other market players at the Benefits Selling Expo.

Benefits Selling is published by Summit Business Media.
