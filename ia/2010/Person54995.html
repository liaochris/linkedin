Derek Jackson’s Summary

Career specialization in search optimization, ad matching, routing.

Over the years, and almost by accident, I've developed a niche in my career. It's all about searching and matching. Currently, I'm working for a market research company that offers thousands of online surveys on topics ranging from movie trailer/advertisement preferences and gaming console of choice to "why you like the soft drink you prefer". Here, my team gathers copious amounts of data on people that flow through our system that is used to produce their profile. That profile runs through our rules-based engine and proprietary algorithm to direct the user to the most valuable and highest chance of completion survey, ad, loan app, etc. in a way that is as quick as possible and attempts to be transparent to the user.

In previous engagements, although the subject topic was quite different, the end result still came down to the same core ingredients: searching and matching.

At one of the largest tech companies on the planet, I built radio stations for users based on their profile and previous song preferences. At the world's largest electronics manufacturer, I chose ads to display to a user while they played flash games online. The ads displayed were based on information gathered across the company's disparate, global databases. At a large online auction company, I used previous purchases to recommend items for purchase. At the world's largest shipper, I determined the quickest, shortest, and cheapest routes for shipping items and algorithms for the most compact way to ship huge orders. At one of the largest communications companies, our team's software located malfunctioning communication devices throughout the world by triangulating information from their peers to determine the location to dispatch engineers.

I mentioned "almost by accident" because it did not become apparent to me the connection between these things until I had already become somewhat of an expert on the topic.
