Jacob Dettmann, CPA, CISA’s Summary

Jacob Dettmann has over 6 years of experience at Ernst & Young in IT audit and advisory services and at IBM in desk side support services. He has a balanced mix of industry and technical experiences allowing him to provide consistent service across all aspects of a business. Jacob has experience in leading complex teams and interfacing with both internal and external executives. In Jacob’s time at Ernst & Young LLP, he has developed a specialization in financial audits, SAS70 reporting, and internal audits. Jacob has worked in a number of industries in the Midwest sub-area and his primary focus is in the Financial Services and Consumer Products industries.

Jacob is currently in the part-time MBA program at the University of Minnesota - Carlson School of Business. Jacob earned a Bachelor of Arts in Accounting and Management from Luther College.
