Stacy Stehle’s Summary

I am an Energy & Wellness Coach and Mom. I live in Colorado with my wonderful husband and son.

As an Energy & Wellness Coach, I apply the universal spiritual principles, like the law of attraction, deliberate creation, the art of allowing into my life and my business and help others to do the same. I truly believe in practicing what I preach and doing the work myself in order to help others succeed.

I really connected with Universal Laws after watching the movie “The Secret”, reading “Ask and It’s Given” by Jerry and Ester Hicks and then being introduced to Sharon Wilson and her “30 Day Spiritualpreneur Challenge”. It was during that challenge that I realized she could live abundantly and have a greater sense of peace in business and in my life.

I am also a Certified Healthy For Life Team Leader and have actively been coaching others on health for over 3 years.

I also have a BA in Marine Science and a passion for the Ocean and marine life. I have worked as a Co-pilot on a passenger submersible, a Scientific Aquarist and Sea lion & Dolphin Trainer. I am able to pull my knowledge and passion for this field into my wellness practice.

Because of this I has been able to LOVE what I do! I also enjoy staying home with my son while running a household and a home based business. My passion is helping others empower themselves to create health & life balance.
