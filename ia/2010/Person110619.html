karen / moran’s Summary

Windy City native Karen Moran cut her teeth in advertising in the media department at Leo Burnett. She has since managed a prestigious array of off-line and on-line B2C and B2B clients at BBDO, Publicis & Hal Riney, WestWayne (now 22 Squared) and Mobium Creative Group as a media buyer/planner and most recently an account director. In her lucky 13 years of experience, her inquisitive nature and passion for trouble-shooting marketing challenges, has allowed her to holistically strategize and successfully implement creative and innovative marketing solutions for: Oldsmobile, Canyon Ranch, Dodge, Partnership for a Drug-Free America, OfficeMax, Southeast Toyota, Kissimmee Convention & Visitors Bureau, Sealy Global Hospitality, Del Webb, Disney Cruise Line, Post-Hurricane Katrina marketing efforts for the Mississippi Gulf Coast Convention & Visitors Bureau and Time Warner Cable.

Most recently she raised $520 for the Jacksonville Humane Society all via social media and even got a Major League Baseball player...to Tweet.
