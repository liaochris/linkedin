Cory Savage’s Summary

I am currently the President of Savage Solutions, LLC.

We are unique in that we are not an ad agency, not an interactive firm and not a public relations firm. We are more a synergistic combination of the three which makes us a solutions company.

We work in strategic partnership with our clients in order to create results via our many different solution offerings.

We don't just design things. We never just do a website. We never just create a brand.

Rather - we determine the objective and strategy to accomplish that particular objective - and therefore our marketing communications, our web sites, our brand creations are creative executions of markteting strategies.

This way of thinking is the best way, in my opinion, to create the tools (the solutions) with the communicative power to create results.

Get in touch with us and see what we can do for your company.
