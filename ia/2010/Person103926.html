Jonathan Lettvin’s Summary

I write high-performance, high-quality code that performs
reliable computation on unreliable distributed systems and
is designed to be Small, Fast, Correct, Secure, and Tested.

I am a seasoned Agile, cross-platform C++/STL/boost, iAPX86 ASM, script, xhtml, xml, Unicode developer. I've also been principal or sole developer of editors, compilers, debuggers, disassemblers, compressors, antiviruses, integrity checkers, automated code production, surface to volume translators, web listeners, biometrics, signal processors, noise reduction, image enhancers, 3D reconstructors, market-feed lexers, multicast OS patch delivery, magnetic field distortion calculator, charge distribution emulator and visualizer, neuron emulators, and much more.
