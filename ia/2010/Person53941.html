Dee O'Horan CTA’s Summary

Description of my position - Concierge

It is my pleasure to provide maps, and dinner reservations, but the wealth of information available comes from the networking of contacts that I have spent years in culivating. As a Concierge I am a part of the ultimate insider of the city, filled with the what is happening knowledge. I enjoy spending time visiting museums, exhibits, openings of restaurants and new venues in the city. Time is also spent visiting galleries and shows to gain the knowledge needed to better help the guest. A lot of times is spent dining at different restaurants and learning their style and menu. All of this is done to tell the guest what is worth seeing, doing and eating while they are visiting the city. The best of all this is the knowledge that is available to the guest to make their stay at the hotel a memorable one.

This is done because I am dedicated to give the guest excellent service.

Visiting the city is not just about the city but the experience during the visit. My goal is to raise the bar of that experience.
