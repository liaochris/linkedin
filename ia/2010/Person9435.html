Paula Nunez’s Summary

America’s Ballet School has been recognized as "Outstanding School" three years in a row by the Youth American Grand Prix. A true hidden gem located in the Temple Terrace area, America’s Ballet School offers classical ballet and modern dance instruction to children and adults of all ages. Their students range from hobbiest to pre-professionals with many receiving coveted scholarships, awards and invitations to join the distinguished American Ballet School of New York.

America’s Ballet School was founded by Paula Nunez, Artistic Director. Paula was a Prima Ballerina at the age of fourteen with the Venezuelan National Touring Company. She has shared the stage with prestigious ballet dancers Cynthia Gregory, Rudolf Nureyev, Fernando Bujones and Julio Boca and has been teaching ballet for nearly twenty years. Students at America’s Ballet School are being recognized and awarded throughout the United States as highly trained and accomplished dancers.

For more information on America’s Ballet School, please visit www.americasballet.com or call 813-558-0800.
