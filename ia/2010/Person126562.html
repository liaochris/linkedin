Christopher Hill’s Summary

Litigation Support Group is a Kansas City based legal services provider dedicated to assisting legal professionals identify the most efficient and cost-effective ways to manage litigation.

Winning complex litigation requires utilizing every advantage. LSG helps the Midwest's most prominent law firms and their clients achieve success in their most critical, high-stakes cases. Litigation Support Group was founded by a team of ex-intelligence analysts. At LSG, we put our intelligence gathering, analysis and presentation skills to work for you.

From case inception to verdict, Litigation Support Group provides legal professionals a one stop resource to successfully navigate complex litigation.

We utilize a nationwide network of top-tier contract attorneys, expert witnesses, medical specialists, and jury consultants to supplement our in-house team of paralegals, SIU investigators and legal researchers. The end result is a legal “All-Star” team dedicated to winning your case.

Whether you need a certified record retrieved, a 3-D model of a contaminated site, blood samples from hundreds of potential claimants or specialist resources to support complex litigation, ISG can provide the solution. We have helped some of the nation’s largest law firms achieve success in high stakes litigation.

Our services include: on-site records retrieval, witness interviews & recorded statements, evidentiary photography, legal video production, class action canvassing, trial graphics creation, digital animations and chartered jet service.
