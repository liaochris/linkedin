Jason Horsley’s Summary

eFitness for Life is a team of certified nutrition coaches and personal trainers providing an online weight management solution as an affordable, convenient alternative to conventional, expensive nutritionists or personal training. This group of individuals are certified through various agencies such as ACE, Expert Rating, ISSA and NPT..

eFitness for Life was founded by Jason Horsley, CPT, with the sole purpose of reaching clients who might typically not have the means to work with a nutritionist due to costs, location, schedule, etc. Jason's vision was to provide a program that allowed clients to work with their personal nutrition coach from anywhere in the world, at any time. Seeing this work for professional athletes and movie stars, Jason believed there was no reason this could not work for everyone.

With the use of state of the art, web based fitness and nutrition software, eFitness for Life has created a proven means of helping anyone, anywhere, control their weight, get in shape and live longer, healthier lives. With 24/7 access to their nutrition coach through the use of email, the internet, even text messaging, the personal attention eFitness for Life clients receive, creates a level of comfort and trust not typically seen in today's society due to the use of technology.

eFitness for Life has found a way to bring personal attention back, through the use of the World Wide Web and help those looking for a total wellness solution.

With a long list of healthier, satisfied customers now in place, Jason and The eFitness for Life Team are convinced that the use of virtual nutrition coaches and personal trainers will be "The Future of Fitness!"
