Home Delivery America’s Summary

Founded in 1965 by owner, chairman, and CEO Richard S. Merians, Home Delivery America has its roots in the home furnishings delivery and distribution field.

Our management team currently has over 200 years combined experience in the fields of home delivery, logistics, distribution, and warehousing operations.

With advances in technology, we have computerized significant portions of the delivery process. In 2005, we created an integrated delivery solution combining a number of different technologies including computerized routing, GPS tracking, and automated customer calls. The system forms the basis of a comprehensive suite of delivery services we now offer.

In 2008, Home Delivery America completed its transition from a privately owned sole proprietorship to an employee owned company through an ESOP transaction. With a vested interest in the company’s performance, our team of employee owners is actively engaged in process improvements and business expansion.
