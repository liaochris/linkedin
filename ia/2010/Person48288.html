Danny McDaniel’s Summary

My wife and I started a business 11 years ago with $130 in our checking account, no one to borrow money from, no credit cards, and we were in Chapter 13 Bankruptcy. We took that $130, invested in a proven business model, and became debt free within a year. We earned $140,000 our first year, had our first million dollar in earnings year during our fourth year.

Our goals with Advocare are to help distribute these products all over the nation, and affect hundreds of thousands of lives with the finest nutritional products in the market place. We have a desire to help thousands of people reach their weight loss goals, and we have a desire to help people become debt free and earn great incomes.

We believe that 67% of all Americans HATE what they do; therefore, we have a vehicle in which they can eventually break free from their dead end career or job, and have an opportunity to design their life while being self-employed.

Since 1999, we have been heavily involved in leadership growth and development. We have a gifting and calling to impact peoples' lives in the areas of belief, chasing your dreams, changing mindsets, growing personally, breaking chains from the past, becoming more, having more, giving more, and becoming a better servant leader.

Our goals are to have a significant impact on our community, our church, our city, our state, our nation, and our world. We have a desire to teach and train in the areas of men's leadership and women's leadership, respectively. We have a desire to conduct marriage seminars and help couples grow in their marriages.
