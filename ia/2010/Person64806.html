Emmanuelle Archer’s Summary

I am a consultant and blogger who helps expat professionals, entrepreneurs and accompanying partners live their expat lives to the fullest.

My focus is on addressing the emotional and motivational aspects of expatriation, through a combination of brainstorming, strategic planning and consulting.

The tips and resources I share on my blog, www.winningaway.com, are not location-specific and can be used anywhere in the world.

I draw on my unique combination of life experiences, strong business background and solid training as a personal coach to ensure that my clients make the most of their experience abroad.

I am fully bilingual (English/French) and all of my workshops, presentations and coaching services are available in both languages.

Born in a Franco-Belgian family, I grew up in the cosmopolitan environment of the French Riviera. I quickly developed a passion for foreign cultures and caught the travel bug, moving 13 times and learning 7 languages along the way. My travels took me to the Benelux, Sweden, Lebanon, Egypt and finally Canada. I now live in beautiful Vancouver, BC.
