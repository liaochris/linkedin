Teryl Jackson’s Summary

Photographing people in different arenas is my specialty. Shooting company and special events including receptions, awards dinners, golf outings, conferences, speakers, book signings, retirement and holiday parties are just a few of the events I cover for my corporate clientele. I provide immediate shot selection for the client when shooting corporate headshots, whether on site or in my studio.

I enjoy the creativity of photographing local actors, models, and talent headshots and composites for their portfolios and I work with local agents to ensure that we get the right "look" for their image. I work in tandem with make-up artists and our team approach provides the most professional look possible. I shoot mostly on location and I'm very flexible with hours and locations.
