David H. Crean’s Summary

David H. Crean is the Senior Director for Corporate Business Development at Allergan, Inc. Allergan, Inc., a multi-specialty healthcare company, discovers, develops, and commercializes specialty pharmaceutical, medical device, and over-the-counter products for the ophthalmic, neurological, medical aesthetics, medical dermatological, breast aesthetics, obesity intervention, urological, and other specialty markets worldwide. Allergan is a Fortune 500 Company with an +$18B Market Cap and listed on NYSE (AGN).

Dr. Crean has responsibilities for new business generation and transaction execution in support of growing many of Allergan's business units including injectable and topical facial aesthetics, therapeutic skin care and movement-related neurological disorders on a global basis, as well as specific transactions in support of Allergan's Asia Pacific business including India, Korea, Japan and China. Current products in these areas include BOTOX, BOTOX COSMETIC, JUVEDERM, LATISSE, ACZONE, TAZORAC, PREVAGE and CLINIQUE MEDICAL.

Dr. Crean holds a Doctorate in Physics, a Master's in Natural Sciences and a Master’s in Business Administration with a Finance concentration.
