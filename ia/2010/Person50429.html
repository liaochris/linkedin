David Powell’s Summary

Accomplished senior manager with proven ability to manage high performance product design, UI and user experience teams and deliver customer focused creative design solutions as well as influence key strategy makers and the industry. Extensive knowledge of the mobile, internet, computer entertainment and software markets and involved with the production of many award-winning and first to market handsets, mobile services, products, software titles and web/mobile portals including the groundbreaking Vodafone Live!, 3 Skypephone S1, S2 & INQ Mobile INQ1 handset.

A hands-on democratic management style, able to build solid relationships both internal and external, establish credibility and convince key decision makers on strategic direction. Experience on all levels of project management including staff supervision, project liaison, scheduling, costing, competitive product analysis, asset tracking and product/user testing.

Creatively a very strong skill set and able to achieve high quality results with numerous graphics packages including Adobe Photoshop, Premiere, Illustrator, 3D Studio Max, Macromedia Flash and Fireworks. A well developed technical skill level with the ability to code HTML, DHTML, CSS, XML, VRML, Javascript and WML (WAP1 & 2) and experience using Content Management Systems, including Vignette, CAS Agent, eGrail and several bespoke systems.
