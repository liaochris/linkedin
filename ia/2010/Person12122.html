Andrew Plante’s Summary

While attending Boston University, I gained valuable experience in the heavy site work industry by working for a large, regional contractor. In the spring of 2008, I formed my company with the intention of serving commercial, residential, and municipal customers in the excavation and vegetation control markets. After graduating from college, I expanded my business with the addition of several key clients and pieces of equipment. I now offer a full line of land clearing and vegetation maintenance solutions for electrical utilities, cross country pipe lines (both crude oil and natural gas), as well as for municipalities and commerical and residential entities. In addition to the vegetation and land clearing solutions that are my specialty, I offer various excavation, grading, and site work solutions to make A.R.Plante Industries, LLC an attractive, competitive, safe, and knowledgeable one- stop, shop for my clients.
