I began as a Recruiting Coordinator at Novotus in September of 2006. Within a month, I became the Apps Administrator (SME) for HireDesk (applicant tracking system). In May of 2007, I was again promoted to a Recruiter position within Novotus. I was fortunate enough to make my first placement within 3 days.

In almost the past three years, I have placed close to 350 people across all industries including: executive, occupational, technical, and administrative. I love the thrill of the search - taking on a search that others see as difficult or impossible and finding the perfect person quickly and efficiently.

My professional career began as an ordained and licensed minister serving churches in Texas and Arkansas for close to 20 years. Honesty and integrity are two values that I hold tightly and try to maintain in all of my professional and non-professional relationships.
