Anouschka Scholten’s Summary

Always from a user centered design perspective: service the interactive client or user, anticipating on his or her needs.
Transelate this to the dynamics and possibilities of internet marketing communication: signalling new opportunities and help translate them into viable marketing-, communication- en conceptstrategies which meet the interactive user.
