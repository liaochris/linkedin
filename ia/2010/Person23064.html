Beverly Michaelis’s Summary

I am a Law Practice Management Advisor with the Oregon State Bar Professional Liability Fund (PLF). I provide confidential law practice management services to Oregon attorneys. My goal is to help attorneys reduce their risk of malpractice claims, enhance their enjoyment of practicing law, and improve their client relationships through clear communication and efficient delivery of legal services. Prior to joining the PLF, I wrote articles and delivered CLE programs on law practice management topics for the Multnomah Bar Association, located in Portland, Oregon. I have been a member of the Oregon bar since 1986 and began my career in private practice.

Beverly Michaelis’s Specialties:

Law Practice Management for Oregon lawyers; frequent speaker, writer, and blogger on a variety of law practice management, technology, and malpractice avoidance topics. Oregon Sole and Small Firm Practitioners Section Profile available at http://www.osbssfp.org/?page_id=835&b_num=862461.
