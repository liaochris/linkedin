Brian G Thornton’s Summary

Brian Thornton's design studio in Las Vegas Nevada focuses upon upper-end residential and hospitality projects. In 2009, he launched LIGHT COUTURE, a new lighting design company in association with Feng Shui Lighting. Years in industrial design have led to a forthcoming collection of custom fixtures, a lifelong passion. Mr. Thornton is also the founder of designMINDS, a networking consortium to foster relationships among creative people. designMINDS was recently published in Boutique Design magazine.

While Director of Design for MGM MIRAGE Design Group Thornton’s involvement included multiple projects for the Bellagio, NY-NY hotel, Monte Carlo, Mirage, Treasure Island, and four small exhibit designs for CityCenter.


Designer of specialty retail centers for RTKL including the first bi-level IKEA store in the USA 1987. He has led and contributed to award-winning projects in Puerto Rico, Canada, and the USA.

Thornton was principal designer for former President Bill Clinton’s groundbreaking Inaugural Commemoratives retail outlet store, a 1992 clearing house for the campaign memorabilia. Also in 1992, he was selected to design former President George Herbert Walker Bush’s Transitional Offices in Houston, Texas.

Mr. Thornton is Past President, of the Washington D.C. chapter of the American Institute of Architects. In addition to AIA, he is also a member of the International Interior Design Association.

Mr. Thornton is a former visiting Professor in architecture at Hampton University, in Virginia. He volunteers and lectures at area schools and universities and is actively involved in industry mentoring. His contributions as Director of Intern Programs both at RTKL and Gensler led to a National AIA award for the programs he directed.

His projects have been published in Interior Design magazine, Boutique Design, The Virginia Record, and Building, Design, and Construction, The Washington Post Times. He currently resides in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA.

Brian G Thornton’s Specialties:

Brian Thornton is not only an architect and interior designer: he is a seasoned industrial designer. Thornton launched Flora & Fauna the first series of a new collection of custom fixtures for Feng Shui Lighting called LIGHT COUTURE. His signature designs are a fusion of high fashion and decorative fixtures for hospitality, and residential applications. The soft launch was unveiled at the 2009 Hospitality Design Expo in Las Vegas, NV at a private reception for the media and industry leaders.
