Ketan Mali’s Summary

Objective

As president of Graystone Realty Group, my goal is to build and execute strategies for clients seeking to invest in Real Estate along with foresee issues before they occur thus preventing my clients from making costly errors. As an investor myself, I have experienced many of the pit falls a property owner will encounter when buying, seller or leasing Real Estate.

We focus on four core areas: listening, providing advice, community management, and outreach programs.


Philosophy of Graystone Realty Group

Thomas Jefferson said: "Nothing can stop the man with the right mental attitude from achieving his goal; nothing on earth can help the man with the wrong mental attitude." By using Jefferson's philosophy, I have created Graystone Realty Group, a full service Real Estate and Property Management Compnay, that provides excellent service for a fraction of the price. Along with providing a combination of online tools and professional service for a fraction of the price.


Ketan Mali, Broker, REALTOR®,

Ketan Mali founded Graystone Realty Group in Cerritos, California. He is one of eight co-founders of California State University, Fullerton's Real Estate and Entrepreneur Alumni Association. Since 2002, he has led numerous loan officers to successfully close over 600 transactions while consistently being a top producer himself. He has personally closed more than 400 transactions in the five years he spent as managing director for Integral Equity Mortgage, LLC. In 2008, he shifted his career path from estate financing to start his own company and is currently a member of the National Association of Realtors. His ability to thrive and reach new heights has allowed him to acquire an alliance with Bank of America. Ketan's goal for Graystone Realty Group is to unlock the dream of home ownership and return 2% of its commission back in cash!
