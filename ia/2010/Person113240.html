Kees de Kooter’s Summary

Kees has been an independent software developer since 1997 specializing in open source Java enterprise technology since 2002.

In projects he always goes the extra mile to create solutions that are simple, easily maintainable and highly extensible. Kees is on a continuous quest for “the right tool for the right job”.

His extensive knowledge of both the Java ecosystem and related (internet) protocols and standards enables him to think outside the box and almost invariably come up with solutions that are agile and elegant.

Next to his contract work Kees is the founder of metronome.nl an online service for timetracking and invoicing. Metronome.nl is not only a service aiming to deliver worry free timetracking but also a flagship of Kees' skills as a software developer.

Key technologies: Spring Framework, Spring MVC, Hibernate / JPA, AspectJ, Eclipse, Maven 2, Lucene, TestNG, Camel, ActiveMQ, jQuery, Ubuntu
