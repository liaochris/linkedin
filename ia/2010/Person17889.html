Jody DeVere’s Summary

Jody DeVere is currently President & CEO of AskPatty.com, President of the Woman's Automotive Association International , the premier women's organization for women automotive professionals, a member of the Car Care Council Women's Board , a member of the California State Advisory Board for SkillsUSA, Board Member and Chairperson of the United Spinal Motor Sports Committee, and a member of the SEMA Businesswomen's Networking Association and is a Contributing Editor of the Car Channel on Blogher.com.

She is currently a spokesperson for the Rain-X Division of Shell Oil (Winter Driving Safety - Satellite Media Tour - Jan/Feb 2009) and the Mobil1 Motor Oil division of Exxon/Mobil (Sustainability - Satellite Media Tour - May/June 2009)

In the past, she has been a speaker with the National Automobile Dealer Association (NADA.org), National Independent Automobile Dealer Association (NIADA.org), JD Power Automotive Internet Roundtable, GYNYDA, NAWBO, Car Care Council Women's Board, BlogHer.org, SEMA, SEMA Business Women's Networking Association, Northwood Auto Show and the Association of Automotive Internet Sales Professionals. She has appeared in O Magazine, Pink Magazine, F&I Magazine, iMedia.com, Paul Gillin's book "The New Influencers," "Secrets of Social Media", and has been an expert guest on many radio and television shows, including Motor Trend Radio, Car Concerns, Oprah & Friend's Radio Network on XM, Greenstone Media's Radio Ritas, National Public Radio's "Talk of the Nation" with Marti Barletta, Fox 11 Morning News in Los Angeles as a guest on "Ask the Expert" with Jean Martirez, and many others.

She continues to do speaking engagements on the automotive industry, social media, marketing, and everything in between.
