Ruben Rodarte, MBA, MS, CCRP’s Summary

I have worked with federal and pharmaceutical industry sponsored clinical trials and projects for the past nine (9) years. My professional experiences have provided me with effective leadership and management skills to support and oversee multi-disciplinary teams to successfully meet established timelines, objectives, and deliverables. I have managed multi-million dollar budgets while simultaneously overseeing operations across multiple clinical trials. With a strong understanding of GCP and project management fieldwork, concepts, and processes, I had the opportunity to work on a diverse portfolio of projects. My concentration on clinical trial operations centers on eliminating non-value adding processes and task duplicities that are prevalent within the clinical research arena. Recently, my focus has been on developing new processes and tools that provide a strategic business advantage by linking clinical trial related processes together to minimize project timelines and maximize resources. Throughout my professional experiences, I have also directed the participant accrual strategies for hundreds of clinical trials and developed a novel “collaborative” participant recruitment method that has been proven to significantly accelerate the accrual timelines for trials. Notable contributions that are representative of my professional and business impact include:

INNOVATION:Developed and implemented a novel “collaborative” participant recruitment model. Result: Participant accrual rates increased 50% within the first year of implementation.

TRANSFORMATION:Tasked with the responsibility of turning around an underperforming department. Result: I redesigned and re-tooled work processes that infused accountability, autonomy, and flexibility into daily operations. Productivity increased by 32% and reduced operational costs by 26% within 18 months.

I enjoy networking and exchanging ideas with professionals from any industry and encourage you to connect with me.
