  • President

    A2B Franchise Consulting

    (Management Consulting industry)

    2010 — Present (1 year )

    A2B Franchise Consulting is a free service that is designed to match you with the perfect franchise. Initially, we need to determine if franchising is indeed the right thing for you. If so, we then begin the process of identifying your goals, lifestyle, and skill set. I then gather all that information so that I can customize my research to your criteria and present you with your best franchise options. If one sparks your interest, I will arrange an appointment between you, myself and the franchise company. As a consultant, I am there for you throughout the whole process to coach and advise you as much or as little as needed. I will help you to be prepared for your meetings with the franchise company, other existing franchise owners, and help you develop the right questions so that you can understand all the components of the business. I am fully aware that there is always a certain amount of fear that people feel when there are changes in their life, decisions to be made, and feeling like everything is “on the line”. My ultimate goal is to ensure that you are comfortable and excited about moving forward with your (fully informed) decision.
    My services are at no cost to you, nor bind or obligate you in any way. I have been in franchising for 12+ years, have owned 6 businesses, and worked with an international franchise corporation in training, operations, sales and development. My experiences allow me to understand franchising from all angles so that I can provide you with comprehensive, expert advice and successfully coach you through, what would otherwise be a very time consuming, overwhelming process. I am committed and passionate about connecting you with a franchise in which you will achieve success, and (at the risk of sounding like an old cliché) make your dreams come true.
