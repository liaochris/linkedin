Darshan Mujumdar’s Summary

Darshan is one of the founding members of the IP group and also one of the founding employees at UnitedLex. He has been instrumental in managing the IT infrastructure and IT sourcing at UnitedLex during its start-up phase. His contribution to UnitedLex IP services, and specifically to research & analytics services, include establishing best practices, outlining project management and execution methodologies, and spearheading automation of analytics services using technology solutions.

Darshan has a wide variety of IP experience including consulting, execution and management of transactional IP services and complex IP processes. He has acquired extensive experience in patent analysis by executing patent analytics, licensing and competitive intelligence studies for a variety of companies and technologies. Darshan has successfully executed and led a number of IP search projects including validity/invalidity searches, FTO searches, patentability searches, gap/white space analysis etc. He has consulted a number of clients on different aspects of IP lifecycle including R&D strategy, IP creation/acquisition, IP management, IP monetization and IP litigation.

Darshan has extensive experience in implementing and ensuring consistent delivery of patent drafting and patent prosecution services. Darshan leads a number of accounts providing patent drafting and office action response services to fortune 500 companies.

Darshan has a BS in EE and MS in Communications and Signal Processing from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB), India. Darshan’s research focus has been network routing algorithms and network processors. His MS project involved implementation of a patented routing technology on Intel Network Processor (IXP1200) with the objective of creating data path optimized routers. Along with his Advisor, Darshan was responsible for setting up network processor lab for undergraduate program at IITB.
