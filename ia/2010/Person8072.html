Allison Lambert’s Summary

I have been making short movies since I was seven years old, editing video in Adobe Premiere since 5th grade, creating websites since 7th, got interested in graphic design by 9th and I've been enjoying media ever since. My strengths lie in graphic and web design and editing video. I used to aspire to make music videos when I got older, but now I would like to own my own social networking site and do what I love best: create media! I have a very logical mind, and combined with my experience with graphic design I make an excellent Information Architect. Designing how a user will navigate a website is like second nature to me.

I also do freelance graphic design. I am used to being given a brief description of an advertisement or flyer in need of being created and coming up with a look that is fun and noticeable, yet to-the-point and organized. Many people hear about me through word of mouth from other happy clients, so you can be assured that my work is solid and with quick turnaround.

I have been taking Japanese for several years and my conversation skills are at an intermediate level. I would love to be able to keep practicing Japanese now that I am out of school. Yoroshiku onegaishimasu!
