Nabil Ghadiali, PMP, CISSP, CAP’s Summary

- Subject Matter Expert on HSPD-12, Personal Identity Verification (PIV) and the Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)

- Supported the development of (including being a named author on) several NIST publications including FIPS 201, SP 800-79-1 and SP 800-85B. Extremely familiar with SP 800-18, SP 800-37-1, SP 800-30, SP 800-53, SP 800-53A and other publications in the area of information security.

- Supported the creation and management of the GSA FIPS 201 Evaluation Program (http://fips201ep.cio.gov) . Gatekeeper for the Approved Products List - currently used by 40 countries to identify validated products in the areas of PKI, biometrics, smart cards and card management systems.

- Experienced in the area of Certification and Accreditation, FISMA and OMB Circular A-130.

- Experienced in public key infrastructure, cryptography and their usage within secure applications and solutions.

- Skilled in developing technical guidance, analysis reports, and white papers in the areas of IT security.
