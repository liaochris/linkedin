Kristi Daeda’s Summary

KRISTI DAEDA is a renowned executive coach and consultant specializing in supporting strategic career management for mid- to senior-level corporate employees. As a speaker, trainer, coach and author, Kristi has shared her Personal Opportunity Funnel™ methodology for cultivating career opportunity with over 50,000 career strategists to date.

Kristi didn't set out to be a career expert. "I started by helping people," she explains, "I answered questions, tweaked their tools and approaches, and celebrated with them when they had success." As interest in her services grew, she developed guides and processes to help others implement the building blocks of career success.

Marrying her corporate background in HR, recruiting and organizational development with her extensive success in marketing strategy for individuals and businesses, Kristi's personal marketing approach guides professionals through simple, step-by-step strategies to build their own personal marketing campaign.

A prolific writer, Kristi's career insight has appeared on dozens of websites including major portals like AOL.com and in both local and national print publications.

Through her publications and training programs, Kristi shares powerful insights on effective networking, career management, personal brand building, management, collaboration, communication and more with corporate audiences and individuals. As an executive coach, she consults with select motivated professionals on strategic career management and job search initiatives.

Moving beyond simply sharing best practices, Kristi seeks to connect concepts with our individual capabilities, providing practical actions and examples of how others have found success leveraging these learnings. Her fundamental belief: "The first step towards success is a shift in perception. The minute someone moves from wondering 'if' to asking 'how' -- they're halfway there."
