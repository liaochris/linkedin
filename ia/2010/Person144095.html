Michelle A. Diaz’s Summary

MICHELLE A. DIAZ, FOUNDER & CEO, Personality goes a long way and Michelle A. Diaz has plenty. Founder of MAD Events Inc., Michelle has always enjoyed the smiles and reaction of her guests after every event she produces. She has been producing events ever since she can remember. At the age of 12, she was preparing gourmet meals for her family of 6 accompanied by candelabra’s, perfectly folded napkins and just the right music. Michelle prepared a Thanksgiving dinner for 30 students during the first break of her freshman year in college. The 30 lb bird barely fit in the oven, but she successfully fed all of the ‘college orphans’, (foreign exchange students who couldn’t leave for the break), along with a perfectly manicured display of food with wine and lighting. Michelle has always been the “organizer” of her family and friends and the one who was forever preparing the events for the day, evenings, and weekends before contemplating making it a career.

Michelle attended Northwood University in WPB, and studied both Accounting and Business Management with an emphasis in Sales and Marketing. After successfully landing a position with Williams Island Country Club in North Miami Beach, FL. It was there that Michelle discovered the catering and banquets department and never looked back. It was a familiar feeling, experiencing the reaction of the guests after a successful event was completed. A feeling Michelle would continue to seek and strive for many years to come.

With natural ability to network and meet people, Michelle quickly started amassing an array of contacts and quickly began putting the contacts together by producing small events for friends and family. She started putting the right dj and photographer with the right clients. Soon it was the right band or the right venue for an investment bank or hedge fund. Michelle has set out to be New York City’s premier event producer and welcomes the challenges of taking her clients to the next level of experience.

Michelle A. Diaz’s Specialties:

In 1999, Michelle founded MAD Events Inc. Within 2 years, formed a limited partnership with Cititaste Events to help expand and promote these incredible experiences not only in the world of corporate, non-profit, charity, but now also in event management as well.

Presently, MAD Events Inc. has produced successful events for over 100 clients by word of mouth.
