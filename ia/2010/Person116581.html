Kimberley Allan’s Summary

Kimberley has delivered more than 10 years of strategic and tactical marketing excellence across a variety of sectors. By no means your 'average' marketer, Kimberley has led national research programmes evaluating broadcast and print media in New Zealand courts, written government policy on getting enough physical activity, helped guide corporate giants through industry crises, fronted up to national media on energy pricing and issues, and devised and delivered a whole raft of effective and creative marketing and PR programmes for publishing, software and business information providers.

A senior marketer with true ROI focus, she brings considerable experience in developing and delivering customer acquisition and retention strategies for enterprise software, SaaS and online subscription products selling in to the global financial services, energy and academic sectors.

She is a high-impact team manager who brings experience of building, leading and motivating loyal teams who demonstrate enthusiasm, dynamism and initiative to exceed delivery targets.
