Shelly Mudd’s Experience

  • VP of Sales and Business Development

    TopUSAJobs.com

    (Online Media industry)

    October 2005 — Present (4 years 8 months)

    Launched in 2003, TopUSAJobs.com is the first pay-per-click job search engine with over3 million visiting job seekers each month. Job seekers may search over 1 million job postings from several job boards, staffing agencies and companies on one site.

    Shelly's role within TopUSAJobs.com is to devise business strategies to increase job seeker traffic to the website. She searches out strategic partners and generates innovative business models.

    She is also responsible for building the sales team to sell the job posting program to job boards, staffing agencies and companies.

  • Senior National Advertising Sales Representative

    Business.com

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    May 2003 — July 2005 (2 years 3 months)

    Business.com is a business focused search engine and directory and was acquired by R.H. Donnelly in 2007. Shelly's job as Senior NASR was to sell advertising to non-agency clients and to train and support new salespeople.

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Internet industry)

  • Senior Account Executive

    Media Plus / Belgium

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)
