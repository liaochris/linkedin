Rebecca Lightfoot’s Summary

Rebecca Lightfoot was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas. She later attended the Art Institute of Houston obtaining her Associates in Applied Arts of Interior Design and then moved to Austin, Texas. Rebecca has worked hands on in construction with local, high-end, custom residential builders as the in-house and contract interior designer. She has experience working with tile, stone and wood flooring. Her projects include on the 2000 Austin Parade of Homes, Trading Spaces Austin, a Presidential Reception, Texas 4000 Tribute event, Four Seasons in addition to multiple custom homes starting from one million plus. She created RL Design in 2005 and has been practicing independent residential design.
She spent three years training with an established financial advisory firm who specialized in working with small business owners. The focus was wealth management, financial products including insurance and retirement plans as well as developing a working plan for each client’s individual needs.
Rebecca is devoted to growing her skills to meet the changing needs of her clients. Acquiring her Texas real estate license from the Champions School of Real Estate and joining the Women’s Council of Realtors has helped take her to the next level in serving her clients with a one-of-a-kind process. Her devotion is to a customized experience for each client utilizing her skills and expertise in, real estate, finance and investing, interior design and staging, Rebecca partners with buyers, sellers, investors, homeowners, renters, builders, architects and realtors to accomplish their goals.
