Balaji Vajjala’s Summary

Over 15+ years of experience in IT Industry with specialization in Configuration and Quality Management, Project management, including planning, executing, and closing projects for many Fortune 500 customers. Proficient in stakeholder management in order to gather, analyze, communicate and validate requirements for changes to business processes, policies and information systems.

Strong communications skills in a wide array of media: small and large audiences, written and graphical, on-screen, online and in print. Dale Carnegie trained professional with strong presentation, public speaking, and negotiation skills.

Master's engineering graduate in Computer Science with Pre Doctorate in Electrical Engineering. Has performed as SCM Lead in multiple PVCS/VSS/CVS/CMVC to ClearCase migrations, establishing and training client staff on enterprise SCM (build, release, management and administration) processes, and in developing ClearCase/ClearQuest/BuildForge environments.

Primary skills are in the deployment of Software Configuration Management Process using Rational Tools. Has deployed the best
SCM processes with seamless integration between Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), Change Request Life Cycle and Product Life Cycle Management for many software projects and at many Fortune 500 Companies.

Experience also includes many years as Systems Engineer, Network Administrator, PL*SQL Programmer, SQL Server DBA, Project Leader and Turnkey Project Consultant. Loves integrating and manipulating Rational Tools with Perl and K-shell scripting. Other than IT, preoccupied with yoga, meditation, spiritual discussions, walking and running.

Balaji has a unique ability to address strategy and plans as well as work hands-on technically. He is experienced in delivering presentations and reporting status to management.
