Douglas Funk’s Summary

I have earned the NABCEP entry level certificate. I am experienced in the principles of solar power from PV systems from array sizing through inverter selection to mounting the array on the roof and the inverter on the wall. I am experienced in the use of insolation meters, solar pathfinder, PVWATTS, DSIRE and CSI EEPB I have recently designed and installed a 6KW system using a Zantrex inverter with a battery back-up and an automated generator assist. I held a State of CT. Certified Electronic Technician license. I held a C-67 contractors license in AZ. where I contracted installations residential intercom, alarm, air filtration systems and ceiling fans. The routing, pulling and sizing of conductors as well as roof ceiling and floor loading stresses were required for both of these licenses. Site planning, estimating and procurement of materials were all carried out.

In my current position I act as the liaison between the customer and contractors regarding the site preparation during the pre-sale, post-sale and installation period for extremely sensitive equipment. Site preparation includes heating/cooling, airflow, room size, floor loading, workflow, electrical power routing and codes, plumbing and workspace safety.

I perform post- installation customer service which includes maintenance, training and applications. I prepare and present operator training material and courses. I assist sales representatives with answers to any questions from customers that are of a technical nature.
.
As a customer service engineer, I operate independently from my home office. All aspects, including preventative and remedial maintenance, cost accounting, inventory control, scheduling and transportation needed in the managing of a given territory are carried out without direct supervision. Many MS Office and Adobe applications are used to create documents, email and databases.
