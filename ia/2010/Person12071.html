Andrew Ng’s Summary

Andrew launching Fat Mouse Productions Ltd when he was 17 and still at school. Editing film scripts, raising finance, and creating offshoot ventures; at its peak and within 12 months, Fat Mouse Productions had a team of over 15 individuals spread over China, Poland, and the UK.

Merging the design arm of Fat Mouse Productions Ltd with Expanding Web, Andrew took on a new role as Business Development Manager. Within a yera turnover had almost tripled, and profitability doubled. He puts this success down to a massive overhaul of the brand, and launching a number of new and innovative web-based ventures.

Fat Mouse Productions Ltd in July 2009 and Andrew developed i-Showreel - an innovative web and mobile showreel product designed for marketing through networks, online and offline - in partnership with Bloodstone Productions. i-Showreel launched in October 2009.

Summer 2009 saw Andrew join startup Tempus Fugit Concierge as the third member of the management team. He remained in his role as Business and Marketing Strategist until December when the founder pulled out of the project.

Upping roots and moving to Dundee in September 2009, Andrew began studying architecture at the University of Dundee. He is due to begin a new part-time post at the University's "Enterprise Gym" in 2010.

Most recently, his work at Expanding Web has seen him launch Webster49 - a venture creating affordable websites for startups, and Wordpress Developers UK.

Andrew is available for consultancy, speaking, writing, business opportunities, and Director roles.
