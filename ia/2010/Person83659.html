Eric Denton’s Summary

Creative and technical designer, work well with vague to strict specifications. Strong interpersonal and customer service savvy through close contact, honesty, and conviction.

Well organized through self-motivation, priority setting, and multi-tasking. Good written and oral communication skills, independent of circumstances or audience, while staying aware of recipients needs, wants and limitations. Enjoy working with and motivating others. Responsive to constructive criticism and critique.

Qualified through over six years field experience, with competencies from print design to prepress to photo retouching. Print design experience includes logos, posters, retail products and displays, business cards, newspaper advertisements, vehicle wraps, store fronts and other promotional pieces. Two years of prepress experience with technical layout configurations, postscript troubleshooting and client education and assistance. Photography experience include fine art, portrait and product photography, retouching, compositing, color correction and general editing skills.

Comfortable in both PC and Mac environments. Proficient in Photoshop, QuarkXPress and Microsoft Office Suite. Familiar with CorelDraw, Illustrator, Acrobat, and InDesign. Willing and able to learn new programs, techniques, trends and systems.
