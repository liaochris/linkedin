Shala Girolami’s Summary

I am public relations professional living in New York City. I have been working in the field since graduating from Susquehanna University in 2008, where I obtained a Bachelors Degree in Corporate Communications with a minor degree in Legal Studies.

At Lotus PR, I have held roles as the Account Coordinator and Assistant Account Executive. I have supported various clients in the poker, printing, tech, finance, and entrepreneurial industries. With all clients, I have played an integral role in supporting the strategy/execution of PR plans.

My experience ranges from working with trade publications to national top-tier media including USA Today, Maxim, Vice, Asylum, ESPN.com, South Florida Sun-Sentinel, Charlotte Observer, San-Antonio Express, and the Boston Herald to name a few.
