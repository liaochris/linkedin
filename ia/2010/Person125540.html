Lindsey Lewis’s Summary

I am currently the Manager of Development at StoneRiver, Life Insurance Division, and have over 30 years of full time IT, Software Development, Teaching, and Electronics experience in the following areas; Insurance Life and Health software, ERP Accounting and Manufacturing Logistics software, Real Time Machine Control software, Hardware Device Drivers, Data Warehouse and Data Mining software, Electronic Hardware Control design and Circuit repair, with the last 10 years focusing on emerging SOA technologies including Web, Windows Apps and Smart Clients, based on using the building blocks available in .Net, Java, Hibernate, Spring, ETL and SQL Tools. I have taught at the secondary and post secondary levels for computer operations and programming for 5 years, being Colorado State Board certified; after leaving the electronics business sector for 4 years and having served in the Air Force and National Guard for 6 years. I have received post secondary education from Bob Jones University, Mesa State College, Colorado State University and the University of Northern Colorado.

Recently I've done the following:
Application Developer, Database Developer, and Project Manager in a wide variety of business applications. I really enjoy the design, layout, and wiring of hardware control systems. I'm also interested in Java and C# N-Tier application development. Database design using MS-SQL Server, Viso, and Hibernate for middle tier stack. Tremendous experience in ETL process writing custom code using Java, C#, and ETL tools such as Data Junction and Kettle.

My secret desire is to write a real-time control software system based on plugable software technology for the communication layers written in C#.

Lindsey Lewis’s Specialties:

ETL Processing, Middle Tier design, Real-Time software design. Control systems hardware layout and design.
