Steven Chen’s Summary

A business and technology strategist with a broad experience ranges from technology to operation management, from start-up to exit, and from CEO of small company to CTO / corporate development of mid-size public company. I am familiar with the government market, Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) grants, contracting, identifying key technology trends, strategic partnership, m&a and corporate development. I am a good team player while capable of getting jobs done with minimum support. As a manager, I hired eagles and encouraged them to work as a team. People want to work for me because I challenged them and have increased their value. As a technologist, I have been involved in IT/network security, sensor & sensor network, piezo and MEMS sensors, land mobile radio, mesh network, condition based maintenance, location based solutions and energy management projects for government, industrial and commercial markets. I have been successful in developing, managing and expanding strategic partnership with Intel, Cisco, Honeywell, Virginia Tech, Lockheed, SAIC, etc. Have also been involved in multiple m&a projects on both sell and buy sides
