    Emergency Excellence (EmEx) can help your emergency department achieve excellence in emergency care.

    EmEx-Compare
    Our external emergency department benchmarking services help your emergency department implement best practices to improve quality, performance, and satisfaction.

    EmEx-Award
    Top-performing emergency departments are eligible for the Emergency Center of Excellence designation, the first national award to recognize superior emergency care.

    EmEx-Consult
    Our 2-day emergency department consulting engagement includes practicing emergency physician and emergency nurse leaders who create a rapid improvement plan with detailed recommendations for performance improvement.

    EmEx-Transform
    Our intense, 6-month emergency department consulting engagement brings our emergency medicine consultants on-site, long-term to directly help you implement needed solutions and completely transform your emergency room into a top performer.

    EmEx-Contact
    Our patient callback (discharge phone calls) and patient satisfaction survey solution helps you to dramatically improve patient satisfaction and mitigate risk by obtaining real-time positive and negative patient feedback.
