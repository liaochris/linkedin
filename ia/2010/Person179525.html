Ryan Freitas’s Summary

Ryan Freitas is the founder and principal strategist at Second Verse,
an experience design consultancy in San Francisco, California.

Prior to Second Verse, Ryan was Director of Interaction Design at
Adaptive Path, a leading experience design consultancy. Among other
engagements there, he lead the successful redesign of MySpace's
navigation and home page, which had a dramatic impact on both user
satisfaction and ad revenue.

Over the course of his consulting career, Ryan has cultivated
expertise in streamlining business processes and refining web-based
products effectively. He enthusiastically pursues the opportunity to
work on emerging user experience principles, and he has a strong
interest in informatics, empathic design, and democratizing access to
technology. He lives in San Francisco with his wife Kristen.

Ryan Freitas’s Specialties:

Product Strategy and Design, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Experience Design, Business Development
