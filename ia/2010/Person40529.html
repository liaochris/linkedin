Cindy Droog, APR’s Summary

Accredited public relations practitioner, focused primarily on reputation management and awareness-building on the web, including on- and off-domain strategy. Specializing in search engine marketing (SEM), corporate blogging strategy, conversational marketing, web copywriting, social networking strategy and execution, and dabbling in search engine optimization (SEO).

I have taught public relations and communications at the collegiate level, and for many years, specialized in media relations and executive communications. Public relations is all about two-way communications, and this background has prepared me for what I've been doing over the past year, which is immersing myself in all things digital marketing.

I am interested in career opportunities that will allow me to use this knowledge, teach it to others, lead a team, and reach out to influencers that will help a business grow.

Cindy Droog, APR’s Specialties:

Search engine marketing, social networking strategy, reputation management, influencer communications, media relations, speechwriting, copywriting, editing, internal communications.
