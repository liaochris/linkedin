Robin Fox’s Summary

If you are looking to get the most out of social media and social networking, let's talk.

Just know -- whether it’s Linkedin, Twitter, Facebook, Plaxo or a niche network -- collectively social media is a robust communication tool, not a stand-alone business strategy.

With social media, it’s not enough to just show up. It's what you do once you log on that matters. Something best informed by business experience – yours leveraged by mine.

Yours, because social media success starts with a strategy that draws upon what you know best: You, your products, your company, your industry, and/or your competitors.

Mine, because I bring to the table more than social media best practice training certificates. I bring business experience and enthusiasm that helps me better understand your strategic goals.

Skills honed throughout my career, in roles such as...

► Corporate Finance Client Manager
► Corporate Banker
► Small Business Owner/Entrepreneur
► Not-for-Profit Community Communications Leader
► Business professional who Blogs
► Freelance Writer, Journalist and Editor

There’s no need to align with a “social media coach” just because he or she knows how to navigate log-ins and privacy settings better than you do.

I learned quickly. So will you. If needed, I'm here to teach you.

The bigger challenge is optimizing the "what, where, why and when" of social media options for your particular situation.

Beware social media "gurus" who promise sure-fire quick results. The only guarantee is that social media's impact on business is growing.

Isn't it time to see how social media might work for you?

If I can't help you, maybe you can help me...

1. I am looking for businesses and associations whose leadership is seeking to leverage social media.

2. I am looking for individuals ready to optimize their online business presence with social media while protecting their privacy. This includes optimizing Linkedin profiles.
