David Nicol Williams’s Summary

Dave Williams, recognized as one of the nation’s leading experts in online media and search engine marketing, founded BLiNQ Media in early 2008. The company is a social media advertising firm that maximizes advertiser performance by targeting and optimizing advertising campaigns based on relevant consumer profile data.

Prior to founding BLiNQ Media, Williams was co-founder and chief strategist for 360i, one of the country’s leading search marketing firms. The company was recognized by Advertising Age as one of the “Hottest Digital Agencies,” honored by Google as its #1 US partner with its Marketing Machine Award, and by OMMA as the 2007 Search Agency of the Year. Williams led 360i from its inception in 1998 to its sale in 2005. From 2005 to 2007, he assisted with the strategic integration of the business and ongoing sales and marketing strategy. Currently, he serves as an investor and strategic advisor to the business.

Williams has served in executive roles on several boards of directors, including those of the Search Marketing Professional Organization (SEMPO) and the Atlanta Interactive Marketing Association (AiMA). He has also served on the advisory boards for the Yahoo and MSN Search Councils. A distinguished speaker and recognized industry expert, Williams is a popular presenter at trade events and educational institutions.

Prior to co-founding 360i, Williams was an internet strategy consultant with IBM Global Services. He began his career as a CPA with Price Waterhouse. Williams earned his undergraduate degree in business administration and accounting from Washington and Lee University and an MBA from Emory’s Goizueta Business School.
