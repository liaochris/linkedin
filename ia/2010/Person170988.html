Remko van Uden’s Summary

Remko van Uden is an experienced Project Manager with a breadth of business and technical experience, who operates well in both management and consultancy roles.
After graduation in the study Business Economics at the University of Maastricht in 1997, Remko has been employed in ICT service provision. As (senior) project and programme manager he has been responsible for the overall and financial result of projects within large and culturally diversified organisations in diverse market sectors and in an international field of play.
Besides project and programme management Remko is as business and management consultant involved in translation of business specific issues into solutions supported by information technology.
