Sean Pelletier’s Summary

Throughout my career, I've had the unique opportunity to experience the PC industry from a variety of different perspectives. Over the course of roughly a decade, I have studied Electrical and Computer Engineering, written reviews as an Editor for several of the world's largest websites, successfully launched multiple new platforms as a Notebook Product Manager for Alienware, effectively tracked all existing and upcoming home theater products as the Chief Project Manager for Russound, and have acted as a Technical Marketing Manager for the vast majority of NVIDIA's consumer product portfolio. Overall, the assortment of my previous positions provides me with a wide array of technical experience and gives me the versatility to adjust to nearly any role.
