Misti Fragen’s Summary

Tutor Doctor is a leader in providing affordable one-on-one supplementary education to students and adults through offering in-home tutoring to families. The company also leverages a leading-edge e-tutoring platform to reach cities and regions throughout the world not yet serviced by a local Tutor Doctor office.

The Tutor Doctor platform offers many advantages compared to the traditional "learning center" tutoring model including:

No more wasted time fighting traffic to arrive at a learning center

No more sitting in a parking lot waiting for your child's session to be completed

An exceptional one-to-one ratio of student to tutor

Personalized tutoring that leverages the student's own curriculum and partners with the student's own teacher

Flexibility in scheduling to accomodate each family's unique and busy schedule

Tutor Doctor's exceptionally trained tutors offer private tutoring in all grade levels including college and university.
