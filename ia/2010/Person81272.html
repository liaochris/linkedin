Helen Clover’s Summary

Financial Executive with broad experience of management and financial accounting practice in small business environment. Hands on Property Developer including project management of complete refurbishments. On Going Property Management with associated financial management.

A recent MSc Graduate (Property Management & Investment) is actively seeking opportunity to become a full member of RICS. Generous exemptions from the board will allow qualification within 12 months and 48 hours in house experience. (Adaptation Route 1) would commit 100% to Commercial property firm for this window of opportunity.

Driven by a passion for property and with a sound head for figures is 'Edinburgh's Positive Face of Finance' as an Independent Financial Adviser (non Investment)

Sourcing Conventional Mortgage products, Buy to Let Mortgages for Property Investors & Commercial Finance for property & business developement, all supported with a range of Protection Products so that exposure to risk is minimised. A fully FSA compliant service.

In addition, because it's hard to put property developing down always looking for a development or refurbishment to get involved with; whether this is on my own behalf or for a client.

The business or investment plan comes first involving an investigation of the market, finance houses & the competition; never has this strategy been so important and I can help you too.
