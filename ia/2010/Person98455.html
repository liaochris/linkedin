Jim Ingersoll’s Summary

I received my degree in Electrical Engineering from Rochester Institute of Technology. I then went on and received my Masters in Engineering Management.

I spent a lot of years working at Acu-Rite, MRC, Viasystems and Barber & Ross. I really loved manufacturing, Quality, and Engineering but found that I truly loved operations and managing all the functions including HR, Sales, Production, etc. while working as a General Manager. I found I really enjoyed the full business development including developing a business plan, strategic planning and building a strong team to deliver great results.

A few years ago II decided to explore opportunities to work for myself and build cash producing assets. I started investigating franchises and many, many businesses. I concluded that the easiest, best way to accumulate wealth was to buy, sell and rent real estate. Cheryl and I have been working our real estate business full time for 4 years now and we love the challenge.

We make offers on real estate nearly every day. We have bought and sold tons of houses in the past couple years. We have also developed great systems for property management. We have no problem finding great tenants to work with. We operate several different profit centers that provide positive monthly cash flow.
