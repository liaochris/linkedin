Dr. Indu Arora’s Summary

Dr. Indu Arora is a highly accomplished international speaker, acclaimed Yoga Therapist, Master Teacher, Ayurvedic Consultant, Healer and Author with more than 30,000 hours of teaching experience. She is a regular presenter at International Conferences & Symposiums.

Dr. Arora prefers to apply a holistic approach to heal her clients through a combination of Yoga, Diverse Meditation techniques, Bach Flower remedies, Acupressure, Reflexology, Magnet Therapy,Reiki, ESP, Herbal remedies, Mantra and Mudras.

Dr. Indu Arora’s book, “Yoga: Ancient Heritage Tomorrows vision” is honored, acknowledged and highly appreciated by Yoga Masters & Professionals.

A board member of the World Movement of Yoga, an International Advisor to World Yoga Council and a Diversity Committee member of the special task force under the Mayor of Woodridge, IL; she is a regular columnist for acclaimed Yoga & Wellness magazines “Tathaastu” and “Yoga Chicago”.

The Mayor and Chief of Police have honored Dr. Indu Arora two times in a row for contributing to the Village & City of Woodridge. A recipient of “Hall of Fame” award by the NRI welfare society, she was also chosen as “Dupage Woman You should Know” by Dupage Woman Magazine. She has recently been awarded with two titles “GEM OF INDIA” and “RAY OF HOPE” on an international platform.

Dr. Arora offers inter-disciplinary approach to the ancient practice of healing through yoga, meditation and alternate healing modalities with a full variety of offerings, including workshops, teacher training, body work, therapy sessions & consultations, private sessions, seminars, corporate yoga sessions and educational media.
She holds a Grand Parenting E-500 T-500 experienced registered yoga teacher certificate from Yoga Alliance.

Dr. Arora is presently pursuing her PhD in Metaphysical Sciences from University of Metaphysical Sciences, CA.
