Cynthia Hagenhoff, DVM’s Summary

I am a 2009 graduate of The University Of Missouri College Of Veterinary Medicine and I am currently helping to set-up a low cost spay/neuter clinic in the Springfield area. When this clinic opens in August, our goal is to reduce the local animal overpopulation problem, so that Springfield area shelters can be reduced to no-kill status by 2018.

I also co-own a family business- Sticks 'N Stones Gifts. We sell locally at craft fairs, as well as on commission at the Busy Beads store in Fair Grove, MO. We are currently building an Etsy page to reach a broader clientel, but until then, we have a Facebook fanpage showcasing our work.
