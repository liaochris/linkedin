Maureen Schumacher’s Summary

Transformed the two year old Aqua Star Spa at the Beverly Hilton Hotel through several different strategies. Changed the treatment menu, marketing strategies and product lines, creating a brand image and responding to the demographics of the existing spa clientele. Hired more customer service driven individuals who would place the needs of the guest first. Conducted several sales training seminars for the staff to teach strategy and drive additional retail sales. Also restructured the operations of the facility eliminating some positions and revising the scheduling and compensation as well as reviewing all expenditures and dramatically reducing costs. These accomplishments resulted in an increase in Profitability of 24% for 2008 and 36% for 2009.
