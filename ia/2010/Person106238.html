Josh Seidenfeld’s Summary

Joshua Seidenfeld is the Director of Business Development for Practice Fusion, the fastest growing, free, web-based EHR. He is responsible for strategic partner relations and is constantly looking for organizations and individuals that can help make a impact in the health care industry.

Joshua is a graduate of Santa Clara University with a JD/MBA. Throughout Law and Business School he focused on the technology sector with a particular emphasis on start-up businesses and strategic decision making. During his studies he was recognized and honored by receiving the Belotti Award for the best strategy report on business policy in a high technology firm, continuously being named on the Dean’s list for high academic achievement, recipient of the best oral argument in appellate advocacy, and was recognized as a leader amongst his classmates by being named to several governing bodies and review boards at the university.

Before attending Santa Clara University Joshua founded GoodLife Electronics, an online consumer electronic company. Within its first year Joshua was able to form partnerships with international manufacturing facilities and developed a national distribution channel that allowed for organic growth within the target demographic.

Recently, Joshua married his college sweetheart, Megan, whom he has been dating for over seven years. The two of them share a love for dogs and have a seven year old pug named Buggsie.
