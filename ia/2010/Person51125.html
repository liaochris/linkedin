  • Director of Marketing

    Sycamore Networks

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    September 2006 — September 2008 (2 years 1 month)

    Joined the corporate marketing team at Sycamore upon their acquisition of Eastern Research. Activities included introducing and managing web-based and email marketing tactics, and helping to merge Eastern's marketing activities, collateral, and budget items into the Sycamore portfolio. Directly supported Sycamore's global outside sales teams as a de facto inside sales function for inbound lead filtering and progression; also co-developed and executed demand generation projects involving a 3rd-party teleprospecting firm, resulting in significant new business pipeline among targeted telecom network operators. Other projects included public relations planning, media research, outside agency support, customer-focused PR, development/placement of contributed articles and white papers, "Web 2.0" marketing tools evaluation, industry event planning, and channel partner marketing. International travel to Hong Kong while in this position.

  • Director of Marketing

    Eastern Research Inc.

    (Telecommunications industry)

    July 2003 — September 2006 (3 years 3 months)

    Integrated into the New Jersey-based marketing team after Eastern's acquisition of Avail Networks. Managed, analyzed, and prioritized the marketing budget in collaboration with the president and marketing VP. Managed two remote direct reports, including annual performance reviews. Introduced web-based marketing tactics to the organization, including webinars, search-based marketing, and white paper programs to boost corporate visibility, reputation, and demand generation. Led existing team and outside agencies on renewed PR efforts, resulting in positive coverage in relevant trade publications. Co-authored contributed articles for placement in targeted online and print media. Assisted sales personnel with industry-specific presentation materials as needed. International travel to Japan, China, Thailand, Canada, and France during this position.

  • Marketing Director

    Avail Networks

    (Telecommunications industry)

    June 1999 — July 2003 (4 years 2 months)

    Led and managed the marketing programs of this VC-backed telecom startup. Projects included initial business planning, web site and collateral development, product branding, company and product launch initiatives and associated PR/AR activities, advertising and event marketing, and web-based promotional efforts. Was a lead author on the business plan that helped secure first-round VC financing. Also assisted with sales efforts in Japan and Canada, and other business development activities, including identifying and contacting business merger targets. International travel to Japan, Canada, Belgium, France, and Denmark during this position.

  • Sr. Product Manager

Developed and maintained product plans, authored a white paper, assisted with PR/AR efforts and other marketing programs, and conducted numerous joint sales calls with Cabletron's sales force.
