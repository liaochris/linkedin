Nick Silkey’s Summary

Eight years professional experience working within large-scale enterprise information technology framework. Initially served upwards of 4,500 departmental engineering users (ece.utexas.edu). Briefly served over 80,000 campus-wide users (utexas.edu). Currently serving 30,000 campus-wide users (yale.edu). Wrangled the server-side infrastructure and highly-available services for one of the largest, most-distributed higher-ed campuses in the world. Currently engaged within the Ivy League in a heavily-centralized, business-oriented, project-fueled environment. Being an edu is challenging but fun; there sure are a lot of ways to do things.

-Deeply-desire to leverage emerging technologies whereby 'the new hotness' saves my time and your money.
-Ability to engage directly in customer communication/relationships.
-Experience directly supervising technical FTEs from day-to-day operations to long-term projects
-Obsessed with highly-effective means to build infrastructures in the right way to make them harder, better, faster, stronger.
