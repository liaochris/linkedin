Gary Irwin’s Summary

Gary Irwin is the IT Director at Acceleration eMarketing, working out of their Cape Town office. Gary works with Acceleration's Business Unit Directors and Executives to deliver effective global IT solutions to its enterprise.

Prior to joining the company in May 2006, Irwin worked for Enterprise Connection as a System Engineer helping companies to maximize IT systems ROI. Further Irwin, helped to provide the best possible service to customers by maintaining information security and optimizing business application availability — whether the application was Web-based B2C, internal ERP or a core service such as e-mail.

Previous roles include his position as Operations Manager of Computer Networking Services (Cape Town), a specialized company offering network integration between various enterprise platforms; as well as the Technical Support Lead for AppleCentre in the City, working with enterprise clients and agencies to deliver Apple Macintosh services and integration of B2B application.

Gary Irwin is currently completing further postgraduate studies at the the Universtity of Cape Town doing his BCOM IS Honours focusing on BPM and Enterprise Architecture.

Future goals include managing large enterprise organisations to realize IT investments not as a cost centre or the next step of a business enabler, however to make IT a strategic asset creating ROI to the enterprise.
