Mary Roth CPCU LUTCF HB ITP DTM’s Summary

Member of Society of Insurance Trainers and Educators (SITE) and Texas Real Estate Teachers Association (TRETA). I hold the "Legendary Trainer" designation.

A member of the Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter (CPCU) Fort Worth Chapter. Currently have a General Lines P&C Insurance License and Life and Health Licenses. Have taught insurance throughout my career.

I do P&C pre-licensing test preparation for agents and adjusters. We also have over 38 Continuing Education classroom classes. Eight of which are Ethics courses, each with a different flair.
