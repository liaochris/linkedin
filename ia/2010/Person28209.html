Brian Colin’s Summary

28 years of Professional Game Design & Development Experience
=======
I am a vastly experienced Game Designer, Art Director, Producer and Development Group Leader with over twenty-seven years in the industry and over sixty titles to my credit in the coin-op Arcade, PC Action, Legal Gaming, Mobile Platform, and Home Console markets … including the creation of some of the most-popular highest-earning, best-selling, video games of all time! (Odds are good that I helped create some of the games that you grew up with.)

As founder and CEO of GAME REFUGE INC I am responsible for all aspects of the day-to-day operation of the corporation, including project scheduling, hiring, training and management of all development personnel, forming and maintaining client relationships, and leading the creative effort from concept to completion.

Brian Colin’s Specialties:

As a Game Designer, I'm probably best known for creating original, successful intellectual properties (i.e., RAMPAGE, GENERAL CHAOS, SPACE GOBLINZ etc.), but in addition to being the Principal Designer and Art/Creative lead for most of my 60+ career titles, my responsibilities typically include Team Management, Risk Management, Project Management, Asset Management Contract Negotiation & IP Protection ...as well as the hiring, training and mentoring of Talent.
