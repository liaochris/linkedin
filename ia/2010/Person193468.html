Rick Stavanja’s Summary

A multi-skilled professional with 25+ years of broad experience in the CAD industry, ranging from product designer to business entrepreneur to corporate executive. Particularly adept at communicating the business value of design technology in markets such as MCAD, PLM, AEC, ECAD, GIS, Plant Design, and Computer Graphics.

Co-founder of PacifiCAD, Inc. in 1990. Founder of CADwire.net in 1999.

Open to discussing new business ventures, partnerships, and writing opportunities.
