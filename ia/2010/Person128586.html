Lucrezia Cippitelli’s Summary

Scholar, curator, art critic
- teaching activity in graduate and postgraduate level.
fields: contemporary art (historical and Neo-Avantgardes, utopian movements post second world war; video, cinema and media art); representation of not western art in the international art system (biennals and periodical international exhibitions); comparative modernism, contemporary african art, contemporary latin american art, postcolonial critic.
- editorial activities:
art magazines and book series related with contemporary art criticism, theory and art practices.
- curatorial activities:
from theory to installation: exhibitions; cultural cooperation projects in the field of arts and activism.
Curatorial practice developed both in institutional contexts and as freelance.
Special attention given to site specific projects and displaying practices.
- film-making and journalistic activities:
conception, production, edition, post-production and distribution (also web based) of audio or audio/video documentary projects.
