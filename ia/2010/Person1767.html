Jonathan Abramson’s Summary

Abramson + Cleaves Company offers consulting services to nonprofit organizations. The partners are motivated by their desire to see this significant sector excel in providing for the needs of our communities.

Your nonprofit organization has needs. We work with you to:

* Improve your fundraising programs and grant proposals
* Make your organization’s expansion dreams become real by conducting your feasibility study and capital campaign
* Create a variety of philanthropic income sources
* Introduce effective leadership principles for board members and volunteers
* Streamline management, staffing and operations to produce the highest levels of efficiency, accountability and results
* Develop sustainable practices and procedures so that your organization can provide for your constituencies for years to come

Abramson + Cleaves Company can be trusted to listen to what you want and to work in partnership with you to create your next steps. We are also ready to support your needs for board retreats, training and classroom instruction, business planning and technology planning. We can work with your organization for a day, a week, or years.

What you can expect: We deliver what we have committed to on time and on budget. The information you receive will be accurate, and you will hear honest opinions from us.
