Brent Brooks’s Summary

Brent is a media producer and owner of Blank Stage Productions. Blank Stage is a creative media company that pioneers innovative ways to communicate ideas. Blank Stage writes, produces and edits original media content for television, online and film.

We do this through ImprovAstorm, ThriveAMERICA, The Blank Page and our full service video production services.

Through ImprovAstorm companies can walk away with up to twenty engaging ways to communicate their message to their target audience.

ThriveAMERICA is an online video show that attracts top business leaders to discuss ways we can all thrive in this economy.

The Blank Page is a hub to emerging and established screenwriters based in Georgia.
