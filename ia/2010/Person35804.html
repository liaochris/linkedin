Charlene Day’s Summary

Highlights of Charlene's career include a busy Massage Therapy practice, Therapeutic Movement instructor, Spiritual Healing and Meditation teacher, Holistic coaching, teaching at The Institute of Holistic Nutrition, and as an environmentalist she focuses on environmental links to breast cancer. She was one of the presenters at the First World Conference on Breast Cancer.

Her study in the area of sales, goal setting, money management, personal growth and training has led her to coaching others. Her first hand experience as an entrepreneur for over 30 years aids others in their journey towards becoming entrepreneurs and leaders.

Currently, she works in Toronto as a speaker and coach, helping others recognize their full potential. She inspires others to deal with issues that prevent them from achieving the quality of life they deserve.
