Gary Lee’s Summary

I have a successful track record of taking startups and products in uncertain markets from their concept / seed stage through market introduction and growth stages. I have taken several products and companies from $0 to $ millions in annual revenues.

I have strong hands-on experience in International Markets, with offices, sales and operations established in Europe, Israel and China.

I also have a proven ability to work with Private and Public Investors, having successfully raised $70M for my first startup, and over $20M for my second including investments from Accel Partners, St. Paul Ventures, Lucent and others. I am experienced in presenting at large and small Investment Conferences and also working directly with Financial Analysts and Financial Firms on Wall Street.

In additional to my startup-experience, I have also held Management, Marketing and Technical Positions at public companies including Sprint, General DataCom and Northern Telecom (Nortel).

I presently work with public and private companies, assisting with their business and marketing plans and strategies, to offer "outside-in" advice on fundraising, go-to-market, PR, M&A, product, competitive and other corporate strategies.
