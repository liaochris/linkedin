Susan Myers’s Summary

The Center for Wealth Management was established in 2003 and became an office of MetLife in 2004.

As a Senior Financial Planner and a Financial Services Representative at MetLife, I focus my practice on comprehensive financial planning. I also enjoy working with individuals through all stages of retirement. I am a frequent speaker at community and corporate events.

With 23 years of experience in the financial industry, I will help you create a financial plan – a roadmap to guide you through you financial journey.

My clients range from young adults ready to make their first investment decisions to entrepreneurs, successful business owners, and retirees. No matter their age, income, or the size of their portfolio, they share a common desire for unbiased investment advice and consistent guidance provide by a qualified team of experience representatives dedicated to help build and preserve their life’s wealth.
