Vincent Vanderbent’s Summary

Helping others grow is the main thing that motivates me. I am always looking for ways to help people live better, more productive lives. I have worn quite a few hats over the years but am mostly known for my critical perspective and ability to turn underperforming teams around.

Solving problems, acting as a change agent and project manager are my primary roles. I have a background in the legal, financial and management consulting fields. In an organizational setting, people know me for my ability to build strong, sustainable relationships with internal and external clients. I believe that stakeholders deserve to get the most value for their money, while team members stay motivated and prepared to perform to the best of their ability.

My passion for healthy living and the environment made be become an independent distributor for Shaklee Corporation, the first climate neutral company in the world making a range of health & wellness, cosmetics and other products. Ask me about Vivix, the most potent resveratrol product on the market today. Shaklee's products are tested by the U.S. Olympics teams and used by NASA. It's nice to be part of a winning team!

Some of my accomplishments include:

- facilitated strategic reassessment of international logistics for a chemicals manufacturer, eliminating risk and reducing transit times by 20%
- doubled brand recognition for a leatherwear manufacturer and marketer by determining the main purchasing motivation for its customers
- 35% vendor cost reduction in the marketing budget for a major investment bank and brokerage
- 40% reduction in project turnaround time at a legal firm by improving quality control and job tracking
- streamlined legal document production team through layoffs to work 20% more efficiently and reduce production costs by 30%.

I am looking forward to helping you grow too!

Vincent Vanderbent’s Specialties:

Problem solving, workflow optimization, and cost control. Analyzing the competitive advantages of internal departments, I guide staff and management through the process of adapting efficient work methods and procedures. I proudly represent the number one nutritional company in the US and guide you to focus on your health first!
