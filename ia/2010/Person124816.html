Julia Stege’s Summary

As an Intuitive Branding Artist, Website Designer, Social Media Expert, Attraction Marketer and Google Strategist, I help conscious entrepreneurs and organizations to authentically attract and inspire their perfect customers.

Though I began my career as a designer and marketing expert on Madison Avenue, the traditional "brainwashing and hype" approach to marketing was not a fit for me. I eventually moved west to discover a new paradigm for marketing that is authentic, inpiring and meaningul.

To that end I create communications that project people's heart-felt missions and attract an abundance of people to forward those missions. Whether it is a product or service or an organization that is out to transform the world, I help leaders and entrepreneurs to express themselves authentically and attractively.

My expertise is in creating beautiful branding and marketing pieces that speak your mission to the world and attract your perfect customers.

I also love to help people attract perfect customers using the Strategic Attraction Plan TM combined with thrifty self-marketing techniques. I offer regular teleseminars combining Law of Attraction Marketing, Branding from the Heart, and Social Networking Strategies to help entrepreneurs become wildly attractive.

In an effort to protect our last remaining wild rainforests, I am also a Rainforest Ambassador for the Amazon Herb Company, engaged in a sustainable eco business that preserves the rainforest through a trusting partnership with indigenous tribes.
