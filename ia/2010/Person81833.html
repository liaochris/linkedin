Hersh Davis-Nitzberg’s Summary

Hersh Davis-Nitzberg graduated from the class of 1999 at Princeton University with a Degree in Religion. While attending university, he was actively involved in athletic, humanitarian and entrepreneurial pursuits. He was recruited as a Varsity Football player winning the Ivy League Championships in 1995. While attending Princeton University, Davis- Nitzberg founded two corporations: a non-profit and public event publicity firm.

His first venture, in which he is currently the acting CFO, "The Ghana Education project", is a full 501-c-3, non profit tax exempt organization that currently has built and maintained 26 literacy and AIDS education centers in rural Ghana, West Africa. He is currently
the acting CFO of this organization. In addition, while at University he and another partner founded LA2nite.com, apublilc relations and event marketing firm that through 2004 produced, promoted and organized well over 1, 000 events.

Now Davis-Nitzberg has well over 15 years as a strategic consultant actively financing Media investments from nightclubs to movies. He has excelled in celebrity, publicity and event handling experience as well as image consulting, strategic financing, and business development. Based on his strong relationships and insider intelligence, he has quickly gained a reputation as a successful entrepreneur.

Davis-Nitzberg is now working in the field of film financing and image consulting, acting as CEO of both Regal Image Consultants and Dionysus Films.
