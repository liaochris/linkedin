Colin Mathews’s Summary

I believe that software development is a craft and that the key to producing great software is simplicity from conception to production.

My primary work is for Dialawg.com, a startup company where I am the co-founder and sole technical member. My partner and I are jointly responsible for all marketing, but all other aspects of the system are my work alone.

Dialawg is currently a bootstrapped operation, so to fund this endeavor I am available for contract work through my consulting company, Something Future, or through part-time employment.

A few misc items: I am the technical reviewer of a book that is coming out in the near future on the Yahoo User Interface Library. I created an open source project called YUI.NET (now defunct) that was mentioned on the YUI blog. YUI.NET served as a wrapper to easily add UI elements to .NET projects.
