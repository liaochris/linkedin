Eric Fullerton’s Summary

The bulk of my practical experience rests in finding creative and innovative ways to market and sell ski resort destination products. I have spent the last 8 years intimately involved with the marketing and sales strategies for Aspen/Snwomass Resort. Over the past four years this has culminated in developing and experimenting with online channels particularly in the fields of search engine optimization/marketing (SEO/SEM), exposure through various internet distribution channels, and more recently Web 2.0 implementation strategies for capitalizing on new trends.

I love architecting creative solutions for companies that want to utilize technology and the internet to build business with low cost/high ROI solutions. The experience and knowledge I have gained with my passion for knowledge of technological trends, sales acumen, and marketing education has created a unique path for business development.

I am currently transitioning out of my role at Stay Aspen Snowmass Central Reservations to pursue a life long goal of developing my own company. This company, Marketing Stimulus (2.5 years old), will have a main focus of developing business for medium size to Fortune 500 companies through "new media/Web 2.0" channels and consulting for future strategic internet implementation.

I have presented at and helped produce a ski industry conference known as Partners in Group Sales annually.

Currently I serve as President and Chairman of the Board for Roaring Fork Leadership (formerly Leadership Aspen).

I also volunteer and participate in several local non-profit organizations including Susan G Komen's ride for the Cure, Aspen Winternational World Cup, and a variety of others in the past.
