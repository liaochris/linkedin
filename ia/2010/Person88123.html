James Heckman’s Summary

I offer business development services to companies in the technology and marketing fields. I bring more than a decade of marketing experience ranging from developing full marketing plans and opportunity assessments to the practical implementation of Internet, social media, online video, email, referral, direct mail and mobile marketing programs.

Little Wolf is a marketing consulting agency that helps companies and organizations make the most effective use of new technologies - from social media, video and mobile to good old-fashioned websites and email. Little Wolf specializes in working with membership organizations and professional societies, but works in other fields as well.

The bulk of my work with JAK Sales & Marketing is to serve as the business development arm of a variety of agencies and consulting firms. My client mix ranges from peer-to-peer, email and mobile marketing programs to IT services. For most, I offer a combination of strategic marketing consulting and sales.

James Heckman’s Specialties:

Lead generation, online video, email marketing strategy, mobile marketing strategy, social media strategy and implementation, consulting sales, copywriting, presentations, business and marketing plan development, business coaching and training.
