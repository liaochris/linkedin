Lisa Raymond’s Summary

My goal is to work freelance in my field and build my company into a rock-solid design company. I want to build my business so this is the only career I have.

I worked at the West Valley View from 2002-2008, creating ads for both print and web, as well as preflighting client-provided ads for ad specification requirements. For the past three years I have co-taught newspaper design/layout one semester each year at a local high school. I have worked freelance for the past 12 years. I hold intermediate knowledge in Adobe CS3 Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign and currently am enrolled to achieve my B.A. in Web Design/Multimedia.
