Rich Roberts’s Summary

I manage my own consultancy, RDR PR LLC, write a column for Hotel News Now (www.hotelnewsnow.com), the news and research portal of Smith Travel Research, and work with:

* O'Rourke & Partners, which produces This Just In, a cost-effective advertorial product that provides PR and marketing professionals a guaranteed way to get on cable and satellite TV. One-minute This Just In segments are designed to offer the look and credibility of a news broacast. Clients can specify the day and time for broadcast and choose outlets that target their specific demographics. The cost to reach 8 million viewers is $45K. Detailed reports are provided after each broadcast that document outlets reached and total impressions. See these examples:

* Mondographics, a Web-development and graphic design firm that specializes in e-commerce, animation, search engine optimization, e-mail marketing and online newsletters (www.mondographics.com)

* Patrick Barry & Associates, a Long Beach, Calif., communications firm that specializes in advertising, publicity, sports marketing and cause-related promotional campaigns (www.prpros.com)

* Riverview Graphics, a graphic design firm that specializes in print and PDF design, development and printing.

My background: I have worked for two of the world’s largest lodging franchisors, Wyndham Worldwide and Choice Hotels International; two full-service advertising agencies; the resorts and attractions of Hershey, Pa.; and the daily newspaper in Harrisburg, Pa.

My clients include Dolce Hotels and Resorts, Hilton International, Royal Caribbean Cruise International and CCRA Travel Solutions.
