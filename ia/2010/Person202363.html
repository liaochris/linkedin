Theresa Hathaway’s Summary

The Hathaway Group has a blog that is visible at www.thgmarketing.com. The information we provide will help your business to make sense of the constantly changing marketing and media world. The "Media World" can be rewarding and constant challenge for a business owner or decision maker. The overwhelming infiltration of technology has brought into question the ease of reaching "your" best customers. No longer can we work with the philosophy "if you build it...they will come". We have learned that when we build it...we must market "it".

Our professional experience is based on dealing with multiple types of businesses as a both a media sales person and a marketing consultant. Both aspects are essential and we consistently look for new and interesting ways to use our experience to benefit our clients.

The ultimate goal is to create a marketing plan that will increase the profitability and grow the sales of small and medium sized local businesses. The ultimate goal is to build new business and improve existing business through improvement of visibility/recognition.
