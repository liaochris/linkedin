Jeri Cartwright, APR’s Summary

Thanks for your interest!

Founded in 1996, my company is an insightful team of senior-level business communicators. We were all once journalists. Newsroom experience and perspective is critical during tumultuous times in the news industry. We have trusted reporter/editor contacts. We understand the need for a careful hand in community, shareholder, and employee communications. And we have practical business ideas for social/citizen/online media formats.

Often, because PR firms use their senior people in management, they employ less-experienced individuals to do the frontline work, which is often risky. We don't. We understand the need for a careful hand.

Clients include a number of well-known national, regional and local companies in the areas of education, healthcare, finance, commercial real estate, telecommunications and business-to-business. Among those: University of Phoenix, Sprint PCS, Nextel, Bank of American Fork, Commerce CRG and Reebok.

Services include:

PR, Media & Community Relations
Image & Issue Management
Spokesperson Training
New Media / Blogging / Social Networking Strategies

My prior experience: Ten years in television and radio news as a reporter, producer and anchor in Salt Lake City, Utah and Tucson, Arizona. Additional background: Communications Director for the Salt Lake Convention & Visitors Bureau and Salt Lake County Commission.

Honors:

Cartwright Communications received a 2002 Giant Step Small Business Award from the Salt Lake Chamber. We Magazine selected my blog www.mediarelations.blogs.com as one of 101 to watch in 2009. I was honored to be one of Utah Business Magazine's 30 Women to Watch in 2005.

I am a past president of IABC (Utah Chapter), past president of the Utah Advertising Federation, past board member of PRSA (Greater Salt Lake Chapter), Tracy Aviary, Better Business Bureau of Utah. Member: Society of Professional Journalists, PRSA Counselors Academy, Social Media Club of Salt Lake City.
