Jesse Stay’s Summary

I am a Social Media technologies consultant. Recently named by Mashable as one of 20 developers to follow on Twitter, I specialize in Facebook, Bebo, MySpace, Twitter, Open Social, and other technologies that enable you as a business to better reach your customers in a viral manner, leading to more targeted ad and marketing positions, leading to faster adaptation of your brand through the social networks.

I have firm experience developing and providing quality applications that have already had strong successes. I have both developed and consulted for successful social networking applications, some of those which currently have over a million users (the top 10 on Facebook!).

I write on these topics through my books, my own blog StayNAlive.com, LouisGray.com, InsideFacebook.com, AllFacebook.com, and others. My writing has been featured on Techmeme, TechCrunch, Mashable, ReadWriteWeb, and elsewhere. I understand the blogosphere and know how bloggers think (as I am one), and can help your business gain the exposure it needs.
