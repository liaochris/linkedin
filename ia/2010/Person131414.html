Marcia Cole’s Summary

A gifted content creator and editorial strategist with expertise in beauty, fashion, entertainment and lifestyle topics targeting general market and multicultural female audiences. More than twenty years of superior management, editorial and business development experience. Has held executive-level positions at leading media corporations including Time Inc., Fairchild Publications, Vanguarde Media and Latina Media Ventures. Effectively supervised multi-million dollar editorial operational budgets, and launched or re-launched print and online brands for marketplace relevancy and long-term strategic growth. Impeccable work ethic and judicious hiring manager with experience recruiting and managing editorial, art, photo, sales and marketing professionals of all levels including freelance and offshore personnel. A passionate and results-driven leader, with the skill and talent to create the mission, define the goals, plan and execute strategic and tactical endeavors.
Seasoned producer of engaging, high-quality content across video, print, and digital platforms with freelance articles about beauty, fashion and entertainment that have appeared in Elle, Essence, InStyle Weddings, Vibe and The New York Times. Sought-after media commentator, moderator and panelist on entertainment, career advancement and style topics for professional media organizations and networks such as TVOne, BET, and media outlets The Biography Channel and Urban Talk Radio.
