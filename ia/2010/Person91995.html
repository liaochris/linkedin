Bryan Phillips’s Summary

Our mission includes helping conscious clients, who have products and/or services we believe in, grow their business online and do it with integrity.


Over six years of hardcore internet marketing experience combined with the ability to take multiple perspectives (client, customer, partner, competition, systems) have contributed to our model for providing custom holistic online marketing strategies. We use multiple online mediums, based on the niche, competition, and goals to most effectively attract & retain visitors, leads, and customers.

Our clients' offerings become our offerings. We research their target market (tribe) and view the offerings through the tribes' eyes in order to find, grow, and influence the tribe. This includes understanding the competition as well as the tribe and the offering.

We attract the tribe to your website with search engine marketing (SEO/PPC), social marketing (Facebook, Twitter), email marketing, industry forums, etc.

We don't stop at traffic. We are NOT simply an SEO or a PPC company. Using Web Analytics, metrics are used to guide website optimization efforts to take advantage of the targeted traffic our clients receive. Analytics allows us to track each of our campaign efforts whether it be PPC, Email, Banner ads, SEO, etc. to determine a true ROI on your online marketing investment.

I am a technologist with over 12 years of software development experience (tester, programmer, technical lead, architect, manager) turned internet marketer (2003) and business owner (2006).

We are confident in our ability and approach & our results are GUARANTEED or we will work for free until your results are achieved.

If your company has products or services that are congruent with our mission and are willing to work WITH us to help your business grow, please drop me a line.
