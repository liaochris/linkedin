Ed Wills’s Summary

I am a business consultant specializing in franchise and small business education, selection, financing and management. I enjoy a broad background in accounting, management, information technology, teaching, writing, and theology. I am a former US Navy officer having served on the USS America and in the Amphibious CBs. I have owned several businesses. My current businesses include the Hard Bean Cafe, Inter-Networking.net and WFA Franchise Consultants.

I start with the premise that everyone should enjoy what they do. To that end I provide a free service for my clients, connecting them with a franchise or other business opportunity that is a perfect fit for their interests, expertise, and financial and lifestyle goals. My objective is for my clients to experience the benefit of a successful future in personal business ownership.

The American Dream is no longer embodied by the steady job and a house in the suburbs. Top employees have found themselves laid off with there house in the suburbs in jeopardy. Security is never found working for someone else. Security is only found in owning your own company and your own future. My varied experiences help me understand the true needs and desires of people seeking to transition from job to self employment. I seek to help people discover their purpose in life and fulfill their desires through writing, speaking and one on one coaching and counseling.

I am currently writing a book under the working title of “Success is Closer Than You Think”. Through key principles and step by step activities, you can experience success in both your business and personal life. I would like to help you enjoy that success.
