Thomas Gilmore’s Summary

I am a branding and design professional with a strong background in the strategy, design and implementation of consumer packaged goods innovations and initiatives.

Making an emotive connection with consumers—by leveraging strategy and design—is at the heart of my work.

Throughout my career, I have delivered the best possible level of creativity, service and leadership for a variety of consumer products clients including: Procter & Gamble, Lowe’s, Tyson, Heinz, AT&T, Valvoline, Pﬁzer, Wendy's, Hunter, Borden, World Finer Foods, World Kitchen and John Morrell.

Currently, I am a graduate student with College of Design, Architecture, Art and Planning (DAAP) at University of Cincinnati, pursuing a Master of Design degree in visualization with the School of Design. Additionally—working with UC’s College of Business—I have led a graduate level MS-Marketing Capstone project.

As an undergraduate, I studied design and communication at Bowling Green State University where I earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design. I am committed to lifelong learning through intellectual, creative and personal growth. I am also a member of the Adjunct Faculty with the School of Fine Arts at Miami University–most recently teaching the graphic design senior thesis and business of design classes.
