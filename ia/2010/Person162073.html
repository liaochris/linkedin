Peggy Mengel’s Summary

Peggy (Spilker) Mengel's 25-year career in the financial services industry has been a unique blend of sales, marketing, training and management. As a result, Peggy brings a pragmatic and "real life" approach to her coaching and consulting practice, The Biz Sherpa.

Ms. Mengel is passionate about helping her clients build business from the "inside-out" by focusing on their unique strengths and managing their struggles.

Her strategic partnership with DNA Behavior International allows her to offer her clients unique online personality profiles and programs.

As CEO and co-founder of Business Women Retreats, Peggy has taken the philosophy, "To build better business, build a better you", and created one-day retreats. Incorporating mind, body, and spirit concepts, business women will develop strategies to align their business activities with their core strengths and values.
