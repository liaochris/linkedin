Irwin Kramer’s Summary

As Managing Partner at Kramer & Connolly, Irwin Kramer and his AV-rated litigation team provide strategic and aggressive representation of litigation clients to meet their objectives in state and federal courts. Working closely with their clients, Kramer & Connolly assist both large and small companies, professionals and insurance carriers in making informed decisions with a full understanding of their legal rights and options in specific situations. This partnership, combined with cutting-edge technology that enhances the efficiency of its services, has enabled the firm to maintain longstanding and loyal relationships with clients around the globe.

Mr. Kramer's experience as an advocate also feeds a passion for making the law more accessible to the public at large. Combining more than two decades of trial experience in high-profile cases with 13 years of on-air television experience, Irwin Kramer formed The Legal Television Network. Under the CLIENTELEVISION® brand, LTVN produced a variety of innovative features for syndication via cable, satellite, broadband, video on demand, and traditional broadcast media. Understanding the needs of professionals to market their services, Irwin works with accountants, lawyers, financial planners, real estate agents, insurance agents and a wide range of other professional services providers to enhance online marketing efforts with CLIENTELEVISION®.
