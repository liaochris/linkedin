Julie Montgomery- jmontgomery@plexent.com’s Summary

Please feel free to contact me directly at julesmontgo@gmail.com or jmontgomery@plexent.com

I have spent 17 years experience in both executive search and corporate human capital management, with leading firms such as Richard, Wayne and Roberts; Paranet; Sprint Paranet; and Sprint Enterprise Network Services.
During my tenure with Paranet, I built, trained and managed the 50 person recruiting organization that grew the company from approximately 250 technical consultants in 1994 to over 1,700 in 1997. In addition to my expertise in executive search, interview process development, candidate sourcing techniques and employee retention, I am a certified Behavioral Interview instructor. I authored the recruiter training class entitled, "Direct Sourcing in a Crazy IT Market,” as well as the People Capability ModelTM, an employee development and selection tool.

In 2001, I took on marketing responsibilities and have loved helping build brands, web sites and social media programs. I'm an avid writer and even have my own blog entitled The High Tech Mom (NOT representing the opinions of my employer, of course!)
