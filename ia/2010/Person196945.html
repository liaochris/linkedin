Sue Blaney’s Summary

ChangeWorks Publishing is dedicated to supporting parents in successfully raising teenagers. I'm an author, speaker and publisher; I speak nationally to parents of teenagers, educators and faith leaders on parenting issues, improving communication, increasing parent involvement and creating parent discussion groups.

My flagship product is titled Please Stop the Rollercoaster! How Parents of Teenagers Can Smooth Out the Ride. It is a parent discussion group program that has been connecting parents in living rooms, schools, churches and synagogues across the country since 2003. All of our products enhance communication between parents and teenagers; this is our focus and our passion.

Our 3-hour audio program has won two national awards: in January 2010 we received a "Mom's Choice" award and in 2009 we received a silver Ben Franklin award for best audio book. You're Empowered! Parenting Teens with Conviction, Communication and Love is available in a 3 CD set and as an MP3 download. Visit our website for more info and a free excerpt.
