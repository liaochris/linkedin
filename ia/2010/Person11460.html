Andres Barreto’s Summary

President of Socialatom Group, an online communication and technology firm focusing on social media, public relations and startups in Latin America.

Previously, I co-founded Grooveshark (2006), a music-streaming app, and helped take it from an idea on paper to a thriving young startup that today reaches over 22 million monthly people online and is quickly becoming the number one contender in the online music scene.

After this success, I shifted my focus towards Latin America, and expanded Socialatom Group to include Communications/PR and New Ventures.

With Socialatom we have co-founded and helped launch startups including:

MarcaSocial (2009) - Customer service and market research tool for brands in Latin America.

PulsoSocial (2008) - the leading online media publication about social media, entrepreneurship and technology in Latin America, with an audience comprised of the most influential bloggers, journalists, investors and entrepreneurs in the region.

My experience is in building new businesses and products of large impact, user experience design, community building, social media marketing, social application development, public relations and social entrepreneurship.

I am available to speak at conferences (English/Spanish) on the subjects of entrepreneurship, innovation and social media. For a list of previous conferences, please see the Honors & Awards section.
