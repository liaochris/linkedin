Caitlin Fitzsimmons’s Summary

I have a track record of 10 years as a writer and editor. My journalism credentials include four years at The Australian in Sydney, regular work at The Guardian in London, and writing credits everywhere from Rough Guides to Business Week. I have several years experience as a features editor and as a news editor, so I am accustomed to commissioning work, setting editorial direction, managing staff and budgets, liaising with other departments and copy-editing articles. I am detail-oriented, motivated and positive.

I am also a blogger and heavily involved in social media. I have been working on my own travel and food blog Roaming Tales (http://www.roamingtales.com) for over two years, although I have had a personal blog for longer. I do photography and video as well as writing and I am proud of the site and the following I have built up. I also write for sites such as EcoSalon and my work has been syndicated to the Huffington Post. I am well-connected with other bloggers and as a participating blogger in the 2009 Passports with Purpose charity fundraiser I was able to use my blog as leverage to secure prizes worth over $2,700.

I have international experience. I am from Australia and I lived in London for five years, before moving to San Francisco. I was an exchange student in Germany in high school and I worked as a volunteer on community development projects in Costa Rica and Guyana in my early twenties. I hold a Master in International Studies from the University of Sydney.
