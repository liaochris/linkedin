Su-Jit Lin’s Summary

A bit of an idealist, I still subscribe to the old-fashioned belief that hard work will yield fruitful results and that opportunity is what you make of it. Therefore, my professional reputation for beating deadlines, exhibiting a diligent work ethic and attention to detail, and being easy to work with is one that I'm particularly proud of.

I've a creative, entrepreneurial spirit, a fearless, positive attitude, and a sharp and inquisitive mind -- which means if you need something done, I can find a way, whether I've done it before or not. And if it's never been done ... well, I learn quickly and love adding more knowledge to my personal store and sharing it with my team.

With three years of experience writing for various travel guidebooks and regional publications in New Orleans (St. Charles Avenue magazine, Where Y'at Magazine; New Orleans Bride; and Louisiana Cookin', to name a few), online, and various copywriting assignments, I'm experienced enough to know that working with words is what I want to do for the rest of my life. However, I'm young enough to be flexible in my ways and to know that there's a lot to learn still out there, and always will be as media evolves.

With red pen in hand, I'm ready, able, and willing. Let's write on.
