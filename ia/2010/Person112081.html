Kathryn Funk’s Summary

I have worked in the information industry for five years in an editorial capacity. Currently, I work full-time as a production editor and part-time toward my Masters in Library and Information Science. I will complete my graduate degree in December 2009.

I am passionate about developing high quality information and making it accessible to as wide an audience as possible, which has led me to both my current career and educational path. Among the courses I have completed toward my degree are Cataloging and Classification, Online Information Retrieval, Information Sources and Services, Collection Development, Media Services, Libraries and Information in Society, Organization of Information, and Library and Information Systems. To complete my degree I will be participating in Catholic University's Federal Library Institute and taking Database Management, Foundations of Digital Libraries, and the History of the Book.

Kathryn Funk’s Specialties:

Internet and database (Dialog, ProQuest, Factiva, LexisNexis, Web of Science, PsycINFO) searching; cataloging and classification (MARC, Library of Congress Subject Headings, Dewey Decimal Classification); HTML coding; social media (blogs, wikis, Facebook); annotated bibliographies; writing and editing
