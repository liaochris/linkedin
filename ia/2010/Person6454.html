Alex Feinberg’s Summary

Software Engineer with operations experience. Presently focused on developing highly available distributed systems and systems programming at Internet scale. Additional interests in search/information retrieval, operating systems, programming languages and functional programming. Always curious to learn new topics in Computer Science and related areas.

I believe in importance of first-class infrastructure, built with specific and realistic goals. Server-side software should also be built with operations goals in mind. A long time open-source lurker and a recent contributor, I've come to see the benefits of (when feasible) open sourcing generic infrastructure components.
