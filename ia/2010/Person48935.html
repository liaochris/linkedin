Darrell Evans’s Summary

Darrell has been in the real estate, financial services, and mortgage industries since 1992 all in Las Vegas, NV where he was born and raised.

As a mortgage lending authority with significant finance, economic background and real estate background, he's been instrumental in helping people reach their financial goals as it relates to real estate and other financial services. He is client focused and strives to not only meet, but exceed his client's expectations. He's built a strong referral base of past clients and business referral partners to include top Las Vegas Realtors and financial planners.

Darrell Evans’s Specialties:

Expert mortgage origination and underwriting, understanding of today's mortgage guidelines, credit enhancement techniques, counseling and guidance, strategic investment advising to investors, first time home buyers and secondary home buyers with an emphasis on economic and secondary market influences and dynamics and how they impact the industry. Consumer groups served - first time home buyers, investors, move up buyers, vacation home buyers, homeowners seeking to refinance
