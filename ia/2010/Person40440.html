Ciera Holzenthal’s Summary

Ciera at The H Agency:
Some projects for established clients just sail through the agency. We have a remarkable turn-around time for the complexity of our process and the number of projects we’re working on at any given time. On the ones that sail through with a strong tail wind, Ciera is usually the designer in the driver’s seat. Her incredible focus and organization leave the rest of us in her wake when it comes to sheer efficiency and effectiveness. But don’t let her quiet exterior fool you, there’s a creative dynamo whirring inside her and an astounding computer-like memory of where everything we’ve ever worked on is located. And she can put her hands on it in a moment.

The H Agency is a strategic branding, marketing and design agency founded in 1990 by president and creative director Winnie Hart. The company has grown from a local New Orleans design firm into a regional agency that specializes in developing marketing programs and brand strategies for health and wellness and financial services companies. In our 18+ years of service to clients, we have been recognized with more than 120 top industry awards for excellence in design, marketing and communications.

Ciera Holzenthal’s Specialties:

branding and identity, creative strategies, internal and external communication, media coordination and placement, client relationships, visual identity and industry research.
