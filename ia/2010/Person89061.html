Jan De Volder’s Summary

Managing Director of the Malta-based internet company Volo Media Ltd.

Providing services like Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Advertising (SEA) and Online Business Consultancy to clients worldwide.

We are also what they call a "Super Affiliate", working for networks like TradeDoubler and Commission Junction. We specialize mainly in Keyword Buying (SEA), but are branching out to development and maintenance of our own websites as well.

December 2007, we launched PLAYdiplomacy.com, a website where you can play the boardgame Diplomacy online. In 1.5 years time the website attracted 20.000 registered members of which more than 500 paid for a "Premium membership".

In 2009, Volo Media is releasing several Facebook applications. The first one is Scratchix, a free scratch card game to win prizes, which launched in February 2009 and already has 40.000 monthly active users (April 2009).
