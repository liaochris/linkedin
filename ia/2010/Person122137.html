Laureen Earnest’s Summary

My goals are two-fold based on 30 years in technology with a creative entreprenuerial spirit:

1. to be an authentic reliable resource to my ever expanding network for all things digital, social, and strategic to their business success.

2. to help song writers and authors with their conversion to digital content by creating books with music where both the lyrics and the song are infused into the pure reading experience.
