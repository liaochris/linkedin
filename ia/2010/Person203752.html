Tim Cox’s Summary

For the past 12 years, I have developed strategic and tactical public relations campaigns for companies of all shapes and sizes, across many different industries.

I particularly like working with start-ups and emerging companies - I thrive on the faster pace, the pioneer attitude, and because it's always fun to work with entrepreneurs like myself.

I offer a comprehensive set of services, which work best when integrated into a broad program:
- messaging and positioning formulation
- PR strategy
- media relations
- analyst relations
- news programs
- blog programs (fully or partially outsourced)
- thought leadership programs
- social media (e.g. Facebook, Twitter) programs
- customer success programs
- newsletter programs

I also work with a network of marketing services providers that can offer anything from deep marketing research to website development/SEO/SEM and Salesforce/bizdev optimization.

Prior to founding and heading up ZingPR, I ran corporate marketing, marketing communications, promotions and product marketing functions for companies such as Alias, Apple, Autodesk and Schlumberger.
