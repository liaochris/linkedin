Jeffrey Sicuranza’s Summary

I am a seasoned (but not salted) networking technology professional with a sense of humor and a proven track record of achievement in the IT Industry. I recently completed a entrepreneurial venture in which I took time away from my consulting practice to pursue. My venture was based on a Green IT solution.

I developed an IT based alternative energy harvesting solution to assist enterprise data centers in reducing their power consumption costs. I created a line of IT Server and Switch prototypes that convert waste heat into usable electricity.
