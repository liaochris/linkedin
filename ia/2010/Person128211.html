Louis Rothschild’s Summary

Having worked in a variety of settings and with diverse individuals, Louis has developed expertise in cultural and individual assessment. To that end, he specializes in facilitating change, both in regard to healing/repair and growth/renewal. These may be considered divergent aspects of what is often considered essential to psychotherapy on the one hand and coaching on the other.
