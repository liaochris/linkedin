Tracy Cote’s Summary

An active member of the San Francisco human resources community, my extensive HR experience includes over 15 years as a human resources professional in various industries, including tenure in digital marketing, financial services and retail sectors, where I cultivated HR programs designed to position my companies as premier providers in their industries. These programs include compensation, benefits, employee development and training, employee relations, international HR, recruiting and workforce planning.

I am currently the Executive Director of Human Resources at Organic, Inc., in San Francisco California, where I manage all HR programs worldwide. Organic, Inc. is a digital marketing agency that designs and builds exceptional interactive experiences for leading companies, driving brand awareness and more profitable customer relationships for its clients.

Originally joining Organic in October of 1999, I was instrumental in the development of the HR function from the company’s earliest days as a start-up company and subsequent international growth and IPO.

I left Organic from 2002 through 2004 to join Charles Schwab, to provide counsel and support for a variety of different client groups, including its technology division and its Asia Pacific services group. Prior to 2002, I spent time as the Regional Manager of Human Resources for Mervyn’s, a Dayton Hudson subsidiary and retail chain of department stores. I also worked as a Manager of Human Resources for Lucky Stores, a retail chain of supermarkets.

Currently, I am a member of the faculty of San Francisco State University where I have taught classes in the Human Resources Management Program since 2005.

I am a graduate of Sonoma State University, with a Bachelors Degree in English. I regularly contribute quotes and articles on HR thought leadership to various media outlets and blogs.
