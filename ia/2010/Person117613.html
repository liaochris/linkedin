Richard Kivel’s Summary

Rich presently serves as Chairman, CEO and co-founder of Rhapsody Biologics, a personalized vaccine company based in Singapore. Rhapsody is focused on improving the efficacy of existing vaccines and the development of new-generation vaccines, which are optimized for an individual’s immune response.

Between April 2006 and May 2009, Rich served as CEO of TheraGenetics, Ltd, a UK-based genetic diagnostics company focusing on the development and commercialization of pharmacogenetic diagnostic tests to guide and improve the treatment of Central Nervous System (CNS) disorders. In Jan 2009 TheraGenetics was successfully acquired by UK based Avacta Group plc. LSE: (AVCT). Rich remained as CEO after the acquisition and continued to lead the growth of TheraGenetics under the new ownership until the summer of 2009.

Prior to this, Rich has worked with multiple startups and served as CEO of MolecularWare, Inc., a MIT spin-out and leader in genomic and proteomic microarray technologies. Under his leadership, MolecularWare secured venture capital and debt funding. In June, 2003, the company was acquired by a California based company, CalbaTech, Inc. in a cash & stock transaction.

Rich presently serves as Chairman of the Global Board of Directors of the MIT Enterprise Forum and a member of the MIT Alumni Association Board. Rich holds the position of Senior Lecturer at the MIT Sloan School of Management and serves as a judge for the MIT $100K Entrepreneurship Competition.
