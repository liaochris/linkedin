Troy Brown’s Summary

After 8 years with Timberland, I've recently joined Demandware, a startup in the ecommerce platform space. There, I'll be helping to shape marketing, product, sales, and service support by integrating a retailer's mindset into the way Demandware delivers it's Software As A Service business model.

At Timberland, I led a team of ~20 in both the U.S. and in Europe to drive P&L results through multiple branded ecommerce sites. I was responsible for the global Ecommerce business unit P&L's for The Timberland Company and oversaw all aspects of timberland.com including Online Marketing, Product Merchandising, Web Design & Development, & Operations. Our most recent focus was driving comp growth from existing businesses while launching new sites through geographic expansion. Prior to joining the Ecommerce team in early 2004, I worked with Timberland International and led strategic planning efforts in Europe & Asia, managed Latin America, launched the Canadian subsidiary, and launched women's apparel in Europe.

Prior to Timberland, I held a variety of positions in manufacturing, sales & marketing, consulting, and finance & operations at Pepsi, IBM, McKinsey and Disney, respectively.

My goal is to always work with great people in a winning culture with a product or service that has the potential to become a market leader in its space.
