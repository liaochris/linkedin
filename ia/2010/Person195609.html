Steve Williams’s Summary

I have been in the Computer industry since 1988. I joined Dell in early 1993 and advanced there until March, 2006, I rose throught the ranks of sales and management and ultimately found my niche when I became became a Systems Consultant. In March of 2006, I began a new chapter, in my career when I joined Ardence. My role with Ardence was to be the primary technical contact for Dell and key distributors regarding our virtual disk technology. Ardence was acquired by Citrix in January of 2007. Because of this acquisition, my role expanded to also cover XenServer as an OEM offering with Dell.

In February of 2009, I experienced a layoff due in large part to the downturn in the economy. I found myself fortunate in that my reputation helped me find a new role with a great company that leads the industry in high performance, and low latency interconnects for super computers. In April of 2009 I became the Business Development Manager to Dell for Mellanox Technologies.
