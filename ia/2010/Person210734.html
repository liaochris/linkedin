Virtual Assistant Suzanne Roy’s Summary

Having finished high school in England at age 15, I began working as a secretary to a local lift company and quickly became knowledgeable in the many facets of the administrative position. After a few years I decided to move back to Canada and continued my career in the administrative field, working for various insurance companies and the Government of Ontario's Ministry of Industry, Trade and Technology.

Finally finding my home in my current Florida location, I began working for a realtor as a side job and within a short period of time found that I was attracted to the industry as a whole and wanted to pursue my own business in assisting other realtors throughout the U.S. with the skills I had gained and continue to gain today. The beautiful thing about working as a Virtual Assistant is that 100% of my work is done via the internet, and will not be replaced anytime soon. It also allows me to keep up-to-date with the ever-changing online business practices and using them within my business - including such applications as SEO tools, blogs, twitter, facebook and more.

I am working toward assisting other individuals who want to have the same freedom that I have in working from home and putting together a series of blogs and tutorials for such individuals to learn the ropes of working as a virtual assistant. My goal is to help as many people become independently wealthy and happy, just as I am.
