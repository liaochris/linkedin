  • Owner/Developer/Lead Instructor

    Little Linksters, LLC

    (Privately Held; Recreational Facilities and Services industry)

    August 2008 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Since July of 2008, the Little Linksters Golf program has brought the game of golf to a typically non-tradional sect of the golfing population; the "Pee-Wee" Golfer (Ages 3-8). The Little Linksters Golf Program is a great introduction to the game of golf for for all children in this age group. The program is taught in a fun and interactive way using a combination of both traditional golf instruction methods and very non-traditional ways. The bottom line with the Little Linksters program is FUN!

    The program is run by PGA Golf Professional Brendon Elliott. Brendon is the Head Golf Professional at the historic Winter Park Country Club located in downtown Winter Park, FL and he is also the owner of Little Linksters, LLC and LittleLinksters.com which is his Junior Golf Instruction Business in Deltona as well as Brendon Elliott Golf, LLC and BrendonElliottGolf.com.

    The Little Linksters program has been run at various daycare centers, schools, and municipalities in the Lake Mary, FL and Deltona, FL areas since July of 2008. Expansion of the program is in the works. After almost two years of running our "pilot" or "test" programs, we have started taking steps toward taking this central Florida based, part-time business and program to a broader, national market. We are rebranding, creating an easy to follow program guide for both the kids and instructors, and putting a great package together for other PGA Professionals that may want to share in the rewarding work of bringing golf and all of the lessons that it teaches to the littlest of golfers

    The NEW BrendonElliottGolf.com and LittleLinksters.com websites launch in March 2010. Complete details of the newly renovated program and information on packages that are in the works to be licenced out to PGA Professionals will be on the site very soon.
