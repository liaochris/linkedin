Kristina Courtnage Bowman’s Summary

Kristina manages web communications and social media channels for the University of Washington Department of Communication. She also coordinates instructional resources for faculty and students. While working as a journalist in print media at The Bellingham Herald and The Spokesman-Review, she began noticing a shift in newspapers to digital media and wanted to learn more. In 2006, she started the UW Master of Communication in Digital Media where she experimented with different methods of storytelling and social media. She now spends time working with future communicators on digital media skills that will give them an edge in the work place. She also maintains communication channels such as the e-newsletter, web site and social media such as Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn. She believes strong writing and communication skills are invaluable for creating a usable product and works to nurture these skills in others.
