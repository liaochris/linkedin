Audrey Tan’s Summary

An inspired explorer on a mission to empower children in the world, beginning with Financial Literacy. Dedicating her time, energy and passion to creating an educational tool that teaches children financial education. Audrey Co-Founded Moolah, an online tool to teach children to appreciate the value of money.

Audrey loves children, they bring tremendous joy! Much enjoy the beauty of Technology for it enables, intrigues and adopts creative solutions to aid learning. Passionate about learning and education, how our young learns, how our brain adapts to learning and how we make choices.

Keen in alleviating issues surrounding Poverty in parts of the world, GiveMoolah cooking in our labs!

Research areas includes,Triggers in Persuasive Technology and Persuasive Games in Savings and Money Management. Epic Win, Gamification of effective task managers. Currently, exploring the extended concepts of Learning through Gamification. Maria Montessori Methods for child development and learning.

Having spent time in California and studying at Stanford University. Audrey is pursued a degree in Communications and New Media, at the National University of Singapore.
