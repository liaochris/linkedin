Catherine Whitney’s Summary

I have always been comfortable initiating partnerships. I enjoy building enthusiastic buy-in from traditionally competitive entities and have supervised collaborative partnerships with state and federal government agencies, economic development entities, professional associations, and universities. I have presented keynote speeches as an expert on inter-organizational partnerships and have programmed several educational conferences including a technology pavilion for the City of Chicago.

With my MA in Communications and an International MBA, I serve as an advisor to the De Paul Graduate Program, providing CEO’s and top business consultants for the Coleman Entrepreneurship Center. I am a published songwriter, perform internationally with jazz groups, and as a TV producer/director/writer/editor, supervised cross-functional teams, designed and implemented marketing projects, presented highly technical demonstrations. My experience in relationship management and my work as a consultant to senior level IT executives provides strategies for the changing economy.

Catherine Whitney’s Specialties:
