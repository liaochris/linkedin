Jeff Wolf’s Summary

Go-to-market expert with global marketing, business development, sales and operations leadership experience in the electronics industry. Client engagements have focused on driving strategic planning, promotional campaigns and events for advanced technology solutions to IDMs, foundries, fabless semiconductor and IP companies, ODMs, OEMs, chip designers and consumers. Broad supply and design chain experience with semiconductor technologies, components, embedded systems, EDA, intellectual property and services end-products. Applications experience in computer, multimedia, networking, wireless, communications, consumer and storage markets. Six years board experience.

Jeff Wolf’s Specialties:
