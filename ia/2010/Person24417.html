Ray DuGray & Maria Ngo’s Summary

Ray DuGray and Maria Ngo are a married team known as 'The Entrepreneur Doctors' for their doctoral research studies into entrepreneurship.

SUCCESS TELEVISION TALK SHOW

They are the hosts and producers of 'The STRIP VIEW Live' Success Television Talkshow broadcasting weekly on Vegas TV (KTUD) and globally on more than 50 Web TV and social media networks.

On the show, top celebrities, entrepreneurs and industry experts reveal their success secrets and profound advice ... on the red carpet ... from their homes ... in the studio ... and at live events.


LIFE PURPOSE, VISION & MISSION

Ray and Maria share a purpose to empower entrepreneurs in starting and growing highly successful enterprises and lives filled with abundance and prosperity. They have focused their entrepreneurial doctoral studies and their entire life on this purpose and are dedicated to sharing their advice and their associates expertise in the process. Their research began by targeting interviews with top entrepreneurs, wealth coaches, celebrities, leaders, philanthropists and industry experts worldwide.

Maria & Ray are committed to building the world's largest virtual Mastermind alliance of social entrepreneurs who share the vision of spreading a positive message throughout the world - a message that inspires, motivates, educates, empowers and entertains. Their plans involve building a vast distribution network for their positive success talk show to achieve the greatest reach of any talk show in history.

To learn more about how you may join the Entrepreneur Doctors' Social Entrepreneurship movement, please visit www.EntrepreneurDoctors.com and click on the 'About' link underneath the profile photo on the right.
