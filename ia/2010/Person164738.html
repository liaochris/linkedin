PJ Sands Johnson’s Summary

After years of planning celebrity weddings, concerts & special events at Atlantis Paradise Island in the Bahamas - PJ Sands Johnson launched A Design For Destiny International Group of Companies in 2002. Specializing in destination events in the Caribbean, A Design For Destiny is a supplier of fine retail items for weddings, special occasions and corporate clients.

Our mission is to provide the highest quality in personalized special occasion merchandise at the lowest prices - converging quality and selection with outstanding value and customer service with a vast array of products from world renowned retailers like Carlson Craft, Regency, Birchcraft Studios, Encore Studios, Masterpiece Studios, Beverly Clark, The Knot, Lillian Rose, Krepe Craft, Cathy's Concepts, Hortense B Hewitt, Treasure Masters, Cathy's Concepts, Kate Aspen, The Knot, NuArt and others.

With a flagship location in Nassau, Bahamas a signature boutique in Ocho Rios, Jamaica and a Florida based online retail operation, PJ Sands Johnson's company has become a force to be reckoned with the destination wedding market internationally.

PJ Sands Johnson is a member of the Association of Bridal Consultants, International Special Events Society, Association For Wedding Professionals International and the Bahamas Bridal Association.

event planning, destination event management, special occasion retail including custom invitations, programs, accessories, favors and engraved gifts. Full online store http://www.design4destiny.com.
