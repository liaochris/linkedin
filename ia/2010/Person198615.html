Brian Swichkow’s Summary

Recent graduate of Champlain College with a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia and Graphic design I have always been fascinated with the messages thrown off my marketing and advertising. My fascination quickly grew into an obsession after college upon the launch of my business.

I have since been providing social media consulting and brand strategizing to a variety of small and medium sized businesses. Social media has been the epicenter for the development of my marketing strategies, but is never the sole means. I'm always looking to market every business differently as each has it's own strangest and therefore will have a different experience in effective social marketing.

Marketing can be as social as you want it to be. Businesses that interact with their customers on a genuinely personal level are more likely to get testimonials, referrals, user generated content and other invaluable things that will skyrocket any business.

"Marketing is about doing things for people, not just saying things to them."
