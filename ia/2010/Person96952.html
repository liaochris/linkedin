Jessica Estrada’s Summary

Build your business - expand your network with the Greater Seattle Chamber of Commerce!

I'm a young professional with extensive marketing and events experience from both the corporate and non-profit world.

I aim to make things happen - for myself, the Chamber and for your business. I'm currently the Chamber's Event & Program Manager. I specialize in creating professional development programs to help build your business toolkit, and in producing networking events that help you make connections and possibly meet your next client or customer.

I serve on the Downtown Seattle Association's Retail Core neighborhood marketing committee; the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) Seattle's Program Council; and am proud to lead the Chamber's Young Professionals Network.

I honed my skills in the past for Nike - and still love the brand! - as well as Seattle clothing line Soy Clothing and the Downtown Seattle Association. I earned a B.A. in Communication and a certificate in Retail Management from the University of Washington in Winter 2005.

Hope to see you at a future Chamber event!
