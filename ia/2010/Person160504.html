Paula Woolley’s Summary

My passion is marketing for Estate Planning Attorneys and Certified Divorce Financial Analyst. Both professions offer a valuable service that improves the quality of life of those they serve. My mission is to educate the community about the value they provide and bring potential clients in to the offices of these professionals to get the assistance they so desperately need.

I provide both Marketing Strategy and Development and Implementation to my clients. I have 5 Step process to make sure that the right message gets to the right people at the right time.

Five Step Marketing and Implementation Process:
1. Review of Target Market
2. Establish Marketing Goals and Objectives
3. Development of Marketing Strategies
4. Implementation
5. Determination of ROI

I only work for a few clients at a time to ensure that I can do the best job possible and meet the marketing objectives of each client.
