Parth Suthar’s Summary

I am a designer, researcher and a conceptualist with keen interest in people, technology and the paths where they intersect.

My expertise lies in creating holistic experiences that traverses through different vectors of design practice - information design, branding, ethnography, online presence, as well as deploying technologies that blurs the differences between art and code.
