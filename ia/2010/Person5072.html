Alan Douglas’s Summary

Mr. Douglas is the Vice President - Revenue Cycle at Renal Advantage in Brentwood, TN. Renal Advantage is the third largest provider of outpatient dialysis services with 135 facilities and $500 million in net revenue. RAI has teamed with Welsh, Carson, Anderson & Stowe (WCAS).

From August of 2006 to July of 2008, Mr. Douglas served as Vice President of Revenue Cycle Operations and Controls in the Surgery Center Division for HealthSouth in August 2006 and continued in that position through the divestiture of the Surgery Division in July 2007 from owners HealthSouth to Texas Pacific Group with the entity now known as Surgical Care Affiliates.

From March 2005 until his transition into the Surgery Division, Mr. Douglas served with HealthSouth Corporation as Director of Internal Controls - Revenue Cycle. From August 2001 to March 2005, Mr. Douglas served in a management role with HCA, Inc. in Internal Audit. From 1995 to 2000, he served in various operational and financial management roles with Baptist Hospital System and Tennessee Christian Medical Center Affiliates, Nashville, TN, including Director of Corporate Services and Director of Occupational Medicine, the leading provider of physician practice management, occupational medicine, and primary care services. Prior to acquisition by Baptist Hospital, he served as administrator of Corporate Health Institute, a community leader in occupational and preventive medicine.

Mr. Douglas is a Certified Public Accountant. He is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants.

He is past president of The Massey Graduate School of Business Belmont University Alumni Association.

A native of Tennessee, Mr. Douglas earned his Master of Business Administration at Jack C. Massey School of Business, Belmont University and a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from Tennessee Technological University.
