Margaret Molloy’s Summary

Margaret is a tech marketer with 15+ years of diverse experience. Irish-born, she is a HBS grad and member of the Siebel Systems CEO's Circle. Focused on B2B marketing with an emphasis on strategy, sales enablement, corporate marketing, customer engagement, and product marketing, Margaret is a problem solver with a remarkable capacity for inspiring and delivering breakthrough customer-focused strategies that increase revenue. She is a captivating presenter and motivational general manager. Throughout her career she has demonstrated deep knowledge of enterprise software, media, and economic development sectors.

Margaret Molloy’s Specialties:

B2B marketing across technology, economic development, media, and services. Marketing strategy, general management, team building, CRM, communications, sales effectiveness, strategic messaging, field marketing, customer engagement.
