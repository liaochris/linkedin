Rob Veldt’s Summary

Consultant on (Open) Innovation implementation. Serial entrepreneur and investor; owner and founder of www.ideavents.com, www.ThinkTankThursday.com, www.cash4idea.com, www.ZuidWesterSloep.nl.

Twitter: http://twitter.com/RobVeldt

Mission: leverage innovation by making it easier, more accessible (incl. cheaper) and fun.

Rob translates new (online) trends in the area of knowledge exchange and innovation to useful tools and adaptable advice for business.

Special interests: open innovation, sustainability, eHealth

  • Founder

    ThinkTankThursday

    (Think Tanks industry)

    2009 — Present (1 year )

    Initiator of a free open innovation label which makes it easy, accessible and fun to share knowledge in favor of innovation.

    By the use of trust and experience in the old fashioned 'putting the heads together' ThinkTankThursday provides a low barrier initiative for making the first steps on open innovation. It's 'just' brainstorming with different frames of reference...

    It's our aim to force a global shift to easy access innovation (it's already there!).

    (Shipbuilding industry)

    The 'low-budget' aluminium launches (Dutch: 'sloepen') are free of maintanance and extremely sustainable.

    We provide "Pure pleasure without the hassle".

  • Owner

    ideavents

    (Think Tanks industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    'open knowledge consulting'

    ideavents guides organizations in the process transforming from a closed organization to an open organization. As a result the open organizations outperform in sharing knowledge both internal and external in order to gain innovative capabilities. This by using the full opportunity of collaborative creativity from inside and outside the organization.

    As part of its main activities ideavents organizes events (in- and external) related to knowledge sharing and innovation, and facilitates workshops and lectures on the subjects of open innovation and crowdsourcing.
