    Marketing Director at Code Factory, a Barcelona-based software company that makes mobile phones accessible to the blind and visually impaired. Code Factory is the world leading provider of screen readers, screen magnifiers, and Braille interfaces for the widest range of mainstream mobile devices.

    Project Manager for Vodafone Speak launched by Vodafone Spain in 2008. The first mobile screen reader that can be downloaded by SMS. Vodafone Speak is an application allowing people with restricted sight to hear everything that appears on the screen of their mobile by means of a voice synthesiser.
