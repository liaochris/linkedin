Ben FARHANGUI’s Summary

With a degree in Electrical Engineering, and MBA in strategy and marketing, I am specialized in the management of Telecommunications and Information Technology.

I have spent a major part of my career in managing large Telco and IT engineering disciplines with substantial yearly budgets (up to €40 Mio).

Why enterprise governance for Information and Technology assets?

- Companies with the right governance focus generate an above average return on their technology assets.
- The operating and investing cash flows driven by Information and Technology assets represent a substantial stake in the GDP of industrialized counties.
- The IT and telco legislation and the demanding industry regulations influence the access to capital market, and in some industries such as card payment, the license to operate
