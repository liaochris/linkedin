Justin Spring’s Summary

Justin is the Co-founder of Adept, an internet marketing company, and BringShare, a SaaS application launching Q1 2011.

Justin created Adept with one very specific goal in mind, "Be the best internet marketing company in the world." In this vision, the word "best" is defined as doing more to help a client grow their business than anyone else in the industry. With this mindset, Adept has been able to help several start-up companies go from "0 to 60" in less than 12 months. Through a unique ability to combine financial performance with customer acquisition, Adept has developed a niche in helping venture-backed, high-growth internet companies grow faster and more profitably. Recently, Adept helped JackThreads.com grow 1000+% and get acquired by Thrillist in less than a year.

BringShare.com is Justin's latest venture. Through seven years of marketing management experience, Justin personally experienced a need for a software application that could make life easier for marketing people. As a result, he created the idea for BringShare.com while on a flight to Boston. Upon landing, he told his business partner, Danielle Walton, that he just had a $30 million idea. 10 months later, Justin and Danielle are within 12 months of launching BringShare.com. They are currently in development and, using a grant from Ohio, executing a comprehensive research project to validate the tool and technology. If you are an interested investor, please contact Justin for details.
