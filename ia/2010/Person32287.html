Carl Chapman’s Summary

Entrepreneurial management professional with years of hands-on experience in helping to optimize business processes. I founded and am currently the COO of an Internet start up Interview on Demand, LLC [www.interview-on-demand.com] which provides online video job interview services and video career profiles.

Our mission is to dramatically improve industry hiring practices by providing an online video interviewing service so powerful and effective that our clients cannot imagine hiring without it.

We have specifically tailored product offerings for hiring managers/corporate recruiters, third party recruiters, colleges and universities, and jobseekers. We believe that the time for using video effectively in the hiring process has come, and we want to lead the charge.
