Nicole Wicks’s Summary

Nicole Wicks is a dynamic business development and sales expert with more than fifteen years of entrepreneurial experience principally in hi-tech, bio-tech, international finance, and filmed entertainment.

Nicole’s professional experience has included working with political committees, the Hollywood entertainment community, and international business entities to maximize their impact on specific and targeted demographics. Her personal style gives her the ability to problem-solve quickly and find common sense solutions to complex business growth matters.

In her earlier years, Nicole founded an international money transfer company, formed two filmed entertainment companies, worked extensively in the non-profit sector, and was a senior director of a Seattle based private capital formation and executive management consulting firm serving domestic and international clients.

Nicole is proficient in communication skills; analytical research, marketing, networking and business plan development and implementation.
