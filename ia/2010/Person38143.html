Christopher Labatt-Simon’s Experience

  • CEO

    D&D Consulting, Ltd.

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 1992 — Present (17 years 7 months)

    President and CEO of D&D Consulting, Ltd., a New York State Certified Woman Owned Business (WBE) IT Consulting firm located in the upstate New York region focusing on mid to large private sector and public sector organizations.
