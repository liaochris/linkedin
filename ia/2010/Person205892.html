Tom Chung’s Summary

Tom is unique in online marketing—a classically-trained creative director who has fully embraced the internet. Having helped shape some of the world’s most venerable brands with breakthrough work in every medium, Tom brings his deep knowledge of brand building to new technologies.

Currently consulting for a portfolio of web-based brands in the experiential and e-commerce space, Tom truly understands that functionality is nothing without clear communication and a strong brand.

Prior to his online transition, Tom held the post of Creative Director at agencies big and small, as well as running his own successful shop for a number of years. Fluent in both B2B and B2C communications, Tom’s deep yet equally broad experience encompasses industries as varied as telecommunications, financial services, travel, entertainment, and even the United States Government. For both blue-chip Fortune 10 firms and regional start-ups, Tom has brought a unique blend of creativity, strategic insight and passion for making a connection with the consumer to bear consistent results.

Tom Chung’s Specialties:

Marketing, branding and relationship building to general and niche markets including High Net Worth, Asian in the U.S.—industries include financial services, education, entertainment, and gaming.
