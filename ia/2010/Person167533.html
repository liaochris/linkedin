Howard Gluss Ph.D’s Summary

HOWARD'S HISTORY: Creating in 2002 the KeyLight Company, Dr. Gluss has worked on many films and television projects aiding writers, actors, producers and directors in mastering their creative processes through consultations, coaching and seminars. In 2006, Dr. Gluss created DAIS – A Platform of Executive Coaching to the Business World. DAIS offers business seminars on Financial Psychology, Executive Coaching, Leadership Training and Organizational Development.

Dr. Gluss is the host of two successful radio talk shows. He has presented guests from the world of Medicine, Psychology, Business, Politics, Sports, and Entertainment.

The Dr. Howard Gluss Show on wsRADIO.com began airing weekly in November of 2007. wsRADIO is the world’s leader in internet broadcasting with over 30,000 servers worldwide to serve a virtually unlimited number of listeners for both live and archive/on-demand listening.

LIFE w/Dr. Gluss on KFNX1100 AM radio, Phoenix Arizona began in 2008. KFNX110AM is one of the Top Ten Radio Stations in Arizona reaching almost 5 million people throughout Arizona and millions more through the Internet broadcast. Life with Dr. Gluss shares the KFNX line up with Lou Dobbs, Jerry Doyle, Don Imus, Dr. Laura, and CNN News.
