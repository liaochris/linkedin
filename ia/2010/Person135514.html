Martin Keen’s Experience

  • Founder, Owner

    Keen Footwear, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    November 1999 — Present (10 years 6 months)

    In 1999, having smashed my toes one too many times while racing sailboats wearing sandals in Newport, Rhode Island I set about designing and developing the product that became the foundation of Keen Footwear. Two years later after an exhaustive search I found a partner to fund the idea. I built a "hydrid footwear" marketing plan, a catalogue and trade show booth, expanded the product offering beyond just sandals and launched the brand to the trade in February, 2003. We hit retail the following April. The concept was an instant hit. We were awarded the "2003 Launch of the Year" by the shoe industry's leading trade publication, Footwear News.

    Today the company offers a full range of footwear, bags and socks and is available in thousands of specialty US retail locations throughout the United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Asia, Central America, South America and Europe.
