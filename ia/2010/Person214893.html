Wes Adams’s Summary

With an extensive track record of experience in interactive and new media advertising and marketing, I have led teams in both big and small agencies, with an eye for quality and a drive to push boundaries for the web, mobile and digital campaigns.

Prior to joining AKQA, I served as Director of Production / Executive Producer for Firstborn, NY where I produced several strategic and award winning campaigns for clients including Nokia, Lowe’s, XM Radio, Corona, Ford, Tommy Bahama Rum, and Starwood Hotels.
