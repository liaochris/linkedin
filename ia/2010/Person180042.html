Mads Rydahl’s Summary

Since the mid 90s I've managed software research and development teams and brought numerous products and consumer services to market. I'm a compassionate leader capable of establishing consensus and inspiring peak performance on my teams through a combination of technical insight, expectation management, and emotional intelligence.

My ability to inspire and bring out the best in the people I work with has ensured the timely and successful completion of all projects that I have managed.

In the 90s, my own production work received several prestigious international awards, and has been featured in dozens of books. I am an accomplished expert in the fields of user centered design, user experience and interaction design, and I have years of production experience in a number of programming environments, tracking tools, and most visual design software.

I work in Silicon Valley and live in San Francisco with my wife and son.
