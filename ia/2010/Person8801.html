Amanda Brzoska’s Summary

Hello. My name is Amanda Brzoska, and I am one of the managing members of My Small Business Assistant, LLC. My goal is to help entrepreneurs in areas where it is more cost effective to outsource such as bookkeeping and payroll processing.

As a small business owner and office manager of over ten years, I understand your needs. I realize that you are on a budget, and your time is valuable. I also understand the need for accuracy, integrity, and confidentiality when it comes to handling sensitive business information. It is my mission to provide honest and accurate information, useful tools, valuable resources, and above all, excellent customer service. Let me help you with your small business. Contact me today!
