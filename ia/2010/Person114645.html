Kenneth Ottino’s Summary

During my career with DaimlerChrysler, Daimler Financial and now Daimler Trucks NA, I have gained ever increasing experience in the Human Resources field starting as a HR intern in a unionized manufacturing setting and then working my way up through Employment, Safety and Labor Relations, HR Consultant and then HR Manager for the non-union workforce at the Daimler Financial Services division. Presently I am the Human Resources Manager of the unionized Cleveland Truck Manufacturing Plant in Cleveland, NC, responsible for the daily HR functions for 1000+ employees.
