David Evans’s Summary

Fifteen years of consulting experience with many groundbreaking and influential international web sites and Internet advertising projects. Extensive involvement with technical and marketing teams on a variety of leading-edge projects.

Past three years consulting for startup and established social networking and dating sites ($900M industry).

Consulting engagements with strategic thinkers and key innovators at a variety of clients ranging from startups to Fortune 100 businesses: Matchmine, Engage.com, Trufina, Backgroundchecks.com, Anderson & Lembke (Interpublic Group), Date.com, Chemistry.com, IBM, Modem Media, Tangent Design and StratsoftLLC.

Extensive market research & analysis across a variety of technology sectors: social networking and dating, telecom, data centers, fiber optic networks, WiFi, business intelligence, consumer technology products.

David Evans’s Specialties:

Creative and broad-minded, able to quickly comprehend new technologies driving new forms of communication and their market potential. In particular: blogging, semantic web, social media, open profiles and videoconferencing.
