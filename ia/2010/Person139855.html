Ciaran Delaney’s Summary

I have worked as a marketing director in tourism industry in Ireland and Europe for over 12 years as outlined below.

Meetingsbooker.com

Meetingsbooker.com is an online booking engine for meeting rooms. It allows meeting planners to book meeting rooms online or receive immediate online quotes. We now offer over 1,900 meeting rooms in 19 countries.

Hotels and conference venues can also add our software to their own websites.
http://www.meetingsbooker.com

Hotelwebsitemarketing.com

Hotelwebsitemarketing.com provides hotel sales managers with a platform to learn more about online marketing through conferences, blog and seminars. Topics include: hotel revenue management, hotel booking engines, online reservations, social media for hotels, hotel mobile marketing, email marketing, third party booking websites and many more.
http://www.hotelwebsitemarketing.com
http://www.hotelwebsitemarketing.com/blog

Mansfield Group Director of Marketing

In Sept 2005 I worked under renowned Irish Entrepreneur Jim Mansfield. I was responsible for marketing Irelands largest hotel - Citywest Hotel Conference Leisure and Golf Resort, The PGA National Ireland at Palmerstown House and Weston Executive Airport.

Tourism Ireland

I spent 7 years with Tourism Ireland which was an amazing learning experience. My most recent role was Internet Development Project Manager, working on the €2.6 million discoverireland.com website.

I also spent four years in Milan, as Market Manager for Italy, Greece, Malta and Cyprus.

My first role straight after university was Market Manager for Norway. I spent two amazing years in Oslo trying to convince the Norwegians to forget about sun holidays and come to Ireland.
