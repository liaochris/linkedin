Emily Harris’s Summary

I am currently an Executive Recruiter specializing in Permanent Finance & Accounting Placements.

I joined firstPRO Philadelphia in 2007 as the Recruiting Manager of the Contract Accounting Division. I was promoted to Division Manager in 2008 and was responsible for managing the sales and recruitment process for the consulting A&F division. In 2009 I was promoted to an Executive Recruiter role with our Direct Hire Executive Search team. Prior to joining firstPRO, I was a Senior Account Executive with RemX Financial Staffing and Robert Half International specializing in the staffing of Finance and Accounting professionals. I has been servicing the Philadelphia community for 5 years. My background lies in account management with Dun & Bradstreet before entering the Staffing Industry.

I moved to Center City Philadelphia in 2005 and I am very involved with various networking organizations including the IAPP, APA, HFMA, SHRM, and ASWA.
