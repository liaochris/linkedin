Daina Reed’s Summary

Originally from Detroit, Michigan, I moved to Florida right after high school to get to warmer weather. My career began at age 19, working at internet start-ups in Boca Raton. The 2000 dotcom collapse was my cue for some world exploring, which led me to Silicon Valley East- Tel Aviv- in ‘03. I’ve been back in the biz since ’04, leading an instantly successful freelance web development business.

I bring you a decade of experience, creating sites that have it all: beauty, brains, and business sense. Not only do I turn out boutique quality, custom-tailored sites, I assemble a team and manage your project from start to finish to create your dream site in real time.

Together we will discover and produce the site you never thought you could have, but always wanted.

Daina Reed’s Specialties:

I come from a background of hand coding sites according to the current standards. I can bring professional front end code with in depth knowledge of what is possible and what is not in a dynamic site.
