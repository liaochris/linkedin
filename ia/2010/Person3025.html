Rick Goldman’s Summary

I began making websites in 1995 at a time when the Internet was relatively unheard of. Most of the time I'd go to visit a client and ask if they had ever seen the Internet and the answer was usually "No". I would connect my 28k modem and show them the "world wide web". Yes things have changed and now we build complex web based applications. Our clients include people who had a website made elsewhere and it can never be found in the search engines. We do search engine optimization to improve their ranking. We also make ecommerce websites for all types of products. Our clients are lawyers, medical doctors & dentists, manufacturers, retailers and more. We even create websites for The Miami Herald.

Our company helps other businesses to make a great first impression in print, on TV and on the Internet.

Rick Goldman’s Specialties:

We build websites that people can find when they search for what our clients do. For example go to yahoo.com or google.com and search for "South Florida ad agency" and you'll find ad-excellence.com. We help our clients to be found on the Internet.

We like to handle all of the advertising needs of a client so that they will have a cohesive marketing package. We want the brochure design, website and TV commercial to all have the same look and feel. This is critical for branding purposes.
