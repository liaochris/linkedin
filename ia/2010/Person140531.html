Melina Taylor’s Summary

Property management, rental leasing, residential real estate consulting, property maintenance. Serving landlords, investors, homeowners and tenants throughout Southern California.

We also special in REO preservation repairs and renovations. We can do everything from sales cleans, trash outs, landscaping to full service remodeling. We have an in-house construction company that has been doing this for over 20 years

One of our goals as a property management company is to bring a level of service to the industry that has been long forgotten. Personalize Customer Service that empathizes on both ends of the spectrum. We stand behind our character, morals, spiritual beliefs and our integrity. We hope to change the industry by treating our investors with the utmost respect and loyalty and exercise extreme diligence and caring towards our tenants. We don't look at contractual agreements as a successful sell, but an opportunity to build a long lasting business and personal relationship.
