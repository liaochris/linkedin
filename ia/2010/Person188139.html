Shawnda Kennedy Parker, CSP’s Summary

Shawnda has over 16 years of recruiting experience. Her background includes temporary and direct hire placement, internal corporate recruiting (primarily hiring sales and recruiting professionals for one of the fastest-growing Medical staffing firms in the US) and business development. She has won numerous awards for her recruiting abilities. Shawnda has her Bachelors and Masters degrees from the University of Texas at Tyler and has also earned her CSP (Certified Staffing Professional) certification. Shawnda is responsible for recruiting top talent, cultivating client loyalty and coordinating High Profile's training and development initiatives.

Since 1987, High Profile Staffing has provided literally hundreds of Dallas companies with people everyday to fill office administrative, human resource and financial positions. We offer contract, contract to hire, and direct hire placement.

The High Profile staff are experts in their field holding designations for Certified Temporary Staffing Specialist (CTS), Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC), Certified Staffing Professional (CSP) and Professional in Human Resources (PHR). Our recruiters have been trained and tested on employment law.

High Profile is a member and has held leadership positions with the Dallas Human Resources Management Association, the Society of Human Resource Management, the Texas Association of Staffing, the National Association of Temporary Staffing Services as well as various local Chambers of Commerce.

We believe strongly in community involvement. We partner very closely with Girls Inc. Girls Inc is a safe haven after-school program. This year we developed a program called Good Works! where a percentage of our total sales is contributed to charity.

High Profile has been named by the Dallas Business Journal as one of the "Best Places to Work" in the Metroplex and awarded "Employer of Excellence" by the Greater Dallas Chamber for multiple years.
