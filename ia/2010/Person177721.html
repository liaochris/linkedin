Rose Johnson-Tsosie, DTM’s Summary

I love being an American Indian (Navajo); a woman; a Christian. My theme for life is: "Light the Fire Within, then Share It."

My Navajo mother, Helen, was 13 years of age when she gave bith to twin girls in 1950. She could not read, write, and knew only Navajo. The "White doctor," gave her a piece of paper, she put her thumb print on it and an "X"... she thought the hospital was taken care of her babies and to return in a couple months. When she returned, she realized her error as the paper she signed was for adoption. Fortunately my twin sister and I grew up together in northern Utah.

After 33 years of hopes and dreams that one day her babies would come home, Rose did... it was a miracle! She also found her 14 other siblings. "Finding Helen - A Navajo Miracle," book authored by Rose is a true story.

Rose travels throughout the United States as an author, recognized by the Navajo Nation; Speaker, Storyteller, and genealogy specialist for American Indian referencing and family history writing.
