Tim Woda’s Summary

Tim co-founded KidSafe.me and has been instrumental in shaping KidSafe’s product and growth strategy. Tim is an experienced entrepreneur with a successful track record in rapidly growing revenue. Tim is responsible for the company’s business development, sales, marketing and customer support efforts. A frequent public speaker and an expert on Internet & mobile phone safety issues impacting children, he also informally serves as the company’s “Chief Child Safety Advocate”. Tim is the author of Keeping Kids Safe: A Guide for the Parents of Social and Mobile Children.

Prior to KidSafe, Tim was the Vice President of Business Development & Sales for buySAFE, Inc., the world’s leading e-commerce trust and safety company now partially owned by Verisign. Under his direction, buySAFE’s revenue grew 600% in the five quarters he served in that role. Tim initially joined buySAFE when the company launched and prior to assuming revenue responsibility as VP, he served as buySAFE’s Director of Business Development.

Before this, Tim was the founder of The Telecom Recruiter, LLC, an executive search firm serving the telecommunications industry. He also held the position of Southeast Area Director for MCI Communications where he managed several district sales teams and was inducted into the MCI Small Business Hall of Fame.

An entrepreneur with 20 years of experience in marketing, sales, management, customer retention, collaborative negotiation and operations, Tim brings to KidSafe a refined ability to execute best-practice business processes. He has a successful track record of developing a deep understanding of a strategic partner's business, their strategy and key initiatives and clearly identifying the business benefits of a proposed relationship.
