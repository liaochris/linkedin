Fred Tanzella’s Summary

A veteran technology executive with a track record of starting and growing companies to successful acquisitions.

As Chairman and CEO of Colocube, Fred leads strategy and direction of global data center and IP transit growth. In addition to heading up Colocube, Fred oversees Glick Interactive, an award-winning web development firm.

Fred has held a variety of senior management positions at EAS, i2Go, Air2Web, BEA Systems, WysDM & AirDefense in roles including COO, CTO, Chief Security Officer, VP of Engineering, and VP of Product Management.

Fred was EVP at Kaneva, a MMO game platform and virtual world company.

Fred was Chief Security Officer and founding member at AirDefense (acquired by Motorola), the leading wireless security provider.

Fred was a founding member of WysDM (acquired by EMC), a global data management company that provides real-time infrastructure monitoring.

At BEA Systems (acquired by Oracle), he headed up WebLogic Pervasive Computing.

Prior, helped define strategy & direction of the Air2Web Mobile Internet Platform, the leading enterprise wireless enabler handling traffic for 500+ carriers in 170 countries.

Fred was a founding member of i2Go (acquired by Audiovox), and led engineering in design and development of the world's first interactive MP3 player.

Regularly invited to speak at conferences nationally and abroad & has appeared on ZDnet TV, CNN, & The Money Channel. Often quoted in national publications, considered a trusted source for both analysts & media. Authored multiple patents in Internet security, wireless security, mobile, & multi-media in addition to books & white papers.

Fred serves on the Board of Directors for the Association of Telecom Professionals (www.atpconnect.org).

Fred serves on the Board of Directors for the Technology Association of Georgia (TAG) serving on the Education Collaborative (www.tagedonline.org).

Fred holds a Bachelor of Science degree from Georgia Tech.

Fred Tanzella’s Specialties:

Data Centers, Telecom, NOC Management, Mobile and Wireless, Internet Security, Game Development, High-End Web 2.0 Applications, Technical Due Diligence for VC's
