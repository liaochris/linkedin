Channa Borman’s Summary

I began work in small law firm as associate attorney directly out of law school here in Bryan/College Station. After about a year, I left there and became Lead Associate with 20 year plus, two partner law firm, Searcy & Gandy, P.C., in College Station, and worked there for approximately 5 years. I began my own firm with a partner and after about two years, dissolved the partnership and became self-employed. Now I have three full-time attorneys on staff with me and one of counsel attorney. My law firm practices family law, civil law, criminal defense and administrative law, including social security disability work. I was formerly very involved in the Texas Young Lawyers Association and met lawyers from all over Texas, many of whom I continue to consider dear friends. I am currently involved with the Bryan/College Station Junior League, on the Board of both Project Unity and Safe Harbour Supervised Visitation Center and teach part-time at Blinn College. I have two children, Emma and Lilly, and a wonderful husband, Tom.

Channa Borman’s Specialties:

MY practice focuses on family law litigation: divorce, modifications, enforcements, conservatorships in general and child support issues. I enjoy adoptions and ad litem work for CPS and enjoy my various community service commitments. The remaining attorneys in my firm handle various types of cases.
