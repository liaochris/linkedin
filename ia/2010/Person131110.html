Marble Slab Franchise’s Summary

We specialize in Franchise Management.

Franchise training and support includes, but is not limited to the following:

Marketing/Public Relations - Support with building brand awareness, driving traffic to the store, creating brand loyalty and increasing frequency of visit.

Design & Construction - Provide store design and project management through build out.
