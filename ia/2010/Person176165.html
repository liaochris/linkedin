Rocco Tricarico’s Summary

Rocco has been “plugged into” the Consumer Electronics business for more than 15 years. During that time he has taken scores of key brands, products & services to new levels of revenue and market success. Throughout his career in Consumer Electronics, Rocco has led the launch of new products, repositioned established products, and created both niche and mega-brand images that resonate with consumers across all age, gender, social, cultural and economic demographics.

His exceptionally unique insights have developed from real-world Executive tenures at leading Retailers, Manufacturers & Service Providers giving him an unparalleled perspective into each of their strategies, tactics & operations. As such, his well-rounded knowledge of the CE landscape gives his clients a very powerful tool to make big things happen even when they have limited resources. Rocco is recognized within the industry as a driving force for creating and managing industry leading ROI-based programs that have led to award-winning marketing innovations, and resulted in stronger market positions, brand recognition, corporate images and hundreds of millions of dollars in revenue.
