Leah Segedie’s Summary

Leah Segedie is a communications professional, creator of niche web communities, social media junkie and Chief Mom of Operations of Bookieboo LLC, a publishing company and social media consulting firm. She specializes in connecting companies with "digital moms," & creating brand awareness and brand loyalty. Bookieboo LLC also operates several campaigns and sites in social media: Bookieboo, Mamavation, Bookieboo Rave Reviews & Giveaways and Serenity Now.

Leah Segedie, known as @bookieboo on Twitter, has been recognized for several feats in her professional and personal life. She has a big presence in social media, recently placing 1st on "Top #50 Tweeples" on PRSarahEvans.com for 2009. She has also been called a "Social Media Maven" by Hybrid Mom Magazine. She's known most for losing over 170 lbs. and has been featured in several magazines, websites, and periodicals for her accomplishments, such as Ladies Home Journal, Fitness RX, Hybrid Mom, The Acorn, Mom It Forward, The Well Mom, etc. She has also worked with or alongside some of the following brands: Earth Footwear, EA SPORTS Active, Gaiam, Smooth Fitness, Sportline & People's Choice Awards.

Leah spent years working for the California State Assembly, where she learned a great deal about community building, effective networking, persuasive communication,and public speaking. She then worked as a professional fundraiser where she worked on projects with Mayor of Los Angeles Richard Riordan, CA Governor Arnold Schwarzenegger, LA City Attorney Rocky Delgadillo, LA Mayor James Hahn, CA Assemblywoman Lynn Daucher, CA State Senator Tony Strickland, and several ballot propositions. She then worked in the corporate world as a Communication Manager at Employers Direct Insurance Company.

Leah Segedie has a Masters degree from the University of Southern California in Communication Management. She lives in Southern California with her two boys and husband.
