Patricia Hayward’s Summary

Executive Sales & Marketing recruitment specialist working predominantly with private businesses across a diverse industry sector throughout the UK.

Prior to joining the world of recruitment I worked for 22 years for three major companies within the Office Stationery, Print & Furniture arena.

I have over 27 years of B2B and recruitment experience offering my clients an honest, professional service for all their recruitment needs. In January 2010 I established Uno Executive Recruitment Limited where I'm focused on developing business relationships with clients in Nottinghamshire, Lincolnshire, North, South, East & West Yorkshire operating from our office based at The Turbine Innovation Centre, Shireoaks, Worksop, Nottinghamshire.

Having worked in the recruitment arena for over 5 years now I have a solid knowledge and understanding of what is expected when my clients are looking to fill a vacant position within their orgainsation. I have recruited across a broad range of vacancies from Office Support to Sales Director Level for SME's and Blue Chip Organisations.
