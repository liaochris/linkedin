Brett Michelson’s Summary

With 25 years in the medical industry, our recruiting expertise extends to most areas of surgery, diagnostic & therapeutic imaging, radiology & navigation, plus IGRT and HIFU. We support manufacturers of medical device implants: including MIS, LIS & SILS procedures in IMD, AIMD and Class I, Class II and Class III devices with special emphasis on orthopedics, spine, neurosurgery, neuroscience, biologics, ophthalmic, cardiology, cardiovascular, thoracic, vascular, otolaryngology, robotics, drug delivery, healthcare IT and most emerging medical and surgical technology related to PMA, 510(k) & IDE device classes.

Our leadership’s extensive travel includes attendance at 16 Medical Conferences in 2010 allows us to maintain clinical expertise and industry knowledge of leaders in the medical device space.
