Buck Black MSW, LCSW’s Summary

I am a Licensed Therapist (LCSW) focused on my passion for anger management and couples therapy, as well as helping truckers with these issues while they are on the road. My specializations include anger, anxiety, and sexuality.

I have seen multiple sides of sexuality. Whether it is sexual offending, sexual victimization, sexual dysfunction, sex addiction or people who just want to have better sex. I am that person people can talk with who can help them reach their goals.

I run www.TruckerTherapy.com, which is a service that allows truckers to have access to me, a therapist, while they are on the road. Due to it being almost impossible for a trucker to earn a living and also keep therapy appointments, I created www.TruckerTherapy.com

I enjoy writing articles and am currently involved with www.mentalhelp.net, www.theCyberTruckStop.com, and www.lifeontheroad.com

I am interested in advising corporations on mental health issues, providing EAP services, and educating groups about anger management. More information is available at www.BuckBlack.com

I have a great deal of experience working in community mental health centers with a variety of client populations. It is very fun and exciting to work with people online and to help others though my private practice office located in Lafayette Indiana.

Buck Black MSW, LCSW’s Specialties:

I have evidenced based tools for teaching effective techniques for dealing with anxiety, anger, and depression (which impact virtually every aspect of our lives). I specialize in helping truckers via phone and email when they are on the road.
