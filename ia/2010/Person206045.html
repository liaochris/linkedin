Tom Hall’s Summary

I am President and CEO of Hall Web Services, a web development and internet marketing firm located in Portland, Maine. I founded the company in 1999. We are honored to be certified as a Pine Tree Development Zone Company in the State of Maine for information technology (http://www.mainebiz.org/pine_tree_zones/default.asp).

At Hall, we focus on all aspects of internet marketing strategy including search friendly web development, internet marketing (SEM/SEO), social media marketing, content management and delivery, and ongoing strategy management, support and validation.

Our internet marketing tools help in-house staff at larger companies get the most from their effort.

We also offer those tools with ongoing consulting services where our pros can deliver advice, additional hands, auditing or ongoing support.

Of course, we are a full service firm able to function as a complete web development and internet marketing solutions provider.

I spend most of my time managing the company, helping clients, working on our SEM software and losing at Wii Golf.

-Tom
