Chris Davis’s Summary

Christopher (Chris) Davis, is a LEED® Accredited Professional and advocate for organizational sustainability. His role as Senior Associate with The Pinwheel Group, Inc. brings his talents for informing and educating about issues of sustainability and corporate responsibility to a vast
array of businesses and nonprofit clients. Chris is the project manager on the Pinwheel Group’s Web2020 initiative and the client liaison for implementing this unique take on the world of social networking.

Chris founded PULSE Design Group, LTD. as a multi-disciplinary design and consulting firm, which has provided thoughtful and customized programming for its clients including Fortune 500 corporations. Chris authored “The Little Green Book” - the quick reference guide to office sustainability. He has been invited to share his point of view at numerous industry events, and has been featured at USGBC Chapters to discuss greening of the workplace.

Chris is an active participant on two local task forces including Community Partnerships and Outreach and Operations & Maintenance for Sustainable Jersey which is a certification program for NJ municipalities who want to go green. He is also a member of the 2010 Manhattan Chamber of Commerce Subcommittee on Corporate Social Responsibility.
