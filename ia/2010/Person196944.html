Sue Beth Murphree’s Summary

Offering 10 years of effective strategic planning, contract negotiation, competitive analysis, and decision making within the health care industry. I understand how to identify profitable opportunities in complex insurance regulations and coding laws because of my attention to detail and my tenacity for finding creative solutions. I have supervised various product and program launches as well as administered start-up operations.

My roles in the past have included Senior Policy Development Representative, Regional Account Manager, and Director of Network Development. A sample of accomplishments from throughout my career includes:
Actively negotiating and contracting network development with a National Managed Care Organization for a government program.
• Generating market interest and growth through the development of a product marketing campaign targeting insurance companies regarding key medical technologies (Independent Contractor).
• Propelling sales by effectively marketing reimbursement coverage for a product, including drafting a dynamic collateral packet/letter, and gaining favorable policy status, developing strong active relationships with major insurance companies covering more than 56 million covered lives. (St. Francis Medical Technology).
• Securing favorable medical policy and coding guidance, including a policy for over 12 million commercial covered lives.
• Garnering a policy with Medicaid to cover 2 million lives, while expanding guidelines and actively initiating policy review or development for over 45 commercial plans (Smith & Nephew, Inc.).
