Lindsey Weinger’s Summary

Lindsey is a results-driven strategic digital media professional with extensive experience developing and managing the monetization of new media content, technologies, & convergent businesses. Entertainment & Technology industry focus bolstered by progressive strategic and market-focused roles at Flycell, BT & BSkyB. As VP of Customer Experience, Lindsey is responsible for the most valuable assets for any company: THE CUSTOMERS! In her current role, Lindsey has excelled at improving the user experience across platforms as well as successfully managing all merchandising, retention & social marketing, customer communications, information architecture, usability, web analytics, customer service, content creation, branding, pricing, & new product integration.

Lindsey has demonstrated unwavering passion and unparalleled foresight in the areas of Strategic Marketing, Customer Experience Management, Product Development, Brand Marketing, E-Commerce, M-Commerce, & Digital Media. She has extensive knowledge of computer/web/mobile technology platforms used for e-commerce and entertainment. It is rare for an analytical mind to also have a creative side, yet Lindsey has demonstrated a sense of design and understanding of how to consistently convey a brand image or message in the execution of all elements of the customer experience.

Receiving her MBA at London Business School supplemented by Certification in Customer Centricity at Columbia Business School and Exchange Program at UCLA's Producer's Program compliments the BS from Cornell University’s College of Engineering. Prior to business school, Lindsey quickly rose to the top in Wall Street with her determination to create successful trading strategies in the tumultuous equities market (featured in many industry publications and events). Her interest in entertainment began with watching Jeopardy with her family -but was crystallized with the rise of progressive networks such as HBO and Comedy Central.
