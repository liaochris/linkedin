Jeffrey J. Podolski, CMCA, AMS’s Summary

Over 15 years of residential and multi-family property management experience. Mr. Podolski is proud to run the fastest growing, most modern and customer service oriented property management company in the metro-Detroit area. Attention to detail, time management and excellent customer relations are all attributes that Mr. Podolski has mastered. Jeff has obtained his CMCA® (Certified Manager of Community Associations®) and his AMS® (Association Management Specialist®) designations through CAI (Community Associations Institute). Currently Jeff is attending classes to obtain his PCAM® (Professional Community Association Manager®) designation also through CAI. Mr. Podolski is driven to make JBC Property Management your property management company.
