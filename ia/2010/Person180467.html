Sai Allavarpu’s Summary

A successful and driven software business executive with over 19 years of experience in Business Strategy, Product Management, Marketing & Engineering, Sai has managed businesses up to $200M in revenues in multiple international locations, combining innovation and strategic planning with operational effectiveness and relationship building to deliver results.

As a results-focused executive, Sai has a proven track-record of designing, developing and marketing early stage, award-winning & industry-leading products, including emerging products. He specialized in strategic planning and operational execution for establishing and scaling emerging businesses. He has demonstrated management and leadership skills in building, growing and leading effective small and large global matrix organizations.

Leveraging his MBA, Sai strategically grew business both organically and inorganically through many acquisitions in large companies such as HP and Sun, gaining broad experience in M&A including pre-M&A diligence and negotiations, financials and post M&A integrations.

He continues to focus on fast-paced operating environments in rapidly evolving markets with the goal of delivering marketing-leading and profitable solutions.

As an avid learner and versatile executive, he gained broad and extensive experience in diverse technology and business domains including security, virtualization, cloud computing, identity management, network & systems management, application networking, application delivery, WAN Optimization, IT service management, portals and SOA/web services.

Sai holds an MBA from Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley and a BS in computer science. He also holds 13 patents in diverse technology areas.

Sai Allavarpu’s Specialties:

Security, Cloud computing, Application and Desktop Virtualization, Identity & Access Management, IT Service Management, Network & System Management, Application Delivery & Networking.

Also specializes in general management, leadership; establishing and scaling emerging businesses to mainstream businesses organically and inorganically (M&A).
