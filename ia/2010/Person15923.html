James Prouty’s Summary

I have a B.S. In Criminal Justice as well as a graduate certificate in Emergency Management with a special focus in Homeland Security and Counter-Terrorism.

The focus of my practice is Continuity of Operations Plans (COOP) aka Business Continuity Plans (BCP) where I assist corporations and small businesses to develop emergency preparedness and continuity of business plans for emergencies and disasters pursuant to the new Federal law : Public Law 110-53, Title IX making them compliant.

I have over 30 years experience in criminal justice, fraud investigation and emergency management response. I am currently completing my Master's degree in Emergency Management.

James Prouty’s Specialties:

Continuity of Operation Plans, Emergency Management, Business Continuity Plans, Anti-Terrorism and Anti-Fraud matters
