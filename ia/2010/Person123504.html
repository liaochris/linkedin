Lee Plotkin’s Summary

Lee Plotkin is a partner with the New Orleans personal injury law firm of Gertler, Gertler, Vincent and Plotkin.

The firm focuses on the following injury and accident practice areas:

* Mesothelioma
* Wrongful Death Claims
* Automobile Accidents
* Injuries Suffered At Birth Including Cerebral Palsy
* Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) Resulting From An Accident
* Product Liability
* Toxic Mold
* Semi-Truck Accidents
* Dangerous Property Conditions
* Train & Railroad Related Accidents
* Hospital & Physician Malpractice
* Nursing Home Abuse & Negligence
* Construction Site Accidents
* Burn Injuries
* Factory Accidents
* Injuries Suffered While On Oil Rigs
