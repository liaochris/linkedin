Christopher Vincent’s Summary

Accuvant is one of the largest and fastest growing information security and compliance consulting firms in the nation. What we do is help clients assess their existing environments, secure their data and infrastructure, and improve their compliance posture. We are also a strategic partner with top-tier technology manufacturers.

BUSINESS MODEL: We have a unique business model that is dedicated to security & compliance. We focus on strategy first, then policy/process and finally, technology for support and automation. Accuvant delivers, therefore, a complete solution that identifies, measures and reduces risk using 3 dedicated practice areas:

* Security Technologies (Data Protection Services / Wireless Services)– Site Survey, Design, Deployment, Security Audit, Spectrum Analysis, Intrusion Prevention, WLAN Design/Deployment/Integration Sensitive Data Discovery, Data Classification & Inventory, Posture Assessment, Architecture Design, Data Leakage Assessment & Protection, Encryption, Event Log Mgmt, Firewall/IPS/UTM, NAC

* Compliance Management – Gap analysis, Program automation, PCI, GLBA, SOX, FISMA, ISO 27001/02, CobiT, BITS Shared Assessment, NERC CIP

* Assessment Services – Enterprise Security Assessments, Penetration Test, Application Code Review/Design/Threat Modeling, Policy Design

Christopher Vincent’s Specialties:

To assist our clients in understanding and navigating the increasingly complex security landscape, Accuvant has developed three specialized practices - Assessment, Compliance, and Security Technologies - to reflect key areas of concern in the information space. This approach enables Accuvant to provide assistance in very specialized fields with highly experienced and extensively trained resources.
