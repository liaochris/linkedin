Perry Brown’s Summary

Now in my 15th year of web development, I've always worked with clients at their point of need. As a result, my web "tool box" is wide and deep. In some instances clients already have functional web sites, but want to add another element like database interactivity or ecommerce. In other cases I've designed and built sites from the ground up.
