Valentina Bloomfield’s Summary

Owner at Little Paris Noel: Pup Models
Photography and Modeling industry for Pets
January 2004 – Present (6 years 3 months)

Quote from Modern Dog Magazine - "Two Yorkshire Terriers named Paris Noel and Bella LaRue are being hailed as the Gisele Bunschen and Heidi Klum of the pet fashion world."

I am the owner and photographer of my four yorkshire terriers; Paris Noel, Madeline Monet, Lili Patou and our newest addition, Eiffel Bleu for many well known manufacturing companies in the animal industry.

We model girl & boy dog clothing, accessories, pet beds, pet carriers and toys.

We are quite famous in the animal industry. You can find my work on, on line dog boutiques, in magazines, print ads and packaging.

Some of the companies we model for are: Petco, SimplyShe for Walmart, I See Spot, Monkey Daze, FouFou Dog, Hip Doggie, Pawsitively Posh, Pet Flys, Roxy & Lulu to name a few.

Inside Paris's Closet and Paris's Paw-Me-Downs resell many of the items they model in at close to wholesale prices. We are proud to say we donate a portion of the proceeds of the clothing and items we sell to The Animal Rescue Foundation (ARF) which is located in Walnut Creek, California. We also donate ALL of the beds we model.

With great sorrow, our beautiful model Bella LaRue passed away peacefully in my arms on February 11, 2010 at 3:44 PM. She was diagnosed with a disease on November 1st, 2008, called GME. GME is an acute, progressive inflammatory disease of the central nervous system (CNS) of dogs. Sadly there is no cure at this time. I currently am in the process of starting "The Bella LaRue Foundation" to help find a cure for this deadly disease. It will be a mission of love in Bella's honor. She has brought so much joy and love into our lives, as well as her many fans. I will treasure those memories forever.

Valentina Bloomfield’s Specialties:

Photographer for my four Yorkshire terrier dog models in the animal industry. Each dog is trained to pose and catch that special "wow" factor in all of our photos.


Consultant for clients for dog related products.

I am the agent for my Yorkshire terriers.

The manufacturing companies send me the clothing or items to be photographed. I use my own models and I take the pictures, edit them to my clients specifications. I then mail them back on a disc or email them to our clients.
