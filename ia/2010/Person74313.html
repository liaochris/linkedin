George Smith’s Summary

George G Smith Jr is a blogger, social strategist, and digital native currently working for the Digital advertising agency R/GA. George graduated from the University of Massachusetts – Amherst with a degree in Communications and a minor in African Studies. Having always had a passion for blogging, he has blogged professionally and personally since 2000. In 2008, he helped create the Social Media Communications and Marketing program for Crocs, Inc, which he oversaw until late 2009. He has spoken at SXSW, Izeafest, Blog World Expo and other professional conferences on the relationships between brands and bloggers, as well as other topics.

Social Media Marketing, Sales, copy editing, PR, Community Management
