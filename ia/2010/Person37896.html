Chris Heuwetter’s Summary

Chris Heuwetter has implemented strategic marketing and public relations programs for an array of clients, in various industries during his years as a professional. Before launching TwiloPR, Chris worked as an Account Executive for the firm M. Silver Associates, a lifestyle/consumer public relations agency in Fort Lauderdale, handling accounts in industries that range from travel and education to financial services and non-profit organizations. His work with M. Silver Associates can be demonstrated by his dense portfolio which contains media placements he has secured for clients that range from magazine cover stories, to national AP placements, to local broadcast segments in top regional markets around the country. Chris is extremely technological savvy and his new media expertise has allowed to help launch and work on the new media practice of M. Silver Associates.

Prior to working with M. Silver Associates, Chris spent some time with Estrela Marketing, an interactive marketing firm located in Delray Beach learning the "ins and outs" of Web 2.0 PR initiatives, social networking and new media platform development. Through utilization of various Internet marketing techniques, including email marketing, online advertising and Search Engine Optimization, Chris' tenure at Estrela proved to be an invaluable asset for clients looking to capitalize on "PR 2.0."

Chris also spent some of his early career at Cohn & Wolfe - New York City, a public relations powerhouse and subsidiary of Young & Rubicam. At Cohn & Wolfe, Chris worked with very prolific brands such as Ford and Hewlett Packarde, mostly on the corporate/technology side.

New/Social Media and Marketing, Media Relations, Direct Mail and Email Marketing Campaigns/Design, Ad Placement and Design, Event Management, Press Release Drafting and Media Pitching, Media Interview Training
