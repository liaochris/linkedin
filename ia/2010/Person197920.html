Susanna Perkins’s Summary

Susanna Perkins has been writing for business for over 20 years. After graduating from Middlebury College in the heart of the beautiful Green Mountains of Vermont, Susanna has successfully juggled a career, a husband, raising five children, and several moves.

Susanna is eager to help you extend your business reach by producing top-quality white papers, case studies, landing pages, sell sheets and newsletters. Having created and managed websites since 1999, Susanna understands the difference between writing for a digital or a print audience, and can convey your message clearly and succinctly to either or both.

She is also available to help with SEO (search engine optimization) and blogs, especially WordPress setup. Despite her baby-boomer status, Susanna is quite comfortable with social media such as Twitter, Facebook and all those other new-fangled "new" media, and can assist you to get your business message to new clients by leveraging Web 2.0 applications.

Her career has included direct sales experience in retail, advertising, business and consumer services, recruiting and real estate, along with marketing for a variety of business and not-for-profit organizations.

She has researched and written about medical, health and wellness, consumer fraud, real estate, music and the arts, education, engineering and design topics. With wide-ranging personal interests, Susanna is fearless about learning new subjects, and knows how to research quickly and efficiently.
