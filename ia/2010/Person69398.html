Michael Feigin’s Summary

Michael is a Dynamic Construction Industry Executive and Senior Business Leader with extensive domestic and global experience at the most senior levels of premier organizations. He is firmly established as a thought leader in enterprise wide risk management, complex problem solving and dispute resolution, team building and insurance and surety strategy and structuring. Michael most recently has been recognized for leadership in the risks and opportunities underlying green design, construction and ownership. Michael has built a reputation as being the individual that the Company’s most senior leadership turns to to solve complex problems or achieve key results. Michael also is known for building high performance teams focused on the highest level of client satisfaction to deliver superior results that exceed expectations.
