Ira Kaufman Integrated Marketing Strategist’s Summary

There is a revolution in marketing. Traditional advertising is being integrated with social media and online marketing. Businesses need guidance to develop effective strategies that incorporate new media to gain competitive advantage .

I use my marketing and online experience and vast network to make sense of the WEB 2,0 and social media for business. I challenge owners and senior managers to clarify their objectives and translate them into a consistent brand and Marketing2.0 strategy. My ultimate goal is develop the potential of companies and organizations to achieve excellence and be truly responsive to their clients.

What will bring competitive advantage to a company or a non profit?
- Developing an integrated marketing strategy that leverages social media and online marketing
- Attracting and retaining the best talent


I work with senior executives and management teams to develop their branding, competitive advantage, and social media and marketing strategy. I speak publically to small groups, Chambers and larger audiences.
