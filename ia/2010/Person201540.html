Tim Montague, M.S.’s Summary

Helping alumni, friends and faculty of the Beckman Institute for Advanced Science and Technology at the University of Illinois stay connected and spur interdisciplinary research and technological innovation. The Beckman Institute is home to leading edge research in the fields of chemistry, life sciences, materials science, electrical and computer engineering, and biological intelligence. We embody Arnold O. Beckman's legacy of fostering the invention of methods, instruments, and materials that open up new avenues of scientific and technological discovery and commercialization.

Current initiatives include growing corporate partnerships, private foundation, and alumni relations.

Groups and affiliations: Association of Fundraising Professionals, American Wind Energy Association, Illinois Solar Energy Association.

M.S. University of Wisconsin-Madison; B.A. University of California, San Diego.
