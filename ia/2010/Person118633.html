Kristen @kris10haley Haley’s Summary

Hello :) I’m Kristen Haley and I am a Digital Strategist. I created my business, x10industries, to provide companies with the tools they need to get their message on the internet. In addition to developing websites, I specialize in executing social media marketing in local, national and international spaces to help amplify brand awareness, increase traffic and improve your bottom line. Online advertising, inbound marketing and lead nurturing using tools such as Google, LinkedIn, Facebook, Foursquare, YouTube, Twitter, Constant Contact, Skype and Blogging can attract a massive amount of visitors to your website if utilized correctly and efficiently.

When your business wants to sell a physical product online or off, or an intangible service offering, publishing periodic business updates and promotions on a consistent, contextual basis can help start that conversation. Social media can help earn more opportunities and ultimately sales when consumers engage in communication with your company directly through these channels. In addition, reputation management can help your business take a proactive approach to managing your brand online through feedback sites.

In addition to online branding, x10industries can also provide your organization with a one-stop solution for all your printing and promotional product needs. From logo or business set conceptualization to print marketing and unique promotional items, we understand the importance of branding your legacy in order to become attractive to potential customers.

In addition to offering these services, I also host a monthly social networking event where 50+ professionals and unprofessionals get together to chat about social media and other things to help grow businesses.

Please email me at kristen@x10i.com if you'd like to hear about what I can do for your company or if you know anybody who could benefit from my services :)
