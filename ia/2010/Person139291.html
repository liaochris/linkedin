Marianne Holman’s Summary

Over 25 years of multi-site residential property management.

Owner of "On-Site Focus,LLC", providing Fair Housing training and consulting to property management professionals.

Licensed Real Estate Broker in Michigan, Certified Property Manager (CPM), Certified Apartment Property Supervisor (CAPS), certified Advanced Training Instructor for the National Apartment Association, actively involved with the Detroit Metropolitan Apartment Association as Chairperson of the Education Committee. A recipient of the 2009 Leadership Award from the Fair Housing Center of Detroit.

Marianne Holman’s Specialties:

Fair Housing workshops and seminars.
