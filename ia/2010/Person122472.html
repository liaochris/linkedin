Lauren Switzer’s Summary

Hi there! My name is Lauren Switzer, and I am a senior public relations student with a minor in communication studies at the University of Oregon. I graduate this June (2010) and am currently seeking employment. I have experience working at a small, intercultural communications agency, a student-run public relations firm, various nonprofit organizations and an international public relations agency. From July 2009 through November 2009, I had the amazing privilege of studying abroad in Melbourne, Australia. While there, I also had the opportunity to intern at the Melbourne office of Edelman Worldwide. This experience made me all the more passionate about public relations and agency work in particular. I hope to convert this passion into a career in the near future.
