Armando Santana’s Summary

Armando is currently part of the FLO TV product management team, responsible for defining and delivering on the roadmap of products and services.

Armando has over ten years of experience in the technology and entertainment industry. Armando has a proven track record in successful interactive product development roles at Walt Disney Imagineering’s Research & Development group, the primary R&D arm of the Walt Disney Company. At Disney he worked on various strategic and confidential projects in the areas of Mobile/Wireless, Interactive TV, Internet & New Media, Advanced Animation Technologies, Real-Time 3D graphics, Theme Park concepts, Digital Imaging, Interactive Experiences and Product Development.

His last role at Disney was as Principal Mobile Product Development for a Disney Parks Mobile product scheduled to launch later this year. Armando was also responsible for various mobile strategic initiatives for Walt Disney Parks. Some of the Disney projects that have gone public include Interactive Stitch Phone Booth attraction at Disneyland Innoventions, Turtle Talk with Crush attraction, MovieBeam, and Adventures by Disney. Prior to Disney he was a Program Manager in the Kids Interactive Entertainment group at Microsoft. Armando holds a BS in Computer Engineering & Computer Science from the University of Southern California.
