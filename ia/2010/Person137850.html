Matt Konigsmark’s Summary

Matt Konigsmark brings more than 15 years experience in the marketing/communications industry to the Receivables Exchange, including a dozen years of experience in interactive media. His expertise is identifying and recommending appropriate interactive technologies to extend brand relationships, raise consumer awareness and ultimately create meaningful interactions between businesses and their customers that help marketer's achieve their goals.

Clients at Peter Mayer Advertising included: The New Orleans Saints, Whitney National Bank, CenturyTel, Louisana Office of Economic Development, Ogden Museum of Southern Art, Entergy Corp., New Orleans Tourism Marketing Corporation, CenturyTel, Louisiana Department of Education, Luzianne Coffee and Teas, Louisiana Tourism and the LA/SPCA.

Recent awards include: 2007 EFFIE for WhyYouTeach.org, Adobe Site of the Day for SaintsSuperfan.com and Horizon Interactive Awards for WhyYouTeach.org, SaintsSuperfan.com and EndBitterness.com

A native of Atlanta, Matt is a veteran of the Internet world, having jumped into that burgeoning industry in 1994 and wearing every hat from writer to marketer to general manager. He worked for AccessAtlanta.com, The Atlanta Journal-Constitution's online newspaper. He was the creative force behind Y’all.com, a critically acclaimed webzine devoted to Southern culture. Matt came to New Orleans in 1998 to found InsideNewOrleans.com, then moved to San Francisco to revamp BayInsider.com. He returned to New Orleans in 2002 to become the first Director of Marketing for the City of New Orleans.

Matt is a graduate of the University of Georgia and began his career as a newspaper reporter. Journalistic adventures included writing for Southern Living Magazine and covering Olympic soccer for The Atlanta Journal-Constitution. Matt is one of the founding members of the Southern Food and Beverage Museum (www.southernfood.org) in New Orleans and serves on the New Orleans Habitat for Humanity board.
