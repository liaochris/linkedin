Owen Ahearn, CPM®, ARM®, ACoM®, PHM®’s Summary

In the last twenty years, I have managed residential, commercial, industrial property, and raw land throughout most of New England. I specialize in custom property management for owners and investors of property from single family homes or condominiums to, duplexes, triple-deckers and multi-unit properties.

My goal is to professionally manage the owner's property during their absence and adhere to their goals for the property. I often recommend improvements for the asset/portfolio and provide detailed property reporting.

I have attained four professional certifications: Certified Property Manager® (CPM®), Accredited Commercial Manager® (ACM®) and Accredited Residential Manager® (ARM®) from the Institute of Real Estate Management (IREM®) in Chicago, Illinois and the Public Housing Manager® (PHM®) from the National Association of Housing and Redevelopment Officials (NAHRO®).

I additionally possess my OSHA - 30 Hour General Industry card.

Owen Ahearn, CPM®, ARM®, ACoM®, PHM®’s Specialties:

I specialize in unique properties to manage - single family homes, duplexes, triple-deckers, multi-plexes, and small portfolio scattered site management (under 150 units per owner). I provide property management services in the Massachusetts, Rhode Island and Connecticut area.
