Bharath Chinamanthur’s Summary

Seasoned result oriented, high-energy executive with a 16-year track record of delivering superior business value to a new or existing business unit. Confident in his ability to analyze complex business/technical issues and provide solutions. Proven expertise in all areas of the software development life cycle. Exceptional leadership and communication skills. Capable of engaging Cx0, Senior Management and IT Leadership on complex technical and business issues.

Bharath has an MBA from the Anderson School of Management, University of California - Los Angeles. He has extensive programming , management and entrepreneurial experience with companies such as Citigroup, Citech (Co Founder), VI Engineering in different verticals such as Retail, Automotive, Manufacturing, Heavy Engineering and Financial Services. Bharath previously held a position at Trilogy - Bangalore where he was the Director of Product Management – Automotive Business Unit.
