Raeford Dwyer’s Experience

  • Director

    INTO THE WOODS

    (Non-Profit; Philanthropy industry)

    March 2010 — Present (10 months)

    INTO THE WOODS is essentially an Outdoor Ethics Program for middle school students disguised as an Adventure and Hiking Club. It is a non-profit education organization created in conjunction with the Sierra Club’s ‘Building Bridges to the Outdoors’ project and aims to further its stated goal of “giving every child in America an outdoor experience... leave no child inside”. It has been created for 6-8th grade students that have limited access to nature and may be unaware of green spaces in their own community.
