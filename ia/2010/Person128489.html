Lucia Candu’s Summary

I have 5 years experience in urban economic development in administrative, analytical, research, grantmaking and managerial roles. I've worked for business associations, non-profits, governments and international development organizations.

I've done work in local government reform, international sustainable development, regional development, voter education, community relations, and corporate social responsibility, and I'm excited by recent developments in the green economy.

My greatest satisfaction comes from working with a team to develop and implement effective programs and projects that provide value to organization's clients and stakeholders as well as the society at large.
