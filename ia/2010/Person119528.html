Kevin McKenzie’s Summary

Real Estate Investment Management
Kevin is a founding principal with Palisades Equity Partners, an opportunistic, value-based real estate investment firm based in Orange County. Formerly, he was President of Parker Properties, a Southern California-based developer of tenant-oriented, campus environments. While at Parker, Kevin developed over 750,000 sf of class A office including three LEED Silver buildings.

Since 1986, Kevin has built his career as a developer, principal investor, advisor, investment banker and architect encompassing over $3.5 billion of property investments involving multifamily, industrial, office and retail. Kevin brings extensive executive management, acquisition, development, asset management and capital markets/workout experience. He was formerly with Buchanan Street Partners, AEW Capital Management and a Goldman Sachs/J.E. Robert Companies venture.

Kevin earned his undergraduate degree from the University of Colorado and an MBA from The Anderson School at UCLA. He is an active, full member of ULI, a LEED Accredited Professional and a licensed California Real Estate Broker. As a strong supporter of Scouting, he sits on the Executive Committee for the Orange County Council of the Boy Scouts of America. He is also a adjunct professor at The Paul Merage School of Business at UC Irvine teaching Real Estate Capital Markets, and is active on the Executive Advisory Board for the Center of Real Estate.
