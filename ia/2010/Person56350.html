Dick Post’s Summary

After graduating from Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan, I went for nearly two years to John Marshall Law School. Didn't finish but got excited about the real property course that I took, whereupon I took my real estate Brokers License. After working for a little over six months I opened my own office in July of 1973. Also I obtained my full Insurance Brokers License at that time as well.

Over the next 35+ years or so, I had has many as 5 real estate offices in Chicago and Southwest suburbs where we had over 125 sales agents and had sales of $125 million dollars.

My goals have always to provide "excellence in service" for my clients, giving the highest degree of integrity and remembering the golder rule " do what ever is best for them."

Dick Post’s Specialties:

Having sold residential real estate for 37 years I expanded into the Commercial Real Estate field by taking all of the required Certified Commercial Investment Members classes, giving expertise in analyzing Investment properties for both sellers and buyers.
