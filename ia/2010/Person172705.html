Richard Caizza’s Summary

I am a passionate seeker and inventor of solutions to improve healthcare delivery and patient safety. Over the 23 years that I spent with BD, I was privileged to have the opportunity to positively impact a variety of widespread global healthcare issues through my designs. I have also developed numerous well-known consumer products.

For the last ten years, I invented and helped launch a broad range of technologies designed to protect healthcare workers from accidental needle sticks and blood borne diseases. I also traveled extensively in the developing world, working with clinicians and end-users, as well as for-profit and non-profit entities to address the challenges of disease spread through unsafe injection practices.

My career began in manufacturing, which helps to bring a unique pragmatism to my designs. Many clever inventions are unrealizable because they are impractical to manufacture, don’t meet customer requirements – or both. Because my designs are rooted in an understanding of how they will be made and used, more practical and actionable solutions result.

After leaving BD, I formed Fulcrum along with several other former colleagues who share my passion for delivering better and safer healthcare. Today, we provide our collective expertise to medical technology companies as leverage, so that they can innovate faster and more confidently.

Richard Caizza’s Specialties:

design and manufacture of drug delivery devices, especially those associated with injection; prefilled injection devices; design-to-manufacturing; manufacturing set-up and design; rapid prototyping; creative thinking and problem solving; invention.
