Jerry Ashton’s Summary

Focusing strictly on two avenues of satisfying clients: (1) Independent consulting in the application of "Relationship Management" via social media to A/R controls, credit and collection, and (2) delivering Cost Segregation services to help accelerate depreciation on commercial properties.

In both cases, I work with and direct a team of sales and industry specialists who are devoted to keeping your cash flow at maximum and tax liabilities at minimum.
