Bill Shockley’s Summary

I have spent the last ten years helping companies innovate by integrating idea management, collaboration, and suggestion tools into their workforces. These tools help create and maintain cultures of innovation that are proven to provide continuous and long-lasting results.

During that time I have worked with a number of idea management software applications as they have adapted themselves to social networking.

Also, I have published over a dozen white papers (three are available below for download) and contributed to two books, Jim Schwarz's "A to Z of Idea Management" and P.S. Gopalakrishnan's "Ideas, Creativity and Innovation - Keys to Survival."

Spigit is a new paradigm, combining idea management, social networking, and financial tools into a single package.
