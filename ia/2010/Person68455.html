Bob Robinson's Summary

Bob Robinson is recognized as one of the country's foremost experts on human resources and corporate leadership. His sales and operations expertise have helped build one of America's most successful companies, Express Employment Professionals.

Bob has over 25 years of experience in Human Resources Consulting, Staffing, Recruiting, and Professional Search, Information Systems Technology, IT Operations Management, Business Systems Analysis and Systems and Applications Programming.

• Kennesaw State University, BBA
• Reinhardt University, AA
• United States Army Veteran

Previously, Bob has held leadership positions including, information technology manager, systems/ business analyst.
