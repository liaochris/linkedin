Mark Heap’s Summary

Currently leading PHD China, a strategy focused media agency and part of Omnicom Group. Since I took over PHD, we have increase billings over 20x and revenue 15x. The profitability has improved substantially and our new business wins include HP, Estee Lauder, Tiffany&co and Unilever.

We were the only market in the world to win the Unilever business off their incumbent agency in their Q4 2009 review. This win is the largest piece of media business to change hands in China.

PHD are the reigning Media Agency of the Year across Asia.

Prior to PHD, I spent 12 years with MindShare working in London, Sydney and China in roles across different disciplines. Whilst at MindShare China, I learned to bridge cultures and to manage large workforces effectively to develop the company wide product offering.

I am passionate about marketing and communications. I love to contribute and be involved and I enjoy working with others who want to add value to the conversation.
