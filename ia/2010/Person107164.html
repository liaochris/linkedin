James Segil’s Summary

Serial Entrepreneur & Technology executive with demonstrated success at creating, growing and turning around small businesses. Adept at managing priorities, building businesses and pulling together a team around a common vision.

Current Co-founder and President of EdgeCast Networks Inc., a new Content Delivery Network for Video Streaming. Edgecast has built a global edge network optimized for distributing and streaming large media files (www.edgecast.com).

Former President & Founder of KnowledgeBase Solutions Inc, now a division of Talisma Corporation. Successfully built this privately funded Enterprise Software company into a profitable industry leading company. KnowledgeBase was ranked by Forrester as the Industry Leader in eService and was acquired by Talisma Corp in March 2005 (www.talisma.com).

Former COO for Virtualis Systems Inc (rebranded Hosting.com), now a division of Allegiance Telecom. Successfully ran and sold venture backed hosting company between years 1999-2000 (www.hosting.com).

Former Product Manager for Hot Wheels Brand at Mattel Toys. Prior to that, Associate Product Manager for the Boys Toys division for all markets outside of the US (www.mattel.com).

Graduate of the Harvard Business School - class of 1999

James Segil’s Specialties:

Hands-on tech start-up experience including: business planning, fund raising, sales, business development, recruiting, finance, etc. Deep industry experience in Web and Applications Hosting, Content Delivery, CRM, Knowledge Management, Document Management, eService, Consumer Products, Juvenile Products and Retail merchandising.
