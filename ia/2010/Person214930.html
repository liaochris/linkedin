WESTMED Medical Group’s Summary

At WESTMED Medical Group you’ll find highly qualified, committed physicians and staff providing compassionate, coordinated, convenient, efficient and high-quality health care. With 170+ physicians & 700 staff, we are a high-growth, financially thriving multi-specialty group practice.

WESTMED strives to be a "provider of choice" and an "employer of choice". We provide a modern office environment, state-of-the-art equipment and technology as well as highly competitive benefits and compensation, including profit sharing and bonus opportunity.

Our two main locations at 210 Westchester Avenue, White Plains, NY and 1 Theall Road, Rye, NY provide full-service care, including primary and specialty care, radiology, lab, urgent care and on-site pharmacies. Our satellite offices in Westchester provide primary and specialty care. Our full-service approach and our partnerships with White Plains Hospital Center, Greenwich Hospital, and Lawrence Hospital make us a complete resource for health care.
