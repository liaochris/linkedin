Maria C. Villani, M.D.’s Summary

Dr. Villani is a scientist, medical doctor and business executive with successful track record of developing innovative products and organizations, specializing in dermatology, building quality brands to achieve maximum market potential.

Dr. Villani graduated from Russian State Medical University, one of the most prominent and respectful medical schools in the world.

She is attending the Executive MBA program in healthcare management at The Paul Merage School of Business at University of California.

Dr. Villani’s interest in research in dermatology and development of new topical pharmaceuticals led her 18 years-long investigation of the effects of bioactive compounds and secondary metabolites of invertebrate species to development of innovative therapeutics for the treatment of dermatological conditions.

Her inventions of are protected by patents worldwide.

Dr. Villani continuously contributes into research in dermatology. One of her fortes is the development of novel methods for modification of the chemical structures of naturally occurring compounds of the marine sponges.

As CEO of Villani Pharmaceuticals, she is leading company in development of the products for the treatment and prevention of various dermatologic conditions, such as acne, rosacea, actinic keratosis, dermatitis, folliculitis, seborrhea, hyperpigmentation, sun damage and aging.

Dr. Villani is the CEO of Villani Medical, a research-based pharmaceutical company that develops professional skin Resurfacing and Rejuvenating Products based on Patented Technologies that activate therapeutic compounds and nanoparticles produced by unique fresh-water sponges.

As a Scientific and Medical Officer of Villani Spongilla Technologies, Dr. Villani works on mechanism of action of the bioactive compounds that are derived from marine sponges and isolated through biologically guided purification.

Language: Fluent English, Russian
