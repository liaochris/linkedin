Andy MacDonald’s Summary

I have had a varied career in computing and information technology. My latest passion is connecting people with information. For that purpose I co-founded Blogarithm.com with Max Minkoff (sold to RSSFWD.com in September, 2007), worked as tech director at the Seattle Times Company, and now on Microsoft's Bing Search team.

Before that I spent seven years at Amazon.com. Prior to that I was a virtual reality researcher and developer, creating and commercializing software to bring an immersive VR experience to the average consumer.

Seattle has been my home since 1991 (with a brief digression to Chicago). I am committed to preserving and improving the beauty and quality of life of the city. To serve that mission, I serve on several local boards and councils and write for the blog Sound Politics.com.

Andy MacDonald’s Specialties:

Internet information architecture, blog formulation and promotion, online organization and activism.
