Mario Salman’s Summary

• 20 years of advertising experience, 16 of them on direct response, one-to-one communications.
• Strong cross-media creative experience, with substantial Broadcast, Print, Direct, and Interactive expertise.
• Fully bilingual creative writer (English/Spanish).
• International and multicultural expertise, having worked in the United States, Chile, Peru, and Argentina.
• CD and ECD positions held at agencies such as Wunderman, Dieste, Grey Direct, and MRM Worldwide.
• Work recognized at Cannes Lions, The DMA Echo Awards, The New York Festivals, London International Advertising Awards, John Caples Awards, D/FW Interactive Awards, AMDIA, ACHAP, and the ADDYs.
• Copywriting and Creativity teacher, speaker at the DMA Annual Conference & Exhibition, and judge at The John Caples Awards, The Globes Awards, FIAP, and Lapiz de Oro.
