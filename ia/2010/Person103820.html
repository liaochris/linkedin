Jonathan Giles’s Summary

At present, I am a software engineer in the JavaFX team at Sun Microsystems, where I am involved in the development of JavaFX user interface controls, and to a lesser degree the design and implementation of other JavaFX APIs for future releases of the JavaFX platform.

Jonathan Giles’s Specialties:

Primarily my skills are in Java and JavaFX development, especially in rich clients, plugin-based software, semantic web software and visualisation technologies.
I do know a number of other languages and technology, and I'm always keen to learn new things.
