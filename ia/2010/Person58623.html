Dorian West Blair, J.D.’s Summary

As an HR professional and employment attorney with 15 plus years experience, I have strong analytical, problem-solving, planning, organizational, risk management and communication skills. I use these skills as an HR leader to develop and foster an aligned HR strategy to drive the business strategy and ultimate success of a company. I proactively and quickly learn a company’s business, challenges, strategy, organization and desired culture; identify HR and organizational gaps; and develop creative, value-added, cost-effective, compliant and practical people strategies to support the business. I use metrics/analytics to develop and monitor plans and use my energy and negotiation skills to partner, communicate and obtain buy-in and support. While I have advanced HR generalist skills, I am particularly skilled in Organizational/Process Design, Recruitment, Compensation and Employee Relations, having developed numerous strategies and solutions in these areas for the business' desired outcome. I am passionate about supporting innovative businesses with a progressive culture.
