Brandon Smith, LEED AP’s Summary

BASmith Design Associates is a full service multi-disciplinary design firm. Started in 2004 under the premise of providing systems furniture spaceplanning and development to a host of clients, BASDA has continually strengthed its position in the San Diego interior design community. During the past few years, BASDA expanded its realm to include interior and exterior tenant improvements as well as permit processing and construction document development.

Since 2004, we've had the priviledge of working with clients from all industries including office, retail, industrial, goverment, and hospitality, both Fortune 500 and small "mom/pop" type operations. Additionally, we've completed projects on an national level - providing spaceplanning and design for projects in New York, Virginia, Washington DC, Texas, and New Hampshire as well as internationally, having provided our services to large scale oil and civic projects in Dubai and Kuwait.

We are continually growing, adding both employees, team members, as well as new clientele to the mix.

Current Projects:
- Premier Nutrition Tenant Improvements
- Penthouse & Architectural Management at The Mark
- Coronado Cafe Interior Design
- Midway Village Site Improvements
- Hole in the Wall Restaurant Design

Representative Clients:
The RADCO Companies, Accredited Home Lenders, Iomega Corp., US Navy, Wells Fargo, City of Trenton New Jersey, Pacifica Companies, CB Richard Ellis, Paragon MedSystems, 7-Eleven, Gateway Property Management
