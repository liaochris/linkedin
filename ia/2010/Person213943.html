Wayne Cozad’s Experience

    Managing Partner of Cube Management LLC, a Portland, Oregon-based company which helps businesses optimize their performance.   • Partner & Sr Vice President

    Cascade General, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Shipbuilding industry)

    March 1995 — December 2000 (5 years 10 months)

    From 1995 to 2000, Mr. Cozad served as Partner and Senior Vice President, of Cascade General in Portland, Oregon. Mr. Cozad participated in the acquisition and turnaround of the company in 1995, when it was $35 million in debt and on the verge of bankruptcy. His leadership in upgrading and modernizing the facility and workforce helped the company grow from $44 million to over $132 million in average annual revenues, and emerge as the US market leader. Mr. Cozad managed effective negotiations and regulatory reporting with the Port of Portland, the Portland Metal Trades (11 Unions), various federal, state and local agencies which impacted the Shipyard’s ability to profitably operate. In mid-2000 Mr. Cozad was instrumental in facilitating the company’s successful acquisition of the 56-acre shipyard complex from the Port of Portland.

  • Co-Founder & CFO

    Associated Environmental, Inc.

    (Environmental Services industry)

    March 1990 — February 1995 (5 years )

    From 1990 to 1995, Mr. Cozad was Co-Founder and Chief Financial Officer of Associated Environmental, an engineering services company which grew from a single location in Atlanta, Georgia to having operations in five states. Clients included Fortune 100 companies (BP Oil, Unocal, Chevron, CSX Transportation, Goodyear Tire and Rubber) and major financial institutions. With P&L responsibility for the corporation, Mr. Cozad grew it from start-up to more than $5 million in annual revenues.

  • Founder & CEO

    CoComm, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    January 1985 — February 1990 (5 years 2 months)

    From 1985 to 1990, Mr. Cozad was the Founder and CEO of CoComm, Inc. a leading computer retailer specializing in the leasing, sale and service of new and used personal computers and peripherals, as well as software training and custom database design services. Clients included both corporate accounts and individuals.

  • Regional Manager

    Group W Satellite Communications

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    March 1983 — January 1985 (1 year 11 months)

Clients included both corporate accounts and individuals.

He secured one of the largest cable television advertising and promotional campaign contracts and helped pioneer the establishment of the company’s national cable advertising business.
