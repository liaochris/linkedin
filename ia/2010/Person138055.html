Matthew Pine’s Summary

Mr. Pine is a seasoned commercial professional with a proven track record in leadership, coaching, and strategic planning. He is a skilled, participatory manager with hands-on experience in staff administration and department operations. Mr. Pine has held a number of leadership positions, spent 7 years with General Electric Healthcare Systems in Medical Devices and Health care IT. He recently joined US Oncology to lead their US Sales Organization for Physician Services. Mr. Pine is highly analytical with sound judgment and decision-making capabilities. A results-driven leader with with a passion for developing people and high performance teams.
