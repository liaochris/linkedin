MICHELLE HANCOCK, OWNER, DESIGNER’s Summary

A strong entrepreneurial spirit and ownership of several enterprises, Michelle saw an opportunity to take her Event Planning, Design and Production company to a new market level. In June 2009 she transitioned fulltime to the Events Industry where she has long had a footing in delivering full service planning, design and execution of weddings and all events for clients representative of business, political, government, community, fraternal, personal, social etc., spectrums.

Michelle formerly worked as an Executive Search Consultant with World Bridge Partners specializing in the recruitment and placement of Business Professionals and ‘Top of the House Executives’. She boarded with the same enthusiasm, engagement and resourcefulness that she has delivered on more than 2400 career placements. She has 25 years experience in the Human Resources and Staffing Solutions fields, is an HR Practitioner, entrepreneur and has owned four successful businesses. She understands the broad scope of business and the challenges that come in hiring impact players at all levels to grow revenue, brand and retention.

In November 2008, Michelle joined the Contract Staffing Division. She soon became Director, Healthcare IT&IS. Meeting the pressing needs of client companies, Michelle sourced and recruited 2,632 top-flight talented candidates from passive and active status.

On April 1, 2009, Michelle stepped into the role of National Director of Product Marketing to promote the company's prestigious Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) branded under the company name, WorldConcert™. This RPO product has proven to save business partners up to 25% of their staffing budget and a reduction in open positions at any one time by 80%. WorldBridge Partners' RPO has multiple direct benefits from the outset and a long term ROI.
