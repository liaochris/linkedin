Jim Masters’s Summary

An award winning Broadcaster/On-Camera/On-Air Host-Correspondent/Reporter/Spokesperson/Voice Talent/Emcee/Commercial Actor/Writer/Producer in the communications, broadcasting, entertainment, advertising industries for 25 years.

Solid, professional experience primarily focused on On-Camera and On-Air Host/Correspondent/Spokesperson & Talent related work, yet with a solid foundation and background behind the scenes. Have worn many hats in the industry for broadcast outlets, production companies, marketing firms, talent and advertising agencies, news operations & the web.

Host programs and productions that broadcast locally & nationally and have also been featured worldwide streaming on the internet.

My talent and experience is utilized by a number of respected entities including Commercial Television & Radio, as well as, PBS - Public Television, Internet sites, and talent agencies. I've conducted countless TV and Radio celebrity & news-maker interviews, performed in numerous commercials and corporate videos and have filed many news reports. Freelance Print Model for various agencies. "Voice Of God Announcer" & Emcee for corporate shows & LIVE events.

I also have extensive on-air sales experience hosting and producing, marketing and selling on-air consumer shopping shows generating millions.

Host for numerous national TV infomercials and have great experience in that area. Game Show hosting is another area of expertise.

Live, ad-lib TV with personality, bouncing off the energy of the audience and co-host, different segments, topics and themes. I love the energy of LIVE, yet am thoroughly skilled in scripted work.

Producers often refer to me as being easily directed, fun to work with, a team player, professional, credible, quick-witted, loyal, dedicated, passionate, flexible, adaptable, ability to play a wide age range and offer different looks, believable, trusted, engaging, warm, sincere, yet with authority.
