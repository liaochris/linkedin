Jawad Ayaz’s Summary

Providing seed capital and advisory services for India-based, early-stage start-ups that leverage mobile and web technologies.

Background - technologist and entrepreneur who founded multiple companies in the mobile software space. Most recently - Openera Technologies a mobile applications company sold to NMSS Communications (NASDAQ: NMSS) in 2006, and before that Xybridge Technologies a mobile networking company sold to Zhone Technologies (NASDAQ: ZHNE) in 2001.
Featured on the cover of Red Herring magazine as one India's leading technology product innovators (http://www.redherring.com/Home/11498).
