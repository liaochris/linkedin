Mindy Flanigan, PHR’s Summary

I’m an energetic, results oriented Human Resource professional, with a diverse background that includes Recruitment, Selection, Performance Management, Compliance, Payroll, Benefits and Worker’s Compensation Administration. As an effective communicator, motivator and pro-active thinker I have the skill set to satisfy my client’s goals as I strive to create or maintain a positive work environment.

Highlights
PHR Certification, through Human Resource Certification Institute (HRCI), since 2002.
National Member of Society for Human Resource Management.
Member of Richmond Human Resource Management Association.
Member of Chesterfield Chamber of Commerce.
16 years progressive Human Resource experience. All but 1 year in HR Outsourcing industry, servicing small businesses in 10 states. Serviced or supervised those who have serviced over 200 clients and over 2,000 worksite employees.

Graduate of State University of New York at Geneseo, with a B.S. in Management Science, concentration in Human Resource Management.
