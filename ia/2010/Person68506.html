Clint Walkingstick’s Experience

  • Owner

    Eye Say

    (Design industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    Eye Say is a visual communication/print marketing firm located in central Oklahoma. We assist financial councilors in positively affecting lives by creating targeted print marketing that stresses the benefits of living debt-free. We do this by researching their story and relating that story to their audience in a visually consistent and compelling way. We bring eyes to what you have to say.

    The idea for the company was born out of a desire to help families and individuals to become and live debt-free by engaging them in a dialogue with financial councilors whose heart is to do just that.

    We do this by creating printed marketing materials for financial councilors that set them apart in their market by visually projecting their message and professionalism in a unique way.

  • Graphic Designer

    BGCO (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Religious Institutions industry)

    September 2006 — Present (4 years 1 month)

    At the BGCO, I report to the Print Operations Specialist and work on a daily basis with our print project manager who collects all client info, project specs, budgets and timelines.

    I use the project information she gives me to create custom designed print marketing pieces from the ground up for our internal clients (ministries). These projects usually consist of event marketing materials that are printed and sent via U.S. Mail or by email.

    Types of printed materials I've designed for our ministries: logotypes, business cards, post cards, buttons, pens, web graphics, advertisements, multi-panel brochure/mailers, multi-page brochures, magazines, posters, t-shirts, trade show graphics and vinyl banners.
