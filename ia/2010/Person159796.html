★ Patrick Bourbon, CFA’s Summary

Highly analytical and talented global analyst/portfolio manager with 15+ years' investment experience and passion, blended with a wealth of academic credentials. My portfolio management experience includes asset allocation, risk management, stocks/funds selection and due diligence. My aspirations are to help individuals achieve their goals and to increase my investment knowledge.

My passion for investments started when I was 12 years old when I visited the Paris stock exchange. At that time, I was given a $2 bill and I started to check every week its value in French Francs! Since then, I am fascinated by currency management.

I understand the importance of long term planning. I knew that I will study Finance but I decided to obtain a Master in Engineering first, because I aimed to have a different background and training. During my undergraduate studies, I created an investment club, interned at JP Morgan and BNP Paribas, and I started to manage a fund of funds in euros in 1995. My early work experience in financial services impressed upon me the values of excellence and integrity.

In 2009, after 15 years of helping friends with their investments, I created Bourbon Financial Management, and I decided to bring my institutional money management experience to help individuals and families live the life they want. Being competent was no longer enough. I wanted meaning.

Following my strong interest in academic research, education, and helping communities, I became adjunct Professor in Finance at the Illinois Institute of Technology, the President of UFEC, and a member of the Board of the South Shore Drill Team and Stuart Investments. Additionally, prior to founding BFM, I was Director at UBS Asset Management for 10 years.

I thrive in an environment of challenges that give opportunities to solve complex financial problems. An avid competitor with a strong will to succeed as illustrated by 10 years of competition in ski and tennis (certified instructor in both).
