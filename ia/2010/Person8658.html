Alyson Boyd’s Summary

Marketing & social/new/digital media, fitness, nutrition and sports. These are may passions, both professionally and personally. They may seem broad or perhaps even disconnected, but it comes down to one idea: uniting people with what brings them happiness.

Ok, I admit it sounds cheesy, but whether it's helping someone be healthier through diet & exercise so they can have more energy to pursue their dreams, recommending the perfect spa service so a worn-out mother can relax, tweeting out an article that resonates a beneficial message, improving the connection between a customer and their favorite brand, or feeling the excitement in a stadium when the home team scores, these experiences make that person's life better, whether it's for a moment or a lifetime.

Do what you love and love what you do. This belief has driven me to continue to expand my knowledge and gain experience across an integrated realm of disciplines. I look forward to connecting with people and companies who share a similar commitment enhancing their lives along with those of their clients/customers/consumers.

Currently looking for my next professional adventure!
