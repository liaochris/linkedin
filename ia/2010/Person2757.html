Adam Bradley’s Summary

I'm a web programmer experienced mainly with development in a LAMP environment. Server-side, I'm comfortable with the CodeIgniter and Kohana frameworks, and I use primarily jQuery for the client side, though I've been tinkering with Prototype/script.aculo.us lately. Recently I've been designing several web services for internal (Brown University) use.
