Jim McQuaid’s Summary

Filmmaker, writer, producer and thinker about the problems of communicating, convincing and connecting. Also a guy who likes to shoot and get together with other filmmakers. The mixture of my high tech skills, my love of film & story telling and my teaching background enables me to tackle any kind of training video project from planning to delivery.

From my high tech career, I have years of experience delivering customer-driven product requirements and effective sales support & communications. I'm versatile in the face of change, like on a film set! Good at managing by influence.

In addition to years of Product Management experience at NetQoS, Netiq, Ganymede, Netscout and Wandel & Golterman, I also have run Marketing Communications, analyst & press relations and advertising for a variety of high tech organizations.
