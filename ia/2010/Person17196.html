Conall Lavery’s Summary

Conall has had a successful career to date in the areas of business startup, radio engineering, sales and marketing.

Conall has a proven track record as an entrepreneur. He was nominated as a finalist in the Ernst & Young’s Entrepreneur of the Year program in 2000. He has founded a number of companies, the last being Entropy. Since leaving Entropy, he has completed a diploma in Corporate Governance in UCD, played a role in a charitable foundation, and provided consultancy services to Mainstream Renewable Power.

His day job is in the start up AscenTrust, but he is also seeking to set up a small portfolio of start up companies where he can provide strategic input, sales and marketing expertise and add value with best practices in Corporate Governance.

Conall is a board member of the Corporate Governance Association of Ireland, and the Marketing Institute of Ireland.

    As the founder and general manager of Entropy, I was responsible for the startup, then scaling the business and eventually to a trade sale of over €5 M in March 2006.     I was responsible for developing the initial strategy of the company, and later managed the process of strategy development with the other shareholders in the management team. At the time ownership was widened I set up a board of directors and appointed a non-executive chairman, Eddie O’Connor (Bord na Mona, Airtricity and Mainstream Renewable Power) to the board.
