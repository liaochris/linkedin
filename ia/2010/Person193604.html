  • Head of Engineering

    Siine Ltd

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    July 2010 — Present (4 months)

    Siine is one of the most exciting projects currently under development in the Web 3.0 and technological innovation space. With its core focus on communications interface design, the team at Siine has created a whole new paradigm for communication and text input on touchscreen devices. Siine is revolutionizing text-based communication, radically improving the texting process for social, IM, gaming and business communication.

    However, Siine goes beyond just an innovative new way to type – its features liberate the user from the time consuming logistics of organising your day to day activities, freeing you to live your life more completely.

    Siine’s revolutionary concept is the result of three years of structured research into Linguistics, Human Gesture and Group Communications, which started at Stanford University. Currently being incubated in Barcelona, Siine has a serious and talented team working from the full-featured prototypes to create launch ready beta products in Q4 2010.
