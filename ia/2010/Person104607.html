Jon Tucker’s Summary

I've been making money on the internet and providing expert marketing consulting for the past 5 years. I'm currently looking for a long term project where I can bring value with my marketing experience while being surrounded by driven team members that I can learn from.

For the last 5 years, I've worked with small businesses, professionals, and entrepreneurs from all around the world. Through providing professional internet marketing consulting services to clients, I'm able to help them better understand how to leverage online tools to increase new business coming in.

My priority is to make clients aware of the resources that are available to their business and provide guidance on how to best utilize these tools and strategies to generate increased revenue for their business.

In addition to providing professional consulting to businesses, I also run a range of my own online ventures. Through launching and running my various ventures, I've been fortunate enough to learn many extremely valuable lessons at a young age and also support myself through 4 years of college and various adventures traveling around the world.

Through programs I've been involved with (including SDSU's "Lavin Entrepreneur program"), I've met with founders and CEOs of large multi-million dollar companies, including Alberto Culver Corporation, Volcom Clothing, and many more. Through this experience, I've learned that the key to building a successful company is to follow your passions and always continue learning. I integrate these values into any new project I take on, whether it's my own project or a client project.

I'm currently searching for an opportunity to work with a existing or serious startup company long term, where I can bring value to the table with my marketing experience and be surrounded by driven team members that I can learn from.

Feel free to send me an email anytime, as I always enjoy a great conversation and chance to learn from one another's experiences.
