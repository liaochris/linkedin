Ed Luck’s Summary

Ed Luck has over 15 years of sales and technical experience in the IT industry and over 25 years of first-line, corporate management experience.

Today, he passionately serves as the Technical Solutions Sr. Manager for Tushaus Computer Services and is responsible for development of its 60+ person, practice led Technical Services team. His primary focus is first line management, visionary leadership, and delivery of consistent and repeatable, high quality IT solutions to all Tushaus Wisconsin and National markets.

Ed, with the help of many others, have built strong and respected vendor relationships with Microsoft, Cisco, and Citrix by focusing on infrastructure consulting and high quality / cutting edge IT solution services. Strength and commitment to these relationships coupled with a broad understanding of IT solutions have enabled the Tushaus team of professionals to provide organizations with world class solutions while delivering unparalleled customer satisfaction.

Prior to Tushaus, Luck co-founded Bedrock Managed Services and Consulting. He served as a C-level leader in focused development of the company’s Managed Services and Advanced IT Solutions offering efforts with over 95% of its revenue coming from services and solutions delivery.

Prior to Bedrock, he served as the VP of Sales and Service at Office Technology, Inc. For over six years, under his leadership, Office Technology transitioned from a product-centric hardware reseller to a top-tier solution service provider generating 75% of its revenue from consulting services.

Over the years, Ed has heavily influenced the growth and startup of a number of IT services companies in the Wisconsin markets that he serves.
