Rachael Steidl’s Summary

Rachael Ross Steidl, founder and publisher of the ParentClick.com Network and SBParent.com, is a mom of three girls and a Santa Barbara native. Prior to becoming a mother, her work experience included many years in marketing and management and a M.A. in Clinical Psychology. Rachael was inspired by her 11-year-old twins and 8-year old to start a website for families in Santa Barbara 8 years ago as a way to connect parents with resources in the community and help provide them with choices. Rachael is actively involved in the community serving on several local boards and volunteering in her daughter's schools.

ParentClick.com is an online publisher that provides a pulse on the local scene and a city guide for families in communities nationwide. ParentClick.com gives parents choices 24/7 by connecting them to resources in their community. ParentClick.com provides local parenting information in more than 20 U.S. markets. ParentClick.com provides product reviews, children’s book & movie reviews, family travel reviews, a recipe club, blogs, parenting articles and a monthly e-newsletter.
