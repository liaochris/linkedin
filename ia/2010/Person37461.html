Chris Broyles’s Summary

While completing Columbia College Chicago's Graduate School program with an MA in Arts, Entertainment & Media Management, Broyles dove into production with WMAQ/NBC-5, Producer/Program Director for The Museum of Broadcast Communications, Producer of The '97 & '98 Radio Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony hosted by Casey Kasem, and the nationally syndicated talk show "Beyond the Beltway with Bruce DuMont" for flagship WLS-890 AM Chicago.

Part of this experience between 1996 - 2000 involved expanding the use of computers within the media industry, and creating new production methods in tandem with the growth of the Internet. He was actively involved in the advent of streaming media via Windows and RealNetworks, and made headlines when BTB became the first political talk show to air regularly on the web via streaming video.

Chris spent 1999-2002 working for CMGi owned Magnitude Network (200+ streaming radio stations nationwide) and Divine's TV House Inc. as a Director, Streaming Media. The latter included developing and producing various content offerings and business media. One firm project, the Morningstar Report for morningstar.com, offered the challenge of using persuasive media to explain complex information, thus making it accessible to a mass audience. It was this work that inspired the move in early 2002 to visual media consulting in the complex world of litigation.

Since 2002, Chris has worked in litigation consulting. Broyles has the ability to consult directly with attorneys on graphics and media strategy during the pre-trial phase, as well as staffing and supporting on-site trial teams using 2D and 3D animations, graphics, trial technology, expert witness visualizations and more. These trials, arbitrations and hearings take place in State and Federal courts and venues around the country including Investigations before the International Trade Commission (ITC) in Washington, D.C.

Chris Broyles’s Specialties:

Visual Consulting for Litigators, Media Management, Trial Site Management, Graphic Design, Video Deposition Editing, Courtroom Director, War Room Producer
