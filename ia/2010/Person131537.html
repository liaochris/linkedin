Marci Rodgers’s Summary

Experience: I’ve been fortunate to work with a variety of organizations over the past 20 years and am always impressed with the different ways people and businesses work. My clients get the best results when we use a combination of business development, marketing, and sales techniques to achieve their goals.

Diversity: The diversity of companies and clients compliments the services I provide. Each client is unique and receives a customized plan based on his/her goals. I’ve worked with a variety of companies in high tech as well as commercial and non-profit businesses – large and small.

Passion: A previous Silicon Valley resident, I have now lived and worked in the Tri-Valley for the last 10 years. Silicon Valley is a great example of what the Tri-Valley can strive to become – even better because of our people, technology (LLNL and Sandia) and wine country. With a broad set of services and local network we strive to meet and exceed a clients’ expectations. Moving your business forward is what we strive to do!
