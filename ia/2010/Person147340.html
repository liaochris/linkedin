Michael J Lambie’s Summary

Michael is a Digital Research Director for The Nielsen Company's Strategic Marketing Sciences group previously designing online research applications for the primary television research group. He currently works on ad effectiveness, brand strategy, product tracking for clients across television and technology. Many projects span across the Nielsen company and fuse behavioral, attitudinal and psychographic data from proprietary Nielsen data sets including TV viewing, online and mobile behavioral data. During his four plus years at Nielsen he has developed qualitative research apps, and co-created a network of digital labs for hands on testing with a national sample focusing on testing television pilots, digital media usability and cross-platform ad effectiveness. Michael helped bring in EEG brain wave research, eye tracking hardware and galvanic skin response. He has also consulted on entertainment opinion engine based social network HeyNielsen and helped to create ViewerCast, an online video portal for collecting qualitative research from webcams.

In addition to his duties at Nielsen Michael also serves as a Board Director for the Southern California Market Research Association. He has consulted on projects for the American Film Institute's Digital Content Lab. He previously co-coordinated the LA Geek Dinner and the LA Dataportability meetups. He speaks at events on a number of topics including interaction design, online metrics and modern consumer research. Most recently at SXSW, Santa Monica Public Library, East LA College, Loyola Marymount, Mindshare LA, BarCampLA, the IxDA LA Salon and is a co-host of the Digital Family Reunion.

Michael is most currently interested combining marketing science with behavioral social data to form predictive models.
