Harun Razith’s Summary

Harun Razith is a designer, educator and a creative enthusiast. He is a Senior Consultant, Product Design and User Experience at Allurent Inc. specializing in next generation online shopping experiences and designing RIAs and Branding kits. Prior to this he was the senior Interface designer at Soliloquy learning Inc., an educational software company committed to helping children learn to read and improve fluency and comprehension.

As an ardent freelancer Harun has consulted various companies and institutions in the field of education, video game design, publishing, medicine, theater and arts. His client list include Boston Public Schools, Institute for Global Work, AT&T, Dover Publishing, Massachusetts College of Art, Courier Corporation, University of New Hampshire and Cecropia Inc.

A few of his experimental work have been exhibited at the Boston State House and The Revolving Museum at Lowell. Harun holds a MFA in Interactive Design from the Massachusetts College of Art, Boston;
