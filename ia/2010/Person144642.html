Mickey Moore’s Summary

THE SCIENCE OF HIGH PERFORMANCE

High Performance Organizations (HPOs) do not serendipitously occur. HPO’s rely on systems and technology in order to achieve their operational nirvana. Unfortunately, with today’s web-based maelstrom of bloated misinformation, accurate critical decisions are impossible without a scientific approach to high-level strategic planning and opportunity analysis.

SAILING THE HIGH PERFORMANCE SEAS

Creating high-level strategy is similar to navigating a ship at sea. The first step is to plot an initial course and with any luck the eventual myriad of subsequent ‘course corrections’ will not delay the ship from getting to its final destination on time. Maximum return is achieved with the most scientifically accurate initial course plotting. The same is true for high-level strategic planning; success and high performance is only achieved with the most accurate initial decisions.

HIGH PERFORMANCE STRATEGY CONTINUUM

Once an organization makes their initial high-level strategic decisions, execution requires alignment with Investors, Management, Employees, Vendors and especially Customers. Unfortunately today’s systems and technologies are designed to stand alone, without the ability to truly align all constituents to strategy and enable managed systematic interaction throughout an organization.

SOLUTIONS FOR HIGH PERFORMANCE

Systems and technology convergence is the key to delivering the scientific discipline required to achieve High Performance. This is HPO Ventures, LLC’s mission, to empower all organizations big and small with the solutions to achieve High Performance. HPO Ventures, LLC dba goHPO develops web-based software, training programs, execution resources and social networks to make any sized organization a High Performer.
