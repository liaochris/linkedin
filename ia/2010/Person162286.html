Performa Architects’s Summary

Performa Inc. is a Wisconsin based corporation licensed in 42 states in all areas of the practice of Planning, Interior Design, Space Planning, Architecture and Engineering.

We were founded in 1995 by a small, tight-knit group of experienced planners, architects and engineers for the singular purpose of creating more practical solutions for clients' business challenges; solutions that deliver measurably higher operational and financial performance.

Our approach is to always place our client's business objectives ahead of all else. We listen and strive to fully understand your business strategy, goals, and priorities in the same way you do. As a result, our solutions are consistently a natural extension of your business priorities for purpose, function, cost, schedule and image.

Most architectural firms are driven to create award-winning designs that garner the most accolades. Performa is the exception. For Performa, the right solution is the one that most efficiently and effectively satisfies the client's objectives.

Business owners from a broad range of markets have repeatedly chosen Performa for facility solutions that deliver on their business objectives. Isn't it time you worked with a firm that cares as much about your business as you do?
