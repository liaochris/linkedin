Lee Smallwood’s Summary

Lee is an experienced marketer with specialist knowledge in integrated online/offline using direct, data and digital marketing techniques. In addition to providing overall support to the account management and creative teams, Lee works with clients to explore, develop and implement the most effective ways of reaching those 'hard to reach back teeth' – their target audiences.

Purposefully absorbing a market history, Lee reviews organisation's DNA, KPI's and market status, in order to devise and drive bespoke brand, communication and promotional strategies that achieve specific objectives and its short and long-term goals.

Lee has worked on projects and campaigns ranging from the development of digital iconography for Orange to researching the commercial and domestic markets for the NFU Mutual. He has project managed the development and implementation of the Gloucestershire Development Agency’s 3R’s campaign: reduce, reuse and recycle and established USP’s and positioning statements for Ecotricity and NexGen.

Lee is a Fellow of the ISMM, a member of the IDM, CIM and a Business Leader with the Marketing Society.
