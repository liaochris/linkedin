Keith Cornell’s Summary

Keith Cornell brings twelve years of executive management, marketing and corporate development experience from the international telecoms market. Most recently he was CEO of Empower Interactive, a messaging platform service-centric software company. He has also served on the management teams of several leading mobile operators, including O2, as President for European Operations, with direct responsiblity for Viag Interkom, Telfort, Digifone and Manx; as well as Board Member overseeing BT's investment in Cegetel/SFR. Prior to O2, he held the positions of VP International Operations for AirTouch and Director Southern Europe for Vodafone AirTouch. There, he was on the Boards of Omnitel, MobiFon, Europolitan (publicly-traded), and Airtel. He has also held a number of non-executive roles in the wireless industry, including Oplayo, End2End, Digital Rum, Intellisync (NASDAQ: SYNC), Celltick, Startrak and Cellglide. Prior to joining AirTouch, Keith was with the management consultancy Marakon Associates in San Francisco, New York and London. He received his MBA from Carnegie-Mellon in 1980, has a BS in Engineering from Harvey Mudd, and lives outside London with his wife and two daughters.
