Paul Johnson’s Summary

"Sales Operations puts Sales Team Performance on the Fast Track."

I work with chief sales officers who want to permanently improve sales efficiency and productivity, who want a smooth-running, high-performing selling team.

As an award-winning salesperson and sales manager, I know what it takes to excel at sales. I've spent decades amassing the tools, templates and experience that enables me to quickly build out sales organizations and remove the impediments that keep talented salespeople from selling all they should.

I've worked as a Sales Operations consultant since 1998, working with clients like ADP, Nortel Networks, Akzo Nobel, Elekta, Sunbelt Marketing, Chromate Industrial, and AutoNation. My work has been published in Selling Power Magazine, Sales and Marketing Management Magazine, American City Business Journals, and more.

You'll benefit from my expertise in these six areas:
- Measurement and Reporting - pipeline management, win/loss analytics, process monitoring and analysis, and more.
- Talent Management - selection, on-boarding, training, career development, ...
- Sales Strategies - competitive analysis, new product deployment, RFP conversion, ...
- Tools and Technology - CRM (Salesforce.com, others), buying process maps, proposal templates, ROI models, ...
- Rewards and Accountability - compensation and incentive plans, quota targets, policies, processes and procedures, ...
- Marketing - lead generation, testimonials and reference accounts, drip marketing, ...

In short, I make sure that nothing is holding your salespeople back, and that they are prepared to win. Call me at 770-271-7719, send me an invitation to connect, or visit my website at http://SalesOperations.US where you can e-mail me or download my resume.

Give your company the competitive advantage you need. Get on the Fast Track to win more business with Sales Operations.
