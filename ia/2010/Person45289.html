Curtis Krechevsky’s Summary

Attorney specializing in intellectual property law and related fields. Partner and Chair of Trademark & Copyright Department of full-service intellectual property law firm. Experience includes advising, counseling, and representing clients of all sizes – corporations, partnerships, small businesses, start-up ventures, sole proprietors, entrepreneurs, and other individuals – in all intellectual property and related matters except patent clearance and prosecution. Substantial national and international experience (20+ years) in all intellectual property matters. Travel for business to more than 20 countries on five continents. Frequent invited author and speaker on intellectual property and other legal profession topics. Prior positions include Assistant General Counsel for a Fortune 500 corporation, and Partner / Head of Trademark & Copyright Practice Group for a major Boston law firm’s Intellectual Property Department. Business-oriented, pragmatic, innovative, and cost-sensitive approach in advising and representing clients of all sizes and from diverse industries. Clients include individuals and start-up ventures as well as mid-size and Fortune 500 companies, operating in diverse industries such as motion picture and television entertainment, Internet and e-commerce, pharmaceuticals, electronics, computer software and hardware, high-technology measurement devices, footwear and clothing, musical instruments, publishing, lighting products, events planning and management services, financial services, consumer products.

Curtis Krechevsky’s Specialties:

Trademark, unfair competition, copyright, rights of publicity and privacy, Internet, advertising, marketing, promotions, licensing, and trade secrets law.
