George J. Fruehan, MBA’s Summary

After two decades experience at helping organizations increase performance, accountability and profit, I have distilled some of my experience into a simple web-based product, ProfitCentral that finds your lost profits in seconds!
I also train CPAs to immediately add value to their clients, and to distinguish themselves from the competition.

Significant experience in financial, transportation, educational, sales and service industries, including public sector and not-for-profit clients. Taught for CPA Society and at UC Berkeley. Testify in state and federal courts on the same issues as I consult to clients.

Clients range from smaller, closely-held corporations like Otis Spunkmeyer, to larger, public companies, including Sun Microsystems, Philips Semiconductor, Bank of America, and American Savings; Not-for-Profit clients include KQED and the UCSF Medical Center.

Expert testimony on economic damages and on technical issues.
Analysis resulting in depositions and testimony in federal and state courts. Issues include: lost profits; valuation of contract damages; projections and historic studies of revenue and costs; patent, trademark and copyright damages; software valuation; disputes over hardware and software performance; investigation for fraud; forensic accounting; evaluation of appropriate personnel compensation and calculation of lost wages.
