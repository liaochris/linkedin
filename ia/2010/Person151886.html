Nate Butler’s Summary

L Nathan (Nate) Butler is President of COMIX35, a comics ministry based on EXodus 35:30-35, and has been offering international coaching, consultation and seminars on the production and effective use of comics-style literature since 1996. Nate is one of the original founders of the organization and serves with a Board of four other Directors currently. He acts as the main instructor at all COMIX35 seminars.

Nate made his living for more than 20 years as a cartoonist, commercial artist, and writer/illustrator of comics before going into full-time ministry in 1999. His former company The Nate Butler Studio, Inc. produced artwork for clients such as Jim Henson Productions, Weekly Reader, Children's Television Workshop, Consumer's Union, DC Comics, and King Features Syndicate, as well as script writing for Modern Publishing, Archie and Marvel Comics.

Nate has worked on over a dozen internationally known characters such as The Muppets, Jughead, Popeye, Barbie, Heathcliff, Bugs Bunny, Tiny Toons, Mighty Mouse, Rocky & Bullwinkle, Snuffy Smith, Animated Batman, The Jetsons, Berenstain Bears, Tom & Jerry, etc.

He was also editor/co-publisher/packager of Christian Comics & Games Magazine, editor/publisher of Christian Comics Catalog, and he wrote and packaged the Aida-Zee Christian comics and magazines, the Truth For Youth comics, and the Brother Yun graphic novel. He created the Christian Comics International web site (the first site of its kind) which is now maintained as an additional ministry service of COMIX35.

Nate Butler’s Specialties:
