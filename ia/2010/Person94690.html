Jennifer A. Long’s Summary

I have been working as a search marketing professional for nearly seven years with companies both large and small, as well as an independent consultant. My experience includes PPC, SEO, social networking, banner design and implementation, basic HTML design, ad trafficking, marketing copy writing for print, web and SEO, press release copy writing, budget management, database reporting design, marketing and financial analysis and projections, link building and lead generation.

Some of the marketing verticals I have experience with are: lawyers and legal professional services; beauty and fashion; online education; campus education; real estate; information technology; web design services.

My education has focused primarily on writing and literature and other liberal arts. However, I have also studied engineering and architectural design as an undergraduate. Marketing has been my primary career focus, although writing is and always will be my first love.

Published work includes short fiction, essays and web content. I hope to publish my work as novels and nonfiction essay anthologies in the future.
