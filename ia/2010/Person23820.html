Bill Holmes’s Summary

My law practice at Stoel Rives is concentrated on energy law and transactions, with a special emphasis on wind, geothermal, biomass, tidal and ocean power, and other forms of renewable energy. I've been selected to be included in the 2007, 2008, 2009, and 2010 editions of The Best Lawyers in America in Energy Law and Environmental Law. I served as the Energy and Telecommunications (ENTEL) practice group leader at Stoel Rives from 2005 through 2009, and I am now chairing the firmwide Renewable Energy Initiative.

Bill Holmes’s Specialties:

The focus of my practice is on renewable energy law; wind, solar, geothermal and biomass power purchase agreements; wind energy EPC agreements and asset sale agreements (build transfer arrangements). (Technically, lawyers in Oregon and Minnesota don't get to claim "specialties.")
