Vincent Eggen’s Summary

I'm an experienced software development manager with pragmatic experience on financial software development and maintenance (Reporting, CRM).

- optimistic early adopter by nature
- quite visionary sometime
- quick learner
- good communication skills
- always looking at pragmatic rationales to embrace emerging technologies and practice, bringing them to live for the benefits of organizations and customers.

With experience on
- IT organization Lean industrialization through Agile practices adoption & business process improvement
- CMMi compliance and assessment
- Value Stream Analysis and Process Streamlining
- Development tools portfolio management and integration
- People management and coaching
- Offshoring
- Professional trainer
- Project and Program management
- any sort of Java related technology and tools

Other interests:
- Any sort of Project Management methodology, from PMI to Prince2 or CCPM
- Atlassian's products line (Jira, Bamboo,...). It's not perfect, but they have one of the best approximation of an agile ALM integrated toolset. And Confluence is a great knowledge management tool.
- Lean Six Sigma for Services - just at the beginning of my learning curve, but if anyone is ready to give me the opportunity to practice...
- A little bit of Cognitive Psychology. I'm convinced that the Agile paradigm success currently relies on the "embedded" recognition of the intrinsic true nature of our minds.
- Enterprise 2.0 - how to deal with the Y Generation expectations - the coming "Entreprise Facebook" (Bluehouse & Co).

I'm also an occasional speaker at the Valtech Agile days. See my last speech (Agile & Offshore - in French, slides are in english) on http://www.valtech-tv.com/

As there is no such thing as the absolute truth, I don't see myself as an evangelist. I rather belong to the generalizing-specialist kind of profile, happy to share thoughts and experience with any modern open-mind.

Contact me at veggen@mac.com
