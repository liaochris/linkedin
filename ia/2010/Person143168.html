Michael Motyka’s Summary

Winston Financial is a benefit’s advisory and technology solutions company which provide a suite of SaaS applications to mid and large market organizations which automate, streamline and reduce the cost associated with administering employee benefit programs. The suite of applications focus specifically on the automation of the open enrollment process across an organization, as well as the communication, education and decision support of benefit choices offered to the employee.
Winston's Integrated Benefits Solution (IBS) is a turn-key solution which can be implemented as a complete suite or deployed by individual components based on an organization’s existing HR/Benefits technology solutions and or processes. In addition to the employee self service and manager self service capabilities, IBS also has a proprietary billing and administration component which streamlines, automates and consolidates an organizations billing, auditing and reconciliation processes which significantly reduces the Benefits/Carrier administration requirements for an organization. This billing and administration component is integrated with over 35 payroll systems and has bi-directional integration with every major group and voluntary insurance carrier.

Michael Motyka’s Specialties:

Enterprise application solutions consulting and deployment. Benefit administration consulting and outsourcing. Voluntary benefit plan design and implementation.
