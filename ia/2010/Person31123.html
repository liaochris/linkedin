Byron Kennedy’s Summary

My passion is helping startups and established organizations evolve, bring new products to market, and enhance their impact. Those who know me best have described me as a natural leader, a reluctant artist, and a teacher at heart. These traits have presented themselves throughout most of my professional career by helping organizations excel through the use of Information Technologies.

A brief overview of my professional background:

* Proven technology leadership, operations management, and entrepreneurial background, equally comfortable in the boardroom and the data-center.

* Over 18 years experience in Information Technology, Consulting, and Operations Management - building and managing IT environments and cross-functional teams across North and South America, Australia, the United Kingdom, India and East Asia.

* Extensive experience bringing new products and services to market leveraging both public and private Cloud infrastructures.

* Deep technology consulting and management expertise across all IT functions including corporate, small business and E-business - Internet infrastructures.

* Proven track record in design, security, management of ongoing IT operations with uptime in excess of 99.99%.

* Proven track record managing IT spending to plan including multi-million dollar projects and operating line budgets.

* Experience with offshore management including IT, quality assurance and software development.

* Deep practical experience with business continuity and disaster recovery planning.

* MBA, University of San Francisco, graduating with honors - Beta Gamma Sigma and co-valedictorian of the Executive MBA cohort (GPA 3.98).

* Numerous industry certifications including VMware Certified Professional (VCP) | Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE) | Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) | Juniper Certified Internet Associate (JNCIA-FW).

Byron Kennedy’s Specialties:

Strong operations management experience including technology leadership, capital budgeting, project management, and vendor management.

Deep technical expertise in datacenter architecture, server virtualization, data management, security, networking, and highly-available, real-time transactional systems.

Broad virtualization consulting experience including business case justification, architecture and operational planning, capacity and performance analysis, hands-on consolidations.
