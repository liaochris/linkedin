David Otto’s Summary

I have been in the Massage Therapy industry for 8+ years, assisting over 6,000 bodies achieve balance and normal function. I believe that you have the power to heal yourself through utilizing the many modalities found in therapeutic massage designed for your body and on each day you receive your session. Please call or visit my website www.HandsInMotion.info for further information about massage, how I can assist you, and options for you to begin traveling your road to better health and wellness through massage therapy. Nationally-Certified and Licensed in the state of Nevada, unincorporated Clark County and Henderson, Nevada. Contact me today for a complimentary consultation about how therapeutic massage can help you be well. Gift Certificates and Rewards for referrals are available!
