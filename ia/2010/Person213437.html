Wagner Albuquerque de Almeida’s Summary

Innovative and seasoned finance professional, with experience in the Brazilian market, Latin America and on few American and European markets.
Formerly acting as CROs of a relevant international bank in Brazil, and currently as the Credit Strategy, Treasury and Barter & Commodity Lead in a Global agricultural / chemical company.
International experience, having worked for approximately 5 years in US (New York), The Netherlands (Amsterdam) and Germany (Frankfurt). Ability to work in multicultural environment and on complex or global companies with matrix reporting lines.
Proved management skills, leading large group of people.
Hold a diversified finance background, with focus on Risk Management particularly on Credit Risk. Experienced in most aspects and phases of the credit process (e.g. financial analysis including socio & environmental aspects, transaction structuring, approvals, and managing distressed credits). Member of several risk committee, and currently managing various functional finance activities such as budgets, forecasts, account receivables and required provisions for losses, cash flows and hedging among others, in additional to the risk approval process and recovery of non performing clients. Professor of Credit Risk Management – Corporate at the FGV Risk Management MBA (taught course in 2008).
Natural candidate to join Risk Management or Finance Areas of both financial institution and corporate entities.
