Don Monteith’s Summary

Our firm had 45 full-time employees and thousands of great temporaries annually serving a base of over 750 clients in our marketplace. Today, MonteithPartners works with a group of executives/professionals in network marketing through The CREATIVE Team. Our goal is to provide guidance in building your home based business through our MasterMind Group of talented and successful entrepreneurs.
