Anne Merkel, Ph.D.’s Summary

My passion is supporting others to live up to their full potential - in their lives and businesses!

From my own experience - healing from chronic fatigue, back injuries, and my own "dark night of the soul" - I truly believe that NOBODY needs to suffer!

I have successfully served individual and corporate clients in 80+ countries world-wide and all over the USA and Canada.

I served as both in-house and out-sourced Director of Training & Development for six large multi-national corporations, working with top CEO's, managers, teams, employees. I supported a chiropractic office as associate manager and coach. I published a weekly business newspaper column for five years, and I was an adjunct professor teaching for twenty colleges & universities in the US and Mexico, including offering multiple courses in four MBA programs.

Anne Merkel, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

As full-time Energy Psychologist, I facilitate Neuro Emotional Technique NET & Emotional Freedom Technique EFT among other tools to support people in conscious life or business transformation as well as those going through major change or trauma. I guide others to re-create their lives and to return from their "dark night of the soul". I also support my clients to understand the Law of Attraction in their lives and business endeavors so that they can attract what they really want.
