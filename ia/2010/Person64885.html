  • Student worker & full-time student

    Hutchinson Community College & Friends University

    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    August 2002 — July 2007 (5 years )

    I began Hutchinson Community College in August 2002. The following year I accepted a student worker position in the Fine Arts Library as I continued my career as a student. By May 2004, I had earned my Associate in Arts degree with Hutchinson Community College. I than began my Bachelor of Science in Organizational Management & Leadership degree with Friends University in May 2004. I continued working as a student worker. In August 2005 I completed my Bachelor's of Science degree and began attending the Marriage & Family Therapy program at Friends University. I began working as a student worker in Adult Student Services while working towards my Master's Degree. I began my internship with the Robert J. Dole VA Hospital in Wichita Clinic in April 2006. I then discontinued my part-time student worker position to focus on my internship and completing my Masters degree program. I completed my internship in May 2005 and I earned my Master's degree in Marriage & Family Therapy in June 2007. I then applied for my temporary LMFT license and opened my private practice in July 2007. In October 2007, I sat for and passed the National MFT licensure. I am on-course to apply for my Clinical licensure in July 2009.
