Geoffrey W. Holman’s Summary

Geoffrey Holman combines extensive engineering knowhow and experience with superlative consultative sales expertise garnered over two and a half decades. Thoroughly informed about IT, sales, and e-Commerce, he founded Coaction Group in 2001. The company is dedicated to helping businesses optimize their use of technology to improve customer service, increase productivity, streamline business procedures and reduce operating costs. Coaction Group’s consulting services include project management; Web and customized software development; sales automationn; and business recovery planning and implementation.

Geoff’s skills were honed over 29 years working for IBM, both as a Sr. Engineer and a Marketing Representative. Solid experience in manufacturing engineering enabled him to increase manufacturing yields & product quality and to design specifications that enhanced maintenance. IBM also offered a good foundation in selling to Fortune 500 companies. Experience as an Account Executive with J. D. Edwards increased Holman’s ability to find & develop solutions to clients’ strategic business needs. At Qwest, he focused on financial services, creating and supporting strategic business partnerships and alliances. In charge of a sizeable sales team, Holman exceeded revenue objectives, doubling revenue for Qwest’s new products and services. As Business Development Manager at Deloitte Consulting, he identified new clients and business opportunities in the tri-state area, increasing revenue. More recently, as Director of Sales at three successive companies—Genosys Technology Management, Net2Phone, and Covasoft—Holman has defined and disseminated a unified set of product offerings to create brand definition and industry-wide identity; built a sales team and devised successful sales strategies among Fortune 1000 companies and identified, marketed to and acquired new customers and business opportunities for software and e-business applications.
