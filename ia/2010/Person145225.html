Michael Davis’s Summary

My goal is to change the way the business of academia, research, healthcare, government and technology development is performed throughout the world, in a company with a strong academic culture that also understands business requirements.

I would love to be (and currently am) paid to develop, transition resources for, and implement strategic plans in academic, research, government and technology development institutions using sound economic, statistical, bibliometric, business (finance, operations, and human resources), and cultural analysis. Ideally, the strategic planning processes would include research program development, e-learning, faculty and staff development, key performance indicators (KPIs), benchmarking, and technology transfer. I also enjoy reading, writing, teaching, and discussing "learning institution"-based organizational behavior.

I currently reside and work in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates (next to Dubai). I am comfortable living, working and traveling in many environments. I have lived in the United States, Belgium, and People’s Republic of China (PRC). In addition, I have worked and traveled in over 25 countries, including the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Republic of China (Taiwan), and Oman.

Michael Davis’s Specialties:

Strategic planning processes, KPIs, policy development, economics, technology policy, bibliometrics, statistical analysis (SAS/SPSS), business operations, computer technologies (Cisco, Microsoft, Linux, Mac), financial analysis (Bloomberg, Excel modeling, valuations, statements), university-industry collaboration/technology transfer and intercultural competence
