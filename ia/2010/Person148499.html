Monica O'Brien’s Summary

Monica O’Brien is a Marketing Consultant specializing in marketing strategy and consumer insights at the juncture of new media and traditional marketing. She specializes in small to mid-sized companies with an online presence.

After she's done with her day job, Monica attends the Chicago Booth School of Business (at the University of Chicago), currently ranked the #1 MBA program in the country by Business Week, and is one of the 2007 Chicago Business Fellows. She concentrates in Marketing, Strategy, and Entrepreneurship.

Monica holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, with a minor in Physics, from Truman State University.

Monica O'Brien’s Specialties:

Monica has years of experience in business, marketing, and technology and operates best at the intersection of these three fields.

She specializes in online marketing, including blogging, SEM, SEO, social media marketing, email campaigns, and affiliate marketing.

Monica hates to get pigeonholed as a techical person, but the truth is she's an awesome web developer too. She has programming skills in VB.NET, Oracle SQLPlus, PHP, CSS, XHTML, MySQL, Wordpress, and Drupal, to name a few.
