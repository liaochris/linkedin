Chad Siwik’s Summary

Chad Siwik has always carried an interest in music, songwriting, and people. He studied guitar, voice, and piano. After high school he took classes in Art, Astronomy, Film, Literature, Photography, Psychology, Speech, Theater and Sociology at Palm Beach College.

Around this time, Chad managed a team of people for the Palm Beach location of Hollywood Costumes. He also contributed to aspects of Hollywood Costumes online store, including buying for it and handling large sales.

After graduating with his associates, Chad relocated to Orlando to attend Full Sail University.

After getting his second Associates in Recording Arts, he earned a Bachelors Degree in Music Business.

Chad has acquired functional understanding of countless creative tools and processes across many mediums.

Throughout his educational career, Chad has spent his spare time passionately pursuing the absorption of as much art and live performance as possible of other artists from Salvador Dalí to Dolly Parton.

Helping others has always been a focus of Chad’s. Whether it’s donating his time via Full Sail’s Pure Motivation monthly missions, or volunteering to perform at nursing homes or charity events, he has made giving a consistent part of his life.

Currently Chad is working on an album of original music to be released by 2011.

Chad's primary areas of interest and expertise are Album Design, Sound Mixing, Music Production, Music Video and Film Editing, Image Development and Conceptual Design.
