Cassandra Pierce’s Summary

Who We Are:
Catalyst Search is quickly becoming one of the fastest growing national IT recruiting and consulting companies for the healthcare industry. Established in 1999, Catalyst Search is an organization built on quality and integrity.

Catalyst addresses the challenges our clients face by providing a unique, collaborative approach that ensures contract, contract to hire, and direct hire resource needs are met. By focusing exclusively in the healthcare vertical, Catalyst Search has differentiated itself from general consulting organizations by delivering specialized offerings that specifically address the needs of healthcare payer and provider cleints.

What We Do:
Catalyst Search provides IT and business staffing services to many of the country's top payer and provider organizations. Additionally, Catalyst Search serves as a valuable resource to several of the industry's top healthcare IT vendors, enabling a "cross-pollination" of product and technology skills that benefit the entire industry. Our expertise in both payer and provider systems and associated business processes has enabled Catalyst Search to become a leading supplier of contract, contract-to-hire and direct hire resources for the healthcare vertical.
