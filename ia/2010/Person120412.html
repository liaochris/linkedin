Kyle Griffith’s Summary

Kyle Griffith is a Senior Business Intermediary with New York Business Brokerage, Inc. Kyle is an enthusiastic, highly motivated professional with over 10 years of diverse experience in various business operations. His background includes providing technical education & training programs to entrepreneurs, operation of several retail businesses, as well as managing operations for a family owned printing and shipping franchise. He has a solid background in sales and marketing and is highly skilled at designing and implementing successful social media marketing strategies for business owners to effectively brand and market their businesses online.

Kyle is recognized as a “master connector” as demonstrated by his ability to quickly develop professional relationships with business owners of small to mid-sized privately held companies.
