Tara Hustedde’s Summary

Tara Hustedde has 15 years of experience in strategic public relations, fundraising, marketing, branding, media relations, special events, and field marketing. She has worked in a variety of industries including restaurant, hospitality, healthcare, entertainment, lifestyle, retail, telecommunications, development and construction.

Before launching her own consulting agency in 2007, Tara worked for several national restaurant groups. Most recently she was employed by Outback Steakhouse Inc., as the National Manager of Public Relations and Marketing for Bonefish Grill. As company spokesperson she developed relationships with national and local media outlets, created and managed a national public relations plan and budget, created and implemented national promotions and special events, developed and supervised partnerships with non-profit organizations and coordinated all collateral material development.

Prior to this position,Tara worked to rapidly advanced brand awareness in new and existing markets while with Chipotle Mexican Grill and Checkers/Rallys, Inc. This success was a direct result of her ability to capture an evasive customer base through development of comprehensive marketing training for individual units; creation and implementation of customer education programs; implementation of aggressive community relations and fundraising efforts and development of highly effective marketing teams.

Tara has also worked with clients such as Tiffany & Co., Pfizer, Rubin Development, Baylor Heart Hospital, The Ford Amphitheatre at the Florida State Fairgrounds, Blue Bell Ice Cream, Epix Holdings Corporation, Maggiano’s Little Italy, The Palm Restaurant, Robb & Stucky, Tractor Supply Co. and more. These clients relied on her expertise to develop and implement business positioning; local and national public relations and overall image development.
