Nancy Richardson’s Summary

Nancy's passion is focused on industry engagement and the dynamics of the Healthcare industry. She engages with several industry committees and associations focused on key industry initiatives including Health IT and Healthcare Reform, working to identify opportunties in business process improvement that impact patient care and meet objectives through new legislation in Healthcare.
