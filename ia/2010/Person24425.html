Chris Li’s Summary

Chris is a senior BI architect with in-depth expertise in all subject areas concerning a BI solution including, but not limited to, business knowledge, BI technology, data conversion, data warehousing, and package software applcations, which includes both Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP).

Chris holds an MBA and a Master's degree in Computer Science. He is a CFA candidate Level I passed.

As an expert in Enterprise Data Warehousing, Chris has articulated sophisticated and satisfactory solutions that dealt with multi-tera byte data volume and met extremely complicated business logic and reporting requirements.

Chris has kernel level knowledge in Teradata, Oracle, DB2, and SQL Server.

Chris has mastery knowledge in complete functionalities of Oracle BI EE, aka, Siebel Analytics, platforms and applications, including backend (DW data model, ETL, and DAC), middle tier ( Metadata), and front end (Answers, Dashboards, and Requests); published various best practice white papers on those topics.

Chris has intensive knowledge in Siebel CRM applications and Oracle 11i e-business Suite applications, data model, and infrastructure.

Chris has in-depth knowledge in manufacturing, financial services, retail, high tech, and pharmaceutical industries.

Chris is a Siebel Certified Master Analytics Consultant, Oracle Certified 10g DBA OCP, Teradata Certified V2R5 Professional, IBM DB2 UDB Certified Solution Expert, and Siebel Certification CRM Consultant.

Chris Li’s Specialties:

Enterprise Data Warehousing, Real-time Business Intelligence, Data Modeling (OLAP and OLTP), Performance Tuning (Oracle, DB2, Teradata, and SQL Server), Project Management, Sales and Marketing applications, Order Management, Financial and Supply Chain Management applications, standalone and integrated Siebel Analytics Solutions (ETL and Metadata)
