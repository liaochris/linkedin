Vaishali Gadgil’s Summary

Vaishali started with a computer engineering degree, a discipline that provided the core competence in presenting an idea in a structured and logical manner. In her 14 years of subsequent professional experience, she has worked in different capacities and domains as a developer, project manager, system designer, marketing executive, product evangelist and finally is an entrepreneur and CEO.
In this serial transition, she had to cope with the origination and development of an idea while ensuring that notions of simplicity and familiarity do not filter out the critical ideas for development. Her main preoccupation is to find the simple idea from the whole lot and design a suitable execution strategy for the same for your company, merchandise, service or any marketable commodity. New and interesting Ideas are easy to come by but a simple and executable idea is very rare!
