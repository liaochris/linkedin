Jim Murray’s Summary

Senior executive with 25 years of experience in industrial & powerhouse operations, engineering, project management, and business development focused on energy markets and technologies (both supply side and demand side), energy efficiency and practical energy management.

• Knowledge of the energy markets and power generation technologies
• Ability to assess energy usage and develop energy efficient alternatives and programs
• Experience managing industrial processes, power plants, waste water treatment plants
• Ability to mentor and guide junior staff regarding business development and project management
• Brings a project developer’s approach to energy management, creates value
• Capable of envisioning, designing, contracting and executing complex energy saving projects:
- Cogeneration (CHP) generation projects; gas & diesel engines, gas and steam turbines
- Boilers and steam systems, steam distribution, economizers and low-NOx burners
- Chillers and CW systems, cooling towers, DX systems, HVAC, AHUs, controls, EMCS
- Compressed air systems, dryers, controls, distribution, storage and end-use efficiency
- Lighting systems, controls, VSDs, motor controls, power factor correction
- Sub-metering and telemetry, power monitoring, web-based energy management
- Renewable energy projects; solar, wind, biomass, biogas and landfill gas

Jim Murray’s Specialties:
