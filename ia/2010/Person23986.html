Bill Pirtle’s Summary

I combined my ten years of drug store and big box retail cashier training and store management experience with six years of Merchant Processing experience to create a detailed guide to help start up business owners and small retailers understand the basics, the pricing structures available and potential risks of credit card processing.

The book will also include bonus chapter by business experts in about 25 different fields to help make dream businesses thriving realities
