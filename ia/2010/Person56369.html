Diane Crompton’s Summary

I’m a Social Media Strategist / Enthusiast and a Career Coach skilled in providing a broad range of social media solutions for companies and individuals interested in building their brand through leveraging social media. Usage of social media is treated taking into consideration overall business objectives and practices in a customized, thoughtful approach.

My interest in technology tools contributed to a recent co-authoring of a book “Seven Days to Online Networking.” This handbook provides “how to” tips on using social media with a special focus on LinkedIn, to advance your career and business. This expertise is shared through frequent speaking to professional and networking groups, organizations and as a contributor to media.

The consulting services I provide are tailored to your professional needs and can be delivered in a one-on-one workshops or large group setting and via live or web based instruction. I create and deliver webinars for geographically dispersed audiences focused on leveraging social media.

Areas of expertise include:
- Reinforcing / Protecting Corporate Branding
- Promoting Corporate Services, Products
- Advising on Creation of Social Media Policies
- Lead Generation / Prospecting
- Business Development
- Cultivating Contacts, Networking
- Developing Powerful Professional Online Profiles
- Blending Various Forms of Social Media
- Showcasing Subject Matter Expertise and Personal Branding
- Ongoing Social Media Strategies
- Professional Development
- Career Transition and Job Search

My background is diverse and includes experience as a nationwide recruiter (retained and contingency), an educator, and an independent consultant. Most recent experience includes more than nine years as a Senior Career Consultant providing career transition services to clients and personnel from all organizational levels and a wide range of functional and industry backgrounds, including Fortune 500 firms in Atlanta and nationally.
