Brad Davis’s Summary

Brad serves as a senior communications advisor to the Chairman of the Board of Standard Renewable Energy, and to the Past President of the U.S. Conference of Mayors.

He has over 25 years experience building high-growth brands and award-winning communication strategies. Named one of the Top 50 Marketers in America and inducted into the Retail Marketing Hall of Fame in Chicago, he served as Chairman of the Board of the Retail Advertising & Marketing Association from 2004-2006.

In 2007, Brad was invited to London to produce a series of events on building new markets and reaching communities with the power of customer empathy. The events were attended by the world's leading brands in London, Madrid, Toronto, Hong Kong, Sydney and Johannesburg, South Africa (visit: www.jbraddavis.com).

He produced a ground-breaking market launch behind New York's schools—uniting business, social and community leaders—and buying out every seat on Broadway to celebrate New York's teachers, receiving an Effie from the American Marketing Association, New York. The former Chairman of 20th Century Fox commended his teams for messaging which resonates with the community.

This year, he keynoted the Retail Advertising & Marketing Conference in Las Vegas and the Retail Green Conference in Los Angeles. He was among 100 business leaders invited to join the Dalai Lama at the 2009 Vancouver Peace Summit's 'Connecting for Change.'

He served on the executive committee of the Seattle Art Museum during a $200 million dollar transformation which included cleaning up a toxic open-space to create a sculpture park on Seattle's waterfront Time magazine called “One of the 10 Best New and Upcoming Architectural Marvels.”
Brad lives in Monte Sereno, California.
