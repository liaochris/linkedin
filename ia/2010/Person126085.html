Lisa Jeffries’s Summary

Where I Came From:
I spent my undergraduate years at North Carolina State University in Raleigh obtaining two bachelors degrees: one from the College of Textiles (Textile & Apparel Technology & Management with a concentration in Brand Management and Marketing) and the second from the College of Management (Business Management with a concentration in Marketing).

While at NCSU, I spent a good deal of time learning as much as I could about brand management and marketing, advertising, public relations, corporate identity, professional communication, strategy, and so on. What I learned outside the classroom about social media, has become a major component of the marketing strategy of many businesses (small and large alike) and I'm now using my understanding of both, to help business owners maximize their online presence while using new media to connect with existing and potential customers in ways that are constantly changing each day.

What I Do:
On a daily basis, I spend a lot of my professional time with my clients putting together marketing plans and managing online formats including websites, social media, and email marketing. My company also provides traditional advertising, marketing and public relations offerings including print advertising, graphic design, press release development and distribution, and SO much more. There really isn't anything I won't do for my awesome clients. If they can dream it, I can make it happen!

Where I'm Going:
The sky's the limit! I'm having a lot of fun and still learning so much each day about how to connect businesses and professionals with the world around them. New this year has been the launch of http://www.iheartretail.com - with other information marketing publications coming soon!

Lisa Jeffries’s Specialties:

Brand management, traditional marketing and advertising, email marketing, social media, advertising placement, communications, branding and identity, blogging, event management, event marketing, web publishing, networking, public relations, event planning, and more!
