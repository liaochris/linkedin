Ryan Berman’s Summary

Fishtank is a full-service idea generation, advertising and brand design agency that injects life and personality into our partners work. Some of these partners include Ringling Brothers and Barnum & Bailey, Nike, Von Dutch, UNICEF, Bumble Bee Foods, Unitrin Direct, Earl Scheib, Cymer and Pony.

Ryan, Founder and Chief Creative Officer of Fishtank, is an award-winning idea generator with 12+ years' experience in brand strategy, advertising/design and creative business solutions. Big brand experience includes Aetna, Amgen, Cymer, Evian, MCI, Nike, Pony, Schering Plough, Subway, UNICEF and Volvo. Ryan develops and/or oversees all of the creative that comes out of Fishtank. Latest work includes naming and advertising the 2010-2011 Ringling Brothers circus, ‘Barnum’s FUNundrum’ and the incubation of Bumble Bee Foods’ Beewellforlife.com 2010 wellness program.

Prior to starting Fishtank, Ryan spent seven years at Euro RSCG in New York. Ryan helped bring Jared Fogel to millions of television sets all across America, Canada and Australia. This campaign holds up as the most successful quick-service-restaurant campaign in fast food history—with annual sales jumping over 25% nationwide. Ryan also created work for such clients such as Evian, MCI, Schering-Plough Pharmaceuticals and Volvo. If you know these campaigns it would seem Ryan had a fixation with the letter ‘J.’ He has written campaigns for Jared Fogel (Subway), John Stamos (MCI), John Madden (Tinactin) and Jamie Pressley (MCI). He swears this was never on purpose.

While at Fishtank, Ryan founded gohangten.com (2007)—a website that ‘hangs’ ten, mind-at-rest marketing trends/initiatives, found in the marketplace every week. HangTen continues to be released every Friday with the intention of pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the world of nontraditional marketing.
