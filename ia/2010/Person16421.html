Arman Glodjo’s Summary

I am interested in projects in finance and e-commerce that are founded upon, and advance the principles of the theory of ‘Rational Expectations.’ Of particular interest are those projects where the 'dominant paradigm' is at odds to the dictates of modern financial theory, or is inefficient due to slow adaptation of technology that is able to help.

I invest my time to explore and build upon ideas that represent the limit-point of the sequence of evolution of business models in that particular space. In other words ... to forge an ideal business model as dictated by sound financial theory and commercialise the model to hopefully change things for the better, forever, or at least for a very long time.

3. A Mathematician and Scientist.
Research keywords: Kolmogorov complexity of fully reflexive systems, general theory of rational expectations, generalised autoregressive conditional hazard models, generalised-hyperbolic and Levy distributions, multifractals, flows in breathing networks, network-decentralised dealing systems, decentralised single-period auctions, optimal proximity measures, equitable liability and true pricing
