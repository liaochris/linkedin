Anthony Cirillo, FACHE, ABC’s Summary

Anthony's firm Fast Forward Consulting empowers organizations to change the health care experience and leverage it in their marketing. He has assembled a diverse and dynamic team that works with health organizations to change not only the patient and resident experience but also the experience of families, staff and others involved in the health care continuum.

Anthony is an international marketing expert with a varied background to include start ups, Fortune 500, and health care. He strategically and tactically works with clients to move them forward quickly. He is also an NSA member and professional speaker and author. See www.4wardfast.com and www.anthonyssong.com.

Anthony is considered a thought leader in the healthcare arena, a frequent contributor to Health Leaders, Hospital Impact and a Fellow of the American College of Healthcare Executives. His article The Chief Experience Officer became the basis upon which the Cleveland Clinic started an office of patient experience and hired the first ever hospital chief experience officer.

Anthony is a a member of The Society of Industry Leaders of Vista Research, a division of Standard & Poors; a member of the Guidepoint Global Advisors; and a members of the Gerson Lehrman Group Councils.

Anthony has a mission side of his life devoted to older adults. A local advocate, he spends hundreds of hours with seniors listening and learning while using his performance talents to brighten their day.

His book Who Moved My Dentures? has positioned him as an expert on boomer and senior issues. Anthony has spoken at the national conferences of AHCA and AAHSA and has keynoted many state associations, agencies on aging, and caregiver meetings. He is a guest blogger for Advance in Long Term Care, Long Term Living, Hospital Impact, McKnights.

Anthony can not only help organizations reach the boomer and senior population through his expertise but also act as a conduit in reaching them through his versatile talents.

Anthony Cirillo, FACHE, ABC’s Specialties:

Patient experience solutions, long term care resident experience solutions, marketing plans, marketing audits, crisis communication, national and international healthcare marketing, identifying and cultivating influencers, public and media relations, community and government relations, motivational keynotes, in-services, self-branding and promotion, baby boomer marketing, medical tourism.
