Melissa Antonelli’s Summary

Melissa Antonelli is a San Francisco based copy-writer and blogger who blends her background in journalism and marketing to create compelling, well-researched content. As a freelance writer, she delivers SEO copywriting, social media best practices, expert articles, blogs and more for businesses from a variety of industries, from wine importing/exporting to Internet technology. She has also worked as a Marketing Communications Specialist for TrialPay, a Silicon Valley-based e-commerce company, where she wrote and edited press releases, Web copy and other marketing collateral for the company and its partners, which include McAfee, Lavasoft, and Match.com. She continues to write the company’s blog and monthly newsletter. Prior to working at TrialPay, Melissa held several editorial positions in print and digital media in New York City. Melissa holds an M.A. in Journalism from NYU.
