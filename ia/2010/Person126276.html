Lisa Olinda’s Summary

Lisa Olinda has talent in administrative work, added with a love for technology. This talent and love merged to created Olinda Services in 1996. With over 26 years of experience serving the corporate, non-profit and small business sectors, she has proved the success of this merger.

Lisa started secretarial work in 1988 and worked her way up to Administrative Assistant in a Fortune 500 systems department.

In 1993 she retired from the corporate world and began volunteering for a missionary friend who needed a secretary. She was able to assist him for several years with general admin work and setting up a yearly major event based in Philadelphia dealing with sports and thousands of teenagers.

In 1996 she continued by bartering services with consignment shops setting up business practices and paperwork. Missionaries internationally have benefited from her skills with computers and graphic design.

The primary focus of Olinda Services now is assisting businesses and non-profit organizations with their important administrative needs. These can include anything from daily administrative needs, blog development and maintenance, social media profiles and updates, or desktop publishing.

Lisa has extensive experience and background in computers, both Windows and Mac, dealing with many administrative areas any small business may have.

Today’s technology is expanding and advancing swiftly, and businesses need to keep up. Social media is exploding, providing businesses with newer, more streamlined way to construct professional relationships. Lisa makes the time to track and understand these evolving technologies and apply them to her own business.

Olinda Services is committed to keeping you on the path to success!

Lisa Olinda’s Specialties:

Professional Business Services including but not limited to administrative services, blog design and maintenance, social media setup and maintenance, desktop publishing services, Excel, PowerPoint, word processing, database management and software services.
