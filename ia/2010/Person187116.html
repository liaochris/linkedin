Laura Boyajian’s Summary

Originally from Chicago, Illinois, and prior to becoming an Arizona Real Estate Agent, Laura Boyajian, a.k.a. Laura B., moved to Phoenix, AZ in 1983 and settled in her first investment property in Arcadia which she still owns today.

She became a News Reporter and Radio Disc Jockey throughout the country. After obtaining a BA from Arizona State University in Broadcast Journalism from the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism & Telecommunications, her on-air career originated in Phoenix, AZ, leading her to Columbus, Ohio, Atlanta, Georgia & back to Phoenix where she has been investing in real estate & lives in North Encanto Historic District.

As a morning drive award-winning News Reporter, Laura B. covered daily leading stories heavy in national politics, investigative reporting, national & historic criminal trials, Olympic games & much more. Her stories have been aired around the globe on CBS and ABC World News. Many of her written stories were also published on world newswires such as AP and Reuters.

Also a die-hard Chicago Cubs fan, Laura B. has had the privilege of having years of mentoring by Hall-of-Fame Announcers such as Jack Brickhouse, Lou Boudreau, Milo Hamilton, Harry Caray and Vince Lloyd and has had the honor of two personal one-on-one meetings and newscasts with Walter Cronkite early in her career.

Being extremely well-connected, well networked and well-known in the Valley, Laura Boyajian is one of the most prominent Real Estate Agents in Phoenix. She specializes in downtown Historic Phoenix Single-Family Homes, Lofts, Condominiums, Foreclosures, Short Sales, New Builds and Multi-Family properties. She also works in Scottsdale, Paradise Valley and surrounding suburbs.

Laura B. is also a Certified Life Coach with Bob Proctor and LifeSuccess Productions, Inc. She is available for small to large group speaking and is available for a variety of topics. If you would like her to speak to your group, just call her directly at 602.400.0008.
