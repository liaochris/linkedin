Jeff Gampel’s Summary

Over the past 25 years Jeff’s career in executive search has included roles as VP, Managing Director and Senior Business Development Executive. Jeff is a recognized search consultant/trainer who has successfully completed assignments in a number of business sectors including manufacturing, e-commerce, software, high tech, consumer products and retail services. He has recruited for well known companies such as EMC, Revlon, Melissa & Doug, Inc.,Taubman, Warnaco, Execunet, BJ's, Sym's, Carters, Philip Morris, as well as numerous manufacturing facilities. His searches have included the functional areas of sales/marketing, operations management, engineering, finance, general management and human resources. Jeff is married with two grown children and holds an undergraduate degree in Business Administration from the University of New Haven.
