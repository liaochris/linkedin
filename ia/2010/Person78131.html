Greg Ray’s Summary

I've been working in start-ups my entire life, some I've founded, some others have founded. Start-ups force you to be on your toes and you have to know how to roll with the punches. I have a particular interest in the mobile space, I started developing my first iPhone application before Apple had even announced the SDK. I've worked at the most successful iPhone company, Tapulous - makers of Tap Tap Revenge, and was privileged to be there when Disney acquired the company. I've also been spending all my waking time working on a cross platform gaming engine soon to be released by the Seek Mobile Interactive team (http://www.seekmobileinteractive.com).
