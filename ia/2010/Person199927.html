Tariq Ashraf’s Summary

I am a British citizen; I do hold a MSc in Management from EM Lyon Business School, as well as a MSc in Finance and Accounting (Equivalent to Chartered Accountant).

I have worked at Arthur Andersen, serving the Financial Services industry on Corporate Finance assignments (Due diligence, Mergers and Acquisitions).

I have a 6 year-long Consulting experience: Consultant at PwC Consulting (Financial Services industry) and Senior Consultant at Accenture (Technology, Media & Telecommunications Industries).

Now part of Peat Marwick's TMT Practice, I am in charge of Technology and Market Research (Issue Papers, Business Cases...)

As a Senior Consultant, my focus is on Marketing, Customer Operations, Process Optimization and Finance Transformation.

I have conducted several Consulting assignments in European countries (France, UK, Belgium, Italy, Switzerland), North America (USA and Canada), North Africa (Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia and Egypt) as well as in India.


I am an EM Lyon Business School 2003 MSc in Management intake Delegate, and do participate in EM Lyon's student application and selection process (MSc in Management as well as Business Consulting Specialized Master Entry Exams Jury member)


I take pride in being active as far as the overall EM Lyon Business School network is concerned, via Alumni promotion and networking initiatives.

I did create both private and public EM Lyon LinkedIn Alumni groups: indeed I did establish the public group as the official EM Lyon Business School Alumni group on LinkedIn, and am now working as a Networking Advisor for the Alumni Association.

Last but not least, I am a Volunteer at the Lance Armstrong Foundation.
