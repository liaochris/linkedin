Dusty Melling’s Summary

I am Dusty Melling and I am a fine artist and illustrator in Medicine Hat, Alberta. Illustration is a great part of my day job and I do fine art commissions and projects in my spare time. Portraiture is the most common subject for me, I mostly work with realism and try to incorporate unique color and bold marks into my work.

Dusty Melling’s Specialties:

Oil painting, watercolor, portraiture, graphic design, website design, illustration, graphic arts, still life, vector art
