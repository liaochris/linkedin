Jessica Eaves Mathews’s Summary

Jessica Eaves Mathews is passionate about helping entrepreneurs launch wealth-building businesses infused with authenticity & passion.

Jessica is a seasoned business lawyer, advisor and coach for business owners and entrepreneurs. For the last fifteen years, she has practiced law as a business litigator and business advisor. Her practice has included assisting start-ups and small businesses and partnerships in structuring and launching their businesses, drafting and negotiating contracts, and acting as an advocate in partnership and corporate disputes. She is also a multi-passionate entrepreneur herself, having launched a number of successful ventures of her own throughout the past 15 years.

Her Business Brilliance™ brand is dedicated to empowering women with the tools they need to create a life of personal and financial brilliance through entrepreneurship.

Through her dynamic coaching programs, Business Brilliance Mastermind™, SHEngle Mastermind™, home study programs, live events and ezines, Jessica guides women entrepreneurs and women lawyers through the emotional and practical steps toward a well-founded business that is based in authenticity, passion and that can provide personal and financial wealth.

For more information on her business and lawyer coaching programs, Jessica can be found at:
