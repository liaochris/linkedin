Matteo Fabiano’s Summary

Matteo Fabiano is an international executive with 12 years of management experience in Online Media, Video e Web Technology.

As COO at Your Truman Show, Inc., Matteo managed product marketing, planning and operations. Under his leadership the company launched three online video products in 15 months, including TagLift, the innovative technology for video metadata validation and ad matching.

Since 2005, Matteo has been holding leadership roles at software and internet startups, with increasing levels of responsibility. As Head of Marketing and Business Development for P2P collaboration software maker Collanos, Matteo led the company’s US market entry, achieving double-digit monthly growth rates. Previously at Centrata, a KPCB portfolio company, he managed software development and delivery teams for clients such as British Petroleum and DHL.

Based in Europe from 1998 to 2005, Matteo held international service management roles at Hewlett-Packard, Procter & Gamble and IBM. While at HP, he turned around the multi-million dollar business serving P&G global web infrastructure, ending a 3-year loss-making record. At P&G, he launched the company’s eCommerce operations team in Europe, covering 40 countries. Prior to that, as Project Manager in IBM Global Services, Matteo managed a portfolio of software deployment, infrastructure and re-engineering projects in the Southern European region. Before being recruited by IBM, Matteo was platoon commander and training officer in the Italian Air Force.

Matteo is president of the board of BAIA, Business Association Italy America and makes time to volunteer his management skills to the Taproot Foundation, “the non-profit marketing agency”.

Matteo has lived and worked in Italy, the Netherlands, Belgium, Switzerland and the USA and holds a Laurea degree (MS) in Engineering from Milan Politecnico and an MBA from IMD.
