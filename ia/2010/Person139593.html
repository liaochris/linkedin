Mark Payne’s Summary

Architecture, design and development of enterprise labor-intensive financial management package for the Aerospace and Defense industry. It must meet government DCAA and GAAP commercial audits on labor as well as indirect burden pools. I designed and wrote much of the JAMIS Financial Software system such as Job Cost, Billing as well as revenue recognition and many other features. I started with the company shortly after its inception at S-Cubed Business Systems in 1985. My role has grown to CTO as well as Architect for all the companies existing products especially its newest web based e-products such as e-timecard using C# .NET and Web Services as well as N-Tier architecture. The products are 2 Tier and N-Tier Client/Server and Web/Internet Technologies. I registered the jamis.com domain name years before JAMIS set up their web site insuring the name would be available to the company when it was ready. I worked directly with JAMIS small, medium and large enterprise customers to guide them in thier setup, configuration, and security and scalability challenges. With Sassan Chakamian then president of Maxwell Business Systems as well as Portera Government systems I Architected, designed, and built our JAMIS Data warehouse which is extremely popular with our customers and prospective clients. I also directly manage the JAMIS Data Warehouse, e-timecard Telephony application reporting directly to Naomi May and Jeff Noolas owners.
