Arun V’s Summary

Worked for 2.5 years in industry and 6.5 years in academia on Ultra Wide Band (UWB) RF circuit design and modeling specializing in antennas, filters and oscillator design.

I've been an RF engineer responsible for the development of a variety of innovative ultra-wideband (UWB) antennas, burst oscillators and microstrip filters. I've published work in the Journal of Geophysical Research, and IEEE Journal on Antenna and Propagation. I co-authored a discovery paper on Whistler mode waves in the plasmasphere. I have 1 pending patent and a dozen conference papers to my credit. I earned my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Alaska at Fairbanks working on an innovative antenna based encryption scheme.

Arun V’s Specialties:

RF design and building specializing on oscillators and antennas, EM modeling of RF circuits, parallel scientific programming.
