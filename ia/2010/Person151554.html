Nick Schmitz’s Summary

EXPERIENCE
Over the last 5 years or so I’ve meant some very nice people and companies that agreed to pay me money in return for my ideas and services. These people and companies are, but not limited to:

3rd Edge Communications, starting as a Junior Designer, this is where I began learning the ropes of agency life and over a three year span I worked my way up to Senior Designer, where I was responsible for many things including the conception and execution of design solutions to their completion. They even trusted me enough to mentor and art direct the junior designers that were on staff... Awesome!
I also have gathered a collection of freelance gigs over the years where I learned many things about being a designer both from the creative end and the business end. Some of my past clients have been LucasArts where I had the joy of working projects for two of my childhood obsessions; StarWars and Indiana Jones; Jeff Fassnacht Design where I worked on projects for Actual Films, O’Reilly Publishing and Breakthrough Collaborative, amongst others. Some of my independent clients include Highlife Music, RGH Lighting, Scheimpflug Digital and Element Tattoo.

When I'm not working on client work, I’m usually working on the development of 00one, the very site that you at! I hope you are liking it. This is a site where I showcase my portfolio as well as sell my personal creative work which include prints and paintings.

Nick Schmitz’s Specialties:

I specialize in designing and art directing projects related to print, branding and interactive design.
