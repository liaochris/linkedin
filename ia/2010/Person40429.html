Cicely Sweed’s Summary

As Director and Founder of ÀŞĘstudio (Art_Social_Enterprise_Studio), an independent curatorial practice that also functions as a creative social enterprise/community action think tank, I curate, market, and produce socially engaging exhibitions, events, fundraisers, and happenings. With a strong commitment to creative communities, interdisciplinary arts education, and entrepreneurial growth, ÀŞĘstudio's mission is to encourage broad and significant community engagement among a network of creative visionaries that will mobilize the philanthropic growth & support of economically and ecologically sustainable cultures & communities.
