Pam Hanbery’s Summary

Pam has an extensive accounting background across a wide range of industries with entities at all stages of growth. She has successfully set up systems and procedures for accounting, operations, inventory control, cash management and job costing. She has successfully developed budgets and fine-tuned accounting procedures generating top notch financial management. Pam has managed the general accounting function for small and emerging business for more than 15 years and is proficient at all stages of the process. Pam maintains strong relationships with prior employers and colleagues and thus enjoys a wealth of knowledgeable, respected and qualified resources from which to draw information and craft solutions.

Pam has an in-depth understanding of a variety of industry standard accounting software such as QuickBooks, MYOB and Sage Business Works.

Future plans include a CPA and Masters in Accounting and Non-profit Management from Regis University.
