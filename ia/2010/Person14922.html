Anthony Mansour’s Summary

Anthony has built a career on defining strategic direction and leading innovation for emerging industries.

He spent over a decade in video games and the ‘dotcom era’ establishing the industries both in Australia and overseas for companies such as Sega, Nintendo and Spike Networks.

Anthony is a powerful asset when it comes to proven skills in mobile strategy and innovation. A perfectionist with strong Telco and non-Telco experience, he comprehends the big picture.

Anthony’s intelligence, integrity and ambition deliver a unique perspective and understanding of the mobile space, driving innovation through the development of sound commercial opportunities
