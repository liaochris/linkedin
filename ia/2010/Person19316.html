Catherine Azzarello’s Summary

My company serves clients who require custom design solutions for their web & print needs. This client base knows that DYI options are insufficient/unprofessional, and large agencies can be overkill/intimidating. They seek quality, value, and guidance through the maze of options and technology at their disposal. Working directly with business owners, Azzcat Design delivers beautiful, well-crafted websites and print materials--on time and on budget.

Past work experience includes: senior designer at medium-sized agency (Chicago, IL); in-house graphic designer at international corporate headquarters (Lincolnshire, IL); production artist in small studio (Chicago, IL).

Volunteer commitments include: newsletter editor/designer; art literacy instruction; illustration; print and web design/production for local sports & school organizations.
