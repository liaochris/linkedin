Christopher Brown, MIRM, CSP’s Summary

* Distressed Assets - Bulk REO and Note sales. We currently represent both buyers and sellers of Bulk REO tapes, Performing and non-performing Notes. We are direct to several sellers and single intermediary to many others able to do location specific carve outs as well as larger nationwide tapes. Our specialty is the difficult California Tape market.

* Bank owned (REO) liquidation specialist. Currently representing two national banks and several private investor groups, facilitating their retail exit strategy of distressed assets. This service includes due diligence, asset management, brokerage services, and through our channel partner, rehab and property management. We also represent buyers looking to invest in this opportunistic market.

* Fifteen years of executive sales and marketing experience in the home building and development industry. Experienced in creating and running sales teams and marketing programs for a variety of product types. These range from traditional suburban homes in all price ranges to urban infill SFD and mid-rise mixed use building.

* Sales trainer for new home sales teams. I create custom programs specifically designed to enhance and motivate sales teams throughout the western US.
