Ian Rossi’s Summary

Ian Rossi has over a decade of experience conducting and managing business on the Internet and has used his visionary skills to assist startups in entering the Internet business arena.

His consultancy has benefited clients in many verticals including financial services, national retail, manufacturing and software development.

He is the founder of aimtheory CRM, a SaaS product that helps customers spend less of their money on business software and focus on customization for their business model and processes.

Ian's specific areas of expertise are open source strategy, open source software identification and selection, business process identification, modeling and improvement.

Ian founded aimtheory CRM in 2007 after identifying a major problem faced by small-to-medium sized businesses: After paying for expensive CRM software and tools, there's no money left for real help implementing your business model.

Ian identified open-source software as a way to help companies spend more of their hard-earned money on their own business model than on software. The end result is a SaaS CRM called aimtheory CRM, which is based on the open source CRM solution, vtiger CRM. Priced at only $29/$49/$99 per month with no user limit, companies can spend their time and money on a real implementation of their business model, rather than on software alone.

aimtheory was able to create an optimized SaaS platform, called CELERASOL, to reduce the performance overhead and keep our hosting costs low. aimtheory passes that savings on to the customer with very low margin on the aimtheory CRM service itself.

The real value that aimtheory brings to the table is know-how. They offer strong business process-related services and then implement a company's business processes into aimtheory CRM and integrate with other necessary business systems.
