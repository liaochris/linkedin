James Hayhurst’s Summary

Over 26 years experience specializing in business development as well as sales & marketing within the natural gas utility and propane gas industry. Energy Conservation Program development manager. Pioneered Gas Advantage Dealer program network in Central Florida by building relationships with and training over 50 contractors for Florida's largest natural gas utility. Engaged in natural & propane gas Green Energy initiative movement in the states of Florida and Georgia.

I currently specialize in uncovering new business opportunities for my clients through a consultative approach. Provide solutions and recommendations related to natural & propane gas projects as well as value engineering services to speed profitability. I am also pursuing compressed and liquid natural gas (CNG) and (LNG) technologies on the sales & marketing side to help reduce the carbon foot print within our country thus reducing our countries dependency of petroleum based fuels.
