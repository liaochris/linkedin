Jeffrey Alderman’s Summary

Seasoned in web/digital strategy, search engine marketing, online marketing advertising and eCommerce executive. Over the past 10 years Jeff has specialized in paid search marketing (SEM/PPC), natural/organic search marketing (SEO), affiliate program marketing and business development for retail clients. Always looking to build rich and deep relationships with strategic partners and explore new opportunities. ROI direct response focused.
