Taylor Snyder’s Summary

I'm an Art Director working and developing my abilities in Interactive Design at Rockfish Interactive (named Best Small Agency of the Year by Ad Age).

I have internship experience at Campbell Mithun and MRM Worldwide. Along with in-class experience at Zeus Jones and Martin Williams. I'm a graduate from the Advertising Design program at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design under the tutorship of Nancy Rice (co-founder of Fallon Worldwide). I am also sustainable design certified.
