Sean Leckey’s Summary

An analytical and creative professional whose skills range from the financial and economic to the engineering aspects of power markets. Experienced with working directly with traders in a high pressure environment. Experienced guiding technically oriented projects through a merchant generation company and through regulators.

Current responsibilities include:

- Analyzing fundamentals (fuel price, weather, generation, transmission, load and imports) and using production cost modeling and power flow software (Dayzer and Powerworld) to develop views on power prices on a locational/nodal basis.

- Analyzing proposed or actual changes in bulk power system conditions and presenting my view on the resulting transmission congestion.

- Investigating congestion-related events on the bulk power system and offering appropriate congestion hedging strategies

- Monitoring proposed and actual changes to regional bulk power systems and market rules with potential impacts on power markets.

Sean Leckey’s Specialties:

My primary focus is on MISO, PJM and ERCOT.
