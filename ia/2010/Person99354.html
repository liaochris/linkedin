Jeremy Lightstone’s Summary

Online advertising and the technologies behind the medium are my primary business focus and professional motivation. I have worked in the online advertising space since the time when people felt it was a 'Passed Fad', but I was stubborn and so much enjoyed the code behind the technology and ultimately telling and selling people on it. I just kept with the industry as people’s perception of the internet changed. It certainly did change!
I have enjoyed working with a mix of international ad agencies and media providers in America, Canada and England to help advertisers learn the ‘net’ and leverage the technology to micro target their web audiences. I have had the opportunity to work on many projects for Fortune 500 brands, local brands and also for charitable organizations.

On a personal and creative note, I have always been interested in the visual arts, producing more than a few arts events. These events have been intimate affairs or even events with attendance over 500 people, including exhibitions and performances by national film organizations and international musicians. Ever since the internet started, I took a different interest in creative thinking and have found that computer programming was the next creative step for me. I have been self taught since the mid 90´s and later managed teams to build various enterprise apps. Most have been tools like ad servers, search engines and auto-content builders, and various advertising data research tools. I am fluent in PHP, CFM and reasonable with JAVA. I have worked extensively with MYSQL, MS SQL and POSTGRESQL. I am fairly capable at many elements of Linux server admin and application compilation. I really like to problem solve and develop the rich layers of logic based code for web apps.

Even though I am a competent coder and interested in computer geek stuff, I do so to be a better team leader and ultimately a great seller to a client’s digital advertising and technology aspiration!

Jeremy Lightstone’s Specialties:

Online media buying and sales negotiation. Ad server development and coding. PHP and Java coding, HTML and Javascript, MySQL, MS SQL Server, ad server campaign management and optimization, fluent in French, basic-moderate Spanish, preliminary Japanese, artistic painting.
