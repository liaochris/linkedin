Tish Robinson’s Summary

NOW:
Corporate blogging/social media marketing -- working remotely in Texas. Just completed Webinar on increasing social media marketing ROI. Currently completing Social Media Marketing certification program.

--Travel blog since 2006: http://travelswithtish.com for Midwest Airlines (featured in book by IBM's Sandy's Carter "The New Language of Marketing 2.0 -- How to Use Angels to Energize Your Market." Also received SCNR Award for this blog for innovative use of a corporate blog.
-- Consumer photo blog for http://lifephoto.com. Launched August 2009. http://simplephotogifts.com.

BACKGROUND:
Advertising, public relations, corporate (employee) communications, community relations and social media marketing. Experienced in developing, creating and managing corporate blogs for niche markets. Currently manage three blog sites and a Web site. Have created several corporate employee magazines for Master Lock Company, including a 4/c, 20 page business-focused employee magazine and a 2/c, tabloid newsletter. E-newsletters are a specialty. At Midwest Airlines, I also wrote the Midwest Miles monthly newsletter (both print and e-mail versions), weekly customer sales e-mails, and internal mid-management newsletters.

As Community Relations Manager at Master Lock Company, I revitalized a sagging volunteer effort for this inner city neighborhood. Worked closely with employees to understand their interests; found volunteer/contribution opportunities that met both employee and company objectives. Included collaborating with Milwaukee city departments, neighborhood organizations and schools to coordinate their efforts in home rehab, neighborhood safety, job opportunities and other avenues of improvement. Also generated significant increases in employee participation in annual United Way campaign even though most employees knew their jobs would be transferred out of the country in the coming year. Today these activities are viewed as social innovation.

Tish Robinson’s Specialties:
