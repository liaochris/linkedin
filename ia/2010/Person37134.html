I’m a “pulling guard” with a Harvard MBA. My background includes 15 years in finance. During that time I was exposed to numerous industries as an Investment Banker and gained hands-on experience as the CFO of an early-stage digital media company. I’ve also started and run my own company.

When I played football at Dartmouth College, my responsibility was to understand our game plan and my job was to call the shots on the line and then execute my responsibility. As we came to the line, I had to analyze the opposition we faced and call out the blocking scheme for the play. Then I'd lead the way for our backs and deal with any challenges that arose along the way.

My approach to business is the same - to provide results focused leadership in a dynamic environment, keeping an eye on both the big picture and the bottom line. I'm equally comfortable in the Boardroom and the trenches and work best in an environment in which I can build and lead teams.

Intellectually curious, I am a quick study. My education and diverse experience help me rapidly break down complex situations, identify opportunities to enhance the competitive position and potential of organizations, and exploit opportunities to create value.

I have a strong desire to utilize my talents and leverage my background as part of a team committed to to help create organizations of lasting value that have a strong positive impact on the lives of their shareholders, employees and our community as a whole.

I believe firmly that businesses have responsibilities that extend beyond their balance sheets, but also understand the maxim “without a margin, there is no message”.
