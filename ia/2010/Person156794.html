Carlton Brown, Caterer’s Summary

Founded in 1994 by Carlton A. Brown, Occasional Occasions by Carlton has been serving clients, corporate and private, throughout the Atlanta Metropolitan area.

Providing intimate Private Dinner for two, up to large gala events. Box luncheons, Corporate Breakfast, Corporate Luncheons, Corporate Snacks, Cocktail Parties, Cook-Outs, Dessert Parties, Weddings, Receptions, Family Re-Unions.

Corporate and Private Menu Planning | Event Planning | Professional Wait Staff | Floral Design |
Beverage Service and Equipment Rental | Photography

Off premise catering for corporate and private clients. Specialize in Refined Southern Cuisine, with a contemporary presentation. Also provide personal Chef services.
