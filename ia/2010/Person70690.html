Rick Matthews’s Summary

We apply a proven mass-market fractional ownership business model to various commodities in regional markets. We network with good people or businesses that desire to be a part of an innovation whose time has come. Our clientele are good people who wish to own or operate a special asset they have always wanted but cannot or will not justify the cost of sole ownership. Such assets include:
Private, Sport, or Business Aircraft
Recreational Boats / Yachts
RVs, Classic Cars, Hotrods, & Sports Cars
Beach Cottages / Mountain Cabins
Horses / Equestrian Facilities
Shared Businesses such as Restaurants

Am also keenly interested in discussions with sharp people with integrity regarding the business itself as a career or investment.
