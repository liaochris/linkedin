Tina Wilson’s Summary

As a IT Site Manager, I work with senior leadership to meet the business goals of the organization with IT solutions. Current responsibilities include project portfolio management for IT initiatives, IT Manager for WellStar Enterprise Support, and individual project management. I participate in strategic project planning, research and budgeting. Prior to my current position, I worked as a Manager and Project Lead for McKesson, assigned to WellStar. I have over 20 years experience in healthcare IT. Credentials include MBA, certified PMP, ITIL V2 and V3 Foundations certification and Certified Professional Healthcare Information Systems (CPHIMS).

My goals are to continue to develop business process analysis skills and project management skills. I currently serve as the InSight (McKesson User Group) Council Chair Person for Resource Management (Enhancements) for 2009 -2010 and serve on the Alumni Board of Governors for Reinhardt College.
