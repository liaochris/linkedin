Todd Ablowitz’s Summary

Todd Ablowitz is a recognized and respected industry leader with nearly 15 years of experience working for the acquiring and payments industry's top brands. As Founder and President of Double Diamond Group LLC, Ablowitz leverages his unique skills, expertise and industry relationships to ensure clients achieve their business goals.

Ablowitz has developed strategy based on trends and market demands, created and launched new products, and built and led successful sales organizations. Investors, analysts and industry watchers frequently rely on Ablowitz for expert advice, trend insights and consulting on payments and payments innovations. He has a vast network of diverse contacts that spans the industry in every major region of the world.
