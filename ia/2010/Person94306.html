Jen Kramer’s Summary

Expert at developing the front end of websites with Joomla, a PHP/MySQL open-source content management system. (X)HTML, CSS, usability, information architecture, content organization, and website critiques also offered. Lynda.com author: "Joomla! Creating and Editing Custom Templates", "Joomla! Advanced CSS", "Website Strategy and Planning", and "Preparing CMS Web Graphics Using Open Source Tools". Author, "Joomla!
