Terry Wells’s Summary

At Henning software as a helpdesk analyst I learned their database program called Esti-track, designed for manufacturers to operate their entire facility. I supported customers by phone and usually had to go remotely into their computer and network to fix the data base or train them on the use of our software. Because many of our customers where all over the United States I had numerous calls that came just at my quitting time. I never left a customer hanging. At least three days a week I worked over to handle a call. Once I worked with a customer until 12:45AM on a Friday night past my quitting time. I was salary at Henning. I am passionate about meeting customer needs.

On the weekends I teach high school kids how to drive. You could say I have a lot of patience especially in February when my student took out a telephone pole.

Most of my experiences have been in the aerospace industry doing acceptance testing and calibration on test and measurement equipment. The rest of my experience has been in the manufacturing industry. As a quality assurance technician I acquired a strong background in ISO 9000 and TS 9000 certifications.

My goal is to work in the Information Technology field. I am currently taking classes for IT at Wayne College.
