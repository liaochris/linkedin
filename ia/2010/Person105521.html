Joseph Vito’s Summary

I’m a thought provoking visionary with over two decades of effectual leadership in the areas transforming business through technology in leadership roles traversing global centers of excellence in technology, information architecture, customer operations, product development and financial re-engineering.

Most recently, I’ve been leveraging my expertise in enterprise architecture and commercial/consumer content to collaborate with partners who want to help companies look beyond the hype of social media and context aware processing, to realize the full potential of social business and ubiquitous computing to create relevant business models in a post-desktop/website digital marketplace.

My success is rooted in my ability to think strategically, implement tactically, and execute against multi-phased road maps that drive innovation.
