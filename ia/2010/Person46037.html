Dale Tate’s Summary

Following a 5 year detour to experiment in entrepreneurship, Dale has returned to his successful career in commercial real estate property management. He now serves as General Manager for Seagate Properties at the San Rafael Corporate Center, a class-A 320,000 sq. ft. office campus in Marin County, California.

Dale Tate’s Specialties:

Commercial real estate operations management, leasing, engineering, security, construction and maintenance administration, personnel, budget creation and management, tenant relations.
