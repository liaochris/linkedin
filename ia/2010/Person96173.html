Jeremy Sluyters’s Summary

I am a Professional Engineer, and a certified Project Management Professional. I have a passion for business process improvement and collaboration, leading to a greater awareness and realization of efficiencies and employee engagement.

One of my missions is to increase the awareness of social media as a business tool. Most people don’t take the time to recognize how fast the world is changing, and how people are collaborating on the web, how people are using social networking to build real business relationships.

Social Media is not a fad. It is a fundamental shift in the way we communicate and do business, both internally as knowledge workers, and externally as marketers and service providers.
