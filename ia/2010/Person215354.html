Will Gandy’s Summary

I have worked in the financial services industry since 1999, first as State and Local Tax Specialist with KPMG and currently as a Residential and Commercial Real Estate Finance Specialist.

My specialties include providing financing for commercial and residential real estate purchase and refinance transactions.

My experience allows me to quickly process client information in order to align specific transactions and property types with the optimal loan programs offered by my lenders.

I am also proficient at providing no fee summary credit repair advice.

Will Gandy’s Specialties:
