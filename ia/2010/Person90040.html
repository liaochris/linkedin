Jason Keller’s Summary

Jason Keller is the Marketing/PR intern at Greenlights for Nonprofit Success, where he assists in writing, publishing, and following up on public announcements and external communications. Jason joined Greenlights in 2010 after a year of volunteering and interning with different nonprofits in Central Texas.

Prior to joining Greenlights, Jason was the senior communications intern for the U.S. Green Building Council’s Central Texas – Balcones Chapter. There he worked with the newly hired executive director to implement a new communications plan, evaluate and research the organization’s technology, and manage the chapter’s website and external communications.

Jason’s expertise ranges from communication technology and web development to social media and internal communications. His is a leader of two different student organizations and regularly assists the Central Texas - Balcones Chapter of the U.S. Green Building Council as a technology volunteer.

Jason is an avid billiard player, playing in collegiate and amateur tournaments across the state and even in some national events. He also enjoys promoting sustainability and is currently starting a new student organization at the University of Texas devoted to green building and sustainability. His favorite color is orange.

Jason is a graduating senior at the University of Texas pursuing a B.S. Degree in Communication Studies with a concentration in Corporate Communications.
