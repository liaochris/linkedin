Martin Sansone’s Summary

Martin Sansone (born 15 February 1969) is a creative entrepreneur, CEO of UltraCom Inc. and Asylum Creative with stake holder positions in five other companies.

Also known as TheDon Sansone for his input on the Internet, forum moderation and Search Engine Specialist work for numerous clients. Snr Sansone was born in England and has four younger sisters. With 18 years through-the-line marketing experience and corporately trained in Blue Chip organisations he has also spent 7 years of his career at an advertising agency down at Tower Bridge London. He had a hand in the initial recruitment drive of Capital One Credit when they came to Britain and also was key in promoting BlockBuster Video's launch into the UK.

He began Asylum Creative back in 2000 and continues to attract both B2C and B2B clients with a professional service offering from Consultancy through to providing most aspects of the Marketing Mix.

Notable SEO Specialist - highly recommended from a wide client source for legitimately raising the profile of commercial websites in competitive markets. Raising them to the top 5 positions on Google SERP's , Martin is very aware of the convergence in Media and what that means to corporates in the 21st Century. Technically trained in 6x programming languages he is an excellent project leader and actively involved in multi format broadcasting/production.

Latest client to SEO services is the Rentin Group and now www.rentin-marbella.com watch them rise over the coming months for search phrases such as: Apartments Marbella; Accommodation Marbella; Villas Marbella; Renting in Marbella. The Nerja region has been achieved.
