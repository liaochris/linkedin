Pam English’s Summary

My focus is to introduce local and national businesses, membership organizations and universities to the Home Advantage "Cash Back" Affinity Program. If you are interested in adding value to your employee/member benefits package without any additional expenses, this is the program for you! Implementation is easy and I will provide you with all the materials necessary to present this information to potential participants.

As a licensed REALTOR, I understand buyer and seller needs and will work diligently on your behalf to earn your trust and continued referrals.

I received a call from a client who asked me to assist a friend of theirs with the purchase of a new home. After asking a few key questions, I learned that this friend worked for EVMS, one of our Affinity Partners. Once they learned of the CASH BACK opportunity, they were thrilled! I connected the buyer with a William E. Wood real estate expert to assist in their home search. Since they are also first time home buyers, they will qualify for the $8,000 tax credit. They are SO EXCITED! This is just one of the many success stories we get to be a part of, which is very rewarding!
