"Glasnost combines Contact Management, Project Management and Image Management.

Glasnost Contact Management is great for sales teams, or just keeping track of all the people you work with. It helps you deal with companies, contacts, tasks, documents and comments. It also allows you to create and distribute quality email newsletters.

With Glasnost's online project management module operations teams have an intuitive, flexible and productive platform for getting things done. Online document management and project collaboration becomes simple. Task management and task reporting is effortless.

Glasnost's Image management allows you to create and share any number of galleries, link them to other resources (projects, companies), tag and search them, as well as create lightboxes and slideshows.

Glasnost stands for openness, transparency and deep productive collaboration.

Wherever you are, all you need to know is : ‘It’s on Glasnost!’
