Jason Brownewell’s Summary

For more than 15 years, I've helped companies leverage user experience to improve their business and build deeper relationships with their customers. Along the way I've helped launch new websites, products, software applications, and internal systems, as well grow new internal practices and develop core competencies in the area of user experience and interactive design.

Prior to joining Aquent, I founded and managed several successful design practices. As Director of User Experience at Sears Holdings, I built and led a large, multi-discipline user experience group responsible for one of the industries largest online retail businesses.

Before joining Sears, I freelanced as user experience consultant leading engagements for several high profile clients including: McDonalds, BankOne, Harley Davidson, Dell, The Gap, Motorola, Walgreen’s, and UnitedHealth Group.

I've also served as a consultant and practice manager for several internet consulting firms, including: USWeb/CKS, marchFirst, and divine Interventures.

I have worked on web sites, enterprise applications, new product development, and ecommerce with businesses across a range of industries including financial services, insurance, consumer products, real estate, and health care.

Jason Brownewell’s Specialties:

User experience design, information architecture, interaction design, usability testing, user research, visual design, design management, product management, e-commerce, staffing and recruiting.
