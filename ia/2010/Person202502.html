Tom Hesterman’s Summary

BS Aeronautical Engineering, Ohio State summa cum laude. MS Engineering Mechanics, Univ Missouri Rolla. 11 years structural engineering at McDonnell Douglas. Director of Online Operations for startup CDM Fantasy Sports, a pioneer in operating nationwide fantasy sports contests. President of Primary Network, an ISP honored as one of the fastest growing tech companies in the country from 1994 to 1999. Became VP of Customer Operations for publicly-held Mpower Communications when Primary was purchased by Mpower in 2000 and remained in that role until Mpower was sold to privately-held TelePacific Communications in August 2006. Joined River City Internet Group as VP Strategic Initiatives in October 2006. In April 2007, moved to VP Operations and Engineering for Progession LLC dba GameRail, a spinoff from the RCIG incubator focused on improving network performance across public and private networks through unique proprietary technology.

Tom Hesterman’s Specialties:

Operational management with technical depth, P&L responsibility, startup environments as well as large corporate settings, Internet and telephone service provisioning, field service techs, customer service centers, network operations, software development, backoffice systems and development, product development.
