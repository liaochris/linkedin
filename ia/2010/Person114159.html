Kelsey Worrell’s Summary

PinPoint Solutions is a recruiting firm with over 40 years of combined recruiting experience on staff. We offer a variety of recruiting services on professional and management level openings. PinPoint Solutions, based in Greenville SC, is a division of Westport Intl, a retained search firm based in Cleveland, OH. PinPoint Solutions is a client driven organization focusing on providing customized solutions to a select number of key clients based on their strategic staffing goals. Our combination of experience in the third party recruiting industry combined with our experiences leading internal staffing functions for global Fortune 500 organizations gives us a unique perspective and customer orientation typically not found in the recruiting industry.
