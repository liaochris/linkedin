Hervé Kabla’s Summary

In 2008, I co-founded blogAngels with Deborah Elalouf. blogAngels mission is to assist french companies in their "Corporate Blogging" and "Social Media" strategies.

Corporate Blogging and Social Media are the new trendy way to develop a useful web 2.0 approach in a corporate website, in order to increase audience, start 2-way discussions with customers/users/partners, and develop a positive image of your company on the Internet.

Is your company already using a Corporate Blog? If the answer is "no", you should check :

www.blogAngels.net

In 2009, I co-founded Media Aces with Yann Gourvennec.
Media Aces is the first European community of Enterprise Social Media experts, with members from large and small companies in France and UK. If you're looking for a fast moving social media experts association, with a focus on business and enterprise, you should join us at:

www.media-aces.org
