Denyse H. Turner’s Summary

DENYSE H. TURNER is an active travel professional. She specializes in Spa and Hawai’i vacations, group cruises and fundraising for non-profit organizations and churches.

Additionally, Rx 4 Fun Travel offers honeymoon registries for couples contemplating marriage. You can browse through our sample honeymoon registries for suggestions on all the wonderful items your friends and family can give you as a wedding gift. Couples design their own custom wedding registry from ideas we’ve showcased or include their personalized honeymoon wishes. Create a stunning honeymoon registry and wedding website FREE ~ you’ll be on your way to the honeymoon of your dreams.

Entering the travel industry through an MLM, she has personally mentored and trained business partners how to market their travel businesses without cold-calling leads or approaching family or friends, but by using the product of travel. Although she is no longer wholly involved in MLM, she is still willing to share with you how to use the internet to grow your travel business across Canada, Bermuda, Puerto Rico and the Bahamas using travel products. She has since transitioned to align herself with a traditional host.

Seeking to maximize her knowledge in the travel industry, she is currently working toward an Accredited Cruise Counsellor (ACC) certificate and a Certified Travel Associate (CTA). She also holds Cancun, Disney, Hawai’i, Spa and Las Vegas Destination Specialist Certificates and a Recommend Destination Specialist. She is also a Princess Cruises Commodore, a Freestyle Advanced Specialist and a Non Profit Funding Specialist.
