Bill Taney’s Summary

An experienced manager of engineering groups supporting high performance multi-protocol global networks. With over 15 years experience in network architecture, operations and engineering, I have had extensive hands on experience Cisco, Juniper, Netscreen, F5 and Linux, Solaris and OS X systems. Currently I manage a global network organization for the premier trading & EMS system provider. My responsibility includes all of the data, voice & video connectivity worldwide including a large high-performance trading system. This network processes and disseminates market data from all of the worldwide markets as well as process real-time trades back to market at all of the worlds major exchanges 24x6. I have had 10 years of financial industry experience as well as experience in the time critical printing, publishing and creative industries in both the media and retail sector.

Bill Taney’s Specialties:

Network Engineering, Operations & Design in mission critical real-time environments. Managing complex network, voice and video projects and exceeding expectations under challenging deadlines and budgets.
