Chuck Jines’s Summary

After more than 20 years in the roofing industry, I am happy to retire my hammer and shovel. This year I launched Cyber Smart, a website development and management service.

I now have multiple web clients in the Chicago area and I am excited about expanding. I have six years blogging experience, and well as SEO and WorPress expertise. As well as serving as SEO webmaster for businesses, I also currently own 22 websites covering a verity of topics, products, and services.

I offer many services for business websites, from general consultation, to total webmaster services.

Back in late 2003 I began blogging about politics and political philosophy. At the time I had no idea that political blogging would turn into a new career as a small business web developer and marketer.

The fist business website that I built was for my own roof repair business here in Chicago. Then I built a website for my wife's counseling business. From there things just grew, and I now have 22 of my own websites, and also provide webmaster services for other businesses.

I have many interests including politics, philosophy, history, theology, music, and most recently taking photographs of Chicago.

As well as being a working man, I am also self-educated and have had a life-long passion for reading and learning. I grew up in a political family, so to me politics and philosophy are a part of life just like eating and sleeping.
