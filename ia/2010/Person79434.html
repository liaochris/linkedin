Gerard Finnerty’s Summary

My professional focus is on Digital Advertising and Web Development; more specifically dealing with Media Aggregation, User Profiling and Rich Internet Application Development.

I was an Internal Communications Specialist in the U.S. Navy under the SeAL Challenge Program. I was honorably discharged due to injury from the Naval Special Warfare Center, where I was attending, Basic Underwater Demolition / SeAL training in class 237. I immediately returned to my pursuits in Web development, analytics and theory, which led to achieving a Bachelor of Science and Digital Media from Drexel University. I am a member of ACM SIGGRAPH and 3-year contributor to their International Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques. Since my military service, I have pursued and filled different professional roles in various new media productions such as programmer, designer, copy editor, and project manager. The underlying motivation for this has been my fascination with all the interconnected disciplines necessary to achieve a viable solution, application or otherwise successful creative expression; though my overarching fascination is with the whole human dynamic.
