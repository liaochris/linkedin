Heather Vitaglione’s Summary

A versatile wordsmith with over 20 years of international experience in editing, translating, researching, marketing, teaching and assisting individuals or teams. Open to students of any age or background.


A Few Highlights:
*Tailored lessons to students' needs and interests. Set up a topic request system for students to request a topic a week in advance. Did science and creative projects while enhancing the English and always taking it up a notch. Aimed for native level fluency at all times.

*Created task-specific English courses for several European companies in Monaco for individuals or groups of employees. English courses were a highly requested benefit offered under the employee training programs.

*Worked with a Monaco R&D team and Project Managers on technical manuals, project presentations and database maintenance for multilingual applications. Online help documentation in English was one of the major selling points for the software products in a competitive market.

*Assisted French-speaking teams working on cosmetics and sun care products through the process by teaching English to laboratory assistants, coaching executives on presentations of research on product lines such as Healing Garden, Manifesto, and anti-age products at Coty Lancaster, Monaco.

*Streamlined the housing process for entering students and visiting faculty at an international business school in Monaco by creating uniform applications, contracts, roommate matching forms and coordinating this with housing providers in the area around Monaco. This was a deciding factor in enrollment for over 75% of entering students.

*A passionate problem-solver and enthusiastic trouble-shooter who enjoys team work and who respects the efforts of each team member.
