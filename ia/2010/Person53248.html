Dean Prelazzi’s Education

  • University of Ottawa / Université d'Ottawa

    MBA , Specialized in Strategy , 1996 — 1997

    Thesis-style Graduate Research Project: An examination of the sales and marketing cycle associated with positioning complex 'big ticket' technology solutions, namely the criteria and mechanics of complex decision making processes and the variability of buying criteria used by decision makers from different levels in an organization who are participating in the evaluation of technology solutions.

    The results indicate that a one-size-fits-all marketing message is destined to be ineffective and that an effective sales and marketing strategy for high end tech solutions should include several value propositions each aimed at specific audiences who may be involved in product evaluation. This theory was tested at CrossKeys Systems Corporation and proved effective.
