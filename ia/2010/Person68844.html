Lee Fainman’s Summary

I currently work at Fainman Law Office as a Lawyer. After graduating from the University of Wisconsin at Madison, I worked as a pharmaceutical Representative for Johnson and Johnson. I then moved to Los Angeles (L.A.) and pursued acting and music. I hosted a local TV show there called "Madline." I had a great opportunity to really learn how the entertainment industry works, being an extra and featured extra on numerous TV and Movie sets, including 90210, the movie "Rock Star" and many other popular TV shows and Movies. After about 2 years, I moved back to my hometown of Chicago, worked as a sales rep. for ChrisAd, a marketing specialty group, then worked as a music agent for 21st Century Arts and Entertainment and Bradley Young, for about 2 and a half years. It was this job which interested me in becoming an attorney, with the hopes of someday focusing on Entertainment Law, with an emphasis on music. I have been a professional musician for over 10 years, perform with the Lee Fainman Quartet, and perform with the Chicago Bar Association's "Scales of Justice" which is a Jazz combo. composed of professional musicians who are also Lawyers.

Lee Fainman’s Specialties:

General Law Practitioner, includes a wide range of cases, Credit repair and issues with Identity theft, fraud, collection activity, Rights of the disabled under the ADA, former Fair Housing Clinic Intern, former Extern at the Office of the Attorney General, State of Illinois, Entertainment Law, Music Law, Copyright, Trademark, and Intellectual Property Law (IP).
