M.J. Wivell’s Summary

MJ founded BTI in 2004 with the idea that building teams focused around collaboration was the missing element for the U.S. Intelligence Community's technology arm to achieve its mission. MJ's vision for BTI is to build an organization of collaborative, software-minded teams that work with our clients to see long-term results, not just short term wins.

BTI believes that collaboration is not just achieved on the human level, but also on a system’s level. MJ is passionate about leading BTI as a thought leader in applying Web Services to transform disconnected applications into collaborative systems.

As the CEO, MJ maintains the strategic vision of the corporation and oversees business operations. MJ also provides guidance to BTI clients on the implementation of Web Service.

MJ holds a B.S. in Computer Science from Lynchburg College and a M.S. in Informations Systems from George Mason University. MJ is a Sun Certified Java Programmer and Certified ScrumMaster.

M.J. Wivell’s Specialties:

BTI believes that collaboration is not just achieved on the human level, but also on a systems level. To that end, BTI’s has a deep technical understanding in architectural design, implementation, and deployments of Web Services. Leveraging Web Services, BTI has set out to turn systems from silos2services - our way of transforming disconnected applications into collaborative systems.
