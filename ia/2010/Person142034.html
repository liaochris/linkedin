Michael Banner’s Summary

I have been a mortgage broker/banker in Florida for the past 27 years. Self employed for the majority of my career I founded the largest for sale by owner mortgage company in the nation in1995. Operated Buy Owner Mortgage as it's President until 2002.

From 2002-2005 I was the National Sales Manager of Real Estate Mortgage Network. A New Jersey based mortgage banking company I operated out of Florida building a national branch network.

Left REMN late in 2005 and formed LoanWell specializing in the emerging reverse mortgage market.

Michael Banner’s Specialties:

An accomplished public speaker and accredited to teach continued education classes, on reverse mortgages, to certified financial planners, elder care attorneys, CPA's and insurance agents. Featured in the Wall Street Journal in May of 2008, Tampa Bay Business Journal, a monthly contributor to The Reverse Review & many major reverse mortgage internet sites. .
