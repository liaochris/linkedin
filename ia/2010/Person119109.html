Kristi Jones’s Summary

Kristi is a Senior-Level Recruitment Specialist and HR Consultant who appreciates the importance and significance of the employment function in supporting strategic and operational needs of an organization. She is experienced in sourcing, selection, internal staffing, coaching and counseling. Kristi is self-directed, resourceful and results-driven with excellent business and decision-making skills.

Kristi is currently working with Ryan Estis & Associates a Speaking and Consulting practice focused on helping companies more effectively engage with their two most important audiences, employees & customers. Our seminars blend interaction, energy and actionable content designed to elevate business outcomes. The message is a compelling crusade on corporate culture, communication, client acquisition, brand ambassadorship, change and preparing to thrive in the ultra competitive, hyper connected business environment we now know as the new normal. Our guiding principles include:

Passion: Driving higher levels of employee and customer engagement to enhance business performance.

Purpose: Helping guide individual contributors, leaders and organizations on this journey.

Premise: Put people first...profits follow.

Promise: Delivering experiences that people never forget.

We provide HR/Talent Acquisition Consulting, Employer Brand Strategy, Communications and Recruiter Effectiveness Training..

Our Sales Effectiveness launch includes Sales Training and Consulting solutions to drive business performance.

In a knowledge economy where the best talent wins, communication and competency are critical to ensuring you have the right people, in the right seats doing the right things to deliver on the business strategy. Our mission is to help your vision become a reality.
