Stephen Lairmore’s Experience

  • President

    Private Equity Consultant

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    January 1995 — Present (15 years 8 months)

    Stephen is a seasoned, thoughtful, decisive entrepreneur and operating corporate finance executive with over 30 years experience in the investment securities industry. He has spent the past 15 years in the private equity and venture capital consulting business. As an accomplished private equity investment professional, he has advised on over 45 private equity transactions. Lairmore has extensive relationships with strategic venture capital and private equity firms in the United States. He has advised on transactions ranging from $1 to $55 million spanning early to late stage equity, debt and M&A.

    Stephen believes that businesses have the ability to make significant contributions to people, society and the environment. His philosophy combines his passion for business and investing with an altruistic vision to impact the world for good. He is a hands-on, goal-oriented executive with diversified experience in marketing, business development, strategic planning, turnarounds, finance and operations. He is an innovative business strategist, highly motivated, detail-oriented, concise, and well organized.

    Prior to acting as a private equity consultant, Lairmore successfully founded, capitalized, operated and sold two securities brokerage firms with over 100 employees - Newport Asset Management and Opex International. Mr. Lairmore made regular appearances on FNN (Financial News Network), seen in over 40 million homes, as an analyst and forecaster in the late 80's. Mr. Lairmore holds a BS from the MERU Research University of Lucerne, Switzerland. He resides in California and France with his wife, a French national, and two sons.
