Kaliya Hamlin’s Summary

I have extensive experience designing and facilitating unconferences for professional communities. I enjoy bringing together people around topical issues that make a difference in the world and supporting a deeper dialogue and more productive work then is possible at traditional conferences.

My clients include an emerging programming language (Scala), emerging standards for the social/identity layer of the web (Internet Identity Workshop), regional technology organization (Mass Technology Leadership Council), and world wide design nonprofit (One Club for Art and Copy).
