Brian Beyst, MBA’s Summary

Currently working as a Principal of AcQuity Investments, overseeing all accounting and finance management, single family residential property acquisitions and sales, and back office services provided by the company.

AcQuity is an active, principle buyer / seller of single-family, multi-family, commercial, industrial, and undeveloped real estate. We actively conduct transactions on both single properties and bulk packages.

Transactional funding is another exciting area AcQuity Investments provides, both through our own funds and through syndicating with others for larger financing needs.

Businesses with sector-expertise that lack critical infrastructure or business-expertise can make use of AcQuity's back office services such as accounting and finance management, negotiating with institutions, legal processes management, securing transactional funding, and deal-flow management. Businesses may either enter into a joint venture with AcQuity or secure our back offices services through a professional services agreement.

Most of my professional career I've been a serial entrepreneur: I've successfully started and exited 5 previous ventures, both for profit and not-for-profit. The longest running business to date was Beyst Construction, LLC in which, over ten years, I built the company from nothing to a net worth of $2M, with $1.5M annual sales and a sustained 20% net profit. Throughout my 22 years of business and professional experience I have developed a broad range of skills and expertise including business management (including finance, human relations, risk management, marketing, strategic planning & analysis, and sales), public relations (including public speaking, writing, publishing, and graphic design), pastoral ministry, and individualized instruction/coaching.

My current goal is to work with businesses and entrepreneurs to help them succeed in business, realize positive cash flow, and obtain stable and significant profitability.
