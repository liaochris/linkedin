Otis Collier’s Summary

My name is Otis Collier and I am a Personal Branding Coach. I specialize in helping three types of people:

1. Career minded individuals – employees who are interested in learning how to leverage the internet and social media to brand themselves as recognized authority leaders in their field of employment.

2. Small business owners – brick and mortar businesses who wish to reduce their traditional advertising costs by 50-70% while increasing their ability to market directly to their customers and drive new traffic to their business.

3. Network marketers – representatives involved in multilevel marketing companies who are looking for professional ways to attract and recruit new talent into their organization.

My methods of branding and marketing are accomplish through the use of social media using a strategy called “attraction marketing”. This is simply a way to position yourself so that others view you as an expert and seek you out to:

1. Hire you
2. Purchase your products/services
3. Join your team

Otis Collier’s Specialties:

personal branding, internet marketing, attraction marketing, career counseling, performance coaching, salary negotiations, public speaking, internet marketing, employment selection, sourcing, interviewing, cold calling, networking and diversity recruiting.
