    August Rising is a unique blend of music and influences, composed of many variations of transcending melodies that lead to explosive dynamics. For the past 2 years the group has been working hard self-promoting, recording, and touring to accomplish their goals. August Rising has become a seasoned touring and studio band that is not only polished on CD, but also electrifying on stage!
    August Rising's music has been been demanded by large and diverse audiences, which in turn has aired them on various Television, News, and Radio programs through out various parts of the United States and the World. Most notable on one of Los Angeles biggest radio stations, Star *98.7 FM.

    ------------------

    Jarod Przewoznik is a distinguished American Guitarist and Producer. He is currently the lead guitarist of August Rising. Having joined the group in early 2008, he has been key in completing their overall sound, performance, and chemistry.
    He has had the fortune of being taught and groomed for two years by The Steve Miller Band guitarist Kenny Lee Lewis. Under his tutelage, Jarod received “hands on training” so to speak, and has gotten to meet and work with several legendary artists.
    Throughout it all, Jarod has remained an enigmatic original. A lead guitarist without the compulsion for fretboard pyrotechnics, Jarod will re-define guitar playing in the new millennium.
