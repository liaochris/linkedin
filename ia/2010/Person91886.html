Jeff Geller’s Summary

Principal owner of J. Geller & Associates, Inc.

For the past 8+ years, have built and established a staffing practice specializing in the placement of contract and full time Information Technology Professionals.

My business background consists of 20+ years of dedicated service to the IT Sector of Southern California. 17+ years of this has been in providing both contract, contract to hire and full time staffing augmentation of Information Technology Professionals to Corporate Clients throughout the Southern California area and as well as beyond.

Other states of IT staffing accomplishments have included New York, Indiana, Texas, Arizona and Washington State, etc.

Successful IT staffing partnerships have been established over the years with such companies as 21st Century Insurance, The State Bar of California, WMC Mortgage/GE Finance, Warner Brothers Studios, Universal Studios, Los Angeles County ISD, EDS, IBM Global Services, GTE & Countrywide, etc.

I enjoy meeting new people and organizations and learning about what they do and how our services might benefit them.
