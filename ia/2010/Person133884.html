Mark Hebblewhite’s Summary

Mark has extensive experience within the internet, design, video and photography business, he has worked at a spread of agencies; MotorTrak, 1eEurope and also ran his own freelance agency for five years. During this time he worked across a range of integrated accounts including Mercedes Benz, Financial Times, Porche, Fidelity Investments, Jupiter Asset Management, Peugeot, Jaguar, Euro Tunnel, HR Owen, British Telecom, Ceridian and Corgi Gas. Over the past three years he has been working full-time at Jupiter Asset Management as their Multimedia Manager looking after their website, overseeing the Marketing teams content creation; he also creates all the corporate videos and covers all photographic needs.

In his spare time, Mark runs a non profit business education news website; which has over 30k visitors per week and a team of international journalists who writes the content for the site.

His skills are an important marriage for any company striving for a high standard in their multimedia business developments.
