Andrea Pirrotti’s Summary

Andrea Pirrotti is a ROI-driven marketing executive with a proven track record in delivering business success. She is the President of Pirrotti Marketing Group LLC (founded in ’05) and utilizes an empirical approach to marketing that provides clients with a maximum return on investment.

From 1999 to 2005 Andrea was at The Regus Group [LSE: RGU] where she rose from Director of North American Marketing to Vice President of Global Marketing in charge of building a powerful brand that would yield maximum revenue / ROI across 65 countries. Andrea was a member of the Leadership Team for this $1 Billion enterprise. During her tenure she crafted the marketing strategy for Regus’ IPO, the firm’s product, location, online and country launches, bankruptcy (and early emergence) and acquisitions.

From 1991-1999 she headed up marketing for Ernst & Young LLP’s Technology, Communication and Entertainment tri-state group, graduate programs at the Princeton Review and served in product management roles at WarnerVision (now Warner Home video) and Gramavision Records.

Andrea is a consummate public speaker delivering thought-leadership content both at industry conferences and via Webinar. She earned a BS in Communications from Boston University and an MBA in Marketing from Pace University’s Lubin School of Business. She lives in Fairfield, CT with her husband Johnny and children Johnny and Sophia.

Andrea Pirrotti’s Specialties:

Creating & executing marketing strategies for companies transforming markets and industries. Public Speaking. Training. Licensed Real Estate Broker.
