Tony LaCivita’s Summary

Tony LaCivita is an Associate Partner in IBM's Global Business Services - Business Analytics and Optimization Practice. He specializes in the Automotive and Electronics Industries. Formerly Tony LaCivita served:

- As an entrepreneur and an IT Executive Tony LaCivita founded his own software and hardware development company LaCivita Design, LLC building applications and hardware for the award winning Apple iPhone and founding a Marine Division of this same LLC, a developing and manufacturing one of his own patented marine inventions to the recreational boating market in 2009.

- Delivered a $700 Million Dollar global commercial IT out-sourcing program with Sales, Marketing, Customer Support, SAP, and ERP Integration at General Motors Corporation. He won the 2007 General Motors Chairman’s award for this achievement.

- Delivered Global CRM and 1:1 marketing program capabilities at General Motors working as their CRM/eBusiness Business Process Engineering & IT Information Officer.

- Successfully delivered ERP, Siebel, Oracle, CRM and Customer Facing Contact Center Integration capabilities around the world for GM acting as the agent for change and delivering new business capabilities through IT.

- Was the leader who built the first Application Systems Development / Deployment Center in GM.

- Extensive background both in Manufacturing and Engineering delivering technical CAD CAM and Design solutions with GM, Toyota, Nissan and at Chrysler Corporation with their CATIA implementation.

- Mr. LaCivita has worked in top rated IT organizations including: Apple, IBM, and Oracle. He has over five years of Management Consulting & Business Process Re-engineering Leadership experience at A. T. Kearney and at IBM working with C Level Executives.

- Tony LaCivita's style of execution is straight forward - build for speed and quality, and deliver results quickly. He has done so with his Global Team Development, Global Program Management & Leadership experience.
