Jenn Simpson’s Summary

Jenn relocated to the Charleston area in 2002, bringing with her a solid background in marketing communications. As a freelance marketing consultant, Jenn oversees the marketing efforts for her clients, including strategy and planning, public relations, advertising, event planning, media production, website development and digital marketing - email, search engine optimization (SEO) and marketing (SEM), video and social media. She is available for new project work.

A native of West Columbia, SC, Jenn holds a degree in integrated marketing communications from Winthrop University in Rock Hill, SC. Prior to going out on her own, she held marketing positions with the Medical University of South Carolina (MUSC), Charleston Regional Development Alliance, Blackbaud, Inc. and The Marketing Consortium. Jenn’s diverse background has given her experience in multiple industries, including healthcare, software and technology, nonprofit, economic development, advertising agencies and public companies.

She is a member of the Charleston Advertising Federation and the Charleston American Marketing Association, where she serves as the Board’s Director of Marketing.

In her spare time she enjoys jogging, traveling and volunteering in her church and community. Jenn resides in Mount Pleasant with her husband, Chris, and her newborn son, Brayden.

Media planning, buying, interactive marketing, social media, marketing strategy and planning, email marketing, event planning, budget management, dashboard reports, search engine optimization and marketing, direct marketing
