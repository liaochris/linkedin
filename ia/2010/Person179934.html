Ryan Spohn’s Summary

I’m currently working as a user experience designer in Boulder, Colorado. I specialize in the areas of user interface design, interaction design, visual design, graphic design, usability, and front-end web development. I’ve worked with several notable software/Internet startups including Pearl Street Software (acquired by MyHeritage 2007), HiveLive (acquired by RightNow Technologies 2009), and FamilyLink.com. I'm a creative problem solver, self motivated, and have a keen eye for detail.

I earned a BFA in industrial design from The Columbus College of Art and Design in 2002. When I’m not perched in front of a computer, I enjoy spending time with my wife, traveling, and further educating myself in the areas of design, architecture, politics, and our environment.
