John Bickel Jr’s Summary

Licensed commercial insurance broker with experience in structuring corporate insurance programs. Heavy emphasis in the transportation industry and now managing a corporate safety and insurance program for the largest transporation driver provider in the United States and including transportation services in Canada.

CPC Logistics, Inc. has over 30 years of experience in providing professional truck driver services to private truck fleet operations throughout North America. Our strong, steady growth and strategic acquisitions have positioned us with operations in over 40 states across the U.S., as well as Canada and Puerto Rico, with thousands of our professional drivers supporting our clients.

In my role as Director of Risk Management, my focus is cost containment of insurance costs through proactive claims management and optimizing our internal safety resources.

What we can offer you as a client:

• Best-in-industry customer service
• Professional excellence
• Professional and quality drivers, dispatchers, clerks, mechanics, warehousemen
• General liability insurance
• Third party fidelity bonding
• Labor Advisory Service
• Safety Advisory Service
• Log scanning and compliance
• On-site fleet management solutions

I'm interested in connecting with professionals that would be referral partners to the transporation indusrty for companies that currently operate or would consider operating a private fleet or dedicated transportation arrangements.
