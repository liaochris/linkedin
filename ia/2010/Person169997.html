Rob Stolle’s Summary

If you need assistance filling a position or are looking for a new career opportunity in Los Angeles feel free to contact me at robert.stolle@jobspringpartners.com or give me a call at (310) 996-0200.

Jobspring Partners is a nationwide technology recruiting firm. We place highly skilled software developers/programmers, network engineers/administrators, systems architects and interface professionals in growing, technically-advanced companies. We help companies with challenging technology needs locate the talented IT professionals they require to move their businesses forward. Jobspring Partners uses a positive, team-based model that’s unique in recruiting and placement. We are committed to setting the standard for client and candidate satisfaction in our industry.

Jobspring Partners has been in Los Angeles for over a decade. We are split up into two different practices which allows us to be extremely specialized; one practice places software professionals in the Java, PHP, Python, Perl, Ruby, and Quality Assurance realm. One practice places professionals skilled in ASP.NET, C#, VB.NET, Flash, Flex, ActionScript and/or SQL.
