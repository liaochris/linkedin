Shoubhik Doss’s Summary

Lead Power Electronics Engineer with expertise in high voltage, high power converter design. Direct experience with design of High voltage power semiconductor modules and gate drives for power converters. Adept in mixed signal and high speed embedded circuit design. Thorough knowledge of advanced thermal management techniques and modeling thermal systems.

Shoubhik Doss’s Specialties:

Smart Grid Systems, Power Electronics Design, Power Semiconductor devices, Gate driver design, Mixed signal & high speed digital embedded system design, Advanced thermal management techniques, High voltage/ High power testing of prototypes.

Matlab, Saber, Orcad Spice, PSIM, ModelSim, TI Code Composer Studio, Eagle PCB layout tool, AutoCAD, Solidworks, Ansys, Solidworks Simulation, Verilog, C, assembly code for TI’s C2000 µcontrollers, windows, linux.
