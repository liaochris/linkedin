Jef Harris’s Summary

Over the past 15 years, through extensive discipline and training, I have acquired the most admirable qualities an employer looks for. In addition to being dedicated and productive, I have gained a superior ability to multi-task, sustain, and maintain focus while meeting or exceeding goals and objectives. I Work well as a team leader, or self-directed and am able to organize work, set priorities, and meet deadlines.
With my extraordinary interpersonal skills, I’m able to adapt and thrive in creative high pressure work environments. There is a challenge to be enjoyed in learning environments. In the past, the often sensitive nature of my work has allowed me to develop a sound sense of judgment, discretion, tact, thoroughness, and dependability.
Given my experience, knowledge, and leadership, a side project of my own has naturally formed. To best symbolize the abundant and artistically experimental projects I produce, I decided to call this side project, ‘Chaos Academy’, for it best represents the diversity and range of my talents as well as representing my dedication to organization and love of knowledge.
Since Chaos Academy’s inception, I have worked tirelessly by producing 12 music videos, 3 fashion style videos (for designer Rosa Costanzo), and a critically acclaimed short movie. All of my personal projects have enjoyed the benefit of cult status among their niche demographics. My work with Chaos Academy has also allowed me the opportunity to become seriously involved in the local arts and fashion communities as I have become active in creative & fashion photography. (Most recently worked with KaviKavi.com).
