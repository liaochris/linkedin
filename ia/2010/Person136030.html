Mary Dee Freeman’s Summary

I am a teacher, a motivator, a master communicator, a problem solver and a creative thinker. My motto is "you can be successful too!"

In the past, I was a teacher and coach for 12 years in Colorado Springs where I founded and developed the leadership and community of caring programs at Mesa Ridge High School. After that, I began working for a fortune 500 company where I shot to the top 2% of the sales force within six months.

I founded WOW in 2005 because I wanted to share what I had learned with others. I've worked with many small business owners and self employed professionals as well as large companies like AT&T, MoDot and Shaklee to help them become more successful.

I currently hold weekly and monthly webinars, publish a weekly newsletter. offer home study courses and individual coaching. Every day's an adventure!
