Rikkert Koppes’s Summary

Rikkert Koppes is a graphics and interaction designer with a professional experience of more than a decade. A range of courses in the field of cognitive ergonomics provided him with a solid scientific foundation, while working with different media like offset press, print and electronic imagery taught him how to implement design at various levels. A profound knowledge of HTML, Cascading Stylesheets and JavaScript assists him in quickly creating prototype designs, while ensuring feasibility.

In the past he has been working on static interaction with a vast range of printed media like books, booklets, flyers, flags and banners, vehicle decorations, packages and clothing, as well as static and dynamic interaction with digital media like web pages, web and desktop applications. Alongside, he has been a consultant and teacher on HTML development.

Furthermore, Rikkert is an active contributor to the Web Hypertext Application Technology Working Group (WHATWG), paving the path for the next version of HTML. Lastly, a Master of Science degree in Applied Physics at the Eindhoven University of Technology brings the skills for an effective, analytic, result driven approach to problem solving. Or as the Eindhoven University puts it, makes him a "universal problem solver".

Rikkert can be contacted for Interaction Design and Frontend Consultancy work.
