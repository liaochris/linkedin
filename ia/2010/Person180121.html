Saara Vakil’s Summary

An Ivy League graduate and MBA candidate at USC's Marshall School of Business, Saara moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in the entertainment industry. Saara found success working with top executives at Sony Pictures Entertainment and Warner Bros Entertainment, an experience that lead her to pursue her natural entrepreneurial curiosity. As a strategic marketing consultant, Saara helped launch a 4000-square-foot, state-of-the-art surgery center in Beverly Hills. Saara followed up this success in 2007 with the launch of Just Perfect, a full service event planning company that caters to both corporate and private clients. At Just Perfect, Saara is responsible for all marketing and business development efforts. Saara is currently enrolled at USC's Marshall School to enhance the skills she first developed in the field and emerge a stronger leader.
