Matt Sharples’s Summary

Co-Founder, CEO, and current majority owner of TriCom Technical Services in metropolitan Kansas City.

TriCom is a nationally recognized leader in the Information Technology Staffing and Consulting business. We have successfully recruited and staffed over 1000 I.T. professionals with over 100 diverse organizations in 24 states nationwide. We primarily work the midwest marketplace with an emphasis in Kansas City, Minneapolis, Wichita, Des Moines, Omaha, St. Louis, Chicago, and Denver.
