Cindy Augustine’s Summary

Kazoo is a media planning and buying agency based in Kansas City. In today's ever-changing media landscape, it is hard to sift through all the options that you, as a local or regional advertiser should be considering. At Kazoo, we believe in a blend of traditional and non-traditional media choices, and advise our clients on the right mix to reach their target audience.

But, our efforts do not end with strategy. We negotiate vehemently to ensure that our clients are paying the best price possible. Finally, we follow up and monitor all advertising campaigns to make sure our clients are getting what they paid for and what they were promised.

Media, marketing, and a whole lot of mirth.
