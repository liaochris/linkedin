Adrian Rubio’s Summary

Adrian Rubio is SVP of Corporate Development at Flycell, one of the main off deck mobile companies in the US. He is in charge of overseeing the Licensing, Product Development and Business Development departments. Previously he worked as SVP of Business Development helping technology and media and entertainment companies shape their content, platform and marketing strategies for mobile. Adrian joined Flycell coming from Zingy Inc. where he held roles such as Vice President of International Operations, Vice President of Business Development and Director for Latin America. While at Zingy, he closed key deals with major online and offline media companies and carriers in the U.S., Canada, Latin America and Europe. Prior to that, he worked in the Washington D.C. area for Internet and software startups in the public and private sectors. On a previous life, he spent several years in the music industry in his native Mexico City. He, along with his band, recorded 2 albums for BMG, toured internationally and directed award-winning music videos.

Adrian holds a B.S. in Computer Systems from the Instituto Tecnológico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey and an MBA in finance and marketing from The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
