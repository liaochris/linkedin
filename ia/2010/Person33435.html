Carrie Morris’s Summary

Carrie Morris is a 14 year veteran of consumer, entertainment, technology public relations and corporate communications. A big thinker, unparalleled team leader and detail-oriented account manager, Carrie has specialization in consumer, media relations, global account management and social media programs. Upon joining Edelman in 2002, Carrie was responsible for managing the consumer launch of MSN 8.0. More recently, Carrie managed global consumer PR programs for Microsoft Xbox and Xbox Live, including leading efforts in the worldwide launch of Xbox 360.

Before joining Edelman, Carrie owned strategy and drove consumer product launches for Microsoft, Gateway and Sony, and delivered integrated media campaigns with strategic partners for Microsoft, Intel, HBO/Cinemax, Showtime Networks and Ford Motor Company, to name a few. Carrie was also responsible for establishing AtomFilms as the leader in short-form entertainment and a global entertainment brand. Her work and that of her team was recognized by PRSA, which awarded Atom with the Silver Anvil Award.

Carrie holds a bachelor’s degree in Communications from Boston University, and currently resides in Kirkland, WA, with her husband, two children, two dogs and one very misunderstood cat.

Carrie Morris’s Specialties:

Consumer public relations, media and analyst relations, corporate communications, global account management and social/digital media programs.
