Libby Romfh’s Summary

How do you change the behavior of 40,000 employees and implement consistent standards in more than 800 different locations nationwide? In the fast-paced retail world, where most employees are part-time/hourly, and the daily “to do” list is a combination of Home Office priorities, local management decisions, and the needs of the customers standing in front of you right now, effective communication means more than writing a good memo. A background of retail experience, change management, project planning, and strategic thinking help me develop multi-channel communications strategies that get sustainable results for business owners throughout the company, while keeping front line employees focused on customers and sales.

Libby Romfh’s Specialties:

Change management, retail management, communications, workload planning and reduction, project management, training and group facilitation.
