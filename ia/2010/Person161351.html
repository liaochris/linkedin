Paul Rouke’s Summary

Commercially experienced usability consultant, trainer and information architect having worked for and with blue chip brands for over 10 years.

Paul is passionate about helping deliver compelling customer experiences that fulfil consumer goals and align to key business drivers. In addition Paul has a proven track record of working with and managing multi-disciplinary teams to develop usable, engaging and accessible online/offline web applications and retail platforms.

As well as providing training directly to PRWD clients, Paul is one of the few industry professionals providing both public and in-house training for the highly respected Econsultancy, the leading source of independent advice and insight on digital marketing and ecommerce. Through his training courses Paul thrives on helping businesses and individuals learn how they can improve the performance of their online operations.

Paul is also an event speaker and this includes speaking alongside Google on the subject of advanced web analytics, conversion optimisation and split/multivariate testing.

When delivering new web applications and IT systems using PRWD’s user-centered design process, Paul provides services including requirement gathering, persona development, competitor benchmarking, stakeholder analysis, documentation, business process and operational analysis alongside winning the hearts and minds of end users involved in such projects.

Brands Paul has worked with include Shop Direct Group, JD Williams, The Cotswold Company, The Post Office, Mencap, Abbey, The Scout Association, Speedy Hire, Econsultancy and Littlewoods, along with many lesser known brands who have benefited from Paul’s expertise.

Paul’s goal is to continue establishing PRWD as the leading usability agency in the North West of England, through consultancy, training, event speaking and constantly delivering improvements to their clients bottom line.

Paul Rouke’s Specialties:

The user-centered design lifecycle (analysis, design, implementation and on-going evaluation and optimisation) usability, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, conversion rate optimisation, e-commerce best practice, heuristic evaluations, training in areas of usability, e-commerce best practice and Google Analytics, advanced analytics with Google Analytics, split and multivariate testing with Google Website Optimiser, event speaking in areas such as on-site conversion improvement

  • User Experience Director

    PRWD

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 6 months)

    PRWD are a highly experienced usability and web/software development agency, specialising in bringing all the benefits of good usability to businesses looking to improve the ROI from both online and offline IT systems.

    PRWD work both directly with clients such as Royal Mail, JD Williams, The Scout Association and Speedy Hire, as well as through their agency partners who harness PRWD’s expertise in usability, information architecture, conversion optimisation and web analytics when working with their own clients.
