Bill Bender’s Summary

Anothen Life helps others through teaching, consulting, webinars, seminars, and personal ministry.

* Social Media Goals for the Electrical Industry, Job Seekers, & Ministry:
After learning the power of LinkedIn, I realized that most people don't use much of the real power so I'm doing webinars & seminars to help people, companies, and ministries discover and utilize all the power in LinkedIn to expand their reach and grow their business.

Visit our LinkedIn website www.AnothenLife.com

* Ministry Goals:
We help people deal with issues that they thought they would just have to live with like anger, fear, depression, rejection, addictions, etc.

Our ministry enables individuals and couples to discover and heal their hurts, fears and false perceptions, freeing them to express their God-given capabilities, maximize their potentials, and fulfill the life God intends for them to live.

* Church Leadership Training:
We teach inner-healing and deliverance processes (including RTF) that allows Church Leadership Teams to achieve results that are successful, sustainable and repeatable in healing their local church and community.

* Corporate Ministry:
We help corporations (churches and business) that have been unable to break free from spiritual issues that inhibit their effectiveness and successes.

Visit our Ministry website at www.AnothenLife.org
