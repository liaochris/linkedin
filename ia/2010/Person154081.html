Nick Lemcke’s Summary

I am a Senior Account Manager/Recruiter with a successful recruiting & staffing firm located in San Diego, CA. I perform everything from New Business Development to successfully Recruiting Talented Candidates for our valuable clients-and everything in between. We separate ourselves from our competitors through unparalleled service and by going the extra mile for our customers. I personally strive to become an expert in whatever industry I'm serving at that time. My mission is to build my business by harvesting relationships with current clientele and new prospects. We have the resources of a large company, but the flexibility and personal touch of a boutique firm. Our HQ is located in Northern California, allowing us to serve the entire state with a personal touch, face-to-face. We work with contract, contract-to-hire, and direct hire candidates in all industries and positions. We are experienced with just about every industry, with extensive experience filling the following positions: Accounting/Finance, Admin/Clerical, Engineers (all types, from Building to Software), HR, and Sales/Marketing. We are also experts in the Technology and Medical fields.
Our goal is to become an invaluable resource to both our clients and our candidates. We accomplish this with a professional approach that achieves a win-win outcome at the finish line.
