Bryce Ebeling’s Summary

BUSINESS EXPERIENCE: I have over 15 years solid experience in both B2B and B2C private enterprise markets and non-profit organizations ranging in size from $200 million publicly traded enterprises to venture backed startups and small business firms; providing products and services direct to consumers, small businesses and Fortune rated enterprises including: eCommerce (integrated brick-n-mortar operations), Cross Media Marketing (development and integration of print, web, SMS, IVR, email and data services), Franchise Services (all areas of corporate and franchise operations), Applied Technology (software design, development and integration), and ISP Serivces (commercial, consumer and education services).

PROFESSIONAL PROFILE: Solid interpersonal and communications skills complement my breadth and depth of experience in all functional areas of business, including: leadership, strategic development, general management, financial administration, marketing (product development, pricing, placement and promotion), sales (products and solutions), technology (design, development and integration), corporate and field operations (serving both internal and external clients) and enterprise training. This broad range of experience has helped me to understand and appreciate the often sensitive but important relationships between order-acquisition and order-fulfillment.

CAREER GOALS: leveraging my expertise and achievements in Marketing / Sales / Operations / Technology, continue to advance in executive / entrepreneurial leadership roles applying what I’ve learned in designing, developing and implementing strategic cross-media business-to-business and business-to-consumer marketing initiatives that marry web/technology solutions to brick-n-mortar operations and services.
