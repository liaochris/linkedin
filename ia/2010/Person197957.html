Susan O'Neil’s Summary

I am passionate about PR and my interests lie around the world of Web 2.0 and social media channels/tools. My professional experience spans over several great accounts including: Hydropoint, a green initiative company; Gigya, a large widget network; Friendster, a social networking Web site; Plantronics, a leading manufacturer of Bluetooth headset; E2open, a supply chain management company and an agency-specific Web 2.0 team.

Susan O'Neil’s Specialties:

Client counsel and tactical execution of traditional and social media PR programs, developing and maintaining media relations with editorial and analyst communities, creative writing, coordination of global efforts, social media, consumer electronics, event planning, API, database management, research, campaign development and implementation
