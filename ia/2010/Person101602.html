John P. Benson’s Summary

John has over thirty years of multi-line insurance experience, both strategically and tactically (life and health, disability and personal and commercial lines, including auto and workers’ compensation). John developed his management and leadership skills by leading large operational units for established carriers and by owning his own businesses. John started out in the insurance industry as a Home Office Representative for MONY Financial Services (Mutual of New York) . Prior to entering the insurance industry John owned and managed investigative firms serving the insurance, legal and business markets. John has served on editorial boards for insurance industry publications, and chaired committees for industry organizations and for a state Department of Insurance. He has had significant leadership involvement with industry associations including the California Workers’ Compensation Institute (CWCI) (committee chairperson), NHCAA, IASIU, AISG/ISO Insurance Fraud Management Committee, and the Antifraud Sub-Committee of the California Department of Insurance (co-chairperson). John is frequently sought out for public speaking opportunities on fraud, technology and insurance issues. His certifications are from the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (CFE), the International Association of Special Investigation Units (Certified Insurance Fraud Investigator) and the National Health Care Anti-Fraud Association (Accredited Health Care Fraud Investigator). John holds private investigations licenses in several states. John holds a Bachelors degree in Journalism and a Juris Doctorate form Loyola University of Los Angeles.

John P. Benson’s Specialties:

Prevention, detection, investigation and civil prosecution of waste, abuse and fraud in insurance (all lines), reimbursement and entitlement systems. Health care data analytics (informatics) and the development of health care data solutions (systems).

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    2007 — Present (3 years )

    Delivering since 1992, we started as Government Management Services, Inc. founded by a former Assistant Secretary and Acting Undersecretary of DHHS and the former IG for DHHS. Re-branding is currently underway to Verisys Corporation. We are well-known for our FACIS™ product. Verisys is made up of a team of specialists in health care regulatory compliance, risk management, data acquisition and management, data quality assurance and IT. Verisys acquires, aggregates and publishes data electronically from thousands of primary sources, both federal and state, to surface an historical and current view of a health care provider. Verisys obtains primary source content on persons, professionals and business entities relative to licensing, sanctions, disciplinary actions, DEA registrant, SSN - Master File, NPI, UPIN, Abuse Registries and other key data sets and links them, clustering and house-holding, and then matches Verisys content to client input using proprietary entity resolution technologies. The Verisys team handles millions of transactions annually across the health care system, including hospitals, hospital systems, health care provider systems (IPAs), pharmacies, pharmaceutical manufacturers, medical equipment manufacturers (DME), payers, managed care organizations, medical malpractice carriers, background screening companies and credentialing organizations. Verisys provides information to help its clients assure regulatory compliance, manage legal and financial risk and protect their reputation. We help organizations know that their: employees (people), providers (licensed professionals, internal and external), and vendors (businesses) that they engage with are not high-risk individuals or entities. Verisys/GMS, Inc. is NCQA Certified for the following verification services: medical board sanctions, Medicare/Medicaid sanctions and ongoing monitoring of sanctions. Verisys is a fully accredited Credentials Verification Organization (CVO) by URAC.
