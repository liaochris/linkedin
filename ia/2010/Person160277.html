Patty Adams’s Summary

I've been in sales and marketing for my entire career, starting with telecommunications sales in Los Angeles, California. Over the years, the bulk of my sales experience has been in educational technology. As my knowledge of internet marketing increased over the years, as well as obtaining a Master's degree with a focus in marketing, I've been able to successfully apply my sales and marketing skills to not only further my own professional goals, but to better serve my clients at Vertical Measures as they seek to enhance their existing internet marketing efforts.

I find all aspects of internet marketing fascinating, and agree with the experts who feel that future marketing dollars for most major corporations will rely heavily on internet marketing. My job is to help our clients understand the options available for internet marketing and website publicity, and effectively help them leverage our services to enhance their internet marketing efforts.
