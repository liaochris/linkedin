  • Director of Emerging Ideas, Board of Directors

    AIGA Boston

    (Non-Profit; Design industry)

    June 2009 — Present (1 year )

    • Inspiration for this new role came from several factors including member feedback, an ever-changing design industry, the speed of technological (and social) evolution and a challenging economy.

    • As former Membership Director, I received a lot of feedback from our members and a common thread throughout has always been a desire for more diverse events and programming especially relating to interactive media.

    • In formulating this role to best deliver on those requests, I knew we had to make this inspire more than just “learning Flash” and “making web sites”. You can expect to see future events, classes, workshops, student outreach and partnerships established that will include some of the following themes such as:

    + interface design
    + user experience design
    + mobile technology, location-awareness and augmented reality
    + information design and data visualization
    + human-centered design, design thinking & innovation
    + ethnography at the root of cultural anthropology
    + social media
    + design for social change
    + business entrepreneurship & more...

    • We’re currently working on some exciting events and lectures for the upcoming season, as well as great partnerships with Apple, the MIT Media Lab, The Dynamic Media Institute at Mass Art, and more. Stay tuned at: boston.aiga.org!
