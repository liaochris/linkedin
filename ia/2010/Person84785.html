Inanc Inan’s Summary

Research scientist and development engineer mainly focusing on communication systems and networking, specialized on MAC, PAL, Network, Transport layer protocol design, network modeling, embedded firmware and software development. Broad knowledge and research, system design, development experience on WLAN/WPAN standards (e.g. 802.11bgn, UWB Bluetooth), internet protocols; in particular, TCP/IP and QoS mechanisms. Theoretical background on digital communications (PHY layer), wired/wireless networks, probability and random processes. Hands on experience on SOC embedded firmware, Linux Kernel SDIO and USB driver development, network simulator design and implementation.

Inanc Inan’s Specialties:

MAC, PAL, link, transport layer protocol design, SOC embedded firmware, Linux Kernel SDIO and USB driver, Analytical network modeling and performance analysis, Communications systems and networks simulation module design and development, C/C++, Tcl/OTcl, Matlab, Network Simulator (ns-2), 802.11, UWB, Wireless USB, Bluetooth, TCP/IP, USB and SDIO interface firmware development, Perl, Awk, Python, Latex, HTML, Network and protocol analyzers, JTAG/UART debugging, Assembler (MIPS, Motorola).
