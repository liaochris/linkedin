Trane DePriest’s Summary

As an award winning graphic designer and publisher with over 20 years experience, I've used Illustrator, Photoshop, and Quark Xpress to produce everything imaginable in print.

As a television artist and producer, I would create many shows, spots, and promos on a daily basis. I'd shoot both on-set and on-location, then edit in Avid Xpress, Quantel, Premier, and Final Cut Pro environments. I use Blender3D, Motion, and After-Effects for custom 3D art and motion graphics.

And now as the Director of Business Development, I work with advertising managers and agencies on creating brand and revenue focused campaigns utilizing online marketing and traditional media.

It is my goal to unify the creative expression of a company's brand, product, and sales materials in order to establish a single and effective message. to measure that message's penetration through conversion tracking, and to optimize the Key Performance Indicators to yield the greatest return on investment.

Trane DePriest’s Specialties:

As an entrepreneur, I have an in-depth understanding of the unique marketing needs of the "start-up" small or medium sized business. I've managed advertising campaigns ranging from two figures to six figures.
