César Idrovo’s Summary

12 years in Financial Services mainly sell-side IT, most recently buy-side.

In 2000 I Created my 1st hyper-productive team at JPM. Recruited a highly heterogeneous group, implemented "continuous collaboration", and achieved high team cohesion and tangible results. Several of my team's tactical solutions were adopted as strategic and are still in use today.

Since 2003 focused on Agile and adaptability to high rates of change in requirements, creating hyper-productive teams. As project and program mgr, I have applied Scrum and XP, inc. 100% pairing. Also developed innovations for management and executive levels.

In 2008 I have given over 50% of my time to nonprofit initiatives, focusing mainly on BayAPLN.org
