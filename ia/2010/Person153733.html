Nicholas Pratley’s Summary

Five years of analyzing short-term and long-term behavior of wholesale electricity markets (nodal LMP esp. New England, New York and Midwest ISO, Ontario and Scandinavia), developing and using highly detailed fundamental market simulations.

Deep understanding of market price drivers, generation technologies and transmission network design. Strong quantitative / mathematical skills and experience in team leadership, project management and presentation.

Prior experience as electrical power engineering consultant, specializing in power system analysis and design for EHV transmission, MV and LV distribution and heavy industry.

Specializations include complex analyses such as stability (transient, voltage and small-signal), protection coordination, fault analysis, power flow and production cost simulation.

Very interested in technological developments and policy issues in Renewable Energy and Electric Vehicle technology.
