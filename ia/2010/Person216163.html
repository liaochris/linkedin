Wally Kolcz’s Summary

I am current a ColdFusion Architect who loves the MVC Framework Model-Glue and use it in majority of my development. I working heavily in Design Patterns for CF doing all my business and model logic in CFCs.

I am also a Flash Developer specializing in the Flex and AIR Frameworks, using ColdFusion as my data middleware and LiveCycle DS and Blaze DS. Currently I am learning/doing my application development using Mate MVC/MVP framework. My goal is to replace HTML!

I work a lot with analytics and have a passion for data visualization and data dashboards.

I am beginning to experiment more with interactive Flash movies using Adobe After Effects CS3 to add new effects and am delving into 3D flash using papervision3D, away3D, and five3D. And looking more into APE (actionscript physics engine). Who knows where this will go.

Currently working at University of Michigan Health Systems as a developer. Not interested in contract work...unless its remote.
