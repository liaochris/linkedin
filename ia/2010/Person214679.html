Wendy Mantel’s Summary

Mantel Coaching, Inc.

I am a professional personal branding and career coach who works with people whose careers are in transition and helps them turn their situations into opportunities. I work equally well with new-to-work clients and seasoned careerists.

I have particular expertise coaching women--from those just beginning their careers to those who are highly experienced and evaluating the next step in their work lives. I provide individual coaching as well as group workshops.

My state-of-the-art coaching services are designed not only to help clients envision what's next, but also to look at what they are passionate about, how to create more fulfilling workpaths and how to position themselves for success in their pursuit of employment. I use a collaborative, highly motivating process founded in client strengths and interests; skills developed in a successful 30-year product branding and advertising career, and learnings from ongoing studies in professional coaching.

Whether you are looking to get back on your feet after a lay-off; get started in a first job; transition to a new career; advance your current career, or re-assess your overall direction, I can provide the support and motivation you need to accomplish your goals.

I offer a FREE initial coaching session to potential clients to see how we might work together to address your needs. All sessions are private and confidential.

Member, International Coach Federation.

Wendy Mantel’s Specialties:

Personal branding--those personal and professional attributes that differentiate you in the workplace. Interview skills coaching. Presentation skills coaching. Career trouble-shooting and brainstorming as well as strategic and tactical career planning. Real life and real time problem-solving that addresses and resolves issues as they come up.
