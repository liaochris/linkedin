Christos Koukoumides’s Summary

7 years clinical research experience (responsible for the start-up/conduct of Phase II, III, IV, compassionate clinical trials) in the following indications: Oncology, Genitourinary, Cardiology, Hepatology, Fungal Infections, Skeletal, Immunology, Dermatology, Allergic Rhinitis, Neurology.

More than six years experience as a translator (Greek, English, French), specializing in clinical research and other technical documents. Principal collaborations include the publishing companies “Pronomio” and “Savalas”, 3 CROs and the Consultancy “Network TwentyOne SA”.

- Knowledge of histological, microbiological and haematological techniques.
