Michael Schofield’s Summary

Challenging and thought provoking, Michael’s empathetic yet probing approach allows people to discover the solutions best suited for their personal style and situation.

Michael has over 20 years experience in Sales and Marketing in both the Corporate and the dot com world. This provides him with a wealth of experience and a platform from which to understand and relate to the challenges of today’s business environment.
His passion for leadership and coaching combined with superior communication skills are the corner stones of his own success and have led him to a career developing others at all levels from Executive and Senior Managers to teams and their leaders.
Michael has the ability to quickly understand the specific needs of his clients and produce the solution which will best deliver the results the client is looking for.
His values best describe what he brings to coaching; Passion – Honesty – Creativity – Optimism – Challenging
His track record as a coach encapsulates a multitude of
areas such as:-

~ The Power of motivation and self belief
~ Leadership – Creating a culture for success
~ Challenging limiting beliefs
~ The impact of effective man management on performance
~ Building High Performing teams
~ Adversity management
~ Creative solutions and problem solving
~ Effective communication in a changing world
~ Strategic thinking

Michael has a B.Sc in Finance and Accounting, a diploma in Marketing and has also built on his coaching experience by successfully completing an ILM accredited Executive Business Coaching course and a Post Grad course in Personal and Business Coaching.
