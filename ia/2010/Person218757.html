Ziad Ataya’s Summary

In-depth experience in managing and maintaining an enterprise level IT Infrastructure environment made up of multiple disaster recovery datacenter sites supporting over 1200 servers (Windows, LINUX and HPUX), over 25 SAN frames (EMC DMXs, CLARiiONs, HP EVAs and HP NAS). Responsible for IT strategies, design, and implementation of FDA qualified IT infrastructure systems, computer networks, SAN storage, Operating Systems and Directory Services.

Work with business and IT application support across the organization on a local, regional and global levels to set standards, drive efficiencies, and harmonize infrastructure systems.
Manage CAPEX/OPEX IT budgets; deliver IT projects on time and on budget. Lead and manage an infrastructure implementation team made up of architects and Sr. Engineers responsible for architecting, designing, and implementing enterprise servers and SAN storage.

Job duties include, defining strategies and standards, architecting, designing, implementing, and support 1200+ Windows, LINUX and HPUX servers, 25+ SAN storage frames (EMC DMXs, CLARiiONs, HP EVAs and HP NAS),50+ Citrix servers and 30+ VMware ESX host servers in a datacenter farm hosting over 500 virtual servers serving 9500+ clients, sales force reps. and Boehringer Ingelheim operating units.

Manage, coordinate and implement large scale local, regional and global infrastructure IT projects. The server and storage team manages over 40+ global and local project at a given time that have major impact on the operation of the business.
Develop and implement standards and guidelines for server and storage technologies.
Architect, design and implement Windows server 2003/2008 Operating Systems, Directory Services, Citrix, VMWare vSphere, SAN and NAS technologies.
Specialize in EMC CLARiiON and Symmetrix SAN storage utilizing Brocade DCX Backbones fabric switches implemented in core edge architecture. Administered and managed over 100PB of high-end and middle tier SAN storage.
