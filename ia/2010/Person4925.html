Al Adamsen’s Summary

Al Adamsen, Founder and Managing Partner of People-Centered Strategies, LLC, is a renowned consultant and sought after speaker in the areas of talent management, leadership development, workforce analytics, and workforce planning. Al has worked and spoken around the world on these topics. He’s also created and implemented innovative ways of aligning HR strategies to the economics of a business and testing the relationships with predictive models. Al founded PCS to fill the critical, yet under-developed niche of integrating and analyzing data from the full employee life cycle. In doing so he and his collaborators help leaders successfully meet the ever-increasing challenges associated with attracting, developing, and nurturing key talent.

Prior to founding PCS Al worked with leading companies such as Charles Schwab, Ernst & Young, Gap Inc., and Infohrm. He was also a successful entrepreneur having established a consumer products business that still prospers today, 10+ years after its inception. At Gap Inc. Al was responsible for HR metrics & reporting, employee surveys, and workforce analytics. At Infohrm he led the North American Consulting Practice managing the delivery of workforce reporting and analytics services to companies such as Starbucks, Safeco, and Tektronix. Supporting Al’s experience is his background and academic work in Recruiting, Human Resources, I/O Psychology, the Balanced Scorecard, and ERP and HR technologies. Al holds an Economics degree from the University of California, Davis as well as a Masters degree in HR and Organizational Development.
