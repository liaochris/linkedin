Luc Van Hove’s Summary

Goal: Provide senior leadership in diagnostics to improve patient healthcare.

Clinical Pathologist, MD, PhD and Six Sigma Black Belt.
Fourteen-year successful track record as Medical Director with Abbott Diagnostics worldwide in hematology, oncology, viral markers and system development.
Two-year sucessful track record with Exagen Diagnostics as Executive Manager, Chief Medical Officer and Vice President for Quality, Regulatory and Medical Affairs.
Currently, third year successful track record with Asuragen, the microRNA molecular biology company and clinical services laboratory, as Senior Director Medical/Clinical Affairs, Regulatory Affairs and Quality. Manage IVD clinical trials and their regulatory submissions and manage the development of companion diagnostics through our CLIA laboratory; direct a Quality System that is compliant with 9001:2008, ISO 13485:2003, US QS, Canadian and EU IVDD regulations.
Directed numerous clinical studies for US (510-k and PMA) and International submissions.
Brought several in-vitro diagnostic devices to market in the US, Europe and Japan through their regulatory bodies. Obtained most recently CE-IVD marking for Signature LTx Leukemia Translocation Panel and RNARetain for Asuragen and CLIA laboratory introduction of the worldwide first miRNA test for Pancreatic cancer detection.
Led an inter-company FDA Advisor Taskforce that succesfully changed the regulations on automed cell counting.
Effective ambassador for Asuragen and in the past for Abbott Diagnostics and Exagen Diagnostics at scientific meetings, standardization bodies, regulatory and investor meetings.
Posses strong experience in managing highly effective cross-functional teams through strong leadership competencies, i.e. drive for results, strategic vision, leading by example, making difficult decisions, empowering my people and open and knowledgeable communications.
