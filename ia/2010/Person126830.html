Liz Neporent’s Summary

I have spent my entire 20+ years of a career as a fitness, wellness and health professional. MY mission is to make people healthier and help them to navigate the healthcare system as well as the mountains of information about health, fitness and wellness.

Much of my career was spent in corporate fitness and corporate wellness. As a consultant, I was a team leader who created the wellness department at Goldman Sachs as well as for many other well known corporations. Besides designing the space with my partners, I also created the fitness program and managed for 10+ years. I also was instrumental in creating other aspects of their wellness program including the new center at 200 West Street.

I have an equally successful dual career as a health, fitness, wellness and medical writer. I have written for the New York Times, NY Daily News, Shape, Self, AOL. com and others. I have appeared on many local and national radio and TV programs and had my own radio show for more than a year.

My company, Wellness 360 focuses on Healthcare and Medical Advocacy and general wellness consulting for corporations, individuals and high end developers.
