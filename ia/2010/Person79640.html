Marlene Moore Gordon’s Summary

Blogger at www.HandPrintsOnMyHeart.net and entrepreneur creating an online social networking community which compiles true short inspirational stories for a series of books to enrich lives globally.

Extensive experience in licensing my own artwork/designs to renowned companies in the gift, paper and home decor categories.Clients include Waterford Wedgwood, Tiffany & Co., George Caspari, Shurman Fine Papers, Block China, Simon & Schuster, Random House, Cartier, Shaw Living, Stephen Lawrence, and many more.

Art work may be viewed on digital product line www.CreateDigitalArt.com

Former Director of Merchandizing and Gift Shop Manager for The Parrish Art Museum Southampton, NY

Experienced in Sales for Real Estate and Retail Management

Certified Reiki Master
