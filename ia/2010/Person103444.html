John Woods’s Summary

Millennium Consulting Group (MCG), is a Cambridge, Massachusetts based research, consulting & training firm specializing in reenvisioning and recharging corporate identity and performance. With valued clients in business, educational, governmental, healthcare and non-profit institutions, MCG specializes in training front line managers to develop world-class sales, service and business development skills. Respected advisor to the financial industry since 1999, we have worked with a long list of the industry’s premiere institutions. Results have earned us a reputation as a preeminent resource among some of the most respected front line professionals and industry insiders. In the world-wide banking industry, we are known to provide the best marketing training available. (www.milleniumconsultinggroup.net)

    Share Our Strength

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    Share Our Strength® is a national organization committed to ending childhood hunger in America. Hunger is a reality for 12.4 million American children, impairs healthy development and adversely impacts all of society. Share Our Strength recognizes that the problem is not a matter of food production but of access and education. We weave together a network of community groups, activists and food programs to catch children at risk of hunger and provide them with access to nutritious food where they live, learn and play. We work with the culinary industry to create engaging, pioneering programs like Share Our Strength’s Taste of the Nation®, Share Our Strength’s Great American Bake Sale®, Share Our Strength’s A Tasteful Pursuit®, Share Our Strength’s Great American Dine Out™, and Share Our Strength’s Operation Frontline®. We build partnerships, provide grants and seek service and financial partners who share our goal to end childhood hunger in America.
