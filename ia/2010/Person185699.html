Christine Chappell’s Summary

I started Sea Squared Marketing due to a desire to help self employed professionals build their businesses and branding with e-marketing. I am passionate about e-marketing, and am a student of the field. My background in marketing began when supporting my husband's client follow up campaigns to his mortgage clients. I saw first hand the struggle he had, as well as others, to devote time, energy, and discipline to a focused and professional email marketing campaign. His busy schedule did not leave open the time to tackle this valuable marketing tool, let alone on a consistant basis. As I became Program Manager for USA Help, Inc., I increasingly became aware how badly self employed professionals are in need of an e-marketing manager at an affordable price. It is my goal to use a quality product, flexible pricing, and marketing innovation to help my clients grow their business and take advantage of their referral base.
