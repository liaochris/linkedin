JP Morgenthal’s Summary

JP Morgenthal is a world-renowned thought-leader and visionary in enterprise computing. He has over 25 years experience in designing and delivering mission-critical systems for business. JP has both breadth and depth in knowledge about IT issues and topics. His work is well-respected by the EA, EAI, XML, SOA, BPM and Cloud Computing communities. In addition to his technical acumen, JP also has acted in an executive role with P&L experience, twice heading up his own companies, twice as CTO/VP Engineering of startups and once as Chief Architect for a $500m software company. JP excels in technology leadership regardless if working in volunteer and salaried roles. He has held board level positions with IASA and CAEAP, both leading enterprise architecture advocacy organizations. The combination of skills enables JP to be an agile component of any product or services company, providing credibility and confidence to customers while helping to guide the business in a path of growth and opportunity.
