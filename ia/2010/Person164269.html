Phyllis Garland’s Summary

Phyllis Garland is a Silicon Valley, California website design and development specialist, who loves assisting clients with one-on-one service to achieve dynamic results. She helps small businesses establish a solid web presence, converting ideas into action and effectively tackling any technical challenge that comes her way. Phyllis works in both Microsoft and Mac platforms, and is comfortable with a variety of web design software applications, such as dreamweaver, javascript, flash, html. She also does press release writing, blog design and maintenance, online marketing, SEO optimization and social media strategy.

A mover and shaker, and technical guru from early on, Phyllis earned her degree in Computer Science, and gained invaluable experience in website development and network and administrative support through years of training in the corporate world and at prestigious start-ups. With over 13 years of combined website design, managerial, and administrative experience, Phyllis not only brings her expertise to her clients, but also her energy, creativity, and great sense of humor.

Phyllis is also an active member of many professional groups and keeps ups with the latest cutting-edge technology trends.

Known amongst her peers as a “go-to-gal,” Phyllis is not shy about taking on new challenges, as her experiences in running a marathon and earning her “wings” as a former flight attendant prove. Phyllis makes sure that all her clients reach the finish line with flying colors when they choose her as their website development company.

A mother of three, Phyllis enjoys spending her free time with her family, working on her handcrafted jewelry line “Phyllis Estella Original Designs,” digital photography, and reading.
