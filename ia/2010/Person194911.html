Steve Walker’s Summary

Ex Cirencester Agricultural College studying Estate Management, I went on to qualify as a Chartered Surveyor and Auctioneer. I made a career change post MRICS to run the valuations department of a national brokerage.

I gained professional grounding in the financial services sector with Midland and General Direct and Purpleloans from 1988 onwards. The organisation was a secured loan and mortgage brokerage which I ran from 1998 until 2002 taking it from a loss making position to annual pre tax profits of over £7.5million by 2002. The business was aquired by GE in 2001 and I left in 2003. As a consequence of an amicable depature from Purpleloans/GE, I formed Promise Solutions together with a team of Ex Purple employees.

The Promise Group is a technology led organisation which has developed its own award winning loan sourcing and sales platform for intermediaries plus internal application processing systems. This technology supports two of the group businesses, of which I am MD and principle owner, comprising a number of businesses offering services on both B to B and B to C basis.

These include Secured/unsecured Loan Brokerage and technology, Debt Management and IVA's, Financial focussed Contact Centre Services and the business also supports it's own charity, helping terminally and seriously ill children.

Steve Walker’s Specialties:

For many years I was a Director and eventually Chairman of the Corporation of Finance Brokers. Reacting to the changing market I led the winding down of the CFB and the creation of the Association of Finance Brokers and stood as Vice Chairman to Sir John Gummer for the first two years of the AFB's existence.
