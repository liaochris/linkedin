Graeme Brown’s Experience

  • Attorney

    The Brown Law Firm, LLC

    (Law Practice industry)

    May 2001 — Present (9 years 7 months)

    The Brown Law Firm, LLC was established in 2001 as the Law Offices of Graeme S.R. Brown to provide high quality, cost effective legal and business guidance to companies and individuals. Drawing on Attorney Brown’s significant experience in business and finance, the Firm provides practical advice and informed legal counsel. The Firm has clients throughout Massachusetts and the District of Columbia. Representative matters include: disputes related to contracts and business transactions; actions to recover damages for breach of an employment contract or for unpaid wages; actions to recover damages for sexual harassment and race discrimination; defense of various actions including employment discrimination; ethics compliance, including conflict of interest; and contracts between government agencies and private vendors. The firm strives to provide its clients with resourceful, intelligent and aggressive representation.
