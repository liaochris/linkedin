Steve Bookbinder’s Summary

Linking business requirements and talent, Steve Bookbinder provides solutions for improving organizational performance and the effectiveness of the workforce and leadership. Working with senior leaders, boards and line management for many years, he has acquired the deep experience and insights that enable him to create change and impact in many different types of organizations. Partnering with his clients, he offers new thinking and customized approaches to complex people-related problems on a project or on an over-the-shoulder basis.

Steve Bookbinder’s Specialties:

change management, workforce planning, team development, leadership and workforce effectiveness, employee research, coaching, succession planning, organizational design, performance management and improvement
