    Sun Microsystems, Inc.

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; SUNW; Computer Software industry)

    Founder and leader of Advanced Development group in the Sun ONE Communications division. This group was responsible for technology pathfinding - looking ahead 1-2 years for opportunities to provide competitive advantage through integration of new technology.

  • Chief Architect

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)
