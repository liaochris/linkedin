Jenn Mathews’s Summary

Over 10 years experience in search optimization (SEO) and Social Media Marketing (SMM) with a strong background in online marketing, user interface design, and web development having worked as the SEO Manager for Classmates.com, managing the Online Marketing SEO/SEM at Concur Technologies, and currently Director of Online Marketing at Winshuttle. Excellent verbal communication skills with an ability to work individually on a project or managing a team of up to 10 direct reports. Able to prioritize tasks in a fast-paced environment along with the ability to accept interruptions as part of the routine – am a self-starter with approach to work, an eagerness to consistently meet and exceed objectives and take on more responsibility.

Have been interviewed for various Search Marketing websites and blogs as well as major marketing publications including Marketing Sherpa.
Asked to speak at Search Engine Strategies Conferences, as well as moderating panels for the Search Insider Summits. Currently sit on the board for the Search Insider Summit.

My approach in a work environment is to operate as an individual contributor and step in as needed acting as a moderator or to help individuals come together to solve a problem. When managing I work with each direct report individually to help them obtain their goals effectively offering guidance where needed.
