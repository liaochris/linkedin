Luis J Perez, SCLA, AIC’s Summary

I have 10+ years experience in the legal liability insurance claims field with major corporations and seek to transition into the legal arena. The paralegal profession is similar to my previous role as a Liability Claim Examiner. Through comprehensive claims investigations, I have resolved premises liability, product liability, personal auto liability, and commercial liability claims. I have collaborated with staff and fee Defense Counsel to settle claims through mediation and negotiation. Analyzing and reviewing large amounts of information and discovery are critical to formulating strategies for case resolution. This skill arose from working closely with attorneys over many years. My competencies in time management and multi-tasking are also key characteristics that are valuable for the paralegal profession.

I have developed interpersonal skills that allow me to establish rapport with a large cross section of the community in a multitude of business settings. This ability is critical in achieving a high rate of success in mediations and resolving sensitive matters especially with difficult clients. I also have a reputation in the personal injury legal community for creating compelling arguments to persuade plaintiffs and their attorneys to agree to settlement, thereby bringing resolution to longstanding disputes.

My business experience together with my personal traits of integrity, maturity, dependability, and strong work ethic make me a unique candidate. I have excellent written communication skills, and I am fluent in Spanish. The Center for Advanced Legal Studies, an ABA approved program, has exceeded my expectations in preparing me to assume substantive legal duties with my future employer. I look forward to my new duties as a Certified Paralegal.
