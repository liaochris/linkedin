Jeffrey Meltzer’s Summary

Exobit Networks, Inc. (ENI) is a Long Island-based Network Services Provider, specializing in Internet connectivity, e-mail services, web design, and customized programming services. Our focus on professional, high-quality services and personalized customer support have distinguished us as one of Long Island's foremost Network Service Providers. Our client list includes many public & private school districts, universities, large corporations & small businesses, telecommunications carriers, as well as individual consumers.

LIFE solutions are designed and structured to significantly reduce costs while improving performance and increasing the services available to your organization. Customized to your needs, these solutions can be utilized in many ways providing flexibility not available from other providers.
