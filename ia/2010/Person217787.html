Carrie Corbin, MBA SPHR’s Summary

Open to ALL linkedin networking requests.

Follow me on twitter: @thealphafemme

A human resources generalist turned Talent Attraction, with 10+ years of corporate experience as the senior human resources professional in various organizations. A solid business partner with a proven ability to leverage HR & Staffing strategy with business results. Moonlighting as adjunct faculty teaching in both the business management and human resource management fields.

Accredited as a Senior Human Resource Professional (SPHR) by the National Society of Human Resources Management and formerly life and health licensed in the state of Oklahoma. MBA in Human Resource Management as well as a BS in Business Management with a social psychology emphasis.

Active in various networking and trade associations, including the Society for Human Resource Management, the Oklahoma City Metro Employers Council, as well as the Oklahoma City Human Resources Society.
