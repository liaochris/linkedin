David C. S. Lima Cohen’s Summary

I'm a self-taught programmer who started very young to develop applications and games just for the challenge of it (not even having my own computer at that time). Later on I started developing web sites, first for myself and later for customers (small/medium business and professionals). I took several jobs related to the computer industry, while I kept working on my own projects and connecting with new clients.

I'm "lucky" (I really don't believe in such thing) to now be in a very good position that allows me to do what I'm really passionate about: creating great and simple applications for everyone to use. That's my framework for each of my projects. My professional goal: working in a team of people that shares this vision, but with each one having their own set of skills and ways of doing things, so we can together make our projects come true benefiting from each other in the best collaborative sense.

Besides all this technical background, I define myself as an enthusiastic entrepreneur willing to challenge himself to new things all the time, so I'm open to any ideas anyone is willing to share.

Thanks for your time, have a nice day!
