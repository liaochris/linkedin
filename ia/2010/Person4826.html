Alan Nevel’s Summary

Alan is currently Director of Diversity & Inclusion for Limited Brands.

Alan’s primary focus is in leading the successful implementation of associate resource groups across the enterprise and leveraging diversity and inclusion education programs to raise awareness across the corporation. He is also responsible for helping to drive enterprise diversity and inclusion initiatives associated with talent, community & philanthropic involvement, and supplier diversity.
