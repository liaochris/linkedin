Andrew Heilman’s Summary

A sometimes storied history since graduating from the University of Maryland in College Park, Andrew Heilman has held such titles as staff photographer, youth leadership ambassador, communications and events coordinator, assistant account executive for PR and success partner at various organizations and businesses throughout northern Nevada. He currently holds the title of President and owner of Xpositioning, a new media and marketing firm that works with professionals to develop their personal networks and establish themselves as experts in their chosen field.
