Sally Falkow’s Summary

Since 1999 I've been fascinated with the possibilities of communicating online.I was working with Universities in the UK on a distance education program and they had an online component that was amazing!

In the next few years I transferred my knowledge to the Internet and now work mainly in that arena as a web content strategist and social media specialist.

One of my long term interests is helping other PR people learn more about the profession. I am an APR and I've been teaching PR classes for 15 years. Now I write the Proactive Report as a guide to social media, I teach classes on social media for PR agencies and in-house on how to integrate social media with a traditional PR strategy.

I'm also very interested in consumer and buyer behavior and how this has changed online. I love research studies about how social media and search is influencing how w communicate with each other and with businesses.

I am a Senior Research Fellow with the Society for New Communication Research and have authored books on PR, Branding and Web Strategies.

I was recently voted the best presenter in social media and technology for the Bulldog Reporter and PR University seminars and conferences.

I blog about these subjects at The Proactive Report

Sally Falkow’s Specialties:

I have been in the marketing and public relations industry for over 25 years. I write and speak at conferences on Internet PR and Internet Marketing Strategies, blogs and RSS feeds as a Public Relations tool.

I co-developed a news feed and social media tool called PRESSfeed http://www.press-feed.com.




At Expansion Plus we specilize in content syndication as an online PR and social media strategy using RSS feeds, optimized press releases and articles.

Sally Falkow’s Interests:

The shift in media consumption and how the Internet is changing the way we communicate, access information and make decisions in both our personal and business lives is one of my major interests. How people change and react to technology affects the way they purchase, which in turn affects the way we do business. This is the focus of The Proactive Report, an online resource for PR and marketing professionals. I learn more about the Internet and new communication technology every day. I am interested in being a change agent and bringing more people into communication across the world. I'd like more people to become aware and investigate the world around them, so that they can think for themselves and make up their own minds about information. I also have a very strong interest in education and handling the illiteracy rate around the world.
