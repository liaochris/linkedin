Vadim Lavrusik’s Summary

I enjoy writing and reporting, but specialize in online journalism and social media. I worked as a reporter, managing editor and editor-in-chief for four years at The Minnesota Daily, fourth largest newspaper in Minnesota. I have also written for the Minneapolis/St. Paul Business Journal, Mashable.com, OnlineJournalismBlog.com, Poynter.org and the Star Tribune.

I worked to re-design Mndaily.com with the Minnesota Daily online staff and increased Web production. I also directed new Web enterprises to the site, including: blogs, multimedia, forums, Campus Tweets, the Daily Facebook application, Twitter account and more. My interests are in digital media and how best to utilize social media to reach audiences. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Digital Media at the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism.
