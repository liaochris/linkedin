Rob Harrigan’s Summary

I have been solving visual problems and creating impactful design for over 7 years. I am the perfect fit for any creative group passionate about creating beautiful design and shaping everything around them. I have an extensive background in art direction and design working with clients ranging from mom–and–pops to Fortune 500 companies. After I established a firm background in animation design, motion graphics and design systems, I was promoted to Art Director in 2009. I have since established a track record of delivering creative, emotive, and tactical solutions to meet my clients’ needs.

Rob Harrigan’s Specialties:

Typography, print production, hand-rendered type, project management, art direction, strategic advertising, branding and identity exploratories.
