Rory Batchilder’s Summary

Certified experiential adult educator and counselling therapist. Seven years experience in mental health and wellness, individual therapy, adult education, and event facilitation. Service oriented with six years of board experience. Formerly served as an officer in the Canadian Navy.

In a northern aboriginal community treated clients, worked at the treatment lodge, and trained safe house staff. As a college instructor developed courses, created a positive educational environment and evaluated students' progress. Strengths in group work, computer skills, creative thinking, experiential education, mind-body approaches, mediation. Improved peer communications and service programs in several complex environments.

Rory Batchilder’s Specialties:

Working as a leader within a team environment. Communications systems and group facilitation. Mind-body approaches, experiential education, complex conflict environments, team building.
