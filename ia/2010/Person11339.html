Andrei Iancu’s Summary

Entrepreneur with over 17 years of experience in the computer industry, constantly pushing the leading edge of high-tech. With a thorough understanding of technical issues and a clear perception of the big picture in business and in the market, built and managed development teams to create new innovative products and technologies.

Mentor, trainer, and enabler that educated in computing technologies over 300 undergrad and continuing education students at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, and hundreds at the Economic Studies Academy in Bucharest. Currently teaching iPhone application development at BCIT in Vancouver.

A good grasp of cross-cultural international business issues after working both in North America and Eastern Europe, and with teams and customers in UK, US, and Japan.
