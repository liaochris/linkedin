Sarah Morris Smith’s Summary

Sarah ‘Mo’ Smith has over 10 years of high-tech, IT Recruiting experience hiring for Fortune 500 Companies on a national level. Sarah has worked with companies such as Microsoft, IMB, Lucent, Lotus, HP, Compaq, Lockheed Martin, BAE Systems, Kodak, EMC, and FedEx. She has an Advanced Online Recruiting Techniques Certificate and is currently pursuing an advanced SEO certificate. Sarah has been using the internet and online tools (beyond the big job boards) to find candidates and clients since 1998.

Sarah has the proven ability and experience to find the ‘purple squirrel’, ‘needle in the haystack’ and while she hasn't actually sold ice to Eskimos' it’s only because she hasn’t tried.

With a strong sales and recruiting background Sarah is currently building a team for a new venture with the Trump Network.

Sarah is an open networker and accepts all connection requests (sarah @ sarahmosmith . com)
