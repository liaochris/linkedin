Simeon Simeonov’s Summary

Simeon Simeonov is the founder of @FastIgnite where he helps entrepreneurs shape promising ideas, raise capital, build teams and execute across all stages of the startup lifecycle.

Sim is always looking for great entrepreneurs who want to change the world.

Sim co-founded Thing Labs, Tollbit and The Better Advertising Project. Before FastIgnite, Sim was a VC at Polaris Venture Partners.

Sim blogs at blog.simeonov.com and lives in the Greater Boston area with his wife, son and an adopted dog named Tye. Follow @simeons to stay in touch.

Simeon Simeonov’s Specialties:

Guiding early-stage startups from concept through fundraising to revenue and enabling later-stage companies to achieve their potential. Building great teams. Not re-inventing the wheel.
