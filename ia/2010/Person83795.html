Ian Hannigan’s Summary

Ian has worked for over a decade across a range of industry sectors including product design, software interaction and usability design, graphics, film & TV.

Ian works for Rebellion Games based in their Derby UK office (formerly Core Design) working on current-gen projects for all major platforms. Prior to joining Rebellion Ian worked as Lead Game Designer with Nephin Games.

Ian holds a BA Honors Degree in Design from the University of Ulster.
