Gene De Libero’s Summary

25 years getting my hands dirty building businesses in the technology and digital/interactive media spaces for myself and with others. From early digital start-ups in the 80's to working with one of the largest media company's in the world today, I've had (and continue to have) fun innovating and riding the crest of change.

Enjoyed lots of great project work with some incredibly smart people and have had a number of turns as a senior business strategy, interactive marketing and technology exec with public and privately held organizations in various verticals.

Instructor at NYU's School of Continuing and Professional Studies since 2000. Currently teaching platform and online classes in the Certificate in Digital Media Marketing program; "Digital Strategy: Creating Synergy in Your Marketing Program", and "Narrowcasting: Using Digital Media for Selective Marketing and Advertising."

Co-author of "Strategic Networking: From LAN and WAN to Information Superhighways" in 1996. The book was a definitive text about the Internet and World Wide Web and using computer networks as a tool for learning, communication and e-commerce. Finally making time to work on a couple of new writing projects; an update to my first book, "Strategic Networking" as well as a new book on the topic of targeted digital marketing.

Gene De Libero’s Specialties:

Interactive marketing and technology innovator with a propensity for discovering and solving problems many others can't see. Business leader and digital tech dork with a deep knowledge of competitors, trends, and market conditions across a wide variety of industries. Broad understanding of digital channels and related tools including content management tools, e-commerce engines, ad serving platforms, profiling and personalization systems and social/community and customer service applications.
