Simon Salt’s Summary

CEO of International IncSlingers. He is a co-founder of the company. He has been recognized for his ability to provide strategic marketing guidance that covers both Traditional Marketing communications & Social Media. He is the author of popular blog "the IncSlinger" located at www.theincslingers.com/blog.

He holds Associate degrees in Business & Finance and Information Technology, a Bachelor of Science in Behavioral Science with a major in Anthropology, and a Masters in Information Systems with a focus on Logic, Text & Information Technology.

He has previously worked in the UK for an e-business consultancy, Company Net, where he started as web-developer & Project manager and rose to become the Head of Business Development, where he pioneered a partnership with Microsoft and created a first of their kind series of new technology briefings for businesses in Scotland.

Following Company Net he joined eClerx an India based outsourcing organization as Associate Principal, Client Relations. Having moved to the US, he captured the company’s first Fortune 100 account and grew annual revenues exponentially.

His consultancy experience spans providing Fortune 500 companies with guidance for their online marketing strategy and leveraging online shopping behavior metrics, startups in Latin America looking to break into the US & European markets.

Outside of work he currently serves as the Director of Technology for the Austin Chapter of the American Marketing Association.
He has a strong community focus and is an instructor for Volunteer Search & Rescue teams and is a FEMA certified Community Emergency Response Team trainer.
