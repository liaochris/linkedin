Dan Miller’s Summary

EVP, Business Strategy - INgage Networks f/k/a Neighborhood America. INgage Networks helps customers build Business Networks that deliver positive business results.

CEO - MOVO Mobile, LLC . MOVO provides on-demand interactive mobile marketing and mobile social networking solutions to organizations that want to extend their advertising, marketing and outreach efforts to the public through mobile phones and PDA’s.

Founder - BizTank Ventures is a privately held firm that focuses on providing advisory, strategy and capital formation services to high-growth entrepreneurial businesses.

Managing Director, Startup Florida Ventures - Startup Florida promotes the creation and acceleration of technology businesses by providing capital, mentoring and a supportive community for entrepreneurs. Our early-stage venture funds serve to connect entrepreneurs with active angel investors throughout the State of Florida.

Founder and President - PlanetResume.com was an early pioneer within the on-line recruiting industry. PlanetResume.com grew from a start-up in 1994 to a thriving, profitable company with regional offices in Boston, Washington DC, Charlotte, and San Francisco.

Dan successfully completed the sale of PlanetResume.com to CareerShop.com in October, 1999, and the sale of CareerShop.com to Personnel Group of America (NYSE:PGA)in June 2000.

Senior Vice President of Sales & Marketing - Software House, Inc., a leading provider of facility access control security systems sold worldwide to Fortune 500 and governmental customers. In the summer of 1994, Dan consummated the sale of Software House to Sensormatic Corp. (NYSE:SRM).

Dan lives in Sarasota, FL with his wife, Nelle, and their son Henry. He is a Tae-Kwon-Do Karate Instructor, enjoys tennis, soccer, reading, and beach vacations.
