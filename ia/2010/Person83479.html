Hussein Ahmed’s Summary

More than 9 years of experience developing web solutions ranging from very small projects to full fledged enterprise scale projects. Experience in analyzing complicated systems to get a solid database design and going up to the architectural design and the top application layers.

With the ever changing web technologies, I have delivered projects in Java, PHP, ASP and Web 2.0 applications.

From the project management perspective, I had the privilege of managing a small team of developers and designers going from concept to product delivery and maintenance.

In addition to the industrial experience, I have been teaching in higher education for around 7 years. Starting as a Teaching Assistant, then promoted to an Assistant Lecturer after getting my Master Degree in Computer Engineering. Working for the Arab Academy in Egypt, as an entity of the Arab League, I have been dealing with a vast diversity of students. Throughout this period, I was well known to deliver the information well matching the student level without compromising the curriculum objectives.
