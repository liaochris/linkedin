Rod Long’s Summary

Four plus years connecting talented professionals from the military with great careers in the upstream oil & gas industry. Total of seven years in the energy industry from land rigs to industrial facilities. Scope of experience includes hands-on operations experience in remote locations to technical meetings with senior level, energy industry decision-makers. Keenly focused on creating true Win-Win situations in any setting and take great personal pride in helping others achieve their professional goals.

Additionally, have over twenty years of combined active duty and reserve service in the US Marine Corps where Honor, Courage & Commitment is the ethos and where integrity and sacrifice for others is the accepted norm. Strive to apply those lessons in daily life and enjoy building long-lasting partnerships based on trust with all who initially start as clients.
