Hal Rosen, CPA’s Summary

hrosen@halrosencpa.com

I started with a “Big Eight” international accounting firm. I’ve also held a partnership position with a local CPA firm, as well as financial and executive positions with companies in numerous industries.

I’ve spent a third of my career as an entrepreneur myself, involved in day-to-day operations of small companies. I've co-founded more than twelve companies in various industries, including manufacturing, coal mining, tool and mine supply distribution, real estate development and financing, employee benefits, and a travel agency.

They’ve given me the real-world, “hands on” experience to help you in today’s difficult economy. Why trust your business with an advisor who’s never faced the same challenges as you – from the same perspective as you?

I’m a firm believer in properly accounting for business activities. I’ve used QuickBooks since it was first introduced as a DOS based program in the early 1990’s. I’m an Advanced QuickBooks Certified Pro Advisor and I’ve taught classes on how to use the system to help make smart business decisions.

How can this work for you? I recently helped one company restructure three years of their QuickBooks data to properly reflect their operations. After completing the accounting we amended the three years of tax returns and they received approximately $75,000 in refunds to the two owners.

In another company a year earlier we did the same thing and the owner received about $45,000 in refunds. Both companies had their tax returns prepared by CPA's, but in both cases the CPA did not adequately understand QuickBooks and in both cases they also made glaring errors in the tax returns.

We have the experience and knowledge to help you run your small business.

    Do You Want More Than "Just" an Accountant?

    If you run your own business, you how know important it is to find the right business financial partner. You know how important an accountant can be. But not all accountants are created equal!

    Most accountants do a fine job recording the history you give them. They compile monthly, quarterly, and annual reports. At tax time, they put the "right" numbers in the right boxes on the right forms. But then they call it a day.

    At my firm, we're different. We don't just record history. We help you write it, with a proactive attitude and complete menu of tax and accounting services.

    It doesn’t matter how good your accountant is with a stack of receipts on April 15.

    To minimize your taxes, you need a plan. You need concepts and strategies that leave more on your bottom line.

    Have you organized your business right?
    Do you have the right benefit plans for yourself and your employees?
    Do you have the right retirement plan to prepare for your future?
