PMP Project Manager & Senior Business Systems Analyst


As a long time Rochester, NY area resident, I have a strong sense of pride in our community. I currently chair The Employment Network, assisting others like myself in strengthening the Rochester work force and building business back up in Rochester.

Over the years I have worked for several major Rochester employers as a project manager and senior business systems analyst with experience in the application and infrastructure side of information technology.

I enjoy working with businesses in a team environment. I am a life long learner and a dedicated worker that will get any job done.

As IT/Telecom Project Manager for Center Information Services, Inc., I manage projects, large and small, for many non-profit agencies in the Rochester, NY area. For the University of Rochester, I was a key project manager, coach, and mentor for the Data Center Relocation effort. At Kodak, I served as project manager of an initiative that saved the company over $78,000 annually in support costs by implementing a SOX compliant sales compensation application. I improved work processes and managed the work load for Sun Microsystems at Frontier Communications.
