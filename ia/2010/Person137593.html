Matthew Stephens’s Summary

I started getting into design and UX back in 1999, when I created deviantART.com and found that creating personal online experiences was my passion. Through design and implementation, I have created hundreds of sites and applications that have helped shape my unique insight into how people think and react to the web.

I am always looking for new opportunities to share my thoughts and creative solutions so message me if you would like to chat.

Matthew Stephens’s Specialties:

Designing the entire user experience, from user surveys and usability studies, mood boards, wireframing, mock ups, HTML, CSS, jQuery, etc.
