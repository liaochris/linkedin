Ramesh Dewangan’s Summary

Ramesh has 20+ years of experience in the EDA industry in the areas of Engineering, Application Engineering and Product marketing related to RTL to GDSII design automation.

Currently at Atrenta, Ramesh manages SpyGlass product line at Atrenta Inc as Sr. Director, Product Marketing. Prior to this, he has held key positions at Cadence Design Systems, Tera Systems, Synopsys and Texas Instruments.

His objective is to utilize proven product management skills as part of Product Marketing, Application Engineering or Higher Management of a technoology company, especially in a multi-site environment.

Ramesh Dewangan’s Specialties:

IC Design Automation, Semiconductor Design, Product Management, International Operations Management, People management, Customer relationship management, Public speaking, Community leadership
