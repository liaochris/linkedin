Vice Božić’s Summary

Eight years advertising, communications and digital experience in senior roles within leading multi national full service and specialist digital agencies, unique integrated communications perspective from having worked on both the creative and media sides of the business.

Extensive knowledge in all aspects of digital marketing including strategic planning, digital consultancy, digital integrations with traditional media, web design and development, search, display, mobile, social media, gaming, analytics and CRM.

Worked on a number of award-winning digital and integrated campaigns for some of the leading global and local brands such as Unilever, Fujitsu Siemens, Adidas, Visa, MTV, Wrigley, Podravka, T-Com and Toyota, among others.

Active member of Croatian online community, member of IAB Croatia, conference speaker and a frequent commentator in the press on the subject of digital marketing and online media.
