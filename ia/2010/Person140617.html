Melissa Bellomo’s Summary

I am currently the Director of Membership at Inverness Club. Inverness Club was designed by the renowned architect, Donald Ross. Our Club is currently rated 2nd in Ohio and 40th in the USA. Inverness has been the site of eight major championships and will soon add the 2011 Senior Open to the list.

I am working everyday to ensure that rich traditions, coupled with first class service and amenities, create the welcoming atmosphere Inverness Club members experience and appreciate everyday.
