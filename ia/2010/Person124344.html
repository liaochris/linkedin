Lesly Cardec’s Summary

Public Relations Manager for SFN Group, Inc., the 5th largest U.S.-based staffing firm (formerly Spherion Corporation) and its family of specialty businesses, including: Technisource, Mergis Group, Provali Group, SourceRight Solutions, Spherion Staffing Services and Todays Office Professionals.

Direct responsibility for strategic cohesiveness of all external communication for all brands. This includes: planning, coordinating and executing all public relations activities, writing and distributing company news, building and maintaining strong relationships with national, trade, and local press, acting as the primary company spokesperson in crisis situations. Owner of all thought leadership programs. Responsible for SFN Group's social media Twitter strategy.

In addition, provide media counsel to senior executives on messaging and work very closely with the Marketing Director to execute various corporate-wide initiatives.
