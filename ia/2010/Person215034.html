William Hermany’s Summary

User Experience Designer, Information Architect, User Interaction Designer, Design Technologist and Strategist with strong usability and graphic design background. Particularly interested in innovating and developing products and services that are holistic and have cross-platform interactions (i.e., desktop to mobile environments). I enjoy working on projects that allow seamless, holistic user experiences. Designing and branding for the large screen as well as the small screen. XHTML, AJAX, DHTML, CHTML, CSS, WML and JavaScript. Ability to Team Lead.

Currently researching, designing and developing mini prototypes for a multi-touch groupware environment, and continuing to explore next-gen advertising models. Incorporating new knowledge around Semantic Web.
