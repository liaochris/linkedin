Alok Bhardwaj’s Summary

I have been in IT industry for more than 6 years and have a PeopleSoft Administration, DBA and architecture expertise for more than 5 years. Experience of full life cycle of PeopleSoft ERP implementation in the business area of HRMS and Global Payroll. During my various assignments, I had been involved starting from implementation phases like requirement gathering, architecture, integrations, go-live phases, maintenance and support to the business users and solving technical issues.

-- Experience in all phases of PeopleSoft implementation, technical architecture, upgrade, conversion and Maintenance project life cycles.

-- Expertise in PeopleTools components like database, application server, process scheduler, web server and underlying technologies.

-- Experience in installation and configuration of PeopleTools and PeopleSoft application, performance tuning (technical app server, web server, DB, scheduler etc) and functional (GP, WFA etc).

-- Experience in Oracle 9i/10G/11G database administration (Hot/Cold backup, cloning, maintenance etc) and SQL Server.

-- Knowledge of Informatica (ETL), ImageNow
(Documentation software) and Business Objects (Business Intelligence tool).

-- Functional experience in full life cycle in the areas of requirement gathering, design, Fit/Gap-analysis, Implementation, Functional specification, Upgrade, Production Support, Test scripts, testing and User Training of PeopleSoft HRMS Application.

-- Experience in PeopleSoft HCM modules which comprises of Talent Acquisition Management (TAM), Workforce Administration (WFA), Global Payroll (GP - India Extension), e-Recruitment, employee self services(ESS) and manager self service (MSS) modules.

-- Planning and Implementing Post-Production support.

-- Experience in end user Support for TAM, WFA and GP modules like handling issues with job openings/postings, employee/departments, security in WFA, PIN Packager, Payroll processing, rule, non-rule packages in GP etc.
