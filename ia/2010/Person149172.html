Michael Perry’s Summary

I was in the newspaper business for 25 years. Much of that time was spent in Sports as a copy editor, pager designer, reporter (mostly college basketball) and editor. I am a former sports editor at The Cincinnati Enquirer.

In 2006, I moved into non-daily products and new initiatives. I was involved in much of the organization's strategic planning and worked on content plans for magazines, books and Web sites as Enquirer Media expanded its menu of products. I developed executive summaries, P&Ls and was project manager for the rollout of several new initiatives. During that time, I worked closely with marketing, advertising and online development departments.

I also started my own company in 2008 (Pone Press) in order to self-publish "Xavier Tales: Great Stories from Musketeers Basketball" -- the second book I wrote. I also authored "Tales from Cincinnati Bearcats Basketball" in 2004 for Sports Publishing LLC. I've contributed to two other books and been contributing editor for four books.

Michael Perry’s Specialties:

Project management, staff and budget management, writing, editing, audience development, strategic planning, teaching/coaching/mentoring, marketing, social media, creativity, teamwork and collaboration.
