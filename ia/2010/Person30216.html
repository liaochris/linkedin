Bryan Taylor’s Summary

In addition to several years in web design and programming, I have spent the last 5+ years programming PHP/MYSQL, database design and management, SQL design and interactive user experience. As a part of these responsibilities I have worked extensively with social marketing concepts and planning and coordination.
