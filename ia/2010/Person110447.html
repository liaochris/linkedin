Karen Annarino’s Education

    Stephen's College allowed me to find my voice. The opportunities gave me the security and the confidence to become a better performer, a stronger director, and a more confident individual. The support system allowed me to fail in order to learn to trust my creative instincts. I was shown what collaboration meant to the success of a vision, and it marked my path in the theatre with new extended family. I still enjoy the art of collaboration, to work together to achieve a goal. Stephen's a historic, private, liberal arts college geared towards the education of women. I feel today that this camaraderie amongst students provided a solid belief system that has followed me to this day, to know and believe that anything is possible, and that in the most difficult of situations or in the darkest of days, the show must go on.
