Bret Berneche’s Summary

Bret A. Berneche has been involved in the building and manufacturing of Building Systems since 1971. During that time he gained first hand knowledge and experience in all aspects of the business including production, delivery, installation, drafting, sales and service, seeing each process from the perspectives of line-worker, builder and executive. At the executive level Bret has served in various Vice-President and President roles including the start-up and operation of a building division and the acquisition and operation of a modular manufacturing company.

Mr. Berneche has served the Building Systems industry associations as President of the Virginia Industrialized Housing Association, President of the Modular Building Systems Council, Chairman of the Building Systems Councils of NAHB and as a member of the Executive Committee of the National Home Builders Association.

Mr. Berneche also serves as a Trustee on the Board of Halifax Health Systems (since 1994); chaired the Halifax Physician-Hospital Organization: serves as a Director on the Board of Raffles Insurance, Ltd. (since 2002).
