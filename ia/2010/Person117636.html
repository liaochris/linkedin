Kevin-James Bennett’s Summary

Multiple Emmy Award winner Kevin-James Bennett has spent over 20 years exploring and mastering the multi-faceted world of makeup. From backstage to boardroom, he has honed the skills that have brought him accolades from industry peers as well as viewers of his work in television, film and print.

KJ (an admitted "Product Junkie") posses an impressive array of professional skills and is regarded as an industry expert and cosmetic reference source. After a highly successful career in the entertainment industry, he has returned to the corporate sector as Director of Business Development & Artist Relations for MAKE UP FOR EVER where he looks forward to maintaining a strong voice in the evolution of the cosmetic industry.

Kevin-James Bennett’s Specialties:

* Makeup Design (including SFX) for Film, Video and Print.
* Cosmetic Concept, Design and Development.
* Brand Marketing, Visual Identity and Art Direction.
* Makeup Education, Lectures, Seminars & Workshops.
* Beauty Editorial, Blogs and Product Review.
