Jantima Danford’s Summary

Eighteen years experience as a Convention Organizer managing international convention and exhibition in several specialties and assist in all aspects of the convention and exhibition industry. Offered full-service organization for convention and exhibition of any size and type. Planned, programmed, budgeted and directed the acquisition of conventions and exhibitions involving millions of dollars, thousands of people, and state-of-the-art technical equipment in coordination with both civilian and government agencies in Thailand and other countries. Managed staff, payroll, office management, equipment procurement, and financial matters. As project manager, wrote proposals, solicited International Convention Projects, and developed concept for conventions, authored bid and requisitioned projects on competitive basis. Handled all financial/banking/quotes for services. Produced high quality intro packages for each convention. Represented Thailand internationally.
