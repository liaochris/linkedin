    To be really effective, a public relations campaign should consist of a variety of marketing and public relations activities that all work in concert to promote visibility. The My PR Tools products, available at www.myprtools.com, include:

    Personalized PR Coaching teaches small business owners how to create and carry out their own marketing/ PR campaigns. An affordable alternative to high-priced agencies, this easy to follow step-by-step program is designed to help small companies and entrepreneurs stand out in their target market and increase sales.

    My PR Tools Handbook, the definitive guide to marketing and public relations, clearly lays out a step-by-step campaign to raise visibility and increase sales. The handbook shows companies how to gain media exposure; maximize their Web presence; create print and electronic marketing campaigns that make an impact; host successful client events; become prominent in the right business organizations, and much more.

    My PR Tools Media Guide details how to get exposure where it really counts, in the key print and online news sources that the target market depends on. This is the complete guide to understanding what makes news; creating targeted media lists; writing powerful press releases, articles and guest columns; the secrets of pitching to the media; and developing a comprehensive media kit.

    My PR Tools Resource Guide is an all-inclusive list of services and vendors to help companies carry out a public relations plan.
