Mario Grimes’s Summary

As of February 2007, I have been working for General Dynamics Information Technology, Army Solution Division, contracted to the Fort Eustis, Virginia, NCO Academy as a Functional Analyst to the Convoy Leadership Decision Trainer (CLDT) Simulator. Using the virtual gaming environment of the CLDT, I train NCOA students to be better Convoy Commanders, giving them the opportunity to make decisions and to respond to ambush situations, counter improvised explosive devices (C-IEDs) and other situational awareness exercises. “Subject-Matter-Expert” (SME) on the Force XXI Battle Command Brigade-and-Below (FBCB2) and Blue Force Tracking (BFT) system and teaches how to communicate within a convoy using the FBCB2. I also developed the Blackboard Suite for the NCO Academy to automate testing and grading for all BNCOC and ANCOC courses done from a single website.

As of October of 2009, been promoted to Training Specalist and upgraded the gaming software to Virtual Battlespace 2 (VBS2) integarated with FBCB2 using "SIMC4I Interchange Module for Plans, Logistics, and Exercises" (SIMPLE), MySQL Database and Distributed Interactive Simulation (DIS) software.

Strong written/verbal communication skills, critical thinking, problem solving and troubleshooting skills.

Security Clearance: active cleaerance on record
