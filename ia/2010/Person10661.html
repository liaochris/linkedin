Ana Pejcinova’s Summary

Experienced communications & PR manager in development in post-conflict environment. Strong scholarly background. Specializes in technical reports, analysis, publications, social media, and graphic design. Advanced software skills.

Worked with a variety of international development agencies, including USAID, UNDP, IOM, UNOPS and UNAMA - three of these contracts in Afghanistan, and one in Iraq.

Post-graduate degrees in two faculties: a PhD in Comparatistics and an MA in Political Science, the latter focused on a published thesis “Afghanistan: Creation of a Warlord Democracy.”
