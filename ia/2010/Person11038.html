Andrea Kovac’s Summary

Andrea Kovac is the Alumni Relations Coordinator for the College of Communication Arts and Sciences at Michigan State University and is part of the CAS Advancement Office. She works directly with the CAS Alumni Association and Board, coordinates alumni events and programs, and assists with the college's communication efforts. Prior to joining the team at CAS, Andrea worked for the Michigan State Senate and Teach for America. She earned her bachelor's degree in English and her master's degree in public relations from Michigan State University.
