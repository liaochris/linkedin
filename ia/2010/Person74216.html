Georg Burwick’s Summary

Currently Georg Burwick is the Director of Digital Media for UCR ARTSblock, which is the umbrella institution for the UCR/California Museum of Photography, The Sweeney Art Gallery, and the future (2010) Culver Center of the Arts. Burwick has twelve years of fine and applied arts experience and has focused on digital video and new media for the past six years. Burwick received his B.A. from Pitzer College and his M.F.A. from the University of Chicago where he specialized in digital video and new media. He has taught Sculpture and Art Appreciation in Phoenix and Chicago and was the Curator of Digital Media at the UCR/California Museum of Photography where he headed the Museum’s Digital Studio, a community imaging and technology center.

Georg Burwick’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge of video and audio editing utilizing Media 100, Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio Pro, Cubase, Soundtrack, Shake, Motion. Intermediate knowledge of Adobe Premiere; command of DVD authoring methods and encoding. Advanced command of digital imaging software including programs such as Adobe CS4. Advanced understanding of Director MX, MS Office, FileMaker Suite. Languages include are Lingo, (X)HTML, PHP, LDML, ODMC, Lasso, JavaScript, AppleScript, and essential Unix, XML and MySQL.
