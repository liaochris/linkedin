Adrienne Graham’s Summary

Please DO NOT send me your resume or inquire about job openings. I am on hiatus from recruiting.

You only have the limitations you place on yourself! I prefer not to have limitations- self imposed or otherwise.

Go Ahead, Talk to Strangers- The Modern Girl's Guide to Fearless Networking- http://www.goaheadtalktostrangers.com

Views From the Top- http://www.blogtalkradio.com/viewsfromthetop

Adrienne Graham is the Founder and CEO of Empower Me! Corporation, a Media, Publishing and Professional Networking company for women. Known as a power relationship broker, she created and leads the Empowered Black Women's Network, Empowered Latinas Network, and Empowered Career Woman. In addition to the networks, the she created Empower Me! Seminars, Empower Me! Careers, Views From the Top Radio Show, Lunchtime Strategies, a series of books, CDs, DVDs, podcasts, seminars, webinars, boot camps, the Fearless Networking- Without Asking Permission™ series & Power Brok(H)er™ Club. She also offers career, social media branding and networking coaching and consulting services. Fearless Woman Magazine & Fearless Woman Internet TV will debut in January 2010.

Graham is also the CEO of Hues Consulting & Management Inc; an international Diversity Recruitment Consulting firm based in Atlanta, GA.

She holds memberships in Society for Human Resources Management, National Association for Female Executives, the International Telework Association & Council, American Society for Training & Development, National Career Development Association, Society of Industry Leaders, and League of Black Women. She is a mom to an entrepreneurial18-year-old college son, president of an investment club, and active mentor.
