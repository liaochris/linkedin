Bill Garner’s Summary

Bill was born and raised in Augusta, Georgia, where he went on to graduate from Augusta State University. While in college, Bill served as a youth ministry intern for Young Life for three years.

In 1996, Bill began working as Head Counselor for Boys at Camp Mac, a summer camp for boys and girls located near Cheaha Mountain in Munford, Alabama. Bill still enjoys spending his summers "Among the Pines" and is very thankful for his friendship with his Camp Mac family.

In 1997, Bill began his teaching career by returning to the elementary school where he grew up. He taught fifth grade for four years at Episcopal Day School (EDS). In 2001, Bill relocated to Birmingham, Alabama, and began teaching Sixth Grade at Crestline Elementary in Mountain Brook. In addition to teaching, Bill has been a volunteer football coach for Mountain Brook Athletics.

In 2005, Coach Zach Skipper and Bill Garner founded a new ministry in Birmingham called Bigtime Ministries. Bigtime is the realization of a dream for Bill. The mission of Bigtime is to lead students to Christ and coach them as ministers in their school. Bill is excited about what is going on in the lives of students in Mountain Brook. He is an active member of Church of the Highlands in Birmingham, Alabama.

Bill is working with a group of Mastermind Leaders across the globe for an exciting business opportunity with a company called World Ventures. One of Bill's passions is to help establish integrity and credibility to the MLM industry. We are adding high quality, success-oriented people to our team every day.
