Charles Moster’s Summary

I co-founded MosterWynne in 1996 which serves as a general counsel to businesses in Austin, Texas, and nationwide. The Firm is known for its creative approach to law and business and success in offering multifaceted services including IP, Patents, Trademarks, Litigation, and Corporate Law. Go to www.mosterwynne.com

Also actively involved as a playwright, composer, and artist having written four musical plays produced and broadcast on National Public Radio (2006-08). My latest venture is a musical about Texas stripper Candy Barr which was co-written with Alice Adams with arrangements by Adam Donmoyer. For information go to www.cottoncandycreative.com. Candy Barr was named one of Texas' most prominent women by Texas Monthly.
