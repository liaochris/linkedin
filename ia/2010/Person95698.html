Jennifer Cain’s Summary

Jennifer Cain (Yang) is currently the Advertising Operations Manager at Gale | Cengage Learning (formerly HighBeam Research) and has over 6 years of experience in the online advertising industry. She currently manages the advertising operations across 6+ sites. She has worked both on the agency side (iProspect & The Netcom Group) as well as on the publisher side (MIT Technology Review, Advance Internet, and Gale) of online advertising. At iProspect and The Netcom Group, Jennifer managed direct clients of various industries and publishers in search marketing, mobile marketing, affiliate marketing, and display advertising. At MIT Technology Review, Advance Internet and Gale Open Web Markets, Jennifer managed online advertising campaigns for agencies and top spending Fortune 500 clients and also ad networks. In her spare time, Jennifer mentors new graduates from local universities and plans activities with her alma maters Loyola University New Orleans and Ben Franklin High School. She is fluent in Chinese Mandarin and loves traveling and cooking.
