Peter Donaldson’s Summary

I have 25 years of experience as a journalist and writer of features, news and comment and as an editor of publications including bimonthly and quarterly magazines and annual directories covering the aerospace and defence industries.

I have built a reputation for delivering accurate, insightful and entertaining articles on a broad range of complex technical, operational and business subjects.

I have also been involved in shaping conferences on civil and military helicopters, electronic warfare systems, night vision systems, search and rescue. I have also acted as programme consultant for Shephard's Night Vision conference.

Peter Donaldson’s Specialties:

Subjects I have written about in depth include: engines and power transmission systems for helicopters, fixed-wing aircraft, unmanned vehicles of all kinds, ships, submarines and ground vehicles. I have also covered electronics and mission management systems for these vehicles, military command, control, communications, intelligence, surveillance, targeting and reconnaissance systems in addition to guided weapons, guns and ammunition. I have also covered civil helicopter operations.
