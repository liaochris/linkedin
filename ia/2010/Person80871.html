Heather Decker’s Summary

I'm currently working to obtain my MFA in Interactive Design and Game Development through SCAD. Previously, I graduated from AIPOD with a BS in Game Art and Design. I also completed an AAS in Graphic Design at a local community college, where I was the tutor for the program and the work study lab monitor. Since then, I've been pursuing freelance/contract work.

Soft Skills: communication, cooperation, leadership, integrity, perseverance, and a positive attitude!

Bonus: I'm a self-starter who's not afraid of anything. If I can look it up and read a little about it, I'm good to go!

Heather Decker’s Specialties:

Short version: Jill-of-All-Trades tech/art hybrid.

I create both digital and analog art. I'm flexible enough to work in nearly any medium requested of me.

Areas of focus: technical art, environment art, Flash animation and game prototypes, project management, planning and documentation.
