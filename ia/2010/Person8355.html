Alok Garg’s Summary

Alok is an entrepreneur, market maker and an independent thought leader with global experience across Telecommunications, Mobility and Financial Services industries. Alok brings twenty years of business & technology leadership experience with key strengths across complex solutions sales, P&L, portfolio management, M&A, change management, strategy & business development and innovation. Alok successfully managed corporate transformations by effecting efficient strategies and operational models. A well-rounded executive with demonstrable experience, Alok brings transferable skills for business leadership and CxO roles.

An evangelist and thought leader in IT Transformation, Global delivery & Multi-sourcing, Managed Services, IT Security and Enterprise Architecture, Alok is passionate about cloud computing and SAAS and brings global best practices and market maker experience including specialization in emerging market across India and Asia Pacific.

Alok is incubating a payments venture to drive channel agnostic commerce transactions, IndiePay. Alok is also keenly interested in IT enabling business optimization in Energy, Education and Healthcare domains and mentoring startups across these domains.

In his most recent role as General Manager Solutions with BTGS, Alok developed a portfolio of repeatable solutions for delivering improved profitability, margins and customer experience across large transformational engagements. Prior to that as VP & GM Service Line for BT, Garg was responsible for leading solution sales, professional services and outsourcing business in India & Asia Pac region.

Previously, at HP, Alok led and managed HP”s transformation to an end-to-end enterprise solutions provider during and after the Compaq merger. A key leader at HP, Alok was with HP Company for ten years and in his last role as Managing Partner was responsible for leading the IT Strategy, Architecture and Transformation practice in Asia Pacific & Japan.
