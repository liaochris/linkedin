Brady Lutsko’s Summary

Brady Lutsko is a seasoned marketing communications strategist with more than 15 years of experience gained through various agency and corporate marketing positions, as well as management positions with two leading trade show production companies.

Prior to becoming an independent consultant, Brady was marketing director for the exhibitions unit of PGI, where he oversaw audience acquisition efforts for GEOINT Symposium, Destinations Showcase Conference & Expo, and other industry events. He managed marketing efforts behind the successful launch of The Auto-ID Center's EPC Symposium at Advanstar Communications and directed attendee and exhibitor marketing for Frontline Solutions Conference & Expo. Brady also oversaw e-marketing efforts for North America's largest technology event: AIIM & ON DEMAND (a Tradeshow Week Top 200 show).

Prior to joining Advanstar, Brady successfully managed trade show participation and event marketing efforts for eJiva, Mastech Corporation, and other high-tech start-up organizations. He began his career as a public relations account executive, where he served as project manager on a key Fortune 500 client's cause-related marketing program, and later moved into corporate communications.
