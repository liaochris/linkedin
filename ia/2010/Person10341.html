Amy Ravit Korin’s Summary

Amy Ravit Korin is the founder of interactiveAmy Social Media Consulting LLC, a consultancy focusing on social media and integrated marketing strategies, digital publicity and personal branding for individuals, businesses and brands.

Amy has represented major publishers, ad networks and social media communities and has helped develop digital strategy for top tier clients including: Procter & Gamble, H&R Block, Target, Arizona Jeans, JCPenney, General Motors, Sun Microsystems, Kao Brands, Zappos, L'Oreal, Ocean Spray and ConAgra to name a few.

With over nine years of experience in social and emerging media, online advertising, branding and digital communications, interactiveAmy is available to amplify your online!

Amy earned her MBA from Loyola University’s Graduate School of Business with a concentration in Marketing and Human Resources and currently serves on the Board of the Chicago Social Media Club and is also member of the Steppenwolf Auxiliary Council. Visit interactiveAmy.com for Amy's Chicago Networking Event Calendar which features daily events in and around Chicago where you can connect with industry thought leaders.

Amy lives and breathes social media personally and professionally. Amy is even an online dating success story – reconnecting with the first boy she ever kissed and marrying him in 2008!
