Steve Harmon’s Summary

Technology has played an important role in shaping my career … from 1975 as I worked on a DEC PDP-11 micro-computer implementing an accounting and estimating system for the family construction company to today as I am researching and developing best practices for the use of the social media to impact sales and marketing. Always the gadget geek and a true nerd from the beginning, I keep a keen eye towards the future and am never satisfied with past accomplishments. Raised by entrepreneurial parents where dinner table conversations ranged from payables to cash flow, the art, or science, or game of business is in my blood. Combining the passions of technology and business became the natural career path choice upon graduation from the University of Kansas which culminated in the incorporation of Executive Automation Consultants in 1987. Add to that Network Institute of America in 1989, rename them US Connect in 1993 and then sell them to IKON in 1996 and you get the idea of an entrepreneurial pattern which continues today. Applying technology to business is nothing new … but the new frontier developing before our eyes related to web 2.0 tools for sales and marketing reminds me of the awesome power possessed by the few early adopters who really get it.
