Brad Garland’s Summary

My expertise is where people and technology collide.

My passions are in entrepreneurship and the Web. Both have limitless possibilities and are becoming a standard part of any successful business.

Although have been in a "executive and management" role now for a decade, I not the manager type. I don't want to manage, I want to develop people. I put a lot of trust in my people and expect them to be committed to the company vision.

The Clients are our bosses and as long as we keep that as focus, our business can't falter.
