SalonSpa.com Directory’s Summary

Since 2002, SalonSpa.com has been an industry leader in generating online and phone referrals for salons, day spas, stay spas, resort spas and med spas nationwide. The owner clients and national franchise customers benefit from the one-of-a-kind dual strategy deployed by SalonSpa.com combining the benefits of cutting edge Internet technology along with one of the most recognized and memorable 800 numbers in the industry today. SalonSpa.com is an advertising "must have" vehicle for all salons and spas hoping to maximize their return on advertising investment. SalonSpa.com is a proud member of The Day Spa Association.

SalonSpa.com Directory’s Specialties:

SalonSpa.com is the premier online directory of fine hair salons, spas, day spas, stay spas, resort spas and med spas nationwide. At our SalonSpa.com web site, you'll be able to search locally to find the perfect salon or spa near you.
