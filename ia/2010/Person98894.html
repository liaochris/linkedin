James Weiss’s Summary

Successful, entrepreneurial media/tech executive and consultant looking for new opportunity. Broad skill set and exposure to wide variety of industry issues. Preferred role combines deal-making, relationship development, and input into strategic vision for a fast-growing - or quickly evolving - media/tech business.

My 12 years in the industry span both the creative and business sides of media and technology, giving me a unique perspective on how this industry functions. My early career was spent primarily as a creative in music and film, starting out "at the bottom" as an assistant in film music and rising to lead an Internet music company and a division of a mid-sized media/tech firm. I've had a variety of line roles and have managed the day-to-day operations of several different businesses, as well as driven change management, restructuring, and strategic planning.

As a manager with Bain & Company, I've worked with "blue chip" media/tech companies, as well as with the private equity industry's most successful (and demanding) funds. Recent industry focus areas include television (broadcast and cable), magazines, digital content, gaming, film, and e-payments. My work is well-regarded and rated in the firm, and results have included execution of operating plans for clients' most important issues, 35+ detailed diligence assignments, and initiation of new opportunities for the firm. I've also driven development of our intellectual property, such as our media industry point of view and our perspectives on electronic payments.

I've recently (mid October 2010) left Bain to look for innovative media/tech opportunities - either as an investor or an operator - in digital content, video / TV, advertising science, and/or e-payments. Startups are fine (early-stage preferred). Project work OK. Some international angle is preferred, either as a foreign-based company, or a US company with an international perspective. I'm willing to relocate for the right opportunity.
