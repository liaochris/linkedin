Bob Locascio’s Summary

Certified Professional Coach

This practice leverages the non-profit work I‘ve been doing for the last 4 years+; and is also based on training and certification I received as a professional coach.

For those unfamiliar with it, coaching is not therapy, advising, counseling, or giving people the answers. Coaching is about empowering, guiding, encouraging, supporting and providing accountability, so people can achieve their goals and create the life they want for themselves.

My website: www.BobLCoaching.com has more info on my new practice.

Bob Locascio’s Specialties:

My coaching practice has 3 components:

• LIFE COACHING
Helping individuals create the lives they want for themselves.

• CHURCH GROUP COACHING
A series of experiential workshops for church groups (small groups, ministry teams, church staffs) designed to develop deeper levels of community enable individual growth.

• BUSINESS COACHING
Help in taking businesses to the next level.
