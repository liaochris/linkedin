Alex Cohen ►LION◄’s Summary

Alex has joined Fircroft on a intensive graduate scheme where he hopes to become skilled in all areas of the business and can progress into management.

So far Alex has placed a number of candidates for high profile companies such as Agip, Noble Denton and Bentley whilst spending a considerable amount of time in both the Contracts and Permanent departments within Fircroft.

--------------------------------------------

FIRCROFT is one of the world’s leading providers of recruitment solutions to specialist technical industries. The company was founded in 1970 to provide recruitment services to companies in the Oil & Gas sector, active in the North Sea industry. The company has grown to provide more than 4,400 contractor workers to more than 100 companies in more than 40 countries worldwide. In addition, the company also provides permanent recruitment solutions and we place over 500 permanent white collar workers in the technical industries each year.

The company was formed to provide recruitment solutions to the Oil & Gas sector. Oil & Gas remains our most important sector and we are one of the most respected names in the industry. We have proactively and strategically moved into other key technical sectors. Our other active sectors now include: Petrochemical & Process, Automotive & Aerospace, Nuclear & Power, Mining & Minerals and General Engineering.

From providing solutions to companies active in the North Sea industries, we now have a global network of offices supporting our clients' worldwide operations.
