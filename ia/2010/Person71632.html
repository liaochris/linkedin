Fred Levko’s Summary

LEAN PRACTITIONER ACHIEVING PROSPEROUS PROCESS IMPROVEMENT to successfully implemented and sustained Lean Operating Systems at 30 global sites for industrial control, automotive, charitable, and healthcare operations. Engaged Senior Management to practice Lean and lead the transformation process. Accomplished engineer for project management, product design, manufacturing, and transactional improvements. Awarded eleven patents. Hands-on, creative problem solver working with the shopfloor on kaizen teams to reduce waste and increase value.
