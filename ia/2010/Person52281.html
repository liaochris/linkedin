David Webb’s Summary

A specialist in dealing with all aspects of Asset Based Lending including Factoring, Discounting, Trade Finance, Asset Finance, Commercial Loans / Mortgages and Structured Finance.

In addition to the above I also have an extensive knowledge of the UK Commercial Banking sector and links in to private equity partners.

I have worked within the UK Banking and Commercial Finance market for over 20 years holding positions at all levels with 10 years operations experience, over 10 years new business development activity and more recently twelve months as Head of Sales.

Currently with Bibby Financial Services working as Head of Sales for the East Midlands, East Anglia and Northern Home Counties. I have a dual role in managing a team of 7 BDM's covering this area and also work on new business myself specialising in Factoring and Discounting together with all types of Asset Based Lending solutions. I had been working as a Business Development Manager for Bibby's for over 7 years and was promoted to Head of Sales in September 08. I am one of the U.K's top performers within the industry sector averaging over 40 deals per year generating in excess of £1m of new income.

Over the years I have built and developed a loyal introducer base from many backgrounds that trust me with their clients needs.

David Webb’s Specialties:

Excellent negotiation and communication skills at all levels dealing with introducers, prospective clients and staff. I have the ability to identify and nurture both sources of introduction and potential business opportunities in an efficient and effective manner, converting these opportunities into deals at a consistently high percentage. I lead by example and encourage people around me to adopt successful strategies focusing on their core strenghts.
