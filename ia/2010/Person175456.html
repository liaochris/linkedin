Rob Glass’s Summary

As President of Glass & Gold, Inc. I develop strategic business solutions for improving enrollment at colleges & universities. Enhancing the student educational experience is my passion.

I provide collaborative enrollment solutions by identifying underlying institutional issues preventing maximum performance and partnering with leaders and solution providers throughout the higher education industry.

Services also include projecting tuition net revenue utilizing ten variables of performance, communication flow strategy, marketing communications project management, senior administration advising and consulting, career planning & placement consultation, student orientation review and recommendation, student life experience enhancement.

With more than 25 years and 60+ clients within the higher education community, I've come to understand that long term success is achieved through collaborative partnership, not through isolated effort. Effective educational marketing solutions touches all aspects of the student and administrative experience.
