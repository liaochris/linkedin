Cris Popenoe’s Summary

Sometimes you can learn a lot about a person by how they start out. While still a graduate student, Cris co-founded Yes! Inc. as a retailer of wellness and holistic living products. She grew the company into an international purveyor of books, videos, and music. She was the first to computerize an independent bookstore and sold the software to the largest bookseller in Europe. Her book, Wellness, was the first to popularize that term.

Since then, companies small and large have sought her out to create businesses that touch the lives of the people they serve in fun, innovative, and practical ways. At Universal Studios she founded their interactive media wing, Putnam New Media, and guided it from the age of CD-ROM to the age of the Internet.

Cris is renowned for her impressive breadth of business relationships in the media and entertainment world, and amongst marketers, technologists, venture capitalists, and entrepreneurs. Her goal is to create real value in people’s lives - and burnish the brands she serves with an afterglow. She’s been a digital media maven for as long as there’s been digital media. She has produced numerous award-winning digital media projects, including videos, mobile apps, games, online applications, and widgets.

In January 2010 Cris was celebrated by the New Media Council of the Producers Guild of America as Member of the Month.

Her professional and personal passions include the body-mind-spirit realm, wellness, green initiatives, and East Asian culture. If you asked many people what word comes to mind when they think of Cris, it may be her energy or passion, but it is likely to be her ability as a connector and her desire to use these connections to make the world a better place, and our individual lives more authentic and honorable. That’s what she values above all in herself and in others. She’s connected. She’s effective. She cares.
