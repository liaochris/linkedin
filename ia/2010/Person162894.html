Peter Krasilovsky’s Summary

Specialist in interactive media and e-commerce since 1986.Focus since 1995 has been on the development of local online media and online marketplaces.

Client list for research and consulting has included "who's who" of media and telecom firms (NBC, Hearst, NFL, Verizon, eBay, Discovery Channel etc.) as well as interesting startups. Also involved in Washington D.C. with public policy groups and lobbying firms concerning telecommunications.

Editorial contributor to the Newspaper Association of America, Frequent judge of local media awards. Recipient of fellowship grants from John and Mary Markle Foundation and Benton Foundation. Qualified to testify as an expert witness.

Bachelor's degree in liberal arts from Sarah Lawrence College. Master's degree in Communications Management from the Annenberg School of Communications at The University of Southern California.

Peter Krasilovsky’s Specialties:

Strategic research, analysis and consulting.

Specialties in local media positioning (ie newspapers, yellow pages, portals, city guides, classified and directory verticals); business models for ad-supported, paid and targeted services; affiliation agreements; external relations (ie press, analyst and investors); event development; grass-roots lobbying.
