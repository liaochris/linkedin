Charlie Stogner’s Summary

50+ years in media, from printer to publisher; reporter to editor; still photographer to videographer. Owned several newspapers. Past 11 years in cable TV programming and distribution.

Now developing nation's first cable network of local sites focused on local content taking advantage of 'leased access', a little known law that requires cable sites to provide anywhere from a half hour per month show to a full channel. Basically a 'poor man's' network.


We're pioneering in the application of MPEG4 technology to build our infrastructure that enables us to use the internet rather than satellite to deliver video.

Charlie Stogner’s Specialties:

Reasonably good journalist, good at research and composing report. Have become expert in 'leased access' law and FCC materials on the subject to the point I more than often know more than the cable attorneys.
Have developed a business plan for TV patterned more along the lines of newspapers than traditonal TV and a return to community journalism.
