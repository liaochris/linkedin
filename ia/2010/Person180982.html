Samuel Santos’s Summary

Currently working as a Senior Software Engineer and Lead Developer in a wide variety of projects in the private and public sector.
Particularly interested in software design and web/mobile development using web standards and Java technologies.

Most of my work has been related with scalable and performing enterprise applications for clients from banking, government and telecommunication industry.

Public speaker and educator. I have worked as an IT Trainer, lecturing subjects such as advanced web development and markup languages, for over two years. I have also spoken at various conferences about Java technologies.

Strong advocate for web standards and best practices, working as an Invited Expert for two W3C Working Groups.

Sparse knowledge of PHP, .NET, Python and several other languages and technologies.
