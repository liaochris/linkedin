Erin Peacock’s Summary

Veredus Corporation provides highly skilled Information Technology professionals for contract and permanent placement. Our veteran team of professional recruiters utilize an aggressive, personal and thorough process of discovering ideal candidates and matching them to client companies, based on that firm's unique corporate culture and needs. With the core values of professionalism, integrity and world class customer service, Veredus is committed to providing the highest caliber of staffing solutions and maintaining long term relationships with both its client and candidate partners.

Our motto, Advancing Business With People, is a statement of our belief that people make the difference in business. Having extensive experience in the staffing business, we know that you will succeed if both candidate and company is a best match in skills and personality. It is our goal to understand the needs and unique aspects of a company and match it to the candidate or candidates who best meet those criteria. What's more, we strive to do this quickly and with outstanding customer service.

Veredus Corporation was formed in November of 2000 and has more than doubled in its first year. A privately held company, Veredus has locations in Tampa, Florida and Orlando, Florida, with plans to expand to other locations in the near future.
