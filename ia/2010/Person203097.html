Thomas Pasieka’s Summary

I have over 12 years of professional experience in 3D Graphic Design. My application of choice is CINEMA 4D, but also am proficient in BodyPaint 3D, Silo, Modo and zBrush, Photoshop, Freehand, as well as various other Graphic Design applications. I have a wide range of previous work including Architectural Design, Product and Package Design, Pre Visualization and Game Content and Assets Design.

I pride myself in my ability to understand the importance of timelines/deadlines and good communication with the client. I provide frequent updates, have a sharp eye for details and strive for excellence in all I do. I am an action oriented individual and am able to follow directions and perform many tasks at once.
