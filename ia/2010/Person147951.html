Michael Becker’s Summary

I am passionately committed to furthering the growth of the mobile marketing industry and assume the roles of industry entrepreneur, volunteer, thought-leader and academic. I am the Co-founder and EVP of Business Development at iLoop Mobile, Inc., a leading and award winning mobile marketing software and application solutions provider. iLoop Mobile won the 2007 MMA Innovation award for its mFinity Platform. I bring to my role at iLoop Mobile over 17 years of business development, marketing, and sales expertise from a number of industries—mobile marketing and instant messaging, medical, electronic book, and custom solution test industries.

As a mobile marketing industry volunteer I fulfill a number of roles. I sit on the Marketing Association (MMA) North American Board of Directors (2004, 2005, 2007, 2008), Co-chair of the MMA's award winning Academic Outreach Committee (the AOC won the MMA 2007 Overall Excellence Award) and am Co-editor of the award winning MMA International Journal of Mobile Marketing—winner of the 2007 ASAE Gold Circle Award. In addition, I am a member of the DotMobi MAG Steering Committee and Chair the DotMobi MAG Education Council. In recognition for my contributions to the industry I received the 2007 MMA Outstanding Individual Achievement Award.

As a thought-leader and academic in the field of mobile marketing, I have a number of pursuits. I freely share my mobile marketing knowledge one-on-one, through webinars, and conferences. I am currently pursuing a Doctorate of Business Administration at Golden Gate University, San Francisco, and am focusing my research on understanding the efficacy of Mobile Enhanced Customer Managed Interactions (CMI) Marketing Systems, an emerging marketing model. I’ve authored over 35 articles on mobile marketing and am the editor of a number articles, columns (such as the MMA Academic Review, a bi-monthly column for the MMA Messenger) and speaking series on wide range of mobile marketing topics.
