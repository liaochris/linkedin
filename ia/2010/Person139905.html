Megan Phonesavanh’s Summary

As an artist, I am dedicated, open minded and always looking for new, creative solutions to common production problems. I believe in the importance of always meeting deadlines, while also striving to improve my work and my knowledge.

I have experience creating animation and assets for game engines, and I currently work creating realistic 3d assets, and finalized 3D animated sequences for video and television. I am a very proficient character and lip-sync animator, character modeler, environment and asset modeler, texture artist, and UV editor. I also have experience creating character rigs in Maya. I am also skillful in other programs such as Motionbuilder, Lightwave, 3ds Max, ZBrush, Photoshop, and Flash. Furthermore, I have a longstanding foundation in traditional art, and I love to draw!
