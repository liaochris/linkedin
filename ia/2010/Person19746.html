Yuri Baranov’s Summary

- My professional experience includes various positions in software/Web services marketing in functional and senior roles both in the start-up and enterprise environment. Currently, I act as an Internet entrepreneur.

Speaking idealistically, my ultimate goal is to create a healthy environment of happy people who live on a common purpose and make this small world a little better through continuous changes that inevitably lead to (r)evolutionary breakthroughs. It's somewhat close to the philosophy of "kaizen" and Kazuo Inamori's ideas.

Now, speaking practically, my focus is entirely on the social aspects of the Web, commonly known as Web 2.0. Behind this massive social phenomenon that has recently become a buzz word and subject to a lot of hype and speculations, I can see great opportunities for creating meaningful and highly profitable businesses.

I believe that opportunities the Web has created for sharing and spreading knowledge and skills are under-utilized. That said, the domain of eLearning and interactive distance education is of my peculiar interest.
