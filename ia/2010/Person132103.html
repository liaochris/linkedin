Margaret Chind’s Summary

Using the avenue of my blog Creative Madness (http://cherryblossommj.blogspot.com) I review Christian Fiction, some non-Fiction, and quality General Market Fiction. As a member of CFBA, Christian Science Fiction & Fantasy Blog Alliance, and as an Assistant Director and member of FIRST Wild Card Blog Tours I post blurbs, reviews, and other information of up and coming Christian Fiction titles to share with readers what is available.

As of this time I have worked with Glass Roads PR, Waterbrook-Multnomah, Thomas Nelson, Simon & Schuster-Howard Books, Zondervan, Bethany House, Revell Books, Source Books, Barbour Books, MIRA, Harvest House, Hatchette Book Group USA, Whitaker House, Tyndale, Random House, Sheaf House, B&H, Kregel, Penance Publishing, TBBMedia, and others.

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Currently, I run the blog Creative Madness, where I post book reviews, first chapters, author interviews, and more to promote sells of books. This is not a real company in the sense that no money exchanges hands. I do my work for free books be it final press prints or galley copies. I am here as a ministry to promote reading. My blog also includes various personal posts about life in my family as well as crafts including quilting and cross stitch.
