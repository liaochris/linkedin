Luca Zullo’s Summary

Goals: To develop viable process for renewable energy and bio-derived fuels that are sustainable in market economy conditions and utilize waste and by-products of the farming sector or new crops that do not compete with the food supply and do not put additional stress on water, nutrient and land resources. Working on ancillary technologies to address these problems and to reduce the energy footprint of bio-based transformation processes.

Background: Chemical Engineer by training, I have experience in process modeling, control and automation. I have worked for many year in the computer industries were I worked in various functions often at the interface between commercial and technical functions. In that environment I have contributed to the design of advanced computing environments for Industrial and Academic R&D. Today may main interest are in the area of renewable energy, biomass and waste utilization for energy and other product of industrial interst

Luca Zullo’s Experience

  • Co-founder and CEO

    BioCee, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Biotechnology industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year )

    BioCee Inc.’s mission is to enable the cost-effective and environmentally sound production of clean fuels, chemicals, and water treatment by harnessing the full biocatalytic potential of microorganisms. BioCee’s patented, biocoating innovation is a true platform technology that enables the development of novel applications of biocatalysts as well as making existing biocatalysts more competitive by reducing capital and operating costs. Founded in 2007, the company is based in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
    In October 2009, BioCee was among the first recipients of ARPA-E grants for transformational innovations in the energy sector.
