Dan Guenther’s Summary

Mr. Guenther brings more than 20 years of notable accomplishments as a dynamic senior sales executive with extensive experience in the telecommunications & technology industries.

Prior to becoming the VP of Sales & General Manager for Viawest in Utah, Dan was a founder and VP of Sales for a premier wholesale Voice over IP (VoIP) telephony company which built a "private-labeled" web-based VoIP offering that could be completely customized for smaller regional ISPs & WISPs. IPTelesis was one of the first of its kind in the telecommunications industry. Prior to building IPTelesis, Dan was the Director of Business Development and Channel Sales for an innovative VoIP software company called LignUp. He was instrumental in creating valuable partnerships with many traditional telecommunications Service Providers, ISPs, Wireless companies and VARs looking to enter into the market to provide VoIP services or infrastructure. With his engineering background in network management disciplines combined with training in SIP and VoIP technologies, Mr. Guenther was able to help these providers gain an understanding of how to implement the next generation in IP-based telecommunications network service delivery capabilities. Dan also hired and managed a dynamic and productive team of sales associates and sales engineers to exceed revenue goals.

Prior to his experiences building LignUp & IPTelesis, Dan held executive positions at MicroMuse, Northrup-Grumman and GE Capital IT Consulting. As General Manager of MicroMuse’s Federal Government sales team, Dan increased the business unit’s sales tenfold and was instrumental in designing and implementing some of the largest communications command centers in use by the military today.

Mr. Guenther received a BS in Business Management at Arizona State University and has completed certification training in many network disciplines, including Cisco’s CCVP
