    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    September 1994 — June 1999 (4 years 10 months)

    Widely recognized as the first sales executive to sell Web advertising when hired as the ad director for HotWired, the online companion to Wired magazine. HotWired is regarded as the world’s first ad supported, professionally published Web site. Strong year-over-year sales results, and the extensive online reach of the Wired Digital sites, led to the acquisition by Lycos, Inc. for $270 million in stock in 1999.
