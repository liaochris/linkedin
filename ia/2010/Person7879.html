Allan MacKinnon’s Summary

Allan MacKinnon is a technologist and entrepreneur who has developed innovative software platforms for the mobile, finance, transportation and manufacturing industries.

At Pixel I/O, Allan is directing the development of internet infrastructure products that are powered by GPUs.

Allan MacKinnon’s Specialties:

2D/2.5D graphics -- engines from rasterization upward. Leveraging the latest GPU technology. Real-time databases. Distributed systems. Mobile platform guru. Brokerage and financial technology. CUDA, OpenCL and DirectCompute. High-value problem solving. Technology management and strategy. Team-building and product delivery.
