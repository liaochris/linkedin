  • Editor

    Accounting Technology

    (Publishing industry)

    March 1997 — May 2009 (12 years 3 months)

    As editor of Accounting Technology, I strove to "Give our readers a view into the other guy's medicine cabinet." Our goal was to provide readable, intelligent and useful information to tax and accounting professionals, and to accounting software resellers and consultants.

    I told my writers to ask tough questions because we can't possibly be as tough as our readers, who have to be tough to run their own businesses and will challenge vendors more than we will.

    Our goal was to make people think about how technology can be used in this professional setting, how it changes how they do business, and to drag them kicking and screaming into the modern world if we had to.
