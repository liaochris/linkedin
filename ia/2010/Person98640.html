Jimmy Hendricks’s Summary

Hendricks co-founded Collar Free Inc in 2008 and has quickly established Artistic Hub as a leading white-label contest application company. The team he built excels at rapid development and strategic sales for white-label technology products. Hendricks has 10 years of experience in sales strategy, product development, advertising sales, and team building. While finishing a master’s in corporate taxation, he developed a summer sales team for Cutco Cutlery that generated $310,000 in revenue in 13 weeks. In 2006, he started the fourth office and helped define the initial sales strategy of N2Pub.com, a national newsletter company for affluent communities, specializing in localized advertising offerings. In 2007, he was hired as an industry manager for Active.com in San Diego, an online registration company with over $300M in annual revenue.

The #1 goal is to build a profitable and aggressive company that identifies market gaps and quickly builds technology to capture market share.
