Lesley Ropp’s Summary

During my first two years at Vanguard, I was part of the Technology Leadership Program. This program enabled me to rotate into three roles within the User Experience Group: Information Architecture, Information Design, and Interaction Design. As an Information Architect, I created site maps, process diagrams, and navigation diagrams for a new, improved process designed to help clients open an account with Vanguard. These skills have proven very helpful in my continuation on to my current position as an Interaction Designer.

As an Interaction Designer at Vanguard, I work closely with the user experience team, business clients, and the development team to create low and high fidelity prototypes, applying user centered design principles to ensure an experience that is useful, usable, and desirable. One such experience was helping clients transfer their assets to Vanguard. My prototypes enabled clients to easily invest thousands of dollars in a simple process. Since the launch of these pages, 51% more clients have trusted their assets with Vanguard than before.

In addition, I am the lead designer in creating mobile applications. This included a complete redesign of Vanguard’s mobile site, Vanguard.mobi, along with a new, updated version of the iPhone app. For this project, I researched mobile design best practices, created prototypes, as well as kept up wireframes for our development and business teams.

In the future, I want to continue to help create and define better online user experiences. I also want to continue working with mobile devices and how users interact with them as well as creating consistent experiences between the multiple devices.
