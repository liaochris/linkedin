Cathleen (Cathy) Lewis, CDR,SPHR’s Summary

My passion is to assist small businesses reach their business goals by finding the right talent for the right position and by offering my HR expertise to meet business challenges. I have had the opportunity to develop and drive recruitment initiatives in a competitive environment. I have gained a great reputation for sourcing great talent, establishing influential relationships with line managers and senior management to deliver high impact results. Additionally, I had the good pleasure of successfully serving as President of Urban Financial Services Coalition, a non- profit financial services organization for four years.

I also have a passion for diversity recruitment. I believe that diversity is a business imperative and as such I am often asked to assist companies with their diversity strategies

I enjoy several business aspects of HR including, recruitment, management, negotiation, communication, problem solving and making presentations. Being a proactive self-starter and effective strategist who is results oriented, diplomatic and flexible is critical for any HR professional to exhibit in the workplace and I enjoy doing just that!
