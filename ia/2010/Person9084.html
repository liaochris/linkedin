Amanda Willenberg amanda@avalonexec.com LION’s Summary

Amanda Willenberg is a co-founder and partner of Avalon Executive Search, LLC. Previously she was Vice President of Recruiting at another niche firm and worked for several non-profits before her recruiting days. Amanda is originally from Columbus, Ohio. She has a Masters of Labor and Human Resources (MLHR) from the Fisher College of Business at The Ohio State University and a B.A. in English from the University of Missouri. She began recruiting in 2003, after several years of working in human resources. She is a Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC).
