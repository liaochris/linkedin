Craig Welsh’s Summary

I have ten years' experience in financial services and specialise in addressing the financial planing needs of expatriates across Europe.

My areas of expertise include Wealth Management, Savings, Retirement Planning, Capital Investment and Tax Planning. I have also developed a broad range of professional connections and can assist in other legal, accountancy and mortgage matters.

Clients are typically professionals working in the Netherlands / Germany / Belgium and come from various levels of wealth and experience. I will consider working with anyone who takes their financial planning seriously.

My philosophy is to offer first-class service, clear and impartial advice, act with professionalism and integrity at all times, and I aim to build a long-term business relationship with clients.
