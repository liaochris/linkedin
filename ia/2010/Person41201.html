Claudia Carbone’s Summary

see website: claudiacarbone.com

Author of groundbreaking book WomenSki; expert in women's issues in alpine skiing. Also covering ski resorts, travel, arts & culture, restaurants, spas, personality profiles. Editor of Out of Denver - the suburban lifestyle magazine for southeast Denver. Arts & Culture blogger for the Denver area on tripvine.com; Denver Opera Examiner for Examiner.com, covering Opera Colorado and Central City Opera.

Claudia Carbone’s Specialties:

Writing and editing non-fiction for magazines and websites.
