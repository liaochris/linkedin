Sylvia R.J.Scott’s Summary

Sylvia Scott is the founder of Girls CEO Connection (Creating Enterprising Organizations). She is a leading advocate of the transformation of today’s young women and high school girls into the next generation of creative leaders and entrepreneurs. Widely recognized for her broad vision and resourceful execution of numerous initiatives and events, her career spans industries from fashion to business entrepreneurship.

Today, Scott is at the forefront of national programmatic conference development for women and adolescent girls. She launched the acclaimed Your Money/ Your Power, a first of its kind mother-daughter teen financial literacy and entrepreneurship symposium, sponsored by the City of Boston and hosted by the Patriot’s Trail Girl Scout Council and Center for Women & Enterprise. In addition, she was instrumental in the design and launch of the Women’s Leadership Exchange, a national conference series.

She is presently completing a book entitled, “Creativity Means Business: The Next Generation Female Entrepreneur.” In addition, she is currently an MBA candidate at Babson, with a concentration in social entrepreneurship.

Girls CEO Connection, an organization dedicated to the development of entrepreneurial and creative leadership skills in high school girls and young women. .

By being introduced to the fundamentals of strategic thinking, financial accountability and creative leadership, they will succeed not only in business but in their own lives, achieving a new and profound sense of self, as well as access to the extraordinary possibilities that are open to them.
