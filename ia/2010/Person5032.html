Alan Booth’s Summary

Leadership Advisor- Coach, helping executives build stronger teams.

Inspiring staff to deal with change initiatives, creating greater owner mindsets, creating accountability with direct reports and getting sharp focus on key business and people imperatives so that the whole team is better focused on achieving corporate goals.

With my General Management experience, coaching-advising-training is anchored in business outcomes...from sales production, manufacturing productivity & quality, reduced risk of regulatory penalties, physician retention of patients, succession of new leaders to execution of new strategies or business models.
