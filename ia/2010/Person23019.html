Betty Sullivan’s Summary

Currently, I am a principal at a comprehensive luxury tile and stone company in the Wash. DC area.

My background is planning and operations by going into companies via investor/owner by reference and executive turnarounds. Tasks have included sales planning, development and oversite, site selections, operations and accounting mostly while creating a strategy for high growth and maximizing profits.

A few jobs/start-ups have been more high tech., patent licensing in the telecommunications industry representing inventors securing royalties from manufacturers such as Sony, Mitsubishi. Sales of data storage at Zytron, div of Dun & Bradstreet.

When placed in companies, the flow is usually verifying accounting, internal systems, business plans, placement of management, execution, creating a brand, presence in the marketplace, and rainmaking (my specialty).

I am then able to complete the plan through execution, while not afraid to roll up my sleeves and get into the fray. My experience allows me to be familiar with the business models of different industries and be the first to apply a workable model from one industry to another.

On a personal level, designed and built houses and stores, particpated in show houses. Consulted designers. Designed product lines for manufacturers. Speaking and teaching on small business, sales training and the tile. I am available for industry questions as in the attached link. industry.http://www.washingtonian.com/articles/homegarden/11739.html

Charitable organizations include: Habitat for Humanity, National Symphony Orchestra Show Houses, Charity Works, Blair House Foundation, ASID Foundation, Best Buddies, foster parenting.
