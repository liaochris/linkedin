Roger Hawk - Business Coach’s Summary

Daily Blog: http://hawkseyeonbusiness.com Daily insights and ramblings of a business coach. Weekly podcasts from my radio show also.

Weekly podcast: http://hawkseyeonbusiness.com/feeds or search for Idaho Business Coach on iTunes

My goal is to help small business owners spend less time working in their business and more time working on their business. In the end, you'll be spending less total time working and will be making more money. I’d also like to help you put the FUN back in your business and your life.

My experience really starts with the U.S. Army where I was in the 82nd Airborne division, 5th and 1st Special Forces Groups. This is where I picked up my love of teaching. Traveling to countries such as Honduras, Korea, Thailand and others to train their militaries and make friends was a great experience.

My Travels continued with the Foreign Service to Eastern Europe where I was a part of the budding democracies with new businesses and development of new emerging markets.

As I returned to the Washington D.C. area, I enjoyed a number of New and exciting Internet companies such as Planning Technologies, Klein Technologies, Olicom, Cisco Systems and AT&T. From Startups to Corporate, Sales and Marketing of Services, Hardware or Software. Managing teams to training. The Internet wave was gnarly and I was fortunate to shoot the tube on it.

I am now sharing my knowledge and experiences (new economies and Old, Good Times and Bad, Foreign and Domestic) with like minded business owners as an ActionCoach.
