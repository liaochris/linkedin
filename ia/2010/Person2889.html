A.D. Cook’s Summary

Accomplished artist specializing in realistic fine art paintings on canvas and metal. My art has sold in galleries nationally, and I am published in several magazines and books.

I also do some graphic design... mostly web sites, logos and collateral. My interest is in working with creative individuals to produce memorable and worthwhile projects. Visit ADCook.com for more.

A.D. Cook’s Specialties:

Commissioned artworks, contemporary abstracts on metal, portraits, Hollywood-style wall murals

Logo design, web design, image creation

A.D. Cook’s Experience

  • Managing Partner / Artist

    ADCook LLC

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    2007 — Present (3 years )

    After relocating to Las Vegas, we re-purposed our company from ADCook Inc. to ADCook LLC and we're currently specializing in fine art commissions for the discriminating collector. Most my paintings are created as one-of-a-kind originals, while some are offered as Giclée reproductions offered through various galleries and online.

    We still offer some graphic design, branding and project management for interesting projects.

    We also own and manage AirbrushTour.com, an online Blog for the art community in an effort to share various art and related culture.

    Airbrush instruction.

  • President

    ADCook Inc.

    (Graphic Design industry)

    1994 — 2006 (12 years )

    Graphic design and brand development, web site design and implementation. Developed brand strategy and collateral design for various clientele including Iwata-Medea, PIAA Corporation, Crimson Trace Corporation, WebTrends Software and Hollywood Video stores.

    We created a considerable amount of packaging design and produced a book for the airbrush community, while developing a body of fine art paintings.
