Jim McNett’s Summary

Over 17years of staffing and recruitment experience. Have been with ABR Employment Services for over 12 years. ABR is a staffing solutions company providing a wide array of staffing solutions in a variety of segments. ABR acquired Kinsa Group in January of 2009. Kinsa Group is a national search placement firm that specializes in the food & beverage industry.

Jim McNett’s Specialties:

ABR specializes in all areas of staffing including Office/Clerical, Professional/Technical & Light Industrial. Our staffing solutions are customized to meet the needs of the organizations we partner with.

Kinsa Group provides search & placement services with a specific focus on the food & beverage processing industries. Serving companies throughout the US for the past 25 years, we deliver highly qualified professionals and senior-to-executive level management candidates.
