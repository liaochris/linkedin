Robert Matney’s Summary

I architect team, partner, client, and community relations via comprehensive web strategy, social web community management, software collaboration tools, customer support methods, and expert copy editing. During my experience of leadership and management in the software, service, performing arts, and not-for-profit sectors I’ve built a portfolio of online and offline communication skills which I use to foster learning and team excellence.

I'm interested especially in the intersection of technology, community interaction, business needs, identity performance, and joy. I manage communities and direct technical support and marketing structures that pride themselves on transparency, courtesy, and promptness.
