  • Founder, CEO

    MusicBank

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    August 1999 — July 2001 (2 years )

    Founder, CEO of Musicbank, the very first online technology platform for delivering on-demand access to music in partnership with the 5 major US recording companies Sony, BMG, EMI, Time Warner and Vivendi/Universal and the major publishing companies. Established the first licensing basis for online track-by-track access to the major music catalogs and pioneered a flexible and secure technology delivery platform that could be leveraged by a number of different traditional brick and mortar retail brands such as Virgin Mega-Store.

  • COO

    Sonique

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

Before Microsoft, REAL Networks or AOL were in the game Sonique grew quickly as a popular digital audio application becoming the number one download at www.download.com for a period of time. As big brands and leading media properties recognized the digital audio and MP3 movement was taking off, we sold the company to Lycos in 1999.
