Tara Radzinski’s Summary

The ultimate goal of sustainable development is to minimize environmental impact, maximize economic well being, and promote the social health of communities and people. Sustainable Solutions Corporation applies an integrated approach and ecological thinking to all of our endeavors. We carefully evaluate each project and design the plans, programs, systems and processes needed to balance the environmental, economic and social welfare in a way that will best suit that project. Our sustainability services provide long term competitive advantage to all of our clients.

For green building design, where each element is co dependent; good design is created through efforts to be economical and sustainable. Green building practices include choices regarding the site, energy use, water use, material use, and indoor environmental quality. Sustainable Solutions combines a deep understanding of the practices of sustainable development and green building with years of experience in engineering, design, and construction services. We provide comprehensive solutions that integrate high performance sustainable design into each project.
