Solex Eke’s Summary

+[::: My goal is to streamline the process of relocation :::]+

Rather than consumers getting frustrated and/or taken advantage of by searching for a new home on their own, my team of licensed professionals offers to do all of the work for them FOR FREE. We offer a Same-Day service guarantee as well.

Our experienced specialists first send custom lists of homes matching each client's unique preferences via email, which will include street addresses, maps, and other pertinent information. We then schedule a private home showing tour with our Realtors to view the inside of the home(s) the client is interested in, including Guard-Gated Estates and High-Rises (which the general public can't access) as well as Houses, Condos, and Apartments throughout Southern Nevada. We negotiate for the best lease terms and pricing available, and coordinate the delivery of the new keys!

"Dial (702)-RENTERS" (702-736-8377) and "www.WeLoveRenters.com" have become excellent resources to utilize when planning a relocation to or within Las Vegas & Henderson! Neighborhood brochures, local news feeds & blogs, southern nevada utility connection information, and much more can be found on WeLoveRenters.com!

Follow us on Twitter (@WeLoveRenters) for the hottest new listings! www.twitter.com/weloverenters
