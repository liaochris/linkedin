Teena Rose, Exec Resume Writer/Career Coach’s Summary

Thanks for "clicking" in ... my name is Teena Rose. I'm *one of the best resume writers* you'll find on the Internet today. I state this not because of my credentials or my years in business, but for the results I generate clients.

Clients love my customized resumes right from the start:

*** "I love it! Wow...Better than I anticipated...Looks clean now...and worded very nicely!!" L. Moraco

*** "Hello Teena! I must say, I think this resume is absolutely fantastic. I love the design of it. I've looked it over and I can't think of anything that I want to change." R. Palanza

*** "Hi Teena! I'm very impressed! I wish he [referring to her husband] had this done a long time ago." M. Gatto

Here are comments from a client who used my resume for an internal position. Even after 2 years, the resume I developed and wrote is still benefiting this client:

*** "About 2 years ago now you wrote a resume for me just want to update you. I am still with X and with the help of your resume have been promoted to Program Engineer. Thanks for your great work." G. Adams [comment was modified for confidentiality purposes]

PLUS, my clients are some of the happiest, most successful, and most progressing job and career strategists "on the block."

I *love* working with managers and executives because they understand that professional writers/copywriters are how you gain advantage. Professionals who lead with a strategic and effective team in tow, whether it be writers or otherwise, know that collaboratively the best results are achieved.

Those in the early stages of their careers, such as new graduates and support personnel, are sometimes in tune with the mindset of my visionary clients; think big; think broadly, think profitably.
