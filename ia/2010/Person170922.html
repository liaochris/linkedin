Regi Ellis’s Summary

I’m a firm believer in developing quality creative solutions, over pushing out bland bloated work to fill a quota. Much of this
belief stems from my education in art, experience in the field, and my love for the profession. I first entered the web world
with the concept and deployment of my University’s online student newspaper, creating and establishing the first online
regular publication in the school’s history. Since then I have develop a love for parallels in digital art and have spent the
last several years finding ways to combine 3D concept design and animation, progressive graphic design, and overall user
functionality into my work. With this attitude towards the industry I have been lucky enough to work with some of the
most impressive talent located around the world and gained knowledge that has proven to be invaluable.

When not working on client or company tasks, I have been known to spend my time with my beloved SLR digital camera,
capturing images of the randomness that is life. Other activities include attending IxDA and industry events, reading or writing articles on the motion graphics or web industry, or in front of a professional application learning the “ins & outs” of
a new program. Outside the web world, I am an active indoor volleyball player as well as a avid video game and Anime junkie.
