Susan Matiak, CFM’s Summary

I have spent the majority of my career working in facilities and construction management.

Major achievements include planning, building and managing Sub-Zero's Fitchburg campus; including nearly one-million square feet of manufacturing, office, production support, laboratory, sales training and distribution space.

Construction projects and facilities I have managed include:
- Sub-Zero's state-of-the-art training facility, The Westye F. Bakke Center (2002-04)
- 400K SF addition of manufacturing and office space for Sub-Zero's redesigned and automated Built-In Refrigeration product line (2005-08)
- 400K SF of manufacturing and office space for Wolf Appliance

You may see a visual representation of these projects by visiting Zingg Design's website at: http://www.zinggdesign.com/content_p.html
