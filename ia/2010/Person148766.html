Jim Moran’s Summary

LongmeadowBiz provides a web based connection between the Town of Longmeadow, Massachusetts and local businesses looking to deliver products and services to town residents. If a local business does not have an Internet presence, we can design and implement a low cost attractive website that will become their business card on the web. Inclusion in the Longmeadow Business Directory will make their business much more visible to local customers. For additional opportunities, view the short video below in Slideshare presentation for example of low cost web video ads that we offer to clients.

I have been working with computers and using many types of software programs for over 25 years during my career at Monsanto/Solutia and in my post retirement business- LongmeadowBiz, LLC. My interest in the Internet started around 1995-1996 with the arrival of Netscape, Cello and other browsers.

In 1997 I started a website for the town of Longmeadow, Massachusetts- www.Longmeadow.org- when many people did not know much about the World Wide Web. 12 years later it has become the key communications medium for our town.

Five years ago I created an business/ community extension of the town website called www.LongmeadowBiz.com which has become a very popular destination for many town residents. The website offers many free services to town residents and is supported financially by local advertisers.
