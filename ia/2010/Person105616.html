Jose Umbert’s Summary

I practice in a variety of litigation and arbitration matters. I have experience in complex first-party property insurance and reinsurance matters, domestic and international antitrust cases, commercial contract disputes, and class actions.

About Zelle Hofmann:
Zelle Hofmann is a national litigation and dispute resolution law firm with offices in Boston, Dallas, Minneapolis, San Francisco, and Washington, DC. We represent clients in their most challenging insurance-related disputes, antitrust claims and other complex litigation in venues across the United States and around the world. Our experience in successfully resolving high-profile, high-exposure cases and our commitment to efficient and responsive service supports everything we do.
