Gail Nowacky, ALHC’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience managing health insurance claims, resolving payment disputes, and creating and managing cost containment programs. I have reviewed over 500 managed care networks nationally including HMOs and PPOs. I have audited hundreds of self funded health plans, TPAs, HMOs, and MGUs. I have settled many disputed claims and have served as an arbitrator between insurance companies and health plans.

I have been involved with the reinsurance and managed care industries since 1987, holding executive positions within each of them. I have also managed various direct insurance companies and a Third Party Administrator.

As the past president and senior executive of several companies, I have developed successful marketing strategies, and allocated proper resources resulting in first year profits, producing over $1m in first year sales, and have raised significant capital investments. I have been a member of various Board of Directors and held bottom line accountability.

I have frequently traveled to client locations for sales presentations and problem resolution.

Today, my entrepreneurial spirit has resulted in sole proprietorship of two different businesses. HIA helps health plans, plan administrators and reinsurance companies evaluate the financial value of managed care programs, and contain medical costs, and TeamGail provides administrative and professional support to the financial and insurance industries.

Gail Nowacky, ALHC’s Specialties:

Analyzing the financial value of managed care programs, negotiating hospital bills, resolving insurance disputes, providing patient advocacy, managing business offices and operations for financial managers and insurance companies.
