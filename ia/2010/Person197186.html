Sumana Harihareswara’s Summary

I'm a geek-suit interface, best as a producer, software development manager, product manager, or customer relationship manager. I like to work on products, services, or sites that delight people. Most recently I was a lead project manager and personnel manager with an open source software consulting firm specializing in mobile, multimedia, and real-time communications.

I've worked in nonprofits, schools, the arts, periodicals, product companies, consulting firms, and service companies. That breadth led me to see a need for managers who can identify users' needs, translate them into business tasks and technical requirements, and shepherd development to completion. That's who I am.
