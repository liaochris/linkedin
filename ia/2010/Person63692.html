Elizabeth Allen’s Summary

♦Elizabeth Allen: Consultant | Speaker | Author
♦Creator & Developer of The CODE®: A Revolutionary Sales Process

Self-employed since age 25, Elizabeth Allen noticed a need to create a sustainable sales process for her company and others. Elizabeth recalls, “I became painfully aware of my own skills as an entrepreneur and realized that the universal issue faced by all business owners is: ‘How do I replicate my skills as a rainmaker?’ If you don’t have a predictable sales pipeline as a business, nothing else operationally matters.”

Challenged to build a program to not only help herself but the diverse clients she works with, she developed The CODE®, a proprietary sales and marketing system developed to transform the selling culture within organizations. CODE stands for the acronym: Communication, Organization, Documentation and Evaluation.

The CODE® will help you:
►Create a sustainable exit strategy for owners
► Harness the power of the "human brand" across the organization
►Generate & convert highly qualified leads
►Achieve an “All Hands On Deck” mentality throughout your organization so your people generate leads with urgency and intentionality
►Drive sustainable top line revenue
►Effectively farm core customers & hunt for new ones
►De-commoditize what you are selling


“My mission is to improve the stability of companies by increasing the probability of sales,” Elizabeth says. “I feel satisfaction in continually adapting CODE to best practice within industry verticals, and measure my success in terms of my ability to translate self- sustaining sales systems to organizations determined to reach the next level of strategic growth.”

Elizabeth Allen has served as a facilitator for The Kauffman Foundation, FastTrac for Entrepreneurs and as a mentor for the KU MBA Graduates Program. She has also been a strategic expert for projects at Sprint, IBM, 3M, Pfizer, Mutual of Omaha, Subway World Franchise and Calvin Klein Fragrances.
