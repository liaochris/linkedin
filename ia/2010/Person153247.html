Melissa Giovagnoli’s Summary

Over 17 years of expertise providing social networking strategy consulting and implementation in organizations such as Motorola, Hewitt, Office Depot, BF Goodrich, AT&T, American Express, Medtronic, CNA, UBS and hundreds of emerging companies looking to build key account relationships with large organizations, innovate new products or build their brands through our exclusive strategic marketing process called Networlding.

We are leaders helping you: build digital trust, grow your markets, manage your online presence and integrate social media with your other marketing initiatives.

In Chicago and around the world we offer a proven process for improving sales, marketing or leadership performance. We also apply our process to help companies prevent brand erosion.

My background includes:

♦ Social media/social neworking keynote speaking
♦ Author of 11 books, four best sellers on networks
♦ LinkedIn Authority
♦ Top Ten LinkedIn Trainer – Over 2000 Individual, professional and executive coaching and 500 group trainings

I value connecting to make a difference. To that end, I have developed these simple guidelines for connecting with me:

1. Email your request to melissa@networlding.com;
2. Tell me how you hope to benefit from our relationship; and what you would like to offer for support in return.
3. Set aside a few minutes for us to have a phone conversation.

I will talk with anyone, but prefer those who seriously network to those who simply collect "LinkedIn connections.”

*Ask about our world-class social media implementation team. We have one of the largest implementation teams in the U.S and now expanding internationally.

We are currently licensing the Networlding program, certifying top business leaders to facilitate and coach the process. We now have licensed facilitators in Boston, Columbus, Phoenix and Indianapolis. If you are interested in getting licensed contact me (see the contact info below).
