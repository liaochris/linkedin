Ellen Block, Esq.’s Summary

After many years of following my passions in the workplace environment as a teacher and an attorney I have realized my true passion as an Attorney/ Career Coach utilizing my ability to break down the complex nature of things into an understandable format and my highly competitive nature to help my clients get to and maintain the peak of success in the careers for which they, too, have a passion. I am inspired on a daily basis by the determination and unrelenting attitude my clients exhibit as we work together to manage their careers. One of our most popular programs "Solomon's Way" teaches our client's a very fundamental and successful approach to life and career management: "Eat, Drink, and Enjoy what you do"; and includes a nutritional and quality-of-life component for all around career/life management.

One of my personal goals is to implement our coaching programs on a corporate level. In my experience as an employment law attorney I found that the corporate values of respect and personal empowerment are equally as important as the wages employees are paid. These corporate values foster employee loyalty and a sense of pride in the work they do for a company. Companies whose mission statement reflects those values experience minimal turnover and maximum profit. Companies can avoid major legal problems in the workplace by implementing a few minor adjustments. Employees and managers who have completed our program create a more empowered and productive workplace.

I believe in "preventive law", "preventive health" and "preventive career management" ; or maybe the better word is "preemptive". I try to anticipate the challenges that will arise to prepare my clients well in advance so they are always on the cutting edge.
