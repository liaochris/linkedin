Coach Rick, The Personal Golf Coach’s Summary

My golf story goes: I once met a woman who said "How'de you like to play golf?" Well, now she's my wife and now I teach golf!

Since then I have become a United States Golf Teaching Federation, USGTF certified teacher and a full Class A member of the PGA of America. I bring all my experience to golfers and those just starting out from the working for the NCGA and NIKE golf learning center,and from working with Long Drivers of America, Golf Digest Schools, Dave Pelz short game centers and more. All this experience provides students with a combined Swing mechanics, Game/Shot management and Mental/Self control approach to develop a consistent golf game, On the Course, Where It Matters!
