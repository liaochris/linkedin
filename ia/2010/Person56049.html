Diana Murphy’s Summary

EDITOR IN CHIEF, CREATIVE DIRECTOR, CONTENT STRATEGIST, AND BRANDING EXPERT experienced in all phases of strategic planning and creative development of brands, magazines, websites, eblasts, newsletters, online media programs, interactive media, books, catalogs, direct mail, advertising, media kits, and brand guidelines.

EXTENSIVE BACKGROUND IN PUBLISHING, MARKETING, AND RETAIL with emphasis on content strategy, creative development, and branding of editorial, advertorial, and marketing content in all forms of media, including ecommerce, custom content, licensed brand extensions, and licensed content negotiation.

EXPERTISE IN WOMEN'S AND HOME CATEGORIES including cooking, entertaining, decorating, home building, gardening, crafts, travel, family, health & beauty, sustainability, environmental issues, and green living.

OUT-OF-THE-BOX IDEA GENERATOR with a finger on the pulse of trends and what consumers want, and knows how to package it into beautifully designed, highest quality, compelling creative that resonates and keeps people coming back for more.

EXPERIENCED TEAM LEADER AND MANAGER of staff, budgets, projects, and deadlines. Media trained spokesperson with numerous appearances on national television and radio, and lecturer at consumer and trade shows.

AVAILABLE FOR CONSULTING PROJECTS developing content strategy, editorial content, creative direction, brand identity and style guidelines, licensed content negotiations, client relations, idea generation, editing, and writing services for brands, websites, magazines, marketing materials, online media programs, books, catalogs, newsletters, direct response vehicles, brand guidelines, and presentations.
