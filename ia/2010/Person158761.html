Pam Earle’s Summary

I have been at the forefront of web design since 1999 and I am well versed in branding, architecture, interaction design, and production. I use innovative design concepts and user-centered interfaces to extend established brands as well as provide new brands a fresh and exciting look. My award-wining designs include new projects and ideas for Acura, Bank of America, Bristol Myers Squibb, Connexion by Boeing, FAO Schwarz, Gillette, Hewlett Packard, Honda, IBM, Intel, Liberty Mutual, McDonald’s, Microsoft, National Dairy Council, Pottery Barn, Sears, Sports Illustrated, Starbucks, Subway, Tribeca Film Festival, United Airline, YMCA – just to name a few.


My recent work was created using Rich Internet Applications and navigation design principles which illustrate my skill at using smart architecture to solve global content management challenges. I specialize in conceptualizing, communicating and delivering ideas for creation of new or existing brands through design direction.

I have a solid foundation in traditional marketing components such as logo design, production of new business pitches and support materials, and print advertising. My portfolio contains innovative work with high-end graphic mediums such as Web Site interface and animation, computer graphics and production, interactive marketing, and multimedia interface.
