Peggy McGuinness’s Summary

Multi--industry global communications expert in employee communications, marketing communications and events management.

26-year career at IBM Corporation followed by Director of Communications postion at Devix Corporation and communications consultancies with GAB Robins, MasterCard Worldwide and Diageo North America. Communications expertise includes the unique ability to develop and distill IT and CRM content into concise user-friendly language.

Most recent consulting position was with Diageo North America as a Communications Management Consultant in their Global Marketing & Innovation function. This role required IS knowledge combined with top communications skills to support internal IS projects, jointly sponsored with Marketing to further Diageo Marketing goals in the areas of Digital and Relationship Marketing.

Held a variety of communications and managerial positions over many years at IBM in: sales support, administration management, special events, video production, industrial show design/development/production, integrated marketing communications and employee and executive communications.

Additional business areas of interest and expertise include strategic planning, business transformation, change management and business/life coaching.
