Gail Drury, CMKBD’s Summary

In 1987 I started Drury Design as a home based business to allow me to work from home and care for my two small children. As my business grew we moved our studio to downtown Glen Ellyn and in 2002 moved to our second Glen Ellyn location, a 7,500 sq. ft. studio on Main Street. In 2007 we celebrated our twentieth anniversary and the completion of over 1,000 projects in the greater Chicago area.

As more and more clients use the web for inspiration and to research kitchen design and home renovation, we decided to launch Drury Design Newsroom to share our knowledge and client projects online as well as impart news about our staff, awards, our community and friends and in-studio events.

Here, you can learn about what goes into making a kitchen your home’s signature. We’re excited to share our client-focused design™ projects with you. And, we’re thrilled to give you the opportunity to tell us what you think about what we do.

Our clients receive a signature custom-designed kitchen to fit their home, family and cooking and entertaining lifestyle. We find our clients are informed, stylish, and confident. They have their own design vision, like to entertain, enjoy being taken care of and appreciate fine design details as well as functional practicality…regardless of whether their kitchen design is “attainable” or over the top.

Our client-focused design™ approach positions you as a partner in the process.
You . . .
- meet with top designers who listen
- have a dedicated full-service studio to rely on
- select from an infinite number of custom cabinetry options
- choose from an array of high-end appliances
- enjoy the professionalism of a trained team of installation experts ready to finish your project to perfection.

Ready to get started? Call us at 630.469.4980 to schedule your complimentary preliminary consultation.

Stop by our showroom at 512 N. Main Street in Glen Ellyn and make someday one day – soon.
