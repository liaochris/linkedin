Chris Brown’s Summary

I provide a full range of estate planning, asset protection, estate administration, and tax planning services and also focus on advising family owned business owners.

I was the youngest person ever to be elected President of the West Michigan Estate Planning Council and the Grand Rapids Bar Association Probate and Estate Planning Section.

In October, 2008, I was recognized by Michigan State University and received MSU's Distinguished Young Alumni Award. In October, 2009, I was named one of Grand Rapids "Forty Under 40" Business Leaders by the Grand Rapids Business Journal.
