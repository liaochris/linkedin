Brian Hughes’s Summary

Committed to developing innovative educational tools and multimedia products. Recent experience: co-founder of EdLab, a research, design, and development center for post-industrial education at Teachers College, Columbia University; co-founder and executive producer of After Ed TV (a web channel featuring videos about the evolving education sector); co-developer and co-designer of PocketKnowledge (an online, social archive for educational institutions).

Brian Hughes’s Specialties:

Generalist with a background in design leadership and multimedia production. Theoretical and historical scholarship in the visual arts (B.A.), moral philosophy (M.A.), and education (Ed.D.).
