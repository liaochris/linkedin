Bart Schrooten’s Summary

Hi, I am an ambitious entrepreneur and business developer, born in The Netherlands, living in Finland, working where ever there is an internet connection.

Most of my experience comes from TIME (Technology, Internet, Media and Entertainment), negotiating on executive level (both as seller and buyer) and working with some of the brightest and most innovative people in business.

In my free time I enjoy good food, rock climbing, traveling, spending time with my wife, my little boy and dog.

Bart Schrooten’s Specialties:

developing people and organizations with a commercial and global mindset, mobile and web 3.0 technologies and services, keep a positive and go-for-it attitude!
