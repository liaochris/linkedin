Nigel Collin’s Summary

Leading Creative People:

I help business leaders within the creative sector lead their creative people with the objective of making creativity commercially viable.

I am obsessed with helping people understand and support their creative people to get the best out of them, to making sure creative people are satisfied and content and with the sad fact that business is really good and killing the creative potential of their people.

My background as a creative director and producer allows me to stand in the midst of the two different mindsets and create harmony.
