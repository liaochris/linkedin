I am an IT professional with over 15 years of experience in information technology industry in the areas of enterprise architecture, business/systems analysis, project management.
- Ability to effectively manage both people and IT projects. Hands-on project leader who generates easy-to-use applications in record time. Coach, mentor and on-the-job trainer of software developers and architects.
- Attentive to details, ability to analyze business requirements and provide solution to specific business problems.
- Create documentation for all phases of product development (PRD, Project charter/plan, FSD, design) using UML standards, methodologies and tools. Maintaining architecture documentation and artifacts.

- Ability to see “big picture”, analyze technology trends and business requirements, provide technology guidance at the enterprise level, define enterprise-level architectural principles, oversee project design/implementation.
- Specialties in databases architecture, Master Data Management, financial applications development, transaction processing, web programming, project management.
- Continual learner, with an ability to rapidly achieve organizational integration, assimilate job requirements and aggressively employ new ideas, concepts, methods and technologies.
- Extensive track record in systems architecture and software development from concept to release resulting in quality products that exceed expectations.
- Well-organized and thorough, able to prioritize and generate results to achieve organization objectives within time and budgetary expectations. Motivated self-starter and team player. Enjoy building and expanding skills, and invite challenges and new technologies.
