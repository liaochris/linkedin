Deb Dib’s Summary

Visionary, gutsy, fun $500K to $1M+ executives with a strong profit motive and a social conscience are my sweet spot.

My clients are entrepreneurial leaders of small- to mid-caps /start-ups, heads of enlightened Fortune companies, or rising stars targeting the C-suite, and they are fascinating to me!

They are not interested in the status-quo and they haven't allowed the personality and passion to be sucked out of them by the demands of work. I help them stop, take a breath, figure out why they tick and how that translates into success -- and then help make them even more successful.

It’s an intense (but FUN) process, so I can work with just 2-3 clients each month doing in depth C-level personal branding, resume, and career momentum projects (that extend for upwards of 4 months). My most recent clients landed in record time, and with an average 25-50% bump in compensation.

A while back I took a fun political quiz that pegged me as a "liberal leaning libertarian" and that contrarian description seems to fit my "profit with a purpose" vision. My unabashedly idealistic (and practical) mantra? "Changing the world one executive at a time!"

I LOVE my job - I get to help the coolest people catapult their careers AND achieve their particular dreams. What's better than that?

    Career Thought Leaders Consortium

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    July 2009 — Present (10 months)

    We are the career innovation think tank for the now, the new, and the next in careers.

    On a hot summer's day in July 2009, around a table overlooking the beautiful mountains surrounding Wendy Enelow's 35-acre farm in Virginia, 11 career innovators gathered for a two-day summit and became the Career Thought Leader's Consortium.

    Wendy's vision was to share the collective wisdom and best practices of 11 career industry innovators with the universe of knowledge-hungry career professionals and job seekers who want to achieve better (and more fulfilling) success.

    Our goal is to make innovation the cornerstone of career momentum, keeping job seekers and career practitioners on the cutting edge of what will be working today, tomorrow, and in the future world of work.

    www.careerthoughtleaders.com houses a rich repository of trends, best practices, and useful information for job search, resume writing, personal and career branding, interview prep, networking, and much more -- for practitioner AND job seeker education.

    We encourage colleagues and job seekers to join and contribute ideas, opinion, and resources to the Career Thought Leaders Consortium Linkedin group.

    I'm proud and honored to have been invited to join CTL and very exited to contribute to the growing body of knowledge about what works (and will work) in today's "nano-speed" marketplace.
