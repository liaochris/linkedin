MonsterClean Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning’s Summary

Monster Clean is a commercial and residential carpet, tile, and upholstery care service targeting businesses, government, and rural middle and upper-class homes. When Monster Clean was founded, the economy was strong, and consumer confidence was high. Businesses and families freely spent their money on Monster Clean’s core services to improve their living and working environments. Recently, the economic landscape in our geographic service area has suffered along with the rest of the country, but demand for services from Monster Clean has not declined, in fact, it has dramatically increased. Our services in prosperous times are welcome luxuries, and in less prosperous times, money saving alternatives to expensive replacement options. Our strong reputation for top quality work at a reasonable price has allowed us to slash our advertising budget from our very aggressive $30,000.00 first year in 2004 to less than $4,000.00 in 2008.

Monster Clean is a sole proprietorship, owned by James Michael Stallings. For the last eight years, James has worked to grow MonsterClean from very humble beginnings into a profitable, well respected, and very capable business. While James was still on active duty in the US Navy he purchased of a Rotovac rotary power extractor “business startup kit” and started cleaning carpets on nights and weekends. The company name at inception was Better Cleaners, and evolved into Monster Clean in 2004. From humble beginnings, our company has seen phenomenal growth.

Monster Clean is unique in several important aspects, and fills a market niche that has been overlooked by all of it’s competitors. Our massive truck mounted equipment is capable of cleaning at distances far beyond the reach of our competition, allows us to drive high volume rotary floor tools that clean better, and inspires a belief in our customers that we are uniquely able to “get the job done”.

  • Some of our Larger Commercial Clients

    Monster Clean - Commercial Clients

    (Environmental Services industry)

    2005 — 2009 (4 years )

    Samples of some of our larger commercial jobs include:

    * Dominion Enterprises - (20-story, 500,000 square foot building): This was a complicated project with difficulty in cleaning construction dust, debris, and soil, while simultaneously keeping the building operational. MonsterClean’s equipment reached the 18th floor with the truck parked on the street with no reduction in power / capability. (See the video on our website!)

    * MS Maasdam - (55,451-gross-ton, 720-foot long, luxury cruise ship in the Holland America Line): Monster Clean completed this massive carpet and upholstery cleaning project in four nights allowed by the ships schedule without interruption of either pier-side operations or shipboard activities. The quality of MonsterClean work is evidenced by the ship’s decision to alter their work order while still in progress to include cleaning the carpets in an area previously slated for carpet replacement.

    * Navy Legal Services Office Building - (30,000 square feet, carpeted) Monster Clean completed the project inside of a three-day holiday weekend allotted, with no disruption to business operations. The customer has requested and received services on three separate occasions, increasing the scope of work each time.
