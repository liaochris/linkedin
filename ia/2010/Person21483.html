Ben Wu’s Summary

Innovative strategic problem solver with a passion for sustainable energy technologies and 20+ years developing exploratory technologies in fuel reforming, hydrogen production, gasification, combustion, and waste-recycling from conception through commercialization. Team-first leadership style helped successfully deliver four multi-million dollar projects for developing leading edge innovations in the area of environmental technologies and homeland security.
