Mike Lee’s Summary

My background in media and technology have led me to some exciting life experiences. Currently, I run two growing companies and manage more than 15 people as I continue to work with technology startups to build world-class teams.

Silver Key is one of the most successful technology staffing firms in the SF bay area. We specialize in working with early stage startups from seed level to C Round. I am one of the top producing Headhunters in the industry and am one of a small number of Headhunters to bill ~$1M personally.

Gling is an exciting startup in a niche lifestyle I know very well ... the Gluten-free world, of which my family has been a member for 7 years. My experience working with multiple startups, and background in communications have been a huge asset in creating an application startup that is the fusion of media and technology.

Mike Lee’s Specialties:

Building startups, both as a Headhunter and Entrepeneur. I enjoy the science and art of working with people as they make challenging decisions. I am a committed father and husband and enjoy life with my wife and four kids.

    Our purpose is simple: see your vision and be the resource you choose to build your company.

Our purpose is to be our clients' Agent in achieving all their staffing goals. Each Agent and their team are highly trained to take the initiative in proactively understanding your vision, values and priorities. Together with your Silver Key Staffing Agent, you can develop a strategy to reach your goals. people with focus and determination naturally rise, like cream, to the top of their profession.

    How do we work?

    Our time-proven process is built around follow-through and a commitment to time management. Blended with a unique team-search approach, our work ethic and accountability to your goals is what defines our distinctive style. While our roots are in the San Francisco bay area, Silver Key Staffing Agents work with the most reputable companies and investment firms across the country. We have completed searches in every major geographic region in the U.S.

    This strategy, our unique combination, is what separates great recruiting from our competitors.
