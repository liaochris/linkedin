Bill Kasko’s Summary

Frontline Source Group provides technical services, information technology, accounting finance, human resource, legal, administrative and clerical employment staffing. Frontline is both a short term temporary staffing agency and direct placement firm with offices in Austin Dallas Fort Worth Plano Houston Sugar Land and The Woodlands Texas and Nashville Tennessee and Phoenix Arizona. Two time winner as the #1 Best Place to Work and Best of the Best Places to work in Dallas Fort Worth Texas by the Dallas Business Journal 2007 and 2008. Southern Methodist University- 2008 and 2009 SMU Dallas 100 Award for fastest growing company in Dallas Fort Worth and 2008 Staffing Voice Communications Award from the American Staffing Association - ASA. Host of the first weekly employment talk radio show on CBS Radio - KRLD.
