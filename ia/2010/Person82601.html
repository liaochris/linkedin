Holly Timberlake, PhD.’s Summary

As founder of Nakaia Healing Arts, my passion is to help people live more vibrant, empowered, authentic and successful lives. My orientation is very holistic and energy-based.
As a trauma therapist, I help people to both overcome the effects of physical, emotional and sexual trauma and stress, and to uncover their natural joy and lightness of being. I have helped hundreds of women and men over the years to reclaim their lives, their trust in themselves and others, their enthusiasm for living and their capacity to succeed and feel joy!
I have taught graduate courses at KSU, engaged in cultural diversity work, worked in community mental health and have been in private practice for 20 years, starting my own business in 1999.
Due to my involvement with Norma Rist's Boardroom Groups for Women, I have learned a great deal about business ownership, and now am beginning to act as a consultant to counselors and psychologists who are creating both brick and mortar and on line businesses.
For the past few years, I have been developing an online presence where people from anywhere in the world can benefit from my services. It has been a goal of mine to help women internationally through emotional healing and education, and I look forward to the day when this is realized!
