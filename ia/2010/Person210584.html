Van Vandegrift’s Summary

Van Vandegrift is a multi-award winning Producer and has been written about extensively in Variety, The Wall Street Journal, Advertising Age, Brandweek, The Hollywood Reporter and other leading publications.

Recent film projects included Get Smart (Warner Brothers), Journey To The Center Of The Earth (New Line) and Planet 51 (Sony). He has produced many exciting television projects and his credits also include hit series such as Gene Simmons Family Jewels (A&E) and other FOX and NBC series as well as innovative viral marketing web series and mobile series.

Van founded matrixx in Santa Monica, CA in 2000, and his creative and entertainment marketing execution for studios, networks, leading brands and innovative new companies has helped pioneer branded entertainment, social media and interactive marketing.

He has been an active board member and venture capitalist to several successful start-ups including yesmail, a leading interactive marketing company. Van discovered the firm with just one employee and helped guide it to a $320 Million IPO and subsequent acquisition for over $770 Million. In recognition of his entrepreneurial achievements, he was named to the prestigious Inc. magazine 'Birthing of Giants' group.

Van is a former Senior Executive of Compaq Computer Corporation and holds B.S. and M.S. degrees in Engineering. A veteran of NASA and the U.S. Navy, he is an avid adventurer and accomplished pilot.
