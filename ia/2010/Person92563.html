Jeanette Fors-Andrée’s Summary

My goal is to improve change processes through better communication. My current research work concentrates on how companies should manage mergers and acquisitions towards external actors (e.g. customers, suppliers and media) and use communication as a tool to achieve desired reactions. A recently conducted case study points to the essential role of communication in minimizing uncertainty, speculations and rumours following a change. It shows the importance of change and crisis communication, business relationship management and facing media in a proper way.

Jeanette Fors-Andrée’s Specialties:

Crisis and Change Communication, Business and Media Relationships, and Research Methods.
