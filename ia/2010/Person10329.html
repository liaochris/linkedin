Amy Pollack’s Summary

As Design Director and Principal of Twist’nShout, I work primarily with non profit organizations and small businesses to help them connect with the people they need to reach so that they will listen and respond.

I am inquisitive, creative, organized and accurate. I meet deadlines and am respectful of budgets. My clients partner with me in the creative process, so that we produce communication materials that are visually appealing, clearly written, strategic and effective.

Along with writers, editors and photographers, services include the production of annual reports, newsletters, white papers, capabilities brochures, development materials for capital campaigns, special event invitations and signage, logos and corporate identities, op-eds, speeches, feature articles, advertising and Web site content, design and development.

Clients include social service organizations, environmental groups, foundations, religious institutions, educational institutions and cultural organizations.
