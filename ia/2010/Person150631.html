myles mcmorrow’s Summary

I have been around pools and spas most of my life. I am 32 years old and a single father of one son. I grew up in Arlington VA and started working at my first pool at 13 in the snack bar at the Arlington Knights of Columbus. At 15 I became a pool operator and life guard dealing with the day to day operations of the pool.. The following year I became the manager of the whole pool and snack bar ( that is when the summer job got out of control) Shortly after my 17th birthday I was offered a job at Washington DC's premier pool company The Pool Service Company as a crew chief . Three years later I moved to Santa Cruz Ca. where I took on pool fiberglass gel coating and pool building. I was then certified by Independent Pool and Spa Service Association Two years later I had my own company and had every hotel pool at the boardwalk (15) and 35 residential on a route and had 1 employee. I then sold the company 4 years later and moved back to Arlington. Working back at Pool Service Company they sent me to the APSP Tech II pool school and was a certified pool tech with the Association of Pool and Spa Professionals

For the past 5 years I have worked for POOLCENTER.com as a swimming pool expert giving free advice over the phone about pool issues and products. I am in contact weekly with reps of all the top manufactures and in the know about product in or about to hit the market. I am in charge of the social media department and post on the worlds first community for pool owners and techs . I also have 2 twitters poolnewsfeed that covers swimming pool news worldwide and the Pooldrownings , I set up for swimming pool safety organisations to track pool injuries and safety issues associated with swimming pools in the news.
