Frederic Kerrest’s Summary

I am passionate about enterprise technology and have spent the last 12 years helping companies improve their operations by embracing software solutions.

In early 2009, I teamed up with my friend Todd McKinnon to start SaaSure with the vision of helping companies more easily adopt business web applications.

Previously I spent my time
- Evaluating early stage enterprise software companies at Hummer Winblad Venture Partners
- In Business Development and Sales at Salesforce.com (2002-2007)
- As co-founder and director of technology at an award-winning high-tech consulting firm
- As a software engineer at Moai Technologies, Sun Microsystems and Applied Materials

I received a BS degree in Computer Science from Stanford University and an MBA from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology's Sloan School of Management.

Frederic Kerrest’s Specialties:

SaaS (Software-as-a-Service), on-demand computing, customer relationship management software, business and channel development, enterprise sales and sales engineering, sales effectiveness and operations, product development and marketing, operations management, venture capital and private investing.
