Tawana Wood’s Summary

As Founder and Chief Resume Writer of CountrysBestResumes.com, I am proud to have built a market driven and values-based business that helps America's top performing executives understand, effectively express and successfully market their skills, knowledge, and abilities.

I spent more than a decade writing grants and exciting marketing copy for leading organizations including The Conference Board and Junior Achievement. During my last years in the corporate world, I had been asked by several friends and colleagues to help craft attention grabbing and market specific resumes. After much success and tons of hugs from grateful friends, I knew my talents could be used to help others. So I decided to create a business model for experienced leaders, new beginners and fast track executives all struggling to tell the world their story.

TCBSolutions.net was born in 2001 and CountrysBestResumes.com was online in 2002 to meet the growing demand for strategically tailored and powerful career marketing documents. TCBSolutions.net quickly grew into a diverse, talented network of elite resume writers who collaborate to meet the ever-changing needs of today's multi-skilled executive. Today we based in the heart of Manhattan at Madison Avenue and 42nd Street with branches in California, Georgia, and Texas.

Our team is comprised of writers with years of experience in direct marketing, human resource management, branding, and communications.

Executives save money and time by paying TCBSolutions.net for expertly written and results driven resumes. Our resumes reflect what the market wants thus saving time and lost opportunities for our clients. Our clients simply outshine and outmatch other applicants on paper.

"We Make You Look Good On Paper!"
