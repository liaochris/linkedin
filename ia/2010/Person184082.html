Scott Burkey’s Summary

I have worked in all phases of web-based application development, interactive marketing technologies, project management, business consulting and systems integration from pre-sales to implementation to maintenance. I began in technology as a website developer and then moved into positions as Webmaster and Director-level technology management.

Throughout my career I have worked with companies to transform their business through the use of Web 2.0 technologies and interactive marketing tactics. I'm looking to network with people interested in utilizing interactive marketing tactics to drive increased revenues as well as using the power of online advertising to impact their revenues. I spend my time working with clients to realize new revenue streams, increase online revenues and streamline business operations. It's all about making money and cutting costs and I do it for some of the best.

I've done permission based email marketing campaigns and strategy for some of the top brands in the country. I am also a speaker on permission-based email marketing. Search engine marketing and email marketing, along with web site analytics, are what I'm doing a lot of consulting on for clients. The proper use of online video, SMS and social networks are also tactics that I consult clients on how to use effectively.

Currently I am managing the sales efforts for one of the top political media companies in the country. Townhall.com and Townhall Magazine are leading the charge in the conservative media sector. We help grass-roots organizations build email lists, connect with donors and promote their cause. Townhall helps non-profits build donor lists and we help some great advocacy groups gain petition signatures and obtain new sources of funds. PAC’s love Townhall because our audience is exactly who they want to connect with and many consumer goods/services companies have great successes advertising with Townhall.
