Liz Guthridge’s Summary

Works with department leaders of Fortune 1000 companies when they need to introduce a complex new initiative and they're concerned about the adverse impact on affected employees and others who already may be confused, angry, or in denial. I figure out the best way to integrate their initiative into their organization and operations so it supports their strategy and brand. Along the way, I make sure they're clearly communicating and involving all the different affected groups of people so the change will stick. As a result, leaders reach their goals, stay on schedule and budget, and turn employee resistance into acceptance and optimism for the future.

Liz Guthridge’s Specialties:

Change leadership, strategic communications and teamwork. Brings an objective perspective, free of blind spots, echo chambers and other baggage. Tailors solutions that align with the company's identity. Builds a network of internal champions who take ownership of the initiative and ensure that it sticks. Also uses lean principles and practices to add value.
