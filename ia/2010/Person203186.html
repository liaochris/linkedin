Thomas Edmund Turner’s Summary

After attending NYC's Stuyvesant High School from 1972 to 1975, I was admitted to MIT and remained in the Boston-area for more than 30 years. I mainly provided hardware and software support, programming and teaching assistance at Harvard, MIT, and other organizations. As a volunteer, I helped the public access TV and free+open source communities . Recently I returned to my native New York to help reorganize the Port Washington Twin Pines Food Cooperative & Charitable Thrift.
