Isa Alsup’s Summary

VFX-CG Supervisor:

I author “The Art of CG Supervision”, http://cgsupervisor.blogspot.com, which looks at CG from the management point of view.

I work with producers to develop pre-production breakdowns and shot concepts, consult on set, and manage cg post production.

I design pipelines and assemble teams suited to project demands and limits, then manage the vfx post production.


Compositor:

I use After Effects, Digital Fusion or Nuke in compositing or designing vfx shots. I also consult on compositing pipelines and “best practices”. I teach and mentor.


Maya Shader Developer, Lighting Director:

I work with Maya 2009 to design efficient and suitable shaders for Maya or Mental Ray renders. I also am an expert scene lighter.


Maya Shot layout and Shot Finaler:

I layout and block scenes to get interesting and powerful shots. I provide 3d pre-viz. I also prep shots for render, handling final tweaks.


Filmmaker:

I provide vfx-cg supervision, consulting, 3d, and compositing, for film, with about 48 completed projects, many“A” list features. I have worked on three 70mm films and three stereographic large format projects.


Documentarian:

I design and build story graphics for documentaries. I have worked on 30 some documentaries, including more than a dozen series, to provide story graphics for over 130 broadcast hours in SD and HDTV.


Motion Graphic Artist:

I design motion graphics in 3d and 2d, with some 300 or so projects completed. I have an Emmy for SPORTS GRAPHIC DESIGN for my work on NBC's 1992 “Games of the XXV Olympiad”. I have an Aegis for the 2001 “Citation X Experience” corporate sales DVD.


Developer and Technologist:

I support my work and team with scripting. I write scripts to streamline pipelines, meet production needs, and improve artist efficiency. I have developed a particle system renderer, image processing tools and a plug-in suite for compositors.
