Stacie Ross’s Summary

Stacie Ross is a designer and creative consultant with extensive experience in corporate and non-profit marketing communications, corporate identity, brand development, managing large-scale projects from creative through completion and coordinating project teams. As a former Design Director for Nesnadny + Schwartz, she worked with some of Cleveland’s most prestigious employers (Cleveland Clinic Foundation, University Hospitals of Cleveland), as well as renowned educational institutions (Vassar College, Laurel School, Case Western Reserve University). Following a dream of owning her own company, in 2008 she created the visual communication firm Impel Creative, Inc. With this endeavor, she continues her quest to provide attentive guidance to clients looking for skilled, detail oriented project management, timely results, and novel approaches to visual communication challenges. Along the way, her work has garnered dozens of professional awards from design journals, associations and professional competitions. Stacie holds a Bachelor of Arts and Master of Arts, both from Kent State University.
