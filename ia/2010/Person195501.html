Steve Snyder’s Summary

As Entrepreneur-in-Residence at Boston’s premier law firm for technology companies and emerging businesses, I work with entrepreneurs to help them grow their companies.

My passion is providing extraordinary business advice and utilizing my network to create enormous value. And I really enjoy using my financing experience to help companies grow. As a result, I have been able to transform young companies into successful, well-financed organizations, some of which have been acquired or gone public.
