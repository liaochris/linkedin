Bethany A. Riebock, MA, CCC-SLP/L’s Summary

I am a rehabilitation manager and speech-language pathologist for Select Rehabilitation in a 210 skilled nursing facility. Duties include projecting RUG III levels for patients on caselad, collaborating with the MDS coordinator, and directing the interdisciplinary therapy team consisting of occupational therapists, physical therapists, speech-language pathologists, therapy assistants, and rehabilitation technicians.

I am also a foreign accent modification trainer. I am certified in the Compton P-ESL method and offer services through my website, http://innovaspeak.com.
