Kim Roberts’s Summary

I mainly work as a 2d Artist and Computer Games Designer. Games so far include, Wheelman (Xbox360, PS3 & PC), Squibs Arcade (iPhone), Flips interractive book series (DS).

I also illustrate for pre-production (such as concept art and storyboards), colour for Comics, create graphics for Websites and Logos, edits for photography (such as retouching / matte painting / photo manipulation), animate for 2d animation (and sing badly).

My goal is to eventually work as an in-house artist/colourist, or art director or creative director, as well as illustrate for children's books.
