Zac Pasmanick’s Summary

My team of real estate consultants (both listing partners and buyer specialists) have been helping thousands of families with their Atlanta, Decatur, and Buckhead real estate needs for years! I've been in the business personally since 1979. Our services have been expanding to include short sales and helping our Atlanta neighbors avoid foreclosure.
