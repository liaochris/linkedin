Intensely curious, occasionally stubborn - but always passionate about user insight, marketing strategy, branding, product innovation and development.

I develop marketing and product strategy for top- and bottom-line performance. It's a simple recipe... identify market opportunity, understand customer behavior, drive innovation in all you do, differentiate from the other guys, leverage brand (if you have it), execute your plan and tell your story in a unique way that entertains and informs.

Skilled in strategic planning, brand management & product life cycle marketing. Also P&L management, market segmentation, VOC research, ideation exploration/concept exploration, new product development, product road map & horizon planning, product positioning, pricing, sales forecasting, SKU rationalization, outsourcing, cost-out efforts, product launch, co-branding & private label programs, promotions, trade show management for retail & commercial customers in B2B/B2C channels.

Qualified in advertising & communications role including agency experience, campaign strategy, creative development, account planning, copywriting, collateral design & production, packaging, POP/merch, public relations, media buying, public speaking, event planning, vendor relations, web site design & client service management.

Diverse background working at private businesses, public corporations & a Fortune 100 company. Knowledge of chemical, steel & aluminum mfg including kaizen events, value stream mapping, cost-out efforts & SAP.

Whether it's a creative new product strategy or a product strategy needing new creative, the goal is to inspire, motivate & "move the sales needle". Oh, and it's always nice when the boss or client smiles!
