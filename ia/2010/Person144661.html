Mick Thornton’s Summary

My passion is connecting people with information and empowering them to act.
I am a Microsoft Office Specialist Master Instructor and MCAS. I teach employees how to use many of today's most popular computer programs, including Windows SharePoint Services 3.0 and MOSS 2007, Microsoft Office Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Access, Microsoft Project, Adobe Acrobat, and e-learning.
