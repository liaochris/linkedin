Bonnie Landau’s Summary

With nearly 20 years experience in the fields of design and marketing, Bonnie had developed an expertise in design, usability and marketing strategy and implementation. She was an early adopter in web design with over 13 years designing and building sites, and over 7 years doing business analysis, information architecture and user interface design for custom web-based applications. In the past 5 years she has coached dozens of clients in online strategies, website implementation and streamlining business processes through the use of web tools and custom web-based applications.

In addition she is known as somebody who can accomplish a goal, and has extensive experience with project management and working with sole-proprietorships as well as large corporations such as MGM, Sony and NATPE.
