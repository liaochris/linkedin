Gene Hall’s Summary

I love to solve puzzles and organize chaotic information and situations. That skill has been honed through organizing new-to-the-world processes at start-up companies, large data sets in market research, and personnel at fast-growth companies. After my regular job I work on my genealogy - a giant, challenging and never-ending puzzle. I am equally comfortable in a sole-contributor or management role in a start up or small division of a larger company.
