Shailesh Thakkar 8,040+ TopLinked’s Summary

Shailesh Thakkar has over 18 years of broad-based and successful experience in Project Management, Program Management, and Portfolio Management within IT and IS domains and Consulting, Banking, Mortgage Insurance, Logistics, and Manufacturing industries. He has led and managed Project Management Offices (PMO), multiple projects and teams, enterprise-wide initiatives, and large-scale ERP and EPM system implementations.

He managed projects and programs in the areas of infrastructure, application development, product development, and system integration utilizing System Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and Project Management framework.

He is talented and experienced at defining, modifying, and implementing Project Management methodologies, processes, standards, best practices, and IT governance and compliance.

Shailesh has a Master of Science Degree in Project Management. He is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), certified Program Management Professional (PgMP), Certified Information System Auditor (CISA), and a Certified Information Security Manager (CISM). He holds a Master’s Certificate in IT Project Management and a Six Sigma Green Belt Certificate (CSSGB).
