Michelle Leung’s Summary

Michelle Leung is scheduled to graduate from the University of Washington in June 2010 with a major in Business Administration (Concentration in Marketing) and a minor in Women Studies.

Michelle is poised to start her career in Marketing after acquiring diverse and valuable experience in Seattle and New York City through six competitive internships in Advertising, Promotions, Public Relations, and Event Coordination.
