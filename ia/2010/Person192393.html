Michele DeMarco Wilkie’s Summary

Multimedia content creator in the areas of wellness, resilience, and human flourishing. Published author of three books ("Spirit Wellness," "Living Proof," and "Stirred Not Shaken"). Founder of "Build Pathways for Peace," a grassroots advocacy movement committed to building sustainable peace and flourishing around the globe. Creator and practitioner of a new model for health and wellness counseling and conflict transformation. Social commentator on spirit, culture, and wellness.

Current projects include: "The Desire Project," "the Millenium Peace Goals"; "Thriver, Not Survivor," The Center for Spirit Wellness, among others.

Michele DeMarco Wilkie’s Specialties:

Spirit wellness; "The Six Fundamental Human Desires": meaning, purpose, value, connection, resilience, and transcendence; spiritful counseling and advising, Self-HEALTH, conflict transformation, interfaith/intercultural relations, ordained minister, social commentary.
