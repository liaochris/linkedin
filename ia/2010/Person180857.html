Sam Alpert (sam@davidandsampr.com)’s Summary

I help people and companies look good so others want to do business with them.

My job is to tell my clients' stories effectively and to build their brands and reputations in their communities - online, in the media, in front of customers and prospects, in the business community, at work and in social circles.

I have worked with public, private, B2B and B2C companies, start-ups, non-profit organizations and individuals. Some of the industries I have represented include real estate, technology, restaurants, fashion, golf, law, consulting, publishing, healthcare, hospitality/travel, transportation and education.

My personal philosophy is that success is built upon:

-hustle
-respect for others
-integrity
