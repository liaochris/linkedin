Oded Pelled (Felled)’s Summary

Over 12 years of experience in corporate management for consumer products and high technology. Served as CEO, VP of Business Development, and Co-Founder of lifestyle, technology, and investment companies – Best-TV.com and GetFit.com. Recently, founded The 22Group. Managed all corporate activities and strategic partnerships.

Presented to over 50 private investors and 30 venture capital firms in Silicon Valley and elsewhere, negotiated and secured over $15 million in venture financing from a number of venture capital and angel sources.

Devised several highly successful marketing campaigns and won the ‘Best of Show’ and ‘Best Web Service’ awards at the Upside's Digital Living Room 2000 Conference, one of the most prestigious high-tech conferences in the U.S.

Also received the ‘Company to Watch’ award at the prestigious Demo 2000 conference. In August 2008, Oded won the 'Best Start-up' contest for innovative online video companies at a NY prestigious Conference.

A professional International entrepreneur (dual nationalities French and Israeli) with a detail-oriented, balanced and responsible management approach. Oded holds a BA in Economics and a minor in Mandarin Chinese (Graduated Magna Cum Laude) from Stanford University.
