Johnny Campbell,DTM, AS Social Media Marketing,’s Summary

Johnny Campbell is an Professional Speaker and business consultant, who specializes in helping people learn that dealing with: Difficult People, Change, and social media marketing can be Easy,Productive and Profitable.

My expertise and knowledge was gained in the Insurance industry where I participated in leading hundreds of people through multiple Corporate Downsizings and Mergers. Based on those events and my experiences in life he became “The Transition Man”.

My Change, How to deal Difficult people, and social media marketing programs,offer organizations a formula for productivity and profitability.
