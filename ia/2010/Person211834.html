Vikas Khanna’s Summary

Vikas Khanna is an accomplished entrepreneur and executive with domestic and international experience in business operations, technology, and off-shoring. Vikas was a founding team member of NextWeb, Inc. (a VC-backed Wireless Internet Service Provider) where he was tasked with leading the technology strategy for the company. During his tenure, NextWeb grew to dominate its market segment, achieve national recognition as an Inc 500 (#104 in 2005) company, and delivered a successful exit with the acquisition by Covad Communications (now Megapath) in February 2006. At Covad, Vikas continued to serve as the Vice President of Technology where he provided guidance for the company's overall technology growth.

Currently, Vikas holds the position of Managing Director of Zoovee Ventures, a boutique investment and business advisory services firm. In addition, Vikas also serves on advisory boards for venture capital, Internet, Telecom, and non-profit organizations in Silicon Valley.
