Steve Bosso’s Summary

Promoted early in fire/EMS career during major building boom in the city of Twinsburg (Ohio) 1990-current. The fire department expanded three-fold in call volume and staffing. What followed was a very fast-paced transitional phase with fulltime and part time hiring, coaching of entry level fire/medics and mentoring of potential fire officer candidates and all while handling a steadily increasing call volume.

Educational opportunties were bountiful as well as experience gained from commanding incidents across all emergency response disciplines.

Chosen as the #1 suburb in the Cleveland area, Twinsburg remains a great place to live and work. See more at: http://www.mytwinsburg.com/

Work concurrently as Fire Chief for Ravenna Township, which has half the call volume of Twinsburg, but three times the amount of fire and trauma. Mentoring young fire/medics and officers through their formidable years has been the most satisfying part of my seven year tenure at RTFD.

Authored and successfully managed two FEMA Fire Act Grants totalling over $750,000 over a three year period.
