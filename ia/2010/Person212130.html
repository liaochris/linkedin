Vincent Orrico’s Summary

Vince is an experienced Technology professional with over 30 years of success managing teams in the implementation and operation of technology to meet business objectives. Recently he served as CISO and Director of Information Security and Business Continuity at Hudson United Bank until it was acquired by TD Bank. He enhanced IT Governance by directing the implementation of the ITIL framework and coordinated IT related activities to demonstrate regulatory compliance. He worked effectively with executives, managers, and the Board to establish key policies and standards. His success is based on the understanding of organizational behavioral in integrating change and in operations design to achieve necessary synergies with the business and technology.

He practiced financial risk at GE Information Services, Algorithmics, USA, and Deloitte & Touche Capital Markets. His operations management experience included Citibank and he performed business development at AT&T, GE, and SunGard.

Vincent Orrico’s Specialties:

Certified IT Governance (CGEIT), Information Systems Auditing (CISA), Information Security (CISSP), Business Continuity (CBCP), and Project Management (PMP). Also certified as Instructor for Disaster Recovery Institute International.

Vince holds a Doctorate of Professional Studies in Managmeent with Dissertation in Risk Management, an MBA in Management Science, an MS in Math and Computer Science, and a BC in Mathematics.
