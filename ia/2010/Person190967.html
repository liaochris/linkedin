Smart Tax’s Summary

Smart Tax was founded in 2005 by CEO Nick Rizzi, who noticed a void in tax service options, strong customer service, and speed of return in an under-saturated tax preparation segment. After growing Smart Tax to four successful operating locations, Rizzi decided to begin franchising the concept – thus offering the same services in communities nationwide. Built on knowledge, experience, training and years of hard work in financial planning, taxes, insurance, and more, the Smart Tax franchise will launch on January 3, 2010 with 10 locations. Smart Tax is a tax preparation service offering a quick and hassle-free way for its clients to file their taxes each and every year. With access to up-to-date information, technologies, and a proprietary system, Smart Tax projects 100 locations open by January 1, 2012. For more information, visit www.thesmartwaytofile.com.

  • Providing Tax Services

    Smart Tax

    (Financial Services industry)

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    Income Tax Preparation
    We offer excellent tax preparation for individuals, based on efficient and accurate information, without sacrificing customer service. Our goal is to ensure that your tax benefit is available to you, while still complying with the law.


    Electronic Filling
    Filing a paper return means you have to wait for your refund, in most cases, weeks!

    E-file reduces the time it takes to get your tax refund; however, you must have a valid Social Security number for every person included on the return. Our return preparation process is designed to ensure accuracy with every step, helping to keep delays down to a minimum. The next step in the electronic tax filing process is to transmit your tax filing to the IRS and the appropriate state Department of Revenue, if applicable. We receive acknowledgment from the tax authorities, so we can assure you of providing your refund within the time you have chosen. You will know when and how you get your refund money.

    If you qualify to e-file, you may also be able to get a loan on your tax refund in as little as one day by requesting a Refund Anticipation Loan.


    Cash in a Flash
    Refund and Loan Options – With the Refund and Loan Options Program, you can obtain funding almost immediately. You can also select refund and loan methods, such as a Check, Cash Card or Direct Deposit.


    Smart Tax Drop-Off Service
    Bring us your tax information and leave the rest to us. Just complete our quick questionnaire and leave your documents with us. Your preparer will call when your return is ready to be approved and filed. If the preparer needs additional information, they will call and most times are able to collect what you need right on the phone.

    Smart Tax Drop Off Service is a convenient way to help you file your taxes and save time. There is no cost for this service; however you will be responsible for your tax preparation fees.

  • Franchising Opportunities

    Smart Tax

    (Financial Services industry)

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    The Smart Tax franchise program is a business ownership opportunity that is different from others.

    No need to work 52-weeks a year, 7-days a week.
    No need to have a background in financing, accounting.

    The Smart Tax system is well-planned, well-established and well-received. And it can be yours to use when you join as a franchise owner. When you become a part of Smart Tax, you'll have immediate access to training, materials, programs, and documentation that are all created to make your business start-up and operations as efficient as possible.

    Best of all, you can count on the Smart Tax organization to guide you and your clients, both during tax season and - most important - after tax season. You don't even have to keep your office open during the Summer months; just turn everything over to us, and we'll be there to provide the assistance your clients need. What other business model allows you to do that?

    As a Smart Tax franchise owner, you will be provided with the tools you need, every step of the way:

    • Professional training, both at our corporate headquarters and at your location, to ensure you have the knowledge you need to open your doors for business.

    • Documented daily operating procedures.

    • Plenty of start-up assistance, from choosing your location to assisting you in real estate support, including purchasing the right equipment, furniture, and systems.

    • Guidance on marketing to your local community to get taxpayers to your door.

    • Ongoing remote and onsite support during and after tax season!
