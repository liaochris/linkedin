Jack Lucas’s Summary

I am a highly-experienced, technical, Business Development professional, with a broad background in Marketing, Sales, and Field Applications and Design Engineering.

As a veteran Business Development professional, I have been successful at establishing and fostering customer relationships at all levels of the organization, where I have been the key business and technical interface. I have demonstrated talents for developing and executing strategic business-development plans, peeling back the layers to customers’ complex requirements, evangelizing technologies and services, negotiating contracts and pricing, and leading remote Sales teams in pursuing new opportunities.

I have a strong collaborative spirit and am adept at concurrently driving a host of complex issues among geographically-dispersed, multi-cultural, cross-functional teams, including Sales, Marketing, Engineering, upper-management and the factory. In addition, I have significant experience in driving innovative solutions with customers, formulating comprehensive Design and Production Agreements, and justifying engineering costs to upper management, backed by ROI and market analyses.

I have a deep understanding of the Semiconductor industry, and strong technical knowledge of PC/Server hardware and software, embedded design, RISC/CISC/DSP architectures, semiconductor processes, IC design flows, application-specific design considerations, mixed-signal I/O, IC packaging, Semiconductor Intellectual Property and other such subject matter.
