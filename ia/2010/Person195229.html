Steve Levy’s Summary

I have been in practice for over 20 years and have represented clients who are senior executives in companies at the top of the Fortune 500 list as well as those who are local and just starting their business. I have developed a strong international focus and expertise covering a broad range of legal and management tools to clear, protect, license and manage intellectual assets, strategically negotiate business deals, set legal policy and manage tax-advantaged holding companies. I take a very pragmatic, creative and cost-effective approach to counseling and problem solving.
