Vanessa LaClair, CMP’s Summary

Meeting professional in upstate New York, with a passion for travel, shoes and meeting new people!

I am currently the membership services & events coordinator for IPPNY, a trade association in Albany, NY. I have been involved in the meetings & events industry for 10 years. As an active member of Meeting Professionals International (MPI), I currently serve as the vice president of communications and marketing for the Northeastern New York Chapter (@mpineny). In July 2010, I will assume the role of President-Elect for the Chapter.
