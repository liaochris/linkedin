Rollis Fontenot III’s Summary

I specialize in helping Small to Medium Sized Business to increase their sales through helping to either create or improve upon an existing sales process and sales team. I can help you to start your sales department from scratch, or compliment what you are already doing in terms of the sales process and/or execution. I also offer sales training and coaching services for salespeople as well as sales managers including formulating or revising sales training material. I can also serve as either a consultant or project manager when working with clients as I already have working relationships with outside contractors who do exceptional work. I bring over 20 years of personal sales production as well as sales management to the table. I speak professionally to sales departments and business on the subject of sales, personal growth and finance. I also wrote a book on building a sales career entitled "Go Out and Sell Something!" available on Amazon.com.
