John Edwards’s Summary

With 10 years experience handling the recruitment of senior managers and board executives across a variety of disciplines, I am ideally placed to assist as you develop yourself or your business.

I personally work exclusively on the recruitment and retention of senior managers within the power industry, and largely within the nuclear sector. This includes nuclear new build and nuclear decommissioning markets.

My business is conducted through effective communication and regular, honest dialogue. This has lead to a strong personal network which is constantly growing. I am always interested to hear from new individuals who consider this style of communication as an integral part of business.
