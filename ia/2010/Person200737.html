Ted Smith’s Summary

Have been in the interactive media industry since leaving my post-doc at MIT. Most recently co-founded and served as President of MedTrackAlert.com which was acquired by Health Central in 2008 where I work today on analytics and advanced user engagement programs. I also serve as a Senior Research Fellow for the Armstrong Interactive Media Studies program with a focus on social media as it intersects with consumer media and marketing.
My formal education is in Cognitive Science with both a MA and Ph.D. in Cognitive Science. Designed and managed a virtual environment experiment which flew onboard the Space Shuttle Columbia in 1998. Have one patent in real-time 3-D visualization applications in learning.
I greatly enjoy civic involvement and have spent countless hours applying social network analysis approaches to fundraising initiatives for the Obama For America 2008 campaign and more recently I have served as a volunteer evaluator for the federal Open Government initiative.

Ted Smith’s Specialties:

Interactive Media Product Management/Marketing, Market Research, Online Consumer Health care, Social Networking and Advertising. Social Media, Political fund raising online. Non profit fund raising online. Lead Generation, Behavioral and Pyschographic Data Models, Government Transparency through New Media.
