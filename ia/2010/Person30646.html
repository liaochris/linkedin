Buckley Brinkman’s Summary

I am a life-long Change Catalyst, running Vallon, a company that provides high-octane talent for interim situations. We are changing the way companies staff their organizations, by providing custom, C-level talent at the speed of business – usually within 48 hours. Vallon invests a significant amount of time tracking and understanding talent markets, so that we can react quickly and effectively to client needs.

Our model works best in three situations:

1. Vacancies. We can provide the person needed to bridge a gap in the organization created by a vacancy. For example, one client needed to replace their Controller due to performance issues. We found the right candidate and had her in front of the client in 24 hours and on the job within a week. The client was able to strengthen their financial systems and change Controllers without any issues.

2. Activity Surge. Vallon can provide the extra talent to handle upticks in activity without adding to permanent headcount. In these times of uncertainty, organizations can add needed talent without having to commit to new hiring.

3. Projects. Many projects require more expertise than an organization has available. Vallon provides the talent needed for execution without a long-term commitment. In one case, our client needed to put a call center in place. Our expert was on the job within a week, got the call center up and running, and was able to successfully leave the company 100 days later.

Vallon is a great staffing solution for most organizations. We know the talent in the market, we move fast, and all of our principals have held C-level positions. Our expertise matches talent and temperament to needs and company culture.

Call us when you need talent at the speed of business!
