Andrew S. Bonci, BA, DC’s Summary

My interest is in providing high quality health care solutions at affordable rates. This includes forming alliances with other qualified health care professionals and provider groups who are interested in the same goal.

Local, national and international health solutions/tourism is becoming a major thrust in my clinical practice and experience.

I hold a US trademark on a joint care supplement called Lubríca®. This is an avocado-soy unsaponifiable (ASU) which has shown itself to be very effective in the clinical support of cartiage and joints.
