John Hager, PMP’s Summary

I have over 30 years combined experience in the engineering, construction and environmental fields. My experience includes the design and construction of municipal water and wastewater treatment facilities, construction of fossil-fuel power stations and nuclear fuel processing facilities, as well as the environmental restoration of nuclear facilities, including the Chernobyl nuclear power plant in Ukraine. I have also been involved in the execution of demilitarization projects in the former Soviet Union. For the past few years, I have been involved in the remediation and redevelopment of industrial brownfields in the eastern United States. Currently, I am working on the environmental remediation and redevelopment of the Hunters Point Shipyard in San Francisco, CA.

John Hager, PMP’s Specialties:

Heavy construction, nuclear facility construction, design-build, environmental remediation.

  • Sr. Project Manager
