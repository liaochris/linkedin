Brian Fowler’s Summary

Brian's background includes sixteen years of market research, direct marketing, communications, and business analysis. Industry experience includes Green / Efficiency initiatives, Research technology, Advocacy, Retail: Home Furnishings, and Fine Art publishing.

Brian has served on the boards of not-for-profit for over ten years and is currently the President of the NorthWest chapter of the MRA primarily. Positions held with the San Francisco and Nashville chapters of the AMA include Treasurer, VP Meetings, and VP of Special Events. He has served as President for both chapters.

Brian holds a BBA and an MBA, both in Marketing, from Middle Tennessee State University.

Brian Fowler’s Specialties:

Business operations, Market research, database analysis, customer profiling, direct marketing, internet communications
