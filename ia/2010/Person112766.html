Kaustav Bhattacharya’s Summary

My professional career has always been in digital media and entertainment. In the early days of the dot com boom I started out my career as a junior web developer for the BBC. I have since worked my way up through the ranks to senior developer, head of development and head of technology - rolls which have involved managing and building teams, architecting solutions and leading vision and technology strategy. I have spent time working in collaborative environments at Sky TV, Endemol UK, Liberate Technologies in Silicon Valley and time in digital marketing and communications at Publicis Dialog, UK.

My most recent focus has been in social media, monetisation of digital media content, enterprise collaboration technology and cross platform integration of content (online, mobile and set top box).

I'm a pragmatist when it comes to development technology. I have equal experience in developing code using Microsoft and Open Source (LAMP/JAMP) technologies. This helps me to keep a broad perspective on the technology landscape and choosing the best solutions for the projects I am involved in. At work and home I continue this theme by using a Mac, Windows 7, Windows XP, various flavours of Linux and a number of mobile operating systems.

Mobile applications are also a specialist area of mine. I have conceptualised, architected and launched a number of engaging mobile apps, primary for the media and entertainment space.

I am an avid public speaker both in the line my profession and my outside interests beyond work. I regularly gives talks to large audiences on the subject of astronomy, space and observing deep space objects using amateur telescopes.
