    Obstacle Detection System for the Visually Impaired
    - Developed an Obstacle Detection System for the Visually Impaired at the National Association for the Blind, India, for final year engineering project. It is an electronic system to detect an obstacle in the travel path of the user by using sonar sensors and sending appropriate feedback in the form of a buzzer or a vibrator. The system is strategically placed on the traditional white cane, changing the role of the cane from being the primary source of object space sensing to that of being a complementary and especially sensitive safety probe optimally used in conjunction with the greater knowledge of the travel path ahead as provided by sonocular perception. The project was selected for the All India Biomedical Conference 2007, Manipal
