Jonathan Lehman’s Summary

Business and Technical savvy professional with over 8 years of specific Product Management experience and nearly 15 years of professional experience in Marketing, IT, Manufacturing and Management. Exceptional strategic and ideation skills with the ability to quickly spot relevant patterns and issues to identify connectedness between seemingly disparate phenomena. Innate ability to maximize best practice solutions through processes and controls, with strong communication skills as a conversationalist, listener and presenter.
