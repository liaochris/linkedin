Dinesh Katre’s Summary

- Edited book on Human Work Interaction Design: Usability in Social, Cultural and Organizational Contexts has been published by Springer Verlag, Germany in 2010.

- Appointed as Vice Chair for WG TC 13.6 on Human Work Interaction Design of IFIP.

Presently, Dinesh Katre heads the Human-Centred Design & Computing Group at C-DAC, Pune. In this responsibility, he manages multiple teams and coordinates between diverse technology development groups.

During past 17 years at C-DAC, he has conceptualized and implemented many R&D sponsored projects that deal with Digital Museum Archives, e-Learning, Interactive Game Design and Multimedia Authoring. He is the principle designer of many software, content and training products. He has published around 30 research papers in international conferences and journals.

He is invited by universities from USA, UK and Denmark to share his expertise in the area of HCI and Usability. During past 2 years, he has delivered over 70 invited talks on wide ranging topics related to HCI and usability at multinational IT companies, research and academic institutes.
