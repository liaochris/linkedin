Gayl Murphy ~ Dynamic Media Expert’s Summary

Hollywood Correspondent, Media and Presentational Coach, Speaker, Author and Creator of "Interview Tactics! How to Survive the Media without Getting Clobbered."

As a veteran radio/TV broadcaster, journalist, showbiz reporter, media coach, author and speaker; my personal mission is to teach businesses how- to create messages that make millions!

I coach and consult entrepreneurs, authors, showbiz pros, experts and CEO's how-to use the media to tell the world about who they are, what they do and what's next. So they can "pitch it to promote it and tell it to sell it!"

My clients included have included such well-known companies as; NBC, Geffen Records, Parsons Engineering, The Walt Disney Company, XPrize Foundation, Warner Brothers, Nederlander Concerts, Sony Pictures, Gaiam Media, Nettwerk Management, Jensen Communications, Valley Presbyterian Hospital and LaserShield among others. I also work with speakers, life-style gurus and the experts-next-door.

As a Veteran Hollywood Correspondent, my broadcast and media clients have (and in some cases, still do) include BBC Radio, BBC TV, ABC Radio News, Sky News, E! Entertainment Television, Sirius Satellite Radio, APRadio, The Reelz Channel, BigMediaUSA, Ultimate DVD, The Works and HollywoodToday.net
