Aaron Geiger’s Summary

PROFILE:

I have always been fascinated with the hypermedia contextualization of literature and more traditional forms of prose and poetry. In the age of the Internet and sensory media, my goal has been to immerse myself in all forms of literary media and hybridize those forms with new technologies. After I left the U.S. Navy, I combined a powerful work ethic with academia, and quickly learned how to incorporate many types of media while continuing to write poetry and creative nonfiction in order to explain my situations in the world around me. Today I not only write on a daily basis, but I incorporate my writing into film, newsprint, blogs, audio files, stenciling and graffiti, photography, flash media, and videos.
