Megan Pulskamp’s Summary

I am a soon to be graduate of the University of Kentucky. My degree is in Integrated Strategic Communications with emphasis in public relations and I minored in business. Finishing up my internships in the next couple of weeks means I will be in need to a job. Interning at Cornett-IMS has given me wonderful insights to the marketing and advertising world. As an intern with UK Athletics in their media relations office, I was able to assist in writing press releases for various sports. Also, I helped produce and update media guides for the University. With both of these experiences coming to an end, I am looking forward to the next chapter in my life and hope I will be given the chance to learn and succeed in this field.

What makes me different...
I swam for four years at the University of Kentucky where I was extremely successful. As an NCAA All-American and a 2008 Olympic Trial participant, I know how to work hard for what I want. The determination and balancing skills I learned through swimming will greatly help me in the future. I am always one to set high goals, because the higher the goals are set the greater the reward.
