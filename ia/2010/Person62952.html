Elaine Hanzak’s Summary

I provide meaningful, memorable and moving presentations to healthcare professionals and any groups wanting to learn about poor mental health. 1 in 4 of adults are likely to suffer from this at least once in their life. What do you do if it is one of your staff, or loved one or you? Let Elaine Hanzak give you an insight into how her life unraveled due to extreme postnatal depression; how she recovered but most importantly how we all can learn from her journey.
I speak to audiences of any size. Clients include Royal College of Nursing, Open University critical reader, Healthcare Events, Capita, universities, Marce Society, JRA Associates.

Elaine Hanzak’s Specialties:

I motivate health care professionals who may be suffering from low morale!

I inspire health care students to go and make a difference with their patients and convince them that their chosen career is worthwhile!

I educate society and change attitudes that poor mental health affects a certain 'type'.

I offer hope to those currently affected by mental distress.
