Luis Barreto’s Summary

Luis Barreto has over 20 years of broadcast experience, from shooting news packages to editing promos and shows to directing newscasts and now show running, Luis has "done it all."

With this wide range of production experience comes critical thinking, which helps to anticipate, organize and avoid problems in pre-production, production or post production.

Beyond Luis' extensive experience in series and show production, he has also worked in over 30 countries around the globe and feels as comfortable in Shanghai as he does in Beverly Hills.

My goal at the moment is to connect with a company looking for a solid team player like me, with extensive production experience who can assist that company to the next level of development and growth
