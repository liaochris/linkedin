Wojciech Lorenc’s Summary

FILMMAKING
Films in 80+ film festivals in 20+ countries. Winner of 5 film festivals. Canada’s Calgary Fringe Film Festival awarded "And Now a Word From Our Sponsors" “The Best International Short Film” award and Portobello Film Festival in United Kingdom chose is as “The Best Foreign Film”. In the US, The South Padre Island Film Festival awarded it the “Best Short” of the event and the PEC Independent Film Championship picked it as the Winner in the Action/Adventure Category.

COMMERCIAL WORK
Directed over 20 commercials,intros, and DVD/Online promotional videos. ONLINE VIDEO
