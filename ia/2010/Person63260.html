Elias Khnaser’s Summary

Elias is an author, speaker and IT consultant specializing in Virtualization and Cloud Computing technologies. Elias has written many books and articles on the subjects. He has over 12 years of experience in the IT field and has designed and deployed some of the largest Citrix and VMware implementations in the world across multiple verticals including Financials, Manufacturing, Healthcare and others.
