Kenneth C. DeWitt, CPA’s Summary

Businessman and CPA serving clients in a variety of industries, including as an Interim or Part-time Chief Financial Officer, Advisory Board Member, and for Corporate Renewal and Turnaround Management Consulting.

I work "on the ground" with my clients and their management teams in setting, then implementing strategic initiatives that improve profits, cash flows, banker relationships, and quality of life for the owners.

Past associations to various entrepreneurial ventures, including accounting, retail, agriculture, consulting, consumer finance, overseas travel companies, and commercial real estate.

Experienced in not-for-profit boards (Healthcare, Arts), including heading Finance and Audit Committees.

Enjoys formulating and executing business strategy and integrating accounting to serve as the real-time measurement tool of strategy implementation.

Published author of over 300 articles and 2 books on financial and business matters. Included six years authoring the column "Bottom Line" for The Tuscaloosa News, and eight years writing the "Finances" column for Commercial Carrier Journal Magazine, a Randall Reilly publication.

One of the two books I"ve written is linked on this profile in "Websites". See "How to Use Financial Statements".

Speaker, presenter at numerous trade conferences across the USA and Canada, active in recruiting for Reznick on university campuses.

Ken is married to Pamela Mund DeWitt for 22 years, and they are raising a daughter, Morgan, age 12, in Tuscaloosa, AL.
