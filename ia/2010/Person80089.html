Pat & Wayne Harriman’s Summary

Pat & Wayne are the owners of Harriman Real Estate, LLC in Wallingford, CT. They service New Haven, Hartford and Middlesex County, particularly Wallingford, Meriden, Cheshire, Southington, North Haven, Hamden, Durham, Middlefield, North Branford, Cromwell and Waterbury.

They have been consistently recognized as one of the top listing and selling agent in their market and are also known for their consistently high standards of ethics and customer service.

Pat & Wayne also both hold the e-PRO designation, which provides expertise in the use of Internet technology and is held by less than 8% of all Realtors. In addition, Wayne is an Accredited Staging Professional, holding both the ASP and AHS designations, and Pat will soon be adding the GRI designation, which is held by less than 20% of all realtors.

Harriman Real Estate prides themselves on helping their clients fulfill their dreams of owning the perfect home. Through their aggressive marketing, attention to detail and caring philosophy, Harriman Real Estate is the right team to help you meet all your real estate needs.

Pat & Wayne Harriman’s Specialties:

Harriman Real Estate specializes in the purchase and sale of residential and income property within New Haven, Hartford and Middlesex counties.
