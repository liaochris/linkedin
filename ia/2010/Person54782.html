Dennis Sellers’s Summary

I love games and puzzles—card games, board games, crossword puzzles, brain teasers. Even more than playing with someone else's games, I love to build them myself. I enjoy creating the graphics for the playing pieces and backgrounds, but most of all, I enjoy the challenge of designing player interactive experiences and building them in ActionScript.

In my current internship with Fly Design Media, I'm learning a lot about the "real world" of game development with ActionScript 3 AND I'm learning a lot about the kind of games young kids like to play.

I've been a teacher and I'm very much interested in creating EDUCATIONAL games. The Sippy Cups website (where my game appear) seems like an excellent place to teach the kids as well as to entertain them and I'm looking forward to moving into that area.
