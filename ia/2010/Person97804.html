    (Public Company; NCR; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2006 — March 2009 (3 years 3 months)

    Engineering\Project Manager, (Hardware Engineer IV) EasyPoint Automatic Teller Machine (ATM) and Kiosk product line

    Led a six person team of three electrical engineers, one component engineer, and two quality assurance technicians in product lifecycle management of Easypoint electronic assemblies. Coordinated new product introductions (NPI) with contract manufacturers (CM) in USA, Mexico, China, and India.

    Developed keypad for securely entering personal identification numbers (PIN) into automated teller machines (ATM) or Kiosks which led to US patent US 7270275.

    Served as subject matter expert for all required agency approvals (ANSI, UL, ETL. CE, CSA, TUV, RoHS, and FCC).

    Designed project management templates used during product research and development (R & D) cycle.

    Hired, trained, and coached staff. Maintained the highest employee retention rate in the business unit.

    Negotiated prototype and production pricing on printed circuit board assemblies (PCBA), discrete digital and analog components, and cable assemblies with external vendors.
