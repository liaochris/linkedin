Anamaria Setti’s Summary

Anamaria has been a design pro for fourteen years spanning several industries, a client pleaser in any environment be it agency, design studio or corporate. Super creative and stylistically diverse, Anamaria thrives in dynamic environments and she's comfortable working independently or collaboratively within multi-disciplinary teams. A natural problem solver and skilled communicator, she understands marketing goals and translates them into unique creative solutions. Her strong interpersonal skills enable her to build and maintain client relationships as well as work closely and efficiently with outside vendors. Project management experience supports her creative side with the business edge that delivers results. From brainstorm to final artwork, signoff and press approval, Anamaria gets the job done on brief, on deadline and on budget.
