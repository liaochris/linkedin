Peter Trudelle’s Summary

Senior software manager with exceptionally broad experience in software development and engineering, including software project and program management. Expert at building superb, globally distributed teams and leading them to reliably create world-class products and services on time. Proficient in concurrent development of large, complex, date-driven projects implementing consumer-facing applications for web and desktop platforms. Strong record of success in planning and driving major projects from concept to launch. Able to draw on a wealth of experience, yet quickly drill down to resolve deep technical issues. Willing and able to do what it takes to get the job done right, tempered with good judgment in determining what that is in each case.
