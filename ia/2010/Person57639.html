Dara Noble’s Summary

An enthusiastic and dedicated advertising professional, based in the Philadelphia area. Passionate about all forms of emerging technology and always seeking to gain a deeper understanding of its use in the development of effective, interactive campaigns and online experiences, to truly connect with consumers.

I embrace the digital lifestyle to the max (always plugged into the iPod, reading the news on my Droid, tweeting commentary about interesting articles on Twitter, and checking in on Foursquare, etc), but also recognize and appreciate the importance of giving back to the community.

Using my unique blend of skills, I serve on two committees in the Philadelphia area. Recently, I joined the Philly Ad Club Young Professionals Committee and work with an amazing group of people to plan events focusing on the professional and personal growth of young professionals.

Honing in on my natural talent for event planning, I was appointed Executive Assistant for Philadelphia Fashion Week, and have been involved since its inception last fall. (Mark your calendars for this year's event in October - http://www.philadelphiafashionweek.org)

Each day, I continue to be fascinated and inspired by the ‘creative revolution’ in the advertising industry. The emergence of social media, adoption and ubiquity of mobile, advancements in web standards, and the list goes on and on … is changing every aspect of our daily lives as consumers (and advertisers). Words can not express how excited and honored I am to be apart of this!
