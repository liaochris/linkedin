Alisa DellaRocco’s Summary

I am experienced in effective online and offline marketing, merchandising and sales. I have experience with catalogs and direct mail as well as ecommerce, which is where I remain today. I have managed all levels of accounts both from a business development standpoint and from an account management perspective. I've worked at start up companies and at larger established companies.

Currently I am responsible for developing partnership strategies for new and existing ventures. Negotiating the terms that will work, on boarding and executing the launch.
I work to bring on key anchor accounts and to maintain and build these relationships moving forward.

I am experienced in management of product merchandising & buying, negotiate pricing, terms and inventory levels. I enjoy building relationships and being creative with clients/retailers and watching as we all grow and become more and more profitable.
