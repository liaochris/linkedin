Dr. Assaf E. Sagiv’s Summary

Global IP was formed as a result of a dream come true out of the understanding that unique technologies are always in a search for investment to manifest. It is the mission of Global IP to connect technologies to the appropriate funding that will allow them to come alive.
My Ph.D. is in Pharmacology and I bring 12 years specializing in Pharmaceutical Sciences and Drug Research and Development.
Additionally, an MBA in marketing, strategy and entrepreneurship help me with my last three years of business development, strategy, finance, marketing and alliances experience, focusing on the life sciences industry (pharmaceutical, biotech, medical devices and diagnostics) and in the cleantech industry.
I help firms, individuals and technology transfer organizations in scouting for them the appropriate partner or investment or in establishing new technology start up companies, negotiating license deals and bringing them all to their next milestone.
I love to meet people and help them make their dreams come true.
