Dean Guadagni’s Summary

My goal is to be the driving force behind the development and implementation of direct marketing driven social media strategies that help companies deliver their messages to strategically targeted audience and develop a loyal customer following. I work with clients to leverage blogs, Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin and other social networks as vehicles for marketing, direct to consumer sales, business development, customer service, and reputation management with a methodology for measuring the effectiveness of these efforts.

A member of the content syndication service Blogburst.com, I author a nationally recognized business self help blog, as well as co-author our Inner Architect blog. I have had over 140 articles published by mass media agencies Reuters.com, FoxBusiness.com, Nielsen.com, Chicago Sun-Times, Computer Shopper, and Ziff Davis Enterprise.

I am a frequent public speaker and workshop facilitator on such topics as Web 2.0 and social media strategies,blogging, and networking.

Prior to joining Inner Architect, I worked with management consulting firm Domus Consulting Group. Our mission was to help real estate brokerages and affiliates develop technology marketing strategies in real estate.
