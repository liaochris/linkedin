J. Anthony Chavez’s Summary

Admitted to practice before the bars of the States of California (1981), New York (1986), and Texas (1982), the United States Supreme Court (1986), and the United States District Courts for the Central (1983), Northern (1982), and Southern (1982) Districts of California; the Southern (1982), Western (1983), and Northern (1991) Districts of Texas; and the Eastern and Southern Districts of New York (both 1986).

Mr. Chavez is a member of the American Bar Association and the Houston Bar Association.

J. Anthony Chavez’s Specialties:

Antitrust, Information technology, International commercial transactions, technology licensing.
