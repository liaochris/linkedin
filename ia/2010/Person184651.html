Scott Neslund’s Summary

Experienced media and marketing executive with a substantial background in agency management. Have led media agencies in the U.S., Canada, Europe and Asia with a proven track record in creating turnaround situations, leading digital communications and growing revenue.

Ability to act as a change agent who can quickly assess and then capitalize on a company’s strengths while shoring up areas of weakness. Have guided the restructuring of many agency businesses including Mindshare North America, Mindshare Chicago, StarLink and Starcom Canada. All reinvention plans generated substantial revenue growth and service innovation while positioning for long-term success.

A leader in media innovation with the ability to successfully communicate a vision and manage people. Media work in my career has been recognized by the top industry awards: Cannes Media Lion, the Effie’s, Mediaweek’s Media Plan of the Year, Media’s Creative Media Awards, and Jack Myers’ Media Innovation and Creativity Awards. Named a 2008 Media Maven by Advertising Age and Mindshare was featured as the only media company named an “A-List Agency” for 2008.

Believe it’s time for media services to play a more elevated role in the marketing and business process. My experiences below highlight how I’ve put this belief into action over the course of my career.
