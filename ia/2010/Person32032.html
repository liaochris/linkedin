Jill Clarvit’s Summary

I am a Certified Professional Coach and Energy Leadership Index Master Practitioner with over 25 years experience in business including marketing, advertising and 4 years in network marketing. I was happily downsized from my corporate job of 12 ½ years and have never looked back.

As a Certified Professional Coach, I work with gastric bypass and lap band surgery clients as well as individuals who want to get healthy. My own journey has led her to a 180 lbs weight loss over the past 7 years. My distinctive background and experience has enabled me to help her clients to achieve wonderful results and breakthroughs.

My coach training and certification is through IPEC (Institute for Professional Empowerment Coaching), a training program accredited by the International Coach Federation (ICF).

Owner of several home based businesses, I have experience working with women, entrepreneurs, small business owners and employees to help them to ascertain their relationships with their customers and their colleagues. I believe anything is possible and my intuitive ability aids me in guiding my clients to achieve their goals. I am currently accepting new clients. E-mail me at cj77coach@yahoo.com.
