Jeff Dyck’s Summary

I’m a dreamer, idea guy, and entrepreneur. I get to work at the things I enjoy most and help others do the same. Following my heart has always been important to me, and at the moment that translates into taking my passion for the outdoors and fanatical pursuit of fly fishing to transform the way fly anglers experience their pastime. Rooted in a deep understanding of the relationships, experiences, and stories of anglers; we’re rethinking, reshaping, and re-energizing the business of fly fishing.

Earlier this year I was involved in a consumer goods start-up in various capacities including writing the business plan, locating start-up capital, setting up logistics and operations, and forming relationships with both suppliers and customers.

I’m a very inquisitive person. When I’m not working on start-ups I spend a lot of time networking, learning, and researching many different types of industries, business models, and concepts. I recently turned this passionate pursuit of information into a part time career as a market researcher. Past projects have included work for clients in the contract innovation, consumer goods, and national clinic spaces. I just finished working with a client on gaining understanding of cultures within pharmaceutical companies and am now working on a brand monitoring project.

In my free time you’ll find me in the woods or sand trap at the local golf course, untangling fly line at the local trout river, learning a new computer program (Flash & Dreamweaver right now), learning to speak a new language (broken Spanish), working on completing my education, reading, or hanging out with friends.
