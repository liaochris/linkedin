Paul Murad’s Summary

Paul Murad is a small business owner, community activist, author and candidate for Nevada's Lieutenant Governor.

Paul grew up in the former Soviet Union, living with his mother in a one-room apartment after his father passed away when he was young. He dreamed of coming to America and when he was a teenager that dream came true. Since coming to the U.S., Paul has received his degree in Finance and International Business, became a citizen, worked in international business development, started his own small business and been actively involved in his local community. Paul has lived the American Dream and now wants to bring all Nevadans the opportunity to pursue the American Dream by creating jobs and diversifying the economy.

He plans to do this by focusing on economic development, expanding tourism and green jobs.

Paul has over ten years experience in business development and marketing and will use his expertise to help Nevada’s economy. Paul is committed to a proactive 50-50 Plan where he will open discussions with fifty top domestic and fifty top global corporations. As Nevada's ambassador, Paul will encourage companies to open corporate headquarters, manufacturing facilities or distribution/warehouse space in Nevada. This will create jobs, bring investment to and diversify our economy.

He will work to establish new direct flights to both McCarran International Airport and the Reno/Tahoe International Airport. Paul will work to move the high speed train connection between Las Vegas and California forward, which will bring more visitors to Nevada. He will also be actively involved in the submission of Reno/Tahoe’s bid for the 2022 Winter Olympics.

The development of green jobs is a natural fit for Nevada because of our abundant sources of clean energy, such as solar, wind and geothermal. Paul will make the expansion of green technology and manufacturing a priority in our state in order to create jobs and export green expertise to our neighbors.
