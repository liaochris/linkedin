Claudia Carral-Allouche’s Summary

Claudia Carral Allouche is the LATAM Recruiter for RIM. She has Business Development/ Executive Recruiter Experience.

Claudia has over 15 years of experience in the IT and telecommunications industry with a skills set that is well leveraged across all aspects of marketing, sales, management and business development. Her executive leadership and in-depth knowledge of the industry complements her innate abilities to conceptualize, communicate, and support expansion strategies in fast-growing organizations.

Her previous experience as a partner in CONFISA shows Claudia’s efforts have directly impacted the fast expansion of the business. Previously, Claudia held strategic positions in telecommunications and technology companies such as AVANTEL MCI Worldcom, VIA NET.WORKS (ISP Security Broadband Services) and ISAT where her sales and marketing talents were leveraged across the Latin America region. She holds a Masters Degree in International Marketing, a BA in Business Administration and speaks several languages fluently.

Currently she is in the "Executive Recruiting Industry." By way of background, Claudia specializes in search for professionals with over 8 years of experience providing Human Resources Consulting Services to Executive firms. Her services are provided considering a wide variety of industries and organizational areas. Claudia has successfully recruited professionals for lead positions throughout the United States, Central and Latin America.
