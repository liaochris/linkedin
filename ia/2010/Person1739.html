Andrea Bauer’s Summary

Andrea brings both national and international event planning experience to the EPIC team.

Prior to joining EPIC in 2007, Andrea managed collegiate events in the United States and abroad for Oklahoma City University. Her event management experience, paired with an Arts Management degree from Oklahoma City University brings to EPIC’s clients a multifaceted and creative approach to developing customized events tailored to each client’s needs and objectives.

In speaking of her desire to use events to convey a message, Andrea says, "There is a tipping point when an event, paired with a clear purpose and professional execution, inspires a passion." Andrea's passion for life in and out-side the office is seen in her involvement in the community, her active membership in Kansas City Meeting Professionals International and her desire to cultivate new relationships in the Kansas City business community.
