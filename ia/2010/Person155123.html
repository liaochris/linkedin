Nikki Fowler’s Summary

Owner and Creative Director of GLOBE NEW MEDIA. Nikki Fowler has served as Publisher and Creative Director for a luxury brand publication, GLOBE LUXE, circulated in high end distribution points in Europe, South Beach, NY, LA and the Caribbean. For the agency, she has served over 200 clients large and small including travel, fashion, beauty and global beverage companies such as Heineken and Diageo Worldwide. She has been a hands on publisher and creative director, overseeing staff and freelancers worldwide as well as directing, designing and producing photo shoots.

Nikki Fowler spearheaded the publication at the age of 23 and served as Publisher and Partner for the company internationally. She served as Creative Director for the magazine as well as the in house ad agency. Not only did she acquire over 200 clients including LVMH, Versace, Dior and Chaumet, but she also oversaw the acquisition of new business and branding outside of the publication. She has recently oversaw the development of the company from print to digital, with it's latest offerings for online publishing including glittermagrocks.com. In 2003, she was named a top ten female publisher by the American Airlines, in flight publication, Skywritings.

She has expert web and print design and development skills and a highly developed aesthetic sense, with an expert eye for design. Her background in ecommerce, fashion, luxury and entertainment publishing, traveling globally and supervising large production staffs as well as internal departments, gives her a wonderful advantage in design and publishing. Her global travels, strong sense of color and cutting edge technique blends with the industry standards to create eye catching colorful projects which draw in consumers. Currently she works on multiple contracted interactive and print projects including magazines and e-magazines, merchandise development for luxury and teen markets, as well as branding and development of e-commerce companies.
