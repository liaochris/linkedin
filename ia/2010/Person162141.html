Peter Ellis’s Summary

I have a passionate interest in information organization and how information influences our lives. I am a technology evangelist, a believer in and strong proponent of environmental sustainability, a writer, a thinker, and above all, a synthesizer. I live by the mantra of "everything is interconnected; context is king": we cannot ignore connections between ideas or actions, but we also cannot ignore the context of those connections and the context in which actions are taken. These two things taken together determine how information is interpreted.

I believe I can help people conceptualize the role that information plays for them, and concentrate primarily on information organization.

I live at the intersection between environmental sustainability and information management, seeking to describe and enrich the connection between the two disciplines whenever possible. In my ideal world, I am allowed to leverage both passions.
