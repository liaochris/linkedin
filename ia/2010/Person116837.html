Kim Conger’s Summary

I have been selling real estate in the Charleston, South Carolina area since 2002. Before that, I worked with Charleston's Building and Remodeling Magazine and various local newspapers. I am eCertified and am a Certified New Home Specialist. I have sold both Commercial land and buildings as well as Residential land and homes in Mt. Pleasant, Awendaw, Cainhoy, Daniel Island, downtown Charleston, North Charleston, Summerville, Goose Creek, West Ashley, James Island, Johns Island and the Isle of Palms.

I enjoy both the marketing and representation of my sellers as well as finding the perfect home for my buyers.

If you have any specific questions or would like to sign up for my newsletter, please contact me by email, kjs.conger@gmail.com, or phone 843.452.6652. You may also sign up to receive automatic updates about sales and listings in your neighborhood, or for properties that may be your next home or investment. You may also search the entire Charleston area MLS on my website www.KimConger.com.
