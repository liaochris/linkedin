Jeffrey Taylor’s Summary

Mercury Public Affairs specializes in high-value public affairs at the intersection of business, government, politics, and media. Core capabilities include public affairs campaign management, grassroots coalition building and management, public opinion research, advocacy advertising, crisis management and political consulting. In addition, Mercury owns Crosslink Strategy Group, a leading national grassroots consultancy and IGR Group, a national bi-partisan government relations firm.

Mercury's corporate division provides counsel to some of America's leading companies. Mercury and its operating units have offices in Washington, DC, New York City, Albany, NY, Tampa, FL, Sacramento, CA, Los Angeles, CA, Chicago, IL, and St. Louis, MO.

Mercury's political division has been involved in winning national, international, state and local political campaigns.

Mercury Public Affairs is a part of Omnicom Group Inc. (NYSE: OMC), a leading global advertising, marketing and corporate communications company.

Mercury's government relations arm, IGR, is a well regarded and substantive bipartisan lobbying firm based in Washington, DC.

IGR’s political experts, policy specialists, and skilled professionals have served as strategists, appointed officials, and elected leaders at all levels of government, giving them a unique insight into how leaders think and make critical decisions.
