John-Chris Trinder’s Summary

I manage 3 teams in the Michael Page Banking and Financial Services division based in New York.

Michael Page Accounting and Finance is a specialist team which is divided in to 3 key business units; Product Control, Corporate Control and Investment Management Accounting. Each business unit has consultants who have previously worked in the financial services industry

Michael Page Tax is a dedicated tax division which specializes in working with tax professionals and finding them new career opportunities in the New York, New Jersey and Connecticut region.

Michael Page Treasury is a new team within Michael Page which works with Treasury, Liquidity and Funding professionals to find roles within banks and other financial institutions.

Michael Page International is a publicly listed global executive search firm. With over 35 years of recruitment experience and offices in 28 countries, Michael Page offers specialist and tailored recruitment services to a diverse range of clients.

I have an MA from the University of Nottingham and a BA from the University of East Anglia in the UK. I have recruitment experience in both the London and New York markets
