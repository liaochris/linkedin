Barry J Holmes’s Summary

Barry began his career at Timeout magazine london where he was taken on as the 1st full time staff photographer. His assignments began with shooting documentary images for the various sections the publication was best known for in london. After a short while the magazines image changed as the face of reporting changed during the 90’s.

His art directors and editors started him on a route towards the images he’s best know for in the celebrity, music and lifestyle area of the business. After eight years of what he calls his double university, he went freelance.

Today he’s based on the West Coast of the United States, shooting commissions around the US and in London for many publications, record labels and advertising companies. He also has some of his music work Featured at the Hard Rock Hotel in San Diego.

Barry J Holmes’s Specialties:

Shoot Producing and production from commission, conceiving and art direction of the photo shoot to post production and delivery.
