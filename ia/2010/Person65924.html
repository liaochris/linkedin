Eric Hempler’s Summary

I’ve never really considered any one town in the Twin Cities as my hometown mostly because I grew up in the suburbs when I was a kid. We lived in Brooklyn Park when I was in elementary school for a short time and then Plymouth and finally Farmington, where I finished High School. After High School I went over to Eau Claire for a short time studying music and later decided to focus on Real Estate and decided to get a two year degree in Real Estate at Dakota County Technical College. The program they have is pretty well rounded and covers all the different careers in Real Estate as well as providing the classes you need to get your Real Estate License.

I first tried Property Management and ended up helping my dad out with Commercial Appraisal. At the time our main focus was Apartment Buildings and I found myself playing a few other roles, such as remodeling an apartment building, caretaker, leasing agent and apartment manager. During that time I flipped a couple of homes and did some accounting work. After finding out I would be a father I decided to work from home a Realtor. I chose Keller Williams because of the educational material they have available on and offline. I also enjoy the environment because we work as a team and share ideas.

Aside from Real Estate my other interests include Home Remodeling, Landscaping, Blackjack, Poker, Horse Racing, Baseball, Sim City, Child Development, Grilling, Investing, Muscle Cars, Music Theory, Economics and Current Events. I still play my Sax from time to time and listen primarily to Jazz and Blues Music. My all time favorite musician is Miles Davis with John Coltrane in a close second.
