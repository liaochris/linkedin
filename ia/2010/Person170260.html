Rebecca Burch’s Summary

I am certified to teach Art K-12. I currently teach art to grades 6-12. My middle school classes are 9-week "exploratories," where I give a brief overview of art. My sixth graders study world art, focusing on a different continent each week. My seventh and eighth graders focus on a different element and principal of art each week.

My high school courses are challenging year-round art courses designed not only to teach art creation and aesthetics, but also to give an understanding of major movements in art history and world art, and to also gain insight into practical applications of art in the "real world." When students leave my classroom, they have design skills that will help them in any field -- digital photography and editing, video editing, animation, drawing and painting, along with design skills that can help them in creating neat and aesthetically-pleasing presentations, websites, slideshows, home decor, etc...

I am also a freelance photographer and painter. I work primarily in digital photography at the moment, but I also work in oil paints, mixed-media, collage, and experiment in many other areas. You can find my art at http://www.rebeccaburch.com and also at http://carpeyousomediem.etsy.com
