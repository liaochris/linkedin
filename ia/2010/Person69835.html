Steve Filipiak’s Honors:

I have been developing complex Web sites since attending the 2nd annual NCSA Mosaic conference in 1994.

My first commercial Web site launched in February 1995 and I haven't slowed down since. Later in 1995, I was the first person placed on the team that went on to create ChicagoTribune.com. After exploring the forefront of online news information design, I moved on to design the first Digital City Web site, Digital City Chicago.

In 1997, I became one of the founding members of Classified Ventures, where I was responsible for their real estate websites: NewHomeNetwork.com, Apartments.com, and MovingCenter.com.

Two years later, the lure of adventure travel became too much, in August 1999 I joined iExplore.com as Executive Producer, where I stayed for over 5-years.

In 2006, I joined Abercrombie & Kent, as Director of Internet Marketing and Operations.
