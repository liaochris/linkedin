Pete Oakley’s Summary

Pete Oakley is an experienced and passionate brand marketer with a background in fashion / lifestyle & entertainment marketing. He possesses an extensive network of celebrity, media and influencer contacts / relationships, a "passion for fashion" and lifestyle entertainment marketing, along with a holistic approach to brand marketing.

A driven entrepreneur, Oakley owns Nuance Brand Consults LLC., a marketing consultancy with a focus on youth and urban lifestyle cultures and created to aid fashion and lifestyle companies in thier attempts to reach and appeal to tastemakers.

Nuance provides strategic direction, brand integration, media & public relations, integrated marketing. He proactively identifies opportunities to extend the reach of client brands via editorial opportunities, special events, fashion shows, television credits, etc. Oakley is also a great "connector", often being the liaison between various parties.

He has garnered many brand placements in a variety of mainstream and "urban" media outlets, spanning print, broadcast & digital / online, including, but not limited to: Life & Style, Essence, Teen People, Elle Girl, MTV, BET, VH1, HBO, FUSE TV, Access Hollywood, Seventeen, Source, KING, Vibe, AllHipHop.com, Antenna, XXL, DNR, Fashion Market Magazine, Black Men, Dime, Dub, Slam, Teen Vogue, Hip Hop Weekly and more.

Since venturing out independently, Pete has had the opportunity to work with several brands & companies in a consulting capacity including: Kani Ladies (by Karl Kani), Live Mechanics, FLUD Watches, Liberation Lab, LaVie Clothing, SoJones.com, Producer's Advantage (celebrity green rooms), Deuce Brand (sports-fashion watch)
