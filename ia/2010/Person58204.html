Don Lyons, Partner and Consultant

Donald J. Lyons, Partner and Founder of InterOPERANT assists healthcare providers in insuring their clinical and business technology initiatives support the business goals and the company mission.

Mr. Lyons has over 20 years experience in the Healthcare Technology Industry. He began his career as an MT (ASCP) Medical Technologist, and Board Certified, Clinical Microbiologist - American Society of Clinical Pathologists. After Graduate school he developed, and pursued a keen interest in Healthcare Information Sciences, and Technology, and completed an MBA, Finance. Also, Don has achieved recognition by the ACHE (American College of Healthcare Executives) as Fellow (FACHE). He has worked in all sectors of the healthcare technology industry, providing senior leadership in advanced technology, and business solutions world-wide.

Prior to forming Stonewall Consulting LLC, and and then InterOPERANT, Mr. Lyons has served key roles in many notable organizations. He was the Vice President of Operations at Emerging Health Information Technology, a spin-off subsidiary of Montefiore Medical Center in New York. The President, and CEO of Life Biosystems AG, Basel, Switzerland, a private shareholder company founded while he was the Deputy CIO of MD Anderson Cancer Center at the University of Texas. At Life Biosystems, Mr. Lyons international team of scientists, bio-informaticians, and businessmen developed applications integrating patient Molecular Data and Clinical Data for the pharmaceutical industry. His prior roles include Chief Technology Officer of a Wall Street healthcare investment firm (Equity Healthcare Partners LLC), Deputy CIO, The University of Texas, MD Anderson Cancer Center, CIO and Senior Administrator of the largest public hospital in Washington (Valley Medical Center), and Principal, and Founder of Insight Healthcare Partners in Washington.
