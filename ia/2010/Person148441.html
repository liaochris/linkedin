Monica S. Flores’s Summary

I am a principal at 10K Webdesign, now celebrating our 6th year in business. I have been an active web developer since 1999, starting with hand-coded HTML sites and moving into custom content management systems, Wordpress and Joomla, and Drupal custom installation and development.

My focus as a web developer is in creating websites that build community and foster a feeling of connectedness. Our company currently supports women entrepreneurs and fair trade, organic, holistic, sustainable, and green businesses. Typical clients include public agencies, national, state and local nonprofits, membership organizations, and e-commerce vendors.

Past speaking engagements include seminars and workshops with: Podcamp and Wordcamp Hawaii, Hawaii Women's Business Center, Mills College, Young Women Social Entrepreneurs, The Filipina Women's Network, Young Nonprofit Professionals Network, Oakland City Council, and Asian Pacific Americans for Progress.

Find over 500+ articles on women, web design, and business at ASuccessfulWoman.com, WomenonBusiness.com, TheGreenGirls.com and GreenBusinessWomen.com. I'm the author of Fifty-one Ways to Build your Community of Clients Online and Social Networking for Women in Business, available on Amazon or as e-downloads.

I am passionate about educating, empowering, and connecting women of color, particularly those using business as a vehicle for personal and professional growth.

Connect with me by searching for @monicadear
