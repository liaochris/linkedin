Dean DeLisle’s Summary

Thanks for visiting my LinkedIn profile today. I’ve helped over 4,000 clients connect with over 25 million leads and close over a billion dollars in sales by helping them make the connections that count. I’d like to help you too.

“Online networks have fascinated me since I was young,” says Dean. “When I started out in the early eighties, leading the night shift at Merrill Lynch’s first data center in Chicago, I caught my first glimpse of online networking power. During my shift, I had instant online access to hundreds of people in other financial data centers across the globe. I learned fast that online networks were an unbeatable source of new friends, valuable connections, and unmatched power to get business done.”

For the past 25 years, I’ve been helping businesses accelerate their contacts, build their revenue and streamline their operations using leading-edge strategies, sound business practices, powerful technology, and cost-effective consulting, coaching, and training.

Let’s connect and start working together to build your business today.
