Who is Eric Benson?

Whether developing film and media concepts, directing a production or producing behind the lens, my defining fingerprint is a nurturing yet efficient work-style; a motivating charisma that has taken seasoned talent from great to greater; the ability to generate creative messaging by designing and delivering in creative ways.

Recent films:
"Sundown" (2008), "Digital Cell" (2009), "My Name is Joe" (2010), "Gideon's Gift" (2011). In development: "Flight School", "Elfego".

Recent video product launches:

Recent industrials:
American Eurocopter: segments showcasing the US Army’s UH-145 aircraft were featured on CNBC’s Power Lunch. Cosmopolitan: promo trailer for Cosmo's new resort & casino in Las Vegas.

Recent stage:
Avenged Sevenfold's preliminary conceptual video elements for "Taste of Chaos". Broadway's Beauty and the Beast, Spamalot, Hairspray, The Lion King, Tarzan, and Wicked.

Recent concerts:
Pat Green, Hootie & The Blowfish, Jo Dee Messina, The Eagles' Don Felder, and The Charlie Daniels Band.
