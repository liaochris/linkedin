Anas Qtiesh’s Summary

I'm passionate about social media and citizen journalism, and how technology helps bring people together. Working with Global Voices Online and with Meedan gave me great insight on the interwebs, especially in the MENA region.

Working at Weber Shandwick PR for over a year and half gave me a unique understanding of the world of PR and honed my press release writing skills. I worked on a variety of projects including MTN, Syria's largest mobile phone provider, and Microsoft, among others.

Also, my experience volunteering with the Syrian Red Crescent made me a well organized team player capable of working under pressure and in harsh physical and psychological conditions.
