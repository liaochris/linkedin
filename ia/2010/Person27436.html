Brenda Krueger Huffman’s Summary

Brenda Krueger Huffman is a thoughtful & easy to relate with business development & marketing social media content & collaboration writer. Her articles & posts display observation & opinion from her highly successful executive business experience & intelligence incorporating substance, relativity & humor.

Her writing focus is national political commentary, business, technology, & social media with an occasional article in the animal welfare & family resource sector. Brenda is a Correspondent with World City Press' AXcess News. She is the featured columnist of "The Point" with TNNW publications in addition to writing & collaboration content for various blogs, websites, corporate newsletters, corporate annual reports, and by consulting agreement.

She has been an executive at some of the foremost national information technology corporations such as Transamerica Intellitech – a subsidiary of Transamerica Corp, Homestore.com, Realigent, REBIG, Virtual Information Systems, & Planning Research Corporation. Her consulting company Dona, LLC’s client list includes start-up regional & national companies as well as industry directing, predominant national corporations; a majority of the various state & local government agencies in the US; & association owned or member owned companies that are non-profit or for-profit. Brenda is a business development, marketing, & operations executive & entrepreneur specializing in the data services, information technology & social media sectors.
