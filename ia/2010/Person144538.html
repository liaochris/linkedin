Michelle Ulrich’s Summary

Michelle Ulrich is an Online Business Manager, Internet Marketing Strategist and International Speaker in her company, Virtual Business Marketing. She works with authors, coaches and speakers to help them build their online platform and get more visibility. Michelle is also the Chief Villager and Owner of The Virtual Nation™, a grassroots organization dedicated to the community and professional development for Virtual Assistants (VAs) and Virtual Professionals (VPs) worldwide.

Michelle worked for Practice Pay Solutions as their Learning Management System National Training Director. They offer online business solutions as well as certification programs for VAs and VPs.

Michelle is a Certified Professional Author’s Assistant, Shopping Cart Strategist and Learning Management System Strategist. She has been an instructor at Shasta [Community] College located in Redding, CA. She frequently consults with educators worldwide assisting and creating Virtual Office Professional (VOP) programs.

Michelle is an assistant organizer and member of the Sacramento Speaker’s Network, member of the Business Education Statewide Advisory Committee (BESAC) and the International Virtual Assistant’s Association (IVAA).

After joining BESAC as a member, then as a Steering Committee member, Michelle assisted in the implementation of a 27 unit VOP curriculum for California community colleges. She served three years on the grant project through completion.

Michelle is considered a leader in the VA community; she coaches and mentors new VAs through her own monthly expert teleseminar series as well as group coaching where she helps VAs gain the confidence they need to build a successful practice.

Michelle has two teenage daughters and a 14-year old cat and resides in Carmichael, CA. She enjoys working from home, reading historical fiction by her favorite author, Diana Gabaldon and entertaining friends or traveling.
