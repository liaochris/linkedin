Shane Richmond’s Summary

Since August 2009 I've managed technology coverage online and in print for Telegraph Media Group.

Between January 2007 and August 2009 I was Communities Editor at the Telegraph. I launched My Telegraph, our innovative reader blogging platform, as well as our journalists' blogs and managed reader comments on other areas of the site.

It's more than 10 years since I started at the Telegraph and in that time I've been a night sub, Deputy Night Editor, a breaking news sub and Online News Editor.

From mid-2000 to mid-2001 I was Editor of an internet radio start-up which became a casualty of the dotcom crash.
