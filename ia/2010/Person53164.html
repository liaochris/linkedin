Dean Marshall’s Summary

Running a web site development company - focusing on the Mambo and Joomla CMSs (Content Management Systems). Personally I'm a generalist with broad experience in all aspects of web site development and management. From server admin, through to software installation, trouble-shooting and of course building and maintaining websites.

Currently expanding the company through local recruitment (Lancaster and Morecambe) in order to gain greater depth of skills.

Particularly interested in government, local authority and large corporate site projects utilising Open Source Content Managment Systems.
