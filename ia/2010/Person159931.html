Patrick Dillon’s Summary

Patrick Dillon has 10+ years of sales & sales management experience. A serial entrepreneur, he has built a successful printing company, and a clothing brand. He currently resides in San Diego as President of Artistic Hub, a company he co-founded. Artistic Hub is software application company that develops simple and effective online contests.

Artistic Hub works with brands like Sports Illustrated, Active.com and The Oakland Raiders to create fan engagement.

Artistic Hub works with small businesses to create lead & data capture to grow their business.

Artistic Hub works with partners like PR firms, Ad & Interactive Agencies, SEO companies and others to help execute on ideas and land more work.

Patrick Dillon’s Specialties:

Patrick’s strengths include branding and identity, sales development, partner development, promotion, event direction, and integrated marketing.
