Michael Wynne-Jones’s Summary

A PhD-qualified Chartered Engineer, risk & capital modeling specialist, capital model developer & project manager, with 12 years’ diverse reinsurance, risk & capital analsysis experience, of which 7 was spent developing and leading development of Guy Carpenter’s Solvency II / economic capital modeling platform, MetaRisk.
Main skills include specifying and developing economic capital models, project management, strategic business analysis, and in building understanding & communication between management, underwriting, actuaries & the regulator. Greatest non-technical challenges & personal successes include projects in France and Japan, with complete immersion in local business practices.

Michael Wynne-Jones’s Specialties:

Model construction in various platforms (MetaRisk, Remetrica, Risk Explorer), capital measurement & capital allocation using VaR, TVaR, co-TVaR, etc., IFRS financial statements, all aspects of collective risk modeling & discrete event simulation, (re)insurance retention analysis & optimization, economic scenario generators including their limitations, reinsurance contract design & modeling in software, credit & lease portfolio modeling.

    During my tenure, we redeveloped a pre-existing proof of concept into an excellent and robust platform compliant with Solvency II, and serviceable as an ERM internal model as well as a reinsurance decision support tool.
