Garry Duncan’s Summary

►President & Chief Selling Officer, Leadership Connections. 303-462-1277. garry@leadershipconnections.com◄

▌Strong presentation skills are no longer closing sales in today’s B2B environment. The Internet has dramatically changed selling, driving the need for training that addresses the unique challenges salespeople and managers face in this new era. Buyers are more knowledgeable and are no longer willing to pay for the best presentation!

▌Creating value requires a broader set of skills, including deeper trust and the ability to assess the complexity of a prospect’s business and solve problems quickly. Salespeople need fresh, effective ways to prospect, get referrals, access decision makers, stimulate interest, create value and close sales. Today, HOW you sell is just as important as WHAT you sell.

▌Standard off-the-shelf sales training programs can be rigid, dry and poorly suited to your industry or target market. Custom programs are expensive and can be risky.

★ WELCOME to Leadership Connections & the Sales Mastery® Training Program!

Our Sales Mastery® program features proven components, principles, techniques and strategies tailored to fit your needs. We emphasize:

►Collaboration vs. Pressure
►Mutual Agreements vs. Closing Techniques
►Problem Solving vs. Closing
►Listening vs. Talking
►Conversations vs. Presentations
►Collaborative vs. Adversarial
►Trusted Advisor vs. Skeptical Buyer
►Prospect’s Timetable vs. Salesperson’s Deadline
►Creating Value vs. Justifying Expense
