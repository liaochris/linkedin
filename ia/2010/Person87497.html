Jacob Levenson’s Summary

Creative, people-oriented professional with mastery, knowledge of, and comprehensive experience in public relations, education outreach, marine operations, data collection and analysis, and project management, as well as relationship development within the fishing industry (National Marine Fisheries Service) and government and community partnerships (NOAA National Marine Sanctuary Program). Demonstrated leadership, initiative, and analytical abilities with excellent problem solving skills and meticulous follow-through. Proficient in various fishing technologies and equipment. Highly skilled in digital photography and high definition cinematography. Expert technical scuba diver with nearly 5,000 dives. Extensive travel and field experience. Dedicated and energetic; enjoys new challenges.
