Vanessa Suggs Wallace’s Summary

B2B Marketing Communications Professional. Vanessa leverages nearly 14 years of marketing communications, public relations, and management experience within a variety of industries to deliver editorial solutions.

As a skilled practitioner, she strategically develops content messaging and governs its launch across communication mediums. Vanessa collaborates with clients to frame clear, consistent, credible, and compelling messaging.

Her ability to tailor internal and external messaging to resonate with intended audiences has resulted in successful delivery for client industries including, but not limited to: financial services, land planning, medical, non-profit, process improvement, project management, and transportation.
