Jared Meyer’s Summary

Jared Meyer is a branding consultant, entrepreneur, speaker, and author that inspires businesses and brands to become unforgettable. In 2000, Jared launched MakStar, a creative marketing company. MakStar's branding consultants and designers have contributed to marketing programs on behalf of national and global brands like Cadbury, AT&T, and Discovery Health Channel. Given their experience, they now work with smaller businesses using marketing philosophies, techniques, and strategies that big brands use. During the past two years, Jared spoke to business owners on behalf of American Express OPEN while serving as an experiential marketing event manager for OgilvyAction, the brand activation arm of the Ogilvy Group. With over ten years of marketing experience, he has contributed towards marketing programs for over 100 popular brands.

Jared Meyer’s Specialties:

Branding and identity, experiential marketing, presentations
