Randy Schrum’s Summary

Randy Schrum currently serves as Director of Strategic Accounts at Centimark Roof Systems. North America's largest commercial roofer. Centimark has led the roofing industry for several reasons,however two things stand out. Centimark's quality workmanship and ability to manage our clients risk.

CentiMark is a full service commercial and industrial roofing contractor offering a full complement of roofing solutions: roof systems, emergency leak service, roof repair, maintenance programs, green roof systems including daylighting, photovoltaic, garden roofing, & reflective coatings, along with multiple site roof inspections. Our re-roofing services are typically on flat roofs utilizing EPDM roofs (rubber roofing), TPO, bitumen roof, spray polyurethane foam and coatings. We proudly serve North America as the largest roofing contractor with over 65 local offices nationwide.
