Keith Trexler’s Summary

Product strategy & design executive with successful achievements as a designer, product manager and profit-oriented business manager demonstrating strong leadership, analytical, organizational and creative abilities.

Basically, I'm a "product guy" - I can take a product from concept through research, design, development and launch. I bridge the gap between development and marketing, defining what we’re building, for whom and why, how we’re going to promote it and why we’re better than the competition. I can evaluate existing products, figure out how to make them better – or conceive of entirely new products, verticals and strategies. I work best in entrepreneurial environments where I’m required to serve in a variety of tactical roles during development while helping to shape corporate strategy.

Seeking consulting opportunities or a product leadership position in business or consumer software, online technology, gaming, eCommerce or social networking fields with an entrepreneurial organization, whether as a start-up, growth-stage or fast-paced unit in an enterprise company.
