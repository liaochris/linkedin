Jill Heisterkamp’s Summary

Copywriter and content editor with over 10 years of experience in various industries including corporate and general aviation, technology, healthcare, engineering, education, printing, and more.

From press releases and advertisements to brochures and websites, Heiste Communications provides high-impact content and strategic marketing input on a per-project or contractual basis.
