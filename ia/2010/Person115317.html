Kevin Weber’s Summary

As economic conditions and cycles have followed us for centuries, the ability to respond and react to the current market conditions enables the most successful outcome through these events. Mr. Kevin Weber has a diverse background spanning 22 years in a variety of positions centered around real estate development, brokerage and contracting industries. This experience has led to develop a multi-faceted approach across a broad range of real estate disciplines with the formation of AZ Professional Realty, AZ Devco and VonKemper Investments.

These organizations have a common goal, to provide a full range of services committed to excellence with the ability to operate independent and congruently with one another capable of adapting with market changes. Over the years, the operating principals have formed many relationships and strategic alliances that benefit all of our customers, clients and associates in meeting their real estate objectives. These relationships include lenders, asset managers, investors, wholesalers, trustees and individuals interested in contemplating their specific real estate transactions as buyers, sellers and investors.

These organizations coupled with a turn-key approach offer multiple service-line solutions to investor, corporate, and individual clients. Specific strategy based solutions are developed across a broad range of locations, properties, investments and business objectives with our clients. In partnership, we maximize the value of their real estate asset and focus on their core strengths, with the confidence our team of experts is attending to every strategic and operational detail.
