Gavin Cherrie’s Summary

Gavin Cherrie has 28 years experience working in Technical, Operations Management and General Management roles in large and medium sized manufacturing firms in New Zealand and overseas.
Gavin has worked in strategic planning, lean manufacturing, product stewardship, green building design, product and process design and marketing.
His experience has a common theme of taking a system wide approach to radically improve company performance.
Gavin holds degrees in Chemical Engineering and Marketing and a post Graduate Diploma in Dairy Science and Technology.
His interests include surfing, fishing, sailing and family. He also volunteers with the Otanewainuku Kiwi Trust, helping with introduced predator control, to ensure a future for the North Island Brown Kiwi.
