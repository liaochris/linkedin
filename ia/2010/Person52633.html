Dawn Tulman’s Summary

ToiBocks provides a line of discreet storage products to protect people's privacy while looking beautiful, elegant and functional. The entire line is designed to "hide in plain sight". Our goal is to continue to develop these types of products so that people can keep the things they want secure and also have convenient access to them.

Our future goals include implementing new products currently in R & D and eventually getting manufacturing set up in the USA.
