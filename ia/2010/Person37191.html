Florinel Chis’s Summary

Started working in eCommerce industry 2002 for eMAG.ro (largest online retailer in Romania) while still in highschool.
I was involved in all business processes as a Software Developer, eBusiness Consultant, Business Analyst and Project Manager (internal ERP system and eCommerce website).
The company had a continuous 300% growth from 2001-2007 (reaching 76,7 mil EUR in 2007).
Relevant work:
• Development of internal ERP + CRM system (web based)
• Development for eMAG.ro website
• Implementation of Socrate ERP modules and integration with internal ERP system and eCommerce website
• Analysis and Consultancy for improving internal processes (Sales, Product Management, Financial Management, Warehouse Management, Trade & Logistics, Business Intelligence)
• Analysis and Consultancy for implementation of a new custom ERP system
• Analysis and Consultancy for implementation of Microsoft Dynamics AX + Microsoft CRM
Left the company at the end of 2006 to start my own company.

During 2004-2006 I was involved also in a project for Telecom Italy (budget management, cost tracking and project management) as a freelancer. Developed a part of myjob.ro website before it was bought by NetBridge Investments.

Founded CANDOO in July 2006 offering eBusiness Solutions and Graphic Design services.

My current role is Business Development Manager, Sr focusing on increasing CANDOO customer’s portfolio and developing new strategic partnerships.

Developed strong business relations at CANDOO with both small and large (multi-national) companies:
• Autodesk
• eMAG.ro
• Romstal Group
• top eCommerce websites in Romania (PC Garage, Marketonline, ShopIT)
• and over 50 other companies

In 2008 CANDOO was awarded "e-Commerce Software Solution of the Year".

Our objective for the next year is to deliver results to our customers improving their business, provide small and medium size businesses with application and products that improve their business and provide results.
