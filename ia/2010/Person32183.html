  • Producer / Manager

    Marlo Productions

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Marlo Productions, a successful publicity, marketing and promotions company in Palm Desert, CA, has positioned itself as an established force within the industry.

    The Desert Cities Woman’s Show led by Diana Marlo, Owner of Marlo Productions and Marketing Director at Desert European Motorcars in Rancho Mirage. Marlo produced her first Woman’s Show in Ontario, Canada in 1986 attracting thousands of women of all ages and backgrounds. She sold her show in 2002 after coming to the Desert, and is pleased to see that the Toronto Women’s Show is going strong and attracting more than 37,000 attendees each year.

    Marlo productions is also gearing up for the second annual Desert Cities Charity Bachelor Auction which raised over $28,000 last year for charity. This year, The Valley’s Top 12 Bachelors are Set to Raise $50,000 Against Domestic Violence.
