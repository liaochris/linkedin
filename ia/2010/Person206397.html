Tom Poser’s Summary

I represent tenants in the San Francisco and Marin office markets, helping them negotiate leases. Common reasons companies hire me include:

1) To renew existing office leases & extract the landlord’s profit
2) To relocate an existing facility or secure new space
3) To acquire space for start-up companies
4) To upsize, downsize, or reconfigure existing space
5) To reduce rent prior to lease expirations via restructuring
6) To lower occupancy costs & increase space efficiencies

Tom Poser’s Specialties:

Tenant Representation. I advise and consult companies on how to make the most effective and efficient decisions regarding their office requirements.
