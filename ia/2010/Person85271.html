IAN (Advertising CEO Group)’s Summary

There are about 27,000 ad agencies in the U.S. 5,000 of them close each year. The rest - the agencies that endure - do so because of their consistent high level of professionalism, sound business practices, creativity and long-range planning. They also share a constant passion to help their clients succeed. These practices and attitudes have helped some of our members not just stay in business, but grow steadily for more than 60 years.

These are the members of IAN - a nationwide organization with links to associations in Europe, South America and Canada. By limiting membership in each major market, our members do not compete with one another which allows us all to freely share our experiences, ideas and resources.
