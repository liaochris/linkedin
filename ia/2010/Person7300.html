Alicia Blain’s Experience

  • CEO

    The Millennial Lab

    (Management Consulting industry)

    August 2009 — Present (1 year )

    The Millennial Lab offers leading-edge educational services and products (including consulting, training, research and media outreach) to organizations and associations.

    As a former technology executive of a Fortune 500 company, Alicia Blain began to hire Gen Y’ers years ago. At first, she found herself frustrated with a group she just couldn’t figure out. She took drastic action and turned her frustration into action by putting Millennials under the microscope and closely observing them in the workplace.

    She shares innovative, proven methods with leaders on how they can challenge their conventional management practices, recognize and capitalize on the value of the Millennials and achieve unprecedented results in record time. In short, she shows her clients how to create their own Millennial Labs and harness the talents of this young workforce.
