Johnny Hanna’s Summary

Prior to becoming a Wyoming real estate broker, I spent over 35 years in the accounting and finance area in the private business sector. I graduated from Arkansas State University with a Bachelor of Science degree, with a major in accounting and a minor in military science. I was a certificated public accountant for many years. During my years of business experience, I was involved in the acquisition and disposition of many types of businesses including cattle ranching, real estate, mining, manufacturing, distribution, and other smaller retail and customer service businesses. I was raised in a small town in Arkansas. After living in eight different states, I now live in Wapiti, Wyoming, with my wife Dorenda. My background and my love for this area give me a unique advantage in the real estate market.

Johnny Hanna’s Specialties:

Real estate, relocation policies, financial reporting and analysis, accounting, accounting systems designs, due diligent analysis, crisis management, employee benefits, computer information reporting systems, bankruptcy consulting.
