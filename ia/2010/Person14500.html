Ann Klein’s Summary

Product marketing executive with expertise in strategic and product planning.

Experience includes SVP of product management at Risk Management Solutions (an enterprise risk management company) and 6+ years at Siebel in product design and marketing (healthcare and insurance verticals). While at Siebel, was awarded the CEO's circle award each year and received a patent for the design of the complex plan design product for Siebel Insurance/Healthcare. Prior to Siebel, was a Senior Manager at Accenture in the Healthcare Strategy Group (6 years at Accenture).

Attended the Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth (MBA class of 1993). Developed a customer satisfaction and customer information management strategy for Astra/Merck during a business school internship. Prior to business school, was an analyst in the Business Strategy Group of Abt Associates (Cambridge, MA).

Received a B.A., magna cum laude, from Princeton in 1986. Focused on Slavic Languages and Literature and the Russian Studies program.

Ann Klein’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, product management, market and competitor analysis, product design, vertical/industry market evaluation and planning. Industry expertise in insurance and healthcare.
