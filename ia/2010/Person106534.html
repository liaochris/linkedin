Joy Kennelly’s Summary

I have been producing events since high school and love bringing together groups of like-minded people. Whether it be for writing, goal setting, networking, technology, entertainment, non-profits, education, or simply for fun, I love connecting people and helping them create quality relationships.

I also love to write and have been an avid blogger for over six years. As a result, my blogs consistently appear in the top ten searches of Google because of the wide variety of events I participate in, the clients I have represented, and my diverse interests.

My favorite part of writing is bringing people's stories to life. That can be as simple as writing a bio, or covering an event with a personal slant to highlight the wonderful people I meet, and/or a charity I want to support, or simply writing about someone's business I'm visiting or working with.

When I'm done and show it to them, it's as if they begin to see themselves for the first time as others have seen them all along. Their sense of pride and self-esteem grows right before my eyes and continues to develop long after I've gone.

That's my favorite part! The editing to get to the final product? Not as much, but I'll gladly endure a 100 changes to have a client satisfied and excited about their new future potential.

Makes me happy every time.

I've covered LA Fashion Week since 2006 and love fashion. I have an AA degree in Fashion Merchandising with credits from FIDM and years of experience working in retail. I understand quality of workmanship and style as a result.

Lastly, I was trained by Delta Airlines and Alaska Airlines in customer service and sales techniques which I have never forgotten. To me, PR & Marketing is selling. Either you believe in what you're selling, or you don't. If I believe in something I can sell it which is why my career has been so interesting.

I've enjoyed the variety. I am excited to bring my skills, connections and talents to a company who will respect it.
