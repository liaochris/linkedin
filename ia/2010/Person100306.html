Jodi Hersh’s Summary

Jodi’s passion for art dictates her attitude toward life — her devotion to her work, her thriving and award-winning studio. Jodi has over 20 years of experience as a designer and earned her BFA in graphic design and photography from the University of Michigan School of Art & Design.

After graduating, Jodi returned to her hometown of Miami where she worked as part of team designing signage and graphics for Universal Studios and Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines. Jodi broadened her range of experience by art directing for a printing company where she gained valuable working knowledge of developing technologies and processes.

Since starting Orange Star in 1993, Jodi has worked on and led projects for clients as large as ALLTEL, Centers for Disease Control (CDC), The American Cancer Society, and Styx... and as varied as environmental graphics, logos & corp. identities, concert/tour merchandise, event graphics, publications, and interactive new media to complex database driven websites.

Jodi believes form follows function and good design is about more than great aesthetics. Her approach has always been to partner with her clients and other creatives to realize the best possible solutions.
