Peter Denbigh’s Summary

I've started, run, and sold three IT companies over the past 8 years. Because of this, I've experienced many aspects of business, from contract negotiation, employee issues, sales, marketing, finance, and more. Consequently, I am both a good leader and a good employee or team member.

Currently I am furthering my education by completing a MBA - Masters of Business Administration - at James Madison University.

I volunteer at the JMU Alternative Fuel Vehicle Lab. My time here has been stimulating and exciting, and I really hope to further my involvement with alternative energy, transportation, and environmental sustainability. It is important to me that I am making a positive difference for society.

As a student at JMU's College of Integrated Science and Technology, I took advantage of as many opportunities as I could possibly find. Examples include Photovoltaic research, Orientation Program Assistant, constructing a 2 engine hovercraft, my Senior Thesis electric vehicle, various TA positions, trips, clubs, and more.

My goal is to become part of a growing and exciting organization where I can apply my skills and talents to be a catalyst for change our society's energy and/or technology infrastructure and philosophy.
