Jenna Langer’s Summary

I went through college learning about communication, photography, digital media, and human-computer interaction. Then I found this thing called social media, where I could play with all of these things while communicating with real people!

With a B.A. digital media from Sacramento State, I concentrated on how technology changes the way we communicate. My obsession with the Internet began at eight years old when I wrote a short story titled, "Trapped in the World Wide Web." Since then I have gained experience in a variety of web-related fields including social media, human-computer interaction, and web design and development. I am an event junkie, and helped organize several conferences including my own BarCamp. I love startup life and am working on every aspect of the Livefyre that isn’t code.
