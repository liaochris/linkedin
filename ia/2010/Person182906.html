Sarah Toppins’s Summary

I've been a real estate agent for more than 23 years, all with Long & Foster, the largest independent real estate company in the U.S. and the number one company in the mid-Atlantic. I'm an associate broker with the number one office in the company, the Bethesda Gateway office, which serves the Washington, DC Metropolitan area. I sell in Washington, DC and in the close-in Maryland suburbs, especially Montgomery County and northern Prince Georges County. I'm part of the training team for our office.

I've been very active in real estate leadership, having been on the Greater Capital Area Association of Realtors Board of Directors, an officer with Long & Foster's Gold Team, a Board member and committee chair of the MD-DC chapter of CRS, and on numerous state and local real estate committees.

Having been a college journalism professor (University of Illinois & American University), I'm a big believer in continuing education and have numerous real estate designations - ABR, CDPE, CIPS, CRS, e-PRO, GRI, GREEN, RECS, SRES, TRC, One America, historic property designation & more. (If you want to know what the initials stand for, send me an email & I'll give you a more detailed explanation).

I stay on top of changes in the industry (I was a journalist for my 1st career) and provide my clients with a lot of information, tailoring my services to their needs and time frames. I'm known as a good real estate problem solver who believes in "win win" outcomes.
