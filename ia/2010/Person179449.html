Ryan Diem’s Summary

Currently heading into 10th season with Indianapolis Colts.

Balancing offseason time with 2 privately held companies - Modern Muscle, Inc and CloudOne.

Modern Muscle specializes in High Performance Vehicles, Computer Tuning, and Custom Fabrication, and Restorations. Modern Muscle owned and operated by two Mechanical Engineers with a staff of several ASE certified mechanics. Partner, Justin Meyers, our tuning guru, can calibrate any combination of performance parts on our DynoJet Chassis Dyno. Parts are available from every manufacturer, and customer service and satisfaction is our number one goal. If you can't find the right part, we will make one - designed and manufactured in house in our 10,000 sqft facility. All engines are hand built, balanced and blue printed, in our engine building "clean room".

Our Goal is to provide the best customer experience by being professional and timely in our pursuit to give the customer the highest quality performance build.

CloudOne is based in suburban Chicago, IL. CloudOne makes IBM Rational technology easy-to-deploy and affordable for everyone - particularly for small and medium-sized organizations. Their unique Software-as-a-Service license model, coupled with cloud-based delivery of software from their own data center speeds up the deployment of Rational solutions with no up-front capital investments for hardware or software, allowing customers to “pay by the drink” on a monthly subscription model. This saves money on infrastructure, staffing and configuration costs, while facilitating the move to cutting-edge IBM Rational technology and distributed, Agile development.
