Ajdin Kolonic’s Summary

My Life-Drive
What focuses my mind, gets my heart racing, and brings a smile to my face are CHALLENGES! Both in my professional career as in personal life, I have always strived to search out the limit of my abilities, break boundaries and show myself and the world that with a right attitude and a pure intention anything can be achieved.

My Passions
I feel very strongly about physical and mental health and the role nutrition plays in it. SPORTS! Aside from regular fitness workouts, I play tennis, football, and basketball; and have started paragliding and kite-surfing. BOOKS on physics, philosophy, and personal development are another passion. I am also a big MOVIE buff. Finally, I believe that our greatest joy is always with other people and try to therefore create true, honest, and deep RELATIONSHIPS.

My GOALS
About a year ago I have made a switch to offering my services as an independent contractor. Recently I have also focused on becoming a Motivational Trainer. My vision:
1. Offer professional analysis and consulting services independently.
2. Create a motivational training and coaching business teaching skills for success, in sports and in life.
3. Initiate, and participate in, several entrepreneurial ideas.
4. Shift my life more towards Slovenia; create a business there, based around health-consciousness and skills for success in life, with a not-for-profit section teaching these skills to children and young adults.
5. Study philosophy and physics, and keep challenging myself in form of regular and extreme sports.

HOW CAN I HELP YOU?
+ My professional experience has been in dynamic change-environments. From quick and flexible MS Office reporting solutions to complex business analysis projects in large organizations, I have the ability to work under time pressure, put things into action and create results fast.
+ And if you need a professional website or want to become a mentally-stronger tennis player, I can also help you
