Jim Thomas’s Summary

As a partner at Minor & Brown, I am a business lawyer, trusted advisor and designated dream catcher for some of Colorado's best privately owned companies. I am grateful to my clients for the opportunity to like what I do and the folks I do it for.

I also serve my community in a variety of ways, including promoting the concept of community stewardship to all who will listen. A community is a co-op. You put something in, you get something out. You encourage others to put something in, you get a lot out.
