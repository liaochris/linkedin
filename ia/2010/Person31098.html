James Rowe’s Summary

I write and direct movies, and I've been skilled and lucky enough to see my ideas realized on screens big and small.

Those screens are now expanding—and shrinking—along with their changing markets. But whether the final product is destined for 70-foot 3D IMAX or the 4.5-inch iPhone, inventive, compelling storytelling will always be at the core of any film or video experience. This belief drives my approach in every arena, as a writer, director and educator.

Through my work with young, talented students from around the world, I'm in a position to see how the audience landscape is changing, up close and in the moment.
