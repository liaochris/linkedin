Steve Evans’s Summary

Internet/E-Commerce/New Media/Marketing specialist with over 14 years experience. I have worked with many industries, online communities, retail, portals, financial services and the online travel industry.

My career began at a start-up providing web based services to the London insurance market. Launched and managed two highly successful insurance industry portals and had a hand in developing many insurers’ websites.

I then became senior developer for one of the world’s largest financial services organisations, continuing to run the portals which they then owned and also leading development of websites and intranet for the company.

Working in the travel industry I managed all technical aspects of the E-business department and provided strategy and direction to all other online activities for Virgin. I was also involved in all aspects of online marketing (PPC, SEO, affiliates etc) and provide advice on all campaigns which touch the online world. At Lastminute.com I ran all UI and frontend development teams and activities.

I have a broad spectrum of skills, my aim is to focus on providing strategic insight to organisations to enable them to reach their potential online.

I'm also quite keen on managing web build and online marketing projects from start to finish :)
