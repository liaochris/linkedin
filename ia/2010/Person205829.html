Tom Barker’s Summary

As a solutions architect I have created numerous enterprise SOA systems from the ground up; from automated asset generation and distribution systems to integrated middle tiers that leverage web services and shared libraries to allow the expansion of product offerings from desktop to web to mobile products.

As a developer and technical lead I have worked with and led both small and large teams of developers, while managing internal and external resources, to the successful completion of numerous web and enterprise projects.

As an adjunct professor I teach both undergrad and graduate classes on web development technologies.

As an individual I thrive on challenges and constantly look to grow my skillset.
