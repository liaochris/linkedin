Julie Magnuson’s Summary

Julie Magnuson is the founding member of the San Diego HR Roundtable (SDHR), and has held the position of SDHR President since the group was founded. SDHR, formerly known as San Diego Recruiters Roundtable (SDRR) was founded in 1998 as a vehicle for a small group of HR Professionals to share information on HR and Recruiting related topics. Today, we have grown to be one of San Diego’s largest and best known HR Professional Networking Communities. Our membership base is targeted to a focused group that includes Human Resources Professionals and Consultants. Our members range in experience from new-to-the-industry to seasoned executives. Learn more about us at http://sandiego.hrroundtables.com

Julie is President of HumanIT Resources, a consulting firm specializing in providing strategic and day-to-day Human Resources and IT Technology support to start ups, small businesses and large HR and IT Departments. Julie Magnuson began her career in Human Resources in 1995, and started her first HR Consulting practice in 2001. Julie has provided HR Consulting Services to over 80 companies in a wide variety of industries, with a focus in High Tech and Biotech. HumanIT Resources provides a unique approach to HR and IT Consulting, offering clients access to a fully staffed HR Department or IT Department in a more affordable and efficient way than most organizations can hire independently. We allow our clients to have flexible resources and access to our expert staff while leveraging our efficiencies for a better ROI.

Julie Magnuson’s Specialties:

Senior HR Management, start ups, high tech, biotech, pharmaceutical, medical device, recruiting & selection, employee retention, interviewing candidates, policy and procedure review and development, compensation and benefits, employee relations
