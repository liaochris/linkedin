Moss Kardener’s Summary

Insightful executive whose strategic marketing orientation, aesthetic sense and creative vision add unique value to projects in which brand, design and marketing determinations are critical in creating a roadmap for future success.

• Passionate, creative professional experienced at defining, developing and leading diverse corporate and consumer brands and products.

• Adept at evaluating brand equity and developing implementation strategies that increase revenue and gain competitive advantage within limited budgets.

• Skilled at identifying consumer trends and market opportunities, setting actionable strategy and managing implementation to yield meaningful business results.

• Experienced at launching brands, championing product development and licensing initiatives, and managing product lines through all lifecycle stages.

• Perspectives and insights that offer distinct advantage to each new project.

Over 300 products launched to date in an array of categories—from Food and Beverage, Health and Beauty Aids (HBA) and Housewares to Toys and Juvenile Products, Computer Software and Apparel. Engaged on projects for companies ranging from Armor All Products to Discovery Channel, and brands from Colorforms® and Quark Express®, to Hilton Hotels. Directed brand strategy at the world’s first Apple® Macintosh-integrated design firm.

Moss Kardener’s Specialties:

Corporate Identity Development; Naming; Consumer Products Innovation (including: Market Positioning, Packaging, New Product Launch and New Category Introduction); Product Positioning; Marketing Communications and Creative Management; Intellectual Property Acquisition and Licensing; Manufacturing and Merchandising.
