Ronda Lane’s Summary

I have over 20 years of experience in accounting and management and have worked in almost every industry. I hope to put that experience to use helping small business owners start and grow their businesses.

In the Black Accounting is a bookkeeping and accounting firm that partners with other small businesses to implement, consult, and provide them with the tools that they need to make smart business decisions.

Our business is built on integrity, commitment to our clients, and the quality of our work. Our clients' success is our number one priority and we work hard to give them the tools that they need to run their business.

My clients are businesses with less than 50 employees and under $10 million per year in revenue in any industry. I prefer an ongoing business relationship with my clients but am available for individual projects such as setup and training. I am always available to answer questions and am a pro at internet research so if I don't know the answer I will find someone who does!
