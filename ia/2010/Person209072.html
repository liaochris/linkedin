Ty Bohannon’s Summary

Ty has over 15 years experience in technical and management aspects of operating a business. During the last few years he has focused on launching or improving businesses on the Web. His portfolio includes successful projects for organizations such as the U.S. Navy, NTT/Verio, Cisco,Microsoft, and Visa.

Prior to entering the business world, Mr. Bohannon served the U. S. Navy close to a decade where his work was focused on systems integration and cryptology. His collateral duties included emergency medical services, physical security management, and law enforcement.

His approach is to integrate business strategy, process design, technology architecture, and the selection of technology products, services, and vendors.

Ty Bohannon’s Specialties:

Specialize in building teams of core competency experts under a single dynamic project umbrella. These teams develop achievable business and technology goals. Then, assist with the execution of those goals. Clients range from small businesses in Canada, start-ups in Denver, and large global enterprises like NTT/Verio & Cisco.
Provide Product Management and Architectural Strategy for eBusiness and Portal ASP.NET applications. Develop and deliver nationwide IT support services.
