Steven Ganz’s Summary

My passion is in applying the rigor of sound theory to obtain new perspectives and insights into the solution of relevant problems. I always try to look beyond the immediate problem at hand to see how recognition of a common pattern might lead to a potential for adding greater value than previously contemplated.

I apply insights from research to hard problems and provide solutions that make a difference. I have found that my dual backgrounds in research and industry effectively reinforce each other. Experience in building large systems gives me an appreciation for the sorts of abstraction that are most valuable in generating economies of scale. And an understanding of issues of language design enables me to design products that are intuitive to use, robust in their application, and efficient in their implementation.
