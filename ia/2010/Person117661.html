Detailed understanding of board/paper based and flexible film laminate packaging solutions
Experience of costing projects/packaging solutions and delivering against budget

Complex multiple project management within the FMCG industry
Developed packaging solutions that are optomised in material, supplier, machine application and supply terms

Flexibility to travel domestically and internationally
