Brian R. Smith’s Summary

Analog electronics (circuit board and system level) engineer.

Have been described as a tenacious problem solver and a conscientious team member.

Enjoy bringing sick products to viable status by isolating and resolving tough technical problems.

Experienced with product definition, design and development, release to manufacturing, writing test procedures, and managing a test department.

Happy and comfortable with components as well as humans.

Some Sucesses:

* Reduced customer returns by 40% with improved production and test procedures.

* Reduced manufacturing cost of a disk R/W head flying height tester by 67% while also redesigning for linear performance.

* Saved company from expensive system redesign by detecting theoretical flaw in paper tracking system for electrostatic plotter, and providing an effective solution.

* Redirected costly custom encoder project into inexpensive off-the-shelf components.

* Brought unusable friction tester to marketable status through corrective redesign after proving theoretical problems in predecessor design.

* Conceived/developed multi-function preamp for MOS image array with improved specifications and 30% less circuitry vs. the inherited design.

* Saved employer from a lawsuit, having developed a self-initiated system test procedure.

Brian R. Smith’s Specialties:

Hands-on bench experience with op amps, sensors, analog filters, oscillators, PLLs, power amplifiers, photo-diodes, servo control of motors & lasers, digital logic, pcb layout, electronic and electro-mechanical prototype construction and debug, oscilloscopes, generators, OrCAD Capture, LTspice, AutoCAD. Moderate fluency in conversational Spanish.
