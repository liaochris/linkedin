I am a Visual Artist, Graphic Designer and Prepress Specialist experienced in Art Direction, Graphic Design, Prepress (Flexo & Digital), and Department & Small Business Management.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS
• Established myself as department point person between managers.
• Established and maintained ArtPro final prep files.
• Established and maintained Nexus workflows.
• Color management for consistent cross production color management throughout product line.
• Implemented perfect highlights screenprint technology for seamless gradients.
• Trained on, implemented and maintained Esko Backstage FlexRip/PackEdge final prep.
• Trained on, implemented and maintained Deskpack plugins for expanded Adobe Illustrator performance.
• Trained on Flexo Tools and Equinox Ink Wizard for enhanced Photoshop color corrections including expanded gamut color separation support.
