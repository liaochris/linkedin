John Delaney’s Summary

I have quite a bit of experience in full-cycle recruiting within a variety of approaches (internal, external, RPO, etc.). And I have a long track record of filling positions directly, driving placements as a player coach in small-medium sized recruiting teams, and running large recruiting organizations (as an executive).

For direct placements, my strength has always been technical recruiting (and I don't use "technical" loosely either).

John Delaney’s Specialties:

High Tech recruiting, sales, marketing, business development, contract negotiations, closing deals, building/managing/developing teams.
