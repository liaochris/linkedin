Jorg Janke’s Summary

I combine business background (accounting degree, MBA) with technology experience (Java, Force.com, Flex, Oracle/DB2) for customer success.

Model Driven Applications were my passion since I experienced Smalltalk - I created the first active dictionary driven business application - Compiere, the premier Open Source ERP system.

Recently, I concentrate on delivering Professional Services Automation and Service Resource Planning functionality on Salesforce.
