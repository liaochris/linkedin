William Sobel’s Summary

I have a diverse career of over 20 years with 13 years focused on building distributed financial systems and SaaS web applications. After leaving finance and doing a brief stint teaching at UCB, I authored the MTConnect standard to facilitate machine tool connectivity. Recently, I founded a company to bring the technology and architecture used in real-time financial systems to manufacturing and discover new ways of increasing profitability and quality using the latest in cloud computing and machine learning.

William Sobel’s Specialties:

* Manufacturing Data and Infrastructure.
* Financial Applications.
* Distributed processing and messaging.
* Fault tolerant architecture for 7x24 availability.
* Secure 3-tier web architecture.
* XML Document Engineering.
* Ruby on Rails based web applications.
* Relational database design and modeling.
* Real-time database caching with push synchronization.
* Domain specific language design and implementation.
* Languages: Ruby, C++, Java, C#, XSLT, Perl, SQL, and many more
