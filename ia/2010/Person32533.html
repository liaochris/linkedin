Carlos J. Arboleda’s Summary

SJA prides itself in discretely representing its candidates & clients, & confidentiality is always respected. Offering permanent - contract - interim opportunities for well qualified, Sr. Banking & Financial Services professionals.

SJA’s Banking & Financial Services Group specializes in Direct-sourcing and confidential placement of mid to senior executive level Management / Leadership in Corporate Banking, Commercial Lending, Private Banking, Business Banking, Credit Leadership & Analysis, Risk, Retail Banking Leadership, Bank Operations, Investment & Wealth Management professionals, International Banking, Insurance, Brokerage, Securities, Mortgage & related positions within the Banking & Financial Services Industry.

The Banking & Financial Services Group at SJA is a premier search consultancy serving the banking & financial communities. Unlike traditional search firms, SJA utilizes a highly interpersonal approach to finding candidates with the particular skills our clients require. We specialize in recruiting & placing professional, mid to executive-level positions, throughout the Banking & Financial Services Industry in Permanent, Contract / Interim, & Project / Consulting opportunities. SJA represents it's clients on Retainer & a Contingency basis.

For JOB SEEKERS at the professional, managerial & executive level, we offer the advantages of absolute confidentiality, deep experience in the fields we place, & insider access to high-level positions and top execs at leading companies.

For EMPLOYERS, we streamline the difficult task of finding excellent candidates for managerial & executive positions. Top performers are often employed, & the best candidate for your position may not even be looking for a new opportunity... but we’ll open the door.

Recognizing the complexity and importance of finding candidates with specific skills and abilities, SJA employs a hands-on recruiting approach to guarantee that they deliver the best candidates available.
