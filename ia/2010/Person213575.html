Walter DeVenne’s Summary

As Walter attended Rollins College in Winter Park, FL where he earned a BA in Political Science and an Associates degree in Business Management, he realized after years of northeast living that Florida provided the culture for him. Immediately after graduation Walter was recruited and joined Westgate Resorts/CFI. Here he excelled rapidly and became a top Sales Manager throughout the company. Walter eventually was given the opportunity to implement a new and aggressive sales approach via call center marketing and incentive-based touring mechanisms. With the business climate changing and the internet gaining consumer traction, Walter was lead back to Boston to join an early-stage technology venture, Web Messaging Technologies, where he shined as a Business Development Manager with a focus on the B2C market.

Following his successful tenures at Westgate/CFI and Web Messaging, Walter changed industries and entered into the Title Insurance industry where he experienced lots of success in sales and marketing. He worked for many years as the top account executive and Marketing Manager for Eagle Title & Abstract in St Petersburg, FL. He later moved on to become the VP of Sales and Marketing and co-owner of Title Marketplace, LLC in Clearwater, FL. Walter then joined forces with the largest title underwriter in the United States with First American Title in Largo, FL as their national account manager.

His final stop is with an exciting new title company called Clear Title America, LLC in Tampa, FL where he presently the Director of Marketing. CTA is working on opening up its third office in a little over a year and has emerged as Florida’s fastest growing independent title company.


Our goal is to become the industry leader in title insurance nationally.

"Adversity breeds opportunity."
