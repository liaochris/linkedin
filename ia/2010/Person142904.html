Michael Keplinger’s Summary

Michael has 10 years of professional work history including finance, technology and entrepreneurial experience. He was recently working with Lighthouse Venture Capital Group, assisting in due diligence, business case modeling, and investment recommendation for current and prospective portfolio companies in the Web 2.0 and Software as a Service (SaaS) industry. He is a co-founder of consumer health products business in Phoenix, AZ where he currently sits on the Board of Directors, and has previously served in various roles such as COO and CFO to grow the company to over $3M in annual revenue within three years. While there he raised needed expansionary capital by recruiting a new managing partner who would become the new COO and invest in the company. Michael has assisted the CONNECT (www.connect.org) springboard program with financial modeling and business development advice for San Diego based start-up companies. He also previously served as a Senior Systems Engineer for Raytheon, a $24 billion government contractor, where he managed a team of engineers and led the integration of a custom command and control software system for satellites on a $40M contract.
