PROFILE

Joe Sloboda is the Vice President of Project Development for Exclusive Visas and has more than 14 years experience in handling complex strategies for a foreign national clientele with regard to personal finance, commercial & residential real estate financing as well as property development.

Having been featured on CNN, CNBC and quoted in local media, Joe Sloboda has also personally guided hundreds of affluent foreign national investors through the intricate process of purchasing real estate in the United States and provided advice on personal finance. Joe has consulted to the Burgess Law Firm since January of 2007 and works conjointly with Fred Burgess on all matters pertaining to EB-5. Joe is also recognized as a leader in the fields and frequently lectures on personal financial strategies, mortgage financing and the EB-5 program to audiences throughout the United States and around the world.

Joe Sloboda is an active member of several organizations in South Florida which include the National Association of Mortgage Brokers, Florida Association of Mortgage Brokers, Rotary International and the Broward Professional Alliance.
