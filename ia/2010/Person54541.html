Denis Gianoutsos’s Summary

As the Global Lead Executive Development and Key Talent Delivery, Denis is responsible for the management and delivery of HP’s Executive Development and Key Talent offerings and solutions across the globe.

Denis comes to the Executive Leadership Development team through the EDS acquisition. Denis is a highly valued professional coach and business leader having spent 20 years in the IT services industry. Prior to joining the HP Centre of Leadership Excellence team he was the EDS Global Coaching Offering Lead and Asia Pacific Regional Lead Coach.

He is a respected coach specializing in Transformational Leadership Coaching, Executive Coaching and Team Coaching. A certified and experienced trainer and facilitator in delivering workshops in coaching skills for leaders, leadership development, strategic vision, building effective teams and performance management. He also works with leaders transitioning into new positions to ensure a successful and effective uptake of their role.

Denis has worked 15 years in both the Global Sales and Solutions Delivery organizations in leadership roles with various organizations. At the same time, he has coached executives, senior leaders and employees for the past ten years in Asia Pacific, North America and in Europe. He has also spoken at seminars in New Zealand and Europe on leadership, goal setting and communication. Denis demonstrates a strong passion for assisting leaders to reach their full potential and is committed to making a difference.

Denis is the past President of the International Coach Federation – Wellington NZ Chapter. Denis has also featured in a book called Secrets of Inspiring Leaders Exposed where he has written a chapter about his perspectives on leadership and coaching. Denis and his fiancee Mary are competitive and award winning Ballroom and Latin dancers.
