Chuck Beckmire’s Summary

Chuck has more than 30 years of experience in the financial services and equipment management industries, with an emphasis on Japan/Asia markets and strategic OEM alliances. Prior to joining EQUVO, Chuck was with Babcock & Brown where he was a co-founder of its Electronics Management subsidiary and the Director of Global Financial Services Sales. Prior to B&B he served as Senior Vice President, Comdisco Electronics and Chairman & Managing Director for Comdisco Equipment Solutions KK, Japan. Throughout his career, he has developed a strong network of electronics, semiconductor and financial services industry relationships throughout Asia, the US and in Europe. Chuck holds a B.S. in Accounting from the University of Colorado and an M.B.A. from Pepperdine University.
