Rajeev Jain’s Summary

Rajeev is a Published Author, Trainer, and Expert Technical Writer with expertise in API / SDK Documentation. He has worked with and managed globally distributed teams including China, Singapore, US, UK, Australia, Mexico, Philippines, Canada, Taiwan, and our own India.
He started his career with Hindustan Times (as Marketing Executive) in 1992 and later moved to programming. He worked with World Health Organization and DBS Bank (Singapore) as System Analyst. He has worked as System Analyst, Project Manager, Technical Writer, Instructional Designer, and MIS consultant.
His first book, titled “Y2K and India” was published in the year 1999 for which he even received complement from “Narayan Murthy” and from US Embassy.
He has conducted workshops / seminars at various prestigious institutes, colleges, schools, and corporate houses like “Jain College, Bangalore”, “DAV School, Delhi”, Cathedral School, Bangalore”, “S G T B Khalsa College, Delhi University”, and many more private and government schools and colleges.
