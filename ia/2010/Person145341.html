Mike Flanagan’s Summary

20-plus year veteran entrepreneur in digital media, with over 16 years in entertainment post production. Skilled in business development, marketing, and legal aspects of small business formation and management. Previously a practicing attorney in California, with knowledge of corporate securities, labor law, general litigation.

At various times I have been involved with the Los Angeles Venture Association (including as a Board Member), Sales & Marketing Association, American Society for Training & Development, Cal Tech/MIT Enterprise Forum.

I also help many people find jobs in the entertainment industry. Since 1995 I have lectured and written on techniques and data for successful job searching. My book "Hollywood Jobs" is profiled at www.hollywoodjobs.tv.
