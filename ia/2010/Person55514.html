Devorah Firestone’s Summary

Tests, evaluates and offers solutions for clients concerned about their software and website's abilities to allow the disabled user population to reach them. In this day and age where we want computers to be the great equalizer, many companies fail to provide access to a huge number of users. Many of those companies are getting sued or are in danger of getting sued. In addition, a strong, smart, powerful and worthy sector if the population is being ignored due to fear and ignorance of the unknown. Some of the smartest and most dedicated workers I have ever met have had disabilities, and once they are without a job may take 2 or 3 years to get employed even with the best of skills and are often underemployed or underpaid once they get there. I am dedicated to helping them participate and to help companies make use of this under tapped group of outstanding people.
