Sonja Falvo’s Summary

Professional Speaker, Entrepreneur, Author, International Body Transformation Specialist.

Sonja's mission when she started Real Body Enterprises was to develop a program that would provide a healthy alternative to dieting for real everyday people who wanted to lose weight or simply wanted to make healthier lifestyle choices.

From her 7 years in the health and fitness industry, Sonja had met thousands of people who felt 'trapped' in what she calls 'dieting hell'; constantly feeling starved or deprived, feeling guilty when they ate their favourite foods, feeling like they had no will-power when they 'fell off the wagon', and all the other negative emotions and feelings that are usually associated with dieting.

Feeling frusrated that there was no real alternative program available to people that covered the three main areas she felt were most important for permanent weight-loss: A Positive Mindset; Quick & Easy Meal Plans and Recipes, and; A Simple Exercise Program, Sonja set about researching and developing her own program.

The Real Body program is based on Sonja's 7 years of experience and research including utilising the latest scientific findings to provide people with the quickest methods for burning fat and losing weight via eating the right foods at the right times and in the right proportions, as well as an exercise program that is quick and effective and can be done anywhere, even at home.
