Brandt Page’s Summary

Brandt Page is the founder and CEO of Launch Sales and Marketing, an outsourced B2B sales company that fills their client's sales pipeline by providing lead generation, qualification, and appointment setting services.

Brandt is the Emerging Executive of the Year 2009 by the Utah Technology Council. American Express selected him to advise their national OPEN Forum Pulse on Twitter and his Twitter @BrandtPage and @LaunchSales were selected as a Top 40 business tweets to follow. Brandt was nominated for the "INC 30 Under 30" award in 2010, and was elected into the 2010 vSpring Capital Top 100 Venture Entrepreneurs. Launch was recently featured in the Utah CEO magazine as the 6th fastest growing company in Utah. He was also selected as a Junto Partner in 2008.

Along with speaking at several events, Brandt was a speaker at the Utah Governor’s Economic Summit. He is a mentor for the Community Foundation of Utah and works with at-risk youth in Salt Lake City. He been on numerous panels and has judged various business competitions.

We are a B2B Sales Accelerator for technology companies. Launch is located in Salt Lake City, UT.

What we do for our clients:

1. Gathering Targeted Lead Lists
2. Qualifying Leads through Cold Calls
3. Setting Highly Qualified Appointments

Now you can let your sales team focus on closing more qualified prospects. We call the decision makers that you want to talk to and get them interest in your product or service, then schedule an appointment for you.

www.launchsalesandmarketing.com

Contact us at Sales@launchsalesandmarketing.com or call 877-466-0111.
