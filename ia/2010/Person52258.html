David Wakeman’s Summary

I am a former Navy Sonar Technician who entered into the corporate business world as a Field Engineer for the second largest computer manufacturer at that time. I succeeded in grabbing the attention of executives within the organization and quickly rose thru the ranks.

I moved into Data Communications at a time when Data Communications was at its infancy. Again, doing whatever it took to grow within the company and improve my worth. I have been a Field Engineer, a Technical Analyst, Trainer, Network Designer and Business Development Manager.
I have worked with companies in all industries, community development and city governments. I have had the pleasure of working with CEO’s, CFO’s, Sales Executives and every other level of employee throughout my career.

My wife and I started a lucrative Real Estate business together and worked to grow our presence in our community until a recently. I realized that the changes in the economy and the housing market would hamper our ability to control our income and growth. I felt the need to seek out a new path for our financial future and entered into network marketing. Utilizing our business skills, we have developed a business that has not only given us financial security, it has given us the ability to control our time.

I found that helping others accomplish their goals was inspirational and that it was the most rewarding part of my career development. I currently own a real estate business with my wife, as well as, a network marketing business.

It is my mission in life to build relationships and assist others to meet and exceed their goals. For more information on building your business and improving your marketing skills, visit: my website http://7FigureMoneyMaker.com and my blog http://davidwakeman.com
