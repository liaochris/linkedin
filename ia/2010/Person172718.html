Richard Conti’s Summary

I have had a 25+ year career in consumer products with P&G, Clorox. Wilton Brands, and now SC Johnson. My senior roles have included Group VP, COO, and CEO positions with full P&L responsibility for multi-billion dollar businesses. This “big company” experience has been extensively augmented by both entrepreneurial and private equity leadership roles. Highlights of my experience and skill set include:

• Full P&L responsibility for multiple large (over $500 million), multifunctional businesses. I deal well with complexity.
• Delivery of stretch revenue, profit, balance sheet targets in businesses with a variety of strategic roles. I thrive on diversity and accountability.
• Track record of success in building and reorganizing as well as restructuring organizations. I understand the transformational potential of the organization.
• Entrepreneurial experience creating new business models in a complex, regulated environment. I have vision but don’t mind getting my hands dirty.
• Strategic M&A leadership in both the private equity and corporate context. I’m a broad-based partner.

Richard Conti’s Specialties:

Operating leadership of complex businesses...Building focused, high-performance teams...Strategic assessment and re-direction...Revitalizing neglected businesses... Developing new revenue streams...M&A activities from evaluation to operating integration.
