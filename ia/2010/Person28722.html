Brian Mathers’s Summary

Brian Mathers Director of ICT ADVISOR.COM is an Internet Marketing Consultant, Search Engine Optimization Specialist, and a Technology Means Business accredited Business Advisor.

His company, provides ebusiness solutions using his virtual team of 6 eBusiness Advisors that support both corporate and small medium businesses with up to 250 employees.

Brian Mathers once a core member of the Scottish based team for the now dissolved UK Online for Business government technology advice program is a trusted, well respected and much sought after advisor who specialises in Ecommerce and Ebrochure website usability and design. His other team members are specialists in Database design, sourcing and supporting the deployment of hardware networking infrastructures, plus there are technical website consultants who either support a client's incumbent web agency or provide short term fixes to sites that migh not have any form of contractual support.

With a Diploma in Business Advice and Counselling awarded to him by Scottish Enterprise you can be sure he will deliver high quality, advice and guidance.

His 'Premier Advisor' award certifies a personal commitment to his continuous professional development. Other qualifications he holds is an HNC in Hardware/Software Support and HND/NVQ5 in Business and Finance, and he is Technology Means Business accredited plus ebusiness engineer certified by the University of Paisley under their E-Skills academy program.

In 2003 Scottish Enterprise presented Brian Mathers with an Inspiring with every Individual award for being one of the most customer focused, forward thinking motivated advisors and specialist consultant supporting the small business network.

Brian is currently working on launching the University of Ecommerce Excellence Initiative with a selected number of Commercial Business Partners. This Ecommerce Excellence program will address Internet Marketing skills currently lacking in Marketing Graduates.
