Bill Goodin’s Summary

During my 15 year career in interactive development & management, I have amassed a wealth of skills that make me a very versatile team member. My background includes programming & management creating Dynamic Websites, Online/Mobile Contests & Promotions, Online Games, HTML Emails, Rich Media Banners, Social Media, Connected Hybrid CD-ROMs, ECDs, Desktop Widgets, DVD Videos, Touch Screen Kiosks and Video/Audio Editing & Encoding. My experience includes interactive programming, creative & technical management and running my own interactive business for 5 years. All of this experience has given me the keen ability to listen to client requirements, provide unique strategies to reach their goals and lead teams of designers and programmers through detailed but easy to understand documentation to reach deadlines on time and within budget. I have learned how to ask the right questions in order to find the right answers. I have a sensitivity as to how much time to spend in research and to know when it's time to stop and get the work done. I also enjoy pulling statistics that create a unique picture of how the customers interact with the projects I have built and lead. The industries I have served are Entertainment, Promotions, Advertising, CPG, B2B,Training and Education.

My desire is to use this experience to guide agencies through the jungle of interactive strategy and development to create truly unique interactive campaigns.
