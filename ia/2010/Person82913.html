Howard Litwak’s Summary

I am part of a national network of Executive Level Coaches and Consultants. We provide solutions in 80 different industries.

I help develop people and facilitate higher performance for entrepreneurs, professional sales people and across organizations.

Some common challenges I deal with include: removing obstacles in the way of success, increasing revenue, doing more with less, lack of training for management, increased competition, losing clients to price, increasing the ability to innovate, and having a better ability to attract and maintain clients.

The development of people is the key to greater growth and profits for both individuals and an organization. Human capital is the true measure of an organization's wealth base (not plants and equipment) and is it's only source for long term stability and competitiveness. Can you be better here?

I am a specialist in the following areas: developing vision and values, increasing profits, managing time better, getting better results, entrepreneurship, motivation, developing people, strategic action, clarifying roles and objectives, communication, problem solving, decision making, goal setting, and more.

If you are frustrated with your current results and are not accomplishing what you want, I can help.
