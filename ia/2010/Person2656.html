Adam Rouman’s Summary

Began professional working career at large electronics retail chain, Best Buy, in 2005. There I was responsible for managing inventory, sales and attachment goals, and educating customers on all products in the computer and computer accessories department. Here I gained an invaluable appreciation for customer service, business acumen, and all things technology.

In the Fall of 2007, I began a marketing internship for the Cal Poly Athletics Department. As part of a 5-man team, we handled all marketing duties for the athletics department. Some of these activities included designing flyers, creating and executing campaigns for upcoming matches to boost attendance, and running and organizing in-game promotional events.

While assuming the duties of my marketing internship, I co-founded a clothing company with a friend of mine in my fraternity and named it GreekApe Clothing Co. He was the design arm and I was the sales lead. In a 2 year period, we sold approximately 15,000 shirts and had an average transaction sale of $2000 (50% increase from when we started).

Once I had graduated and we both had mutually decided to pursue other ventures, in the summer of 2009, I started my post-graduate career with international insurance company, Sun Life Financial, as an Employee Benefits Sales Representative. In this role that I currently hold, licensed by the State of California to write life and health insurance, I am responsible for maintaining and manicuring current accounts and grow my book of business to be as successful as possible while meeting my sales objectives. Cold calls, face-to-face meetings, luncheons, association meetings, and networking sessions are all part of my daily schedule.

Interested in communications, technology, and always looking for a challenge to learn more!
