Claire Kuhl’s Summary

Versatile, results-oriented professional with proven skills in leadership, communication, and organizational development. Qualified by more than 25 years of progressive experience leading departmental and cross-functional teams. Successful at starting up new ventures within dynamic organizations. Known for motivating diverse groups, challenging the status quo, and making effective use of humor. Diplomatic and persuasive at all levels. Works well independently or on a team. Career goal is to be an agent of positive change in an organization that seeks to delight its customers while respecting its associates.
