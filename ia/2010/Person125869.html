Lisa Snyder’s Summary

Lisa Snyder's primary focus, as an insurance agent at Kistler Tiffany Benefits for the past 12 years, is listening to employers and their employees' needs and concerns. She helps employers tailor their group health plans and related employee benefit programs to attract and retain employees in a changing and challenging economy.

In addition to helping employers design and maintain their group health plan, Kistler Tiffany Benefits also provides KTB Advocacy Services powered by Health Advocate. This service helps employees get the most out of their healthcare experience.

Lisa was Vice President of Marketing for 2007-2009 and continues as website administrator for NJAWBO Mercer Chapter. Lisa has served as President and Vice President of Education for CUH2A Toastmasters, and is immediate past president for the club for 2010-2011.

Lisa volunteers with several local non profit organizations to tune up their web presence and provide basic website administration.
