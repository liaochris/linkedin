Aman Ashraff’s Summary

A Creative Strategist/Writer/Actor/Director/Movie Buff/Comic Freak with over twelve years professional experience in media production, advertising and creative brand building solutions. Armed with a PhD from the University of Life which comes in handy from time to time.

Aman Ashraff’s Specialties:

Specializes as a Producer for Commercial Radio and Television. An accomplished Voice Artist having lent his vocal talents to a variety of productions. His accomplishments in the field of advertising have won him accolades from The Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing, The Chillies Advertising Awards, The Reggie Candappa Craft Awards and The Sumathi Awards, in Sri Lanka.
