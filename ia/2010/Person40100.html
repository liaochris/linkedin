Christopher Vail’s Summary

New Paradigm Sales Group (www.NPSalesGroup.com) is a sales-outsourcing company that builds commission-only sales teams for client companies looking to grow regionally, nationally or globally. If your business has a healthy gross margin, scalable production capacity, a short sales cycle and compelling value proposition, we will recruit, hire, train and manage 50 reps working for your company for the same amount you would spend on an average team of three salary-plus salespeople. You will never waste another dime on salespeople who don’t sell and only pay for performance – we guarantee results.

MyEmailDatabase.com (www.MyEmailDatabase.com) is my new startup. After years of building and maintaining a huge email database I've used to do my own B2B marketing, I've learned what works and what doesn't, and how to use email marketing to open doors and get the phone to ring. Now I want to build a custom e-marketing database for your company, and share my techniques for creating campaigns that work.
