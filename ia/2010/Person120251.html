Kyle Cherrick’s Summary

Kyle Cherrick is a Project Development Associate with Axio. In this role he is responsible for managing all aspects of development for a portfolio of wholesale distributed generation photovoltaic (PV) projects, including, project origination, land acquisition, grid interconnection, permitting, and power marketing.

His work for Axio covers five states, and a total of over 2000 acres of solar power projects under development. Kyle is an early member of the Axio Power team; he joined the team in 2008 after graduating from the University of Arizona.

During college, Kyle interned for First Solar, the world’s cost-leading manufacturer of solar modules. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Entrepreneurship & Marketing from the University of Arizona.

Please visit his blog in order to learn more about him: REpreneur -- One young entrepreneur's adventures in renewable energy and electric vehicles -- http://www.repreneur.typepad.com

Kyle Cherrick’s Specialties:

Renewable Project Origination, Interconnection, Land Acquisition, and Project Development.
