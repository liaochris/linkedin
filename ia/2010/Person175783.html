Robert Kramer’s Summary

Executive with sales management experience, skilled in leading teams within the fields of mobile media, entertainment and new media technology to strong growth. Sales management experience with companies and divisions that generate $50-100 million a year in revenue.

Clearly defined management philosophy. Excellent business planner and strategist. Strong sales leader and negotiator. Skilled in guiding direct reports and cross-functional teams to achieved targets/objectives utilizing competitive assessment, market analysis, effective market penetration techniques and innovative sales strategies. Profit-oriented leadership philosophy, driven by open communication, strong business planning, executive responsibility, and commitment to excellence.

Currently managing VeriSign's (Formerly m-Qube) global sales team with a focus on mobile gateway aggregation, mobile marketing applications, mobile web/WAP, mobile storefronts and content distribution.

Created, developed and managed industry leading mobile applications and mobile marketing campaigns for major media companies, social networks, portals, brands, agencies and mobile operators.
