Tim Slavin’s Summary

Tim is an award-winning writer, technologist, and entrepreneur. His combination of business, creative, and technical skills make him a perfect fit for organizations who need people who work well with all disciplines. He understands first hand how business, technology, creative, and support people can mix to make great products and services.

As a communicator, Tim helps businesses communicate effectively with their customers and stakeholders. He has written technical documentation, online help, speeches, articles, email newsletters, and press releases. He also works as an internet marketing expert and web producer creating web sites and email and search marketing campaigns.

As a technologist, Tim has 14 years experience with web sites and web applications, from business planning and documentation to information architecture, interface design, usability testing, database design, software programming and management, user acceptance testing, rollout, promotion, and analysis of results.

He has worked as line staff and management with profit/loss responsibility, as well as hired and managed staff and consultants.

Finally, Tim is passionate about teaching and public service. Whether teaching clients, writing a detailed how-to article for his blog, or helping his non-profit clients do internet marketing, Tim believes strongly in sharing knowledge, helping others succeed, and giving back to the community.

Tim Slavin’s Specialties:

technical writing and technical editing, content management, lead management, online help creation, web content creation, documentation, processes and procedures, web blogs, web publishing, marketing communications, software documentation, usability, information architecture, interface design, internet strategy, presentations, database design, software programming
