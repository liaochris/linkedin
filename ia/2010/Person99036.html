JJ & CHRIS Lord’s Summary

Having come from different parts of the world, award-winning composers JJ & CHRIS have managed to combine their unique abilities into a collaborative music composing partnership. Their synergistic relationship helps to create music in new and unique ways. Thus in 2006 they formed Overtone Productions based out of Los Angeles and have since worked on notable projects.
