Blair Evan Ball’s Summary

beball7@comcast.net. I welcome all connection requests.

Hello, and thank you for visiting my "Linked In" summary. Blair is passionate about increasing revenues. He utilizes his sales, marketing, social media, and operations skills to help build teams and business revenues.

Blair started his sales career with the Xerox Corporation and became one of the youngest marketing managers at their office in Houston. He then had an opportunity to get in on the ground floor of mortgage banking when Chase opened up its first office in Texas. He progressed into management handling Houston and eventually managing Texas and Oklahoma.

During his tenure in Texas he formed three non-profit mortgage associations. The state of Texas, Houston, and Dallas chapters, and was instrumental in forming Austin and the San Antonio Chapter. When he was Legislative Chairman he helped arrange the first meeting with the Secretary of HUD, Henry G. Cisneros in Washington.

Shortly after that he was promoted and transferred to Memphis to head up the Eastern Region. It was a new Tele-Sales concept to call on banks, credit unions and mortgage brokers. He created teams to cover specific geographical areas of the country and subsequently was given the West Coast. He grew the customer base to over 1000, revenues to $1 Billion and profits of $1.4 Million.

His most recent work was changing the charter of a local credit union that had been in existence for 74 years. Opening it up to businesses and associations. He worked with the CEO and the National Credit Union Association to get this approved. Blair then helped compile over 110+ articles on finance, debt, credit and the economy that is being utilized on the company website to educate members. He helped create a new auto finance promotion increasing revenues 35% year over year in a down market.

Blair is currently the President of the Memphis Camera Club and was recently voted to serve a second term 2010.
