Ron Palmera’s Summary

President of Fibrenew - San Diego, the International leader specializing in the restoration of leather and plastics, servicing five major markets: Automotive, Residential, Aviation, Marine and Commercial.

Founded in 1985 there are over 200 franchises in the USA (nearly 100), Canada, New Zealand, Mexico, Australia, Puerto Rico, Ecuador, Colombia and Italy.

Fibrenew we can use our technical skills to restore your home or office / business furniture, auto, boat and aircraft seats / interiors without having to to buy new items expending substantial funds earmarked for basic goods and services for your home or to run your business.

I am certified in repairs and restoration of leather, plastics and vinyl. We formulate our dyes and adhesives to achieve maximum strength and durability while not sacrificing the visual end result. With the strength of hundreds of franchises around the globe and their experience we can always find a solution or a recommendation for your problem or need.

Interests:

Developing and expanding relationships with individuals where we both benefit from referring individuals who we interact with or have knowledge of that we can't directly service in our respective businesses but can be the go to person to possibly satisfy their need. Also working as a vendor to support repair related needs encountered in your business.

Relationship Targets:
Managers, Facilities or Claims Personnel in the following fields: Furniture Stores, Restaurants, Hotel and Spas, Health Clubs, Residential and Commercial Realtors, Property Management Companies, Moving Companies, Upholstery Stores, Used and New Car Dealership,Auto Club Enthusiasts, Auto Body Shops, Auto Detail Shops, Hospital (including veterinary) or Medical Offices Dental Offices, Property Insurance Companies, Aviation Mechanics and Engineers, Aviation Fleet Service, Marina's, Boat Dealers and Crew
