Sharon Jackson’s Summary

I am an IT graduate who discovered a keen interest in web design. I have designed sites for an entertainment consultant, a drama school site, a photographer's gallery and a CMS driven site for a local singer amongst others.

My goal is to create a healthy work life balance through hard work and lots of fun. Money is seen as a necessity that would be good to have in excess but of secondary importance to a happy and fulfilled lifestyle.
