Bill Percival’s Summary

WHAT I DO: Strategy Development, Process & Cash Flow Improvement, Turnaround & Start up Leadership, Financial Analysis. -- I help companies identify effective and efficient solutions to financial, operational and strategic problems.


WHAT QUALIFIES ME: I have a deep analytical background based on engineering, economics and business administration.

My professional experience ranges from large institutional environments on Wall Street to entrepreneurial technology and consumer goods start-ups.

At the core, I'm a expert in financial analysis and operational improvement with a solid understanding of sales, marketing and operations.


WHAT MOTIVATES ME: I like projects. I like to build & fix things. I’ve found that entrepreneurial companies provide great environments for that. There is more energy, less political drag and, at the end of the day, small to mid-sized companies are growth-focused and generally more motivated to move forward.

I'm driven by perfection. I know that sounds cliche, but its really 99% about the journey and 1% about the destination. The process of dogged, relentless pursuit reveals details that really matter - those that separate exceptional results from the merely good.


WAYS THAT I CAN HELP YOU: The current recession has been disruptive. Cash flow has been impaired and it's still unclear whether that will be temporary, or longer term. Developing a clear understanding of your primary economic drivers and properly aligning your operations with them is critical to positioning for future success.

Right "here & now" in most businesses, staffing is minimized to conserve cash. Managers are operating at the red-line just to keep up. Nevertheless, emerging problems and opportunities need to be thoroughly analyzed and expertly addressed. Finding capable capacity is key.

I get up to speed quickly and can work independently if necessary to achieve your goals.
