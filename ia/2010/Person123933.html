Leo Gaggl’s Summary

General:
Thinking sustainable and long-term. This is one of the guiding principles in everything I do. Not just as an afterthought, but as part of the overall strategy.

Technical:
I have worked with databased web applications ever since they became mainstream (1996) and kept focussed on this ever since. Because of my background in Business & Marketing I have always been particularily interested in Business Software as I can not only contribute on the technical side but also have some insight in the way critical business systems (should be) operating.

- Database Backends: MSSQL, Oracle, Ingres, CA Datacom, CA Ingres, PostgreSQL, MySQL, various embedded mobile DB's

- Web(server) environments: IIS (Version 1 upwards), Apache (Version 1 upwards), Apache Tomcat (Version 3 upwards), various embedded webserver daemons

- Operating systems (Server): Windows 2000, 2003, 2008, RedHat EL (Version 3 upwards), CentOS (Version 3 upwards), SuSE EL (Version 8 upwards), HPUX, MacOSX Server

Operating systems (Client): Windows (all versions), Linux (various distributions but mainly SuSE & (K)Ubuntu), MacOS (OSX upwards), SymbianOS, various embedded OS's

- Programming (Scripting): C#, Java, ASP, ASP.Net, Python, JSP, PHP, VB, Javascript

- Application Development Frameworks: Microsoft Sharepoint Server, Adobe Flex, Open Laszlo, Apache Tapestry

Leo Gaggl’s Specialties:

My particular technical focus at the moment is in mobile (phone) software (FlashLite, J2ME, Symbian, Windows Mobile) and the integration of mobile software interfaces in business systems (SOA, Webservices, Legacy Systems, Middleware, Learning Management Systems such as Moodle, Sharepoint Learning Solutions).

In terms of vertical industries most of my work tends to be in the fields of Education (m-Learning & e-Learning), Government Administration and Agriculture.
