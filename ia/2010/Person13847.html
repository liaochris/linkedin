Anna Fizyta’s Summary

www.heartpsychotherapy.com
Helping people to connect is my soul's calling and I feel blessed to be doing this work.
I help individuals connect to their hearts and to that quiet, persistent voice that keeps whispering to them about what they really want for their life.
I help couples open their hearts to each other, and nurture a vibrantly fulfilling connection that allows for each person's vulnerable feelings and intimate truth to be valued and supported. I view the presenting "problem" as a guide to pointing our attention to an inherent solution, that when bravely faced, is the first step on a conscious and loving growth path as a couple.

www.awakenedcreativity.org
This is my creative outlet merging my spirituality with my life-long experiences as an artist, and my recent exciting insights on creativity from my experiences as an entrepreneur. I create public art projects such as www.missionwishingtree.com - I also am offering a workshop on Awakening Your Creative Spirit, using art and guided meditation to help people tap into their creative power to change their lives.

------Additional Consulting Experience
I have extensive experience using Non-Violent Communication in conflict resolution work with couples and with small businesses dealing with interpersonal friction complicating collaboration. I am available for consultation as a communication coach.
