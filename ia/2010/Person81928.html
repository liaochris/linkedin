    Medimix International is a dynamic provider of advanced marketing solutions for the healthcare industry worldwide. Medimix’s proprietary panel provides direct access to over 500,000 physicians and healthcare professionals from around the world, making it one of the largest panels in the industry.

    Medimix International has expertise in key therapeutic areas including oncology/hematology, neurology, endocrinology/metabolic, rheumatology, musculo-skeletal, cardiology, infectious diseases, respiratory, ophthalmology, and dermatology as well as niche areas such as organ transplantation and orphan diseases.
