Ionut Oprea’s Summary

I worked for more than 6 years within the print media, both at a daily newspaper and at a weekly publication, then I decided to transfer my knowledge and skills to online marketing, communication and the internet, helping both these industries grow in Romania. And I'm doing this for over 6 years now.

In 2003 I started working at Neogen SA, where I have established a marcom strategy including both performance-oriented online marketing and a high-profile public relations program, with national success, with several marketing activities that produced both quantitative and qualitative results.

After a year with Danish start-ups Spleak and Zyb, where I coordinated marcom activities, I returned to improving the communication strategy of the Romanian internet, at netBridge, until 2006 and at MediaPro Interactiv, where was for more than a year Director of Communication, coordinating both marketing and PR activities.

In 2007 I started my own online marcom agency, Blog Agency, delivering in the beginning blog marketing consultancy and support. We later expanded our portfolio of services to online marketing, from an unconventional perspective. We're still the only Romanian agency working permanently with blog marketing projects and campaigns.

My Romanian blog, focused on the Romanian internet market, but also on marketing and media analysis at large, is located at www.clickio.ro.
