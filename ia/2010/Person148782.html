Peter George’s Summary

As a speaker, author, and coach, Peter George teaches self-employed professionals and other small business owners how to finally achieve the success they have been striving for. He shows them how they can consistently attract not only more clients, but specifically more profitable clients.

Peter offers presentations, seminars, and workshops, for organizations, associations, events, and companies. His presentations are designed to inspired his audiences to move from where they are to where they have long-envisioned to be. His audiences vary from 12 to 2,000, totalling more than 26,000 people in the United States, Canada, England, Bermuda, the Caribbean, and on the seas.

Peter is the author of the 200-page More Clients More Profits Workbook. This interactive book offers its users the ability to follow Peter's More Clients More Profits Marketing System at their own speed, while using the same exercises and templates that Peter uses when personally assisting businesspeople achieve greater success.
