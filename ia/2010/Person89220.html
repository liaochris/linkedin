Jane Stretton’s Summary

Life's all about finding your own way, isn't it?

Put simply, I had enough of received wisdoms and thankless hours spent on things I didnt believe in, and found a way of doing more of what I wanted to do.

I work even longer hours now, and yes, my tasks do include a certain amount of stuff that I don't enjoy, but at least it's my shit, and I accept responsibility for that!

Not trying to sell 'The Dream' here - because my life would be some people's nightmare - but simply being around farming, countryside, and animals - is completely grounding. I love that it's hard work, and equally love how the farm creates a reflective space, where I can think, develop ideas and branch out in new directions.

'Previous life' saw a wide-ranging professional career, including teaching, business management and consultancy.
Experience and qualifications have earned me professional credibility in a classroom, in a company boardroom, or working alongside community reps.

But that was then, and this is now - You can never stand still in business, and life doesn't let you anyway. There's always a new stone to turn.....

We have diversified our farm in all sorts of ways, including holiday cottages and a business meeting room. At Dove Farm, we give a little bit of ''breathing space" to anyone who comes here. We specialise in short breaks for groups of family and friends, special occasion breaks and corporate retreats. workshops too.

I am a network leader for WiRE (women in rural enterprise) and represent rural business and women's enterprise, as a member of the Enterprise Board at Advantage West Midlands.

Engaging with and working alongside colleagues and partners, is important to me. Respected for my integrity, straight talk and generosity of spirit, I like to see people move forward in their lives. More importantly, in a way that enables them to be true to themselves.
