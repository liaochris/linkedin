Anish Jain, CPF (www.ibf.org)’s Summary

I have a passion for fostering the growth of Demand Planning & Forecasting and the careers of those in the field.

I encourage you to join my network and the Institute of Business Forecasting & Planning – IBF Group! Get lessons learned & aid research in the field!

If you have Forecasting, Demand Planning, and Supply Chain Management responsibilities. If you have experience in S&OP and CFPR processes. If you’re either new to recognizing the importance of these areas or have growing knowledge of the discipline as a practitioner for some time, join my network.

I’ve been with the IBF since 1995 and continue to do so because of my belief in the role Demand Planning & Forecasting plays in business. From IBF's benchmarking research, we continue to see growth of upper management support for the function, now over 54%, which is much higher than what we had 5 years ago. We continue to see investments being made in training, education, and certification of company staff.

Furthermore, as per Dr. Chaman Jain & Mark Covas’s recent Wall Street Journal article "Thinking About Tomorrow. Seven Tips for Making Forecasting More Effective" (Dr. Jain is Chief Editor of the IBF’s Journal of Business Forecasting & Mark Covas is an IBF Board Member who is with P&G) we continue to see companies benefiting from collaborative efforts both within and outside the enterprise. It also continues to prove the value of forecasting & planning and how transparency and group efforts can bring real measurable results.

WSJ Article: http://www.ibf.org/wsj1.cfm

We continue to encounter companies that base their forecasts on plans/ goals, and purchase software before having a proper process in place. We continue to encounter companies that don’t prioritize forecasting and feel investments should NOT be made in fields where you know you’re going to be wrong.

Therefore, the mission continues and with your help we can further the field together.

Email me at: linkedin @ ibf.org
