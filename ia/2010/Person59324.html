David Posada’s Summary

Recently, David Posada joined Havas Media Colombia team as the Digital Director, where he is responsible for the growth and leadership of the local digital operation, as well as the development of the entire digital strategy of the group that has two main brands in Colombia: Lattitud and Media Contacts.

David started his digital carreer at Tribal Colombia where he began working as operational director. After having multiple positions at Tribal, including the direction of a new local office and the direction of the regional digital media unit, in October 2006, David became the managing director of the company, facing the challenge of developing interactive media as a fundamental strategy to reach its client's brands.

David is involved in online advertising industry associations such as the IAB Colombia, where he was member of the first board. He also speaks to universities about e-marketing and online advertising industry in the country.

Finally, David has consistently exceeded sales goals and customer service expectations and has experience in handling international clients.

He has a degree in Industrial Engineer.

David Posada’s Specialties:

Online media business, interactive agency managing and direction, e-marketing, e-advertising, digital media strategy, planning and buying.
