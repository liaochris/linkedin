Cheryl Kranz’s Summary

CMP with solid management skills and leadership experience in experiential event design and execution.

Began career at Business Incentives, spent several years at Carlson Marketing Group as Director of Operations, Incentive Travel, started the Meetings Division (Business Theatre, Productions and Communications) and eventually, became the VP Operations for a second start up when CMG moved into Event Marketing.

Leaving Carlson, I opened Creative Events focusing on special event design and management services for 75 small to mid-size corporations. While growing the company from a start-up to $900,000 in annual revenue, key accomplishments included:

Executive-produced NFL Alumni Player of the Year Awards Dinner, Superbowl XXX;

Directed international award-winning special event for 2500 on the Mpls Central Riverfront;

Cofounded the Minnesota Chapter-International Special Events Society (ISES) ;

Served on local review committee for Convention Industry Council's APEX initiative to develop Best Practices for the Meetings/Events Industry.

In late 2006 when it was announced that the GOP would hold their convention in MSP in Sept, 2008, Creative Events continued to support volunteeer efforts with the Minneapolis Riverfront District, while I joined forces with Equinox Creative as part of Atmosphere Events, a creative collaboration of leading event design and operational talent in the Twin Cities. Pooling sales and marketing resources, we supported event marketing efforts of local companies during the Republican Convention.
(www.AtmosphereEvents.net)

In March, 2009, Atmosphere Events received the Minnesota Star Awards for "Best Corporate Event over $75,000", "Best Event Design and Decor over $25,000", and "Best ISES Team Event" for a Medtronic hospitality event executive-produced by me during the convention.

With the GOP Convention behind us, I have returned to Creative Events and look forward to connecting with you via Linked-In!
