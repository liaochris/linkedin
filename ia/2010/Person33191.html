Carol Voss’s Summary

Community relations and management leader in for-profit and not-for-profit settings. Passionate and loyal PR professional that gets the job done with enthusiasm and positivity. Goals include: mentoring new professionals, identifying new opportunities, offering expertise and energy in volunteer opportunities that make my community a better place to live and work, while adding value to my employer through my innovation and excellent, consistent deliverables.

Carol Voss’s Specialties:

Community relationship building, event planning and coordination, public speaking, branding and identity building, board development, sponsorship relations, fund development. Additional skills derived from working in the health care and disability services sectors: consultant on universal design for products and environments, speaker specializing in disability awareness training with many contacts who have disabilities themselves.
