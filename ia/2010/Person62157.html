Eduardo Crespo’s Summary

A bilingual/bicultural professional with over 20 years of regional/national US Hispanic and Latin American marketing and recruitment experience. Increasing awareness about these markets and providing strategic thinking, generating new business, creating “out of the box” solutions, and managing accounts are my core strengths:

1 Generated new business and managed accounts. Clients included Auto Nation, Dr. Pepper, Varig, Visión, Telemundo. Earned Se Habla Español national award.

2 Created successful strategic branding campaigns combining the brand’s internal resources, our market know-how, expertise, and media buying capabilities. Clients included Pepsi, MLS, Red Sox Radio Network, NE Ford Dealers, Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce.

3 Team player that provided promotional advertising and recruitment services through integrated media channels such as direct response TV, radio, print, web campaigns. Research, contests, co-branding, product sampling, and field marketing opportunities were utilized to position the brand and generate more awareness, clients and sales. Clients included American Airlines, Domino’s Pizza, Trans National Travel (TNT), GOYA, Western Union, Bank of Boston, Radio Shack, Dominican Republic Tourism Bureau, Budweiser, National Mortgage.

4 Developed successful integrated promotional and recruitment campaigns for multiple ethnic outdoor summer events in major markets that presented major entertainers, bands, and performers. Tapped major national clients to participate such as Nabisco, Budweiser, Raytheon, TJX, 7 Eleven, Blockbuster, Univision.

CAREER GOALS

To use my entrepreneurial bilingual-bicultural background and expertise in senior level client service, marketing and business development in a progressive brand or agency that caters to the Hispanic/multicultural markets, appreciates diversity, and needs to grow.

Eduardo Crespo’s Specialties:

Hispanic communications, multicultural marketing and advertising, minority and diversity assessment, training and recruitment, marketing, advertising, new business development, bi-lingual bicultural (Spanish-English) communications, community relations. Exports promotions consultant specializing in Latin America. Public speaker in diversity topics, including HR, recruitment. Boston and New England is our main territory.
