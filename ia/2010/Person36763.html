Cheryl Contee’s Summary

My firm, Fission Strategy, provides innovative online consulting services to nonprofits and foundations. We have a dynamic and dedicated team. Clients have included: MomsRising.org, Greenpeace, ACLU of Northern California, American Cancer Society, Alliance for Climate Protection, Grameen Foundation and many more.

Prior to launching my own firm, I was the lead digital strategist for Fleishman-Hillard on the West Coast (U.S.) I specialize in helping Fortune 500 companies, major non-profit organizations and leading trade associations manage their brands and their campaigns online. I have extensive online outreach, branding, communications, advocacy, and fundraising experience. I am one of many pioneers in the field of Web 2.0, social media, mobile and blogger relations.

Before Fleishman-Hillard, I led the interactive team as Vice President at Issue Dynamics Inc, a boutique public affairs firm based in Washington DC, now Amplify Public Affairs. While at IDI, I launched and led the group blog BloggerRelations.com. Previously, I was the Web Director for Oceana, an international marine conservation organization. Before Oceana, I launched 40 multi-lingual Web sites for Discovery Communications as Senior Producer for International Networks for television brands Discovery Channel, Travel Channel, Animal Planet, TLC, Discovery Health, Discovery Kids and more.
