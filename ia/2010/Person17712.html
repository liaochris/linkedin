About me:
========

I’ve been in Sales & Marketing for around 15 years with diversified industries ranging from Oil Marketing to FMCGs and from Hospitality to Telecom. During my career with Chevron, Tapal Tea, Hashoo Group (Pearl Continental & Marriott Hotels) and Worldcall Group, I’ve gone through B2B selling, relationship management, sales promotions, trade marketing, retailing, merchandising, appointment of dealers, distributors and franchisees, product launches, sales planning, distribution, brand activation, trouble-shootings, town-storming activities, lucky draws, hiring of staff, trainings, motivational sessions and appraisals etc.
