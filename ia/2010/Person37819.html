Chris Gregory’s Summary

At Enterprise Leadership New Zealand we help to transform disfunctional small to mid-sized businesses using our coaching systems and business development tools into manageable, well-run, successful enterprises that provide for and serve everyone they touch.

Business owners gain leadership, marketing, management and other business skills that allow them to effectively and profitably guide their business, find new business, do the business and run their business.

Our more than 60 years in the public accountancy, advertising and marketing, and business coaching industries bring a wealth of experience to owners of small and mid-sized businesses, including more than 5 years as Senior E-Myth Consltants.

Chris Gregory’s Specialties:

Business coaching concentrating on helping owners of small and mid-sized businesses develop strategic management skills, systems-based thinking, operational systems building, financial understanding, and market sense to create businesses that deliver to everyone they touch.

Chris Gregory’s Experience

  • Owner and Director

    Enterprise Leadership New Zealand

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2000 — Present (10 years 5 months)

    Transforming small businesses to deliver results

    Chris Gregory is the co-owner of Enterprise Leadership New Zealand (Formally Business Development Academy), a business coaching company that assists business owners to get direction, structure, systems and measurement processes into their businesses to allow then to gain more freedom to live a life independent from their business.

    Enterprise Leadership New Zealand is a founding member of The Alliance for Enterprise Leadership Inc. (AEL), an international alliance of business coaching professionals that offers Full Spectrum Business Development programs and coaching to owners of small and mid-sized businesses.

    Our primary focus is to deliver measurable results through the creation of busineses that work to business owners, their customers, suppliers, employees, and everyone that the business touches.

  • Director and Owner

    Business Development Academy

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2000 — January 2007 (7 years 1 month)

    Business Development Academy Limited is the former name of Enterprise Leadership New Zealand Limited, a business coaching organisation that offered coaching programs developed by E-Myth Worldwide. Its owners and directors were trained and licensed by E-Myth Worldwide to deliver The E-Myth Mastery Program.

    In December 2006 Business Development Academy terminated its relationship with E-Myth Worldwide and was accredited and licensed by The Alliance for Enterprise Leadership Inc to deliver its Full Spectrum Business Development Program to owners of small and mid-sized businesses.

  • Partner

    Grant Thornton, Auckland

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Accounting industry)

    July 1977 — December 2000 (23 years 6 months)

    Trained as a chartered accountant, Chris graduated from The University of Auckland in 1971 with a Bachelor of Commerce degree and was admitted as a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of New Zealand in the same year. From 1971 to 1974 he worked in London as and audit manager for a mid-sized UK chartered accounting firm that specialized in the audit of mid-sized companies and charities. From 1974 to 1976 Chris worked in the audit division with Price Waterhouse before becoming a partner in the mid-sized international accountancy firm, Grant Thornton as an audit partner. He was a member of the firm's National Audit Task Force (with responsibility for technical development and training) and was the New Zealand firm’s representative on the Asia Pacific Audit Task Force. Chris was also responsible for advising small and mid-sized client businesses, specialising in strategic and business planning.
