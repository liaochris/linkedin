Matthew Carrozo’s Summary

New Yorker by nature, European by nurture. I was born Stateside, raised in Portugal and attended higher education in the UK. I often wake up in fear that I sound like Loyd Grossman.

Born in the year Orwell gave weighting to, I remember what life was like before we logged on for an uninterrupted stream of information, but love discovering the opportunities in sifting and sorting through it all. It's made me that rare breed of digital native - one who can bridge the gap for those who are overwhelmed or under-versed & the rest of us who take it for granted.
