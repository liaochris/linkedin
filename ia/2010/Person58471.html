Don Power’s Summary

If you know what Archie, Veronica and Gopher are, then you'll get a rough idea of the time I started my career in Information Technology.

In fact, one of my first technology assignments was to "go out and find out what this thing called the Internet is all about..."

Well, needless to say I've seen a lot since then.

For over 17 years running, I've been immersed in all facets of Business Technology, from Help Desk to Hardware, from Programmer to Project Manager.

I worked in the data centers of clients like The Toronto Stock Exchange, Bank of Montreal, IBM - in the lead up to Y2K.

And yes, I did have a Y2K bunker...

I was never so glad to be wrong in all my life... The world as we knew it did not end, and I didn't have to buy groceries until 2002!

For the last 7 or 8 years, I've specialized in IT Sales, Marketing, Business Development and Client Relationship Building.

I thoroughly enjoy connecting the dots... Moving ideas from a well executed plan to a successful reality.

I'm a student of both Philosophy and Business - realizing that pondering the Big Picture is fantastic, but at the end of the day you still gotta make a living...

My name is Don - and I invite you to contact me - from one person to another.
