Fred Ullrich’s Summary

As part of FredUllrich Photography and Consulting I am a resident artist at Water Street Studio in Batavia, Illinois. I use this space as gallery, meeting and studio work. I have continue to work on projects in the dark room making several new fine art black and white prints. I should have this new work on display in early March. I have over numerous framed and matted images available for viewing in my studio with lots of other fiber base black and white prints not mounted. I am now able to print large format ink jset prints using Canon's newest large format printer. My plans are to develop my fine art printing capability using this additional imaging technology.

Fred Ullrich’s Specialties:

Specialize in directing creative teams in solving problems. I have expertise in all areas of communication technology consulting as well as scientific photography, portraiture and group photography.

Manager of creative teams and projects, fine art black and white photography and strategic business development planning.
