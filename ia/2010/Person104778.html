Jordan Grossman’s Summary

An innovative entrepreneur who's biggest strength is the cross-roads between analytics and creativity, I draw on a diverse background of successes that includes founding a media production company with over 30 people on 4 continents; earning an MBA in marketing and finance and degree in physics; teaching high school biology, physics and biophysics; and winning an NYU film producing award.

I also have a patent application on file that is a next-generation solution to targeted television advertising and solving the fast-forwarding dilemma. Need to wait for cable box technology to advance, but any other ideas are welcome!

Currently, I enjoy working as a marketing and strategy consultant at Rosetta in NJ and NYC.
