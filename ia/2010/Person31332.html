Caitlin Nicholson’s Summary

I graduated in May 2009 from the University at Albany (SUNY) Summa Cum Laude with a Bachelor's Degree in Journalism with a double Minor in English and Sociology. My overall GPA was a 3.7. As part of my course of study, I learned the basic foundations of media ethics, hard news writing, features writing, investigative reporting and research, public relations writing, layout and page design as well as the fundamentals of reporting on religion and spirituality. I also volunteered at the Albany Times Union on Election Night 2009 helping to cover the party shift in the New York State Senate.

While at SUNY Albany, I was a member of Pi Sigma Epsilon Nation National Co-ed Professional Fraternity for sales, marketing, and management. I held the position of St. Jude chair, and served as Executive Director of the Up 'Til Dawn E-Board. Together with the help of the E-Board, we planned a first year fundraising event that raised nearly $9,000 and brought in 300 students in the campus community.

Recently, I have moved to the Washington, DC area seeking employment in the Journalism field. Experience aside, I am looking to reciprocate my academic success into a successful career. I am very comfortable in a fast paced environment and pride myself on being very level headed in chaotic situations.
