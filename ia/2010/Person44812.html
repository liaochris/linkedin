Venkatesh C.R.’s Summary

Began IT career in the year 1997 by starting a Web Design Company CRV Infotech when internet was in the nascent stages in India. President of the Internet Users Community of India, Madurai Chapter and was instrumental in bringing Internet access to Madurai. Growing Dot Com Infoway from 5 staff to 300+ staff within a gap of 8 years. Currently handling the entire team of professionals with experience on small as well as large integrated projects. Extensively traveled all over the world and dealing with overseas clients for the past 10 years. An Engineering Graduate and has studied at Anna University, Chennai.
