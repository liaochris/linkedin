Michael Jordan’s Summary

As part of the Personal Injury Practice of the Law Offices of James Scott Farrin, my focus is on helping injury victims throughout North Carolina. I have practiced law since 1993 and have been with the firm since 2001. I am also a published author in the legal field and active in leadership positions within the statewide legal community, particularly North Carolina Advocates for Justice. My goals are simply to protect victims by offering thorough, honest advice along with quality representation (when appropriate) and to help other attorneys develop in their careers.

Michael Jordan’s Specialties:

We don't use the word "specialize" or any derivative due to regulations by the NC Bar; however, I am a plaintiffs' personal injury attorney.

We gladly accept referrals from other firms and would like to speak with you if you need assistance on a personal injury case.
