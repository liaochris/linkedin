debra thompson’s Summary

My focus:
I am a senior technical recruiter who helps technology companies in high growth stages find the most in-demand engineering talent who share their vision, are a strong technical and cultural fit and can hit the ground running. I have been in the industry since 1994 and have built a strong network of qualified candidates.

I enjoy working with VPE’s, CTO’s and hiring managers who are under a tremendous amount of pressure with deadlines and day to day tasks to help ease their burden by sourcing candidates who will fit both technically and culturally into their environment. There is no greater satisfaction for me than finding the “right match.”

I am passionate about recruiting great technical talent and love to see people really challenged and excited about what they are working on.

Key Highlights:

I have successfully placed technical professionals including VPE’s, Directors, Java developers, PHP developers, Linux Admin, UI designers, Flash Developers, and Search Engineers. I have also worked with venture backed startups and their CEO’s, CTO’s and VPE’ s to help build and round out engineering and ops teams. Some of my recent placements include:

• Glam Media – Placed a number of senior PHP developers, Director of Engineering, Director of Datawarehousing/BI as well as campaign managers

• CurrentTV/Media; FanIQ – Placed VPE’s

• Scoutlabs –Senior Search Engineers

• Playdom – senior flash engineers

• Partial list of past clients I have worked with in the technology industry include Ink2, Pandora, Technorati, Podshow aka Mevio, Looksmart, Dean & Delucca, EHow, Mpower and other venture backed firms. I started my career in 1994 placing software engineers with Fortune 500 companies including Charles Schwab, Wells Fargo, Bank of America Securities, Genetech, and Chiron.

    Major Strength: Ability to target quality engineering talent and consistently make placements that add to the core strength of engineering and operations teams whether building a core engineering team from scratch or filling a specific role.

    I use the following approach to find the best match for my clients:

    • Understanding your culture – I take a good amount of time understanding your companies’ vision, needs and culture as well as your specific requirements for an engineering candidate.

    • Focusing on Quality over Quantity – You don’t need to worry about me filling your inbox with unqualified candidates just to say I delivered something. I will only send you screened and qualified candidates so your time is spent well only looking at people who are a good technical or cultural fit for your organization.

    • Being Straightforward and Honest – I do not misrepresent the qualifications of candidates to clients or misrepresent the client’s needs and expectations to candidates. I understand the needs of both parties and do my best to deliver what I see as true potential matches for both parties.

    • Staying plugged-In -- Because I’ve been doing this work for many years, I have a good sense for what the current technical recruiting needs, challenges and best practices are in the industry. I am able to bring my expertise into all of my client engagements to help you through the process with greater efficiency and ease.
