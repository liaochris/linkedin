Xantha Bruso’s Summary

Carbon management professional with quantitative and policy education and experience in the utility business. Current work at PG&E includes climate change policy development and advocacy, GHG emissions reporting, and corporate environmental strategy. In previous positions at PG&E, worked on commercializing emerging renewable energy technologies and on administering the California Solar Initiative. Past experience in environmental conflict resolution, environmental consulting, and biodiesel and green building start-ups. Detail-oriented, ambitious, and passionate about achieving economic AND environmental gains. Goal is to be at the forefront of our transition to a competitive, low-carbon economy.

Xantha Bruso’s Specialties:
