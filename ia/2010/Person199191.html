Tal Kramer’s Summary

My wife and are Active, Full Time Licensed Agents Since 1990. We serve Sellers, Buyers and Investors. We also own and operate Avalon Property Management Services specializing in the Leasing and Management of Residential Properties.

Sellers - Our Marketing background has enabled us to provide Marketing Tools and Systems well beyond that typically provided by most real estate agents.

Buyers - We begin every Buyer experience with a Buyer Orientation meeting to learn our client's requirements and share information about local conditions along with the latest market data. In doing so, our Buyers are better informed and as a result, we believe they make better buying decisions.

Investors - We have personally invested in rental properties since the 1980's and been very successful in doing so. We bring that expertise to Investors seeking to maximize a Return on Investment. In addition to buying and selling investment properties, we assist our clients in securing Tenants and Property Management as well.
