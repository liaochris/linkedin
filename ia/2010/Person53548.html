Deborah Downie, CFA’s Summary

Seasoned corporate credit research professional / Charterd Financial Analyst with excellent & lengthy track record in determining relative value within the bond universe including leveraged loans, high yield bonds, mezzanine financing, distressed debt, convertible bonds, investment grade corporates and private placements. Generated top quartile performance within sectors by utilizing strong analytical and financial modeling skills. Excellent presentation abilities with experience speaking to audiences up to 250 participants. Have published over 200 written reports including individual recommendations and industry updates.
