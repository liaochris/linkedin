Mawdud Choudhury’s Summary

With over twelve (12) years in the IT industry, I am an experienced manager cum executive capable of shouldering significant responsibility. Successful in all endeavours, help build organisations, lead change programs, and enhance value at every stage. I am well versed in all areas of technology management (planning, development, architecture – hardware/software, delivery, operations, finance) and I am an excellent team builder focused on the people, process and technology dimensions of the challenge.

I have specific expertise in Programme/Project Management, Service Delivery, Business & IT Transformation/Change programs and IT Operations. Experience in many industries including investment banking/financial services (10 years), Government (2 years), Energy (2 years) and Telcos (2 years). Well versed in Lean Six Sigma process improvement and design, DMAIC, DFSS, value stream mapping (VSM) and cost reductions.

My current role is Chief Information Officer for Universal System Technologies’ (UST), a leading IT Systems Integrator, Solutions Provider and Consultancy in Brunei Darussalam. I lead a team responsible for developing UST’s enterprise architecture, internal business systems, IT standards and policies, and information security program. Also responsible for overall management of the Project Management Office (PMO), Service Delivery, Operations, Support, & Business Development Divisions.

Before joining UST, I worked at HSBC Bank plc for 8 years within the Global Banking & Markets Division in numerous roles including Service Delivery Manager, Support Manager, Programme/Project Manager, Business Analyst, and IT Consultant.
