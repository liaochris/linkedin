Marilyn Jess, DTM’s Summary

My twin passions are public speaking and wellness coaching. I believe that one should never have to choose one thing from life's menu, and I never have.

Speaking Of... is my business giving workshops on improving public speaking skills, and coaching speakers individually. Working in healthcare for over 20 years, plus my many years in Toastmasters, has given me the unique perspective of recognizing communication challenges that staff face, and helping them communicate better.

People want targeted, simple communication--that's what I specialize in coaching people to do.

I have written a home study speaking course that conveys my philosophy of public speaking, that can be used by any healthcare professional, and offers both dietitians and registered nurses continuing education hours.

http://www.gettingthatjumpstart.com/store/item_view.asp?estore_itemid=1000029

I also work as a licensed wellcoach. My specialty is email private coaching, which is a distinct writing and coaching skill. In 2010 I joined the facilitator team for Vtrim, an online weight management program, University of Vermont.

I co-host The Wellness Coaches radio show on www.blogtalkradio.com.
