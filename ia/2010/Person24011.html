William Rich’s Summary

Principal at Bowen Advisors, Inc., a mergers and acquisitions and strategic advisory firm for emerging and established technology companies in the enterprise, service provider, government and defense market sectors. We provide a full suite of advisory services including buyside and sellside M&A, private capital raising, fairness opinion and valuation analysis. www.bowenadvisors.com

Three-time technology CEO with over 24 years of experience in data networking, communications software, hardware and services, network security, IT, and mobility. Deep background M&A, fund raising marketing, turnarounds, sales, business development, strategy development, and team building.

Based in Boston area.
