Laurie Kalmanson’s Summary

Expertise in digital and mobile user experience, customer experience, website structure, social media, interaction flows, wireframes, interface requirements and project communication, to discover invent and document solutions for matching business goals and audience needs.

UX/IXD/IA, content strategy, social media strategy, online community management, technical writing and BI/BA online since the beginning, at Yoyodyne Entertainment / Seth Godin Productions (acquired by Yahoo!), digital agencies, tech startups, Fortune 500s, financial services, law firms and non-profits.

Before the internet (and before cellphones), I was a newspaper and wire service reporter. My interviewing and story telling skills built my bridge from journalism to interactive media in 1995, in NYC, when we were calling it Silicon Alley.

My masters degree in Public Policy Studies is from the University of Chicago.

Question: Is the similarity of the aspect ratio of the backlit, overhead ads on the NYC subways and the 468x60 banner ads of the early web a conscious or coincidental artifact of the roots of the digital agency business?

Clients and Projects: Fortune 500 companies, startups, and non profits, including The Actors Fund, The Climate Project, American Express, AT&T / Downtown Digital, Bideawee, CBS.com, Columbia/Presbyterian Medical Center, Consolidated Edison, Continuum Health Partners, Dilbert/United Media, Dogmatic, Ford, Hugo Boss, Mercedes-Benz, Passalong Networks, Pitney-Bowes, Standard & Poor's, The Climate Project, Time Inc., TGLLAW.com, Yoyodyne (acquired by Yahoo!), Vanderbilt University Medical Center, and WebMD/Emdeon.
