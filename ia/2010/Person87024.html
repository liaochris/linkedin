Jacqueline Arroyo’s Summary

Graduate of CIDA accredited Interior Designer Program at West Valley College. Currently seeking position in Hospitality Design, Visual Merchandising, Retail Design, Themed Design, Event Design.

Excellent communication skills that allow for a thorough understanding of overall project requirements, and client's needs. Through active listening, collaboration and negotiation skills, (which have been developed after 20+ years of experience as a psychologist), I am able to translate design philosophies, functions and forms into cohesive design concepts, which meet and/or exceed the client's expectations.
