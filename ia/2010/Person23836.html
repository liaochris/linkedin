Bill Hunt’s Summary

Creative and digital director with a big agency background that includes Hill & Knowlton, Cohn & Wolfe and Porter Novelli.

I offer consultancy advice on online and offline PR without the exorbitant hourly rates that come with working with a big agency.

My interest in the web came about from teaching myself web design back in 2003. This has also been useful in getting to know the ins and outs of SEO.
