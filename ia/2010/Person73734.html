Geary Lewis’s Summary

Prior to GDL Media LLC, as Vice President of Sales at CyberOptics Semiconductor Inc (CSI), I worked with the executive team on corporate strategy and oversaw worldwide channel development of its semiconductor equipment products. Prior to CSI, I was Vice President of Sales & Marketing of Imagenation, a machine vision company startup, which was eventually purchased by CyberOptics for its management team—of which I was part. I had developed expertise in sales and marketing in the technology industry with various management roles with such companies as GSS/NCD, (graphics hardware and software manufacturing). SCO-IXI Division (PC X terminal software ), Phase X Systems (Xterminal hardware distribution), Conetic Systems (financial accounting database development), and Money Tree Software (financial planning software). I hold a Bachelors and Masters degrees from Brigham Young University.
