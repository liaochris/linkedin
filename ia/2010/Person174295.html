Robb Webb’s Summary

It's all about Sleep health.

Sleep health is my forté, I have never felt more right about any other career.

I have been a CPAP (continuous positive airway pressure) therapy patient for over eight years. I have had Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) for probably more years than that. When I underwent my own sleep study in 1999 I had no idea that it would have such a profound effect on my life. It changed my life. Until that night I did not know what it meant to have a truly restful night's sleep. I have since been a supporter of CPAP compliance. I have identified friends and family who may be suffering from OSA and not even know it, and through education I get them to ask their physicians about having a sleep study. This seems like something that I was meant to do.

My one goal at this time is to become a fully Registered Polysomnography Technologist (RPSGT)

I have over 17 years in the customer service industry, 14 collective years in Graphic Design/Digital Prepress, and a year and a half of Business Analysis/Project Management.
