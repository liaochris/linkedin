Dr. Shaan Kumar’s Summary

Dr. Shaan Senthil Kumar is Chairman of iGLOBAL Investment Group, an international angel investment and technology incubation house. He is Chairman of United States Global Entrepreneurs Association (USGEA); an organization that promotes and nurtures entrepreneurs globally. Dr. Kumar is also the founder and CEO of California InterContinental University, a nationally accredited university offering bachelors, masters, and doctoral degree programs.

Dr. Kumar was President & CEO of NETed Inc, an E-learning technology and services company, funded by the Singapore Economic Development Board in 2003. He also serves on the board of H2B Studios, a hollywood2bollywood cross-cultural film production and entertainment company. He has served as a chairman or board member on numerous other boards and committees, including eVisions iBRA - International Bioinformatics Research Academy silicon valley, ERM Anywhere.com, Qsoft , EdNET Solutions & Technology Group, Sun Microsystems Asia Pacific technology review committee in 2004.

As a global entrepreneur and strategic angel investor, he has funded many hi-tech software companies some of which went IPO under his leadership. He has tremendous experience in mergers and acquisitions, global franchise development, establishing strategic partnerships and joint ventures in many industries.

Dr. Kumar is an author of many technology books, journals, and articles. He has written many titles under the Just-N-Hour Series. His recent release “Vision Brilliance Success,” a handbook for every entrepreneur, provides a step-by-step approach on growing one’s enterprise. As an educator and a social entrepreneur, Dr. Kumar started the K-foundation which contributes to empowerment of women and child education related programs. He has offered scholarships, grants, and free education to students worldwide. He aspires to fulfill his dreams of providing affordable education, evergreen careers and vision to thousands globally.

Dr. Shaan Kumar’s Specialties:

Mergers & Acquisitions, Creating Innovative Business Ideas and Plans, Investing & Mentoring.

SK established global alliances with IT giants including Microsoft, IBM, Sun and established more than 150+ franchise centers and certified more than 200,000 IT professionals in 4-5 years worldwide.

  • Founder, Co-Chairman & CEO

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    January 2003 — Present (7 years 10 months)

    In 1996, Dr. Kumar established an Education Network known as EdNET, a network of IT education, training and certification centers worldwide with franchise centers in many countries including the U.S, Canada, U.K., Norway, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia, New Zealand, and India. EdNET was supported by Dr. Simon Sim and Dr. B Singh. Between 1998 and 2000, EdNET established educational partnership alliances with Microsoft, IBM, Sun Microsystems, Lotus and CIW to offer Information Technology Certifications, Internet Software Services Training and Certification.     In 2003, California InterContinental University was incorporated in the State of California and began to operate in the city of West Covina. Its initial founding members were Dr. Michael Zullo, Dr. Richard Allen and Mr. Larry Golda under the leadership of Dr. Kumar, Co-Chairman and CEO. CalUniversity began to operate as a Distance Education and Learning (DEL) University. Its educational programs were developed by Benchmark Online (a Division of iGLOBAL) who assigned the rights of program curricula and degree to CalUniversity. Benchmark Online continued to provide e-learning platform solutions, instructional design and e-learning content development services to CalUniversity in the earliest stages of the university’s formation.

    In 2005, CalUniversity received California State Approval to grant undergraduate and graduate Degrees and has been fully operational since that time. Dr. S Kumar and Dr. Finian Tan, together as founder and co-founder of the University, have been funding its growth jointly through iGlobal and Vickers Financial Group. It has been their passion to build a truly global Online University.
