Keith Knudsen’s Summary

I have enjoyed a very diverse and challenging professional career. Being people oriented I began my career working with troubled high school students as a Special Education Instructional Assistant in the Bay Area. My work experience then centered on production control work and warehouse management in the bio-medical industry. As I grew as an individual and gained experience, I realized my natural abilities and tendencies were better suited for a career in sales and marketing. After twenty years of gaining this experience, my main goal has become to join a group of talented, driven people I call friends, and create and grow our own company.

I am now realizing that goal with Renegade Labs Inc.
