Michael Potter, Esq.’s Summary

MICHAEL POTTER, Esq. is a national speaker, fellow life traveler, author, humorist, serial entrepreneur, loving and supportive dad, zen outdoorsman, Scout leader and attorney whose books, webinars and live workshops are an uplifting and engaging mix of humor, imagination, energy, inspiration and practical knowledge.

Michael knows that 'who-you-are' is not defined by 'what-you-do' for a living, and so he is on a mission to help 1,000,000 people build a legacy and achieve their goals through Integrated Planning based on their 'Core-Values' .
