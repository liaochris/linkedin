TRUE art’s Summary

Highly creative and detail-oriented Graphic Artist with 15+ years experience in traditional and new media; 4+ years specifically in gaming. Exceptional collaborative and interpersonal skills; dynamic team player / team leader with well-honed written and verbal communication abilities. Passionate creator of fun and engaging characters and content; accustomed to performing in deadline-driven environments. Creative experience in Illustration; 2D Pixel / Sprite Animation; Design (Character; Graphic, Icon, Logo, Typeface; UI); Art Direction; and Production, utilizing primarily Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Flash, and asset management systems on both Mac and PC. Seeking immediate full-time position; also interested in freelance work.

Freelance clients include Adobe, Barnes & Noble, BET, Clairol, Clear Channel, Dannon, Disney, HBO, Latina, MGM, Microsoft, Ms., NBA, Nickelodeon, PBS, Scholastic, Simon & Schuster, Sony Music, The Source, Time Warner, USA Networks, Vibe, Yahoo!, and many others.

Recognized internationally as among "the design world's A-List" (HOW Magazine), one of the world's "leading design innovators" (International Design Magazine), and a "rising star of the new millennium" (Metropolis Magazine).

Taught and / or spoken on Art, Design, and New Media at institutions such as The Cooper Union, The New School / Parsons School of Design, Pratt Institute, Rhode Island School of Design, Sarah Lawrence College, and others.
