Scott Penberthy’s Summary

We build apps.

My teams dwell in media-rich applications for the iPhone and Web. We use cloud platforms to handle tremendous, dynamic loads from millions of avid consumers.

We believe in agile development, where results can be seen in the very first week of an engagement, optimizing the transformation of capital into working code.

We utilize open source and follow the mantra of authenticity, transparency, and involvement with elegant, crisp design.

I've personally led engineering teams for an online video destination site with 5M consumers, an online photo sharing site with 50M consumers, an invitation-only e-commerce company with 1M members, and world famous sporting events at IBM.
