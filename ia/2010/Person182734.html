Sarah Mae Hoover’s Summary

I write.

I write at my personal blog likeawarmcupofcoffee.com which is geared towards women who are seeking to find the eternal value in who they are and in all they do. I am launching two new blogs this year: getasponsor.net and raisinghomemakers.com. I also contribute to the DaySpring (division of Hallmark) blog, (in)courage.

I am the founder and co-host of the Christian women's blogging conference, Relevant.

The Relevant Conference exists to offer a place of face to face interaction with Christian women bloggers who want to be relevant in the larger blog and social media world.

I love helping women grow their blogs in order to get their message/products to a larger audience.

I'd love to be of service to you.

If you're looking for help in growing your blog, I'd love to chat with you. I can help you in the areas of creating content that people will read, gaining an audience, defining and enhancing your brand, strategic placement of visuals, and other facets that are specifically tailored to your website.

Perhaps you're looking for a meaningful way to connect your business with mom bloggers for outreach or partnership - I'd love to help you make that connection.

My full-time "job" is as a wife, mama, homemaker and homeschool teacher to my three small babes.
