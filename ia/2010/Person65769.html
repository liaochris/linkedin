Eric Kleiman’s Summary

Twenty plus years maintaining, expanding and managing ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems in production environments including Internal & External audits, Quality System Documentation, Continual Improvement, Root Cause Analysis, Employee Quality Training, Production and Product Data Collection and Analysis, Nonconformance Trend Analysis.

Over 16 years applying Chemistry background in Quality Control laboratory analysis of raw materials, intermediate production, and finished goods for companies involved in the manufacture of medical diagnostic products, adhesives & sealants and plastic products. Instrument audits, customer complaint analysis and customer interface, research & development, participated in numerous product/process development teams.

Four plus years purchasing bulk and specialty chemicals and Thermoformable plastic sheet. Material contract negotiations, developing strategic supplier relationships, supplier/material evaluation and approval.
