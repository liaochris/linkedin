Julio Varela’s Summary

Born and raised in Puerto Rico, graduated from the University of Puerto Rico. Presently living in Orlando, Florida with wife Margarita. Over 40 years of International & National Sales/Marketing/Operations experience with start-ups and helping established companies, mostly with Direct Sales/Network Marketing challenges. Ample experience as consultant, corporate officer and/or independent distributor for network marketing companies that experienced sizable growth when implementing programs developed during his tenure: Unilife, United Sciences of America, Network 2000 (Sprint), Puerto Rico Telecom, & LifeNet International, just to name a few. Fully bilingual in Spanish & English with an intrinsic, from the heart ability in developing, training & motivating employees and independent distributors. Highly specialized in helping companies through all levels of development & growth. Actively involved in providing products & services through Internet technologies to Hispanic markets in North and South America. Access to high-level contacts throughout Latin America, Caribbean, Europe, Asia, and North America.
