Al Simon’s Summary

I’m a Tai Chi and Qigong master with 30 years experience, and a three-time inductee into the U.S. Martial Arts Hall of Fame.

Today, I focus on teaching Tai Chi and Qigong online (yes, online!) for health, stress relief, and Chi development. I have about 2000 students in my online program, with about 65 students in my personal coaching and certification program.
