Anand Thaker’s Summary

My professional experience and commitment towards work are surpassed only by my enthusiasm to effectively meet the challenges before me. As a final year Masters student at Columbia University, I am seeking new and stimulating opportunities with an organization .

A highlight of my management and leadership skills was demonstrated by my success as a planning engineer at Cipriani Harrison Valves Private Limited. I was embarked upon with the responsibility of planning the production, assembly and timely delivery of sanitary stainless steel valves to Cipriani Corp. USA. It also included managing a state of the art manufacturing workshop consisting of 12 multi-axis fully automatic CNC machines, a QC Lab and over forty personnel.
exceeded an established department productivity goal of 60% and raised the bar to 75% for the following year. My effort was greatly appreciated by the Managing Director and other senior managers.

One of my most valuable contributions to the company was to develop a new design for a Plug Valve, replacing the metal plug of the valve by USFDA approved rubber plug. Though a very unconventional solution it reduced the assembly times by 70% and the overall cost by 30%. I also played a key role in convincing our customer to adopt the new design, which later was readily accepted in the commercial market and increased the revenue of the company by about 10% in that fiscal year.

Prior to that I have worked as a Trainee Engineer in India’s largest construction company L&T (Process Engineering Division) wherein I was rotated in the Project engineering, Process Engineering and Supply Chain Management divisions of the company. I gained considerable knowledge about the Process industry while preparing the Layouts and Piping & Instrumentation diagrams. I also enhanced my MS office skills while budgeting and costing for potential projects, preparing presentations for my superiors, creating databases etc.
