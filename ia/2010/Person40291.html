Chuck Lapinsky’s Summary

I manage the installation, implementation, training and technical support program for a nationwide remote Print On Demand client. We utilize Roland printers and cutters at all sites.

I have 30+ years in the printing industry in offset, screen and digital printing technologies.

I am also a freelance photographer/photojournalist, and shoot throughout the US and Canada as my travels dictate. Commercial, corporate, product, creative and special event photography. I am available for corporate and personal assignments in the San Diego area. Nikon professional. NAPP member.
I can be reached at: chuck.lapinsky@gmail.com

Chuck Lapinsky’s Specialties:

Certified on Roland, Mutoh, and Epson large format inkjet printers, OYO thermal printers, Wasatch, Versaworks and Harlequin RIP software.
Experienced on Windows and Mac platforms.
