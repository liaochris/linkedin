Melissa Ward’s Summary

Is your web site helping you?

In today’s world developing a web site can seem a daunting task. Most people have a lot of questions... I’ve got your answers….

At NewWard Development, LLC™ our goal is to make that seemingly insurmountable pile of tasks in to a smooth, pleasant experience.

We design custom web sites and develop applications and databases from scratch. We can also customize Wordpress, Joomla and Mambo for blogs and CMS sites.

My job at the initial meeting is to discover who the client wants to talk to and what they want to say. Sometimes, I have to help clients sort through that.

After that initial assessment, I come up with a task list and a proposal. It’s not really about designing a web site; it’s about getting a message out. The goal is to create an emotional response in the person who visits your web site. The marketing and design pieces are handled by me. Bob takes all the application development and database work (although he does come up with some pretty funky marketing ideas).

I am known as a subject matter expert in the field of Social Media Marketing. I train businesses how to use Social Media Sites (Facebook, Linkedin, Twitter, YouTube, etc), to get into the “conversation” with the right audiance. I also create and implement Social Media Marketing Strategies for my clients. I can often be found at the front of a room giving a presentation or hands on training session in one of many areas of Social Media Marketing. I love talking about Social Media Marketing; my presentations are enthusiastic and informative – loaded with successful philosophies and facts (and fun). I see Social Media Marketing as a melding of the art and science of Marketing.

At NewWard Development, LLC™ it is our job to Make Your Website Your Best Employee.
