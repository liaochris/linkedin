Jeff Schreiber (1,000+ LinkedIn Connections)’s Summary

Accomplished Sales and Internet Marketing Executive with more than 20 years of professional experience building and leading high-caliber sales and marketing teams.

Jeff Schreiber is presently Vice President of IGD Solutions in Clarkston and Brighton, Michigan (your connection for websites that produce ROI). His main areas of responsibilities are sales, business development and revenue generation. He was made a partner in IGD Solutions in June of 2007 while maintaining his office in Brighton, Michigan. Jeff has been in the high technology industry for over 20 years and has held major sales management positions with AT&T, Novell, Quantum, Compaq Computer Corporation and Allot Communications. He has traveled internationally during his career.

Jeff has been an active member of the communities in which he and his family have resided. In Naperville, Illinois he was a long-term member of the Naperville Jaycees where he served as Vice President of that organization from 1986 – 1987. That same year, Jeff was elected “Key Man of the Year” with the Naperville Jaycees. He was appointed by the Naperville, Illinois City Council to serve on the Naperville Zoning Board of Appeals, where he served for six full years.

Presently, Jeff is a member of the Livingston Sunrise Rotary Club in Brighton, Michigan. He was named to "Who’s Who in America" in 1994. Presently, Jeff is currently a member of the Brighton Chamber of Commerce, Founding Sponsor of the Martin Luther King, Jr. National Memorial in Washington, D.C., member of the Livingston (Brighton, Michigan) Diversity Council.

Jeff received his Bachelor of Arts Degree in English from Wayne State University in Detroit, Michigan in 1975 where he was elected to Phi Beta Kappa. He received his Juris Doctor Degree from The College of William and Mary in Williamsburg, Virginia in 1978. Jeff resides in Brighton, Michigan along with his son Joe. His company, IGD Solutions, is headquartered in Clarkston, Michigan.
