Jeffrey Thrall’s Summary

Jeff brings extensive experience and expertise in the areas of telecommunications, information technology, and interactive communications. He spent the last fifteen years working in a variety of roles as both a purchaser and provider of technology based solutions to the Fortune 1000.
Jeff’s views on technology can be summed up in a single word: solutions. He views technology as a set of tools for solving business and communication challenges. Jeff’s approach to technology is rooted in solid business ideals gained through his diverse experience. This approach ensures the solutions Jeff develops are practical and in support of true business needs.
Working with his clients, he identifies specific challenges that can be solved through the effective implementation of technology. He believes that a project can only be considered a true success when positively measured in meaningful results.
Jeff led the development of a large number of solutions for such notable names as: DuPont, Honda, Purdue Pharma, Volvo, Eastman Kodak, Xerox, Pitney Bowes, LaQuinta Hotels, Nickelodeon, and many more.
Jeff passionately embraces the information economy that exists today and believes that the power and immediacy of digital technologies to help in rapidly and effectively developing business efficiencies and effective communications solutions are nothing short of revolutionary. Applying these tools from an astute business manager’s perspective, he is able to help his clients quickly adapt and excel in an increasingly demanding business environment.

Jeffrey Thrall’s Specialties:

Technology Solutions Specialist focusing on consultative approach to solving complex challenges in large enterprise environments with the application of technology.
