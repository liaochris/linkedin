Cameron Vokey’s Summary

Generally focusing in mixed media and performance. Theatrical manager with background in collaborative ensemble and experience in production managing five mainstage college productions. Playwright interested in dark comedy, the absurd and the grotesque and have staged three original works. Installation artist and designer looking at what theatrical elements can bring to another art form. Video and sound designer interested in creating designs that don't just support a performance, but are integral to the stage action and the narrative. Currently experimenting in collaborative writing while simultaneously developing realized design and technical devices. Very excited about art and technology within performance and continuing to develop an aesthetic in interdisciplinary art.
