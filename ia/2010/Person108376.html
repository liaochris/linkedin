Julie Gander’s Summary

Carlson Wagonlit Travel (CWT) is a global leader specialized in business travel, group travel, events & incentives, as well as the analysing of business travel patterns and travel management. With offices in nearly 150 countries and territories, CWT assists her clients around the world. With our new leisure service, CWT Préférence – Your Personal Travel Service we can not only assist you in arranging your business travel in every detail, but also your leisure travel.

With CWT Préférence, we tailor to those clients that demand the very best in service and privacy worldwide, as well as those clients that have very little time available to plan and organise their leisure travel. Our clients have an exceptional selection of all upmarket leisure on offer within the Dutch market. We offer top-end accommodations and destinations worldwide and ‘once in a lifetime’ unique experiences. Above all, we offer high quality.

The travel on offer is based on 4-6* hotels and resorts worldwide, includes private islands, planes and villa’s, is selected with the utmost care and is delivered in combination with a high level of personal service. If you book your leisure travel with CWT Préférence, you will have a ‘Personal Travel Counselor’ (PTC) assisting you at all times. Our travel experts have many years of experience and posses extensive knowledge of upmarket tourism destinations worldwide.

As part of our service, your PTC'er will visit you to discuss your vacation, entirely at your convenience. At your office or in the comfort of your home, during the day or in the evening! Once in discussion with your PTC'er, you will find the ‘sky really is the limit’. You only need to indicate where you have set your travel heart on! Then according to your wishes your PTC'er shall return with a custom made travel proposal so that you can go entirely satisfied on your travel.

This combination is the unprecedented strength of CWT Préférence.
