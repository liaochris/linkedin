Aaron Craig’s Summary

For the past eight years Aaron has done a variety of work in branding, marketing, communications, public relations and market research, specifically in the life sciences and medical device industry and marketing FDA regulated products.

Aaron has a true passion for writing and has authored content for a variety of communication vehicles, including the Web, press releases, style guides, communications plans and marketing collateral. Regardless of what he writes content for, Aaron can always rely on his basic fundamentals of writing.

Aaron is a self-starter who takes a multi-faceted approach to marketing and PR by working with many different departments to ensure that all areas have been represented. He then promotes the key message decided upon and promotes the messaging through a variety of communication vehicles including, news releases, print and electronic ads, e-newsletters, digital signage, flyers and social media. Aaron is able to successfully launch these programs on-time and is able to keep everyone motivated while working on the project.

Aaron has successfully launched and implemented a lead generation program that allowed a company to obtain a better understanding of the market need, and increased leads for a product line by 30% in one quarter.

Aaron is a social media enthusiast, and has more than 10 years of experience in writing, editing and designing marketing collateral for both print and online. I also am very experienced with Web site marketing which includes search engine optimization (SEO).

The 30,000 foot view of Aaron's online marketing strategy adheres to these tactics:
• Use your Web site as the hub of a marketing/PR campaign
• Leverage social media, print materials, press releases, company's blog, etc.
• Use these networks to direct consumers to your site so you can get your message across
