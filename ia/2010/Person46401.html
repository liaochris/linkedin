Dana Wagner’s Summary

I've spent the last ten years working my way up the marketing ladder, with a passion for word-of-mouth promotion since I was creating and distributing promo CDs of my favorite independent musicians in college. I like to think of that period of time in my life as the root of my current fascination with social media marketing. Now I'm a digital marketing professional who has spent my career focusing on customer retention and engagement strategy for multiple, diverse brands across entertainment, media, retail and direct marketing industries. I have a strong rrapport with my clients, delivering marketing solutions that have helped enhance their brand identity, increase conversion and retention rates on their websites and improve open, clickthrough and conversion rates of their e-mail campaigns.

While the majority of my experience has been supporting e-commerce business units, I frequently welcome the opportunity to apply my knowledge to product, brand and community marketing initiatives whenever I can.

I'm strategic-thinking, consumer-focused, brand-conscious and highly attentive to user interface detail. I have a passion for innovation, collaboration, outstanding user experience, cross-platform consumer engagement, and fun and edgy brands that embrace all of the above. I value authenticity and transparency in marketing and in my life.

I'm currently immersing myself in digital technologies that are changing the way people interact with one another in both virtual and physical environments. I blog, tweet, share what's on my mind, and check-in as frequently as possible.

In five years I want to be doing something that doesn't exist today.
