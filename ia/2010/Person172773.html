Rich Hadden’s Summary

I have taken several courses in the field of multifamily apartment investing. I've listened to entrepreneurs such as Bill Bronchick, Alan Cowgill, Scott Scheel, Bob Diamond, DC Fawcett, Dwan Bent-Twyford, Wendy Patton and many others. The two that I have put my faith in are David Lindahl and Lance Edwards. I've taken the Apartment House Riches course from Dave Lindahl and am in the process of completing the Keys to the Vault - Raising Private Money success course from Lance Edwards.

Through the education and experience I've obtained from these 2 gentleman, I've acquired the confidence to locate, analyze and fund big ticket apartment deals. I've analyzed hundreds of deals and am working with many talented brokers to make this vision a reality.

I am also a Luminis (Web Portal) System Administrator at Wright State University along with a great friend of mine, Denise Anderson. Together we are tackling all of the issues presented by this system. I am also part of a team that manages the ERP system (Sungard's Banner) at WSU. I am responsible for installing all upgrades and patches to the ERP system as well as troubleshoot any desktop issues related to the ERP. There are also approximately 10-15 systems for which I am the primary or secondary administrator / support contact.
