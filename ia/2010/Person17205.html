Andrew Scholnick’s Summary

Since 1980, Mr. Scholnick has had extensive experience managing computer operations and developing applications software systems. He has been in the forefront during the evolution of the Internet and was a principal contributor to AOL’s internet infrastructure. Mr. Scholnick has excellent managerial, communications, teaching, programming, and people skills tempered with a realistic business perspective. He possesses a well developed understanding of computer and network security, facilities operation and management and also has a firm grasp of the software product development life cycle, proposal writing, UNIX systems, and large-scale DBMS design. Mr. Scholnick has managed computer operations and, hands-on, the development of numerous applications for the consumer market, security, and communications arenas. He is expert at system and application levels, as well as with a wide variety of computer and networking equipment.
