Deidre Siegel’s Summary

Human Resource Management Company with core competencies in: employee acquisition, employee retention, comprehensive human resource compliance audits, outsourced human resource management, behavioral assessments on individuals and at the team level.

PEAR also offers job seekers career counseling services for job seekers in all fields at all levels, including resume critique and development as well as mock interviews.

PEAR has the ability to do as little, or as much, as needed for companies that require outsourced HR services. And, if you have an in-house HR department, we are a compliment and back-up to help your professionals with assistance when they require extra hands.
