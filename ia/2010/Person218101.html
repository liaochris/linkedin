Yvette Ervin’s Summary

From globetrotting around the world as a model and spokesperson to starting and running businesses inside and outside the U.S., my international experience has brought me the tools I need to run a global cosmetics company and a Beauty Full of Color; an image consultancy group.

Results driven cosmetics and fashion industry veteran with over 20 years experience and a track record of passionate visionary work. A proven business leader with a pragmatic understanding of the factors that influence success or failure. Demonstrated track record in providing a skilled approach to operations, communications, and process.

Highly skilled in developing merchandise strategies including hands-on buying, sales and distribution experience. Clear understanding of the economics of retail and wholesale environments. Seasoned professional who exudes integrity in business management and client relations. My background provides a powerful combination of talent and experience that is unique and unmatched in the industry.

I am currently leading design, research, package design and product creation for a new cosmetics line launching globally in 2009.
