Steve Cadigan’s Summary

Working in the HR field for over 20 years I have practiced HR across Asia, Canada and the US. I have lived outside the US for over 10 years. I have worked in the fashion(wholesale & retail), insurance(service), semi-conductor, networking and entertainment (games) industries. I have led WW HR efforts on over 60 M&A transactions in 3 large Global organizations since 1998.

My overall objective is to make individuals, teams and organizations the best they can be.
