Paul McDonald’s Summary

In less than two years I was promoted to a Account Recruiting Manager and Trainer. That was all with Aerotek. In January 2008 I moved to Dollar General as a Senior Field Recruiter. I have the luxury of being the Recruiting Coordinator here, and it is my job to help us continue to grow rapidly. I am excited about the road ahead here with all of our growth and unlimited potential.
