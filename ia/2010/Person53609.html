Deborah Harrison’s Summary

Senior communications manager with experience in Web site writing, editing and content management. Wide-ranging skills in public affairs and media relations for leading trade associations. As a journalist, covered business and entrepreneurial topics at daily newspapers, including The Wall Street Journal. Improved media monitoring system at NPR (National Public Radio). Proven strengths in launching new projects and managing newsletters and e-publications targeted to corporate executives.

Deborah Harrison’s Specialties:

Web site readability and reader enhancement, e-newsletter development and management of publications and other communications projects. Photography and branding are added strengths.
