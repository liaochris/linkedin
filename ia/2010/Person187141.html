Shalini Bahl’s Summary

Researcher & Consultant:

I combine my entrepreneurial and academic experience (PhD in marketing) to inspire and guide people to live their highest potential by providing marketing, social media, and mindfulness skills.

My services include marketing consultancy and workshops in the fields of mindful branding, authentic leadership, and innovative thinking. All my services are based on in-depth research and geared to helping clients build a unique positioning, authentic brands, and sincere relationships with their customers.

Also provide one-on-one or corporate training to use social media with a special focus on Facebook, Twitter, Blogs, and LinkedIn.

Professional Speaker:

I have been invited to speak across a wide spectrum of organizations including international corporations in the Middle East, American colleges, non-profits, business networking events, and the Massachusetts Small Business Development Corporation. I speak in areas of my passion - using marketing and social media in mindful and authentic ways to promote collective well being of all stakeholders.

Author

I have authored many research articles published in top marketing journals like Journal of Consumer Research, Journal of Public Policy & Marketing, and Journal of Advertising.

TV Show Host and Producer

My new TV show, AWEtv, will broadcast conversations with authentic women entrepreneurs to speak to new ways of being in business that are joyful, creative, and sustainable. For more information please visit my website:
http://iam-bc.com/blog/awetv-coming-soon

Creator of Business Group for Women

I started the iAM Woman in Circle as a non judgmental forum of exchange for women with a purpose. This is a free resource for women to discuss innovative, authentic, and mindful marketing solutions. Meetings include multi-media presentations, group exercises, and discussions. For more information please visit the website:
http://iam-bc.com/events/iam-woman-circle

Shalini Bahl’s Experience

  • Founder and Principal Consultant

    IAM Business Consulting

    (Partnership; Management Consulting industry)

    October 2009 — Present (8 months)

    iAM Business Consulting is a natural outcome of my professional experience in marketing (both as a corporate executive and as an academic) and my personal journey as a meditator. I have successfully started and managed new enterprises in the business and non-profit sectors. As a marketing professor I enjoyed teaching undergraduate and PhD students and have published in top business journals. While I value the practical side of making things happen, I also enjoy thinking and integrating different ideas to understand how things work and find solutions to emerging business challenges.As a researcher and consultant I am working to find mindful ways of doing business such that it is profitable, purposeful, and makes a meaningful contribution to all stakeholders. And yet to be profitable and purposeful can require breaking away from old thinking patterns and finding new answers. Through my varied experiences in different professional and cultural environments, my academic training, and personal mindfulness practices I have developed the iAM approach - which stands for the Innovative, Authentic, and Mindful approach to business.
