Jose Catalini’s Summary

Jose has been developing his experience in several IT companies by successfully conducting software design, complex process reengineering (purchasing, drilling, HHRR), systems implementation and technology-based transformations projects, deployment of enterprise wide ECM and BPM initiatives.

He has high technical skills in ECM, ERP and BPM concerns (he has an Advisor Level in Enterprise Content Management) and in process improvement projects, together with experience in Bussiness Strategies and Human Resources models (competence maps, competence evaluation systems, training and educational programas, career).

He has developed an extensive background in managing people, planning product development roadmaps, strategy and bussiness development, optimization, cost / benefit analysis, with high organizational skills and leadership abilities. Results-oriented achiever with excellent track record for identifying opportunities for improvements

He is well recognized as a leader with reputation for building highly motivated and multidiscipline work teams (including multicultural environments).
