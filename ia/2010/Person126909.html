  • Principal

    Green Technologies, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    January 1998 — Present (13 years )

    In 1998, Green Technologies began operations in the state of Maine, USA. The corporation was moved to Virgina in 2004 and began operations in 2005. Green Technologies is a female owned company certified by the Virginia DMBE and is a WBE certified by the Women's Business Enterprise National Council (WBENC) out of the Washington, D.C. office. Our specialty IT consultants are Project Managers, Program Managers, Team Leaders, staffing teams for small to medium size projects, and staff augmentation resources. Our non-IT consultants are in the energy, nuclear, utilities, and semiconductor industries; Project Controls, Schedulers, Estimators, Proposal Writers, EVMS, and Engineers. Our goal is to have personnel with prior Primavera, DOE (Department of Energy), and Nuclear Trained Navy experience. We use every resource available to us in order to locate and place the absolute "best in the business". We have received many referrals from contractors who have worked with us and from people in the business whom we trust. Years of experience working in the corporate environment provides an advantage in locating talented individuals, understanding their capabilities, and being able to relate to their needs. Our goal is to build positive, effective, and rewarding working relationships. Green Technologies provides services to our direct clients and subcontracting clients; we continue to look for teaming opportunities with other companies in the region. Please visit our website at www.green-it-tech.com.
