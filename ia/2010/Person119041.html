Kristian Bjørnard’s Summary

My experience in the field of web and print design has been quite varied. I have worked in various capacities as design staff at small studios and with a staff of my own. I am well versed in web-standards, XHTML, CSS and understand the underpinnings of PHP/MySQL style websites. In the realm of print work I can vectorize and rasterize with the best of them. I am capable of letterpress printing, many hours have been spent perusing typography tombs, and could even help consult on a sustainable project or two... only let's not call it sustainable, let's just call it good design.

Kristian Bjørnard’s Specialties:

CSS, XHTML, Drupal; Adobe CS - Acrobat, Dreamweaver, Flash, Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop; PrePress; Brush and Canvas, Pen and Paper, Photography; Vandercooks & Chandler & Price printing equipment; Sustainable / green / vernacular design
