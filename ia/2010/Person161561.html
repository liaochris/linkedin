Paul Williams’s Summary

I have been in the full service research industry for over 20 years, and having been MD of a large UK agency, I have now joined forces with EMS Research.

EMS Research, Europe's leading in-house fieldwork company, offers its clients a centrally owned and operated fieldwork resource across Europe, including:
* Offices in London, Paris and Berlin
* 160 call-centre seats in London and Berlin
* Dedicated European recruitment force
* Team of multicultural, multilingual project managers covering all of Europe and beyond
* 6 viewing facilities
* Vast, internally-built respondent databases covering more than 150 medical professions and 42 ailments

Being a one-stop-shop provider means that we do not rely on outside resources in carrying out pan-European fieldwork. As a subcontractor to many research companies, serving the most demanding brands in many industries, we can guarantee absolute quality and transparency for our clients, including 100% traceability on respondents and 100% veracity of fieldwork.

Our in-house set-up means that you can rely on us to shorten field times and reduce costs without compromising quality.
