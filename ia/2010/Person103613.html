At Cambridge Appleton Trust we offer full-service Trust & Wealth Management solutions solutions to affluent families, executives, business owners, nonprofit institutions and foundations. We are deeply committed to developing long-lasting personal relationships with our clients and their tax and/or legal advisors. Our seasoned professionals provide customized investment and estate planning strategies to meet the ever-changing needs of our clients.

Cambridge Appleton Trust is nationally chartered and located in Boston, MA. The company was formed in 1999 as a joint venture between Appleton Partners, a $3.9 billion private investment firm and Cambridge Savings Bank, a $2.1 billion mutual bank headquartered in Cambridge, MA.

Our comprehensive wealth management and unbiased, holistic approach focuses on the overall needs of the family and their legacy. The process begins with in-depth client discussions about long-term objectives, concerns and wishes. We try to understand the family dynamics and tax situations before making or implementing any investment or advisory recommendations. Our commitment to client service and high-quality solutions have allowed us to maintain a 97% retention rate.

The Investment Philosophy at Cambridge Appleton Trust is a combination of allocating assets across different investment strategies and picking the 'best of breed" managers after a tedious due diligence process. Our Investment models are continually evolving as we conduct micro and macro economic research to reflect changing global market condiations.
