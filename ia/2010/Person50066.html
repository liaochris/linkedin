David A. Amdur’s Summary

Through experience in curriculum planning, teaching, writing, delivering presentations and conducting training, I have honed skills in researching diverse domains of knowledge, determining essential learning outcomes, and designing engaging instruction and accurate assessments. I have regularly facilitated diverse individuals and cooperative teams in performing complex tasks in fast paced environments. In addition, I am adept in learning and teaching software applications and offer expertise in visual communication.
