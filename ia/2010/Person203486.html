Tiffany Ho’s Summary

Tiffany is a sophomore at Yale University majoring in Sociology with a focus on community dynamics. Tiffany currently manages and is a co-founder of ReadySetLaunch, a nonprofit organization that provides free college counseling to low-income students. Tiffany's interests include meeting new people and exploring foreign cultures and food. She hopes to eventually pursue a career in corporate social responsibility and become a social entrepreneur.

Tiffany Ho’s Specialties:

Experience in concept development, marketing and branding, strategic management, and team building.
