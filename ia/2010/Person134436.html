Mark Robertson’s Summary

Mark Robertson is founder of ReelSEO.com, a first-of-its-kind website for the online video marketing space offering both valuable and objective insight into news, tips, trends, and overall guidance for those wishing to leverage video marketing, social media, and search engine optimization.

As a passionate and experienced search engine marketing professional. Mark is a popular speaker, consultant and passionate thought-leader within the online video and search engine marketing industries. Mark has had the honor of speaking at several high-profile industry conferences (Search Engine Strategies, PubCon, Video Commerce Summit, Streaming Media West, and more) in addition to leading numerous seminars nationwide on the power of search engine marketing and online video.
