Stephen Hand’s Summary

Stephen Hand focuses on the Small Business market, providing comprehensive legal and Identity Theft Protection across North America. As a former small business owner himself, he appreciates the value of these services and works to ensure each and every client gets optimum value from the wraparound coverage provided.
Steve wants to empower companies and individuals to control their own destinies and avoid being blindsided by less scrupulous competitors, regulators, customers, and so on. Additionally, he works with fellow entrepreneurs across the country to make the phrase “Equal Justice Under Law” a reality for everyone.
Individuals are also protected from the quickly growing crime of Identity Theft by enrolling in the protective service developed by Kroll Background America.
