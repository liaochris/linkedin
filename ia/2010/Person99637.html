J. Michael Cavitt’s Summary

I have been working in business development, organizational development and management consulting in the public, nonprofit and private sectors since 1972.

A management consultant with the University of Iowa working with top management in Iowa state government for 5 years.

An owner of an Investment Advising Firm for 18 years.

Goal: To assist volunteer leaders in nonprofits from
'start-up' to 'staff-up' who feel frustrated and unproductive because they aren't realizing their dreams. I assist them by helping them solve their problems and take advantage of their opportunities so that they achieve their dreams. Then, they feel fulfilled because they are contributing to a successful organization and are realizing their personal goals.

J. Michael Cavitt’s Specialties:

Specific solutions to problems which consistently plague nonprofits.

Knowledge, skills and abilities from over 30 years working as a consultant in management development, organizational development, and business development in small and large organizations in the public, nonprofit and private sectors.
