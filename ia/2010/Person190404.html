S. Jennifer Mannino’s Summary

Servicing a number of business sectors, Jennifer conducts background investigations on behalf of commercial lending institutions and alternative investment firms, and specializes in matters of international and domestic intelligence gathering. Jennifer also conducts research in matters concerning litigation support, including identifying PRPs and examining expert witnesses, inspecting acquiree executive management teams in M&A transactions, researching assets and locating witnesses, identifying conflicts of interest and vetting contestants of proxy fights, and investigating senior executives and lateral attorneys as part of the pre-hire screening process.

Jennifer is actively involved with the non-profit, 100 Women in Hedge Funds, serving as co-chair to its Gala Auction Committee in 2007, 2008 and 2009. Jennifer also served as a co-chair of the 2008 and 2009 Backpack Drives for High Water Women. Board appointments include on a parochial grammar school, local parish, and homeowner's association (180 units). Memberships include through the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners, ASIS International, Association of Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialists, Association for Corporate Growth (Arizona Chapter), the Phoenix Chamber of Commerce, and the Arizona Association of Law Libraries (AzALL).

Jennifer currently serves as the Chairperson for AzALL's Legal Research Institute and is a member of the AzALL Programs Committee.
