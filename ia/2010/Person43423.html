Corinne McKay’s Summary

Certified by the American Translators Association for French to English translation. Eight years' experience translating legal, international development and corporate communications documents for clients throughout the U.S. and Europe. Author of "How to Succeed as a Freelance Translator" (over 3,500 copies in print) and translator of "Dictionary of North American Indians and Other Indigenous Peoples." M.A. in French Literature, ATA certification, President of the Colorado Translators Association and Chair of the ATA Public Relations Committee.

Corinne McKay’s Specialties:

Discovery documents, intellectual property materials, contracts, shareholder agreements, real estate leases, internal corporate e-mails. International development funding applications, executive summaries and project progress reports. Press releases, company newsletters and promotional materials. Non-fiction books.
