Charles Taylor’s Summary

I am an executive IT professional who has spent the past 15 years working at all levels of Quality Leadership from a Lead Quality Liaison to VP of Quality Assurance. I have a proven track record of successfully managing quality programs at every level. My 27+ year IT employment history includes positions held in nearly all aspects of the SDLC including developer, designer, performance architect, product support, program/project management, release management, quality assurance and testing. Below are some key career achievements worth noting:

* Recognition: Winner of the 2007 Quality Leadership Executive (QLE) Visionary Award for the Southeast Region. The Executive Alliance recognizes outstanding executives and their organizations that have achieved measured success through the deployment of quality assurance applications and processes within software development.

* High Achiever: Recognized as a leader in Quality Assurance. Promoted 3 times in 8 years from Quality Assurance Manager to Quality Assurance Director, to Associate Vice President, to Vice President.

* Top Performer: Selected as a top management performer by the Executive Management team to participate in an “invitation-only” conference “Leading ChoicePoint 2005”.

* Highly Rated: Ranked in the top 10% of managers surveyed nationally in the Gallup Workplace Survey.

* Methodology: Implemented industry “best practices”, standards, and procedures at multiple organizations. Oversaw the implementation of an enterprise level program that successfully achieved ISO 9001 and TickIT certification within 9 months of inception.

* Change Management: Spearheaded the design and implementation of enterprise level automated Change Management System.

* Customer Service: Recognize by peers for a relational style of management that focuses on customer satisfaction, open communications, and interdepartmental cooperation and collaboration.

Charles Taylor’s Specialties:

My areas of expertise include the insurance industry, commercial and mortgage banking, warehousing, logistics, supply-chain, test automation, and industry best practices.
