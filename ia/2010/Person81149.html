Heidi Miller’s Summary

My passion is helping others build passionate communities online. Yes, I know I used "passion" twice; that's how strongly I feel about it! More powerful than having a blog, hosting a podcast, creating a Ning community, Twittering incessantly or zombie bites on Facebook is the true power of social media: to create active, engaged communities of real people speaking in their real voices about the things they care about.

In Cluetrain Manifesto, the authors say that one definition of community is "people who care about each other more than they have to." This is where the power of social media lies--in creating a space where your most passionate enthusiasts can participate actively and humanly, both with you and with each other.

I have spoken at Podcast and New Media Expo, the Graduate School of Business, various Chambers of Commerce, and the Chicago and Rocky Mountain Direct Marketing Associations as well as a smattering of local and national topic-specific conferences and teaching regular podcasting classes in Chicago.

I write on podcasting, Twitter and using social media to build community on my blog, http://talkitup.typepad.com, my podcast, http://heidimiller.libsyn.com and Twitter at www.twitter.com/heidimiller.

Clients include the John S. and James L. Knight Foundations, the Knight Pulse community, Spoken Communications, Africa10
