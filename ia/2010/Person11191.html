Andreas Jablonka’s Summary

Visual Effects Compositor with over 6+ years of compositing experience. Feature Film at 4K, 2K, HD, and SD working in Log, Linear, HD(Rec709), Genesis (Panalog) and nuke's SRGB colorspace. Music Videos and Commercials. Stereo experience.

Reliable and self motivated, team-oriented artist used to tight deadlines and wowing the client. Budget conscious and able to scale detail level and workflow standards.

Experience as a Compositing Supervisor, Lead compositor and Senior Artist. Mentored junior artists and thought compositing classes. Proven technical problem solver and established template and pipeline builder. Deep understanding of color & lighting, shadow and grain matching, camera lenses, tracking and integration tricks.

Lighting TD (Maya/Katana) experience as well as wealth of 3D Knowledge.

Often praised for the ability to explain a complex technical situation in clear terms.

- Developed Macros/Tools for comping Team at Rhythm & Hues to help junior compers in character integration.

- NUKE Gizmo/Pipeline development at Luma Pictures to achieve transitioning from Shake to Nuke Pipeline.

- Improved Maya to Nuke exchange work flow at Sony Imageworks.

Expressions and scripting skills in Nuke/Shake in python, ability to setup a template/pipeline/passes/workflow for studio productions.

21 feature films including academy award winners and nominees such as the Golden Compass, Pirates of the Carribean 2&3, Harry Potter and the half blood prince, the recent g-Force as well as upcoming blockbusters like Roland Emmerich's 2012 and Tim Burtons Alice in Wonderland.

Several commercials and music videos including Time Warner Cable (LAG) and Toyota (Venza).
