Brandon Green’s Summary

Enthusiast of all music-related topics; energetic creative with a business sense.

On the business end, killer community engagement & customer service rep with 2.5 years experience working for a blossoming small business in the music industry. Extensive work with reporting, analysis and systems management, plus a knack for problem solving and casual & professional writing, with additional experience with social media. Interested in artist/band promotion and the development of technology to aid in the struggle to "make it big." Notorious for efficiency.

On the musical end, alternative rocker with an appreciation for the avant-garde and electronic. I write and produce largely for my solo project, Taken By Name, as well as quite a bit for solo piano, small ensembles, and electroacoustic media. Amateur pianist of 15+ years, semi-professional bassist of 7+ years. 4 years experience in music production with a recent interest in mastering. I've composed for one film soundtrack, and will be producing an album with a local Boston artist later this year.
