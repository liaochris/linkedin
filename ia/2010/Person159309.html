Parrish Construction’s Summary

If the prospect of remodeling your home or office, or building a new home seems daunting, you need an experienced guide. You will enjoy the benefits of having an experienced team, efficient systems, and an unparalleled record of success that you can count on. Whether you have a simple handyman task or a substantial and complex design-build project, you will receive the same commitment to excellence.

If you need custom cabinets for your business or home, our in-house cabinet shop can design and create anything, from reception counters to elaborate home offices and libraries.

If you want the peace of mind of working with someone who is the best at what they do, we have the awards, recognition, and testimonials to set your mind at ease.

Our clients’ needs come first, so we make a point of listening to what you tell us. We say what we will do, and we do what we say. For over forty years, we have built our business on a solid foundation—client satisfaction. The majority of our projects are for previous clients or their friends and business associates, and they tell us they will build with us again!

Parrish Construction’s Specialties:

Since 1969, the Parrish family has been building beautiful homes, remodels, custom cabinets, and furniture.
We have a well-credentialed team (see Our Team). We were early adopters of CAD CAM design and the Design/Build option and are knowledgeable about Radon, Lead and fiberglass abatement. We’ve been Green Points certified since the very beginning of the program.

Parrish Construction’s Interests:

GREEN PLEDGE - As Remodelers, we are already using the greenest building method: We RE-USE! - Green option in Design/Estimation: We solicit green options from our sub-contractors on bids and advise you of them. - We are knowledgeable in the area of radon, asbestos and lead mitigation & removal. - We typically design with classic, long lasting materials, not trendy ones that are unproven. - We source local wherever we can; not only does this support our local economy; it reduces the shipping costs of having it brought here from afar. - We have a design sensibility concerning a smaller footprint. Good design makes additional square footage an option, rather than a necessity. - We recycle building products and have for 20 years. - We practice deconstruction rather than demolition. We are supporters of the ReSource yard.
