John Mullaly’s Summary

Business development executive with strengths in business strategy and early market development, focused on in-market execution with clients and business partners.

Experienced in all aspects of corporate entrepreneurship, including successes and failures in starting new businesses, in overseeing a portfolio of early stage businesses, and in implementing an innovation management system
to more effectively leverage new technologies and business models.

As a pioneer in early Internet user experience design, generated over 30 patents in areas related to software user interface, digital media, 3D web and electronic commerce. Helped develop IBM’s "on demand" strategy, and launched initial business development effort worldwide. Led IGS Market Development’s ‘Growth & Innovation Agenda.’ Founded and led a startup services network for IBM business partners serving SMB clients.
