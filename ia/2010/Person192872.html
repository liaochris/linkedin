Sanjay Singhal’s Summary

Offices & field representation in many countries worldwide including Africa, Middle East, Europe & Asia. www.sintelsat.com

Extensive international network and partnerships in more than 50 countries, two decades experience in business development, technology solutions provision, critical problem solving, negotiations, strategy, high end consulting. Advisor to several US domestic and off-shore telecom services, software and IT support businesses. Relationships extend from large multi-billion dollar organizations to SME with unique technology solutions. Working on projects in East Africa for infrastructure & connectivity solutions and in West Africa with humanitarian and social uplifting goals, providing employment to youths which have far reaching economic impacts in the West African region.
