Beth VanStory’s Summary

Insightful executive-level consultant with deep expertise in online media and consumer marketing.

Exceptional general manager with experience in retailing, entertainment, online media and telecom. Success driven by leveraging technology, employing consumer insight, and exercising financial rigor to achieve growth. Kellogg MBA.

Proven ability to deliver results and build companies and teams within startup and Fortune 1000 companies. Strong ability to understand and use technology to develop customer solutions and drive business in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
