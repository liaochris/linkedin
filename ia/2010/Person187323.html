Sorensen, Shane’s Summary

I have over 13 years experience working in the Oil and Gas Industry in various different positions. I have experience working with most of the major oil refinery processes and I have a strong operations background. I have worked numerous small refinery projects and refinery unit upgrades. I also have experience working major capital project. I worked on a heavy oil upgrader that was built in Venezuela and had the opportunity to work in Venezuela on two separate occasions. My most recent experience has been working in Heavy Oil Upgrading and Delayed Coking.

My career has focused on the technical side of the business although I have had opportunities to work in Operations Management on two different occasions. My future goals and interests are to move my career in the direction of leadership and management rather than purely technical work. I really like the work of Stephan R. Covey and his ideas of Leadership and Personal Management and I continue to read and study on my own that sort of material.
