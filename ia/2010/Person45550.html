Cyndee Todgham Cherniak’s Summary

International trade law (i.e., export controls and economic sanctions, free trade agreement negotiation and analysis, NAFTA dispute settlement, FTA dispute settlement, WTO dispute settlement, bilateral investment treaty analysis and planning and international investment arbitrations, anti-dumping, countervailing duties, safeguards, customs duties (tariff classifications, valuation, royalties, rules of origin, certificates of origin, verifications, appeals), excise duties, excise taxes, import controls, food safety, consumer product safety, food and drugs, permits for imports of cosmetics and other health products, non-tariff barriers, sanitary and phyto-sanitary measures, technical barriers to trade, national security and trade, anti-corruption, anti-money laundering, government procurement bid challenges), goods and services tax, Ontario retail sales tax, Ontario employer health tax, Ontario land transfer tax, drop shipments, tax audits, notices of objection, notices of appeal, sales tax litigation, sales tax planning, etc.

I am an adjunct law professor at case western Reserve University School of Law and teach a course on NAFTA and preferential trading arrangements. I am a consultant to the Asian Development Bank on preferential trading arrangements (customs unions, economic unions, free trade agreements, comprehensive economic partnership agreements, comprehensive economic cooperation agreements, trade preferences agreements, economic partnership agreements, etc.)

Cyndee Todgham Cherniak’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge of free trade agreements - an advisor or arbitrator in trade-related cases. Legal practice focuses on international trade law (i.e., export controls & economic sanctions, FTA negotiation & analysis, NAFTA & WTO dispute settlement, BIT / FIPA analysis & planning & arbitrations, anti-dumping & countervailing duties, safeguards, customs duties, import controls, food & consumer product safety, national security, anti-corruption & government procurement), GST & ORST.
