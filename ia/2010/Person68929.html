John Fallon’s Summary

I'm an educational presenter who concentrates on helping people with Presentation Skills development. As a Speaker / Presenter, I have presented at the following educational venues and for various companies: NFTE (National Foundation for Teaching Entrepreneurship), ASCD (Association for Superintendents and Curriculum Developers, HSTW (High Schools That Work), EBS (Educational and Business Summit of South Carolina and YESCarolina (Youth Entrepreneurship in South Carolina).

It doesn't matter what you know, what skills you have or what your intention is--- if you can't present yourself, you will always be selling yourself and your abilities short.

I can help you by:

1) helping you improve your delivery skills
2) helping you design a more effective message
3) helping you design a supporting visual presentation

Invite me to connect if you feel I can help you.
