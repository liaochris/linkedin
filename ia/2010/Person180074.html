Rythee Jones’s Summary

Higher Focus Leadership Development is an executive coaching and HR consulting practice dedicated to helping leaders discover their strengths, values, interests and passion so that they can create meaningful goals that will push them to their full potential to be phenomenal at what they do.

We work primarily with leaders in higher education, non-profit organizations and government agencies. Those who seek our services are at all organizational levels but we have a particular passion for working with those who are early in their career, rising leaders, those who find themselves in a leadership capacity without formal training, as well as mid-career professionals who want to proactively manage their careers.

Our coaching approach is to be a partner with leaders as they work on their goals and to be the often-missing catalyst that they need in order to make significant forward progress toward success.
