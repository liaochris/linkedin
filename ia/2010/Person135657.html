Martin Schecter’s Summary

Award-winning eBusiness strategist with over sixteen years experience launching over 200 groundbreaking destination websites, social networks, and Web2.0 interactive marketing efforts for Fortune 100 brands and startups. Published author, speaker, and educator who has launched several successful startup ventures. Effective team builder and leader / mentor with experience spearheading multi-million-dollar product development launches for public companies.

Currently, Director of eBusiness Development for Wiley Global Finance, developing digital strategies for John Wiley & Son's publishing programs globally in institutional finance, trading, corporate f&a, accounting, and personal finance.
