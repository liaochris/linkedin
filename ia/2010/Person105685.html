Josh Case’s Summary

As a Financial Advisor at Merrill Lynch I provide corporations and individuals with wealth management services and financing solutions.

For the previous 7 years I led M.J.M. Capital, Inc. the marketing and sales arm of DMP Services, LLC. I formulated and implemented a marketing plan that successfully covered internet, print, cold calling, networking, trade show, vendor and broker strategies. The results of which produced the funding of 698 transactions totaling over $52.3 million in financing in 39 states. These transactions produced over $4.7 million in profit margin.

The 4 years prior I led S.C. Associates, Inc. where I oversaw a 186% increase in sales during my time there. I also managed a $1.6 million expansion, implemented several cost cutting initiatives, managed 12 employees and over 50 independent contractors.
