Esme Vos’s Summary

I am currrently running:

- Pajama Entrepreneur: site for early stage startups around the world

- Muniwireless, the portal for news and information about citywide Wi-Fi and WiMAX networks. Muniwireless also covers wireless applications (especially those for iPhones and other Wi-Fi enabled devices) for consumers, governments and enterprises.

- Mapplr: boutique hotel and restaurant reviews worldwide
- Shopplr: shopping site for fashion, beauty, style, paper goods.

I am also marketing director of ShopGossipGirl.com, ShopMadmen.com and ShopGlee.com.

Esme Vos’s Specialties:

(2) Turning blogs into publishing enterprises: I turned Muniwireless.com from a blog to a publishing company.

(3) Social media consultant to companies that want to know how to generate leads via Linked In, Twitter, Facebook and blogs.

(4) Languages: English, German, French, Dutch, Tagalog.
