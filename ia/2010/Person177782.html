Rose Reyes’s Summary

Since I can remember fashion and design have always been a part of me. I always dream of having my own boutique. I thought it should have fashionable clothes, but unique at the same time. I imagine dressing all types of women and helping the find the right look for them. I envision working with up and coming designers and help them get established as well.

To achieve that, I graduated with an MBA in Human Resources in 2007 and BBA in Management in 2005. I kept my fashion cravings by modeling, learning to saw, and styling clients on the side. It wasn't until 2006 that I decided it to pursue my dream full-time. After two years of planning and making preparations. This September I was finally able to realize my dreams of opening unikapparel.com my own online boutique. In my boutique, I offered stylish apparel for women of all sizes, and I specialize in helping women find their own look. Our clothes are new, used or vintage. My fashion expertise has leaded me to successfully scope trends that are desirable for every style. Check us out at unikapparel.com
