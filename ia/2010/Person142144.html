Michael Bunes’s Summary

Objective:
I work with global companies to integrate systems and products to ensure lifecycle quality and project success. Use my skills to accurately define challenging problems and implement creative solutions.

Summary:
I specify products and develop testing procedures, resulting in deliverables that are intuitive and functional to the users. I ensure compliance between the Human Machine Interface (HMI) and planned procedures, as well as industry and regulatory guidelines. I define technical requirements for data management and control using plant-wide systems. I test the electronic interaction and integration requirements of third party and customer organizations. I've worked with three primary Distributed Control System (DCS) suppliers: Honeywell, Yokogawa and Emerson.

Nuclear Quality Assurance:
I verify documents and methods throughout all phases of the Nuclear Quality Assurance (QA) Software Life Cycle - Requirements Phase, Design Phase, Implementation Phase, Test Phase, Installation & Checkout Phase, Operation & Maintenance Phase, and the Retirement Phase.

Michael Bunes’s Specialties:
