Danielle Mai’s Summary

As Senior Designer and Principal of Banana Creative, my role is to design and deliver smart print and web-based marketing tools for start-ups, growing and established companies. Using my 15+ years of experience as a Graphic Designer and the expertise of my seasoned team of strategists, writers, developers and printers, we focus on creating recognition, respect and response in our Clients’ marketplace. Our specialty—doing more for less.

Outside of work, I enjoy blogging about Graphic Design and Marketing in print and on the web. My blog at www.bananacreative.com/blog allows me to share my knowledge and educate others about topics ranging from logo development to building website traffic. I am also an active member of CommArt Network, Contra Costa Graphic Design Professionals, Women in Consulting, and Print Buyers’ Group of Northern California. Volunteering for Tony LaRussa’s Animal Rescue Foundation is also close to my heart.

Selected Clients: Holiday Inn Express, Genentech, Irwin Home Equity, FrontRange Solutions, SafeAmerica Credit Union, Business Objects, E&J Gallo, and Fremont Bank.

Certified, Women-owned, Business Enterprise (WBE)
Certified, Bay Area Green Business
