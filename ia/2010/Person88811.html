Jamie Fox’s Summary

I have been in the fashion industry for about two and a half years and I absolutely feel at home. I have worked for Club Monaco, Cole Haan and BCBG Max Azria. I have also managed a contemporary fashion boutique marketed towards women ages teens, college, moms, and beyond. The name of the boutique is Beyond Pink.

I feel that working in a corporate setting gave me a lot of foundation and made me learn the format and standards of specialty stores along with interactions of consumers in high fashion retail. I loved managing a privately owned boutique where I can keep the standard of the customer relationship and the look of the store while giving it a relaxed and trendy edge. Now I am with White House Black Market a fun and very happy working environment where selling is our great focus. Gaining more knowledge in this market to a new demographic has been interesting and very helpful. I am lucky to have been invited into so many different stores to learn so much.

I am currently studying Marketing and Communications at Columbia College Chicago and have been educating myself in fashion through working. I am a senior and am aspiring to have a career in the fashion industry, whether it be in retail or my other love publishing. Working for any magazine selling advertising space or anything else is truly a dream. Nothing is better than sitting down with a magazine. I hope to help keep them the number one media for women.
