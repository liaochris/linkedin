Joe Lindley’s Experience

  • Principal Consultant

    Quality Forensic Solutions, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    April 2010 — Present (4 months)

    I provide Process Improvement consulting specifically for Software (SW) or Hardware (HW) Peer Reviews (Formal Inspections) or CMMI Process Performance Models.

    Conducting Peer Reviews to remove defects during Software or Hardware Product Development is a well-established process for ensuring quality and lowering cost. Projects often fail, however, to conduct Peer Reviews well enough or often enough. In addition, most projects can’t measure Peer Review performance (Return on Investment), so little is known of what needs correction or what to do about it.

    I can provide solutions for these problems in the form of analysis and corrective action suggestions for improving a company’s processes for and execution of SW or HW Peer Reviews. The goal for these improvements is a sustainable and high Return on Investment for Peer Review execution.

    High Maturity CMMI level 5 requires the development of Process Performance Models (PPMs). I have developed and patented PPMs and provide assistance in developing them for clients.
