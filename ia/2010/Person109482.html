Jim Venable’s Summary

Global marketing executive who’s background blends the classical marketing principles of iconic consumer mega brands with an entrepreneurial passion for launching new products, brands and programs.

Together, these deliver a track record of success, increased revenue, improved profits and accelerated expansion for Fortune 500, mid-sized and start up brands across a range of consumer products categories and commercialization of medical device technologies.

CORE COMPETENCIES

- Strategic Planning: Transform quantitative and qualitative data and hypotheses from concept to market launch. Creative visionary with unique ability to bring trends to life in creative design.

-Brand Strategy: Develop and grow brand positioning & awareness in an uncertain marketplace that evokes trust, differentiates between competition, and gains increased share of wallet.

-Traditional and Digital Media: Successful at evoking desired consumer behavior through strategic application of traditional and digital. Mediums: direct response, print, e-marketing, search, social networks, viral, and PR.

-Marketing Analytics and Business Acumen: Proven ability to analyze consumer, competition, marketplace dynamics to drive profitable business plans. Understand and implement interactions between all marketing system components for flawless execution and maximum growth.
