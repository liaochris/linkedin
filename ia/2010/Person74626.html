Gerald Combs’s Summary

I'm the original developer of Wireshark, the award-winning network protocol analyzer. I started the project in 1998 (under the name Ethereal) while working at an ISP. Since then many bright and talented people have contributed to the project. I work at CACE Technologies, which sponsors Wireshark and WinPcap.

In past lives I worked as a consultant for firms in a variety of industries, ranging from telecommunications to pharmaceuticals to finance. I've also been a system administrator, managing web sites for Apple, the Kansas City Chiefs, AT&T, Ditch Witch, and the Rolling Stones.

I have the good fortune to work with two amazing development teams: the guys at CACE Technologies (Pilot, AirPcap, TurboCap, and WinPcap), and as the lead developer of Wireshark.
