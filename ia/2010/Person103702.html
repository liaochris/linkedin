Jonathan Blum’s Summary

Jonathan Blum is the principal of Blumsday LLC, a web-based custom content company with a specialty in technology news. He has written for, been quoted by, or appeared on ABCNews, CNN, Entrepreneur Magazine, TheStreet.com, Outside Magazine, CNNMoney, CNBC, The Associated Press, and many more.

He and his company's content routinely generate above-average traffic and exposure. He also is co-host of The Sports Circuit, an online sports technology brand. (www.thesportscircuit.com)

Mr. Blum recently won a Best In Business award from the Society of American Business Editors and Writers for his blog, www.blumsday.com. He passed the first level of the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) designation.

He is married and lives in Harrison, NY.
