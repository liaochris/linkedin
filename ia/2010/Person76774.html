Gordon Dymowski’s Summary

Everyone has a cause - a mission that drives who they are, what they do, and how they engage others who share the same passions. In my work, I strive to help people build communities - online and offline - one cause at a time through traditional channels (networking, community building, special events) and newer ways of communicating like social media). With over ten years' leadership experience in the non-profit arena, I help corporate clients and non-profits gain profit by outperforming their competitors and strengthening key messages.

Past work includes:

*Building and running Facebook page to outreach to college prep alumni to drive awareness and engagement around upcoming reunion and to build alumni-funded endowment
*coordinating live luxury car reveal on Facebook, resulting in over 1,800 views
*passage of clean air ordinances in three communities in the Metropolitan St. Louis region
*Raising over $80,000 for various non-profits in the St. Louis region through grant/mini-grant funding, special event planning, and donor solicitation

Gordon Dymowski’s Specialties:

Social media outreach strategy/engagement, word-of-mouth marketing, social media metrics & measurement, data analysis, outcome-oriented program development & implementation, leadership & board development, word of mouth marketing, volunteer recruitment & retention, non-profit advocacy & legislative work, grant writing and administration. Also extensive experience with community mobilization, grant administration, and consultation on outcome-oriented program planning/execution.
