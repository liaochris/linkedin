Vladislav Solodkiy’s Summary

I am responsible for advertising, merchandising and product PR in Russian Standard Bank. I report to the first deputy chairman of the board and supervise over 21 employee (4 groups). On April, 2010 I won 2 prestigious awards «The best brand / EFFIE 2009» for advertising campaigns – "gold" for launch of the product «Bank in a pocket» and "bronze" for segment deposits advertising campaign.

I have some interesting strength and characteristics:
• 12-years work experience - 7-years experience in marketing in several areas of business – finance (Russian Standard Bank and Alfa-Bank), telecommunications (MTS), entertainment (work with FMCG-leaders) - allows to achieve synergy effect from mix of experience in different areas.
• High-quality education in strategic marketing (E-MBA, Stockholm School of Economics) and public administration (postgraduate study in the Moscow State University, magistracy of the St.Petersburg State University, grant from Moscow State Institute of Foreign Affairs) allows to look at a situation from different sides and on macro-level.
• Successful experience of team management and in working with big marketing budgets. Wide experience in work with regions and with advertising agencies.
• Focus on innovative kinds of marketing and distribution - including online-sales and mobile marketing.
• Distinctive personal characteristics: a fresh overview on problems, processes and products, the European approach to management and marketing, high level of communication skills and mobility, ability to see new possibilities and directions for development.
