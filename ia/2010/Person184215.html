Scott Moser’s Summary

Director of Communications and Chief Information Officer at Special Operations Command Europe. Recently completed the National Defense University, Industrial College of the Armed Forces, Ft McNair, Washington D.C. with a Master’s Degree in National Resource Strategy. He was commissioned in 1989 following his graduation from the Ohio State University. He has served in a variety of assignments, including in-garrison and deployed squadron command, operational test and evaluation, program management, flight command, network systems operation and maintenance, and joint action officer. He was a distinguished graduate in both the Basic Communication Officer Training and the Advanced Communication Officer Training. Col Moser deployed as Commander of the 506th Expeditionary Communications Squadron at Kirkuk Air Base, Iraq from April to July 2003. He was promoted to Colonel in August, 2009.
