Dan Berne’s Summary

Dan Berne, Principal of the design4brand consultancy, is a veteran Marketing Manager of Hewlett-Packard Co. He is a recognized expert in creating brand and design strategies for high-tech products and services. He is the author of the forthcoming book, “Your Opinion Does Not Matter - creating brand value by design.” Dan directed the brand and product design strategies for Hewlett-Packard's Inkjet printing and photo businesses. His leadership provided HP market leading designs that increased channel and customer acceptance of new products. He revamped HP’s low-end printer business model, turning that market segment from negative to positive profit margins, while increasing worldwide market share leadership. Dan has provided strategy programs and workshops to technology-based groups throughout the U.S., and in such diverse locations as Singapore, China, Japan, India, Brazil, and Germany.
