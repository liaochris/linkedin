Moshe (Mike) Benjamin’s Summary

What do I do for a living?
Well, for once I try not to be like everyone else and use words like professional, dynamic, & seasoned. You ask why? Simple, those words are meaningless and old school. The world has moved beyond stale words and trite phrases. We are living in a new and exciting era, one filled with an ever-changing technology, information, innovation, creativity, and capability. A place where we must apply book smarts, street smarts, and immerse ourselves with what's around us, observing, studying, practicing, analyzing, learning, and testing.

Bottom line: I am a marketer! I am immersed in the marketing world, especially online. I seize new opportunities, apply art and science, analyze, think, innovate, and deliver phenomenal results.

For "old schoolers" out there, here's who I am:
A marketing strategist and a certified usability analyst with an extensive and proven track record in architecting, developing, leading, and directing successful online campaigns and complex digital initiatives for several leading Fortune 200 companies.

Having one foot in marketing and one foot in IT, I approach work from both an analytical/rational POV yet apply a creative/innovative POV to deliver a unique well-grounded and well-rounded balance that consistently exceeds expectations.

Programs include:
- Developing powerful marketing, customer experience, and loyalty programs in the high-tech, retail, financial, and consumer-goods industries.
- Architecting and planning hugely profitable digital strategies. Several, with returns well into the hundreds of millions.
- Directing the design and development of several award-winning web sites, applications and interfaces.
- Overseeing and managing the design of powerful MARCOM campaigns both on and offline (emails, ads, games, apps, and in-store kiosks)
- Managing a team of directs, indirects, & 3rd party suppliers working in traditional, Agile, and UCD-methodologies.
- Managing business development efforts.
