Tamia Stinson’s Summary

I believe in creating things I'm passionate about, and using my talents to create opportunities for other people. I believe in using the power of the web to help individuals and organizations successfully do what they love.

I help businesses grow through creative and effective marketing and efficient operations. I have over 6 years experience developing, planning, and managing integrated marketing campaigns including print and digital design, branding, and sales support.

My (very determined) inner entrepreneur led me to create Style Sample magazine, where I assume the roles of Editor-in-Chief, publisher, designer, chief marketer, and pretty much any other "-er" you can think of! Business management sense is crucial to running a publication, so I rely on my obsessive need for organization, picky attention to detail, creativity, and communication skills to make it a success.
