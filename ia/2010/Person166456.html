Who am I:

HQ'ed out of beautiful Portland, OR, I am medical device recruiter of who works on both a contingent as well as retained basis with client companies across the nation.

I specialize in bringing the top firms in the Medical Device / Pharma / Bio-tech industries together with the premier talent in the marketplace. My clients range from a local Pacific Northwest start-up of 10 employees to that of a European headquartered worldwide organization. With my team's expertise and the vast reach and resources of the MRI Network's 1000 offices, we have placed candidates across a broad spectrum of niches and geographies (IE: Director of Manufacturing in Seattle to Cardiac Rhythm Management Sales Reps in various cities to a Sr Analytical Chemist Manager in Germany with Drug Eluding Stents).

I primarily partner with clients that are looking to top-grade and/or expand:

Sales
Marketing
QA / QC
Regulatory Affairs
Clinical
R&D
Engineering

I target talented med device professionals with a track record of success, solid work history and appropriate education and experience.

VP's, Directors, Managers, and Engineers with experience looking for new opportunities are encouraged to call me directly for an immediate and confidential consultation (503-290-1125 or 1-800-979-8701 Ext. 1125).
