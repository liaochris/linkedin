Leslie Baer’s Summary

After working for a couple of years at the Michigan site of the Human Genome Project (and Molecular Biology Department) I began seeking positions that would permit me to improve the communications of science and health information to lay people. I worked as a research associate responsible for communications in the nursing industry for a few years, then launched Mountain Muse Communications. The goal is to not only improve communications, but to better integrate areas like print and web, marketing and internal communications, project and information management. The goal was also to serve sustainable and socially responsible industries. Today, Mountain Muse does just that, selected the areas of communications integration appropriate to each client, and helping clients such as SRI financial companies, sustainable business organizations, local sustainable merchants and more. My current focus is on designing the communications projects and implementing the required project management.

Leslie Baer’s Specialties:

Project design and project management for integrated communications management, including print, web, and information management projects. Increasing the efficiency and efficacy of clients' communications projects and work flows.
