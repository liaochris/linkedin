Benjamin Forbes’s Summary

A gifted visual artist and aspiring businessman who has demonstrated a
strong sense of leadership and exceptional creative vision to strategically
position global brands among competitors by implementing sound design
solutions according to current market trends in the visual arts,
entertainment and fashion industries.


The art of Benjamin Theron Forbes III can be classified as unique and visually enriching. The vibrant color combinations embellished with organic shapes and forms leave viewers awe stricken by the intensifying emotions formulated by the intricately applied hues. Forbes’ works are indicative of the painstaking hours taken to craft quality inspired art for those who dare to delve into his unconventional way of expressing his message. There are no underlying theories embedded in the production of his pieces, every work is a continuation of the previous ones; establishing dialog with the current to produce a cohesive conversation conveying beauty in its purest form with out distorting people’s perceptions.
