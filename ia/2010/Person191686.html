Sonali Agrawal’s Summary

Sonali has helped multiple clients create and maintain their web sites. She has vast experience in Dreamweaver, (X)HTML, Photoshop, Illustrator, CSS and PHP/MySQL(Beginner Level). Her list of clients includes AngryCorner.com (www.angrycorner.com), Silver City Inc., Sivy Technologies (www.sivytech.com), Butler Education Foundation (www.ecoyouth.org), Ananda And Johnny, Inc. (www.anandaandjohnny.org) and many more.

Sonali is Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Manipal Institute of Technology, Karnataka, India. She has also completed various courses on Multimedia, HTML, Dreamweaver, Maya and Photoshop.

Her aim is to be an Exceptional Web Designer by focusing on quality and hard work.
