Sal Christ’s Summary

I love the performing arts and have been involved in one form or another for more than twenty years -having fulfilled roles of theatre actress, stage manager, playwright, ballet dancer & classically-trained violinist. I started a video production company at 21 and served as VP of Design Services for a graphics studio at 22 before working as a market supervisor and project/product manager in the advertising world for two years. Since 2005, I've also worked as a freelance producer and sent two shorts to the film festival circuit in 2008 & 2009.

New and social media, as well as anything related to the guts of the music/film industry fascinates me. I blog regularly on these things when I'm not busy with a new film project or working on my book.

In August of 2010, I'll be relocating to New York City to begin work on my next degree with Columbia University.
