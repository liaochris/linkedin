Marcel Olislagers’s Summary

Marcel gains every day new inspiration from his prefered profession. He is a marketer and passionate communication professional focussed on all kind of new developments within the digital world.

His passion lies in creating and developing new strategies that can be effectively deployed to communication campaigns.

He owns his agency MCM Connexion and is also a innovative entrepreneur in web-based business concepts. He is involved in a new concept around Online Personal Branding named Myreputation.nl. Besides he is developing a new European "matchmakingportal" for investors and interesting start-up companies.

Within his daily profession Marcel is very into "design" focussed on corporate identites with distinctive visual elements. Marcel gives very often lectures and publishes frequently articles. He has a large network of people and companies in and likes to bring his network together.

Marcel has worked as Marketing Communications Manager within several international companies. He gained professional experience within blue chips companies as well start up organisations. Before starting his own agency MCM Connexion in 1998, Marcel worked at:

Marketing & Communications Director at Wang Global
Manager Interactive Media at Atos Origin
Marketing Director at Canal Plus
Business Development Manager at Viacom Outdoor Advertising
Marketing Manager at ActMedia Europe
