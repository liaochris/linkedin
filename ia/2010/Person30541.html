Bryan Srabian’s Summary

A Sports and Entertainment Marketing professional, who specializes in Social and Digital Media. With over 15 years experience working in professional sports, I am now overseeing the social and digital media landscape for the San Francisco Giants.

My background is in marketing and communications, and have always had a passion to be creative. Working for the Giants (and other small projects) in social media has been a wonderful experience, and wake up every day learning more about this growing and evolving world.

I love to help people understand social media and work with many small businesses and professionals on learning how they can effectively use social media with their current marketing and communication mix. I am very interested in learning about new technologies and how to help the Giants and others best utilize to achieve their goals.
