Tony Ceresoli II’s Summary

With a proven leadership style and a passion for advertising, I have been able to take Ad Partners from a start-up to a "Top 5" ad agency in Tampa Bay in just under five years.

Prior to Ad Partners, I spent 11-years as the Vice President & G.M. for Devine Communications, a regional ad agency in St. Petersburg, where I was responsible for managing the day-to-day operations and securing a number of high profile accounts.

Other career highlights include:

At 23 years old, served as the youngest radio station   manager in Tampa Bay; leading WGUL-FM to "Top 10" status.

Owned and operated a successful local AM talk radio station for 3-years; in 1989, responsible for being one of the first radio stations in the U.S. to sign on the Rush Limbaugh Show.

Served as the advertising sales manager for largest cable system in the Tampa DMA.

Owned and operated a direct mail company based in Clearwater, FL.
