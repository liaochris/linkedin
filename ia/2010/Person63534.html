Elizabeth Hansen’s Summary

In addition to being the Travel Editor of Ranch & Coast (www.ranchandcoast.com), I freelance articles and photos to other magazines.

I also maintain three travel-related blogs."Staying Healthy on the Road," "Authentic Luxury Travel" and "La Jolla Travel Information" can be found on my website www.ElizabethHansen.net.

And, with Richard Adams, I operate ADAMS / HANSEN STOCK PHOTOS consisting of more than 80,000 images. These include travel photos and an extensive collection of classic car pictures - Bugatti being our featured marque. Our images can be viewed at www.ElizabethHansen.net or at www.agpix.com/adamshansen

Elizabeth Hansen’s Specialties:

My specialty is writing about 1) Authentic Luxury Travel, 2) my hometown - La Jolla, California - and 3) my home-away-from home - New Zealand. I also write about 4) Peace Tourism (making a difference in the developing world through voluntourism).
