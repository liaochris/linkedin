madeline faiella’s Summary

Interestingly enough, my professional experience spans 3 main areas; art and design; singing; and the law.

I've been an artist all my life. I studied in New York and then went off to experience the world - I was singing then. I returned to the US in '92 & faced new challenges. A flexable person, I became a Nationally Certified paralegal after rigourous exams. I am also a Florida Registered Paralegal registred with The Florida Bar.

I enjoy illustrating to refinishing furniture. I go where my visions take me or where the my client needs to go.

In the last two years I have taken to desiging jewelry but find myself sliding into polymer clay and PMC jewelry for more original expression. I teach jewelry making to a few students locally.

My goals are to work full time as an artist and writer so I am forming my foundation now. Not without it's pitfalls, I test and express! I want to eventually license my work and currently I'm working on a children's book project with my sister. It's exciting.

I hope to also speak to people who need to hear that there will always be others who will make you feel that you need to fit their mold in order to be recognized; I don't agree. Let's see where that goes. I like helping people. With lots of experience in front of people this will be another natural for me

I have had a lot of experience as a writer. Working in the legal field has made better writing a necessity. I know it's a two sided fence that I walk but as an ambidexstrous person, it's more understandable.

Another goal is book writing and I have touched upon that from time to time. Right now, the book with my sister will take precident. It will be a children's book taken from a pscycholgist's and the illustrator's points of view and experience to help children. We are excited!

I donate images for tee shirt designs for various fundraisers and have done so for about 5 years.
