Thomas Gay’s Summary

Certified Facilitator-Executive Coach...
At TAB, I create the environment to help business owners discover meaningful goals and strategies to achieve their personal and business objectives. Executives come together into a collaborative board meeting process that gives each business owner the combined experience of 8-10 other CEO's who provide fresh thinking on how to face very specific issues in their own companies. As a mentor and coach and leader of one of the nations largest coaching companies, I've helped numerous business owners find break-through strategies needed in times like these.

Relationship marketing...
Building a competitive advantage, loyal client relationships or solving internal issues in a company requires a deep understanding on how to build trusted relationships and team environments. My background as an innovator, sales trainer and teacher of TQM and consultative selling processes has enabled me to help many firms become more effective in the marketplace and differentiate themselves in times like these.

Entrepreneur-Business builder...
Solving complex problems using information technology has led to my starting several successful companies. Focus has always been on bringing new methods to address market problems and to attain financial leverage for both the customer and my firms.

Team/Organization Builder...
Every business I've been involved with has had as a core value the principles of individual growth, team work, leadership development, succession planning and mentorship. These companies continue to grow today as well as they've spun off numerous successful new firms under the leadership of former team members.
