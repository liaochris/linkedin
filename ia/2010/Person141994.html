Michael Roach’s Summary

Who Am I? And How Does My Background Serve to Help Your Business?

I'm not your typical copywriter... I'm also an engineer. I have a Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng) in Engineering Physics and I carry a license to practice as a Professional Engineer. I've led a successful career as an aircraft structural engineer, working for major companies around the world, such as Lockheed Martin.

When you market to engineers and other busy professionals, I don't just understand your target audience... I AM your target audience.

Combine my engineering experience with all my training as a direct-response copywriter... and you can count on me having a pretty good idea what constitutes good, professional, persuasive and compelling copy for business professionals.

Sometime during my busy and successful engineering career, I discovered a passion for copywriting. Over the years, I've invested thousands of dollars and hundreds of hours studying copywriting masters. And I continue to do so, enthusiastically. I take my professional development in copywriting very seriously.

What Does All This Mean for You?

What that means to you, my client and partner in business, is my commitment to put forth every effort, every time, to making your marketing promotions as strong as I know how, in order to get results -- the kind of results that mean sales -- and money in your pocket.

I also commit to being a valued member of your team... to be someone that you can count on to complete your projects in a timely and professional manner... to be a pleasure to work with.

So if you're looking for that type of copywriter, then I encourage you to visit my website, contact me and discuss your needs.

Michael Roach’s Specialties:

I specialize in writing direct-response advertising copy for business-to-business products and services -- especially those targeted at engineers, sophisticated professionals and serious hobbyists.
