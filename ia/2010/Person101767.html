JOHN CUONG LE’s Summary

Mr. John Le joined Crescent Financial Partners, Inc (CFPI) in January 2010 to provide support in technical analysis, business plans and private placements (PPM). John Le’s business focuses primarily on assisting clients to develop and review business plans before compiling PPM to investors.

Prior to joining CFPI, Mr. Le was the founder and manager of iScholar Tutoring, a service of Future Investment Tutoring Education, LLC (F.I.T. Education), serving college and executive MBA clients in Arizona and Southern California.

Mr. Le also had some experience in international business when he served as the business plan assistance for VICETCO, a Vietnam based start up clean energy technology company, where he assisted importing wind turbines from the US and China.

Before moving to the US in 2005, Mr. Le studied International Business at L’École Supérieure de Gestion et Commerce International (ESGCI) in Paris, France for two years. He holds double Bachelor of Science degrees in Economic and Finance, with Honors, from the W. P. Carey School of Business, Arizona State University, and he is currently an MBA candidate.
