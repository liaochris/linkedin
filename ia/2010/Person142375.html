Michael Kaczkowski’s Summary

Michael Kaczkowski has over 20 years experience in alloplastic and prosthetic reconstruction. He is an artist and inventor known for creating new alloplastic / prosthetic skin technologies that can only be recognized as non-flesh under harsh scrutiny. Among these alloplastic technologies include Dermatos®, Livingskin®, Derma~Flex, MyoDerm, Derma~Hair, Derma-Tan and RealLifeSkin™ to mention a few. His technologies are worn by thousands of patients world-wide and have been highlighted in the press, both locally and nationally, as well as in main-stream international magazines including Men’s Health, The Ladies Home Journal, and Stuff Magazine. Additionally his work has been seen on television broadcasts including The Learning Channel, The Discovery Channel and the hit TV series ER. Mr. Kaczkowski has also been profiled in several national publications and newspapers. He has authored various articles for amputee, prosthetic and health journals, including In-Motion, First Step, In-Step and The World Health Organization Journal. He is an active public speaker, delivering lectures and in-services to many professional organizations, hospitals, rehabilitation centers and private companies both nationally and abroad.
