Carlton A. Morris R.’s Summary

I have been instrumental in the establishment of The Cruising Club in the USA.

With 20+ years of franchise management experience in various companies and positions - I worked for over 8 years with Jani-King International in several of their domestic and International offices between 1986 – 1994. I held various upper management positions at Jani-King, including that of National Marketing Director, Vice President of Franchise Development, and Regional Director in Dallas, Seattle, Fort Worth and London, England.

In 1995, I purchased the Master Franchising rights to a commercial cleaning company in the territory of Miami, Florida. After early successes, the company grew to include offices in Chicago, Atlanta and Houston. I sold the business in 2006.

From 1985-1987, I worked as the Assistant to the General Manager for Ben E. Keith Beers of Dallas, Texas.

From 1982-1985 I was employed by C. Lloyd Johnson Company in the former Canal Zone, Republic of Panama. With C. Lloyd Johnson Company, I was a Vendor Representative for 40+ US manufacturers, including General Mills, General Electric, Ore-Ida, Dial Corporation, Scott Paper Company, Nabisco, and Best Foods.

    The Cruising Club Fractional Boat Ownership concept and business model has been successfully operating in Australia since 1998 and was first registered in the USA in 2008. The Cruising Club fractional business model has become well accepted in the Australian marine marketplace, with many boats located at multiple locations on Australia’s East Coast.

    The Cruising Club-USA was established as the vehicle to undertake the marketing, sales, implementation and operation of The Cruising Club Fractional Boat Ownership business model in the territories of the USA, the Bahamas and select Central American boating locations, under license from International Franchisor company The Cruising Club International Ltd.

    The company intends to market and sell a range of large luxury fractional power boats from 44-130ft in length, at a number of marina locations throughout the territory, utilizing The Cruising Club fractional boat ownership structure, management and operating system.
