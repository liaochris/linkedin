Timothy Edwards’s Summary

Tim’s key expertise is in the area of energy management. His passion for emission and energy reduction has engaged Tim in developing world-first technology and services to meet this end. Tim consults as expert in the field of strategic planning and implementation of energy management for the carbon economy.

Tim has over 23 years experience in the utility, industrial and mining industry and has extensive technical experience within its asset and energy management areas. Developing and implementing strategies for whole-of-utility cost optimisation, leading project management groups, and regulatory compliance within utility, industrial and resource environments has long been part of Tim’s encompassing job description. He continues to plan, lead-design, develop and supervise major projects as part of an extensive smart-grid leadership and engineering role. He promotes a culture of sustainability, trust, transparency and vision.
