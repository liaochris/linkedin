Brian Clark’s Summary

Business is about relationships- customers, partners, co-workers, vendors... the list goes on. Successful business requires a constant focus and attention to detail in these relationships.

As the Customer Experience Manager for Sierra Trading Post (a Top 75 Internet Retailer), I have the opportunity to work as the chief customer advocate within the company. I work directly with the directors and managers in all departments of Sierra to identify and take action on areas for customer experience improvement. A systematic Voice of the Customer program being implemented 2009-2010 has enabled the company to collect, analyze, and act on customer feedback. I currently collaborate on multiple cross-divisional systems projects to address customer and internal user needs.
