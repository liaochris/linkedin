Deb Maney’s Summary

Senior business operations leader with a comprehensive knowledge and experience developing, leading, and managing organizations in a large variety of business operations and technical functions.

Organizational Development and Leadership
Selected twice by leaders to create structure, develop, and manage large start up operational organizations, in support of two of Sprint’s leading technology initiatives. These teams grew to an employee base of 43 and 60 respectively. The larger team delivered the Voice Over IP product to 27 of Sprint’s Fortune 500 business customers.

Emergency Management

Built 52 site specific incident response plans for the critical nodes in Sprint’s nationwide network. Led operations in Sprint’s 24/7 war room, coordinating and supporting all Field Operations response and recovery during Hurricane Katrina.

Project Management
Perfected project management skills through years of initiatives such as migrations of the Sprint “next generation” call processing platforms, numerous technical equipment upgrades, organizational structure changes, multiple real estate control center and distance learning construction projects, and strategic planning process.
