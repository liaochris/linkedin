James (Jim) Misener’s Summary

I have a proven record of excellence in multi-institutional and multi-disciplinary settings that uniquely qualifies me in this leadership position. I provide technical and management credibility to university faculty, the public sector and industry alike; I provide an ability to create alliances and collaborations with applications focus; and I provide a demonstrated ability to create and foster teams of individuals. I am able to quickly grasp and comprehend technical, management and political issues – and from that, I deliver programmatic, project and task solutions creatively, on schedule, and within budget.

I also have in my background considerable experience in defense technologies ranging from research in rocket exhaust plume observables, through ground combat systems countermeasures. I have conducted direct work and/or consulted with companies and have addressed all DoD Departments.
