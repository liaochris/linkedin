Mark Colacioppo’s Summary

Online entrepreneur having founded three online marketing companies and an angel investor in two others (LongTailVideo.com and Enduro Products).

Launched latest web startup, Globalizer, LLC in June 2008. Globalizer, LLC (http://www.globalizer.com) is a full service web marketing firm specializing in Social Media Marketing (Facebook and MySpace Advertising), Search, Display, Email and Affiliate Marketing (http://www.globalizernetwork.com).

Previously founded and ran online marketing agency Hot Rocket Marketing, Inc. which was acquired by Traffix Inc. in January 2005. While under the Traffix umbrella, created and ran the RocketProfit (http://www.rocketprofit.com) affiliate network.

Was formerly a co-founder of online agency Network60 Inc., which ran numerous web properties including EasyWinning and OneStopWinning.com which was sold to About.com in 2000.

Started online marketing career in 1996 while at PCH.com, launching commerce platform for the site. Subsequently played a key role in the relaunch of the PCH.com site in 1999.

Mark Colacioppo’s Specialties:

Social Media Marketing (Facebook, MySpace and application advertising), Display Advertising, Search Marketing, Affiliate Marketing and Direct Mail.
