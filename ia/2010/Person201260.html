Terry Eccles-Pettet’s Summary

I don't believe any Realtor dreamed of becoming a Real Estate Professional as a child. Instead we evolve into a caring, focused, detailed minded agent. I am no different. Starting my professional life at Citicorp headquarters’ at 399 Park Avenue in NYC as a management trainee by day and acting in off Broadway shows in the evening, until I married my high school sweetheart and traveled the world. Courtesy of the United States Marine Corp we were moved 13 times to 3 countries 4 states 12 cities with 3 children 2 dogs 3 cats and a really big gold fish. While in the Middle East I began to study the Art of Feng Shui which ultimately lead me to my love of Real Estate. Now helping families transition smoothly, making their homeownership dreams come true and helping families creating wealth for their future is my passion.

TEP, Producers, Inc., was created out of my passion to help my fellow Realtors to become the best they can be. By teaching, coaching and enlightening my colleagues, I think together we create a stronger and more professional real estate industry, based on ethics and integrity.
