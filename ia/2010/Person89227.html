Janet Andrews’s Summary

My mission is to help clients bring to light and communicate their unique gifts, talents, qualifications and expertise clearly and directly to create a competitive advantage in the marketplace.

I coach clients and train groups on the strategies, tools and techniques of launching a successful job search campaign through self-marketing, tapping into the hidden job market and achieving a fulfilling career transition. Expertise includes consulting sales, career transition, outplacement services, executive coaching, career coaching, marketing, business writing and resume-writing. I have a Masters in Counseling Psychology and am a Certified Career Coach.

Career Coaching:
I help individuals successfully maneuver through every phase of the job search and career transition process. Many of my clients have landed their ideal job utilizing their talents, skills and abilities at the salary they are seeking. My specific areas of expertise include action planning, networking, interviewing, negotiation and communication strategies.

Resume Writing:
With a depth of experience across multiple industries for producing stellar resumes, I customize and tailor an individual's background to his or her target industry and company of choice. First impressions count, and the resume is many times your first "calling card" when contacting a company. I will make your resume not only stand out from the competition, but be outstanding!
