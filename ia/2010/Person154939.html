  • Director

    Nomatica SA

    (Internet industry)

    June 2004 — March 2005 (10 months)

    Responsible for reorganising warehousing and after sales service operations leading to increased efficiencies and reduced headcount.

    Orders used to take an average of 7 days to get from the website to despatch, after negotiations with the union and staff, the processes were changed and this time was reduced to 20 minutes. We were able to increase capacity in the warehouse from 500 orders per day to 5,000 and were able to reduce headcount from 30 to 12.

    Director of International Development, responsible for growing Nomatica's business in all European countries except France and UK. This was a PLC board position and I was responsible for 15 Country Managers and had full P & L responsibility for each operating region. I established banking and fulfilment relationships in all these markets and changed the methodology of shipping everything from France to customers to a methodology of bulk shipping all orders, country by country, to a specific country and then locally despatching the goods, saving 2% of net margin.

    During this time I also negotiated the purchase of Scandinavian Photo from management with the backing of two Sedish banks who provided debt finance against the targets balance sheet, making it a cash and equity neutral deal for Nomatica shareholders.
