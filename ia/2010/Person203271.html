Thor Hinckley’s Experience

  • Manager, Renewable Power Program

    Portland General Electric

    (Public Company; POR; Utilities industry)

    September 2000 — Present (10 years 1 month)

    At Portland General Electric (PGE), I provide the strategic thinking behind the nation’s leading renewable energy programs. These programs have been rated #1 in the U.S. by the US Dept. of Energy National Renewable Energy Labs for total customers.

    As a market maker for renewable power products at PGE, I am also responsible for the strategy and development of PGE's renewable power options residential customers and its medium to large commercial/industrial accounts.

    In the area of solar energy, I have been responsible for the funding of numerous utility scale photo-voltaic (PV) systems. I also have responsibility for PGE’s corporate “green branding” strategy including its new www.GreenPowerOregon.com micro-site.

    I am deeply involved with the expansion of the Oregon Solar Highway project and PGE’s “Plug In Vehicle” electric vehicle charging station initiative. I’m currently developing PGE’s future retail offerings related to green energy .

    I have lectured at numerous universities and sustainability conferences and presented at national seminars on Marketing Green Initiatives, Green Pricing Programs, Renewable Power and Carbon Offsets.
