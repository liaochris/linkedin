Chris Peck’s Summary

We are a law firm fully committed to the business and property needs of our clients. Exclusively devoted to commercial transactions, business start-ups, corporate law, contracts, mergers and acquisitions, intellectual property, and real estate, for more than twelve years The Peck Law Firm has been intimately involved with the success of our clients by identifying hidden risks and providing sound counsel and timely advice. We understand that the complexity and volatility of today's market intensifies the needs of clients for experienced and skilled counsel, and we provide many of the tools needed to enable a business to thrive in the most challenging of market conditions.

Located in North Raleigh, our firm is uniquely adept at working with both newly formed and existing companies. We work closely with our clients and strive to learn as much as possible about the business objectives and operations of our clients in order to anticipate potential problems and develop a full range of options and innovative solutions. Relying upon our transactional experience, knowledge and creativity, our client’s are consistently achieving their personal, financial and business goals. Regardless of the size of the matter, we are committed to providing high quality legal services and delivering the personal attention needed to assist our clients to reach their goals.
