Diane Kulisek’s Summary

I don't mess around. I get in, get the job done and, when the time is right, get out. Depending upon the size and complexity of the challenge, this could take just a few hours or more than a year.

The employer information I tend to deal with is often very sensitive and there is also a great deal of urgency associated with resolution of most compliance issues. Hiring me as a regular salaried employee has, therefore, proven to be the best value for all concerned, although I am willing to entertain agency contract opportunities.

High-level cross functional interactions and collaborations have been required to create the desired changes or resolve the pressing problems I've been hired to address. Accordingly, I have proven to be most effective when working at the director level or higher.

I have held 7 such senior management positions in the past 10 years, all with quantifiable achievements of great value to my past employers, as indicated by their public endorsements, many of which appear on my LinkedIn profile or the CAPAtrak website.

I provide outstanding support for product, process or service quality system implementation, root cause investigation, corrective and preventive action, quality assurance, verification and validation, risk assessment, metrics, technical writing and regulatory compliance. My track record of consistent success is beyond compare.

My MS Word resume can be downloaded here or directly from this url: http://www.capatrak.com/DianeKulisekResume.doc.

I welcome your inquiry and can be reached most quickly via email to: dkulisek@capatrak.com .
