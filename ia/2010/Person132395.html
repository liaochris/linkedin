Maria Mihalic’s Summary

After graduating from Florida State University, I received my Master's Degree from Appalachian State University. I am a Seminole at heart, but the Mountaineers are making a strong run for my allegiance. For the vast majority of my life, I have lived in Miami.

I came to answerQUEST after graduation ... and becoming an adult. I interviewed with the company thinking I had a firm idea of what the world of recruiting was all about. A week later, I found myself at the company kickoff trying to stop my head from spinning.

I work with a fantastic team and couldn't have made a better career choice. In 2006 my team was ranked #1 out of the 4,500 recruiters in the global MRINetwork.
