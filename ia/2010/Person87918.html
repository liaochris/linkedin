James D. DeCamp’s Summary

I have been an award winning professional multimedia photojournalist since 1979 working for newspapers thru-out Ohio. I have covered events from presidential campaigns, to the devastation of tornados as well as typical daily feature and local event stories for daily newspapers.
I have covered every major sporting event in the region, from high school to collegiate sports and the World Series. In the past I have been a Photo Editor for various publications.
I have the ability to go anywhere with my camera and transmit images that have great story telling impact for both my clients and their viewers.

My professional goal today is the same as it was 25 years ago: To continue to learn everything I can, apply my abilities for the highest standards of photojournalism, improve each day and provide my clients with the best photography possible.

My work can be seen at: http://www.OhioPhotojournalist.com and I can be reached by email at: Jim@OhioPhotojournalist.com or (614) 462-8027
