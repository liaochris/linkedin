Chris Stone’s Summary

As a UX designer *or* Interaction Designer, depending who you ask, I'm on a quest to make software, services and products more intuitive and useful for humans to interact with as they connect to a clients' business goals.

Since 2004, I've successfully delivered products across mobile, desktop, web and interactive display platforms. I've worked directly with Adobe Inc., Vancouver Aquarium, PayPal, MSFT, Nokia, Sierra Wireless, Palm, Toura Inc., TckTckTck and many others.

I'm also active as a founding member and organizer of the Vancouver chapter of the Interaction Design Association (IxDA).

Random info: My journey into UXD began when I made the transition from print design to interactive media back in 2004.

Jumping on grenades. Wrangling the last breath out of scope creep. Getting it done, whatever "it" may be

    IxDA is an international non-profit organization with a mission to advance the discipline of Interaction Design. We do this by creating platforms for our members to gather on, such as a 10,000+ member discussion list, annual conferences and local events.

    The IxDA Manifesto:

    We believe that the human condition is increasingly challenged by poor experiences. IxDA intends to improve the human condition by advancing the discipline of Interaction Design. To do this, we foster a community of people that choose to come together to support this intention. IxDA relies on individual initiative, contribution, sharing and self-organization as the primary means for us to achieve our goals.
