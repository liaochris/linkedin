Angie Vaughn’s Summary

I’m a seasoned direct marketer whose results-driven approach has been crucial to exceeding client expectations, both in terms of achieving goals and providing agency value. I’m always looking for ways to make my clients’ communications efforts more relevant, targeted and response-driven (even if they don’t ask me to).

For almost a decade, I’ve helped companies across multiple industries—telecom, utilities, healthcare, non-profit and restaurants—build strategies around ROI goals. Along with that focus comes a dedication and understanding of measurement, analysis and optimization that I’ve applied across multiple tactics, such as loyalty programs, direct mail, email and branding. In fact, I’m often heard saying, “how about we test that?”

A self-proclaimed “data geek,” I’m also experienced in customer segmentation and modeling, development of marketing databases and data analytics.
