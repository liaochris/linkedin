Court Bradley’s Summary

Court Bradley is a commercial real estate advisor and strategic consultant. His passion for entrepreneurship led to the design of several internet businesses including the creation of the online platform of DataVest.

As a student of internet marketing, demographics, and trends, Mr. Bradley believes even through the current recession, the world is evolving at a never before seen pace. Capitalizing on specific trends, coupled with the power of internet marketing, provides tremendous opportunity for the prudent investor.

Mr. Bradley understands that many of the fundamental business practices of commercial real estate are rapidly changing. The potential of harnessing the unlimited power of digital internet marketing creates an innovative advantage for today’s discerning investor.

Arming with real-time data points and comprehensive market knowledge, creates impactful new metrics. The investor who leverages these tools and actively prepares for future asset decisions will be in an agile position to easily acquire future assets with the desired yields.

During his undergraduate education, Mr. Bradley worked with major retailers and marketers, including Abercrombie & Fitch and GMR Marketing. Bradley led A&F in recruiting, and during his tenure with GMR, he increased the brand’s sales by 22.4% in less than a year. After a brief venture in South Texas, Bradley began working in commercial real estate, and helped secure a $48-million-dollar portfolio his first year.

A licensed Texas real estate professional, Mr. Bradley currently is pursuing his CCIM designation and becoming fluent in Spanish. He earned his communications and business degree from Texas A&M University, where he served in the Corps of Cadets Company E-2, “The Mascot Company.” He enjoys sports and adventures, and spending time at his family’s ranch and lake house.
