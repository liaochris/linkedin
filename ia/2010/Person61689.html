Ed Callaway’s Summary

Wireless communication engineer specializing in low-power WPAN and wireless sensor networks, with mixed-signal, RF, and analog systems and circuit design experience. Extensive work in international standards, especially IEEE 802 and ZigBee. Now executing corporate technology strategy for IC platforms and external relationships.

Ed Callaway’s Specialties:

Low-voltage (< 1 V), low-power circuit and system design; standards development strategy; IC platform development.
