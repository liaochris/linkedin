Ron Antonette’s Summary

Ron Antonette is a 20-year brand communications and public relations executive, based in Southern California, with experience that includes in-house and national agency roles leading programs for Fortune 100 companies, start-ups, established consumer brands and entertainment properties. He provides "Full-Service Thinking, Self-Service Overhead."

Ron's approach to PR and marketing is that “everything about your brand communicates” -- every aspect of marketing communications should be working together holistically to share one message through a variety of disciplines and vehicles, anywhere your audiences receive branded information. Public relations veterans are uniquely qualified (both by education and through media consumption) to be leading the dialogue about companies and brands.

In his role as an independent consultant, Ron supports companies and agency teams as both a Communications Director For Hire -- providing group leadership and strategic counsel for small in-house communications teams -- and as a Micro Agency. He partners with and taps into a preferred network of other independent professionals and mid-sized marketing agencies – friends and professional colleagues – who can round out marketing campaigns with additional services from SEO and Web design to media buying and broad-scale media relations projects.

Throughout his career, he has been involved in promoting family & parenting products and programs, including more than a decade as account lead for client Playmates Toys; agency and client roles for Mattel; launching Lego’s first learning-focused line; and projects for Amazon.com and The Walt Disney Company, among others.

Ron is experienced and passionate about projects that impact parents and families, corporate social responsibility programs, green marketing and projects related to sports & recreational activities.

Ron Antonette’s Specialties:

Consumer brand marketing; social media strategy; corporate social responsibility campaigns; creating and partnering on integrated marketing campaigns; internal communications; crisis communications and plan development; special events development and management; news and creative copy writing and editing.
