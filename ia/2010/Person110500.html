Karen Cole’s Summary

Rainbow Writing, Inc.
Karen Cole
Executive Director

www.rainbowriting.com

We have been on the Internet since 2003 as inexpensive ghost writers, rewriters, analyzers, copy editors and proof readers of book manuscripts and screenplays, and I personally have performed many other types of freelance and contracted writing jobs. I have a combined degree in journalism, creative writing and the fine arts, and have been creating and editing writings, documents and papers for people since 1980.

I have worked or helped out on some 150 books, directly and indirectly, over my lifetime: freelance writing, ghost writing, rewriting, copy editing and proof reading for book authors. We usually only take credit as the “editor” when we write or edit for an author, and our fees are lower than industry standard rates for most professional ghost writers, rewriters, copy editors and proof readers.

Please visit our website at www.rainbowriting.com for more info about Rainbow Writing, Inc. We do inexpensive and affordable freelance writing, ghost writing, copy editing, proof reading and almost all other forms of writing and book services. We also offer a links exchange service and screenplay writing, editing and analysis as well as book graphics and digital photography - and some publishing assistance and marketing services for certain select broad-selling clients.

I have won awards for my journalism, poetry, short stories and articles. I am multiply published and have had my own novels, novellas, short stories and scripts published. I am currently working on “The Rainbow Horizon,” a humorous fiction novel set in a small town in Washington State. I have contributed to national and international magazines and newspapers, as well as a multitude of online publications.

I am always on the lookout for brand-new projects and first-time book author and screenplay clients.
