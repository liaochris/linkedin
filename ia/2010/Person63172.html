Elena Rapisardi’s Summary

Experienced in conceiving and managing web content projects in a web 2.0 perspective. Developing content strategy based on client objectives and users needs, analysis of content requirements and internal/external content sources; conceiving content representation and presentation; developing a common knowledge with clients so to enable them to switch from “paper” communication to digital one; conceiving guidelines for the editorial staff; defining labeling and visual briefing; defining the use of social media and crowdsourcing approach and solutions; creating presentation materials; design, supervision and co-ordination of web content work-flow.

Proficient across a wide range of sectors, business areas, and disciplines. i.e. communications, marketing, public administration and civil protection, SMEs, non-profit organisation, broadcasting, social disciplines.  Proven ability to give public speeches and lessons [university and vocational training courses].
