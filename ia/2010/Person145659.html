President South Carolina Marketing Council SCMC

The mission of the SCMC is to unify the South Carolina marketing community; to enhance educational, informational, and networking opportunities; to build membership regionally and statewide.

We do not compete with the various marketing associations but rather utilize their strengths and guidance and or direct our members to these associations if further details about a particular area of marketing are needed.
