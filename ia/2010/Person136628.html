Matias de Tezanos’s Summary

Entrepreneur. Advisor to Startups. Optimist. Superhero.

I like to build companies - I don't know why - but I have a blast doing it. I self-ignite.

I think that the best things in life are free, although some of the most fun are really expensive. Sushi. Speed. House Music. Beautiful Family. Blessed and incredible life. Avid Reader. A gentleman that like to smile. I accept all people. Always looking to meet great minds.

In the year 2000 at the age of 20, I drop out of college and started the travel site Hoteles.com from my room, which is now one of Expedia's leading hotel reservations sites targeting Spanish speaking Internet users worldwide.

In 2002 I co-founded a company that sells Internet advertising across many popular websites in Latin America; ClickDiario.com Network. Our team was so fantastic that 3 years after starting the company, we end up reaching 6 out of 10 Internet users in the region. Now the business is a subsidiary of .FOX Networks (Newscorp).

In 2005 I started working with early stage Internet companies around the world; crazy, dangerous, chaos, non sense, plans always change, however, seeing how many great things are built with all these factors is a lot of fun.

In 2007 I started Healthcare.com, Inc. - with amazing partners and staff. I'm currently the CEO of the company.

I've been honored a couple of titles... Superhero by my nieces and nephews, and YGL by the World Economic Forum in 2009.
