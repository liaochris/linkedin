Richard Beldyk PE’s Summary

Twenty-seven years of engineering experience in a variety of fields and endeavors. A Bachelors of Mechanical Engineering from the University of Delaware, a Ph.D. in Forensic Engineering. Licensed Professional Engineer, in two specialties, Mechanical and Welding Engineering, in multiple states and Chartered Professional Engineer in Australia. Welding engineering experiences include FCAW, GMAW, GTAW, SMAW and SAW (single wire and tandem) processes with materials ranging from ferrous, nickel, titanium to zirconium alloys. Mechanical Engineering experiences span from plant design and construction , manufacturing, pressure vessels, stress analysis to quality. Numerous certifications which include; ASNT NDT Level III, AWS Certified Welding Engineer, ASQ Certified Sigma Six , NASP Certified Accident Investigation Technician and Fall Protection Specialist, Ohio DOT Certified Quality Control Painting Specialist, and OSHA Authorized Trainer. Honored by; America Registry of Professionals, Marquis’ Who's Who in Engineering and Science, Marquis’ Who's Who in the World, Ohio Society of Professional Engineers, and Sigma Xi. Published author and orator, having presented at local and international conferences on welding, safety and education. Member of several USA and international committees on; Materials, NDE and Welding. Instructional experience includes teaching at West Virginia University at Parkersburg, Ohio University, Athens, and Washington State Community College, Ohio. Professional memberships include AWS, ASME, ASTM and NSPE.
