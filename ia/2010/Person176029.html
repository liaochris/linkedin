Rob van Drunen’s Summary

In 2004 I started with the bachelor 'International Business' at the University of Maastricht. During my first 2,5 year, next to my studies, I have been a very active member of SV Circumflex, where I took part of many commitees organizing a wide range of activities. Then I left for Peru, to study half a year at the Universidad del Pacifico in Lima. When I got back I finished my bachelor and left the entire summer to Kenya to volunteer at an education project. Right after I got home I started a full-time board year as the Vice-President of AIESEC Rotterdam. In september, after I went back to Kenya for 6 weeks, I started my master 'Business Studies - Entrepreneurship' at the University of Amsterdam which I finished in 1 year. Currently, I am working at DOET - Dutch Organisation for Electric Transport, which is an organization aiming to develop the electric transport market.
