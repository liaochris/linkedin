Michael Seaton’s Summary

A strategic marketing leader with a multi-disciplinary background, Michael’s digital expertise spans brand, direct, loyalty and CRM with a vision for innovative, return-on-investment based marketing. His work has been recognized with numerous national and international marketing awards.

A top-ranked speaker, Michael educates audiences across North America on digital strategy and customer loyalty. His marketing blog and podcast, The Client Side, was rated a “Top Ten Must Read” by Marketing Magazine as well as being listed on Advertising Age’s "Power 150" marketing blogs. Currently, he teaches Canada’s only Professional Certificate Course in eMarketing.

He has a proven track record of improving the effectiveness and efficiency of sales and marketing to increase revenue and decrease costs.

Strengths include leadership, innovation, strategic planning and execution, superior communication skills, and a drive for results.
