Tony Brucha’s Summary

Director in the Advanced Services Global ONM (Operations & Network Management) Practice of Cisco Systems’ Denver office. Experience and expertise in the telecommunications, internetworking, high technology and consulting services industries, including functional business areas such as business operations, sales and sales management and marketing operations, with particular strengths in information technology management and systems integration, program and project management, and strategic business and technical consulting. Extensive experience in growing and managing results-oriented teams in fast-paced, high-energy environments and effective at leading large complex project teams in the analysis, design and implementation of enterprise and service provider Business Support Systems, Operations Support Systems and Network Management Systems (BSS/OSS/NMS).
Have held increasing levels of management and leadership with full P&L responsibility including Vice President OSS Solutions at a networking and systems integration company, Manager Consulting Services at a Big-5 management consulting firm, and District Manager at a large telecommunications carrier. MBA earned at Rutgers University.
