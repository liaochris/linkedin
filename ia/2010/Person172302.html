Richard K Howe’s Summary

Richard Howe is the Chief Executive Officer of Internet marketing services innovator Inuvo, Inc. Previously, Richard was Chief Marketing, M&A and Business Strategy Officer at the Acxiom Corporation.

Prior to Acxiom, Rich served as General Manager of the Fair Isaac global marketing services business. Before joining Fair Isaac, he founded and was the CEO of ieWild Inc., a software as a service marketing services company which he sold to HNC Software where he had worked in both sales and product management capacities.

Rich graduated first in his class with a Bachelor’s degree in engineering from Concordia University, Canada, and a Master’s degree in engineering from McGill University, Canada. Rich is a patented inventor and currently sits on the board of Business for Diplomatic Action.

Rich has been on the forefront of technological innovation for 20 years having operated businesses ranging in size from startup through $300 million in annual revenue in countries that include USA, Canada, UK, Australia and China.
