Kevin Ferguson’s Summary

I've worked for a lot of different companies over the past 20+ years and I've gained a lot of experience not just in technology but how companies acquire and secure talented individuals. I started Craftlogic because I saw how bad the staffing industry did their job. They simply scraped job boards for resumes, made sure the resume had all of the required acronyms and then submitted them to their clients while hoping for the best. I decided that was wrong and there was a market for a company capable of finding talent. This is done by intensively interviewing all candidates by our expert staff to make sure the talent is not just on paper. We perform background and reference checks to ensure there is nothing in the past of a candidate that would raise a red flag. We have meetings on a social level to make sure the person can fit in with our client's team. Then, and only then, do we submit a resume. For any one open position, we may submit only one or two resumes but those resumes are on target with what our client wants.
We not only provide staffing services, but also custom software development, project management, project outsourcing, and consulting. Craftlogic is owned and operated by technlogists and not sales people and we understand your technology needs.
