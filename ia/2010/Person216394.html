AREAS OF PRACTICE
I am an experienced trial attorney in the areas of Family Law, Probate and Criminal Defense. I practice statewide and I am an experienced mediator.

PROBATE AND FAMILY LAW
I have litigated hundreds of divorce and divorce-related cases to the satisfaction of my clients. I handle:
• Divorces
• Modifications of Divorce Judgments
• Contempt Actions to enforce Probate and Family Court Judgments
• Paternity Actions
• Cases involving Child Support, Custody, Visitation, Medical and Dental Insurance, and College Tuition

• Prenuptial Agreements
• Adoptions
• Guardianships
