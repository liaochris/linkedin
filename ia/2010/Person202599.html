Jennifer Frazier’s Summary

Since 1988, The Writing Stable has provided Tampa Bay companies and non-profits with creative advertising that is compelling, refreshing, and motivating. Made up of a national network of seasoned professionals in the fields of creative direction, copywriting, web design, art direction, and broadcast production, The Writing Stable is not an advertising agency. Rather, we’re an advertising and design studio that brings together all the resources needed to create multi-media campaigns, craft a special piece, or breathe new life into a tired brand.
