Nicolás Calabrese (nico@sikker.com.ar)’s Summary

I am a web designer and front end developer living and working in Tres Arroyos, Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I am interested in the design and development of (X)HTML websites, with emphasis on accessibility, semantics, usability, and particular focus on clean xHTML, sensible CSS, and clean but sophisticated layouts.

I have multiple years of experience working for Internet, as independent profesional and as employee. I have performed diferent types of works targeting small and big clients, working by my own and as a part of a team, managing projects, and dealing with providers and clients.

Capabilities and competencies:
• Extensive experience on the interactive field, working for leading interactive agencies in Argentina and frontline brands.
• Knowledge of Internet-related technologies and ability to interact effectively with colleagues from other areas.
• Experience managing and coordinating web-related projects.

Feel free to contact me, If you think I could contribute in your web projects. You can also take a look at my portfolio to see my work, at www.sikker.com.ar
