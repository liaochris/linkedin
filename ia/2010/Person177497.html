Ron Scott’s Summary

Web development, sales, and business experience have lead me the current position of Manager of eBusiness Strategy.

What do I do? I manage the Web analytics and usability testing/engineering program at my company. It is my job to find areas of our Web assets that are causing confusion and revenue leaks through carefully analyzing both qualitative and quantitative data. Some people understand Internet technology and some understand sales. I understand both.

I am working to tie together financial data along with the qualitative and quantitative data to make predictions (forecasting) of Web site performance. Along with that I plan on calculating marketing costs per new member gained to help us better plan the most profitable marketing initiatives. We will also be able to better understand internal rates of return (IRR) for various Web projects that are requested.

For current members, providers and brokers I intend to use data to build a better user experience for them which will help the company with member retention and satisfaction which in turn will save money in the long run. This will also cause the member lifetime value to increase.

Through better understanding how people buy (and search) for a health plan through our various data feeds we can better tune/throttle our search engine optimization and online advertising campaigns.

All of these initiatives will give us competitive advantage in our industry.
