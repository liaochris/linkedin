W. Mike Presz’s Summary

Before you read my "official" bio below, please know that I'm passionate about the work that I do and enjoy working with talented people. I'm consumed by exploring the opportunities that the internet presents, humbled by the experience I've gained, and proud to have worked along side such great talent in my career. Now, the formalities:

Mr. Presz is a business focused, highly technical, and team oriented executive with over 23 years experience. He has a unique combination of skills that are well suited for today's global businesses: product/technology background as CTO and SVP/GM of an international business with responsibility over $120M in revenue, 40 countries, 22 languages, 26 currencies and multiple global partnerships, including Microsoft, Y!, MySpace, and AOL. Mike has been involved in the internet since late 2000. He has a strong people network, working knowledge and direct experience with enterprise systems and many internet business models through interaction with his peers within InterActiveCorp (such as Expedia, Hotels.com, Ticketmaster, HSN, EPI, Hotwire, LendingTree, RealEstate.com, Citysearch, and Ask.com) as well as through partnership/due diligence efforts.

Mike has proven experience as part of an executive management team with experience running large P&Ls, product & technology, financial reporting support, legal interaction, HR interaction, marketing, and customer care. Mike is a successful partner and due diligence resource involved in major contract negotiations, company evaluations and acquisitions, both domestically and internationally, including major players such as MSN and AOL. He is experienced with supporting an influential parent company, InterActiveCorp, as a member of the executive team and the due diligence team.

Mike's a no-nonsense executive who can be trusted to put business objectives and results first and to work relentlessly and within a team structure to help the company and its employees succeed.
