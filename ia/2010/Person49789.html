Dave Ozment’s Summary

On June 1 I joined iHealth Technologies as an Implementation Services Director.

Thus far, I thoroughly enjoy the opportunity as I learn more about our organization and in the serivce and value we deliver to our clients.

I'll continue to update my summary as I settle into the role.

In the meantime, here's a little background.

After 12+ years, January 2009 marked the end of my run with Accenture. Buyouts were offered, and I jumped.

My time working in the Healthcare Senior Products space has been my most rewarding experience. I was part of 3 Part D projects - including a lead role in an intensive go-live effort for a large regional Blues Plan (IBC), and I've spent about 10 months leading 2 CMS compliance assessments - one for a leading national senior products provider (Humana) and one for an outsourcing arrangement sponsored by Accenture (with Cigna).

I enjoyed my time with Accenture and feel that we parted on good terms... but after so many years on the road, it just 'felt right' to take the buyout and look for something closer to home requiring less travel.

I believe that I'm a determined, inquisitive, results focused leader/team member with a successful record of building, developing, and leading teams in delivering complex projects and managing complex operations. I'm detail oriented and methodical in my approach - it sometimes drives my wife crazy when I unconsciously apply these techniques at home. I enjoy working as a team to resolve issues and deliver quality and impactful results.

I've been successful in Accenture's fast-paced, collaborative, team-first, project-oriented, client-value and delivery-focused environment and fully expect to tranfer these skills and experiences to my next opportunity with similar success.

Clients with whom I've enjoyed working include: Humana, United Healthcare, PacifiCare, Rx Solutions, Cigna, Highmark, IBC, Pfizer, AstraZeneca, BellSouth, EarthGrains, EastmanChemical, and FERC
