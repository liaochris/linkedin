Andrea Ness’s Summary

Andrea joined Martin Waymire Advocacy Communications during its inception in 2004 after spending four years at Marketing Resource Group, Inc. of Lansing . At MRG, she served as account coordinator specializing in advertising, public relations, community and media relations, and Web marketing.

At Martin Waymire, Andrea provides public relations, social marketing, design and Web services for various clients, including Comcast, Greektown Casino, MESSA, the Michigan Association of Health Plans, the Michigan Roundtable for Diversity and Inclusion and more . She also manages all print production projects that run through Martin Waymire

Andrea is a graduate of Michigan State University, where she received her bachelors degree in advertising. During her college years, she interned for two semesters at Walt Disney World in Orlando in marketing and personal service training. She is an active member of the local and national chapters of the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA) serving as Director-At-Large and Communications Chair for the Central Michigan Chapter and also a member of the Mid-Michigan Creative Alliance.
