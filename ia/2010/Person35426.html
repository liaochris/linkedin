Chad Mosher’s Summary

I'm an aspiring television host with a lot of experience for my age. I worked for over a year on an internet game show, in many capacities including writer, production assistant and most importantly, host. In the summer of 2009, I worked with Game Show Network to host a game on their live call-in program "GSN Live." High school broadcasting courses allowed me to produce and host a two-part series based upon the game show "Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?", which was one of the most popular pieces produced for our in-house school news program. I hope to eventually gain more on-camera experience to secure a presenting job.
