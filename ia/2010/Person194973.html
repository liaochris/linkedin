Steve Fawcett’s Summary

Steve learned the art of persuasion years ago when he sold magazine subscriptions door-to-door. For over 25 years Steve has combined street smarts and marketing insight to create customized marketing solutions with killer results.

Beginning as a copywriter, he rose to being Creative Director at J Walter Thompson, and Doner Advertising, and the Chief Creative Officer of Quicken Loans, America’s #1 Online Lender.

His most memorable campaign: The Super Bowl launch of web giant AutoTrader.com’s brand with the ‘I Need A Car’ campaign, resulting in a 1200% increase in web traffic, and name recognition 3 times higher than their closest competitor.

In addition to creating award winning TV and radio campaigns, Steve has created everything from DRTV campaigns that generated hundreds of millions in sales, to successful medical marketing, recruiting marketing, search engine marketing, even high gloss custom publications.

To see a career retrospective of Steve’s work, go to www.bzzsaw.com
