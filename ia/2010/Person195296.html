Steve Mertens’s Summary

Steve Mertens is currently Sr. Vice President of Research and Development for Boston Scientific. Steve has been with Boston Scientific for the last eighteen teen years in a variety of positions including research and development, quality and manufacturing. Before joining Boston Scientific, Steve was in the electronics industry working in both research and manufacturing. Steve’s current responsibilities are the technical and people management of the global R&D organization. Steve has a B.S. in Chemical Engineering and an M.B.A. from the University of St. Thomas.

Steve is currently chairman of Lifescience Alley. Lifescience Alley has over 500 members in the upper Midwest. As chairman, Steve interacts with all the major medical device companies and helps the broader industry achieve its goals.
