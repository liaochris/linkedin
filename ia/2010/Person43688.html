Mike Cottmeyer’s Summary

Enterprise portfolio management, program management, and project management professional. Able to leverage traditional and adaptive project management methodologies to establish high performing project cultures. I deliver results through team building, accountability, and creating environments that empower individuals to deliver.

Co-developed an industry first Agile Project Leadership qualification in partnership with the DSDM consortium (UK) and GoAgile (Denmark). Certified at APL foundation, practitioner, and examiner. Founder of APLN Atlanta, member of the Agile Alliance, member of PMI, member of PMI Atlanta, and a member of the PMI-ISSIG. Honorary member of the DSDM Consortium and founder of the Lean Software and Systems Consortium.

Upcoming speaking engagements: PMI Global Congress in Orlando, Agile Development Practices in Orlando, and Oredev 2009 in Sweden.

Past speaking engagements: SQE Better Software Conference in Vegas, Agile 2009 in Chicago, Agile Edge in Washington DC, Agile 2008 in Toronto, Agile Business Conference in London, SQE Better Software Agile Development Practices in Orlando, and the Vancouver Agile Conference in Vancouver.

Local speaking engagements: PMI Alpharetta Luncheon, PMI Agile panel, Atlanta Scrum Users Group, Agile Atlanta, Thomson Technologies, LexisNexis (ChoicePoint), and Turner Broadcasting

Mike Cottmeyer’s Specialties:

Agile Project Leadership, Scrum, Rational Unified Process, DSDM, Traditional Project Management (PMP), Teamwork, Collaboration, Mentoring and Process Facilitation
