    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 1991 — December 2003 (12 years 4 months)

    Located in the southeastern corner of New Hampshire, St. Charles Children's Home is an intermediate group home for children from families in crisis. As a religious sister doing this work, the job entailed 16 hour days, 7 days a week. St. Charles is not a school, it is a home, so our primary work is parenting. During these 12 years I parented anywhere from 5-15 girls at a time--and handled the emotional and behavioral issues that children in placement experience. I also wrote treatment plans, facilitated team meetings that involved parents, Division of Welfare workers, and other outside providers. As the state found adoptive families for each child, I worked toward giving each child and family a smooth transition, and also provided support services after placement into the family as needed.
