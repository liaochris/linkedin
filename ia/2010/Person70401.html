Frank Marmo, CPA, MBA’s Summary

Commercial real estate investment and finance professional with extensive experience in commercial real estate investments, which includes identifying and structuring direct equity acquisitions, joint venture equity structures, and mezzanine debt investments. Over the course of my career, I have participated in and executed in excess of $1 billion of transactions on behalf of public institutional, Taft-Hartley, and high net worth client funds. I am also highly experienced in asset management and finance, where I closely monitored financial performance of assets under management, completed annual business plans, created and updated quarterly and annual valuations, and evaluated competitive and economic conditons in formulating ongoing asset strategy and hold/sell recommmednations. I am a CPA, MBA in finance, and a licensed Illinois real estate broker. Seeking new career opportunity in commercial real estate, private equity, or corporate finance.
