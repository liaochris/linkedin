Mondher Toumi’s Summary

Creativ-Ceutical provides strategic pricing, market access and health economics consulting for the life science industry. We provide both the strategic advice and the technical services to deliver results that meet or exceed your expectations. We help you make the right development choices, so your product demonstrates real value for money and your business clears the “fourth hurdle” of market access. From designing clinical trial plans that maximise your drugs potential to executing post-launch observational studies, we offer a complete range of professional solutions.

The company already has more than 15 employees, with offices in Luxembourg, France, UK, Tunisia and the USA. We have also developed a partner network that extends across Europe, the USA and the Far East.

Mondher Toumi’s Specialties:

Pricing, market access, health economics, pharmaco-vigilance, Drug licensing, drug development.
