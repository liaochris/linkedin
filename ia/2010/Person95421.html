Jennifer Whitt, PMP’s Summary

- Founder of Optimo, Inc. and PDUs2Go - the Portable & Affordable™ courses for project managers on the go™, an Atlanta-based consulting, training and technology solutions firm

- Keynote speaker, author of Optimize Your Thinking™, and a thought leader on the quantifiable and strategic value of global project management best practices

- Extensive experience with complex project / process optimization, talent identification, development and retention, as well as leadership development of project and program managers within the information technology industry

- Previous roles include President, Chief Project Officer, Project Director, Project Manager, Program Manager, and Management Consultant; Large company experience at Bell South Telecom & Accenture; Startup experience at Optimo and BeOne Now.

- An active member of professional organizations including the Project Management Institute (PMI), Women In Technology (WIT), and the National Speakers Association (NSA)

- Civic and Non-Profit contributions to the National Alliance for Autism Research & the Georgia Big Picture Foundation.

- Featured in a variety of publications, including PMNetwork and Project Management Leadership Magazines.

- A native of Alabama, Jennifer earned an MBA from the Keller Graduate School of Business, a graduate certificate in Project Management from The George Washington University, and Dual Bachelors Degrees in Information Technology and Natural Science and Mathematics from The University of Alabama at Birmingham

Jennifer Whitt, PMP’s Specialties:

Project Management Human Capital, Process Optimization, Project Management Instructional Design, Information and Financial Services Technology pre-acquisition due diligence and post acquisition integration, Carnegie Mellon Capability Maturity Model, ISO 9000, Process Improvement initiatives for iterative web-based development and deployment.
