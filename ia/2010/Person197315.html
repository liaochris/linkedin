sundeep ahuja’s Summary

** Bio **
Most recently, Sundeep Ahuja was Co-Founder & President of The Extraordinaries, which enables organizations to crowdsource from their communities. He currently serves as an Advisor there, as well as to a few other cause-oriented platforms including Kiva.org. Previously, Sundeep was Co-Founder at richrelevance, Marketing Director at Kiva.org, Product Manager at MySpace and eBay, and an actor with spots on entourage & 24. Sundeep holds a BS in Computer Science from Stanford University.
