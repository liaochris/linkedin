Ashok Ramaswamy’s Summary

Ashok has over 22 years of manufacturing, Transactional Processes, Project and consulting experience in the area of Six Sigma ,Lean Manufacturing, Design for Lean Six Sigma, Process Reliability & Maintenance Reliability, TRIZ, Operations Excellence, Safety, Health & Environmental Management, He has worked on various projects in Singapore, India, Malaysia, China, Thailand, USA, Korea, South Africa and Australia.

Ashok has developed and conducted training programmes for various levels of management. He was Regional Master Black Belt and Operational Excellence Leader for Asia Pacific for DuPont in Singapore. He is a Master Black Belt and has conducted the Six Sigma & Lean Manufacturing Training for the Green Belts, Black Belts and Master Black Belts in the Asia Pacific region.

Prior to becoming a trainer for Six Sigma, Lean Manufacturing, TRIZ, Operational Excellence, Process Reliability, Maintenance Reliability, Lean Behaviour Based Safety Management and Process Safety Management, Ashok had rich operational, Process and project experience in multi sites both in India and Singapore.
Ashok has done enormous amount of work in the Manufacturing, Supply Chain Management, Human Resource Excellence and Top Line Growth project in DuPont and GE.

He has trained, coached and mentored over10,000 belts in Asia Pacific Region with a great demonstrated successes in Bottomline, Transactional & Top Line Excellence Model. Ashok was pioneer in implementing ATC-FTC model for the TLG projects very successfully.

Ashok is one of the few experts in the Globe to implemented Process Safety Management System from Grass Root Level for High Hazard Processes and Low Hazard Operations.. He has developed a Prediction model using Six Sigma tools to predict the incidents based on the data gathered on the Unsafe acts, Unsafe Conditions, Near Miss Incidents, Safety and Process Incidents etc.
