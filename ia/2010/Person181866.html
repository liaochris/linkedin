Sandro Fusco, PhD’s Summary

Skilled Quantitative Analyst with expertise in financial engineering and extensive knowledge of Risk Management and Portfolio Management. Ability to analyze difficult subject matters and successfully present them to resource groups with diverse backgrounds.

Sandro Fusco, PhD’s Specialties:

Expert in (Market and Credit) Risk Measurement and Risk Attribution Methodologies for both Sell-Side and Buy-Side communities: “return-based” simulation (Parametric, Historical and Monte Carlo) and Multi Factor Models (explicit and implicit approaches).

Extensive knowledge of Portfolio Management functionality, including Performance Measurement (time weighted return, GIPS requirements) and Attribution Methodologies (e.g., Brinson Model).
