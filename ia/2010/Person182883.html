Dr. Sarah Stebbins, C.P.C.’s Summary

20 years experience as a trainer, coach and organizational development specialist in public, non-profit and private sectors. Expertise includes facilitating organizational change through successful coaching, training and compassionate, inclusive, innovative and integrity based strategies. Strengths include leadership development and authenticity. Viewed by others as strong leader and outstanding trainer with a great sense of humor.

My goal is to return to the private sector as an internal "change agent" and to develop effective training programs that support organizational change. I want to work for a medium-large organization that recognizes the value of investing in its employees as a way to increase productivity, retention and improve the bottom line.
