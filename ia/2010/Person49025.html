Darren Wright’s Summary

Founder and Principal of Wright Wealth Management LLC. A private wealth management firm in Arizona. With over 17 years' experience serving clients, Darren advises how to build and protect wealth focusing on holistic & macro wealth management strategies. He is a frequent guest commentator on the markets on Arizona's Channel 3, Fox 10, ABC 15 and KTAR radio and is a featured speaker at national and regional financial symposiums including NAIFA and Mass Mutual's Academy. Darren is a life time qualifying member of the Million Dollar Round Table, and has been recognized each year with the National Service Award in 1997 and 1999, and the National Quality Award 96, 97, 98, 99, 00, 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06, 07, 08 and 09.

Darren Wright is licensed to sell insurance in Arizona (16805), Iowa (2152910), Nevada (725873), New Jersey (1274315), and securities in Arizona, California, Florida, Indiana, Minnesota, Nevada, Ohio, Illinois, Virginia and Washington. I cannot communicate with, nor respond to, requests from people who reside in states where I am not licensed to conduct insurance and/ or securities business. CRN201112-129105

Darren Wright’s Specialties:

Focusing on holistic wealth management, investment management, and retirement planning analysis.

I am a registered representative of and offer securities, investment advisory and financial planning services through MML Investors Services, Inc., member SIPC. (Supervisory Office: 17550 N. Perimeter Drive, Suite 450, Scottsdale, Arizona 85255; (480)-538-2900).
