Deedy Alvarez-Sample’s Summary

Seasoned, professional, and personable 20-year IT veteran with a Master’s of Science in Project Management and a wealth of systems and project management experience. I’ve worked in a variety of industries and led/contributed to multi-year, cross functional, companywide initiatives, including those of a global nature. I've also managed multi-million dollar IT project portfolios, governing and reporting on hundreds of projects and overseeing 25-50 Project Managers through all phases of the project lifecycle.

Consistently recognized by executive management, peers, and subordinates for being a strategic thinker and a tactical executor. My key strengths are:

• Results-oriented
• Versatile, dependable and well-organized with a strong work ethic
• Interpersonal prowess
• Effective communications
• Innate analytical and problem-solving abilities
• Optimism
• Technical competencies

I am eager to undertake exciting and challenging projects that leverage my vast experience as well as provide an opportunity to grow and learn new things.
