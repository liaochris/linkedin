Denis Mohr’s Summary

Currently working as a consultant/member of Alternative Revenue Development. A group of dedicated individuals, that have joined forces for the purpose of developing sustainable and non-traditional revenue streams for school districts, via the creation of commercially sponsored networks.

Along with my work at ARD, I have started my own company along with my long time associate and friend Rick Yardley called "YGROUP", specializing in program and project management.

I enjoy what I do! I am an accomplished advertising and marketing executive with extensive and successful experience in managing both experiential marketing programs and traditional advertising and marketing campaigns.

I am skilled in coordinating the design and delivery of strategically driven, large-scale programs for some of the largest automotive events in the world. I have the proven ability to manage large account relationships to assure continued revenue growth and profitability and I am strongly committed to customer satisfaction.
