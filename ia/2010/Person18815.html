Avi Dan’s Summary

Avi Dan, President& CEO of Avidan Strategies LLC, is one of the most respected advertising agency insiders. Avi’s has 30+ years experience managing agencies, and overseeing flagship global brands at Y&R and Publicis; he also served on the boards of Saatchi & Saatchi and Euro RSCG and was featured in one of the most popular case studies of The Harvard Business School. He is an Advertising Age and AdWeek columnist, and is often quoted in the press.
