Sallie Kraus’s Summary

Attorney specializing in claims and litigation management with substantial experience managing environmental, mass tort and other complex litigation.

Additional experience in researching new products, organizing seminars and editing publications on emerging coverage issues, product liability, litigation management and insurance technology tools. Most recently, I have been researching and analyzing potential insurance coverage issues related to climate change. I contribute articles to Lexis/Nexis and have spoken at industry conferences on the subject.

Defense analysis, negotiation, mediation and litigation management for environmental, mass tort, professional liability and other complex claims.
