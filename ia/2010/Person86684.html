Jackie Parker, MBA, PMP’s Summary

Jackie is the President of Harvest Trends, Inc. (www.harvesttrends.com) providing business intelligence solutions specifically designed for the unique needs of the gaming industry.

Jackie first developed a passion for the industry while consulting to Harrah's and is grateful to all her friends at HET for teaching her the ropes!

Through an innovative approach, Harvest Trends offers casinos of all sizes access to the same technology and benefits as the biggest gaming operations. With its intelligent analytics, gaming operators can quickly and cost-effectively transform data for faster, better decision making that will grow revenue and increase profitability.

Patron Trends is an affordable database profiling tool designed by gaming professionals for gaming professionals. Please contact Jackie at jparker@harvesttrends.com or sign up for a webinar@harvesttrends.com
