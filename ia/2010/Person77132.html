Graham Farrar’s Summary

Graham is currently a Sr. Account Executive at Network Hardware Resale (NHR). NHR is the world's largest provider of heavily tested pre-owned and new sealed Cisco equipment. Graham run's the fastest growing team at NHR - helping clients operate their networks more efficiently by drastically reducing costs and lead times. NHR has been providing safe and reliable access to the pricing and availability benefits of the secondary market for over 20yrs for the world's largest networks.

Graham is a founder and partner at iStoryTime, a publisher of electronic children's books for mobile devices. iStoryTime is the largest publisher on the iTunes AppStore and has had many #1 ranked titles. iStoryTime publishes for DreamWorks, Readers Digest, Cartoon Networks among others.

Graham was part of the founding team of Sonos Inc (http://www.sonos.com) which designed and manufactures a next-generation multi-zone wireless digital home stereo. As one of the early members of Sonos, Graham was involved in Product Design, Software/Hardware Validation, Press, Sales and Customer Support.

Prior to Sonos, Graham was part of the original team at Software.com, which developed the first high-end email infrastructure servers for telephone companies and ISPs. Graham worked with Software.com as they grew from a private company to its initial public offering on the NASDAQ in 1999, during which time he was involved in Product Development, Product Testing, Network Design, Implementation and Sales.

He is the founder and owner of Santa Barbara Luxury Rentals, an upscale vacation rental management company located in Santa Barbara, California. Santa Barbara Luxury Rentals has been in business since 2003.

He is an avid adventurer, music fan and environmentalist. He has just returned from his second multi-year ocean adventure exploring the south pacific, the Mediterranean and the Caribbean seas. He also sits on the Board of Directors of The Santa Barbara Bowl Foundation (http://www.sbbowl.com).
