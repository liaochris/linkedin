Cari A. Wolfson’s Summary

Cari A. Wolfson is President of Focus on U!, a user-centered design and usability consulting firm. The firm specializes in analyzing, designing and iteratively testing websites in order to create products that are measurably easier to use, learn and understand. Clients include: the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the United States Mint, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Department of State, Department of Treasury, Department of Homeland Security (DHS), the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA), General Services Administration (GSA), www.usability.gov, National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), and numerous others. Wolfson also teaches several courses for the Federal Web Managers’ University.
