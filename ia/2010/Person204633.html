Tina M. Holden’s Summary

A 24+ year wireless industry expertise as a sales and marketing professional, Business owner for the past 16+ years. Operations, sales, management, new business development, Strategic planning within the wireless telecom industry, including a high value B2B base focusing on Technology services and Sales.

Growth to our business is to continue forming strategic partnerships and alliances that offer unique products and services and opportunities to grow our businesses together.

Products of interest are Voice Over IP, IT products & services, Third party applications and solutions along with much more.

For the customer it is how we build our business not for the venders we represent. We are your technology concerige.

I have always enjoyed seeing how technology can help all have a more streamlined Life or business. Getting others to want to learn more or understand the benefits of using it to run their business, their home and lifestyle but also keeping a solid balance is our vision.

If they do decide to put it in their life, the last thing they want to do is to read a manual, try to follow along behind some techy online demo using words that are foreign and frustrating.

I started in 1986 with my career as a sales representative selling Uniden Bag phones for Bellsouth Mobility. From there moving to the Cellular One side learning the most exciting technology at the time .... the CELL PHONE - which has evolved into the SMARTPHONE (Blackberry and Treo phones).

In working with businesses and people on their phone selection and set-up, I have always focused on service first, Sales second the opportunity will follow.

I love teens, their Passion and Drive to learn something new.

What better way than to share my knack for technology and ability to show others how to do that....

When you don't want to read the manual or figure out how to set it up - call us and we will help you choose your device and get it working for you!
