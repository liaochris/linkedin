Jeanne Jennings’s Summary

Jeanne is an independent consultant with over 18 years of experience in email and online marketing strategy and new product development. She specializes in helping medium- to enterprise-sized businesses become more effective and more profitable online.

As Head of Email Product at Reed Business Information US (formerly Cahners), Jeanne developed over 100 unique e-mail newsletter titles, plus numerous promotional efforts. Her client list includes Hasbro, The Mayo Clinic, The National Press Club, Network Solutions, Siemens AG, Verizon and Weight Watchers International.

Jeanne's first book, The Email Marketing Kit, was published by SitePoint in 2007. The Jennings Report is Jeanne's free email newsletter for online marketing professionals. She also writes a regular column on email marketing for ClickZ.com.

Jeanne is a sought-after speaker on email marketing for industry conferences and workshops. She's based in Washington, DC.

Jeanne Jennings’s Specialties:

Developing Effective E-mail Marketing Campaigns and Email Newsletters, Building or Growing Opt-in E-mail Lists, Optimizing Website Registration, Developing Efficient Information Architecture and Content Flows for Websites, Moving Print Publications Online
