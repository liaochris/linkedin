Susan Bonfiglio’s Summary

Summary
Hello, My name is Susan Bonfiglio and I'm an Independent Licensed LifeSuccess Consultant - and a business partner of Bob Proctor and LifeSuccess Productions. I am also a Blog Talk Radio Host, and published author.

It is my passion and purpose to help people move in the direction of their dreams. I look to help others who want a better life for themselves– realizing that the possibilities for them are infinite. I love helping people get there. A little humor along the way. I passionately believe in the power of laughter. Possessing a strong ability to glean people’s underlying wishes, identify what they want and translate those wants into action, I use a collaborative approach in my work with individuals and companies.

In addition to being a LifeSuccess Consultant, I am a Licensed Certified Social Worker. I hold two Masters Degrees, one in Education and one in Social Work. I am the co-author of the book, Shared Grace: Therapists and Clergy Working Together. I was introduced to energy work while attending a conference and found it to be an amazing tool to use in my work. I practice EFT and TAT. I am a Reiki II Practitioner. It goes hand in hand with the Law of Vibration. Prior to becoming a LifeSuccess Consultant, I directed an outpatient psychiatric rehabilitation program for chronically mentally ill adults.

I write the Prosperity-Consciousness and the Attract the Life You Desire Blogs so I can help people see they are not confined by the circumstances of their lives. Rather they can decide what kind of life they want and make that happen. And as a coach, I can show you how.

I am currently offering a Free 10 week mastermind group based on the book Think & Grow Rich.

I co-host a weekly radio show on Blog Talk Radio called Attract the Life You Desire.
http://www.blogtalkradio.com/attractthelifeyoudesire

For more information about my professional services and/or LifeSuccess study programs, I invite you to contact me.
