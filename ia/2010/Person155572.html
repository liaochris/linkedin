Coach Nisha Ray’s Summary

Empowering, Inspiring, Motivating, Uplifting you to explore the depths of your true essence and (re)discover your Passion & Purpose



Coach Nisha Ray, Single Mother of Three, Author of Saved, Sexy& Sophisticated a Woman's Pocketbook Motivational, Inspirational Speaker and Certified Christian Life &Biz Coach for Women and Teens.

Coach Nisha uses her unique style of upfront and matter of fact speaking blended with humor, empathy and personal experience to empower girls and women to dig deeper into their essence to (re) discover their passions and their purpose. She motivates the motivator to reach for higher levels and stay focused.


Programs Including:

Moms and Daughters Talking My Language - 10 week Program. Communication is the basis for every relationship. When there is a break down in communication misconceptions, false expectations and emotional barriers can grow. When this happens in a mother daughter relationship it effects the entire family life, work and school life. This Program coaches mother and daughter to get back to basics and hear one another by speaking in a language the other can understand which enforcing positive communication.

Women and Teen Coaching Programs:

7 Keys of Inspiration Coaching - 7 week program This is for you if you no longer know or have never discovered what inspires you, what you are passionate about and you're ready to walk in your purpose. If your purpose has has changed and its time to take it to the next level this is the program for you as well
Accountability Coaching- This is a customized program for you if have your plan know your purpose but needs help staying focused and on task. Brainstorm ideas and helps you find resources to keep you moving towards your purpose.

A New You Coaching- Women and Teens customized program for individuals who are looking for a complete Transformation
