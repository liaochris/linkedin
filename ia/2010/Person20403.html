James Bartlett’s Summary

With over 30 years as a Senior Level Retail Executive and an Executive Search Consultant in both Commercial Real Estate Development & Specialty Retail Sectors, I provide customized solutions to clients looking for talent to elevate their organizations while enhancing careers for the brightest talent in the industries. As a seasoned search consultant, I offer full life-cycle retained contained and contingent executive search, direct-hire, temp-hire and staffing services. I have been frequesntly involved in strategic restructuring. My clients range in size from start-ups to multinational conglomerates. With an uncanny sense of urgency, excellent eye for detail, uncompromising standards, highest level of integrity and confidentiality, I am passionate about what I do every day and welcome an opportunity to help my clients in achieving their corporate goals – grow and prosper - as well as help candidates achieve their career goals!

Always looking for Leader & Future Leaders in the Commercial Real Estate Sector (Construction, Engineering, Architects, Development, Design-Build, Project Managers), Asset & Property Managers, as well as Senior Level Specialty Retail Talent.
