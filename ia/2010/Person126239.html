Lisa Rodgers’s Summary

Cactus & Ivy is a manufacturer of cruelty free and vegan natural spa, bath and body products, utilizing ingredients to revitalize and restore the skins normal balance. Cactus & Ivy creates products with a purpose!

We use the finest ingredients from specialty oils to essential oils, high in Omega 3, Vitamin A, C, D, E & F, Oleic Acid, Palmitoleic Acid and Linoleic Acid. Recent feedback from our clients have indicated they would like to see products scented with fragrance oils so we are answering the call by sourcing oils that are phthalate free.

My goal is to have our products distributed all over the world!

World domination with a Southern flair! {Sweet}

Lisa Rodgers’s Specialties:

Creating custom blended spa, bath and body products since 2000. My creations are based on botanicals, oils, scents, etc; indigenous to the area in and around the client.
