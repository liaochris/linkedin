Lynn Shepherd, CFP’s Summary

Board Certified Financial Planner® practitioner, Ohio Women and Money Workshop Leader for Treasurer's Office for 6 years, Retirement/Financial Life Coach and Advisor. 35 years legal and financial experience as probate paralegal, investment advisor, and retirement educator. Business owner. Woman's Advocate. Graduate 18th Class Leadership Stark County, Ohio.

Lynn Shepherd, CFP’s Specialties:

Financial life planning/retirement coaching for Boomer Women over 55. I help boomer women take control and become WOW women. Women of Wisdom - Women of (true) Wealth. Discover how to achieve security, wisdom, alignment, and navigation so you can sleep well at night. Are you experiencing career change, divorced, widowed, or just ready to take control? Create a financial roadmap and eliminate the fear of being a bag lady in retirement.

Lynn Shepherd, CFP’s Education

    CFP , Finance , 2002 — 2003

    With 35 years of legal and financial experience, I realize continued education and ethics are important factors when providing financial guidance to women. Just as I help women set and achieve goals for themselves, studying for and achieving the CFP(R) designation was a goal I set for myself. As of 2006, less than 52,000 financial planners worldwide hold the CFP designation and only 30% of that number are women. I am proud to be included among that number.
