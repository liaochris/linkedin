Kristen Card’s Summary

Kristen Card represents a balanced blend of creativity and practicality in her approach to her profession – communications, and to her craft – copywriting. A “renaissance writer,” Kristen has over 15 years of experience creating any and all types of communications – from print ads to website content, from direct mail to feature profiles – for a broad spectrum of corporations, associations, nonprofit organizations and small businesses.

With a widespread reputation for the highest-quality work, Kristen is known for “getting it” and getting it done. She has extensive expertise in writing clearly and compellingly about extremely complex matters, from energy issues to the telecommunications industry to the state budget-making process. Kristen's journalistic roots trained her ear for storytelling and her eye for detail, which help her consistently take her work to the next level.

Kristen Card’s Specialties:

determining your differentiator & communicating it effectively; creative concepting, messaging & strategizing; translating complex concepts or technical language into "user-friendly" copy; discovering the common thread among disparate elements & using it to weave compelling stories; business features & profiles; website organization & content development; grammar, spelling & punctuation (ask me about compound-modifier hyphenation); working & playing well with others
