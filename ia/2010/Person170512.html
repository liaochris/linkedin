Amy Riechman’s Summary

I am a professional resume writer and HR Specialist. I have over 5 years' experience in staffing for 80% of employment industries. From Administrative Assistants to CEOs.

I am fast and efficient at reviewing resumes for employers which will save them time and money. I can also source candidates quickly and have an established database of qualified candidates who have been pre-screened.

I have worked with Fortune 500 companies along with business owners and hiring managers. I know what they are looking for in quality employees. From this experience, I am able to write professional resumes that will get you noticed and advertise you well.
