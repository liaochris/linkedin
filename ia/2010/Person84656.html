Dan Goodman’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur, consultant and educator with extensive business and technology experience around the themes of renewable energy, entrepreneurship, technology commercialization, and business development. Start-up specialist with nearly four decades of experience in bootstrapping for-profits and non-profits in the roles of founder, executive, board member, consultant and advisor; adept at “doing more with less”. Capable technology assessor and translator between technospeak and business value. Dynamic innovator with broad vision and proven capacity to concurrently execute multiple complex initiatives. Intensely persistent and action-oriented, with a knack for architecting “everyone wins” collaborations. Talented strategist and business development practitioner deeply networked in the start-up, funding, higher education, and renewable energy spheres of influence, particularly in Maryland.

Currently seeking additional independent director roles on boards of interesting post-revenue for-profit technology companies.

Dan Goodman’s Specialties:

Board governance, strategic planning, renewable energy technologies, technology transfer and commercialization processes, startups and entrepreneurship, business development, and various forms of venture midwifery.
