Gilbert West’s Summary

I help people and organisations build and improve their Web sites. I've been doing this since 1999.

I also publish several of my own Web sites so I understand the roles of site owner and site builder very well.

Depending on your needs I can deploy BlueClock's own CMS, create a bespoke system or use an off the shelf solution such as WordPress.

Many of my clients are trade and membership associations so I have a very good understanding of how these types of organisations work. I also have an understanding of the art gallery sector, small to medium e-commerce operations and the legal sector.

Gilbert West’s Specialties:

I produce high standards of (X)HTML and CSS to ensure forward compatibility with emerging standards, good accessibility and good SEO.

Server side programming using ASP and PHP

I use JavaScript and JavaScript libraries such as JQuery and ExtJS on a daily basis.

Use of APIs such as Google Maps. Strong interest and skills in online mapping and geo-location.
