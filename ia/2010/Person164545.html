Pinky Patel’s Summary

With training in communications and psychology, I began my career with Pfizer Inc as a professional healthcare representative. I earned a tremendous amount of success with Pfizer due to my ability to develop and maintain strategic partnerships with my clients and manage a diverse portfolio of products.

My success at Pfizer resulted in my receiving the Pfizer Global Health Fellowship which moved me to South Africa for six months to work with HIV prevention organization, mothers2mothers (m2m). At m2m, I served as a communications consultant, interviewing clients and writing their success stories for use in future donor and partner recruiting.

After returning from m2m, I enrolled at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health with the determination to work in areas of global health and gender equality. I will graduate in May 2010 with my MPH. My goal is to relocate to the DC area and establish myself as an advocate and health communications specialist.
