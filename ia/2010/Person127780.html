lorraine pursell’s Summary

You know how it is- busy parents balance demanding lives while raising their kids. They’re exhausted and worried they’re doing the best for their child. It takes every ounce of energy, soul and spirit to manage your life, and that’s why Lorraine loves supporting and empowering you in your greatest job on earth.

Parenting was the Lorraine’s biggest personal challenge as a single parent. She chose two parent mentors to show her the way at crucial times in her son’s life. Because their help was so valuable, she devotes herself now to supporting and guiding parents like you through the ups and downs of raising a child who thanks you later for how you raised him or her.

You’d probably feel more relaxed if you knew what’s ahead in your parenting journey so you can be prepared. Lorraine has a proven step-by-step method and systems for creating that peace of mind and family harmony you deserve.

You know your child can leave at 18- even newborn parents know the clock is ticking. Yet there’s so much involved in crafting your child’s capable, productive, happy life. How do you handle the day-to-day responsibilities, teach your child what they need to master for successful and still have a child who wants to spend time with you when they no longer have to?

Through Lorraine’s years of service as a family counselor and educational therapist, she’s helped thousands achieve self-esteem and family happiness with her systems that guarantee closeness with your child and a balanced life for yourself. All of this while doing what she calls “The Right Parent Thing” which isn’t always the popular thing with your child.

Lorraine’s son is 29, happily married with 2 children and still very close to his Mom- even though he’s a big, strong Marine. She figured the parenting game out and would love to share it with you.
It all starts now, though, no matter how young or old your child is. Visit www.ClosenessYouCrave.com for more info.
