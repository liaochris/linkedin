Jim Nealis’s Summary

Read the paper, listen to the news reports and you hear that the residential real estate market in Chicago and its suburbs is still reeling from the ecomonic down-turn.

Or is it? Between January 1, 2009 and December 31, 2009, my MLS reports that 49,161single family houses sold and closed in the area served by MRED [formerly MLS of Northern Illinois]. This is an increase of 8% over the 2009 figures. No matter how you cut it, that's a trend in the right direction indicating genuine LIFE in the Chicagoland local housing market!

Fact is, the Chicagoland real estate market right NOW offers a tremendous opportunity to own your DREAM home at a significantly lower price.

To take advantage, you need to do two things:

First- The current owner of the house you want has a problem. Put yourself in a position to SOLVE THEIR PROBLEM. To do that, you need to get your home sold. People tap into my 25+ years of experience in marketing and negotiating to do just that.

Second- Do your homework, identify the best opportunities and negotiate from a position of STRENGTH. Here again, use my experience to help you take advantage of today's opportunity.

Don't "wait until Spring." Do it NOW!
