Thalej Vasishta’s Summary

I have over 12 years experience dealing with UK immigration Law. I head up a team of 22 immigration lawyers split in different teams advising on all aspects of UK immigration and immigration to China and India.

I have consulted on the development of the Immigration module of the Legal Practice Course at Nottinham Law School.

I also train human resources professionals on employing overseas nationals and immigration law. I have also spoken at national Law Society conferences on the subject of business stratergy in relation to legal practices.

Thalej Vasishta’s Specialties:

Assisting businesses of all sizes with registration as Sponsors with the Home Office and follow-up stratergy, advising overseas businesses and individuals to set up in the UK. Advising outward to China and India.
