Luís Belerique’s Summary

I'm a portuguese 2D artist and 3D modeler that currently lives in Madrid, Spain.
Since childhood, I've always loved to draw, and had some skill but as I grew older, I went to college to study Astronomy at Oporto, Portugal.

Near the end of my graduation, I've started to pursue more seriously work as an artist, so I've worked in several projects like computer games, scientific illustration and comics.
Also, I've worked as a trainer, giving many introductory level workshops at modeling, shaders and animation using Blender.

Recently, I've moved to Spain to work as an intern in a 3D restoration of historical areas of Alcala de Henares, a city near Madrid. After the internship, I felt I needed to improve my skills as a modeler and also my drawing skills, so I've enlisted myself at an organic modeling course (learning mainly ZBrush) at CICE and also taking drawing classes at Artium Pena Art Academy, in Madrid.

Now I'm continuing this voyage of self-improvement and seeking work as modeler and/or concept artist at the videogames, movies or TV industries.

Also, I'm available for tutoring in Blender and 3D modeling related topics.
