Elayne Angel’s Summary

I was the manager of the first body piercing studio in the US back in the 1980s, when it was still the only piercing business in the country. I ran my own piercing specialty studio in the French Quarter of New Orleans for twelve years, starting in 1993. I closed following Hurricane Katrina and relocated to the Yucatan of Mexico. I've done approximately 40,000 piercings in my career. I'm known for being an innovator of piercing placements, techniques, and aftercare regimens.

I'm a monthly columnist for the body art industry trade publication, Pain Magazine.

I've written the first book in the English language to delve deeply into the subject of safe body piercing.
