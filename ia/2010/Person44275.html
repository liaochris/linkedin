Craig Silverman’s Summary

Primary career focus has been to build sales teams and generate revenue in growth companies. Having helped to build several successful firms and developed hundreds of salespeople, Craig is a leader who has a passion for recruiting, hiring, and training talented staff and then motivating them to be peak performers.

Craig is a frequent speaker at recruiting conferences such as National Association of Personnel Services - NAPS, CA Staffing Professionals -CSP, American Staffing Association - ASA, MAPSS, Fordyce Forum, NEAPS, and the Staffing Industry Executive Forum.
