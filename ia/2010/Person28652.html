Brian Layman’s Summary

Brian Layman is a full time independent WordPress consultant with expertise in PHP, JavaScript and MySQL development. A featured WordPress Developer in WordPress for Dummies (2nd edition) by Lisa Sabin-Wilson's, Brian left a 17 year career in the corporate world as a Software Engineer to earn a living making custom online solutions for WordPress and other Open Source online projects. Since 2007, Brian has worked with clients such as Borland International, b5media, Rhett and Link Internetainment and Darren "Problogger" Rowse. He conducts business out of http://eHermitsInc.com and posts code snippets at http://TheCodeCave.com.

He is also available to speak on topics related to website hosting, WordPress plugin and theme development, as well as general technologies. He conducts monthly presentations on various WordPress topics at the North East Ohio WordPress Meetup, and has spoken at conferences such as the BlogWorld New Media Expo and the Office Space Coworking Business Expo. He has conducted monthly community enrichment classes at a local church, and is the Board Chair of his local church resulting in experience moderate board and council meetings, perform skits and delivering the occasional Sunday morning sermon.
