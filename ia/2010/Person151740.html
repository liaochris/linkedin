Natalie Sisson LION’s Summary

I'm passionate about two things: Building leading edge global businesses from scratch & creating a world where there are just as many female entrepreneurs as men.

As Co-founder of ConnectionPoint & VP of Product Evangelism I'm intent on making FundRazr a world-class Social Commerce application that helps make peoples' lives easier.

Each day I focus on business development, customer relations and integrated social media campaigns to grow our business and expand our partnerships.

As Founder of WomanzWorld.com - The Ultimate Resource for Female Entrepreneurs, I aim to connect with, learn from and inspire aspiring and established female entrepreneurs.

I've accumulated over 9 years of rich experience across the full marketing spectrum including brand & product management, events, communications, business development, research and budgeting.

I've delivered both at an operational and strategic level in industries including FMCG, Safe Gaming, Education, and Refractive Technologies in New Zealand, England and Canada.

Among other things I have a couple of World Records too, in Dragon Boating and Beach Ultimate Frisbee.
