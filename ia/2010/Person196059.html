Steve Smith’s Summary

I have been in the safety industry through my education and experience for 21 yrs.
-9 years in Radition protection Sr. Health Physics Technician, Alara Technician, Site Manager, DOE projects.

-12 years business owner providing fire safety in commercial restaurants through exhaust system services. Operated in Ohio, Caribbean, and opened a branch office in Mexico. Performed contracts for many customers in multi-state projects. I dealt with all aspects of business ownership from product development, project managment, business managment, Human Resources, accounting/AR, total business managment. Our business involed local services, inter-state services, mulit-state services as well as out of the country.

--3 years, After selling our business to ABCO Fire Protection II have been the Business Development Manager for ABCO developing sales programs for our sales staff and overall business growth and currently the Executive Director of the NIFAST project. NIFAST is the National Institute of Fire and Safety Training. We are taking programs to market to train people in the residential setting through Home Inspectors and home occupant training along with College on and off Campus fire safety programs. Our programs are some of the first in the market place of there kind.
Continuing on as Director of Pure Force Inc.
