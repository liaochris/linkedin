Julia Rosien’s Summary

Social marketing strategist, buzz-builder, information junkie and avid blogger adept at straddling multiple mediums to nurture community and communicate the brand message.

As a team lead, I understand the importance of communication across new and changing mediums and teaching teams smart strategies for using them. I have extensive experience managing a large satellite staff as well as in-house direct and indirect reports. I work closely advertisers, designers, IT, usability consultants and SEO/SEM specialists while continually assessing work flow within company objectives.

I approach everything I do with a no-nonsense, honest attitude that fosters team building, online community development and product lifecycle marketing strategies that have gusto. I’m especially proud of my ability to understand the rules and then jump the gate to deliver on deadline. Multitasking in fast-paced environments while smiling is part of my native culture.

I’m an energetic speaker and present regularly at industry events to help retailers integrate new media into their traditional marketing mix.

Julia Rosien’s Specialties:

Lover of all things digital: web analytics, social media marketing (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, etc.) social media training. Social media strategization and implementation, product lifecycle marketing, blogging and SEO best practices, online community building and moderation, magazine and newspaper journalism.

Learning the rules and then using them to create new ones that help me deliver results on a shoestring budget.
