Bud First’s Summary

I am a highly motivated Team Leader - striving to become the absolute best I can be in all aspects of my life. My Team and I understand the Lifetime Value of Relationships. Our philosophy in our personal lives and our business lives is clear and simple - We are here for the sole purpose of serving and gaining the rich experiences this wonderful life has to offer. We love to serve those that want to be served by a team of professionals that care. Each team member is constantly working toward becoming the best that we can be at our individual tasks including our preferred lender and our preferred home inspector. When working with buyers in an exclusive buyer brokerage relationship - we use our skills to represent the buyer clients interests solely and exclusively - using our experience of over 1,000 successfully completed real estate transactions. When working with sellers in an Exclusive Right To Sell Listing Agreement relationship we represent our sellers solely and exclusively to sell their home fast and for top dollar. We understand the lifetime value of relationships and demonstrate that we care by our actions throughout the entire real estate process. Give us the opportunity to serve you or your kind referrals; we are sure you will be glad you did.
