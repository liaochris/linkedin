Michelle Walag VanDeBogert’s Summary

Michelle is “lightning” personified: brilliant energy hard-focused on transformation. Michelle’s own journey to discover what it means to live in the world of possibility provides her unique foundation to call that forth in others.

  • Founder

    Local Hope Network

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 1993 — Present (17 years 7 months)

    It started with a family fund drive years ago. And grew. Its wonderful because as people hear about this, they want to join.

    We are called to help people face some of life's most difficult challenges... This is a private giving network... I don't know if that is what others would call it... but I made it up and its what it is. The people in this create miracles. 100% of the money raised through the network goes directly to the families we help. Together we have helped many, including a mother who donated a kidney to save her son, families who have lost jobs, or a home in a fire, and even those who would not otherwise have had Christmas, I am humbled to facilitate the miracles the people in this network create.

    This is a calling. I have no other way to describe the process for each of the times I have been driven to help.The people helped often become a part of the network themselves once they are back on their feet.

    The process is simple -- When the need arises, we ask the family to set up a bank account in their name only. Then spread the word to the network and anonymous gifts are deposited directly into the bank account. There is no overhead. No salary. No fundraising dinners. Just people giving to one another.

    THAT my friends is what we do.

    If you think you want to be a part of the network, or have questions, email me: Michelle@LOFInc.org and I am happy to answer them and I hope you will join us.
