Peter Priel’s Summary

Peter Priel founded Tierra Technology out of a passion for technology and a desire to support the needs of local businesses. He has worked as an Information Technology specialist and manager for over 13 years. As Regional IT Manager at marchFIRST, he led a team supporting 400 users in a cross-platform (Windows and Mac) environment. As Director of IT at Cosmos Networks, he built a 50-node, server based Windows network. As Project Manager at Langtech Consulting, he managed infrastructures for 7 corporate clients with diverse network environments. As IT Manager at Anergen he supported 60 users and built a Wide Area Network integrating 3 remote offices. Peter is a Microsoft Certified Professional with a B.S. in Computer Science.

Peter Priel’s Specialties:

Managing Tierra Technology--a great technical support company in the San Francisco Bay Area, networking and security consulting to SMB including security audits and network and process retrofitting, recruiting talented technicians, designing easy-to-maintain network infrastructures, vendor management, heterogenous network integration and troubleshooting, technician management, systems integration; network design, building and support, keeping it simple and elegant.
