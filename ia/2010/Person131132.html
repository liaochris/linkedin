Marc Archambault’s Summary

I am a stone mason with a strong creative spark and a unwavering commitment to old school construction techniques. I am always seeking new opportunities and challenges to build new things and to learn new elements of the craft.

I am also a Grove trained graphic facilitator and recorder. I work as an educational designer with a strong emphasis on visual organization and graphics as teaching tools. I create Learning Murals, gigantic coloring posters for kids as well as business-oriented visual solutions. I am the lead designer for Positive and Productive Meetings.

And I take photographs and write.
