Albert Lo’s Summary

Having a strong mix of working on Agency and Client side, I have gained a wide range of experiences:

Professional and self-motivated UX/ Web Designer with front end development experience.

Strong organisational and leading abilities.

Constantly developing technical ability and expertise in digital media through self development.

Constantly observing developments and trends with online design and traditional mediums of design.

Flexible and dependable; capability to work part of a team or as an individual.

Hand code scaleable, cross-browser and cross-platform web-based (x)HTML, CSS sites. Translate detailed designs into validating, standard compliant table-less sites.
