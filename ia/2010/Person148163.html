Moira Hill’s Summary

Moira Hill specializes in visual and written communication: graphic design, illustration, project management, and editing. Sub-specialties include geography and fine art.

A former college instructor of graphic design and digital prepress, she excels at making difficult concepts tangible and clear.

"Whether you work with a regional, national and international companies or non-profit organizations, I will help you find, refine, clarify your message, investigate what your audience will respond to, and create the most effective way to communicate it through visual and written communication. I manage your project to keep track of deadlines, budgets and the overarching goal. I look forward to work with you."
