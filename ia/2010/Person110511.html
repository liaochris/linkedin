Karen Wise’s Summary

Experienced HR professional and coach. Worked at all levels within the Heathcare sector up to and including Director level.

I am currently working on developing a range of coaching interventions aimed at working mothers - hopefully to be launched in the Autumn in the Hertfordshire area. I am also preparing for the University of Hertfordshire's Coaching & Mentoring Annual Conference where I am co-delivering a breakout session, and a seminar in October.

I am particuarly intersted in areas relating to staff engagement and wellbeing: both as a coach delivering health-based coaching interventions (such as stress management workshops) and as a project manager developing healthy living initiatives within organisations.

Karen Wise’s Specialties:

Coaching, staff engagement and wellbeing project management, management development training, HR best practice, employee relations including disciplinary / grievance investigations.
