John Galante’s Summary

John Galante is president of AE Ventures, a business-to-business media company focused on creating and managing events and integrated media platforms for associations and other third parties. Prior to founding AE Ventures in July 2008, Galante led EH Publishing’s company-owned events business, including the award-winning Electronic House Expos and Worship Facilities Conference & Expo, from 1999-2008. He has additional experience as a publisher, editor and business development leader with EH and worked in event development and management for several years with the large independent event organizer, Reed Exhibition Companies. He also has a wealth of experience in the association realm, having served on a number of industry boards and committees, launched associations and worked as a senior staff member of the Consumer Electronics Association and chief staff executive of the Security Industry Association. He is a current member of the International Association for Exhibitions & Events Services Board as well as a member of their education and strategic planning committees.

John Galante’s Specialties:

Event and media product development, launch and management for growth; association launch, management, program development and promotion; business and market planning and consulting; market research; integrated marketing communications. Market expertise in custom and consumer electronics, residential construction, the B2B media business, retailing and contracting.
