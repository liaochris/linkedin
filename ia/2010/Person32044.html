Mary Rosenbaum’s Summary

Expert in the Art of Positioning You for Professional Success:
As a Certified Career Coach and Personal Branding Strategist I work with careerists and entrepreneurs helping them differentiate themselves in a crowded universe. I help clients create a marketing or action plan designed to enable them to reach their goals whether it is:
- building a business
- growing a franchise
- conducting a job search
- implementing a career change
- transitioning back into the workforce
- growing a professional network

Specific targeted issues include:
- Personal branding
- Create structure and strategy for your job search
- Identify, grow, improve and maximize your networking activities
- Website content development
- Brand Bios
- Evaluation of skills, experience and interests
- Resume critique
- Pre-Interview and interview preparation
- Negotiation techniques

Mary Rosenbaum’s Specialties:

Personal branding, career coaching, career management, grow your business, online id management, job search, networking, interviewing, and career transition.
