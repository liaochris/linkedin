Jeffrey Taylor’s Summary

I would like to start out by saying that I have been involved in Advertising and Marketing for most of my adult life. After receiving my degree in Marketing and Psychology back in 1985. I ran an advertising agency in NY.

I was burned out from the corporate rat race and packed my bags and moved to Scottsdale Arizona in the early 90's. It was at that point in my life I decided I wanted to be my own boss and work from home.

The Biggest Change in my life occurred in 1995!

In 1995 I ran into this newfangled thing called the internet and I have never looked back at the traditional advertising world.

I have done very well with internet marketing, but it was no cake walk. I have spent years learning, applying what l learned. Failing, and learning from my mistakes.

I created a new business in 2009 called http://www.LocalGoldmine.com designed to help Small Business Owners dominate their Local Market using internet marketing.

I am tired of all the internet scam artists and website designers taking advantage of Small Business owners offering overpriced marketing programs that don't deliver.

If you are a Small business owner in need of straight answers, I will talk with you about your business and look at your current website so you can get on the path of attracting new customers through effective local search marketing.

Times are tough enough and Small Business owners need affordable and effective solutions without all BS that is out there.

I do a lot of SEM (search engine marketing) consultant work for Small business owners here in Arizona. As the economy gets worse, many small business owners are realizing that just having a website is not enough.
