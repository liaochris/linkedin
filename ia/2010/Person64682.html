Emily Ross’s Summary

* Bestselling business author, freelance writer and consultant. I work on a portfolio of writing, media training and strategy for the public and private sector - in areas including biotech, resources, medicine, healthcare, education, local government, infrastructure, finance, NGOs, entertainment and professional services. Current clients include SmartCompany.com.au, Australian Gourmet Traveller, BRW Magazine, Media Manoeuvres, Australian Davos Connection, Diversity Management, the age (melbourne) magazine, Random House, HR Monthly, Hardie Grant, Harper's Bazaar, In the Black, TheCoolHunter.net, Floate Design and Herbario. Plus there are a whole lot of clients I can't talk about (from rock stars to gazillionaires).
* Co-author of 50 Great e-Businesses and the Minds Behind Them, Random House, September 2007. 100 Great Businesses and the Minds Behind Them, Random House, 2005.
Goal: Through my own work and through my consulting, create fantastic, interesting, useful content for the media.

Emily Ross’s Specialties:
