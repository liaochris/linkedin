Pam Kassner’s Summary

Pam Kassner is a five-time Ironman triathlon (2.4 mile swim, 112 mile bike, 26.2 mile run) finisher. That probably says more about Pam than an assortment of titles. As an Ironman, Pam has demonstrated passion, discipline, focus, commitment, problem solving, time management, multi-tasking, strategic thinking, risk taking and humor.


Pam knew when she crossed the finish line of her first Ironman, if she could do that, she could do anything. So in February 2005, Pam and her partner formed Super Pear Strategies, a marketing communications firm.

Clients that have engaged Super Pear include USG Corporation, Rockwell Automation, Brady Corporation, Northwestern Mutual, Marcus Investments, Financial Service Group, Kahler Financial Services, Witt Actuarial Services, Spring Bank, CG Schmidt Construction, Eppstein Uhen Architects, James H. Hoffman Builders, Work + Life Fit, Inc., FlexPaths, 100 Monkeys PR, AdviseAire, Centurion Data Systems, ISCorp-Integrated Systems Corporation, OneProspect Technologies, Schlitz Audubon Nature Center, Wisconsin State Fair Park, Bloodcenter of Wisconsin, Lake Express High-Speed Ferry, Careers Industries, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, Potawatomi Business Development Corporation, John Michael Kohler Arts Center, Marcus Center for Performing Arts, United Performing Arts Fund, Great Lakes Multi-sport and Running Expo, Nissan Xterra Championship Series, and YMCA of Metropolitan Milwaukee.

Before forming Super Pear, Pam’s 20-plus year career history included serving as Director of Corporate Communications at both Rockwell Automation & USG Corporation and as VP of Public Affairs at Bank of Montreal/Harris Bank. Pam began her career in broadcast journalism and continued freelance writing with work published numerous times in the Chicago Tribune and other publications.

Pam Kassner’s Specialties:

Marketing Communications; Thought Leadership; Content/Conversations Development; Corporate Responsibility and Sustainability; Reputation Protection and Issues Management, and Social Media.
