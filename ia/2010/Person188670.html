Shelton Mercer, III’s Summary

A captivating speaker, media strategist, technology expert and inspiring coach, Shelton regularly addresses groups from diverse sectors—on topics ranging from Living on Purpose and Leading With Passion to Strategic Visioneering and Social Mobilization.

Shelton is Principal and founder of The Mercer Advisory Group, a management consulting/executive coaching practice that innovatively guides businesses and organizational leaders to channel personal vision, corporate talent and assets into strategy and action in order to achieve maximum impact. With Different Drummer, a Hollywood-based media strategy firm, Shelton oversees social mobilization campaigns which leverage social media and the DD's Wildfire software to mobilize audiences, fans and supporters.

In the marketplace Shelton has risen quickly to executive leadership. During a celebrated tenure at Lucent Technologies/Avaya Inc. where he served as a senior consultant and project leader to Fortune 500 clients, Shelton collaborated with General Electric’s Corporate Strategy group to design and implement the “world’s largest” voice/fax messaging-over IP-network consisting of 130,000 users at 600 global sites. Moving to the non-profit sector in 2002, Shelton has been Chief Operating and Information Officer at Delaware Valley Association for the Education of Young Children, where he launched DVAEYC Computing Solutions a trailblazing, revenue generating social venture that provides comprehensive technology services to childcare programs and Program Officer at Greater Philadelphia Urban Affairs Coalition where he had oversight for the Blueprint for a Safer Philadelphia, a multi-million dollar youth violence reduction initiative.

Shelton is also an engaging emcee and media personality whose last project, “The Power of Love” a weekly talk radio show about relationships enjoyed a recent three-year run on 900AM WURD, Philadelphia, PA.
