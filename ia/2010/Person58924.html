Doug Ivison’s Summary

As a Developer:
-----------------------
Creative, responsive, and communicative data processing professional, with experience handling diverse responsibilities: project analysis, development, technical support, troubleshooting, and instructing & coaching others.

My broadest expertise is with direct-to-consumer retail systems -- these days, it's all about the internet -- especially Ecometry (formerly "MACS"). Ecometry is an application for ecommerce, order fulfillment, customer service, and inventory management. I've developed for Ecometry on multiple platforms: both HP3000 and Open Systems (Windows NT).

I also spent a few years consulting and programming with health insurance management systems -- for HMO companies -- mostly working with the AMISYS application -- membership, authorizations, claims, providers.
