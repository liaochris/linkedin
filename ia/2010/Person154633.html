Nicole Hockin’s Summary

Nicole Hockin has over ten years of success driving brand development and communications for both B2B and B2C companies. With notable experience in building ground-up marketing/public relations programs, she is well connected with extensive network of journalists, editors and producers.

Nicole authors the widely read www.travelsmartblog.com and is quoted regularly in various blogs, magazines and numerous network news stations across the country as well as nationally syndicated programming such as The Rachael Ray Show, the CW’s Daily Buzz, FOX's The Morning Show with Mike & Juliet, providing audiences with practical travel advice and recommendations.
