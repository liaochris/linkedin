tommy pesavento’s Summary

Tommy is a marketing visionary. Communication runs through his veins, whether he's producing short films or managing his own clothing line. Since his start in the creative industry 10 years ago, he's worked with artists, actors, non-profits, comedians, corporations and punks, sometimes all at the same time. He believes you can't solve a problem with the same mind that created it, so his fresh perspective is often all it takes to break down creative walls within an organization.

Lately he's been spotted at spark* Creative Services, where he designs packaging and advertisements, writes copy and conceptualizes marketing campaigns for large corporate clients and startups alike.

He currently writes for his personal blog, which is updated daily, along with managing Twitter accounts for himself (@jenandtommy) and his company (@getyourspark). He is also working on a travel book/journal, which will be a compilation of short stories from his adventures around the country.

His goals include getting published, continuing to provide excellent design concepts to companies for marketing and packaging, and helping make the world a better place through positivity and kindness. A smile goes a long way.
