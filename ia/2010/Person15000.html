Anthony Russo’s Summary

I am a family man first and foremost and work by integrity over money.

Are you looking to get a message out in a webinar format? Are you looking to increase productivity and communication in your business? Do you want to save money?

I can help with these issues and many more with the array of conferencing and collaboration tools provided by Infinity Conference Call.

There is one concern you have when you start a webinar or meeting. You want it to go the way you envision it. You know the experience you want to give your attendees, and at ICC we work to make that a reality. We offer full training and support with all our services. The type of hand-holding training, support, and service that lets you concentrate on your message, not the technology.

And speaking of technology, we utilize only the world-class Compunetix bridges for all of your calls. These are the same bridges used by NASA, the military, governments and anyone with a top concern for quality in their communications and conference calls.

As for WebConferencing, ICC NetMeet is the simplest, most full-featured tool you will come across on the Internet. No downloads or pre-requirements for your attendees to join you and hear and see your message. At the end of your session they leave with your information in their minds, not the technology used to receive it.

When it has to go right, Infinity Conference Call is the company to do it.
