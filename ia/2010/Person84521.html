Illiya Vjestica’s Summary

My experience in digital marketing is vast I’ve worked on every type of online campaign you can mention, whether it be social media, viral, e-commerce, SEO or PPC. I’ve always taught myself everything that I’ve wanted to know about digital, and learnt by being thrown into the deep end!

I love everything web related it is a passion I’ve had ever since I was 12. Nothing makes me happier than running a great viral campaign or producing a great website which fits a clients brief and provides excellent usability to customers. You’ll never meet a guy more passionate about the web than me, it’s more than a job to me it’s what I really enjoy doing.

I like to take the attitude that I treat my clients businesses like they were my own and I am honest, enthusiastic, passionate about what I do. I believe in developing the best websites, no matter what type of business, I am always keen to deliver the best service I can.

I’ve seen the Internet grow and change throughout my life, that is why I can say my understanding of it is a natural thing I’ve grown up with.
