Carlos Montero Morales’s Summary

After getting a top MBA degree with some really awesome colleagues (check my contact list, they are amazing) I have created, together with a very smart and active partner, a company to help lawyers and small law firms improve their business management and results. Things are changing in that sector, and it's a very exciting challenge.

I know everybody says this ;-) but I really find that my skills in marketing, management, technology, research, and communications are being used 100% in this new activity. Before, I have been a business consultant and industrial engineer; my business partner supplies the expert knowledge in law firm operations.

I have been working in international, interdisciplinary teams for 8 years. I am skilled in information research and analysis, planning, project management, documentation, communication, formation, CEO support, etc.

I have succesfully developed projects related to business processes, business software, music, cinema and intellectual property (author's rights), e-commerce, web 2.0 and innovation. I also have a broad basic overview of most other industries and sectors thanks to my studies.
