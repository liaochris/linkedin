Wendy Nan Rees - Pet Lifestyle Advisor’s Summary

Wendy Nan Rees- Pet Lifestyle Advisor™


Her career began when she founded Lip Smackers, Inc., a company dedicated to providing healthy all-natural treats to consumers concerned about their pets’ well-being.

Wendy has come full circle with her new book “The Dog Devotional: 365 Tips, Tricks and Techniques for Living a Rich Life with Your Dog”. It is a collection of many of Wendy’s most popular tricks and techniques used over her 25+ years as a Pet Lifestyle Advisor™, and is one of the most comprehensive “How to” dog books that has come along in many years. Other books published by Ms. Rees include:

• The Natural Pet Food Cookbook: Healthful Recipes for Dogs and Cats published in 2007, which is one of the few books sold by Whole Foods Markets.

• No Barking at the Table: Canine Recipes Most Begged For was self published in 1991 and sold over 5,000 copies with the first issue! The book was so well-received that No Barking at the Table 2 and No Catnapping in the Kitchen were not far behind.

• The Name Game is a collection of more than 100 celebrity essays along with over 1,000 suggestions of pet names. This fun book offers pet owners an entertaining and unique take on how and why people name their pets.

Wendy’s success as an author and dog-treat creator is no less amazing due to the fact that she was born with severe dyslexia and ADHD. Whenever Wendy is presented with seemingly insurmountable obstacles, she charges ahead to prove all naysayers wrong. Her previous successes have led to numerous television appearances and newspaper and magazine articles. She was the “Pet Lifestyle Advisor™” on Animal Planet’s Petsburgh, USA and was featured on The Home Shopping Network.

Presently, Wendy can be heard on her worldwide Internet radio show called, Wendy’s Animal Talk. You can hear her every Tuesday at 1 p.m. PST at www.healthylife.net. You can also read her weekly pet-tip articles in www.lovetoknow.com.
