Scott Barlow’s Summary

Vice President of Sales and Product Management with extensive record of achievement in channel sales and business development; strong technical expertise and domain knowledge in rapidly evolving hosted services (SaaS) markets, including email and Web security, archiving, and compliance.

Scott holds an MBA and a Masters of Science in Geophysics from Boston College.

Scott Barlow’s Specialties:

Proven track record increasing revenue, developing profitable sales operations, and exploiting untapped market opportunities. Highly successful in jumpstarting sales at startup companies.

Scott frequently speaks at industry events on SaaS, Hosted and Managed Services, and ways to maximize drag-along revenue opportunities.
