Sergi Mora’s Summary

Since 2003 Sergi has enabled many entrepreneurs perceive their businesses from new empowering perspectives & find untapped sources of cash.

As a member of the International Coaching Federation, Sergi specializes in the young company that's struggling with the transition from startup to smooth, well-run operation. You at that stage will need a new level of thinking, courage & accountability to take your company to the Next Level.

Sergi has been a Board Member of the EBAN (European Business Angel Network) and is still an active member. His experience in the Business Angels and Venture Capital sector provides him with clear insight on how to find winning business proposals. According to his experience, the 3 most important factors that any venture needs to achieve superior success are:

1) A competent, committed and credible team aligned to the same Great Vision.
2) Have an Investor as a partner that provides them with sound strategic advice.
3) Have an experienced coach who pushes them out of their comfort zone while keeping them accountable and focused on the right track.

Sergi Mora’s Specialties:

1) Turning wasted real state resources into an income generating opportunities while making a difference.

2) Coaching values-driven Entrepreneurs and pushing them to their Next level of Performance and Leadership.
