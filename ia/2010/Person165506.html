Praful Shah’s Summary

Praful brings in-depth expertise from various senior Business Development positions. He has significant global experience in mergers & acquisitions, partnerships, strategy development and marketing. He is a member of the Executive Advisory Board at the AT&T Technology Park & Institute, and a Board member at the Innovation in Insurance web portal. He is the founder of Jasi Inc., a company providing new revenue generation solutions within budget constraints to healthcare IT and telecom companies.

Praful has more than forty years of experience with a number of public, private, and non-profit organizations including Fortune 500 companies Bell Labs, Johnson & Johnson, Lucent, AT&T, Fiserv, and several other small/mid size companies. He has achieved firm successes and solid accomplishments working in R&D, Manufacturing, Product Management, International Business, Marketing, Operations and Business Development. He holds a patent for an improved healthcare product. Mr. Shah is also very active with a number of charitable organizations in support of the community. He received a Masters in Engineering from the University of Wisconsin and an MBA from the Rider University.
