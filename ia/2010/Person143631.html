Michael Berkley’s Summary

Served as CEO of SplashCast Media from 2006 to 2009, pioneering the concept of Social TV in partnership with Hulu, Sony BMG, and other premium content providers.

Involved in the next generation of online and in-home TV experiences, I am consulting on product strategy with a few online media companies. I'm also maintaining the TV News Stream blog covering all things related to online premium video (http://TVNewsStream.com).
