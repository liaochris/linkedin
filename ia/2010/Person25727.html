Bob Trench’s Summary

With more than 20 years experience in consumer electronics, technology and health care market categories, Bob Trench offers proven strategic communications expertise to clients internationally and in Minnesota.

Trench earned his Bachelor's degree from Hamline University and attended the Master’s Program and the University of St. Thomas.

A contributor and columnist for numerous industry publications, Trench has helped craft strategic and highly focused marketing communications for companies large and small. From successful startups to Fortune 100 companies, Trench’s experience includes work with leading brands in categories including:

Consumer Electronics: Ford, Volvo, GM, Qwest, AT&T, Best Buy, MB Quart, Hifonics, Target, Philips, Bel Canto Design, Marantec America, Clifford Electronics, Pro-Fit and Analysis Plus.

Health Care: CHF Solutions, American Telecare, HealthSciences, Rehabilicare, Compex Technologies, Chronic Care and MED CV.

Technology: 3M, Stellent, Stratasys, GearWorks, Rimage, ComSci, MTS Systems, JATCO, ESI Group, Adaytum, illbruck Acoustic and Dimension Printing.

Not-for-profit organizations have also enlisted Bob Trench including the Children's Cancer Research Fund, LiveStrong, Pay it Forward Fund, The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society, The Bridge for Youth and Team Borah.

Bob Trench’s Specialties:
