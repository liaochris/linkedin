Mike Adams’s Summary

I started my career in the video game industry as a game tester at Electronic Arts. I worked there for just short of a year. Next, I worked at Sony Computer Entertainment, America as a Game Test Analyst (fancy name for a game tester) for two-and-a-half years. In 2006, I changed my focus to recruiting for the video game industry.

My experience in the development side of the industry gives me a clear advantage staffing for it. My clients like me because I don't need to be handheld when they give me a req. My consultants like me because I treat them with the respect that can only come from somebody who has been in the trenches with them.
