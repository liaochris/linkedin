W Richard Garrett, III - LEED AP’s Summary

I am a Creative Problem Solver who works towards solutions to tough challenges. Learning and knowledge drive me; fueled by opportunity.

I pursued and achieved LEED® Accredited Professional status. In order to prepare for my exam, I captained a study group and shared many successes among us. I believe that it is within me to create positive change for our environment.

As a zealot for green products and lighting technologies I have used tools such as employee surveys and online-forum analysis to uncover learning potentials and drive foundational education within my peers. I am equally at ease behind a hammer or a computer mouse.

Sustainability is important to me and I apply those principles daily in my life. Within the Pacific Northwest, a lifestyle of sustainability exists in which I can earnestly relate. As a camper, kayaker, and photographer I have seen how humans directly affect their environments. I follow practices such as Leave No Trace.

In the print and advertising industries I have played an integral role as both a "go-to" person for creative resources and for my ability to "get-it-done". I've helped many teams brainstorm and carry out dynamic marketing campaigns to make their clients stand out from the crowd. My experience starts from the end of a production line and extends beyond the printed page to design, holistic project management, sales, training, and customer service to name a few.

I seek to collaborate with a team of talented people who value loyalty, learning, and leadership. I am eager to pursue the next steps towards joining this group.
