Mitch Wagner’s Summary

I'm a technology journalist, professional blogger, and social media strategist.

I write technology articles that help readers solve business problems, and press releases, case studies and white papers that get customers.

I can build social media and Internet marketing campaigns that tell your story, enhance your reputation, and drive sales.

I'm expert at blogs, Facebook, Twitter, social media, Web sites, video and podcasting--whatever tool is right for the job.

I pioneered blogging for United Business Media, launched InformationWeek on Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn, managed InformationWeek newsletters and its Web sites, as well as writing and editing articles.

I'm author of the Computerworld Tool Talk Blog, where I write about Apple, Google, social media, and other tools to help people get work done.

I'm social media director at Palisade Systems, where I'm writing blogs, case studies, active on Twitter, and otherwise working to build the brand for the emerging business-to-business security and compliance vendor.

I host Copper Robot, an interview podcast about books and ideas.
