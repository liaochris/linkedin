Timothy Knight’s Summary

I have always been involved in the fitness industry in some capacity. I started as a personal trainer in 1989 on Nautilus Equipment. I competed as a bodybuilder in 1987, 1989 and 1993. I competed as a powerlifter in 1990, 1991, 1999 and 2004. Fitness was always a "side-job".

My professional experience includes fast food and retail management, telecommunications and mortgage broker. I've worked for giants such as MCIWorldCom and McDonald's. I managed Radio Shack and did mortgages for First Horizon, a bank, and smaller mortgage brokers.

Being a personal trainer allows me to directly impact the lives of my clients. Being a fitness director allows me to reach a larger audience; not only our club members, but also their guests, our visitors and anyone else who reads our monthly newsletter.
