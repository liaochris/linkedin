Cathy Lewis’s Summary

Senior executive with 25 plus years of experience in sales, marketing, product development and delivery in the copier / printer and additive fabrication industries. Recently appointed to the position of VP, Global Marketing for 3D Systems reporting to the CEO. In this position I will leverage my extensive sales and marketing background to extend the brand, create multi-channel marketing initiatives and position the company to capture significant new business in this exciting growth industry. 3D Systems acquired Desktop Factory where I had been CEO for the prior 3 years as an important addition to their existing business. We have a shared vision wherein low cost 3D printers that fabricate physical objects from 3D solid object modeling software (CAD), 3D scanners or websites specializing in downloadable 3D content, will become ubiquitous over the next 7- 10 years. These products, including the Desktop Factory 3D printer, will retail starting at under $5K creating significant market potenial. Longer term as enablers become available and prices decrease further we see an opportunity analogous to desktop publishing 20 years ago. In this era of personal customization consumers and hobbyists will have access to their own Desktop Factory enabling custom products, personalization and the ability to 'print' a replacement part for the home at the click of a mouse.
