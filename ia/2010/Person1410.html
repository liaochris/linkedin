Amy Beaird’s Summary

Highly motivated research engineer specializing in materials characterization and process design. Ability to apply a wide range of analytical techniques to solve complex problems such as hydrogen storage, landfill gas-to-energy and environmental catalysis. Demonstrated leadership and service as a mentor, president of a graduate student organization and teaching from the middle school to undergraduate level. Interpersonal and communication skills evidenced in peer-reviewed journal articles, presentations at national conferences and extensive collaborations. Proficient in Microsoft Office, Origin and Minitab Statistical Package.

Amy Beaird’s Specialties:

chemical engineering, materials characterization, reaction engineering, electrochemistry, alternative energy, biofuels, hydrogen storage, reactor design, thermodynamics, TGA, DSC, Raman, XRD, DVS, NMR
