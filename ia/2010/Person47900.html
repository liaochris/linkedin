Daniel Watson’s Summary

I have a BA in Youth Ministry/Christian Education from Johnson Bible College in South Knoxville. Upon graduating from college I served for seven years as the Associate Minister of Outreach at West Towne Christian Church. From 2002 to 2009 I served as a volunteer chaplain with the Knox County Sherrif Department.

In 2007 after resigning from West Towne Christian Church my wife Mandy and I founded the Restoration House of East Tennessee. I now serve as the fulltime executive director and am actively engaged in the development of a new site include 15 units, three offices, and community space..
