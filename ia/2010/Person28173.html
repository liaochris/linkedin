Brian Campkin’s Summary

My professional goal is to be in a rewarding sales management position that will offer an opportunity for professional growth and advancement based on a demonstrated strength in account management and business acumen.

My personal goal is to continue my role as a professional speaker. The following is my speaking bio:

Brian Campkin is an example that the right attitude can make a winner out of anybody. His competitive spirit helped him to overcome his diagnosis of heart disease at the age of 46. Having had a triple by pass in 2007, Brian took this as an opportunity to take charge of his life and truly take advantage of his 2nd chance. In his first year of recovery he walked a 60KM marathon and also ran both a 5K and a 10K. He also got back to his passion for both tennis and golf. Brian has shared his amazing journey with thousands of people. through his volunteer efforts for the Heart and Stroke Foundation As a result, Brian inspires audiences to transcend their limitations, and strive for those qualities which enable them to achieve their objectives.
