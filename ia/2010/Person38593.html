Christa Faulkner’s Summary

I am a freelance designer and artist based in Louisville, KY. I've been creating art and designs for many years, but transitioned to professional freelancer in early 2009. Most of my experience in design and web development comes from self study. For right now I doing all the graphics, web development, project management, etc. for hmmbird, but I am definitely interested in connecting with other designers and programmers to collaborate with.

Background
I graduated high school in 96' and taught ballet while attending school in Eastern Kentucky. I completed my BA in Studio Art in 2001 and then moved to Louisville to complete a Masters in Expressive Therapies. I worked as an Art Therapist/Case Manager for several years alongside of doing freelance work in design and art. I began to freelance professionally at the beginning of 2009 and started hmmbird design.

Interests
I general I get excited about anything related to the arts, design and the environment, but currently I'm focused on enhancing my Wordpress development, programming skills and understanding of green design and green technology.
