Alex Lely’s Summary

Alex has extensive experience in Sports Journalism working as a commentator, interviewer, writer of columns and production assistant on location. His specialties are pool, snooker, three-cushion billiards and rugby.

As of late, he is extending his field of work to the political arena. He succesfully coordinated the election campaign of a Member of European Parlement in the elections of may '09 and is now Gemeenteraadslid in IJsselstein.

Untill 2006, Alex roamed the world as a professional poolplayer, winning many titles: World Pool Master, multiple European Champion, multiple Eurotour winner and former European nr.1, member of team Europe in the Mosconi Cup. In 2008 he captained (non-playing) the European Side to a historical 11-5 victory.

Job interests: journalism, public administration.
