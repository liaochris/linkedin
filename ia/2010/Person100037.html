JoAnne Berg’s Summary

As an entrepreneur who has owned and run businesses, and successfully juggled family, business interests, community service, and personal development, I am uniquely qualified to help self employed professionals, small business owners, and non-profit managers succeed in business, enjoy their families, and give back to the community.

My business, Peer Coaching Network, Inc., offers the following services:
• Advising business owners and senior management
• Providing big picture business planning and management expertise when needed.
• Teaching, both one on one and in small groups
• Facilitating meetings and strategy sessions.
• Speaking and presenting on various topics

I blog on business and financial management subjects at www.ArtofSmallBusiness.org

I also moderate a discussion group here on LinkedIn, http://tiny.cc/iceUB

and on Facebook: http://www.facebook.com/ArtofSmallBusiness

My approach is always to facilitate the client’s process; there is no “one size fits all” approach. I take a big picture view, and then work from there to re-position a business to be more successful.

My community service background includes many years of probono consulting and board level service within the local not for profit community. I have served on the boards of an arts organization; a social service organization, and the local Chamber of Commerce, as well as been active in various community service organizations such as Rotary.

As a Board member and Partner of San Diego Social Venture Partners, I chair the Business Operations Resource Team, which provides high level consulting expertise to local not for profit entities. My goal is to increase the organization's capacity to efficiently and effectively operate the business side of their organizations, ultimately enhancing their ability to achieve their mission.

I am also a licensed CPA in the State of California.
