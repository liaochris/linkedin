Doug Schaer’s Summary

As an entrepreneurial sidebar to consultancy, Silent Partner Media, helped form The John Daly Clubhouse Collection, a line of golf ball-shaped BBQ grills and accessories in partnership with PGA Tour-star John Daly, a project we are patiently waiting to launch in earnest.

Other projects have included The Art and Science of Pitching, a direct marketing campaign/infomercial for next generation pitching instruction with Dr. Tom House and Nolan Ryan; as well as Race and Sports Specialty Products, a unique, high concept NASCAR licensed product manufacturer, featuring the #48 Jimmy Johnson and #24 Jeff Gordon marks via Hendrick Motorsports.

Formerly, as President and CEO of Concept Entertainment Group ("CEG"), spearheaded the acquisition and strategic integration of various properties including an array of management companies, infomercial projects, TV and feature film projects, licensing, manufacturing and distribution companies, as well as other industry related investments.

New Jersey native. Received a B.A. in Government from Wesleyan University in Connecticut. Was a four-year varsity letterman and two-year basketball team captain.

Following graduation, played and coached professional basketball overseas.

In May 1998, became a certified player agent with the MLBPA, received law degree from the University of Southern California and became a member of the California Bar.

Currently reside in Los Angeles with wife Karin and daughter Jordyn.
