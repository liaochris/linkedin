Tara St James’s Summary

My design degree has a specialization in menswear, however since obtaining this degree in Montreal I have worked designing and producing collections for both men and women for sportswear, denim and urban brands. I started my career as a design assistant in the womenswear department at Parasuco Jeans in Montreal. I have since designed a menswear collection for Dex Clothing, also in Montreal, and a womenswear collection for Spiewak Canada. These two latter collections being designed developed and produced alongside one another. I am presently designing a complete men’s and women’s collection in New York called Covet, an eco-luxe brand that marries sustainable, eco-friendly fabrics with fashionable styling.

I have extensive experience working with merchandising teams in Hong Kong, Korea, Taiwan, India and Brazil, as well as Canada and the United States. I have traveled throughout Asia, Europe, North & South America to oversee development, production and design procedures. I am very comfortable traveling independently and have done so often in the past. I have an excellent rapport with all the factories with whom I've worked and I believe they would readily compliment my organizational skills and efficiency based on the experience we have had while working together. I reach my deadlines on time, reply to questions immediately and always provide as much information as possible during the development stages in order to facilitate the development process.
