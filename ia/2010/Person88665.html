James Yancey’s Summary

James Yancey is an entrepreneur who has owned and operated several businesses. James knew at an early age that he did not want the lifestyle that a job could provide. After graduating from Towson University James began working in the mortgage and insurance industry. While working in the mortgage industry James began to invest in real estate. The success he created through investing allowed him to open a mortgage brokerage. James continued to evolve his business and created a real estate sales team. His focus was the lifetime value of a lead and he's brought this philosophy to his network marketing business along with the desire to develop deep relationships with his clients, colleagues, friends, and family. James' mortgage blog is located at http://www.mortgagelendingman.com

When James was first introduced to network marketing he knew that he had found a way to build a lifestyle that provided more freedom than his previous ventures allowed, and help others do the same. James got off to a fast start using traditional networking methods. However, he did not have the type of duplication that he expected, desired, or wanted for himself or his team. During this time the real estate market had almost come to a complete halt. This gave James the opportunity to implement some of the online techniques he'd used to build his previous businesses and apply these to network marketing. He then stumbled upon attraction marketing and has since launched http://www.JamesYancey.net and http://www.TheMLMSponsoringForumula.com in order to help guide and teach others the formula he used to create success and freedom through the profession of network and online marketing.
