Beth Newman’s Summary

As an image consultant and life coach, Beth Newman is dedicated to helping people of all ages succeed. She assists those who desire more - a better job, a better relationship, a better life.

Beth conducts individual and group sessions and seminars throughout the Houston area. She also conducts courses and consultations online. Beth's a motivational speaker, and works with several schools in Fort Bend County, teaching etiquette and success strategies.

Beth's seminar, Winning Working Ways, shows companies how to get the edge over the competition through office etiquette, professional dress, stress management, and more.

Through First Style, Beth works with young women, providing them with information that will transform and empower them as they grow into adulthood. Girls 11-14 have an opportunity with her to learn about appropriate fashion selections, etiquette, and healthy lifestyle choices.

Beth’s an educator and journalist, and her work has appeared in several publications, including Sugar Land Scoop, River Walk Current,Sassy Sistah, and Focus on Women.

She's producer and host of "Style Watch with Beth Newman", an Internet series that offers fashion and lifestyle tips. Beth is also editor of the ezine, Elegant.

“My mission is simple,” she says, “I truly want to help people look, feel, and live their absolute best!”

Beth lives in Houston with her husband, a local musician, and two very spoiled cats.

Personal and professional mentoring, corporate workshops, motivational speaking, broadcast journalism, print journalism.
