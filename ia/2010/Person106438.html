Joya Helmuth’s Summary

I have been a self-employed graphic designer since 1998, with a strong commercial printing background. My portfolio, viewable at www.sparksite.net/portfolio.php, includes work for a variety of individuals, small and large businesses, non-profits, and higher education institutions. I specialize in identity design and print projects including packaging, corporate brochures, fundraising campaigns, magazine design, and related print items. As a self-taught web designer, I have produced several websites for clients as well as my own business; and also have produced email marketing campaigns.

In 2007, I decided to channel my love for all things paper into opening a retail shop specializing in stationery, paper, and invitations. My goal is to bring new products to the South Bend area, including current offerings like Italian stationery and leather hardbound journals, fine pens, paper fragrances, beautiful letterpress and engraved stationery, glass pens, and custom invitations. Our expertise, experience, customer service and personal attention sets us apart from local and online competitors.

As a strong believer in the value of independent business to our communities, I co-founded ShoLo in 2008 in South Bend. Our community has been extremely supportive of this effort and we couldn't be more thrilled. More about ShoLo on the website, www.sholo.org. Go independents!
