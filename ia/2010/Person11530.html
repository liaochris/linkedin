Andrew Choi’s Summary

For the past three year, my focus has been in the energy industries, with hands-on work specifically in the GreenTech/CleanTech spaces (smart-grid, solar, energy management). I am committed to dealing with a company’s most valuable asset….their people. From start-ups to industry giants, the people make the company! I co-founded ZanderGreen to provide my clients with the highest level of service by providing them with the most experienced and hard to find candidates in our niche space.

I am the eyes and ears for executives and engineers that would like to explore new career opportunites in a highly confidential manner. I work closely with my candidates to understand their career goals and the best way of obtaining those goals. I handle your resume and job search with the utmost privacy and confidentiality. Our team is savvy and discreet in finding you the best opportunity available. We work directly with the hiring managers to quickly get you feedback on your resume and expedite your interview process. We’re here to be your voice from inside the company and to get you the best job offer possible. We also discuss any and all opportunities with you…before your resume is ever submitted. All of our positions are employer fee paid, so our services are always free to our candidates.

ZanderGreen Technologies has successfully helped Executives and technology professional’s fast-track their careers with positions in leading organizations. Our clients range from publicly traded stalwarts to the most stealthy start-ups. Feel free to search through all of our open positions and contact me today about the next step in your career: www.ZanderGreen.com

If you would like to be kept in confidential contention for opportunities matching your background, please send your resume in a word attachment to: Andrew@ZanderGreen.com. If you know of a friend or colleague seeking a new opportunity, please refer them to us! Zandermax has the best REFERRAL BONUS in the business.
