Connie Cornelius’s Summary

I am passionate about saving our amazing attractions and helping communities retain their “sense of place”. Tourism can be as damaging as it is an economic engine – so I would like to share my marketing expertise and strong tourism background with fellow Linked-in connections.

In addition to a MBA and 20+ years experience in marketing management, I have worked with tourism destinations and travel suppliers in more than 30 countries. In 2004, I earned a Master in Tourism Administration - Destination Management to better assist communities and attractions being threatened by over use.

Coupled with my solid research skills, I can help travel and tourism officials develop a balance between nature and tourism. For example I can:
 Conduct an assessment of tourism facilities and infrastructure
 Evaluate tourism services for maximum visitor satisfaction
 Help plan tourism products and improve way-finding
 Develop niche marketing strategies to increase visitation
 Plan for sustainable tourism development

Organizations interested in my consulting services include:
 Convention & Visitor Bureaus
 Economic development agencies
 Tour attractions
 State and local tourism organizations
 Businesses in the travel and tourism industry

In addition to providing consulting services, I also develop customized tour itineraries for private groups. I design and implement leisure travel programs for:
 Professional or Educational Associations
 Corporate or Trade Organizations
 Charitable or Fraternal Organizations
 Museum Membership Groups
 Religious, Gender and Ethnic Groups

Smart companies know that nothing motivates and excites like the anticipation of travel! By outsourcing to me, organizations save time and money leaving the logistics to a professional. Keep me in mind if you need to do raise funds for a non-profit – travel programs can be a great development tool! See Affinity Group Tours for more ideas.
