Michael Mathews’s Summary

Dr. Mathews is a co-founder of CynergisTek and directs all of CynergisTek’s technical information security consulting services. Dr. Mathews brings over 12 years of experience in architecture and security assessments, risk analysis, policy development, network security, and host-based security across the financial, healthcare, pharmaceutical, transportation, publication, Internet, telecommunication, petrochemical, government, and defense industries. He is the former Chief Security Officer at Parkland Health and Hospital System. Mike also served as the Master Security Architect for Exodus Communications and Senior Network Engineer for TRW Space & Defense. Dr. Mathews has co-presented the HIPAA Certification in Healthcare Security workshop for the Healthcare Information Management Systems Society, the course for certifying individuals as qualified to manage HIPAA security in covered entities, for the last two years. He has earned a Ph.D. in Chemical Physics from Tulane University, an MBA from Ashland University, and a B.S. in Chemistry and Philosophy from Spring Hill College.
