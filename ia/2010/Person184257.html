Scott Fogle’s Summary

In the last nine years, Scott Fogle, and his partner, Tim Wise, have grown Advocate Networks to serve over 400 enterprise and service provider clients with a great team of people. Focusing on consulting in the communications and data center markets, and managed TEM services, Advocate Networks has earned a reputation for quality, integrity and results. The company has been recognized for 4 consecutive years (2007, 2008, 2009 and 2010) as part of the INC 5000 Fasting Growing Private Companies in the United States.

Before co-founding Advocate Networks in 2001, he was an executive vice president, sales and operations for Dantis, Inc., a managed hosting services provider in Chicago. Before Dantis, Scott was a senior vice president of marketing and sales for two years at Rapid Link Communications, an international telecom service provider. Before Rapid Link, he spent three years at Scientific-Atlanta and eight years at AT&T/NCR.

Scott received a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering with honors from Georgia Institute of Technology in 1987.

Scott Fogle has 23 years experience in international business development, management and leadership within the communications and computing industries. During his career, he lead marketing and sales for the professional services business of a major computer manufacturer, developed and negotiation major interconnection agreements for a global VoIP network, and managed operations in the US, Europe, Asia and Latin America.
