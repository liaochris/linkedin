Larry Dearing’s Summary

Over the years I've held a variety of technology and support positions from Senior Support Technician and Network Administrator to Freelance IT Consultant and entrepreneur. My background has allowed me to develop a broad ranging skill set. I'm well versed in supporting both internal end users and external customers in areas including email support, individual and enterprise backup and restore solutions, server support and administration, network support, and Active Directory administration.

In addition to technology support, my roles have allowed me to develop peripheral skills and experience in account administration and management, project management, training and course design, and marketing.

In 2005 I started Navisouth.com, a website services and consulting company rebranded in 2008 as Web Marietta. We focus on web presence for individuals and small business including Joomla development, site provisioning and administration, optimization, and marketing. Web Marietta enjoys success and growth thanks to supportive friends, fantastic customers, and excellent strategic partners.

Larry Dearing’s Specialties:

Other areas include off-site and online based technologies for personal and business productivity such as email, collaboration, data backup, storage, and sharing solutions.
