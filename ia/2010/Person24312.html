Zhu Shen, Ph.D., MBA’s Summary

Dr. Zhu Shen is an award-winning business leader, author, media personality & thought leader in cross-Pacific life science business. She is the CEO of BioForesight, a strategic consulting company providing partnering, financing & outsourcing advisory, cross-cultural training, and media/public relations services to clients worldwide.

Dr. Shen is an organizer, speaker, and chair of life science business conferences. Her feature articles appeared on Pharmaceutical Executive, PharmAsia News, Ernst & Young Global Pharmaceutical Reports, and BioExecutive International. She has been featured on CBS Evening News with Katie Couric, Business Week, Fox News, Los Angles Times, China Economic Review, Genetic Engineering News, Cornell Enterprise, San Diego Union Tribune, San Diego Business Journal, Voice of San Diego, San Diego Daily Transcript, and ASIA Media.

Dr. Shen sits on the Editorial Advisory Board of PharmAsia News and serves on the board of SABPA, the largest and most influential Chinese American life science professional organization in Southern California. She also serves on the BIOCOM Asia Task Force Steering Committee, the Athena San Diego Bioscience Committee. She is the Vice Chairman of the Advisory Board of the America Chinese Culture & Education Foundation (ACCEF).

Dr. Shen was honored with the Annual Asian Heritage Award in 2006 and the SABPA Achievement Award in 2006 and 2007. She was also nominated for the annual Athena Pinnacle Award in 2007, 2008, and 2009; and the 2008 San Diego Business Journal’s “Women Who Mean Business Award”.

Dr. Shen also held positions at The Wilkerson Group/ IBM, Bayer, Chiron, and most recently as Vice President of Business Development at ItherX Pharmaceuticals (formerly Immusol), with increasing responsibilities in business development, new product/ technology strategic planning & marketing, corporate strategy, branding/ new product launch, investor relations, public relations, and corporate communications.
