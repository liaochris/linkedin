Jayne Hannon’s Summary

Proven senior consultant, 13+ years IT recruitment experience operating UK-wide. Uncompromisingly ethical in my approach to both candidates & clients - I always tell the truth & am a firm believer in transparency.

We monitor metrics quite closely & our annual review of candidate 'stick rate' is imminent. Across the pond, stick rate is defined as when a 'placed executive has remained in the same, similar, or elevated position for 6 months or more with a company.'

We don't really perceive this to be a great ROI so have our own definition of stick rate: the % of permanent candidates placed during the previous year who are still employees of those companies today.

My personal stick rate in Q4 2008 (covering the period Jan-Dec 2007) was 81% (in the US this figure would be 96%).
