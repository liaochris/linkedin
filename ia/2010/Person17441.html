Ashley Daly’s Summary

I currently work for GolinHarris, a global PR firm, on the BP Fuels, TRAVELCLICK and several other consumer brand accounts. I also started my own professional organizing business, Arrange and Change in January 2010.

Organizing and design have always been a passion of mine and I’ve looked forward to starting Arrange and Change for years now. To learn more please visit: www.arrangeandchange.com

I’ve spent the last four years working in marketing and public relations at global agencies or in-house. Most of my experience is in the oil and energy sector, specifically conducting PR and media campaigns as well as events for Shell Oil and BP Fuels. I also worked in-house for a jobber in Kansas City, CarterEnergy where I developed a new website and branding campaign for the company.

I graduated from Southern Methodist University in Dallas, TX with a BA in Communications and minors in Statistics and Art. Yes, I’m right and left brained!
