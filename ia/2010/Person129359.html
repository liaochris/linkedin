Lynn Forbes’s Summary

Media old and new has been my career; digital media has been my passion. I have extensive executive experience at startups, and at top companies, including Microsoft, Disney, Scripps Networks and others. My core expertise is creating and executing compelling digital strategies that include all website components – editorial, video, community, newsletters, online marketing - social media, ecommerce, business development, audience retention and traffic growth. I am considered an outstanding leader and I have managed teams of up to 150 people, pulling in all the appropriate creative and technical experts necessary to make a plan fly.
