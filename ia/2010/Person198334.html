Suzann Robins’s Summary

Improve all kinds of relationships through my book and talks about Exploring Intimacy: Cultivating Healthy Relationships through Insight and Intuition.

Improve productivity by teaching your employees simple keys to happiness.

I provide a useful background from Western Psychology and combine it with Eastern Philosophy to aid your understanding of self-actualization.

Focusing and creative visualization are valuable skills to learn more about selfloving and loving others. People can only love you as much as you love yourself.

As an energetic high energy speaker, I conduct workshops, seminars, and conferences from coast to coast. I am also willing to present a keynote for your business.

The take home message is that we need to feel healthy before we can have healthy relationships.

Suzann Robins’s Specialties:

Working with college students around issues of sexuality and healthy relationships. Also interested in Permaculture, Intentional Communities, health promotion and the hospice movement.
