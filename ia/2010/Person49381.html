David Boudreau’s Summary

David Boudreau is a relationship manager with Travelex Global Business Payments (TGBP). In this role, David oversees a portfolio of about 85 clients that conduct over $155 million in cross-border, foreign currency transactions each year. Prior to joining TGBP, David was an institutional trade desk analyst with Thinkorswim and a trader with JP Morgan Invest LLC.

David attended both Boston University and the Harvard University Extension School, where he was an economics concentration. He is involved in several non-profit organizations including the Boston Museum of Science, Greener Labs, and the Ugandan Wildlife Education Centre.
