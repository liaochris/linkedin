Matt Davies’s Summary

How I recruit the top talent:

When it comes to recruiting, I am all about quality instead of quantity. Each client and job opportunity is different and have different needs they expect to be met.

My system is to get a solid understanding of the requirements and needs from the client. Then I take the 3-4 needed requirements and find ONLY candidates that have those requirements. I always ask clients which 1-2 asset qualities they would consider a "superstar" candidate for their specific job situation.

From there on in, I only find candidates that have ALL 3-4 key requirements or candidates that have that plus 1-2 asset qualities.

This system allows me to rank candidates and only refer the top talent.
