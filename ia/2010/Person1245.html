Andrea Basagoitia’s Summary

Born in Valparaiso, Chile, I have lived in four beautiful countries: Venezuela, Spain, USA, and, of course, Chile. For many and different reasons, there is a piece of my heart in each one of them.

By the age of 30 I was a Pharmacist and had earned a Ph.D. in Physical Organic Chemistry. I married my wonderful husband and came to live in San Diego without enough knowledge of the English language to further develop my career. I worked as a Pharmacy Technician and as a QA Associate doing Failure Investigations for a Biotech Company. I also studied Regulatory Compliance until I felt that my communication skills were good enough to look for new challenges.

With a desire to expand my knowledge and skills, I learned tax preparation and worked with H&R Block for a season. Then I became proficient in business administration practices through the Small Business Association and started my first company in 2004, In Context Compliance consulting for pharmaceutical companies and clinical research organizations.

After completing many consulting projects, I began longing for patient interaction again. As part of the Kidney Transplant Team in a well-known hospital in San Diego, I went back to work as an Intern Pharmacist. My Spanish was a blessing for the job, but my thirsty mind was not fully satisfied. The desire to help people attain greater levels of health and well-being kept me searching and looking for solutions.

In 2006, after a long visit to my hometown in Chile where I learned about the process of extraction of antioxidants from red wine, an exciting new world opened up. It was then that I made the decision to create my second company. Red Wine Therapy, Inc. in order to make Red Wine Extract—with its unparalleled antioxidant properties—widely available in nutritional and cosmeceutical products. With our health system being more a "sickness driven system", I found a passion for getting information about prevention and natural cures, out into the world.
