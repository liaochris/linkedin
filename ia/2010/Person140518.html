Résumé de Charles Melidosian

Chief Information Officer with significant Operations and General Management experience known for developing long-term business solutions and implementing an array of strategic initiatives in challenging environments. Over 20 years of experience in firm management, financial operations, project management, business process reengineering and systems consulting.

Proven track record in understanding current business challenges and predicting future market trends to design, create and implement game-changing product and service offerings to maintain competitive advantage, growing profitability and market share.

Highly disciplined, visionary leader focused on developing business strategies, managing operations and staff, and improving processes, workflow and organizational structures. Demonstrated success at collaborating with fellow senior executives and boards to achieve measureable results on time and within budget.

Key strength in recruiting and retaining high performing staff. Focus on developing and empowering employees who take ownership for realizing goals and objectives. Known for fostering a high energy work environment in which employees are trusted and valued, resulting in optimal performance and high job satisfaction.

Skilled at initiating and solidifying strategic alliances, contract negotiations, acquisitions, joint ventures, license agreements, intellectual property protection, technology transfers and mergers that optimize and secure the firm’s value. Excellent presentation and communication skills to technical and non-technical audiences of all levels.

Demonstrated success at designing and implementing operational infrastructure in the face of business challenges and financial constraints. Adept at developing and executing business plans across multi-site business operations.
