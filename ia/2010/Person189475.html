Shyam Panda’s Summary

As a Product Manager for Oracle Business Intelligence Applications, Shyam manages Oracle Supply Chain and Order Management Analytics. He manages all aspects of the product line beginning from interfacing with outbound parties like Customers, Prospects, Analysts, Sales, Pre-Sales, Consulting to working with inbound stakeholders like Engineering, QA, Documentation and Tech Support. He can build a product from scratch and launch it to success in the marketplace and is capable of setting up the whole product management processes from ground zero, if there is need for one, including recruiting and mentoring a product management team.

Shyam has managed many customer relationships resulting in successful references. Shyam has been making public presentations during Oracle Open World since 2007.
