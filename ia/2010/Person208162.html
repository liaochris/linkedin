Trish Kirby’s Summary

Trish Kirby started her professional sales career back in 1989 whe she ventured into the real estate market. After six years of helping individuals find and purchase or sell their home, she entered the automotive market for a year. In 1996, she began her career broadcast advertising. Successfully selling for top ranked stations in the Baltimore market for 13 years, and a short stint in print media - Trish broadened her knowledge and gained extensive experience in vast areas of marketing, from traditional radio campaigns, to online and outdoor billboards, as well as event marketing and non traditional revenue.

Additionally, her short but successful tenure with Cavalier Telephone increased her knowledge within the voice and data IT infrastructure, further contributing to her resourcefulness to both the customers and company she represents.

Trish Kirby’s Specialties:

Agency negotiation, non-traditional revenue, sports marketing, radio advertising, new business, online marketing and advertising, branding, web design, social media and photography.
