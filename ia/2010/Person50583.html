David Edman’s Summary

We know that most businesses spend too much of their hard earned income to pay for health insurance. An estimated 30% of what you spend on health care is wasted on unnecessary, inappropriate, and poor quality care. We help employers understand why health care costs so much and what they can do about it. In addition to working with individual employers, we are seeking to build a coalition of health care purchasers in Pennsylvania to exert their collective influence and collective political clout around an agenda of improving quality and reducing costs.
