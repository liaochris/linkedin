Alex Blair’s Summary

LMS Research & Advisory Services is an independent provider of high quality investigative research, intelligence analysis and strategic advice focused on the commercial markets of the Russian Federation. LMS also has good coverage in Ukraine, the Baltic states and the wider CIS.

Russia continues to provide significant commercial opportunities. This fascinating country has undergone an extraordinary transformation in the last twenty years. Once exposed to Russia, it is a country that quickly defies its stereotypes. Business in Russia, whilst not without risk, can produce significant rewards.

LMS provides a valuable insight into this complex and engaging country. LMS offers insight into the Russian commercial environment, intelligence to those seeking to do business with companies or individuals from Russia and advice for those entering the Russian market for the first time to better understand new opportunities, partners or ventures.
