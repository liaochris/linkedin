Patrick Anthony Howell (howellpublications@yahoo.com)’s Summary

Energy, Light, Love, Africa and Financing

Patrick Howell is a business development and financial services professional with over 17 years of experience from Market Street, San Francisco to Southern California (largest banking market in the US) to Wall Street in global and Fortune 500 companies as Wells Fargo, BNP Paribus, RR Donnelley Financial and Fleet Boston Financial (Bank of America).

American Prosperity and Jobs Inc. and Howell-Wagari Capital Holdings, LLC enjoy a strategic alliance with Newport Capital Consulting (www.newportcapconsult.com), head quartered in Dallas Texas, serving as Sr. Vice President of Businss Development in Orange County California (31st largest economy internationally). Patrick Howell is also a founding member of the Southwest Investment Association (SWIA), focussing largely upon the energy sector in Dallas, Texas and Oklahoma. He is also a member of the Fat Cat Club, SCIA and NIBA.

Specializing in "impossible", Mr. Howell is an aggressive sales closer who services clients above and beyond as well as creative thinker who intuitively looks outside the box for solutions. Mr. Howell has business development experience in banking, energy, publishing, international trade, the arts and technology throughout the US, Latin America, Africa and the Caribbean.

Mr. Howell enjoys an extensive network internationally; serves as advisory upon several board of directors for non-profit and Silicon Valley technology companies; energy and oil and gas companies; and currently serves his global community as a member of the 100 Black Men of Orange County.

Mr. Howell and his wife Hanna Wagari were fundraisers and sponsors for the Barack Obama campaign, successfully co-raising $1.2 MM at the Balboa Bay Club in Newport Beach for the President. Mr. Howell continues his service of a renewed America at the 21st Century under American Prosperity and Jobs, Inc. with board Chairman, Gary Bryant.
