Stephen Webster’s Summary

I am a seasoned, senior IT leader with deep experience providing information technology solutions. I have worked for Fortune 500 and FTSE 50 companies in the Energy industry my entire career, and in Accenture’s ITO practice within their Resources vertical. I have been responsible for strategy development, strategic outsourcing, and have run worldwide support and infrastructure operations, most recently at BG Group plc, a world leader in the global energy market with operations in over 27 countries.
Major career accomplishments include:
• Developed and secured business commitment for a global infrastructure strategy and supporting 3 year $220 million program that enabled the implementation of application and business functionality to support strategic business initiatives.
• Provided leadership for the formulation of corporate strategy to adopt Service Oriented Architecture and Service Oriented Infrastructure.
• Spearheaded initiatives to create a program for joint development of competitive advantage solutions with research and educational institutions, government, and strategic vendors.
• Developed, negotiated, and sold Infrastructure Outsourcing and Transformational Engagements for Fortune 500 clients.
• Awarded Calpine’s prestigious “CEO Innovation Award” for leading the project responsible for Calpine’s number 5 overall (number 1 in the Energy Sector) ranking in InformationWeek’s 2005 “Top 100 Innovators”.
• Managed, developed, and deployed technology infrastructure solutions domestically and in over 18 countries worldwide.
• Managed organizations with over 100 personnel.
• Recognized for the ability to negotiate, plan, and sell multi-year, multi-million dollar vendor solutions.
• Experienced manager of application and infrastructure operations including helpdesk, network operations centers, and large heterogeneous datacenter environments.
• An organizational and problem solving specialist - adept in fast-paced, high-growth environments.
