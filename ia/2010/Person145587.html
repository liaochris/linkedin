Mike Klein’s Summary

Editor and journalist at the Georgia Public Policy Foundation in Atlanta. Founder and editor at Mike Klein Online, my website devoted to national and Georgia public affairs and other contemporary writing. I serve on the director boards at The Commerce Club and Global Soap Project in Atlanta, and for ten years on the Atlanta Press Club Board of Directors.

Professional history includes CNN vice president, Georgia public broadcasting executive, traditional network-owned television stations, major market newspapers, television live sports production, university teaching and public service.
