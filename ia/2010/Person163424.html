Paul Frett’s Summary

Experience isn't simply an online discipline; it's a business discipline. At the same time, the online channel has taken us to a new level of dynamic media. Suddenly we have the opportunity to connect with any number of people with like interests, entertain ourselves and others, complete almost any type of transaction we can dream up, and find stories that we truly connect with.

My goal is to help companies harness the power of the online channel, not just to present content or market products/services, but to enhance their business model -- the way they go to market, the way they connect and communicate, the way they tell their story, the way they transact with their customers/clients, AND the way they communicate and work internally. The online channel now plays a key role in all of these pillars of business.

The world of media and communication is shifting and I'm damn excited to help shape where it's going.
