Dan Rondinelli, CCC, LEED AP’s Summary

MY VISION:
I envision an admired industry that consistantly improves worker productivity on an annual basis; where all Construction Professionals have access to world-class educational content and industry best practices.

MY BIO:
I co-founded the Construction Mastery Institute based upon my passion to increase worker productivity with the eLearning platform making world-class Training and Development easily accessible to ALL Construction Professionals.

I'm a Certified Cost Consultant and a LEED Accredited Professional with 23 years of Commercial Construction experience in Project Management, Business Management, Estimating and Business Development.

In addition to my Construction Operational experience, I've also taught part-time since 2000 as an Adjunct Instructor for Colorado State University in their Construction Management Program.

MY GUIDING PRINCIPLES:
* BELIEF that people are good, hard working, honest and want to do the right thing.

* EQUIPPED Workers and Leaders are essential in order to reach their full potential. The Equipping involves defined Responsibilities with Authority and Accountability. Equipping also includes training, encouragement and a professional growth plan.

* STRATEGIC thinking is critical, hard work is not enough, a well thought-out strategy is critical for sustained success.

* TEAMS are more powerful than individuals, especially when members are dedicated and purposeful; team synergy is real.

* COMMUNICATION without consequence; everyone's ideas are important and need to be heard in order to determine the best solution. A solutions oriented approach is the most effective way to solve any problem.

* Unbounded OPTIMISM; I believe in a world of abundance and when this is coupled with a plan that accounts for the worst case; realistic expectations are set and accomplished.
