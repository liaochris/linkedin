Eric Hayashi’s Summary

Eric Hayashi is a well-rounded interactive entertainment industry executive.

He is currently a Technical Project Manager with the Playstation division of Sony.

Prior to Sony, Hayashi was Co-Founder and VP at RocketOn, a disruptive venture-funded social media/MMO company. There, he created several products and a technology platform, along with over 20 patents around the unique innovations.

He was General Manager at Vivendi Universal Games in Boston, where he headed two game studios: Impressions Studios and Papyrus Racing Games. He oversaw key franchises, including Empire Earth, Nascar Racing and others. Prior to Vivendi, he was Executive Producer at Atari / Infogrames / Hasbro Interactive, where he headed the casual and kids games group, which included franchises such as Monopoly, Scrabble and The Game of Life. He also launched commercially successful versions of Wheel of Fortune and Jeopardy. Hayashi was the Head of North American Publishing at Infospace, where he produced and ran the largest mobile massively multiplayer tournament games network in the US. Before Infospace, he was the Director of Product Development at Kayak Interactive, where he pioneered real-time mobile-to-web MMO’s across all major carriers with Slingo2Go and Poker Superstars. Prior to Kayak, Hayashi was VP of Product Development at Chasma Interactive, where he productized a robust mobile MMO platform technology. Earlier in his career, he was a Senior Producer at Virgin Interactive; he was also Studio Manager and Head of Technology at BAP Interactive.

Eric Hayashi’s Specialties:

Identifying great new media opportunities, driving growth and awareness, creating unique and compelling products, leading teams and growing businesses. Managing products to success. Expertise in games production, business and development.
