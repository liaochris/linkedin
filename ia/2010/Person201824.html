Chris Marlow’s Summary

I help individuals find the niche that's right for them so they can build a successful business of their own; then I show them how to get high-quality business; finally, I show them how to excel in their field and become positioned as "guru."

This often entails the development of an information product for sale, allowing for expanded income opportunities. I use my extensive background in high-level marketing and my own experience as a very successful (and entrepreneurial) freelancer. Because I'm a copywriter, I have an extensive following of copywriters.

My goal is to help as many copywriters and new “free agents” as I can to reach THEIR goals and to help them become gurus in their niche market, while showing them how to add additional streams of income to their businesses. This includes those who are newly laid off.
