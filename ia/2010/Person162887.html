Peter Kojesta’s Summary

I started my career as a contract artist. After doing contract art for several years, I went in-studio where I worked on several titles. My time working at a brick and mortar studio exposed me to the serious games movement, where I was able to contribute to a large scale project for Northrup Grumman. After this project, I picked up with contracts again, by reinvigorating Exis Interactive. Under Exis Interactive, I produced my first game: RacerXtreme for the PC. After that, I managed a large scale project in association with General Dynamics. In addition to having worked on some exciting serious games projects, I've had the opportunity to lead Exis in creating artwork for commercial games such as Fracture and Chronicles of SpellBorne. Currently, Exis Is located in scenic Towson Maryland.
