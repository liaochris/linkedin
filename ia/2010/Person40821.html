Chris Kral’s Summary

Christopher Kral is a senior consultant with WSP Environment & Energy in San Francisco, California. He supports Fortune 500 firms in developing and implementing corporate sustainability and climate strategies. As a project director, he has managed strategic and technical projects delivering services that include regulatory risk assessment, product and supply chain management, energy analysis, GHG inventory analysis and reporting, and product life-cycle assessment. His industry experience includes consumer goods, manufacturing, high technology, financial services, and the legal sector. Chris has worked internationally in Asia, Africa, and the Caribbean providing guidance on global and domestic environmental regulation. He earned a Master’s Degree in Environmental Management (MEM) from Yale University.
