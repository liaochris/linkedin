Steven Husak ✔’s Summary

Steven is the CEO of Continents Apart, LLC, Founder of the LinkedIn Drop Ship Network, Founder of the Successful Professionals Alliance Network (SPaN) and is Vice President of the Board of Directors The Hamptons HOA.

Following a successful international career turning around companies in Europe, Asia, Bermuda & North America Steven has become a sought after speaker, having been the keynote speaker for conventions and annual general meetings for organizations such as the Society of Interior Designers (ASID and BSID)and the Physiotherapists Association (BPA) on topics such as effective use of space vs. efficient use of space and ergonomics. He has been awarded numerous industry awards for customer focus and sales volume.

He is also sought after as a consultant by several major multi-national corporations.

Steven Husak ✔’s Specialties:

Steven has led successful turnarounds of failing or under-performing companies and business units in Europe, North America, Bermuda and Asia, primarily in the Commercial Furniture, Residential Furniture and Accessories industries.

  • CEO

    Continents Apart, LLC

    (Privately Held; Furniture industry)

    June 2009 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    The best way to describe Continents Apart is to say we are the first national rep organization to specialize in the drop shipping marketplace, almost exclusively servicing e-commerce and catalog retailers. Our primary focus is in home & garden decor, licensed sports products and gifts.

    Continents Apart is an international group of manufacturer's representatives in the furniture, home decor/accessories and gift space. We primarily service drop ship/e-commerce/catalog and key strategic accounts in the US, Mexico, UAE and Caribbean nations for the manufacturers that we represent. Additionally we handle direct container programs and conventional distribution for mid-sized retailers and national buying groups.

    In our consultative capacity we are bringing wholesalers, importers and manufacturers together with online and catalog retailers in the internet marketplace. We represent drop ship lines for home decor, accessories, garden and outdoor furniture, gift ware, permanent botanicals. As a value added service in many cases we take on projects with suppliers or retailers seeking to enter this arena and guide them through the start up process. Please call us at (310)601-4121 to know more!

  • Founder

    SPaN -- Successful Professionals Alliance Network

    (Privately Held; Civic & Social Organization industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    SPaN is Totally Free Open Networking!

    We are small to mid-sized business owners, managers, salespeople and entrepreneurs working together to grow our referral networks. Each chapter holds a weekly meeting and you will want to attend on a consistent basis to maximize the benefit to you.

    We accept members from all lines of business. We are here to help each other grow our businesses by referrals, not to sell services, products or business opportunities to our fellow members. We learn about the businesses of other members so that when we see an opportunity for them we recognize it and bring the parties together. This is far stronger than a 'leads' group.

    You will get a chance to tell everyone who you are, what you do and what is the best referral the group can provide to you.
