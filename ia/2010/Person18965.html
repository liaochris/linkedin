Anthea Newburn’s Summary

Anthea has over 10 years experience in the international hotel industry having worked in Ireland, Belgium and the UK. Working for Starwood Hotels & Resorts, she focused on Six Sigma and change management working with teams at front line, senior management and VP positions. She trained all staff on understanding process improvement and their role, and management training focused on identifying opportunities and prioritising resources. This experience gave her team skills with international members and also the ability to motivate people at all levels. Anthea won the Starwood President’s Award for her work in Six Sigma.

She transferred to Human Resources within Starwood where she worked on a project team to integrate another global company into the business from the people perspective. The role included coordinating global personnel contracts, managing the communication process, implementing an outplacement service, managing the redundancy process including interviews, criteria, severance payments and budgets.

In her HR role at The Westin Dublin, Anthea delivered Induction and supervisory management training including Time Management, Behavioural Interviewing Skills, Appraisal Skills, Policies and Procedures, and Coaching Skills for Managers.

Anthea arrived at coaching having experienced a busy career and job changes herself, and as a result, she has great empathy and understanding for clients. She has worked with clients who wanted to focus on work life balance, trying to meet work challenges, working with conflicting personalities, setting up a new business or at a cross roads in life. She also works with an Employee Assistance Programme to deliver wellbeing and stress management workshops to companies on their behalf.
