Rich Quintilone’s Summary

Our practice concentrates on Labor & Employment Law, Personal Injury, Construction Defect, Business Litigation, Products Liability, Premises Liability as well as servicing Business and Financial Institutions with their diverse litigation requirements.

This website is a resource for potential clients, attorneys, and other people in the state of California seeking information within the scope of our practice areas.
