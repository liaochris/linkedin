Pankaj Jindal’s Summary

Pankaj is a business head with Aditi Technologies for the past 7 years.

With a strong background in technology, Pankaj has helped manage multiple client engagements as well as all outsourced software dev projects for Microsoft at Aditi. Today, Pankaj helps Fortune 500 clients succeed by delivering hard-to-find technology talent and class leading outsourced product development.

He brings deep insight to clients about their talent and development challenges, and helps build solutions that combine the best elements of Technology and People Management to deliver client success. His extensive experience at Microsoft has led to Aditi becoming one of their premier partners and vendors, including as part of the NXT and NSI partner programs. Aditi has also been ranked Microsoft’s # 1 vendor for Staffing under Pankaj’s leadership.
