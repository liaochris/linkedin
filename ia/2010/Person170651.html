Reed Savory’s Summary

Over twenty-five years of experience leading teams in the planning, design, implementation, operation, management, and sustaining support of large-scale mission-critical corporate networks and supporting user and desktop support services.

Extensive IT operations management and IT project management experience, including network conversions, computer room design, data center management, storage services design and implementation, server roll-outs, Internet strategy, and workstation, operating system, and application implementation and conversions.

Extensive real-world experience working in both the start-up and established company models, and in dealing with both rapid growth and rapid cost-cutting. Expert at working in situations where cost reduction and cost containment are vital.
