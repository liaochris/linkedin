Perry Anne Norton’s Summary

PanRight Productions supplies voice over, audio production and editing, remote interviews, podcasts and music supervision for clients across all industries. CEO and Founder Perry Anne Norton provides a trustworthy, knowledgeable voice for businesses ranging from high-tech training to medical animations; from character voices to documentaries, and consults on audio marketing for increased exposure and retention rates.

PanRight Productions provides a one-stop, inexpensive solution for voice talent, studio, engineer and editor. Your location - local or international - is no obstacle with digital audio technology.

Professionally trained in acting, singing, voice over and music performance as well as music technology and podcasting.
