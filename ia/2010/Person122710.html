Laurie Weber’s Summary

My strength in green and technology marketing is the ability to simplify and create intrinsic value around complex products, services and technical concepts.

I am a strategically driven advertising and marketing copywriter with a proven track record of creating effective, award-winning integrated campaigns, as well as copy that helps build, maintain and extend brands for B2B, B2C, and C2C companies in technology, green initiatives, retail fast food and grocery, packaged goods, wine, travel and national consumer accounts.

That said, being strategic does not mean monolithic, expected or boring. The magic is in the ability to create original, high impact work that connects rationally and emotionally with the target audience. That's how you sell stuff and win awards.
