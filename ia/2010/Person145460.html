Mike Hayes’s Experience

  • Chief Strategy, Development and Operations Officer

    Geomentum

    (Public Company; IPG; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2009 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Geomentum is the leading hyper-local marketing and media strategy and performance improvement agency in the U.S., made up of a network of strategic business units that plan and place more than $2 billion in media annually. Through the use of proprietary econometric models, geospatial targeting tools and business intelligence capabilities, we are able to provide deep insights into our client’s store-level marketing and media performance and then use those insights to optimize media planning (within a highly automated Marketing Performance Management toolset), both across and within multiple media (including traditional and emerging digital, social and mobile) for maximum ROI. We offer a suite of analytic and media planning and activation services, as well as a proprietary technology toolset within a Software as a Service (SaaS) environment. We place over $2Billion in media annually. We employ over 500 people in the U.S. and are a unit of Interpublic Group's MediaBrands.

    Organized and now lead and direct the functional efforts of INSIGHTS, a group of highly skilled analysts, statisticians and operations research professionals providing deep client insights and optimization through the use of econometric modeling, optimization and a suite of proprietary geospatial targeting tools, PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY DEVELOPMENT, a team consisting of subgroups in Business Intelligence, Software Development, Product Development and Infrastructure, and PARTNERSHIPS & INTEGRATION, a team dedicated to the establishment and integration of a portfolio of media partners and acquisition candidates in order to expand our capabilities and grow the business.

    Co-wrote, with the CEO, the business plan for Geomentum in order to provide a value-added consulting capability to three channel-specific media agencies. Primary architect for the overall solutions landscape of products and services to be delivered to the market. Successfully launched the business in August, 2009.

  • Chief Operating & Development Officer

    NSA Media, OSI and The Wahlstrom Group

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2008 — August 2009 (1 year 1 month)

    The network of agencies including NSA Media, Outdoor Services and the Wahlstrom Group, all units of Interpublic Group, represents the largest agency network focused on highly targeted local media in the United States. Each company is the leader in its respective channel.

    I led strategic planning, business architecture, organizational design and ultimately the development and operation of a transformed enterprise that would leverage a heritage of success in providing services to the Retail industry, local market targeting expertise and analytical prowess.

    Implemented a Marketing Resource Management toolset, Aprimo, to provide a much needed agency knowledge repository and workflow management capability. The team implementing the system was able to document and configure over 45 workflow variants and roll them out to the NSA organization of roughly 300 people within a record 4 month time frame with no major issues.

    Implemented new analytical tools from KXEN and Frontline that provided relatively easy to use modeling and optimization capabilities to upgrade the skills of a subset of our existing group of 61 data analysts.

    Implemented SPSS (for modeling) and Express from Fair Isaac (for complex optimization) for our PhD statisticians to use in their efforts. Over the course of 8 months, a project team from within Advanced Data Analysis and Product Development, on behalf of client Wal*Mart, codified the constraints in planning newspapers at the local level for over 14,000 newspaper publications and wrote the optimization routine to effectively automate the process, resulting in a cycle time reduction in print planning for 3,500 stores, from 10,500 man-hours to 90 minutes. This is an innovative break-through and milestone in the media agency world achieved by applying advanced optimization to a traditional media and will revolutionize how agencies strategize and plan print advertising. Full-scale commercialization of this product is underway.

  • SVP Marketing & Administrative Services

    The Bon Ton Stores, Inc.

    (Retail industry)

    June 2006 — August 2008 (2 years 3 months)

    The Bon Ton Stores, Inc. is the second largest regional department store provider covering the upper third of the U.S. with 8 separate nameplates/brands, 298 stores and roughly $3Billion in annual revenues.

    Directed all Marketing, Media and Marketing Operations for the retailer including a $200 Million promotional marketing & media budget and a staff of over 40 media planners & buyers, finance, IT, BI and Marketing analytic professionals.

    Conceived, created, articulated and implemented a multi-year transformation strategy for the Marketing and Sales Promotion organization.

    Implemented a number of technology tools to streamline operations, upgrade skills and provide new capabilities including a Print Ad Creation toolset (Connect3), Advanced Statistical Analysis toolset (SPSS) and a new CRM suite for Campaign Management (Aprimo). This allowed for shortened process timelines, faster speed to market and better post-event evaluation of our efforts.

    Initiated and directed multiple econometric modeling projects on Media Mix Modeling, Print Distribution Optimization, Customer Acquisition, and Propensity to Shop/Respond. These provided us far deeper insights into customer behavior, media responsiveness by local trade area and a fact-basis for explaining the causality in our daily and year-over-year sales variation.

    Led a redesign of our Customer Loyalty program and oversaw the efforts of a newly hired Director of Marketing who successfully rolled the program out across a complex network of 8 different nameplates and two legacy systems architectures.
