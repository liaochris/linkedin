Paul Burns’s Summary

Paul has nearly 25 years experience in the software industry, driving strategy for IT management solutions through product management, competitive analysis and business planning. He has held a series of leadership positions in marketing and R&D. Paul’s extensive technical and business experience offers Neovise and its clients valuable insights, perspectives and guidance in IT management solutions. He also writes articles for industry publications, and speaks at client events as well as industry conferences including IT Financial Management Association (ITFMA), Interworks and Telecom Management Forum. He earned both B.S. in Computer Science and M.B.A. degrees from Colorado State University.
