Jerome Shore’s Summary

Executive Coach since 1992. Have worked with lawyers, accountants, executives and entrepreneurs. Areas of expertise are business development, leadership and resilience.

Soft Skill Workshop Developer and Leader since 1994. Have developed 10+ workshops in areas of Emotional Intelligence such as Resilient Thinking, Managing Change, Stress Management, Leadership Skills, Coaching Skills, Business Development, Performance Coaching and Time Management.

Developed The Online Coaching Clinic 1996-2000 which offers e-coaching content and e-courses. We now have 182 online coaching modules at The Online Coaching Clinic in 10 competency areas and over 100 Coaching Chips which can be used as emailed coursework.

Multi-national advertising agencies 1975-1992. Ogilvy and Mather, McCann-Erickson, MacLaren and Kert.
