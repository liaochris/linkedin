Tony Stone’s Summary

I provide interim / contract specialists which specialise in niche areas of the financial industry. These areas include Mobile Telecom Billing Systems and Front, Middle and Back Office Application Support Specialists; these would include consultants with the following skills: Linedata, Longview / Beauchamp, Charles River Thinkfolio, Fidessa or Latent Zero, HiPortfolio, Eagle Pace, or packages of a similar nature.

The organisation extends Generic’s longstanding reputation in the consulting field to allow it to provide highly skilled Billing Systems Consultants and Project Management resources to meet high level interim and strategic consulting requirements in the Telco, Internet, Utility and Finance Industries.

Generic is committed to offering a quality service, which builds on the excellence of our consultants. Working individually or in teams our Associates can provide Application Support, Software Architecture, Implementation / Upgrade Consultancy, Application Development and Project Management, to ensure our clients Billing Systems support the business needs.
