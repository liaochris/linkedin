John Patrick’s Summary

John is a business executive dedicated to helping small and large business owners, corporations, and medical practices/facilities increase their cash flow and profits by reducing their days in receivable (DSO).

SERVICE/BENEFIT PROVIDER: John is Chief Executive Salesman for Transworld Systems, Inc., a full service accounts receivable management solution for small/medium sized business as well as large corporations.

SPEAKER: John speaks to small and large companies, civic organizations and professional organizations on the subjects of maximizing profits, managing receivables, customer service and many other pertinent business issues of the day.

BLOGS: John authors several blogs on the subject of increasing cash flow and profits, including: The Profit Doctor, Collections Help and John Patrick, Profit Doctor.

POCAST: John is the host of The Profit Doctor Podcast, which can be found via his profile link or on iTunes.

He has the reputation of being a high achiever through team work, leadership by example, and by empowering those around him.

In addition to his role as a profit management specialist, chief executive salesman, speaker and consultant in the accounts receivable industry, he is the President of Centurion Investment Group of Florida, LLC - a real estate investment company.

He is active in his church, is involved in various organizations and capacities serving the homeless community, is an Adminiserve Partner with MGMA and is endorsed by HCMA as a benefit provider.
