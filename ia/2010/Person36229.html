Charlie Riley’s Summary

Well-rounded experience in multiple disciplines of marketing, advertising, public relations and branding. I currently make clients happy with interactive and public relations work, such as email marketing, website development, social media monitoring, and integrated advertising efforts. I have copy-wrote national television and radio commercials, along with website content, executed and constructed social media strategy and campaigns, managed SEO/SEM campaigns, oversaw focus groups and marketing research, formalized marketing strategy, garnered media exposure, executed sponsorships, created and ran events, etc. I'm like a utility infielder, crossed with his agent. I consult with companies and agencies, bringing experience and creativity across several categories such as education, government, digital signage, banking, energy, franchising, professional sports and many more.

I recently judged the 2009 B2B Twitterer of the Year Awards, co-chaired the 2009 Leadership Buffalo Rising Leaders Class and sit on their marketing and enrollment committees. I am the current president of the Advertising Club of Buffalo, the region's American Advertising Federation's affiliate.

I continue educating myself about sustainability, and how to add "going green" to companies I work with to maximize their triple bottom line. I teach as an adjunct professor in the Integrated Marketing Communications curriculum at St. Bonaventure University.

I bring to the table several years of philanthropic service, consultation and event planning, including co-chairing the District II Addy Awards and setting up my own annual fundraisers, like the Buffalo Scavenger Hunt, which benefits Camp Good Days & Special Times.
