David Hymson’s Summary

A seasoned strategic healthcare marketer with broad therapeutic experience including oncology, women's health, cardiovascular, infectious diseases and HIV, CNS, and others. Most passionate about helping brands discover their raison d'etre, the single, most compelling benefit that makes them different from their competitors. Excel at working with creative teams to translate this brand essence into brand stories with provocative messages and unforgettable imagery.

With extensive professional experience on both sides of the Atlantic, and fluency in French, I'm truly a global pharma professional. With an understanding of how brands are built and business is conducted in key markets, and a flair for working with multi-cultural teams, I may be among the most accomplished global pharma marketers in the business.
