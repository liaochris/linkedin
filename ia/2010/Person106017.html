Joshua B. Cohen’s Summary

Joshua Cohen, Director of Content Licensing & Distribution at PR Newswire, develops and manages the relationship with the thousands of websites, databases and content aggregators around the world that publish and distribute PR Newswire’s news release feeds including the rapidly growing multimedia/social media and mobile news release segments.

Josh’s fluency in Mandarin Chinese, Spanish and French has been instrumental in cementing key distribution deals for PR Newswire’s multilingual feeds.

His more than 15 years experience in the development and distribution of business and financial news has given him a unique ability to work with websites and portals in terms of content selection, positioning of the news content on the website or distribution vehicle along with other criteria to maximize the ROI from the feed both for the website as well as for the client issuing the news release.

美通社信息合作部总裁柯佳时 (Joshua Cohen) ，负责建立和管理面向全球数千个网站、数据库和内容整合商发布美通社新闻稿网络，其合作伙伴囊括发展迅速的多媒体/社会化媒体新闻以及手机与移动设备新闻等行业。

柯先生精通中国普通话、西班牙语和法语，这些为美通社签署重大的多语种新闻稿发布协议发挥了重要作用。

他在商业与金融新闻的开发和发布领域15年以上的经验使他擅长处理与各类网站间的合作，可为网站以及发稿客户在内容选择、网站新闻内容定位、发布媒介定位以及其他实现新闻稿投资回报最大化等方面提供有力支持。
