Woody Holliman’s Summary

Woody has an MFA in graphic arts (along with an MA in philosophy) and professional experience as an art director, art critic, illustrator and graphic design professor.

His graphic design and marketing agency, Flywheel Design, was founded in 2004.

Flywheel's reputation for smart, strategically-driven work has allowed them to serve a growing roster of institutional and corporate clients in RTP and beyond, including: Alliance Architecture • American Dance Festival • Artsplosure • Bell + Howell • Bellan Dance • Cirrus Pharmaceuticals • Clearscapes Architecture • Cline Design • CRC Health Group • Duke University • Freudenberg • Full Frame Documentary Film Festival • HSN (Home Shopping Network) • Inspire Pharmaceuticals • Manbites Dog Theater • Nasher Museum of Art • NCCU School of Law • RENCI (Renaissance Computing Institute) • Revolution Restaurant • Shen Wei Dance Arts • SOS Children's Villages • TriVirix • UNC Chapel Hill • Watts Grocery

Flywheel's work has been recognized by PRINT, HOW, Creativity Annual, Logo Lounge, the American Advertising Federation (ADDY Awards), American Design Awards, W3 Web Awards, Summit Awards & Davey Awards.
