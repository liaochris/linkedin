I am a NY licensed clinical psychotherapist, business and branding expert, speaker and author with over 30 years experience consulting, speaking, doing workshops and teleseminars.

Shattering Limitations:

Using the Kazlow Method, I show entrepreneurs and professionals how to shatter limitations and maximize and monetize their business, branding, and social media. The results are greater viability, visibility, and profits – and living a life that works!

On a personal level, I show them how to shatter the limitations that define how they live and how they age. As baby boomers are facing – and fighting – the process of aging, it gives them the tools to create a healthspan (as well as a lifespan) and enjoy Ageless Living!

Power Connecting is a central aspect of what I do and teach because it's a key factor that determines the success of any business or personal venture. Power Connecting encompasses not only your network but also your connection with yourself, your vision, community, and Source. Power connecting also involves our business, branding, products, promotion, development, marketing, and social media.

Social Media & Social Networking:

The key to maximizing and monetizing your Social Media and Social Networking involves 2 factors: Power Connecting and a strategy that aligns and integrates business, branding, marketing, and personal style. Both are essential to grow your business, drive your promotion and publicity, expand your platform, increase your visibility, and become the sought-after expert. I can show you how to make sure that your social media time isn’t time spent but time monetized.
