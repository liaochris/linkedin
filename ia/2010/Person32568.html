Carlos Navedo’s Summary

FUNDRAISING
Healthy Start of Southwest Florida. Increased donations 343% in two years (including a 270% increase in cash contributions) and also increased the donor base by 115%. Worked aggressively to build and maintain partnerships with corporations, organizations and media in the community. Increased the number of events from two to seven per year and shifted the focus from developing community awareness to fundraising. Was successful in negotiating with CHICO’S CHARITIES to be the exclusive sponsor for The White Show at the Barbara B. Mann, which resulted in a $153,000 donation, of which $133,000 was monetary. Seventeen hundred people attended the event. Other events held were the Annual Golf Classic at the West Bay Club, Bonita Blues Festival, Walk for Kids, Bunny Breakfast at TGI Friday’s, NASGRASS Lawnmower Races at the Swamp Cabbage Festival (gate and concessions), Annual Meeting, Christmas Toy Drive and Pictures with Santa at the Bell Tower Mall. Worked with the Lee County Board of Commissioners to proclaim the day of our annual meeting “Healthy Start Day.”

Developed partnerships with the following: Corporations - Bell Tower Shops, CHICO'S CHARITIES, Edison Mall and Coconut Pointe Mall (Simon Property Group, Inc.), Florida Gulf Coast University, Hallmark Stores, Jackson Hewitt Tax Service, Lee Memorial Health System Foundation, Matzaluna, MGS Designz & More, PAETEC, Prawnbroker, Premier Backup Solutions, Sanibel Grill, Starbucks,TGI Friday’s, The Timbers and University Grill; Media - Azteca America, Bonita Daily News, D'Latinos, FOX-4, Gaceta Tropical, Naples Daily News, Telemundo, The Banner - Bonita Daily News, The News-Press, Univision and WINK TV.
Created and implemented television health topic segments, a 10-minute segment on Azteca America and a 30-minute show on Telemundo which reaches 440,000 households per show and is aired twice a month. Significantly increased awareness of the agency in Southwest Florida.
