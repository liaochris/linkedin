John Pettitt’s Summary

My new baby is http://clp.ly - it's a service that makes it effortless to clip or quote a web page for email or a blog post.

I was Co-Founder/CTO and Beyond.com and CyberSource before I "retired" and took up photography.

I do a lot of things. I've been founding companies since I was 18, had one sale and two IPO's. I have patents related to e-commerce and security. I put in the first commercial internet connection in the UK (1989). I've had an email address since 1983. I tried being retired for a while and in the process became a nationally published photographer. I thrive in fast changing environments.

I'm very good at grasping complex problems and understanding the relationship between technologies and business outcomes. I also don't mince words so if you want an opinion on a technology play just ask.
