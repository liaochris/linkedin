Jeff Cobb’s Summary

I show organizations how to strategically blend marketing, technology, and education to increase revenue and customer engagement. I have more than a decade's experience as an entrepreneur, executive, and marketing professional in the field of Web technology and online learning.

I advise organizations on how to leverage new technologies to create successful Web-based learning communities for members, customers, and other stakeholders. I also help organizations increase adoption and sales for their existing online knowledge and learning offerings.

I speak frequently at conferences and for private organizations on topics related to new Web technologies, marketing, and business model innovation. For information about my speaking, visit http://www.jeffthomascobb.com/speaking.

I write the Mission to Learn blog at http://blog.missiontolearn.com as well as the Hedgehog & Fox blog at http://www.jeffthomascobb.com/blog/. I also publish articles, eBooks, and other media frequently. See, for example, the free eBook Learning 2.0 for Associations at http://www.alearning20.com.

I serve on the Professional Development Section Council of the American Society of Association Executives and am an adviser to Philantech, provider of the PhilanTrack™ online grant proposal, reporting and management system.
