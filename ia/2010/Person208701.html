Thomas Sola’s Summary

Specializing in user experience design, interface design, and usability testing, for large, Web based applications. Strong track record in start-up and growth environments. Emmy Award nominated graphic designer.

Thomas Sola’s Specialties:

User Surveys & Interviews, Contextual Inquiry, User Observation, Task Analysis, Card Sorting, Heuristic Evaluation, Storyboarding, Design Guidelines, Style Sheets, Workflows & State Diagrams, Paper Prototyping, Photoshop Prototyping, XHTML, CSS, DOM Manipulation, JavaScript Prototyping & Development with a strong eye towards Section 508 and WCAG compliance. Familiarity with PHP, UML, JSP, XML, and Flash.
