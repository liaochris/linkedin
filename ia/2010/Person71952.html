F. Tyler Shaw’s Summary

Once upon a time, producers had to hire a small group of music and audio professionals in order to have quality custom music for their productions. Amazing new developments in digital audio have dramatically changed this scenario. Made-to-order music is now within the reach of any media producer. If you would like to hear examples of what can be done with these new tools, check out...

www.ftylershaw.com/reel/reel.html

If you have a project that you would like to take to the next level with original music, feel free to get in touch with me. I would be happy to share what I know about the new options available to you.
.
