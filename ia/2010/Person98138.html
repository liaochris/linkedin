Jim Allen MBA, BSEE, PMT’s Summary

Jim has a diverse background in business development, corporate strategy, marketing and operations management across a number of industries including metals processing, residential development, electrical, chemicals and automotive. In addition to his entrepreneurial pursuits, he was most recently (until federal funding expired) Director of the Business Accelerator for Innovation and Collaboration at the non-profit McHenry County Economic Development Corporation. He oversaw programs to help the over 600 local manufacturers become more competitive, sponsored events and created digital marketing venues to showcase and promote the manufacturing capabilities of the county.

Prior to that, Jim was Director of Corporate Strategy and a project manager at a $100+ million residential developer serving the Chicago market. Jim has over six years of residential home building experience and is known for his quality and process improvement expertise across many industries. His other experience includes domestic and international sales and marketing roles for highly engineered products. Jim also possesses significant manufacturing experience in fabricated metal products and is certified as a Powder Metallurgy Technologist.

Jim earned his MBA from the University of Chicago and holds a BS degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Virginia. Upon completion of his undergraduate degree, he served four years as a Surface Warfare Officer in the US Navy.
