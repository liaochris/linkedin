Kelly Welch, MHRM, CPBS, CPRW’s Summary

"Strategic Positioning for Career Evolution"

YES Career Services is dedicated to helping courageous leaders unearth, develop and communicate their strategic position for career evolution. I am a career coach, certified Personal Branding Strategist and certified Online Identity Strategist. All this and more aptly equips me to support your career brand transformation with branded career marketing materials such as biographies, personal branding statements, résumés, biographies, and LinkedIn profiles.

As your Career Advancement Partner, YES brings you premier offerings and programs designed to help you get clear on direction and the path for your future. We are committed to providing provide leadership, coaching, and consulting to help courageous leaders purposefully pursue opportunities which provide career significance and fulfillment.

A feel for our style and what clients report from their work with us:

♦ Clear vision for professionals to align their work with their strengths and brand for career fulfillment.

♦ Demonstrated energy and passion for empowering courageous leaders to identify, develop and communicate their own distinct personal brand.

♦ A strong belief in coaching and the power it brings: knowledge, experience, active listening, creative ideas, and motivation.

♦ Broad range of experience encompassing successful track record in corporate and agency Recruiting, Human Resources, Leadership Development and Outplacement Services experience to propose solutions and proactive strategic vision to colleagues and clients.

♦ A promise to offer consultative, energetic and dedicated service toward the needs of the client and his/her business.

With a love for learning, I continue my professional development...Currently pursuing certification both as Certified Talent Management and Leadership Coach as well as a Certified Career Management Coach.
