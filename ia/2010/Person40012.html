Chris Townsend’s Summary

Chris Townsend earned his B.A. degree from Indiana University-Bloomington in 1996. Upon graduation he moved to Indianapolis, IN where he began his career as a Financial Representative with Northwestern Mutual, one of FORTUNE magazine's Most Admired Companies. His clients included businesses and individuals. Chris moved to Nashville in 1998 to work with Intermedia Communications, a facilities-based provider of commercial communication services. He excelled in providing voice and data solutions to medium-sized businesses while learning the role of site acquisition specialist. In 2000, Chris relocated to Denver, CO to become Site Acquisition Specialist with Crown Castle USA. There he successfully managed over 30 wireless sites until 2002 when he returned to the insurance and employee benefits industry.

Currently, Chris lives in Nashville, TN and is Principal of Icon Benefit Solutions. He works with small and medium-sized business owners throughout the Southeast to provide cost effective employee benefits solutions tailored to the specific needs of each individual client. In addition, he offers his clients the value-added services and resources needed to be competitive in today's economy and job market.

Chris has over 9 years of employee benefits experience.

Chris Townsend’s Specialties:

Employee benefit communication services, web based business management, group health insurance, group life insurance, group disability, group voluntary products, benefit enrollment services, cafeteria plans, HRA's, and HSA's.
