Robert Barlow’s Summary

I have been sailing close to twenty years and have worked for sailing businesses for over fourteen years. This sport quickly became one of my greatest passions and I have worked to enhance my skills and knowledge regularly throughout my adult life. My and therefore Texas Sailing's goal is to help everyone with an interest in sailing to realize their sailing goal regardless of their current skill level. Texas Sailing and our staff will help you pursue the avenue best suited for your lifestyle and hope to help you maintain the passion for life.

Texas Sailing's Marine Industry Customer Service Index (CSI) has been 100% since 2000. Beneteau measures the satisfaction of consumers for three years following a sailboat purchase and we consistently earn top ranking for our customer service provided. Please let us earn your trust and business.
