David Belsky’s Summary

The Newport Beach branch of Jobspring Parters is a "boutique" technology recruiting firm that is localized and specialized. My company fills Senior Software Engineer and Technologist positions in companies throughout the greater Orange County and San Diego Areas. We work with companies ranging from the Fortune 500 to the well funded technology startup. Our philosophy is to HUMANIZE the recruitment process and build relationships with candidates and key decision makers through a genuine desire to advise clients on key strategic hires and further our reputation in the LOCAL technology community. I pride myself on making sure my office works with companies that view technology as a RESOURCE to add efficiency to the workplace, reach customers in new and innovate ways, or better the human experience through creating cutting edge products. Our focus is engineering / software positions ranging in salary from 50 - 150K within the following industries: .E-Commerce, Internet, Media, Online Advertising, Technology, Hi- Tech, Interactive, Medical Device, Home Automation, Green Technology, Power Electronics, Consumer Electronics, Automotive, Software, SAAS, Consulting, Network and Security Products, EMR, EHR, Mortgage, Real Estate, Manufacturing / Retail, Marketing.
