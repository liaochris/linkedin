Guier Fence Franchise’s Summary

Our Purpose:
Our purpose is to enhance the lives of our customers by creating high quality fences for their homes or places of business, provide a superior customer experience, and ultimately earn their referrals.


We choose to do this by:
1. Treating each customer with respect and putting ourselves in the shoes of each customer.
2. Recruiting the highest quality people and training them to be the best.
3. Providing a safe, friendly, and supportive work environment.


Our Values:
1. We will conduct our business in an ethical way by doing the right things.
2. We will continually develop and nurture relationships within the organization, with suppliers, and with customers.
3. We will operate a safe, well-run, and profitable business.
4. We will grow through a commitment to learning and development, both personally and professionally.


Our Picture of the Future:
1. Everyone in our organization is a better person every day.
2. Our employees are motivated and in a role that aligns with their strengths, gifts, and unique talents.
3. The Guier Fence name is recognized here and nationwide for quality, integrity and value.

Guier Fence Franchise’s Experience

    Guier Fence

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    January 1979 — Present (31 years 9 months)

    In 1979 Kelly Guier had post hole diggers and a dream. Since then, Guier Fence has grown into one of the premier fencing companies in the nation. The single guiding principle that has driven Guier Fence is customer satisfaction. Our family of residential, agricultural and commercial/industrial customers continues to grow for three simple reasons: Service, Selection, Value.

    Your investment in wood, vinyl, bamboo, metal, Trex, chain-link or other fence solutions from Guier Fence means you get the value of top quality materials, expert craftsmanship and great value in an installed product.

    Fencing experts will meet with you one-on-one and provide accurate estimates for your project. From knowing the building codes around each municipality to what fence products fit your job best, the professionals at Guier Fence will manage the process through to completion, ensuring your total satisfaction.

  • Franchise Opportunities

    Guier Fence Co., Inc.

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    January 1979 — Present (31 years 9 months)

    With our established systems and tools, we think it’s time we help motivated business owners create their own futures in a dynamic industry, give them the opportunity to grow as they follow our business models and procedures, and take advantage of the resources we’ve collected over the years. We even have different business models available for potential franchise owners:

    1. Our “Guier Fence Professional” Model
    The Guier Fence Professional option provides the opportunity to start your company with a reasonable investment amount and low overhead. This may even be home-based depending on a variety of factors. This concept allows the franchise owner to market, sell, and install Guier-approved fencing solutions in a defined territory, while following our guidelines and leveraging our knowledge and experience with products, promotion, and more.


    2. Our “Showroom” Model
    Perfect for the experienced business manager, or a current “Guier Fence Professional” who is ready to expand, this option includes the opening and operating of a full-scale showroom and warehouse facility, and is designed so the franchise owner hires staff to help operate the business. Guier Fence will of course be there to aid in the site selection, business ramp up, and ongoing operations.

    Bring your skills to the table and become a part of our recognized concept, in a huge market! For more information on the Guier Fence franchise program, visit our website or contact us at 800-845-9839.
