Smartstay Hotel App from O'Rourke Hospitality Marketing’s Summary

Smartstay: The Mobile Solution for Hotels

Smartstay is a revolutionary hotel iPhone app customized for your own hotel brand. The Smartstay hotel app serves as a 24/7 mobile concierge and provides users with a micro mobile hotel website, real-time news about hotel promotions and local events, and an interactive city guide.

Hotels have unlimited opportunities to communicate with their guests and can send them exclusive offers. Smartstay is a mobile marketing solution for hotels looking to capture a wider market share, tap into a new source of revenue, and enhance the guest experience at their hotel and in their city. Add Smartstay to your hotel mobile marketing strategy today!

Try it for Free!
A free Demo Version is available for download at the Apple Store. Just tap on the Apple Store icon on you iPhone and search for Smartstay or visit this link: http://bit.ly/bgSC4Y
