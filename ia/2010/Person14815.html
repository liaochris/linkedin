Anthony Miller’s Summary

Anthony Miller is a Technical & Engineering Recruiter for Volt Technical Resources, the global technical staffing and placement giant. Through deep Internet searching, social media networking, as well as attending user groups, he has amassed a large network of contractors, candidates, and clients as well as go-to people for industry knowledge.

Prior to Volt Technical Resources, Anthony gained technical knowledge through an unquenchable curiosity and interest in all things IT and Engineering related, from building robots, computers, networks, and automated machines in his own time, and from years of technical support. As a self-described "Uber Geek”, Anthony has hired for such companies as Microsoft, Intel, Mentor Graphics, Standard Insurance, and many others in and around the greater Portland area.

When not engaged in Recruitment and Sourcing, Anthony continues to pursue technology no matter how fast it tries to run ahead of him, as well as volunteering and spending time with his family.
