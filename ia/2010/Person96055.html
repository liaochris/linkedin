Jeremy Kipnis’s Summary

BIO:

Jeremy Kipnis, CEO of Kipnis Studios, is also designer and engineer of its multi-award winning Kipnis Studio Standard (KSS) - Ultimate Performance Home Theaters & Commercial Cinema Venues. He is also Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at TERRA-SAN, a multi media importing/grooming/management company, as well as a Worldwide Independent Technology Consultant and Tester for numerous companies including:

Barco, Christie Digital, Digital Projection, Dolby Digital 3D, DTS, JBL, JVC, Master Image 3D, Mitsubishi, NEC, Panasonic, RealD, Screen Essence, SIM2, Sony, Stewart Filmscreen, Texas Instruments, Vutec, and XpandD 3D.

He is responsible for defining and pioneering the Cutting Edge in Motion Picture and Surround Sound Presentation Standards - the Kipnis Studio Standard - (KSS) which is designed to elevate the art and science of the cinema viewing experience to an entirely new level.

An expert with over 41 years of experience in the fields of large format still photography, motion picture cinematography, video feature direction, audio engineering, record album production, technology R & D, beta code tester, A/V equipment evaluator and reviewer.

He advises top film, video, and audio presentation creators and standards groups such as DCI, SMPTE, AES-EBU, CEDIA, 4K & 8K Image Capture & Projection, IMAX, Digital IMAX, 70 mm film, and the Imaging Science Foundation (ISF).

Jeremy Kipnis’s Specialties:

His specialties include: Hands-On testing and evaluation of the lions share of A/V hardware during the last 41 years, both commercial and professional. He is most comfortable talking about the detailed technical aspects of commercial and home cinema projection, display, and sound technologies including 3-D providers such as Disney Digital, Dolby Digital 3D, RealD, IMAX-DMR, Dolby Surround Sound, and DTS Surround Sound.
