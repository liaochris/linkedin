Therese Marie Boldt’s Summary

My practice is dedicated to coaching leaders and developing careers.

COACHING LEADERS: Executives and leadership teams within a variety of industries and disciplines who want to increase their overall capability engage me as a coach. Typically, individuals seek out a coach if they are navigating some significant transition in their life or career or have an idea that their leadership style (or behavior) might be holding them back; likewise, companies seek out a coach when they need to transition their corporate culture or when a group could be holding itself back from working well together. Companies (and executive recruiters) engage my services to provide onboard coaching to leaders hired or promoted into a position… ensuring they will be successful in their new role/environment/culture. As a certified distributor of the DiSC assessment tool, I am able to develop and facilitate workshops that promote better communications within and among teams.

DEVELOPING CAREERS: My book, "Yes! You Can Land a Job Even in a Crummy Economy" takes job seekers through a step-by-step process I developed in over 20 years of recruiting and placing job candidates. In addition to one-on-one Career Coaching, I facilitate workshops designed to build job search skills of people in career transition.

Therese Marie Boldt’s Specialties:

I work with all industries, and the professionals I serve are typically at the senior leadership level of an organization. The workshops based upon the book "Yes! You Can Land a Job even in a Crummy Economy" are sponsored by universities, colleges, vocational training institutions and groups dedicated to building job search skills.
