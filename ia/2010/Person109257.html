Justin Moore’s Summary

My experience in the design field ranges from the creation of logos and corporate identity packages, to colorful illustration and architectural renderings, and everything in between. I have a background in all forms of art and art history which I find helps me in determining the best "look" needed for a particular project. Along with my creative services comes a strong work ethic and ability to art direct and manage the production of artwork.

My goals are simple: be successful and make my clients happy. I have a determination to give you the results your company is looking for. Because lets face it, if you don't look good then I don't either.

Justin Moore’s Specialties:
