Tina Renneisen’s Summary

My goal is to provide high quality mental health care to children, adults, and families in resource poor areas. My interest is the early identification of children with mental health problems through partnerships with school based clinics and early intervention programs. I am also interested in developing collaborative models for the integration of care between psychiatric and primary care providers.

Tina Renneisen’s Specialties:

SPECIALTIES: Individual and family therapy, pediatric mental health and play assessment, parent management groups, Girls Circle groups, Motivational Interviewing, medication management, gender responsive and trauma-informed work with young women in the juvenile justice system
