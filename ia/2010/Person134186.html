Mark Mariani’s Summary

As a Marketing Specialist at Moniker.com, a subsidiary of Oversee.net based in Pompano Beach, Florida, United States, I am afforded the opportunity to work with the world’s leading domain asset management organization, offering a full range of domain-name services, including domain appraisals, escrow, premium auctions and corporate-registrar capabilities.

Working within the marketing department, my primary focus includes market research supporting the sales team on industry trends as they relate to premium domain assets, lead generation, and auctions/sales. In doing so I generate the most valuable information as it pertains to a particular asset and author corresponding sales support documents to acquire business, generate quality leads, and support the sales function.

I also play a key role in the management and execution of Moniker.com and SnapNames.com social media marketing initiatives. I manage all aspects of the life cycle from inception to implementation, and analysis of specific measurables.

Additionally, I assist in managing Moniker.com’s direct mailing effort which includes e-mail marketing and traditional hard copy. I author and edit corresponding marketing and sales documentation as needed for Moniker and Snapnames. Also I assist in content management for Moniker.com and Snapnames.com websites which consists of creating creative copy, editing, and regular updates. I also process accounts payable for our affiliate transactions.
