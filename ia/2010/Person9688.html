Amit Jain’s Summary

Amit Jain joined Indiamart Intermesh Limited (an ISO 9001:2000 certified), India’s largest B2B marketplace & web promotion company, in March 2000 as an e-Commerce executive. Outstanding analytical skills helped him stepping up organization ladders and moving to a senior management profile within a few years.

Amit has been an integral part of enormous web projects of IndiaMART.com including Shopping, Directory, Payment gateway, B2B trade exchange, Trustseal profile & Tenders notification services.

Amit has also been involved with a variety of business roles starting from Data management, Research, Operations to New product development, Media relations, Marketing & promotion, International sales and finally to Strategic direction, business alliances & execution.

With 10 years of techno-commercial, marketing communications and project management experience, interest of Amit Jain lies in Online business management, Product Development, Marketing & Strategic Partnerships.

Amit Jain’s Specialties:

Online business management, Direction & Strategic planning, Product research & development, operations, Process re-engineering, Online Marketing/ SEO/ SEM/ SMM techniques & Alliances.
