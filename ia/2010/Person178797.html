Rune Thode Nielsen’s Summary

A profound scientific and technical background in physics and nanotechnology combined with a sharp focus on leadership defines my professional profile.

By studying MScE in Physics and Nanotechnology, and simultaneously a Graduate Certificate in Business Administration (HD1), I seek to achieve thorough educational qualifications within my primary fields. A couple of years after graduation, I hope to acquire a HD(O) and a Master of Business Administration (MBA).

Practical leadership experience is uniquely gained from my presidency at The Danish Youth Association of Science (UNF). During my previous two years as president at the Copenhagen Chapter, we have achieved unprecedented membership growth in our association (established in 1944), a quadruplication of the annual income, a healthy organisational culture, good press coverage and many other improvements. The promotion of science among youth is a very demanding yet important, rewarding challenge - this means a lot, when you work in a NGO organisation.

Having the opportunity to get comprehensive practical leadership experience in continuation of my education -already during my study period- is an invaluable privilege.

Rune Thode Nielsen’s Specialties:
