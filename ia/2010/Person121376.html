Larry Miller’s Summary

As Managing Partner at Miller & Company, I provide strategic counsel, capital markets and public policy advice to media, entertainment and technology companies in domestic and international markets. Our principle focus is on mergers, acquisitions, capital formation and business strategy in recorded music and music publishing, broadcasting, telecommunications, business information and web services.

As a Vice President at LEK Consulting, I provided growth strategy and M&A/transaction advice to clients in the media, entertainment and technology industries from a platform of 900 smart, analytical and pragmatic consultants and 85 partners in 14 countries and 20 offices around the world.

Prior to joining LEK I launched, developed and sold record label and music publisher Or Music to SonyBMG. Or Music rapidly became a Grammy Award-winning, multi-platinum selling recorded music and music publishing company and home of Los Lonely Boys, Matisyahu and other brilliant artists. Previously worked as Entrepreneur in Residence at Chazen Capital Partners, a NY based private investment partnership; President of Reciprocal Entertainment, a noted digital distribution infrastructure company backed by Microsoft, Sony, BMG, Venrock, Softbank and others; co-founder of a2b music, a digital distribution company hatched in AT&T Labs largely merged into Reciprocal in 1999; consulting partner at A.T. Kearney and EDS; broadcaster and executive at NBC Radio Entertainment and Tribune Broadcasting.

Larry Miller’s Specialties:

Management consulting practice with focus in media and entertainment; strategy and corporate development, M&A, private equity and venture capital, corporate communications and branding, product marketing, distribution, intellectual property and licensing.
