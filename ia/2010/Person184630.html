Scott Moreland’s Summary

With my years in management, even senior management, I never got writing code out of my system. At DataStor I accepted the most difficult projects, projects that we needed complete so we would get paid but no one had the time to make progress on. These projects included writing the Volume Shadowcopy Service requestors for Exhange and SQLServer, the Publish/Subscribe Event Notification component, the Search Engine and others.

I am a talented developer with deep Windows knowledge. I want the opportunity to add value to a talented team of developers by writing code.
