Marcel Meijer’s Summary

I have been a a teacher at the Amstelveencollege for three years. I teach courses in economics and mathematics. I primarily work in the age-group 15-18. It is a school for HAVO and VWO. I also work on the development of the electronic learning environment we use.

Before I became a teacher I worked with the Police of Flevoland. There I was responsible for the education of the employees. It was a chalenging and diverse job. Some employees were send to outside institutions but I also organized incompany training.

I wanted an change in my carreer. I had been working behind a desk for a couple of years and I needed a change. In 2003 I applied for a job as a teaching assistant. It was a step down, but also an opportunity to gain experience. I worked for a year at the Huygens college in Amsterdam. After that I took up a teaching position in Haarlem. That was a VMBO school. That was very challenging at first, but I learned quickly. In 2006 I accepted my current position at the Amstelveencollege.

Since 2003 I have been studying to become a teacher. At that I have been succesfull. I now have three Bachelor degrees and a Masters degree. I am still studying for a Masters degree in mathematics education. Studying has been like a hobby for me the last couple of years. I only have one year to go and then I am finished. I am already thinking about what study I should do next.
