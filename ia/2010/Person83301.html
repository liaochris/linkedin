Hugh Massie’s Summary

DNA Strategic Advisor and entrepreneur with 23 years of unique and diverse international experience in human behavior, financial and tax services, and family and business advisory.

President and Founder of DNA Behavior International, a consulting firm now based in Atlanta GA which was founded in Sydney in 1999.

I also provide independent strategic advice to individuals and families with significant privately held global business interests.

Because it can be easier to connect with others when you know what their communication preferences and desires are, I have been building my network using our DNA Behavior Marketing system (http://www.dnabehaviormarketing.com). This marketing system uncovers a person’s key life and financial motivations so that I can quickly connect with my clients and prospects, and also more confidently connect them to the right choices based on knowing their unique DNA Behavior.
