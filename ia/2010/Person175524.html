Robin Gerlach’s Summary

I Graduated from San Diego State University in May 2007 with a Bachelors Degree in Business Management. Having traveled to 23 countries and living in South America (Buenos Aires, Argentina) for a year I understand firsthand the increasingly globalized marketplace we live in. I taught Business ESL English for one year helping anyone from advertising executives to bankers enhance their English in order to be more marketable in their respective marketplace. Helping others become successful is my largest goal throughout my life and career. After my return from South America, I worked in numerous sales positions and enhanced my sales resume. My latest career interests range from outdoors/recreational (Monarch Ski resort) and sharing the beautiful language of Spanish with others. I have my own Spanish/English tutoring business on the side and this proves to be more rewarding everyday. I enjoy a healthy lifestyle and would ideally love to work in the active lifestyle or travel industry as a business strategy consultant. I am very analytical and perceptive so I feel that I could be a great asset to any organization. I look forward to meeting you!
