Kathy Jordan’s Summary

My mission in life is to of help other people get in better shape, improve their health and end the struggle with their weight once and for all. I have over 25 years of personal and professional experience in behavioral weight management and weight loss, fitness and nutrition coaching. Coaching is a practice that is usually done by phone and online so clients can live anywhere and still benefit by partnering with me to achieve their weight and fitness goals. I also offer classes and workshops in my local area including South Shore/ SE Massachusetts.
I am a licensed, registered dietitian (American Dietetic Association) with both a BS and MS in food science and nutrition. I am a National Academy of Sports Medicine certified personal trainer and a certified wellness coach through Coach Training Alliance.

Kathy Jordan’s Specialties:

eating behavior management, weight loss coaching, personal fitness training, nutrition counseling, public speaking, work site wellness, inspiring and motivating individuals to adopt healthier lifestyles.
