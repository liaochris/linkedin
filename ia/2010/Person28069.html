Brian Ascher’s Summary

I am a venture capitalist with Venrock Associates and focus on Internet, Consumer and Enterprise Software investments.

Currently I serve on the Boards of Vocera Communications, Awarepoint, Atrenta, Retail Solutions Inc, ccBetty, Martini Media and Nombray. I was previously on the Board of Adify prior to their acquisition by Cox Enterprises, Datallegro prior to their acquisition by Microsoft, Relay Health prior to their acquisition by McKesson, and Unicru prior to their acquisition by Kronos. Before Venrock I was Senior Product Manager for Intuit's Quicken.com website and the award-winning Quicken for Windows product. Previous to Intuit, I worked as a Strategy Consultant at Monitor Company where I advised clients on issues of management and strategy.

I have a BA, magna cum laude, from Princeton University and a MBA from Stanford University Graduate School of Business.

Brian Ascher’s Specialties:

Funding early stage high tech startups, particularly Internet, consumer, software, and healthcare IT. My background is tilted towards marketing, product management, and strategy.
