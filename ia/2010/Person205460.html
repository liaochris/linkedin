Todd Laabs’s Summary

Ten year P&G veteran with a wide range of company experiences; from manufacturing to traditional (big brand) CPG marketing to Prestige brand marketing. Worked through the P&G acquisition & integration of the Wella/Sebastian companies and now the integration of Nioxin into P&G Salon Professional division. Classic P&G Brand Building training with a deep knowledge & passion for package design smartly linked to production feasibility & cost optimization. Career focus in beauty care and prestige product marketing.
