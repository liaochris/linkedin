Paul Burgess’s Summary

Paul Burgess is a senior level marketing, digital, entertainment & sales professional with top tier experience in developing brands, leading campaigns and growing revenue. As Executive Vice President for TVT Records he led the marketing, digital, promotions and sales and played a key role in TVT being named #1 Independent Record Label for five consecutive years (Billboard).

Burgess is an integrated marketing expert with deep understanding in digital marketing, strategy, social media, campaign & project management, mobile, content development, partnerships, viral & experiential promotions.

He is currently consulting non-profits, digital media & content companies.
