Anthony Sonego’s Summary

I am an advocate of understanding the user before designing a solution; in essence, research should inform design. I also believe that collaboration benefits the design process. In terms of the user experience team, researchers, usability analysts, interaction designers and visual artists should be encouraged to contribute and bring their skills and insight about the user to the table. This will not only improve the design of the solution, but strengthen the skill-set of the UX team as they work together and learn from each other.

As design lead for T-Hub, I engaged with a variety of stakeholders; product managers, technical leads, quality assurance test teams, marketing groups and content providers. This experience provided me with a broad perspective and an excellent understanding of business and technical constraints which must also be considered as part of a design methodology.

My aim is to work as part of a dynamic UX team on a variety of projects and be able to provide creative solutions which improve not only the quality of products and services offered, but the experience and sense of satisfaction our customers have when engaging with such products or services.
