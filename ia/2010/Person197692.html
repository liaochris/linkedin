Susan Brown’s Summary

My experience, abilities, and passions are uniquely aligned to develop leaders who want to optimize their gifts and maximize their contributions in keeping with their purpose. My clients are people who: 1) lead from their most powerful place where their core talents and life experiences intersect with their core values and 2) embrace their own growth to fully utilize their heart, mind and spirit. I specialize in working with leaders of non-profit organizations.

Results are attained through various coaching models which include: brain-based, performance-based and transformational coaching. Specialty areas include:
Emotional Intelligence
Change Management
Purposeful Leadership
Power of Story
Spiritual Growth and Leadership

Certifications include: Emotional Intelligence; ChangeWorks; Managerial Coaching; Live A New Life Story: The Art and Science of Change, Reinvention and Success; Performance-Based Coaching

Susan Brown’s Specialties:

Change management, educational leadership, emotional intelligence, power of story, life empowerment and success strategies, leadership development, brain-based coaching, transformational coaching and performance based coaching
