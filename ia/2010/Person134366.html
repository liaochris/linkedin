Marko Vujovic’s Summary

I've started as a sales engineer for a company acting as a representative of several leading telecom manufacturers in Serbia and neighbouring countries. My responsibilities were dealing with customers interested in:
CATV passive equipment - connectors (Cabelcon), coax and FO cables (Commscope), coax and FO tools and accessories
power supplies, both AC UPS and DC rectifiers (Alpha)
RF products for GSM operators: coax feeders, connectors and base station antennas, as well as tools and accessories (RFS), low power GSM repeaters (Dekolink Wireless and AdCoCom), hi power GSM repeaters, and FO distributed indoor coverage solutions (Avitec), test equipment (Anritsu), etc...

My next job was project design engineer with Kodar Engineering, one of the major subcontractors for cellular operators in Serbia and Montenegro. Was in charge of project design and site survey. I've been working for two start-up clients, one GSM/UMTS/WiMAX operator in Montenegro (MTEL), and the other a GSM/UMTS operator in Serbia (Vip Mobile).
As the project design buro grew, my next position within the company was head of RR department, working mostly on transmission networks for various clients using various equipment (NEC, Siemens, Nera, Ericsson, Marconi).

As of June 2008. I am working for Nera Networks as a system engineer. Being that I'm a part of a rather small team, my responsibilities greatly vary, from customer trainer to logistics.
