Stephane Beladaci’s Summary

Software engineer with over 4 years experience with Flex SDK and Flash Media Server and 10 years experience building the web.

Agile, scrum trained, very knowledgeable with good OOP design patterns, best practices, social media, the space and open api's.

I combined the skills of an excellent Flex / ActionScript programmer with those of a proficient Flex designer (styling, skinning, layout).

Currently located in Los Angeles and available for full time or part time contract.

-----------------

I focus since 2005 on the development of Web 2.0 Rich Internet Application (RIA) in a Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) using Adobe Technologies, and since 2008 on the integration between Adobe Flex, AIR and Flash Media Server with social networks.

Multimedia and communication background; dynamic entrepreneurial spirit and startup attitude; advanced analysis capability, logical mind, multidisciplinary skills, creative thinking and European culture.

Logical mind and analysis skills allow me to take on any technology or programming language in no time, and deliver production quality project at prototype speed. Creative thinking and cultural background add a unique artistic touch to my work.

Meanwhile, the multidisciplinary nature of my skill-sets creates a synergy and increases coherence between the code, the look and the functionality aspects of a project, breaking boundaries between programmers, designers and marketers.

Design patterns, best practices and user accessibility are the current focus of my work as a developer.

Excellent presentation and written skills (business and marketing centric proposals, technical specifications) in both French and English.
