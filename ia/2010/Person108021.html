Julia Ball’s Summary

My passion is Retail, though I have a wide range of experience that extends to HR and Office Administration and Management. My last two positions have been in Merchandising and Buying, both in Fashion and Home Furnishings. I thought my interest would lean more towards Fashion, but after working in both areas, I have found that Home Furnishings is where my skills shine.

I have also worked in HR and Office Administration/Management for two PR firms in the Bay Area, both of which were very rewarding, being in a creative environment. I've met a great amount of interesting people in PR, and if an opportunity arises to work for another company such as these two, I would not overlook it by any means.

My immediate goal is to find a Merchandising position in the Home Furnishings field of retail, though I am absolutely not opposed to exploring opportunities in Fashion, or HR/Office Administration roles within a creative environment.

My ultimate goal is to expand my Merchandising/Planning experience, and open my own boutique here in the Bay Area.
