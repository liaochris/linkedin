Andrew Stockton’s Summary

Andrew Stockton is the Managing Partner at Gilmore Partners and brings many years of Executive Search experience to the organization. As the Managing Partner, Andrew is responsible for overseeing the overall management and strategic direction of the firm while still actively serving his own client base.

Andrew’s clients range from small boutique businesses to very large Fortune 500 companies. These clients come from a range of different sectors that include but are not limited to: Financial Services, Legal, Accounting, Consulting, Real Estate, Information Technology, and Consumer Packaged Goods. This industry expertise is the key to his success in making the best employer-employee connections.


I was recruited to join Gilmore Partners in 2005 while at another nationally recognized firm and was named a Partner in 2006 and later named Managing Partner in 2008.

Gilmore Partners is a Human Capital Management Firm that has been in the business of identifying, locating and sourcing the top 10% of candidates in the job market for over 25 years at the corner of Bay and Bloor. Our firm has a diverse background of experienced recruiters that come from a range of different backgrounds in the recruitment industry. All brought together to form one highly successful firm.

We constantly deliver top talent for our clients and strong opportunities for our hand selected candidates. We treat each and every single person the way in which we would want to be treated. Your search will be dealt with the utmost respect in a professional and confidential manner.

Andrew Stockton’s Specialties:

My firm deals with recruiting for Accounting, Finance, IT, Operations, Sales & Marketing positions.
