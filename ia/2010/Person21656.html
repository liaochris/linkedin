Benjamin Lichtenwalner’s Summary

Benjamin Lichtenwalner has a broad background in business technology, including experience in support, programming, architecture, analysis, management and leadership roles. Lichtenwalner has implemented solutions or overseen the rapid growth of technology services at companies including Inc. 500, Fortune 500 and non-profit organizations. Currently, Lichtenwalner is the Senior Manager of Internet and eCommerce at Whirlpool Corporation, where he leads efforts globally for the web presence and eCommerce channel across all brands, including Maytag, KitchenAid, Amana, Bauknecht, Jenn-Air and more.

Benjamin is also a strong proponent of Servant-Leadership and works to educate others on the benefits of humble leadership through service first.
