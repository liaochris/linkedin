Lakshman Pernenkil’s Summary

Lakshman Pernenkil has a PhD in Chemical Engineering (Pharmaceutical Manufacturing) from MIT and an MBA from MIT Sloan. He has 6 years of technical and business experience of supporting life-science companies in manufacturing, supply chain operations and international supply chain operations activities. At Deloitte, his experience includes enterprise cost reduction, merger and integration synergy capture and manufacturing strategy and planning within the life science industry.

Prior to Deloitte he has worked in designing a playbook for international operations for one of the largest biotechnology companies and consulted with 8 big pharmaceutical companies (GSK, Novartis, J&J, Wyeth, Abbott, Sanofi-Aventis, Roche and BMS) as part of a research consortium on improving pharmaceutical manufacturing. He has been in an advisory role for the executive management of a start-up in Boston on their market entry strategy for a novel antibiotic coating technology for medical devices and a business lead devising market launch, business and operating model strategy for a business plan on a novel drug delivery technology developed at a lab in MIT.

Lakshman Pernenkil’s Specialties:
