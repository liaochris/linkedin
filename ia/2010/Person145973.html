Michael Schechter’s Summary

Mike helps businesses and individuals on a variety of legal matters, trains on conflict resolution skills, and provides mediation and arbitration services. As the COO and General Counsel of ChartHouse Learning, he designs and negotiates contracts from vendor to sales, drafts international and domestic agreements, protects and licenses intellectual property, oversees HR matters, and manages business and legal issues.

Mike graduated from the highly rated NYU School of Law in 1993, clerked with a US Court of Appeals and Mn Court of Appeals, and practiced in litigation for over ten years. He earned the Minnesota State Bar Association’s President’s Award in 2003 and has been elected to various Bar positions.

Michael Schechter’s Specialties:

Litigation, intellectual property, contract negotiation, distribution, international, employment, risk management and conflict resolution.
