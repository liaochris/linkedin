Stephen McCrudden’s Summary

Innovative mobile broadband and wireless telecom technology pioneer with broad product management, in depth project management, new product development and business development expertise. Senior-level experience and successful track record in the mobile ecosystem including carriers, equipment vendors, content providers and application developers in the wireless, digital media, public sector, security and consumer industries. Pioneering application portfolio development, including streaming video over wireless networks and video sharing.
• Leadership roles and accountability in the product design, development and delivery of wireless services, mobile video content, messaging, information services, collaboration services, land mobile radio, wireless broadband data services, security, location-based services, and content transformation.
• Strategic vision, analytical mindset and detailed business model understanding that results in superior relationship management with principle investors, clients and vendors. Negotiation of business and alliance partner relationships insuring timely development and delivery of products.
• Multiple startup experience with live mobile video, broadband digital media, GPS chip technology, WiFi MESH, metropolitan area network deployment, mobile application development and eCommerce.
• Outstanding record of bringing leading-edge services to market on-time and on-budget across the US including CDMA, GSM, GPRS, EDGE, WiMAX and Metro WiFi (MESH) deployments.
