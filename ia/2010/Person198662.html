Steve Woodruff’s Summary

I provide a free service to life sciences companies, helping identify optimal outsource suppliers for sales training and marketing.

+ Matching training clients with best-in-class vendor/partners
+ Helping top providers find ideal clients and projects to expand their business base
+ Leading workshops on Vendor Selection/Management
+ Brainstorming and implementing creative training and marketing strategies
+ Consulting with clients on training, marketing, compliance, and technical solutions
+ Leading teams in system and product development efforts
+ Creating alliances with quality suppliers
