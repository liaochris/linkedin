Brian Jenkins’s Summary

Custom Computer Solutions, LLC has been in business for over 3 years now. We serve residential, home business, and business customers with evening and weekend hours.

We are a Better Business Bureau member, so you know we're fanatical about service, and also part of the Nordonia and Twinsburg Chambers or Commerce. Owner graduated from the University of Akron in May 2009 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering. People in IT and electronics/electrical engineering will recognize my membership in both the IEEE and IEEE Computer Society.

Brian & company specialize in computer repair and consulting; working on desktops, laptops, and servers. Custom Computer Solutions helps businesses stay in business (disaster recovery, back-ups, etc) and residents repair their computers at fair prices. Virus removal, networking (wired and wireless), upgrades, installations, backups, consulting, general service, and maintenance are a few of our niches. We also do things other IT companies don't, such as repair and replacement of laptop DC power jacks, among other things.

For fun, Brian enjoys exercise, socializing, pool, and geeky stuff such as programming micro-controllers, building computers, robots, and other electronic projects, and reading.
