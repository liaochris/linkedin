Chris Manning’s Summary

At age 23, I have been producing sports videos for over 6 years now; most notably my “LD2k” Los Angeles Lakers videos (non-commercial gain). My work has gained worldwide recognition and has been invited into the hearts of millions of fans. In the summer of 2007, I became a pioneer and co-owner of The Lakers Nation, LLC; one of the largest and most successful Lakers websites on the internet. My work has been seen on LD2k.com, TheLakersNation.com, LakersGround.net, KB24.com, AndrewBynum.com and AM570radio.com among other websites.
