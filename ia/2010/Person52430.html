David Sadigh’s Summary

David is a recognized online marketing expert and entrepreneur.
He is the co-founder and managing partner of IC-Agency (www.ic-agency.com) and a board member of Holistis (www.holistis.com)

David is also the coach of several top executives for online projects and a frequent keynote speaker at industry international events such as AD:Tech, LIFT, INSEAD Alumni, HEC Lausanne (Strategis), Edipresse Digital Day, Publigroupe Micro MBA, Convetion e-Commerce Paris, etc.

He is also member of the scientific committee of the Luxury Postgrad of the University of Geneva and a regular guest writers for the economic press.

About IC-Agency:
IC-Agency is a leading independant online marketing firm working for international clients such as Nespresso, Dior, Alfa Romeo or Patek Philippe. IC-Agency is composed by gifted search engine marketers, paid analyst, web analytics experts and experienced project managers. We have offices both in Europe (Geneva, Switzerland) and in North America (Toronto and Bathurst, Canada).

About Holistis:
Holistis is a fast-growing start-up providing an innovative personalization engine based on smart algorithms able to improve conversion rates. Holistis recently won the TF1 prize at Le Web 3 conference in Paris.

David Sadigh’s Specialties:

online marketing, web analytics, e-commerce, competitive intelligence, reputation management, online market research, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, online advertising, pay per click campaign, social media marketing, etc.
