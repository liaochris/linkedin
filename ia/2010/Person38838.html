Christian Paulsen’s Summary

Christian is an Executive Consultant with a passion for Continuous Improvement and will bring value to your organization. He brings a wealth of hands-on experience and leadership. Christian brings a track record of driving out losses and waster while improving Safety, Quality, and improving Efficiencies. He brings the Continuous Improvement tools of Lean Manufacturing, Six Sigma, DMAIC and Total Perfect Manufacturing (TPM). Christian instills a Zero Loss mentality while teaching how to bring sustained excellence in your organization. He also excels in general Leadership and is available for Leadership consultations / training.

Christian's expertise is recognized in the industry. His work has been published by Food Processing Magazine and Manufacturing Report. His blog is a Recommended Corporate Leadership Resource of Six Disciplines.com

He is services are available if you contact him via paulsen-christian@att.net.

Christian’s experience includes the use of Lean principles in Food and Beverage manufacturing plants. Christian successfully led Kaizen Teams with a track record of yield and efficiency gains, process improvement, cost reduction, as well as safety and quality improvement in ten manufacturing plants competing in highly competitive markets. Christian’s experience also includes the TPM Instructor Course training and leading the implementations of 5S, Autonomous Maintenance, and TPM.

Christian attended Purdue University on a Navy ROTC scholarship and received a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Mathematics. After serving in the US Navy, Christian pursued a career in manufacturing with Frito-Lay, Unilever (Lipton), and Nestle USA as well as smaller private manufacturers. Christian also has MBA coursework at Lehigh and Regent Universities.

Experience also includes:

*20 years in 10 manufacturing plants
*7 years at Plant Manager, Operations Manager, or Manufacturing Manager level
*Change Management
*Lean / Six Sigma / TPM

Christian Paulsen’s Specialties:

Continuous Improvement of manufacturing plants utilizing Lean principles. Kaizen, DMAIC, 5S, TPM, Root Cause Analysis, Cause & Effect Diagram, Control Charts, SPC, Pareto Diagrams, SMED, etc.

Food & Beverage Manufacturing: Edy's Ice Cream, Nestle Drumsticks, Lipton Soups and Side Dishes, Lawry's and Adolph's Seasonings, Lipton Tea, Lipton Instant Tea, Margarine (Imperial, Promise, Country Crock), Skippy Peanut Butter, Frito-Lay Potato and Corn Chip products, Private Label Nut and Trail Mix.
