Bonnie The Media Group 3’s Summary

We’d like to introduce you to The Media Group 3, a full-service advertising agency uniting three of North Central Florida’s most prolific advertising veterans.

Whether you want to grow market share, communicate more effectively with stakeholders, or build stronger relationships with dealers, The Media Group 3 specializes in helping organizations just like yours. We work with you to define markets and goals, then focus on executing marketing campaigns designed to meet those goals.
The founding members of The Media Group 3 have helped hundreds of companies meet their goals for over a decade now. Based right here in Ocala, Florida, our marketing talents have created award-winning campaigns for organizations from multinational manufacturers and retailers to local startups and service companies.

Our group shares a unique commitment to producing exceptionally high-quality results with a keen eye for deadlines and a focus on serving you right.
The combination of our team’s specializations and various professional alliances allow us to offer comprehensive marketing solutions tailored to your company's needs. No cookie-cutter approaches here.

With over 20 years’ experience in competitive markets and dozens of awards and client testimonials, we offer access to client-focused strategic thinking and creative talents typically found only in larger cities.

Approachable Personalities
You’ll quickly see we’re friendly folks who are easy to talk to. In fact, you can even speak directly to the artist working on your materials - we look forward to your input as much or as little as you like to be involved.

Reasonable Rates
We are open and honest about our rates and always transparent with you about costs. We understand the dynamics of the marketing budget and always look to price our services reasonably.

We invite you to get to know us today. Just call 352.304.5304 or email us at info@themediagroup3.com to find out how we can help you meet your marketing goals.
