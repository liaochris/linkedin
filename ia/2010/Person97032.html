Jessica Kitchin’s Summary

Jessica has quickly become a go-to recruiter at Yoh. Since being promoted to Team Lead, Jessica oversees a team of Yoh Talent Solutions recruiters in the King of Prussia office dedicated to fulfillment nationwide for several Managed Services accounts.
