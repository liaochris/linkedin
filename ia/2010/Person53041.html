Joshua R. Beal, MBA, RFC®’s Summary

Schwarz Financial Services LLC has been serving the deaf community since 1971. We seek to provide financial services to the whole deaf community.

Our firm's primary offering is a fee-based discretionary investment management service for affluent and deaf clients which includes executing short and long-term investment trading strategies, ongoing planning and preparation of tax returns, and development of comprehesive financial plans for the present and future retirement years.

Our retail tax service division is branded under DeafTax.com, the longest operating tax preparation service for the US deaf community since 1971. Deaf clients can email their tax documents to us and communicate over the Video-Phone to complete their personal income tax returns. This is all done over the Internet.

For the second straight year, in cooperation with National Disability Institute (www.reitour.org), the DeafTax.com VITA Pilot will serve low-income deaf taxpayers during the 2009 tax season. In 2008,DeafTax.com volunteers prepared tax returns, free of charge, for 80 deaf families and generated $147,000 in refunds.
