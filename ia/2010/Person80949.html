Heather Shirin Thomas’s Summary

Graphic design lead with over 10 years full time professional web and print design experience. Especially skilled in creating and directing user interface design, catalog production and internet marketing campaigns. Expertise with digital imaging software for editing, retouching, printing, and display. Passion for detail, composition, color and layout with a background in fine art.
