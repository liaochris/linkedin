Emma Long’s Summary

I wish to lift indiviuals up to a level of loving languages and their respective cultures, similar to that of my own. I believe that learning language is an intregal part of mental, social and civic developement. I love to guide peoples' focus to the purpse of learning language early on in a school year, and discuss how communication in a different language can open up doors of opportunity for them to meet people from around the world, engage in global projects, enhance their logic and mathematical skills, broaden their musical and cultural exposure, and develop their conflict resolution and problem solving skills.

I enjoy introducing my students to penpals from countries like Taiwan, and sharing with them books, movies and other authentic material and media that showcase what life is like for kids their age in the countries of those languages we are studying. My great love for and background in traditional music from around the world adds wonderfully to my classes and brings even closer the personal aspect that learning a language must include. I bring in my guitar for a class and we sing Brazillian and Chinese folksongs. Being a crafter enables me to bring in such projects as calligraphy, fiesta art, and other cultural crafts that adds the hands-on aspect to language learning.

I am a very innovative thinker when it comes to developing curriculum and assignments that realistically encourages students to master the material. I enjoy greatly the creative energy used in developing curriculum and am very flexible in going more or less in depth in an area of content if I feel the need arise.

My husband and I have recently moved to Portsmouth I am actively pursuing opportunities to share my knowledge of China and Mandarin Chinese in the area.
