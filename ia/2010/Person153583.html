Niama Sandy’s Summary

Niama Sandy is the Vice President of Publicity at Fanfare Management & Publicity. A graduate of Howard University's illustrious John H. Johnson School of Communications and a lifelong creator, lover, and patron of the arts and education, Niama is devoted to excellence and innovation in education, music, media and publicity.

Niama has worked in the Public Relations field for a number of years in a range of capacities at such organizations as Fleishman-Hillard International Communications, the National Retail Federation, Strauss Radio Strategies in Washington, DC, as well, Harriet's Alter Ego, a fashion boutique and art gallery based in New York, and others.

Niama has also worked a freelance writer for various organizations and international publications. Her work has appeared in print and online publications in the U.S., Canada and the Caribbean, including LadyBrille.com, Toronto, Canada's WE Magazine, Jam Rock Magazine, and many others.

Most recently, Niama has become involved in education: coordinating out-of-school time programming with the District of Columbia Public Schools; and facilitating with the ever-dynamic Monster Diversity Leadership Programs.

She knows what she wants out of life and great opportunities have a way of chasing her; but in the words of Louis Pasteur, "chance favors the prepared mind."

Feel free to reach out with any networking and development opportunities!

Niama Sandy’s Specialties:

Branding, Out-of-school-time Programming, Grant Writing, Online Media, Youth Service, Event Planning, Press & Magazine Writing, Relationship Building
