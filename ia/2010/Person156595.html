Christopher Nutile’s Summary

Christopher Nutile joined MediaLink LLC to launch the organization’s Retained Executive Search practice. One of the most respected professionals in the search and talent acquisition sector, he brings more than 15 years of staffing and global human resources experience to MediaLink, with noted success in both the online media and digital advertising space.

Prior to joining MediaLink, he was consulting for Google, where he directed recruiting efforts for the company’s newly formed Agency Relations organization, as well as for the Direct Sales operation within Google Canada.

In addition to working for Google, Mr. Nutile worked for online search engine giant Yahoo! where he headed up staffing efforts for Media Sales; and was instrumental in building their sales organizations within the United States and Canada.

In 2008 , Nutile became Yahoo’s first dedicated HR and Staffing professional embedded with the company’s Miami based Latin American Division. Previously, Mr Nutile worked as Director of Recruiting for digital agency powerhouse Razorfish. He served on the Razorfish Global Executive HR team and was responsible for building the organization’s headcount from 600 to 2200 in two years.
