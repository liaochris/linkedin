Gianluigi Mascolo’s Summary

Spent the last ten years (1999-2009) working as a program manager, industrial designer, mechanical engineer, , graphic designer, and international business liaison for successful corporations such as PowerLight (now Sunpower), Solyndra, Valextra Milano and Coolsystems. During this time, I have earned 26 patents and I have approximately 21 products/services currently in production and sold world wide. I invented and developed PowerLight’s best selling rooftop product (T-10).
At OptiSolar, I lead the design of the biggest SolarFarm in the World (600MW) working as a team with very bright and already successful entrepreneurs.
At Solyndra and Shuco, I am the product Development Manager for the development of commercial solar application, working cross-functionally with groups including R&D, Manufacturing, Sales and Business Development; taking the product from conception to release.
As a very creative designer I have also worked in Milan, Italy for Valextra, a high-end luggage company, where I was responsible for designing new products. While focusing on reducing cost and improving the look and feel of products such as a suitcase, briefcases, I substantiated design decisions by analyses and market studies. I also interned at Ducati motorcycles in Bologna, Italy where I created concepts for the Supermono 600cc body.
I have earned a Bachelor and Master of Science (MS) in Industrial Design from the Polytechnic of Milan, a prestigious Italian University for Engineering and Design. My thesis was on a renewable energy multifunctional product.

Goal: Loking for European partnership for the development of a new Solar Application in the agricultural market.
