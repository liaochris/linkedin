Christy A. Zeyer’s Summary

Christy A. Zeyer is an OSBA Certified Paralegal employed in the Estate, Trust and Guardianship Administration practice areas at the Cleveland Law Firm, Hickman & Lowder Co. L.P.A. She earned a Paralegal Certificate from Notre Dame College of Ohio and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Sociology from Bowling Green State University. She has worked as a Paralegal for over ten years, including employment in the states of Florida and Ohio. Prior to her work as a Paralegal, Christy was a Caseworker/Coordinator for Big Brothers/Big Sisters of Northwestern Ohio and a Deputy Clerk with the Lake County Common Pleas Clerk of Courts. She is a Paralegal Associate Member of the Ohio State Bar Association, Paralegal Member of the Cleveland Metropolitan Bar Association, Paralegal Associate Member of the Lake County Bar Association, Voting Member of the Cleveland Association of Paralegals, Inc. (CAP), CAP President (2010 - 2011), CAP Secretary/Parliamentarian (2009 - 2010), CAP Probate Committee Co-Coordinator (2008 - 2010), created and is the moderator for the CAP group page on Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter, Member of the National Federation of Paralegal Associations, Inc. (NFPA), is a member of the Ursuline College Paralegal Alumnae Mentoring Board, member of the Chancellor University Paralegal Advisory Board, an Alumna of Alpha Omicron Pi, Alpha Psi Chapter, Web Master for the Alpha Omicron Pi Alumnae Chapter, Cleveland - East, and is an Ohio Notary Public. Volunteer for Fugitive Safe Surrender.
