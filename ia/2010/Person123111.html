Leah Graves’s Summary

Background:

I have 14 years of experience in client-facing roles in marketing, client services, and business development. I enjoy working with clients to address their individual project and program needs. From 2003-2010 I worked for technology companies that create products and services for the following:

-Wellness Programs
-Perks Programs/ Employee Recognition
-Culture Assessments and Employee Satisfaction Surveys
-Employee Discount Programs

In 2010 I took on a new challenge and began to work in the insurance field. I work exclusively with dentists and dental students.

Education:

During 2009 I completed my master's degree in Business Administration from Framingham State University. I went to FSU for undergrad as well and I graduated with a BA (cum laude honors) in Communications.

Photography contract work:

I am a member of the Worcester Photography Meetup Group. My first solo photo exhibit was in 2006 in Framingham (at Starbucks!). During the early 2000s I was a contract photographer for the Framingham State University alumni magazine. I enjoyed photographing gallery openings, plays, alumni events and more! Currently I assist photographers in the Boston area as a Photographer's Assistant.

Green living:

In my down time I love to stay in my off-the-grid vacation home in Maine. It is through that home that I am able to educate friends and family about Eco-friendly energy solutions.

This home has been a labor of love for my husband and I since 2003.

Volunteer work:

I also do volunteer work for a variety of organizations including Mass Audubon and Life is good. Currently I am a mentor through the Center for Women's Leadership at Babson College. I mentor an undergraduate student that is part of the mentor program.
