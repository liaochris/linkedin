Benjamin Williams’s Summary

Mr. Williams is an attorney at a property tax law firm in New York City. Prior to joining the firm, he was an appraisal and valuation consultant and engineer.

Benjamin is an Accredited Senior Appraiser of the American Society of Appraisers. He holds a B.S. in Electrical & Computer Engineering and a Juris Doctor law degree. He is admitted to the bars of New York and New Jersey.

Benjamin has vast experience appraising over $75 billion worth of properties from the Sears Tower to nuclear power plants.

Benjamin has attended or presented at a variety of programs including International Association of Assessing Officers conferences, American Society of Appraisers international conferences and local chapter meetings, New York State Society of CPAs – Foundation for Accounting Education conferences, Society of Depreciation Professionals annual training and meeting, Wichita Appraisal for Ad Valorem Taxation of Communications, Energy, and Transportation Properties workshop and program, Financial Consulting Group conferences, and RSMeans construction cost estimation seminar.

Benjamin previously served on the board of the Northern New Jersey chapter of the American Society of Appraisers.

Property tax assessment proceedings, certiorari, abatements, and exemption matters, Cost segregation, Purchase price allocation, Machinery & equipment, Depreciation, Fair [market] valuation, Liquidation valuation
