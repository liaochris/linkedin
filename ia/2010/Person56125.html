Diane Cohen’s Summary

As an attorney and a highly-trained mediator, my practice goes in two directions. As mediator and dispute resolution professional, I tremendously enjoy helping people improve communication and collaboration and work toward a resolution, if possible.

In the other direction, I enjoy using my legal training and analytical skills in my work as an arbitrator, hearing officer and attorney.

My work as a mediator is primarily in the workplace and family/divorce fields, but I also take on cases of all types.

I also work as a coach assisting individuals in thinking about and working toward their goals. I think of it as mediating between the parts of a person's mind that are in conflict. I have a certificate in personal and life coaching from NYU.

In addition, I am sometimes employed as an arbitrator of securities cases for FINRA and as a hearing officer of special education cases.

I am on several panels and rosters in these various realms. I also enjoy writing about mediation and serving as a mentor, and have published articles on the process of mediation.
