Mark Fournier’s Summary

It is through the creation and constant promotion of its brand that a company distinguishes itself in the marketplace. Throughout my career I have devoted myself to maintaining companies’ brand identities through the effective promotion of their products and services, resulting in increased profitability, consumer loyalty and retention. I understand that effective research and planning draws in the best prospects, and providing a strategic follow-up plan turns leads into long-term clients. Whether it's business-to-business marketing or business-to-consumer marketing, I strive to know my clients’ target market. I team with sales, operations and marketing staff to understand the demographic characteristics, lifestyle behaviors and purchase preferences that drive an audience's decisions.

Having worked at Fortune 500 companies like Maritz and companies recognized by the St. Louis Business Journal like Full-Circle Solutions and French Gerleman, I believe that my previous experience in fast-paced, sales-driven marketing environments and success with running my own business is an asset to a corporation or organization in need of a results oriented leader.
