Bjoern Lasse Herrmann’s Summary

I am a 25 year old purpose driven serial entrepreneur.
I founded and led 4 ventures in Germany, Bangladesh & the US.
I also worked as an executive in Russia for 1 year.
My areas of expertise are broadly centered around technology & education.

My personal passion / my "leitmotif" is: unleashing human potential.

Most recently I built Supercool School - a platform with more than 1800 online schools and more than 35000 students. The platform is used by various ivy league universities, Fortune 50 companies and passionate individuals to educate the world. Our Mission it to give people worldwide access to education.

I am also accelerating early stage ventures with techVenture. We do financial and operational investments in high impact companies. Our specialty is to support ventures in their team building, global go to market and rapid prototyping.

As a side project we launched an initiative called Cofounder Network that helps matching high impact founders across the globe. We have numerous local and global partners to facilitate the matching process - including Sandbox, Seedcamp, Khosla Ventures, etc.

In my "free time" I teach on Startup School - school I built for more than 2000 entrepreneurs around the world. With contributors such as Mike Maples, Brad Feld, Jason Fried, Steve Jurventson, Marc Benioff, etc.
+ I speak from time to time at various conferences about entrepreneurship, technology, innovation and education.
+ I mentor & advise various entrepreneurs/startups.
+ I am the Sandbox Ambassador for the Bay Area

Sometimes I blog at:

    Supercool School

    (Privately Held; E-Learning industry)

    November 2008 — Present (2 years )

    Our mission it to give people worldwide access to education. The best way to do that seemed to decentralize and democratize education by empowering passionate educators around the world.

    Therefore we built a platform that allows everybody to create their own school online. Essentially we are doing for education what blogging has done for publishing.

    Each school is a mix of ning and webex. We stripped the learning management component to the minimum and added the functionality to allow people to request classes as well. By doing this we built a simple way for people to build the curriculum collaboratively. Because of its social networking component each school becomes a powerful community that evolves organically over time.
