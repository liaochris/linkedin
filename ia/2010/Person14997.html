Anthony Silvestri’s Summary

Mr. Silvestri has extensive experience with high-volume salons and day spas. Throughout his tenure he demonstrated a consistent record of increasing salon profitability by reducing overhead and fixed salon costs which contributed to achieving salon operational goals and increasing salon efficiencies. Implemented successful ordering and inventory processes to increase cash on hand for each location while helping employees exceed sales and service quotas. Specializes in increasing guest service by implementing internal guest service objectives allowing salons to analyze their employees and customers overall satisfaction. He currently holds the position of Chief Operating Officer at Sella Skincare where he brings his knowledge from the salon and spa industry to create salon and spa friendly products and services to help them with their bottom line by increasing profitability.
