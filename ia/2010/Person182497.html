Sarah Clachar’s Summary

Natural Health Copywriting/Marketing

Despite the economy - or maybe because of it - more people are turning to natural health solutions. If your company serves this market, now's the time to engage them.

And Healthy Marketing Ideas will give you the help you need to do so.

Healthy Marketing Ideas gives you a solid combo of direct response copywriting, marketing strategy and natural health market insights.

Your projects benefit from my experience as a natural health copywriter, nutrition industry trade journalist, health educator, farmer, mother and biologist. One heady mix of communications savvy. www.healthymarketingideas.com.

Healthy Marketing Ideas provides copywriting and marketing services to natural health B2C and B2B companies.

Both online/SEO and offline copywriting.

Editorial/white papers as well as sales copy.

- "17 Health Copywriting Tactics for a Tough Economy" covers several ways to keep your customer base strong.

- "Turn Your White Papers and Website into a Recession-Busting Dynamic Duo" is geared mostly towards B2B companies but can apply to B2C as well.
