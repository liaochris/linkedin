Aleksandra Stalmach’s Summary

I’m Aleksandra – a different brand of communications professional. Sure I have all the basics that a good communications professional has (strong writing and organizational skills, a panache for research, and love of multi-tasking) but I am very different in a number of respects.

1. I love thinking about other ways to engage audiences. My theory is you either stand out from the crowd or get lost in it.
2. I’m service oriented. Years of retail experience flows through my veins. I want to help my clients reach their goals.
3. I love to laugh. I have a sense of humour that keeps me from stressing out when under pressure. Calm, cool and collected…with a smile.
4. I enjoy a challenge. I am always looking at ways to learn and grow. A keener, that’s me.
5. I ask for feedback. I’m not scared of criticism; in fact, if you have feedback I’d like to hear what you have to say!
