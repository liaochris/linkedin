John Rolf Jordan’s Summary

I have a passion for helping couples who are getting ready for one of the most important decisions of their lives, marriage.

Who are good prospects for my service? Pre engaged and premarital couples in the Indianapolis area and beyond, who are wanting to make a relational foundation that can last a lifetime. While I counsel other individuals and couples, premarital counseling is my specialty.

What I may be able to do for you:
1. Feel free to contact me with any self-help, spiritual, and/or relational questions. If you need personal, face to face counseling here in Indianapolis please contact me as well.
2. If you do not need the benefit of premarital counseling at this time, we all know people who may be in need. Please consider referring them to me.
3. I am very open to networking, helping in any way I can, on this service and would be willing to introduce you to the people who are on my contact list.

My life philosophy:
I am a follower of Jesus Christ and my faith is very important to me. I am an ordained Southern Baptist minister who is a member of a nondenominational church here in Indianapolis and love to be as involved with the church as I can be. You may not agree with my life philosophy and that is fine. I just would ask that you would be open minded and non judgmental, just as you would expect me to be.

I am married to my wife Stephanie and we have one daughter named Jenna.

I would be more than happy to add you to my contact list, so feel free to make a request!

John Rolf Jordan’s Specialties:
