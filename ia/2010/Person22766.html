Beth Harte’s Summary

I have over 14 years of experience and a proven track record of implementing successful strategies and campaigns for startups, SMBs, mid-market, and Fortune 500 companies.

I specialize in integrated marketing and communications, which has allowed me to wear many different hats including: strategic planning, branding, public relations, analyst relations, product marketing, international marketing, business development, lead generation, website management, and SEO/SEM.

I am also an adjunct professor at Immaculata University where I've taught:

● Marketing in a Global Economy
● Marketing Principles and Practices
● Introduction to Public Relations
● Writing for Public Relations & Social Media
● Issues in E-Commerce

Beth Harte’s Specialties:
