Tim Murphy’s Summary

Murphy Strategies LLC is a consulting practice dedicated to sales effectiveness, corporate expense management, and outsourced sales.
Prior to founding Murphy Strategies, Tim had a thirty year career with American Express with increasing responsibility and scope. He was integral with several key product launches including Amex’ Corporate Card, Travel Management Services, Corporate Purchasing Card, and Global Client Group. Tim’s (individual contributor) sales career at American Express was highlighted by eight President’s Club awards for outstanding sales productivity – a record that stands today. For his career accomplishments, in 2000, Tim was inducted into American Express’ Hall of Fame with their then and current Vice Chairmen.
In addition to leading new and evolving product launches, Tim adapted American Express products to several markets. He began his career in Minneapolis, then he moved on to Chicago, then to Phoenix to work the Western Region and finally, internationally, when he was promoted to Vice President of the Global Client Group.
