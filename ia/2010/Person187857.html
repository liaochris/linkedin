Sharon Pommer’s Summary

Sharon Pommer joined Career Management with diverse experience in Retail, E-Commerce and Catalog. In addition to working on high level search assignments in the full spectrum of the industry, including the fashion arena and all operational aspects, her responsibilities include business development and marketing initiatives. Sharon’s experience as a multi-channel merchandiser and marketer gives her substantial insight into the talent acquisition needs of her clients and the ability to identify, assess and attract potential candidates.
