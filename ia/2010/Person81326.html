Helen Lacey’s Summary

Red Berry Recruitment Ltd was established in January 2007 by Helen Lacey owner and Director. Helen has had over 12 years experience in the recruitment market having worked on both the industrial and commercial temporary desks as well as the technical permanent desk. She has also been a Branch Manger, a Customer Service Manager and finally a Business Development Manager, prior to setting up on her own, a Corporate Account Manager. Helen decided that she wanted to offer companies and applicants a better service that she felt most agencies were offering. Her customer service background prior to starting in recruitment has helped her gain loyalty and respect with both clients and candidates in the areas she has worked. Red Berry Recruitment Ltd is proud to be a member of the Recruitment Employment Confederation (REC).

Red Berry Recruitment Ltd is a 24/7 friendly yet professional agency which offers a warm welcome, a happy smile and a professional service. Red Berry Recruitment Ltd is about making sure that the client and the candidate get 100% satisfaction and if, in the unfortunate event of that not happening, then Red Berry Recruitment Ltd will do everything they can to rectify the problem.
Red Berry Recruitment Ltd will supply staff in both the temporary and permanent market place.
