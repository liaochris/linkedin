Gay Robbins’s Summary

I have been helping companies increase revenues, profits and customer loyalty as a Marketing Strategist, Senior Program Manager, Product Manager, and leader of Customer Support, Sales Operations and Telesales teams – for 2 decades.

As a consultant or employee, I stretch to accomplish better than expected results and am passionate about improving customer experience as a key differentiator of success. These qualities and the following skills have brought financial reward and increased customer loyalty to Microsoft, Applied Microsystems and organizations in the healthcare, training and education industries.

Define strategy that works right the first time, informed by research and analysis that expresses the “voice of the customer” and key stakeholder feedback, aligned with corporate goals.

Entrepreurial with an eye for opportunities that improve performance and capacity to turn raw opportunities into profitable results, or as one client said, “turn lead into gold”.

Inspire teams to deliver well beyond expectations. Give teams the tools, resources, coaching and training that empower them to achieve their wildest ambitions, in a culture that encourages mutual respect, collaboration, synergy, and innovation.

Drive implementations using PM best practices and tools; bring focus to the right priorities at the right time to exceed schedule goals, and continuously improve processes to improve profits.
