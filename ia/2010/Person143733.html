Mike Walsh’s Summary

Mike Walsh is an internationally renowned public speaker, writer and authority on digital media. He is the Managing Director of Tomorrow, a digital think tank that guides some of the world's leading brands and corporations.

Through Tomorrow, Mike gives speeches on the new media revolution around the world and also publishes 'The Fourth Estate' - a monthly publication read by thousands of professionals in the media and entertainment space.

Mike has been involved in the online world for over a decade, having founded and run the market leading consumer insights house Jupiter Research in Asia Pacific during the 1990s, as well as working in strategy for News Corporation in both the Australian and Asian markets.

In early 2008 - Mike will also be releasing his new book 'Futuretainment' through Phaidon Press. Futuretainment examines how the next generation of consumers will change the nature of media and entertainment forever. Futuretainment is being art directed by the award winning designer Vince Frost.

Mike Walsh travels regularly between Australia, Asia, Europe and the US. If you would be interested in booking him for a keynote presentation or an insights workshop please contact us for information on his current travel schedule and availability.
