Michael Kovacs’s Summary

I am technologist that is a practical, hands-on, hardworking team player. If you subscribe to Myers-Briggs I am an ENTP and so I'm at my best and happiest when I'm working with a great team that's motivated to succeed. I am happy to lead or follow on projects and am definitely not shy about being proactive to add value.

After spending most of my career in engineering where I achieved a few certifications, co-authored a JMS book (http://bit.ly/6f359), and was rewarded a patent (http://bit.ly/W40b6) for some of my work at BEA, I spent a year and a half as a product manager where my largest accomplishment was championing BEA's acquisition of Solarmetric. Working with biz dev I helped craft the business case around making the first ever technology purchase for integration into the company's core and most important product, WebLogic Server. As a result BEA was able to regain first to market leadership.

Since leaving BEA I've spent the last 3 years as an entrepreneur/small business owner. Being in this role has really turned me into a jack of all trades that can add value from an engineering or business aspect. In addition to building software and running my own business ventures I help clients, large and small, with technology and business strategy in addition to helping with implementation of these strategies.

Specific tasks that I perform: I help lead and manage engineering teams in addition to contributing as an engineer when required. I interview engineering candidates, perform code/architecture reviews, question business motivations behind product features and help prioritize, SEO, product design and usability, as well as help present to customers when required. I can contribute wherever there's a need but my strength and roots lie with technology, engineering, building quality and easy to use products, and effectively communicating technical issues with non-technical colleagues and customers.
