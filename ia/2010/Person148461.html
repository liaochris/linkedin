Monica D. Johns, MBA, PMP’s Summary

Monica helps clients who are ready to make the changes needed to revolutionize their businesses. Her experience includes 25 years at General Electric, Corning, and General Motors. Her background includes engineering, proposal management, sales, product line management, program and project management, outsourcing initiatives, and consulting.

Monica holds a Master’s degree in Business Administration (MBA) from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, and a Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Engineering from the University at Buffalo.

Monica also holds the Project Management Professional (PMP®) credential conferred by the Project Management Institute. She earned her Six Sigma Black Belt and Lean Six Sigma White Belt from the Institute of Industrial Engineers (IIE).

Monica is an alumna of the Wharton Global Consulting Practicum (GCP). As a GCP consultant, she worked on a global consulting team that developed a market entry strategy for Israel’s leading supplier of advanced communications products. Her global experience also includes managing a multinational, multi-company proposal team while working in Australia for General Electric.

Monica recently completed a Six Sigma project that involved making recommendations for the future expansion of a health insurance program. She was a presenter at ASQ's 18th Annual Service Quality Conference, as well as the Aviation Week Commercial Aviation Supply Chain Management Conference. Her company, Clarity Management Consulting, is a nationally-certified Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) and Minority Business Enterprise (MBE).

Monica D. Johns, MBA, PMP’s Specialties:

Strategy development, business development, proposal management, cost management, business process improvement, and procurement solutions.
