Camilla Kleindienst’s Summary

I co-own a music publishing company with my songwriting husband Daniel. We represent Daniel's song catalogue but also other songwriter catalogues as well. I have placed several songs in film to date and secured many independent artist recordings from our catalog.

We also are involved in producing music for marketing purposes and recording custom music for marketing purposes. This area of our company is very successful on anyone's scale.

I work with companies to find ways to incorporate music into events, corporate marketing strategies and make recommendations.
