Ryan Kerian’s Summary

Experienced employee benefits and litigation attorney currently counseling clients on employee benefits issues arising in complex domestic and cross-border mergers and acquisitions. Readily able to quickly master new challenges as demonstrated by breadth of experiences that include counseling clients on complex ERISA compliance issues and successfully briefing and arguing a political asylum case in the Seventh Circuit Court of Appeals.

Ryan Kerian’s Specialties:

Employee benefit plans and employee compensation. Political asylum process including affirmative applications and court proceedings. Please note: The Supreme Court of Illinois does not recognize certifications of specialties in the practice of law. I am not certified as a specialist in any of these areas by any certifying body.
