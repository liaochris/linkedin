Roger Warren - Sr. Project Portfolio Manager’s Summary

Certified Project Executive with 23 years experience managing a variety of projects including large business and IT projects / programs in various industries. Extensive experience in Enterprise, Portfolio, Program and Project Management, building and managing Program Management Offices (PMO’s) and implementing enterprise solutions.

Developed and managed several PMO's. Assessed and improved project management environment maturity level. Ability to work in project details up to synthesizing data and developing executive level reports. Successfully inegrated PMO's into internal initiative functional groups and exteral professional services companies.

Perform project management using standard methodologies from IBM, DEC and PMI. Manage projects metrics include: customer satisfaction, engagement profit and loss (costs, budgets, revenues) schedules, pricing contractual deliverables, etc. Responsibility for developing and translating contracts / statements of work into implementing plans / schedules and delivering solutions. Experienced in determining the maturity of project management organizations, implementing project management standards, controls, monitoring methodologies and managing / mentoring other project managers.
