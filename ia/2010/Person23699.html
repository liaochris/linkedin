Bill Crigger’s Summary

Former Human Resource VP with 24 years of corporate experience. The past twelve years experience has been focused in the Career Transition - Outplacement arena. Our clients include individuals as well as Fortune 500 companies located throughout the U.S. In twelve years, we have provided individual and group services in 35 states.

We started our company based on the need for a personalized and customized approach to career transition versus a 'cookie cutter approach" Our mission is: To do whatever it takes to assist individuals in taking the necessary next steps towards successful career transition and to serve as a transition partner and resource to client companies in times of consolidation, downsizing and changes resulting from reorganization, mergers or acquisitions.

The winds of change bring opportunity … It is our ultimate purpose to do everything in our power to identify, facilitate, initiate, and generate opportunities to help individuals and client companies. We are the catalysts for transforming change into opportunities.

WE DO PROVIDE THESE SAME SERVICES TO INDIVIDUALS.

For more information, see our web page at www.compasscareer.com
