Wendy Flanagan wendykf06@yahoo.com’s Summary

Brand4Market (http://www.brand4market.com) President Wendy Flanagan offers contract-based marketing consulting services based on two decades experience operating a full-service, integrated marketing communications agency. Flanagan fills the role of interim Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) for business owners who are aware that brand plays an important part of corporate valuation.

Such companies often:

> Are looking to rapidly grow the company with Venture Capital or Angel Investors
> Have been purchased with the intent to sell
> Have been an entrepreneur running the company for years and would like to exit
> Are looking to be acquired, or
> Are planning an Initial Public Offering (IPO)…

Visual imagery and verbal messages, positioning, and industry and community activities are all aspects of your brand experience and value. Brand4Market can help your brand emerge and take wing.

Flanagan presents to audiences up to 150 people on a variety of topics, including Social Media Marketing, Integrated Marketing Communications and Brand Building Through Technology.

Previously, Wendy Flanagan served as Co-President of Murray, a leading design and technology studio providing agency services such as strategic marketing, creative design, and Web-based technology for mid-sized to large Fortune-level clients including ADT Security, Novartis, Wyndham Hotels, Verizon Wireless, and SimplexGrinnell.

Flanagan served on the Board for the NJ/IABC; Chapter President in 2005. She coordinated the NJ MarCom Council (http://www.njmarcom.org) for joint networking events for the non-profit professional groups in NJ with 400+ attendees. Flanagan served on the board of the NJAMA; acted as chair of the B2B Special Interest Group forum for the American Marketing Association. She is a member of MENG and serves on the board of NJAdClub.

Flanagan has been honored by NJ Biz as one of NJ's 50 Top Women in Business; and inducted to the Advertising Hall of Fame by NJAdClub.
