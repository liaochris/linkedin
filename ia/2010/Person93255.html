Jeff Hathaway’s Summary

I have been in the restaurant business since I was a teenager and my roles have ranged from scraping rice and beans off plates as a busser to running multiple units as a Director of Operations. I have undoubtedly learned and grown from every position I have had in the business. The restaurant business is full of passionate, intelligent, energetic and professional leaders at all levels and that is what keeps it fun and challenging. I have moved my focus from hands on operations to the recruiting piece of the business which is both challenging and rewarding in multiple ways.

Jeff Hathaway’s Specialties:

We have experience and exposure to all areas of the restaurant recruiting business from unit level managers through senior level executives and in all disciplines of the restaurant industry. We can just as easily do searches in all concept types from QSR to Fast Casual, Casual Dining or Upscale.
