Erica Stelcik’s Summary

My goal is to spread awareness locally on eating real food and teaching others how to eat healthy and have healthy habits to be healthy. Real food is food that is minimally processed, cooked or uncooked, and created and eaten with love for oneself, one's family, and Mother Earth.

My current focus now at Lite for Life is helping overweight as well as prediabetic/diabetic/gestationally diabetic clients to lose weight by stabilizing their blood sugar from eating real foods.

I am currently completing my Master of Science in Holistic Nutrition this year.
