Aaron Cheatham’s Summary

With more than fifteen years of experience in venture capital, investment banking and technical sales roles, I enjoy applying my deep technical background to assess disruptive technologies and analyze industry trends in order to build exciting, new businesses. My operating experience includes providing business development, strategic marketing and financial leadership for more than three dozen startup companies at the executive and board levels.

I have developed an extensive cleantech industry network that includes relationships at leading advanced metering infrastructure, demand response, home area network, wireless technology and smart grid software applications companies.

Aaron Cheatham’s Specialties:

Highly experienced business development professional with deep expertise in analyzing industry and competitive landscapes, leading due diligence processes, modeling financial alternatives, structuring equity and debt investments, negotiating strategic alliances and acquisitions and ultimately driving them to a close. Strategic-minded marketing professional experienced at identifying market requirements and customer "pain," developing go-to-market plans and modeling financial impacts.
