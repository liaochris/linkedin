Massimo Carraro’s Summary

Massimo is a copywriter and communication manager in love with his job.

His experience includes traditional mass-media advertising together with significant social media projects, both with large multinational agencies and his own creative shop.

Besides being a blog consultant and teacher, he just finished his first marketing book, to be published in january 2010 (Alpha Test Ed.).

Massimo regularly blogs on the subject of "marketing seen from a copywriter's perspective" on his blog "ohmymarketing!".

His liveblogging of Philip Kotler's conferences in Italy has brought him in the Italian Wikipedia page dedicated to professor Kotler.

Cowo®, his word-of-mouth experimental program of coworking network, has raised 21 affiliate spaces in 10 Italian cities in only 7 months.
