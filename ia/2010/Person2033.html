John Massey’s Summary

AcQuity Investments specializes in buying and selling note and loan portfolios. Our Loan Sale Advisory team works with banks and private investors world wide to buy, sell and trade residential and commercial note portfolios.

AcQuity works directly with banks and institutional investors to strengthen their portfolios, disposition non-performing loans, developing turn around strategies, rehabilitating loans and assisting banks and institutional investors to locate performing or undervalued assets.

Whether your institution is looking to acquire or disposition performing, sub-performing or non-performing notes/loans AcQuity Investments can perform.

If your institution is selling performing residential loan portfolios please visit our website today: www.acquityinvestments.com
