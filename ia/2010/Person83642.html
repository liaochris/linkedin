What: Daily Word – an inspirational email blast sent Monday – Friday at 6:30am to over 2000 recipients.

When/Why: Initially created in 2004 in connection with ashcashfinance.com as a tool to inspire and motivate readers to become financially empowered. With increased popularity the Daily Word blossomed into a service that its audience can’t live without spreading encouraging words to start their morning.

How: The Daily Word is distributed via email, text and posted on a daily blog (www.TheAshCashShow.com).

Honorable Mentions: Some of the daily word readers include Power 105.1 DJ Kut, Author, Politician and Activist Kevin Powell, Rap Artist Keith Murray, Music Executive Kevin Liles and a host of others.
