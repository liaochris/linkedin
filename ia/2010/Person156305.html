Myron Campbell’s Summary

Currently I'm residing in the East-Side of Vancouver and I love it. I still spend a great majority of my spare time animating memory and putting forgotten fabrics into toys for my 2yr old daughter. She is my favorite project.

I am currently working with the National Film Board of Canada to create a stereoscopic short film as well with Vancouver Film School in which I am a full time teacher. On top of that I am also enrolled for a Master's Degree in Media Arts at Emily Carr University of Art & Design and continue to produce and exhibit work internationally.

I don't sleep a lot. And I love gingham....still.

Myron Campbell’s Specialties:

Art Direction, Delegation of work to assistants, drawing, painting, photography, writing, graphic design, web design, broadcast animation, flash animation, sound design.

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    2008 — December 2009 (1 year )

    I would often make meetings with the clients and was the one who presented the idea. It was important to myself and the rest of the team that we realize the client goals and objectives while also making the project help the studio progress in some way.
