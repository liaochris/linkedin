    AAM (Allied Advanced Materials) develops and manufactures non-precious metal ingots, plates and sputtering targets. We have extensive experience in metal refining, vacuum induction melting (VIM), powder metallurgy (PM) and metal forming technologies. We supply high quality products to customers worldwide at low cost and with industry leading delivery times. AAM products have already been shipped to the medical, alternative energy, microwave, semiconductor and magnetic data storage industries.

    Our mission: Delivering low cost products with high quality standards to meet our customer’s requirements and ensure complete customer satisfaction.

    Our Vision: Through superior engineering, precision manufacturing, and dedicated teamwork we aim to be a leading-edge enterprise with first class employees, best-of-breed quality, responsive service and low cost. We are committed to provide world-class excellence in products and service to meet technology-based, industrial requirements for high quality non-precious materials.
