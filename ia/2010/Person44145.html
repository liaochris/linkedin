Craig Huffstetler’s Summary

I like to look at things creatively, analytically and constructively – and make them better. Whether I’m looking at a web site or at ways to conduct an eMarketing campaign, I’m always looking to improve it using the latest analytics (or information available in the digital age from leading analysts).

My early interest was in web design, but I soon learned that I really needed to learn how to direct traffic to web site(s). I also developed a broader interest in computer technology in general, and have accumulated experience in programming and server administration. I first became exposed to marketing and web content management at eTek Network. This was only the start of the journey...

I was finally able to look at things at the "enterprise" level during an internship at Verizon, where I was able to use all of my wide range of technology skills and look at things from a marketing perspective.

Now I'm fulfilling that dream at my current company.
