Marc Beaumont’s Summary

I have spent the last 15+ years developing marketing communication strategies for both B2B and B2C clients, including 10 years spent developing, implementing, and analyzing Internet-based marketing programs. The last five years have been spent in the financial services industry, designing, branding, and marketing Internet-based software solutions to and for Fortune 500 financial institutions, as well as serving as in-house marketing counsel for a leading national online-only bank. I have a broad range of marketing knowledge, but I am most proficient in database marketing with an emphasis on predictive modeling to promote sales growth, increase cross-selling, lower acquisition costs, and boost profitability. Feel free to drop me a line.
