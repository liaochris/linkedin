Alan Spoon’s Summary

Invest in and serve as board member or lead investor for fast-growing companies seeking advice and extensive support, plus capital for expansion and/or partial liquidity. Focus largely on digital media, e-commerce, marketing services, financial services, and web-enabled learning. Polaris is a sizeable, seasoned, diversified venture capital partnership focusing on early-stage technology, life science, and growth-equity investments.

Lead investments in, or serve on boards of: LegalZoom, Art.com, Internet Brands (INET), Focus Financial Partners, HealthCentral Network, Ice.com, Phreesia, AWS (Weatherbug), and LRN.

Previous investments/board service include TechTarget, Inc. (TTGT, NASDAQ), Matrics, Inc. (RFID) which was sold to Symbol Technologies, and Cushcraft Corporation (wireless antennae) which was sold to Laird Technologies.

Previously, 18+ years at The Washington Post Company, moving through a variety of positions, culminating in the Presidency during the 1990s. Spent five years enjoying fast-learning and broad exposure at the Boston Consulting Group.

Current not-for-profit activities include Smithsonian Institution, Vice Chairman Board of Regents; Massachusetts Institute of Technology Member of Corporation (board); Council on Foreign Relations; Combined Jewish Philanthropies, Boston.

Current independent boards include Danaher Corporation, and IAC (InterActiveCorp).
