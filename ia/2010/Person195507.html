Steven Gelber’s Summary

Steven focuses on building long-term relationships rather than transacting short-term business. By having a personal, honest, and open relationship with clients it is easier to identify resources, assess current and future financial goals, devise a strategy and implement a financial plan that is versatile and can be adapted to meet a client's ever changing needs.

Working on the independent side of the business there are no sales quotas, or featured products, Steven is able to focus on consumer education. He has a focus on teaching current and prospective clients about the myriad of financial products and accounts available to help them make the best decisions for their financial future.
