Jay Medina’s Summary

Jay Medina is the founder and principal consultant at HelpfulAdvisor, a consulting practice that is obsessed with providing extreme value for his clients, while reducing the costs normally associated with IT services and management.

Based on the premise of "Technology Insourcing", HelpfulAdvisor is able to provide clients with innovative services that take the burden off small businesses to provide their own in-house technology support and management. This results in predictable costs for proactive services and expert consulting for new technologies.

The biggest benefit to HelpfulAdvisor's clients is that all this can be achieved for far less than a single in-house staff member let alone what a fully staffed IT department would cost an organization.

To keep service levels high, and the quality of service exceptional, HelpfulAdvisor only works with a few clients at any one time. This ensures that the highest levels of service and attention are given to only a small number of clientele without compromise.

Jay is a team player with very strong written and verbal communications skills. Jay also specializes in planning and managing Windows server platform implementations, upgrades, and migrations, and predictable subscription based support services.

His qualifications include a proven track record of results-based deliveries, military leadership experience, and current Microsoft MCP and MCTS certifications. Jay is currently seeking to expand his client portfolio but only has a few open slots left.
