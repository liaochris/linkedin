Pedro Valido’s Summary

I have worked and lived in 9 countries (Switzerland, France, Germany, Portugal, Nigeria, US, Venezuela, Serbia, Ukraine) and do business with more than 50 countries around the world.

During the course of my professional life, I have held management positions since the age of 21, started my first company as early as 25 and reached my first Fortune 500 Country manager position at the age of 30.

I am fluent in portuguese, spanish, english and french. Some good notions of italian, german and serbian.

I consider myself to be an entrepeneur and love working in an international environment. I have worked for and with a good deal of Fortune 500 companies, either as a manager or consultant in IT, FMCG, Telecoms, Media and Advertising, Oil and Gas, Banking & insurance, Pharma,Hospitality and Automobile.

Somehow, I believe a good manager can create and/or manage any company in any industry in any country. So far, I have worked with and met people of more than 80 different nationalities.

But MOST of all, I had and have fun everyday in doing what I do. This is probably the greatest blessing I had and have professionally:-)

I am actively involved in investment management in energy, real estate and pharma and welcome any investment opportunities in those industries.

I am Partner of Leverage Investment Advisory, which is specialized in international expansion of companies who want to enter the Balkans, Portugal and Spain.

I recently accepted the new challenge of being nominated as Business Development Manager for Drilnet, a company specialized in upstream in the Petroleum Industry. We work with majors, national oil companies and service companies and provide technical assistance, training, project management. and consulting.

I welcome potential partnerships, associations and synergies that you may find of interest in the above mentioned business areas.

Initial contact by email, please, to pedro.valido@drilnet.com
