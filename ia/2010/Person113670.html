Kellie Frazier’s Summary

In 2007 I lost my little sister to Cancer, shortly after her youngest son drowned in a boating accident. With traveling back and forth for family, I left my thriving company unattended and returned to close its doors. In December of that same year I acquired a brain injury from a freak accident. These events left me with only one thought after my recovery; "Follow every inspired thought toward a productive meaningful life that brings joy."

CONNECTING:

I created Connecting LLC in 2008 and it is designed with an intent to inspire leaders toward connecting logic and compassion toward a greater purpose in business and in their personal lives.

I now coach Leaders, Businessman, Parents and Teachers in:

* Having more Faith & Fun
* Connecting head and heart
* Leading with Logic and Compassion
* Self Control
* Abundance
* Attitude of Gratitude
* Motivate Yourself
* Removing Success Stealer's

Email me at kellie@connectingyoutobusiness.com for a free, 30-minute coaching call where we will discuss what your blocks are and how I can help you.

EXPERT TELE-SEMINARIAN:
