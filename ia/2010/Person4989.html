Alana Crow’s Summary

I spent more than 7 years down in Hollywood working in acting and voice-overs in the TV and Film industry. Working on such shows as Knight Rider, Days of Our Lives, General Hospital, and Emergency. I left after some personal tragedies hit my family hard and I went off to soul search as Hollywood suddenly became way to superficial for me to handle.

I ended up landing a job as Activities Director on board Royal Viking Line ships for 5 1/2 years, as Activities Director for passengers under the age of 18. The position enabled me to travel the world. In my travels I spent more than a year in Norway and wanted to stay.

I came back to San Francisco to build new skills in web design, in hopes to get a permanent work visa on that side of the pond. Then came the demise of the dot com and 911. Everything changed. I decided to remain closer to my family and decided to just move up the coast a bit to the PNW, where until recently I was operating my own freelance web design business.

Then in 2008 fate brought me full circle and I have landed back in the acting business, in a far lovelier local than LA.

I am represented by talent agent Mary Dangerfield of Dangerfield Talent and Literary. I am also training at Portland Acting with Robert Blanche and Vincent Caldoni. I'm having a blast re-honing my skills and meeting so many terrific people!

I am so glad to be back and no longer the young teen ingenue that lasted far too long! Being older opens up the door to far more interesting and complex characters. Now I just need to book a few more gigs and I will be super happy!
