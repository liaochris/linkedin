Ron Chandler’s Summary

"One's destination is never a place, but a new way of seeing things."
- Henry Miller

I believe in the infinite capacity of the human spirit and in the natural desire for people to apply their skills and talents in the pursuit of happiness for themselves and those around them. My goal is to provide for my fellow citizens by helping them learn how to provide for themselves; for it is the only way that we can build a community based on interdependencies that drive the creation of new wealth. I have owned a gym where people learned to develop healthy and sustainable lifestyles, run a consulting business to help large and small businesses develop systems and processes to drive profits, save jobs and fund new growth initiatives, and; have taught thousands of students about the concepts of self-directed investing. Most recently I collaborated with 23 other Lean and 6-Sigma professionals to write “Driving Operational Excellence”, possibly the most comprehensive manual for business performance improvement ever written. I retired from full time business consulting in order to spend more time working with people at an individual level, to take the lessons that I have learned both pleasantly and unpleasantly over the last half century and use that knowledge to guide and nurture my fellow citizens in their endeavors. Whether it is a young couple trying to save for their children’s college, empty nesters preparing for retirement, someone with a new business idea or who just wants to lead a more healthy and serene life; my assessment will be objective, my advice will always be in the clients best interest and the results will be tangible and sustainable.

Graduate of the University of Michigan with a Bachelors of Science Degree in Chemical Engineering, MBA in Marketing and Finance from the University of Detroit. Certified in Lean Manufacturing and as a Six Sigma Black Belt.
