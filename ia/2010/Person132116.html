Margaret Hampton’s Summary

Local Business Marketing Specialist, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Social Media, Internet Marketing...so Your Business' multiple search engine rankings make you the one chosen when targeted prospects look for what you offer!

Former Caregiver and Health Advocate dedicated to relieving caregiver stress, preventing caregiver burnout, and promoting caregiver health, wellness, and Transformational Health alternatives.

Compassionate Minister to abused, battered, traumatized and otherwise hurting women, co-dependents and people pleasers, to help prevent these situations, assist people trapped in them, and restore women afflicted by damaging after-effects.

Resourceful, results-oriented Executive and Consultant with proven leadership, strategic vision, market opportunity identification, new media, corporate planning, finance, and communication skills helping corporations, local businesses, and health charities.

Creative thinker and niche strategist -e.g., opportunity identified 1986 and plan development in the highly decentralized, rapidly growing "pre-recorded videocassette" industry was applied in the creation of Blockbuster Video.

Noted for identifying problems, opportunities and solutions, Margaret's asset/liability management computer model was used by the Regional Comptroller's Office as the model for banks (1978-82). Youngest female Senior Vice President in Southeastern Regional Banking in 1981, her corporate planning process was emulated (with photos) by Ernst & Whinney/Young's National Bank Consulting practice. Her fall 1985 turnaround plan was dubbed by Merrill Lynch Hubbard's CFO (Merrill Lynch's real property syndication arm) as "the best money we have ever spent."

Margaret was offered the position of Senior Advisor to the U.S. Comptroller of the Currency in 1984, informed that she came in third for Nancy Teeter's replacement on the Federal Reserve Board.
