David Carralon’s Summary

Experienced web project Manager in the areas of International Search Marketing (planning, coordination and roll out of online marketing marketing strategies), Information architecture, writing for the web, accessibility/usability, user centric Web design and standards-compliant web development.

CMS knowledge and experience includes Joomla, Movable Type, Obtree, Wordpress and Magento for e-commerce.

At present I manage the development and promotion of two British Council websites: France and Switzerland, though I provide SEO consultancy and campaign implementation advice to the entire British Council network.

I have been responsible for the writing and implementation of several Search marketing strategies ( SEM and SEO) and have a proven success record of increasing search engine visibility, qualified traffic and the actual business outputs (conversions) at both country and regional level. Tasks undertaken throughout the campaigns: search engine optimisation, paid advertising (PPC), email marketing, online partnership and paid advertising. I have experience of working with a range of suppliers and outsourcing work from developers and editorial teams.

Freelance work: outside my in-house role I run my own Search Marketing consultancy where I have had the opportunity to work with small and medium-size client projects in the UK, Spain and France. My services include both consultancy and advice and/or actual campaign implementation and coordination of bespoke ROI-driven search engine marketing strategies with the business KPIs.

I can also facilitate technical SEO audits and reports: site architecture analysis, content analysis for SEO suitability, accessibility compliance and usability check.

David Carralon’s Specialties:

My genuine passion for the internet and the future growth of search marketing typically take me to find the strengths of the project or clients' online business model and capitalise on that to deliver a successful output for the project.

Web monitoring and customer needs assessment through online data-collection methods. Web tracking, monitoring and analysis of website activity and business-generated traffic.
