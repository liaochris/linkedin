Andrea Santiago’s Summary

Andrea is an experienced recruiter with a proven track record of success, as well as a career expert and job search writer and columnist for About.com, A New York Times Company.

Since 2001, Andrea has placed, and contributed to the placement of hundreds of professionals in healthcare and other industries including commercial real estate, hospitality, sales, marketing, engineering, management, and executives.

Andrea’s health career advice and information website on About.com ranks in the Top 100 Career Blogs Worldwide (by RiseSmart, Inc) and also the Top 100 Healthcare Blogs by Edrugsearch.com. She is a featured expert on JobRadio.fm with a weekly Healthcare JobCast, and has also appeared on Star94 FM, a Top 40 Atlanta radio station, as well as Atlanta’s WXIA-TV 11Alive evening news, and has been quoted in numerous articles, books, and online publications.

Currently, Andrea manages a department of junior physician recruiters as the Resource Director for The Medicus Firm, a national retained physician search firm. The Medicus Firm was formed by the June 2009 merger of Medicus Partners of Dallas TX, and The MD Firm, which was founded in 2006 in Atlanta. Andrea joined The MD Firm in February of 2007. Based on her reputation in the industry as a consistent producer, senior-level physician recruiter, and team leader, Andrea was called upon by a former manager and colleague, one of the founders of The MD Firm, to help build the company by establishing and managing the Training and Resource Development departments. Andrea was named Associate of the Year, 2008, for her contributions to the growth and success of The MD Firm.

For physician search needs, or information about medical practice opportunities, Andrea may be contacted at The Medicus Firm: asantiago[at]TheMedicusFirm[dot]com. For information about health careers, or general job search advice, or for media inquiries, email her at healthcareers[dot]guide[at]About[dot]com.

Andrea Santiago’s Specialties:

Physician recruiting, training, management, team building. Medical sales and recruiting. Experienced with a variety of applicant tracking systems and software programs including Resumate, PCRecruiter, Salesforce.com, and several proprietary programs. Writing, blogging, social media.
