Cheryl Oppenheim’s Summary

Brand & e-Business Strategist and Innovator with proven record of developing and driving the direction of short and long-term business strategies and integrated marketing initiatives across multiple verticals. 20 years experience developing brands and branded experiences that increase revenue, market share, and customer loyalty.

Skilled at establishing long standing, trusted and valued relationships with clients and customers, and fostering business environments that encourage innovation and creativity. Successful in developing pitches, conveying initiatives, and managing strategic, creative, and development teams. Expert presenter, and facilitator of work sessions for branding, persona, online business definition, and interactive marketing initiatives.

Award winning Creative Director with proven ability to win (and win over) clients, and motivate and inspire creative teams.
