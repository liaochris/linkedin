Rob Pledger’s Summary

With over twenty years experience in growing true software startups and more specifically the last 8 years serving as CTO and senior executive positions in entities ranging from global public software companies to small cutting edge software startups, Rob brings exceptional technology experience combined with battle-tested wisdom. He has shipped over 30 commercial software products across 7 startups and has been involved in two IPOs, four acquisitions, due diligence, and raising 6 rounds of VC funding. As a prolific technologist, he has designed and implemented close to a million lines of object-oriented code within thousands of classes and dozens of frameworks across a broad and varied technology landscape. He has authored technology patents, proposals for standards committees, developer roundtables, and spoken at numerous conferences. Rob also has a strong global background in business, having flown more than one million miles internationally gathering feedback from press, analysts, customers, enterprises, and partners. Rob's passions outside of technology include world-travel and spending time with his wife and newborn twins.
