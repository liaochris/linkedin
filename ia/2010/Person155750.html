Nick Scott’s Summary

My professional experience is in Aviation Management. I began as an intern at Centennial Airport in Englewood, CO. Centennial is the major general aviation reliever airport for DIA. I started in Airport Operations and conducted airfield inspection, snow removal operations, and construction coordination but my primary job was emergency response and coordination using the NIMS guidelines. After graduation I was hired on as permanent employee and continued to work in Airport Operations. After nearly 2 years I was promoted to Senior Airport Operations Specialist and I began working with the planning department. I worked with design consultants and contractors on projects including the installation of 2 new high speed taxiways, a major taxiway asphalt overlay, 2 runway overlays, and a ramp rehabilitation.

I then moved on to Denver International Airport as an Assistant Aviation Operations Manager. My job duty was to assist the Airport Operation Manger in day to day operation of the airport. This included emergency response, snow removal coordination, construction, and FAA Part 139 airfield inspections. Shortly after moving to DIA a position as Associate Airport Planner opened and I was hired. I currently still hold this position. My tasks include FAA 7460 Applications, Airside Planning, Landside Planning, Gate Layout, GIS application databases, ALP updates, and Master Plan updates.

As of April 2010 I have accepted the position of Senior Airport Planner at Jviation. This move will allow me to continue to grow as a planner because I will be working on a broad portfolio of airport projects.

My goals are to continue growing as an airport planner. Specifically in the ALP and Master Plan fields. We are also beginning the groundwork and migration of our ALP to an electronic ALP in a GIS format. This is a relatively new concept that the FAA has recently mandated in AC 150/5300-18.

Nick Scott’s Specialties:

Airport Planning, Master Plans, Airport Layout Plans, Integrated Noise Model, Part 77 Surfaces and FAA 7460 Applications, Land use Planning, Intergovernmental Agreements, GIS Applications, and AutoCAD Software.

Airport Operations, Emergency Response using NIMS, Snow removal coordination, Airfield Construction, Part 139 Regulations and Inspections.
