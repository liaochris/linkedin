Paulette Halpern’s Summary

Sandler Training works with business owners & executives who can’t keep waiting for the ‘economy to turn around’ and the phone to start ringing again.

Prospects always wanted better deals, but NOW salespeople struggle overcoming not just the ‘Price is too high’ objection, but every objection a prospect gives them.

Serious business owners turn to Sandler Training to learn ‘recession proof selling skills’. Many business owners talk with us after they have poured money into advertising campaigns that did not produce the expected results, and sometimes did not even cover the cost of the campaign. Does that describe you? What can you do – call and ask about our free 90 minute consultation. Call and ask if we can be scheduled as a speaker at one of your sales meetings. We will lead a 20 minute discussion on “How to differentiate yourself from your competition and close more sales, more easily, without discounting your services?”. Isn’t that what you want your team to be doing?

Learn:

Why salespeople are failing today to close sales….(it’s not the economy.)

Why sales people often deliver their proposal to someone who then says “I have to get approval on this from someone else” -- even though your salesperson thought they were talking with the decision maker.

How to improve the results your salespeople get when they go to networking events.

What is holding your sales team back from asking for and getting good referrals from existing clients that will turn into new business.

Why salespeople often sabotage their sales efforts, and can’t figure out how to stop doing it.

How to take charge of growing sales, even in this economy?

How to effectively differentiate your company from your competition, so the prospect believes you are better for them, even if your price is a little higher?

Call to find out how to make 2010 a great year, not just another year of survival.
