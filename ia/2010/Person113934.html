Kelly Kroll’s Summary

Kelly Kroll has been providing strategic marketing, marketing communications, and internet consulting services for more than 17 years. The founding of Marketing Renovations, LLC follows his successful career in the technology and professional services industries where he developed and implemented strategic plans, new business plans, customer service programs, marketing and sales plans and programs, change management plans, and new e-business ideas both as a line executive and consultant.

Kelly has worked with the management of several Fortune 500 companies as well as with start-up ventures, professional services firms, and government agencies. He has a broad range of experience in launching new ventures and in helping small and medium-sized companies achieve their next stage of growth by improving organizational performance and becoming customer-focused.

Kelly's broad-based knowledge and expertise has been used to provide marketing, technology, and new product development services to companies such as AT&T Corporation, Ford Motor Company, Dun & Bradstreet Corporation, Cambridge Technology Partners, Gartner Group/Dataquest, Fry Multimedia, Reciprocal, Plante & Moran, and Elder Law Solutions.

Kelly's online marketing career has been punctuated by a series of "World's Firsts"--first eCommerce Market Research website, first Outsourced eCommerce Transaction Service, first Outsourced Digital Rights Management Transaction System, first Secure Digital Textbook, and the World's First Secure Digital Novels.

A graduate of Yale University, Kelly earned his M.B.A. in Marketing and Corporate Strategy from the University of Michigan School of Business.
