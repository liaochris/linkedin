Pere Rosales’s Summary

Traditional interruptive advertising, branding and marketing isn't working effective anymore. Now the consumers have the power, you can't no longer interrupt them, but they can interrupt you...

People are not longer targets, people are communities, and they expect to be treated with care and respect. Today the only way to achieve customer loyalty and return business is to adapt your marketing model to engage people. Believe that the world needs, not more communication, but better communication.

I think that to have the ability to understand consumers in the digital space and to use that understanding to help client’s identify opportunities and meet today's business needs on-line, is absolutely crucial.

I have experience in the following areas:

• Strategy – designing solutions that meet clients’ digital business objectives.

• Customer experience – in depth qualitative and quantitative understanding of consumers behaviour on-line.

• Research services – in depth market knowledge of digital developments and their implications for existing business models.

• Market intelligence – competitive landscape reviews and on-going sector monitoring.

• Knowledge share – sector briefings for senior executive and non-digital teams.

• Solution management – providing programme and project management resource to help deliver digital change.

My experience with business processes in the digital area has given me a good understanding of how digital technology supports business.

I am a member of senior management in a consultancy practice including charging/billing processes, marketing and business development. I also have strong people management skills and experience.
