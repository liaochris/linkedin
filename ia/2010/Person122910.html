Lindsay Baish’s Summary

I am a social media manager and consultant from Chicago, IL who is currently residing in Houston. I have helped organizations ranging from small non-profit to Fortune 200 company widely expand their social media presence. My concept of social media goes beyond Twitter and Facebook; it's about building a network of brands, people and businesses that want to be a part of your company's vision. I take your business to where the audience lives online and turn that engagement into profit.

My goals are:
• Obtain a social media position at a financially stable company (preferably in the Chicago or Houston areas)
• Establish a long-term professional commitment to a company who wants to delve deep into social media
