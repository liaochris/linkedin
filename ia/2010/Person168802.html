Randi Destefano, ASID, CAPS’s Summary

Have you ever gone into a friend’s home, taken a look around and felt like every piece of their home was a perfect reflection of who they are and the life they live?
Do you wish your home looked like that?
You're busy. Your life changes. Kids grow up and eventually out of the house. Your downtown office has turned into working from a home office. Then one day, you wake up, and realize that your home hasn’t kept up with all of life’s changes. It’s no longer an authentic reflection of who we you are and how you live.
I’m Randi Destefano and that’s where I come in. I help busy women create authentic living spaces that suit their lives and reflect who they truly are. That’s why I started Authentic Living Interiors, based in Atlanta, Georgia.
Over my career, I’ve had the opportunity to work with hundreds of wonderful clients who have entrusted me with designing their kitchens, bathrooms, window treatments, furnishings, lighting and consulting on their remodeling projects or plans for a new home.
I’ve recently launched a new and unique program designed to help you create the home of your dreams without spending a fortune on new furniture or making a long-term investment with a designer. The Hands-On Home Makeover gives you access to a professional designer (me) at a price point you can afford.
If you need help creating an authentic space that reflects you and your lifestyle, would like to have a professional opinion while you plan your new home or remodeling project or you’re struggling with the amount of time it is taking to do a project on your own…let’s talk!

“When life inspires our home, our home will inspire our life.”
Melissa Michaels
