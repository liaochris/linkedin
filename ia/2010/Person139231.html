Travis McElfresh’s Summary

Product & technology entrepreneur with more than fifteen years of experience in online consumer experiences and platforms, including two multimillion user destinations: msnbc.com and Xbox.com. Strong executive leader who can build highly committed and winning teams. Committed to driving business accountability into organization philosophy, processes and metrics. Passion for building online consumer experiences in media, green, education, music and entertainment markets.

Travis McElfresh’s Specialties:

User Experience, Product Strategy, Product Development, Software Engineering, Network Engineering, IT Operations, Strategic Planning, General Management, High Volume Online Applications, Analytics, Enterprise Architecture
