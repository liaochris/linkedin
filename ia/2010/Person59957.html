Dr. Christina Kovalik’s Summary

Dr. Christina Kovalik NMD, LAc, is a Scottsdale Naturopathic Medical Doctor and Acupuncturist. She is a family care practitioner with a special interest in women’s health specifically infertility, PMS, endometriosis, menstrual irregularities, pregnancy & post partum support, hormone imbalance for men and women, menopause and gynecology.

Dr. Kovalik graduated from Southwest College of Naturopathic Medicine in Tempe, AZ, and the Phoenix Institute of Herbal Medicine and Acupuncture in Phoenix, AZ. She completed an externship assisting an OB/Gyn for almost 2 years and understands the importance of integrating medicine for various women’s issues. She also wrote her Master’s Thesis on Acupuncture and Female Infertility. She has published articles in the local NDNR naturopathic review journal, AZNET News and teaches classes on Expecting Parent Transition, Mind Body Spiritual Connection to Fertility, Meditation, Qigong, Weight loss and Nutrition. She is also a founding member of the Blended Healthcare Consortium.


Her Passion is inspiring women and their families to optimize their overall health and wellbeing naturally.

www.drchristinakovalik.com

Dr. Christina Kovalik’s Specialties:
