Paul Honnen’s Experience

  • President

    PollenTec by Advanced Textiles, LLC.

    (Privately Held; Building Materials industry)

    February 2008 — Present (2 years )

    PollenTec is a newly developed screen material for windows and doors that allows fresh air to flow into your rooms while filtering out up to 100% of air born pollens. The material will also signafently reduce the amount of dust and other particulate matter from entering. Distribution in North America was launched in 2008 with great success. PollenTec won the 2009 Chrystal Achievement Award form Window and door magazine for the most innovative window component. We are seeking distribution channels in China, Mexico and the UAE. Certified and hospital tested by the European Center for Allergy Research Foundation (ECARF).

    REINVENTING THE AIR FILTER
    Developers of the new PollenTec HVAC air filter, Is the finest washable, reusable air filter on the market. PollenTec air filters are made from PollenTec material that has been tested and certified in a hospital setting for its effectiveness in capturing up to 100% of air born pollen spores. The material is made from very specific tightly spun high grade 100% polyester. The filters are constructed utilizing four cross directional layers of the PollenTec material providing unsurpassed performance and durability. ASHARE tested MERV rating of 8+.
