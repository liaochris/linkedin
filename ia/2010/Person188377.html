Brian Sheehan’s Summary

SENIOR LEVEL FINANCE & OPERATIONS EXECUTIVE

Driver of Change – Turnaround or Rapid Growth Situations– Accomplished Problem Solver.

Brian is a hands-on, take charge Chief Financial Officer with 25 years experience leading organizations and teams through dynamic change. He has an extensive background in finance and operations gained from working for major retailers, consumer goods and franchising companies.

An accomplished problem solver, Brian specializes in helping companies with an urgent need to rapidly grow or turnaround their business.

His strengths include:

* strategic thinking and tactical execution
* lean management practices
* building high performance teams
* leveraging the capabilities of business systems to drive revenue and reduce costs.

Brian has a successful track record working in leadership roles for private and public companies ranging in size from $75 million to $4 billion in revenue with quantifiable achievements in finance, operations, distribution and info systems management Over the course of his career, he has contributed over $100 million in profit improvement ideas in the form of revenue programs, cost reductions and tax savings initiatives.

An innovative thinker with a collaborative management style, he enjoys solving complex business problems using innovative solutions.

If your business is going through dynamic change or needs to break through to the next level then contact Brian at once. He also has excellent resources to help you solve any business problem and is always willing share his advice or counsel with you.
