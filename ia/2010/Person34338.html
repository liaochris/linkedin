Christopher Baucom’s Summary

Dents Charlotte is a paintless dent repair(PDR) companying specializing in repair of both major and minor damage to the superficial body of automobiles. The technique used does not involve any degredation of, or repainting of a panel on a car. Rather, the metal is manuevered back into it's original position using a technique of pushing and tapping. Additionally Dents Charlotte offers paint scratch and chip repair, as well as some bumper repair. Dents Charlotte is a mobile service in and around Charlotte, NC, but has also expanded to include and in house service center in conjunction with Matthews Township Collision Center. Through this in house service center Dents Charlotte is looking to be your preferred provider of dent repair for all types of damage including hail damage.
