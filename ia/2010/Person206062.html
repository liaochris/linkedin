Tom Hawes’s Summary

My passions are analyzing strategic issues, creating business visions and inspiring people to perform better than they might have imagined. Specifically, I help high tech companies around the world with their business strategy and competitive intelligence challenges.

For example --

You know what it like when you have to explain and defend a strategy to the Board or senior management? I can help you think through this thoroughly to create a compelling business case that is complete and convincing.

You know what it is like when you have to make a decision about introducing a new product? I can help you assemble a map of competitor products so that you can define and position your product successfully.

You know what it is like when you are trying to make your staff more alert and responsive to the critical competitive issues? I can show you how to create and sustain an on going competitive intelligence program that is personal and powerful for each person.

I like solving hard problems that businesses face. You can trust me to focus on what is most important, to tell you the truth about hard issues and to be dedicated to making you successful.
