Jennifer Griffin’s Summary

Since 2005, I have served in the marketing capacity for law firms, a professional trade organization and a women's publication. I am currently the membership chair for the Raleigh City Group Chapter of the Legal Marketing Association and a member of the Triangle AMA Social Media Committee. Social Media is my passion and I love to teach others to appreciate this tool as much as I do.

As the Marketing & Business Development Specialist for Williams Mullen's Raleigh, RTP and Wilmington offices, I coached attorneys on use of social media, coordinated event planning, public relations, article placements, corporate sponsorship, community presence, award nominations, attorney bios, business development, special projects, advertisements and served as the marketing liaison to the firm's Women's Initiative.
