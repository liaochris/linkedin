Wesley Swanepoel’s Summary

I started tinkering with Flash 4 in my student days over a decade ago. As an all round Multimedia enthusiast my technical skills grew bigger and spilled into literally a larger arena by doing multi-screen productions for live events. Flash was the starting point in my career and allowed me the creative freedom that other programming languages couldn't achieve.

My major achievement was scooping four awards for the project Lynx Blow. This campaign microsite was an industry first in allowing a user to interact with a microphone. A few similar websites soon followed.

Today I am the technical lead for Flash development at Ogilvy One. Besides still actively developing projects, I love to architect larger project builds and actively manage a team of Flash developers. I am constantly looking for new ways to achieve the expectation set out by creative teams and pushing the envelope regarding visual programming experiences.

I consider myself a Creative Technologist even though the term has simply amalgamated from Multimedia, New Media and Visual Programming. It should be noted I am an avid blogger, reader and have tremendous interest in new technologies.
