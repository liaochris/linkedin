Liz Hover’s Summary

I’m a 35-year-old British website manager and online content producer for a Canadian film and television training organisation in Manitoba, Canada.

I author two blogs: Diary of a Web Gal - writing about the social web; and Hi, I'm Sadie Shih Tzu - my dog's blog (nominated for a Canadian Weblog Award).

In the past I've written freelance covering everything from Madonna’s latest album to bio-technology.

I'm also a speaker specialising in how to use social media tools. During my presentations, people laugh. I can talk about strategy with humour (thank God).
