Jerry Dunaway, P.E., MBA’s Summary

I provide facilities engineering solutions for our Navy and Marine Corps clients at Naval Facilities Engineering Command Southwest. I currently manage public-private venture housing programs to greatly improve the quality of life for our Sailors and Marines by creating market-style housing in concert with our private sector partners. My prior experiences range across engineering consulting, military base support operations, military construction management, base closure operations, and hazardous waste site cleanup program management.

Jerry Dunaway, P.E., MBA’s Specialties:

Strategies and tactics for developing facilities, managing operations and solving engineering challenges, partnership management and facilitation, regulatory agency and special interest negotiations, public relations/risk communications management and engagement, and environmental risk management and mitigation.
