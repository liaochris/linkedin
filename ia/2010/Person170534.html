    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    May 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Power Of Change is a Social Entrepreneurship initiative enabling grass-root NGOs to execute their projects in the sectors of Education, Child Development, Rural Employment and Rural Development. We intend to create an ecosystem that results in a paradigm change in the lives of the socially and economically backward.

    We realize one simple fact. The SINGLE REASON that is a hindrance to any country’s development is the presence of major Socio-Economic Divides. Poverty, Terrorism, Child Labor, etc are not the reasons obstructing a country’s development. They are the symptoms of this malady of Socio-Economic Divide. There is a very apt phrase that describes the solution to this problem- “Treat the disease, not the symptom”. And this is what we, Power Of Change, intend to do.

    Bridging the Socio-Economic Divide involves eradication of the root causes of Illiteracy, Child Under-development, Rural Unemployment and Rural Under-development. And based on your interest and available time, there are four different ways through which ALL OF US can help in bridging this divide:

    (a) By getting involved as a volunteer with one of our supported Charities
    (b) By taking our assistance to start your own Charity
    (c) By taking our assistance to start your own Social Entrepreneurship venture, the way we have done with Power Of Change
    (d) By donating to one / few of our supported Charities. If making a donation, we encourage you to actively track through us as to how your donation is being utilized
