R. Adam Smith’s Summary

Circle Peak Capital is a private investment partnership in NYC, solely owned by its Managing Partner and which invests opportunistically for the long-term alongside owner-operators, proven entreprenuers, and/or family businesses.

Circle Peak Capital seeks to invest, with our own capital, and, at times, in conjunction with other equity partners, in firms with between $25 million and $500 million in sales with equity commitments of between $2.5 million and $100 million, in control or structured minority investment situations - we invest on behalf of its limited partners, and have particular interests in lower-mid cap and mid-cap companies in:

• Packaged food and beverage
• Health and wellness
• Beauty
• Luxury; selected retail/apparel
• Household products
• Sporting goods / fitness

R. Adam Smith’s Specialties:

Circle Peak invests in middle-market private firms in consumer branded goods and asset management related firms with significant revenue and brand development potential. Circle Peak is led by experienced private equity professionals and operating executives, and supported by executive advisors and founder partners.

Please visit us at www.circlepeakcapital.com.
