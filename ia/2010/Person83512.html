Linda Strahan Cordial’s Summary

Linda Strahan Cordial's recruiting career began with Snelling & Snelling in 1996. From there she moved to Management Recruiters. Ultimately she followed the path of so many others in her field, leaving the corporate recruiting community to start her own company. Since 1998 Linda has owned and managed Strahan Associates.

Strahan Associates specializes in HVAC, motors, pumps and related industries. Within these industries we recruit for every position related to the manufacturing, product development and business administration of our clients. We recruit for: Human Resources, Product Design, Applications Engineers, Process Engineers, Quality Engineers, Project Engineering, Controls, Software Controls Engineers, Automation, Maintenance, Reliability, Safety and Environmental, Program Mangers, Sales, Finance, Training, Supply Chain, and R&D. reliability.

In addition our expertise is in Continuous Improvement, Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma. We network with VP's of Business Process Improvement, Directors of Six Sigma, Champions, Six Sigma Coaches, Implementation Leaders, Team Leaders, Process Owners, etc. Many organizations use different titles, we ask the right questions, to understand specifically what your requirements are. These individuals are certified Green Belts, Black Belts, Master Black Belts, and Experts, with backgrounds in manufacturing as well as transactional Six Sigma.

Linda is a (CPC), Certified Personnel Consultant. In addition she is an AIRS Certified Internet Researcher and a Certified Recruiting Manager (CRM). Strahan Associates is a proud Preferred Member and Top Producer of the prestigious Top Echelon Network (AV06), the world’s largest network of Independent Recruiters.
