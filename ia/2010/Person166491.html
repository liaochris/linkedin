Pauline Shulman’s Summary

Pauline Shulman is a senior marketing professional with more than 20 years experience in product marketing, strategic marketing, marketing communications, and product management. Journeying through the multifarious evolutions of high-tech since its infancy, Pauline has introduced award winning products, served as industry spokesperson, published dozens of articles, and even patented a cutting-edge development environment.

Pauline has held senior roles in Product Marketing, Market Development, Product Strategy, and Marketing Communications at Hewlett Packard, Wind River, Tealeaf Technology, and Real-Time Innovations. She has a successful track record introducing new products to the enterprise, desktop and embedded markets.

Insightful, with a well-rounded industry perspective, Pauline is an articulate and engaging writer and speaker. As a former software engineer, she has a solid grasp of technology and a unique ability to bridge cross-functional disciplines. She deeply understands the business, the market needs and direction, and the problems facing customers. Her insight into the changing needs of the high-tech market make her a superior strategist.
