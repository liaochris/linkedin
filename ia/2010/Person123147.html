Leah Reynolds’s Summary

Young talent is critical to our future economic success and sustainability. If you're not strategically engaging Gen Yers in your workplace, you're not maximizing your opportunity to connect with Gen Y (and their Baby Boomer parents) in the marketplace. You're also losing valuable innovative ideas and efficiency gains and failing to prepare your future leaders.

I can help you realize a greater return on your Gen Y investment. I combine deep expertise in the attraction and retention of Generation Y (currently up to age 29), with my knowledge of human resource plans and programs to create successful Gen Y talent strategies and solutions. I understand what Gen Y values at work and what it takes to motivate and engage them.

My work involves four primary steps:

1. Develop the business case...What's the opportunity cost of not taking action? What's the up-side, bottom-line potential of fully engaging Gen Y?

2. Assess the current workplace to determine where gaps exist between your employer value proposition and the expectations of Gen Y employees.

3. Prioritize change initiatives and develop a detailed work plan for adaptation.

4. Monitor bottom-line results, institute continuous improvement practices and celebrate and promote successes and lessons learned.

I can help you create your own Gen Y success story! Contact me at 248-417-7961 or leahareynolds@gmail.com.
