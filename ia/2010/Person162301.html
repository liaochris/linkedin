Perishable Logistics Specialists Network’s Summary

PLSN was created for the international cold chain logistics specialists who need reliable and efficient partners around the world who are able and ready to properly ship and store perishable cargo, no matter what the commodity. Our membership is a varied group of professional perishable experts who have years of experience handling this kind of cargo.

PLSN does not admit any pretenders into our group. We do not allow membership based upon having a flag on the map in any particular market. Our members have all provided multiple references for their years of accomplishment in their specialties. You can trust their professionalism and work ethic.

If your company is dedicated to the standard of professionalism required from a PLSN member and you would like to build your perishables trade, why not apply for membership? Our members could always use another good perishables specialist to work with!

Perishable Logistics Specialists Network’s Specialties:

The Perishable Logistics Specialists Network (PSLN) is made up of the world’s leading cool chain logistics companies around the world. We are a non-exclusive group who only admits companies with a proven track record.

The products that PSLN members ship and store include:

Fruits and Vegetables


Meats / Fish and Seafood


Live Animals


Pharmaceuticals


Hi-Tech Equipment with Climate / Temperature Control Requirements


Any other Cool Chain Necessities
