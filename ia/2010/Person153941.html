Nick Farrell’s Summary

I graduated from the University of Colorado with a degree in Environmental Studies and Economics and worked as an analyst for Ventyx (formerly Global Energy Decisions). This position gave me the opportunity to learn about the industry and some of the fundamentals driving new and current energy projects.
