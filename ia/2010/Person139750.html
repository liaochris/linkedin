Jennifer Leggio’s Summary

Jennifer Leggio is passionate about all things social media, especially enterprise, security, privacy and reputation issues, and writes about these issues for ZDNet’s Social Business blog. She has been a communications professional for more than 15 years and currently works at the cross-section of social media and security as director of strategic communications for Fortinet. Jennifer has led or supported interactive social networking efforts for industry conferences including SOURCE Boston, RSA Conference and Black Hat USA, and founded the Security Twits, a community for network security professionals. She co-hosts the Quick’n'Dirty Social Media Podcast with Aaron Strout and is a founding member of Technically Women, a communal blog project. Jennifer was profiled in Silicon Valley San Jose Business Journal’s “40 Under 40″ edition, as a rising star for 2009.
