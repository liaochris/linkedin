Stephanie Miller’s Summary

Stephanie Miller is a gaming professional that has risen through the ranks to achieve success in many areas. She is highly proficient with technical details while maintaining positive relationships with her business owners. .

Her IT gaming career started as a Support Analyst with Park Place Ent., where she project managed the Caesars Support Center Survey System as well as the Phone Directory Migration for the Western Region Support Center.
After accepting a position at the Las Vegas Hilton as a PC Tech position, she rose to be the Point of Sale (POS) & Ancillary Systems Business Analyst.

Stephanie implemented property-wide systems for; Laundry, Security, RF Room Safes, POS to Players Club comping interface, InfoGenesis / MMS hybrid system for 13 Retail outlets. She also implemented 4 new F&B outlets and 2 system-wide POS upgrades.

In Sept. 2006, she joined the MGM Resorts team as a Business Analyst. Stephanie became a valued member of the BA Team by mentoring new Business Analysts, assisting in creation of process & documentation for the Business Analysts and team building. She worked on Enterprise Retail & POS projects as well as being a primary discovery BA.
Her white paper on Kiosks was published in the CIO Newsletter for MGM Resorts.

In Nov. 2007, she joined the Corporate Solutions team. Her skill set includes managing labor resources, technical staff, and contracted labor.
She has managed multiple Contract Management projects within the sourcing and financial groups.
Her responsibilities included Corpate Tax initiative projects, corporate legal software migrations, data migrations & encryption, PCI compliance, & SOX requirement projects.
