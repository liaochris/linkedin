Howard Moorey’s Summary

Hojomo advises businesses, organisations, and individuals on how to use digital media and social networks to build relationships and deliver value.

Working with Hospitality, Road/Rail, Finance & Social Enterprises.

Currently developing my Marketing4Business company through knowledge sharing and networking, in conjunction with www.pubutopia.com. At the same time expanding our debt management franchise throughout Gloucestershire and the Cotswolds, in association with www.debtdr.co.uk - "Prescribing Life Without Debt".

Collaboration is the name of the game!

Passionate about inclusion of location based services in all business recovery, revival or realignment plans. If you’re in business, the key to surviving & thriving is being found, by more & more people! Also love coffee, real ale, wine & pork pies!

Bring with me the experience of nearly 30 years in sales, marketing & business development roles for a variety of corporates in the automotive & financing industries. Love talking to people about their businesses, so the marketing venture was a natural progression.

Now I get the chance to evangelise about the benefits of building a business' online presence and attracting new customers in the process, using the latest digital media channels. Contact me for profile & brand management too.

Always keen to work with businesses or agencies looking to refresh Promotion and Sales Development strategies to include the digital approach. Good marketing now is the foundation for survival, and recovery at the earliest opportunity.

The new world demands new routes to business - I'm your MAN! (Find me in several places on Twitter & Facebook)
