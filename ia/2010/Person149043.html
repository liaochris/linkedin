I believe that God has a purpose for me in using my life’s experiences in sharing myself by serving others.

It is my passion, to help those who feel that they do not have a voice to be heard. I specialize in transition and grief. I am blessed with the opportunity to speak to so many who have a true passion to help heal this hurting world and inspire others live's but do not have a positive direction to know how to motivate themselves and allow their passions to be heard. I simply love to help others use their pain and create a purpose for their lives that they have left in the shadow.

I am determined, even if I have to walk alone to heal this hurting world one person at a time. I am dedicated to do what ever is needed to heal hearts, and inspire lives.

I believe that if you continue to sit idle in this world, your life is not going to change. You have to be willing to get up and be bold enough to take the chance to allow others to hear your testimony. It is awesome to know that your life experience that you may once have felt shamed about, can save thousands of lives, and give them a renewed sense of hope.

If I can assist in anyway please do not hesitate to ask. It could be as simply as prayer, or needing to be coached to motivate you and help assist you to start your own motivational advocacy group so you can also help others heal in their lives.

Professionally, I am committed to investing in individuals’ personal lives in the course toward change. I will be available for as long as I am needed to go the extra mile and I look forward to having our paths cross on what will be a memorable, life changing experience you won’t regret. I stand ready and willing to serve God in my calling, as I serve you.
