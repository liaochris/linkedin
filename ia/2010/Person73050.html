    (Computer Networking industry)

    January 2010 — Present (7 months)

    We decided to create our own piece of technology history by becoming the first ‘real’ social media site in soccer. By owning our own scalable platform it is also possible for us to replicate this social network to any other sports and activities.

    People are now searching for online niche communities where they can share something specific in their lives. We happened to need a networking site dedicated to Soccer-Football unique to our own 'vertical niche'- a business that would cater to our own industry.

    Pass2Me is the collective vision of people who have had a lifelong immersion in all aspects of soccer -- as players, coaches, professionals, administrators, managers and fans of the game. We are passionate about soccer and want to help you have the optimal experience of the world’s game online.

    Pass2Me enables you to post and receive photos, videos and content, chat with friends and colleagues, send updates to friends and teammates, send and receive messages, post career and education information for employers or recruiters, connect to college soccer coaches, manage and invite people to your events, form and join groups, get the latest news and updates about soccer around the world, support your favorite teams and share your opinion and knowledge of the game.
