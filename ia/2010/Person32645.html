Carl Risch’s Summary

As Managing Director, for a consulting company with major clients such as Porsche Engineering, British Steel and Ford Motors, I delivered process re-engineering, project management and 6 Sigma/Lean reviews of their business processes. At Ford, the 18 month effort, saved $400 million out of a $2 billion dollar annual budget.

I have a PMI certification for Project Manager Professional (PMP) and a Certified Six Sigma Black Belt (CSSBB) through ASQ.

As Corporate Manager of Business Processes and Procedures reporting to the CEO, I led the redesign, implementation and integration of a new ERP software package and the optimization of business processes to strategically fit with our primary way of doing business. The $30 million dollar implementation project and the 6 succeeding projects have yielded over $2.2 million is savings in less than one year. We have identified another 44 projects which will take an additional 3 years to complete with an estimated $16 million in savings.

As Client Services Manager, I managed multiple consulting teams and project managers, in the implementation of JDE software. The use of 6 Sigma and Lean tools allowed for robust process design and on time delivery of the promised dates. I was responsible for the billable hours of the entire consulting group.

As Strategic Project Manager, of a $2 billion sales company I managed all the commercial and technical exchanges between Saturn and AK Steel. The project involved the coordination of 6 VPs plus 100 personnel at AK Steel and over 200 Saturn personnel in the startup of the Saturn Car Company. This project was worth over $80 million in sales annually to AK Steel.
