Angelos Gregoriou’s Summary

Angelos Gregoriou is a holder of the M.Sc. Music Information Technology and B.Sc. Computer Science. He has a strong interest in browser-based applications development and commercialization. He is the co-founder and Chairman & CEO of DW Dynamic Works Ltd since inception day, early 2003.

Angelos’ background includes excellent knowledge of Coldfusion / SQL / Oracle programming as well as HTML, Javascript, CSS, Ajax. Good knowledge of PHP, mySQL, Director (Lingo) C, Assembly as well as many other software languages and packages.

Being the head of one of the most reputable browser-based software vendors in the area he has skills in management, is well organized and efficient and prides himself on the ability to approach, assist and consult the client, understand the client's business and organize the team for meeting high targets.

His portfolio of success includes four Banks, Government Departments, the President of the Republic, and International Organizations.

Angelos Gregoriou’s Specialties:

Angelos is a specialist in development of browser-based applications such as web portals and browser-based business software. All solutions his company provided are based around EasyConsole Framework, which is a platform that allows endless customization of any application.
