Brett Blauvelt’s Summary

Brett Blauvelt is the owner and one of the managing members of Red Rock Contractors and Red Rock Pools and Spas, one of Arizona’s leading custom building, pool, and landscape contractors. Red Rock was formed in 1998 and is celebrating their 10 year anniversary. Red Rock has grown and continues to evolve into one of the most dynamic and diverse custom building contractors in the valley; offering their customers a single source solution to all of their custom building needs from commercial building construction to residential landscape design and installation. Red Rock Contractors’ mission is to build lasting relationships with customers, subcontractors, vendors, and employees as well as the communities it does business within. Their emphasis on relationships and quality construction is what has fueled their success and growth.

Brett has always enjoyed in one form or another the construction field. Out of college working for others was not his strong point so starting his own company made sense. After finishing college Brett and his father started a pool construction clean-up company and ran it for over three years before selling the company and moving on to pursue his real passion for design. After partnering up with close and long time friend Rick Chafey they formed Red Rock.

Brett's role in the company started out as not only the designer but also the installer. Having knowledge in all areas of the construction process is what has set him apart and has allowed Red Rock to continue to grow at the right pace. Specialty jobs are the real passion for Brett. The main goal and vision for Brett is to create a company that has no fear of any job and can work with clients that are willing to trust not only the designer but are willing to push the limits of the design aspect. If you can think it Red Rock can build it and that is how Brett looks at all jobs that he sells to his clients.
