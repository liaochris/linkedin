Louise Garver’s Summary

Louise has been partnering with talented executives to provide a clear roadmap on how to identity their unique gifts, strategically position themselves at the compensation level they are worth, and accelerate their job search.

Fascinated with the careers field nearly her entire life, Louise made it her mission to become an expert on the subject. Her education, 7 career-related certifications combined with prior recruitment and corporate management experience, provide her clients with a distinct edge.

Louise's approach to high-level job search centers on designing the right combination of cutting-edge strategies that work for each executive's unique career situation and needs. The value she provides clients results in confidence-building job-search assistance they can count on to propel their careers forward.
