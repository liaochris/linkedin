Dixie Baker’s Summary

Dr. Dixie B. Baker is a Senior Vice President and Technical Fellow at Science Applications International Corporation (SAIC), where she serves as the Chief Technology Officer for the Health Solutions Business Unit. Dr. Baker is an internationally recognized leader in information assurance, critical systems architecture, and health information technology. She has published in a number of technology and health-related areas, including high-assurance architecture, information protection, electronic medical records, and Internet safety.
