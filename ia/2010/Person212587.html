Vishwas Manral’s Summary

I am a believer in technology as a tool to enhance communication and enhance the quality of human lives.

I have worked on a whole gamut of data technologies from routing/ signaling to ASIC design, from DRM to device security, from CLI's to Linux Kernel drivers, from home networks all the way to transport networks - all these for both wireless and wireline technologies. In the process I have authored a lot of IETF RFC's (more than anyone in India) including RFC 5709, RFC 5643, RFC 5329, RFC 5310, RFC 4835, RFC 4222, RFC 4063, RFC4062 and RFC4061 (besides a few others) which have numerous citations.
http://tinyurl.com/27ro73q and http://www.arkko.com/tools/allstats/vishwasmanral.html

I am mainly involved in the product definition, architecture, design and coding. I have also regularly worked with the pre-sales successfully selling the products to a lot of Tier-1 vendors, as well as with marketing to define new features to go into products.

In the security front I have found some of the main vulnerabilities in the IPv6 (most IPv6 devices were affected by this vulnerability - http://www.securityfocus.com/bid/23615 and http://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc5095.txt), routing, switching and other technologies. One of the work has formed the base of the effort to enhance Routing Security. I have also worked on IPsec and filtering/ fire-walling.

Vishwas Manral’s Specialties:

New ideas, problem solving, focused and dedicated. More specifically Security, Routing/ switching, Hardware integration, building energized teams.

Vishwas Manral’s Experience

  • Principal Engineer

    IP Infusion

    (Privately Held; Computer Networking industry)

    February 2006 — Present (4 years 8 months)

    In this role I started out by doing all aspects of the DRM technology. Right from designing to developing and testing code for the DRM technology for home-networking devices(DTCP-IP). This includes a protocol like IPsec (called DTCP) and another like IKE with Elliptic Curve Cryptography (EC-DSA and EC-DH) called AKE.

    I have also worked with different ASIC's as well as Layer2/ 2.5/ 3 protocols. I then worked on routing, routing security, IPv6 and MPLS (RSVP-TE, LDP, 6PE, 6VPE) defining the standards for many of these. Specifically I have worked on all routing protocols(OSPF(v2 &v3), BGP, IS-IS, RIP), signalling protocols (LDP, RSVP-TE) and MPLS/ MPLS-TP with a focus on Provider Networks and OAM (BFD, VCCV, LSP Ping etc).

    I have also architected and developed the BFD module from scratch. I have wokred on extending the MPLS architecture to the GMPLS suite of protocols (intrastructure, RSVP-TE extensions, LMP, OSPF-TE) - especially its use in packet networks - MPLS-TP.

    I am now focused on working on the architecture and features for products for a Tier-1 vendor.

    I have all along been involved with pre-sales to successfully sell the product to various customers including Tier-1 vendors. I have also been involved in defining the ZebOS product features, as well as newer generation technologies like Data Center networking and power optimization and benchmarking.

  • Senior Software Engineer

    SiNett Corp

    (Privately Held; Computer Networking industry)

    October 2003 — February 2006 (2 years 5 months)

    I was as one of the first members in the software teamworking on a wireless(802.11, CAPWAP)/ wireline (ethernet switching/ Routing/ NAT/ IPv6 Tunnelling)/ security(IPsec/ Stateless firewall/ IDS/ IPS) ASIC. Started from working on basic drivers and CLI and then went on to Hardware abstraction Layer.

    I then worked on getting a switch system using the above ASIC up, in the process learning to hack the Linux kernel and finally landed up doing some ASIC architecture. Tried to focus on the security part all along.

    I have been involved in working with the customers evaluating the product to help them understand the various technologies.

  • Senior Software Engineer

    Motorola (formerly Netplane Systems)

    I was among the first few engineers in the team. Worked on writing Highly available and distributed Routing protocols OSPF/ IS-IS/ SNMP MIB development from scratch as well as enhancements to other protocols. Worked on Alarm management, Device management etc at a later stage.
