Norma Huibregtse’s Summary

Want Raving Fans? I am on Linkedin to help small business owners optimize their customer service experience so that they can create raving fans, build customer loyalty, make more money and have more fun!

Are you a small business owner in the service industry, who has been in business for 2-5+ years, and who is serious about improving your customer service experience to set yourself apart from the competition? If that's you or if you know of a small business owner that I should talk to, contact me directly or visit our website at http://www.captivatedcustomers.com.

We specialize in salons, spa facilities, hotels and resorts, chiropractors, veterinarians and the like. Here are some of the services we provide:

►FREE 30-Minute Consultation – A FREE phone consultation is available to you to help you evaluate your service experience and uncover opportunities to improve it. Give us a call. We are at your service!

►One-to-One Coaching/Consulting – We work with small business owners and/or their management team to develop and implement customer service strategies. Services include help with email campaigns, customer surveys, mystery shopping, and social media marketing.

►Employee Workshops/Team Training- We meet with your employees to review customer relationship best practices and principles, discuss current customer relationship issues and resolutions, and teach how to drive customer referrals.

►Speaker/Corporate Educator- We want to speak with your group, association or organization about customer service optimization and how it can drive sales performance. Contact us today for our availability.

►Linked A.C.E. Training - We provide training on how to create a compelling profile on LinkedIn to Attract, Connect, and Engage more customers and Advanced Training to market yourself to your target customer.

Visit our website for additional information.
