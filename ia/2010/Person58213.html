Don McCallen’s Summary

PostNet is the ultimate business support centre.

PostNet makes taking care of business easier than ever and we’re coming to Durham Region!

PostNet is a full service, diversified business centre that has over 900 locations worldwide. We offer Digital Services such as graphic design, computer time rental, scanning services and have the ability to receive and output files in a variety of formats. We also offer Print & Copy Services including digital black and white and colour copies, complete business printing services, document finishing services and much more. PostNet's Pack & Ship Services include Federal Express®, Purolator and Canada Post services providing freedom of choice and a wide range of service options including custom packaging and supplies. And to add even more convenience, PostNet offers a host of Business and Additional Services including passport photos, fax services, signs and banners and office supplies.

So Many Services, One Easy Stop - that's PostNet, the ultimate business support centre.

Don McCallen’s Specialties:

Print & Copy: PostNet can handle all of your printing and document services needs.

Digital Services: We have the equipment and capabilities to design, print, scan and archive all of your important business documents.

Packing And Shipping: Our dynamic point-of-sale technology enables you to easily compare carriers (FedEx®, Purolator , Canada Post), services and rates to make the best choices.
