Bo Taylor’s Summary

Bo has been involved with sports and fitness training for over 18 years. As a student, Bo swam for Radford University where he set a school record in the 1650 yard freestyle. He previously worked for MUSC Cardiac Rehabilitation as an Exercise/Telemetry Specialist and for Carilion Health Systems as a Wellness Center Manager and Cardiac Rehab Specialist. Bo is responsible for the overall management of the fitness and aquatics operations. In addition, he develops exercise programs and personal training for members.

Make the most of your exercise efforts with Bo Taylor, Fitness & Aquatics Manager of the Daniel Island Club & Owner of Bo Knows Fitness; a certified personal trainer who has often been called upon by both local celebrities and athletes for his expertise in sports performance, weight loss management, general fitness, and functional strength training. Bo is a certified personal trainer and a member of some of the fitness industries most prestigious organizations including the National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA), National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM), and American Red Cross (CPR/AED & Lifeguard Instructor).
