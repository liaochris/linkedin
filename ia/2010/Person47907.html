    Daniel is responsible for client administrative and operational needs in our Silicon Valley office, including generating investment portfolio reviews, implementing financial planning recommendations, monitoring and maintaining client accounts, and completing necessary client and custodian paperwork. He passed the March 2009 CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNERTM exam and is a candidate for CFP® Board of Standards certification in 2011. Daniel earned a certificate with distinction in the Personal Financial Planning program at the University of California, Berkeley.

    Daniel's prior experience includes working as an Operations Coordinator and Purchasing Assistant at Kumho's Tires in Southern California, as well as serving as a Marketing intern at Daimler Chrysler in Seoul, South Korea. He is an active member of the Korean American Professional Society and a volunteer for the Financial Planning Association (FPA) Pro Bono Program. He is also a devoted fundraiser for charity: water, an organization that builds wells for villages around the world. Daniel graduated from the University of Michigan with a Bachelor of Science in Economics, and currently serves as vice president on the board of the Michigan Alumni Club of Greater San Francisco.
