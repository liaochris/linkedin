Jesse Taggert’s Summary

I thrive on communication + design strategy and seek projects that value that. I also work from and manage Citizen Space, a San Francisco coworking space that supports entrepreneurs, consultants, and other independent professionals. My work focuses on marketing strategy, branding, and design for small companies that value the process of developing relationships with their audience.

How? I ask good questions. I get to know my clients' strengths and needs. I get to know their audiences' strengths and needs. I discover the competing messages that are in the market. And I work with my clients to craft and share their story so it resonates with their audience.

With over 15 years experience in communication and graphic design, I have helped organizations -- such as technology start ups, professional associations, museums, universities, and even the U.S. Navy -- inspire their audiences through what is said, how it is said, and where it said.
