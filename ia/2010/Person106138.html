Joshua Kaufman’s Summary

I'm an interaction designer and user experience consultant living in San Francisco. My work focuses on design and strategy for mobile and convergent user experiences. I hold an M.S. Human Computer Interaction and I have over 8 years of experience in both the United States and Europe. I'm an active member of the user experience design community, and I currently serve as the community manager of the IxDA San Francisco chapter.

Learn more about my work experience at http://unraveled.com/work/
