douglas foresta’s Summary

I am a speaker, therapist and host of the popular self-help radio show "BreakFree to Success." My mission is to help extraordinary creative people break free from fear and limiting beliefs.

I have been blessed to work with the full spectrum of society. As a radio host, I interview celebrities, CEO's, artists and spiritual leaders about their experiences overcoming fear. For the past 9 years, I have also worked as a social worker, with some of the most marginalized individuals in society.

What I've come to realize is that success is possible at any moment, once we let go of fear-based patterns of living, and start living out of the higher levels of love, peace, creativity and joy. My goal is to help spark creative dreamers from all walks of life to "break free" from fear and fully express their authentic selves.

If you're an artist, entrepreneur, innovator, dreamer or visionary, I hope you'll join the BreakFree Community to share your experiences with others, and to get support around overcoming fear and limiting beliefs.

It is clear that we cannot keep going on living out of fear and "business as usual." We need creators, dreamers and leaders to "break free" from fear, self-doubt, limiting beliefs, and share their gifts with the world.

My goal is quite simply, to touch as many lives, and to support as many extraordinary creative people as I can. And I'm willing to be amazed by the journey!

Doug
