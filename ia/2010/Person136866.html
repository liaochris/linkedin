Matt Demers’s Summary

My career goal is to eventually work in the field of opinion writing. I bring a sharp wit and analytical brain to publications I work at; I am a tireless researcher and have an ear for stories that both engage the reader and leave them wanting more.

I have been blogging since 2006, and have experience with numerous platforms. I have also completed a co-operative education semester at the Hamilton Spectator newspaper, and attend Ryerson University for Journalism. I have taken it upon myself to get published in numerous websites and zines in order to further my experience and to refine my writing.

Matt Demers’s Specialties:

Column writing, editorial research, social media publicity.
