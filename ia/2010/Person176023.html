Rob VandenBrink’s Summary

I've been working in all facets of Networking and Security since 1981, in a consulting capacity with Metafore since 1994. My consulting practice currently covers a large number of clients in Financial and Manufacturing Sectors, with supported networks covering most international timezones.

My current work towards a Masters Degree at STI is opening up entirely new project possibilities for me. Current projects include Powershell automation of VMware, VMware security, Scripting on Cisco IOS, and security in fiber channel architectures.
