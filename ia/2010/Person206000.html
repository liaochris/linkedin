Tom Fox’s Summary

I have been responsible for many mission critical systems and services for over 14 years. I have implemented and supported virtually every type of business system including Finance, Human Resources, Development, and customer facing Production systems. Throughout this time I have managed a wide range of workers, from network and system engineers, Unix and Windows administrators, and programmers. I have identified, hired, trained and promoted many employees and contractors. At times managing larger interim teams during system implementations.

I have reported to the executive staff of many companies. The management of these organizations required and appreciated my attention to detail during the planning and budgeting processes, and my focus on delivering value to the business during deployment.

I differentiate myself by approaching every problem as if it were my own and taking ownership and responsibility rather than avoiding it.

Success is in the details.

My negotiation skills have saved my employers hundreds of thousands of dollars. My teams tend to be extremely productive due to my management style.
