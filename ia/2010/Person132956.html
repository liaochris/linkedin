Marion E. Gold’s Summary

I offer a full range of editorial & consulting services, including writing, ghostwriting, editing, creative press kits, press releases, editorials, books, and publicity planning. An experienced writer and marketing communications professional, my articles and editorials appear in marketing trade journals, national newspapers and magazines - including Advertising Age, Chicago Sun-Times, Chicago Tribune, and the Business Journal of Phoenix.

- Author of the "Personal Publicity Planner: A Guide to Marketing YOU," and "TOP COPS: Profiles of Women in Command." "TOP COPS" was endorsed by former Governors George Ryan (Illinois), George Pataki (New York), and Janet Napolitano, US Secretary of Homeland Secretary (former Governor of Arizona).

- Featured in Working Woman magazine and included by Today's Chicago Woman in its list of 100 Women Making a Difference.

- Past president of the Illinois Woman’s Press Association; past vice president of Arizona Press Women.

My career began in health care, as a journalist for pharmaceutical/pharmacy publications, a group of multi-specialty physician newspapers, and then as an Associate Editor for the Journal of the American Medical Association's Medical News Department. Later at the AMA, I served as director of Direct Mail Marketing/List Management. I moved deeper into marketing and publication management in 1981, as EVP and General Manager of a medical communications agency (part of the OMNICOM group of agencies), where I spent 13 years developing and managing medical education materials, CME programs & sales training materials in all medical specialties, including nursing and pharmacy.

In 1994, I launched Marion Gold Communications and turned to authoring books and articles about women in the workforce, ghostwriting, public speaking, and developing visibility plans and press kits for authors, entrepreneurs and business owners, and nonprofit organizations.

To learn more visit http://www.mariongold.com.

Marion E. Gold’s Specialties:

Marketing & publicity materials and strategic planning for entrepreneurs, book authors, small businesses and nonprofits, "Personal Publicity" and other specialty press kit development, and "Marketing YOU" workshops & presentations. An experienced journalist in healthcare, medical education and women's advocacy. Expertise in publishing, writing, ghost writing and editing for professional and consumer audiences.

Bottom line: Creating Communications That Make A Difference
