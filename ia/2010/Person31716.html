Chad Campbell’s Summary

Senior level sales executive who has consistently surpassed expectations in leadership and direction of start up, high-growth sales and marketing operations in B2B sales environments. Documented record of turning around sales teams and divisions, achieving 200% growth in 3 years. History of industry leading performance in client management and new business development through consultative, solution based selling approach effectively matching product or service to specific need(s) of customers and through the effective management of a lead generation system. Recognized for award winning professional development of direct and indirect reports.

A common theme in my experience has been to seek out the biggest challenges and work in areas of greatest need. I have built a sales division from scratch and led a $68 million dollar division with 5 sales managers, 45 sales reps and a support staff of 14. Over the last nine years, I've recruited, developed and managed collectively over 150 reps with the highest percentage of award winning reps in the company. Acclaimed for the environment of the divisions I've led and the seamless integration of innovative management techniques with existing methods and effective alignment of individual and departmental goals with corporate goals to enhance productivity, boost revenues and exceed established performance targets.

    Our publications work. The HomePages® Directories generate a profitable return for our advertisers, and provide consumers with a convenient guide to hometown businesses, families, and civic information. Over the past twelve years, our company has grown steadily from two employees in 1997 to nearly 500 today, and we are proud of the fact that many of our first advertisers from our first year are still enthusiastic customers.

    Since our beginning in October of 1997, one simple consideration guides every decision we make… what makes sense!

    So what makes sense to us?

    It makes sense to treat business owners fairly and to give them the flexibility to target their advertising to the communities and homeowners that make sense to them.

    It makes sense to publish directories with highly local relevant community sections, a white page section with large, easy-to-read print, a convenient map of town(s) and more ads from local businesses than any other phone directory.

    It makes sense to mail our directories through the US Postal Service to every home, business and PO box holder.

    It makes sense to recruit and retain customer-focused selling professionals and to back this sales team with the best design department, customer service and accounting professionals we could find.

    It makes sense to never settle and to keep refining and improving the products we produce, as well as the way in which we produce them.

    It makes sense to never to take for granted the single book advertiser whose decision to advertise with us is the highest compliment we can be given.
