Nick Gagalis’s Summary

I create accurate, compelling copy and pictures to bring the reader/viewer in. I consider myself an educator and entertainer, because the best journalists fill both those roles.

I have a strong background in Print and Multimedia Journalism to go with my Broadcast Journalism Major. Being able to report from many different angles gives me a better knowledge of the subject, and it shows in my work.

When it comes to sports broadcasting, I try to be part scout, part statistician, part story teller. Covering a team and league as closely as possible allows me a better breadth of information to share with my audience.
