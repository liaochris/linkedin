Robin Hunt’s Summary

Beginning my media career in 1996 at a subsidiary of the newspaper giant, McClatchy Corporation, I paved my way through several retail accounts while developing my print media and direct mail expertise. As the world of traditional newspaper began to evolve, so did I and for the past five years I have planned and placed advertising in every type of media venue: print, broadcast, outdoor and digital. I am media agnostic and deeply ascribe to any campaign media mix that enhances the creative message while showing a definitive increase of ROI for the client.
