Rafael Jimenez’s Summary

Founded, along with my sister Gery and some friends, an interactive agency in the early 90s, merged it with a competitor a few years later, then sold it to Ogilvy in 2000.

Led OgilvyInteractive Mexico for close to five years, and then after a short stint heading Strategic Planning at Ogilvy, was invited to launch Yahoo! Search Marketing in Mexico.

A few months later became Country Manager, and in 2007 was appointed GM, Hispanic America for the Advertiser and Publisher Group at Yahoo!-- which made me realize how much I missed the creative and entrepreneurial side of things and decided to launch an interactive agency again.

To that end, I partnered with Dany Saadia (a good friend and a former partner in interfaz401 and current partner at Dixo), and launched Substance®.

I'm frequently invited to speak at industry events, which I enjoy a lot. This has taken me to cities from San Francisco to Buenos Aires and several others in between. I've also been invited as juror a few times, for regional and local award shows.

I had the honor of serving as President of the IAB Mexico for three years (2007-2009).
