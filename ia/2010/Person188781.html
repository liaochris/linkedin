Sherman Dillard’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL PROFILE

Sherman is a collegiate, basketball coaching veteran with extensive experience at the NCAA Division I level, including ten years as Head Basketball Coach. He has a successful and comprehensive background in planning, implementing and managing all phases of a competitive athletic program, with exemplary skills in recruitment, player development, career guidance, media relations, marketing, promotions, alumni relations, sponsorship and fundraising.

As Global Director with Nike, Sherman demonstrated significant managerial and directorship expertise in the planning and execution of some of the industry's most recognizable and distinguished sports academies and camps, with a premium on superior organization, innovation, and authenticity. Under his astute guidance, the aforementioned academies became the standard by which all others were measured.

Sherman is a dedicated and focused leader with a passion for motivating and inspiring a team, developing team unity and creating a vision with an unwavering commitment to excellence. He is extremely adept at developing long-lasting, positive and cohesive relationships with excellent communication and interpersonal skills. As an executive, Sherman has successful managed million dollar budgets while consistently meeting corporate timelines and achieving annual goals.
