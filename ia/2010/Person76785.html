Gordon Kasper, PMP’s Summary

A certified PMP (Project Management Professional) with a balanced mix of technology expertise, deep business acumen and client-facing experience, consistently delighting customers from entrepreneurs to Fortune 100 companies. Significant project management experience, managing simultaneous multi-phase, multi-disciplinary and multi-location projects, as well as directing client engagements and relationships. Analytical, out-of-the-box-thinking, goal setter and problem solver, with a no nonsense approach to addressing client problems and getting the job done on time and within budget. Strength in client relationships, consulting, team leading, and coaching. Highly ethical, hard worker with a roll up your sleeves attitude. Certified in Six Sigma Green Belt.

Experienced Sales Operations Director, skilled in methods and technologies supporting opportunity pipeline growth & management, and booking of business. Specialty in sales forecasting, financial results analysis and reporting. Expert in CRM administration, customization and user education.

Leader in process definition and improvement, including ISO preparedness and certification.
