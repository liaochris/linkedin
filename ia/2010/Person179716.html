Ryan Mainey’s Summary

Dynamic Sales Leader and Passionate Training Executive

Nothing speaks to peak performance like professional sports, and golf fostered my ambitions to accelerate human performance. After professional golf, I applied what I learned about performance and professionalism to a sales career.

Drive, motivation and a passion for development have led to promotions and success at multiple levels for a $5 billion publicly traded company. For more than 12 years, I’ve demonstrated and been recognized for top performance in sales leadership, team development, revenue generation, and performance in restricted environments.

As a proven district manager, I have taken multiple sales teams to the top of a high performance organization. Most recently, I led the a team to their first Balanced Performance in over 5 years. Through motivation, well executed strategies and skill transfer competencies, my team increased revenues by over $550,000 in 2007.

Motivated and determined by nature, I have a natural ability to rally people behind me to achieve meaningful results for my organization. I delivered sales results for Alcon in Sacramento, Pittsburgh, Phoenix and Fort Worth - this includes improving doctor time for ISR Team from 11% to 19% in 2009. I have been consistently recognized for moving market share, improving market position and enhancing the skills of those around me. Skilled in coaching, DiSC and performance management, I have left a track record of meeting/exceeding company sales goals in various market conditions.

Leading people to discover unrealized potential is my professional passion while I remain diligent in a lifetime of learning and adapting to various situations.
