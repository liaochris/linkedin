Eric Green’s Summary

I have spent the past 5+ years supporting SAP systems in both domestic and global environments. I have experience supporting SAP on AIX and Windows platforms utilizing DB2, SQL Server, and Oracle databases.

I have touched most all aspects of releases 4.6B, 4.6C, 4.7, NW04, and NW70 and some exposure to NW710.

I have installed/assisted multiple installations including BI/BW, ECC, Solutions Manager, Development Infrastructure (NWDI), Enterprise Portal (EP), Process Integration (PI), CRM, SCM, ESS, GRC, SLD, and IS solutions such as IS-OIL. Also, installation of various solutions in a high availability (HA) landscape for mission critical apps.

I have performed/assisted multiple upgrades including BW/BI, R/3/ECC, CRM, Solutions Manager, Enterprise Portal, SCM, GRC, etc.

In addition to installations and upgrades, I have performed routine maintenance and troubleshooting of various components of the SAP system such as Transport Management System (TMS), CCMS alerts, memory analysis and optimization, DB performance analysis and optimization, analysis of various traces to resolve issues, and system documentation.

My current role allows me to take a step back from the hands-on day-to-day system administration activities and allows me to focus on the larger SAP strategy of my organization. I currently work with external service providers to coordinate SAP basis related activities while leveraging my expertise in SAP systems administration with the goal of providing fast, reliable, and stable SAP systems.
