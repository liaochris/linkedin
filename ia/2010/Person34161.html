Cathy Martin’s Summary

As director of the Global Marketing team, Cathy Martin is responsible for the development and execution of marketing strategies that harness the power of the full HP portfolio to deliver solutions globally in key industry segments and with HP’s largest and most strategic partners. Partners include Microsoft, Oracle, SAP and Global System Integrators. Industries include Healthcare, Government, Financial Services, Energy, Telecommunications and Manufacturing.

Martin began her career at HP 21 years ago as an account executive in the company’s Analytical Division. She sold and marketed high-tech analysis instrumentation to commercial customers and government agencies including the Food and Drug Administration and the National Institutes of Health. She also led a team assisting the Department of the Navy in its deployment of HP analysis solutions supporting Operation Desert Storm. Later, Martin transitioned into the company’s commercial computer and printer product group, where she managed international sales and marketing strategies with customers such as America Online, Marriott International and GE Information Systems. In her role as Director of Public Sector Marketing, Martin was involved with HP’s expansion of public sector programs, resulting in substantial growth in the organization’s revenue and increased solution-focused services to HP customers.

Martin recently achieved Lean Six- Sigma Green Belt Certification and holds a B.S. degree in chemistry from Trinity College. She enjoys golf, yoga, reading and marathon walking.
