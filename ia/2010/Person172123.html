Richard Calmbach’s Summary

I am seeking employment and consulting opportunities in the areas of software development, IT infrastructure, unstructured data analysis, and business analytics. I have broad experience ranging from system administration to software development to research in text analytics, bioinformatics, and computer vision. I enjoy technical work and I am also considering managerial positions if they involve the advancement or deployment of open source technologies. For 11+ years, I worked as a Lead Software Engineer in the Advanced Technology Solutions group of HNC Software, LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Fair Isaac Corporation. My work encompassed the design and implementation of software systems for the analysis of unstructured data. Our team primarily performed cutting-edge research funded by government grants in the areas of natural language processing (information retrieval, information extraction), computer vision (object recognition in still images and video, image classification), and bioinformatics (gene probe optimization, interactive visualization). My professional interests also include the transfer of research technology into enterprise-grade information management and collaboration infrastructure; the design of frameworks and enterprise architectures; maximizing the potential of open source technologies; software development methodologies and best practices development. In my 25 years of programming I have also picked up the basics of Unix/Linux system administration and identity management (LDAP, OpenID).
