Valentina Venza’s Summary

I'm a graphic/interaction designer, and a programmer too (mainly PHP, but I can also deal with Actionscript, Javascript, CSSs and Processing). I like to think to myself as a rational person with good taste and some inclination towards bizarre things.
I started with web design on my own at the age of 16, and since then I've developed my attitudes in this field through both school and work. I've grown 4 years experience in dynamic websites (communities, online catalogues, etc), while university gave me the knowledge and the opportunity to work on projects from different fields (typography, brand imaging, packaging, etc).
I aim to specialize in Interaction Design: there is nothing that gives me the same joy and makes me feel as realized as it does; it is definetly the field where I fully express myself and my potential
