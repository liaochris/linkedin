Zita Udrys’s Summary

Zita T. Udrys brings a wealth of experience in facilitating intercultural communications. Ms. Udrys has lived, studied and worked in Germany, Poland, the Czech Republic and the United States. Her experience includes design and implementation of cultural immersion and language training for such clients as REEP, Inc.; language instruction for the Beverly Hills Lingual Institute, Berlitz and others; media language services for SDI Media, Technicolor, Fox Television and other clients; interpreting services for U.S. Immigration Courts in Los Angeles, El Paso, San Diego and Portland; and design services for private clients. Ms. Udrys studied applied linguistics at the University of Mainz, specializing in German, Russian and Polish for law and economics. Being of Lithuanian heritage, she also speaks Lithuanian and has served as a member of the Lithuanian Educational Council of the United States. Since moving to Los Angeles in 1998, Ms. Udrys has been active in the work of local nonprofit organizations, including St. Casimir's School, the Los Angeles-Kaunas Sister Cities Program and the Jewish Geneological Society of Los Angeles. She served as the Executive Director of the German American Business Association's (GABA) Southern California Chapter.
