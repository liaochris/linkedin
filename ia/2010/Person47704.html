Dan Kennedy’s Summary

I have worked in ecommerce, search engine marketing, and web development for ten years.

The last three years have been at Summit Web Consultants helping businesses realize more profit from their online presence.

As VP of online services, I helped lead MonsterCommerce from a small start-up with 200 customers to a successful business with more than 6,000 customers and $20 million in annual revenue.

MonsterCommerce was also recognized by the St. Louis Business Association as:

- The fastest growing company in St. Louis (2004)
- Runner up: 'Best Places to Work' (2005)
- Top 50 influential companies in St. Louis (2005)

In addition to consulting, I currently own and operate two successful ecommerce businesses. The first of which imports and retails remote control toys. The second retails residential solar lighting products.

I graduated from the executive MBA program at the University of Colorado in the spring of 2009.
