David Atkins’s Summary

Atkins International Ltd has developed for many Companies a seamless Supply Chain incorporating the lastest technologies in Information, and Logistics.Some clients have been Campbell Soup, Westinghouse, U. S. Steel, Rhone Poulenc, Oceangate, Transfracht, Port of Montreal. Our firm creates matrix of rates and routes for Manufacturers and competitive information for Ports worldwide.

Atkins Internationanl Ltd has also Consulted for Firms in South America, Europe and Asia. Atkins International Ltd provides training on Exports thru our 'Export Expert Express' Program on the new requirements due to the US Patriot Act.

Vist our webstie www.exportexpert.com for a very detailed bio of our Company and its Resources and Scope

David Atkins is an Ad Hoc Professor at the US Merchant Marine Academy and has appeared on CNBC, CNN and NBC as an Industry Expert and has spoken at Journal of Commerce Industry Conferences on "Doing Business In China"
