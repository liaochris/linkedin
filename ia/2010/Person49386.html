Dave Bradburn’s Summary

Graphic Designer and Typographer with a broad experience of all aspects of design and artwork, both for print and online. Although the majority of my work is focussed around start-ups and SMEs, I also have significant experience of working for much larger organisations. I have worked for clients across a wide range of industries but have particular experience in areas such as education, environmental/green issues, property development and classical music.

Essentially, as a Graphic Designer, I enable businesses to communicate more effectively to their clients, prospects and employees etc, by means of making information more accessible and interesting to read and digest.

Dave Bradburn’s Specialties:

Company logos, branding and identity, stationery, brochures, leaflets, newsletters, print advertising, exhibition stands, websites
