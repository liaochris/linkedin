Monique Ramsey’s Summary

Monique Ramsey is the Principal of Cosmetic Social Media, a social media and marketing firm dedicated to serving the niche of aesthetic medicine. She also serves as Director of Sales & Web Marketing at La Jolla Cosmetic Surgery Centre (La Jolla, California).

She melds her two passions, cosmetic surgery marketing and social media, to bring a unique perspective and skill set to her clients.

Monique is one of the first 37 graduates from the International Social Media Association’s (ISMA) Certification Program. She is an ISMA Founding Member and has extensive personal connections in the social media industry.

Monique had an integral role in the development of Inform&Enhance practice management software systems (now a subsidiary of Johnson & Johnson) and has trained and consulted with cosmetic surgery practices in the US and Canada. In addition, she is a contributor to the book Cosmetic Surgery for Dummies, published by Wiley & Sons.

She holds a Bachelor of Science from The University of Southern California and is an active participant in various San Diego charities and organizations. Monique is a passionate USC fan and sports lover, something she shares with her husband and two children.

Monique Ramsey’s Specialties:

social media strategy, training and implementation; cosmetic surgery practice marketing and branding; patient communications such as e-blasts, skincare promotions, e-newsletters; aesthetic surgery sales; patient surveys and analysis; patient communications; medical writing for the layperson; desktop publishing.
