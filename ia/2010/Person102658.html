Mike Shaw’s Summary

Developed many voice products for WilTel's launching of SBC/AT&T LD services; such as pre-paid calling card, ATM-based integrated access, and an IP termination product targeting large VoIP carriers. We won all of the traffic from the largest VoIP provider in the U.S. and grew to about 400 million minutes in six months.

Led the team associated with building the Thrive SMB product for PowerNet Global. Thrive began in Cincinnati in August of '07 and currently (2/09) has well over 300 SMBs mostly large franchises. This product bundles high speed cable modems with VoIP and managed hotspot services.

Launched two municipal WiFi networks in rural markets over the past year placing company in great position to acquire USDA grants for expansion into more markets in 2009.

Primary goal has been to combine VoIP, cellular and WiFi deployments for the purposes of providing customer's with next gen services in (rural) areas where these services would normally take decades before being deployed to these areas.
