Tyler Rogers’s Summary

Imagine a 27th floor Madison Avenue design suite with a half-dozen workstations and an array of Quad-Core Intels sporting 30 inch flat screens and Wacom tablets.

One would deduce from the volume and quality of work that 24 year old Tyler Rogers has produced over the last couple of years that this is the environment from where he is monopolizing the tween world’s social networks with his team of highly skilled graphic designers.

This is only a half truth. Tyler is monopolizing the social networks, but is doing so single-handedly from his Nashville apartment with his iMac, minus the Wacom tablet believe it or not.

To that end, Tyler has become the ‘go to’ community designer for teen trend setters like Hollywood Records recording artists; Jonas Brothers, Miley Cyrus, Demi Lovato and more, as well as high profile artists such as Tim McGraw, LeAnn Rimes and newcomer Jessie James.

With a passion for anything new and cutting-edge, Tyler’s designs are far from cookie cutter templates and are specific to the artist’s image and style.

Inherently gifted and self-taught, Tyler is well-versed in multiple genres and his ability to adapt and deliver give him the obvious advantage.
