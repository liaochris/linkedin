Tony Croft’s Summary

Tony Croft is a Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist. Tony has successfully expanded his Mortgage Planning practice through alliances with Realtors, financial planners, attorneys and CPAs.

Tony's current vision for his Mortgage Team is to continue to build its residential mortgage planning operations, while expanding the practice to better serve residential real estate investors. He attributes his success to just plain hard work and a tremendously supportive family.

We work with state of the art software and applications and have many different lenders that allow us to offer you no point loans, interest only loans, stated income loans, and loans with no junk fees. We will insure that your mortgage refinance or purchase loan is the best fit for your situation, all things considered.

Ask us for your free, no obligation Total Cost Analysis of your next loan.

We will share the information with you that most mortgage companies and loan officers either don't take the time to share with you or just don't have access to. We have spent thousands of dollars on cutting edge software and systems to show you the important details about your home loan.
