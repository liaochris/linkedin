Susan Davis’s Summary

I like to refer to myself as being in the "SHIPPING" business.... that is, I focus on Relationshipping that leads to Friendshipping that leads to Partnershipping and then bringing Leadershipping to the Partnership!

Although my father often described me as a "Jackie" of all trades but master of none when he was alive, I believe that through all of my many endeavors in life they have all centered around one thing..... service. I have always taken much pride in serving people and making people feel good.

Whether it was by serving a quality meal, great customer service, finding families the perfect loan to buy their dream home, leading a team of people or creating a marketing campaign.

Over the past 10 years I have devoted much of my time to learning about how and why people act the way they act, what drives their buying decisions, how to deliver superior Customer Service, and how to develop your current customer base into a massive referral bank.

I have many different tools in my belt that can help you grow any kind of business, relationship or network in a win-win situation for all involved.

My goal is to make a positive impact in the lives of everyone I come in contact with. To always bring something of worth to the table and to challenge people to think outside of the box.

One of my favorite sayings is this....

"People won't remember what you do or what you say, but they will remember how you made them feel!"

One way I do this is by sending out a heartfelt greeting card every day to someone.

I challenge you to find the opportunity to make someones day today by doing some Random Act of Kindness.

Live life LOUD..... and play full out.

Susan
