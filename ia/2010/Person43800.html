Courtney Curran’s Summary

Courtney Curran has been involved in the field of public relations since the fall of 2006 when she began to study Communication at Michigan State University. Courtney is an active member in the Public Relations Student Society of America (PRSSA) which has allowed her to garner experience in media relations, event planning, creating communication plans, press releases, building client relations, leading teams of students and more.

Courtney is currently on the Executive Board as the Agency Director (2008-2010) for the Michigan State University PRSSA chapter's student communication agency Hubbell Connections. Courtney co-founded the agency and has built the MSU PRSSA chapter's agency from the ground up. Courtney manages the Hubbell Connections' Leadership Board, student account coordinators and clients.

Courtney's professional goals are to continue learning more about the industry of public relations and strengthen her writing and technical skills. She is interested in nonprofits, health care, tourism, community relations and has an invested interest in the future of Detroit and the state of Michigan.
