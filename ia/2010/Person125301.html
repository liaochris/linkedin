Linda Sherman’s Summary

With a strong understanding of corporate marketing and finance combined with social media expertise, I am able to advise companies on how to take advantage of low cost marketing techniques.

Professional Skills
I have an unusual breadth of professional skills including management, strategic planning, marketing, sales, HR, new product introduction, new product development, finance, logistics and operations. I have a proven ability to quickly get up to speed on new industries.

Marketing
I was the acting Marketing Director at both Club Med and Barilla. At Coors, I directed the highly successful launch of ZIMA, using unique pricing and distribution strategies coordinated with an “illusion of discovery” campaign that was one of the first guerilla marketing programs of its kind.

Through the work of my company, the Courage Group, I have an intimate knowledge of professional photography, graphic print and digital film production.

Empowerment of Women
Through public speaking, developing female executives on corporate teams, founding a support group for executive women, and writing a blog called “Its Different For Girls,” I have demonstrated a continuing commitment to supporting women to lead the best lives they can. In addition to providing professional advice, I am an advocate for healthy lifestyle for women of all ages.

Relationships Industry
While working with Singelringen and Singular, I have developed an extensive network of authors who write about rich single lives as well as successful relationships. I was single for 20 years from age 23 to 43 and greatly enjoy empowering single adults to enjoy their lives.

Public Speaking
I am an accomplished public speaker with extensive experience both hosting and participating in speaker programs.

Language
I am fluent in Japanese and a proficient language learner with a solid base in French, Italian and Spanish.
