Brian Suter’s Summary

Although I am still a junior, I have the ability to develop fully integrated campaigns that span traditional and non-traditional forms of media. I understand that in today's market, a brand needs to interact with the consumer - proving its worth and keeping the brand top of mind. Advertising isn't just a brand making promises anymore, it's forming a relationship with the consumer and building an emotional social following behind the brand. That's where I come in - I take the brand's mission & promise, and translate that into a relevant campaign that fully connects itself to the audience, and positions the brand to further itself for years to come as technology develops.
