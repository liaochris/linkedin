Patrick Rafter’s Summary

Patrick Rafter is a "ValueCaster", a communications evangelist with 25+ years experience in PR, AR, branding, marketing & business development, writing; especially promoting and extending the reach of innovative technologies and the Internet.

Present "at the creation" of many innovations over the past 2 decades, Rafter helped introduce Web-based Commerce/Communities/Events/Services, Peer Publishing & Licensing of User-Generated Content, Social Networks, Digital Media & Publishing, Information-Content & Knowledge Management, Inbound Marketing, E-Learning, Enterprise Software & Hardware, Green/Clean technology and services, Wireless Networks, Mobility and Broadband.

Called a "PR Machine" by The Boston Globe, Patrick has delivered award-winning results, analysts' raves, extensive positive press coverage & enhanced brand presence for B2B/B2C startups, public companies, as well as healthcare, education, non-profit & government institutions.

While Patrick experienced a few "dot-bombs"-- he has a been a key team member in growing companies-taking many of them to IPOs or M&A exits.
