Brian Gosur’s Summary

After thirty five years of service with Ford Motor Company, I decided to call it quits. I wanted to go in a different direction.
I worked with my step son for about two years learning the mortgage industry. He owned Fastrack Financial. It was an awesome experience, but it was short lived. With the housing market collapse, he had to close his doors.
I had been involved with MLM businesses in the past, and I thought that I would take a shot at that again.
I am back in the saddle again running my own MLM business and working more of the marketing side now.
Learning MLM Secrets is the system that I use to market all my businesses and products.
I coach other entrepreneurs with the internet marketing strategies and techniques that I use to generate traffic jams and long lines at my websites. I show them how to market their businesses and products through the vehicle of the internet. 1.5 billion people use the internet every day. We have just scraped the surface of what this vast and wide open market mall called the internet can do.
