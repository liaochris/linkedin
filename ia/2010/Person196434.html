Joan Hitchens’s Experience

  • founder

    Storybooks for Healing

    (Publishing industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Storybooks For Healing (SFH) is a social community, a bereavement writing program and a personal storybook publishing system. Through any one or all three of these pathways, people who have experienced a significant loss use the tools to discover, publish and share -- a process which helps validate life, make meaning of loss and bring purpose from grief.

  • Owner

    Mosaic Tributes

    (Publishing industry)

    June 2004 — January 2010 (5 years 8 months)

    As owner of Mosaic Tributes I helped families preserve and celebrate their important life stories through development and production of custom DVDs and Storybooks. Music DVDs incorporated combined photos, slides, home movies, music, memorabilia, documents, voice-overs and video interviews, usually for milestone events such as weddings, anniversaries, funerals and graduations. Storybooks utilized an online program to combine photos and story in print format.

  • Communications Director

    Washington Restaurant Association

    (Non-Profit; Restaurants industry)

    May 1996 — June 2004 (8 years 2 months)

    Responsible for print and online communications on behalf of the Association to its business and allied members statewide.

    My major contribution was in growing and enlarging the role of the monthly newsletter. During most of my tenure writing, graphic layout, interviews and all phases of newsletter production was completed by me.
