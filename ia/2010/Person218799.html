Laurie Zimmerman’s Summary

Since 1990, Zimmerman Associates, Inc. has been one of the most respected research/recruiting/competitive intelligence companies in not only the technical marketplace, but also in banking, biotech, insurance, retail, consumer, etc. Headquartered in Andover, MA, we have developed a vast network of both candidates and employers, both domestic and international, and enjoy relationships with many of the top companies worldwide. Our associates have many years of experience searching/sourcing candidates- the majority of our associates have been with our firm for over eight years, and all possess incredible networks. We assist companies not only with hiring at a fraction of the cost of using agencies, but we also offer our clients the ability to know what/who is making their competition successful.... and we introduce them to people and ideas that will bring them even more success. I am looking forward to networking with past connections and meeting new people.

I am open to all network invitations- laurie@zimmermanassociates.com
