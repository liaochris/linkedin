Sarah Young’s Summary

I am working to build my new company, Rock Your Block. After going through a fantastic experience at Startup Weekend Twin Cities, the company is quickly coming to life. Rock Your Block is a community for teens to find odd jobs within their neighborhood. I am passionate about working with high school students, and RYB will be designed to address the needs of busy high schoolers to find quick ways to earn some extra cash from people they know and trust in their existing networks.

I am also involved in building marketing and branding strategies for New Boundary Technologies, Taste Success, BizLounge, Fuzion Athletics Inc., and Project Skyway.

I have gained five months of international education and work experience in Hong Kong during an undergraduate semester at the School of Business Management at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Concentration of studies: Marketing, Operations Management, and Chinese.
