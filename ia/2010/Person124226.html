Leslie Montgomery’s Summary

An accomplished professional --- as comfortable in corporate as in an entrepreneurial setting --- who has over twenty years of valuable experience and success in women's footwear, handbags, accessories, and apparel from better to designer markets. Business relationships have been integral to achieving win/win outcomes --- understanding that has been invaluable.
The Focus is now on building unique brands which have contemporary appeal and distribute through direct sales channels.
The challenge is to expand these brands in a difficult and uncertain economy.
The Intrigue is with social media and networking and how to make those venues work in the branding business.
