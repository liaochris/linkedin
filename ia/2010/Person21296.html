Belinda Wasser’s Summary

An operations professional with 25 years of experience, Belinda Wasser brings professionalism, enthusiasm and a systems design orientation to every business situation. Her background includes developing, implementing, improving and managing internal operational systems in accounting, human resources administration, facilities management, IT, sales and marketing.

Belinda has worked with a range of high profile companies, including Digitas and The Center for Quality of Management. She’s also the Founder and President of BlueberryBabies.com, an online retail business targeting the gift market for babies, now entering its ninth year of operation. In addition, she has been quoted in The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Parenting Magazine and other national and local media.

Belinda has a BA from the University of Virginia.
