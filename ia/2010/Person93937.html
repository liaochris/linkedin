Jeffrey Yates’s Summary

As a technologist and systems designer, I emphasize integration. By utilizing a broad skill set spanning computing systems, networking, AV presentation systems, and professional contracting services, I provide clients an analysis as a road map to their emerging technology needs.

Responding to the amazing transformation of computing technologies to Web-enabled mobile devices - and helping clients re-purpose their digital assets and transition their sales and marketing efforts to include Smart Phones, including the iPhone, Blackberry, and Android platforms - Jeffrey is now teaming with Mutual Mobile, and specializing in custom designed downloadable applications specific to a businesses’ own unique sales and marketing needs, as well as sales and support team mobile automation solutions.

Jeffrey Yates’s Specialties:

Utilizing the go anywhere-anytime “in the pocket” nature of smart mobile devices, Jeffrey specializes in helping businesses create and stay in touch with customers 24/7 year round. Something no other delivery method - not Radio, TV, Web or Print - can claim.
