Abel J. Bascom’s Summary

Abel Bascom is an experienced best practices, benchmarking and market research specialist with project management experience in education, technology, energy, financial services, and several other industries. Abel’s diverse range of consulting expertise includes a focus on quantitative data collection and analysis, process improvement, information technology, web-based marketing and communication. Abel leverages his skills and background in Psychological and Brain Sciences, technology, research and analysis, and development and utilization of strategic frameworks to develop unique and valuable solutions for his clients.
