Deborah Mersino’s Summary

Mersino brings nearly 20 years of national marketing experience to her role as principal of Ingeniosus. Marrying her passion for gifted education with her marketing communications background, she consults with gifted and talented communities worldwide. Mersino also serves as the moderator for global #gtchat on Twitter every Friday at noon and 7:00 p.m. (EST).
