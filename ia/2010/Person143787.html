Michael Woolston’s Summary

GOALS & INTERESTS

To help emerging renewable energy and other green technology businesses to grow by leveraging my executive leadership experience in the development and manufacturing of nano-tech and thin film products including semiconductors, photovoltaics and solar control products.

EXPERIENCE

• Developed 25 major high technology products, 10 manufacturing lines, 5 factories and 4 new businesses for 3 global, high-tech, industries.
• Headed Engineering for $95M solar-control and flexible photovoltaic equipment businesses.
• Headed Engineering for $1400M semiconductor equipment business and $300M high volume disk drive businesses.
• Established a product division that enabled a $50M portfolio of environmental solutions products.
• Cut manufacturing costs by 20-40% per product release by championing component standardization and modularity, SPC, lean manufacturing and design for manufacturing.
