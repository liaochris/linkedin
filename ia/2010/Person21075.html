Becky Carroll’s Summary

Becky is the president and founder of Petra Consulting Group, a management consultancy focused on creating WOW customer experiences through customer conversation, social media, and customer experience. Becky’s clients have included several Fortune 100 companies such as Electronic Arts, HP, Fujitsu, and Ford Motor Company as well as start-up companies. Projects have included customer strategy, social media strategy, marketing, customer service, customer experience, as well as customer needs and segmentation research.

Becky is a thought leader in the social media space and authors the blog Customers Rock!, listed among the Top 25 Marketing blogs and also a Top 5 customer service blog. She is active in social media circles and helps her clients figure out whether and how to use social media to engage with their customers. Becky teaches at UC San Diego Extension on Marketing via New Media.

Becky is a radio personality for the nationally syndicated program The Big Biz Show. She also hosts her own radio program, Customers Rock! Radio on wsRadio.com. She is a highly-regarded speaker with international expertise, with speeches ranging from keynotes to workshops to webcasts.

Becky also served as the Director of Social Media for Brickfish, a start-up social media marekting platform. She was responsible for all aspects of social media marketing and advertising.

Previously, Becky was a Senior Consultant with industry-leading Peppers and Rogers Group. During her time at Peppers and Rogers Group, Becky was involved with customer strategy, loyalty metrics, contact center strategy, and high tech planning.

Prior to joining Peppers and Rogers Group, Becky worked at Hewlett Packard for 14 years. Most recently, she had served as Director of Marketing for the UK and Ireland.

Prior to her time in the UK, Becky was the Worldwide Customer Loyalty Marketing Manager for HP Services. Becky also taught technical and marketing courses at HP.

Becky Carroll’s Specialties:

Social media, marketing, customer retention and customer loyalty, customer experience, service and support strategy in high technology, information technology, entertainment industry, gaming industry, automotive industry, contact centers.
