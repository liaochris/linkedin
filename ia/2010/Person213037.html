Michael Brette,J.D.’s Summary

VentureNet Capital Group,Inc provides structured financing for public companies with PIPE's, non-recourse stock loans and non-toxic Equity Lines of Credit. The Company prepares all private placement offering documents to allow companies to legally raise capital from accredited investors in compliance with State and Federal security laws. In addition, we arrange presentations with investors qualified to make an investment.

Michael N. Brette,J.D, President/CEO of VentureNet Capital Group,Inc. has published two books, Asset Protection Planning and Raising Capital For Your Business (Amazon.com). Mr. Brette is the host of business talk radio show, Wall Street News Hour and co-produces a national financial television show, Let's Talk Stock. He has appeared on more than 200 television and radio shows discussing various business and financial topics. Mr. Brette has a Juris Doctor (Law Degree) for 31 years and is listed in Who's Who in the World and Who's Who in Industry & Finance. Michael N, Brette,J.D. is a Member of the American Bar Association Business Law Section.

VentureNet Capital Group,Inc. advises clients on a wide variety of transactional business matters from start-up counseling, venture capital, reverse mergers, licensing agreements, capital formation, raising capital for public companies, joint ventures, hedge funds, private equity, corporate formations, public offerings and monetizing intellectual property.


The Company has invested millions of dollars in emerging growth companies and assisted in raising millions more.

VentureNet Capital Group,Inc. assist publicly traded companies with increasing shareholder value and adding liquidity to their stock. The Company prepares and distributes corporate information to 85,000 financial advisors who reach millions of self-direct individual investors with online brokerage account.
