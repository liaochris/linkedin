Craig Fisher’s Summary

The selling business is all about results; it's about breaking down barriers and at times making something happen out of nothing. After 16 years in the selling business this has become more than just a job criterion for Craig Fisher (The Sales Expert), they are the rules he lives his life by. It's not surprising then that at the age of 19 and working as a sales executive for a fashion company he turned over sales figures of around £1 million in his first year! Achievable when you possess a no-nonsense approach to getting results.
