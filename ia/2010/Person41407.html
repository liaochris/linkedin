Clay Moseley’s Summary

I work as a Sales Associate at Blue Sky Factory, an email service provider headquartered in Baltimore, MD. We differentiate ourselves from other ESPs in that we have an incredibly robust platform, and we couple that with the personalized service that you would get at your local Mom & Pop.

Our proprietary software Publicaster, gives our clients the ability to effective manage and segment a large number of lists, analyze in-depth reports, and integrate their social media campaigns.

We work with clients ranging from local restaurants to Fortune 500 and 100 companies. Clients of all sizes in all industries are excited about our product, and are sold on our service.

Before joining Blue Sky Factory, I worked as a Membership and Marketing Associate at APSP where I helped design and run a new internet sales and marketing program.

Prior to starting at APSP, I was a Sales and Marketing Assistant at EFX Media, a video-production company in Arlington, VA. Here I helped to support the sales team, and worked with the executives on general brand management and website layout.

My biggest test (and accomplishment) to date was when I launched an internet-based startup after graduating from college. My primary roles included selling the service and advertising space, developing the business plan, formulating proposals, and building relationships with prospective clients. I was successful in marketing and selling the service, and we launched our website with the most popular establishment in Wilmington, NC.

Unfortunately, the downturn in the economy coincided with our start-up and we were forced to dissolve the company in November of last year. Nonetheless, the experience was PRICELESS.
