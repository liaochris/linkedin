Misae Richwoods’s Summary

I specialise in helping people or companies get to market principally through effective use of internet technologies. A large part of this expertise is based on my ongoing knowledge from running portfolio of ecommerce sites, online publishing and social network assets.

I've provided marketing and/or web sites for a range of well known companies including Dell, BP, Tetra Pak, Ford, Virgin Active, Rockwell Automation, Glaxo Smith Kline, Nestle, Coca-cola, The National Lottery, Boots and Halfords.

I'm currently helping start ups, SMEs and independant professionals through MRMedia Ltd whilst continuing to grow my retail and publishing portfolio.

I'm assistant organiser for the #140conf London meetup where we look at how real time media is changing the rules of business, society and relationships.
