Surbhi Sukhija’s Summary

I am currently working in the capacity of Dy Manager (HR) with Velocis Systems Pvt. Ltd., a one-stop solution provider for IT solutions in India with an ever-growing national footprint. Velocis is uniquely positioned to deliver end-to-end IT solutions, bringing a broader and deeper state-of-the-art range of products, solutions and services to customers.

I have previously been associated with Quint Wellington Redwood India Consulting Pvt. Ltd., a global and independent Management Consultancy specialising in IT-related organisational challenges, in the capacity of Business Development Consultant. I have also been actively involved with M/s Parag International, a family enterprise who are renowned exporters of textile to Europe and the Middle East, in the Administration and Marketing domains. I possess rich experience as an HR professional with JK Paper Ltd., a prominent industrial house, in the capacity of Assistant Manager (HR) for two years.

As regards my academic profile, I am an MBA in Human Resources from the 2004-06 batch at International Management Institute (IMI), New Delhi. Prior to this, I have earned a Bachelor degree in Information Technology from Manipal Academy of Higher Education, Manipal University. My schooling was completed in the year 2000 from Apeejay School, Noida.

As evident from my varied work experience, I am fortunate to have had diverse experience across management profiles and functions in a variety of industry scenarios. My skill set ranges from Group-level and Company-level HR to Marketing and Business Development at a multinational consultancy. I also possess proficiency in Family Business Management in the Administrative and Marketing functions.

Being a committed employee, I wish to contribute towards the growth of the organisation based on my competence, and to further my personal capabilities by learning from my exposure to the organisation.
