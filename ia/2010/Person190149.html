Heather Hamilton-Sims’s Summary

A native Houstonian, planning social events for friends and family was a hobby that she later turned into a career as a corporate event planner. Her previous experience includes sales and marketing positions at Sprint where she planned, designed and executed a myriad of corporate events, meetings and parties for employees and customers in seven (7) states.

Earlier this year, Heather re-named her company and online presence Simply Jubilee Events. Although the company name changed, her commitment to providing exceptional service and extraordinary events to clients has remained her primary goal. Her contagious enthusiasm and energy ensures a positive experience for clients, guests and staff alike.

With Simply Jubilee, Heather continues on the path of planning corporate and social events – marrying her creative talents, organizational and management skills and love of a great party. She believes in building solid relationships filled with mutual respect and trust. Her clients include Chevron, Ikon and E.H. Watts, to name a few. She also designs and plans spectacular weddings and other social events for private clients.

Heather’s attention to detail, unbridled passion, professionalism and customer-focused service has earned her a reputation as a stellar corporate and social event planner and the go-to person for spectacular events in Texas, Louisiana’s Gulf Coast and Georgia.

Heather is an active member of The American Academy of Wedding Professionals, The Association of Bridal Consultants and has recently been honored as Vice President of Communications for The International Special Events Society.

Heather has been quoted in “The Knot” and has received rave reviews from her clients, site vendors and guests of her events.

Contact Heather at www.simplyjubilee.com to discuss your event needs.
