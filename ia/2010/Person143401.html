Michael Peterson (michael at rightseed.com)’s Summary

RightSeed Solutions builds revenue-driven software sales teams. We run our business on one principal belief, executive search is about relationships. We build our network, and our reputation, one person at a time, every time. Representing the industry’s best talent in the areas of sales, sales engineering, and business development, up to the Vice President level, has been in the lifeblood of the company ever since I began serving the software sales community in mid 1998. Numerous different organizations, from up and-coming start-ups to multi-national software market leaders rely on RightSeed Solutions to find them quality talent in a timely manner.

The RightSeed network extends across the US, with core markets in the Pacific Northwest, Silicon Valley, Southern California, Denver, Texas, Chicago, New England, NY/NJ/PA, Atlanta/SE US, and Washington DC.

Our areas of specialty include:

* Online Marketing (Marketing Automation, Landing Page Optimization, Web Analytics, Email Marketing, etc.)
* Social Media (Enterprise Feedback Management, Online Communities, Social CRM, etc.)
* E-commerce (Applications and Infrastructure)
* Enterprise Content Management
* Business Intelligence and Analytic Applications
* Business Process Management
* CRM (Marketing Automation, SFA, Web Self Service/eCRM)
* Knowledge Management (Search, e-Learning)
* Integration (EAI, ETL, SOA, Web Services)
* ERP
* Emerging Software Segments

Whether you’re running a sales team and looking to increase your revenues or a top software sales professional seeking an agent to represent your best interests, I'd like to hear from you.
