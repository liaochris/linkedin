Daniel Crompton’s Summary

Venture Expertise, Application Developer, Database Administrator, Consultant, Project Manager and ING Group Greenfield Award winner. Focused on a wide variety of Internet, WAN and local network (LAN) business applications and User Peripherals.
Particularly interested in client/server and relational database design using Oracle, Directory Server (LDAP), Grid, and mySQL Server; this includes Unified Messaging, Identity & Access Management (I&AM), Security Information and Event Management (SIEM), Enterprise Service Bus (ESB) and Integration projects.

And I am always interested in migration projects, as well as close interaction with DB manufacturers.

I work with development projects for software and hardware, computer networking and network security consulting, technical writing, interface design and usability, market research and outsourcing.

As well as that I do consulting on information technology for low-tech or high-tech companies in the fields of change management, corporate training, project management, program management, corporate compliance and corporate governance; such as PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard); SOX (Sarbanes Oxley); Basel II; ISO/IEC 27001
