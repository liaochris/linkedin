John Ashcroft’s Summary

Visiting Professor at Manchester Metropolitan Business School specialising in Economics and Business Strategy.

Extensive experience of SME and large businesses in the UK, Europe, USA and Australia. Working in family owned, institutionally owned and public companies.

Experience of over fifty corporate finance transactions including $1 billion acquisition in the USA in the late 1980's working with First Boston, Bain and Company, Samuel Montagu, Goldman Sachs and Wasserstein Perella.
