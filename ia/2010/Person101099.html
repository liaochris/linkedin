Joe Plumley’s Summary

I specialize in training design, in both on-site and online formats, executive coaching, project, organizational and business alignment organizational change and research services that are tailored to your business, your challenges, and your people.
In addition, I am very experienced in higher education administration and am qualified to assist you in your efforts to become regionally accredited, maintain accreditation and develop an institutional effectiveness program.

I am available to conduct training that I have designed and your existing training in a face-to-face environment, an asynchronous environment or in a totally online environment.
My goal is to get you implementing, not just planning. My work is grounded in real business and educational issues to produce tangible, bottom line outcomes. I use the context of your organization to develop the strategies and behaviors necessary for success in today's market driven climate.

I am also very experienced and qualified to conduct research to measure the impact of training and awareness of issues both internal and external to your organization.
