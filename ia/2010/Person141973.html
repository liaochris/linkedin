Michael Caruso MichaelAngeloCaruso@gmail.com’s Summary

Michael Angelo Caruso is the owner and founder of Edison House, a Michigan-based communications consulting company.

He is author of the 5 Cool Ideas books, the FastLearnerAudio series and over 75 other information products. More information on his books and audio programs is available at www.MichaelAngeloCaruso.com.

Michael delivers 75 presentations and keynote speeches a year. His clients include Hallmark, The Barbados Ministry of Tourism, Rayovac, Bank of America and the United States Navy.

Mr. Caruso is a lifelong member of Rotary and the Optimists.
