John Wentworth’s Summary

Wentworth has integrated

- recruiting research
- recruitment
- executive recruiting
- workforce planning consulting
- employment process consulting

into a comprehensive services, carefully tailored to the exact needs of our clients. We deliver no less or no more than what is best for those who trust us to provide thoughtful, data-driven and effective solutions to their workforce management challenges.

We have been trailblazers since 1984 when we were the first company to offer on-site contract recruiting to Southern California. We now combine attraction, recruiting, retention, on-boarding and performance management into an effective (98% success, 96% on time) talent acquisition and workforce management service.

We support our work with predictive analysis, process control and other business tools that usually do not find their way into talent acquisition and management.

We deliver solutions effectively nuanced to our clients' needs. Many clients tell us that they had only dreamed of the level of nuance and satisfaction we delivered, previously not believing that it was actually possible.
