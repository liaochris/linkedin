  • VP Marketing and Strategic Partnerships

    SparkSeed

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    May 2007 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Sparkseed is a nonprofit organization that invests in the nation's top student social entrepreneurs.

    Each year Sparkseed selects a new class of campus innovators, and works with them over the next 6 months to develop a strong social enterprise. Students admitted into the program receive six months of high-impact support from Sparkseed:

    * up to $11,000 in seed money towards start-up costs
    * Mentoring from entrepreneurs and executives
    * Pro-bono consulting from business and nonprofit professionals
    * Webinars that teach skills such as creating a business plan, managing a team, pitching to investors, leveraging social media, and measuring financial and social impact.
    * $10,000 in web tools, including web site design, web hosting, & SEO consulting.
    * A four-day summit in Silicon Valley that includes networking, skill-building, and strategic planning.

    Sparkseed is highly-selective, electing to work only with ventures that have a solid business model, clear ROI, and potential to scale. Through intensive skill-building workshops, infrastructure development, and brand strategizing, Sparkseed ensures that student social entrepreneurs will acquire the tools they need to be effective leaders of tomorrow.

    www.sparkseed.org
