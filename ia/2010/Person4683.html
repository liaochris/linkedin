Akansha Khanna’s Summary

Akansha began her foray into films & events as an intern with ‘The Times of India’,
This experience helped her to create a show for Sahara Samay in which she interviewed leading politicians
She later found the challenges of advertising tough to resist & went on to join Lowe Lintas, here she worked in the client servicing department on leading brands like Woodland, Idea Cellular & HFCL Connect.
As a production manager, Akansha worked on commercials & music videos with Intearctive Television.Producing was not far behind & Akansha began to produce commercials
Akansha was in the in the middle of activity & experimented with short films. She directed a 30 minute docu-drama on Journalism called “Khachaak” & a 5 minute short film titled ‘When the lights come off’. Both the films were nominated at the “First Frame’ film festival, in Delhi.
Having tasted initial success, Akansha soon joined Sony Entertainment Television in the department in-charge of programming. There she worked on huge television shows like Indian Idol, Miss India, Filmfare, Gladrags and CRY Telethon.
Longing for international exposure, she joined Fremantle Media where she worked as projects coordinator. She longed for greater challenges and joined in with a leading production house, Optimystix, where she head the advertising films division.
Akansha co-founded two companies -one with well known cinematographer Faroukh Mistry called Black Motion Pictures & an events & creative communication solution company called "eQ" with her partner Vikrant Nath.
Her love for films kept growing stronger & thats when she decided to join Vishal Bhardwaj. Post that she worked with InDepth Entertaining Arts as a Creative Producer. And now she is currently working at Geon Studios which is co-founded by Academy Award Winning Producer (Lord of the Rings & matrix Fame) Barrie Osborne, Seemanto Roy (Sahara Pariwar), Michael Peyser, Jon Labrie & Madhu Sudhanan.
