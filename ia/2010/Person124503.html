Lewis Goldman’s Summary

CMO and GM specializing in identifying tipping points in technology driven trends (Cable narrowcasting, Internet, Social Media, Mobile) to drive organizational and strategic change which accelerates revenue growth. Strong marketing, business development, cross-sell, new product, and E-commerce skills. Best in class brand and direct marketer who has built several successful online and offline businesses through integrated marketing and business development activities.

Key accomplishments include

- Redefining and relaunching the 1800Flowers.com brand and gaining market share while launching companion 1800Baskets.com business

- Establishing CNBC as the pre-eminent business channel brand

- Getting Citigroup "on the 'Net" while generating $1 billion in annual revenue from over 250,000 new accounts through Internet and direct response marketing/cross-sell programs

- Building out or reorganizing several high performing teams

Hands on executive with particular strength in leading organizational change, developing a differentiated brand positioning, creating and exploiting new market opportunities, segmentation targeting, and quickly delivering bottom line results from business alliances and new distribution channels.

Advisory Board, Collider Media (Mobile company), Guest Lecturer, Columbia Business School, Hofstra University, Author, "Innovation in Financial Services, Myth or Reality", Journal of Financial Advertising and Marketing, Volume 5, Issue 2. Frequent speaker - Internet and CMO conferences, Quoted in The New York Times, Ad Age, Ad Week, Internet Retailer, DM News.

Lewis Goldman’s Specialties:

Brand and Direct Response marketing, Internet Marketing, Social Networking, Mobile, Search, Contract negotiations, Agency management, Sponsorships, Business Development, Building organizations.
