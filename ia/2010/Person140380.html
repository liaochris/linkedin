Melanie Cohn’s Summary

I have extensive experience in the areas of public policy, government and non-profit management. I have been responsible for outreach and community relations in varying areas of State policy, including health care, biotechnology, social services, and seniors and children. During the past five years I have forged strong relationships with local representatives from government, non-profit organizations and businesses throughout San Diego.

In a management capacity, I was formerly in charge of overseeing up to five interns at any given time; managed intern recruitment, hiring, project assignment and overall supervision.

I have also filled the role of Acting District Director at the State Assembly. My duties included overseeing the Assembly Member's schedule and time management, reporting to the Fair Political Practices Commission, office management, intern coordination, and managing the public email account.

I worked on special projects at Price Charities, acting as a grant officer for organizations that apply for funding, providing support to these organizations during the granting process, and performing community outreach. I assisted in increasing awareness about Price Charities' granting opportunities and maintaining communication between Price Charities and the residents and organizations in City Heights.

Currently, I am a Manager at the San Diego County Childhood Obesity Initiative. I work on many different projects that are focused on changing policy and environment to reduce and prevent childhood obesity in San Diego County by creating healthy environments for all children and families through advocacy, education, policy development, and environmental change.
