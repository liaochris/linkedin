  • VP Sales and Marketing

    RateVoice.com

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2010 — Present (5 months)

    RateVoice is both a SaaS and licensed application. It was developed in 2009 out of eCommerce necessity. This Social Review Marketing Engine easily integrates across all eCommerce platforms. RateVoice offers SMB & mid market merchants social product interaction capabilities that were, until now, only available to Big Box eTailers. Far from a passive review module, RateVoice is active generator of reviews via post transaction consumer solicitation. After a written review has past the eTailer custom moderation filtering, it posts directly to the product page. Once posted, RateVoice encourages a return visit to the product page, where the reviewer can recover their ( and now your) content for syndication onto the available Social Networks. Ultimately, this process guides socially encouraged followers to the reviewed products pages. This (socially generated) presence pre qualifies their interest in a product. The result is higher sales and greater product & brand loyalty. Additionally, the increased traffic on the product page heightens search engine rankings. For the eTailer, it's an easy win, win, win. RateVoice also offers Hosts & Shopping Carts the ability to provide a feature set that increases sales on their subscribers eCommerce sites. These Value Added Service act as a acquisition retention tool. The Rate Voice program gives power and control of product reviews to the eTailers with a lows barrier of entry in the market place.
