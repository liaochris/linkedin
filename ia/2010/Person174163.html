Robert Nitschke’s Summary

Robert Nitschke, author of Creating a COLLABORATIVE ENTERPRISE. Creating a COLLABORATIVE ENTERPRISE is not a book of concepts; it is a compelling view of how to bring greater success in reach of any organization. It is written in a way that informs, instructs, guides, and encourages; all with insight, honesty, practicality and simplicity. The challenges and solutions Robert Nitschke provides will speak to every leader and employee that yearns for their organization to defy “traditional methods” and walk their own path to greater success.

Robert is also Managing Partner of Arago Partners, who works with all size organizations to provide Strategic, Tactical and Execution support with our wide range of Operating and Performance expertise. We help with out-of-control costs, late projects, shrinking sales, poor customer service, ineffective internal processes and dysfunctional teams.

They offer strong operational background in multiple industries including International Project Finance, Telecommunications, Commercial Internet Services, Enterprise Software Development, Hospitality, and Manufacturing (Automotive/Telecommunications).

Functional experiences include multiple levels of Executive/Managment positions, Program/Project Managment (Certified Professional Project Manager), Certified in Lean Operations Process Management, Information Technology Managment/Services, Manufacturing, Business Planning (Strategic/Tactical), Private Equity Financing, Sales/Marketing, Financial Managment/Budgeting, Process Improvement and Quality Control, Human Resources, Customer Care and Corporate Governance.
