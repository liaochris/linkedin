Moisés Piedra’s Summary

Dynamic, bilingual Sr. Marketing Professional with 15 years of proven experience, building and executing marketing strategies and programs for B2B, B2C, general market and also multicultural (Hispanic) consumer segments. Marketing strategies include customer acquisition, retention, loyalty building, cross selling and product positioning.

Industry and company experience includes financial (Bank of America, Citigroup, MBNA, Chase, and Visa), retail (Walmart, Starbucks, and Maseca), utilities (Reliant and Clearwire), and Insurance (Geico and Safeco).

Through the brand diversity and business case experiences that my background includes, I am able to employ a broader perspective on business marketing, problem solving and, most importantly, a wider range of business solutions.

Actively searching for a direct marketing position and/or consulting project that can directly benefit from my professional background in direct. Additionally, due to my most recent experience in the U.S. Hispanic market, the overwhelming growth of the U.S. Hispanic population, their spending power and, most importantly, the business case opportunity, I welcome any permanent employment or consulting project that could also benefit from my multicultural marketing background.
