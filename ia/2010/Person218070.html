Yvan Goudard’s Summary

● SHORT SUMMARY:
Yvan is a creative in advertising with extensive experience around the globe.

● LONG SUMMARY:
Yvan is an accomplished creative professional with over eighteen years experience in the industry, from Tahiti to Afghanistan, as well as North Africa, Europe and the Middle East. He is responsible for delivering creative TTL campaigns, and for being a key instrument in winning pitches.
His agency roster includes; Saatchi & Saatchi, JWT, TBWA, McCann, Draft FCB, Ogilvy amongst others. He worked on international brands including Danone, Tunisiana, Orascom, Etihad Airways, Saudi Airlines, Roshan, Sony, Ford, Samsung, Arla foods, Ariel, Meditel, Pantene and many others.
