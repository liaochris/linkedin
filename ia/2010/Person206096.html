Tom Humbarger’s Summary

I am an innovative social media, marketing strategy and community expert.

In addition, I was recently selected by 451 Marketing as one of their social media strategists to watch in 2010.

My recent career highlights include:
• Wrote requirements, developed community strategy and completed a beta launch for a start-up professional community
• Relaunched and wrote all marketing content for a corporate website
• Developed social media strategies for a major client of a media advertising company
• Created and grew a niche B2B community from 0 to 4,000 members in 15 months while generating and sourcing the majority of the community content
• Developed marketing programs to drive 75,000 community visits and 320,000 pageviews
• Wrote more than 250 blog posts since March 2007
• Managed successful B2B user-generated content contest that generated 44 entries and more than 25,000 total hits on YouTube

Core Competencies
Besides my core skills of social media, marketing and community, community, I also have extensive experience in evangelism, product strategy and product marketing for enterprise software in companies ranging in size from start-ups to Oracle.

My recent work has been project-based and most of the work has required me to work with cross-functional teams. I became the “go-to” super project guy when my company needed to get something done especially in areas where there was no roadmap or guidelines and a creative solution was needed.

In addition, I am a broad and inquisitive generalist, a quick learner with excellent writing and presentation skills, superb analytical and research acumen, and the ability to distill complex results into key insights.
