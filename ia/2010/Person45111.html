Chandu Thota’s Summary

Chandu Thota has over 12 years of software development experience and extensive Enterprise and Web Services development expertise. He is one of the most accomplished names in the web and geo-spatial industries.

He is a Founder, CTO and VP of Prodct Development of Center'd Corp (http://www.centerd.com, formerly known as fatdoor.com), a local planning and local search company.

Previously he was at Microsoft Corporation as a Program Manager in the ASP .NET group working on the next generation web platform (Silverlight & Tools). Prior to that, Chandu Thota worked as a Development Lead for Microsoft Local Search and Mapping product: Virtual Earth (http://local.live.com) where he shipped core product experience as well as the client-side platforms.

Chandu Thota has filed over 18 patents in the web and mapping technology areas. He is a published author and a well known speaker in developer communities. He is also the founder of the online 'blogmapping' community at http://www.feedmap.net.
