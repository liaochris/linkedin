Asif Khan’s Summary

A proud Pakistani, 29+ years old and born and raised in the Dubai, UAE where all my schooling and life experience began, I moved to Karachi, Pakistan in 1996 where I completed my O & A Levels.

After successfully completing my Bachelors in Computer Science (Hons), I leapt into the competitive world of advertising. For almost 2 years, I have served a valuable position in the advertising industry, working on multinational and local accounts. Moved to Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia in 2008 and in the midst of completing my Master of Arts in Communication Management, I was working as an Account Executive at Asia Media Monitors.

I am presently working as an Internet Monitoring Service Manager at Asia Media Monitors, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Managing a mixed technical/non-technical and multi-lingual team responsible for Media Monitors group wide Internet Monitoring service ensuring it is providing outstanding customer service, proactive service management and leading coverage of internet sources relevant to our customer’s base.

As a Communications professional I have over four years of experience in Central and South East Asia in account management and in managing client’s strategic business activities on traditional & social media. I am passionate about social media, web analytics, media measurement and blogging.
