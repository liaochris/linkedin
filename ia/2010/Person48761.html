Daria Sukman’s Summary

My passion is combining all aspects of digital design (print/motion/video/web design) and art (illustration, painting, photography) into the realm of social media to create engaging and stimulating connections.

I am hooked on how all this comes together with analytics, content, social media and continuously changing dynamics of the internet. It is imperative to me to use the tools and genuinely participate with full creative authenticity. My experience in print and broadcast graphics have provided a solid base to reemerge to the industry and relish dynamic web content and mixed media.

This arts based background also extends into my more altruistic intentions of connecting creativity and community as an Expressive Arts Therapist and Facilitator.

My goal is to secure a new contract or position with a company who welcomes my strong writing, research, organizational and marketing skills while expanding into emerging, online creative trends.
