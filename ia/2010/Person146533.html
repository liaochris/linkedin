Mim McConnell’s Summary

I’ve been building and maintaining websites since 1998 and have many satisfied customers in Sitka, Alaska. I have an excellent reputation for professional work, prompt responses to client requests for website changes, and reasonable rates.

I am also the owner of a well-established visitors guide for Sitka and its website, Sitka Through Four Seasons and www.travelsitka.com.

My goals are to continue to build my web and graphic design business in Sitka and in the surrounding region, and to continue to grow and improve on the visitors guide.

Mim McConnell’s Specialties:

I was employed for five years as a business counselor for a local non-profit that served local small entrepreneurs. Knowledge gained through helping small businesses is used in all the work I do with my clients, whether it's building them a website, creating brochures and business cards, or encouraging them to create a marketing plan.
