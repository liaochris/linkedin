Nick Anderson’s Summary

The focus throughout my career has been to illuminate the links between behavior change and improved performance. After leading behavioral research and sales productivity projects, the last 15 years has been developing a deepening understanding of what it takes to align people for change. I am passionate about impacting bottom-line results through changing behavior and increasing their competitive advantage.

Over the last 10 years I led the PDS Design Team to develop and mature AlEx™ – Aligning Expectations. This journey started with diagnosing misalignments and distractions to a given change initiative. Now, AlEx™ has developed into a management system which progressively aligns and focuses leadership and management teams to develop a climate where accountability and coaching is the norm.

My abilities range from skilled observer and behavioral analyst through being a presenter to large groups to facilitating management and leadership teams in difficult and often tense situations.

Objectives
• To develop a licensee network based on AlEx - Aligning Expectations software and consulting model in North America, Europe and Middle East.
• To work collaboratively with licensees by coaching, advising and running the "back room"
• To collect data on successful and failed change by tracking the degree of misalignment and what was done to create better alignment
