Benjamin Rudnick’s Summary

I was born and raised in North Miami Beach, Florida and after high school relocated 200 miles north to Orlando to attend the University of Central Florida. My undergraduate degree was in Finance and I later returned to do the M.B.A. thing. I had some amazing professors who led me down a road of Entrepreneurship and Innovation. The plan is to one day return to the academic environment and pursue a PhD.

The past 5 years have taken me on an entrepreneurial adventure through Internet Marketing,Web Development, Project Management and the world of Tech Start-Ups (and their counterpart…VCs). The Web has become my passion, and most engaging topic of study. Now that I think if it, i don’t know where else I could have gained the knowledge that i did by embracing the Internet and connecting with its inhabitants. This journey has also introduced me to an incredulous industry filled with some extremely clever and resourceful people!
