Jo Dillamore’s Summary

I am your orginal legal alien. I have worked for the past decade yet only the first 2 years of that was in my home country.

I finished my law degree in England and then spent a couple of years working for a software house in Godalming, Surrey which set the tone for the rest of my career to date.

I then moved to Australia and as most of us recruiters do, fell into the profession. Its a cut throat business but somebody has to do it, and I've met some fantastic people as a result.

Whilst agency recruitment has its thrills (and spills), I am just a little bit rubbish at the sales part. But I love the relationship building and making a difference. So I crossed to the dark side and....saw the light. I learnt a huge amount working within some bigger corporates and am now enjoying applying that experience and developing the HR function for Community Engine.

The big motivator for me is creating an environment, a team and a place to come to every day, that inspires people to be passionate about what they do and dedicated to the cause. Efficiency, effectiveness, viability and value, drive a business and its performance. Its a little bit tragic but I enjoy being able to add value to make it work in its ultimate performing state.

Community Engine is now looking for some serious movers and shakers in the Online and Social Media space.

Most specifically I am looking for a .Net Technical Lead with experience customising Kentico or other Web CMS.

A brilliant Digital BDM and an Account Director to compliment.

A guru in the Front End/UX Development space would complete the team...for now!

Email me at joanna.dillamore@communityengine.com
