Giacomo Bertaina’s Summary

Multi-discipline Digital Marketing Executive
US Hispanic, Latin America, and International Markets

I have a deep understanding and passion for digital marketing but I'm a business problem solver at my core. I tend to look at problems not from an advertising perspective only, but from a marketing and business-wide perspective. I love challenges that demand intellect and opportunities that require curiosity. I'm passionate about challenging the way things are, inspiring a shared vision of better possibilities, and mobilizing the means to affect needed change. Intellectual curiosity, ability to be both strategic and tactical, a passion for solving complex problems, combined with strong analytical skills and innovative thinking are my core strengths.

I'm a "deep generalist" with broad business expertise and deep marketing specialized skills. This is the age of the specialized generalist. I believe being a generalist is far more powerful than being a expert when operating at the edges, intersections, and overlaps where innovation thrives. I'm capable of rapidly developing insights, specialist skills, and knowledge in a minimum of time. I work at my best as higher levels of complexity are added to business problems.

I have 15 years of combined experience in interactive, direct, brand, product and trade marketing, and business development in consumer goods (beverage & tobacco), durable goods (HVAC, home furniture and accessories), wholesale distribution, imports & exports, commercial printing, publishing, financial services, automotive and advertising. Key strengths in marketing strategy, B2C and B2B integrated marketing in Fortune Global 500 and entrepreneurial environments in the USA, US Hispanic market, and Latin America (Peru, Colombia, Ecuador, Venezuela, Mexico, Panama, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Guatemala, Costa Rica) . Bilingual: English / Spanish. BA in Economics. MBA, INCAE Business School. Master degree, IE Business School.
