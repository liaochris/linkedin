Paul Bicknell’s Summary

Once upon a time, the IT department was overhead, a necessary evil, simply a cost center. In today's business environment, IT is required to provide business value and is expected to speak the language of business. My expertise is in speaking both languages; the language of IT and the language of business. I have been successfully bridging this gap for over 15 years in both technical and business roles. I am an expert in IT Operations, Outsourcing, and SaaS, while being experienced in RFI/RFP/Pursuit/Costing, Enterprise Architecture, and Virtualization. I am also a published author.
