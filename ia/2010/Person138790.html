Mayank Dhingra’s Summary

I started my career with Fidelity International as a campus recruit from Delhi College of Engineering. After working there as a programmer for a year and a half I joined the Startup Bandwagon and started experimenting with Social Media.

Worked for slideshare.net at their Delhi office for a year in the capacity of a software developer and contributed to various aspects from analytics to marketing new features online.


After moving from slideshare I joined another startup "Mpower Mobile" which works with mobile payments. Spent a year working on their payment backend. Alongside I also started my own venture kwippy.com with two colleagues from slideshare. Kwippy is India's first conversational/microblogging platform. It was voted as one of the top 25 hottest web startups from India by Dataquest magazine.

I am a passionate blogger and write about business, marketing and social media at http://mayank.name and help individuals, organizations use Social Media to be more effective.

I am also a regular at startup and marketing related events like barcamp, proto.in, digital marketing meetups etc.
