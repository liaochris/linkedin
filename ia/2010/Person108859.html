Jun Huang’s Summary

Jun has nineteen years of strategic and tactical experience from the industries of health care, financial services, e-commerce (B2B and B2C), telecommunications and education following his PhD program from the University of Manchester, England. Over the last few years he has been instrumental in linking strategic technology decisions with business processes and business drivers. His approach to architecture and technology involves thinking strategically but acting pragmatically, connecting IT architecture to business and customer demand, collaborating with key business and IT stakeholders, and taking into account other factors such as people, process and organizational culture.

Jun Huang’s Specialties:

Enterprise Architecture/Technology Strategy, Web Architecture, Service-Oriented Architecture, Business Process Architecture, Technology and Vendor Evaluation, Development Methodology (UP, Agile), Governance, Mentoring/Consulting and Technology Research. Jun also has knowledge of and strong interest in mobile computing and cloud computing
