Lori A. Hall’s Summary

During her 25 years of strategic Marketing and Market Development experience in both “blue-chip” organizations (P&G, Coca-Cola, Avery Dennison) and entrepreneurial environments, Lori has consistently, successfully and effectively played the role of “strategic change agent” helper her employers and clients to “ratchet-up” to the next level in building their business or market opportunities. In fact, she has the reputation of “making things happen” and completing the “mission impossible”.

By demonstrating sharp analytic, strategic market planning, communications, organizational, project management, process-engineering and team-building skills. Lori has played a variety of significant roles in launching over 25 products to market in a variety of industries, as well as developed, managed and executed over 500 marketing, PR and promotional programs, from bootstrapping to $25M budget campaigns. For example, she not only led the test-market and national consumer launch of Coca-Cola’s entry into the non-carbonated beverage segment and played a leadership role in doubling a Housewares company's business in a year, but she successfully built and sold an internet company in less than 9 months.
