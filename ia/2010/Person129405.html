Lynn M. Bryant - Channel Sales Talent

Channel Sales Talent is the only staffing solution focused exclusively on identifying, attracting and placing exceptional sales talent for companies that sell through the IT Channel.

Personally, I am committed to the success of sales channels and channel partners. I believe that a strong channel program benefits both the vendor and the channel equally and should be designed to attract the strongest reseller partners available.

Why I am here on LinkedIn: Channel Sales Talent
- To offer CST as a strategic resource to those companies with the desire to build high performance channel sales organizations, and to those individuals who can make it happen.
