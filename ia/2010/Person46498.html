Dan Barton’s Summary

Having lived in 3 different countries over the past 10 years, I have become an expert at creating strong, long term profitable relationships. I have cultivated strong associations with realtors, property managers, fellow investors, bankers, and property owners alike and have been able to acquire properties that not only appreciate in value, but also deliver an excellent return to my investors, my partners, and to myself.

Over time, I became extremely interested in the Real Estate market and saw it as a great opportunity to grow, both personally and professionally. I also realized that many other people were interested in venturing into this market but found it difficult, not knowing where or how to get started. I saw this as an excellent opportunity to help others and immediately started researching and studying Real Estate Investment strategies. I also attended numerous workshops, classes and joined real estate investment groups across the county, which eventually led to the grand opening of Oasis Properties Inc., my own company, in August of 2005.

Today the Oasis Properties team has grown from a solopreneur business to include one partner, Greg Gillespie, and three other employees with future plans for expansion of experts in their team in the works for 2009. Currently Oasis Properties manages over 100 units with a value well over $20 million.

My steady climb to the top in real estate investing resulted to being featured in Don Campbell’s “51 Success Stores of Canadian Real Estate Investors”, and I have received prominent exposure in such publications as Monday Magazine, Douglas Magazine, Business Vancouver Island, and the Times Colonist. I also am the author of 90 Days to Real Estate Prosperity® which helps demystify the process of utilizing certain principles to achieve success in investing in the real estate market.
