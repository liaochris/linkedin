David Alexander’s Summary

I started my journey into business, in 2007 when I began the market research for an import and export idea a client came to me with. We then set out in early 2008 to sell hot tubs and swimming pools on the European market.

We also spent lots of time sourcing products, and building up good business relationships with our contacts in Asia.
The business went very well, but upon the end of the first year, the credit crunch/currency changes to pound sterling, which meant the product was no longer viable and thus we ceased to continue this venture for the forthcoming future.

I quickly adapted and found work freelancing for individuals, companies and charities, designing web sites, e-mail marketing campaigns, whilst also consulting on the subjects of online marketing, search engine optimisation, data collection and social networking opportunities.

I am now running a small creative design business "One3rdNerd Media Ltd" you can find our website at http://one3rdnerd.org and our recent portfolio at http://one3rdnerd.org/portfolio

We supply creative design solutions to a global clientelle from our Staffordshire base.

We are also running an online culture art etc magazine which can be found at http://grievousbodilycharm.com

Thank you for your time, if you want to network, chat, or talk about our services feel free to drop us a message.

David
