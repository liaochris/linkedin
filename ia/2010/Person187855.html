Sharon Pink’s Summary

The owner and founder of Screen Idol Limited, incorporating Bid Dynamics Ltd, renowned as the only proposal support company offering the complete end- to-end service from setting up proposal centres through to improving proposal quality and delivering the highest quality bid management development programmes.

Screen Idol also encompasses School Dynamics, delivering student leadership and teamwork programmes and staff team development programmes for schools and colleges, Word Dynamics - for the words that win - and Radio Dynamics, specialist radio coaching for business owners and media professionals.

A Fellow of the Institute for Independent Business (IIB), Sharon is also Chairman of the IIB Watford & District Group, comprising 70+ business advisers accredited by the Institute for Independent Business and based in north, west and east London, across Hertsfordshire, Bedfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Cambridgeshire and Essex.

Sharon Pink’s Specialties:

bid improvement, proposal management, proposal training, proposal centres, bid management, bid manager development, business journalism, radio coaching, student leadership programmes, school and college staff team development.
