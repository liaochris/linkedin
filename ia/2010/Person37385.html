Chris Bayol’s Summary

I am currently working at The Mill LA as a 3D artist. We create photorealistic visual effects for commercials and music videos. As a generalist, everyday can be different. My skills include modeling, animating, tracking, shading, lighting, and rendering.

In May 2008, I graduated from Chapman University's Dodge College of Film and Media Arts with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Arts.

I am extremely passionate about the production process and filmmaking in general. Feature films are my ultimate interest, but I am drawn to any branch of Visual Effects and 3D Animation.

Chris Bayol’s Specialties:

3D Generalist: Animation, Modeling, 3D Tracking, Lighting/Rendering. Compositing, Motion Graphics Design, Motion Capture data processing.
