Richard Miller’s Summary

Richard Miller is CEO & Managing Partner of TourismROI LLC, a unique centralized source of information for Travel & Tourism management, development and investment opportunities in more than 12,000 destinations worldwide.

Based on the principle of massive collaboration, the website www.TourismROI.com brings together destinations, business operators, developers and investors from around the world in one easily accessible and useable location. The site provides them with the information, research and resources necessary to make informed business and investment decisions.

Richard Miller’s Experience

  • CEO & Managing Partner

    TourismROI LLC

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2007 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    TourismROI is a unique centralized source of information for Travel & Tourism management, development and investment opportunities in more than 12,000 destinations worldwide. Based on the principle of massive collaboration, the website www.TourismROI.com brings together destinations, business operators, developers and investors from around the world in one easily accessible and useable location. The site provides them with the information, research and resources necessary to make informed business and investment decisions.

    Launched in March 2008, TourismROI is a joint venture between industry veteran Richard Miller, former Executive Vice President of the World Travel & Tourism Council (1991-2007), and MMG Worldwide, a US $100 million full-service global advertising and marketing firm with more than 25 years experience in the travel, hospitality and entertainment industries.

    As founder of TourismROI, Richard:

    • Established and managed a successful business partnership (LLC) with the largest American advertising and marketing firm dedicated to the Travel & Tourism industry. Joint investment exceeded US$1.0m.
    • Developed and launched an international advertising, marketing and PR/communications campaign to establish a new brand with tourism officials, industry leaders and developers/investors around the world;
    • Managed the design, development, beta-test and launch of the website concept and operation focusing on Web 2.0 technologies to drive traffic and content;
    • Developed lucrative partnership deals, endorsements and business development efforts with major international organizations, investment conference organizers and industry associations; and
    • Developed and managed a sales and marketing network covering more than 150 countries worldwide.

    (Government Relations industry)

    January 1991 — February 2007 (16 years 2 months)

    The World Travel & Tourism Council (WTTC) is the forum for business leaders in the Travel & Tourism industry. With Chief Executives of some one hundred of the world's leading Travel & Tourism companies as its Members, WTTC has a unique mandate and overview on all matters related to Travel & Tourism.

  • Management Consultant

    Deloitte

    (Partnership; Management Consulting industry)

    September 1987 — December 1990 (3 years 4 months)

    Provided management consulting services covering a broad spectrum of business problems and activities including strategic planning, general management, organization and development, financial management, systems management, manufacturing operations and marketing and sales. Significant client engagements included:

    • Strategic Plan for Tourism - State of Hawaii: Managed the strategic planning process for tourism for the State of Hawaii's Department of Business and Economic Development. Strategic planning process included identification of opportunities and challenges facing the visitor industry over a twenty year horizon, assessment of visitor markets (potential and market lifecycle), assessment of competitive environment for sun/sand/surf destinations, and development of tourism policy, objectives, action-plans, and measures of performance.

    • Strategic Opportunity Assessment of Travel & Tourism Industry - Unisys Corporation: Performed a detailed business opportunity analysis of the U.S. travel industry with Braxton Associates (Strategic Planning Division of Deloitte & Touche). Examined the industry from a customer, distribution system and provider perspective to assess the potential for business entry, by Unisys, into the computer reservation system market.
