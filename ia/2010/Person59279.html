David Park’s Summary

A web and mobile product executive with more than 25 years of experience in the technology industry working for global companies including Netscape, Visa, AOL, Coopers & Lybrand, Media General, and US Postal Service.

- Experience growing and leading product teams in both startup and large organizations.

- Broad background in product management, business development, systems integration, and management consulting.

- Hands-on management experience in multiple facets of technology including: social media, mobile, search, community, e-mail, personalization, and connected devices.

- Led teams innovating in the mobile space via SMS, mobile browser and native applications across multiple platforms including iPhone, iPad, Android, Palm, and RIM.

- MBA in Marketing and Finance from the Anderson School at UCLA and a BS in Management Information Systems from California State University, Northridge.
