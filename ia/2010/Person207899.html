Todd R. Brown’s Summary

I've written for newspapers such as the Oakland Tribune and the San Jose Mercury News, magazines such as MBA Jungle and VIP Guide Miami, and Web sites such as PaltoAltoOnline.com and Daily-Report.com. I have more than 15 years' experience as a reporter, editor, page designer and photographer. I started out publishing a 'zine, worked my way into freelance reporting, earned a Master's degree from Medill at Northwestern University and along the way became a full-time journalist.

Todd R. Brown’s Specialties:

My strong suit is authoring ethnic community and lifestyle features. I've written about Afghan, Indian, Filipino and Sufi groups and reported on hip-hop crews and glasswork artists. I've illuminated sex-education battles and sprawl development debates. I can craft quick city council articles on deadline and finesse long-term enterprise stories. Lately, I've covered all aspects of business, from small-business loans and petrochemicals to health care and downtown redevelopment.
