Danny Safady’s Summary

I have always had a passion for film as far back as I can remember. I just completed my film Scraps in March 2010. My music video directorial debut came on Miss Issa’s, “Stay Up” music video, seen on a few music channels including BET and MUN2. I love the unity of a production and the collective artistic expression that it brings. I graduated at the Art Institute of Dallas where I improved my visual skills and discovered editing and storyboarding. After graduating in 2000 I moved to Los Angeles. While in California, I pursued a number of things like, Visual FX, graphic design, modeling and acting, although, filmmaking was what came most naturally. I have a few projects currently in the works and under my belt. My focus is on music videos, short/feature films, and documentaries. I enjoy enlightening and moving people through the power of film. It is important for me to show that even the most dark and unlikely places are full of light.

Danny Safady’s Specialties:

I have many strengths as a filmmaker. I have the ability to direct, produce, edit, storyboard and design. When on set I am very decisive because of my preparation. A great project comes from an intangible place, the best we can do is get out of the way of creative flow.
