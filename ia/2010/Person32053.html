Darrell Gurney’s Summary

Thanks for visiting my LinkedIn profile today. I’m the CareerEnergizer—when people work with me, they keep going, and going, and going…

I’ve helped thousands of people make effective career transitions for over 20 years: sometimes as a recruiter and always as a coach. I wrote a book to teach job seekers how to best utilize professional recruiters entitled “Headhunters Revealed! Career Secrets for Choosing and Using Professional Recruiters.” It won some awards and continues to help people make the most fruitful use of recruiting professionals.

My own entry into the careers world occurred when I came to LA over 20 years ago and wanted to get into the entertainment industry. Know anyone who wants into the entertainment industry? Sure, everybody and his brother! I knew I had to do something a bit “different”…so I hired a career coach to teach me some non-traditional job search methods. By instituting an innovative approach, within 2 months I met the CFOs of the top 7 entertainment studios in their offices and was hired by MGM/United Artists…and never even talked to Human Resources!

Would you like to avoid HR like that? Not to besmirch HR—those are great people who work very hard. You just need to find ways to meet the people on the inside—those “in the know”—before getting processed through the HR filter. That’s what I mainly teach: how to make career transitions using the power of networking through the “backdoor”…as opposed to the front door that everyone knocks on.

Feel free to visit my website and hear what people have to say about my approach to landing the work you love in the most effective ways (www.CareerGuy.com/testimonials.htm). You can sign up there for my newsletter, and also get frequent updates and helpful career information on my FaceBook page (www.FaceBook.com/LifeWorkPlanning).

Even in times like these, people who think differently and act differently can always find awesome opportunities. Let me know how I can help you!
