Jose Luis Sanchez’s Summary

J. Luis Sanchez is Sr. Consultant and Founder of Business Trend Solutions, provider of the leading e-commerce platform for consumer brands. Prior to founding Business Trend Solutions in 2009, Sanchez was an e-Commerce Manager and Lead of Website Development and Usability for Goldenwest Diamond Corporation, an online company he led in 1999, built to market leadership. For ten years, he was the primary force in leadership of this highly successful, recognized and profitable online retailer.

Long an e-commerce visionary, Sanchez has been educating clients, colleagues and friends about the meaning of "quality e-commerce"; he currently focuses on customer relationship management technology, analytics and e-commerce architectures that support customer-centric domains.

Sanchez draws on his extensive primary and secondary market research in the architecture, design, evaluation and selection of enterprise--e-business computing infrastructures. Product vendors continue to leverage Sanchez’ skills and insights to develop product requirements and fully understand the competitive environment and deliver intelligent online marketing programs.
In consulting engagements, Sanchez often uses the technology frameworks he has developed with corporate IT organizations, mainly those from the e-commerce industries, to direct them with architecture and product selection decisions. Sanchez has successfully managed company launches, product introductions and product repositioning. He has also developed and documented business plans for raising capital and for mergers and acquisitions.
