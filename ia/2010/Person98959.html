Jinshu "John" Zhang 张晋蜀’s Summary

I have served as lead corporate counsel on numerous mergers, acquisitions, private placements and public offerings of securities, and other sophisticated business transactions involving a full spectrum of firms ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies. In addition to my regular corporate practice, I have served as the lead settlement counsel in many high-profile, high stakes litigation matters, winning a $40 million settlement for a Taiwanese plaintiff in 2005, another multi-million-dollar settlement for a Chinese plaintiff in 2007, a dismissal in 2008 of a major patent infringement case for the main defendant, a Chinese manufacturer, on only a $50,000 payment, and a dismissal of a product liability case involving multiple fatalities and injuries for the main defendant, a Chinese manufacturer, in 2009.

I have been interviewed or quoted on various legal matters by more than 100 U.S. and Chinese news organizations, including The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, The Los Angeles Times, International Herald Tribune, Reuters, Bloomberg, The Los Angeles Business Journal, The People's Daily, China Daily, South China Morning Post, Caijing, Business Weekly, Wenhui Daily, and Sing Tao Daily.
