Ami Woods’s Summary

Throughout my career path, marketing and customer service seemed to always remain constant no matter position I held.

Ten plus years on that path in the corporate world provided me the experience and the luxury to work independently.

I am grateful for each client that shows me their trust and in return, I promise everyone complete satisfaction.

Being independent allows me to work with businesses and organizations who need assistance, but who cannot hire a full-time marketing professional or even a traditional firm with high retainer fees. My goal is to assist by filling in the gaps with creative and detailed services that are contracted and then delivered to exceed all expectations.

Ami Woods’s Specialties:

Small business consulting, service and team training, copywriting, graphic design and other marketing services.
