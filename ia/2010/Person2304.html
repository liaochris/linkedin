Adam Fendelman’s Summary

After being trained as a print news journalist at the University of Missouri-Columbia's School of Journalism, I branched into online technology and business news reporting at MidwestBusiness.com.

While continuing to write, my role expanded to include editing and managing columnists as well as publishing for third-party associations and organizations.

My career then extended to include eXtreme Networking speed events for professionals. I managed the product evolution of the patent-pending eXtreme Networking software.

I've also freelanced for Time Out Chicago, Go2 Media, Blogcritics.org, Hollywood Elsewhere and ReelChicago.com. I was a theatre critic for Centerstage Chicago, which is owned by the Chicago Sun-Times Group. I am syndicated by Examiner.com and top 100 entertainment site Starpulse.com.

I currently am the founder and publisher of HollywoodChicago.com, which is a daily film and theater publication based in Chicago. I'm also currently the director of business development at Best Foot Forward, which is a full-service dating concierge.

I recently was the exclusive cell phones guide at About.com, which is part of the New York Times Company and is visited by 55 million people each month.
