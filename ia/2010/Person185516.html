Sean Doyle’s Summary

I have extensive experience designing and deploying sophisticated, network application frameworks. I have architected many web access control systems, single signon solutions and protocols and integrated many off-the-shelf solutions into LDAP based identity frameworks. I am also well on the way to becoming an expert at SOA and Web Services security after crafting SOAP solutions for many years.
