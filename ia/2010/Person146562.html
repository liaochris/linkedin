Min Cho’s Summary

Min Cho's currently taking the lead of Global Mobile Product Marketing Strategies ranging from Product Concept (including value proposition of smartphones and tablets) to Mobile Content Solution & Platform, and Premium Brand Alliances. As Global Alliance & Partnership Manager, he's also led Samsung Electronics’ alliances with Internet services, Digital content media, and Premium brands.

He had led Samsung's mobile Internet Solution Product and Account Management efforts; with global responsibility for identifying and executing the Company’s strategic partnerships with wireline and wireless carriers, cable MSOs, and solution partners.

Min brings more than 6 years of product management and alliance experience to Samsung. Prior to Samsung, Min was Telecom/Online(Internet Services) Product and Strategic Alliance Manager for DACOM, LG group's wireline carrier in Korea where he directed the company’s online product management efforts which included creation and implementation of company strategy as well as global partnerships with IT vendors around the world. In this role, Min operated as the key collaborator between staff and executive management, providing the company with solid leadership in eBusiness trends and working knowledge of eCommerce, technical and business fundamentals. By establishing and managing relationships with key IT partners and solution vendors, Min successfully extended the company's reach and revenue. Prior to the position, Min served as technical analyst of network & system engineering team where he was responsible for managing the company's IT infrastructure of next generation network and hardware. In this role, Min as a certified Siemens hardware and Cisco network engineer successfully developed & implemented cross-functional IT engineering programs to facilitate the company's IT strategy development and operations.
