Petteri Hiisilä’s Summary

Dear reader,

I'm a strong advocate of the goal-directed way of thinking and a passionate defender of user & customer rights. I've spent most of my career as a bridge between business and engineering, and the last four years running my own design studio.

My core competence is interaction design and user experience. However, I'm also a generalist and a geek who likes to think big. With 14 years of work experience in companies of all sizes, I've had the opportunity to do business planning and low-level technical engineering as well.

Over the years, I've performed over 300 interviews, 30-60 minutes each, with Cooper's Goal-Directed method. This has given me a broad view about what makes different kinds of organizations and industries tick.

I've had more than a year of research and design experience in the following industries: telecommunication, process industry (automation), finance, newspapers, television and web/internet.

You can follow my daily thoughts via Twitter: http://twitter.com/petterihiisila

You'll find what others think about my past work by reading the Recommendations section in the Full profile.

Best regards,
Petteri
