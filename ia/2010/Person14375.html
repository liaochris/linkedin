Anne Wilkinson’s Summary

Hi! My name is Anne Wilkinson and I'm the Managing Director of Executive Playground®, a Business Improvement Consultancy based in Solihull, West Midlands specialising in Sales Growth, Leadership and Innovation.

I'm often asked 'Executive Playground, what a great name - where did it come from?'

I guess the first bit is self explanatory - we work with executives and managers to develop strategy and skills, generate new business and improve the performance of their people.

The 'Playground' is a return to creativity, innovation, exploration and yes fun in business. It often used to be called 'thinking out of the box' until it was so overused - often by consultants who couldn't find the box let alone think outside of it - that it's not so widely used any more. I may be biased but that's not us.

We offer consultancy and diagnostics, management development training, life, business and performance coaching. This year we are also developing our first (highly innovative!) web based business improvement application.

So, 2010 is a very exciting year for us and marks a defining point for our company. I genuinely hope it's great year for your company too.

Thanks for taking the time to read this - don't leave without connecting through LinkedIn and/or Twitter will you, it would be good to say 'Hi'.
