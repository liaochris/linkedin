Juan Lama’s Summary

Innovative biomedical researcher and entrepreneur with 20 years of experience in viral diseases working in renowned academic institutions in the US and Europe. Holds B.S. (Biochemistry) and Ph.D. (Molecular Biology) degrees from Universidad Autonoma de Madrid (Spain). Proven ability to manage and supervise national and international projects, and to secure research grants from Government (US and Europe), non-profit Foundations and private Corporations to finance research programs. More recently, as founder of RetroVirox Inc.: i) oversaw legal corporate and IP procedures through its initial stages; ii) Identified opportunities for collaborations; III) set up research strategies; IV) established a board of scientific advisors; V) set up the company facilities in San Diego; VI) secured over $1 million of seed-capital from private investors and grants from the National Institutes of Health (NIH), and; VII) establish a specialized CRO offering antiviral services and virology assays.

• 20 years of experience in Infectious Diseases and virology, 14 years experience with HIV and human retroviruses
• 10 years as consultant for Biotechnology and Pharmaceutical companies in the topics of antivirals, drug discovery, target validation, and cell-based screening assays
• Strong knowledge of molecular biology and virological techniques, clinical regulations, drug-development process and intellectual property
• Experience in multinational projects, including collaborations with 14 hospitals
• Over over 600 citations to his peer-reviewed publications
• Ad hoc member of grant evaluation committees at the National Institutes of Health
• Proven track-record of grant awards with over US$3.5 million awarded in research grants to date, including NIH R21, R01, and SBIR
• Proven ability to manage, supervise and execute research projects
• Bilingual (English-Spanish)

Juan Lama’s Specialties:
