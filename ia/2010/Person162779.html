Peter Haslam’s Summary

I am an independent BPM consultant, specialising in the architecture, design and build of significant implementations utilising TIBCO products.

I have over 10 years of experience working with the TIBCO iProcess (formerly Staffware) product suite, for clients including Department of Work and Pensions (UK), Aozora Bank (Japan), Commonwealth Bank of Australia, National Australia Bank, Vodafone, SingTel Optus and Westpac Bank (Australia).

I am a specialist in all aspects of the TIBCO iProcess product suite, In addition, I have varying levels of experience with the following TIBCO products: Business Studio, BusinessWorks, EMS and General Interface.

Peter Haslam’s Specialties:

In relation to the TIBCO iProcess product suite:
- Analyse business requirements and translate these into a practical technological solution
- Analyse integration requirements and develop resultant technical designs
- Provide mentoring and skills transfer to both technical and non-technical staff
- Develop appropriate TIBCO iProcess infrastructure designs
- Execute performance and capacity testing
- Implement TIBCO iProcess development standards
- Develop prototype systems
