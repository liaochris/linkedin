Christophe Primault’s Summary

Christophe was born in France and has spent all of his career in the high-tech industry. He began with NCR Corp in 1990 as a Sales Director for international accounts. He then moved to marketing where he led marketing operations for NCR and AT&T in France and at the European level. He relocated to London in 1997 where he embraced a successful international career, reaching the position of Vice President of Global Marketing and Communication for NCR´s 2B$ Financial Division, the company’s largest and most profitable business unit.
Beginning of 2003, he gave a more entrepreneurial stance to his career and joined as Managing Director Fluiditi Ltd, a UK based international company specialized in security solutions for the financial industry. This company has been sold to Oberthur Card Systems.
Lately, Christophe served as CEO for Kinamik, a software security publisher based in Barcelona, Spain.
Currently Christophe is co-founder and director of GetApp,com, a website that helps companies choosing the right applications for their business needs and advise them on how to deploy apps the right way.
Christophe is also business angel in high-tech start-ups based in Paris and Barcelona.
