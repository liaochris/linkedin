Steve Guberman’s Summary

Graphic Designer with a decade of experience in more than a dozen industries and multiple mediums. Have developed integrated marketing campaigns for company's of all sizes, branding campaigns for fortune 500 corporations. My company, Fifth Room Creative, works closely with our clients to help build their brands in the best ways we know how. We live with our clients to get to know them the best and understand their visions, their target markets, and their brands.

Steve Guberman’s Specialties:

Fifth Room Creative is a full service graphic design agency. We provide practical brand development and revitalization programs to businesses of all sizes and in all markets.

Our services include Logo and Brand Development, Marketing Collateral and Stationery Design, Print and Web Design, Interactive Design, HTML Email Marketing, Digital Retouching, Advertising Design and Creative Consulting.
