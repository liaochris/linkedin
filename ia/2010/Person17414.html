Ashley Lynn Beckford’s Summary

Ashley Lynn Beckford is an industrious, ebullient and enterprising MBA candidate of the Zicklin School of Business who has found success as a journalist, management consultant and student activist throughout several internships and fellowships. She is studying International Business at Baruch College with an expected graduation date in 2011.

Most recently, Ashley studied intercultural management and competency building at Université Jean Moulin Lyon 3 and traveled within in the Rhône-Alpes region of France.

During the summer of 2009, Ashley contributed to Organizational Development at the Associated Press as a member of the Operational Reviews team that presented recommendations at the Strategic Management Summit in the fall.

Previously, Ashley Lynn served as Marketing Manager of a five-person brand management team that created a marketing plan for a new watch brand, which was launched and distributed by Callanen International, a subsidiary of Timex Corporation.

In January 2008, Ashley earned a bachelor's degree from Montclair State University after 3 1/2 years of study, which included a semester spent in the nation’s capital where she worked for the Voice of America, and earned alumna status at the Washington Center, a nonprofit organization that supports students in civic engagement activities.

Ashley Lynn has held several leadership positions in student-run organizations at each of her educational facilities. Ashley is also rigorously preparing to enter law school, where she will earn the Juris Doctor degree. She plans to enter law school in 2012.
