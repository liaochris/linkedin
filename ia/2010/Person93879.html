Jeffrey Speiser’s Summary

Business owner and Creative Director with expertise in branding, marketing, business development, and building strong client relationships. Deep understanding of all phases of the creative process from ideation and brainstorming through development of final product. Hire and manage teams of freelancers and vendors as needed. Skilled at relationship building, working with many clients for more than 10 years. Considered by many clients as their marketing partner, rather than their vendor. Successfully diversified business from niche design to full marketing and branding across multiple industries. Top performing brand expert on international crowdsourcing site.
