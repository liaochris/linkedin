Doug Berry’s Summary

Doug Berry is a highly skilled executive with over 20 years of experience specializing in solving complex strategic, customer - product, supply - demand pricing - capacity supply chain business problems. Projects and deliverables include corporate and competitive strategy, econometrics, reengineering multi-geographic supply chain business processes, identifying and implementing key management analytics and key performance indicators, change management and business case benefits realization through implementation and adoption of SAP R/3-ECC and BI IP BO best practices. He is highly skilled in delivering SAP BI IP CPM BusinessObjects architectures and roadmaps, improving existing system performance, phased requirements development, multi-dimensional data modeling, technical specification, phased realization, UAT & Go-Live for local and global implementations of SAP BI IP SEM BusinessObjects Standard and Non-Standard content. He is an expert at identifying corporate and SBU reporting architectures, KPI and analytical requirements using a variety of skills including client engagement workshops, structured interviews and function decompositions.

His International Experience includes Asia Pacific (13 ½ years – Singapore, Thailand, India, Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, Borneo, Japan, Hong Kong, China, Philippines) Middle East (2 years – Kuwait and Saudi Arabia); Europe (12 Months Switzerland, Germany, London, Malta and Spain).
