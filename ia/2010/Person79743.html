Work Experiences:

In 2008 I have been a consultant and team member of the web management team at the financial institut comdirect bank ag (Germany). I mainly took care of user experience, usability of web applications.

Since 2009 I work as a consultant and freelance in the field of eBusiness, public relations, online branding, social networks.


I gained international work experiences in the USA, UK and Switzerland.
