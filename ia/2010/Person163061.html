Peter Ramsden’s Summary

Peter has gained over twenty years of experience selling industrial consumables nationally and internationally. Peter has concluded business in more then 30 countries.

Peter is now harnessing those years of direct experience to manage his own training and business development consultancy. Methods of client delivery include formal, one-on-one coaching, mentoring and some limited open courses.

Peter is also a licensed facilitator of MAP assessment products. MAP is a unique and objective assessment of management competence used for a variety of reasons including skills gap analysis, succession planning and as a precursor to management development.

Peter Ramsden’s Specialties:

Strategy, Marketing, Leadership and Management development, Motivation, Negotiation, Key Account Management, Tendering and proposal writing.
