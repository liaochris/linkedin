Ajay Dugar’s Summary

Specializing in digital content distribution technologies, digital supply chain, protection and monetization of digital entertainment content. Digital Content Ecosystem expert focused on New Media - IPTV, VOD, Interactive TV, Electronic Sell-Through, and Over The Top (OTT) Delivery

Technologist and strategist with strong Product Management, Operations and Marketing experience driving online consumer adoption, market share and profit growth with multi-platform strategies. Excellent general management experience building new media properties from the ground-up for multiple startup video download retailers.
