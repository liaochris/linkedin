FERNANDO CARRION’s Summary

FERNANDO CARRION (Vitoria, Spain - 1965) is a director and audiovisual producer. After graduating in Fine Arts at the Polytechnic University of Valencia (UPV) he starts to work as an art director for the advertising agencies BSB and Delvico Bates.

In 1990 he moves to Japan to study CGI (Computer Graphics and Animation) at the TOKYO INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (TIT - Nagatsuta Campus) with a Monbusho scholarship, and during three years he lives between Tokyo and Yokohama. There he collaborates with the NHK (National Japanese Television and Radio), and helps to organize the Spanish Animation special section for the independent japanese animation festival ANIMATION 80 in Tokyo. He also coordinates the special section dedicated to japanese independent animation for the CINEMA JOVE'92 international film festival in Valencia.

In 1993 he returns to Spain and begins to work as director for the CARTOON-P animation studios, and coordinator of the TOON FACTORY animation training center.

Other parallel activities to his stage at the head of CARTOON-P creative department were the collaboration with the comic artist CALPURNIO for the film ATOLLADERO, directed by OSCAR AIBAR; or the coordination of CIBER@RT'96, the first international conference on ART and VIRTUAL REALITY held in the city of Valencia.

In January 1999 the independent animation studios BLACK MARIA is born: http://www.blackmaria.es

Since 1999 Fernando works as CEO and producer at BLACK MARIA animation studios, collaborating in a long list of international animation productions for film, television, commercials, interactive media and websites.

Fernando is also a professor of COMPUTER GRAPHICS and ANIMATION at the CARDENAL HERRERA - CEU SAN PABLO UNIVERSITY in Valencia (since 2004)

He's a member of the Asociacion de Desarrolladores de Ocio Interactivo Digital (DOID), member of the CPI - Circulo Empresarial, and vice-president of AVEPA (Valencian Association of Animation Producers)

FERNANDO CARRION’s Specialties:

Audiovisual production, 2D + 3D + Flash animation production, games development, film direction, TV commercials, E-learning productions, edutainment, marketing, mobile TV, licensing & merchandising, international distribution, scriptwriting, storyboards, executive production, art direction, international co-productions, CGI training, audiovisual education, VFX coordination, senior producer.
