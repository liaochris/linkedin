Aziz Shawky’s Summary

Experienced programme and change manager, in CRM, Business analytics (BI) and Risk management, with a fous on Financial services
The projects I work on aim to achieve leverage by integrating resources: knowledge, customers, employees, business units to achieve more agile, customer-focused, processes. Across channels and business functions.
Achieving that aim requires both an integrated customer view and an integrated organization. My goal is to help companies to achieve this internal integration, supporting them in understanding their customer's needs, wants and risk profiles. And the internal obstacles to achieve that insight.
Adding value to both sides of the customer equation.

What's in it for you?
What does this mean to a client? In practice it means working to define viable and robust business value propositions, based on new business models. Building the case for change, designing and implementing the required business programs, and then realising the benefits, one step at a time. My motto is: start small, but scale fast.
Often using Pilot projects, Proofs of Concept, etc. My strength is in operating as an intermediary between the Board and the programme, between Business and ICT, bridging the gap, in the areas of Programme mngt., Analytics/BI, Risk Management, Customer Insight, Information Management.

Track record
In previous projects, working for international consultancy firms, I set up the BI Competence center at a large consultancy, led several BI/CRM initiatives (EuroNext, IRS, Ministry of Finance), managed large-scale Risk Intelligence and change management projects (ABN*AMRO, ING, ANWB, RaboBank) and introduced novel business models (PGGM, ANWB).
