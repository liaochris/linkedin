Alex Linsker’s Summary

For online stores, nonprofits and government, Alex's http://ProductPerks.com software leads your online customers to the right product or destination page for them.

Sites powered by Product Perks include:
http://KarenDunlap.org/Tea-Quiz ('find the tea for you').
http://HomeBuildersPortland.org (click the government and nonprofit button: 'BuilderConnect: find resources for building green')
Call for full list and a walk-through of what Product Perks can do for you, your customers, your callers, and your constituents.

In 2006, Alex was marketing director of the 140th largest e-commerce company. Responsible for a $3 million budget, he boosted bottom-line profits by over a million dollars.

Since 2000, he has consulted for many websites on marketing: customer experience, signage, sales and design.

Web industries include: consumer electronics, food & beverage, fashion, apparel, education, publishing, finance, hedge funds, law, outsourcing, non-profit, innovation, civic, community organizing, entertainment, Web 2.0, search engine, dating, social networks, online applications.

From 2007-2009 his signage recommendations were implemented nationwide behind the counters of one of the largest restaurant franchises. His packaging recommendations were implemented by one of the world's largest manufacturers of computer mice and keyboards.

Alex has been interviewed as an expert on Indianapolis' WBAI 93.1 FM, on eBay Radio, and on Hay House Radio's "The Coaching Show." He has also presented at OneWebDay.
