Lois Weiss, MS’s Summary

Lois Weiss is the Founder & Principal of eMarketing to Win. eMarketing To Win's mission is to offer large corporate expertise & know-how to small & medium sized businesses at affordable prices, with online & offline marketing strategies, plans, & programs specifically tailored to grow customers’ brands.

Prior to founding the consultancy in 1991, Lois quickly progressed from Systems Database Software Engineer to Brand Marketing Director with IBM & Apple. Lois has an M.S. in Psychology & Business Coaching & a B.S. in Marketing, both Summa Cum Laude. She is certified through Search Engine College in Advanced Search Engine Optimization & Search Engine Marketing & through eMarketing Association in Advanced Search Engine Marketing, Advanced Search Engine Optimization, & Advanced Email Marketing.

eMarketing To Win has a proven process that has been built & refined over 20 years. We take the time to get to know our customers & their marketing objectives from an unbiased outsider’s perspective. We combine our knowledge, our customers’ own perspectives &, their customers’ perspectives to create a winning marketing strategy. This process helps us create a unique “branding blueprint” that gives all members of the creative team valuable insight & creates a foundation from which to design, develop & implement contracted media.

We believe in one-stop shopping for our busy customers. We create a cohesive & consistent message in
everything from online & offline marketing strategies & programs to website design to print ads & brochures.
