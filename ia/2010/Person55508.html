Devon Scheef’s Summary

Multi-generational workforce; generations; mentoring; leadership development; meeting facilitation.

The Learning Cafe specializes in solving issues related to the four-generation workplace. Our presentations, training materials and workshops have provoked thought, changed behavior and educated thousands of managers and employees on the importance of flexing to meet the needs of a changing workforce -- and practical ways to do so.

Devon Scheef’s Specialties:
