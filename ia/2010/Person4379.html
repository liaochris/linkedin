Alice Irvan, APR, PRC’s Summary

For about 20 years, I've worked in market research, public relations, advertising, marketing, and strategic planning. In 2003, I started this custom marketing research company to provide research for public relations and communication and to help organizations understand their brand and market position.

I've taken time to teach graduate level public relations courses, as well, and have been active in various professional organizations such as the International association of Business Communicators (IABC), Public Relations Society of American (PRSA), Marketing Research Association (MRA), and American Marketing Association (AMA).

Alice Irvan, APR, PRC’s Specialties:

Custom research design for communication campaigns, branding or image. Focus groups and in-depth-interviews. Web-based and telephone surveys. Web site usability testing
