MacKenzie Stonis’s Summary

On the other end of the line, there's a human being. I have worked in retail, experiential marketing, catering sales/event planning, and hospitality. And in every one of those fields, I have exceeded customer expectations. Not because I'm driven to do so for my check, but because I simply can't help it. If it sounds like I'm smiling on the other end of the phone, it's because I am. I may be unorthodox, and have an off-color sense of humor, but for every person that finds me out of the ordinary, there are 100 others that appreciate that.

I hope to eventually pass on my oddly innate skill for customer service to others in a sales or marketing role. Although, I have a knack for direct sales, cold calling makes my skin crawl. I suppose that I prefer to stay in the office and "hold down the fort," as it were.
