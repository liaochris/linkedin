Varun Poddar, PMP, CSP’s Summary

Over the past ten years I have led many technology projects and cross-functional teams. Cumulative knowledge of manufacturing management, information systems and project management has helped me develop a wide assortment of skills, processes and tools that work in any environment.

My supporting credentials include multiple certifications in project management (PMP, CSM, CSP), certifications in web development (ASP, SQL, Java, PHP) and a degree in operations management. I am an avid reader, an aspiring photographer and a travel-enthusiast.
