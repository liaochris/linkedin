Scott Fulop’s Summary

Experienced, versatile publishing and digital media professional with a track record of success producing an array of entertainment and educational products and services for industry-leading companies. Possessing a distinctive skill set comprised of formal business and creative expertise facilitates my superior ability to develop and market content that attracts broad demographic categories and generates revenue.

My extensive knowledge of entertainment-based intellectual properties in the areas of TV, film, toys, games, and literature combined with my ability to analyze business infrastructure, pop culture trends, and reactive industry change is regularly employed to develop original concepts, characters, and publications that feature broad demographic appeal and cross-channel marketability.

As an editor and writer, I excel at utilizing my highly developed literary and visual sense to produce a wide variety of publications – including trade paperback books, graphic novels, magazines, and comic books – from conceptual through print stage.

I am, perhaps, one of the few literary professionals who also possess expertise with digital video and film production. Combining my editorial and digital experience, I am accomplished at authoring and producing training programs that have facilitated major change in media production for global content providers. My complimentary, hands-on experience with nonlinear video editing, visual effects, desktop publishing, and DVD-Video production enables me to author interactive media, and tell a story with more than just the written word.

With every undertaking, the obtainment of superior product and service knowledge is always at the forefront of my business methodology. As such, my skills as an effective communicator are regularly employed to engage diverse professional and consumer demographics with a wide range of marketing and media content, enabling me to help build company visibility, integrity, and revenue.
