William McKee’s Summary

I help businesses make sense of Web technology and how to use it to meet corporate goals. My company, Knowmad Technologies, is a full-service Web agency that provides Web strategy, design, development, marketing and analysis to businesses nationwide.

My background in Web technology started in college where I saw the power of a world-wide network that allowed me to have real-time conversations across the world. I worked my way through school at UNC Chapel Hill in the RAM Shop selling computers and consulting on the side. After school, I traveled and continued to take part in the evolution of the online world. It was during this time that I had the inspiration for the company name which is even more relevant today as smartphones and global networks enable us to connect from almost anywhere.

Having worked in the Internet industry for over 15 years, I enjoy sharing my experience and insight with clients to help them achieve success from their Web presence. As a result, our firm continues to build a growing list of long-time clients who value the service, attention to detail and business results that Knowmad brings to their companies.

When not working in front of a computer, I enjoy dancing with my wife, pushing my boundaries through yoga, reading and playing with our two dogs.
