Blake Miller’s Summary

Blake Miller has driven major product innovations during his career as a senior marketing executive resulting in new product categories and successful business ventures. His expertise is in the translation of customer needs into innovative products through novel marketing approaches and effective integration with product design and development organizations.

Blake was a key architect behind the worldwide success of the Hewlett Packard DeskJet and OfficeJet businesses, both billion-dollar product lines. He helped transformed the peripherals product group at HP from an engineering-centric to a market-driven approach to product development, contributing to HP becoming a household brand. During his seventeen-year tenure at HP Blake conducted initiatives worldwide including a three-year overseas assignment in Singapore where he successfully launched DeskJet in the four major Asian markets. His solid track record made him a prominent innovator and visionary both inside HP and in the industry.

Blake is a C-level executive who has made significant contributions in multiple industries across the enterprise, mid-market, and start-up worlds including hardware, software, services and online categories. He understands the unique requirements to drive profitable growth in consumer and business markets from both an operations and marketing perspective in diverse operating environments. Blake is recognized as an effective leader who knows how to build exceptional teams and foster innovation in cross-functional collaborative environments.

Blake Miller’s Specialties:

Market Segmentation, Customer Needs Identification, Market Research, Product Strategy Development, Product Lifecycle Management. Executive leadership and people development. Significant operational experience and effectiveness within collaborative and cross functional organizations.
