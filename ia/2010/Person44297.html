Craig Taflin, MBA, Esq.’s Summary

I am an accomplished Financial and Business Strategy Professional with almost five years of experience as an attorney and business owner. With an MBA and law degree, I have a unique perspective that enables me to add value as a team member at small organizations, large companies, and in entrepreneurial settings. I consistently offer a combination of strong technical skills and innovative solutions.

Most recently, I utilized my dual degree working on a mortgage-backed security litigation case involving large financial institutions at Dechert LLP. I also briefed the partners about various accounting and valuation issues to assist them in their litigation strategy.

I leveraged my diverse background and training when I launched a technology company during graduate school, when I recognized a problem with the recruiting process. I developed an innovative, virtual interviewing solution that was piloted by local law schools and a publicly traded staffing company.

Representative accomplishments include:
•Assisted attorney in litigation strategy in highly publicized case that enabled client to protect his property interests by defeating the city in court.
•Recreated mortgage company’s financials, valuations, and assumptions.
•Raised $50,000 in seed capital after being interviewed and mentored by an Ernst and Young Entrepreneur of the Year for ten months.
•Developed a cutting edge, TiVo style virtual interviewing platform.
