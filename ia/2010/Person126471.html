Lisa Wieland Handy, Ed.D.’s Summary

My goal is to partner with OD Professionals to continue to develop our talent management practice and further expand on development opportunities. I have 15+ years in learning and development, including 9 years in HR and OD. My experience is in management, consulting, performance improvement, program development and working collaboratively with executives, senior leaders, and cross-functionally. I’ve led all aspects of instructional system design, talent management programs, training technologies (LMS, Blackboard), and communications while meeting strict deadlines, budgets, and meeting/exceeding customer needs. I am familiar with global cultures and gain support through networking, listening, and building trust while driving for results. I like to problem solve and work towards the goal of continuous improvement and development. I am an enthusiastic planner of change.
