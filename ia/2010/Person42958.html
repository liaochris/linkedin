Connie Land’s Summary

I started Just Because Gifts as a side business while in College 1993. I have over 20 years of various hotel, restaurant, hospitality and retail experience. As a personal challenge I decided to turn this company into a full time business.
Our primary market for the past several years has been the Las Vegas home building industry. Our business growth has been by client referrals, and word of mouth. Some of our home builder clients include: Lennar Corp, Toll Brothers, Standard Pacific Homes, Avante Homes, Pageantry Homes. As a small company, I am very proud of my relationships with these reputable companies.
With the changes in the housing market, we have had an opportunity to expand into new markets. We have provided our gifts and services to: Harrah's Las Vegas properties, Cox Communication, Perini Corp, TI, VTN Nevada, Red Carpet VIP Services, to name a few.
We have received an immense response to these new markets. Our syle to customer service and unique gifts has been very well received. Our individualized and custom approach to business, allows our clients to offer a very custom, and personalized feel to their gifts.
My continual goal is to always remember why we started this company, and never waiver from our initial principles. My goal is to provide impeccable service to the loyal clients we have, and offer these same services to new clients. I will not sacrifice my reputation, quality, integrity, and values for the sake of growing this company.
I thrive on pushing the envelope, by creating new and exciting gift ideas. My goal is to never be like any other "gift basket company". I am setting a new standard in customer service and gift giving.

Connie Land’s Specialties:

We are continually developing new gifts, and designs. We specialize in: Las Vegas themed gifts, convention and event specific themed gifts, corporate and executive gifts, weddings, holidays, everyday and special occasions, custom created gifts, and home decor
