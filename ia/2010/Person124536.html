Lex Levinrad’s Summary

Lex Levinrad has been a full time distressed real estate investor since 2003. He has been involved in buying, rehabbing, wholesaling, renting, and selling hundreds of houses in South Florida. Lex is the founder and CEO of the Distressed Real Estate Institute, which trains beginning distressed real estate investors about how to find wholesale real estate deals. Lex specializes in teaching new investors how to buy foreclosures, short sales and bank owned properties. Lex offers Home Study Courses, Audio CD's, Books, Boot Camps, Bus Trips and Private Mentoring Programs to his students. For more information please visit http://www.lexlevinrad.com or call 800-617-2884

Lex Levinrad’s Specialties:

foreclosures, short sales, distressed real estate, property management, acquisition of real estate at a substantial discount to market value.
