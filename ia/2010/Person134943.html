Marla Levie’s Summary

Hi! I’m Marla Levie, President and founder of Focus on Aging. For over 20 years my team has provided marketing services to organizations trying to attract the senior market. These are competitive times, and many of our clients work with us because of our creative approaches to increasing their market share. We provide visibility and also help get projects organized and off the ground…from start to finish. Check out my website and blog at www.focusonaging.com
