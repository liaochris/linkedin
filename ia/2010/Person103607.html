Jonah Holland’s Summary

As president and co-founder of Mox-eMedia Llc, I advise non-profits, helping them discover their full potential in emerging media. Current and former clients include the Richmond Metropolitan Convention & Visitors Bureau and VOCAL. My goal is to provide non-profits with the expertise they need to fully understand and integrate social media into their organization, and to train them to use it in accomplishing their non-profit mission.

As Public Relations & Marketing Coordinator for Lewis Ginter Botanical Garden, I am responsible for the Garden's social media. During my time at Lewis Ginter, the Garden has become a leader in the Richmond Region its use of emerging media and social media marketing. The Garden has seen record attendance as part of its 25 Anniversary events, which I was hired to promote.
The Garden's comprehensive social media strategy includes Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Flickr, blogging, social bookmarking, widgets, podcasts, search engine optimization and other electronic community outreach.

In addition, I've consulted on several social media presentations including Social Media for Social Good (#SM4SG) at the University of Richmond Jepson School of Leadership. In May of 2009 I gave a social media presentation to my peers at the Greater Richmond Tourism Association. In March of 2010 I presented a session: Social Media: Museums Building Communities, at the Virginia Association of Museums' Annual Conference, with VMFA's Suzanne Hall. And have served on several emerging media panels.
