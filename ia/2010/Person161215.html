Paul O'Brien’s Summary

A distinguished marketing, search, internet, and eCommerce executive with over 15 years of experience online and a demonstrated success in online commerce, Local, advertising, search (optimization and marketing), and business roles.

P&L management experience including function or organization, resource and company-wide segments.

Success in both B-to-B and B-to-C enterprise and startup environments.

Proven ability to generate revenue through win/win negotiating and solution based sales strategies.

Solid project and team management skills with the ability to plan strategically and execute tactically.
