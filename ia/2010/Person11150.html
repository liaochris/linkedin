Andreas Akesson’s Summary

In 1995 Andreas started to work as a business developer at a Spanish ISP company (Nexus Comunicaciones), selling internet solutions (internet conections, web pages, hostings).

In 1996 Andreas joined Olé, the biggest Spanish search engine company. The product that he managed was Olé 1-2-3 (which today would be an equivalent to Google Adwords).

In 1997 Andreas started to work as Olé’s business developer/adsales Manager. In ‘98 Olé was sold to Telefónica for 400 million euros (becoming a part of Terra).

In 2000 Andreas founded his own Performance driven Consultancy firm in Digital Media, Active Internet Consulting (AIC). AIC has during its almost 10 years of existance worked with companies such as Autoscout24, Friendscout24, Cofidis, Planeta, Liberty Insurance, Extrafilm, Grupo Godo, Unidad Editorial, and many more.

Andreas also started up a VC consultancy company focusing on new technologies and seed investments (VC More 2000-2001), and IT firm (Glaukopis 2002-2004) and an e-commerce solution company applying artificial intelligence in optimizing results in the exposure of promotional spaces (Aibits/Condusive 2004-2006).

Andreas has worked with Result as an associated partner since the beginning of 2008 and became a Partner in January of 2009. Together with Result, Andreas has worked with market entries in Spain such as Bidster and PAF.

Andreas´s passion is to help companies benefit the most from Internet and any Digital opportunities in order to grow.
