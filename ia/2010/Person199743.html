Vincent Tao’s Summary

- Passionate entrepreneur and respected visionary. Nominated as ‘Canada Top 40 Under 40 Award’ in 2005 (National award for the most accomplished individual who is under 40 years old).
- Uniquely combined competency and record experience in business + technology.
- Hands-on executive management in high-tech industry, corporate strategy, business development, marketing, and M&A.
- Track record on building a successful organization and led GeoTango from a start-up to one of top innovators in on-line mapping (GeoTango was rated as the one of top 10 fastest growing Internet companies in 2005) and eventually to the Microsoft acquisition.
- Extensive knowledge/experience in international business management, global market expansion. As a Microsoft executive, established Microsoft China business in on-line local search and mapping, and mobile services from the ground-up.
- Deep knowledge and exceptional research development experience. World renowned pioneer in location based services and internet media technology. Authored numerous influential technical papers. Owner and inventor of dozens of technology licenses and patents.
- Frequently invited speakers at major conferences. Numerous mentions in media including TV interviews: CNN, Discovery Channel, CTV, BBC, ABC news, Space Channel; Radio interviews: CBC, Radio Canada, CFTB etc; magazines like Economist, GeoWorld, GIM; Newspaper: National Post, Globe and Mail, Northern Silicon Valley, etc; and blog posts, O’Reilly radar, blogspot, eWeek, etc.
- Accomplished individual honored with 26 awards or distinctions for achievements and innovations.

Vincent Tao’s Specialties:

Executive Leadership with Vision and Execution, Hands-on International Experiences (China/US/Canada), World-class Technology Innovator in consumer internet and mobile experiences.
