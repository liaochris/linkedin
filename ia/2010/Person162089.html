Peggy Sweeney Furlong’s Summary

Versatile and dynamic marketing/advertising agency pro with over 20 years' experience in account management, client service and marketing operations -- delivering results for clients.

Leader who is skilled at building client relationships and creating teams to support them. Well-versed in strategy and planning, customer insight gathering, positioning, branding, the creative process and overseeing project & budget management, and tracking results.

Focus is on innovative, integrated/multi-media campaigns and programs based on customer insights: promotional/retail and trade marketing, online and social media, traditional media, direct response, sports/event marketing and public relations support.

Began career as a creative -- first as a retail home center chain advertising manager responsible for layout & design, and then as a copywriter in the catalog division at Montgomery Ward. Orchestration skills lead to account management.
