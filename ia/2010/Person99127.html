John J. Kaiser’s Summary

Results oriented, strategic minded, pharmaceutical business and marketing executive with exceptional interpersonal and communication skills and an extensive background in a number of broad-based competencies.

John Kaiser is a seasoned leader with broad, 30 years of experience and succcess in managing multiple brands across the value chain, with additional multi-functional experience in general management, corporate strategy, business development, and portfolio development.

Prior to founding Kaiser & Associates Consulting, LLC in 2010, John was VP: Commercial & Corporate Development at ACADIA Pharmaceuticals. ACADIA Pharmaceuticals is a biopharmaceutical company located in San Diego, California with assets in development for a number of illnesses of the central nervous system. John led the development of the commercialization strategy including all prelaunch planning and Go2Market launch activites. In addition, John contributed heavily to the successful licensing efforts of the firms Phase III asset pimavanserin.

John left Eli Lilly and Company after 28 years of service in 2008 to join ACADIA Pharmaceuticals. During his tenure at Lilly John held a variety of general managment and marketing and sales leadership roles. He demonstrated strong leadership and a track record of achievement in general management, sales and sales management, brand management, market research, new product planning, strategy and business planning, corporate and business development.

John currently serves on the Board of Trustees of Purcell Marian High School, an inner-city Catholic high school located in Cincinnati, Ohio. John has previously served on the Board of Directors of Junior Achievment of Central Indiana and the Alzheimers Association of Central Indiana.
