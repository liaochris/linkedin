NIGHTHAWK MARKETING

Nighthawk Marketing is a full-service, boutique agency located in the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul. We help companies, ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500s, build brand strategies and market share by generating qualified demand and quantifiable results. Clients who work with our agency come from a wide range of industries, such as Legal, Financial, Health Care, Medical, Retail, and Sports Training, whose markets are business-to-business and/or business-to-consumer. Our product offering encompasses traditional marketing and sales materials, web site development and solutions, and interactive media.

We have one clear, unwavering mission at our agency: to surpass your expectations on every project. We aspire to the highest standards of quality and efficiency. Service, reliability, trust and integrity are the hallmarks we follow every step of the way. Our goal is to build a solid, long-term relationship with each and every client. Clients view our agency as an extension of their company, where with one call or click, they can count on us to be there.

We operate on a 24/7 schedule to ensure we are there for our clients when project arise unexpectedly or deadlines are tight. When the day is done, Nighthawk Marketing's success is based on our clients' success.
