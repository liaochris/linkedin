Terry Curtis’s Summary

Terry Curtis is a “C-Level” Finance Exec with a diverse set of skills & industry experience honing him as an effective international executive. His career path has prepared him as a strategist & business leader as well as a CFO.

He has demonstrated his mettle as a CFO plying his skills internationally (Germany, Japan, China, the U.K.) in a diverse set of industries (multi-channel, e-commerce and B&M retail, consumer branded products, technology products, devices and network infrastructure) under varied business conditions.

Terry has a proven track record in implementing the appropriate & balanced level of financial, reporting, business processes and procedures, including SOX 404, to satisfy regulatory reporting requirements, shareholder needs and mgmt. operating metrics in environments ranging from start-ups to $1 billion companies.

Core competencies lie in strategic & financial planning, reporting and control, but has strengthened this skill through broader responsibilities including operations, IT & Divisional GM roles. As a finance exec, he successfully oversaw M&A activity in emerging markets as well as W. Europe and Asia

As the CFO, reorganized and developed the skills of the Finance org. of a $1 billion Japanese JV of IAC Corp, Liberty Global Inc. & Sumitomo Corp. enabling it to successfully grow sales at a compounded rate of 32% while increasing profitability by 38%.

Launched two UK based start-ups in the interactive media space as the project lead for the company COO of IAC / HSN.

Stepped in as CFO / COO of HSE24.de executing a successful turn-around of this $400 million subsidiary of IAC / HSN regaining double digit growth a return to profitability and significantly increasing cash flow.

Successfully executed acquisition strategy including a number of divestitures. Terry holds an MBA from Boston University and a BSBA in Finance/Economics from Suffolk University.
