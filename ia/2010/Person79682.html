Hank Monaco’s Summary

Henry J. Monaco- Executive Biography

Hank Monaco is Vice President, Commercial Marketing for ADT Security Services, the world’s leading provider of electronic security services. Monaco has held several marketing leadership roles in the security services industry. Prior to joining ADT he led Marketing for the security division of Ameritech. While there he helped the company grow from a regional provider to the number two market share position in North America.

Before entering the electronic security industry, Monaco held brand management and product development roles in the consumer packaged goods industry. At M&M Mars (now Masterfoods) he was responsible for growing and managing a $200 million portfolio of frozen snack brands including DoveBar and Snickers Ice Cream.

Monaco earned his bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of Notre Dame. He resides in Delray Beach, Florida with his wife and three sons and has been actively involved in youth sports and Boy Scouting.
