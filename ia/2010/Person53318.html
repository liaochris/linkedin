Debbie Baxter’s Summary

I started in the Real Estate business in 1995 and was PWAR top producer from 1997 to 2005. I then went into ownership and management and purchased my first Keller Williams franchise in January of 2003 in Martinsburg WV. I served as Operating Principal and Team Leader until October of 2003 when I hired someone for theTeam Leader position. I then opened my second franchise in Fredericksburg, Va in 2004 as the Operating Principal and Team Leader in which I received the Keller Williams launch of the year award for in February of 2006. I then opened my third franchise in Stafford, Va in 2005, my fourth in March of 2006, and my fifth and final in October of 2006. I then was offered the Regional Director position for the Kelller Williams Maryland/DC region in December of 2006 in which I am still in that position currently.
