Monitor Closely Franchise’s Summary

MonitorClosely.com franchisees market, sell, install, and service state-of-the-art digital surveillance systems for commercial and residential use. With the recent advancements in digital technology, the quality of surveillance systems has significantly increased while the cost of equipment has continued to decrease. Today's systems are so advanced that with hi-speed Internet service it is possible to monitor surveillance cameras from anywhere in the World at anytime. The customer base, for the company's franchisees, appears to be unlimited and includes; day care centers, private schools, assisted living facilities, retail businesses, apartment complexes, office buildings, multi-unit fast food restaurants, hotels, banks, and private residences. In addition to selling the digital surveillance systems, MonitorClosely.com franchisees offer a very affordable Internet-based service, through the company's host website, that allows customers to remotely view their surveillance cameras.

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 11 months)

    The benefits of digital surveillance systems versus old analog security camera systems are clear and compelling; improved image quality, less maintenance, multiplexing capabilities, easy to use search and playback functions, along with remote viewing capabilities.

    MonitorClosely.com's state-of-the-art digital surveillance systems have proven to be a significant deterrent to criminal activity and inappropriate behavior. Our business customers are able to use archived information, which is stored on the hard drive of each ClearVision MC surveillance system, to refute both customer and employee claims that could have resulted in lengthy and costly lawsuits.

    POS 800 UnitEach ClearVision MC digital surveillance system is custom built and designed to specifically meet the needs of each application. MonitorClosely.com systems feature industry-leading GEOVISION software to capture high resolution video images that are of DVD quality. Although the technology is sophisticated, the systems themselves are easy to use and provide business owners with a cost-effective solution that is dependable. ClearVision MC digital surveillance systems are low maintenance and self sufficient. There is no need to change tapes or review hours of useless footage since all of MonitorClosely.com's surveillance systems have a motion detection feature.

    Each ClearVision MC digital surveillance system may be remotely viewed over the Internet. This highly secure means of offering decentralized video access, by authorized personnel only, is provided in an ID and password protected environment through the MonitorClosely.com web site. Business owners can now view real time images from any computer that has high-speed Internet access. There are no special or additional software requirements for the remote viewing computer or supported PDA device.
