Robert Wayson’s Summary

Can your firm or agency justify the placement and hiring costs to simply staff one or two projects? Or does that project just have to wait until someone can free up?

How much does that cost you in competitiveness, productivity gains, and lost potential revenues?

FREIGHT SERVICES, at Wilbur Smith Associates, now offers over 50 years of engineering, environmental, GIS, transportation and freight planning experience to the freight community. Previously oriented to fulfill the needs of the public sector (e.g. government agencies and bodies), WSA has evaluated the firm's in-house expertise and roles against those needs present in the private sector; motor carrier, rail, air and intermodal. Staffed with both industry executive experience and leaders from state DOT's, WSA is committed to fulfill the freight community's needs by deploying only WSA personnel. Why look at another firm that simply serves as the middle man?

From assessing local economic development and future freight transportation network needs to establishing route structures and providing education on the most current or future government regulations and policies, we provide a cost effective alternative as your needs grow.

Dedicated to providing a cost effective, appropriately staffed, and on schedule solution to your strategic transportation needs, contact me today!

Feel free to review one of our many public sector projects with "real world motor carrier" implications; Atlanta Strategic Truck Route Master Plan - ASTROMAP. Cut and paste the following link into your web browser:
