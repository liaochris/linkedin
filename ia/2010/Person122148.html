Laurel Coffey’s Summary

Originally licensed in 1986, Laurel has enjoyed working in real estate sales and financing in North Carolina and Virginia. Having worked for national franchises as well as independent companies, Laurel values the resources & technology RE/MAX allows her to bring to her clients. As a RE/MAX Executive Club award-winning realtor, Laurel brings extensive knowledge, solid experience and her commitment to excellence to every transaction. Whether helping a first time homebuyer understand FHA financing or a Seller determine market value, Laurel is up to the challenge. As a Certified EcoBroker, she promotes healthier, cost effective and green choices in real estate. “I am committed to providing my clients the best service possible. Their best interest is my top priority.” Call Laurel today for exceptional service with results.
