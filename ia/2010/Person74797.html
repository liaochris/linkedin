Geri Stengel’s Summary

What do you get when you cross a serial entrepreneur, teacher, online information business expert with making a social impact?

In a word – me.

•President, Ventureneer, online education and peer support for entrepreneurs and nonprofit leaders.
•President, Stengel Solutions, a strategic planning and marketing consultancy that advises and develops businesses, especially those making a social impact.
•Adjunct Professor, The New School: Entrepreneurship, Social Enterprise and Entrepreneurial Growth

Whether you like to do things by yourself, like it done for you, or something in between, I offer guidance for you. You can have help anyway you want: in person, online, by phone or any combination.
