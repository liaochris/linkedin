Sequin Miner Photography’s Summary

It’s been often said “A picture is worth a thousand words.” Part of those words is the message that is being promoted; the other part carries a very strong message about the company itself!

Is this company professional? Is this company committed to detail and concerned about it's brand? Will this company only accept, and therefore produce, only the best? Or, will it take shortcuts and lower standards to save costs?

Commercial images must not only contain the right message, they must also inspire corporate confidence or they raise doubt in the mind of the viewer. Unfortunately, many companies have learned the hard way that using images which are weak and substandard have far greater costs than the initial budget of hiring a professional photographer.

This is why we are committed to “A picture that says what you want, is worth a thousand words!” We understand that your images have as much to say about your project, product, or building as it does about you and your company. We know that your products and services are the best in the business and that you are recognized, respected and viewed positively by both existing and future customers alike. It is our job to create images which reinforce and extend this message.

Can I afford it?”

We know you face a difficult decision. Budgets are tight, times are tough. Stock images are certainly less costly but aren't company or product specific. The amateur photographer is certainly cheaper, but simply doesn't have the technical ability, equipment and experience to successfully plan, execute and render top quality professional images. When it has to be right, it simply isn't worth the risk.

A professional photographer may require a budget but we are competitive and want to win your business. We may not always be the cheapest option but our Clients know we will deliver exactly what they want, on deadline, no matter how tough the circumstances, every time. Let us work for you.

Sequin Miner Photography’s Specialties:

Commercial images are how our clients create their First Impression with their customers. We understand the importance of that first impression and we aim to create human connections, generate desire and initiate a "Call to Action" vital in today's highly competitive markets. We specialize in in providing emotionally compelling images for commercial print and indoor/outdoor advertising, corporate profiles, annual reports, interior & exterior architecture, still life and product photography.
