Dora Vell’s Summary

Dora Vell is the founder of Vell Executive Search. Dora Vell is the President of Vell & Associates, a premier retained technology executive search firm in Boston.

Areas of Specialty: Ms. Vell is an internationally recognized expert in executive search for technology CEOs, COOs, CIOs, Vice Presidents and board members. She works with start-up organizations through Fortune 50 firms.

Consulting Experience: Her consulting experience includes eight years as a partner in the Heidrick & Struggles technology practice.

Technology Industry Experience: Ms. Vell's corporate experience includes heading up a $150 million software business at IBM, where she managed sales, marketing and support activities for a middleware business. Additionally Ms. Vell was a senior engineering executive at IBM, delivering projects of significant complexity and size.

Patents, Articles, Features and Quotes Ms. Vell authored seven worldwide software patents. She has published and been quoted in numerous articles including Global CEO magazine, Mass High Tech, the OPUS for the World Economic Forum, Boston Business Journal, The Globe and Mail, cio.com and IEEE. She has been a featured speaker at various conferences focusing on leadership.

Board Experience: Ms. Vell served on the boards of Goodwill, Mary Centre for developmentally handicapped individuals and on RBC Capital Partners Telecommunications Practice. She currently is on the advisory board of garage.com in Canada. Ms. Vell is also an active member of EO, Entrepreneur's Organization.

Educational Credentials: Ms. Vell received an MBA from the University of Toronto, a Master in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo and a Bachelor in Computer Science from Carleton.

She can be reached at dora@vell.com
