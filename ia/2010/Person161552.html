Paul Wharton’s Summary

Throughout my career, I have always been passionate about what I do. I am a highly adaptable, results driven, marketer with over 20 years experience in services and product marketing. I've obtained the unique ability to interweave B2B and B2C marketing principles with great results. I am a strategic thinker and planner, but with a "roll-up-the-sleeves" attitude. I have the ability to see the large picture and focus on the future while still acting in the present.
