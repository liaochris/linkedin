Jacqueline Charak’s Summary

I’m an artist through and through. I best use my camera to
convey images to the world and strive to convey imagery and ideas through its lens. Whether its video or photo, I enjoy
learning about new techniques for beautiful productions from seasoned professionals.
In the past few years, my production experiences have ranged from student productions, extreme low budget shorts, and
independent films; working as a PA, Gaffer, 2 AC/Loader, and/or set photographer. Starting off in the industry wanting to
produce, I found myself enjoying roles behind the camera more and more. When I was given the opportunity to work with 1st AC's such as Doug Hart, on “Nightingale Sings,” I learned the importance of communication through the entire crew, no matter what department, as well as the art of anticipation.
As with a family, your colleagues can vary in number and as long as you communicate, it doesn’t matter if there are 3 or 30
people working together as long as there’s an understanding it feels like just 2.
I’m excited to evoke emotion through light, mise-en-scène, and camera placement through shoots.
