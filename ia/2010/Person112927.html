    (Environmental Services industry)

    November 2006 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Founder of the These Come From Trees "Guerrilla Public Service Announcement."

    The project revolves around the realization that quite a bit of disposable paper consumption happens "involuntarily" through a variety of design flaws.

    Prototypical example is paper towels in public restrooms, where users have a tendency to pull three, four, or more towels in fast succession, when the use of one or two provides the same utility.

    The project hit upon a solution involving a sticker to deliver a helpful reminder message to the user, which sticker could be applied to any paper dispenser.

    Testing showed a reduction of 15% in paper towel usage at the pilot coffee shop.

    More than 100k stickers sold through the project website, to individuals, enterprises (Clorox, eBay, Yahoo, SanDisk) and education institutions (Virginia Tech, Penn) while numerous blogs have linked to and commented on the project (Seth Godin, Treehugger, etc.)
