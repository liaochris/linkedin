Barbara Bix’s Summary

Want your best prospects to RAISE their hands when they're ready to buy?

They need to get precisely the right message about the right solution at the right time.

I help businesses pinpoint:
* Their most promising prospects
* What they value most
* Where and when they seek information

Then, we use this information to develop products and marketing programs that make it easy to buy.

The Wall Street Journal quoted me as an expert in Social Media and Internet Marketing. I speak and write about:

- Branding the Social Media Way

To find out what a difference these marketing initiatives can make--just see my clients results (under my current position).
