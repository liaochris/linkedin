Angela Lussier’s Summary

Angela Lussier is an award winning speaker, author of "The Anti-Resume Revolution" and owner of 365 Degrees Consulting in western Massachusetts. She has been a purposeful rebel since the age of 3 when she escaped from the confines of a white picket fence and hasn’t looked back since. Angela’s clients are job seekers, entrepreneurs, and non-profit organizations. She focuses on personal branding, marketing & PR campaigns, and helping her clients find success doing what they love.
To buy The Anti-Resume Revolution, go to http://bit.ly/6kupkw
View "The Making of The Anti-Resume Revolution" on YouTube! http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AFmO3HpTOgc

Angela Lussier’s Specialties:

Working with people who realize there is more to life than the 9-5 grind is what I live for! I'd like to see a world of people who are engaged in meaningful and fulfilling work - it doesn't have to be hard.
