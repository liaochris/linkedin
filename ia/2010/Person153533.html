Nicole Heckers’s Summary

I'm on LinkedIn to help individuals needing help with their careers, those in personal transition, to provide corporate training, and business strategy consultation. Over 120 clients have used my expertise in career change, interviewing strategies and career growth. I provide a free, one hour consultation to those seeking career help or guidance in personal life issues.

Nicole Heckers, MA, BCPC, Life-Strategist, corporate trainer, counselor, public speaker, teacher, author, and small business owner, has worked as a consultant to various industries for over 10 years.

Heckers currently works as a consultant to help individuals maximize their careers through extensive assessment, ongoing counseling and education.

Nicole has experience training managers and employees alike to develop core competencies in areas such as:

• Corporate Communications
• Personality Based Sales Training
• Inter-Generational Management
• Corporate Social Responsibility

In 2009, Nicole’s book, Getting Employed which she co-authored with her partner John Heckers was released. A two disc DVD set of Nicole and John training business, professional and technical personnel on career topics also accompanies this book.

Nicole has appeared on Denver's WB Channel 2 as a career expert. She has provided training for and has worked with individuals from corporations and entities including Stryker, The City of Aurora, Chase Manhattan, New Horizons Computer Learning Centers, Medtronic, Prudential Financial, AMG Guaranty Trust, Qwest, FedEx, Hewlett-Packard, Agilent Technologies, Gates Corp., Starz! Encore Media, Gilead Sciences, Jeppesen Sanderson, Lockheed Martin, United States Army, Merck Medco, Maxtor Corporation and many others.
