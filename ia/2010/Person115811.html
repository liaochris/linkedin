Kevin Smith’s Summary

An accomplished global sales executive capable of organizing and motivating sales, marketing and customer service teams while developing the strategic plans and customer relationships that are critical to long term growth and success. This includes over 15 years of sales management experience and overseas assignments based in Asia and Europe. My leadership style is based on continually setting and attaining business goals and priorities, establishing a management expectation of performance for results and holding people accountable, while providing opportunities for personal and financial growth. Results are achieved by establishing aggressive business development plans, targeted customer strategies, penetration of new markets and emerging regions as well as a channel expansion initiatives.
