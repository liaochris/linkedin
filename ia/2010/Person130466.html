Michele Allinotte’s Summary

I'm not your parent's lawyer. In fact, I'm probably not like any other lawyer you have ever met. I believe there is a better way to practice law and I invite you to connect with me so you can see what I mean by that.

So, what is it that I do that is so different? I am approachable, accessible, easy going and responsive. I make sure that we work together so you understand and are empowered to make the right decisions for you and your family or business.

My practice areas are business law, real estate and estate planning.

I'm also working with Twitter and Facebook and showing some business owners and entrepreneurs how social media can work for them.

Follow me on my journey!
