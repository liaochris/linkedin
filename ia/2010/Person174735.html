Robert Gonsalves’s Summary

I am a consultant to CEOs in all industries seeking to grow and profit from their online presence. My work creates lifelong online customers for my clients. My strategies and tactics define, design, develop, deploy and extend brands, increase online audiences, and ensure the loyalty of those followers.

My pioneering work at Warner Bros. Online (founding member 1995-1999) and seven years at Disney Online is supported by my work in operations, technology, customer management, production, budgeting and analysis, spanning a total of 23 years at Paramount, Disney and Warner Bros.

My work developing award-winning websites also tripled online revenues and increased traffic over 600% while delivering double digit cost reduction.

My clients and colleagues say I am “a razor sharp strategist creates actionable solutions is brilliant at operations”… “with a keen sense of how things work” and “new media savvy.” They go on to say I am “an incredible technical visionary who has that rare gift of balancing the technical with the business requirements, harness technology to turn a vision into reality” “amazing breadth of knowledge about a very changing industry.”

My core expertise is leading and growing new and existing brands online and creating/enhancing customer communities, to build new revenue streams and bottom line growth on all interactive platforms.
