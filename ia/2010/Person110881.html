Kari Hulac’s Summary

Experienced daily print and online writer and editor with special expertise in features and lifestyles topics from entertainment to health, fitness and home and garden. Have additional background in teaching workshops, staff training and recruitment as well as extensive experience coordinating print media visuals, including graphic designs, photo styling and print layout/design. Interested in opportunities outside of daily print media that allow me to apply my communication skills and exercise my creativity.

Kari Hulac’s Specialties:

Supervising production of daily sections, special sections, online pages. Writing and reporting on deadline. Mentoring and training students, interns, new hires.
