Matt Turnbull’s Summary

Matt is President of the North American operations at the award winning digital personal media company TigerSpike, which has offices in New York, London, Sydney and operations in Shanghai and Singapore. TigerSpike specializes in Mobile & Digital Strategy, Marketing and Technology for Brands and Agencies. We create iPhone, iPad, Android and Blackberry apps and mobile sites, sms campaigns and digital professional services to help guide your company.

Prior to joining TigerSpike, Matt founded Green360°, which provides experiential marketing and luxury event production for socially responsible and environmentally friendly clients, both nationally and internationally.

Before moving to NY, Matt helped set-up and manage offices as well as run business development, over a 10 year span, for companies that specialized in producing high-end summits and product launches as well as luxury sports hospitality events. These roles allowed Matt to work and live in Tokyo, Shanghai, Hong Kong, London, Barcelona, Monte Carlo, Nice, Sydney and now New York.
