James Dzierzanowski, Ph.D., CPP’s Summary

Technical savvy combined with a comprehensive background in development and oversight of leading-edge technologies for technical security solutions are just a glimpse of the value Dr. Dzierzanowski brings to the table.

Dr. Dzierzanowski's success in advising on technical security issues, computer forensics, security trends, firewalls, and managing risk with world-class financial institutions have become widely recognized within his industry. He holds the ability to identify advanced and leading technologies while promoting business value-- driving them into production, often with the aspect of managing risk.

He is a strong proponent of leveraging people, processes, and technology to jointly solve problems, facilitate change management, move businesses forward to meet business’ technical needs. He enjoys working with people and is skillful at interacting with people on all levels to resolve issues and get the job done.

Dr. Dzierzanowski boasts outstanding leadership, consensus building, budget management and communication skills. He is also a published author of several finance-related articles.
