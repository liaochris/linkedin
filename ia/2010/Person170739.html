Regina L. Burns’s Summary

I am a strategic communicator skilled in news and information, Social Media, digital content production and customer relations with an award-winning broadcast (TV & Radio) background.

Regina L. Burns’s Specialties:

Innovative Web Content Editor who has generated award-winning online audio packages, online slide shows and online video packages targeted to national and state audiences. Experienced in Social Media software, Web content management software, digital video, on-air announcing and TV host/producer. Together with AP photographer Alex Brandon, our Hurricane Katrina work with the rest of the team, took first place in the 2008 EPpy Awards.
