Kim Sarubbi’s Summary

Kimberly is a savvy Sales and Marketing executive with 17 years of experience managing advertising sales for television production. Recruited out of the University of Central Florida where she majored in Business with a focus on Corporate Organization and Development while also being a member or ARMY ROTC and fulfilling her duties as Miss UCF 1993. Sarubbi was charged with the responsibility of establishing the trade show sales program for the newly emerging "My Pet TV" (MPTV). MPTV, a producer of pet programming based at Universal Studios in Orlando, Florida put a half dozen TV series, including the highly successful "Petsville Shopping Club on QVC", into syndication during her tenure. Each of these TV series was sponsored by anchor advertisers secured by Sarubbi. When MPTV was sold to a group of LA investors, Sarubbi relocated to Los Angeles, California to personally supervise a seamless transition for her advertisers. While directing this transition, Sarubbi sourced product for "Petsville Shopping Club" hosted by veterinarian Dr. Jeff Werber. "Petsville Shopping Club", which transitioned from its QVC roots to television syndication, boosted both advertising and product sales for its revenue lines.
