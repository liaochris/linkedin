Gulsah Boye (gulsaholmez@gmail.com)’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneurial-minded leader with a passion for social media, digital world and currently leading Hertz Group’s social media and digital strategy while working with senior management across the organization. My responsibilities are to manage company’s social media strategy, social network sites, digital communications, corporate reputation and online communities, and in that capacity mentor, teach and educate others within Hertz about the power of social media to better serve our customers globally.

I posses leadership, organizational and technical skills which allow me to help Hertz businesses, marketing teams develop platforms and strategies while I gather leadership buy-in for higher impact.

Following is what I do at Hertz:

• Managing digital communications strategies, enabling to proactively manage online interests by pursuing positive communication opportunities and effectively defending against negative online news, commentary and other online messaging

• Supporting marketing efforts by utilizing social media to help create business development opportunities and generating measurable and sustainable growth for the company and its brands while measuring ROI

• Working with key internal and external functions, agencies to lead, support social media initiatives that generate revenue and awareness for the company and its brands

• Engaging in search media campaigns, including initial planning, implementation, monitoring, optimization, analysis and reporting that can tie into social media optimization

• Monitoring the overall social media environment such as online trends, communities, social networks to report key information to senior executives and suggest proactive, reactive strategies and tactics to help manage the company’s online media perception that later translate these trends, technologies and metrics

• Mentoring and training employees with social media responsibilities while implementing company wide policies, guidelines
