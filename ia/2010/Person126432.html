Lisa Taylor Hudson Kim’s Summary

Engaged in private practice in Virginia (Richmond area) and North Carolina (Raleigh/Wake Forest area) in the areas of commercial litigation, bankruptcy, creditors' rights, and collection since completing a federal clerkship with the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Eastern District of Virginia in 2001. Attended College of William and Mary as an undergraduate and obtained a business degree in finance. Consulted in Washington, D.C. for two years after graduation, and then attended law school at the University of Richmond School of Law. Member of the Law Review at the University of Richmond School of Law and published a casenote in the Law Review as a second year. Champion of annual Carrico Moot Court tournament my third year and served as editor-in-chief of Richmond Journal of Law and Technology, the nation's first online law review founded in 1995.

Lisa Taylor Hudson Kim’s Specialties:

Creditors' rights, collections, bankruptcy, and commercial litigation
