Scott Robertson’s Summary

Entrepreneur took a start-up insurance brokerage from $600m to $7mm with $3.7 EBITDA in 7 years. Sold the business to an industry roll-up and worked with fellow founders and investor management to identify and integrate future acquisitions. The parent entity ultimately failed as the 2000 IPO market tanked, however I retained control of the brokerage and later sold the company to another national roll-up in 2006.

Now working as a successful sales coach, strategist, trainer and speaker with a track record of helping businesses in a myriad of industries to flourish in a challenging environment.

Seeking to lead a small-medium sized insurance entity or serve in the capacity of sales leadership at a larger organization.

Scott Robertson’s Specialties:

Expertise in all aspects of developing and managing a fast-growing and dynamic small-medium sized company. Business strategy, leadership and team development. Hiring and training of top-performing staff. The creation of industry leading sales and customer services systems and processes. Have many long and trusted relationships in the insurance industry throughout the Southeastern United States.
