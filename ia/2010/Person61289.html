Scott Carter’s Summary

A skilled web development professional accomplished in architecture, planning, and design. Most recent activities involved the conceptual work and implementation of Social.com. Social.com is a site that offers various social media tools (bookmarklet, Wordpress plugin, widget) designed to help you spread your message to all your followers on the Internet.

My background also includes extensive experience in verification and tool development which has been applied to ensure solid stability of the Social.com tool suite serving thousands of users.
