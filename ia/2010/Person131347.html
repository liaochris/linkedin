Marc Finkel’s Summary

As the President of Influent Solutions, my goal is to bring to the print industry, the kind of innovation I became known for as a leader in the photo industry. Influent Solutions is an all digital print provider offering web to print solutions for corporate customers looking for a more eficient and cost effective way to manage corporate identity materials. We also specialize in variable data printing personalizing direct mail and electronic communications for maximum impact.

As the co-founder and day to day manager of Cashman Professional, my team and I grew the company substantially over a period of six years. We became the dominant professional photographer and video service provider to 14 major hotels and thousands of customers. We received numerous awards and letters of appreciation for the service we provided.
