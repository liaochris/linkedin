John Hamende’s Summary

Strong, well-rounded experience and education in the Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology, and Medical Device industries in analyst, consultant, regulatory/compliance, business development and project manager roles. Roles performed within these industries have been:

• Method development and validation chemist (using analytical chemistry, microbiology, and ligand binding techniques)
• Investigator of out-of-spec results and quality issues for both laboratory and manufacturing facilities
• Auditor of Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) to assure regulatory compliance along with auditing for gap analysis issues and GLP, GMP, & GCP compliance
• Project manager of large scale projects dealing with international product submissions, FDA responses, and medical device testing.
• Reviewer of incoming raw material inspections, batch release testing, quality records, regulatory submissions, and remediation projects.
• Author of Master Validation Plans, protocols, SOPs, testing procedures, and other necessary documents to assure compliant laboratory practices
• Trainer for CAPA, Change control processes, FDA regulations (such as GLP, GMP, GCP, Quality Systems among others), management and organizational behavior practices within analytical and quality control laboratories, and performing computer, software, and instrument validation testing

John Hamende’s Specialties:

Lab skills: All analytical instrumentation involved in development, validation, and testing of pharmaceuticals, biologics, and medical devices.

Consulting skills: project management, quality systems, regulatory affairs, business development, organizational behavior, process validation, remediation, computer and instrument qualification/validation

Computer skills: Windows and Macintosh OS, MS Office (including Project and Visio), SQL server setup and maintenance, SAP
