Michael Lombardi’s Summary

Michael is a freelances undersea specialist, providing scientific and commercial diving services. This work includes all modes of diving, from SSA, to rebreathers, to deep mix, to very shallow hookah. Work has been conducted around the world, and in support of a variety of projects including biological/ecological sampling, oceanographic instrumentation, heavy marine construction, vessel body surveys, and more.

In 2003, Michael founded Ocean Opportunity Inc., a non-profit organization, which through exploration, achieves its mission to catalyze projects and programs in the ocean science and technology communities to foster a new global appreciation for a developing ocean literate society, therefore improving the quality of life on Earth.

scientific and exploration diving expertise, network of exploration professionals, field station access, scientific permitting, technical writing, access to laboratory infrastructure for natural products evaluation, technical writing and editting, program development, project management, expedition logistics, underwater imaging/photography, closed-circuit rebreathers
