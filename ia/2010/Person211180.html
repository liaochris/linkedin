David Vespremi’s Summary

David is an accomplished brand marketing strategist with experience in automotive, cleantech/greentech, and web 2.0 industries. At Tendo, he was responsible for helping lead client marketing initiatives and drive agency business development opportunities. David provided marketing support for clients American Honda and Cisco.

David’s work has garnered 2006 Addy and Webby awards for K&N Engineering, where he served as Brand Marketing Director. David helped lead the company to consecutive record breaking fiscal years during his tenure between 2004-2006. His work has also garnered PR Week finalist nominations for both 2008 Brand Launch of the year and 2008 Technology Launch of the Year for Tesla Motors, where he served as PR/Communications Director. While at Tesla, David architected and deployed 1M+ viral video hits, secured product placements in leading video games, films and toys, and orchestrated prominent spotlight articles and television appearances for the emerging Silicon Valley electric car manufacturer. David has also provided high level launch strategy consulting leadership and support for BBC Worldwide America, for the U.S. launch of TopGear, as well as for Kleiner-Perkins backed Silicon Valley fuel cell start-up, Oorja Protonics.

David earned his B.A. in Comparative Literature from U.C. Berkeley, where he graduated with high-honors, and his J.D. from U.C. Hastings.
