Melissa Shavlik’s Summary

LL Cool J once rapped, "I can't live without my radio." The world has changed since 1985, but substitute radio for iPhone, Blackberry, or the next technology, and one can see the consumer relationship hasn't changed significantly. Convergence and co-evolution are key factors in the online environment. This blurring of mediums and roles has shaped my professional development. I have been fortunate to gain a notable amount horizontal growth in various areas; ranging from graphic design, project management, to marketing and public relations. I look forward to opportunities where I can exercise these skills in a challenging and fulfilling manner, especially using my technical knowledge to facilitate communication between clients and creatives.
