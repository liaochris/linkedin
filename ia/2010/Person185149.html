Steven Stern’s Summary

"I run IT departments, lead IT staff, and deliver strategic technology."

Steven is an Information Technology management professional with over 20 years of experience in association management, higher education, commercial education, and commercial real estate industries. He has hands-on technical skills in addition to his record as an effective manager who builds cohesive and effective team environments. He has expertise in both Windows and open-source environments, with a wide variety of software and systems, including web portals, services and feeds, social networks, blogging platforms, and desktop systems. As a member of the senior management team, Steven also has an excellent background in developing and implementing strategic plans and measures of performance. His training as a research scientist brings a highly organized methodology that meshes well with his ability to find creative opportunities and solutions.

General IT management, budgeting, systems administration, technology strategy, open source systems, Microsoft platforms, website development, balanced scorecards, dashboards. Technology consulting, strategic planning, project management, and implementation and operational support. Recent interests include website development with social networking components using open source tools such as WordPress and Drupal.

Looking for employment --
* Full or part- time: CIO or CTO, senior IT management positions
* Consultant: Research and advice for IT departments, IT Strategic Plan Alignment, part-time CIO/CTO
* Interim: Technology/Strategy consultant, website services and integration consulting, website creation, technology support for small businesses
