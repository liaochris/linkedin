David W. Buckman, AIA, LEED AP’s Summary

How great it is to help people by creating environments for them to work, sleep, heal, pray, and live in!
Buckman Architectural Group has been finishing up Tenant Fit-ups in several Medical Arts Buildings that were built in the last few years. It has been a pleasure working on Architectural and Interior Design with the managers and doctors of these medical practices to design spaces that are convenient for patients, efficient for staff, and cost affective for our clients!
We now look forward to providing the Interior Design and Architecture for Ambulatory Surgical Centers for several locations throughout New Jersey in the next year or so!
We also hope to continue providing pro bono Architectural services for our friends at HomeFreeHome.com and their clients who need assistance getting access to and from, and within their homes.
