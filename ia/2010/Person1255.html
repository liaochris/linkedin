Ashley Bazzone Gordon’s Summary

Provide a proactive interface to the job market for Right's candidates, talented employees in search of their next work opportunity. Consult with and train outplaced candidates around the most up-to-date and effective use of the internet in their job search.

Market these job-seekers through various strategic recruitment events - job fairs, specific industry or company spotlights, guest speakers with professional insight into hiring practices and referrals to appropriate contacts within the hiring community. Provide research and market intelligence to identify industries, hiring trends, and emerging opportunities.
