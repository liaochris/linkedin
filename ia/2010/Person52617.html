Dawn Rivers Baker’s Summary

For the past nine years, I have been writing and publishing one or another incarnation of The MicroEnterprise Journal (formerly Wahmpreneur News Magazine), a business news weekly for microbusinesses. In that role, I report on small business policy and politics, the economy and markets, and public and privately-funded research on microbusinesses.

I have also written and published a number of books under both the fiction and non-fiction imprints of Wahmpreneur Publishing: Wahmpreneur Books and Brighid's Fire Books.

I hope to expand my subscriber base for my newsletter and, ultimately, to expand the level of influence enjoyed by The MicroEnterprise Journal as representative of the voice and viewpoint of the vast majority of U.S. firms.

Dawn Rivers Baker’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, digital and print publishing, small business policy, the microbusiness economy, microbusiness statistics, non-employer statistics
