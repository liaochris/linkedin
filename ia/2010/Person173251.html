Rick Radermacher’s Summary

Creative-driven Marketing/Brand Communications professional, who has developed research-supported, strategically-focused communications that people remember and talk about. Can manage complexity and chaos with strong leadership and team-building skills. Combined client-side/agency pedigree fosters understanding and respect for both corporate and agency processes and personalities, plus the ability to develop trust and consensus.     AGENCY RELATIONSHIP "THERAPY" - Changing agencies is time-consuming and expensive.     • "PROCESS" CONSULTING - Agencies and clients speak different languages. Because we’ve been on both sides, we help you develop a more productive working process, leading to better work, less wailing and gnashing of teeth.

    • CONTRACT CONSULTING AND AUDITING - Fees. Commission. Mark-ups. Timesheets! Relax. We’ll help you structure a transparent contract, rooted in a specific scope of work. You and your agency can stop worrying about money, and start focusing on growing your business.

    AGENCY SEARCH CONSULTING - If you know deep down it’s time to change agencies, we’ll help remove most of the stress from the search. We manage the entire search for you, so you can stay focused on your regular job. There’s no bloated RFP process, just a concise approach to matching the right agency to fit your needs. We streamline the search, reducing cost and risk. You get an agency that shares your values and vision, which dramatically increases the probability of success.
