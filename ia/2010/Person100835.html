Joel Kessel’s Summary

Why I’m on LinkedIn:
I’m on LinkedIn to work with marketing and communications directors of companies, organizations and associations looking to increase their awareness, strengthen their brand, and drive long-term business growth.

I offer the following:
► Strategic communications counsel. I believe that implementation without a strong strategy—and tied to measurable goals—is a waste of time, money and effort. My job is to help clients define and deliver their message, support ongoing marketing efforts and strengthen their brand.

► Media relations. Effective media relations raises awareness, adds credibility and strengthens a company’s reputation. I know what is news and how to work with the media. Through a consistent, strategic media relations effort, I helped the National Runaway Switchboard increase incoming calls to its call center by 32 percent between 2003 and 2008.

► Key message development. Your audience—customers, public officials, media, or other key stakeholders—need to be reminded or informed about who you are and the value you provide. It starts with the messages I help you develop.

► Marketing communications. Consistent delivery of your message through a variety of channels strengthens a brand. That’s why I recommend a multifaceted approach when delivering your message. From your Web site to your newsletters and e-mail campaigns, there are many opportunities to consider when reaching your audience.

► Writing and editing. I do it every day from news releases, letters and Web sites, to newsletters, annual reports and marketing collateral. Copy that is professional and accurate, and aligns with the culture of your organization.

What I’m looking for on LinkedIn:
• Marketing directors, communications directors, public relations directors at companies with revenues of $5 million+, business to business opportunities, executive directors at statewide/national nonprofit organizations and trade associations.
