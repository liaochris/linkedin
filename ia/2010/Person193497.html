Steven Tedjamulia’s Summary

Steven Tedjamulia is a product manager for Bazaarvoice, the leader in social ecommerce. Steven is responsible for Bazaarvoice's social ecommerce community solutions. In addition to working for Bazaarvoice, Steven is the Chief Social Media Officer for IMNO a non-profit mentoring organization.

Steven has focused his career on social technologies and his entrepreneurial drive has led him to:

1. Found an eBusiness research center, which sponsored social media research.
2. Write several professional research papers focusing on social media.
3. Develop a community and collaboration architecture for a billion dollar company.
4. Create, manage, and own several social media products
5. Consult and advise several enterprise companies on their social media marketing and business strategies.
6. Manage several social media product lines for billion dollar companies such as Open Text and Vignette.

Steven has a wide range of experience in social media and he has not only led product development and marketing efforts, but he also has been a social media customer and consultant to many different fortune 1000 companies.

You can read more about different social media application's Steven is exploring at www.socialbeanbag.com
