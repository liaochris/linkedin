Kurt Ghekiere’s Summary

I am a engineer graduated in electronics (master), specialized in computer networking and microprocessor technology.
I was CEO for 15 years in a security and general electric company installing security systems (barries, fences, cctv, rod blockers) and high tech control access systems, both in belgium as 14 european countries.
I was CTO in a leading european clothing group merging at that moment several european an eastern companies together, my expertise was technical coaching of the ICT team and analisys of the operational and sales processes.
I am CTO in a Mid Size European petroleum group residing its own petrol stations, trucks, and bulk silo systems. I'm responsable for bringing all operational systems on line by VPN as head of an ICT team centralising al data into SQL warehousing system and designing furthermore the daily financial and operational reporting.
I am CTO and managing partner in one of the three largest independent B2B application service provider companies, owning two datacenters in Belgium and hiring rack space in 3 other european countries.
I am managing partner in several local and regional internet television projects, my expertise is technical setup and operational and since 2008 general management of several ICT companies.
