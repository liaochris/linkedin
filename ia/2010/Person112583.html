Katherine Kramer, MBA’s Summary

My experience is extremely diverse. I have a very unique mix of sales, corporate training, graphic design, customer service, construction, marketing, and real estate. I enjoy working in an environment where I can utilize these skills in solving problems and creating opportunities for myself and others. People who have worked with me tend to see me as a "facilitator."

Although I'm happy working in many diverse areas, my real passion is customer service. My recent MBA focus was on Services Marketing, where I studied under Dr. Leonard Berry, a recognized expert and pioneer in the field.

Combining this passion with construction, or high-tech with construction, gives me the opportunity to explore, learn, and thrive.

As a very outgoing, people-oriented person, I enjoy working with others and especially enjoy helping others gain knowledge that might enable them to achieve great things. I enjoy teaching and helping others find their potential, and see the success of my students as my real "portfolio."
