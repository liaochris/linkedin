Gustavo Morles’s Summary

I am a hands-on Chief Financial Officer/Comptroller with significant experience in startup, and private family-owned companies. I have senior management experience in strategy execution, and in corporate planning, in particular in the areas of forecasting, budgeting, and control. I have successfully deployed and integrated financial software and performance management tools like Balanced Scorecard, and Customer Relationship Management software. I have relevant experience in management and integration of acquisitions. My international experience includes the United States, Latin America, and the European Union. I speak French, Spanish and English fluently.
Since 2003, I have been teaching Graduate and Undergraduate Economics for Business, and Global Business Strategies as Associate Professor in the John Sperling School of Business at the University of Phoenix (South Florida Campus). I hold a Diplôme D'Etudes Aprofondies, Energy Economics Masters level program conducted entirely in French with emphasis on the international financial issues facing energy producers from Université de Paris-Sorbonnne Paris, France; and two Bachelor Degrees from The University of Tulsa (Tulsa, OK) one in Economics and one in Petroleum Engineering.

Gustavo Morles’s Specialties:

Business Planning, Strategy, Intelligence and Execution Consultant, Investment Banker and Entrepreneur with experience in Europe, Latin America and the US.
