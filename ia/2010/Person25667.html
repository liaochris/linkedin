Robert Schubert’s Summary

Commercial leader at Siemens, GE, ABB, Sermatech, and APV/Invensys for projects, products, and aftermarket in the power generation, energy, oil & gas, aerospace & defense, and general industries. Extensive international experience, including an ex-pat assignment in Paris, France. Successful in matrix, entrepreneurial, and hierarchical organizational structures. BSME, MBA, Six Sigma, Certified Energy Manager, and Certified Cogeneration professional. Conceived and implemented several innovative business initiatives generating over $400M in sales growth. Grew sales $35M through acquisition of three companies. Strengths include strategic planning, product and market development and commercialization, producing customer satisfaction, and goal-oriented team leadership. Professional philosophy consists of leading-by-example, encouraging lifelong learning, and exhibiting confidence and enthusiasm to achieve objectives.
