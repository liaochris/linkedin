Jeffrey K. Mittelman’s Summary

Why choose Washington BBI? Whether you want to buy, sell or build up the value of your business, the processes can be complex and intimidating. Let Washington BBI help you simplify your life and guide you through. Our proven methods are tailored to the needs of owners of mid-sized businesses, providing our clients with great service, guidance and support.

These services include:

Performance Analysis: Over the next five years, how much more valuable would your firm become if you could increase its value 10% in each year? Would you be more effective if you had monthly performance analysis tools that focus your attention on exactly those factors that most effect your business? We will analyze your financial statements every month, highlighting those factors that need your immediate consideration, those that you need to watch, and those that are working well. For a flat annual fee, we'll analyze your data and meet with you monthly to help you understand and interpret the data.

Business Acquisitions: Washington BBI can help you identify, evaluate and purchase business opportunities. We do the research and the leg work. You make the decisions. Thus, we save our clients time, money, effort and aggravation.

Seller Agency: Our Strategic Selling Process targets the owners of competitive firms in related fields.

Exit Planning: About 90% of business owners do not have an exit plan. Do you? An exit plan can make the difference between being forced out of business and successfully exiting at a time and in a manner of your choosing.

Strategic Marketing: You know that right now may be the best time to acquire competitors or to add capacity in other areas, but what should you buy? How do you know what will add the most value to your firm over time? Washington BBI can help you answer these questions and find the acquisition opportunities that can add strategic and competitive value to your firm.
