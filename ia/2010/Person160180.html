Patrick Witt’s Summary

I am currently working as an associate producer and assistant editor at Mars Advertising. I am responsible for internal edit projects, creating animations and graphics for department presentations, and handling dub/conversion requests. I have also been entrusted with organizing and managing their video archives and updating their department layout. I am a graduate of Specs Howard, earning a video production certificate, and U of M - Dearborn, having a BBA in marketing. Throughout my professional experience I have learned the technical aspects of video production, broadcast media, and web-based content. I have worked on a number of different sets and can balance time constraints and crew coordination with great attention to detail. In addition, I feel I possess a creative mind and a great "eye" which is showcased in a number of personal side projects, from product-based ads, to webisodes and sketches, to full-length documentaries. I also have post-production experience working with digital editing software (Final Cut Studios, Adobe After Effects). My talents lie in commercial and skit production, utilizing branding and advertising strategies with creative writing to sell a product. However, I have a passion for film and television that borders on obsession. Video production is not just my career, but also my life.

Marketing, advertising, video production, creative direction, videography, video editing, sound design and editing, branding, customer service, time management, and event planning
