    HafeziCapital is a Global Private Equity Firm and an International Consulting Firm, leveraging the insight of its professionals to generate extraordinary returns across a range of financial products and provide honest, insightful and experienced advice to our clients.

    HafeziCapital was established in 2008 with a mission to invest in undervalued assets stemming from the deleveraging of the global Stock Markets. The firm looks at undervalued projects, with a proven management team, as a catalyst for potential investment. The investments are across a diversified portfolio of companies, investment vehicles, and commodities. The firm investment strategy is global by nature merging international expertise with local insight, which enables us to encompass various sectors of growth

    HafeziCapital focuses on our clients short-term and long-term needs, via the establishment of value driven, fact based paths for growth and development. HafeziCapital is client centric with fact based analysis, development and execution of strategies as its core competency. Such strategies have saved companies millions of dollars in overhead and potential financial loses while preserving enterprise value..
