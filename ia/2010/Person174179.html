Roald Schoenmakers’s Summary

Traffic4u is a renowned Search Engine Marketing company. Due to its broad experience, reliability, and a result driven attitude, Traffic4u has become the most professional player in the search engine marketing field in the Netherlands. In 2007 it has launched it Spanish offices in Madrid and Salamanca.

Search engine marketing focuses on getting a visible placement of a website within search engines. Traffic4u makes sure that the websites of companies become and remain optimally visible within the most important national and international search engines like Google, Live (formerly known as MSN) and Yahoo!.

Traffic4u has very diverse client base including renowned companies such as Philips, L’Oreal, Akzo Nobel, Speurders, Mercedes and ING.
