Katie Swartz’s Summary

I am a Senior Consultant in the Strategy & Operations CFO Services group at Deloitte Consulting LLP. While at GE, I gained experience in financial planning and analysis, controllership, capital markets, business development and risk. Since joining Deloitte, I've expanded my skills to include financial systems strategy, financial governance, global financial reporting and global M&A process integration.
