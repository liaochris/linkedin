Debbie Cohen’s Summary

I am a non-traditional, visionary, Human Resource leader committed to improving organizational effectiveness to attract, develop, motivate and retain talent. My strong business acumen is coupled with the ability to balance the needs and interests of individuals with those of the broader organization. I have a sweet spot for realigning efforts that have lost focus or need redirection. I love the challenge of identifying and building connections that align, streamline and integrate concepts. Where others see complexity, my strong analytical skills let me interpret data for similarities, distinctiveness, and patterns. A source of pride is my history of building focused, productive, loyal teams.

My diverse background includes regional and multinational organizations in the entertainment, media, technology, professional services, medical and retail industries, in addition to government and not-for-profit sectors.
