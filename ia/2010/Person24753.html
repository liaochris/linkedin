Blake Gore’s Summary

Blake focuses his practice on the Healthcare and Education industries. He brings to Stanton Chase a successful background in clinical counseling, healthcare research and higher education leadership.

Blake is a National Certified Counselor and a Licensed Professional Counselor in the State of Mississippi, where he began his career as a psychotherapist. While working in the mental health industry, he honed an ability to work collaboratively with a diversity of clients and issues. Along with strong investigative skills and industry insight developed through published and award-winning healthcare research, he brings to his executive search practice extensive experience in psychometrics and knowledge of group dynamics.

In addition to his work in the healthcare industry, Blake also led corporate engagement strategies and career management services in both public and private institutions of higher learning. Along with solving complex organizational issues in academic affairs and driving quality student life initiatives, he served as a senior-level course instructor and founded an academic consulting business.

An active member of his community, he devotes his time and expertise to a number of professional associations and community service organizations. He is a member of the Leadership Healthcare Council of Nashville and recently served on the South Central Alabama Regional Council of the Arthritis Foundation and the American Cancer Society’s Junior Executive Board.

Blake is a summa cum laude graduate of the University of Mississippi where he received a Masters of Education in Counselor Education and a Bachelor of Arts. He is a member of The Phi Beta Kappa Society and the recipient of the Governor’s Award for Excellence in the Study of Politics.
