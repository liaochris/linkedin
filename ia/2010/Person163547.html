Chris White MD’s Experience

  • Director

    Essential Parenting

    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    April 2009 — Present (11 months)

    Essential Parenting is an educational and practice-oriented program that optimizes the developmental potential of both children and their parents. Based on a variety of teachings including Daniel Siegel’s Interpersonal Neurobiology, Ken Wilber’s Integral Theory, and the Diamond Approach created by Hameed Ali, Essential Parenting’s unique set of practices are designed to help you align with your children as they are in the present moment. By combining cutting-edge developments in Interpersonal Neurobiology with spiritual practices like mindfulness meditation, somatic practices, and inquiry, we empower you to return to your naturally wise and loving heart. In doing so, we support our children in becoming more fully themselves while also creating a ripple effect of wholeness, confidence, and well-being in our communities and on our planet.

  • Director

    Essential Coaching

    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    September 2006 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Essential Coaching is my version of the coaching I was trained in, Integral Coaching. Integral Coaching is a methodology of coaching developed by James Flaherty at New Venture West and is grounded in Integral Theory developed by Ken Wilber. In Integral Coaching, we look deeper then just the "presenting issue" and move to help change the mind and the body of the client. Through both "new ways of seeing" and "new ways of being" we support lasting change that is aligned with the clients goals and deeper desires for their life. See http://www.essentialparenting.com/coaching.html for a more detailed description of Essential Coaching possibilities.
