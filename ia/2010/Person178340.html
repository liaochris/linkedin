Reed R. Heustis, Jr.’s Summary

A member of the State Bar of California, Reed serves employers, insurance carriers, and claims administrators as a workers' compensation defense attorney for a large California law firm.

On a part-time basis through network marketing, Reed secures families and small businesses with affordable legal plans; identity theft restoration; and entrepreneurial opportunity with Pre-Paid Legal Services.

Reed earned his Juris Doctor in 1998 from San Diego's oldest legal academy, California Western School of Law; and his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science in 1993 from University of California at Irvine. In 2001 Reed graduated from the National Litigation Academy with a scholarship endowed by the Alliance Defense Fund, which advocates for religious freedom, the sanctity of human life, and biblical family values.

Reed and his wife raise three daughters, and are members of a local congregation affiliated with the Association of Reformed Baptist Churches of America.

His interests include baseball, soccer, fantasy sports, golf, politics, writing, classical music, constitutional law, theology, and network marketing.

Reed R. Heustis, Jr.’s Specialties:

Defending against excessive and unreasonable claims; negotiating fair settlements with legitimately injured workers; deposing applicants; interviewing witnesses; litigating in all venues in and around the Southern California area; drafting diary letters to claims administrators; advocating at Status Conferences, Expedited Hearings, Mandatory Settlement Conferences, Lien Hearings and Trials; disputing medical provider liens; and more.
