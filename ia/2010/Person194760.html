Steve and Lorri Hose’s Summary

﻿Thank you for your time today. Steve has been licensed since 1998, Lorri since 2000.

Over the years we have assisted hundreds of Clients with their Real Estate needs. Lorri enjoys working with Buyers and is an ABR or Accredited Buyers Representative and I
have found that sales and marketing as the Listing Agent is the challenge I enjoy. However, we both are fully engaged with all of our transactions and go “Above and Beyond The Clients Expectations for Customer Service!” When we work on your behalf you will be dealing with us directly.

We both have extensive backgrounds in Sales & Marketing. Lorri grew up in Florida, but has been in Colorado since 1973. She worked for over 20 years in the food industry working in restaurants and worked her way up to District Sales Manager for Nobel Sysco Foods. Her attention to detail and high level of customer service won her many awards during this tenure along with the hearts of her customers. When you ask Lorri to find you a home, she will spend however much time as you need and she won’t give up until she finds you your perfect home!

I am a Denver native and grew up in North Denver. Over the years I have worked in the packaging industry, construction sales and sold videos to supermarkets across the country. From small ticket items to million dollar sales of videos, we treat each listing with the same degree of respect and have listed and sold properties from $34,500 to $3,900,000. Whatever your needs are, WE are here for you..

We have a variety of No Cost tools to assist you with this process along with DVD’s or Videos, Home Staging Assistance, Free Market Analysis of your home or Free Daily Updates of homes that match your Dream Home Finder wish list.

Please contact us to send you our Customer Comments list of previous Clients. As always, your time and future consideration are greatly appreciated and we look forward to hearing from you.

Thank you,
