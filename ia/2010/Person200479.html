Gregg Gregory, CSP’s Summary

Is your help desk commonly referred to as the "Helpless Desk"? What is the culture surrounding teamwork and collaboration?

How well do your teams work with other departments within your organization?

What is the common thread between the bubonic plague, a category 5 hurricane, and teamwork? All three can have a disastrous impact when appropriate precautions are not taken.

Are your teams Synchronized? What happens if a twin engine plane or boat's engines are not running in sync? The ride is uncomfortable and rough and may be catastrophic.

Today, regardless of the organization, we are all in a team based environment today and there are 4 components essential to every team:

1) Quality Leadership
2) Better workplace relationships
3) Outstanding collaboration
4) Synchronized team members

Gregg works with organizations that are looking to create a culture where people work together better and increase overall productivity. As he puts it -- he helps people play nice in the sandbox.

Gregg's program, "The Art of Collaboration" focuses on getting everyone to play nice in the sandbox, and has received rave reviews from management and front line staff as well. Gregg gets attendees to understand the WHY in what they do and not just the HOW. When employees understand the WHY they will find the HOW on their own.

Gregg's "Team Based Service" program is about getting the correct image out to your customers. Whether internal or external, it is imperative that everyone delivers the consistent and powerful image you want to accomplish the mission vision and values for your organization.

"Synchronize Your Team" is about getting everyone to recognize the different dimensions of the team and how to make sure all aspects of the project are being accomplished.

After a Gregg Gregory program attendees have more

* Team Focus
* Team Cooperation
* Team Productivity
* Impact on everyone else
