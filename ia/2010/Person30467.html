Bojan Savic, Dr.’s Summary

Savic Patentanwälte provides a full range of services regarding prosecuting European, German and International patents, trademarks, and designs. We have substantial experience regarding opposition proceedings and appeal hearings before the European Patent Office as well as the nullity proceedings before the German Patent Court and the German Supreme Court. Further areas include infringement and litigation before the German Federal Patent Courts and German Civil Courts, drafting of patent as well as non-infringement and validity opinions, contracts, including patent licensing agreements and patent assignments.

Bojan Savic, Dr.’s Specialties:

Prosecution and challenging granted European patents in the pharmaceutical area and especially in the area of small molecules.
