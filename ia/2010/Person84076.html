Christine Nicholas’s Summary

I am an experienced transactional lawyer specializing in business sales and acquisitions, finance, commercial transactions (vendor and supplier contracts, trademarks, licensing agreements, joint development agreements and the like), real estate transactions (acquisitions and sales, development, finance and leasing), corporate governance and support of corporate secretary function, with experience in domestic and international transactions. I work collaboratively with my clients to effectively translate difficult legal issues into practical business solutions in a cost-effective manner.
