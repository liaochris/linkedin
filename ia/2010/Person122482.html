Laurent Blondeau’s Summary

Laurent's values can be summarized as a short quote "no masterpiece can't be done without passion". He makes this sentence his daily practice.
Founder and CEO of Buzzed-In, consultant in business development and strategy, he leads his life with lot of philosophy.

With now 13 years in media business (TPS, Canal+), pay TV, web TV, DSL TV and IT development and multi-specialized into commercial matters, from trade-marketing, direct approach, telemarketing and web business, he has become an expert in media, digital contents and contents promotion and every kind of TVs: pay, free, VOD and web tv...
Previously, he worked 6 years for Bouygues corporation in financial matters, treasurery and project management.

Laurent has natural abilities for innovation, development, searching new frontiers and pushing away the limits...but knows everything is done with people: such intention makes him an accurate and efficient manager for teams and project achievements.
He fall into blogging and social networking (finding him as "evidencesx" digital aka), to give, listen and share, because it's a logical way to reduce digital disruptives, and collaborate with others, all over the world. Writer on two blogs (personal and BuzzedInlog), he often takes positions on several top blogs, general or experts topics.
He's now convinced that media, can change the world, and reduce poverty, lack of information and education on "bottom of the pyramid" countries. He's involved in Global Media Center for Development, a large project with several main actors from Asia/Pacific zone. He feels his involvement is a way to give one more sense to his life.
