Rudy Wolff’s Summary

I have a background combining past, present and future, culture and commerce:

Originally, I am educated in culture history and have taught i.e. Norwegian, advertising, history and sociology at junior colleges.

I have also been politically active, with a variety of commissions and tasks, both locally and at a county level: Secretary, reporter, treasurer, auditor, tutor, board member, county leader, electioneer etc.

In addition to this, I have written articles for local trend magazines, specializing in fashion, beauty, home decoration, restaurants and other lifestyle subjects.

After this, I started Master of Managment studies in i.e. innovation and marketing, besides working as a fashion designer with my own brand. Only half a year after the debut show in Oslo, Rudy Wolff as a brand is about to go international.
