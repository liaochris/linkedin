Vielka Holness’s Summary

A seasoned academic and student affairs professional with close to 15 years of higher education experience and an exceptional track record of managing student and academic affairs programs in areas including career development, academic advisement, leadership development, multicultural affairs, student services, international student services, judicial affairs and orientation programs. Distinguished career in student affairs, law school administration, higher education law and litigation. Lead high achieving teams that consistently exceed expectations.
