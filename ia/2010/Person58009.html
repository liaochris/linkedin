Donald (Don) Petry III’s Summary

Mr. Petry has over 13 years of experience with solutions analysis, rapid technology exploration, design, implementation, production support, information technology resource management, standards based full-cycle development (SDLC), documentation, quality assurance and a variety of hardware infrastructures.
