Darren Scott’s Summary

Darren has over 13 years Design & Art Direction experience having joined McCann-Erickson in the mid 90’s. He spent most of his agency time producing award winning work for large national and international brands incuding: Durex, Manchester United, Sony, Britannia, CP Hart, Shell,
Sainsbury’s Bank, UPS, Aldi, Coca-Cola and Peugeot to name a few.

Darren’s work has won numerous design awards from the Roses National Design Awards to Design Week International Design Awards and has featured in many books on both design and typography.

Also during the last 13 years Darren has been the owner of Darren Scott Typographics (DST), a digital type foundry that designs and retails custom font designs and produces custom typography for clients.

DST has created custom fonts for the likes of Coca-Cola and Europay International and and has his general release typefaces in use by the likes of IPG Magazines, Carling and Kelloggs to name a few.

Darren and Jane Kaye (also ex-McCann-Erickson), set up Truth Design in 2006 with the intention of creating a small boutique design agency that has an honest problem solving approach to the design process in terms of both - what is best for your brand, and what it should cost. Offering the big brand thinking and experience to a growing range of clients.

Darren Scott’s Specialties:

Brand Identity Design, Brand Consultancy, Design for Print, Typography, Digital Font Design and Graphic Design & Art Direction.
