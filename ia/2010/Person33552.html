Cary Kozlov’s Summary

I've worn more than one hat over the years...one hat in the entertainment business as a talent agent, personal manager, comedy nightclub manager, script analyst, and musician, and the other hat in the law library realm as a consultant to law firms and managing their print law collections. Both have been and continue to be very fulfilling experiences.

I guess it started when I managed my own band back when I was 15 years old. Because we were too young to drive, our parents had to drive us to our gigs and rehearsals. First show business gig...my twin cousins' sweet 16 party at The Palmer House in Chicago. It was a terrifying experience as we played our entire repertoire...a grand total of seven...mostly Herb Alpert & The Tijuana Brass and Baja Marimba Band tunes. Let's just say we weren't about to open for the Stones. Things haven't really changed much since then except my parents aren't around to drive me to my meetings these days.

My current literary clients include Emmy award-winning writer Anne Beatts (National Lampoon, SNL and creator of CBS series "Square Pegs" that launched Sarah Jessica Parker's career), Emmy-award winning writer Rick Overton (Dennis Miller Live and numerous movie and TV roles), producer-director-writer-casting executive Eve Brandstein, producer-director-writer-comedian Vic Dunlop, and some extremely talented and promising newcomers. As personal manager I represented actress Kathy Garver (known best for her role as Cissy on the popular CBS series, "A Family Affair") and actor/comedian Joe Nipote (known best for his role as Frankie Waters on NBC and later syndicated "Viper" series).

As a law library consultant and manager, I service small to small/medium sized law firms with one to 20 attorneys. Most of these firms have been my clients for over a dozen years. Prior to that I was a library assistant at The Los Angeles County Law Library, and at the Los Angeles offices of Sidley & Austin one of the 10 largest law firms in the world.
