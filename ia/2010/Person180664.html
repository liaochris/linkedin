Stuart Wood’s Summary

Domain name sales & acquisition, content writing, keyword analysis, domain management, traffic building, search engine specialist, domain name appraisals, research analyst, and web research expert.

Stuart has developed a custom domain name appraisal system which has been used by a number of clients to accurately price their premium domain names for sale and/or acquisition. The system is not automated and subjective. The simple truth is any one domain name is only worth what a single individual is willing to pay for it.

Real & Virtual estate development. Management of web design & development for numerous sites; to include WiFi.com, Cessation.com, Scams.net, and a large portfolio of Geo-"wifi" and "organic" domain names.

Domain name investor, Domainer, Domainers.

Extensive experience managing Pay-Per-Click (PPC) campaigns - upwards of 20 search engine marketing campaigns simultaneously - including four distinctive industries. Google, Yahoo!, GoClick, and MSN.

Comprehensive knowledge of Google Adwords, AdSense, and Google Analytics. Prolific user of Google Webmaster Tools - managing numerous websites for Salient Properties and clients.

Quantitative and Qualitative researcher: from methods development to execution or consultation in the Consumer Packaged Goods industry. Extensive experience problem-solving and building studies for determining the relevance of products to the consumer.

Professional in-home interviewer and ethnographer. Ethnographic research and participant observation as specialties. Professional report writer.
