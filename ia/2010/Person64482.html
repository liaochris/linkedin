Emily Aiken’s Summary

I'm a brand strategy consultant. I have extensive experience delivering value during the start-up, re-positioning and market extension stages of companies, products and services. I effectively develop and direct action in both new and old media market channels. I've helped launch emerging technologies, entertainment networks, software companies, design firms and personality brands. I speak the language of the consumer and am proficient at aligning a brand across all platforms, whether it is a banner ad campaign, a graphic user interface or an investor dog and pony. I love what I do as much as I love learning and reading the drift of the marketplace and the innovative forces powering it. And boy... does it ever change, every day.

Emily Aiken’s Specialties:

> Identifying new markets and executing market extensions
> Identifying and seducing strategic partners
> Creating identity collateral and media for all marketing channels
> Managing brand and infrastructure aspects of culture change
