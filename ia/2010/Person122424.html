Lauren Reskin’s Summary

Miami native deeply involved in the evolution and progression of the South Florida independent music scene. I've worked in music retail since 1999, opening my own store, Sweat Records, in 2005.

Besides running Sweat I throw weekly club nights, book local and national acts, DJ, coordinate events, manage bands, publish a zine, edit a blog, and beyond. If you need something done right in Miami, I can usually help or at least find you someone who can. Contact me directly at lolo[at]sweatrecordsmiami[dot]com.

Lauren Reskin’s Specialties:

PR, event production and promotion, music buying, retail management, booking, A&R, artist management, merchandising, branding and identity, art direction, graphic design, copy editing, DJing, music licensing, online marketing, making connections.
