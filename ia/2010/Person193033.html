Stacey Padiernos’s Summary

Born in San Francisco and educated in mass communications and creative marketing with the ability to sense, interpret, strategize, and to think outside of the box. I have an obsession to simplify user interaction on the web and to create beautiful and clean design.

I have become a culturally and socially well rounded individual through life changing experiences and exploration. Some of my best experiences were from studying several different cultures in Hawaii and submerging myself in the beauty of the underwater world off the coasts of Bohol and Palawan in the Philippines. From these experiences, they have pushed me to explore various outlets of art and design.
