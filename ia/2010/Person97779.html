John Galloway’s Summary

I consult and advise on environmental business strategy and technical implementation of environmentally focused initiatives for diverse clients, including private sector businesses, non-profit organizations, and government agencies. My areas of focus are renewable energy technology, energy use and reduction, greenhouse gas management, waste reduction and recycling, and public policy engagement. I have a strong background in energy policy, utility matters, and regulatory affairs, having represented clients and organizations before state and federal agencies. I bring a passion for environmental stewardship and resource responsibility to your organization’s efforts to push ahead of the sustainability curve.
