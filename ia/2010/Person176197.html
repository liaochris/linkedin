Justin Beckett’s Summary

My passion is design. Few things thrill me more than sitting back and seeing a clean, tightly designed page at the end of the night. I have a heavy interest in Adobe products, both in their ability to crossover between one another and the future that offers for the industry. At work, I primarily use InDesign, InCopy and Photoshop from CS1 while at home I have CS2 to design my own projects.

My overall goal is to continue my work in print media with an emphasis on learning different design trends and techniques incorporated both by fellow designers and other publications. Though there is no singular right way to prepare a quality page, there are many ideas and I believe a knowledge of as many as possible can only help to enhance my skills.
