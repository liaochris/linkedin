Jonathan Ploeger’s Summary

I have been in practice since December of 2007 and curretnly own and operate Ploeger Chiropractic Center in Forest Lake, MN. My professional goals are to establish a successful office that serves humanity and betters the community I live in. I offer this service with as few limitations as possible. For more information look to our website or call for an appointment. We look forward to serving you, your friends, and family.

Jonathan Ploeger’s Specialties:

I specialize in finding and removing vertebral subluxation. Subluxation prevents your body's nervous system from controlling all elements of life, which in turn decreases your overall quality of life. We Help Others Live Efficiently and that makes us WHOLE.
