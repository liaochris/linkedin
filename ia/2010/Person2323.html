Adam Gillman’s Summary

I have extensive experience in marketing and product development with an emphasis on branding, user experience design, monetization strategy, and user acquisition.

I am currently the Director of Marketing and Product Development at meetMoi, a company specializing in the development of mobile dating and social utility applications. I'm also the Co-Founder of TechiesGiveBack.org, a non-profit focused on driving volunteerism within the NY and LA tech communities.

  • Co-Founder

    TechiesGiveBack.org

    (Philanthropy industry)

    November 2009 — Present (6 months)

    TechiesGiveBack.org is dedicated to unifying the New York tech industry for the purpose of improving the local community through volunteering and fundraising.

    For our inaugural event in December 2009, we partnered with Foursquare to raise funds for the NY-based non-profit CampInteractive. Foursquare agreed to auction off the leader board in a bidding process that was won by Pepsi. For every point earned on Foursquare during the week of our fundraising effort, 4 cents were donated to CampInteractive. Between Pepsi's donation and other efforts, we raised over $12k.

    We also held an ice skating event for the children of CampInteractive in which we brought down 70 volunteers and partnered them with 40 kids at Chelsea Piers ice skating rink.

    Due to the publicity the initiative received, Camp Interactive was able to raise additional funds in excess of $50k.

  • Director of Marketing and Product

    MeetMoi, LLC

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    As the Director of Marketing and Product at meetMoi, I have five primary responsibilities.

    First and foremost, I oversee all of our user acquisition efforts. This includes the supervision of our marketing managers, facilitating new relationships for paid acquisition, and optimization of all advertising efforts.

    Second, I manage all product design and development. This includes the creation of product and technical specifications, overseeing the development and design of new products and features, management of our technical team during coding and release processes, and formulation of meetMoi's long term product strategy.

    Third, I handle our monetization efforts. Most specifically, this entails leading efforts for customer retention and increasing the lifetime value of our subscribers.

    Fourth, I manage all client accounts for meetMoi's white label services. This includes day to day account management, providing marketing strategy for partners, and overseeing our technical team during development of partner branded products.

    Lastly, I supervise our profile approval and customer service teams.
