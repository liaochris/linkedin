Rob Gutman’s Summary

I focus on listing and selling residential properties throughout Fairfield County including Ridgefield, Redding, Wilton, Danbury, Stamford, Norwalk, Bethel, Newtown and more.

Many banks hire me directly to market and sell their REO properties (foreclosures). They know that my only job is to sell real estate in a business-like manner making sure every detail is addressed to all parties' satisfaction.

I am licensed to practice in both Connecticut and New York.

My primary concern is satisfying the needs of my buying and selling clients. I am a full-time professional realtor based at the Keller Williams Realty office on Main Street in Ridgefield.

My record of closing deals spans over 20 years and my sales career has taken me from Wall Street to Main Street. My resume includes a Finance MBA from The Stern School of Business and stops at Salomon Brothers, Hyperion Software and as an entrepreneurial business-owner prior to starting my successful career in Real Estate.

I actively write my real estate blog called Real Estate Chocolate (you'll have to contact me if you want to find out why I chose this name). You can find the blog at www.RealEstate-Chocolate.com.

Call me for anything: 203-313-0414

"Must Read" blog: www.RealEstate-Chocolate.com
