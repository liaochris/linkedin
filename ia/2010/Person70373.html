Edgar Garcia’s Summary

Been in Sports Television since 1986 working with ABC Sports and ESPN as a PA, Runner and Utility. In 1989 got my big break with WPLG Ch-10 Miami ABC as a AD and Tech Director for news and special programing. The got the opportunity to in 1990 to work with a sports production company in South Florida and a new Sports Start up network called Sunshine Network producing magazine and sports special with the Miami Heat, University of Miami, Florida Marlins, Florida Panthers, FIU and FAU. along with many celebrity golf shows. In 1993 i joined Adcraft Sports Marketing as a Sports Producer/Director/Videographer and Editor, i was in charge of all the half hour programs with the University of Miami Coaches shows and Pre and Post season specials along with producing the year end highlight film for the football team ala NFL Films style production. In addition i produced over 120 PGA Golf Almanac Shows for the PGA of American airing Nationwide on Prime Sports Network, Sports Channel and local over the air stations. other responsibilities included final delivery of shows to networks and station relations.

In 1997 i joined Sports Channel Florida later to become Fox Sports Florida and Front Row Communications a division of Wayne Huizinga Sports Holdings which included the MLB Florida Marlins, NHL Florida Panthers and NFL Miami Dolphins.
I was responsible for producing and shooting special projects for the network.

In 1998 i started FloridaTVFilmCrews.com and Garcia Production Group, Inc. and provided high end broadcast crews for production companies and networks across the country and around the world with broadcast camera packages from Betacam, DV and Now HD. some of my past projects included shows for Discovery Channel, BBC, Fox News, History Channel, A&E, WE Network, Food Network, and Court TV to name a few.

I currently work out of two offices in Ft. Lauderdale, Fl and New York City, and can provide full service Video, Television and Webcasting production.

Edgar Garcia’s Specialties:

In 2004 i joined in strategic partnership with Foglight Entertainment in New York to produce several shows for History Channel called "Brothers in Arms the untold story of the 502", and for STARZ Network "Hollywood Goes Gaming" and "Comics Unbound". along with several on line projects for New LIne Cinema including "Bank Job", "24", "Journey to the center of the earth" and "Semi Pro."
