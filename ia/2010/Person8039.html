Allen Wilson’s Summary

My name is Allen Wilson.

I have nearly 4 years experience working as an Industrial Designer / inventor in a consulting, engineering and manufacturing firm.

My creative duties range from product-concept ideation-sketching to digital sculpting, prototype building, graphics and packaging design, testing and overseas quality control, and I hold 6 co-inventor patents (more pending).

I've held key roles in designing products for Netflix, Polycom, Husky and Giraffe reach system tools, which are sold through Home Depot, Lowes, Target, Frontagte and Skymall. I conceptualized the user-interface redesign for Topcon's GPS software.

Simply stated; I love to make useful products visually and functionally alluring.

I thrive on the challenge of melding the mechanical and aesthetic.

I have a Bachelor of Science degree from San Jose State university and an Associates of Arts Degree in Fine Arts from Columbia Jr. College. I graduated in 2007 with honors as a dean's scholar with 8 design related scholarships.

Again, my name is Allen Wilson.
