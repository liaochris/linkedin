Jeff Stovall’s Summary

Jeff Stovall is a business and information technology executive with a successful track record in top-rated organizations across multiple sectors. He is skilled in all aspects of technology program leadership, from strategy development through implementation and on-going operation. Jeff is experienced in executing a wide variety of change initiatives including IT merger integration, large-scale corporate transformation, and divisional systems evolution with a demonstrated ability to drive superior business outcomes and financial results.

Jeff earned his MBA from the Darden Graduate School of Business Administration, University of Virginia, and his BS in Mechanical Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
