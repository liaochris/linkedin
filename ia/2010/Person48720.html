  • President & Chief Creative Officer

    Coach Darcy LLC

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    December 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Through Coach Darcy LLC, my primary focus is on coaching and mentoring aspiring leaders, especially:

    --People on the verge of–-or who want to accelerate--a promotion, job change, or other significant recognition;

    --High energy professionals seeking more guidance and support than their leader, manager, or company is able to provide;

    --Mid-career high performers feeling “stalled out” in their growth and needing a jump start to reach their goals faster.

  • Principal, Internal Coach, and Senior Communication Consultant

    Hewitt Associates

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; HEW; Human Resources industry)

    December 2007 — December 2008 (1 year 1 month)

    Designed, launched and led an innovative Associate Coaching program, designed to support associate retention and growth in ways that made a tangible difference to Hewitt and its clients. Coached associates one-on-one, plus coached teams responsible for serving multi-million dollar, complex client relationships. Results from the coaching program included achieving double-digit productivity gains for one client-facing team, accelerating promotions for 50% of the coached associates, and 100% participant agreement that the outcomes of the program were worth the time and cost to the business.

  • Principal, Communication Consulting Business Leader, Southeast,

    Hewitt Associates

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; HEW; Human Resources industry)

    2001 — July 2008 (7 years )

    Led 40-person regional Communication and behavior change consulting practice for leading HR consulting firm. Responsible for developing successful people and teams, helping clients create better results, and managing a healthy, growing business.

    As part of North American Leadership team, designed learning initiatives for emerging leaders and people managers, including creating a comprehensive leadership development course for successor candidates covering key topics including financial acumen, negotiation, managing others, and strengthening personal presence. Also designed and led year-long, bite-sized learning programs for people managers, helping managers across the country access information quickly so they could make better decisions and take action.
