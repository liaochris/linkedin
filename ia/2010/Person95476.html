Jennifer Jacobson’s Summary

Jennifer Jacobson is both a social media visionary and results-driven PR professional who accepts nothing less than powerful publicity for the companies and causes she promotes. Her clients have been featured in "The New York Times," "Oprah & Friends," and "The Huffington Post."

With a Masters Degree in Broadcast Communications Jennifer understands the relationship between consumers and media. As former Vice President of Marketing for a social media company and founder of Jacobson Communication, Jennifer excels in generating high impact social media campaigns and content. Her clients include LookSmart Ltd., Impact Marketing Group, Crowd Science and PNN.com. She works with startups, internet companies, authors, and artists, including MerchantCircle, SquareTrade Inc., ImShopping, and Gallery 2611.

Author of the upcoming "42 Rules of Social Media for Small Business" and founder of Jacobson Communication, Jennifer has high-level and real world experience in creating effective awareness campaigns across various media spaces.
