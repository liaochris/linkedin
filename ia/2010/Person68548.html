Evan Shelan’s Summary

Evan is the creator & founder of eZforex.com, Inc. He graduated from University of Texas in Austin with a BA in economics. While creating a niche in the travel industry for over 15 years, he developed eZforex.com, as an adjunct to the travel industry.

In 1994 he transformed the foreign currency exchange business from brick and mortar to a web-based digital environment where he developed a business-to-consumer model of selling foreign exchange services over the internet through travel portals.

In 1996, he created the nations first web based foreign currency exchange network for travel portals and travel related companies. Today with more than 3,000 industry users, eZforex.com has grown into a multi-channel business including the following affiliates: Banks, Bankers Bank, Credit Unions, Corporate Credit Unions, Corporations, Online Travel Portals, & Colleges/Universities.

In 2001, he attended The Wharton School at UPENN. There, he was named a Wharton Fellow" in 2001. This fellowship combines research-based knowledge and hands-on learning to train leaders to develop new ebusiness models and set new, secure directions for his organization utilizing the Internet. There he learned to enhance this transformation process from a brick and mortar to a 100% web based digital environment, while creating a (B2C) business-to-consumer & (B2B) business-to-business model to sell foreign currency through the Internet. He was inducted as a Wharton Fellow for Life.

Evan has delivered lectures throughout the United States to the banking industry. In addition, he has written articles on the Euro for Fox Market Wire in New York and has been featured in numerous travel industry magazines and national newspapers. He currently serves as VP and program co-chairman of the Dallas/Ft. Worth Wharton Alumuni Association.
