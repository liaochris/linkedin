Lesley Forst’s Summary

I recently received my M.A. in Environmental Studies at USC. I received my B.A. in Environmental Studies and Psychology in May of 2009 from USC. I am very interested in pursuing a career in which I can combine these two fields. I believe my studies give me a unique perspective on human behavior in regard to the environment and how to alter this behavior to be more environmentally conscious. Throughout my time at USC I was involved in on campus environmental organizations as well as volunteer recruitment for the University's alternative break program. My graduate level course work emphasized coastal management with a field studies component in various regions of Southern California. I also completed a directed research project which focused on water policy in the Southwestern United States.
