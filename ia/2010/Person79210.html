Gwynne Kostin’s Summary

I've been lucky enough to drive the use of new technologies to solve business problems--from applying new media technologies to old government communications to transforming the way that thousands of medical students apply for residency.

I created the first policy blog for a Cabinet level agency, developed a video communications channel--from policy development to building production capacity--built a government-wide web 2.0 emergency communications strategy, and led the integration of web properties and policies following a merger of 22 agencies into a single department. I created a multi-million dollar annual revenue stream by bringing the application to medical residency online, and I used the Internet to successfully increase meeting and publications sales. I have overseen the launch of multiple websites, applications and projects--leading strategy, technology and communications.

I am confident in both IT and communications--with extensive experience in applying new media to solve operational and communications problems, marketing, branding and change management. I am curious and creative, thrive on building systems, and am always looking to apply my energy and brains to the next project.

Gwynne Kostin’s Specialties:

Strategically using blogs, video sharing, wiki's, twitter, widgets, and other social and electronic media; multi-channel communications, marketing and production including print and Web; application development and product management; search engine optimization: web analytics; and brand development. Experience in government, nonprofit, education, healthcare, start-ups and disaster and crisis communications. Accomplished speaker and writer.
