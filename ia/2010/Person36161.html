Charlie Bernard’s Summary

Charlie Bernard is the Founder, President and CEO of CCB Strategies, LLC., a boutique full service sports/consumer marketing and public/media relations company.

CCB Strategies: Strategic-Connected-Creative-Aggressive.

Charlie's work in the sports industry began in 2000 as he spearheaded Colburn Basketball Competitions (CBC), an enterprise aimed at providing greater Hudson Valley area youth the opportunity to participate in a competitive tournament style basketball environment. He drove initiatives in all aspects of the execution of the event for three years from Sponsorship, Event planning and execution, Public Relations, Grass-Roots Marketing, Budgeting and Stakeholder analysis.

From CBC Charlie accepted a position as an Account Coordinator at Octagon Sports Marketing working in Marketing and Public Relations on a variety of accounts in the Action Sports, Golf, Soccer, and individual Athlete/Event industries.

Following Octagon, Charlie accepted a position as an Account Executive working his way up to Account Supervisor at 5W Public Relations for 2 1/2 years managing a variety of accounts anchored in sports, business, consumer, consumer technology and entertainment, and helping to build a sports industry focused division.

Following 5W, Charlie accepted a position as the Director, Consumer and Sports Marketing at The Hamilton Group, a full service Marketing/Public Relations firm in NYC. He gained valuable experience working on premier sporting events, such as The Barclays PGA Tournament, as well as The Turning Stone Resort Championship and various other entities.

Charlie Bernard’s Specialties:

Public/Media Relations, Sports Marketing, Corporate Communications, Sponsorship Consulting, Athlete/Entertainment Representation, Business/Network Marketing.
