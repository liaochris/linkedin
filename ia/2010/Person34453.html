Chris Schultz’s Summary

I started Voodoo Ventures, LLC in 2003 as an internet incubator focused on conceiving, building, and operating successful internet ventures. We currently operate a portfolio internet companies, including siteMighty, Inc, and Flatsourcing, Inc, in addition to a network of affiliate marketing websites.

Our portfolio projects with Voodoo Ventures are:
* siteMighty.com is web publishing platform for affiliate marketing websites
* Flatsourcing.com is in development and will be a outsourcing marketplace with a built in with project management tool set
* Huckabuck.com was a metasearch engine that we developed in 2006 and sold in August of 2006

We were honored as one of the New Orleans Innovators of the Year for 2006 for the launch of Huckabuck by New Orleans City Business Magazine.

Prior to Voodoo Ventures, I built Destination VIP, Inc, and travel focused internet company, that was acquired by VegasHotSpots, LLC in March of 2005.

I earned an MBA with concentration in Entrepreneurship from University of Southern California in 2002 and a Mechanical engineering degree from the University of Virginia in 1996.

Chris Schultz’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur first and foremost. Internet marketing skills. Branding and experiential marketing. Launching new companies.
