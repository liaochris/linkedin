Stephanie Golden’s Summary

I'm an award-winning author of eight books (including five collaborations with expert authors), copywriter for nonprofits and small businesses, grantwriter, website content writer, and journalist. My gift is to write or edit in the style that best reflects each client.

Services:
--newsletters & copywriting
--book ghostwriting & collaboration
--website content
--grantwriting
--program & training manuals
--book doctoring/editing

Books include:

The Permanent Pain Cure: The Breakthrough Way to Heal Your Muscle and Joint Pain for Good (co-author with by Ming Chew, PT), McGraw-Hill, May 2008

The Ultimate Body Rolling Workout: The Revolutionary Way to Tone, Lengthen, and Realign Your Body (co-author with Yamuna Zake), Broadway Books, 2004

Under the Mask: A Guide to Feeling Secure and Comfortable During Anesthesia and Surgery (co-author with James E. Cottrell, M.D.), Rutgers University Press, 2001

See my website for more of my books.

Websites I wrote:

www.permanentpaincure.com
www.mingmethod.net

I also write and edit reports and program manuals for nonprofits.

Stephanie Golden’s Specialties:

Writing genres: self-help, literary nonfiction, feature stories, training and program manuals, reports, newsletters.

Fields of writing expertise: arts and education, conventional medicine, alternative (integrative/complementary) medicine, health & fitness (including alternative systems of movement and therapy), women's issues, psychology, spirituality.
