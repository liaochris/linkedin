John Wechsler’s Summary

John Wechsler is an entrepreneur with experience in start-up and high-growth firms. He started his career with IBM in sales and marketing positions and has been a founding-level and/or C-level executive in high-tech firms since 1999 including beenz.com, Wishoo, Vontoo & FormSpring. John is a past-president of EO Indiana (Entrepreneurs’ Organization); a past committee member and semi-finalist judge for the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards; and immediate past-chairman of NPower Indiana, a non-profit helping other non-profits with their technology needs.

start-up, business planning, strategy, private equity / fundraising, business development, competitive analysis, sales, sales management, channels, marketing, product management, M&A and general business management.
