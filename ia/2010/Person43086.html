Jeff SKI Kinsey’s Summary

The right people first—then flawless execution™

Founder and Senior Consultant at Throughput.us Jeff ‘SKI’ Kinsey is an entrepreneur, a Constraints Management practitioner, Theory of Constraints (TOC) Jonah, author, publisher, open source software developer, SocMedia producer, public speaker, educator and mentor.

SKI applies Lean Thinking, Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM), sDBR (Simplified Drum-Buffer-Rope), Lean Six Sigma and consults with senior executive teams to develop business strategies and tactical plans. Authored "Purple Curve Effect" (Throughput Press, 2004). Degree in Business Management Technologies from Kent State University (received “Special Recognition Award”), SKI was recently licensed to facilitate the Solopreneur's Sales & Marketing Advisory Boards. Also taught Computer Science for ten years, keynotes manufacturing events, and leads industry breakout sessions on TQM/TQL, TOC, Lean Six Sigma, eCommerce, and Internet Marketing. SKI has numerous articles published in business journals dating back to 1998. Completed additional training in Quality Control and Business Process Engineering at Goal Systems International and A.Y. Goldratt Institute. Received a Letter of Commendation from the USMC for process improvement.
