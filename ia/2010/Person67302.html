Kevin Wilson’s Summary

View current resume at: http://kevinwilson.erpgenie.com/
View my author profile on Amazon at: http://www.amazon.com/Kevin-Wilson/e/B0037OKY0W

I'm the co-founder of ERPGenie.COM (formerly SAPGenie.COM) the largest independent online web resource for ERP consultants on the web. I am the webmaster for all of our websites and manage all parts of operations regarding them.

In addition, as part of the thought leadership team at Q Data I provide consulting services to clients in the areas of EDI / ALE, workflow, XI and SAP Event Management.
Having worked in the SAP field since 1996, I have been fortunate to have worked many projects in the interfacing / integration areas of SAP.

My primary focus now, as an expert in the SAP Event Management area, is to bring this "Best kept secret" to the forefront and show companies what they are missing by not implementing this functionality and reaping the benefits. Take a look at http://www.qdatausa.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=116&Itemid=73 for more on SAP EM. Also take a look at my newly published book on SAP EM entitled "SAP Event Management - SAP's best kept secret" at http://www.geniepress.com/books/sapem.htm.

Prior to my SAP career I worked on implementing large scale integrated information systems where I was both project manager and workflow / image management guru.

I'm also focused on expanding our Genieholdings.com business and making ERPGenie.COM THE place to go, when searching for a job or ERP content, on the web..... podcasts, authoring a book, launching new websites, ...
