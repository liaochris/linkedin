Devon Takenouchi’s Summary

I have a wide array of professional experience from outpatient drug and alcohol counseling (youth and adult), intensive outpatient group facilitation, co-occurring disorders, long term co-occurring disorders residential, developing and implementing family, school based and detention programs, and nearly 3 years of supervisory experience of a youth program. I also have experience working with the judicial and legal system which includes probational officers, detention facilities and the Juvenile Drug Court program.

I have trained numerous staff who have gone on to complete their Chemical Dependency Professional certification through the State of Washington.
