Olessia Smotrova-Taylor’s Summary

OST Global Solutions Inc. (OST) is a full-service consulting company helping businesses grow by winning large government contracts. We offer consulting in capture planning (pre-proposal preparation), proposal management, and writing support; training; and direct placement. We work in a variety of industries in the federal defense and civilian sectors, public sector, and even commercial business-to-business and foreign government bids.

We provide turnkey proposal services, or specific support in the following categories:
• Business Developers
• Capture Manager
• Proposal Managers
• Proposal Coordinators
• Volume Managers
• Proposal Writers
• Graphic Artists
• Desktop Publishers
• Orals Coaches
• Pricers/Cost Strategists
• Schedulers

OST uses its own streamlined and well-documented process based on the latest best practices and trends. Our approach yields more than 68% savings over the traditional approaches to capture and proposal development. Most importantly, our approach is one of the key factors in reaching the win rate of 94% for the single award, and 99% for multiple award contracts. We have won more than $16 billion in government contracts and have contributed to growing several businesses from small or mid-size to large.

Get a free 19-page report on How to Build a Federal Opportunity Pipeline that works at our website, www.ostglobalsolutions.com.

Also, consider registering for the new one-time only webinar series starting May 12, 2010: "Blueprint for Winning Government Contracts for Small Businesses" at http://www.ostglobalsolutions.com/blueprint. The course also provides the full system complete with checklists, templates, and other tools.
