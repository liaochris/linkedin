Roy Zimmerhansl’s Summary

This marks 30 years of experience in the securities services industry with the past 18 years dedicated to the securities lending markets. Broad perspective in securities services gained over a career with investment banks, custodian banks, a central depository and currently an interdealer broker. Experienced in both leading an existing business as well as launching new business lines at banks.

It's all about information, education and transparency. The combination of consulting, my training company and leading the editorial side of two magazines places me in a unique position to inform and help shape the securities finance industry in the future.

Check out my securities lending blog: http://securitieslending.typepad.com

Roy Zimmerhansl’s Specialties:

Securities lending, Securities finance education, ETFs, Emerging Markets, electronic trading platforms, Custody
