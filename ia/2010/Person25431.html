Bob Gilbert’s Summary

Bob is co-founder of The Big Sky Group, a holding company that operates two companies; Travika, Meetings International. He was also co-founder and a principle with HPN Global, the world's most global site selection and meeting procurement company.

Bob's primary responsibility is CEO of Travika (www.travika.com). Travika creatively and effectively assists travel and hospitality related companies to grow international business. It currently operates in over 25 countries on behalf of its current clients portfolio.

Bob has over 35 years of travel and hospitality marketing experience from 5 star luxury to mid-price brands and has provided consulting, marketing and sales services to many organizations including Hilton Hotels Corp, Orbit Travel, Globetrotter, Samsonite, Big Mountain Resort and Travel Agent Magazine.

His résumé includes leading the global marketing efforts of major brands: VP Sales and Travel Operations, Walt Disney Attractions, VP Walt Disney Travel Company, VP Worldwide Marketing & Sales, Best Western International, the worlds largest hotel company, EVP HelmsBriscoe International, SVP with SuperClubs, one of the leading Caribbean all inclusive resort chains, VP worldwide Sales and intermediary marketing with Choice hotels and Utell International (now part of Pegasus). Bob served as EVP to Intrasight, the leader in CRM services to hospitality, travel and tourism. Bob has taken an industry leadership role as Chairman of TIA's TravelCom (2008) and eyefortravel conferences (2007).
