Javier Muñoz’s Summary

Created Just Action, a system to empower individuals or teams in any organization. Just Action's purpose is to motivate the development of a coherent "action culture" that is sustainable and able to respond with constant productive innovations to an increasingly changing business environment. Just Action was created as a result of more than 12 years of research, corporate activity, coaching entrepreneurs, executives, and investors in the United States and Europe.

Javier Muñoz’s Specialties:

Personal and corporate coaching and training to develop innovation, team building, leadership, communication, productivity enhancement, process optimization, cost reduction, post-merger management integration, crisis turn-around, executive selection processes, incentive programs, and soft-skill training. Sectors: Green techs, alt. energies, IT, digital asset/rights management, knowledge management, process automation, business intelligence, e-learning, mobile applications, and payment programs.
