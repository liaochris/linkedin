Laura Kenney’s Summary

Currently a senior editor at StyleList.com, I assign and edit all beauty- and hair-related content for AOL's style channel, focusing on breaking beauty news, developing indepth feature stories, and enriching our multimedia content and market coverage.

Prior to this I was editorial manager at Sephora, where I created Sephora's Beauty and the Blog, YouTube, Twitter and iTunes channels. I was also responsible for their online content, catalogs, national ad campaigns and store themes.

I started my career as a beauty editor at Seventeen magazine.

My freelance writing has appeared in Elle, Marie Claire, Daily Candy, CITY, Parenting, American Baby, The OC Insider, ElitesTV.com and Flavorpill. I've done commercial writing for eLuxury.com and Victoria's Secret.
