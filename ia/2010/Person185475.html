Sean Nelson’s Summary

What happens when insurance, social media, and curiosity collide?

You get my life, my story, and how I can help you.

For the last 8 years I've helped 1,000's of Individuals, the Self-Employed and Small Businesses in Atlanta save up to 60% on their health insurance and employee benefits.

In 2008 I started writing a blog about LinkedIn (now http://www.socialmediasonar.com) to help other small business owners learn how to more effectively use LinkedIn.

The end result is that now I help small businesses and professionals with their employee benefits or health insurance needs and I help them understand how to use social media to drive new business.

I've written three books and several Guides about how to use LinkedIn:

► LinkedIn 101 (no longer available)
► The LinkedIn MBA: Great "How to" reference
► LinkedIn Marketing Secret Formula: Explains how I get between 1 and 5 calls per week from prospects.

Read the Social Media Sonar blog first then if it helps consider checking out the books which can be accessed at the blog.

I've also joined SONARconnects as a Partner to help teach business owners and professionals how to use LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, Flickr, and Business blogs to build communities of people and to monetize those relationships. We offer a hands on training class or complete management of your social media presence.

Saving folks money on insurance and helping them grow their business with social media...it doesn't get any better than this.

If you need help or are just curious call me, send me an email, or visit the websites listed above.

Warm Regards,

Sean Nelson
