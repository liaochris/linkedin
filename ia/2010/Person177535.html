Ron Warkentin’s Summary

I have been privileged to be involved in sales & marketing for over thirty five years. My experience has included direct in home sales as well as business to business sales. I have been successful in every application for a few basic and quite simple reasons. I have been willing to experience failure as the price of admission to achieve a degree of success. Failure is NOT REALLY failure, you see. It is only the feedback needed to redirect our efforts to stay on track to attain our goals. The outcome that we have committed to as contributing members of The Growth Coach Team (myself with Coach Dave & Coach Pam) is to use ALL of our knowledge and experience directly for your benefit, to provide the most pleasant combination possible for balance and success in your business. Give us a call. We will make a difference in your organization as well. We GUARANTEE IT!
