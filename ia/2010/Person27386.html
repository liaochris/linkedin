Breezy Baldwin’s Summary

Eight years ago I started my first design company, and four years ago I launched my photography career... and each day I love what I do more and more. All of my career experiences have fed into my creative ambitions. From working as an Art Director at a major label in Nashville for 2 1/2 years to traveling the world with rock bands as their personal photographer.

I recently moved to Los Angeles from Nashville to take my career one step farther.

Please check out my portfolio of work, and be sure to say hello if you like what you see! I am particularly interested in projects pertaining to music or non-profit aid/relief work. I can and will travel anywhere on assignment.

    January 2008 — April 2010 (2 years 4 months)

Massive production quantities and super fast turn-around times called for a high level of proficiency, speed and quality. I had to be extremely adaptive and versatile in my abilities because I worked with everything from photo editing to flash to new media content creation.

  • Mac Specialist

    Apple Retail

    (Public Company; AAPL; Retail industry)

    November 2006 — August 2007 (10 months)

    At Apple I started as a sales associate (Mac Specialist) at one of their busiest retail stores. I helped customers with questions about Apple products and developed strong customer service and sales skills, as 90% of customers at this time had never even used a mac, and I had to “convert” them. I taught Apple and non-Apple products alike, ranging from iLife, Aperture and Final Cut, to Adobe’s Photoshop and Dreamweaver.
