Pieter Vink (+ 31 6 83160 762)’s Summary

Pieter is specialized in energy trade control activities. In various functions within Essent Trading and Delta he developed valuable expertise in several commodities, like Gas, Power, Coal, Emissions, CDM and Heat. His main skills concern developing trade processes, setup business control framework, translating market developments for BU management in concrete plans, and to be responsible for monthly financial and management reporting activities.

Besides control activities he has also experience as manager of the Budgeting processes of Essent Trading. In this role he was responsible for the Business Plan and Strategic plan. Main activities in this role were translation of Strategic Plan in activities for coming year and connecting the objectives of the trading organization with the objectives of the whole organization.

Based on experiences as mentioned above Pieter has built the capability to be the sparring partner of the VP equipment and to effectively communicate with people at all levels. Pieter’s focus is to advice customers on the most effective integration of Business Control and Business Intelligence with factual knowledge of trade processes in the energy market. As result of this effective integration the customer will get a substantial competitive advantage through better management information regarding their trading (related) activities.
