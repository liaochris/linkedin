    Periscope

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1997 — April 1998 (11 months)

    Responsibility for establishing a design department for an emerging marketing communications company. Manager for a department of ten, acting as creative director. Ongoing development of creative process, resource and workflow management, approval of all design work. Client contact including new business pitches, design presentations. Design work included identity, publications, collateral, signage, point of purchase, CD ROM, and web sites. National and local recognition for design team under my direction from The Show, MDMA’s Arc, How, Graphis and Print.

  • Design Director

    USF&G

    (Public Company; Insurance industry)

    August 1993 — May 1997 (3 years 10 months)

    Responsibility for creating and energizing a new corporate image for USF&G following a massive reorganization of the company and its return from the brink of bankruptcy.

    Management of internal and external design resources to convey a message of stability and vitality in all communications efforts. Projects included publications, collateral, trade and consumer advertising, the annual report, and the USF&G Internet/intranet site launch.

    Management of in-house design team of nine. Growth and development of team through teaching and mentoring. Planning, budgeting, billing, and training. Management of annual publications budget of $200k and collateral budget of $1.2mm for internal clients. Responsibility for managing printer, illustrator, photographer, and other vendor relationships.

  • Senior Designer, Publications Office

    (Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Higher Education industry)

    October 1988 — August 1993 (4 years 11 months)

    Design of college development and admissions materials. Strategic planning and branding for individual departments and special events. Posters, newsletters, brochures, and invitations for campus clients, major donor and alumni groups.
