    (Government Agency; International Affairs industry)

    Founding director of “Volunteers for Prosperity” or VFP, an interagency presidential initiative promoting international volunteer service by American professionals in support of the US global health and prosperity agenda. Developed and managed initiative’s start-up and operation. Served as national spokesperson. VFP was later authorized through the Edward M.     - Facilitated awarding of more than $100 million in Federal grants to partner organizations utilizing skilled Americans as volunteers abroad

    - Created the “Volunteers for Prosperity Service Incentive Program” (VFPServ), a public-private partnership which helps qualified American professionals meet the costs of international volunteer assignments
