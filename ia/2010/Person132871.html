Mariner Bay’s Summary

Welcome to Mariner Bay at Annapolis Towne Centre! We are excited to be the only high-rise apartment community in Annapolis.

Mariner Bay features many spacious floor plans in one, two, and three bedroom styles with options of offices, dens, and luxurious lofts. Each of our apartment homes are highlighted by our stainless steal appliances, granite counter tops, maple cabinetry, and ceramic tile flooring.

Along with our beautiful new community you will also have access to a gated garage, a controlled access building, and our award winning Bozzuto Management service and maintenance. Our community is also accented by our amenities which include: a 24-hour fitness center, yoga room, roof-top pool, barbeque deck, 24-hour business center with Wi-fi access, wine room, and club room at no extra cost to you!

Mariner Bay is located in the Annapolis Towne Centre at Parole. This great area features amazing shopping and great dining all in walking distance. Please take some time to visit their website www.visitatc.com to learn more about the beautiful area!

Please take the time to come out and visit our beautiful new community!
