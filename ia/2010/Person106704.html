J.P. Fernandes’s Summary

The Fernandes Law Firm specializes in representing companies and individuals located or doing business in Wisconsin concerning:

• Contracts
• Corporations
• Limited Liability Companies
• Partnerships
• Business Litigation and Appeals
• Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreements
• Leases
• Venture Finance & Private Equity
• Shareholder and Member Agreements
• Buying and Selling Small Business
• Internet Law

The Fernandes Law Firm also provides advice on other business related matters like internet service contracts, agreements admitting additional shareholders or partners to existing companies, forming, merging and selling companies as well as maintaining corporate records and books.

J.P. Fernandes’s Specialties:

J.P. Fernandes is a knowledgeable transactional attorney experienced in counseling businesses on organizational questions and agreements including shareholder, contract, venture/conventional finance and buy/sell areas.
