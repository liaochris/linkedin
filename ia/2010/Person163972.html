Phillip J. Hatch’s Summary

Mr. Hatch is a 15 year executive and recognized thought leader in the global sourcing and offshore outsourcing industry. Mr. Hatch has worked with the largest firms in the world helping them understand this dynamic market, set global sourcing strategy and invest in this market. Mr. Hatch has authored numerous white papers and reports on global sourcing and is often cited in the media. Mr. Hatch’s clients have included UBS, Credit Suisse, Goldman Sachs, Intel, Microsoft, India, People’s Republic of China, Oracle, Slovakia, Argentina and many others.
