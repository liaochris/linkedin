Darryl Cobbin’s Summary

Darryl Cobbin, is an accomplished, award-winning marketing magnate who is recognized for using innovative marketing strategies and tactics to drive significant Brand and business growth across multiple industries, leveraging entertainment, sports, new media and technology, both domestically and globally.

He has held executive level marketing positions with The Coca-Cola Company, an early stage wireless venture, Boost Mobile (acquired by Sprint Nextel), and in Media & Entertainment with News Corp’s 20th Century Fox Films. Cobbin currently is President of Brand Positioning Doctors. A marketing firm that focuses exclusively on helping clients build a laser sharp Brand Positioning that ultimately drives the development of all other marketing elements.

• Results and work featured in The Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Forbes, Business Week, Newsweek, Brand Week, Vibe, CNN, CNNfn, MTV, VH-1, and The News Hour with Jim Lehrer.
