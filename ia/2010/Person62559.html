Efren Duarte’s Summary

With over 16+ years business & IT experience, Efren Duarte is experienced in the management, configuration, design, development, implementation and delivery of products and applications, IT security, CMS, document management systems, regulatory compliance and SaaS apps. His extensive work experience includes Fortune 500 companies such as Cisco, Toyota, Amgen, Ameriquest and Capital Group.

He has also excelled in providing focused market research and analysis, business requirements gathering, gaining client and/or team consensus, product management and product marketing.

He has demonstrable skills in vendor selection process, developing project scope, timelines and delivery dates, as well as continuous product design improvements, agile development, six sigma and project management methodologies.

Three of his main objectives in any engagement are to:

• Focus on the User Experience and the use of technology to solve business problems by focusing on what is best for the customer and the company rather than technology for technology's sake.

• Focus on integrating processes,products and technologies into a roadmap that works with business initiatives to reduce costs and develop new profitable products and services.

• Focus on meeting ISO17799 compliance, Sarbanes-Oxley, IT Governance, GMP and other regulatory compliance issues.
