Jennifer Jones’s Summary

More than 20 years of venture capital, technology/healthcare and social media marketing experience including 18 years in a broad range of professional services marketing creating thought leadership and partnership opportunities. Creator and Host of social media video/podcast called Marketing Voices™ airing currently on ITUNES and ConnectedSocialMedia.com. .Founder of JJ&P, a successful marketing consulting firm focused on providing professional services marketing to top tier partnerships including Goldman Sachs, Wilson,Sonsini, Goodrich & Rosati, Claremont Creek Ventures, Allegis Capital, Psilos Group and Scale Venture Partners. First marketing partner for Mayfield Fund, a premier venture firm; Executive Vice President of Regis McKenna, a marketing consulting company; Creator/Host of Bay Area’s first technology TV show; High Tech Visions which ran on Bay Area TV in 1988.
