Sandles Ellen’s Summary

Ellen Sandles is the Founder and Executive Director of the Tri-State Private Investors Network, a private investor membership organization that serves the interests of private investors who wish to invest in early-stage opportunities.

Established in the year 2000, the Tri-State PIN is the longest running angel network in the New York Tri-State area. In January 2009, the Tri-State PIN was listed in Inc. Magazine as one of the leading angel networks in the country.
