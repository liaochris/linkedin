Bill Church’s Summary

Bill Church brings 40 years experience as a journalist to his present role as a freelance writer and educator. A native of Pittsburgh, Bill bleeds black and gold for his beloved Penguins and Steelers. After five years as a Florida-based writer, public relations consultant, and college level instructor in communications, Bill has recently moved his base of operations to Texas.
In Florida. Bill founded Sandpiper Public Relations LLP, wrote numerous executive and company profiles for the Scripps Treasure Coast Newspaper chain, wrote a weekly wine tip column for a wine store owner, and researched and wrote a quarterly online newsletter for National City Bank’s Florida operations.
Bill also served as an adjunct instructor in communications at Florida Atlantic University, teaching courses in news reporting & writing and in public relations.
Bill had two careers after earning bachelors and masters degrees in journalism from the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University. His 16-year career in television news at four different major market network affiliates included stints as a news writer, on-air reporter, news producer, and news director, directing all news writing and production operations of a 40-person newsroom.
Bill then began a second career, directing media relations at a Fortune 500 multinational manufacturer, at a large utility, and for a major region of one of the world's largest international professional services firms. A major portion of his time included the writing of news releases, news “pitch” letters, internal print and electronic newsletters, and speeches.

Bill Church’s Specialties:

Writing for print or web, editing, specialities in personal or corporate profiles, media training,especially for broadcast, sports writing, including hockey, football, baseball, basketball at the pro, college or high school levels, business, politics, music, food & wine, corporate writing, both internal and external.
