David Gillbanks’s Summary

A results-oriented communications strategist, tactician and practitioner, David has a particular interest in exploring the potential of new and emerging database and media technologies to facilitate integrated strategic and tactical approaches to traditional messaging.

David Gillbanks’s Specialties:

An innovative, versatile and hard-working marketing and corporate communications practitioner, David is a disciplined brand manager, and a versatile writer of engaging press releases, punchy online content, creative advertising/collateral, and effective direct mail.

David Gillbanks’s Experience

  • Knowledge Management and Communications Specialist

    Nathan Associates: USAID ASEAN Competitiveness Enhancement (ACE) Project

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    ACE Project is one of four USAID – US State Department ADVANCE projects to help the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) realise an ASEAN Economic Community by 2015.

    ACE Project focuses on enhancing the competitiveness of two priority economic sectors—tourism and textiles & apparel—in collaboration with the ASEAN secretariat and private-sector industry bodies.

    As KM & C Specialist, I recommend and project-manage the development of web-based knowledge management and communications solutions, such as the 'Southeast Asia: feel the warmth' website at www.SoutheastAsia.org, and the 'Responsible Tourism Guide to the Mekong' micro-site at www.ExploreMekong.org/responsible, which won a 2009 Pacific Asia Travel Association (PATA) Gold Award.

    January 2003 — October 2008 (5 years 10 months)

    • Analysed and recommended web-based technological solutions to database and CRM challenges
    • Developed and integrated HTML newsletters, RSS feeds, and website and initiated a suite of new online member benefit programmes

    • Managed industry-best information resources on the web in the aftermath of the December 26, 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami

    • Initiated and managed paperless media facilities at PATA events, including “live” web-based coverage of the events
    • Successfully managed multiple deadlines.
