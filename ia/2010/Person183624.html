Sheri Ann Bell-Rehwoldt’s Summary

Raised a military brat, I love the arts, technology, great food, and passionate people. I currently reside in Salt Lake City, Utah. A capable project manager, imaginer, and communicator, I am developing a training program to teach kids to build *big* lives. ;-)

* * * *

Enthusiastic communicator, marketer, project manager, and social media sponge. With creativity, passion, and common sense, I help companies tell their stories and build their brands.

• "Sticky" content provider/editor/researcher for businesses, websites, blogs, and publications: articles, profiles, newsletters, advertorials, white papers, training materials, case studies, and book drafts. Recipient of numerous writing awards, but more satisfying: hearing “Thank you!” from clients. I’ve never missed a deadline or left a client hanging. You want it? I can write it.

• Capable project manager and event planner. Was asked by the American Society of Journalists and Authors (ASJA) to co-chair its 2008 Educational Conference. Secured keynoter, determined workshop content, and managed volunteer committee. Trickiest role: balancing board goals with member expectations.

Asked to join the board of the Social Media Club of Salt Lake City (SMCSLC), I served as the promotions director for five months in 2009 while the director was on maternity leave.
