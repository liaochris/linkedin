Thomas Schoenborne, Ph.D.’s Summary

NABCEP Certified. 10+ years experience in the engineering, production and integration of solar, wind and geothermal power generation technologies.

Data Aqusition System Specialist -- Expert with Fat Spaniel and other monitoring systems. Modbus, BACnet and Ethernet SCADA systems engineer.

Expert in the Installation, commissioning and component-level troubleshooting of inverters, high power linear and switching power supplies, charge controllers, batteries and other power storage and conditioning equipment used in green energy systems.

Strong focus in SatCon, SMA, PV-Powered & EnPhase inverter equipment.

Over 20 years of management and troubleshooting experience in a variety of settings, both civilian and military.

Expertise spanning numerous fields and technologies including: green energy systems R&D, Si wafer and chip fabrication, board and systems-level manufacturing and troubleshooting, process QA/QC, production line design and facility management.

Extensive experience with cryogenic, vacuum (to UHV) technologies and cleanroom environments (ISO 14644/FED STD 209E), as well as MGS->EGS Si ingot production and water processing of mono and polycrystalline Si wafers. Setup of feedstock, component and system processing applications including electron sputtering, vapor deposition, ion implantation, beam etching, laser cutting, and photolithography systems.

Experience with all phases of product life cycle, from conception to retirement.
