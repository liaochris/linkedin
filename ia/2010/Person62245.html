Edward Bailey’s Summary

As a technical analyst and PLC-Automation programmer, my duties have been varied. My experience spans a range that includes electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, PLC troubleshooting and programming, networking, Modem telemetry, SCADA, and more.

I am well versed in a variety of PLC Programming Applications, stand-alone systems, and distributive control system design and implementation.

I have been involved in the development, maintenance, implementation and installation of many automated control systems, all of which were kept under operational control during development.

I developed, and subsequently taught, two computer courses for Port Huron, Michigan Schools, Adult Education Department, which included all language code for students to use. I particularly enjoy performing a mentoring role, and team development. I work well under stress, and difficult circumstances. I am a team player, with solid team, supervision and leadership skills.
