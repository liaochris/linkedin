Kathleen E. Kirk’s Summary

After graduating from Georgia Tech in 2008 with a B.S. in Management I worked at Exel, Inc. in an Operations Supervisor position. While at Exel I was a member of RAMP (the Retail Accelerated Management Program). I worked for Exel in the Seattle area at a distribution center where I managed the facility's relationship with leaders in multiple divisions at Starbucks, our only client. In the spring of 2009 I was accepted into the Digital Media Management MBA program at St. Edward's University in Austin, Texas. During the late summer of 2009 I was in New York City working in a short term position at Columbia University with Academic Study Associates. I was a Resident Coordinator assisting high school students during a pre-college enrichment program. During my first year of graduate studies I interned for ShippingDiscounts.com in Austin as an Internet Marketing and SEO Intern. During the summer between my first and second year of graduate school I worked for ADCETERA, a medium sized advertising agency in Houston. While in the Digital Strategy position at ADCETERA I assisted the company with refining their processes for music licensing, video production, and digital signage projects. I am now interning for GSD&M Idea City in the Communications Department. I report to the Senior Manager of Media Relations and the Internal Communications Manager. I enjoy working in Advertising and hope to find a position with an agency upon graduating.
