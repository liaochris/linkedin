Ramses Man’s Summary

I like to motivate fundraisers to explore the crossroads of content and commerce.

As Partner/ Senior Fundraiser for Nassau, I am responsible for delivering cutting edge fundraising strategies for the clients of the company.

I am Chairman of the Netherlands Association of Fundraisers (NGF) and Stichting Civil Society. I am Vice Chairman of EUConsult.

My vision on fundraising: “A good fundraising programme meets one essential precondition: it explains the situation and the problems we are trying to address. The truth is a very powerful instrument: it usually hits people straight between the eyes. There is no point beating about the bush: fundraisers must tell the truth, the whole truth and nothing but the truth, no matter how unpleasant it may be. That's what brings results."

Ramses Man’s Specialties:

A fundamental principle at Nassau is that any fundraising activity worth doing can – and must – be measured.

Nassau works exclusively on major donor fundraising, annual funds and the specialty communications essential to the success of these programs.

Ramses is a regular presenter at national and international fundraising conferences.
