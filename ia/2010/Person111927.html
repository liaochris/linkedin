Kathleen Crone’s Summary

Broadcast Builder produces an interactive audio program for Australian residential builders.

We promote best practice in building and business management. Interviews on our program provide manufacturers and service providers with an opportunity to engage builders in discussions and to meausre the uptake of their messages.

Broadcast Builder assists builders to meet their licensing requirements and keeps them up to date with industry news, standards and new product developments.

Goals:

1. Assist Australian residential builders to participate in free, time efficient professional development.

2. Assist building industry manufacturers and service providers to engage builders in discussions about their products and to provide measurable results that indicate the uptake and understanding of the messages presented.
