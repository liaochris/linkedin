Kathryn Merritt’s Summary

I have over 30 years experience in residential remodeling and renovation. I am especially fond of working on very old houses, making them function better for families and for active and busy lifestyles (ie. parents!) I am a highly skilled woodworker and carpenter and businesswoman. I have taught "shop" both in the U.S. and in Africa.


My current goals are to expand my business. After 9 years of keeping it small so that I could parent well I am now ready and able to take my business to the next level. I am committed to establishing my business as one of the premier remodeling and construction companies in Portland, I do this as a carpenter and as a woman. I insist that women be recognized for the skilled, essential and capable work that they do around the world.

Kathryn Merritt’s Specialties:

Sales of remodeling projects, incredible customer relations, construction estimating, construction and project management, conflict resolution, marketing, graphic design, carpentry and a very broad knowledge of related trades.
