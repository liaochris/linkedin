Kelcey Lehrich’s Summary

I am a franchise consultant, I serve as the development director for Teresa's Pizza, Ad-Visor Direct Mail, and Specialty Marketing Systems. As a franchise consultant I work with franchisors to grow their brand and improve their operations and marketing practices. I also works with individuals on a buy-side consultative basis to help them compare franchise opportunities.

Beyond my work as a franchise consultant I also own a networking franchise (AmSpirit Business Connections), a partial interest in two Teresa's Pizza locations (Mentor and Concord), and a licensed territory of HOP Speed Networking.

I blog with Mark Keating (also an Area Director for AmSpirit) about networking in greater cleveland. Read my blog at:
www.networkingcleveland.com

My latest franchise consulting project is a Cleveland area franchise expo - get more details at: www.startbusinessexpo.com
