Peter Trumbo’s Summary

Successful C-level financial operational roles from start up, to explosive growth, to restructuring in the face of poor market conditions, combined with significant financing, M&A and transactional experience affords me a unique set of skills. Progressive leadership across diverse industries – software, investment banking, consumer products and real estate. Team player capable of leading the day-to-day operations of a business or department, cultivating its people and maximizing its profit potential. Always focused on the big picture but with the analytical skills and ability to focus on the details. Substantial negotiation experience putting together complex financing arrangements, joint ventures, licensing agreements, business development opportunities and M&A transactions.
