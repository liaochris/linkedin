Shelagh Saénz’s Summary

Shelagh Saénz is the Coordinator for Career Services at the University of Michigan School of Public Health. She has advised public health students and alumni for nearly a decade on the art of job seeking and on tools for enhancing professional competitiveness. Ms. Saènz has a Masters degree in College Counseling and holds the certifications of Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC). An experienced mentor and facilitator, Ms. Saènz has presented trainings covering a variety of topics including building effective resumes and cover letters, networking, developing interviewing skills, negotiating opportunities, and more.
