Craig Richards’s Summary

I began both my careers as an actor/director and advertising/marketing executive in 1977 then, at the urging of my agent, moved to Los Angeles where I lived and worked until 2002 when I relocated to Central Oregon. I continue to work as a producer/director/actor in feature film through my production company Dolphin Heart Productions. Do you have a solid screenplay and are looking for a visionary director? Contact me to discuss...

Craig Richards’s Specialties:

Combining "old school" storytelling traditions and methodologies with the latest HD video technologies, Craig Richards is a hybrid filmmaker with 30+ years of film & television experience, technical expertise and leadership success.

Companies with whom Craig worked over the years include Warner Bros., Paramount Pictures, MGM/UA, CBS, 20th Century Fox, Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences, Academy of Television Arts and Sciences, Scott Mednick & Associates and many others.
