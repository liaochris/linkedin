Deborah Steuer’s Summary

We offer the professional real estate agent a resource for administrative support targeted to real estate.
90% of my team is licensed. All experienced with Top Producer 7i & 8i and all have in-office experience. More than any other year we have been supporting real estate agents across the nation; we had an influx of Keller Williams agents looking for reliable, skilled, trained virtual support. Many of our new KW clients came to us by way of referral from. However, I believe the Gary Keller book SHIFT is also contributing to the agent’s awareness of alternative sources. The agent should be looking for “CAPACITY” talent who can do more than just their job description. We conduct team meetings & weekly KW Training webinars to give our team the tools to support our client’s goals. We began offering Lead Generation using RedX. We trained to call FSBO’s for and set appointments. We recruited team members that can register our REO clients, follow up with asset managers and even handle the BPO’s. All of our efforts to adapt to OUR changing market, we found made us better at supporting our other clients. Each of our 20+ team members brings a different strength to REVStaffing, but none are limited to a specialty.
When I first began my real estate career in 1978 someone told me ‘you are either green and growing or you are dead and rotting’. And that is true of our business. We continually watch for new tools to use to better our support of our team and our clients. Although we strongly urge our clients to us Top Producer, I have found that my business needs really required a PROJECT management tool rather than a contact manager. We now use BaseCamp to track progress on tasks and support of our clients. And we will continue to find new ways we need to adapt in the coming years. The future is very bright for the virtual assistant that will serve with ‘a servant’s heart’ and put the client first. If you take care of your clients, you won’t have to worry about you.
