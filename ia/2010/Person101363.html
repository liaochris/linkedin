Jo Haigh’s Summary

I have 25 years experience in buying and selling businesses and raising finance for owner managed companies. I have also sold 2 of my own companies, one to the management and one to a trade buyer, so I can really say I know what it feels like!!! I have worked in industry and in professional practice.

I am a published author of 3 best selling business books and deliver award winning tailored training for directors and managers in Corporate Governance and Corporate Finance.

I have also just been awarded the IoD Yorkshire & Humber Business Advisor of the Year 2009 Award

Jo Haigh’s Specialties:

Corporate Finance, MBO, Mergers & Acquisitions, Disposals, Due Diligence, Raising Finance & Development Capital, Shareholder Disputes, Strategic Planning Advice, Valuations, Copyright Valuations, transaction value between £1m and £40m. National award winning public speaker and trainer, Non Executive Director, event facilitator Women in business issues
