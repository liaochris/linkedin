    Sears Canada Inc.

    (Retail industry)

    September 1973 — November 2005 (32 years 3 months)

    I spent over 32 years at Sears Canada Inc., starting in the Textiles Testing lab in 1973 after graduation from the University of Guelph - BASc in Home Economics, Textiles.
    The textile labeling act had just been introduced and all manufacturers were charged with labeling the fiber content of the clothing. How to launder was also on the agenda as was flammability of carpeting and drapes. So after 4 hard years of study at University I was washing and drying clothes, dissolving clothing in chemicals and burning carpets and drapes all day long. What a laugh that is NOW.
    Then I went on to many management roles in the retail stores and then back to the headquarters in the buying departments and merchandising of stores. My final role was in IT where I was a project manager and handled the time and attendance system for all hourly associates in Canada.
    Many fond memories of the people and the company and I am still a strong shopper at Sears for my first choice.
