Joel Lopez, M.D., C.N.S., Diplomate, ABAARM’s Summary

I am a holistic medical doctor currently practicing in the Bay area. I have been privileged to work and train with some of the pioneers in alternative medicine. I incorporate nutrition, nutraceuticals, fitness, detoxification, bioidentical hormone therapy, homotoxicology, and other complementary/alternative modalities.

I believe that optimal health is not just the absence of disease but having a well-functioning body, mind and spirit. I subscribe to the Hippocratic oath of "First, do no harm." As such, I use the safest, most gentle modalities to begin with and go from there. I believe in not just treating the symptoms of illness but treating the cause/causes of the problem. I love to work with team-oriented and open-minded health practitioners.
