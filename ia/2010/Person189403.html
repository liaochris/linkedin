Shukre Despradel’s Summary

Registered PE and SE with over 12 years of experience in analysis, design, and preparation of preliminary plans, final contract plans, and services during construction for a variety of Highway, Railway and High-Speed Rail Bridges, Port Structures, and Industrial Structures.

Experience in Seismic Retrofit and Widening of Highway and Railroad Bridges. Experience in Design-Build (DB) Projects in Puerto Rico, Canada, WA, CA and TX. Experience in structural design of concrete and steel water holding and fish-conveyance structures; buried structures, and earth-retaining structures.

PROFESSIONAL REGISTRATION

Structural Engineer (SE): WA, CA
