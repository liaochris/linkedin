Michael Klazema’s Summary

Experienced in acquisition and retention aspects of online marketing and P&L management of seven-digit online marketing budgets for both BtB and BtC oriented ecommerce platforms. Comfortable working in an international environment as well as with remote teams on multiple parallel projects. Both on the agency and client side I have overseen major functional site enhancements, site redesigns and replatforming exercises from a strategy and product management perspective.
