Garvin F. Brown’s Summary

My experience has primarily been in the energy industry, with large fortune 500 companies, as well as a major European Investment Bank here in New York. Most recently I have been a Manager of Global Operations (Power & Gas) at Barclays Capital, New York. Subsequent to Barclays, I worked as an Energy cash trader for Sempra Energy in San Diego - where I was also responsible for the deployment of O&M, capital expenditures, and the dispatching of over a 1000 MWs of generation, owned by Sempra Generation.

Beyond my recent experience at Barclays Capital, I have almost 15 years of experience in the energy industry, with specific Transmission & Distribution engineering experience with Commonwealth Edison Company, in Chicago, IL (now Exelon Corporation). I possess overall knowledge and experience in Energy Trading & Operations (Power & Natural Gas), Lighting Design & Manufacturing, Renewable (Green) Technologies, Asset Management (Power Generation), Transmission & Distribution Engineering.

Energy Trading & Operations (Power & Natural Gas), Project Management, Asset Management (Power Generation), Transmission & Distribution Engineering, Lighting Design & Manufacturing, Renewable (Green) Technologies.
