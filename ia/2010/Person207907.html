Treavion Davenport’s Summary

Davenport began asserting her talent by using her friends as guinea pigs. Her first clients included high school friends Miss Nicci of R&B sensation, Brownstone, with hits "If You Love Me" and Grapevine" and Apollo legend, the critically acclaimed spoken word artist/publisher Jessica Care Moore.
Davenport eventually formed Papillon, a small public relations and artist development company based in her hometown.

While Motown will always be home to the public relations guru, her diverse role as a publicity manager took her across the country and around the world, ultimately landing her in the budding entertainment Mecca, Atlanta.

In 2004, Davenport launched her own firm, Trea Day Management & Publicity, she now represents Big Cat Records along with a host of other gifted clients, the PR Pro has broken stars, Gucci Mane and Island Def Jam's; Chrisette Michele.

She has also worked with other well-known celebrities, including Yung Ralph, Lowell Pye, Steve Harvey, Cedric The Entertainer, Gregory Hines, Wesley Snipes, Kurupt, The Outlawz, Daz Dillinger, Lil Flip, Bishop P.A. Brooks, Pretty Ricky and reggae star Buju Banton. Trea was at the helm of the planning DipSet South Week for Diplomats President, Freekey Zekey & booking the Ying Yang Twins for the 11th Annual Atlantis Music Conference. The feisty wordsmith also facilitated and coordinated the 30th Annual Black College Radio Convention Music Symposium & BET Hip Hop Awards events.

Davenport introduces clients to the world, including Mick Starr, Haben Abraham, Lofat, 2-Time WBC Middleweight Champion Keith Holmes, Platinum Songwriter/Producer Chris Henderson (Jaime Foxx’s “Blame It On The Alcohol”), She by Sheree clothing designer, Sheree Whitfield of The Real Housewives of Atlanta (Bravo), celebrity makeup artist, Kim Elba (Ex-Wife of actor Idris Elba), premiere artist agent The Abby Smith and Keyshia Cole's mom; Frankie.
