Rae Hoffman’s Summary

I've been a search engine optimization specialist for over ten years now. I am a regular speaker at the WebmasterWorld PubCon's, the SMX conference series and the Search Engine Strategies Conference. I am also the moderator of the link development forum and the YPN forum at WebmasterWorld.com as well as a columnist for Search Engine Land.
