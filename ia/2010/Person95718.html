Jennifer Randall’s Summary

Jennifer Randall, UI Designer, is reachable at jenlynnrandall@gmail.com or 703.380.3766.

Jennifer Randall is a DC-based user interface design and social media consultant with 14 yrs. of experience directing web launches for leading entertainment, media and retail organizations. Social Media implementations include launching Facebook Causes, Fan Pages and Group Pages.

Randall uses her intimate understanding of user interface design to produce design solutions with impact. Her critical visual editing skills, high-profile visual communication experience and natural eye inspire impeccable designs.

Jennifer has been designing websites since the web was in its infancy - 1995. Randall aims to lead her team in striking a balance in functionality and design while ultimately producing desired user behaviors across a variety of media.

Randall is skilled at creating and standardizing design processes and mentors team members in managing process. Projects include:
