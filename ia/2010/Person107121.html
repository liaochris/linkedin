Jason Schklar’s Summary

Seasoned game industry veteran and user experience consultant. My goal is to partner with creative and technically savvy people to create fun, immersive, and awe-inspiring user experiences.

I bring to the table a strong background in game development, user experience research, and a commitment to iterative design. I thrive in an environment where honesty, hard work, and a sense of humor are valued.

I have worked with top-tier video game developers and publishers, social game creators, and have served as a judge for major Indie, Student, and Serious Games competitions. I have spoken about game usability and playability at South by Southwest, Casual Connect, and the Triangle Game Conference.

I have over 15 years experience in the field of human decision making research and have studied jury decision making, how people process complex scientific information, and how humans and computers interact in the productivity, ecommerce, and videogame spaces.

I am passionate about all kinds of games: From frivolous to serious, casual to hardcore, solitary to social, and analog to digital.
