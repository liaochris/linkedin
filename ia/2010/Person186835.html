Scott Freauf PMP, IPMA-C’s Summary

For nearly 20 years, Mr. Freauf has directed the practical application of project management processes and associated tools/techniques in support of the effective initiation, planning, execution, control and closure of projects designed to implement strategic initiatives for private and public sector organizations across a broad range of industries. Additionally he's accomplished in the training, coaching, and/or mentoring of project managers within several Fortune 500 companies such as IBM and AT&T.

Mr. Freauf is an active member of the Project Management Institute (PMI®) as well as the American Society for the Advancement of Project Management (asapm®). He has held the globally recognized Project Management Professional (PMP®) credential for more than a decade. His professional volunteerism includes serving PMI as a content contributor and reviewer for the last three updates of the PMBOK® Guide. Additionally, he was a content contributor to the inaugural version PMI’s Practice Standard for Work Breakdown Structures as well as the Practice Standard for Project Risk Management.
