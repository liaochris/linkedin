Daniel Clement’s Summary

The Law Offices of Daniel E. Clement is a full service law firm with a distinctive emphasis on the areas of family law and matrimonial litigation. The firm is thorough and aggressive in its zealous representation of our clients. We are committed to fulfilling our clients' legal needs on an individual and personalized basis.

We are particularly sensitive to the fact that our matrimonial clients are dealing with legal issues that are rife with emotion. For many, divorce is the first introduction to litigation and the judicial system. For this reason, we are dedicated to taking a practical and problem solving approach to service the legal needs of our clients.

We aggressively seek to resolve cases in an efficient and cost effective manner. We have developed, from our twenty years of legal experience, a hands-on, personalized and compassionate approach to dispute resolution.

Daniel E. Clement, is a principal in the Law Offices of Daniel E. Clement, a full service law firm, concentrating in the areas of family law and matrimonial litigation. The firm maintains offices in both New York and New Jersey.

Daniel Clement has practiced law since 1986, and has written and lectured on various family law issues. In addition, he has been interviewed as an expert in the field of family law on television, radio and in the written press.

He is a graduate of Brooklyn Law School and the State University of New York at Albany. Mr. Clement is a member of the New York City Bar Association, where he is a member of the Matrimonial Committee. He has also worked an Arbitrator in the Small Claims Court of the City of New York. and serves on the board of several charitable organizations..

Daniel Clement’s Specialties:

The firm is well equipped to represent clients in New York and New Jersey in a vast array of family law issues including:

* Contested and Uncontested Divorces;
* Equitable distribution and maintenance;
* Child custody and visitation;
* Child support, paternity and enforcement; and
* Pre and post nuptial and separation Agreements
