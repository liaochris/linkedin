Matthew B. Nissen’s Summary

Matthew has worked for various design and consulting engineering firms in the greater Metro-NYC Area. The markets he has worked in are urban and facility infrastructure, power and electrical systems, facility assessment, feasibility studies, energy conservation measures and energy usage analysis. These experiences have helped him achieve a NYS Professional Engineering License in 2009. His long term career goals are to either own or manage a world class Engineering Firm specializing in Sustainable Development and electric power infrastructure.

After becoming a volunteer leader with the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers (IEEE), Matthew was quickly recognized for his ability to recruit and energize others, and received an award as NY Professional Activities Chairman. He enjoys helping other engineers with professional development and career guidance. Matts hobbies include swimming, beaches, traveling, philanthropy, and networking.

Publications of note:
