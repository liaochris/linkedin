Campbell Jefferys’s Summary

I'm an author, journalist, copywriter and lecturer based in Hamburg. My first book 'The Bicycle Teacher' was published in 2006. The second 'Hunter' came out in 2009. I'm also the author of 'A Lord's Revenge', a novella for English language learners published by Compact Verlag. In 2009, 'Hunter' won the general fiction category of the 2009 Indie Book Awards (http://www.indiebookawards.com) and finished runner-up in the overall fiction category. I currently freelance as a copywriter and journalist and lecture at the University of Hamburg.
