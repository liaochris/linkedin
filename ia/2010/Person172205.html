Richard Ferguson’s Summary

As an online sales strategist, consultant and trainer with over 14 years online experience I help businesses to drive improvements and faciliate greater penetration of their online channel. Here are some current & past achievements :

* Facilitated traffic generating strategies such SEO, PPC and content marketing campaigns across a variety of businesses as well as updating and advising on the conversion techniques needed to ensure objectives are achieved.

* Prepared website briefs and sourced the right web providers for a variety of businesses including identifying the right content management system and modules that best matched their requirements against available budgets.

* Defined the online strategy and established the online presence in Australia for a global bathroom design and manufacturer.

* Conducted the market research and created the marketing plan to beta launch Australia’s first online social lending platform.

* Developed the business strategy and created the most effective distribution channel for an early stage online payment provider.

* Developed and implemented new product initiatives such as an online shopping platform, online call centre solutions and online training modules for a leading internet service provider.

* Pioneered a world first in online video banking with Citibank Australia. Working to align this disruptive innovation into a traditional banking model and structure.

* Defined the revenue model and created the sales and to market strategy for one of Australia’s leading online building directories.

* Created my own Web Enhancer program to help businesses properly plan and then implement the steps needed to successfully manage their websites.

I enjoy working with businesses who offer products and services to different segements and have a variety of channels to market. My aim is to help them align these segments to get maximum benefit out of their online channel.
