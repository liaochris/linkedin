Paul Farrer (paul@pfj.co.uk)’s Summary

Established pfj in 1992.
With people in London & Manchester plus the acquisition of RPCushing and a majority interest in a Sydney based media recruiter, ICUR, pfj is developing a global network of leading specialist recruitment companies.
With around 65 employees pfj aims to have a physical presence on four continents within the next 3 years.
pfj is best known for media recruitment where we have specialist teams recruiting for media sales, media agency, digital, editorial, content, creative, conference, exhibitions and events. In addition we also have specialist teams servicing intellectual property, digital IT, corporate sales & Graduate Recruitment.
RPCushing specialise in market research, insight, analysis and information recruitment.
ICUR specialise in media sales, media agency, creative and advertising and digital recruitment.

We are proud of our status as an accredited Sunday Times Top Company to Work for, the international quality standard ISO 9001, Investors in People Award, membership of APSCo and status as a PPA approved Recruiter .
