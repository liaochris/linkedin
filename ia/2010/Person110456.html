Karen Bailey’s Summary

I have the pleasure of meeting 100’s of business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs. I absolutely love talking to business owners, professionals and decision makers about their businesses, careers, challenges, hopes, dreams, and fears. Then I provide a gap analysis or needs assessment, strategies, resources and support to help them get from where they are to where they want to be.

I look for the right people to work with TRKI’s clients. God has blessed me to be able to do what I love! And instead of trying to learn what you know or re-invent the wheel, I form strategic partnerships, coordinate resources and broker deals. I believe individually, we’ll have a measure of success; but by working together we can succeed far greater than we ever thought possible…I call it synergy-in-action©.

Our Service Partners are Professionals, Consultants, Trainers, Coaches, Speakers, and Subject Matter Experts. I believe in Excellence and Integrity first! I will never recommend services that the client does not need or take advantage of their budget. I believe in winning clients for life!
