Scott Graham’s Summary

Today's companies need outcome driven metrics and the ability to statistically forecast their Key Performance Indicators. Web analytics data is only one part of this dashboard. Marketing professionals need both online and in-store customer data to optimize all ad spending, natural search and conversion while delivering customers relevant, personalized experiences by pairing transactional data with website analytics. Some sources include:

• Affiliate management
• Web analytics
• Mobile devices
• Email campaigns
• Social media interactions
• CRM
• Call center
• Point-of-sale systems
• Transactional data
• Fraud protection
• Media player analysis

At BoomYow, we have integrated our Affiliate Pro software with the tools necessary to accomplish the above objectives into a single dashboard incorporating specifically: hub and spoke Affiliate tracking and promotion that is inclusive of all sales channel data, across the enterprise Analytics fully integrated into our Merchant Service Processing platform. This end to end view of the entire enterprise gives insights and actionable data in a single dashboard which leads to better decision making at every level of the enterprise.

Finally, by having the ability to integrate into an enterprises current reporting software the information needed to take action can be interpreted and disseminated to key personnel for “turn on a dime” decisioning.
