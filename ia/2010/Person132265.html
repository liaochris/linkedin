Maria Berte’s Summary

Art Direction, Creative, Graphic Design, Interactive, Multimedia

I am a skilled design professional with a passion for every step of the design process. In August 2008 I left Aerosim to pursue other avenues of design. I have kept myself employed with project experience in interactive and print working with a variety of organizations from non-profits to Fortune 500.

I am interested in a permanent position or long-term contract work.

I am available to work either on-site or at my home office. I am set up with Adobe CS4 and all standard graphics software.

My fine arts background gave me a solid foundation for designing for any subject matter or medium. I love to work with different and even unknown subject matters. It's always an adventure and I always learn a little something along the way!

I have a solid foundation and years of experience in working on all types of creative projects for any medium, whether it be for print, web, or interactive. I love concept development and coming up with original ideas and designs.

I have a BIG love for modern technology and designing for the screen. It's great to be involved with high-tech interactive design projects and to be directly involved with new technologies. I love to work on innovative projects in a collaborative team environment with designers, developers and programmers/engineers.
