Pep Gómez’s Summary

I'm a young spanish entrepreneur, musician, digital native, advisor, marketer, business developer and coder. I think I'm sociable, entrepreneur, creative, proactive and probably leadership.

I'm also songwriter and music producer, teacher in Spain (Plan Avanza) and speaker about innovation and entrepreneurship (Evento Blog, Campus Party).

I have founded some companies (Redondea, Mewid, Floged) and I have also worked for other (StepOne Ventures, Campus Party, Generación Net). Since I was 13 years old I organize some networking events such as Iniciador (www.iniciador.com) or iWeekend (www.iweekend.org).

I write my experiences on my blog and also I use social media for everything in my life (everything means everything). I love startups, hiking, music, surf and girls.

I had a time in my twelve's (more child than now) where I made radio in Spain with a show called Recetas Musicales (musical recipes) which also was one of the first podcasts edited in spanish. I have worked with artists and music labels (also coordinating teams for LOVG - Sony Music group). Finally, I also invest or have shareholding in some little projects.

I know my age is a handicap, but I think I have a good experience which want to improve with knowledgement the coming years..

I think networking is the new business motor.

I've been collaborated with some NGO's and international cooperation organizations.

Right now, I've just finished my high school and I'm making a gap year working at StepOne Ventures and trying to improve my English, pass the SAT and apply to a Californian university.

Please, ¡contact me!
