Michael Brooks’s Summary

Mr. Brooks, who serves as Founder/Principal is the visionary and driving force behind Transaction Management & Solutions,Inc. is a recognized leader and innovator in the electronic payment and direct response marketing industry and is author of the recently published book “Beyond Plastic, trends in the payment card industry.”

Mr. Brooks brings his reputation and ten (10) years of industry experience to TM&S as well as his proven execution skills to build the industry's most cutting edge and vertically integrated electronic payment and marketing technology company. Mr. Brooks is supported by a management team he has assembled and with whom he has worked in the past in a degree of environments.
