Chris Reed’s Summary

Chris has over 25 years experience in marketing, strategy and product management roles, leading the marketing efforts that built several businesses valued at over $1B. For the last 15 years he has been pioneering cutting edge practice in collaborative systems, B2B solutions, human capital management, digital marketing and digital advertising.

Chris' business passion is building new market categories out of innovative software and solutions. He has successfully created new channel structures and built large partner ecosystems. He has worked extensively with many forms of distribution from direct selling over the web, telesales and field sales, value-added resellers, systems integrators, outsourcers and wireless carriers.

At Lotus he led re-positioning and marketing campaigns that enabled Notes to spurt from $20M to over $250M in revenue in 3 years. These initiatives were later characterized by marketing expert Geoffrey Moore as "one of the most extraordinary marketing achievements in years."

Chris holds a BA in Public and International Affairs from Princeton University, an MBA from Harvard University, and has been a graduate researcher at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He has served on the National Advisory Board of the Human Capital Institute, the Board of Directors of the Software Association of Oregon and the Harvard Club of Oregon. He is also the protagonist in a popular Harvard Business School B2B marketing case study.
