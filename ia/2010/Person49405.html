Dave Carlson’s Summary

I worked in the non-profit arena for 16 years, mostly in the media department as a writer and account manager. Since 2000 I have owned and operated Green Chair Marketing Group, a web development and Internet marketing firm located in Denver, Colorado.

My goal is to help my clients grow their Internet presence through their web sites and through the search engines. We are looking for clients who have monthly budgets of $1000-5000 for online marketing.
