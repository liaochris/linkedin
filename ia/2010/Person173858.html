Wayne Vanderhoof CSP’s Summary

I am a Certified Safety Professional (CSP) working as an independant consultant providing occupational safetyconsulting services and safety training to clients in both general industry and the construction industry. I have over 20 years of experience in the public safety, engineering, manufacturing, and consulting industries including 16 years in safety and health beginning with petrochemical plant design, multi-disciplined engineering design companies and manufacturing companies. I have been in positions that include Safety, Health and Environment Coordinator for an engineering design company - petrochemical plant design, Safety and Health Project Coordinator for an engineering consulting company, and Operations Safety Coordinator and Training Coordinator in the plastics industry.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Occupational Safety and Health from Columbia Southern University in Alabama. I hold the internationally recognized professional designation of Certified Safety Professional (CSP). I am a Professional Member of the American Society of Safety Engineers.

I am an Authorized Trainer for the OSHA Construction Safety – 10 & 30 Hour course as well as and Authorized trainer for the OSHA General Industry Safety – 10 & 30 Hour course. I am recognized as an “Accident & Illness Prevention Service Provider” by the Pennsylvania Department of Labor & Industry, Bureau of Workers’ Compensation and am an Instructor for the American Red Cross for Standard First-aid with AED, Adult, Child and Infant CPR for the Layperson, and the Workplace Training Program Modules.

I have had six articles published in safety and construction publications on topics such as personal protective equipment, housekeeping, safety committee training and a specific company’s safety evolution.

Wayne Vanderhoof CSP’s Specialties:

Consultations on Specific Safety and Health Issues/Challenges; Develop/maintain a comprehensive safety and health management system; Ergonomics programs; Conduct Injury & Accident Investigations, Analysis; Workplace Safety Committees; Customized safety and health training; Job Safety Analysis (JSA)/Job Hazard Analysis (JHA); Mock OSHA Compliance Inspections; On-site Safety Coverage for Construction/Maintenance Projects; Part-time Safety Department/Staff Augmentation/Resource
