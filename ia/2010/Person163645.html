Phil Hendrickson’s Summary

Currently, as manager of global talent sourcing for Starbucks Phil oversees talent acquisition channels, Internet tools, social media platforms for recruiting and diversity initiatives. Phil has created a global sourcing process along with business-line sourcing frameworks, configured systems, expanded talent acquisition channels and candidate segment outreach efforts, improved metrics and reporting, negotiated vendor agreements, provided sourcing tools and training and enabled source tracking and ROI measures. Phil has expanded the use of Web 2.0 platforms for recruiting as well as social media tools: LinkedIn, Twitter and Facebook.

Phil Hendrickson spent years in executive search and in corporate recruiting. He has helped build research and sourcing capabilities in both search firms and in corporate hiring organizations. Phil has helped train and mentor recruiters in proactive search methods and strategies. He traveled to India for a business and technology consulting firm, to help build and train a New Delhi hiring team. Phil has expertise in recruiting systems and how technology helps manage and measure recruiting effectiveness in a corporate environment.

Phil Hendrickson is frequently consulted on the subject of talent sourcing and maximizing sourcing return on investment. Phil is leader of a Sourcing Special Interest Group, a group of recruiting professionals from over forty member companies that share an interest in talent sourcing, social media, source tracking & technology and maximizing the ROI of talent sourcing channels. Phil has been consulted by Wall Street analysts and has been the subject of white papers and case studies on sourcing and technology.
