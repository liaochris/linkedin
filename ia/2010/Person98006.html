Jillian Davis’s Summary

My business is a movement -- JOY, and the practice of joy and adventure in everyday life. As such, I run a creative career consulting business, JoyBirds, which is incredibly unique: I help people who want to generate income based on their most passionate interests, and who want to wake up every day to work they love. When we begin a work together, we co-creatively find or connect the client's passions to what will be their life's work and come up with one or more income streams to support their interests. My work is for people who want to make a living without a job-job -- people who are minipreneurs, solopreneurs, entrepreneurs, or suspect they are one but aren't fully sure yet. Very often, people come to me because love to be home and want to work from home or a home-base.
