Jim Smith’s Summary

Jim Smith, SPHR, is The Executive Happiness Coach(r) as well as a speaker, trainer, and OD consultant. Jim spent more than 20 years in senior executive roles in Sales, Finance, Operations, and HR, so he understands what it takes to succeed in business. He has been coaching executives and organizational leaders since 1992.

Through individual coaching, executive and group retreats, and skills workshops, Jim helps to develop and polish communication and positive leadership skills. Working from the philosophy that anyone can be a great leader when they work to raise up those around them, Jim helps his clients focus on their own life balance (integration of mind, body, heart, and soul) as well as on creating the behaviors that result in more powerful and effective leadership.

Jim holds certification as a Senior Professional in HR and is a Certified Authentic Happiness® Coach, focused on the use of Positive Emotion in creating a happier life. He is also a certified Ontological Coach thru the Newfield Network, working in the three domains in which we live as humans -- our language, our emotions, and the body. He attained his PCC (Professional Certified Coach) credential thru the International Coach Federation in 2007; the ICF is the primary worldwide credentialing body for the coaching profession.
