Kathy Hyatt’s Summary

My Commitment:

As an accomplished Real Estate Professional, I have been commended for my ability to serve my Clients’ needs whether they are buying or selling. This is more than a business to me…it is my mission to help my Clients make educated decisions as they make one of the biggest investments of their lives. When you get ready to Sell, I will provide a full Market Analysis allowing you to maximize the potential of your return, provide professional advice for staging your home and a strategic marketing package that will assure you that the information on your property is reaching the Masses.

About Me:

I truly Love what I do. My pleasant and easy-going demeanor enables me to associate with a variety of personalities. My approach to Real Estate is straight forward, honesty with open communication combined with strong people skills. A Sense of humor and a positive attitude are necessities for survival in Real Estate and you will find plenty of both when you meet me.

Prior to being a licensed Realtor, I worked in the Mortgage Industry for over 15 years. Initially I processed loans and soon after started underwriting loans for FHA and VA. Along with an extensive knowledge of Real Estate, Good Communication Skills were developed along with my keen attention to detail.
