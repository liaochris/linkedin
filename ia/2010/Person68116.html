Eva Stein’s Summary

With over 20 years of post-secondary experience, specifically focusing on curriculum design, distance education and student success/services, I am a regular presenter at regional conferences as well as workshops and seminars. My doctoral research focused on instructor-initiated communication and student outcomes in online learning. I am passionate about student success, often referring to post-secondary education more of a calling than a vocation.

Candidate Statement: My experience has placed me in the upper levels of organizational management, but not so high as to lose sight of operational objectives. I am a strategic thinker who is equally comfortable managing the tactics of continuous improvement. My motto: Adapt, Improvise, and Overcome. I am a listener and a doer who accepts nothing short of the very best people can offer. I believe in processes and systems that are not ‘personality dependent’ in that superior results and exceptional customer service should be embedded in the organization in addition to the hearts and minds of staff.
