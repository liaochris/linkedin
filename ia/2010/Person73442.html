Gavin Costello’s Summary

I've worked in Testing and Management in Product Development for around 15 years. This has been mainly at companies in the Media Space (Canon, Apple, Quickcut, Adstream and BigPond/Telstra).

Most recently, as a Product Manager at Telstra, I have extended this experience to a highly process driven, multi-layered environment. Dealing with the diverse divisions within the Telstra Group as well as multiple external vendors I help continue established relationships and develop new ones.

In addition to developing quality products and maintaining existing ones, my focus has been on providing and improving services and value from a users perspective.
