Jason E. Barkeloo’s Summary

"It is a cursed evil to any man to become as absorbed in any subject as I am in mine" (Charles Darwin).

Jason has over twenty years of experience as a technology business founder, researcher, and investor.

With a top-notch team, he is developing a cleantech and alternative energy solution as Pilus Energy.

Prior to founding Pilus Energy, he founded Somatic Digital, LLC and TouchSmart Publishing. Earlier, he founded DiscoverTek, a consulting practice reviewing and investing in Internet and e-commerce security, privacy, and digital rights management (DRM) solutions.

His experiences include raising capital, product development, prototype manufacturing, and sales & marketing.

Jason completed his career in the military after executing assignments in the operations and intelligence, and medical fields as well as five years as a Troops-to-Teachers inner-city high school science teacher.

Jason E. Barkeloo’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur, raising capital, prototype and product development, business development, and negotiations.
