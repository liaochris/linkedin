Brad Heath’s Summary

Currently working with Central Electric Power Cooperative as an Electrical Engineer in the Engineering Services Department. Areas of concern are solid state meter validation using Itron MV-90, load control communication systems,AMI, powerline carrier communications (TWACS, Cannon,Hunt) ,transformer losses, grid-connected power electronics, peak shaving generators, net metering for distributed generation, new transmission line work plans, short circuit analysis and power flow analysis with Siemens PSS/E, power quality analysis,infrared camera thermography, Datastar Recorders, NERC and SERC reliability compliance, and smart grid implementation with 20 member distribution co-ops in South Carolina.


Planning to take the EIT Exam in October and the PE Exam in April.
