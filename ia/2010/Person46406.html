Daniel P. Anderson’s Summary

Anderson Porter Design: Founding principal of collaborative design studio formed in 1994 as Four Architecture. Leads design practice focused on creating high-performance buildings through sustainable design, lean project design and management, and innovative interior workplaces.

Founding group member of New England Chapter of the Lean Construction Institute. September 2009. Member of the Research and Development Committee. Current research into innovative design and construction delivery methods, including Integrated Design Practices, Lean Design and performance based incentives.

Academic: Lecturer in Professional Practice - Case Studies Research at Northeastern University School of Architecture 2000-2006. Masters of Architecture Degree Program Seminars with detailed investigations into innovative architecture and design with close attention to complexities of urban approvals, development financing, project management and construction technologies.

Green Building Case Studies - Massachusetts Technology Collaborative grant funded research in sustainable design of schools and housing. 2004-2006

Daniel P. Anderson’s Specialties:

Architecture and Urban Design, University and Campus Planning, Building Security, Retail and Commercial Development
