Ron Wilkins’s Summary

Ron Wilkins is an internationally experienced executive with successes spanning Fortune 50s, start-ups, and turn-arounds. Mr. Wilkins’ leadership, problem solving and process development skills were honed in the educational programs of IBM, Harvard and MIT among others. His first hand cross-cultural experiences in dozens of countries covering the Americas, Europe and Asia provide important experience.

He exercises seasoned judgement, with an even-tempered, balanced persona and is known for forging close, productive working relationships.

As a bonus, Ron can effectively represent a company in bringing key business opportunities to a successful conclusion, while developing others in the organization.
