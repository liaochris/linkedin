Roberta Packer, CPC’s Summary

We have a 26 year successful track record in R & D, Lean Manufacturing, Operational Excellence, Engineering, Supply Chain, Medical and Scientific Affairs, Clinical Development, Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR, and Business Development.

We purposely work with diverse clients in diverse geographic areas to eliminate conflicts of interest and blackout lists for strategically, targeted recruiting of passive candidates.

Our business model is a shared-risk model that provides our partners with dedicated, customized, high touch, and high priority service with a commitment to a successful hire. As a matter of fact, unlike most executive search firms, we believe in pay for performance based on achieving milestones and delivering results. In this economy, our approach saves
money and is fiscally prudent, but does not sacrifice the retained search quality and importance of the messaging and added value service.

We specialize in overcoming challenges and strategically telling your story- as well as you do- to land key executives and pivotal team players who are not only a cultural fit, but who can also have immediate impact.

We place mid-level to C-suite executives throughout North America and China.
