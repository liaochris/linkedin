Atul Gupta’s Summary

As a co-founder and CEO of Internet services (Web Development, SEO, Search Engine Marketing and Web Solutions company) for the last 12 years, I have had a very vast experience and have witnessed the Internet evolve to what it is today. I have particular interest and specialization in Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Link Popularity Building. I have analyzed the algorithms of various search engines personally and have written several articles on their evolution, behavior and what one can expect in future. Our company www.redalkemi.com offers services in the area of - Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Link Popularity Building, Social Media Optimization, Article & Content Syndication, Web Design & Development, eCommerce Solutions, PPC Services
Staff Augmentation Services & Web Consulting.

Our company has developed some very powerful analytical SEO tools, which we use in our SEO processes. These tools give us the edge over our competition. Our tools can analyze the client’s website, competition, ranking potential, keywords mining and can indicate problem areas which need attention.

Over the last decade, we have helped hundreds of small, medium and large companies setup their online presence and penetrate market through search engine marketing. Our natural ranking case studies show how we have helped these clients leverage free search engine traffic through high search engine rankings in Google, Yahoo and MSN.
