Joan Cozart, M.Ed., Ed.S., OpXGroup’s Summary

Originally from Nashville, Tennessee, Joan Cozart has been certified as a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and Kaizen Facilitator by Accenture and the prestigious George Group, and is recognized as an industry leader in Corporate Strategy Services, Leadership Development and Process Management. Joan has held various positions in Strategic Human Resources and Organizational Development Sectors at Lucent Technologies, Nortel Communications Systems, Computer Sciences Corporation and Intrawest Resorts.

Joan Cozart, M.Ed., Ed.S. & Joe Cozart, Sr. CAM, Principals/Co-Founders, OpXGroup, has more than 25 years of expertise consulting with Fortune 500 clients across a range of industries. OpXGroup is versed in Chamber management and community leadership challenges and focuses on leadership development, organization design, change management strategies and strategic facilitation. They understand the interactions of board, community leaders and staff in Chamber settings and bring independent research on Chamber operations to bear in their work. OpXGroup can help Chambers identify and tackle priorities in both the short and long term. Whether seeking to improve short-term or long-term performance on strategic priorities, applying comprehensive benchmarking or a facilitator to conduct quick retreat style sessions, or draft a multi-year strategy for your organization, OpXGroup is the right consultant to suit the needs, ambitions and budgets of Chambers.

OpXGroup specifically focuses on the Operational Excellence (OpX) methodology and the data-driven results from their work with Chambers, Cities, EDC's, Municipalities, and Nonprofits throughout the United States.

Specializing in "Community Visioning" and "Fundraising" with over $700 million in capital raised since 1988.

OpXGroup is a Strategic Planning “Preferred Provider” of the American Chamber of Commerce Executives, Consultants on Demand.
