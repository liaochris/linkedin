David Austin’s Summary

I am an experienced commercial lending professional recognized as a sales acceleration agent who finds creative ways to get business booked while maintaining risk standards. I’m known as an effective leader who builds cohesive teams with a track record of boosting portfolio performance while decreasing delinquencies. I am recognized as a skilled negotiator who resolves complex situations by delivering creative solutions resulting in stronger lending relationships.

I am experienced in high profile account management, C-level relationship building with extensive experience managing large or difficult loan portfolios. I served as a key bankruptcy strategist for my business unit and have extensive experience guiding collection efforts through bankruptcy while minimizing legal and litigation expenses.
