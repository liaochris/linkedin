David Marsden-Ballard’s Summary

I am a high-achieving systems thinker, a social & environmental problem-solver & a sustainability generalist. I have an extensive understanding, my referees & colleagues regularly describe it as an encyclopedic knowledge, of the sustainability challenges & opportunities facing my region & the world this century.

I have had a paid & voluntary career of 30 years that is varied and diverse. With 2 Trades Certificates, 4 Diplomas, 4 Degrees & active attendance at numerous short courses, conferences & workshops; I have worked hard to gain a transdisciplinary &informed understanding of the world that has both a breadth and depth that is rare.

I am both practical & grounded, able to understand and effectively communicate complex theoretical concepts of a technical or scientific nature to just about any audience. My theory informs my practice & my practice improves my theories.

I am as comfortable:
in a well-equipped farm workshop welding tractor implements, re-wiring a plant trailer, installing some solar cells, on-site with a concrete mixer pouring a shed floor or landscaping an erosion gully back to health with my Dingo mini skid steer loader, bales of hay & a 1000 tubestock native plants, assessing and reducing the Ecofootprint of a business or redesigning a rural property on Permaculture principles;
as I am talking to a community group, training and leading a Greencorps Team in revegetation of a river bank, lecturing & tutoring undergrads and postgrads at University, talking with Indigenous Elders, advising the Head of a Government Agency or briefing a State, Territory or Federal Minister, of Transport, Environment or Science.

I can work with you & your organisation to help you solve your problems whether it is to redesign a property, reduce environmental impact or solve socioenvironmental problems others can't find a solution to.
I respond positively to all contacts so Link In !!
