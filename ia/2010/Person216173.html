Wlamir Mello’s Summary

I believe my natural leadership, management and technical experience helps empower highly effective engineering teams. This in turn means more efficient product development cycles, with fewer NRE costs. In addition, I identify and implement New Product Development Methodologies (NPDM) for hardware and firmware, allowing engineers to spend their time on value-add activities.

I have extensive experience working and leading both highly specialized and multidisciplinary teams in wide-ranging high tech industries, all the way from integrated circuits (micro level) to highly complex systems (macro level) that include electronics systems, mechanical parts, high vacuum, magnetics and high voltages.

I am adept at managing multiple large-scale projects at once. For example, I led both hardware and firmware engineering teams through the New Product Development Life Cycle (NPDLC) of many highly complex electronics systems, all while following tight specs and stringent EMC constraints. I consistently delivered quality multidisciplinary technology products, on time, in these industries:
• Nuclear electronic systems, automation and intelligent instrumentation
• Biomedical electronics and systems
• Mixed analog, RF and ASIC semiconductors
• Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) and test solutions for high speed ICs

These designs ranged from microprocessors/firmware/software integration, high speed analog to hardware/software for laser trim of mixed analog, RF and ASIC ICs.
