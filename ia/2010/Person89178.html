Jane Loveday’s Summary

I have helped a lot of First-Time homebuyers obtain their very first home - each transaction was as smooth as I could make it, to me that is the most rewarding aspect of my business. I obtained my Realtor licence in 2004 after working in the San Diego Unified School District, and my love of teaching helps me to understand that my clients need not only someone to help them find a house, but who will guide them through the whole process from start to finish.

I am a certified E-Pro, which means I have mastered certain courses for Realtors and the Internet, I maintain 3 separate websites - http://www.janeloveday.com, http://www.facebook.com/j-loveday and http://www.myspace.com/sandiegorealestate.

You can also follow me on twitter @sdrealestate

I work all over San Diego County, living here 25 years, it is truly my home!

Jane Loveday’s Specialties:

Simply - knowledge of the real estate climate here in San Diego, what separates one neighborhood from another, where my clients can find the best deals, the differences in architectural styles, the schools, traffic and commutes - simply just what San Diego in it's entirety has to offer.
