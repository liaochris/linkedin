  • Tampereen yliopisto

    Exchange Student, Ph.D , Virology , 2001 — 2004

    My project focused on studying the role of the HIV-1 Nef protein in HIV-1 pathogenesis. I cloned the HIV-1 Nef mutant library by Error Prone polymerase chain reaction. The Nef mutant library was constructed to screen and analyze gain of function mutants of the HIV-1 Nef protein.   • National center for cell science

    PhD , Biotechnology ( Virology) , 1998 — 2004

    Thesis tilted “ Studies on the Nef protein in HIV-1 pathogenesis”. Studies revealed a novel interaction between the transactivating protein of HIV-1 and the accessory protein Nef. The interaction was confirmed by yeast two-hybrid, immunoprecipitation, mammalian two hybrid, and confocal microscopy techniques. The Nef- Tat interaction was established to be important for increased viral production. The increase in viral production was due to an enhancement of transactivation from the HIV-1 LTR. The region of the viral promoter important for the enhanced transactivation by the tat-nef interaction was mapped to the NF-kB sites.
