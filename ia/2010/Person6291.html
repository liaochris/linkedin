Alex Bard’s Summary

I am a passionate, driven entrepreneur and have been part of the founding team of 4 internet software start ups including eShare Technologies (Customer Service Software - 1996), eAssist Global Solutions (Customer Service Software - 1999), Goowy Media (Widget Analytics Platform - 2004), and most recently Assistly (Customer Service Software - 2009).

Through out my career I have key executive roles including VP Customer Solutions - eShare, VP Sales / Marketing - eAssist, CEO - Goowy, and VP Products AOL.

Currently I am the CEO of Assistly and an advisor to several start-ups. I have lived all over the world including NY, Toronto, London, LA, and currently reside in San Diego with my wife Shari and daughter Taylor.
