Jasmine Wilkinson’s Summary

I am a versatile digital producer with strong skills in strategic digital planning, creative marketing, copywriting, and art direction. I combine these skills to produce and oversee innovative, creative and effective digital marketing solutions for a diverse range of clients.

Pushing the boundaries of clients’ and users’ expectations is something I strive for constantly, and I’ve been lucky enough to win several international awards for my work.

In 10 years in digital, I’ve played the roles of designer, copywriter, creative director, account director, producer and client, gaining hands-on experience in all aspects of digital production. I’m as comfortable art directing digital team as writing specifications or crunching web stats; I see creativity, strategy, and analysis all as equally important parts of a greater whole.

I provide convincing contributions to new business development and partnership deals, and can translate digital issues and concepts at the right level for different situations. I can adapt my copywriting styles appropriately for blogging, press, proposals, specifications, advertising and new business pitches, and spot a typo at 50 paces.


Open source, social media, web standards and user experience are keystones in my preferred methodology, and I am heavily focused on ensuring my solutions are scalable, flexible, integrate with existing systems, and will grow with my clients.

I can hack a CSS bug, defuse an IT crisis, and I know mySQL from my XML, XLST, XHTML and Xbox. (As well as loads of other things that don’t begin with X.) I also know when to call in the experts - armed with a pretty useful address book when it comes to finding them.

Problem solving is definitely one of my strengths, and I can gain understanding of different business needs very quickly to provide insightful and creative solutions on a strategic level.
