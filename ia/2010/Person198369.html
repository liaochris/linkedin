Suzie Larsen’s Summary

Suzie, a born and raised San Franciscan, has worked in the Web Industry in the City since 1998. Starting out in the Web world with Online Journalism for Mother Jones Magazine and US News & World Report, she quickly branched out with the Dot Com Boom and worked with three start ups in every capacity she was called upon to do and took initiative where needed.

The start up world has taught her to use her strengths in leadership and maximizing efficiency with little or no resources with the end result of large scale product/project development, brand management, and revenue generating platforms.

She maintains excellent relationships with vendors and clients through professionalism and mutual respect.
