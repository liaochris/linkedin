Gina Herald’s Summary

Your personal success partner. We help companies develop a sustainable competitive advantage in the marketplace by partnering with them to develop their most powerful asset: Their people.

Gina has 15+ years of customer service, leadership, management and sales experience. She has a track record of success in providing great customer service, increasing sales, leading and developing people.

She is passionate about helping companies improve results. By focusing on developing individual and team potential in the areas of customer service, communication and leadership, productivity is improved and profits are maximized. She coaches individuals and groups, trains teams and departments and speaks to companies, educational institutions and non-profit organizations. Her style is dynamic and interactive and her clients are moved to take action on the principles they learn.

If you think training your employees and having them leave is expensive, try not training them and having them stay.
