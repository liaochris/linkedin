Jay Henderson’s Summary

Five years with the Covey Leadership Center helping companies roll out 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, Principle Centered Leadership and First Things First programs ...

After Covey, I spent 3 years teaching leaders and managers in the Fortune 500 how to 'coach' employees to higher levels of business / job performance...

I am passionate about anything that helps people perform better, be better and get better results. So, along with every type of leadership & management course of study I can find... I've studied Sports Psychology for 18 years. They have the best research about people and performance.

It's all about how people learn and how they translate that learning into performance, especially when the pressure is greatest... getting the best out of people when it counts the most.

In 2008, I helped about 20 Cisco Corporation teams with individual leadership coaching, performance improvement work, and team building.

I have 15 years of mastery with a unique science called that measures how people think and how they make decisions...

I work with Business Owners, CEO's, VP's, Directors, Managers and Teams.

Jay Henderson’s Specialties:

Team building, leadership and management development, succession planning work benchmarking top and bottom performers to create a specific performance score for any role and more. Almost anything that will help a leadership team, or any other team perform better, communicate better and get faster more accurate results.
