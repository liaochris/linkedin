BRIAN O'NEIL’s Summary

BRIAN O’NEIL is a charismatic sales trainer, sales recruiter, coach, business consultant, Linkedin trainer, speaker, and the Founding Principal of Sales Empowerment Group, LLC. He is the architect of a revolutionary, high-touch, referral-based business development model that has people/processes/tools at its core. Professional salespeople who leverage Brian’s knowledge, coaching and training realize greater ease in approaching qualified prospects, cultivating more meetings, shortening their sales cycles, and generating more business.

Drawing upon a background that has run the gamut of sales, sales management, consulting and leadership, Brian is cognizant of the essentials for success in selling. A master at inspiring and building confidence, he enables people to set high goals, harness their inner strength and optimize their outcomes. As President and CEO of SEG, Brian leverages his experience in working with Fortune 500 companies to deliver high value and, most importantly, results. “Whether the account is a small to mid-size business or a Fortune 500 company,” explains Brian, “the focus is on creating opportunities.” Known for his rock-solid work ethic, Brian adheres to the pay-it-forward philosophy in his professional and personal pursuits.

Brian advocates the implementation of all platforms in positioning brand and reaching prospective clients. His value-added, content-rich trainings have a galvanizing effect on sales forces. Brian has deep empathy and appreciation for today’s salesperson. “I’ve walked in your shoes,” he affirms, “I’ve been on over 5000 sales calls and know what it takes to achieve at a high level.” Supremely committed to the success of his clients, Brian provides them with the tools they need to achieve greater focus. “It all comes down to people, processes and tools,” he explains, “any sales organization needs this input to install better functioning. Undoubtedly, Social Media is the hot area right now.”
