Jennifer Smith’s Summary

Jennifer Smith’s career has focused on effective application of change management techniques to help major initiatives succeed and deliver results faster.

A Change Management alumnae of Accenture, Jennifer worked with clients Toshiba, Hewlett Packard, BASF, and Weyerhaeuser on both change projects and ERP implementations. After several years at Accenture, Jennifer served as Director of People Development for Silicon Valley-based technology consulting firm Netigy, Jennifer managed training and development and collaborated on the development and implementation of their knowledge management program. Prior to joining Michelin, Jennifer worked in Corporate Development for Vision Service Plan in Rancho Cordova, California. At Michelin, Jennifer was responsible for change management and communications for a multi-year change program within the North American Small Tires division, and is now extending her skills to the digital arena as the Digital Consumer Experience manager.

Jennifer’s outside interests include serving as Vice Chairman of the Board of the Piedmont Health Care Foundation, volunteer work for The Children’s Museum, running, cooking, traveling and chasing after her two children in Greenville, South Carolina. She is a graduate of Washburn University (BA-Psychology).
