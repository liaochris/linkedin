J. Anthony Kosar’s Experience

  • Artist / Owner

    Kosart Effects Studios, LLC.

    (Fine Art industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Kosart Effects Studios, LLC is the work of Freelance Artist J. Anthony Kosar, specializing in Fine Art, Illustration, Sculpture, Creature Design, Make-up Effects, and much more, including Concept Art, Storyboards, Halloween Masks, Props, Maquettes, Byzantine Iconography, Graphic Design & Product Design. Though Kosar has been using the name "KOSART Effects" since 2002, he formed the official LLC in 2008. Despite the familiar connotation of the word “effects,” the name “KOSART Effects” not only encompasses Kosar's alter ego of special effects, but also of all his art and everything who he is. The word “effect” means “end result” and “the extent to which something succeeds.” In a way, “KOSART Effects” is more of a promise to himself and to the world, a promise best said in Mary Shelley’s Frankenstein, “So much has been done. More, far more, will I achieve; treading in the footsteps already marked, I will pioneer a new way, explore unknown powers, and unfold to the world the deepest mysteries of creation.”

  • Freelance Halloween Mask Designer / Sculptor

    Zagone Studios, LLC

    (Consumer Goods industry)

    November 2007 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    In 2008, Kosar sculpted his first commercially made Halloween mask for Zagone Sudios, "Gargoyle," and has two more Halloween masks for Zagone’s 2009 line "SATANIC PANIC" and "Alien." Kosar will be working on several more for 2010.

  • Freelance Artist and Sculpt Painter

    The Bradford Group

    (Privately Held; Consumer Goods industry)

    September 2006 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    Kosar designs products, creates digital renderings and mock-ups of product concepts, critiques sculpts, and paints the cast-resin sculpts.

  • Professional Freelance Artist

    KOSART Effects

    (Fine Art industry)

    September 2002 — October 2008 (6 years 2 months)

    KOSART Effects is the work of Freelance Artist J. Anthony Kosar, specializing in Fine Art, Illustration, Sculpture, Creature Design, Make-up Effects, and much more, including Concept Art, Storyboards, Halloween Masks, Props, Maquettes, Byzantine Iconography, Graphic Design & Product Design.

  • Special Effects Intern Summer 2007

    Stan Winston Studio

    (Privately Held; Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    June 2007 — August 2007 (3 months)

    As an intern, Kosar learned the many aspects of the FX industry, working on the major motion pictures "Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull" and James Cameron's "Avatar," and commercials including Xbox's Halo 3 "Believe" Campaign and the Axe Body Spray's "Dark Temptation: The Chocolate Man."

  • Product Development Intern Summer 2006

    The Bradford Group

    (Privately Held; Consumer Goods industry)

    May 2006 — August 2006 (4 months)

    As an Intern, Kosar learned the Product Development Industry: designing products, creating digital renderings and mock-ups of product concepts, critiquing sculpts, and painting the cast-resin sculpts. He continues doing these things as a Freelance Artist.
