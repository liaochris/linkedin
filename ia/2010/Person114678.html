Ken Cooperman’s Summary

Dynamic, accomplished head of operations for multi-family offices, investment advisors and broker/dealers. Proven track record of building and managing people, processes and systems to achieve corporate goals and objectives.

Open to challenging career opportunities in New England, New York and the Washington DC area.

Practical experience in operations, trading, private client services and performance reporting at world-class firms including Athena Capital Advisors, DE Shaw & Co. and Fidelity Investments. Member of numerous investment technology platform startups including JP Morgan OnLine, FarSight Financial Services and Thinking investments. Managed successful implementations of Advent Portfolio Exchange, SunGard Investran and SunGard VPM.

Currently the acting CFO and advisory board member for AutoFlex/Clean Cities, an electric vehicle fleet management company in Baltimore, that is in the planning stages of electric car sharing ventures with Federal and State government agencies.

Hands-on experience in trading, administration and reporting for stocks, bonds, mutual funds, index/equity options, ETF's, hedge funds, and private equity funds.

FINRA Series 65 licensed. Previously Series 7, 24, and 4 licensed.
