Lauren Beyer’s Summary

I am a young communications professional in Jacksonville, Florida. In April 2008, I moved here to become the Social Media & Fan Outreach Coordinator at the PGA TOUR, working within PGATOUR.COM. I work between the online marketing, operations and content teams to increase the reach of PGATOUR.COM on off-site social channels, including Facebook, Twitter & YouTube. I interact with fans through creating, listening, responding and monitoring our online channels, as well as Fan Feedback submitted through PGATOUR.COM. I also keep an eye on social marketing and media trends, while recommending new opportunities for the PGA TOUR and PGATOUR.COM.

Formerly, I built and led the Communications department at brass|MEDIA Inc., a multimedia and marketing company focused on young adults and money, from January 2007 through March 2008. In December 2006, I graduated from Northern Arizona University with degrees in Public Relations and Electronic Media.

I have a strong sense of responsibility to give back as much as I can. I am the leader of a local niche group, Social Media Club Jacksonville, which connects professionals and like-minded people to share & learn about social media during monthly events. In 2009, I was a Single Greatest Night Honoree for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, raising over $2,000 for the CFF.
I also currently volunteer through the PGA TOUR's Employee program. In Corvallis, I served on the United Way Benton County Board of Directors and the Young Professionals of Corvallis executive board. During college, I worked with Special Olympics Oregon, volunteered for SMART, and was a Community Relations Intern for the Portland Trail Blazers during the 2005-06 season.

My future goals include starting my own company to work with individuals who want to give back to their communities, being an advocate for the underserved, and living abroad. I'd also like to work on breaking par for 18 holes, now that I'm closer to the golf course again.
