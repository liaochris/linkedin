Ash Singh’s Summary

Ash Singh, an improbable mixture of Indian blood, Canadian birth, Chinese education, and Asian business experience, has already established himself as a force to be reckoned with in the world of e-business. While still a student at Chinese University in Hong Kong, he conceived and launched Social Network Business Systems, a business network development company. This venture led to a successful full-time enterprise that was eventually acquired by Ad.WRIGHT Holdings, a specialist in branding and advertising.

Singh concurrently served as the Chief Technology Officer for At-Life, which created in-store plasma screens network and Singapore’s largest customer loyalty rewards program with almost 500,000 members, for McDonald's in all of their 125 restaurants.

Ash's then served as the Vice-President Interactive for Imagine OmniMedia, a Singapore based production company focused on reality TV. During his tenure, he developed exciting digital platforms and product integrations for shows like "The Contender Asia" (#1 rated show on AXN) and "The Biggest Loser Asia" (#1 rated show on Hallmark)

Ash currently serves as MD of Interactive SG along with his role as investor and director to 5 other companies in the digital media space. His recent success includes:

"Star Commentator" an online audition platform to become a SportCenter Presenter on ESPN

"Cut The Fat Challenge" a social casual Facebook game that teaches you how to lose weight

His list of clients include McDonald's, Tiger Beer, Skype, PayPal, eBay, Fitness First, General Motors, Mattel, Nestle and BlackBerry.

Singh is also committed to supporting the local business community and to nurturing young local business talent. He is a lecturer, speaker and honorary judge for business venture competitions at the INSEAD, SMU, NUS and The Richard Ivey School of Business.

As a basketball fan, he is also an shareholder to the Singapore Slingers professional basketball team.
