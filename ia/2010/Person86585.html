Jack Hallahan’s Summary

Jack brings twenty years marketing insight to emerging media, offering senior level brand, sales, and product development expertise. Mixing traditional and new media experience in mobile, website, advertising and integrated marketing, Jack has helped build brands including TiVo, Netflix, Apple, Mattel, Sports Illustrated, Men's Health, MobiTV, and Reebok.

As Chief Revenue Officer for Blancspot, Jack is focused on producing a rich news experience for The iPhone Generation. He has collaborated on every aspect of this successful Start Up, including Staffing, Branding, Product Design, Go-to-Market Strategy, Web Site Design, Partnerships, and Revenue Modeling.

Prior experience as CRO for 1Cast (aquired by ClearWire), VP Mobile Advertising for MobiTV, and VP at AKQA includes multi-channel marketing for Visa USA, Infiniti, Coca-Cola, Nikon, Lion's Gate, Jeep, L'Oreal, Macy’s, Nike, and Anheuser-Busch.

Jack developed his advertising career in New York, launching Men’s Health Magazine after managing Fortune 500 clients for Sports Illustrated. Advertising, Media and Marketing Strategy form the foundation of his agency experience at Chiat/Day and Young & Rubicam, where he began his career on Madison Avenue after graduating from Villanova University.

Jack lives in Orinda, California, with his wife Lisa and their three children.
