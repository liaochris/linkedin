Enrique Cubillo’s Summary

85 Photo Productions Inc. is the production company of Enrique Cubillo. With 20 years of commercial photographic experience, Enrique has played a key role in developing successful images in industries as diverse as finance, music, food, academic services, public relations and advertising.

At 85 Photo our clients are the center of everything we do. This means being accessible, responsive, creative and efficient. Our first priority is to simplify clients' lives by responding to their needs and achieving their communication goals.

The Giving Booth seeks to partner with worthy non profit organizations to help raise charitable donations by operating a digital photo booth at select events at no cost to the participating non-profit organization. Participant’s donate directly to the non profit on site to appear in The Giving Booth. The Giving Booth never handles funds of any kind nor is paid. The service is a donation. All images are later made available for download at no further cost to anyone. The Giving Booth is a charitable project.
