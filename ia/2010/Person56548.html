Richard Butler’s Summary

DigitalMeeting combines the power of the largest fibre network in the world and the systems you already have in place, to bring together audio web and video communications into one simple DigitalMeeting enabling seamless global collaboration for your business.

The DigitalMeeting Communications Process has been especially developed in response to the needs of companies who need to communicate internally and externally, which helps to unify your supply chains and business collaborations. With DigitalMeeting, the time and cost of travelling to meetings is instantly reduced and your communications become more effective as you let our network do your travelling for you. Our conferencing bridges allow any computer to give presentations or show and share documents and applications in real time while global freephone numbers handle voice at no extra cost.

technilink’s vision is to deliver the widest variety of global conferencing solutions through our DigitalMeeting products. DigitalMeeting gives business new and innovative global conferencing services, allowing greater flexibility in your daily business decisions. By developing the existing DigitalMeeting technologies, we have created the simple and innovative DigitalMeeting Communications Process which can be used within any supply chain - Internal or external, linear or complex . This serves one very simple purpose: to unify supply chains through Effective Communication.
