Jocelyn Canfield, ABC – Communication Results’s Summary

Jocelyn Canfield, ABC, (Accredited Business Communicator) uses her strategic communications expertise to help clients increase the effectiveness of their communications tools and tactics. From concept development to all aspects of project execution (writing, design, photography, print coordination), Communication Results offers one-stop shopping AND a pleasant working experience. Her firm has many long-term clients – businesses who continue to get fresh, creative ideas year after year.
