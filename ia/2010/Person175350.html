Robert Vanselow’s Summary

Results-driven sales leader and media executive with 13+ years of experience helping advertisers, direct marketers, and call centers effectively implement campaigns and strategies in all areas of both online and offline media to include affiliate marketing, display advertising, dedicated / exclusive email marketing, e-newsletter / email sponsorships, customized co-reg programs, lead gen, direct mail, run-of-book print advertising, insert media, telemarketing, database marketing (data appends / data modeling / data enhancements), and data monetization. Experienced in CPM, CPA, CPL, CPC pricing models, as well as revenue share structures.

Within companies ranging in size from $5 million to over $200 million, day-to-day responsibilities have included P&L management, strategic sales planning, sales and marketing management, channel partnerships, new business development, product launches, circulation planning, media buying, and data monetization. Successfully led turnaround change in stagnant and under-performing divisions, launched start-up divisions for entrepreneurial-minded companies, and optimized sales within companies to achieve double-digit revenue growth.

Worked with noteworthy clients such as American Airlines, AmeriTrade, MBNA Bank of America, Blockbuster, Cash4Gold, CitiBank, Direct Buy, Fidelity Investments, Forbes, GAP, Hartford Financial, ITT Tech, Kaplan, Matrix Direct, Lincoln Financial, Morningstar, Oreck, Panasonic, Principal Financial, Puma, Reader's Digest, Wall Street Journal, and University of Phoenix.

Education includes an MBA in International Business from the University of Maryland and a Bachelors in Marketing from Ferrum College in Virginia. Also completed an Intensive Study Abroad Program at Argentina's top-ranked Business School - Universidad Argentina de la Empresa. Symposiums attended included: Strategic Planning, Marketing, Economics, Private Equity, and Entrepreneurship. Fluent in Spanish and proficient in German.
