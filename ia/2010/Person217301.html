Yasmin Bendror’s Summary

I am excited to have opened my own social media marketing consultancy as of January 2010, focused on providing strategic consulting to SMS business owner on how to dive into the complex world of social media.

The question is: Where, how to start? What's the best social media outlet for your business, product or branding?

That's where I can help.

Today, the buzz word is SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING and it’s all about integrating your marketing through traditional and new social mediums to get the best results. Social media has become a strong marketing platform and tool. It’s too important to ignore, but challenging to understand how it pertains to your business or company.

I have been developing marketing strategies and programs for companies for over 20 years. Starting in very clear-cut traditional marketing mediums, and now, embracing the new world of social media. Keeping up with the new landscape is an important part of staying in the game. So my marketing strategies and campaigns integrate social media platforms (blogs, Twitter, YouTube, Linkedin, Flickr, wikis, podcasts), but also encompass online marketing tactics such as online advertising, email campaigns, pay-per-click and search engine optimization and offline tactics.

Social media is here to stay. Social media marketing needs to be part of your marketing mix!

I'm also zealous about planning and always tying marketing campaigns to specific goals and targets as well as keeping them within budget. Setting the right expectations for social media marketing is key!

I'm truly fulfilling my vision and passion by providing strategic consulting, in depth advice and marketing services that is hard to find and so often lacking for the small business owner.

Some personal info: I was born and raised in South Africa (and still passionate about going on Safari and saving wildlife), lived and worked in Israel and Seattle, and finally relocated to the Garden State twelve years ago.
