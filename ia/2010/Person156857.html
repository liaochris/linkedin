Oded Ran’s Summary

Product Marketing leader with extensive international experience in launching web and mobile products and driving business growth. Top tier MBA.

I thrive in and am excited about any initiative-oriented positions, especially launching new products or entering new markets. I bring with me an all-rounded, whatever-needs-to-be-done, "can-do" perspective.

My primary interest lies with consumer software/internet companies that are looking for an experienced manager to join their leadership team.
