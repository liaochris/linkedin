Andrew Spence’s Summary

I am a management consultant specialising in HR Transformation. My particular emphasis is aligning HR to achieve organisational goals. I have a strong track record over 15 years for reducing costs and improving customer service for clients.

I have been involved in the design and delivery of different types of HR Operating Models including; HR Shared Services, Centres of Expertise, HR Business Partners, HR Outsourcing and HR Technology. I have worked on HR Transformation programmes globally across many different industry sectors.

I established Glass Bead Consulting to help my clients achieve their HR Transformation goals through consulting services, innovative tools and educational services. I regularly write, speak and deliver training sessions on transforming people management.

HR Outsourcing Advice – from sourcing strategy to RfPs/Vendor evaluation to transition
