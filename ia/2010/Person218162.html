Yvonne Silva’s Summary

Yvonne Silva has more than 10 years of experience in marketing communications, and web content writing. As a web content writer, she writes, edits and proofreads content for web sites and portals. Creates product descriptions, brand support materials, etc. Write content for blogs, online magazines, etc. Compose meta tag descriptions, key words, alt tags for images and titles for web pages. Plus, she update events calendar and other routine site maintenance tasks.

She was the South County & Multicultural Community Relations Project Manager for The San Diego Union-Tribune where she was responsible for identifying, developing, & fulfilling the company's multicultural community outreach strategies with a special focus on the Latino market. Prior to her tenure at the U-T, she worked as the youth marketing specialist for the San Diego Workforce Partnership (SDWP); a product manager for Academic Press, a Harcourt Brace Company; and as the audience development coordinator for the San Diego Natural History Museum. She has a BA degree in Journalism from San Diego State University & has interned for Dan Rather and the "CBS Evening News", and as a writer for a local college. An award-winning writer, Ms. Silva is often invited to speak at conferences, such as the American Marketing Association’s annual Cause Marketing Conference, on creating & cultivating cause-related partnerships, corporate social marketing and media relations. A graduate of LEAD San Diego, she has served on the following boards: San Ysidro Chamber of Commerce, San Diego Women's Film Foundation, Executive Women's Council and the MAAC Project. Plus, she is a 2005 recipient of San Diego Metropolitan Magazine’s “40 under 40” honor highlighting the region’s leaders under the age of 40.
