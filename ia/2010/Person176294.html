Roderic Langer’s Summary

Roderic Langer is an approachable and talented person with strong IT, strategy, analytic, management and leadership skills. He has experience in a variety of industries including military, manufacturing, power generation, telecommunications and hospital IT systems. Rod is a gifted team leader and has lots of success pulling together formerly dysfuctional teams and getting them to work together toward project completion. He works well in a dynamic, creative organization made up of people from across functional areas. Rod is a superb writer and uses this ability in the course of project execution to communicate effectively. His ability to motivate people, form strong project teams, gain management consensus, execute the plan and deliver the product is outstanding.

Rod is active in his community and enjoys working with non-profit organizations and community organizations such as Neighbors Helping Neighbors and The Ridgefield Runners (www.runridgefield.com). Though no longer in the military, Rod has 13 years of service to his country as a Soldier and as an Officer - up to the rank of Captain. Rod enjoys spending time with his family and riding his bike.
