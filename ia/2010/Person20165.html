Sereno Barr-Kumar’s Summary

Hands on Numerate Analyst Developer with wide experience ranging from Fixed Income to Weather (Commodity) Derivatives. Additionally extensive experience using Probability and Statistics in Pricing Models and statistical analysis of large data sets (data mining and satellite images). Authored two refereed publications and over 10 conference proceedings. Presentations at international conferences.

§ Implemented Regression, Variance Reduction Method and Hypothetical Derivative Hedge Effectiveness testing for FASB 133 Accounting in Principia (A Summit / Calypso / Murex like Fixed Income and Derivative Front to Back Office System)

§ Derivatives and Fixed-Income Stress (to Interest Rate Forward Curves) Analysis, Scenario and Hedge Effectiveness Analysis

§ Contributed to Modeling, Analyzing and Accounting for Total Return Swap (TRS) Deals

§ Implemented a Copula Model using Cholesky Decomposition to Price CDO, CDS

§ Designed and Implemented in Java Pricing Models for Energy Triggered Weather Options and Stochastic/Monte Carlo Models of correlated variables using Cholesky Decomposition for Risk Analysis of Weather Derivative Portfolio.

§ Developed a model in Java for estimating Implied Volatility using Weather Option Strike and Price data from the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME).

§ Designed and Implemented an Oracle 9i database, SQL Stored procedures on Linux for historical and daily weather data.

§ Over 15 years of Numerical and Statistical Data Analysis
