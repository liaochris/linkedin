Shannon Hawkins’s Summary

Registered Nurse with 8 years hands on experience with Cutera Lasers and Light system and Cutera clinical laser trainer for 3 years
Clinical Bio: I have over eight years experience with teaching, marketing, products and multiple laser treatment modalities in cosmetic surgery and dermatology.

2001-2008
Circulating and cosmetic nurse in office operations specializing in dermatologic surgery, MOHS, facial plastic, Ear Nose Throat, Tumescent Liposuction, Restylane, Perlane, Sculptra, and BOTOX injections Advanced Dermatology and Laser Skin Care.

I have over 8 years experience performing procedures with different Cutera Lasers including the Altus-Cutera Excel, Vantage, and eventually the Cutera Xeo with Prowave 770, Titan S, V, and XL, Pearl, & OPS600 and LP560 IPL while working at Laser Skin Care.

Other experience with Lasers includes the MedliteC3 and Q- switched Alexandrite Lasers for laser tattoo removal and Pigmented Lesion Removal. I have worked with the Gentlelase Candela laser for hair removal, Sclero Plus Pulsed Dye for Port Wine Stains and lesions, and Ruby Epi Touch for tattoo removal and pigmented lesions, and the Eximer laser for Vitiligo. Product lines used include Obagi, LaRouche Posay, Jan Marini, Prescribed Solutions, and Skin Medica. I have performed Obagi Blue Peels, salicylic/ glycolic/Jessners, TCA peels, microdermabrasion, epidermal leveling and facials quite frequently combining these treatments with different laser applications to achieve optimal results.
