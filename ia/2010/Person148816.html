Morgan Chen’s Summary

My goal is to obtain technical expertise and program management experience that leverages my background in RF/microwave, Micro Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS), circuits, and advanced packaging integration techniques.

Morgan Chen’s Specialties:

Design and development of broadband circuit integration to 67 GHz employing electromagnetic simulation, analysis, and measurement techniques using novel organic, thin-film, hermetic, SiP, CoB integration/packaging technologies, RF/Microwave Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS), and Circuits
