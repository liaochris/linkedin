Dean Nicolls’s Summary

As Vice-President of Marketing & Product Management at LiveOffice, Dean is responsible for driving the product strategy, demand generation, and branding for the company's Cloud-based email archiving and hosting solutions.

LiveOffice™ is an on-demand email archiving solution that addresses three key challenges—email storage management, legal discovery and regulatory compliance—without the headaches of managing an email archive in-house.
