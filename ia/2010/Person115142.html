Kerstin Nelsen Strom’s Summary

QUALIFICATIONS PROFILE:
I am a creative leader with 6 years working experience in product management: from conception to production. An experienced team manager, providing research analysis with a specialty in trend forecasting, color marketing, and sustainable design. I am well-informed in sustainability issues and methods including material selection, product end-of-life strategies and innovation techniques. I offer solid writing and oral communication skill sets; as well as being enthusiastic, goal oriented, and resourceful. I work well in team settings as well as independently. I am a fun and adaptable person who seeks adventure and creative challenges.

I have a strong belief in environmental responsibility for oneself, the company for which one works, and products one chooses to design or support.
