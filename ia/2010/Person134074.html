Mark Kulacz’s Summary

I am a technology consultant, educator, and previous software engineer with 14 years of experience in the information storage industry.

Since July 2009 I am serving as a Storage Technology Consultant within EMC's Education department. Here I work with engineering and marketing, to develop courses and globally present/deliver the training material on how to position, select, integrate, install, tune, and support new (pre-GA) EMC products. As necessary I work directly with engineering to tune and debug products prior to release.

Until early 2009 I served 13 years as a software architect and developer (of device drivers, embedded firmware/middleware), with additional roles of team leader, researcher, customer/sales support, and software development mentor. During that time I was an EMC SME on host-side multi-pathing/HA solutions for asymmetrical (active-passive) storage systems. As a software engineer I traveled to interface directly and successfully with customers, sales, and engineering to troubleshoot issues, develop custom software releases for targeted situations, and process feedback. I am a vocal advocate of improving software quality by having software engineers move beyond cutting code, and being more engaged in the real-world utilization of the product they develop.
