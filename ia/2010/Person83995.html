Mohammad Ibrahim’s Summary

Mohammad Ibrahim has extensive experience in Information Technology, and Semiconductor Business at companies including Intel Corp, AMD, National Semiconductor, GE and Spansion. He established Advanced Information Technology organization at SEMATECH (consortium of industry and government), and introduced Cost of Ownership (CoO), OEE and Virtual Enterprise concepts in Electronics Industry. He was instrumental in establishing "Factory Integration" as a focus thrust area at Semiconductor Research Corporation (SRC). Mohammad has also served as chairman of a number of technical advisory boards, including SEMATECH, SRC, and National Technology Roadmap Committee for SEMI (NTRS). He is senior member of IIE, SME, AMA and AABEA.
