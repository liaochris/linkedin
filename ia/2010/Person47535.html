Danielle Pagano’s Summary

Danielle has a PR powerhouse track record of securing widespread media attention for clients in the travel and food, wine + sprits divisions of Quinn & Co. The architect of numerous award-winning public relations campaigns, Danielle began her career working with South African Tourism, Fodor’s Travel Publications and Stolichnaya Vodka. Danielle currently supervises strategically focused campaigns for Jim Lahey of Company Restaurant and Sullivan St Bakery, Chef Tony Mantuano of Spiaggia and Terzo Piano restaurants, Very Cherre, Waldorf Astoria Hotels & Resorts, Conrad Hotels & Resorts, Rome Cavalieri, Hilton Maldives Collection, The Pond at Bryant Park, Circle Line Sightseeing Cruises and more.

Danielle’s specialties include innovative and successful team management; focused brand building; a keen and constant focus on industry trends; generating creative initiatives that capture media attention; and an aerial media perspective that looks beyond the “obvious.”

Danielle has been instrumental in the development of new divisions at Quinn & Co., including the Food, Wine & Spirits branch, which she spearheads.

Although Danielle’s primary passions are travel, food, wine + spirits, her PR expertise spans a wide range of sectors. She served as director of PR at a vertically integrated marketing agency specializing in interior design and gift industries and worked as a meeting planner for small and large-scale events at a healthcare non-profit agency.

Danielle’s high energy in execution is matched by her unblinking passion for strategy, and her constant flow of high-level results.

Danielle is an active member of New York Junior League and the American Sommelier Association. She graduated from Syracuse University with a Bachelor of Science in Communications.
