Rachel Kadushin’s Summary

As a producer I am developing a slate of 11 films with producing partner Patrick S. Salmon. The films that are ready to go into preproduction in the slate are all controlled by the two of us, most of them being written by either Patrick or myself.

These scrips range through genres from comedy, comedy-romance, action-adventure, action-drama, thriller, romance-drama, and fantasy-adventure.

The fun part about this arrangement is that I wrote the action and action-adventure ones.

Diversity in casting leads and supporting cast is important to me.

I have two letters of interest from previously produced and distributed directors with a large fan-following, and have the script placed with the rep of another director with whom I am interested in.

US Distribution company has expressed an early letter of interest.

Contacted/corresponded cast and desired cast for "The Last Bar" include two LOIs from action-actors, and reps of Oscar-winning actors expressed interest pending letter of financing.

In the comic book world, I'm the co-creator and publisher of "Heroes In Birmingham," as well as an active member in CAG, the Comicbook Artists Guild. I've been nominated for two CAG awards - Best Writer and Best Panel (organizer/leader).

I have been active at the Facebook Comic Con, and have a group/table there where you can read more about my accomplishments. Search for "Rachel Kadushin"

I directed and produced a staged reading series.
