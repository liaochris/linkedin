Vincent Parrilla’s Summary

Provide design and verification services for large scale, next generation Application Specific Integrated Circuits (ASIC). Worked extensively with UNIX based tool systems and programs, along with high level Hardware Design Languages (HDLs).

Vincent Parrilla’s Specialties:

Have been part of numerous hardware development teams for development, integration, simulation and functional verification of next generation ASIC components (SOCs). In addition, have gone through an equal number of successful "tape outs". Skill sets include UNIX tools and scripts, perl, C++, Verilog, VERA, SystemC, etc.
