Robert Rough’s Summary

An investor, senior executive, consultant and board member in technology, service, manufacturing, distribution, and retail companies for more than twenty years. In addition to founding and managing a $100 million private equity fund, I have formed and developed several start-up companies. I have closed over twenty transactions, often with primary responsibility for all aspects of the transaction, including post transaction integration of several mergers or acquisitions. The majority of my consulting and management experience has been with portfolio companies of venture capital and private equity groups including Kleiner Perkins, TH Lee, and the Robert Bass organization.

I formed Pillar Capital Management with some partners to support venture capital in Texas because it is such an underserved market for capital in the $2 million to $10 million range, creating both a market opportunity and a way to improve the state.

Prior to forming PCM, I was Chief Financial Officer for GHN-Online, Inc., a small software company in the healthcare space. In addition to the position of CFO, I was also Chief Operating Officer and Chief Information Officer at various points. At GHN, I oversaw the completion and implementation of the second generation software product and developed the processes and procedures for the conversion of the entire client base to the web-based product.

Previously I was President of Chippenhook Corporation, a manufacturing company with operations in Mexico, China and the US. Chippenhook was experiencing problems so we restructured all the operations of the company, implemented a new ERP system, made several key management changes, and reduced expenses. We shortly turned the company around, achieving excellent financial results.

Robert Rough’s Specialties:

Solving operations, finance and systems challenges across industries, mostly in high growth situations. Value-add is in building the processes and culture to take a company to the next level as it grows.
