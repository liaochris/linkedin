Chris Rimple’s Summary

An executive leader with extensive experience in merging business and technical needs to bring innovative, best-in-class consumer solutions to worldwide markets. Combines comprehensive product strategy and design expertise with deep technical knowledge to guide both product and engineering teams in delivering end-to-end experiences that delight customers. Equally capable of meeting the needs of a Board of Directors or “rolling up sleeves” to work with a team on product and engineering designs. Proven record of defining and building successful products for companies of all sizes, including 12 years with elite corporations like Microsoft and RealNetworks, reaching hundreds of millions of customers. A demonstrated history of P&L responsibility, driving revenue and margin growth through strategy and execution with an entrepreneurial, results-driven approach: creative, pragmatic, efficient, and effective. Expertise in media, cable, mobile, gaming, social networking, social gaming, virtual goods, ecommerce, productivity applications, cloud computing, web services, enterprise, and open source. Monetization experience for B2B and B2C in many forms, including subscription, ASP/SaaS, online purchase, ad-supported, and point-of-sale. Excellent organizational, managerial, interpersonal, communication, business, and technical skills.

Chris Rimple’s Specialties:

COO, CPO, CTO, VP Product, VP Engineering, and similar roles. Partner relations and merger/acquisition evaluation. Defining market, product vision, and strategy. Leading product mgmt and engineering teams. Software design, development, quality assurance, and implementation. Extensive knowledge of SDLC/PLC, Agile Methods, and Scrum. Windows, Mac, Linux, web, and mobile (iOS, Android, Blackberry) development using C/C++, Java, Apache/Tomcat, SQL/Oracle, and other tools.
