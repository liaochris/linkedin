Brad Sanders’s Summary

In search of new opportunities that will enable me to apply my unique problem solving approach and hybrid skill set.

I currently possess over 20 years of experience in all facets of technology oriented business building and operations with specialties in architecture, development and support of data-driven communication programs, transactional systems and new media. As co-founder and later Managing Director of Postfuture, Inc. in 1999, one of the leading Email Service Providers, I created products and teams which resulted in a being ranked among the top such businesses in America in 2004 by Forrester Research, which cited strengths in technology, service offering and management team. As CTO of Postfuture, my responsibilities included creation and management of the R&D, QA and Customer Service departments, architecture and development of all software products, as well as hosting services which Microsoft Corporation cited as key to their decision enable use of the Postfuture platform for their transactional messaging world-wide. Architected the transactional and online systems that led to the exclusive use of our product and service offerings by many Fortune 500 companies including Best Buy, Pizza Hut, Jenny Craig and Disney. Bringing an energy level, common sense, focus, a sense of humor, balance and drive to all professional engagements, I strive to create success for all participants of any business objective.
