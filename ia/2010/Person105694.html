Josh Cohen’s Summary

Josh Cohen has been on the leading edge of online marketing for more than 5 years. Mr. Cohen has become known in the industry as a consultative business development professional, with expertise in large-scale direct response corporate online advertising, focused on producing positive ROI while safeguarding the brand equity of his clients.

interCLICK is an online ad network that combines advanced behavioral targeting with complete data and inventory transparency, allowing advertisers to identify and track their desired audience on an unprecedented level. interCLICK's platform was built to leverage the rapidly evolving ecosystem by offering highly flexible and scalable solutions across a network of name brand publishers. We ensure the right message is delivered to a precise audience in a brand friendly environment.
