Phil Huffman’s Summary

My parents taught me to be honest, work hard, work smart, and hold myself accountable. My education as an accountant, yes, a numbers guy, taught me about accuracy in presentations and getting the job done on time. My Military career taught me about working with others to achieve specific results, oftentimes without proper resources and under less than ideal circumstances.
