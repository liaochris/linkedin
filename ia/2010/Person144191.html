Michelle Potter’s Summary

Customer service oriented public relations and marketing professional with 10 years of experience increasing awareness and support for non-profit organizations and businesses. A creative communicator and energetic project manager that builds relationships, wears multiple hats and produces quality work within a limited budget.

Michelle launched her career in 1998 specializing in public relations and development for hospitals and nonprofit organizations in Oklahoma and Georgia. In 2006, she started her own business as a communications consultant providing PR and marketing services for businesses, nonprofits and professional services firms. In March 2010, Michelle accepted a position as the community relations manager of a behavioral healthcare agency in metro Atlanta.

Michelle Potter’s Specialties:

Writing & Editing, Traditional & Web-based Communications, Volunteer Management, Media Relations, Event Planning, Project Management, Branding & Social Media, Relationship Building and Marketing Strategy
