Graham Davis’s Experience

  • New Media Director

    Office of Governor Jennifer M. Granholm

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    February 2010 — Present (11 months)

Developed the state’s first new media policy for use by state departments, and worked with each department to implement the plan. Served as media staff for the governor at events, working with reporters to ensure they had what they needed for stories. Produced and edited regular web videos and online photo albums to show Gov. Wrote content for online and offline use, ranging from major speeches and press releases to essays by the Governor on sites like The Huffington Post and Grist. Continued to grow Gov.   • New Media Consultant

    John Dingell for Congress

    (Political Organization industry)

Raised nearly $120,000 from fundraising emails and website. Drafted pieces for Congressman Dingell for The Huffington Post and Daily Kos. Assisted campaign with other rapid response and communications efforts, ranging from social media strategy to rapid response at the campaign’s debate.

  • New Media Director

    Granholm Leadership Fund

    (Political Organization industry)

    March 2008 — February 2010 (2 years )

    Planned and executed all Internet activities for Michigan Governor Jennifer Granholm’s political office. Launched one of the first gubernatorial Twitter accounts in March 2008, which has grown to over 16,000 followers. Expanded Governor Granholm’s Facebook page by over 12,000 fans. Worked to build the Michigan netroots, presenting at training sessions throughout the state and at Netroots Nation 2009. Coordinated a door-to-door field campaign during the ’08 cycle in targeted State House races; winning twelve out of thirteen races. Volunteered for the last two months of the 2008 presidential campaign at the Michigan Obama/Coordinated Campaign headquarters, working in the New Media department. Production of other content (written, graphic design, and video) for Governor Granholm’s political activities.
