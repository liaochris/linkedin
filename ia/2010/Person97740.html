Jhaymee S. Wilson, MSPM, IPMA-D™, PMP®, MPM™, CIPM™’s Summary

Jhaymee S. Wilson is a Project Management Professional (PMP®) and Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM®) with over five years of both commercial and government experience in the financial services and IT consulting industries. She is also a Certified Project Management Associate (IPMA-D) by the International Project Management Association. She has extensive experience in strategic business development, proposal management, and business analysis. Ms. Wilson recently received her Master's Degree in Project Management (MSPM) from The George Washington University to enhance her project management knowledge and skills. Her inspirational leadership style, proven track record of project success, advanced education, and devotion to the profession makes her an excellent asset.
