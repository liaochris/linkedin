AK Stout’s Summary

I work at the corporate headquarters of a franchise company called Senior Helpers (www.seniorhelpers.com) which provides professional in-home care so seniors can maintain their independence in the comfort of their own homes. My current position as Franchise Support Specialist involves training & support of our software programs and systems. Additionally I maintain our national social networking accounts.

To fulfill my true passion of social media marketing I started my own company called Saying It Social, which provides Online Marketing, Local SEO, Social Networking, Social Media Marketing Packages & Social Media Training/Coaching for Franchises and other Small Businesses.

Additionally, I sell real estate for Long & Foster Real Estate, Inc, helping both buyers and sellers in their real estate needs. I hold a staging certificate in making homes for sale the most appealing in the buyers' conscious and unconscious mind. In addition to staging in efforts to attract buyers, I also employ several marketing tactics (both traditional and social) to reach the most likely target demographic for a particular property.

I donate regularly to my favorite charities and also fundraise to not only earn additional monies but to also generate awareness for these important and varied causes. This year I am focusing on helping The Miracle for Megan Foundation raise money for genetic disorder research at the Kennedy Krieger Institute.

I strive to utilize my rapidly growing knowledge and understanding of the social media world and its vast possibilities to make a truly meaningful contribution in the marketplace.
