Matt Hewitson MAHRI Harrier HR 3900+ Connections’s Summary

Matt specialises in the appointment of HR professionals across Australia in consulting, contracting and permanent opportunities. With an emphasis on candidate care and relationship management, combined with his specialist market knowledge, Matt has an outstanding track record of recruiting HR professionals in Australia and overseas.

In the UK he provided resourcing solutions across the UK and Europe, assisting in the establishment of HR shared service centres for multinational outsourcing companies.

As a member and Vice President of AHRI in Western Australia, Matt's passion to increase the professionalism of the HR community has led him to convene an AHRI specialist forum titled "HR Business Partnering".

Matt also holds a Bachelor of Psychology degree.
