David B. Goldstein’s Summary

From the age of 7 I was the kid in the neighborhood with the lemonade stand, the paper route, the carnival for muscular dystrophy and I even had a deal with the local deli to sweep their sidewalk in exchange for sandwiches.

It's no surprise that I am today, a successful entrepreneur with multiple businesses and I still would trade work for food :). TeamBonding is the culmination of all my experiences, businesses and ventures and I have finally stopped starting and have started focusing on the growth of a business that I and everyone that is a part of it, is proud of.
