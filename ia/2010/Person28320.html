Brian Shea’s Summary

Brian E. Shea, Esq., is a licensed Attorney at Law in the State of New Jersey and the State of New York, and is an associate with Rubenstein, Meyerson, Fox & Mancinelli, P.A. Mr. Shea currently practices in the areas of matrimonial and civil litigation. Prior to becoming associated with this firm he was associated with Donahue, Hagan, Klein, Newsome & O'Donnell, P.C. Where he specialized exclusively in family law related matters. He received his undergraduate degree from St, Joseph's University and his law degree from Seton Hall University School of Law. Mr. Shea has been in private practice since September 2006. From September 2005 through August, 2006, he served a one year judicial clerkship in the Superior Court of New Jersey for the Honorable James B. Convery, Lead Dissolution Judge, Chancery Division, Family Part, Essex Vicinage
