Thomas Chipain, CRPS®’s Summary

As an investment advisor, I’ve worried that there are millions of people who don’t know how to plan for retirement and are receiving no investment advice and guidance, whatsoever. I’ve met countless individuals who do not have the time, knowledge or inclination to assess and manage their retirement plans. This is why I established ReviewMy401k.com.

No matter how much you invest or how much you have in your plan, everyone deserves affordable, professional financial advice and guidance.   ReviewMy401k.com provides individuals with an affordable, personalized, diversified Investment Recommendation for their Employee Retirement Plan (401k, 403b, 457b or TSP), working only with the Mutual Funds in their plan.
Everyone who takes our service receives four essentials for successful investing:
- A Risk Assessment Questionnaire to determine their investor type
- A personalized, professional Investment Recommendation
- Quarterly reviews and updates to show how funds are performing
- Monitoring market conditions and the mutual funds, so changes can be made when necessary

Corporate Services - ReviewMy401k.com can assess your company’s Employee Retirement Plan with a formal plan review. We also perform a Fee analysis, possibly saving your company and employees money, and a Fiduciary Liability Assessment to make sure you’re in compliance with the latest regulations.

Advisor Affiliate Program - ReviewMy401k.com also outsources services to Investment Advisors. We help Plan Advisors by dealing directly with employees giving personalized advice, as per ERISA and PPA guidelines. Advisors who are in private practice can focus on acquiring new clients and servicing existing clients, while ReviewMy401k.com handles the business of managing their Employee Retirement Plans

Bottom line – Employee Retirement Plans are our sole focus. To us your Employee Retirement Plan isn’t just funds and categories, it’s your future and we treat it that way.
