Robb Lawrence’s Summary

Salient Research and Consulting, LLC is a results-driven and serviced-oriented consulting and market research firm dedicated to improving the product development and marketing strategies of its pharmaceutical, medical device and biotech clients.

Salient's founder, Robb Lawrence developed skills and expertise in new product commercialization from his breadth of commercial experience at Merck and Company. He founded Salient to bring that his perspective and approach to the marketplace.

Salient also works with a variety of research and consulting partners across its strategic engagements.

Salient primary services are as follows:

Product Development and Marketing Consulting Services
New Product Planning
Life Cycle Planning
Portfolio Planning

Consulting for the Diabetes Market
Leveraging Robb Lawrence's deep experience in the Diabetes market, which includes significant roles on the launch of JANUVIA and JANUMET, Salient can provide a unique services and perspective on opportunities within the diabetes market.

Market Research Services
Through its preferred research partners, Salient provides an array of qualitative and quantitative market research across the globe. Salient's research services leverage the deep client experience of its founder, Robb Lawrence, which allows Salient to provide services that are attuned to the unique needs of its clients.

    Recent projects include:

    Development of a Therapeutic Buying Process
    Salient was hired to develop a Buying Process for the treatment of a disease. Salient built a framework to identify key actions and used available patient-level data to provide a quantitative assessment of opportunities in the marketplace. Salient constructed additional market research to answer new questions and knowledge gaps. The Buying Process analysis and market research was subsequently incorporated into the annual strategic planning process and the 5-year forecast.

    Strategic Consulting and Market Research for Manufacturer of Insulin Pump
    Salient was hired by the maker of a new insulin pump to provide a high-level overview of the market and identify potential opportunities for commercial success. Salient was re-hired to provide a thorough and complete review of the commercial strategy and product positioning. Salient provided the client with multiple rounds of market research through a strategic partnership. With support from Salient and its preferred partners, the client was able to successfully update the previously proposed product positioning and advance the marketing of the product.

    Strategic Consulting for New Obesity Product
    Salient was hired to provide consulting support for a new medication for the treatment of obesity. Salient provided strategic insight and consulting services on the key marketing strategies. Salient was subsequently hired to develop a workshop designed to bring consensus to the global product development team. Results from the workshop were subsequently incorporated into the revised launch strategy.

    Strategic Consulting for a Specialty Pharmaceutical Company
    Salient was hired to provide a high level strategic and scientific overview on product differentiation for a specialty pharmaceutical company. Salient coordinated and led a half-day meeting with the Chief Officers (CEO, CMO, VP - Clinical Pharmacology, etc.) of the company.
