Richard Lavely MD JD’s Summary

Dr. Richard Lavely is a physician, business executive, lawyer, author, entrepreneur, venture capital specialist and management consultant. He is the Co-Founder of eMedicine.com, which was sold to WebMD in Jan 2006. It is the leading online source of information for doctors used over 1 million times a day by doctors from 120 countries. He has founded and managed three successful health care companies. He was appointed by the Governor of Connecticut as a Commissioner to the State Medicolegal Investigations Commission. He is a member of the faculty of the Yale University School of Medicine and had previously been on the faculty of the Medical Schools of the University of Maryland, the University of Connecticut and the University of Massachusetts.

Dr. Lavely is an open networker. [LION]
