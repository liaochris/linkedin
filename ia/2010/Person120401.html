Kyle A.C. Rosenbaum’s Summary

Kyle Rosenbaum is a public relations professional for talent and consumer and nonprofit brands. Previous clients include ConAgra Foods, FedEx, Executives' Club of Chicago, Kellogg's, and Geek Squad. Kyle has secured media coverage in major TV and print outlets such as NBC's "Today" show, USA Today, Bloomberg News, U.S. News & World Report, TIME for Kids, Car and Driver, KNTV-TV in San Francisco and KDFW-TV in Dallas to name a few.

Other placements include: Associated Press, Reuters, Real Simple; Los Angeles Times; Los Angeles Daily News; Daily Herald - Cook County; Southtown Star; The Oregonian; Dance Spirit Magazine; San Jose Mercury News; Rocky Mountain News; Pittsburgh Post-Gazette; Journal Times; Asheboro Courier-Tribune; Fremont Bulletin; KTSF-TV in San Francisco; WFOR-TV in Miami; WPBF-TV in West Palm Beach; WPEC-TV in West Palm Beach; WPXI-TV in Pittsburgh; KDVR-TV in Denver; KMGH-TV in Denver; WITI-TV in Milwaukee; KVVU-TV in Las Vegas; KLAS-TV in Las Vegas; WGN-TV in Chicago; WBBM-TV in Chicago; WMAQ-TV in Chicago; CLTV in Chicago.

Much of Kyle's business and nonprofit inspiration comes from serving as a Youth Crime Watch of America board member where he has chaired and co-chaired the Membership and Marketing, Executive Director Search and Nomination committees.
