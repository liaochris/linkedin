David Siegel’s Summary

I am an Internet entrepreneur, futurist, and angel investor living in New York. After two years of research, my new book, Pull, is now on sale. It's about the future of how we use information in business and the transition from pushing information to pulling it. I hope to raise a venture fund to start new Semantic Web companies. I'm now booking keynote speeches about the Semantic Web through my agency, the Bright Sight Group - www.brightsightgroup.com .

The site for my book is www.ThePowerOfPull.com

I am starting a next-generation dating site that will change the way people think about online dating. I have a team together and we are actively seeking investment capital.

I'm currently making investments in Cambodia, both private-equity and raw land. I have made two investments personally so far and am working on a large-scale teak plantation investment. See www.dsiegel.com

I teach classes in dark chocolate.

My art collection is online at www.dsiegelart.com and my photos are at www.dsiegelphoto.com .

Books I've written:
What is Worth Doing? (1991)
Creating Killer Web Sites, editions I and II (Macmillan, 1995, 1997)
Secrets of Successful Web Sites (New Riders, 1997)
Futurize Your Enterprise (Wiley, 1999)
New book on the Semantic Web due out in 2009

I live in New York with my wife Beatrice, our son, and our two cats, Miko and Spencer. I have been to 85 countries and have at least 15 more to go. I enjoy inline speed skating, skiing, running, climbing, and adventure travel. I also collect art, wine, and chocolate. I have a personal blog at www.dsiegel.com and a travel blog at www.dsiegeltravel.com .

I'm interested in talking with entrepreneurs about online dating, the Semantic Web, and Cambodia. I am also doing a very long-term study of story structure in feature films that I hope to write a book about some day.
