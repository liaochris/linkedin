Matthew Trachtenberg’s Summary

Matthew Trachtenberg has a broad base of skills including extensive international business experience in the Asia Pacific region, his management experience spans from director level postings through senior executive management in the renewable energy field as the VP of energy services for a publicly traded renewable energy company, and more recently as CEO and COO of two renewable energy and fuel businesses.

Specialties include:
Business concept kick start
Business plan development and polishing
Business / project proforma financial modeling
Corporate and project finance
In depth technical understanding of Wind, Solar and other renewable energy technologies.



Matthew also has developed and managed a global partner education and enabling program for a venture funded company based in Austin Texas, partners included Johnson Controls and TAC worldwide.

Matthew has a consistent track record of team leadership, excellent communication and analytical skills. He has the demonstrated ability to build and lead organizations from start-up to fully functional operational entities.

He also has over 16 years in the software industry including leadership positions in several startup and ventures funded enterprise software companies such as Vignette (VIGN).

Besides professional experience Matthew brings a “Can Do Now” attitude and a network of worldwide business contacts.

Matthew also is the owner and chief editor of a major renewable energy news portal, www.mattgoesgreen.com
in this role he has covers major renewable energy events such as Windpower 2008, and the Border Governors Conference 2008 and is considered an authority in the renewable energy space.
