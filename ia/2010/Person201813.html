Mike Curtis’s Summary

Experienced Leadership development Coach

Mike has a coaching package that works with Professionals who want to increase leadership. It continues where a 360 evaluation or other assessment normally ends? Taking comments from the evaluation or assessment, Mike is able to partner with the employee to put an action plan in place. Coaching allows the client to stay focused, accountable and encourages stretch goals while creating measurable performance improvement wthin a three month time period.

How does it work?

Through weekly calls and unlimited email support, Mike works with the client to identify the areas that they need or want to improve and then sets realistic goals to obtain the desired results. Weekly coaching sheets help the client work towards their goals and breaks down the actions into manageable results. Pre and post-evaluation of employee’s progress is included.

What type of results can someone expect to see?

Through the leadership development coaching Program, employees can display:
• Greater leadership presence
• Better communication with managers and direct reports
• Focused goal-setting
• A positive influence over other individuals in your organization
• Increased interpersonal skills

Coaching is one of the most powerful tools an organization can use to development, motivate, retain key employees and increase the leadership within the organization.
