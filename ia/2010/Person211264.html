Victor Bullara’s Summary

32 years experience in HR including 19 in consulting (Ernst & Young, Hay Group, DDI). Fourteen years as VP/Director of HR for three high growth firms including one NASDAQ listed company. West Coast "guru" in assessment and selection; Certified Assessment Center Administrator, Targeted Selection (behavioral interviewing) Master Trainer. Certified Executive Coach (June, 2010). Have worked on major change initiatives for 21 Fortune 500 companies including PepsiCo, Boeing, Merck, Lilly, and Johnson and Johnson. Dedicated to aligning HR with the strategic business plan of the corporation. Integrating assessment, selection, development, succession planning and performance management around competencies. "Metrics driven HR." "Leadership Excellence Through Energy Management (TM)"

Victor Bullara’s Specialties:

Assessment and Selection, Employee Engagement, Talent Management, Retention, Performance Management, Leadership Development, Succession Planning and Executive Coaching. Driven to implement HR Best Practices. Executive Development, On Boarding, New Leader Assimilation, Productivity
