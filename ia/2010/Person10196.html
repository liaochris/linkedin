Amy Kvistad’s Summary

I have been working as a graphic designer in the Boston area for the last ten years. My approach to design encompasses creativity with practicality and experience with learning. My background includes various odd jobs including golf course gardener and zamboni driver, living oversees in Geneva, Switzerland, and a degree in sociology—all of which directly inform my work.

Amy Kvistad Design is my freelance design practice, started in 2007. I create print, web and interactive designs for small to large-size businesses. I thoroughly enjoy my clients, design challenges, detailed projects, and hard work.

My business is SOMWBA certified as a women business enterprise (WBE) and disadvantaged business enterprise (DBE).

I'm always open to new projects, so don't hesitate to drop me a message.
