Bill Macaitis’s Summary

Bill Macaitis is an innovative marketing executive with over 15 years of online experience. He has a proven track record of successfully building marketing organizations, monetizing websites, driving top line revenue and achieving exit events. Bill has successfully implemented a wide range of online marketing programs including SEO, SEM, social media optimization, display ads, re-targeting, e-mail, PR, viral marketing, video and multivariate testing. He is a respected thought leader and has spoken at Ad:tech, Search Engine Strategies, Cloud/SAAS CEO Conference, Direct Marketing Association, Search Marketing Expo, B2B Netmarketer, PubCon, Searchnomics, and iHollywood.
