Scott Crutcher’s Summary

Developer of corporate brands, information architecture, and user interface designer for web sites and web applications for over 12 years, applying e-commerce and design expertise to service a diverse client base that includes the Port of Los Angeles, El Pollo Loco, Pfizer, Hyundai and the Emmy’s. Creative leadership has earned Red Meat Design industry honors such as the Davey Awards, the Summit Creative Awards and the Horizon Interactive Awards. Red Meat Design has also been recognized in publications such as Creativity Annual and Los Angeles Magazine.
