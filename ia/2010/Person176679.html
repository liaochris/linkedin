Rogier van Staveren’s Summary

Coming from a technical background, I have evolved in to a broad skilled IT professional in the financial industry. I am focused on short to medium term projects in the field of Project Management, Technical Consultancy and Java/J2EE Application Development and System Integration.

As a pragmatic person I work towards results using procedures not as a limitation but as a way to achieve goals in an effective way. I have the ability to solve problems in the short term, am to the point and a fast learner.

I started my career as an application developer at Dutch Vision Systems, one of the major players in the Dutch machine vision industry. One year later I joined IBM, the beginning of an instructive era had come. I contributed to different customer based projects including VNU, Shell and ING. I performed different development and consultancy roles, most of them in international environments. During the seven years I worked for IBM I gained a lot of knowledge and experiences and in September 2005 I decided to resign and continue as an independent IT Specialist, focused on the financial markets.

The last eight years of my career I have contributed my expertise to projects in the banking and insurance industry. I was involved in the implementation of business changing laws and regulations like Basel 2, Mifid and SEPA. I have a good understanding of the processes and needs of the financial industry, and thus know how to perform accordingly.

- I am not looking for an employment on a permanent basis.
- Projects in the greater Amsterdam area are preferred, the Randstad is no problem, beyond can be considered. Projects abroad have my special attention.
- Contact me for information about my hourly wage.
