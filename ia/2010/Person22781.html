Beth Jackson-Bates’s Summary

I am a jack of all trades in the web world and the owner of Web Strategies Internet Solutions, LLC . I consult with small to mid-size businesses and nonprofits on social media tool selection and strategy and help my clients find effective ways to leverage these mediums to meet business and marketing goals. With over 14 years experience in web development and marketing, I enjoy putting a new twist on traditional web technology. I help my clients not only choose the right tools, but help them understand their needs and how the tools work to fulfill those needs.

I started my career developing small websites and quickly moved into managing custom software development projects. I worked with companies like USA TODAY, where I was a three-time Jewell award winner. I also have ten years experience working with associations and nonprofits. With marketing technology at its height, I am now focusing on social media marketing and what that means to the business owner and/or executive director.

I founded GoodDogz.org, Inc. in 2003 to help fill a void in the animal welfare community. GoodDogz.org is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to educating potential dog owners on dog selection and care and supporting the efforts of rescue groups. We believe that by assisting with good dog-owner matches, we’ll give each family the best opportunity for a life-long relationship with their new dog and help to keep dogs out of shelters. I currently serve on its board of directors as president.
