Christine Faircloth’s Summary

Our team concentrates on finding the perfect dream home for every client we have. Our testimonials give an insite into our level of service. It costs nothing extra to exceed expectations. If you are looking for new homes / new construction, resale properties, condos, forclosures, short sales, HUD homes, land, beach homes, vacation homes or investment properties, we can help. We will guide you throught the complex adventure we call buying and/or selling a home, while eliminating stress. We typically preview homes online as they become available, keeping you involved in the most current market conditions. We have affiliates who offer a whole spectrum of services, another words we are a one stop shop for all your real estate needs. We can assist you with mortgages, title insurance, homeowners insurance, real estate council, moving companies, cleaning companies, contractors and more.

As a life long resident, we've been selling real estate with a commitment to excellence for ten plus years. Areas of specialization are new construction properties and high-end residential properties in exclusive communities throughout Hampton Roads. Chris has been featured on the cover of “Broker Agent” magazine, NBC’s local network on "Wavy TV 10 - Expert On Your Side", and featured in an article by “Mr. Internet” Michael Russer for the use of VA's (virtual assistants) in real estate. Currently she is a Featured Agent with the Dupont Registry. Enthusiastic almost to a fault, Chris and her team have long been strong advocates and believers in exposing properties to the appropriate national and international venues before it was “en vogue”.
