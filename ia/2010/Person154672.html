Nicole B. Lieberman’s Summary

NBL Styling is a well-recognized and well sought out business that lends Nicole's expertise on fashion and eye for apperal that has captured the Bay Area's attention. She has been featured as a top stylist in Northern California and is segueing her way into television.

Nicole's tireless efforts to connect her love for fashion with her non profit education and experience has helped many underprivledged families in the bay area. She has established a one of a kind on going donation service, titled "The NBL Bridge Program" which is set up to bring her clients unwanted apparel to local charities in need. Her successful efforts have been featured in the local news, commending her efforts to bridge the gap between socio economic classes in the San Francisco area.

In addition to her efforts to help those without she has succesfully been lobbying and advocating for Stem Cell research for the past 5 years . She consulted for two doctors at Stanford University to raise money for the non profit: ERIM (ELIXR Institute for Regenerative Medicine). She was the co-host to the world renown SOSCA 2008 conference (State of Stem Cell Advocacy) with speakers that included: Michael J. Fox, Robert Klein and Broke Reeve among the few.

She recently lobbied on Capital Hill for issues such as: Iran, Medicaid, and Autism
