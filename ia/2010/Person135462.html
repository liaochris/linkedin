Job Responsibilities:

Create & deliver client-facing strategic planning presentations to help them leverage interactive communications to improve bottom line results.

Continually gather data, analyze options and develop recommendations and plans to support business improvement initiatives using interactive digital media, including web sites, social media and mobile devices.

Work with internal and client cross-functional teams to conceptualize and implement online marketing campaigns with interactive media, e-mail marketing campaigns, paid search (SEO, SEM, PPC, affiliate programs and web site content optimization.

Directly utilize web analytics tools, including Omniture Site Catalyst, Omniture Visual Science Workstation, Web Trends, Google Analytics to analyze, diagnose and track web site and online marketing performance.

Assess performance to-date, surface trends and issues and most importantly, identify future directions for the interactive communications programs.

Create strategic briefs that help define the current business situation, performance metrics and the strategies to meet the clients business objectives

Conduct content audits to identify key content types, functional elements, information flows.

Assist the new business function with research, presentation development and new business pitches.
