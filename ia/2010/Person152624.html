Neal Kirschner’s Summary

Global expertise in technology risk management, information protection and assurance with extensive experience in IT security, compliance and audit -- including establishing and leading these functions for major organizations.

Neal Kirschner’s Specialties:

Proficient in a wide range of technologies, as well as applicable regulatory and industry requirements.
