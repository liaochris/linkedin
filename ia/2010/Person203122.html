Thomas Del Monte’s Summary

Goal: grow Interra Energy, LLC to be a highly profitable yet socially responsible and environmentally sustainable biomass-to-energy/biochar clean energy business.


Experience: State energy law and policy, papers on Kyoto GHG markets, CDM project analysis, technology market entrance analysis. Chosen to give an oral presentation at the International Biochar Initiative's 2008 Conference in Newcastle, UK on monetizing, in Kyoto carbon markets, the environmental benefits of clean energy production using pyrolysis of biomass to create biochar for soil applications.

Thomas Del Monte’s Specialties:

Business strategy & tax planning. Biomass pyrolysis/gasification technologies. Anaerobic Digestion. Extensive knowledge of clean energy related law and policy. GHG markets.
