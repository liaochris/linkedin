Barry Cutler’s Summary

I led the Buying and Supply Chain teams at Wilkinson delivering strong results consistently over two decades. Now I own my own company called Auditel: a Cost management consultancy. We save business people time and money by managing their overhead Purchasing for them. We become like a member of their management team, delivering results but carrying no cost. We use a comprehensive process to optimise the fit, service and cost of business overheads. Independence from suppliers and taking no commission puts our interests in line with our Client. When they win, we win, ONLY when they have saved money, do we earn, which motivates us to drive down consumption, with environmental and commercial benefits. Outsourcing gives access to specialists in every category of cost, and also negates the hidden costs of managing your overheads internally. Our No Win, No Fee model means the Auditel service is completely self-funding and cash-flow positive. Our clients benefit from a self funding expert member of their management team who constantly drives down cost, whilst managing product and service in line with the needs of their business.
