Petter Thune-Larsen’s Summary

I have been in the consulting business since 1981. Main experience areas are:
- RFID and EPC
- IT-strategies and IT-project planning and management
- IT procurement processes (ERP-systems and integrated solutions)
- Process Development
- Business Case
- Logistics Planning and Supply Chain Management

My main client since 2006 is RFID Innovasjonssenter AS (Norwegian RFID Innovation Centre). I am now doing consulting projects for clients planning RFID/EPC-projects within Supply Chain Management, Asset Management and othe areas. I have done several RFID PreStudy analysis and is now working in several RFID innovation projects, incl "Smart Product flow" (FCMG/Retail) and Nice Travel (aviation).
