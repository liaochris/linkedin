Emily Mullen’s Summary

As a former journalist and senior publicist specializing in the development and execution of national publicity/marketing campaigns, I have over 7 years of experience as a publicist, which includes 5 years in the publishing industry utilizing both traditional print and broadcast media outlets and the latest digital/social media. I currently work part-time as the publicity director for Get There Media, an independent brand development and promotional media firm, and am available for other freelance/PT/contract work as well as potential FT opportunities.

Emily Mullen’s Specialties:
