Katherine Isacks, MPS, RD’s Summary

Katherine Isacks, MPS, RD is a registered dietitian committed to helping individuals achieve wellness and disease risk reduction through Nutrition Education. Katherine earned her Bachelor’s degree in Biology and her Master’s degree in Nutrition from Cornell University. She is also a certified Health Fitness Instructor with the American College of Sports Medicine. Katherine is passionate about Nutrition Education since she feels that it empowers individuals to take control of their health and thrive. She uses an interactive approach to educate groups and individuals about foods and healthy eating to reduce risk for common Nutrition-related chronic diseases such as heart disease, hypertension, and diabetes. Her talks are engaging and accessible to a wide range of audiences since she uses a variety of instructional methods. Her work experience includes diabetes education, clinical dietetics (with hospitalized patients), genetic metabolic diseases, personal training, dance instruction, software training, and research.

Katherine Isacks, MPS, RD’s Specialties:

Wellness, weight control (gain, loss, or maintenance), pre-Diabetes, and disease risk reduction (Diabetes, Hypertension, Heart Disase).
