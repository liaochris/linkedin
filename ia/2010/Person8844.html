Amanda Cameron’s Summary

Amanda is a dedicated, organized, and reliable worker, be it in her work as a stage technician, a PA, or as a photographer. She is currently working as a product photographer for Joseph Gann Jewelers as well as doing overhire stage technician work with such companies as Boston Lyric Opera and New England Conservatory. She has worked for STOMP's Tour of the Americas as well as American Idol Productions.

She aims to continue to work in both film and theatre and is relocating to LA on March 16. She is looking to make connections with theatre folks, art department folk and photographers.
