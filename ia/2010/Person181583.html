Sandeep Giri’s Summary

Technology Entrepreneur. Solar PV Systems Developer/Distributor in Nepal. Business Intelligence (BI) software expert with broad experience in launching On-Demand (SaaS) BI applications for advanced interactive reporting and predictive analytics.

Sandeep is the President of Gham Power. When his off-shore software development work was impacted by the energy crisis in Nepal (with up to 16 hours of daily load-shedding), he decided to launch Gham Power as a provider of quality solar technology for Nepali homes and businesses at affordable prices ("Gham" means "Sun" in Nepali).

Sandeep is also the Project Lead of OpenI (http://www.openi.org), an open source business intelligence software company, which provides BI technology for on-demand (SaaS) deployments. Sandeep and his team at OpenI provide complete software support, integration and customization services to build domain-specific business intelligence solutions.

Prior to OpenI, Sandeep was the CEO/CTO of San Francisco-based Loyalty Matrix, an on-demand marketing analytics software company, which he grew from a 5-person startup to a leading provider of marketing analytics with over $2 million in annual revenue, and led to a successful exit when Loyalty Matrix was acquired by Responsys, a major email marketing company, in 2007. Post acquisition, Sandeep helped Responsys integrate and market this technology as their own analytics product line.

Sandeep holds a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Nebraska, and has been published in numerous software industry journals. He is part of the Clean Technology Program at University of California at Berkeley. Sandeep also serves on the board of directors of Himanchal Education Foundation (http://himanchal.org), a non-profit focused on developing self-sustainable K-12 schools in Nepal.

Read his blog at http://sandeep-giri.blogspot.com or follow his tweets @sandeep_giri or @ghampower
