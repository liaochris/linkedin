Stephen Mohr’s Summary

Mohr and Associates, LLC, is a boutique, contingency search firm specializing in placement of finance and accounting professionals throughout the Philadelphia Metropolitan Area. We offer financial search experience within our local market as well as other major markets, including the New York City Metropolitan Area and the San Francisco Bay/Silicon Valley Market.
