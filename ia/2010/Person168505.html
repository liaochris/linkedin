Corri Taylor’s Summary

I am a freelance Graphic Designer and the owner of Ram Head Graphics, Inc. Through school and self-teaching, I have learned HTML, XHTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and have enhanced my techniques in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, and Flash. My focus has always been computer enhanced graphics and I am broadening my skills by also creating websites.

I have achieved my A.A. in Graphic Design and my goal on LinkedIn is to establish a strong network of like minded people who, like me, are ready to share information and contacts and provide good advice.

As I develop my reputation and network both in the United States and overseas, I am looking to generate a client base. At this early stage in my career, I am willing to work Pro-Bono or as an intern in order to enhance and grow my portfolio and global knowledge.

I would love to be hired as an employee somewhere, but I also know that you have to take it step-by-step to get ahead (at a fast pace of course ... lol). When it is all said and done, my goal is to not only be one of the best at my skills but to understand my craft and use it as a tool to help others as others have and are helping me.

Corri Taylor’s Specialties:

Logo design, envelope design, invitations, business cards, flyers, playbills, letterheads, website design/layout, postcards, website maintenance, brochures, desktop publishing, e-invites, posters, flash animation, car magnets, photo restoration/correction, HTML, XHTML, CSS, Adobe Creative Suite 4, and Microsoft Office XP.
