Anaisabel Garcia’s Summary

As Chapter President, Anaisabel Garcia has been an achieved PRSSA member for four years and has played an active leadership role within the William C. Adams chapter. Her professional background includes Press and Production Manager for a small luxury handbag company, Posse, interning in the PR Department at Bloomingdale's and currently assists in planning all the events for the government municipality, City of Doral.

As a proud Florida International University Golden Panther, Anaisabel participates and holds campus leadership roles as the 2009 Marketing and Publicity Chair for FIU's Relay For Life, a proud sister of Alpha Xi Delta and Student Government Association Senator of the School of Journalism and Mass Communication.

Anaisabel has represented her chapter locally by receiving the PRSA Miami EV Clay Scholarship as well as represented her chapter on a national level by attending PRSSA National Leadership Rally and PRSSA National Conference. Upon graduation, she hopes to move to New York City and pursue a career in Fashion/Culture Public Relations and dreams of working for a firm that concentrates on luxury and international products and services.


Her interests in PR are in event planning and fashion. She holds a position with The City of Doral as the Events Coordinator Assistant, planning all of the City's annual events as well as the online marketing chair for the City's Relay For Life. She has recently completed a fashion internship managing all of the PR conducted in-house for a fashion handbag line called Posse. Due to her outstanding work with Posse, she has been hired as a freelance Marketing and PR Specialist, where she handles all the social media networks, website and customer service for the line.

Her future goal is to open her own PR Firm specializing in fashion, art, and cultural events in Miami, New York, and LA.
