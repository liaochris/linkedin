Jesse Janson’s Summary

Hip Hop Demographic Has New Online Home.

GetRightMusic.com is one of the fastest growing blogs on the internet today, according to Alexa.com. Dedicated to posting and reviewing promotional hip hop mixtapes, GetRightMusic.com is updated daily with the latest hip hop mixtapes that are released on the market. Mixtapes are perhaps the most important pre-release promotional tools used by the hip hop record labels, and somehow Get Right Music reviews these “tapes” virtually as soon as they hit the streets of New York and LA. Of course today they are CDs, not tapes, or more accurately, digital files, and GetRightMusic.com finds those files faster than anyone. The hip hop blog sources the latest mixtapes from such well known talents as Dj Clue, Dj Drama, Big Mike, Clinton Sparks, Dj Kay Slay and many more. As well as posting hard-to-find links to free downloadable mixtapes, the website is updated daily with the latest hip hop news, music videos and promotional music.

Get Right Music provides “one stop shopping” for the fast-growing hip hop market. Informal demographic analysis of the site indicates that the overwhelming majority of its visitors are urban and suburban males from the United States between the ages of 15 and 35. The site has also seen a steady increase in its international traffic. This is a highly sought-after demographic to Madison Avenue agencies, especially those representing products geared to hip hop culture, ranging from music CDs to Hollywood movies and DVDs, from urban fashion labels to automobiles, from soft drinks to imported vodkas, from iPods to cell phones. GetRightMusic.com is a viable outlet for targeted online marketing of your company or product to the 18-35 Hip Hop Demographic, whether for motivated clickthroughs or for brand building impressions.
