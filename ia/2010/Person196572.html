Stuart Smith’s Summary

Experienced international recruitment and executive search professional posessing strong experience of working in the staffing and recruiting industry with a particular focus on banking. Experienced at both contingent and search and selection recruitment for clients in the UK and Middle East.

Founded in 1960, reed is a leading global recruitment business with a proven track record of delivering human capital solutions across Europe, Asia Pacific and the Middle East. Reed employs over 4,000 people in 400 offices world-wide who operate in over 24 vertical markets. This global network gives access to specialist consultants, each with superior knowledge of specific professions and industries.

Reed established their office here in Doha, Qatar in 2007 and are licensed by the Qatar financial centre (QFC).

I offer a full consultancy service to ensure that I really understand my clients requirements and strongly believe in building long term relationships with candidates and clients alike.

My business is built on reputation, referalls and the ability to meet my clients requirements, so I constantly strive to deliver beyond expectation and I am glad to say that I have a long list of very satisfied customers.
