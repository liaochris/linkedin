Steve Outing’s Summary

Since 1993, I've been immersed in Internet media and news industry innovation -- as a writer, columnist, editor, author, researcher, blogger, consultant, and entrepreneur. Currently spending most of my time founding the Digital Media Test Kitchen at the University of Colorado at Boulder, a cross-disciplinary research initiative working to shape the future of news via:
- New news business models
- New techniques for the practice of journalism and digital news publishing
- New technologies adapted to furthering the news industry (old and new)
I also do advice/consulting for the news industry.

Most recently, I founded a web-media company (applying grassroots media and social networking to adventure sports) that closed in late 2007. I was the interactive-media columnist for Editor & Publisher Online ("Stop the Presses!") from 1995 until December 2009. In 2000, I was awarded an "EPpy" for Outstanding Individual Achievement in serving the online-news industry.

From 2001 to 2006 I was a senior editor at the Poynter Institute for Media Studies and headed up the Eyetrack III study. Prior to 1993, I had a traditional newspaper and magazine writing/editing/graphics career. My last non-online job was at the San Francisco Chronicle, where I happily toiled for 7-1/2 years before realizing that the Internet was more fun.

Steve Outing’s Specialties:

News innovation; grassroots/citizen media; social networking; digital publishing; news websites; mobile content & services; online comics; eyetracking research; multimedia content; online-media business models; etc.
