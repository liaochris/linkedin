Stephen G. Rutledge’s Summary

With over twenty five years experience in professional broadcast production and operations, plus skills in business development, project management, strategic planning and digital asset management, Mr. Rutledge is a seasoned Film, Video & Station Automation expert.

Past Projects:

Mr Rutledge Produced & Directed One Hr, National PBS Special called "The Brain in Love", with Dr. Daniel Amen. The special started to air nationally in August 2009. The special was shot at the Orange County Performing Arts Center, and was delivered on HDCAM Masters.

Mr. Rutledge was the Producer/Director for Live Concert Series “ Power to Change” Recorded at the LA Forum in May 2007 & Anaheim Convention Center in March 2006. Using the Shulman Mobile Video HD Truck. The four 1 Hr Specials are airing around the USA, and in Latin America

In collaboration with Partner Giacomo Moncada, Mr. Rutledge was the producer for national television commercial campaigns on behalf of JVC Canada, Warner Music Group, Exit Reality and others.

Mr. Rutledge has also been working nationally in the Corporate Event & Tradeshow market for many years. He has provided support and production for Hewlett Packard, Baxter Healthcare, Bausch & Lomb, VISX Inc., Fluke Networks and many others.

-Directed Satellite Feed of the Papal Tour of Canada for the CBC
-Produced & Directed Teen Challenge 9/11 Project
-Co-Produced & Directed 4 Day Live Event - Annual Sales Meeting EXIT Realty
-Directed 13 Episodes Slim-down TV currently airing in the North Eastern and North Western US on UPN
