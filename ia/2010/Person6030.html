Alex Kong (江庆恩)’s Summary

Graduated with a B.Sc in Travel Industry Management, Mr. Alex Kong founded AsiaTravelMart.com in June 1996 steering the Company in travel and information technology industry. The business diversified from a mere B2C travel portal to offering full-fledged travel technology, solutions and services in the B2B and B2B2C segments for the global travel industry through a company - AFOOFA. Today, the travel technology and solutions from his group of companies can be seen powering airline websites in Malaysia, Singapore, Hong Kong, Thailand, and Japan; over 3,000 hotel websites; Cruises, Express Buses, as well as numerous multinational corporation’s in-house business travel solution and travel agent websites in the Asia-Pacific region.

Alex has been personally honoured with numerous Entrepreneur Of The Year Awards. Likewise, AsiaTravelMart & AFOOFA have garnered various prestigious local and international industry awards and recognitions. Alex supports and promotes major trade issues for the industry and was an active speaker & member of several professional tourism and IT organizations.

Multilingual in English, Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia
Other Chinese Dialects: Cantonese, Hokkien, Foo Chow and Hakka
