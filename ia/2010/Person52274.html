David Waxman’s Summary

David Waxman has over 23 years experience as a Private Equity Advisor focusing on the Secondary Market, a Venture Capitalist, CEO and Entrepreneur.

I am interested in networking and connecting with other private equity professionals, especially those that are either:

- Investment Managers at Institutions considering a divestment of private equity holdings (LP interests in across all asset classes or Direct Investments in Private Companies)

- Fund Managers and investment professionals active in purchasing, advising on or investing in secondary transactions

- GP's who have insight into interesting assets at attractive pricing

- GP's that have LP's that have capital call risk issues

- GP's Managing VC, Corporate or Buyout tail end portfolios

- GP's focusing on credit strategies that are raising capital for at least a Fund III

David Waxman’s Specialties:

Strong Private Equity & Alternative Assets network across LP's & GP's, Secondary transaction management and structuring, Secondary direct portfolio management, Private Equity Investment, value-added to portfolio companies, LP and GP capital raising, entrepreneurial & operating background, executing exit strategies, strong finance background (MBA, CPA, Series 7).
