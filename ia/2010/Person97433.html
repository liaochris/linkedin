    Jewel Bracy DeMaio prepares resumes for candidates on the most executive levels, taking the time to sincerely communicate with them, envision their big picture, and then create an intelligent written document that will advance them to their next position.

    My role is to define the vision for the executive's resume, and then build the facts and achievements into an authentic, cohesive story. I partner with the executive to make sure that vision projects the person as a leader, innovator, expert, and one who motivates and empowers people.
