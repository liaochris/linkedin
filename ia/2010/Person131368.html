Marc Goldman’s Summary

As an entrepreneur and proven leader Marc understands the potential power and frequent challenges of technologies in the Architecture, Engineering and Construction industries. He is one of the leading experts in Building Information Modeling (BIM) and its impact on the building industry.

Marc began his career in the first days of CAD, and he brings over twenty years of success defining, developing, and delivering services and technologies for design, engineering, manufacturing, and construction.

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT & STRATEGIC PLANNING
- Skilled business advisor with expertise in identifying & analyzing processes, and translating them into profitable products & services
- A strong international network of AEC executives leveraged to create winning joint-ventures, partnerships and customer relationships
- Expertise in defining profitable products & services based on financial modeling, market research & industry segmentation
- Two decades of delivering products and services for Building, Construction & Manufacturing

PRODUCT MANAGEMENT & MARKETING
- Lead the effort to unify dozens of applications into a cohesive family of Design, Visualization and Analysis products
- Championed and authored Autodesk’s Interoperability and Product Consistency roadmap leading to a new company-wide product management approach
- Defined, developed and presented project visions, product plans and technology demonstrations worldwide – one-on-one meetings up to audiences of hundreds

TEAM BUILDING & LEADERSHIP
- A 20 year track record of forming, building, launching and managing profitable technologybased product and service businesses
- Lead the creation of teams, processes and new ways of working in small and large organizations - typically requiring gaining buy-in and participation from all levels, all functions and all product teams
- Formed, hired, managed, mentored & inspired technical sales and development teams in large organizations and startup environments

Marc Goldman’s Specialties:

Deep understanding of the technical and business processes of Architecture, Engineering, Construciton & Manufacturing industries with a great depth of experience in CAD and BIM.
