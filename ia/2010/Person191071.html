Brian Smiga’s Summary

Career Mission: Build rapid-growth digital transformations for large brands and business.

Business Development, Marketing, and Product Management Professional who develops large-scale, long-term products and relationships. 20+ years experience.

Most recently, built the partner business at www.smilebox.com resulting in 125% Y/Y increase in partner revenue & installs.

Before that, as CEO built Preclick Corp which developed co-branded image sharing and management software that shipped over 100,000,000 copies with HP, Walmart, Costco, SamsClub, Walgreens, Meijer, 3M & Sandisk. (sold to Smilebox, Sep 2009)

Before that sold ExchangePath to CMGi for 700% return to investors, and product managed "Best of" Palm, Mac and Lotus services at Actioneer and Portfolio, including co-authoring 4 software patents.
