Greg Bowman’s Summary

I have over 13 years of leadership experience with sales, recruiting, and management. I started my recruiting and staffing experience with Robert Half and Accountemps 10 years ago and have had the good fortune to work with other agencies during that time. I found that specializing in one area has served me well and returned as Division Director of Accountemps. I grew up in Greensboro, went to college here and have always loved the area. I had the great opportunity to start the linked in group LINKING GREENSBORO. Aside from my career with Robert Half I have the great pleasure of networking with other professionals through social media. If you are interested in networking check out linkinggreensboro.com, if you know of anyone looking for accounting professionals please contact me at greg.bowman@accountemps.com.
