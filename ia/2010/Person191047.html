Sebastian Fernandez Quezada’s Summary

Founder, CEO. He is in charge of strategy, business development and operations at Corvalius. He has graduated as a Software Engineer from the Universidad Abierta Interamericana at Buenos Aires, Argentina. After obtaining his degree, he completed its post graduate studies in Management and Administration also in the Universidad Abierta Interamericana. He still work there as a Professor.

Before Corvalius, Sebastian worked at Huddle Group from 2007 as a Project Leader and lately as a Pre-Sales Technical Leader. He also was a Software Factory Manager, Functional Analyst, Software Developer and Web Designer in its more than 10 years’ of industry experience.
