Karen Parnell’s Summary

Responsible for product marketing of audio and video solutions into consumer portable products. ARC provides total solutions that take sound to silicon in consumer products such as Portable Media Players, Portable Navigation Devices, Digital Televisions, Set Top Boxes, Digital Still Cameras, PCs and Digital Photo Frames.
Prior to joining ARC in 2009, she worked for CSR for 4 years heading up the Consumer BU Marketing team and at Xilinx Inc. for 5 years in European marketing and then moved into global product marketing for automotive products. She has also held field applications positions at Philips Semiconductor & Avnet. She gained a excellent grounding in electronics design at British Aerospace where she spend the first 13 years of her career. Parnell holds a B.Eng Hons in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Hertfordshire and an MBA from Aston Business School.
