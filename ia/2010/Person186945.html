Shadakshari Swamy’s Summary

Shadakshari, fondly known as Shadz among his friends and peers is an accomplished healthcare IT professional with many years of experience in healthcare information technology and Pharmaceutical industries. He is currently associated with iSOFT one of the leading healthcare IT organisations as Product Manager and holds responsibility of designing and developing organisation’s flagship large scale SOA based product.


Shadakshari has an extensive experience in Product Management, design and development of healthcare information systems for various markets & care setting.solutions

An entrepreneurial individual with passion for innovation & creativity he has right mix of domain and technical knowledge propelled with leadership skills to motivate and inspire teams to drive towards vision.

Holds a graduation in Bio-science from University of Mysore, MBA from Vishveshwariah Technological University and an MS from BITS, Pilani.
