Nancy Bruning’s Summary

Nancy Bruning is the bestselling author or co-author of 25 books about health and fitness including books about weight loss, vitamin and mineral supplements, beauty, spirituality, swimming, belly dance, breast implants, menopause, memory loss, Indian Medicine (Ayurveda), homeopathy, psychotherapy, and more. She has also written dozens of articles and pamphlets.

Since earning her personal trainers certificate and her master's degree in public health, she has coached people one-on-one and in groups about how to be fit in body, mind, and soul.

She is founder and president of Nancercize, a unique approach to optimizing health and body weight by customizing food, exercise, nutritional supplements, and stress management. As part of her philosophy that healthy lifestyle changes can and should be pleasurable, she has developed an outdoor exercise program called Fitness Alfresco.

For Nancy, personal and professional life are intertwined. As an undergraduate art student in the go-go era, she was a TV and runway model. After a stint living in Europe, she put her art skills to work as an editor at an arts and crafts magazine. And when she was diagnosed with breast cancer at the age of thirty, she turned that life-altering experience into two books--the beginning of a career dedicated to helping others live a healthier life.

Today, she balances her editing and communications work with a career as a wellness coach and fitness group leader.

Nancy Bruning’s Specialties:

Communicating scientific information and concepts in plain English, organizing information, public speaking, promoting health and fitness in underserved communities.
