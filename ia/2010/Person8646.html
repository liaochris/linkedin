Lynn Moran’s Summary

Former President of Ethel M Chocolates, a gourmet chocolate retail and direct-to-consumer business owned by Mars, Inc. Demonstrated business success and senior leadership with experience in consumer products & gift industries. As President of Ethel M, was a member of the Masterfoods Management Team responsible for delivering the business and financial results for a multi-billion dollar company with confectionery brands like M&M's Chocolate Candies, Snickers, Milky Way; Dove frozen snack products; pet brands such as Whiskas and Pedigree and Uncle Ben's food products. Demonstrated success in business leadership, management development, new product development, branding and financial and business results.

Certified Coach. Six Sigma Green Belt.

Breadth of experience attained via leadership positions in Human Resources, Sales, and Licensing, prior to becoming President of Ethel M. Attended high performance leadership training (Center for Creative Leadership, University of Virginia Darden School of Business training program; NTL). Undergraduate degree in Business. MBA from Fairleigh Dickinson University.

Business Advisor. Experience in executive and leadership coaching, team and company performance. Extensive coach training including Adler School of Professional Coaching.
Myers-Briggs certified.
