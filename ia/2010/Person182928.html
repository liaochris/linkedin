Sarah Wefald’s Summary

I coordinate co-op and micromarketing at Caroline Distribution/EMI Global Music Services. I'm a marketing manager skilled in working with labels, bands, and artist managers to create and execute marketing plans. I have experience working with a team specializing in artist development involved with all facets of a band's campaign.

I take great personal pride in helping talented developing artists find their footing, and I'm exploring the various ways there are to do this -- record labels, music venues, licensing opportunities, and other outlets both mainstream and off the beaten path.
