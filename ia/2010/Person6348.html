Alex Henderson’s Summary

I have many years experience in software development and have had a passion for programming languages of all shapes and sizes from a young age.

In the past 6 years I have focused my attentions on the .Net Framework and have been employing it in numerous projects, both small and large.

My current goal is to grow the DevDefined Limited business I started in 2005 to the point at which it delivers beyond expectations to clients, and internally promotes a development environment in which my staff and I would be happy to work. Additionally DevDefined is looking to partner with other businesses in New Zealand to deliver across-the-board solutions which are of a high value to all stake holders.
