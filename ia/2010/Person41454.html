Chris Lona’s Summary

My intent is to enhance and expand your credibility and experiences through design and multimedia. Your experience with us will result in a superior brand identity and multimedia presentation all while making you feel as if you are our only client.

What is your intent for your business? What kind of experience do you want your customers to have?

Located in Bellevue, Washington, CL Design provides creative and functional design for brand identity, multimedia, web, print and architecture.

We specialize in multimedia that expands the experience of your hospitality or luxury business.

CL Design creates Invention that reflects your Intention.

My goals for our clients are:

• To help them enhance and maintain credibility in their field through design and multimedia that is thoughtfully and creatively applied to the product or service they offer.

• To help them gain peace of mind and pride from knowing that their brand always reflects professionalism and confidence.

• To establish for them the freedom to focus on higher priorities other than the management of their brand and collateral materials.

• To inspire them to innovate new ideas and uses for their brand.
