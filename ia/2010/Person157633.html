Michael Martin’s Groups:

S.I.G.H.T. by M.I.K.E.
Seeing Individuals Getting Help Tonight by Martin i-Kommerce Engineering is an initiave that donates clothes and other survival tools to the homeless in Chicago. More often than not, the homeless in Chicago does not have access to the many charities catering to their needs or they do not have the motivation, information or ability to reach these charitable organizations. SIGHT by MIKE collect clothes from donors and create "survival bags" that includes bottled water, toothbrushes, tooth paste, mouth wash, hand sanitizer, facial cleanser, gloves, hats and anything else we can think of that might help out. We then pass these bags out to those living in many places including lower Wacker and State Street in Chicago.
