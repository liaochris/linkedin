Leigh M. Drake’s Summary

Purple Trout provides state of the art search engine marketing (SEM) services including: Organic Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Multilingual SEO, Pay-Per-Click (PPC) campaigns, Social Media marketing and Optimization (SMO), Video Search Engine Marketing, Usability Analysis, Conversion Tuning, Web Analytics, Competitive Analysis and more.

Headquartered in Newport, Kentucky (Cincinnati / Northern Kentucky) Purple Trout services international, national and regional clients and brands, helping our clients strengthen their presence on the internet by targeting on-site and off-site web optimization factors.

Serving as Vice President of Purple Trout, LLC is allowing me to grow professionally and technically at an exciting time in the digital world. Using my skills from Aranelle Consulting www.aranelle.com, I enjoy coaching via the proposal development process to help our clients build capacity in their organization, improve their business skills on the web, and better serve and tell their story to clients and customers.

Maintaining a preferred client list working as President and Founder of Aranelle Consulting, LLC, I still serve as a Technical Assistance Provider (customized coaching) in topics of professional development and organizational capacity building, and use our web development and technology experience as business coaching tools.

My projects have included: IT coaching, social media coaching, software support, personalized technical training, financial management & database coaching, grants management, business development, small business and non-profit organizational management services, web design and content development, business plans, technical assessments and reports with broad expertise in community and economic development topics.

As my schedule allows, I accept freelance web content development, technical consulting and nonprofit and small business strategic planning projects that might not fit within an ordinary SEO project scope.
