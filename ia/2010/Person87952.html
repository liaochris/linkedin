James Duran’s Summary

I serve as CEO of Duran HCP. Duran Human Capital Partners Inc., which has grown to be one of the largest high tech staffing companies in the Silicon Valley. I was an early contributor to the development of Resumix and an early adopter in the use of the Usenet and follow on internet tools for recruitment. I maintain my technical recruitment edge by continuing to work myself on a part time basis directly for clients while leading the company. I am also very active in legislative affairs impacting employment and employers in California.

Duran HCP focuses on providing various recruitment services including: providing contract recruiters and sourcers (individuals & teams), and Sourcer/Recruiter training/internet recruitment training programs on and off (client) site. Some of our major clients have included: AMD, Bay Networks, Chevron, Cisco, eBay, Google, Intel, Novell, SGI, Yahoo, etc.
