Stuart Reid’s Summary

I am a facilitator, trainer, mediator and coach working throughout England and abroad (I have run workshops recently in Sweden, Denmark and South Korea). I work mainly with public sector managers and their teams, and the managers of not-for-profit organisations.

I help people have conversations that bring about change - sometimes personal change through coaching, sometimes in teams through training or mediation, and sometimes with groups or whole organisations through facilitation.

When not working I play with my two boys (aged 7 and 5), play bridge, run, find geocaches, and am a member of the London-based improvisation group Just Add Lib.

Stuart Reid’s Specialties:

Facilitation, training, mediation , coaching, process facilitation, team building, post project reviews, organisational development.
