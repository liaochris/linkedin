Jene Thorne-Bryant’s Summary

Thorne-Bryant Consulting Group offers strategic knowledge and expertise in the areas of Federal, DoD and Intelligence Community Talent Acquisition and Strategic Personnel Sourcing. We deliver our services as an extension of existing efforts by seamlessly embedding ourselves into your culture and day-to-day business operations.
