• Identifying $3.5M in reusable assets establishing a standardized asset reuse function.

I’m adept at negotiating agreements, preparing proposals, and responding to RFPs, RFQs, and RFIs. I’ve been effective at overseeing large-scale, long-term projects, representing companies to professional organizations, and managing process optimization and complete development life cycles. I hold multiple certifications, including my Project Management Professional, Master Certified IT Architect, and Program Manager designations.

I am able to design and introduce effective policies, procedures, and processes in technical and business areas, and I’m skilled at working with customers to identify needs and resolve issues effectively and quickly. Throughout my career, I have consistently produced successful technology improvements that have led to dramatic increases in profitability, efficiency, and productivity.
