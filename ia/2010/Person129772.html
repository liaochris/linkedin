Madelyn Bonnett’s Summary

An experienced copywriter, editor and advertising/direct marketing/communications professional with a reputation for producing high-quality, accurate work. Thrive in creative and collaborative business relationships. A stable, flexible and consistent team player with experience on both the corporate and agency sides. Through MadWritz, I assist clients with their communications strategies and fulfill their writing, editing and/or proofreading needs.

Through my original photography & photo arts business, MadArtz, I create and offer high-quality nature, landscape & inspirational photo prints, note card sets & other photo art items for sale direct or for distribution through retail establishments in the central Alabama area.
