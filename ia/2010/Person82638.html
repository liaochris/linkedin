Mark Sangerman’s Summary

National Recruiting Director for ShipTransportal, Inc., a rapidly growing 3PL and provider of transportation solutions. Performs nationwide recruitment of logistics sales professionals while providing assistance to existing agents.

Mark Sangerman is the founder of professional training web sites http://www.homebasedrecruiter.com and http://www.recruitertrainingvideo.com where he promotes a complete training package on how to start a recruiting business or staffing agency. Mark's training package features a 3 hour training DVD called "The Complete System on Achieving Success in Recruiting & Staffing", a 2 CD audio series, "Start Your Homebased Recruiting Business!", additional training CDs on how to become an independent recruiter, and a full set of recruiting fee agreements, contracts and forms for conducting business.

In addition to being a trainer since 1997, Mark has over 15 years of hands-on experience as a headhunter, reatined recruiter, staffing consultant, and on-site employment advisor to numerous companies both small and large. Today, Mark's primary goal is to provide outstanding training products on recruiting and staffing, while maintaining the highest standards for integirty and quality in the marketplace.
