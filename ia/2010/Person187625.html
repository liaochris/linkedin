Sharad Sharma’s Summary

My personal mantra is to lead big and small companies through orbit change.

Most recently I was the CEO of Yahoo! India R&D and was responsible for emerging markets engineering and several key global products. My 23+ yrs of experience in internet, software and wireless sectors involves a turnaround (of VERITAS India ops), a startup (now part of Cisco), and an intrapreneurial setup of AT&T/Lucent’s R&D in India.

I am an evangelist for developing technology product businesses in India and participate in efforts to nurture the supporting ecosystem. I chair the NASSCOM Product Forum, actively mentor startups, and speak frequently on industry issues.
