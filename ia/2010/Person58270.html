Donna Fedor’s Summary

Donna Fedor is the founder of Results Thinking Solutions and the Re-THINK! Program. As a Master Results Coach, Corporate Trainer, Speaker and Recruiting Expert, she specializes in educating people on how to maximize their results in their job search, career, business and personal lives. She amplifies self confidence and teaches people how to move beyond their limiting thoughts. Donna is a Certified Performance Consultant and Master Results Coach and utilizes her background where she has many certifications in Accelerated Learning, including Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), Neurological Repatterning, and Hypnosis to assist her clients to improve their thinking by successfully reframing and retraining ineffective thought patterns.

With over twenty years of coaching, career counseling, recruiting, training and sales experience, Donna brings a wealth of knowledge and inspiration to her clients. She works with corporations, outplacement groups, one-on-one and with mastermind groups. In addition, she leads ongoing workshops, telecourses and seminars.

A dynamic seminar leader and speaker on today's most critical issues, Donna uses stories from her own life, leadership, coaching, recruiting, and sales experience to bring the qualities of learning to life. Donna’s speeches and workshops are completely different. She specializes in uplifting participants through simple, specific, easy‐to use solutions for complex problems. This is verified by raving testimonials from all over.
