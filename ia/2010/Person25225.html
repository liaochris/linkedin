Bobbi Hoff Beehler’s Summary

Bobbi K. Hoff Beehler is a certified public accountant and is a manager with Pisenti & Brinker LLP. Mrs. Hoff has also received her Chartered Financial Analyst license as well as her Real Estate Salesperson license. In 2008 she received her EcoGreen certification and was recognized as one of the top “40 under 40” business professionals in the North Bay by the North Bay Business Journal. Mrs. Hoff Beehler serves on the Measure O Oversight Commission for the City of Santa Rosa. She is the Vice-President of Finance on the Executive Committee of the Board of Directors of the Boy Scouts of America, Redwood Empire Chapter. She also serves on the Chamber of Commerce Economic Vitality committee and is the Chairwoman for the 7th North Bay Growth and Investor Forum. Most recently she was a candidate for Santa Rosa City Council.

Bobbi K. Hoff Beehler, CPA, CFA is also a licensed REALTOR, EcoGreen Certified and Accredited Staging Professional serving Sonoma, Marin, Lake and Mendocino Counties. Hoff Beehler was nominated for Santa Rosa REALTOR of the year in 2004.

She holds a BA from UC Santa Barbara in Business Economics and Accounting as well as a BA in Sociology. Mrs. Hoff Beehler has previously held positions as a manager with Deloitte & Touche LLP where she performed audit services as well as assisted companies contemplating public stock offerings. She was an Associate Research Analyst with RBC Dain Rauscher covering branded consumer and specialty retailing stocks where she prepared financial models, determined valuations, wrote research reports and gave investment opinions on the companies she followed. She has also served as the Director of Investor Relations for a publicly traded internet company where she participated in three private security placements and led teams in a public offering and eventually the sale of the Company to WebMD.

Hoff Beehler is ia long-time resident of Santa Rosa and a graduate of Santa Rosa High School and UC Santa Barbara.

Bobbi Hoff Beehler’s Specialties:

As a CPA, Bobbi concentrate on business valuations, management advisory services, corporate taxation and assurance services. In 2008 she co-launched the Sustainable Business Practice Group at Pisenti & Brinker where she advises companies on adopting sustainable practices including operational reviews and examinations of green tax incentives and rebates.

Bobbi also specializes in Residential Real Estate helping clients buy and sell homes and land in Sonoma, Lake and Mendocino counties.
