Deborah Rodney’s Summary

Creative Director, Advertising/Marketing professional

Specializing in separating our clients from their competition and raising their profile by creating unusual, attention-getting advertising and marketing communications, both on-line and in print. Developing and strengthening clients' brands and helping them grow through smart strategic marketing coupled with creative executions that get noticed and get results.


I began my career working as a staff art director at several large ad agencies in Los Angeles, including Foote Cone & Belding and Ogilvy & Mather. Very early on, I left the corporate world and began a freelance career as an independent creative consultant/art director and was called on to work for most of the major Los Angeles advertising agencies on a freelance basis. I also worked for smaller agencies and marketing firms as their outsource creative service.

As the ad industry began to change in the early 90's, I shifted my business model to that of a design firm, working directly with clients to create marketing communications such as corporate identity programs, brochures, ad and direct mail campaigns, packaging and other printed collateral.

In January of 2000, I opened The Next Level Marketing & Creative, LLC with partner Don Spector, a former Creative Director with BBDO West and Foote Cone & Belding. Over the past 10 years we've succeeded in providing our clients with the high level creative work that springs from our experience in the big agencies, but at costs that are affordable on their more modest budgets.
