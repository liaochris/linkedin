Paul Nicholas Carlson’s Summary

- Have gained news writing/producing/editing experience in several news formats, including radio (CBS News in Seattle), online (MSN.com and MyNorthwest.com) and print (The Bellingham Herald).
- Principle journalism interest is news judgment, specifically 'why' certain stories are elevated to the level of a 'top story' while others are demoted if not completely ignored. This means always asking what is 'news' - and how do various media sources cover news stories differently? And what do these differences mean and say about the journalism industry?
