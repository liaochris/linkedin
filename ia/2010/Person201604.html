Thang Nguyen’s Summary

In 1977 I watched "Future Shock" at Penn State. Little did I know, I have been living it: from covering the Three-Mile-Island-nuclear incident with ABC- news affiliate as a video intern (technology) to volunteering in the South China Seas and West Africa (NGO). Then, throughout my 16-year international telecom (sales) career, I have assumed the role of an "innovation diffuser" i.e. to commercialize tech inventions (e.g. Voice mail, VoIP, wireless) to both Business Market and International Consumer Markets. I look forward to connecting with good team players like yourself as we brace for "future shock" and "culture shock" together.
