Stuart Bartram’s Summary

Stuart Bartram leads advertising sales across Australia and New Zealand at LinkedIn, the world’s largest professional network with over 65 million members. His team works with the world's most widely recognized brands and advertising agencies to help them connect with LinkedIn’s audience of affluent, influential, and ambitious professionals.

Stuart has 13 years advertising sales experience, with the last 7 years in high growth channels of online advertising including search marketing, performance display, affiliate marketing and now social media marketing. Prior to online, he spent 6 years in print media sales. Stuart has worked for global leaders including Google, DoubleClick, ValueClick and Fairfax with international experience covering Australasia, North America and Europe.

Stuart is a well respected advertising professional with executive level interpersonal, communication, relationship building, business development and negotiation skills. He has a strong network of contacts across marketers, agencies, publishers, ad networks and technology providers.
