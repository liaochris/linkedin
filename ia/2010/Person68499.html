Shona Lepis’s Summary

Eye Design Studio is a boutique design studio in Portland, Oregon. We work with lifestyle brands to translate your vision into artisan handcrafted Web site and print design to effectively brand and market your business.

Eye Design Studio partners with clients to develop their unique identity that conveys their brand and their philosophy using standards of classic elegance and simplicity the rich language of form and color.

Visiting a Web site, receiving a newsletter or seeing an ad produced by Eye Design Studio informs the viewer about the business through From Web site design to print design clients love the personal attention and creative results. Clients range from the luxury hospitality industry such as inns, restaurants, boutiques and wineries, to small law firms and independent business owners. Eye Design Studio collaborates with a network of select like-minded professionals to meet each project’s specific needs.
