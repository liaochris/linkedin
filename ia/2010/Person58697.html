Dorothy Lubey’s Summary

A full service interior design and consulting firm, Parker Rose specializes in home merchandising. Our services provide a strategic advantage to homeowners, home builders, realtors, spec buildres and developers, property managers, and the apartment industry with unique and cost-effective interior design solutions to help sell or rent a property quickly, and at the best possible price.

The Parker Rose philosophy is founded on a commitment to delivering the highest standards of excellence in design, service and investment value. By joining the unique features of each space with stylish, high-end furnishings and accessories, our innovative and experienced interior designers know how to create a lasting impression with potential buyers in every home we work in.
