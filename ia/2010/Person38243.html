Chris Leach’s Summary

310 Creative is a subsidiary of Media, LLC located in Los Angeles, CA. providing digital creative and production services, resulting in successful digital branding launches and campaigns for global entertainment talent and corporate brands. Our creative and production teams are made up of highly talented and award-winning seasoned professionals with a strong integration of multidisciplinary talents in online, branding, marketing, print, packaging, motion graphics, 3D, television, film and business management. We value the authenticity and vision of its custom creative solutions, implementing a genuine, innovative values-based approach for each client project and we rely on our core values to provide inspiration for every client project.

Our strategic process combines industry expertise with working knowledge of our client’s business models in order to create increased brand and economic value. This process allows our creative and production teams to identify specific industry challenges, opportunities and the best practices within a client’s unique competitive business environment. Our teams analyze a clients future technology and creative needs to determine strategic and quantifiable goals to ensure that clients initiatives effectively communicate through the appropriate media and message vehicles for each unique brand market, customer demographic, product or service.
