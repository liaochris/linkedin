Brent Peterson, PMP, MS, MBA’s Summary

Hi! Thanks for visiting my profile. I have over fifteen years of experience in corporate mangement and consulting. I am a proud graduate of JMU (twice!) and UVA. I am also a certified Project Management Professional (PMP).

I am a proud employee of the Federal Reserve Bank. I am also the founder of a company called Interview Angel, Inc. based in Ashland, VA, the Center of the Universe. We deliver our product nationwide and have helped customers as close as the University of Richmond and as far away as Honolulu.

My company in Ashland also organizes career speed networking events to get professionals connected and to raise money for charity.

I enjoy meeting good folks at Starbucks to discuss career strategies, entreprenuership, football and the latest episode of Lost.

My goal is to help 100,000 people fulfill their professional dreams.
