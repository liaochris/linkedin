Kathy Nelson’s Summary

Are you looking for an experinced coach to help you live your dream life, rocket your career, and start or build a business?
Would you like to zap procrastination or become a toleration free zone using your full potential?

Do you need a coach to help your employees add more value to your company and improve your profits?

Before starting On Track Success Coaching, Kathy spent two decades in Corporate Business Development (sales) and was Sales Manager for San Diego County. She helped clients with biz planning, marketing campaigns, branding and goal setting. She was #1 in her territory and reached gold and platinum achievement levels consistently. Kathy mastered providing great customer service and taught it to her team.

Starting OTSC in '96, Kathy continued to support and partner with clients for success in their goals and dreams. Kathy coached thousands of Realtors for By Referal Only, wrote and led their teleclasses nationwide for over four years.

Kathy is a Certified CTI Coach and offers both business and life coaching for change, values clarification, planning, goal setting, identifying purpose, finding your passion, happiness, enhanced performance, self-development, and navigating transitions and transformations.

As a *wired senior* she helps clients go viral with social media, start blogs, write online, building their expert status and visibility.

Clients have come from Media companies, Air Freight, Cal Trans, City and County employees, C level executives, Entrepreneurs, Bio Pharmaceuticals, Attorneys, Medical professionals, Real Estate, Small business owners, Teachers, Soloprenuers, Financial planners, Insurances, Marketing, Non-profits, Sales, Coaches, Retirees, and Creatives (authors and artists).

Please visit her web site www.ontracksuccesscoaching.com
and join her here and on Twitter and Facebook.

  • VP Sales and Marketing/Business Development

    Fidelity Nat'l Title Insurance Company

    (Public Company; fnf; Real Estate industry)

    1978 — 1995 (17 years )

    Kathy moved to San Diego in 1978 after a few years working retail with Nordstroms and a Marshall Field store, in Seattle. She benefitted from the great customer service training that is the Nordstrom's way and handling the Esteee Lauder and Elizabeth Arden cosmetic lines.
    As a single Mom, starting anew in San Diego, she landed a sales position in Title Insurance. It was perfect for her. Kathy was promoted and spent over two years as Sales Manager with 50 sales people covering the County of San Diego. Kathy returned to the field with a move to Commonwealth Land Title Company. When Fidelity Nat'l came into San Diego County, they hired her for North County S.D. and for nine years Kathy was a consistent #1 top producer in her North County territory and made the highest level of Gold in the President's Club. Kathy formed the first title partnership in San Diego County when inviting Mark Boeh to join forces with her. They had a very successful team until 1995 when the market contracted and the team was dissolved by management.
    Kathy enjoyed helping all her realtors, mortgage, escrow and builder-developer clients and her partner over the years. Coming to San Deigo and starting over, it was a perfect career that led to friends, success and financial well being. She is very grateful for the many friends and business education she received. She had many great teachers.
