Lynn Ryder’s Summary

I have 25 years of experience in Leadership Development and Change Management reporting to VPs at Fireman's Fund (owned by Allianz of America), AAA, Kaiser Permanente, General Electric, Pacific Gas and Electric, and Salesforce.com. I have maintained good contacts in each of these companies.

In my Organizational Change Management work I have applied my talents as a Six Sigma Black Belt and as a quality expert and examiner for the State of California . At Kaiser Permanente I reduced IT adoption time on the HealthConnect online medical records project. At AAA I used my expertise in communication and change management to ensure smooth integrations following acquisition of Western United and an alliance with M&I, a national bank. At salesforce.com and Fireman’s Fund, I collaborated with leadership to increase customer and employee satisfaction. At PG&E, I facilitated the agreement on strategic direction for several business units, and I ensured a smooth roll-out of the Field Automation Project. In my own consulting business, Ryder Communications, I helped over 50 companies improve business results.

My experience includes: Wharton MBA Program instructor; Stanford University graduate; Masters from Boson University ; Graduate of Leadership San Francisco; Author; and Certified Sr. HR Professional and MBTI consultant. I have also served on the leadership teams of the Bay Area Organization Development Network and the American Society for Training and Development.
