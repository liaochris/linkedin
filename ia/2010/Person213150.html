David Weinstein’s Summary

I have worked for many of the premier advertising agencies, direct marketing and design firms in New York (ABC News, The MacNiel Lehrer News Hour, PBS, Saatchi & Saatchi, Wunderman, McCaffrey McCall. There I learned visual problem solving, and to target and develop marketing strategies.

I am the Director of Marketing for Sunmark Federal Credit Union, an Albany NY based, full-service financial institution. My role is to build brand and execute multi tiered marketing strategies that empower our customers' financial growth, while increasing membership.

I owned and managed Vox Design as Creative Director and CEO, where I developed strategic and web based and multi-tiered marketing solutions and communications. We have serviced a broad range of international clients since 1994. My beautiful wife Lisa still runs the company.

I have joined forces with a network of technical visionaries in expanding this level of service to include technology and data driven applications that provide end users with exactly the information they need and use. I believe this precise delivery of information will revolutionize marketing strategies, and outgrow even conventional computers into a more ubiquitous environment.
