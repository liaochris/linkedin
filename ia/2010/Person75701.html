Giovanni Isaksen’s Summary

We have partnered with a team that specializes in restructuring commercial loans on behalf of property owners. The team members are experienced bankers, lenders, FDIC auditors, CMBS originators and attorneys who can speak to your bank in language bankers understand. They are successful in renegotiating both small and large loans and their fees are based on results achieved. If you have been unsuccessful in refinancing your commercial property loan or know of someone who needs help with their loan please contact me at giovanni@ashworthpartners.com.

We acquire 100+ unit multifamily and self-storage properties that are undervalued compared to their competitors in the sub-market and can achieve market valuations with improved management, professional marketing and/or skilled renovation. If you own a property fitting these criteria that you would like to sell quietly in an off-market transaction please contact me at giovanni@ashworthpartners.com.

An outgrowth of our work is the development of an analysis tool to quickly screen income properties to find those that can be acquired with positive cash flow and have additional upside. Version 3.4 is in pre-release right now and we are sending out copies to our key influencer group for feedback and testimonials. If you would like evaluate the latest release please send an email with "Dealizer Group" in the title.
