David Ryan’s Summary

I have a real passion for the professional services sector, in particular, how these firms go to market, capture and nurture client relationships, and secure/sustain revenue. Unfortunately, most firms in this sector don't do this part of their business very well! They’re technically competent, but lack the discipline and awareness of how to effectively execute the business/client development effort.

To address that issue, and feed my own need to make a difference with this constituency, I started Gray Matters Group. We work only with Professional Services firms, helping them "tune their revenue engines", if you will. Through skill building and consulting services, we enhance these firms’ abilities to expand and nurture relationships, develop new clients, and drive firm revenue.

Our anchor offering is called "Relationship Xpansion (Rx) for Professionals"; a proprietary set of workshops and after-care programs developed exclusively for consultants/technicians/professionals. The premise of “Rx” is to help professionals expand their relationship “comfort zone”, and become more professional in engaging current clients, networking with non-clients, and able to recognize new opportunities for incremental revenue.

The goal is not to create sales people or "rain makers" per se or have these people pursue and close business. Instead, the program helps activate client facing consulting professionals to assist in relationship building and new opportunity identification. Rx provides a straightforward, immediately deployable process for consultants to follow in the field…supported by tools, templates, and ongoing behavior maintenance initiatives.

Gray Matters has successfully delivered all or portions of this content to thousands of consultants/professionals in the Big 4, as well as other professional services firms of varying size and disciplines including Tax, Audit, IT, HR, Operations, Financial, and Advisory Services.
