Patty Kimball’s Summary

Did you know that the average small business owner spends 40% of their time on administrative tasks? (Reference: U.S. Small Business Association). Wouldn't you rather spend your time building your business?

Benefits of Hiring a Virtual Assistant

1. Time/Increased Revenue - Hiring a Virtual Assistant frees up your time to work on growing your business and increase revenue.

2. Focus/Productivity - No distractions to keep you from focusing on the more important tasks associated with your business.

3. Expertise - Virtual Assistants typically start as secretaries and have real world office experience. Their work experience benefits a small business owner by offering a wide range of specialized knowledge in areas such as tradeshows, graphic design, marketing, and sales to bring to your business.

4. Save Money - Many Virtual Assistants develop relationships with key vendors and can help you save money by connecting you with reputable printing companies, graphic designers, writing services, promotional items, etc.

With these benefits, what are you waiting for?
