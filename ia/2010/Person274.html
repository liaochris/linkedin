Gary Shields’s Summary

I have more than 11 years of experience in the deployment of Communications OSP infrastructure. Although it has been a roller coaster ride of sorts through the Telecom Boom and Bust era of 1999-2001, it has truly been a blessing to do what I do and to know the people that I know. I started in this industry as a fiber optic technician and now I am a system owner and a Turn-key Contractor of all of the services that are required to build and maintain an operating network.
My goals are to continue the expansion of our service area and to deploy FTTH infrastructure throughout the current system footprint and the Northern Front Range of Colorado.
Location, Location, Location is the key and we are in one of the most unique situations available to a small system today. We are centrally located between Metropolitan Centers along the Interstate 25 corridor and are ready to grow our system.

Gary Shields’s Specialties:

My area of expertise is construction and maintenance of the OSP infrastructure. I have a knack at being able to engineer, build and troubleshoot OSP construction. I literally built my system from the ground up so I am well versed at all aspects of constructing, operating and owning a system but I enjoy the construction aspects of our business the most.
