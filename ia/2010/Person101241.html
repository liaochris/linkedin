Joe Vales’s Summary

Joe Vales works with the top managements of Global 1000 companies in building great businesses that seek to dominate their markets or market segments. Joe is best known in the business community for his strategic thinking about marketing and sales, and for developing and launching many innovative products/services that have become market leaders generating billions of dollars in revenues.

During his 30-year career, Joe has held senior management and consulting positions in strategic planning, new business development, marketing, and sales with PricewaterhouseCoopers, Price Waterhouse, Citibank, E.F. Hutton, Booz Allen Hamilton, and other industry leaders. He brings clients special expertise in business strategy, marketing, professional services, outsourcing, financial institutions, and consumer products.

Joe’s extraordinary knowledge of the outsourcing business is much in demand by private equity firms that make major investments in outsourcing companies. He is a business advisor on outsourcing investments made by Accel/Kravis Kohlberg Roberts (KKR), Baird Capital Partners, General Atlantic Partners, FT Ventures and Warburg Pincus. Joe knows the challenges of financing start-up companies and new ventures, and the financial resources needed to fund and nurture new opportunities while also meeting the investors’ return on investment goals.

Joe’s larger retainer/project clients include: eight of the Top 10 IT services and BPO outsourcing firms; two of the top five global software providers; seven private equity firms with major outsourcing investments; three leading offshore BPO service providers in India; two international law firms with large outsourcing practices; and several mid-market providers that are outsourcing leaders in their industries.

Joe Vales’s Specialties:
