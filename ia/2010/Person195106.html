Steve Huber’s Summary

Steve Huber is Tractor’s principal and founder. As Tractor’s leader, Steve is responsible for the company’s daily operations and for keeping Tractor focused on its short-term and long-term goals. Working for more than 15 years as a lead producer for various companies working in every type of media has helped Steve to develop the broad range knowledge and experience that is necessary to run a full service interactive agency.
