Megan Kline’s Summary

Megan Kline & Associates: Small Business Success by Design

Megan has combined her love of event planning and her love of marketing and helping people into a new venture: Megan Kline & Associates: Identity. Ideas. Action.

Success for small business owners does not happen by accident. Success is planned, success is designed.... Megan Kline & Associates focuses on helping the small business owner reach both their personal and professional goals through coaching and consulting. We specialize in helping those in the Special Events Industry achieve success given special marketing circumstances that surround that industry. She also helps other business owners in the Engineering, Architecture & Automotive industries market their specialty services as well.

After working in the event planning field since January of 2003, Megan realized that many of her business partners and associates were very successful, yet not reaching all their goals. By offering a coaching and consulting programs, she helps small business owners move from working "in the business" to working "on the busines".

Learn how to grow and expand your business and increase your revenues. Accountablility coaching is a main factor in the success of most small business owners.
