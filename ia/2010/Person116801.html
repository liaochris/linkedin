Kim Blake’s Summary

Kim has been a solid leader in the Telecommunications industry since 1998 with cross-fuctional leadership abilities. She provided leadership support during successful mergers of Global Crossing/Frontier Cellular, Bell Atlantic Mobile and Verizon Wireless. She is a creative and innovative leader with a focus on the outcome to create a win-win situation.

Kim was the Verizon Wireless Recipient of the Customer Service 2005 & 2007 Leadership Quarterly Awards.

In addition, Kim is the Promotions Manager for ACME SAFE & LOCK and she has worked with ACCESS LOCK & SAFE in promotions and advertising, both Rochester, NY, locksmith companies. She builds and maintains the websites for ACME SAFE & LOCK and promotes the business with collateral, social networking, direct communications, mailers, flyers and internet promotions.
