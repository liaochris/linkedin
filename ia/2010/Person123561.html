Lee Watters’s Summary

With over 28 years in the music recording and sound for film & TV business, I now produce inbound marketing and new media content for the web. Over the past 12 years my experience and education with the Internet has brought me to fall in love with social media and Inbound Marketing.

My passion is working with small local businesses, teaching and managing their online and inbound marketing. My four phase system has proven to lower marketing cost and drive more targeted customers to my clients businesses. My company's specialty is content development and audio/video marketing tools for companies.

Lee Watters’s Specialties:

Inbound Marketing, Podcast and video webcast production, planning, content development, SEO, social media and new media production.
