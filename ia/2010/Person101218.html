Joe Talik’s Summary

Joe is a self-motivated and well rounded leader who has a fast-track of success and a wealth of expertise in both business and technology disciplines in multiple industry verticals such as Financial Services, Insurance, Pharmaceutical and Publishing. Leverages thought leadership and expertise to resolve complex business problems, deliver critical initiatives in business transformation, creates successful methodologies and governance models, and provides solid leadership. Driven to ensure companies operate with the most optimal business and technology models based on industry best practices saving millions of dollars for companies.

Joe serves on the Board of Directors, as the Vice President - Program Chair, of the Garden State Chapter of the Association of Information and Image Management (AIIM).

Joe is also an active member of the International Association of Outsourcing Professionals (IAOP), The Outsourcing Institute and Society for Information Management (SIM).

Joe has received his MBA in Finance and Accounting with Honors from Regis University. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from the University of Detroit - Mercy and Masters Certificates in Organization Leadership, Six Sigma, Applied Project Management and Information Systems and Technology Project Management from Villanova University.

Joe holds certifications as an ITIL V3 Foundation Professional, Outsourcing Professional (COP), Project Management Professional (PMP) and Six Sigma Black Belt (CSSBB).

Outside of work at SunGard Consulting Services, Joe is currently working on his Masters of Engineering in Engineering Management from Stevens Institute of Technology. During his studies, Joe will also receive Graduate Certificates in Systems Engineering & Architecting, Systems Engineering Management and Systems & Supportability Engineering.
