Carla (Lee) Griffin’s Summary

I spent 10 years as a Financial executive with broad experience in all aspects of accounting, auditing, and financial management with Philco Ford Microelectronics, Fairchild Semiconductors and Advanced Micro Devices (AMD).

Following the semiconductor career, I obtained a real estate license and worked directly with real estate (lending, new home sales, business opportunities, and mergers/acquisitions) and financial consulting with an emphasis on early-stage startups.

As a designated Seniors Real Estate Specialist (SRES), my advanced training helps me to help the client make wise decisions about owning real estate, obtaining a reverse mortgage, moving to a retirement community and other important financial and social issues that may arise.

My client's say that "I am very honest, friendly, extremely knowledgeable about the community, and listen to their "fears". I am the EXPERT in The Villages Golf & Country Club who understands and cares about all the concerns of our retirement-aged clients.

My goal is to educate and serve the needs to the growing population of "Baby Boomers".
