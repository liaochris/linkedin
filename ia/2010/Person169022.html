Randy Siegel’s Summary

As a communications and leadership trainer and coach, Randy Siegel helps professionals bring power, passion,and purpose to their lives and careers.

Organizations hire Randy to transform high-potential employees into a new generation of leaders. Randy gives them the leadership and communications skills they need to rise through the organization. CEOs hire Randy to show them how to become more charismatic leaders, spokespeople, and ambassadors for the organizations they serve. Finally, individuals retain Randy when they find themselves at a crossroad in their careers or lives. Randy helps them regain their internal navigation systems.

Randy has published two books: PowerHouse Presenting: Become the Communicator You Were Born to Be and Engineer Your Career: Building a Blueprint for Your Best Self. Both are available on Amazon.com.

He has been quoted in USA Today, the Washington Post, the Atlanta Constitution and Journal, Southwest Airline’s in-flight magazine Spirit, and many other media outlets.

His clients have included such industry leaders as Coca-Cola Enterprises, Earthlink, The Recording Academy (The Grammy Awards), the Dallas Museum of Art, Viking Range Company, the University of Georgia MBA Program, State Farm Insurance, The Honor Society of Phi Kappa Phi, and Fleishman-Hillard International Communications.

One client summed up Randy's work this way: "You teach far more than communication; your message is about life issues, growth, awareness, and openness. It's powerful stuff, and your ability to communicate it and share your life is inspiring."
