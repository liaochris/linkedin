Ross Peterson’s Summary

Website developer with substantial design, production and integration experience in both large and small-scale projects. I provide custom graphic design alongside clean & valid XHTML/CSS and PHP/Javascript integration. I specialize in finding solutions for my clients that match up with their needs and budgets.

Ross Peterson’s Specialties:
