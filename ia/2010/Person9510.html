Amie Crohn’s Summary

Amie Crohn is a seasoned Recruiter who enjoys finding great talent for Land O'Lakes, Inc. Amie enjoys working with a variety of hiring managers in Winfield Solutions and has filled over 500 position thus far at Land O'Lakes across Feed, Dairy Foods, Corporate and Winfield Solutions, both exempt & non-exempt positions.

Amie Crohn has done guest speaking engagements at UW-Madison, School of Business, SHRM, Concordia University, St. Paul, MN and Univerity of St. Thomas, SHRM.

Amie primariloy recruits for Winfield Solutions a division of Land O'Lakes. Winfield Solutions has strong brands in Seed (Croplan Genetics), Crop Protection Products (Agrisolutions), Micronutrients (MaxIn) and Adjavents (InterLock). Recruitment for Seed, Chemicals, Agronomy, Crop Protection, Agriculture, Marketing, Sales, Location Managers, Answer Plot crew, Corporate Office employees, Managers, Directors, Professionals and Office/Clerical. Have also recruited for CPG Marketing in Dairy Foods, Feed Marketing & Sales and a variety of other corporate and field based positions with the Land O'Lakes!

Visit our website at www.landolakes.jobs to view our current job posting!
