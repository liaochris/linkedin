Parek Maduot’s Summary

BStar Sudan is a startup company working to provide professional services in the construction and business services sector. The initial phase of the company's growth envisions direct management service work in the local US Mid-Atlantic region.

An ambitious second phase of the business plan is to get into the emerging markets of east and west Africa, and be an effective player in providing tools in the IT, construction and engineering sectors in that region, An initial assessment has been completed in the Sudan, Kenya and uganda, and the hope is that the 1st quarter of 2007 will feature a rollout of services to government and commercial clients in these three countries.
