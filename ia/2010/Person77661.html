Gregg Peterson’s Summary

Award-winning instructional designer/developer, video and multimedia expert with over 13 years experience producing high-end self-paced interactive online and classroom instruction, television-quality video, motion-graphics/animation, easy-to-understand information/documentation, and professional graphic designer-level products at KLA-Tencor Corp. Passionate about high quality instructional design and a firm believer that corporate training does not need to be, nor should be boring.

Awards include Brandon Hall awards for both “Custom Learning” and “Electronic Performance Support Systems” (EPSS) training products, two KLA-Tencor General Manager’s Awards for personal elearning work excellence and a team award for an outstanding video training product, difficult-to-achieve 5-out-of-5 yearly performance review ratings 10 out of 15 years at KLA-Tencor, 5+ team awards as a key member of the KLA-Tencor Corporate Training Department that has won numerous Training Magazine awards for being one of the top training departments in the world.
