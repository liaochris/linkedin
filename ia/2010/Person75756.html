John Girvin’s Summary

After 25 years of service with the Rochester Police Department, John retired at the rank of Commander. He now owns J. Martin Solutions, Inc., a general management consulting company with a focus on the public sector and public safety. Services include: public safety labor relations, performance accountability, strategic planning, policy and procedure development, project management, supervisory development, law enforcement agency administrative assessment and consolidation, and law enforcement accreditation. J. Martin Solutions, Inc. also owns The Leadership Development Group, providing leadership training in partnership with active and retired military personnel to public and private sector clients.

He is now serving his first term as a Town Justice in the Town of Clarendon, NY.

    J. Martin Solutions, Inc. is a general management consulting company, with a focus on the public sector and public safety. Company President John M. Girvin is a retired Commander from the Rochester, New York, Police Department, with twenty-five years of law enforcement experience. The last ten years of his career were spent in executive positions in the second largest city police department in upstate New York.

    John also served as a partner in the State University of New York at Brockport’s Institute for Leadership Development, assisting in curriculum development and delivery of training. He is a founding partner of the Leadership Development Group, a subsidiary of J. Martin Solutions, Inc., and a joint venture with active-duty and retired military personnel, providing leadership training based on the military model.

    John has extensive experience in performance accountability systems, and has been involved in the design and implementation of such systems on a municipality-wide basis.
