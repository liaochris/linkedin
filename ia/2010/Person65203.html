    BoxSmart's aim is to promote health, well being & social inclusion through sport.

    We deliver non contact Amateur Boxing Association of England (ABAE) boxing awards to students and young people, primarily between 5 and 19 years old, in schools (PE classes and after school clubs), youth clubs and other organisations. We work in mainstream areas, challenging social demographics & with children with disabilities.
