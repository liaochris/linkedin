Eric Thiegs’s Summary

Eric is a 14-year veteran of the member benefits, partnership marketing and merchant funded discounts/rewards arena. He’s worked on building merchant partnerships for Fortune 500 clients ranging from Visa U.S.A. to Wyndham Resorts as well as the non-profit sector, e.g. expanding member benefits for 2.1 million US teachers/school staff that are part of the NEA state education association network.

His resume covers a six-year tenure as a senior account executive in the partnership marketing division at the world’s largest marketing agency, Carlson Marketing Group, out of Minneapolis, MN to stints at small boutique branding houses on the east coast, e.g. Barry Group in York, PA, to mid-sized partnership marketing firms, e.g. his tenure as the Director of Partnership Marketing at the Salt Lake City based Access Development Corporation. In this last role, he increased Access' national merchant partnerships, coupons and discounts from 22 brands to over 300 in fewer than three years.

In total, Eric has personally negotiated over 1,500 merchant funded partnerships, sponsorships, and discounts on behalf of his clients, including promotions with the NFL, Olympics, National Education Association members, CapitalOne and more.

Stage of Life LLC was born out of this decade+ of experience in building merchant relationships for clients. Eric found that there is a high need for a) merchants to target consumers specifically by their stage of life and b) financial, community and philanthropic organizations to get access to an affordable, custom member benefit platform and partnership services that could strengthen member recruitment, usage, and retention/loyalty.

Eric launched Stage of Life® as the CEO and Managing Partner. His co-founder, Joseph Thiegs, is a lawyer living in Minneapolis. Stage of Life LLC operates out of Pennsylvania, where Eric lives with his wife and two daughters.
