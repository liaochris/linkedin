    As the Search Engine Specialist (SES), I head up the Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM) strategies and techniques for all our clients. Since Swipht specializes in Technical SEO, my focus is onsite optimization to identify the bottlenecks that will prevent the Search Engines from ranking the site higher. If our clients are going to invest significant resources in marketing their site, they expect the site to be optimized to get the best results from their campaign.

    So what separates our search engine optimization strategies from other SEM firms? Marketing firms are great at understanding how users are searching for and using your content. What they probably don’t understand is how Search Engines use software (called "bots") to crawl your web pages, save what it finds in a database (indexing), and return what it indexes as Search Engine Results pages (SERP). We are talking technology, not marketing. That’s where a Technical SEO Audit comes in. In simple terms, you have to develop your site to be Search Engine friendly before you actually expect the search engines to be friendly back.
