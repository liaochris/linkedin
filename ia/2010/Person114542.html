    (Self-Employed; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2010 — Present (2 months)

    CIO by the Byte™ is a small business and provides CIO leadership, products, and services to companies whose business model or budget do not support a full-time CIO staff position. CIO by the Byte™ provides "just the CIO services that you need, when you need it," or "by the Byte." A Virtual CIO so to speak! CIO by the Byte™ will assemble the imperatives of your company's goals and strategies into a cohesive whole, maximizing the delivery of business value.
    A company can contract with CIO by the Byte™ for a long-term engagement with a fixed number of hours, the delivery of a specific service, or the development of a specific product. We work with the company to establish the best fit for their business goals.

    CIO by the Byte™ can deliver on critical initiatives that are often difficult for the company to prioritize, budget, schedule, and allocate resources. These include strategic, business continuity, and disaster recovery plans, establishment and socialization of performance measurements, development of business cases, and delivering value propositions to stakeholders.

    Our Goals

    To help our customers envision business possibilities as never before.
    To help our customers move from their "as is" state to their "to be" state.
    To position our customers to realize value from new sources.
    To build enduring, mutually rewarding relationships.
    To have fun doing it!
