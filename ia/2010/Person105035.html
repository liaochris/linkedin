  • Boardmember

    Unida Vida

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Unida Vida is synonymous for ‘living together’. We believe that we can make a difference in the world by directly connecting people from all across the globe with one common goal: alleviating poverty and improving the quality of life for the disadvantaged.

    No boundaries, no large scale organizations, no high overhead costs, just the internet as a low-cost platform for enabling direct and real-time contact between those in need of support and those who want to be a supporter.

    We work with established local field partners around the world, who are involved in micro-finance and social development projects in their own country. Our field partners are local operating micro-finance and non profit organizations. They are carefully selected and have a proven track record. The most important requirement is that their work methods are transparent and that we have insight in the progress of each supported project.

  • CBI-expert

    Centre for the Promotion of Imports from developing countries

    (Government Agency; International Trade and Development industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    CBI is the Centre for the Promotion of Imports from developing countries, an agency of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and part op the Development Co-operation effort of the Netherlands. Operating since 1971 within this policy framework.

    CBI’s main objective is to contribute to the economic independence of a selected number of countries in Africa, Asia, Central and South America, Mediterranean and the Pacific by promoting the exports of goods and services from those countries to Western Europe.
    In order to achieve this objective, CBI has developed a varied package of services and Export Coaching Programmes available to Business Support Organisations and manufacturers/ exporters in mentioned countries

    A CBI expert provides both local training in specific areas (in my case eBusiness & Website Promotion) as well as consultancy & coaching programmes.
