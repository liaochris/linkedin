Vivek Khurana, M.S.E.’s Summary

Biomedical Engineer turned Interactive Video Producer. Began career as a biomechanical researcher at Vector Scientific, visually presenting biomedical research on injury biomechanics studies at conferences around the world, preparing court exhibits for the Big-5 automotive companies and managing a team of medical illustrators and modelers. Created anatomy models, injury mechanisms exhibits, and occupant-kinematic simulations to reconstruct real-world accidents and communicate biomechanical concepts to a jury. The passion for three dimensional modeling and physics based analysis led to interactive media production. Currently apply medical education to production of medical animation and interactive video along the medical and healthcare vertical, with the objective of improving electronic Continuing Medical Education (eCME) and digital detailing practices. See portfolio at: http://vloungevideo.com/portfolio
