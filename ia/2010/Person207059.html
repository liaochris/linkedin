Tony Schreiber’s Summary

An Internet developer since 1993, Tony has managed both creative and technical departments and acquired skills in system administration, database administration, network security and web development. Primarily a Cold Fusion developer, Tony is proficient in applications relating to both community and social interaction as well as e-commerce, inventory and order management.

As a photographer, Tony specializes in wedding, dance and automotive photography. Sure it's an odd mix, but what do you expect from someone who couldn't make up his mind between art and engineering majors?
