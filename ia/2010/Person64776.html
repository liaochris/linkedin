Emma Moore’s Summary

I'm a creative communications professional specialising in web and online formats, multi-media and social media in civil society organisations. I have a background in publishing and marketing. I have strong management and leadership skills which I use both at work, and in a voluntary capacity as a trustee and running my own campaign Pinkstinks and organisation Cooltobe.me. I'm passionate about finding creative solutions to problems which are innovative, forward thinking and which meet the brief.

Emma Moore’s Specialties:

Marketing and communications, publishing, print, design and websites for civil society organisations. Campaigning, multi-media, social networking and web 2.0. Governance and leadership.
