Nathan Therrien’s Summary

At the company level, Business Insurance & Investment Services of MA works to implement comprehensive employee benefits programs.

Amongst the core functions are the design and implementation of company sponsored health care plans, company sponsored benefits such as dental, life and disability and qualified retirement plans. Business Insurance & Investment Services of MA works toward addressing other needs of owners and management as well, including business continuation planning, key-man planning, and executive benefits.

At the personal level, Business Insurance & Investment Services of MA works to address the specific individual and family needs of business owners and their employees including personal planning, and portfolio management.

Learn more at http://biisma.com

Nathan Therrien’s Specialties:
