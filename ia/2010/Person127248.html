Lara Pearson’s Summary

Lara Pearson is a Partner at Rimon Law Group where she heads the trademark practice.

Prior to joining Rimon, Lara was the Leader of the Law Office of Lara Pearson, Ltd. (“LOLP”), an environmentally and socially responsible intellectual property law firm located on the northeast shore of Lake Tahoe.

Lara's practice focuses on serving other LOHAS (Lifestyles of Health and Sustainability) businesses and individuals in their trademark and copyright matters, including: IP identification; clearance; registration; transfers; dispute resolution; and litigation and on serving those in the live music community.

Lara co-founded the Intellectual Property Law Section of the State Bar of Nevada in 2005 and served as its Chairperson in 2006. She writes and speaks frequently on issues relating to trademark and copyright law, as well as on issues of corporate social responsibility and sustainability.

Lara proudly serves on the Advisory Board of Rock the Earth and donates pro bono legal services annually to Rock the Earth, 1% for the Planet, Green America, Social Enterprise Alliance, the Rocky Mountain Sustainable Living Association, the Tahoe Rim Trail Association, HeadCount and the Children’s Cabinet.

Lara Pearson’s Specialties:

Trademark and copyright law: IP identification; clearance; registration; transfers; dispute resolution; and litigation.

Empowering and training clients to handle routine USPTO filings internally while I provide the legal support necessary to ensure the success of those filings.
