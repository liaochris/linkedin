Deborah Brauser’s Experience

  • Freelance Medical Writer

    Multiple Publications

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    In addition to my corporate video writing and producing work, I have taken on more and more medical writing.

    In fact, over the last year and a half, I have covered over 20 medical conventions (from Orlando to Hawaii) and written over 125 articles and/or videos on the latest research developments (especially from phase 3 studies) presented on site.

    Clients have included Medscape Medical News, Medscape Psychiatry and Mental Health, Doctor's Guide, The Oncology Nurse, The Oncology Pharmacist, the Journal of Multidisciplinary Cancer Care, and Dothen Healthcare Press.

  • Freelance Writer/Producer

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    I am finally back to my first love -- freelancing! I have over 15 years of copywriting, producing, and/or directing experience and have been freelancing off and on for over 10 years. Some of my recent clients have included: Orlando Attractions Magazine, the Doctor's Guide, Yum! Brands, Nemours, Fry Hammond Barr, Compass Knowledge Group, Orlando Regional Medical Center, Dynetech and MyMediaWorks TV, American Airlines, Boston University, Island One Resorts, Florida Hospital, and XOS Technologies.

    Please check out my website for numerous samples of past print, TV, radio, video, and web projects at deborahbrauser.com.

  • Writer/Producer

    Dynetech Corporation and MyMediaWorks TV

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2005 — January 2007 (2 years 1 month)

    After 5 years of freelance writing and producing (for clients such as Yum! Brands -- Pizza Hut, Taco Bell, KFC, A&W, and Long John Silver's -- American Airlines, Taco Bell International, Ryan International Airlines, Koch Industries, Mobil Travel Guides, and NetG - National Education Training Group) and moving to Orlando, I accepted a generous offer from Dynetech Corporation to work in their in-house production facility as a Writer/Producer. This department was eventually merged into MyMediaWorks TV, a full-service off-site production house.

    While at MMW-TV, I wrote, produced &/or directed half-hour infomercials, :30 & :60 TV spots, & business videos for clients such as Carleton Sheets, J.G. Banks, Robert Allen, Quizno’s, Optionetics, Disney's Animal Kingdom, 4X Made Easy, and the Learning Annex. In addition, I worked on several conference and convention productions in various capacities including stage managing, "highlights video" producing, and on-camera testimonial gathering.

  • Writer/Producer

    Cox Communications/Multimedia Cablevision

    (Public Company; Broadcast Media industry)

    1996 — 2000 (4 years )

    Wrote & produced TV promos, infomercials, and radio spots promoting the company.

    In addition, wrote & produced TV commercials and training, sales, and business recruitment videos through its in-house production department for clients such as the Mid-Continent One-Call Group, Eby Construction, Southwest Paper Corporation, and the American Red Cross.

  • Freelance Writer/Producer & On-site Consultant

    Pizza Hut Inc. (as well as other local Wichita companies)

    (Public Company; Restaurants industry)

    1995 — 1996 (1 year )

    Wrote and/or produced TV & radio spots, newsletter and magazine articles, news releases, brochures, print ads, marketing plans, and more for a variety of local Wichita clients including Stephan Advertising, the Wichita Police Department, Multimedia Security Service, Sirloin Stockade International, and StraightLine Manufacturing.

    Started a long-term relationship with Pizza Hut Inc. -- that continues today! This includes writing and/or producing training, orientation, new product roll-outs, how-tos, and convention videos; writing speeches from the President; and contributing to several training manuals.

  • Writer/Producer

    Associated Advertising Agency

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1989 — 1994 (5 years )

    Along with print, radio and video projects, wrote and produced film ads for clients such as Intrust Bank, St. Francis Hospital, Chance Industries, and The United Way.
