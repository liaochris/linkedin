Chris Harrison’s Summary

I am the Founder and President of The Pendo Group, LLC. I have extensive experience in values-based organizational development, lean manufacturing systems, and supplier partnership initiatives. I have overseen the global operations of one of the world's leading electronics sustainability companies, which included direct responsibility for the North American facilities as well as operations in Latin America and Asia. I was also responsible for operations of three metal and plastic Tier 1 Automotive trim facilities (metal forming and stamping, injection moulding/extrusion, and vacuum forming). My common-sense approach to business operations will help your organization identify and achieve strategic objective accomplishment, improved work flow, and waste elimination.

I love Southeastern Michigan and am proud to be a member of several civic organizations including the Detroit Regional Chamber, Economic Club of Detroit, Rotary Club of Grosse Pointe, Metro East Chamber of Commerce, and am a Past Commodore of the Grosse Pointe Farms Boat Club. I was a member of Leadership Newton County and have completed the Disney Institute's People Management series. I serve on the Michigan Quality Council's Board of Examiners. I am also proud to serve as a Subject Matter Expert for Wayne State University's Executive and Professional Development Department.

My family and I spend as much time as possible on Lake St. Clair and the remainder of our time wishing that we were.
