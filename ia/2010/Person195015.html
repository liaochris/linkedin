Steve Gibson’s Summary

Let my firm put over 30 years of Restaurant Industry Recruiting experience to work for you! Discover for yourself why so many Restaurant Industry Professionals turn to RMR for help when navigating career moves. Here’s what we do best:

Introductions

We’ll introduce you to the best companies in your area; companies that are looking for leaders like you — right now! In many cases the best career opportunities are never advertised publicly, instead they’re filled from within, or companies ask for our help.

We’ll also introduce you to new perspectives. Few people, even seasoned restaurant professionals, are privy to all the inside details and the richness of growth opportunities that exist within many restaurant companies. We’ll share our inside knowledge with you as we work together to find the best, career-advancing fit for you.

Confidentiality

We’ll partner with you to conduct a completely confidential job search. Our reputation and the future of our business depends not only on our ability to introduce you to the companies you’d like to meet, but also to ensure the complete confidentiality of your search. We take this responsibility seriously. Our interest is aligned with yours - if you win, we win.

And most importantly, we’ll partner with you to get the career you want. We have spent years building personal relationships with the companies we’ll introduce you to. So we know the players and we understand how and why hiring decisions are made. This experience gives us invaluable insight into what qualities they’re looking for in new leaders, and the pitfalls that may derail you. As your “friend on the inside” we’ll make sure you have the knowledge you need to succeed.

Advancement

If you’re truly ready to advance, we’ll get you moving toward that exciting new opportunity! Let us help you to get underway.

Let’s get started… Contact me today for an eye-popping view of the landscape of opportunities that are available to you right now.
