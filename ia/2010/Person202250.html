Janet Powell’s Summary

Through many years of interacting with parents and children, including eight years working in kindergartens and child-care centres, I've gained an understanding of how families work and what makes them work better. Add to that over thirty years of parenting experience, studies in childhood development, accreditation as a parent trainer, and a passion for wonderful families, and you have a picture of me as The Parenting Coach. I get great joy out of helping parents become less stressed and more effective in their important roles as nurturers of the next generation of adults.

Janet Powell’s Specialties:

Parent Effectiveness Training run as a group course, the unique, personalised program Parenting Your Way, private coaching and mentoring of parents, parenting articles and seminars, speaking engagements.
