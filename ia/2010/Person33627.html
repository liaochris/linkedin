Kim Casey, PMP’s Summary

Decisive, action-oriented, and results-focused professional with a demonstrated ability to successfully lead business or technical initiatives in program and project management, process improvement, and business analysis. Accustom to larger and more complex initiatives in the implementation of Business Strategies to Systems and IT Infrastructures. Ensure strategic alignment of resources with business priorities and comprehensive assessment and management of project scope, risks, and requirements from initiation to post implementation.
