Jim Kimo Storke’s Summary

Kimo has 20 years of business experience and 6 years of U.S. military experience.

Kimo is focused on Business Development in the Clean Tech and Hi Tech industry.

He is currently working with one of the largest Solar Integrators in the United States on project financing and business development.

He is representing one of the nations leading Renewable Energy funds with over $21B under management and a $2B Renewable Energy Fund.

Responsible for bringing in $20m of revenue for Energy Company in 2006

Established multiple funding relationships for clients of which are ongoing and active, has access to funding for green energy in the form of debt, equity and leasing.

Facilitator and co- leader of the Texas Greenbelt Coalition – group made up of rural utilities, local government, private sector and public sector executives whose purpose is to bring green energy companies and solutions to Texas.

He is on the advisory board for the Clean Technology Sustainable Industries Organization CT-SI.org www.ct-si.org

Kimo was a Global Offering Manager at IBM for 3 years working as a Product Manager of managers and as a team member was responsible for $450 million dollars in revenue for IBM in 2003.

Kimo was a Regional Manager of Business Development at RSM McGladrey for 4 years, The seventh largest accounting/consulting firm in the nation.
