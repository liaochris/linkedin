I'm Matt Murray. I guess I should finally update my LI profile being that I've made most of my money as a headhunter. Yet it goes beyond that. I'm fascinated about how inter connected people really are, at the same time, I want to engage others who are equally as passionate about their professional/personal lives to broadcast it's details.

Business is personal- Keith Ferrazzi

Just a few days ago, one of my partners in my national pharmacy initiative were discussing youth politics. A few comments later and she shared with me her immediate connection with two very successful movie producers. I perked up. As I have a screen play! She graciously agreed to read 5 pages and if she liked it, pass it on to the two brother directors.

All of the companies in which I've been involved are simply the same blue print of a franchise I bought years ago. A staffing company. All we do is connect the dots. Of course the trick is to determine what dots to connect.

Here are some of the companies and organizations in which I'm involved and why I'm passionate about them.
