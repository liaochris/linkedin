Hal Spice’s Summary

Hal is a senior executive with 30+ years experience consulting for Fortune 100 companies and governments. He has special expertise in strategy formulation, private equity financing, and asset backed lending and securitization. Hal has substantial international business experience performing consulting work in Europe, and he has 12 years Asia experience (Japan, China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Philippines) where has worked on private equity transactions, creation of joint ventures, strategic alliances, and distribution agreements,

At UBS Wealth Mgt. he led a team that provided advisory services to highly affluent clients. He also developed an Asset Backed Lending method that provided low cost capital for PE investment. Prior to UBS, Hal was a GP at Delta Venture Partners, a PE firm that secured financing from Asian investors for early stage technology (IT & Bio) companies. He raised $65+ million in early-stage financings

Hal possesses expertise in strategically applying IT to the financial sector. He has designed, developed, and re-engineered complex financial systems. At American Savings, he designed one of the first mortgage-backed securities pricing systems, and developed a sophisticated system that managed a $6 billion asset portfolio. He also designed an asset portfolio system for the Resolution Trust Corporation. Hal was the principal designer of asset portfolio systems for Federal Home Loan Bank - San Francisco, Citicorp and the Bank of California. He reengineered and developed VISA's Global Arbitration and Compliance System.

He has trained clients on the strategic uses of IT, and has led specialized seminars for clients including World Bank, US Depr. of Justice, and Silicon Valley Bank, where he developed the acclaimed “Entrepreneur 101” seminar series. Hal has authored and published a number of articles on a variety of investment and information technology subjects.

He is a graduate of the UC Davis, and holds NASD Series 7 /66 securities licenses.

Hal Spice’s Specialties:

Consulting with early stage companies seeking capital, Opportunity Assessment, Business Strategy Creation and Execution, International Business Development, Due Diligence, Capitalization Strategy, Venture Formation
