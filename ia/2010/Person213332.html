Valerie VanBooven RN, BSN’s Summary

http://www.MySeniorService.com
http://www.TheLTCExpert.com

Valerie’s motto and favorite saying is: “Impact is not created by big budgets, impact is created by innovative marketing ideas!”

Valerie VanBooven is President and CEO of LTC Expert Publications LLC., a company devoted to empowering elder care entrepreneurs and senior service providers with the tools to enjoy thriving businesses and freedom-based lifestyles.

Valerie has been a regular guest on NBC’s Today Show for their “Forever Young: A Guide to Life After 50″ segments. Valerie has also appeared on CNNFN’s Your Money with Ali Velshi, and has been interviewed in numerous publications including Time Magazine, and various AP articles.

In 2007, Valerie published her second book, “The Senior Solution: A Family Guide to Keeping Seniors Home for Life!”

She has become the foremost authority in driving sales via the internet, seminars, and e-mail for senior service providers and elder care entrepreneurs. From there Valerie began sharing her complete online marketing and information marketing formulas via products, coaching programs, her own seminars, webinars, and conference calls.

Elder care entrepreneurs and senior service providers from all corners of the market come to Valerie for guidance including home care providers, elder law attorneys, financial advisors, senior living specialists, senior movers, long-term care insurance specialists, assisted living providers, durable medical equipment providers, geriatric care managers, authors, reverse mortgage specialists and more.

Valerie not only serves her own clients, but also serves the Reverse Mortgage community by being the National Marketing Director for Next Generation Financial Services located in Baltimore, MD, and writing articles monthly for The Reverse Review.

Valerie VanBooven RN, BSN’s Specialties:

Marketing and advertising to the "mature" market. Marketing to adult children of aging parents. Education and information resource for long-term care planning, crisis management, and reverse mortgages.

Valerie VanBooven is a registered nurse, professional geriatric care manager, author, professional speaker, and radio show host. She is one of the nation’s leading experts on long-term care planning, reverse mortages, and crisis management. She has her insurance health and life licenses.
