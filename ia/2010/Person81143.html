Heidi Mankoff, MBA’s Summary

I'm a small business growth consultant with 10+ years of experience working for organizations in the non-profit, education, healthcare, theme park, entertainment, technology, sustainability, real estate and financial services industries.

Having managed several entrepreneurial/small business ventures myself, I have a deep-rooted understanding of the opportunities and challenges that small business owners face each day. In many cases, these owners juggle operations, human resources, sales and marketing, finance and accounting, information technology, legal (among other responsibilities!) with extremely limited budgets and a limited amount of human capital. After awhile, many of the daily activities account for "keeping the business afloat", and owners have little time/energy left for development and/or expansion activities.

I combine my passion for developing business leaders with a unique three-pronged approach to strategic business growth. Ultimately, I help organizations grow revenue, expand their customer-base, and increase cash flow.

My strategic direction will help you grow your business—by working smarter, not harder.
