  • SVP/GM Univision Interactive Media, Inc

    (Privately Held; Broadcast Media industry)

    June 1999 — Present (11 years 8 months)

    Part of the small core development group of the site that has become the largest Spanish-language portal in the United States.
    Set up and supervise Univision Online's bureaus. Assisted in/or supervised many facets of the site: information architecture, content development, editorial structure and workflows. Managed some of first Online/TV integration efforts. Before heading the programming dept. of Univision Online, Lopez had a 20-year journalism career; serving as Univision’s Mexico City Bureau chief; roving West Coast correspondent for CNN Español; bureau chief for the Telemundo newscast Mexico City; The Arizona Republic's Mexico correspondent; worked for ABC News and United Press International.
    Knight Fellow at Stanford in 1998-99, concentrating on business and media studies also a former fellow of the American Studies Program at the World Press Institute in Macallester College.
