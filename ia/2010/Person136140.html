Mary Joyce (Masko)’s Summary

Mary Joyce is the President of Nicayla Enterprises, Inc. which holds three subsidiaries, The Michigan Business Directory ( www.MichiganBusiness.us ) which is a free online community and directory for Michigan Businesses, MichiganPR.net, specializing in providing free press release exposure for Michigan Businesses and Nicayla - a Michigan Web development company.

Furthermore, Ms. Joyce provides contract SEO and database work for corporations throughout Michigan.

Previously, Ms. Joyce founded a successful internet retailing enterprise, in which she remains as a silent partner.

She has spent the last 10 years at the forefront of search engine marketing, social networking innovation and methods to integrate technology and marketing on the Internet. Her company services over 150 clients nationally, while the subsidiaries help over 25,000 companies in Michigan.

Primary interests include organic search engine optimization of complex database driven websites using a combination of web 1.0, 2.0 and 3.0 techniques.
