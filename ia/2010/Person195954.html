Stever Robbins’s Summary

I help people be happy and bring more fun into the world. I happen to have a lot of business experience and good credentials, so I end up working a lot with executives. We do the standard career development, leadership skills, interpersonal intelligence, and all that yada yada yada stuff. What I really love is helping people find and pursue meaning and passion in their lives.

The creds:

Co-founder or early team member of nine startups (3 IPOs) over 25 years. Background in cognitive psychology, systems theory, business, and educational design (was part of Harvard's MBA program redesign).

I'm looking for media opportunities to turn 16 years of collected content into a TV and/or radio show. In the meantime, I'm coaching, facilitating, and speaking for successful people who want to play a bigger game in their business or life.

I measure Return on Investment in the ultimate currencies: Happiness, meaning, and impact, not dollars. Be prepared.
