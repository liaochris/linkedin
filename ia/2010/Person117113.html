Kim Sherrell’s Summary

About

Kim Sherrell is a director, tech personality, and integrated digital media specialist. A huge advocate for the arts with a background in education and multimedia design, Kim is the founder of http://artwalk.tv.

Kim Sherrell’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include design, visual arts, integrated marketing, broadcast communications, digital filmmaking, project management, content curation, collaboration, community building.

Fluent with tools such as XHTML/CSS, Apple's Final Cut Studio, Quicktime Pro, Final Draft, Breakdown, Soundtrack Pro, Flash, Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Wordpress, Plancast, Facebook, Friendfeed, YouTube, Twitter, Buzz, Blackboard/WebCT, Moodle, Sakai.

    Artwalk.TV

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    ArtwalkTV is a global initiative that brings artists and art patrons together. The initiative consists of two components:

    [1] A community-supported virtual gallery that removes barriers to access for artists and their public.

    [2] A monthly real-time virtual art crawl that employs the tools and systems of social media to answer the question: "Where is the best art now?" Visit http://artwalk.tv for more information.
