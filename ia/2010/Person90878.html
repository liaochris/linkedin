Jason Diehl’s Summary

In January 2010 I joined Buckman Enochs Coss & Associates as an Associate Recruiter. Looking for a change in my career path I had the pleasure of finding a great position with this dynamic company. Buckman Enochs Coss & Associates is the largest health care recruiting company and has over 30 years of great history in this market. I recruit individuals within the medical device, health care IT and pharma sales markets. 2010 is looking to be a great year for growth in this industry and I am excited to be a part of this great team.

Before Buckman Enochs Coss & Associates:

Graduated from Ohio State in 2002 with a degree in Hospitality Management. I entered the culinary side of this industry directly after school and worked there until 2005. At this time I was ready for a change.

In 2005 I entered into hotel sales. I had the chance to work at a small property to learn a the basics and get to see this industry from many angles. I made my goals during this time and had the chance to go to the Key West sales incentive trip for Remington. In 2008 I made the change to the new Hilton Columbus/Polaris and joined the opening team. We opened the hotel on June 19th, 2008. I had the chance to work in a new market and with a totally different product. After a little over a year at the Hilton I had the great opportunity to open another Hilton Family product. In July 2009 I started at the Embassy Suites Columbus-Airport. We opened the hotel on September 1, 2009. I now have the great opportunity to start another hotel and get back to the corporate market.

I have become very interested in Social Media and using it to its maximum potential. The sky is the limit with Web 2.0. I hope to stay on top of all the new applications and exciting developments.

I have completed Steinhart sales training 1 & 2 and also Hilton CFS Training as well.

I have been the VP of Membership for the Ohio Chapter of MPI for two years.
