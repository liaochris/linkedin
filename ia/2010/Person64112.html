Elmer Kerr’s Summary

Elmer is currently developing THREE new on-line platform projects that will launch in 2010. The emphasis this year to concentrate in the retail on-line sector in Ireland and UK. Retail sales have slumped heavily during the recession, retailers have cut margins to rock-bottom in support of retaining turnover and cash flow but they can only sustain this for short period and little prospect of reducing rents and operating costs. The solution lies in combining the "bricks & mortar" business with an on-line solution, not stand alone but within suitable platforms to attract volume traffic (or footfall as they used to call it)
