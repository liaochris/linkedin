Ryan Rigoli’s Summary

I am a brand and personal development coach with extensive experience launching consumer brands and helping individuals and teams bring forth their unique contributions into the world with greater confidence, ease and joy.

I have had more than 15 years of brand marketing experience and have held leadership and management positions at Yahoo!, Homestead.com (now a subsidiary of Intuit), and IRI Inc.

Having left the corporate world, I now work with solo entrepreneurs and organizational leaders to build heart-centered businesses that inspire change in the people and communities they wish to serve.

Specifically, I help them to uncover, articulate and embody a personal brand that aligns their unique purpose with their life’s work. The result of my approach is a unique, personal brand that will attract and build meaningful connections with their audience and help them to stay true to their own inner compass.

I integrate both brand marketing principals as well as practices from many wisdom traditions to help my clients increase their awareness of and compassion for themselves and more fully engage in the world to support the communities they are passionate to serve.

You can learn more at http://rigolicoaching.com
