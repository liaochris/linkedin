Kate Bello’s Summary

My background consists mainly of design and sales. I graduated from FIDM in 2002 with a degree in Textile Design and from there spent 3 months in Turkey as a design intern at Kucuker.

I was at Chris Stone for two years as a colorist and textile designer. I learned all about color separation and printing which was great experience! After Chris Stone, I had a need to get out, meet people and be in control of how much I earn which is why I entered sales. I spent about a year at Konica Minolta which was such a learning experience. After gaining knowledge of sales, I spent a year at G&K Services where I ended the year #12 out of 250 reps nationwide. Super proud of that!

I moved down to beautiful San Diego in 2007 where I currently reside and love! As of now, I am working part time as a graphic designer and part time as a cocktail waitress at an awesome sushi restaurant, Tomiko. I also update my blog Sweet Fuzz daily which now has over 50,000 page views a month after only being up four months.

I am currently looking for a day job that I can use the skills I have learned over the years; working with people, designing, administrative duties, and selling.
