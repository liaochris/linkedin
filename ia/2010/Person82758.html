Lorraine Hopping Egan’s Summary

I love to work in a variety of media. I thrive on creative challenge.

GAMES: I've invented or nurtured a dozen successful board games from concept to published product, including my bestseller, Mars 2020. I designed and scripted engaging games for websites and am now expanding into digital formats.

BOOKS AND CONTENT: I've authored 50+ books for children and educators and hundreds of articles, essays, and other content for print and websites.

FICTION SCRIPTS: I wrote several software scripts (Escape from Antcatraz! The Ballonatics!) and radio scripts for Kinetic City Super Crew—a children's science show. I can inject a game or program with strong story lines, characters, and narrative voice.

Lorraine Hopping Egan’s Specialties:

Writing nonfiction and fiction (print and digital) and designing games.
