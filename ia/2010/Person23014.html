Betty Parker Ellis’s Summary

Betty Parker Ellis has spent over 30 years honing her communication skills and professional presence in the corporate and academic worlds. With a bachelor’s degree in French and Education from Furman University, she holds a master’s degree in Media Management from Kent State University in Ohio. Having spent the first part of her career with Michelin Tire Corporation, she is president and owner of Communication Works, LLC, a 12-year-old, Greenville, SC-based public relations and training company.

Over these years, Ellis has established a network of strategic partnerships with other service providers and specialty companies that allows her to create customized teams that produce turnkey results based on each project’s needs. With Ellis as project manager, she can assemble a team from a wide variety of expertise such as in graphic design, printing, web design, social media, photography, videography, language translations, awards production, program development/administration, writing, mystery shopping, and more. From beginning to end while supported by the right combination of team members, she manages the details and leaves clients with the results.

Betty Parker Ellis’s Specialties:

As a PR pro, Ellis has provided strategic planning, media relations, customer relations, community relations, and employee relations services to clients in industries such as automotive, healthcare, information technology, executive coaching, and textile finishes.

As a training pro, she offers customized training in business writing, business grammar and business etiquette. Clients include the Cliffs Communities, Palmetto Bank, City of Greenville, Charter, Lockheed Martin, and Fluor.
