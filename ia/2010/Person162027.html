Pedro Zabala’s Summary

I am an attorney concentrating in the area of Creditors' Rights. I have been practicing Creditors' Rights law since 2000. I began my legal career with Smith Debnam in Raleigh in 2000 and joined Sessoms & Rogers, P.A. in 2006. I am a member of the National Association of Retail Collection Attorneys (NARCA) and the Debt Buyers Association (DBA). I am admitted to practice in all the state courts of North Carolina. I am admitted to practice in the Western, Middle and Eastern District Courts of North Carolina. I have handled appeals with the North Carolina Court of Appeals and am a member of the North Carolina Bar and the North Carolina Bar Association.

Pedro Zabala’s Specialties:

Although the North Carolina Bar does not allow us to hold ourselves out as "specialists" without specialization certification, I have vast experience in civil litigation, alternative dispute resolution, Federal and State regulatory compliance and Creditor defense litigation. I am also fluent in Spanish and conversant in Portuguese.
