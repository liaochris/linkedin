Petra Sell’s Summary

design is so much more than just the pretty looks, it establishes, incorporates and enforces brand values. it creates an experience by alluding to our senses and emotions.
-----------
While studying Communication Design, I took a sabbatical year going to London and gaining work experience in the interactive design field, which was pretty new and hot at that time. I was lucky being offered work opportunities at agency.com and RedKiteNewMedia, a sister company of Saatchi&Saatchi. In 2000, I graduated as a Diplom Designer (FH) in the Fachhochschule Munich. This is the start of this website, showing my final work 'zappness - an experience of perception'.

Shortly after, I moved to the Netherlands, where I started working at Strawberryfrog, at that time an underdog in the international advertising world, challenging the market with Guerilla Marketing Strategies.

Love took me back to London where I found work in a startup company called 'white channel', but unfortunaley, the internet bubble burst got in the way and so I had to leave London and arrived in Belgium end of 2001. After designing about a year for OskarD, a small design firm in Antwerp, I was offered a job at i-merge in Leuven in 2003. And that is where I am still today, working as Design Director.

In the meantime, I do some freelance work for different clients including private persons, companies as well advertising agencies all around europe. In collaboration with viva architecture, I work on intermedia projects such as DU·BI·ETY and several architectural competitions.

Petra Sell’s Specialties:

design, interactive design as well traditional print design, Art Direction, corporate design, conceptual design thinking
