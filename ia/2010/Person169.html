Parisa Shokoohi’s Summary

Idea-Engineer. Problem Solver.

Past: An overachieving, multitasking, type A personality who is comfortable with more than one job title or role to say the least; the cliche of "wearing many different hats" would be a gross understatement.

Currently: Write, direct, produce, edit, and host a cooking, traveling, & lifestyle show created for young adults (18-40) that boasts critical thinking and intelligent living for those seeking happiness through healthiness.

Grounded in research and science, created at an elite academic institution but available to all universally online, this infotainment is designed for consumers by consumers with health and nutrition standards for excellence - consumer reporting with a conscience.

The show concept was born in 2006 for college students at Emory University. What was meant for campus television quickly appeared to be in demand globally online when a simple facebook fan club (for students by students), without any intentional marketing, gained 2/3s of its fan base internationally via good word of mouth rep online.

Emails from our early Facebook fan base have led to supplying this unique demand via webTV. With the help of approximately 25-30 crew members, consultants, and expert affiliates, presently working on: redesigning website, developing a webTV channel, knowledge work for convergence to smart phone apps (and other various mobile devices), while continuing to produce a new pilot webisode series for Eat This!

With a "student for life" philosophy, hope to continue learning through work and graduate study these interests: business/entrepreneurship, New Media: mass communications & journalism, film/digital media production (more about the related technologies: lights, cameras, sound), film/media editing (Final Cut Pro & Avid), and web/app design.
