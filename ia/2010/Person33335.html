Timothy Johnson’s Summary

An exciting and dynamic keynote speaker, who talks about project management (focus on communication and planning), office politics, creativity, strategy, and systems thinking. I provide take-back-to-your-desk application in a meaningful and entertaining way to audiences across the US. (NOTE: I am willing to entertain last minute speaker needs. If I can fit it into my schedule, I would be happy to accommodate you should you find yourself with a last minute cancellation.)

A workshop leader and trainer who focuses on results and end learning. I am experienced in all levels of team facilitation, so regardless of the industry or the issue, I focus on results.

An accomplished writer who has had three books published (Race Through The Forest - A Project Management Fable and GUST - The Tale Wind of Office Politics and SWAT - Seize the Accomplishment). I also publish my own web content, and I am a contributing author on both www.officepolitics.com and www.iowabiz.com

An experienced project manager who has driven successful results in multiple organizations. Using my skills in communication, planning, issues management, I have been able to drive multiple projects to successful accomplishment. My clients have included Harley-Davidson, Marsh, Wells Fargo, ING, Principal Financial Group, and Teva Neuroscience, Proficient in MS Project software, I am able to clearly build a plan that can be understood and used throughout the project life cycle. In addition, I train others on the use of MS Project. The projects I've led have included a multi-billion dollar class action lawsuit settlement, a branding merger, a video production training project, HIPAA compliance, software reporting selection, CAFM (computer aided facilities management), among others. I also excel at project rescue and recovery.

A Business Solutionist accomplished in many forms of business analysis, flow charting, process mapping, data analysis and interpretation.

Timothy Johnson’s Specialties:

Keynote speaking, Workshop leadership, project and program management, business analysis, Microsoft Project, Microsoft Visio, communication, writing, coaching and mentoring, facilitation
