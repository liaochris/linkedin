Will Genrich’s Summary

Will Genrich is the co-founder and Partner of Austin Real Capital. Will’s primary focus in Austin Real Capital is underwriting real estate investment opportunities the firm is considering. He is also a specialist in partnerships and real estate law. He has significant input into each of the divisions of the firm as well as leads the accounting and legal matters for the firm. As an experienced real estate investor, Will has a vast knowledge of real estate development and management. His knowledge and background put him in an advantageous position to be an expert resource for the firm and its clients. Will is in charge of creating all entities for the company and the management thereafter. His duties also include attracting in-state and out-of-state investors to the firm.

Prior to forming Austin Real Capital, Will has been a partner and asset manager for many commercial real estate limited liability companies which have developed and/or own interest in land; multi-family properties; medical facilities; office and mixed uses buildings; hotels with retail and restaurant leases; and shopping pavilion located in Western New York and St. Croix, USVI, as well as first lien mortgage interest in Central Texas.
