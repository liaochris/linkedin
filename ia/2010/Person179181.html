Rick Valderrama’s Summary

I have over 21 years of experience in voice and data network engineering. I've gained much of my engineering experience while serving 11 years in the United States Air Force. During this time I specialized in designing and implementing tactical local area and wide area networks with specific expertise in BGP routing and network security in converged environments. After separating from the Air Force I was hired by Quantum Technology Services, a Florida based military contractor, to launch their IT Engineering Division where I was first introduced to Telephony Partners. 5 years later I joined the Telephony Partners executive team and share in management responsibilities for the overall strategic and tactical direction of the company. In my newest role as the Director of Channel Sales at Telovations my objective is to develop and manage the indirect channel partner program.

Rick Valderrama’s Specialties:

Enterprise WAN, LAN, VoIP, MPLS design and implementation, carrier services and products, network security and business development.

Certifications:
