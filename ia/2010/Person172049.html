Rich Rinaldi’s Summary

Mr. Richard A. Rinaldi is President of ORA Consulting.
His practice specializes in Pro-bono Mentoring, Training and Strategic Planning.
He sold his business in 2005 and had been President and Chief Executive Officer of CCX since May of 1991.
He has over 40 years of broad industrial experience with companies such as General Electric, General Cable, Gulf & Western Industries and Coats Viyella. After joining CCX in 1991,
Mr. Rinaldi disposed of several non-performing divisions and turned the Company around from a loss position to record sales and profitability.
Mr. Rinaldi also navigated the Company through a complex restructuring program in 1994 that took it from a publicly held company to a privately held company.
Mr. Rinaldi has expanded the Company’s domestic and international sales and has firmly positioned it as a leader in its respective markets.
He holds a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering Degree and a Master of Science in Management Degree from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
Rinaldi also completed the Harvard Business School Program for Management Development.
