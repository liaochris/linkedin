Tim Murphy’s Summary

UpNorth-Vet, Inc. is a Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) based in Minneapolis, Minnesota that provides IT Contract Services to 1) Fortune 500/1000 companies, 2) Defense and Aerospace Industries, and 3) Federal, State, County and City governments. We are an ASAP-IT contract holder with all State agencies and CPV's.

Tim Murphy’s Specialties:

UpNorth-Vet, Inc provides IT Project Management, Business Analysis, Technical Architects and JAVA based open source Technologists. We have extensive Data Warehouse, SOA, Social Media and Legacy conversion applications experience spanning 25 years in the IT industry.

  • Owner

    June 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Sole owner of UpNorth-Vet, Inc, a Service-Disable Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) headquartered in Minneapolis/St. Paul. UpNorth-Vet, Inc is the culmination of 25 years of information technology industry experience supporting Fortune 500/1000 companies across the United States as well as locally to clients in the Twin Cities Metro Region. UpNorth-Vet specializes in IT Project Managers, Business Analysts, Technical Architects and JAVA based open source technologists.

    Tim has over 30 years of enlisted and officer military service, retiring as a Lieutenant Colonel from the United States Army Reserve.
