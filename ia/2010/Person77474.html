Greg Allbright’s Summary

Greg Allbright is a dynamic visionary and take charge problem solver who maximizes resources for nonprofits, governments, and businesses. An award winning leader with over ten years of combined management and finance experience he is uniquely suited to relate to the challenges organizations face and activate desired change.

Whether running for public office or starting his own social entrepreneurship Greg continually looks for opportunities to improve his knowledge and abilities, network with people, and give back to the community.

A native Texan, Greg is most at home grilling food for his wife, jogging the streets of Lakewood, and counting the days until the next Dallas Cowboys Super Bowl victory.
