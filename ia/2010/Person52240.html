David Vine’s Summary

Throughout my career I have been interested in practical research and communications. At first it was print and broadcast but later turned to electronic/computer communications as well as database research for a variety of clients.

Worked for several years as a business consultant in New Jersey and began to focus on presenting seminars about computer-derived information during the early 1990s. This led to the development of my Internet For Investigators(tm) seminar and Advanced Internet Search For Investigators(tm) workshop in 1996. This program continues to evolve and as of this date I have presented it in 47 states for more than 6,000 investigators.

We present a variety of webinars on topics ranging from free public records to time management with Google as well as several others. These are 90 minute live online meetings and slides presented are provided to participants afterward.

We also maintain a substantial online resource for attendees and I periodically publish related articles on my web site.
