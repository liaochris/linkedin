Bryan Davies’s Summary

Bryan Davies is a long standing Public Relations professional within the Video Gaming industry. Most recently he was the Global Public Relations Director For GamersFirst, a online free to play games publisher based in Irvine,Ca. As the PR Director Bryan was in charge of global PR and managment of PR Agencies. One of his main objectives was to develop a strong presence through PR in developing countries such as India, Turkey and Brazil.
Before GamersFirst he was the Media Specialist with Bender/Helper Impact (BHI), based in the San Francisco bay Area , he worked on many accounts including such companies as Webzen, Codemasters and Konami. Before BHI Bryan had been a Public Relations consultant for many companies, most recent being Climax, promoting their company as a worldwide developer along with proprietary technology, unsigned products and their Next Generation games, he has also worked with Sega promoting Rome: Total War Barbarian Invasion and Medieval 2. Bryan also worked with Totally Games focusing on their corporate public relations needs and unannounced products. During this time Bryan had also worked with Michael Meyers PR and J.Stokes & Associates creating a digital entertainment department.

Previous to Bryan’s successful consulting work he was the Public Relations manager for Novalogic , who is a global publisher and developer of computer and console games, and Eidos Interactive also a global publisher and developer of computer and console games. He has promoted many successful titles such as Deus Ex, Hitman, Fear Effect, Thief 1 & 2, Time Splitters, Comanche 4 and the Tomb Raider series to name a few
