Ryan Boyle’s Summary

Ryan Boyle received his Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Animation from the University of Central Florida. Ryan has done an extensive amount of freelance work as an illustrator, storyboard artist, compositor, and most recently a producer for various music videos and short films.

Additionally Ryan is employed at a local Media Arts College as a Content Developer creating content for online degrees in the form of motion graphics, videos, animations, and illustrations.
