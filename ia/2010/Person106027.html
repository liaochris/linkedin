Joshua Box’s Summary

I am an experienced, versatile and self-motivated professional with significant corporate and commercial experience. I have worked with a diverse range of multi-national companies, most of which that undergone mergers or significant change. I am passionate about driving business success whether through challenging the status quo or through the development of creative solutions. I have broad operational experience beyond the law in both the UK and EMEA working with multi-disciplinary teams to deliver in challenging business and financial environments.

I believe in the value of relationships. I am results orientated and have proven leadership, team building and strategic planning skills having successfully lead teams through the early stages of the “credit crunch” to deliver strong performance whilst transforming the legal support structure into an operationally effective and nimble team that recognises and can easily react to changing strategic priorities.

I have delivered efficiency gains and re-engineered third party relationships to more effectively support the business including appointing a commercially aware and pragmatic legal panel reducing the size from over 60 to 18 primary firms with a reduction of over 30% to the hourly rates charged. Commercially, my innovations resulted in reciprocal business arrangements with panel firms that have generated significant deposits and revenue in 2008 resulting in a short-listing at the 2008 FT Innovation Awards and an invite back to assist the judging of the 2009 Awards.

Joshua Box’s Specialties:

I have developed considerable skills in leading and coaching cross functional teams through change including lawyers, para-legals, HR professionals, logistics and various administrative functions. I have also delivered substantial initiatives on strategic planning, organisational development, team building and training exercises, project leadership and departmental management efficiencies.
