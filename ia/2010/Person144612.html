SUMMARY

As Director at Oneinfocus, Michoel manages new projects, the viral marketing campaign, social media marketing strategy, fundraising and coordinates team meetings. Michoel is also the principal blogger and photographer.

At UBC Financial, Michoel is the coordinator of online marketing where he leads the company's lead generation and branding strategy through social media marketing, blogging, online advertising campaigns, search engine optimization and email marketing.

Michoel received a degree in Physical Therapy with 1st Class Honors from Curtin University in Australia. He has carried over entrepreneurial, management and analytical skills as well as problem-solving abilities from the medical world to the Internet Industry.
