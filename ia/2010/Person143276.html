Michael Crane’s Summary

I’m a creative enthusiast, as well as a designer, marketer, and strategist.

I love ideas. I love the idea of ideas. I love the process of discovering ideas, evaluating them and even take a certain pleasure in shooting some down as well. Mostly, I love helping others unleash their creativity and venture into a place of unconsidered possibilities.

I've created, designed, developed and marketed products.

Having a curiosity for the workings of both the natural world and the business world, I earned undergrad degrees from Le Moyne College in both Biology and Business, and also hold an MBA from Presidio Graduate School (an intense business program that was among the first in the country to teach every course through the lens of sustainability).

Where I find myself today is playing in the nexus of design, natural systems, and the fast-changing landscape of business, helping companies develop business models and solutions that create positive social impact.
