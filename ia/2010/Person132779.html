Marilyn Cohodas’s Summary

Marilyn Cohodas is a creative and versatile editorial leader with exceptional content development, writing, editing, organizational, social media marketing and team building skills. Her 10-year track record in Web publishing includes launching and managing ten B2B sites, each serving a distinct audience and generating more than one million page visits a month.

Marilyn brings a rich journalistic background and deep intellectual curiousity to her professional work, having written about or consulted in technology, financial service reform, healthcare, the environment and education for consumer, business, academic and government markets.

She launched a Web content consulting practice in January 2009.

Marilyn Cohodas’s Specialties:

Editorial content development and management, online content strategies, social media marketing, online communities, Internet startups, site launches, search engine optimization (SEO) best practices for writers and editors, repurposing content from print to online media, building custom Web sites for small business, team building, editing and writing projects, advertorial content, editorial, marketing communications and media relations consulting, event planning, market research
