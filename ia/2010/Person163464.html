Patti Perdue’s Summary

Patti G. Perdue, CPA.CITP. Shareholder in the firm of Jackson Thornton & Co., P.C. specializes in practice management, strategic planning, technology planning and business consulting for medium sized businesses primarily physician practices, nursing homes and sole proprietors.

Patti has worked for dozens of physicians and group practices helping to improve AR collection, cash flow and internal control procedures.

She manages her firm's technology consulting company and provides strategic direction and financial oversight for the firm's internal IT work group.

Patti is an experienced speaker and frequently presents at meetings and conferences on such topics as: Managing Costs in Your Practice, Improving Cash Flow, HITECH Planning and many more.

Patti Perdue’s Specialties:

Practice management solutions, strategic planning, technology planning, compensation reviews, budgeting, tax, business accounting and reporting, operational reviews and practice staffing.
