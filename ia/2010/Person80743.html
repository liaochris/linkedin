Heather E. Coleman’s Summary

Innovative workforce development trainer and motivational speaker, creates and implements several programs to assist dislocated, unemployed and career-changing candidates to re-enter the job market. Social media specialist - trains customers and small business professionals in the utilization of social media PR. Owner of Career Savvy. Develops and leads over 25 specialized seminars and workshops, special events, networks with community business leaders, oversees professional marketing sites, and creates marketing pieces for programs. Teaches approximately 600 clients on a monthly basis.

Available for local and regional speaking engagements on current employment trends, social media for career success, job loss and recovery, experienced worker, leadership in the workforce and related topics.

Educational experience includes teaching and school administration at all levels, including adults. Specialities are Teaching, Program Development, Adult Education, Motivational Public Speaking, Gifted Educational Research and Marketing.
