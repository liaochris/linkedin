Matt Hannam’s Summary

Ten years experience working with nationwide telecommunications providers for both the wireline and wireless industries. Experience working with both regulated and non regulated providers.

As a professional recruiter I strive to serve the job seeker by placing him/her in a job that will provide them with both financial security and personal satisfaction.
