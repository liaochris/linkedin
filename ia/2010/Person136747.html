Mohawk Matt Berman’s Summary

Bolt Barbers™ is the brainchild of Global Marketing Entrepreneur Matt Berman, who spent 25 years in the corporate world in senior marketing and general management positions with Procter & Gamble, Coty, Novartis, and Royal Numico (Acquired by Groupe Danone). Matt has lived, worked, and gotten his haircut in Paris, Lisbon, Amsterdam, Warsaw, Annapolis, Atlanta, & Cincinnati. Matt is from New York & has resided in Downtown Los Angeles, USA since 2008. Matt has an Advanced Management Degree from Wharton School of Business and graduated The Real Barbers College with a 1500 Hour Master Barber Certificate in June, 2009.

Matt, who rocks a mohawk, completed his 1500 hour training requirement and became a licensed state barber on July 2, 2009. Bolt Barbers™ will open its very first authentic barbershop For Social Beasts™ in Downtown Los Angeles, USA at 460 South Spring Street, on November 12th, 2009.

Mohawk Matt Berman’s Specialties:

Seasoned Global Corporate Product Experience Managing Hair Care, Men's Grooming, & Cosmetics Brands in Europe & The USA at P&G & Coty and Authentic, Practical, Hands on Barbering Experience at the Real Barbers College.
