Bill Sanders (tweet@crushandpress)’s Summary

Bill Sanders is an evangelist for authentic handcrafted extra virgin olive oil. He educates on how to discriminate and incorporate the flavors and benefits of these "juices" into our lives, paired with expert wine and exotic food selections. Bill, a widely recognized authority in the olive oil and wine industries, offers speaking engagements, demonstrations, consulting, videos, and virtual events on the benefits of these natural nectars. He also serves as the U.S. spokesperson for 100% Tunisian Olive Oil and is a regular writer for “Olive Oil Times,” You can enjoy "insider" insights, recipes, and recommendations through his blog, at crushandpress.com. Follow his adventures while he travels the world visiting the world's finest olive oil farms and wine vintners. Bill founded Crush and Press (CrushandPress.com), an online boutique for purchasing high quality extra virgin olive oil and gourmet products in 2009, so he could bring these treasured discoveries to you. They’re the real deal!
