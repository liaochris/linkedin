Richard Lewith’s Summary

Engineering Recruitment Consultant with a wealth of experience supplying recruitment services to small and global organisations in Melbourne, Victoria and across Australia. I specialise in a range of disciplines from Civil Engineering Design (Structural, Highways, Bridges & Civil Infrastructure) & Construction, Geotechnical Engineering (mining & civil) to railway and electrical engineering.

Richard Lewith’s Specialties:

Over the years I have placed engineers from graduate to Principal Level. I am always seeking for experienced engineers in the following areas: Geotechnical, Structural (buildings & bridges), Civil Highway Designers & Engineers, Railway Engineers (p-ways, OHW & signalling), Electrical Power Designers (primary & secondary protection), Civil Estimators, Project Managers.
