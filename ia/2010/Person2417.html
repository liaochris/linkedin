Adam Max’s Summary

An ownership-minded, results oriented, corporate trainer with a 15-year track record of accomplishments in process improvement, business development & management, recruiting, sales & marketing, conflict resolution, and public speaking. Excels in developing & delivering training programs that create substantial business development, profitability improvement, and operational results for companies with multi site operations.

The common thread with Adam’s endeavors is his ability to think and act quickly and efficiently, keeping the company’s best interests at hand. His passion, enthusiasm, and belief in the power of relationships permeate throughout his training programs, inspiring his teams to improved results through individual accomplishments.

He brings a unique blend of skills and experience to simplify, improve, and duplicate training & operational aspects, with the result of increased revenues. He is a leader in Word of Mouth Marketing who combines a “back to basics” work ethic, relationships building, and forthright communication, with years of experience in operations, training and sales.

Adam’s greatest joy is looking at a profitable operation, knowing that success has been created where previously less existed, and that individuals are more productive members of increasingly profitable teams.
