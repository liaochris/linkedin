    (Real Estate industry)

    REOLockbox.com is an Automated Lockbox Delivery Service for the REO industry. This site streamlines the process of delivering lockbox codes to agents that are interested in showing REO listings.

    The company was founded in Florida in 2007 by REO industry professionals and investors who saw the need for a better way to deliver lockboxes to agents for showings. With the explosion of new foreclosures it seemed like every time we would want to view a property it took 4 phone calls to get the lockbox and any other details on the property. There are other services that attempt to fill this need in the market, but most require phone calls and annoying license verification processes. REOLockbox.com is the only fully automated service that is also entirely online, where most real estate professionals spend their time searching for properties.
