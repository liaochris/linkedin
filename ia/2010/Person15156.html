Antoni Guillén’s Summary

Antoni Guillen has 18 years of experience as an executive in the IT market. He is founder and CEO of Essi Projects SA since 1996 and Edustance SL Since 2002. From 1990 until today has worked as a trainer and consultant for various companies in the sectors of education, IT and health. From 1999 to 2002 was Associate Lecturer in the Departament d'Informàtica i Matemàtiques of the Universitat Rovira i Virgili. It is one of the founding members of the public network Tinet.

Antoni Guillén’s Experience

  • CEO and Founder

    EDUSTANCE S.L

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2002 — Present (8 years 7 months)

    Founder and owner of the company. Edustance S.L. was created in October, 2002 as part of the developing business of its parent company, Essi Projects S.A.
    In 1998, Essi Projects, together with various Spanish universities, began a research project which produced one of the first on-line training programs in the world written entirely in Java programming language. In 1999, Essi Projects created Edustance, a department dedicated specifically to the development and commercialization of this product and training services.
    Edustance has had collaborative agreements with Sun since 2002. At present it provides multiple on-site and on-line training services for Solaris, Java, RedHat-JBoss and Zimbra.

  • CEO and Founder

    ESSI PROJECTS

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1996 — Present (14 years 7 months)

    Founder and owner of the company. Essi Projects is an Information Technology and Communications Engineering company, specialized in application infrastructure and system integration projects. It primarily develops its activities in the Iberian Peninsula. The company focuses on the execution of system infrastructure projects, specifically in the areas of operating environments (servers, storage, Unix/Linux systems), application infrastructure (databases, application servers and frameworks), information and collaboration systems (communication tools, portals), software architecture (JEE) and security
