Tanya Marie Vlach’s Summary

The same day Hurricane Katrina hit, Tanya Vlach was driving on the other side of the country in Northern California on her way to one of the largest arts festivals in the world. At dusk she was found unconscious from a traumatic accident. Barely coming out alive, she lost her left eye in the tumble. Becoming this intimate with death, Tanya is driven by the consciousness of the fragile nature of life and her work is imbued with this sense of urgency.

5th generation San Franciscan, Tanya considers herself a trans-disciplinary artist, having an extensive background in dance, theater, visual, and literary arts. The founder of COLIBRITA, a multi-disciplinary organization committed to showcasing innovative talent into the public eye, Tanya has produced and curated several arts events throughout the Bay Area.
