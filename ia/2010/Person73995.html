Genevieve Margherio’s Summary

At Red Lemon Creative, Inc. I provide graphic design for print and web, including web sites, e-newsletters, blogs, logos, business cards, letterhead, promotional posters, direct mail postcards, trade show signage, fact sheets, brochures, CDs, DVDs, educational materials, and more.

Because graphic design is interrelated with so many other business needs, I also write, edit, project manage, and provide general marketing guidance and advice.

For small organizations, I am a right-hand woman to a busy CEO or ED who doesn't employ full-time staff to handle marketing and design needs.

For larger organizations, I work with in-house teams, consultants, and executives to support or complement in-house marketing and design efforts.

I make a practice of working with dedicated, on-the-ball professionals who enjoy what they do, and I enjoy helping them do it!
