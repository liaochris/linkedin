Michael Gottsacker’s Summary

Unify Systems. Unleash Efficiency.
The pursuit of efficiency in manufacturing operations is constant. To gain efficiencies manufacturers must sense and respond to meaningful events. Data collection and visibility solutions, while important, do not address the underlying causes of inefficiency. Efficiencies can only be gained by a response. The problem is complicated in manufacturing operations by the dramatic diversity of assets and systems found on the plant floor.

As One Technologies developed its Catalyst® suite of products to address the challenges found in manufacturing operations and provide a common platform to unify systems on the plant floor and to the enpteprise. With open connectivity to a wide variety of manufacturing assets and systems and the ability to simply and easily assemble and distribute the logic required to interpret and respond to meaningful events, Catalyst® is the key to unleashing efficiency.

Catalyst SDP™ - System Development Platform
Catalyst® is a growing suite of applications developed by As One focused on making real-time manufacturing operations more efficient. The foundation of the Catalyst suite of application is Catalyst SDP™, our System Development Platform. Catalyst SDP™ combines three powerful concepts into one software product: a composite application development framework, unifying service architecture and distributed execution capability. Catalyst SDP™ dramatically simplifies software and system development for manufacturers, system integrators and OEMs/ISVs, allowing them to quickly and easily integrate highly disparate manufacturing systems and address a variety of application needs within the manufacturing environment.
