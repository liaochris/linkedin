Ronald La Serra, CPA’s Summary

Extensive knowledge and firm resources in the areas of employee benefits and pension options, financial planning, and retirement income and senior planning.

My goal is to provide families, business owners, and professionals with assistance in building their financial freedom. I have the skills, knowledge and experience required to help establish and attain my client’s financial objectives.
