Kerry O'Malley’s Summary

Are you responsible for marketing in a manufacturing or industrial service company, and looking for cost effective ways to build your brand, gain credibility, move into new markets and bring in new business?

Check out my blog: Industrial Marketing Muse

I am passionate about BtoB marketing communications, especially in manufacturing and industrial companies. Before starting my own business, I managed large, marketing communications programs "on the other side of the desk" in several manufacturing companies. I realized there was a huge gap between what professional marketers in manufacturing and industry needed, and what was available to them. I set out 12 years ago to bridge that gap, and haven't looked back!

Since then, I have helped many small to mid size industrial companies who need to outsource their marketing communications initiatives. I help them develop strategies and plans to increase their brand recognition; change their image; expand into new markets; develop new product line identities; and generate new business through creative marketing communications solutions: advertising; P/R; trade show displays; marketing literature; e-mail marketing; web site development and content management; and corporate identity development.

I don't have a list of degrees or wall full of awards; but I have a client base that trusts me to manage their projects flawlessly; appreciates my creativity and willingness to work with impossible budgets and deadlines; and who have seen their businesses positively impacted by my work.

I am most recently immersing myself in the world of social media marketing and helping my industrial clients understand how it translates to their businesses and how they can best capitalize on its opportunities.
