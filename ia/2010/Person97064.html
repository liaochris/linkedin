Jessica Hansen’s Summary

Public relations professional with more than seven years of experience. Began career working in fast-paced agency environments in integrated marketing roles and as the account lead for PR clients in a variety of industries including architecture design, automotive aftermarket, education, healthcare, hospitality, interior design, nonprofit, and travel/tourism. Made decision to move in-house when offered a consumer PR position with Intel’s Global Communications Group, which is enabling me to expand my PR expertise and also work in technology again.

Interest in continuing to advance the communication profession is showcased through leadership role as past president of International Association of Business Communicators (IABC) Phoenix chapter. Passion for cause marketing efforts is expressed through dedication to serve in volunteer role of marketing communication chair of American Diabetes Association Valley of the Sun Community Leadership Board.

Have increased visibility of companies with proven success measured against objectives that are strategically aligned with business goals that positively impact the bottom line. Skilled in developing optimized press materials, with a particular area of expertise in clearly communicating technical subjects to then clearly resonate with a variety of audiences. Adept in effectively engaging audiences through social media marketing efforts.
