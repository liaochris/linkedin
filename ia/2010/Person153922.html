Nick Douthat’s Summary

I have completed an assignment as General Manager at an automotive ride control manufacturing facility in Wuhu, China. I took over this role after completing the capital equipment installation, which included supervising the Chinese staff in setting up their production processes and systems. The facility is an automotive supplier plant designed to service the growing Chinese market, and I played a leading role in the strategic development of the facility and was the manager responsible for the budgeting of the capital equipment. In addition, I was the primary advisor during the plant design and building stage, utilizing my experience to determine the layout and support structure.

I have extensive experience in lean manufacturing and its application on the production floor, and have successfully installed and improved multiple process lines. My experience, as both an Engineering Manager and Production Manger, includes implementing QS-9000, ISO 14001, and TS-16949. It also includes multiple product launches, many taking place simultaneously. I have also built kanban, maintenance, and production control systems where none previously existed, leading to increased production efficiency and lower quality and safety costs.

From a global standpoint, I have purchased equipment from multiple countries, and have done so both on-time and within budget. My exposure includes both Europe and Asia, and I have extensive experience training new workers in the implementation of world-class production and quality standards.
