Karima-Catherine Goundiam’s Summary

I am a business consultant with professional experience in strategic, business, product, campaign and project management. I have managed projects for Telecommunications, Banking, Pharmaceuticals and Air line companies.

I launched Three.angels marketing in 2009 and work with companies to integrate social media in their overall business strategy. Thereby, I help brands define, implement and monitor their social media programs and attain their business objectives. My client base ranges from small businesses to big companies in various industries like retail and publishing.

With years of marketing and project management experience in corporations as well as agencies, I tie in the pieces to create a solid and sound business approach. I also offer coaching, training and seminar sessions.

Aside from my consultancy practice :
I co-organize the Social Media Breakfast in Montréal.
I am Social Media Advisor and Member of the Editorial Commitee for the Relational Marketing association.
I volunteer at the YWCA Entrepreneurship centre in Montreal where I coach business women from various industries integrate Web 2.0 and more particularly, social media.

I publish my blog at 3angelsmarketing.com where I write about business, social media and random events that spurs my curiosity.

I would be happy to discuss how I could help you understand social media and how to integrate it in your business; I work with big companies, medium-sized businesses as well as entrepreneurs, you can reach me through here or through my contact information provided at 3angelsmarketing {dot} com
