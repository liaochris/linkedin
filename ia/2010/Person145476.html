Mike Hilbert’s Summary

Sales Pros 4 Hire's mission is to increase your sales and take your company to the next level. We do this through our Great Prospects Sale Leads program and our outsourced sales process.

We work with you to develop your target market and find and qualify prospects in that target market. Then we arrange selling opportunities for you to show how you can solve the prospect's problems. We follow up the opportunity to its conclusion so you can return to running your business and then create retention programs to keep these new customers

Great Prospect's Sales Leads gives you qualified leads in your target market which allows you more time for making face-to-face sales presentations and closing deals. And more time is more money.

Great Prospects saves you time by doing the research for you. Daily, we diligently search over a dozen local business news publications and web sites and gather current, local and fresh sales leads of business leaders in the Philadelphia area who are making news. These decision makers are receiving a promotion, moving to a new company, joining a board of directors or receiving an award for achieving a milestone or their charitable activities.
