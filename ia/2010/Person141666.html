Maureen Haga’s Summary

With more than two decades of experience in sales performance development, change management, and leadership development, I understand how to motivate a workforce to execute customer-centric strategies for growth. As principal of M2Execution, I utilize a collaborative approach to enable clients to implement tools and strategies needed to strengthen mission-critical skills and achieve business objectives. I have served as a trusted advisor to corporations and Fortune 500's successfully managing significant cultural transformation changes within these organizations. My leadership and guidance has enabled clients to achieve new business development and expansion growth goals, as well as raise Net Promoter Scores (NPS) to increase customer loyalty.
