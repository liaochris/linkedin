Terra Terwilliger’s Summary

Brigid Executive Consulting offers practical advice on how to make technologies work for you. We talk cutting-edge tech. But we focus on what technologies mean for your business.

As Founder and Principal at Brigid Executive Consulting, I advise companies on how the latest and greatest tech trends will affect their bottom line.

Then I help them make it happen--from writing a business plan to helping to put together a team and launch a product or business line.

Current areas of interest to clients are social technologies, social media, subscription-based business models and SaaS. I stay in touch with the Silicon Valley scene by working closely with a number of tech start-ups here, but I also advise Fortune 500 companies outside of Silicon Valley.

Services include marketing strategy, planning and execution; product management strategy, planning and execution; business planning, product development and new market entry; and interim CEO, COO and CMO services.

Terra Terwilliger’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy, marketing to small/medium businesses, marketing to consumers, consumer internet, mobile, social media, Internet marketing, channel strategy, interim executive services
