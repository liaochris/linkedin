Brent Campbell’s Summary

With active participation from two out of three global internet users, traffic figures rivaling the top search engines, and buzz that shows no signs of slowing – in just a few years social networks like Facebook, Twitter and YouTube have fundamentally changed how people share information. Social networks now enable a brand’s biggest fans to advocate on its behalf - provided the brand gives its fans a compelling reason and the tools to spread the word.

I have worked with the top brand marketers and their agencies - across the automotive, beverage, financial and pharmaceutical categories - to develop and maintain their strategic presence on these crucial communication channels. Given my comprehensive experience across digital and traditional media – with the last year and a half focused exclusively on social – I am acutely aware of the specific elements required for a successful integrated marketing program.

Looking ahead my objective is to help brand marketers build more effective marketing platforms that will evolve in step with today’s technologically-empowered consumers, harness the quickly-evolving emerging media channels’ unique abilities, and create relevant and engaging brand experiences.
