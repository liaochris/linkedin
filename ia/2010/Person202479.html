G. D. Stanford, CCIM’s Summary

I have spent a number of years establishing and running a Residential Real Estate Brokerage in SW Atlanta, Ga. I gradually transitioned into the Commercial R.E. Industry, first buying and selling several Commercial properties in the City of Atlanta and eventually becoming involved in assisting clients with the acquisition and disposition of Commercial properties.

In the process, I received a Master's Degree in Urban Planning and Economic Development from the Andrew Young School of Policy Studies at Georgia State University. This lead me to Project REAP; a Commercial Real Estate Initiative, founded by Mike Bush, and designed to increase the number of Minorities involved in the management side of the Commercial Real Estate Industry.


As a result of my involvement with Project REAP, I took a position with Halpern Enterprises, Inc. in Doraville, Ga., serving as a Property Manager for a little over 4 years. I managed a number of Shopping Centers and Specialty Properties for HEI.
It was at this point that I became interested in CCIM Institute and the advanced training available to those involved in the Institute. I acheived my CCIM Designation in October 2005.

I established G.D. Stanford & Associates, just prior to completing my CCIM training. We serve as Commercial Real Estate and Small Business Consultants. Our goal is to provide Small Business client with Services and Resources that help them reach the Success that they seek.
