Erik Andersson’s Summary

Erik has an academic background from Industrial Engineering and Management, with a minor in Applied Mathematics, from Chalmers University of Technology and from Business Administration from University of Gothenburg. He is currently studying his last year to a M.Sc. in Innovation & Entrepreneurship at Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship. His professional experiences ranges from Production Engineering to Project Management and Business Development. Erik’s background vouches for a thorough understanding of the interplay between technology, business and management. The courses at CSE focuses on value creation in the knowledge economy, with courses in e.g. Intellectual Property Strategies and Entrepreneurship in high-tech ventures.This final year the education revolves around a real-life research-project, in Erik's case from Material Sciences at Chalmers. We are aiming to commercialize a new way of strengthening structures in need of reinforcement with composite materials.

Erik is also a business bootstrapper at BootstrapLabs, where his principal project concerns the development of an international Innovation Tournament.
