Amanda Hite’s Summary

Amanda Hite inspirationalist and founder of TALENT [rev-o-lut-ion] is a change agent who's bringing sexy back to corporate thinking and people performance. An Army kid who living in 32 homes by the age of 18 earned her Masters in Street Smart Psychology. Amanda started her career in the service industry 15 years ago. Most recently Hite has been the Director of Training and Development for Thomas & King the nation's eighth largest Franchise. During her tenure with T&K Amanda and her team became well known for developing innovative T&D tools like LeaderQuest a web based career development application. http://leaderquest.tandk.com/LQDetails.aspx


Hite is known for using tools like youtube, podcast and social networking sites to reach the youth: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=egh2C7TUjUo
The LQ video won a Gold Addy award.

Hite is a member of the National Speakers Association and her passion for People has been displayed several times during public speaking engagements. Amanda has been featured multiple times in publication's including once on the Cover of Nation's Restaurant News. Her training approach is sharp, focused and funky. Amanda Hite is a paradigm buster that will inspire you to drastically change the way you think and behave with talent.
