Bob Paff’s Summary

Bob Paff is founder and president of Integrated Benefits Corporation, a full-service financial services boutique for individuals, families, professionals, the self-employed and small businesses. A nationally recognized, skilled professional in the field of insurance and benefits, Bob is a sought after speaker around the country on a variety of topics including healthcare and selling to achieve success.

The father of six, he and his wife, Dr. Laura Herrera, a trained Family Physician and Director of Women's Health for the Veteran's Administration in Washington, D.C., reside in Roland Park.

Currently, IBC provides financial services, insurance and retirement benefits for over 1000 clients in 32 states as far west as California.
