Kevin Day’s Summary

Dynamic and dedicated entrepreneur with ten years of experience developing and managing both private and public companies. Exceptional problem solver particularly with regard to finding solutions to challenges faced by early stage companies. Expert in bootstrap funding as well as capital raising for startups.
