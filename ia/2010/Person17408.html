Ashley Wheeler’s Summary

I am a highly organized, meticulous individual seeking employment as a Program Director, Corporate Trainer, or Technical Writer. As a state employee for over 12 years in education and program/project management, I have extensive experience with following legal procedures and being accountable for them, while ensuring total compliance with government mandates and guidelines. I am interested in positions related to marketing and potentially sales as well.

While at Auburn University, I majored the first two years in Business and later completed five Ph.D. level graduate courses in statistics. At Jacksonville State University, I completely redesigned the entire Clinical Experiences Department for Alabama’s largest College of Education, ensuring compliance with Alabama State Department of Education standards (ALSDE). I supervised and trained over 450 individuals each semester and authored a 225+ page handbook for the department.

Having an extensive background in education makes me a unique candidate. I believe the experience documented on my posted resume, bears witness to my natural ability as an adaptable and meticulous individual with a propensity for learning various things quickly and easily. I possess the ability to multi-task, work within time constraints, and as a member of a diverse team. Working in a public relations capacity while at JSU, was invigorating.

I am enthusiastic about putting my critical skills to work with a company in the Huntsville/Madison area (either on or off the arsenal). I truly desire to work with a reputable company with which I can stay and eventually retire. I am not looking to relocate, but would consider the Birmingham or Nashville areas. Thank you for checking out my profile. I am an enthusiastic person with a strong work ethic.
