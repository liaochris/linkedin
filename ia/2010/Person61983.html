Edward Lortz’s Summary

I am looking for part time contract clients in the office administrative and bookkeeping fields. My ideal client is a one to three person professional firm in SF with low to mid six figure cash flow and only needs a bookkeeper two days a month.

My integrity is most important, but i also have very strong, detail oriented organizational and analytical skills, having maintained clients' as well as my own personal, business, and rental accounts for decades. I have extremely good writing, oral, and interpersonal skills.

Extremely proficient in Quicken and Quickbooks (both Windows and Mac OS), MS Word, Excel; Proficient in Timeslips, Windows OS, Apple OS; and familiar with PrimePay, SurePay and ADP payroll systems. Limited experience in HTML, Macromedia Fireworks, Adobe Photoshop Elements
