David Simons’s Summary

OVERVIEW:
20+ years of professional Supply Management experience across a broad range of businesses and commodities. They include telecommunications, specialty chemicals, aerospace, and nuclear industries. Responsibilities encompassed Strategic and Demand Planning, Strategic and Tactical Procurement, Supplier Selection & Monitoring, regional, national, and international Logistics including import and export, and Inventory Management in conjunction with 3PL warehousing. Leadership positions on major ERP software implementations including SAP and Oracle. Effective leader and motivator. ISO facilitator and process owner.
HOW I GOT STARTED:
I earned my engineering degree but found that the economy was so bad that engineers with 20+ years experience were out of work. At that point there was an opportunity to join Production Control which I did. Eventually, my employer realized I could count and gave me inventory management responsibilities. At one point I was a regional manager for an industrial inventory service specializing in year end inventories. Eventually someone figured out that I can also read blueprints and understand manufacturing flow, and asked if I was interested in working in procurement auditing and qualifying suppliers. This led to high level procurement and contracting positions. During that time I earned my B.Sc. in Business Management, gaining Honor Society recognition.
WHAT I LIKE TO DO:
I like to work in a small to medium manufacturer or distributor of almost any commodity, I like a broad range of Supply Management responsibilities that value contracting and procurement of critical goods & services while having managerial responsibility. I control resources and tasks by managing through data and KPI’s.
