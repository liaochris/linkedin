David Grossman, ABC, APR, Fellow PRSA’s Summary

A leading consultant, speaker and author, David Grossman is one of America’s foremost authorities on communication inside organizations.

David is president and founder of The Grossman Group (formerly dg&a), an award-winning Chicago-based communications consultancy focusing on organizational consulting, strategic leadership development and internal communications. With a roster of Fortune 500 clients including Cisco Systems, Heinz, Intel, Lilly, Lockheed Martin, McDonald’s, WellPoint and Virgin Atlantic, dg&a works at the highest levels within organizations helping drive turning point moments for its clients.

David also currently teaches the only graduate-level course in internal communications in the U.S. at Columbia University.
