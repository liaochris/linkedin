Eric Rousseau’s Summary

I've worked in the Information Technology business since 1996 and have a wealth of information and experience to successfully design, operate and manage large technology environments. I have worked in permanent positions where I was solely responsible for an environment, and I've also worked as a consultant where I had to learn configurations quickly for various clients.
My last fixed position was as a Senior System Administrator working for ACS Healthcare Solutions in Connecticut where I managed all servers, applications and infrastructure that was running Microsoft. During those seven years, my list of managed items include HP/IBM Servers, Microsoft NT4.0, 2000 & 2003, Citrix, VMWare, SQL 7.0, 2000 & 2005, IIS, DNS, DHCP, WINS, Exchange 5.0, 2000 & 2003, and Clustered configurations. I also worked with various applications relating to business and health care such as PeopleSoft, Impac, Cerner, ProgRIS, SoftMed and MicroMedex. In this environment where I supported 80+ servers and 3000+ nodes, I made enterprise changes with domain upgrades and consolidations, server patching, Exchange Migrations, security remediations and development in scripts for single sign-on.
Currently, I work for ASI System Integration in the consulting and integration group. I am based in Manhattan, New York, and have consulted for various businesses including Merrill Lynch, The Rockefeller Group, Church Pension Group and New Plan Excel Realty Trust Inc. I've performed work such as data center relocations, Microsoft Active Directory audit, analysis and issue remediation, security audits, Exchange Server development and migration, Citrix installations, upgrades and migrations, and lab data-center designs and builds.
My specialties include, but are not limited to, Microsoft Active Directory and all Microsoft application and server products, Citrix, VMWare, VDI, server hardware, networking and VBScript scripting for mass reporting and manipulating.
