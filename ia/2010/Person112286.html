Kathy O'Reilly’s Summary

Produced nationally-syndicated TV talkshows and magazine format TV news and entertainment programming for 15 years before moving over to the Internet in 1999. Developed and directed all PR, Social Media Strategies, Corporate Communications and Media Relations for one of the pioneering Internet brands. Currently oversee Social Media Strategies/Outreach and manage traditional Media and Analyst relations for the leading job search matching engine & online global career/recruitment web site.

*Manage Traditional PR and Social Media for product announcements and outreach to boost brand awareness and drive traffic.
*Develop and maintain media relationships (broadcast, print, online, bloggers) with business, trade and consumer leads.
*Manage social media messaging and networking.
*Provide content leads for company Blog.
*Direct/Staff media interviews.
*Serve as company spokesperson.
*Oversees the message development process, drafting message documents, proof points and delivering potential qualified answers.
*Pitch, coordinate and monitor media opportunities for content spokespeople.
*Act as a content manager for Monster’s editorial calendar, news features, bylined articles, etc.
*Identify, create and post content to a range of social media/social network sites.
*Monitor social media activity & leverage opportunities.
*Oversee analysis and reporting on social media activity.
*Write press releases and media alerts.
*Develop and manage pitches for media and analysts.
*Manage external relationships with analyst firms including IDC, Forrester, Gartner, Aberdeen Group and comScore.
*Develop ongoing speaking calendar for spokespeople, identifying top-tier conferences for senior management and product leads and identify spokespeople within organization.
