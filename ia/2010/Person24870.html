Ben Lewis’s Experience

  • CEO + Founder

    PurBlu Beverages, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Food & Beverages industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    PurBlu Beverages is an emerging and pioneering marketer of innovative natural brands –
    GIVE Water, GIVE Energy, and Potion Herbal Remedy.

    GIVE Water is a fast-growing beverage brand on a mission. Through a simple, everyday purchase, consumers choose how to GIVE – Life for children, Hope for breast cancer, Love for the environment, Strength for muscular disorders. For each bottle sold, 10 cents is donated to local charities in communities where GIVE is sold. The first and only oxo-biodegradable bottle in North America, GIVE bottles are 100% recyclable and return to the earth in 10 years.

    GIVE Energy is a healthy all-natural fuel for the brain and body – a clean, sustainable energy drink. For each can sold, 10 cents is contributed to grassroots sustainable and renewable energy initiatives such as wind, solar, and bioenergy projects.

    Currently sold in thousands of coffee shops, natural foods, grocery, drug, and convenience stores throughout the United States and Canada, GIVE has triggered a movement of consumer philanthropy. The brand has received millions of national and international media impressions, including recent features by CNN, PBS, Fox News, Entrepreneur, and Inc. Magazine.

    PurBlu Beverages is also the creator of Potion Herbal Remedy, the first all-natural functional health shot. Potion blends proven herbs, botanicals, and supplements in a 2.5 oz shot platform – including Power, Calm, Focus, Burn, Detox, and Immunity.
