Robert Vasquez, C.P.M. (CPM)’s Summary

Proven results oriented professional in procurement, strategic sourcing. A (CPM) Certified Purchasing Manager (C.P.M.) and passionate about helping company's achieve their procurement and strategic sourcing goals. I have managed multi-million dollar projects, developed teams of procurement and strategic sourcing professionals dedicated to customer service, team work and procurement collaboration. Developed procurement partnerships with suppliers (vendors) and exceptional procurement relationships with internal clients in their procurement, strategic sourcing goals to ensure compliance to procurement processes in a collaborative team based manner. I have managed in the government and public sectors (Telecommunications, Financial Services, Pharmaceuticals and Health Care).

I have expertise in the following areas:
*Contract writing/creation
*Technology contracts
*Commodity contracts
*Service contracts
*Healthcare contracts
*Personnel development
*Personnel management
*Manage supplier (vendor) relationships
*Create proactive client relationships
*Develop and write policies
*Develop and write procedures
*Resolution of inter-departmental operational issues
*Negotiation (win-win)
*Create an environment of teamwork and collaboration
*Create and manage RFX's
