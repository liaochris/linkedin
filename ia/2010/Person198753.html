Syl Orsi’s Summary

I am a Chief Financial Officer with 20 years of finance experience that combines strong financial management expertise, with valuable legal and communication skills. I have added value to both public and private companies, including a private equity-backed company. My skills include financial management, capital transactions, accounting, treasury, mergers & acquisitions, corporate governance, employment matters, tax and litigation oversight. I am self-motivated and a proven leader with the ability to anticipate issues, and to respond with sound judgment. I am a CPA with Big Four experience and strong academic credentials, graduating in the top 10% of my law school class.

Syl Orsi’s Experience

  • Owner and CFO

    Redimensions

    (Real Estate industry)

    July 2006 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Founded real estate development firm focused on pedestrian-friendly and environmentally sustainable communities. Successfully navigated a condominium project with a project value in excess of $4.5 million through challenging housing and credit markets and produce profitable results.

    Responsibilities. I was responsible for virtually all facets of the business, including financial modeling and business case preparation; contracting with suppliers and contractors; negotiation and drafting of asset acquisition agreements; negotiating debt agreements and tax-increment finance (TIF) agreements; zoning changes; project management, construction management and marketing and sales.

    Selected Achievements.

    Financial Modeling, Business Case and Financial Reporting. I prepared a financial model and a business case for an underserved segment of the Omaha condominium market that was evaluated by lender as “one of the best he has seen.” I originated a $1.5 million construction loan and a $0.5 million TIF loan during a challenging credit market. I also oversaw all financial statement preparation.

    Contractor and Supply Management. I directly negotiated dozens of contractor agreements and managed the approval and disbursement of payables. I controlled costs by negotiating a fixed-fee construction contract, by directly contracting and managing the finish work, and by incrementalizing the build.

    Tenacious Project Management. After excessive delay from the City of Omaha TIF administrator, I drafted the City’s documentation and tracked daily progress until TIF was approved in a timely manner. I assembled a “turn key” package leading to early Fannie Mae and FHA project approval.

    Value Realization. I secured option rights on significant parcels surrounding the project site and currently am in negotiations on those parcels that are expected to yield significant profits.

  • Chief Financial Officer

    PenneCom B.V.

    (Telecommunications industry)

    June 1998 — March 2006 (7 years 10 months)

    Upon the recommendation of four private equity funds, I was hired to manage the financial affairs of their portfolio company that built telecommunications companies in Poland and Hungary. The Hungarian operations grew from negative EBITDA to $10.5 million of EBITDA in five years prior to its sale in 1998. The Polish company was a start up operation that over 7 years grew to $14 million in revenue and was contracted for sale to a strategic acquirer for $140 million before becoming operationally profitable.

    Selected Achievements.

    I led an effort to refine and develop the build-out plan to focus on high ROIC business customers and to reduce debt requirements. We managed to get an IRR of 41% for the private equity investors. During the same holding period Budapest stock exchange index returns were -11% in USD.

    Oversaw the annual audit and financial statement preparation, as well as periodic reporting, that led to timely financial reporting to each investor group.

    Worked with divisional financial personnel to prepare annual operating and capital budgets, and to disburse capital within the budget or pre-approved variances from the budget during each year of investment period.

    I managed the due diligence process for transactions totaling over $200 million.

    I negotiated and drafted a $20 million convertible debt investment, $30 million project finance facility, $50 million term credit facility and vendor finance contracts.

    I identified an opportunity for and led a tax reorganization that involved a Dutch holding company, a disregarded foreign subsidiary, a US public company and several other US corporations. The strategy saved in excess of $15 million in taxes.

    I managed a team of lawyers, and expert and fact witnesses that led to a recovery of $49 million in damages for breach of contract following an international arbitration.

    I negotiated and drafted the contracts for corporate transactions totaling nearly $250 million.
