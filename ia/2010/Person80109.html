Harrison Wise’s Summary

Mr. Wise has a proven 14+ year track record of working with established & emerging companies who seek results and excellence in: national/international media relations; strategic and crisis communications; media training; community relations; social media; new product/company launches; internal/external communications.

Recognized as an outstanding PR practitioner Harrison is a powerful and effective communications pro focused on garnering great results for clients.
