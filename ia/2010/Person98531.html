Jim Letourneau’s Summary

I'm a geologist, speaker, and editor of the Big Picture Speculator investment newsletter editor living Calgary, Alberta.

I have created a powerful social media web that integrates the power of Facebook, Twitter, a 5000 person email list, and my blog. I'm routinely on the Facebook and Twitter Elite lists.

I have toured mining and exploration projects in many parts of the world and I regularly meet with oil and gas executives in Calgary. I have served as an expert witness in front of the National Energy Board of Canada. I'm frequently quoted and interviewed in national media including CBC, CNBC, Resource World Magazine, Business in Calgary Magazine, The Korelin Economics Report, HoweStreet.com, 321Energy.com, SeekingAlpha.com, The Energy Report and Minyanville.com. I have spoken at mining and investment conferences around the world.

Jim Letourneau’s Specialties:
