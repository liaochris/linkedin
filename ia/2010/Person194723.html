Sterling Zumbrunn’s Summary

Sterling Zumbrunn’s Specialties:

Award winning underwater photographer. Expert in digital photography and high definition video, with flair for compelling storytelling. Specialist in digital asset management, delivering solutions to complex technological problems involving rich media. Enthusiastic and highly knowledgeable utilizer of Macintosh operating system; excellent trainer of both still photography technique and Macintosh optimization, particularly for photographic purposes.
