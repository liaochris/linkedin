Gary Scanlon Jr’s Summary

I am seeking a mid-to-senior level video game / real-time simulation programming position. My education is in mathematics and real-time simulations, and my experience is in video games. I have worked on several team-driven projects to develop and ship games for PC, PS2, Xbox, Xbox360, Wii, and DS.

I have a strong aptitude for the 3D animation pipeline. I have been responsible for implemeting, updating and maintaining many elements of 3D animation systems, including exporting data from the 3D graphics package, optimizing the output during the build process, importing the build data at startup, processing the information in real-time using matrix manipulation and quaternion interpolation, and providing high-level functionality for game-play programming. I have also written math libraries, UI, collision detection, camera systems, and 3DSMax & Maya/Mel plug-ins.

Before developing games, I taught upper level mathematics for several years. I enjoy the detail and sophistication of object-oriented programming, and I get a great deal of satisfaction in seeing the material I’ve studied come into a virtual life. Being able to simulate an environment by implementing definitions and equations representing mathematical constructs and physical phenomena is quite rewarding.
