  • Graphic Designer

    (Graphic Design industry)

    January 1998 — August 1999 (1 year 8 months)

    Tom Wallerich (and his partner Michel Berard) provided me with a design apprenticeship (a job, really) and a terrific design mentor in himself upon my arrival to California in the rainy winter of 1998.   • Art Director

    (Textiles industry)
