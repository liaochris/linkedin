Dean Tatooles’s Experience

  • Photographer/Owner

    Southern Cross Galleries

    (Photography industry)

    September 2005 — Present (4 years 11 months)

    Dean Tatooles specializes in panoramic landscape and wildlife photography. Dean's talent for photography is evident in each of his striking images. Attributing his inspiration to photographers Peter Lik and David Muench, Dean's passion and dedication to his craft are unsurpassed.

    Dean's travels have taken him across all seven continents. At a young age, however, Dean became fascinated with the topography, culture and wildlife of certain locations in the Southern Hemisphere. In that spirit, Dean created Southern Cross Galleries after the constellation that bears its name and is only visible from below the Equator. Dean's hope is that his work touches that sense of adventure in everyone and that viewers of his photographs will be transported to another time and place, even if its for just a moment.

    Dean works with Nikon digital SLR equipment to capture his spectacular wildlife images and with a Linhof Technorama 617 S III medium format panoramic camera to produce his majestic landscapes.

    Dean's photographic prints are of the highest quality. It is important to remember that images viewed on the Internet pale in comparison to the actual print in person. Subtle details and nuances of color are impossible to convey in a low-resolution web image. The sight of the large, crisply printed image in person will simply take your breath away.

    All of Dean's digital images are shot in RAW format at an extremely high resolution. Those images that are not shot in digital format are created using the highest quality negative or transparency film. After choosing only his best negatives or transparencies for printmaking.

    After carefully processing his digital images or scans, Dean personally creates each individual print in his own lab affording him complete creative control. All prints are of the finest archival materials and created on 100% cotton rag fine art paper suitable for any decor.

    Dean's customers span five continents.

  • Partner

    Tressler LLP

    (Partnership; Law Practice industry)

    September 1999 — Present (10 years 11 months)

    Dean focuses his practice on litigation involving construction, surety and fidelity law, and complex commercial matters. His practice also includes the representation of several municipalities in the Chicagoland area. Dean’s professional philosophy is one of being accessible and responsive to clients, colleagues and peers.

    Over the years, Dean has distinguished himself in numerous mediations, arbitrations and bench trials. In addition to his professional accomplishments, Dean is committed to the legal professional and continuing legal education. He has spoken at many legal seminars and, most recently, Dean was featured as a key note speaker at a Lorman Education Services seminar discussing delay, disruption and extra work claims arising from Illinois construction projects. Dean promotes the legal profession by serving yearly as a professional mentor to local law students and participating as a judge in the Annual Chicago Public School’s Intra-city Mock Trial Competition.
