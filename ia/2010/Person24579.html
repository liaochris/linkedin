Bryan Howland’s Summary

After spending 11 years as a project manager in the publishing industry, I returned to school, where I earned my master’s degree in public relations from Cal State Fullerton and graduated at the top of my class as a member of both Kappa Tau Alpha and Phi Kappa Phi honor societies.

I have a wealth of social media knowledge along with a great deal of traditional public relations experience. I recognize the importance of social media and its impact on public relations and brand identity. I am able to use this knowledge to maximize the presence of Maples’ current and future clients in the emerging digital media world.
