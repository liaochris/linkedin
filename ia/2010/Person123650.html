  • Project Leader, Online Development

    Tower Travel Management

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    October 2006 — April 2010 (3 years 7 months)

    In 2006, Leigh Anne Espinosa was hired to be a new addition to Tower’s growing Online Solutions Team. Leigh Anne started as a helpdesk representative, and over the years has taken on more responsibilities. Currently Leigh Anne serves as Project Leader, Online Development.

    As a helpdesk representative, Leigh Anne would assist with everything from password assistance, creating new profiles, to giving travelers site navigation tips. Understanding what travelers and arrangers are dealing with on a daily basis serves as a strong foundation for what a makes a travel management company’s success grow and what grew Leigh Anne’s interest in bettering the clients’ experience. This led to becoming a certified administrator for three online booking tools: Cliqbook, GetThere, and Rearden Commerce.

    Transitioning into an Online Solutions Specialist, she began implementing new customers’ booking tools. With each implementation comes the responsibility of working with customers’ existing policies and procedures to ensure the configuration exceeds all their goals, and to train and support the end users; ultimately steering high levels of online adoption. This work ethic awarded her team the GetThere Luminary Award for Best Overall Program, three years in a row.

    Now Leigh Anne continues to play an active part in ensuring our customers and travelers continue to be satisfied with their experience, as well as innovating new ways to make their lives easier. As a creative developer of Tower’s Travel Portal, she’s put in place an umbrella of services pertinent to both travel managers and travelers.

    Leigh Anne continues to grow into the future of travel management by attending industry functions, like BCD Affiliates Meetings. Employing this knowledge will continue to keep herself and Tower ahead of the curve.
