Sam Fine’s Summary

• Publicist for Dalai Lama 2008 visit to Ann Arbor, MI including US, Canada and Europe media relations and news distribution
• Integrated marketing/branding specialist
• Created award winning Web animation for Exatec, a joint venture of General Electric Plastics (US) and Bayer Polymers (Germany)
• Managed media projects for Ford, and PR programs for AT&T, A&W Restaurants, Long John Silver’s, Captain D's, Marco's Pizza, and marketing activities for other regional and global franchise systems
• Owned the first advertising agency retained by Domino’s Pizza, Inc.
• Selected the Domino’s Pizza brand name, designed the original logo, developed the corporate identity and earliest series of ad campaigns
• Later, served as Domino’s vice president of marketing (domestic and international) during five years of record growth from $1 billion to $2.7 billion in annual sales, and led marketing in 23 countries
• Planned management information centers for the nation’s fossil energy program, was long-term consultant to the Federal Emergency Management Agency
• Prepared IRS recruitment ad guidelines for all 10 US regions and managed advertising for three IRS regions.
• Opened and co-managed the Washington office of Bozell (& Jacobs)/Public Relations
• In DC, Intermedia was advertising agency for a presidential primary candidate
• Healthcare marketing consultant since 1971
• Helped the Extraterrestrial Research Center present its concept which led to implementing NASA's Apollo Lunar Surface Experiments Program
• Managed program communications and film production for Bendix Aerospace Systems’ government contracted satellite communications, oceanographic and lunar exploration programs
• Was managing art director at a leading technical publications company serving such aerospace firms as Boeing, Convair, Douglas, Grumman and Lockheed.
• In the UK, was technical and advertising art director with Rotax, the aircraft electrical division of the Lucas Group
