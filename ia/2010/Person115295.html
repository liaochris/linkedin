Kevin Brimhall’s Summary

As a believer that leaders are made, not born, and that there is unlimited potential in all of us, it is my goal to help companies and business professionals Strive, Perform, and Achieve...to be the best they can be.

A GREAT referral for me is a business or professional without a clear sense of direction or not satisfied with current levels of performance and results.

I am a results-driven strategic thinker with a proven track record in executing on critical business objectives and making teams and individuals more successful.

 Collaborative planning and execution of critical business initiatives; working with and leading teams to success.
 20 years experience in marketing, sales, business development and analysis, operations, service, quality, product development and management, business planning, profit orientation, contract/deal structuring and negotiations, and project management.
 Proven group and leadership facilitator and business coach
 Consultative approach to assessing client needs and then translating those needs into solutions.
 Experience across a variety of market sectors and verticals, business cycles and market conditions.
