Marilyn Oliva, Marketing Strategist’s Summary

Marilyn is the President of MPower Philosophy and a specialist in marketing and strategy. Marilyn spent more than a decade in senior marketing management positions for large international corporations in the New Jersey and Florida markets prior to launching MPower Philosophy.
Marilyn’s broad marketing experience working for companies such as Citrix Systems, American Express, AT&T, and Research International helped her understand the enormous need for a more analysis focused and strategic approach to marketing. This knowledge led her to develop an empowering approach that provides a combination of marketing assessment, marketing insight, marketing strategy, and marketing consulting appropriate for all types of organizations, regardless of size or budget.
Marilyn holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and a Master of Business Administration degree, both from Rutgers University. She also holds an Associate of Arts degree from the New York School of Interior Design.

Marilyn Oliva, Marketing Strategist’s Specialties:

Marketing Assessment and Recommendation, Marketing Insight/Research Analysis, Marketing Strategy Development, Marketing Consulting on all aspects of marketing, including; agency selection and management, employee/team leadership, resource procurement, marketing metrics and dashboards, budget recommendations, internet marketing (website analysis, SEO and PPC, social media and blogging), mobile and email marketing, general marketing advice.
