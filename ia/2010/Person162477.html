Pete Hwang’s Summary

Multi-disciplinary designer-strategist with a passion for "systems thinking" and HW+SW+Web interaction.

Presently working on web and mobile strategy for HP's Consumer Inkjet business. Swimming upstream, evangelizing the importance of openness and community in a HW-centric business.
