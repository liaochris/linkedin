Alisa Guralnick’s Summary

As a human resource professional and consultant, I have helped self-funded and venture-backed technology startups, as well as Fortune 500 telecommunications and Internet industry leaders, through all levels of development. I have worked with companies in high tech, biotech, financial services, construction and non profit.

My areas of expertise include legal compliance, technical and professional staffing, compensation design, performance management, manager coaching and training, payroll and benefits design/administration, and HR software systems.

I am certified by the Human Resources Certification Institute as a Professional in Human Resources (PHR). I have a Professional Certificate in HR Management from the University of California, San Diego, and completed the inaugural session of their HR Leadership Program. I hold a Bachelor's degree in English from the University of Rochester (NY). Additionally, I am an exam question (item) writer and reviewer for HR Certification Institute's California Human Resources Certification exam. I was recently selected "Member of the Year 2008" for the San Diego Recruiters' Roundtable (www.sdrecruiters.com).

Alisa Guralnick’s Specialties:

Organizational change, compensation and benefits, employee relations, training and development, wage and hour law, multi state business operations, recruitment and staffing.
