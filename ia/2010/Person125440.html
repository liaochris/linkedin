Lindsay Kaye’s Summary

I have my BA in Psychology and my minor in Human Sexuality from California State University at Northridge. Currently, I am completing my Masters of Science in Family Studies and a member of the American Association of Family and Consumer Sciences. www.aafcs.org

My work experience includes educating in a variety of subjects such as special populations about the prevention and awareness of HIV/AIDS, STD transmission. My outreach has also included working closely with other non-profit agencies to have clients tested for HIV and STD's. Counseling, advocacy and referrals were done for clients who needed treatment beyond the scope of services offered at the Greater Los Angeles Agency on Deafness. I have conducted research on a joint project with the Greater Los Angeles Agency on Deafness and University of California Los Angeles on Deaf and hard of hearing women about their knowledge of Breast Cancer. I spearheaded several committees that focused on providing community events for Los Angele's Deaf population . I have cared for and educated children and young adults ranging from infancy to 25 . I subcontracted for the Los Angeles County Department of Health's HIV Epidemiology program and abstracted information from thousands of medical records and worked closely with the Centers of Disease Control in Atlanta, Georgia.
