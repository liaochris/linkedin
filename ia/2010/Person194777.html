Steve Baldzicki’s Summary

Steve Baldzicki is the Founder and President of Big Fish Networking (Central Ohio's Premier Networking Event since 1999). He is also one of the founding members of NLA (Networking Leaders Alliance) an organization formed in 2008 to bring together the top networking organizations in Central Ohio to support each other by sharing information, supporting each other's events, sharing resources, and growing attendance/membership. He is also a Principal of Monarch Title Services and has over 11 years experience in the real-estate field. Steve develops and executes effective sales and marketing strategies to create business nationwide for residential and commercial title insurance. He is profiled in two books; Social Capital (Author: Andy Chiodo) and Foundational Networking (Author: Frank Agin) as being recognized as an expert in the networking field. He was recognized as Entrepreneur of the month for April 2003 & October 2007 in Business First/Columbus. Steve has also been regionally and nationally publicized for his efforts with Big Fish Networking and its growth over the past 10 years. Currently ranked #1 for “Columbus Networking” in Google since 2007. Steve also serves on several boards, committees, and councils within the Central Ohio area, because of his skill as one of the premier networkers in the area. A frequent speaker at many networking organizations, companies, local colleges and universities, he also consults on event development and management, as well as marketing and sales strategy with Central Ohio area businesses
