Rajeev Mohindra’s Summary

Hands-on technology and products executive with significant track record in developing and deploying best-in-class enterprise and consumer mobile applications globally on all major handset platforms and carriers. Strategic thinker with an in-depth understanding of mobile industry and enterprise mobility.

Extensive experience in building highly scalable J2EE transactional enterprise systems that facilitate business processes that integrate data from multiple enterprise systems like ERP (SAP), SFA (Siebel), Web services, custom enterprise stores (databases, files, intranet) etc. Experience in developing SaaS Sales Force Automation and Field Service applications and in developing the full Business Intelligence Stack, from ETL to Data marts to Reporting and Analysis.

Entrepreneur with a track record of founding and leading a successful venture capital backed company for seven years. Extensive global professional services experience having created and managed the P&L of fifty person professional services team that built custom applications.

Innovation and thought leader with a successful track record of delivering five new (version 1.0) products from concept to product launch at four different companies. Exceptional in execution and in driving continuous software process efficiencies to deliver consistent on-time, on-budget, on-scope delivery of high quality products and services.

Rajeev Mohindra’s Specialties:

Mobile Application development on all leading smart phone platforms (Android, Windows Mobile, Symbian, Palm OS, iPhone), Security and Management of enterprise mobile deployments, Highly scalable SaaS multitenant Web applications, Social Networking, Web 2.0 application development, SFA (Siebel, Salesforce.com), ERP (SAP), Database Kernel, OS internals, Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence, Replication and Synchronization Servers, Agile & SCRUM development methodologies
