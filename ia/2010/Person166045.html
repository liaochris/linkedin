  • Artist and Business Owner

    Entwine Jewelry

    (Fine Art industry)

    September 2005 — Present (5 years )

    The basis for the name "Entwine" is inspired by the structuring techniques traditionally found in woven, knit, and crocheted textiles and basketry. I use very fine gauge wire of silver or gold to hand fabricate each piece. Designs often start from a sketch and a palette of beautiful pearls and faceted gemstones. With a vision in mind, I string each bead onto the wire spool and then "entwine" them into each piece. I love to work in an organic way, watching the shapes and textures evolve naturally.
