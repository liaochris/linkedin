Miguel Toribio-Mateas’s Summary

I'm a Nutritional Therapy Practitioner with a Functional Medicine systems approach. I'm also a Training and Research Consultant with over 12 years of business experience in the Online Learning and Information Management industry, 6 of which I dedicated to designing and running training courses for the medical, academic and scientific community throughout Europe and the UK. Originally Spanish but living in London from 1994, I'm fluent in 4 European languages.

I run two busy clinics in London (Harley Street and Belsize Park) where my specialist areas include inflammatory conditions, skin disorders and gastrointestinal and hormonal imbalances. At my Harley Street clinic I work in collaboration with the London Sleep Centre, following an integrative clinical approach to managing sleep disorders. I also work at nutrition-based retreats overseas.

I combine my clinical practice with an Academic Tutor / Lecturer role at the Institute for Optimum Nutrition, where I am currently leading a research project on Chronic Fatigue Syndrome and mitochondrial integrity. Other of my research interests include oxidative cellular damage and the improvement of vital cellular energy, which is essential for biotransformation and detoxification processes.

As a Director of BANT (British Association for Applied Nutrition and Nutritional Therapy), a member of BANT Council and Chair of the Membership Committee, I aim to attain the highest standards of integrity, knowledge, competence and professionalism in the practice of Nutritional Therapy.

Outside my academic and clinical commitments, I provide training and research support to academics and clinicians across Europe through my ongoing collaboration with scientific publisher Springer and offers consultancy services to the nutritional supplements industry in the UK and Spain.

Please don't hesitate to get in touch. I shall be happy to help in any way I can.
