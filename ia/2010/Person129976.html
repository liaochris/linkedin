Meg Russell’s Summary

I have spent most of my career to date as a portfolio accountant and small business consultant involving, but not limited to, assessing various real estate businesses and investment portfolios. Most recently, I have opened a real estate business of my own that involves consulting in the design of new Energy Star homes under contract, or green retrofitting or renovation of existing homes (resales) under Home Star or other Re-Greening programs with an emphasis on representing and protecting buyers. I am a REALTOR, NAR Green Certified Broker, an Ecobroker (an internationally recognized green designation), an Accredited Buyer Representative and luxury home specialist. I am also the Treasurer and design coordinator for Cedarbrook Construction, an Energy Star builder. I have recently been elected to the Board of Directors of Chix in Business, a registered 501(c) non-profit that builds confidence and leadership in women and supports less fortunate families and children in our community.
