Mark Evans’s Summary

For executives and investors, managers of Fortune 500 companies and other clients requiring expert assistance with competitive research in the financial services and high-tech industries, Mark Evans brings a wealth of experience.

For more than 12 years, he has earned a reputation for providing skilled and practical guidance as a market researcher, analyst, consultant, and entrepreneur.

Mark is the founder and principal of Bancroft Group, LLC -- a market intelligence firm.

Bancroft was founded in 2005 to help executives and business teams track their competitive, industry and market landscapes through the delivery of customized market intelligence reports (Dossiers) to clients as diverse as the tech giant HP, aerospace and defense company, ATK and to consumer product companies like Xango.

He is also currently serving as President of the San Francisco American Marketing Association.

Prior to starting his own research firm, Mark was director of sales and business analysis for the consulting firm, Business Design Corp.(BDC), specializing in developing and implementing new growth strategies for both BDC and its clients.
