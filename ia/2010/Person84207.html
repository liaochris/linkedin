Israel Y. Ganot’s Summary

Israel Ganot was appointed to the position of President and CEO of Gazelle in November 2008. Under Israel’s leadership, Gazelle has emerged as the nation’s largest and fastest growing place for consumers to responsibly trade-in gadgets for cash, with more than 100% growth during the past 6 months. Israel has established Gazelle’s leadership position by focusing on building a world-class brand, technology, and customer experience that so far has attracted more than 50,000 consumers. For his accomplishments at Gazelle, Israel has been featured by top national media and Ernst & Young named him as a 2009 finalist for their Entrepreneur of the Year award. Prior to Gazelle, Israel held senior positions at eBay, PayPal, and Amazon. He earned his BS at NYU’s Stern School and his MBA from Harvard.
