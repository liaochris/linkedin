Igor Anatsko’s Summary

Gnxpert Color Inc. scientifically formulates new classes of glowing and reflection enhancing additives for industrial uses, such as: paints and coatings; injection moulded plastics; vinyl; inks; leather; textiles. Cosmetics, automotive, marine industries, and safety applications can benefit greatly due to the imparting of properties of enhanced aesthetics, impact resistance, durability, luminescence and reflectivity.

After 14 years of research in lasers, optics, visual psychology, nano & neuro technologies, electronics, computer software and a number of other critical disciplines, we continue to develop and refine our 3D revolutionary holographic flat panel piezo screen which displays real 3D images in motion. Gnxpert Holographic 3D Display Technology does not require glasses, head- tracking gear or viewing aids of any kind. These are real 3D images that jump off the screen.

GNxpert Neural Technologies develops and markets artificial intelligence analytic software applications based on neural network and genetic algorithms. We are focusing on graphic applications such as image recognition, movement prediction, restoration to a full 3D object from a flat 2D image, common television signal, 2D computer operational system and other 2D display units will also benefit from our 3D Neural Conversion Process.

GNXtech is developing a Laser Device for high-speed communication systems for transmitting holographic bandwidth information. Photonic devices (semiconductor lasers, photonic band-pass filtering, modulators and detectors) are integrated by design into one device ready for integration into optical fiber networks. GNXtech Device is similar to an IC Chip and can replace most current electronic applications of this type, our device having improved response times, bandwidth and reduced power consumption.
