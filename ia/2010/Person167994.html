Raja Raman’s Summary

Raja has over 15 years of Business Development experience, most of it in the US market. In the past 11 years, won several IT-enabled Services contracts from Fortune 500 companies like Ford, Pratt & Whitney, Cameron, Flowserve, Baker Hughes, Praxair, Dover Corp, Danaher, among others. Currently Raja is based out of Chennai-India.

Founded an Engineering Services Outsourcing company into the Oil & Gas and Engineered Products industry with facilities in Bangalore. Sold the company to the UCA Group in June 2009.
