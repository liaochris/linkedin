Saqib Khan’s Summary

My ultimate goal is to elevate a brand, any brand - to the pinnacle where it resonates with the heartstrings of the consumer, that is, it gets firmly entrenched in the mind of the consumer.

And if I get paid to do it, that would be the icing on the cake. That’s how I view my career in brand management.

Most ‘brandsmen’ relax at home after work. I watch documentaries about branding, Freudian and Edwardian Psychology. Most brandsmen spend the weekend sleeping in and relaxing. I spend the holiday formulating branding strategies for brands I’ve observed and experienced as a consumer but other than that, have no business poking my nose into, other than my passion.

However, I do realize the importance of a ‘can do’ approach which I strive to inculcate in my professional repertoire on a day-to-day basis. It’s this attitude that has enabled me to take on added responsibilities in addition to brand management at Atlas Battery. For instance I’m acting as the account manager of major customers for ABL including such popular brands as Toyota, Honda, Suzuki and Land Rover; streamlining the supply chain operations as well as customer service.
