Matthew Harmon, CCIM’s Summary

Matthew Harmon is the President of HP Property Group, Inc. and holds a California General Building Contractors license, ULI (Urban Land Institute) Development certificate along with his CCIM designation.

Matthew E. Harmon has been primarily involved with land development, property management, marketing, and construction including new construction, renovations, repairs and maintenance services, since 1999. He has gained expertise in construction management and corporate affairs under the direction of his father, Martin A. Harmon; a leader in California’s convalescent hospital business and long-term care industry for over forty years.

Matthew Harmon has been heavily involved with the, coordination, and execution of several projects. Recently completed projects include Montano De El Dorado an upscale retail shopping center in El Dorado Hills, California. Where he’s worked on the brokerage and property management since its completion in 2007. Under Western Care Construction he focused primarily on constructing a new 43,000 square foot skilled nursing facility in Riverside, California.

Matthew Harmon, CCIM’s Specialties:

Retail and senior care land development, Brokerage, and Property Management.
