Gregory Urroz, CRS, GRI, ABR’s Summary

I started in Real Estate as an Investor. After several years of owning and managing my own Real Estate portfolio, I obtained my Real Estate License in Arizona. Currently, I am a Certified Residential Specialist (CRS), (less than 4% of the Realtors in the United States have earned this prestigious designation). In addition, I am a Graduate of the Realtor Institute (GRI) and an Accredited Buyers Representative (ABR). As a veteran Realtor, I have closed hundreds of transactions including condos, town homes and single family homes including horse properties and active adult community properties. I have also closed a variety of commercial properties. I service all of Maricopa and Pinal Counties. Some of the major cities include Phoenix, Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Peoria, Glendale, Surprise, Casa Grande, Florence, Queen Creek and Apache Junction.

I love paying and receiving referral fees with other licensed Brokers. If you are a licensed Broker or Agent and know of someone looking to relocate to the Valley Of The Sun, give me a call.

As an unpaid volunteer member of Pinal County Sheriff Search and Rescue Posse, I have a history of putting the interest of others before my own. I love helping people often at great personal sacrifice. If you would like to join me on a search, let’s start searching for your new home. I am an expert in navigating mountain passes and the Multiple Listing Service. I would like to make your home search my next adventure. For more information go to http://www.GregUrroz.com
