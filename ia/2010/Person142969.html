Michael P. Lazarus’s Summary

Digital Media Executive & President of Latin Pulse Music Inc.

Latin Pulse Music Inc. is an internet-based, publishing and technology company that owns and operates a network of latin music & culture websites. Revenue sources include on-line retail sales of digital content, advertising, audio mastering & mixing services as well plus music publishing, song placement & licensing.

The network includes various popular portals with a broad international base of visitors, Timba.com for example, that is supported via digital content sales and delivery provided by Latinpuslemusic.com. LPM can interface with aggregators, labels and artists alike.

Our core sales techology is provided by the Latinpulsemusic.com website, a proprietary web app that handles sales of all types of digital content: album downloads, videos, eBooks, sheet music and production tracks. Divisions of LPM include a record label and licensing arm that supplies music for TV & Films.

Michael P. Lazarus’s Specialties:

Latino / Hispanic focused Digital Media Development, Distribution & Sales, digital media ecommerce, website development and infrastructure management, internet promotion, content creation, music production, audio mastering
