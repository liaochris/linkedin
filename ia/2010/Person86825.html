Jack Tillman’s Summary

Junction Consulting provides access to human-resources SaaS applications as well as strategic business consulting to small and mid-sized companies that market services and intangibles in the business to business sector. We specialize in growth and expansion initiatives that help maximize sales, customer retention and team productivity. Jack's particular focus is working with companies to implement Lean business processes throughout their end-to-end sales cycle in order to increase close ratios while decreasing the cost per sale. Our tools can also help companies increase their customer base as well as customer satisfaction with their products or services.

Jack Tillman's background includes business-to-business sales and marketing management with P&L responsibility, strategic and marketing planning, development of structured sales methodologies, business process improvement, market development, and, product development in the health insurer, managed care, health systems and Third Party Administrator industries.

Jack is recognized for his team building and leadership skills as well as an outstanding written and verbal communications skill set. He is often considered the “go to” person for difficult or complicated projects.
