Kevin Lynch’s Summary

In my first 10+ years in advertising, I built an award-winning portfolio as a copywriter and creative director at highly regarded agencies such as McConnaughy Stein Schmidt Brown, Chicago; Roche Macaulay, Toronto; and DDB Chicago, on top marketers such as Jeep, Reebok, Corona, BMW, Walgreens, IKEA, Anheuser-Busch, and Circuit City, to name a few.

In 2001, I co-founded Chicago creative boutique Hadrian’s Wall (later sold to MDC Partners to become the US office of zig). Our simple goal was to “do good work for people you like.” And we did, for people such as Harvard Business Review, National Geographic Channel, Elysian Hotels & Resorts, the Illinois Department of Public Health, Hewitt, Stir Crazy, DePaul University, ethel's Chocolate Lounge, MagneCote, Microsoft, and Playboy, among others.

In 2009, I launched 15 ideas, a dumping ground for nefarious side projects and small client engagements. Today, I am at Proximity Chicago, energyBBDO's digitally-minded sibling, working on brands like Bayer, Hyatt, and Illinois Lottery.
