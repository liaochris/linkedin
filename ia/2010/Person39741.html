Chris Stanton’s Summary

Mr. Stanton has built sales organization from scratch; has re-engineered poor performing sales units to top performers; and has profitably increased revenue and margin targets at the division level of major corporations.

Mr. Stanton began his career at Unisys Corporation (formerly Burrough’s Corporation). He was promoted rapidly and became the youngest zone manager in the company history in recognition of his excellent sales, revenue growth and new account acquisition success.

While at Memorex Telex (MTC) Mr. Stanton held the post of Director of Sales and was instrumental in the formation of the automated tape library business unit. Mr. Stanton left MTC in 1994 to become VP of Sales for Gandalf Systems, a telecommunication company specializing in wide area network and remote access solutions.

In 1995 Mr. Stanton was recruited to EMC Corporation, then a 1.3 billion dollar company.He led a district that had revenue of $44MM and developed a region responsible for $410MM over a period of four years. This dramatic increase in revenue was equally balanced with a growth in margin from 44% to 68%. Mr. Stanton’s sales management career is both extensive and very successful which resulted in his promotion to direct a $840MM dollar Division for EMC.

In 2003 Mr. Stanton joined Avamar Technologies,. Avamar was introducing a new product into an emerging market which provides efficiencies in the backup and recovery arena, Mr. Stanton led Avamar’s rapid growth an during 2003 and 2004 Mr. Stanton recruited, hired and developed sales organizations to support both a direct and an indirect go to market model.
In 2007 Mr. Stanton joined Sepaton Inc, a De duplication and virtualized data protection platform and helped Sepaton achieve it’s current market position as a dynamic growth company in unquestionably IT’s game changing technology.
