Howard Fitch’s Summary

HUB International Personal Insurance, a dedicated practice within HUB, offers one of the largest and most sophisticated personal insurance practices working with high net worth families today. We bring a wealth of experience to individuals and families of means and the business partners who serve them. Our national network of offices allows us to provide clients with both expert guidance and the convenience of local service. HUB is licensed in all 50 states and the territories and provinces of Canada with access to more than 100 insurance carriers and intermediaries. We offer creative, comprehensive and cost-effective solution. We understand and can provide solutions to a broad range of coverage needs for homes, automobiles, valuable possessions, and personal liability or specialty products such as yachts, equine, aircraft and other interests.
