David Shar’s Summary

Open Source Innovations Ltd (OSIL) is an international organization with a presence in Australia, Singapore, UK and USA. OSIL was formed in 2008-09 and plan to enter the Indian market in 2009-10, since then we have been making strides into the Indian Education market. The directors of OSIL, David Shar, Justin Hills and Christopher Catlin have a firm vision, which is to advance Open Source in India and to create prosperity for all involved, this vision is in the spirit of open source, by making OSIL ventures about the community and not just the individual or organizations.

Open Source Innovations Ltd inroads into the Education market have been made by forging partnerships with Universities & Colleges, our goal is to introduce a vocational GNU-Linux Syllabus into these education institutes which will be a great benefit for students once they enter the workforce. We have put together a comprehensive library of resources for educating students in the ways of Open Source. The skills that the students gain will enable them to develop applications for the open source market and would make the student more employable to a lot of employers.

Our Business model currently designed to derive revenue from training of people & utilizing the skills learned by students to develop open source applications for various business segments, while this business model does have multiple revenue streams we are always open to suggestions as to how we can improve our business revenue model.
