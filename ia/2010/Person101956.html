John Callan’s Summary

John Callan is Founder and Mission Commander of SpaceHelpWanted.com, the premier Florida-based destination for employers and job candidates in the New Space Industry.

John has extensive international leadership experience recruiting and managing technical and engineering teams in US, Eastern Europe, China, India and Latin America.

As World News editor at MSNBC and global leader of Microsoft Sales support outside the US, John implemented technology upgrade and training programs, business improvement programs and career development plans which improved service quality, employee morale, and the respect of workers for each other.

John has lived and worked in the US, the UK and Hungary, and done business in more than 25 countries. He is currently based in Melbourne, Florida, in the heart of Florida's Space Coast.

John Callan’s Specialties:

Technical recruiting, International business management, global delivery, nearsourcing, media startups.
