Dr. Natalie Petouhoff’s Summary

Dr. Nat guides CRM, Customer Service, Social Media professionals as well as C-level executives to develop a business strategy formulated to incorporate the most important asset of a company --their customers. Dr. Nat is also a highly sought after TV and radio segment commenator.

Dr. Nat is quoted in NYTimes, USAToday, BusinessWeek, CRM Magazine and Peppers and Rogers 1-to-1 Magazine.

An accomplished public speaker, Dr. Nat is an invited keynote at: Enterprise 2.0, WOMMA, Forrester IT, Business Technology and Consumer Forums, Gartner CRM Summit, CRM Evolution, ICMI, Pepperdine Business Forum, Vendor Users Conferences, IQPC, GSA and SOCAP.

Dr. Nat's practical thought leadership helps companies gain a clear vision of their customers today, taking them into the future, to understand the changing needs and the bottom-line value of acquiring and retaining loyal customers to improve revenue and profits in a competitive, social marketplace.

Her research focuses on helping organizations establish and validate strategies; build executive consensus; facilitate vendor selection; and plan for project success by integrating Change Management as part of the project management schedule and plan.

Dr. Nat has extensive industry experience; over 15 years of leadership at: Hitachi Consulting, PricewaterhouseCoopers and BenchmarkPortal. Her years of practical industry experience were gained at companies like General Motors, General Electric and Hughes Electronics.

Dr. Nat is the author of four books:
• Reinventing Your Contact Center: Managers Guide To Managing Multi-Channel Contact Centers
• Integrating Your People with Process and CRM Technology: Change Management That Provides An ROI
• CRM: The Bottom Line to Optimizing Your ROI and
• Recruiting and Retaining Call Center Employees.

Dr. Nat obtained a Ph. D. in Engineering from UCLA and a M.S. and B. S. in engineering from University of Michigan.
