Stanley Alpert’s Summary

Former Chief environmental civil and criminal prosecutor with substantive environmental experience, investigations, and team management skills built at the U.S. Attorney’s Office and in private practice. Commercial and environmental litigator with dynamic New York firm. Planning and contract assistance to green startup companies. Speaker to environmental organizations on cleanup and sustainability issues involving air, land, water and products liability. Spoke at Wal Mart's Green Expo in Bentonville on sustainability. Motivational public speaker regarding life-changing near-death experience chronicled in The Birthday Party: A Memoir of Survival. Media appearances covering the memoir and environmental issues, including CNN and many radio shows.
