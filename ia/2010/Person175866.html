Rob Murray’s Summary

19 years of commercial electrical design experience.

20 years experience with AutoCAD v10 through 2008.

11 years design experience in larger multi-million dollar commercial projects.

Skillful application of ASHRAE/IES 90.1-1999-2007 utilizing proven, cost effective, engineering design practices.

My goal for every project I work on is to eliminate RFI's, change orders and additional post-bid costs to the Owner.

My passion is life safety and emergency egress solutions that are cost effective and safeguard the general public.

  • Senior Electrical Designer

    Webb Engineering Services, Inc.

    (Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    I have completed the electrical design for the new Mississippi River Bridge (http://www.newriverbridge.org/) with HNTB Kansas City and MoDOT. The bridge is scheduled to be open to traffic on January 1st, 2014 and will provide a vital link of highway I-70 in Missouri and Illinois.

    Design included electrical systems for the Missouri Approach, Main Span and Illinois Approach. This includes the design and utility coordination of the 13.8kV utility service entrance switchgear and power distribution to the bridge, layout and design of 1.25 miles of roadway lighting and controls, design of a lightning protection system for twin 406'-0" tall cable stay towers, river navigation lighting and controls, aviation obstruction lighting and controls, bridge maintenance systems and power and fiber connections to the MoDOT/IDOT Intelligent Transportation System.

    I have also completed a 5kV switchgear replacement for the Missouri Botanical Gardens, an Emergency Services (ER) renovation at the University of Missouri-Columbia and a renovation of historic Spink Pavilion for the Missouri Botanical Gardens.

    Currently I am working on ARRA streetscape and pedestrian street lighting upgrades for parts of Manchester Avenue and Vandeventer Avenue. I am also starting a lighting replacement and upgrade to the four outdoor racquet ball courts in Forest Park.
