  • Founder

    Better Futures Film Festival

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    2010 — Present (less than a year)

    The Better Futures Film Festival (“BFF Fest”) is a sustainability film festival that will engage filmgoers as not just passive spectators but, rather, empower them as active participants—-in collaboratively reframing the issues, reimagining the future, and generating solutions. Using the latest strategic foresight methodologies, such as learning journeys, change labs, and the World Café conversational process, the BFF Fest will bring participants together to collaboratively explore present and future realities, crystallize insights, and put new strategies and commitments into practice.

  • Strategic Foresight Practitioner

    Adaptive Edge

    (Management Consulting industry)

    November 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Adaptive Edge's consulting engagements enable powerful insights into the future. We help leaders see new possibilities and understand future probabilities, so they can make better decisions today. We enable leaders to be more adaptive and resilient in the face of change. Using a range of strategic foresight methods--from scenario planning to change labs--our clients learn to:

    * Transform their strategic thinking, beyond the conventional wisdom.
    * Improve their decision-making, and ability to navigate future uncertainties.
    * Embed these adaptive skills, tools, and mindsets within their organization.
    * Engage their stakeholders in a strategic dialogue about the future.
    * Act with greater courage, confidence, and creativity.
    * Create better alternatives for the future - and more sustainable ways to create value.

    My current projects at Adaptive Edge include working on the future of the health care safety net in Detroit as well as implication of climate change for Bay Area businesses, municipalities, nonprofits, and communities over the next 40 years.
