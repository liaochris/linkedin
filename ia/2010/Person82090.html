A San Francisco & Silicon Valley institution for 25+ years, Heller Immigration Law Group, is one of the premiere immigration law firms headquartered in Silicon Valley (Menlo Park), California. We obtain immigration and green card status for corporate, business & family based clients. Our clients include: multinationals, to large and small corporations, to Silicon Valley startups, outstanding and pre eminent scientists, post-docs and other researchers and academics, many of whom often wish to self-petition. We also handle family immigration, i.e., marriage, VAWA, and fiancé/e cases, as well as I-751 petitions to remove conditional resident status.
