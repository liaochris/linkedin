Bob Danziger’s Summary

Thirty-five years experience in alternative energy and environmental technology, crafting creative and practical solutions. Served as consultant, CEO, inventor, advisor and in project manager roles. Technology, project finance and scale issues are areas where my unique talents and experience have fostered important alternative energy and environmental technologies.

Bob Danziger’s Specialties:

• Due diligence
• Invent, research, develop, demonstrate, commercialize, make, sell, service and guarantee new energy and environmental technology
• Design, build, finance, own and operate pioneering alternative energy/independent power projects
• Energy price forecasting and policy analysis
• Scale
• Leading multi-disciplinary teams
• Ultra-low emissions

  • Founding CEO, Advisor

    Goal Line Environmental Technology

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    1991 — 2009 (18 years )

    Goal Line Environmental was formed to market technologies originally developed for use at cogeneration power plants owned and operated by Sunlaw. Goal Line went from a start-up to counting 80 of the top 100 Power Producers, all of the major Boiler Manufacturers, as well as all of the major engine manufacturers as their clients.

    Goal Line sold a full range of catalysts for natural gas-fired power plants, engines and industrial facilities. Among these are SCONOx (EM-x), which was co-invented by Bob and has the distinction of being the catalyst system on the cleanest fossil fuel power plants ever built.
