Lonnie Anderson, AIA, LEED AP’s Summary

My experience as an Architect in the design-build environment, new construction, remodeling projects, specifications and a wide variety of special projects, including a super-insulated passive solar home, would integrate well with needs in the greening of Architecture. I also have experience with hotels, corporate office design, as well as multi-family residential, retail and various project management experiences in recreational (themed) projects and renovation. I have DataCAD, Sketchup, MicroStation, AutoCAD, Excel, and Word experience and have managed drawing and project databases. I am currently learning to implement BIM, IPD and registered for LEED Certification.

Lonnie Anderson, AIA, LEED AP’s Specialties:

My background also includes passive solar, PV, super-insulation design and construction techniques. I’ve assisted in many projects documenting heat loss, air or water infiltration and the correct remediation necessary.
