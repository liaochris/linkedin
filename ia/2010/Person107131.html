Jamie Schwartz’s Summary

Currently, the President and COO of the Haley Stuart Group of Companies, a specialized executive search and staffing firm. HSG operates a few distinct brands in select verticals/ functions, including: legal (attorney and legal support), specialty retail (corporate positions) and information technology.

Also a principal in Mattson Resources, an affiliated Orange County, CA staffing firm supplying professional (accounting & finance, engineering) and commercial (clerical & light industrial) staffing services, as well as Stevens Legal Staffing, a premiere provider of legal staffing solutions to the SoCal market.

Prior to serving in current executive/owner role, served as EVP & COO of a $350 million publicly-held staffing company.
