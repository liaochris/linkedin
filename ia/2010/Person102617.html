John McKimm’s Summary

John is a senior, bottom-line focused executive with extensive experience investing and providing advisory services to companies in the public and private sector. He has over 30 years experience across industries including telecom, distribution, manufacturing, waste management, computer hardware and software, financial services, petrochemical, food processing, mining, and oil and gas.

John has successfully assisted and been associated with many companies over the past 20 years with executive level support, financing and more.

John McKimm’s Specialties:

Financing, Strategic and Financial Re-Structuring, Mergers and Acquisitions, Emerging Company Strategies
