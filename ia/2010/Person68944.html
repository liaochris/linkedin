Family RTC’s Summary

This page is dedicated to the wonderful women of the first ever "family & parenting" co-op with RTC Publicity. This page is meant for linked-in contacts and media to follow the progress of this project and this amazing group of NYC women entrepreneurs. This idea has been stirring in Rebecca Crowley's head for quite some time. That "idea" became a reality Wednesday, February 17th when the 5 business owners below signed with RTC to do a huge collaboration: the family & parenting co-op.

  • Owner, Executive Director

    Child's Play NY

    (Primary/Secondary Education industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Child's Play NY offers young people the opportunity to explore and perform theater and in doing so builds confidence, compassion and creativity.

    In young kids classes (ages 4-6), students interpret characters, tell stories and connect with their intuitive ability to make-believe. Childrens literature provides the springboard for their own adventures and on the way they learn about acting, voice and movement. Visual arts, mask, puppetry and costume augment the workshop and support imagination.

    For older kids (ages 7-10) Child’s Play NY offers an enriching and lively array of summer intensives. The unique focus of each weekly session allows for a concentrated immersion in various specialties: Campers can sample what inspires them the most. “Acting for the Camera”, “Let’s Make a Musical” and “Shakespeare Players” are just some of the exciting focuses. Guest teachers are specialists in the field of study and each week culminates in a performance where campers get to share all that they have accomplished.

    Jocelyn Greene, founder and executive director of Child's Play NY is an actress, director and educator. She has an MFA in Acting from NYU's Tisch School and currently teaches and directs theater at Packer Collegiate Institute and the Berkeley Carroll School. She believes that a child’s innate ability to make-believe is the seed from which fine acting and creativity is born.

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Wall Street isn’t just for grown ups, it’s also a great place to bring the kids! Our kid-friendly walking tours are educational and entertaining, perfect for school field trips and family outings.

    We know a walking tour is most successful when it is fun and age-appropriate. So we tailor our tours to the age level and specific interests of your group and provide an enjoyable but structured environment that stimulates and educates minds young and old.

    Annaline Dinkelmann founded Wall Street Walks after spending almost a decade at Morgan Stanley. She offers original insights into all aspects of Wall Street, past and present.
