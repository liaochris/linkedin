Jonathan Kantor’s Summary

In today's hyper-competitive business environment, executive decision makers are looking for solid, accurate business information – minus marketing fluff.

With more than 26 years in the technology sector, I am the principal of The Appum Group, "The White Paper Company", specializes in the development of professional white papers for companies of all sizes and across all industries.

Since 2000, I have produced over 300 white papers for established enterprise organizations such as Microsoft, Intel, Adobe, Lexmark, Proctor & Gamble, and Juniper Networks, as well as a host of SMB organizations across many business sectors.

I am also the author of the book: Crafting White Paper 2.0: Designing Information for Today's Time and Attention Challenged Business Reader. It provides white paper marketers with a fresh set of idea to engage today's Social Media business user.

My blog is entitled: White Paper Pundit which is followed by thousands of white paper writers and business marketers every day. You can read my blog at: www.whitepaperpundit.com.

You can also follow me via Twitter at: Jonathan_Kantor

Jonathan Kantor’s Specialties:
