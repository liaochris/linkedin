Gregory Bowers’s Summary

Raised in an artist's household to appreciate colour and form, genetically predisposed to analytical dissection and the occasional jaunt into overt verbosity, Greg finds joy in contrast—such as understanding that even the most beautifully simple things have an underlying complexity. Finding a good designer is hard enough; finding one with both a grasp on the elements of design and style and a solid base in the technical spectrum is something else entirely.
Designing since childhood—and more specifically, the web since 1998—Greg has undertaken projects ranging from web to print to illustration to apparel, with the occasional excursion into writing, audio production, and even being on air "talent." Let's use that term loosely...

Gregory Bowers’s Specialties:

layout, usability design and testing, illustration, designing for web, user experience, optimisation, user interface design, web copywriting, xhtml, css, IA, blogs, social networks
