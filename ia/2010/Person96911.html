Jessica Garcia’s Summary

I do marketing and PR for Puccini Group. We create destination restaurants for the world’s great hotels.

Lucky us. We create what we love most – beautiful places to eat, drink and be social. From concept through operations, Puccini Group designs and manages popular restaurants and social spaces that add value to hotels and resorts worldwide.

Check out our website to see what we've done and what we have on the boards: www.puccinigroup.com

I manage our social media program. Check us out on Twitter http://twitter.com/PucciniGroup & Facebook http://www.facebook.com/pages/Puccini-Group/57478460151

Our original concept for Livingston Restaurant + Bar in Atlanta recently was named "Best New Restaurant in Atlanta' by Jezebel magazine. We designed + operate it.

In San Francisco, we also did the same for Ducca which Michael Bauer has favorably reviewed twice and which won the Conde Nast Top 100 Hot Tables in the World and Top 10 Italian Restaurants in SF from Gourmet.

Before The Puccini group, I worked as an interior designer at Fee Munson Ebert Architecture + Design. I worked on a combination of commercial & retail projects. While there, I learned how to specify FF&E, produce accurate construction documents in CAD, and handle the stress of taking a project from start to finish with grace and style.

Previously, I worked at a high end furniture manufacturer, McGuire. I worked in sales + marketing. I helped select furniture for show houses with designers, fabric and finish application, transit time/logistics for products, photo shoots, and how to launch a product from start to finish.

I also did some custom furniture design work while at McGuire. I was given rough sketches from our head of product development and worked with the factory to create accurate and detailed drawings of newly designed furniture which later went into production. We did installations in five star resorts and some of the world’s finest homes.
