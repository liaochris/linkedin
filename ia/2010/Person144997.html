Mike Anderson’s Summary

Entrepreneurial executive with over 17 years of business and product development, venture capital, finance, operations and technology experience in large corporate and early-stage, rapid growth companies. Hands-on experience and significant network of contacts across a broad range of industries including: telecom (wireless and land-line), banking & financial services, venture capital, software and media industries.

A long-time member of the Alliance of Angels, the largest angel investor organization in the Pacific Northwest and served on the screening committee for five years (2003-2008).

Some of my personal engagements and investments include: Delve Networks (acq Limelight), Affinity Global Services (Mozido), GotVoice (acq. Spoken), HomeTeam, Global Networks, American Challenge, Terabeam (acq YDI Wireless), Match.com (acq IAC), and Convergence Corp (acq. Amazon.com).
