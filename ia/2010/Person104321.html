Jon Guidroz’s Summary

Free Flow Power Corporation was founded to produce clean, reliable, cost-efficient, renewable energy by harnessing the power of moving water without building new dams or diversions (hydrokinetic generation). Our proprietary technology employs a fully submersible turbine generator is designed to extract energy from tides, ocean currents, rivers, streams, canals and conduits. Our mission is to develop energy projects, initially in rivers in the United States and ultimately in a wide variety of free flow environments throughout the world.We believe our technology will not only create a new source of renewable energy but will also create jobs in manufacturing and maintenance as well as fostering further research and development into hydrokinetic generation. Our team of employees, consultants and strategic partners bring together dedication to our mission and values and the diverse skills and experiences required to make the vision of hydrokinetic generation a reality.
