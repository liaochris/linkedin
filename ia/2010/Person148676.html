Monte Gast’s Summary

Highly successful track record in Media & Advertising Ownership/Management/Production as well as domestic & international Sales & Marketing in the Beauty/Suncare/Spa industries; plus extensive Adjunct Professor teaching experience in Entrepreneurship, Project Management, Marketing, Branding, Finance, Advertising, PR, Business Communications, Critical Thinking, Public Speaking; & Graphic Arts/Video/Film. The dual backgrounds in Business and Academia provide both a real world perspective and academic research & analysis to every project I consult or work on, and the clients & students I coach, mentor or teach. Have written 3 college online courses: Principles of Project Management - (Academy of Entertainment Technology - Santa Monica College); Corporate I.D. & Branding (L.A. College International); Advertising Design (L.A.College International). Authored 1 book (Getting The Best of L.A) and a number of magazine articles.

Monte Gast’s Specialties:

MULTI-MEDIA/ANIMATION PRODUCTION: Award winning production, direction and creative concepts. BUSINESS: Entrepreneurship; Domestic/International Sales & Marketing Strategy & Execution; Branding; Advertising; Business Communications, Project Management; New Business Initiatives & Product launches; and Sustainable Profit Maximization strategies. EDUCATION - Business/Media/Communications; maximizing web 2.0, case study & project creation tools; Online Course and Curriculum development/authoring.

Won a number of major advertising awards: New York Festival (Gold,Silver, 2 bronze), Belding, Lulu, etc. plus 2 time Clio finalist for spots & campaigns and set repeated client revenue records.

  • General Manager

    KWEST 106 FM Radio

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    January 1975 — November 1979 (4 years 11 months)

    2 year stint as General Sales Manager for Century Broadcasting's L.A. outlet KWST (KWEST 106FM), setting numerous sales records while increasing revenue over 200% and profits 245%. Was then appointed General Manager in 1977, becoming one of the youngest major market radio GMs in the country at that time. Installed Digital Read Out Sign on top of Whiskey A-Go-Go and launched Whiskey's Time's Square West New Years Countdown on the Sunset Strip on the sign. Produced 4 annual Free Concert Series (32 rock and jazz fusion shows) with independent promoter Bill Gerber - former manager of Devo, ABC & The Cars) at John Anson Ford Amphitheater in conjunction with the L.A. County Dept. of Parks & Recreation. Hired in radio programming and talent luminaries Tom Yates, Rachel Donahue and Shadoe Stevens which boosted ratings 105% then left to start a lifestyle/media/music ad agency in 1982 as station prepared to change format and ownership.

  • Co-Founder/Co-Producer/Pres.Sales & Marketing

    BBC Productions

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1974 — February 1978 (3 years 9 months)

    Co-owner/Co-Producer & Sales/Marketing exec. for BBC Productions with partners Shadoe & Linda Stevens. BBC's Sunset Strip Studio created, recorded and produced thousands of radio commercials, corporate projects and numerous TV commercial voiceovers over a 4 year period for clients such as University Stereo, Pacific Stereo, Avalon Attractions, Fred Segal Clothing, Oshman's Sporting Goods and other major California retailers, plus numerous record companies, film studios and local TV stations with a unique blend of results driven messaging and powerful cutting edge production techniques. During that time BBC's produced Flo & Eddie show became an L.A. radio phenomenon and the BBC produced weekly Great American Rock Album Countdown (GARAC) became the #1 rated radio show in L.A. Rock Radio - beating out the legendary Dr. Demento over 2 straight rating periods before being discontinued when both Gast and Stevens left host station KMET.
