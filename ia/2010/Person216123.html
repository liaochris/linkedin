Witt Salley’s Summary

I am a senior-level academic administrator at Ozarks Technical Community College (OTC), Missouri's third largest community college district. My primary appointment is as College Director of OTC Online, the virtual college within the OTC system. My secondary appointment is as Co-Coordinator of the HLC Self-Study Process, which focuses on continuous quality improvement and regional accreditation compliance. In both roles, I report directly to the Vice President of Academic and Student Affairs.

In addition to my administrative posts, I teach and serve as the subject matter expert for OTC's educational technology course (EDU 225: Technology for Teachers). I previously taught SSM 201: The State of Education and PLS 101: American Government and Politics at OTC. With 22 graduate credits in English, I have also taught composition, technical writing, and business communications.

I am active in professional organizations that advocate for online education and community colleges. Currently, I serve as President of the Missouri Distance Learning Association (MoDLA). Along with Dr. Leo Hirner of Metropolitan Community College – Kansas City, I facilitate a special interest group for Missouri community college chief distance learning officers. I am also involved in the eLearning Institute, serving as Editor the Journal of Community College Learning, Leadership, and Service.

Finally, I am a graduate of the inaugural class of the Institute for Emerging Leadership in Online Learning, sponsored by Penn State University and the Sloan Consortium. I expect to complete an Ed.D. in E-Learning and Teaching Online at Northcentral University by December 2010. My dissertation studies the relationships among online instructor employment status, teaching load, and student performance in OTC Online courses.

Witt Salley’s Specialties:

E-learning administration, supervision and evaluation of online teaching, educational planning and development for online programs, online faculty training and development, online course development, online assessment methodologies
