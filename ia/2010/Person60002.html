Miriam Franco’s Summary

Miriam Franco, MSW, Psy.D. is a psychotherapist in private practice and an Associate Professor and Chair of the Sociology Department at Immaculata University. She is certified in Integrative Guided Imagery by Beyond Ordinary Nursing Organization, in The Prepare for Surgery Program by Peggy Huddleston, M.A., and is a professional member of Imagery International. She is also a Multiple Sclerosis Certified Specialist. Dr. Franco has taught Stress Reduction and Guided Imagery to patients, families, caregivers, nurses, social workers and psychologists including Bryn Mawr Graduate School of Social Work and Social Research, Immaculata University and Pennsylvania Society of Oncology Social Workers. She has conducted numerous workshops for the public on Stress Reduction and particular applications of Guided Imagery for specific health populations, such as The Delaware Valley Chapter of the National Kidney Foundation, The Wellness Community of Philadelphia, The Philadelphia Corporation for Aging and The Multiple Sclerosis Association of America. Dr. Franco is on The Health Care Advisory Board of The Multiple Sclerosis Association of America and on the Board of 4kidsneeds.
