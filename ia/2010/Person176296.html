Rod Frantz’s Summary

Rod Frantz is the President of New Wave Strategies and founder of the Urban Prosperity and Resilience Initiative (UPRI). UPRI represents a consortium of leading edge planners and practitioners in the field of urban sustainability, resilience and transformation.

Rod, a member of the Resilient Futures Network (resilientfutures.org), has recently consulted with the Reinventing Stamford: Redefining the Recession and North East Corridor Connects (NECC) projects, and is working with authors in Australia and the US on two books detailing resilience/whole systems thinking and strategies for cities, communities, and corporations. These books, Resilience Unleashed: A Strategies Handbook (April 2010) and ZOOM! The Secret to Prosperity and Sustainability in the 21st Century (June 2010) will serve as guides for companies, schools, cities and organizations seeking to position themselves for prosperity through resilience in the emergent economy.

For five years, Rod served as President of the Richard Florida Creativity Group (now the Creative Class group). In collaboration with Florida, he spearheaded the development of the Creative Cities Leadership Project and delivered training in creative leadership in major urban centers around the country. He served as the Assistant Director on the largest riverfront regeneration and visioning plan in the country, the Riverlife Task Force in Pittsburgh, PA. Rod previously served as Executive Assistant to Allegheny County Commissioner Bob Cranmer.

Rod is the co-author of The Yes Child: A Simple Guide for Raising Great Kids, which will soon be airing nationally on The Telos Alternative Health & Mind Body Spirit TV Channels.

Rod is a lifelong entrepreneur who loves transformative ideas, art and beauty in all of their abundance. In an earlier life, he was the lead singer/lyricist for the groundbreaking New Wave band, Urban Verbs (Warner Brothers), and with his bandmates co-founded the world-famous 930 Club in downtown Washington, DC.

Rod Frantz’s Specialties:

Rod Frantz is an expert at identifying and promoting causes that fundamentally change people’s lives and perspectives. He is a highly creative, persuasive, and inspirational communicator and a passionate advocate who connects with a broad audience. He is a superb coalition builder and negotiator as well as an accomplished team builder and team player. Frantz maintains a global network of key stakeholder relationships. His entrepreneurial drive and skills have ignited multiple start up efforts.
