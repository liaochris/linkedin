Pierrick Thébault’s Summary

I am a PhD candidate at Alcatel-Lucent Bell Labs France and Arts et Métiers ParisTech.

I have a Master’s Degree in Industrial Design from L’Ecole de Design Nantes Atlantique. I worked as a freelance user interface designer for one year, where I helped companies to enhance the user experience of their web applications.

Since 2009, I am part of a multidisciplinary team at Bell Labs Applications Domain. My research aims to explore a variety of strategies that seek to enhance the user experience design of the web of things.
