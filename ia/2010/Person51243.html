David LaPlante’s Summary

David LaPlante is senior vice president of sales and marketing for Boston-based One to One Interactive, recently ranked by Inc. Magazine as one of the fastest growing private companies in America and named one of the world’s 20 hottest independent digital marketing firms by Ad Age.

Previous to his current role, David was CEO of Reno and Salt Lake City based Twelve Horses, an online brand marketing and messaging technology firm, which merged with One to One Interactive in September of 2009.  

A frequent public speaker, David provides audiences with his futuristic perspectives on marketing innovation, technology, social dynamics and game-changing brand marketing strategies.

David is chairman and co-founder of Nevada’s Center for Entrepreneurship & Technology (NCET) and serves as a director for the boards of Artown, EDAWN, Holland Project and the Reno Bike Project. David is a ten-year member of the Entrepreneurs’ Organization. When not immersed in brand marketing technology, David can be found skiing, cycling, climbing and camping in the Sierra Nevada with his family.

David was recently named to Nevada’s “Technology Hall of Fame” and Nevada Business Journal named him to Nevada’s 2009 Top 20/20 visionaries. In 2008, David’s alma mater, the University of Nevada, recognized David with a “Lifetime Professional Achievement Award”. David was an inaugural class recipient of the Reno-Tahoe Young Professionals Network’s “20 Under 40” award in 2007, the same year Twelve Horse was named “2007 Technology Company of the Year” by the Technology Business Alliance of Nevada. The Economic Development Authority of Western Nevada (EDAWN) recognized Twelve Horses as its “2005 Business of the Year”, and he was recognized as “Marketer of the Year” by Northern Nevada’s American Marketing Association in 2004.
