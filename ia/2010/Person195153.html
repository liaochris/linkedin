Steve Jursa’s Summary

Steve combines his passion and experience for technology and marketing communications with building companies and growing organizations. Steve built one of the industry's strongest worldwide networks of communications professionals focused on marketing technology products as diverse as mobile phones, enterprise software and cutting edge semiconductors. He has worked with CEO Ned Barnholt to craft the corporate story as Agilent spun out of HP. Steve also helped communicate the CEO transition at Qualcomm from Irwin Jacobs to his son Paul, and helped both father and son navigate a number of corporate communication challenges that faced the company. Steve has worked with a wide range of companies including Intel, HP, IBM, Qualcomm, Analog Devices, nVidia, Fujitsu, Iomega, BellSouth, Schneider Trucking, Agilent, Philips Electronics, DriveCam, Softbank/Kingston and BMC Software.

Steve Jursa’s Specialties:

High-tech and deep-tech marketing, communications and public relations. Strategy, global program management, business management.
