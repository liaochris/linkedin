Peter Osborne’s Summary

My strengths and accomplishments can’t be neatly captured by a job title or resume. Most of my background is in marketing and business journalism, but people with my skills are better known as “go-to” types or “problem solvers.” My most important successes have come when executives asked me to fix problems that kept them awake at night. I'm proud that MBNA's chief marketing officer once described me as a "bulldog" because of my tenacity, energy, and passion.

I am looking for “chief-of-staff” type positions where I can focus on execution while you focus on vision (although I'm comfortable doing that too). I deliver consistency and synergy across business “silos,” crafting sales messages, curriculum, communications, and RFP and Negotiations tools that drive accountability. I’d like to do this on a full-time basis…but will take on projects to demonstrate my long-term value.

Over the years, I have:
-- Made negotiations an organizational core competency, introducing classes and tools that drove effective preparation and creating “Real Deal Roundtables” to assess strategy before and after deals.
-- Streamlined RFP processes to focus on value. I have developed a template that helps evaluate opportunities and develop response strategies that are easily executed and tracked.
-- Helped companies and individuals crystallize their value propositions and communicate business strategies and brand messages to key constituencies.
-- Turned around a card portfolio's performance with a focus on execution and cross-selling opportunities.
-- Restored operational control of marketing production processes after some internal audit failures by revising desktop procedures; introducing exception reporting and curriculum; and clarifying job descriptions – all in the face of workforce reductions.

In summary, I break down barriers to marketing success by asking great questions and mobilizing people across areas to find a common language to solve problems.

Peter Osborne’s Specialties:

Executive-level sales and account management, negotiations strategy and deal coaching, RFP-response strategy and execution, forward-looking strategic communications counsel, media relations, financial public relations, elevator speeches, affinity sales and management, employee communications, business-strategy-focused curriculum development and delivery, business process efficiencies, website usability, employee engagement, and production management.
