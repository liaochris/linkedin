  • Store Operations Manager

    State of Iowa - Liquor Contol Commission

    (Government Administration industry)

    1972 — 1979 (7 years )

    1972 –1979 State of Iowa, Liquor Control Department, Des Moines, Iowa
    Hired as a Liquor Store Clerk, New Hampton, Iowa and promoted within four months to Manager II. Selected as the first “Liquor Store Manager of the Month” which was an honor based on overall store performance including losses, inventory control, record management and budget. Promoted to Liquor Store Manager IV, Ames in 1974. Sent to the Ames, Iowa store as it was an operation in severe trouble and was charged with turning it around. Operation was judged to be a success and within satisfactory inventory and loss standards and guidelines of conformity within six months.
    Promoted to Store Operations Manager, 1975, Des Moines, Iowa, corporate location. Supervised the day-to-day operations of the State’s 210 liquor stores. Maintained budget of 12.5 million dollars and directly supervised two office assistants, six State Mangers, 210 Store Managers, 60 Assistant Managers. Overall responsibility for 1100 permanent full-time employees and the negotiation with State Employee’s Union and acted as second step in Grievance Process. Responsibilities included product training, protocol issues, maintaining and enforcing State Liquor Laws, setting of all store policies, writing of 210 page product manual, responsible for customer service, maintenance and operation of sales registers, store layout, setting of hours of operation, publication of weekly Store Letter, store audits and financial bottom line. Reported to the Deputy Director.
