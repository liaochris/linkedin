Robert Fransgaard’s Summary

A London based Creative Lead and Digital Communicator who believes in the value of delivering effortless and enjoyable user experiences that create happy customers and meet business objectives.

I am passionate about all things digital and am constantly on the lookout for new technology trends and innovations that can help develop and enhance the online presence of my clients and create longer-term communication opportunities where the conversation is happening.

  • Freelance Web Designer

    (Online Media industry)

    January 1999 — August 1999 (8 months)

    During the time it was live it became one of the top ten most visited sites in Denmark. And it was a great portfolio piece to get me start in London.
