James Wydick, MBA, Broker, Realtor®’s Summary

Professional: Allen Tate Company operates as a residential real estate brokerage company in the United States. The company’s services include property search, mortgage and insurance, relocation, and marketing of new homes for local, regional, and national builders. Allen Tate Company was founded in 1957 and is based in Charlotte, North Carolina with offices from in Upstate, SC, Charlotte, the Triad region of NC, and the Triangle region of NC. Allen Tate Realtors is the #1 real estate companies in the Carolinas, #8 company in the country, and a premier member of the Leading Real Estate Companies of the World Network, the #1 referral network in the world.

Personal: I am a recent graduate of an International Masters Degree Program where I had the opportunity to study in Valencia, Spain for six months. In this program I received two degrees, an International MBA and a MA in International Marketing. I live in the Raleigh area where I am real estate broker with Allen Tate Realtors® at the Brier Creek office.
