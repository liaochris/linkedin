Claire Celsi’s Summary

I'm a seasoned public relations and marketing professional with category experience in insurance, financial services, construction, telecommunications, economic development, renewable energy, government and agriculture. I have worked with numerous clients to develop and implement integrated marketing, public relations and social media programs.

My strengths are in strategic planning and integrating new media into existing marketing plans. I'm a project manager and multi-task on many levels. I enjoy managing and mentoring people, and have a special affinity for young professionals.

I believe that blogging is a great medium for companies to connect with their customers and differentiate themselves in the market place. At the very least, corporate communicators should be listening to what the blogosphere is saying about your company.

Public speaking is one of my favorite things to do, because I'm passionate about my subject, and love teach people new things.
