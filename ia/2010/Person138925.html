Michael Davidson’s Summary

Michael Davidson is an award-winning, results-oriented B2B and marketing executive with over 10 1/2 years of marketing and communications experience with proven success working in highly matrixed, challenging and ever-changing business environments. He recently served as a Field Marketing Manager at Forsythe Technology, Inc., a large IT infrastructure integrator headquartered in Chicago, IL that helps companies optimize IT spend, improve performance and manage risk. Prior to Forsythe, he served as a Marketing Manager at other leading IT solutions and services companies, including CNSI and Ingenix, a subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group. He spent the bulk of his career serving in various marketing capacities at BearingPoint/KPMG Consulting, one of the world's largest providers of management and technology consulting services. He began his career working for a boutique telecommunications provider, ACN.

Marketing Planning & Strategy, Business Development & Lead Generation, Direct Response Marketing, Market Research & Intelligence, Alliance & Channel Marketing, Program Design & Implementation, Program Measurement & Reporting, Sales & Marketing Automation, Product Marketing, Brand Management, Media & Analyst Relations, Marketing Communications, Advertising and Events Management.
