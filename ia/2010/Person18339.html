Aaron Watkins’s Summary

Mobile applications have quickly emerged as the future of the mobile environment, however with the explosive growth comes the need for developers to learn how to make their products stand out from the noise. Traditionally... app developers are not marketers, and marketers, are not app developers. I created Appency to fill this need and bring the skills of seasoned marketing and PR professionals to the app development community. In my time as Co-Founder of Appency, I have helped successfully market dozens of high profile apps and continue to look for new and interesting ways to help app developers establish a brand and stand out from the crowd.

Please feel free to contact me, I love app marketing, and am happy to give out advice when I can.

Note: If you are a development company not looking for app marketing services and just trying to sell us development, we do not take on development projects.
