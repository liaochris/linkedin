Jim Torpey’s Summary

InsynQ, Inc. has Three Key benefits: Freedom, Simplicity and Value

The Freedom - is obviously to have the ability to access all of your applications, your desk tops, documents and all of your company data from anywhere.

The Simplicity - it is actually very simple as long as you have a web browser and an internet connection you can log in and gain access to all of your applications and company data.
It is also simplistic as it removes the need for business to divert management’s time and resources for setting up and managing an IT department.

The Value - is that it removes the need for capital expenditure it is simply a pay as you go monthly type of model.

It is truly transformational technology we are transforming technology from being a simple box product solution to a subscription base service. It can transform and change the way you do business forever. As the flexibly can be a huge value to your employees, management and owner’s alike.
