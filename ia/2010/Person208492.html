Troy Goode’s Summary

Troy is a software architect with a long history of high profile projects for international organizations. Troy has nearly 10 years of consulting experience from across North America including developing applications for the World Bank's board room and president's office, developing the Institute of Medicine's website and content management system, and developing websites for an online grocer, multiple science foundations, an Internet radio company, and many others.

A passionate follower of technology, Troy has kept a keen eye on Microsoft's .Net platform since the middle of 1999 when it was still known as Next Generation Windows Services. Troy brings more than 6 years of professional .Net development experience to the table, and his expertise in the platform extends as far back as .Net Beta 2 when you still had to compile Notepad text files from the command line.

Most recently, Troy has been recognized as a prominent voice on Microsoft's new MVC framework and has worked closely with Microsoft to relay feedback and contribute helpful articles and code to the blossoming MVC development community.
