Peter Wehrle’s Summary

In my current position I am the Student Lead at the Memorial Library Circulation Desk, providing circulation, reference and document delivery services.

I am seeking a position that allows me to expand upon my library experience. I am looking to grow as an instructor in group setting while expanding my knowledge of resources for service to individual users.

In previous positions I designed and delivered instruction sessions focusing on use of Promethean and SMART interactive whiteboard systems. I also co-taught instruction sessions for undergraduate students focusing on information literacy skill development.

My technology experience includes fluency in Voyager circulation software , in addition to Trillian and Velaro virtual reference applications. I also have a working knowledge of xhtml coding principles.
