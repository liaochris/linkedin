Gregory Sieradzan’s Summary

I've always been a very ambitious individual, never afraid of taking the difficult route to get to where I want to be. I started my career at the bottom at Universal Music in The Netherlands and worked my way up to the international marketing department, overseeing all of the artists on Island/DefJam Music Group and Universal/Motown/Republic. Over a course of six years at Universal, I evolved into an expert in music marketing - cross all genres - with international experience and an in-depth knowledge of the music business.

In 2008 I started a management company called Beat Brokers in Amsterdam, which now has an HQ in Los Angeles.
