Jonathan Huie’s Summary

Sign-up for my Daily Inspiration - Daily Quote email at www.DreamThisDay.com

Jonathan Huie has directed the product planning and implementation of numerous advanced technology products, including software, microcomputers, and ultra high speed network semiconductors. For 30 years, Mr. Huie coordinated the most advanced technologies with the realities of venture economics and customer acceptance. He has consulted for some of Silicon Valley’s highest technology venture backed startups. Mr. Huie now focuses on the application of socio-psychology to the facilitation of collaborative group planning. His other interests include on-line marketing and web site development. Mr. Huie also writes a free daily email containing his quotes and thoughts on Joyful Living which is available at www.DreamThisDay.com

As the owner or executive of a 21st century business, know this: your most valuable and creative employees have discovered life beyond the office. Pure salary has lost it's power as a motivator. Your best opportunity is to fully engage your entire staff - at all levels - as committed partners in the success of the business. I can help you to achieve that cooperative commitment to mutual success.

Visualize your conference room alive with a product planning meeting that will determine the future direction and success of your company. Is the atmosphere filled with excitement, cooperation, and mutual respect? Or is there bickering, egoistic points-of-view, and fear? As a skilled outsider with no "ax-to-grind," I can facilitate your collaborative group planning on a win-win basis. Ideally, I will meet with a number of the participants in advance to understand their points-of-view, dispel their concerns, and fully engage their participation in the collaborative process.
