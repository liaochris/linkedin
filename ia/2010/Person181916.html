Sandy Horner’s Summary

I help my clients to set and achieve aggressive sales and business growth goals. Under my direction, client teams consistently produce record profitability for their business units.

With a career spanning over twenty-five years in sales and business operations, across business start-ups, franchise organizations and Fortune 1000 corporations, my career path has revealed the good, the bad and the ugly associated with organizations in transition. I honed a broad range of business development skills while working for companies as diverse as PPG Industries, PC Solutions, MicroAge, Productivity Point International (PPI) and New Horizons Computer Learning Centers.

In 2005, I launched a team-selling concept and strategic account planning process for a technology company that resulted in 110% revenue increase in a targeted set of accounts. Today, pipeline management tools and techniques, developed by Horner Consulting, help clients to increase sales by as much as 40% while measurably improving the accuracy of business forecasting.

Although the firm has expanded over the years, I continue to work directly with business owners to plan and execute growth strategies.

I work with small and mid-sized businesses looking to increase revenue by improving their sales processes. In addition to my client work, I am active in the Pittsburgh Consulting Community, I founded and currently facilitate My Business Advisory Council™, a mastermind group for small business owners. I also serve on the advisory board for the Women’s Small Business Association, and volunteer as a PA eMentor and as a PowerLink panel advisor, an organization devoted to helping women-owned businesses achieve greater levels of success.

Sandy Horner’s Specialties:

My passion is growing sustainable high-performance businesses by improving the company’s productivity, focus and flexibility. Employing practical solutions based on proven techniques allows my clients to build a better sales “back-office,” which results in them gaining new clients, more sales and increased profitability. I help to close the gap between the aspiration of and the achievement of aggressive sales goals.
