Joseph Svitek’s Summary

Seasoned Finance Executive with over 20 years of successful experience in the Financial Services/P&C Insurance Industries. My finance/accounting foundation started with Deloitte&Touche's Stamford Connecticut office, and now includes 10 years of GE Finance experience and 5 years of experience as a Berkshire Hathaway Business Unit CFO. In my most recent role as the SVP Finance/CFO/Treasurer of The Medical Protective Company($700MM DWP MedMal Carrier), I was responsible for all aspects of the Finance Function, including Financial Reporting, Treasury, Tax, Controllership, Financial Planning, Model Audit Rule Compliance, Capital Management, Credit and Collections, and sourcing/purchasing.

Joseph Svitek’s Specialties:

Insurance Accounting, both STAT and GAAP, Financial Services/Leasing, M&A...both buying and selling, IT project management, Thrifty Expense Management, Sourcing, and Employee Benefits.
