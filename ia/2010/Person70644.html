Karen Fox’s Summary

Karen has more than 20 years professional experience with major interior architecture and architecture firms in Boston, MA and Washington, DC. As a Senior Associate, she has assumed leadership roles in a full range of project types including corporate, educational and medical facilities.

Today, she is seeking new challenges as a project manager in programming and design of corporate, secondary and higher educational and research institutions.

Karen Fox’s Specialties:

Her expertise includes programming, master facilities planning, workplace strategies, conceptual design and project management for large-scale interior design projects.
