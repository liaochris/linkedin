Jon DiPietro’s Summary

My professional mantra is “domesticating information technology.” I show businesses how to identify and implement the simplest, most cost-effective IT strategies that will get them to the next level. I’ve developed a broad range of technical and business skills over the past 20 years, complimented by twelve years as an entrepreneur and consultant.

Most recently, I became a Certified Inbound Marketing Professional, offering consulting services and training workshops to companies who want to generate and convert more leads online.
