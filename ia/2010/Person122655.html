Laurie Mandato, AICI FLC’s Summary

Laurie A. Mandato, Wardrobe and Style Consultant, is a graduate of Chamberlain School of Retailing, where she studied Fashion Merchandising. An first level certified member of the Association of Image Consultants International (AICI), President, AICI New England Chapter. She is a certified Color Consultant, Fashion Feng Shui Facilitator and consultant to well known radio personality Candy O'Terry of Magic 106.7.

As a Wardrobe and Style Consultant, my goal is to empower my client to present their best and most authentic self to the world. Through wardrobe/style consultation and image coaching, we will work together to uncover your personal style- the style that best represents who you are.

Laurie Mandato, AICI FLC’s Specialties:

Working with speakers, authors, coaches, consultants, executive leaders, and conscious business owners who are stepping into the spotlight. Helping them to feel confident and ready to step into that role.


Fashion Feng Shui Facilitator, Closet Audits, Individual Color Analysis, Style and Wardrobe Analysis, Personal Shopping
