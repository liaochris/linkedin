Daniel Gross’s Summary

I am currently the Director of IT for Waterford Union High School.

My formal training is in both elementary and adult education, and I have been practicing various forms of distance learning and using technology for learning since 1976. Professionally I have held many jobs across K12 and postsecondary education, from teacher & administrator to school board member.

In addition to formal educational experience, I have worked as a consultant to the museum sector for years, hosting & speaking at conferences, developing programs and other educational consulting opportunities. Museums, zoos and cultural centers hold a special place in my heart and I would love to become more involved in this sector.

I seek to continue in a leadership position where I can enhance my skills, redefine cutting edge technology, teach and learn with others, and advance both professionally and financially.

Outside of my professional life I have discovered leadership through service. I am the Cubmaster for Pack 323 in Waterford, WI. Being a Lion is also very important to me. Occasionally I also volunteer helping out with SWAT swimming doing 1:1 coaching, referee or as needed. Life is better when you serve!

Daniel Gross’s Specialties:

If it plugs in, I probably deal with it. For over a decade I specialized in various distance learning technologies, but today get my hands dirty with everything from server virtualization & thin clients to streaming media production.
