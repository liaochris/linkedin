Scott Lockwood’s Summary

Scott has more than 15 years experience in Real Estate sales, marketing and development as an executive. He has developed targeted sales, marketing and real estate development execution strategies for numerous real estate projects in the residential, resort, commercial and multi family sectors.

Scott has established large-volume, high-profit accounts for employers, partners and clients with excellent levels of retention and loyalty, while successfully managing multiple tasks simultaneously and ensuring projects meet stated sales goals and objectives.
