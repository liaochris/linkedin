Andy Weir’s Summary

I really enjoy helping people tell their story. Connections and interaction are fascinating and I'm getting more and more passionate about the effective use of technology to improve all that good stuff.

Knowing the power of measured and effective communication, I just want to get better at it and tell the world what I've learned.

I'd loosely describe myself as a musician as well - I am active in playing drums, guitar & singing in a few different contexts. I also teach drums to a number of young people.

From my first job in retail, it was the interaction with people that caught my attention. That and design.... Then playing with a band for seven years, working on live tours and recording projects, it's about making connections and telling your story. My self-motivation and relentless people-focus has given me exposure to a heaps of experiences - that shaped my view of the world.

With the pace of life continuing to change, it's an incredible time to continue to be involved in the area of communication. I want to make the most of now (to quote a certain communication company...)
