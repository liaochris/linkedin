Jarno Baaijens’s Summary

Purchasing experience for over 10 years.

Started with buying responsibilities at a small start-up company, followed by larger responsibilities and targets in a pharmaceutical company, Jarno joined a purchasing-related consultancy-firm in 2000 where he had the chance to work with high-skilled procurement professionals at different companies. He had a variety of responsibilities: Working as a tactical buyer at one company, in the other company he was the person to develop the procurement strategy or to help the department reaching new levels of expertise, at others he was the procurement member in a project group. Jarno left the consultancy-world in 2006 for some interesting years at Campina. In 2008 Jarno decided to start his own company, fully focussing on procurement consultancy and interim-assignments.

Specialized in the procurement of product-related articles (Raw Materials, Ingredients, Packaging Materials) and Investments/CapEx, Jarno provides sustainable and measurable added value to your business.

Procurement Education:
NEVI, which is equalled by
- Chartered Institute for Purchasing & Supply (CIPS) Professional Stage; and/or
- Certificate of Competence -International Federation of Purchasing and Materials Management (IFPMM)
