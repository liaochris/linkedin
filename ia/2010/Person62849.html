Efrat Kasznik’s Summary

Efrat Kasznik is a valuation expert with over 15 years of extensive economic and financial consulting experience. She specializes in performing business valuations and valuations of intangible assets for a range of purposes, including: Mergers & acquisitions, financial reporting, technology commercialization decisions, tax compliance, transfer pricing, and litigation damages. She led complex valuation and litigation engagements for clients, ranging in size from Fortune 100 companies to start-ups, from a diverse range of industries such as automotive, aerospace, telecommunications, industrial chemicals, software, hardware and entertainment.

Efrat is the founder and President of Foresight Valuation Group, LLC (FVG), a boutique Intellectual Property consulting firm providing high quality analytical services. Prior to that, she held a series of partner level positions in leading consulting firms. As Vice President of ICMG, a consulting firm specializing in intellectual asset management, with which she has been affiliated over the last 12 years, she led the Valuation practice, working primarily with Fortune 100 corporations. She also served as a Director with LECG (NASDQ: XPRT), a global management consulting firm, where she was recruited to create a new practice area around Intellectual Asset Management. She later left LECG and was co-founder of Ennovationz, a residential energy efficiency consulting firm, developing analytical energy models. Efrat also held several accounting and financial management positions, including the position of Chief Financial Officer for AcrossWorld Communications, a Silicon Valley start-up engaged in building global data communication networks.
