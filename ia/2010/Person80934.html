Heather Rosen’s Summary

I am FranNet's franchise consultant for Northern and Central Virginia and Washington, DC. FranNet is the oldest and most respected Franchise Consulting firm in the country, with 75 offices in the US, Canada and Puerto Rico.

If you are thinking of starting or buying a business, or if you are looking for a master franchise opportunity, why try to conduct the research and analysis on your own? I have been providing business counseling to individuals and businesses my entire career and I can give you guidance, information and support if you are looking for a business and are open to looking at franchises in the B2B, B2C, retail, auto or food sectors that are expanding in your local market.

I will -

1. work closely with you to understand who you are - not just your work experience and skills, but also your personal goals, values and interests.

2. coach you through the FranNet Four Step Research Process that matches you, the client, with franchise opportunities and ensures that you get the information you need to make the best decision.

3. assist you in finding the franchise that most closely aligns with your skills, goals and financial capability.

Best of all, my services are free. Contact me today to get started: hrosen@frannet.com.
