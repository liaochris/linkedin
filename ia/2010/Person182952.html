Sara Isenberg’s Summary

I'm the principal consultant at:
Sara Isenberg Web Consulting & Project Management

I provide web consulting and project management services to web developers, businesses, and non-technical individuals for web, social media, eNewsletter, software application, and other projects.

My services include:

= Project Management for the web production process, including defining requirements, finding resources for design, branding, production, information architecture, SEO (search engine optimization), and managing general communications between the client and the production team.

= Social Media strategies.

= Referrals to web experts.

= Managing web application projects, including defining requirements, facilitating and documenting weekly team meetings, finding resources for information architecture, best practices, production, and QA.

= Training. I help business professionals become more comfortable with technology, including the marketing value of using social media, how to get started w/ blogging, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, email marketing, eNewsletters, and more!

= Strategic Partner Management.

= Technical Account Management.

= eNewsletters and marketing email production. Editing and proofreading for other writers.

= Functional management, interviewing, hiring, training, and advising.
