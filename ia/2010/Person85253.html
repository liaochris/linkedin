Camille Fetter CFetter@TalentFoot.com’s Summary

Camille is an Executive Recruiter for TalentFoot in Chicago. As the Managing Partner, she specializes in internet marketing, direct marketing, advertising, media and sales. She partners with both advertising agencies and corporations of all sizes, nationwide.

During her career as an executive recruiter, she has repeatedly matched stellar candidates with blue-chip clients with unsurpassed enthusiasm. Camille’s intense industry focus is complemented by her engaging personality and is what has led her to success which has been nothing short of extraordinary.

Her thorough understanding of interactive marketing and media continues to make her successful regardless of the nature of the search - retained or contingent. To better partner with her clients, Camille continually goes the extra mile, whether it be for a CMO, a VP, a Director, or Middle Management position, she gets the job done with the very best talent possible.

A graduate of the University of Colorado at Boulder, Camille holds a BS in Advertising and a minor in Technology, Art & Media.

She currently serves on the Executive Board of the Chicago Interactive Marketing Association (CIMA) as the Vice President of Academic Affairs.
