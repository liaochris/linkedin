Arena Lodge’s Summary

I have 20 years of experience in the New Zealand Army from 1985 to 2005 as a Logistician. My main trade was Supply specialsing in the field of Petroleum management. Throughout those years I progressed through the ranks to Warrant Officer. I had postings in all the main operational camps with a stint at the Supply Wing of the Trade Training School. I finished my career in Palmerston North as the Company Sergeant Major of the main Supply Company in the NZ Army.

My wife (Stephanie) and I have invested in Arena Lodge (Motel) since leaving the NZ Army in 2005. We have established a very successful business over the past 4 years gaining strength and confidence in the industry each day.

Our goal is to stay in the hospitality industry where we feel it has the potential for further business opportunities.
