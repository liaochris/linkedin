Sandra Garcia’s Summary

I have been freelancing as a Communications and Public Relations Specialist since 2005 in various capacities including non-profit capital campaign fundraising, event promotions, corporate sponsorship, public company marketing, and most recently publicity in the art, beauty, retail and dance industries.

Currently, I am the Marketing Director for the Vancouver Short Film Festival in 2009, formerly the Vancouver Student Film Festival, established in 2005.

My goal is to become a successful Public Relations Consultant in Vancouver, specializing in feature film and event publicity, and campaigns that make a difference in the community at large.

With the power of the Internet and mobile networks today, I believe that businesses of all sizes have an obligation to give back to the community and it is possible now, more than ever, to make a positive contribution to social welfare through online and offline communications.
