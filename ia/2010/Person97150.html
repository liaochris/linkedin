Jessica Odenbach’s Summary

New ventures in to the social media realm have opened up a variety of new opportunities and strategies for myself and my clients. Helping to mold new ideas and areas to participate help clients, co-workers and myself always stay fresh.

My current role allows me to further develop my strengths in brand management, analytics, monitoring and much more with social media.

In the past I have worked with such clients as Archer Daniels Midland (ADM) on both environmental and political efforts. The Chicago Spire building-before it was simply a hole in the ground. The Hershey Company on new product launches and their health and wellness programs. Goodyear and Microsoft Advertising in both marketing and public relations outreach programs I've also worked with a variety of major Hollywood movie studios for Chicago-based red carpet events and promotions.
