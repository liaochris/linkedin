Jessica Pettus’s Summary

Skilled communications managers and consultants play a key role in the success and expansion of market leaders by supporting employees at all corporate levels. To get the right message to employees, a strong communicator first hears what they say, then designs systems to change behaviors and motivate for results.

My work has involved planning, developing and executing both internally and externally-targeted communications. The overall goal of my work is always the same: to support and promote the cause of clear communication and effective engagement at all levels of a company and/or of an external audience.

I educate and evangelize for the prudent deployment of social networking technologies and platforms, as well as more traditional methods and vehicles. I enjoy combining cutting edge tools with traditional channels to create a compelling communications architecture that will reach each audience with the right message, through the right medium, at the right time.
