Dr. Arnold Plotnick’s Summary

I am a veterinarian and owner of Manhattan Cat Specialists, a full-service, feline-only veterinary hospital in New York City. I am board-certified in veterinary internal medicine. My particular areas of interest and expertise are feline endocrinology, geriatric medicine, and chronic renal failure.

In addition to my veterinary work, I author the blog "Cat Man Do" ( http://catexpert.blogspot.com ), as well as a monthly "Ask the Vet" column for Cat Fancy magazine, and I am the medical editor for Catnip newsletter. I am on the editorial advisory board of Veterinary Forum magazine, and I am one of the cat advice experts on www.CatChannel.com. I have appeared as a guest on numerous podcasts, radio, and television programs, most notably on a segment of The Martha Stewart Show. I also run a veterinary advice website, www.FelineVeterinaryAdvice.com
