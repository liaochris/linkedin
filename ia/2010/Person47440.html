Danielle Crino’s Summary

I am a 2009 graduate of Nazareth College in Rochester, NY where I received a Bachelor of Arts degree after completing a double major in Communications and English. Throughout my four years of college I was heavily involved in campus activities, such as the college newspaper, radio station, and ski club. Simultaneously, I maintained a 3.5 overall GPA and expanded my marketing, writing and design skills through several internships, college courses and self study.

As a young professional with big ideas and creative tendencies, I am always looking to expand my opportunities in the field of marketing, including but not limited to, website design, brochures, business cards, email blasts, and print advertising. Photography is also a hobby of mine.

I have worked with small companies, non-for-profit organizations, and individuals in a range of fields from health care to information technology. I have the design and writing skills, knowledge of effective company branding, and savvy social networking ability to help your company or organization grow.

Take a look at my portfolio to see some of my photography and design materials. If you would like a writing sample, please send me an email through my contact page. I also have references upon request.
