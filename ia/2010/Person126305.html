Lisa Plendl’s Summary

Technology writer Lisa Plendl melds her strong mainstream journalism and technical marketing backgrounds to make complex material understandable for technical and non-technical customers, buyers and influencers. What are the business or other benefits of your technology? And why should the reader or his or her company care?

Lisa's highly-seasoned approach to technical marcom writing provides value to your company by cutting through the technical fog that often predominates, to put your software, hardware and related use strategies in true perspective.

Lisa:

•Gets up to speed quickly on a wide variety of technologies (has written on well over 200 technology topics)

•Writes engaging, user-friendly marcom copy that moves beyond (unnecessary) technical jargon to reveal how your product or service can improve user or customer efficiency (and when relevant, the customer's bottom line)

•Can work directly with your internal experts to manage technical marketing writing projects, make required edits and gather required approvals within deadline

•Works and plays well with others

Lisa Plendl’s Specialties:

Technical marketing writing, technology writing, business and communications writing, business software, B2B, Fortune 500, SMBs
