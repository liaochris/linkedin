Ken Jones’s Summary

MEDIA DAMAGE CONTROL EXPERT.
CEO of JONESCO MEDIA CONSULTANTS. This is a high end public relations firm operated by broadcast veterans. Jonesco Media Consultants prepares company CEO's and other officers to handle any kind of television, radio or internet situation. We also specialize in compelling, attention grabbing news release writing to get your company, issue or story the media coverage it deserves.

My skills were developed over 30 years as broadcast journalist. I have extensive experience producing, writing, and reporting, compelling, hard hitting award winning stories from across the country. Most recently those stories have included the colorful and often twisted Louisiana politics. Most notably I covered the destruction and devastation of Hurricane Katrina and the massive recovery that followed.

Ken Jones’s Specialties:

NEED PREPARATION FOR TELEVISION, RADIO, INTERNET OR PODCAST? Contact JONESCO MEDIA CONSULTANTS, I specialize in preparing my clients for ambush and damage control interviews. I can teach you how to stay on point and not get tripped up by reporter questions. I can show you how to remain relaxed and calm during a presentation or interview and still command the attention of an audience. Learn how to turn a negative interview into a positive opportunity to get your message out.
