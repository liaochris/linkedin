Barbara Coll’s Summary

Founded Webmama.com in 1996 as a consulting firm to help companies get more traffic to their websites - and just evolved from there into owning and running a large, virtual search engine marketing company.

Previous experience at Sun Microsystems and two internet start-ups gave me the entrepeneurial bug and the knowledge of the challenges of being a marketing type in the bay area.

In 2003 I founded, with a number of other passionate search engine marketers, the Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization (www.sempo.org) to education marketers worldwide about the value of SEM. I was President and Chairperson until March of 2005 and remain on the Board of Directors as co-Chair of the Sponsorship and Biz Dev Committee.

Barbara Coll’s Specialties:

fast-moving, search engine marketing industry expert, conference and seminar speaker, lots of start-up experience, and provider of good :) advice to all who ask.
