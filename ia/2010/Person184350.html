Scott Hayim’s Experience

  • CEO

    Rug Decor

    (Retail industry)

    August 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Rug Decor is a national franchisor of Area Rug specialty retail stores, primarily located in the Southeastern part of the U.S. Rug Decor has more than twenty locations as well as an E-Commerce web presence. Originally a division of CCA Global Partners, Rug Decor was purchased from CCA in August 2009 by three of its franchisees and its senior management. Today seven stores are owned and operated by the franchisor. Rug Decor is the only national chain of area rug specialty stores in the U.S.

  • Division President

    CCA Global Partners

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Retail industry)

    June 2004 — February 2010 (5 years 9 months)

    President of the Retail Area Rug Division of CCA Global Partners, which has grown to over 30 Retail Specialty Stores around the U.S., with heavy presence in Florida and the Carolinas. Responsible for refocussing and upgrading the Rug Decor model with the support of franchisees through the Rug Decor Advisory Council with the primary strategy to create unparalleled rug shopping experiences for Rug Decor customers. Rug Decor has created www.Rugdecor.com and individual store microsites as an integrated online sales tool and marketing strategy to drive store traffic.

  • President & Founder

    Ruglink

    (Wholesale industry)

    April 1998 — March 2002 (4 years )

    I created Ruglink as an agency to represent and assist area rug manufacturers and importers in developing products and programs to sell to retailers around the U.S. and to assist them in presenting and selling those products on a cost effective bais. Ruglink represented the custom rug divisions of Shaw Industries, was the U.S agent and selling arm for Van Den Brink & Campmen, a 100 year old Dutch manufacturer of area rugs, and represented several U.S. area rug importers. Ruglink handled importation and distribution for Van Den Brink & Campman. Ruglink had more than thirty commission sales agents in major markets around the U.S. to represent and sell the products of Shaw, Van Den Brink, and others.

  • Outside Consultant

    Shaw Industries

    (Public Company; BRK; Textiles industry)

    January 1996 — January 2002 (6 years 1 month)

    Designed Initial Vendor Structure (Selected Vendors and Introduced Principals to Shaw Rugs Management) and Created initial Rug Assortment Plans for Retail Area Rug Departments when Shaw went into the Retail Business in 1996.

    Created Training Materials for Area Rugs for Shaw's Retail and Wholesale Sales Forces

    Built an outside Sales Organization to represent Shaw Rugs' Custom Rug Divisions to wholesale customers around the U.S.

  • General Manager of Florida Division

    ABC Carpet & Home

    (Privately Held; Retail industry)

    April 1993 — April 1998 (5 years 1 month)

    General Manager of a 60,000 square foot home furnishings satellite retail location with special emphasis on area rugs, furniture, carpet, and high end linens and domestics. As General Manager of ABC Florida, I was responsible for lease negotiation, renovation and build out of the premises, management of dozens of sales and operational employees, marketing the brand in Florida, and for the financial results of the business. ABC Carpet of Florida was an instant sensation that opened in February 1997 and did over $10,000,000 during its first year.

  • President

    Absolutely Rugs

    (Retail industry)

    July 1987 — May 1993 (5 years 11 months)

    First of its kind area rug specialty retail store. Absolutely Rugs was founded in 1987 and located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and featured 5,000 to 8,000 square feet presentations of the full spectrum of area rugs in an innovative format of display racks and platforms. Absolutely Rugs featured rugs from around the world in every category and design with the full range of price points, exposing consumers to area rug choices to a degree, and in a manner, that had never been done before. ABC Carpet & Home purchased Absolutely Rugs in 1993.
