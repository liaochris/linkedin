Megan Van Petten’s Summary

Megan Van Petten • Business Management & Development • Strategic Sales Consulting & Execution

Megan Van Petten is the co-owner and business development partner of Van Petten Consulting, Inc. With over 15 years in business and sales management experience, Megan has developed a keen sense for the attributes and strategies that drive a high-performing, successful business.

Megan has served in a number of sales and management capacities over the past 15 years. Most recently, Megan was the national director of media sales/associate publisher for The Journal of the Dry Cleaning/Coin Laundry Association in Oak Brook, Illinois. Previous to joining the Coin Laundry Association, Megan was the product sales representative for Reed Business Information, (The largest business-to-business publisher in the U.S) with a concentration in online advertising sales programs for 26 b2b magazines and websites. Some of the projects include ControlEngineering.com, RIMag.com and HousingZone.com.

Before beginning a career in publishing, Megan managed business development for WebSource and eCorp,( multi-million dollar search-advertising publisher) managing and growing online accounts for Fortune 500 companies. New internet businesses launched including DSL.com (SEP #1 on Google, Handyman.com (SEP #1 on Google), and SearchCenter.com (Next generation Search Advertising Platform).

Megan holds Masters of Industrial Organizational Psychology and Bachelor of Business Administration from National Louis University.

Megan is married to Scott Walton, executive chef of Markethouse Restaurant in the Doubletree Hotel in downtown Chicago Magnificent Mile.
