Diab Elmashni M.B.A.’s Experience

  • Global Senior Marketing Manager

    Thermo Fisher Scientific

    (Public Company; TMO; Biotechnology industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    In this position I am responsible for all the global strategic marketing and product management of a 50 million dollar liquid chromatography and mass spectrometry division. I built and manage a team of marketing professionals to consistently deliver successful marketing campaigns and product management initiatives that enable the division to meet and exceed the revenue and market growth targets. I have been able to drive my division to success through leveraging the breadth of Thermo Fisher Scientific by working with other divisions to co-market products that create effective productivity solutions for our customers. Some of my responsibilities include creating global marketing strategies, working with the commercial team to increase sales, and organizing customer facing events that amplify my division’s presence in the industry. In addition, I represent my products at all tradeshows, internal and external meetings, to customers, the press, and market analysts.

  • Global Product Manager

    Thermo Fisher Scientific

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; TMO; Biotechnology industry)

    January 2003 — December 2007 (5 years )

    Global product management and marketing operations of a 30 million dollar liquid chromatography division composed of three product lines encompassing twenty five hardware and software products and their consumables. I interfaced regularly with sales, customers, and R&D to create a product management strategy that produced growth for the product division. I steadily increased the sales and revenue of my products by creating strong and innovative branding, messaging, advertising, and marketing collateral. Among other responsibilities were budget management, forecasting, pricing, business and market analysis, and identifying new opportunities for product growth. I developed business and launch plans and directed all aspects of the global product launch of next generation and new products. Along with managing all external and strategic partnerships, I conducted global product sales training and global product seminars, and provided sales support for all three of my product lines.

    Thermo Fisher Scientific

    (Public Company; 10,001+ employees; TMO; Biotechnology industry)

    March 2002 — December 2002 (10 months)

    As a Global Marketing Product Specialist I provided application and sales support for all of the High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) instrumentation and software. I provided hands-on training to employees, customers, and dealers. I developed application notes that increased awareness of the features and benefits of the HPLC instrumentation.

  • Research and Development Chemist II

I regularly consulted with a team of scientists and engineers in the development of current and new products to suit our clients’ needs.
