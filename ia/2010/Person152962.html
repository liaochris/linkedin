Neil Rhein’s Summary

I provide writing and editorial services to financial services companies and other businesses, specializing in newsletters, whitepapers, articles, website copy, and shareholder communications. I also provide email marketing expertise through the Constant Contact Business Partner program.

I launched Bull's-eye Communications in 2005, after a 15-year career at Fidelity Investments. I’m based in the Boston area, but work with clients across the U.S.

I operate as an extension of my clients’ marketing department by helping them complete communications projects on time and on budget.

My clients are usually overworked marketing professionals looking for a communications professional who can get the job done without a lot of handholding. Current and past clients include Fidelity Investments, AARP Financial, Ameriprise, Fremont Bank, and JP Morgan, among others.

As a 20+-year veteran of the financial services industry, I’m experienced in virtually any topic related to financial services. I’m a hands-on guy, so depending on the size of the project, I either write the copy myself, or tap into my team of freelancers. My clients outsource projects to me with the full confidence their communications will get the attention they deserve. I also collaborate with two graphic designers who have extensive experience in this industry.

To learn more about my services, visit www.bullseyecommunications.net or simply give me a call at 774-719-2324.

When not taking care of my clients, I take advantage of every opportunity to play basketball, golf, tennis, ride my bike, hike, or enjoy any activity that gets me outdoors. I am also active my hometown of Mansfield, Massachusetts as Chairman of Keep Mansfield Beautiful (www.keepmansfieldbeautiful.com).

Neil Rhein’s Specialties:

Customized newsletters, articles, whitepapers, case studies, mutual fund shareholder reports, and editorial project management. I specialize in topics related to personal finance, investing, or the financial services industry. This includes retirement and college planning, mutual funds, stocks, bonds, insurance, charitable giving, employee benefits, and institutional brokerage, among others. I also welcome opportunities to write on other topics, especially environmental or “green” issues.
