Lisa Pike’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    ScribeRight Transcription Agency

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    1999 — Present (11 years )

    Lisa Pike, President and CEO of ScribeRight Transcription Agency, is an accomplished networking guru and entrepreneur. She doesn’t just see a need and fill it, she has a gift for connecting people and businesses who need each other, often forming long-lasting and successful relationships! She discovered her passion for business when she was 7 years old and made Christmas tree ornaments with her sister. Her sister hung hers on the tree, but Lisa sold hers to the neighbors. In her search for a business to build, Lisa came across a screaming need for quality medical recording and discovered that there was not only a high demand for it, but that a great deal of it was not being done well. Lisa took the time to become educated in the field and to work for the largest medical transcription company in the country before she formed ScribeRight, and then she built it from scratch to a million dollar company within the first 3 years. 10 years later ScribeRight continues to grow and has set standards in the industry to help health care facilities reduce their headaches, increase their bottom lines and stay out of courtrooms. The agency has expanded to provide transcription services to businesses, government agencies and the media, building on her foundation of excellence in filling the needs of her clients. Lisa’s philosophy can be summed up in her favorite quote from Erma Bombeck, “When I stand before God at the end of my life, I would hope that I would have not a single bit of talent left and could say, ‘I used everything You gave me’.”
