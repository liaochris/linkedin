Joyce Weiss’s Summary

I'm the founder of Joyce Weiss Training and Development LLC, author of Full Speed Ahead and Take the Ride of Your Life! I am a communications & conflict resolution consultant who provides bold solutions to boost the bottom line to individuals and groups.

I help companies improve communication by using conflict management and brainstorming techniques. The SWOT analysis and team building activities increase employee motivation and productivity Based on my books Full Speed Ahead and Take the Ride of Your Life!, I uncover communication obstacles and bring them to the surface to be confronted and demolished. Finally, I show people how to maintain their now successful working environment through strategies that will continue to bring a healthy balance to the workplace and growth to their business.
