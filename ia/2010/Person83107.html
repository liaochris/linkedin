Scott Griggs’s Summary

Scott Griggs is a seasoned leader in business growth. His executive-level strengths are in global business development, regional operations and marketing and he has conducted business in over 90 countries. USA, Brazil and China are his business favorites. He is conversational in Portuguese and Spanish. Scott has delivered results for large and small organizations and is fluent in the vertical markets of the consumer products,industrial, and technology areas.

Through his business strategies companies have enjoyed greater returns, resolved crises and experienced the birth of new profit centers where there were none. As Vice President of Operations, Scott was responsible for all aspects of a $100 million per year global business – profit and loss, sales, marketing, applications engineering and subcontract manufacturing. Whether launching new products, supervising multi-site operations or increasing revenue through acquisitions, Scott has always been appreciated for his leadership.

Scott assignments include maintaining 20% annual growth and raising revenue to $100 million from $20 million through acquisitions.

Scott has held various senior level positions in operations, sales and marketing while previously employed by Bureau Veritas, Underwriters Laboratories, Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories, Honeywell and Invensys.
