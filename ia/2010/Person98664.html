Jimmy Ray Purser’s Summary

Jimmy Ray Purser is the technical co-host for Cisco’s TechWise and BizWise TV. His hands on technical experience, camera presence and ability to work with or without scripting on the fly, makes him the technical go-to guy for many of Cisco's video projects outside of TechWiseTV like Tech Tutor, Switching Kitchen, TIO, etc...Jimmy Ray also conducts advanced training for engineers across North America and Europe and regularly speaks at industry conferences such as VON, CeBIT, NetWorld+Interop, Networkers, RSA, VoiceCon, Comdex, HP World and numerous regional events.

He has been an active participant in the information technology community for more than 18 years. He is a member in the IEEE and the Ethernet Alliance. Jimmy Ray has designed, installed and tested numerous networks for Fortune 500 companies, the United States military, Internet-based businesses, universities and other education institutions around the world. He is a regular columnist for BMighty his column can be read here:
http://www.bmighty.com/columns/showAuthor.jhtml?authorID=purser

He is the "Geek to Geek" blogger for Network World which can be read here:
http://www.networkworld.com/community/blog/6690

He's "The Network Sheriff" blogger/mentor on the Cisco Learning Network which can be read here:
https://cisco.hosted.jivesoftware.com/blogs/network-sheriff

Purser holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from Southern Illinois University is currently pursuing a Master of Science degree in Electrical Engineering. He holds 3 U.S. Patents on for Ethernet security algorithms. He maintains the highest vendor certifications from Cisco, Microsoft, Novell, SCO, HP, ISC2, DOL, IBM and others. He is also a Honorably Discharged 9 year veteran of the United States Navy where he participated in Operations: Desert Shield, Desert Storm, Southern Watch and Restore Hope. GO NAVY!
