Alisa Cooper’s Summary

As an English professor at Glendale Community College, Alisa teaches hybrid and online freshman composition and literature courses. Alisa currently serves as the assistant chair for the English Department as eLearning coordinator and works with faculty to improve their online courses or to create them. She also is currently serving as interim instructional technologist for the Title V grant at PC. Previously Alisa served as podcasting specialist for the district providing a series of podcasting and vodcasting workshops throughout the year for the Maricopa Center for Learning and Instruction (MCLI). Although podcasting is still an interest, currently Alisa conducts workshops on mobile learning for the district and national conferences.
