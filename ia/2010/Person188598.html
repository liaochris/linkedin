Shelley Pearson’s Summary

My experience has been really 'hands on' on the operational side to business organisations. Since 2007 I have specifically focussed more on educating business owners on how to document and model their businesses to add value in the long term.

I run my own franchise consulting business and am particularly interested in centering on organisational communication and implementation of business vision. To me: consistency and a positive attitude to change are two of the most important skills a business leader can have.

My outlying motivation to get up in the morning is to be a catalyst for change for better leadership and employee job satisfaction.

Shelley Pearson’s Specialties:

Having worked predominantly in franchising in varying industries, I have an integral understanding of the complexity of this organisation type, as well as the effects of maintenance of the brand on both the franchisee and the franchisor. My focus tends to be on existing and start-up franchisors in trouble shooting their organisations to enhance their business model strength. I also spend a lot of my time educating small business owners on how to remove themselves from their business models.
