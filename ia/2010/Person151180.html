Nancy Burkart’s Summary

BrightLife Direct is your best source for all the leading brands of compression hosiery and support stockings on the web. We stock the full lines from Jobst, Mediven, Juzo and Sigvaris, plus our "value" brand Allegro. Not sure which brand to choose? Then take a look at our Brand Guide. It clearly explains the different lines of support hosiery offered by each manufacturer. And if you're still not sure what to order, pick-up the phone. Our friendly staff is ready to answer any of the questions you may have.

As we've grown over the years, so has the selection of products we offer. For people with lymphedema we have compression arm sleeves, gloves, and gauntlets from Juzo, Mediven, Jobst and LympheDiva's. Arthritis sufferers will love the new Therall products from FLA Orthopedics. For individuals with sensitive feet or diabetes, we have diabetic socks from Jobst, Juzo, SmartKnit and Allegro. And finally we recently started carrying Orthaheel footwear. Their popular flip flops and sandals realign your foot with a built in orthotic, relieving foot and leg pain.
