Tamara Nicholl-Smith’s Summary

Tamara Nicholl-Smith is a Management Consultant possessing extensive communications, relationship building, project management, business analysis, and behavior change management experience with leading companies in the international energy, dot.com, software, non-profit, and financial industries. Her success arises through a blend of innovating processes and solutions while consistently integrating with client teams to successfully deliver sound business solutions. Certified in Information Mapping, Tamara also brings over a decade of experience in technical communication and consulting which includes the development of context sensitive e-learning/help modules, training materials, one-on-one and small group training, policy and procedure documents, as well as voice-over work. Tamara was also the project creator/spokesperson for a downtown renewal public arts project in Albuquerque, NM for which she received recognition from the mayor’s office.

Tamara Nicholl-Smith’s Specialties:

Change & Project Management, Business Development and Strategy, Information Management, Information Development, Technical Communication, Process Improvement, Work Flow Management, international energy industry, public spokesperson, voice-over.

    (Civic & Social Organization industry)

    December 1998 — June 2000 (1 year 7 months)

    Conceptualized, pitched, and implemented downtown public arts project in the context of Albuquerque's downtown renewal effort.

    Project Description: The Rhyme and Meter Project was conceptualized as a way to bring poetry and art together in the public realm using the parking meter as the channel. Excerpts of poems by well-known and local poets were affixed to certain meters in downtown on a plaque or by being directly painted on. Then the meter housing and pole were painted by local artists as an illustration of the poem. Three separate projects were completed in a two year period encompassing over 25 meters. Themes included: The Dignity of the Human Spirit, Dialogue, and Work.
