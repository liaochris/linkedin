Eric Bescak’s Summary

I have recently accepted the position of the Americas Marketing Manager at GE Healthcare Lunar, the bone densitometry market leader. I have 8 years of professional experience acquiring the following skills:

STRATEGIC MARKETING: Developing and executing advanced, closed-looped, and permission-based marketing strategies
EVENT MARKETING: Overseeing tradeshow events.
WRITING: Published work includes articles, case studies, webpage and brochure content
EDITING: Strong grasp of effective grammar and style, completed copyediting training at the Graham School
WEBPAGE DEVELOPMENT, MANAGEMENT: Managed pages, improved search engine results by optimizing metadata and other best practices; Developed web sites for small businesses
MEDICAL MARKET TRENDS: Researched trends for medical imaging, women’s health
DATABASE MANAGEMENT: Created databases, utilizing data management best practices

In my marketing positions, I have developed both creatively and analytically to strengthen GE's marketing and sales. I created case studies, copy and design for brochures and advertisements, and oversaw their production and placement. In this role, I enabled a shift in the messaging from the company’s historically product-focused approach to content and design that appeals directly to consumer needs.

I developed analytically by reporting on GE's business performance. I brought a closed-loop marketing discipline to the department and improved its ability to link its efforts to sales with more accurate reporting practices using dependable data. I was able to identify trends in the market and new areas for improvement in website and print advertising.

I am a keen problem-solver, problem-finder and communicator with the ability to effectively work independently and in a team. I am diligent with balanced professionalism.
