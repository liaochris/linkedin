Don Hollis’s Summary

Don believes great design improves lives, elevates experiences and amplifies stories worth telling.

His company; Hollis Brand Culture, innovates lifestyle brand strategies and identity development for Hospitality, Retail and Technology clients. We build engaging brand solutions across print, interactive and architectural environments.

Don's professional affiliations include: Museum of Photographic Arts - Board Trustee, SEZIO Advisory Board, American Institute of Graphic Arts - AIGA, San Diego President Emeritus and High Tech Marketing Alliance - HTMA VP of Brand Communications. Don serves the creative community as a leader, author, speaker and judge for juried exhibitions.

Don Hollis’s Specialties:

Brand and Identity Design, Logomarks, Print, Packaging, Web, Interactive and Environmental Graphics (sign programs, wayfinding, district branding, exhibition design), for lifestyle brands, products and services in hospitality, retail and technology sectors.

Don is an innovative solution provider and passionate industry leader recognized for continued design excellence.
