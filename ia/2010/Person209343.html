Marco Carbajo’s Summary

Marco Carbajo is a Business Credit Specialist with over 18 years experience in the business and consumer credit industry. As an entrepreneur he has successfully built two multi-million dollar companies in a few short years.

He is founder of the Business Credit Insider’s Circle, the nation’s leading online business credit community for small business owners providing business credit consulting, training, resources, support and private access to vendor credit lines, cash credit sources, and financing.

He is a weekly columnist for Dun & Bradstreet Small Business Solutions, a business credit blogger for All Business & American Express Small Business and author of ‘Eight Steps to Ultimate Business Credit’ and ‘How to Build Business Credit with No Personal Guarantee’. His articles, blogs, and e-zines are featured in ‘The Scotsman Guide’, ‘Alltop’, ‘Entrepreneur Connect’, and ‘Active Rain’.

His vision is to empower small business owners with the much needed support and resources necessary to build business credit separate from personal credit plus have access to the necessary funding sources to grow, operate, or invest in their businesses.

This was the birth of the ‘Business Credit Insider’s Circle’

His motto is ‘Make Building Business Credit Simple, Easy, Effective and Affordable!’

Marco says “Small Business is what drives our economy and they are the lifeblood of the American Dream!”
