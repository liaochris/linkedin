Patrick Ross’s Summary

Thirty years in advertising, marketing and public relations in a senior management capacity, working with leading transnational blue chip client brands across European, Middle Eastern and Asia-Pacific markets with extensive experience pioneering AOR centralized media planning and buying operations across multiple markets, a thorough knowledge of traditional and e-marketing/e-commerce concepts and effective project management skills.

Seasoned executive with direct P&L responsibility, have measurably contributed to increased revenues and earnings for each of my employers to date. Track record of leading teams to produce exceptional results with proven ability to drive in-market success through innovation. Culturally aware, with twenty years of experience in Asia and eight in the Middle East.

Co-founded and currently manage my own company, EnvironMedia LLC, producing solar powered 3-stream recycling stations, bus shelters, lamppost signage and media portals for worldwide export.

Patrick Ross’s Specialties:

Branding and corporate identity. International media relations. Company start-up structuring. Experienced in negotiating and writing agency vendor contracts, remunerations (commission and fee structures) and performance review procedures.
