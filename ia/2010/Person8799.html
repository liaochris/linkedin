Amanda Brown’s Summary

I started my career with the Finance Department of Ford Europe having completed a BA in Accountancy and an MSc in Management Science at Imperial College, London.

I then spent 11 years in the City working for Barings and Phillips & Drew Fund Management as an Investment Manager. Subsequently, I set up my own independent consultancy advising investment companies on the application of new technology.

A move to the countryside, a passion for gardening and a year studying Landscape and Garden Design, led me to establishing a landscaping business in 1997 which I have recently merged with Hollywell Landscapes.

At Alterra we work on strategy and planning for professional service companies developing social media policies and strategies, LinkedIn strategy and training and co-ordinating social media marketing.

Amanda Brown’s Specialties:

Business planning, consulting for change, consulting for growth, cost reduction, LinkedIn strategy applications for business to business and Social Media Policy consultancy. Our clients are predominantly in Accounting, Legal and Financial Services in south east England and London.

Registered with Business Link Supplier Brokerage Service.
