Douglas Miller’s Summary

SOCIAL CURATOR | CONTENT GENERATOR | PERSPECTIVE SHIFTER-ATOR:

My professional experience and goals converge exactly where media (digital video and beyond) meets the social web.

I've worked in media since the days of 3/4" U-Matic and 16mm film and make every effort to stay in the loop of where media is headed in the digital future.

I've been a part of "Social Media" since the days of the dial up BBS (thanks, Dad) and marvel at how far it has come. I take great pride and pleasure from devising social media strategies that solve real world problems with digital world solutions.

I believe that there is no such thing as "Social Media" there is only "Digital Media" and all such media is inherently social. More than anything, I look forward each day to the emergence of community coordinated information distribution - I love that people I like send me stuff I think is cool every day.

Final Cut Pro Studio Proficiency with Adobe Creative Suite, Visual Studio, and SEO/SEM experience have positioned my skills in such a way as to both lead and execute complex multi-media tasks.

My areas of interest currently involve coordinating communication via digital platforms strategically to maximize social relevance and user engagement, but mostly I just think digital media technologies could bring about a new age of global peace and prosperity if used correctly - like music, the Olympics or soybeans.
