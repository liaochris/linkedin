Robin W. Foster, Esq.’s Summary

In 2008 I joined Sterling Commerce (an AT&T Company), a vendor of B2B software & technology solutions, as a commercial contracts manager for a team of negotiators/attorneys in the general counsel's office. [IBM purchased Sterling in August '10, thus I joined IBM.]

From 2002-'08 I was employed as a solo practitioner in Cincinnati, representing small & mid-cap companies and independent professionals in a wide range of commercial transactions.

From 2000-'02 I was an Associate Attorney, Intellectual Property Group, @ Frost Brown Todd (fka Frost & Jacobs, an AmLaw 200 firm).

Prior to becomming a licensed attorney I worked for 10+ years in various sales and management roles in the paper and packaging industry.

As an entrepreneur, from 2000 to 2004, I founded and ran a small company providing specialized pharmaceutical packaging solutions.

I am an honors graduate of Salmon P Chase College of Law (magna cum laude, 2000). I received my undergraduate degree in 1989 from Miami University (Oxford, OH), and have been admitted to practice law since 2000 (Ohio #0072694).

I have substantial experience in a wide range of practice areas, including:

- Drafting & negotiation of commercial contracts and software/technology agreements; SaaS (hosted) applications; and network, professional, and managed services (outsourcing/ BPO);

- NDAs & independent contractor agreements;

- Counseling for Internet/eCommerce law; web site development & ICANN disputes; content licensing; compliance with FTC, state & international privacy/data security laws and best practices audits, e.g., ISO 27001-27002;

- Employment disputes & litigation (wrongful discharge; non-compete & separation agreements)

- Intellectual Property law;

- Arbitrations & Mediations;

- Corporate governance; formation of business entities (corporations, partnerships, LLCs, etc);

- Wills/Living Wills/Health Care POAs (estates below the $2 Million estate tax exemption threshold for 2008).

Robin W. Foster, Esq.’s Specialties:

Internet/eCommerce law, technology law, software licensing, SaaS/hosted applications, commercial contracts, intellectual property, privacy & data security law, employment law
