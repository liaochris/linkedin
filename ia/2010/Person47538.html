Danielle Powers’s Summary

Danielle Powers has been an account manager with The Employment Store for 5 years held the position of General Manager for over three years. She has received her Bachelor’s Degree from State University of New York at Brockport and has an extensive background in staffing and recruiting as well as in client development. Prior to joining The Employment Store, Danielle was a product manager for a national healthcare staffing firm where she was in charge of the Upstate NY region.

Danielle has experience in client management and development, staffing and recruiting for businesses of 5 - 5,000 employees from Fortune 100 companies to high demand start ups while complying with local, State and Federal regulatory requirements.

Working with Danielle you will always have consistent and regular feedback on your positions and the candidates she and the team are working on. A recent board member of NHRA (National Human Resource Association), 2009 Recipient of RWN Up&Coming Business Woman Award, and a constant networker, Danielle has incredible access to those difficult to find, passive candidates.

Seven years of recruitment and business development experience with a focus on creating business partnerships and recruitment solutions I currently am the overall manager of Recruitment and Business Development of an incredible team of professionals.

I consistently align myself with individuals who are forward thinking and focused on what matters most and that is the people and the culture that build, grow and maintain our business community.

With a strong emphasis on finding the right match for you and your team I encourage you to connect with me; if you have any Staffing or Recruitment Questions please feel free to give me a call or shoot me a note.

All the best success to you!

Feel free to contact me at dpowers@employmentstore.com or 232-4880 x114
