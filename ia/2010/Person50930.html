David Hesse’s Summary

I am an experienced IT-Support-Engineer with 7 years experience working for different Blue Chip Companies including IBM, U.S. Robotics, Novell, Hewlett Packard, LANDesk Software and recently VMware in a variety of roles and I have also 2 years experience in Software Localization Testing. My main areas of expertize are technical support for hardware, Windows and Linux Operating Systems, a variety of business software, TCP/IP networks, IP and Fibre Channel Storage and Virtualization Software from VMware. In my roles, I have worked on a number of projects with a wide variation in client and customer areas with emphasis of providing quality customer service and effective troubleshooting. I am constantly widening my knowledge and I am trying to improve my work patterns every day to deliver a high quality product AND good customer service. I constantly received good feedback from my customers and from some of my Managers. I achieved the VMware Certified Professional accreditation and now am preparing myself for the Linux Professional Institute and/or the RHCT/RHCE Certification later this year to differentiate myself and increase my value in the job market.
