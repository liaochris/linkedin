Julie Kademiya’s Summary

I have a strong six-year background working in a team and independently to successfully manage teams, budgets, timelines, business goals and new project initiatives. I am skilled in the areas of marketing, public relations, communications, project management, business development, and community outreach. My entrepreneurial spirit and self motivation allowed me to start a successful event planning company by conducting market research, analysis, business modeling and development plans. I have expertise in identifying opportunities for profitability improvement through budgeting and strategic planning. Additionally, I have a proven track record of working in a team and individually to lead business development campaigns to success.

In addition, my multicultural background led me to develop a global point of view. Through extensive travel and study abroad I had enhanced my cultural awareness and sensitivity to customs and cultural differences. In addition, living and learning abroad led me to gain awareness of global economic and political issues and realities. By being active in international communities I had expanded my skills of cross-cultural communication and knowledge of international protocols of business etiquette and business casual. Additionally, I have an advanced background in Russian and Eastern European languages, culture and community.

I received my education at the University of Denver where I graduated with a BA degree in International Studies and Business Administration. Additionally, I took business and management courses during my year abroad at Oxford Brooks University in Oxford, UK.
