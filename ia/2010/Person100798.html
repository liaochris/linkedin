Joel Goldstein’s Summary

Joel Goldstein, nominated at age 24 for SCORE entrepreneur of the year, is a professional speaker, author and consultant. He has been featured on television, newspapers and magazines as an expert in Internet marketing strategies. He adopts a “Work Hard, Play Harder” lifestyle approach that was guided by role models such as Rolf Potts and Tim Ferriss..He runs a multinational firm from wireless locations worldwide, and has been a popular guest lecturer at University of Central Florida, where he presents entrepreneurship as a tool for ideal lifestyle design and world change..

Joel is an active philanthropist for education and has dedicated his time to organizations such as Junior Achievement, Big Brothers Big Sisters, and DonorsChoose.org – an educational non-profit and winner at Fast Company’s 2008 Social Capitalist Awards.

Since his debut presentation on Social Media for Professionals in Winter Park Florida, Joel has been invited to the masters’ program at Harvard School of Business to pursue a higher education. With a certificate in management from the Wharton School of Business, Joel has been able to apply managerial principles from classroom to boardroom.
