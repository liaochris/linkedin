Brendan Mitchell’s Summary

I'm currently driving www.nopaper.com, a business hell bent on creating solutions to facilitate in your daily handling of information and dramatically reducing paper wastage specifically in the live events industry & corporate environments. We plan to do this by creating convenient solutions that enrich usage of current technologies & communications. Phase 1 of no paper is currently in beta, with a public release due in 2010. You can follow the development of nopaper at www.twitter.com/nopaper

Imagine a future with no paper™

I have 2 other companies plus a non-profit currently in very early development, but these are taking a back shelf as I concentrate on no paper.

My background:
Two decades in the Digital Media industry, with experience of onscreen graphic creation for motion pictures (inc Golden Eye, Tomorrow Never Dies, Da Vinci Code, United 93 to name a few), B2B & B2C websites, GUI design and corporate identity. Specializing in Online Branding, Brand Alignment & Digital Brand Protection. I have founded & built several companies, the first in Singapore in 95, and vision360 being one of the most recent in the UK, which became the UK Division of the Swedish based Cordovan Group in 2007. I stepped down as a Director of Cordovan in March 2009.

Brendan Mitchell’s Specialties:

Founding & Building Companies, Interdisciplinarity, Future & Trend Watching, Web 2.0, Branding & Identity, Digital Brand Protection, Interface Design, Digital Media, Corporate & Live Events, Corporate Blogs, Mobile Applications, Social Media.
