Karen du Four des Champs’s Summary

Change Management, Organizational Development, Learning & Performance, HR Business Partner. Connects organizational redesign & development initiatives to strategic business goals. Takes a macro approach (organizational development, leadership development) to implement micro best practices and changes (HR, performance improvement, change management). Adept in diagnosing barriers to organizational effectiveness. Natural talent for building trust and rapport quickly (counseling background), combined with a judicious manner of asking questions that lead to strategic thinking and problem-solving (legal advocate, HR, OD experience) with a strong desire to see others succeed (results-driven, management experience). Tactical practitioner (HR generalist, employee relations), and strategic business partner. From HPT/front-end gap analysis, to learning design & blended solutions (OTJ, classroom training, online learning, job aids, coaching) to facilitating high stakes meetings & creating long-term strategies to address corporate change and improve performance. Acknowledged by clients and colleagues for strong organization, innovation and creativity; with an adaptive, goal-oriented work style characterized by high standards, flexibility, and EQ. Track record of managing projects on time, on budget, netting tangible results.Perennial learner keeping abreast of new theories, methodologies, current trends, & best practices. Thrives in culturally diverse & global environments; experience working in Malaysia, Costa Rica, Kenya. Passionate about stakeholder engagement. A PR natural. Perceptive listener. Creative thinker. Strategic advisor. Heart of an advocate. ENTP.
