Steve Frey’s Summary

Experienced architect and designer interested in collaborating with others in creating buildings and places which contribute positively to our communities while taking care of our environment and generations to come.
Registered Architect (Colorado) and LEED ® AP with substantial experience in green architecture and planning in commercial, public, development and residential sectors.
Broad experience in all aspects of early stages of design, permitting, programming, schematic design through construction. Greatest strengths are in front end of design process, pre-visualization and collaboration with owner and design team.
Particularly interested in workspace design and the cultivation of dynamic, mission driven interior environments fully integrated with their surroundings.
Enjoy creative learning process with clients and team members with complex organizations seeking to develop and evolve during times of growth and change.

Steve Frey’s Specialties:

Quick and collaborative design process with clients, Strategic thinking and creative direction, design charettes, masterplanning and site assessment, climate responsive design, design presentation and graphics skills, architectural illustrations and renderings
