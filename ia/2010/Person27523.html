Brendan Wlas’s Summary

A hands on Senior Performance Test Consultant/Lead/Manager, I have been involved in all phases of the performance testing lifecycle. My projects have ranged in size and I am comfortable leading teams or operating alone.

My experience has been in the Finance (Banking and Insurance), Oil & Gas and Retail sectors and I have worked on a number of large-scale global transformational programmes.

I have been heavily involved in quality assurance process development and as such I have the ability to build a team and embed new (or improve existing) processes. I have worked using a number of models (onshore, offshore, outsourced) and believe in choosing an approach that is fit for purpose.

I have experience in the load & stress testing of different technologies including SAP (Portal, NetWeaver, BI/BW, ECC/R/3, CRM, APO, EBP/SRM, PI/XI, KM, TREX), eCommerce (ATG, Oracle PIM, Sterling OMS, Endeca), Microsoft (.NET, MVC, SSIS, MRS), Database (Oracle, SQL Server), Sabrix, Informatica, J2EE (IBM WebSphere, JBoss), Identity Management (Tivoli Access Manager WebSEAL), Siebel, Citrix

I am very experienced in the HP (Mercury) toolset with specialisation in LoadRunner and Performance Centre (including SiteScope). I have competence with WinRunner, QuickTest Professional and Quality Centre. I have experience using other diagnostic tools including CA Wily Introscope.

I hold the following qualifications:
- ISEB Foundation in Software Testing
- Mercury Interactive Certified Product Specialist (LoadRunner)

I am currently working as an independent contractor in central London. I’m always looking for interesting opportunities so feel free to get in touch!
