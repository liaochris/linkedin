John Humphrey’s Summary

Pariveda Solutions is a technology consulting firm that helps companies solve their most complex business challenges through process and technology. From IT Strategy to the creation and implementation of custom technology solutions, Pariveda delivers on time and on budget.

As Co-Founder and Chairman of the Board of Pariveda Solutions, Inc. Mr. Humphrey has been instrumental in guiding the company and assisting our clients since inception.

Mr. Humphrey has over twenty-five years of experience in business operations and technology working around enterprise-wide solutions in services, manufacturing and distribution. Mr. Humphrey has saved his clients millions of dollars in both software license costs and implementation efficiencies. He has extensive background in both selling and implementing Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems for companies like Lawson and Oracle. While at Tactica Technology Group, he built an Oracle implementation practice that was profitable within the first year of operation. As an Oracle practice leader, he as a deep understanding of the pitfalls often encountered implementing ERP without proper planning.

He continues to support several distribution companies with their technology planning and IT support. More recently, he has been working with services companies and large IT shops as they tackle the challenge of managing intellectual assets and the need for business analytics in real time.

Mr. Humphrey received a B.A. in Economics and a B.B.A. in Finance from Southern Methodist University in 1984. Mr. Humphrey also received an M.B.A. from The Cox School of Business at Southern Methodist University in 1990. He serves on the advisory board for the Cox School of Business at Southern Methodist and is on the advisory board for the D/FW IT Resource Group. He serves as Chairman of the Board of Pariveda Solutions, Inc and is Chairman of the Board of Trustees at Pantego Christian Academy located in Arlington, Texas.
