Ashish Deodhar’s Summary

Give a multi-million pounds budget and backing of a major corporation and anyone could build a brand! I believe that brand management is most fun when resources are limited, brands largely non-existent, and staff (and even management) usually marketing illiterate! This is the stage where a brand manager’s skills, ability, patience, knowledge and business understanding are truly tested. I am one who loves to take on such challenges, nay, relish in them!

For more than 6 years, I have serviced, managed, and helped build many such brands from diverse industries and in different geographies. Brands are my passion and building them from scratch is my obsession. So if you have similar ambitions for your brands, I think we should talk!
