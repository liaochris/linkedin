Philip Greenwood’s Summary

My consulting work:

* Do you have an ailing project that needs fixing?

* Do you have a project that absolutely must succeed?

* Are you tired of the daily grind of "projects as usual"?

* Do you have skills gaps in your project team that need attention?

* Is your project trying to deliver to an organisation with "change fatigue"?

These are my areas of expertise, and the challenges that I thrive on - Project Leadership - and I specialise in enterprise transformation projects, particularly with IT elements.

Project Leadership is related to Project Management, but with many important distinctions. After some 20 years of consulting across multiple industries, around the world and in a diverse set of projects, I started looking at what made projects work: How the stellar projects succeed, and how the doggerel projects fail.

The research took years, and the results are astounding! Through the use of a diverse set of performance psychology techniques,I am able to bring these insights to bear on your project and for your organization.

Let me share a secret: Many projects fail! Over 50% in some sectors. And the single biggest risk of projects is the "people" element - risks can be mitigated inexpensively!

Beaufortes offers project leadership, project leadership facilitation and coaching. Our objective in these offerings is to enhance the approaches and actions of the project team, including the project leaders, so that they grow and use a set of "project behaviours" that lead to the high performance of the project - and future projects to come.

Philip Greenwood’s Specialties:

Perhaps you have experienced when a team or an organization is totally engaged in making a change happen? Do you understand the benefit of people who are so truly "on-board" with the goal that there is enthusiasm, motivation and a natural alignment of actions? That is what I can help you achieve!
