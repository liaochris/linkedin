Jackie Borchardt’s Summary

I cover education (preschool through college) in Casper and Wyoming. I'm responsible for finding and planning stories from idea to research and reporting to layout. I write breaking news and story updates for Trib.com and keep a blog, The Report Card, to discuss local, state and national education. I have also written for the business, sports and outdoors sections.

In past lives, I edited international features for a magazine and education stories for a daily newspaper. I've captured and edited photos, audio and video. I’ve worked with InDesign, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Wordpress, Soundslides, Final Cut Pro. What I’ve learned is that the software and hardware don’t matter so much as having an open mind to try things.

My interests are varied and topics I've covered include post-traumatic stress disorder in post-Katrina New Orleans, college rodeo, the state Legislature, the incredible home of artist James Michalopoulos, county government elections and outdoor ice skating.

I am available for freelance work in Wyoming, Colorado and the other great western states.
