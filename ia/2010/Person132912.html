Mario Coryell’s Summary

Superior sales and marketing results have been my hallmark. I assist existing and early stage companies in developing branding and distribution strategies for their products and services.

This includes differentiating their offerings from the competition, determining the most viable markets and the appropriate strategies to successfully compete in those markets. I focus on the actual sales process down to lead generation strategies as well as the actual sale presentation and how to respond to objections in order to actually close the sale.

My background includes broad experience in the Securities, Banking, Mortgage Banking, Real Estate and Insurance industries as well as portfolio management.

My strength is developing and overseeing the implementation of business plans for new ventures as the principals often have great ideas but they need assistance in coordinating the many aspects of operating a business that they have not experienced before.

Mario Coryell’s Specialties:

Investment analysis, new business development, marketing/sales strategy development and structuring of debt/equity in investments and acquisitions.
