Edward Safaryan’s Summary

Edward Safaryan graduated from University of Delaware in 2004 with Masters Degree in Environmental and Energy Policy. Prior to this, he obtained BSc and MSc degrees from State Engineering University of Armenia. Edward has a comprehensive work experience with the energy industry in Armenia and the US, as well as with government strengthening programs. Currently, Edward is employed as Programs Manager at the National Competitiveness Foundation of Armenia.
He is the author of a series of publications on energy security and renewable energy issues.
