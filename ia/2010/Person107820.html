What Specific Outcomes to Expect from Two Month's of Judy's Coaching

1. You will complete a model chapter or more with Judy's chapter blueprint of fast-writing with only one edit. After that, it's easy to write natural, engaging copy that flows well, making your book the best book because it's compelling and easy to read.

2. You will know, write, and follow Judy's pre-marketing plan called "The Essential Nine Hot Selling Points." to speak directly to your best audience in you book. These include your best benefits, audience, and title. With this pre-marketing know-how, you can start selling the minute your book finished.When your audience knows you care specifically about them, they will finish each chapter, recommend it to their associates, and become your 24/7 sales team.

3. You will get the big picture of self-publishing including Print on Demand (POD), eBook publishing, or author print book publishing with a little help from different professionals. You will get educated on pros and cons of traditional publishing. You'll know if you need an ISBN number or not.

4. You can finish your short book when you do the high level activities Judy recommends to be on the fast track to publishing success.

5. You will save more time and money than the coaching sessions because you won't make expensive time and money mistakes.

Just know that your book dream will manifest when you choose a dedicated and wise partner.
