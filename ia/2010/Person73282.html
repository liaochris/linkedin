Gary Young’s Summary

We help companies source products and services in China. Our company headquarters is in Houston TX and our Representative office is in Shanghai China.

Avela Corporation offers the best possible client services and opportunities in China because we have systems, procedures and the right people in place, in China, that foster success.

I believe you must be fully transparent to maximize trust, synergies and planning for China. I strongly believe that every corporation should develop of a strong China policy as part of it's strategic plan. China is either a source for products and services, a vast market or a significant competitor. Sometimes all three.

Client thoughts that we address:
• “I know my competitors are already working in China” “I need to be there too.”

• “China has become an attractive market that I need to consider.”

• “Considering China, I worry about quality, consistency and communication.”

• “I have been reading how Chinese companies have invested significant resources that I can take advantage of.”

• “I thought I hit a wall until I realized that China offers a way to not only cut cost, but allow me to expand without investing in additional infrastructure.”

• “I can see China playing a major role in growing my business; but up till now, my efforts in addressing China have fallen short.”

• “Overseas competition, slowly eating away at my margins, indicates that business is becoming global.”

• “I can initiate important projects, on hold because of resource restraints, using the resources Chinese companies can offer."

• “China has given me a way to repurpose my present workforce to higher and better use.”

• “To of the major obstacles for working in China is the customs and logistics hassles.”

• “I am hesitant to work in China because of a lack of control”
