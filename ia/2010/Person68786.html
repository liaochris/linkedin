Steve Davis’s Summary

Own and operate FacilitatorU.com, a virtual university offering training, tools and resources for group leaders. I design and deliver live and virtual trainings in the areas of virtual facilitation, learning facilitation, meeting facilitation, facilitative presenting, and integral facilitation and collaboration. I train, coach, and assist people with their business and group leadership challenges, speakers who want to better engage their audiences, and trainers who want to be more facilitative.

Key Offerings:

- Meeting Transformation. My latest book, "This Meeting Sux: 12 Acts of Courage to Change Meetings for Good," offers basic leadership skills to a unique audience: meeting participants frustrated with their meetings. This book offers them tools to do something about it. We lalso offer private-branding and reseller oportunities for facilitators, coaches, and consultants. http://ThisMeetingSux.com

- Training Teleclass and Virtual Meeting Facilitators. I offer an integral approach to facilitating virtual training and meetings.
http://Facilitatoru.com/teleclass_facilitator.html

- FacilitatorU.com offers the largest online collections of tools, resources, and training in facilitative leadership, much of which you can learn without leaving your office.

- Teleclass trainings, coaching, resource guides, expert interviews, articles and other resources to help you effectively lead and facilitate groups.

- Lifetime memberships offering total access to all our resources.

- Licenses and group rates for corporations.

- Onsite training and kickoff sessions to get your groups aligned for results.

- Executive coaching for skill building and enhanced results.

- Experience a free sample with our Facilitative Leadership Ecourse at http://www.FacilitatorU.com
