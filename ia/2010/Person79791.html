Hansel Luis’s Summary

Experienced executive with an extensive background in the service parts industry. Over 10 years experience in the operational implementation of repair services, supply chain, logistics, and related software development to support major client engagements with consumer electronics retailers, insurance underwriters, service companies, and dealers. Excel at consultative selling, building relationships, and creating customized programs that mold to the client’s requirements. Able to comprehend complex business models and processes, filter the information and provide comprehensive knowledge to technical and non-technical personnel. Involved in industry and professional organizations.

Major accomplishments include the project coordination of a .NET platform ERP system for a major parts provider able to integrate to multiple accounting systems. The ERP system incorporated modules for inventory management, product repair tracking, warehouse management, reporting tools, whole unit depot repair tracking, and on-site labor management systems. Other accomplishments include securing a major contract for notebook depot repair that generated over $2.5M annually and managing relationships with Fortune 100 companies.

Hansel Luis’s Specialties:
