Cita Walsh’s Summary

Visit http://www.citawalsh.com to see medical marketing case studies for medical device, instrument and diagnostic products that I have branded, marketed, and launched.

Medical marketing executive with broad experience in strategic marketing, product life cycle management and marketing operations. Cita leverages her knowledge of customer behaviors and selling cycles to create integrated marketing and interactive solutions that ignite sales. Areas of expertise include marketing communications, branding, social media, practice development and consumer pull-through programs. Extensive experience leading internal teams and agencies in the creation of collateral, web initiatives and advertising as well as sales training, PR and direct response programs. Proven ability to work with sales, regulatory, clinical, and legal affairs to commercialize products.

Cita Walsh’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing, product positioning, marketing operations, product development, marketing communications, branding, advertising, consumer pull-through, practice development, and the development of sales promotions, websites, collateral, trade shows, direct response and training programs. Expertise in agency & vendor management, internal creative teams, web 2.0, social media, business planning, product marketing, and team building.
