Jack Shaw’s Summary

For over 20 years, provided expert guidance and best practices to Fortune 500 Companies (including General Electric, Coca Cola, Allstate, PacBell and many more) as well as providing advisory services to their key technology suppliers (including IBM, SAP, Oracle and others). A visionary technology and business strategist, I've spent my career leading companies in numerous industries to better, more efficient ways of conducting and operating their businesses.

Widely recognized for expertise in advanced internet-based business technologies such as e-Commerce, e-Procurement, Cost Management, Process Improvement, Supply Chain Virtualization, Intelligent Systems, Enterprise Decision Management, Business Intelligence, Business Rules Management Systems & Cloud Computing.

Marketing Strategist, Industry thought leader, and noted Author of three critically acclaimed books and numerous White Papers. Founder and Editor of the e-Commerce Executive newsletter and of the EDI Buyer’s Guide. A charismatic speaker, has delivered more than 1000 paid keynote addresses and executive presentations in more than 20 countries.

The advisory services of the Management Consulting Firms I founded, Breakthrough Business Technologies and e-Commerce Strategies, have saved corporations billions of dollars over the last twenty years by enabling them to implement more efficient processes and by leveraging technologies positioning them to compete better in today’s market and in the future. Keen perception and vision into emerging technologies and business practices have also enabled IT Suppliers to build products and services that anticipate where the market is headed thus generating hundreds of millions of dollars in revenues for those organizations.

Uniquely talented at translating business technology into business value propositions. C-Level Executive credibility and dynamic delivery accelerate new business through the sales cycle.
