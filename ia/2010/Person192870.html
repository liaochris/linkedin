Surinder P. Singh’s Summary

CEO / CFO with 28 years of wide-ranging domestic and foreign experience in the Retail Financial Services Sector, excelling in analytical, problem-solving and management skills. Significant experience in business turn-around, growing profitability, restructuring and identifying, analyzing and negotiating acquisitions and divestitures.

• As CEO, developed and executed a turnaround plan for Citi’s retail bank and consumer lending businesses in the United Kingdom that included management team staffing and development, integration, restructuring, closing of facilities, significant credit policy and process changes, business process re-engineering and an acquisition, which resulted in year-over-year improvements of $100 million.
• Successfully implemented the consolidation of consumer finance operations in Western and Central Europe into the existing retail banks, which resulted in annualized savings of $25 million.
• Developed and implemented an operations and technology convergence plan for all products in Europe, including end-to-end set up of a back-office and customer call center in Barcelona, which saved Citi over $50 million per year.
• Identified and divested under-performing assets, which increased the Division’s profitability by 45% and reduced the number of loss making entities from nine to four in two years.
• Managed the de-novo expansion of Citi’s retail financial presence in Central and Eastern Europe, including Poland, Hungary, Czech Republic, and Egypt.
• Developed and implemented a robust self-assessment process in Central & Eastern Europe, which resulted in 100% successful audits and a broader implementation across the Corporation.
