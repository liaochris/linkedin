Charu Jain’s Summary

My forte lies in helping companies achieve growth through a better understanding of their customers and identifying means of increasing value from them.

My experience has been centred around retail & FMCG companies- helping them grow through insights about their customer behaviour( who they are, what they buy, where, when and how much they spend) and thereby allowing companies to develop Loyalty/CRM programs for their valuable customer segments.

I have worked extensively in the UK and across markets in Asia helping blue-chip consumer/retail brands refine their marketing plans based on these "behavioural" insights & developing Loyalty Marketing/CRM programs for them.
