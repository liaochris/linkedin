Matthew B. Brown’s Summary

After years of writing and editing experience, I am putting that expertise to use as editor of Nevada Magazine, the official state tourism publication. The challenge is to appeal to both residents and tourists, meaning we have to stay on top of travel news while offering the Nevada reader something engaging. It is my job to make that happen editorially — in print and online at nevadamagazine.com. I also act as editor and writer on Nevada Commission on Tourism products, such as the annual Nevada Visitors Guide. I'm heavily involved in the marketing aspect of our magazine, and I manage our social-media websites.

I'm a freelance writer and experienced photographer, so I consider myself an all-around journalist. I have worked in the newspaper and magazine industries as a copy editor, writer, and page designer. I am experienced in feature and column writing, as well as breaking news, and have been involved in two major redesigns.
