Jason Ambler’s Summary

My primary goal as a filmmaker is to create screen worthy content that will enlighten, inspire, and challenge audiences to reflect more deeply about themselves and the world they live in.

⁘ Collaborated on over 50 student film sets in various positions including Writer, Director, Producer, Director of Photography, Production Designer, 1st / 2nd AD, 1st / 2nd Assistant Camera, Loader/Clapper, Gaffer, Key Grip, Best Boy Grip and Best Boy Electric.

⁘ 3 years production in a wide range of high end promotions, corporate seminars, and special events.
