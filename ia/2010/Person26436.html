Bradley Dewey’s Summary

Based in Geneva, Brad is a Managing Director of ChampionScott Partners. He brings over 40 years of experience in the global information technology industry as a Board Diredtor, Chief Executive Officer, Venture Capitalist and senior executive search consultant. Brad specializes in building senior leadership teams for a wide variety of clients, including multinational corporations, mid-cap and VC/Private Equity backed companies. Brad also serves his clients in a broad leadership advisory role, offering complementary services including executive assessment and coaching.

Prior to joining ChampionScott Partners, Brad was the founder and Managing Director of Christian & Timbers' (now CT Partners) European executive search operations. Prior to Christian & Timbers, he was a partner with the European search firm Hightech Partners.

Before becoming an executive search consultant, Mr. Dewey was a founding partner of Advent International's central European (Poland, Hungary, Czech Republic and Slovakia) private equity funds. Prior to joining Advent he was a venture capital partner with Baring Venture Partners in London and Geneva, where he specialized in early-stage information technology, semiconductor and telecommunications investments. Earlier in his career, Brad held executive sales and marketing positions with Apollo Computers Europe including 16 years with Digital Equipment Corporation in the US, Denmark, Holland and Switzerland. Brad has held non-executive positions on 14 boards of directors and has lived in the U. S., Denmark, Holland and Switzerland.

A native of Concord, Massachusetts, Mr. Dewey received his BA from Dartmouth College and his MBA in Finance and Marketing from the University of Chicago Booth Graduate School of Business. He is fluent in French and speaks conversational Danish and Dutch. A former ski instructor, Brad and his wife enjoy the Alps, skiing in the winter and motorcycle touring when time and weather permits.

Bradley Dewey’s Specialties:
