Natasha Thompson’s Summary

I have had a career in Information Technology and the Energy field for 15 years. I have been in the concentrated field of energy trading/risk management since 2001. I have worked with the Endur application for 7 years, with 4 of those years being employed with the product vendor, OpenLink.
I began in IT as a developer coding in COBOL, C/C++, and PowerBuilder. I later transitioned into the role of Consultant/Business Analyst. My analytical skills include helping the client define business processes, gathering data using Use Cases, assisting clients in deal modeling, developing Business specifications to meet their business needs, liaising between technical team and client, testing technical solutions once developed by technical analyst, and training clients on using the technical solution. I have Endur experience from front to back office, which includes Deal Modeling and Analysis, Risk, Credit, Scheduling, Confirmations, Contracts, and Settlements. I have Endur experience in implementing he following commodities: Natural Gas (phys and finc), Power, Coal (phys and finc), Refined Products (finc), and Crude (finc). I am experienced analytical resource possessing an excellent work ethic and a commitment to providing quality services.

LeadingChoice Solutions, LLC (www.LeadingChoiceSolutions.com) was formed to offer my knowledge and skill set to clients as an independent contractor.
