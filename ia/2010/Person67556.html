Estella Reed’s Summary

I currently specialise in recruitment within financial services/investment banking and telecoms throughout UK, Europe, and also in the Middle East / MENA region, with a focus on banking, financial services, telecoms - mobile/wireless, high technology and full life-cycle development recruitment. Particular interest includes telecoms - OSS/BSS, Billing, VAS space, cutting-edge technology, start-ups and providing RPO (recruitment process outsourced) services especially to start-up/seedling companies. Having previously worked within the financial services, telecommunications industry and consultancy arena, providing IT recruitment resource and consultancy, including ITT and preperation for bids to the EPO, ECB, Euroepan Space Agency and United Nations, I have worked at both blue-chip (privately owned), and high level European public sector. I have specialised in Middle East / MENA recruitment for the past 2 years, networking at high level within the telcoms and financial services arena, keen to provide resource particularly within new and emerging technologies.If you are considering a career move, or simply want to discuss current opportunities, or are a VC/ start-up company looking to grow, or are an SME looking to make the next transition through increse in head-count, please feel free to contact me.
