Amy Hui’s Summary

Amy is a dedicated hard worker with a natural knack for testing. She currently works at Microsoft as a Software Design Engineer in Test on an exciting innovative team - Automotive Business Unit. Products that she has shipped include Fiat's Blue&Me and Ford's Sync. She is a media tester and owns features such as indexing and playback on a wide variety of media devices available on the market. A large part of her job is designing and architecting test collateral to test these devices in a managable way. On the side she manages the team's prebuild system which impacts the entire product team in an essential way - it prevents the team from checking in code that will break the official build. As well, she is involved in automation test tools to help her team fully automated their testing.

Amy was educated at the University of Waterloo in Canada and holds a degree in Bachelor of Applied Science - Computer Engineering.

Amy Hui’s Specialties:

On the side, as a hobby, she enjoys knitting and any other arts and crafts activity that involves creating things with her hands.
