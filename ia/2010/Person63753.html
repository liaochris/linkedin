Elizabeth Rusnak’s Summary

I have been involved in the SBA industry since 1990. My goal is to work with growing businesses to provide financing to purchase a commercial building and/or major equipment for their company's operation.

Northwest Business Development Association is a Certified Development Company (CDC) that is authorized by the U.S. Small Business Administration to facilitate the SBA 504 loan program for business owners purchasing commercial real estate or major equipment for their operations. The SBA 504 program provides up to 90% financing for these types of assets. The program works in conjunction with a lender (either a bank or a non-bank lender). The lender finances 50% of the total project, and the SBA loan (which I facilitate) finances up to 40% of the project. The business owner contributes as little as 10%. The SBA loan carries a FIXED interest rate for the entire term of the loan. Real estate loans have a term of 20-years, and equipment loans have a term of 10-years. Please go to our website at http://www.nwbusiness.org to find out the current rates or call me at 425-286-6673.

Elizabeth Rusnak’s Specialties:

Through my experience in all facets of the SBA loan programs (both 7a and 504) I started an SBA department for a regional bank. My background includes marketing, underwriting, servicing, reporting and auditing SBA loans.
