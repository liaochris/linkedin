Brian Wong’s Summary

Brian has a background in corporate finance having graduated from the University of New South Wales, Australia. He is a well-rounded entrepreneur with successful ventures in businesses spanning across many industries.

Brian is an Internet marketer and social media marketing expert. He has conceptualized and developed online websites and programs that have generated more than $5 million in sales to date.

Brian is also the founder and owner of a wellness center called "Pure Health" that specializes in products and services to cleanse and detoxify the body for optimum health.

Brian is a Diamond Executive Leader with Pharmanex - a publicly listed network marketing company specializing in clinically proven nutritional supplements for general health and well-being.

Prior to starting his own business ventures, Brian was the Chief Operating Officer of a leading Internet company based in Singapore.
