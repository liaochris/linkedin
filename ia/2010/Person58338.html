Donna M. Williams’s Summary

Donna's career spans 14 years of experience in the areas of Internet Marketing, E-Marketing, Human Resources, Consumer Services and B2B Sales, Sales Management, Training & Development, Call Center Consulting, Management Consulting and Coaching.

Donna believes in "Servanthood Leadership" and that a great leader is a great steward over his or her organization and clients. She knows that total commitment must always come before great success and that highly effective teams operate with a mindset of partnership with organizational leaders and with each other. Her style shows that synergy is created when mindsets are changed, principles embraced and then success always follows. Donna operates within and teaches a set of character principles that are used to create a successful organization, career and a successful life.
