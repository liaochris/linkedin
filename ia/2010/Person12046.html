Andrew S Miller’s Summary

Attorney with fourteen years of experience litigating complex business and product liability cases. First-chair trial experience in Federal court. Will provide superior legal representation in all types of business and corporate disputes. I have tried cases in Federal and State courts involving sophisticated business transactions, focusing on mergers and aquisitions. I have practiced law with three national law firms. I was a partner in the law firm of Duane Morris LLP for two years - a prominent international law firm. I have in-depth experience in alternative dispute resolution including mediations and arbitrations. .

Andrew S Miller’s Specialties:

The focus of my practice is litigating disputes arising from mergers and acquisitions, other complex business transactions, and corporate governance disputes, including claims against corporate officers and directors.
My representative cases include defending company that chair of board of directors sued for breach of self-dealing employment agreement.
