Alfred McComber’s Summary

Alfred McComber is "America's Head Coach On Personal Security, Safety & Emergency Preparedness!" He has been involved in law enforcement, high-level dignitary protection, search & rescue and emergency preparedness operations since 1983.

He has worked as both a full-time and reserve sworn police officer in California, Virginia and Washington, DC. He is a graduate of 3 police academies and 2 dignitary protection schools. His police assignments have included uniform & plainclothes patrol, harbor patrol, gang detail, background investigations, and helicopter patrol.

Years ago, McComber was re-located to Washington, DC assigned as a full-time dignitary protection agent to a special team providing high-level dignitary protection to members of a prominent royal family. He’s also handled dignitary protection assignments in New York City, NY; Princeton, NJ and West Palm Beach, FL. Since leaving the royal family detail, he’s guarded numerous dignitaries, corporate executives and celebrities.

McComber also spent several years as a Loss Prevention Consultant (and later Administrative Chief) to AMTRAK traveling the nation’s railway system investigating fraud, theft, embezzlement, and service measurement issues.

McComber is still involved in law enforcement as a sworn reserve police officer where he's a certified "Train The Trainer" Instructor for the national Community Emergency Response Team (C.E.R.T) program.

Since 2004, he has hosted the Code Red! radio show dedicated to personal security, safety and emergency preparedness. The show has previously been available on Fox News Radio's local Washington, DC affiliate station and Federal News Radio, WFED 1500AM in the nation's capital.

McComber spends a good deal of his time conducting seminars and coaching private individuals, companies, and organizations on personal safety, and emergency preparedness issues.
