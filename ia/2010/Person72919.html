Gary Ceder’s Summary

I have personally led or been involved in a wealth of commercial real estate work performed on a local, regional and national level varying in size, scope and breath of services involved exclusively on behalf of tenants.

My goal is to continue to build my business by gaining the trust and confidence of key decision-makers with their companies' real estate planning, strategy development and decision-making.

Gary Ceder’s Specialties:

My focus is on the transaction (brokerage) side, with experience in the technology, retail, telecommunications, investment, marketing & event planning, travel, banking & finance, non-profit, healthcare, legal and staffing industries.

I also specialize in tenant representation. As a core competency, UGL Equis is conflict-free and not distracted by landlord demands or pressures faced by a more "traditionally-aligned" broker because we only represent users of space (tenants).
