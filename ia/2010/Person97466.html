Jessica Becker’s Summary

After spending a year living and working in New York City, I have relocated back in St. Louis. I am currently working on my masters degree in Media and Communications at Webster University. I majored in Photojournalism at the University of Missouri, and have had some amazing internship opportunities come my way in the field of photography and photo editing. This past year I became really interested in the field of PR and social media. While working at AOL, I started to realize the great impact that social media can have on how we interact with others and obtain our daily news and information. When I returned from NYC, I worked for my family's catering business and helped them build up their presence on Facebook and Twitter. In December 2009, I was fortunate enough to be hired on as the Social Media Intern at Scottrade, Inc. As of June 1 2010, I was promoted to become a full time associate.
