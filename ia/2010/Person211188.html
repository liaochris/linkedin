Steve Homan’s Summary

I am a veteran journalist, with 25 years of reporting and management experience at dailies, trades and the nation's leading wire service. Most recently, I completed nine years as Legal Editor (editor in charge of content, editing, trafficking, and the signing off of pages 2, 3, and 4) of the New York Law Journal, the No. 1 daily trade newspaper for New York’s attorneys. It was so respected, it also had a national following.

I have superior writing skills and communication experience having been in constant contact with more than 80 attorney-columnists and our sales and art/production staff.

I am seeking a position at which I can apply my superior writing skills, my understanding of how to prioritize stories for a targeted audience, and my knowledge of how to please highly motivated, critical readers and writers.

Prior to the Law Journal, I worked in a similar environment at Standard & Poor’s ratings service. Other highlights of my career include managing coverage of Hurricane Gloria in 1985 as it directly hit the Connecticut coast and initiating coverage of a Minneapolis “decoy squad” sent out to arrest drug dealers.
