Sidneyeve Matrix’s Summary

Assistant Professor at Queen's University teaching mass communication, popular culture and trends in marketing and consumption.

I'm also involved in blogging, tweeting, consulting, and public speaking about (1) what's new in online culture and emergent developments in consumer electronics, (2) what's effective in social media marketing, (3) what's emergent in digital culture including social networking and gaming, and (4) all things concerned with mobile technology.

I have expertise in teaching with technology, especially: (1) video coursecasting and using iTunesU to digitally distribute lectures, (2) social media tools in the classroom such as Facebook and Twitter, and (3) using clickers in large groups to make lectures interactive.

Happy to connect on Twitter.com/sidneyeve
And on Facebook.com/sidneyevematrix
