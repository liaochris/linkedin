danielle moon’s Summary

Founded on the principles that a recruitment company should be ethical, honest and take a real interest in people, Major Players has earned its reputation as one of the most respected names in the industry. Our vision? Nothing less than to be creative Britain’s favourite recruiter.
For 15 years, we have supplied talent into the creative industries. Our markets are complex and we take a specialist approach to each sector. We use dedicated permanent and freelance recruiters, armed with the knowledge and experience to offer advice and guidance on industry trends and movements.

We have a matrixed structure consisting of 4 client focused groups (Media & Broadcast, Retail, FMCG and Consumer Electronics & Telecoms) working across 10 specialist marketing and communication divisions – Marketing, Creative Services, Digital & Technical, Sponsorship & Events, PR & Communications, Mobile, Creatives, Sales, Research & Insight and Editorial & Content. Each division has a team leader and 3-8 consultants who work under their divisional area.

Our recent incorporation into Randstad Holdings nv, the second largest HR services company worldwide, means we now have even greater reach into global markets and candidate databases.

Winning the Best Medium to Large Recruiter to Work For at The 2008 Recruiter Awards and earning a place in the 2004 Sunday Times Top 100 Best Small Companies to Work For recognises the investment and energy we put into ensuring our recruiters have the right skills and attitude to consistently produce the best candidates and advise their clients on the market.
