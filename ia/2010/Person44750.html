Crosby Noricks’s Summary

﻿﻿Passionate communications professional with demonstrated expertise in social media, public relations and digital marketing for consumer brands.

As Red Door’s Senior Social Media Strategist, Crosby Noricks develops and executes social media strategy for a variety of consumer clients. She is known for known for creative, solution oriented thinking, superior writing skills and an integrated, consumer-centric approach to brand-building.

Crosby is founder and editor of PRCouture.com, a fashion PR blog currently ranked #1 on Google. Through PR Couture, Crosby has been interviewed and profiled as a fashion marketing expert in several industry trade publications, fashion and PR blogs. In 2009, PR Couture was named one The Top 50 Niche Blogs by Evan Carmichael. Several testimonials from top practitioners, media publications and fashion designers are here: http://www.prcouture.com/press/

Previous professional experience includes multifarious PR agency work in the fashion, interior design, hospitality and non-profit sectors specializing in new media and blogger outreach. In addition, Crosby spent 2 years managing the in-house public relations and marketing department for an online fashion jewelry and accessories company, where her skills in brand-building and online promotions regularly led to high-impact media placements in national fashion and lifestyle publications.

Crosby received a MA in Mass Communication and Media Studies from San Diego State University and a BA in Media Studies and Gender/Feminist Studies from Pitzer College. Her master’s thesis, “From Style to Strategy: An Exploratory Investigation of Public Relations Practice in the Fashion Industry” was the first academic study of its kind. Highlights from this work were presented at the 2009 Popular Culture Association Conference in New Orleans in April, 2009.
