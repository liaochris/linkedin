Andrey Golub’s Summary

Born and grew up in the ex-USSR, citizen of Belarus, immigrated to Italy in 2002. Andrey Golub brings 10+ years of experience in IT/ Software Development, and 5+ in TLC/ high-tech and Internet sectors. Scientific Research activity in the field of Artificial Intelligence after successfully completed PhD studies on Systems Analysis and previously earned bachelor's diploma with honors in Applied Mathematics/ Computer Science.

Andrey has gathered some big and unique experience with Social Web and Open Community projects, holding both technical and marketing skills, and has a deep understanding and a good vision for Social Networking and Social Computing scene. Since 2009 he works as an independent consultant for Social Computing, Social Media and new technology R&D projects.

Andrey is co-founder and a Vice President of a Milan-based Business Networking Club "Milan IN", an Official Linkedin Supporter Club in Italy (we created the first in the Word supporter Club for LinkedIn that has been recognized as "official").

Andrey Golub has been assigned in 2008 such important recognition as "Who's Who in the World" from Marquis Who's Who committee.
