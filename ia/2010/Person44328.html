Craig Weller, SPHR’s Summary

I am a Strategic TQM-oriented HR Professional with ten plus years of broad business experience in dynamic fast-paced environments.

The following core values will give you insight into the culture I champion and how I conduct myself on a daily basis (and what I expect from my department):

• Always remember Customer Service is CRITICAL
• Know who you are and what you believe. Don't let circumstances or other people 'force' you to change who you are.
• Always go above and beyond & have a “Can Do” attitude
• Never believe or say “That is not my job”
• Question everything. Never accept “That is just how we’ve always done things around here” as an answer. Why do we do this? Why are things done this way? Is there a better way to do things?
• Make suggestions & recommendations / Make your voice heard
• Always give your honest feedback when asked. Lying to someone only holds them back in the long run.
• Fully support your fellow team members & your manager / Do not get involved in office politics
• Always give credit where it is due & Always accept accountability for your errors. Do not try to hide it or blame others.
• Remember that work is not your entire life. You have a life and family outside of work, and you should have time to enjoy them.
