Del Ross’s Summary

Expert in building new organizations or accelerating the growth and maturation of emerging businesses. E-commerce pioneer, with relevant experience dating back to 1994, I have a particular focus on consumer marketing and sales. In my current role, I am responsible for delivering over $7.6 billion in revenues through our system distribution channels (voice, Internet/e-commerce, and 3rd party) to more than 4,400 hotels worldwide with a specific focus on the 3,500 IHG hotels in the USA. Previously I was responsible for the growth of our e-commerce sales channel, leading it to over $1.5 billion in annual sales from under $200 million in less than 3 1/2 years (it has since become the single largest source of revenues for the company). Expertise in performance-based online marketing has been a key element of my success, and IHG's Performance Marketing program (which I was hired to develop) is now recognized as one of the most powerful assets of the company.

I also have experience in intellectual property asset management. With 3 issued e-commerce/Internet marketing patents and one more pending plus more than a decade of trademark management experience, I have become expert in IP licensing, enforcement, and the patent lifecycle management process.

My goals are to continue to apply my interdisciplinary skills and experience to challenging, creative, and rewarding opportunities as they arise.
