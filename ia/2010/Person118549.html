Kevin Ramsdell’s Summary

Kevin began his career as an intern at Dean Witter Reynolds (now Morgan Stanley) for a Senior Vice President on campaigns to acquire new business. At this time he was completing his undergraduate degree in Finance. Upon graduation, he established himself as a Financial Services professional specializing in Business and Estate Planning for clients in the greater Boston area. After 7 years of success, he decided to pursue his interest in the technology side of the business and attended Graduate School.

He obtained his Master's Degree in Information Technology knowing his focus would be in the Investment Banking and Financial Services sectors. Shortly after graduation he moved to New York City and was hired by the Bank of New York to support their Structured Finance & Fixed Income transactions. He managed a team responsible for $5.6 billion in Assets and oversaw all Trade Executions, Compliance and Corporate Reporting. After leaving The Bank of New York he started his own consulting practice.

Throughout his career he has worked closely with firms including:

• Goldman Sachs
• Morgan Stanley
• JPMorgan/Chase
• ING
• Prudential
• Bank of America
• Citigroup
• Oppenheimer Funds
• Travelers
• Jefferies & Co

Kevin is now the Managing Director at Software by Design, a Software Solutions Provider specializing in Business Intelligence and Corporate Performance Management. His firm is authorized to resell and customize products from companies like IBM/Cognos, SAP/Business Objects, Microsoft, MicroStrategy, & Oracle and as well as build entirely new applications to meet client demands. In addition, their highly skilled Professional Services organization is well renowned throughout the industry.
