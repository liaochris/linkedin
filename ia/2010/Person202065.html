Ankush Gupta’s Summary

The Learned Man! eLearning Resource & Blog covers research, news, and trends in instructional technology and eLearning. It aims to connect the Indian eLearning industry to the rest of the training and development world.

I started out this blog in early 2004 hoping to connect with other people working in the fast growing Indian e-Learning Industry. Those were the days before Facebook and LinkedIn, where getting connected and staying connected weren't as easy as it is today.

As time went on, I realized that this blog was also starting to perform a useful function to the industry. Indian eLearning companies do not have an association or a trade magazine that carries relevant news and briefs on a regular basis. We filled this gap and quickly became the go-to place for the latest news, research and trends.

Today it seems like this blog has taken on a life of its own. The Learned Man! currently has over a 1,000 subscribers to its newsletter and is read by tens of thousands of regular readers from around the world.

I hope that you will find the content on The Learned Man! to be useful. Please do write to me with your suggestions that will help me improve. Do also feel free to contribute news and views either through email or as comments on the posts.
