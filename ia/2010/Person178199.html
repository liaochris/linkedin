Roy Rumaner’s Summary

Over twenty five years experience in software development. For the past 18 years I have specialized in Lotus Notes and Domino Development. I have worked in all versions of Lotus Notes from R2 through R8.5.2 and XPages. I am a Certified Lotus Notes Professional (CLP) and an IBM Business Partner. I am also a member of the Lotus Foundations Design Partner Council.

I have worked on-site and remotely for various companies across the country as a full time employee and as a consultant.

Roy Rumaner’s Specialties:

Experienced working for a variety of organizations including, but not limited to, home building, health care, insurance, automotive parts manufacturing, packaging and shipping, defense contractors, printing, electronics manufacturing, banking and consulting.
