Scot Duke’s Summary

I am a Business Golf Social Mediaist for HIRE.

My Mission is to use my 32 years of real live business operations management experience along with my 5 years of 24/7 social media experience to mentor business people to accomplishing their goals through developing a balance of online and traditional business skills.

I have a true passion for the game of golf. I also have a tremendous desire to help the golf industry improve by showing more business people how productive GOLF can be towards a business' revenue horizons and mentor the golf professional into how to change to doing business in today's economy.

My goal is to show how golf can be used as a powerful business tool.

My mantra is to "Keep It Simple to Get Things Done!".

I am inspired by "Big Ideas" and the people that dream them.

I choose to ONLY work with honorable individuals who believe in making a buck while making a difference.
