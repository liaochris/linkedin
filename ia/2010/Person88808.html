Jamie Wallace’s Summary

Jamie has been called a “triple threat” because of her proficiency in three arenas: writing, project management, and account management. With fifteen years of experience across a multitude of industries and projects, she is well equipped to add value in a number of different roles.

A dedicated virtual partner, Jamie's track record demonstrates a consistent ability to over-deliver. Her business, Fetch the Moon Marketing, conveys her goal of helping small- to mid-sized businesses shoot for the moon (and get there!). Whether the task at hand is strategic, tactical, or managerial; Jamie approaches each challenge in a holistic way that leverages all of her expertise.


SKILLS:

* As a Writer:
Employing strong interpersonal, investigative, organizational, and narrative skills to deliver copy that is well researched and carefully tailored to the target audience. She is comfortable working in both B2B and B2C including branding concepts, business plans, Web content, newsletters, white papers, case studies, eBooks, blogs, and more. She is well-versed in social media strategy and execution which she writes about on the Savvy B2B Marketing blog which she co-founded with her five "Savvy Sisters" in the spring of 2009.

* As a Web Development Project Manger:
Leading teams; developing and managing integrated schedules, budgets, and resource allocation plans; drafting scopes of work; managing multiple projects and teams simultaneously; developing processes and work flow; training resources and clients; collaborating with information architects, designers, and technical resources; working with and managing multiple partners and vendors; managing risk; adding value in the areas of social networking and social media

* As an Account Manager:
Building and managing relationships, managing expectations and scope creep, anticipating and resolving issues, maintaining in-depth documentation, developing incremental business

Jamie Wallace’s Specialties:

Thinking both strategically and conceptually, delivering clear and consistent written and oral communications, working both independently and as part of a team, developing integrated solutions, paying attention to details, maintaining high standards of dedication and commitment, exercising strong interpersonal skills, always coming to work with a good sense of humor
