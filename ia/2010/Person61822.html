Eden Clark’s Summary

I am a passionate Entrepreneur with "in the trenches" expertise in brand development, new media, web, business development, and strategic planning.

I was a founding team member of Onvia.com, Inc. in 1997 which we grew from our original team of three in Vancouver, Canada, to Seattle, WA with 300+ people, $140 million/year in revenue, and a $240 million IPO (Nasdaq: ONVI) by its fourth year. After the initial rapid growth period where I oversaw web and business development, marketing, and PR, I directed the company's Corporate Communications strategy and brand building efforts in to 2001.

After moving to California shortly thereafter, I aided in the growth of G4H Solutions LLC, a software consulting business, where I designed, built, and implemented web and online marketing initiatives for nearly a dozen companies.

In 2004 I co-founded Be Jane, Inc. (www.BeJane.com) and as CEO led the company into a nationally recognized brand with more than $1 million in revenue in its second year, content across web, print, radio and television, and major partners that included MSN, Bank of America, Better Homes & Gardens, Sony, and more. Having a year-long show on MSN, I produced and was the co-host of 10 episodes and 100+ video segments on our exclusive MSN channel (bejane.msn.com), garnering more than 3 million downloads. Our content also became syndicated to more than 250 newspapers and other media entities across the US & Canada.

As a TV personality and "Jane" I also frequently appeared as a guest expert on The Today Show, CNN, The Fox Morning Show, The Tony Danza Show, and others, as well as feature articles in more than 150 publications including People Magazine, TIME, Entrepreneur, Glamour, The New York Times, The Los Angeles Times, and more.

I'm excited to have recently joined eDivvy.com, a dynamic consumer web site and payment technology that's leading the group gifting market and is quickly gaining momentum...
