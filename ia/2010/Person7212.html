ali Bullock’s Summary

I am interested in and have been lucky enough to work on everything mobile, social and online related over the last 10 years of work.

I was here from the start of HTML and the most basic of web sites right the way through to the beta days of Facebook, when I closed my account as I didn't thin it would work (douh!) To todays socially integrated sites. And I'm still as keen on everything internet related as I ever was.

My experience has taken me from London to Hong Kong, working for many global companies including KPMG, Kelloggs, Samsung, Motorola and Cathay Pacific.

In my spare time I work with various charities in Hong Kong and China, including the SPCA HK, HKDR and Animals Asia donating over 50% of my holidays in 2010 and helping to raise over 2 million dollars through projects that I have run since 2008.

We have four rescue dogs in Hong Kong that were all adopted through these projects. If its an animal welfare project I'll go to the ends of the world to photograph it (and have done so.) From earth quakes in Sichuan, China to dolphins in the Azores I'm there.
