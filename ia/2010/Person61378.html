Ebbe G. Haabendal Brandstrup’s Summary

I am a game developer and gamer at heart.

I have roughly 5 years of experience in the computer games industry, ranging developing previous and next gen console engines to co-founding and developing a game middleware company.

I am passionate about game development on various levels. Game play and game design, the technology behind it all, new distribution channels, monetization models and freemium concepts are currently the main areas of interest to me.

In 2008, I participated in the Nordic Game Jam event (now named the Global Game Jam) as programmer and game designer on a four man team. The game, Love Child, received the jury's award for "Best Game".

During my computer science studies I took every chance I got to apply traditional computer science in a computer games domain. As a result, I developed and used a number of different games and game engines and specialized in decision support systems applied in game AI.

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    November 2009 — Present (10 months)

    Having previously lead the development of Aller Media's price comparison platform, I was brought on board to implement a series of new initiatives for the platform, including a new website, http://www.priser.dk.

    My responsibilities on the project have ranged from:

  • Programmer and co-founder

    Aptocore ApS

    (Computer Software industry)

    December 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    At Aptocore I co-founded AGON Online, a middleware library for iPhone and iPod Touch game developers.

    The library offers all the peripheral functionality of an iPhone game, such as global and location-aware leaderboards, achievements, etc. In addition, it is a social gaming network, in which gamers can compete and recommend each other new games.

    AGON is built on Google's App Engine infrastructure, allowing for massive scalability and fast response times anywhere in the world. The native AGON iPhone client is embedded in the middleware library, allowing developers to make use of all of AGON's functionality using just a few lines of initialization code.

    For a list of the games currently connected using the AGON Online library, go to http://agon-online.com.
