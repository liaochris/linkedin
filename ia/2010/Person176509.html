Roger Buttacavoli’s Summary

Hands-on senior operations and engineering executive with 20+ years experience in roles as EVP, Vice President of Operations, Director of Operations, Director of Manufacturing, Quality and Customer Service, and Director of Manufacturing Engineering.

I have a passion for process improvement and operational excellence. I build strong teams that are empowered through open and honest cross-functional team communications to foster organizational awareness, competitive spirit, and a winning attitude. I am the person that can take the company mission, vision, and values and turn them into executable steps that drive success. I share freely my experience/knowledge with others for their advancement.

For ITT, I implemented a lean culture that reduced past due orders from $1.4M to < $50K and improved On Time Delivery to 97% without slash and burn. I was able to activate the Six Sigma culture through execution and implemented Lean Logistics that cut waste and lowered cost. I operated effectively with direct reports in multiple timezones.

My Supply Chain Management experience with Asian suppliers is extensive and have initiated a web based supply chain tool that allowed Vendor Managed Inventory from China. I authored a Supplier Development Program complete with metrics and boiler plate contracts.

Recent accomplishments include:
-Saved over $1M through Cost Improvement Projects (CIP) in the areas of COGS, Freight, Labor, and PPV
-Reduced inventory by over $2M to increase turns through Supplier Collaboration, Kanban Management, Improved Cycle Counting techniques, and Waste Reduction.
-Consolidated 10 manufacturing buildings, 140+ people in 6 departments from 4 locations, into a 60,000 sq.ft. facility in 4 months.

I have a strong P&L understanding from diverse companies and I lead with a strong technical background. I have completed several turnarounds and am skilled at lean implementations using Six Sigma techniques.
