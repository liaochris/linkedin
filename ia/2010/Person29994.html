David Bruerd, CITRMS’s Summary

After becoming a victim himself in 2001, David Bruerd founded the Identity Theft Awareness Group™ (iTAG). iTAG has grown to become a national identity theft education and protection organization. While others were scrambling to tell people to lock their mailboxes and buy a shredder, Mr. Bruerd was warning consumers of potential health risks, Social Security and tax issues, financial disasters and the reality that they could face false imprisonment as a result of someone stealing their identity. He has helped to implement Identity Theft Protection programs which today protect thousands of American identities. Mr. Bruerd has provided over 640 trainings to numerous organizations across North America.

Mr. Bruerd has been a speaker for the Oregon Medical Association (OMA), Oregon and Washington Group Medical Managers Association (OMGMA), Oregon Health Underwriters Association (OHUA), Pacific Northwest Hardware & Implement Association, National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors ( NAIFA), Cascade-Willamette Compensation Board, Confederation of Oregon Superintendents Association (COSA), Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI), American Society of Safety Engineers (ASSE)

Because of his early work in the field, Mr. Bruerd was the first professional in the in the nation to receive the professional designation as a ‘Certified Identity Theft Risk Management Specialist’ (CITRMS). The CITRMS certification program is the nation’s only training program specifically developed for professionals who are dedicated to educating and assisting clients, customers, businesses, and the general public in combating the epidemic of Identity Theft and related fraud. David’s trainings address the ever-increasing threat of this emerging crime and the critical nature of providing business owners and the public with the knowledge and tools to address and manage risk. His trainings qualify as Continuing Education for several professional industries.
