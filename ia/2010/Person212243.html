Vinh Nguyen’s Summary

I currently hold the title of Associate and a position of Structurer within the team "Debt Solutions - Americas" (Debt and Principal Finance division) at NATIXIS North America in New York.

As an investment banker, my work consists in analyzing the clients' needs and deliver adapted and innovative financing solutions. I am mainly involved in the structuring process of the deal flow, the modeling of the asset and sizing the debt and capital structure for the transaction.

Vinh Nguyen’s Specialties:

Negociation, Structuring, Model Implementation, Quantitative and Risk Analysis, Regulatory Capital Optimization transactions, Debt Engineering and Re-structuring.
