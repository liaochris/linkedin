Maelean Sanders, PHR’s Summary

Maelean Sanders, PHR, is a Human Resources professional with demonstrated knowledge in the areas of HRIS, policy implementation, employee relations and employee benefits. She earned her Bachelors degree in HR Management from Baruch College in 2003, followed by her PHR certification from the Society of Human Resources Management (SHRM) in 2008. Maelean continued her passion for knowledge at Fordham University, where she earned her M.S. degree in HR/ Organizational Development.

Maelean took on her first professional role in 2003 as Assistant to the Director of HR for MortgageIT where she facilitated their recruiting, HRIS and administrative efforts. As an HR Generalist, Maelean displayed her charismatic presentation skills, facilitating both new hire and ethics training programs. Maelean’s genuine personality and willingness to assist others contributed to an energetic, collaborative work atmosphere.

Pursuing her passion for career growth and new challenges, Maelean joined AllianceBernstein L.P. in 2005 as Assistant to the VP of Compensation and Benefits. She executed various functions for the firm, including their tuition assistance and relocation programs, employment separations and COBRA administration.

In 2007, Maelean was promoted into the role of Benefits Specialist, where she managed leaves of absence benefits for 3,500 domestic employees. One of her many achievements involved the introduction of several initiatives geared towards the maternity leave program. Such efforts improved morale and encouraged a family-friendly workplace for its employees.

Maelean now seeks to expand on her Generalist skills with opportunities focused on client relations, strategic and talent management.

Maelean Sanders, PHR’s Specialties:

ADA, FMLA, STD and Workers Compensation administration, employee relations, policy implementation, HRIS and change management.
