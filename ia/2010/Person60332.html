D. Scott Angle’s Summary

Sonia and I are working with a high-volume publishing and marketing company to help people develop themselves and their businesses. We also help by offering true quality at great prices.

Our industry operates under two, interwoven, business models. The 'small business' model, deals solely with retailing the product. Whereas the 'big business' model, which is where the true opportunity is found, involves a modest amount of retailing and a large amount of leadership and development. The particular model followed is totally down to the trader and what they want from their business.

A trader can choose at any time to 'go big', at which point they start to develop a team of their own and train and coach that team on how to achieve the type of business they want, whilst still maintaining a certain level of retailing activity themselves. The trader will then be given a bonus from the company based on how well their team and their retailing is doing. This acts as a true incentive to provide quality training and coaching (whether it is in business or personal development).

One of the great things about this model is that the more people that decide to go for the 'big business', the greater their earnings potential. Basically, it's about lots of people selling small amounts, while growing both personally and professionally.

If you like the sound of this, let me know and I'll send you some free, no-obligation information. At the moment we are particularly able to help people in the US, UK, AU, NZ and FR.
