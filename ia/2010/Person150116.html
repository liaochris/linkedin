Mukesh Mowji’s Summary

Currently, Mukesh is principal in Penta Hospitality (www.pentahospitality.com), a hotel development and management company located in San Jose, California. Mukesh and his family have been in the hotel business since 1969 and currently own and operate several franchised and independent motels in Alabama, California, and Florida under a company, Tuscany Investments www.tuscanyinvestments.com). Mukesh is also an advisor and investor in a number of hospitality vendor related companies that have a broad range of offerings in the industry. Mukesh is also a member of the Board of Advisors for Going to Publications a west coast based hospitality marketing company that uses multiple media methods.
