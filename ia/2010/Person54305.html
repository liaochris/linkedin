Demetrius Thompson’s Summary

2010 Mr Thompson is considered one the worlds youngest innovators in the telecomm industry for Global Mobile Alert safety solution.

Demetrius Thompson, Chairman of Global Mobile Alert Corporation (GMA), and inventor of ACTIVATE IT™.

Demetrius Thompson received his patent for ACTIVATE IT™ in December 2007. He came up with the idea after he was struck not once, but twice by distracted drivers using cell phones. After reconnecting with a prominent patent attorney Robert Berliner, Demetrius worked towards developing his product and acquiring the patent. His company was a finalist at the Telematics Awards 2009 for the following three categories: Best Portable Telematics Solution, Best Aftermarket Device, and Best Telematics Service & Application for Commercial Vehicles.

ACTIVATE IT™ is a crash-avoidance application, which is the world’s first Patented location-based services platform that alerts drivers when approaching signaled intersections or other hazardous areas, via an audible sound through the cell phone or hands-free device. The system can be customized to use tone or voice alerts. It is available for license and can be implemented on mobile phones, navigation systems, and in-vehicle Telematics systems.

“Former Managing Director of the National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) I have investigated far too many accidents where vehicle operators were distracted or simply not paying attention. The tragic toll of these kinds of “human performance” accidents is growing both in the public sector and for individuals. Activate It provides a real time solution by alerting operators to potentially dangerous situations in time to make a difference. It is a simple life saving application that can and should be deployed today” Peter Goelz

We expect to see the technology adopted by a number of companies. For more information visit www.gma4.com Contact demetrius@gma4.com to discuss requirements.
