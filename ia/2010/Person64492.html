Emily Williams’s Summary

Emily is an MBA graduate from the Daniels College of Business at the Univeristy of Denver, who also holds a BS in Business Administration and Marketing from the Univeristy of Colorado. At DU Emily was awarded a position as a teaching assistant for the department of Statistic and Operations Technology for 2007-2008. Additionally, Emily graduated at the top of her class and as a member of the Beta Gamma Sigma Honor Society with a 3.69 GPA. Prior to beginning the MBA program Emily worked as an assistant store manager for Pier 1 imports from 2000-2005. Emily’s professional interests are in business to consumer marketing and she views her professional strengths as strategic problem solving as well as understanding and analyzing consumer trends.
