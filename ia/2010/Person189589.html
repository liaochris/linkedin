Managed all the generation energy bids and capacity auction offers in NYISO and NEPOOL. I welcome the challenge of new tasks, roles, and projects because it adds value to the company as well as expands my experience and skills.

    Prior to Noble Environmental Power, I have over six years experience as a Power Scheduler in wholesale and retail energy trading with AES NewEnergy, Constellation NewEnergy, and Select Energy. Primary duties included scheduling retail load and wholesale power in NEPOOL, PJM, NYISO, MISO, ERCOT, CAISO, creation of operational procedures, operational leadership, project management, management and training of personnel.
