Angela LoSasso’s Summary

I'm passionate about customers and building long-term value, loyalty, satisfaction and revenue through customer and partner relationships in digital and traditional media.

I thrive in environments where entrepreneurial inspiration and collaboration with executives, specialists, and partners merge to drive the business forward. I love the challenge of building innovative answers to challenging business problems, tapping my creative resourcefulness, and recognizing and developing nascent opportunities into rewarding business outcomes.

At HP, I am responsible for building the worldwide social media strategy and execution plans to drive long-term loyalty, value, satisfaction, and revenue on behalf of HP's Imaging and Printing Group -- a $24 billion business.

My day-to-day responsibilities and contributions include:
-- Develop and execute the social media strategy and programs within digital marketing and media operational structure and go-to-market mix.
-- Governance and collaboration to execute consumer, small business and enterprise social media and digital marketing plans to support global marketing campaigns
-- Manage agency and partner relationships
-- Develop and oversee social media marketing key performance indicators and reporting
-- Conceptualize, brief, assign, and manage digital content, media, communications and programs to provide an engaging experience for new and existing customers to drive awareness, preference and purchase
-- Manage and contribute to HP blogs, wikis, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, and external blogger outreach and engagement
-- Collaborate with Search and Digital Marketing leads to build strategic and tactical plans to generate and grow qualified traffic and engagement at significant cost savings
-- Evangelize and consult on social media best practices and execution
-- Author internal HP blog for digital and social marketing
-- Conference speaker, including Web 2.0 Expo, M2C, Social Networking Conference
