Mike Supple’s Summary

Wine enthusiast beyond a normal healthy obsession who is tired of the old guard ways and snooty mannerisms surrounding wine. Yes, they still exist in full force. Pushing full force to change the image of wine and alcohol in the US and make it, and therefore life, more pleasurable for everyone.
A background in French studies, restaurants, importing, wholesale distribution, retail and internet marketing/consumerism combined with further studies in wines through the WSET has laid the foundation to more effectively take on the wine culture revolution happening today.
