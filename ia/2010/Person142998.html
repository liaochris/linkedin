Michael Lewkowitz’s Summary

Michael Lewkowitz is an accomplished entrepreneur and instigator with an aptitude for raising viable ventures from complex situations. Over the past 15 years Michael has been engaged in a dozen ventures across a range of sectors from design, to finance, energy, coffee, health, and the web. He invests himself into creating opportunities for systemic impact with partners who have a passion for people and the planet.

Michael holds an Honours Business Administration Degree with a Minor in Economics from Wilfrid Laurier University and is a proud father living with his family in London, Ontario (Byron).
