Michelle Bowman’s Summary

Experienced leader with the know-how for developing creative programs that promote value, credibility and visibility of dynamic companies. Pragmatic and strategic driver of major corporate initiatives, including marketing, sales and communications areas that consistently deliver strong ROI. Strong project management and communications skills needed to drive company-wide efforts, demonstrated through more than 20 years in positions at major companies such as Volt/VMC, WaMu (now Chase) and Activision. Public relations lead for Microsoft Consumer Division, Dell Computer and Panasonic (via agency). Executive coach and communications/media trainer with a love for working with start ups to accelerate business success!
