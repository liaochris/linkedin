Drew Knapp’s Summary

Unconventional thinker and strategist. Detail-oriented, sees the larger picture, connects the dots.

Practicing artist, www.drewknapp.com, widely collected, trained at Cooper Union, NY and Villa Schifanoia, Italy. Rotary Fellow.

Thirty years real estate experience. Lived, worked and purchased property in the United States and Tuscany, bilingual Italian.

Background includes visual arts, non-profits, education, construction and property management, historic restoration, real estate investment and brokerage, marketing, sales, branding, trade shows, business development and web production.

Licensed educator, licensed Realtor, passionate chef, loyal friend, out-of-box thinker, networker, biz dev extraordinaire, visionary,
Founder & CEO of goodideazs, LLC, dreamer of A Greater Town.
