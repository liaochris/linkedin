Erika Roe’s Summary

Enthusiastic professional pursuing MBA with five years of fundraising and gift processing experience in the nonprofit (arts, higher education) environment. Excellent communication, attention to detail, problem solving, organizational and interpersonal skills as well as dedication to the principle of continuous improvement.

I am an experienced marketing communications & web professional. I enjoy assisting with organization's websites, online marketing, and designing personalized e-mails. I enjoy learning the latest marketing approaches to give my company the edge. I enjoy any new challenge that comes along in my work and I like to use it to increase my skills and meet new people.
