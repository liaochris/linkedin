Laurie A. Couture’s Summary

Laurie A. Couture is the author of Instead of Medicating and Punishing: Healing the Causes of Our Children's Acting-Out Behavior by Parenting and Educating the Way Nature Intended. Her book was chosen as a finalist in the ForeWord Magazine Book-of- the-Year Awards in 2009. She appears in the documentary film, The War On Kids (2009). Laurie offers parenting coaching and consulting as well as attachment parenting workshops and seminars through her website, LaurieACouture.org and her blog, laurieacouture.blogspot.com. Laurie was a recipient of the 2010 Manchester Union Leader’s Forty Under 40 honors.
Laurie also works as a mental health counselor, a writer, artist and photographer. She is a contributor to Life Learning, Kindred, Juno, Mothering.com and several other media. She is the author of the comic series, The Hypocrisy Chronicles.
Laurie’s passionate writing and advocacy on behalf of children have reached an international audience. She is a champion of human rights and the preservation of nature. Laurie is a Board member of Parents and Teachers Against Violence in Education (PTAVE) and mentors a disadvantaged teen. She previously worked in the fields of social work and education. Laurie unschools her 16 year old son, Brycen R. R. Couture, who is a musician and the creator of Feendz homemade stuffed toys. Brycen is also in the process of writing a fantasy trilogy.
