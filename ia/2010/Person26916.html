Bram Van Londersele’s Summary

I was born on the 28th of May 1986 in Aalst, Belgium.
After I grew up and had a lovely time in Meerbeke (Ninove), I went to study at the Ghent University.

At this moment I am a 2nd Master student in geomatics and surveying, so normally, I will be graduated in July 2010.

My bachelor thesis (2008) dealt about 'airborne LIDAR hydrography'.

This year I am writing a master thesis. I monitored moving objects by using bluetooth, so called bluetooth tracking.
The data collecting has already been executed. I was tracking the bluetooth devices at Rock Werchter in 2009.
Here for I had an alliance with 2 Dutch companies: Flucon B.V. and Realworld Systems.
The analysis of the data is done as well.
Therefor I was querying the data in a DBMS and GIS suites.
At the moment I am finishing my thesis.
