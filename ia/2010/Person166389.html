Allan Tone’s Summary

ProVDN Inc’s purpose is to provide videographers and independent video content producers a complete package of quality tools to upload, proof, encode, embed and stream their videos over the Internet. ProVDN believes it is critical to provide a high quality service that is easy to use and affordable.

ProVDN Inc is the collaboration of several brilliant technology companies under the leadership and vision of Allan Tone. Having a background in videography, Allan realized a serious lack of OVP offerings geared toward independent videographers and small to medium sized media and advertising companies. Allan's desire to find an OVP for his own use quickly turned into a quest to build the perfect platform for this market and to do it with the quality of the enterprise OVP's but at a price that anyone could afford.

Allan Tone’s Specialties:

ProVDN is an affordable, easy to use, rapid video publishing and delivery platform. Everything you need to start encoding today - ProVDN, Powerful online video publishing. Made easy
