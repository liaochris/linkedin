Chuck Martin’s Summary

Owner of Chuck Martin Production since 2005, providing complete commercial photo and film production services to clients such as LL Bean, Vogue Magazine, Eddie Bauer, Land's End, Wrangler, REI, AT&T, Orvis, Boden, J. Crew and more.

With over ten years of production experience ranging from Fashion to Adventure Sports, I can manage productions of any size with finesse and calm while keeping a keen eye on budget and creative flow.

My goal is to provide each client with the best possible shoot experience while having a little fun in the process.

Chuck Martin’s Specialties:

I've produced shoots throughout North America as well as New Zealand and Chile. My Maine-based production company is a one stop resource for clients wishing to shoot in Maine and New England. We have locations, crew, full production services, equipment rentals, talent, props and more!
