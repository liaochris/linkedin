Katie Reiter’s Experience

  • Owner

    Health Quest Enterprises

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    1994 — Present (16 years )

    Health Quest Enterprises specializes in providing Employee Health and Wellness Program Consulting services to small companies in Southeast Michigan and Northern Ohio. A strong Employee Wellness Program is designed to address modifiable health risk factors and increase employee knowledge about non-modifiable risk factors, consequently reducing overall employee health risks, increasing employee productivity, increasing employee morale and saving money. As consultants, we make sure the best and most cost effective program providers are used in the delivery of all services. We ensure that programming is consistant and regular for all employees at all company locations, and we make certain that employee confidentiality is maintained at all times. In short, we work with a company to create and manage a high quality, comprehensive and effective Employee Wellness Program.
