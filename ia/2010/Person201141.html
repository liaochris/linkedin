Terri Cohen Alpert’s Summary

Terri Alpert is a serial entrepreneur and consumer marketer, with a strong technical bent. She has created two direct-to-consumer companies from the ground up, developing the proprietary technology which runs the businesses as she developed the business models. Financing rapid growth (INC 500 three years in a row, then INC 5000 three years in a row) predominantly from internally generated cash flow, Terri has built a multi-brand enterprise: “Uno Alla Volta” which sells items made “One At A Time – From the Hands of Master Artisans and “Cooking Enthusiast – the passionate cook’s resource.”

Alpert is a founding member of Connecticut’s only organized angel investment group. She is a frequent coach and mentor of entrepreneurs. Having supported Brown University’s Entrepreneurship Program since its inception, she serves as a board member, lecturer, frequent business-plan judge and board-mentor for the annual business plan competition. She is a founding board member of Zeo Inc. (formerly Axon Labs), a venture funded company which is revolutionizing the consumer's understanding of sleep..

In 2007, Alpert was one of three women selected by Working Mother magazine for their first ever “Entrepreneur Mom” award and in 2009 she won the "Enterprising Women" award. A frequent source of inspiration to other entrepreneurs, she has received considerable coverage in publications such as Inc. and Entrepreneur magazines. She was the subject of a cover story on CNN/fn’s “Entrepreneur’s Only” television show in 2000 and has appeared on radio talk shows on numerous occasions.

The US Chamber of Commerce recently awarded Uno Alla Volta with its Blue Ribbon Award for outstanding customer service. It is one of only 52 companies in the nation to receive this distinction.

Terri Cohen Alpert’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurship, direct-marketing, bootstrapping, creating emotional brands, process-engineering, systems design, and of course, single malt whisky.
