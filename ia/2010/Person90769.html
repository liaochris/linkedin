Jason Morga’s Summary

Jason Morga is the Director for Kelly Services’ Americas Marketing group. Kelly Services (NASDAQ: KELYA, KELYB) is a USD 4bn business, operating in over 30 countries and employing over 8,000 people. The marketing division at Kelly has a footprint in several cities around the world, and delivers a wide range of interactive and media-rich solutions support to Kelly’s internal and external clients.

In total, Jason has been engaged in Human Resources and Marketing functions for 12 years. Prior to heading to Kelly’s America’s marketing division, he led critical HR and Marketing initiatives at the global automotive supplier, Yazaki North America, Inc. In early 2003, he was promoted to Manage Yazaki’s Creative Services department. In this role he developed and led many of the company’s marketing and internal communication activities, including key trade shows and critical brand management strategies.

In 2007 his role expanded to include the management of the Organizational Effectiveness and Development department within HR. In 2008 Jason joined Kelly Services as a Senior Manager for the Americas Marketing Group directly supporting Kelly’s product and operational leadership teams with a focus on strategic business development and sales initiatives.

Promoted into his current role as the Americas Marketing Director in September of 2010, he leads the department’s strategy, performance and delivery for Kelly’s US, Puerto Rico, Mexico and Canadian marketing operations.

Educated at Pensacola Christian College, he holds a BS in Advertising / Public Relations with a minor in Graphic Design. In 2003, he completed his MBA with a concentration in Marketing from the University of Phoenix. In addition, Jason holds a PHR (Professional in Human Resources) certification from HRCI.
