Doug Morrison’s Summary

I have over 15 years of information technology experience in a variety of industries including manufacturing, distribution, health care, pharmaceuticals and consumer products. For the past 5 years my focus has been on delivering robust enterprise application architecture for global deployments of PeopleSoft: Financial, Human Resources, Enterprise Performance Management, and Customer Relationship Management.

Most recently, successes include the execution of large strategic IT projects including: automation of IT processes, reduce IT costs, and improve FDA and SOX compliance. By implementing ITIL best practices and HP PPM, I have brought improved operational efficiency and consistent service delivery to help desk, problem management, change management, system monitoring, data center consolidation, password self service, batch scheduling, and automated testing.
