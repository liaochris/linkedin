Kent Ninomiya’s Summary

Author of Demand Studio Secrets, I Want Them Fired!, and Emerging Dragon. Veteran journalist with more than 22 years experience as a television news anchor, reporter and managing editor. Professional writer and editor of more than 2,000 articles. More than 2 million Internet page views. Media and broadcast talent consultant. Television critic, topic editor, article moderator, bio reviewer, writer application reviewer, and frequent contributor on the subjects of mass media, fitness, health, consumer affairs, finances, martial arts and world travel.
