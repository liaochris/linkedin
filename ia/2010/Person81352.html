Helen Reynolds’s Summary

An experienced recruitment professional with working knowledge as well as demonstrable expertise of the recruitment industry. A business troubleshooter with project and management consultancy experience, project and goal orientated with the ability to manage change and deliver required results.

Postions held at all levels within the recruitment industry, from consultant, through to Manager, Director and latterly as Acting Chief Exective of the Recruitment and Employment Confederation ( the trade body of the UK recruitment industry).

As an entrepreneur and business coach, provides invaluable support and guidance to fledging and established businesses up to £50m t/o, through coaching, mentoring/training, harnassing the best recruitment talents in the UK. Able to assist with equity and M & A's

Helen joined forced with James Caan and his private equity house Hamilton Bradshaw to form HB RIDA. She now works closely with their talented team, Chris Ledbury, Jonathan Wright and Tristan Ramus, providing innovative advisory and management consultancy services within the recruitment sector. HB RIDA partners with SME's via its equity programme to assist recruitment business owners to grow and develop their own recruitment firms and if required,lead them to a successful exit.
