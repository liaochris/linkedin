ELI KEREN’s Summary

ELI KEREN, C.P.A. is an experienced business consultant, financial facilitator, and a partner at the accounting firm of Shenfeld & Company, Ltd. His practice is concentrated in the areas of tax planning, accounting, and business consulting. In addition to these core areas, Eli delivers practical and confidential advice in the areas of tax preparation, financial statements, projections, business startups, mergers/acquisitions, and accounting software consulting. His forte is business and personal tax planning.

Eli is a conscientious and dedicated financial consultant who handles his clients with the utmost of professionalism. His mission is to provide accounting and financial services to his clients so they may maximize the value of their businesses and minimize their potential tax burden. He serves small- and medium-sized businesses as well as high income and high net worth individuals. Eli takes a hands-on, proactive interest in the success of his clients and their companies and always strives to exceed their expectations.

Eli provides year-round planning for his clients with the goal of preventing financial difficulties. His clients know they can expect his undivided attention regarding their financial matters; their questions will get answered in a timely manner, and their confidentiality will be maintained. Eli consistently builds strong, cohesive relationships with his clients predicated on trust. He strives to help clients achieve all their business objectives. Eli prides himself on being detail-oriented and always accessible to his clients.
