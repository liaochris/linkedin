Michelle Dunn’s Summary

Michelle Dunn, author of an award winning book, columnist, one of the Top 5 Women in Collections and one of the Top 50 Most Influential Collection Professionals according to Collection Advisor and her peers, shares her hard-won expertise on debt collection with the titles in her “Collecting Money Series.” She is the founder and president of Never Dunn Publishing, LLC and her 11 year old American Credit & Collections Association with thousands of members. Michelle started and ran M.A.D. Collection Agency for 8 years and has worked in the debt collection industry for over 21 years.

An internationally-known expert on the topic of credit and collections Michelle Dunn is a leading authority on collecting money to businesses everywhere. Overseeing renowned authorities from the financial, legal and accounting communities, she brings them all together to bring solutions and unique opportunities to every member of her Credit and Collections Association.

Michelle is often quoted in the Wall Street Journal and has been featured on CNN Money, Forbes, Smart Money Magazine, MSNBC, Home Business Magazine, Entrepreneur magazine, Professional Collector, Credit & Collections Risk, the NH Business Review, and in many books & publications worldwide. Michelle has been a featured guest on (NPR) National Public Radio, the Jim Bohannon show and many other radio programs as well as being a repeat guest on television shows such as The Book Authority and Process for Profit.

In addition to writing and marketing her books, Michelle is a published columnist for 10 newspapers and national magazines including ‘Entrepreneur.com’, CollectionIndustry.com, Collections & Credit Risk as well as an Editorial Advisor for “Collector Mentor” magazine and shares valuable credit & debt information with business owners on her blog.

Michelle is also the author of "Mosquito Marketing for Authors" available now!

Michelle Dunn’s Specialties:

I specialize in consulting about credit and debt collection and starting a collection agency. I write books to help people be better collectors, manage A/R and improve credit policy procedures.

I write books about marketing in my "Mosquito Marketing Series" The first book is "Mosquito Marketing for Authors"
