Kevin Monroe’s Summary

Thirty-three years experience of refineries, chemicals and plastics plants and offshore platforms with thirty years experience in pipe stress analysis. Experienced in using CAESAR II pipe stress computer program. Working knowledge of ASME B31.1, B31.3, B31.8 and many other ASME, ASTM, API, NEMA and NORSOK codes and standards and the PED. Experience also includes writing valve and piping specifications. In-plant field experience totals four years including on-site experience in the U.S. Virgin Islands, Aruba and the Republic of Panama.

Kevin Monroe’s Specialties:

Pipe Stress Analysis, Upstream & Downstream, Topsides for Fixed Platforms, Semi-submersibles & Spars, Norwegian North Sea, Gulf of Mexico and Africa. Revamps and Grassroots units for Refineries, Plastics Plants and Chemical Plants including: FCC Units, CCR Platformers, Crude Units, Hydrotreaters and HDPE Units.
