Erick Danzer’s Experience

  • President

    Frontier Digital Media, LLC

    (Online Media industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    As owner of Frontier Digital Media, I run a portfolio of digital properties - online magazines, blogs, and websites - in the fields of photography, Wordpress, and web development. These include Photocrati.com, DSLRBlog.com, SLRGeek.com, WP-Blogger.com, and SiteCreative.net. Frontier Digital Media is relatively new start-up, but I expect us to have one of the top ten websites for both photography (Photocrati) and Wordpress (WP-Blogger) by the end of 2009. I currently employ 30 people, including writers, web development teams, data entry people, and editors, located all over the United States as well as in Eastern Europe and India. We have a flat and globally dispersed structure, made possible by high speed internet and sites such as Elance that allow for flexible hiring, outsourcing, and management of great talent.

    Twenty percent of after tax profits from Photocrati.com, our flagship site, are donated to the Photocrati Fund, which will provide grants to photographers working on important humanitarian and environmental projects. The first Photocrati grants should be announced later this year.

    Along with FDM, I continue to work as a professional photographer. I specialize in Asian editorial imagery - environmental, humanitarian, cultural (www.erickdanzer.com). My still photography is represented by OnAsia, one of the leading global providers of Asian imagery. Clients and credits include Asian Geographic, the BBC, Conde Naste Traveler, Reader's Digest, the Wall Street Journal, and many others.

    Prior to Frontier Digital Media, I served briefly as a US Foreign Service Officer. I have a PhD in Political Science and an MA in Agricultural and Applied Economics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and I'm a two-time Fulbright Scholar to Indonesia. I speak French and Indonesian.
