Randy Macon’s Summary

After earning a Master of Human Relations degree from OU-Tulsa in 2007, I entered the world of philanthropy as a Fellow with the Charles and Lynn Schusterman Family Foundation (CLSFF). My exploration of the nonprofit world continues with on-the-job learning, now as a Program Associate with the CLSFF as well as my work in corporate philanthropy through Samson.

I have returned to the classroom to work toward a doctorate in philanthropy and to realize a life-long dream -- to have a PhD. If all goes as planed, I will graduate in summer 2014.
