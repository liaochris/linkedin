Jose Radzinsky’s Summary

Radzinsky is the President and CEO for Renewable Power Solutions Inc,(RPS) the premier distributor, designer and installer of renewable energy products such as solar, wind and hydro power systems for residential and commercial customers in California, providing top quality design, service and support to residential, commercial and governmental agencies. RPS is completely committed to the sensible and effective use of our planet's limited energy resources for the essential living and operation of individuals and organizations. This focus on conservation, renewable energy generation, and the efficient use of electrical energy has been adopted by Renewable Power Solutions Inc as our way of doing business

Jose Radzinsky’s Specialties:

Provides the best electric solution for the future. Using renewable energy such as solar electric power, wind power, and micro hydro power help you become independent of the grid. Power Solutions was established in 1998. We are solar electrical contractors and distributors of solar panels, inverters, wind power and micro hydro. We provide sales and service in the Bay Area, the state of California and the USA.
