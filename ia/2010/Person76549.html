Burton M. Goldfield’s Summary

I am an accomplished, goal-driven leader, who has more than 25 years experience in sales operational leadership and technology management positions. I have successfully directed corporate strategy and operations for leading software companies such as Hyperion, IBM, Rational and Ketera, and am currently President and CEO of TriNet, a privately held $200M company.

Since I joined TriNet 2 years ago, my focus has been on building an enduring company that produces long-term value for our clients, our shareholders and our colleagues. Our vision is to have a dramatic and positive impact on small businesses owners and entrepreneurs to support their investments in growing a company. We’re doing that by impacting the customer experience on a quarterly basis; we’ve launched 22 products and services since we formalized our quarterly product release schedule in January 2009. We’ve exceeded our business plan every quarter and will have grown the company from $86.7M in 2007 to more than $200M by the end of 2010. Last year, despite the difficult economic times, we raised $80M to acquire a larger, public competitor, Gevity, and have grown our client base from 2,000 to 8,000. As a newly combined company, last fall I unveiled a new brand identity with an updated logo and new tagline.

Prior to TriNet, I was with Ketera Technologies. In January 2007, Ketera raised $14M in an over-subscribed mezzanine round and went on to achieved the corporate objective of being cash-flow positive by the fourth quarter of the same year. At Hyperion Solutions, I was the SVP of worldwide field operations, which included direct sales, channel management, strategic alliances and consulting services. At IBM, I was the VP of worldwide sales for the Rational Software brand, leading the global direct and indirect sales, consulting and field operations organizations. Prior to IBM's purchase of Rational in 2003, I led Rational’s annual revenue growth from $25M to more than $700M.

Burton M. Goldfield’s Specialties:

Driving product innovation, mergers and acquisition, process optimization around global teams, consulting organizations and sales for software companies.
