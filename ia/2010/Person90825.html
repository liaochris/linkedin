Jason Paez’s Summary

Jason Paez is an investment banker, technologist and entrepreneur. Currently he is an active corporate advisor at Belstone Capital, a featured finance blogger on the Huffington Post and the editor of The Polifinancial Times. Prior to founding Belstone he was with the Leveraged Finance practice of UBS, which he joined from general corporate finance with a focus on industrial M&A. Prior to UBS he was with Merrill Lynch beginning in 2000. From 2006-2009 Jason was a technology startup CEO.

Since 2004 Jason has also been an advisor and fundraiser in national politics. From 2007-2009 he served as a founding member of President Obama’s National Finance Committee, as well as a member of his national economic and technology advisory teams. During the 2004 presidential election, Jason worked with the Co-Chairman for Senator Kerry’s Fortune 500 Chairman/CEO recruitment.

He is a member of Mensa, has been interviewed on NPR's Marketplace radio and has an economics degree from Columbia University.
