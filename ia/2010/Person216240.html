Wm Marc Salsberry’s Summary

Wm. Marc Salsberry

With over 20 years experience in creative design and direction, Wm. Marc Salsberry is a team builder, mentor and evangelist whose had the pleasure and good fortune to work with many industry innovators and professionals. From his initial work in Multi-Media Production to his more recent work with New Media & Social Networks, he has been able to use his talents to help start and develop companies and products that have won awards and accolades from industry organizations, print publications and his peers.

After helping to launch a company that designed and manufactured high-end home theater speakers Marc was asked to join another project called mStation Audio, which produced iPod speakers. Soon after the successful launch of mStation, Marc recruited NBA All-Star Carmelo Anthony as an investor/endorser of their products. After that he acquired ‘Mophie,’ an award winning and innovative iPod case and accessories company, for mStation. Within months of the Mophie acquisition Marc helped release the first-ever external battery for the iPhone, ‘The Juice Pack,' which went on to become one of the most sought after accessories for the Apple Smart Phone netting nearly $1 million in sales in its first year.

After the release of the Juice Pack, Marc left mStation/Mophie to explore the emerging world of Social Media & The Real-Time Web. From that moment in early 2008 Marc has used his Photography and Social Media skills to quickly become one of the most recognized personalities in the LA New Media/Tech/Startup scene. Working with companies like TechZulu, Mashable, Microsoft, Mahalo and TechCrunch, Marc has traveled the U.S. photographing the stars of Tech & Social Media. His Rock Star treatment of his subjects has won him the Praise and Trust of the people he has photographed, as well as, the community at large. Currently he is working closely with a charity called MEND (Meet Each Need with Dignity – www.mendpoverty.org) while developing new projects.
