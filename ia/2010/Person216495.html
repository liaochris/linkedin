Imran Syed’s Summary

Imran is currently an MBA student specializing in Marketing Research and a Teaching Assistant for an undergraduate International Business course. He has most recently completed a summer internship as a Marketing Insights Intern at MeadWestvaco Corporation where he worked on multiple Innovation and New Product Development projects among others. Prior to starting his MBA Imran was working in dual roles with ADK - a top 10 global marketing organization headquartered in Tokyo, Japan. As the Director of Marketing Planning for ADK's Asia Regional Center he was responsible for helping clients come up with an effective marketing strategy for Nine countries in the Asia-Pacific and Middle-East regions. Additionally as the Director of Strategic Planning for ADK's Singapore office he consulted local and International clients on marketing and general strategy issues.

Imran has an intense passion for helping his clients become leaders in their industries and this passion has also driven him to recently complete a book on marketing strategy titled: 'Wowability: How To Achieve It & Why It Matters'. Wowability was published by John Wiley in April, 2008. Imran has been featured in multiple newspapers and magazines internationally and has recently, on invitation, given a talk on strategy for the International Women's Federation of Commerce and Industry. He also co-presented a seminar on conducting marketing research using social media at the Market Research Event 2009. Imran fell in love with marketing research after realizing that "strategizing for business without marketing research insights is like driving a speeding truck on the highway on a dark stormy night without headlights. Not only can you not see where you are going but also you are guaranteed to crash".
