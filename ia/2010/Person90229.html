Jason Brothers’s Summary

Legacy Exchange Group is an asset sales advisory firm for all major regulated institutions and mortgage servicers. We are often contracted through the FDIC and/or other primary regulators to assist these organizations with divesting of troubled assets (generally whole loans and RMBS) and cleaning up their balance sheets, effectively minimizing potential losses to the deposit insurance fund.

LegEx also operates Empire REO which is an REO asset sale advisory firm that assists these institutions with the quick disposition of REO assets nationwide. Empire REO has partnered with Bank of Commerce Mortgage as our exclusive direct lender for all REO financing.
