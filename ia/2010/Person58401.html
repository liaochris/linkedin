Donnetta Campbell’s Summary

Executive Strategic Media Consultant/Freelancer with over 20 years of experience in media outreach, corporate positioning, special events and publicity campaigns; Campbell has campaigned for Fortune 500 corporations, global public relations and branding support for senior corporate executives, leading non-profit organizations and social media programs. Campbell develops strategic action plans in an evolving contemporary public relations environment for clients with visibility issues and special messaging needs. The company specializes in developing cornerstone press relationships and executing quick turn-around partner projects. Campbell excels in solving challenging consumer perception issues, formulating branding and advertising initiatives, organizing media teams, and managing viral publicity project development.

Campbell Publicity fosters successful relationships between clients large and small. The company has 20 years of experience in all communications and public relations outlets and situations. Campbell has held successful events and publicity campaigns from Fortune 500 companies to professional athletes to the United States military. Campbell has a team of indispensible talent that specializes in executing quick turn-around projects, solving consumer perception issues, profit to non-profit initiatives, viral publicity campaigns and new social media tactics while staying focused on a one-on-one client/agency relationship.

Recently delveloped a next generation communications plan (a innovative approach to today's media challenges and opportunites) for a emerging Cloud Computing company. Aplicor is a global software publisher of subscription-based and hosted CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) web-based software solutions for high growth, mid-market and enterprise organizations.
