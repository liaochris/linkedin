Brooke Sommers, CMP, CMM’s Summary

Passionate strategic project management of events. Driving to bring awareness to corporations of the value of the sales force and customer base. Events bringing these groups together to network, learn and grow will guarantee ROI to corporate bottom lines.

I have strategically planned and implemented multi-million dollar events ranging from small leadership meetings to world wide incentives programs to 3500 person sales kickoff meetings.

Would love to bring a better awareness of strategic meeting management programs to corporations to show the value of events and WHY we should host meetings, F2F is a key element of business development.
