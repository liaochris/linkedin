Dibyendu De’s Summary

All that I ever loved doing was to solve problems for organizations for the last 30 years after my post graduation in Engineering Design and Management. I have wrestled with different types of problems -- engineering, organizational, process, quality, machines, maintenance, knowledge, product design, workplace design etc. The method used in called Rapidinnovation (TM) that includes concepts of System Thinking, Complexity, Chaos & Design Thinking helping companies to ride new waves of possibilities to enrich and develop themselves.

Once done, it gives companies on-going benefits for years to come.
