Rick Nipper’s Summary

Born and raised in Raleigh until age 7, I lived in other parts of North Carolina before returning to Raleigh in 1984. Since 1997, I have been evaluating, repairing, and improving houses in the Triangle:
- Interior and exterior home repairs
- Home improvements including remodeling and deck/porch additions
- Home maintenance
- Radon Testing and Mitigation
- Properly permitting unpermitted space
- Preparing houses to be listed for sale
- Consultations

I listen to my customers, find the root cause of problems, and "do it right the first time."
I am thoughtful about the changes I make to houses.
I always evaluate each house as a dynamic working system.

I take education and my credentials seriously. Therefore, I have the following licenses and certifications:
- NC Licensed General Contractor
- NC Licensed Home Inspector
- BPI Certified Building Analyst Professional
- BPI Certified Building Envelope Professional
- NEHA-NRPP Certified Radon Testing and Radon Mitigation Provider
- NEHA-NRPP Trained to Inspect Radon Mitigation Systems

Rick Nipper’s Specialties:

I am passionate about:
- Improving energy efficiency
- Improving indoor air quality
- Making your home a healthier and more comfortable place for you and your family to live.
- Evaluating your home as a whole house integrated with numerous systems that affect each other; and therefore you and your family as you live in your home
