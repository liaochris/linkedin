Carolyn Higgins’s Summary

What are your personal Goals? What dreams did you have when you started your own business? Are you living the life you wanted to live?

My personal mission is to help you achieve your goals by developing an effective and affordable marketing system that brings you more business – consistently.

With more than 15 years of advertising and marketing experience, I have created, analyzed, implemented, executed, managed, and automated marketing and advertising strategies for companies from Solano County mom and pops to large multinational corporations like Nestle, Intuit, TiVo, Delta Dental and Kaiser Permanente. My diverse experience in marketing, sales, advertising, project management, print, fulfillment and customer service will benefit you as you work with Fortune Marketing Company to grow your business.

While I bring an extensive background in sales and marketing to Fortune Marketing Company, I have also partnered with the Duct Tape Small Business Marketing System; becoming an authorized Duct Tape Marketing Coach. This allows Fortune Marketing Company to bring you the benefits of an internationally renowned and well respected brand with a powerful system that has been tested, implemented and perfected with thousands of small business owners over 3 decades.

I am also University of California, Berkeley graduate and certified in the New Dynamics of Direct Marketing from the Direct Marketing University of the DMA (Direct Market Association). I have lived in Solano County for 13 years and worked with small businesses in Fairfield, Vacaville, Vallejo, Benicia and Suisun.

I founded Fortune Marketing Company because I am passionate about helping business owners realize their dreams. Fortune Marketing Company will work with you to generate more business, make smarter business decisions, be more profitable and start building your fortune.

Visit www.FortuneMarketingCompany.com to find out more.
