Justin Patrick Pierce’s Summary

Justin earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Communication Design, where he focused on multi-media production, website development, branding and design, and advertising. Immediately following graduation, he pursued his long-lived passion in health and fitness by becoming a National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) Certified Personal Trainer at New York Sports Club in Croton, NY. Within 6 months, Justin was promoted to Fitness Service Manager, and within the second month in position brought the club from regularly generating 70-80% of projected monthly revenues to a consistent 101-130% for the remainder of his time with the company.

Justin remained passionate about starting his own company revolving around the world of communication design, and moved to Los Angeles, California at the end of 2008 to pursue his dream. Justin currently works as a part-time private trainer and a full-time Tier 3 Personal Trainer at Equinox Fitness in Santa Monica, California, where he focuses on identifying, understanding, and improving the great and not-so-great ins and outs of the industry. With a background in Communication Design and years experience working as a Personal Trainer and manager in the fitness industry, Justin has become the ideal candidate to lead this company towards success.
