Clark Weber’s Summary

My personal mantra is "Focus. Play." Focus on your current goal/work/project and play afterward. And while you're at it... play hard. It feels a lot better when you get after it -- no matter what you're doing

I founded a start-up, MotionBased, with a friend using our personal savings and a lot of all-nighters. In just over 2 years, we added two employees to the roster; built the leading web application in the fitness category earning revenue with paid subscriptions; and sold our business to Garmin.

At Garmin, we built a new and improved web application - Garmin Connect - with a more robust infrastructure and feature set. After migrating all of our MotionBased customers and activities to the new site, Garmin Connect became the largest repository of activities recorded by GPS devices in the world — surpassing 20 million in February 2010.

In addition to my experience with start-ups, location based services and personal monitoring, I have extensive knowledge with internet banking and finance.

I was the first product manager hired at Wells Fargo to build the world's first internet banking service in May 1995. In addition to internet banking, I led online banking initiatives at Wells Fargo with Intuit, Microsoft and the smart cards industry.
