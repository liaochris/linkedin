Steven Morris’s Summary

Over the past 15 years Steve has led MORRIS to be a nationally recognized cross-media branding firm that specializes in influencing kids and young adults. His firm’s clients include Sony Electronics, Mattel, Disney, Upper Deck, The San Diego Chargers, ESPN X Games and Northwestern University.

Steve has been honored to serve as an editorial advisor for HOW and STEP Inside Design and as conference advisor for the MYOB and HOW conferences. Steve has also worked with Business WEEK as a column contributor on branding, interactive, culture and design issues. He is a regular writer for a wide variety of design industry and business publications. Steve has taught Portfolio and Branding Systems at San Diego State University and San Diego City College, and has an MFA from Temple University, Tyler School of Art.

Steven Morris’s Specialties:
