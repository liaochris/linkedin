Mack Miller’s Summary

As Vice President of Energy Services, North America at OpenLink, I currently direct all client professional services, account management, and office operation and administration functions for the North American energy division of a $200+ Million energy trading and risk management (ETRM) software company.

Previously I directed the North American professional services department for the energy division of a venture-funded software company.

I have over 20 years of experience in directing, selling, managing, and implementing information technology solutions, primarily for the oil and gas industry. Since spending five years at Accenture in the early 1990's, I have held senior management positions at several small (100-300 employee) software and consulting companies, including two venture-funded start-ups.

I have a BS degree in Electrical Engineering from Mississippi State University, where my degree program included two years cumulative professional work experience as an Engineering Co-op for General Dynamics/Fort Worth Division.
