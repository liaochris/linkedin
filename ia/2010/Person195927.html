Steve Pinchuk’s Summary

A decisive and analytical self-starter. I have been a Director, VP & SVP for top international travel companies (Harrah’s, American Airlines, Princess Cruises, Club Med, etc.) and travel consulting companies (SABRE, PROS, OPUS2, etc.). This very unique perspective allows me to be accepted in both worlds as both a visionary and an operational manager.

Beyond RM....multi discipline/ industry experience in Revenue Management (RM), Marketing, Sales, Distribution, Dynamic pricing, CRM, Market Planning/Analysis, Business & System Development. Both worked in and consulted for hotels, resorts, casino hotels, cruise lines, tour operators, airlines, rental cars, ferries, Broadway theater owners and retail businesses.

Customer centric revenue management began in hospitality & can be applied to any industry with variable and/or dynamic pricing and identifiable customers International consulting & living in Europe/Asia gave me global perspective/skills from many different industries/countries. Unique ability to strategize/teach/implement/manage in the above areas in companies in many industries including travel, transportation, retail, finance, and shipping..

My background allows me to adapt and implement new practices. Experience from being an economics advisor to Sr. US Senator who ran for President allows me to understand how to apply these practices in new industries and markets.

Patent pending & extensive experience in customer centric 360 degree profit optimization. Based on predictive analytics integration/automation of revenue management, pricing, CRM, marketing & loyalty processes.

Steve Pinchuk’s Specialties:

Revenue Management (RM), Marketing, Distribution, Pricing, CRM, Loyalty Marketing, Market Planning/Analysis, Business Development, & System Development
