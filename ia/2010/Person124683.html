Liane Janovsky’s Summary

Working Solutions for Workplace Dilemmas(SM)

Attorney Liane A. Janovsky of Janovsky and Associates is Board Certified in Labor and Employment law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization. Ms. Janovsky uses her background working for employers and employees to help clients find down-to-earth solutions to their on-the-jobs dilemmas and emergencies. Ms. Janovsky’s experience in the courtroom and the boardroom helps her clients achieve the best results possible for every workplace situation.

Ms. Janovsky is AV-rated by Martindale-Hubbell, and has been repeatedly named a "Texas Super Lawyer" in the fields of Labor and Employment Law and Employment Litigation by Texas Monthly and Law & Politics Magazine. She has also been repeatedly named one of the "Top Lawyers" in Fort Worth and Tarrant County, Texas by Fort Worth Magazine.

Ms. Janovsky is licensed to practice law in both Texas and California.

Liane Janovsky’s Specialties:

Labor and Employment Law; Litigation; Mediation; Arbitration; Representation before Administrative Agencies; Manager Training in Human Resources Practices and Law; Development of Employment Policies and Practices.
