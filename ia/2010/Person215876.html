Will Warner’s Summary

I have a small non-profit company designed to meet the needs of the community, by creating programs which take kids off the streets and places them in an environment that is both entertaining and educational.

The program is multi-faceted in it's purpose and promise. First, it as I have already stated, creates a unique opportunity for kids to be placed in an educational environment that offers to them skill sets in the field of filmmaking techniques. Two, it teaches them apporpiriate social skills such as communication, respect for others, teamwork, etc. Three, it gives them a chance to enhance their sense of self-worth.

I also hold a Masters Degree in Counseling.

I created THE BLACK GHOST PROJECT nearly two years ago in response to the growing problems of juvenile violence here in New Orleans after Hurricane Katrina. What i saw, was that there really was nothing for our kids to do, or latch on to that would be both engaging and rewarding. So, the only thing that they had to turn to, was inappropriate behavior, violence, and immense anger. Since its inception, THE BLACK GHOST has been recognized by The American Counseling Association, The City of New Orleans, and The Louisiana State House of Representatives. My cast and crew, which initially started out with just myself, has grown to a staff of 30 people. All who have been volunteering their time and talents to me, because they believe in what i am trying to accomplish.

  • THE BLACK GHOST PROJECT

    I.C.E. Studios, Inc.

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    October 1995 — Present (14 years 10 months)

    In 2007, Will Warner changed the direction of I.C.E. Studios by becoming a non-profit organization designed to educate youth on how to utilize healthy self-expression through the use of a variety of media, i.e., film, radio, and television production. Participants in the program gain actual hands on experience during production of the popular live action internet series, THE BLACK GHOST. With the help of the many supporters that we have accumulated, I.C.E. Studios moves forward in its efforts to educate both children and adult alike in learning how to change their perceptions of the world around them, and to become active and contributing members of an ever-evolving society. We believe, that in order to make positive change in a persons life, there must be opportunity. However, most people follow the concept that there is no opportunity to change or grow, this is very evident especially here in New Orleans, because there are many who have developed a sense of "learned helplessness". They have lost connection with available resources, and developed a belief system that there are not enough caring individuals and organizations to provide them with their basic human needs. We see this in our urban neighborhoods, and in the behaviors of our children. They have lost hope, and have nothing to believe in. I.C.E. Studios means to assist in changing that perception. Thus was born THE BLACK GHOST.
