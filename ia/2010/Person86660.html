Jackie Lee Choon Yau’s Summary

Jackie Lee is the CEO and Founder of Hardware Zone Pte Ltd (HWZ) since 1998. He is pivotal to development of Hardware Zone as a project manager of joint ventures and strategic partnerships and is responsible for managing expansion plans and fund-raising activities to fuel expansion.

During his term, he has founded HardwareZone.com which is the No.1 Technology portal in Singapore and HWM (HardwareMAG.com) which is a printed Leading Technology Monthly publication. HWM now publish 5 editions in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines. HWZ also publishes GameAxis.com and GameAxis.com.cn (China) and GameAxis Unwired Gaming Magazine and PVi (a Photo/Video magazine).

With 3 years of formal engineering education from the National University of Singapore (Electrical Engineering), He took sabbatical leave from his course to concentrate his passionate efforts.

HWZ was then acquired by SGD 6Billion Mainboard-listed Singapore Press Holdings.(SPH is the leading media organisation in Singapore, with one of the region's most advanced printing assets. It is licensed to publish 17 newspapers in four languages in Singapore and together, these have a circulation of more than one million copies a day. SPH also publishes and produces more than 100 magazines titles in Asia) in 29th September 2006. He continues to run as CEO for HWZ and as a part of SPH Magazines Senior Management.

Armed with more than 10yrs of managing online marketing and optimizing successful online network, Jackie went on to further develop his interest on the internet arena and started clickTRUE.

clickTRUE Pte Ltd is also the 1st Google Analytics Authorised Consultant appointed in SE Asia. He has attained the Google AdWords Professional level and brought the company to become an Google AdWords Qualified Company status, a Microsoft adExcellence Member and a Microsoft adExcellence Company.

He also sits on the Main Advisory Board of NUS Business Incubation of Global Organisations.
