Gary D. Harmon’s Summary

Security Systems Design and Consulting is the primary focus of Valor Concepts. Over the years, we have evolved into a professional services company that specializes in providing much needed technical and administrative services to our customers. Valor Concepts is a "Service Disabled Veteran Owned" small business that was incorporated in North Carolina in October 2004. We deliver our services to our customers with our own staff of employees or contract employees as required.
Valor Concepts offers security/ IT design, consulting and related services that are essential in the security/ IT industry. With customers ranging from local alarm shops to large regional and national security system integrators, our wealth of experience and range of services is vital to their continued growth. Valor Concepts can get involved at virtually any stage of a security project. At the initial stages of a project, Valor can provide services ranging from risk assessment, concept and requirements analysis to security systems design including equipment configurations, drawings and scope of work, proposal support and much more. For projects already underway, Valor can provide submittal analysis and preparation, program and project management services, compliance analysis and more. For projects nearing completion, Valor can provide project/contract closeout services including services such as “As-Built” drawings, maintenance, support and training development and planning.

Valor Concepts is technically competent and/or certified many of the Security Industries top systems but we do not sell or represent any manufacturer or product. This independence maintains our unbiased design integrity and assures our customers trust.
