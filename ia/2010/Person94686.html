Jennifer Akers’s Summary

Freelance writer, book reviewer, copywriter, and editor, Jennifer Akers writes regularly for online and print media. Her articles focus on human interest stories, local events, family activities, writer resources, business, and social media marketing. Jennifer writes articles, web content, book reviews, and blog posts under her own name, in addition to ghostwriting for business owners, other writers, and individuals.

She has edited author manuscripts, other writers’ work-in-progress, business newsletters, fliers, and web content.

Branching out in social networking, she quickly discovered Squidoo’s strength, both as a community and as a marketing platform. One of her first lenses earned the Squidoo honor, Lens of the Day. Since then, Jennifer has been interviewed by Carolyn Howard-Johnson about using Squidoo for author branding / book promotions and by Blog Talk Radio about Squidoo’s power in social marketing.

In addition to creating her lenses, Jennifer contributes to several blogs and communities:

*Facebook Fan Page ~ her personal Fan Page to share lenses and teach Squidoo tips
*Squid Writing ~ her personal blog, highlighting hers and others’ Squidoo lenses.
*SquidLog.net ~ a central news hub of all topics on Squidoo, where she writes a column on social networking, writer resources, and making money online.
*Social Media and Online Marketing ~ a Facebook group, where she contributes tips on social media marketing, Squidoo, and article writing.
*Indian TV Today ~ a blog about entertainment in India and the world
* Rocket Moms ~ an official Squidoo niche of mom lensmasters

Jennifer Akers’s Specialties:

Non-fiction articles, social networking, research, copywriting, editing and web content. Publications in both online and print media.
