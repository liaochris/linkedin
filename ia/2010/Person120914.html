Lance Tyler’s Summary

Lance Tyler is the Managing Member of the Tyler Law Firm, LLC. He is responsible for coordinating firm resources to maximize client representation and associating counsel in complex litigation and appellate practice. He focuses his practice in the area of DUI Defense.

Admitted to practice in all Georgia Courts, U.S. District Courts for the Northern and Middle Districts of Georgia, U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Florida, 11th Circuit Court of Appeals and the U.S. Supreme Court.

Mr. Tyler received his Juris Doctor degree from Georgia State University College of Law in 2003, where he was a member of the National Mock Trial Team. Mr. Tyler was a Bleckley Inn of Court Pupil and received the Excellence for the Future Award in Advanced Criminal Litigation. Mr. Tyler earned his Bachelor of Science, cum laude, from Georgia State University in 1999, where he majored in Criminal Justice.

Prior to Law School, Mr. Tyler served in law enforcement. During his career he served as a sergeant commanding a K-9 Unit, a supervisor in the Field Operations Division, an officer in the Patrol Division, a member on the SWAT Entry Team, a Sniper/Observer, training corporal and as an adjunct instructor at the Northeast Georgia Police Academy. Mr. Tyler has advanced police certifications and is a State certified Senior Police Instructor.

Mr. Tyler served honorably in the U.S. Army Infantry and is a combat veteran of the 82nd Airborne Division.

Mr. Tyler grew up traveling the world as a military dependant. He currently lives in Lawrenceville with his wife, Margie.
