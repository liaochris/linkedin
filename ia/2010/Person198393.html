Serge VAN BELLE’s Summary

Since July 2009 : Working in close collaboration with Inno.com, specialized in Enterprize Architecure & Change, IT Roadmaps etc.

Specialized in Change Management through Project, Process, Interim and Crisis management. From advice services to organizing the change, we are able to provide the right answer to the customer problematic.

How? By working at an organization level to implement company’s strategy. Develop departmental strategy, adapt the organization, lead and motivate team, control the costs in order to maximize efficiency, select the best candidate to manage the team after crisis or interim.

Generalist and polyvalent: thanks to continuous learning and transversal programs, I'm able to work in/with any departments, having a helicopter view, I can use the right vocabulary and identify the impacts of a decision on other departments.
