Charles Heatherington’s Summary

After completing my MBA in 2007, I worked as a financial analyst and deal manager for Arrow Financial Services, a Sallie Mae subsidiary. Focused primarily in purchasing and managing distressed non-mortgage consumer debt portfolios, Arrow was the flagship entity of SLM's Asset Performance Group and a recognized leader in the receivables management industry.

I am currently working as an analytics manager for Collins Financial Services, a firm similarly focused on debt purchase and receivables management, as a member of their Decision Science team.
