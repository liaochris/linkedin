Prashanth Krishnaswami’s Summary

The most common definition that's associated with me is that I'm an energetic and hard-working person with a significant tint of sense-of-humour.

After Blogging for over 2 years, I decided to get into content writing and wrote for a few websites, the most prominent of them being iSport ( http://isport.in/ ). I then switched to ghost writing content for magazines and internal corporate publications.

I have a bit of experience in unofficially working for startups. This was largely because a few friends of mine started off on their own with their ventures and I got a chance to watch them and help them out from close quarters.

I then did a bit of grass-root marketing for Mozilla Corp in the final year of college. My job was to keep the entire college aware of Firefox and other relevant Mozilla products. I managed to be significantly successful in doing the same.

I have been running a social forum called Sit, in Chennai, for over a year now. Apart from all the perspectives gained through discussions, Sit has given me endless scope to experiment with and learn some fund-raising, event management, non-profit organisation management and soft-selling. I have been involved in Chennai's social sector, trying to do my bit to help social ventures in whatever way I can.

I am currently working on Social Media Marketing and contextual targeting algorithms. I'm also trying to get involved in a lot of community-based initiatives in Chennai. I'm also working on some startup ideas in Event Management, Contextual Advertising and Social Gaming.

I love to have conversations and meet new people.

If you find any of the above interesting, please don't hesitate to drop me a note/invitation.
