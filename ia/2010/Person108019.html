Julia Williams’s Summary

Julia E. Williams
President & Recruitment Specialist

Julia's key talents are focused on developing and maintaining professional relationships with hospital administration from the C-level down to the manager level. This affords her the rare ability to fully comprehend the complex reporting structures of varied hospital settings and service lines resulting in timely introductions of appropriately experienced candidates. With her proven and sustained success specializing in the oncology & research service lines, she is often sought out by both her clients and the candidates she represents for consultation regarding current industry trends and her proven methods of professional recruitment and employee retention.

Julia has been fortunate to be able to work with some of the most advanced cancer programs in the nation, and has experience working on the following positions: Multi System/Corporate Oncology Service Line Director, VP of Oncology Services, Oncology Service Line Director, Cancer Center Administrator/Director, Clinical Oncology Director/ Manager, Clinical Med/Surg Director/Manager, Radiation Oncology Director/Manager, Bone Marrow Transplant Director/Manager, Nurse Practitioner, Physician Assistants, Clinical Nurse Specialist, Oncology Research Director/Manager/Coordinator, Patient Navigator, Stem Cell Manager/Coordinator, Neurosciences Service Line Director, Radiology Director, & VP of Operations.

Julia is now the President & Managing Partner for JW Friday, Hooker & Associates; however Julia continues to recruit on specific positions because of her passion for healthcare recruiting. Her background includes more than twelve years of healthcare recruitment experience that includes both contract and permanent placement.
