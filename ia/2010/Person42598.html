Colleen Newvine’s Summary

I have worked as a reporter and editor at daily, weekly and monthly publications, ranging from covering county government in far northern Michigan to helping launch a business journal in the northwest suburbs of Detroit.
I pursued my MBA at Ross Business School out of a desire to help the newspaper industry survive and thrive in an era of declining readership and increasing profit pressures.
At the Associated Press, I get to use my newsroom experience and my business education to look for ways for AP to better serve its many customer groups.

Colleen Newvine’s Specialties:

news business, marketing, market research, social media, communications strategy, CRM and customer database management, product management, customer relationships
