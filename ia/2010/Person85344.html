Inteva Products LLC’s Summary

Inteva has deep roots of more than 90 years as a leading supplier of Interior Systems, Cockpits, Latch and Closure Systems, Door Module and Window Lift Systems.

In 2008, Inteva was launched as an independent company, dedicated to excellence and leadership in our areas of expertise and beyond.
