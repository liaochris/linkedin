Orlando Delbert’s Summary

Orlando Delbert has a strong and diverse skill-set in Broadcast Design, Motion Graphics, and Art Direction, with over 15 years of experience in television show and network branding, promotionals, and close-to-air graphics.

Orlando Delbert’s Specialties:

Orlando specializes in Television Network/Cable ID's and Packages & Television Show Titles and Opens. He also designs film titles. A listing of different projects he has worked on can also be found at http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0216618/
