Scott OBrien’s Summary

As founder and Marketing Director of Explore Engage, I aim to use Augmented Reality (AR) to help overcome the everyday market problem of engaging, entertaining and informing in highly contextual and influential environments.

My current areas of interest and expertise currently include digital strategy, mobile and eCommerce, human-computer interaction and experiential marketing. At Explore Engage we are interested in discussing any partnership, consulting, media or investment opportunities which are related to our core skills in utilising augmented reality.

Developing from a background in Psychology I gained experience in marketing campaigns for Energy Australia and Telstra. Later I launched a new sport in Australia which provided a community element to my professional outlook.

Experience operating tourism businesses in Norway, playing professional sport and setting up the first Sydney Christmas parade whetted my appetite for digital in the media and marketing mix.

The more interactive campaigns interest me so gesture based control, motion capture, voice, image and body movement/tracking recognition are high on the list of most exciting delivery methods and development for a campaign, industrial or lifestyle solution.

Now AR technology compliments the above amongst mass consumers and satisfies the highest form of informative, entertaining experiences and rich creative work across the web, mobile, kiosk and other more immersive platforms.

The development and marketing of AR sunglasses with Explore Engage is the most exciting goal of my professional career. We look forward to presenting and partnering on many exciting applications of this product.

Please feel free to get in touch about creative work, partnering, JV props and also follow us on twitter and facebook.
