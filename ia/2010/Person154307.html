Nick Wilsdon’s Summary

Marketing consultant and project manager for my company e3internet, with broad experience of web and application development. I have a long standing interest in search and marketing, having started my career promoting shareware.

Relocating to Russia gave me a chance to apply this knowledge to a completely different market. We work with leading UK/US companies who wish to break into and establish their business in Russia. Watching internet development here, in such a relatively untapped market is very exciting. I get to document these events in my role as Russian Editor for the e-publication Multilingal Search (http://www.multilingual-search.com)

Our clients at e3internet include Opera Software ASA, PartyGaming, Harrods, MySpace and Porsche
