Ron Person’s Summary

Ron Person works with small- and mid-sized businesses to increase profitability and productivity. Ron helps his clients:

* Create a clear, focused strategy

* Create systems to monitor strategy execution

* Increase performance and productivity

* Reduce waste and operating costs

* Make better decisions faster with Next Generation
scorecards and dashboards

The majority of Ron's work is with clients in:

* High tech and internet commerce

* Consumer products

* Biotech and biomedical devices

* Food and beverage

* Chemicals and metal

* Financial services

Ron is the author of four international best-selling books with more than one million copies in print. His latest book from J Wiley Press is
"Balanced Scorecards and Operational Dashboards with Microsoft Excel"
rated 5-stars on Amazon.

He is currently researching the forthcoming book
"What Worked Before Won't Work Now:
Next Practices to Thrive in the New Economy."

If you would like your company to participate in this research and receive pre-release Executive Summaries and Analysis, please have a company executive or director contact Ron.

Ron Person’s Specialties:

Strategic Execution. Balanced Scorecard. Executive Dashboards. Critical Success Factors. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). Metrics. Performance Improvement. Productivity Improvement. Cost Reduction. Excel and Next Generation dashboards in finance, operations, supply chain, sales, and marketing.
