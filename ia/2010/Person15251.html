Antonio Reyes’s Summary

Antonio Reyes is a proficient speaker in Mandarin (Chinese) and has extensive international experience, including three years of professional work abroad in China, and international travel. Antonio’s passion about international business and education development and social media has lead to his involvement with international corporations, trade organizations, higher education, consulting, and social media development. Antonio is the co-founder and International Business Development Officer at SocialSwell, a social and new media company and contributor to several firms on business development, social media development, and negotiations in China as well as a visiting lecturer at University of Texas at San Antonio and Seattle Central Community College. Previously, Antonio served as an Education Coordinator for the Foundation of International Understanding Through Students (FIUTS), not-for-profit aimed at international culture exchange between American and international students and Foreign Expert of Chinese language and Culture. With excellent interpersonal skills, ability to work in diverse settings and establish excellent rapportwith clients and colleagues, Antonio brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to any organization looking to expand their organization into the international arena. Antonio holds an M.A. from the University of Washington in international studies specializing in East Asian business development, trade, and management, and a B.A. in Asia Studies from the Temple University.
