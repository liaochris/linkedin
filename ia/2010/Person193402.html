Starley Murray’s Summary

As a Nationally Syndicated Televised Image Expert and Celebrity Makeup Artist, I am honored to be able to share my signature techniques & lessons related to image, beauty, and self esteem on local and syndicated TV morning shows across the nation.

My company is structured under the belief that Image is more than what you wear; Image is how you represent yourself in life. As dedicated service leaders, we provide our diverse clientele base with superior image services and products to give them an edge in business and in their personal lives.

I am dedicated to sharing my valuable lessons with a greater number of people whom I reach through Image related workshops & seminars. My seminar & workshop audiences range from corporations, women's and youth groups and business professionals.

Fifteen years of unique work experience as a professional makeup artist, working with television networks, film production studios, fashion/entertainment magazines, and celebrities, has allowed me to develop my expertise and gain insight into how image can affect one's self esteem, income and reputation.

With the supporitive tools I've developed such as two professional cosmetic lines, Image handbooks, and other learning mediums, I am focused on teaching people how to be their own Image Consultant.
