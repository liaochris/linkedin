Mari Huertas’s Summary

I coordinate seamless work across multiple in-house and off-site departments to produce media and web pages, ensure on-time delivery of all content for builds, evaluate functionality options, create online tutorials, and outline the framework, function, and customer target base for new sites.

Mari Huertas’s Experience

  • Associate Manager for Digital Products

    World Book

    (Public Company; Publishing industry)

    October 2008 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    I recently completed the second launch of World Book Classroom's Science Power, which pairs Lexile-leveled text with custom animations, photos, illustrations, videos, quizzes, experiments, critical thinking questions, and extension activities. The material teaches and expands upon basic science concepts in a way students will find engaging and teachers will find easy to use, manage, and implement in the classroom.

    In early 2010, I directed the recording, production, and delivery of a massive audio project – more than 3,500 files – for Early World of Learning, a children's site that incorporates highlighted, read-aloud text, games, interactivities, and more.     In January 2010, I presented Dramatic Learning in a webinar for Booklist to 2,500 registrants. I regularly conduct global webinars on our English-, French-, and Spanish-language sites and attend trade conferences on behalf of my company. Additionally, I routinely conduct quality assurance testing on new project launches and existing site updates and enhancements.

    I work in marketing to assist our sales division in promoting current projects, analyze and address our competition, and do focus group testing for upcoming sites. I present our products during trade shows and sales conferences for our sales reps and customers.
