Sarah Stevenson’s Summary

I am a writer, editor, and communications specialist, currently working as a contractor and freelancer in Cincinnati, Ohio. I enjoy the variety this line of work offers and the chance to learn about different companies, industries, and subject matters.

I started my career as a technical writer, building my writing, editing, usability, and design skills. As a contractor through OfficeTeam, I have transitioned into more marketing-focused roles while continuing to hone my writing and design skills. In my spare time, I enjoy doing pro-bono web design work, including copywriting and graphic design.

I have a B.A. in Technical and Professional Communication from Cedarville University. My courses and internship provided a solid foundation in writing, editing, proofreading, marketing communications, web design and usability, print design and production, digital media, and related software tools and technology. The underlying principles I learned in every class were identifying a project's audience and analyzing those audience needs.
