Jovana Grbic’s Summary

Jovana Grbic, PhD is a dynamic, engaging science writer who has made it her life’s mission to change how we perceive and pen science and technology one movie, TV show, advertising campaign and news article at a time. With over 11 years of education and experience, Jovana’s training has spanned the fields of chemistry, genomics, cancer biology and infectious diseases at world class private and academic laboratories, including Northwestern University, Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, The Scripps Research Institute, The Genomics Institute of the Novartis Research Foundation, Los Alamos National Laboratory and UCLA. As head writer and editor of ScriptPhD.com, Jovana occupies a unique niche in the creative intersection of science and entertainment.

Anchored by Jovana’s strong management, leadership, collaborative and creative skills, ScriptPhD.com has developed a wide array of consulting services in the entertainment, media, advertising and freelance writing sectors. Whether you are looking to doctor a script, kick-start a sci-fi marketing campaign, announce your company’s innovative technology through a press release, white paper, or web copy that stands out from the crowd, we are ready to make your project shine creatively without sacrificing scientific integrity. Clients have included the UCLA School of Film and ABC Television.

Originally born and raised in Belgrade, Serbia, Jovana is a proud adopted Seattle native, and still roots for the Mariners and Seahawks to no avail.
