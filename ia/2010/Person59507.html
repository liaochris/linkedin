Caitlin Williams’s Summary

I am passionate about the power of possibilities for individuals and organizations. I focus on: 1) helping experienced professionals find career opportunities inside organizations where they can contribute their talent to make a difference, 2) helping organizations engage, develop and retain workers to bring out their best performance, and 3) challenging everyone I work with to consider more options than they ever imagined. Some highlights of my work include:

* Speak and write about the “working worried” to help HR, managers, and career and mental health professionals better understand the challenges these workers face and the consequences for organizations

* Trained hundreds of healthcare professionals, and emerging leaders across the US, and in the UK, Canada and Europe in problem solving, influencing, visioning, motivation, interpersonal skills and developing performance metrics to help increase their effectiveness

* Author of award winning book on women’s workplace strengths and ongoing coaching and corporate presentations on how women can advance their careers inside their organizations

*Help national associations implement career coaching services at their annual conferences as an added value for their members

*Frequent guest speaker at conferences and workshops nationwide on professional development topics, and the workplace of tomorrow

* Coach individuals about the emerging workplace: how to prepare for it, help shape it and move toward it

*Assisted 100’s of healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, and
telecommunications professionals successfully transition through the outplacement process

* Featured in Training & Development Magazine, Executive Female, Investors’ Business Daily, Counseling Today, Career Convergence, and Fortune.com

My goal: To help employers and employees build a shared commitment to making their workplaces “work” for everyone in terms of profitability and mutual respect.
