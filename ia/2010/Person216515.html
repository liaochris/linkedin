Bill Mortimore’s Summary

Hello, my name is Bill Mortimore and I'm the founder of Strategic Customer Solutions, LLC (SCS); an executive search, consulting and career coaching business. SCS works with small to mid-size information technology companies to find, hire and manage their most valuable asset; their people.

For executives and senior level professionals SCS also provides career coaching services using a structured methodology to guide you to your next career position. If you haven't searched for a job in the past few years you need to know the old methods are no longer effective. SCS can help you achieve success in this highly competitive new job market.

Finally, I want you to know I believe that honesty and integrity is the bottom line in every business and personal relationship. We always strive for positive outcomes for clients and candidates through a clear understanding of wants and needs, and an obsessive attention to detail.
