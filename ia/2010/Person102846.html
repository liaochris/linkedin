John Paolella’s Summary

After realizing my passion for a challenge and willingness for competition, I joined the real estate industry. I quickly accelerated and joined the number one producing team of Chicago’s top broker, @properties; my team of three was able to accrue over 120 million in sales since 2006.

This has fueled me in many ways, and through a myriad of contacts and business relationships I have started to branch out from residential real estate. I am now representing many large end buyers and funds sourcing Distressed asset pools, Real property packages, development close outs, large end commercial deals, and private real estate ventures.

I am heavily involved with the Colorado Alumni here in Chicago as one of the chapter presidents. We are always looking for more support, assistance and people to have fun with. Please check us out on Facebook or LinkedIn by searching “CU in Chicago…” We are here to gather fellow alumni together with networking, friendship, guidance, social gatherings, charities or council in any area. We now have a website www.chicagobuffs.org Check it out because we have a lot of events coming this fall.
