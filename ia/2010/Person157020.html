Jason Franklin’s Summary

For the past five years, I have consulted directly with various companies on topics ranging from business continuity management and safety initiatives to US financial compliance laws and business-wide goal setting strategies. My clients have included Albertsons, Boeing, Bright Horizons, Bristol-Myers Squibb, CVS Pharmacy, ImClone, Deutsche Bank, Farmers Insurance Group, Rockwell Collins, Wachovia Bank, and Yum! Brands.

Currently my focus is on developing long standing Strategic Development Partnerships - helping my client’s transition from the typical project-by-project vendor relationship to a more holistic and enterprise-wide view. Utilizing expert people, processes, and proficiencies, an Allen partnership streamlines development and eliminates redundancy to get the best results for your every effort.
