Ian Berman’s Summary

Director (Transaction Support) in the San Francisco, California office of RSM McGladrey Inc. With 8,000 employees and combined annual revenues of over $1.4 billion, RSM McGladrey Inc / McGladrey & Pullen rank as the 5th largest accounting, tax and business-consulting firm in the USA.

Bringing more than 20 years of experience exclusively focused on M&A advisory, I have worked in both the Big 4 (11 years Deloitte) and investment banking worlds. My deal size experience has primarily been middle market focused, including international and domestic transactions.

During my career, I have worked as an advisor on over 150 closed transactions, including cross-border, buy-side, sell-side, private placements, joint ventures, minority investments, and technology royalty agreements. I have worked closely with fast growing private companies as well as large public international conglomerates including Microsoft, Sony, Panasonic, Chronicle Publishing, Sumitomo Realty, Capcom, Matra Hachette/Grolier, News Corp., Kubota, Viacom/Virgin, Nippon Electric, and Segasoft. First M&A advisor hired by Microsoft in over 10 years

Prior firms include Deloitte, Salomon Brothers, Royal Bank Of Canada Capital Markets Group. I was one of the founding members of the TAS group at Deloitte Touché, climbing to the rank of senior manger. At RBC Capital Markets, I held the position of Managing Director Co-head M&A technology and consumer groups.

Industries: Extensive industry experience within technology, software, interactive media, consumer electronics, digital content and financial services.


CPA; Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting/Finance), Witwatersrand University, Johannesburg, South Africa; Completed national recognized Advanced Negotiation Program Stanford Graduate School of Business; Competed numerous undergrad and graduate courses (accounting, tax, finance, law) at Rutgers, USF and Golden Gate.

M&A Due diligence. Private Equity. Cross Border.
