    Lisbeth Calandrino is co-founder and vice president of Peachtree Communications, a full-service marketing, communications and training firm which focuses on the flooring industry and which also publishes FABULOUS FLOORS MAGAZINE, the international consumer flooring publication. As owner of Improving Human Performance, Lisbeth works with companies to motivate their employees to expand their horizons and maximize their potential.

    Lisbeth's inspirational, humorous and high-energy approach to training makes her one of the most in-demand business management consultants in industries ranging from independent flooring retail stores to Fortune 500 corporations. Similarly, her experience as a trainer and motivator, and because she also is a former principal for a chain of carpet and home furnishing stores, Lisbeth has made her a highly sought-after speaker for industry events and seminars. She also does commercial voice-overs and has written and directed specialized training films.
