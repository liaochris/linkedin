Annie Kirschenmann, MS, BC-DMT, NCC, CBC’s Summary

Business, executive and management coach with extensive "in the chair" leadership experience. Non-verbal communication expert. Strong background in creativity / the arts, and using them in business. Experience directing and developing both corporate and non-profit companies, as well as running small businesses. Urban and rural; domestic and international experience. Extensive experience and qualifications coaching, training and developing staff / teams.

Mission: Our mission is to inspire and help people ignite their growth and discover and live their full potential in joy.

We create dynamic environments in which clients can exceed their own greatest expectations.

Corporate Coaching (management coaching) focusing on business and professional goal attainment, as well as increasing productivity, efficiency and effectiveness.

Entrepreneurial Coaching focusing on the special needs of creative business leaders and owners.

Executive Coaching focusing on the unique needs of executives and leaders. Executive Coaching Packages provide custom-designed, comprehensive services, including: private and "shadow" coaching, personal and company assessments, goal achievement, professional / personal vision and mission, stress management and life / work balance, as well as communication, organization and management development.

Project Coaching is a coaching service unique to A.K. A Coach and Company! Do you have a specific project or time limited topic for which you would like some very targeted support? Then this is the option for you. Coaching focused exclusively on a defined project, providing you with support, feedback, accountability, goals, timelines and action/results orientation.
