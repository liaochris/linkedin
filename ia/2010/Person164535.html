Eldonna Lewis-Fernandez’s Summary

Eldonna is affectionately known as the Pink Biker Chic. She is the GPS for Feminine Transformation and the GPS for the Federal Contracting process. She helps people take control of the handlebars of their lives ™ and go from the back seat to the front seat in their own lives.

Eldonna has developed coaching programs and workshops for women on negotiation and also a program to move women from heels to wheels which includes transformation, empowerment and learning how to ride a motorcycle.

Eldonna is an expert in the Federal Contracting arena with 30 years of contracting experience; 23 years as a Government Contracting Officer and Contracts Specialist and 7 years as a commercial Contracts Negotiator and Contracts Administrator. She has been a trusted agent for the U.S. Government for 30 years on both sides of the table. She coaches businesses through the Federal Contracting process and saves them time and money by showing them how to streamline the process and eliminate unnecessary steps.

Eldonna is certified in at Level III Contracts in the Acquisition Professional Development Program. She is also a certified Coach and Consultant. She is a master at teaching people goal setting, negotiation skills, how to navigate the Federal Contracting Process for maximum results and how to build a rockin 30 second elevator pitch.

Eldonna is an award winning speaker and a co author of Heart of a Military Woman and contributing author in Heart of a Woman in Business. She is a member of Toastmasters including the District One Toastmasters Community Speakers Bureau.

She is the board of the International Empowerment Education Foundation and heads up the speakers bureau for the Women's Peace Campaign, a non profit that helps single moms and teens in crisis. www.womenspeace.com

Eldonna is a member of the Diamond Posse, a 4 woman Harley group that rides across country to raise funds and awareness for veterans and veterans issues. http://diamondposse.us
