Stephen Zarick’s Summary

VO Group is a sales and lead generation company.
VO Group is a growing international sales development company.

We generate Pre-Qualified sales leads for Healthcare, Manufacturing, Distribution, and other major market sectors.

We tend to focus on Sales vs Marketing in our approach.
Our product is called Fractional Ownership of a Prospecting Team.

To address the black eye that our industry has (telemarketing - demand generation) VO Group operates in a very capitalistic fashion allowing for self-employment in a performance based environment. We have an agent program.

We have spent much of our sales time representing the channel partners in the indirect channel of brand name technology companies. Our client is likely a channel partner of name brand product such as IBM, SAP, Oracle, 3Com, Symbol, Intermec, Motorola, Psion, and others.

As we grow this model into industry sectors, we need qualified independent sales people. We need industry people, such as Healthcare, Banking, Logistics, Supply Chain, Wireless Mobility, Gaming and Casinos, Automotive and more.

VO Group not only has US based clients but international clients as well.

Sales is measurable; we expect our efforts to be measurable as we grow.

VO Group is moving to be involved in all aspects of a closed loop sales and marketing effort:
- Consulting on how to build a channel program and a go to market strategy
- Hosting web sites to use SEO to drive leads
- Partnering with Salesforce.com and other CRM platforms to score/track leads
- Implementing outbound call campaigns for a focused lead gen program
- Presenting and then selling for our client.
- Providing inside sales support in a post sales support role
