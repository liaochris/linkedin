David Sloane’s Summary

Manager, architect, strategist and guy-who-plugs-things-in for large and small technology infrastructures. My primary goal is, generally, aligning technical staff, technical management, and business stakeholders to a common set of goals. This involves substantial collaboration with all levels of an organization to define priorities and develop strategies for organizational success.

Too management-speaky? I like to talk to people about technology, whatever their level of knowledge, so that I understand what they need and they understand how I'm going to get them there. When everyone agrees about a goal, my team agrees on a design and implementation schedule, and then we make it happen. If I can get my hands dirty with real technical work, that's even better.
