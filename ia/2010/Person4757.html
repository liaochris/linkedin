James Akhbari’s Summary

I am a self-made digital media director, consultant and freelancer looking to bring a wealth of experience as well as my MBA to develop strategic and successful digital direct response, social and branding media programs.

Throughout my career in media, I have worked on many different blue chip accounts. I started my traditional media career working with Euro RSCG and I worked on MCI’s Emerging Markets, 1-800-COLLECT, and MCI Hispanic accounts.

After gaining valuable digital experience, I spearheaded the creation of Bates Interactive Media Group and created the fastest growing division within the organization. In addition, I was the first to present and to persuade the federal government to utilize interactive advertising for the "White House Office of National Drug Control Policy"(ONDCP). During this time period, I led the strategic campaigns for Clinique Cosmetics, EDS, Cable & Wireless, Wendy’s Restaurants and Moet & Chandon.

While at Beyond.com, I continued my quest for new business and knowledge. I was responsible for adding new clients to the company’s client list. My quest to push technology led me to pioneer mobile advertising on Cell Phones and other mobile devices.

After receiving my MBA in Marketing, I collaborated in starting a new digital agency at JL 360, and created many strategic Web 2.0 media campaigns for my clients.

How I Can Help You:

I help individuals, companies, and vendors who need a fresh, experienced ROI point of view on bringing their brand online or moving the needle. If you are unfamiliar with Social Media, Web 2.0, and other Digital Media opportunities, I can help you easily and effortlessly overcome this fear to achieve your goals.

My Contact Information:

Feel free to contact me at j.akhbari@yahoo.com if you have any questions, or for a free consultation on how I can grow your business or brand online. Questions related to digital media are highly encouraged!
