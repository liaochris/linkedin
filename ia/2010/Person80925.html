Heather Powell’s Summary

Experienced pharmaceutical regulatory and compliance attorney with extensive expertise in small to medium-sized pharmaceutical and medical device companies and the commercialization of their products.

Heather Powell’s Specialties:

Compliance program creation and administration, Corporate Integrity Agreement requirements and implementation, promotional material review, investigation, auditing and monitoring of commercial and medical initiatives, state law/aggregate spend data collection and tracking system design and implementation, implementation of social media initiatives, and integration of compliance into commercial sales and marketing organizations.
