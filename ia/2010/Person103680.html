Jonathan Azu’s Summary

Jonathan Azu currently serves as Executive Vice President of Business Development & Marketing/GM for Superfly Marketing Group, a New York based entertainment company. Superfly is best known for producing the Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival, the largest music event in North America. We develop entertainment solutions for major brands, produce festivals and manage music talent.

Prior to joining Superfly in 2009, Jonathan spent ten years at CBS Radio, most recently as VP of Music Partnerships. Jonathan joined CBS Radio in September of 1999 as Marketing Coordinator for CBS Radio- St. Louis. In April of 2001, CBS Radio promoted Jonathan to Director of Marketing, where he oversaw station events and sales promotions. While handling his Marketing Director responsibilities, Jonathan also oversaw the development of the station’s first Internet Development department, later becoming Internet Development Director.

In the spring of 2003, Jonathan accepted a position, in New York, as Account Manager in CBS Radio’s newly formed corporate marketing and sales division, the Altitude Group. As Account Manager, Jonathan coordinated program activation for many of CBS Radio’s top grossing clients. Less than one year after joining CBS Radio’ corporate office, Jonathan was elevated to Director of Marketing and Sales. In his new role, Jonathan was responsible for managing sales development as well as a generation of new and incremental revenue opportunities from prospective and current clients.

Jonathan, who is currently working towards a Certificate in Brand Strategy from NYU School of Continuing and Professional Studies, graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Marketing from Drake in 1999. Jonathan was the 2007 recipient of Drake University Young Alumni Achievement Award, Jonathan currently serves on the Board of Directors of the Drake National Alumni, Louis Carr Foundation and holds a seat on the Global Angles Advisory Board.
