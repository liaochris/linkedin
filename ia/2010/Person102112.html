John Gaskin’s Summary

The film and TV production is what I love. From 1986 through to 2006 I worked as a Film Production Auditor working on 43 large and small film/TV productions (I worked for Ron Howard twice). You can find me on IMDb.
I have developed an expertise in budgeting, cost reporting, dealing with bond companies, managing contracts of every description, working with producers, production managers and studio executives to ensure the production was produced on time and on budget. Some studios and bond companies (for example, Disney and Film Finances) rely heavily on the Film Production Auditor, in some cases trusting and demanding action from my position over anyone elses.
After returning from a Disney feature film production in South Africa (summer of 2006) I took on the position of VP Operations for Canada for a major payroll service, increasing the sales stats by 60% in a year. I loved the extroversion that offered me, especially dealing face-to-face with independent producers. It gave me a great deal of confidence to know that I was more knowledgeable than many of them in many basic areas.
Now that I am currently working with an active independent producer I am again reminded that my 20+ years of experience is making a huge difference for him.
So, my goal is to work as a film/TV Production Executive at some level - preferably more in the area of production than finance.

John Gaskin’s Specialties:

My specialty is in the area of training and smoothing over upsets amongst people of various temperaments. I have a several articles and a few courses/seminars which I have delivered, as well as a book "Walk The Talk". I sell the book on the internet and it is doing well. See www.talkfilm.biz
