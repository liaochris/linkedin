Brad Turner’s Summary

I am a strategic thinker and problem solver who leads customer-facing teams to develop and execute strategies that increase profitability, enhance organizational change, and improve sustainable customer relationships.

With previous experience in a top-tier strategy-consulting firm and in private equity operations, I am known for my versatility and ability to “jump in” and learn a new industry quickly. I have demonstrated an expertise in leading complex, cross-functional projects/programs and delivering them on time and on budget. I am recognized for my ability to build strong interpersonal relationships, which I capitalize on to lead and inspire teams and to foster client relationships.

Outside of work, I am passionate in my support of various charities in the areas of healthcare and early childhood education.

My most notable career accomplishments include:


• At Bartlett Holdings, I managed the strategic planning process and delivered a five-year strategic plan, operating budget, and execution plan for five business units. New plan identified several initiatives to dramatically increase growth.

• At Waterworks, I spearheaded the turnaround of an unprofitable commercial division by improving customer service and nurturing relationships with sales partners, such as developers and architects. Strong leadership and customer relationship management drove sales growth by 20%.

• At Gate Gourmet, I revamped the “Buy on Board” food program by implementing operational efficiencies and by aligning business operations with customer requirements, saving firm more than $1M annually in operating costs.

Brad and his family reside outside of Boston, MA
