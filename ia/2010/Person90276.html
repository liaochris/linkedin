Jason Ciment at LaDezign’s Summary

Having run a corporate magazine subscription business for almost 12 years at www.magmall.com, I've repackaged my experiences into a new consulting for hire company (www.LaDezign.com) where we provide SEO, Web Site Design, and Social Media marketing.

We've partnered with an insame team of developers (not outsourced as they are partners in blood, spirit & money) and are really thrilled to have our own almost open-source content management and ecommerce solution. The software platform is at www.adrecom.net where you can see dozens of clients running their sites on our software. You can also find some derivations of the Ecommerce platform at www.webcart.net and a new Corproate Portal solution at www.worldclassportal.com.

On a more personal note, I think it's worth sharing what I expect motivates me to wake up way too early every day and give it my all. It's not just about making money to pay tuitions for school, or to pay the ubiquitous obscene mortgage (because we live in a bubble community to be near the shul we enjoy) or any other financial incentive that would enable me to become a consumer. It's really about the excitement of dreaming up and channeling my creative energies into new projects that can help people do something better, buy something smarter or just be more efficient with their time.

I am sure that my Dad taking me out to buy the first IBM PC was just the first step that seems to have compelled me into the world of Technology. Oh, and did I mention I also owned stock in the original Commodore computer company because of their wonderfully unsuccessful Amiga product. Most recently, I've even spent 4 months cataloguing the techniques we've created for our search engine optimization services (for our very happy clients I might add) and recently written a book on these SEO tactics at www.seotimetable.com. Definitely a labor of love and I think it evidences how much I just enjoy the whole technology scene.
