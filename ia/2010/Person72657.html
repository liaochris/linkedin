Mary Beauchamp’s Summary

Gardens by Mary (GBM) specializes in creating, renovating, and maintaining gardens. Mary designs personalized spaces based on the client's goals and preferences. This process is approached from the perspective of an artist and photographer, to create an emotional response to the space, interest in all seasons, or creating a seasonal vignette. Her approach is based a common-sense, unpretentious passion for designing beautiful spaces. Utilizing the latest in design software,such as DynaSCAPE, Mary produces landscape plans as well as digital images of the proposed projects. Project Management services include estimating, phasing projects, coordinating subcontractors, sourcing materials, and on site management of project implementations. The gardening specialists at GBM provide professional gardening services through weekly maintenance visits. Mary also provides consulting services for staging properties in the real estate market.

Mary Beauchamp’s Specialties:

Project Management Skills, extensive knowledge of the characteristics and care of perennials, annuals, and shrubs. Experienced in using Dyanascape software, as well as using digital imaging to create visual tools to communicate concepts.
