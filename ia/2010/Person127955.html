Provide support to families an au pairs to help them have a successful relationship. My aim is to help families like yours find carefully screened, flexible, affordable live-in childcare providers who are appropriately matched with you.     (Privately Held; Individual & Family Services industry)

    July 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    I am currently working as a Local Childcare Coordinator for Cultural Care Au Pair, promoting the au pair program nationwide. The au pair program is regulated by the U.S. State Dept. and helps families find flexible, affordable childcare that costs approximately $340 per week, per family not per child. It also provides families with a great cultural exchange opportunity. Many families are interested in exposing their children to another culture and language, and the au pair program makes it easy to help your child learn more about the world. Cultural Care is the leading au pair agency in the U.S., having placed more than 75,000 au pairs over the last two decades.
