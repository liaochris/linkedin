Charles Leary’s Summary

I'm a Management Consultant with more than 7 years consulting experience in core business strategy and administration, project management, and marketing analysis. Interested in small business start up solutions/ analysis, stimulating demand through innovative pricing analysis, web portals and related areas. My experience ranges across several industries from business consultation, non-profit, retail, entertainment to hi-tech.

I lead a recently developed team of professionals and a team of seasoned consultants. Our company, Project IN:FUSE, aspires to fill the void of cooperative economics between social programming (non-profit organizations) with business development (for-profit models).

Our specialty is crafting strategies and deliverables for our customers to help acquire, retain, up-sell, cross-sell, and win back their customers. We do this by providing industry standard technology at a price sensitive cost point. Our approach is very focused on accountability, measurability and ROI.

I am motivated, an always a personable business professional with a talent for quickly mastering technology. I thrive in a deadline-driven environment, with excellent follow through and a creative, detail-oriented mind.
