Judith Lewis’s Summary

I am a Digital Marketing Specialist who has been working actively in the online environment since 1996. My expertise spans communities, audience development, SEO, SMO, PPC and other digital marketing channels.

I have written chapters on: analytics and SEO, measuring social media and general SEO for various industry publications. I write a semi-regular column on search and social media, have written guest spots on various marketing websites and am an in-demand speaker at conferences around the world including Internet World, A4U, SMX and SES.

I have managed an SEO hub where I taught SEO/M and helped develop peoples skills and abilities. I have worked on social platforms to develop communities, managed online reputation, worked with PR and revolutionised the approach to SEO within different companies I have worked in.

After being first introduced to the internet over 20 years ago during a programming course, I went on to study social interactions via live online systems, writing my undergraduate thesis on the topic, eventually moving in to the IT industry full time after relocating from Canada to the UK in 1996. I have worked within e-commerce and high tech as well as publishing before entering my current job agency-side.
