John Thompson’s Summary

We teach clients how to make SMART mortgage decisions that help them build financial freedom. We do this by helping them integrate a mortgage planning strategy into financial goals. Our clients are typically families who are in the early stages of real estate and wealth accumulation and are looking for professional advice on how to most efficiently achieve their goals. We tend to do a lot of hand-holding, explaining how different mortgages work and help people see the bigger picture as it relates to their home and mortgage.
