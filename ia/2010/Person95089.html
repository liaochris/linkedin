Jennifer Leuer’s Summary

I am the General Manager of a full-service provider of identity theft protection, detection and fraud resolution. My business division, ProtectMyID.com, is owned by ConsumerInfo.com, an Experian company.

My time is focused on developing ID theft products that enhance our ability to protect consumers' identities; implementing engaging, valuable and informative marketing campaigns; and providing an overall useful experience for the consumer seeking to avoid identity theft and identity fraud.
