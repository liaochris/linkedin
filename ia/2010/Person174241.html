Robb Hendrickson’s Summary

Founded Jellifish Int'l Corp. USA January 2002. Hold utility patent 6,133,516. Implemented company's initial direct-response marketing strategy creating wide awareness of the Jellifish brand within the guitar community and led company through sucessful transition to traditional two-step retail model. Company now at 7 SKUs, including Jellifish plectrum effect and HotRods bridge pins. Products sold through 1,000+ retail storefronts in the US and Canada, including Guitar Center, Sam Ash, Musician's Friend, Long & McQuade, Daddy's and George's as well as hundreds of independent retailers. Sales to 43 countries worldwide. US wholesale distributors include KAMAN/MBT ("Musicorp"), SLM MarketPlace, FMI Wholesale (Fender Musical Instruments Corp.) and HarrisTeller. Int'l wholesale distributors include The Power Group (Canada), Trinity UK (UK) and MyMusic (Australia) among others.
