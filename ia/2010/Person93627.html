Jeffrey Brandt’s Summary

Mr. Brandt is a former Chief Information & Knowledge Officer with over twenty five years of experience in the field of legal automation. He has worked with geographically distributed organizations in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom on information and knowledge management projects including: long range strategic planning, needs analysis, workflow management and reengineering, knowledge management, IT structure and personnel requirements, budgeting, system management/administration and complex systems design. Mr. Brandt is also a former Board of Directors member of the International Legal Technology Association/ILTA (the premier peer networking organization in the legal market). He is a frequent educational speaker at regional and national user, trade shows and industry conferences.
