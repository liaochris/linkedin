Shawn Pate (sylquard@gmail.com)’s Summary

I'm a geek at heart, and most of my life has been spent supplying or consulting on IT installations, but prefer people-centric rolls - building, developing and fostering long-term relationships.

After recently immigrating to New Zealand I decided on a change of direction and set about saving the world. As with most crusades you pretty quickly find you're not alone, and guess what: Thousands of others are already doing what I wanted to, and most of them probably doing a better job than I ever could.

Being a slightly technical type of guy my interests linger around alternative energy production, with a specific leaning toward solar energy solutions and hydrogen supplementation of petrol and diesel engines.
