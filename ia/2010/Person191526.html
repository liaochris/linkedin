Sarah Okumura’s Summary

I have over 25 years in the field of veterinary medicine, working as a veterinary technician, hospital administrator, and educator. My goals are to continue expanding my skills, to share my knowledge and to promote welfare for all animals.

Sarah Okumura’s Specialties:

Veterinary staff education and training, instructional design, veterinary management consulting, writing, development of training materials including Powerpoint and online presentations.
