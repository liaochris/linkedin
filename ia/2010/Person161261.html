Paul Pannone’s Summary

In January, 2010, E- Formal News and The Wedding Report entered into a join venture called EWedNews. The mission to formulate a fast paced, hard hitting news and consulting resource took root immediately, leveraging resources and contacts made over the past decade by both companies.

eWedNews launched ahead of schedule and under budget. http://www.ewdnews.com

In recognition, Paul Pannone was accepted by the Cambridge Who's who for life time experience and investigative reporting.

After 10 years of covering the formal wear busines, E- Formal News has been intergrated into the eWedNews system, reacher a broader readership in the wedding industry.

From 2000-2009, we've been providing formal wear and working with major TV shows and personalities, including Regis Philbin and guests of ABC's Live! With Regis and Kelly.

Other work includes MTV, Comedy Central and NBC specials.

Paul Pannone’s Specialties:

Since 1984, we have an extensive working knowledge of running a high volume retail establishment. We've successfully tied in the use of technology with old world, traditonal values.

We've been involved in photo shoots that include Moonlight, Bridal Guide magazine and often quoted in Fairchild publications, as well as other media. We also were involved with Steinway & Son's move to create a luxury line of formal wear.
