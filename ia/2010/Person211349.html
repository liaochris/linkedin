Vicki Prussak’s Summary

A successful business woman for 20+years I have gained much knowledge as to the strong connection between physical health and strength and mental and career competence and achievement.

As a Wellness coach I can offer effective support to other business people as they work toward achieving their personal and career goals.

A life-long health and wellness enthusiast, I meet people where they are. I fully understand the difficulty of commitment to a healthy self, and the necessity of it for optimal living – personally and professionally.

Passionate about helping others find their inner truth and believe everyone has inner strength to do what they need to do for themselves.
