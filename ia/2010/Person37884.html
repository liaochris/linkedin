Chris Heintz’s Summary

I'm a marketing communications pro with a history of helping gaming and technology companies meet their public relations, social media and marketing goals.

As an Account Executive at TriplePoint, I have gained invaluable experience working with products and teams of all shapes and sizes; from Mythic Entertainment, the EA studio responsible for 2008's Warhammer Online: Age of Reckoning, to Critical Thought Games, the one-man iPhone development shop behind the geoDefense franchise.

My media relations track record includes high-profile hits on a wide array of outlets, including The Tonight Show With Jay Leno, The New York Times, The Associated Press, USA Today, Washington Post, MSNBC, Forbes, VentureBeat, TechCrunch and more.

I believe it's supremely important for communications professionals to have not only knowledge of the markets they operate in, but genuine enthusiasm for the companies, products and technologies therein.

Knowledge and enthusiasm have guided my professional growth, helping to establish my position as one of the pioneers in iPhone game marketing and communications. Forbes.com quoted me about achieving success in the App Store!

My interests extend well beyond games into spaces I'd love to one day explore professionally: from nearby entertainment industries such as music and film, to emerging markets like craft beer.
