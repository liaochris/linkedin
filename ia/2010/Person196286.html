Mark Stibich, PhD’s Summary

Dr. Stibich specializes in creating efficient solutions for public health problems. He received his doctoral training from the Johns Hopkins School of Public Health and has been involved in multiple international research and intervention projects.

Currently, Dr. Stibich is a founder and Chief Scientific Officer for Xenex Healthcare Services, a company that uses a patented pulse xenon disinfection system to make patient care areas safer by reducing the microbial contamination (from "superbugs" such as MRSA, VRE and C. diff.). Dr. Stibich is responsible for creating effective protocols and training programs to assure that the technology is being used in an optimal manner within hospitals and other healthcare settings.
