Robert Chang, DO’s Summary

I’m a psychiatrist in the West Hollywood/ Beverly Hills area and I use cognitive behavioral therapy to help my patients look at ways their thoughts affect their feelings and behaviors.  I teach them skills and habits that will benefit their lives and help them to achieve their goals.

As a well-experienced clinician, I treat difficult conditions such as anxiety, depression, OCD, ADD, ADHD, PTSD, phobias, relationship issues, addiction and substance abuse.  If medication is needed, we’ll use it.  If one’s goal is to be medication free, we’ll work together toward it, at our mutually agreed pace. 

Mental health happens when every area of one’s life is in balance.  Mental health incorporates financial, physical, and spiritual health.  It also requires that one learns the necessary tools to cope with stress and to react appropriately to life’s uncertainties.  We’ll explore all these areas together. 

I can be contacted via my website: www.insightchoices.com or via phone: 323.375.0950.
