Debbie Burak’s Summary

I don’t really have a "Bio". What I have is a story. No degrees other than the one I have earned living 58 years. Still enrolled in that course with ongoing education and studies. My mom passed away 4 years ago this coming Aug 25th. I have spent the past 4 years doing everything in my power to bring this pension benefit to national attention through my website and supporting forums.

The majority of my day is consumed with responding to emails and acting as moderator on the forums. I continue to look for media opportunities and send out correspondence with the hope someone will see the story of a lifetime to change the lives of millions.

I am just a daughter who discovered something and fought to my mom's last breath to get the VA to release her funds. Just a daughter who stood in the gardens of Hospice House of Tallahassee and made a promise that my mother did not wait in vain. I have been that daughter who had run out of hope, money, resources and had no where to turn to pay for care. I'm that daughter who discovered that over a 9-year period my parents were entitled to $160.000 to help pay for their care and would have offered them more than they had.

I’m the daughter who knows she makes a difference for thousands of people just by sharing information, and I am humbled by that knowledge. It gives me a way to pay it forward to another son or daughter who is only just beginning this journey, and by doing so it allows me to rewrite the ending of my mother’s life.

I have taken what was an unknown VA Pension Benefit and brought it to national attention. I was instrumental in forcing former Secretary Nicholson of the VA to make a public statement about the pension in December of 06.

My goal is to make this pension as common knowledge as Social Security, Medicare etc. so that no veteran or surviving spouse who is in need or entitled will go without this financial resource.
