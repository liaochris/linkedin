Daniel Simone’s Summary

Participated in the Apollo Space Program. In the early '70's, under the tutelage of the novelist, Norman Mailer, wrote for The Village Voice. In the '80's, developed real estate and formed a famous restaurant in partnership with the actress/dancer, Chita Rivera. It was located in the Broadway District of New York City. In the early 2000, ghost wrote biographies of two prominent female actors. Currently writes a monthly feature for LONG ISLAND PULSE magazine, titled,BETWEEN THE LINES. It entails the summary of interviews conducted with famous authors and their profiles, and the review of their latest books. Presently, is on the roster of celebrity correspondents for THE BOULEVARD magazine.

Daniel Simone’s Specialties:

Writing fiction,teaching many genres` of writing, celebrity interviewing, financing, networking, real estate development.
