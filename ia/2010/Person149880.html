    The Law Office of Tanya M. Lee, PLLC

    (Legal Services industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    The Law Office of Tanya M. Lee, PLLC is a boutique law firm specializing in Immigration and Nationality Law. We provide reliable, efficient, and affordable legal representation for individuals and businesses located in all 50 states and abroad as authorized under Federal Law.

    We understand that the process to obtain a visa, green card, or citizenship can be complex and confusing. So we translate the legal-eze for you, explain your options in easy-to-understand terms, and walk you through the process step-by-step.

    We also understand that different people need different levels of assistance with petitions and applications. Therefore, unlike most immigration law firms, we are proud to offer three levels of service:

    1. Do-It-Yourself (DIY) Immigration Legal Kits

    2. Review (only) of Self-Completed Forms

    3. Legal Analysis of Your Case and Preparation of Your Petition Package
