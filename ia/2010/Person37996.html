Chris Johnson’s Summary

I work as a recruiting manager at CME Group. The Chicago Mercantile Exchange merged with the Chicago Board of Trade in July of 2007 and formed CME Group. We're a $30 billion company in the financial services industry that focuses on the trading of futures and options on futures.

I partner closely with our Products and Services, Corporate Development and Technology Divisions. We are currently looking for people who have a strong background working in the OTC and credit derivatives markets. We are also looking for people who have sales experience in the energy fields- specifically in the natural gas and metals areas.
