Duncan Smith’s Summary

Internet and New Media Consultant helping businesses and non-profit organisations gain from the new styles of communication and marketing the web now offers.

I am particularly keen to partner with individuals or businesses that want to develop online training courses on the membership website model. I would work with partners to create course material or re purpose existing courses creating a rich multimedia experience for the student. (Don't worry I do all the technical bit!). These would be joint venture, revenue sharing projects of equal mutual benefit to both parties.

I am a multimedia designer with many years producing photography, film, video and audio. Now wanting to fully embrace the potential of Internet based new media. I work with web sites, 3D animations, live action, online video and podcasting.

As an qualified teacher I am keen to work with groups large and small to make the most of existing skills and to develop new ones. I offer one to one and small group coaching in Internet marketing, building a complete web presence and personal branding.
