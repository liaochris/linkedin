Bryan Church’s Summary

Located 45 minutes north of downtown Phoenix on beautiful Bartlett Lake in the heart of the Tonto National Forest, Bartlett Lake Marina's family atmosphere and beautiful surroundings makes it a wonderful location for your friends and family to enjoy an unforgettable Arizona day on the water. Bartlett Lake is the second largest lake in the Phoenix Metro Valley area, with boatable surface area of 2,815 acres - more than Canyon Lake and Saguaro Lake, combined. Our lake isn't like many of the others your familiar with, it is better! It is often remarked by lake visits how much "less crazy" Bartlett Lake is over other popular valley lakes. Join us at Bartlett Lake for a stress-free day at with your friends and family on the water?

Bartlett Lake Marina is a state-of-the-art facility with full service boat storage (covered & uncovered), complimentary member boat launch's seven days a week, on-the-water gas pumping dock and private secure wet slips with full utilities (water, sewage & power). Our entire facility is protected behind pass coded protected gates, security fencing and closed-circut cameras. Your property's security and protection is our primary concern. Rest assured, your boat is safe with us!

Bryan Church’s Specialties:

Bartlett Lake Marina offers everything you need to enjoy a peaceful day boating, skiing, camping or just relaxing under the warm Arizona sun. Don't own a boat or interested in throwing a party on the water? We have an extremely well maintained fleet of pontoon and ski boats as well as a 45 foot, two story "Top Deck" party yacht with a slide, restroom, bar w/ sink, refrigerator and microwave will support 35 guests.
