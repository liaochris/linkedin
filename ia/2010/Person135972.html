mary beth janssen’s Summary

Mary Beth’s company, The Janssen Source offers experiential mindbody healing seminars, workshops, retreats. She speaks at prestigious events worldwide—and has shared the same stage with the likes of Dr. Mehmet Oz, Bob Greene, and Suze Orman, amongst others.

Personally certified by Deepak Chopra as MindBody Health educator for The Chopra Center for Well-Being, she's also certified massage therapist, aromatherapist, herbalist, and registered yoga teacher. She writes and is beauty/wellness resource for several media outlets. Her fifth book Pleasure Healing: Mindful Practices and Sacred Spa Rituals for Self Nurturing releases early 2009.

An organic beauty/ lifestyle expert Mary Beth’s been intimately involved in development/launch of organic beauty companies. As writer and educator, she speaks to organic, sustainable living in all its many facets~physical, environmental, spiritual.
