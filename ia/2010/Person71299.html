Frank Scotti’s Summary

Currently a presenter at Whizinars with Impact and partner, Creative Director at Recipe Food & Beverage Marketing.

I started my career in advertising working on video games and beer accounts in Los Angeles. As a young creative, I knew I had found the right career path. I learned the great fundamentals at Ogilvy, Atlanta. Ogilvy turned out to be a great experience, and a great brand in itself. I practiced the old school philosophies of David Ogilvy, and was exposed to new school advertising technologies, internet marketing, blogs and the beginnings of social media.

This brought me to Leo Burnett, Chicago. Leo Burnett gave me valuable brand experience working on long time clients like Proctor & Gamble, Allstate and McDonalds. Another highlight was being able to work, learn and collaborate with some of the best creative and strategic minds in the business...in the world.

These days, I've traded beer and video game clients for wine and cheese clients. I still use a sketch pad. Social networking is the norm. Clients are more engaged than ever. And there has never been a better time for relevant, engaging, memorable and actionable marketing solutions.
