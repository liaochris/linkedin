Kevin Pugh’s Summary

Author, trainer, presenter, and consultant to non-profit, government, corporate, and educational organizations. Coordinator of municipal/community partnership program in juvenile Restorative Justice since 2005. Professional experience since 1994 in Higher Education Administration, including Restorative Justice, student development, leadership development, diversity & social justice education, and staff supervision. Professional photographer since 1994, specializing in wedding photojournalism, portraits, and commercial photography.

Avid marathoner, triathlete, and competitive skier ranked #1 at Keystone Resort, #3 in Colorado, and #11 Nationally after the 2007 National Championships.

Kevin Pugh’s Specialties:

Alternative Dispute Resolution, Restorative Justice, Leadership Development, Social Justice & Diversity Education, Personal and Group Vision Development, Organizational Change

    (Law Enforcement industry)

    August 2007 — July 2009 (2 years )

    As a nationally certified Crime Prevention Specialist and Self-Defense Instructor, I've developed partnerships between police, government, schools, businesses and community to prevent crime and repair the harm caused by crime. Helped to reduce crime by 31% over four years, bringing it to the lowest level since 1971. The Restorative Justice program brings victims and offenders together for face-to-face meetings where they collaboratively design a contract for repair of harm. To date 98% of offenders who have completed the program have not re-offended, and 97% of all participants were very satisfied with the process.
