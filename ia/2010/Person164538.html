Grace Kang’s Summary

Retail is no stranger to Grace Kang, former buyer for Barney’s New York, Bloomingdale’s and consultant to DailyCandy’s retail venture Swirl.com. Having lived by her motto ~ keep it simple ~ Grace has been able to leverage her experience in the retail industry to help aspiring designers and store owners with simple ingredients for success. Grace has extensive buying experience ranging from contemporary sportswear to accessories to baby, gift and home decor. She also operates two retail stores in NYC, voted by Best of Citysearch New York for Best Children’s Clothing and Toys in 2009. Pink Olive has been featured on DailyCandy, TimeOut New York, Gotham, InStyle and Lucky Magazine and is a one-stop shop to find that perfect gift!
