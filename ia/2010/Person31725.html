Tom Campbell’s Summary

Our firm handles complex and class action cases for individuals and businesses victimized by unfair and deceptive business practices, fraud, false advertising, wrongful denial of insurance claims, harmful pharmaceuticals, systemic employment discrimination or catastrophic personal injury. The firm also refers cases to specialists when other lawyers may have more directly relevant experience.


Campbell Law focuses on class action (one or a few people representing a larger group), mass action cases (dozens to hundreds of individuals coming together as plaintiffs in one case), and individual cases in which the plaintiff has suffered significant financial losses.


Martindale Hubbell awarded Campbell Law and its principal, Thomas F. Campbell, its highest rating: “AV.” The AV rating signifies that lawyers and judges rate the firm’s legal ability as “preeminent” and that work meets the highest ethical standards. Campbell has tried dozens of matters to a successful conclusion from teacher tenure cases to class actions.

Thomas F. Campbell is presently serving as vice-chair of the plaintiff’s steering committee in the Hoffman v American Express litigation which is pending in the San Francisco Bay Area. Class members in this nationwide class action may get additional information about the case at the following web site:

http://www.hoffmanvamexinsuranceclassaction.com/
