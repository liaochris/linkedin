Jonathan Romano’s Summary

In year 2006, I started working with The Learning Annex, America's leading producer in Adult Education since 1980. We work within a multitude of events and celebrities such as, The Wealth Expo's, featuring Donald Trump, Richard Branson, Suze Orman, George Foreman, Warren Buffett & more.


In year 2005, I founded U-S-E Me an Internet Marketing firm based out of Manhattan, Las Vegas & Los Angeles. U-S-E Me, is an acronym for Understanding Search Engines. I have worked with notables such as: Disney, Michael Jackson, WB11, ABC, ESPN, Howard Stern, Hot 97.5, channel 5, JETS football team, The Knicks, Rupert Murdoch, Verdura, The Oscars, Al Pacino, Law & Order, Dove, Geico & too many more to list.


In year 2002, I produced multiple events working with many of the USA's well known & famous news channels on TV & the Radio as well as Sony Entertainment, Madison Square Garden, The Beacon Theater, Radio City Music Hall, The Gateway Playhouse, Leonards of Great Neck & many other venues.


In year 2000, I toured world-wide performing with various artists for charity events. These events included performances with Stevie Wonder, The Allman Brothers Band, David Gilmour, Robert Randolph, Gerardo Velez (Jimi Hendrix Woodstock percussionist), Richie Cannata (Billy Joel's saxophonist), Steve Boyer (Billy Joel's Recording Engineer on The Stranger Album), Buddy Guy & Buddy Miles.
