Mihir Karkare’s Summary

That companies and brands will have to adapt themselves and their strategies to embrace Social Media is a given. Seeing the emerging need in this space, and with the intention of being part of this exciting process, I became part of Social Wavelength. We offer Outsourced Services of Social Media Management to global organizations.

Since college, I've always been a self starter, having held various positions in different Societies and Councils in college, including the National Service Scheme (NSS) chapter, the NEN Chapter (of which I was one of the core members) as well as the students council, where I was the General Secretary. I've been part of the college Debating team.

In my first job, as a Software Developer, I worked mainly on the Microsoft Platform, where I developed Enterprise Grade applications for the Desktop, Mobile as well as Web environments.

I have always been an early adopter when it came to Web2.0 and Social Media technologies/tools/platforms. The very premise of Social Media, of people having an opportunity to make themselves heard, to connect with like minded people and to make sense of information together is exhilarating for me.

Mihir Karkare’s Specialties:

Social Media Campaign conceptualization and execution. Creating long term Social Media strategies. Strong Technical grounding, experience in building enterprise grade applications.
