Anna Kim-Williams’s Summary

I am an accomplished executive with more than 15 years of progressive experience as a savvy business communications professional with a strong background in public affairs, media strategy, branding and positioning, strategic counsel, reputation risk management including issues and crises communications and executive communications.

I am known for delivering business results, building and leading high performance teams, leading change and creating and executing communications strategy to meet the needs of the business. My management experience ranges from driving global strategic public relations campaigns and driving sales growth through creative tactics to internationally covered crises with multiple objectives and audiences.
