Anna Altic’s Summary

Anna is a proud member of the East Nashville community and has been since 2002 where she lives with her husband and 5 children. She began her career in Real Estate the latter part of 2003 and has enjoyed a great deal of success selling in and around Nashville’s urban communities.

Greener Living has been a multi year labor of love intended to create a “one stop shop” for everything green and sustainable in Middle Tennessee. While green homes and green building are an integral part of the growth of the sustainability culture locally, lifestyle choices and access to things such as safe sidewalks, green spaces, and healthy locally grown food are also an integral part of Nashville’s emergence as an environmentally progressive city and Anna intends to provide comprehensive info about both.

She has spent the last few years studying and receiving her EcoBroker and NAR Green designations. In addition, she petitioned and consulted with the Middle Tennessee Multiple Listing service to have over 25 green features added to the MLS and is currently chairing the Environmental committee for the Greater Nashville Association of Realtors.

Anna has also become a contributing columnist for Agent Genius Magazine and in March of 2010 was asked by Trulia to write a column for their national newsletter which lead to a week long stint as their nationally featured blogger. She has served on GNAR’s Top Producer Panel and served as Vice President of Lockeland Elementarys’ PTO 2009/2010.

Upcoming goals include furthering sustainability in Nashville, expanding vegetable gardens in metro schools, and achieving my LEED Green Associate Certification.
