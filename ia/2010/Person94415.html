Jen Hale’s Summary

Boston based Graphic Designer specializing in Print and Web Design. My background is in retail collateral, presentation materials, and editorial design. Clients include: Gap, The Children's Place, Lantis Eyewear, Campus Magazine, and Providence Media. I am currently in the process of completing a Web Design and Development certificate at RISD.

Jen Hale’s Specialties:

retail marketing collateral, editiorial design and presentation materials.

Jen Hale’s Experience

  • Graphic Designer

    Providence Media

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    October 2007 — July 2009 (1 year 10 months)

    Primarily responsible for the design and re-design of advertisements running in the publications; Providence Monthly, SO Rhode Island, East Side Monthly, In Prov, and Campus Magazine. In December of 2008 I re-designed the look and logo of Campus Magazine. I was also in charge of updating the Providence Online web site, home to all of the publications online.     (Public Company; GPS; Retail industry)

    June 2005 — December 2006 (1 year 7 months)

This often included detailed illustrations of beading, embroidery, and various denim techniques. These were then used in presentations for the adoption of each seasons’ line, for the girls' department. I have also assisted with mechanical drawings and illustrated a graphic for use on a garment.
