Ashwanth Fernando’s Summary

Passionate about building robust, high performance enterprise applications that deliver business value.

I primarily work on Java/Java EE and languages and stacks that are built atop Java and Java EE.

In spite of me being a developer at heart, I increasingly find myself comfortable in projects that allow me to execute in broader roles such as tech lead, architect, technology evaluation, task management amongst distributed teams, increasing agility.

I strive to be technology agnostic, believe that the right technology stack should be used (not necessarily the latest) to solve a particular business requirement, reduce complexity and to increase development agility.

I find that I focus a lot on the work that I do, and not on what I am. This prevents me from getting locked down to a particular technology or framework and limiting my career from becoming diversified. What I bring to the table are my expert design and architecting skills which can be applied to the usages of specific technology stacks and platforms with a few days of learning.

I am an agilist at work and always try to do away with unnecessary processes and focus on building high performance teams that are lean and mean.
