Lisa Fields’s Summary

Lisa Fields, founder of Lisa Fields & Associates, is a creative trainer, public speaker and social media consultant. Her passion for visual storytelling, customer relationship management, digital communication, leadership development and conflict resolution enables her to produce results that strengthen organizations and motivate leaders and employees.

For more than twenty years, Lisa has empowered individuals and challenged organizations to reach their full potential. An adult education scholar with a master’s degree in adult education from North Carolina A&T State University, her clients have included small businesses and nonprofit organizations as well as Fortune 500 companies.

Her strong dedication to needs analysis and defining growth opportunities has led her to explore under homes with pest control specialists, shadow paramedics on medical assistance calls and work side-by-side with 911 operators.

Lisa has completed post-graduate work in the study of exceptional learners and is certified in the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator assessment. She holds a paralegal certificate and is a scholarship recipient for the Center for Creative Leadership. Lisa also serves as an instructor for the American Management Association and an adjunct professor at a number of higher education institutions in North Carolina.
