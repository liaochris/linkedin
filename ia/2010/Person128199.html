L.R. Hand’s Summary

“Formal education will make you a living; self-education will make you a fortune.” – Jim Rohn

My name is L.R. Hand and I am a professional internet marketer in Texas. I have been in the Home-based business industry for over 9 years. While receiving my college education in the mid to late 1990’s, I never felt completely satisfied with the idea of working for someone else for the next 25-30 years of my life.

I learned from watching struggling adults throughout my life that I didn’t want to be a slave to anyone or anything — including my boss or my job. With this in mind, I decided to explore the world of Free Enterprise and being my own boss. I soon found out that this industry offers many “Freedoms” that just aren’t available anywhere else.

I have been blessed to have been mentored and trained by the best in this industry in both offline and online marketing. I have also learned to be proficient in both old school and new school techniques.

My goal is to provide today's network marketers with the best of the best in Leadership, Mentoring and Personal Development. I believe it takes three major things to be very successful in this industry: The Dream, The Team and The System.

If you are struggling in your current network marketing business, contact me and let me assist you in building your marketing empire.
