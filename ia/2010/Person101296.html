  • Chief Strategy Officer & Founder

    Peer Squared (Peer²)

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    Frustrated with advertising and marketing options online that deliver dismal results and poor consumer engagement, I conceived a new way to market online.

    Peer Squared (peer²) is a peer-endorsed online marketing platform that rewards consumers for promoting the brands and products they love across the Internet.

    Web users choose advertiser content from our site and share it with contextual messaging to their friends and family. If their friends engage with the content, they earn reward points that are redeemable for a broad selection of items on Amazon. All for free. The idea is simple: people on social networking sites are more likely to engage with something their peers have posted on their profiles, than they are with the banners that are served up on every page. This model rewards consumers for making the contextual connection with their friends and our advertisers. And as a result we deliver high quality peer endorsed engagements to our sponsors.
