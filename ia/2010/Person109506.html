Johann Van Tonder’s Summary

Over ten years experience in online media, having started out in newspapers (internet wasn't around). In 1995 I was one of the earliest bloggers - before the term existed - uploading daily photos with discussions and inviting comments, using raw HTML and FTP.

After an exciting career of assignments ranging from conflict to rock concerts, and following some time as photo editor, I became one of the first multi-media editors. This allowed me to combine two passions - photojournalism and internet business.

I've served in various management roles in the digital space. Currently, I manage a labs-environment with a portfolio of e-businesses at Naspers, reporting directly to the group CEO. Naspers is a multi-national media firm with listings on the JSE and LSE.

Having completed an MBA, with a dissertation on e-strategy, I am currently doing a PhD. Through this process I aim to fine-tune my ability to navigate information-overload and make sharper decisions that can help solve complex problems in the organisation.
