Michael W Cerkas, MS’s Summary

Meeting & Working with Michael reinforces two images: Compassion for People & Passion for Writing. He is an incredibly perceptive individual with an acute ability to see the BIG picture, express himself and effectively communicate concepts, emotions and information via writing. Experiencing his easy reading style results in vibrant and crystal clear images of thought. Michael can effectively distill and translate complex or technical information into content that is easy to comprehend and retain.

Michael is a life-long learner, optimistic and self-aware. He strives to be innovative and fair, always searching for the good in others. Michael's experience encompasses organizational design, strategic planning, visioning and judgment. He excels at leading and developing others, problem solving and writing.

Michael has led empowered teams toward accountability, improved performance & process. He has coached, mentored and remotely managed many others. Michael leverages his seasoned interpersonal skills with enthusiasm, creativity, humility and change management. His professional development includes key leadership roles with industry-specific associations.

A varied background characterizes work in the technology and utility industries for more than 30 years, always writing. Michael has owned a computer sales & consulting business, developed systems and managed GIS operations, IT performance and projects. His academic accomplishments include a Master of Science degree in Management and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Marketing. He is equally comfortable in the technical aspects of technology. Michael was the Conference Chair for the 2008 GITA Geospatial Infrastructure Solutions Conference, held in March in Seattle, WA. He is proud to be included as a biographee in nine (9) professional publications, noted at bottom.

Michael dedicates himself to several areas, including family, writing, golden retrievers, technology and change management.
