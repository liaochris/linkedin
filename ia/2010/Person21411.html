Ben Boissevain’s Summary

Investment banker with twenty years experience in mergers & acquisitions, initial public offerings and venture capital. In-depth technology, media and telecommunciations expertise combined with strong international background with on-site European experience and fluent German. Aggressive and creative valuation, structuring, negotiation and execution of transactions in the technology, media and telecommunications sectors.

Mr. Boissevain frequently speaks at industry conferences, appears on TV with Fox Business News and is quoted in the press, including articles in The New York Times, Inc. Magazine, The Daily Deal and numerous industry newsletters. He is a member of the New York Software Association, Assistant Treasurer for the Huguenot Society, and Board Member and Chair of the Programming Committee for the Board of the MIT Enterprise Forum of New York.

Ben Boissevain’s Specialties:

Mergers & acquisitions in the technology, media and telecommunications sectors. Cross-border transactions.
