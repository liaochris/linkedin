Adam Feinberg’s Summary

Prior to joining 7city, Adam was the Global Head of Training and Development for GlobeOp Financial Services, a leading hedge fund administrator. Before this, he worked in an Operations capacity at GlobeOp covering more than 20 different hedge funds using a variety of trading strategies. Adam’s career in training started at a boutique financial services training firm where he created technology-training modules for the trading desks of JP Morgan Chase and Paine Webber. He received his B.S. in Finance from New York University, Stern School of Business. Upon graduation, Adam worked at AIG Trading Group in the foreign exchange back office and base metals middle office before becoming a Product Manager in Precious Metals Structured Derivatives and Special Business.
