Dale LIttle’s Summary

Dale Little is a Business Strategist based in Northern Virginia. Her clients range from entrepreneurs with a budding idea for a new business to established businesses throughout the United States. Dale bases interactions with her clients on four main principles: Objectivity, Innovation, Competence, Confidentiality

Excerpts from a recent interview share insight into what drives Dale:

What makes your process different from that of other Business Consultants?
I really believe that every business can succeed. I understand that the change that makes the difference doesn't always have to be monumental. Every business works with budgets and financial documents, sales and marketing, employee retention, customer service, and growth and development - what I call business direction. Approaching these areas from a different perspective, reviewing existing processes with an objective eye, and then tweaking them... pushing them... can mean all the difference to the bottom line.

How did you develop the strategies that you currently utilize? During the first 15 years of my work life, I was employed by multiple types of businesses. Everything from retail to office admin to property management to a national sales & marketing rep. Regardless of what position I started in, I always rose fairly quickly to a management position. I recognized the business components that were lacking and initiated programs and techniques that took those components from fiscal liabilities to fiscal advantages. I developed a reputation for raising productivity and income to substantially higher levels in as little as 90 days.

I believe that every business can succeed. Being a success takes a lot of hard work, time and energy. If a business owner is willing to do the work, together, we can make it happen... some times maybe in as little as 90 days.

    Dale Little, understands that you’re in business for two primary reasons — to make money, and to be your own boss. There’s a third reason that should fit in there somewhere — enjoying what you do. Dale works with Small Business owners and entrepreneurs to review existing practices and plans and then develop revised or new programs that help business owners once again find joy in their work, and Make A Profit!

    It’s often said that one must spend money to make money. In many instances that’s true, but it doesn’t mean you have to spend every penny of your capital or your profits in an effort to stabilize or grow your business. Dale firmly believes that many positive outcomes can be achieved with little or no money.
