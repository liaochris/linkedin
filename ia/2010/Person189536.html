Siddharta Govindaraj’s Summary

My primary interest is in improving the way software is delivered. I take great interest in lean and agile software development methodologies. I am also interested in the social aspects of software development and how it relates to the technical aspects. I started a company, Silver Stripe Software Pvt Ltd, to work further in the area of software delivery.

I've been a developer, tester and project manager (sometimes all at the same time!) in a variety of projects. Have worked in C, with a little bit of Java and Perl at various points. I currently work with Python and Javascript.

I jointly led and managed a team to develop a mobile browser from scratch. The browser was developed in C by a team of 10 developers.

On a personal level, I am interested in expanding the startup and tech ecosystem in India. I've been an organizer in two unconferences, with two more coming up this year. I have a passion for self-organisation and decentralised systems :)

I'm also one of the organizers of Proto.in, a bi-annual event that showcases startup companies to an audience of venture capitalists, technologists and media, and the co-organizer of the Chennai OpenCoffee Club, a place where entrepreneurs from Chennai meet once a month.

I help conduct Lean and Agile software development events and seminars in Chennai through the Chennai Agile User Group.

Have spoken at

- Agile Chennai
- Agile India Bangalore, 2009
- Symphony Agile Day
- Lean Systems And Software Conference, Atlanta 2010
