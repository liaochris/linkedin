Jennifer Filla’s Summary

I believe every development office should have the benefits of professional prospect research. At Aspire Research Group we provide academic institutions and local and national nonprofits with in-depth prospect research and advice on using research throughout the development program to grow donor support.

How do we use prospect research to help fundraisers raise more money? We crunch the numbers and find patterns through data analysis and summarize the stories of fascinating, philanthropic people and organizations with prospect profiles and we create moves management systems that work for you, the fundraiser.

Sign-up for our free bi-monthly newsletter or view a sample prospect profile and other free resources at http://www.AspireResearchGroup.com
