Tom Clark’s Summary

Turning Goals into Marketplace Success

We have a track record of success. We apply strategy, encourage creativity, demand communication, instill collaboration, manage costs, negotiate win-win agreements, train sales forces, design marketing plans, and eliminate bottlenecks. We do whatever it takes...whatever you need, to bring a product to market.

Our past and current client base included Fortune 500 companies to entrepreneurial start ups. We handle entire development projects, or work on a specific part of the process that needs a breakthrough. We also can, and have, applied our skills to develop and entirely new business.

At Clic Now, we combine a C-level perspective with the willingness to hit the pavement. We make things happen. We apply our experience, perspective, and leadership to clear hurdles and each stage of development, including:

Concept Product Planning Design
Packaging Manufacturing Marketing
Distribution Licensing Shelf Positioning
Sales Strategic Planning Global Perspective

Whether you have a new product idea or need to extend an existing product line, we can spearhead the process of developing it from vision to revenue contributor

Vision and passion are important to us. Without it, energy fades, progress is impeded, and success fades away.

Let us help.
