David Biernbaum’s Summary

David Biernbaum...

o Since 1977, is a consumer packaged goods professional in the field of marketing, retail sales, broker management, and business development.

o Has a far-reaching background and resilient career with major national brands, niche and licensed products, private label, and all types of specialty brands.

o Has a history of building efficient approaches that constantly result in true success, equity, and rapid development in retail sales.

o Leadership was highly instrumental at Zooth Inc. during its most propelling growth years, leading to the profitable sale of the brand to Gillette and later, Procter and Gamble.

o Helped develop Vi-Jon Labs to be a major force in HBC personal care products leading to its highly profitable industry merger.

o At the Gillette Go's Oral B, David led sales forces through high-impact maneuvers often shattering record sales and breaking competition.

o At Beecham, David led the initial launch of Aquafresh toothpaste, a major global brand.
o Has written hundreds of columns for trade magazines such as MMR, Chain Drug Review, PL Buyer, and quoted on CNBC.

o Works directly with retail executives on numerous industry committees ...also an instructor at colleges and frequent speaker for industry seminars and panels.

o David is a "BrainTrust" panel member at Retailwire.com., the very popular blog read
o every day by retail buyers and executives.
