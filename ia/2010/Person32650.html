SUMMARY

Computer Scientist professional with more than 17 years of experience in software development in the transportation, finance, telecommunication, biotechnology, clinical trial and medical device industries.

Excellent track record of directing software engineers and program managers in regulated environments (FDA, cGxP, HIPAA, Clinical, SEC), non-regulated environments (R&D, BioTech) and reporting to C-Level Managers (CTO, ESVP, SVP, CEO).
