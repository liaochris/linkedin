Nikki Scaplen’s Summary

Professional creative director, art director and graphic designer with a clear understanding of business marketing needs and how to design and create solutions that not only look good, but solve a problem, connect with an audience, and communicate a message.

Projects and range of experience extend to branding, corporate identity, trade advertising, marketing collateral, direct mail campaigns, exhibit graphics, and web design/development with knowledge of SEO. Develops strategies to deliver measurable results and help organizations differentiate themselves through better and impactful design. Work has appeared both nationally and internationally in a variety of industries. Key clients have included The Church of Christ, Oxygen Snowboards, Atomic Ski, Teletrol Systems, Array USA, Centrix Bank, Mother Caroline Academy & Education Center and Spruce Floral, Boston.
