Quantitative:
1) Strong knowledge of Statistics,Algebra,Stochastic Calculus, Differential Equations and Probability and in the process of taking several graduate level courses in the topics this Fall Semester at Baruch-CUNY
2) Published papers in Computational Fluid Dynamics and Financial Econometrics.
3) Currently researching and developing statistical arbitrage strategies. (Cointegrated commodity ETF pairs)

Programming:
1) Currently in the process of taking advance graduate level Object Oriented Programming for Finance course utilizing C++.
2) Have conducted optimization and statistical analysis using MATLAB and SPSS.

Financial Modelling:
1) Have built models in C++ to utilize bootstrap method to attain yield curves and several other bond price,duration,convexity related calculators.
