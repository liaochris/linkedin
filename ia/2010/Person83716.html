Ian Bissell’s Summary

Experienced online strategy consultant with over 20 years experience within the media industry. Previously was responsible for developing and implementing the online strategy for a global media business before moving into a consultancy role.

Specializes in the business-to-business media sector and has worked with information, magazine, conference and exhibition businesses.

Ian Bissell’s Specialties:

Developing a strategy which drives revenue and profit growth;

Shaping the strategy through analysis and research of the business’ current market position;

Working with operations managers to draw up practical and achievable implementation plans;

Building online products in collaboration with customers;

Leveraging technology to provide easy-to-use online tools that help customers do better business.
