BIO

At AT&T, I oversee strategic business development and product management for wireless roaming and managed services partnerships with top companies in the Telco, MSO, Satellite, Mobile Handset and Consumer Electronic sectors. Since joining the company in 2004, I have been instrumental in developing long term, C-level relationships with strategic partners, significantly enhancing company profitability and increasing the visibility of the AT&T brand.

Prior to joining AT&T, I held key sales and marketing management positions at Global Crossing, Dynegy, Level 3 and Dell Computer. I have a BS degree in Mass Communications from the University of Texas and an MBA from Colorado Tech. I am presently enrolled part time at Northwestern California University School of law.
