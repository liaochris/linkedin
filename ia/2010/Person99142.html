  • Executive Producer

    Domino Day 2001 (Endemol USA)

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    August 2001 — December 2001 (5 months)

    Showrunner for this prime-time nonfiction special from Endemol USA for ABC. This hour profiled five twentysomething Americans who journeyed to Holland just weeks after 9/11 to join a multinational team of 75 Europeans in an attempt to set a new world record in domino building. That their knees ached from 10-hour days of crouching to build intricate domino structures was about the only thing that didn't surprise the Americans in this remarkably affecting fish-out-of-water chronicle.. The special was #1 in its time-slot in households and the demo, and retained 100% of its lead-in. As the first "reality" show produced for American TV after 9/11, "Domino Day" answered viewers' calls for a new kind of reality: one based on character- and story-development, not insults and humiliation. Soon, though, it was back to business as usual
