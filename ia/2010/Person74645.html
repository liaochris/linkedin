Geraldine (Gerry) Wendel’s Summary

It's been a long, long journey! And I'm on yet another adventure! But where have I been?

At the age of 13 I worked in a real estate office, keeping MLS listings up-to-date (in the days of paper and pen). During my high school years, I counseled kids at a Summer day camp.

After high school, worked part-time as a cashier at Farrell's Ice Cream Parlour (anyone remember that chain?), then shortly after plunged directly into the corporate world, spending 5 years with Scholastic, maintaining their vast database of educational facilities and doing some programming as well. Started attending college in the evening during that time.

Next, Volvo Cars of North America, initially as a Data Analyst, then moving up through the ranks of various positions, mainly in the market research realm. In the 80's I became particularly nerdy, programming extensively in SAS.

My analysis skills were honed through many years of working with fascinating data on the car industry, including customer satisfaction, buyer behavior, product quality, after sales, advertising, oh, the list goes on.

Later, I moved into management roles while completing my 4-year degree...whew...took a long time, but it felt GOOD!

I managed many, many projects, product clinics, researching advertising concepts and campaigns, and created a brand premiumness score card (I'm especially proud of that one!).

Caught the school "bug" yet again, made studying my "hobby", and earned my MBA three years ago.

But all good things come to an end. I chose not to move with Volvo back to New Jersey. Instead, I chose a path where I have truly come into my own. I'm involved in so many things now, and absolutely love it! I always was a multi-tasker; combine that with the high-energy everyone tells me I have, and I'm unstoppable!

Looking forward to directing, managing, supporting, and assisting you in future ventures. Please let me know how I can help.
