    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

  • Founder & CEO

    B2L

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    May 1996 — December 1999 (3 years 8 months)

    B2L was one of the first web agencies in France. I created it with a students loan of 10k € on the HEC campus in Paris. With clients such as Chanel, Peugeot, 20th Century Fox, Mars etc, it quickly became one of the leading web agencies. I sold it to the ad network BBDO at the end of 1999.
