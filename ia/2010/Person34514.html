Carolyn Dempsey’s Summary

I am a San Francisco-based writer, marketing copywriter, editor, researcher, and grant writer with marketing communications, event planning and PR experience. My specialties include wine and enology, food and nutrition, dining, travel, health, and lifestyle. Some of my other research focal points include brain science and neuroplasticity, chronic pain research, psychology and psychopharmacology.

Currently working as a freelance copywriter and editor, my primary client since 2005 has been the marketing department of the San Francisco Chronicle.

A varied background and a creative spirit contribute to my personal ethos of continuously striving to be the best I can in mind, body, and soul. This characterizes my professional endeavor to provide a valuable contribution and productive interaction for each work project and with every colleague.

I am also an abstract painter, and have co-written two screenplays.
