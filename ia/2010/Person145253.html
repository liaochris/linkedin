Mike Dicks’s Summary

I set up Bleedinedge in 2008 as a digital consultancy, training company and development resource so I that could work with the best producers and broadcasters, developing innovative new 360 degree formats..

I have managed companies, worked alone, created teams and worked in teams, I enjoy them all.

I specialise in developing and delivering entertainment content, utilising games, television, the internet and mobile phones, most notably as Executive Producer on the BBC’s online soap Wannabes. Nowadays I spend a lot of my time training and speaking about Multi-platform production and commerce, and have been lucky enough to provide training for Channel 4, the Entertainment Master Class and Skillset, as well as speaking at conferences that include ITVT,Showcomotion, Games Eden and Cinekid.

As well as listening to the sound of my own voice, I’m currently working as a consultant with Gameware Development to develop an international market for their talents, ARTVPS, a Cambridge based Technology Company, developing a radical new rendering engine and Eye Film and Television working on their new interactive drama, Tempting Fates.

With the small amount of time I’m left with I’m developing an interactive movie called TXT, a great new game-to-TV format for kids called GAJT and a few secret projects you’d have to buy me a shandy to find out about. Oh and I’m creating a Crowd Funded Film Fund called Who Wants to Make a Movie? (WW2MM)

This year I was appointed to the board of Northern Fim and Media and as a judge at the Rose D’Or International Media festival in Lucerne.

Prior to launching Bleedinedge, I worked for leading production companies Somethin Else, Illumina and Bomb Productions where I helped develop the pioneering project Fightbox, a BBC2/3 TV series that combined online gaming with the TV style Robot Wars. Previous clients also include Channel 4, Nickelodeon,Scalextric and the BBC.
