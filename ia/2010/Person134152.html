Mark L. Rosenberg’s Summary

I specialize in representing medium-sized businesses in all aspects of their operations. My goal is to help them become or stay successful in their fields. From initial incoporation through capital infusions through contract negotiations, my focus is assisting these companies in each of their stages. I have been fortunate enough to see a number of my clients grow into multi-million dollar companies.

Mark L. Rosenberg’s Specialties:

Corporate formation, private securities offerings, structuring of debt and equity placements, tax and corporate planning, and all other aspects of legal counseling for businesses.
