Billing Boss By Sage’s Summary

We asked some small business owners why they don't use accounting software, and they explained that it ﻿didn't really meet their needs: too complex, too hard to learn accounting, and no real payback.

So we started from scratch and built something to solve their #1 need: get paid quickly and easily. Billing Boss is focused on getting that job done for small businesses, and on keeping things simple – and easy to hand off to an accountant or bookkeeper who can handle the rest. We want Billing Boss to be simple and easy to use.
