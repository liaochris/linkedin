Lisa Chipetine’s Summary

Lisa builds companies "Roadmaps for the Future".

Lisa is a Technology Translator, Certified Social Media Strategist and Teacher, Business Process Specialist and PMO Builder. Lisa spearheads cutting-edge initiatives from R&D through implementation. Currently spearheading Video/Multimedia and Mobile SMS/Text Messaging Strategies.

Seasoned professional with an extensive background in technology, operations, training, document and human resources management responsibility (finance/energy/communications). Domestic and heavy international exposure. Active leader in business initiatives involving IT teams: business process development and documentation, training development and implementation, enterprise content management structure build-outs, including web-based training and on-line communities specializing in wiki development. Strong project and portfolio management (PMO) and communications expertise (both verbal and written). Able to evaluate and implement advancements in business processes and technology.

Lisa actively volunteers her talents and heralds a monthly mentorship program, whereby she teaches artists business and marketing skills. She is the President of the Studio Art Quilt Associates (http://www.saqa.com) and is currently launching a special project called "The Visioning Project".

She and her husband, Jeffrey Chipetine, participate in the Pilot n Paws rescue program for animals.
