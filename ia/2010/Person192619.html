Simon Hayhurst’s Summary

Simon has over a decade of general management experience across hardware, software, SaaS and solutions products in both enterprise and channel driven go to markets.

Simon has been and continues to be an advisor to numerous start-ups, with a focus on business strategy, product strategy and go-to-market.

Previously, Simon led the Video and Audio Tools business at Adobe through a turnaround from declining revenues and 21% market share to 45% share with market leading revenues and units. His final release, CS5, was heralded as a breakout release showing 60% version over version growth. During his tenure he spear-headed a shift in go-to-market at a suite level, investments in production workflows to enable searchable and internet enabled video, driving wins with marquee customers such as BBC, CNN, Hearst, and the shift from pure shrinkwrap software to include SaaS offerings.

Prior to Adobe Simon led the advanced graphics business at Silicon Graphics, a portfolio of software and hardware solutions with sales in excess of $300M. He specialized in bringing new technologies to market innovating across many markets including scientific and medical markets, government intelligence, digital media, and tele-presence.

Prior to SGI Simon was a programmer and engineering manager at IBM focused on video conferencing and collaborative working products, with 2 patents filed in this domain.
