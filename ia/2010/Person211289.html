Vicki Caldwell’s Summary

Vicki has been the Director of Relocation and Business Development at Rusinak Real Estate since 1993 having personally relocated hundreds of families to the Colorado Springs area.
Rusinak Real Estate has always had an award-winning relocation division that constantly strives to remain at the forefront of the industry. In 2005, the relocation division earned the coveted RELO Quality Certification award from Leading Real Estate Companies of the World. In 2008, they earned their re-certification for another three years. This highly sought-after designation speaks volumes for the quality of the company and their reputation in the relocation industry.
Vicki is currently serving on the Leading Real Estate Companies of the World Advisory Council and is heading up a military project team.
Vicki and her team of relocation professionals can help relocate individuals and families to and from the Colorado Springs area or virtually anywhere in the world.
