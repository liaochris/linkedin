stewart.linkedin@gmail.com No IDK

I am passionate about social media and helping companies create a Powerful Online Digital Footprint.

As a New Media strategist, I generate conversations with like minded individuals and profit by turning those conversations into relationships. I provide one-on-one coaching as well as group seminars on Social Media. But, for every lecture and training I do, I attend at least 2 conferences or training to learn more about this quickly evolving media. For that reason, I don't consider myself an expert in the field of Social Media but a lifelong student always eager to learn and share as I apply my education.

My marketing and branding knowledge has expanded over the last several years to include industries such as consulting, healthcare and recruitment. I show companies how to apply social media strategies to target leads for successful results using Blogs, Twitter, Facebook and of course LinkedIn.

I am an open social networker. My experience includes integrating traditional marketing with new media and social marketing strategies as well as recruitment marketing. I serve as project manager for small and large projects. I create and manage word press blogs and websites for various clients. I receive personal satisfaction when my clients see results from my experience and efforts. I document their beginning digital footprint and show the results after applying the critical steps that create their powerful Online Footprint.
