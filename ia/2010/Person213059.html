I'm voice talent, based in Denver, Colorado, but from my digital recording studio I provide professional voiceovers for clients in all parts of the world.

►Specialties

Voiceovers for industrial and corporate narrations, e-Learning courses, commercials, radio station imaging, TV imaging, IVR systems, streaming web audio, telephone on-hold messages, voicetracking, and more.

►Clients

My voiceovers for commercials include companies such as Vectra Bank, Qwest, DISH Network, Case Logic, Shane Company, McDonald Audi, and many others.

I've also been the narrator on e-Learning courses for Harvard University, Canon, IHOP, BestBuy.com, Republic Waste Services, TeleTech, KPMG and more.

My industrial and corporate narration voiceovers include those for IBM, Sun Microsystems, Credit Union of Colorado, the Denver Convention & Visitors Bureau, HP, American Water Works Association, SAP and many more.

►Complete Project Management

Along with my experience as a voice talent and narrator, I'm an experienced producer and writer. This means that in addition to providing voiceovers for your project, I can handle the entire project for you from start to finish.

In addition, I have working relationships with graphic designers, photographers, camera operators, plus video and film producers who can be included in your project.
