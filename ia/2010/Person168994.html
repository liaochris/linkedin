Randy "Dr. Box" Phares’s Summary

I am a globally recognized senior manufacturing, packaging and distribution executive who has had great success in every capacity I have been given. The plant I manage for Temple-Inland won the "Best Achievement of Organizational Improvement in Manufacturing" Award for 2009 presented at the Global Six Sigma and Business Improvement Awards Ceremony in Orlando, FL. This was for my operations in Edinburg, TX and Reynosa, Tamaulipas, Mexico. I have proven success in Sales, Scheduling, Distribution, Production, Maintenance, Accounting, Quality, Safety, Purchasing and Human Resources in the Corrugated, Folding Carton and distribution industries. I bring the ability to motivate a team to reach uncommon goals. I have facilitated several plant turnarounds and managed multiple facilities throughout most of my career. I am an expert in Lean Manufacturing with training in Six Sigma methodologies. I have managed Start Up's, Shut Downs, Turnarounds, and Sustaining Success situations across three industries with rapid promotions and increasing responsibility. I have been on the Board of Directors of Tucson Transatlantic Trade Holding Group, Vice Chair of Membership and Vice Chair of Adiministration for the American Society for Quality - Customer Supplier Division. I am currently The President of the Paperboard Packaging Group and on the Board of Directors of the United Way of South Texas and the South Texas Manufacturers Association.

Randy "Dr. Box" Phares’s Specialties:

I have proven expertise in the following areas. Plant turnarounds, performance improvement, world class team building, cost controls, value added sales, Corrugated Packaging, Folding Carton packaging, Kaizen, Kanban, SMED, Theory of Constraints, 5S, Six Sigma, Quality Management, General Management, Logistics Management, Purchasing, Cost Accounting, printing, diecutting, performance metrics, supply chain, Human Resources, and more.
