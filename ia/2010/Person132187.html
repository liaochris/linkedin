Margaret Grady’s Summary

I completed a year of national service through the Americorps program in Baltimore as a caseworker in a inner-city housing project after I graduated from University of Illinois-Champaign in 2002. From there, I taught English in China and then moved out to Utah to experience the amazing nature of the West. I saw and did a lot of amazing things before settling into my career at Harrington College of Design. I have held three positions at Harrington in two different departments. I worked in two administrative roles in the Admissions Department and I now work as a Career Adviser and Internship Coordinator. My goals are to develop and grow my career even further through Harrington now that I have completed my degree in Interior Design. I hope to pursue a related Master's and one day teach students at Harrington!
