Jim Gardner’s Summary

As a writer, speaker, instructor and advocate of marketing as a core strategic role, I consult with companies in technology and service industries to improve marketing accountability, credibility and influence by measurably impacting revenues, profitability, and broader corporate goals. My experience allows me to uncover new ways to deliver greater efficiency and effectiveness from marketing programs.

Experienced developing successful marketing teams and profitable campaigns worldwide, I also operate as a change agent, where I leverage traditional marketing skill sets internally and externally to increase a company’s ability to communicate its value and compete effectively.
