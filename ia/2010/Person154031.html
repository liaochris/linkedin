Nicki Dugan’s Summary

Communications executive with 20 years of communications and editorial expertise. Deep experience in all aspects of corporate communications for consumer technology companies. Demonstrated pioneer of social media strategies that build engaged audiences. Expertise includes content development, message and positioning development, executive communications, speechwriting, and strategic media/influencers relations. Counsel CEOs and senior executives on communication strategies. Effective leader with proven experience building brands as well as navigating corporate reputation challenges.
