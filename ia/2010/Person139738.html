Timothy Eng’s Summary

I am Timothy Eng, owner of Perth company Media Machine, creators of professional, rich media content such as broadcast-quality video productions, cutting-edge website design and development, contemporary graphic and print design, and universal interactive multimedia.

Put simply, if it is seen on television, a DVD, a computer, or printed material, we can create it for you.

I have personally been part of many media productions for some of the state's largest corporate bodies including John Holland, WestNet Rail, GoCrete, Australia Post, Sandvik Mining and Construction, City of South Perth, Youth Affairs Council of WA and The West Australian Football Commission.
