Nicci Talbot’s Summary

Nicci Talbot is a freelance journalist and author specialising in health, wellbeing & sexuality. She writes for various markets including Suite101, Hastings Online Times, Jo Divine, Buy Association, Onjoy, The Sun, Scarlet and More magazines. Her blog about women's health, sex, relationships & parenting is www.inrudehealth.com.

In February 2010 she is launching www.naughtynicci.com, an adult dating site, which will feature a newsletter, online chat, sex tips, and an Amazon store with recommended books and products.

Nicci has written six health and sexuality books, the latest being The Menopause: An Essential Guide. Other titles sold worldwide are: Fast Sex, Orgasm, Unzipped, Get Over Your Break-up, and The Beauty Book.

Nicci studied sex therapy at Birkbeck, University of London in 2005 and is currently studying for an diploma in holistic massage. She has a BA degree in English from Anglia Ruskin University, and a postgraduate diploma in magazine journalism.

She is currently working on social media & marketing at The Wellington Centre, a holistic health centre based in Hastings, East Sussex.

For more information about Nicci join her network on Twitter and Linked In.

Nicci lives in East Sussex with her partner Matthew, a lawyer and legal trainer, and their three year-old daughter Julieta Anouchka.
