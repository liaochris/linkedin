Knut Ole Thoreplass’s Summary

I am originally an IT-specialist who has a broad range of skills and technical background from Systems Administration of operating systems and databases. I have now had 13 years of experience administering and being responsible for systems in a variety of enterprise critical production systems such as HR and Financial systems. I enjoy challenging and like roles that require a variety of skills.

Systems I have been responsible for includes systems with high complexity and high demands for availabilty.

My skills includes strong knowledge against processes around ITIL (version 3 certified), monitoring systems and technical project managment.
