Letitia Green’s Summary

Letitia is one of the founding members of the Virginia Active Angel Network, begun in Charlottesville, VA in October 2005. Now with a second meeting location in Roanoke/Blacksburg and a third meeting in Richmond, VA. Letitia leads due diligence teams, negotiates term sheets, provides support to the screening committee, and manages membership and expansion.

Letitia and her husband founded ECorp Management Associates, Inc., a real estate co. in Virginia with associates in Los Angeles and Denver. Letitia holds real estate licenses in California and Virginia and has negotiated the sale of millions worth of commercial and multi-family real estate in California. ECorp's current retail client projects include LegoLand, Ron Jon's Surf Park, Orlando, Gladstone Restaurants and US Cellular (mid-Atlantic); previous clients include VAN shoes and Big Dog Sportswear. ECorp also purchases, develops, leases and manages commercial retail properties with private investors across the country.

She earned her Series 7 at Oppenheimer, went to Mocatta & Goldsmid as assistant to the President providing analysis on LME trades, and was recruited to The Blackstone Group, then in its infancy, to provide spreadsheet modelling for deal analysis.

Letitia was on the 2007 and 2008 Annual Business Forum at the Darden School of Business; she recently wrapped up a 3-year stint on the board of the UVA Club of Charlottesville (Chair of the Membership Committee).and is currently a board observer for several portfolio companies to which VAAN is invested. She is a current member of the Charlottesville Venture Group, The Richmond Venture Forum, the Albemarle Yacht Club, The Historical Society of Charlottesville and the IEEE.

Letitia Green’s Specialties:

PE funding raises, negotiation, consultation, mergers and acquisitions, real estate negotiations, private equity, profit and non-profit board development, affinity groups and events.

Letitia Green’s Experience

  • Managing Partner/co-Founder

    Virginia Active Angel Network

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    April 2005 — Present (4 years 11 months)

    The Virginia Active Angel Network is a private club meeting monthly to review early and seed-stage opportunities for investment. Member meetings are held in Charlottesville, Blacksburg/Roanoke and Richmond in three consecutive evening dinner meetings 10 months a year. Entrepreneurs are screened in Charlottesville and if suitable, present at a VAAN dinner. VAAN meets 10 months a year, provides educational seminars through the Kauffman Foundation's Angel Capital Association, and seeks to support entrepreneurial endeavors from elementary through graduate school. Letitia manages member recruitment, as well as leads due diligence and provides deal sourcing and screening support for her partner, Richard D. Crawford. New Vantage Group is a minority partner of VAAN providing an extended network for co-investing, information on valuations and terms, and serving as a liaison to deal opportunities from all over the region.

  • CEO

    ECorp Management Associates Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry)

    September 1993 — Present (16 years 6 months)

    ECorp Management Associates was formed in 1993 in Los Angeles. ECorp represents retailers in their site selection and lease negotiation worldwide (previous and current clients include Van Shoes; Big Dog Sportswear; LegoLand; Ron Jon's Surf Park - Orlando; US Cellular - Mid-Atlantic). ECorp also invests in and/or consults for real estate projects, manages the real estate portion of family trusts, and oversees development prospects and projects for private equity clients. ECorp is now incorporated in Virginia, with affiliate offices in Denver and Los Angeles through associates.
