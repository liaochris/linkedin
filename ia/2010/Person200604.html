Ted Casey’s Summary

Ted Casey brings more than 17 years of experience and award-winning digital media, mobile content and Internet business success to the strategy, marketing, and business development practice at Casey Digital Media LLC. Previously, Casey was the head of Verizon Wireless' $200 million mobile music business. Prior to joining Verizon Wireless, Casey was General Manager of Lycos Music and Lycos’ award-winning HotBot search engine, Manager of Apple Computer's QuickTime VR, and Co-Founder and SVP of Corporate Development for Betawave (formerly GoFish Technologies.)

Casey holds an MBA from the Anderson School of Management at the University of California at Los Angeles and a BA from Duke University.

Ted Casey’s Specialties:

Digital music, Internet search, mobile content, online media, business strategy, product management, content programming, marketing, business development, venture funding, acquisitions, Internet publishing, user-centered software design, and venture initiation
