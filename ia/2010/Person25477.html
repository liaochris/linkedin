Bob Hunter’s Summary

Naval veteran who prefers to work with a competent multidisciplinary team, though proves capable when working alone. Excels in situations where creativity is king, and solid interpersonal skills are demanded. An eager lifelong learner who incorporates lessons learned into a broad problem-solving resource pool. A firm believer in continuous process improvement, its application, and the organizational psychology that drives its success.

Critical thinking, problem-solving generalist, written and spoken communications (formal and interpersonal), comfortable in the boardroom and/or on the shop floor, and able to work and communicate well with everyone in between.

Well traveled with a good sense of cultural acuity, and moderate language acquisition skills. Traveled to: Japan, Scotland, Ukraine, England, France, Portugal, Spain, Italy, Germany, Switzerland, Palma de Majorca, Greece, Israel, Egypt, Bermuda, Canada, Mexico.
