Bill Greene’s Summary

I am an accomplished versatilist who has been involved in bringing appropriate technology solutions and options to business and industry for my entire 30-year career.

My current passion is consistent with this path. I am providing very advanced ultra-high-accurate Computer Aided Inspection (CAI) Services to all kinds of precision manufacturing processes, for example: investment cast jet engine parts, biomedical prosthetics and orthopedic joints, among other demanding development and production parts.

As a consultative business development professional, I pride myself on focusing on strong value propositions for the client business, and never selling hype.

As an award-winning listener, I am exceptionally good at gathering needs and desired outcomes, in addition to facts, specifications, and preferences. I use these insights for my mission of matchmaking between needs and solutions, between wants and opportunities, between people and deals.

I strive for win:win:win relationships and long term success by being creative and strategic in my plans and actions.
