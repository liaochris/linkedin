Pradeep Sanyal ’s Summary

- DataWarehouse, BI and reporting team and Data Quality management

I have led business solution conceptualization and development. I have good experience in developing business solutions, developing reusable frameworks, project management, architecture and design and marketing. I have published thought leadership papers in renowned publications. I am a keen follower of trends in the software industry.
