Renate Schedl’s Summary

Got in touch with Internet August 2004 looking for a new Job. Learnt to know different possibilities to earn money online and tried with several MLM's.

This was not my way. Came across the Yorgoo Network 1,5 years ago and found exactly what I needed. Education, seminars, workshops and 1,5 years of studying and learning.

Decided to build my own business under my own branding. By teaching other people what I have learnt I noticed that there is a big lac of practical tools one needs to start reselling.

So I got the idea, to prepare unique template collections, including each template you need for selling and promoting online.

Purpose of the single collection is to be recognized by colors, logo design and layout. Customer only has to fill in the text. Templates are created in close cooperation with customer. Each collection only exists one time on the Internet.

I prepared 3 sample collections and am offering this service including free hosting and a free autoresponder, where you can use text, html, audio and video messages.
