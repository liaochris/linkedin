Dave Abrahamson’s Summary

In June of 2008, I joined PSI, Inc. as President and partner with founder Jeff Lucas. Jeff founded PSI in 1986 and as a result of his leadership ,PSI has become Georgia's largest optical disc manufacturer; specializing in the production, packaging and distribution of CDs and DVDs. Equipped with an expansive warehouse and distribution facility, PSI also provides shopping cart, credit card processing, fulfillment and distribution solutions for ecommerce sites.

Since joining PSI, we have launched a full-suite of Internet-based solutions to become the single source provider for ALL of our customer's digital media needs. With a proven, high-performance global content distribution network, with over 20 datacenters throughout the world, we have the ability to stream high-definition content to anyone, anywhere.
