Cathy Hains R.D.’s Summary

Nutrition and physical, spiritual and mental well-being have been life-long passions of mine for as long as I can remember! I have a hearty appetite for these subjects. Disease is such a sad thing, and my wish is to see everyone healthy and spiritually whole.

Because nutrition is such a new and exciting field, I find myself researching something on the subject every single day on my own, even if it has nothing to do with an assignment or job.

I've had the pleasure to work in the areas of: WIC, long term care, dialysis, community education in primary and secondary school settings, hospital inpatient/acute care, private practice consulting, small cardiology clinic and commercial kitchen management.
