Quynh Nguyen’s Summary

I value diverse experiences and have many different interests that center around my sense of caring for people, my interest in technological innovation, and my intent to make a positive difference in the world.

In 2010, I'm plan to look at consulting again, mainly in the user research, usability and UI design field. I plan to use my 10 years of diverse research experience to help companies and people discover new product opportunities, improve the user experience of products and websites, and better meet their customer's needs.

I am also interested in how to apply a business perspective to product development research in order to assure the product will be successful in the marketplace .
