Jim Stephens’s Summary

Over the last 9+ years, Jim has assisted in the successful completion of over 650 real estate transactions! He has been affiliated with the RE/MAX brand since 2002 and has received numerous awards and recognitions within the network, including the coveted RE/MAX Hall of Fame Award. As a RE/MAX Individual Agent, Jim ranked #10 for the entire state of Maryland in 2009.

Locally, Jim is affiliated with RE/MAX American Dream, a two-office franchise, with locations in Perry Hall, MD and Bel Air, MD. The franchise is owned by Cathy Werner, who is also President-Elect of the Maryland Association of Realtors.

Jim is a full-time agent and employs a full-time assistant to help with marketing and transaction management. He is committed to providing great service to first time buyers, all the way up to the most seasoned real estate investors. Jim graciously embraces new technology and quickly implements it into his real estate business.

Every time a client buys or sells a home, Jim donates a portion of his earnings to The Children's Miracle Network, which locally benefits the Children's Center at John's Hopkins Hospital.

Jim Stephens’s Specialties:

First Time Home Buyers, Existing Home Sales, New Construction, Investment Properties, Land & Farms, Luxury Homes, Waterfront Homes, Foreclosures, Short Sales, Multi-Family Properties, Residential Offices & Office Conversions and 1031 Tax-Deferred Exchanges. Jim regularly represents Landlords and Tenants with Rentals and Property Management as well.
