Tracy Tuthill’s Summary

While at the Minneapolis Community and Technical College, I was hired as a part-time legal secretary with the Minneapolis firm of Kutak, Rock & Huie. After obtaining my Legal Secretarial Certificate in 1980, I obtained full-time employment with Doherty, Rumble & Butler, located in the IDS Center. In 1982 I moved to Los Angeles, California and continued to work as a legal secretary at a Beverly Hills law firm. I returned to Minneapolis in 1985 and began my employ with Lindquist & Vennum in the litigation department, with a primary focus in securities litigation. In 1991 I developed my own full-time business, In Other Words, a home-based secretarial/word processing service. My business focuses on the administrative aspect of business, utilizing my experience and skills to assist in my clients’ productivity, efficiency, and organization. Although a number of my clients are outside the field of law, the majority of my work is with solo practitioners and/or small firms whose legal practices range from bankruptcy to litigation to real estate.
