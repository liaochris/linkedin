Steve Conine’s Summary

Steve Conine, as president of Talent Framework and AccuStaff, works with numerous professional companies throughout Nevada on all employment related issues. Steve is available to discuss how employers can recruit, engage and retain top talent in the workplace; effective interviewing processes and industry related trends.

Steve's company offers executive search, training workshops, assessments and coaching customized to each company to meet their individual needs, objectives and culture. Steve works to maximizes management’s effectiveness by providing tools to attract talent and manage sensitive people issues such as low productivity and morale, absenteeism and turnover

Steve Conine’s Specialties:

Executive recruiting, management training, interviewing, warehouse operations and process consulting in a distribution environment
