Alex Sherstinsky’s Summary

Previously, Alex Sherstinsky was the V.P. of Product Development at NebuAd, Inc., where he was responsible for Product Management, Product Development, and Network Operations of the company's online advertising service.

Prior to NebuAd, Alex Sherstinsky was the Senior Director of Product Management at FaceTime Communications, Inc., where he led the development, delivery, and marketing of FaceTime's technology platform, product lines, and partnerships since the company's founding in 1997.

Alex's 25-year industry experience also includes LSI Logic, AT&T, and Hewlett-Packard as well as several startups (including two where he was a co-founder).

Alex Sherstinsky received B.S. degree from the University of California, Berkeley, in 1985, and M.S. and Sc.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1989 and 1994, respectively. His doctorate and post-doctorate research work through 1995 was conducted at the M.I.T. Media Laboratory.

Alex Sherstinsky is the holder of U.S. Patent No. 7,596,599 and U.S. Patent No. 7,673,004 (with several other patent applications pending).
