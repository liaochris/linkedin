Ari Bendersky’s Summary

My nearly 15-year writing career in the print and Internet arenas has afforded me incredible experience and access to fantastic opportunities. Whether working as an editor at web start-ups like RollingStone.com or as a marketing manager at Shutterfly.com (long before the IPO and the Web 1.0 crash), I've been able to capitalize on my writing, marketing, networking and other skills to create great products. That experience led me to a successful freelance career, working with agencies, Fortune 500 brands and print/web publications around the world. I have been the editor in chief of a regional Chicago magazine and have been a stringer for the Associated Press. My current status has allowed me to entertain the idea of writing/editing various books while continuing to take on other writing projects.
