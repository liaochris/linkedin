Capital City Toastmasters #639, Lansing, MI’s Summary

We are a local chapter of Toastmasters International who meet at the Capital Area District Library in Lansing, Michigan every Tuesday evening from 7:00 - 8:30 pm.

We are a non-profit organization that offers a proven way to practice communication and leadership skills. Our club is filled with supportive individuals who all have common goals.

Our members range from those who have just begun the Competent Communicator track to those progressing towards Distinguished Toastmaster status. We offer mentoring for new members, ensuring that their Toastmaster experience will be supportive, interactive, and friendly.

Becoming a member of our club means joining a group of individuals who all want to become more comfortable and competent in front of an audience. With Toastmasters, there is no instructor, instead, each speech and meeting is critiqued by a member in a positive manner, focusing on what was done right and what could be improved. This is self-directive learning at its best!

To learn more about our club and meet our members, please visit us online at http://www.capitalcitytoastmasters.com/
