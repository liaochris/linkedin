Jim Clemmer’s Summary

For nearly 30 years Jim Clemmer’s practical leadership approaches have been inspiring action and achieving results. His keynote presentations, leadership workshops, management team planning retreats, seven international best-selling books, articles, blog, and monthly newsletter have helped hundreds of thousands of people worldwide. Jim has delivered over 2,000 customized keynote presentations, workshops, and retreats, as well as consulted to and coached executive teams in hundreds of major companies, the public sector, universities, family businesses, and healthcare organizations.

Jim co-founded and led The Achieve Group to become Canada’s largest training and consulting company. Both were purchased by Times Mirror and merged with Zenger Miller, Learning International, and Kaset, to form AchieveGlobal.

Jim is constantly distilling his exhaustive research, extensive experience, and collection of best practices into easily understood, highly energizing, and practical applications. Organizations hire him for:

- An internationally recognized expert with an authoritative and authentic presence to reinforce, provoke, instruct, and inspire participants
- Highly energizing and practical workshops tailored to the team, event, or organization's key theme, values, or objectives
- “Edutaining” approaches balancing rich content, appropriate humor, inspiring insights, and practical how-to implementation steps
- Powerful self or team assessments, priority setting, and planning guidance to move participants from inspiration to application
- A wide selection of relevant and practical follow-up and implementation resources such as best-selling books, how-to workbooks, handouts, audio/video materials, webinars, or electronic services

Visit Jim’s web site for hundreds of practical leadership articles, dozens of videos clips, books, archived interviews and webinars, blog posts, free newsletter services, keynotes, workshops/retreats and consulting.
