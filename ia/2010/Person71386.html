Frans Dekker’s Summary

BACKGROUND & APPROACH

Mr. Dekker has a proven international track record in delivering Business Transformation, Business Development, Organizational Effectiveness, & Performance Improvement results in (Interim) Management, Consultancy, Training, & Coaching roles. His industry experience covers Consumer Electronics, High Tech, Printing & Imaging, Telecom, Automotive, Financial Services, Government, Medical Equipment / Devices / Consumables, & Healthcare.

  While working towards envisioned objectives, his approach is both people and results-oriented, and incorporates the development of job related & interpersonal skills, as well as the development of team dynamics.

He is business fluent in English, Spanish, German, and Dutch, and so far worked with people of 51 nationalities.

  SERVICE OFFERING

 Mr. Dekker works with clients in organizational, program, or project leadership positions across Europe. He supports them in developing scenarios, strategies, strategic programs & projects, and works with them, their team, and people from across their organization to deliver tangible results.

He has a proven track record of adding value to organizations in the following areas:
1). Preparing & Leading Business Transformation, Performance Improvement, and Strategy Implementation projects in Interim Management, Project Management, Program Management roles;
2). Facilitating Strategy Development, Change, & Stakeholder Alignment;
3). Supporting & Coaching project managers, executives, change leaders, and (project) leadership teams;
4). Organizational Development & Alignment;
5). Strategic Planning, Mission, Scenario Development, Vision & Strategy Development, Strategy Mapping / Alignment / Execution, Balanced Scorecard, Corporate Performance Management, KPI Reporting, and Project Portfolio Management;
6). Training & Coaching people to develop their inter-personal skills.
