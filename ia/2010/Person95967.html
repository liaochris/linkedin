Jeremy DiTullio CRPC®, AWMA®’s Summary

Through 212 Capital Group my goal is to create and maintain a best in class client and advisor service model. Over the past 10 years as an advisor and field leader in financial services, I feel privileged to have been able to help numerous clients and advisors towards reaching their personal and professional goals. I also feel fortunate to have found a platform that can help my team and I create a client support model that gives us an optimal environment for our practice management and growth while focusing towards our clients financial achievements.

That said, from my experience, I have also learned that words such as “resources”, “support”, “team of experts” and “being part of our family” are wildly overused in business. Rest assured that our actions follow our words.

When you join our team, there are several opportunities that set us apart.
Including;
*Local brand, our clients know who they are working with!
*Strategic alliances with Tax and Legal professionals in our local market(s).
*Relationships with close to 250 life, annuity, long term care and disability income carriers.
*Ability to truly serve the high end business owner with a robust selection of group insurance and retirement programs, i.e., The 412i Defined Benefit Plan.
*Access to discretionary trading accounts.
*Our Financial Repesentatives are aligned with FORTUNE® 500 employers coordinated through our parent company to enable employers to have knowledgeable, trusted professionals to provide on site financial education for their employees.
*A leadership team that is invested in your business for the long term.
*Our representatives are invested in their clients with equity in their practice.
*Practice management decisions made locally. We are essentially independent without being completely on our own.

The list goes on but what is most important our ability to provide what our clients and team members need for themselves to grow and be successful.
