Tammy Pike’s Summary

After working within the IT recruitment industry for the past 5 years, I've realised that anyone can offer basic, bums on seats recruitment. That’s what I call being a recruitment agent.

It's miles away from where I see myself; as a Account Manager. Working with my clients as a recruitment partner is the only way I like to do business. To offer a complete service I need to completely understand your problems and understand where I can help you in order to tailor a solution to meet your needs.
