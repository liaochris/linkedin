Kelly Higgins’s Summary

Kelly Higgins is an independent CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional and a CPA. Kelly’s practice concentrates exclusively on providing comprehensive financial plans for individuals and small business owners. She has been helping people to make financial decisions for 15 years. Her background allows her to take into consideration a broad range of issues and to make sure all the moving parts of an individual’s financial plan, are working together to the fulfillment of their unique life goals.

Kelly believes that the financial planning process serves as the foundation upon which clients build investment programs that can increase personal wealth, consistent with their risk tolerance. The value she brings to her clientele is achieved by identifying, quantifying, prioritizing their goals and developing a solution tailored to best meet their needs. Specifically, she helps her clientele develop retirement and investment strategies as well as sift through the various college funding choices. The financial planning process is critical in the selection of the overall asset allocation or mix of stocks, bonds, cash and real estate. She draws upon her background as a CPA, to consider any tax implications of the strategies she recommends. Also, Kelly serves as a strategic partner with your estate planning attorney to help them evaluate and keep their estate plan current, so as to achieve their stated wishes.
