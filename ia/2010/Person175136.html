Bob Paden’s Summary

A small business owner and past corporate executive with a broad background in gas turbine supply chain, aftermarket management and component repair management.

In my corporate past, I was a successful leader of small and large teams responsible for delivering significant cost benefits in gas turbine overhaul, repair and component procurement in an international $14B business over a progressive twenty year career. Key skills in project and people management complemented by strong technical and commercial acumen. Demonstrated ability to develop and communicate a vision and lead teams to deliver significant business objectives.

I am now focused on delivering value added consulting services for clients in a range of environments, both big and small, primarily focused on products and services surrounding gas turbine technology and associated power generation markets. Opportunities for improvements in the alternate energy supply chain are also an area of interest.
