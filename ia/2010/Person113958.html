Kelly Lux’s Summary

I connect students and alumni for career advice and guidance through the Mentor @ SU program. I truly enjoy meeting new people and helping them discover their passions and then connecting them with others who can help them reach their goals. I'm very interested in utilizing social media to reach out to people and help them with information on careers, branding, marketing, mentoring and networking. Always looking for new programming ideas to connect alumni with students, to engage alumni on campus and assist them with their career needs. I have a great time planning and executing events that bring alumni and students together. I am a Certified Job and Career Transition Coach, and a Certified Career Development Coach. In past lives I've taught elementary school, run a well-funded community relations program at a Fortune 50 company, planned and implemented professional development programs, managed large-scale events, and an employee wellness program. During the time I'm not on LinkedIn, I enjoy my family, my dogs and the occasional 150 mile bike ride.
