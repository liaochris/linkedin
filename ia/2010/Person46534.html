Dan Bock’s Summary

A senior level strategic manager with 13 years of success managing e-commerce, online marketing and direct marketing programs for small, medium and large multi-channel direct marketing companies in sporting goods, fashion apparel, business to business, and other consumer goods. Proven ability to drive direct and online marketing programs to achieve revenue growth and ROI improvements.

Wide breadth of experience in launching and growing online marketing initiatives, including email, affiliate, SEM, SEO, comparison shopping, strategic partnerships, re-marketing, display advertising, and social media.

Project manager with a proven track record of delivering on-time and under-budget project implementations. Strong technical experience with several web site launch and CRM solution implementations.
