Martin Metzmacher’s Summary

I have been working with WordPress CMS for a few years and I consider it a premium tool in the web 2.0 toolbox. I am an expert in WordPress SEO and Internet Marketing Strategy.

My goal is to enable humans to communicate with each other online in the same fashion that we communicate with each other in World1.0: from human to human, in a genuine manner, with each person one voice.

WordPress is my tool of choice to give people a voice online in a easy and comfortable way. Search Engine Optimization plays an important role in the online marketing to get that voice to be heard. I am also a passionate blogger myself.
