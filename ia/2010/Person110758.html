Karen Ribeiro’s Summary

Karen Ribeiro is increasing energy efficiency AND economic development in Western Massachusetts and is currently writing a research paper on financing renewable energy. Former director of ener-G-save, a community service outreach program of the Grinspoon foundation, Ribeiro promoted residential audits and insulation measures. Ribeiro has 20 years of business development & marketing experience including 8 years in regional and international commercial banking 6 in radio advertising and 6 as a partner with Get Marketing! which she co-founded in 1997. After being recruited into banking by a client's board member she initiated various socially responsible banking initiatives including a standing room only regional sustainable investment summit in 2008 and launched a health savings product at The Bank of Western Massachusetts. In addition, Karen is an avid journaler and has turned her life-long experience into an innovative tool for developing one's creativity. This Inner Fortune journal notebook is a growing endeavor and she looks forward to incorporating it into various women's networks.
