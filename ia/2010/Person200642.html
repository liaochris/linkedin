Ted Faris’s Summary

I am an expert at acquiring talent in the Health Information Management Systems vertical. In 1999, I began as a Technical Recruiter in Telecommunications recruiting for CLEC's and Network Providers . I migrated to Health Information Management Systems in 2000. From 1990 -1998, I served as a private Vocational Rehabilitation Consultant to the Washington State Department of Labor & Industries and worked with Healthcare providers and employers to assist injured workers back to work. At that time, I also served as an Expert Witness to the Assistant Attorney General. My references are excellent and come from small start-up internet based companies and mid-sized software development firms to large organizations such as 3M Health Information Systems.
