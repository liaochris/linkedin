Joyce Dierschke’s Summary

Joyce Dierschke is a Marcom Copywriter specializing in emerging, broadcast and print media. Joyce’s varied background ties up neatly into a package of versatile skills and knowledge designed to meet the needs of any busy Marcom department. She can help you manage the overwhelming marketing opportunities available in today’s world of advanced technologies without losing that valuable personal touch.

Joyce is also well-versed in graphic and web design principles including but not limited to layout and image creation using Adobe Creative Suite software and QuarkXpress software, design and copy techniques to maximize SEO, and web languages such as html, css, php, etc. She has produced space ads, catalogs, brochures, flyers, one-sheets, logos, and more, as well as collaborated on several high-end, multi-function web sites.

Software expertise includes: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign & Dreamweaver; Quark Xpress; MS Word, Excel and Outlook.

Joyce is currently accepting new clients on one-off and long-term basis.

See samples of Joyce's work at: www.joycedierschkecopywriting.com.
