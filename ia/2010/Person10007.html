Amy Boone Thompson’s Summary

Amy is the Chief Operating Officer for Stroller Strides, LLC and is based in San Diego, California.

Amy has first-hand experience and expertise in development, implementation and management of franchise operations and fitness programming. Under her leadership Stroller Strides has more than doubled in units and expanded to 3 countries and 47 states. Amy is passionate about paying it forward and has been a presenter and author for the franchise and fitness industries for the last decade. She currently serves as Program Director Committee Chair for IDEA and Co-Chair for the San Diego Women's Franchise Network Chapter through IFA. She can be contacted at amy@strollerstrides.com.
