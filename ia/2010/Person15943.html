Doug Davis’s Summary

Archifects was established in 1989 providing services in all aspects of residential architecture. Visual Simulations, 3D Modeling, Multimedia Presentations & Internet Development were added as clients needed to understand and see their vision. Archifects has provided multimedia services for developers, architects, real estate agents, engineers, land planners, city governments, contractors, attorneys, and building suppliers.

Archifects also continues to build it's affiliate marketing presence in the online media development arena and has redesigned a domain it's owned for a long time...www.relocationguide.com. "We are building this site to provide people who've been relocated with information and tips on the relocation and moving process".

Archifects' objective is to provide the architectural, engineering, real estate, and online media development community with cutting edge presentations and graphics for a wide range of applications.
