Lennart Nacke’s Summary

The focus of my research is multi- and cross-disciplinary, including the areas of (1) game development, (2) computer science, (3) psychology, and (4) human-computer interaction (HCI).

I am particularly interested in gameplay metrics, player psychophysiology (EEG, EMG, GSR, BCI), biometrics, player experience, user experience, playability, usability, aesthetics, fun-of-gaming, engineering, challenge, frustration, presence, flow, immersion, engagement and game interaction techniques.
