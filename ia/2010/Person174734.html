Robert Goldbloom’s Experience

  • Sr. VP

    AIG American General

    (Insurance industry)

    January 1990 — September 2009 (19 years 9 months)

    Managed Profit Center for maximum profitability and growth. Led team of 45, performing underwriting, pricing, marketing, administration, legal, compliance, product development, risk management and planning functions.

    Grew income of Profit Center from $3 million to $50 million per annum. Profitability of new business consistently surpassed target returns (generally 10-15% after-tax ROI).

    Built several stable blocks of business:
    • Group annuities (primarily DB pension buyouts). Quadrupled block size to $2.7 billion of non-surrenderable, long-term business.
    • Canadian pension buyouts. Sold $C250 million in 2008 from first sale in 2006.
    • Guaranteed Investment Contracts. Built from scratch to $2 billion in force while maintaining investment and pricing discipline.

    Developed several innovative products. Successfully designed, priced, sold and administered:
    • Retirement GOLD: a group-offered payout annuity platform with fixed, variable and inflation-indexed options; product features, investment options tailored to channel sponsor.
    • Variable Terminal Funding Annuity. 1st ever of its kind.
    • Funding Agreements of various types, e.g., Floating Rate, Offshore, 529 plan.

    Organized and managed profit center for optimal efficiency, resiliency, mobility. Maintained lowest expense ratios in industry. Adapted to business cycle changes and post-acquisition reorganizations.

    Represented AIG on ACLI Pension Committee and LOMA Group Pension and Investment Product Committee.

    Served on due diligence teams for AIG acquisitions of SunAmerica and American General.

  • CFO, Global Pensions

    American International Group

    (Public Company; AIG; Financial Services industry)

    March 1996 — September 1998 (2 years 7 months)

    Reviewed, evaluated and approved pricing and product design submitted by AIG's local pensions operations. Proposed changes or solutions when appropriate, e.g.:
    • Designed novel stable value product, incorporating equities, for Hong Kong MPF market.
    • Developed efficient investment strategy, using cross-currency cash flow swaps, for Spain buyout market.

  • Actuarial Associate

    New York Life

    • Annuities -- Product development and pricing. Quoted non-standard business.
