Elizabeth Bowman’s Summary

Background:
Elizabeth has a degree in the field of Industrial and Systems Engineering from Virginia Tech, along with 5 years experience in the defense contracting and consulting industry. Her experience in designing systems and tools to provide efficiency to large corporate clients and government agencies allows her a unique perspective when creating systems for your home or office space. While working for Accenture (a Big 4 consulting firm), Elizabeth focused on creating and delivering training presentations for corporations as well as designing, building, and implementing new software systems for various government agencies. Elizabeth also has experience in giving presentations and facilitating extensive corporate training courses.

Currently:
Elizabeth currently owns and operates Innovatively Organized LLC, a professional organizing services company. Here Elizabeth works with clients to create organizational systems to help them streamline the many facets of their busy lives. Innovatively Organized provides the following services:

Residential Organizing
Business Organizing
Workshops

Not only does Elizabeth work hands-on with clients to develop organized spaces, she also leads several organizing workshops on the topics of Paper Management, Time Management, and Organizing Small Spaces.

Leadership:
Elizabeth is a member of the National Association of Professional Organizers, where she currently serves as the WA state liaison to the national association. Elizabeth is also a sitting member on the Board of Directors for the Seattle Area Chapter of the National Association of Professional Organizers, where she currently servers as the Director of Membership. In addition, Elizabeth is a current member of Toastmasters International and attends weekly meetings in the Seattle area. She is well on her way to accomplishing her goal of gaining certification as a 'Competent Toastmaster'.

Elizabeth Bowman’s Specialties:

Elizabeth, along with Innovatively Organized, specialize in the organization of small spaces. Elizabeth likes to focus on urban areas where space in offices and homes (such as apartments or condos) is limited and creativity is needed to design organizing solutions. She provides services to busy professionals and families that recognize the need for organized systems but do not have time to do it themselves.
