Louis Gray’s Summary

I am a strategic, results-driven consultant with deep roots in technology, new media, marketing and public relations. With more than a decade embedded in Silicon Valley startups, I have developed processes and insights that deliver real business benefits that lead to product differentiation, high quality branding and revenue.

The author of more than 2,500 blog posts on louisgray.com and other sites, my work is frequently referenced by top online media, and the site is considered among the first to accurately predict technology trends and recognize new market entrants.

In addition, I have frequently been asked to speak and participate at global events, including SXSW, LeWeb, BlogWorld, Defrag and the Inbound Marketing Summit. I am also available for speaking to groups or more dedicated 1-1 consulting, through Paladin Advisors Group, where I am the managing director of new media.

Tools in the arsenal include new media, consultancy, advisory, public relations, branding, corporate communications, internal communications, Web strategy and competitive positioning, in both print and Web media.
