Heather Hill’s Summary

Marketing and Advertising Professional with 17 years combining both agency and client-side experience.

Extensive entrepreneurial experience specializing in branding and communications with start-up companies; and advertising/marketing background working with clients and companies including Vail Resorts, Rudy Project Eyewear, Coors Brewing Company, AT&T, Go Fast Sports & Beverage Company, Titan Motorcycles and Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A..

Highly-respected in the endurance sports industry (cycling, triathlon, running, swimming) with relationships across endemic and non-endemic brands, media and events. Keen sense of brand strategy and cross-promotional opportunities to reach targeted and viable demographics. Experience extends across snow sports and action sports industries.
