Eric Gilboord’s Summary

As a marketing speaker, marketing coach and marketing workshop leader I believe: "Marketing should not be a mysterious black hole an entrepreneur is afraid to enter. I am dedicated to working full time at explaining marketing in simple terms so anyone can understand. I will empower every entrepreneur with the power of marketing." Eric Gilboord, 2010

The Expert Small Business Calls for Marketing Advice ... That's Easy to Understand

www.EricGilboord.com

Eric is a specialist in marketing small and medium-sized businesses. He is a popular marketing speaker, marketing columnist, and author of many articles and books on marketing and moving businesses up to the next level. Including the best seller 'Just Tell Me What To Do-Easy Marketing Tips for Small Business'. Visit his site for a free e-copy of his marketing book.

He prides himself in his ability to communicate and connect with SMB audiences. Offering them a clear understanding of marketing in a simplified language so any business owner can understand. He removes the mystery of marketing and makes it accessible to everyone no matter how little their previous marketing experience may be.

He has worked as a marketing executive for the past 30 years in Canadian and International advertising agencies as well as specialty shops. As a marketing expert, he has solid experience in all aspects of marketing. His work history includes national, retail and business to business advertising as well as direct marketing and promotion, covering various industries. His services have been provided to Ontario Lottery Corporation, Ontario Hydro, Ford, Converse, AT & T, Swiss Chalet, CBS Records, Budget Rent-A-Car and many other companies.

For the past 16 years he has worked as a marketing coach and a marketing supplier to hundreds of small & medium-sized businesses.

To learn more about Eric visit www.EricGilboord.com or call 416-686-2466.
