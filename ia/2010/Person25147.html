Brian Fletcher’s Summary

I help individuals, businesses, academic and research institutions and government agencies create, protect and leverage their intellectual property, both in the United States and in other countries. I also work with international law firms to help their clients obtain intellectual property rights in the United States.

We are a fixed-fee, value-oriented law firm whose strength is intellectual property law, in particular patents. We also help clients obtain trademarks and copyrights.

Broad experience in technologies including electronic circuits, electrical and mechanical devices, sensors, image forming and processing systems, photonic and radio frequency systems, directed energy systems, communications systems, network protocols, network security and network management systems, target acquisition and tracking systems, electrical power management and distribution systems, computer hardware and software, semiconductor manufacturing, medical devices and medical information systems, business methods and Internet applications.

Brian Fletcher’s Specialties:

Obtaining, protecting and leveraging U.S. and international intellectual property rights through patents, trademarks and copyrights.
