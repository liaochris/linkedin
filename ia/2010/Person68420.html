  • CEO - Co-founder

    Agora Media Group

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    September 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    What do we do? We research, write, investigate, build, and play in the realm of Convergent Media. We are a group of professionals who revel in living on the cutting edge of technology. We are available as TV application and Widget developers, consultants, innovators, project leaders, event panelists and more. Please visit our new site at http://www.appmarket.tv .

    Forty years ago, the brilliant Canadian media theorist Marshall Mcluhan, the “patron saint” of Wired magazine, (who brought us Electronic Interdependence, The Global Village and The Medium is the Massage) metaphorically considered the TV to be an ‘electronic’ hearth – a collective centralized event for the family on the cusp of it’s appearance in the home 50 years ago. By the end of the millenium, TV’s peppered houses and became a more individual experience. In the USA, the average house now has 2.24 TV’s and 66 per cent of households have three or more TV’s.

    It's clear that TV is about to become social again (says Forbes, The NYT, The BBC and the Wall St. Journal) But who, what, when, where, why, how and how much? The questions have yet to be answered.

    And as founders of Appmarket.tv... and curators of a fair chunk of market intelligence - we truly have a firm grasp on the emerging landscape.

    Some of our team have built Social TV already - back in 2007, with the Twinners Platform which piloted on SuperRTL, RTL, SBS and TV Copenhagen in Denmark, Germany, The Netherlands and Belgium. We have built open source communities, we have built scores of applications and widgets, and we have have concepted new formats around both augmented reality and social TV. Just as social networking reshaped the online landscape we feel the same will come for television. The convergence of TV, internet and social media is an awesome model and we are in the heart of it.
