Ben Schieber, PMP CSP’s Summary

The application of an outcome based and process driven approach has, for over twenty years and within a diversity of corporate environments, allowed Ben the opportunity to identify and deliver effective enterprise solutions.

Motivated with strong leadership skills and an aptitude for learning, Ben have been effective at working closely with clients and associates to understand problems and identify solution opportunities. Experience and a structured management style prove effective in delivering solutions within the framework of formal methodologies and defined processes.

Ben has had the opportunity to manage and direct an ERP implementation, a SharePoint 2007 (MOSS) implementation, multiple legacy system modification projects, multiple customer application development, and multiple process engineering projects for mid-size to Fortune 500 companies.

He is now charged with developing and executing an eBusiness strategy and tactical plan for Children International.
