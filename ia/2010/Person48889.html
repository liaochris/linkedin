Darlene Guinto’s Summary

Dr. Darlene Guinto is president of HydroPlan LLC with over 18 years of experience as a planner, economist and civil engineer with the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, including Baltimore District, the Institute for Water Resources (IWR), the Jacksonville District, the Northwestern Division, and Task Force Hope, New Orleans District.

Dr. Guinto has developed national benefit evaluation methodologies; assisted in the development and partnering agreement for the Chesapeake Bay Oyster Recovery Program; developed program and project implementation guidance for the Comprehensive Everglades Restoration Plan; served as the Corps representative for the South Florida Task Force and Working Group; defended benefit evaluation procedures to USACE higher authority and the OMB; and guided the development and implementation of a risk-informed decision-making framework for the Metropolitan New Orleans and Vicinity Hurricane Protection System and the Louisiana Coastal Protection and Restoration Study.
