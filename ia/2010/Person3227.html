Jeff Gies’s Summary

If I followed my natural talents, I would have been a world class accountant. Instead, I made myself a commitment that sales was going to be where I made my mark in the professional world. In the beginning, hard work made up for my lack of sales ability. Over the years as my ability caught up to my work ethic, I was able to achieve higher and higher goals. I now consider myself to be a well educated and street proven sales professional. I have sold everything from products to services to concepts (during the dot.com era).

What motivates me now is competition and helping others to get better at what they do. My basic belief is that people buy from people they like and trust, so my ability to connect with people and my desire to help those around me helps drive my success.
