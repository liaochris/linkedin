Moty Koppes, MA, PCC’s Summary

I am a certified professional coach who helps individuals produce extraordinary results in their personal and professional lives.
Through an ongoing partnership, I support my clients reach new levels of success by identifying and removing barriers that have previously stopped them. They go on to define a unique mission statement and create a powerful vision, designing their life rather than reacting to the circumstances in it.

A psychologist (B.A., Psychology; M.A., Clinical & Experimental Psychology), counselor and certified coach, I have extensive experience teaching, counseling and coaching an international, multi-cultural client base.

Moty Koppes, MA, PCC’s Specialties:

As an executive coach with degrees in education, psychology and extensive international experience, I bring unique depth, power and perspective to the coaching process.
