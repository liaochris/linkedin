Alexis Petersen’s Summary

You know those people that really enjoy winning? Who want to solve a problem, generate cool ideas, find new ways to do things? That’s me.

I’m curious, intense and like to get results. And I have a passion for breakthrough creativity.

I also believe that safe usually sucks. Safe is OK for some, because safe is easy. But to me, if an idea isn’t a little scary, doesn’t get your heart beating just a little faster, then it’s probably not that good. And it usually won’t get noticed.

It’s not that I don’t believe in marketing principles. You need consistency, you need a logo, you need to know your audience. But I’m about taking it one step further. How can I satisfy all of those marketing guru's yet reach farther? How can I do it differently than everyone else?

You see, safe is easy. But safe won’t get you a win, and in my little world, I want to win.


My beliefs:
• Safe usually sucks.
• Big ideas trump big budgets.
• Learn one new thing each day.
