Scott Cronister’s Summary

Real estate executive with over 20 years commercial real estate experience including extensive asset management, disposition, leasing, and management expertise across a portfolio of office, industrial, retail and multi-family projects.

Currently responsible for the strategic planning, asset management and disposition functions across a nationwide, institutional portfolio of joint-venture development projects, opportunistic acquisitions, workouts, land holdings, and ORIX Realty Investment eXchange's Tenant-In-Common commercial real estate syndications.
