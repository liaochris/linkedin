Brian Dass’s Summary

A highly accomplished travel industry executive, Brian Dass brings 20 years of specialized experience spanning multiple facets in global hospitality. Starting at the hotel property level, Mr. Dass co-founded two successful web-based hotel technology firms that serviced a range of proprietary online products and services. In addition, he held NYC based hotel general manager positions that entailed overseeing all sales, marketing, operations and administration.

A successful entrepreneur with a proven industry record, Mr. Dass' two online hospitality marketing firms, Timeless Hospitality and Open Hospitality encompassed a series of integrated online systems for hoteliers, by a hotelier. Includes but not limited to, generating industry wide record revenue, booking engine software, progressive web design, search vertical, brand development and overall management of performance-driven teams.

Brian Dass’s Specialties:

Extensive, executive management expertise in: global hospitality, hotel revenue management, online channel distribution strategy, technology, product development/services, eCRM, travel supplier-direct relations, corporate strategy, international expansion, lifestyle hotel marketing, social media and mobile marketing, development of boutique/independent hotel positioning.
