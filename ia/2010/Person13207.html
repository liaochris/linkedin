Angel Kubichek-Beyers’s Summary

I relocated to Madison, WI after graduation from college in Green Bay, WI in December 2000. I worked at a Madison title company for 3 years which allowed me the opportunity to learn a lot about the area and real estate in general. In 2003, I teamed up with another real estate agent who lead a team of 5 agents. I obtained my Real Estate License in May 2003 and my Broker License in November 2003. Over time, I became the Vice President for this Real Estate Team, and continued in that role for 2 years. In mid 2007, I decided to create a Real Estate Team of my own, and I continue to build this team today.

Real Estate Salesperson and Broker License since 2003
Sales and Customer Service experience for over 10 years
