Tracy Brisson’s Summary

I am the Founder and CEO of The Opportunities Project, a new social entrepreneurship initiative that provides career coaching to college students and recent college graduates. Like the name says, we help young people learn how to create opportunities for themselves through the education and coaching process.

As part of The Opportunities Project’s umbrella of services, I also consult with career centers and college programs who want to provide exceptional services to their students. I help hiring organizations, including schools and non-profits, recruit and hire better employees, too.

Before becoming a certified coach, I spent a decade successfully working in recruitment and education. In my “spare time,” I am writing a book about improving teacher recruitment in our nation’s public schools and doing research for my doctorate at New York University.

I am passionate about helping people achieve their dreams, whether it's by providing resources and knowledge, holding people accountable to their own career goals, or making sure that our society's most important institutions are staffed with the best people possible.

For information on hiring me as a coach, consultant, or speaker, check out my service listing at http://opportunitiesproject.com/services or schedule a call with me at http://tungle.me/tracybrisson.
