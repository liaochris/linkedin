Background: Multi-faceted manager with operations, financial, project and program management expertise. Solution oriented with a strong understanding of organizational design, performance and productivity measures. Proficient in designing and implementing programs and processes. Effective communicator, skilled in negotiation and problem solving; able to build cohesive work teams at all levels.

  • Vice President

    Trammell Crow Company

    (Public Company; CBG; Real Estate industry)

    May 1997 — June 1999 (2 years 2 months)

    I was the Account Manager for the Xerox account and later served as a consultant for new account start-ups and other client assignments. At Xerox, the assignment was largely consultative in nature. We directly managed the leased property inventory for Xerox in Monroe County, NY, a portfolio in excess of 1M sq. ft. We managed the landlords, interfaced with the business units on building related issues as well as construction projects. We benchmarked the operating costs across the portfolio and worked with the landlords to get costs in line as needed. Additionally, I managed the re-design of the space planning process for Xerox and performed a space utilization study. I managed an energy usage audit of the Wilson campus. After the Xerox assignment ended, I worked with the Frontier Telecom account performing lease abstraction and broker management for their network expansion program. I also participated in a number of consulting studies with Columbia/HCA, Mercantile Bank, Detroit Gas & Electric and IBM Canada.

  • Director, National Services

    Koll Management Services

    (Real Estate industry)

    February 1991 — May 1997 (6 years 4 months)

    I joined Koll as a Property Manager and initially did business development for the Los Angeles Regional Manager. I was subsequently assigned to open 550 South Hope Street, a 500K Class A office property in downtown LA. I worked with the developer and general contractor during the months prior to occupancy to develop the required fire & life safety programs, oversee the initial tenant improvement programs, hire the maintenance staff and select the primary vendors.

    After the initial phase was complete, I was asked to join the start-up of the facilities management outsourcing effort at Koll. I wrote proposals, met with prospective clients and sought out business opportunities. After we were awarded the first account with Sun Microsystems, I served as the Account Manager for the first year of operations. I oversaw the hiring of a staff of 50+ employees, the management of three campuses in Mountain View and Milpitas California and suburban Boston with over 40 properties and 3M sq. ft.. I oversaw the move from four disparate properties in to a single campus in Chelmsford, MA. I oversaw an operating budget in excess of $7M and a capital projects budget of $18M (for projects under $1M).

    After the Sun assignment, I joined the Technical & Consulting Services group within Koll. This was a 4 man group that performed a variety of assignments for Koll clients and other companies. I oversaw the audits of 12 facilities for AT&T which resulted in a savings of $1.75/sq. ft. I also managed five diverse consulting assignments for external customers including staffing strategy for a new corporate campus, staffing reorganization as an alternative to outsourcing, site assessment for lease renewal negotiations, mechanical and electrical survey of high-rise office building and site audit for start-up of management account. Additionally I assisted with several new account transitions serving as an interim account manager while a permanent staff was hired.
