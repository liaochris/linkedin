Menno Koehoorn’s Summary

HP (Enterprise Services) provides a broad portfolio of business and technology solutions to help its clients worldwide improve their business performance. Our core portfolio comprises information technology, applications and business process outsourcing services, as well as transition and transformation services to clients around the world.

Menno Koehoorn is Client Sales Manager Outsourcing for the HP General Western Europe (GWE) region. In this role he is leading the sales cycle and pursuit team to sell the company’s core IT outsourcing portfolio. He is responsible for growing HP' business through differentiated, innovative offerings that can be delivered through the HP Global Delivery Network.
