Susan Hart’s Summary

Prior to becoming a bodyworker I worked with children, first as a preschool teacher then as a professional nanny. Have been an ABMP certified massage therapist since 1998. In 1999 I began working with a chiropractor who helped me to discover my ability to do intuitive energy work. Discovering that massage therapy alone is inadequate to comprehensively address the causative factors leading to the conditions which brought clients to see me; I began to study alternative holistic approaches to meet the needs of clients on more than just the physical level. I have studied and include the following modalities in my practice: cranio-sacral therapy, neuromuscular therapy, Reiki, Seichem, frequency balancing, distance healing, sound/vibrational healing, work with crystals, brain therapy for adults and children, active isolated stretching, chakra balancing, aromatherapy and myofascial release. Bodywork sessions with me are nurturing and unique, combining intuitive guidance, energy work and advanced massage therapy techniques. It is this combination, backed up with over 10 years of experience that sets my work apart, allowing the client the opportunity for complete healing.
