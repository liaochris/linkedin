V Ivana Foster The Confidence Architect Lifestyle Coach’s Summary

V. Ivana Foster, The Confidence Architect™; author, lifestyle coach, and empowerment speaker, is dedicated to moving many Success Driven Individuals by sharing her blessings, gifts, and talents at every opportunity.
With over 18 years of solid experience in instructional designing, coaching, training, mentoring and consulting; Ivana has produced and facilitated successful workshops, seminars, and conferences that have covered topics ranging from interpersonal/ intrapersonal communication, organizational and workforce development, to strategic planning as well as individual & group coaching towards peak performance. Ivana is credited as one of the two instructional designers who provided the solid foundation for a Georgia State Agency's Management Development Program (MDP). Designed with a focus on the competencies for successful leaders, this program has produced successful classes of executive leaders for its agency.
Her extensive career includes instructing, facilitating, and HR consulting within the renowned Georgia Leadership Institute and Enterprise Learning Division (GLI), one of the many initiatives sponsored by Commission for a New Georgia (CNG).There she provided corporate training, facilitation, and coaching to executive leadership members and teams for all Georgia State Agencies.
Currently, Ivana serves as CEO/founder of The Confidence Architect, LLC, Director of the Continuing Education Department, Curriculum Designer, and Faculty Member in the Jewell Jackson-McCabe Emerging Leaders Institute (ELI). Past clients / audience participants include: Jewell Jackson-McCabe Emerging Leaders Institute, Inc.®, The National Coalition of 100 Black Women, Spelman College Bonner Scholars Program, Bright From The Start: Georgia Department of Early Care and Learning, Department of Corrections, Community Affairs, Education, Labor, Georgia Juvenile Justice, Revenue, & Transportation as well as Representatives and Team Leaders of Avon® and Mary Kay®
