Joseph Russo’s Summary

Currently the E-Learning Manager at Navy Federal Credit Union world headquarters in the Washington DC area. We have an internal staff of professional e-learning developers and specialists as well as well as instructors who are dedicated to training over 7,000 of our employees. We were selected by Training Magazine as a Training Top 125 company for 2008 & recently in 2009.

Navy Federal is the largest credit union in the world with $41 billion in assets and 3.4 million members. We have 180 branch offices and 417 proprietary ATMs worldwide. The credit union serves all Department of Defense (DoD) military and civilian personnel, contractors who provide service to or work for DoD on a regular basis, and their families.

President of DigiLore, Inc. in Cincinnati, Ohio. Developing a Learning Lifecycle Management system that plans and organizes content, it also manages and validates the learning processes and objectives.

An e-learning business development manager for O'Neil & Associates that produces several types of technical manuals. This also included IETMS (Interactive Electronic Technical Manuals) and CMS (Content Management Systems).

Founder and CEO of Avtech, an e-learning training company in 1975. We provided our clients with e-Learning course development, Learning Content Management Systems (Avtech created our own LCMS and LMS). Avtech was acquired by O'Neil in 2001.
