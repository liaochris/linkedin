Philipp Nell’s Summary

My professional way until today made it possible to know SAP's BC and BI solution from both a very technical and functional point of view. I had the luck to work with the best technicians I ever met in the SAP HQ in Walldorf, then occupying a position which introduced me to the functional application of SAP technology and especially its still growing Business Intelligence Solution. Several years of Sales Support, Business Development and Product management were the reason for the advance in this direction. During the last year the new dimension of responsibility of colleagues and their work was added in a company that combines teamworking and professional excellence.

The above described experiences made me a very flexible and serious 'allrounder' who combines strong technical understanding with broad functional vision, which helped me a lot to win the trust of my customers.

I'm interested in challenging projects and positions of (SAP) BI or CRM on a national and/or international niveau. First objetive is that the job must add new experiences to the existing ones and allow me to feel that I still enjoy my profession.

Current areas of investigation are predictive Business Intelligence, dynamic planning processes, integration of SAP Business Objects in SAP BI, SAP BO’s arquitecture and Data Federation.

In 2007 I founded Cubexx as a special expertise company for SAP BI and now enhancing it together with other specialists to a small but highly qualified expertise center for SAP Netweaver Technology.
