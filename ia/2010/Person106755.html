Jeffrey Kuchta’s Summary

Professional veteran with a great sense of SolidWorks and how to utilize all it has to offer in todays fast paced industry. Wishes to continue to serve top firms wanting to execute strategy which will give them a strong competitive advantage.

With my twenty years of experience in the manufacturing environment and excellent communication skills I feel that I am well suited for any Project Manager position. While working as a Project Manager at Innovative Concepts in Entertainment; I was able to successfully assist a team of designers on multiple projects to ensure the deliverables were on time, on budget and technically accurate. Our development cycles ranged from 8 weeks (from design to production) to up to a year. Throughout those periods I would hold regular meetings & be in daily contact with all the team members to discuss the projects timelines and deliverables to ensure that the companies and the customers needs were being met.

One of my many strengths is documentation, and giving attention to the smallest of details. I worked very hard to ensure that the engineers ‘design intent’ was fully conveyed on all production drawings/specs. This would help ensure that our CNC programmers, external metal shop providers and our internal assemblers all had the proper information to make the product exactly how the customer intended it to be.

Once on the Production Floor, it was my responsibility to make sure that the assembly drawings & service manuals were correct and readily available to all assemblers.

I feel that my years of experience in the manufacturing environment together with my excellent communication skills make me well suited as a Project Manager.

I have extensive training in various CAD programs and are well suited for the day-to-day training and troubleshooting that those CAD programs require.
