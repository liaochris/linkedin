Sarah Lockwood’s Summary

I am young professional who is currently working at a experience design firm called People Design in Grand Rapids from Ferris State University. I graduated with honors in May 2009, earning a Bachelor of Applied Science, Graphic Design major and Marketing minor.

I have gained experience through a working at People Design for one year, three design internships and through two years of working as a student designer for University Advancement and Marketing at Ferris State University.

Because of my education in design and business and through work experiences, I have developed a strength in developing user-experiences through the implementation of a disciplined research and design process.

I am committed to effectively portraying the essence of a message while satisfying the needs of the users, consumers, and/or stakeholders.

My goal is to bring value to a team of dedicated individuals who share a similar passion for intelligent design solutions derived from design fundamentals and developed with best practices in mind.
