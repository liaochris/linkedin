Dan Cooney’s Summary

Dan is a user experience expert practicing information architecture and interaction design.

Strengths include designing effective user-centered solutions and strong interpersonal skills for collaboration between users, clients, visual designers, and development teams.

Dan Cooney’s Specialties:

business analysis, user needs assessment, requirements gathering, requirements documentation, competitive evaluation, workflow analysis, ethnographic field research, persona & scenario creation, information architecture, interaction design, interface design, iterative prototype development, usability evaluation, development support
