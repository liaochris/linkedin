Jack Carton’s Summary

Senior financial professional with over fifteen years of both digital and traditional media and entertainment experience. I have the expertise to lead and, as necessary, turnaround organizations financially and operationally. Strong analytical skills, superior communication ability and the capacity to craft effective partnerships with creative, professional and administrative personnel. I have been a partner to both division and group chairman at both Sony Music, BMG and Eagle Rock Entertainment Inc. I also partner with Senior Marketing and Technology executives to produce optimal results for the organization.I have the ability to understand and work across the platforms of digital media, film, music, video and television. I have also helped in the production of an independent feature film as Associate Producer and Music Consultant.

I have significant expertise in both traditional and digital media areas.
In the digital media area, have been involved with projects related to Mobile Entertainment, Online Media and Video Games.

In recent years, I have developed skill sets in the entrepreneurial area. I have consulted to and worked for startup and small companies. I have provided expertise in fundraising, finance, strategy and business development .

I am currently involved with a Worldwide Event and Music Production company known as 7th Level Entertainment Group. I am also involved with a Film Production Company which has a slate of films and reality shows in process.

I now have the ability to impact companies on all sizes, bringing both large and small company management expertise to bear.
