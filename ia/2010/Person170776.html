Regina Ryan’s Summary

Regina Ryan Coaching helps entrepreneurs and business leaders to achieve positive behavioral change for themselves, their staff, and their organization. As a certified business leadership coach, Regina helps clients maximize their potential through creating a safe, high energy environment to identify and explore what excites them and ultimately feed their vision, goals, and values.

Through creative facilitation and inspired self-discovery, clients are empowered to navigate through the following steps: Reflect, Reveal, Refine, Response, and Reinforce to REACH their measurable, attainable, relevant, and desired goals.

Regina's integrated approach to coaching leverages her 23 years of professional experience in business management, leadership development, sales, client service management, training as well as her cognitive behavioral counseling expertise.

Regina has worked for various Fortune 500 companies as well as start-up organizations within the nonprofit, media, professional training, marketing consulting, and the telecom industries. Throughout her career, she has achieved various performance awards in leadership and account management for exceeding individual and team objectives. Regina possesses a dedicated work ethic, strong leadership, exceptional relationship development, and highly developed presentation and communication skills.
