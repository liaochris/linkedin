Dominique Levin’s Summary

Visionary leader who successfully built five businesses, evangelizing new markets:
1. CMO & CEO at Sequoia funded LogLogic (grew from $0 to $50 mn)
2. Polivec, pioneer in security policy automation (founding investor and acting VP)
3. Voice-over-IP business for Dialogic, including world’s first VoIP gateway
4. Remote access and hosted messaging businesses for NTT (Tokyo, Japan)
5. New PDA concept for teenage girls for Philips Consumer Electronics

Previously, Dominique was also a venture capitalist and created more than $630 million in shareholder value through investments in Resonext (acquired by RFMD), Navini Wireless (acquired by Cisco) and LGC Wireless (acquired by ADC).

Dominique holds a Master of Business Administration (with Distinction) from Harvard University and a Master of Science (Cum Laude) in Industrial Design Engineering from the University of Delft, The Netherlands.

Dominique is now advising young companies while working on her next venture as founder/CEO. She is also available to lecture or teach on topics related to venture funding, social media marketing and high-tech product management.
