Emery Chiszar’s Summary

Emery Chiszar is a Senior Recruiter with Common Agenda. Emery’s recruiting career started in 1996 in the telecommunications industry with functional focus in Operations, Engineering, Development and Design. He brings the unique experience of having recruited for the Service Provider, Product (NEM) and Enterprise sides of the industry. Emery has successfully completed search assignments for project work, contract assignments, and direct placements.

Emery has successfully recruited and managed in high-pressure high-demand situations such as providing specialized disaster relief professionals to BellSouth simultaneously for hurricanes Rita and Katrina. Also, providing teams of professionals for major PBX cutovers located inside the United Nations buildings in New York. Emery has hands-on experience certifying DSL Systems and ADSL cutovers inside Verizon’s central offices throughout the Northeast and MidAtlantic regions with a team of professionals from Orius and Spirent.

Emery has successfully completed search assignments for a long list of clients that include: Verizon, Lucent Technologies, Tyco Submarine, MCI WorldCom, Avaya, Aastra Intecom, BellSouth, Cable & Wireless, Marconi, Motorola, Sprint, Port AuthorityNJNY, USMA (West Point), Raytheon, Coastal Communications, Emerson Network Power, Dialogic, Camelot Group.
Prior to his recruiting career, Emery won several quality assurance awards as well as an employee of the month award as a Quality Assurance Engineer for Coca-Cola Enterprises in Tampa Florida. There he implemented a software package (STAT-PAC) that digitized the quality assurance laboratories data for all Florida production facilities.

Emery is a graduate from the University of Florida with a duel Bachelor Degree of Science in Industrial & Systems Engineering with a minor in mathematics. A dedicated husband and proud father of a two daughters, Emery lives at the Jersey Shore.
