Kirsten Judd’s Summary

As a social media strategist I assist business professionals, corporations and event managers in developing social media campaigns that are intertwined with traditional advertising and business development tactics. The strategy of such campaigns is as important as the message, however the keys to a successful campaign are consistency, communication, and creativity.

NLC Social Media offers services to individuals, businesses, & event managers. Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, & the many other networks are a powerful interactive experience for businesses to communicate directly to customers. NLC Social Media also also provides additional advertising and marketing needs including but not limited to web design, multimedia advertising, graphic design including logos and other marketing materials, e-newsletters and electronic communication with customers, etc.

NewLife Consulting (NLC)~
NewLife Consulting, Inc. consults with organizations of all size to develop Strategic Crisis Plans that would enable an organization to avoid the chaos, struggles, financial burdens, & other hardships that can occur with such adversity as the death of a business partner or significant illness, fire or loss of physical property, bankruptcy or severe financial struggles, conflict among business partners, etc. Although it is often hard to think through to the worst possible scenarios and situations, NLC strives to help organizations do so, so that a plan can be put into place 'just in case.'
