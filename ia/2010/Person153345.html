James Mattison’s Summary

New London Communications, Inc. is North Atlanta's leader in visual marketing communications. Since 1987 we have provided our clients in metro Atlanta and nationally the best in marketing services. We offer creative support in brand management, concept development, design, photography, and color management. New London is also a market leader in display graphics and wide format imaging from POP to tradeshow graphics. In addition, we offer a complete range of offset and digital printing with emphasis in 1 to 1 marketing and variable data printing. With our in-house direct mail marketing division we offer a complete turn key service on marketing campaigns, data management and list aqusition. With a complete suite of marketing services all in-house, we provide our clients piece of mind while managing complex marketing programs from one point of contact, all the while adding value.
