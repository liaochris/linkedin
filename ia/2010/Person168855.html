Randy Bachman’s Summary

I never thought much about job descriptions until after I left newspapers for the world of business communications. People simply didn't connect writing with running a business.

The short description of being a writer evolved into an explanation of developing content for brochures, training materials, video scripts, speeches and business meetings.

The job description changed over time as more people began to develop their own copy, I began to focus more on helping clients understand effective communication and integrating a consistent message across all business communications.

The growth of social media is changing my job description again. Businesses today need to be part of the social media conversation which requires constant management.

So now I tell people my job is to help businesses manage their social media message.
