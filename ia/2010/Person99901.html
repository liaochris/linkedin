Joan Graci’s Summary

Why is it that some people are fortunate enough to find their life’s passion in work while others muddle through job after job finding fulfillment only in the fact that they are able to put food on the table? It has taken me 25 years to fully understand this phenomenon, and I've made it my commitment to share that knowledge with anyone who has the courage to look at their career in a different light. I've made it my business to study those who are happy with their careers and watch them as they obviously display a strong passion for the work they do.

I have witnessed first hand the misery and utter disappointment that a wrong career move may result in. Our young are the "career lost" and more experienced workers are not sure what job trends are going to provide a secure future. My years representing a diverse group of companies as an employment recruiter have given me the knowledge to: identify top talent and match them to today’s growth companies, act as a career coach at all levels, help our young people find career paths that exercise their core strengths and preferences and allow them to make a living right out of school.

As the "employment translator" I beg to ask the question; Could there be a more important time in world history to obtain the necessary skills for survival in this volatile employment landscape? There are great jobs out there. Do you have the new tool box to get in on them?

Joan Graci’s Specialties:

Joan’s primary interest resides in helping others discover and apply their passions to their career path.

Her mission to spread invaluable career advice nationally will inspire an awakening throughout today’s employment landscape.

As a premier talk radio show personality and aspiring author, Joan frequently demonstrates her ability as a powerful and influential speaker while addressing audiences here in Western New York, as well as on a national level.
