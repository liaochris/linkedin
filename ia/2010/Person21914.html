Ben Lamm’s Summary

Based in Richmond, Virginia, Ben is responsible for Capital One's customer fulfillment operation and new capability development. In this role, Ben leads all direct mail, statement, letter, credit card, and payment processing operations supporting 33 million customers. Ben is active in steering postal, supplier management, and environmental sustainability efforts within Capital One and across the industry.

Before taking this role in 2010, he helped to create cutting-edge targeting capabilities for Capital One's online card acquisition business. Prior to joining Capital One in 1999, Ben was an intelligence officer in the US Army.
