Patrick Schmitt’s Summary

Patrick Schmitt, designer Inc. is a full service custom home design firm. I have over 20 years of experience in the residential design and construction industry both running my own design firms, as well as, working directly for builder/developers on several large scale developments. Our goal is to service both individual clients who have a building site and need design services for their new home as well as partnering with builders who want a designer who understands their needs and wants a close working relationship. We try to seek out those builder partners who are building in the mid to upper end levels, are small custom home builders and who take great pride in their work.
