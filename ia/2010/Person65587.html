Eric Stephens’s Summary

Eric Stephens is Director of the Enterprise Architecture & Integration team at Excellus BlueCross BlueShield. He specializes in Enterprise Architecture practices, SOA maturity, and Enterprise Architecture modeling. Prior to joining Excellus Eric provided architectural leadership with AppliedTheory Corporation servicing a number of U.S. Government clients. He also led innovation at Questra Consulting and Mutual of New York during his 20+ years in the IT industry. Concurrently with his responsibilities at Excellus and AppliedTheory, Eric held an adjunct faculty position with the Center for Business Information Technology at Syracuse University. Eric lives in Baldwinsville, NY with his wife and four children.

Eric Stephens’s Specialties:

Enterprise architecture, enterprise modeling, systems thinking, design thinking, innovation, software design, organizational change, leadership, software development, communication/mentoring/teaching.
