Craig Leopard’s Summary

A self-motivated and results oriented Sales, Marketing and Operations Executive, with demonstrated ability to successfully lead diverse teams of professionals to new levels of achievement in a variety of highly competitive and fast paced environments. Strong business, finance, technical and consultant qualifications with an impressive track record of more than 25 winning years of hands on experience in strategic planning, business unit and corporate development, project and product management, sales and marketing, and mergers and acquisitions. Proven ability to successfully analyze an organization’s critical business requirements, change deficiencies into strengths and develop innovative and cost effective solutions for enhancing competitiveness while increasing revenues and improving sales and customer service offerings..
