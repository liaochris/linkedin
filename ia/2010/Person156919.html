Maggie Langley’s Summary

Maggie Langley, entrepreneur and founder of OfficeHounds, has years of experience as an administrator and Personal Assistant.

OfficeHounds specialises in providing administrative support to tradesmen, but we help a variety of business to do less paperwork and earn more money. OfficeHounds provide call answering service (9am - 10pm) and a range of outsourced administrative tasks suitable for any business. Services offered include creating/updating CRM database with business card contact details, invoicing, chasing late payments, keyword research, mail shots/mail merge, data entry, tailor made services/products for tradesmen and more.

OfficeHounds' customers benefit from efficient and friendly administrative support on demand without having to pay employee related costs like sick pay and PAYE. Customers only pay for hours worked, so we flow along with our customers' business peaks and troughs.

I have years of experience working as a Personal Assistant. I previously worked in an office in London to support one person based in Scotland and one in York, so I have experience working remotely to give administrative support. For further information about my experience, my CV can be found on the 'About Us' section of the OfficeHounds website.

I am also a qualified secondary English teacher. Working in schools in London certainly made me resiliant and taught me how to stay calm under pressure. Teaching accustomed me to working evenings and week-ends, so I am happy to use some of my free time to support businesses.

I am an American living in Whitstable with my British husband and our two Bassett Hounds, Dave and Iris.
