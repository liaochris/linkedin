15 Seconds on Leadership and Management...

Leaders who are truly effective are qualified, not by credentials or accomplishments, but by their character. Leaders understand that their personal success depends entirely upon the success of those they lead. The self-efficacy of the strongest leaders comes not from titles or promotions, raises or recognition, but from seeing those over whom they’ve achieved some measure of influence become better for the experience. Regardless of rank or title or position, and independent of any designated authority, leaders influence those around them. Superior leaders are set apart by their strength of character, discipline, humility, courage, and vision.
