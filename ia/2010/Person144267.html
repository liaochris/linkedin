Michelle Gordon’s Summary

My career started out as an interior designer, rapidly moving to providing builders and sellers with home decorating and staging advice. I wanted to use my talents and expertise to sell my own homes. Selling real Estate just seemed to be the natural next step.

However, once in the real estate field I have found myself directed in another way. Helping people! So I specialize in the more challenging fields of real estate. Relocation and 1st time home buyers (many of which are low to moderate income) I love helping, and the feeling I get when a plan comes together.

This all lead me to starting a foundation, F.L.O.W. (friends landscaping our world)
Landscaping low to moderate income home buyers new homes in the city of Grand Rapids.

I love what I do, by doing more then just my job!

Specialties
Relocation, 1st time home buyers, credit challenges. I help many buyers purchase bank owned and short sales as well.
