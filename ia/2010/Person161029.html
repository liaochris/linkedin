Paul Kincaid-Smith’s Summary

Looking to work with remarkable people to build, market and sell products and services that improve people's quality of life. In the past I have focused on networks of large-scale, reliable, secure Web and Email systems. (The largest of my projects serviced over 25 million subscribers.) I love to evangelize beneficial technologies by writing white papers and by participating in industry forums.

I bring over 20 years of high-tech experience, including software engineering, QA, technical sales, business development, product marketing and product management. I sold email and wireless messaging platforms to high-value customers (ISPs and telecommunications companies like Sprint, Telus, Excite, Onebox, Telecom Italia, Telecom New Zealand, Ameritech, USA.net, BellSouth). I developed and marketed reseller-friendly Email and Web filtering services (SaaS) for small and medium sized businesses. I currently architect and sell cloud, web hosting, email and SaaS solutions to telco-sized resellers.

I have a passion for bringing new products to market. I have one patent application pending. My interests span the front end of the product life cycle: opportunity identification; business plans; competitive analysis; requirements definition; technology partner identification, evaluation, and relationship development; product management; beta programs and customer relationships; interacting with industry analysts; educating sales teams; technical selling, team building.
