Patricia Robinson’s Summary

Have a nagging feeling that your website could be doing better in the search results? Feel like you might be leaving much needed revenue on the table in this crazy economy? Keep hearing about Twitter, Facebook and Social Media Marketing, but don’t know how to use them for your business? Let me help you!

I’m an experienced Internet marketer with a passion for all things search engine optimization (SEO) and social media related. My talents lie in looking at your website and finding ways to make it more user-friendly and search engine friendly, with the end result being more traffic, fewer people 'bouncing' off your site and most important - increased revenue. Sometimes it’s as simple as seeing what keywords people search on to find your type of business and creating new meta tags. Often it involves changing page content to reflect these terms. And many times it means digging into a much larger bag of tricks, including strategic linking, blogging, PPC (pay per click) and other internet marketing strategies.

I'm currently seeking consulting or full time opportunities in SEO, SEM, PPC, web content writing or editing, social media marketing strategies and web analytics. Email me at pattiorobinson@gmail.com. I look forward to doing business with you!
