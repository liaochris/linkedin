John Barry’s Summary

Editor-in-Chief, Freelance Book Editor, Writer and Public Speaker. Capable of creatively and thoughtfully developing a publication concept and launching it as a marketable product. Focus on biblical studies in confessional organizations and in the academic arena. Long-term goals include positively affecting the lives of others in ministry, writing and business.

Published (or have forthcoming) over 50 articles. Produced one of the top 10 magazines launched in 2008, according to Library Journal. Edited 4 books (scholastic and general titles). Nearly 190,000 total page and RSS reads of blog in 12 months. Articles featured on high-profile web sites. Interviewed by national radio hosts, industry experts and magazines. Subjects covered: the Bible, writing, editing and the magazine industry.
