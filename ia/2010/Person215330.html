Willem Bult’s Summary

My background is in software engineering, and I have from an early age been interested both in programming and software development as in entrepreneurship, and turning product ideas into business. During the shift of the millennium I was selling my first “real” application which counted the days until your computer would crash from the millennium bug.

At age 17 I created fully working in-building navigation software, and I successfully participated in programming contests while in high school. I have since come to be increasingly interested in developing conceptual product ideas and turning them into business opportunities. While pursuing my Bachelors degree I started two high tech companies and won 3rd prize in a university business plan competition. I am interested in contemplating about how to get a product idea to work in the market; and I am always interested in new venture opportunities.

I can be characterized as entrepreneurial, optimistic, introvert and analytical. I thrive in chaos.
