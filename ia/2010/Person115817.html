Kevin Ainsworth’s Summary

Represents corporations and individuals in intellectual property disputes and complex (often international) business disputes. Advises clients and assists with development and implementation of effective strategies. Experienced at resolving disputes through litigation, arbitration, mediation, and negotiation.

Was selected for inclusion in NY Super Lawyers 2010 - Metro—one of only 5% of NY attorneys to be so honored. And was rated AV Preeminent in Martindale-Hubbell peer review ratings in 2007, 2008, 2009, and 2010.

Member of the U.S. District Court Local Rules Committee (EDNY/SDNY), Member of the Federal Bar Council's Committee on Second Circuit Courts and the ABA Copyright Litigation Committee, Past Chair of the Committee on Science and Law of the Association of the Bar of the City of New York, Member of the American Intellectual Property Law Association and the New York Intellectual Property Law Association.

Author of: Evidence Rule 502 Revamps Waiver-of-Privilege Analysis, New York Law Journal, Vol. 240, No. 68, Oct. 6, 2008. (The article can be viewed at http://www.mintz.com/media/pnc/5/media.1705.pdf.)

Has appeared numerous times on national television as a representative of the Marine Corps Scholarship Foundation.
