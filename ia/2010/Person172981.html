Rick Bakas’s Summary

After spending 8 years at NIKE, the most valuable lesson I learned was how to create and manage a brand. Sometimes that means taking the less traveled road, but by doing what is right for the brand, the result tends to strike a chord with consumers.

In the last 12 months, the way consumers interact with a brand in the social and mobile space has become a new area of focus for me. Understanding how to converse with an audience requires a shift in thinking.
