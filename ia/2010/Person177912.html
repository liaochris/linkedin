Ross Joel’s Summary

Ross Joel is CEO and Co-Founder of OR-Live, Inc., a leading worldwide, medical broadcaster with a concentration on internet broadcasting. He has led sales growth at OR-Live that has seen the company’s revenues grow at 50% annually for three consecutive years, and has helped to create and establish OR-Live.com as the number one destination for surgical broadcasting on the Internet.

He has gained a national reputation in healthcare Internet marketing through appearances on such news programs as NBC “Today”, CNN, BBC, ABC News and CBS Evening News. He has also been featured in print news outlets such as Newsweek Magazine, The New York Times, The Boston Globe and The Washington Post.

Before co-founding OR-Live in 1994, he spent nine years as a television anchor/reporter for NBC-affiliated TV stations in the Burlington, Vermont and Hartford, Conn television markets. He is an Emmy Award Nominee and Telly Award Winner.
