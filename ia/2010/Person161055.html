Paul Kowalski’s Summary

Making a living as advertising professional since 1985, Paul began his career in the days of drawing tables and key lines. This longevity brings not only gray hair, but quite a bit of knowledge in the world of marketing, advertising, campaigns, strategy, design and production. After all, you would hope he has learned something in nearly a quarter of a century in the biz. He learned a lot working with clients such as: General Motors, Buick, Dow, Dow Corning, Chrysler, Toyota, DeBeers, but keeps an open mind to new techniques and methods to help strengthen his clients marketing programs.

Paul earned a Bachelor of Arts in Design from Saginaw Valley State University and then returned there to teach for eight years. After a few years as an adjunct professor, they decided to let him head up the Graphic Design program (he suspects mainly because no one else wanted it). While in that position, he developed a BFA of Arts Degree, restructured the Graphic Design curriculum, established and internship program an saw the enrollment double in the program…not to mention forging many wonderful memories of the 100’s of students he taught.

Spending most of his adult life in the field of graphic arts, Paul began his career at Bradford LaRiviere as a Designer/Illustrator, moved to CSC Advertising as Art Director and then to Holt Marketing as Creative Director. Along the way he also created and produced his own comic books, developed in-house graphic design departments and freelanced his services…while having a pretty good time no matter what he was doing.

Paul Kowalski’s Specialties:

Working with small to mid-size businesses to develop marketing strategies, advertising campaigns, customer contact programs and internal marketing.

Have worked with over 40 commercial construction clients and over 50 manufacturing clients.
