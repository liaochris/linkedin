Bob Andelman’s Summary

Bob Andelman is the host and producer of "Mr. Media Interviews" on BlogTalkRadio.com. He is also the author or co-author of 9 biographical, business, management and sports books. These include: "Will Eisner: A Spirited Life" (Dark Horse/M Press; October 2005); "Built From Scratch" (with Bernie Marcus & Arthur Blank, co-founders of The Home Depot; Crown Business, 1999); "The Profit Zone" (with Adrian Slywotzky; Crown Business); "Mean Business" (with Albert J. Dunlap; Crown Business, 1996); "The Corporate Athlete" (with Jack Groppel; John Wiley, 2000); Stadium For Rent (McFarland and Company, 1993). He most recently wrote "Fans Not Customers" with former Commerce Bank founder and CEO Vernon W. Hill II; the book would have been published by Portfolio/Penguin in September 2007 but Mr. Hill stepped down from the bank in June 2007 after the book was completed but not yet published.

Bob Andelman’s Specialties:

Collaborating with CEOs and entrepreneurs who have expertise in their field but need assistance telling their story or sharing their wisdom in book form. I work with title credit or without.
