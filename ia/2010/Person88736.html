Jamie Holdcraft’s Summary

I am an Entrepreneur at heart and have been that way since I was very young. I started at a young age (with my dad's help) buying horse tack wholesale and selling it at horse shows on the weekends. Since then I have always enjoyed being creative and most of all helping others to see the best about life and their situations.

I have found that many individuals just need a small encouraging push to head in the right direction with their business. I started Social Butterfly Media Coaching to help and encourage those individuals.

I am a young and very energetic person. My glass is always overflowing - not even close to half empty :-) I have found great pleasure in helping "social media newbies" to use social media to boost their businesses. Most of them have heard of Facebook, Twitter and the other sites, but have no idea where to start or really how it would benefit their business. That is where my energy and encouragement comes in!

For many of you that are entrepreneurs may well know it is difficult to just be involved in one business. I am also a part of Vollara which is a cutting edge, leader in the Green Technology sector. I was introduced to this company in June of 2008 and I have never been more impressed with products that are so beneficial to families and businesses.

Our products range from air/water purification, home energy management, laundry system that allows the elimination of bleach and detergent & over 40 other hi-tech products to protect and enhance life.

We are an AMERICAN company, but we are able to service international needs. I welcome any questions that you may have on either of these awesome companies. I also enjoy connecting with other positive, like minded individuals.
