Brandon Steele’s Summary

Brandon Steele is a Certified Competent Search Engine Marketer and completed the Web Analytics program at the University of British Columbia. He majored in Economics, Politics and International Relations, and holds a master's degree in Marketing. He also holds certificates in managing multiple projects and employee coaching, and is a graduate of the Disney Creative Institute.

Brandon Steele has expertise in marketing, marketing engineering, messaging, content management, web analytics, usability, and all facets of Search Engine Marketing (SEM) including: Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Pay Per Click (PPC), banner campaign management and social media.

He is a Veteran of the Marine Corps, where he was awarded the Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal, the Meritorious Unit Commendation, the Good Conduct Medal, the National Defense Service Medal, and 3 Meritorious Service Awards.

In 2004, he was the Co-Chair of Volusia County for Dean, served as Director of Recruiting for the Volusia Chapter of the UNA-USA, and volunteered as a Model UN Judge and a youth basketball coach. In 2010 he helped out The Susan Komen Foundation for the Cure with fundraising activities. He also volunteered for the Presidential campaigns of John Kerry in 2004 and Barack Obama in 2008.

He founded The Progressive Current, Pleasant Surmise, Vanmark Cleaning Solutions and Verodus Direct. He is a contributor to OpEdNews.com and The Progressive Current.

In 2009 an independent consulting firm ranked one of his websites in the top 15 for SEO in the education industry - one of only two traditional colleges to make the list.
