Herve Cuviliez’s Summary

Herve Cuviliez, is a serial entrepreneur in the internet industry since 1996.
He started his career in Paris, France by creating his first interactive agency and sold it to DDB Worldwide (Omnicom group) in 1999. Since then he has been in charge of all the digital activities of DDB in France (1500 employees).
In 1999, Herve also started his investment activities. His active portfolio is Cashstore.fr ( Ecommerce in France), MyID.is (online identity certification startup in London) and more recently Wikeez.com (a social entertainment platform in the Middle East)
Herve is the Founder and Managing Director of Kuv Capital, he is also involved in Tribal DDB as executive vice president for Middle East, French Ambassador for Webby Awards, member of Appfrica.org advisory board.
