Ryan Sullivan’s Summary

I am a quick learning, entrepreneurial, leader with a solid technical foundation and superior business acumen. My experiences have exposed me to both B2B and B2C environments, technical and non technical presentation to multiple levels of management (including C-level), program management and customer relationship building.

My blend of technical and business knowledge gives me a unique view into Web technologies, especially those centered on internet marketing, participant marketing and digital entertainment platforms. I am able to help businesses harness these technologies and leverage their power to improve business process or access new markets.

My current professional goals are to develop analysis tools and techniques that allow clients to:
1. Project future opportunity with their current search marketing program(s)
2. Gain greater insight into their current campaign's impact on their search business
3. View ROI measurements that track real search marketing investment return (by removing noise like YOY trends, the impact of current events, etc.)
