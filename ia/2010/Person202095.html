The Malone Group, Inc. has two primary divisions, a traditional Real Estate office, headed up by his wife, Terri Malone and a traditional Mortgage Loan Brokerage which he manages, specializing in challenged as well as perfect credit.

  • Director Of Operations

    Loan Review, Inc.

    (Financial Services industry)

    April 2007 — May 2009 (2 years 2 months)

    Christopher joined Loan Review, Inc. in early 2007. As Director of Operations, he was responsible for the essential task of reviewing and analyzing loan application packages to insure that they are properly structured to comply with technical lender requirements and applicable financial guidelines. Christopher concentrated on researching and cataloging the standards and specific review process employed by underwriters at various lenders, and trained members of the Loan Review sales staff to efficiently tailor loan application packages to comply with such standards. Christopher personally conducted a final review of all loan applications, prior to transmittal to lenders. He identified underwriting nonconformities, anticipated and dealt preemptively with potential problems, and routed the application to the lenders whose specific requirements are most closely met by the particular package being submitted. Christopher’s work greatly increased the firm’s efficiency and effectiveness in successfully placing loans.
