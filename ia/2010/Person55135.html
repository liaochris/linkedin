Derrick Aviles’s Summary

With a special interest in the graphic design industry. I have successfully completed the required courses to complete the 4 year degree that I've earned. I have 5 years of experience in the field of design and proficient with programs such as Microsoft Office 2007 and Adobe Suite CS2-CS4.

My studies have also led to practical experience at the Minnesota Timberwolves and Lynx as part of my graphic design internship program. While working at the Timberwolves and Lynx, I was responsible for assisting the creative department in various projects such as updating and designing Timberwolves or Lynx flyers, forms, brochures and web elements. Other related experience from the job I've gained experience putting together mock-ups for proofing and creating, editing, and producing layouts and artwork for both web and print delivery.I have also had experience freelancing for various small business & communities around Minnesota.

Currently I'm looking for a full-time position in the field of design.
