Neil Aaron’s Summary

A versatile, innovative financial executive with Big Four accounting firm experience with more than 20 years of experience leading and coordinating global teams in the development and execution of financial statement audits, internal audit programs and the development of several specialty practices. Throughout the over 20 years, received increasing responsibilities and recognition with promotions in client service and National office roles. Highlights include:
• Results-focused, strategic leader with a track record of building, leading and re-engineering high performance financial, operational and advisory-focused global internal audit teams
• Accomplished professional with international experience leading risk assessments, progressive risk management programs and cutting edge internal audit transformations for some of the world’s leading companies
• Creative problem solver that developed and deployed high-value internal audit methodologies, contract management solutions and high quality recommendations to solve complex business issues
• Influential interpersonal presenter, motivator and negotiator with boards, senior executives and internal audit teams
• Strong people leader in building, developing and deploying diverse teams with a proven track record for diversity and people development
• Deep media & entertainment industry experience; specifically in magazine and book publishing, cable broadcasting, film production and distribution, talent agencies and advertising
