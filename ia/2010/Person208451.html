Troy Johnson’s Summary

I am an IT Project Manager with an expertise in Knowledge Management and Web Content Management Systems. I have been responsible for the entire life cycle, from content creation to data archival, of web portals for much of my career. My achievements have included significant contributions to cost savings efforts at the global level and I have led teams that consistently met their goals on time and on budget.

Recently, I have moved more into the area of marrying my communications skills and my technical background to speak to a non-technical audience. My goal has been to empower my customers and show them the real power of the Web and Social Networking technologies to drive their goals and their business success.

I enjoy leading teams of creative people to attack and solve technical problems. My real passion is to always help others succeed at their goals.
