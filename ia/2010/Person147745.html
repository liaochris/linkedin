Michael Mandanis’s Summary

My career in merchandising, starting in 1987 and continuing for 21 years, encompasses sustained hands-on experience and accomplishments of three years at Brookstone, five years at Vermont Country Store, four years at Real Goods Trading, six years at Restoration Hardware, nearly two years at Eton and over one year at Frontgate. I have brought to each of these management positions leadership skills, entrepreneurial enthusiasm, passion and commitment to innovation and commercialization of high-value products.

Michael Mandanis’s Specialties:

I have extensive knowledge in global sourcing having gained much of my experience in an items-driven environment. I am extremely well-connected in the industry, with a deep base in the vendor community and an excellent reputation for building and maintaining productive and viable relationships. I am sensitive and responsive to country-specific business practices and relationships.
