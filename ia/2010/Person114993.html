Keri Bailey’s Summary

Marketing management experience spans Fortune 500 corporations (Yahoo!, Microsoft, Gemstar TV Guide, Visa, 3Com), technology startups, as well as agency experience (Digitas, Millsport).

Keri is currently responsible for acquisition marketing at Move, Inc.

Previously, she led all marketing aspects of Yahoo! Real Estate, from customer acquisition strategy and consumer insights, to engagement and retention. Keri has also held marketing lead roles in Yahoo! Personals and Yahoo! Travel.

Prior to Yahoo! Keri managed client relationships with Microsoft while employed with Digitas, focusing on increasing customer retention, renewal, and satisfaction via online and integrated marketing methods.

At Storymail, a digital media technology startup, Keri provided Internet marketing solutions to companies like M&M Mars and ABC Studios. She joined Storymail in it's early stages and enjoyed two years developing and refining product positioning, market focus and launching the company. Storymail was acquired by Gemstar TV Guide March 2002.

At NetReality Keri managed worldwide marketing and supported sales for the hardware/software bandwidth optimization solution creating more efficient networks. NetReality was acquired by Allot Communications in September 2002.

Keri's professional experience began at 3Com, in Global Marketing, managing national and international corporate events/seminars, participating in global advertising and supporting the early involvement of the company in Sports Marketing.

She brings strong leadership competencies, strategic marketing abilities, fresh creative approaches balanced with a solid analytical mind and intense attention to detail. With great energy, optimism, and commitment to excellence, she enjoys working with companies that offer the same, as well as opportunity for growth and development in a highly motivated environment.
