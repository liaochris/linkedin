Mark Backer’s Summary

Experienced, versatile executive with deep expertise and a record of success in developing biological products from applied research through licensure (7 approved products over 25+ years). Currently providing consulting support to the industry and investors, with a focus on technical development and manufacturing activities. Drives projects aggressively while managing technical and regulatory risk. Understands the big picture, and has the technical experience to drill into the details, troubleshoot and make the right decisions.

Management:
Company operating plans, financing and diligence activities, budgets and financial models, product development plans, project management, IP and deal assessment

Technical Operations:
Vendor selection and management of contracted activities, development and manufacturing operations from clone selection through final dosage form, validation and tech transfer, team leadership

Products:
• 12 years vaccine development and manufacturing experience
