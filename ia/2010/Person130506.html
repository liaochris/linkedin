Carlos 'Charly' Maltagliatti’s Summary

In the year 2000 Charly finished his degree in graphic design in U.A.D.E and focused on digital media and Flash. For four years he worked in multimedia agencies (e-volution and Latin3) as interactive designer and actionscript developer. He Produced websites, characters, interfaces, animations, illustrations, applications, games, DVDs, and code for the most important regional and European brands.(i.e. Disney, Coke, Microsoft, Sony, Aiwa, Peugeot...)

After that he created and managed for two years e-volution's media unit focused on creativity, production and deploy of all online campaigns of the company.

During 2008, he became part of Globant as Design Tech Leader for Electronic Arts working on UI Design for Godfather2 (Xbox360-Ps3) in Argentina HQ and customer offices in San Francisco, USA.

In 2009 he joined Microsoft as Brand Solutions Specialist for Latam, with the challenge of improve the Latin American top brands with innovative strategies and creative solutions.
