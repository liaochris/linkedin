Cheryl Lancaster, CMP’s Summary

Professional meeting and event planner with over 17 years of experience in both non-profit and corporate planning. Managed a non-profit department planning large religious conferences and an annual convention. Corporate meeting planner for Toyota Motor Sales for 10 years. Active in Meeting Professionals International (MPISCC) and a Certified Meeting Professional (CMP) with an intent to complete the Certification in Meeting Management (CMM).

Cheryl Lancaster, CMP’s Specialties:

Management expert in incentive planning and creative program design with strong organizational and budget management skills. Dynamic interpersonal skills, detailed contract negotiator and logistical leader. Avid golfer!
