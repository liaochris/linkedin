Glen B. Alleman’s Summary

Vice President, Program, Planning, and Controls.

I developed Lewis & Fowler's Deliverables Based Planning (sm) method and applying it to aerospace, defense, and commerical enterprise projects.

This program management method is based 25 years of Project and Program Management executive positions stakeholder value by identifying, developing and delivering mission critical solutions for the commercial, industrial, and government agency marketplace.

My management capabilities center on building performance based operational excellence; strategy based solution delivery; portfolio management; financial controls; systems architecture; product, services and customer management; business development.
