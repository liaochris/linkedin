Chris Kimble’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience as an academic and have published over 80 articles in books, journals and refereed conferences. I am the Senior Academic editor for the journal Global Business and Organizational Excellence, published by Wiley, have supervised and/or examined 17 PhD students and published two books.

My research area is Information Systems / Information Management. My approach is socio-technical, in the sense that I am interested in how best to 'manage' the fit between technology and the social world.

My main area of teaching is Management Information Systems. I have also taught courses on Information Systems and Strategy, Systems Design Methodologies and the Social Impact of Computers.

Chris Kimble’s Specialties:

Most of my recent work has focused on Communities of Practice and Distributed Collaborative Working. I am also interested in Knowledge Management, the problems of cross-cultural working, the extent to which technology can support virtual teams and communities and the application of Social Network Analysis to geographically distributed organisations.
