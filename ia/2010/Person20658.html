Stéphane Baziadoly’s Summary

Having obtained my Engineering degree in computing science from the INSA of Lyon in 1992, I had the opportunity to develop a strong understanding of the financial services sector with customers like Clearstream, Flemings Asset Management and Credit Agricole.
