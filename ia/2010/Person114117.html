Kelly Williams’s Summary

I am the owner and operator of KellyWilliamsVA, a bookkeeping and shopping cart support specialist. I began my virtual assistance business following 25 years in the corporate world as a project and cost control manager in the building and interior design industries. After the birth of my son, I wanted to continue sharing my valuable expertise and welcome the opportunity to partner with small business owners with an online presence who just can't do it all. My current service focus is eCommerce business support utilizing the 1ShoppingCart service, bookkeeping and WordPress blogsite maintenance. I operate as Kelly Williams, LLC from San Diego, California.

My goal is to develop long-term professional partnerships with each client by providing invaluable support that improves productivity and increases peace of mind. Effectively freeing my clients' time to focus on the challenges of championing and growing their business is my priority. My mission is successful, when my clients are successful!

Ecommerce experience includes shopping cart setup and maintenance, creation and management of product, autoresponders and affiliate programs, as well as email marketing duties.

Wordpress and website experience includes installation of blog platform with theme and plugins, setup and maintenance, sales pages incorporating product buy buttons graphics and header modifications, and list building opt-in boxes.

Kelly Williams’s Specialties:

I am Master Cart Certified by Practice Pay Solutions in 1ShoppingCart, a Certified QuickBooks User, and a Microsoft Certified Office User.

I am gifted at developing and writing procedures manuals, creating interactive forms, spreadsheets and presentations. I have an excellent memory, am exceptionally detail-oriented and organized. Process-minded.
