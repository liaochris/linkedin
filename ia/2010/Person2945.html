Alexander deHilster’s Summary

EXPERIENCE
ADH Meetings & Events is a Chicago based meeting management and event planning firm, partnering with in-house corporate and non-profit organizations with the planning of their programs. This allows for the in-house planner more time to focus on the contents of the program, the attendees, presenters, sponsors and internal clients, while we at ADH take care of all the associated logistics such as venue and hotel searches, contract negotiations, audio visual needs, catering, decor, entertainment, transportation and more.

Each of the key members at ADH Meetings & Events have a minimum of 20 years experience in the hospitality, catering, entertainment and meeting and event planning industries, with the balance of the staff a minimum of 10 years.

The majority of ADH Meetings & Events staff members have an international background, whether they were born in a different country, speaking multiple languages or have lived and/or worked on different continents, with the exception of Antartica.

We're here to make your valuable time more productive whether it's planning a board or meetings, training seminars, investigator meetings, pharma meetings, alumni events, diversity retreats, incentive trips, team building events, guest tour programs, client dinners or fundraisers.

Whether your group is just a handful of attendees or 1,500 guests, ADH Meetings & Events can take care of small portions or your entire program, if it is only the hotel, or F&B and ground transportation, or only AV, entertainment and/or decor and everything in between.

FOREIGN LANGUAGES
At ADH we speak a multitude of languages, among them all the European ones, allowing us to plan your programs anywhere.

CLIENTS
ADH clients are in healthcare, consulting, property management, banking, insurance, education and non-profit.

CERTIFICATION
In June 2008, ADH became certified by the NGLCC, allowing us to be registered as a diversity supplier with Fortune 500 companies.
