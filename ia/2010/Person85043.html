Ingrid Glenn’s Summary

I am a Relationship and Personal Counsellor based in Frankston, Victoria, Australia with 25 years of professional and personal experience in the helping ministry. My varied experience assists me to provide counselling support for the whole person - including the psychological, social, physical and spiritual aspects.

I am also a Creative Memories Consultant because I love scrapbooking and have personally experienced the psychological healing that comes from creating personal memory albums.

I also make environmentally friendly scented soy candles.

Ingrid Glenn’s Specialties:

Personal Growth, Relationship Counselling, Recovery from Addictive and Self Destructive Behaviours, ADHD, Support for Pastors and Ministers, Christian Counselling plus Scrapbooking, Papercrafts and Soy Candlemaking
