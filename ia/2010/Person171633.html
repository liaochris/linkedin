Roger Gonzalez’s Summary

20 years experience in software development, with a track record of mastering complex problem domains and leading small teams to produce quality products.

• Leadership experience includes engineering management, numerous assignments as team lead, and roles as a “thought leader” and evangelist. Experienced with agile methodologies (certified Scrum Master), project and people management, recruiting, process creation and optimization, product strategy, and full SDLC.
• Roles often required exceptional communications skills, such as giving talks at conferences, writing technical papers, working with the community through blogs and mailing lists, and giving presentations to investors or potential customers. Communications skills exercised via weekend teaching activities, training thousands of students using adult learning principles.
• Significant experience in multimedia software, including managing development of a 3D action game through commercial release, developing the audio subsystem for the Curl RIA language, media transcoding for the Flash virtual machine, work on distributed SIMNET/DIS simulations, and a variety of OpenGL and DirectX programming.
• Expertise with low-level and systems software, such as device drivers and hardware interfacing, memory management, virtual machines, compiler internals, optimization techniques, and IP network protocols.
• Extensive web software experience both on the server and client; notable achievements including helping create Adobe Flex, writing a sophisticated HTTP/1.1 web server from scratch, and developing a scripting engine that was used for several years as the core of several prominent web sites, including Ryder Trucks and the Monster Board job search site.
• Skill at architecting maintainable software frameworks that reduce application-level complexity through effective abstraction and decoupling.
