Linda Van Dillen’s Summary

Registered Nurse with experience in Emergency and Occupational Medicine, prior to working as a Nurse Consultant for a TPA. Experienced in Medical Records Analysis and developing cost projections for future care. Currently manages the Marketing Division and develops products and services for S&H Medical Management Services, Inc. Assisted in developing a software program specific to the needs of case management.

Linda Van Dillen’s Specialties:

Developing software for the case management industry, researching and developing services and products to meet the needs of insurance companies, self-insured employers and third party administrators, developing relationships with municipalities to assist with their health insurance needs.
