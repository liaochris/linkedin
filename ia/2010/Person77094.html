Graeme Thickins’s Summary

Graeme has seen many waves of technology in his career, and ridden a good many of them. He's also survived three business cycles since 1980 -- whoops, make that four. He figures all this couldn't help but bestow upon him a certain amount of wisdom. (Then again, he could be wrong.)

Graeme founded GT&A Strategic Marketing, a strategy, branding, content development, and startup advisory consultancy. Over 25+ years, he's helped more than 100 tech firms launch, grow, and prosper -- many from the concept stage -- with marketing & launch strategy and execution. He's also helped several clients take advantage of social media to advance their business goals. GT&A is currently working with:

- a cloud computing provider
- an IT consulting firm
- a sales enablement SaaS platform company
- a global video-sharing news startup
- an Internet identity software firm
- and others

In past years, Graeme has also served in key interim-executive roles, including for the launch of BestBuy.com as interim Director of Content, and as interim Director of Marketing for an IT services firm. More recently, he has served as a VP of marketing for a software firm (acquired 2008), and as interim VP of marketing for an Internet and mobile apps developer, among other assignments.

In addition to his consulting and advisory work, Graeme has reported for years on tech conferences via his own blog and for media outlets such as eWeek, Network World, Computerworld, and others. He's also written extensively for publication on various tech topics (www.graemethickins.com). His large national network of leaders and influentials in the technology and investment communities has been built over more than two decades. Recently, he's written for the investor publication The Angel Journal, ReadWriteWeb, Chief Executive Officer magazine, and eContent magazine.

Graeme has an alternate base of operation in Southern California, where he enjoys working and writing "from the beach" when he can.
