Sarah O'Keefe’s Summary

We fix publishing problems. Perhaps your localization costs are rising too quickly? Maybe it takes too long to get information out the door to your customer? Do you need to ensure that content is accurate and consistent in all your forms? Scriptorium Publishing specializes in evaluating and redesigning content production workflows.

Most of our solutions are built on XML as a foundation, and we are also starting to integrate user-generated content as part of the overall strategy.

For more information, see http://www.scriptorium.com.
