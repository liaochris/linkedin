Log Homes and Log Cabins’s Summary

Avalon Log Homes - Log homes, Log cabins, Luxury log homes, log cabin homes, free custom log homes information, Log home prices, and log home plans by Avalon Log Homes.

Blending Luxury with Nature, Avalon Log Homes is your Log Home connection. Our expert team provides the finest quality log products and planning services to make your dream home become a reality.

While Avalon Log Homes is certainly not the oldest log home company in the business, or the biggest, we are absolutely committed to becoming one of the nation's premier log home suppliers.

Avalon Log Homes offers a full line of services for luxury log homes, custom log homes, milled log cabins, handcrafted log homes, and timber frame homes all over the USA & Canada.
