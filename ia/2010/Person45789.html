Daan De Kroon daan.dekroon@diageo.com’s Summary

Daan is an ambitious, outgoing and confident world citizen with an extraordinary drive and passion for the drinks industry. As his father´s wife owns a wine company and his family has brewed ‘Kroon’ beer since 1623 he has gained a great understanding of the drinks business since he was young.

Due to working, studying and traveling in multiple countries he has a sound knowledge of various cultures, languages and ways of working. Last but not least he loves challenges, which reflects his ´nothing is impossible´ approach.

In 1999 he was selected for the Heineken ´Stars on Stage´ international traineeship in the Caribbean. Subsequently he wrote his thesis with Bavaria, the Dutch brewery that bought the Kroon brand, on ´marketing communications planning for Kroon beer´.

In 2003 he started working with Diageo, based in London as telemarketeer, with the objective to gain as much experience as possible with the drinks industry. Within 6 years he has gained priceless insights and experiences across a range of continents, brands, categories and functions such as (major) account and territory management, trademarketing, brand management, business development & strategy and innovation.

Currently, in the Commercial Manager role, he is responsible for building amazing, mutually rewarding relationships with 13 Licensed Breweries in the Caribbean and Central America with the objective to explode growth for Guinness, Jamaica's pride Red Stripe, Dragon Stout, Smirnoff Ice and D&G Malta.

Gaining an MBA has been an essential part of his professional preparation that has filled gaps in his knowledge on business finance, strategic planning and operations, to mention just a few. This is why he completed an Executive MBA program at Florida International University.

His mid to longterm goal is to achieve a Commercial Director position followed by a GM position within the beverage industry, although he is always open to entrepreneurial activities as well.
