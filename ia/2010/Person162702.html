Peter Emil van den Berg’s Summary

As pixelliquid I work as an independent Senior Designer, with experience in both the traditional (eg print and broadcast) and new media.
In the past year the focus has shifted somewhat towards design for rich internet applications, animation and interaction; I like to expand my expertise in design & animation (2d/3d) for ria's, broadcast or independent projects for the coming period.

I am always on the lookout for talented design agencies to work with as a graphic or visual designer, be it on projectbase or as part of their staff.

I tend to work alone, but feel strongly attracted to creative groups for the realization of creative projects.

With above mentioned further specialization in the area of animation I sure will push the envelope ...
