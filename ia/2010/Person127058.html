Lydia McArthur’s Summary

Lydia McArthur is a Senior Managing Partner of the SET corporation. Along with a group of senior executives, HR Directors, and others with experience in the employment field, Lydia, has helped grow this firm into a leader in the employment field. She is accomplished executive with a record of success in sales management, sales, key account management and consulting roles.

Most of her career has been in B2B sales environments where she has sold software training services, telecommunications services, CRM service and more to the Fortune 50 as well as to smaller organizations as well as government entities. She is effective in establishing and nurturing key accounts including some of the country's top corporations and government entities.

Since 2004, Lydia has logged notable achievements in consulting, sales management and sales roles. For Fanfare Mediaworks, the largest national grocery advertiser, she signed up 62 new customers including the US Army in a five month stint. Finally, for Corporate America Publishers, the leading niche marketing guide publisher, she increased sales 20%.Her career began in 1991 with ATT PDC, the software training arm of AT&T. Using her cold calling and closing skills, she established key account relationships with the City of Denver, CoBank and Celestial Seasoning; recruited by MAI/VIT, an IT staffing solutions company. She grew this $100 thousand per year business into a multimillion dollar market force. As National Account Executive and, later, Regional Manager, notably she and team were awarded the prime vendor role with IBM Global, FedEx and MCI. Recruited by Convergent Communications, a start up telecom to win and handle corporate accounts. She increased the revenue from her territory by 30%. Among her triumphs was selling a multimillion contract with the City of Denver.

Lydia has lived in Parker, CO for the last thirty years - raising her three children plus a few peacocks and horses as well.
