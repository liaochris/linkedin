Jeff Ernst’s Summary

I'm a software product manager, strategist, and marketer who has defined and launched web applications aimed at improving recruiting, sales, marketing, and service effectiveness.

One thing I'm passionate about is helping employers and jobseekers find the RIGHT fit between the personality and values of the person, and the culture, management style, and values of the company. I am co-founder and recruiter at TalentReef, a startup company developing a Social Recruiting application (also known as a Social Media Recruiting) that helps companies promote their employer brand, increase employee referral, and attract great candidates for current and future openings by leveraging social media.

I developed the "Are You a Social Recruiter?" self-assessment, which you can take by going to www.AreYouASocialRecruiter.com.

I'm the author of The New Rules of Sales Enablement and co-author of How to Create Killer Sales Playbooks.

I'm a frequent public speaker and blogger on the topics of social media, social recruiting, sales enablement, sales and marketing effectiveness, and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS).

When not trying to match people with their dream jobs, you’ll find me driving around New England watching my kids play competitive sports and living up to my reputation as a physical fitness fanatic.

Actively seeking career and business opportunities.
Common misspellings of my name include Jeff Ernest, Jeff Earnst, Jeff Earnest, Jeff Urnst, Geoff Ernst, Geoff Ernest, Geoff Earnest.
