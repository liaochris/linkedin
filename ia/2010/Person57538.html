Battery Technology – Technical sales and marketing
A total of 20 years experience (Technical, Marketing and Sales) in the utilization and selection of secondary cells (lead acid batteries), in the preparation of external Micro Grids (UPS / Inverter and storage batteries), leading to the marketing and selling of battery storage and charging options for Nickel Cadmium, Lithium Iron and lead acid battery combinations.
Detailed technical and application knowledge on Pulse Width Modulated (PWM) Inverter technology – Business Development and Marketing collateral.
Specifically and a part of the 20 years, 10 years experience in the application of all battery types for use in traction battery applications, emergency lighting and the storage of energy for inverter and power supply (DC) solutions.
