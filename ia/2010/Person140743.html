Résumé de Melissa Lawrenz

I'm an editor. I work in short forms, long forms, and episodics. I'm quick, creative, and an easy person to work with you. Is that a stupid thing to say? Not when you want your editor to try things, change things, and work with you to create the best product possible. Have you worked with someone who refuses to make that change? I'm not that type of person. I'll try anything to see if it will make the film/show better.

I love a challenge and new opportunities. So, get in contact with me if you have a project that needs an excellent, fun, and easy going editor.

Compétences de Melissa Lawrenz

AVID- Media Composer, Xpress Pro. FCP, Compressor, DVD Studio Pro. Steenbeck, KEM- can sync film dailies. Can handle SD & HD workflows. Multi-camera grouping, syncing, Excellent troubleshooting skills. Mac & PC proficient.
