Kevin Fink’s Summary

My passion is solving business problems and creating business opportunities using modern technology. I love building and driving teams of excited, dedicated engineers to create excellent products and services that make a difference (not necessarily change the world, but make someone's day a little bit more fulfilling).

I am:

An experienced problem-solver adept at selecting (or inventing, if necessary) and applying the optimal technology to resolve business problems.

An excellent verbal and written communicator, with extensive and diverse experience ranging from technical architecture talks to business overviews for Congressional committees, and from internal technical white papers to marketing documents.

A confident, credible, and persuasive cross-functional leader, experienced in guiding technology teams into partnerships with business units.

Experienced at: Perl scripting and programming, with special interests in data analysis, modular software architecture, and database-driven websites. Database design and architecture, with hands-on experience with Oracle, MySQL, and PostgreSQL. High-availability website design, architecture, implementation, and operation. Use of XML, XSLT, CSS, HTML, AJAX, and a plethora of other techologies to solve business problems.
