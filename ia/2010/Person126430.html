Lisa Goodwin’s Summary

I have worn many hats as a professional, all have contributed to the unique skill set that make up my money making side. I love collaborating with success focused people and businesses to make things happen. I've been successful owning businesses and working as an executive for others but mostly I am good at helping others move from being stuck or wandering aimlessly to getting the most out of their lives, products or business.

My goal is to arrive at the end of my life, having made a difference on this planet. I want to rest my head at night knowing that I gave it my all and I want to wake the next morning with a chance to live my vision, passion and purpose.

If you want to collaborate with someone like me, shoot me an email. Who knows, we might just be the perfect fit for business!
