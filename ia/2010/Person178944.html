Russell Lewis’s Summary

When searching for a Real Estate Agent to Represent YOU . . .You should not feel like you are dealing with a USED CAR SALESMAN . . .

With thousands of realtors entering and leaving the market every year, it can be confusing and frustrating to be pressured by an agent for your business. My name is Russell Lewis, and I am a native Texan who has called Austin my home since 1974. As a real estate Broker, I have 19 years of proven success representing buyers and sellers in the marketing and sale of upscale homes throughout Downtown, Central, West and Southwest Austin, Westlake and the Eanes School District. My clients are treated with respect, and they feel their needs are the focus of a qualified and experienced professional.

I am keenly aware that whether marketing your current residence or searching for a new home, you want to move at your own pace, and want to gather as much data as possible to make a truly informed decision. As one of the top 1% of realtors in Austin, I am successful because I listen to my clients, respect their needs and help them establish realistic expectations with any real estate transaction. My role is that of a real estate consultant rather than a salesperson.

Russell Lewis’s Specialties:

Representing buyers and sellers in Austin's finest neighborhoods, my work is divided between listing and selling giving me a unique real time perspective My success in the upscale marketplace is based on service, knowledge and a sense of humor, giving me flexibility to match discerning buyers and sellers. Strong, intelligent negotiating skills coupled with an established background within the industry are essentilal components of the prefessional service I provide my clients.
