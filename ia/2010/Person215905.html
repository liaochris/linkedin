Wil Ford’s Summary

A native of New York City and also a U.S. Marine veteran, Wil Ford has been a Web property developer since 1996. In 2002, he founded a popular Web site called Wil's Domain, a human-edited and well-categorized directory of blogs, RSS news feeds and Web sites from around the world.

In addition to posting Weblog entries and developing Web properties, Wil writes articles on many and varied subjects in which he is sincerely interested. When Wil is online, he is blogging and developing innovative Web properties like the social/information network called Juaxoo — pronounced wah-zoo. When he is offline, the U.S. Marine helps humanity through random acts of kindness, for which he absolutely refuses to take credit.

Wil Ford’s Specialties:

Web property development, writer, Web application developer, Web 2.0 technologist, Internet branding and identity, Web architecture, non-profits, real estate investment, and Web 2.0 technology consultant.
