Carole Gray-Weihman’s Summary

Looking for more gallery representation, fine-art collectors, consignment work and freelance writing opportunities for the arts. I am a devoted painter of the “Color Visualist Movement" also know as the "New American Impressionist Movement". My entire focus is working "en plein air" – studying the “Hawthorne-Hensche Principle" while broadening my scope of learning by studying the academic approaches that were specifically derived from "the Bogart School of Art". I've had 12+ yrs of training and experience in the field of plein-air, having studied with many of the PAPA Signature Members at Scottsdale Artists' School.

Carole Gray-Weihman’s Specialties:

I have an honest approach of seeing and understanding color relationships and the many light keys of nature and in interpreting these relationships on canvas. I have a firm understanding of color, values, drawing and composition and can effectively teach these principals to others. I have devoted my life to learning, painting and teaching.
