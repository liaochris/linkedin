Francesco Amigoni’s Summary

Francesco Amigoni (a.k.a. Franz) has got a IT background of 15 years.

He built a thorough knowledge of the SAP environment mixed together with human relationship skills. In more than 11 years, has had the opportunity to work with many companies, in different market areas, including: Manufacturing, Process Controls, Insurance, Media, Fashion, Pharmaceuticals.

As a member of IT consultancy companies, he had the possibility to being involved in projects working in medium and big companies, often in international environments.

SAP Specialist/Technical Lead with demonstrate experience of leading teams or projects in the SAP development workbench.

Apply deep process and technical skills to supervise and manage the design, build and implementation of SAP solutions across a range of functions.

Full project lifecycles experience in the SAP development workbench related to WM/MM/PP modules.

After having had various work experiences - CAD layout developer, process controls programmer, aid-network manager (1992-1997) - in a international process control company, he began his career in SAP ERP as a ABAP/4 developer in 1998.

Among his extra-work interests Francesco counts Literature, History, Geopolitics, Philosophy, New Technologies' impacts on society. He believes in sharing ideas and hopes; ideas like OpenSource; websites like TED and like MIT Open CourseWare.
He loves Music (and plays piano). FA reads a lot of books and is an Anobium Punctatum. He's the owner of the Fellowship of the Book LinkedIn Group.
