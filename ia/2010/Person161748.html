  • Director of Sales

    UDL/Mylan

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; MYL; Pharmaceuticals industry)

    April 1989 — July 2004 (15 years 4 months)

    Developed as well as managed both the internal and external sales team for UDL Laboratories, Inc. Established standard operating procedures for sales group including call cycles, bonus programs, new product launches, and wholesales stocking. Managed customer service and instituted UDL’s first CRM and telesales programs. Oversaw the integration of the burn and wound care line into UDL from a sister organization. Was lead project manager and liaison for the Clozapine Prescription Access System a unique program linking physicians, pharmacy, and patients via the internet to monitor drug safety (http://www.mylan-clozapine.com/).
