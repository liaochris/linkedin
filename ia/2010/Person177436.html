Ronn Schuman’s Summary

Mr. Schuman, CEO for CTYX.PK, has an extensive background in healthcare business administration and executive strategic planning. He currently serves as the CEO of Connectyx Technologies which is a healthcare technology company that has launched and inovative ePHM - electronic personal health manager, MedFlash - www.medflash.com, to the consumer market which specializes is health and wellness . He has served as Vice President of Business Development/Operations for Liberty Medical from 1997 to 1999, a $100M medical supply company, (PLMD) and was their Compliance Officer for two years. Mr. Schuman has owned and operated numerous HME companies over the last 25+years. He is a registered and licensed respiratory clinician and has a bachelor's degree in business from the University of Phoenix, AZ.
In 1987 Mr. Schuman created ACT, Accessible Care Together and ACT Software, which were the beginning models for one call seamless healthcare networks. The companies had over 50 subscribers throughout the NY - TRI State area. Some of Mr. Schuman's other areas of expertise are, team facilitation, medical billing, call center development, mail order fulfillment and management - process consulting.
