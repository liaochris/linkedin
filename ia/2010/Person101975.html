John Lester’s Summary

OVERVIEW:
John Lester is an expert in online communities and market development using social media and virtual worlds. His background includes neuroscience research, creating patient support communities at Massachusetts General Hospital, and developing online medical education tools at Harvard Medical School. Most recently, John worked at Linden Lab, the creators of the virtual world Second Life, where he led the development of the education market and evangelized the use of virtual worlds for distance learning, business collaboration, healthcare training, scientific simulation and entertainment. John is a thought leader on how minds and cultures adapt to new communication modalities, and he currently works to help organizations create and cultivate successful online communities using innovative technologies.

MY SKILLS:
-Expert at helping organizations cultivate innovation in social networks, virtual worlds, online communities, business and education.
-Deep domain expertise in education, academia, healthcare and neuroscience.
-Excellent communicator (written word and public speaking).
-Excellent connector and networker.
-Expert in online communities across wide range of platforms.
-Accomplished evangelist and customer advocate, acquiring high-value customers and cultivating their success.
-Thought leader in developing virtual worlds for collaboration and learning.

WHAT I'M LOOKING TO DO NEXT:
I'm currently a free agent looking for new strategic and leadership opportunities. I want to help innovative organizations develop successful strategies for capturing and leveraging key markets in virtual worlds and online communities.
