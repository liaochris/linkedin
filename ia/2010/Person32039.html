Hank Rennar CPC {LION}’s Summary

With over 20 yrs experience in Executive Recruiting and Career Advisory Services, Hank Rennar leads a solid team of recruiters, HR and Career Advisors. The team at Career Advocates Int'l provide key impact players for client companies as well as HR training and Corporate Relocation sevices. Career Advocates specializes in difficult searches for strategic "play makers" and established leaders in various industries including IT, Finance, Energy and Engineering. Our clients are growing Corporations in the US, Canada and the UK with a common goal....."to successfully take their company to the next level" Our objective is to help our clients identify, recruit, acquire and retain the best talent available in order that they may successfully reach their goals. In short, our goal is your success!
