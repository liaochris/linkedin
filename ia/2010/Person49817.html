Dave Reinhart’s Summary

Since 1989 Sports Group International (SGI) has specialized in executive search assignments for sporting goods companies, placing middle and senior levels throughout all product sectors of sporting goods as well as "soup to nuts" Career Advising services for industry and non-industry candidates.

Our recruiting expertise is in sales, marketing, product design, product development, general management, supply chain, sourcing and retail management.

SGI's client base includes companies involved in footwear, hard goods, performance apparel, cycling, triathlons, golf, accessories, licensed products, team sports equipment, fishing, hunting, camping, ski, optical, action sports, retail and sports marketing.

Our Career Adviser services range from an assessment of a candidate's career background & objectives through a written questionnaire to assistance in targeting specific companies, tips for electronic resumes, how to effectively "cold call" hiring managers, interview preparation, navigating slowdowns in the hiring process and negotiating the offer.

Dave is a Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC) earning this distinction through the National Association of Personnel Services and a former national class distance runner, including 4:02 mile and 2:23:20 marathon bests. He can be reached directly at 413-587-0022 or e-mail at dkreinhart@aol.com.
