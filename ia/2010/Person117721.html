Kelli King-Jackson’s Summary

Kelli King-Jackson is an innovative national non-profit strategist, consultant and public speaker with expertise in healthcare for children and youth leadership. She brings more than 10 years of experience developing community partnerships between schools, nonprofits and businesses. Currently serving as director of outreach for Children’s Defense Fund Texas (CDF), Kelli has brought passion and accomplishment to this state by leading an award winning partnership that has assisted families of more than 35,000 uninsured children with health insurance.

Kelli has delivered presentations on successful outreach and partnership strategies to national, state, and local audiences. She has presented to audiences at Families USA, Texas Juvenile Probation Commission and Texas Association of School Administrator’s conferences. In 2009, the America’s Promise Alliance profiled Kelli’s leadership in outreach to uninsured children in Texas on their website.

Kelli believes that “a healthy community includes responsive, strategic partnerships between individuals, businesses and schools. No one group can create productive citizens alone.” With this end in mind, Kelli has made it her life’s work to help groups develop innovative outreach and partnership models.

Kelli currently serves on the board of directors for Leadership Houston, Waymaker Life Strategies and for the National Policy Committee of the Community Developers Program of the General Board of Global Ministries (United Methodist Church).

Kelli is also the founder of Verge Consulting Group, LLC (VCG). The mission of VCG is to build the capacity of nonprofit organizations through program design, proposal writing and partnership development. VCG works with organizations looking to incorporate strong community programming into their strategic plans. Kelli aims to help organizations on the verge of greatness to increase effectiveness and service to their communities.
