Steve Anderson (LION)’s Experience

  • CEO and co-founder

    SPARAK Financial Systems

    (Computer Software industry)

    February 1984 — September 2002 (18 years 8 months)

    I formed SPARAK Financial Systems in 1984 with a co-worker from NCR. We created core accounting software for community banks. We built the business to over 250 banks in 34 states before selling the company to Harland Financial Solutions in 2002. I stayed with Harland for a couple of years as SVP & GM to establish the Core Systems Group.

  • Systems Analyst

    June 1981 — February 1984 (2 years 9 months)
