Cosmin Ghiurau’s Summary

Since 1994 I am immersed in all things digital.

Striving to be a dynamic leader combining digital marketing, strategy, consulting, and program management skills.

My professional career spans a number of industries. I've worked within various automotive design studios, including the General Motors Creative Styling Studios. In 1994, I turned my focus to the world of interactive and digital marketing. My skills were immediately demonstrated in the successful launch and growth of a former web development company, Jumpeye Creative Media.

That entrepreneurial skill set became a successful attribute to apply to the digital agency world.

Currently I am leading the Social Media strategy efforts for Samsung Mobile.

I obtained my Bachelors in Fine Arts degree with an emphasis on Product Design from the College of Creative Studies in Detroit, Michigan. Today, I continue a strong personal commitment to self-education on emerging technologies and digital strategy.
