Christian Vetter’s Summary

Accomplished consultant with 10 years of combined experience - highlights of my extensive experience include:

•Project Management - Finished national and international projects in a variety of areas in real estate and land use economics
•Economic Impact - Evaluated the economic and fiscal benefits created by the construction of a variety of projects
•Partner Solicitation - Preparation of the RFP Book, identification of RFP recipients, the RFP distribution and assistance to submitters, evaluation of developer submittals.
•Brownfield Redevelopments - Conducted market analysis and identified financial performances of proposed development elements and the overall economic and fiscal impacts
•Mixed-Use Developments - Evaluation of demand and supply as well as trends in residential, office and retail markets to identify target markets, estimation of the construction cost, absorption rates and financial performance.
•Corporate Attractions - Analyzed national and international corporate attractions and museums in terms of visitation, entertainment, experience and branding.
•Resort, Theme & Waterpark Development - Provided a market analysis, including demographics, behavioral patterns, defining the geographic trade areas, developing activity participation and market penetration,
•Public Private Partnerships - Assisted in bond hearings, contacting, soliciting and selecting private developers, investors and managers and in negotiations to establish successful long-term agreements
•Economic Revitalization - Defined implementable strategies to help offset economic losses through future redevelopment in a realistic manner, in which the reuse activities are sensible for the community by analyzing the site, local economy, infrastructure and demographics, ongoing economic programs and surrounding uses
•Gaming - Analyzed available information regarding proposed projects, including field analysis and research and related experience with gaming facilities and programs.
