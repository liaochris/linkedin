Daniel East’s Summary

In addition to his experience as a professional touring musician beginning at just 17 years of age after graduating from Philadelphia High School for The Creative & Performing Arts, East also has four years experience as Regional Manager for a One-Stop music distributor; two years with A&M Records marketing; one year with RCA Records marketing; eight years of concert touring in the U.S. and Europe in various capacities with a variety of national and international artists; Production Manager; four years artist management for national recording artists including all aspects of touring, promotion, motion picture appearances, broadway and international radio promotion; founder and president of Size Thirteen Music (including consulting firm, record label, production company, and marketing group): ten years as consultant for Future Sonics Incorporated that included on-site production training, international promotions, artist relations, product design and events coordinator prior to becoming Director of Marketing & Artist Relations. Today, East is the President and CEO of a new class of creative marketing consultants at The ECI Group.

Daniel East has also been a featured in the media and at conferences/events including: ABC, CBS, NBC, HBO, MTV, AES, NAMM, Macworld Expo, InfoComm, Apple User Group University, McGraw-Hill publications, radio, podcasts, major newspapers, trade publications, and others.

Daniel East’s Specialties:

Image/brand awareness, market perceptions, business models/planning, strategic programs, promotion design, performance coaching, media coaching, audio engineering, addressing the entire scope of each production and product to create the best possible results appropriate to the situation for each client.
