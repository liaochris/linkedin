Justin Barton’s Summary

Justin Barton leads the creative strategy, design and development of websites and interactive content for Nowspeed, Inc. He has directed Internet marketing and branding programs for over nine years, creating websites, eNewsletters, blogs, flash animations, advertising, sales collateral, direct mail and much more. He’s an accomplished writer and designer with extensive experience in design software, Flash animation, CMS development and CRM system integration.

Justin Barton’s Specialties:

1) Team Leadership: Managing creative teams that execute results-focused interactive campaigns

2) Thought Leadership: The ability to articulate the trends shaping the Social Web

3) Art Direction: Improving usability and engagement in interactive design

4) Branding: The creation of dominant selling ideas that profoundly influence purchase decisions

5) Technical Execution: Expert in designing with Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator and Flash, and hand-coding HTML, XHTML and CSS
