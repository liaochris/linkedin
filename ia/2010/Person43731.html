Courtney Walsh’s Summary

With almost a decade of public relations and journalism experience, Courtney is a seasoned marketing communication professional. Courtney has both corporate and agency public relations experience for consumer and business to business companies. Prior to public relations, Courtney served as a journalist for local and national TV and Radio news outlets. Her ability to craft newsworthy stories to help position companies as leaders of their industry and executives as experts in their respective fields has allowed her to establish strong working relationships with reporters and editors at top-tier business and news publications. Courtney has placed impact stories for her clients in every news medium including print, web, television and radio.

Courtney has a deep understanding of social media best practices helping companies build an online presence driving thought-leadership and expertise. She has secured keynote and panelist speaker slots for executives at some of the most recognized tradeshows and conferences in the nation. Courtney is detail and deadline-oriented with a proven track record for meeting and surpassing goals.
