Ed Lisogar’s Summary

Ed has been investing in real estate for his own portfolio for twenty one years, having bought and sold millions of dollars of residential and commercial real estate. His personal real estate portfolio covers three different countries.

He is well regarded as one of the leading experts in secondary market acquisitions, is the author of three best sellers on generating cash flow through paper investment and has been nominated three seperate times as "Arizona Entrepreneur of the Year" by USA Today Newspapers and the accounting firm of Ernst and Young.

Ed is an international key note speaker and corporate trainer and has personally taught thousands of individuals in the United States and Canada how to generate wealth through real property and secondary market paper investing.

Ed and his wife Sheba live in Scottsdale, Arizona. When not travelling, they can typically be found beating the hell out of Arizona's beautiful golf courses.
