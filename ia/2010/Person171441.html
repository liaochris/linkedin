Michael Moody’s Summary

Revival Fitness Philosophy

Most personal training experiences provide a simple, fitness approach. The Revival Fitness philosophy adheres to a greater dedication beyond the rudimentary movements in a gym.

Essentially, I also teach you the knowledge and tools necessary to understand your physical and mental self. Most important, I help you recognize any psychological blocks (ie overeating when you didn't want to, want to run but watch tv instead, etc.) and physical limitations and appropriately adapt the changes needed to reach your goals.

I draw on an extensive background in psychology, education, and fitness to help you achieve this. Basically, I examine the patterns of your life-sleeping patterns, flunctuating weight, eating when stressed, etc-to help you find physical symmetry and mental/emotional balance.

With my guidance, you will not only achieve your goals, but also learn how to sustain weight loss and/or maintain your optimal health.
