Sean Zetts’s Summary

I am a recruiter ("headhunter") that recruits IT professionals for direct hire (permanent / full-time), contracting, & contract-to-hire employment positions for companies that range in size from the Fortune 500 to start-ups. As a "full desk recruiter", I work with hiring managers to understand requirements & then personally recruit candidates to fill positions.

Hiring Managers: Do you find yourself wasting time with resumes & interviews of candidates that are not a fit? I am networked with highly qualified IT professionals that are passive (not searching for a new job; will not see your job posting) but open to learning about opportunities I present that meet predetermined criteria. Are you only interested in candidates who happen to see your posting? Or are you also receptive to candidates that may be more qualified than the active marketplace?
