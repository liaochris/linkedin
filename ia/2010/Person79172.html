Gwen Orwiler’s Summary

My perfect customers are women who want to live their strongest lives. I believe that we are all here not to make "A" difference, but to make "THE" difference we are uniquely destined to make.

However, often as women, we are blinded by our FEARS, "SHOULD's", "Yes, but's..." and feelings of "not good enough" that we stay stuck living medicore lives vs the strong lives that we are meant to live. Are you living YOUR strongest life?

Contact me at gwen@discoveryourdharma.com with any questions or comments or to schedule your FREE 30 Minute Consultation. Let's talk and see how I can help you to get unstuck!
