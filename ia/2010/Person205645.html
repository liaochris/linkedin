Donna Serdula’s Summary

I love technology! I love the Internet! And I love helping people find ways to use the Internet to make money and deliver value. Working in sales and marketing over the past 10 years, I learned how valuable the Internet is in forging relationships and creating value. I learned that cold calling and brochure distribution isn't the only way to find business. Blogging, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and other social media tools helped me grow my business and market my products and services. It enhanced my reputation and most importantly, I was able to use social media to attract new and repeat business.

I started DonnaTechDesigns to share what I learned and help you make efficient and effective use of all the tools in the Social media space.

My focus is on helping businesses, executives, and professionals enhance their visibility and attract more business through social networking and internet marketing activities.

Let's create your Social Media strategy together and let me show you how you can make more money and better market your product and services through this amazing technology!
