Kathryn Hawkins’s Summary

I am a writer/editor and marketing consultant experienced in both print and online media, with a strong background in social media and content marketing. With my husband Jeff, I am co-founder of Hawkins Multimedia, which provides premium web design and content services to a variety of clients.

Kathryn Hawkins’s Experience

  • Editor/Owner, Gimundo.com

    Gimundo

    (Online Media industry)

    August 2007 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Gimundo.com, a website and daily newsletter dedicated to good news, launched in 2007 under a venture-funded web startup based in Los Angeles. I began working for the site as a freelance writer upon its June launch, and was promoted to editor in August. Since then, in addition to writing new articles each day, I have used content partnerships and social media to build an audience exceeding 100,000 unique viewers a month, and have received links and mentions from news sources including the Washington Post and the Huffington Post. I also managed a content licensing arrangement with Maxwell House's "Brew Some Good" advertising campaign.

    In May 2009, my husband Jeff and I purchased the site, and have redesigned and relaunched it independently. We currently oversee design, development, editorial, marketing, and all other aspects of the business.

  • Owner/Editorial Director

    September 2006 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Hawkins Multimedia LLC is a small business based in the Portland, Maine area. We specialize in providing high-quality design, development, marketing, content, and social media consulting for companies and nonprofits. In April, we acquired, re-designed, and re-launched Gimundo.com, a website and daily e-newsletter dedicated to good news and positive stories, which reaches over 100,000 unique readers each month.
