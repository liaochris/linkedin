Bob Berstein’s Summary

12 years of investment banking and venture capital experience. Technology investment banker at Revolution Partners, a division of Morgan Keegan. Prior affiliations: Needham & Co., Jefferies Broadview, Merrill Lynch, BMO Capital Markets, Advanta Partners L.P. Education: Cornell University, BA, magna cum laude. Co-founder, former Chairman, New York Private Equity Network (NYPEN).

Bob Berstein’s Specialties:

Finance and M&A deal execution, both private and public, and corporate/business development. Specialization in Software, Services, Digital Media, IT.
