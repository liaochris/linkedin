Sarah Murphy-Katz’s Summary

Through December 2009 was a full-time MBA and MS in Television Management graduate student at Boston University's School of Management and College of Communication where program focused on writing, development, creative producing, and programming. Currently pursuing a career in these disciplines.

This summer was awarded the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences' only internship in Programming and placed at ABC Family in Original Programming. Won the Summer 2008 NBC Universal Intern Pilot Competition with our original pilot 'Start-Up.'

Prior to graduate school, worked for the Boston Red Sox in client relations roles for four years (during which time the team won two World Championships), in addition to working professionally in theatre and non-profit.
