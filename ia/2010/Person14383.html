Ann Frederick’s Summary

Fascial Stretch Therapy™:

The cutting edge service I developed with my husband Chris Frederick, that markedly transforms individuals, families, companies and sports teams by helping them achieve a higher quality of life and performance.

In 1997, defended her master's thesis and established that my proprietary stretching technique (Fascial Stretch Therapy™) outperformed conventional methods with lasting flexibility gains of 36 to 52 percent. Today, professional athletes and Olympians from all over the world use this system to achieve higher levels of performance.

Stretch To Win Institute:

Is where manual therapy and movement therapy professionals worldwide get hands-on, intensive training to become a Certified Fascial Stretch Therapist using our proprietary Fascial Stretch Therapy techniques.

Stretch To Win Center:

This is our clinic in Tempe, AZ where we work with our "everyday athlete" clients, as well as our professional athletes from the NFL, MLB, Olympic athletes and more to achieve optimal flexibility, strength, performance and pain relief using our non-invasive, unique FST™ techniques.
