Donna Bellafiore’s Summary

I have been a therapist for over 25 years and so far I haven't burned out. I have to say, I love what I do!
As an author on infidelity issues, I have turned down offers to appear on "Oprah" and other talk shows. Some of my colleagues and friends think this was a foolish decision because I could have sold more books, received more exposure and have gotten more popular. Honestly, I was flattered and excited to receive the phone calls. Perhaps from a marketing point of view my decision was foolish and maybe I lost the opportunity of claiming my fame. The belief that my client's healing would be compromised if they appeared on national television as my guest prevented me from accepting their invitations.
My goal is to help my clients reduce their pain and suffering, adjust to lifes unexpected grief, trauma and circumstances, and help them see the power within themselves to make changes when they can.
Another goal is to be successful and satisfied with what I do, and how I conduct my business. I strive for opportunites and see the possibilities to create and execute my ideas for making it a better, kinder, world.
