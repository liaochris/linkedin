Bill Walton’s Summary

I am an accomplished Industrial products Marketing and Sales executive who is an enthusiastic, strategic visionary that is able to drive change. My expertise is building strategic alternatives for growth. My passion is corporate development.

What you might find unique about me is that I have spent 17 years in sales and the last 12 years in marketing. I understand how both functions work. I am a sales and marketing professional who knows the difference between "upstream" and "downstream" marketing and who has made a "cold” call or two. I have implemented and driven processes in strategic planning, new product development, sales and operations planning and corporate development; and led successful sales teams.

Today, I am in pursuit of an opportunity where I can drive strategic growth either by combining my sales and marketing backgrounds or utilizing my marketing skills to lead a marketing function while engaged in corporate development.

I have a history of manufacturing success for both my employer and myself. I have never left a job or a company without making it better than I found it.

Available for short term consulting opportunities.
