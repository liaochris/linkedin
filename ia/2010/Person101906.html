John Johnson’s Summary

I enjoy working out innovative strategies to bring worthwhile drugs to market. Among the techniques I use are adaptive trials, Bayesian statistics, computer graphics, and alternative Phase 2 design strategies. I author statistical content for regulatory submissions and appear on behalf of clients at FDA meetings when statistics is to be discussed. I mentor, advise, and supervise other statisticians in statistical planning and production of datasets, tables, listings, and figures.
