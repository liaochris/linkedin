Mark Toney’s Summary

SmithGeiger is a custom media research and strategy firm, headquartered in Los Angeles, serving all facets of the media industry. We are evangelists in the areas of Online video, Broadcast Networks, Local Stations, Cable Networks, Major Studios, Online Networks, Broadband Video Companies, Gaming Developers and Online Publishers.

Our unique research methodologies uncover consumer attitudes and preferences and leverages this insight to drive strategic and dramatic transformation in media companies.

SmithGeiger works with a variety of online companies to provide a clear sense of how consumption patterns vary in relation to key content elements, daypart differences, purposive and casual consumption, and levels of engagement including video as a social experience. Identifying the key touch points, drivers, needs, and current navigational paths across these different video user segments become an integral part of our overall approach.

We have a deep history in helping large and small online clients succeed including past engagements with Yahoo!, AOL, Weather.com, ABC.com, Real Networks, Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, NBC Universal, and Warner Bros., to name a few.
