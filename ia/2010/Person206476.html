Tom Shipley’s Summary

After over 20 years in Sales, Brand Management, and Interactive Marketing for the worlds largest beer company, followed by a senior leadership role with an independent marketing agency, Tom Shipley founded Cole-Dalton, LLC in 2010.

C-D provides clients with insight gained from years of "traditional" sales and marketing coupled with immersion in the continuing evolution of "digital" marketing.

Shipley began his career in 1989 at Anheuser-Busch and held an escalating variety of positions in sales and marketing.

In 2005, he was tapped to create and implement the “Here’s To Beer” campaign, a non-branded industry effort designed to elevate and enhance the image of beer.

In 2007 Tom was named the Senior Director of Budweiser Marketing. He led marketing tactics for Budweiser and Budweiser American Ale that included traditional TV, print, outdoor and radio advertising. He also evolved the brand's message delivery into the digital space through Budweiser.com, BudAmericanAle.com as well as integrations with digital media like ESPN.com and Facebook.

In 2008, he was promoted to Senior Director Digital Media and Marketing. In this role Tom was responsible for delivering cutting edge strategy and digital integration for all of the companies brands including Budweiser, Bud Light, Michelob, Beck's and Stella Artois.

After Anheuser-Busch, Shipley joined the Brighton Agency as Vice-President Interactive Marketing where he led a team of interactive strategists, web designers, back-end developers, and social media specialists.

Shipley's interactive team at Brighton served clients such as Monsanto, Scottrade, P&G, and Furminator. Shipley led Brighton to the Radian6 Social Media platform and continued their leadership as an Exact Target Silver Tier Reseller.

Tom is a graduate of the University of Dayton with a bachelor’s degree in American Studies. He resides in St. Louis with his wife, Rachelle, and their two children
