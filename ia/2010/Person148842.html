Morgan Lindqvist’s Summary

I have been working with multimedia and standardisation of multimedia for over 10 years. I started out in MPEG participating in the standardisation of MPEG-4 AAC.

Since 2001 I am involved in Bluetooth SIG and is currently chairing the Audio Video WG. The working group is responsible for standardisation of audio and video streaming over Bluetooth as well as remote control of media over Bluetooth.

Morgan Lindqvist’s Specialties:

Project management, Speech and audio coding, Bluetooth architecture and profiles.
