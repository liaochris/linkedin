Lee Andrese, CUA’s Summary

For over 20 years I've focused my efforts on improving the image of the staffing industry by delivering exceptionally high levels of customer service and results to internal and external company clients and Talent. I've held positions from agent to manager of a $60M region, and most recently transitioned from the role as head of Aquent's Learning Services team.

As of 2010 my new role at Aquent is User Experience Agent, with the responsibility of growing Talent portfolios and their earning potential by solving typically complex User Experience issues across all industries for companies in DC, NYC and Boston.

Previously, as the head of Learning Services and working with the senior management team, I led the development and launch of the Peer, Mentor and Employee Learning Fund programs. Working with the CIO, IT project managers and content development experts, led the introduction of Google Apps and Video Conferencing technology to the organization.

- Reduced LMS spend by 35%, while increasing virtual training capacity
- Reduced training travel costs by 40% through the use of video conferencing and virtual classrooms
- Introduced Power Messaging to the sales organization
- Led and developed the initial process and managed the vendor relationship to conduct NetPromoter customer satisfaction surveys
- Coach and mentor new team leads, sales and sales management
- Maintained learning and development support given a 70% reduction in staff

As a published writer for the trucking industry, I created and continue to write Driven Women, a monthly column in Over the Road, ProTrucker and Driver Health publications.
