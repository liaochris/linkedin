Eric Dix’s Summary

Eric Dix is a Six Sigma Master Black Belt with 15 years experience in Lithium Ion batteries and 25+ years in all aspects of Coatings and Converting technologies, Chemical Formulation, Statistical Process Control, Design of Experiments and Reliability Engineering. Eric's experience spans from Research Engineer to the Board of Directors with over 35 successful new technology product introductions.

Dix has developed Advanced Engineering Methods specifically adapted to Electrochemical Systems, including Lithium Ion / Advanced Battery Systems and Roll to Roll Coatings. Specialized Courses for Engineering, Scientific and Operations staff have been developed for these sectors and are only available at Innovation Associates, LLC.

Visit our website for more detail at www.innoassoc.com.
