Dan Fisher’s Summary

Some days my client is looking to tell a riveting story, while at other times the image is "king." Each project demands its own set of rules and requires a new approach. This is what makes the craft of editing so challenging... so exciting. The post production process begins long before the first frame is edited; in the mind of the Producer, Director, and Editor. In the edit room we expose reality, improve reality, and even create reality. It is an exhilarating process that is not for the faint at heart.
