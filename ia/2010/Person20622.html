Baxter Box’s Summary

After graduating from the University of San Diego with an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering and Mathematics ( B.A./B.S.E.E.) I worked in research and developement for Respiration Technologies in San Diego, Ca. As a R&D Engineer for Restech I designed Dx-pH Sleep Adapter which allows connectivity between the Dx-pH Measurement System and PSG (Polysomnography) sleep lab machines. Additionally, I used my antenna RF design expertise to optimize the telemetry of the wireless Dx-pH Measurement System.

In November 2007 I was hired as a Research Analyst/ Equities Trader for Bonanza Capital's Management Program in Dallas, Tx. I assumed the head trader position in June 2008, handling $20MM in monthly equity and option trades.

In May 2008, I founded NastyGoat Corporation, a website investment group, developing creative and innovative solutions to capitalize on online market opportunities. As President and Chairman I oversee site development, research, marketing, finances, and external investments. N.G.'s first product, SurfReel.com, is the first site dedicated to connecting surfers around the world with each other and the surf industry. Our second product, AmberVenz.com, was launched August 2009. AmberVenz.com was developed in partnership entrepreneur Amber Venz to sell high-end jewelry.

Currently, I'm a full time MBA student at the Cox School of Business (SMU) studying Finance and continue to manage NastyGoat Corporation.
