Juli Testa’s Summary

I am an MBA Graduate from NSU and have lived in Florida my entire life! I have obtained my CSAM Certification, and I am currently working as the VP of Advertising and Media Recruitment for our Plantation Office. My team has been a blessing to me, and they have really transformed our goals. We have become experts in the media, account services, account planning, SEO/SEM, creative services, trafficking, and advertising operations. We handle both permanent and temporary staffing solutions, and we work on a contingency basis. Our team has a streamlined process that we follow and we work as true head hunters. We actively source our candidates and work on a great referral system in order to present Impact Players in the industry to our clients. Every client is an "A" list client here at Searchmax. We appreciate the business we have won, and we enjoy partnering with agencies to truly bring the best talent to them. If there is anything I can ever be of some assistance with, please feel free to reach out to me. If you have referrals of candidates that you think would be a good match for any of the openings we have, if you refer them to me, I will send you a great check in the mail simply for supplying a name! Our goal is to make a difference in the lives of our candidates and to provide top talent to our client companies in order for them to continue to be the most successful in the business. If you are a potential client and need some assistance with any of your recruiting efforts, I would love to help. I like to think I am a strong communicator and a strong relationship builder, and my promise to you is I will work as hard as I can to fill the position. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit more about myself, Searchmax, and my team. We pride ourselves on being the best in advertising recruitment, and I hope we hold up to that.
