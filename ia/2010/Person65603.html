Erica Zucco’s Summary

I'm a digital media producer/strategist and convergence journalist committed to finding and sharing new ways to "brighten up media."

I'm passionate about the potential new media has to make a difference on a global scale by telling stories, sharing information and facilitating conversation in more engaging, interactive ways. I explore and communicate ways through which media can function positively, and research and share ways journalists can report on controversial issues with sensitivity and understanding. I am interested in innovating new ways to gather, organize, produce and distribute journalistic, information and entertainment media and developing strategic social and digital marketing campaigns.

I believe that media can- and should- positively influence humanity. My goal is to find new ways we can use digital media, hopefully in part to achieve that effect. Want to work together? Shoot me an email at erica.zucco@gmail.com!
