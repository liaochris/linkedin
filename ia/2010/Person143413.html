Michael R. Neece’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur, Fortune 50 executive, published author and instrument rated pilot with expertise in business development & product development for both Fortune 50 and startup companies. Co-founder of 5 companies including Health Care, B2B, B2C and Software as a Service (SaaS) enterprises. Possessing a unique ability to articulate strategic vision and execute tactical level deliverables to build businesses that are highly scalable and sustainable.

Industry and market experience includes Health Care process optimization & automation, e-commerce, multiple segments of high-tech markets, government (state and national), and enterprise-class software.
