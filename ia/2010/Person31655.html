Camille Calichon’s Summary

Hello everyone, my name is Camille. I was born in France and raised on a Caribbean island named Saint Martin. I am currently a student of advertising at The Art Institute of California - San Francisco.

Advertising is my passion! I love the creative thinking process. To come up with a unique concept, and being able to demonstrate to the client that the idea is on strategy is what
I aim for.

I can see in my mind’s eye what I want, and that’s where I first do my work. I think having big ideas is admirable, but I find it also important to be able to make them happen. And to me, knowing that people are expecting great things motivates me to do well.
