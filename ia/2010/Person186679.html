Seth Kravitz’s Summary

I am very proud to be the CEO of InsuranceAgents.com and I welcome all agents to join our LinkedIn Group here http://www.linkedin.com/groupRegistration?gid=1821064

As far as how I got started, when I was 19 I launched Token20 Media, which was a small design company. After merging Token20 with Cyber Technology in 2004, I assumed the role of COO and began growing the company one client, one employee, and one month at a time.

The InsuranceAgents.com team now includes 40+ great employees and two offices (Columbus and Chicago). I am very proud of working with the amazing set of employees, contractors, and partners we are privileged to have.
