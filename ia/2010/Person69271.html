Federico Cascinelli’s Summary

my name is Federico and I’ve been working in the Industry for the last ten years.
During my career I’ve travelled a lot (Italy, UK, Canada, Spain, Belgium) having the chance to work in different companies and production environments.
I started in my home country in 1997 as 3D generalist and technical support for a software house, where I learned not only the basics of 3D but also all the aspects of the production and the day by day problems our clients were involved in.
Essential for that position was being smart and fast trying to help them finding the way to go back in track in no time.
Strong communication skills as well as the ability to communicate clearly with people under pressure was a must together with a solid problem solving ability.
Then I moved to UK where I spent three years as animator working on Academy Awards and VES nominee projects such as “Charlie and the Chocolate Factory”, “Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire” and “X MEN 3”.
After London I moved to Canada, as animator and previz artist, working on “The Day The Earth Stood Still”.
In the last few years I continued to travel in different countries acquiring both further experiences and skills.
I finally spent the last couple of years in Italy working as well as animator, project manager and head of CG for smaller companies having the chance to develop new skills as supervisor and training young artists.

Most recently this year (2009) I updated my skills with Pixologic ZBrush and Side Effects Houdini.

Federico Cascinelli’s Specialties:

Ability to work to tight deadlines and integrate quickly with an existing team, as well as successfully communicating complex technical information to non-technically minded people.

I have a solid knowledge, understanding and interest in classic animation principles & fundamentals, problem solver and experience at troubleshooting technical problems and creating solutions for 3D animation. Ability to show a strong visual style and excellent eye for detail.
