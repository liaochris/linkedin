Gasher-Brum Energy Partners is a recruiting, staffing and consulting firm that specializes in the Oil and Gas industry. We are a full service, fully integrated firm providing strategies and personnel for every facet of the Oil and Gas and Energy Industries. Whether a company needs recruiting or staffing services, a single consultant, a team of specialists or outsourcing entire departments, Gasher-Brum Energy Partners provides experienced industry professionals and innovative out of the box solutions that create exceptional value to our clients.

Our knowledge and understanding of the oil and gas and energy business along with a strong ability to listen comprehend and understand the needs of both our client companies and candidates Gasher-Brum Energy Partners has grown into one of the premier oil and gas recruiting firms in the United States.

With over 30 years combined industry recruiting experience, our staff consists of seasoned oil and gas professionals who utilize their years of experience and vast network to bring the most qualified contract, permanent and consulting experts to fill our clients personnel requirements.

Gasher-Brum EP is pleased that our clients work with us repeatedly. The talent our clients choose from is carefully evaluated and thoroughly investigated for performance, character, personality and work ethic traits, along with the usual skill-set criteria, saving you both time and money. We use state of the art technology and have a constantly updated database consisting of experienced and trained professionals that include both national and international contacts.
