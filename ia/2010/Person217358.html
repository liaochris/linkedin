Mark Sinclair (tomark@ybc.tv)’s Summary

The great thing about this role is that I get to interview and connect with such interesting people and find out great things from experts who are quite simply brilliant at what they do.

Recent interviews have included Google, Skype, Deloitte, Microsoft, PricewaterhouseCoopers, networking Guru Dr Ivan Misner, viral marketing guru David M Scott, Kevin Roberts - Global CEO of Saatchi and Saatchi and NYSE record breaking entrepreneur Bob Young.

We've also pulled together a number of ground-breaking collaborations involving talented and successful business people from around the globe, focusing on solving some of the biggest problems in business. The latest is the Million Dollar Challenge, which offers selected businesses the opportunity to work with experts from yBC and get access to proven campaigns and tools valued at over US$1M, on a no-risk basis.

It doesn't get much better than that!
