  • Policy Advisory Board Member

    dotMobi

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    The Policy Advisory Board (PAB) provides a vital link between mTLD and the dotMobi Advisory Group (MAG). The PAB is a sounding board for mTLD and it apprises the company of any wider public policy issues which may have an impact on dotMobi's operations. The PAB develops policy proposals for consideration by stakeholders and/or mTLD and consults with interested parties, including the MAG, where appropriate. The ten elected members represent a broad spectrum of the mobile, Internet and user communities.
