James Charboneau’s Summary

Jim Charboneau is a respected and accomplished solution architect, project manager and industrial maintenance excellence professional.

Jim is a proven relationship builder and consultative seller. Having solid business acumen and expertise in enterprise solution architecture, information technology and capital project management, Jim brings a good balance of leadership and technical expertise to the table.

A results driven, highly energetic professional with 30 years of combined systems integration, business consulting and project management experience. Recognized for thought-leadership in the area of system-knowledge worker interfaces to mission critical systems with a strong focus on master data management and solution ergonomics. Finds himself on the soap-box often evangelizing the need to master enterprise data structures resulting in pristine transactional data as a foundation for trusted business analytics and high-integrity decision automation.

Brings a shop-floor orientation to the challenges of asset-intensive and high-risk capital project rich environments - in the areas of governance, risk and compliance. Has consulted to heavy equipment industries - aviation, marine, railways, multi-modal public transportation, mining, oil/gas and government sectors, and has played a key role on numerous engagements for positioning, selling and delivering mission critical business solutions.

Possesses deep business process experience and an ability to leverage people, processes and technology to achieve a client’s desired business outcomes. Has excellent communication skills and is a team player, with a focus on relationship development and a proven ability to lead complex multi-project initiatives. Keeps the batteries charged by cycling, hockey, weight training.

Finds moments for community service in the areas of advocacy for children’s health, education and prosperity. Active in philanthropy for cancer and special children and adults.
