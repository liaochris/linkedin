Andres Botero’s Summary

Before coming to Babson for my MBA, I worked for six years with one of the leading consumer products companies in Colombia. During this experience, I was able to understand the consumer behavior through several marketing research projects, and at the same time, to understand our buyer when I was the distribution channel manager. This helped me to create new strategies the brand I worked for.

As the Brand Manager for Yamaha Colombia and also the Trade Marketing Manager for the Diaper Division of a large consumer products company, I understood that is not only about finding and covering the client’s needs. It is a really interesting mix of dealing with distribution channels, distributors, wholesalers, and many store owners. This is a world where you have to be aware for the competitor’s moves, price changes and marketing strategies to attract the customers using some tools as Nielsen, market research and client’s visiting.

This experience gave me an important chance to put into practice what I had learned in my business and marketing studies, where I understood the importance of searching out new market opportunities for existing products. Also I have a lot of experience not only building a marketing plan, but also executing and controlling it to be sure to have outstanding results.
