Sporty King’s Summary

Sporty King is a Conference Speaker who works with people who want to help others feel good about themselves and their contribution to the bottom. An expert on Life, Leadership and Literacy... he uses his gift of manipulating the English language to deliver positive words and thoughts to help people feel good, believing...
• that winning is personal (W.I.N. - What I Need). As we each understand our personal win we become a more valued part of any team.
• you have to be U.G.L.Y. (Unique, Gifted, Lovable You). There's something about you that no other person has that adds your piece to this puzzle of our existence.
• you have to enjoy this roller coaster of L.I.F.E. (Lessons Intended For Everyone).
• that we hear the same messages over and over... only the messenger changes... and we don't get the message until our messenger delivers it.

Imagine sharing that message with your students, parents, sales staff, conference, and meeting attendees. Sporty has had over a quarter of a century to practice and perfect practical proactive leadership skills that you can use to energize and remind…
• your students to achieve academic superiority, and become productive members of society by staying in school and developing the skills & character necessary to be successful
• your parents of the key role they play in giving children direction
• your salespeople to increase business by competing with themselves
• your members to enhance their interpersonal and communication skills for personal and professional growth
• your team to have confidence… they are good, not lucky
• your staff that Customer Service pronounced slowly states "Customers Serve Us"… thus it behooves us to also treat one another as valued customers.

Jack of all Trades… Master of None? Not really!!!
Which life would you deem unimportant???
