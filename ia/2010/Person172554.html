Richard Simpson’s Summary

Tayburn is a strategic brand design consultancy. I'm responsible for developing and implementing the group's marketing and business development strategy across our UK businesses.

Tayburn has four core design specialisms: corporate identity and branding, consumer brand packaging, corporate reporting and digital communications.

Tayburn focuses on core sectors that include:

FMCG
Financial Services
Education
Destination and leisure
Health and wellbeing
Utilities
Professional Services

Part of my remit includes the development of new 'products' for the consultancy in areas like corporate responsibility, internal communications and digital communications.
