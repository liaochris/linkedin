Stephen Todd’s Summary

Stephen specializes in working with facilities to reduce operating expenses and green house gas emissions. He has over 20 years experience with identifying and selling investment grade opportunities to C-level decision makers to achieve double-digit reductions in energy consumption. As a frequent speaker on sustainable (green) building operations and no/low-cost strategies to reduce utility expenses in existing buildings he welcomes opportunities to share his passion for these topics.

He holds a MBA in finance from the University of St. Thomas and a BA from the University of Wisconsin in Madison. He is also a Certified Energy Manger (CEM) and a GSGBC LEED Accredited Professional (LEED AP).
