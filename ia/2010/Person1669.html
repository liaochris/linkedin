Ab Kuijer’s Summary

Yes, it is true: the advertising world has changed forever. Traditional communication strategies deliver insufficient ROI, the established media is desperately trying to stop the declining advertising revenues, while consumers are increasingly avoiding advertising. They see advertising more and more as entertainment without a strong call to action for the brand. What will you do differently this year to retain your customers and find new ones?

Investing more money in more media is not the solution. Knowledge about social media and use of innovation is. JuniorSenior can help your company with workshops on social media, train your employees and redefine your message in new communication channels. The original story of your company told in a creative two-way dialogue provides authenticity and transparency. Those are the key words for success. JuniorSenior teaches your employees where the digital dialogue takes place and helps you to build your virtual reputation. And that is important, because consumers see and read your information, but will ask their friends for advice and then go online to find reviews written by others.

If you want to anticipate for the future, call me today at +31 (0) 20 788 26 60. It is time for a new strategy aimed at a new generation. Welcome at JuniorSenior!
