Trish McGarrell’s Summary

Our firm has 2 divisions. Our Staffing division places experienced professionals within the staffing industry nationwide. Commonly filled positions include District/Regional Managers, Branch Managers, Sales Reps, and Recruiters.

Our firm works in the following niches: Accounting and Finance, Administrative, Healthcare, Legal, Manufacturing, Light Industrial, Creative, IT and Engineering.

Our newest division focuses on professional staffing and we place candidates on a temporary, temp to hire, direct hire, and payrolled basis.

I have over 10 years experience in staffing and have experience placing a wide range of candidates from administrative, sales, management, call center, accounting, and technical. Because we are not a high volume candidate provider we can focus on quality placements and more attentive customer service.
