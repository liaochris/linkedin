Peter R. Johnson’s Summary

Peter has spent his career in retailing, financial services and technology. He began his career with IBM in Australia. He then joined The Myer Emporium Limited, a large retailer in Australia, as Corporate General Manager and CIO. In 1984, Peter moved to California and founded PRJ & Associates (PRJ&), an enterprise software company that specialized in merchandising and related financial systems for retailers. IBM invested in the Company in 1988 and used it as their primary channel in the retail industry worldwide. IBM's investment enabled PRJ& to establish (i) an international presence (ii) Credit Management Services, Inc. (CMS) expanding PRJ&’s software offerings to include private label credit and (iii) QuickResponse Services, Inc. (QRS), a leader in electronic commerce.
Peter was PRJ’s Chairman and CEO from its inception through the time it was sold in May 1993. Peter was also QRS’ Chairman and CEO from its inception in 1988 through 1993 and Chairman through May 2002. QRS went public in 1993 and was acquired by a private equity firm in 2004.
In addition, from 1994 to the present, Peter has been an investor in and a consultant to a number of technology companies including (i) NSB Retail Systems PLC, a publicly held company in the United Kingdom, where he served as the Chairman from 1995 - 1999, (ii) TRM, Inc. a software company specializing in labor scheduling where he served as the Chairman from 1995 until it was sold to ADP in 1998, (iii) Tradeweave, Inc. where he was the founder and Chairman and (iv) BlueArc Corporation where he was a member of the Board and the Audit and Nomination Committees.
From 2004 through May 2010 he was also a member of the Board and the Audit and Compensation Committees of Dillard’s Inc.
He is Chairman and CEO of PRJ Holdings, Inc. and Karum Group LLC (formerly CMS). Peter is also a member of the Board of The Wine Group, the San Francisco Opera and a member of The World President’s Organization (WPO).
