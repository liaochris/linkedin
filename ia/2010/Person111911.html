Kathleen A. Marron’s Summary

POWER * PURPOSE * PERSUASION
Kathleen is a catalyst igniting the power, purpose, and persuasion in leaders and their teams, especially in transition. As a certified executive coach & consultant, trial lawyer, speaker and mediator, she is respected for her work with executives, teams, lawyers and leaders seeking the next level of excellence in their companies and careers.

Kathleen equips leaders and their teams to devise creative solutions, build consensus, navigate transitions, and elicit their own pearls of wisdom and strategy to project them into their visions of the future. Drawing upon her over two decades of leadership, teambuilding and storytelling experience as a business trial lawyer, Kathleen brings her clients a fresh and insightful perspective (and sense of humor). Her passion is to equip leaders to communicate more persuasively, to lead more powerfully, together.

Most of Kathleen's professional activities have involved persuading, advising, teaching, team-building, strategic planning and mentoring. Savvy at connecting individual strengths and passions to business and career objectives, her services include:

Identifying individual & team Strengths, Values, Mission & Vision;
Facilitating cross-functional team Strategy and Culture;
Creating Visionary Practice Development Plans;
Enhancing Leadership Styles & Communication Skills;
Coaching and consulting on Trial Advocacy;
Equipping cross- functional teams to embrace diversity;
Advising individual leaders embarking on a sabbatical or career/practice area transition;
Planning for Change- half day workshops;
Renewing Teams in Changing Times- full day workshop;
Providing customized CLE and keynote speeches & seminars; and
Consulting on Trials, appeals and mediation.

Kathleen A. Marron’s Specialties:

Planning for Change ™ and Renewing Teams in Changing Times ™ workshops; executive/professional coaching; Leadership & Team Development; speaking on leadership, communications and diversity; Emotional Intelligence (EQ in Action); conflict management. Advisor, coach, mediator and consultant to senior partners, executives (C-Suite and midlevel), leadership teams, and corporate boards.
