  • Managing Director - Stone Carlie Corporate Finance, L.L.C.

    Stone Carlie

    (Privately Held; Accounting industry)

    2010 — Present (less than a year)

    Stone Carlie Corporate Finance, L.L.C. (SCCF) assists clients with recapitalizations, mergers and acquisitions and financial restructuring. SCCF is a subsidiary of Stone Carlie, a St. Louis based professional services firm which provides accounting, business consulting, outsourced CFO services and wealth management to corporate and individual clients.

    My focus at SCCF includes:

    Helping shareholders and management teams optimize the amount and cost of their capital through the proper mix of, for example, traditional bank debt, asset based lenders, mezzanine lenders and equity investors - There are funding options which may free up cash flow and cost less than current capital providers.

    Recapitalizing companies where a replacement is desired for the current bank lender, whether the company be healthy or distressed

    Assisting companies with financial restructuring:
    - Negotiating with current bank lenders where a company is in default or in the bank's workout group;
    - Raising the capital needed to support the management team's operational restructuring and turnaround - I work closely with the CEO,COO and CFO;
    - Finding and closing with the right strategic investor to strengthen the balance sheet;
    - Negotiating and closing a sale to a strategic or financial buyer;
    - Working to limit the liability of exisiting shareholders and maximize the value of their equity now and in the future.
