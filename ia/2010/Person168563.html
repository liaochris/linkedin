Rammohan Sundaram’s Summary

Ram launched Networkplay.in with a clear focus on audience segmentation using the vertical route as differentiation.

NetworkPlay raised venture funding from Capital 18.

Before this, as National Sales Head - Performance Accounts, for Yahoo! in India, he was responsible for driving revenues across Performance Display (Graphical), Yahoo Search Marketing (YSM) and Inside Sales Organization (ISO) and was based out of Gurgaon near New Delhi.

Ram has over 14 yrs of media sales, marketing & general mgmt experience in both traditional and new media having worked with organizations like Indian Express, Media2india (Microland Group Company), Encore Events, Jobsahead.com (Now a Monster Worldwide Company), Madison and Tribal DDB India before he joined Yahoo India where he was responsible for business of over 10 Million USD annually.

Before Yahoo India as National Sales Director of Tribal DDB, he built the media business for the agency from scratch having won many a accounts to make it the number one agency during his tenure with a capitalized billing of over 15M USD annually. Accounts that he led include Times Business Solutions, Honda Siel Cars, Yatra.com, HCL, Volkswagen India & Apollo DKV to name a few.

Ram in February 2010 won the Star Youth Achiever Of The Year & Innovative Start-Up Of The Year Awards, In 2006 won Several Golds @ Asia Pacific Advertising Festival & was also nominated to Cannes Cyber Lion Awards and in 04 won the Ideas Man Award @ Jobsahead.

He has been a speaker at many industry forums and was the voice of Yahoo India in the marketplace for all performance related product launches and initiatives including implementation of Right Media in India.

Ram has traveled extensively worldwide on business and understands cross cultural/functional/professional working styles to ensure smooth execution of global partnership initiatives.

Ram is a BE in Computers and an MBA in Marketing.

NP is the only passion that currently rides in Ram.
