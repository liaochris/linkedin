Jeff Berger’s Summary

JMB Communications, which I founded in 1990 and where I am currently CEO, is a results-focused marcom / PR / web content creation / website design agency serving clients worldwide with highly seasoned, effective counsel. We are a marketing company that does highly effective websites, not simply another "website design house." We cover everything from strategy to content creation to fully optimized design to program execution.

JMB offers the benefit 25+ years of experience helping Fortune 1000 and smaller clients worldwide get more business, more efficiently. See www.jmbcommunications.com for more on our PR / marcom capabilities and www.websitesthatwork.info for more on "websites that work." Our clients are high technology, financial services, travel/hospitality, electronics, and manufacturing companies for which we do both B2B and B2C communications.

We also own www.everythingstmaarten.com, the largest St. Maarten travel website.

Jeff Berger’s Specialties:

Newsletters, news releases, white papers, backgrounders, trade / general magazine articles, collateral, web content, editing, ghost-writing, direct response packages -- if it has to do with words that convince people to act, we do it -- for technology, financial, hospitality, and other B2B and B2C companies worldwide. Highly seasoned strategic counsel with on-the-money execution skills. See www.jmbcommunications.com for capabilities / samples and www.websitesthatwork.info for web creation info.
