Thanh-Nhon Ma’s Summary

I started my professional career in 1999 on the trading floor of The Bank of New York. Between 2001-2006, I gained more than 5 years of experience in the carrier & wholesale industry, holding managerial positions at Belgacom International Carrier Services, MCI WorldCom and iBasis. I have been responsible for a wide-array of projects in strategy, finance, quality management and product management. An avid tennis player, I trained at the Nick Bollettieri Tennis Academy boot camp in Florida in my youth. During my spare time, I still enjoys coaching and playing tennis at Harvard University. I was born in Belgium and speaks French, English and Dutch fluently. I studied at the Maastricht University and obtained my Masters in Economics from the University of Brussels (ULB). I am also a graduate in management of Harvard University.
