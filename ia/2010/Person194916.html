Steve Davies’s Experience

  • Managing Director and CEO

    Fitch Media

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Fitch Media develops and launches web based ventures applying proven techniques for utilising social communities to deliver superior customer engagement and business performance.

    The first offspring of this venture was Drivers Republic (drivers-republic.com) which was launched in May 2008 and financed by my own seed capital.

    Recruiting a team comprising the leading automotive journalists and designers in the UK, I also retained the services of renowned Adobe web agency Lightmaker, whose clients include M. Night Shyamalan, J.K. Rowling and Man Utd, to build a web platform enabling brands to reach users in our bespoke social community. The website was awarded ‘Best in Class 2008’ by the Interactive Media Awards™ for its design and innovative use of technology, and subsequently featured by Orange in their business magazine (Exchange).

    The site was implemented within a business model that valued the engagement of site visitors rewarding them for their contribution and viral behaviour, this engagement was then monetised with brands, advertising and PR agencies.

    During the first 12 months of operation the website attracted over 100,000 visitors and 2 million page views per month, making it the fastest growing automotive site in the UK and we launched strategic partnerships with Sony Entertainment (SCEE), The Times Newspapers (News International), Silverstone Circuits and Porsche GB.

    Clients included Mercedes, Renault, Peugeot, BMW, Aston Martin, Ford, Volkswagen, Audi and Porsche. Agencies included; OMD, Mindshare, MediaCom, SmartMedia, Brilliant Media, John Brown Media, Hill & Knowlton, Performance PR.
