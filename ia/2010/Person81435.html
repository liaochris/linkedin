Helping Animal Shelters’s Summary

At a time when more and more animal rescue services are desperately needed, shelters are struggling. With demand rising and donations declining, the need for new sources of revenue for animal rescue is vital to your survival. That’s why Helping Animal Shelters was created — to introduce America’s animal rescue community to a proven, ongoing source of revenue based on providing the pet care and comfort that our beloved dogs and cats deserve.

Current economic conditions have created a barrier to raising funds through donations that is affecting shelters everywhere – we all know animal lovers who would love to give, but have had to choose instead to provide for the care of their own pets. And that’s what creates the perfect opening for you to work with Helping Animal Shelters to realize an immediate positive impact on your shelter’s operating funds and provide a lasting revenue stream into the future.
