Karen Moloney’s Summary

Since training as an Instructional Designer in 1994, I've been involved with many aspects of the professional training & coaching industry including instructional design, CBT, IT applications training (including LinkedIn and other social media), soft skills & induction training and eLearning. My work has taken me all over the UK, Europe, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Hong Kong & Japan.

After many years contracting in the corporate sector, I founded my learning services company Get Me Enterprises Pty Ltd in 2007. The flagship division Get Me Learning Resources launched in June 2008, followed by SpeedTrain in September 2009 and The eLearning Experts will launch in March 2010.

My core skill - and passion - lies in being able to analyse and find solutions for elearning and IT training needs, whatever the size of the organisation. I have a quality network of learning professionals and firms I partner with, so if I can't provide a service myself I'm always always able to find someone else who can assist.
