Nicolena Stephan’s Summary

I'm a public relations professional and social media strategist with experience in traditional media relations, online marketing, new media, community relations, crisis communications, branding, advertising and event planning. I've worked with local, national and international companies in various industries, including health care, nonprofit, auto/tech, entertainment and retail.
