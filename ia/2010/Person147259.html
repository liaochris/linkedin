Mike Jalonen’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur (always will be) that has had several technology centric business ventures. Over the years I have become very focused on building scalable international companies that leverage the web to sell and support services and products. I enjoy collaborating with other entrepreneurs so that we can help each other grow and be as successful as possible.

I am direct and transparent in my communications, always striving to maintain a high level of integrity. Through my relationships with others, I am always trying to help others succeed. I believe business is all about people.

My strengths are:

1. Entrepreneurialism. I enjoy envisioning new ideas and bring people together to execute and build organizations of all types.

2. Internet Marketing. I am very familiar with internet marketing concepts, tools, and resources that help companies find customers, close business, and support their products online.

3. Sales. I enjoy people. I personally enjoy selling products and services, creating new sales channels, and recruiting, motivating, and challenging sales people to perform at higher levels than they have before.

4. SaaS. Several of my previous companies have been thought leaders and contributors to the Software as a Service concepts that are popular today. I have lots of expertise in developing scalable business models, in particular SaaS.

5. Networking / Recruiting. Sincerely wanting people to succeed has helped me build a comprehensive network of people specialized in many areas. Through this channel, I am able to help people and find trustable resources quickly.

6.Simplifying Complexity. I am able to boil down complex thoughts, ideas, and plans into very simple actions and messages that enable organizations to communicate effectively with employees, customers, and partners.
