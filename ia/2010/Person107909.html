Judy Turpin’s Summary

I am the owner/president of Turpin, Inc. horizontal-boring company, along with my husband, John, and sons, Keith and Ashley.
Turpin, Inc. was formed in March of 2000 from a previous firm, Turpin Contracting which specialized in water and sewer work.

Turpin is a State Certified Licensed Utility Contractor,drug free, Wbe, and long time members of the National Utility Contractors Association and Georgia Utility Contractors Association. I currently serve as the 2009-2010 President of the GUCA organization.

In a nutshell, boring is what you do when you need to get utilities, typically water and sewer, from one side of the road to the other. We also bore to get to the other side of railroads, rivers and streams, and evironmentally sensitive areas. We specialize in bores for all major utilities, including, critical grade bores, boreable rock, solid rock bores and conventional bores. All work is performed according to County and State regulations.

We are one of the largest Boring Contractors in Southeast Georgia.
We employee around 25 people and all of our employees have been OSHA and State Certified in the construction industry.

My company is the only Boring Contractor to ever win the prestigious "GUCA Hard Hat Safety Award" three times in 2004,
2005 and 2007.

Our goals, at Turpin, are to provide competitive pricing, the highest quality of service available with a professional team, and to build a relationship with your company as your road boring specialist.
