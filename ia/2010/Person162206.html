Penny Legg’s Summary

Freelance writer, editor, photographer, writing tutor and public speaker. I am the editor of 'The Woman Writer' magazine for the Society of Women Writers and Journalists. I am a tutor for The Writers Bureau. I have five books being published by The History Press in 2010/2011 and I take photographs to illustrate my work. I have just been appointed the official photographer for the Swanage Armed Forces weekend in June 2010. I am the founder of 'Writing Buddies', the group for writers in Southampton, UK. I write for magazines and newspapers.

Previously I edited 'ACOCI Connection' for the Anguilla Chamber of Commerce and Industry and was a columnist for 'The Anguillian' newspaper.

Aspects of my life and work have been featured in magazines and newspapers in the UK and Bangladesh.

Penny Legg’s Specialties:

Strong writing skills, layout and design of publications, photography, tutoring
