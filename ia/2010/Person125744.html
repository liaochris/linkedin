Lin Woodson, PMP’s Summary

Lin's gift is in bringing clarity from chaos and motivating diverse cross-functional teams. She is a strong, seasoned leader and highly adaptable utility player, having led initiatives ranging widely in subject matter, complexity, and size. Her project management skills span the entire system development lifecycle. An effective communicator who speaks both business and “tekkie,” Lin combines strategic thinking, tactical execution, and a sense of fun to align teams and processes with the desired business outcome. Her unique leadership style and business understanding make her a welcome addition to any team or initiative.
