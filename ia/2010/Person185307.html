Sean Lyons’s Summary

Sean has been a Big Ticket Salesman from the beginning of his professional career. He started out selling Enterprise Software for Computer Associates in New York City. His success selling Enterprise Software into the C Level of Fortune 500 accounts led to a promotion and a transfer to the Chicago office. It was here that he grew and managed the Channel Sales Team to help distribute Computer Associates Enterprise Software through third party vendors.

After a successful run in Enterprise Software Sales, Sean made a decision in an effort to help further develop his sales skills. He determined that "If I am going to be in Sales, I might as well sell the biggest thing that I can". This idea is what led him into Investment Sales and selling multi-million dollar transactions at Marcus & Millichap.

Sean went on to earn National Sales Achievement Awards every year he was at Marcus and Millichap with the exception of his first year in the business. He built a Team of six Investment Sales Professionals and consistently ranked in the Top Ten for gross sales in the Downtown Chicago office. He closed over 100 transactions with close to $500 million in total sales volume.

When the market took a turn for the worse, Sean began to explore new opportunities with a keen eye on growth areas utilizing his original Technology background. His goal was to apply his established consultative sales approach to help companies leverage Information Technology and solve real business problems. He was introduced to e-Zest through an established mutual contact who was also a client and described them as best-in-class. Sean and e-Zest soon joined forces in an effort to expand their US client base and grow the existing Chicago operations. His immediate goal is to significantly increase e-Zest's US revenues and Operations.

e-Zest has offices in Downtown Chicago as well as London. Their IT development offices are headquartered in the rapidly advancing city of Pune, India.
