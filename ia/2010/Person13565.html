Anita Brady’s Experience

  • Vice President and General Manager, Social and Business Stationery Division

    Crane & Co., Inc.

    (Privately Held; Paper & Forest Products industry)

    May 2004 — October 2009 (5 years 6 months)

    Recruited to develop new strategy, reverse declining sales, and improve profitability for key $60 Million division of this stationery market leader. Staff of 350, with seven direct reports in marketing, sales, e-commerce, commercial business and factory management.

    • Reversed six-year sales decline and achieved four straight years of growth in a declining market. Repositioned the brand, developed Martha Stewart, Kate Spade, and Disney partnerships. Grew new product contribution from 5% to 30% of sales.

    • Generated $14 Million in new revenue via innovative partnerships, licensing with Chloe B, Neenah Paper, and doubling e-commerce sales by re-launch of web site.

    • Set up and led profit improvement team that reversed a $2.5 Million loss to a $2.8 Million profit. Negotiated divestiture of unprofitable store model. Improved factory efficiency with 36% headcount reduction while improving on time delivery from 19% to 90%.

    • Generated $14 Million in sales and $2 Million profit by introducing four new product lines, expanding national gift store distribution, and successfully launching the company’s first web site.
