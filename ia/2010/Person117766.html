Krista Abbott’s Summary

Krista Abbott is:

An ENTREPRENEUR and NETWORK MARKETER who sees opportunity in every situation. Be it a blessing or a challenge, there is always opportunity. Krista has always had the entrepreneurial spirit, looking for and using new and innovative ways to do business and achieve winning outcomes. She loves embracing new and creative approaches to business practices and implementing the action steps needed to achieve success.

A CONSULTANT who provides valuable advice in the areas of business, marketing, management, leadership, writing, and strategy. Through her professional experience, she has gained a wealth of knowledge about organizational cultures and performance excellence. She is able to share with her clients the strategies and techniques needed to make any business successful and productive.

A COACH who cares deeply about helping others discover their potential, grow personally, and create success in their life. Krista offers one on one coaching sessions for individuals seeking to advance their business or personal life to the next level. She works with her clients to develop a plan customized to their individual needs.

A WRITER who loves to create and use the art of words to communicate and connect with those she encounters. Krista has had the privilege of collaborating on several writing projects with leaders in her community and network. These writing projects have included book manuscripts, stage productions, web copy writing, marketing copy writing, public relations materials, magazine articles, newspaper articles, blogs, and sales copy. Krista continues to freelance as a writer for both the individual and corporate clients.

A MEDIA PERSONALITY who is host and executive producer of Destiny Talk Radio, dubbed the place "where caterpillars become butterflies". It is a faith based radio show committed to helping people find their purpose, identify their passion, and reach their goals.
