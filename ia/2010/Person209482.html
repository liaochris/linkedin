Ulrik Falkner Thagesen’s Summary

During my carrier I have been working with consulting on Communication, Media and Marketing. A driving force in my life has always been challenge the status quo and move companies and the people I work with to try out new things when there is proper business to be made. This has given me the chance to work in different countries and allowing me to start up my own companies several times

In my carrier I have been consulting companies as Coca-Cola, Unilever, T-mobile, L’Oreal, H&M, Aller, Ikea, GM, SBS TV, SBS Radio, Egmont, Prima TV and many more.

In my job as CEO in MediaCom Denmark I have the privilege of leading the best 100 media agency people in Denmark in a company with an excellent trackrecord in research, creativity and digital understanding.
