Laura Poole’s Summary

I am an ICF-credentialed Associate Certified Coach, an Erickson Certified Professional Coach, and a member of the International Coach Federation. I support working professionals and entrepreneurs in creating a powerful, meaningful career and a balanced life. I provide on-site corporate coaching for business clients (Roving Coach).

I am also a professional scholarly book copy editor with more than 15 years of experience in publishing. I provide training for up-and-coming editors and proofreaders.
