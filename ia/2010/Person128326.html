Tyler Nelson’s Summary

Silverback-Media is the corporate umbrella underwhich we're building a next generation mobile marketing software platform business. The firms core technology and business assets we're acquired and integrated under the SBM umbrella and have collectively grown to become one of the leading mobile marketing service and technology providers to leading brands globally. As the global demand for it's unique products and services grows the business, under the guidance of a new executive leadership team, is preparing to extend it's market presence globally and accelerate it's growth to meet demand & opportunity.

I enjoyed a brief but very interesting stay working with the very talented and committed executive team at QNX Software Systems (A Harman International Company) exploring ways to significantly increase the enterprise value of the business within the context of it's corporate ownership/structure

Joined Bridgewater Systems Corporation in November of 2004 in the capacity of Vice President, Business Development & Marketing, bringing with him more than 16 years of experience and achievement in mobile wireless software, hardware, high technology marketing and strategic business development and management. Prior to joining Bridgewater Systems, Nelson held the position of Vice President, Global Business Development with Research In Motion (RIM) known for it’s highly success efforts to establish BlackBerry as the leading mobile office & messaging solution. During his time with RIM, Mr. Nelson was responsible for a number of mandates including; acquisition and commercialization of the company’s global network of wireless operator partnerships, channel development and management, business affairs and inception, development and commercialization of the firm’s BlackBerry Connect and BlackBerry Built-In business unit.
