Henrik Moltke’s Summary

I try to understand the dynamics of the network society and economy better, and by doing so, communicating this to others. I'd like to remove a bit of the fear of the new in "new media" and work on projects that connect the dots towards a freer, more vibrant culture.

Henrik Moltke’s Specialties:

Documentary, online storytelling and PR, copyright stuff (Creative Commons, sharing etc.), sound montage, online video. Multilingual (English, French, German, some Spanish plus Scandinavian languages).
