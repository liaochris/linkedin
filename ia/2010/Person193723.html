Steffen Fiedler’s Summary

Interface designer / lecturer focusing on information and installation design with generative design and typography. Currently teaches creative coding principles to undergraduate students at The Berlin Technical Art School using Processing as well as workshops about computational information design and advanced software developement for designers at the The Berlin University of Arts and University of Applied Sciences Potsdam.

Steffen Fiedler’s Specialties:

Information visualization, Computational Design, Generative Design (Java / Processing / ActionScript3 / various data interfaces)
