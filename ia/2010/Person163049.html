Peter Philip’s Summary

Peter Philip is the Chief Executive Officer of InvestmentLink/SuperChoice and describes his role as one of an evangelist for technological and business change in the financial services industry.

He is responsible for the overall strategy and performance of the InvestmentLink Group of companies. He is closely involved in the marketing, product management and operations of the company’s extensive set of financial services technology services.

Peter has been in the business of using technology to re-engineer business processes for over 20 years. Since taking on the job as Chief Executive in 2000, Peter has re-positioned and broadened the business into a key provider of hub-based technology infrastructure to the Australian wealth management industry and has seen the SuperChoice service grow to a predominant position in supplying major Australian superannuation providers with a shared e-commerce infrastructure.

Prior to emigrating to Australia in 1997, Peter held senior roles within Oracle Corporation and BellSouth Corporation in the UK and prior to this he co-founded a computer training and technology consultancy practice in his home town of Halifax, Nova Scotia.
