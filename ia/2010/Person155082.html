Niki McKay’s Summary

Blue Danube Productions joins together video, sound, lighting, staging, décor and more to tell your story in a memorable fashion that works. We bring a wealth of experience and knowledge to every event we produce. Combined, our team has spent over 75 years orchestrating production, design and coordination for every kind and size of event imaginable. We have worked with a wide variety of clients from corporations such as Microsoft to non-profits such as TheFilmSchool. We enjoy dedicated relationships with Chateau Ste. Michelle, Columbia Tower Club, Herban Feast and Pravda Studios. Value and personal service set Blue Danube apart, while Telly Award-winning creativity and vision ensure your story is unforgettable. www.bluedanubeproductions.com
