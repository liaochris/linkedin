PERI .’s Summary

The Pharmaceutical Education & Research Institute is a not-for-profit organization that has been serving the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industry since 1989. Our entire operations revolve around providing you with the highest quality education available to assist you in your current position and provide you with educational opportunities to enhance your long-term learning.

Which ever area of the healthcare products industry you work in, PERI has Open, Distance Education or Coporate Education Programs that will provide significant value to both you and your company. The PERI staff, Board of Directors, Advisory Committees, and our expert industry Faculty strongly believe that we are education today's professionals to develop tomorrow's cures. Visit us at www.peri.org to learn more about our programs!
