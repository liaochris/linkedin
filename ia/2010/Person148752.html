Idris Mootee’s Summary

An authority in strategic Innovation, a system and design thinker, Idris partners with clients in all sectors globally to identify their highest-value innovation opportunities, address their most critical challenges, explore strategic options and develop breakthrough ideas.

Idris also provides C-level executive and board-level strategic counsel, innovation and strategic guidance and frequently facilitates executive strategy sessions.

Idea Couture combines the power of D-School+B-School to solve complex business issues. IC consistently exceeds clients' expectations by bringing together the best team of design and analytical minds from different disciplines and functional experience–to bear on every engagement.

A frequent speaker at international conferences and author of several books (in many languages) in business strategy,experience design and marketing. Visit his blog www.mootee.typepad.com
