Ania Rodriguez’s Summary

Ania Rodriguez is a customer experience / market research expert with over 12 years of experience consulting to C-level executives on the topics of UI Design, Usability and Online Marketing. She has worked with over 40 fortune 500 companies as well as start-ups to define their customer experience strategy. She has moderated over 320 studies for web-based, desktop, and mobile applications with about 40 studies conducted in Spanish.

Her company, Key Lime Interactive, conducts user research and market research using both moderated and unmoderated approaches. For moderated studies her team conducts studies in Miami and travels as needed. Depending on the research needs, KLI can use their eye-tracker to provide additional insights. KLI runs online quantitative research including voice of customer surveys, true intent studies, user experience surveys, competitive benchmarking, and prototype A/B testing.

Ania holds a B.S. from Carnegie Mellon and her M.S. from University of Miami in Industrial Engineering with a concentration in Human Factors. She also professional affiliations with Usability Professionals Association, American Marketing Association and Human Factors and Ergonomics Society where she is the program chair for the Internet group. She frequently presents at conferences and online symposiums. The KLI team provides quarterly webinars on syndicated research conducted by the KLI team.

Her industry exposure includes the financial, retail, public, travel & transportation, distribution, industrial, gaming, media and entertainment sectors.

Current/Past clients include: American Express, AT&T, Avaya, Avenue A/Razorfish, CDC, Chase, Citibank, Co-Operative Bank, COX, Dell, Disney/ESPN, eBay/PayPal, eRisk, General Motors, GE Money, GEICO, IBM.COM, Intuit, NCL, RCCL, Carnival, La Salle Bank, Liberty Mutual, Louisiana Department of Revenue, MasterCard, THQ, Travelers Insurance (Life and Annuity), US Department of Homeland Security and YELLOWPAGES.COM.
