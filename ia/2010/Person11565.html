Currently employed as both the primary User Experience Web Designer & User Interaction Web Developer at Dodo Group, across a variety of sub-companies on St.Kilda Road, Melbourne. Previously, I was contracted to the CFA as Online Project Officer for Public Affairs.

I've acted as occasional media liason for the CFA, usability research consultant, online editor, conducted site training, walk-throughs and more.
