Darian Rashid’s Summary

Darian Rashid is a Lean Six Sigma Master Black Belt and Agile Software Development instructor, coach and practitioner specializing in large-scale, complex projects. Darian works with clients in numerous industries around the world to identify and solve some of their largest product-development and process problems. In addition to consulting, Darian is a skilled instructor with a keen understanding of adult education methods. Darian is also an active software engineer and actively leads teams through software development projects.

Prior to his current role as coach and instructor, Darian had founded, acquired two rounds of venture capital funding for and sold Salsa Systems, Inc., a company that created software development tools for rapid application development and deployment. Prior to his experience with Salsa, Darian was a manager within the Corporate Computer and Network Security group of Lucent Technologies. Prior to his time with Lucent, Darian held positions in Telcordia (formerly Bellcore) and AT&T Bell Labs.
