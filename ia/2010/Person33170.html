Carol Stearns’s Summary

If you would like to work with an Interior Decorator who listens to your needs and budget, Carol Stearns is that person. Carol is an active community volunteer and dedicated to her profession. As Chairperson of Decorating Den's Board of Advisors, she was instrumental in updating the brand and logo of the company. In 2009, Carol was a guest instructor for the University of West Florida, teaching a class on interior design for the home.
As a retired military officer, Carol can easily work with military clients newly assigned to the Fort Walton Beach area and help them to furnish and decorate their new homes. Her goals are to create beautiful interiors and many satisfied clients. Her twenty five years as a franchise owner and community leader have given her great satisfaction. Call Carol today!

Carol Stearns’s Specialties:

Designing Window Treatments, Helping clients Pull it all together from furniture to windows to table tops to walls and to floors. Everything in a home can be touched by Carol
