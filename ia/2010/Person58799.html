Douglas Billings’s Summary

VP of Sales & Marketing (Empire Screen Printing)

"There is only one boss. The customer.
And he can fire everybody in the company from the chairman on down, simply by spending his money somewhere else."
Sam Walton

Relationship selling is about building friendships with your customers and listening to their needs. This is the way I approach my job. I try to show my customers that I care by building the relationship and earning their trust and turning that into a friendship. My goal is not the sale but to listen to each person and find solutions to problems or needs they may have. If I show solutions and the value of doing business with myself and the company, my customer is happy and I will be happy. In the end there is only one point that matters, one perspective that matters, and one voice that matters….you, my customer or future customer.


Adjunct Faculty (Marketing & Supervisory Management-Accelerated)

A Chinese proverb says
“Teachers open the door. You enter by yourself.”

This proverb is a testament to the hundreds of wonderful students I have had the pleasure to learn with. I am privileged to not only have taught these students but now call many of them my colleagues. The reason for my passion and joy of teaching is the opportunity to not just give students textbook theory, but add real life experience to the classroom as well. I then get to see the classroom experience put into action in the “real world” by these bright minds. From business owners, sales people, marketing managers, purchasing agents and many other positions the results speak for themselves. I hope in someway I have slightly changed their lives or at least challenged them to be a successful part of the business community. To all of my former students I remind of you one of my favorite sayings in class “Knowledge is power, power is everything.”
