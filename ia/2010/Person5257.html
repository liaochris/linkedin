Alan See’s Summary

Alan See, Vice President of Marketing at Berry Network Inc., is a senior marketing and sales executive with the rare ability to speak Web 2.0 and Sales 101 in the same sentence. He also serves as an associate faculty member at the University of Phoenix where he facilitates courses in Marketing, eBusiness and Management Theory. Alan has 25 years of industry experience helping companies develop marketing strategies and sales initiatives that power profitable growth. He has performed in senior marketing, senior analyst, management consulting, and sales management roles at Seapine Software, AberdeenGroup, Teradata, SAS Institute, Cap Gemini Ernst & Young, and NCR Corporation. He holds BBA and MBA degrees from Abilene Christian University.
