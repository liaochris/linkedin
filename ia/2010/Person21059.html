My passion is helping companies grow and create value for themselves and their customers. I have held leadership positions as Director of Strategic Marketing & Licensing for Printed Electronics firm Soligie, as well as in Strategic Marketing and Business Development with Honeywell’s Sensing and Control business. I also served as Corporate Officer and VP of Business Development & Marketing for biotech firm Surmodics, President of Everest Marketing (a strategic marketing consultancy), and held global Sales & Marketing roles in medical electronics, implantables and diagnostic products.
