Greg Eckler’s Summary

I am a conceptually driven graphic designer well versed in working in a fast paced environment. A greater description of my passion and aptitude is in the inspiration I draw from the words of Winston Churchill, “A pessimist sees the difficulty in every opportunity; an optimist sees the opportunity in every difficulty.” I work in all aspects of design and welcome creative challenges of all kinds.

My goal is to push the boundaries and find solutions that are unique, novel and provoke the initial reaction of “Why didn’t I think of that before?” from others. I create compelling and appropriate solutions to the needs of the client, while drawing upon research, creative thinking and years of experience and education in the fields of design.

My work has been featured on websites such as Juxtapoz, won awards such as the Monotype Type Design Scholarship, and has been featured in shows as far as the Manchester International Festival in Manchester, England and The Yodex Young Designers Exhibition in Taipei, Taiwan.
