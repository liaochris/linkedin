Dan Gellert’s Summary

Developed kids music website Jitterbug.tv. A place for parents and young kids to listen to great independent kids music and videos - Music for hip kids!

Also a Grammy award winning audio mixer, engineer, producer. Worked as chief music recording engineer in New York, Los Angeles, and Seattle. Experienced in remixing, mastering, and restoring theatrical movie mixes for DVD home theater.

Interested in enabling creative people accomplish their desire through technology, psychology, and a bit of luck.

Dan Gellert’s Specialties:

Being the liaison between the creative and the technical.
Music recording and mixing.
Audio mastering and restoration.
