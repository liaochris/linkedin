Jochen (Joe) Krebs’s Summary

Agile Coach, Trainer and Consultant specialized in agile transformations using Scrum, Lean and eXtreme Programming. These services are offered through "Incrementor" (http://www.incrementor.com)


My two-day Scrum Training receives rave reviews from participants and is often an integral part of project kick-off's.
During my 15+ year career I have taught thousands in topics such as Smalltalk, Java, object-orientation, RUP, iterative-incremental processes as well as agile.

Over the last 10 years I have been focusing on agile project, product management with Scrum, Lean, XP and RUP.

I wrote two books, "Agile Portfolio Management" and the "RUP Reference and Certification Guide". I am the organizer of AGILE-NYC, a local user group of in Manhattan.
