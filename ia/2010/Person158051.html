Orvel Ray Wilson CSP - Guerrilla Selling Speaker’s Summary

An award-winning motivational speaker and best-seling author, Orvel Ray shows sales teams how to sell at higher prices and better margins.

From a general-session keynote to a full-day workshop, he will show your group literally hundreds of simple sales tactics to build sales and increase profits.

• Voted one of America's TOP5 Sales and Marketing Speakers in 2010

• Author of UPSELL! - How to Close More Business at Higher Prices

• Award-winning professional keynote speaker, motivator, sales trainer and Guerrilla Marketing Coach.

• 30 years platform experience conducting seminars in more than 1,000 cities, in 42 countries, on every continent except Antarctica.

• International acclaim from clients including IBM, Choice Hotels International, Marriott, United Airlines, Apple, Oracle, Ingram Micro, DISH Network, and many others.

• Co-author of 5 books in the legendary "Guerrilla Marketing" series, including:
Guerrilla Selling
Guerrilla Trade Show Selling
Guerrilla TeleSelling
Guerrilla Negotiating
Guerrilla Retailing

• His work has appeared in INC, Fast Company, The Wall Street Journal, and dozens of trade and industry journals.

• A contributing author of How to Position Yourself As the Obvious Expert: Turbocharge Your Consulting or Coaching Business Now!, with Elsom Eldridge et.all.

• A contributing author of The Six Figure Speaker with Cathleen Fillmore.

• Adjunct professor:
University of Colorado - Leeds School of Business
Arapahoe Community College (Denver)
Tyumen School of Management (USSR)
Canberra College of Advanced Education (Australia).
