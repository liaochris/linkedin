Karen Rands’s Summary

Karen, known inside IBM as "the Deal Maker", left IBM in 2001 to catch the dot.com wave of a terrific start up and began her REAL education on the venture capital formation market. Launched Kugarand Holdings in August of 2001 initially as a finder for Venture Capital firms. Kugarand Holdings evolved into a full service provider for start up and early stage companies seeking venture capital that may come in the way of debt or equity. We do this through 3 key initiatives: NBAI, a community for high net worth men and women to come together in a social environment and look at alternative investments and private equity investments to gain a greater yield; LAUNCHfn to work with companies to ensure they are seeking the right kind of capital for their stage of business and executing on a specific capitalization strategy; and KYRMedia to deliver necessary education products and our own angel investor training, to the marketplace for entrepreneurs and investors.

NOTE, to engage our Access to Capital System, companies must visit http://www.launchfn.com/registration.html and choose your preferred next step.

Entrepreneurs can also opt in for our Free "Investor Secrets" for raising capital by go to the home page of www.LAUNCHfn.com and filling in the optin form on the right hand side. Upon completion, it will route you to our page for arranging an initial consultation

Our goal is to help 1000 companies by year 2013 build assets of value that provide return to their share holders and operate efficiently and effectively in the marketplace. This will primarily will be achieved through the infrastructure built with the Launch Funding Network, Inc.(LAUNCHfn.com) and the establishment of chapters for the Network of Business Angels and Investors (nbai.net)This combination has been a powerful force in the early stage investor market and is gaining momentum.
