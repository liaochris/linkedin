Louis Bellock’s Summary

My current position is at Bosch Security Systems in Lancaster, PA in the Moving Camera division.

At my business, Prime Technical Solutions. I provided technical consultation and expertise to businesses seeking turnkey CCTV systems.

At Aventura Technologies, I was Director of Product Management to Vice President of Production and Development. I performed a complete review and alignment of the product line and provided sales/technical support and oversaw Product Management and Technical Publications.

When at Speco Technologies, my focus was to drive the Technical Support and Quality Assurance/Test departments and the DVR product line. I managed products and vendors. I wrote technical white papers and participated in trade shows.

When at Vicon Industries, I served as Technical Communications Manager, responsible for managing the Marketing and Technical Publications staff. I wrote and edited product documentation.

I have worked extensively in product development, designing and configuring electronic components and systems. I have analyzed, tested and troubleshot electronic devices, creating schematic diagrams and preparing MTBF and FMECA/FMEA reports. I served as Manufacturing Engineer, implementing Statistical Process Control on manufacturing processes. I have also done project management using MS Project.

I have worked in manufacturing MRP planning systems and participated in ISO 9000/9001 and MIL- configuration management programs, responsible for environmental testing, CE, FCC and UL/CSA certifications.

I can configured and manage networks (wireless and wired LAN and WAN) utilizing routers, smart switches and PoE compliant edge-devices. My software tools are MS Office, Publisher, Project, FrontPage, Visio, Autocad, and Adobe.

I have a minor in RF Communications and worked in the defense industry. I hold an inactive DOD Secret-level clearance. I am an electronics hobbyist and active Amateur Radio Operator with FCC Extra Class license N2JPR.
