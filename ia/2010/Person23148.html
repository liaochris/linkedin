Brandon Fuhr’s Summary

Mr. Fuhr has been involved in the marketing, sales, and service of IT products and services since 1996, when he was Service Manager of TTI, a large regional network integrator. He helped grow TTI to be one of the leading providers of networking, internet access, computers, and peripherals in a four state area.

In 1998, Fuhr became Marketing Manager for Atipa Linux Solutions, a leading provider of supercomputers and high capacity servers based on Open Source technologies. While managing marketing efforts, the company raised $40M in Venture Capital and grew from 2 to over 100 employees in a two year period, bringing on numerous high profile government, educational, and Fortune 500 clients along the way.

In 2001, Mr. Fuhr joined Equus Computer Systems, the largest custom system builder to the Channel, where he led the second largest branch in new business development during his 2.5 years with the company, consistently exceeding sales goals.

In 2004, Fuhr accepted a position as Director of Business Development for Nuvio, an early pioneer in VoIP, that leveraged Open Source technologies to deliver residential VoIP and Hosted PBX services to SMBs. He developed the initial channel sales and marketing strategy for Nuvio, then executed on that strategy, building Nuvio from zero sales to cash flow positive with over $3M in yearly recurring revenue in only 2.5 years.

In 2007, Mr. Fuhr joined Fonality in a Channel Development role. Fonality develops Open Source based PBX solutions for the SMB market. Mr. Fuhr built channel revenues through a combination of new partner recruitment, major account management, and strategic business development initiatives. Fuhr led the channel team in sales on a number of occasions and was instrumental in the company's successful launch of the trixbox Pro product line.

Mr. Fuhr enjoys roles with a mix of channel sales, marketing, business development, and thought leadership for companies with disruptive technologies.
