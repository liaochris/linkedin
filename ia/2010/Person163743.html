Philip Kearney’s Summary

Accomplished Senior Executive with 15-plus years in Digital Media focused on business transformations. Demonstrated success in generating and re-starting profitable growth for digital brands in environments ranging from large corporations to new ventures. Deep experience building and leading teams focused upon the ability to attract, engage, retain and monetize target audiences and existing customers.

Fifteen years and 150+ sites / approx. 100 digital brands from Fortune 500, mid-caps and ventures - working as an executive or consultant with executive management teams to build, integrate and grow business in Digital Media, specializing in pharmaceutical, publishing and consumer packaged goods industries.
