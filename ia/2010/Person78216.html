Greg Tenuta’s Summary

Currently:

In February of 2010, Greg graduated Titan America's Management Develoment program and he is now assigned to Florida Concrete Products with Tarmac America. As a Management Development Candidate (MDC), Greg gained experience and knowledge in Titan America's rotational training program including Quality Control, Operations, Sales, and Administration.

Outside of work, Greg is an active member and Advisor in the Jaycees organization and also a member of the World Affairs Council of Jacksonville.

Certifications:

Greg's obtained professional certifications for Hazardous Materials (maritime, ground, & air), ACI concrete testing, DOT Concrete Batch Plant Operation, is a certified NRMCA Green Building Specialist, and also has a Teaching English as a Foreign Language certification.

Background:

Previously while in college, Greg was employed by the U.S. Air Force as a civilian employee. Also during school, he took advantage of being a member of the Delta Chi Fraternity and held positions including President, Special Events Coordinator, Fundraising Chairman, Recruitment Chariman, and Alumni Coordinator. Upon college graduation from Northern Arizona University with an Extended B.S. in American Political Studies, Greg accepted a position in the logistics industry in Phoenix, AZ. Looking for a career shake-up, he relocated to the Czech Republic where he worked two jobs. He privately taught English as a foreign language, and also was a conference coordinator for financial firms. Upon returning to the U.S., Greg found a home in Jacksonville, FL where he resumed his professional career in the logistics industry.

Greg Tenuta’s Specialties:

Non-profits, logistical commerce including both domestic & international shipping and distribution, concrete/cement, supply chain management
