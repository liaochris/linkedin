Matthew W. Marsh’s Summary

Matthew Marsh, Founder & President of Lead Research Group Inc., helped build LRG into a multi-million dollar marketing and advertising agency which specializes in performance-based lead generation techniques. Matthew's leaner, meaner, faster and smarter philosophy has helped catapult the company to INC 500 status in 2009 and establish recognition as one of Orange County's most successful growing businesses by OC Metro Magazine.

As one of the key architects behind Lead Research Group, Matthew is responsible for providing valuable insights of performance based marketing initiatives for some of the largest and most successful businesses nationwide.

Matthew is the catalyst behind Lead Research Group's highly strategic, innovative and efficient marketing solutions for a variety of Industries such as Debt Settlement, Loan Modification, Tax Relief, Mortgage and more.

Under Matthew's leadership, Lead Research Group has grown from a start-up company with little capital to it's current ranking of #37 for all marketing and advertising agencies in the U.S. by the INC500. As a budding entrepreneur at a young age, Matthew also serves as consultant to fellow business owners in leveraging their capital, skills, and operations to optimize corporate success and growth.

Matthew currently resides in scenic Newport Beach one block from the Pacific Ocean and is a proud father of one young son.
