Alison Ray, CMP’s Summary

Founder of Metropolitan Meeting & Event Group, an elite meeting, corporate incentive travel and event planning firm dedicated to designing and delivering memorable experiences. Over the past decade, Metropolitan has assembled a team of experienced and trusted professionals that are committed to providing exceptional customer service with a proven background in domestic and international travel, logistical and budget management.

■Over a decade experience planning sales and marketing meetings, corporate incentive trips, executive meetings, trade and product launch events along with corporate holiday parties.

■Active member of Meeting Professionals International Chicago Area Chapter and Executive Director of International Network of Travel Directors.

■Featured in a variety of publications including MPI One+, IL Meetings & Events, Corporate Meetings & Incentives and Midwest Meetings Magazines, Plan Meetings Publications, Plannerwire.com and Face2Face MeetingsNet.com Blogs.
