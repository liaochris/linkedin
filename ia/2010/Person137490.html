Matthew Perosi’s Summary

Internet experience began in 1988. Of course the "web" didn't really begin until the Mosaic browser in 1993. Been programming websites since 1994 & have worked with Perl, miniSQL, MS-SQL, StormCloud, ASP, PHP, JavaScript & WebDNA.

I'm the developer that created the Psiclopedia(TM) eBusiness CMS.

Guru-level web developer using WebDNA specializing in Standards & Usability, with a love for wireless websites! I love .mobi and think everything will move there one day.

I cannot create web designs from scratch, I'm too much of a math and science geek to be artistic. BUT I do have the ability to look at a design and tell you what's wrong with it, and correct it myself. Perhaps that ability comes from all the projects I've overseen or directly worked on. As of August 2008 I passed my 500th website project mark! Ack!

When it comes to application development I always concentrate on database integration and figuring out how to get those in-house systems tied to the internet. It's always an interesting challenge.

Primary current focus is the independent retail jewelry store. I have create several little programs (funny that people generally call them "apps" now) that allow the retail jeweler to interact with their customers online. All together I put together a low cost web system that can easily bring a behind the times jewelry store to a for-runner in their local market.

Internet Application Developer, Database Administrator, and Project Manager for the retail industry where we offer many applications for customer interaction. Particularly interested in web and cell phone applications that provide information on demand. Always interested in mobile technology projects.

My past experience required me to learn a lot about computer and internet technologies. Through the years I've held certifications and worked in various areas including: Network Wiring and Fiber Optics, CCNA, CCDA, SCO Administration, DNS Administration, PhotoShop, Flash...
