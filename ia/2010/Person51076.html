David Newman’s Summary

Through seminar and keynote presentations, David Newman helps CEOs, business owners, franchisees and marketing staff attract, engage, and win more customers - NOW more than ever!

David speaks at conferences and associations, runs marketing meetings and retreats, and delivers high impact programs on marketing, branding, and business growth.

In this economy, every marketing dollar must work twice as hard if your company is going to meet its business goals.

And you certainly can't afford to do marketing for the sake of marketing. We can help you do marketing the right way using our Strategic Marketing Process and straightforward approach, which can be summarized as

Define > Organize > Implement > Track

Whether the delivery method is speaking, seminars, or consulting with individual clients, David knows there is no cookie cutter. YOU are no cookie!

David Newman is the author of 8 books on marketing and sales, including "Relish for Business" and "21 Secrets of Simple Marketing Success."
