Ryan Brandle’s Summary

• I have a wide range of experience from brand strategy, embroidery/wearable design, screen-printing, digital and interactive applications of design.

• I am an experienced traveler and photographer. I have been to over 40 countries on 5 continents. I love to learn about and experience new cultures and people. I love to share stories.

• My area of expertise involves merging the understanding of people, cultures and perceptions with the knowledge, skill set and tools to effectively communicate a message. The more people are exposed to the world of design... the more channels of communication we open between each other. We are building bridges of understanding between people in ways that the pre-computer era couldn't achieve.

• It is a wonderful feeling to know that something I have created has had an effect on someone. I can provide an opportunity for someone to see something in a different way; to expose them to something, or inspire thought.
