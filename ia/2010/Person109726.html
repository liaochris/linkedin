Mark S. Blitz’s Summary

With 30 years of manufacturing experience behind me, I've put to use more of my electronics interests and joined the telecommunications field as an RF technician, with a strong interest in learning more about circuit and packet switching, SONET, T-Carrier, SS7, etc.

My work background consists primarily of 30 years of hi-tech & precision mfg. plant design, layout, installation, commissioning, operation and maintenance, primarily CD & DVD replication. All phases of equipment installation, maintenance and repair: preventative, routine, overhaul and rebuild, including but not limited to: injection moulding, incl. drying & conveying systems; pneumatic, hydraulic, and electrical systems; PLC-based controls; industrial robotics; high-voltage AC; vacuum metallizing & sputtering; lacquer spin-coating & UV curing; Six-color screen printing & prep thereof; jewel case, Amray box, automated sleeve packaging, and overwrapping of every type. Responsible for everything:
-Drafting the floor plans
-Preparing electrical; compressed air; chillers & pumping systems; drainage, ventilation & exhaust
-Meet & comply with OSHA, EPA, State & Local codes on all levels, for all utilities
-Layout, stocking, & inventory of parts warehouse and toolroom
-Instruct & supervise up to 30 employees in all phases of the manufacturing process
-Establish procedures & methods, and improve upon as needed through time/motion studies
-Establish machine processes through strict quality assurance for optimum yield/minimum downtime
-service and repair both equipment and facilities: machinery, process control, rebuild/rework moulds, plumbing repairs, A/C servicing, replacing lamps in the parking lot, etc., etc.
-Established excellent rapport vendors yet worked continously sourcing parts, supplies & services at not just the lowest price, but for the best value in terms of cost vs. quality
-Set up & maintain corporate ethernet, incl. server, file, scanner & printer sharing, DSL internet access
