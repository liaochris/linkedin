James Stewart’s Summary

I'm a passionate entrepreneur and intrapreneur who has spent the past few years learning the ropes in the IT scene.

I'm currently working part time at Urban Media in Christchurch learning graphic design for print and web which I love.

My previous job was Video Production and Community Manager for MadeFromNewZealand.com. I managed a four person production team to create sharp and innovative videos to help companies to profile their products and services to their potential customers. As the Community Manager I maintained the social media and support emails as the face of the companies customer service.

Before working at Made From New Zealand I worked on my own start-up ArtKlick.co.nz which was an online gallery for New Zealand artists to profile and sell their artwork.

I obtained a Bachelor of Science in Geography from Canterbury University in 2005 and have also worked in retail for Quest Skate and Snow and McMillians Water Wells as a Geotechnical Site Investigation Assistant.
