Rebecca Osberg’s Summary

Services Offered at RebeccaOsberg.com

Ghostblogging and editing — Business blogs can be high-maintenance and demand a lot of attention, but they turn readers into customers, and can be used as a powerful social networking tool. In order for a business blog to be successful they need to be updated often. The posts don’t have to be long, but they do have to be constant so that your readers are engaged and return to your site again and again. If you just don’t have the time to put into keeping your blog up-to-date I offer ghostwriting services that will create search engine optimization (SEO) responsive content (while still being interesting, entertaining, and written in the voice of your business). Think of me as your own personal blogger. $25 per post/ $100 for 5 posts

Website About page and Bio — Your website is set up, but your About page leaves a bit to be desired? Do you have a blank spot where your picture should be and your name is spelled wrong? After a conversation about who you are and what you want your customers to know about you, we can create a custom and impressive bio, whether it’s an overview of you or your company. $50

Web copy — You have an amazing product but no idea how to write about it on the web so people will know how amazing it is. Make sure your product copy actually sells your product rather than just describes it. Prices available upon request.

Social Media Bios — You’re ready to plunge headfirst into the world of social media, but don’t have your Facebook, LinkedIn, or Twitter set up. Let me help create your bios so that people can recognize how creative and awesome you really are. $50 per bio. $125 for all three.

Press Releases/Newsletters/Email Blast — Want to get your word out in ways besides a blog or website? A press release or newsletter is a fantastic way to do that. Let me help write it so that when you hit send, you know your product is being described in the most excellent way possible. $50 per each.

    2010 — Present (less than a year)

    I’m Rebecca Osberg: writer, editor, working professional, and blogger extraordinaire. I am also the driving force behind RebeccaOsberg.com, a social media writing boutique. I aim to help small businesses ramp up their profile and sales through the use of social media — particularly the writing aspect.

    I graduated from University of Wisconsin-Madison, having spent my summers writing countless press releases and newsletters for Marketing Support Inc. I took my marketing experience, my BA in English, and pursued my life long dream of living in New York City. I moved to Manhattan and attended a publishing program at NYU. From there I landed jobs at InStyle Magazine and Entertainment Weekly Magazine working in the sales/marketing department, helping to conceptualize and execute marketing plans and living that good New York City life. But it only took three years before I realized my home and my heart were in Chicago, so back to the Midwest I went.

    Fast forward four years and I am glad to be living in my hometown of Chicago and taking advantage of all city life has to offer (especially in those warmer months). The publishing world, particularly Publications International, has been my professional home since my return to Chicago. I work on children’s books, which I love (particularly those Disney princesses), but I realized there was so much more I could be doing with my background in writing, editing, and marketing.

    To create RebeccaOsberg.com, I combined the last six years of my professional life with my obsession concerning all things social media. I read hundreds of blogs, write blog posts everyday for both myself and my clients. I understand the power connecting with your customers via the Internet can have for a small business. Fostering relationships with your clientele is so much easier, economical, and more crucial now that the world has shrunk to the size of a smart phone screen. I can help you cultivate those relationships and grow your business.
