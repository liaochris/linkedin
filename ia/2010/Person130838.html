Manoel Amado’s Summary

Manoel Amado is a highly successful technology visionary and architect with extensive experience in developing new products that utilize emerging technologies. Mr. Amado is a creative and solutions-oriented leader with a proven track record of leading major product development, deployments and support. Mr. Amado has a strong knowledge of a wide range of technologies and has a consistent history of meeting or exceeding customer expectations.

Mr. Amado is the Founder, President & CEO of PhoChron, Inc., where he is focused on the use of the Internet to organize, collaborate, and publish photo-based publications. He has developed a patented collaborative publishing technology which has been deployed to the school yearbook markets in the US, Canada, UK & Australia.

Mr. Amado previously served as Vice President of Engineering for PictureVision, which was sold to Kodak. PictureVision pioneered the first online photo processing, sharing, and printing service. He developed joint products with major key technology partners such as SONY, Eastman Kodak, AOL, Canon, Fujitsu, Noritsu, and Konica. He developed products deployed to North America, Europe, Japan, and Australia.
