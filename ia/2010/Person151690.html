Natalie Hooper’s Summary

Natalie Hooper worked has with Perkins Eastman since her graduation from Cornell University, College of Architecture, Art and Planning. As a Junior Architect with Perkins
Eastman Natalie was able to participate in all phases of the architectural process, providing assistance in schematic design on several residential projects, as well as helping
in the preparation of Construction Documents for various buildings. She has spent most of 2008 reviewing shop drawings and Construction Administration for a 3 million SF mixeduse development in Flushing Queens.

Natalie is LEED certified, and assisted in the documentation and recording of LEED credits for 2 projects within our Studio, a residential building and a major remodeling at the
United Nations. Natalie worked and communicated very well with other team members and was regarded
as an essential member to the various teams that she had worked with. She is proficient in all phases and had shown dedication to the numerous projects that she was involved
with. As a Junior Architect Natalie has grown and committed herself to learning as much as she can about architecture and its processes.
