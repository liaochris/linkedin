Gabriel Mizrahi’s Summary

Gabriel is a management consultant, entrepreneur, athlete and writer.

He currently works in the Strategy & Operations practice of Deloitte Consulting, where he has advised companies on M&A due diligence and integration, operational transformation and revenue enhancement in the technology, retail, financial services, telecommunications and media industries. Fluent in Spanish and proficient in French, his work has brought him to various locations in the U.S. and South America to work on his clients' global ambitions. He also leads several internal teams developing Deloitte's thought leadership and training capabilities.

Before joining Deloitte, Gabriel co-founded The Veridical Group, a consultancy focused on start-up and small-business growth strategy. He also served as a summer analyst at Citi Smith Barney and an economic policy analyst at the American Enterprise Institute, a Washington, D.C. think tank.

Gabriel now writes and speaks about topics in strategy and management. He is a Riordan Fellow at the UCLA Anderson School of Management and plans to continue working at the intersection of management, entrepreneurship, fitness and public policy.
