Anthony Sclafani, PE’s Summary

Converting wasted energy into revenue is how I make money for myself and others. In my current position, I do this through my role as a mechanical engineer leading teams of 5-10 engineers through the audit & analysis process in California, Arizona, Nevada, and Hawaii.

Most of my experience is in performance contracting (from all phases of energy audits through commissioning), energy efficiency retrofits (equipment upgrades, system re-design, and control technology), renewable energy systems, risk management, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

My primary goal is to continue expanding on what I already do - apply state of the art energy engineering practice on projects in construction; in other words to make ideas reality. Aside from money, although economics are the driver in any business, the fact that this work helps the environment and makes peoples' lives better makes it worth doing over the long term.

Outside my day to day activities another goal is to be one of the engineers at the forefront of breakthroughs in energy efficiency. I work toward this by working with my team to develop creative solutions for improving energy systems and publishing the results. Professional journals represent the latest developments in any industry and I enjoy sharing results, experiences, and lessons learned so that these ideas may be adopted more widely.

Anthony Sclafani, PE’s Specialties:

Energy auditing, data acquisition, thermo-economic modeling and analysis with DOE-2/eQUEST, design and analysis review, technical communication (scopes of work, analysis reports, presentations), and commissioning.
