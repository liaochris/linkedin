Art Lenehan’s Summary

Multimedia leader
art.lenehan@kinnective.com

Recognized nationally for leadership in multimedia content development on the internet. Successful in many seven-figure digital development projects, both in publishing and on the net.

Founder of Kinnective, a company that builds visual information -- focusing on interactive video, charts, photos, maps -- supported by social networking.

Special expertise lies in marrying the content vision to the rich media technology, Flash or Silverlight, and finding the most efficient route to execution based on options "in the box." A vision that is well matched up with the technical possibilities (as when we know that a certain tool set includes software objects with X and Y ready-made capabilities), can be executed much more quickly and economically than a vision that is at odds.

Special focus has been video/multimedia features. These features have proven that rich media engages the user much more intensely than html pages, increasing both clicks and time-on-site by nearly 100%.

Built and launched first "inline" video player deployed at MSN and the first major multimedia experience on the portal.
Winner of The Loeb Award for online business journalism for Keeping Up with the Wangs, a 2007 series of stories that explored the rise of China's middle class (http://articles.moneycentral.msn.com/Investing/StockInvestingTrading/ChinaIntro_SeriesHome.aspx). Winner of "Best Online Project" from SABEW for the same project. Winner of a second consecutive Loeb Award in 2009 for "Middle Class Crunch," an explanation of the financial pressures bearing upon the American middle class and how those pressures related to the Presidential election of 2008. See it at http://articles.moneycentral.msn.com/Investing/HomeMortgageSavings/MiddleClassCrunchConundrum_SeriesHome.aspx. Winner of Best Investigative award from national Society of Professional Journalists for Toxic Legacy (http://toxiclegacy.northjersey.com).

Art Lenehan’s Specialties:

Flash, Actionscript, C#, XAML, Blend, SQL Server, writer, editor, builder of staffs that have won numerous national awards for investigative reporting, photography, design, and interactive information on the web.
