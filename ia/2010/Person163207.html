Peter Johan Welleman Engineer’s Summary

As a Project Manager I create 3D simulation for training, research and presentations. I manage an international team, partly in the Netherlands, partly in Vietnam, overseeing the quality and time constrains of visual artwork for Virtual Reality Environments. Having been a 3d modeller myself before, I know how to turn technical difficulties and limited resources into a successful project.

Both technical/nautical knowledge and "soft" skills are required to manage these projects successfully: from organizing pilot interviews at the ships, to setting up training scenarios, to realizing the visuals for own or external simulators. In India and Trinidad I have set up marketing campaigns, which generated further prospects. Working closely with the marketing department, I have been responsible for many of the company's promotional activities such as web-design, social networks or organizing mailings for clients. During the last year I was heavily involved in the implementation of new professional software such as Paradigm Creator and Vega (Prime). I usually create planning schedules and reports with MS-Word or Excel.

Goal: combining my passion of flying (I'm pilot at the local aero club) with my professional life.
