RJ Bucaria’s Summary

Through his journey in the entertainment industry, Mr Bucaria has held positions in A&R, Artist Management, Director and C-Level Executive positions. He has also had the pleasure of working with some of the music industry's top names such as Paul McCartney, Elton John, Russ DeSalvo, Moe., Crash Test Dummies, Ozzy Osbourne, Linkin Park, Vanessa Williams, Brian Doyle, Aerosmith, Wycleff Jean, Rob Kos, KRS-1, Naughty By Nature, Public Enemy, Chuck D, Curtis Blow, Suffocation, Sevendust, Ja Rule, The Dave Matthews Band, and Paul Vance.

Music has always been a central part of Mr. Bucaria's life, by the time he was admitted to the prestigious Berklee College of Music at 17, RJ had already recorded 3 full length albums, performed Off Broadway, produced 3 benefit concerts, written/directed/scored 2 original plays, was the chief lighting designer for over 20 stage productions, and had added over 15 instruments to his performance repartee.

Currently, Mr Bucaria is the C.E.O. of the 7th Level Entertainment Group based in New York City. As an industry leader in the international event production and talent management world, 7th Level has developed a wide portfolio of A-list corporate and artist clients including Clint Black, Sony Music, Universal Music Group, The Black Eyed Peas, Atlantic Records, Wyclef Jean, The Beastie Boys, the Government of China, the Government of India., Conceptual Elements, FOX Television, Elvis, India Ari, Eminem, Emiko, Public Enemy and many more.

RJ Bucaria’s Specialties:

Out of the Box Thinking, Complex Deal Structures, Creative Partnerships, Business Development, Contract Negotiation, International Business, International Event Production, Multi-site/ Mulit-Media Projects, Cross Platform Revenue Generation, Musical Composition, Music Production, Filmscoring, Buisness Consultation, Music Performance, Talent Management, Event Planning, Marketing and Promotion, Artist Development, Event Coordination/Planning, And I Cook.
