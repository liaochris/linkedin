George J Kanuck III’s Summary

George is a high energy sales and operations executive with nearly 20 years of success creating high growth revenue streams by applying a strategic lens and an entrepreneurial skillset to corporate and start-up environments. His creative approach to building and developing clear, precise business strategies and driving large high-performance organizations has delivered outstanding new business results profitably in startup, turnaround, and high-growth situations. He has worked extensively with global customers in high-tech hardware, software, financial services, pharma and telecom, including Cisco, HP, AT&T, UPS, Eli Lilly, Deloitte and Duke Energy, as well as the federal government in various branches of the DOD, DHS, USPS and Census Bureau.

George is also a partner at PSD3, the sales performance experts that help sales leaders achieve sustainable performance, and a Principal at iSalesman, Innovator in outsourced Enterprise and Mid Market Sales organizations.
