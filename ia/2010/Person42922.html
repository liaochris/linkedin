Scott Hapner’s Summary

As a "Connection Consultant" my passion and mission is in some small way to better our communities quality of life.

Picture this; I help “Business A” start a conversation with “Business B”. Soon they find common ground and do "business" together. After awhile, A&B grow their network, make more $, and hire maybe a few new employees. Those new employees now gainfully make a few extra bucks that they spend at some business, say... in old town. That business now has a new customer, they collect sales taxes from their new customer, pay their sale tax obligation, and the city uses that sales tax to keep our parks clean. Now this may not solve the national economic crisis, however if there are a few more connection consultants around we sure would have some nice parks!
