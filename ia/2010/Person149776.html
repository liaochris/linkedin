Michael Sidorov’s Summary

This life is full of choices and possibilities, I am with Azoft - Russia's most global R&D service supplier for Telecommunications & Mobile Financial industries. We are based in Russian Academy of Science and deliver our services all across the globe.

Our expertise we gained in projects for Tier-1 wireless operators and telecommunications service providers of all continents as well as been a subcontractor for international remittance providers and telecom product & service suppliers. The major areas are B/OSS transformation and data migration (Cramer certified), including prototyping and development of new functionality in CRM, billing, provisioning, mediation, network management and QoS. MNO and MVNO we equip with bespoke Mobile Financial systems, Mobile Money Transfer, and location-based sevices.

For telco consultancies & suppliers we operate as a fully-fledged strategic business unit capable of on-time and on-budget BSS/OSS/VAS engineering. Also, we can share our prototypes in mobile apps, which you can show potential client to prove you have the required expertise.

To minimize partner's transition risks new cooperation we start with traceable boxed scope that allows for evaluation of our competence, quality, work process and business ethics.

To reduce project management costs and boost performance we provide on-site project managers who gather requirements, control development and deliver you the results with detailed reporting.

Our key focus is long-term collaborations allowing challenging telecom projects and constant work flow.
