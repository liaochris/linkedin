Barbara Clarke Ruiz’s Summary

Entrepreneur: BCSWIRL, Inc. – Activewear Design Consultant

I am the creative director and CEO of BCSWIRL, Inc, a fashion design consulting firm for the activewear/sportswear industry located in the New Jersey/ New York area. I have designed in this industry for 18 years. I have experience both as a corporate designer and a consultant, as well as having lived and worked in abroad. My unique talents has allowed me to work with industry leaders in Europe and the U.S. such as Nike Europe, Adidas USA, Adidas Germany, Fila Speedo Int’l, Wal-Mart and New Balance just to name a few. My design studio is a fashion resource for the American and European market. Most recently, I was the Creative Director of Activewear for six divisions at the worlds largest retainer, Wal-mart. My studio specializes in all apparel categories for men's, woman's and children’s. Over the years, I have built a company that spearheads new design direction for corporations in the men's and women's markets. As a consultant, I am always in search of exciting and challenging design opportunities.

Entrepreneur: Fortune Girls, Inc.
Co-Founder/Chief Creative Officer
Brand: Fortune Girls

Fortune Girls is a multi-cultural lifestyle brand created by my now 12 year old daughter and myself. The brand is dedicated to the empowerment of tween girls age 7-12. Our mission is to empower tween girls and encourage them to discover their own unique gifts and talents and begin to think about what she wants to be when she grows up, through our new Virtual World, www.fortunegirls.com.
