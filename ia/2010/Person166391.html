Lisa Qualls’s Summary

An 18-year technology and business management veteran, Lisa delivers high-energy, fun, and a broad array of professional experience to her client engagements. She has held a variety of leadership positions in sales, program management, product development, business integration and executive management. With a strong reputation in the industry for her customer-first leadership approach, she enjoys building relationships, has a passion for communication and is a creative problem solver.

Lisa successfully built, managed, and consulted on teams that developed over $1B in sales opportunities, implemented a $100M next-generation technology program and program managed a $75M technology migration & system integration effort. She has worked with global companies such as Cisco, Sprint, General Electric and John Deere. She most recently co-founded LightThread, LLC developing a successful next generation strategy, integrated services and web development firm specializing in optimizing business performance and brand.

Lisa currently serves as President of Social Media Club Kansas City, volunteers as a member on the Blue Springs Officials Association executive board, and often speaks on topics such as business integration, digital tools and social media. You can find her on Twitter: @lqualls4444
