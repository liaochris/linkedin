Sterling Ashby’s Summary

Currently, Mr. Ashby is an attorney at Leftwich & Ludaway, LLC and leading Titus Venture Group, LLC in developing a number of interesting projects, including History In Action Toys (www.hiatoys.com).

After graduating from law school, Mr. Ashby worked for five years as an Associate in the Energy Group of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP. His wide-ranging community involvements have included leading fundraising efforts for elected officials, as well as for numerous non-profit organizations; serving on the Board of Directors for DC Scores, which helps middle school students achieve both on the soccer field and in the community; acting as Debate Coach for Kelly Miller Middle School; serving as a trustee for the Lehigh University Alumni Board; advising the Founding Board of the Septima Clarke Public Charter School; serving as a reunion Co-Chair for his law school class; and organizing more than 25 speakers for The Breakfast Club, a group devoted to wealth creation and institution building in the African-American community.

Sterling Ashby’s Specialties:

counseling start-ups, mergers and acquisitions, corporate finance, commercial lending, regulatory and general corporate matters.
