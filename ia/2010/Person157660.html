Jasmine Sandler’s Summary

Agent-cy provides small-mid sized businesses full online marketing services including SEO, PPC Management, Social Media Management, Web Design and Development, Content Development, Distribution and placement; Online PR; Online Media Buying; Affiliate Marketing Management; Web Video Production. Agent-cy specializes in serving online branding solutions for both small and mid-size professional services firms .Agent-cy has a strong client success track record in achieving longevity in brand awareness, audience growth and market position.

Agent-cy is led by Jasmine Sandler, a known online marketing strategist with over 15 years client experience. She is available to executive management teams for consulting and troubleshooting in any areas of online marketing and strategy. Miss Sandler is a frequent industry speaker at such road shows as The Online Marketing Summit, The Samsung Technology Series, Manhattan Chamber Marketing series and NYXPO. She is also a contributing writer to Raintoday.com, Searchenginestrategies.com, ASM newsletter, and The Online Marketing blog.

Jasmine holds an MBA from The University of Miami as a Summa Cum laude and spent 7 years as a data networking manager at IBM. She lives in NYC and is currently engaged to a fellow business owner, has passions for business, travel and global issues. More information about Miss Sandler and her company can be found at http://www.agent-cy.com.
