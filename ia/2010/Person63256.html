Elias Bizannes’s Summary

"... we have just started scratching the surface of the enabling power of the Internet. Whether it is called "Web 2.0" or "New Media" or "Enterprise 2.0," Internet services are going to drive the world's economies for the foreseeable future. To me that doesn't spell bubble, that spells opportunity."
-- "Is 'Web 2.0' Another Bubble?", Wall Street Journal, December 27 2006

The Internet gives us ubiquitous access to information and computing from anywhere in the world, which is creating new possibilities. I want to be a contributor in that evolution, working as an executive, entrepreneur, and evangelist.

- An economic theory regarding information businesses on the Internet: http://eliasbizannes.com/blog/2010/01/the-information-value-chain-and-its-network/
