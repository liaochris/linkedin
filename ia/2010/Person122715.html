Laurie Wiggins’s Summary

Twenty-five years experience in systems engineering, business development and environmental services. Domain experience includes aerospace, information technology and environmental systems. Program experience includes development and operation of crewed space station, launch vehicle, space experiments, air traffic control, software, and carbon credit programs. Entrepreneur that has established and operated companies providing space payload launch services, systems engineering consulting and environmental services.

• Information Technology: Software architecture development, Rationale Architect/Testmanager, website development

• Energy Systems: Performed solar cell R&D, RTG and fuel cell studies, geophysical seismic analysis, oil and mineral lease management
• Entrepreneur: Established, operated a space payload services company, a consulting company, and an environmental services company
