    Export Connection

    (Privately Held; Research industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Export Connections is a forward thinking international research agency. By using our specialist research techniques and in country researchers we are able to provide the knowledge and expertise to help companies to expand their business internationally.

    The services we offer include identification of the best international markets to enter, locating suitable potential agents and distributors in export markets, finding your end user, providing in country data and general advice on international markets and how to best approach the new markets.

    If you are looking for a low cost way to expand your business then a good place to begin is by locating suitable and appropriate distributors and agents for your products. Our in country experts have the knowledge of their specialist country and are able to establish local connections through local research. Our experience, knowledge and expertise saves you time and takes the headache out of doing it yourself providing easy access to many markets.

  • Consultant

    Export Connection

    (Privately Held; Research industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    Export Connections is a market research agency that helps companies, particularly UK manufacturers to expand their business internationally.

    This is achieved by locating suitable and appropriate agents and distributors in new markets, specialising in the Middle-East and Europe.

    So what can Export Connections do to help? Well the overall objective is to build business and achieve sales for the companies, by using niche research techniques to identify new markets and business connections for these manufacturers.

    These companies contact details are then passed on to the manufacturer, giving them their own network of companies therefore saving them the hassle of finding contacts themselves.

    By building their business in export markets through distributors it also builds their confidence and encourages international trade.

    Export Connections offers a unique service and is the only company in the UK of its kind.
