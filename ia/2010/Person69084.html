Farrukh Naeem Freelance Copywriter & SEO Specialist’s Summary

Farrukh Naeem is an advertising copywriter, journalist and ad blogger. He has worked as a copywriter for advertising agencies in Asia and the Middle East, a direct marketing writer for Wunderman under the Y&R banner, and a TV commercial scriptwriter. He is also a journalist and a popular online commentator on advertising, marketing and creativity in the UAE and Middle East and author of a popular ad blog at http://www.copywriterinuae.com
