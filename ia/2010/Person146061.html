Mike Sutton’s Summary

With over 17 years of experience in the financial services industry, Mike Sutton founded Independent Strategic Advisors ℠ in 2010. Previously a director at Experian Decision Analytics, Mike fulfilled many roles during his career at Experian including managing strategy consultant, collections solutions marketing director and software product director.

Prior to Experian Decision Analytics, Mike worked for the Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta as a financial services analyst. He was also was a manager at First North American National Bank focusing on fraud, customer service and risk management initiatives. Mike also served as a collections manager at Wachovia Bankcard Services.
