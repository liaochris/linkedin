  • Owner / Operator / Administrator

    midrange.com

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 1990 — Present (19 years 7 months)

    I created midrange.com originally as a BBS, which morphed into a collection of mailing list. It was originally created as an exercise in enlightened self interest. I was looking for a free forum to discuss S/3x topics, but the only forums I could find were pricey.

    midrange.com now hosts a number of active email based discussion forums, wiki, FAQ, and resource links site, all dedicated to the IBM i platform.

    midrange.com runs on a pair of Dell PowerEdge servers running Linux. It's main web site (http://www.midrange.com) is powered by a IBM iSeries (hosted by Franken i [http://www.frankeni.com]).
