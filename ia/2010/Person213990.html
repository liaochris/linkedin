Wayne McFalls’s Summary

Performing as the Senior Security Manager/Architect within the Hosting Systems Organization, my primary responsibilities dealt with infrastructure policies, legislation, industry standards, audits, and security services within the infrastructure. This infrastructure had over 700 healthcare related clients with terabytes of data secured (HIPAA). As part of the new infrastructure design and development team, my responsibility was to ensure alignment with regulatory and industry standards in the requirement development and design stages, documenting the correlation between them or justifying the difference. This infrastructure included primarily Microsoft computing platforms and extensive Cisco networking, with Linux, AIX, Websphere, Tivoli, IBM and other platforms a part of the overall infrastructure.
