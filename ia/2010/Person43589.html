Cory Jurentkuff’s Summary

Since 1999 I have made the web my career. I focus on developing online strategy for large-scale website redesign projects, and managing the redesign process. With two interactive journalism degrees, I also have a passion for online content development. I work with clients to define their messaging goals, develop information architecture and write audience-appropriate content for websites, email marketing and social media.
