Raymond Fong’s Summary

Oh sweet nectar of freedom! I use to work in the 9-5… I don’t anymore.

After getting my BS from Harvey Mudd College in engineering, I went on to work full item the aerospace industry while getting my MS in computer engineering from USC. I quickly learned that I age 72x faster inside a cubicle and calculated that’d shorten my lifespan by well… about 874.9 years. At least.

I figured that’s a no-no so I scratched my head figuring what I should do instead. Well, after years of struggle in both the network and internet marketing industries, I finally “arrived”.

I quit my job and now do Internet Marketing (IMO) full time. I work harder than ever but I also party harder than ever. And I am loving every minute of it :)
