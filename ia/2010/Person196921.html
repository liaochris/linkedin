Sudipta Sarkar’s Summary

Goal: Make a difference, for the better, in any organization through sound decisions and performance backed by solid knowledge and perseverance.

Geospatial professional with over 6 years of industrial experience as Project/Production Lead/Sr. Analyst/Application Developer in diverse verticals of climate and atmosphere, forestry and environment, global agricultural commodities, parcel and landscape mapping, navigation, higher and better usage, groundwater etc. In addition I have another 5 years of experience in designing cutting edge research using geospatial techniques in reputed academic and scientific institutions. Have a solid background in analysis, design and development of Geospatial decision support systems and possesses thorough familiarity with most off-the-shelf and open source Geospatial tools, scripting and programming languages, spatial databases. A robust background in numerical and statistical concepts provides a further edge to my repertoire of Geospatial skill sets.

Committed team player yet independent and self reliant, I have a proven track record of projects executed successfully within stipulated timelines and requiring quick adaptation and mastering of new domain know-how’s. A self motivated learner, I can quickly re-orient myself to the requirements of new domains and the needs of a fast changing industry and provide necessary feedback for my team members and higher managements.
