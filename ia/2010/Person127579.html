Lori Ann Mertens’s Summary

My goals are to be one of the Best REALTORS in our area to take care of your real estate needs. With all of my real estate training and customer service,

Lori Ann Mertens comes to Port Charlotte from Colorado where she worked in customer service for over 20 years. Her training in customer service prepared her well for a career in real estate.

An ad for Burnt Store Marina in Sailing Magazine got her husband Dwight's attention with its appealing reference to a "fun lifestyle". They fell in love with the area on their first visit!

Lori Ann has been married to "the love of her life" for over 27 years and their proud parents of 3 awesome children and 4 grandchildren.

Lori Ann holds an Associates Degree in Real Estate and General Business from Colorado Mountain College. Having spent several years in the Charlotte County real estate market she is well equipped to serve your every need whether selling or buying. Here is to an awesome 2010 for everyone!

To experience "world-class" service from a consummate professional, contact Lori Ann Mertens today.
