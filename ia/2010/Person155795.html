Noah Lang’s Summary

Noah Lang is the Director of Business Development at ReputationDefender. With individual and corporate customers in more than 40 countries, ReputationDefender is the global pioneer in online reputation management. Its products help customers protect their personal and professional online reputation and ensure their privacy.

Noah currently leads business development efforts building high-volume, channel sales across 11 key industries. Noah has established partnerships with premier professional associations, top 5 deep-Web data providers, international people-search engines, leading employee benefits programs, and a top 10 global financial institution.

As an early-stage employee of ReputationDefender, Noah Lang has also led the development of two new product lines, managed internal and external affiliate marketing networks, developed analytics and reporting platforms for partner revenue sharing, launched new advertising initiatives, and directed integration planning and implementation for strategic partnerships. He enjoys collaborative environments and the opportunity to work closely with colleagues across corporate divisions.

Additionally, Noah Lang dedicates his time to advising and sponsoring the Internet Keep Safe Coalition (iKeepSafe), a non-profit that works for the health and safety of youth online, and advising the Online Reputation Management Association (ORMA), a newly-launched association of the world's leading Online Reputation Management Professionals.

Prior to joining ReputationDefender, Noah was a financial consultant with Navigant Consulting, Inc. Noah studied Product Design and Economics at Stanford University, focusing on need-finding, user interaction and product development cycles. He was captain of the Stanford Triathlon Team and co-chaired sponsorship for the Palo Alto Local Organizing Committee of the 2008 Tour of California cycling race. Noah is an avid triathlete, skier and endurance sports junkie.

Noah Lang’s Specialties:

Online Reputation Management (ORM), Online Privacy Management, Business Development, New Technology Acquisition: Valuation & Integration, Investor Relations & Related Financial Modeling, Waterfall Forecasting, Venture Capital Due Diligence, Product Development, Online Product Marketing, Channel Marketing, Reputation Establishment & Shaping Strategies.
