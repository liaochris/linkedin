Eric Johnston’s Summary

Eric spent the month of January 2010 in Cambodia working with Teachers Across Borders to provide in-service training to Cambodian teachers and administrators. As Technical Program Director, Eric is working to integrate distance learning and collaborative technologies into TAB’s programs.

Eric Johnston’s Specialties:

With an extensive background in video broadcasting on the one hand and database design on the other, Eric founded Pangaea Multimedia in 1987. Eric's work has focused on developing blended learning programs and custom LAMP applications, creating elearning, marketing/communications and collaboration solutions. Projects have ranged from piloting distance learning programs for MasterCard, Sun Microsystems and Motorola ISG to designing and building custom eLearning and content management systems.
