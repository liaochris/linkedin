Craig Ward’s Summary

I am a highly dynamic, professional, honest and loyal worker who thrives on working in a busy IT environment. My roles to date have required me to have a flexible approach to my duties with the need for the ability to switch between projects and tasks when required. I have experience of people management, both with direct reports and external contractors. I have always built and maintained good working relationships with external contractors and suppliers. I am now looking for a fresh challenge in my career.

After a successful time in IT management I am now looking to move into web development, an area I have always maintained a strong interest in. As IT is moving more and more into cloud computing, online presence and SaaS I see a career in web development as the most exciting area to be working in.

I have had full commercial exposure to industry standard leading applications such as Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Flash and Fireworks in my previous roles in web development/design. I can hand code HTML, XHTML, CSS and have some exposure to PHP, javascript MySQL and PHPmyAdmin. As a project to develop my skills I have started to redevelop my own website. The main part of this site will be a web presence timeline where I take the feed from all the sites I use such as Twitter, Facebook, Delicious, Digg and Stumbleupon. I feed them into a MySQL database and display the mashed up results in date order. I am comfortable developing on a Mac or PC and have commercial exposure to both.
