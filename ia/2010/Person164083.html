Matthew Philpott’s Summary

I started my career at the Carphone Warehouse back in 2001 working part-time while studying Advanced Business at A-Level. After my studies were completed I was quickly offered a full time position which led to the start of a long successful career within the company.

As I progressed from a successful Senior Consultant to Assistant Branch Manager then eventually to Branch Manager I had developed a huge customer base in the derby area by working in all 3 of their retail stores.

In 2007 I decided to take a career break in which I ventured on a journey of a lifetime to Thailand, Laos, Malaysia, Australia, New Zealand, Fiji, Argentina, Uruguay, Bolivia, Peru, Ecuador and finally Colombia before returning home for 2010. I am now looking to establish myself within the IT recruitment industry.
