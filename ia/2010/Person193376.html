Stan Weatherby III’s Summary

I have been involved with Internet Technology since 1996, starting in email marketing then later moving forward on developing enterprise anti spam software and several other control mechanisms for email as a whole. Some of this included the design of Load Balancing Software, Email SMTP [MTA] Software and complete email systems that were used for high volume Internet Service Providers.

I was with FlexMedia and was in charge of the design and implementation of rich multimedia flash applications. Much of the technology that I designed for FlexMedia is cutting edge and unlike anything that is currently on the marketplace.

I am currently the Managing Director of Weatherby Technology Group, LLC. This company was what I consider, hobby technologies. Some of these technologies are very cutting edge also, but they have developed into technologies that have allowed me to work them full time.

I feel confident that Weatherby Technology Group is positioned to be one of the most exciting businesses that I have ever been involved in. I am looking forward to taking the company to the next level, by unveiling some of the processes and technological aspects to the general public over he next 12 to 16 months.
