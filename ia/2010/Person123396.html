Lee Fox’s Summary

Lee is a passionate entrepreneur, parent, team builder and volunteer. Her experience spans the entertainment, technology and new media sectors where she has taken title as CEO (internet/mobile); VP of Marketing (professional services); Content Creator (brand management); Writer / Associate Director (consumer TV); and has been honored with multiple board / advisory roles. Mother of three, coach, and active humanitarian, Lee has focused the last two years on the development of a revolutionary internet youth property, KooDooZ. Lee encourages social responsibility (both personal & corporate) via her blogs & public speaking engagements that emphasize the importance of CSR, cause-marketing and youth-empowerment.

Lee Fox’s Specialties:

From concept to completion, Lee's industry expertise includes: * operations strategy & management; * team building; * performance metrics; * content development & direction (printed collateral, video, TV, internet & new media); * message continuity; *brand management; *cause marketing; * social media integration; * speech writing; * competitive business analysis; * start-ups; *social entrepreneurship

Lee Fox’s Experience

  • Founder & Chief Innovation Officer

    KooDooZ

    (Computer Software industry)

    July 2009 — Present (10 months)

    KooDooZ is a cause-based social networking site for youth (5th thru 10th grades) designed to provide equal opportunity for kids to affect change in their world.

    The site offers kids an array of social impact initatives that are co-authored as online challenges by partner educators, profit-for-purpose experts and nonprofit leaders.

    While the discovery of the challenge happens online, the "doing" happens real-world. Our system documents evidence of youth progress towards meeting specific service goals and learning outcomes. As we are a certifying organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award, KooDooZ has the ability to credit time served for those tasks that meet volunteerism, community service or service learning standards.

    For more information, please contact info@KooDooZ.com
