Cameron Uganec’s Summary

Cameron is the co-founder of Be Bold, Do Good, a company dedicated to designing tomorrow’s enterprises. He is a unique marketing leader, with deep experience in the dark arts of persuasion and marketing. He founded Be Bold, Do Good, with the belief that business innovation, digital media and entrepreneurship can have a positive impact towards some of our greatest social and environmental challenges.

Cameron brings 15 years of business leadership experience to Be Bold, Do Good, from both independent entrepreneurial environments and global professional service firms. His marketing leadership experience spans research, product design, brand development, multi-channel global marketing campaigns and customer relationship strategies. His career is jam-packed with high-profile product launches for clients such as Four Seasons, Cadillac and Xbox. He has international experience having lived and worked in San Francisco, Toronto and Vancouver. Cameron has led projects in Europe, Mexico, South Pacific and throughout North America.

Digital media is second nature to him, having started his career in digital marketing after completing a diploma in Applied Information Technology in 1995, before Google became Google. After 5 years of working for big agencies on big brands he moved to the client-side at Playground, an Intrawest Company. However, this didn’t slow down his exposure to new projects, methodologies, teams and vendors. Playground was essentially an incubator of resort real estate projects, responsible for over $5 Billion in sales, during his tenure. As the VP of Marketing Services for Playground, Cameron created a new revenue stream for the company starting a new venture, Playground Advisory Services.

Today, he is focused on perfecting the balance between money and meaning, as a social entrepreneur. Simply put, he is interested in using his experience in the dark arts of marketing and his knowledge of digital media to Be Bold and Do Good.
