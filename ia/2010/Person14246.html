Anne Morrison’s Summary

Founder and Managing Member of Law Office. The Firm focuses its practice on commercial real estate, business law, general commercial agreements, and bankruptcy matters. Clients include commercial landlords and tenants in local, national, and international lease transactions, and the simultaneous support of corporate real estate portfolios. The Firm also represents owners and developers in real estate acquisitions and dispositions, due diligence activity, development, renovation, and financing. Additionally, the firm focuses on commercial contracts including procurement, telecommunication, franchise and brokerage agreements. We are also well versed in debt restructure and bankruptcy matters,.

Prior to establishing her law practice, Anne was an executive and senior real estate attorney within a Fortune 100 Company, a S&P 500 Company, and a top tier law firm. She also clerked for the Honorable Rosemary Gambardella, United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of New Jersey.

Anne Morrison’s Specialties:

commercial real estate, esp. leases (landlord and tenant), commercial contracts, bankruptcy, & due diligence.
