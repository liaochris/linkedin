Alan D. Widgren, PE’s Summary

Highly motivated, self directed and meticulous engineer. Experienced in complete life cycle development, implementation and validation of automotive electrical and electronic products. Main focus in powertrain related products.

24 years experience in design, development, & releasing of automotive electronic powertrain controllers. Complete Project Management experience from RFQ creation to system design to production launch & manufacturing support. Program lead on many major powertrain programs. Experience includes component manufacturing, core-h/w releasing, vehicle h/w & s/w integration, component & vehicle issue resolution, & vehicle PTEE-Systems Architecture and Integration.
