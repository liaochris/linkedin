Steven Brooks’s Summary

Steven Brooks is the founder of Guugos.com. Born and raised in New York City, Steven moved to Chicago in the 80’s. Guugos.com was born out of Steven’s 20+ years in technology sales and marketing. He has successfully built and managed sales teams in 23 countries. He brings a wealth of knowledge and experience from working at large companies such as IBM and Computer Associates. Steven has also worked with several start-up companies introducing new technologies to the marketplace.

Steven has always been focused on finding better ways to help people. He received his bachelor degree in Marketing from Drexel University and his Masters Degree in Organizational Development from Antioch University.

Steven Brooks’s Specialties:

video production, job recruiting, social media marketing

Steven Brooks’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    Guugos.com

    (Privately Held; Media Production industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Guugos.com is improving the way employers and job seekers meet and engage. Paper-based resumes are adequate to document skills and accomplishments, but people are not a collection of skills and accomplishments. You can't learn much about the person from a traditional cover letter. Our goal at Guugos is to put the humanity back into the process.

    Guugos.com produces broadcast quality video cover letters to enable job seekers to “introduce” themselves to employers. For job seekers who are frustrated with the process of submitting their resume and cover letter to potential employers, video cover letters enable these individuals to stand out from the crowd and get noticed. Guugos.com produces video's for local job seekers at one of our studio locations in Chicago. Our production teams can also be found on mid-west college campuses working with Career Services and Alumni leadership to provide this capability to their students and alumni.

    We live in a visual society. Lets take advantage of the technology and introduce people to people.
