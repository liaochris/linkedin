Cherry Bezdek’s Summary

Ms. Cherry Bezdek will bring over 8 years of professional recruiting experience to include full life cycle recruitment for Information Technology and Federal Government clients. She has successfully sourced, recruited and hired for Java/J2EE, .Net, Microsoft Server and Oracle product architecture, design, development and integration technologies support. In her current role as a Corporate Recruiter for T-Rex Corporation, she has successfully placed candidates for security cleared positions on large production scale programs to include clients such as US Customs Border Protection, US Census Bureau, National Archives and Records Administration, Internal Revenue Service, Department of Homeland Securities, and Human & Health Services. In addition, she has proven ability to identify and effectively analyze resumes for technical content to ensure candidates meet minimum job requirements. Furthermore, she possesses excellent written and interpersonal skills and operates with the highest degree of integrity, skill and professionalism. Ms. Bezdek will bring her full lifecycle recruitment experience as well as her strong networking and sourcing skills to effectively support the recruiting efforts of IT professionals focused on software development, system engineering and system integration experience for large scale government programs.
