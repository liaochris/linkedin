Mbugua Njihia’s Summary

I am a business savvy – techpreneur with over 7 (seven) years experience in the new media industry with focus on web and mobile engagements. Instrumental in the set up and operations of one of the regions inaugural value added services, and having worked at key positions in leading web and mobile service firms, he possesses unique insight that marries technology with the market place. My role is to implement the strategic goals and objectives of Symbiotic, to give direction and leadership toward the achievement of the organization’s philosophy, mission, strategy, its goals and objectives as well as seek out new business and partnerships that continue to grow Symbiotic Media going into the future.
