Laura Allen’s Summary

You’ve only got 15 seconds to make an initial impression; ever find yourself tongue-tied? I specialize in making sure that this doesn’t happen.

My name is Laura Allen, and I teach people to market themselves more effectively. After learning who you are, what you do, and what makes you stand out in a crowd, I partner with you to distill a concise clear message of what you offer—a message which can be delivered in 15 seconds.

I offer clients three ways to engage:

• Through my interactive website, 15secondpitch.com, you can design and refine your own pitch for free and create personalized business cards that stand out.

• Through my 15SecondPitch™ Workshops, I facilitate a series of short exercises designed to help you capture the essence of your personal marketing message.

• Through my private consulting and coaching practice, I offer clients individualized attention and personal services. My clients include executives and entrepreneurs, many of whom work with me for years as their business and professional careers evolve and grow.

Contact me for more information on upcoming workshops and consulting availability.
