Jeremy Burkhardt’s Summary

Jeremy Burkhardt is the CEO of SpeakerCraft, Inc., the world's largest manufacturer of architectural speakers and custom installation products. He recently served on the Board of Directors for CEDIA and has been involved in the custom instalation industry for twenty years. He contributes articles to a variety of industry trade publications, and lectures at numerous venues worldwide. Jeremy sold SpeakerCraft for 58 Million dollars in a record setting industry team up with Nortek, Inc. in 2003; as a share holder of Niles, Elan, Sunfire, Panamax, Omnimount and many other CEDIA companies, Jeremy has his finger on the pulse of the custom
installation market. In addition to being one of the largest manufacturers and industry shareholders, Jeremy has been a custom installer as well as an custom audio video retail store operator. He actively studies Psychology and Philosophy, reading over a hundred books a year. In his free time Jeremy is a skydiver, mountain biker and snowboarder. His motto of Live Until You Die and Be Here Now rule his life and he sucks the marrow out of every day he is lucky enough to be in the custom installation industry.
