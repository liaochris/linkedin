Laurie Barkman’s Summary

With 10+ years internet experience, Laurie Barkman is a recognized specialist in the field of online marketing. In May 2007, she joined Education Management Corporation (EDMC) and currently is the VP of Online Marketing for The Art Institutes. At EDMC, her focus is direct response lead generation and website strategy for the 40+ on-ground schools across the US and Canada. Prior to joining EDMC, Laurie led the global online marketing efforts for American Eagle Outfitters for six years, launching multi-channel branding, conversion-oriented programs, and social networking initiatives. As a result of her team's leadership in performance-based marketing, she was a speaker at Internet Retailer and Direct Marketing Association conferences. In addition, Laurie managed search-focused internet products while at Lycos and MediaSite video. In addition to her online tenure, Laurie gained operational and HR experience at Ingersoll-Rand Company. Laurie holds an MBA in marketing and technology from Carnegie Mellon University and a BS in Industrial and Labor Relations from Cornell University.
