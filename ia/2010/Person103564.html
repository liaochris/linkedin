Jolie Veltmann’s Summary

Ms. Veltmann is a partner of the firm Veltmann & Leto with an exclusive focus on family law with an emphasis on contested custody issues, paternity matters and high-asset dissolution cases. She also has expertise in obtaining and defending domestic violence restraining orders. She joined the firm in 2008 to help drive the firm's commitment to fulfilling clients' needs. Prior to becoming a partner at Veltmann & Leto, Ms. Veltmann managed her own Law practice for 16 years specializing in family law.

Ms. Veltmann has been featured as a family law expert on station KUSI and she has been court appointed to represent and act as Minor's Councel as well as appointed to represent Sevicemen under the Soldiers and Sailors Relief Act. Ms. Veltmann was honored as Mentor of the Year with the VIP Mentor youth program (Volunteers in Parol) founded in 1972, and participates in the San Diego Lawyer Voluteer Program. She is a featured speaker on Family Law at San Diego City College and Grossmont College. Based on her prior experience as a deputy district attorney, she is also court appointed to represent defendants in Family law contempt cases.

Ms. Veltmann heads up a Family Law team to provide clients with high standards of representation. Whether clients desire settlement, litigation, or mediation, she can support the interests of the client. The team can settle cases through mediation and will obtain the best possible result given the unique facts of each case. However, in cases where settlement and mediation are not possible, Ms. Veltmann's team is comprised of experienced litigation attorneys who will pursue the client's rights and interests to obtain the best result possible.
