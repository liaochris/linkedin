Kellie Whitehead’s Summary

Enjoying 10 years experience within the Marketing and PR sphere, my invaluable 'training' comes from a fabulous 4 years working within the Arts & Entertainment industry in the UK. From a 'business' perspective, this grounding was invaluable as you gain experience in every field - not many theatres employ outside agencies, put it that way. at the very least it gave me enormous experience in getting 'something for nothing'!! From a personal perspective, well, the stories I could tell, but that's showbiz, darling! I managed to write and edit the family page of a Yorkshire newspaper for a couple of years also

After some voluntary fun at BBC Jersey (not 'qualified' to be employed within the Channel Islands) , I found myself in Malta, where for three solid years I wrote scripts for literally hundreds of radio and television commercials (as well as the odd restaurant review and mag article)

Onto the Middle East for the next leg of the tour where I find myself in the fabulous position of enjoying more work than I can handle!. apart from the inability to say no, my reliablity, professionalism, experience and quality of work sees me writing copy for web (site copy, blog pieces and articles) and, well, most collaterals you could think of, not forgetting the radio ads! Haven't done any telly yet here in the UAE, but there is still time! Thoroughly enjoying my little foray back into the world of 'journalism' via my pals at GN, and will be forever grateful to them for the chance to see my name/work in 'The Hollywood Reporter' and am delighted to have been recently commissioned by The National.

Yes, still the same old failed theatrical I was 10 years ago!
