Amy Gahran’s Summary

VISION: It strikes me that journalism is just a means to an end. The goal is to help people and communities understand their world, share their perspectives and concerns, and make better decisions.

...At the same time, media is moving much faster than most established news organizations and journalism institutions could ever hope to keep up.

MY PASSION is to discover the best ways to continue to achieve the goals of journalism, while thinking beyond the tools, organizations, strategies, and players of the news biz as we've known it so far. In the process, I hope to find ways to carry into the future the best from journalism as we've known it in order to help shape the future.

SO: WHAT DO I *DO*? I help organizations and individuals achieve their goals through effective, creative use of online, social, and mobile media. I also work on my own projects in these areas, and help build project teams.

Much of my work has involved journalists, news organizations, and related institutions. I'm expanding beyond this group to work with community-focused projects, librarians, Government 2.0, and technologists of all kinds (especially for collaboration, mapping, and social media).

MY BACKGROUND is in journalism and editorial management -- but my skill set has advanced far beyond writing stories and managing editorial systems. I keep abreast of the latest tools and services. I spot opportunities and pitfalls, manage experiments, and craft successful strategies.

I can show you how to constructively participate in the public conversation in ways that will engage and energize you, engage your community, leverage your strengths, and support your mission. More importantly, I can help you understand how participating in the public conversation can help you reach and refine your goals.

Consulting services include strategic consulting, seminars, one-on-one coaching, weblogs, writing, editing, technology assessment, and project management.
