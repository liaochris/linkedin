Christopher Stanley’s Summary

Christopher Stanley and Associates, P.C. strives to provide competent, agressive representation to its clients in civil litigaton and arbitration matters. The firm handles all civil business litigation with an emphasis on construction in commercial and residential cases. The firm also provides lien work for all levels of construction services from general contractors to suppliers as well as construction contract drafting and negotiation.

Christopher Stanley’s Specialties:

Civil litigation and arbitration with an emphasis in construction. Mediation of all types of cases.
