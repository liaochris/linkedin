Paul Mullen’s Summary

Known for pulling a handful of priorities from a mountain of problems, I catalyze business by rapidly creating and executing a strategic plan for growth and/or transformation. We jumpstart momentum, boost morale, grow revenue and profitability, capture marketshare, and build a path to a sustainable future.

I lead businesses poised for growth, companies requiring swift turnarounds or organizational change, or start-ups requiring proven / innovative leadership. Background includes extensive P&L management and board-level experience in $100-600M publicly held companies (experiential marketing, business services, manufacturing). Typically achieve >30% revenue / margin growth. Representative impact:

GROWTH: Grew MICE North America into a profitable $100M business within just four years (despite industry downturn). Transformed it into a global competitor.

TRANSFORMATION: Revitalized GES, drove revenues 35%, to $573M, reversed negative cash flow, grew operating margins into double-digits, integrated acquisitions under one brand.

INNOVATION: Built successful strategic development plans: bolted-on multiple lines of new business for MICE, created First National Ambassador Service Program for GES, spearheaded Giltspur’s first design animation technology initiative.

CHANGE MANAGEMENT: Fast-tracked at Giltspur from account exec to General Manager, President, and COO / CEO, revitalizing two divisions and leading sale of the ten-location company (exceeding the target price by $8 million).
