He has fifteen years of Program Management and Systems Implementation experience in the Financial, CRM, ERP, Pharmaceutical, Manufacturing and Power Supply industries with the top global companies & Clients. Involved with the full system life cycle projects, right from conception, initiation, design, development, test, implementation and maintenance phases for most of the systems. Helping companies / clients with their short term (tactical) and long term (strategic) goals on where they want to be in the market and working with them to lay out the roadmaps on how to achieve it including the best use of technology. Successfully managed multi million dollars global programs that also involves offshore / distributed delivery model. Worked with top company / client executives on the overall offshore delivery model and have already successfully delivered many initiatives using this model.
