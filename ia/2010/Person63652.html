Elizabeth Hendler’s Summary

I have worked in the non-profit field for over a decade, and have experience in:

* fund raising, grant writing and relationship management - with foundation, corporate, government, and small donor groups/family foundations;
* organizational capacity building - from small to large organizations;
* board development;
* individual donor solicitation and relationship management;
* events creation and management, and;
* Public relations, branding and re-branding, as well as cause related marketing.

I have additional skills in assisting with re-branding projects, from start to finish:
* creating the team to bring organizations new perspectives on their visual messaging,
* revamping or updating of pr materials, logos, design,
* With my background in web production, and the tech savvy needed to reach all resources today, I can assess web/sms/social networking integration from message and mission to the fund raising plan,
* With my background in film production I have the teams available to create a film, video, documentary, or other related investment.

My skills emerge at their best when I am working with organizations that allow me - like an internal consultant - to provide an assessment of what is working, not working, and strategies for improvement, to then be empowered to deliver the desired results.

I aim to assist in non profit growth. This is done through outcomes that promote staff, constituent, and donor agreement and satisfaction, increased capacity, internal systems organization and management that work and increase productivity, and most important - increased funding and other resources.

Currently, I am interested in government and ARRA grants, I am thinking a lot about the direction that fund raising will take over the next 2-5 years in NYC and nationally. I hope to help groups maximize their fund raising potential through creatively addressing current realities and opportunities.

Elizabeth Hendler’s Specialties:

Fund raising, Grant writing, Events, Advocacy, Consulting, and general non-profit cheer leading.
