David Brewster’s Summary

Mr. Brewster has over 20 years experience in the environmental consulting industry. His primarily areas of interest include: environmental sustainability, biofuels, green manufacturing, waste minimization, contaminant fate and transport, environmental assessments, remedial activities, property redevelopment, ocean sciences and policy.

I am an expert in the performance of due diligence studies for industrial and commercial property. I provide expert project management focusing on cost effective solutions concentrating on the reuse and rehabilitation of contaminated properties. Services include expert evaluation and technical review of assessment and remedial actions, as they relate to selected remedies, allocation and funds distribution.    

The base clientele for services include industrial, commercial, real estate, legal, banking and public sectors.  Closure techniques are implemented with a cost effective approach in concert with considerations for environmental quality, regulatory compliance and site aesthetics, bringing contaminated sites to final closure and resolution for sale/redevelopment.

Projects are conducted with an effective and balanced evaluation of risk, cost and benefits to human health and the environment. Business concerns also include green manufacturing, waste minimization and environmental sustainability.

David Brewster’s Specialties:

Mr. Brewster specializes in the fate and transport of environmental contaminants, regulatory compliance, waste minimization, and finding cost effective solutions to complex problems.

Specialties include industrial compliance, soil and groundwater contaminant fate and transport studies, facility closure and remedial activities and environmental due diligence.
