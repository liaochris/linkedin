Bobby Markowitz’s Summary

As Vice President Events, Sponsorships & Promotions for Earthquake Media in New York City, I am responsible for the company’s overall events strategy, promotional development and sponsorship activation for the company and its clients.

Since July 2005, when I joined Earthquake Media to help manage its #1 client PartyGaming, I have spearheaded the rapid growth of Earthquake Media’s client’s event production, sponsorship placement & activation and customer acquisition efforts.

I have more than 15 years of experience in both online & offline advertising and marketing and I run my division of EQM with a distinct business strategy promoting and integrating a commitment to ethics as the best way to attract and retain customers. In doing so, I am shaping a new paradigm for events, sponsorships and customer acquisition that I believe can deliver the most memorable brand experiences.

Prior to joining Earthquake Media, I built and directed the marketing for Ziff Davis Media’s video gamer social network 1UP.com in San Francisco and in 1998 I was one of 3 key employees relocated to Europe to build and market Sports.com, a sister site to Viacom’s CBS SportsLine, into Europe’s largest online sports entertainment destination. Also while in Europe, I developed business and activated sponsorships for RPMC, an LA-based experiential marketing excitement agency.

Before moving overseas, I served as both radio marketing manager and international marketing manager for SportsLine.com after being recruited from SportsRadio 560 WQAM where he was Sports Sponsorship Specialist for the Florida Marlins Radio Network.

I have a BS in psychology from the University of Florida (Go Gators!) and I am an MBA graduate of the University of Miami, Florida with a focus on international business.
