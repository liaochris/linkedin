Phil Lewis CSP, TSC’s Summary

Since 2000, I have been serving the staffing industry and assisting numerous companies in the midwest to overcome staffing painpoints, create retention plans, develop personnel policies and develop workflow analysis to control costs.

I am responsible for the development, implementation and execution of creative recruiting efforts to exceed my clients needs for talent in the midwest. Equally, I am the leader of the business development efforts for my four state service area and strive to develop true partnerships with my clients.
