Taylor Murphy’s Summary

A Green Thumb is a horticultural design and consulting firm specializing in bamboo and tropical and sub-tropical plants with Tropical and Zen/Asiatic themes. We also design California Native, Spanish Colonial and Xerioscape, and Low Water Use Landscapes.

A Green Thumb Landscape & Design
WEB: http://www.agreenthumb.net
EMAIL: agreenthumb@cox.net
PHONE: 619-269-9501

Taylor Murphy’s Specialties:

We specialize in landscape renovation and rehabilitation, we offer for the existing landscape at older homes and buildings, professional solutions to problems with plants, plant diseases, soil amendment, and irrigation. Our services include a plant budget, plant list, irrigation layout, lighting, water features, and referrals to other qualified, licensed professionals.
We are water wise, organic & chemical free and affordable, low maintenance.
Our motto: The Right Plant in the Right Place!
