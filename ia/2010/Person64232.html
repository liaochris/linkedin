    Color Us Well

    (Philanthropy industry)

    October 2009 — Present (7 months)

    Color Us Well is an inspirational and charitable project improving the lives of children in the hospital through creativity and self-expression.

    Uniting local talent, Color Us Well has put together coloring books, featuring original artwork by diverse artists in the community. Both acclaimed and aspiring artists have designed a coloring book page with their unique style and message of hope, enriching each child with a new creative experience.

    With increasing budget cuts of local art support and art education, it is imperative to keep the passion for and knowledge of the art community alive. Color Us Well hopes to branch out into other cities, involve more prominent artists and continue to provide the gift of creativity for children who need it the most!
