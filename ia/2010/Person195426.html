Steven Danon’s Summary

Prior to Marcotte, Steve held positions with two of the largest business insurance companies in the U.S. and with the second largest business insurance agency in the world. A sampling of the businesses he has worked with have included, Nebraska Furniture Mart, Creighton University, Godfathers Pizza, Pizza Hut, Douglas County, Boystown-National Operations, Gordmans Stores, Commercial Federal Bank, Huntel Communications, Dillards, JC Penney’s, Sears, Younkers, Security National Bank, Millard School District, Oriental Trading Company and United Parcel Service.

He has been a reserve lieutenant firefighter for a large Nebraska suburban fire department, nationally and Nebraska/California certified Emergency Medical Technician, and a First Aid/CPR national instructor (train-the-trainer). He is a past Board member of the American Society of Safety Engineers-Great Plains Chapter and serves as an adjunct Occupational Safety Program Instructor for Metropolitan Community College Omaha, Nebraska. Steve has recently served on the Command Staff as the Safety Officer for the tri-county (Douglas, Washington, Sarpy) Urban Areas Security Initiative (UASI) a national Homeland Security program to enhance security and overall preparedness to prevent, respond to, and recover from acts of terrorism.

He is also serving on the Douglas County Health Department’s Pandemic Influenza Policy Committee. The Governor of Nebraska recently re-appointed him to the Nebraska Emergency Response Commission.

He has a Bachelors of Science Degree in Management of Human Resources and is an Authorized OSHA Outreach Instructor and has presented hundreds of compliance and risk reduction workshops.
