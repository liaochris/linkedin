Bruce Garfield’s Summary

ARTIST MANAGEMENT AND CREATIVE CAREER CONSULTANT

I started off in the merchandising end working with artists like Led Zeppelin, Cream, Jimi Hendrix, The Doors and many more. Then worked for producers and indie labels before a 14 year run at Capitol/EMI Records as head of Artist Development and Press Departments, then A&R.


At Capitol/EMI I led the career development of Duran, Duran, Bog Seger, The Band, Steve Miller Band, Thomas Dolby, The Motels, The Knack, Missing Persons, Iron Maiden, Billy Squier and many others.

Moved in management as label life became too corporate and have managed Sinead O'Connor, Tricky, Grace Jones, Isaac Hayes, WAR, Will Downing, LFO, Ziggy Marley (consultant), Inner Circle, Nadine Coyle, and Hayley Sales.

Working on branded entertainment properties, media and career consultancy, and whatever suits my fancy.

Bruce Garfield’s Specialties:

I am a creative and well connected artist manager. I develop, promote, and market concepts to expand careers, assets, and income streams of clients. I build and reshape career paths to insure longevity while maximizing an artists' craft in a custom tailored manner that is a good fit for their life. A successful career is not marked by simply reaching a paramount plateau of popularity which might be short lived, but rather to have it provide a fulfillment and comfort that lasts your lifetime.
