David R. Young’s Summary

I am a motivated and creative ecological design and management professional with 20 years experience in architectural design, land use planning and the use of information system technologies to communicate, manage and meet design goals. I have a strong aesthetic sense coupled with analytical, strategic planning and project management capabilities. I have proven abilities to coordinate and integrate large amounts of divergent information, to design the elements that organize it into coherent tools for decision making, and to create the platform by which information ecologies become accessible to all stakeholders.
