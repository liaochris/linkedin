Christopher Powers’s Summary

Prior to starting my own design and marketing firm, I worked with Fig. (formerly Advanced Lipo Dissolve), where I helped this medium-sized company become a national brand.

During my tenure at Fig., I helped create concepts and executed many of their direct mail campaigns, magazine ads, and poster and billboard designs before striking out on my own.

I am a graduate of the Center for Visual Technology and the St. Louis Community College at Meramec. I am an active member of the American Institute of Graphic Artists (AIGA), the Graphic Artists Guild (GAG), the Kirkwood-Des Peres Chamber of Commerce, the Chesterfield Chamber of Commerce, the Webster Groves-Shrewsberry Chamber of Commerce and the Better Business Bureau (BBB).

Christopher Powers’s Specialties:

I worked for a large company with an aggressive marketing department. While my degree is in graphic design, I received a hands-on education in the trenches. While at Advanced Lipo Dissolve aka Fig. I feel what I learned from my experience with this company and the marketing department gives me the experience and the edge to propel the smaller business customer to greater heights.
