Donna Beestman’s Summary

View my VisualCV:
http://www.visualcv.com/donnabeestman/mypublicvisualcv

A seasoned career strategist and coach with over 17 years experience helping clients successfully address career issues and challenges.

Works with executives and professionals one-on-one locally in person and nationwide via telephone/internet. Provides an "executive coaching" approach to career management.

Over the years Donna has guided hundreds of professionals to:
strengthen their networks; conduct successful job searches ; change careers; launch consulting businesses; and create balanced "portfolio careers."

Experience in career consulting, executive coaching, general management, business development and international projects. Held positions with regional and international career management firms providing career services, assessment, leadership development, & executive coaching.

She enjoys helping clients strengthen their skills and experience increased success and satisfaction in their personal and professional lives.

MA from the University of Wisconsin-Madison
BA from Macalester College.
