Penny Lamb’s Summary

Today, first impressions of apartment communities are not based on drive by curb appeal, because today's prospect is "surfing" by on the web to get their first view of their new home. My goal is to help property management professionals realize their optimum "Web Appeal" by giving their prospects what they want, a complete inside view of your community right at their fingertips, where they want it, when they want it.

We produce and host on-line HD video tours for the apartment industry to link to existing websites.

Additionally we design/host new websites featuring video tours for those clients who have outdated marketing websites (or possibly no website).

Video Tours are quickly revolutionizing how prospects view apartment websites and providing leasing professionals a fantastic tool to use to lease over the internet, follow up with prospects, and utilize to pre-lease occupied styles.

We host the video tours on an on-line, menued player customized to match your existing website or your newly designed (by us) website.

We encourage clients to compare our tours to any others
on the market.
