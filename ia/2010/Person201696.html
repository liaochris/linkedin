Andrew Cohen’s Summary

Experienced in both web and print design, I know what it takes to transform your ideas into effective, efficient and expressive design.

Well-versed in numerous design disciplines, I can translate your company’s message all the way from initial concept to a compelling and polished final creation: be it a full-service website, user interface design, or corporate branding elements.

No matter the size or scale of your needs, I bring an attention to detail and a comprehensive understanding of the power of design to each unique project.
