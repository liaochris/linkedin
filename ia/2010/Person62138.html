Steu Mann, M.Ed.’s Summary

Life is all about education. Whatever your age is or what you are "doing" at this present moment - doesn't matter - we are all being educated. The nuts and bolts rest in how we educate K12 students as whole beings. The better that education is; the more opportunities will exist for our world to be without violence, hatred, or greed.

My personal journey at this time is to embellish and savor the heartfelt beauty of living life fully. This means unwinding much of the habits and cognitive trends that I established to produce a well rounded business and academic life over the last 30+ years: business owner, project manager, educator, and author. The tangible reward in this beauty is uncovering how "to be" instead of racing "to do" and do some more.

I must admit that releasing my tendency “to do” is one I work on every day. I am committed to growing my inner satisfaction, which at times does include striving to reach specific objectives based in external circumstances. And, there are always specific goals that I strive to reach within me that invariably produce a range of external circumstances that come into my vision. Even though there is a limited degree of satisfaction that comes from doing and doing to reach a goal, there is a greater sense of wholeness and completeness that arises as I build myself spiritually.

I think that being spiritual isn’t a religion. It’s not about one God being better than another. Instead, as I become more spiritual I recognize more of the supreme “I AM” Presence that exists within the heart of each person. Pursuing my spirituality has opened many doors for me: reiki, healing energy, intentional living, more meaningful experiences, more satisfaction with who I am, and the possibility of living life in universal brotherhood with all mankind.

My work now is being an author, consultant, and support system to individuals pursuing a balanced life: spiritual, family, and career.
