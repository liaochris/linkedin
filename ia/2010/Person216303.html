Wolf Hoelscher whoelscher@bullseyewriting.com’s Summary

With 10 years of publishing experience, I have a solid editorial background in the educational, travel, and children’s book markets. By splitting the bulk of my career between these areas, I have continually proven my versatility to employers. From editing manuscripts and managing a staff of editors to writing marketing literature and coordinating large projects, my experience has covered a gamut of tasks.
