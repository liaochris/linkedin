Steven Raz’s Summary

Steven Raz, is a Managing Partner and one of the founders of Cornerstone Search Group, LLC. Steven has been helping companies in the NJ/NYC area recruit, select and hire professionals since 1997. Prior to his involvement in the professional recruiting field, Steven worked as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) for one of the "Big 5" Professional Service Firms (KPMG). Steven is a graduate of The Pennsylvania State University.
