Kelly O'Connor Mitchell’s Summary

HGTV Real Estate Expert, Featured on "My House is Worth What?"
Visit my website at www.kellymitchellinc.com

Ms. Kelly Mitchell has 20+ years of strategic marketing, business development, and sales experience. Growing up in Minneapolis, Minnesota, and attending high school in Orange County, California made Hawaii the next logical step for college and career. As a resident of Honolulu for over 30 years, her expansive knowledge of the local market and her base of professional, business and personal contacts are an asset for buyers and sellers alike. Kelly is a member of the Honolulu Board of Realtors (HBR), the Hawaii Association of Realtors (HAR) and the National Association of Realtors (NAR).

She serves on the Boards of following organizations: Learning for a Lifetime Foundation (Board President), Hogan Entrepreuners Chaminade University(Advisory Board), and Locations Foundation (Board Member).

As a real estate consultant with Prudential Locations and her company, The Kelly Mitchell Group, Ms. Mitchell draws from over 28 years of local Hawaii expertise and knowledge. Additionally, her experience in strategic marketing, business development and digital media expertise provides added value for her Prudential Locations clientele.

She holds a B.B.A. in Marketing from Chaminade University of Honolulu, served as an adjunct Professor in Communications and Entrepreneurship. She also serves on the board for the Prudential Foundation, a non-profit focused on making a difference in our local community and donates 1% of her gross annual income to the Foundation.
