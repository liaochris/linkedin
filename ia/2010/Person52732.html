Chief Executive with extensive experience in multi-site business to business service companies and site-based K-12 education companies. Energetic leader. Proven track record of value creation through building highly motivated teams, generating fundamental strategic and cultural change and delivering the numbers.     (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Real Estate industry)

    1986 — 2000 (14 years )

    VANTAS operated business centers that provided furnished office space and business support services on short notice with flexible contract terms. Improved the operating results of the acquired locations on average by over 30% in the first year. Actualized significant return on investment for all shareholders at the time of the merger.
