Sid Chowdhary, PMP’s Summary

Senior IT executive experienced in working with small and mid-size technology businesses. Ability to manage remote teams and customers, and in leveraging existing capabilities to establish new markets. Strong program management background with extensive IT experience. Leader in the IT community—working with professional organizations both in the USA and in India that connect government to industry on technology policy issues.
