Juergen (Jay) Heuer’s Summary

I create success and change by instilling a drive to excellence through state-of-the-art leadership and innovative processes focused on capturing markets and reducing risk. Because I’m fluent in Technology, Finance and Marketing, I’m able to mold silos into communities and create internal relationships that drive the bottom line.

High-Value Product Development is one of the key components of a successful business, especially in a turnaround situation. That means sharing risk, reducing waste and increasing employee participation. I am experienced in all three, having delivered strategies and implementations for open innovation, low-budget scenarios and low employee morale.

If you are looking for the executive to revitalize your product development processes and department, I am your man. I know how to trade off between short-term deliverables and long-term strategies. I have shown how to get things back on track, how to make people want to come to work again and how to excite customers.
