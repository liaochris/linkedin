Rob Flaherty’s Summary

Rob Flaherty Incorporated is a market solutions consulting company working with owner / developers of resort real estate, fractional real estate, and investors in the hospitality industry. Issues addressed include strategic sales & marketing solutions, brand positioning, site acquisition & disposition, and workouts of under performing assets. Rob Flaherty is a dynamic executive with an impressive history of driving rapid business and sales growth, launching luxury brand segments and maximizing ROI. Rob spent the past eight years in various senior leadership positions with The Ritz-Carlton Club & Residences generating more than $750M in contract sales. He has more than 20 years of progressive career achievement.

The Ritz-Carlton Club is a fractional ownership real estate offering combining the benefits of second home ownership with personalized Ritz-Carlton services and amenities. Designed as a private club, Members have the opportunity to access their Club or other Ritz-Carlton Clubs. The Ritz-Carlton Residences are luxurious residential condominiums which feature the company’s legendary service. Rob is responsible for maximizing productivity and efficiency of marketing initiatives within the East / Caribbean Region (Jupiter, St. Thomas, South Beach and Abaco, Bahamas). For information, please visit www.ritzcarltonclub.com.

Rob Flaherty’s Specialties:

strategic direction & implementation, brand management, budget preparation & execution, creative campaigns & collateral design, community relations, direct & event marketing, multimedia platforms, strategic channel marketing, public relations, real estate sales, staffing & staff management.
