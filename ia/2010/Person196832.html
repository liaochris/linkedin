Su Butcher’s Summary

I see myself as a safe pair of hands, and ‘the grit in the oyster’. Reliable and challenging, I can help you think strategically.

For the last 12 years I have been the Practice Manager to a number of Architects practices in the UK.

I have hands on experience running a business and in growing businesses. Client liaison is a key part of my work, and I am confident explaining design to lay people. I have a wide knowledge of systems and help businesses improve efficiency and focus on their objectives. I am also an active use of social media for professional networking.

Prior to my business life I trained in architecture and became an academic and design teacher. I ran a team of 10 at the University of Liverpool and am experienced managing people in an educational setting. I enjoy teaching, and identifying what people can contribute.

I love networking! Maybe I can help you?
Ring me on 07815 935736 if you think you can help me.

Currently I am looking to develop my current employer's business through partnerships, short and long term opportunities, particularly increasing our work in Essex and London.


Sustainable Affordable Housing
Barefoot & Gilles have considerable experience in sustainable housing and routinely build to CSH Code 4.
We developed the GreenGauge Homes concept, an approach to building housing that can improve on government guidance on energy efficiency and still be built within the HCA Standard Grant Funding Structure.

Residential Care
The care home sector continues to grow and Barefoot and Gilles architects are involved in both new build and alteration works for developers and operators across the sector.

We offer a bespoke solution to maximise development potential whilst recognising that the relationship of the building and surrounding landscape can enhance both the quality of life for residents and the level of care provided.
