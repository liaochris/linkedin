Christopher Hytry Derrington’s Summary

I love solving the complex problems of how to take a new technology to market; building the team, investing seed capital, arranging the angel funding, and setting up the new entity for rapid growth. Fun Stuff!

As a serial entrepreneur, I has served as CEO or interim CEO in 13 companies over the past 16 years, combining skills in product/service innovation, marketing, total P& L responsibility, strategic vision, and tactical leadership critical to success in today’s rapidly changing environment.

The common theme of my endeavors have been to create shareholder value, ranging from startups created to address market opportunities, to providing interim leadership to companies on the edge of insolvency seeking to find a way out, to advising well established enterprises in need of fresh perspective.

I have a demonstrated track record of raising capital though the arrangement of numerous angel equity investments, venture capital financing, and structured senior debt packages totaling over $62 million.

Two years ago, I discovered the needs of the nonprofit industry. My team is using web based technologies to create new tools for nonprofits in fundraising and building donor loyality.

Industries in which I, and my teams, have increased shareholder value include advanced materials, wireless, computer software, computer distribution, aerospace, ecommerce

Startups include: Rural America IT, echoDonations.org, Alta Financial Technologies, IsoTruss Composite Manufacturing, Wind Tower Composities, iLifeSite.com, Safari Beach Software, Arztec Computers, JeNaire Software, Terra Aerospace.

BS in Engineering. Management-Aerospace Engineering. from Missouri University of Science and Technology. Advanced materials is one of my current areas of interest

Please contact me with new technology opportunities or partnerships
