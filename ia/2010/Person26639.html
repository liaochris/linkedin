Bradley Mann, CCIM’s Summary

Real estate investment and development executive with twenty (20) years experience developing and marketing over $720 million of various branded hotel, residential and retail assets. An entrepreneurial spirited, pragmatic and results driven leader with creative aptitude, aesthetic orientation and propensity for detail has successfully consummated all facets of development projects.

Intuition, determination and adaptability have proven to compose an influential problem solver who has utilized effective communication and organization skills to deliver value added leadership, consistently achieving stated goals.

Bradley Mann, CCIM’s Specialties:

Demonstrated strengths include: strategic planning, business development, project conceptualization, project feasibility, budgeting, market and financial analysis, program and design management, equity and debt financing, contract negotiation, development administration and operations integration.
