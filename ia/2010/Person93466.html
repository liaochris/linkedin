Jeff Miller’s Summary

***Industry peeps, I am accepting LinkedIn Requests to connect! Don't worry if we have not done business yet, just personalize your note to connect.***


With over 15 years working in the music business, Jeff brings a vast array of talents & experience to every project. Jeff’s varied roles have included concert producer, merchandising design & sales for bands such as Gov't Mule & Widespread Panic, and art and food vendor manager for the Austin City Limits Music Festival. In addition, he is a highly sought after concert poster artist. His fanatical love for live music and energetic work ethic are well known by anyone who has worked with him. His drive for a successful event is evident by the countless hours of thoughtful planning and insight that can only come from someone with his extensive & varied experience. Jeff’ current projects include Event Production Services (Founder & Director of Events), Cryptical Development Graphic Art & Design (Owner & Artist), and Production Director for several high profile events.

  • Owner/Lead Artist

    Cryptical Development Graphic Art & Design

    (Graphic Design industry)

    January 2000 — Present (10 years 10 months)

    As a rabid music fan and after years of working with bands and in the music industry, Jeff has melded his love for music and art, into what is a seemingly endless run of art and posters. Since 2001, Jeff's posters have been used to promote shows nationwide. His art and logos are used to brand the look and feel for dozens of popular acts, as well as selling goods bearing his images. Check out the ever-growing client list.

    The Art:
    Inspired by 1960's psychedelic poster pioneers such as David Singer and Stanley Mouse. Jeff creates works of art that are accessible to music fans. Strikingly familiar, yet new and fresh, Jeff uses a mixed media approach. He combines both found images and hand drawn elements (often by his wife kristina) mixed together in a witch's pot, otherwise known as Photoshop. Jeff likens using computers as an art medium, to "stepping into a mystical world where anything is possible. Where all your dreams can come true". Just like any medium, you start with just a blank canvas and an idea.

    The Company:
    Cryptical Development is here to service the heart and the mind of both artist and fan. Where all the elements combine to create a memorable image which causes an impact. All with fast, friendly & expert professional graphics service, only an industry veteran can offer. No black t-shirts or artist egos here, just a love for art and music.
