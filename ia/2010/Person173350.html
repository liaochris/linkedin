Rick Wahlberg’s Summary

I solve business problems.

Senior project manager with extensive experience in all areas of IT and business support. Worked in many roles and industries gaining a deep understanding of IT and business process that enhances my ability to deliver business value.

Though I worked my way up on the technology side I've also worked on business planning and strategy to gain a clear understanding of the need to focus on business results.

I believe project leadership is key to success in project management. And that you tailor the methods used to the problem at hand. I've expedited large traditional waterfall projects. And used agile approaches to execute dramatic change in small and affordable increments.

I bring value to an organization by executing projects or mentoring your team to improve their capabilities.
