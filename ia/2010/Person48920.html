Darrah Courter, CDHC’s Summary

Darrah Courter is passionate about helping others. She is a Benefits Specialist that offers financial protection through various insurance products. What sets her apart is that she was trained on the insurance carrier side, then grew her industry knowledge as a General Agent and is now YOUR personal advocate! She knows the insurance industry inside and out! She uses those experiences and product knowledge to provide Ohio clients with a simple solution to their benefits needs.

In addition, the team at ADM Benefits will service you even after your policy is in force!

ADM Benefits represent carriers such as Anthem, Aetna, Medical Mutual, United Healthcare, AFLAC, Humana, MetLife, Hartford, and others.

We also go beyond the benefits. We can also set-up tax saving programs like Section 125's (Premium Only Plans, FSA) and Health Savings Accounts.
