Sherman Chin’s Summary

Sherman started game development in 1999 after graduating with an honors degree in Computing from the University of Portsmouth, UK and obtaining his Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer status.

In 2002, Sherman was awarded a scholarship by the International Game Developer's Association (IGDA). Sherman's business plan writing skills also won him the 2nd-runner up place in Venture 2002.

In April 2003, Sherman and his wife founded their own company, Sherman3D Sdn Bhd ( www.Sherman3D.com ), a graphic design and multimedia development company specializing in 2D game pixel art outsourcing. Sherman3D was initially funded by the Malaysian government Cradle Investment Programme and was accredited Multimedia Super Corridor (MSC) status. On the 7th of July 2006, Sherman3D Multimedia Inc. was incorporated in Vancouver, Canada.
