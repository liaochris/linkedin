Terry Tan’s Summary

mAPPn Inc. is a creative Mobile application and platform development company based in Southern California, which primarily focuses on developing a network of applications spanning various mobile/web platforms such as Android and Facebook. The company's emphasis is largely placed on the development of personalized, localized and cloudlized applications for Android OS .

mAPPn's advanced and creative apps design capabilities allow the company to frequently and rapidly introduce innovative mobile and SNS apps that are designed to cater to the changing customer preferences. Since December 2008, mAPPn has launched Discover, aFile, aHome, Melody Match, Super Jack, Bubble Twirrl and so on. In May of 2009, mAPPn successfully became the strategic partner of T-Mobile and co-published the key app for T-Mobile's MyTouch 3G, FreshFace. Currently, mAPPn apps hit more than 10 million downloads and usage worldwide.

mAPPn Inc. has offices and branches in Los Angeles, Beijing and Xi'An China.
