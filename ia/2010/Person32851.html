Carol Briney’s Summary

I started out my business career working in the corporate arena.
I soon found that I was not employable material. I did not like working for someone else's dream. Working the hours they told me I had to be there. Taking vacations only when I was allowed. Dealing with glass ceilings. Mostly I was unhappy with the lack of loyalty big corporations show their employees. I knew I had a HUGE entrepreneurial spirit so I founded Catering By Carol and Company in 1983. Twelve years later I founded Universal Order. A professional organizing and speaking company located in Pittsburgh, Pa. After many years of extremely hard work. very long hours and two successful companies I finally "got it" there is a better way. I now own http://www,CarolBrineyWorkFromHome.com I have learned to leverage my time and make a wonderful income while most importantly helping others do the same. If you are looking for a work from home business , or if you know someone who is entreprenural minded, and looking to work from home, feel free to give them my information. I would be happy to show them what I have and see if it might be a fit for them.
