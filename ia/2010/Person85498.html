Ira Cohen’s Summary

As a talented and dedicated Multichannel Marketing Consultant, Media Buyer, Project Manager, as well as a Social Media and Engagement Specialist in a range of business-to-business and business-to-consumer market segments, I have a documented track record of developing successful programs that convey a powerful message, increase brand awareness, open market channels, and capture profitable new business.

With a unique ability to establish solid working relationships with numerous stakeholders, I thrive on the challenge of taking on assignments that require a fresh perspective and innovative thinking to meet stretch goals. Using strong analytical skills to identify issues before they become major hurdles, I am considered a “go to person” that will always travel the extra mile to not only meet, but exceed expectations.

I am a high energy, self-motivated performer who thoroughly enjoys the creative process. Delighting customers is what drives me to succeed. My clients have remarked that my positive, can-do attitude, quick thinking, meticulous attention to detail, and integrity are among my strengths. I am passionate about learning, sharing and utilizing emerging marketing trends and technologies, best practices and metrics and a student of multichannel marketing and media, as evidenced in my posts on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and my membership in numerous marketing associations.

I'm currently seeking new projects and career opportunities where my skills and interests are utilized to engage clients, prospects and other stakeholders to grow mutually rewarding and beneficial relationships.
