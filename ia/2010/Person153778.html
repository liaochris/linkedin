Nichole Bazemore’s Summary

Great copy is about more than punchy, snappy, clever sentences.

It's about more than "hot buttons" and "pain points."

It's about building a connection, one that lets your audience know that you're more alike than not. That you speak the same language.

After all, customers don't buy your product or service. They buy your story.

I'm a copywriter, and I tell stories. What's yours?

My story: I have more than 15 years of experience in sales, marketing, and communications. I'm a former TV news reporter, writer, producer, and international flight attendant.
I've also worked as a career coach for individuals transitioning from "the system" to the workforce.

In all of these roles, I developed a few pretty great skills: the ability to listen, a keen eye for observation and detail, an ear for "voice," and an amazing ability to communicate clearly, concisely, and engagingly. In other words, I learned to tell great stories.

I write case studies, brochures, white papers, website content, sales letters, email blasts, memoirs, blogs, ebooks, feature articles, newsletters, TV/radio scripts, ads...if you can name it, I can write it.

Don't see what you need listed here? Call or email me, via this site or nichole@simplystatedsolutions.com.
