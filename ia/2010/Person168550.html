    Thin Film Coating:
    Successfully coated nano thin films of porous TiO2 on the quartz rod using sol gel method
    using self recipe of Solgel solution and ESA(Electrostatic self assembly), Sputtering

    porosity.

    Simulated 3D model of novel photocatalytic reactor using COMSOL (Femlab) and generated
    the flow field from the model was related to the physical model of the reactor, the flow
    rate and reactor configuration was optimized using it, the residence time distribution of
    the reactants along the reactor was evaluated using the model and the reaction kinetics
    was calculated using the UV light transmission along the reactor. The model was used for
    sizing the future large scale reactors.

    Projects:
