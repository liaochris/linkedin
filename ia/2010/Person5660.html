Aleesa Adams’s Summary

Aleesa has worked on big and small budget films, webisodes, music videos, trailers and video tutorials as a Producer, Director and Editor - and most the crew or assistant positions in between.

Recently Aleesa worked as an Associate Producer and Assistant Editor for Larry Jordan, the Final Cut Pro and digital media guru at www.larryjordan.biz, and www.digitalproductionbuzz.com.

Skills:
- Final Cut Studio, Avid, Photoshop, Toast, Filemaker Pro, After Effects, MS Office, ACT, PowerPoint and more.
- Experienced with multi-day trekking and outdoor survival skills. Worked, studied, and traveled all over Europe, South America, China and Australia.
- Photography.
- Fluent in French and Italian, beginner level Spanish.

￼Aleesa is a member of L.A. Final Cut Pro User Group, the Alliance of Women Directors, and Film Independent.
