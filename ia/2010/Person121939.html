Through identifying and executing resource efficiency solutions clients will be able to utilize their financial and labor resources more effectively.

    CCMC’s goal is to enhance both the indoor and outdoor environmental quality through advising clients on how to take advantage of low impact land use, existing building utilization, cost-efficient retrofit, water and materials conservation, renewable energy technologies, emissions and waste minimization, socio-cultural considerations, assessment of economic impacts and most importantly: community and stakeholder engagement and participation. All of these contribute to sustainable development in a more socially and fiscally responsible manner, enhancing corporate image and reputation while improving people’s lives.
