BILL SCHRAER’s Summary

I received my Master of Science degree in Management of Technology from NYU Polytechnic, and I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Technology Education from the State University of Oswego. I have been the Director of Global Training for NOV before joining NOV, I operated a consulting Services Company that specialized in Organizational Development and Training Further, I served in progressive positions with Sony (MRC) and later to Tokyo Electron Limited (TEL), which bought MRC, during this time, I started as a trainer, advanced to the Manager of Domestic Technical Training/Development, and to the Senior Manager of International Support.

I developed and directed training programs for employees on a diverse array of technological equipment and services. I have developed and implemented innovative training methodologies including simulators and animation that incorporate the most up to date, leading-edge scientific and industrial development. In this capacity, I also serve as the liaison between NOV and community colleges and universities to develop educational and training programs for the work force.

In addition, I have served as a Faculty for University of Phoenix teaching various Business and Business Management courses. I am on the Training and Automation committees for the International Association of Drilling Contractors (IADC) and, the Houston Community College Advisory Board for Chemical Engineering, Instrumentation/Controls Technology, Petroleum Engineering Technology, Process Technology, Engineering Technology, and Power Engineering Technology.
