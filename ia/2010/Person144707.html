Martin Middlewood’s Summary

An accomplished writer, Martin has ghosted nearly 200 trade press articles for executives and technology experts in addition to his own writing. He’s written nearly 200 case studies for companies like Microsoft and Intel, as well as numerous white papers. He frequently works with public relations and advertising agencies to create targeted strategic marketing collateral. His corporate and agency work requires him to interview industry experts and company executives as well as manage relationships with their customers and clients.

He held a director position with Waggener Edstrom, where he worked for 12 years. He was instrumental in moving the agency’s PR practice to the Internet. As a marketing communications manager for Tektronix he was responsible for managing multiple product lines.

He founded Frontline Strategies to publish the “Cognizer Report,” a monthly newsletter about intelligent technologies that circulated internationally. Later he evolved the company into a communications consultancy.

He set up editing processes for both Tektronix and Waggener Edstrom, and managed the editing department for Buildernews. He’s worked as a stringer for online and print publications, including CRM Buyer, ChannelPro, and Lacamas Life. His experience in publication writing includes, writing daily news items, features, profiles and Q&As.

Martin Middlewood’s Specialties:

Much of the work Martin does requires him to translate complex subjects into understandable language. He finds the intersection of business, technology and society fascinating. He provides strategic writing support for ad and PR agencies and their clients as well as science- or technology-based companies in a various markets, including construction, healthcare, legal and software.
