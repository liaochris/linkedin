Al Meyers’s Experience

  • Board Member

    Atlanta Music Project

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 2010 — Present (6 months)

    The Atlanta Music Project, Inc (AMP) is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization established in February 2010. Inspired by El Sistema, Venezuela’s nationwide system of youth orchestras, AMP uses the practice and performance of ensemble music to develop youths’ self-esteem, confidence, creativity and expressiveness, while at the same time preventing and combating youth crime and violence. El Sistema was founded in 1975 in Caracas, Venezuela and has since served over 3 million kids, including Gustavo Dudamel, the new conductor of the Los Angeles Philharmonic.

    The mission of AMP is to inspire social change by providing Atlanta’s under-served youth with the opportunity to learn and perform music in ensembles. AMP achieves its mission by using music to prevent youths getting involved in gangs, violence and drugs while at the same developing their confidence, character, creativity and expression.
