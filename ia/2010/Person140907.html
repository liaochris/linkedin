Melita Long’s Summary

Managing Director of Career Coach Consulting since early 2005 and over five years in career coaching and recruitment industries, providing career coaching, writing CVs/Resumes and providing job hunting strategies and interview training. Have worked with clients in New Zealand, Australia and the United Kingdom with very high success rates of clients gaining exciting new careers and better paying roles.

Previously had eight years experience in corporate marketing, four years of which were in the financial services industry in Melbourne and London. Work experience in four countries: UK, Germany, NZ and Australia and speak three languages, English, German and Spanish.

Have volunteered on committees in a marketing and event organising capacity including AIESEC Victoria, the Melbourne and London Junior Chamber of Commerce and AFS Intercultural Exchange organisation in Wellington. Am a member of JCI, AIESEC Alumni, Victoria University Alumni, CPANZ, Wellington Regional Chamber of Commerce and NZIM Central Region.
