    Company Profile: In 1998, Hayes Medical, Inc., a medical device company, began its research, development, production, marketing and sales of world class orthopaedic implants and instruments for the total joint replacement industry. The Company is a recognized leader in the development of modular and materials technologies. Principle areas of expertise include executive leadership and operational execution, corporate finance and equity structures, product development, and human capital development. I personally negotiated the acquisition of several companies and technologies, supplier contracts, and international distribution agreements. Additionally, I negotiated the acquisition of patents from surgeons and inventors and the licensing of over a dozen patents to different companies in exchange for cash, royalties, annual payments, and other consideration. Hayes Medical currently sells orthopaedic implants both domestically and internationally.

    Hayes Medical Consulting, Sacramento, California – 1992 to 1997.
    Founder and CEO
    Company Profile: Hayes Medical Consulting was founded in 1992. The Company served a diverse list of medical device clients as their products progressed through the phases of research, development, regulatory affairs, manufacturing, and distribution. Industries served include joint reconstruction, cardiovascular grafts, kidney dialysis, rigid spinal fixation, podiatric, trauma, OB/GYN, safety needles, and other medical devices. Corporate clients included Colorado (now Medtronic), DePuy, Howmedica, National Medical Specialty, Norian, Techmedica, W.R. Grace and many others. Many clients were individual inventors or surgeon inventors.
