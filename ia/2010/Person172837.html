Richard Mairs’s Summary

Currently COO of Falling Pixel Ltd, I oversee the company's strategic global business development and operations, work with other directors to bring the company vision to life, manage recent growth and future expansion plans, establish new strategic partners for the business, and drive internal innovation within the company.

My passion is in new, innovative and emerging technologies with a focus on digital (but not exclusively).

One of the '09 NCGE Kauffman Global Scholars, I spent 6 months in the U.S. developing new ideas, expanding my network and knowledge at the world renowned Kauffman Foundation in Kansas City. This included time at Harvard Business School, Stanford and M.I.T. understanding how to better commercialize technology, and develop my business acumen as well as visiting innovative companies.

Part of the DigitalCity cluster in the NorthEast (UK), I was part of the first DC Business Growth cohort, which focused on developing knowledge in project management and business development aimed at high growth digital technology ventures.

Proud to have been born and raised in the North East, I actively promote the region and its people. I also manage and run the Adobe Flash Catalyst UK Usergroup. Catalyst is a interaction design tool for rapidly creating application interfaces and interactive content without coding.

Actively involved in encouraging entrepreneurship in young people I'm an ambassador for IWCYC, (a part of the Entrepreneurs Forum) and the Make Your Mark organization..

I'm always keen to develop new ideas, my network and learn about other ventures, startups and technologies. Do get in touch.
