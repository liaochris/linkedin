David A. Rude, MA, PCC, Life & Leadership Coach’s Summary

David Rude is an expert in Customized Leadership Assessment and Development and works with exec teams to increase their leadership awareness, abilities and collective performance.

David designs and delivers innovative, customized training sessions focused on solving real world business problems with solutions that can be implemented immediately. As a highly successful Executive Leadership Coach, he works one-on-one with business professionals across diverse industries, geography and cultures. He has helped many business owners and managers analyze their leadership style and skills and refine their strengths so as to be better able to create more realistic and meaningful goals and make changes that measurably improve their performance and business. His clients have included companies from the nonprofit sector, inc., environmental and educational and banking industry and other financial institutions.

He has spoken at colleges, universities as well at local, regional and statewide associations. He recently began work in Ireland and the UK.

For nearly 30 years prior to starting his own company in 2005, David successfully worked as a college/university instructor, principal and superintendent and served as a Master Teacher. David has been published in both professional journals and local and regional newspapers. He was awarded (2004) the Best of Newspapers in Educ Award in the General Excellence category by the Natl Newspaper Assn for efforts in literacy. David was awarded 1st prize among Calif newspapers for the most successful promotional program for a literacy campaign for 2001-02 by the Calif Newspaper in Educ Assn.
