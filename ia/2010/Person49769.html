Dave Nelsen’s Summary

Executive management - President of Dialog Consulting Group (social media strategy for business - www.WeSocialize.biz). Founder and former CEO of www.TalkShoe.com where anyone can run their own "internet talk show" connecting live with their on-line groups - up to 250 people talking and chatting via landline, cell phone, and computer (VoIP). Previously co-founded and led CoManage Corporation as CEO - sold to Syndesis in 2005.

Professional speaker / Vistage CEO network - social media for business (www.get121.biz/Speaking.html).

E&Y Entrepreneur of the Year (2000); PTC (#1 tech trade association) CEO of the Year (2004); raised more than $70 million in angel and venture funding.

Business development - worked for FORE Systems developing partnerships with Microsoft, HP, Nortel, Sony and others.

Product management - defined and launched AT&T InterSpan's ATM Service; product managed FORE Systems' telecom and video products.

Dave Nelsen’s Specialties:

Using social networking and social media to improve business performance. Starting a tech company, hiring the best people, raising angel and venture capital, building a team, negotiating partnerships, selling enterprise software, running a consumer internet website, creating a killer corporate culture!
