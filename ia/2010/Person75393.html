Gillian Renault’s Summary

In this economic downturn, many companies are cutting back on advertising and ramping up their PR. If this describes you and your company, we can help you prepare your spokespeople for media interviews. We guarantee they will be more confident, focused and effective.

For ten years, throughout the U.S. and internationally, Gillian has coached CEOs and senior management in a variety of fields -- from healthcare to entertainment, businesses and not-for-profits.

Our in-depth workshops give clients greater confidence when handling difficult interviews. We also help clients develop media messages that really work. You will learn how to craft concise talking points and develop sound bites that reporters will love.

During the training, mock, on-camera interviews and candid, constructive feedback give you the practice you need to ace important interviews.

Our presentation training gives clients the tools they need to effectively deliver their message to large or small groups.

Gillian is also a speaker and panelist.

We also consult with a strategic planning firm, Level Smart Consulting, to help nonprofits map out their future in positive and sustainable ways.

Gillian Renault’s Specialties:

We have worked with several clients in the disabilities field, and specialize in People First Language.

We're also specialists in the entertainment field, TV in particular and have trained on-camera talent and producers for everything from "Tonight" and "Today" show appearances, to AP and the New York Times.
