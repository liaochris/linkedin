Jeff Herrera’s Summary

I am a business owner and Entrepreneur focused on business development, goal achievement, marketing and growth of a web based service, www.RequestMaster.com.

Requestmaster.com is a free service and resource for Homeowners, Consumers and Real Estate agents. Requestmaster.com helps you to get quotes from local businesses such as Home Inspections, Contractors and local professionals. We are also working towards expansion of the types of businesses available through Requestmaster.com

At Requestmaster.com one of our main goals is to gather local businesses who believe in being part of a network where information sharing, collaboration, providing referrals is valued. Requestmaster is actively seeking Entrepreneurs, Business owners and Professionals who believe in constantly improving their businesses and who would like to grow using Requestmaster.com as a tool to increase their bottom line. In this day and age, if you are not continuously improving your quality and performance you will definitely get left behind.

If you would like to consider joining our network or exploring Requestmaster.com I would encourage you to register your business. You may do so at http://www.requestmaster.com/business.register.php

I am also seeking like minded business owners to create win-win strategic relationships. I am seeking opportunities with existing networks, advertising agencies, local construction material distributors or manufacturers and individuals who would like to be affiliates. Send me a message if you have a compatible, but non competing service and would like to discuss the possibilities.

My past experiences have been in Business development, Marketing, Residential and Commercial Inspections, Chemistry, Environmental laboratory testing, Field construction testing and inspections, Nuclear Industry inspections and Quality assurance both Federal level and at the private level.
