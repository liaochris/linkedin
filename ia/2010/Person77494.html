Greg Bayer’s Summary

Greg is the General Manager of Adknowledge's Affiliate Division and holds over 15 years experience in affiliate marketing and digital media. He began his career with barnesandnoble.com where he oversaw the launch of its affiliate network, and was responsible for driving the strategy and growth for one of the earliest and most successful affiliate programs on the web today. In 1998, Greg joined LinkShare as the company’s Sr. Director of Product Development and was responsible for overseeing product strategy, planning, management and partnerships for LinkShare's affiliate network. Following LinkShare, Greg joined Sony and managed the team responsible for launching Sony’s online digital bookstore, the e-commerce companion to its breakthrough Reader product. Sony’s Reader received “best of 2006” awards from Engadget, Popular Mechanics and Travel & Leisure Magazine. In 2007, Greg returned to affiliate marketing and joined Epic Advertising (fka AzoogleAds) as the Vice President of Product where he oversaw the company’s product strategy and development. Greg holds a B.A. in English and American Studies from Princeton University.
