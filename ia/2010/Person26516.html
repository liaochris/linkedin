Brad Haley’s Summary

I am currently working as a freelance broadcast television editor. My post production experience includes episodic television, short film, feature film sound, direct marketing and advertising, broadcast news, and corporate video.

As the senior staff editor at Alchemy, I was often tapped to direct segments and shows because of my proximity and first hand knowledge of the material that needed to be shot to achieve the ultimate goal. My talents in broadcast design, titling, animation, compositing, color correction and sound design were also employed throughout my projects.

My experience in broadcast news editing was a superb training ground in meeting multiple tight deadlines everyday. This is where I learned the art of speed editing and handling high stress situations.

picture and sound editing, post supervision, directing and production
