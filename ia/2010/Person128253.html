Larry Singer’s Summary

A seasoned professional with over 12 years experience with the IT Staffing arena, with both full life cycle recruiting and Account Management/Executive side. Successful in both candidate AND client driven markets. I've worked with dot.com companies such as Idealab, eToys, CitySearch, NetZero, CarsDirect, Edmunds and more traditional companies such as Warner Bros, Disney, Farmers Insurance, Honda, Toyota, The State Bar of California, City and County of Los Angeles, LA DWP, and have placed individuals in almost all IT positions, from the Network Side to the Application Development side, Desktop to CTO and most everything in between. I provide clients with pure contract/consultant individuals as well as more "project based/project driven" candidates and candidates looking for FTE (full time employment).

WeStaffIT, Inc. and J. Geller & Associates, Inc. have joined forces to provide the best in IT Contract Staffing Services
to the Information Technology arena in both Southern and Northern California as well as beyond.
With a combined 35+ years of industry experience, we’re able to provide complete sourcing and “Just-In-Time-
Delivery” of Information Technology Professionals to Corporate IT Departments nationwide.

In addition, we’re also able to provide Payroll Services and act as immediate “Employer of Record” at a moments notice for any skilled individuals that you’ve identified for yourself and would like to bring on to support your project.
