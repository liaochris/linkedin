Skip Wilson (PONY)’s Summary

I have never been one for Corporate America, I went to Yale it was too simple, I worked at IBM and Northrop Gruman both were to congested with bureaucracy. I never fit the “mold” as they like to call it. Iam a free spirit. I am like a wild pony roaming free on the plains I am.

I was almost a Manager 4 times in Corporate America and I understand that I need to manage the people, but I would rather turn those people into leaders themselves and be their friend. So that's what I do, I don't give people a fish, I teach them to garden (I'm a member of PETA so I don't agree with eating animals and fishing is a bad industry). If you didn't know that was a play on Confusious.

In 92 I was hostage of LOVE! Yes love, I met the love of my life (now deceased so you ladies have a chance). She introduced me to a new line of spiritualism that I never before thought possible. We travel from Ashram to Ashram across the U.S. studying there I was told that “God is everywhere, God is in you”. That clicked for me and that means I’m a person incarnation of God, I am my own God.

I don’t necessarily believe that I am God himself or that he even exists. But we have the power to choose who we associate with, how we react with everything, and we are the only ones in control of our path and destiny. I start the Be Your Own God motivational inc. Here I teach people to be the master of the universe, to improve their perception, clarity and the visualization process.
