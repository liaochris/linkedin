Gareth Jones’s Summary

I am the managing director of Learnalot Ltd and Quak Multimedia Ltd.

Learnalot is an exciting educational portal that uses case study problems to engage and challenge its learners. Each case study is different but they all provide pupils, teachers and parents with problems that are fun and engaging while also being a rich learning experience.

Quak Multimedia is an eLearning and multimedia company that offers bespoke development of eLearning, multimedia and game software for clients who have specific needs, and we also develop our own line of products that are in some way aimed towards the education market.
