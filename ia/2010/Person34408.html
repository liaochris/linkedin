Christopher Lees’s Summary

Results-oriented professional with 19 years experience in law enforcement, loss prevention, and security management. Served in the U.S. Air Force for two tours as a Security Police Officer. Worked at Nordstrom for 5 years as Regional Investigator, Loss Prevention Manager, and Area Manager before moving on to The Home Depot where I spent 5 years as Loss Prevention Manager, District Asset Protection Manager, and Corporate Security Divisional Investigator. Also was with the Walt Disney Company as Regional Loss Prevention Manager for 9 months before joining Verizon Wireless as a Corporate Security LP Area Investigator in 2009. Bachelor of Science in Organizational Security and Management, and Masters in Administration of Justice and Security with hopes of teaching some day.
