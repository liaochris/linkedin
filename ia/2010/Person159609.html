Patrea Bullock’s Summary

I am on a mission to educate discerning consumers, who understand the need to invest in ageless skin, about the latest in non-invasive, skin care technology from the most innovative company in the history of the industry.

People thought it “too good to be true” when man walked on the moon. I will tell you about products that you would think “too good to be true”. You really can look in the mirror and say “wow, I love the way my skin looks.

Having been a trial lawyer for top insurance companies for 14 years, I was no stranger to research. When I turned 43 and saw dull, dry, saggy skin with ever increasing wrinkles, I began to research skin care. I discovered a company (huge in Japan and Europe) with 125 full-time PhD level scientists that created cutting edge technologies. After using one patented cleansing system, people noticed a huge difference. Then, a small hand held electronic device, made it to the US. After witnessing this device literally iron wrinkles away and performing market research New Age Vogue, Inc. was born.

I offer personal skin consultations and work diligently to educate top medical estheticians around the country to bring these products to those who need to stay ageless. The age of your skin is your business, how young it looks is mine!

As the organizer of a community “Meetup” group, called the Coffee Break Networkers I allow local business owners a place to meet other business owners, share resources and introduce who they are, what they do and what sets them apart from their competitors. The group meets weekly with the intention of helping each other build and grow.

As a leadership member of The Association of Women Entrepreneurs of Greater Sacramento I help connect business women to the education, support and motivation to build a successful business and a balanced life. My major role is to research, interview and secure professional, expert speakers for our monthly Business Success Seminars.

Civil defense trial work, negotiation, sales practices, training, corporate creation, taxation, networking. social media, etc.
