Dave Ingram’s Summary

Co-founder and CEO of www.brownbook.net, the free worldwide business directory that anyone can edit, currently listing 35 million businesses worldwide. Brownbook was inspired by www.wikipedia.org, www.craigslist.com, www.wikinomics.com and www.thelongtail.com

I started selling e-commerce systems back in 1995 before anyone else, then created a range of e-commerce products for www.scoot.com when it first floated on FTSE and NASDAQ, I've managed global e-commerce products for Infobank, before helping Sony develop their sales process for a range of digital asset management products. I've consulted for UK blue-chips helping them monetize their online products, and helped take a startup out of a garage in Redditch to 30 people in Oxford and LA.
In 2008 I co-founded Brownbook.net with long-time colleague Marc Lyne (www.linkedin.com/in/marclyne) and now growing it out across the globe.

Aside from my main businesses I put together an extreme sports recreation concept for Extreme Sports Channel, set up a wakeboarding lake in Milton Keynes and spent a year playing a lead part in a London West End musical.

~Leisure~
Wakeboarding - I've competed in two wakeboard world championships where I placed 3rd and 4th in my division
Extreme Kayaking - Generally throwing myself down Alpine rivers and creeks with the spring snow-melt or bashing weirs in flood
Snowboarding - As often as I can go (which is never often enough). Mostly the European Alps, but recently up at Tahoe
Surfing - a more recent activity since I moved to San Francisco in Feb 1010. Learning fast!

~Family~
I'm married with a gorgeous young daughter (aren't they all) and we have two dogs. We have a barn conversion in the UK's Test Valley (great fly fishing, not that I've ever tried it), but we're currently living in Mill Valley, overlooking San Francisco, CA
