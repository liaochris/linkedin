Luke li’s Summary

I am Luke Li,
Otherwise known as lukeiscrazyman.

I was made in China,
And developed in the U.S.

I have a BA in interior design
And an MA in advertising.

I am an idea person, an art director both visually and conceptually,
And a creative presenter.

I’m also a dancer, a TV host, and a full-time crazy man.

I am a new-generation creative that’s passionate about technology, culture, and I believe I am a representative of innovative media techniques.

I developed those techniques through my internship at Modernista!, a creative shop in Boston. I was inspired by their creative energy and helped to create stop motion videos, viral videos, and launched campaigns for Hummer. I also had the opportunity to understand clients such as Cadillac, (Red), and Palm.

It was there that I began my life as a digital thinker, a blogger, a social media expert, a viral video communicator, and an advertising observer. You can learn more about me by googling lukeiscrazyman.
