Rod Smith’s Summary

A highly analytical operational professional with strong collaborative leadership skills and well-developed business acumen. Coupled with that, having the ability to present complex new ideas with clarity and simplicity. Recognized for an ability to provide innovative and effective customer focused solutions. My passion is to create operational efficiencies within organizations through effective management, team building, and organizational improvement.

The past twelve years have been as President of R-Squared. Primarily focused on data storage solutions and document imaging products.

Prior to R-Squared: an Operations Manger for Vangard Technology and a Senior Business Analyst with Fujitsu Computer Products of America. At Fujitsu, developed numerous business plans, participated on a five member Operational Team for the North America Repair facility, and co-founded a multi-million dollar new business involving reverse logistics.

Rod Smith’s Specialties:

Since 1984, R-Squared has distributed, supported and serviced Fujitsu storage, document imaging and printer products. Professional services and support of current Fujitsu products as well as discontinued products is our specialty. We have been a "ProPartner" reseller for Fujitsu Computer Products of America, since 2002.

We also provide parts and consumable support for document imaging scanners manufactured by Kodak, Ricoh, Bell & Howell, and Panasonic.
