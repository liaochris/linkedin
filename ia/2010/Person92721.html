Jeannette Paladino’s Summary

How successful is your business in communicating with employees, customers and the public? Are your key messages positively impacting your brand and reputation? Strategic communications can enhance a company’s image and be a key driver of its success.

I can bring a company’s values, strengths and messages to life with business writing that is clear, concise and compelling.

Over the years, I’ve helped improve business communications for a lot of organizations – either inside companies, as a senior communications executive, or in prominent public relations firms and, now, as a business writer.

My work spans marketing communications, training and development for brand-name organizations such as --
- Deloitte
- Deloitte & Touche
- DTT
- Marsh & McLennan
- JPMorgan Chase
- Citicorp
- KPMG
- A.T. Kearney
- PricewaterhouseCoopers

I’ve also been fortunate to work for nonprofits like --
- American Heart Association
- Aging in America
- Center for the New American Workforce at Queens College
- Women’s Venture Fund.

A number of honors have come my way: Fellow of the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA); New York Women in Communications (NYWIC) Matrix Award as PR woman of the year, and PRSA-New York’s John W. Hill Award for outstanding leadership in the practice of public relations and service to PRSA and the public.
