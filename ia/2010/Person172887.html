Rich Ramsell’s Summary

In my 18 year career I have amassed the comprehensive understanding and practical experience of multi-color publication printing and packaging production processes, advertising, direct mail, trade shows and final press run experience. I'm the bees knees when mixed together with photographers and talent, or products, or talented products. Introduced early in all phases of project management, I can deliver and ensure high quality outcomes even when constrained by time and budget. I'm all the rage when it comes to articulating design preferences and choices to creative director, staff designers, sales and support team, clients, pre-press and print vendors. Since I'm well organized, I'm able to initiate and complete multiple projects under difficult situations. In response to frequent change and criticism of work, I do so positively. Usually.

Word on the street is I have thorough knowledge of several Mac-based applications. For starters, Photoshop and I have been a thing since the late eighties. Illustrator and Quark are long time friends as well. Applications such as InDesign, Dreamweaver, Flash, Fireworks, Powerpoint, Acrobat and DAM systems showed up later but we get along swell.

Rich Ramsell’s Specialties:

Print, packaging and web design. Brand identity, presentations and large format graphics.
