Emmanuel Essien’s Summary

Emmanuel is a Certified Information System Auditor (CISA) with a Masters in Business Administration (MBA). His business and management skills are complemented by an extensive career in the corporate environment and as an independent consultant. Emmanuel has a proven track record and continues to consult to the likes of Ernst &Young and Deloitte. He is an entrepreneurial individual who has a combination of technical and business expertise. He is diligent and enjoys working with others in a collaborative manner.

Until recently he worked as a Senior Auditor within the Group Internal Audit division of Absa Bank in South Africa, where Emmanuel added experience in the banking and finance industry. Prior to joining Absa, Emmanuel was a Senior Audit Consultant with the chartered accounting firm, SizweNtsaluba vsp.

He has a passion for the investment banking and asset management sector with great emphasis on Africa market environment. Emmanuel has consulted widely on Investment Analysis, Capital Raising and Business Valuation in South Africa, Nigeria, Ghana, Swaziland, Mozambique, Namibia and Zimbabwe.

Emmanuel is currently enrolled and studying towards his Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) qualification.

Emmanuel Essien’s Specialties:

Emmanuel presently consults on Investment and Financial Management, Business Development, Risk Management and Governance, Project Management and Audit, Information and Communication Technology, Assurance and Forensic.
