Dragos MANAC’s Summary

2009+

Advisor for a few Romanian technology companies:
Appnor - Romanian Cloud Integrator, operated by Sinergetic Clouds

Hotnews - Online Sales Consultant for Hotnews.ro, the biggest online newspaper in Romania

Blogger, covering technical, entrepreneurial and personal development issues (http://www.manac.ro). Designated 2008's Best Business Blog in Romania.

2002-2008

Lead Operations of System & Network Solutions (http://www.SNS.ro), a company specialized in programming and system/network administration. Driven by the Open Source philosophy SNS was one of the first companies that covered the niche of Linux/UNIX systems in Romania.

Initiator of the Romanian Black List (http://rbl.sns.ro), the first Romanian antispam list.

Member of the Association for Technology and Internet (http://www.apti.ro).

Advocate of the Linux operating system and the Open Source movement, member of the Romanian Linux User Group (http://www.lug.ro) and author of Linux Starter's Kit (http://lsk.sns.ro), the first Romanian manual for Linux newbies.

Part time journalist. I have published regularly in PcWorld, ComputerWorld, NetworkWorld, Academia Catavencu and from time to time in other newspapers and magazines.

Founder and co-editor of Solvabil.ro (http://solvabil.ro) an educational personal finance website.

Founder of Ghelir.ro (http://www.ghelir.ro), a one product per day online shop massively successful in Romania.
