Andy Grant’s Summary

Email me on andy.grant@bluemarblerecruitment.co.uk
Call me on 07720 841 444

Following graduation I spent a number of years in engineering where I designed optical systems for imagesetters and scanners for the pre-press industry.

I subsequently moved into recruiting personnel with a similar technical background and I am now in my third recruitment role.

I have recruited engineers, project managers, applications and sales people in fields including scientific, software, electronic hardware, mechanical, semiconductor process, buyers etc.

I am always interested in speaking to clients & candidates in connection with roles in embedded software & electronic hardware design (analogue & digital), mechanical CAD design, purchasing, quality, sales/marketing/applications, project management, optical design, optoelectronics (lasers, optical fibres etc.), electron microscopy, EMC, technical support, service engineering, proposals engineering etc.
