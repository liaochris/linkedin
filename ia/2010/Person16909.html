Art Seaborne’s Summary

Founder/CEO American Venture Capital and President/Trustee of Southeast Capital Investors; a Real Estate Investment Trust. With 35 years of financial and investment experience structuring over $100 million dollars in start-ups and early stage funding. Serving as special advisor to Ponderosa Partners, Alternative Capital Resources and Granite Woods Ventures.

We are the Managing Partner for several strategic funds, each with different investment criteria. We are passionate about the venture capital industry and making a significant contribution to new businesses.

Art Seaborne’s Specialties:

Creating value added solutions and out-of-the-box paradigms for our current and future portfolio companies.
We continue to seek new opportunities to invest in high growth companies with great business concepts and exceptional management teams.
