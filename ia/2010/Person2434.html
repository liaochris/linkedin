Adam Justin’s Summary

Adam works as a Financial Services Staff at Ernst & Young in Minneapolis providing accounting and tax services to financial services companies in the asset management industry.

Adam recently graduated from the Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota. Adam completed concentrations in accounting, finance, and political science. At the University Adam was a credit analyst with the Carlson Fixed Income Fund which manages assets of over $16 million. Adam focused on the finance sectors of insurance and banking. During 2008 Adam and his peers returned 7.8%.

Also as an undergrad Adam spent a semester at the top ranked business school in Asia, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Adam has interned as a Financial Analyst at MedicalCV, a medical device company developing laser-powered minimally invasive surgical products. At MedicalCV Adam designed and implemented an inventory management system, assisted with the development of internal financial and accounting controls, and worked on a variety of other projects.


Adam has personal interests in international business trends such as IFRS, international capital flows, foreign exchange rates, interest rate differentials, international tax, and developing economies such as China.
