Pablo Goldbarg’s Summary

I am a bilingual writer/producer and software engineer born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. After ten years working in the I/T industry I moved to U.S. and graduated from The New School's Masters in Media Studies in New York. During that period I made two short films, and participated in two international film festivals in Havana and Los Angeles.

After graduation I wrote for Remezcla.com, Cinema Tropical and Cineaste, I worked as a writer/producer in the advertising industry, and I was hired as a bilingual language consultant for Steven Soderbergh's double feature film about Che Guevara. Tim Burton recently chose one of my tweets for his new project @ burtonstory.com—it's a tiny collaboration, but I'm proud of it!

I currently represent Zarlek in the U.S., an Argentine production company. I am the Editor for a soon-to-be-launched website. I also work as a freelance copywriter, translator and producer, and I have my own bilingual blog called REALFIC(c/t)ION.

Beyond my professional career, I wrote a feature screenplay and a fiction book, both in Spanish, now in a polishing phase and looking for producer and publisher.
