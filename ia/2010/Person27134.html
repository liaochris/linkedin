As a recent MSBA graduate who specialized in Marketing, and did my undergraduate work in Integrated Marketing Communications, I have a solid background in the knowledge and expertise needed to make an immediate impact. In my course work I completed hypothetical work for global brands, as well as actual marketing plans and consulting work for startups and local brands.
