Ted McGrath’s Summary

I am a sales trainer, professional speaker, business coach, leader and agent of change, who is driven by my passion to help others take their professional and personal lives to the next level. My strong business background, homodynamic certification and inspirational speaking abilities bring my clients the very desired results.

I attribute my success at a young age to my mentor I met at his first job, New York Life, when I was 21. I experienced tremendous success within the first year at New York Life. By age 23, I became partner which allowed me to lead 120 financial advisors. I received the very rare distinction of becoming a Million Dollar Round Table Producer in the Insurance Industry. At age 28, I was ranked as the 5th partner out of 500. It wasn’t until I was offered another dream promotion that I realized my deep desire to help others achieve their success. Despite others thinking I was “crazy”, I left my high paying career at New York Life. It has proven to be my best decision.

I have 10 years experience coaching entrepreneurs and Sales Professionals. I am a 7 year member of Strategic Coach, a top entrepreneurial coaching program. I have facilitated several mastermind groups which helps others live their passion and vision by learning sales skills and goal cultivation; taught “How to increase your Confidence” for 4 years; attended a masters course in Creativity in Business- Stanford Course taught by Michael Ray in 2005 and trained in Master in Influence and Sales under Marshall Sylver.

In addition to teaching new skill sets, I believe deep internal work needs to be done to make significant external change. I have traveled to India to receive training and certification in "mastering ones inner fears" from Oneness University, India; have been mentored by The Elite in the Personal Growth Industry and received certification in process of Holodynamics - Helping others transform limiting beliefs and reach their full potential
