Bret Pawlowski’s Summary

For 13 years Brands In Motion and our divisions have specialized in integrated digital and mobile branding for a select group of clients. Our client list includes Fortune 500 companies and brands such as Jack Daniel's, Procter & Gamble, Paramount Pictures, Hilton and Infiniti. From gigantic digital billboards in high traffic areas, JumboScreens at races, concerts and events to connecting via personal mobile devices our company delivers unique brand content to consumers when and where they are the most receptive.

Brands In Motion's array of mobile solutions include: interactive applications, SMS, check-in / rewards,mobile sweepstakes, mobile couponing, mobile booking, voting & polling campaigns, trivia applications, mobile App / Web development and social mobile advertising.

Our "MobileCoupons" mobile delivery platform is optimized to enable clients to "rapid launch" and securely manage their mobile coupon programs through a Web-accessible services platform that requires little up-front financial or resource investment. We have perfected the art of mobile performance marketing with innovative redemption tracking and mobile booking services.

Additionally, our GoSquads, Brand Teams and Activation Ambassadors add a human touch to digital campaigns by interacting with audiences and engaging consumers for specific results. By strategic interaction our unique blend of services bridge the gaps between brands and consumers using Mobile Coupon Marketing with Digital Mobile Billboards and Out-Of-Home Media.

Brands In Motion services America, Mexico and Canada from our Headquarters in Las Vegas, Nevada. European Brands In Motion initiatives are managed via the BIM European partner branch based in Belgium.

Mobile Brand Marketing, Digital Media Development, Content Management, Proximity and Mobile Coupons, Interactive Consumer Promotions, Guerrilla and Event Marketing, Out-Of-Home Media Placement and Digital Media Buying for LED, LCD and 6D Displays and Interactive Kiosks. Check-In and Reward Programs for (LBS) Location Based Service and SMS Promotions.
