tobias gillberg’s Summary

I graduated with a Masters degree in Computer Science 2005. I then firmed my own company in Marbella Spain in the mortgage business and set up a website for it and got into SEO as a "cheap" way of marketing our business

After that I took a job as a SEO technician at www.seo.se working with clients from all sorts of businesses. I stayed with them from 2006-2008. By then I had my own PPC, SEO agency, working with everything from adsense sites, ppc arbitrage and clients.

Getting a bit bored with that I started working with another agency which led me to my role as a SEO manager on Servitor Marketing on Curacao (island close to Venezuela) in January 2009 where I currently live and work.

My goals are to learn more of course and also get more into the business side of things.
