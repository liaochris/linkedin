Sandra Placide, CSEP’s Summary

Combining the science of event planning with the art of design TM.

Illumination is a boutique event planning and design firm based in New York City that serves corporate and social clients. Sandra is a Certified Special Event Professional. This designation is the hallmark of professional achievement in the special events industry. Her experience is based on a decade in the advertising industry and six years working as a freelancer for many production companies; four of those years working for a prominent event producer developing proposals, planning events, executing marketing strategies, procuring vendors, and coordinating logistics. Highlight events include the Clinton Global Initiative, Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week, 2004 Republican National Convention (Utah & Texas Delegations), IMG New York Marathon, U.S. Open, and Agenda XPO + Conference.

Sandra’s warm personality, calm demeanor, attention to detail, and commitment to high-quality service has garnered an excellent reputation in the industry. As President of Illumination, she intersects her experiences in advertising with her years in planning events to create custom programs for her clients that communicate their message or feeling. They trust her to plan their event and steer their vision through the world of entertaining. She closely manages her select group of seasoned vendors and professionals for each event and uses resourcefulness, ingenuity, and a sharp eye on the budget to produce your corporate or social event into the experience you desire.
