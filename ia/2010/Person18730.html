Ava Diamond’s Summary

Expertise...

As President of Feisty Woman Productions and The Diamond Success Group, Ava is a sought-after professional speaker, consultant, facilitator, and workshop leader.

Ava is the author of Great Quotes from Feisty Woman, which combines quotations from famous women with Ava’s down-to-earth wisdom.

She is also a featured author in Real World Management Strategies that Work, Speaking of Success, with Jack Canfield and Ken Blanchard, and Mission Possible, with Stephen Covey and Brian Tracy. She holds a Masters Degree in Management.

Experience...

Ava speaks from over 20 years experience as a Fortune 100 manager, executive director of a not-for-profit agency, successful entrepreneur, and organizational consultant. She distills this experience into powerful lessons that inspire immediate action and get results.

What is Ava Known for?

Inspiration: Ava helps people get a bigger vision for their work and their lives and helps them figure out how to get there from where they are.

Empowerment: She is known for helping people change their mindset and take action. Ava helps them discover their own path to increased success.

Motivation: By having them really feel what’s possible, Ava gets people fired up, powered up, and ready to really go for it!

Tools: Ava gives people tools, tips, and techniques they can apply immediately.

Excitement: Her passion, enthusiasm, energy and humor engage people in her message.
