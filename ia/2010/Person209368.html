Uday Subbarayan’s Summary

An enthusiastic and dynamic technical leader of product innovation and development with excellent leadership and mentoring abilities. More than 14 years of web, software & systems engineering experience in design & development of various products. Developed and delivered web infrastructure products, internet-scale cloud services, private cloud storage, application networking appliances, e-commerce servers, virtual call center products and highly secured financial products. Thrived and enjoyed working in both big corporations and startups.

Technical expert in Distributed Computing, Cloud Computing, SOA/WebServices/J2EE/Middleware, Grid computing, XML Networking Appliances, Databases/LDAP, Storage and Computer-Telephony Integration. Good exposure to Mozilla XPCOM,XUL, JavaScript & Objective-C/ iPhone technologies.

Have applied for several US patents in Grid computing and Application-Networking technologies.

Strong international experience including 4 years in Switzerland and India, followed by 10 years in Silicon Valley. Experience in establishing and leading geographically distributed development and QA teams. Excellent experience working with business teams such as product management, marketing and sales to articulate technology and products to
customers and ISV’s.

Great communication, analytical skills with strong educational background. A hard worker, fast learner and team player.
