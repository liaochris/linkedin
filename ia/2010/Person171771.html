Rhonda Shear’s Summary

For years lived and worked in Los Angeles in the Entertainment field. Acting, hosting in numerous televison shows and films. Also, headlined as a stand-up comic and produced and starred in stand up comedy show entitled, Rhonda Shear's "Up All Night" Pajama party. For eight years hosted the very successful late-night televsion show "USA: Up All Night" on USA network.

Most recently, Shear has added designer/developer of women's intimates and apparel to her resume. Van Fagan and Rhonda Shear-Fagan are partners in Shear Enterprises LLC. The company designs and manufactures women's shapewear, intimate apparel, bath and beauty products that are sold on HSN (Home Shopping Network US), TSC (The Shopping Channel, Canada.) Also, Rhonda Shear line can be found on www.MaisonRouge.com and www.HSN.com. Shear Enterprises also owns "Maison Rouge," an exclusive retail boutique in St. Petersburg, Florida. The Shear Brand caters to all women, all ages, all sizes. "Behind every great woman, is a great behind."

The most recent endeavor is a new beauty and bath line called Rhonda Shear Enchante. It will debut on HSN in early 2008.
