Jeff Korhan’s Summary

Jeff Korhan is a new media marketer who works with entrepreneurs and small business owners to strategically use social media and Internet marketing for maximizing Web visibility, reputation, and referrals. Jeff blogs at http://jeffkorhan and resides in Naperville, IL.

Jeff Korhan’s Specialties:

Social media and Internet marketing, the green industry, Web marketing strategies, relationship selling, and entrepreneurship trends.

Jeff Korhan’s Experience

  • New Media Marketer, Consultant, and Speaker

    True Nature Inc

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 1997 — Present (13 years 2 months)

    Jeff Korhan is an award winning entrepreneur, marketer, and green industry expert. He applies over three decades of marketing experience to help companies maximize marketing results by building communities of loyal fans and customers, especially with social media and Internet marketing.

    After earning his MBA, Jeff began his career as a Fortune 50 Sales and Marketing Manager. He later started and operated a landscape company that was twice named Business of the Year in one of the fastest growing communities in the country. After 20 successful years he sold his business and continues to consult with that company today. Jeff is a former board member of The Professional Landcare Network (PLANET) and a green industry expert with credentials as a Licensed Landscape Architect, Certified Arborist, and Certified Landscape Professional.

    Jeff is a thought leader whose experiences includes triathlons, cliff diving, and meditating in the deserts of India. Today he maintains a busy schedule of speaking and consulting on the topics of new media marketing and social entrepreneurship. He is a faculty member of social entrepreneurship at North Central College in his hometown of Naperville, Illinois, a new media columnist for GIE Media - the largest publisher in the green industry, and a Leadership Consultant for the National Speakers Association. He is the author of Strategic Social Media Marketing for Entrepreneurs: Take Control of Your Business Growth Now!
