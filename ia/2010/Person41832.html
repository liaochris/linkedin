Chris Luther’s Summary

I have a passion for creatively combining technology and training to break down barriers (organizational/knowledge) and create strategic advantages!

Most recently I’ve been consulting with leading technology firms on designing partner channel certification programs to increase quality, build sales and enable rapid growth. In addition to the design and delivery these training programs, I’ve often contributed to teams selecting and deploying of performance support systems. Depending on the business requirements, these systems could be Content Management Systems (CMS), Learning Management Systems (LMS) or combination (LCMS). Though I strive to be platform agnostic, through these engagements I’ve built-up broad experience with a few proprietary and open-source platforms including Salesforce, SharePoint (with Brightwork), SumTotal, ILIAS, Moodle and Drupal.

I continue to follow my muse by writing and presenting on topics related strategic learning and rapid instructional design techniques. I remain involved in ASTD (American Society of Training and Development) at both the local and national levels, and maintain memberships in Project Management Institute (PMI), the eLearning Guild and the Society for Technical Communication (STC). In my free time I like to cook, hike, camp or simply travel with my Airstream trailer in tow.

Chris Luther’s Specialties:

Web Development (Drupal, PHP, Flash, ActionScript, Java, Struts, UI Design), Learning Communities, Software Project Management, Partner Channel and Certification Program Development, Agile Development Processes, Rapid Instructional Design, Learning Communities, Requirements Gathering, Platform Selection and Deployment (LMS/LCMS/CMS).
