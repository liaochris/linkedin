Dr. Michael Lange’s Summary

Fortifeye Vitamins
Dr. Michael P.Lange ocala optometrist believes strongly in nutritional intervention, having long understood the nutritional connection to healthy eyesight. As a result he has developed nutrient-based vitamin formulas (Fortifeye Vitamins) that are currently being used throughout the world by eye doctors. These formulas are a combination of synergistically blended whole foods, phyto-nutrients, vitamins, enzymes and essential fatty acids. When combined with proper diet and exercise, these vitamins can help.

"These vitamins help to slow down free radical damage and thus the disease process in the eye and body," Michael P. Lange, an optometrist with Lange Eye Care in Ocala stated. "These vitamins help with total body wellness. And if you give the body the nutrients it requires through supplement and proper diet, it will slow down degenerative diseases in the eye."

Dr. Michael Lange the ocala optometrist, travels throughout the world to speak with eye care providers and the public about the nutritional importance for healthy eyesight individually ,in groups and at large conferences. In 2010 Dr. Michael Lange ocala Optometrist spent weeks in Norway researching omega three fatty acids from squid, krill and different species of fish, as a result of this research trip and many other studies he formulated Fortifeye Super Omega the latest in triglyceride form omega three fish oil supplements. Michael P. Lange,an Optometrist with lange Eye Care in Ocala and certified nutrition specialist is constantly staying abreast of the latest in nutritional research to continuously upgrade the fortifeye vitamin formulations.
