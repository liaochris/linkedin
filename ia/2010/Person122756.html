Lavie Margolin’s Summary

My role as a Job Search Advisor is to act as a coach, educator and helping hand to you. I will work with you closely on reviewing all aspects of the job search process and sharpening the necessary skills. I clearly define all advice given and cite practical examples for applying methods to increasing job search success.

I have six years of experience assisting job seekers in career development and counseling. I have worked with individuals and groups from a variety of backgrounds including Information Technology, Graphic Design, Accounting, Finance, Social Services, Human Resources, Advertising, Marketing, Public Relations, Legal and Education.
