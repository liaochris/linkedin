Sterling Hawkins’s Summary

Sterling Hawkins has been an active entrepreneur for the better part of his career, constantly staying involved with new ideas and ventures. His background is solidly in the FMCG retail industry; however, he works handily across multiple business verticals filling both strategic and operational roles while bringing a progressive and innovative spirit to each of his engagements. Across all his ventures Sterling applies a proven philosophy focused on a results-oriented approach to drive value, interest and ROI.

Sterling enjoys living a very active lifestyle, giving back in the volunteer world, and traveling for business and pleasure. He has worked and studied across the US, Europe and Asia while presently residing in Los Angeles, CA.
