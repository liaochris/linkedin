Doug Davis’s Summary

I've been in the technical communications industry for 26 years. I started as an entry level technical writer (called an Associate Product Information Analyst) with Burroughs Corporation. Burroughs later merged with Sperry and became Unisys Corporation. I worked through the ranks at Burroughs/Unisys for the next 13 years with increasing levels of responsibility. My last job at Unisys was as a Department Manager with a staff of about 30 writers, editors, desktop publishers, and graphic illustrators. My group was a profit center within Unisys and eventually the decision was made to outsource my group's work. With that decision came an opportunity. With the help of some colleagues, we formed ProEdit and hired some of my laid-off employees from Unisys. Since that time, ProEdit has become one of the largest technical writing companies in America. We do technical writing on a by-the-project basis, as well as function as a staffing agency specializing in technical writers, editors, and instructional designers. I have only one goal...to help put people to work. Why? Because 25 years ago a lady named Beth Ahola at Burroughs gave a job to a skinny kid with no experience or qualifications, straight out of school, and I feel compelled to pay it forward.
