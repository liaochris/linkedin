Glyn MacLean’s Summary

Oikos Music Ltd is a company owned by New Zealanders which manages leading New Zealand music brands for live performance, publishing and licensing to media networks, television, movie, gaming, brand campaigns, radio and digital download.

The artists we manage are:

YULIA
(Classical, Opera, Popular Vocal)
http://www.yulia.co.nz

THE MACLEANZ
(Pop Folk Rock)
http://www.themacleanz.co.nz

ALMS ELECTRONICA
(Electronica Dance Ambient)
http://www.almselectronica.co.nz

GLYN MACLEAN
(Jazz Film instrumental)
http://www.glynmaclean.co.nz

Our primary objective in 2010 is to introduce YULIA to the United Kingdom (UK).

We are currently exploring potential partners for Yulia's upcoming 3rd Album and release of her multi platinum NZ albums INTO THE WEST and MONTAGE into UK, USA and other international markets.

YULIA's initial releases achieved 7 times platinum sales and winning New Zealand Music Awards for BEST FEMALE VOCALIST and HIGHEST SELLING ALBUM OF THE YEAR.

Yulia is the first NZ artist to achieve two back to back number one hit albums.

We're also seeking out booking agents, touring partners and territorial managers to manage Yulia's activities in each country/region. We are open to discussion in terms of appointing international management to manage Yulia's career.

If you'd like to book YULIA for your venue please contact us through the web site http://www.yulia.co.nz
