Frank Palmieri’s Summary

A master of the game. At the top of his game.

Frank is the President and Co-founder of M&M. An entrepreneur and “businessman’s businessman,” he has been the proactive, guiding force of M&M since it’s inception. A true visionary who is continuously evolving by consistently introducing new products, services and processes to the gaming industry, Frank brings more than twenty years of advertising, marketing and television production experience to the game—keeping our clients ahead of the technology curve, their messaging resonating with customers and their EBITDA growing.
From humble beginnings, Frank’s business and leadership talent was the primary ingredient needed to develop, expand and sustain a company of international scope. Realizing early on that developing a team with the requisite skills in both advertising and gaming across all disciplines of a full-service advertising/marketing communications company was his genius—and the key to becoming the agency of record for some of the Biggest Names in Gaming!
Currently servicing over fifty casino clients operating in multiple jurisdictions across the country, Frank has grown M&M to become one of the leading MarCom companies operating in the Gaming, Hospitality, Entertainment and Retail industries. One of his greatest strengths, is his ability to cultivate and foster a team culture outside the agency
and within while encouraging “M&Mers” to find innovative solutions to every project while simultaneously anticipating the long-term strategic needs of our clients’ brand. Under his supervision, M&M has developed strategies and integrated brand campaigns for major accounts such as the Trump Entertainment Resorts, Isle of Capri, Hard Rock Hotel & Casino, Argosy, Boyd Gaming, Tropicana Casino and Peninsula Gaming among many others.
