David Alpern, MBA’s Summary

Currently freelancing with several clients and looking to return back to an in-house role on the client side. Accomplished Internet Marketing MBA from a top 25 program with an 11-year track record in interactive digital advertising.

Being early to Internet advertising (’99), I have gravitated to an in-house mentality having worked with clients in many industries including: automotive, education, media, retail, credit, travel, and e-commerce, executing on initiatives from conception to implementation.

I’ve seen big budgets, big names, big results and have trained and taught advertising in-house and at the university level.

Perspective of different spokes of the wheel enhances my ability to get things done: the client side, the agency side, the vendor side, and the media side all taught point-of-view.

With social media's rapid development as an "earned" media (where others are talking about your brand), I have augmented my search engine, online traffic and lead gen activities with social media and public relations engagements conceiving and launching both creative campaigns and viral seeding programs.

I understand the macro picture alongside the minute details necessary for delivering revenue and profit growth solutions within organizations operating at Internet speed.

The secret to success is to work like it’s your own buck and when you do find a big mistake, sometimes the best step is to go for lunch and share a margarita. Then you go back and fix it.
