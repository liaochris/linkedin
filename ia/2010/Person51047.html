David Burden’s Summary

David Burden is an entrepreneur and one of the true pioneers of interactive marketing services in Australia. David founded Australia's largest and best-recognised interactive and mobile services company, Legion Interactive, in 1994. He joined full-service digital media company, Webfirm Group (formally Ansearch), as CEO in early 2008; under David’s leadership Webfirm has returned to profitability, successfully re-launched in North America and continues to win accolades for its rapid growth.

David has been Chief Executive Officer of Webfirm Group Limited – a full service digital media company since February 2008.

During his tenure the company has grown dramatically: in October 2008, Ansearch was ranked seventh on the 2008 BRW Fast 100 list of Australia’s fastest-growing companies (third in the information technology sector) and a month later placed fifth in the 2008 Deloitte Technology Fast 50 list of Australia’s fastest-growing technology companies. In December 2008 Ansearch was named among Asia-Pacific’s top 100 fastest-growing technology companies in the Deloitte Technology Fast 500 Asia Pacific 2008.

David has also led Webfirm’s return to profitability, successful re-launch in North America and continued expansion of the company’s three divisions: Searchworld, Webfirm Media and Webfirm.

Before joining Ansearch David was CEO of Legion Interactive from 1994 to 2006. Under his direction, Legion developed a suite of first-to-market products that enabled the media and marketers to utilise emerging interactive and digital platforms to communicate interactively with their target markets.

David was awarded Australia's Mobile Marketer of the Year 2007/8 in recognition of his contribution to the industry over two decades.
