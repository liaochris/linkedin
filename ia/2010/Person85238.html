Alberto Calvo’s Summary

Interfaces & Web are my passion. I'd developed for web since many years ago until 2008, when I finally started my professional career on a world wide Advertising Agency.

I feel very comfortable both on design and coding tasks. I have good artistic taste, and I'd learned a lot about user experience and web layout development this last years.

I'm strong in coding semantic, accessible and valid XHTML Strict (currently learning HTML5) markup with separated presentation in a strong and flexible CSS3 layout. Cross-browser has no secrets for me (with IE6 permission).

I'm specially interested in learning more and more each day to apply that knowledge on every new project I start and end. I'm basically self-taught and I really love to learn new stuff.

I'm obsessed with detail, cleanliness and order.

My goal is to keep growing my skills every day and offer my vision and experience to my partners. This is a very exciting job!
