Susan Weeks’s Summary

Hello, and Thank You for your curiosity today!

I am an Online Professional (aka Virtual Assistant) providing consultancy, coaching and specialist services for:
Inbound Internet Marketing,
Social Media Marketing,
Blog Marketing
and my latest addition,
Online Video Marketing, as a Traffic Geyser Certified Virtual Assistant.

I am an avid 'lifelong learner' who loves not only business and technology, but helping people to learn and develop and be the best they can be. This has provided a strong foundation for my new career direction! I love the opportunities that having a 'virtual career' can bring, and its fair to say that I have found my 'niche'!

I love working with solo entrepreneurs, small business owners and particularly with textile artists, fibre artists and creative crafts. I work to develop your online business freeing up your time to develop your art or focus on your own clients and services.

My last role before my career break enabled me to develop my passion for training and personal development where I initiated and grew the Training function at Strategic Systems Solutions. I gained valuable experience in remote working and training skills development and started to explore the possibilities in remote training delivery, e-learning and general mentoring and coaching skills.

The earlier phases of my career have led me through different roles in the IT sphere, ranging from technical trainer, project leader and analyst/programmer, working in the Financial Services sector and the Automotive industry.

Living in rural France provides me with an excellent opportunity to develop my online business and take full advantage of my current lifestyle.

Developing my online profession will also enable me to support my love of travelling and experiencing different lifestyles & cultures without the constraints of corporate life!

And my biggest love of all ?... spending precious time with my son.
