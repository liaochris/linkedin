Michelle Zmijanac’s Summary

30 years experience in the medical field has finally led me to Meteor-Link which provides patient communication systems to medical offices. For 16 years, I worked directly with patients and doctors wishing for services to improve patient communications while observing privacy laws. The other 14 years were spent in education trying to improve how we educate nurses and medical staff.

In 2007, I was introduced to the creator of Meteor, LLC. David developed a service that eliminates phone tag, reduces hours on the phone trying to relay lab results and next steps in treatment to patients. The system is HIPAA compliant and reduces potential liability. Every medical office NEEDS this service desperately.

My goal is to add 20 medical offices to my service in 2009. A perfect referral is your family doctor.
