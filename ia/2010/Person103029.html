John Rose’s Summary

A veteran marketing executive and award-winning writer, producer and creative director, John Rose has represented some of the world's best-known brands, including Coca-Cola, DuPont, GlaxoSmithKline, Kodak, Marriott and Samsung.

John was among the first Americans to recognize business opportunities in the former USSR and launched an office for his Boston-based company in Moscow in 1989 as Russia’s first independent advertising and public relations agency. Today, Rose Marketing Ltd is a diverse communications group.

John is a highly regarded business strategist, public relations specialist and experienced investor relations professional. His unique expertise has prompted numerous companies to retain him as an outsourced executive to build their brands, expand their businesses or manage their transition from private to public– including Golden Telecom, a leading telecoms provider, Amtel-Vredestein, Russia’s largest passenger car tire producer, and investment bank CiG -- as CMO and Executive Board Director.

John has written hundreds of articles and made numerous speaking, radio and television appearances around the world. He founded the Russian chapter of the International Advertising Association and was a founding member of the American Chamber of Commerce in Russia and the Russian Public Relations Association. He has written and produced a documentary for American television as well as thousands of television and radio commercials and print advertisements -- winning him awards in the USA, Europe, Russia and Japan. His early career prompted Boston Magazine to feature him as one of Boston's Most Intriguing People.

John has also written extensively about food and travel. He conceived and edited the best-selling Marriott Hot Shoppes Cookbook. And most recently he wrote The Vodka Cookbook – Sponsored by Smirnoff and winner of a Gourmand World Cookbook Award -- published and distributed worldwide in several languages by Kyle Cathie Ltd., UK.
