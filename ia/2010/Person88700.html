Jamie Ashe’s Summary

Jamie Ashe is a pioneer of the digital online and mobile advertising industry. A graduate of high school at the young age of 15-years-old, Jamie, went on to graduate with a B.S. in Computer Science, quickly followed by a B.A. in Journalism and continuing her education until earning her MBA by the age of 21 and a Ph.D. just four short years later.

She led the technology industry as Vice President of Sales for Top Speed Corporation and later Sales Manager for EurekaGGN during the dot come boom.

It was in 2001 she joined media giant AOL Time Warner. During her tenure at AOL, she was the number one sales and advertising executive. Recognized for outstanding sales achievements, awarded "Top Sales Person of the Year for 2001 and 2002". She also won the prized 'Managers Over Achiever Award' , and in her last year, was nominated for "Samurai of the Year Award" for overall contributions to the company.

Determined to be an industry leader she continued to contribute to the worlds most prestigious advertising companies such as ValueClick and Vendare Media. In her position as Senior Advertising Executive, Ashe once again became the Top Sales Person.

In 2005 she created Medium21, an advertising and marketing agency in which she served as Vice President. Recognized as one of the most powerful female executives in the advertising industry with her strength and abilities to ink lucrative celebrity endorsement deals as well as award winning marketing campaigns for her clients.

As Vice President of Business Development for top Los Angeles advertising firms, she continues to be one of the “Industries Movers & Shakers” sited by Media Post, and most recently was inducted to the Presidential Who’s Who for her media expertise.

Over the last decade Jamie has been summoned to speak at advertising conventions and selected as one Microsoft's 'Top 500 Executives In The World' at the Summit in Washington.
