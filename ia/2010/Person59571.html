Diana Wehrell-Grabowski, Ph.D.’s Summary

Since establishing my educational consulting business in 1987 I have conducted over 600 teacher training workshops for public and private educational institutions, corporations, and educational organizations. I have been a keynote speaker, guest speaker and presenter at 80 conferences throughout the nation. Additionally, I have provided over 100 Family Science workshops, reaching over 5000 individuals.
Lastly, I have provided over 14,000 "hands-on" science programs to Pre-K- 12th Grade students at hundreds of public and private educational institutions including summer camps.
