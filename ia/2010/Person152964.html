Neil Robinson’s Summary

Neil joined AKQA London in 1997 as a founding member of their creative team. During 7 years there he worked on all of their major digital accounts including Orange, Microsoft, Durex, BMW, Sainsbury's and Nike.

In 2004 AKQA tapped Neil to help them establish the interactive team in their San Francisco office. Neil is currently the Group Creative Director Nike, Target, Autodesk and Claire's.

Neil's award winning interactive experiences and integrated campaigns have helped AKQA garner a host of awards including inclusion on Advertising Age’s 2009 ‘Agency A-List’.

A native of Northern Ireland, Neil studied at Edinburgh College of Art graduating with a Masters degree in Design. His work has been published internationally and he has served on juries for a variety of advertising and design awards.

Outside work, 3 delightful daughters and the NBA League Pass account for Neil’s ‘spare’ time!
