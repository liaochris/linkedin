Augustine Fou’s Summary

Dr. Augustine Fou is Group Chief Digital Officer of Omnicom's Healthcare Consultancy Group. He has over 13 years of Internet strategy consulting experience and is an expert in data mining, analytics, and consumer insights research, with specific knowledge in the consumer payments, packaged goods, food/beverage, retail/apparel, and healthcare sectors. Dr. Fou writes a monthly column for ClickZ's Experts Columns on Integrated Marketing. Dr. Fou completed his PhD at MIT at the age of 23. He started his career with McKinsey & Company and recently served as SVP of digital strategy at McCann/MRM Worldwide. He is also an Adjunct Professor at NYU in the Integrated Marketing Department of the School for Continuing and Professional Studies
