David M. Kaufman, CCIM’s Experience

  • Principal

    DK Realty Partners, LLC

    (Commercial Real Estate industry)

    October 2000 — Present (9 years 6 months)

    Auctioneer and broker of all types of real estate nationally.

    Was first to conduct auctions of large numbers of residential properties nationally for the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development, Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac and the F.D.I.C.

    Conducted the first, last and most auctions for the RTC (Resolution Trust Corporation) during the Savings & Loan Crisis. One highlight was the completion of construction on 30 patio homes in Palmdale, California and then their immediate sale by auction.

    Conducted auctions for various bankruptcy courts (including the auction of The Omni Shoreham Hotel in Washington, D.C. for $40,000,000 and Neumann Homes auction).

    Conducted auctions for many banks, other thrift institutions and pension funds. A highlight was the $20,000,000 sale of 450 single family houses, condos and town homes throughout the State of Texas for A. Robert Abboud when he recapitalized First City Bank (Houston, Texas). (Former U.S. Treasury Secretary, John Connally was instrumental in helping to obtain this assignment.)

    Purchased 80 condominiums from Norwest Bank (Minneapolis, Minnesota), corrected mistakes of previous developer and then auctioned and sold all the units.

    Syndicated ownership of $50,000,000 in nine various investment properties five of which are still owned. DK Realty Partners manages four of those properties.

    Highlights of recently held auctions include the $6,500,000 sale of Lakewoods Corporate Center in Schaumburg, Illinois, a 32 unit apartment building in Albany Park, Chicago for $1,280,000 and a residence at the corner of 3rd and Central, Wilmette, Illinois for $1,650,000.

    Was editor of The Commercial Investment Real Estate Journal (a publication of the National Association of Realtors® - Realtor® Marketing Institute's - Commercial Real Estate Council (of which Mr. Kaufman was also a counselor).

    Mr. Kaufman is a public speaker having addressed many national conventions of both Realtors® & Auctioneers.
