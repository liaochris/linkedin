Kenneth Emery’s Summary

Following a golf scholarship at Wayne State, Kenneth Emery played on the mini tours in Florida. Yet a desire for balance and incredible passion for entrepreneurship sent Ken into the business world. In 1993 Ken began designing, building and operating casual dining restaurant that shaped a neighborhood and later sold in 2001.

Since, Ken has achieved #1 in sales at 2 successful companies, and trained some of the largest sales forces in America. The path to training has been a wild ride, and a humble one. Ken began sharing his thoughts about "why humans do what they do" with schools, colleges, and community organizations. Ken just loves to see people do their best, and inspire the best in others. That passion has led to PAL Companies, International Sales University, and now the Human Element which will lead the way for "experiential training."

Wouldn't you agree that the things we "do" and "emotionally experience" will have the greatest impact on our results and lives? Ken and the Human Element Team bring emotion and experience into every training event we offer. Emotion changes behavior, not intellect is our motto.

"Fear Ball" is our flagship program and is exclusively offered at more than 100 private clubs in America. And, Ken still gives back by visiting an endless list of schools and teens each year!

Kenneth Emery’s Specialties:

Speaking, Training, Writing, and inspiring others to achieve their peak personally, professionally, emotionally, and athletically! Courage is not the absence of fear, rather the ability to ACT even in the face of fear. Do it!
