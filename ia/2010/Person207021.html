Tony Popowski’s Summary

Marketing has radically changed over the past year. The proven methods that once sealed the deal and got customers through the door simply don’t work anymore. We can go around in circles explaining why this occurred: the economy, shifting consumer needs, new technology, etc. At the end of the day, small businesses and the local “mom and pop shops” don’t care about the why; they care about the how - how they can enhance brand awareness, how they can set themselves apart from the competition, and most importantly, how they can stimulate sales.

The answer is an integrated inbound approach. This exciting marketing revolution uses different cost-effective communication approaches to have your audience coming to you. For more information, visit: http://grmwebsite.com/inbound-marketing/
