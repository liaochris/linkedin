Sherry Frazier’s Summary

Overview of Consulting Practice

Sherry Frazier has over 20 years experience-- in sales, sales management, sales training, public relations and development of marketing programs designed to increase revenue. Sherry Frazier is a master marketing implementer whose value-add is an ability to take corporate goals and objectives, fit into an organization, create viable, logical tactical plans, and then implement them successfully.

Special skill is the ability to process highly technical or complex information and create a teachable, repeatable way of communicating information and benefits of a product or service, making them understandable and easy to explain to a sales organization, prospects, and to a non-technical audience.


Typical consulting engagements include:

Book Editing and Publicity. Book editing to include content and flow consultation as well as final editing. Publicity to include radio, television, print interviews and book tours. Media campaigns designed to drive book sales and enhance the author or book's profile. Will also work with writers to attain an agent or publisher.

Focused public relations campaigns for small and large companies. Press kits, press/media strategy, press releases and media follow-up, media relations, media training, and turn key public relations solutions.

Acting Marketing Director to companies who need full service support to implement marketing programs quickly.

Ad Campaign Development. Radio, Print, Television. Creation and implementation of advertsing, public relations and marketing campaigns designed to increase revenue.

Experienced in all aspects of marketing communications including analysis of organization and development of print collateral materials, corporate identity, logo design and style guide, direct mail, website, trade show exhibits and materials, advertising campaigns, annual reports, video, events and video entertainment devices.

Sherry Frazier’s Specialties:

Book Editing and Publicity. Public Relations, Marketing Implementation, Website Copy Development, any kind of copy designed to position, sell, or promote a company/product/service, Printed or electronic collateral materials, Radio Ads, Video, Events, Tradeshow, Sales Training.
