Eno Sarris’s Summary

Firmly straddling the educational and trade markets is my specialty: teaching well and selling well at the same time is the way to go. Whether it's working to educate young people through fantasy sports, or more conventional products, helping people grow is my number one goal. My writing and editing skills come with a good sense of organization, structure, and relationships, but having creative input will always be important to me, no matter what role I take on.
