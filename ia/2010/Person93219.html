Jeff Graham, SIOR’s Summary

Beginning his real estate career in 1987, Jeff joined King Industrial Realty, Inc., one of the largest Industrial Real Estate firm in the Southeast, in 1989. In 1998, Jeff, along with Charles King, founded King Industrial Realty of Florida, Inc. in Jacksonville. In 2001, Jeff was inducted into the Society of Industrial and Office REALTORS ®. To achieve the SIOR designation, Jeff completed at least 5 years of creditable experience in the highly specialized field of industrial real estate; passed a stringent qualifying written examination; and demonstrated to the Society, professional ability, competency, ethical conduct and personal integrity. Jeff is also very active in CORFAC International, an international network of some of the top, privately held real estate firms in the United States. CORFAC International also has an alliance with King Sturge, one of Europe’s largest and most highly respected real estate companies. Jeff served as President of CORFAC in 2009 and on the Executive Committee of CORFAC International.

Jeff has a client list of local and international companies including ING Clarion, Exeter Property Group, Rock-Tenn Company, True North Investments, Investment Property Group, Crowley Logistics, Solar Energy Inc., O’Neal Steel, Coastal International Logistics, Del Monte Fresh Produce, TruServ Corporation, Yankee Candle Company, Owens Corning Fiberglas and Ball Corporation Plastics Division.
