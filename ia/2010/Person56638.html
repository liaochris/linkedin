Girts Dimdins’s Summary

I am an Associate Professor in Social Psychology at University of Latvia. My research interests include attributions, stereotyping, intergroup conflict, and moral judgments. I teach courses in Social Psychology and related subjects to undergraduate and graduate students, and courses in Scientific Communication and Thesis Writing to undergraduate students.

In addition, I work part time as a Faculty Advisor for the Executive MBA program at Stockholm School of Economics in Riga. There my duties include teaching a Research Methods course and being in charge of the Diploma Project process.
