Mike Grimme’s Summary

Mike is President and CEO of AMC Liquidators, which focuses on buying and selling upscale hotel, home and office furniture and equipment as well as manufacturers closeouts. AMC Liquidators is part of Grimme’ Holdings which was founded in 1996. Grimme’ Holdings was awarded the 2001 South Florida Small Business of the Year by the South Florida Business Journal.

Mike moved to Fort Lauderdale after an 18-year career in the international crude oil trading industry, based primarily in Europe. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from the University of Delaware, and a Master of Business Administration from Widener University in Pennsylvania.

Mike has a wife, Pamela, and four children, Ashley 20, Michael (deceased), Chelsea 16 and Amanda 14. He has a pilot’s license and considers flying and participating in sports as his main hobbies.
