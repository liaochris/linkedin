George. Spafford’s Summary

George is a senior manager with Cognizant and an experienced practitioner in business and IT operations. He is a prolific author and speaker, and has consulted and conducted training on regulatory compliance, IT Governance, and process improvement in the U.S., Australia, New Zealand and China. Publications include: "The Governance of Green IT", "Greening the Data Center" and co-authorship of “The Visible Ops Handbook" and “Visible Ops Security”.

George holds an MBA from Notre Dame, a BA in Materials and Logistics Management from Michigan State University and an honorary degree from Konan Daigaku in Japan. He is a ITIL Service Manager, TOCICO Jonah and a Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA). George is a current member of the IIA, ISACA, ITPI, ITSMF, and the TOCICO.

George. Spafford’s Specialties:

Consulting on the strategic use of IT, professional author, strategy, IT governance, IT process improvement, ITIL v2 and v3, CISA, Theory of Constraints (TOC) Jonah, ISO 27001:2005, ISO 27002:2005, COBIT, regulatory compliance, compliance frameworks, and training.
