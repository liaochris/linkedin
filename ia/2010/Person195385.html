Steven Burles’s Summary

Steve has been producing digital content for some of the worlds biggest brands for over 10 years.

After completing his degree he was head hunted by the world renowned design agency ATTIK and given the job of Senior Interactive Designer. Working there for just under 2 years he worked on brands such as Dawsons Creek, Nike, Sony Europe, British Council and MTV.

In 2001, due to the dot com bubble bursting Steve's decision to start his own agency was forced. EXSTATIK was born. Over the next 5 years he built EXSTATIK into a digital agency that was producing work for the likes of Disney, Nickelodeon and Cartridge World. One of their main clients towards the end of the 5 years was The Intermarketing Group and in 2006 the two merged.

Now 7 years old EXSTATIK still looks after its own clients such as Sony Europe and Dollond & Aitchison. But it also facilitates the clients of The Intermarketing Group who include adidas, HBOS, GE Money, Next, Otto Group, RSA Group and Ted Baker.

Steve has had extensive experience across all aspects of producing digital content. He has a proven track record of getting clients and keeping them. As a business owner he understands and performs the many roles required within a digital agency and in the past has been Managing Director, Production Manager, New Business Manager, Designer, Account Handler, Art Director and Tea Maker!
