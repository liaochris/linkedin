Brent Edwards’s Summary

Brent is an accomplished Information Technology professional with over 5 years of experience in developing innovative solutions with Microsoft technologies. Brent is familiar with all phases of the development life-cycle and has contributed system design, process modeling, testing, and documentation skills. He possesses strong leadership qualities and is able to mentor and train junior developers. Brent is self-motivated with a strong work ethic, equally effective working individually or as part of a team.
