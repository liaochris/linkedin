Michael Klotz’s Summary

I currently run Health Data Vision, Inc., a technology startup company, which provides next-generation solutions for medical records collection and analysis.

Prior to starting Health Data Vision, Inc. I was VP of Solutions Stragegy at Ensynch, Inc., which had aquired my previous company, eBI Solutions, in August of 2008.

I started eBI Solutions in 2002 and managed strategy and operations of the company, guiding it from a self-funded startup to become a nationally recognized, award-winning Microsoft Gold Partner focused on Business Process Integration, SOA, Business Intelligence and eCommerce solutions.

Before founding eBI Solutions, I built the integration practice and lead dramatic process improvement initiatives at both my employer, Rainbow-Spectria, and our clients.
In previous positions, I implemented ERP systems and Enterprise Workflow solutions, always with an eye on systems integration and process automation.
Prior experience includes process automation in manufacturing, which had exposed me to programming when programmable control systems just started to take hold in the late '80s.
