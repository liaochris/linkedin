David Torchinsky, Attorney, CPA’s Summary

Mr. Torchinsky practices in the areas of estate planning, trust and estate administration and tax matters.

The firm, Stein, Sperling, Bennett, De Jong, Driscoll & Greenfeig, P.C. is a full service law firm practicing in the Greater Washington, DC Metropolitan Area.

David Torchinsky, Attorney, CPA’s Specialties:

Mr. Torchinsky is a leader in the areas of living trusts, wills, family limited partnerships, charitable trusts, private foundations, and other wealth-preserving and tax-saving strategies. In addition to being an attorney, Mr. Torchinsky is also a Certified Public Accountant.

  • Of Counsel, Attorney

    (Law Practice industry)

    May 2010 — Present (2 months)

    Mr. Torchinsky concentrates his practice in the areas of wills, trusts, estate planning, wealth preservation strategies, charitable planning and federal and state tax matters. He assists clients with controlling their assets during life, taking care of family members in the event of a disability and providing for survivors upon death. He takes special care to assist families with preserving wealth within the entire family unit. Before joining Stein Sperling, Mr. Torchinsky was a principal in a small Montgomery County estate planning and tax law firm, and prior to that he practiced as a Certified Public Accountant for nearly ten years.

David Torchinsky, Attorney, CPA’s Groups:
