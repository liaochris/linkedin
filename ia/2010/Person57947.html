Donald Cummings’s Summary

Don is a founding principal and managing partner of Blue Haven Capital which is an independent, privately owned investment management firm that provides cost efficient, highly experienced investment advisory services for public charities, private foundations, and individuals nationwide. The firm focuses on the $1mm to $15mm asset size market- often overwhelming to retail brokers and frequently overlooked by the bulge bracket firms. The firm's partners bring 25 years of previous experience with top tier institutional investment management and offer a depth of understanding that is not often found in the smaller asset size arena. Blue Haven Capital is able to compete head to head with many of the largest trust departments and money managers in the United States and win business simply because of the low cost, high experience, and complete objectivity that the firm offers. The firm is fee only, and acts as a full fiduciary to its clients.

Donald Cummings’s Specialties:

The firm's institutional level background and relationships enable it to trade with a number of market participants instead of being captive to a single broker's inventory. It has an exceptionally strong background in bonds and the fixed income markets, and fees are significantly lower than most bank trust departments and brokerage firms. The fixed income expertise is especially important to retirees who are looking to establish an income substitute as they move from working into retirement.
