James McKie / TopLinked’s Summary

While the majority of my professional background has been in the real estate sales and development business, I have redirected my career into the retirement/assisted living field, working exclusively in this industry since August 2008.

As Director of Marketing with Silverado Senior Living, I had the opportunity to learn how I could translate my many years of real estate sales and marketing experience into Assisted Living Sales and Marketing. My move into Resident Engagement/Activities within a residential community for Silverado gave me the opportunity to verify that my career path had indeed taken the right turn by providing me the opportunity to make a difference in the lives of others.

At 2020 Vision for Long Term Care, I developed the qualities necessary to engineer a start-up private care home company specializing in memory care needs; and as Executive Director at Mountain View Assisted Living by LaVida Communities, I had the opportunity to further master my skills as an effective leader in the field of Long Term Care.

Currently serving in the capacity of Executive Director for Emeritus Senior Living, I enjoy an association with an experienced team of care professionals who are dedicated to the health and happiness of our residents. At Emeritus Senior Living, “Our Family is Committed to Yours.”

James McKie / TopLinked’s Specialties:
