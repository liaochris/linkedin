Paul Scott Adamo’s Summary

Paul Scott Adamo has over fifteen years experience working as a Celebrity Booker, Casting Director and Producer for many national Television Series and Specials, Talk Shows, Reality Programs, Radio Shows, etc. He is also a Casting Director for Reality and Nonfiction Television Series. Paul is an active member of The Producers Guild Of America and The Academy Of Television Arts & Sciences.

Paul has booked thousands of celebrities including: Sarah Jessica Parker, Clint Eastwood, Colin Powell, Alec Baldwin, Anthony Hopkins, Beyonce’ Knowles, Susan Sarandon, Alicia Keys, Ashley Judd, Serena Williams, Mary J. Blige, Matt Lauer, Dr. Mehmet Oz, Maya Angelou, Rachel Ray, Sally Field, Kevin Bacon, Michael Jackson, Sarah Ferguson, Patti LaBelle, Susan Lucci, Chris Rock, Larry King, Lil’ Kim, Clive Davis, Regis Philbin, etc.

Paul has a well developed Rolodex with thousands of contacts to celebrities, show hosts, real people, highly credentialed authors and experts in all areas such as health, fashion, personal development, business, finance, psychology, interior design, cooking, breaking news commentary, etc.

Paul Scott Adamo's experience includes working as a Celebrity/Talent Booker, Casting Director and Producer for many national television series including:

"TV Land Confidential" (TV Land Network - Brainstorm, Inc.)

“The A List On Animal Planet” (Animal Planet – Discovery Networks)

"The Montel Williams Show"

"B. Smith With Style" (nationally syndicated and also aired on The Discovery Channel and The Food Network - True Entertainment, an Endemol Company)

"Back To The Blueprint" (The History Channel, A&E Networks)

"Honey, We're Killing The Kids" (TLC - BBC Productions USA)

"Kitty Bartholomew: You're Home" (HGTV - Weller Grossman Productions)

"Small Space, Big Style" (HGTV - BrainBox Productions)

Paul is also the Talent Executive for SUCCESS Magazine. He books Celebrities, Best Selling Authors and CEOs for Cover and Feature Stories.

Paul Scott Adamo’s Interests:

Paul also produces and directs documentaries. Paul Co-Produced and Directed "Renee' Taylor and Joseph Bologna: Still Made For Each Other", which features interviews with Jane Fonda, Martin Sheen and Joan Collins. Footage from this documentary is featured in Renee' Taylor and Joseph Bologna's Broadway Show "If You Ever Leave Me, I'm Going With You." Mr. Adamo's television documentaries have been shown in Los Angeles at The American Film Institute, EZTV Gallery and on KCBS-TV. In New York, his work has been seen on Broadway at The Cort Theatre, The Kitchen, and has aired on PBS. Paul's documentary "Alice" is part of the permanent collection of The Theater on Film and Tape Archive At The New York Public Library For The Performing Arts At Lincoln Center. Mr. Adamo's documentary "The Enigma Of Mark Kostabi" was shown at The Producers Guild Of America Documentary Screening Series and at The Martin Lawrence Gallery.
