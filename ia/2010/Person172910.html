Richard Stangroom’s Summary

Market Development Director with Applied Language Solutions - A brief history of the company is below.

Applied Language opened it’s first office in sunny Huddersfield. This was soon filled with talented and enthusiastic staff, but the parking was terrible and things were getting a little crowded. So in April 2005, Applied Language moved to much swankier offices just outside Huddersfield. But we soon filled that one as well. So in March 2006, we all moved again to our current offices in Armitage Bridge.

From here the team swelled and grew shoots in Bulgaria, where the project management and IT departments are based, and a sales team in California to enable us to help our USA customers with their translations. Shortly after, we opened offices in Barcelona, Paris and an office in Guatemala to support the sales operations in California.

More recently, we have set up offices in Asia with the opening of our Hong Kong and Indian offices. Not only will this allow us to help our Asian customers with their translations, but this will give us almost 24 hour global coverage, meaning our customers will have access to our services at any time of the day.

A huge part of the company’s success is down to the 7,000 plus highly qualified translators we have based around the world. To help the translators we launched, in February 2006, Talkbase.net. This dedicated web portal allows them a secure way to keep track of the projects they are working on and, just as importantly, ensure they are paid on time.
