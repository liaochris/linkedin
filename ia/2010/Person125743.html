Dear Business Owner,
I’m guessing you have a web site. And I’m guessing you have a google adwords account. What is your monthly budget on that account? $500 a month? $1,000 a month? $10,000 a month? No matter. I’m writing to tell you you’re wasting your money on that program. Why? Because you’re on the RIGHT SIDE of the page—The paid side. Everyone knows you’re selling something. That why the other side of the page—the natural listing, organic side gets 9 times more clicks than the paid side.

I can get you on the left side—where everyone “knows” it’s an organic listing. Information! Not a “pushy” “salesy” website.

I can get you there… on the top… fast! Not in 2 or 3 months of behind the scenes search engine optimization work. NO! I can get you natural, organic TOP OF PAGE ONE listings sometimes in 6 hours after we do our set-up work. It’s almost magical. But it sure beats google adwords pay-per-click—where you get maybe a ton of “impressions” but only a few clicks… and those clicks cost you a bundle… and they don’t always BUY. Remember, 90% of the clicks come from the organic side of the page. Let's talk.
