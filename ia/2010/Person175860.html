Robert Santos’s Summary

Robert Santos is a multi-talented internet professional and entrepreneur with experience encompassing a variety of disciplines. With roots as an account manager and programmer for the web space venture Staghosting, Robert's history also includes work as a graphic designer, web developer, internet marketer, and entrepreneurial business owner. Currently, Robert runs Digital-Rebirth Web Design which has had great success with various clients, many of whom become repeat customers. Robert plans to increase the variety of his operations into additional forms of interactive media through Pixelular Interactive, LLC. In addition, Robert has contributed in a variety of positions to the dynamic and quickly-growing start-up Juggle, LLC for over a year and currently serves on the marketing team.
