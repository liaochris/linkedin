Andrew Kinnear’s Summary

I introduce big brands to emerging technology marketing. I can share complex ideas with any audience at any knowledge level.

As a marketing and technology nerd with over 10 years in the interactive marketing and loyalty space, I bring ideas to life within an organization. My forte is finding projects, concepts, tools, or ideas that a group can rally around.

I've spoken internationally and lectured on topics like Green Business, Digital Marketing, Social Media Strategy and Customer Engagement. I'm comfortable speaking to an executive team or an audience of hundreds.

In 2008 I was lucky enough to be trained by Nobel Laureate and former Vice President Al Gore as part of the Climate Project.
