Jitesh Madhwani’s Summary

jitesh@quadrasolutions.com

Jitesh Madhwani has lived and worked in 6 cities around the world (including New York, Melbourne, Dubai, New Delhi, etc) having held positions of responsibility in some of the world's largest companies as well as small businesses.

Jitesh has 12 years of experience in Marketing, Advertising & Strategy, Business Development, IT Outsourcing, eBusiness, Consumer Goods Retail & B2B, Processed Foods.

In 2004, he started Quadra Solutions with the vision of creating a premier IT services company which would become a partner of choice for businesses seeking to outsource their IT requirements.

Jitesh has also been involved in the Creation, Management & Growth of profitable eBusinesses which have been spun off.
