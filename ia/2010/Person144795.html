Miguel Dorneles’s Summary

Online marketing professional with a 2-year academic experience in Computer Science and a 5-year experience leading teams in digital media and marketing while working for large corporations in Brazil, both local and multinational.

Key industries include newspapers, internet and pay-TV, having developed successful work teams and new areas across Grupo RBS, the biggest media conglomerate in the south of Brazil, and Fox Latin American Channels, one of the world's leading media companies, part of News Corporation.

Speaker at important industry events in Brazil such as SMX São Paulo 2008, Digital Marketing 3, Web Analytics Wednesday, Social Media Brasil 2009 and Customer Loyalty Summit 2009. Also, a former member of IAB Brasil's SEM committee and a contributor for several initiatives such as Web Analytics e-books and the SearchCast search marketing podcast.
