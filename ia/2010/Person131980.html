Marcus Colombano’s Summary

Marcus has over 20 years of street and online marketing experience and 10 direct years working with mobile computing products. He helped Handspring/Palm launch the Treo by developing a community of influencers. As a Co-Founder of NuvoMedia, he managed the branding and launch of the Rocket eBook, a paperback-sized electronic book and delivery system. Franklin Electronics OEM’d the product in 1999, and Gemstar/TVGuide purchased the company in 2000. As a managing partner of Avantgarde, a marketing firm he co-founded in San Francisco, Marcus has also worked as a marketing consultant for Eastman Kodak Company, Xerox Corporation, Brøderbund Software, Live Picture, Packet Design and Vernier Networks. His marketing work ranges from general communications and branding to the strategic development of influencer communities. Marcus recently co-founded LightPole, Inc., a company that creates carrier-grade back-end server infrastructure and applications to enable publishers to publish geo-specific content to mobile devices.
