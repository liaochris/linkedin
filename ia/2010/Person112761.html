Kaushik Mitra’s Summary

A marketing communications professional with extensive experience in brand building, corporate communications,advertising, direct marketing, customer & trade relationship management, alliances & partnerships, integrated marketing services and digital marketing

Having started out with JWT, have worked with FCB Ulka, Response, McCann Erickson, Enterprise Nexus Direct, Micrographics, Powerstrat and Kankei Relationship Marketing Services and have garnered over two decades of experience.

Over the years, have been closely associated with and helped build over 50 international, national and regional brands across diverse categories, positively impacting customer experience during the pre,during and post- buy phase and strengthening their lifetime value.

Currently engaged in the social sector to build a pioneering social-entrepreneur model in the field of productive ageing.

Interests lie in building brands from scratch in both corporate and not-for-profit sectors.
