Mark Williams’s Summary

Mark is a successful marketing professional with more than 20 years experience managing marketing functions and creating effective B2B communications programs for Fortune 1000 companies, domestically and internationally. Mark is known for creativity, relationship-building, and thorough program execution.

Mark is the former owner and president of Cho Creative, Inc., a Dayton-based design and communications company. Mark sold the business to Nova Creative in March 2008. Mark and his team joined Nova Creative.

Mark's career history includes key roles in several design/advertising agencies and a number of marketing management positions in Dayton area companies, including: LexisNexis, Standard Register, Reynolds & Reynolds and Carlson Marketing.
