Andrea Tannenbaum’s Summary

Building or remodeling for 'Aging in Place'? Visit AdaptMy.com

For your home - the kitchen, the bath, the entryway and the bedroom - AdaptMy.com has the largest collection of accessible products to help seniors stay at home.

Seniors may need assistance opening doors, getting into bed, reaching into cabinets, preventing falls, sitting and standing. They want products that fit in with their décor; products that don’t look institutional.

From simple to simply beautiful, AdaptMy.com is your source for UD (Universal Design), ADA compliant and accessible products.

Visit www.AdaptMy.com or call 866-993-0783 for more information.

Andrea Tannenbaum’s Specialties:

We are interested in all products for the home that offer more comfort and safety as people move through changes in their lives. These life changes can be family changes, injuries, disease or aging; all require an environment that can flex to changing physical needs.
