Alexandra Piotrowska psychologuk at gmail.com’s Summary

Alex graduated in 1992 with MSc degree in Psychology from the University of Lublin and trained as a Counselling Psychologist.
She gained postgraduate qualifications as a psychotherapist at Metanoia Insitute in London.
She is an experienced counsellor (BACP accredited) and Psychotherapist (UKCP registered) . She has worked in various settings for over ten years, for BUPA Employee Assistance Programm and in the NHS trust as well as private practice. She is bilingual in Polish and English.
Her specialized training covers integrated approaches to counselling and psychotherapy utilizing Transactional Analysis, CBT ,NLP, and family systems processes and techniques.
She runs monthly "Roots of Love" Family Constellations workshop in Ealing, London.Her gift is to explain in easily understood language, just how you can bring around the change that you want in your life.
She offers 101 course in Transactional Analysis "Winning Togheter" and superision in TA
She is available to run a systemic constellation workshops for orgasations and companies .
