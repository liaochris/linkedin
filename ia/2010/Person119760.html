Kimberly St Julian’s Summary

I am a Junior studying history and political science at Swarthmore College. I thrive in stressful environments and work best with a hard deadline. I am a task master with a habit of list making and completion.

I have studied Russian, Soviet, Eastern European, and German politics and history extensively, culminating in multiple research papers focusing on these areas.

I am hoping that these interests will culminate in a J.D. focusing on international law along with a Masters degree in Russian and Eastern European Studies. I plan on using these together to work with the United Nations or the United States Government in some capacity.

I also am directing multiple philanthropy projects including one in Madagascar that I created, budgeted, and executed my freshman year of college. I love volunteering and social work and plan to continue this work when I graduate.
