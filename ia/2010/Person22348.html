Bernadette Martin MBA’s Summary

Bernadette Martin, Founder of Visibility Branding, is a certified Personal Brand Strategist, Storyteller and Author of “Storytelling About Your Brand Online and Offline”. After a 15 year international corporate career with brands such as Proctor & Gamble, Ford, Saatchi & Saatchi, American Express, Citibank, DHL, Jaguar and Nestle, she transitioned in 2003 to working with personal brands offering career development services for individuals, corporations and academic institutions in virtual and on-site environments. Clients are connected to brands such as Cisco, Chanel, Goldman Sachs, Orange, Mercedes, Publicis, DBM, Cordon Bleu, Thunderbird, IBM, INSEAD, IMD, Science Po and HEC. In addition to speaking engagements on Personal Branding and 2.0 Storytelling she hosts the Visibility Branding Webinar Series providing online training and interviews with authors and experts on career related topics. Participants join from around the world.

Bernadette is multi-lingual with an MBA from Thunderbird, School of Global Management and certified as a Reach Personal Brand Strategist, Online Identity Expert and 360Reach Assessment Analyst. Fully aware of the value of giving to her networks, Bernadette is President of her Alumni Association, recent Vice President of the American University Clubs of France and an active member of the European Professional Women’s Network.

Originally from Los Angeles and of Irish, German, and French descent, she is truly “Euro-American” with an early interest in cross-cultural experiences and foreign languages. A long distance runner and swimmer, she finds her “zen” on trails and in the water with memorable runs in the Canadian Rockies, French Alps, Taipei and the Grand Canyon. A favorite Tibetan proverb is “Happiness is not at the end of the road, it’s the road that brings happiness”. In her words it is not the “fait accompli” that matters but the excitement, fun and challenges the road (even if it’s uphill) presents in getting there.
