Marci Schnapp-Rafael’s Summary

Marci Schnapp-Rafael leads New Media Links in fulfilling the corporate vision: to be a strategic search partner for organizations with eBusiness talent requirements. Marci founded New Media Links in 1998, recently launched Xsearch an executive retained search division and New Media Links became a certified solution provider of Role-Based Assessment™.

As the leader of this dynamic search firm, Marci is responsible for making the services of New Media Links and Xsearch indispensable to clients who need to attract talented eBusiness or interactive experts. Through strategic process development, Marci has structured the company to deliver the highest quality candidates to surpass the expectations of clients and garner unqualified referrals.

Prior to starting New Media Links, Marci developed her sales and marketing experience in media advertising and direct marketing. She studied Communications and Sociology at York University and Advertising Sales at Humber College.

New Media Links was amongst the first ten companies to receive certification from WEConnect Canada as a Women’s Business Enterprise. Marci is also a member of Association of Canadian Search, Employment and Staffing Services and Women’s Executive Network (WXN). Marci is currently a volunteer on the recruitment committee of Dress Your Best – a not-for-profit organization that changes the lives of Canadians and New Canadians by outfitting women, men and youth with clothing for employment. To sustain her knowledge, she attends a variety of conferences, seminars and events on eBusiness, interactive marketing, leadership and executive search services. Marci lives in Toronto with her husband Ben and son Myles.

Marci Schnapp-Rafael’s Specialties:

Role Based Assessment, recruitment of eBusiness and Internet star players at all levels and for companies in all sectors.
