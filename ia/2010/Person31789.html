Candace Riddle’s Summary

Candace Riddle is the Standards Manager at the National Institute of Governmental Purchasing (NIGP) in Herndon, VA. Since 1944, NIGP has worked to develop, support, and promote public procurement practitioners through educational and research programs, technical services and advocacy initiatives.

Candace received a B.A. in Political Science (2005) from Coastal Carolina University and is currently pursuing an M.A. in Diplomacy - International Trade & Commerce from Norwich University.

Prior experience living abroad has added depth to Candace's understanding of international cultures and the important role that multinational corporations fill during this time of globalization.

Licensed: Series 7, Series 66, Life Accident & Health Insurance, Property & Casualty Insurance, Real Estate License, SC Public Notary.

Languages: English, Spanish
