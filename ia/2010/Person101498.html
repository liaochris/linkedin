John Henderson’s Summary

I have provided high level leadership and sales expertise with an emphasis on outside the box solutions that create high levels of profitability. I spent many years as a social entrepeneur and parlayed the unique global connections I developed over several decades to create a global business that pursues profitability in four spheres; economic,social,environmental,spiritual.
Currently, I have extensive connections in the Middle East and leverage them to create a not of profit organization called Planet Ventures that features a 'transformation leadership' adventure for the emerging youth leaders of the world called the Jerusalem Project.
Primary global endeavors include an emphasis on the selling and buying of large purchases of gold , diamonds and other rocks along with Fuels , especially Crude Oil and all of its derivatives.

John Henderson’s Specialties:

Trust, Connections Globally, Outside the Box solutions to create innovate business solutions for emerging companies in the most strategic sectors of the economy, alternative energy and sustainable solutions for solid and wet waste remediation, movie-making , entertainment, etc.
