Neal M. Brown’s Summary

“Money gets made when you Connect ideas in your brain with people who want your ideas”

Robert Kiyosaki, author of Rich Dad Poor Dad, recently said; "knowledge is the new money". Upon hearing this, I became depressed! See, I also heard; "You don't know what you don't know" and I knew I didn't know a lot of things. Was I in trouble?

See, we all have these "Blind spots" or gaps in our own knowledge base. We're just BLINDED to new things because of our existing points-of-view. It's like having a giant barrier directly in front of you and it totally blinds your vision. We eventually settle into our "comfortable methods" because "they've always worked in the past".

Today, selling on the internet has changed. Most companies operate in a 'NET NEGATIVE’ marketing position. It describes efforts such as advertising and promotions that you 'HOPE' the consumer finds and eventually purchases something your website...NEGATIVE being the sales HUGE LOSS IN SALES because of what you don’t know about OUR core selling strategy.

This described 100% of companies prior to 2008.

Obviously, a face-to-face relationship is the best way to connect with the consumer but how does a company reach a national audience when face-to-face isn’t possible? How do you CUT through the Marketing “FOG” that surrounds our target audience and inundates their attention with all those other “shiny objects?

NET Landing, Inc. helps you develop a "Targeted Core Strategy" to expand your brand and experience increased sales conversion rates during all product launches. In the process, we build awesome customer LOYALTY, TRUST and a LIFETIME RELATIONSHIP with your ever expanding target audience.

Isn't it time to find out what’s really working on the NET? Don't be NET Negative...let's discuss the opportunities.
