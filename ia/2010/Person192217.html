Patricia Alexander’s Summary

Speaking to groups and leading seminars and workshops is my passion and my joy! Every professional and personal experience I've had has led me to embrace this work with gusto and fascination.

I believe an audience wants to be entertained, motivated, included, challenged, educated and captivated. My impassioned platform (and award-winning book) is about the biggest stress-buster, career builder and life enhancer we can all access: the power of conscious self-comforting, which helps us create more balance and joy. I specialize in customizing my speeches to the needs and personality of a specific group.

I also pride myself myself on being able to address a multitude of topics that relate to the human condition - and have been well-received speaking on creativity, spirituality, relationships and finance. Yup, I'm a thinker, a wordsmith and a humorist, so I can talk meaningfully and entertainingly about pretty much anything - and LOVE doing it!

My background began in theater, continued in entertainment journalism (writing for Rona Barrett on Good Morning, America), and transitioned into a 35-year career as a staff and freelance writer/producer. Along the way, I worked in animation, PR, advertising, marketing and corporate business.

Ultimately, I became a personal perspective columnist for newspapers and wrote an inspirational book. I also lost 40 pounds in 2001 and began teaching Weight Watchers, becoming a Diamond Star Leader.

My work is to empower people to develop simple, potent strategies for handling stress and for creating more joy...so that they can live their lives to their fullest potential.
