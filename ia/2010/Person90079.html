Jasmin Tragas’s Summary

Jasmin is a creative marketing and communications professional with a background in eLearning and digital media.

Jasmin is a member of IBM’s Technical Experts Council in A/NZ, a ‘Blue IQ Ambassador’ of social media and innovation programs at IBM, and a member of the Advisory Committee for the VCA Centre for Cultural Partnerships (University of Melbourne).

She initiated and led the Arts Special Interest Group for AIMIA Victoria from 1996 to 1997, helping artists to connect, discover and explore digital media, before returning to study and completing a Graduate Diploma and Masters of Interactive and Creative Media (RMIT Uni).

Jasmin writes about inspiration, communication, connection and creativity on her blog, Wonderwebby, and enjoys collaborative projects for social good. In 2008 she contributed a chapter to the book, The Age of Conversation 2 helping to raise funds for Variety Children’s Charity and also coordinated a free eBook about women using Social Media for Social Good in 2009, called ‘WorldShapers’.
