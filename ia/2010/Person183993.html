Scott Applebee’s Summary

I have been fortunate to work for 26 years in all aspects of marketing management. During this time period, I have gained progressive experience, culminating in marketing leadership positions in product management and development, marketing communication and customer service. My expertise includes:

• Building brands in turnaround and start-up companies
• Developing and implementing strategic plans
• Established a new product development tollgate process, incorporating customer need research.
• Introduced numerous successful new products in multiple industries by incorporating the “voice of the customer” throughout the development process.
• Developed a company-wide competitive information program that supported the sales team, product development and senior management.

I have had the opportunity to gain extensive experience by working for large, global companies in the commercial appliance, office furniture, consumer goods and computing services industries. I am the type of person who thrives on tackling complex business opportunities and issues. My background includes creating an entire marketing organization and strategic plan for a start-up operation, introducing a new $50 million product line and discontinuing an unprofitable business venture.

My value to a company starts with integrity and respects the opinions and capabilities of all members of an organization. I have a passion for serving the customer, continuous learning and achieving business objectives.
