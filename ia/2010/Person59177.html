Doug Rozen’s Summary

Doug Rozen is responsible for developing and leading Carlson Marketing’s global creative, interactive, media and mobile capabilities – ensuring innovative ideas exceed present and future client needs. Based in Minneapolis, he oversees teams around the world.

With consumers in greater control, Doug has the expertise in developing campaigns that truly grab attention. He believes in “creativity that works”, where “great ideas have no boundaries”, and Interactive is any channel that leverages technology to create meaningful two-way dialogue. Doug’s vision is helping to position Carlson Marketing as a distinctive leader in the industry leveraging the agency’s Relationship Strength approach. Some evidence: Doug and team created The Search Consortium, an industry-first partnership with Google and Yahoo!; launched Carlson Mobile, a group focused on mobilizing relationships and won Adweek’s most integrated campaign of 2007. Clients include: Visa, JP Morgan Chase, General Motors, Hallmark, Delta, P&G, Amtrak, Coca-Cola, Best Buy, JetBlue and Ford.

Before joining Carlson Marketing in 2006, Doug was Senior Partner and Managing Director at JWT’s RMG Connect. During this time, he established digital@jwt, combined JWT’s digital, direct and data service offerings to form connect@jwt, which ultimately lead to RMG Connect – one of the top non-traditional integrated communications agencies in the world. At RMG Connect, Doug ran the unit’s digital marketing operation and was responsible for notable clients such as Unilever, Nestle, Kraft, Macy’s, U.S. Marine Corps, HSBC, Orkin, Tribune Company, Western Union and Ford.

Doug’s prior experience includes building an Intranet for General Motors, developing an Extranet application for First Union National Bank, designing a lead acquisition campaign for a national Internet service provider, creating an e-commerce solution for an international computer company and streaming the first collegiate football game.

Doug Rozen’s Specialties:

A regular speaker at many of the top industry events, Doug is an active member of the Mobile Marketing Association, a regular feature for WBBM Chicago’s Noon Business Hour and was previously Senior Advisor and Adjunct Professor for DePaul University.
