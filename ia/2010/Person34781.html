Charlotte Cooper’s Summary

I am a thorough and competent researcher and writer with experience in marketing copywriting and features writing for both print publications and online.

My work has often followed the lines of mashing feminism, social and ethical politics with lifestyle and fashion, something I've enjoyed dearly. This has included writing articles for numerous independent press and copywriting for advertising and marketing.

I have a keen eye for proof reading, an interest in design and a nod in the direction of online and social media as well as the old hat tools of the marketing trade.

I have a working knowledge of HTML and high working knowledge of Photoshop and InDesign. I have designed and produced online and print materials for a range of commercial and third sector clients, including implementing templates and branding materials which can be adapted and used in house.
