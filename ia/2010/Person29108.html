Brian Veltman’s Summary

I'm kind of "addicted" to the Google Services, it's interesting how a search engine grow so fast into worlds largest website with all their handy and especially helpful tools like; Google Apps which is very attractive for small businesses.

I've got also experience in sound and light equipments.
