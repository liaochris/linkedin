To my customers, I bring honesty, dedication and over 30 years of sales and marketing experience. I joined CENTURY 21 Superstars so that I could bring the resources of the largest real estate office in Orange County and the most recognizable brand name in real estate to my customers. I have fully embraced the newest technologies that are available on the internet, hand held communication devices and the ever changing social media outlets. Our industry is rapidly changing from a protected knowledge base to an open, free flowing information exchange.

I specialize in residential real estate in the South Orange County communities including: Rancho Santa Margarita, Mission Viejo, Ladera Ranch, Coto de Caza, Lake Forest, Laguna Hills, Laguna Woods, Aliso Viejo, Laguna Niguel, Laguna Beach, Dana Point, San Juan Capistrano and San Clemente. My clients are first-time buyers, sellers (with or without equity), people relocating into or out of California, families with probate and estate settlement matters and investors seeking a profit center with real estate.

My industry network includes real estate agent / broker connections in all 50 states as well as throughout the world through the Century 21 Relocation and Referral network. Through this network, I am able to help individuals as well as corporations relocate employees into and out of the local market area.

I am a member of the National Association of REALTORS®, California Association of REALTORS®, and the Orange County Association of REALTORS®.

Being born a "natural drummer", I have been playing drums since the age of five. Although not my main focus these days, I still enjoy performing in a variety of musical situations from working with name artists in major venues in Los Angeles to playing in the bands in the local clubs.

John Alesi’s Specialties:

Assisting clients in buying and selling residential real estate is my specialty. Through my network of industry professionals I can also assist with commercial transactions.
