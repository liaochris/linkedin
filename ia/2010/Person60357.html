David Shaffer’s Summary

Our mission is to ensure that our clients "Stay one step ahead". Our clients have between 10 and 100 computers and most do not have an information technology employee on staff, however technology is as much a driver of their business as it is for a Fortune 1000 enterprise. We as a Managed Services Provider answer this call with a wrap-around solution called e-Care.

Our e-Care delivery platform monitors their network environment 24x7, and provides us alerts to trouble situations BEFORE they result in downtime and loss of productivity. e-Care also includes spam and virus control as well as an automated ticket system that allows clients to email us with problems and have it directly delivered to our help desk where the problem is solved by remote control of the troublesome computer or it is analyzed and passed on to a Field Engineer for a site visit and remediation. We offer this service as a complete package for a reasonable monthly fee, facilitating a stabilization of our client's IT budgets.

It is our friendly and professional "can-do" attitude combined with our proactive e-Care delivery system that enable our clients to realize productivity gains and "Stay one step ahead".
