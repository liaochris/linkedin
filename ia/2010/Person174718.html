ROBERT GEARHART’s Summary

Co-Founder at Corporate Health IQ, a wellness solutions provider headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio. Corporate Health IQ is our Trademark solution for improving health and connecting the gaps in reporting between annual Health Risk Assessments (HRA) and/or Biometric Screenings. One of the obstacles facing employees and employers who are doing annual HRA or annual biometric screenings is that they are unable to effectively monitor improvements in either measuring standard throughout the year. We can load employee HRA and/or biometric data into a personalized MyTrak M2 device helping the employee follow their track toward improved health in real time. We connect the annual HRA or biometric measures for employers allowing them to view improvements in health hour by hour, day by day throughout the year on the Employer Dashboard. Our solution is an interactive employee HRA throughout the year allowing them to progress on their own track toward improved health through the use of the M2 device.
Managing Partner at Insurance Marketing Resources, a master general agency headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio specializing in developing and delivering evidence based insurance solutions for our national network of General Agencies that give their brokers the competitive edge they need in today's marketplace.

Former President of Gearhart Insurance Agency, a full service regional employee benefits agency headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio which specializes in providing the best individual and group insurance products at the lowest cost from the leading companies in the industry.
