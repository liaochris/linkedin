Andy Besser’s Summary

Providing legal service plans and identity theft protection as employee benefits has been found to decrease absenteeism, increase productivity and gives ‘peace of mind’ to the employees knowing their entire families legal rights and identities are protected. These benefits can be provided as a ‘voluntary benefit’, at no cost to the company/business owner.

If you had the opportunity to contact a law firm for any reason and you wouldn't receive a bill, would you call them?? We provide affordable access to top-quality attorneys nationwide. By helping Small Business' with the Business Owners Legal Service Plan decrease their everyday legal expenses, business owners no longer need to worry about the high cost consultations, document & contract review or debt collection - these are just a few of the covered benefits.

The pairing of our legal plan and identity theft coverage has been documented as being the most robust product on the market today. We also provide legal service plans for: Individuals, Commercial Drivers, Law Officers, Real Estate Professionals & Foster Parents.
