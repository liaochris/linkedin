Greg Fleischaker’s Summary

Louisville Real Estate

As a life long Louisville resident and dedicated real estate professional, my sole goal as a Louisville real estate agent is to sell my client’s home just as quickly as possible for the highest price achievable, given the prevailing market conditions. To help achieve that goal, I immerse myself in studying market trends, not just for the city as a whole, but also for smaller markets, such as the Highlands, St. Matthews, Prospect and Anchorage.

In addition to being on top of real estate statistics for the Greater Louisville marketplace, and being quoted frequently by the Courier Journal, I also relentlessly research the most progressive sales techniques available, such as advertising on Craig’s List, single property websites, oversized yard signs, as well as utilizing YouTube, Facebook, Twitter and many other social media sites in order to sell my client’s property.

Social Media Consulting

As my Louisville Real Estate business has grown, and my website started generating lots of traffic, real estate agents from across the country began asking questions and seeking out my advice on how to leverage social media into new business. To help answer all of the incoming emails and phone calls, I founded a new website, www.GregFleischaker.com, to host free social media tutorials for real estate professionals, as well as small business owners from just about any industry.

Professional Real Estate Blogger

As part of my venture as real estate blogger for hire, I now offer blogging services for real estate agents all across the country. For a reasonable fee, I set up a WordPress blog for their market, and write an article once or twice a week, with graphs of market statistics included, to help boost their main website’s search engine rankings, and increase business at the same time.
