Barry Pearce’s Summary

20 years as a Financial Adviser first with the Pru then as an independent financial adviser from 1993.

As a company, we like to guide clients and their families towards the future they desire by forward planning and dealing with changes in circumstances and needs.

My business philosophy is that through good times and the current not so good times, people always seek advice from people they know and trust. A good reputation brings us far more enquiries via satisfied clients than any advertising.

Barry Pearce’s Specialties:

Retirement planning for those young enough to plan for it and for our "more mature" clients wishing to get the most from whatever savings and pensions they have accumulated in their working life.
Individual and corporate protection
Mortgages, re-mortgages buy-to-lets etc.
