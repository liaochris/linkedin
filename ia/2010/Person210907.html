Veni Markovski’s Summary

I am consulting a number of businesses and governments on different Information Technologies- and Communications-related issues.

I am online since September 1990, and I have been involved since the early ages in the deployment and development of the Internet in Europe, in particular in Bulgaria.

My work is focused not only on using technology, but helping businesses, non-profits and whole countries by using technology to develop people-oriented knowledge society.
