Cathy Sexton’s Summary

Cathy is a workplace productivity training. Cathy brings a wealth of experience in business management, training, and maximizing productivity and employee performance. Her knowledge of systems and processes that improve productivity and simplify life, she develops solutions for each client’s specific needs. Helps Individuals and companies save time and money. Reducing stress and frustraction by being more focused, organized and productive then they have ever been before.

During Cathy’s professional career, she has had the opportunity to provide workplace productivity,accounting services, QuickBooks training, and organizational management services to hundreds of small businesses and individuals. These services include: Info-Excellence™ Email Training, The GO System ©Training, Office Organizing Day™, Productivity Assessment™, Cathy has built a reputation for organizational solutions that guarantee 300% ROI, 10 – 25% increases in productivity, and save 10 - 15 days a year just by controlling your email.

Cathy is a co-author of “Focus, Organization, and Productivity,” and “Exploring Productivity” both books are based on improving success in the workplace.

Cathy is a member of The Network for Productivity Excellence, the National Chapter of Association of Professional Organizers (NAPO) and NAPO St. Louis Chapter and the local chapter of American Society of Training and Development (ASTD).

Cathy has been recognized by the Fenton Chamber of Commerce as the Small Business of the Year in 2006, was nominated for Financial Services Champion of the Year in 2005 and 2006. Cathy is certified through the Hemphill Institute of Productivity, Certified GO System© Trainer, Certified by Cohesive Knowledge for Info-Excellence Training, Harold Taylor’s Time Management Consultants Program, Certified Productivity trainer and the Women Entrepreneur Program through St. Louis Community College.
