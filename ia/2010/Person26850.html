Brady Augustine’s Summary

Mr. Augustine is a value-driven leader who served as a Senior Advisor to the Administrator at the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services and as a Chief of Health Systems Development for Florida Medicaid. He has an extensive background in health policy, economics, quality improvement, and information technology and has served as a direct report to three chief executive officers or administrators of large health care organizations. His direct experience spans Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payors, providers, and patient organizations. While at CMS, he led the development of the Agency's flagship quality improvement activity - AV Fistula First - and the development of its flagship IT system - CROWNWeb.

Brady Augustine’s Specialties:

Management, health policy, health information technology, quality improvement, operations research
