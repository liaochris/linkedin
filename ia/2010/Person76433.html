Chris S. Thompson’s Summary

With nearly 30 years diverse experience in the energy consulting industry, Mr. Thompson has provided innovative solutions and expert analysis to clients ranging from small businesses and home owners to large industrial plants and investor-owned utilities. Mr. Thompson has supported an extensive array of services to include technical and financial analysis, hardware and software development for energy information systems, and all levels of energy auditing and technical consulting. He was instrumental in the development and management of service bureaus providing residential and commercial energy audit services and reports, as well as numerous demand response programs and supporting operations centers. In the field, he has conducted thousands of residential energy audits and hundreds of commercial, institutional and industrial surveys and audits.
Mr. Thompson provides technical expertise and project management services to included residential, commercial and industrial energy audits, project management for energy measure installations, software and web development, demand response programs, and retro-commissioning.
