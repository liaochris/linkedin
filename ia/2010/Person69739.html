Daniel Feygin’s Summary

Daniel Feygin has a strong technology background and entrepreneurial experience in U.S. and Russia. Mr. Feygin began his career with IBM while still in high school and later worked at ENOVIA, a U.S. division of French PLM software leader Dassault Systemes. He's held top-level positions in several innovative software companies with a total of 8 years of product research and development experience.

Daniel's current research includes SOA design, enterprise architecture, policy-driven network computing, large-scale evolutionary metadata management and business-IT alignment.
