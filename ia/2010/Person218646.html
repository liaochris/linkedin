Zenobia Garrison’s Summary

Zenobia Garrison’s Specialties:

Zenobia caters to women of spirituality and Christain faith looking to live in purpose through business ownership. They are new business owners trying to launch their business while they work. She attracts women who are interested in building their solo-business, from the ground up — not just obtaining casual contract work from time to time. Success Transitions serves a community of coaches, consultants, speakers, trainers, and other independent service providers.

    Success Transitions, LLC

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Zenobia Garrison is an avid educator and passionate about helping to improve the lives of others and supporting individuals in meeting their most important professional and personal goals. Her message to new women soloprofessionals is: You can achieve business success by being intentional in all aspects of your life and business, and becoming entrepreneurial minded. Zenobia has devoted the past 15 years of her professional career to counseling, teaching, training and coaching. Her coaching philosophy revolves around three principles: bringing passion and purpose to your business, being your authentic self in every aspect of your business, and taking care of self. A certified coach, Zenobia has received her formal training exclusively through International Coaching Federation (ICF) approved agencies. Zenobia currently resides in Maryland with her husband and two children.
