Giuseppe Andretta’s Summary

I love photography since I was 12. My career began in the late 80's as a film printer. I experienced a brief period as an assistant still-life photographer in 1991 and then I started my own business as a freelance. In 1996 I co-founded a digital printing service, specialized in large format inkjet prints, fine art prints and digital images edting and retouching. Since 2004 I am an independent and skilled consultant specialized in digital photography and digital color management. I am an Adobe Certified Instructor in Photoshop CS4 and an Apple Distinguished Professional in Aperture 2. I have been teaching for five years in a couple of tecnichal instiutes for photography and grafic arts. In 2004 I founded Color Connection together with Claudio Marconato, an Adobe print Specialist. Our main business is to provide professional training courses and seminars on Adobe and Apple softwares together with digital mangement consultancies and services. In the last years I was engaged as trainer and/or consultant by Apple, Wacom, Nikon, Epson, X-Rite, CNA, FIOF and Teacher in a Box.

Giuseppe Andretta’s Specialties:

Digital color management. Industrial photography. Digital images editing and photo retouching. Raw capture and digital imaging software training: Adobe Camera Raw, Adobe Photoshop Lightroom, Apple Aperture and Adobe Photoshop CS4
