Daniel Perez Lacera’s Summary

Responsible for the overall management of NACA's local offices and National Operations. The Operations Department ensures that the NACA Program is properly implemented and operating effectively throughout the nation.

Primary Point of Contact for any actions required in response to congressional inquiries. Develops and implements strategies for expanding NACA's reputation and presence in Congress.

NACA, (NEIGHBORHOOD ASSISTANCE CORPORATION OF AMERICA) has established the National Standard in its Homeownership Programs. We are a National Non-Profit Homeownership HUD certified counseling and community advocacy organization started here in the city of Boston in 1988. Our qualified members have obtained America’s Best Mortgage through – no down payment, no closing costs, no fees, no requirement for perfect credit and always at a below market FIXED interest rate.

NACA has been in the forefront of fighting discriminatory and predatory lending for over 20 years. NACA provides the most affordable mortgage solution for both homebuyers and homeowners.
