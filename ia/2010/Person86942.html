Jacob Morris’s Summary

Jacob Morris is a Senior Product Manager on the Customer Experience Planning & Development team within the marketing division at Delta Air Lines’ world headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia with over 10 years experience in the travel industry.

In this role, Jacob influences and guides cross-divisional teams in defining, developing, and implementing customer experience initiatives with touchpoints relevantly designed for both high value and essential customer segments.

Jacob is no stranger to the marketing industry, having spent previous years as a strategist and conceptualizer charged with opening delta.com to the world in five languages and 68 countries, as well as Delta’s first foray into social media with the Delta Blog & YouTube, as featured on CNN, USA Today, and the New York Times.

With an uninhibited sense of creativity and charismatic personality, Jacob is driven by the emerging ideas that help shape and enhance human interactivity.

Jacob Morris’s Specialties:

customer experience management, product development, product management, product marketing, user experience design, website content & design, e-commerce development, relationship management, project lifecycle management, website internationalization, contract negotiation, web standards & web 2.0, social media strategies, information architecture and communication design
