Cherie Hebert’s Summary

Brand strategist. Problem solver. Entreprenuer.
Helping BBR Creative grow into a top notch advertising agency has been my focus for the past 12 years. My primary role is account strategist, devising plans to help organizations grow and improve brand recognition. I also oversee new business development and account service - attracting new clients and keeping them! I use my creative background as a designer and experience as a marketer to help clients and non-profit organizations reach their potential. I am passionate about each client's business that I serve. I'm here to help clients succeed.

Personal mission: To identify potential, create opportunity and enrich life - for myself and others.

It is my ultimate goal to one day establish and run a business built on the principles of social entrepreneurship.

Cherie Hebert’s Specialties:

Brand management. Marketing, advertising and public relations strategies. Identity development. New business development. Graphic and web design. Account service. Connectivity. Experience in the areas of health care, community banking, oil & gas service companies, temporary utility rental industry, communications and food products and service.

Cherie Hebert’s Experience

  • Partner

    BBR Creative

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1997 — Present (13 years 5 months)

    Cherie Hebert is the senior account planner and one of the owners of BBR Creative,
    an advertising, design and marketing firm based in Lafayette, Louisiana. Since its inception in 1997, BBR Creative has become the leader in the market for their creative advertising campaigns, design and marketing materials.

    BBR Creative is a brand communications agency that works in partnership with organizations and corporations providing creative solutions to increase brand awareness and grow business.

    BBR provides develops brand strategies for clients in a variety of markets including healthcare, real estate development, oil & gas services, finance, telecommunications and the food industry.

    Versatility, vision and strategic planning prowess have been the hallmarks of Cherie’s 19 years of experience in the marketing industry. Throughout her career, her responsibilities have included strategic planning, creative development and design, broadcast and print production, and leading an entire marketing team as creative director – all of which have helped shape her into the superb marketing strategist that she is today.

    Cherie founded BBR Creative, along with partners Sara Ashy, Cathi Pavy and Karla Meche in June of 1997. An agency that the founders envisioned would be driven by creativity, hard work and professional integrity – a place of vibrant energy and serious marketing. Over the years, BBR has served hundreds of satisfied clients in many industries, won numerous prestigious awards and established themselves as a major force in the Louisiana advertising marketplace.
