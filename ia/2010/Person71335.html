Frank van Eijkern’s Summary

After my study in Nuclear Physics, I have been working at the University of Utrecht, the University of Groningen and I was the first director of the Association of Universities in the Netherlands. After that I worked as the general manager at the educational examination institute Cito.

After more than 28 years of working in leading positions in the world of universities and 6 years as the CEO of Cito, I decided to spend the last years of my working career as interimmanager and consultant.

I have been doing this now already for 7 years. I had about 25 assignments.

I am specialized in businessprocesses in organizations with a public goal but a commercial way of thinking. I also work as an executive coach.

For more than 35 years I am also involved in all kind of social responsabilities. In all kind of branches: health care, hospitality, education, culture, leasure, nature, development etc.
