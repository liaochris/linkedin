Scott Cohen’s Summary

Scott Cohen is the Founder/CEO of Red Tie Media, an entertainment, technology, and media consulting company.

From 2007-2009, Scott was Co-Founder & CEO of Dimestore Media, Inc. which was sold to Knowledge Networks in 2009. In the years prior to Dimestore, he was a Co-Founder and senior level executive of two companies that went public: 24/7 Media, now a division of WPP Advertising Group (NASDAQ: WPPGY), and LivePerson (NASDAQ: LPSN). Scott also served as President of Game Trust., which was acquired by Real Networks (NASDAQ: RNWK).

Scott earned an MBA in 1988 from the William E. Simon School of Business at the University of Rochester, after ten years in commercial and investment real estate. He is a frequent speaker and panel moderator for industry conferences in interactive media. Scott currently serves on the Board of Leadfusion, a financial software company, and the Advisory Board of Dimestore Media, a company owned by Knowledge Networks.

Scott Cohen’s Specialties:

Scott has accumulated over thirty years of experience in strategic deal making, negotiation and leadership in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments; since 1995, he has played a major role in developing businesses during the growth of the Internet in the USA and Europe.
