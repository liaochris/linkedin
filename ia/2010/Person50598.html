Dave Eichorn’s Summary

28 years in Television as a Meteorologist.

Left TV at the end of 2008 to go back to school and work toward a masters in Environmental Science and have more time with family.

Am skilled at communicating complex scientific topics to the ley person without reducing quality of scientific information, and doing so to people with relatively short attention spans. Love teaching. Have been teaching adjunct at SUNY Oswego for 4 years. Am also currently teaching adjunct while going to school at Onondaga Community College while taking some Gen Ed. classes which will be need toward advanced degree.

Dave Eichorn’s Specialties:

Am effective as a Teacher, able to explain, persuade, and manage.
