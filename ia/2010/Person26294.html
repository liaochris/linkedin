Ivan Božulić’s Summary

I have recently obtained a deegree in molecular biology from the University of Zagreb, Faculty of Science, but most, if not all of my work experience has been in the field of journalism and publishing.

I am currently employed as Editor-in-Chief of FHM Croatia, and my previous experiences include working as Executive Editor and writer for said magazine and as a writer and, for a short time, a member of the editorial staff at Mobil Media Magazine.

Although my field of study is in no way related to my current line of work, there is always a chance that I will return to biology. I am no longer interested in laboratory work - both because I am personally not willing to make the sacrifices that that particular job requires any more; and because I strongly feel I have the abilities and potential to take on more demanding positions.
