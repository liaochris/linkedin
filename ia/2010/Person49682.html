David Linhardt’s Summary

Professional Summary

Dave Linhardt is a serial entrepreneur with 18 years of direct marketing experience in both online and offline channels.  He’s been a corporate executive, a marketer, a solutions provider and a pioneer in developing new direct marketing models.  David got his marketing stripes at Procter & Gamble, the inventor of modern-day brand management.  David invented the Digital One Rate program at AT&T Wireless, creating $1.5 billion in enterprise value and transforming the wireless industry.  After turning around a $60 million division at Experian, David helped get Yesmail, Inc. to profitability for the first time in its history.  After a successful sale to InfoUSA, David left Yesmail to start Maverick Direct Marketing.  With only $5K in total investment capital, David grew Maverick to $3 million in revenue with 48 months of consecutive profitability.  Today, David leads Life Phase Media, an innovative developer of break-through direct marketing models that combine technology, data and analytics.  

David has a BS in Chemical Engineering from Purdue University and an MBA from Harvard Business School.  David and his family live in the north shore area of Chicago.
