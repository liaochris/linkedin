Jennifer Goldin’s Summary

Having worked with Fortune 500 brands in nearly every major business sector, the diversity of Jennifer’s experience enables her to deliver a fresh perspective and differentiating solutions to her clients’ toughest marketing challenges.

In her current role as a founding partner of The Collective | PR and previous role as an executive for leading PR agencies, Jennifer has launched brands, established strategic alliances, conducted consumer education programs and cultivated influencer networks. Her strategic know-how has made her an invaluable client counselor, driving award-winning campaigns for leaders such as Pfizer, Unilever, Philips, P&G, Schick, Revlon, Nike and industry groups.

Her clients value her research driven approach, which challenges them to not only define their audience, but to understand what drives, inspires and ultimately moves their target to action. The result - a track record for delivering laser-focused, high impact campaigns.

Prior to forming The Collective | PR, Jennifer ran her own consultancy specializing in marketing to women. Her understanding of what triggers women’s decisions has fueled influential strategies in a range of categories - from launching an eco-friendly bridal collection to educating moms on teen medicine abuse to tackling the most “sensitive” women’s issues. Jennifer was also tapped by top agencies to lead creative strategy sessions and act as a behind-the-scenes expert on top beauty, health and lifestyle accounts.

Earlier in her career, in senior management at firms including MS&L and GCI Group (now Cohn & Wolfe), Jennifer led the agencies’ largest consumer products accounts. An expert collaborator, her ability to mobilize the right marketing mix translated to tangible business results. Zest Body Wash’s double digit sales growth and Schick Intuition’s slot as the #1 selling women’s razor are just a few examples of her work.

Jennifer lives in NYC. She is easily swayed by good chocolate.
