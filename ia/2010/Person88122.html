James Heaton’s Experience

  • President, Creative Director

    Tronvig Group

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 1997 — Present (13 years 5 months)

    I believe in the truth—its power, efficiency and effectiveness. There is no better basis for a brand.

    The context of brand communications is rapidly changing, and brand messaging can no longer be controlled entirely from the top. Brand consumers can now easily find out what's behind almost any messaging strategy. I therefore advocate using the truth as the foundation for all brand communications. This approach is both effective and efficient. Tronvig Group's task is to unearth the essential truths of a business and its best practices, devise custom branding and marketing strategies around these truths and use them to enlighten and inspire loyalty and trust among the brand's consumers.
