Keith Seay’s Summary

I have spent the last ten years of my career working in the food and hospitality industry in a variety of capacities.

I first became versed in customer care while working as a server in the Washington, D.C. area and at the nationally-known, historic Birchmere Music Hall in Alexandria, VA. It was at Trader Joe's where I ultimately polished these skills and also still work as a Crew Member.

Trader Joe's is a "specialty" grocery store specializing in one-of-a-kind international gourmet foods at rock-bottom prices. I would take it a little further to say it is more of an experience than a grocery store. We want the customer to feel as if they are on a tropical vacation.

Crew members kick "grocery clerking" up a notch with fanatical attention to providing the customer with an overwhelming sense of "wow." I begin every shift desiring to make at least one customer's day and I'm used to getting better than 100% results every time.

In the long term I would like to be a consultant for companies seeking to optimize their customer service departments, or if they don't yet have one then guiding them in its creation.
