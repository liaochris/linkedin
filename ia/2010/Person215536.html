Billy Mitchell’s Summary

A graduate of the University of the Arts in Philadelphia, I attained a BFA in graphic design in 2006. After five years in the city of brotherly love, I set my sights on the Big Apple. For the past two years I have worked in Manhattan for ad companies including Hill Holliday, Atmosphere BBDO, Blast Radius, One Source VMS, and Solefood NYC, a sneaker boutique and art gallery in Tribeca. In my spare time, photography, skateboarding, and biking are among the things that fullfill my zest for life. I'm always interested in meeting new artists and collaborating on design pieces, so feel free to drop me a line.

Billy Mitchell’s Specialties:

Branding and identity, logo design, photo retouching, grid system and layout structures, web banner design
