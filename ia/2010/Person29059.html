Brian Gavin’s Summary

Brian Gavin, "Brian The Cutter", is an internationally recognized diamond expert and the designer/creator of A CUT ABOVE diamonds - the first branded hearts and arrows diamonds to be sold on the internet in 1999. He co-founded Whiteflash in 2000, and was Executive Vice-President from June 2001 - December 2008. He introduced his BRIAN GAVIN SIGNATURE hearts and arrows diamonds in March 2009. He is also well known for his creativity in jewelry design and photographic techniques, and is sought after for his deep knowledge of all aspects of e-commerce in the diamond and jewelry industry.

Brian Gavin’s Specialties:

Diamond cutting techniques, strategic diamond development and purchasing, technical sales training, custom jewelry design, diamond and gemstone photography, and all aspects of e-commerce in the diamond and jewelry industry.

    (Luxury Goods & Jewelry industry)

    I purchased Alpha Creations, Inc. in 1993, selling loose diamonds wholesale to jewelers across the country. There was a small diamond cutting and polishing factory on the premises. "A Cut Above" diamonds were the first branded hearts and arrows diamonds to be sold on the internet. I was involved in the creation of the e-commerce model and all processes and techniques related to my involvement in diamonds, jewelry customization, photography and customer interaction online, which continued to be perfected in Alpha Creations until May, 2001.
