Timothy Haley’s Summary

Advocate Wealth Management is an independent, full service, fee-only financial planning and investment advisory firm exclusively serving doctors and dentists.

As trusted advisors and specialists we expertly serve medical professionals with a thorough understanding of their unique needs.

Our exclusive system helps clients make smart choices about their money while it organizes and secures all of their personal/business documents.

At Advocate Wealth Management, we believe that financial planning is first and foremost a helping profession, and like those that we serve we've taken an oath to put our client's interest first.

Timothy Haley’s Interests:

As a life-long Massachusetts resident, I'm committed to helping improve the lives of others through my community involvement. For the past nine years I've been privileged to mentor a young man through Big Brothers Big Sisters of Massachusetts Bay -- a wonderful organization. Having witnessed firsthand the tremendous gift hospice was to my own family, I became involved as a hospice volunteer -- unlike any volunteer work I've ever done, I find it deeply moving and meaningful. I'm proud to play a small role in the Massachusetts 'Financial Planning Association's award winning initiative, serving as a financial coach to cancer patients at The Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. As a Personal Financial Counselor I provide support to soon-to-be deployed military personnel and their families. I also lend my financial talents to an inspiring grassroots effort that provides hundreds of girls in East Africa a secondary school education -- http://growththroughlearning.org/
