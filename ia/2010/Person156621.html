Peter Kusterer’s Summary

Founder and driving force behind NvestNtech and N2tech. More than 30 years of sales experience, having served in a number of Executive Sales Management positions with a wide range of companies – company revenue ranged from $3 Million to over $10 Billion. Touched numerous markets and industry segments as well as stretching geographic boundaries across countries.

I enjoy the opportunity to write guest articles on a wide variety of business topics as well as serving as the Editor for our monthly eNewsletter; that opened the door to the salesbarriers blog as an additional soapbox for discussion.

Doing commercial voice-overs encouraged me to embark on NvestNtech’s popular One Goal - Revenue Generation (R) series of Podcasts – from the idea of a book, we took the audio route instead. Multiple channels of distribution for our Podcasts led to the formation of the LinkedIn Group “One Goal - Revenue Generation”; I would encourage you to listen and become a member of the Group on LinkedIn.

Peter Kusterer’s Specialties:

Putting in place a disciplined process to accelerate sales and revenue contribution; we often overhaul CRM systems and software. We leverage others to do the heavy lifting for our client’s business development needs.

Our work may lead to technology licensing, or new business ventures; that is where N2tech comes in. N2tech develops software and IT solutions on a contractual basis. This may include new ideas and unproven models as well as revamping existing applications developed by others.
