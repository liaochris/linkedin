Nathaniel McNamara’s Summary

I am the Founder and President of SocialFeet. SocialFeet enables activity stream publishing - to push information about the activities on a site into the streams on Facebook, Twitter, and SocialFeet. Our premium partners are seeing 40%+ of their referral traffic coming from us as a result of a simple integration.

Entrepreneur:
I was an entrepreneur twice before, serving as President of Chintano and Director of Business Development for PrivacyBank.com.

While at Chintano, I managed all of the day to day operations, including business development, negotiations, project management, and relationship management. I was extensively involved in fund raising and hiring of new staff. Datran Media acquired Chintano in 2007. PrivacyBank.com, an online commerce company designed to streamline online transactions. The company was acquired for $36 million in 2000 by InfoSpace and the technology was used to help serve AT&T, Cingular, American Express, and others in their mobile and online commerce platforms.

Investor:
I have also been an investor, most recently working for HIG Ventures, identifying opportunities in new media, wireless, and advertising.

Other:
I also worked as a Vice President at JP Morgan Chase where I led the release of four new wireless/mobile products to grow the credit card business. I was part of the original team that developed the PayPass/Blink card for contactless payments.

My career began at McKinsey and Company, and I previously held internships at Andersen Consulting (now Accenture), Millennium Technology Ventures, and the White House.

I was also the founder of NextMiami, an organization designed to bring together entrepreneurs, technologists, and investors.
