Jesper Andersen’s Summary

Public relations and communication specialist. I started my career in PR in 2001 as a freelancer, working mostly for smaller companies and non-profit organisations doing theatre and musical productions. In 2003 I joined Mannov – Denmark’s largest PR consultancy – where I became a consultant upon completing my master’s degree at the start of 2006.

Since then I have worked with a wide variety of aspects of PR and communication including press relations, consumer goods product PR, event planning and execution, corporate PR, campaign and project management, crisis and issues management, strategy development and public affairs.

During my time at Mannov I have had the pleasure of working for some of the largest consumer brands and major corporations in Scandinavia as well as a few international clients.

Later I joined VisitDenmark as their head of corporate press relations for 17 months until cutbacks unfortunately ended that employment. At VisitDenmark I helped set long-term strategic communication goals and improve the strategic use of media monitoring and analysis.

My latest interest in the field of communication is the new dimension of social/digital media and networks – the complexity and possibilities they add to our way of communicating with the world at large, and also the social context in which they emerge, exist and expand.

I blog (in Danish) about social/digital media at Start snakken! (www.startsnakken.wordpress.com) and in English at www.askaboutsocialmedia.com.

I am involved in several professional networks for people in media and public relations and use both social media and these networks to stay informed about the latest developments in the field of communication. Since 2005 I have also used a professional mentor to advance my career.

I am currently on the lookout for a new job in either press relations/PR or social media and communication in general - international openings are also of interest!

Jesper Andersen’s Specialties:

Public relations consulting, media consulting, social/digital media, campaign and project management.
