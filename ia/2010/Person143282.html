Michael Peart’s Summary

After years of studies and investments in Stocks, Real Estate, and Nationwide and Private Lending, Copper River Funding, LLC (CRF) was designed and created to offer investors safety, security, and a contractual rate of return. CRF specializes in the unique opportunities that now exist in the commercial Real Estate and private lending marketplace. We have combined our unique competitive advantage of multiple referral sources nationwide (over 300 net branches), in addition to developing a proprietary web-based system that allows our Committed Capital Partner Network (CCPN) to “pick and choose” their best Return on Investment options.

CRF has identified “niche” markets specifically between $100,000 to $1,500,000, and we have teamed up with our national network of brokers to screen the best lending opportunities available today! Our focus is to fund deals that have a solid exit strategy, in addition to a sizable equity position, thus allowing our investments to receive a strong return while protecting principal.

Copper River Funding stands ready to be proactive and allow our partners investments to grow while mitigating risk. CRF considers itself a service provider to both lenders and borrowers by identifying projects in need of rapid, short-term capital, and teaming them with Committed Capital Partners in need of productive capital/investment projects. Money always grows where it is treated best, and CRF is committed to diligent, wise and patient steps that allows our partners to enjoy a successful strategic relationship as we grow forward. Copper River Funding is proud to empower others to take control of their investments.
