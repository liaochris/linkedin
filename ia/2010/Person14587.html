Ann Stovel’s Summary

Business development professional - highly skilled in identifying new opportunities, resources & partnerships and turning opportunities to value. Specialty within sales is "hunting" - new sales acquisition & relationship management.

Experienced in fund-raising; grants, government contracts (RFP), joint venture equity, debt financing, state & federal tax credits. Raising money is an adrenaline rush – I love it!

I am "on the market" and searching for an opportunity with one company where I can focus my energy. I crave being part of a team who can relate to Entrepreneur Insomnia aka “I am Thinking Instead of Sleeping” syndrome. As a life-long entrepreneur who has started, and participated in running successful companies, I have the ability to act in the short-term and think long-term, in a way that results in meeting immediate goals while planting seeds of success for the future. As a big picture thinker, I see the company as a whole and therefore work to support all departments and ensure the success of my co-workers.

I'm the type of person who if I see someone on the street holding a map, I go up & ask if I can help. I enjoy connecting people & problem-solving - just ask me & I will help you if I can.
