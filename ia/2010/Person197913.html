Susannah Greenberg’s Summary

Susannah Greenberg Public Relations is a full-service public relations firm for book publicity. The firm uses leading edge communications technology to maximize media exposure and offers customized publicity campaigns to meet your needs.

Susannah Greenberg’s Specialties:

Public relations for publishers, authors and the book industry. Placing features, reviews and interviews in radio, TV and Internet. National and local media. Best sellers represented include "BIRD SONGS: 250 North American Birds in Song," "Who Moved My Cheese?" and "Longaberger." Best selling authors include Alan Dershowitz, Monty Roberts, Dr. Robert Coles, Robert Mankoff, Roz Chast, Charles Panati, Joan Wester Anderson, Dr. Robert Atkins, and more.
