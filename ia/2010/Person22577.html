Charles Besondy’s Summary

Charles Besondy is recognized as a champion for B2B marketing optimization and transformation. He is also a leading authority on the topic of interim management.

His book, "Leadership On Demand . . .", and his blog, "One Riot - One Ranger" present useful advice on how companies can drive growth by leveraging interim leadership in marketing and sales functions.

His other blog, "The Sales Funnel Fanatic", presents perspectives on how companies can increase revenue growth by aligning Sales and Marketing to the sales funnel.

If a company suspects their marketing is under-performing or is not aligned with business goals, Charles steps in to right the ship with hands-on management, consulting, training and/or coaching.

Besondy is an executive with eight years consulting and interim management experience. This is underpinned by 25 years of marketing leadership primarily within small and mid-market software/Web businesses that place an emphasis on action and growth.

Charles Besondy’s Specialties:

B2B marketing training, revenue planning, sales and marketing alignment, strategic positioning, messaging, competitive analysis, market analysis.

Speaking on topics related to funnel management, sales and marketing alignment, and interim management.
