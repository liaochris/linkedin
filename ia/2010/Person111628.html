I am curious, courageous, and driven to new achievements for your teams in customer sevice, teamwork, communication, and change.

My Mission: Inspire, educate and deliver tangible improvements in people-skills especially among diverse people. This includes managing different personality types, interacting with different cultures in global business, collaborating across job specialities.

Delivery venues: Keynotes, workshops, webinars,
videocons, blogging and practical advice through coaching and consulting.

Customers:Fortune 500 companies, gov't, and mid-size organizations (including non-profits). In middle management and out through the the teams, I activate behavioral change for business success.

My Approach
-------------------
Keynotes: Inspiration to action. Audiences consistently say that I rivet them with energy, humor, facts, insights, and solutions on improving their people-skills for greater long term career success.

Workshops: Absolutely customized to your team's needs. Beyond the standard perspective, I guide you to discover the hidden strengths of your teamwork, customer relations, and change-ability. Interactive applied learning that subsequently leads to peer-to-peer learning and coaching.
