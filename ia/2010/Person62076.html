Ed Reese’s Summary

I provide search engine optimization (SEO) and analytics consulting for advertising agencies, web design firms, and marketing communication companies that don't have internal resources dedicated to SEO. I work both behind the scenes (typically for site reviews and recommendations) as well as directly with clients on behalf of agencies (implementing recommended search engine optimization strategies).

My 15 years of sales and marketing experience has been a great compliment to my search engine optimization and analytics expertise. I understand the importance of preserving visual identity (look & feel), committing to accurate project timelines, and staying on budget. My continued work with Hotbed Media has provided a solid understanding of what agencies need and how to work with them efficiently. It's also hammered home the importance of quality content, perspective, and story.

For agencies without dedicated SEO resources, this is a great opportunity to expand their scope of work. It also generates new accounts that are currently evaluating search vendors but will require design work or marketing support in the future. Having a project plan with web design, marketing initiatives, and search engine optimization in mind from the onset is an advantage for both the client and the agency. I'm available at any point during the sales process as well as client presentations (whether in-person or via conference call).
