Stephen Dill’s Summary

Throughout my experience—Army officer on a Division-level R&D presentation team, systems integration consultant, sales manager, directing a Web development team, a Web evangelist in a Fortune 100 company, CEO of a start up, and sales & marketing consultant to businesses and agencies—understanding and communications have been my strength. I present well, I listen well, I understand strategy and market dynamics, and I ask the right questions to get to fundamentals quickly. What I don't know I learn quickly and I am told my ideas are on target and timely. I am persistent, flexible and understanding. It has been my good fortune to become a consultant to most of my clients and a friend to many.

One use of strong communication skills is teaching. And the best teachers are great listeners with the domain knowledge to know when their students have learned all that the teacher can share. With the incontrovertible evidence of social media's ability to accelerate brand awareness, community, and competitive advantage, I have begun to offer my experience in social media to schools and corporations whose constituents (students, employees, clients and shareholders) desperately need to attain a comfort level and mastery of the tools of social media. Personable, humorous, concise and efficient in conducting my classes, I look forward to helping many to learn and leverage a new skill set. Download my course outlines from my Slideshare tab or SRDInterActive.com for more information.

Stephen Dill’s Specialties:
