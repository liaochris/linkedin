Julie Cantu’s Summary

My business is helping you market your business better.

People start businesses because they know how to do, build or fix something. But they don't always know how to effectively market that thing they do, build or fix.

It's easy to get caught up in the daily operations and not take time to step outside of your business to work on your business. That's where I come in.

I help small business owners make sense of marketing. I fix their problems and come up with creative solutions and fresh new ideas.

I analyze everything, from their printed and web materials, any advertising they investing in, sales strategy, customer service processes, how they answer their phones...

Everything you do that you do that interacts somehow with your customers and prospects is what makes up your marketing. Not just the ads you buy or the email blasts you send.

So why am I qualified to do this?

I'm an entrepreneur myself. I've owned / ran small businesses for the past 14 years. Feb '10 will mark 10 years completely self-employed! I understand the struggles and needs of small business.

I've made a business of marketing other small businesses.
I eat, sleep and breath marketing. I have since I was a child.

I've invested years of my time and countless $$ on furthering my education on every subject pertaining to marketing. I talk to area business owners everyday, and with every conversation I learn something new.

I was born with an overload of creativity. Creative problem solving and idea generation is my biggest asset. I can see things that aren't there yet. Most importantly....I can make them come to life!

In addition to consulting, I own 360 Graphics, LLC, a full service design & print firm. I custom design anything my clients need for print or web at great prices and focus on what's going to solve their problems and produce the best results.
