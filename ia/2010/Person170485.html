Monica Rebella’s Summary

Monica Rebella, CPA offers 20+ years of accounting and tax expertise, which includes 13 years with the "Big 4", and "Big 8" firms. She is president of the Orange County/Long Beach chapter of the California Society of CPAs and is a member of the AICPA. She has conducted training sessions for accounting firms, as well as lectured on tax and business matters to the California Society of CPAs, UCLA Dental School and the Orange County Dental Academy. She currently has her own firm in California, providing accounting, tax and consulting services for over 300 businesses throughout California, specializing in dental offices and small businesses.

To learn how she can help your dental practice or small business with accounting, tax, and consulting, call now at 714-619-0667 for a free, 30 minute consultation
