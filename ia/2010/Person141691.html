Mara Hawks’s Summary

I enjoy working with First-Time Home Buyers - NEST EGGS come in all sizes. When you first begin thinking of buying a home, there are many questions and concerns, anxiety and excitement. Taking time to research and look at different shapes and sizes of homes is a big part of the process, (sort of like sitting on the egg!), and definitely the first step after getting some finances in order. Once you decide on neighborhoods you like, and also on a few basic things that your home must have, I can set you up with a customized New Listings Notification, which can assist you in your search for a home.is You can be notified by email of new listings that come on the market and meet your criteria. This tool helps you in a very timely way (you are notified within 24 hours of a new property being listed on the Lee County MLS). It also helps you understand the local market and can help you stay on track with your search. When a property comes on the market that looks like a possibility your future home, just contact me and I can make a showing appointment for you. Sometimes, new buyers find exactly what they want on our first trip out to view properties!

Mara Hawks’s Specialties:

I can assist you in buying or selling a home. I love the EDUCATION part of the Real Estate industry. I take great care in listening to you, learning of your needs, researching, finding solutions, and helping you to understand the homebuying or selling processes.
