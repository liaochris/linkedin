Victor Rey’s Summary

My business life as an entrepreneur started at the tender age of 10 and did many things to earn my own money all the way through high school and my short college experience. I started my first official business fresh out of high school and quit college when I knew I would never work for another person in my life. It was a long 16 year roller coaster ride, but the education I have received is far beyond what I ever could have learned in any university. The financial rewards unparalleled too! Once a year I personally go to China to find reliable manufacturers for individuals and companies for a fraction of the cost if you went yourself. I have a college-educated, English-speaking representative in China, along with other working relationships with manufacturers who is ready to assist when needed.

Now, my expertise is a-mile-from-the-box marketing. I like to call it Future-Forward thinking. If you want to start a business and hit the ground running and watch it grow, I am the man to do it. Creative thinking is my strongest asset, as well as the ability to bring your ideas and mine to fruition! Unrealistic thinking is a mindset, not reality, but if you have an idea, or an existing market that has not blossomed into volumes of money for you and you are willing to do what it takes, call on me!
