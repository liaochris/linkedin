Michael Feldman’s Summary

President of TOP BUTTON (topbutton.com)
President of The Top Secret (TheTopSecret.com)
President of Chameleon Platform Technology

Michael has 25 years of Software / IT background including software design, development, system architecture, product development, IA analysis and design, business objects, relational architecture, front-end, DB as well as middleware.

Michael has over 12 years experience in Brand Building, Strategic Analysis and Planning, Product Launches, Online Marketing, Team Building, Michael brings tremendous insight into consumer behavior and first-hand experience with the highs and lows of building an online brand specielizing in e-commerce, media, advertising, fashion, luxury goods, and interactive business solutions.

Consulting for many Fortune 50 companies Michael’s last project of 5 years entailed analysis, architecture, design and development of many trading floor systems on the New York Stock Exchange.

TopButton,com - Online Media / Marketing / Advertising / Fashion Community - 1 million + subscribers

TheTopSecret.com - Online Sample Sale / Private Sale Business Model

Chameleon Platform Technology - White Label B to B and B to C platform solution specializing in Private Sale model (ie Gilt.com & RueLaLa.com) as well as a wholesale model.
