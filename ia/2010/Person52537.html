Dawn Del Russo’s Summary

I ambitiously turned my childhood dream into a reality December of 2004. I always dreamed of opening the perfect boutique girly with a luxe flair. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Business Management, and after three years of preparation, I held my first private showing of a few up and coming designers. I opened BellaDawn.com and local shop, offered personal fashion expertise and a place to put my best, and impressively stylish, foot forward. The trendy upscale boutique caters to young women that have the desire to live a “glam” lifestyle and don't want to have to travel to NYC, L.A. or Europe to find the hottest styles.

I have been featured as a fashion expert in print such as InStyle, Teen, Real Simple and Elle Girl, as well as televised media outlets such as Fox 5 News. My knowledge and style advice has successfully lead me to create trendy, chic, and desirable fashions for every style. Bella Dawn up and coming designers are available to the world at www.belladawn.com
