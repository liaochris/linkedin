Amy Anderson’s Summary

Coaching is a safe, non-judgmental and courageous space within which to explore your dreams; discover what is holding you back; and to ( when you choose) take bold action to achieve what you want most in life.

Amy is your companion/guide who helps you keep what you want most front and center. Together you design how to make the best use of your coaching time so that it is supportive and always challenging in the best possible way.

We cannot teach people anything; we can only help them discover it within themselves. - Galileo Galilei
