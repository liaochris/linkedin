Davit Sahakyan’s Summary

I am PhD, Doctor of Mathematics, have graduated Yerevan State University department of applied mathematics in 1994.
I have experience of executive position at banking system (Armenian Savings Bank (currently VTB-Armenia), Inecobank and Haypost. I have been approximately 5 years in advertising busniess and another 5 years in consulting business (Smart Consulting, Antares Business Consulting) and currently Key Solutions.
From 1994 I am teaching at various institutes of higher education (Yerevan State University and Armenian State Pedagogical Institute) and, currently at Russian-Armenian (Slavonic) University.
I experienced on modern methods, such as business process modeling and reengineering, strategic management and balanced scorecard, 6 sigma and total quality management, lean production, rating systems, motivation systems, etc. I have rich experience of financial situations modeling and similar models solution, various publications concerning to mentioned field.
