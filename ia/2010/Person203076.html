Thomas Neergaard Hansen’s Summary

Born, raised & educated in Denmark, Thomas has since the early ‘90s been working & living in Africa, Asia, Europe and the Middle East, and is currently General Manager for Worldwide Distribution at Microsoft’s Corporate Headquarters’ in Redmond, Washington.
Thomas joined Microsoft in 2001 as the SMS&P Director in South Africa. In 04 he was promoted to Area Director for SMS&P for Middle East & Africa. For the period of 06-09 Thomas was the subsidiary General Manager for Microsoft in West, East, Central Africa and Indian Ocean Islands, a territory spanning 48 countries.
Prior to Microsoft, Thomas worked for amongst others Naspers, Dell and Olivetti in diverse leadership roles, e.g. leading Sales, Financial Services, M&A and new business incubation functions as well as Internet start-up’s. Thomas also served on the Board of Directors of Enablis for respectively Southern Africa and East Africa for the period of 2007-2009, and has since late 2009 served on the Board of Directors for Enablis globally.
Thomas holds a Masters and a Bachelors degree in Economics & Business Administration from Copenhagen Business School, Denmark.
