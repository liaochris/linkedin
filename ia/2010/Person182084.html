Sanjay Sathe’s Summary

Transition Concierge by RiseSmart offers something unique in a transition management solution: a win-win for the employer and the employee. Transitioning employees want a faster, easier path to their next opportunity. You want a smooth transition, reduced risk, brand protection, and assistance for former employees. RiseSmart is able to meet these goals while fostering the goodwill of your workforce at a price that makes sense to your bottom line.

Transition Concierge offers you what you need, when you need it, and at your convenience. We provide winning technology paired with a distinctive human touch to customize our services to your needs. With Transition Concierge you’ll benefit from a professional team dedicated to making you successful, taking your career to the next level, and presenting you with new opportunities. We are committed to offering you fast, supportive service and putting our technology to work for you. Our wide range of services include: Job Concierge, Resume Writing, Transition Specialists, Career & Interview Coaching, Corporate recruiting, social media guidance, Rich career content networks, advanced web applications.
