Nedra Fabito’s Summary

www.TrueAlcove.com

Nedra founded her Marketing Agency, True Alcove, in 2003 after more than a decade of working inside large and small multi-channel retail, manufacturing and fashion companies. A key player in major brand launches, Nedra thrives on creating and re-defining brands, and most importantly putting them in a position to make profit. She is an analytical, data-driven merchant and direct marketer who combines these rational skills with her intuitive ability to recognize consumer demands. She is an excellent creative director and is equally able to art direct a project as to develop a financial plan. She develops high-trust relationships with her coworkers, superiors, clients and all stakeholders including customers, vendors and strategic partners. Central to all of Nedra’s work is her belief that the brand is sacred and her understanding that she must develop it with care, creating a financially successful business.
