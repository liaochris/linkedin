Xiaofeng Wang’s Summary

Xiaofeng has almost 10 years experience in the utility industry specialized in transmission and distribution operation and control. Throughout his career, he has developed strong system thinking and methodology for engineering and managing architecture design for complicated real-time control systems. He offers the following capabilities to help transform business requirements and vision to high quality software system.

Strong solution strategy, development, and architectural design capabilities with extensive experience and technical leadership in Smart Grid real-time operation monitoring and control area.

Extensive experiences on strategic consulting and system integration services with worldwide utilities and solid background in power transmission and distribution engineering.

Expertise in enterprise information management, integration technologies (SOA, Web Services, XML, RDF, OWL, UML), and industry standards (IEC TC57 61968 and 61970)

Active participant and key contributor to several industry standard and user groups including IEC TC57 WG14/19, UtilityAMI, and CIGRE D2.24

Frequent speaker and publisher at utility industry leading conferences

Ability to effectively communicate both verbally and in writing in English and Mandarin
