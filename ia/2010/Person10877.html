Andrea Bales’s Summary

Over 15 years of experience in Early Childhood Education in both direct and indirect services, as well as over 10 years experience with supervision of teaching staff and program development. Very strong understanding of Child Development theories, both current and those that serve as a foundation for our profession. Additional knowledge of Community Care Licensing regulations, NAEYC Accreditation Standards and Head Start/Early Head Start Performance Standards. Experienced in program opening and development, faculty professional development, strategic development and facilitating dynamic small group and large group presentation. Ability to create strong partnerships with families, colleagues and outside agencies. Proven track record of successful achievement of goals and projects. Dedicated, creative and flexible.
