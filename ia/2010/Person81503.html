Ginny Hendricks’s Summary

Ardent markets the online scholarly resources from publishers that are looking to increase customer demand or (re)engage with their librarian and researcher customers. Major clients include Elsevier and Wiley-Blackwell, as well as society publishers such as SPIE. We are a full-service marketing consultancy offering strategic planning, public relations, brand communications, creative, digital marketing, and multichannel campaign management.

See www.ardent-marketing.com for details.
