Scott Miller Pharm.D.’s Summary

I received my Doctor of Pharmacy degree from Nova Southeastern University in 1999. I am an Innovative program design and implementation specialist with 12 years service at Walgreens and Walgreens Health Services/Initiatives. I have developed cutting edge pharmacist delivered healthcare programs in both a retail pharmacy and pharmacy benefit management environment, and have managed personal from store to corporate levels.
