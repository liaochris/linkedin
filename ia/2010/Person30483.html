B. Scott Cassell’s Summary

B. Scott Cassell is currently the VP of Operations with Cinedigm's (formally AccessIT) Digital Media Services Division. He is responsible for the administrative, operational, and engineering requirements of the division. B. Scott possesses significant experience in the satellite and terrestrial communications technologies used to transport file based video and data, broadcast live standard and high definition video, and digitally secure and encrypt those transmissions for use in television and cinema applications. For the past 10 years, B. Scott has been dedicated to designing, implementing, and improving the technologies, systems, and processes used to provide the solutions required to meet the company’s and it’s customer’s goals. B. Scott is highly respected and liked by his peers, colleagues, and customers.

B. Scott holds a Master of Communications Technology from NRE Texas; a Bachelor of Science in Business Management from UOP Arizona; a Certification in Accounting from LTU California; MCSE, MCDBA, MCNPS, MCPS, MCPSI, and MCT certifications from Microsoft; CCDA, CCNA, CIPCESS, CIPCES, CIPCCES, CIPTDS, CUDN, CUDS, and CSEv2 certifications from Cisco; a GRO License from the FCC; and an A+ certification from CompTIA. He currently serves as a member of the Department of Home Land Security, an Assistant District Staff Officer of Computer Services and Certified Instructor in the United States Coast Guard Auxiliary, and is an FCC Licensed Satellite Teleport Engineer. He is a member of Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE) and the Society of Satellite Professionals International (SSPI).
