David Jacobs’s Summary

Over 12 years experience in web development, with 7+ years focused on the JVM. I’m proficient in architecture, object-oriented analysis and design, systems integration, requirements analysis, business process improvement, database design, rich web user interfaces, leadership roles, and mentoring. I have broad exposure to technologies, languages, frameworks, methodologies, architectural patterns and business cases.

I will do for you what I've done for previous clients and employers:

* I will quickly get up to speed on all of your current projects, technologies and architectures, along with their political and strategic history, and give a large amount of my off-hours study time to deeply understand them within the context of industry evolution, contributing intense expertise and ongoing analysis of pathways for incremental improvement.
* I will develop relationships with management, specialists and clients to gain insight into the big picture of the business, strategic goals, and departmental relationships, to craft appropriate tactical solutions, to identify and alleviate pain-points for key stakeholders, and to build trust, team work, and common goals.
* I will get to know developers and infrastructure folks, discuss their activities, learn their expertise and barriers to efficiency, to identify opportunities for high-yield improvements, provide mentoring or connections to mentors, facilitate cross-team communication and help bridge communications throughout the organization.

You will quickly appreciate bringing me on-board because I will deliver first-rate visible results; you will gradually learn of my deeper but less visible impact, as I'm relentlessly oriented around broad optimization of business value, fostering increased collaboration, and widely inspiring others with increased enthusiasm for skill development and deeper engagement.

I do all this because I enjoy it, and I judge my success by the success I bring to those around me.
