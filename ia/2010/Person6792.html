Alexander Mengel’s Summary

I am a freelance video producer, director, videographer, photographer, editor, audio engineer, and motion effect artist. I also provide project management for web design and interactive media applications.

I provide business consulting and viral media marketing services to start ups, media companies and recording artists.

I started a production company in 2003 called Alternate Innertainment, LLC we provide the aforementioned services for hire through several strategic business units. I also enjoy working on collaborate creative projects. We are also developing new business models for independent media makers.

My goal is to be able to support my lifestyle and a family by doing what I love which is working on creative projects and new business ventures.
