Tim McAlpine’s Summary

Tim McAlpine is a passionate credit union advocate who believes that credit unions and the credit union movement need to work harder to prove that there is a difference and that it matters.

He has extensive experience in the credit union marketing and communications business. As Creative Director, visionary and team leader at Currency Marketing, Tim believes strongly in the power of social media. In fact, Currency is a pioneer in social media challenge marketing—a unique new form of integrated marketing that combines strong product branding with the power of social media to drive product sales and member growth.

Tim is a popular speaker on the credit union conference circuit. In his dynamic presentations, Tim tackles relevant marketing issues in his trademark no-nonsense yet humorous way. He has presented at numerous credit union and finance conferences throughout North America for many top organizations, including Forrester Research, the Credit Union National Association and the Credit Union Executives Society. His writing has been published in numerous credit union publications, such as the Credit Union Management Magazine.

Currency Marketing is located in Chilliwack, British Columbia, Canada and can be found on the Internet at www.currencymarketing.ca.

Tim McAlpine’s Specialties:

Marketing, advertising, graphic design, web strategy, social media exclusively for the credit union industry.
