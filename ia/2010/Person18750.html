Antony van Zyl’s Summary

New technologies have always fascinated me. The computer and Internet era has been my bailiwick for many years now and I live the constant challenge, vibrancy and ever changing environment that it provides.

The latest marriage and integration of entertainment, marketing, advertising, social networking and community has enraptured me.

The future is bright with the promise of non invasive advertising, user selected engagement, on demand entertainment and a whole new understanding of advertising and marketing.

For the past few years I have been engaged to speak to and/or consult with advertising agencies, brand managers and marketing teams on how to adapt their messaging to this new media - beyond web sites, beyond social networking, beyond webisodes - and still create far more effective measurable ROI.

If you want to see how your company or brand or message can break out - contact me. If you want to participate in a grand project that ties all of this together out of the magical State of Montana - contact me.
