Syreeta Gates’s Summary

Syreeta Gates born in Jamaica, Queens is 21 years old, a social innovator and creator of SWT. SWT (Service We Trust) was created as a response to wanting to create “cool” community service projects that young people would be excited and passionate to participate in. After doing many community service projects SWT is creating a new culture of young people that “GIVE MORE” to the world. (www.theswtlife.com)
She is involved in a wide array of community initiatives. She’s currently completed Team Revolution’s Fellowship Academy, Public Allies N.Y, StartingBloc and is one of the leaders of the “I Love My LIFE” Campaign with LIFE Camp Inc. in Queens, which is geared towards stopping gang violence, and developing leaders/young entrepreneurs. In addition, which Syreeta has created a Hip-Hop and I Love My LIFE Conferences, HIV/AIDS testing events, and painted murals in the community for beautification.
