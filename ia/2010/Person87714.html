James A. Bates, CPESC, CPSWQ’s Summary

I have been working in both the Environmental and Aquaculture & Fisheries fields since graduating from Mansfield University in 1986.

Working in both the Environmental & Fisheries fields for over 20 years has provided me with a vast exposure to and experience with many aspects of the environmental arena. Projects I have been associated with have ranged from the designing and building of fish farms as well as operations therein, as well as working with large utility companies and their unique environmental concerns to SEQRA and site plan work and approvals for large and small residential developments.

As President of Ecological Analysis LLC, I oversee an environmental planning and natural resources consulting firm that is located in Middletown, New York. We are committed to sustainable development and the preservation, restoration, and protection of the natural environment. We posses both the engineering background and scientific expertise needed to help our clients minimize potential impacts on the environment and human health from proposed projects, while realizing our client's goals and while complying with the laws and regulations that govern this work.
