Steven Paul’s Summary

With over 10 years in the automotive and trucking industry from maintenance, repair, sales, autobody, inspections and marketing. I have gained deep knowledge of what buyers in the automotive industry are looking for when purchasing a pre-owned vehicle. I have recently opened a new inspection business that serves the St Louis, Southern Illinois, Springfield, Peoria and Chicago Greater Metro Areas that serves as a full set of eyes, ears and hands for all types of used car buyers. Test Drive Technologies strives to help all types of clients that have to deal with the confusing mess of the current automotive market by performing lease turn-in inspections, pre-purchase inspections, litigation inspections for accidents, insurance fraud prevention inspections and even dealership pre-owned certifications. Just in the short 6 months that TDT has been open we have helped pre-owned auto buyers save $10,000 by pointing out repairs that are needed before the purchase is finalized.
