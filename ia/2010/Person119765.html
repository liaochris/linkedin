K.Sue Duncan’s Summary

A master psychotherapist committed to providing a supportive environment for individuals, couples and groups to achieve personal growth. I welcome opportunities to speak and teach in businesses, schools, churches, clubs, or other settings on topics such as conscious communication, getting healthier as we age, grief and loss, and homophobia to name a few. I am available for brown bag lectures, short talks or day-long trainings.

K.Sue Duncan’s Specialties:

My specialties include: life transitions, conscious communication, couples work, grief therapy, pre-commitment counseling, lgbt issues and supervision of Marriage & Family Therapy interns and trainees.
