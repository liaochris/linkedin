Joanna Butler’s Summary

I’m an SEO and online marketing consultant currently based in London, UK.

I’m a member of the Chartered Institute of Marketing and both a Google Analytics and a Google AdWords Qualified Individual. I enjoy both working on and speaking at events on all online marketing topics and dealing with the whole online marketing mix.

My main specialties include: search engine optimisation (SEO), website analytics (e.g. Google Analytics), social media and online reputation management. I also enjoy related topics such as pay-per-click advertising (e.g. Google AdWords), accessibility, usability and online marketing-related technology.

With my strong technical background that includes being a web developer (XHTML, CSS, XSLT, XML, PHP, ActionScript, some JS, .NET and MySQL) and designer, I have a thorough understanding of all areas of digital marketing from technical and creative to conversion optimisation and overall brand and campaign strategy. Having gained invaluable insight through working both in-house and agency side, I offer a broad and high level of support to Latitude's SEO clients.
