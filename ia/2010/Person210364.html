Vamsi Vanka’s Summary

I have been in the new media space for over a decade now... been with four startups... one went so bust that there's no sign of it today, the others have been very successful though - thankfully. :)

I have seen the Internet in India grow from a marketer's fad to an important part of some brands' marketing mix... and been lucky enough to have had a part to play in this transition... and of course we're seeing mobile coming out of nowhere to being the medium with the largest reach. Where will digital media go from here?

I've had some very exciting / insightful conversations with people on Linkedin - a truly professional network. I look forward to making some more interesting connections out here.

I've just moved (Nov '08) to Mediaedge:cia as head of the Interactions business. I'll be able to share more on my experiences here as I go along out here.

After spending years in underdog startups, here I am among the Big Bad Boys of the media business. Looking forward to performing at a larger platform.

cheers to all! And wish me luck!
