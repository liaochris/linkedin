Gary Gonzalez’s Summary

Accounting, Business Development and Mortgage Finance has been my career path since 1980.

Even though the mortgage business has been my longest career, accounting for the offices my wife and I owned seemed to evolve naturally. Along the way, business development had to be a part, satisfying the customers, retaining the clients and receiving referrals became a way of life.

Our business models were passion, personal, productive and profitable. Passion drove me everyday to work with clients and allow them to grab ahold of the "American Dream;: Personal allowed me to meet some great people who worked hard as they fought the daily challenges of raising their families and getting on my knees to a small son or daughter and say "Have you seen your room in your new home." Production flowed into our office and it was a blessing from God to meeting deadlines, to get the numbers accurate and hear that voice of joy over the phone when they received the keys to their home. Profitable was the result of Passion, Personal and Productive.

My Goals in life has been to help others receive what they wanted and to fill their dreams through processing mortgages that delivered a home to 1000's of families. Sales is selfishly helping others and the result is to become a better person for it. Cabeat Roberts, a motivational speaker in the 1950's, made this statement; "If a town wanted to tar and feather you and run you out of town, and if you were a SALESMEN, you had to act like you were leading a parade."

Those words are so true, because being positive is a must to plow through all of the steps in processing mortgages.

Gary Gonzalez’s Specialties:

The mortgage business has been my greatest highs and lows. It has been the most challenging job I have ever had, yet the most fulfilling. Guiding your clients into their first home or their dream home always gave me a personal respect for the mortgage business. From the Heart.

I will never forget the family I worked with for two and 1/2 years who had great income but had some credit challenges that stole the wife's dream. She got her dream home and there are many stories like her.
