Chip Wickenden’s Summary

Chip is a business development executive in payments and electronic commerce with a track record of building successful businesses from concept to profitability. Entrepreneurial approach to leadership in matrixed environments has created repeated positions of market leadership at IBM, NACHA and Barnett Bank. Recently he has been in global role payments developing business for IBM, primarily focused on alliance opportunities with IBM and ACI. Earlier at IBM he developed successful solution areas in Anti-Money Laundering and Electronic Bill Presentment and Payment.

At Barnett Bank he built lines of business around Electronic Commerce, Small Business Electronic Banking and Corporate Electronic Banking. Under his leadership the NACHA Council for Electronic Billing and Payments became a collaborative body that accelerated the growth of a new industry. He has a reputation for creative approaches to business issues in the payments, risk, product, marketing and management areas. Chip has a proven sales track record, is a strong presenter in large or small groups and is also effective in a consulting role. Recently, Chip has focused on strategic opportunities in Mobile Payments and Healthcare.
