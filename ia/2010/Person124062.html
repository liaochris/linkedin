Leon R. Williams’s Summary

A practitioner of change management. Well respected national and internationally for Leadership, vision, passion, ethical behavior and integrity over a thirteen year executive career.

Honorary Member of Caribbean Association of National Telecommunications Organizations (CANTO). Twice elected by acclamation as Chairman of CANTO. Recipient of a number of civic awards.

•Represented The Bahamas at International forums: World Trade Organization (WTO) on Telecommunications, International Telecommunications Union (ITU), Caribbean Association of National Telecommunications (CANTO), Caribbean Telecommunications Union (CTU), Commonwealth Telecommunications Organization (CTO), International Telecommunications Satellite Consortium (InTelSat), International Telecommunications Satellite Organization (ITSO). GSM Association (GSMA).

•Conference Chairman, Presenter, Speaker at IBC’s Annual CARICAMs, CTO’s Conferences, GSMA Latin America Conference, CANTO Annual Conferences and Trade Shows and CTU’s Conferences.

•Successfully negotiated with the Government of the Republic of Haiti the building of its first and only fiber optic submarine cable connection to the outside world.
