Joe Nahman’s Summary

Duties
Primary contact person with overall oversight of day-to-day operations, long-range planning, quality control and financial management.

Joe has a strong background in the hospitality industry and has been managing resort properties on Hilton Head Island for over 20 years. His leadership abilities have earned him the presidency of the Lowcountry Property Management Association, a place in the Leadership Hilton Head Program, the “Silver Award” from the American Resort Development Association (ARDA) for “National Timeshare General Manager of the Year” in 1992 and nominations for a number of other ARDA awards including “National Resort Executive of the Year”. Joe holds ARDA’s highest designation - Registered Resort Professional (RRP).

In 2000, he was recognized by the ARDA Educational Institute with the “Seal of Achievement” for adherence to an industry code of ethics and continuing education. In addition, Joe is the past president of the Preferred Business Association of Hilton Head Island and is a six-year board member of the South Carolina state chapter of the Community Association Institute (CAI). He is a licensed Property Manager-In-Charge through the South Carolina Real Estate Commission, an RCI Master Broker and holds the Certified Manager of Community Associations and Association Management Specialist designations with CAI.

Joe is active in the Chamber of Commerce, Rotary International, and has served on the boards of several charities including the American Heart Association and Hilton Head Deep Well Foundation. He graduated cum laude from the acclaimed University of Massachusetts Hotel Management School and completed the Hyatt Corporate Management Training Program.
