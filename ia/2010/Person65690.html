Eric Anderson’s Summary

Who We Are
Internap Network Services Corporation (NASDAQ: INAP) is a leading Internet solutions company that provides The Ultimate Online Experience™ by managing, delivering, distributing and monetizing content and applications with unsurpassed performance and reliability. With a global platform of data centers, managed Internet services, a content delivery network (CDN) and content monetization services, Internap frees its customers to innovate their business and create new revenue opportunities.

Our Customers
More than 3,700 customers across the globe trust Internap to help them achieve their Internet business goals. Our customers range from some of the most trusted names in business today, to cutting edge user-generated content and video blog applications, to relatively unknown start-ups.

The Internap® Advantage
Internap provides the tools you need to change the way you do business. And no single company can match the depth and breadth of the Internap technology portfolio and services delivery platform:

Single-source Internet solutions provider
Secure data / applications facilities
Proprietary intelligent route control technology
Reliable, resilient connection to customers
Rich media streaming and distribution
Maximize value of content/web presence
