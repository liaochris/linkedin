Natalie Farinacci’s Summary

I joined PR 20/20 as an associate consultant to support the team with campaigns and projects involving the evolving online industry of PR and marketing. I help achieve measurable results for PR 20/20's range of clients with work in the agency's specialty areas of Website Development, Search Marketing, Social Media, Content Marketing and PR.

Prior to moving to Cleveland and starting at PR 20/20, I interned and then worked as an assistant account executive at the Pittsburgh-based PR agency, WordWrite Communications. As a graduate from Ohio University E. W. Scripps School of Journalism, I earned a degree in public relations with a minor in Spanish and a specialization in organizational communication.

With my experience in agency and non-profit public relations, I developed various skills in many areas in the PR and marketing industry. I'm also able to reach various audiences with social media skills and proficiency in Spanish.
