Rosario Porpora’s Summary

Rosario Porpora is a globally experienced management consultant currently located in the greater Metro DC area. With nearly 15 years of experience, Rosario has been a trusted partner and advisor to numerous Fortune 500 companies worldwide in the pharmaceutical, sales, manufacturing, financial services and telcom industries to name a few. He has also worked with Local and Federal government entities in the US. His area of expertise is attitudinal survey research, comprising employee engagement, corporate & public image, market research, and customer satisfaction and loyalty studies.

Rosario has advised companies and worked with C-suite executives on such areas as devising best-practice change management practices during restructures, improving innovation at all levels, and understanding the impact of merger-related activities on employee and customer satisfaction. He has worked with colleagues who have developed successful interventions in companies around executive coaching, leadership and talent management, strategy development and implementation, and rewards (compensation and benefits).

Rosario has been directly involved in business development strategies, approaches and implementation activities. Proactive and creative, Rosario has been successful in writing proposals and delivering sales presentations that win new business.

Rosario is dedicated to his clients and always seeks to provide the highest level of quality and support. A true team player, Rosario leads project teams to effectively create an atmosphere of inclusion and openness, motivating and inspiring those he works with.

A New York University alumni, Rosario is a member of the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) and enjoys volunteering his time with NYCares.
