Debra Larsen’s Summary

Strategem, a business management consulting practice, helps businesses grow and cross through complex changes. We work with companies of all sizes in various industries including high tech, manufacturing, retail sales, professional services, internet marketing, and nonprofit organizations.

I have served as an interim executive with small companies during crucial times of start up, turning around crisis situations and entering new markets. My fundamental focus is always on achieving the highest levels of business performance possible.

Strategem Services:

Consulting for Business Growth - Assistance with launching new endeavors, growing revenues, containing costs, and surviving difficult economic times. Reality checking your vision and goals with experienced help to successfully find your way as fast and effectively as possible.

Facilitation, Mediation, Intervention - In-the-trenches support to progress where it may seem impossible: solve big problems, reconcile differences between people – get to the heart of the matter and walk out of difficult times and into opportunity and prosperity.

Interim Executive Management - Interim management for senior level executive positions – hands-on leadership now, when you need it.

Prior to beginning my consulting work, I was with Hewlett Packard Company in marketing and administrative management for twelve years. I consider those years at HP my boot camp and advanced training for leading in an environment of rapid business growth.

At Arrow Partnership, I secured engagements and provided consulting services to small and emerging businesses.
