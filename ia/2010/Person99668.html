Jodie Miners’s Summary

Microsoft Office SharePoint Server (MOSS 2007) consultant, specialising in delivering a SharePoint solution that meets the business and technology needs of the organisation.

I have excellent business analytical skills which allows me to engage all levels of the organisation, from the end user to senior executive teams, and then translate the business needs to the development team. I work with the staff to understand their requirements and then plan and implement the SharePoint customisations required, or work with the developers if custom development is required.

Technically, I have a very broad range of experience across much of the Microsoft product suite and can implement or design solutions involving integration with other line of business applications within the organisation, ranging from InfoPath forms, analysis in Excel, Reporting Services reports, PerformancePoint Services dashboards or even ERP suite integration.

My passion is keeping up to date with the latest technology and Internet trends. I also have a great interest in keeping up to date with the full range of Microsoft products and attend MS conferences and user groups regularly.
