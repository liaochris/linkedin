Tim Behany’s Summary

As a former Wall Street executive, I discovered the recruiting industry after a ten-year journey on the Street. As a former Vice President with Smith Barney and Wachovia, I gained in-depth understanding of the financial services industry and its talent requirements. My 10 years of financial services experience coupled with my sales, client management and business development success, make me uniquely qualified to assist my clients.

My experience and contacts help me identify talent for top-level positions in investment banking, capital markets, and asset management. My successful placements include Managing Directors, Vice Presidents and group heads in corporate finance, M&A, and equity and debt capital markets. My prestigious client base utilizes my services for permanent and contract employees on both retained and contingency basis across the financial services community.
