David Schmidt’s Summary

Experienced editor and writer with six-plus years of in-house editing experience at two award-winning magazines. I place a high priority on meeting deadlines in the often-stressful environment of a monthly magazine, and I have a commanding knowledge of how work flow directly impacts the publication’s final product. Creating superior editorial results is one of my passions, and I have found that there is simply no substitute for working hard and demanding the absolute best out of my writers, my co-workers, and myself.

I have also spent the past ten years working as a professional freelance writer, penning articles for many respected outdoor and travel publications. This career has taught me the importance of self-discipline and self-motivation; two attributes that continue to characterize my work. I am comfortable taking on challenging assignments with minimal oversight or management.

In addition to strong writing and communication skills, I have a deep outdoor resume that includes mountaineering expeditions to the Himalaya and the Andes, thousands of miles of offshore sailing, hundreds of days spent rock climbing and skiing, and countless weeks spent backpacking, camping, and hiking. I regularly drawn upon my outdoor experiences to fuel my writing.

While the majority of my professional work has been in the outdoor and marine trades, I have a degree in environmental studies, which is a field that I strive to stay current in through reading, travel, and personal interest. In future years I hope to tap into environmental writing as a viable career path.

David Schmidt’s Specialties:

I have traveled to more than thirty different countries on five continents. This has offered my a wide perspective on culture, and has given me a set of reading glasses that are not limited to traditional Western experience.

Experienced with the Mac OSX platform, Office 2008 suite, Abode Creative Suite 3, Quark, and other publishing-related software. Working knowledge of the Chicago- and New York Times manuals of style. Basic knowledge of HTML and web editing.
