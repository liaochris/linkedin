Kelly Rued’s Summary

I’m an entertainment designer, product manager, and interactive marketing consultant. I specialize in social apps, games, and virtual world locations for both niche adult and mainstream markets. My special areas of expertise include market analysis and market-focused interactive entertainment design, and adult (erotic, romantic, and humorous) casual, role-play, and virtual world experiences for niche markets.

Currently, I manage indie adult entertainment studio Black Love Interactive LLC. We create erotic fun and fantasy for adventurous adults. We have exciting new projects underway on the web and in Second Life (our Multiverse development is currently on hold awaiting the next platform release).

In the past, I participated as a panel speaker at FuturePlay 2005, spoke on two panels, and moderated a third panel at the first Sex in Video Games conference (2006). I also moderated a Sex in MMOGs roundtable at the first annual IMGDC 2007. I gave casual presentations about leveraging opensource CMS/Groupware for game dev projects at the April '06 Twin Cities IGDA chapter meeting, and about my work with ISER Games at the University of Minnesota's Games as Learning Tools course in July 2005 (and again in July 2006).

I enjoy designing and developing for Joomla!, Flash, Multiverse, Second Life, and Android. I also genuinely love the problem-solving, strategic, and marketing aspects of entrepreneurship. I returned to college in 2009 to study small business management/entrepreneurship.
