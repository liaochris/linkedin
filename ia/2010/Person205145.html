Jeff Lack’s Summary

To be well suited is a fashion consultancy specialising in Personal shopping, wardrobe appraisal and image consultancy.

Our stylist comes to you with 15 years fashion experience, and takes great pleasure looking after the needs of clients of all age groups, genders and various backgrounds.
