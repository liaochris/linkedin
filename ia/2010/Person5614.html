Al Drewke’s Summary

Experienced technical professional in the electronics and communications fields. Proven track record of working turn-around situations and handling technical management/guidance required to redevelop product lines to support "rebirth" of the business. Well versed in working both inside the firm and outside within the customer community to create win-win situations that allow company growth. Versatile and able to successfully adapt management skills and approaches to succeed in the defense, domestic commercial, and international commercial communities.

Now embarked on a second career within the wine industry as we face the challenge and work to turn around and regrow Roudon-Smith Winery!!

Al Drewke’s Specialties:

Wide band communications, satellite communications, embedded computer systems, defense communications and intelligence systems, winery management, program management, engineering management

    • Participated in international business development and relationship building, resulting in strong customer “buy-in” for our products. Recognized as the senior technology leader on the executive management team and represented the division at executive level meetings worldwide.
