Michael Reid’s Summary

I solve problems and make people happy. Helping small/med business owners alleviate headaches, leverage time, and strategically become more profitable. Been in the business since 1994 and understand it better than most. Expert in all aspects of digital commerce. Sell payroll & check solutions; credit cards processing, gift & loyalty card programs, remote check deposits (no more physically taking checks to the bank &/or dealing with bad checks or crazy returned item fees - and no, this ISN'T the same service that your bank may offer), and some other things. Have setup close to 4,000 merchant accounts and over (800) gift/loyalty card programs representing over $3 billion in volume.

If what you're using isn't working for you, we should talk. If you don't understand how this works with that, or not sure what you're paying for *exactly*, we should talk. We typically replace services which are less productive &/or overly costly.

Heartland is a DIRECT Visa/MasterCard processor and a DIRECT payroll company -- not a reseller, bank, or a broker. Knowing the differences is helpful whether you currently utilize any of these services and are looking to replace what you have, or in considering them for the first time. We compete most favorably against the likes of ADP, PAYCHEX, and others in the small business payroll processing arena. We are unique in our commitment to fight on behalf of small business as advocates to ensure fair and transparent dealings in using payment processing services industry wide. Our company is publicly traded, and endorsed by (50+) national associations, and countless banks as "best provider." There are reasons why. We're different than the norm.
