Karl Robe, APR’s Summary

Karl Robe, APR, possesses 15 years experience, during which he has coached and counseled c-level executives from multi-national companies to start-ups in business-to-business, public affairs and leadership communications. He has crafted campaigns to reach influential audiences of global, national, state and local clients. His client roster includes professional services, manufacturers, healthcare, food services, energy and technology companies, for which he has managed relationships with media, government, and community activists. As an author, presenter and coach on a multitude of communications topics, his insights have appeared in The Strategist, Crisis Manager, Wisconsin Law Journal and numerous other venues. Karl achieved an Accreditation in Public Relations (APR), certifying a candidate's knowledge of public relations practice, communications theory, management science and ethics.

Media relations and coaching, PR strategy, issues management, editorial writing, professional services, manufacturing, construction, energy and more.
