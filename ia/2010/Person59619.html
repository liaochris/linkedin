Kyle Dreier’s Summary

There's probably no better title for Kyle than "problem solver."

He loves being presented with a task which involves the need for a creative solution.

It's this mind-set that has directed numerous ventures throughout Kyle's creative career – wearing monikers such as art director, graphic designer, illustrator, internet business developer, brand consultant and commercial photographer – each with its own set of creative problems to solve.

Over the past two decades his work has served clients such as American Way magazine, United Airlines, IBM, Better Homes & Gardens and hundreds of other corporate and small business clientele.

Kyle is blessed (or cursed) with the unique ability to simultaneously work analytically and creatively. It's this left-brain / right-brain collaboration (and struggle) which energizes him and keeps his work fresh (and challenging) regardless of the assignment.

Others consider Kyle an out-of-the-box thinker, but he recognizes that not everyone's box is the same size. He continues to push the confines of his own box and when asked to "think outside the box" his first thought is "your box or mine?"
