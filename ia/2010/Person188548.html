Sheldon Johnson’s Summary

Multi-Media Publishing & Packaging replicates and duplicates CDs and DVDs as well as provides full-service printing, packaging, project management and assembly.

More than 20 years ago, Multi-Media got its start producing the audio, printed materials and packaging for the Hooked on Phonics reading program. Today, speakers and trainers rely on Multi-Media for production of the CDs, DVDs and printed materials they sell at seminars and on their Web sites. Human resources and communications departments at corporations use Multi-Media for producing benefits packages, new-hire kits and training materials. The company serves the music and film industries by providing custom CD and DVD media packaging in addition to “For Your Consideration” DVD packs for awards shows such as the Emmys. Multi-Media also produces specialty retail packaging as well as products for infomercials, QVC, HSN and large church organizations.

Speakers and trainers hosting multiple seminars or events can save time and money by using the company's seminar project management division, Keynote Media Solutions. Keynote has helped clients reduce the cost of hosting seminars by more than 20% through economies of scale in warehousing, human resources, logistics and purchasing. Keynote helps speakers and trainers stabilize their employee count across event dates and locations. Further, its programs help clients manage purchasing to avoid elevated fees from vendors for expediting orders placed just prior to events.
