Jim Sanders’s Summary

I am the Managing Partner of LevelC Search, the exclusive source for recruitment by many of the most successful Private Equity, Boutique Banks and Venture Capital firms in the nation. We have built our solid reputation by having access to the best positions and the most talented people in the business.

LevelC implements a customized process which allows us to better understand the specific needs and objectives of clients and candidates alike. We recognize the importance of working closely with companies and prospective candidates to ensure that we are creating the perfect match. It is our mission to find the people who possess the precise skill set and qualifications that companies are looking for. We bring industry insight and exclusive personal connections to all executive searches.

If your portfolio firms are looking to add at the C Level or you would like more information on our services please contact me.
