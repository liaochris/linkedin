Veronique Martin-Place’s Summary

I am a freelance writer, editor and translator. I can write all kind of projects in French and English. I edit and proofread in French only. I translate from English to French. I really enjoy to influence positively people through my writing. Moreover I have a strong experience in research and fact checking.
