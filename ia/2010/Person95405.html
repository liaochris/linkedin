Jennifer Borba von Stauffenberg’s Summary

Olive PR Solutions works with clients who are ready to make a major impact by generating positive news coverage and opportunities to guide them to branch out, think bigger and expand their reach.

I have over ten years of experience in marketing and public relations securing top tier news coverage and developing and executing creative promotions. I have worked in a wide array of industries and specialize in developing strategic full-scale marketing and public relations plans for clients regionally and nationally to align with overall goals of the business to positively impact their bottom line.

Komen for the Cure San Diego, and Truly Networks.
