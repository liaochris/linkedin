Gene Burd’s Summary

Gene is the Managing Director of the Marks Sokolov and Burd's Kyiv, Ukraine representative office. He is a native of Ukraine and is fluent in Russian and English.

For over 10 years, Gene advised international and domestic companies on various aspects of doing business in the countries of the former Soviet Union including: international corporate, business, and investment structuring, legal and corporate risk assessment, international distributorship and sale and purchase agreements, trade financing, letters of credit, and international real estate investments and management. Gene also represented client in major international commercial litigations and arbitrations involving multimillion dollar contractual and fraud claims. His arbitration experience includes representations in arbitrations under the rules of the American Arbitration Association, International Chamber of Commerce, London Court of International Arbitration, and London Maritime Arbitration Association.
