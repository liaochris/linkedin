James Fenno’s Summary

I have over thirty years of experience as a senior manager in early stage biotech clinical product development having gained experience in both large companies (SmithKline, Sterling-Winthrop, Wyeth) as well as small companies (early Genentech and Amgen, Ingene, Inhibitex et al). I have successfully managed or participated in over 25 IND filings and have participated as a central player in FDA successful meetings. I am an expert in guidance documents and regulations pertaining to biotech product development.

I have worked extensively with mammalian cell culture-derived (mostly monoclonal Abs) and bacterially derived products. I have built and managed functions including Regulatory Affairs, QC, QA, Process Development, and Project Management. I have qualified (from a capability and compliance viewpoint) and managed outside contractors including CMOs, CTOs, pharmacology and toxicology contractors. A favorite niche is the start-up environment where one must wear many hats to successfully get the investigational product into the clinic as rapidly as possible, safely and within budget.
