    My name is Craig J. Phillips MRC, BA. I am an educational consultant, an author and a motivational speaker with a message of encouragement, empowerment, inspiration and hope for anyone who has been impacted by a brain injury.

    On February 6, 2007 I created Second Chance to Live to share my experience, strength and hope through the principles and strategies that have enabled me to succeed far beyond all reasonable expectations during my life time.

    Per my experience, I sustained a severe brain injury with right frontal lobe damage, a severe brain contusion with brain stem involvement in 1967 when I was 10 years old. I remained in a coma for 3 weeks.

    Although I was not expected to live or succeed beyond high school academically, I went on to obtain both my bachelor of arts and my master's degree.

    On February 6, 2007 I created Second Chance to Live http://secondchancetolive.wordpress.com. To date I have written a total of 670 articles for Second Chance to Live. These articles are listed with in my Site Map http://wp.me/P3atD-1H

    To view how Second Chance to Live has positively impacted lives please visit my Testimonial / Endorsement Page at http://wp.me/P3atD-nD

    During the past 3 1/2 years articles from Second Chance to Live have been published through out the United States, in Canada, in the United Kingdom, in Europe and through out Australia. Please see my Publications Page http://wp.me/P3atD-oK
