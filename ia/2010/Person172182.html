Richard Heller’s Summary

Greener By Design renews customer value, creates spaces that revive the senses, and restore nature. We do this through designing and building gardens that are both environmentally appropriate and beautiful. As the CEO, my responsibility is to make sure that we hold to our strategic vision as individuals and as a company, and that the vision is in alignment with the values of our clients.

With twenty five years of experience and learning as a landscape professional and ten plus years in greenroof design installation and maintenance, the firm and Richard have substantial resources to offer in our field.

Our goals at GBD currently are to provide even greater ease of use and service to our clients and to remain a vehicle where our staff can develop and feel fulfilled in their work.

Richard Heller’s Specialties:

As a greenroof pioneer, and a leader in sustainable design, Richard has done presentations on greenroofs and sustainable practices at the NESEA conferenece, the battery park city authority, the NYSTA educational tracks, the AIA and numerous communty groups and architectural firms . He ahas also authored hundreds of articles on landscape practices and greenroofs for local newspapers, professional magazines, Real Estate Associaions, and PLANET
