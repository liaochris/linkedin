Leslie Mullarkey’s Summary

As a freelance copywriter, Leslie has spent the last 11 years building brands and growing business for agencies in Philadelphia, Wilmington, and Lancaster.

In that time she's written campaigns for Siemens Medical, Zurich Financial Services, PNC Bank, Comcast, and the Jefferson Health System.

Leslie began her career at Scali, McCabe, Sloves in New York. She then moved to Los Angeles, where she worked for Benton & Bowles and McCann-Erickson, writing national campaigns for Hilton Hotels, Coca-Cola and Lockheed. After moving back to the Philadelphia area, she worked for several agencies before beginning her freelance career.

She has been recognized by The New York Radio Festival, PRINT Magazine, Delaware ADDE, and numerous Philadelphia ADDYs. Recently, her work received two Gold Awards at the 25th Annual Healthcare Advertising Awards in New York, for product launches for Siemens Medical. She was also honored at the Medical Marketing & Media Awards with Gold Award for advertising she created for the audiology division of Siemens Healthcare, winning “Best Individual Product Advertisement in Professional Press”.

Leslie Mullarkey’s Specialties:

A specialist in making the complex simple, Leslie has deep expertise in healthcare, financial and technical copy.
