John Harris’s Summary

* John enjoys sharing ideas with a team of product managers and developers to define new product features. Solving complex problems and following through with testing and maintenance is important to help customers realize their goals.
*John is a senior software design engineer with a reputation for developing software that drives devices and application interfaces to perform quickly and reliably. Projects have included the full life cycle of software development, from object oriented analysis and design to installation and maintenance. He can analyze and solve very complex technical problems. He has developed products with many software languages and tools.
* Background includes embedded Windows CE applications, UNIX (AIX) applications, Windows desktop, chart components, and building controls. A strong background in telecommunications (cable & telephony, exposure to wireless). Wrote a state machine for testing Bluetooth connections. Recent work was developing applications for a handheld instrument C# and embedded C++ on a Windows CE platform.
* Enjoys increasing skill base and enhancing development practices in ways that produce great products. Earned a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics and a Master of Science in Computer Science.
* Object Oriented Developer with Design Pattern experience. Favorite patterns are adapter (for compatibility), singleton, and observer. Some experience in plotters and web support. Built live data chart controls for a released handheld product.
