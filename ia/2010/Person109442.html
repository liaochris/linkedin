Justyn Faber’s Summary

New Business Development Professional responsible for uncovering opportunities for SDL Language Technologies Division into Fortune 1000 organizations.



SDL Language Technologies

Providing technology and best practices to help simplify and optimize the translation and authoring processes for global organizations so they can:

-Reduce translation costs
-Improve global customer satisfaction
-Get their products and product information to global markets faster
-Eliminate delays between domestic and international product releases
-Improve global brand consistency


As companies expand globally, the way they deliver their multilingual content is crucial to their long-term globalization strategy and success. Naturally, their processes must be lean and efficient.

Large global organizations are challenged with finding new and innovative ways to drive down translation costs, improve consistency and quality of multilingual information, and deliver that content to market faster.

SDL Technologies assists companies solve issues related to their globalization strategies.

The organizations that adopt a strong global information management approach are seeing translation and localization costs savings of around 30-50% a year, delivering their multi-lingual content to market 70% faster, and significantly improving their global brand equity.

As part of the business development team, my responsibilities lie in introducing global companies to best practices for managing their global content.

SDL's technology solves critical business issues such as

-Inconsistent and poor global brand
-Long delays in time-to-market for new products resulting in lost -revenue and competitive disadvantage
-Poor customer experience
-Risk associated with conducting business in highly regulated industries and regions
