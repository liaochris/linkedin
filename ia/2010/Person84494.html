Ilise Benun’s Summary

I am the founder of Marketing Mentor, a business coaching program for creative business owners, solopreneurs and independent professionals.

We offer both one-on-one and group coaching, online and on the phone, to teach you how to integrate marketing into your day-to-day process so you can get the clients you want, instead of feeling pressured to take what comes along.

We’ll show you how to set realistic goals and how to reach them and we’ll keep you on track and accountable for it.

We'll teach you how to choose a target market and position yourself or your firm as the expert in that market. Then we'll teach you how to use choose from all the possible marketing tools you could use, which ones are the most effective (and least inexpensive) to reach your market.

With your Marketing Mentor by your side, you’ll get the work you want to grow your business.

Ilise Benun’s Specialties:

We specialize in working with creative professionals (designers, writers, video producers, photographers) as well as professional service providers (lawyers, consultants, speaking coaches, personal trainers, computer experts and even other marketing consultants) -- anyone offering a B2B service.
