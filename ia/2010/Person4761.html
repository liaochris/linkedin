Akhilesh Gupta’s Summary

Akhilesh has vast experience in handling Intellectual Property cases especially patents. He holds an engineering degree (B.Tech.) in Computer Science from UP Technical University, Lucknow and a Post Graduate Diploma in IPR Law from Indian Law Institute, New Delhi. He is also topper of the Patent Agent Examination (Kolkata zone) held in Nov 2007.

Akhilesh has assisted to independent inventor, technology companies, attorneys and law firms. He has successfully executed different projects including patentability assessment, validation assessment, infringement analysis, FTO/clearance search; technology landscape; patent application drafting, patent illustration/drawing, patent filing and prosecution before the Indian Patent Office, and Office action responses.

Akhilesh Gupta’s Specialties:

Prior art search, Patent drafting, Patent prosecution for aerospace, automotive, computer (hardware and software), human necessity article, green/clean/renewable/alternative energy, medical devices, and telecommunication sector invention.
