Steve Gershik’s Summary

With over 16 years of experience in web marketing and production, Steve believes that an organization's online presence must be consistent and highly functional, across all media.

Having worked in the capacities of interaction design, graphic design and project management, Steve brings extensive, broad-based leadership to projects on which he leads and participates.

During his career, he has consulted for medical device and IT companies, and taught business writing and online communication at Stanford University, Claris, Hewlett-Packard and AMD.

You might not know that Steve was the technical editor for several books from Que publishing, a division of MacMillan. Titles included:

• 10 Minute Guide to Netscape Communicator
• Special Edition Using CGI
• Special Edition Using JavaScript
• Easy Web Publishing with HTML 3.2
• Using Shockwave
• Using Netscape Composer

Steve's been a member of the Association for Computing Machinery, the Association of Internet Professionals (chairing the e-commerce special interest group), and the HTML Writer's Guild.

Steve stays active in the community, chairing the technology advisory committee of the local school district, serving as an advisor to the Webby award-winning Palo Alto High School Voice, and as board member of Camp Tawonga.
