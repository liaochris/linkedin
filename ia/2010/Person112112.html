Kathryn McLellan’s Summary

My primary interest is understanding how people create meaning and individuality through consumption of mass media, social interaction, and consumerism. I've done work on everything from kawaii culture in Japan to how individuals create meaning through blogging to habitual DVD shopping behaviors.

My experience includes public policy and market research along with a background in editing and information organization, including library work and research planning. I excel at interdisciplinary work, combining methodologies and theories from different fields into a coherent whole.

I am currently open to all research-based opportunities, either virtually or in the Chicago area.
