Sarah Phillips’s Summary

I am a freelance designer & animator, available for both large and small projects. I am currently based in Melbourne, Australia, but regularly work with clients both interstate and internationally. I am able to work with you remotely or in house - or a combination of the two!

My experience lies mainly in website design, HTML/CSS, Flash, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Final Cut Pro and DVD Studio Pro. In the past I have worked on websites, TV commercials, Flash animations, music videos, interactives, promotional displays, instructional videos, HTML email newsletters, and DVD production. I have worked for small not-for-profit organisations, right through to government departments and everything in between.

If you have a project you’d like to talk about, don’t hesitate to contact me. Together, we can work out the best options for you and your project.
