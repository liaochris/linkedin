Barbara Giamanco’s Summary

Barb is a well known sales professional, keynote speaker, author and accredited sales coach with over 25 years of business experience. She capped a corporate career at Microsoft, where she led sales teams and coached executives.She has received numerous leadership and sales awards.

Barb specializes in helping clients integrate the "effective" use of social media and social networking into their sales process in order to increase sales. Anyone can teach you to use the tool, but do they know how to help you strategically attract more buyers and close more sales?

We:

- Assess your current networking strategy to determine what's working and what's not.

-Teach you a Lead Generation Model that blends the use of social networking with effective face-to-face activities in order to increase sales.

- Show you a buyer assessment process that will help you increase your visibility with your target client.

- Assess current sales strengths and weaknesses.

- Teach you an online communication strategy that creates competitive advantage, demonstrates your expertise and capabilities, expands your pool of qualified sales opportunities.

- Teach you time mastery principles to achieve greater sales success.

-Educate you about the various social media tools - LinkedIn, Facebook, Blogs, Twitter, Social Bookmarking and more.

Barb signed a contract with Praeger Publishing in March 2009 to co-author a book titled The New Handshake™: Sales Meets Social Media. Expected Spring 2010.
