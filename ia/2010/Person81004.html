Heath Schadegg (LION)’s Summary

I started my sales career as "pitchman" selling household convenience items at shows, and exhibitions. It was the perfect setting to learn the fundamentals of the craft of sales and a great way to see the country. I say craft because an early mentor and veteran of countless fairs, swap meets, boardwalks and rodeos told me early on that what we do as sales people is neither solely art nor completely science, but it is a craft that when mastered will always earn a living for yourself and your family.

After a few years on the road, I settled back down in Sedona, AZ. It was in Sedona that I was introduced to the Vacation Ownership industry where I would spend the next decade of my career. I determined early on to learn every facet of the industry and held positions in sales, marketing, support and administrative roles. Much of my time was spent in leadership positions teaching the fundamentals of sales to new recruits and providing training within both sales and marketing. I was eventually asked to be part of the team building a program that would become one of the successful in the industry in the industry by volume and closing percentage. It was also at this time that I relocated to Las Vegas.

I was approached in the mid 2000s to join a small company that was beginning a new venture in the wireless industry and joined on to face new challenges in telecom. The limited resources the start-up had to offer meant that my training in technology was OTJ. I used my timeshare background to develop web based marketing as well as old fashioned cold calling while learning everything I could about wireless and related technologies. My traditional sales and relationship marketing strategies have served me well and I am proud to say that I have successfully increased penetration, loyalty and longevity in the accounts I have serviced.

I relocated to Austin in 2008 and joined the seasoned team at SourceInc last year.
