Scott Tucker’s Summary

When I was 17, and the Gulf War was just starting (1990), I was a senior in high school. I signed-up, on delayed entry, to become a Navy cryptologist (codes).

At age 23, I was selected for duty at the White House Communications Agency, but turned it down, to get out of the Navy, and finish my bachelors degree.

At age 25, I'd finished my bachelors degree, in business administration, and moved to Chicago to enter the ("forward") mortgage business.

In 2006 (age 33, if you're keeping track), I was named to Chicago Real Estate Magazine's Top 40 Lending Champions.

That same year I was also named Glazer-Kennedy Insiders' Circle Marketer of the Year.

In late 2008, I entered the reverse mortgage business as an originator.
