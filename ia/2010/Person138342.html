Maura Keller’s Summary

For more than 10 years, I have worked as an award-winning freelance writer and editor. I have written thousands of articles for more than 50 different regional and national publications as well as Fortune 50 employee communication materials. I also provide copywriting and marketing communications writing and editing and proofreading services to various clients across a multitude of industries.

In addition, I am writing the fourth book in the Design Matters series by Rockport Publishers. It is entitled Design Matters: Portfolios and will be published May 2010.

As the Keller Ink's tagline states, I am, "A writer so into her work, she dreams of creating another letter in the alphabet."
