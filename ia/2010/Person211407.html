Vic Nole’s Summary

Experienced business executive and advisor in the Life Sciences industry - have led the design and launch of new businesses, as well as the rebuild, transformation, strategic alignment, and growth of existing businesses to higher levels of profitability.

Have held senior leadership and P&L roles within Life Technologies and Invitrogen Corporation. Have led the operational turnaround, capital expansion, ISO certification, portfolio management, and implementation of LEAN/Six Sigma management techniques that expanded production output while significantly improving operating income.

While managing my own business development practice, I have applied entrepreneurial skills to assist researchers in commercializing their products and technologies. Have launched three start-up companies and participated in the launch of four others.

Building companies, operational infrastructure, and growing revenue, gross margins, and loyal, high performing teams in regulated environments are my strengths. I am interested in learning about any opportunities to participate in the launch and growth of new businesses, or improving the operating results of existing companies within the life science, biotechnology, medical device, and pharmaceutical industries.

Vic Nole’s Specialties:

Strategic planning and alignment, margin improvement, process analysis and productivity improvement using LEAN concepts, operations management, profit and loss management, new business creation and development, organization design, team building and leadership.
