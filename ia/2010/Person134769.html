Markus Lehner’s Summary

Right after fulfilling his military duties at the Intelligence Service in Austria, Markus Lehner started to make first Investments into special opportunities, always aiming for absolute returns. Starting soon to be successful with his private Investments, he travelled to the United States to discover and study the Investment world.

Back again to Vienna in 1990 he founded his first Investment company and in 1993 passed the state exam for “Investment Consulting and Asset Management” with distinction. In 1995 he became the first expert witness by law for “Investment Consulting” at the court of Vienna.

Since then he became a successful Investor and founder of different specialized Investment Companies in several countries under the brand name of LEHNER INVESTMENTS. Till today those companies have created Investment Funds, which reached more than 2 Billion Euros under management and attracted more than 30.000 Investors.

Based on his performance and know how there were various TV-interviews given and reports broadcasted, more than 100 press articles published and thousands of people have attended speeches and presentations of Markus Lehner.

Beside his success in the Investment Industry and among other achievements he became also a successful racing driver by winning the European Championship of the Ferrari Challenge in 2003 and is a passionate pilot who is rated with the highest possible degrees in Private Aviation.

Still today he is the controlling shareholder and ultimate decision maker of all companies carrying the name of LEHNER INVESTMENTS.
