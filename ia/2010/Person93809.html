Jeffrey Miller, CISSP CGEIT’s Summary

Highly effective team member with senior manager experience in international and US federal governance, risk and compliance (GRC). Trained and skilled in the identification and remediation of process deficiencies within complex and previously uncertified environments. Outstanding ability to quickly illustrate complex information structures and processes. Instant rapport with auditors of fortune 10 and international organizations.
