Mark Muskardin’s Summary

Direct path from thought to web. This is the core theme that drives Mark Muskardin’s everyday work in web development.

As a Front-End Engineer, Mark has perfected dynamic front-end interfaces for such high-profile clients as Yahoo!, Estee Lauder, UPS, The Home Depot, Pepsico, COX Communications, Humana, Emory University and more. Working in conjunction with an expert team of Rails Engineers, Designers, Information Architects, Analysts, and Software Engineers, Mark has a track record of delivering performance-optimized and compelling UX-driven web applications.

Mark earned his Masters of Science in Information Technology with a specialization in eBusiness Technology from the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. His practicum project on Mobile Content Rendering Engines, created and prototyped on behalf of American Express Interactive, won the 2007 Carnegie Mellon MSIT eBusiness practicum award.

He currently works for Primedia (NYSE: PRM), a leading provider of Internet, print and mobile solutions to help consumers find a place to live. Prior to Primedia, Mark managed a distributed pattern-recognition network for an online Silicon Alley competitive intelligence agency in New York City.

As an avid web technologist, Mark is an active participant in the cutting-edge national and Atlanta-based Ruby on Rails community.
