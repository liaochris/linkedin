Bill Winans’s Summary

Well versed in Business Intelligence concepts and reporting tools related to SAP including ABAP programming and BEx. Developed and maintained Process Chains as well as performed troubleshooting as requested by end users.

Fully designed and implemented databases in Access and MS SQL. Designed C# applications in Visual Studio. Technical drafting in AutoCAD and in print.

Received training from Wipro Technologies in SAP BI/BW and ABAP programming for SAP ECC 6.0, Adobe Interactive Forms using WebDynpro Java, and the SAP S&D module as well as exposure to the Oracle PTP module.

Graduated from Georgia State University in May, 2009 with a degree in Computer Information Systems from the Robinson College of Business. According to a nationwide business school exit survey that assessed retained knowledge, ranking was in the 95th percentile for all business students in the US.
