Laura Scott’s Summary

I am a designer and web developer working primarily in Drupal. I've been designing websites since 1995. I have also been involved in video production and post-production since 1990.

I have led an interactive media development company focused on development and implementation of Drupal-powered websites, contributing to the Drupal developer community and supporting the Open Source movement.

My goal is looking towards the convergence of media (Internet, television, handheld), the growing understanding and parsing of information that is increasingly available, and how these things affect how we relate to each other.

We are creating our own new world. The information technology sphere is just one part of the big picture. These are exciting times.
