Tamsin Fox-Davies’s Summary

I mentor businesses who do something a bit special, and want to promote themselves more effectively.

I provide Individual Dedicated Marketing Mentoring and run the Enthuse Marketing Mastermind Groups.

My clients get high quality advice and direction, without paying the kinds of fees that agencies charge.

I also run events and training workshops on specific elements of marketing, such as:

- Marketing strategies
- Online marketing
- Social media
- Video marketing
- Email marketing
- Wordpress blogsites
- Public speaking
- Newsletter design and creation
- Customer communications

To find out about my latest events please visit www.enthusemarketing.co.uk/training-events/, or to request me as a speaker for your forthcoming event, please email: info@enthusemarketing.com
