Michael Spano’s Summary

Dr. Michael R. Spano is an Associate Partner in IBM Global Technology Services and serves as a SME for IT Business Transformation, Strategy and Resource Optimization. Mike has been a Global IT Optimization and Strategy Executive, driving client engagements at the CxO level, for all sectors, with recent CTO/COO/CIO roles in private and public sector. Michael was previously the Global Solutions Executive for the IBM Automotive Common Environment (ACE) in Business Consulting Services (BCS).

Before rejoining IBM, Michael was the CTO of Indomitus Anatis Consulting Services, specializing in IT Services and organizational efficiency. Michael was also previously the CIO for Siemens Power Transmission and Distribution, where he developed an Shared IT Services strategy/organization for M&A growth. Before leaving IBM, Michael worked for IBM for over 18 years, in the areas of architecture, manufacturing, advanced manufacturing technology, software development, and establishment of World Class Re-engineering and Information Technology projects. His duties and responsibilities have ranged from basic research and development, to motivational leadership of high-risk competitive startup ventures (IBM Biomedical and PC Company) focused on sustained profitability through revenue growth and expense reduction.

Michael holds a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering, with a minor in Artificial Intelligence, and a MS in Integrated Manufacturing Systems from NC State University, and a BS in Engineering Technology from New Jersey Institute of Technology. Michael has been teaching Graduate classes at North Carolina State and Duke Universities for over 10 years. Michael has created and taught seminars in the areas of Robotics, Instinctual Intelligence, and electronics in interactive Art.
