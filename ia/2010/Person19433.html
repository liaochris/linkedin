Bob Newman’s Summary

Security Industry Professional since 1981. Started Security One Systems, largest privately owned electronic security integrator in the SE United States in 1990. With over $25m in annual revenue, sold to Ingersol-Rand in 2005.

Started Badge Express, Inc. to provide corporate clients with a high quality, low-cost alternative for Access Control ID Cards and Badge Holder Accessories.

Owner and Manager of the Nexus Business Center. Nexus is a 23,000 sq ft Office Building located in the Cypress Creek area of Fort Lauderdale, Fl.

Newport One, LLC is a specialty internet portal design and publishing company providing unique web applications to help people work towards living a more "balanced" life.
