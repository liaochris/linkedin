Marc van der Erve’s Summary

Specialized in organizational and behavioral emergence. Is regularly invited to address diverse audiences on organizational and leadership behavior.

Born and educated in the Netherlands. Holds a BSc in Applied Physics and a PhD in Sociology from Tilburg University. Currently resides both in Europe and in South Africa.

Authored eight books and recently completed the first two books of a trilogy on our inevitably changing perception of both society and leadership.

Identified a new phenomenon of emergence (The Emzine Phenomenon) and the resulting breakthrough in the way society will improve its functioning.

Designed the first iPhone App for leaders, Emzine Profiler (2009), which helps its users asses the state of their organization and find the most-fitting leader.
