Carol Linden’s Summary

Internal Organization Development Consultant with experience in leading cross-functional teams (10 years), in management (6 years), and, in my current career, developing leaders, managers, and teams. My expertise is in using coaching, teaching, and OD consulting with both management teams and work teams to help improve their effectiveness.

Carol Linden’s Specialties:

Certified in MBTI and the EQi, certified in Coaching, experienced in leadership development, using 360-degree feedback instruments, conducting OD interventions with teams, coaching managers, and in helping managers deal with the complexities of cross-functional teams and management-at-a-distance.
