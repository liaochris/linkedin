  • Senior Director, eCommerce

    Univision Online

    (Public Company; UVN; Online Media industry)

    November 2002 — November 2005 (3 years 1 month)

    Headed up all ecommerce and services for Univision.com, the online division of Univision Communications. Univision is the largest media company for the Spanish language consumer in the United States ,with the largest television network, largest cable network, largest radio network, largest Latin music label, and the largest online presence in the country.

    My responsibilities included overseeing the build and launch of the largest Spanish language internet superstore in the country; also managed Spanish language services, including a personals venture with Match.com, a travel site with Hotels.com, an online horoscopes service, an online games service, and a music downloads service. Headed up company strategy to develop and build an integrated consumer CRM datamart across all the online businesses.

  • Senior Director, eCommerce, CRM, and online marketing

    PlayStation.com

    (Computer Games industry)

    November 1996 — July 2002 (5 years 9 months)

    Started as manager of consumer loyalty marketing and CRM for Sony Computer Entertainment (the PlayStation division), launching and building a consumer loyalty club called PlayStation Underground. After three years club grew to over 3 million members, and during this time I headed up the development of a multivariate CRM data mart that included over 40 variables of consumer purchasing and game playing behavior on each and every member of the club.

    I was promoted in 1998 to handle all online, being responsible for launching and managing Playstation.com. We then spun off Playstation.com to become an ecommerce play, in time for the launch of PlayStation 2, and I directed all sales and marketing efforts for the division.
