Eric Gould Bear’s Summary

Communication Arts described Eric Gould Bear as "one of the most thoughtful and provocative interface and interaction designers working in the field.” He has been a leading force in the design of award winning human-computer interfaces since 1984, has published numerous articles on the subject, and is first-named inventor on over 100 software and hardware interaction patents and patent applications. Specializing in interactive telecommunications services and devices, Bear has established user experience strategies for many well-known corporations. An enthusiastic and inspirational leader, Bear is known for his collaborative approaches to creating easy-to-use technology and engaging digital media.
