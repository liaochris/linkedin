Chris Rogers’s Summary

Chris currently resides in Northern Virginia where she is working as a Graphic Designer at Level 5 Advertising. She recently completed her MFA in Graphic Design from the Savannah College of Art at Design. While at SCAD she refined her formal skills while expanding her theoretical knowledge of design practice. She received her BFA in Studio Art with a concentration in Graphic Design from James Madison University in Harrisonburg, Virginia.

Before enrolling in SCAD's graduate program, Chris worked in the D.C. metro area in advertising with an internship at R.O.I. Advertising, a studio position at Merrick-Towle Communications and freelancing through One Design LLC. This past summer she worked in a graphic design internship at Cartoon Network. She also recently completed a teaching internship at SCAD with Professor Mary Ann Casem and is a member of the AIGA, S{o}TA and Type Directors Club.

Working at ROI, Merrick-Towle and Cartoon Network has allowed Chris to experience different levels of graphic design structure, from studio, to firm to national broadcasting company. She has worked on projects ranging from small-scale advertising and collateral to large, outdoor displays. She is proficient in concept ideation through delivery while balancing multiple projects with a variety of Account Executives.

These opportunities have also given Chris a strong foundation of production knowledge as well as the ability to work in teams (large and small), manage and organize materials and time as well as communicate effectively about her work and the client's needs. Additionally, her time as a lab operations manager and research assistant has given Chris valuable experience in client service, office management, database retrieval and research methodology.
