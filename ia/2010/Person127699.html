Lori Philo-Cook, CFMP’s Summary

Senior marketing professional with 20+ years experience, primarily in bank/financial services marketing and PR.

Strengths include:
• Strategic planning and budgeting
• Creative strategies and branding
• Advertising and direct marketing
• Web site content
• Avid student of social media
• Writing and editing
• Product development/communications
• Marketing and customer satisfaction research
• Public relations management
• Team development & internal communications
• Financial marketing workshops/public speaking
• Bank acquisition communications and new start-ups

Develop fully-integrated, multi-media marketing campaigns, coupled with strong internal sales promotions for maximum impact and employee support. My forte is bringing all of the aspects of marketing together to meet campaign goals and build ongoing customer loyalty and employee satisfaction.

Customer satisfaction and loyalty are critical to ongoing financial success, and I can bring extensive experience in developing customer service programs to your business.
