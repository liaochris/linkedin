Matt Kennedy’s Summary

I am an experienced internet and software design professional with broad experience across all business lines. I have applied skills in mining and processing business rulesets and applying them to software strategy, product management, and user experience - all focused toward meeting critical path goals for the business.

My essential work is as a multidisciplinary "intepreter," who is capable of participating and driving discussions from a technical, business development, marketing, sales and UI design perspective in both leadership and contributor capacities.

Output includes business plans, development strategies, dataflow and process/workflows, software requirements, specifications and use-case analysis, storyboarding and full-case regression test documentation.

Development contributions include "front-end" markup (HTML/XSL/XML) creation, middleware development, graphic design and imaging.

I have worked across a full spectrum of industries, including: online/digital publishing, financial services, distance learning, online advertising, knowledge management, analytics, direct marketing and advertising, and high-volume e-commerce.
