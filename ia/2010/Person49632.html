Dave Kerpen’s Summary

Dave Kerpen's history of buzz and word of mouth marketing began even before he graduated from Boston University. As a stadium vendor in 1996, he raised sales of the stadium's lowest selling product Crunch n Munch by 400%. When he graduated, he was the top seller at the Boston Radio Disney station, before leaving to form his own company, Dave Kerpen Strategic Consulting, working with clients such as Diageo and Stop & Shop Supermarkets. Dave has always attracted massive buzz- whether it's training thematic vendors for Guinness, planning a multicultural celebration outside his office in Queens, NY or getting married on a baseball field in front of thousands.

That wedding, dubbed "Our Field of Dreams"-achieved Word of Mouth GOLD-landing him on ABC World News Tonight, CBS's The Early Show, CNBC's On The Money, and hundreds of blogs across the internet. Dave always understood the power of "buzz"-but it was his wedding concept that convinced him to start theKbuzz, a marketing firm dedicated entirely to generating Word of Mouth.

As social media emerged as a set of tools that could rapidly increase word of mouth, Dave had the foresight to form partnerships with Facebook, along with several agency
partners that had yet to fully understand the power of the medium. As Chief Buzz Officer at theKbuzz, Dave took a firm of two that was run out of his daughter's nursery, and grew it into a premiere Word of Mouth and Social Media Marketing Firm, with triple digit year to year growth for 3 years in a row.

theKbuzz has added offices in Boston and Chicago, and new clients, such as Heineken, IMAX, Cumberland Farms, Uno Chicago Bar & Grill ,Verizon,1800Flowers.com, & Stride Rite. Dave is proud that theKbuzz is the only agency to win WOMMIE Awards from the Word of Mouth Marketing Association for excellence in Word of Mouth Marketing in 2 years (2008 and 2009), but prouder of his two little girls at home-- Charlotte and Kate.
