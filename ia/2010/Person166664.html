Garner Wireman’s Summary

School Support Programs
-PB&J (Percussion, Behavior, & Jamming)
Encouraging positive behavior and expression through drumming. These
unique 30-minute sessions allow a child the opportunity for meaningful
non-verbal self-expression. Weekly behavior is discussed and progress
reports generated.
-The Three R’s (Reading, Rhythm & Relaxing)
Drum circles focusing on vocabulary words, sentences, and syllables as well
as creativity. The Three R’s program helps establish a connection between
language and rhythms while the student has fun. These sessions are
tailored to meet the reading needs of the student.

Corporate Solutions/Retreats
This drum circle encourages team support, expression, accountability, and
stress reduction for management. Garner assists businesses reach their
potential and find solutions through drumming.

Employee Wellness Program
Encouraging wellness within the workplace. Research suggests that drum
circles in the workplace have reduced stress and turnover while strengthening
the culture of the corporation.

Group Dynamics (available for churches, schools, organizations, businesses)
Offering stress relief, camaraderie, exercise, inspiration, and self-wellness.

Facilitator Training
Offering training to become a successful drum circle facilitator.

Community Drum Circle
Inviting experienced and non-experienced individuals to express and share
their life rhythms.

Pulse Beat Fitness®
Pulse Beat Fitness® combines live drumming and movement incorporating a
whole-person approach to fitness. Pulse Beat Fitness® is “Self-Paced” and
“User Friendly”, with no complex choreography or rhythms to execute making
the program perfect for all ages.
