Megan McWilliams’s Summary

As an author, multi-media content producer and public relations & marketing professional, I have successfully applied my experience and skills to extol the value of healthy, sustainable living.

The big-picture goal is help make ideas for simple, low-stress sustainable living accessible to a broad and diverse audience using humor, credible information and technology.

I'm a multi-media diva. Love to interact and I have experience with writing, producing and performing in nearly all media platforms (Print, Radio, TV, Web, Blog, Social Media).

Having emerged as a leading green product reviewer, I do experiential reviews that are read and heard by an interested audience of over 100,000/mo.
