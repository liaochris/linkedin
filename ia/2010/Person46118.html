Damani Short’s Summary

My career began at GE Healthcare on a 2-year leadership program. In addition to serving in a variety of global leadership roles, I drove process and technology improvements throughout IT as a Six Sigma Blackbelt and Master Blackbelt. In November, 2000, I left GE to gain more diverse business exposure by joining NewellRubbermaid as eBusiness manager. I served in a variety of leadership roles including IT, category management, product portfolio management, and data management. In January, 2006 I joined Banta Corporation to serve as VP & CIO where we focused on an IT, shared service transformation focused on delivering benefits internally and externally through the use of Lean techniques. After a 2-year stint in the sports business serving as CIO for the U.S. Olympic Committee, I've recently taken a position with SC Johnson, as Developing Markets Director. My business philosophy encompasses principles of an open & flexible leadership style, boundaryless teamwork, and a strong results orientation.
