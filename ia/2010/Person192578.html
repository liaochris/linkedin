Skip Reardon’s Summary

SUMMARY: Strategy execution fanatic, certified business coach, small business blogger, social media advocate, guitar enthusiast, competent cook, wine aficionado, Droid fan

Skip Reardon is a veteran internet marketing and communications executive. He's currently the Director of Digital Marketing and a Certified Business Coach for Six Disciplines, the leader in strategy execution coaching.

A former Manager of Industry Analyst Relations for Microsoft in the Business Solutions Division, and before that Solomon Software, Skip now regularly publishes a variety of blog postings about strategic planning, performance management, strategy execution, employee engagement, competitive intelligence, and leadership development.

He's also been an instructor in the Department of Business for Owens Community College, (www.owens.edu) where he taught internet marketing, web design, marketing management, and computer networking.

Skip received his Masters degree in Marketing and Personnel from Bowling Green State University. He is also a graduate of Allegheny College (Meadville, PA) with a bachelors degree in Clinical Psychology.

Skip enjoys spending time with family, reading everything, learning about new ideas (particularly computing), cooking, playing guitar, and enjoying sporting events.
