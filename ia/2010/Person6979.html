Alex Storch’s Summary

Though I have so many responsibilities, at the end of it all, I'm creating. Constantly creating.

As a journalist, I've written features on resorts and hotels, restaurants, music festivals and concerts, products and gadgets, art exhibits, clothing and accessories, and even some fairly risque material.

I've interviewed celebrities and artists, musicians and designers, but also have reached out to readers and clients, finding inspiration in all of their words and drawing them together to build the business.

I have at one time or another taken on every possible role of our staff, from photographer to videographer, edited both film and copy, managed business strategy and social networking, and have some pretty big dreams left to accomplish.
