Mayur Palta’s Summary

Mayur Palta has been an evangelist, technology consultant, and thought leader in Silicon Valley. His fields of experience span from hard core technology disciplines like technology consulting, enteprise architecture practice to sales consulting in a global business environment.

His current focus, however, is primarily in the realms of technology consulting on Oracle Corporation’s Database and Fusion Middleware platform. (High Availability & Performance, SOA, Middleware, Business Intelligence, Acquired technologies). He has a particular interest in Technology Adoption Trends, Marketing High Technology, Sales 2.0, Cloud Computing and Selling SaaS to the Enterprise

Mayur has a proven track-record in delivering business value through innovative technology solutions and has been a Top Performer by Revenue Impact, Leadership, Corporate Citizenship, and Strategic Influence on Customers at all levels (C-level, middle management, front liners)

As a technolony consultant, Mayur has consulted with over 100 of Oracle’s Fortune 500 customers, including public companies like GE, Verizon, Time Warner, Amazon, Yahoo; Technology partners like Intel, Sun, HP, Cisco, Apple, Dell, NetApps, EMC and consulting firms such as EDS, CSC, Deloitte, Accenture .
