Ruth Brajevich’s Summary

Ruth oversees strategic planning, marketing, communications and graphic design services for the firm. A member of the firm’s Executive Team, she is an exceptional communicator, innovative marketer and visionary leader. She has played an important role in the firm’s transformation from a regional to an international design firm. Ruth developed a company-wide leadership training program and an innovative strategic process for annual company planning. She also orchestrates and facilitates strategic planning offsite meetings for Ware Malcomb’s senior staff.

Ruth joined Ware Malcomb in 1998, and was challenged with developing and leading the firm’s marketing efforts. She implemented a new strategic approach to marketing and business development. In 2004, Ruth was promoted to Principal and has helped the firm strategically expand through geographic reach, product diversification and expansion of services. In 2007, Ruth was promoted to Chief Marketing Officer. Her strategic vision of redefining every aspect of Ware Malcomb’s marketing methods has been instrumental in increasing the firm’s brand recognition in all of its markets.

With the fusion of marketing and graphics in the firm’s branding strategy, she has redefined the way the company approaches marketing and has taken Ware Malcomb’s marketing to a new level of creativity, innovation and effectiveness.

In 2005, Ruth initiated and successfully launched “wm graphics,” the firm’s studio that offers innovative, integrated graphic design services to clients including branding, website design, digital marketing and environmental graphics, including signage design. The marketing and graphic studios have received three national marketing awards from SMPS.

Ruth works on marketing-oriented graphic projects for our wm | graphic clients. Her expertise includes branding, market research, marketing strategy, as well as writing and editing.
