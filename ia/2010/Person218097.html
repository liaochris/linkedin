Yvette Craddock’s Summary

As if running one company isn’t enough, I decided to run two. Both of them allow me the freedom to express myself creatively, solve problems and be of service to others.

In 2002, I established Click Marketing Solutions which received immediate industry recognition and has garnered over a dozen awards, including international recognition. I was the the VP of Marketing and Communications for the American Marketing Association Phoenix Chapter from 2001- 2004. I rebranded the organization, created the first comprehensive marketing plan and created the Spectrum Awards.

My background includes positions in Fortune 500 Corporate America and in marketing and sales roles for numerous companies. During my career, that has spanned over a decade, I have created and/or implemented marketing campaigns for and in conjunction with world-class brands such as Best Buy, Blockbuster, Disneyland, Wendy’s, Qwest, CIGNA, Subway, NMFN, Gold’s Gym, Chicken Soup for the African American Woman’s Soul, etc. Intel, Gateway and The Phoenix Suns are just a few campaign partners.

In 2007, I resurrected an idea that I had penned years before which led to the development www.ShopStyleFolio.com. It is devoted to bold, modern and luxurious women’s jewelry. Each piece is one-of-a-kind, wearable art.

My life and career has been a blast. My repertoire includes serving as a national television spokesperson, program guest and program host. As an award-winning creative writer, I have authored articles for local and national magazines, newspapers and web sites; some of which have featured internationally acclaimed business people and celebrities
My celebrity interviews include conversations with Billie Jean King, Tom Hopkins, Connie Hawkins, Oscar de la Renta, Diane Von Furstenberg, Jeremy Roenick and many more. I've served on many boards and currently serve on Collins College's Advisory Board.

Through my entrepreneurial outlets, I continue to design my life and inspire others.

Yvette Craddock’s Specialties:

Brand development, events and unconventional marketing campaigns.Fashion and personal style, with an emphasis on jewelry.
