Kathrin P. Ivanovic’s Summary

I am a writer, consultant, social media correspondent, speaker, connector and social justice advocate. I am bi-lingual, with real talent in bringing the imagination and creativity of others to life in both English and German.

I am a nonprofit resource development professional with nearly 10 years of experience working to effecetively communicate the value and impact of social change organizations. In addition, I have experience in developing and executing annual fundraising campaigns, organizational development, special events management, and marketing communications, with the utmost emphasis on integrity, attention to detail and results.

I work with nonprofit and non-governmental organizations, small businesses and individuals who are committed to diversity and inclusion and are seeking high quality, professional, results and mission driven, and culturally-competent strategic fundraising, communication and marketing services for their communities and organizations. It is an honor to play an integral part in providing them with the tools necessary to bring their ideas to life.
