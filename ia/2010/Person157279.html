Oliver Spiers’s Summary

Simply put...I enjoy what I do.

The analytics and quantitative sectors are easily the most dynamic and interesting parts of the financial world and contain some incredibly interesting people. I learn new concepts and ideas every day and this only adds to the enjoyment and passion I experience whilst doing my job.

I chose to establish BlackOcean Recruitment so I could break away from the typical recruitment stigma. I believe in a 'more substance, less sales' approach to recruiting and this is one of the driving reasons why I chose to specialise in the quantitative, risk and analytics disciplines. It's very hard, if not impossible, to fake knowledge or interest in these areas and I believe I can demonstrate both.

Having worked alongside quantitative analysts, risk managers, analysts and funds managers for many years, I understand the industry and take a great deal of pleasure in still being involved in the financial markets. As a recruiter I can now assist in adding to strategic direction, suggesting new business ideas and fostering networks of people that extends beyond recruitment.

I have strong morals and integrity and have always 'done the right thing', sometimes to the detriment of my business.
