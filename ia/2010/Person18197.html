Valerie Swanson Breslow’s Summary

While I was at Sales Technologies I was transferred to the office in the Philadelphia suburbs and of course brought my beloved dogs, Baxter and Trixie, with me. I still owned my house in Atlanta and had to fly back and forth every other weekend to take care of it. I tried boarding my dogs but that meant leaving work at noon on Friday to drop them off at a kennel near the airport and then either returning to Philadelphia on a 7 am flight out of Atlanta early Sunday morning to get my dogs out of the kennel before they closed at noon or taking a later flight and then coming home to my depressing empty rental house without my pets plus having to pay for another boarding day because I couldn't pick them up until Monday morning. One afternoon before I looked up pet sittting services in the local Yellow Pages phonebook and found a service in my Main Line neighborhood and called the gentleman who owned it, immediately signed up with him as a client and it made my life SO easy. I left town on whatever flight I wanted to, his company came in and looked after my dogs for as many days as I wanted and I always returned home at any time I wanted to find my very happy dogs at home awaiting my return.

After a new CEO came in at Sales Technologies and, to everyone's surprise laid off the entire marketing department (including me) en masse, I decided to move back to Atlanta and open my own petsitting business in order to provide clients there the same level of service that I had experienced in Philadelphia. I have grown the company to 30 sitters covering the 5-county metro area and our goal at AtlantaPetSitting.com is to continue to be the BEST pet care provider in the metro Atlanta area and provide each of our clients with an exceptional petsitting experience every time they use our services.
