David Landau’s Summary

An ideal career path is one that doubles as a way of life. My primary aim is to be a leader in evolving our society towards living sustainably.

Can we really do it any other way?

In other words, I'm an environmental zealot. By working in Cleantech, I hope to merge my entrepreneurial and artistic background with a passion for living in harmony with the earth. This isn't a hippie thing; it's simple practicality.

We have the means to live more cheaply, cleanly and sustainably. Our current energy infrastructure is based on 70-year-old technology and 100-year-old philosophies. We can solve most of our environmental problems with currently existing technology, *and* be better off economically. My goal is to help spread awareness and feasibility of this fact.

I am a long-term, big picture thinker. If you are too, lets work together. I look forward to talking with you soon.


-------
For me, entrepreneurship is about bringing ideas to life. It's about solving problems and bettering people's lives; it's about creating functional and sufficient systems so time can be invested in bigger, better, and higher-impact projects. Cleantech is - and will continue to be - grounded in entrepreneurship, both formally and as a mindset.

I'm fortunate to have trained in Zen philosophy, and attempt to see every side of a person and situation as both existing independently and as a whole.

I currently run a green renovation business (Elysium) and art studio (Tribbio) that doubles as a creative mentoring organization. Through workshops, lectures and an internship program, people at Tribbio learn how to determine their career vision, set goals, and manage their resources to become prosperous and self-reliant.

We can bring our ideas to life through entrepreneurship and creativity. Tribbio is a word I invented from the Latin roots "To give" and "Life," and literally embodies this purpose.

David Landau’s Specialties:

Marketing, mentoring, and mediation. Design and entrepreneurship. Helping people find their vision and intrinsic motivators.
