Steven Van Yoder’s Summary

Steven Van Yoder, author of Get Slightly Famous: Become a Celebrity in Your Field and Attract More Business with Less Effort, is a recognized expert on public relations, executive branding and thought leadership marketing.

Yoder has been featured in over 200 publications including Entrepreneur, Inc, American Executive, The Washington Post, PR Week, Financial Executive, Home Office Computing, Costco Connection, Industry Week, Brand Marketing and dozens of trade, business and consumer publications. Steven speaks regularly to business groups about marketing, branding, social entrepreneurship and thought leadership marketing.

His firm, Get The Word Out Communications, a San Francisco-based marketing, branding and public relations firm, helps companies become recognized authorities in their industries, reach their best customers, and develop a unique brand identity. Steven harnesses both traditional and online media to help companies become recognized leaders in their industries.

Steven is a graduate of Miami University. He is the former co-chair of the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce's Market Success Series and a graduate of the San Francisco Renaissance Entrepreneurship Program. Steven is a past member of the Institute of Management Consultants, The American Society of Journalists and Authors, and past president of Rotary Club of San Francisco Golden Gate.

Steven is a dedicated social entrepreneur and founder and Executive Director of Global Initiative to Advance Entrepreneurship (GIVE), www.givefoundationonline.org. Steven is an expert in cause-related marketing and is busy assembling an alliance of business and nonprofit partners around the goal of alleviating poverty in the U.S. and abroad. He plans to spend the coming years forging alliances within the social and business sectors for initiating and promoting global poverty projects and teaching global entrepreneurship.

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years 11 months)

    The Global Initiative to Advance Entrepreneurship (GIVE) is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) corporation founded by Steven Van Yoder of Get Slightly Famous™ that is dedicated to addressing the growing problem of global poverty in the developing world.

    GIVE works to reduce global poverty by partnering with established NGOs and microfinance institutions. We bring a unique, multi-faceted approach to reduce urban poverty through community economic development, social entrepreneurship, microcredit and small business skills training among individuals and nonprofits in struggling countries, with a special focus on South East Asia.

    GIVE's members and project partners are drawn from the small business, corporate and nonprofit sectors. We forge global alliances with individuals, businesses and organizations that have a stake in targeted communities.
