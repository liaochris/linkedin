Andres Garcia’s Summary

MyBusinessMarketing.org has devised an online marketing strategy that combines Search Engine Optimization and Social Media Marketing in a uniquely powerful fashion. Our intent is to provide corporate level online marketing services to small businesses throughout the world. Our cutting edge social media marketing strategy incorporates demographic intelligence with a psychological understanding of how consumers engage and establish relationships on social networks.

We offer much more than top of the line Search Engine Optimization services. We establish an engaging presence on Social Networks relevant to the clients customer base. We continually and aggressively expand these networks while implementing a strategy that will display a unique corporate personality. The end result is an army of repeat and word of mouth business from the Social Networks and a consistent stream of highly targeted traffic from Search Engine Results Positioning.

The cost of active management of Social profiles on networking site is currently at a level where it does not make sense for smaller businesses to outsource because they simply cannot afford to invest the amount of money required for this service to be performed a high level of competency.

In reality our commitment to the small business owner runs much deeper. We are a new age company built on the unwritten rules of the new economy. We should not count on anyone to help us, we must help each other. The backbone of the recovery will be the small business owner. By stimulating small business we are stimulating job creation at a time where our nation is hurting. By stimulating job creation we are stimulating the economy and directly helping thousands of people along the way. This gives us purpose, a purpose bigger and more important than our own bottom line. We believe in profit, we detest greed. In the old economy greed ran rampart, this greed has not destroyed our economy but it has changed it... forever.
