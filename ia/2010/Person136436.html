Massimo Martinotti’s Summary

Massimo Martinotti is the founder and president of the Mia Films, a group of production companies, with offices in Miami, Mexico, Buenos Aires, Costa Rica and associated production companies in Spain, Portugal and Italy.
Massimo has shot in more than 30 countries.
He shot commercials for clients such as, Toyota, VW, GM, Ford, Chrysler, Reanult, Peugueot, Citroen, Kia, Verizon, Allstate, BK, McDonald’s, P&G, Wal-Mart, and many others.

Massimo has recently created a video-portal with versions in Spanish (http://www.vesyves.com) and Portuguese (http://www.verever.com).

Massimo has also crated MWM, a company specialized in webmedia, communities and transmedia storytelling.

In 2000 Massimo Martinotti has been elected President of the Florida Chapter of AICP.
In 2002 he has been elected member of the Executive Committee of the AICP (Association of Independent Commercial Producers).
In 2006 he has been appointed Chairman of Aicp.next: the committee created by the Association to investigate the new business models.
