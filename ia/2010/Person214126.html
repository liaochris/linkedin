William D. McGrath’s Summary

A financially trained executive now acting more as a General Manager focused on delivering operational results. My formula is simple, surround yourself with a high quality team and lead by example.

I find myself uniquely qualified to lead large or small organizations. My experience is anchored in new age industries such advanced internet infrastructure and internet media companies. But I often find myself drawing from lessons learned during my tenure in fields such as aluminum, tobacco and petrochemicals. Many lessons are transferrable and I often find myself amused as people try to reinvent age old business practices.

I have worked and functioned in global organizations as well as focused in country environments. I am always interested in new challenges and believe firmly that good operational discipline coupled with intelligent strategic planning can solve any problem.
