Marcello Manso’s Summary

I’m an interaction and interface designer, sometimes working as a freelancer, and a front-end coder from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

I love to create professional looking websites, conforming to highest quality standards. My goal is to always provide an efficient and elegant design, a clean mark-up and search-engine friendly code to my projects.
