Patrick Widen’s Summary

For me, design is a passion. I've been a designer since the age of twelve and there is no doubt in my mind that I'll still be a designer at the age of ninety. What I'm designing, I'm sure, will change a bit between now and then. From backpacks and cellphones to skin care products, I jump into a bit of everything.

My current passion (I've named it Blend) integrates my love for design with my never ending curiosity of people. By mixing design thinking with neuroscience, psychology, sociology, and more, I've finally found a way to have more fun each day than I'd ever imagined.

Patrick Widen’s Specialties:

Design Thinking, Illustration, Human Centered Design, Brand Identity Development, Conceptual Design, Web Development, Marketing, Consumer Psychology, Experience Engineering, Social Experimentation, Social Media

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    For the Nashville Chamber of Commerce, I designed a website and national marketing campaign to bring new entrepreneurs to Nashville.

    Alongside over 100 hours of MBA coursework, I learned how to effectively leverage the strengths of a team to accomplish a goal within an intense timeframe. Vanderbilt's world-class faculty provided both hands-on and academic insight into the world of business that have given me an invaluable experience that has truly prepared me for life in the business world.
