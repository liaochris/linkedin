Ricky Potts’s Summary

As a content specialist and freelance writer, I stake my reputation on education and an obsession with keeping current in the content I push. I also take a huge interest in graphic design and how the end user views the content. This includes font and color theory, layout, and more. I am always looking for what's next, not what's now. I feel that keeping in touch and in some cases ahead of the curve when it comes to design standards that I will produce industry standard work with every project I complete.

Building relationships with clients is my number one priority. In the end, the only person that must be happy is the client. If I can find a way to make the client happy, and provide with the best product that I can in the time that was given, then I have succeeded on every possible level. My goal is to always be challenged and forced to think outside the box.
