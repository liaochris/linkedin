Melissa Harris’s Summary

As Advertising Director with eBaum’s World, an entertainment website, I secured sponsorships for new media items, managed outside and inside advertising sales teams, maintained all pricing strategies, and created strategic alliances with CPC and CPM advertising networks, traffic generation companies and direct advertisers.

With Clear Channel, I led the interactive department to a 400% increase in e-commerce revenue as well as streaming, banner and special interactive projects. I also created and implemented “Captured!” which was recognized by ClearChannel corporate as the “most creative idea, 2007”.

Xtreem Vibe is an e-commerce retailer with 11 brick and mortar stores in New York City. Its fashion publication, YRB Magazine, is a national magazine targeting the urban youth lifestyle. As Marketing and Promotions Manager, I supervised the promotions staff, organized all magazine launch parties, created effective press releases and e-newsletters, increased subscription renewals and developed media kits. I also formed strategic alliances and sponsored events with other urban lifestyle organizations. I created promo code programs and utilized Webtrends to analyze the effectiveness of each e-commerce campaign, and initiated all CRM database programs to increase catalog subscriptions.

My responsibilities as a Media Manager with Reprise Media included determining the client’s needs, creating advertising copy and implementing a sales plan, carefully adjusting search engine activity to suit the client’s goals and CPC budget. With Perfect Present Picker, my work involved analyzing client’s products and marketing them to a target audience. I began my marketing career as a Project Executive with Greater Niagara Newspapers, a position that required effective time management and the ability to work efficiently under deadline.
