Sylvester DiDiego’s Summary

Sylvester Di Diego is Managing Partner of Strategy Dynamix, LLC. He serves as a trusted, confidential business advisor, facilitator of business design and implementation, and as interim management. Sylvester facilitates, empowers, advises and leads entrepreneurs, executives, owners & investors to commercialize technologies & to conceive, design, start up, finance, scale, lead & transform businesses for growth and profitability.

His expertise is in business incubation, acceleration & launch; international trade, finance, supply chain, business development; global operations, investment management & management consulting. He has an extensive track record building value throughout the Americas & Asia for science, technology, manufacturing, distribution & service based businesses of various sizes.

Sylvester has a BA from Harvard College & was awarded a Harvard University Michael Clarke Rockefeller Graduate Fellowship to Japan. He is an alumnus of Leadership New York III & of the Mitsubishi Bank Forum of Emerging Global Leaders. Previously, Sylvester held Board/CEO Advisory, global operations, investment management and management consulting roles with Scient Corporation and with Mitsubishi Corporation covering the Americas and Asia.

INDUSTRIES: Biotechnology, medical devices, semiconductors, IT, eBusiness, eCommerce, web services, electronics, semiconductors, automotive parts, machinery, steel, pulp/paper, food, energy, materials, mining, transportation, procurement, supply chain, logistics, fulfillment, market entry, HR services, legal services, project finance & private equity.
