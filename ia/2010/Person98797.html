Jim Shelton’s Summary

Headhunter | Executive Recruiter | Corporate Recruiter

For more than ten years now I have specialized in IT recruiting. One thing that separates me from other IT recruiters is that I am a former programmer that has decided to leverage his computer industry knowledge and experience as a recruiting specialist instead of as an internal IT resource. As a recruiting professional, I consider my role to be that of a consultant. I review the entire staffing scenario and advise appropriately. My approach is to build long-term relationships rather than an opportunistic one that is short term.

I primarily work with clients in Northeast, Central, and Southwest Ohio as well as Western PA and the states surrounding OH. Position titles I have filled recently include - Director IT, Project Managers, Sr. Programmers, Application Architects, Business Analysts, Unix Admin's, DBA's, SAN storage specialists, etc...

Being that I am a Bible believing follower of the teachings of Jesus Christ, I have been able to build a partnership with Campus Crusade for Christ (CCCI) in Orlando, FL. CCCI is a key client of mine and has opened my eyes to a passion I have for being a resource for other Para-church ministries. I'm looking forward to seeing how God is going to use me to provide resources that truly desire to use their vocational skills in helping to serve the para-church community.

By the way, if you are a small business owner looking to outsource your IT function or bring in a consultant or IT services company for some project work. There are so many of these type of vendors out there that it may seem overwhelming to evaluate them properly. Contact me and I will provide you with referrals to the firms that we know are well established and have strong references. After all we are in the IT talent evaluation business. The same process applies to both individuals as well as IT service provider companies.
