Nicholas Montgomery’s Summary

Nicholas Montgomery is a 15 year old technology blogger, podcaster and entrepreneur living in Toronto.

Nicholas Montgomery began off hosted a live podcast, about web 2.0 and the latest startups. This gave Nicholas the ability to learn more about what was happening on the web, interview startups, share the technology weekly news and explore his passion.

In 2008, Nicholas worked with Leo Mancini (New York based graphic designer) and launched "Goodie of the Day", where designers could submit their own design related work (screensavers, icons, wallpaper) and users could vote on the one featured daily item. Goodie of the Day exploded to over 35 000 unique visitors in the second month.

Nicholas has written for numerous technology blogs including, MacApper, Listified, Appletell and iAppBlog.

Nicholas Montgomery has also produced, hosted and edited Blastr.tv, a weekly video podcast featuring the best of the web. This podcast had a focus on the teenager demographic, and highlighted most interesting and bizarre web stories of the week. With over 60 completed episodes, this gave Nicholas experience with video editing, script writing and promotion.

Blastr.tv, leaded to Nicholas being featured on CityTV, CP24, Digital Journal and other blogs.

Nicholas has lately working as a social media research assistant for a professor at Ryerson University.

Outside of the realm of technology, Nicholas enjoys running, rowing and bonsai trees.
