Katherine (Ferrara) Johnson’s Summary

Journalist. Editor. Fact checker. TV producer. I'm a versatile career journalist with over 16 years of experience in magazines, newspapers, broadcast and cable television, and book publishing.

My life centers around telling a great story. I meet people every day with amazing stories and gifts and I enjoy talking with them about their lives.

I've written everything (and I mean everything in between) from the REO auction boom to Orlando's famous theme parks for local and national magazines. I also occasionally produce local TV specials and other content for broadcast and cable stations. In March 2009, I wrote a half-hour behind-the-scenes special on Wheel of Fortune's visit to Orlando.

When I'm not storytelling on deadline, I also provide clients with a full-range of editorial services from copy editing, research, fact checking, writing press releases and showing the benefits of embracing social media.

I am innovative, creative, deadline-oriented and love a challenge! Drop me a line and let's talk about how we can collaborate on your next project!

Current writing samples available on request.
