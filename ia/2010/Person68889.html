Faith Pincus’s Summary

I own a company that provides two primary services. Pincus Communications, Inc. provides public speaking seminars and in-house training programs across the country. Products include a Public Speaking for Attorneys DVD.

Pincus Professional Education is a division of Pincus Communications, Inc. Pincus Professional Education provides unique, quality, practical Continuing Legal Education (CLE) seminars in California, Washington and Illinois. We average approximately 40-50 seminars per year and we offer audio recordings of our programs.

Our CLE seminars have a strong practical focus and we work direclty with our speakers and panels to ensure they are not only well qualified but they deliver essential information on their promised topics.

Most of our programs are comprised of a combination of judges or justices, their staff attorneys, and top legal practitioners with excellent reputations.

On the public speaking side, I have more than twenty years of experience training executives, political leaders, candidates, non-profit leaders and attorneys to be better public speakers. As a licensed attorney, I am the only public speaking trainer for attorneys that has both a communication and legal background.

I began my career as a political consultant for nearly ten years, earned a Masters in Communication, earned a law degree, clerked for a Federal District Court Judge and then litigated at a top Large law firm in Los Angeles. After that I created Pincus Communications, Inc and Pincus Professional Education.

I am a professional public speaker. My seminars are for large and small groups of attorneys, executives, business people, non profits and anyone else who needs to improve their public speaking skills and their presence. I also do one-on-one coaching or small group coaching. Frequently we combine a short seminar with one-on-one coaching in a small group atmosphere.
