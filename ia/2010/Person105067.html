Jose Angelo da Costa Pinto, MIS, PMP’s Summary

José Ângelo Pinto has over 20 years of experience in project, program and general management for many organizations.

He holds a degree in Management, a Masters in Information Systems and he is currently carrying out research on Economics for his PhD.

Mr. Pinto is a certified Project Management Professional, a certified accountant and a certified trainer.

He is involved in the development of small companies, investing on entrepreneurship for young people who have strong technical capabilities, but need advice on selecting projects, managing finance, in short, how to establish and run companies, as they typically lack this experience. Some examples are Bullet Solutions, Vivacidade and Josorac.

He is a Professor for part-time assignments at the High School “ISLA”, where he is in charge of the development of several courses offered by this institution. Mr. Pinto is the President of the Pedagogic Council of this school. He is also a professor on several master classes, essentially on Project Management and Finance. He has published several papers, on Project Management and on Balanced Scorecard amongst others.

As an example of his volunteering work, Mr. Pinto is involved in the parents’ association movement, has founded and sponsored a local newspaper and gives some time for a children’s protection organization.

He is a founder and finance director of the PMI Portugal Chapter and presently is the EMEA Regional Director for the IT&T SIG. He has produced several key note speeches for PMI related organizations, in places like Kazakhstan, São Paulo, Cairo, and Geneva.

Jose Angelo da Costa Pinto, MIS, PMP’s Specialties:

Project Management, Strategic Management, Business Process Development, Change Management, Balanced Scorecard, Project Cost & Finance Management
