Indira Bisht’s Summary

Passionate about ideas, people and committed to doing things differently and adding value.

I want to participate in the transformation which will result in HR being viewed as a business contributor not just as a business support function. I also look at creating a more awareness about career planning, employability and helping individuals find fulfilling jobs and career options through writing, communicating and liasing with relevant stakeholders in order to bring these ideas into focus.

I also leverage my business journalism experience and MBA in the area of corporate writing and communications for corporate projects.
