Hugh Baker’s Summary

Hugh Baker is an expert in energy and utility markets. In the fall of 2008, he launched HD Baker & Company to engage in renewable energy project development and to provide advisory, project management and investment services to utilities, developers, investors, energy technology companies, and large energy users. Current projects include transmission line development for renewable energy projects, evaluation of investment opportunities for a private equity firm, and business development for clients involved in electric vehicles, energy management, and microgrids.

Mr. Baker has 30 years of experience in the utility industry as a project developer, power marketer, consultant, entreprenuer and investor. His background spans the entire value chain of the electric utility industry including fuel supply, generation (coal, nuclear, hydroelectric, natural gas, wind, solar), transmission, distribution, metering and in-premises load control. During his career, Mr. Baker has been on the leading edge of utility industry transformation including non-utility generation, open access transmission, power marketing, advanced metering and competitive markets.

Mr. Baker has been an instructor on utility related topics, a conference speaker and has authored several articles. He has testified before several regulatory agencies including the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission. He was an original member of the NYMEX Electricity Futures Advisory Committee.

A complete biography for Mr. Baker can be found on the HDBaker & Company website at www.hdbaker.com.
