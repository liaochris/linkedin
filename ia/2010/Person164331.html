Eric Pierce’s Summary

Pierce Group is a full service fractional ownership consulting firm. We specialize in the design, sales and marketing of upscale Private Residence Clubs. Our clients are developers, land owners, senior lenders, and private equity firms involved in the development of fractional real estate projects. We have consulted and managed properties from Florida to Flagstaff, Chicago to Cabo and Idaho to Israel.

Our Mission is to bring the highest possible IRR to our clients while creating the best possible vacation experience for their buyers. Both are achieved through the elimination of costly mistakes in the project development stage, the design of more appropriate reservation systems, more efficient sales process through advanced marketing and messaging techniques and advanced owner communication programs.
