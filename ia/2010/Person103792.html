Jonathan Fine’s Summary

*** An accomplished senior financial analyst with eighteen years of well-rounded experience supporting high profile corporate programs and business initiatives with industry-leading companies. Managed a wide range of business and financial activities as a Senior Financial Analyst in the corporate offices of Toshiba (TAIS), Technicolor, ConAgra (Hunt Wesson), Citi Residential Lending (Ameriquest), James Hardie, and Fluor (Fluor Daniel).
