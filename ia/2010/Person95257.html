Jennifer (LION) Powers (jennifer@mrsacramento.com)’s Summary

I bring 13 years of construction experience to the Sacramento MRI office and I utilize my experience and industry connections to seek out and place talented construction professionals.

I began my career with Caltrans (California Department. of Transportation) at a soils and materials laboratory and worked my way up to become an integral part of the environmental cleanup during the reconstruction of the Cypress Freeway replacement project in Oakland, CA. I was later recruited by a general contractor and continued my career in the private industry, and advanced to become the Program Manager on a large State contract. My longevity in the construction industry and eagerness to master the recruiting business has made me an all around success as an executive search consultant for the construction and engineering field.
