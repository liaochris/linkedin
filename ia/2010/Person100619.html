Joe Hardy’s Summary

I am one of a handful of lawyers in Atlanta that specialize in aviation law- particularly transactions involving the purchase and sale of airplanes, financing airplanes, developing operating and ownership structures and other strategies for complying with Federal Aviation Regulations. I flew airplanes for a living before becoming a lawyer and enjoy bringing my experience from that industry to help clients in Atlanta, across the U.S. and the world.

Joe Hardy’s Specialties:

Aviation Law, Lending Law
