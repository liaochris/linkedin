MJ Leman’s Summary

Director of Interactive Marketing at Crown Partners

MJ currently leads the Interactive Marketing Center of Excellence at Crown Partners, an award-winning marketing technology consulting firm engaged with Global 2000 companies including a full one-third of the Fortune 100.

The Interactive Marketing group at Crown Partners works with CMOs & CIOs to develop enterprise-level digital strategy and manage the multichannel customer experience through technology-enabled marketing programs.

Crown's exceptional line of enterprise-level technologies includes solutions for:

Interactive Marketing
Social Media & Collaboration
Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
Digital Asset Management
Mobile Marketing
Multichannel Analytics Architecture
Revenue Cycle Analytics
Web User Experience | Usability
Enterprise Marketing Management
Web Content Management

Prior to joining Crown Partners, MJ was the Founder and President of an interactive marketing firm where he established his results-oriented reputation by growing client services 20-30% year over year and building a high-performance team of 40 marketers and technologists.
