Christopher Kwiatkowski’s Summary

I am an entrepreneurial web developer that specializes in open source web development with a concentration on cutting-edge web technologies.
I work at the University of Georgia as an IT Specialist for the Office of the Registrar. My primary role is to develop web applications that save money and more efficiently address business processes. In my first year with the Office of the Registrar I have developed four new web applications and released three of the four to production.
Prior to joining the Office of the Registrar I developed large scale financial applications for UGA's Finanace and Administration division. I was responsible for the development and maintenance of the core web-based financial applications that handled all Personnel and Budget Amendment documents. I also developed and maintained the Electronic Approval application which eliminated the need for paper documents for the purposes of Personnel and Budget Amendment processing.
Outside of my work at the University of Georgia I perform contract work for a number of small businesses in the greater Atlanta area. Currently I am developing a service application that will serve entrepreneurs across the United States.
I am always on the lookout for opportunities to make more money and put my expertise to good use. Please contact me with ideas and contract work if you are interested in putting together a web project.
