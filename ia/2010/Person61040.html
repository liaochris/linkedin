Dwight Sinclair’s Summary

Mr. Sinclair is a highly-successful entrepreneur having founded and built several top-tier brands including Oregon Chai and Sharkies Inc. He is one of the most passionate people in the natural foods industry showing an uncommon commitment to helping Americans lead healthier lives through healthier eating.

Mr. Sinclair is the President of Sharkies, Inc. makers of the category creator, all-organic energy "sports chews" for active kids and healthy-minded adults (www.sharkiesinc.com).

From 1995-1999, Mr. Sinclair was an investor and strategic partner at Oregon Chai. He helped build the company from $200,000 in sales to a $15MM brand in a span of four years.

Mr. Sinclair is the co-founder of The Sinclair Group, an independent marketing, branding and sales business that has supported many natural food mainstream brands: including Gardenburger, Clif Bar, Orgon Chai, Annie Chun's and Numi Tea.

Mr. Sinclair graduated from Ferris State, Big Rapids Michigan in 1982, with a B.S. in Business and a focus on Marketing & Advertising. He received a full scholarship and was a member of the swim team for four years having competed in the NCAA II national championships for three of four years. Mr. Sinclair has been an active U.S. Masters swimmer and triathlete for 25 years. He successfully completed the 1984 Ironman World Triathlon Championship in Kona, Hawaii -- generally regarded as the world's most grueling one-day sporting test.

Mr. Sinclair lives in Auburn, Calif., located in the foothills of the breathtaking Sierra Nevada mountain range, with his beautiful wife Kelly and three wonderful children: Taylor, Hunter and Dylan.
