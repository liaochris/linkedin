Jim Geiger’s Summary

Executive Recruiter with over 25+ years of Financial Markets experience. I specialize in the placement of people in the financial markets with strong analytical skills. My practice is nationwide and covers many financial sectors including: Market Risk, Credit Risk, Derivative Product Sales, High Frequency Trading Modelers, Product Control and Valuation and more. My expertise is in staffing positions that emphasize superior analytical talent and the use of advanced quantitative / modeling techniques and technology in business decision making.

I have worked for several bulge bracket investment firms as a Government Bond Trader, Interest Rate Swap Trader, Relative Value Prop Trader and Fixed Income Sales Person. I was also a key member of a start up Fixed Income Electronic trading platform that has become the industry standard for transacting US Treasuries, Money Markets, Mortgage TBA's, and Interest Rate Swaps.
