Donna Klein’s Summary

We take pride in providing high quality therapy to children throughout Westchester county for nearly 20 years. Our State of the Art therapy clinic is an ideal location for private therapy services. We also provide OT & PT in the convenience of a child's home, his / her preschool or daycare center. In addition, we provide therapy services to school districts throughout Westchester, NY.

My goal is for my company to always provide high quality therapy services and for us to continue to have the excellent reputation that we currently have.
