Paul Grulke’s Summary

Information Technology Executive with twenty years of experience in various IT roles and over ten years in senior/executive management from the Director to the CIO and as a strategic, third party consultant advising long-term direction and guiding change to the organization.

Experience in building world-class operations in various industries. A proven record of driving change through the organization with the ability to take instability and uncertainty and create a stable, well organized business unit capable of delivering service and value.

Possesses a substantial consulting background, and a strong business orientation with a broad experience in managing Information Technology, Telecommunications, Facilities, Call Centers and other related business units. Ability and vision to align the IT services to the business, as opposed to the other way around, to allow technology to deliver the additional energy and support to an organization to increase productivity--The best way to use technology to competitive advantage.

Paul Grulke’s Specialties:

Enterprise-wide leadership of Technology, Enterprise Training Initiatives, Sarbanes Oxley, HIPAA, Risk Management, Strategic and Tactical Outsourcing. Core responsibilities include business-technology alignment, IT and operations strategy, initiating, managing, and prioritizing mission-critical enterprise-wide projects, and the identification, prioritization, avoidance and mitigation of risks.
