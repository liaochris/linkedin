Melissa Gonzales’s Summary

I work within Communications, Event Coordination, Social Media and Promotions @ The Chemistry of Sound Productions in San Francisco, CA. My current and recent work includes corporate communications and social media for GenAudio, developer of AstoundSound a 4D audio technology that can place virtual sound sources directly above and behind the listener’s head in real-time and for various marketing agencies including ADD, Attack!, Muscle Marketing and Demo Deluxe. I'm especially interested in producing sound, music and art events while promoting artists and matching talent.

I have a combined 15 years work experience as a Manager of Corporate Communications, Photographer’s Assistant, Photography Merchandising Coordinator, Product/Fashion Stylist and Photography Manager for companies such as GenAudio, JCPenney, Pier 1 Imports and Sportmart Sporting Goods Company.

I have a Bachelor’s degree in Communication and Studio Arts with an emphasis in Photography from the University of Colorado at Boulder. I volunteer at the Women's Audio Mission and for Me'di.ate's Soundwave Festival and am a member of Artnauts an art group from the University of Colorado at Boulder that focuses on presenting exhibitions that address global and social issues.
