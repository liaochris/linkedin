Ryan E. Yip, Ph.D.’s Summary

Ryan is an executive coach focusing on strategy and personal branding. He recently appeared on Fox News Live giving advice on career transition in the current economy. Ryan is certified as a 360 Reach Personal Brand Analyst and StoryManager Corporate Brand Consultant.

"...good advice for everybody looking for a job..."--Julie Banderas, Fox News

As a marketing consultant with 10+ years of experience in the software and biotechnology industries, Ryan develops strategic and tactical plans for new products and is recognized for his creativity and communication skills. He has provided marketing, training, and documentation for companies such as Hewlett-Packard, Varian, Hitachi, Johnson & Johnson, Monsanto, and Bio-Rad. Ryan is currently focusing on sustainability projects, and is marketing The Best Bottle, a compact, reusable water bottle that will eliminate the two million plastic bottles that are thrown away each hour.
