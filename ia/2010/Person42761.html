Christopher Sewell’s Summary

Managing Director, directly responsible for negotiating the principal purchase of Multifamily and Self Storage Facilities for the firm.

Former real estate agent with Long & Foster with an extensive educational background in the acquisiton and management of commercial property.

Whether you are an investor, broker, or bank executive - when you have commercial real estate for sale - what's most important to you is dealing with a buyer who can close.

It's really that simple. At Commercial RE Holdings, we specialize in closing deals we are under contract to purchase. We have access to capital. We perform our due diligence quickly and we don't waste time.

We also offer equity positions to private investors who want to be part of our deals. A Private Investor can get 25% or 75% of the cash flow and equity when they partner with us. We do all the work of finding the profitable deals, the private money investor contributes money towards the deal, and they get an equity portion.

This is a great way to be a passive investor in commercial real estate and still earn the high returns. Get our private money investor package at:
