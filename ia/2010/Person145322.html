Mike Farley’s Summary

I help entrepreneurs gain faster and better sales results from their initial marketing budget. I have spent over 25 years aiding companies big and small by consistently improving their branding by helping them relate more clearly to their clientele.
Small business marketing, personal brand development and social media engineering are specialties for my business.

Mike Farley’s Specialties:

Brand and marketing expertise across all channels: advertising, design, media and web. Invited speaker to industry events. Award-winning creative: Paragon, Telly, Summit, AIGA, BMA Bell Ringer and Wow! Web... among others.
