Richard Murphy’s Summary

Richard Murphy is a GIS Developer/Analyst in Critigen's Atlanta, GA office. He has more than 6 years experience in the application and integration of GIS techniques in supporting environmental, water resource, and municipal decision making.

Mr. Murphy has provided support to various project types including:

» Environmental remediation
» Water and waste water master planning
» Floodplain delineation mapping
» Environmental impact reporting/Environmental assessment
» Environmental justice demography
» City and local government GIS
» FEMA hazards mitigation planning
» Aerial photo and satellite image interpretation
» Global Positioning Systems (GPS)

Additional areas of expertise include cognitive cartography, natural and environmental hazards, website development, and climatology.
