Tony Moreira’s Summary

My last 9-5 job was with the once very successful entertainment software publisher Hasbro Interactive as the company’s IT Manager from its conception through its demise. I was responsible for all things technical, and working for such a dynamic startup placed me in the position where I was directly assisting PR, Marketing, Sales, Quality Assurance, and Development – gaining valuable industry experience, and developing business insight outside the traditional scope of IT. My work earned me published technical credit for support and contribution on dozens of software entertainment titles such as NASCAR Heat, Rollercoaster Tycoon, Pac-Man, Mechwarrior, and Frogger. My expertise also afforded me the opportunity to act as the technical coordinator for major industry trade shows/media outlets such as E3, Toyfair, CES, Gencon, and the Today Show.

After the fall of Hasbro Interactive, I studied theatre and earned a Fine Arts degree in Acting. I've met some great people, had some fun, gained some experience, and earned a few small IMDb credits along the way. The Pheonix and The Boston Globe have given me personal positive review for some of my performances, and when time allows - I continue to act.

I also have the fantastic pleasure of partying and giving away cool prizes throughout New England as a regular personality/talent for Radio Disney's Providence and Boston stations: AM550 & AM1260.

I continue my professional interest in technology by consulting and teaching Microsoft, Adobe, entertainment, and interactive technologies as a private corporate instructor, and as faculty at Johnson & Wales University and New England Institute of Technology.
