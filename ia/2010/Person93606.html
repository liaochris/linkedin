Jeffrey Stewart’s Summary

As CTO and a founding partner of Trekk Cross-Media, Jeffrey Stewart designs system that help clients improve processes, reduce costs and optimize their communications, sales and distribution.

With a Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Iowa, and graduate work in Engineering and Computer Science from the University of Illinois, Chicago, Jeff uses his extensive technological knowledge to provide creative solutions for clients’ communications needs. Areas of expertise include multi-tier client/server database integration, web-based applications and content management systems, cross-media formatting technologies and variable data communications.

A frequent speaker at industry conferences, Jeff regularly shares his expertise on topics ranging from variable data marketing and web-to-print technologies, to web content management solutions and the future of web development. Jeff is equally committed to the local community. He has served as chairman of the Rockford Chamber of Commerce as well as participated in numerous public task forces and technical advisory boards. He also helped to found the Rock River Valley Technology partners, which is focused on building a regional broadband infrastructure.

Jeffrey Stewart’s Specialties:

Trekk Cross-Media is a full-service marketing, communication and promotional agency that provides a unique blend of strategic business thinking, marketing creativity and best-of-breed technologies.
