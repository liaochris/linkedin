Luke Morris’s Summary

I've been working with newspapers since high school. My passion to keep the industry afloat has grown as I have become more involved with it. I've spent time as a copy editor, reporter, copy chief, and designer.

My goal is to be part of the solution to the currently falling newspaper industry. I always keep my ear to the street to explore new ways to improve newspapers' presence in the Internet.
