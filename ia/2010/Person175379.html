Biography
Robert Weinberg Background and Experience: ROBERT WEINBERG is chairman and president of First Boca Associates, Inc.(FBA), Boca Raton, Florida-based merchant and investment-banking firm. FBA provides the following services to its clients: 1) M&A: Confidential representation of mid size companies ( those with sales in excess of $10 million or earnings in excess of $1 million) seeking acquisition or strong equity participation. FBA can provide introductions to number of private equity firms seeking these investments. Alternatively, FBA can advise these private companies regarding transition to the public market via reverse merger with a public shell corporation. 2) REAL ESTATE: FBA represents clients seeking the acquisition or divestiture of commercial or luxury residential real estate. 3) CONSULTING & ADVISORY: FBA provides consulting or advisory services at the board or CEO level for selected clients. Experienced analysis and innovative strategies can often resolve complex, non-ordinary-course-of-business matters for clients. 4) FINANCE: FBA undertakes corporate or real estate finance engagements on a selective basis. FBA can offer innovative strategies to lower the cost of financing for qualified projects exceeding $30 million Mr. Weinberg has received national recognition as a business strategist as well as for award winning marketing programs and publications. He has extensive experience in corporate finance, governance, structure and restructure. Some of his innovative strategies for funding companies were the subject of US Patent Office filings. First Boca Associates, Inc. has been continuously involved since 1994 in consulting engagements with a U.S. and international client base that focuses on M&A, strategic planning, marketing opportunities, capital formation, and private to public market transitions.
