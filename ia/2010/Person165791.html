Scott Shumaker’s Summary

My goal is to work with organizations looking to outsource their graphic and web design. My ideal clients are those that seek high-quality design, with a Marketing or Publications Director who values an ongoing relationship with a dedicated freelance professional.

I bring a broad range of design, marketing and technology experience to Precision Design. After 8 years working for high-tech companies such as Apple Computer, DocuCorp and SER Solutions, I focused my attention locally, working as Marketing Director for The Studio Theatre where I managed a quarter-million dollar marketing budget.
