Helena V. de Vengoechea’s Summary

Helena V. is a Product Photographer in NYC that has worked for companies such as Polo Ralph Lauren, Cosmo, Allure, Marie Claire, Travel + Leisure, City magazine + more. What makes her different from all the other photographers in the metro area is that not only can she provide sharp,colorful and creative photographs but since she has extensive experience in Art Direction she can also offer you promotional design services, with the photos she shoots, to assist you in marketing your business. Additionally she is diligently working on her photo fine art concept titled Discreet Messages.

If you'd like to review her work you may click on the links in her profile.
