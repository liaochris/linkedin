Allison Moore’s Summary

I am a motivated, hard-working recent graduate who is interested in pursuing a career in book publishing. I graduated (summa cum laude) from Boston University in January 2009 and am seeking an editorial position in the NYC, North Jersey, or Boston area.

This summer I attended the Columbia Publishing Course, where I learned about all sides of the book publishing business and networked with other future agents and editors. Please see the "Education" section below for details on my special projects at the CPC.

I've completed several internships, where I worked to perfect my writing and editing skills and learn more about publishing. I've worked with and reviewed virtually every type of book - fiction, nonfiction, children's, young adult - and have also learned how different parts of the business work together to create quality products.

I also took classes to make myself proficient with desktop publishing and multimedia software, as well as research techniques:

- I designed a multimedia news website using Adobe Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Premiere and Audacity. I constructed an interactive map, created graphics, filmed and edited video and sound clips, and wrote feature stories.

- I worked with an editorial team to create "Nudge," a Boston-based alternative arts and culture magazine. We created a full first issue, for which I wrote stories and used Adobe Quark and Photoshop to design spreads. I also copyedited other students' work.

- In a news research class, I learned how to use Microsoft Excel and Access to organize and manipulate data. I also used deep search techniques to find hidden information on the internet.
