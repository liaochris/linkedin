Dave Gibson’s Summary

I am passionate about learning and teaching and I bring that passion to the classroom. I don't just want to go for the usual "a-ha" moment, I seek the times in which my learners get it, embrace it, and then go evangelize it. It's never enough to just convey the material...I want to create zealots.

It's because of that attitude that I have been described as, "a shockingly effective trainer." I feel as though I can connect and communicate with any audience. I love the challenge of making the insanely technical things easy to understand. I am at my best when I am creating new training programs and breathing new life into existing ones.
