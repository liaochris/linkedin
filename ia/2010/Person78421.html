Gordon Routledge’s Summary

An entrepreneurial engineering professional with a broad and deep knowledge of the lighting and associated technology industries.
- Strong engineering background and proven capabilities in managing and developing new technology based businesses.
- Detailed knowledge of the lighting industry from end users and specifiers through to OEMs, technology providers and component manufacturers.
- Excellent knowledge of LED technology from chip level, through to lighting fixtures encompassing LED devices, power drivers, thermal management, optical design.
- Proven ability in developing markets for new technology and translating market needs in to industry leading products.
- Experience working with leading international component distributors, creating demand and motivating sales force.
- Full understanding of intellectual property creation and protection processes
- Able to build relationships at all levels within an organisation to maximise opportunities and close deals.
- Regular contributor to conference proceedings and trade publications worldwide
- Extensive business travel and understands markets for components and solutions in USA, Europe, China, Japan, Dubai, Australia
