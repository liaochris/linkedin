Karla Silver’s Summary

Twenty Year Corporate Veteran/Evacuee

After the DotCom crash in 2002, I found myself at a crossroads, both personally and professionally.

I knew I didn't want to return to corporate America. I never wanted anyone or anything else to determine my results. Not the economy, not Wall Street, not some management group, or boss.

I made a decision to be the captain of my own ship from that day forward. If I was able to make millions of dollars for Fortune 100 companies, why couldn't I make money for myself?

I happened to bump into two other successful women entrepreneurs, and the rest is history. We (along with other leaders) built a company into a multi-million dollar company, doing business in over 150 countries. All this from our cell phones and our lap tops!

Recently we've been tapped by another well-known entrepreneur (a billionaire) to bring our internet marketing skills and leadership coaching to kick off a new company.

I'm excited to be part of starting up another business as a successful woman entrepreneur.
