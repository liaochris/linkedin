Eric Kronthal’s Summary

Eric is an experienced operator and marketer who is proficient in directing teams that build custom solutions to meet online advertising and marketing objectives. He has led all phases of the product development life cycle, from strategic planning and requirements definition to testing and deployment. Among his accomplishments, Eric is credited with the launch of several industry leading online advertising solutions including Behavioral Targeting, Interactive Television advertising, and several self-service applications. His experience in several startups has shown that Eric is prepared to roll up his sleeves and do whatever is required to meet his firm’s objectives.
