Gretchen Ramsey’s Summary

Winner of the International Council of Shopping Centers’ gold Maxi Award, the industry’s highest honor, Next Gen Women Leaders Award from the City of Philadelphia, and the coveted Best in Show award from the New Jersey Ad Club, Ramsey insists on industry-defining themes for campaigns.

For more than 16 years, Ramsey has managed the reputations of hundreds of organizations, including branding powerhouses DuPont, Time, Inc., KFC and Prime Retail. A noted strategist, writer, media relations specialist and trend spotter, Ramsey advances a progressive voice in the branding realm that inspires believers through a true, interactive communications experience.

Branding, strategy, social media, public relations, crisis management
