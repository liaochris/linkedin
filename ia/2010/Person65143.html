Enrique Varela Couceiro’s Summary

Enrique Varela Couceiro is the President of FUNDACIÓN TECNOLOGÍA SOCIAL (FTS) (social technologies foundation) which carries out plans and projects based on a friendly, usable and easy to use technologies for an inclusive society, as well as head of Accessible Technologies and R&D at the Fundación ONCE for solidarity and integration of persons with disabilities, which carries out projects aimed at training, employment and mainstreaming. This foundation is funded by 3% of the sales of the ONCE’s CUPON, a daily lottery hosted by the Spanish Blind Organisation (ONCE).

As representative of the ONCE and its Foundation, he is also member of the W3C Advisory Committee and the WAI (Web accessibility Initiative) Steering Committee.

As the President of FTS, he is member fo AAATE.

Amongst other current activities, he is one of the shareholders of ALTAIR Multimedia Solutions, a company especialiced in contents for e-learning, development of courses in different platforms and multimedia bassed courses.

Previously, he was CEO of one of the companies belonging to the FUNDOSA GROUP, TECHNOSITE, which specialises in developing internet technology.

He was also Head of the R&D Department of CIDAT, the ONCE’s research Center in technologies for the blind and visually impaired, and Technical Director of “CANAL 11”, an innovative project of radio broadcast system for Spain’s blind persons.

He has likewise held posts in the field of R&D within the ONCE itself, as well as having been a member of its General Council.

His broad experience covering over 27 years in technology has projected him to being a reference both in Spain and abroad in matters related to technologies, disabilities, and design for all.

He has participated as a speaker at several national and international congresses and in many working groups set up by the CEN, CENELEC and other bodies in Europe and the U.S.

Enrique Varela Couceiro’s Specialties:

European projects, web accesibility, ICT consultancy, testing new products, localisation.
