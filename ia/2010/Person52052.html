Dave Sondergeld’s Summary

A proven leader in both the corporate and the entrepreneurial world, I have enjoyed and continue to enjoy a wide variety of technical, business, and industry experiences. While the majority of my career has been focused on technology and the design, support and maintenance of large scale corporate applications, I have also enjoyed career roles on the Vendor side, leading a professional services team, Consulting, running my own management and software consulting company as well as running a small US based import sales and operations firm. As a consultant I have worked in and have had expose to the operations of Retail, CPG, Banking, Finance, Government, Health Services, Banking and more. I have managed a very diverse work force and have built teams from the ground up including onshore, offshore and near shore.

Outside of the office I am a passionate volunteer and have dedicated a family lifestyle to participate in:
* Special Olympics Nother California Volunteer Corrdinator and head of Officials for Soccer
*AYSO Soccer - Management Instructor, Referee Instructor, Assessor, Coach, Ref
* 15 years running Coastal Cleanup Captian Daly City- Coastal Commission
* General volunteering for GGNRA, Daly City Park and Rec
* APA and USHGA past board member - Current Advanced Pilot, Observer and Site Adminstrator
