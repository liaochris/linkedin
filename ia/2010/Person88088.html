James Reinhart’s Summary

James Reinhart is the CEO and Chief Knitwit of thredUP.com an internet platform that helps you exchange great clothes you (or your children) don't wear for great new clothes you will.

He recently graduated from the Harvard Business School and the Harvard Kennedy School. Prior to HBS/HKS, while working in the Bay Area, he helped develop one of the nation’s premier public schools, Pacific Collegiate School – recently named the #3 school in America by US News & World Report. After Pacific Collegiate, he co-founded Beacon Education Network, a charter management & school turnaround organization.

He was a Goldsmith Fellow in Social Enterprise at HBS and a Bill George Fellow at the Center for Public Leadership. He holds a BA in History from Boston College.

He serves on the Board of Directors of American Legion Jersey Boys State and the Young Alumni Committee (GOLD) at Boston College.

new business development, charter school development, public-private partnerships, fundraising, public relations
