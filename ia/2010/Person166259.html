Prof. Saeid Eslamian’s Summary

Professor Saeid Eslamian has published a large number of scientific books, reports, journal and conference papers. He has been successful in a number of research grants. He has been editorial board member of 8 international journals and technical reviewer for over 25 scientific journals. He displays an extensive teaching experience in hydrology, hydrometeorology, water resources and groundwater, particularly in civil, agricultural and natural resources area. Dr. Eslamian has taught and developed course material for about 30 subjects at various levels and supervised over 60 graduate students. Professor has worked with several companies in environmental hydrology area. During his Princeton University affiliation, Eslamian has recently developed a multivariate L-moments coupling GIS for analyzing environmental data and climate parameters. This program has a great application for risk analysis, not only for natural hazards but also for market and financial data. More recently, he had a scientific visiting from Switzerland and has written the joint research proposals with ETH Zurich and for scientific collaboration.
