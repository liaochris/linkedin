Laura Miner’s Summary

I am a recent graduate of Massachusetts College of Art and Design, where I received a BFA in photography with distinction and departmental honors. My main goals are to be a product, art work, and food photographer. I have a passion for using my creativity, and wish to enhance commercial product photography with my artistic flair.

I currently work at Stone Photo in downtown Boston, where I assist in custom framing and use Adobe Photoshop to edit images for printing. I held an internship at Boston Common Press in 2008, in which I became experienced in strobe lighting and Adobe Bridge. I also have extensive black & white darkroom experience with knowledge in chemistry, and color darkroom experience with knowledge in color correction and some processor maintenance.

I would love to work for a museum, gallery, or a publication.

Laura Miner’s Specialties:

Adobe Photoshop, high resolution scanning, digital SLR cameras, inkjet printing, bookmaking, view camera, 35mm, film development, framing, exhibition hanging.
