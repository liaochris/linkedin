Doug Pepper’s Summary

Doug invests in digital media & consumer internet businesses and technology-enabled services. Pepper has led investments, including: Flurry, Marketo, Offerpal Media, Varolii, INVIDI Technologies, Lombardi Software (acquired by IBM), and Achates. In addition, Doug serves as a board observer at ePocrates and Markettools.

Previously, Doug has worked in business development at Amazon.com, helping launch Amazon's Auction and Z-Shops businesses. Prior to Amazon.com, Doug worked in Corporate Finance at Goldman Sachs in New York. In this role, Doug spent time in Hong Kong, advising clients in China, India and Korea.

Doug received his B.A. from Dartmouth College, and his M.B.A. from Stanford University.
