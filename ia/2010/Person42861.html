    Conbeth Construction & Development

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    1985 — Present (25 years )

    The reason that Randy and Sue are so successful in selling new homes is because they have the perfect balance of sales, marketing, and design. Sue Meyer handles all marketing of the business. She accomplishes this by ordering and producing brochures, ads, follow-up letters, website content, and other marketing tools. She is the first person with whom a potential buyer communicates when they contact Conbeth, Inc. Randy is involved in the floorplan design and building aspects of the business. Randy helps Sue close the deal by helping a potential buyer envision their dream home through the floorplan design. With proven experience designing floorplans that are stylish and timeless, Randy is able to really sell the customer during the design phase. Randy and Sue use several techniques to get the word out to potential customers. One of these techniques is their unique brochure, which is sent out as a hard copy and a CD. The CD is interactive and gives an overview of the company as well as directs potential buyers to the company’s website as well as the websites of their suppliers. Randy and Sue believe in full disclosure of their process, being completely open about the products and services they use in building their homes. Another way that Randy and Sue are able to get the word out is by their use of networking. Most people in the home building industry know Randy and Sue by name because of their in-depth involvement in community organizations. This has garnered them a 40% referral rate for homes that they sell.
