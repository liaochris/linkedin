Walter Korfmacher’s Summary

Responsibilities include the management of a group of about 20 scientists (includes 4 Ph. D. scientists) utilizing LC-API/MS/MS for the rapid PK screening of new candidate drugs. Additional responsibilities include acting as a Drug Discovery Project Manager with the responsibility of managing the Drug Metabolism support of a major potential new drug product for the company—this has resulted in three recommended compounds being progressed to development in the last three years. Other duties include overseeing the maintenance and operation of over 15 LC-API/MS/MS instrumentsand one Sciex QSTAR MALDI “Q-TOF” instrument.

Walter Korfmacher’s Specialties:

Management of a large group of scientists. Expertise in mass spectrometry as well as drug metabolism support of new drug discovery. Editor of the book: Using Mass Spectrometry for Drug Metabolism Studies (CRC Press). Over 125 publications in the scientific literature and over 75 presentations at various scientific forums.

Additional Information

Walter Korfmacher’s Interests:

Application of mass spectrometry to drug metabolism participation in the drug discovery process. This includes the development of procedures and methods to increase the throughput of mass spectrometry assays for new chemical entities. In addition, basic and applied research in the area of analytical methods development, particularly organic qualitative analysis and trace organic quantitative methods. Most recent experience is in liquid chromatography atmospheric pressure ionization mass spectrometry and tandem mass spectrometry applied to pharmaceutical molecules.
