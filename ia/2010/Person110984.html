Karin Schwartz’s Summary

CEO's and business leaders come to Schwartz Associates to increase sales opportunities, increase revenue and gain back their time. It's a minimal investment, about 1/4 the budgetary expense of hiring a full time experienced business development professional yet substantially less risk and the results are an increase in closing ratio, greater profitabiity, and a higher return on investment.

Schwartz Associciates provides outsourced business development services to small and medium sized businesses in the Mid Atlantic Region that are looking to improve the quantity and quality of their sales opportunities to drive revenue. Current clients focus in waste reduction, recycling, electric and fuel supply, energy auditing, alternative energy, composting, etc.

Our clients are looking for help in meeting decision makers, gaining access to their desired markets, and turning introductions into sales opportunitites. Most clients struggle to find the time to focus on business development activities like networking, research, and creating partnerships. We handle all these activities and more to help you focus on what's most important - growing your business.
