Jennifer Wilson’s Summary

I am the co-founder of and partner with ConvergenceCoaching, LLC, a national consulting firm that develops leadership, succession, and marketing strategies for CPA and IT firms and the channel organizations that serve them. I have had the good fortune to be named to Accounting Today’s list of the “100 Most Influential People in Accounting,” and have worked in both the public accounting and IT sectors. As a partner for BDO Seidman, LLP, I ran a national financial systems consulting group. Prior to BDO, I was Vice President of Sales and Customer Services for publicly traded State Of The Art, Inc., now known as Sage Software, Inc., where I was responsible for Sage's sales, marketing communications, education, and customer services functions.

Today, I am a frequent speaker, teacher, facilitator, and writer within the CPA profession and am a member of the Association for Accounting Administration, the Association for Accounting Marketing, American Marketing Association, International Coaching Federation, Information Technology Association and the New Horizon Group.

Jennifer Wilson’s Specialties:

Succession planning, strategic planning, training and development, coaching, consulting, distance learning, marketing, business development, and organizational development for CPA and IT firms and the channel-based organizations who support CPA and IT firms.
