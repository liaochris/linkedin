    KOLABO

    (Graphic Design industry)

    July 2009 — August 2009 (2 months)

    KOLABO is a Communication Research Laboratory. KOLABO boasts a powerful architecture of participation, with workshops, lectures and guerilla-style actions. The aim is to make plausible the many intriguing scenarios for our future. It brings together some very interesting personae that collaborate to investigate and visualize the amazing possibilities and implications of advanced social technologies and human behaviour. These experts come from many fields, they are Inventors, Visionaries, Futurists, Artists, Designers, Media Researchers, Writers, Scientists and Programmers.
