About the Organizations:
Corporation Service Company® (www.cscglobal.com) is a leading provider of legal and financial services for the world’s largest companies, law firms and financial institutions. CSC does business with 75% of the Fortune 500 companies. Founded in 1899, CSC® is a service company dedicated to reducing risks and costs for our clients by providing corporate identity protection; corporate governance & compliance; litigation & matter management; and deal and transaction support services.
