Juan Carlos Morales’s Summary

Juan Carlos Morales is Managing Director and Chief Financial Officer of BNY Mellon Wealth Management, a top 10 U.S. wealth manager and one of the key businesses of BNYMellon, the oldest financial services company in the United States. With $156 billion in client assets and $1 billion in revenues, BNY Mellon Wealth Management is a leading provider of private investment management and banking solutions to ultra high net worth individuals. In his role as CFO, Juan Carlos oversees all elements of financial management, strategic planning, performance and capital management, accounting operations and internal control.

Before joining BNY Mellon Wealth Management, Juan Carlos served as Senior Vice President and division CFO at Bank of America Merchant Services. He began his career with PricewaterhouseCoopers Financial Services Industry Practice, where he ascended to Senior Manager and provided assurance and consulting services to Fortune 500 companies.

Juan Carlos serves as a member of the Boards of Trustees for Boston Medical Center, BMC’s HealthNet Plan, The Partnership Inc. and Wheelock College and on the advisory boards for Color Media Group and the Boston Chapter of Association of Latino Professionals in Finance and Accounting, which he co-founded in 1998. Juan Carlos graduated cum laude from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and is a licensed CPA.

He and his wife Jennifer and their children live in Beacon Hill, Massachusetts.
