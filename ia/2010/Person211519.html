Victoria Lang’s Summary

Victoria Lang is a Partner of BigVoice Unlimited, an integrated communications company born to champion brands that need a larger share of voice, Co-President of PLUS Media, a Broadcast Services company, and Co-President of PLUS Entertainment, a live entertainment production company.

For her work as a Producer on the popular nationally syndicated television show LIVE WITH REGIS & KATHIE LEE, Victoria received 3 Emmy Award nominations. She was Executive Producer of HAVEN, a syndicated weekly sponsored program starring Joy Philbin, and co-produced and wrote the successful Specials for PBS: “GUY LOMBARDO: THE SWEETEST MUSIC THIS SIDE OF HEAVEN” and “GLENN MILLER’S GREATEST HITS,” starring Kathie Lee Gifford. For A&E, she produced the star-studded, top rated network special “ROSEMARY CLOONEY’S DEMI-CENTENNIAL."

For film she produced the independent feature MACBETH IN MANHATTAN,which premiered on Showtime (Best Feature, South Beach Film Festival; Audience Award, Long Island Film Festival; dramatic feature competition, South by Southwest & Florida Film Festivals), DARK TIDES (Showtime), PERFECT LIES and THE FOUNTAIN OF DEATH, all of which are currently in worldwide distribution, and the digital Internet feature CHAOS THEORY.

Ms. Lang worked in publicity and sales at CBS before serving as Senior Vice President and Director of the Broadcast Department at the Rowland Company, a division of Saatchi and Saatchi.

Theatre: Producer of LISTEN TO MY HEART (Drama Desk nomination), the NYCFringe Festival Winner MATT & BEN (Time magazine Top 5 shows of 2003), JOHNNY GUITAR (2004 Outer Critics Circle Award, Best Off-Broadway musical), BAT BOY: THE MUSICAL (West End), SHOUT! The MOD Musical (Off Broadway, Chicago,National Tour, West End, UK Tour, Japan, Korea), SILENCE! THE MUSICAL (London, Winner 2005 NYCFringe), FOUND. People Find Stuff. Now It's A Show (Ars Nova, NYC).

Victoria Lang’s Specialties:

Broadcast production, online casual games for the PR industry, integrated media marketing,event production, theatrical production.
