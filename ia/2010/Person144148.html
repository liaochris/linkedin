Michelle Braden’s Summary

Michelle Braden has been coaching and training for seventeen years. She is an advanced certified human behavior consultant, certified business coach and facilitator of GET CLIENTS NOW!

Michelle’s down to earth approach makes her easy to work with. She has a sharp business sense, ability to stretch, inspire and motivate leaders to accomplish their dreams.

Michelle has successfully coached: business owners, executives, non-profit organizations, teams, new managers and individuals in transition. She specializes in assisting leaders with their greatest asset… their team!
