Jade Y. Gunver’s Summary

I work with people who want to know what they are doing right and where they need more work. I will visit your site, read your content, look at your products from the eyes of a consumer, then from a business prospective. I will look at your social media presence and the general public awareness of your company, brand and it’s products.

Whether you have been in business for ten years or just starting your first blog, having direction is vital in the midst of millions of people all screaming to be heard, I can help you make sure you are being listened to.

As Promin Group we add Social Media Marketing into our body as of 2006. We truly understand how a business works, how to integrate social media tools into an organization, the challenges and obstacles that organizations are faced with when it comes to social media and finally the difference between ROI and impact. Not only we want to help businesses how to tweet or create a facebook and YouTube account, but also help them succeed in this tough economic times.

Specialities:

Create, track and maintain a social media strategy for Company

Implement online marketing plans for social media and word of mouth driven programs

Monitor and engage in brand related online conversations

Coordinate facebook pages & applications, blogs and twitter profiles

Create integration and syndication between all social profiles and platforms

Work with Product Teams to develop content to push through social media

Work with internal teams to create compelling viral campaigns to drive awareness and sales

Research and propose new opportunities for social media marketing and advertising campaigns

Stay current with social media trends and incorporate as appropriate to improve the customer experience, drive sales and leverage ongoing customer involvement

Investigate and implement new technologies, services and capabilities and/or vendor solutions that enhance social media efforts
