Roberta Rosenberg’s Summary

A direct marketing strategist, consultant and copywriter for 25 years, I'm the Founder and President/CEO of the marketing consultancy, MGP Direct, Inc. (1987) ... founder/owner of AdoptShoppe.com, online gift store (1999) ... and owner of AdoptShoppe Press, an adoption/alternative family baby book publishing company (2003).

I blog at CopywritingMaven.com, write the popular landing page makeover series at Copyblogger.com, teach new copywriters for the American Writers and Artists Institute, and review children's books for Adoptive Families Magazine.

Roberta Rosenberg’s Specialties:

B2B and B2C marketing strategy, copywriting and design, SEO/SEM, web development and coordination, and social media consulting. Deep expertise in medical/health science, social service, information publishing, high-tech, web application, and small business start-ups.
