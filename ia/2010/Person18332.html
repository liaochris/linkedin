Atul Gupta’s Summary

An entrepreneur focused at Business Development & Operations in IT Solutions Space. Started my venture in Bhutan focused on that geography for 3 years. I have handled the complete customer engagement and the sales cycle of software products and solutions. My engagement with the customers has been during project delivery phases as well.

As a venture we have achieved success starting from scratch the venture went on to being a leading solution vendor it the region. There were plenty of customers that we won in the Manufacturing, Trading, and mostly in the Public Services Vertical providing them with ERP / Custom developed software solutions. I have been instrumental in sales, bidding, proposal submission, negotiation, agreement, delivery and sign off of all the projects.

My current venture is based out of Kolkata, India focused at Small & Medium Business Accounts in India. My role involves customer engagement, account management and company administration.

My personal goal is to continuously increase the customer base and maintain relationship with the accounts.
