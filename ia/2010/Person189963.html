Simon Eyre’s Summary

Simon is a successful Mobile Commerce & Change Management consultant with over sixteen years experience in the telecoms, mobile and internet industries. He is also joint founder and CEO at Mobivize.

An MBA graduate, Simon has a sound academic background that he combines with his business acumen and experience to deliver a complete, rounded service to his clients and past employers.

He employs a creative approach to developing new strategies for innovative products & services and the development of new business. His previous roles include product and proposition management, strategy setting, business case development, solution sales, change management consulting, business management, business valuation and product and channel marketing.

Having worked in a range of business types, from start-ups to blue chip multi-nationals, in a number of countries Simon has an international flavour to his repertoire. He has a strong reputation of leadership and innovation up to CxO level in many corporations.

Simon is currently looking for interim contracts in the Mobile Commerce space, in a Business Development, Marketing or Strategic role.

Key achievements include:

• Key leadership role in launch of new Internet Service Provider start up
• Leading solutions and proposition launches in UK, Europe and the USA delivering double digit million £ revenues.
• Rollout of an Italian voice network
• Key role in re-establishing BT's presence in the Mobile services market
• Developed and managed SMS services for large scale business users
• Successfully launched a number of mobility data and voice products in several countries including USA, Italy, Holland and the Isle of Man
• Development of large scale flexible working solutions for major UK corporate customers

He is a Link Governor at Saxmundham Primary School responsible for the ICT curriculum. I am a volunteer consultant with the Cranfield Trust which provides free consulting to the not for profit sector.
