Garry Grant’s Summary

Garry Grant is one of the premier experts in the fields of search engine optimization and the Internet. Garry is responsible for setting the strategic direction, developing proprietary technologies, overseeing the staff, and setting corporate sales and marketing goals. He has also earned a reputation as an educational — and captivating — speaker.

At heart the entrepreneur, Garry also founded DAG Web Studios in 1995. One of the hottest design shops on the Internet, DAG Web Studios specialized in high-end customized Web development, e-business consulting, multimedia Web-casting, and video streaming services. Clients included Artisan Motion Pictures (The Blair Witch Web Sites) DistantCorners.com, the Premiere Radio Network (Clear Channel communications), Cream Ridge Winery, Executive Software Inc. (Developers of Diskeeper), World West Music, and dozens of others.

Garry Grant is also a member of the W3C World Wide Web Consortium. The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) develops interoperable technologies (specifications, guidelines, software, and tools) to lead the Web to its full potential.

Garry and senior team leaders have also created a college credited syllabus for the University of California San Diego. The class, titled “SEO & SEM: The Fast Track to Search Engine Optimization and Marketing,” will equip participants with strategies for optimizing websites to achieve higher rankings in the top search engines. Improved search engine rankings help companies increase their Web visibility, brand awareness and online sales.

Among his diverse talents and interests, Garry has played guitar with Bruce Springsteen, Bon Jovi, Ritchie Sambora, Sly Stone, and Foghat.
