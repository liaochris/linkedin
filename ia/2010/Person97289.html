Jessie.Bechard @yahoo.com’s Summary

Would you like to construct pipelines 10X faster?

Do you have internal corrosion problems?

Are you tired of constant welding failures?

How would you like to eliminate X-ray testing?

We have developed a system to construct pipelines that is 5-12X faster and eliminates all the headaches associated with conventional welding. In the past 10-years we have completed 2500KM (200,000+ installations) with a 100% success-rate (ref. Saudi Aramco).

Here’s basically how the system works:

We engineer a coupling with a smaller inside-diameter (ID) than the outside-diameter (OD) of the line-pipe. The pipe ends are pressed into the coupling using a joining unit. This process creates a significant level of interfacial bearing pressure between the pipe and coupling, resulting in a coupling/pipe connection much stronger than the pipe itself.

Here's the payoff:

100% tested and approved for high-pressure water injection, oil and gas flow-lines (current in-service locations are: USA, Saudi Arabia, Oman and UAE).

100% capability for maximum allowable stresses under recognized piping codes (we can engineer a coupling for almost any line pipe from 2-12").

You get your production online 5-12X faster.

1. Your pipeline and internal service specifications.
2. Estimated size, location and time line of your project.
3. Any questions or concerns you have.
