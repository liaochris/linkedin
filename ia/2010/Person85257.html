Stephen W Anderson’s Summary

Stephen W. Anderson is the President, CEO and Chief Creative Officer of iMediaPro, a Palm Desert, California based company, formerly known as Cold Call Cowboy Productions. Founded by Mr. Anderson in 1988, iMediaPro is the leading provider of custom personal marketing tools with over 24,000 clients in the United States, Canada, and internationally as well. The company’s premier flagship product is the Multimedia Business Card. With initial emphasis on the financial services and real estate industries, the company has now expanded its offerings in over 100 different industries. The Multimedia Business Card is much more than just a CD ROM. It’s a full size interactive multimedia CD ROM with a 3 minute customized presentation incorporating audio, video, animation, text, graphics and photos, all housed in a beautiful album about the size of a best selling book. As Mr. Anderson says, “packaging is the key to success in marketing.”

The Custom Magazine Reprints are for any business, big or small that has a need to tell their story and get their message heard in a unique way that establishes instant credibility.

In addition to iMediaPro, Mr. Anderson has created a new division, Interactive MediaWorks, specifically to establish distributors with a unique new business model perfect for today’s financial times. The world’s most detailed and comprehensive training tool, The 100 Day Game Plan To Prosperity was created to aid new distributors in creating their own prosperity.

A masterful speaker in the areas of business development, personal marketing and sales, Steve has been called a “visionary”. He has always been and remains in the forefront of cutting edge, state-of-the-art marketing technology, and shares his visionary philosophy via 200 annual business development seminars he presents across the United States and Canada and weekly conference call presentations.
