At the Buzz Factoree LLC, We spread PR pollen and online marketing buzz with and for people who fear they are dinosaurs, non-geeks who never bargained for the digital age and other professionals who weren’t born yesterday. We specialize in working with people who are either reluctant to use online tools because they don't understand them, fear they are too old to learn or simply are unaware of them.

We work with professionals, non-profits and small businesses, helping them manage their reputations, gain visibility and reach their marketing goals. We do this through:

1) Coaching -- Training your entire office to use social media, online marketing, effective business writing or other skills your staff needs to communicate in today's world, regardless of whether they grew up with the internet or "don't get this stuff."

2) Consulting -- Working with you to develop and implement a social media platform that meets your organization's needs.

3) Copywriting – Developing high-quality content for online and print marketing needs or PR campaigns.

4) Campaigns – Implementing a turn-key strategic communications program for your business or non-profit.

We provide a full range of marketing and public relations services focused on helping your organization reach its potential. My experience includes 30+ years of demonstrated success in nearly every aspect of communications, marketing and public relations. I earned the accredited business communicator designation (ABC) from the International Association of Business Communicators and have won more than 350 local, state and national awards for my communications work.

But we don't work for awards; we work for our clients, and we're eager to help you reach your goals. Let's get buzzing!
