Hung Le’s Summary

Technology Veteran and Executive with 25+ years of hands-on, technical management experience in IC process technology, IC design and manufacturing. Holder of 9 patents with others pending.

Expertise lies in the Mixed-signal IC product development with intimate knowledge of device and circuit levels spanning from product definition, technology and IP development, circuit design, design methodology, test development, to release to manufacturing. Expert in the most advanced ESD mixed-signal solutions.

My goal is to operate an electronics company with a revenue of $US 50M-100M leveraging extensive US-based industrial experiences and professional network coupled with Far-East background and language.
