    Gary's knowledge and advice totally blew away any barriers or preconceived ideas I had with breaking in to Hollywood as a screenwriter.

    2009 — 2009

    Hollywood Producer meetings.

    A great opportunity to meet Hollywood producers, directors and agents.

    Three days of amazing meetings, very well organised and professional.

    I got to meet a great bunch of people, the meetings were held in Hollywood and Beverly Hills in really nice locations.

    This gave me an insider view of Hollywood. I also learnt what the business is all about and what producers really want.

    This is something every aspiring screenwriter, who is serious about making it in Hollywood, should do!

    http://sherwoodoakscollege.com/
