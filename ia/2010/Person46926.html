Daniela Duncan’s Summary

I was born and raised in Brazil. Portuguese is my native language, and I am fluent in English and in Spanish.

I earned a Bachelor's Degree of Arts in Mass Communications, with a major in Journalism, at Universidade Federal do Ceará. I worked as a journalist in Brazil for five years before moving to the United States. In South Carolina, I had the opportunity to work as an in-house translator for an international law firm and I became fascinated with this profession. I have been working as a translator and studying the art of translating for over seven years now.

I’m a freelance Portuguese and Spanish translator. My purpose is to promote communication and growth - and to provide my clients with high-quality, culturally appropriate translations.

Daniela Duncan’s Specialties:

I have experience translating: marketing materials, brochures, pamphlets, letters, biographies; court documents, medical records, financial reports; certificates and decrees; Curriculum Vitaes and resumés; abstracts, monographs, essays and news articles. I can perform consecutive Portuguese<>English interpreting during conferences and presentations. I can do website localization (adaptation to the Brazilian culture and market).
