Michael "Duke" Davis’s Summary

I am a published author in both the fiction and non-fiction fields with five novels and 23 short stories to my credit. As an Instructor and Trainer I designed and wrote my own curriculum for various subjects ranging from software like Lotus 123, Microsoft Office, Windows, Digital VAX/VMS All-In-One, Word Perfect and many others for advanced and beginning users and Computer Basics and many other subjects for beginners. I managed many different size computer systems and worked on the maintenance end as well training new technicians to maintain the equipment and missing no aspect of the work involved. I programmed extensively in Digital VAX/VMS All-In-One, Lotus123, dBase III and IV, Basic, Basica, CS1 Monocode and several languages used strictly in Classified Situations with the US Government. As first a Repair Technician, then Maintenance Department Head, and later Vice President and Head of Research and Development at Dynamic Computer Systems, I became heavily involved in software testing as we were a Computer Systems House. I hold three patients on IBM Microcomputers and was the first person to do true multi-user on a microcomputer.
Extensive training and experience with high vacuum technology involved in the Ion Implanter Industry where Solid State Devices are made. A good grounding in Robotics and Chemical Engineering were required for that.
With twenty years in the US Navy in both the Special Weapons and Computer Fields I am qualified to do just about anything that comes down the pike.I'm a qualified combat veteran with several tours in Vietnam and other conflicts around the globe.
