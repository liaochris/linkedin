Elizabeth Gordon’s Summary

Part-time Chief Marketing Officer for growing companies.

Trusted confidante and advisor to an elite group of entrepreneurs and business executives.

I'm also author of The Chic Entrepreneur: Put Your Business in HIgher Heels - an Amazon.com 50+ week bestseller for women in business - pioneering a feminine approach to business, helping women entrepreneurs and business leaders to elevate their power and their value in the marketplace.

I've given hundreds of presentations, workshops and seminars for business owners, business students, executives, women's organizations and trade groups.

I work with clients to:
- build and grow strong businesses with a solid foundation,
- expand into new markets, segments, products, services
- increase their value in the marketplace and raise prices,
- boldly differentiate with style - personality and pizzaz,
- build momentum though communication and community,
- get people within the organization excited and on board,
- create a culture that uplifts and expands.

The Chic Circle mastermind concept has met with great success and achieved 10- 50 X ROI results for clients. We are now launching groups around the city for an exclusive group of high achieving businesswomen.
