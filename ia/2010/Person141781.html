Mia Davies’s Summary

My name is Mia Davies and I want to thank you for stopping by my Linked In page. I have always had an entrepreneurial spirit and a drive to positively and powerfully impact people's lives. This desire is what had me enter the world of Speech Therapy and this this calling is what lead me to the industry of network marketing.

Network marketing is, by far, the most powerful avenue I have found to impact lives and empower people to live to their full potential. That being said, I have seen so many people fail in this industry because they don't have the marketing and mindset training required to succeed.

This is what lead me to learning and mastering one of the single most powerful skills a network marketer can have...online marketing, more specifically, online attraction marketing. I have been coached and mentored by some of the top internet marketers in the industry and can now pass this knowledge on to other network marketers.

The internet is an ever expanding force in our world and especially in business. Every business owner and home based business owner must know the ins and outs of marketing online and how to leverage the power of this incredible tool we have.

I provide coaching and training to people in network marketing to empower them to succeed in online marketing through attraction marketing and relationship marketing techniques. These same principles can be applied to marketing any business online.

My goal is to empower hundreds of network marketers to become online marketing success stories and enjoy a lucrative residual income without ever having to leave the house..more time for family, fun, and LIFE!
