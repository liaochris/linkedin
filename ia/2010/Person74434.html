George Nolan’s Summary

Head of Web Development Strategy, responsible for defining the future development objectives of TUI Travel, UK and Ireland's mainstream websites and ensuring that the business processes, programme and project governances are in place to achieve them.

As e-Commerce Manager, was responsible for the design and development of the UK’s leading holiday website. Lead a team of three e-commerce managers, three web designers and a usability analyst as well as liaising with the managers of the Sales, Marketing, Content and IT development functions.

Led a major redevelopment of www.thomson.co.uk. This website uniquely offers customers the choice of Thomson’s own stock of package holidays and those created dynamically from third-party components within the same search and book flow. This has enabled Thomson to offer customers a greater range of options in order to address the declining package holiday market and achieve greater than market growth in year-on-year sales.
