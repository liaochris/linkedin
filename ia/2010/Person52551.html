Dawn Gluskin’s Summary

Dawn Gluskin is the founder and CEO of SolTec Electronics, a company which has received many awards & accolades for rapid business growth & expansion, even in a down economy. A true entrepreneur story - Dawn started the company by herself, working from her home office & has grown to a multi-million dollar company with office & warehouse space & 10 new hires in less than 2 years! Her success is attributed to her strong focus on quality & customer service, which has earned her the trust & preferred vendor status of electrical manufacturing clients around the globe.

She has over 15 years experience in sales and marketing and an extreme passion for entrepreneurship, which fueled her to take the plunge into starting her own company. This past February, she was a Make Mine a Million $ Business awardee from the Women’s Business Summit in Houston, sponsored by American Express OPEN and Count Me In & a finalist in the Ernst & Young Entrepreneurial Winning Women competition.

SolTec Electronics sources obsolete, long lead-time and other hard-to-find electronic components for manufacturing clients around the globe and helps them keep their production lines up!. Their product line includes an array of board-level component including semiconductors, integrated circuits, connectors, power modules, resistors, capacitors, and more. With technology constantly becoming obsolete or superseded and with component manufacturers often having trouble keeping up with current demands, SolTec is in a great niche market with a highly sought-after service.

Dawn Gluskin’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    SolTec Electronics

    (Privately Held; Semiconductors industry)

    July 2008 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    SolTec Electronics offers strategic procurement solutions to electronic manufacturing clients around the globe. With years of industry experience, strong established relationships with a global supply chain network, and an emphasis on customer and quality focus, SolTec Electronics is the independent distributor to turn to for obsolete, long lead-time, and other hard-to-find semiconductors. SolTec Electronics works with clients to help them find solutions that help them avoid costly board redesigns, production "line down" situations and other undesirable circumstances. SolTec Electronic's business model is based on long-term customer relationships with commitment to on-time delivery, quality, service, and product expertise.

    As founder & CEO, Dawn Gluskin is the company's visionary and leader. She overseas all aspects of the business, sets goals & delegates tasks in order to achieve the companies vision most effectively & efficiently. Her major focus is in sales, marketing, and business development which are the areas in which see has excelled throughout her career. She also plays an active role in the company's social media & PR campaigns & enjoys the creative outlet those tasks allow.
