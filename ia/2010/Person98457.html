Jimi Ogunnusi’s Summary

Jimi is the Founder and Managing Director of MOTO-SUPREME GLOBAL LTD and BQM CONSULTING LTD

Everything about him epitomizes the ethos and culture of the company. His desire to deliver value and excellence permeates his everyday life. In his own words, he states – “My word is my bond, my integrity is my currency, my passion and my vision are my assets”

He has over fifteen years of consultancy experience securing competitive advantage and increased profitability for his clients and, as a consequence, has maintained long-term business relationships with many of them.

When dealing with risk and securing competitive advantage, he believes the best way to predict the future is to create it...

He is currently expanding his management consultancy practice into Nigeria – An emerging and global powerhouse. His company’s objective is play its part in ensuring that the country achieves its vision 2020 – To become a fully developed global economy by the year 2020.

Jimi is also spearheading the introduction of Nulon fuel treatments into Nigeria. Nulon fuel treatments will, without doubt, comprehensively solve the problems associated with Nigeria’s poor performing petrol and diesel.

His vision and passion is to significantly improve the motoring experiences of every driver and car owner in Nigeria. The use of Nulon products is a crucial prelude to achieving this objective.

He strongly believes that Nigerian motorists deserve the best…and shouldn’t settle for anything less.

Jimi describes himself as a hybrid leader – he is a strategic practitioner, a motivator, an enforcer, an entrepreneur and a professional manager, all rolled into one. He has the vehicle, vision and passion to ensure that your change journey is a pleasurable one, and that you reach your destination regardless of the terrain.

He has a tenacious appetite for getting the best out of people. To this end he subscribes to the notion that ‘motivation is a frame of mind’.
