Scott Addyman’s Summary

Mr. Addyman Is an accomplished, energetic CE Sales Executive with a passion for technology.

His remarkable career comes fully equipped with experience managing national accounts and support staffs, launching products and championing coordinated marketing efforts with North American Consumer Electronics Retail Accounts including Mass Merchant, Big Box, Specialty, Etailers, Superstores, Clubs and National Distributors.

Scott is accustomed to delivering multi-million dollar revenues for manufacturers of Consumer Electronics, Video Games & Peripherals, Mobile Entertainment Devices, Computer and Console Accessories, Home Entertainment, Flash and Memory Products, Digital Devices, Gaming and Wireless Devices and Consumer Software.

An authentic and compelling communicator he has the proven ability to influence merchandising decisions at the highest senior levels of leadership.

As a National Accounts Executive of an award winning Midwest CE and Video Games Manufacturers Representatives firm, Scott managed national accounts and pioneered the launch and growth of some of todays most recognized brands with retailers, Target, Best Buy and GameStop.

As CEO of LaserSoft (sold to GTI) he lead the world's first casual games publisher while negotiating first time license's and publishing agreements with; Activision, EA, Microsoft, Ubi Soft, Disney, Sony, Sega, Blizzard, Eidos, Bethesda, Epic Mega Games, Interplay, Virgin Interactive, Time Warner Interactive, Fox Entertainment, Sierra On-line, and dozens more exploding onto the scene at Target, GameStop, Best Buy, Wal-Mart, Toys-r-Us and many retailers shelves igniting the value category.

His In-depth mastery of product placement at retail and online, implementing product launch, sell thru and end of life cycle strategies with an eye for the top and bottom lines, can only come from his understanding and track record in penetrating & growing markets while directing organization business processes and procedures.
