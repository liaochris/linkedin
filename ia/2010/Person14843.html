Anthony Gold’s Summary

While my love for open source and its societal enablement of true “mass collaboration” hasn’t waned one bit, I knew I wanted to use those “movements” to do more than just help businesses save money and reach new heights. I found the perfect opportunity in an organization called Healthy Humans.

Healthy Humans is an amazing company consisting of some of the world’s top medical doctors in integrative medicine (including folks like Dr. Mehmet Oz of Oprah fame). Their mission – help people with chronic illness (like diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, crohn’s & colitis, and many more) get unbelievable outcomes. All built on the premise that instead of just treating the symptoms of illness, but by looking at the underlying physiology and psychology of an individual that you can dramatically enhance the condition. The underlying software engines are built on open source software (of course), and leverage the evidence-based research and MD clinical expertise to build personalized wellness plans for individuals.

The moment a Healthy Humans user told me, “Your program has changed my life” (she has type 2 diabetes and was able to significantly reduce her A1c’s and lose weight she could never lose before), I was hooked. Consumer-directed healthcare is big, and by leveraging open source concepts with an incredible medical team, many lives can be touched.

Prior to Healthy Humans, I grew a software & services startup business from scratch to over $600m, and was named as one of the “top business leaders who matter the most in 2008” by LinuxWorld magazine. I was one of the original inventors of the ES7000, which is now in the Guinness Book of World Records for hosting the largest number of concurrent gamers. I also invented and designed several mainframes which subsequently generated billions of dollars in total revenue. I have eight patents around computer design and architecture. My passion: leveraging technology to do amazing things for the world.
