Mike Whitmore’s Summary

President and Co-Founder of Fresh Consulting, offering comprehensive business strategy consulting services and specializing in Web 2.0 and Enterprise 2.0 technologies. Fresh also runs effective Social Media campaigns for a variety of our clients. We work with small, medium and Enterprise-level clients delivering both strategy and execution / implementation work.

As a former Client Account Executive with Unisys I managed the $60 mil P&L, C-level executive relationships and sales team efforts between Unisys and Washington Mutual (WaMu). I represented Unisys' full portfolio of solutions, services and thought leadership from our worldwide financial domain expertise. WaMu was a Top 50 account for Unisys with a multi-year, multi-million dollar BPO relationship.

I am the former President and now a Member of the Board for Work Force Development Center, a non-profit dedicated to helping at-risk teens with critical job skills development. We exceeded our financial objectives during both years of my tenure as Board President. Fifty-eight students graduated from the program in May 2009 with over 800 students graduating the program since its inception in 1990. www.wfdcenter.org

Additionally I served as the President of the BYU Management Society, Seattle Chapter - the most successful US chapter in terms of attendance and financial objectives. The Society's motto is, "Building Moral and Ethical Leadership" in business around the world. As a charitable organization we provide scholarships for merit and need to prospective students from Western Washington. For 2007, 2008 and 2009 the Society provided scholarships totaling over $100,000 every year - www.byuwa.org
