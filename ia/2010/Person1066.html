Aaron Young’s Summary

Laughlin Associates is a leader in the incorporation services industry and has, for 38 years, helped business owners protect their companies, families and assets.

The Laughlin team is uniquely positioned to help business owners answer three critical questions when it comes to incorporating, tax planning and asset protection:

1. Which type of entity is right for my business?
2. How can I best protect my personal and corporate assets?
3. How can I maximize the tax benefits available to me and my corporation?

I recently had the opportunity of speaking with a client at one of our asset protection seminars. He expressed his relief at finally understanding how to use his corporation in a way that would protect not only his company, but his family and assets. Because he got the right information this particular client can now focus more clearly on building his business, with the peace of mind that comes from understanding how to avoid unnecessary risks.
These stories are common among our clients and that is why I’m passionate about what we do. It’s why we continue to reach out to small business owners and potential partners. Helping to protect your business is our business!
