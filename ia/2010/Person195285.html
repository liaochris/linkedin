Steve McCoy’s Experience

  • Principal Consultant

    McCoy Power Consultants

    (Utilities industry)

    December 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    McCoy Power Consultants (MPC) specializes in all aspects of compliance with NERC and Regional Reliability Standards. MPC has a unique understanding of the development, organization and presentation of evidence necessary to show compliance with reliability standards and the auditing methods used by auditors and investigators. MPC has the experience of having been on the “other side of the audit or investigation.” MPC prepares an entity for audits or investigations through the eyes of the auditor.

    MPC also provides preparation for settlement conferences and hearings associated with an alleged violation(s) of a reliability standard.

  • Vice President and Director of Compliance

    Western Electricity Coordinating Council

    (Utilities industry)

    September 2006 — December 2008 (2 years 4 months)

    Developed the Compliance Monitoring and Enforcement Program (CMEP) and established the initial compliance department for the WECC region. Hired a staff of over 25 full time employees and six full time consultants. Key writer in the development of the NERC pro-forma CMEP and the WECC CMEP. Developed auditing and investigation processes and led the first year of audits. Investigation team lead on all major Compliance Violation Investigations. Established data handling and filing processes and procedures for all compliance data. Trained all new employees. Worked with NERC on compliance enforcement programs and tools.

    Developed the premier compliance education and outreach program for the Registered Entities in the WECC Region that many other regions have now adopted.

    Developed the hearing and settlement processes and was the principle decision maker at all WECC settlement conferences. All settlement conferences ended successfully with a settlement in principle reached.
