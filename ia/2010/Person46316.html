Dana Fowler’s Summary

I’m a dynamic, results-driven interactive strategist/producer with a 16-year record of achievement and demonstrated success building and expanding branded campaigns in the interactive arena.

For the last year and a half I have worked with Momentum Advertising, St. Louis’ largest advertising agency, building interactive campaigns for their clients. I have also served as Director, Interactive Marketing for 2 other St. Louis agencies. As such, a primary responsibility was developing strategic digital applications for our clients; as well as creating the concepts for video, web, viral, social networking, social media and interactive kiosks (including new “gesture-based” technologies). I also have managed the development of many of these projects from conception to completion.

My experience in television broadcast news and video production also brings added value for your company.
