Jia (Jasmine) Liu | 刘佳’s Summary

With academic and professional background in language and marketing communications, Jia had been focusing on new media marketing and research both in B2B and B2C sectors during her graduate school years.

Jia brought her research and new media knowledge into the current business development position with CSOFT, a leading supplier in the translation and localization industry. Her main responsibilities include prospecting clients with needs to launch products and services to other countries and serving key accounts with customized localization solutions to support their globalization strategy. In the meantime, she keeps a close eye on emerging marketing channels/best practices and propose fresh ideas to sales/mkt team for increasing corporate brand visibility.

After work, Jia volunteers for the English version of TedtoChina initiative to bring to the global TED community creative ideas from the Greater China region & observations drawn from cross-cultural context. She is also interested in food, health, and branding and may pursue another degree in food psychology. Last but not least, Jia is a big fan of photography and cooking.
