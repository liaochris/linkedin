Alan Harrison’s Summary

With over twenty years of experience in digital animation for visual effects, film, television and games working with industry leaders Electronic Arts, Mainframe and CIS Rainmaker, Alan’s career has covered everything from national television commercials, music videos, feature films, simulator rides, television series, and of course and most recently; video games, which he has shipped over 18 AAA titles. Some of Alan’s key roles have been, Creative Director, Senior Animator, 3D Animation Supervisor, Senior Technical Artist.

Alan’s specialties include lighting, rendering, visual effects, and prototyping the look and feel for cinematics. An excellent 3D generalist Alan's additional skills include modeling, rigging, dynamics, texturing and animation. He is passionate, a dedicated collaborator, and a superb trainer. Many of Alan’s graduates have gone on to studios such as Pixar, Double Negative, Sony Pictures, and Weta Digital.
