Maricel Del Sol’s Summary

Maricel Del Sol joined Edelman in March 2007 as an Account Executive based out of the Miami office. In her time with the company, she has worked on Oracle Corporation, the world's largest enterprise software company, acting as a writer and coordinator for Oracle’s Reference Program in Latin America. Her job quality and reliability with the customer granted her extended responsibilities in July 2009, when she was asked to also act as an advisor for account executives in Edelman Brazil throughout the development of Oracle’s Reference Program in Brazil.

Before Edelman, Maricel worked as a copy writer for Bromley Communications were she was primarily servicing accounts such as BellSouth and Burger King. Maricel’s professional experience includes launching several publications as part of prestigious publishing houses such as Editorial Televisa and Condé Nast Latin America. Maricel graduated from Kean University, New Jersey with a bachelor’s degree in Visual Communications, combined with International Studies at University of Brighton, England and Language studies in Sorbornne Universite in Paris, France.

Outside of Edelman, Maricel strives for the perfect life-work balanced and her passion for volunteering, writing and traveling. She treasures her volunteer projects and working experiences in several countries, such as England, France, Mexico,Venezuela, Ecuador, and the Dominican Republic. Maricel’s motto is “don’t count the days make the days count by touching a life”.
