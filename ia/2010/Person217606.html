Catherine Boskovic’s Summary

I've always worked with people in all different settings being involved in a committee or organization to serving peoples needs in the professional environment as well as on a personal level.

I have been a yoga enthusiast since 1994 and have been studying at various studios throughout the Chicago land area.

After several years of my extraordinary experiences at the Temple of Kriya Yoga, I’ve discovered that my yoga hobby had become a passion. I’ve decided to share the innumerable benefits that I have received from my yoga experience with others by teaching practical methods of Hatha Yoga.

As I worked in the corporate environment for years of long hours sitting at a desk, I recognized that our minds and nearly everything in our world, is frenetically moving forward.

I realized to obtain balance and health, our bodies need the opportunity, the time, and the commitment to rest and reverse the effects of stress.

Catherine Boskovic’s Specialties:

Hatha Yoga Instruction for groups large or small, as well as one- on-one private instruction. Corporate yoga specializing yoga for the computer user. Relaxation and meditation techniques for stress relief. Specializing in yoga for the Visually Impaired, Physically challenged, Seniors, and Children.
