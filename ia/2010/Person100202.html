John O'Brien III’s Summary

By day, Mr. John O’Brien acts as Special Assistant to the US Department of Defense (DoD) Business Transformation Agency's (BTA) Director focused on the delivery of high profile IT / ERP projects in an effort to improve DoD business operations in the areas of finance and procurement.

At night, he focuses on various web start-up ventures including Twangu (a group buying, reverse auction application on Facebook), Twapper Keeper (a Twitter archiving service), 10 Rounds of Battle (a game application on Facebook), and Call-My-W&ll (a Facebook / Twilio telephony mashup that allows Facebook users to call in voice status updates to their Newsfeed / Wall).

Extensive hands-on knowledge and experience in:
- Commercial business applications (ERP, PLM, CAD/CAM, etc)
- Web 2.0 / social networking applications and platforms (Facebook, Twitter)
- Infrastructure / server hosting / networking
- LAMP development
- RESTful web service integrations (e-commerce [Paypal / Amazon Payments], telephony [Twilio], imaging [ShrinktheWeb], mapping [Google], etc.)
- Online marketing on social platforms (acquisition, retention, viral loops)

--------------

12 years experience transforming the way Private Sector and Federal Government organizations do business by architecting, developing, and deploying web-based applications and capabilities.

Focused on pushing the limits of technology, aggressively meeting the needs of the business / customers, containing cost, and meeting schedules.

Industry expertise spans high tech, automotive, heavy equipment, discreet manufacturing, life sciences, aerospace & defense, government, and consumer goods.

Functional business expertise in engineering, manufacturing, procurement, supply chain, and personnel management.
