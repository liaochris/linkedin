A TOGAF and ZapThink certified Architect with broad understanding of industry and commerce.

Task-oriented, organised and adept at communicating across business and technical lines using skills acquired from practical experience in operations, development and strategic planning.

Motivated to deliver quality results in many roles including:

• Project manager of customer-facing application development.

Excellent oral and written communication skills (e.g., guest panellist at Gartner conference) backed up with proficient use of Microsoft tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio, Project, etc.).

Active in business and process transformation through collaboration with senior leaders and external consultants (including McKinsey, Deloitte).
