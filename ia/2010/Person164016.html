  • Owner/ Consultant

    Building Measures

    (Facilities Services industry)

    January 1991 — Present (19 years 9 months)

    Formerly known as Wise Solutions.

    BUILDING MEASURES specializes in accurate, field verified, as-built drawings and reports. We have over 19 years experience creating facility floor plans and space reporting, working with virtually every type of building in a variety of industries.

    SERVICES
    Asbuilt Floorplans
    * CAD Drafting – We offer a variety of basic CAD drafting services.
    * Field verification – To insure accuracy we can field verify your facility and insure the accuracy of your floor plans.
    * Paper to CAD Conversion – Paper drawings are inefficient and at risk for loss. We can convert those old paper drawings to industry standard CAD format.

    BOMA/IFMA
    * Standard Space Plans and Reports
    Maximize revenue with accurate property information utilizing accepted methods.
    * The BOMA Office and Industrial Standard and IFMA Standard Practice are the most common guidelines for measuring leased office space and industrial buildings in the United States. We accurately measure your facility using state of the art laser technology and create a CAD floor plan. We then create a complete report showing a breakdown of your facility.

    Other Space Standards
    * There are many situations where no measurement standard is appropriate. In these circumstances, we work with our clients to implement a strategy that satisfies their unique requirements.

    CAFM
    We can create CAFM Ready Floorplans for you. We create accurate drawings ready for you to catalog into your CAFM system.

    If you don't currently have a CAFM system, it is still possible to generate CAFM reports using only AutoCAD and your favorite Office software suite. We can create custom:

    * Space Utilization Reports – Identify the ways your space is being used.
    * Space Allocation Reports – Identify boundaries of your cost centers or departments.
    * Department Charge Back – Customized reports to charge departments for the space they occupy.
