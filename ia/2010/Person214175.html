Shirley Sexton’s Summary

Shirley Sexton is an Internet strategist, marketer and fundraiser specializing in nonprofits. Sexton has over 20 years of direct response and Internet marketing experience.

Sexton founded the interactive marketing group at Easter Seals, the nation's 8th largest charity, and developed their online infrastructure of over 200 Web sites used for fundraising, event registration, marketing, and staff development. Sexton established the Easter Seals Online Fundraising Program, resulting in over $1.5 million in income for the organization in 2008. Sexton led Easter Seals' Internet strategies, development, implementation and integration of organization-wide online initiatives.

In her previous role as director of nonprofit services at Commerce One, she provided Internet strategy leadership to a number of large nonprofits, and supervised the creation of www.afpnet.org, www.pewtrusts.com, www.safekids.org and many more nonprofit Web sites.

Sexton is a frequent speaker at national conferences on Internet strategy and interactive marketing for nonprofits. She has served on the board of the ePhilanthropy Foundation, the Convio Advisory Board and the Network for Good Advisory Board.

Sexton lives in Chicago, IL with her husband Jake.
