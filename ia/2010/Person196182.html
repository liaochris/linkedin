Steve Wood’s Summary

Scalar Enterprises is owned and run by Steve Wood and is based in Southampton in Hampshire on the south coast of the UK, offering a range of services including web site design, search engine optimisation and internet marketing to small businesses and start-ups in Hampshire and the surrounding areas.

I designed my first web site more than 10 years ago for Toshiba and have done many more since then. These days I design them to meet the latest World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) standards, using XHTML, and CSS layouts and optimised for search engines (SEO) to make best use of the websites for e-marketing and really make them work for your business.

I offer a complete service to businesses to help you get up and running and establish a web presence or improve / redesign your existing web sites to get more out of them. I can also provide help and advice with defining overall marketing strategies to work with your web site.

I am committed to providing a first class, reliable service. My aim is always to build customer relationships for the long term.

I aim to provide a friendly, personal and professional approach, including face to face discussions, advice and suggestions, cutting through the jargon, talking to clients and ensure that I clearly understand your requirements so that I can deliver exactly what you want, not what I think you want.

I have many years experience working with businesses, developing customer relationships, requirements and providing customer support pre and post sales along with project management to meet tight timescales and budgets.

My objective is to give you a total solution that fits your needs, without any unexpected unnecessary extras at an affordable price.

I always favour an open and honest approach and I will never tell you that I can do something if I can’t.

See my website for more detail at http://www.scalarenterprises.co.uk
