Rachel Karl’s Summary

Rachel Karl has over a decade of experience in conceptualizing, developing, and executing effective copy for corporate, non-profit, and small business sectors.

Her expertise as a copywriter spans a broad spectrum - from Emails (short and long copy), Landing Page Copy and Sales Letters, to Web Content (optimized for search engines), Press Releases and Social Media Marketing Campaigns.

She has the ability to see to the core of who her client is and what they stand for to create impactful marketing and PR campaigns that get results.


* * *

After graduating from her Alma Mater, with a major in Theater and Fine Arts, Rachel spent the next four years immersed in an intense scholastic theology program focusing her studies on philosophy, religion and humanism.

She currently resides in the Portland, Oregon area where she runs her copywriting business.
