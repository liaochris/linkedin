Douglas Wolfe’s Summary

My name is Douglas Wolfe; I am 50 years old & have been married for over 28 yrs. We have 5 children, and 12 grandchildren. I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. We moved out of the city in 2000, hoping to give our children a less congested, less corrupted & safer environment. We still live within commuting distance to Dallas.

I started working with computers in 1979, with a Commodore 64. By 1980 I was accessing Bulletin Boards & writing simple code for my Commodores. I have extensive experience in many internet related programs, languages, applications & systems over the last 13 plus years. My work has caused me to study & continue to learn many new things & keep up with new developments in the areas that I work. I am a consultant & contractor in many internet fields including: advertising, marketing, web design, web development, network/database administration, SEO, Affiliate Programs & Graphics Design. My specialty is upgrading & modernizing business systems to take full advantage of the time & cost saving benefits of today’s applications, programs & technologies.

I believe my commitment to excellence & my dedication to customer satisfaction help me to achieve the superior results needed by the worlds emerging enterprises seeking to use the internet to increase their efficiency, productivity, market reach, name recognition & profit margins. Let me show you how internet systems can save you from 10% up to 40% on your labor, general office, CRM, & communications expenses. While this is a great benefit to any company today, it is only the beginning of what I can do for you with today's technology and the Internets reach.

I have been selected as an honored member of the Biltmore Who’s Who of Internet Professionals in the 2007, 2008 & 2009. I am greatly honored & humbled by this recognition of my activities and abilities.
