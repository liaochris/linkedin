Andrea Sercu’s Summary

An award-winning creative marketing communications manager, copywriter and editor with 20 years' experience, Andrea Sercu specializes in the professional beauty, health and wellness industry. Bylines have appeared in the Los Angeles Times, DAYSPA, MedEsthetics, GreatLife and Angeleno magazines as well as industry websites, such as behindthechair.com. Andrea has played an integral role in driving the marketing direction for Procter & Gamble Professional, Joico Labs and G.S. Cosmeceutical USA and has gained significant brand awareness for manufacturers including Jan Marini Skin Research and P&G's personal care brands Wella, Sebastian, Clairol Professional and Trucco Cosmetics.

Andrea Sercu’s Specialties:

Professional and consumer marketing communications in the cosmetics, personal care and health/wellness fields.
Well-versed in both creative and technical copy, advertising/PR, collateral development and sales.
