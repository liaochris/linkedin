Rony Camille’s Summary

Rony is a media specialist based in Southern New Hampshire with experience in web editorial and P.E.G. access, public administration, public relations and social media.

He earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in English and Mass Communication from N.C. Central University in Durham, NC in 2007.

Prior to joining The Town of Tyngsborough, Rony worked with the Medical Unit at ABC News as a freelance production associate and video editor. Prior to ABC News Rony worked as a web producer at WHDH-TV Boston and as a General .Assignment Reporter for The Cabinet Press in Milford, NH
