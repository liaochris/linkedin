Parker McCauley’s Summary

Over 25 years experience in the design, development, deployment and testing of complex electronic systems. I have designed analog and digital hardware for sonar systems in the defense industry, designed hardware and written embedded software for turbidity and suspended solids measurement in the water and waste water treatment industries, infused companies with advanced technology, and significantly expanded their technical expertise in the process.

I have spent the last 15 years supporting and promoting automation of the electrical utility industry, contributing to open standards, interoperability, efficiency and security.

My goals are to apply my unique background and talents to bringing the smart grid into being. By sharing my knowledge and helping the industry integrate generation, transmisson and distributon of precious resources in efficent, secure, and innovative ways I plan to make a positive contribution to the lives of future generations.

Parker McCauley’s Specialties:

System Architecture and Design, Understanding the Big Picture and envisioning innovative solutions, C++ programmig (from embedded systems to Graphical User Interfaces), International expert on DNP3 prototocol, cyber security and smart grid solutions, developing software tools (utilities, configuration, visualization, test sets, protocol monitors, etc). Familiar with the usual set of hardware and software tools including surface mount technology, sytem hardening, XML/XSD/XSLT, HTML, JAVA, etc
