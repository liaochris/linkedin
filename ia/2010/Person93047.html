Jeff Clark’s Summary

Jeff’s unique background spans both large media companies (Yahoo! & CBSi) and successful early stage startups (Dotspotter). In his current role as VP of Entertainment at CBS Interactive, Jeff runs CBS.com and the CBS video distribution network also know as CAN (CBS Audience Network). Prior to CBS.com Jeff ran TV.com and The Insider for CBS Interactive.

Prior to CBSi, Jeff was a co-founder of Dotspotter, an early social media platform which was acquired by CBS a year after launch in September, 2007. Before Dotspotter, Jeff spent 15 years in a number of sales management and business development roles including the prior 5 years at Yahoo! as Director of Sales and Director of Business Development. Jeff has also held various sales and sales management roles at companies including Intraware, Genuity, and Inacom. Jeff received his B.A. from the University of California at Santa Barbara.
