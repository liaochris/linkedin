John Forge’s Summary

Accomplished leader with record of success building high-growth marketing and go-to-market strategies, and overseeing major product development projects. Provides operations leadership to heighten top-line growth and bottom-line results. Adept in team building, accelerating business development, forming international partner alliances (Europe, Middle East, Africa) to open new markets.

• Founded the first European market research organization for the software package industry (CXP, now part of the Forrester Group.) Founded the first US base for French software distribution (U3S, now part of Thomson-Syseca group).
• Executive experience working with large multinational telecom organizations (Sita-Equant, Bouygues Telecom), and eCommerce Group (Louis Vuitton)
• Successful experience with small US based startups: ExperTelligence in artificial intelligence, Boma Systems in wireless, HSDC in claim management.
• Management of complex software/telecom projects (Sita-Equant, HSDC).
• Designed and managed innovative marketing campaigns for artificial intelligence, software/telecom products, and SaaS (Software as a Service) solutions. Credited with inventing the word “progiciel”, officially added to the French language to translate “software package”.

  • Founder

    CXP

    (Computer Software industry)

    February 1975 — April 1981 (6 years 3 months)

    CXP (Center for Expertise on Progiciels) was the first market and product research organization for the software package industry in Europe.
    - Developed the methodology to evaluate software packages and developed a series of seminars for IT executives.
    - Built the first extensive database of software packages available on the European market.
    - CXP grew to include 80% of all French companies using large computers. It employed 50 people with an $8M in 1980. CXP is now part of Forrester Research.
    - Led technical missions in Europe (Spain, the Netherlands, Romania), Africa (Gabon, Ivory Coast), Middle East (Tunisia, Morocco), and Asia (Malaysia).
    - The organization became so well known that the word “progiciel” was officially added to the French dictionary to translate “software package” for French speaking countries.
