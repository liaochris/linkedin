INFO ABOUT THE ALBUM:
In a consumer and corporate driven, competitive market, very few records are being produced to cater the needs of the audience who seek the rediscovery of inner harmony and the most basic human emotions through music. Egry's Sentimental Piano Concert was created to fill the gap and supply this "silenced” but massive niche market with a product of substantial value and quality.

Sentimental Piano Concert is classical music but it is also an album that is capable of communicating values of tradition and excellence through the language of music to a wide range of audience. The target audience, which is composed of individuals of the modern era possessing a refined musical taste, will be equally compelled by the album regardless whether they are or are not familiar with the musical form of a multi-movement piano concerto.
