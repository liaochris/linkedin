Ed Rodriguez’s Summary

Strong leader with proven ability to think broadly and deeply, simplify complex issues and situations for decision-making, build and lead cross-functional teams, develop and grow new lines of business, and get things done; highly focused on relationships and results.

Sourcing financing for: 1) emerging domestic markets-focused SBIC and non-SBIC funds; 2) green industries and alternative energy funds; and 3) innovative socially-responsible/green mortgage financing, disaster-resistant/green construction, and environmentally/economically sustainable real estate development business.

20+ years experience in the public (federal and state levels), private (government relations, management consulting, medical technology, information technology and telecommunications industries), and not-for-profit (think tanks and chambers of commerce) sectors as well as politics and military service. International experience in Europe, Asia and Latin America. Fluent in English, proficient in Spanish, beginner in Portuguese.
