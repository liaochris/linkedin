Terry Crosby’s Summary

You can’t wait another day to create and/or improve your presence on the web because. . .

1. your competitor is already on web
2. that is where you will find your current and potential customers
3. your customers no longer trusts traditional advertising
4. your customers rely on the opinions of their friends on the social sites
5. social media marketing is free

Social media is the most effective and economical vehicle to attract all the customers you want. However, results only come to those that create a strategy. I can help you develop, implement and maintain a social media campaign that will succeed.

I will. . .

Assist in defining your goal (examples are social popularity, lead generation, building and/or strengthening your brand, find out what you customer wants/needs)
Develop social media strategy
a. strategy will be unique to you and your business
b. Identify 4 social sites important for your business to join
d. Define purpose and goal of each site
e. Create activity list for each site
Initial implementation of strategy
a. Open accounts on social sites
b. Create an "attraction" profile
c. Train you and/or your staff how to effectively participate in the "conversation"
d. Train you and/or your staff how to find and "friend" your target customer
e. Train you and/or your staff how to find and "friend" your industries "influencers"
e. Train you and/or your staff how to generate leads (list building)
Listen
a. Train you and/or your staff how to listen to what people are saying about you and/or your business
b. Train you and/or your staff how to listen to what people want/need
c. Train you and/or your staff how to listen to what people are saying about your competitors
Evaluate
a. Train you and/or your staff how to measure efforts
