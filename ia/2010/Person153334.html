OUR PAINTING PASSION

At New Hue, our teams produce work with a high level of skill, thoughtful attention to detail, and genuine personal pride, all the while having a good time!.

We use only high-quality and eco-friendly paints, at an affordable price; and we happily provide consultations of general color theory to all of our clients. Our loyal customers live throughout the metro area, and their satisfaction and evaluation of our work is a critical component to our success.


OUR TRIPLE-BOTTOM-LINE VISION:
people, planet, and profit

Breaking with conventional business models (profit-only focus), we believe in creative solutions to synergize financial, societal, and environmental gains. This is why we give 10% of our profits to local charities, as well as provide volunteer projects for local schools, shelters, non-profits, and community centers.
