Christine Kalmbach’s Summary

Currently leading a fantastic team of sales professionals in delivering Agile Project Management and Software Development talent into our client accounts based throughout the Puget Sound metropolitan area. SolutionsIQ has over 30 years history as a Seattle based technical staffing firm.

As an Agile company we practice what we preach. We have applied Lean Office and Agile principles to our recruiting supply chain operations. The result is extremely rapid fulfillment that is continuously fine-tuned through client feedback & collaboration.

We offer our clients complete IT System Lifecycle Support.

SolutionsIQ consultants expertise is spread across the software development spectrum:

Agile & Scrum development
Operations support
Project and program management
Business and systems analysis
Software engineering
Business application development
Quality assurance
Usability and user interface design

My background includes Sales and Management leadership in the Staffing/Recruiting industry since 1989. For the past eight years, lead the HealthcareIT division for a fast-growing, privately held recruiting and placement firm. Part-owner and Vice President of Parker Staffing Services, LLC.
