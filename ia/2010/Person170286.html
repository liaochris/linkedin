Rebecca Violette’s Summary

Rebecca’s experience in the financial services business community made for a seamless transition into the real estate profession. Rebecca has worked for 25 years in the financial services industry in Silicon Valley; she is a widely regarded expert in financial planning, as an investment specialist and life insurance specialist. Rebecca currently has a California real estate license and California investment and insurance licenses. She has been active on the Board of Directors for the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors in Silicon Valley as the V.P. of Finance. She joined Intero in 2009. Rebecca has worked diligently over the years to develop and maintain working relationships. Trust and honesty are essential.
