Imran Syed’s Summary

Over the years I have accumulated a wealth of experience that has transformed the way I approach business management and marketing. I hold a Bachelor of Commerce degree from Ryerson University and a Marketing Accreditation from Sheridan College.

Currently I am a Marketing Manager for one of the largest software companies in North America within the Sage Software Channel. Previous positions include working directly for the Senior Vice President of Ontario Power Generation, Darlington Nuclear as an Internal Communication Specialist for the Executive Office, where my key focus was on improving employee engagement and communication within the organization. Prior to Ontario Power Generation I worked for Walt Disney Parks and Resorts. Here I worked with the Publicity and Public Relations department and was an integral part of the strategy and visibility for the organization within the Canadian market.
