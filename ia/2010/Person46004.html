Dale Donovan’s Summary

Dale L. Donovan, Principal and Vice President Land Services, specializes in large scale land acquisitions and sales, corporate service assignments, retail, industrial, life sciences and biotech industry site selection, market analysis, sales, leasing, due diligence services and consulting.

Client services include positioning, managing, and marketing key properties to and for commercial and major community developers. Her expertise also includes site selection for boutique builders along with tenant/buyer representation.

Dale Donovan’s Specialties:

Ms. Donovan’s broad range of experience in commercial real estate brokerage, business development, corporate services, and consulting spans over 25 years. Prior to joining NAI Realvest in 2006, Ms. Donovan was president/owner of DLD Commercial Realty, Inc., a full service brokerage firm, founded in 1994 in Boca Raton, Florida with branch offices in Stuart and Winter Haven, Florida. Ms. Donovan relocated from South Florida to Orlando and opened DLD Commercial in Downtown Orlando in 2001.
