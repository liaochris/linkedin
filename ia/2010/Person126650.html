Rachael King’s Summary

I am currently the Events Co-Chair for the Social Media Club (DC Chapter), a community that provides people in the Greater Washington DC metropolitan area with opportunities to explore and share ideas and information about Social Media. Responsibilities include planning, executing, and marketing of networking socials, charity fundraisers, and other events on behalf of SMCDC.

I am also the Events Administrator for 20 Something Bloggers, a 15,000+ strong community for, well, 20 Something Bloggers. Members participate in regular Blog Swaps, Blog Carnivals, Debates, and Vlog Days, as well as gather for local and nationwide meetups, which I am responsible for coordinating with a team across the country. We are currently assembling the first ever 20SB Summit, planned for Summer 2011.

As an active member of the DC blogging community, I am a Contributing Editor at DCBlogs.com, which entails a weekly round up of the most original/interesting/thought provoking, and otherwise important posts from local bloggers. I also help to manage and promote the @DCBlogs twitter account, as well as organizing monthly informal happy hours and networking tweetups for local DC bloggers and social media folk alike.

I've recently begun to take on social media campaigns for local restaurants, managing their Twitter and Facebook accounts to promote specials, increase business, and for general marketing purposes.

Over the past year, I have taught myself the ins and outs of Blogger, Wordpress.com, and subsequently, self-hosting and Wordpress.org, as well as designed 20+ blogs and websites by referral using the Thesis theme. My portfolio can be seen at http://livitluvit.com/portfolio.

Finally, I am an administrative assistant at the International Monetary Fund- an extremely fast-paced and high pressure working environment, which requires extreme organization skills and attention to detail.
