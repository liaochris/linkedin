Ron Hutcheson’s Summary

Ron Hutcheson, a small business growth catalyst, has always had a passion for helping people financially. From 1996-2004, while building his first financial service company in New Jersey, he helped develop and train over 300 sales representatives that educated 10,000+ families in saving for retirement, college education, debt reduction and adequate life insurance. In 2005, he was the Sales Training and Development Director for one of the largest privately owned alternative funding companies in the U.S. In 2006, Ron launched Smart Funding Solutions.

Ron knows the challenges of growing a small business. It takes financing and the know how. Knowing how to fix a car or having a great recipe are building blocks to starting a business, but managing and growing a business is a whole other thing. Many small businesses have the capacity to grow, but lack the working capital and strategy. That's where Consultant Financing comes in. Consultant Financing pairs proven, top-tier business consultants with small business owner to help them grow their business.

Small businesses are bloodline of a community's economic base. 80% of Americans are employed by small to midsized business, so when small businesses fail, the community fails. Employees lose jobs, families are not able to pay their mortgage, fund retirement or even continue their health insurance. The loss of small businesses decreases the amount of money being invested back into the community. Consultant Financing comes from Ron's passion for helping small business owners and entrepreneurs and the communities they work and live in.
