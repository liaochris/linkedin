Tom Drews’s Summary

We deliver consulting, training and speaking programs on a variety of topics, including the following:

Virtual Presentation and Webinar Skills
Presentation Skills
Sales Presentation Skills
Sales Skills
Time Management

We have delivered our programs to all kinds of companies, including Google, Symantec, Oracle, WebEx, Riverbed, CLIF Bar, McKesson and Cisco.

Tom Drews, the CEO and Founder of What Works! Communications, previously served as Vice President of Sales for Zoom Systems, a well funded e-commerce company (www.zoomsystems.com). He was also Vice President of Sales for a CRM company, selling software to major corporations across the United States.

Tom also founded Active Stick Wear, which manufactured and distributed active wear to 87 department store chains including Macy's, Penny's, Sears, Kids R Us, Footlocker and Wal-Mart.

He has developed entertaining and engaging programs as a result of spending time in Hollywood as an actor, where he studied improvisation, acted in commercials, and played small parts on soap operas including Days of Our Lives and General Hospital. Tom's real claim to fame was playing Bachelor #1 on the Dating Game.

For more information, visit www.whatworks.biz, email us at tom@whatworks.biz, or call us at 415-674-1373.
