Amy McIlwain’s Summary

SOCIAL MEDIA CONSULTING
Amy McIlwain is CEO and founder of Financial Social Media Marketing, a social media marketing consulting firm and internet marketing company specializing in the financial and insurance industry.

Previously Amy worked for top B2B insurance magazine and brand, Senior Market Advisor, where she played an integral role in the marketing campaigns of top insurance agencies, carriers, and IMO's nationwide.

With extensive experience in both Brand & Identity along with Direct Response Marketing Amy is able to work with clients in a consultative role to create custom marketing plans that will enable clients to achieve their goals in the most cost effective manner through a mix of various advertising media.

SOCIAL MEDIA CONTENT CREATION
One of the biggest challenges of social media marketing is content creation. Financial Social Media Marketing offers a monthly customizable content package including articles for your blog, LinkedIn and Facebook, twitter posts, and a custom enewsletter. To learn more please visit the website.
