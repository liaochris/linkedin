Scott Velicer’s Summary

Data driven Product Manager with deep User Experience, Platforms and Partner Integration experience. I strive to continually innovate and create new products that excite users, are easy and fun to use, and push the boundaries of technology.

Currently Product Manager for several Location Data and Advertising products at 1020 Placecast. Placecast is a location technology company that enables application developers and content providers to translate location data across any location based service and match it to the right "place." Placecast's continuously updated location content management platform is licensed by Fortune 500 companies as well as available to Location Content Providers and Application Developers as part of MatchAPI.

Before joining 1020, I consulted for a range of Fortune 500 companies, helping to define and implement technology based solutions for a wide range of clients including Cisco Systems, Symantec, EMC, MGM Studios, Arizona Department of Revenue, and several others.
