Martin Ackerman’s Summary

Career Profile

I am the owner of Signs By Tomorrow in Boise, a locally owned and operated digital graphic design and sign making business. We specialize in all types of interior and exterior signage including banners, real estate, retail, vehicle graphics, and event signs. We also provide expert design consulting and digital imaging services. Our signs are made using the latest in digital imaging techniques and equipment. Signs By Tomorrow in Boise is part of the Signs By Tomorrow franchise network with over 180 locations nationwide. We want to become your sign partner and help you grow your business.

Signs By Tomorrow in Boise is located at 3413 N. Cole Road (Cole and Ustick) in the Cole Village Shopping Center. In addition to visiting our store, you can contact us by phone (208-345-9900), fax (208-345-9915), email (boise@signsbytomorrow.com), or through our web site (signsbytomorrow.com/boise).

Prior to starting Signs By Tomorrow in Boise, I had twenty-seven years of experience in Research and Development, Manufacturing, and Technical Marketing at Hewlett-Packard Co.. I managed and participated in software, firmware, and hardware projects on some of Hewlett-Packard's largest selling products. I have extensive experience in managing software development, vendor relationships, and leading both cross-organizational and cross-functional teams. My experience includes providing leadership in all phases of product definition and development. I achieved success by establishing and fostering challenging and productive work environments based on teamwork, customer focus, time-to-market, and technical excellence through the use of strong people management and mentoring skills. I am a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) through the Project Management Institute (pmi.org).
