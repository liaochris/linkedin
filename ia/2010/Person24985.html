ABOUT DAMIEN REILLY
Dip.Fin. 1987 , Member Institute of Export , APRA Full Writer Member 1997
Member of AIR , Member of ARIA

His diverse and far reaching global networks in technology provide Blue Pie with a market leading edge in web based communication systems and digital entertainment networks. The practical application and integration of web based communication systems into business are his specialist skill. This provides significant market leverage to the Blue Pie team. Damien is a sales focussed CEO. Under his leadership Blue Pie now has offices in 6 countries and growing.

Damien has over 25 years of I.T experience. He is an accomplished musician, composer and producer with numerous international recording credits to his name and Top 40 chart success. Blue Pie continues to receive global recognition and its artist and labels are now charting in over 10 countries and growing.

Blue Pie and the team are working on more International music projects and have their eye on the commercial chart success for 2010 in the USA, Canada, UK and Australia.

Damien continues to study eastern philosophy with past times including Tae Kwon Do, Music, Family, Charity work, and playing live music.
