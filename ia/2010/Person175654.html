Robin Marshall’s Summary

I've been on the air, in radio for 25 years. Maybe I should rephrase that... I've SURVIVED in radio for 25 years! My dad was on the air at KDKA, as a talk show host and is probably the only radio person I've ever known to keep his job at one station for 25 years. Amazing!!!
Most of my abilities are in writing, voicing and producing for radio and TV. I image stations nationwide and internationally as well. I have a show called "Jayne." So far, it's on the air in Canada, LIttle Rock, Princeton,Syracuse and it's about to air in St Croix! There's a lot of interest in the benchmark piece called "Cuppa Jayne!" If you'd like to hear a sample please go to www.JayneFM.com.

I just recently did a campaign of spots for P Diddy's fragrance, 'Unforgivable,' and would like to expand upon that end of my career as well.

Robin Marshall’s Specialties:

Writing and voice acting are my specialties. Also, listening. Not too many people have the patience these days...
