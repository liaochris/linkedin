Kevin Beatty’s Summary

Currently Kevin serves as the Business Development Sales Account Executive at ActiveSports, a division of Active.com, the largest service provider of web-based technologies and marketing solutions for sports, events, and community-based organizations. The company is also named among the fastest growing technology companies in North America (Source: Deloitte & Touche Survey) and recognized as an Inc. 500 company. By developing sales promotions, augmenting strategic partnerships, and creating new positioning strategies, Kevin has helped ActiveSports become the #1 technology solution in several sports markets. Specializing in larger sporting clients he successfully develops long-term business growth strategies utilizing web technology, social media interactive campaigns and creative marketing programs.

Also known for his entrepreneurial skills, Kevin consults with several online technology companies and interactive promotional firms to build brand awareness.

Prior to that, Kevin brings over 8 years of top-performing sales and business development experience, landing sizable Fortune 100 corporate contracts such as Alcoa, and Heinz, and Fortune 500 corporations Wesco International, and Calgon Carbon Corporation. Kevin also has a strong background in creating effective corporate sales training programs for several start-up organizations. He’s been responsible for increasing employee retention, development of advanced sales technique workshops, and management consulting for both consumer and business markets.
