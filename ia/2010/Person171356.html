Chip Cameron’s Summary

Cameron Resources Group LLC [CRG] provides a full range of engineering, technical, and program support services. As an 8(a) certified, Small Disadvantaged business located in Orlando, Florida, we offer both engineering support and personnel services. Our support services include on-site & off-site requirements analysis, design, build, testing, program and project management. Our engineering staffing services include permanent placement, contract support, and project staffing for a variety for engineering and R&D disciplines.
