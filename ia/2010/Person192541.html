Stacey Alexander’s Summary

Stacey Alexander's passion lies at the cross-roads of business & technology strategy. Her business acumen and experience deliver revenue through strategically integrated channels. She's been responsible for business development activities in limited resource start-ups and has proven results in managing complex global sales in Fortune 500 organizations. Stacey has high energy and high integrity for exceeding internal and client-facing service levels. For this, she's valued for growing relevant and measurable value for the brand and for clients.

Stacey's been a featured speaker sharing her low/no budget actionable advice for small and medium sized technology providers working with IT industry analysts. She also plans and executes monthly association meetings for the Society for Information Management. In 2009 Stacey helped plan and execute the SIM - Youth in IT event held at the Leed's School of Business.
