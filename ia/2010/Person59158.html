Doug Pratt’s Summary

My job has me looking for talented, technical people who are interested in jobs in the Chicago area.

Prior to becoming a recruiter, I focused my career on various aspects of doing business on the Internet. these include: website design and creation, managing a team of webmasters, developing an eCommerce strategy for a Fortune 100 company, helping several schools and small businesses learn how to use the Internet successfully and, most recently, using Web Analytics to build a sensible continuous improvement model for Internet businesses.

I began my career teaching Mathematics and as an administrator for several small schools. I was always the Math teacher who built a computer lab as part of my role. I left education to work in industry and spent several years programming, doing system administration, IT management and corporate planning.
