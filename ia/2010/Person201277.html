Terry Griffin’s Summary

Hi! I'm a 14-year veteran of the Memphis graphic design scene; having worked as a editorial, information, and web design; in the Publishing, Legal Services, and Retail industries, respectively.

I'm currently seeking new employment as a graphic/web designer or art/creative director--as well as business opportunities for my freelance practice, Terry Griffin Design.

I also enjoy consulting with entrepreneurs and small businesses on how to improve their marketing; as well as opportunities to use networking and social media to connect with other professionals and businesspeople.

Terry Griffin’s Specialties:

Design for identity, marketing, publishing, information, and web. Management of design departments; including creative personnel, project coordination, production oversight, and client/vendor relations.
