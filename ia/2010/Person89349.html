Janet Tracy Keijser’s Summary

Independent film is all about me.

Penned the tongue-in-check article "FX for Guerrilla Film Making". Cannes issue of Independent Film Quarterly.

Two scripts finished recently. "The Boys Club" & "Monkey Bait". Both are thriller/horrors based on very topical issues. I wrote them with low budget in mind. Locations are minimal, as are effects. Stories are original and extremely current.

My motto: Life can be a horror/thriller, so just tell the story good. The rest takes care of itself.
"The Boys Club" What would Jack & Karen do in Deliverance?
"Monkey Bait" What comes first; hate or drugs?

Have a long list of credits in the indie film market and am well known in that genre. I have 3 feature films coming out in 2010. Am very versatile and known to be a low maintenance kinda actor who does her own stunts. No trailer needed. Am SAG - Fi Core.

Looking to finance my scripts. Anyone with a Red Camera who wants to swing a deal.
And of course, always open for work as an actress.
Check my website for updates on movie screenings.
Live on stage soon.
An evening with the Janice Kennedy, the original short plays of an up and coming, influential, new playwright.
Janet stars in "Blink" & "The Dark" on stage in Hollywood. Dates TBD. Limited engagement in February.
