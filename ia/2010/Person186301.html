Justin Jung - SEM SEO Expert’s Summary

SEM Expertise, Managing Director and Founder, Justin Jung is one of the most knowledgeable and experience Search Engine Marketing (SEO & PPC) experts, consultants and Internet entrepreneurs in the world & he has been in the game since 2001.

Justin’s job is to make sure that companies target the right audience to drive qualified traffic to their website as well as increasing their online ROI with the following Search Engine Marketing (SEM) strategies.

1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) - he optimizes websites search engine friendly so that search engines will rank them higher as well as increasing qualified traffic.

2. Pay per Click Advertising (PPC) - he builds and manages 100% measurable PPC campaigns that send qualified leads to the website.

3. Social Media - he develops a Social Media strategy that will help clients achieve their goals (reputation, sales, influence, credibility, charity, branding, traffic, etc.).

4. Local Search Optimization - he promotes local businesses so that they can reach local customers while they seek local information, services and products.

5. Web Analytics - he measures and analyzes the results of online campaigns through using Web Analytics in order to maximize the effectiveness of the online campaigns (PPC, SEO, Social Media, local search, emails, banners, etc.).
