Basil Harris, Jr.’s Summary

Senior marketing and product management professional with extensive experience defining and launching new and next-version software product and services offerings for a range of companies - from venture-backed startups up through global systems and services companies.

Strong and varied technology grounding that includes database and date warehousing, enterprise software (CRM, ERP, BPM, Linux and Open Source), Web-based and mobility applications and tools, and business intelligence products. Known for building strong relationships with sales, engineering, and support organizations to define and deliver high-value solutions to customers.
