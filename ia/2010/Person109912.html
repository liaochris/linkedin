Kaiser Fernandez’s Summary

Technology Consultant at Hewlett Packard Asia Pacific, Ltd - SAP ASIA Finance Systems. Experienced in SAP FI/CO with specialization in Costing and Profitability Analysis.

Graduated Honorable Mention at De La Salle University Manila ranking 2nd in his course in the September 2008 batch with a degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering.

Recently passed the Electronics Engineering Board Examinations conducted by the Professional Regulations Commission and landed 5th place in the topnotcher's list in March 2009.

Graduated valedictorian at Marikina Catholic School in 2000, then went to Philippine Science High School Diliman with full academic scholarship for four years before entering DLSU with a degree in engineering in 2004.

A merit scholar of the Department of Science and Technology from 2000 to 2004 and a financial assistance grantee and scholar of DLSU.

Currently a member MENSA Philippines and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers.

Highly involved in values education programs and has headed a number of social outreach projects through the Maynilad Study Center.

Selected to be the Electrical Student Team Leader of the First Philippine Solar Car. Also selected to be part of the team which competed in the World Solar Challenge in Australia in 2007.

Interests lean towards telecommunications, information technology and the energy industry.

Free schedules are usually spent in blogging, reading tech related articles/news, playing soccer, basketball and other sports.
