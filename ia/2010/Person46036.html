Dale Anderson’s Summary

Crafting sensible and successful technology solutions is my obsession! I am passionate about delivering results, and deliberate about doing it right. My extensive industry knowledge, experience, and skill set are anything but ordinary.

My key areas of expertise include:
> Database & Data Warehouse design, development, optimization, & migration
> Business Intelligence, data analytics, metrics, reporting, dashboards, & DSS
> eCommerce, Client/Server, N-Tier, Internet, & Cloud Computing (SaaS/SOA) applications
> Process Life Cycle (SDLC/DDLC) & Engineering / Project Management

My primary skill set includes:
> MS SQL Server, Oracle, & MySQL
> C#, .NET, ADO, SQL, ASP
> LAMP stack

The results from having worked on many projects have instilled a significant understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and the Database Development Life Cycle (DDLC). So much so that I have devised methodologies based upon CMM, Agile, and Scrum into a scalable and mutable system called JEP, or Just Enough Process. JEP represents a model that defines the best practices and organizational instructions for a Software Engineering/IT environment. JEP’s flexibility and adaptability balance between having enough process to get the job done without having the process get in the way.
