Wallace Ryland’s Summary

A world-class change agent and new market catalyst with over 15 years experience in leading sales, marketing, and business development initiatives.

As president of BestofNorthCarolina.com, Wallace has been charged with launching the company and generating the company’s first revenue streams. BestofNorthCarolina.com exceeded subscription revenue targets by over 150%, increased traffic by 134%, and the Facebook Community by 791% within the first quarter of operation.

In addition Wallace serves as an advisory board member and investor with Evryx Technologies, where he was part of the initial executive team. He has previously served interim executive roles with Sprout, Inc., mediabistro.com, and ISU.

Wallace was the Vice President of eFinancial News and head of sales for both efinancialCareers.com and Jobsinthemoney.com. He was recruited to reorganize the company and accelerate sales for a profitable acquisition. He grew revenues over 126% while reorganizing the sales staff. As a result, the company was successfully acquired by Dice.com(NYSE:DHX) for $88 Million (at over a 7X multiple).

In his previous position as Vice President of Sales for ChildrenFirst, he was charged to rapidly enhance the value of the company. His team increased new sales revenue by 346% in less than a year and expanded the company into 12 new markets. His ability to execute the company's sales strategy contributed to the company’s $58 million acquisition by Bright Horizons Family Solutions (BFAM: NASDAQ).

In 1999, he was Director of Business Development and co-founder of blue-silicon, a Softbank funded software company. His first executive role as a change agent came when recruited as Publisher and Vice President of Sales for Marcoa Publishing. While at Marcoa, Wallace increased revenue 775% over a 2-year period and creating a matrix sales organization allowing for the marketing of multiple products simultaneously.
