Dick Wingate’s Summary

With more than three decades of experience in the music and digital media industries, I am currently Head of East Coast Client Relations for TAG Strategic, a digital entertainment consulting firm, where I consult new clients on business development, content licensing and distribution strategy.

Previously I served as President, Media Development & Chief Content Officer for Nellymoser Inc, an early provider of rich media mobile services, overseeing content, licensing and biz dev strategies, and worked closely with media companies such as Sony Music, Warner Music, ABC, and MTV. I also programmed Nellymoser’s music discovery services, currently operating on AT&T, Virgin Mobile and Alltel.

I helped pioneer the digital music business during my years as SVP, Content Dev., at Liquid Audio, Inc, playing an integral role in their IPO in 1999 and was responsible for managing music licensing, label relations, affiliate mgmt and content mktg.

My in-depth experience in the recording industry includes positions as SVP, Marketing, Arista Records (BMG); SVP, A&R PolyGram Records; Dir. Talent Acquisition, Epic Records; and Dir.,Product Mgmt, Columbia Records. I have been integrally involved with the careers of artists such as Bruce Springsteen, Elvis Costello and Aimee Mann.

I have spoken at numerous industry events, including: CTIA, MEFCON, Billboard Live, Jupiter Plug-In, CMJ, Midem, Bear Stearns, Credit Suisse, Business Week and SXSW, have been featured in the Sunday NY Times Business section, a front page LA Times article as well as quoted in the Wall St. Journal, Time Mag, USA Today, the NY Times Mag and Billboard Mag. In 2000 I was selected as a rep of NY’s Silicon Alley to meet President and Mrs. Clinton and five US Senators.

I was an early music industry supporter of digital interactive media, and in the early 90’s served as VP, Market Dev., Intouch Group, developer of the i-Station, the first networked, interactive music- previewing kiosk for record stores.

Dick Wingate’s Specialties:

Music, digital media and mobile business development & licensing for online, mobile and kiosk services.
