Michael Kellogg’s Summary

For the past ten years Michael has worked with large and mid-size companies in the US, UK, Japan, India, Pakistan and China to successfully define and execute supply chain solutions tailored to their unique business requirements. Michael has continuously proven and improved on his capabilities as an engineer, systems integrator, consultant, project manager, and personnel developer since joining Kurt Salmon Associates from Cal Poly in 1999. As a result, he has risen to the level of Manager through progressive promotion.

In addition, Michael has utilized his skills at a number of non-profit and community organizations to lead people, projects and forge partnerships with outside groups.
