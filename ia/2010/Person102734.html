Johnna Gray’s Summary

As an author, Johnna Gray gives her readers a comprehensive, yet easy to understand insight into the world where science and spirit meet. The goal is to inspire and activate the reader to participate in creating a life that is on purpose with compassion and gratitude for the profound and powerful gifts of spirit.

As a professional integrative trainer and coach, aka Insight Catalyst, Johnna Gray assists her clients in understanding and applying the holistic mind/body/spirit connection to make achievements beyond expectations.

With goals as varied as each individual, the results continue to prove inspiring!

Often the client's initial aim will begin with concerns ranging from weight loss and body composition modifications, to relationship and career challenges. There is no limit to the useful application of these programs. The most exciting part of all, is the way the results profoundly effect not just the individual, but also their entire sphere of influence.

Johnna Gray's programs are designed specifically to the individual's needs and current understanding.
Through consultations, evaluations and progress assessments, Johnna continually develops a personal, unique and exciting program. The process, application and tools used are incorporated in a simple, easy to follow format that is inspiring and enlightening. From there the program evolves to deepen the gained insight and further the progress. Equipped with infinite resources both internal and external, each individual recognizes their ability to master any undertaking with confidence and enthusiasm.
