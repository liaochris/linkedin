Carl King’s Summary

I believe that my clients find me to be a knowledgeable, competent, and professional mortgage consultant. My business model is not to "sell" loans, but to add value by analyzing the financial profile, risk tolerance, and long-term plans of potential clients, with the goal of providing the optimal solution at the lowest costs. I strive to establish an enduring relationship with my clients based on mutual trust. The residential mortgage industry is generally a complex, low-volume, high-margin business in which clients should have high expectations of their provider, without the need to pay higher interest rates or fees to meet those expectations. Many providers focus their spending on advertising to continually obtain new clients; my goal is to focus on existing clients and generate new business through their referrals.

Carl King’s Specialties:

Although the bulk of my clients use straightforward financing options, I can also help structure creative financing to enable homeowners to purchase a new residence prior to the sale of their current home. I also have access to private money lenders or alternative lending for the increasing number of borrowers who don't quite "fit the box".
