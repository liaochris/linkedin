Ken Revenaugh’s Summary

As a problem solver, my goal is to join a company, implement winning solutions and work myself out of a job once the organization is successful as a result of my expertise.

With an impressive history of driving record levels of revenues, profits and market share, I partner with sales leadership to develop and deploy tools, systems, practices and procedures that result in dramatic improvements in global sales performance.

My methodology? Using a high-energy style, my programs introduce a disciplined approach to sales, forecasting and client retention. As a result, I help turn under-performing teams into rock star sales forces, reduce organizational expenses and foster a culture of accountability and respect.
