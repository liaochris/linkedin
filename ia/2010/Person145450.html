Mike Harrison’s Summary

Highly credible and versatile non-Union ISDN Voice Over Actor/Narrator. Top-notch quality; fast turnaround. Extensive background in corporate/industrial and e-Learning genres for many Fortune 500 companies, such as AT&T. Innately fluent with technical material. Expert copy interpretation and delivery in a manner appropriate to both the subject matter and the audience... in a familiar, conversational style. Neutral North American accent. Extremely adaptable: straight reads, real-person, or a wide palette of character voices. Authoritative, announcer, conversational, funny, guy-next-door, sincere, wacky, warm. Highly directable, excellent acting talent, a true 'utility man.' Studio exceeds broadcast audio quality, ISDN and Source-Connect for live sessions, or delivery in many formats via ftp. Free, no-obligation custom demos on request. Numerous online demos downloadable from: www.mike-harrison.com.

High-end Digital studio featuring the Source-Connect plug-in with Pro Tools on the Power Macintosh G4, Sennheiser MKH 416 microphone, Focusrite Platinum Voicemaster Pro mic pre, and ISDN via Telos Zephyr.

I set type on one of the first (and few) fully computerized phototypesetters, Itek's Quadritek 1200. Programmed slides on Audio Visual Labs (AVL)'s Show-Pro V and Eagle systems.
