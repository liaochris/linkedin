Kaye Kendrick’s Summary

I help organizations with strategic planning, leadership, and process development. I work in several ways: one-on-one with organizational leadership, with groups of leaders within organizations, and I also periodically establish groups consisting of non-competitive organizations for group coaching development.

After spending almost fifteen years auditing organizations, and then almost fifteen years in business helping organizations with “performance”, I decided to focus my recent efforts on “coaching” organizations and individuals.

I affiliated and trained with a company headquartered in Pennsylvania, Research Associates Corporation. Research Associates Corporation provides research tested coaching tools to a network of over 600 consultants. Coaching is a concept I desired throughout my career and personal development, but a service that wasn’t available to me. I am pleased I can use my experience and training to coach others.

I have almost three decades of experience in auditing and consulting, in and for both the public and private sector. My work included serving as the Inspector General for the Florida Department of Management Services. Approximately fifteen years of my career has been as an entrepreneur, establishing an accounting franchise, LedgerPlus, where I had the honor of being recognized as Franchise of the Year. I also established two consulting firms during this time.
