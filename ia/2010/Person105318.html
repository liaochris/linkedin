Joseph Puckett’s Summary

Allstate Insurance, Investments, and Banking professional proud to serve the Shelby County, Alabama communities. I love my wonderful wife, have a great and loving family, have two crazy dogs, and enjoy making life better for those around me!

I grew up in Etowah County, Alabama and graduated from Emma Sansom High School in 2003. My high school sweetheart, Lauren Machen Puckett, and I married on our 6 year dating anniversary in 2006.

I attended The University of Alabama and graduated in less than 3 years with honors and remain a very proud alumnus! Roll Tide!

In October of 2009, Lauren and I opened The Puckett Agency, Inc. of Allstate Insurance. We look forward to helping families protect the things that are most important – their family, home, car, business, and more. We can also help you prepare a strategy to achieve your long term financial goals. Our goal is to make you feel as though you are a part of our family. Our knowledge and understanding of the people in our community help us provide customers with an outstanding level of service.

You deserve excellent service and we aim to exceed your high expectations! Please consider us as a resource for your insurance needs.
