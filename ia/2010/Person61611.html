Vickie Larson’s Summary

Four years of experience in the ownership and operation of a full service consulting firm, Ecospatial Analysts, Inc. Twenty-two years of experience in Florida natural community ecology and threatened and endangered species, including population assessments, impact analysis of fauna and flora, wetland ecology, and conservation and restoration design. Experience includes thirteen years of direct involvement in environmental and ecological projects for NASA at Kennedy Space Center that included the ecology of wetlands, wildlife, fire and wildlife ecology, and land use planning. Nineteen years of technical experience using and teaching geographic information system (GIS) and in environmental, land use planning, and natural resource applications, including data collection; database design, analysis and application development using ArcGIS, ArcInfo, ArcView, and ERDAS software. Expertise in communicating user-defined objectives into functional database designs and GIS applications and analyses provides unique qualifications. More than seven years of management experience included proposal writing, contract negotiations, and budgeting for several contracts valued at more than $300,000. Certifications: State of Florida Woman Business Enterprise (WBE); GIS Professional

Vickie Larson’s Specialties:

GIS, GPS, Florida natural community ecology, threatened and endangered species, including population assessments, impact analysis of fauna and flora, wetland ecology, and conservation and restoration design, land use planning, fire and wildlife ecology, HCP, NEPA
