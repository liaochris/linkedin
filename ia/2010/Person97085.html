Jessica Searcy Maldonado’s Summary

As an Accredited Investment Fiduciary Analyst™ designee and Professional Plan Consultant™, my areas of expertise are in helping retirement plan sponsors, foundations, investment committees, and other stewards abide by the Global Standards of Fiduciary Excellence.

Searcy Financial Services, Inc. is a Registered Investment Advisory firm that offers a highly personalized financial planning and investment management service for successful individuals and businesses. All of our services are based on your personal situation—your values, beliefs, preferences, and the degree to which you want to be involved in the process of managing your finances, giving you the freedom to focus on the things and people you care about most.

We offer the following:
► Planning for the Future. We help you focus on the things that truly matter, coordinate the details and help you make the right choices to most efficiently use your resources to keep you moving towards your destination.

► Investment Management. We can take the burden of managing your portfolio off of your shoulders, so that you focus on living your life. Once we have a clear understanding of your preferences, risk tolerance, goals, and timelines, we select an investment allocation specifically tailored to meet your needs.

► A Better 401(k) Solution. What makes a retirement plan successful? We will guide you through a process to help you clearly define the criteria you have set for plan success and serve as an ERISA 3(38) fiduciary to your plan.

► Business/Practice Consulting. Having worked closely with doctors and small business owners for over 34 years, we are familiar with the issues specific to both. We offer specialized services to help ease the administrative burdens of running a successful practice or small business.
