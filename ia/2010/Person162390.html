Jamie L. Allen, Esquire’s Summary

Welcome to the Law Offices of
Jamie L. Allen P.A.

At the Law Office of Jamie L. Allen, P.A., we concentrate in personal injury law. Whether your case is an unfortunate automobile accident or complicated medical malpractice, our firm will take your case through preparation, mediation and to trial, if necessary. Our firm takes pride in providing personal contact and attention to our clients. We will guide you through the legal process and provide you with all the necessary information you may require.

Our attorneys, investigators, and support staff all work together for you, the client. We prepare every one of our cases as if it were going to trial. Because of this, we have earned a reputation as effective trial lawyers. This often translates into larger settlement offers and jury awards.

Our success is predicated on the results we achieve for each client, not on the number of cases we handle or the number of insurance settlements we achieve. Our firm is dedicated to providing personalized and focused attention to each client, skilled advocacy, legal advice, and trial experience.

At our firm, we help to relieve the problems that an injury can bring, and allow injured clients to attend to immediate health, safety, and financial concerns while we pursue a variety of legal means to obtain justice. We understand that, in many cases, no amount of money will make up for a life changing injury. Our goal is to get the compensation necessary to help people move on with their lives. We know that to our clients and their families that their case is the only case that matters and that specialized legal representation means everything.

Jamie L. Allen, Esquire’s Interests:

Before opening the doors of her own office, firm founder Jamie L. Allen worked as a Trial Attorney specializing in Personal Injury on both sides starting by representing Insurance Companies and for the last several years, representing victims who have been seriously injured as a result of the negligence of others. Having handled complicated personal injury cases, Jamie L. Allen understands the methods and procedures that insurance companies use, and is well suited to guide her injured clients through the frustrating process of a personal injury case and to obtain the maximum recovery. Jamie L. Allen is well known and highly regarded by the judges, attorneys and insurers in the jurisdictions where she practices.
