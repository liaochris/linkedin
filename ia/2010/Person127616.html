Lori Edmonds Stegeman’s Summary

Presently, I manage the ecommerce division for Peruvian Connection. I have worked for Fortune 250 companies managing and leading ecommerce and digital marketing efforts. I have extensive experience in website management and conversion optimization, paid and organic search, email and affiliate marketing, shopping portals, display advertising and web analytics.

My business service background, analytical skills and financial acumen combined with my interpersonal skills and work experience has provided a solid foundation for success. Strong business sense coupled with online marketing expertise has proven to be a powerful combination - resulting in quantifiable increases in return with concurrent decreases in spend at both Embarq (now CenturyLink) and Hallmark Cards, Inc. Within the first 6 months of working at CenturyLink, the online channel achieved a 40% increase in revenue with a 70% decrease in spend. Results were driven by:

Employing online and business best practices
Making data driven decisions
Utilizing test and target
Developing standardized Omniture reporting
Staff training
Developing an attribution model to better forecast and allocate budgets specifically targeted to maximize revenues at the lowest cost
Creating collaborate divisional communication; stating clear and complete objectives, KPI’s, measurements and accountabilities.

Similar results were achieved at Hallmark Cards, Inc. In addition to both managing and individually contributing to online success, I have added value to the overall organizational structure, business processes and team development. I am personally dedicated to my staff, making their training, development and success a high priority.

I have proven results in billion dollar companies. I have experience training, managing and promoting direct reports and building collaborative teams. I have a well rounded understanding of data needs, functionality, conversion opportunities, and partner needs.
