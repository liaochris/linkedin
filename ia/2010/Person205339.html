Todd Colletti’s Summary

Todd Colletti joined Moblyng in 2009 as VP, Engineering. Moblyng is a leading developer of cross-platform games for mobile devices and social networks.

Before joining Moblyng, Colletti was the VP of Operations at Mediazone. MediaZone.com, Inc. was a global online broadcaster of sports, entertainment and international programming.

Prior to joining MediaZone, Colletti was the Director of Engineering & Product Planning at CNET Networks Entertainment/GameSpot, where he was awarded "Inventor" commendations for developing four important technology patents.
Prior to joining CNET, Colletti was the VP of Production for wireless games developer 3d4W, Inc.

Before joining 3d4W, Colletti was the CTO for episodic entertainment publisher ARUSH games, where he was responsible for technical strategies supporting episodic game development and delivery.

Prior to joining ARUSH Entertainment, Colletti was Executive Producer for Europe at GT Interactive Software working on "Driver," "Imperium Galactica 2", "Powerslide" and "Jet Moto 2."

Colletti was one of the first employees of Sony Computer Entertainment. He served as manager of research and development during Sony's launch into the video game industry. Colletti was instrumental in establishing the PlayStation™ as the leading entertainment platform.

Before moving into the interactive entertainment arena, Colletti began his career as a financial analyst at Aeneas Ventures, a Harvard Management Company.

Colletti earned advanced degrees in technology and society from Boston University.
