After graduating, I worked as a Production Designer at a local printing firm, where I gained experience in pre-press and pre-flight processes, high-volume, quick turnaround projects and successfully communicating with print vendors. I am a hard worker, have a great eye for design, and am meticulously organized and unafraid of challenges.

I am extremely versatile, confident, and passionate about Art and Design. I am a savvy, tech-minded individual, with a keen eye for detail and a solid grasp of the required job functions. I also have a knack for creative writing and am extremely grammar conscious.

I am looking for a career that challenges my skills, offers positive growth, is cutting edge and creatively focused. I am interested in Marketing, Advertising, Design, Photography, and Sustainability.
