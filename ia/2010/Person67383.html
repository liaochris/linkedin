Esbon Griffin’s Summary

My educational and professional background is in radio & television production, film making, photography, digital imaging and professional sales.

I consistently performed as a Million Dollar A Year Sales Professional for the last three years of my tenure with Robyn Color, a leading custom photographic color lab in San Francisco. According to John Altberg, with Eastman Kodak, I’m the only person in the country who has ever done this in the photo industry selling traditional services.

My main focus has always been on building relationships with prospects and clients that foster trust, respect and integrity. I’ve found this to be the best way to promote new business, repeat business, fortify an existing client base and build good will in the community. I am a seasoned professional and team player.

I possess a unique combination of management expertise, technical skills, creativity, sales ability and “people skills”. One of my greatest strengths is the ability to gain the trust and confidence of business associates and customers.

I’ve been with Best Buy in Marin City, CA, for almost five years and work as a Business Technology Consultant helping local businesses. I’ve enjoyed excellent performance reviews with a performance rating of 3.9 - 4.0 on a scale of 1 to 5. I was awarded The Most Valuable Player Award 2006 and awarded The Most Valuable Player of Most Valuable Players Award 2007.

In November 2008, I was honored with a special stock option award for exemplifying the Best Buy Promise: A Trusted Perspective for Inspired Living. I feel especially honored as this award is presented to less than 2% of the eligible employee population base.

With my experience, understanding of the business world, proven track record as an Achiever, management and motivational skills, I am a valuable asset to any company who is looking for a top performer.
