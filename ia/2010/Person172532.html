Richard Williams’s Summary

Richard Williams was born on the island of Barbados to a Canadian mother and Barbadian father and after secondary education in Canada, he returned to the island to join the tourism industry.
He has been prominent in the Caribbean hotel industry for many years with an outstanding track record throughout the islands of the Eastern Caribbean as well as the USA and Canada.
Key highlights of his professional career include:
• Managing Director and part owner of Cobblers Cove Hotel, Barbados www.cobblerscove.com
• Managing Director, Kuoni Hotel Management, Swiss Hotels in the Caribbean http://www.kuoni-group.com/
• Chief Executive Officer of Sandy Lane, Barbados www.sandylane.com
• Vice President, Barbados Tourism www.VisitBarbados.org
He is a Past President of the Barbados Hotel Association and has been a central figure in Caribbean tourism marketing for many years. He was chosen by the Caribbean Hotel Association as the first Barbadian honoured as Caribbean Hotelier of the Year. During his tenure as Chairman of the CHA Marketing Committee from 1982 to 1989, industry changing events such as the annual Caribbean Marketplace, the Caribbean Gold Book and CHARMS (Caribbean hotels Reservations Service) were all conceived and born.
As national recognition for his contribution to the development of the Caribbean tourism industry, the Barbados Government awarded Williams the Silver Crown of Merit in 1992.
He is a motivational and visionary leader with a proven ability to develop business revenues whilst at the same time raising the product standards on offer to exceed customer expectations. Richard is a self starter and a key asset in any executive team. He is highly respected by his colleagues in the hospitality industry.

Richard Williams’s Specialties:

Destination & resort sales, marketing and PR plus a range of resort development services to hospitality clients around the world.
