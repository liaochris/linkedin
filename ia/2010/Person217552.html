YK (Yue-Kai) Lo’s Summary

Smart Product Platform Group (SPPG) – Mission Statement
The mission of this group is to provide added-value, turn-key design and support services to speed-up the processes from product conception to mass production of customers using SPPG well-experienced full services from product definition, application, circuit, and layout design, verification, end-product integration, validation, wafer fabrication, and back-end testing and packaging technologies.

SPPG will work hand-in-hand with customers and with system and circuit design platform, IP library, device and foundry/process design kits to fine-tune product solutions, circuits, layout rules, and device-yield models (with the purposes of producing the most optimal cost, performance and fastest possible time-to-market products and with customer-tailored services) for full-custom analog and mixed-signal Smart Product-based products covering the major analog circuits, telecom, mobile and stationary multi-core DSP/uC SOC in today’s IC market.

In Addition, SPPG will help resolve customers’ issues on IC and system levels to guide key accounts into volume production; SPPG goals will be working hand-in-hand with customers to make sure that CSMC customers will be successful in using SPPG customer-tailored turn-key services and foundry resources on developing and mass-producing semiconductor products.

About CSMC Silicon Foundries:
Pioneering the open foundry business model in China since 1997, CSMC is a leading pure-play specialty analog and mixed-signal foundry providing fabless design houses and IDMs with a wide range of manufacturing services. CSMC manufactures integrated circuits (IC) and power discrete processes at production analog and mixed signal process technologies nodes ranging from 0.11 micron to 0.5 micron. CSMC ICs and power devices are utilized in a broad range of end market applications including consumer electronics, communications devices, personal computers, automotive & industrial products.
