Joe Leonard’s Summary

I have worked in the pharmaceutical and nutritional supplement industries for over 10 years as a science and technical research writer. With a background in journalism, biology, and public health, I have a diverse set of knowledge and expertise. I decided to become freelance and offer my skill set to a wider audience.

My medium term goal is to offer my freelance scientific research and creative communication skill set to industry and advertisers. I can research and write on almost any topic with short turn around time and competitive rates. I specialize in shorter length projects (articles rather than books).

My long term goal is to own my own record label and write/produce songs for industry (film/TV/other artists). I currently host House Concerts to develop social networks in the music industry. I am a blogger and social networker too.
