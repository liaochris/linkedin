Bari Winter’s Summary

I have been working in Post Production since attending The School of Visual Arts in New York City. While on the east coast, I worked as an editor on news, sports and corporate videos, but I was looking for more. I moved to Los Angeles October 2002. Since then I have worked in reality TV, corporate videos, documentaries, and some scripted dramas.
