Paul Ward’s Summary

Ever since having to explain the power company position to a Fox reporter in the case made famous by the Erin Brokovitch movie, I have found myself in the intersection between business and law. As the internet changed the world I helped start, with friends law.com, to make that intersection easier. I consulted in Business Development and Client Focus, at big law firms such as Orrick,Gibson Dunn, and Bingham. I launched new companies at Incubate.com, but I also enjoy helping law firms change and grow. My latest loves were Stradling, a jewel in Newport Beach...and Ryley Carlock, which has an innovative approach to the delivery of legal services that makes it attractive to some of the best brands in America. (they also were our lawyers when we sold law.com!)
