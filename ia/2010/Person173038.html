Rick Clancy, APR’s Summary

Now heading public relations for Covario, a leader in search marketing and optimization (SEM, SEO), as well as social media advertising services. In addtion, the San Diego-based software company (SaaS) has just launched a fully integrated CMO dashboard, which gives marketing and financial executives an ROI picture of how a brand's total media spend (both social and soon traditional) is impacting sales lift and brand value.

Until recently, the face and voice of Sony in America.

A recognized authority in the CE/consumer tech space, a high-profile corporate blogger and media spokesperson, a highly respected corporate communications thought-leader, and a skilled PR counselor/practitioner.

An adept business communicator with years of experience as the head of corporate communications at Sony Electronics. Most recently, as Senior Vice President, member of the Management Committee, and "Executive Champion" for company-wide social media/networking initiatives.

Sony experience was preceded by executive, managerial and diverse communications posts at leading Wall Street investment firms (Drexel Burnham Lambert, E.F. Hutton) and top PR/ad agencies (JWT, Creamer Dickson Basford), representing consumer, financial, tech, B2B and industry association clients.

Currently, consulting with a unique e-commerce start-up called socialbetty.com, while also working with two industry organizations CommNexus and CONNECT that bring technology innovators and other entreprenuers together with leading corporations and investors looking for new opportunities.

Also speaks before customer care professionals, PR practitioners and other industry groups about trends in social networking, the consumer experience corporate reputation and crisis communications.

Rick Clancy, APR’s Specialties:

Crisis communications, social media, social networking, corporate blogging, media relations, analyst relations, company spokesperson, executive communications, product marketing PR, employee communications, public speaking, environmental PR, consumer engagement/promotion, business partner communications, technology PR, and investor relations support.

    Rick has been targeting a chief communications and/or executive PR role where his global business insigts, industry experience, leadership skills, communications capabilities, media/analyst relationships, social media initiatives and character/judgment can be applied on behalf of a dynamic company that is clearly on the rise.
