Gaylene Rogers Lonergan [LION 8000+]’s Summary

I have practiced Real Estate Law and Banking Law in the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex area for over 25 years. I am certified by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization in both Commercial Real Estate Law and Residential Real Estate Law, hold an "AV" in Martindale-Hubbell and am a Certified Title Insurance Associate by the Texas Land Title Association. In addition, I am a closing agent for Attorneys Title Company closing both commercial and residential real estate transactions. My firm, the Lonergan Law Firm, PLLC, is a Certified Woman Owned Business by the Women's Business Council - Southwest as well as being an Accredited Business with the Better Business Bureau.

My firm specializes in dealing with real estate investors and we are a full service provider to investors and other real estate professionals in connection with their real estate business, from assisting with their contracts, formation of their LLCs, closings, owner financing transactions, assignments, subject to's, etc. We also have an active litigation practice, focusing primarily on real estate related litigation and a consumer bankruptcy practice.

I am a co-owner of Texas Real Estate Investors Circle, one of the fastest growing real estate investment communities in DFW. We presently ahve over 1000 members. We generally meet on the 2nd Wednesday of every month at Crowne Plaza Suites on Coit Road at IH 635. Check us out at www.texasrealestateinvestorscircle.com.
