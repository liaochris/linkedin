Alex Velasquez’s Summary

Alex began his IT career in 1983 programming applications for Price Club Warehouse financial staff, reducing the 16 month lead time for applications to a matter of weeks.

In 1994, Alex joined CASEL Inc, the jewelry supplier for Costco Wholesale, as Data Processing Manager and Programmer Analyst. Alex reduced IT costs and provided critical reports for management.

Alex joined Amylin Pharmaceuticals Inc. in 1996 as Programmer Analyst. In 1998, Amylin reduced the workforce from 300 to 37 people. Alex was asked to remain as the sole IT staff member.

During his 12 years at Amylin, Alex helped build the IT department from 1 person to a staff of 167 people. As Associate Director of Information Technology, Alex implemented over 200 software projects. Alex also served as System Administrator for 30 critical Sarbanes Oxley and FDA Validation controlled applications for the Finance, Legal and Human Resource departments.

Alex is currently working as an IT consultant in the San Diego area, providing strategic planning, budgeting, staff hiring, project management and technical support for his clients.

Alex specializes in assisting pharmaceutical startups with IT setup, management and support.

Regulations are an important aspect of today’s business world, and Alex provides expertise in such areas as Quality, FDA Validation and Sarbanes Oxley regulations.

In addition, Alex is Managing Director for Velasquez and Associates, a web consulting firm specializing in best practice websites and search engine optimization (SEO). Velasquez and Associates attracts more web visitors for their clients by improving the website's ranking in the search engines, and with an enhanced website these visitors become customers.

Alex also provides Microsoft Sharepoint development for Intranets and Extranets.

Pharmaceutical IT startup, IT strategic planning, software implementation, system administration, project management, Sarbanes Oxley, FDA Validation, website development, search engine optimization, Sharepoint development.
