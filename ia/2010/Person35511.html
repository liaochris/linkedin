Chad Yurich’s Summary

I have been in sales since 1999 in the lighting and commercial construction industries. From 2003 to 2008, I managed the horse program for a Guest Ranch (Bar Lazy J) in the mountains of Colorado. In the falls of 2004 to 2007 I helped guide elk hunters for Samuleson Outfitters. The winters of 2008 and 2009, I guides snowmobilers for Aspen Canyon Ranch also in the mountains of Colorado. In the beginning of 2008, I recieved my Real Estate License in the state of Colorado and in the summer of 2008 sold just under $1M in mini ranches.
Currently, I am selling the most energy efficient lights in the industry for high bay buildings. With these products, energy savings range, on average, from 15%-50% on an electric bill. I have both florescent and metal halide fixtures (www.bestlights.com). If you are interested, contact me at (248)515-9913
