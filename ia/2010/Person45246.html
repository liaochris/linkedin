Curt Doty’s Summary

Curt Doty, creative executive, creative director, designer, director, has over 20 years experience in the entertainment world. Curt, a graduate of Art Center College of Design, got his start in television at ABC News in New York, designing for all the national news shows and ABC Sports. He moved on to entertainment design and promotion at international agencies such as Pittard Sullivan and 3 Ring Circus in Hollywood. At Pittard Sullivan, Curt led in their Branding and integrated media campaigns as head of all Creative in the Culver City Office, responsible for the product, process and structure of all divisions. In 2004, Curt was recruited into the Home Entertainment world of Universal Pictures. Marketing campaigns for Universal’s DVD’s included the 40 Year Old Virgin, Serenity, Evan Almighty, The Bourne Ultimatum and Hellboy II: The Golden Army. He focused on the Dreamworks and Universal Family business, including Shrek 2, Madagascar, SharkTale and Curious George. Curt pioneered Universal DVD Originals for franchise hits such as Carlito’s Way: Rise to Power; Bring it On: All or Nothing; American Pie Presents the Naked Mile, American Pie Presents: Beta House, Battlestar Gallactica: Razor and Scorpion King 2: Rise of a Warrior. Since 2006, Curt was responsible for the creative and technology development for Universal’s Hi-Def initiatives starting with HD DVD. Ultimately winning HD DVD of the year award for The Bourne Ultimatum from the DEG. Curt went on to creatively lead the launch of Blu-ray for the studio. Industry firsts for the format included My Chat, My Commentary and Director Chat. Universal has now established an award winning leadership role with interactivity and connectivity. Curt’s architecting of U-Control and BD-Live for the studio was a major contribution. Curt now works at the entertainment ad agency, Trailer Park, where he heads up Advanced Content as Executive Vice President.

Curt Doty’s Specialties:

Branding, Identity, Trailers, TV spots, Sizzle reels, Publicity reels, Websites, UI design, Navigation Architecture, Experience Design, Creative Writing, Keyart, Project Management, Career path development, Team building, Managing creative staffs, large and small.
