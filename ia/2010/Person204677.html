Tina Whitfield’s Summary

My passion is mobile. My global report on mobile local search and other location-based services is now published.

Mobile Local Search 2010 as well as reports on Mobile Wallets Leveraging LBS and Mobile Social Leveraging LBS can be reviewed at:

www.mobilelocation.com

Recently, I consulted to Acrossair.com, AT&T, MyShape.com, and Goldman-Sachs as well as writing regular columns eConsultancy.

In 2008, I was appointed Vice President of Marketing for Vindigo Inc., where I was responsible for operations of corporate and product branding, customer acquisition/retention, PR, and customer service. I joined Vindigo from Nokia where I headed global consumer marketing for mobile content and applications. I brought to Nokia my deep experience building mobile ecosystems and mobile marketing campaigns as a consultant to Silicon Valley start-ups.

Before launching my consultancy, I was a Director of Global Alliances for marchFIRST/USWeb where I lead negotiations for strategic alliances with mobile, e-commerce, and e-procurement cos., delivering hundreds of millions of dollars in revenue. Also, I was a key contributor to the 3Com-USWeb deal to deliver WiFi e-commerce solutions to the San Francisco 49ers and NFL. As a Director of Client Relations, I lead the development of the SEGA Dreamcast ecommerce website and online multiplayer portal which contributed to a 16% year-over-year increase in market share.

I have two decades of experience, was a founder of WirelessKnowledge, a Qualcomm-Microsoft Company, began my career at Qualcomm in the Eudora Email Products division, held engineering management roles at Northrop-Grumman, and marketing and media relations roles Phillips-Marketing.
