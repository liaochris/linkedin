Joe Mannion’s Experience

  • Instructor

    Logan College

    (Sports industry)

    May 2009 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    I teach "Psychology of the Athlete" in the Masters program in Sports Science & Rehabilitation. We examine basic sport psychology theory and practice, especially in the service of enhancing athletic performance, rehabilitation, and well-being. Although the grad students are not studying to be sport psychology professionals, we strive to produce more psychosocially adept sport scientists in order to better serve the needs of their clients (e.g., athletes, coaches, performing artists). The class has included student role-plays, an innovative term paper, technique demonstrations, and the college’s first in-class (live and interactive) Wimba webconference with an Olympic track athlete, 2 weeks prior to the World Championships.

  • Author

    [Working title] Undefeated: Iraq's Elite Athletes Tell Their Stories

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    2004 — Present (6 years )

    ***ATTENTION: ACTIVELY SEEKING AN AGENT & PUBLISHER***

    A nonfiction, popular press book (in progress) detailing stories of the Iraqi athletes who were imprisoned, tortured, and even executed under Saddam Hussein's regime. Includes a brief journey through the history of Iraq's Olympic & FIFA programs, the rise and fall of Uday Hussein, and the reconstruction of sport in Iraq - all told through developing friendships and fascinating interviews with, among others:

    ~ Tony Blair's special envoy to Iraq & chair of the Iraqi war crimes tribunal in London
    ~ The first elected president of the new Iraqi Olympic Committee (since kidnapped and presumed executed)
    ~ The Iraqi delegation to the 2004 Pan Arab Games in North Africa
    ~ Numerous surviving athletes who had firsthand encounters with Uday Hussein, including one who defected to the US during the 1996 Olympic Games

    I've given 2 peer-reviewed presentations at an academic conference and have a small mountain of video, photos, and audio. Much more TBA!!!
