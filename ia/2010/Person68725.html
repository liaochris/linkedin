Fabrizio Picca’s Summary

I want to be a focal point in Digital Communication area, mixing together creativity, alignment with business and a strong technical background.

During my experiences I always kept in mind the vision of the “Digital Nervous System” (cit.) while building architectures to spread informations between people, processes and customers. That's why I'm now working on Communication and Collaboration Technologies and Social Computing.
The only way to face the ever-changing competitive scenario is wiring together people and making them communicate easily : when everything changes rapidly, the key of success is how fast you can communicate the change.

I've started my career path during my studies, working in different roles of IT ranging from System Administration to Web development in SMB sector. Once obtained my grade in Computer Science I started working for Merrill Lynch International Bank as CRM software developer. I continued to develop my skills in software development moving to one of the main software houses in Italy: TXT e-solutions.

During this very intense period I was selected by IBM to join a work/study program developed in conjunction with MIP, the business school of Politecnico di Milano.

Once completed the program I was definitively hired as IBM regular employee and started working as Information Technology Architect focused on Digital and Unified Communications.

In 2008 I was also selected to participate to the writing of IBM redbook "IBM Smart Business Unified Communications and IP Telephony", which gave me the opportunity to participate to an international assignment within an international team.

I'm now working in the pre-sales technical team, working on solution design, solution portfolio management and technology scouting within Integrated Communication Services, part of IBM Global technology services.
