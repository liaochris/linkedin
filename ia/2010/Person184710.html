Scott Pickett’s Summary

Scott started working for Pickett Suite Hotels and Pickett Development Company during high school. I assisted with site selection, construction management preparing development proformas, market analysis and loan packages used to secure equity and debt financing. During my tenture these companies developed, managed and operated over 2,000 hotel rooms and 1 million square feet of office product.

In 1992 Scott joined Continental Real Estate Companies as Vice President responsible for leasing and management of approximately 1 million square feet of Office Product. During my 11 years with Continental, I was promoted to Vice President of Development responsible for development of new office product developing numerous office building in Central Ohio.

In 2003 Scott joined CBRE's Columbus Office as Vice President providing portfolio management and brokerage services for such clients as Cardinal Health, Scotts, Nationwide, Medco, McGraw Hill, and BMW Financial Services

In 2008 Scott formed Pickett Holstlaw Byrne Realty Advisors a full service commercial real estate provider specializing in tenant representation, buyer / seller representation, syndication and property management throughout Ohio

Today Scott provides such services as:
1. Multifamily Investments - Syndication and Management
2. Buyer / Seller representation
3. Tenant and Landlord Representation
4. Economic Incentive Negotiation / Consulting
5. Build to Suit Projects
6. Site Selection Services
7. Real Estate Advisory Services
8. Real Estate Portfolio Management
9. Development of Commercial Real Estate including Hotel
10. Multifamily, Retail, Office, and Industrial Projects
