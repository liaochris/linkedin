Bellandra B. Foster’s Summary

Currently President and Principal Engineer of BBF Engineering Services, PC. Obtained BS in Civil Engineering (1983), MS in Civil Engineering (1989) and Ph.D., in Civil and Environmental Engineering (1999). Formerly employed by Bechtel Power Corporation (1983-1984), Oakland County Road Commission (1984-1985), Michigan Department of Transportion(1985-1992). Was appointed by Mayor Maynard Jackson to the position of Director of Highways and Streets (1992). Founded BBF Engineering Services, PC (1994).

Currently goals includes being a loving and nurturing wife and mother in addition to prioritizing my life in the manner God would have. Specifically in the areas of marriage, children, career and entrepreneurship.

Bellandra B. Foster’s Specialties:

Civil Engineering Consultant in the areas of construction inspection and testing, construction engineering, project management and project administration, utility coordination and small and disadvantaged business technical consulting.
