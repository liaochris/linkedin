Lisa Hudgens’s Summary

I create marketing, public relations and management solutions for nonprofits and small business clients. My work is based on a simple concept: Smart planning and clear communication are the keys to any successful venture.

I'm an energetic leader and manager with 18 years' diverse experience helping organizations fulfill their missions, foster trust and accountability, and build recognition and respect for a job well done.

I provide a wide range of services for organizations that need:

* Web and social media marketing campaigns

* Media and public relations

* Writing and editing

* Fundraising support

* Risk management plans, issues management and crisis response

* Employee handbooks, policy and procedure manuals

* Executive coaching and management support


If you've ever worked with a charitable organization, you know that ours is a world with it's own unique set of challenges and opportunities. A successful nonprofit volunteer or employee needs drive and determination, an "out-of-the box" approach to solving complex problems and most importantly, a true passion for helping others.

It's tough, demanding work and everyday offers new opportunities to learn something new...that's why I love what I do!
