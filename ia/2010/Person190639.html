Skot Pare’s Summary

I have been a Director of Development for ten years with responsibility for everything from annual appeals and major gifts to special events and direct mail campaigns to public, media and community relations. I am a team player, but am happy to take a leadership role or work independently if that produces the best result for the organization. I enjoy public speaking and have strong verbal and written skills. I serve on the Board of Directors of the Association of Fund Raising Professionals Northern New England Chapter.

I believe in being active the community in which I live and work. I moved to Windham, NH in August 2009. I have joined the Kiwanis Club of Manchester serving on their Board of Directors and being Chair of their Music Committee. I also play drums and percussion with a group of members from the club called emanent Vacation, playing a wide variety of music mostly from the 60s, 70s and 80s. I am on the Advisory Board of the Manchester Corps of the Salvation Army. While in Worcester, MA, I was a Rotarian and, in Paxton, the President of the Lions Club. In the past, I have had the privilege of serving on the Board of Directors of such outstanding non-profit agencies as FirstNight Worcester (President, Marketing Committee Chair); Jeremiah’s Inn (Development Committee Chair); WCUW Community Radio Station and Quinsigamond Village Community Center.
