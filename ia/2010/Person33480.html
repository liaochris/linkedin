Carson Holmes’s Summary

Carson Holmes is an IT management consultant with over 20 years of software engineering experience. Carson has a proven record of successful projects across a variety of industries, including finance, trading, banking, defense, healthcare, and automotive. Carson is a natural leader skilled at building great teams and delivering technology solutions that maximum business value.

In 1996, Carson first partnered with Rational Software as an instructor of OOA&D and Rational Rose. Carson's passion for process and his professional attitude has elevated his consulting career from software development to IT Governance. Now as a management consultant, Carson is an agent for change and often works with senior level executives to fine tune their organizations for performance, efficiency, and effectiveness.

Currently, Carson Holmes is a Principal Consultant for Fourth Medium Consulting and helps numerous clients achieve their IT Governance and process improvement goals, with pragmatic process, policy, and metrics. Carson regularly works closely with IBM to provide industry feedback on RTC, RUP, OpenUP, RMC, WBM, RQM, Jazz, and the Green Thread projects.

Carson is the President of the Global Rational User Group, and the Los Angeles, Orange County, and San Diego chapters. Carson regularly presents at conferences and user groups. Carson recently became the Vice Chairman of the SCQAA.

Outside of work, Carson enjoys spending time with his wife and three children, mountaineering, and playing music.

Carson Holmes’s Specialties:

IT Governance, Process Improvement, Project and Portfolio Management, Application Lifecycle Management (ALM), Rational Team Concert (RTC), Rational Method Composer (RMC), Lean, Kanban, Agile, RUP, OpenUP, Scrum, XP, ITIL, ITUP, CMMI, and more...
