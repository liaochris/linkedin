Greg Montgomery’s Summary

Mr. Montgomery co-founded Coast Energy Group LLC in 2009 and serves as the EVP & Chief Financial Officer. Mr. Montgomery has over 20 years of experience leading and directing the finance activities of midstream energy companies focused on transportation, terminaling and storage of crude oil, refined petroleum products and natural gas. Coast represents the third consecutive private equity sponsored midstream energy company Mr. Montgomery has served as the CFO for. Prior to this experience, Mr. Montgomery served in various senior finance roles for major integrated energy and public MLP midstream energy companies. Mr. Montgomery has a vast network of established long-term relationships in the financial community and has spent most of his career leading and building the finance operations for high growth midstream energy companies.

Greg Montgomery’s Specialties:

Private equity relationships, banking relationships, acquisition evaluations & integration, treasury management, insurance & risk management, human resources, information technology, legal, tax, accounting, finance in the midstream energy industry.
