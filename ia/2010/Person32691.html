Carl Trett’s Summary

Professional Game Designer, working primarily on hand-held and PC titles in both 2D and 3D.

I am a graduate of the Vancouver Film School's Video Game design program.

My goal as a designer and artist, is to bring a fresh open minded perspective to every project I work on.

I am interested in making games that help people use their brains in creative ways.

Carl Trett’s Specialties:

Level Design, Conceptual Design, Pre-Production and Pre-Visualization, Mechanical and Architectural Design. Design documentation, and Prototyping. Texturing, 3D Modelling and Animation.
