Randy Waskul’s Summary

Randy recently joined Global Environmental in Dalton, GA as a partner. A full service environmental firm focused on risk minimization for our customers, Global provides all aspects of waste management, remediation projects, Phase I/II efforts, EH&S auditing and consulting, EH&S plan development, as well as 14+ years sustainability planning and implementation, with a proven track record working with small businesses, up to Fortune 500's.

Most recently, I served as the Vice President of Environmental Services for Mohawk Industries, Inc. located in Dalton, GA. At Mohawk, I focused on global environmental and sustainability issues, environmental risk minimization, Sarbanes-Oxley reporting, acquisition/divestiture due diligence, and establishing strong corporate environmental policies and procedures. I implemented system based processes for numerous environmental and sustainability efforts. I provided assistance to Mohawk's marketing team on sustainability related issues, implementing a state-of-the-art sustainability data management system.

I am also is in my fourth year as Commissioner, District 3 for Whitfield County Board of Commissioners. As a commissioner, I represent Whitfield County on the regional water council, the ACCG Natural Resources Committee and the National Association of Counties Energy, Environmental and Land Use Committee.

Prior to joining Mohawk in 2004, I served as the Vice President of Sustainable Development for Danisco A/S, a global food ingredient business headquartered in Denmark, focusing on EHS/sustainability issues for 24 sites in the Americas and Asia regions.

I have has over 20 years of practical environmental, health, safety and sustainability experience while working with various multi-national organizations.

I received my B.S in chemical engineering from the University of North Dakota in 1986.

M&A/divestiture due diligence, EHS auditing, systems development, environmental risk minimization, sustainability reporting and recordkeeping, development/Implementation of management systems, strategic approach to environmental/sustainbility issues through integration into business planning
