  • Interactive Marketing Project Manager

    Project Management and Asst. Project Management of sites at:

    CASE STUDY

    Traffic Standardization of All Holding Sites
    There was little traffic data on early 2008 about all Holding sites and agency was using Statcounter which is a priced service, less detailed and had errors. Instead, free, continuously enhancing, faster and more reliable Google Analytics (GA) is implemented on all sites at all pages. Even onpage actions are recorded via GA to see detailed user movements on site. Now, all 90 Holding site has GA and Interactive Communications Department (ICD) received over 100 million GA data to evaluate even a single user. With this data, ICD had analyzed the total number of visits, page views, unique visitors, which keywords incoming visitor had searched, what time does a visitor spend on our site from a certain advertised site and even set goals to record conversion rates to calculate ROI and performance of the web campaign. Calculating the performance and quality of incoming traffic helped the media plan to eliminate sites with less targeted traffic therefore increasing total customer experience. Data on sites showed which ads and products perform better and which keywords are searched more, meaning a continuous market research on every major site is executed. ICD gave restricted access to brands and agencies to help them understand the movements and reactions of the consumer viewing the web page. Top 10 best performing sites are published via monthly Interactive Trend Report to most executives and all marketing team meanwhile more detailed and in-depth analysis are done internally and between departments/brands/companies.
