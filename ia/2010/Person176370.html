Rodney Sampson’s Summary

Rodney Sheron Sampson is a globally established and respected intellectual and economic innovator, business developer and advisor.

He regularly advises nations, businesses and bishops that lead millions of constituents throughout the planet. Over the last 15 years, Sampson’s clients have generated more than one billion ($1B) in revenue from utilizing his advisory, management, business development and integrated marketing services.

A serial entrepreneur and investor, Sampson co-founded Multicast Media Networks (Streamingfaith.com) in 2000 (sold in 2010), Intellect in 2002, Mobile Currency in 2006, Legacy Opportunity Funds in 2007 and EFactor in 2008.

EFactor is the fastest growing online and onEarth social networking utility for entrepreneurs and investors worldwide. To date, nearly 1 million entrepreneurs from 165 nations have joined EFactor.

Sampson also serves as a Board Member of numerous private sector and not-for-gain organizations.

Sampson has a B.S in Psychology from Tulane University and a MBA from Keller Graduate School of Management. He also completed studies in the M.D. Program at the Pennsylvania State University College of Medicine from 1995-1999. In 1998, Sampson received an honorary D.Min from the I.G.F Theological Seminary.

Sampson is the author of Your Manifest Destiny: 7.5 Words to Transform Your Future, Yes We Will: Solutions for Realizing Your Personal Power, and blacks & their Trillions: Introducing Symbiotic Economics.

Sampson was consecrated to the Holy Office of Bishop on June 27th, 2010 under mandate of The Old Holy Catholic Church in intercommunion with The International Bishops Conference (Collegium Episcopi) and Kingdom Manifestation. A strong believer in global reinvestment, Sampson has been a philanthropic friend of Africa & America. For his contributions to humanity, Sampson was awarded The Phoenix Award – the City of Atlanta’s highest honor – by Mayor Shirley Franklin in 2004.
