Ryan Paddock’s Summary

As a member of the Digital Scientists team, Paddock is responsible for helping his clients to apply digital tools and new media to help their businesses grow sales, share, and profit. Prior to his time with Digital Scientists, Paddock finished his degree at Emory University's Goizueta Business School with a joint concentration in organization & management and communications. He also served as the President of the Student Government Association of Emory University, acting as an emissary between the 12,000 students of Emory University and the administration to foster a healthier campus life.
