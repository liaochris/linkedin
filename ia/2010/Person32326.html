  • President

    Crowdsourcing LLC DBA massolution

    (Management Consulting industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Crowdsourcing LLC, dba massolution, is developing an on-line “crowdsourcing” community that will go live early next year.

    Crowdsourcing is the process of gathering relevant groups of people together with defined characteristics and tapping their knowledge to create something new of value.

    Crowdsourcing applies Open Source principles to fields outside software and enables companies to engage in collaborative approaches and techniques with constituent and stakeholder groups that:

    • Improve the innovation process
    • Develop new products and services
    • Improve results and reduce the cost of R&D
    • Accelerate time-to-market
    • Increase customer engagement
    • Build customer loyalty
    • Enhance brand value
    • Create new channels to market
    • Increase marketing and distribution

    Crowdsourcing is sometimes otherwise referred to as Mass Collaboration, Open Innovation, Community Production, Mass Solutions, Constituent Driven Innovation, Connected Intelligence, Collective Wisdom, and Intelligent Human Networks.
