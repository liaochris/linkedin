Robert Ian Goodman’s Summary

Goodman Law (Robert Ian Goodman, Esq.) is based in Westchester County, New York, but represents clients worldwide. Goodman Law focuses on business development law, domestic and international commercial litigation, employment law, international trade law, intellectual property law and immigration law. Goodman law provides general counsel services to entrepreneurs and start-up businesses and counsels foreign businesses with an interest in establishing a presence in the U.S. marketplace and U.S. businesses interested in expanding abroad. A proponent of inter-firm collaboration, Mr. Goodman has developed a network of attorneys and non-attorney-service providers that has enabled him to provide comprehensive assistance to clients confronting complex legal and business issues.

Goodman Law is also a participating firm in Choudhury-Goodman Immigration and Commerical Law Affiliates ("CG"), an affilation between his firm and H. Taufiq Choudhury, P.C., based in New York City. C-G focuses on providing immigration counsel to domestic and foreign businesses and individuals, including in the areas of immigration law compliance, business immigration, and consular practice.

Among Mr. Goodman's professional goals is to promote his business model for sole-proprietorships and small firms as an efficient and cost effective alternative to traditional law practice.
