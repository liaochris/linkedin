Niamh Arthur’s Summary

My name is Niamh (pronounced Neev). I am a mother, a business owner, a mentor. I work my business from home, or around the world…wherever I happen to be! My lovely little daughter Darcy is my inspiration, and I hope to teach her that she can Be, Do and Have anything in the world. I live life On Purpose, and I’ve made it my mission to reach as many people as I can around the world to help them do the same.

When I first became an entrepreneur I was living in Ireland with my boyfriend Mike jumping from job to job moving up the ladder, each time thinking that a better job with more responsibility would mean more respect and a better life. I discovered that the higher I went the more unhappy I became and the harder I had to work to make money for someone else. 12 hour work days had me so tired that by the time my days off rolled around I slept through most of them. Mike and I longed to run our own business, but start-up costs and stingy bank managers kept getting in the way. We had big dreams to travel, own multiple houses and contribute to the world as successful entrepreneurs.

Then we discovered our business.

We are so thrilled to have found a community of like minded people who have helped us and supported us in creating our lives by design. We had the desire to make a change, we had the vision we needed to make a decision and to make it happen. And we have never looked back!
