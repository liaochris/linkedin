Meg Guiseppi, Reach CPBS, MRW, CPRW’s Summary

I love collaborating with forward-focused corporate leaders to differentiate their unique value proposition in vibrant career marketing tools, demystify today's world of executive job search, and strategically position them online and offline to land their next great gig.

My clients are typically C-suite, senior executives and rising stars (CEO, CXO, CMO, CIO, COO, CFO, CBO, President, VP, EVP, Director)

Along with writing career success documents for my clients, I help them build a brand-solid online footprint so they’re more likely to be found by recruiters and hiring decision makers who source and assess top talent by searching online.

VALUE-ADDS ─ Personal branding and career marketing development yields much more than interview-generating documents for my clients. The process:

* Invigorates them and bolsters their confidence in their unique value in the marketplace.
* Fortifies them with a clearer focus on how to get where they want to go next.
* Arms them with personal marketing tools that will resonate with their target audience.
* Energizes them for the job search journey.

    Personal branding is the best way to differentiate their unique promise of value, generate chemistry in them, and position the best they have to offer to hiring decision makers.

    I believe everyone deserves to work their passion.
