Jonathan Betts’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, business development and marketing professional. Experience and interests in management of early stage technology businesses from pre-startup, early series financing through to profitability.

Interested in organisations looking to supercharge supply chain performance. Science Warehouse powers connections between B2B buyers and vendors to enable efficient online trading. Typical customers include (i) purchasing organisations with complex user and supply base and (ii) vendor companies seeking to optimise distribution of catalogue content across multiple channels (e.g. websites, customer purchasing applications, market exchanges, print).
