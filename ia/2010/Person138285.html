Matt Ryan’s Summary

Matt has been an investor and entrepreneur since 1996 and is co-founder of Five Star Memories. An incentive travel company based in Evergreen, CO that works with fortune 500 companies.

Prior to starting Five Star Memories, Matt founded Network Management Systems in 1996. Revolutionary provider of on site financial trading systems that enabled retail clients to make instantaneous trades with Wall Street firms. Network Management Systems was acquired by Cornerstone Securities as part of Instinet's public offering in 2002.

Prior to Network Management Systems, Matt was a member of the Sales and Marketing teams at two start up software companies. The first, Online Financial in Atlanta pioneered the use of intelligent terminals in the financial industry before being acquired by Fuqua. The second, Byers Engineering also based in Atlanta was a leader in the CAD/CAM software market, primarily serving the Intergraph market.

Matt holds a BBA degree from the University of Georgia's Terry College of Business. Finance

Matt was raised in Colorado and graduated from East High in Denver before attending UGA in Athens. Matt is an avid cyclist and skier.
