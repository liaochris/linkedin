Jennifer Weis’s Summary

You haven’t done something until you’ve done it, then you’ve done it and can forever put it on your résumé. As proud as I am of my background and achievements, I am even more confident to face the unknown as learning is a perpetual part of life.

My technical skills are self-taught. I worked my way through Seattle University’s professional MBA program. Most fortuitously I’ve learned and grown through collaboration with engineers, QA, finance managers, accountants, system administrators, user experience designers, business developers and my exceptional colleagues in project and product management.

There is no magic formula. The technology landscape is perpetually shifting, and what worked for one project or product may not work for the next. But I am a champion of the customer, an adaptable manager, and a collaborative individual whose varied talents and flexible style will bring value to your business.
