Brad Janssen’s Summary

Airline Transport Pilot with over nine years of extensive flying experience. Over six years as an Aerial Survey pilot, planning and flying missions at various altitudes and terrain. Experience with several different aerial platforms to include: large format film cameras, digital cameras, and LIDAR.

Over two years as an airline First Officer with an FAR Part 121 Airline. Position was in the Northeastern United States flying at some of the nations busiest airports and most adverse weather.

Current Certified Flight Instructor with over 100 hours of flight instructing in single engine aircraft.

FAA licensed Aircraft Dispatcher with three years experience at two seperate FAR Part 121 Airlines. Experience with turboprop and jet regional aircraft and all regions of the United States. Trained and supervised personnel.
