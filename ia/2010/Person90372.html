Jason Drakeford’s Summary

Currently in New York working as Art Director of MTV and moonlighting as a film/music video director in Brooklyn. I am constantly pushing myself to create new original work and break the boundaries of storytelling through design.

My adventurous commercial career has spanned 12 years, working with design studios, advertising agencies, small independent studios and major tv networks creating a wide range of award winning creative work from short films, tv spots, music videos, motion graphics, online experiences, ad campaigns, to even opening up an art gallery.

Jason Drakeford’s Specialties:

Design, Motion Graphics, Illustration, Animation, Music Videos, Photography, Short films, Commercials, Interactive, Mobile Ad Campaigns (select work at www.jasondrakeford.com and current film/animation productions at www.drakefordfilms.com).
