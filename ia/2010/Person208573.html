Tom Robinson, CFA, CAIA, CFP®’s Experience

  • Managing Director, Education

    CFA Institute

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Financial Services industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years 7 months)

    Managing director of the Education Division at CFA Institute. Leads and develops the teams responsible for producing and delivering educational content and examinations to candidates, members and other investment professionals encompassing the CFA Program, CIPM Program, Lifelong Learning, Private Wealth, Publications, Conferences, and Executive Education.

    Previously Tom was an Associate Professor of Accounting and Director of the Master of Professional Accounting program at the University of Miami. He also was also Managing Director of a state registered Investment Advisory firm. Tom primarily taught Financial Statement Analysis, Personal Financial Planning and Valuation. He has a B.A. in Economics from the University of Pennsylvania and a Masters and PhD from Case Western Reserve University. Previously Tom practiced public accounting and financial planning for ten years, primarily with Deloitte & Touche and Pritchett Dlusky & Saxe in Columbus, Ohio.
