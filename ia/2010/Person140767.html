Melissa Mayntz’s Summary

Melissa Mayntz has been a freelance writer and creative editor since 2003. She creates clean, edited copy for both print and electronic publications, including features, keyword optimized (SEO) articles, profiles, news briefs, how-tos, and essays. As an editor, she specializes in helping other writers refine their work without sacrificing their individual style and voice through a "fresh eyes" perspective of editing that includes grammar, spelling, formatting, punctuation, flow, logic, and overall quality.

Melissa has written more than 2,000 articles for a variety of print and online markets, and she has presented at writers' conferences and workshops both locally and nationally.

For selected writing samples, a full resume and publication list, and project rates and details, visit www.MelissaMayntz.com.
