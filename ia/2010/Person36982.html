Chi Chi Okezie’s Summary

Chi Chi Okezie has always displayed originality and being eccentric which can date back to her early childhood of growing up in Brooklyn, NY. At a neighbor's birthday party, the girls were asked what they wanted to be when they grew up. Most repeated teachers, nurses, mothers, doctors or lawyers. Chi Chi, on the other hand replied, "A Ninja". Although, there were not many female Ninjas to model, Chi Chi could see the discipline, grace and beautiful art forms that were associated with being a skilled fighter against evil. This discipline, grace and art form, she incorporates into her style of work as she presents seminars, networks and builds relationships.

Chi Chi looks forward to building her business to higher heights and being creative in how she develops professionally as owner/producer of SIMPLEnetworking, LLC.
