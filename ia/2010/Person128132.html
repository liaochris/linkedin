Donna Ranieri’s Summary

- My goal as a lending specialist is simple; I want to help my clients develop methods with issues related to spousal buyouts and the marital residence during times of transition.

- At Wells Fargo, we offer our clients dedicated and experienced professionals to guide you during what could be potentially a very difficult time.

- As part of the collaborative law network, with our seasoned group of professionals, guiding you along the divorce process and addressing any issues related with your marital home is our top priority.

- With over 33 years experience in the banking industry, I have spent the last 12 years specializing in the residential mortgage area.

- My position at Wells Fargo allows me the opportunity to find a mortgage product to fit almost anyone's needs.

- Being a member and advisor to the Collaborative Law Group allows me the opportunity to work with some of the most respected and experienced attorneys and mental health professionals in the state of New Jersey.

- The main focus of my lending is to provide clear and accurate information early on in a divorce case.

- As a lending specialist, one of my primary goals is to free up equity in a client's home when they need it most. In addition, as part of the collaborative professional community, we all share the same goal of bringing a peaceful and amicable resolution to what could potentially be a very difficult situation.
