Tim Grice’s Summary

I have been an SEO and Internet Marketing addict for over 6 years now. My early experience involved running affiliate websites in the health and technology industries.

In 2007 I began to freelance as an SEO consultant working with small businesses to achieve results on a local and national level.

In 2009 I launched http://www.seowizz.net/ a blog dedicated to helping people understand SEO and apply simple techniques. I am strong believer that SEO does not need to be a dark art and can be understood with even a basic understanding.

SEOwizz was also a way for me to demonstrate what I could do and gave me the opportunity to work with multiple companies in the US, in the technology and real estate arenas. I also gained more experience working with UK based businesses in multiple verticals, all achieving the rankings and prominence in Google they desired.

Since SEOwizz has become a recognised SEO authority with a weekly readership of over 4000 and top rankings for keywords such as "search engine optimisation" and "google seo".

In 2010 I recognised a need to expand my experience and opted to take my skills to Branded3 to manage their day to day SEO processes.

Due to rapid growth and success in multiple verticals Branded3 is now a major player in the Search Arena and I agreed to the role as Head of Search where our constant aim is deliver industry leading results in SEO, PPC, Social Media Marketing and Affiliate Marketing.
