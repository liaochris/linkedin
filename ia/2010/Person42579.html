Colleen Lindsay’s Summary

Adaptable, innovative, and highly tech-savvy publicity/marketing professional with 13+ years experience spanning online and traditional book publicity, marketing, and brand management for publishing industries; 15+ years event planning and marketing in retail bookstores; expertise in building effective online presence for books, authors and imprint brands.

I have eleven years experience in marketing, publicity and brand management in publishing. For five years I served as Assistant Director of Publicity for Ballantine Books, a division of the Random House Publishing Group, specializing in science fiction, fantasy, pop culture, YA fantasy, graphic novels and third-party licensed media. Teaming with our directors of marketing and licensing, I helped build and manage relationships with our third-party licensors, coordinating marketing and media campaigns with such notable media clients as Sony Pictures, Lucasfilm LTD, Microsoft Xbox, New Line Cinema, Fox, and Turner Broadcasting.

I excelled in using the Internet as a viral marketing tool, utilizing blogs, message boards, chat rooms, street teams and e-newsletters to help break out Del Rey and LucasBooks authors, and to build the Del Rey Manga, Del Rey and LucasBooks brands.

Tech skills include: MAC & PC platforms; MS Word / Excel / PowerPoint / Outlook / Access / Entourage; Adobe Photoshop / Acrobat / InDesign; Quark Xpress; Publicity Assistant; Cision; Lexis-Nexis; SAP; FileMaker Pro; Bento; HTML 4.0; RSS & blogging tools, social media tools & platforms, Constant Contact and most listserv clients.
