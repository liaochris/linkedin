Michele Whitley’s Summary

As a native Atlantan with a family background in building & development, Michele has over 20 years experience in the Metro Atlanta area with sales & customer service.
Michele’s real estate career includes education & experience in the sale of residential homes, including luxury homes, commercial, & commercial leasing.

Michele has a list of satisfied & repeat clients who know that her professionalism & expertise are foremost in consistently superseding time & price expectations.
