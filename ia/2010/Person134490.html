Mark Robeson’s Summary

We began Global Microfinance Partners |GMP| with the simple vision of helping the poor around the world help themselves. In much the same way the "green movement" swept America in the mid-2000's, we believe the "microfinance movement" is next in corporate and church culture as Americans see that helping the poor is far more effective with a hand-up rather than a hand-out. Small (micro) loans and savings education services are the most effective way of doing that, in our opinion. Microfinance is our passion and we think it will become yours, as well.

GMP is an Asset Manager bringing the microfinance needs of the poor directly to the capital markets by making microfinance investment products available for institutions and individuals.

The need for microfinance globally is currently ten times the supply. As investors can realize investment returns while helping the poor we believe this investment category is set for an historic increase over current levels.
