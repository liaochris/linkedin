Peter Luyckx’s Summary

Peter Luyckx is the Managing Editor of Flip the Media, a blog written by students and professors in the University of Washington’s Master in Communication in Digital Media (MCDM) program. In this role, he has assembled a stellar team of subject experts, multimedia contributors, and editors. He also established editorial processes and guidelines, which aim to encourage and nurture the writing skills of tomorrow’s thought leaders in digital media.

Previously, Peter was a Web Producer and Editor at Microsoft’s MSN Shopping (now Bing) and MSN Health & Fitness.

Peter Luyckx’s Specialties:

Digital Media Strategy & Production, Web 2.0, social media, editorial strategy, storytelling that appeals to online audiences.
