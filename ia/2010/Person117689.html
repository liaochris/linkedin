Kristin Kash’s Summary

My employment qualifications were evident immediately upon graduation from college...degree in hand, restless, excited, fearless and driven. Luckily, those exact qualifications made me the ideal candidate for an Online Sales Executive at a little start up named CitySearch.com that just so happened to be in the market for a lot of newbie employees who would enjoy working long hours for peanuts in an office with a foosball table. With the dream of being part of something bigger down the road, we spent our time pitching the benefits of advertising on this new thing called the internet. I am thankful for that opportunity as those "qualifications" provided me the unique opportunity to join an industry during its infancy. Over the years I have worked with amazing people who have gone on to achieve great things both personally and professionally. I am grateful to have witnessed busineses and clients who reluctantly joined the craze back in the 90's and now are reaping the benefits of having robust, interactive, revenue generating websites. Much has changed since those days. I retired from Citysearch.com after a 6 year run and for the past 8+ years I have been working at Capitol Broadcasting Company. I started in the WRAL-TV division focusing on TV and Online Sales Promotions. 5 years ago I was drawn back to a a more specialized focus in Digital Marketing. As a Digital Specialist at CBC's New Media Group, I work with clients to create, design, launch and optimize comprehensive digital campaigns that result in measurable results. Client List Includes: Kane Realty Corporation, Wake Med Hospital, Kerr Drugs, Carolina Hurricanes-NHL, The Salvation Army, The American Kennel Club, ESPN 360, and NextCare Urgent Care.
