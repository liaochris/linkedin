David Grubman’s Summary

David Grubman has spent the last 4 years redefining the online investor experience at Financial Engines. As a Senior Product Manager on the Investor Experience team, David has driven the redesign of multiple key online components. As of late, he has focused his attentions on serving the income needs of investors in retirement.

Working for Financial Engines is the logical continuation of a 20 year career focused on solutions delivery in the Retirement Plan processing industry. Prior to Financial Engines, David led product, professional services and software development teams for the Birmingham Alabama based start-up, Pyramid Digital Solutions (INC 500 winner and successfully acquired by Sungard Data Systems). With Pyramid, David took multiple products from concept to market delivery.

David is a passionate product guy who defines products by truly understanding customer needs and delivering outstanding services. Once the products are built, David has had the pleasure to deliver hundreds of product presentations to customers, partners, prospects, media, and government audiences.

David is active in the local community by serving as a director for Silicon Valley Product Management Association and on a government advisory board. He lives in San Jose with his lovely wife and two daughters.
