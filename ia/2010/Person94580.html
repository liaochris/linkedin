Jenna Waites’s Summary

I specialize in helping others build their businesses and make money through stronger marketing and an increased online presence. I work with small groups and one-on-one to get people started with a user friendly web presence they can then use to market to their target audience and increase their revenue by creating individualized marketing strategies.

In 2006, I left engineering to find my true passion. I was lead into home sales and then real estate investing right in the middle of the crash and I found myself sitting in one of the worst housing markets in the country. The biggest problem I found was that everyone was having trouble selling their property. Trying to figure out a way to unlock the key to selling homes in a buyers market, lead me to study marketing tactics and embrace the methodology of guerrilla marketing and round robin auctions.

Having become successful with round robin auction in my local market, I created a consulting business to teach home owners, Realtors and other real estate investors how to successfully implement this process to sell their houses quickly.

At the beginning of 2009, I was introduced to the world of internet marketing through a local weekly mastermind group. Understanding how our marketplace is changing rapidly and the power of the internet, I immediately dove into learning how utilize the internet to enhance my own business.

This has lead me to the business I am in today, working with entrepreneurs and small business owners to teach them the marketing tactics I've learned and how to implement them to increase their businesses. Running my own internet marketing mastermind group has also allowed me to educate local entrepreneurs by bring in experts to share their specialized knowledge and experiences.
