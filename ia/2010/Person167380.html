Rachel Pradhan’s Summary

Blaze Interactive, an award-wining web design and internet marketing company in Orange County, California. Our internet marketing and social media marketing company helps small businesses in the OC build word of mouth marketing campaigns through the web. We start with the 'home base' - the website. Our websites are custom build with Wordpress technology so that they're easily found by Google, and easy to edit by our clients, yet build a brand that sets our clients apart from their competition. We also provide our small business clients with blogs, enewsletters, podcasts and online video.

Indra Successful Coach Marketing is an internet marketing company for business and executive coaches.

Our ongoing support includes social media marketing coaching, monthly internet marketing maintenance at low rates, per project and hourly rates.

Great Power Partners for us are Marketing Consultants, Copy Writers, Virtual Assistants, and Graphic Designers.
