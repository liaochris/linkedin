Wendy Ackland’s Summary

With more than 12 years experience in professional marketing practice, Wendy’s background is varied and crosses several industries. Most notably, she was employed as Marketing Strategist with News Limited’s The Cairns Post, having been introduced to market research with the Channel TEN Network.

Her experience is not limited to media however. She has also enjoyed roles in Private Health and the Not-For-Profit sector, and managed the sales process for the Cairns port of Australian air Express.

In recognition of her skills and experience, Wendy was awarded Certified Practicing Marketer (CPM) status by the Australian Marketing Institute in 2009. CPM is the peak professional benchmark for Marketers in Australia.

Wendy now owns and operates BurntToast® Marketing, an agency that specialises in marketing strategy and data interpretation for small business. The business was founded in 2007 and provides tailored and professional services to a range of industries.
