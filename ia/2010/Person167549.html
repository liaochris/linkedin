Radley Moss’s Summary

I have more than 11 years of public relations and communications experience.

Before starting my current, in-house position at IntraLinks, I was with MWW Group, one of the nation's top 10 PR firms. At MWW, I led the Adecco Group account, as well as managing the Samsung Consumer Electronics Division business and providing corporate communications support to Samsung.

I was previously a PR consultant to TheInfoPro, a research network and leading supplier of market intelligence for the IT industry. Prior to that, I was with Text 100, a global PR agency. I led the Gartner team throughout my entire time at Text 100, was on the eBay business, and also managed a number of other corporate, consumer and technology accounts. Additionally, I was responsible for working closely with the Text 100 CEO and senior team in promoting the agency's brand visibility.

Before that, I spent 18 months with Publicis Dialog, a large PR agency, where my clients included Consumer Electronics Association, Coldwell Banker, OKI Data and Nikko Hotels. Finally, my first job out of college was with a boutique PR and corporate communications agency for five years where I worked on corporate, consumer, technology, lifestyle and nonprofit accounts.

Radley Moss’s Specialties:

Account leadership (includes global coordination), implementing communications strategies/programs, managing budgets, counseling senior executives, developing both external and internal key messages, team leadership and people management, written and verbal communications skills, developing awards programs, media relations, supporting events/tradeshows, social media, new business savvy, excellent presentation skills, and developing marketing collateral (newsletters, direct mail, etc.).
