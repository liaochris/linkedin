Megan Le’s Summary

I am an MBA graduate from ESADE in Barcelona, Spain, a school with a very international focus and student body. Beyond my core classes, I concentrated my studies in Marketing, Strategy and Corporate Responsibility (Social Entrepreneurship was my favorite class!).

I'm searching for an opportunity to work with a results-driven organization that shares my same values of using business for good, and that focuses on a triple bottom line of people, planet AND profit. Due to my background, I can quickly learn technical industries and products, am an expert in start-ups, and also have experience working with and for non-profits.

I am very interested in taking a holistic approach to international development and solving our biggest social and environmental issues through social business. Because of this passion, I am constantly learning about the limitations of government and traditional aid, and how fair trade, technology, micro-credit, education, corporate responsibility etc can all work together to address the issues surrounding global poverty. I would love to solve business problems for the Corporate Responsibility department of a corporation, a fair trade products company (coffee? chocolate? clothing?) or for a micro-finance institution. In addition, I hope one day to have the skills and guts to be a social entrepreneur...
