Caroline Maerten’s Summary

I have a 10-year experience in online marketing services within some of the leading Belgium web agencies. In August 2006 I launched Rolling Talks (www.rollingtalks.com) to explain to companies how social media conversations are impacting their business. I started consultancy on my favorite topic a year later.

Today, I help international companies develop a strategic framework to integrate Social Media platforms within their communication strategy. Some of my clients: Attentio (European monitoring company), Belgacom, Cherry&Cake (agency), Citibank, Cleverwood, Club Med, Concerto (agency), Eurostar, European Commission, European Parliament, Infrabel, Interel (PR agency), Nivea, Mostra (agency), Microsoft, Taatu, The House of Marketing, Wolters Kluwer

I'm also contributing to educate the Belgian market through several national marketing publications and associations (Inside Internet, Media Marketing, Digimedia, Belgian PR Center). Since September 2008, I'm leading the Belgium IAB Social Media Task Group.

Caroline Maerten’s Specialties:

Social Media workshops (from beginners know-how to practical implementation).
