Jed A. Reay’s Summary

I am CEO/Founder of LifeStyles Unlimited, Co. and R&R InfoSystems, Co. A corporate development and training company, and an information/educational service firm (respectfully).

A 27 year veteran of the health care industry, I also hold/held a position as a manufactures rep with Biotronik, Inc. and previously, St. Jude Medical, Inc. and Intermedics, Inc.

I have taken the 27 years of health care experience of working with Cardiologist, Surgeons, hospitals, and hospital administers and am applying it to aiding the industry in re-envisioning change to a more pro-active health care system. With a background in negotiation, and mediation (University of Oregon) I have helped to develop and continue to train corporations and organization in developing streamline profitable systems in the area of health care business development, as well as the bio-engineering field.

I am an author, working on book 2. The first book addressed the importance of communication in the corporate environment. "Why Can't We Say What We Mean: Developing Meaningful Businesses Relationships Through Effective Communication."

The 2nd book will address the more esoteric concept of Thought Transformation. It will be co-authored with my colleague, and business partner Christine Stiller (corporate trainer with a deep background in the Canadian health care system).

Goals: Speaker, Transformational Thought Leader, Author, Trainer, Health Care Transformer, Radio and TV host,
