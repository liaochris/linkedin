Amy Hoppenrath’s Summary

AccelerAction is the only agency in Kansas City dedicated to fueling successful professional services firms by creating powerful branding messages, developing strategic lead generation campaigns and driving business development programs.

Our clients are smart. They are true professionals who are excellent at the technical aspects of their businesses but realize they need help with their marketing and business development efforts.

We offer:
► MARKETING CONSULTING
Do you want to focus your time on what you do best? Are you looking for professionals who will not only develop your marketing plan but implement it as well? Our four-step process helps you build awareness, generate leads and attract new clients.

► MARKETING MENTORING PROGRAMS
Enjoy doing your own marketing but just want an experienced marketer to help direct and guide you? That’s exactly what we do. We work with both individuals and firms to help them get past the roadblocks and accomplish their goals.

► SOCIAL NETWORKING TRAINING
Do you have an online profile? You have made connections and wonder what to do I next. AccelerAction provides social networking training to help individuals and organizations learn how to increase their online visibility and credibility while building relationships.

AccelerAction was created to help professional services firms fuel their marketing, branding and lead development efforts. Our principals, Amy Hoppenrath and Kristin Wing, have built marketing departments, coached professionals to develop selling skills, created strategic marketing plans, helped clients understand how to use social media and launched powerful public relations plans. We work strategically, value your time and deliver the results you need to move your firm forward.
