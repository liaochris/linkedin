Shannon Snowden’s Summary

I am a senior level IT project consultant. My business management educational background coupled with my project experience and project management knowledge allows me to successfully deliver complex, time-sensitive projects. It's not unusual for my previous clients to ask for me by name to return to participate in their most challenging projects.

I have significant experience with virtualization technologies and have always been an early adopter of the technologies as evidenced by my (VMWare Certified Professional) VCP # 12. Additionally, I was the only certified XenSource Trainer teaching classes in North America on version 3.2 and 4.0. I did the Train the Trainer classes for Citrix for XenServer 4.0 and 4.1 and I'm teaching the classes for XenServer 5 for Citrix.

Shannon Snowden’s Specialties:

Often responsible for designing and implementing large physical and virtual migration projects using project management skills and technologies from various virtualization vendors including VMWare, Citrix XenServer, Platespin.
