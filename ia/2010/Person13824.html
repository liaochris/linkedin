Ann Adams’s Summary

Communication consultant who develops and implements global communication programs that drive business success, including communicating leadership transitions, spin-offs, acquisitions, litigation, culture change, new products, employee benefits and restructurings. Education and experience in both mass communication and law.

As former vice president of global internal communication and marketing for Motorola, Inc., led international team that boosted employee engagement and business IQ, strengthened the employee brand, increased operating efficiency and communicated significant and ongoing business changes.

Also served as public relations director for The Dial Corp, an international consumer products and services company; manager of corporate communications for Kerr-McGee Corp., a major independent energy company; and head of the public relations division of an advertising and marketing communication agency.

Ann Adams’s Specialties:

Strategic communication planning, internal communication and marketing, writing and editing, executive communication, crisis/issues/change management, public relations, community relations, law
