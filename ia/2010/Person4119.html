Ahmad Sulaiman’s Summary

The Law Offices of Sulaiman & Associates is a Consumer Right’s Law Firm which specializes in consumer credit transactions with a focus on loan workout agreements and mortgage related litigation. The Firm is dedicated to defending the rights of Borrowers in their relationship with Lenders and Collection Companies in their attempt to collect from Borrowers on potentially overreaching consumer credit transactions. The Firm has become the industry standard for an effective, results oriented Consumer Right’s Law Firm.

The goal of the Firm is to either negotiate with or litigate against unscrupulous Lenders and Collection Companies in search of the most equitable outcome for our Clients. The Firm works to ensure that our Clients receive the best legal advice available to guarantee that our Clients make informed decisions as it relates to their legal rights and obligations in all consumer transactions.

Lenders often charge phony fees, excessively high interest rates and more often than you would guess completely ignore the Federal and State Laws that exist to protect consumers. So entrenched is this disregard for the law, consumers, even when aware of the violations, believe that it is just part of being a consumer.

Our forensic audit team will analyze your mortgage and other credit related documents for potential Federal, State and Common Law violations. Such violations, if found, may allow you to turn around and demand a full accounting of your rights against the Lenders, putting the Lenders and their affiliates on defense. Depending on which laws were broken, the Lender may be required to pay all your legal fees to help you protect your property and get back what was illegally taken from you. As always the best DEFENSE is a great OFFENSE.
