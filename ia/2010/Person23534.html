Bigyan Kar’s Summary

Experienced GIS (Geographical Information Systems) manager with over four years of international business development / sales experience and another seven years of project / program management, technical analysis experience in the areas of geospatial mapping and software services / solutions for various verticals such as Utilities (Electric, Oil/Gas, Telecommunications, Water, Waste water), Municipalities (Land Administration, Public Administration), GPS Navigation sector (Road, Trail, Sports), GIS data sellers, Map publishing companies and Private sector clients.

Have hands on experience in analysis, design and development of Geographical Information Systems with in-depth knowledge on ESRI, Autodesk suite of products and working knowledge on MapInfo, Bentley, Open Source technologies and other online mapping tools. Exposure to different types of projects like land base, cadastral / parcel mapping, AM/FM utility mapping, navigational GIS map creation, electrical / gas pipeline networking, golf course mapping, Web GIS, customized geospatial application development and ability to implement GIS concepts on any other projects.

Proven track record with accomplishments in penetrating into new markets, new clients development, analyzing market and competitions, implementing effective marketing strategies to achieve business goals, negotiating, closing complex partnership deals, client relationship management, planning and scheduling, technological design and project / program management.

Goal and outcome oriented professional with the unique ability of operating with self-initiative approach and to perform within complex environments in order to reach full spectrum sales and business goals.

Trilingual: Fluent in English, Hindi and beginner level in Spanish (Español)
