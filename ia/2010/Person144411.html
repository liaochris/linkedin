Michelle Langston’s Summary

Michelle Langston has just completed a Master of Science in Information Science at The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where she focused on web design. Her interests lie in web standards, usability, accessibility and search engine optimization. She is also interested in open source content management systems and how such systems can serve as cost-effective alternatives to commercial platforms.

Michelle's strongest skill set consists of XHTML, CSS, web interface design and web standards. In addition, she has extensive experience building themes for the WordPress platform. She has a working knowledge of PHP, MySQL and JavaScript libraries such as jQuery and Prototype, and is actively working to increase her proficiency in all of these areas.

Currently, Michelle is seeking full time employment as a web designer. She is also developing Citizen Press, an open source newspaper theme framework for WordPress. The subject of her Master's thesis project, Citizen Press will help small community newspapers and school newspapers to build great sites on the WordPress platform.

Michelle Langston’s Specialties:

XHTML, CSS, Web Standards, Content Management Systems (installation, configuration and customization), WordPress theme development, Adobe Photoshop, Social Media. Working knowledge of PHP, MySQL and JavaScript libraries (jQuery, Prototype, Moo Tools).
