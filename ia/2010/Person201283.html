Terry K. Harrigan [LION 2350+]’s Summary

Terry Harrigan has worked in the mortgage industry since 1999. She started by working with people in foreclosure and doing difficult loans for people in need. She currently does loans for all types of clients, specializing in helping people who have poor credit, no credit or those who are in bankruptcy or foreclosure. She feels that most anyone can help a client who has good credit close their loan. The key to this is being able to compete on rate and having excellent customer service.

Prior to the mortgage industry, Terry worked as a programmer in the computer field for 15 years for Accenture, Anderson Consulting, Merrill Lynch, and Brooks Fashions.

Her career prior to this was working in a psychiatric unit at St. Vincent's Hospital where she did individual and family therapy. She met her husband Bob while working at the hospital (he was a staff member!) and they worked together there for 5 years.

Terry Harrigan graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Hofstra University in Long Island, NY. She took computer science classes in the evening while working full-time in order to effectively change her career from mental health to computer programmer. Later, she simultaneously worked and educated herself in the mortgage industry before leaving the computer field.

Terry takes a consulting approach when dealing with clients, and works with each person to carefully determine the best program for them.

Terry created Regal Home Enterprises, LLC as a way to help people who absolutely MUST sell their homes.
