Ferran Moreno’s Summary

Ferran Moreno is VP of Business Development at ExperienceOn Ventures, where he holds maximum responsibility for the mobile location based service Bliquo, which in its debut year has achieved more than 70.000 users just in Spain, being available both in iPhone and Android, already in monetization mode with its online bookings at restaurants and about to be expanded to the UK. Ferran has an outstanding track record building startups for internet and telecom sectors.

Previously, Ferran founded Whisher.com, a patented software solution to get free WiFi access worldwide. He managed to transform an idea into a business, raising two rounds of funding from world-class venture capital investors, such as Benchmark Capital Europe (now Balderton Capital) and Swisscom. Ferran succesfully sold the business to Seattle-based Wifi.com LLC, participated by venture capital investor Monster Ventures Partners.
Prior to that, Ferran was the Managing Director of Air Bites in Spain, the first European Residential WiFi Operator, where he founded the business on behalf of Swisscom, providing deep technical insight and contributing to scaling the business internationally. After buying back the business from its previous owners, he managed to turn around the company, reducing costs and increasing revenues, putting the business in the black and finally selling it to a Grupo Monsan holding.

Ferran started his career in the Telecom industry, in wireless and Internet consulting, for national incumbents such as Swisscom (Switzerland) and Retevision (Spain). His natural curiosity for solving problems made him file several patents in different domains.

Ferran has been educated across Europe and graduated with honors from UPC (Universitat Politecnica de Cataluña) with an MSc in Telecommunications Engineering in Barcelona. He also holds executive education (MBA program) from the London Business School in UK.
