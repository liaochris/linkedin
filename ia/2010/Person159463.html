Pat Anderson’s Summary

“When the government fears the people there is liberty; when the people fear the government there is tyranny.” — Thomas Jefferson

An active State Auditor is what I was, and an active State Auditor is what I will be. I have a proven track record, attested to by the fact that in my reelection bid I was endorsed over the current auditor by virtually every newspaper in the state. Even those papers that regularly editorialized that my “essential services” policy was too stringent agreed that on my watch the State Auditor’s office was in effective and competent hands.

In today’s financial climate, every penny really does count, but I will be a State Auditor that does more than look for loose change under the couch. I will be an active State Auditor that uses the office to push for the necessary reforms Minnesota must make to be a competitive and prosperous state. Minnesotans expect no less from any elected official. And I expect no less from myself.

Pat Anderson’s Specialties:

The executive experience to be our next State Auditor with emphasis on renewed individual liberty and fiscal responsibility - within a transparent and limited government.

Pat Anderson’s Experience

  • MN State Auditor Candidate 2010

    PatAndersonforAuditor.com

    (Political Organization industry)

    January 2010 — Present (3 months)

    A proven leader and state government innovator, Pat Anderson was elected to serve as Minnesota’s 17th State Auditor. She is a former Mayor and council-member in Eagan, and served as the last Commissioner of the Department of Employee Relations – a department she eliminated in 2008 in an effort to promote greater efficiency in government. She also served as the President of the MN Free Market Institute.
    A successful business person, Pat started and later sold her company which grew to over 75 employees under her leadership. She received numerous recognitions and awards for both her business and public service over the years and was endorsed by every major newspaper for re-election in 2006.
    Only Pat Anderson has the life and professional experience that uniquely qualifies her to serve most effectively as our State Auditor. Pat will return thoughtful, common-sense management to the Office of the State Auditor. She will again implement inventive reforms to ensure a proactive approach to government oversight. With Pat Anderson back as State Auditor, the citizens of Minnesota are guaranteed not only a determined fiscal watchdog, but a battle-tested leader guided by a core of prudent, conservative principals.
