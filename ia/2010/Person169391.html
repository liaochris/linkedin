    Raven At Your Service!!! was developed as a foundation for our service-oriented entrepreneurial ventures, where our mission remains focused on giving back by offering goods, services & resources that enhance the lives of those seeking positive change. Our portfolio of services also includes marketing, business development and social media assistance for small business owners in need of recapturing their valuable time, so that they may focus on what they do best!

    The Gifted Pet came to be back in August of 2005 as an “Aha!” moment, while witnessing the disasters that were Hurricanes Katrina & Rita and a deep, burning desire to do more for animal rescue & nature conservancy. This enables pet lovers everywhere to shop for unique items for their pets or themselves and know that a generous portion of their purchase will benefit various animal rescue & nature conservancy organizations. We strive to find the most playful, fun, interesting & unique items that are pet, people and Earth friendly and offer them to you at a reasonable price. Shop Where Your Purchase Matters @ The Gifted Pet!

    It's My Michigan is an amazing promotional program developed by Pat & Dennis Kaiser. . . two entrepreneurial brothers who had a vision for a better Michigan. The business membership entails a State-wide program that provides one full year of advertising, a customer discount card that virtually pays for your ad and a ZERO effort income exclusively for Michigan owned & operated businesses. The customer discount card is for consumers who wish to support those Michigan owned & operated businesses and to help us all be part of the solution, instead of part of the problem. This helps to keep money, jobs and revenue flowing in Michigan! There is also an awesome fundraising program for schools or any non-profits looking for a no-cost and simple way to raise funds for their organization and support Michigan in the process. How cool is that?!
