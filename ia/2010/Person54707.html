Dennis started digital awareness agency Words of Wisdom in 2008, through which he offers training, consultancy, coaching and interim services in the field of online marketing-(communications).

He has written several publications on subjects like interactive experience marketing, gamevertising and viral marketing in among others the Webdesk Online Marketing 2004 (THE Dutch source book for online marketing).

Furthermore he regularly gives lectures at universities and congresses on among others digital (marketing)communications, the usage of opinion leaders in interactive marcom and interactive Direct Marketing (iDM).

In 2006 Dennis was one of the three nominees for the DeMeter Award 2006. A prize to encourage young Dutch Direct Marketing talent.

And last but not least he is a member of two boards of advisors. He is a member of the board of advisors of the ´Institute for Interactive Media´. Part of the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. And he is member of the board of advisors of ´Generation Next´. A Dutch association for students in commercial studies.
