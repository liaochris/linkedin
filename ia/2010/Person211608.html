Victor Pitts’s Summary

Having led Moniker’s Sales organization as Vice President of Sales through a five year period of unparalleled growth and industry leadership, Victor now leads Moniker’s Premium Domain Broker Group with the industries next generation of domain asset brokerage services. Victor has considerable experience in every major aspect of the online spectrum, having provided every type of service from Web site development services, to hosting, to infrastructure hardware, to Search Engine Optimization, to domain name services. Victor is an established leader and voice of the online community as demonstrated by the frequent demand for him as a speaker at business and industry tradeshows and by his hosting the popular Domain Masters radio show in syndication on major podcast locations such as iTunes, Google, and Yahoo. These experiences make Victor well connected and well versed in the language of domain investors, corporate leaders, small business owners, and entrepreneurs. All important factors in choosing a domain broker where excellent communication skills and insightful knowledge are key.

Victor Pitts’s Specialties:

Victor is a subject matter expert on global domain portfolio management, domain sales and acquisitions, and domain traffic monetization. Victor is the host of the popular Internet radio program called Domain Masters featured on webmasterradio.fm.
