Ajay Tipnis’s Summary

As Chief Technologist and Enterprise Consultant Ajay provides architecture and technical leadership to leverage existing investments, accelerate time-to-market, streamline business processes, improve customer satisfaction, increase market share and promote self-service.

17+ years of multi-industry, enterprise consulting and delivery experience with thought leadership and proven track record in implementing Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Web Service Management, SOA Security and Enterprise Portal applications using cutting edge technology solutions. Key contributor in many business development/sales pursuit/new business generation activities in both commercial and government segments.

An accomplished Enterprise Architect with many years of multinational experience and has managed all facets of software development life cycle. Served as Lead Architect/Technical Leader on various prestigious client engagements. As a SME (subject matter expert) in the Web Service Management and SOA Security and Enterprise Portals, pioneered a few key initiatives within EDS as well as for EDS clients. Ajay has rare skills of a project manager, enterprise consultant and lead architect with hands on experience in deploying enterprise solutions.
