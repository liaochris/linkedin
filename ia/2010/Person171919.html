Ricardo Robles’s Summary

In 2008 I graduated from Sacramento State with a Business Entrepreneurship degree. While finishing my degree I helped open a mortgage office and was practicing real estate as a Loan Officer. I also jumped into a startup (Capsity Offices) with a business partner managing executive suites and coworking spaces. During my time at Capsity I focused on Marketing/PR, Customer Service and Event Planning. Some events that I have planned and organized included Global Entrepreneurship Week, Social Media Presentations and Workshops (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.), Sacramento TweetUps, and other various business networking events. In November 2008 I started the Kaufman Foundation's Global Entrepreneurship Week in Sacramento which gained global attention by the second year it was hosted (November 2009). Now I am involved in online media marketing through website development and social media platforms.

In the past I have also been actively involved with networking organizations such as the Sacramento Hispanic Chamber of Commerce, Sacramento Asian Pacific Chamber of Commerce, and National Society for Hispanic Professionals. Currently on the board of Entrepreneurs Organization of Sacramento.
