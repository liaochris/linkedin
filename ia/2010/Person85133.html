Bob Eckert’s Summary

For the last 15 years, I've been working primarily with large transnationals to foster increased innovation in their cultures, people, leadership practices, products, services and processes. A very diverse background over the last 30 years has been instrumental in training me to work in a wide range of settings with sticky, complex dynamics. Essentially, I'm in the business of helping people be more effective in their work and lives via whatever method works. I am, or have been, a: Psychotherapist,Neuropharmacology trainer, wilderness guide, manufacturing facility owner, retail manager, tree farmer, innovation specialist, husband and dad.
My primary goal is to continue in the same direction, fostering improved, more innovative people and organizations.

On the side, for six weeks each spring, I produce some of the most incredible tasting Organic Maple Syrup that you'll ever treat yourself with.

Bob Eckert’s Specialties:

Facilitating meetings that require a deft touch, or an innovative outcome, Building Innovation Teams, Coaching leaders to build high performance organizations, Training groups to work together more productively and creatively, Facilitating New Product Development Sessions, Delivering energetic, impactful Keynotes at conferences and events.
