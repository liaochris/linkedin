Ivan Scannell’s Summary

Ivan is a leading male model who has been featured in both Australian and International magazines. Having just arrived back in Australia after modeling in the US (LA, New York and Miami), Ivan met with a number of clothing designers, Photographers, Agencies (Wilhelmina, Ford, Next, Adam), 2(x)ist underwear (new York) and shot for a collection of magazines ie. Mens Fitness.

Since being crowned Mr Supermodel Australia in 2006, he has had the pleasure of working with some of Australia's top photographers and designers, proving his versatility in editorial, runway and high fashion.

Ivan is also a superb athlete and will look to represent Australia at the 2012 Olympic Games in London as a track sprinter.

In 2004, Ivan received a sports scholarship to Macquarie University, Sydney, where he completed a bachelor degree in Commerce Accounting and Corporate Law. With a number of years experience in Financial planning, Ivan had the fortitude to leave the corporate realm and begin to explore more entrepreneurial ventures.

Since then, Ivan has established two flourishing companies: the thriving psychology practice, Sydney Psychology & Wellbeing and International Opulence, a successful niche consulting firm that specializes in the provision of controlled financial solutions. It consults exclusively to Health clubs and personal trainers, in the multi-billion dollar Health and Wellness industry.

Due to Ivan’s ongoing success and well established networks he will be looking to expand these companies globally in 2010 and begin developing a fashion label. In his down time, Ivan enjoys surfing, playing his beloved guitar and enhancing the services he provides in both his companies.
