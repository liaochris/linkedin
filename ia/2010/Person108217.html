Julia Shackelford’s Summary

I am a newbie to the e-learning and serious games industry, an aspiring author and auteur, and a life learner. During college I explored both the industry and experimental aspects of film and video as well as the importance of storytelling and the written word.

I am a recent graduate from the University of Texas at Austin with a BS in Radio, Television, and Film and a BA in English. My college work included an emphasis in screenwriting and television production. I have written, produced, and directed numerous experimental works as well as a number a student films. I acted as Producer and Writer for Movie Junkies, a student television show at TSTV, from 2004 to 2007 and performed the roles of Director and Executive Producer for the last two years. I also participated in numerous graduate, undergraduate, and professional productions around Austin involving both digital and analog media.

I am currently working towards expanding my own talents as a writer and film maker.
