Laura Clancy’s Summary

From 2003 to 2005 I was Associate Director of Corporate and Foundation Relations with the national office of Big Brothers Big Sisters. I then entered Columbia Business School while working halftime as a development and strategy consultant at New Visions for Public Schools, best known as the main conduit for Gates education funding for New York City. Over the summer, I worked at Community Resource Exchange, an amazing management consulting firm focusing on grassroots nonprofits. For two years, I worked as a consultant at The Bridgespan Group, a spinoff from Bain offering management consulting to nonprofits. My clients included the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, the Edna McConnell Clark Foundation, the Annie E. Casey Foundation, the Boston Foundation, College Summit, Communities in Schools, and Center for Health Care Strategies. Today, I am serving as Managing Director and Boston Executive Director of New Sector Alliance.
