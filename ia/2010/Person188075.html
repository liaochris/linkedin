♦ DAVE SHAVER: Entrepreneur │ HL7 Expert │ Healthcare Integration

I hope to help others on LinkedIn by sharing my network. If you discover something in my profile that might help to you, please let me know. I would be eager to help.

My passion is integrating healthcare applications and that brought me to be CTO, President, and Founder of Corepoint Health (previously NeoTool), a company specializing in healthcare software integration and interfacing.

Formed in 1997, Corepoint Health has a singular focus on healthcare systems integration and offers software solutions, consulting, and training for a broad variety of healthcare application interfacing.

We specialize in HL7 and healthcare-related integration software. My background includes teaching thousands of people the HL7 messaging standard.

My goal is leading a team that constantly strives to make interfacing more efficient and cost-effective. More than 2,500 users worldwide have selected our solutions for their HL7 interfacing and training needs.

Our customers include healthcare providers (e.g., hospitals, imaging centers, labs, and clinics), healthcare software application providers, and medical device manufacturers.

Prior to founding Corepoint Health, I was Chief Architect and Manager of Development at Quovadx (now Healthvision). While at Quovadx, I was the Chief Architect of the Cloverleaf product.

Prior to entering the world of healthcare integration in 1993, I was a Software Engineer at Convex Computer Corp., a supercomputer company now a division of Hewlett-Packard, where I was responsible for UNIX kernel development.

I am an Iowa native and received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa.

HL7 is certainly no different -- it is hard work and a tough balancing act.
