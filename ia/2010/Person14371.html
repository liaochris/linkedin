Anne Wahrmund’s Summary

More than 35 years of experience in management of information technology focused on business applications development, with over 30 years of leadership experience. I am especially adept in team leadership and people development, while being effective in group facilitation, cross-organization coordination, problem solving, business analysis and consensus building.

I have a gift for being able to turn visionary concepts into working solutions.

• Created new organizations to introduce & support new technologies. Significantly shaped the successful introduction of internet for Weyerhaeuser in the 90’s.

I find joy in working with people, and am please to know that many of those I have coached and mentored are now in successful leadership careers.

I contribute through volunteer work.
