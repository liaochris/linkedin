Stephen Mount’s Summary

Stephen Mount is a business consultant and entrepreneur based in Salford Quays (Manchester) with a portfolio of business investments, property and successful joint ventures.

Stephen is aiming to regenerate the North West business scene and drive more business to Greater Manchester. He brings his no nonsense approach to business and expertise to small businesses with fewer than 50 employees.

His business interests of SM5 include media production, manned security and website development. He has a team of 31 virtual employees. He is a networking fanatic and insists that businesses use networking as due diligence to find new suppliers, clients and businesses to collaborate with.
