Scott Sheldon’s Summary

Scott started his sales career in 1977 selling consumer goods door-to-door. He took a nine year break from sales to serve his country in the United States Air Force. Scott returned to professional sales in the business-to-business arena in 1987.

Selling Power Magazine honored Scott with its sales achievement award in 1988 - naming him one of the Top Ten Salespeople of the year for 1988.

Since that time, Scott has sold more than $19,870,000.00 in marketing services and programs. Those programs have resulted in more than $3,974,000,000.00 in sales by his clients.

Scott has managed and trained dozens of salespeople in the art of consultative selling. Industries include insurance, medical equipment, computer software, advertising, luxury apparel, furniture manufacturing, national defense, professional services, engineering and architecture, financial services, food services, restaurant supply, hospitality, and trade show services.

In 1994 Scott began consulting with companies to profitably increase their revenue using his ingenious marketing and sales programs. In that time, he’s worked with dozens of companies in more than 38 industries – from small local businesses to multi-national corporations.

Currently Scott spends 60% of his time writing B2B copy for his clients and 40% advising and/or developing marketing programs to generate leads and acquire new customers for them.
