Geri (Cassidy) Allen’s Summary

I am responsible for the strategic leadership of Pepperidge Farm’s corporate and brand communications, especially the management of media relations programs that enhance the visibility and positive reputation of Pepperidge Farm and its brands at the local and national levels.

Prior to Pepperidge Farm, I worked at several major public relations companies including Edelman in Chicago, and DeVries, Ogilvy, Ketchum and The Rowland Company all in New York City. I have been in the public relations industry for more than 13 years.
