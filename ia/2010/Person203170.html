Thomas Stirling’s Summary

I have an extensive background when it comes to designing for the world wide web. With well over 150 web designs under my belt, I have worked on a broad spectrum of projects that range from sites for growing businesses all the way up to major brands such as Mercedes Benz, Toyota and Lamborghini.

I was born raised and educated in Glasgow Scotland and gained a H.N.D in Visual Information at the Glasgow College of Building & Printing and then a BSc in Graphic Design from Glasgow Caledonian University.

I am a valid green card holder with United States & United Kingdom work authorization.

My expertize ranges from creating initial client mock ups to final coding and implementation of SEO. My main focus is to design websites that are marketing-focused to drive client revenue.

Thomas Stirling’s Specialties:

Front End Web Design, XHTML, CSS, Flash, Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Image Ready, Fireworks, Illustrator, Soundforge, SEO, Traditional Artwork, RSS Feeds, Wordpress Blogs, Artisteer, Social Media & Networking Expertise.

I am able to work with Javascript, PHP and have also worked with MySQL
