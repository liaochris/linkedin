Kevin Doyle’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    PE-Nexus

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    July 2010 — Present (1 month)

    PE-Nexus is an exclusive online community for M&A participants. Founded by former investment banking, private equity, and corporate finance professionals, PE-Nexus is a groundbreaking business development, direct marketing, and professional networking tool for mergers & acquisitions participants. It is the first site of its kind to seamlessly integrate a secure database of qualified middle market deal opportunities with a versatile professional networking platform. Combined, these resources offer a unique value proposition: to enhance deal sourcing, deal and firm marketing, and principal-to-principal communication among members, while saving them precious time, money, and resources. Visit www.pe-nexus.com to learn more.

  • Associate

    HIG Capital

    (Privately Held; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    June 2009 — June 2010 (1 year 1 month)

    H.I.G. Capital is a leading global private investment firm with over $7.5 billion of capital under management. H.I.G.'s family of funds includes private equity, venture capital, distressed debt, real estate and public equities. H.I.G. has invested in and managed more than 200 companies worldwide since its founding in 1993, with combined sales in excess of $8 billion. H.I.G. has offices in Miami, Boston, Atlanta, New York and San Francisco in the U.S., and affiliate offices in London, Hamburg, and Paris in Europe.

  • Vice President

    Cross Keys Capital

    (Privately Held; Investment Banking industry)

    January 2008 — May 2009 (1 year 5 months)

    Cross Keys Capital is an investment banking firm dedicated to serving middle-market companies, with offices located in Ft. Lauderdale, Chicago, Philadelphia, Baltimore and Tampa. The firm's business is mergers and acquisitions, and the financing of such transactions primarily with institutions and industry participants. Cross Keys is focused on divestitures and raises of growth capital for profitable, privately-held firms with equity valuations from $10-$300 million.

  • Director

Worked with and advised many international, blue chip organizations including: AEGON, BASF, Lloyds TSB, Royal Dutch Shell, Shinsei Bank.
