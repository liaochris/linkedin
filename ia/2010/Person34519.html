Cris Challender’s Summary

Accomplished corporate Learning and Organizational Development leader, and dynamic presenter and speaker. As a learning organization leader I have developed and managed corporate learning departments, launched a profitable e-learning business, developed custom learning and performance solutions, delivered hundreds of highly acclaimed presentations, and contributed to the success of organizations crossing the barriers of multiple industries.

Following are some my career highlights:

• Chief architect and project manager for the development and launch of a successful multi-million dollar revenue generating e-learning business.
• Developed a comprehensive technical skill development and professional certification program for technicians in gaming technology.
• Constructed and implemented a full service corporate learning department, WMS University.
• Secured tens of thousands in state funded grants for workforce development.
• Developed and launched successful leadership development programs, including the WMS Leadership Institute.
• Managed widely distributed teams of training professionals.
• Developed and implemented industry leading product education programs.
• Implemented large and small scale Learning Management Systems.
• Managed organizational learning budgets greater than $1 million.
• Implemented and managed organizational performance improvement initiatives.
• Designed and implemented an innovative new employee job orientation program that resulted in reducing new employee learning curve by 50%.
• Developed and delivered custom learning content on countless technical and soft-skill subjects.
• Traveled globally and consulted with C-level partner organization executives on deployment of customer service and contact (call) center technologies.
• Designed and implemented highly acclaimed organizational performance management solutions.
