Larry Tolpin’s Summary

Ever since Larry was a kid, he has loved to build stuff.

Today, it's no different. Only now, instead of building go karts and backyard hockey rinks, Larry gets excited building brands, creative departments, ad agencies and his client’s business.

What's more, he does it while building and creating other fun stuff from branded content and new online communities to innovative digital and 360-degree, integrated marketing campaigns.

Larry is the President and Chief Creative Officer for Ypartnership, the world's leading travel, leisure and entertainment marketing company as well as the largest independent advertising agency based in Florida.

Larry joined the agency after 14 years with J Walter Thompson and BBDO Worldwide. At JWT, he served as one of the company’s two worldwide creative directors and member of the JWT Worldwide Board of Directors. He was also CEO of JWT West and JWT Canada.

At BBDO, Larry was the CEO and Chief Creative Officer of BBDO South, BBDO North American Regional Creative Chairman, Chief Creative Officer for BBDO Canada, Chief Creative Officer of BBDO Toronto, ECD of Baker Lovick/BBDO Vancouver and and member of the BBDO North American Board.

Some of the global brands he has worked on include Apple, Delta, FedEx, Jeep, Kraft, Miller, Molson, Nike, Pepsi, Pizza Hut, Sprint, Unilever, VISA and the Walt Disney Company.

In Larry’s spare time, he digs up golf courses, plays tennis and tries to escape to the wine country.

Larry Tolpin’s Specialties:

Larry Tolpin’s Experience

  • President & Chief Creative Officer

    Ypartnership

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    2004 — Present (6 years )

    Larry Tolpin is the President and Chief Creative Officer
    for Ypartnership, formerly known as YPB&R.

    Larry sets the creative tone and standards for the agency.
    He oversees all of Ypartnership’s creative operations as well
    as leads the entire organization to think beyond the traditional
    process and to engage brands holistically across multiple
    platforms and touchpoints.

    Serving in his dual role, Larry believes creative is not just a
    department, but it is a way of thinking for the whole agency.

    He strongly believes that there are no boundaries, no silos and
    no rules. There are only brilliant ideas from creative partnerships
    across all disciplines. And all those brilliant ideas must create
    added value for brands, personal value for consumers and asset
    value for clients.

    A writer by craft, Larry has penned strategic integrated campaigns
    for select brands and clients including the Walt Disney Company,
    Wynn Resorts, Bahamas Tourism, Canyon Ranch Resorts & Spas,
    Darden Restaurants, Hard Rock Resorts & Casinos, Steamboat
    Ski & Resort and Fairmont Resorts.

  • JWT Worldwide Creative Director and CEO/Chief Creative Officer of JWT West and JWT Canada

    J. Walter Thompson Worldwide

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1998 — 2003 (5 years )

    In the fall of 1997, Larry was recruited by Chris Jones, CEO
    of J Walter Thompson Worldwide to be one of two Worldwide
    Creative Directors for the entire company as well as CEO of
    JWT’s U.S. Western Operations and all of Canada.

    Larry was also appointed a member of the Worldwide Board
    of Directors, the Worldwide Executive Group and the North
    American Management Board.

    At JWT, Larry helped develop a new global creative philosophy
    and set of creative standards that led the entire company to
    think beyond the traditional agency process and to take a more
    holistic approach to brand solutions, including a major emphasis
    on digital and new technologies.

    Larry also help developed and managed a semi-annual, total
    communications creative review and report card for every major
    global brand and JWT office.

    In less than two years, he helped earn JWT an overall “B” rating
    from Adweek up from a “C” and a creative rating of “B” up from
    a “D”.

    Some of the major brands and clients included Cadbury, Chevron,
    De Beers, Diageo, Ford, Kraft, Nestle, Nike, Sprint and Unilever.

    In addition, Larry helped JWT West win 14 pitches including 10
    new clients and 4 major new assignments from existing clients.

  • CEO/Chief Creative Officer of BBDO South and Chief Creative Officer of BBDO Canada

    BBDO Worldwide

    (Public Company; OMC; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1987 — 1998 (11 years )

    Larry cut his teeth as a creative director at BBDO and made
    his mark in Canada, the United States and around the world.

    He rose from Executive Creative Director of Baker Lovick/BBDO
    Vancouver to Chief Creative Officer of BBDO Canada
    to CEO, President and Chief Creative Officer of BBDO South.
    In addition, he was named Regional Creative Chairman for BBDO
    Worldwide and a member of the North American Board.

    At BBDO Canada, the agency won Agency of the Year
    five times by major trade publications, including Advertising
    Age's International Agency of the Year, the only time a
    Canadian agency has ever won that honor. He was also
    named Creative Director of the Year three times.

    What's more, the agency won over 30 new pieces of business
    including leading brands such as A&W, BC Lottery, Bell Canada,
    FedEx, Frito-Lay, Kraft, Jeep, Mars, Miller Brewing USA, Molson,
    Nike, Pizza Hut, Pepsi International, Sony, TSN Sports Network
    and Yellow Pages.

    The result? Billings increased by over 50% making BBDO
    Canada the largest agency in the country.

    Under Larry's creative guidance, BBDO offices won over 500
    creative awards including the Cannes Lions Festival, the Clios,
    The One Show, CA and the New York Art Director's Show.
