Jef Loeb’s Summary

Professional experience: writer and creative director serving the integrated global, worldwide, transcontinental, transnational and planetary needs of clients and advertising agencies everywhere. Of course, if your tiny, small, diminutive, petite and otherwise local pawn shop needs new creative, that sounds pretty good, too.

Goals: to write and direct creative. Or is that creatively write and direct? Well, while I figure that out, know that I laugh in the face of danger, while taking an illicit amount of pleasure in making a 360 degree difference for the brands and the lovely people generous enough to put blinis, environmentally virtuous caviar and fine vintages on the table.
