Karen Ventura’s Summary

A highly motivated and effective senior operations manager with a track record of delivery and rapid career progression.

I have extensive experience with managing large teams of technical and managerial staff, managing teams in excess of 50 people across all technical delivery disciplines.

I have recently managed the cross-functional end-to-end integration of 2 large ISPs as part of BT’s acquisition of the Brightview business in 2007/8; migrating a large broadband customer base and associated work-flow into the Plusnet business.

I have successfully completed several offshore transformation programmes in India, Poland, Australia and New Zealand, moving existing work-flow to offshore teams.

I come from a technical background, with hands-on Java, Lotus Domino, HTML, XML, JavaScript and Visual Basic experience.

I have extensive Project Management and Business Analysis experience; being responsible for the design and delivery of Internet Access Products, Internet Banking, CRM systems and Business Automation tools and processes.

I am motivated by the freedom to make a positive impact on the business at large, focusing delivering to the customer need and the personal and professional development of my staff, within strict budgets.
