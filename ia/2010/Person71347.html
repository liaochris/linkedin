Frank White III, PhD’s Summary

I have a PhD in Molecular Biology and spent a number of years in the pharmaceutical & biotechnology industry, initially doing laboratory research and later moving into bioinformatics. For the past 10 years I've worked on the business side of the life sciences industry in both small and large companies. I've done a variety of jobs focused on marketing, communications, product development, alliance management, sales management, technology evaluation and strategic planning.
