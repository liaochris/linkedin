Andrea Goulet’s Summary

First of all, let’s establish what I’m not. I’m not just a copywriter. My degree is not in English, History, or Journalism. Nope. After earning my degree in Marketing started a successful career in B2B sales where I learned first-hand prospect psychology. And it's this field experience that sets my writing and user experience design apart.

I ventured out on my own on a mission to rid the world of corporate babble one concise sentence at a time. Through my experience I discovered that most people are yearning for a voice that is natural and authentic. And it's this authenticity that has earned me a reputation of results-oriented copy that provides perfect balance of personality and professionalism.

In 2009, I partnered with one of the best coders I know, and expanded my scope to include User Experience Design. Websites follow a very patterned sales process, so it's a great place to leverage my marketing, sales, and writing experience.
