Rob Heighington’s Summary

For the past 15 years Mr. Heighington (Rob) has been managing large Enterprise Information Technology projects including; Oracle (HR & Financials), PeopleSoft, Cognos BI Solutions, Dynamics as well as Healthcare Informatics, Data Warehousing, Software as a Service, CRM, Financial and Billing systems.

Rob has won three major awards for his work in IT Project Management:
• 2008 Distinction Award from Alberta Advance Education and Technology
• 2006 Premier’s Award of Excellence – Alberta Government, Canada
• 2004 CIPA (Canadian Information Productivity Award of Excellence)

A former Fortune 500 CIO Rob’s experience covers a variety of high profile, fast paced, technologically sophisticated environments across all levels of Government, Healthcare, Energy & Utility, Teleco, Financial and Education Sectors.

An ultra-positive leader Rob has been helping to rebuild PMO and IT organizations resulting in streamlined Solution Delivery Frameworks which focus on end to end process improvement, project management metric dash-boarding and 'flex team role & responsibility' leadership.

Business Skills include Certified Business Management Consulting (Deloitte & Touche), Business Analysis and Process Engineering (Bell Canada, Intria-HP, EDS, D&T), Strategic Visioning and Long Term IT Planning (Intria-HP, GPC, EDS, D&T).

Rob is an Award Winning Public Speaker (Toastmasters International Champion - 2009), conducts Improv Workshops for his clients and community groups. He is a well known volunteer in the community and is involved frequently in amateur sports. Rob is also an active watercolour painter.

Rob Heighington’s Specialties:

Certified Project Manager (Deloitte and Touche 1998), Member of PMI Global - Project Management Institute #1363839 as well as an active member of the PMI’s Northern and Southern Alberta Project Managers Association as well as PMI Vancouver.

Rob’s technical expertise includes; In-depth Complex and Distributed Software Systems Architecture, Database Design, Middleware, Network and Infrastructure Architecture.
