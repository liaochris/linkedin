Paul Fingerman’s Summary

I am an executive leader who excels at the IT enablement of business vision and goals, through my ability to inspire IT staff and to bring to bear my own technical expertise to solve business challenges.

I am seeking to leverage my experience and expertise in an executive or senior position with company that I can help to compete and grow. The ideal position would be a challenging assignment as Chief Technology Officer, Chief Information Officer, or Chief Enterprise Architect that will exploit my leadership and judgment to help IT better enable the business of the company.

I am a seasoned and successful IT professional with well over 20 years of experience in delivering and directing information technology architecture, engineering, and design. I am a proven team leader and effective manager of large and small IT elements at major engineering, manufacturing, financial, biotechnology, and healthcare organizations. I am able to leverage my technical expertise to develop and deliver cost-effective solutions to business problems and to enhance organizational efficiency. I partner with the business to make IT a strategic enabler. I translate complex technical ideas to non-technical stakeholders, and represent business vision back to the technology producers. I am an exceptional problem solver and implementer in both the technical and business domains; as a natural enterprise architect, I have the ability to see the overall landscape simultaneously from 500,000 feet down to 5,000 feet, down to 5 feet.
