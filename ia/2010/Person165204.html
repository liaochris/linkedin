Luis Ponce de Leon’s Summary

Luis Ponce de Leon is an award-winner and published multimedia designer. He has over ten years of experience working for design firms such as Agile Industries, Razorfish, Manhattan Transfer, Modem Media, AlloyRed, and New Interactive. His TV, Interactive and Print work includes clients such as IBM, L'Oreal, Wired Magazine, Red Cross of America, Heineken and General Electric. Luis also teaches multimedia courses at Portland State University and the Art Institute of Portland.

MFA, Video, Savannah College of Art and Design, GA
BFA, Communications, American University, Washington DC
