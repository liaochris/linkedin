Michael Liehr’s Summary

Michael Liehr is associate VP of New Business, Alliances and Consortia at the College of Nanoscale Science and Engineering, and a full time professor at the College. He is responsible for strategic research and development for semiconductor and 3D packaging alliances at CNSE. His experience spans semiconductor research, product and process development, manufacturing and foundry business P&L. Michael holds a Ph.D. in Physics, is a certified executive project manager, and has authored or coauthored over 20 patents and over 90 publications.
