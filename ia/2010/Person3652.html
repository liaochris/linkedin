Christine Peterson’s Summary

Born and raised in Portland, Oregon, I am a lover of art, nature and technology. Photography was an early passion of mine and ultimately laid the foundation of my career. I’d always been advised to go into math and computers, but couldn’t ignore my deep appreciation for aesthetic beauty. With time and experience, I found my niche in digital art and visual effects.
