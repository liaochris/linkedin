Geri Lafferty Virtual Assistant’s Summary

Capital Virtual Assistance provides virtual assistant and virtual business manager (Online Business Manager) services; I partner with business owners to provide administrative support remotely.

I love what I do, and I think it shows. My clients—who represent a wide range of professions and disciplines—are terrific! They are bright, successful women who love what they do, too, but struggle to find enough time to manage it all. We work hard and appreciate what each of us brings to the partnership. My clients are happy, creative women who have a great sense of humor (and appreciate mine), and because I am also a small business owner, I understand both the perks and the pressures involved in running your own show.
