Jim Logan’s Summary

Known for developing business concept and process models live, in front of customers and SMEs with accurate UML 2 semantics.

In a way that is clear to non-technical clients, models business concepts, business processes, requirements, information, messages, and systems to streamline business processes and generate operational software.

Uses SoaML at the business level to model enterprises as participants providing services. Uses UML 2 and SoaML to model logical system architectures and generate operational software.
