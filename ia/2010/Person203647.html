Alexey Timanovsky’s Summary

Architect, experienced developer of high-load, low-latency multimedia systems of any scale - from embedded systems to Internet based global projects. Solid CS, mathematics and physics background. Successful teamlead and project manager.

Current interest is in YouTube-scale internet projects. Broadcasting and processing of multimedia streams - live and recorded. Storing and processing of huge data volumes.

Experience includes:
* Building HA/LB Internet services. Developing complete system, including DB, application stack, deep monitoring and troubleshooting tools.
* Deep knowledge of various network protocols from IETF, ITU-T, IEEE, ETSI & proprietary ones.
* Deep understanding of computer systems. Low-level programming (Windows, Windows Mobile and Linux), porting of Linux kernel to new HW platforms and device driver development.
* Embedded and DSP platforms experience; assembler code optimization, reading schematics, HW debugging.
* People management: lead up to 20 developers.
* Programming languages: C, Java, Python, Erlang.

Goal is to unveil the full power of modern technologies for mass users on the Internet. Fusion of state of the art technologies from different domains will undoubtedly give us many surprises and wows.

Alexey Timanovsky’s Specialties:
