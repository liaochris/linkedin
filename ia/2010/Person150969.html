  • Poet, Freelance Editor

    (Publishing industry)

    September 2005 — Present (5 years 1 month)

    In 2005, I began writing poetry as a way to put his thoughts into words. Throughout all of the events of my life, I have always found reason to believe that there is beauty in all aspects of life.
