Jenise Fryatt’s Summary

I have served in a management capacity as Owner and Marketing Director of Icon Presentations since 1990. Icon provides video, audio, lighting and wide-screen technological support to event producers and planners across the country.

We are passionate about creative sound and video enhancement and we bring that passion to all of our projects. To that end, we are very big on finding and maintaining a wide selection of the best event production equipment available. We have everything from lavalier mics and LCD projectors, to widescreen projection equipment and high-end video cameras.

Our team of technicians and account executives/project managers offers decades of experience to clients seeking to create engaging presentations for corporate gatherings, live broadcasts and special events. We will set, operate and strike the AV equipment for your event, and we will help you plan the best and most cost effective use of it.

Past clients include Palm Springs International Film Festival, Honeywell, Catapillar, Pfizer, Baxter, Roche, United Brotherhood of Carpenters, Cookie Lee, Shaklee, Sunrider, Radio Shack, Merle Norman, Acord Loma, Toshiba, Ani Awards, Snap On Tools, American Heart Association, Kraft Foods, The Bob Hope Chrysler Classic, Transamerica, MONY, Marriott International, CDW, Ford, Met Life, HP, Abbott Labs, CTIA , Cardinal Health, Motorola, Office Depot, SC Johnson, Starbucks, many more.

I am very excited about recent developments in the events industry and the use of social media to enhance events. I have a blog called Sound n' Sight which features information on the events industry with an audio visual and social media bent.

Goals include: Meeting the demands of a changing meeting planning community with new technology and responsive customer service and using social media as a way to help others and grow the company.
