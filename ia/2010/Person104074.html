Jonathan M. Soares’s Summary

Entrepreneur with a pragmatic understanding of the “business systems” across various industries – product development, product commercialization, national retail execution, manufacturing, distribution and guerrilla marketing. Customer-focused brand builder and marketer with the ability to build teams, sales strategies, manufacturing partnerships and develop effective solutions in a timely non-bureaucratic way. A strong focus on speed to the marketplace while balancing team synergies to create the sense of urgency needed to develop and execute products or programs. Successfully modified and adapted traditional and non-traditional marketing strategies to compete against rivals with significantly larger marketing budgets. Ability to provide vision to unite teams across functions to assess unrealized markets, develop business opportunities and create synergies that result in significant growth. Success-oriented and motivational personality with a sharp creative sense balanced by strong business acumen. Fluent in Spanish and Portuguese.

Jonathan M. Soares’s Specialties:

New Product Development, Brand Strategy & Brand Building, Manufacturing & Distribution Partnership Development, Sales Management, Consumer Marketing, Trade Marketing, Social Media & Public Relations, Promotions & Special Events, In-Store Merchandising, Packaging Design & Development, Public Speaking / Presentations.
