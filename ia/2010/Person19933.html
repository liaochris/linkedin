Barbara Saunders’s Summary

My twenty-five year career combines two threads, communication via the written word and teaching. Within those broad categories, my experiences are diverse.

My writing, editing, and publishing background ranges from public relations writing for animal shelters to technical and business communications for major corporations to the publication of a biography for middle-school students.

I've taught physical education to five-year-old children, ninety-year-old women, and young and middle-aged adults; entrepreneurial skills to low-income women and leadership skills to managers; and communications skills to students, executives, and entrepreneurs.

Among my current projects is developing a suite of entrepreneurial education products for personal trainers and others in the health, wellness, and fitness fields. These resources will help people develop business models that keep them in the business and out from behind the desk.

I am adept at connecting people with the people and ideas they need to reach high-priority goals for themselves, their organizations, their customers, or their causes.
