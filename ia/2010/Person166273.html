Jim Wright’s Summary

B.S. Degree in Mechanical Engineering (BSME) with over 30 years of industry experience. Engineering background includes airfoils, ATP, blade books, controls, composite, EEC, EICAS, electrical, electro-mechanical, FADEC, failure analysis, fairing, filtration, flight test, fuel, gas turbine, ground support equipment, hydraulic, industrial turbine, nitrogen inerting, mechanism, MRB, nacelle, power plant, pneumatic, project, research and development, RVDTs, STC, systems engineering, tooling, Teamcenter PLM and wire harnesses. Holds dual citizenship with U.S. and Canada, and is a licensed Mechanical Engineer (PE) in the state of California. Proficient with ASME Y14.5-1994, CAD and PC packages including ANSYS, CATIA V4 and V5, Solidworks, NX6 and Microsoft Office 2003 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
