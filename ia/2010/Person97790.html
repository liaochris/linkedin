Jhilmil Jain’s Summary

My objective is to drive teams and lead in the area of user-centric innovation by designing and executing research to deliver actionable insights that influence the product strategy, design, and marketing. I aim to create engaging customer experiences that inform, inspire and entertain.

jhilmil.jain@gmail.com

• Proven track record in problem definition and analysis: excellent skills in identifying and describing the "right" problem using qualitative and quantitative methods
• Responsible for leading and managing the entire UX pipeline (starting with user research, to design, to evaluation) in a number of verticals. Primarily responsible for communicating results to senior executives in business units, marketing and sales
• Recognized for superior communication skills in customer facing roles. Have engaged with high profile HP partners such as P&G, NBA, Sony, Best Buy, Walmart etc.
• Consistently held leadership positions (conference chair, PC member) at both academic and industrial conferences
• Experienced speaker, published author, and patent holder.
• Honors and awards: ACM Recognition of Service Award (2009), SPIE Best Paper Award (2010)

Jhilmil Jain’s Specialties:

• Experience with user research collection methods such as contextual inquiry, ethnography, diary studies, focus groups, observations, interviews, and analysis/deliverables such as affinity diagrams, card sort, persona design, task/work flow
• Experience with interface design methods such as paper prototyping, storyboarding, wizard of oz, wireframes
• Experience with usability testing methods such as in-lab experiments, heuristic evaluation, think-aloud protocol, and surveys/questionnaires
