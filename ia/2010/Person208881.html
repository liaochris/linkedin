Michael Thompson’s Summary

Michael Thompson is Founder & CEO of the Turas Group, a leading Federal and Intel Recruiting & Consulting Firm.

He is a Talent Acquisition leader with the ability to find candidates in hard to fill industries and strategic positions to grow business. He has a deep understanding of full life cycle recruiting (corporate and IT in Intel and DOD space) and a track record of designing strategies, structures and processes leading to significant enterprise wide hiring changes and improvements.

Michael is a Human Capital Consultant with extensive experience in Talent Acquisition, Talent Manaement, Contract-Vendor Management, Change Management, Corporate Transformation and Transition and HR ERP and Project Management across a variety of industries (federal government, power, construction, defense, education and hi-tech).

Mr. Thompson is also organizer and advisor for the Business Transition Network Group on LinkedIn, a non-profit organization helping the unemployed in Montgomery County find jobs.

Mr. Thompson also assists as a business consultant with the launch of English Schools around the world.
