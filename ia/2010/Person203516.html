Tiffany Monhollon Wilson’s Summary

Writer & Editor
My team produces eight publications that reach over half a million subscribers and focus on the job search, career development, management, HR, small business, and leadership. Our award-winning work ranges from e-mail marketing to blogging to targeted newsletter programs.

Social Media & Media Relations
From corporate brand management to site and program development, I run our in-house social media program which focuses on equipping all our readers with knowledge to help them succeed. My team also consults with our franchise owners on their personal and local social media efforts. Our media relations program works with reporters, bloggers, and publications from a national to a local level and equips our local franchise owners to build expertise within their local markets.

Personal PR
My personal goal is to equip professionals with advice to help them navigate the personal/professional crossover in social media. I write about how public relations tactics and strategies can apply to your personal brand to help you build an authentic, credible presence online.

Speaking
I also speak about professional development, leadership, and social media and am in leadership for professional organizations such as the Public Relations Society of America.
