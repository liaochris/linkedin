Robert Holloway’s Summary

I am well-versed in systems and network administration, security, engineering, troubleshooting-- you name it. Computers have been my passion since the age of 7. I have been working personally with them since that age, and professionally since I was 10... I used to make some money by helping friends of the family with their computer systems that they used for their business. I saw a very wide range of computers at that time, and saw the explosion of the technology age. I used the Internet before it was called the Internet, and before there was any such thing as the "web". Few are lucky enough to do professionally what they love-- even fewer are lucky enough to be one of the best.
