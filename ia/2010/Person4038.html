Allen Hammock’s Summary

I have a broad experience defining, developing, selling, and promoting computer software products and web sites. I am what some have called a "jack of all trades"--my abilities range from public speaking, to software engineering, to sales and marketing, and business management. I have built my own companies and worked for a variety of small- to medium-sized private and public companies all along the west coast.

I have direct experience in the following industries:

* Internet Advertising - display ads, sponsored search, contextual
* Internet Filtering - content filtering, parental controls
* Computer Security - biometric authentication
* Online Publishing - working with traditional print and radio properties to monetize content online

My special ability is to get at the SIGNIFICANCE of a problem. I help others find solutions to problems, be they technical or not and I am able to communicate with others in their own domain vocabulary. I avoid waste by figuring out not only HOW to solve a problem, but WHY a problem is significant. This makes me a valuable asset to any team.

I enjoy working with other experienced professionals and I feed off others' energy and passion. I am a natural leader. I am outspoken but not bullish. I am confident but not arrogant. I know my strengths and I know how to use my peers to protect the team from my weaknesses.

Because of my technical abilities, I work well with developers. Because of my extensive knowledge of software systems, I work well with system architects. Because of my communications and organizational skills, I establish workflow and process. Because of my writing and analysis skills, I write requirements documents. Because of my experience building and running a business, I build cost models and profit-and-loss statements. Because of my passion and compassion for others, people seek me out to help them find solutions to problems.
