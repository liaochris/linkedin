Yevgeniy Pazekha’s Summary

I’m a software developer with over 12 years of experience in design, development and implementation of computer systems.

Until recent time I was working in Embedded Software profile. I created software for various devices, including mobile phones. I have rich experience solving all sorts of problems involving embedded development – high performance, realtime and multithreaded processing, robustness, and software quality. Quality is a foundation stone in Embedded development. Unlike websites, with embedded software you cannot fix problems at any moment because devices are already sold, so you have to do things right from the beginning ;-)

From 2010 I’ve turned towards Android platform. I was keeping eye at it since its early releases. Versions prior 1.5 were buggy and miserable, but after version 1.6 it appeared to be mature enough so I’ve decided it was time to move. Now I’m working at Stanfy; we port applications from iPhone to Android, and make new apps for Android too.

My professional experience includes over 5 years of in Embedded Systems, over 3 years in QA (including automated), and about a year of project management. I have deep skills in software design and architecture, including classic “GoF” design patterns and algorithms. I have experience in Scrum development process, and other Agile practices as well (such as Continuous Integration), and RUP too.

I’m used to work in various countries such as Israel, Denmark and others. My spoken languages are Russian (native), English (fluent), and German (beginner).


Currently my goal is a position of Software Engineer in Netherlands.
