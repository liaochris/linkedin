Preeti Bhat’s Summary

Preeti is a Performance Consultant and loves to create purpose out of chaos. She is a researcher at heart and loves to experiment and validate based on her studies and work. She regularly contributes to the ASTD publications and works extensively with ISPI and ASTD Research Councils. She has made significant contributions to the area of Evaluation Methodology; some of the other areas that she is currently working on are HR Strategy and Motivation.
In her previous roles

Preeti has worked in various HR roles with organizations like Fidelity Investments, Accenture, Vedanta Resources PLc, etc. As a performance consultant Preeti’s passion lies in enabling leaders and teams to discover their potential to the fullest.
