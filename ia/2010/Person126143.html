Lisa Larson’s Summary

As a creative director, I'm committed to building strong brands by gaining deep insight to the consumer/end user and develop a smart, strategic one-to-one marketing plan that resonates with them. I always deliver strategic execution, design, and user experience be it in the digital or traditional platform.

I have the skill and experience to communicate a vision of where a campaign or project needs to go. I am passionate about my work and lead by example—I’m a hands-on creative director because I love the creative process of developing relevant communications. My experiences have led me to work closely with, and build trust and respect with clients, developers, programmers, production, directors, editors, producers, photographers, and illustrators.

I thrive in a “many hats” position and always ensure that projects are understood, supervised and accomplished. Goal setting, problem solving, and decision making are innate to me. And I’m always exploring new technologies and techniques to create stronger bonds between brand and consumer.
