Beth Buelow’s Summary

Are you challenged by trying to navigate an extroverted world with an introverted heart and mind? Growing up, I thought I was socially inept and shy, alternately energized by people and exhausted by them. By most outward appearances, I was outgoing and friendly; inside, I was tired and more than a little stressed! I thought I was just low energy and perhaps not a "people person."

What I didn't know back then that I know now is that I am an introvert, and that is a strength! It's a fabulous personality trait that is meant to be understood, owned and leveraged.

With me, you'll find a uniquely safe space for you to process and succeed on your own terms. This space is created for you to journey into deep self-awareness, which enriches every area of your life (esp. career and relationships). You develop skills that help you work WITH your introversion, not against it. It's not about changing who you are so you can be more extroverted; it's about embracing and loving who you are, trusting your inner wisdom and truth.

Why I Do What I Do: All my life, people have been drawn to confide in me, share their stories and seek counsel. My clients often comment that I help them to connect the seemingly random dots in their lives to form a complete picture that represents their best and brightest selves. Working with me, you will begin to see possibilities and choices where previously none were visible.

My strengths as a coach include creating a safe, trusting relationship; gentle directness; ability to peel back the layers to get to the root; being able to not have the answer; laughter!

My Services: I offer one-on-one coaching, teleclasses, webinars and workshops. In addition, I'm a Living Your Vision licensed facilitator, which can be delivered individually or in a group setting. My continuing education includes studies in advanced coaching skills, Jungian psychology, Voice Dialogue and the Myers-Briggs assessment tool.
