John Barrow’s Summary

I have been working as art director and interactive designer since 1995. I have worked for major media companies, including Associated Press, Newscorp and Hearst Corporation on such products as TVGuide.com, Hearst-Argyle Television, CosmoGIRL!, Harper's Bazaar, Cosmopolitan, ESPN Zone and Esquire. Previous clients include Sony Home Entertainment, Estee Lauder, the NBA Store, ConsumerSearch and the Smithsonian Institution.

I have extensive knowledge of cross-media design for interactive, mobile, film/video and print. I also have a solid understanding of designing within the parameters of current technology and platforms. My online portfolio, which highlights the full range of my expertise, is available at www.john-barrow.com.

Previously, as Art Director for Accoona Corp., a global internet company, I developed creative plans for new product initiatives, employing an iterative user-centered approach that produced scalable and flexible design solutions for search, lead generation, social networking and e-commerce products.

In my most recent position at the Associated Press, I am responsible for high-profile interactive news and information products and services for the global newspaper, magazine, broadcast, interactive TV, mobile, online and emerging markets.
