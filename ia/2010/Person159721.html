Patricia Phelan Clapp’s Summary

As a transition coach, I specialize in businesses going through change.

Whether you are hiring new employees, adding new services, downsizing, or you want to jump start your marketing program (that may have been dormant), my job is to help you clarify those goals and come up with an action plan on how to achieve those goals.

By doing this, I encourage my clients to explore the world of social media. This can be done by utilizing these platforms as a form of education, to seek new professional goals and reach relevant target markets with direct online interaction.

Business coaching methods include (but not limited to):
- Strategies on how to align your business goals
- How to identify your professional brand,
- Tips on reaching your target market
- Methods on how to use the world of social media to achieve your goals.

Having studied the pros and cons of many social networking sites, such as Twitter, Linked In, Facebook and the various blogging websites, I will assess your business goals in conjunction with social media action plan.

With executive coaching (which combines personal and professional coaching), I assist clients in navigating the waters of change while creating an environment that enables you to move forward. Too often, professionals have a hard time aligning their business goals to the their day to day goals. As a solution, we will identify overall business and personal goals, and put together a step-by-step plan on how to achieve those goals. This will help you maintain personal/professional balance.

On a final note, think about your overall business plan and your desire to expand your professional horizons.

What are you doing today that will lead you one step closer to a your ideal professional/personal future? Staying in alignment (day to day) with you goals is the key.

“We can’t solve problems by using the same kind of thinking we used when we created them.” Albert Einstein
