Doug Garnett’s Summary

Doug Garnett is founder and CEO of DRTV agency Atomic Direct and a leading expert on innovative uses of DRTV, infomercials and other in-depth TV and non-TV messages to build brand and drive sales.

Doug sits on the editorial board of Response Magazine, is an adjunct professor of general advertising at Portland State University, and is a member of the Jordan-Whitney Greensheet Panel. He speaks regularly at marketing and advertising events and a regular contributor to magazines where he shares his unique vision of how brand and retail clients can develop their businesses and brands using the power of TV and strong messaging.

Doug's clients include Rubbermaid, Limbo, AT&T, Ugobe, Presto, DisneyMobile, AAA of California, Professional Tool Manufacturing (Drill Doctor), Kreg Tools, P&G, System Pavers, Apple Computer, Sears, Braun, DuPont (Teflon, Stainmaster), Bissel, and Hamilton Beach.

His diverse career started on a music scholarship followed by two degrees in applied mathematics. This led to an early career as a rocket scientist (Atlas/Centaur rockets, Tomahawk Cruise Missile, & Space Shuttle), where he indulged his innate fascination with the universe. His move to full time focus on communication started while selling supercomputers and presenting technology internationally. Doug brings together a unique combination of creative skills (art, music, writing), analytic skills, and the street-smarts to drive product sales and brand through communication.
