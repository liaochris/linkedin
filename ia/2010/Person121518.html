Nicholas Webb’s Summary

Nicholas Webb is a world-renowned author and expert in the area of Customer-Driven Innovation. He speaks not just as a theorist but also as a highly successful inventor with more than 35 patents, each a success in the marketplace. As a Certified Management Consultant and CEO of Lassen Innovation, Webb has helped some of the world’s best companies become great. He is the author of The Innovation Playbook - a revolution in business excellence and The Digital Innovation Playbook - Innovation in the digital age..
