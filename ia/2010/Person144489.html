Michelle Sandlin, CRP’s Summary

Since 1985, I have enjoyed an exciting and well-rounded real estate career that has allowed me to successfully combine my extensive experience in real estate, relocation, marketing, business development and technology.

I currently provide the strategic leadership and direction for our Global Relocation Services Division, which serves many international corporations, relocation companies, and Brokers throughout the U.S. and internationally through our membership in Leading Real Estate Companies of the World.

Over the past year, I have developed and implemented a very comprehensive Relocation Training & Certification Program for our company, and have established other minimum standards and requirements in order for our agents to be eligible for relocation referrals. This ensures the very best in real estate/relocation services to our clients. I have expanded this training to include additional classes, which I offer and provide to our relocation company clients and corporate clients on a regular basis. As an added benefit, all classes are approved for CRP credit from Worldwide ERC.

I love writing and have a passion for my Houston Relocation Blog, which addresses a variety of topics of interest to the relocation industry, corporate clients, transferees, relocation professionals and Houston. I am also a contributing writer for Mobility magazine and a member of its Editorial Advisory Committee.
