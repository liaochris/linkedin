Alison McDougall’s Summary

Alison is the owner of Relevant Risk Limited, an organisation that works with individuals and businesses that plan or are actively involved with events. She assists in ensuring that compliance and legislation that is directly relevant to events is known and understood, not only by employers but, importantly, by actively involved employees.

In essence, Relevant Risk acts like a radar operator by assessing the situation (within a business) and pointing out the hazards ahead. The resulting information allows the business to take the necessary action to avoid trouble (ie, breaking the law!).

More than twenty years working in event management have provided the necessary experience to understand the pressures of the industry. She has personally been involved with organising car and powerboat racing, conferences, product launches and sampling, hospitality and exhibitions, roadshows, seminars and sports sponsorship. All with blue-chip clients.

Work in the last few years has meant adding to the existing skills and knowledge attained previously as the owner and managing director of an agency specialising in event management. Extra studying and courses with CIEH and IOSH have helped filled the gaps.

And the offer from Relevant Risk has also been examined. Prior to launch, Alison has been working with insurance corporates, international golf events, specialist conferencing companies, outdoor activity organisers and others to test this knowledge and proposed service - to their satisfaction.

Alison is a `not-bad' golfer and `shocking' tennis player !
