Steve Nielsen’s Summary

Steve is currently Chairman of MacroVentures, Inc., an investor in early stage companies in the technology and medical spaces.

Most recently, Steve served as President and CEO of PartnerUp, a wholly owned subsidiary of Deluxe Corporation (NYSE: DLX).

PartnerUp is a leading enterprise web 2.0 software company and the first and largest online community for entrepreneurs and small business owners. Steve co-founded PartnerUp in 2004.

Steve is a veteran internet and technology executive and thought-leader, having started and led a number of technology-focused ventures since 1996. He invests in and currently serves on the board of directors for several internet, high-tech, and medical device startups.

Steve is passionate about the internet/information technology and the powerful effects that it can have on every aspect of a business. In addition to technology, his specialties/interests are entrepreneurial management, product strategy, marketing, customer acquisition, and M&A.

Prior to PartnerUp, Steve served as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of HostForce, a leading managed services and web hosting provider, from 1998 to 2004. In 2004, the HostForce business was acquired by SilkRoad Technologies.

Before HostForce, Steve served as Vice President of Marketing at a Twin Cities-based manufacturing company and founded/co-founded several other internet startups and a manufacturing (CAD/CAM) software startup.

Steve Nielsen’s Specialties:

Leveraging marketing and technology to build companies and win against competitors by creating a durable competitive advantage.
