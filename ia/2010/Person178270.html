Rachel Perlmutter’s Summary

Highly ethical and honest Consultant and Project Manager. Excellent guide for those who desire Social Media Marketing training, coaching and implementation. Over 8 years experience consulting to a variety of business clients performing competitive analysis, acquisition synergy review, process review/recommendations, marketing strategy development and implementation, and business coaching.

Additionally, Rachel has over 15 years experience as a Sales and Marketing Professional managing telesales and field sales people, managing national accounts, and developing all marketing campaigns, collateral and rebate structures for retail, wholesale and key account customers. Experienced in solution selling/marketing, vendor consolidation and strategic sales, social media marketing, and strategic comprehensive marketing plans. Has managed complex print projects, as well as trade show events, name changes and direct mail campaigns.

Seeking consulting, coaching, speaking and training opportunities. Find me at rachel@businesssuccessexperts.com or 714-330-1982.
