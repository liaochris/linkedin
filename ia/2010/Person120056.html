Kurt Unkel’s Summary

Digital advertising expert, with over 10 years experience in creating digital media strategies, developing measurement models, and creating scalable applications of existing and new internet technologies.

Extensive understanding of the digital advertising industry's technological infrastructure and systems, including ad-serving, inventory/campaign management software, rich media vendors, syndicated research providers, and data management & analytics tools.

Most enjoy developing creative ideas and inspiring talent within others to solve any number of advertising-centric challenges.
