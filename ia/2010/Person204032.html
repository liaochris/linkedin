Timm Etters’s Summary

Goal 1
My first & foremost mission is to create original works of art that inspires people of all ages.
I want my work to convey positive thoughts and feelings to the people who view my work. There are far too many people in this world creating art because they are told to; because it is part of their “job” or a deadline. I don’t ever want my art to become “my job” or just another deadline to meet.

I’d rather create works of art because they are inspired; they are created from life experiences, not because they are “on the schedule”. The great Michelangelo did not finish the Sistine Chapel mural because the Pope told him to. He finished them because he was inspired to do so. He had a vision of what the ceiling should look like and he began to paint. Although he was indeed hurried by the Pope to finish this monumental task, he instead took the time needed to create a masterpiece and as a result of that decision, we are left with one of the greatest, single works of art known to man.

Goal 2
Another one of my goals has always been to create work that on some level, everyone from any walk of life can afford to own. Too often, quality art has been something only the financially well-off could afford. My only response to that is, “Why?”
Art reaches to all walks of life; young and old alike. It is not created to be only for the financially sound part of our population. There is a message in art that speaks directly to our soul. It can move us to do things we might not have done before seeing, hearing or reading it. Yes, we first need our eyes or ears to first experience it, but then it goes beyond that, sometimes reaching our childhoods or more recent points of reference in our lives. Sometimes it reaches as far as our subconscious long before we know even about it. I don’t think an experience like that should only be allowed to people who have money. This is the thinking that backs up my decision to create Public Art. It is for everyone.

Timm Etters’s Specialties:

I have 25 years in the mural painting business, working in schools and teaching kids the importance of art as well as the business of art (to those it applies to). I also paint portraits, design logos, and have recently started selling reproductions of my work on canvas. I am always trying to better myself and my work.
