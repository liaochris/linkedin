Christine Zebrowski’s Summary

Overbrook Law is a boutique law firm specializing in employment law. Headed by Christine Zebrowski, Esq., Overbrook provides high quality legal work with outstanding personal service. Ms. Zebrowski has seventeen years of experience as an employment law attorney, including representation of employers and employees.

Christine Zebrowski’s Specialties:

Advise on harassment, discrimination and retaliation, wage-hour issues, family and medical leave, disability, performance management, employee handbooks, employment contracts, severance agreements, non-compete agreements, contract worker classification, terminations and reductions in force. Conduct internal investigations of employee complaints.
