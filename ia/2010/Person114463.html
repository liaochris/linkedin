Kenneth Kahn’s Summary

I am a seasoned entrepreneur, and advertising and direct marketing executive, currently a principal and owner of Life-Event Marketing company, Your Gift Certificate, LLC. (2001- present.) I am unusual in that I have deep experience in both general advertising and direct marketing, two disciplines that ideally work in highly coordinated fashion, but too often do not fundamentally understand each other.

I am also fully immersed in the fast-moving world of online retailing as the owner (or Player #1, as it says on my card) of Oh! Toys (www.oh-toys.com), an online store that offers unusual, hard-to-find imported, high-quality toys. 2000-present.

My career in marketing and advertising has positioned my well for my current roles. My previous experience includes:

Agency principal at Direct Results Group, providing direct marketing strategy, creative, and production services to high-tech, financial services and consumer goods companies. Roles included Managing Partner and SVP, Account Planning. 1995-1997, 1999- 2001.

VP, Account Director, CPS DIrect. Sales and marketing team leader for full-service direct marketing and lettershop organization. 1993-1995.

Director of Marketing at Cahners Business Information, largest publisher of trade journals in the U.S. Responsible for brand identity, reader-side loyalty programs, trade event management, and marekting support to over 140 trade publications. 1997-1999.

VP, Account Supervisor at Arnold Advertising. Managed the Digital Equipment Corporation advertising account (Corporate, product and industry-specific campaigns). 1986-1993

Account Executive, Darcy Masius Benton & Bowles, New York. Zest Soap and MCI accounts. 1984-1986.
