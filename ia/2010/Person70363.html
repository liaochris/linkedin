Flower Explosion’s Summary

FlowerExplosion.com is the result of a combined worldwide effort between more than ten farms in Ecuador, and an alliance with FedEx. Together, we make sure every day that you receive fresh, farm-direct flowers without hassles and at a convenient price.

When we say farm-direct, we literally mean that your flowers are cut and packed at our farms and shipped straight to your door. We avoid unnecessary steps and unnecessary delays. Therefore we can provide you with a wide array of premium-class flower varieties that will be fresh when you receive them.

Our method of operation results in an overall satisfied client-tell. Event planners and florists are able to save on bulk flower purchases, while brides can order professionally made flower arrangements, or DIY roses and fillers at a fraction of the price.


Welcome to Flower Explosion!

Flower Explosion’s Specialties:

FlowerExplosion.com offers more than one hundred varieties of flowers and roses; from classic red roses to novelty colorful roses. All are naturally grown and certified for export since each of our farms complies with the highest quality control standards and growing procedures.
