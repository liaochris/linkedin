Jeff Merlet’s Summary

Strong technology and system architecture background paired with proven success leading small to midsize engineers development teams.
Multiple products, supporting several millions of users, shipped and deployed successfully.
Eighteen years experience in R&D, system architecture using object-oriented approach, best of breed blueprints and design patterns, RUP and XP engineering
process, and overall engineering management.
Technical expertise in performance tuning, database sharding, synchronization and

Domain expertise with PIM synchronization, mobile email, web 2+, p2p, browsing, detection and unified communication
