Susan Ehrlich’s Summary

A high-energy executive in consumer financial services, I lead Sears Financial Services' efforts to provide innovative financing and payment solutions--including credit, layaway, prepaid, gift card and transactional banking services--that enable customers to do business with Sears and Kmart and that ensure a smooth and satisfactory purchase experience wherever and whenever they choose to buy from us.

I joined Sears Holdings in 2006 from Wamu where I was responsible for strategic planning and market research for Wamu's San Francisco-based $20 billion credit card portfolio. Before the acquisition of Providian Financial by Wamu, I managed new product development, loyalty & brand development, internet, business card, prepaid card, and played a key part in the successful turnaround of Providian's credit card business.

Qualities:

* Proven track record utilizing strong analytical skills to develop innovative solutions with bottom line results
* Hands-on experience operating in fast-paced organizations with ability to lead and grow enterprise value
* Strong in problem solving, planning, and project management
* Experienced team builder & leader with solid business case orientation and business growth skills
* Highly committed, dedicated, focused and loyal professional

My focus is on making a difference for customers and employees. I find value in the work I do that provides options for people to enhance their quality of life.

As a recognized leader in the financial services industry, I am often interviewed by the media and have been featured in print and on TV including SmartMoney.com, Times-Dispatch, and Fox Business News.

In October 2009, I was selected one of the Top 25 Women in Finance by U.S. Banker--an honor highlighting top performers in the securities, asset management, retail finance, insurance, and private equity businesses.
