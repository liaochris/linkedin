Siska Flaurensia’s Summary

Being an intuitive-marketer and a proven top sales-person I can offer a lot to a company's bottomline. But, my true passion is in designing, specifically graphic and web designing.

Being an "out-of-the-box" creative thinker and having a deep curiosity in learning new things, I find myself surprising me every day from the things that I came up with!

"Squeeze of Lime Studio" was one of the idea that was squeezed out among other creative juices. It gave birth to my new professional design identity which I use online and as my vision for a future brick & mortar design company.

Professional Goals:
Apart from launching a successful design company with a fresh twist, I would like to use both my marketing and design background for a position as either an Art Director or a Creative Director for a marketing/advertising/design firm.

Siska Flaurensia’s Specialties:

Adobe Creative Suite CS3 & CS4; both PC & Mac environment: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Flash and Fireworks. Web: XHTML, CSS, PHP, Actionscript 3.0, jQuery, SEO, Web 2.0 design plus Magento e-commerce implementation and design. Digital print & publishing. Photography.
