Joel Greenberg’s Summary

I've been involved in interactive _______ (everything) since 1986. Programmer, producer, executive producer, director, analyst, strategist, freelancer, etc. I've worked on a campaign to prevent a nuclear power plant from being built in East Texas because of the water it would consume. I write about the future of Energy for TheEnergyRoadmap.com.

I've built an ad network for Virtual Worlds and sold enterprise social media software. I was an account planner at the ad agency GSD&M, where I specialized in consumer trends in technology. Understanding why and how people use technology to live their lives allowed me to see that people "get" technology before marketers do.

I've been fortunate to provide innovative marketing strategy to clients such as Chile's, Southwest Airlines, Motorola, The US Airforce, The American Legacy Foundation, Land Rover, BMW, and more.
