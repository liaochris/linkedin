Lorraine Allen’s Summary

Obtaining affordable medical insurance has become a challenging proposition. Continued legislative changes at the state level, spiraling health care costs, and the insurance companies' reactions to those factors have resulted in more rules and requirements being imposed on the consumer.

REGIT champions your health insurance needs and helps assure maximum value. We work for you, NOT the insurance company. REGIT can't change the rules and requirements, but we can help you work within them to your best possible advantage. As an insurance professional, I understand the nuances of each of the companies we represent which enables me to select the company that matches your needs. I make certain that you have access to insurance plans that provide the right combination of affordability, flexibility and benefits.

I am available to assist Companies with finding the right medical insurance options to help combat the high cost of inflation on health insurance premiums while still providing your employee's with an attractive plan. I bring with me experience and assistance with Health Reimbursement Account and Health Savings Account Plan options. Consider these types of plans as they are the way of the future and can save you thousands of dollars annually. Ask me how this might work for your company!

If you are an individual or a family that is struggling each and every day to pay your high medical insurance premium, call me, my service is free, and I personally consult with you and come up with ideas you may have never thought of to help reduce your insurance premium obligations. If I feel that what you currently have is your best option and better than what I can provide I will let you know this, I will not try to sell you something that won't work in your favor.
