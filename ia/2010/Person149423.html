Mrim Boutla, PhD, GCDF’s Summary

Mrim is a brain scientist turned career coach turned social entrepreneur. Both at Brown University and at the Kelley School of Business, Mrim has successfully coached hundreds of liberal arts graduates, MBA students and PhD candidates interested in pursuing careers that successfully blend financial return with social impact and environmental responsibility.

Mrim created a career management and career transition process that is tailored to the needs of professionals interested in socio-eco innovation careers in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), social enterprises, and nonprofit management.

By combining her 10 years of studying how the brain works with her in depth knowledge of career planning, career switching strategies and job search techniques, Mrim has developed a proven career management and job search system that will make your career and job search faster and easier.

Start today by visiting www.purposeu.com to participate in one of Mrim's webinars or to schedule your free 30-minute career consulting session!

Career coaching, social impact careers, environmentally responsible careers, career switching strategies, branding, internship search strategies, global job search strategies, interviewing skills, professional correspondence review, resume development, negotiation skills, entrepreneurship, leveraging virtual classroom environments.
