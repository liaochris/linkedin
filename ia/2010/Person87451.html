Jake Thompson’s Summary

Financial Strategies is one of my primary interests. I work closely with my Dad in teaching people how to use strategies and concepts to create wealth, rather than talk about your traditional forms of investment. Helping people see new avenues for creating wealth and not having to rely on risk brings me a lot of satisfaction.

On the other hand I am still young and very energetic. My generation is very involved in creating a transparent online presence, so I have been able to work with my brother and brother-in-law in developing and implementing Social Media as a business venture. Part of which involves a free class we teach on the subject.
