Tim Majors’s Summary

Am an Australian based attorney who has specialized in taking a small cap Australian company national. Grew a small cap from $5 to $55m over a 12 year period, before selling to private equity in 2008.

In January 2009, I relocated to the US, and now have the business goal of growing Pedal to Properties into a major real estate firm in this country.

My firm goals are to turn Pedal to Properties (www.pedaltoproperties) into a recognizable and significant brand both in this country and beyond. We have engaged the services of PCMS Consulting to assist us with these goals. Our franchise model will commence selling franchises on 10/1/2009
