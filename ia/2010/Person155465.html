Nino Rajacic’s Summary

I've being composing music for more than 15 years and have experience in sound recording and engineering. In last few years I'm working as freelance Composer and Sound Designer in Computer Games industry where I delivered Music and sound mostly for indie and casual games.

In 2008 I founded with fellow composers Jaap Visser, and Chris Hipgrave international studio www.3peakaudio.com in order to boost up our services quality and fast delivery of audio assets.

Our goal is to develop our business and to provide Audio assets for major game developers.

Nino Rajacic’s Specialties:

* high quality music and sound design in a large variety of styles
* use of top notch software and libraries in production (EWQL, Vienna)
* quick and fast delivery, high level of communication
* foley recording, adaptive music and sound design
* live orchestra recordings
