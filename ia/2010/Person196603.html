Stuart's Personal Background:

Beginning his franchise career in 1997, Stuart set sales records and turn-around achievements as a multi-unit franchisee & area developer with Cousins Subs and Dunn Bros. Coffee. He opened eleven stores in just five years (six owned / five independent franchisees), and outpaced his development schedule, winning recognition in marketing, franchise development and same-store sales growth.

After developing a retail strip center with a partnership in Eagan, MN, Stuart sold his franchise interests and began consulting business owners in commercial-investment real estate and exit strategies.

He's an expert in creating unique business solutions in tough circumstances involving business sales, partnerships, dispute resolution and workout negotiations.

An engaging and motivating speaker, Stuart is active in the IFA and an instructor to realtors seeking continuing education in retail leasing and franchising.
