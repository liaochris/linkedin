Jason Gerlach’s Summary

I am a Principal and Director of Strategic Planning for Sunrise Capital Partners of San Diego, a leader in alternative investments which proudly celebrates its 30th anniversary in 2010.

Since its inception, Sunrise has pursued innovative systematic macro strategies that have yielded outstanding, risk-adjusted results for investors while offering high levels of transparency, liquidity and regulatory oversight. Utilizing a robust trading system, Sunrise trades a diverse range of global futures and currency markets. Sunrise currently manages over $800 million in client and proprietary assets and has delivered 13% plus net annual returns over the past 15 years. Sunrise's returns have historically low correlation to traditional investment categories, have frequently been negatively correlated with major drops in the S&P Index, and have outperformed all major asset classes on an absolute basis since 1980.
