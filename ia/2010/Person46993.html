Daniel Berkowitz’s Summary

Postsecondary Education Management professional with experience focused upon Disability Support Services, academic and technology support, and program development and implementation.

Provider of consultation and training on matters of access technology and access to alternative formats for students and individuals with disabilities.

Co-Founder and CEO of Digilife Media, an independent company dedicated to supporting institutions and organizations that provide direct services to students and individuals with print related disabilities.
