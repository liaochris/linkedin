Carolyn Coleridge, LCSW’s Summary

I have been a psychotherapist for 15 years in NY and CA and an energetic healer for 10 years. I am also a spiritual life coach and a speaker at seminars and workshops. I have written articles for wholistic health magazines,and write a monthly newsletter. I have appeared on TV Shows including CNN International and several Internet radio programs discussing spiritual topics. I have a deep inner insight into the motivations of others. I am a visionary helping others to see their higher life purpose.


I assist individuals to listen to their bodies messages, trust their heart and align with their soul's messages. I love inspiring groups and individuals to live their life with purpose and trust their intution. I am a strong advocate of meditation, creating and listening to your inner truth. I graduated from Columbia University with a Masters in social work and I am becoming a Certified Natural Health Practitioner.
