John Mayfield’s Summary

I wish I could say that I have been in the "importing and exporting" business like George from Seinfeld, (since this is listed as a category for LinkedIn) however, I have only been involved with real estate most of my life, (since the age of 18, 1978). I owned and operated a real estate company for 28 years with three branch offices, and as many as 35 professionals during my tenure, and helped thousands of families with their real estate needs. I sold my company in 2006, and currently work for RE/MAX Best Choice in St. Louis, MO as a broker/associate, and available to help consumers with their real estate needs in the South St. Louis area. I also teach and coach real estate agents around the U.S. I am a senior GRI (Graduate REALTORS Institute) instructor for the Missouri Association of REALTORS, and the Arkansas Association of REALTORS. I am also an instructor for the Council of Real Estate Brokers and Managers, CRB, in Chicago, IL. I operate www.RealEstateSalesMeetings.com. and www.RealEstateTechGuy.com, where I help real estate brokers and agents from around the world with their real estate needs. Maybe importing and exporting doesn't sound that exciting after all.

I write for the National Association of REALTORS online magazine, "Sales Coach" column, and also write for HP's (Hewlett Packard) web site.

I live and breathe real estate and love to help brokers and agents with technology and marketing applications.
