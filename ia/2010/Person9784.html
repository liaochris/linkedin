Amjad Hussain’s Summary

Amjad Hussain, the President of SilkRoute, has 20 years of experience leading global supply chain and software technology corporations. His present focus is on implementing SilkRoute "Any Source to Any Shelf" trading platform for world's largest retailers and their suppliers using a Software as a Service (SaaS) model.

SilkRoute Global (SRG) is a world wide provider of VMI and supply chain solutions with offices in the US, United Kingdom and India. The SilkRoute team has been designing, building and operating robust, scalable vendor-managed inventory and supply chain solutions for more than a decade. Indeed, the SRG team has grown up operating the home entertainment businesses of the world's largest retailers. With a product suite that has been deployed across three continents and dozens of retailers and home entertainment content owners, SilkRoute delivers a level of maturity and capability that is unparalleled in the industry.

Prior to founding SilkRoute, Amjad has held several business unit head and CIO/CTO positions with major public and private corporations in North America .

Amjad is a General Partner of NextWave, a $100 MM VC fund and accelerator focused on emerging technologies. He coaches the leadership of the portfolio companies.

Amjad is active in Metro Detroit Community. He is involved in several projects aimed at enhancing the local economy. Amjad is a member of the board of trustees at RIM. His efforts to help the local economy have been recognized by the Governor of State of Michigan.

Amjad is an Honors Electrical Engineering graduate. He earned his Masters from UMIST, UK with focus on Cybernetics. Amjad is a Kellogg Executive Scholar. He is an executive member of Global Coalition for Efficient Logistics. He completed the Advanced Management College at Stanford. Amjad has been recognized in Crain’s Detroit top 40 executives under the age of 40 for his pioneering work in the field of Business Intelligence.
