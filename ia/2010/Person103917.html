Jonathan Lea’s Summary

I work on a wide range of corporate and commercial matters at Follett Stock LLP, an entrepreneurial law firm with offices in Truro and Exeter in the South West of England. I previously worked in the City and Dubai and have experience of many complex international transactions, together with smaller-scale legal and business issues.

I aim to provide clients with a highly practical, very responsive, solutions-driven and cost efficient level of service. I provide rounded commerical advice as well as advising on the usual legal angles of deals. I ensure that I communicate in plain English and properly articulate my advice.

I have a highly collaborative way of working and like to use an array of legal contacts and specialists, together with IT and social media to provide clients with bespoke and innovative solutions and ways of doing business.

Jonathan Lea’s Specialties:

Mergers and acquisitions, equity capital markets and private investments, debt finance, private equity transactions, commercial contracts, insolvency, intellectual property and dispute resolution.

    (Privately Held; Law Practice industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    v) on many share transfers, subscriptions and asset transactions for SMEs and individuals.
