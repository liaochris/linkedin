Thomas Coolidge’s Summary

Direct Response Pros, Inc specializes in custom direct response marketing and sales campaigns. DRP uses Internet, Radio, TV, Print, Call Centers and SEO, to drive consumer inquiries and/or hot transfers. Direct Response Pros, Inc. is one of the few companies out there that has the ability to not only drive leads but close them using one of our call centers.

A sample of our verticals include debt negotiation/debt settlement, tax debt, loan modification, diabetic supply, satellite TV, travel, EDU/Education, life and health insurance leads.

If you are looking to create a custom lead generation and marketing campaign or exploring alternate media placement opportunities for your service and product, feel free to contact us for a no obligation consultation.
