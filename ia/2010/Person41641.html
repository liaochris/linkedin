Clifton Anderson’s Summary

A gifted communicator, coach and leader, Clifton has a unique ability to bring out the very best in people. He has worked with the most respected companies, organizations, and universities and brings a wealth of leadership experience. Clifton speaks from a place of personal experience, not just something he has merely read or studied. His methods of leadership development and personal growth are not only tested, they are also proven.

As a former senior executive, Clifton now shares the leadership secrets that propelled him to an outstanding record of achievement and success in corporate America. While these keen insights were developed in a professional setting, he explains how they also can be applied not only in your career, but also to everyday living--enabling you to live a more fulfilled life.

Upon hearing Clifton speak, Les Brown, the world-renowned motivational speaker, dubbed him "The Voice of Leadership." Clifton brings a potent but rare combination of creativity and analytical thinking to his speaking engagements. As a result, he can inspire audiences in a way few motivational speakers can.

Clifton holds an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. He has worked in or traveled to over thirty-five countries and six of the seven continents.

Clifton Anderson’s Specialties:

Demonstrated accomplishment in Keynote Addresses, Leadership Development, High-performance Teams, Coaching and Development, Facilitated Problem Solving, Executive Communication, Strategic and Long-range Planning, Large-scale Change Management, Global Expansion, and Productivity Improvements.
