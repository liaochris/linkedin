Abel Thomas van Staveren’s Experience

  • CEO

    Pelican Shipping Limited

    (Maritime industry)

    February 2010 — Present (3 months)

    Pelican Shipping Limited is the commercial agent of SKS

    SKS is the operating brand of two shipping companies sharing common interests.

    SKS OBO Limited, Bermuda, is a company owned by a subsidiary of Kristian Gerhard Jebsen Skipsrederi A/S, Norway (KGJS).

    SKS OBO Limited controls 12 Aframax-size Ore-Bulk-Oil (OBO) Carriers – 10 of 110,000tons deadweight, and 2 of 121,000tons deadweight.

    The concept of ships that are able to load both wet and dry cargoes is not new, however it has been refined in recent years to counter the operational shortcomings of earlier generations of such vessel. The criticisms levelled at older generations of OBOs have been answered one by one by the design of the vessels of the SKS fleet. The company controls two modern Aframax size product carriers on long-term timecharter and is taking delivery of a series of large coated Aframax tankers in 2010-12.

    SKS Tankers Limited, Bermuda, is a company also owned by a subsidiary of KGJS and controls eight 160,000tons deadweight Suezmax-size tankers. These innovative vessels have pure epoxy coated cargo tanks and certain additional fittings to enable the vessels to transport sensitive cargoes such as jet fuel. As such, these vessels are the largest engaged in this trade.

    Technical management of the SKS fleet is handled by KGJ OBO & Tankers Fleet Management AS in Bergen, and Columbia Shipmanagement in Hamburg.
