Kelly Tompkins’s Summary

Accomplished entrepreneur with 15 years of experience in developing large scale, transaction based travel and ecommerce companies.

My current project, ReserveX is a service that aggregates primary and secondary event ticketing markets, while applying airline yield management practices to increase venue profitability. My previous company, AdventureLink provides a centralized reservation platform for the adventure travel industry. Previous to this, I founded LeisureLink, the leading global reservation network for vacation rentals.

As an entrepreneur, I have learned over the years how to effectively turn vision into reality and would like to leverage my expertise to guide other organizations in creating new products and services.

Kelly Tompkins’s Specialties:

New product & business development, online travel technology, distribution, ecommerce, content aggregation strategies, leveraging emerging technologies & building strategic partnerships.

    (Entertainment industry)

    December 2009 — Present (2 months)

    ReserveX is a content aggregation switch for event ticketing providers. We aggregate primary and secondary ticketing markets, while applying yield management technology to increase overall profitability for venues. For affiliate partners we provide a standardized XML interface for integrating event ticketing within your web site or through mobile, location based applications. ReserveX is founded by travel and technology industry veterans and will be launching in the spring of 2010.
