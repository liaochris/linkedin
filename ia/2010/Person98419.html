  • Lead Author

    IBM White Paper on Green IT

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    2008 — February 2009 (1 year )

    Leading organizations are gaining 3 significant benefits by reducing CO2 emissions:

    1. Generating revenue
    2. Cutting costs
    3. Reducing regulatory risk by emissions reduction leadership

    Data Centers consume HUGE amounts of power
    Once a company sets a goal of reducing its carbon footprint, data centers immediately come into focus, as they consume 10 to 30 times more energy per square foot than regular office space, and at the extreme up to 100 times more electricity.

    IBM is saving $450 million over 5 years -- and 5 billion kWhrs in the 5th year -- by virtualizing the applications from 3,900 distributed servers onto just 33 large servers -- cutting electricity use by >80%, square footage by >85% and licensing costs by >99%!
