Evan Eggers’s Summary

As president and co-founder of the SureCruise® Internet Cruise Superstore (GoDifferent LLC), Evan has grown SureCruise into one of the top 1% of cruise sellers nationwide, without debt and without reliance on venture capital, all while delivering a customer experience where more than 9 of 10 customers would strongly recommend SureCruise’s services to a friend and no Better Business Bureau complaints have been received--a nearly impossible feat in this business segment.

Evan previously led marketing, sales and software engineering efforts at travel, transportation, and technology firms, including as Director of Marketing & Sales at travel powerhouse Sabre. This ideal background enabled Evan to succeed in the cut-throat and low-margin online travel sales business, ensuring SureCruise’s high-service, high-expertise, high-technology model carved a successful niche against the Goliath order-takers of online travel and generated enviable per-unit premium cruise sales.

Outside of work, Evan is an avid amateur triathlete, having completed, along with wife Jana, his first two full Ironman 140.6-mile events in 2008. His next Ironman is November 29, 2009, Ironman Cozumel.
