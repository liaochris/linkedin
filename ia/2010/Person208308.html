Trish Friberg’s Summary

As a top Real Estate agent with Mira Vista Properties I hold the designation of GRI which is achieved by only top Real Estate agents. I am a member of SEVRAR Multi-Million Dollar Producer, 100% Club, President's Club, Executive Club . I am a member of the South East Valley Association of Realtors, the Arizona Association of Realtors, and the National Association of Realtors.

With great experience in Scottsdale, Gilbert, Tempe, Phoenix, Paradise Valley, and Mesa Real Estate as well as intimate local area knowledge, I work extensively to help buyers and sellers meet their Real Estate goals.

If you are looking to buy or sell a home or would just like to know some more information, feel free to call or Email me.
For buyers, I help negotiate the best possible deal and search until I find the perfect property. Nothing satisfies me more than seeing the smiles that come with a successful sale or the purchase of a lifetime.

As a listing agent in Maricopa county, my extensive marketing plan gives a homeowner the opportunity to get the maximum value for his/her property.

I believe in giving back to the community. I have been highly involved in the Susan Komen Foundation for the past 12 years. I also give to the Children's Miracle Network. I recently dedicated my time to assist in planning several successful charity events, (Phoenix Children's Beach Ball) for the Phoenix Children's Hospital, the ProPlayers Classic Golf Tournament, The Michael Finney Dry Heat Classic just to name a few. I highly believe in a quote by Sir Winston Churchill. "We make a living by what we get, we make a life by what we give."

Thank you for choosing me,Trish Friberg as your on-line Scottsdale, Gilbert, Tempe, Phoenix, Paradise Valley, Mesa and the rest of Maricopa County's real estate source.
