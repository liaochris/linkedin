Divya Gunaseelan’s Summary

“I regard a great ad as the most beautiful thing in the world.”- Leo Burnett

Just like Leo Burnett said, I believe when great minds combine they make great ads. I’m striving to make great ads. My name is Divya Gunaseelan and I recently graduated from Michigan State University with a Bachelor in advertising. Even after my four year degree in advertising, I feel like there’s always more to learn about the industry since it’s an ever evolving field. I’m currently seeking an opportunity to pursue my dreams in this field. I’m a self-motivator who drives to excel. I don’t settle for mediocre therefore I’m a perfectionist when it comes to meticulous details, organization, time management and other important responsibilities.

What’s my passion?
My passion. The Advertising industry. The variety that you can never find in any other field. The immediate results. The fast-paced lifestyle. The casual fun. The creativity that never sleeps. The flexibility in working hours. The clients that believe in us. The media that gets us out there. The competitors who help us grow. The witty copy. The eye catching colors. The graphics that make you stop whatever you’re doing. The stress. The feeling of accomplishment… Basically everything about it. When I interned at JWT, my favorite part was when I work at a recording studio on one day, maybe at a photo shoot the very next day and something completely different the following day. I particularly chose the management side of advertising because I have better people skills than a good eye for graphics.
To sum it all up, I’m a Spartan from MSU who fears nothing, ready to venture into the exciting field of advertising, armed with my advertising degree, passion for advertising and my willingness to learn.
