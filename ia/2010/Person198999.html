Tad Benson’s Summary

I am a marketing executive with nearly 20 years of direct and online marketing experience. I am looking to continue my career in an executive level marketing role at a company where interactive marketing is a key to corporate success.

I led the Internet channel marketing for 21st Century Insurance (now owned by Farmers Insurance) and demonstrated top and bottom-line success by increasing monthly online leads by 772% and monthly online sales by 1,097%. I did that while simultaneously reducing the channel’s cost per sale by 88%.

I recently ran a software company’s re-branding campaign that generated well over 50 million free press impressions and increased traffic to company’s Web site by over 2,000%.

I have run everything from multi-million-dollar integrated on and off-line campaigns to free social media campaigns. I was also the 38th employee at GoTo.com where I ran online marketing for the company that invented paid search (SEM).
