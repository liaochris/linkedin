Paul Zimmerman’s Summary

Currently Marketplace Director for Play.com, one of the UK's largest e-commerce retailers. Play's marketplace platform is PlayTrade.

Member of the Executive Leadership Team (ELT), reporting to the CEO.

Prior to this I served Play.com as Head of Business Development from 2005-2010.

Previously I was a senior manager at both Amazon.co.uk (4 years) and Woolworths Group (2 years), where I led the business units responsible for driving e-commerce retail sales for both physical and digital music products.
