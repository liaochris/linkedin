About Mýa
In 2010, Grammy Award Winning Artist Mýa is celebrating 12 years in the entertainment industry as an internationally known, respected and loved singer, songwriter, dancer, actress and philanthropist. Most Recently, Mýa entertained the world and impressed millions as the highest scoring contestant on ABC’s Season 9 of Dancing With the Stars. Mýa finished out the decade as one of Billboard’s Hot 100 Artists of the Decade, a Screen Actors Guild (SAG) Award-Winning Actress and as the President and founder of her own non-profit foundation, The Mýa Arts & Tech Foundation. Mýa, whose extensive repertoire includes the hits, "Case of the Ex," “Ghetto Superstar,” “Free,” "Lady Marmalade" and "My Love Is Like...Wo,” is currently preparing for the eagerly anticipated world-wide release of her next studio album due out later this year. Mýa will continue her philanthropic endeavors in 2010 serving as the national spokesperson for the North Shore Animal League. In May 2010, Mýa had ankle surgery and post-recovery/healing will release the single, "Love is the Answer"- a collaboration with Cedric Gervais of Ultra Records in August 2010.
