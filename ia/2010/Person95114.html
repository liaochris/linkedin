Jennifer Waxman-Loyd’s Summary

Central Florida-based Company, Sustainable Synergy, Inc., is a green business-consulting company that merges environmentally sustainable practices with profitable results. We work with organizations that want to enter or expand into the Green marketplace; focusing on a triple bottom line. Specifically, we focus on strategic planning, business development, and marketing within the Green and Sustainable marketplace.

The key to our success is accessing the marketplace for Lifestyles of Health and Sustainability (LOHAS). This market encompasses health & wellness, green, and sustainable business & living segments. While once considered a niche market for health-conscious consumers environmental activists, LOHAS has grown mightily, and was valued at $209 billion in 2006 in the U.S. alone. That figure has grown substantially. Sustainable Synergy works with businesses already serving LOHAS to grow and prosper, as well as helping established companies expand their market base to reach LOHAS buyers.

We provide professional services, including the fulfillment of virtual Executive Management roles if needed. Our professional services include, but are not limited to: developing Business Plans and Marketing Plans along with the implementation and follow-through for each Plan (we will assist your team to reach the goals and action items of each Plan). Therefore, SS helps to assist organizations seeking to capture the LOHAS marketplace with Strategic Positioning, Fund Raising (including Private, Debt and Grant funding vehicles), Product Development, Sales Distribution Channels, Marketing, Public Relations and Education to serve the US LOHAS (Lifestyles of Health & Sustainability) marketplace.
