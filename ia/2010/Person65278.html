Everett Pope’s Summary

In 2001 I embarked on this journey to become a successful young professional. In the month of June with just 2 weeks training under my belt, I hit the sales floor at Tracy Toyota in Northern California. In just 30 days I was named the Top Sales Associate. And in 60 days I was honored with Top Sales Associate once again. At which point I knew I was destined for sales.

Over the past 8 years still treading the course to become a successful young professional I have been fortunate to receive some of the best training and work experience many could ask for.

Sales, Marketing and Promotional work has been a passion of mine since the very start back in 2001, and I have soon come to find out there is no end to the knowledge and tools to be learnt in each field.

With my vast knowledge of sales, promotional and marketing work I have often times been recruited for management. At this point in time I am no longer in management and focusing on honing my craft however, I am ideally working towards a senior management level position and eventually ownership.
