Nick West’s Summary

I use my experience to help other business owners define and acheive their goals. In doing so I want to spread my business philosophy to others who have skills, experience and ability to share with local businesses and boost their local economies.

I've been working with and in entrepreneurial businesses for the past 24 years as a chartered accountant, consultant, trainer, coach and professional yachtsman.

Nick West’s Specialties:

Training seminars, Mastermind groups, business coaching, strategic and financial consulting and CFO services.

  • Group Financial Control

    The Pelican Group Plc

    (Restaurants industry)

    April 1994 — December 1998 (4 years 9 months)

    As the company grew my responsibilities changed considerably as Group Financial Controller. I was responsible for the internal reporting, budgeting and forecasting. I developed a team of 35 staff, ran training courses for restaurant managers and chefs, acted as IT manager for the first 18 months. I was responsible for the implementation of and upgrade of a group wide reporting system, Sun Accounts on a SQL platform, and it's integration with the POS systems in use across the business.
