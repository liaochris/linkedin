Mark V. Cerney’s Summary

Mr. Cerney is the President and founder of the National Next of Kin Registry (NOKR). Mark has been involved in a number of organizations committed to connecting families in crisis situations. Mr. Cerney has also been involved with the California Youth Authority, San Diego County Medical Examiners Office CA, Clark County Coroners Office Las Vegas, NV and Fire District 3 in WA.

Mr. Cerney is a US Marine Corp veteran who served Honorably with the 1st Marine Expeditionary Force 3rd Battalion 1st Marines from 1986-1993. In 1989 the United States Navy Amphibious Forces Seventh Fleet recognized him for his role in saving 16 U.S. Marines survivors of a CH-53D helicopter crash in Korea. Mr. Cerney also served Honorably in the United States Army National Guard as a combat medic from 1994-1997.

Mr. Cerney has trained through the National Emergency Response and Rescue Training Center (NERRTC) at Texas A&M's Domestic Preparedness Campus. His public service efforts are being mentioned in a new book due to be released soon called Wisdom Out by Elle Allison. In 2009, Mr. Cerney became a Law Enforcement Officer, commissioned by Governor Mark Sanford. Mr. Cerney's work with NOKR has been recognized as a national standard and endorsed by the American Red Cross, Governors of 46 states, numerous Congressional leaders including John McCain (R - AZ), Jim DeMint (R-SC), and then Senator - now President Barack Obama. Under Mr. Cerney's leadership, NOKR has reached out across the oceans and has a presence with volunteers in 87 countries worldwide.

In 2005, then Senator Barack Obama now United States President introduced the National Next of Kin Registry to the 109th Congress in S.1630, The National Emergency Family Locator Act. The Next of Kin Registry was referenced in this bill as a standard for the Secretary of Homeland Security to consider in establishing the National Emergency Family Locator System.

Mark V. Cerney’s Specialties:

In 2007 the US Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) consulted with the Next of Kin Registry in an effort to answer HR5441 (Department of Homeland Security Appropriations Act, 2007), SEC. 689c. NOKR put forth the requested solution for the National Emergency Family Registry and Locator System (NEFRLS), which was established in compliance with Congressional Legislation SEC. 689c of H.R. 5441 to help family members separated after major disasters to communicate with one another.
