Tiffany Trotter, MBA’s Summary

I am an finance professional with 15+ years of experience.

My experience has allowed me to work in varied industries such as energy, chemical and manufacturing. As a result my experience has allowed me to work with Operations personnel as well as Product Managers/Sales Persons and Start Up Managers. Often times these people are not experienced financial managers so I provide a liaison between Finance and the business operations.

My key responsibilities have been financial statement analysis, data gathering and mining, creating management reports for review, providing variance analysis, building models, preparing/uploading forecasts and managing budgeting processes full cycle that would include consolidation and upload of financials. In addition, I have prepared authorizations for expenditures, managed capital budgets and tracked capital spending and worked with Fixed Assets.I also have experience with tracking job cost activity on production activities and managing the related invoicing process.
