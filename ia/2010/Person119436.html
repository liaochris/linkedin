Kristoffer Sheather’s Summary

Kristoffer Sheather is the founder and CTO of Cloud Central, an Australian based startup company focussed on Cloud Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS).

Kristoffer Sheather has over 10 years experience in the IT industry, including experience in a number of roles such as consulting, software development, and solution architecture roles with organizations including Hewlett Packard, Radiant Systems, Praxa, Melbourne City Council, Department of Employment, Education and Workplace Relations (DEEEWR), Department of Industry, Science and Resources (DISR), InTACT and Cloud Central.

Kristoffer Sheather has a Bachelor of Science majoring in Computer Science from Deakin University, and am currently engaged in a Postgraduate Graduate Certificate of Entrepreneurship and Innovation via the University of Adelaide. My other qualifications include being an Australian Computer Society (ACS) Companion Member, and a member of the Canberra.NET IT cluster.

Kristoffer Sheather has been an Army reservist within the Australian Defense Force (ADF) for over 11 years. Kristoffer SHeather has extensive experience with a range of technologies and methodologies, including development tools, database systems, internet, networking, systems analysis, business analysis, process design, systems architecture, and more.
