Nathan Brown’s Summary

Nathan Brown followed his passion for art before he even read well. By the time he was 6, the impact of visuals and the powerful emotion they can convey had his full attention. Comic books and graphic novels led to the serious practical and theoretical study of art and photography through his school years and into college. With a major in Graphic Design and Multimedia, Nathan graduated in 2000 from the prestigious Art Institute in Dallas, Texas.

His first corporate job found him designing and authoring DVDs and interactive CD-ROMs for two years. That position led to another as lead designer for a company where his primary role was to maintain client relationships and design and author websites based on project needs. All the while, he maintained a steady stream of happy freelance clients, as well, creating web design, book covers, movie posters, album covers, corporate branding concepts, and logo illustration. One of those freelance clients was Writers of the Round Table Inc. The sum of that eight years of experience was an apt proving ground for his joining the Writers of the Round Table team in October 2007.
