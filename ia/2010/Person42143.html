Sylvia Moore, PCC’s Summary

Sylvia Moore is a Professional Certified Coach (P.C.C.) credentialed by the International Coach Federation (ICF). This situates her at the top of certified coaches both nationally and internationally. She coaches clients on leadership, strengths building, creativity in the workplace, career development and transition, and emotional intelligence. As the founder and CEO of Moore For YOU Coaching, her clientele bridges from solo-preneurs to the corporate world (such as Texas Instruments, IBM, GE Capital, FedEx, Pepsico, Blue Cross Blue Shield, to other small businesses). Sylvia’s coaching practice spans the globe into Sweden, France, Great Britain, Israel, Canada, Italy, Ukraine and the Caribbean.

Sylvia attended coaching school at one of the first three universities in the country to include coaching in their curriculum: the University of Texas at Dallas (UTD) Executive and Professional Coaching Program (EPC) through the Graduate School of Management. She has over 1800 hours of one-on-one experience coaching clients and has completed more than 600 Coach training hours at two different coaching schools: one track focused on life coaching, the other focused on business and corporate coaching. Both are specially approved programs with the ICF. Sylvia is also on the coaching staff of the UTD Executive MBA Program and is an active member of the very first local ICF chapter, ICF-North Texas, and also of the parent group ICF, (see www.coachfederation.org).

Sylvia Moore, PCC’s Specialties:

Sylvia coaches on leadership, strengths building, creativity in the workplace, career development and transition, and emotional intelligence for managers, software developers, for students (undergrad & grad students, eMBA), and the healthcare industry. She is also a certified career coach and works with clients on career development & management.
