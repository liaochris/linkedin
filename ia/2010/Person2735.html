Adam Stein’s Summary

As the co-founder of a social enterprise committed to addressing climate change, I have deep understanding of the political, technical, and financial dimensions of clean technology and greenhouse gas reduction strategies.

More generally, I have spent my entire career working in entrepreneurial settings bringing innovative products to market. Much of my experience is in software product development, where I have been able to draw heavily on my problem-solving skills, technical expertise, and ability to communicate effectively with a variety of different stakeholders.

My goal is to continue to apply my skills to areas where they have the most leverage by directing my energy and creativity to hard problems that affect the greatest number of people.
