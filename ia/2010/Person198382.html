Suzy Teele’s Summary

Suzy Teele joined Matrix Solutions in 2009 as the company’s President and Chief Operating Officer. Ms. Teele brings to Matrix Solutions over twenty years of experience in software product management, strategy, marketing and development, with an expertise in customer relationship management (CRM). As President and COO, Ms. Teele will be responsible for leading software development, product management, technical and customer support, training, sales and marketing.

Matrix Solutions is the leading provider of sales management software for media companies in broadcast, cable, radio, newspaper, and the internet. Our software products, Matrix SalesCenter™ and MatrixPlus™, help sales professionals develop and implement strategies to grow advertising revenue by providing the answers to questions about both sales history and sales trends, and the CRM tools to manage and grow opportunities. Founded by a media company executive in 1992, Matrix Solutions serves approximately 10,000 customers at over 800 outlets representing more than 50 media groups throughout North America. Some of our customers include Cox Television, Entercom, Entravision, TimeWarner Cable, LIN Television, the Tampa Tribune and many others.

Most recently, Ms. Teele was founder and managing partner of Aceda, a management consulting firm that provided companies across the United State with interim executives for sales, marketing and business development.
