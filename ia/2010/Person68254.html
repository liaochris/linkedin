Evelyn Torres’s Summary

Evelyn Torres serves as Sr. Manager of Purchasing Process and Technology for Church & Dwight Co., Inc. developing and steering the department’s process redesign and technology enablement projects. Evelyn Torres has recently been assigned the role of User Lead/Project Leader collaborating with IT to build the global framework for all technology within a C&D manufacturing facility.

Prior to Church & Dwight Co., Inc. Evelyn worked for 8 years at Pfizer, Inc. in the roles of Buyer, Purchasing Specialist, and Project Manager. In these roles she developed continuous improvement programs, cost savings initiatives, created Buyer training materials and supervised in the purchasing and inventory management space. Evelyn served as relationship manager between the Materials Mgt Team and IT group in data mgt, systems and process enhancement as well as representing Procurement in projects throughout the business.

She has a passion for humanitarian endeavors with a particular focus on projects for the homeless and economic empowerment of the poor through training and micro-enterprise support. Through many projects opportunities have arisen allowing her to also be of service to children impacted by homelessness, HIV/AIDS, and poverty. Evelyn was one of the 1st Pfizer Global Health Fellows (October 2003) to be assigned abroad partnering with Non-Profit Groups on projects related to the AIDS pandemic. As a health fellow, Evelyn worked as a business strategist for Project HOPE in Honduras.

Other past philanthropic projects are: Habitat for Humanity, NYCares Team Leadership (soup kitchens, developed/facilitated basic business skills course for spanish speakers, and childrens projects); Holiday projects for the children of Women in Need (NYC).

Evelyn graduated from New York University with a Bachelors degree in International Studies (Global Political and Economic Studies) and obtained a Graduate Certificate in Project Management from Stevens Institute of Technology.
