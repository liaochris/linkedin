Iri Amirav’s Summary

Currently President, Chief Marketing Officer and Co-Founder of iMedix, the owner of the award winning website iMedix.com and a platform for publisher solutions on the web. The iMedix platform reaches more than 150 million unique visitors every month and allows dozens of Internet and media companies around the world to increase revenues, repeat visits, content, pageviews and brand loyalty.

Formerly, established and led Metacafe's online marketing activities to position it as one of the world's largest video sites, attracting more than 40 million unique viewers each month (comScore Media Metrix) watching 500+ million page views per month.

Prior to that, supervised the creative content and business partnerships department for Pelephone communications Ltd - Israel’s pioneer mobile operator. Increased revenues from content and services to a record of $12MM, turning the company to profitability and placing the mobile content division as one of the top growth engines of the entire company ever since.


Formerly, managed the marketing and business development for the A.F.D group, a Washington DC based private investment company of entertainment ventures.

Prior to that, lead various cross-platform marketing activities for AOL Time Warner (NYC Time Warner Cable) and directed new advertising applications projects for McDonald’s, Pepsi and Sony on behalf of MVT Inc.

Served 3 years in the Israeli Defense Forces as a combat soldier in the Golani commando Reconnaissance Platoon and later on as a budget officer. Received his BBA with honors from Baruch College, City University of New York.
