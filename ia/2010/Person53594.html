Deborah Evans’s Summary

Spa and Hospitality Consultant and Principal of Deborah Evans & Associates and SpaSense Group, LLC, and has created and designed, developed, consulted, marketed and managed select destination, resort and day spas. Providing expert spa and hospitality advice to both new and existing spas and hotels/resorts for over twenty-six years. Interested in developing unique, handcrafted spas and lodging facilities.

Deborah Evans’s Specialties:

Spa concepts, design, operations, management and marketing. Spa operational audits. Hotel and resort operations consulting and audits. Designing wellness and recreational programs.
