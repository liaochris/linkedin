Jonathan Hall’s Summary

Cranberry Panda was launched with a clear vision of matching great opportunities with great people. Over the past 10 years I have worked with many ecommerce teams and passionate individuals who are committed to making ecommerce a success within their business.

The world of ecommerce is constantly evolving and improving which makes it fantastic sector to work in. My aim with Cranberry Panda is to use the wide ecommerce knowledge and contacts I have built up during the last decade to match people with roles and vice versa.

The people behind Cranberry Panda have set up agencies, online shops, emarketing departments, worked with big high street names so we know what you need.

If you are looking for a great person or a great opportunity then talk to me and I’ll help. Check us out on twitter also http://twitter.com/cranberrypanda
