Michael Cosentino’s Summary

I have been in Real Estate Finance, Real Estate Investments and Training for over 20 years. I have held positions as CEO, Vice President, Managing Partner, Branch Manager and Loan Consultant.

I take great pride in helping distressed property owners find solutions thru short sale mitigation or private investor purchase.

Some of my accomplishments include, Top 100 originator in the U.S (Mortgage Originator Magazine), Top 25 FHA originator in the U.S. (Mortgage Originator Magazine), 5 time national retail originating champion for one of the largest mortgage companies in the U.S., CEO of a 45 office Multi state mortgage company, designed, built, and acted as one of the lead instructors for a Corporate University and have created many successful C.B.A.'s with both real estate brokerage firms and title companies. I have created several successful web based training platforms for the Real Estate industry.

I am an expert in the field of Real Estate Investing and Financing distressed Real Estate.

I am passionate about helping Real Estate Agents and Real Estate Investors learn how to become better professionals and how to reach the highest levels of success. I accomplish this through training, products, and articles I create.
