Gee Ranasinha’s Summary

Primarily, I’m a strategist and communicator of your business value.

I established my professional and creative reputation by founding and running a successful media production company, working with advertising agencies such as J. Walter Thompson, McCann Erickson and Leo Burnett for clients such as Ford, Unilever, LVMH and Nestlé.

For almost seven years, I was Director of Marketing for a European-based company that develops software for the printing and publishing industries, with clients such as Brown Printing, Axel Springer, Vertis, TAGWorldwide and Hachette Filipacchi.

Today, through my company KEXINO, we help your company reach its goals by partnering with your sales and marketing staff to deliver a range of strategic and operational Marketing and Business Development services. KEXINO is based in France, though our current client base stretches from San Diego, USA to Frankfurt, Germany.

In short, we support your company with the experience, expertise and resources of an extensive marketing department - without the fixed and costly overhead of full-time staff.
