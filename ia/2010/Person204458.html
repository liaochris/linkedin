Tim Syrianos’s Summary

He entered the Real Estate industry in 1989 and over the years developed extensive knowledge in all aspects of real estate including organizing and speaking at seminars, Interviewed in Newspaper media and TV (CBC, CFTO, CTV and Global), involved in 100's of sales of all price ranges, personal and corporate relocation as a Registered Relocation Specialist (CERC), new home Land assembly, Commercial, Investment and Property management.

During sales career continued to be the recipient of many RE/MAX achievement awards every year and has achieved the RE/MAX Hall of Fame.

Entire career has been spent building a reputation with both clients and other Realtors as a loyal, professional and dedicated individual.

With the support of his wife and 2 young children, Tim has pursued his dream of building a Brokerage that is second to none, to focus on his passion of Mentoring and Coaching his entire sales Staff to be successful. RE/MAX Ultimate has a full in house training centre and we’re always providing our agents the opportunity to keep up to date with seminars and programs in every area. We keep our associates on track to grow their career, updated on all necessary industry changes, technology and market trends.

Since 1985, the firm has continued to maintain a fantastic reputation in the central core of our vibrant city and continuous to thrive as more successful Realtors join the company with each passing year.
