Bert Kleinman’s Summary

Highly motivated, innovative, broadcast executive and consultant with over 40 years of successful U.S. and international media experience. Demonstrated ability to solve difficult problems by combining strong leadership with visionary, strategic, and tactical expertise. Excellent written and verbal communications skills.

Accomplishments in a wide variety of areas including:

* Developing and implementing focused strategic plans and cost-effective budgets
* Launching start-ups efficiently and on schedule
* Designing and implementing compelling programming
* Managing effectively and hands-on for impressive results
* Planning and implementing targetted marketing strategies
* Designing and utilizing quantitative and qualitative research to increase audience and achieve specific goals

Bert Kleinman’s Specialties:

radio management, radio programming, branding and identity
