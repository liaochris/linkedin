Emily Grotta’s Summary

A seasoned communications professional with more than 25 years of experience in media, public relations, business communications and website development. I'm an excellent writer and strategic thinker who develops and implements cost-effective communications strategies for a wide range of clients, particularly those in the not-for-profit world and small businesses that do not have a communications specialist on staff.

A skilled and versatile writer of all types of documents, including newsletters, brochures, press releases and websites, I can adapt clients’ messages for various audiences and outlets.

A pioneer in the development of websites, I use new media—blogs, web sites, email mar-keting and social media—to achieve greater visibility for clients.

If you've recently downsized and need to outsource your writing and PR, I can help.
