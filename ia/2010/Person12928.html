Angela Byrd’s Summary

As a writer, lyricist, songwriter, copywriter, poet & member of ASCAP, my work has been published, recorded & produced for tv, radio, web & print.
I am President & CEO of Global Peace Concert, Inc. We are a non-profit based out of FL with offices in MD, PA & IN. We are comprised of every day folks who combined talents & passions to make a difference, not just globally but locally. The Mission of the Global Peace Concert is to produce, promote and support worldwide events, concerts and festivals that champion and nourish basic human rights, nonviolence, world peace, animal welfare and environmental awareness. We hold as our creed: the fostering of mutual respect & compassion for all races, religions, cultures, creatures and peoples in our global community. We believe music is the universal language and through music peace and harmony can bring unity and change. We believe that peace begins within and each person can do their part to make a world of difference and live the dream. What began as a concert to promote peace has evolved into more than a concert, it's a mission to evoke change & encourage hope that together we can inspire peace, love and unity.
I am Owner of MoonRanch Music Publishing, United Souls Music multi-ethnic Booking & Promotion Company and Founder of Artists For Earth & Peace.
I worked 14 years in broadcast media at abc27 WHTM-TV as the Programming Satellite Coordinator, Promotions Assistant & Floor Director.
I co-wrote a song/video that was produced exclusively for TV which has been submitted for EMMY nomination 2 years in a row. I also co-wrote jingles.
Prior to TV, I worked in the music industry. I was a crew member of the U2 ZooTV Outside Broadcast Tour. I also worked with artists such as Janet Jackson, Tim McGraw, Phil Collins, Luther Vandross, ZZTop, The Spin Doctors & Travis Tritt. Years of working with a local band led to my work with national & international artists.
As Ghandi said, we must be the change we want to see in the world.
