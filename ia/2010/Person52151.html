Dave Blanchard’s Experience

  • Business Designer

    IDEO

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Design industry)

    June 2008 — Present (2 years )

    IDEO's Business Design group helps clients identify and implement opportunities for innovation. Working as integral project team members, Business Factors specialists facilitate critical decision-making throughout the innovation process. This includes defining the innovation challenge, and identifying relevant assets and competencies that can be leveraged to achieve success.

    Business Design provides strategic services to help clients enhance their ability to create new ventures, identify new markets, commercialize technology, and engage best practices for innovation.

  • Co-Founder

    (Entertainment industry)

    February 2004 — May 2006 (2 years 4 months)

  • Co-Founder, Tour Manager, & VP of Marketing

    DiscLive

    (Music industry)

    May 2002 — March 2004 (1 year 11 months)

    Developed the first technology enabling live recordings to be mass produced on CD within minutes of a concert’s end. As Tour Manager, led a diverse six-person crew on company tours while managing relationships with artists, managers, agents, promoters, venue owners, and suppliers. Completed sale of company in 2004 to a publicly traded firm.
