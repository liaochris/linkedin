Ron Barenburg’s Summary

Ron is a visionary, innovator, inventor, speaker, sales executive, and an entrepreneur. Holder of three U.S. Patents, (and over 30 patent-pending applications), for novel track and trace and anti-counterfeiting uses, Ron has experience founding three start up companies, International Bar Code in 2000 (now BTI), BarCode Advisors Group (now Secure Symbology, Inc.) in 2003, and Lynxstreet, Inc. in 2008.All three known for their ground breaking solutions.

Known for thinking 'outside the box', Ron is working on innovative solutions using bar codes and digital watermarks to enhance covert Brand Protection as well as providing Consumer Interactivity using today's internet and camera ready smart phones, and is available for consulting assignments in this area.

Ron Barenburg’s Specialties:

Ron is able to spot trends, events, and moments 'before the curve', respond with innovative solutions that set standards. This has resulted in not only coming up with patentable ideas, but has included making the presentation that makes the sale.
