Manuel Ponce de Leon’s Summary

A straight decade of hands-on, technical Online AdServing experience has shaped me into the hardcore industry professional I am today. I have done it all: tech support, road-warrior consulting, cutting edge Rich Media application development, customer and peer training, product and team management. There are very few technical problems that baffle me, as chances are I've tackled them before.

I've worked for both industry behemoths, and ground breaking startups. My passion for this industry drove me to relocate and work for extended periods to some of the greatest, most exciting cities (and industry strongholds) on both sides of the Atlantic: Dublin, London, New York and San Francisco - always in search for the next exciting opportunity where my contribution could make a difference.

I am a clever and open-minded person with a generous and friendly nature. I work well in teams or as a highly motivated solo artist. I am sharp, direct and honest, with a good eye for detail and a methodical and critical approach to my work. I posses good self-starting capabilities, plus the ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously and prioritize based on best judgment. I adapt well to circumstances, do my homework, and always work as hard as the task as hand demands for efficient completion.
