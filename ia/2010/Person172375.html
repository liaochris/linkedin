Professional Summary
Key Words: Professional, Compassionate, Analytical, Driven, Learner & Conscientious

My educational background, professional and academic career indicates a relentless drive for achievement, growth and learning. I am energized by challenges and relentless in the pursuit of knowledge, understanding and accomplishment. Status quo is not an option for me.

Satisfaction from being busy and productive, combined with a high level of self-motivation, allows me to focus for extended periods of time. Completing Master level degree courses whilst working full time demonstrates my work ethic and endeavor to progress from competence to excellence, by continually acquiring new skills and knowledge. There exists a constant daily need for learning and achievement.

The need for opportunity to learn and grow is paramount. In return for this opportunity I will become a more productive and loyal individual. I seek credibility by approaching tasks in a methodical, careful and analytical fashion. As a cautious strategic thinker, I adopt a no nonsense, professional and detail orientated approach towards problem solving. With rigor and healthy skepticism, I dispassionately seek objectivity and validity when analyzing problems and solutions.

A British reserve combined with naturally good judgment ensures a conservative and effective approach towards work. A career in healthcare, offering compassion and professional care to patients, allows me to provide counsel and act as a role model. I lead people with sincerity, humility and vigilance. I place greater emphasis and interested in the character and personality of an individual than their status or financial worth.

For me, the goal of economic profit is insufficient; I seek challenges that impart a greater meaning, intrinsic worth and the prospect of professional growth and development. In return I offer due diligence in a professional, methodical and industrious manner.
