Zac Hester’s Summary

I am a relatively recent graduate of South Dakota School of Mines and Technology. My chosen fields of study include embedded systems, distributed computing, client-server architectures, remote sensing and control, robotics, GPS integration, real-time operating systems, and software engineering.

As a means to living close to family, I have taken a non-engineering job in the field of web development. While nothing in my formal education was directed at web technology, I found the application of the formal software engineering process to be fun and interesting. Web development appeals to my desire to develop embedded systems as the constraints on operational time and resources are limited in similar respects to that of an embedded environment.

Currently, the company I work for is making long-term decisions about the direction of their online efforts which may or may not be compatible with my personal interests. Therefore, I am just starting to look at the possibility of a regular, entry-level engineering position in just about any field of digital electronics and/or software engineering.

About my only desire for relocation, at this point, is that I find a clean, safe environment to raise my family, where there is at least one or two mountains within easy driving distance. As long as those requirements are met, I would be happy living anywhere on the planet.
