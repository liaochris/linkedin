Catherine Mattice, M.A’s Summary

Catherine specializes in providing universal organizational solutions for bullying in the workplace, conducting strategic communication audits, implementing effective communication processes, and ultimately designing and realizing positive corporate cultures.

Catherine has published several works, including a recent article featured on the cover of HR TImes Magazine, and has presented her research on workplace bullying both nationally and internationally at a variety of conferences. She has also appeared several times on news stations such as FOX, NBC, and ABC as an expert.

Aside from her current position on the Board of Directors of the American Society of Training & Development (ASTD), San Diego Chapter, she is serving with the International Association of Workplace Bullying and is on California College’s Program Advisory Committee for the Business School.

Catherine's experience in human resources, business management, and training has reaped over $2Mil in charitable donations, improved productivity by 30%, and resulted in a 50% increase in total monthly revenue.
