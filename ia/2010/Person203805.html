Tim Emmerling’s Summary

After a four-year sales stint at State Farm Insurance, I transferred from the College of Dupage to Eastern Illinois University where I studied and obtained my B.A. in Journalism. Since then, I've continued my writing, doing various free-lance work and took a full-time role with H.T. Associates, a 27-year-old executive search firm located in Arlington Heights. As a recruiter, I provided career assistance to professionals and represented some of the biggest players in the Chicago area.

After my time with H.T., I moved into a recruiting position at Casey Accounting & Finance Resources where I was engaging with candidates in the Accounting and Finance arenas. At Casey, I communicated with my clients regularly to better understand their needs and deliver the quality candidates they were seeking.

Currently, as a Recruiter with Allstate Insurance, I communicate and network with professionals in the Insurance and Financial Services industries regarding opportunities with Allstate in the Capital, South East and Florida regions.

If you're interested in hearing more about our financial advisor opportunity or networking, feel free to drop me a line at TEmme@Allstate.Com or call me direct at 847-402-8247.
