Mel Lockcuff’s Summary

My experience as wife to my Hubs of 10 years and stay at home, homeschool mama to my two boys has taught me more about life than any outside job. This job takes commitment that is beyond explanation, and there are no substitutes to take my place nor sick days. My work with MamaBuzz has given me the opportunity to make connections with fellow bloggers and companies; the connections are probably the most exciting part of this work, especially when you get to meet face to face with people you've only known on an online basis.

I love reading, writing, photography, crafting, baking, home decor, outdoor activities, etc, and I've recently fallen in love with Blackberry Grape Sobe Lifewater. As a family, we love to travel, especially when outdoor adventures with hiking are involved.

My ultimate goal & hope is to raise my boys to become Godly men; other goals would be to become a published author; become an advocate for those who have been trapped in the sex industry/human trafficking; and to provide social media services to companies/brands that I work with.
