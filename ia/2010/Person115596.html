Kevin Monahan’s Summary

•A professional in the broadcast media field, with 20 years of pro experience, chiefly as a video editor.

•Considered an expert in the core applications in Apple Final Cut Studio.

•Internal Editor for Apple Computer for the release of Final Cut Studio in 2009. Inside contractor specializing in testing Final Cut Pro 7 and Motion 4.

•Technical writer for Apple Computer for the release of Final Cut Studio in 2005. Wrote manuals and workflows for Final Cut Pro, Final Cut Express, Cinema Tools and LiveType.

•Provide editing services, motion graphics, color correction, post-production consulting and training.

•Considered as one of the top Final Cut Pro trainers worldwide.

•Video effects artist, animator and compositor for such companies as Pixar and Electronic Arts, including "Finding Nemo" and "Wall•E").

•Author of "Motion Graphics and Effects in Final Cut Pro".

•Writer: "Edit Well: Final Cut Studio Techniques from the Pros"

•Writer for PeachPit Press' highly regarded "Edit Well" magazine.

•President and Founder of the World's First Final Cut Pro Users Group, SF Cutters

•A longtime San Francisco Bay Area resident.

•Looking for work in the post-production community in the Bay Area or Los Angeles. Editing with Final Cut Pro and creating motion graphics with Motion and After Effects are specialties.
