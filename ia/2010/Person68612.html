Fabian Gonzalez’s Summary

Consultant in the area of information privacy and security; and the full information governance lifecycle. Extensive experience in performing privacy and security assessments (including attestations) for Fortune 500 clients in the software (including cloud computing), healthcare, and financial services industries.

Experience with California, U.S. and International data protection and privacy laws, including the Safe Harbor, the EU data Protection directive (EU/95/46), HIPAA privacy and security rules, the HITECH Act, GLBA, CAN-SPAM, and breach notification laws for California and other states.

Most recently, I have assisted my clients by acting as interim Director of Privacy for a large healthcare insurer, performing large scale privacy assessments for HIPAA (including HITECH) and GAPP, deploying full-scale privacy programs, performing SAS 70 audits, WebTrust attestations, ISO 17799 assessments, and general IT reviews.

Representative clients include: Visa, Ariba, McKesson Corporation, Blue Shield of California, Gap Inc., Wind River Systems, Yahoo!, Google, and Varian Medical Systems.
