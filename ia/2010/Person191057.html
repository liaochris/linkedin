Stephanie M. Harwood, APR’s Summary

A strong resource with a unique mix of corporate, agency and non-profit credentials. Accredited (APR), with more than 25 years' experience in external/internal communications, in industry segments ranging from professional services and health care to social issues and industrial. Able to help companies, enterprises, agencies and corporate departments with hands-on planning, problem solving, communications tools and professional development. Direct experience in counseling senior management and in working with staff at all levels, and a trusted asset in agency client teams and in managing pieces of business. Contact: smharwood@juno.com.

Stephanie M. Harwood, APR’s Specialties:

Public relations/communications planning and implementation, situation and operations analysis, interpersonal and group communications, marketing communications, media relations, cause-marketing, crisis communications, writing and editing.
