J "J.J." Sanchez’s Summary

HEADQUARTERED AT UNIVERSAL STUDIOS:
The Fireball Run (R) Adventurally Series & The Race to Recover America’s Missing Children (R).

The Fireball Run is a brand interactive live event shaped into a road rally. The 3500 mile, 75 team, 18 city, 9 day road adventure.

Rally participants are: Business Owner, C-Class Executive, Person of Independence (Net Worth 3.5 Mil+), Industry Celebrity.

MEDIA POWERHOUSE: Fireball Run earns nearly 12 hours broadcast "TV news" and well over 25 million in exposure annually.

MAKE A DIFFERENCE Cause Related Alignment. Fireball Runs "Race to Recover Americas Missing Children" has aided in the recovery of 32 missing kids so far- NOW THAT'S POWER.

GRASS ROOTS JUGGERNAUT: Talk about VIRAL, we drive across America in everything from Fords to Ferrari's... people are phone picturing, blogging, Facebooking, twittering, and YouTubing every where we venture- we literally drive your brand into a viral media storm. Thousands of fans come out to support America's Adventurally.

BEST IN CLASS: Fireball Run "looks like" an auto rally on the outside- on the inside its a powerful engine of rolling leadership. Imagine nearly 150 key decision leaders using your product or service or visiting your city... what would you do with 150 new (influential) friends?

The ultimate EXPERIENTIAL MARKETING event. We don't slap your brand on top of our event- we build you into it... think of us as "Amazing Race meets motoring". Teams are assigned missions they must successfully accomplish to gain points & correctly navigate the country.

SAFE & SANCTIONED: This is NOT an open road race. Its a carefully scripted interactive live game. Our Safety Director is (Ret) Albuquerque Police chief Sam Baca, America's top Chief. Fireball Run is sanctioned by United States Auto Club Racing, one of the major sanctioning bodies.

CHEAP TO SPONSOR: Yep- no kidding. Our unique operation keeps overhead low along with sponsorship costs.
