Ron Armstrong’s Summary

With Keller Williams Professionals, one of the most progressive real estate firms in the country, Ron represents first-time homeowners, as well as experienced buyers, sellers, and investors. Using in-depth understanding of current opportunities and market trends, and extensive knowledge of the greater Asheville area, to fulfill their real estate needs with the highest degree of professionalism. e-Agent and e-PRO® training/certification allows for greater service to the client, and insures optimal usage of the internet and other electronic technology in regard to staying abreast of cutting-edge real estate marketing strategies, research and development, and communication. Extensive background and certification in environmental issues, land conservancy, and energy efficiency, insures expert guidance to those consumers who are ecologically-minded, and supports them in making informed decisions about any possible real estate transactions. A love of the land (which includes organic gardening), a natural extroversion/ “people personality,” extensive volunteer experience, and a BA in Ethics, help bring an enlightened, humanitarian aspect to all real estate dealings.
