Connecting through diverse networks creates sustainability


Building a network is an art and a science
• The effectiveness of your network is function of your contribution to the network not the number of connections you have in specific social networks.
• Diversity is essential to the healthy growth of any network – actively seek to contribute in multiple industries and levels not just the industry or peer group you operate in.
• Exposure to non-linear networks will add value to your own network.
• Depth of connections versus number of connections –It’s is a paradox. “The Strength of Weak Ties” highlights the value of a diverse network but as professional business people we only tend to recommend people we know well and connect with often.
• Social networking technologies have the ability to scale a message very quickly. This is a dual edged sword as a hard won reputation online can be destroyed online equally as quickly.
• Connect the real world and the online world – a phone call is more effective than email and face to face beats telephone.
• The value of your network = ((N x S)xD )xCx10 where N=Number of connections, S=Strength of each connection, D=diversity of network and C=contribution to network. This formula is a bit of fun and unproven but a mental model I keep in my head to make sure I help people 10 times more than I expect them to help me. That’s the ultimate paradox it’s about giving more than you get.
