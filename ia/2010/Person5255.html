Alan Schulman’s Summary

Chief Creative Officer with integrated experience across digital marketing, advertising and global branding.

Currently develops custom content solutions for global brand marketers, digital publishers, media agencies and emerging technology platforms. Serves on the faculty of Upstream's Habitat Group – providing eLearning and creative best practices curriculum to both Media Sellars and Brand Marketers. Winner of numerous awards across the branding, advertising, and digital spectrum including One Show, Clio, AICP, AIGA, FWA, ADDY, OMMA, and ProMax/BDA. Recent recipient of the Gold MIXX Award for Best Broadband Video by the Interactive Advertising Bureau as well as Global Branding and Marketing Awards from both the Johnson & Johnson and Procter & Gamble marketing organizations.

Currently serves on the Agency Advisory Boards of TiVO, OVAB, and Tremor Media. Previously served on the AAAA Advanced Television Committee, the National Television Academy’s New Media Committee as well as Interpublic’s Convergence and Branded Entertainment Advisory Committee.

Speaker on creative applications for digital media for numerous Marketers and Organizations including the ANA, AAAA's, IRTS, NATPE, MIPCOM/MILIA, CES and NATAS. Guest speaker for such brand marketers as Johnson & Johnson, Coca-Cola, SONY, Verizon, Microsoft, and others.
