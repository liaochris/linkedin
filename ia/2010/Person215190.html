Wibo Koole’s Summary

Create2connect enables companies, governments and non-governmental organizations to realize sustainable transformations.
We listen attentively, ask questions and search for underlying patterns. We enquire to generate new opportunities.
Together with our clients we create solutions, because that provides a firm commitment to change and the prospect of transformational leadership to embody itself. We enable our clients to create a vision of what can be realized by reaching out into the future that emerges.
The next step is to make that image of the future very tangible. And with those tangible results in mind we support our clients to take full responsibility for executing plans, and get into action.

Wibo is a entrepreneurial managent consultant and executive consultant with a strong sense and open mind for what is happening in markets and the social and political arena’s. He has proven to be able to lead a team to realize a vision
Wibo has a strong experience in managing complex projects. He led several nationwide campaigns and organizational change projects with succes. He is able to deliver on time and with high quality in a fast changing environment. He is a sound thinker, with a broad network in science, politics, business and society.
He has a broad experience as a non executive president of several organizations and is able to keep on track and really cooperate in a board of directors. He is an experienced negotiator with business and government. He has many years worked in an international environment.
He is political scientist by education and completed the Advanced Management Program at the Wharton Business School (University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, USA), he is a transformationeel coach, certified trainer in project management and vision creation (KernKonsult) and Insights practitioner. He was trained by Otto Scharmer en Peter Senge (Presencing Institute, Boston, USA) as facilitator for Presencing/Theory U, and is a mindfulnesstrainer.
