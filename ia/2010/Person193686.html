Stefan Silverman’s Summary

Franchise broker and business coach with 25+ years' business ownership experience with 7 startups employing over 500 people. Experienced executive leader with C-level achievement in marketing services, advertising, construction, commercial property, equipment leasing and business development roles.

A key focus of my franchise business coaching practice is in making an expert assessment of clients’ strengths, skills, interests and financial resources and matching them to leading franchise brands that offer the greatest potential for career satisfaction and financial success. My goal is to guide clients effectively through the complex process of identification, validation, acquisition, financing and startup so that he/she can look back in five years and see that all the right choices were made.

ACHIEVEMENTS, EXPERIENCE AND EXPERTISE
• 3-yr. elected member of the Mr. Handyman® Franchise Advisory Council representing 350 franchise owners.
• Built productive relationships with Minnesota’s congressional delegation and provided legislative guidance on franchising issues as part of the International Franchise Association’s Government Relations activities. Member of the Franchise Business Network in Minnesota.
• In constant contact with industry leaders to stay up to date on brand performance and identify which ones to stay away from. Frequent webinars, conference calls and industry research provide inside information and insights valuable to my clients.
• Provide client education on enhanced franchise ownership types and investment options such as multi-unit buys, Area Developer and Master Franchisee opportunities.
• An exceptional referral resource for the expert industry, financial, regulatory and legal advisors necessary to complete a franchise purchase.

Stefan Silverman’s Specialties:
