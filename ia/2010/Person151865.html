Nat Bourre’s Summary

Interested in social media in healthcare (Health 2.0)? Listen to a 5-minute audio sample of Natalie's presentation ; http://ow.ly/eByg . Contact Natalie if you would like her to provide a presentation or workshop for your organization.

Natalie Bourre, founder of Marketing 4 Health Inc., is a Medical Marketing and Social Media Consultant with over 13 years of experience in pharma sales and marketing. She has experience with brands at all lifecycle stages and has also led the marketing team for an Rx brand that became 5th largest brand in Canada. She has successfully acted as co-promotion alliance manager for several partnerships.

Natalie is passionate about Social Media. She is currently managing the Social Media strategy and implementation for her clients in healthcare, consumer and non-profit industries.

Nat Bourre’s Specialties:

Social media (with consumer and health care focus), Strategic brand planning, Marketing medical brands at all life-cycle stages (product launch to generic entry), Managing co-promotions, Coaching Product Managers
