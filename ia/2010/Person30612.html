Kinetic Die Casting Company (KDC)
was established in 1994 at the current location in North Hollywood, California, United States of America. Kinetic Die Casting is an American Jobshop Aluminum Die Casting Company. KDC is a corporate member of North American Die Casting Association NADCA. KDC is a jobshop aluminum die casting company produces aluminum parts for OEM's (Original Equipment Manufacturer) for their products. A Die Casting Company uses a process in which molten aluminum is injected with a die casting machine under force using considerable pressure into a steel die cast mold or die cast die to produce aluminum die cast parts. Manufacturer of Aluminum Die Casting Parts. Founded company in 1994 and increased company sales through internet marketing and SEO.
