Melissa White’s Summary

Technical Innovation is in a very unique position in providing AV solutions. Unlike many other “AV” or “Networking” companies, who specialize in one discipline, and offer other services outside of their comfort zone through the use of subcontractors, Technical Innovation actually has a proven track record and expertise in audio visual and video conferencing systems solutions. We do not just claim to wear many hats and offer to be a one stop shop. We take pride in hiring the best engineers and technicians in both the AV and the Video-Conferencing industries.

Our staff boasts ANSI recognized certifications, credentials, and experience that no other integrator in the southeast United States can match. We are your one stop shop and we are able to deliver on that promise.

Here are some attributes that set us not only apart, but head and shoulders above our competition:

• We are a 70 year old, financially stable organization.
• Technical Innovation is the only AV integrator in Tennessee to have dedicated customer support team in every office. We provide the highest quality support for our client’s audio video and video conferencing needs. We maintain a fully staffed Help Desk to ensure that the assistance you need is just a phone call away. Our dedicated service staff ensures that we do not have to pull another technician off another assignment to make sure that your problems are taken care of in a quick and efficient manner.
• We are an InfoComm Diamond Level certified company. This certification recognizes that 75% of our Sales and Technical staff have earned their ANSI recognized CTS certification, and that as a company we have agreed to comply with Ten Standards of Excellence developed in collaboration with industry experts to emphasize the best practices of audiovisual businesses.
