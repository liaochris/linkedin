C.P. Shankar’s Summary

Demonstated success in energy efficiency, climate change, wireless mesh networks, managed services, wireless (TDMA, GSM, Wifi, Wimax, satellite) and broadband networks.

Solid reputation among colleagues, customers and investors alike as a result oriented executive with a unique balance of strategic insight and hands on operational expertise.

Offers a creative & strategic mind and an intimate knowledge of computer and telecom technology, their role in helping businesses compete more effectively, as well as a compelling vision for the future of such technologies and how they can transform organizations and society at large.

Increasingly responsible senior executive positions in sales, global marketing, product planning, customer care, network operations at Orblynx (content distribution), E::CARGO (high frequency transaction processing), Orion (satellite services), Hughes (cell phones, DirecPC & OnStar), Fujitsu (cell phones, digital media multiplexers, private networks), Doelz (remote access devices), GTE (pbx), Nortel (pbx & mini computers) and Burroughs/Unisys (production planning & mrp systems).

Helped top-tier private equity funds to successfully turn around/restructure investments in satellite based content distribution, CLEC, and international IP transit services.

Served on the Governor’s task forces on aerospace and high technology; and on the boards of several local technology companies. Currently serving on the Board of Visitors at University of Maryland, College of Computer, Mathematics and Physical Sciences.

Received several industry awards, presented at industry seminars and analyst conferences - Testified at the U.S. International Trade Commission on wireless industry matters and represented the U.S. in international trade missions.

University of Madras, India (B.Com), University of Detroit (MBA), and post graduate credits at University of Michigan and Wharton business Schools.

C.P. Shankar’s Specialties:

Building high performance teams, value added services, strategic alliances, sales, marketing, finance, engineering & operations - uncnanny ability to comprehend complex problems and provide, simple, effective solutions.
