Marty Thomas’s Summary

NOOK Web had its humble beginnings out of a bay window (literally) in Wrigleyville, Chicago. The bay window become quickly known to my roommates as "MARTY'S NOOK." After long days of working my white collar job in the loop, I would return to my nook and dream about the future. This is when NOOK Web was born.

Originally a web design company, NOOK Web has transformed over the past year to focus it's attention on a marketing software named Purlem.

Although based out of Chicago, I have recently enjoyed spending time traveling the country with my wife as she picks up positions every few months as a traveling nurse.
