James Kenigsberg’s Summary

My years of technology focused experience blended together with the business acumen I’ve attained from the corporate world has provided me with a depth of knowledge that is paramount in driving a business forward.

I have come into organizations and put my arms around them to instill processes to exceed existing service levels. This has been achieved by taking the role model and mentoring approach to ensure team members understand the importance of technology in business.

My background covers over 10 years of management, technical, customer services and sales in the wide variety of businesses. I have lead successful implementations and management initiatives for customer and corporate business objectives and technology solutions in the Financial, Marketing, Advertising, and Educational services.

I have a proven and consistent track record to create, deliver and manage successful and cost effective services by listening to business objectives. I have aptitude for understanding relationships between business needs and the technology that fulfills those needs. My experience come from the successful communication with stakeholders, establishing processes and developing team members, seeking out the critical paths and developing contingencies, creating a sense of urgency and motivation, and a persistent follow up to ensure projects and team members meet their business, time to market and financial objectives.
