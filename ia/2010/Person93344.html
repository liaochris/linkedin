Jeff Kimmel’s Summary

Jeff led the sales initiatives for both traditional and digital platforms at major publications including Playboy and Men’s fitness. As the Vice President of Sales at Playboy and Playboy.com, he built innovative cross platform solutions for major advertiser brands. Jeff brought his strategic understanding of the importance of creating 360 programs to Men’s Fitness where he was responsible for 3 consecutive years of revenue growth. His team broke the first-ever digital deals for advertisers including Chrysler, Coke and EAS.

Jeff’s more than 20 years of ad sales experience and almost a decade in digital sales alone, provide him with the skill sets needed to grow revenue across multiple platforms, hire and manage successful teams, and build profitable new business strategies for digital and multi-platform brands across the media landscape.
