Rhonda Hurwitz’s Summary

I am a veteran marketer and business development consultant. My company, HMR Marketing Solutions, uses inbound marketing tools and techniques to help businesses market smarter, by increasing their effectiveness online.

We provide small and mid-sized businesses an outsourced solution for a range of online marketing needs, from strategy development to project management. We start by providing big picture perspective for business owners, helping to identify smart strategies to meet growth objectives. We are also available to implement marketing plans as needed.

This includes guiding clients on website development and redesign, content marketing, search engine marketing, and social media strategy; we use Analytics tools to optimize campaigns, and provide hands-on training as needed (Twitter, Facebook, Linked in, You Tube, Blogs, and other social networking platforms), to ensure successful implementation; we also draw on a more traditional marketing toolkit to strengthen branding, positioning, communications, and content development as needed.

I hold a certificate in Social Media Marketing from Purchase College, inbound marketing certification, and an MBA from Duke University. I was formerly a brand marketing executive for several Fortune 100 companies, including Lipton, Kraft, Playtex, and Diageo.

I volunteer my time as a judge in the Annual Social Impact Exchange business plan competition for Non-Profits.

An experienced presenter, I am available to speak to community groups and conferences on a variety of topics, including social media as a marketing tool for business.
