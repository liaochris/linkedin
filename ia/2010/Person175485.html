Rob Hale’s Summary

I began my career in recruiting back in 2003 in the insurance industry and have progressed over the years within the staffing services world with a proven track record of success. I have worked for Global, national, and now local staffing firms and have acquired many strengths and knowledge through my diverse and varied background recruiting for Fortune 500+ clients to small and mid-sized companies.
