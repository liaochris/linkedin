Rich Cohen CLTC’s Summary

RDC Financial Group - Overview

Founded originally in 2001 as LTC New England, and now called RDC Financial Group (RDCFG), is an asset preservation, long term care funding, and retirement planning firm that provides a broad range of financial, business, estate, and retirement planning services, financial education programs, and risk and investment management products to individual and business clients throughout New England.

RDC Financial Group is an Independent Insurance and Investment broker. Direct affiliations with top rated firms such as Jackson National, John Hancock, Prudential, AVIVA, Genworth, United of Omaha, Allianz and other leading insurance and financial products and services enable customized financial solutions to meet suitability needs of clients.

Richard D. Cohen is a Registered Representative of and offering securities through Foresters Equity Services, Inc. a registered Broker-Dealer. Member FINRA, SIPC. 6640 Lusk Blvd, Suite A202, San Diego, CA 92121 858-550-4844
RDC Financial Group offers insurance and financial services from top rated companies and is independent of Foresters Equity Services, Inc.

Rich Cohen CLTC’s Experience

  • President

    RDC Financial Group

    (Financial Services industry)

    February 2010 — Present (11 months)

    Rich Cohen has 25 years experience as a personal producer and field manager. During that time Rich was also a Division Vice President for two major LTC Insurance carriers and was responsible for recruiting and training hundreds of agents and managers in long term care insurance planning strategies.

    Prior to establishing his career in financial services, Rich spent 15 years in executive level corporate sales and sales management. After personally experiencing the impact that a long term care illness can have on a loved one and their friends and family. Rich secured his insurance and securities licenses in 1985. His passion and purpose since 1991 has been to develop financial programs and strategies designed to address the unique financial needs and circumstances of our country’s growing mature population and their adult children.

    Through education and experience, Rich has garnered a broad base of knowledge which enables him to provide strategies and direction to individuals looking to help:
    • Pass their estate to their heirs in the least costly most efficient manner
    • Protect and preserve their hard-earned assets
    • Maintain their independence and dignity throughout retirement
    • Structure retirement plan assets to provide a guaranteed income for life
    • Reduce current income taxes

    Rich frequently gives seminars and workshops on long term care funding strategies, retirement and estate conservation. Rich provides answers to common concerns such as:
    • Are my assets and income structured in such a fashion to last as long as I do?
    • How can I protect my income from the volatility of the market and from a chronic illness?
    • What resources are available to pay for services that informal family caregivers usually provide?
    • What is long term care insurance and should I consider it?
