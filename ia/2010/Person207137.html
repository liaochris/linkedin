Kathy Price’s Summary

I am an independent consultant in the areas of education, business and leadership training and development. I work on as needed basis for many companies, schools, and other organizations. Some of my clients include the Arkansas City Wide Leadership classes, Alltel Corporation in Little Rock, and Wal-Mart.

My background education is in education, psychology, and leadership. I have been a teacher, professor, presenter, and trainer for 16 years now. I also write for publications and present at regional and national conferences.

I would love to design a presentation or retreat that is just right for you and your employees! When giving presentations I try my hardest to combine my expertise with practical strategies and enthusiasm. My goal is to give you a presentation experience that is enjoyable and packed with useful ideas you can immediately begin to implement. Please contact me for more information and let me know how I can be of service to you!

Kathy Price’s Specialties:

My presentation topics cover subjects such as -- Personality Differences, Emotional Intelligence, Bringing out the Better Side of Difficult People, Conflict Resolution, How to Speak Up Without Putting Others Down, Constructive Feedback, Teambuilding, Decision Making, Diversity, Effective Communication, Time Management and Organization, Meetings that Work, The Challenge of Change, Stress Management, Negotiating to Win/Win, The Art of Delegation, AND many more!
