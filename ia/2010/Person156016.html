Noel Engelman’s Summary

Noel has been working in Power Distribution and Electronic Integration/Support/Maintenance for over 20 years. Experience includes work on the NASA Space Shuttle/Spacelab Program, USAF Joint STARS (E8C) platform, GTC ECS (SCADA, Revenue Metering, and Communications), and GE. Interestingly, the jobs have been similar in nature (power and data) and knowledge gained from one position has transfered to each subsequent one. Customer focus and common sense approaches are a specialty, as well as an ability to look at things at many levels and see why they work or what needs to be done to make them work.

Current Goal: Provide the EMCs of Georgia the Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) expertise and support needed to keep the lights on and burning bright across the state. Provide support to the customers of GE across the South on all automation equipment and developing the "Smart Grid".

Noel Engelman’s Specialties:

Multi-vendor integration between field equipment (RTU, IED, PLC, A/D Converter, etc.) and master devices (master station, RTU, data concentrator, controller, etc.). Protocols to include DNP3.0, TEJAS V, TEJAS III, SES91, Modbus, QMS, Prologic, Cooper, CDC, and Landis & Gyr. Networking and communications in Frame Relay, Analog Leaseline, TCP/IP, Radio, Satellite, Microwave, Cellular, Fiber Optics, RS232/485, and other military or proprietary media.
