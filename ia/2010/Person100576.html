Joe Forgét’s Summary

Joe Forgét is president of Technetium Advertising & Web Design, a full-service marketing and creative design firm. An innovator in marketing and advertising for more than 15 years, he is responsible for overseeing operations, business development and the strategic planning of all aspects of the agency. He has established solid business relationships with local, national and international companies during the process of helping them achieve their marketing and branding objectives.

Prior to founding Technetium, Forgét was responsible for many roles in business administration and marketing for Recoton – an $800 million manufacturer and distributor of consumer products. His marketing programs, creative graphics packages and ability to build successful relationships within the highly competitive consumer products industry often exceeded corporate expectations and revenue goals.

Forgét is an Eagle Scout and serves on the Executive Board of the Central Florida Council, Boy Scouts of America as VP of Marketing. Active in the community, he also serves as a board member of the American Advertising Federation – Orlando, is on the Board of Directors of the Central Florida Economic Cooperative and is a member of the Executive Committee of Orlando Business Journal's Executive Advisory Board Exchange. Forgét is a proud graduate of Leadership Orlando Class 67 and was recognized as a member of the Orlando Business Journal’s 40 Under Forty “Class of 2008.”

A native of Albany, N.Y., Forgét graduated from Syracuse University. Forgét, his wife, and their two daughters have made Orlando home.
