  • Intern on the iPod team

    Apple Inc.

    (Public Company; AAPL; Computer Hardware industry)

    June 2006 — September 2006 (4 months)

    Worked on all iPod products shipped Sept. 2006, but was primarily focused on the 2G Nano (http://bit.ly/2GNano). My team was responsible for accessory compatibility for the new release. There was a major architecture redesign in that product version and I worked with the development teams to solve a number of problematic edge cases the new design introduced. As a side summer project I built an automated testing suite that allowed testers to just 'hit go' and it would spit out an Excel result, rather than require manual log entry. I presented this to Tony Fadell (iPod VP) at the end of the summer.

    Most interesting part of the job: seeing what goes into pushing an Apple product out the door in the last 3 months and the chaos that ensues!
