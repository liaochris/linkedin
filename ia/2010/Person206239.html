Tom McKay’s Summary

I help small- and medium-sized service providers differentiate themselves from competitors, attract new customers and nurture relationships with existing ones.

20+ years in journalism and broadcasting (CBS, etc.) taught me to uncover and present your company's unique story in a way that resonates, establishes relationships, and turns prospects into loyal customers.

In addition to traditional print and online marketing materials, I develop information products that clearly demonstrate your expertise. Instead of chasing prospects, you attract them. Instead of being "just another vendor," you're positioned as a respected expert and trusted advisor.

Follow me on Twitter: @tom_mckay

Tom McKay’s Specialties:

-- Online marketing: website content, landing pages, PPC ads, e-newsletters, blogs

-- No-hype Direct Response copywriting: B2B and B2C sales letters and landing pages that don't suck or embarrass you.

-- Info-marketing: e-books, special reports, case studies, presentations, courses, annual reports, etc.

-- PR and traditional marketing collateral: media kit, news releases, brochures, ads, product spec sheets, etc.
