Susie deVille Schiffli’s Summary

President of InnovationCompass, an executive & entrepreneur coaching and consulting firm. Areas of expertise include fostering creativity and innovation, niche marketing, sales training, leadership development, and strategic planning. An expert in entrepreneurship and anthropology, Susie applies her acumen in human behavior toward helping executives and entrepreneurs achieve break-through results and growth. She is available for speaking engagements, seminars, one-on-one coaching, and customized, group training.

Susie received a Master of Entrepreneurship degree from Western Carolina University. She earned with Honors a Bachelor of Arts degree in Anthropology from UNC-Chapel Hill. She is a graduate of the William C. Friday Fellowship for Human Relations, the Duke Certificate Program in Nonprofit Management, and the Western North Carolina Rural Economic Development Institute.

Instructional Design & Training Specialist for a nonprofit entrepreneurship education organization. Develops educational and training programs and leads instructional design for all courses.

Executive Director of nonprofit entrepreneurship education organization. Responsible for developing educational programs. Assisted in coordinating marketing, fundraising, and grant writing.

Director of Marketing & Sales for-profit entrepreneurship education company. Co-wrote and produced an online, multimedia series on The Entrepreneurial Mindset for Cisco System’s Entrepreneur Institute.

Consistent top producer with 2005 individual real estate sales volume exceeding $13 million. Recruited and trained new agents for office.

Founding executive director of nonprofit community service center. Assumed fiscal responsibility for the center, particularly in the areas of grant seeking and fundraising.

Founding executive director of nonprofit literacy organization.

Extensive book publishing experience in U.S. and U.K. firms including acquisitions, editorial, production, and marketing.
