Minnie Titus-Glover’s Summary

K & A Solutions works with businesses or business leaders who are looking to improve their organization or business performance and achieve outstanding results. We will work with you, as you take the journey towards achieving High Performance, with your vision, persistence and hard work.

The purpose of our programs, which are tailored to your needs, will teach you or your employees to get your organization or business to:

Take control of your future
Achieve Outstanding and sustainable results
Gain total commitment from your employees
Exceed your customers' expectations

We assess the needs and provide the necessary training or developmental opportunities to clarify your strategy, streamline processes and create a culture in which each person is a contributing partner in the business or organization.

We have the Compass Series and Leadership program, which are designed for organizations that recognize the enormous capability of their employees and are willing to invest in their development. The combination of subject matter in the series were carefully selected to represent the most important topics in employee development. The Compass Series (a 12 month program) and the Leadership Program (8 or 10 month program) have classes held once a month with follow up action items and coaching.

As participants attend these programs, they will develop
knowledge and skills that will significantly increase their personal effectiveness and ability to successfully lead others. They will develop habits of success that will make them a more capable person as well as a valuable employee.

Call us or contact us by email for more information on our programs and we do provide a complimentary needs assessment for your business.
