Michelle Foster Entrepreneur Extraordinaire’s Summary

I am the creator and CEO of Macon Beads™, LLC. My mission statement is to create original handcrafted jewelry for fashion conscious consumers, with the highest quality components. My success is driven first and foremost by having the most outstanding customer service in the field.

I manage the small but ever growing business, have created an extremely effective marketing and promotions plan, I design and create beautiful and unique artisan jewelry, I host the fun and productive jewelry shows, make all the buying decisions and am the distribution department. I am Macon Beads™, Wearable Southern Charm™, and I am a success!

My short term goal is to have my own line of products in boutiques around the SE.

My long term goal is to have major companies buy my products.

Then I'd like to help other women start companies. I haven't thought through that far yet in exactly what capacity that will happen, I just know I'd love to give other women the fulfillment I have received from owning my own business and being able to make all my own decisions.
