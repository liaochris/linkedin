Eitan Cohen’s Summary

At Media Stream Direct, we believe that superior results, exceptional customer service and cost-effective pricing can (and should) go hand in hand. We strive to bring high levels of profitability to our clients, while simultaneously offering valuable media time slots and one of the highest levels of personal service in the industry. We understand what needs to be done, and we are honest and relentless in doing it. We understand the essence behind achieving consistent levels of ROI. We also understand the importance behind the reliability and breadth of direct response media experience that our clients and partners have come to count on.

With over fiftty years of combined industry experience working with some of the largest marketers, retailers and brands in the country, our expertise lies not only in surpassing ROI and profitability goals time and time again, but also in building and maintaining relationships that outlast any single campaign.

Eitan Cohen’s Specialties:

We specialize in long-form media buying on over 100 national cable television stations. Our experience and most importantly, our relationships with each station, give us an edge on the competition. This means, lower rates for our clients, access to a large inventory of high-profile media, and the ability to book last-minute time slots and to make quick changes based upon real-time results.

Eitan Cohen’s Experience

  • CEO

    Media Stream Direct

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 2007 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Eitan Cohen is an 11-year veteran of the direct response advertising industry. Eitan has managed one of the longest lists of top ranked IMS shows in the Direct Response industry since 1998. Cohen has earned a reputation for being one of the most well rounded media experts. His focus on overall clients’ success and campaign developments has allowed his clients to grow their shows and discover hidden revenue streams. Prior to founding Media Stream Direct in 2007, Eitan spent 7 years at Mercury Media, one of the largest Direct Response Television media agencies in the country, where he was Vice President of Client Services. Under his management, the company greatly expanded its media presence and client base. Preceding his time at Mercury Media, Eitan worked at direct response advertising agency, Williams Television Time.

    Cohen has personally guided many of the industry’s most prestigious and successful infomercial and spot campaigns such as Revo Hair Styler, Natural Cures, Body By Jake Bun and Thigh Rocker, Power 90 by Beach Body, Hewlett Packard (HP), EyeQ, Griddler by Cuisinart, Shark Steam Blaster by Euro Pro, Walk Fit by Sylmark, Paint Buddy by Rubbermaid (short form), Roto Sweep by Sanyo Fisher Company (short form), Western Dental (short form), Providian Visa (short form), Pioneer Entertainment and Hoover Company.

    Mr. Cohen has also held the title of Co-Chairman of a Broadcast Council Committee for the Direct Marketing Association (DMA). Eitan earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Marketing and Corporate Decision Making from Boston University’s School of Management.
