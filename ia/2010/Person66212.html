Eric Brown, PMP, IT Project/Test Manager’s Summary

As an IT Project and Test Manager, my expertise lies in making web based application user experiences smoother, faster, more pleasant and more profitable. My teams have successfully simulated production level (and higher) workloads on hundreds of web based applications to determine maximum load capability, hardware needs, software efficiency and transaction level response times. The ultimate goal of this excercise is quite simply to make sure customers stay engaged and complete their purchases and other online activities.
