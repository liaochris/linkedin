Kirsten Sibilia’s Summary

A vocal advocate for the value of design, the power of the built environment, and the importance of sustainability, Kirsten Sibilia has been in the architectural industry for 15 years. Kirsten is Dattner Architects' Chief Marketing Officer.

Previously, Kirsten was Marketing Principal at FXFOWLE and later CMO at JCJ Architecture.

Kirsten is on the AIA New York Chapter board as the Director of Communications. She is the former co-chair of AIA NY Chapter’s Marketing Committee, chaired the Chapter’s PR Task Force, and a founding member of the PR Advisory Committee. She authored the chapter on Public Relations for the 2008 issue of The Architect’s Handbook of Professional Practice, published by the American Institute of Architects. Kirsten was on the board of Society of Marketing Professional Services New York Area Chapter from 2005 to 2007 and still remains active in the organization.
Kirsten is an associate member of the AIA and a member of the US Green Building Council. She is a LEED accredited professional.
