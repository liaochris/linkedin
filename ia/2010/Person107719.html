Jude Gilford’s Summary

Millions of employees face financial obstacles they do not have the skills to overcome. Recent statistics from the Federal Reserve say that employees spend 28 hours a month trying to resolve financial issues. Human Resource groups predict that businesses lose over $7,000 annually in lost productivity per affected employee. All of this results in business experiencing smaller bottom lines due to lack of focus, poor job morale, and an increase in employee stress related health costs.

Money Awareness Program (MAP) offers a 2-hour financial literacy workshop to employers so their employees can become debt-free while repairing their credit and beginning to save for the future. MAP is focused to provide skill acquisition and solutions.

MAP is a win-win for both the employer and the employee. MAP sells no financial products of any kind and has no hidden agenda. We pride ourselves as offering honest information for honest people. Our largest client is Tucson Medical Center. We do quarterly workshops for their 3000 employees. We are also providing a series of seminars for MAC--Microadvancement Business Center, some of which are in Spanish. MAP has bilingual capability.

We also coach individuals. MAP provides a complete do-it-yourself guide based on the premise that no one can take better care of your money than you. We build financial confidence and well being. We restore hope.

Our website is under construction again because we will soon be offering webinars for people outside Tucson.

MAP donates at least 10% of all proceeds to http://YouthEducationSecurity.org. Education is always the answer.

For more information about any aspect of MAP, please visit our website: http://MoneyAwarenessProgram.com.
