Dennis Salvatier’s Summary

Great and effective design is the product of creativity, knowledge and information. I pride myself in bringing those elements together in every project. Whether it's branding, marketing collateral, web development, signage, illustration or apparel design, rest assured that my goal is to wow the senses and develop a marketing strategy sure to impress your clients and customers.

For 11 years, I have been designing for companies and organizations such PacSun, OC Partnership, Re/Max Int., the City of Irvine and Bite Size Media, to name a few.

Dennis Salvatier’s Specialties:

Print Design, Branding, Illustration and Apparel Design

  • Media Designer

    Inland Hills Church

    (Religious Institutions industry)

    August 2008 — January 2010 (1 year 6 months)

    Originally hired to do graphic design, I quickly excelled at handling all areas of media design within IHC, including print, branding, environmental graphics and video. I also kept and maintained relationships with all the various vendors and the individual ministry leaders.

  • Sr. Graphic Designer

    Newsong: Third Culture Community

    (Non-Profit; Religious Institutions industry)

    March 2007 — August 2008 (1 year 6 months)

    Arriving as a contracted designer, I attained the role of Newsong’s full-time designer within weeks of starting. With strategic planning, I and the Art Director successfully came in under budget for Newsong’s annual leadership conference in ’08 by designing a stress ball with the registration url; saving $4,000 in printing costs. My duties included designing for print, web and signage, but also serving the North OC, L.A., and Mexico City sites.

  • Marketing Director/Head Designer

    Assist Marketing

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 2005 — February 2007 (1 year 10 months)

    I came onboard as a designer to the Marketing Director, but earned the title after expanding our services to local business and entrepreneurs, as opposed to just real estate professionals; gaining notoriety within the community and higher gross profit margins. I oversaw operations, production, two designers and met with clients to plan their marketing solutions, while still designing. I took the company from direct-mail to a full marketing solutions company in 2 months.
