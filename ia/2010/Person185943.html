Ron Segura’s Summary

Segura & Associates continues to provide services to the Facilities Industry. Since 2001 we have assisted our clients to set and attain company goals. We are one of the very few organizations that can bring the vast diverse experience to our projects.

Segura & Associates has had the opportunity to be involved in Green Cleaning initiatives from the very inception of the first pilot programs both Domestically and Internationally. We continue to assist our clients in the developement and implementation of Green programs

Segura & Associates has been recognized as an expert in the implementation of Team Cleaning Programs and is always aware of the latest Industry technologies.

Segura & Associates is a Strategic Partner in Tripod Leaning Associates a group that provides training programs in the form of workshops, podcasts, and marketing materials. Tripod assists their clients in the implementation of new programs.

Segura and Associates is now providing Colleges and Universities assistance in the developing rfps and leadership in the rfp process.

Ron Segura’s Specialties:

Developing and implementing Cleaning Programs that provide highest quality levels within designated budgets.

Analyzing outsourced cleaning programs.

Implementing of programs to increase productivity.

Assist and development of Green Cleaning Programs that contributes to LEED EB certification credits.
