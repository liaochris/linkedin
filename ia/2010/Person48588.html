Dan Walter’s Summary

Dan Walter, is the President and CEO of Performensation (www.performensation.com). He has provided solutions for equity and performance-based compensation for more than 15 years. He is also available as a dynamic, engaging speaker who uses expertise and humor to provide innovative and practical solutions.

Dan has extensive experience with both executive and broad-based programs with an emphasis on the design and management of performance share and units.

Performensation assists public and private companies of all sizes with a focus on the "not Fortune 1000". He creates effective, company-specific solutions paired with post-consultation support. Expertise includes diagnosis of issues, plan design, communication, administration best practices and reporting solutions.

Dan is the founder of Equity Compensation Experts (ECE), Board Member for the National Center for Employee Ownership (NCEO) and an award winning member of the National Association of Stock Plan Professionals (NASPP). Dan is producer of ShareComp 2010 and 2011, virtual equity events. He is also an active member of World at Work, Global Equity Organization (GEO), Silicon Valley Compensation Association, and the Society for Certified Equity Professionals.

Speaking engagements include: Annual conferences for the NASPP, GEO, NCEO, AICPA, Ohio Stock Forum, numerous webinars, regional and local events each year.

Dan is also a partner with Virtual Conference Partners, LLC. VCP produces computer-based conferences. "Impactful events, without the expense".

Keywords: Performance-based, Compensation, Stock Option, Restricted Stock, Restricted Units, ESPP, Employee Stock Purchase Plan, Equity Compensation, SARs, Stock Plan Administration, Consultant, Performance, Restricted Stock Units, "Pay for Performance", Performance Units, GEMS, RSU, Remuneration, Share-based, FAS123R, IFRS 2, ShareComp, Performance Units, Equity Compensation

Dan Walter’s Specialties:

Equity and Performance Compensation Design, Communication and Best Practices.

Performance Awards, Shares Units and Incentive Compensation. Goal and Metric creation and evaluation.

Employee Communication, Financial Reporting and Administration of Stock Option, Restricted Stock Shares and Units, Stock Purchase Plans and Stock Appreciation Rights (SARs), Provider Searches, Evaluations and RFPs. Dan also has deep expertise in the administrative and technological best practices for these programs.
