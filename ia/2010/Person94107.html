Jeff Ullman’s Summary

Jeff started in the call center industry in the mid 80’s. For the past 20+ years he has owned and operated call centers. Since he sold his call center in early 2000 he wanted to maintain his network and continue to work within the industry, that’s what led him into executive recruiting and outsourcing. The Call Center Group A-Z specializes in the placement of mid level managers to upper executives. The Call Center Group A-Z has clients all over the world and has a complete understanding and knowledge of call center operations and what it takes to be successful. Whether it’s a start up operation or an established operation Jeff has placed executives with success and endorsement from some of the most respected executives in the industry.

OUR GOAL IS TO MATCH GREAT CANDIDATES WITH GREAT EMPLOYERS!!!!
