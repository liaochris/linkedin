Ryan N. Shanks’s Summary

Ryan has spent the past 9 years developing strong business relationships between Financial Advisors, Registered Investment Advisors and Broker Dealers.

Ryan is the Founder of Finetooth Consulting, a research based consulting firm with offices in Illinois, Indiana, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Ohio, Washington and Wisconsin. Before founding Finetooth, he was a member of the executive team for Associated Securities Corp., an independent Broker Dealer located in Los Angeles, CA. Prior to joining Associated, Ryan was a Director with First Financial Planners, an independent Broker Dealer headquartered in Chesterfield, MO. During his tenure, Ryan has worked hard to earn a reputation in the industry for his ethical approach to the business as well as his unique ability to provide win-win solutions for all parties.

Broker/Dealer evaluations, contract negotiations, market research and analysis, relationship strategies, mergers and acquisitions, business growth initiatives, start-up consulting, succession planning, CPA/Attorney referral programs, RIA formation
