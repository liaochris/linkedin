Veronica Ybarra’s Summary

Veronica Ybarra began her sales career as a Timeshare Sales Representative with Bluegreen Corporation. She was named "Salesperson of the Year" in her first year with this publicly traded company that was recognized as one of Fortune Magazine's 100 fastest Growing companies. She was quickly promoted into a Senior Sales role and eventually assumed a Branch Sales Management position.

Veronica graduated from Bowling Green State University with a 4.0 GPA as a Broadcast Journalism Major. She worked in the newsroom at Bowling Green's local station (BG 24) and was eventually promoted to anchor. She is also an active member of her Sorority: Kappa Tau Alpha.

Her educational awards include the National Honor Society, Ohio State Board of Education- Award of Merit, Golden Key National Honor Society, United States Hispanic Leadership Institute- Certificate of Merit, and Ohio State Board of Education- Certificate of Recognition for Proficient Honors.

An extreme extrovert, Veronica builds strong and trusting relationships with the candidates she engages.
