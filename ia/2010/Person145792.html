Mike Briggs’s Summary

25 plus years of managing portfolios with specific focus on Supply Chain networks. Experience includes rationalizing global real estate portfolios and properties to meet the requirements of UPS Supply Chain Solution's diverse logistics offerings including parcel, air freight, ground, and ocean freight. This work resulted in significant occupancy cost reductions while also improving operational productivity metrics.

Areas of complimentary expertise include transaction management, property development, build to suit, automated facility design, project management, sourcing, and asset management. Expert distribution analysis and design to attain the best combination of capital, labor, and space utilization for projects throughout the world. Specific build to suit experience includes: PDMA compliant pharmaceutical facilities, high tech, return and repair, retail, data centers, large scale campus projects, large scale offices, and expansions.

Managed Projects/Project Teams/transactions in Canada, Mexico, Argentina, Brazil, Puerto Rico, Australia, Singapore, Germany, France, UK, Netherlands, Italy, Switzerland, Spain, Finland, Belgium, Hong Kong, Shanghai, and China.
