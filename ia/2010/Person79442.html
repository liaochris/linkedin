Haleh Alemzadeh Niroo’s Summary

Haleh Alemzadeh Niroo, Principal of Haleh Design Inc., has been practicing high-end design since 1992 in Atlanta, Georgia and Potomac, Maryland. Her work is inspired by her travels across Europe and her interest in art history. With over 2 decades of experience creating luxurious, graceful and lively spaces, she is dedicated to helping make your home distinctly your own, from pre-construction design to final fine details.

Haleh’s goal for each project is to create an artistic and functional environment that is elegant and grounded in the rich history of European architecture. Drawing from her experience abroad and her design education, Haleh’s work is inspired by the world of art and architecture. Her design is both creative and practical, and her attention to detail and enthusiasm for design give Haleh an edge over her competition. From charming, old-world style to clean, modern elegance, Haleh’s designs will lend a classic yet unique touch to your space.
