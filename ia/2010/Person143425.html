Michael Rogers’s Summary

With twenty years of experience as a California Contractor and now a General Contractor in Oregon, Rogers has exposed himself to many sides of the building industry. Industry recognition led to Rogers as the choice for some of the most distinguished names of sports, motion pictures and the recording industry like Frank Sinatra and Andy Williams.

Michael Rogers’s Specialties:

Managing and operating two successful contracting companies in California and Oregon. Creative Director and designer for Michael Rogers Wallcovering. Marketing and nationally distributing this wallcovering line. Consultant and Instructor of all decorative finishes from cabinetry to flooring. Trained and certified by Bomanite in micro-top, stain concrete and advanced decorative concrete methods. Selected and certified by SkimStone in1995 to be one of twenty nationally instructors.
