Mark Gotlib’s Summary

As a professional in the field of electrical contracting, my extensive experience guarantees professional results when it matters most; when it’s your project, it’s on time, it’s under budget, and it’s done right.

In my 27 years of construction experience I have developed my skill sets and responsibilities through hard work and accountability, my reputation as a Division Manager of Ray Angelini, Inc. Industrial, Mission Critical, & Service Group was made possible through my past achievements as Vice President/Branch Manager, Senior Project Manager, Estimator, and Consultant. Each of these steps helped build my thorough understanding of electrical construction within the business of contracting.

My knowledge of complex electrical systems within a project’s perspective, along with administrative and engineering skill sets, and applying leadership skills brings it all together. Through these wide ranging experiences I can make sure projects flow efficiently, I can correct distressed projects , I can make well-administered projects more cost effective. My extensive knowledge gives me the confidence for accurate operational and financial oversight, intelligent planning and directing of resources, as well as troubleshooting and streamlining of operations. All of which require understandable and precise communication with individuals and organizations. When communicating facts and figures in a timely manner important decisions can then be made through understanding and knowledge.
