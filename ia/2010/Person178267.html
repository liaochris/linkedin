Richard Pérez-Feria’s Summary

I'm an award-winning Editor-in-Chief for magazines and Web sites. Most recently, I served as Senior Vice President, Editorial & Entertainment at República, a boutique advertising and public relations agency. Concurrently, I served as COO, Editor-in-Chief to PLANETASTIC, a Web development company I co-owned where I worked on celebrity and sports sites.

I was formerly the Editor-in-Chief of Time Inc’s People en Español, the nation’s best-selling Spanish language magazine. a position I held for nearly four years from 2003.

During my incredibly celebrated tenure, People en Español enjoyed unprecedented growth, including year-over-year rate base increases, record advertising sales and numerous editorial and creative awards. Every year I was Editor-in-Chief, People en Español was on Adweek's “10 Under 50” List, a first for a Hispanic title. The magazine also won two FOLIO: Gold Awards as “Best Entertainment Magazine” and an additional Gold Award in 2006 for “Cover of the Year” featuring Jennifer López and the 50 Most Beautiful people.

In 2008, I was the founding Editorial Director to BRASH.com, the men's Web vertical launched by Glam Media (glam.com).

I've also been the Editor-in-Chief for scores of magazines and Web sites including 7x7, gym, Celebrity Style, SHAPE's Fit Hollywood, Strong, Teen Celebrity, JUMP, music choice Magazine, TennisMatch, Fit Pregnancy Style, Burn! International Business Chronicle, POZ and many more.

A graduate of Tulane University, I'm currently writing my memoirs: It's Exhausting Being Fabulous...And Other Things I Told My Mother.

Richard Pérez-Feria’s Specialties:

Launching and re-launching magazines and/or Web sites into powerful, cohesive, beautiful, buzz-worthy and money-making enterprises.
