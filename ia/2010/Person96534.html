Jerry Mitchell’s Summary

.Jerry is president of Jerry R. Mitchell and Associates Inc. I am an entrepreneur with a passion for helping companies grow. I've worked in the Fortune 500 world, been part of twelve companies eight of which either went public or were sold/merged with larger companies. Jerry is also Chairman of Modularis Inc. http://www.modularis.com.

In November 1997 he and three other entrepreneurs were described in Fortune Magazine as serial entrepreneurs because of their accomplishments starting multiple successful companies.

He is President/Founder of The Midwest Entrepreneurs Forum.

Extensive experience in business consulting services including globalization strategy, strategic marketing, international business development, professional development seminars, operations, and the business management of both large high-tech and start-up businesses.

I enjoy the excitement of developing new companies and bringing new products to market. I I have built companies and product lines from $0 revenue to hundreds of millions in revenue. I am an active advisor in the technology industry and an entrepreneur mentor


Jerry is an accomplished public speaker, able to craft and deliver accurate and convincing messages to customers, media and co-workers. His speaking experience ranges from Chicago-are networking groups to international technical conferences. He was a key note speaker November 6, 2008 at The First China Jiangsu Conference International Technology Transfer and Commercialization


If I could only share one thing with you about me it would be that I want people to know I am passionate about helping others create a financial reality for their lives. In a day where so many people chase money to pay their bills, could it be they have never found the formula of creating wealth?

Financial wealth is not a secret formula, there are rules that if followed produce success every time. It's my mission to get that formula in the hands of people looking for a better way.
