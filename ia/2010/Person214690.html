Wendy Montes de Oca,MBA’s Summary

My diversified background includes over 15 years of experience in marketing/media, financial services and law. I have a proven track record with both acquisition and retention (CRM) efforts as well as has both editorial and copywriting experience. My speciality is multi-channel marketing with expertise in direct response marketing for the Web. My marketing initiatives helped bring in millions of dollars in revenues for the companies I have worked for. I was the editor of a quarterly investment newsletter and had several investment articles published in Hometown News. My marketing articles have appeared in enewsletters such as Early to Rise and Makepeace Total Package as well as on various blogs and directories. I have also spoke at prominent marketing conferences/forums such as Specialized Information Publishers Association (SIPA) and Financial Publisher's Association (FPA).

Professionally, I have worked as a consultant as well as for entrepreneurial and Fortune 500 companies.

I received a Bachelor of Science degree from St. John’s University and a Master of Business Administration degree from Nova South Eastern University.

I have been recognized throughout my career with various awards for quality, innovation, teamwork, and new product/new business development.
