Rachel Green’s Summary

Professional Speaker | Coach | Author.
Award winning communication specialist, emotional intelligence expert and job interview coach.
Specialises in helping people keep calm, cool and clear-headed even with difficult and frustrating people.

2010 keynotes include: "Water off a duck's back", "Meet, great and speak: How to make your networking work", "Mastering emotional intelligence" and "Sparkling with confidence."

Also builds self-confidence and self-esteem in women and is the Founding Director of Confident Woman Australia. Her site features stories on over 55 inspiring Australian women and it provides over 75 free, top quality articles on self-confidence, self-esteem, self-concept and managing anxiety.

Author of 9 CD sets on managing difficult people without getting upset, confidence for women, communication skills, public speaking, networking and meditation.

Has been meditating since 1987, and using the Feldenkrais Method for her own personal development since 1989.

Author: "The beginner's guide to becoming a brilliant Master of Ceremonies," and "Midlife and Happiness", and many E-books.

Has tertiary level qualifications in speech pathology, psychology, adult education and the Feldenkrais Method. Is an accredited user of the MBTI personality types and the Genos Emotional Intelligence assessments.

Has coached leading TV and radio presenters at the ABC, been an academic at Curtin University, and a voluntary counsellor for Lifeline.

Likes to celebrate life, connect with nature and LAUGH a lot ... and make others laugh too.

"Who would ever have thought that you could make emotional intelligence so interesting and exciting! Wow! The room was buzzing and people were having such a good time learning how to manage their emotions and it was so relevant to their work. We will certainly be booking you to speak at more of our conferences in the future."
Monique Williamson, Conference Convenor, National Disability Services Conference.

Rachel Green’s Interests:

People - I have a passion for understanding people and what makes them tick. This is the basis of my work and all my studies - always looking for further jigsaw pieces to explain and help people in understanding themselves and interacting with others. Emotions and emotional intelligence. Personal development - I believe I am a better coach, mentor, trainer, speaker, boss, wife, daughter, friend … the more time I put into being the very best I can be. Spending time with my husband of 35 years, spending time with friends and family, bush-walking, comedy shows, and the footy!
