O.G. McClinton III’s Summary

I have trained EPIC at several different medical facilities across the country; Kaiser Permanente, Southern California, Chowan Community Hospital, University Health System North Carolina, Dallas Children’s Hospital, Parkland Community Hospital, and the Cleveland Clinic. I have provided both workflow and functionality based training and support to clinical and non-clinical managers and end-users.

I possess a firm understanding of EPIC design and build having trained ASAP, STORK, Beacon, ClinDoc, CPOE, OpTime, both Inpatient and Ambulatory. I have support Pharmacy during EPIC COE and CPOE Go-lives.

I developed the NoteWriter Curriculum, created all classroom materials, and trained all classes for the NoteWriter Pilot at Cleveland Clinic in Cleveland, OH. My other curriculum development experience includes Participant, Trainer, and Go-Live Support Manuals for CPOE and ADT Navigators, as well as a lesson plan for Beacon Training all at the Cleveland Clinic. I have also helped to develop CBT/WBT (Beacon), Quick Start Reference Guides, PowerPoint Slideshows and other classroom materials to support Training. In addition, I have built patients in Training Build Community to be used for training

I have provided both one-on-one and classroom training in classrooms with as many as 20+ participants. I have participated in workflow discussions and issue meetings with clinical/system analysts, nursing managers, physician champions, and medical managers. I have synthesized the needs and expectations expressed during these meetings into effective presentations of training and training materials that reflect the desires of the facility and team.
