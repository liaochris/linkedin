PureHealth Medical Centers’s Summary

Background
PureHealth Medical Centers deliver retail-oriented, primary and urgent care healthcare services under a “Patient-Centered Medical Home” delivery approach. We provide health care that is convenient, affordable, and comprehensive.

Our Vision
Transforming the Delivery of Healthcare in America

Our Mission
• We are the leader in providing affordable, convenient, and comprehensive healthcare in the communities we serve and;
• We create environments that promote compassionate care and genuine concern for our patients and;
• We provide significant personal and career rewards to everyone contributing to our success.

Comprehensive Healthcare
Our comprehensive services include primary care, general illness, sutures, x-rays, laboratory tests, occupational medicine (PureWork™ program), and preventative medicine such as immunizations, physicals and well women services (PurePrevention™ program).

Convenient Healthcare
PureHealth Medical Centers are more convenient than a traditional doctor’s office or a hospital emergency room. Our centers are open every day, including nights and weekends. Care is delivered on the customer’s schedule with no appointment required for urgent care services. An online check-in service, Net Check-In™, is available on our website allowing patients to wait at home, work, or while running errands. PureHealth also provides in-house dispensing of the most common prescriptions so the patient can avoid a trip to the pharmacy after leaving the center. Most insurance co-pays are accepted.

Affordable Healthcare
Our services are affordable to the uninsured and underinsured (those with high deductible insurance plans) by enrollment in the PureSavings™ program. The PureSavings™ program gives a 40% discount on services for a nominal annual membership fee of $49. The program covers the entire family.
