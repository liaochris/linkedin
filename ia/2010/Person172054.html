Richard Harrison’s Summary

I have 20 years' experience of solving business communication issues working as a copywriter and marcoms specialist - mostly in the technology sector. See some customer success stories and examples of my work at www.richwords.co.uk.

Since mobile is one of the most dynamic communication platforms emerging today, I've teamed up with Richard Jennis and Simon Brooker (both on LinkedIn) to form Applicology. Our apps development company and consultancy - http://www.applicology.co.uk - aims to help organisations take advantage of the:

* growth in SmartPhone ownership
* success of Apple's App Store
* likely expansion of other apps outlets
* the massive increase in application downloads

Applicology will run in parallel to RichWords, where I specialise in creating copy for technology clients such as COLT, Dell, Genesys, Pioneer, Sony, Toshiba and WTG.

I'm a business writer for non-IT clients too, completing projects on behalf of the building industry as well as management consultants, publishers and retailers. My work in these areas includes communications for AXA, Benetton, Capgemini Consulting, CRU, The Economist and Economist Conferences, Metal Bulletin and TK Maxx.

If you need to improve your internal or external communications, I can:

- critique your existing plans and materials (and improve them)
- advise on social media and mobile marketing
- train your staff to write more effectively
- create original and engaging copy from scratch

I'm comfortable generating copy for websites (with SEO in mind), brochures, case studies, direct mail packs and email campaigns, podcasts, videos, press and radio ads, presentations and other marketing communications. And I can draw upon my network of trusted professionals to fulfil the elements of your marcoms plan needing specialist skills.

Please contact me via LinkedIn or send an email to richard@richwords.co.uk.
