Alison Day’s Summary

I am the Marketing Director for TextbooksRus.com and Bookstores.com, two web-based companies that are based out of Columbus, Ohio. My background includes experience with RMD Advertising, a full service advertising agency where my jobs have included press release writing, media pitching, and event planning. My current role as Marketing Director includes the RMD tasks as well as social media strategy, strategic marketing planning, branding, web writing, product launching, communicating and connecting.

In addition to working, I enjoy being on the leadership board of Social Media of Columbus. I manage our college connections. Check out www.smccolumbus.org and get involved today! I am also a volunteer at Riverside Hospital.
