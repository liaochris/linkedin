Roger Hendrick’s Summary

Working in varied multimedia development and video production since 1996. Expert in professional DVD Authoring/Compression and Multimedia Authoring for CD/DVD, Blu-ray and the Web. Experienced DP, online editor, graphic designer, 3d modeling and animation, effects compositing, as well as Flash, PHP, SQL, Adobe Flex/AIR and Silverlight.

Currently working in HD Video Production, HDMV and BD-J Blu-ray authoring, rich internet application development, and Digital Asset Management. Recently added an Autodesk Lustre Color Correction suite here in our CT studio.

Owner of The Airship Factory, a video production company serving clients coast to coast with offices and affiliates in CT, MA, AZ and CA. We offer full service HD Video Production, Post, VFX, Green Screen Compositing, Color Correction and a vast array of other specialized services. We provide script writing, translations, captions, produce TV spots, corporate training videos, web optimized video and provide event production.

Owner of Electric Hoster, a full service web design and hosting company. With Data Center locations across the US, Electric Hoster provides reliable hosting and personalized one-on-one service to large and small businesses alike. We manage the entire online presence of each of our clients and offer class leading up-time and up-grade possibilities. We offer everything from simple business identity sites to full CMS, rich e-commerce solutions and database connectivity options.
