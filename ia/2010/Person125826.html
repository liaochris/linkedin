Lisa Sullivan’s Summary

Upon graduating from college in 1992, I spent six years in business working my way up from an answering service operator to an office administrator. The skills & experience I received through that time were valuable as I transitioned to the field of education, a field that I trained for originally. I then spent nine years as a teacher in both the public and private sectors. Even that experience has helped me to grow in my craft.

Transitioning to a media-based career in 2007, I served our viewing public and our station, WNCN NBC 17, as a liaison between the two. Cultivating relationships with members of the community in which I was charged helped our online product, MyNC.com to grow and prosper. While I am no longer with WNCN, I know that I have taken valuable and marketable skills that I have developed over the last two years with me to a position that continues to cultivate them.

Currently, I am Copywriter / Social Media Specialist at Media Two Interactive in downtown Raleigh while still occasionally speaking on the subject of Social and New Media marketing to area nonprofits, business round tables, and forums. This fall, I will be teaching a four-series beginners workshop at Johnston Community College in their Small Business Center.
