Sparky Powell (LION)’s Summary

Founder and President of K.W. Powell & Associates, a B-to-B Marketing Communications firm, Creator of the R:center Dealer Marketing Software and KW360 Web Software, Creator of the Entrepreneurial Ninja Marketing Series. Seasoned 20 year veteran with experience in the analysis, design and implementation of Small Business, Corporate and Dealer (Distributor/Agent) Sales and Marketing Solutions. My programs are always focused on new business development and profitable growth through smart, tech-savvy sales and marketing strategies.

Quickly into my career, I realized the inefficiencies of traditional marketing efforts, and began reshaping the old MarCom model. I moved clients in the direction of a large number of smaller, highly targeted and personal marketing offers, implemented regionally throughout the entire sales channel. These changes resulted in increased response and conversion rates, incremental profit and increased return-on-investment for marketing expenditures.

I have proudly helped 1000’s of companies grow their businesses and offer dozens of courses to train entrepreneurs on becoming Entrepreneurial Ninjas, providing a quick path to understanding and leveraging today’s fast moving sales & marketing technologies. Courses help entrepreneurs build a low-cost, highly effective sales and marketing machine that allows them to do what you do best while making the money they deserve.

I am an agent of change, a visionary in sales and marketing technology and am emerging as the expert in sales channel marketing. My actions demonstrate the ambition, drive and passion of a top-shelf leader.

I am an expert who coaches, trains and speaks to corporations, dealer organizations and associations on effective integration of “Sales 2.0 and Web 2.0” marketing strategies, branding and positioning, social media, professional selling skills, lead generation and new business development.
