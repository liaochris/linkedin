Wendy Maynard’s Summary

I co-own Kinesis, Inc. - a marketing and design firm. We specialize in branding. Our clients benefit from our experience at creating marketing materials that help them increase their business revenues. Our work includes websites, identity packages, brochures, trade show booth design, logos, catalogs, and more. View our portfolio at http://www.kinesisinc.com/portfolio/index.html

I am absolutely passionate about providing effective and proven marketing ideas, strategies, and tools. My number one goal is to show my clients exactly how to attract more customers and increase revenues while being their authentic self and creating effective marketing systems.

The marketing systems that I have developed work in the real world and have been successfully applied by my clients. My approach is based on some simple principles: when business owners focus on helping their customers, automating their systems, and developing their reputation as an expert, they EASILY bring in more revenue and work with their DREAM customers.

Best of success, Wendy
