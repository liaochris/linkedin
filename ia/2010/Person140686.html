Melissa Farrelly’s Summary

Well seasoned Sr. Human Resources Practitioner and Business Analyst, with the ability to lead change and streamline processes through the use of technology and best practices. I began my career as a temporary benefits & compensation analyst at South Shore Hospital and have assumed more progressive roles over the years, including Director & Manager positions in Healthcare, Human Services, Home Care, Affordable Housing and Financial Services. In my career, I have experienced great accomplishments in many areas of strategic and operational support, including HRIS/Payroll implementations, billing & scheduling implementations, applicant tracking system design & implementation, onboarding software, retirement plan redesign, benefit vendor negotiations, workers compensation & safety programs and administration and more!

I thrive in an environment that is creative and entrapreneurial in spirit and I crave change and love the process improvement responsibilities I face every day. I'm a well respected leader that motivates my staff to do their best, not for me, but for themselves. I am committed to my continued education in my field and consistently strive to remain abreast of best practices in HR as well as actively tracking legislative changes. I am pursuing my degree part time as a part of my continued education. The opportunity to pursue my education after I have established a career has proven to be much more rewarding as I am ensuring the knowledge I gain can be applied immediately and will enhance my already highly technical and professional approach to my craft...in short, I love what I do! This is evidenced by my completion of my SPHR certification, my completion of a Master's certificate in HR & Six Sigma and my training in AIRS Certified Diversity and Internet Recruitment.
