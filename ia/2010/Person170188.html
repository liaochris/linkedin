Adam Brett’s Summary

Real Estate is in Adam’s blood…he is a second generation real estate agent who was answering phones at his father’s realty and mortgage companies as soon as he was able to walk and talk! Adam soaked up all there was to know about the industry, as well as his family tradition of providing superb, high-touch customer service.

Leveraging the power of information …However, Adam took his thirst for knowledge one step further. Recognizing the opportunity to embrace technology as a way to enhance customer service, Adam earned a B.S. in Computer Science. He leverages the power of information technology and partners with companies such as LendingTree.com, HomeValues.com, HomeGain.com, and Realtor.com to offer his clients unparalleled visibility to home buyers.

Technology/Marketing award winner….By using the latest technology communication tools Adam streamlines the home buying and selling process for his clients. This ensures that his clients receive prompt and accurate up-to-the minute communications, as well as a fresh marketing strategy that encompasses both traditional and the latest online mediums. In fact, this high-tech strategic approach has won Adam national recognition as the 2006 Hewlett Packard National Realtor Technology Award Winner and more recently as a 2007 Realtor.com “Online Marketing Award of Excellence” honoree.

Integrity and experience….As an advocate of the six-sigma methodology, Adam is constantly improving his business model. He employs a level of integrity to which many aspire and few achieve. Adam’s success is evident in his work ethic of ‘don’t take the easy way – do it the right way’ and that’s his clients say about him…”Adam works hard, and he does it right!“
