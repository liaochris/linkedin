Julie Crabill’s Summary

Julie Crabill is the founder and CEO of Inner Circle Labs, the un-agency where every project is a passion project. As the CEO, Julie is focused on helping startups build communications into company DNA. She is also responsible for the overall vision and direction of the company and team. Before founding Inner Circle Labs, Julie spent 10 years in agency side PR – most recently as vice president of the Consumer Lifestyle Practice at SHIFT Communications where she led the practice regionally and oversaw all consumer accounts. Prior to SHIFT, Julie worked at Edelman, Weber Shandwick and the Benjamin Group. In addition, she worked in the Global Advanced Technology practice at Korn/Ferry International, an executive search firm. Julie earned a bachelor’s in PR from San Jose State University and has been recognized with awards ranging from PR Week's prestigious PR Student of the Year honor in college to PR News’ 15 to Watch Under 35 in 2005.
