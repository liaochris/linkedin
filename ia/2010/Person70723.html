Francesca Prada’s Summary

I'm driven to create fully immersive entertainment experiences offered through digital, cinematic and theatrical means.

I'm interested in continuing to serve as a creative catalyst and bringing out the best performances in all members of a team, as well as in finding the best available resources for ventures involved in creating film, television, advertising and hybrid forms of entertainment, marketing and media.

Innovation, piloting new methods and mentoring seem to be my strong suit along with a global perspective.

Francesca Prada’s Specialties:

Creativity. Diplomacy. Team Building. Mentoring. Strategic insight of creative overview, messaging, story development & structure, marketability, packaging & production operations. Knack for identifying, recruiting & developing talent in film & art, as well as for identifying trends. Business Affairs orientation & skills.
Multilingual communication skills – written and oral. Integrated Branding Campaigns, UGC, Curatorial skills. Advocate for independent artists of all kinds.
