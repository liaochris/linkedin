Kate Elliott’s Summary

Kate Hartnick Elliott is the President and Founder of Hartnick Consulting, a boutique firm that provides executive search services to e-commerce, marketing, media, and nonprofit organizations.

Prior to founding Hartnick Consulting in January, 2001, Ms. Elliott was Vice President of the Strategic Planning Group at interactive agency, i-traffic; Vice President of Advertising and Business Development at award-winning online cultural magazine, FEED; the first Director of Corporate Sales at Internet research firm, Jupiter Communications; and a Marketing Executive at The New York Daily News.

An active member of the New York business community, Elliott is on the Advisory Board for Columbia Business School's Lang Center for Entrepreneurship and was previously a member of the Board of New York Women In Communications (NYWICI), Editor-in-Chief of NYWICI's Matrix Times and co-chair of the Columbia Business School Alumni in Media Group.

Ms. Elliott received her BA, Magna Cum Laude, from Amherst College and her MBA from Columbia Business School.
