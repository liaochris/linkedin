François Guérette’s Summary

Experienced Web professional with a rich online local media expertise. With 14 years of experience in leading Print & Internet advertising companies, as well as in the Telecommunications sector, he has developed a solid approach to product development, marketing strategy and strategic planning, powered by national and international collaboration. In his previous position as Marketing Director reporting to the President at Quebecor MediaPages, he was part of the leading team in charge of creating the new Print & Online directory company. Prior to that, he has held a key position as Senior Product Manager at Yellow Pages Group in Canada, responsible for the online products and pricing expansion strategy.
