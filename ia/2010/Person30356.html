Bryant Nielson’s Summary

I am currently the Strategic Alliance & Acquisitions Director for FinancialTrainingSolutions (FTS). FTS is a global training firm that specializes in "Delivering Innovative Training Solutions" to the corporate and financial markets. With offices in New York and London, FTS provides programs that cover the major financial markets. FTS's approach to training is to AVOID "off-the-shelf" programs; preferring to work with clients on customizing programs that integrate their business objectives with the programs delivered. This extra effort in client attention clearly separates FTS from the competition.

FinancialTrainingSolutions is a division of CapitalWave Inc.

Most recently, I was the North American Director for ACF Consultants Ltd & Acumen Technology Inc. ACF is a Global Banking Consultancy, training the capital market and investment banking community. Acumen Technologies specializes in the development and delivery of capital market elearning and tools. Additionally, I was the Global Training Director for the open enrollment programs at: ACF Academy an open enrollment academy.

Previously, I was Executive Director for Lengthen Your Stride! LLC a Coaching, Training and Personal Development company located in northern New Jersey. I was the Director eLearning Strategy and Development for MortgageMae University (MMU) and for the Realtor Development Center (RDC). Taking material, refined over many years of experience and active training, and condensing the coursework and training into high impact, natural learning, and comprehensive programs.

I have over 25+ years of Senior Management in the training and mortgage markets, encompassing running my own mortgage firm, in New York City for 15 years; in addition to working with national and regional mortgage companies.

I am a believer that the coursework is not to be static and will continue to expand and grow and change over time.
