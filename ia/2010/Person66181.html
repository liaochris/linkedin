Eric Matrullo’s Summary

• Eric Matrullo is the Founder and President of WorldWise Fashion Consulting, a global consulting firm for fashion, luxury and sportswear companies, based in New York. Eric advises companies on how to maximize their international growth and/or find or negotiate licensing or distributor partnerships in North/South America, Asia, Europe and the Middle East.
• Prior to creating his own company, Eric directed International Sales and Licensing at Michael Kors, Coach, 7 For All Mankind and directed Strategic Planning for the U.S. at Nine West Group. He has over 20 years of experience in developing brand awareness and increasing market share for consumer products including collection and bridge/better sportswear and accessories, premium denim and fine leather goods.
• Having lived in France, Japan and the U.S., Eric has extensive international sales, licensing and strategic planning experience with global fashion brands as well as close relationships with premier distributors, licensees and partnership with top regional consulting firms. This makes him uniquely qualified to advise companies, from start-ups to prominent brands, looking to enter new product categories in the U.S. and/or grow their international markets.

Eric Matrullo’s Specialties:

Skills include licensing, retail and wholesale sales, negotiation of distribution and licensing agreements, strategic planning, direct marketing and management. Fluent in French and English with an excellent working knowledge of Japanese.
