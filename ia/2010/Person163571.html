Phil Baddock’s Summary

Worked briefly as a Retail Lending manager for a bank after leaving school. Went on to work for Sensis (Telstra's wholly owned directory subsidiary) in a variety of roles beginning with Help Desk Officer/Team leader, PreSales Support/Solutions Architecture, Project Mgmt, Systems Design, IT Management, then moved into General Management and became involved in commercial strategy and aquisition work with Sensis.

In 2000 I left the corporate world, and moved into a small business (Anstat Property Info - now part of SAI Global) to help lead the systems and commercial development of an online property information ASP business and helped develop it into one of the largest players in its market.

Then experienced a commercial failure when working on an attempt to create an online conveyancing business, then moved into helping with a managed exit for the owner from an Legal accounting software business....then moved into LAWLEX, an online publishing and regulatory change management business. Helped craft a strategy to take LAWLEX into online Risk and Compliance management and develop a large scale online ASP business servicing the Australian Financial Services market, and played a leading role in winning 3 of the big 4 banks, and a number of large insurer accounts.

Recently joined my own business - Salsa Internet (www.salsainternet.com.au), full time in order to build up the Search Engine Marketing and Search Engine Optimisation practice of the business. Developed go to market strategy, and SEO & PPC product packaging from scratch, marketing collateral - and implemented key operational processes & procedures to drive the success & growth of the practice.
