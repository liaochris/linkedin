Russell Case’s Summary

I have been told my background is a bit unique for a corporate and securities lawyer. I prefer to think I have just been lucky. What else could explain why I have been able to move in and out of private practice, combine ten years working with great lawyers with ten years of "in the trenches" experience at multi-national and publicly-traded corporations? Even fewer lawyers have been able to shift from law to business and back, moving from in-house counsel to director of international business development, vice president of mergers & acquisitions and board member of private and public companies. All the while traveling the world helping great companies like Scientific Atlanta, BellSouth and Concert expand their businesses. And now, living happily in Boise, Idaho, instead of my usual airline seat 12A, I am still helping people build and expand businesses, putting my business side to work by evaluating company business models and creating strategic plans while applying my legal skills to structure complex multi-party ventures and partnerships in the renewable energy and other arenas. Really, how lucky is that!

Russell Case’s Specialties:

Multi-party joint ventures; renewable energy funding and development; international and national business expansions via mergers, acquisitions, partnerships, ventures and distribution networks; funding options/structures for entrepreneurial and early stage companies; strategic planning; international business operational support.
