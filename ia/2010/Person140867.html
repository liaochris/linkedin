Melissa Taylor’s Summary

Melissa Taylor writes articles, newsletter content and blog posts on topics ranging from literacy to circus life but her primary focus and passion is with literacy and education. Her writing has appeared in Colorado Parent, Fiber Arts Magazine, Mile High Sports Magazine, Colorado Expression and she just recently sold an idea to Family Fun Magazine.

Taylor's taken on a new path which she loves, social media. Recently, she's began freelancing to assist businesses with marketing online - on blogs, Twitter, Facebook and so forth. "It's helping match my client with their customer so it's win-win," says Taylor.

Taylor's a freelance writer, a mom of two and a teacher with a M.A. in Education. She's worked training teachers as a literacy trainer for the Public Education and Business Coalition in Denver, Colorado.

Taylor created and blogs at Imagination Soup, a learning website for inquisitive children. www.imaginationsoup.net

She's starting a book review blog for Colorado Parent magazine - stay tuned for that.

Taylor's a writer who writes to be readable -- she finds the positive in everything and lives life with humor, empathy and compassion.
