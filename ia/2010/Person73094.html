Gary Gray’s Summary

• Mr. Gray in 2008 acquired EnXergy, LLC (www.enxergy.com) where with a world-class team of environmental and energy professionals they have developed a unique architecture of stored energy solutions used to embed alternative energy into today’s complex life style platforms. EnXergy looks to acquire and create a diverse array of energy and environmental Intellectual Property and alternative energy partnerships. Prior to EnXergy Mr. Gray was part of a diverse array of start ups that included Vcommand (voice activated GPS systems for Delphi and Sagem) where his responsibilities included creation of content relationships and business development for distribution, manufacturing, intellectual property, and global sourcing; Modius (disparate device management solutions) where he identified channel partners and global sourcing; and Mr. Gray is also a founding partner of HDReady, LLC (content productions specializing in HD – High Definition Content) which currently owns and produces the number one nationally syndicated HDTV show on PBS “HDSoundstage”. Mr. Gray has been involved in the Computer/Electronics industry for over twenty-five years and has held senior executive sales and marketing positions within the Fortune 500. Mr. Gray has a diverse technical marketing back ground which includes: Semiconductor, System on a Chip (SOC), Digital Content Management, Energy Solutions, and MPEG. Mr. Gray has served on various boards including Greatbox Corporation, Mediaworks, Fusionmedia Systems, Bright China Group, and Enxergy. Mr. Gray is involved in fund raising for local charities and youth athletics in the Chicago area.
