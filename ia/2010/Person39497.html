Christopher Aaron Hughes’s Summary

Chris Hughes is the co-founder of Purple Hair Promotions, a company that manages online marketing campaigns for offline businesses. He is a business owner, social media advisor, juggler and an avid blogger. Sick of the traditional “interruption” marketing approach to business, he was drawn to social media, visualizing the potential to create and engage worldwide communities.

Through his activities in social media, he has attracted business deals, internet radio appearances, and plans on hosting workshops teaching Internet Marketing to offline business owners. His mission is to help businesses grow by using technology.

Who I Help:
I am looking to help offline business owners and MLM Business Owners drive traffic to their websites using Internet Marketing techniques and Social Media strategies.

Public Speaking:
I have gone through public speaking training throughout the course of my life and love the opportunity to help groups of people. I speak to businesses, networking groups and associations on the power that social media and Search Engine marketing can have on building their business and personal brand.
