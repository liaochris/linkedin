Sohaib Thiab’s Summary

Sohaib Thiab, an Arabic internet entrepreneur, started off with a web hosting company while finishing his Bachelor’s degree in computer science, he then started launching websites ranging from video humor sites, flash gaming and web hosting related sites, www.wh-d.com a web2.0 hosting directory was covered in many press releases as the 1st web2.0 hosting directory. He then co-launched www.faye3.com one of the 1st Arabic social networking websites after winning the 1st place in the Queen Rania National Entrepreneurship Award, the project was later on acquired by Maktoob.com the largest Arabic portal.

Later on Sohaib got into browser based games, as a gaming veteran Sohaib was anxious to get into this line of business, he acquired www.mafiacorruption.com which was the base that started his current company Go3 Media and helped in the launch of Wizards Productions an Arabic gaming company specializing in browser based games.
