Derek Robinson’s Summary

Heavybrea Multimedia is a collective of freelance designers that work in all areas of graphic design – brochures and printed materials, complete identity packages including business cards, logos, stationary, magazines, books, web design and much more. Our creative approach and personal attention to customer satisfaction are essential aspects of our work. We offer outstanding visual value and impact by providing high-quality, client specific design and fresh visual solutions executed at a highly professional level. Our combined years of experience in the graphic arts industry sets us ahead in proficiency, creativity and work flow expertise. Were fully equipped to provide efficient, timely service, as well as, a meticulous ability to take a project from start to finish.
