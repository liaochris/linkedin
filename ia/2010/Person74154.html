Geoff Rutledge’s Summary

I have been active as a physician, teacher, researcher, consultant, and medical executive who applies computer-based technologies to improve healthcare. Was priviliged to develop consumer health sites (WebMD.com and Wellsphere.com) and to select and implement physician-focused information solutions (as CMIO at San Mateo Medical Center).

I'm excited now to return to my passion of creating technology solutions to help clinicians deliver healthcare more efficiently! At Epocrates, we are building new and exciting handheld applications to help clinicians at the point of care.
