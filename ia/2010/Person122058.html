Laura Sharpe’s Summary

Sharpe Recruitment is a North East based consultancy, dedicated to placing professionals in rewarding jobs in Newcastle, Durham, Sunderland, Middlesbrough and beyond.

We cover a variety of disciplines including Digital Media, Creative, PR, Marketing, IT and Business Operations.

Run by mother and daughter team Julie and Laura Sharpe, we aim to provide an efficient, reliable and trustworthy service.

We make it our goal everyday to always maintain high levels of customer service and make a real difference to our Candidates careers and our Client's businesses.
