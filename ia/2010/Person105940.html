College instructor with experience in online and face-to-face teaching, specializing in the following classes: Art Appreciation, Art History Survey, Postmodernism. Research interests include twentieth century art, surrealism, and surrealist photography.

Museum educator specializing in designing public programs for adult audiences such as lectures, symposia, gallery talks, book clubs, performances, and after-hours events. Have also functioned as docent coordinator and developed docent training based on permanent collections.
