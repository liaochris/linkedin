Polly Capots’s Summary

A senior-level HR Business Partner with over 10-years of comprehensive experience leading all HR functions & teams in the financial services industry for small & mid-size, team-oriented, evolving companies. Polly Capots possess well-rounded business acumen, resulting from roles in operational, inside sales & client relations divisions. She has a demonstrated track record of success, earning higher-level positions with decision-making flexibility & responsibilities throughout her career.

Polly's commonly acknowledged for constructing & sustaining sound, long-term partnerships with business leaders. She has proven experience collaborating with executive management (C.E.O., President, E.V.P. of Operations, E.V.P. of Sales & Marketing, C.F.O.) & line-of-business & functional leaders. Deemed a major contributing partner & a staple at board meetings, Polly provides valuable HR input to strategies to enable an organization to more effectively source, evaluate & motivate employees in increasingly turbulent business environments. She excels at not only establishing objectives that support the attainment of the overall business plan & objectives, has also successfully integrated them, resulting in process & organizational improvements & cost savings.

Polly has extensive involvement in fast-paced, changing & matrix organizations in cross-functional, multi-state team environments. She has direct experience working with blended corporate cultures & has an astute ability to address & implement HR strategic plans for acquisitions, retention, & recognition/rewards programs. Proven knowledge in employment law including complaint investigation to thwart legal action, organizational development, & performance & talent management. Polly's well developed communication skills is demonstrated through training & motivating staff, group speaking, and presentation skills.

Polly has substantial interest in senior-level HR Business Partner and HR Consulting roles.
