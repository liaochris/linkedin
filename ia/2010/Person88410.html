James Payton’s Summary

James C. Payton
Cell Phone (740) 972-6854
Jamespayton2009@aol.com

Senior equipment appraiser well versed in equipment and all aspects of risk/asset management. Worked directly with credit and risk management to review and suggest strategies to exit or upgrade loan relationships, including bankruptcy, capital structures, and collateral valuation and liquidation strategies. Provided strategic direction designed to maximize the collection of principal and interest in a timely fashion and minimize loss. Portfolio’s ranged from smaller ($100k) to larger ($70MM), more complex, adversely graded loans. The final results were to minimize the loss exposure in adversely-graded commercial lending relationships, including participation and agent loans, by understanding the collateral, documentation and industry trends. Successfully obtained improvements in the collateral package and corrected or improved documentation or structural weaknesses. Occasionally liquidations had to be organized as a final effort to mitigate loss. Demonstrated expertise includes Medical, Health Care, Oil and Gas, Energy, Construction, Trucks, Trailers, RV’s, and Automobiles. Was instrumental in the asset management department assisting with the bailout of Penn Square Bank in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and orchestrated the sale/liquidation of the assets.
