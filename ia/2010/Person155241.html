Nikon Pascual’s Summary

As the Manager, Agency Recruiter for the Chicagoland Agency, I help people learn more about how a career as a financial sale professional can provide the rewards and opportunities of independence, income, and having a positive impact on people's lives.

As a financial sales professional, you have all the advantages of being in business for yourself, but not by yourself. The Chicagoland Agency will help you develop your business and marketing skills, provide extensive training and coaching, and offer on the job development through joint work with knowledgeable financial professionals to help you launch your career.

Learn more about career opportunities with Prudential at www.prudential.com.sc. And please contact me at (847) 627-3950 ext. 6723 for additional information.

My goal is to build long lasting relationships by creating successful partnerships. I seek to understand people, identify their career goals, and match them with the best opportunity in line with their career objectives.
