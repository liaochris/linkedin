Rowan Malling’s Summary

Core strength is to take a vision or idea and form it into a working production ready application from the ground up. The ability interface between clients and dev teams - add and assist defining strategy and what technology suits, then get it into a completed product. Rare ability to speak to two audiences, both the business audience and the development teams.

14 years of experience in total across all facets of web and software development. The last 7+ years focused on Web Application Development in open source technologies, built on a solid 6 years of experience in QA, Business Analysis and Project Management roles. Proponent of Agile development.

Personally developed/deployed and lead teams that have deployed and deployed multitudes of production web apps for the biggest brands in the world including ABInBev, Dell, Johnson & Johnson, across many varied environments.

Rowan Malling’s Specialties:

Design/architecture development and deployment of community based web sites and functionally complex web sites/applications. System architecture of high availability, replicated systems. Specialist domain knowledge in complex product configuration. 5+ years Ruby on Rails based sites, 6+ years PHP. Flex, AIR and various other technologies.
