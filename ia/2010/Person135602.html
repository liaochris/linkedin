Martin Op 't Land’s Summary

Martin Op ‘t Land is Principal Consultant and Certified Enterprise Architect at Capgemini. He has 25 years experience in Management Consulting and Information Technology, especially in the sectors Financial Services and Public. Mr. Op ‘t Land is specialized in coherently reshaping organization and information of Extended Enterprises, a/o using concepts of Shared Service Centers and Business Process Outsourcing.

On behalf of Capgemini, Martin Op ‘t Land is responsible for education and research in the University-based Master Architecture in the Digital World (ADW), delivered by Radboud University Nijmegen in alliance with Capgemini. He received his MSc degree in Mathematics from Leiden University. As member of the CIAO! Doctoral Consortium he received his PhD in the area of Enterprise Engineering on the subject "Applying Architecture and Ontology to the Splitting and Allying of Enterprises".

Martin Op ‘t Land was chairman of the Working Group Definition Architecture of the Dutch Society for Information Architects (GIA) and is a member of the NAF Working Group Extensible Architecture Framework (xAF). He lectures amongst other the subjects Principles & Architecture Frameworks and Business Architecture (ADW), Extended Enterprise (Master of Informatics, Hogeschool Utrecht) and Design & Engineering Methodology for Organizations (DEMO) (Capgemini Academy).

Martin Op 't Land’s Specialties:

splitting and allying of organizations, methods for splitting and allying of organizations, Shared Service Centers, Business Process Outsourcing
