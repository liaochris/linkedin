Lukas Treu’s Summary

I believe that lapses in communication, whether it be a deficiency of quantity or quality, are the root causes of the majority of this world's problems. Effective communication can be both a catalyst for change and a safeguard against controversy on a corporate, governmental, and personal level. I hope to share this notion with those that I meet, learning and teaching throughout the course of my life.

The notion of learning and sharing is fundamental to my professional aspirations. Whether I find myself spending time in a classroom, at a conference table, or simply engaged in enlightening conversation, I vow never to be educationally stagnant. By continually expanding my knowledge base while maintaining a "servant mindset", I intend to be ready with a smile when I find my calling to make a lasting, positive impact on this world.
