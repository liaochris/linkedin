Laura Wheeler’s Summary

Active since 1999 in developing website solutions for small and micro-businesses. Highly experienced in all areas of website operations and construction for startups and very small businesses. This is our preferred clientele, we are not here because we cannot compete with bigger contracts, we choose to serve the smaller businesses. We build sites, and train in-house personnel to do routine updates, both locally, and through phone and email.

We have formed a trade association, and long term we intend to help in defining microbusiness web services as a separate specialty from enterprise solutions. The goal includes teaching web designers to serve this clientele more completely, educating microbusiness site owners in critical ownership issues, and fighting rampant fraud and deception in this end of the website development and design arena.

microbusiness web solutions, dynamic websites, shopping cart, ecommerce, website sustainability documentation, technical instruction writing for microbusinesses, in-house microbusiness website design training, recession survival consulting, site monetization consulting, search engine optimization, graphic design, copywriting, web marketing, and web business startup consulting
