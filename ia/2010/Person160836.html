Paul Golitz’s Summary

Sales professional with a focus on new business development in emerging markets with industry leading companies. Experiences include direct sales, management, and independent recruiter. Success working within both large organizations as well as entrepreneurial technology start-up ventures.

Over 15 year of successfully building new sales organizations for early stage companies and rebuilding existing sales organizations for more established industry leading companies. Developed a repeatable process to identify, recruit, hire and retain top talent. Achieved above quota success 14 of 17 years in emerging markets and drove 200% growth with four separate companies.
