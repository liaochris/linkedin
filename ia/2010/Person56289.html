Dianne Krause’s Summary

Formerly, I spent ten years not only inspiring students to learn the French language and its structures, but also molding their understanding of the cultures and the people who speak it daily. By speaking French and integrating the latest technology, I aspired to create an educational environment with meaningful learning experiences appropriate for 21st century learners.

Now, ambitious, conscientious and eager for change, I am charged with the mission to educate and coach colleagues in the effective integration of technology. I strive to improve my skills as an educator so that the next generation can thrive in schools that benefit them. Enthusiastic about the future, I aim to connect and collaborate with local and global colleagues to help in the integration of the essential skills necessary to educate today’s children.
