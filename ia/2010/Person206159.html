Tom Koppel’s Summary

• An experienced speaker who gives talks to groups and employees about the physiological effects of food so that people understand the benefits and consequences of their choices and how we can all make better choices. Talks stress that healthy weight loss is a natural side effect of adopting good lifestyle habits and that weight loss comes naturally with improved health.

• Overriding principle in business is to help others who want to help themselves. Proud to work with Pam in helping people change their lifestyles so their bodies don't betray them when they are ready to enjoy what are supposed to be their golden years.

• Business owner for over 30 years and presently Healthy You Program Coordinator. Passionate about helping people live lives of true health. True health is not merely the absence of disease; rather, it is the freedom to live life and do everything we want to do in our lives free of pain and suffering. Would add the right to live free of pain and suffering to the three unalienable rights of life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness Thomas Jefferson laid out in the Declaration of Independence. Another passion is helping people start their own businesses.

Tom Koppel’s Specialties:

• Speaking to groups small and large with passion, excitement, and sincerity.


• Thirty years of hands-on experience owning my own businesses, CPA consulting with small businesses, MBA from the Wharton School have given me a unique ability to help people achieve their goals by starting businesses, especially in a down economy.
