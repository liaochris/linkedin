Jim Custer’s Summary

The ROI Performance Group specializes in the strategic design and development of sales and marketing communication tools, with a niche in high impact methods to engage the specific audience in question. Our philosophy revolves around the creation of media and programs that are meaningful, functional, and aesthetic, while delivering exceptional results to the top and bottom lines.

ROI has worked as a consulting and production firm for a variety of businesses: large and small, public and private, local and international. Our creative team specializes in modern, contemporary designs, and/or can work with an existing brand strategy. Our objective is to deliver media that will stand out from the crowd, be memorable to the end-viewer, while delivering solid results to our client. We take a collaborative approach with our clients, believing a team-effort is most advantageous.

Formed in 1993, Results by Objective Int’l (dba ROI Performance Group) and its associates have decades of experience in developing successful communication methods to achieve the client’s business objective.

Our online communication tools are being utilized by many leading national and international firms, including Intercontinental Hotel Group, Seattle Seahawks, Discover Cards, Nextel and many others.

For additional information on ROI's solutions, visit us at www.roipg.com
