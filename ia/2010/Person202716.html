Sungpa Brad Park’s Summary

Brad, the founder of Thirdwave Soft is a renowned technologist and the entrepreneur of startup company in Silicon valley.

His third startup is focusing on entertainment and advertisement platform on the latest mobile OS, iPhone / Android with his ample experience from the past e-commerce and online game industry.

First, as a principal engineer of premiere shopping site mySimon, he designed and developed multiple award winning technologies that helped to establish mySimon as the leader among the 1st generation of comparison shopping sites. After huge success of acquisition by CNET Networks (now a division of CBS corporation), he worked as the director of the commerce system and integrated multiple e-commerce services into a unified solution.

After CNET, Brad found Hyperpia (renamed Arcadia Entertainment later) and developed the 3D virtual world. His innovations has been embraced by leading online game developers worldwide and its game server engine has been licensed and released in China, Thailand, Taiwan, Japan and USA. As an avid gamer, he has the vision for the future social games with 3D virtual worlds so that he could solely raise the multi-million dollars of investment from venture capital in silicon valley and angel investors. Arcadia launched its own beta virtual world in public on 2009.

Over the past 13 years, he has worked with world class team in all major software disciplines: multimedia communication, ERP/EDMS, intranet, ecommerce, digital content, online games and mobile software. He is particularly creative in founding new businesses that demand strong technology, innovation and creativity.

He holds two patents in software methodology and architecture and is an accomplished writer who has published several books. Brad has a B.S. degree in Computer Engineering from Seoul National University in South Korea. As a social activity, he works for the government as member of National-Unification Advisory Council.
