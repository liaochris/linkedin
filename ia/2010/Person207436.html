Tracey R. Crawley, RN, LMFT, LPC’s Summary

I am a former nurse turned mental health professional. My first love is serving the military, so I am now working for the Navy as a therapist and educator. There is never a dull moment. My passion lies in helping marriages work. My educator duties include domestic violence prevention, anger management, stress management and several couple enrichment programs.

I also volunteer in the faith community leading seminars, retreats, speaking etc. in the area of couple enrichment. I manage a lay group at my chuch called Life Helpers. I do a lot of premarital counseling using rhe Prepare/Enrich Program, for which I am also a trainer. This is a counseling program and assessment tool used for premarital and marital counseling. I hold training seminars regularly in the area. See the website: www.prepare-enrich.com.

I also teach a variety of other marriage education programs to aid in forming good relationships including Couples Communication Program: http://www.couplecommunication.com I am available as a speaker, seminar leader and to conduct marriage retreats. My husband often facilitates with me.

We are very outdoorsie people and love to hike, ski, scuba, bike, etc. We are also fitness freaks, eat healthy and make frequent trips to the gym. As empty nesters, we thought life would go smoothly only to find ourselves wrapped up in elder care with extended family. That seems to be a new ministry opportunity waiting to happen. It is so common our church started a Care Giver Support Group.
