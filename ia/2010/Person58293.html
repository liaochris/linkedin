Donna Jo Merrifield, CRB, GRI’s Summary

Licensed Principal R.E. Broker in Albany & Corvallis, Oregon for leading real estate firm in Linn and Benton Counties, with more listings and sales, and the largest staff of professional agents available to you. Based on our productivity, we're Premier Offices in the Coldwell Banker network.

I am a member of Women's Council of REALTORS, Willamette Association of REALTORS, Oregon Association of REALTORS, Willamette Valley MLS and Regional MLS.
I am a Certified Residential Broker (Manager), a Graduate of the REALTOR Institute, and a past member of REBAC, Real Estate Buyer's Agency Council.

If you are thinking about a career in real estate or are thinking you may want a change of scenery, then email me at ColdwellBanker@ValleyBrokers.com to set up an appointment to discuss the benefits Coldwell Banker and Valley Brokers offers their brokers!

Donna Jo Merrifield, CRB, GRI’s Specialties:

Relocation, buying, selling all types of residential and commercial real estate in the Mid-Willamette Valley in beautiful Oregon. We pay a 25% referral fee!
