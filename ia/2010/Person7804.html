Al Kalbach’s Summary

INSTRUCTOR / CONSULTANT (Safety & Health)
Highly accomplished firefighter, paramedic, safety and health practitioner and trainer with more than 30 years experience in private and public sectors. Extensive consulting and training experience in all phases of safety & health and first responder skills, with particular expertise in the use of simulations in EMS training, law enforcement and wilderness venues. Built a reputation as an expert in safety management; have repeatedly demonstrated the ability to create effective training products and to develop/coordinate innovative programs. Place public access Automated External Defibrillator (AED) programs in many venues across the country. Author/editor for national responder curriculums.
