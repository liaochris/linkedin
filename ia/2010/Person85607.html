Irina Lust’s Summary

Carley & Lust, Attorneys at Law PLLC is a general practice law firm based midtown Manhattan. We provide prompt and cost effective legal services in a variety of matters related to immigration law including naturalization proceedings, family and employment-based green cards, investor visas and non-immigrant visa petitions, throughout New York, New Jersey, the United States and worldwide.

We are also dedicated to helping our clients through bankruptcy (Chapter 7, Chapter 13 and Chapter 11) and various family matters such as divorces, separations, annulments, pre-nuptial agreements, child custody, child visitation and child support.

Finally we fight for the rights of our clients who have been injured in a car or construction accident or fell victims to medical malpractice.

We are admitted to practice law in New York and New Jersey both in state and federal courts. We are affiliated with the New York State Bar Association, the New Jersey State Bar Association, the New York State Trial Lawyers Association, the American Immigration Lawyers Association, and the American Bar Association.

We pride ourselves in offering our clients quality cost effective legal services with focus on personal attention and prompt communication. We are happy to answer questions and concerns at any time throughout the legal process.

Please call or email us for a free consultation.
