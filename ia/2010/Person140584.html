Melinda Whitemarsh, MPA’s Summary

Five years experience in project management, international business development and international relations, working with small to medium size businesses; experience in consulting international businesses on entering the US market. Two years experience promoting international academic programs and linking graduate level education to the professional international and business community.

I worked with the South African Consulate General just over 2-years. I worked on promoting trade and investment with South Africa through business connections, international trade missions as well as information seminars and networking events.

I have my Masters of Public Administration (MPA) with a specialization in International Public Management from Depaul University. My undergraduate focus was political science and international studies. I have studied, travelled and worked abroad extensively.
