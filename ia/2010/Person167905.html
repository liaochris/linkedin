Raina L. Fields’s Summary

In Fall 2009, I began a fully-funded Master of Fine Arts graduate program in English Literature and Creative Writing (Poetry) at Virginia Tech. In this role, I serve as an instructor of College Composition and teach English 1105 and 1106. I also work as a consultant in the university's writing center.

I also serve as a freelance writer for several companies, organizations, and websites. Prior to attending graduate school, I was a full-time professional in the nonprofit arts management sector, specializing in public relations and marketing.

Following graduation in 2012, I plan to pursue a career in academia or the nonprofit sector.

Raina L. Fields’s Specialties:

Writing and editing press releases, media kits, and marketing materials, including copy for print and web advertising and promotional emails; facilitating cooperative organizational partnerships; providing social networking marketing and media; coordinating events.
