Jon Thompson’s Summary

With over 5 years experience in the web, Jon advises clients on web strategy by developing and executing strategic plans and leading analysis projects. Jon's background in web application development has allowed him the ability to advise clients all the way down to the application level. He uses this experience to effectively bridge communication between technical users and business users. Jon has worked extensively in gathering project requirements and constructing project plans. Jon has lead several client projects and guides his strong strategic decision making by utilizing web metrics and industry best practices. Jon has effectively utilized Web analytics in breaking out KPIs and developing actionable insights for clients.

Jon has built online marketing strategies utilizing email marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC or SEM), lead generation, and social media.

As an application developer, Jon worked on delivering complex solutions through architecting and developing web applications. Jon has also has experience in project management and account management.
