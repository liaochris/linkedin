Dawn Harris’s Summary

Registered Yoga Teacher with Yoga Alliance at the E-RYT 200 hour level. My professional yoga training is with YogaFit, Inc. based in Calif. including: YogaFit Levels 1-5, Integration Levels 1-5, Seniors, YogaButt/Core, PreNatal/PostPartum, Anatomy & Alignment, and Restorative. Also many other yoga trainings like Barron Baptiste Power Yoga & SilverSneakers YogaStretch to name a few. Currently focusing on Yoga Therapy and looking forward to a trip to India in the near future to complete my RYT 500 yoga training program.

I've had a personal yoga practice since I was a teenager and have been teaching the joys of yoga to others since 1997. My yoga practice & study has brought so much peace & happiness to my life, I feel so fortunate to be able to share that with others. Teaching weekly yoga classes at our new location, Crescent Yoga Studio located in MidTowne in Midlothian. Also offer group, corporate, & private yoga sessions. Please contact me with any questions. Blessings & Peace be your journey!

Dawn Harris’s Specialties:

I specialize in yoga styles for stress relief focusing on pranayama (breathing exercises) to calm and center the mind, relax the body, and bring harmony & balance back to your being. Including: Yin Yoga, Restorative, Gentle, Low Flow, Seniors Yoga
