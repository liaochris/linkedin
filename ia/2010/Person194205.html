Stephan O. Krause, PhD’s Summary

Dr. Stephan O. Krause has held leading roles for quality and regulatory functions for several biopharmaceutical firms. Currently, he is MedImmune's SME for AMQ/AMV/AMT and global specification coordinator in Gaithersburg, MD. Before, he was the Director of Quality at Mpex Pharmaceuticals, Inc., in San Diego, California. Before Mpex, Stephan was the Director of QC at Favrille, Inc. Before this, he was manager of QA/QC Technical Services at Bayer’s Biologics Division in Berkeley, CA. Stephan was Bayer's SME for analytical validation and transfers and Bayer's compendial liaison. In this function, he impacted industry standards by working closely with other industry leaders.

Stephan has published many articles on Quality in respected industry journals in the US, Europe, and Asia. His bestseller book on risk-based method validation strategies (PDA/DHI Publications, April 2007) won the 2007 PDA Distinguished Author Award. This yearly industry award recognizes significant contributions to improve industry standards.

Stephan often serves as session chair and lecturer at major conferences worldwide. He is a member of several Advisory Boards and is PDA's task force leader for Analytical Method Validation. He is a member of PDA TRI Lecture Staff and the task force leader for PDA's PCMO Initiative (CTM Manufacturing).
