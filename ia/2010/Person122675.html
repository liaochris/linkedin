Laurie Orlov’s Summary

Laurie is an industry thought leader on the technology for aging in place, and the owner of a website and blog: www.ageinplacetech.com to research and write about technology for aging in place, a new market area that will target the 'senior value chain'.

Following more than 30 years in IT and and industry analysis at Forrester, Laurie is a writer, speaker and elder care advocate, and the founder of Aging in Place Technology Watch, a market research firm that provides thought leadership, analysis and guidance about technologies and related services that enable boomers and seniors to remain longer in their home of choice. In addition to her technology background and years as a technology industry analyst, Laurie has a graduate certification in geriatric care management from the University of Florida and is a long-term care ombudsman and the author of When Your Parents Need Elder Care.
