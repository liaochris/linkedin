Dawn Windsor’s Summary

Our goals through the efforts of all of our divisions are to assist other business owners in their start up and or development stages.

Whether you need a Virtual Assistant to outsource your time consuming tasks to or whether you need a website built to help you be competitive in our global online market we have solutions to suit your needs.

In addition; the "Dig Your Heels In" Women Entrepreneurs Virtual Resource Center has now been launched to offer women in business in any sector a 24-7-365 membership opportunity that will allow them to find hundreds and hundreds of business building tools, techniques and strategies all in one remote location - Again, another way we are helping small business grow and provided them with the services they need.
