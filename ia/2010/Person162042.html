Patrick Egan’s Summary

Patrick Egan is the founder and Publisher of CM Crossroads the webs Social Business community dedicated to configuration management and application lifecycle management. With over 20 years experience in both development and product management, Mr. Egan has held technical and executive positions with organizations such as Allstate Insurance, the LEGENT Corporation, SERENA Software, 4th Dimension Software, PLATINUM Technology, Computer Associates, eLabor.com and Catalyst Systems. He received his BS in Mathematics and Computer Science from Loyola University of Chicago.
