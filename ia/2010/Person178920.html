Michael Russell’s Summary

Michael & Tracy Russell have over 10 years of experience helping buyers and sellers. They started their real estate careers in Colorado before moving back to Kansas so their children could be closer to family. They pride themselves on providing 1 on 1 service. They will take the time to find out what their buyers and sellers needs are and focus their time on providing them with the finest customer service possible. Michael & Tracy do over 90% of their business based on referral or repeat clients. If you are looking for the right realtors to send your referrals to, call Michael & Tracy they will make sure that your friends, family and clients will received the attention they deserve

Michael Russell’s Specialties:

The Russell Home Team specializes in Johnson County Kansas. Michael & Tracy believe it is best not to be jack of all trades but instead to be a master of one. The Russell Home Team uses all of the latest technology available when working with buyers and sellers, from custom webpages to email alerts for news properties to online virtual tours & so much more... When looking for a buyers or sellers agent look no further than The Russell Home Team
