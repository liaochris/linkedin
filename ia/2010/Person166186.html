Laura Perez’s Summary

Laura is a film producer and consultant, specializing in multiple forms of Latino media and entertainment. A Juris Doctor degree, a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology with a minor in Communications, and an Associate’s Degree in Theatre are among the academic tools Laura utilizes in the film business.

Laura is a principal at Converge Entertainment, LLC, a film development and production company specializing in Latino entertainment. She manages production of Converge’s projects, ensuring that production and distribution are aligned with the company’s mission and strategic parameters, oversees the company’s internet ventures, and strategically positions the company’s projects in the marketplace for maximum exploitation and profit.

She is currently producing and directing a Converge Entertainment feature documentary entitled “AMERICAN ORANGE” (http://www.americanorange.com), which explores the “mysterious” decline and demise of an impoverished citrus producing Latino border community that coincidentally also housed an Agent Orange producing plant during the Vietnam War.

Laura also co-wrote and produced the feature film entitled HARVEST OF REDEMPTION (http://www.harvestofredemption.com), winner of the “Best Foreign Feature Award” at the 2007 International Family Film Festival and now available at Blockbuster, Target and WalMart. HARVEST features the touching true story of a Latino migrant farm worker family and an unpunished murder that they must not only face, but learn to let go.

Laura is a 5-year member of the National Association of Latino Independent Producers. She has been a screenplay judge for Final Draft's Big Break Script Competition, The American Screenwriter’s Association International Screenplay Competition, Scenarios USA, a consultant and panelist for The CineSol Latin Film Festival, and honored for her contributions as a speaker by The Migrant Students College Assistance Program.
