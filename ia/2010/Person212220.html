Vineet Kapoor’s Summary

A management graduate with specialization in Service Innovation activities with over seven years of experience in the Indian telecommunication industry. He is an exceptional senior IT and Telecom Leader, a commercially driven senior manager & budding researcher, who is visionary and strategic, he also possesses a strong business to business experience in exceeding targets and objectives.

He has in the past initiated and executed “pre-sales” activities for ‘new city launch’, ‘account / revenue management’ and devising ‘go to market’ strategy by proactively defining the approach & plans. He has experience in the area of marketing of IT, technology & solution sales for “key accounts & retail market segments”. He has strong affinity for tasks involving creative problem-solving skills and customer interaction.

His natural talents include leadership, impact & influencing, high doer and enthusiasm. Some of his attributes include highly confident, committed, focused, resilient and relentless. He is an inspirational leader and motivator of people with a healthy competitive nature inspiring and engaging teams to become the best. He aspire to continually develop by possessing the ability to learn at a fast pace to broaden and develop his skills further in other areas.

Vineet is also a Doctoral Candidate at the Management Development Institute, Gurgaon, India in the area of Information Management. His research interests include Global Initiatives of IT firms¸ Innovation and Strategic Management¸ Knowledge Management and Organizational Learning. He has extensively worked on new research, models, and analysis on the emerging mobile & wireless marketplace for growing consumers and enterprise use of mobile technologies.

His PROFESSIONAL GOAL @ MDI was to conduct a mixed method research to arrive at framework outlining the process through which firms develop their core capabilities using Strategy and Innovation for developing innovative services.
