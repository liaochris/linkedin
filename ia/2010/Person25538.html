Bob Linger’s Summary

For nearly three decades I have helped companies build brands and increase sales with traditional marketing and advertising strategies. Today, that often is not enough.

The rapidly changing marketing landscape and business environment lead me to develop my company to assist businesses in selecting the right mix of traditional marketing strategies that still deliver and to ensure their internet properties work 24/7. Business Link Marketing's team of associates will get the most out of traditional marketing tools for your business and link those efforts to appropriate internet marketing strategies designed for the new market conditions. We develop and implement Internet Marketing Plans, including Search Engine Optimization, website design, landing page design, website copywriting, Social Media Marketing and Online Video Marketing.
