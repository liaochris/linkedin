Klaas Kroezen’s Summary

Beside writing the book: 'Review! Het succes van online mond-tot-mond reclame' together, Klaas and Henk Kroezen are the founders of WUA!, the market research company that has been a subsidiary of Effectory since January 1, 2009. WUA! regularly performs their Web Performance Scan to research the online performance of organizations in various commercial industries, and is market leader in the field of web performance research.

With the extensive qualitative report that accompanies the Web Performance Score, organizations get a clear view of what they and their competition are doing wrong, and what they are doing right. Using the handles given to them by WUA!, organizations can begin working on permanent improvements to their website and their online strategy. Since WUA! repeats all studies on a regular basis, these organizations get a clear view of the impact their changes have made.


If you would like to know more:
please visit www.wua.nl or klaas.kroezen@wua.nl
