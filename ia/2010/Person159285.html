Bill Park,O.D.’s Summary

I have been in private practice since 1977. Our goal in our practice is to provide compassionate and comprehensive personalized eye care utilizing the highest technology and continuing education. Our three doctors are licensed to use topical as well as oral medications. We perform well vision and diabetes exams. We treat eye diseases such as bacterial, allergic, viral, and glaucoma using the newest ganglion cell complex OCT High Definition technology to detect glaucoma in very early stages and to provide treatment before vision loss occurs. We have a vast network of specialists that we refer for oculoplastics (example: droopy eyelids), pterygium removal, crossed eyes, bifocal cataract surgery implants, bladeless wavefront LASIK comanagement, and retinal diseases such as diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration. We enjoy fitting difficult to fit contact lenses.

Iport Media- A high tech way to learn about new innovations in the optical industry. A high definition TV is now installed in our waiting to educate our patients of new technology currently being used in our office, new products and medications.

We are now using I Terminal to create a personalized custom pair of no line lenses based on patient's individual frame selection, pupillary distance, tilt of frame on the face,distance from back of lenses to front of the eye, micro etched initials, and wrap angle. The patient can view their selected frames with different types of lens options such as Transitions, Anti-reflective coating on a computer screen.

We are performing school vision exams and have the forms needed by patients to comply with the new state regulations.

Our doctors are certified motor vision specialists for the State of Illinois. We can fill out forms for your driver's license renewal so that you do not have to wait in line for vision screenings at the driver's license station.

We accept VSP, Eyemed, Davis,BCBS PPO, Cigna, Tricare, Aetna, Medicare, and many other health plans.

Bill Park,O.D.’s Specialties:

Our specialties include LASIK comanagement, Cataract comanagement, difficult to fit contact lens cases, specialty eyewear including sports goggles and safety glasses. We have a large magnetic sunglass clip selection.
