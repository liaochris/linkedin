Pepper Makepeace’s Summary

Everything changes, and people who are in the midst of a major life transition see this very clearly. I help people who have found themselves in a life, lifestyle, or mindset that no longer serves them.

I work with individuals who are:

Unhappy at work or feeling "off-purpose" in their career,
Feeling overwhelmed or lacking clear direction in their lives,
Have an idea of their direction and purpose, but aren't sure how to make it a reality, or
Dealing with major changes in their family life (new retirees, empty nesters, newly married, recently divorced, new parents, etc.).

Change is many things: scary, intimidating, disorienting, beautiful, necessary, inevitable. But most importantly, CHANGE IS AN OPPORTUNITY.

I am here to help facilitate your inner process throughout the transition so that you can have a more meaningful, more purposeful, and more empowering experience.

I do not have any answers, magic bullets, or mystical potions to "fix" you. (You're not really broken anyway.) What I do have to offer are powerful coaching tools and resources to help you uncover your own answers, an uncanny ability to hear and see the truth behind your words and actions, and a lot of hard-earned personal experience in major life transitions.
