Leigh George’s Summary

I am passionate about the power of brands to engage and connect with people.

I have nearly a decade of agency and corporate experience creating and managing integrated brands and offer clients extensive expertise in brand positioning, interactive strategy, integrated marketing, web strategy, online marketing and social media.

I have a Ph.D. in branding history and theory from Binghamton University—one of only a handful of people in the country with that distinction. And I love sharing my insights and expertise through presentations, thought leadership pieces, blog posts and tweets, or even better, over a drink.

On January 28, 2010, I organized a webinar panel on integrated social media campaigns that combined a live review of social networks with a twitter hashtag #ISMC that allowed people to interact in real time. The event was sponsored by LMA, AAM and MDSG and drew over 200 participants. One participant described it as the "most effective/useful web event E-V-E-R." Another said, "This has been phenomenal."

I started the Integrated Social Media Campaign (ISMC) group on LinkedIn as a forum to continue the conversation by discussing best practices, posing questions, sharing expertise and connecting.

Together with Jeremy Hoders, I offered recommendations to boost your brand in the January/February 2010 issue of MarkeTrends, the magazine of the Association for Accounting Marketing (AAM).

My insights on social media appeared in a Washington Women article in October 2009, and in July 2009 I shared my analysis of business development trends in the legal industry on Larry Bodine's Law Marketing Portal: http://bit.ly/bizdevasbranding

Leigh George’s Specialties:

Brand strategy, content strategy, IA, interactive strategy, web design and development, integrated marketing plans, online marketing, social media strategy
