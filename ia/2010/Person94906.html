Jennifer Ferrero’s Summary

In my career I have focused on developing websites with a focus on usability and web-based communications. Now, I also work with Shelley Hepler and Debbie Nania to publish the online magazine, Mermaids of the Lake.

Tony DeStefano and I operated Spokane Web Communications for seven years, until selling the company to Greg Green of Tahoe. Tony continues to work full-time in SWC/Tahoe.

At SWC we have developed hundreds of web-based projects, working with all industries and sizes of companies. As a former owner, and consultant to the company, I have always enjoyed the client relationships. Meeting with clients and interviewing them about the business of their business; and obtaining a solid understanding of their client base has always been important to me.

My favorite part of the job is in discovering and implementing client goals. Hearing about new web-based projects, and assimilating the potential needs of the target audience is what I specialize in.

I truly enjoy interviewing and writing stories about people who own businesses why they do what they do.
