Jon Austin’s Summary

We provide clients with senior level counsel and support in the areas of corporate counsel, internal communications, crisis communications, brand management, government/public affairs and labor relations.

Before starting my own company I worked for nearly six years in the Minneapolis office of Fleishman Hillard and prior to that served for nearly ten years as the principal spokesman for Northwest Airlines, the fourth-largest airline in the world.

While at Northwest, I was responsible for the development and execution of all external corporate communications and was the principal communications strategist and spokesperson in all crisis situations involving corporate operations or reputation. I provided direct and indirect supervision to all communications functions with the company, including media relations, internal communications, community affairs, investor relations, government affairs and advertising.

In addition to my experience in the airline industry, I have worked in politics, government and the software industry. Prior to moving to the Twin Cities, I was the director of product development for General Information, a software and data publisher based in the Seattle area, where I was responsible for the company’s software and data development and supervised a staff of 50 programmers, data editors, technical writers, quality assurance engineers, technical support personnel and researchers.

During the 1980s, I started a political consulting and software development firm based in Washington, DC and held a variety of positions in the office of U.S. Senator Thomas F. Eagleton. I worked extensively on a variety of political campaigns at the state and national levels.

Jon Austin’s Specialties:

Crisis communications, media relations, corporate counsel, media training, speechwriting, messaging, issues management.
