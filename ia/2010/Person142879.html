Michael J. Karlin, Esq.’s Summary

I recently passed the CA Bar Exam. I received my Juris Doctorate from Chapman University School of Law. I graduated Cum Laude with an Entertainment Law Emphasis Certificate. Most of my experience in the Entertainment business has been on the independent side of the movie and music industry. Before law school I worked in College Radio as both a DJ and a Music Director. I also played in and managed a nationally touring indie rock band. During that time I also interned at a successful independent record label. During my law school career I worked as a law clerk at a law firm doing general legal work. I also externed, doing legal production work, in a Clinic that provided free legal work to low budget independent filmmakers. Currently I am working as a an attorney at the Karlin Law Firm (main offices in Tustin, with branches in Newport Beach and Los Angeles) focusing on the fields of Entertainment and Business law.
