Alexander von Welczeck’s Summary

Alexander von Welczeck is an innovator and entrepreneur in the CleanTech and Solar Energy industry. As an industry leader he works closely with trade groups & associations, investors, as well as strategic global partners to accelerate the clean power revolution. He has 25 years of experience as a manager with proven success in identifying, building and managing technology-based businesses with investment grade systems. His primary focus has been on infrastructure projects and the commercial building industry.

Current engagements include; Mr. v. Welczeck is a Principal at CLEAN POWER GROUP (CPG) with the mission to develop meaningful World-class PV (photovoltaic) solar energy power plants in the Western USA including; Maricopa Solar Farms and SunRise Solar Farms. Mr. v. Welczeck also is a Principal at CLEAN POWER ADVISORS (CPA) that provides executive advisory services to ground-breaking CleanTech companies including; SOLAR LAND PARTNERS (SLP), CLEAN ENERGY ASSET FINANCE an AdamCapital Company, CLEAN REIT, and SOLAR POWER PARTNERS (SPP). He is a founder of SPP and as CEO from 2007-09 his team completed Nationwide (41) different & top-performing commercial PV solar systems based on PPA energy service financing.

Prior to current engagements, Mr. v. Welczeck served in both domestic and international (EU) management and executive sales & marketing capacities at ThyssenKrupp, DOVER, DORMA, and Friendlyway. He lived and worked in Europe from 1990–2000. He is a University of San Francisco business graduate (1985) and is a member of the Construction Specification Institute (CSI), US Green Building Council, The American Council on Renewable Energy, SolarTech.Org and The CleanTech Circle.
