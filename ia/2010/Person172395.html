Richard Lord’s Summary

Richard has spent the last twenty years involved in various aspects of IT. He started as a developer and progressed to become the IT Manager for the largest independent steel producer for the automotive trade in the UK. He then became the Operations Manager for a company that conducted all supply chain management functions for major UK companies. Richard then become the Test Manager at the European Central Bank in Frankfurt, Germany where he was exposed to the benefits of Automation Testing. For the last nine years he has specialized in QA, for both functional and non-functional testing, of enterprise computer systems for leading businesses in Europe, Scandinavia and Canada. He has expert knowledge of the market leading HP (formerly Mercury) toolset of Loadrunner, Quick Test Pro, Quality Centre and different methodologies which include the V-Model and Agile.
Richard considers both contract and pemanent Senior QA positions.
