Kasper Kristian Koch’s Summary

FP is the leading Danish corporate finance house specialising in strategic advisory on the Nordic markets and offers financial advisory services on acquisitions, divestments, mergers, privatisations and capital market transactions. The position as VP entails generating and executing mandates. In doing this work I participate in the full process from pitch preparation/delivery to deal execution – i.e. process management, client interaction, documentation (IM, MP, DD etc.) and negotiations. As VP I am usually placed at the middle of the deal-team and by now have extensive experience in dealing with clients, managing and collaborating with people below/above me as well as other advisors and deal participants.

I previously worked in the Equity Capital Markets team at Deutsche Bank in London. The ECM team is part of Deutsche Bank's Global Corporate Finance division and works closely together with the M&A team. ECM is focused on equity issuance in the primary as well as the secondary market. Day to day work was split between analysing companies and markets, preparing presentations and executing mandates.

Before joining Deutsche Bank I worked for BankInvest Asset Management in Copenhagen. I worked in the performance and risk measurement team which specialises in reporting to institutional clients and internal consultancy to portfolio managers.
