Tom Anichini’s Summary

I have managed US and global institutional equity portfolios, including long and long-short strategies, since the late 1990s. Previously I spent much of the 1990s conducting due diligence on investment managers of all asset classes and strategies, including private and public alternative investments.

My in-depth knowledge includes such far flung topics as linked performance attribution, optimal manager structure, and building a reverse optimization routine in Excel.

I rather enjoy making presentations to small and large audiences and am happy to engage clients in person.

Tom Anichini’s Specialties:

Quantitative stock selection signal testing, portfolio construction methods, reverse optimization, optimal manager structure, traditional and hedge fund manager selection, Excel dashboards
