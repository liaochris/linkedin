Bruce Reid’s Summary

Bruce Reid, a 19-year Mills James veteran who most recently served as vice president of the company’s Event Technologies division. As vice president of the Cincinnati office, he will head up a staff that provides a full complement of creative media solutions. Reid joined Mills James in 1991 as executive producer of the Film and Video group where he led the company’s creative services unit of writers, producers and directors. In 2000, Reid was named director of event technologies, where he headed the technical support arm for the company’s corporate meetings and special events business. In 2002, he was promoted to vice president. He oversaw the growth of the company’s event staging and audiovisual services businesses, and established the Hospitality AV Services group, which provides on-site audiovisual services at larger hotels and conference centers in Columbus, Dublin, Newark and Wilmington.
During Reid’s leadership, the Event Technologies group purchased its own warehouse and operations center in the Westbelt complex off Roberts Road and I-270, then later expanded the facility with a scene shop and enclosed dock area. Reid has managed all forms of video, film and event production for many major clients, including Chase Bank, Cardinal Health, Ohio State University, NetJets, Executive Jet, Nationwide Insurance, Ethicon Endo-Surgery, Procter & Gamble, Nissan, Volkswagen and Victoria Secret Stores. Reid’s experience in streaming video, Webcasting and satellite videoconferencing technologies has made
him one of the Midwest’s authorities in distance meeting applications for business. Prior to joining Mills James, Reid managed production companies in Kansas City, St. Louis, Baltimore and Phoenix. A native of Central Ohio, Reid earned a bachelor’s degree in broadcast communications from Ohio State University. Bruce has led production workshops and professional seminars around the country and have written articles for industry publications.
