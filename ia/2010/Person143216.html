Mike Brady’s Summary

Seasoned Infrastructure Technology Executive who can build, lead and operate exceptional global organizations that are highly focused on continuous service quality improvements, cost efficiency and customer satisfaction. Results-oriented leader with extensive hands-on experience across a breadth of technologies, recognized as one of the Top 50 Executives by Network World. Unique Data Center and Virtualization strategies that were recently featured in CIO.com reduced business cycle time and operational costs. Organizationally streamlined through selective implementations and renewals of infrastructure outsourcing/out-tasking with individual contract values in excess of $1B, establishing governance functions delivering on long term success.
