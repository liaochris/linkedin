Dripta Roy’s Summary

I am a qualified industrial designer and graphic artist. I have worked in different areas of product design and product promotion: specifically in product conceptualization, modeling, usability, development of prototypes and market communications including product launch marketing and funding procurement. In addition, I have worked with individual clients on creating websites, brochures, videos and profile books.
