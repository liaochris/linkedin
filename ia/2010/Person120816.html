Lana Morrow, Ph.D.’s Summary

Located at 350 Central Park Wet, 1Q in Manhattan; tel. 646. 338.7676; Featured in the Manhattan Guide to Private Schools; rated the best neuropsychologist ICAFS professor, 2008, 2009; 2010; Neuropsychological and Psychoeducational evaluations; remediation and treatment of learning differences, deficits, memory deficits, reading difficulties, organization and executive functioning. Dr. Morrow works with students from numerous private and public schools in Manhattan and is recognized as one of the best psychologists in the sub-field of organization and planning, as well as working memory issues. Her methods are proven effective in treating attention-related issues, reading difficulties, and difficulties with organization of written assignments. She devised a unique system of strategies that helps children and young adults organize and prioritize material in a systematic way, which in turn, enables them to gain fulfillment in academic setting and succeed in school. Her focus is on building students' love of learning and fostering success through appreciation of efforts; the results are remarkable student success stories reported both by the parents and the schools.
