Willa Edgerton-Chisler’s Summary

Over ten years of experience in diversity strategy and leadership development, and recognized for innovative techniques, fostering an inclusive work environment. Provide people inclusion consulting and coaching services to small and large businesses, consulting clients on human capital development and how to attract and retain talent and reduce the “revolving door” syndrome. Coach clients to analyze their business strategies to increase their Return on Investment (ROI) through establishing, cultivating and maintaining positive customer and employee relationships. Develop integrated proven solutions for employees to create inclusiveness, team building, career development, and leadership skills, utilizing their diversityto create successful teams and drive business objectives. Experienced in training, community relations, non-profit and sales .
