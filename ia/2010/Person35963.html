Charlie Hartley’s Summary

Charlie founded Impression DP at the end of 2007. The company started out mianly in Print Management and publishing but has now grown to have a very strong graphic design, brand strategy and web designs departments/services...

He has been very careful to select only the most talented creatives individuals to work on Impression DP's projects for the company's client.

In the first year Impression DP managed a frightening amount of print and since has won printing contracts with Local Authorities, large commercail firms and some of the UK's top design agencies.

Impression DP has a main office in Harrogate and a small office based in London.
