Solofo RAFENO’s Summary

A multicultural Serial Entrepreneur with large corporate management experience
Born and raised in Madagascar, studied in Europe, has worked and conducted business in Europe, the US, Latina America, Africa and China for more than 15 years.

Solofo is the founder and CEO of Beezbox SAS, a leading Entreprise 2.0 software company in Europe.

Beezbox provides Businesses and Organizations operated (SaaS mode) Social Networking services that let them creating greater Value by:
- developing more revenue through 'friends recommendation process'
- reducing customer support operation cost,
- creating always up-to-date customer databases
