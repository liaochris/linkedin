Hakan Akbas’s Summary

Dynamic chief executive, creative dealmaker, visionary strategist and social marketer with a proven track record of start-up, aggressive turnaround and high-growth companies, joint-ventures, mergers & acquisitions, strategic alliances, merger integration, cross-cultural team building and leadership. P&L responsibility for businesses with 5,000 employees, 1 billion in premiums and 2 billion in funds managed. Hands-on experience in taking a Fortune 100 firm from hardware selling to services/solutions selling.

Broad industry and investment experience in high-technology, enterprise software, insurance, diversified industrials, cement, retailing, power generation, renewable energy (solar, wind) and consulting & business process outsourcing services.
