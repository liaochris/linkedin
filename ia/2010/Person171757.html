Rhonda Lowry’s Summary

I am an intrepreneur, focusing on the emerging technologies and markets of social media, with an emphasis on virtual worlds and online communities. I have a passion for participatory media, online communities and transmedia story-telling. My diverse background and experience allows me to move between the technical, strategy and business worlds effortlessly. I'm a requested speaker about social media and making the transition to the new renaissance of digital and participatory media.

My personal mantra is: "vision, values, verve". I've had it posted over my office door/cubicle entrance as long as I can remember as it reinforces simply the things that are most important to me - the ability visualize the end game - the values to lead ethically and with purpose - and the verve to execute with shameless audacity. I've established my self as a collaborator, developer, systems thinker, integrator and executor of new technologies.
