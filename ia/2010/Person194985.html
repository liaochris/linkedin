Stephen Flick’s Summary

Product Director for a publisher of policy and procedure manuals designed to help SMBs develop, implement, and continually improve processes and management systems. We help organizations like yours grow and prosper by developing and implementing Quality Management Systems.

I've managed the design and development process for some of our best selling products, like our Computer & Network, Sales & Marketing, and Finance manuals. In addition, I've had primary responsibility for our internal quality management system, serving as the ISO management representative (i.e., coordinator). Bizmanualz QMS is ISO 9001 certified, by the way.

I've taken an active role in Bizmanualz social media efforts in the last 6 months. Writing articles and blog posts each week on policies and procedures, quality, and related issues. Have primarily responsibility for prepping and blasting weekly e-newsletter.

I track response to our newsletter (i.e., which articles, topics are most important to our readers); use this and other info to write what more of our readers want to see, write "stickier" articles and posts, and promote relationships (as well as, of course, bring companies into the sales pipeline).

Instituted several performance measures for e-newsletter; according to those, email performance is improving and gaining consistency.
