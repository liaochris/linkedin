John Crouthamel’s Summary

Back in 2008, zConnex Group partnered with IVR Technology Group to create a leader in design and execution of IVR and automated telephony programs. The partnership continues today with the addition of SMS/Text and a newly developed suite of application modules.

My role in both companies is to use my years of direct marketing experience and expertise to develop and expand the use of automated "contact" programs for both telephone and SMS text applications. Design, implement and fine tune - a continual 360 approach - to fashion "best in practice" applications.

John Crouthamel’s Specialties:

Managing, building, fine tuning companies, Interactive Voice Response (IVR) application building/implementation, direct marketing, teleservices (20+ years of inbound and outbound service operation, marketing and technology support), new program set up, program fine tuning, collections, quality assurance, corporate management, and new business development.
