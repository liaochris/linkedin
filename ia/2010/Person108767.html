    - Developed $2m of advertising revenue with a 50% margin from a base of no advertising revenue two years beforehand.
    - Changed a loss-making credit reporting business into the market leader, with a $3m t/o and $1m o/p. Achieved through restructuring, automation, new sales practices, new products, increased production, and rebranding/repositioning.
