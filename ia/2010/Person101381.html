Johan Ekelund’s Summary

Johan Ekelund is a producer, composer and entertainment industry entrepreneur who co-founded digital media distributor Klicktrack, independent music publisher Kobalt Music and independent record label Diesel Music. Appointed to the boards of several companies and organizations Johan possesses day to day involvement in all aspects of the global music community. Prior to focusing on business management in creative industries Johan spent 15 years in the studio working as composer and music producer for artists Lisa Nilsson, Anne Linnet, e.s.t - Esbjörn Svensson Trio, Mauro Scocco, Peter Jöback, Fra Lippo Lippi, Stina Nordenstam and others.

Johan Ekelund’s Specialties:

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT AND CORPORATE STRATEGIES IN CREATIVE INDUSTRIES; INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY MANAGEMENT; MUSIC PRODUCTION.
