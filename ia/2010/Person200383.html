    Total Carrier Solutions

    (Telecomunicaciones industry)

    noviembre de 2006 — Present (4 years 2 months)

    Total Carrier Solutions is a strategic advocate or partner for businesses that are looking for Carrier class services such as local dial tone, long distance, internet access, and data connectivity. Total Carrier Solutions can help any business cut through the sales clutter and propaganda and evaluate the real from the sales fluff. Total Carrier Solutions can help any business find the best solutions at the best prices from the best of breed carriers. Total Carrier Solutions works with all of the telecommunications carriers so your business does not have to waste time and effort sifting through all the myriad carrier offerings, proposals, sales materials, marketing materials, etc. Total Carrier Solutions will cut through the clutter and make a recommendation based on facts, best price, service quality, reliability, etc. Total Carrier Solutions works with the wholesale side of the carriers or sometimes called the alternate channel. Total Carrier Solutions brings businesses any carrier's best offer and best price and the business signs contracts directly with the carrier of their choice. The main difference between what Total Carrier Solutions does and going to the carrier directly is the contracts are generated from the wholesale side of the carrier not the retail side. The pricing on the wholesale side is usually much lower than the retail pricing and the savings are passed on to the clients. In essence each carrier competes with itself for business in the open marketplace and as a business I would much rather pay wholesale than retail. If you have services that are not under contract or are coming up for renewal please call or e-mail us today for your free no obligation meeting to determine which carrier can best meet your needs.
