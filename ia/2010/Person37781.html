Chris Gerardi’s Summary

My partner (Carol Phillips) and I own a local Chicago metro branch office for Home Helpers & Direct Link. We operate in Bartlett, Bloomingdale, Carol Stream, Hanover Park, Warrenville, Wheaton, Winfield, West Chicago, northern Naperville & northeast Aurora.

At Home Helpers, we are committed to Making Life Easier for our clients by providing them with superior, non-medical, personal care. We service seniors, new and working parents, those recuperating from an injury or illness, and anyone with an ongoing challenge.

Our promise is to care for each client as if we are caring for a loved one in our own family. We recognize our clients are the foundation of our business and we continually nurture and grow this relationship. We value your trust in us and will give you peace of mind knowing your loved one is happy, healthy and well cared for.

Home Helpers' personal care services are tailored to meet the needs of each and every individually unique client. Selection of services and flexible scheduling enable Home Helpers to develop a custom care plan for each client that can be adjusted as clients functional needs change.

Direct Link In-Home Emergency Monitoring enables you to feel safer and live more independently in the comfort and privacy of your home longer than might otherwise be possible.

If you need help, day or night, simply press the small, wireless button worn as a pendant, wristband or belt clip and help is sent immediately. Feel secure knowing you have Direct Link and help is as close as the touch of a button.
