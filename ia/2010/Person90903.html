Jason Roberts’s Summary

Owner of Charge Communications, an integrated brand, marketing and production house especially focusing on e-media solutions. We offer a full suite of email marketing products including the newly developed e-marketing product called LiveLinks. At the time of writing, we are about to launch the Enterprise version. The consumer version is already up and away.
In a nutshell, LiveLinks makes it easy for any email user to easily pick and send multi-media marketing collateral directly out of their 1 to 1 email client and then know in real time if their email has been opened. The outcomes include improved channel communications and the effective delivery of collateral, compressing sales cycles (and value), plus increasing customer loyalty.
Other;
Consulting and contract roles in the IT communications, healthcare and pharmaceutical sectors, along with consumer retail marketing.
Private business interests have included sole trader, partnership and directorships.

I'm a ‘people person’ with a passion for new ideas and innovation that solve business problems.
