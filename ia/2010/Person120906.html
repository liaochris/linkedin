Lance Westbrook’s Summary

Business Development, OEM Sales, Corporate Development and Channels executive with broad Enterprise Applications, SaaS, Storage, Database, BI and Integration experience. Managed field engagement with major ISV and SI partners and built indirect revenue streams with SI, VAR, OEMs and Distributors. Developed award-winning partner programs, recruited strategic partner relatiosnhips and built successful global sales teams. Produced outstanding results managing in large organizations such as Sybase, Cognos and Avnet; and early-stage companies like Network-1, Applimation and Docent through acquisitions, mergers, spin-offs and IPOs.

Lance Westbrook’s Specialties:

Penetrating new markets, buidling incremental revenue streams, developing partner programs, understanding the technology, explaining the market dynamics, articulating the business value, negotiating the deal, getting the contract done, creating M&A opportunities, recruiting target partners and managing complex relationships.
