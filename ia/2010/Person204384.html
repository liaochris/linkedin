  • Account Executive

    GPI Prototype

    (Privately Held; Mechanical or Industrial Engineering industry)

    September 2009 — Present (6 months)

    GPI Prototype is a premiere service provider of Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS), the latest technology in rapid prototype and manufacturing services from EOS GmbH. As one of the first DMLS service providers in the country, GPI produces metal parts for applications ranging from prototypes to series products and production tools. DMLS technology is ideal for a variety of applications including the creation of conformal cooling channels within tools and tooling inserts, as well as customized medical implants.

    GPI Prototype uses a variety of cutting-edge technologies to produce unmachineable parts in a wide range of materials. Using some of the latest machines available from EOS and Objet, we have the capability to produce complex prototypes with unparalleled quality.

    Additionally, we also continue to provide legacy techniques such as RTV Tooling & Urethane Casting to produce quick parts for short-runs that do not qualify for production status. GPI maintains relationships with a number of strategic partners to enable the complete service and delivery of all your prototyping needs.

    GPI Prototype also can provide you with traditional prototype needs, for instance; SLA, SLS, FDM and CNC. Beside offering prototypes GPI can help you with finishing of your product along with the manufacturing stages.

    Visit http://gpiprototype.com for more info.
