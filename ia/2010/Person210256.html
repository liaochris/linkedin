Valerie Romanoff- Starlight Orchestras’s Summary

Why do clients like Michael Douglas, Catherine Zeta-Jones, Clive Davis, The Dallas Cowboys, The President of The United States, NASCAR, and The Sopranos, call Starlight? Starlight Orchestras and Productions is widely recognized as one of the premier live music companies in the country. Based in New York, the four Starlight Orchestras are known for superstar-quality performers, uniquely polished presentation, and the most varied repertoire imaginable. Each bandleader excels in customizing every performance, reading the crowd and delivering a nonstop high energy event. The company's flagship ensemble, "The Starlight Experience" is a valuable alternative to celebrity headline entertainment, providing a musical extravaganza in a dazzling presentation. Whether performing at the Presidential Inaugural Ball ,The Kentucky Derby, or Donald Trump's Mar-a-Lago, Starlight's level of entertainment is without compare.
