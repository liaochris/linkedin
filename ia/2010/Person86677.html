Jackie Ngai’s Summary

Jackie has successfully launched specialty products in multiple therapeutic categories and brings with her diverse perspectives and experiences. She enjoys project management, creative opportunities and strategic thinking. Prior to joining the RADical Group, Jackie was an Account Executive/Internal Communication Manager at a boutique public relations firm. There, she led multiple accounts, spearheaded the social media division of the company and served as a copy writer and proofreader, as well as a media specialist. Jackie received her B.A. in Communication from Rutgers University and her M.S. in Corporate Public Relations from Boston University. Jackie has also completed the London Graduate Mass Communication Program, where she was able to spend a summer studying global marketing communication and mass media while participating in a full-time internship at Brunel University.
