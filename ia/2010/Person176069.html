Rob Wiener Rwiener@lucasgroup.com’s Summary

For Rob, the key to building a successful Search and Recruitment Practice is to continuously develop and nurture long term relationships. Delivering results and performance driven searches, Rob prides himself on the ability to build partnerships that yield high levels of success.

Rob Wiener is currently the Managing Partner for the LucasGroup Los Angeles Executive Search Practice. Under his umbrella, he manages Executive Search for Accounting & Finance, Real Estate, Human Resources, On-Line Media & Insurance. Rob brings over a decade of both Accounting & Finance and Executive Search experience to the table.

Rob worked in the Fortune 100 as a Finance Manager both in Manufacturing and Distribution both overseas in Europe as well as in the U.S. His responsibilities included Management Reporting, Financial Systems, Public Accounting and in depth Business Analysis. As a Search professional, his experience covers CFO’s, Controllers, VP’s of Finance as well as Asset Managers, Portfolio Managers and Acquisitions professionals in the Real Estate Arena.

Rob joined the LucasGroup after many successful years as a top performer with Parker & Lynch in San Francisco. He has conducted searches on the West Coast as well as throughout the entire U.S. With his deep expertise in the Fortune 500 world of Accounting & Finance professionals, he continues to support the growth and development of his clientele’s staff with results driven searches
