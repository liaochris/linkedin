Scott Hyman’s Summary

I am Vice President of Development at Creat Studios in the greater Boston area. Creat Studios is a video game development studio and online publisher with titles released for Sony PSP and PlayStation 2 and 3, Microsoft Xbox and Nintendo DS. We published nine titles for PlayStation Network in 2009, three in Q1 of 2010, and have emerged as the top independent developer and publisher on PSN.

Previously, I was a Producer for Vivendi Games Mobile, the mobile start-up division of Vivendi Games, publisher of IP such as Crash Bandicoot, Spyro the Dragon and Starcraft 2 and World of Warcraft. Duties were focused on the emerging opportunities of entertainment applications in the mobile space.

Prior to completing my MBA at Pepperdine University, I created art and animation for video game properties including Star Wars, Jurassic Park and Sesame Street, specializing in the creation, development and animation of 3D characters for over a decade.

Scott Hyman’s Specialties:

Media business development, digital download, console downloadable games, mobile games, character animation, video game art, technology start-ups
