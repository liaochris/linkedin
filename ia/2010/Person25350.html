Bob Cramer’s Summary

Bob is a CEO, private investor, board member and advisor with 26 years experience in high-growth venture-backed businesses.

Recently, Bob was CEO of LiveVault, one of the first software-as-a-service businesses targeting the SMB market, which was acquired in 2005. Bob was also CEO of FirstSense, which was acquired in 1999. Cramer has also held senior sales and marketing positions at Oracle, CenterLine and Ingres.

He received his BS from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and his MS from the University of California at Berkeley.
