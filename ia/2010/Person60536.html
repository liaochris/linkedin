Duane Morin’s Summary

My background in sociology as well as computer science ends up placing me at the front lines between technology and the end users. Whether it's customer relationship management, R&D, marketing, or even tools development, I'm usually most interested in the impact of my work in the world, and not just whether my algorithm was the most optimal in some hypothetical situation. I don't just like writing code, I like thinking about it, writing about it, presenting it and evangelizing it. I can hack bytes and debug like a fiend when I need to, but I'm just as comfortable sitting down with customers or on technical architecture committees to look at the big picture. Solutions to problems come in many shapes and sizes, after all.

Duane Morin’s Specialties:

I've worked in financial services, medical devices, the travel industry, education and computer maintenance. I learn new domains and technologies very quickly.
