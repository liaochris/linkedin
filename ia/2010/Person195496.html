Steven Forth’s Summary

CEO of LeveragePoint Innovations Inc. Our goal at LeveragePoint is to help B2B companies provide value to their customers. We do this by providing online tools for customer value management. Our tools support the full cycle of creating, communicating and capturing value based on a common understanding of what value is for a specific customer compared to an alternative. Our work extends Tom Nagle and John Hogan's ground breaking work on value pricing to the product development, marketing and sales functions. Integrating and aligning around value allows companies to make better decisions faster.

Steven Forth’s Specialties:

Merging business architectures with technical architectures and communities.
Customer value management including value-based pricing and value-based sales.
Learning, knowledge management and performance support.
Semanitc web and web services. Social software. Software internationalization and localization. Standards and standards development such as SCORM. Semantic web and Semantic MediaWiki.

  • CEO

    LeveragePoint Innovations Inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    LeveragePoint is a SaaS (Software as a Service) company spun out of Monitor Group in January 2009. Our mission is to help companies deliver value to their customers. In an age of Customer Capitalism, the value a company provides to its customers is the most important thing to manage. It is only by providing differentiated value to customers that value will be created for shareholders.

    LeveragePoint provides the essential on-line tools that B2B companies use to understand how they provide value to their customers. This understanding is used in the creation, communication and capture of value and results in higher prices and faster sales.

    We offer a suite of online tools that cover the full customer value management cycle. These tools combine with data search & integration and with collaboration & learning to activate and embed customer value management in an organization.

    As CEO my role is to build a superior team that can execute our own strategy while living into our values of constant learning, diversity, transparency and accountability.
