Steven Sinclair’s Summary

Objective

A position with a quality exploration and production company that will utilize my 25+ years of experience in the petroleum and energy industries.

Petroleum Landman – Worked in multiple production basins from in-house land offices for companies in Oklahoma City and Dallas. Duties included acreage acquisition, title curative, settling well site location damages, pooling hearings, negotiating and drafting trade agreements including farm-in and farm-out agreements, joint operating agreements, area of mutual interest agreements, and exploration agreements. Worked closely with exploration teams made up of geologists and geophysicists on prospect generation and development. Tested and passed the AAPL’s CPL examination.

Natural Gas Marketer and Supply Representative – Traded daily equity and off-system gas to Mid-Continent spot markets for pipeline marketing affiliate. Primary gas supply representative for Tulsa, OK based electric utility purchasing long-term, seasonal, monthly and daily supply to run load following gas- fired generation units. Negotiated supply contracts with producers and marketing companies, negotiated transportation agreements with gas pipelines, and interfaced with the state regulatory staff on business practices and processes.
