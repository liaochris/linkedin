Carey Hepler’s Summary

Hepler Bros. LLC is a franchise of Fibrenew, International, and we have the exclusive rights to the Jackonville, Florida metropolitan area.

Fibrenew specializes in the restoration of leather and plastics, servicing five major markets: Aviation, Automotive, Commercial, Marine, and Residential.

We are a home-based mobile service franchise, catering to an executive clientele in the above mentioned markets.

Fibrenew is a global leader in the environmental movement. We prevent thousands of tons of leather and plastics from entering our landfills.
