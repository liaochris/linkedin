Harry Sherman’s Summary

Harry Sherman is a corporate transactional attorney with over 10 years of professional experience gained at premier "Wall Street" firms and as in-house counsel. His areas of expertise include mergers and acquisitions, investment funds, capital markets, commercial real estate and private equity transactions. Harry specializes in international transactions, with extensive experience in Latin America and European markets. He is a native Spanish speaker, with proficiency in Portuguese.

Harry enjoys providing business and legal counsel to financial institutions, investment funds and corporate entities, where sound judgment, a strong work ethic and integrity are valued.
