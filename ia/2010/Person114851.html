Kent Lawson - Private WiFi’s Summary

I formed Private Communications Corporation, after reading several articles in the Wall Street Journal and elsewhere, discussing the vulnerability of internet communication in public wifi hotspots to being intercepted by hackers. It seemed to me to be a very serious problem, but with a simple solution. We developed Private WiFi, which encrypts all traffic on public networks (wired or wireless), preventing your sensitive communication from being overheard by others.

I had run my prior business, Magna Software, from 1977 to 1997, when I retired. Magna was responsible for several successful software products, which are still in use around the world.

I was enjoying a very active and rewarding life in retirement. At first, I tried volunteering, including tutoring kids in Harlem. But, I found myself drawn more toward the organizational aspects of non-profits, and have found great pleasure in serving on several Boards, including as President of two and treasurer of a third. I also had time to pursue other interests, such as travel, theater music and – to my surprise – cooking.

It was definitely not my plan to start another business, but the more I looked into the need for Private WiFi the more compelling the idea seemed to be. Fortunately, I am having a ball doing it. Through some of my old Magna contacts, I have pulled together a great staff, we have built a solid foundation and I am sure we will be very successful.

Kent Lawson - Private WiFi’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    Private Communications Corp.

    (Computer Software industry)

    February 2010 — Present (11 months)

    Private Communications Corporation markets Private WiFi, which protects your identity and secures your sensitive information when using public networks, such as wifi hotspots. Private WiFi works by encrypting all the data going into and out of your computer, making you invisible to hackers.

    Private WiFi is a subscription-based Virtual Private Network (VPN) that works just like the anti-virus software you probably already have. Every time you connect to the Internet, Private WiFi activates invisibly in the background, creating an encrypted pathway to our server in seconds. In addition to protecting your data, this secure connection also masks your I.P. address, giving you an added level of privacy.

    Private WiFi uses industry standard 128-bit encryption. It’s the same technology used by your bank or your credit card company, but we use it to secure everything you send and receive, including all website traffic, emails, attachments, and IMs.

    See PrivateWiFi.com
