Cymantha Higgins, PMP’s Summary

Your company will gain:
• On-target business project management – a structured, realistic sense of how to get the right inputs and outputs, with or without formal PM structure.
• Business sense for compliance – well practiced sense of what compliance and regulation mean, how companies make business decisions and how to get operational groups to keep producing while they come into compliance.
• Compliance expertise on IT projects – the ability to blend compliance and procedural requirements into processes and system functionality that people know how to use.
