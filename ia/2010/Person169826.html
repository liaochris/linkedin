Richard Benyon’s Summary

CEO at Decideware, Inc. the North American arm of Decideware Development, an organisation specialising in 'on-demand' marketing procurement and supplier relationship management applications.

We licence Marketing Agency Evaluation, Marketing Procurement/ Finance and Strategic Supplier Relationship Management software to tier one companies using the SaaS model. Our client base includes Procter & Gamble, Pfizer, Burger King, Walmart, Dell & Cadbury.

I am a long time application & database developer and have hands on experience in building enterprise applications, as well as evangelizing the products themselves.
