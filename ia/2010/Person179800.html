Ryan Andrew Pallathra’s Summary

As a recent graduate from Tufts University, with a B.A. in International Relations, my main career goals are to work professionally in the fields of international law and diplomacy, and international politics.

Having worked in law firms, the British Parliament and the U.S. Department of State, I would like to continue building upon my experiences both domestically and abroad so that I may achieve a greater understanding of international affairs and directly play a role in American politics and diplomacy.
