Luca Minudel’s Summary

Extreme Programmer and Lean-Agile Coach , experienced in team and coding practices with Scrum, XP and Lean-Agile. Has designed and developed enterprise level applications and worked with large code-bases and legacy code-bases. Has a long experience with Microsoft technologies and Microsoft .NET.

Typical duties include: facilitate the adoption of Agile and the advancement of agile practices within the organization, small teams start-up, development environment definition (practices, tools, guidelines, standards), delivery of high quality software on time - support and consulting on challenging software projects, requirement analysis, technical infrastructure selection, architecture modelling and effort estimation - support and consulting throughout technological transitions, new technologies exploration, strategic technologies evaluation, technology selection and people training.

Main aims are : expand experiences and improve skills on Lean-Agile coaching and software development; improve soft skills , team building abilities and leadership with self-organizing teams; keep in touch with emerging technologies.

Luca Minudel’s Specialties:

Scrum Master trained by Joseph Pelrine Europe's leading expert. Holds advanced ScrumMaster certification (CSM, CSP). Experienced in TDD and refactoring of legacy code-base.
