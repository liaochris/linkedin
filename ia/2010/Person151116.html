Namita Gyanchandani’s Summary

Having undergone 6 years of formal training and education as a physical and infrastructure planner, some of the key skills I have picked up through my academic career are organization, strategy building, and problem solving. Following my heart and making use of these acquired skills, I chose a career in a field that deeply interested me – travel.

I have been working with PhoCusWright Inc., a global travel research firm, for more than a year and half, gaining experience in the travel markets of the Asia Pacific region. This helped me develop substantial domain knowledge in a short period of time and eventually led to my co-authoring the global edition report "PhoCusWright's Asia Pacific Online Travel Overview". Besides this, I have contributed to the marketing of our research reports by writing marketing copies, blogs, tweets and other articles.

I was excited to be a part of travel conferences across the globe such as ‘Travolution’ and ‘Webintravel’ which provided professional exposure. I also got an opportunity to work out of the U.S. headquarters of the company and interact with colleagues globally, which, helped in strengthening my communication skills to a great extent.

Through the experience I gained working on the development of PhoCusWright's Learning Solutions, I also acquired skills in project management and product development.

Apart from travel research I am also interested in travel and social media. I am keen on exploring the travel industry further, and am looking forward to learn more.
