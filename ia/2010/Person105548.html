Sunil Joseph’s Summary

Sunil is a project manager with over eleven years experience in the pharmaceutical / healthcare IT industry. He has assumed roles of increasing IT management responsibility including serving as functional and technical manager. Most recently, he was the IT Project Manager at Alkermes, Inc. where he was responsible for working with medical affairs, drug safety, clinical development, and R&D stakeholders in supporting current applications and developing a long-term application portfolio strategy.

Sunil Joseph’s Specialties:

IT Project Management; Integrations management; Datawarehouse projects; EMR/healthcare IT project management; IT Governance (PMO); IT compliance with Sarbanes Oxley (SOX) /General IT Controls, HIPAA compliance, and Systems validation including FDA / regulated systems
