Gene Nokes’s Summary

I have always worked in an environment that requires high performance and in that environment I have seen many very fine people lose their jobs, many times through no fault of their own. My experience has given me compassion for those that have lost their jobs. In reaching out to help them, I was led into lay ministry and I founded, with others, several job support groups called Joblink.

I am a Certified Career Coach with The Five O’Clock Club. My private career coaching practice is focused on lay ministry to Christian Churches in Northern California. My specialty is coaching individuals employed in the High Tech industry in Northern California’s Silicon Valley.

I am a skilled sales professional with deep experience with RFID, Barcode and sensor based solutions.
Channel Development and Management experience with Value Added Resellers, OEM’s, Distributors and Dealers.
Product management of hardware and software subsystems using RFID, Bar code and PDA’s.
Program management for direct and indirect channels leveraging application software to market hardware subsystems.
A Barcode and RFID and data collection expert with a diverse background in industrial applications. A generalist in Marketing and Sales focused on success formulas with a track record of industry firsts.
