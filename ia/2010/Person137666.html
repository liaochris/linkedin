Matthew Leach’s Summary

As a business analysis and requirements consultant I work with my clients to successfully incorporate technology into their organization providing solutions to their business problems. My expertise is in the delivery of business analysis and requirements services on critical client projects and the development of highly effective business analysis and requirements organizations.

Throughout my education and career, technology and its application to real world problems has always been a focus. I find nothing is more rewarding then architecting a solution to a significant business problem.

Being a consultant with a technology background I am in a position to bring my business acumen and technical expertise to bear on projects serving as a bridge between the two capabilities. I have worked with companies of all sizes in industries such as: financial services, life sciences, high tech, software, and defense/aerospace to align technical solutions to their business needs. In the past my project roles have included:

• Development requirements and architecture solutions
• Definition of enterprise architecture and future visions
• Building business analysis competency centers/centers of excellence
• Leading and mentoring business analysis and requirements efforts

A committed and passionate business analyst I not only work in the trenches, but also speak/write on the topic and am dedicated to the growth of the profession. I am always seeking my next challenge, opportunities to learn new skills, and ways to grow my career.
