Elena Verlee | Public Relations’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur who currently runs two companies in public relations - a full service International PR agency for technology clients, and a Do-It-Yourself PR and Social Media company for entrepreneurs, coaches, authors and smaller businesses wanting publicity. I also have a coaching company where I help entrepreneurs build a successful business without sacrificing their personal life. All websites can be accessed at www.ElenaVerlee.com

PR for Technology Companies:

I help technology companies with key messages they need to communicate to media, customers, investors and other key PR audiences. My PR consulting team and I develop full public relations plans, media lists, conduct press and analyst tours, write press releases and contributed articles, place executives in industry speaking opportunities and industry awards applications. We specialize in Publicity Consulting - getting covered by analysts, media and bloggers. Clients have included innovative start-ups, as well as billion dollar software companies. Our website is www.CrossBorderPR.com

PR and Social Media for Entrepreneurs and Small Biz:

A few years ago, I co-founded a quirky company selling men's socks over the Internet. We generated so much PR that we attracted a buyer for the company for six-figures in less than a year. I believe anyone, and ANY business can benefit from PR. So, I've written an ebook teaching consultants, authors, coaches, moms and other small business owners how to do your own PR in just a couple of hours. Through inexpensive ebooks, teleclasses and a ton of free resources, I teach practical publicity tactics that get results at www.PRinYourPajamas.com

Business and Life Coaching for Entrepreneurs:

I am a certified professional coach and I love working with clients to build businesses and marketing systems they are passionate about, without sacrificing personal relationships and balance.

To consult about PR or coaching, visit www.ElenaVerlee.com
