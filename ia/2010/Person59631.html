Dr. Erica Goodstone’s Summary

As a baby boomer, my life's work is healing through love, creating love and creating healing. I have been a psychotherapist, marriage counselor, mind-body therapist and somatic body psychotherapist using the Rubenfeld Synergy Method of talk and touch. I specialize in healing love relationships, marriages, sexuality, and the mind, body and spirit connection.

The Rubenfeld Synergy Method is a gentle yet powerful style of somatic body psychotherapy combines talk with touch to elicit habitual tension patterns, memories, emotions and help to create new and currently working pathways in the brain.

For many years as a Professor of Health and Physical Education,
I taught wellness, holistic health, fitness, stress management and human sexuality, yoga and meditation, as well as such traditional sports activities as tennis, volleyball, slimnastics, body conditioning, gymnastics and even dance.

My book, Love Me Touch Me, Heal Me: The Path to Physical, Emotional, Sexual and Spiritual Reawakening is a powerful guide to healing all these imporant parts of your life. There are simple self-reflection, guided imagery, writing, meditative and partner exercises.

I am a Platinum EzineArticles.com Author, Miami Relationships Examiner and West Palm Beach Healing Examiner for Examiner.com, and have articles and blogs posted on many online sites and offline newsletters and journals.

Dr. Erica Goodstone’s Specialties:

For healthy baby boomers and everyone else, my counseling specialties are love, healing, relationships, sexuality, marriage, sexual dysfunctions, sexual addictions and compulsive sexual behaviors, body image concerns, anxiety and depression, anger management, stress reduction, wellness promotion, anti-aging, and creating the life and relationships you truly desire.
