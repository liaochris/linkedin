Steve Herrera, CLU, FFSI’s Summary

I specialize in helping Agents, Agencies, Registered Rep's, and Independent Broker Dealers with maximizing the revenue from their Life Insurance, Annuity, and Long Term Care Insurance businesses. I work with over 30 Life Insurance, Annuity, and Long Term Care Insurance companies, and I’m confident we can find a product and underwriting program to meet your client’s needs. I have a team of people that support my agents with Carrier Licensing and Contracting, Application Processing, and Commission Payouts after the Life Insurance, Annuity, and/or LTCI policies are placed. And these services come at $0.00 cost to the agents that are affiliated with me.

I have the Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU) and Fellow Financial Services Institute (FFSI) designations. I help the agents affiliated with me with wealth accumulation, asset protection, and wealth transfer concepts that meet their clients needs.
