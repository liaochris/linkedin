Janet Aronica’s Summary

I am a writer.

When I was a kid, if I got bored I'd write stories with colored pencil and construction paper. Then I probably dressed up in my mom's clothes and made everyone watch me do a little performance of the story.

I don't perform my press releases - although, I still play dress up. And still, I maintain a passion for finding just the right words to craft just the right message and create just the right results for my clients.

I'm a social media hippie and I like it that way. I truly, deeply, whole-heartedly believe that what is happening right now in social media marks a historic, revolutionary shift in how humans communicate. I am an enthusiastic evangelist of what it can do for brands. This means that I take blogging seriously. Also, I like to contemplate the geeky things in life - like how to measure the ROI of a Twitter account. I like to find new ways to engage empowered consumers and to constantly improve my work as a communications professional. I am @janetaronica on Twitter - connect with me there! I also love getting comments on my blog, Social Health Nut - so stop by and say hello. --> www.socialhealthnut.com.

Fittingly, I work as an Account Coordinator at Kel & Partners, a web 2.0 marketing and public relations agency. I wear jeans on Tuesdays, there's a keg in my office, and I get paid to strategize about Twitter and find blogs that might care to write about one of my awesome clients. Yes. It is that great.

I'm always on the lookout for freelance writing and social media consulting opportunities. Know of one? Email me at janetaronica@gmail.com.
