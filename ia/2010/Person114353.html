Kendra Wilburn’s Summary

Kendra Wilburn is a Certified Executive Coach and Leadership Management specialist who works with executives to make the most of their career while increasing the ROI not only for themselves but for their employees as well.

In addition, Kendra works with companies who want to get the most out of their organization through employee and leadership development.

Kendra has over 13 years of industry experience where she has gained an appreciation for the importance of being in a fulfilling and rewarding role and enabling her staff to do the same.

Kendra is passionate that people should be the best that they can be, in whatever field they choose. She has an unwavering conviction that we all can change what we're not happy with and imparts this passion in her coaching sessions with 100% commitment and dedication to the client.

Kendra Wilburn’s Specialties:

Executive Coaching, Employee Development, Leadership Development, Public and Motivational Speaking, Interview Preparation, Professional Networking, Resume Writing, Cover Letters, Professional Development, DiSC Profile Assessment, and Personal Communications Styles Inventory.
