Lisa Tener-Book Writing Coach’s Summary

I help people write their nonfiction and how-to books and get published.

Join my VIP list and you'll receive free bi-weekly writing and publishing tips to help you write your book and get published: http://www.lisatener.com/newsletter

I teach book writing and publishing subjects on the faculty of Harvard Medical School's CME publishing course. I also offer an intimate, private book-writing course. You can watch a video about it here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=haRcPERZc6o

Who do I help? Some of my clients who recently became published authors or just signed publishing contracts include therapists, doctors and entrepreneurs. I love helping authors in the self-growth / human potential market.

Some of my favorite projects are helping people get clear what book to write and truly clarify the whole concept of their book, helping people write a book proposal to help them get an agent and publisher. As a writing coach, I also help people with finding time to write, breaking through writer's block and other writing challenges. And I can help you navigate the publishing world.

One of my clients said I can "see a diamond in the rough." I pride myself on seeing a person's potential and helping those who don't necessarily know how to write (or feel comfortable) and teaching them to become a skilled writer.

Writing a book is an amazing experience. It changes your world, your reach, your impact, your lifestyle. It's an expansive process--and it's so much fun to see someone grow and become a published author, bringing their books and dreams to life.
