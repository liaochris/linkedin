Ron Kaniuk’s Summary

I am a business lawyer and real estate attorney, helping clients in a wide-variety of matters in the areas of bankruptcy and foreclosure defense, as well as corporate, commercial and real estate law.

I am licensed to practice law in Florida, New York and New Jersey, and am admitted to practice before the U.S. District Courts for the Eastern, Northern, Southern and Western Districts of New York, the District of New Jersey & the Southern and Middle Districts of Florida.
