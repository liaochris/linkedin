Teppo Kotirinta’s Summary

Teppo is one of the founders and chairman of Nordkapp. His job title is Principal, Interaction Design. He has been building things with computers since he was 13 (1991), and has since seen the rise of the BBSs, Demoscene, PC, Internet, WWW, mobile phones and digital cameras and social media. He is currently excitedly waiting and dreaming up the Internet of Things and Ubicomp.

During his last 19 computerized years, he’s been a webmaster in a national energy company (1997), worked in a startup and in a legendary digimedia company, designed and implemented a mobile power plant maintenance system, joystick controlled DJ software and a global touchscreen media management and distribution systems and countless other sites, services, user interfaces and systems.

He has graduated as a BA of Media Engineering (2002) and MA in New Media (TAIK Medialab 2005), both with very high grades. He also studied abroad in the Shanghai University of Technology in China and in The National School of Design in Melbourne, Australia. In effect, he’s an engineer by body and designer by soul.

Some work highlights are winning a best of the year digital / social marketing award already around 2005 and Emma, the Finnish Grammy, in 2008 for work in advancing digital music. There are some patent pending innovations, too. Nordkapp is already a second design company he has founded with his friends.

On his free time Teppo creates electronic music, still DJ's occasionally, lifts weights and develops his photography skills. Recent addition to his family is the cutest baby girl in the world.
