Jon Payne’s Summary

Jon Payne is a marketer and serial-entrepreneur who operates primarily with web-based media. He's the type of guy that starts six new projects before finishing the three he already has on his plate... and he sees nothing wrong with this. He holds an MBA from Loyola College.

Jon is the founder and president of Ephricon Web Marketing, an internet marketing agency that provides search engine marketing services to include both search engine optimization (SEO) and pay-per-click management (PPC) for companies looking to generate new leads online.

Ephricon is based in Baltimore, MD with a satellite office in suburban Charlotte, NC. He frequently travels back and forth from Charlotte to Baltimore :)

As a "side gig", Jon Payne is a partner at Lead Storm Media, an online lead generation firm operating in a variety of verticals. He also created the Baltimore SEO Meetup Group and is a member of the Charlotte SEO Meetup Group as well.

Jon Payne’s Specialties:

SEO, organic search engine optimization, PPC management, lead generation, web design & usability, online marketing strategy
