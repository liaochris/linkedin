Roelof Roos’s Experience

  • Contributing Editor

    One More Thing

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    June 2010 — Present (3 months)

    One More Thing is the biggest Apple internet community which serves over 40.000 registered users in The Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg, with daily news, audio & video podcasts, and a large forum. One More Thing is named after a phrase Steve Jobs every so often uses at the end of his famous keynotes.

    On One More Thing I share my insights on Apple, its products and its market. Being a developer for Apple products for more than 25 years and having multiple iOS apps in the iTunes App Store, I try to offer a perspective that differs from most other media.

    One More Thing's vision is simple but clear. We want to be the starting point for people who want to know everything about Apple. That in an Apple way.

  • Owner

    The Dutch Republic

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    1997 — Present (13 years )

    When I co-founded The Dutch Republic b.v. we focused on consulting and technology around management information systems or, if you prefer, business intelligence. Later on we also created websites and web-applications that applied the knowledge we acquired building the management information systems. We always stayed a relatively small company (with about 30 employees at the maximum). Through the years we built some pretty impressive sites/applications.

    To get a general feeling of our projects please take a look at this overview: http://www.thedutchrepublic.com/selectedprojects/.

    At The Dutch Republic I am the technical lead. We didn't center our company around a particular technology supplier, we tried to use the best tool for a particular job. We did a lot of things using open source technology but we also built systems using Microsoft products or Java. Next to being the tech guy I am the general manager and am responsible for the bigger part of our sales and our business development.

    A few years ago I decided I wanted to do more coding and other tech stuff myself. One of the things I do now is developing iOS (iPhone / iPad / iPod touch) apps from start to finish. You can view our current apps at: http://itunes.com/apps/thedutchrepublic.

    For more information about The Dutch Republic please visit http://thedutchrepublic.com/.
