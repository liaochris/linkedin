Joan Bennett’s Summary

We are here to serve the legal industry, legal professionals and their assistants, government departments, associations, legal support service businesses and all companies both large and small as well as the public at large. Find a document server, a process server, a registry, a bailiff, a court bailiff, a collection agent, an investigator or any other legal support service company in North America. We are here to help you on www.processserverscanada and with our print directory. Our two directories work very well independently but best of all together. Call today for a copy of Canada's Legal Support Directory for your desk or briefcase or go to our web site at www.processserverscanada.com. If you cannot find a legal support service company where and when you require one, call us at 1-888-891-4141.
