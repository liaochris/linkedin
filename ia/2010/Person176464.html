Roelof Botha’s Summary

Partner at Sequoia Capital

I am currently closely involved with the following companies: Challenge Games, Eventbrite, Gene Security Network, Jawbone, Mahalo, Meebo, Tokbox, Unity Technologies and Xoom. Prior portfolio companies include YouTube (acquired for $1.65bn by Google in 2006) and Insider Pages (acquired by CitySearch in 2007).

http://www.sequoiacap.com/us/roelof-botha/

Prior to Sequoia, I was the CFO at PayPal. I helped to take the company public in February 2002, and participated in negotiations for the sale of PayPal to eBay for $1.5bn in October 2002.
