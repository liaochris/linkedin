Kevin Rutherford’s Summary

I am a senior agile project leader, coach and developer with over 25 years' experience in hands-on software delivery. I am a Chartered Engineer and a Certified ScrumMaster, have been Managing Director of a successful consulting business and architect of several million-pound enterprise solutions; and in recent years I have coached numerous software teams to more than double their speed and quality of delivery.

I am also author of the book "Refactoring in Ruby" and the Reek code quality tool for Ruby.

In my own practice I rely on agile principles, with strong emphasis on rapid, sustained, incremental delivery of tangible results and the creation of defect-free software.

I am one of the leaders of the agile community in the UK. I founded the UK's AgileNorth group and I'm a member of the programme committee for the British Computer Society’s SPA (Software Practice Advancement) annual conference. I am also a leading speaker on the agile conference circuit, and I have run workshops as an invited speaker at numerous events of the British Computer Society and the Agile Alliance.

To find out more about me and how I can help you, or to join my network, email me (kevin@rutherford-software.com) or visit my website (http://www.kevinrutherford.co.uk).
