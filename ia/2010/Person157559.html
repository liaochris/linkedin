Omid Mousaei’s Summary

Mad Mind Studios is a graphic design company providing exceptional graphic design, strategy development, programming and marketing solutions. With an integrated approach I work to bring together three distinct fields of expertise: creativity, strategy and technology. By applying these key elements I understand all aspects of your business and deliver a comprehensive solution.

My company is small in size and I see this as a unique advantage. I accept a limited number of projects, giving each my complete and full attention. MMS’ size also allows me to comfortably adapt to the customs of my clients, and become an extension of their team. I operate a full spectrum of creative skillsets and use them to develop a rapid workflow.

Mad Mind Studios is the essential partner to any business in need of a design presence or seeking new concepts to energize and reinvigorate old brands. Mad Mind Studios develops and delivers unique, skillful graphic design products that enable businesses to present a clear message and professional image to their customer markets.
Mad Mind Studios has continued providing quality creative solutions to its clients. I love creative diversity and am always looking for new challenges and the opportunity for fresh brand creation.

Let Mad Mind Studios bring its experience to your company.
