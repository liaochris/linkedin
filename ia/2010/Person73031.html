Gary Hogue’s Summary

I am a recent graduate with a BS in Media Arts and Animation. I prefer to work in 2D animation including hand drawn and flash (or other animation programs.) Of course I'm willing to work 3D as well ;)

I use/have used many different creative software packages like the Adobe creative suite, Corel Painter Maya and Toon Boom.

My animation career is just getting started and I'm hungry for more. I chose this industry because I believe in following your heart rather than trying to make a living doing something miserable. Each day is a learning experience and I'm hooked :)

I'm inspired by everything around me. I find that the most interesting things have a wonderful mix of the beautiful and the ugly sides of life.

Gary Hogue’s Education

  • The Art Institute of Portland

    BS , Media Arts and Animation , 2004 — 2007

    The school was very focused on teaching us computer skills, which I am all for, but I also went there to get traditional training first. I made it a point to get as much training in traditional art principles, values and techniques as possible. I was fortunate enough to learn these from instructors like Lee White, Joe McMurrian, Brian Larson, Za Vue and even some of the digital people like Josh Jones and Tim Oberlander.

    Activities and Societies:
    President and Co-founder of F.A.T.S.O.F.A.C.E. .Animation, the schools animation club. Our name comes from an acronym that animator Brian Larson made to help remember the principles of animation: Flexibility. Anticipation. Timing and Spacing. Squash and Stretch. Overlapping action. Follow through. Arcs. Counter action. Exaggeration.
