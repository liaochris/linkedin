Jon Schuler’s Summary

Applied mathematician and numerical algorithm developer

Data mining of relational observations in the context of computational sociology. Projects include statistical analysis of document corpuses, social networks, transactional and user-rating environments, organization modeling, and event-records analysis.

10+ years experience in image and signal processing for electro-optic tactical-imaging sensors, involving infrared, night vision, and hyperspectral sensors. Specialization includes image registration, scene stabilization, change detection, color image fusion, anomaly extraction, and multi-frame super-resolution.

Client-solutions built upon the statistical techniques of data mining, decision trees, regression analysis, tensor decomposition / multi-linear algebra, graph-theoretic co-clustering, and non-parametric methods.
