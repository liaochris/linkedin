Sundaranarayanan Subramaniam’s Summary

A positive, proactive and dynamic senior I.T. consultant and Technical Architect specialising in .NET technologies and also in the lifecycle management of complex software design and development projects from the initial concept and requirements gathering through to testing and deployment.
