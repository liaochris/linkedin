Ailsa Scott’s Summary

Started out in Weir Pumps as an apprentice and have worked through the ranks there ever since. Company name has now changed to CLYDEUNION Pumps. My current role is Sales Support Manager in the Water & Industrial Business Unit and is focused on Product, Knowledge & Information Management.

My current goal is to study hard and finish my MBA at Strathclyde Business School, I'm reaching the end of 2nd year of a 3 year part time course, whilst still working full time and raising two kids... Doesn't leave much time for anything else!

My ultimate goal is to be the best I can be and to be someone my kid's are proud off, a surprisingly difficult task but worth every minute and I'm enjoying the ride!

Ailsa Scott’s Specialties:

Product Management, Knowledge Management, Information Management & Process Improvement, specifically in managing configured pump products in the most efficient way possible. Making the best of technology such as PDM, 3D design, business rule engines & ERP systems (SAP) also trained in Kaizen and Six Sigma techniques. Man Management skills including over 10 years of practical man management, an SVQ level 4 in management and currently studying for a Masters degree in business administration.
