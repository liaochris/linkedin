Anthony Vaccaro’s Summary

3D/Environment artist skilled in created rich and lush game worlds utilizing the industry's most current standards while encompassing composition, lighting, color, detail, story and silhouette in all my work.

I have a broad range of experience ranging from one of the most popular Xbox games on the market, Halo to MMO experience working on the first EverQuest game.

I strive to create the best worlds possible within my given constraints weather it be for an MMO that is 10 years old such as EverQuest 1 or pushing the Xbox as far as the engine would let us on Halo Reach.

I have worked on projects and with other artists with very strict deadlines, specifications and wishes and always have managed to hit or exceed there expectations. I have also had the opposite and worked with no given time line, very little direction and no chosen concept and exceeded there just as much.

My passion for the art that not only I work on, but the art of the entire project is what helps me stand above the rest. I enjoy working with others who share this passion and who want to push the project they are working on to be better then anyone could have expected.
