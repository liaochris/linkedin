Timothy Staiano’s Summary

I am a consummate Sales Professional with a B.S. degree and over ten (10) years of outside sales experience. I was most recently involved in building the premiere Environmental & Industrial Hygiene Testing/Sampling Equipment company in the U.S. Tasked with building & growing a territory in the Southeastern U.S. (ranging from MD to FL west to the Mississippi River), TRS-Environmental provided me with the opportunity to fully realize my abilities as a consummate sales professional. The prospect of building a company from the ground up was both exciting & rewarding.

Having formerly worked for full-service Environmental & Waste Management companies with footprints covering multiple counties within 1-2 states to large, multi-state regions, the career opportunity and path within TRS-Environmental was a welcomed change and the pinnacle of my professional efforts.

I would like to continuously grow my professional resume to include either management of large National Account base or have the opportunity to motivate and build a successful sales force that may be in need of growth and direction.

Through my career, I have worked my way from inside (Tech Services) representative to Field Sales Representative with Clean Harbors; state (NJ) wide Account Manager with PSC; successfully managed broker/TSDF segment for DuPont Secure Environmental Treatment; successfully expanded the single-largest geographic territory within the most successful branch office/location of VeoliaES Technical Solutions. While employed with Ecoflo, Inc. I not only met, but exceeded my revenue goals in under two (2) years. Increased revenues in 2007 by 150% vs. 2006, and exceeded 2007 budget projections by 20%.
