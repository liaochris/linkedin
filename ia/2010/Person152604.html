  • Manager, Operations Centre

    Brookfield Lepage Johnson Controls

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; JCI; Automotive industry)

    2003 — 2004 (1 year )

    This role involved complete autonomy to improve a contact centre operation for a leader in property management and get it to a world class level. The main focus was to build out the fundamentals while improving a sagging employee engagement score. Key tactics were to establish competencies and an organization structure to support Workforce Management, Training and Quality, Operations Management, Client Account Management and Technology/Telephony. Additional tactics were to complete a root-cause analysis of low employee engagement, build an action plan and execute. Results were an increase in employee engagement from 22% to 85% in the first 8 months and through the rollout of the new competencies, for the first time, the centre was able to hit or exceed SLA's for all 13 clients consistently.

  • Manager, eChannel and Innovation

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; ADS; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2000 — 2003 (3 years )
