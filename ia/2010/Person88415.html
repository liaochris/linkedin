James Pearson’s Summary

I have 20 years of experience in international risk management consultancy. I have been posted in three countries, United Kingdom, Hong Kong and Thailand; and have conducted 600 projects in 25 countries worth over 30m USD.

I have extensive experience in management consultancy, project management and direction of multi-jurisdictional and multi facility studies addressing corporate governance, business, management and compliance risks from an EHS perspective. My clients have included many Fortune 500 companies in a wide range of industries from real estate, infrastructure, finance and investment, manufacturing, chemical, mining and extractive, oil and gas, utilities and food and beverage. I have worked directly with clients or through teams of advisors including legal, financial, engineering and real estate.
