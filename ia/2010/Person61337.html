Becky Henry’s Summary

Certified Coach focusing on bringing back joy to families living with a health crisis/serious health issue/addictions. Specializing in setting healthy boundaries/self care tools/support for family members. Interested in publishing, continuing education for health care providers.

Current Projects:
Finishing book: Just Tell Her to Stop: Stories of Families Coping with Eating Disorders - due Spring 2010

Eating Disorders Parent Support Group

A chance for families to talk with other families who have lived this horror. We'll have some group coaching, someone to listen to you, acknowledge the crisis you are in as well as the losses you are experiencing, give you some encouragement and connect you with your greatest source of strength. This is for anyone who cares about someone with an eating disorder.

Details: 10-Week Family/Friends Support for Eating Disorders
Location: Ridgedale Library - 12601 Ridgedale Dr. Minnetonka, MN 55305
Space: Mtg. room on 1st floor below library
Dates: Wednesdays
Time: 5-6:30pm
Cost: $10/person or $15/couple per week or pay for all 10 weeks up front and SAVE $10
Led by: Becky Henry, CPCC
Register in advance so we can reserve the room – must have at least 5 people signed up.

Check out the website for more information http://www.HopeNetwork.info or email me with questions.

Please feel free to pass this along to anyone who helps people with eating disorders.

Health Care/Behavioral health professional organizations:
CE Course for health care providers - Do you wish you had more tools for helping your patients with weight issues?
www.movingmountains4health.eventbrite.com We look forward to helping your organization know more about healthy weight issues. We are available to speak at your conferences.

And as always:
One on One coaching - I am that gentle, loving KICK in the backside that will get you unstuck when you are ready to move forward!

Becky Henry’s Specialties:

Managing fear and panic, reclaiming power, finding resources, support without enabling, letting go of drama, reclaiming joy.
