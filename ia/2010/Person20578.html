Bas van den Beld’s Summary

I am blogger, consultant, project manager, coach, search marketer and publicist on the web. I live in The Netherlands and am the owner of my own company NetTraject and Chief Editor of Searchcowboys.

My expertise is very broad and covers several areas.

Blogger/Publicist
As a blogger I am well informed about what’s going on in the world of Internet Marketing and Search Marketing, with a special interest in Search Marketing. As a blogger for Dutch Cowboys I have written more than 300 articles about Internet Marketing. In 2008 we also started Searchcowboys (www.searchcowboys.com) . As Chief Editor I am responsible for the content of the website and I am also one of the most active writers.

Project management / consultant
I am a experienced project manager. As a project manager I have implemented a lot of websites from all different sizes. I have been on both the implementing as the client side. I know what makes a project go successful. I therefore setup the company NetTraject (www.nettraject.nl) to help companies get online and make the right choices.

As a consultant I can help select the right partners to use when building a website and I can see which pitfalls you will run into and know what to do to avoid them.

Coaching
Because I know my way around internet projects and implementations and because I have been working for and with several internet companies I know how difficult it can be to make a young internet company grow. I can help these companies by coaching them through the difficult times. My advice has helped several companies already.

Workshops
To help my clients I give a lot of workshops. In these workshop I try to get as close to the ‘real world’ of my clients as possible. My workshops cover all areas of my expertise: workshops on project management, search marketing, internet marketing and how to run a young webcompany.
