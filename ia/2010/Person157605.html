Adrian Ulsh’s Summary

Adrian Ulsh is an author, lecturer and consummate entrepreneur who has a passion for helping business owners learn the secrets to generating leads, attracting clients and making more money than they have ever made before.

His expertise in helping organizations and individuals achieve business and life success has landed him on / with:

• The Inc 500 List of the Fastest Growing Businesses (3X)
• The American Management Association
• SkillPath Seminars
• AlliedSignal Aerospace

In the last 30 years, he has helped to build two multimillion dollar companies including Y2 Marketing where he licensed marketing consultants in both the US and Canada.

Adrian is a veteran of more than 3500 presentations and author of the highly-acclaimed “How To Supervise & Produce Real Results.”

Today, Adrian is the Vice president of OneCoach, a company committed to helping small and mid sized business owners and entrepreneurs grow their business revenues, profits and value so they can achieve financial freedom and live extraordinary lives.
As the Vice President of Training and Product Development, Adrian has recently written, developed and produced three business growth programs that will directly impact the ability of business owners worldwide in their quest to build multimillion dollar businesses. Attract More Clients, The Business Momentum Club and the Instant Cash Flow Strategies Program are now available to both OneCoach members as well as non-members.

His loves are his family, exercise, cooking, travel, writing and helping entrepreneurs understand how to incorporate the strategies and tactics required to build a highly successful business that operates with confidence and certainty.
