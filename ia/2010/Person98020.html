Jillian Ryan’s Summary

I am a freelance writer and journalist with a specialty in travel and family. My work has been published on familytravelform.com, divinecaroline.com and in NY Metro Parents magazine. Recently, I have started to blog (travelvictories.com) to gather more of an audience and keep my writing fresh. I am also slowly developing my presence in the world of social media. With the mission to write about travel and the things I am passionate about and fascinating by, I am have been trying to establish myself as a freelancer in more solid markets. Although I love the flexible nature of the freelance world, a full-time gig is also something I am seeking as well. I am looking to make connection, network and learn as much as I can about this crazy industry.
