Brooke Donelson’s Summary

Regional Property Manager with a broad experience in Multi-Family Housing and a passion for the industry. Specializing in increasing income, creative "out of the box" marketing plans, economic occupancy and NOI on distressed communities/portfolios and as well as lease up portfolios.

Currently managing a portfolio of 14 buildings in the urban core of Kansas City, Missouri with MAC Property Management. MAC is committed to building better neighborhoods wherever they are, not only for its residents, but for the greater community. In addition, MAC feels it is important for us to preserve the environment, improve infrastructures, and enhance the beauty of our settings by completely rehabbing, restoring and reopening some of Kansas City most historically rich buildings.

Brooke Donelson’s Specialties:

Distressed portfolios and communities, creative marketing, lease ups, positive leadership, multi-tasking, budget compliance, due diligence, communication
