Dan Sanchez’s Summary

I have been an automotive journalist, editor and photographer for more than 23 years. My intent has always been to approach everything with integrity and trust and it has helped establish a bounty of excellent relationships with many automotive aftermarket and OEM industry leaders. My agency simply started as a way to help some of my friends and colleagues with their PR and Marketing that I was doing on the side while performing my duties as a magazine editor-in-chief. With the last five years pioneering new marketing campaigns and creating online content that attracts readers, my goal is to provide aftermarket manufacturers and retailers with an easy way to expose and brand their products on numerous autmotive networks, and manage their online marketing and advertising goals at a cost effective rate.
