SNAPSHOP OF SERVICES

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Content Development

I utilize specific keywords targeted toward your site’s audience and create web content around those keywords. By optimizing the content on your website, you increase exposure and clientele, which essentially increases profits.

Website Content/Blogs

I develop general information content pages, SEO articles, product/service descriptions and About Us/FAQ pages. I also create blurbs/newsletter blasts or blog content to post in social networks for website advertisement. Or I can create content-specific blogs to post directly on your site.

Ghostwriting (Print and Online Content)

If you need content that requires no bylines, or you want to place your name on quality information, I’m the person to write it for you. By asking simple questions about your project, the audience you wish to address, and the tone you want to project, I can create works that will carry the essence of You, without putting pencil to pad (or fingers to keys).

Online Newsletters

I can create quick blurbs/teasers to entice readers to visit your webpage, a full newsletter with original articles, advertisements, and more.
