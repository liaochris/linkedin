Richard Villasana’s Summary

Richard Villasana, known as The Mexico Guru, is a leading authority on Mexican business practices, culture and communication. He has fifteen years of experience marketing to Mexico as well as importing products to the U.S. Richard is a sought-after international speaker by corporations such as Wal-Mart and AT&T as well as universities in both the U.S. and Mexico. His presentations are in both English and Spanish. He is also fluent in French.

He has been profiled in U.S. newspapers such as the San Diego Business Journal. EFE, the world's largest Spanish news agency, interviewed Richard because of his success with the Hispanic market in the U.S. His articles on marketing and business practices in Mexico have been published in industry trade magazines such as Customer Relationship Management (CRM). He is also the top-selling author of "Insider Secrets for Doing Business in Mexico", now in its third edition.

Fortune 100 companies such as Cisco Systems and AT&T value Richard Villasana, for his expertise and experience in diversity and doing business in Mexico.

A key goal is to expand my public speaking career to include corporate cross-cultural and diversity training and international marketing. Looking for corporate sponsors who want to target U.S. Hispanic consumers.
