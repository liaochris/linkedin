Larry Rudwick’s Summary

I primarily coach/consult & "tune-up" small privately-owned companies (up to 250 employees), helping them to set & reach new goals. First we look at "where we are": strengths, weaknesses, obstacles, goals, etc., & then make a road-map (typically simple business plans or a projects list) to get to the destination in realistic, practical, cost-effective ways. There's much more specifics on my website.

I also occasionally do career coaching & mentoring for people wanting to become more productive & content at their careers & lives.

INVENTOR: I received 3 patents in the 1970s, all for small wheeled vehicles.

INNOVATOR/BUSINESS OWNER: From 1977 through 1996, I co-founded & built a manufacturing/distribution business from scratch. It became the leading supplier of wheelchair parts and accessories for the replacement market. After capturing 45% of the US market, the business was sold to the #1 wheelchair manufacturer, a large public company • • our main competitor.

COACH/CONSULTANT: More recently, I've received certification in executive coaching and apply what I have learned and continue to learn from my business experiences, to my clients' situations.

I consider myself both Right-Brain and Left-Brain. Although an engineering graduate that can look at technical issues, I typically spend more time dealing with "people issues". REMEMBER THIS: Most patents and new innovations do have useful applications, but they never make it to market or, if they do, are business failures. It's typically because there 1) wasn't a proper business plan, 2) the implementation of the plan was poor, or 3) the right people either weren't involved or weren't effective. These 3 factors are "PEOPLE ISSUES", and are typically the central focus of my work.
