Mike Cutler’s Summary

I have been an Automotive Photographer for over two years. I was involved with Festivals of Speed, Classic Audi, Ultimate Motors, Porsche Citrus Region, and the SCCA Club; during my time at Full Sail University.

My work has made it into the March 2009 issue of CMYK Magazine, and hopefully more publications to come!

My future goals are to move my photography to greater heights and get into agencies and magazines.

Mike Cutler’s Specialties:

Using my Canon EOS 400D with a set of 5 lenses I can capture any image that comes to mind. Post processing is done in Lightroom, Photoshop, Fluid Mask, and exported to the highest quality.
