Susan Schaffer’s Summary

With a proven track -record of creating profitable growth, I have consistently developed analytically based, market-driven strategies and lead teams in successful execution.

Holding marketing positions in both public and privately owned firms in a diverse cross section of industries, I’ve consistently driven sales growth, brand development, product development, market share, distribution expansion and operational efficiencies.

As a key strategic and tactical contributor in efforts that tripled sales for Fujifilm digital cameras and established Olympus as a #1 brand leader in cameras, my core focus is to revitalize businesses and build fearless competitors.

Most recently, at Market Inc., I led a number of consulting projects that resulted in increased brand exposure and new business development projects for business-to-business services in the both the online and traditional world. Prior to that, at Royal Sovereign, I guided the transition from an import/export company to a market-driven model, leading to double-digit sales increases and expanded distribution.

At Fujifilm, I led the marketing team in taking a product category that had limited market penetration, was positioned as an ‘opening price point’ brand and an unprofitable bottom line into a profitable, growing business. With a multi-faceted strategy, we tripled sales, doubled market share and created one of the company’s more profitable divisions. To support the lagging P&L, a broad range of programs were initiated - forecasting protocols, returns reduction, online customer support tools and the launch of new high-profit product lines.

With a commitment to holistic strategy that is created and executed across the organization, I have a passion for revitalizing businesses and creating fearless competitors… with a balance of big-picture strategy with hands-on implementation, analysis with responsiveness and leadership with team building.
