Karen Saunders’s Summary

Why I am here on LinkedIn:
I am seeking opportunities to form Joint Ventures/Strategic Alliances with small business owners, entrepreneurs and training organizations who participate in or support the marketing, advertising, writing and speaking industries.

My book, Turn Eye Appeal into Buy Appeal, provides easy-to-read instructions for people to develop marketing pieces such as flyers, brochures, logos and presentations that have a balance of “eye appeal” and “buy appeal” to make sales soar without having to hire a designer. This book won the “Best of Show” and 1st Place in the “How To” category of the 2007 Colorado Independent Publishers Association EVVY Book Awards competition. It also won an 2006 APEX award.

Sample topics in my book:
*10 Questions to Answer Before Designing Your Next Project
*Branding: An Umbrella Concept You Can’t Ignore
*Think in the Prospect’s Perspective
*6 Elements That Make Your Design Effective

My book is available in PDF and soft cover format and may be sold within my affiliate program or bundled with one of your products. In addition, I also teach sections of the book through webinars, or my book may be included in the curriculum of your training program.

The Experience I Bring to a Joint Venture/Strategic Alliance:
The focus of my company, MacGraphics Services, which was founded in 1990, is to assist business owners to professionally represent themselves and their products/services through well-branded graphics and marketing materials.

My designs have played in the success of 28 award-winning and several best-selling books with a recent project that earned 2005 Writer’s Digest “Grand Prize” for the best-self published book in America. In March 2006, I presented a breakout session on visual branding at Mark Victor Hansen’s “Mega Marketing Magic” conference. I have given live teleseminar and webinar interviews and design presentations to many professional and trade organizations.

Karen Saunders’s Specialties:

Graphic Design for Corporate Identity, Marketing and Advertising, Visual Branding, Book Cover and Interior Design, Speaker One-Sheet Design, Referral Systems, Send Out Cards Consultant (Senior Manager).
