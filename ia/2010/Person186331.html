Sera Bishop’s Summary

Sera Bishop has a BFA in Interior Design. She is fascinated with environmental psychology, and particularly interested in revolutionary health care and office design.

She began her studies focusing on humanities and communication before committing to design. She thoroughly enjoys networking opportunities and has experience and ideas to offer businesses interested in using social media.

She is currently an Intern for red dot studio, Inc. She is a member of the St. Louis chapter of Architecture for Humanity and a volunteer for Habitat for Humanity. Sera is fascinated with the concept of Urbanism. She is continually looking for opportunities and connections within the design industry.

She is working towards NCIDQ certification and LEED-accreditation to aide in reducing and correcting the environmental impact of our interior design decisions. She embraces the importance of Universal and Evidence-Based Design.
