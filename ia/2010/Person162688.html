Peter D. Revers’s Summary

A career retailer and business development consultant.

Always looking for new clients from the consumer goods, special concept, retail, web, direct mail arena to help introduce to the huge US marketplace.

Introducing a leading British retail concept into the USA, and living in the USA has made me uniquely positioned to help other British or foreign retail concepts develop the massive US consumer marketplace. Whether you are looking for someone to head up your US operation, help you develop a US presence, or merely act as an occasional sounding board on things American, I know that I can add much value to you and your company.

Brief Biography:
• Brought international retail concepts into and out of the USA
• Responsible for nationally known designer retail brands up to the 400 million dollar level
• Involved with start-ups, turnarounds and strategic realignments, licensing and international business.
• Ex Chairman & CEO Laura Ashley (North America), Co-founder & Ex Chairman of Revman International, Ex President & COO The Nature Company,
• Honored by HM Queen Elisabeth II of England by the award of "The Order of the British Empire" (OBE) for developing British business in the USA
• Held CEO, COO, EVP, Chairman, and President positions, currently President of PDR Associates an international retail consulting company.
• Good with people, great success in building teams and motivating them. A compassionate manager, a good listener . I believe in the democratic process, but business is not a democracy.
• Detail orientated when I need to be but consider myself to be more of a strategist and entrepreneur
• Un-impeachable have great integrity and believe that I can bring a wealth of experience to any organization
• Very computer and Web literate
