Jarno M. Koponen’s Summary

"An experienced creative professional specialized in the design, future, research, and development of experiential digital communication."

I’ve been working in the field of digital media as a designer and researcher for a decade, focusing on the design, concepting and branding of the interactive digital products and networked environments.

Additionally, I’ve been working with the explorative design and communication concepts as well as artistic projects in varying contexts. I’ve been researching future-oriented game-like environments, social media and related phenomena at Media Lab Helsinki and Helsinki Institute for Information Technology (HIIT).


METHOD AND PROCESS

I have a dual background as a design professional since I am a designer specialized in digital communication with my roots deeply in the soil of human disciplines.

In my design work, project management and research praxis, theory and practice are always functionally united.

My broad knowledge of different fields of culture, art and science provides unique intellectual tools and perspectives to make decisions and create solutions that are visionary yet enduring, open-minded yet comprehensible and well-justified.

The knowledge about processes and their management, the understanding of the context, resources and the affecting aims and intentions ensures decisions that are based on strategic wisdom and durable thinking.


INSPIRATION

I’m inspired by thinkers and doers varying from philosophers and media researchers to graffiti artists, experimental musicians and critical design professionals.

Similarly, I’m inspired by the unique possibilities of new emerging digital environments and the inter-connected nature of all cultural and social phenomena.
