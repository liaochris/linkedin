Melissa Gratias’s Experience

    (Management Consulting industry)

    July 2007 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    MBG Organizing Solutions works with businesspeople to streamline processes and design organizational systems that lead to success. Being organized is a means to an end. I can help you accomplish your goals through being organized and effective.

    Specifically, I can help you with:
    * Paper
    * Electronic Files
    * Email
    * Time Management
    * Office space

    Got groups of people needing to be organized? I develop custom workshops and training classes to help groups of people reduce paper and electronic files, raise productivity, and lower stress.

    Need a speaker? I have prepared speeches and mini-training classes ready to deliver to business associations in the Chattanooga, Tennessee area.

  • Sr. Manager - People Technology and Service Delivery

    BlueCross BlueShield of Tennessee

    (Non-Profit; Insurance industry)

    July 2005 — July 2007 (2 years 1 month)

    In my two years at BCBST, I worked with an amazing team of specialists to ensure that our HR technology operated efficiently and effectively. We always looked for ways to continuously improve our utilization of technology.

    I was also privileged to help lead a project that centralized all HR administrative processes into a customer service and transactional center. We mapped out the processes and designed a call center that could handle hundreds of different HR transactions.

  • Visiting Assistant Professor / Lecturer

    The University of Tennessee at Chattanooga

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    2004 — 2005 (1 year )

    The best part of teaching is seeing students think about issues in new ways and develop into well-rounded professionals. I loved being a part of the learning process and teaching my graduate and undergraduate students at UTC. I still see them from time to time and appreciate it when they share stories of my teaching antics.

  • Recruitment and Retention Director

    Protective Life Corporation

    (Public Company; PL; Insurance industry)

    1998 — 2004 (6 years )

    I started my career at Protective Life straight out of graduate school. I was in charge of the annual Employee Feedback Survey. I also became an internal consultant for statistical measurement and quality improvement.

    What I loved the most about the early years of my job at Protective, though, was when I functioned as an executive coach for our top 200 people. If you can imaging a senior leader allowing a twenty-something HR newbie help them be a better leader, go ahead. All I can say, though, is that it worked. I found a calling to work with people struggling through real, day-to-day issues in their jobs. I learned that you can never assume that just because someone is a senior executive that they don't want to learn and grow.

    Later, I grew into a leadership role and managed the employee communications, organizational development, and recruitment process areas. I left Protective as a trailing spouse when my family relocated to Chattanooga, Tennessee.
