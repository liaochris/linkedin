Matthew Hawk, Ph.D’s Summary

As Principal at Retention Specialists, Matthew Hawk brings 20 years of professional experience to help sales, customer service, and customer retention teams increase revenue. He has delivered thousands of training and consulting events for large sales-focused organizations including AIG, A.G. Edwards, America Online, American Express, DIRECTV, GE CareCredit, GE Money, MetLife, MSN, Microsoft adCenter, Morgan Stanley, State Farm, Toyota Financial Services, Southern Connecticut State University and Yale University.

Matthew's sales and sales management career includes sales roles at Gartner and U.S. Interactive, where he was Vice President of Corporate Sales. As a consultant, he has designed and delivered hundreds of performance improvement programs for individual contributors, line managers, and senior managers. As a Project Leader, Matthew has successfully delivered solutions for large enterprises involving 1,000's of salespeople in multiple onshore and offshore locations.

Dr. Hawk earned his B.A. from Westmont College, an M.A. from Yale Divinity School, and his Ph.D. in comparative religion from Yale Graduate School. He lives in Los Angeles.

Matthew Hawk, Ph.D’s Specialties:

Sales, Sales Management, Sales Training and Management Consulting. Matthew’s areas of expertise include professional services and consulting business development, call centers sales, service and customer retention training, leadership training, and train-the-trainer programs.
