Bob Hobbs Jr.’s Summary

With over 400 associates and total company sales volume exceeding 100 million, I was on my way to becoming the President and CEO of one of Arizona's largest privately owned businesses. What the outside world did not know about me was my daily battle with Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD) and the havoc it played on my life "behind the scenes." A disorder so severe it often left me pondering how I was going to survive another single minute. Battling OCD since I was a pre-teen, there were many times I couldn't function – I couldn't get myself dressed, I couldn't even leave the house. Bottom line…I worked really hard at appearing as though things were great to be sure nobody knew what I was going through. Then, even when my OCD was somewhat under control I still faced other challenges in life. Both my parents endured difficult battles with cancer and my high-school sweetheart, who I ultimately married, was diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis.

So what did all of this teach me? Well…if I was capable of hiding my own vulnerabilities from the outside world, so was everyone else. These vulnerabilities can be related to finances, health (mental & physical), faith, relationships, employment, parenting, gender, race and many others.

Today, my mission in life is to teach you how to discover your own purpose in life and provide you with key tools that will allow you to enjoy the benefits of self empowerment. I am blessed to be able to work with ALL types of people. (CEO's, Doctors, Athletes, Parents, College Students, Struggling Teens, etc.) I travel the world sharing stories about my battle with OCD and other life speed bumps, emphasizing the importance of CHOOSING to have faith and be happy regardless of our difficulties in life.
