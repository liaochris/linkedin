Mike Mooney’s Summary

I have a comprehensive marketing and distribution background with industry-leading financial services/consumer products organizations.

After honing my financial skills at Price Waterhouse, I have worked with leading Financial Services organizations including MasterCard, Household Credit, AIG, and MetLife. Additionally, I have served as a consultant and managed an entrepreneurial start-up.

I have developed strong quantitative training into broad consumer marketing skills that have enabled me to drive brands (consumer and B2B) and revenue, cost-effectively. I possess a strong general management foundation leading marketing (including advertising, product marketing, market intelligence and database marketing), distribution, operations, and finance/accounting functions around P&L, acquisitions, business integration, strategy, negotiations, and restructuring initiatives. Finally, my career demonstrates substantive increases in job scope, products managed, staff, and fiscal responsibility.
