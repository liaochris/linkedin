Linda Heil’s Summary

Paw Au Pair is your PROFESSIONAL in home pet sitting and dog walking service. Providing TOP quality care for your pets in the familiar surroundings of their own home while you're traveling or at work. We would love to answer any question you have, please call us 303-485-8498.

We understand how important it is to find high quality services as well as professionalism, and pride ourselves in providing such a service.
Whether you need our services for the occasional walk, trip to the groomers or vet's office, regular scheduled visits or for extended travel, we can meet your needs.

Not only do we take care of your pet, we also provide additional services such as watering your plants (indoor and outdoor), bring in the mail or newspapers, alternate lights while gone to provide a "lived-in" appearance and take out the trash and recycling bins.

It is our goal to offer you and your pets the highest quality of care, so you return home to happy healthy pets.

For your peace of mind, Paw Au Pair is fully bonded and insured to give you the highest quality care for your pets and home.
