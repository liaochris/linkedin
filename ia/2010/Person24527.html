Bjorn Roche’s Summary

Digital audio software designer and consultant, specializing in workstation design, DSP/DASP, internet and web audio technologies, with almost 10 years experience in Java, C, C++ and Web technologies. Designer of award winning XO Wave mastering software for Mac OS X and Linux. Creator of Inadaba Media’s browser-based recording software (now called “Mantis”) endorsed by Rivers Cuomo of Weezer, and other software used by major companies including Sterling Sound, Bondai Digital Publishing (aka Cover to Cover) and Z-Systems Engineering.

Accomplishments and Projects
+Three patents pending.
+Lead development of the first ever Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) that could record, edit, mix and apply real-time effects in a web browser (now called “Mantis” and maintained by Indaba media).
+Developed XO Wave, a digital audio workstation for Linux and Mac OS X.
+Maintainer of Mac OS X (Core Audio) implementation of PortAudio, an open source portable audio I/O library.
+Publication: “Automated assessment of small airway disease from low-dose lung CT: a preliminary study” Proceedings of SPIE Vol. 5030. Björn Dittmer-Roche, Henry Rusinek, Jane P. Ko, Georgeann McGuinness and David Naidich.
