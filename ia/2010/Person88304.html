Jim Barton’s Summary

I have over 20 years of software development experience covering everything from assembler to C/CC+, Delphi, VB, and Java, and under operating systems that range from mainframes to Windows and embedded devices.

I thrive in high pressure situations with aggressive schedules in which I can immerse myself in highly intensive software development. Although I have often been team leader/architect head on development projects my personal preference is to stay more involved with the hands-on ‘nuts and bolts’ of software development.

I particularly value in a position the opportunities to grow professionally by assimulating new technologies and skills.
