Lucinda Hart-Gonzalez’s Summary

Former university professor and academic director, as well as union bilingual actor on stage and film, I focus now on organic farming and dairying and writing, while doing consulting in writing and language teaching and testing (especially federal government). I do many things, each with equal enthusiasm. I like to live life to the fullest and so far that has been very much the case.
