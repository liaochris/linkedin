Doug Allen’s Summary

Douglas Allen is the Digital Sales Manager of Pittsburgh CBS Television Station digital properties: kdka.com and wpcwtv.com. As Director of Digital Media, Douglas is responsible for developing cross-platform marketing solutions for traditional KDKA-TV advertising clients, while developing programs for new revenue streams.

Douglas began his career in television as a sports producer in 1991. After a brief stint with a start-up tech company during the internet "boom" of the late 90s, Douglas returned to television with the technical knowledge that would later be applicable to his current position.

Douglas graduated from Robert Morris University with both a B.S. in Sport Marketing Management in 1993 and M.S. in Communications Information Systems in 1999.
