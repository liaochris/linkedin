DJ Edgerton’s Summary

Marketing professional with 20 years of both Traditional Large Agency and Interactive experience. Has executed global interactive marketing initiatives for Fortune 500 Companies. Specializes in developing and leveraging technology to deliver ground breaking business solutions that have measurable results for customers. Created a proprietary research vehicle that utilizes VOC and human behavior tracking to allow large enterprises to identify unique new business opportunities.

Has been an entrepreneur for 18 years. Featured in The WSJ, HOW Magazine, and multiple other trade publications. Finalist at Cannes, winner of The Grand Global for Pharma Interactive Marketing, NY Festivals, AD Club of NY as well as multiple other interactive industry awards. Webby Honoree in 2008.

Likes to over deliver. Committed to changing American citizens and industry's negative opinion of Colombia.

DJ Edgerton’s Specialties:

Advanced Usability and User Interaction Research as well as VOC and SixSigma processes. Large scale enterprise-wide custom application development. Award Winning Flash Design, Gaming, SEO and Interactive Marketing. Business leader with experience in managing a large distributed network of professionals. Have negotiated large contracts. Acquisition negotiation. S-1 offering document and company positioning experience.
