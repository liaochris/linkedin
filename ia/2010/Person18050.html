Armando Vieira’s Summary

I have been working on several projects developing applications of data mining tools.

Since 1998 I have been developing and applying Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) and Support Vector Machines to problems ranging from physics to economics.

I developed a new algorithm to train Neural Networks for classification problems and its application to complex tasks, namely financial distress prediction of private French companies. See http://aires.dei.uc.pt
