Rob Ross’s Summary

I started my career at Apple and went on to build 5 companies that resulted in 2 IPO’s and 5 acquisitions after raising $112M in financing: CRI, Zero G, Adero, ValiCert & Individual.

My companies have ranged from consumer to enterprise services with a focus on mobile, ad/subscription and secure content distribution. I've built profitable revenue & strategic relationships with media and technology companies including Microsoft, Yahoo, AT&T, Verizon, IBM, Intel, News Corp/Fox, Sony, Disney, AOL and several others.

VC investors in my companies have included Kleiner Perkins, Bessemer Ventures, August Capital, Draper Fisher Jurvetson, USVP, Spectrum Equity & MC Ventures.

Strategic investors in my companies have included Microsoft, Intel, Reuters, Fidelity, AOL, France Telecom, Gemplus, Thomson, Knight-Ridder (now McClatchy), Financial Technology Ventures, Mitsubishi, Mitsui & Korea Technology Banking.

Rob Ross’s Specialties:

  • Dir, Business Development

    Individual, Inc. (IPO, acq by Thomson Reuters)

    (Public Company; TRI; Computer Software industry)

    1991 — 1997 (6 years )

    I left Apple to join Individual as the 15th employee. We built one of the first major content services delivered via email, Web and mobile with an advertising and subscription business model. Apple subscribed to the service and I loved the idea of "the newspaper of the future", after exploring Walter Bender's work at MIT's Media Lab. We got venture funding from Kleiner/Perkins, Knight/Ridder, Microsoft and several others. After our IPO, we merged with our largest competitor to become NewsEdge, which was acquired by Thomson (now Thomson Reuters). Along the way, I made several life-long friends which led me to several of my other companies. Our IPO filing with the SEC: http://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1002536/0000927016-96-000615.txt
