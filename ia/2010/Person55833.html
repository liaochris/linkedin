Dheeru Mundluru’s Summary

- Agile software engineer/manager with over 8 years of (start-up) experience in developing innovative object-oriented software in domains spanning e-commerce, social bookmarking, local search, and metasearch engines.
- Extensive end-to-end software development experience with solid track record in all phases of application software development life cycle.
- 5+ years of experience in developing Web data mining algorithms with an emphasis in information retrieval & filtering (search relevancy, personalization), structured data extraction (wrapper generation), Deep Web crawling, classification & clustering, opinion mining, reputation systems, etc.
- Experience in successfully initiating, leading, and executing large projects involving both in-house and outsourced development teams.
- Extensive experience in using Java, J2EE (JSP/Servlets), and popular technologies such as Spring, Hibernate, MySql, Eclipse, Maven, SVN, Jersey (RESTful Web services), JQuery, Java Script, DBUnit, etc.
- Experience in using IR/data mining tools such as Solr, Lucene, and WEKA.
- Published refereed research papers at various conferences and workshops in Web data mining area.

In my spare time, I am developing a comprehensive social bookmarking & personalized recommendation service called fatNeuron (www.fatneuron.com) that allows one to bookmark, share, and discover Web pages, movies, books, restaurants, shopping products, etc.
