John Toniolli’s Summary

John Toniolli, an associate on the Strategic Communications Team of Booz, Allen, Hamilton is a senior communications executive with progressively responsible media experience. John works with multiple DoD clients focusing primarily on video production and delivery. He has written communications plans, social media, newsletters, articles, brochures and other public relations material. He also brings 22 years of military experience with him where he focused primarily on television/film/print operations and public relations. He has honed this experience in peace-time and war-time producing and directing television and film projects. He has directed videotape and Interactive Video Disc training modules for the Air Force. His leadership stood out while creating a radio and television news staff tasked with assembling a weekly 30-minute newscast communicating news to the entire Air Force and DoD. He also conceived and designed the DoD’s first weekly video news release program. He worked six years in broadcast management for the American Forces Radio & Television Service. Mr. Toniolli excelled as an Air Force and DoD liaison with the entertainment industry working on major feature films and television series including: ARMAGEDDON, THE PERFECT STORM, PEARL HARBOR, STARGATE SG-1 and various music videos.
