Robert S. "Bob" Horton’s Summary

Most of our environmental challenges are fundamentally behavioral in nature. More specifically, they are caused by our collective norms, not just personal behavior. In fact, undue focus on "individual responsibility" can prevent our studying the social systems that nurture counter-productive social behavior. I have spent over 30 years practicing, studying and teaching the normative system culture management process that responds to the need to take on whole communities as our clients. Much of this experience has come in connection with my successful operation of, the Keep America Beautiful System at the local community level.

My delight as I entire retirement is that I am getting the time to further explore this wonderful tool and finding the time to share my experience with others.
