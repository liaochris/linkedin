Thomas Piwowar’s Summary

Computer consulting, support, and training specializing in print and Internet media publishers. Computer support for print publishers (Macintosh and Windows). Training in web publishing, digital graphics, print publishing, and computer use. Offering over 60 regularly-scheduled training classes. See www.tjpa.com.

Consulting includes pre-purchase planning and advice, computer system and workflow assessment, workflow automation, publication templates (InDesign and QuarkXPress), database development (primarily FileMaker), website design (Dreamweaver and Contribute), and website coding (XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, and CGIs in Perl and PHP).

Computer support for Macintosh and Windows includes computer setup and configuration, on-going support, hardware and software upgrades, troubleshooting, emergency assistance, and support via telephone and email.

Classes cover popular programs including: Dreamweaver, InDesign, QuarkXPress, Photoshop, Contribute, Adobe Acrobat, Illustrator, Mac OS X, and Windows XP. For larger groups we provide private classes either in our classrooms or at the client's site. We also develop custom classes to meet our client's unique training needs.
