Bradley Gauthier’s Summary

A highly motivated self-starter, Bradley Gauthier graduated in three years from an elite college ranked at the time by US News as the number three liberal arts college in the Midwest. After achieving a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration with a minor in Psychology, Bradley started his career at a Fortune 200 company based out of Chicago, IL. In addition to receiving extensive training in office technology implementation and Internet marketing, Mr. Gauthier honed his sales skills through the corporation’s sales university and Huthwaite SPIN selling. In under a year he ranked among the top account managers in the United States.

Bradley decided his substantial training allowed him to move back to Lac du Flambeau to pursue his dream of entrepreneurship. Mr. Gauthier's current endeavors has him as the Founder and Senior Project Manager of Visible North, An Internet Marketing Company, as well as a Co-Owner and Director of Marketing of Gauthier Realty. Furthermore, he is actively pursuing investment opportunities locally and abroad with his real estate holding company, Eco Green Properties.

Through his endeavors and life experiences, Mr. Gauthier has found a life passion in the earth and has started the Bradley Gauthier Foundation. Ten percent of all revenue (not profit) from his businesses are pledged to eco-friendly technology investing and green education.

Recently, Bradley Gauthier has started training and consulting businesses and organizations on advanced marketing strategies, cutting-edge business methods and innovative outsourcing techniques. He has spoken on the corporate, group and university level. If you would like to contact Bradley for your speaking event or for strategic business consulting, he can be reached by phone: 715.614.1737 or email: contact (at) bradleygauthier (dot) com
