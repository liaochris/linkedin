Diane Helbig’s Summary

As a Professional Coach I work one-on-one with people to create strategies for personal and/or business success. Together, we develop a plan that will help them achieve their best, most fulfilling life. As a team, we embrace the possibilities. I focus on small business owners and salespeople who want to improve their business development strategies.

Diane Helbig’s Specialties:

Outstanding networking skills, as well as several years of successful sales experience. Intuitive possibilities thinker with the ability to see outside the situation. Genuinely interested in the success of others and believe in building long-term relationships.
