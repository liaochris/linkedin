George C. Schwelling’s Summary

I represent more than 1,000 CEOs, CFOs and COOs and other high level executives including new business development candidates who have run start up companies up to $100,000,000 and they have net worth's of $1,000,000 to $40,000,000 or more. Some are willing to work on an advisory basis and/or invest in a company they would work for or run. I charge modest recruitment fees for these candidates. (Employers pay my fees, not candidates.) I am currently working with 9 private equity firms which say that my candidates are the best they have seen in the industry and plan on using my services for future executive search needs.

We seek professional money raisers to fund our client firms. We have structured payouts to our money raisers which are far more than just getting finder fees if you are hired by our firms and are put on salary, bonuses, percentage of profits and stock but you must be willing not to ask for any fees upfront and work on some performance to bring in some money before your contracts kick in. This is not for every money raiser but those who can raise millions of dollars have no problem with working on some performance basis in return for more compensation on the back end.
