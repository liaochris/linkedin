Brian Ward CSD, IDSA’s Summary

I have six years Industrial Design / two years Architectural Design experience in Silicon Valley California, the UK and Cyprus, a Masters degree in Industrial Design from Central Saint Martins College in London.

I continue to run independent Industrial Design projects involving two Studios- WEDID-ID and Iterative Industrial Design. I am also co-director and Technical Designer for a Fashion Accessories Label: www.mariannapenga.com

Iterative Industrial Design is involved in Industrial/ Product Design projects Internationally and building a client base / body of work with Companies and Design consultancies Please do contact me if you are seeking an Industrial Design professional to work closely with.

I have also partnered with another Industrial Designer Constantinos Economides in Cyprus and are developing our own Studio Brand- WEDID-ID, developing Mainly: Furniture, Housewares, Interior Architectural Design and Industrial Design Products. We develop designs constantly and are looking to either:

-Design for Furniture or consumer products Companies.
-Find manufacturers and sales conduits for our products
-Find Furniture companies that would like to buy our designs.
