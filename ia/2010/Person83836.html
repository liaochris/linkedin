Ian Mawhinney’s Summary

Proven management professional, self-starter who rose from R&D Scientist through increasing Sales & Marketing roles to Regional and Business Unit Management. Competencies gained in the medical industry have been honed while managing the US business of a leading professional services organization that delivers on over 200 concept generation & new product development projects/year for a wide range of Medical Device and Pharma clients, from small start-ups to established multinationals.

Now using this experience to establish a global sales and marketing presence for Plasma Surgical, making the innovative PlasmaJet system available to surgeons world-wide.

A passion for engaging with customers and colleagues, together with a deep understanding of the processes & tools that drive innovation in a practical, realizable way. Outcome-focused, deadline-driven. Record of success by developing a clear vision and executing effectively through motivation of colleagues and customers.

- Sales & Marketing, including Portfolio Analysis & New Product Development
- International Business Management
- Established Businesses in Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong & China.
- Established Consultancy in Malaysia to source products for US enterprises.

I am looking forward to catching up with old colleagues, and working closely with clients and friends.
