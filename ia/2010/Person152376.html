Rosemary Somich’s Summary

I am an RN with a Masters in consultation and education now practicing as a family relocation transition expert. I coach on the telephone to family members who are moving anywhere in the USA to provide support and assistance in coping with the stress of relocation. I am enthusiastic and upbeat, viewing relocation as an opportunity for personal and professional growth. Having moved many times myself with our family over the 33 years of our marriage, I know the stresses firsthand ! I also provide coaching one-on-one to families relocating to the Raleigh/Durham area.
My perfect client : An employer who is interested in adding value to recruitment efforts by protecting their investment in relocation, increasing retention, decreasing time off for moving concerns and having a new hire focused on work. I strive to get personal addressing each family members unique transition issues to make the relocation a positive, growing experience for self and family.
