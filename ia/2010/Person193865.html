Stephane Kiss’s Summary

I strive to create best-in-class user experiences that are highly usable and visually compelling. To do so, I like to mix strict analytical thinking with wild creativity.

I hold a Master's in EECS with added visual / graphic design skills, and have about 2 years of professional experience.

I take a global approach to UI & UX Design, and combine skills in :

• Standard compliant XHTML / CSS, Javascript (Scriptaculous, jQuery), PHP / MySQL.

• Interaction & User Experience Design, Human Factor.

Focus on usability, information presentation, information clustering (grouping, hierarchization), with a taste for consistency and simplicity.
Use of clear & effective communication, affordance, and call to action, to ensure optimum conversion.

• Visual / Graphic Design.

Have worked on 40+ projects, with a great track record of producing appealing designs that combine good typography, nice color harmony, perfect composition (empty space, alignment), and that are overall clean and uncluttered.
To me, a good design is a piece of work that will perfectly match the context in which it will be used.
