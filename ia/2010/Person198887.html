    I would like you to know, I’m still as positive as the first day I stepped into the recruiting business. It, like everything else, has changed dramatically, and I’m still up for a good challenge!

I specialize only in the area of Oracle’s - JD Edwards World and One World. Don’t get me wrong, I don’t use the computer to make matches, I still do that with my own experience!
