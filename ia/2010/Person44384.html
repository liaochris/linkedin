Charles R Deyo’s Summary

With the advent of energy deregulation, customers now have greater options than ever before when it comes to their energy provider. Now, they have the power to turn on greater savings, better customer service, and the use of renewable energy. These factors have created very dynamic and exciting circumstances for customers and the entrepreneurs who would compete for their patronage.

Customers are provided electricity and gas by our parent company, who delivers more than just low rates. Our advantage over other providers is our community of Independent Associates who serve as personal energy points of contact. When you make the switch, your Independent Associate offers you tailored one-to-one customer service you're not likely to find elsewhere.

As an Independent Associate, you benefit from our easy-to-understand, heavily incented compensation plan. It has several features that make it immediately attractive to both the discerning entrepreneur and the first-time business owner.
