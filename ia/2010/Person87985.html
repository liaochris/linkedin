Jim Rea, CMA’s Summary

Most recently, I was Chief Operating Officer for Ardea Beverage Company, where I was involved from the point the product, Nutrisoda was an idea in a basement startup through the acquisition by and integration into PepsiAmericas. There I was responsible for planning, finance & accounting, logistics & supply chain and IT. As Lead Principal, I built the local IBM Global Services CPG, Retail and Manufacturing consulting group to over $47M revenue. I was part of the leadership team that built the local Deloitte & Touche Management Consulting practice.

There’s more:

• I am a Certified Management Accountant (CMA), so not only do I know how to build and read financial statements,I know cost accounting and how to use numbers to drive an efficient business.
• I am principally known for my work in CPG, retail and manufacturing companies. But I also know eBusiness. I have been in charge of web sites and ecommerce since there were web sites.
• I have gone beyond simple web channel management to implement full 360-degree CRM – call center, order fulfillment, accounting and multi-channel customer relationship management.
• I have created interactive media such as the 3D animated interactive branding sodapup.com, the Subservient Chicken of soda, and others.
• I know the future of social media interaction via the web channel. I'm in the top 5% of Twitter users on earth, at least according to http://twitter.grader.com/JimRea. I write about the best (and worst) of the current thinking about the Web and Social Media on my blog at http://JimRea.blogspot.com.

According to The Gallup Organization, my strongest attributes are that I am Strategic, a Learner and Achiever. I love ideas and have significant focus on achieving goals. You can find out more about those on my web site at http://www.Jim-Rea.com.

And I can bring all of that to help your company grow.
