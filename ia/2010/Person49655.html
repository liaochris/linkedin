David Kurns’s Summary

I have experience in creating a strategy, building a profitable enterprise, building a creative and dynamic team, and creating content across a variety of media: newspapers, magazines, Internet, video, etc.

In addition, I have led a group of Internet professionals in a startup environment. I believe I excel at building a team and focus around successful consumer communication -- no matter the platform. I have done this for consumer brands and for client brands, as well.
