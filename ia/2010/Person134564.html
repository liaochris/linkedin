Mark Schwanhausser’s Summary

I study consumer behavior and preferences and forecast trends in the banking industry for Javelin Strategy & Research in Pleasanton. Previously, I worked for nearly 26 years for the San Jose Mercury News, the bulk of that time as a business reporter covering personal finance and money. My focus then was on how we earn it, save it and keep it from the taxman. For Javelin, I am still covering money. But now my eye is on how financial institutions can empower consumers to monitor it, manage it and move it.

Mark Schwanhausser’s Specialties:

Multichannel financial services, including online banking, mobile banking, personal finance management tools, financial alerts, online account opening, "onboarding," Gen Y, online storage "vaults" and more.
