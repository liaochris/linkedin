Emilio Navarro’s Summary

I am a creative, results driven technology leader with over 25 years of professional experience in the software development and management area.

Currently I manage a team of software developers at DigitalGlobe responsible for all aspects of the execution of $30M+ development of applications geared towards the delivery and usage of commercial satellite and aerial imagery, both domestic and international via Custom Processing Systems and/or Mobile devices (PDA, smartphones, iPhone, etc.)

I have also developed applications for the iPhone which are currently in the AppStore and Windows Mobile for 3D imagery using OpenGL.

I consider myself a problem solver with a passion for technology; skilled in grasping the big picture, conceptualizing, developing, and implementing solutions, and partnering closely with business leaders and C-level executives.

I also have experience supervising and managing international teams of software developers from countries such as India, Russia, Peru, Venezuela, Argentina and Chile to help develop applications for US companies.

Knowledge of iPhone development, Objective C, C#, VB.NET, .NET, C++/OOD, Visual Basic, Java, ASP, JSP, HTML, XML/XSL, JavaScript, VBScript. Experience in agile methodologies (Scrum and XP) used to create shorter software development iterations.
