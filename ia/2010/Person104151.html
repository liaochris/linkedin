Jonathon Fishman’s Summary

Jonathon Fishman is an accomplished Senior Level Leader with diversified, in-depth experience in online media, broadband Internet and Interactive Television. With over eighteen years of strategic successes delivering unparalleled value to multimedia, digital distribution, gaming, advertising, software development and broadband interactive media entities.

For the majority of his professional experience, Jonathon has focused on early-stage start-up technology companies within emergent business categories. Utilizing his many affiliate relationships, his stellar communication skills, as well as his well planned education; Jonathon has created and conducted several popular lecture series including; “Take Charge of Your Child’s Technology” and “Make Your Small Business Feel and Act Big”, while concentrating on providing definition across various organizational areas, positioning product and service offerings that defined the respective developing markets.

His lead roles define and execute each organization’s business strategies and shape the Companies’ through a variety of growth genres. His leadership and managerial skills are best demonstrated in these areas by his valued solutions to the technical, creative, business and management challenges that these environments create. Jonathon’s net worth has been proven by his previous successes with well known company giants, such as; Intel, Microsoft, Cisco, Quokka Sports, and Sony Pictures Entertainment. Most recently, Jonathon founded and worked as CEO of Ben’s Ranch; an IT services company focused on the home and small business markets, currently serving over 400 clients. He created this business to alleviate the hassle of technology ownership, transforming the relationship of IT service and innovating electronics retailing. Ben’s Ranch was Jonathon’s fifth successful startup company; each being transitioned to new owners enabling Jonathon to further new ventures.
