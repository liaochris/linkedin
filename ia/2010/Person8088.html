Allison A. Bailes III, PhD’s Summary

Residential building science expert running a company that offers consulting, training, home energy ratings, heating & cooling load calculations, and home performance assessments. Accredited by RESNET as Home Energy Rating System (HERS) provider. Has taught HERS rater and field inspector classes. Improved performance of many existing homes through crawl space encapsulation, air-sealing, duct-sealing, and insulation. Built an ENERGY STAR house with many advanced, green features: structural insulated panels, composting toilet, greywater system, radon vent, passive solar design, mechanical ventilation, and more. Academic background in physics (PhD from University of Florida) with 9 years of teaching experience (3 at high school level and 6 at college level).

Allison A. Bailes III, PhD’s Specialties:

Building science, training, crawl space encapsulation, air-sealing, duct system performance, building envelope, insulation, heating & cooling load calculations, Home Energy Rating System, sustainable building, structural insulated panel (SIP) construction, modular housing
