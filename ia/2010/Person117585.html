Kit Sim’s Summary

Multi channel is not about merely having an e-commerce web site. It is about executing an integrated strategy and providing the customer with a seamless multi channel experience. Over 10 years of leadership success in brick-and-mortar, catalog, and e-commerce channels with some of the most successful multi-channel fashion retailers, including Victoria’s Secret Direct, Lands' End, and Aldo Group. Extensive e-commerce experience covers strategic planning, merchandising, marketing, technology solutions, processes, operations, and organizational functions. Complete in-depth understanding of Internet trends, tools, and business models. Solid background and qualifications in all core business functions. Astute motivator with excellent leadership and entrepreneurial skills. MBA/MS in E-Business. International experience. Multilingual. Passion for fashion.
