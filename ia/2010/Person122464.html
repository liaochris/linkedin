Lauren Steele’s Summary

Worked as the Public Relations intern for Luckie & Co. my senior year of college and have worked for Red Bull North America for the past two years doing marketing and promotions. Upon graduation, I became a sales assistant at Morgan Keegan in downtown Nashville and really enjoyed it but decided to pursue my sales drive further. In January 2009, I decided to take advantage of a great opportunity at the Nashville Business Journal, where I am currently the subscriber consultant for the paper. I had a great tenure with the Business Journal and learned a lot about sales. I then took a job with the Emory Group, LLC and I am working specifically with their t shirt division of the company called Geneologie. I love my job and I am excited to start helping people brand their business, whether they're looking to make shirts, hats, pens, or cups. I can put your company logo on absolutely anything!
