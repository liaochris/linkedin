Kersten Kloss’s Summary

SALES AND MARKETING CONSULTANT - WEB STRATEGIST - PROJECT MANAGEMENT - RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT, SALES and CUSTOMER SERVICE

Coming from 15 years of e-competence experience, applied to product research and development, marketing, sales and training, Kersten Kloss shares his passion for new-media web strategies, and introduces interactive website technologies to enhance a company's creativity, collaboration, work efficiency and communications to help attract new sales opportunities and solidify existing business relationships.

Entrepreneurial, success-driven product developer with a career working with small to medium sized business spanning two decades, involved in new product research and implementation, new business development, training, problem solving, and customer support. He has researched and implemented cutting edge technology tools for business owners and their employees for most of his career.

Innovative in the creation of new revenue streams, a creative promoter and a dependable troubleshooter in technical environments. Nimble and persuasive at directing a team to meet market shifts. Broad range of skills learned from working in small and medium business environments and from field projects internationally.

High level strategic technology planning for small to medium sized businesses, with experience in sales, support and training. Consulting in website architecture, communications, sales team optimizing, content management systems, CRM/SFA/ERP, collaboration, media content delivery, graphic design & security. Research & development specializing in technology innovations and trends.
