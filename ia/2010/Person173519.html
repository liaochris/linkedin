Chris Riley’s Summary

Chris Riley is a pure Technologist and recognized industry expert in document recognition, ECM, and analytics technologies. Founder of LivingAnalytics and now Senior ECM & Imaging Architect at ShareSquared, Inc., Chris lives and breathes technology and has been helping companies buy, use, and optimize advanced technologies given their business process and use case. Chris’s focus on market education has helped expose underlying problems in the acquisition and use of advanced technology and has helped both end-users and vendors mitigate the challenges to achieving ultimate success. Riley has 12 years experience in this arena, owned three software companies, and obtained several technology and business awards. He has degrees in Business Administration, Computer Science, and Mathematics, and holds certifications from the enterprise content management (ECM) trade organization AIIM as “Enterprise Content Management Practitioner (ECMp)” and “Information, Organization, and Access Practitioner (IOAp)”. Mr. Riley is a sought after speaker, and educator throughout the content gathering and delivery space.
