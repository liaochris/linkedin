Catriona Cornett’s Summary

I am a User Experience Designer with a passion for creating useful, usable, and desirable products and services. My primary interests lie in the areas of Design Strategy, Information Architecture, and Usability. I view myself as a User Experience Designer with the ability to specialize in various disciplines. My long-term career goals include leading cross-discipline user experience teams with a focus on user experience strategy.

Through my blog, inspireUX (www.inspireux.com), I share my passion for user experience by posting quotes to inspire other User Experience Designers.

I am currently a User Experience Strategist at The Archer Group, where I perform a variety of UX related tasks ranging from Design Strategy to Information Architecture and Usability.

I was previously an Information Architect at Vanguard where I worked on the Institutional Investors website. Previous to that role at Vanguard, I completed Vanguard's Technology Leadership program with three eight-month rotations within the User Experience Group.

I am a graduate of The Pennsylvania State University's College of Information Sciences and Technology. I have had experience across the field of Information Technology. I have been a Web Developer at AccuWeather and a Project Manager/Business Analyst at General Electric. My background in IT has helped me understand how User Experience fits in with business strategy and the development of products and services.
