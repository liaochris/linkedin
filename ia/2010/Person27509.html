When you think facilitator. Think Brendan O'Keefe Brendan O'Keefe - Innovation Facilitator

Brendan calls himself a 'creative generalist' or 'naive expert'.

A naive expert is an individual with a wide range of life experiences and has worked in a diverse range of industries. A multi faceted being with a desire for many experiences and a will to fulfill those desires. A highly motivated and skilled person who can bring to any project many inspirations, perspectives and opportunities for innovation.

He has 20 years experience in Idea Generation, Brainstorm Facilitation, Branding, Visual Identity, Marketing, Web Design & Development, Online Research, SEO, Mobile Applications, Reverse Mentoring, Youth Work, Pathway Planning, Business Coaching, Life Coaching, Public Speaking, Team Building, Workshop Design, Social Enterprise, Mind Mapping, Goal Setting, Music Composition, Performance, Management and Media Production.

He is known throughout his network as the 'Ideas Guy'. His obsession with expressing his creativity and passion for life long learning has brought him many blessings and wonderful experiences in the past 20 years. He has recently re-focused his pathway and he has spent the past 3 years training in the world's leading creativity techniques.

Brendan has conducted creativity workshops with young people and dozens of brainstorming sessions in the UK, Australia, USA, Philippines and online in his open brainstorming webinars.
