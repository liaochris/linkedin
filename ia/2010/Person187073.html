    Youth Forum for RTI was created on 14th October, 2007. Its main objective is to create awareness about the Right to Information (RTI) Act among the citizens of India. The reason behind creating the forum is the ignorance of Indian citizens about the Act. Although it came into force on October 12, 2005, still many people aren’t aware of its usefulness. The power of the Act lies in its simplicity which makes it quite robust and unchallenged.
