Bert Verdonck’s Summary

Bert Verdonck
I am an outstanding coach, extraordinary lifehacker and a creative mind. I also contribute through public speaking (internationally), connecting people, writing blogs, presenting a radio show, giving lectures and sharing knowledge. I am living up to my personal motto: Enjoy every day!

As founder of Genius Shortcuts (www.geniusshortcuts.com), I help people saving 2 hours/day through smart thinking, free tools and a happy lifestyle. Read my blog (bertverdonck.com) or buy my audio CD now!

I created my personal coaching programme: Create, Connect & Contribute. I coach people, especially entrepreneurs, who want to live the life of their dreams. I help them to step out of their daily pattern and inspires them to find their true passion(s), not only by giving a framework and tools, but also by providing practical (business) ideas and connections to realise them!

I am also one of the few Certified LinkedIn Trainers in the world. With Networking Coach, we launched the first LinkedIn Live sessions worldwide. Ever need info or help on LinkedIn, just drop me a message!

My background is in biometrics (fingerprint, iris scan and facial recognition) and in online communication and services. I studied Commercial Engineering at the University of Antwerp, Belgium.

More info about Bert Verdonck is available at www.bertverdonck.com
