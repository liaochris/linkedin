Cliff Yurman’s Summary

Cliff Yurman is a partner, and president, of MDstream, Inc., a digital communications company serving large corporate clients in the Pharmaceutical and Healthcare markets.

Cliff has also been a founding partner at Marketing Pro Resources, Inc., a staffing and recruiting firm specializing in marketing and communications positions.

Prior to MPR, Cliff founded Media Consulting Services, specializing in the production of video and digital media projects for Fortune 500 companies. Clients have included Citigroup, AIG, UBS PaineWebber, JP Morgan Chase, KPMG, American Express, Johnson & Johnson, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Dreyfus, Cadbury/7-UP, Knoll Pharmaceutical Company/Abbott Labs, Pharmacia and MetLife.

Prior to running his own firm, Cliff was Director, Coporate Development for Rupert Murdoch’s company, News America Publishing. Reporting to the Vice Chairman, he handled numerous communications and business functions including mergers and acquisitions analysis, industry analysis, and corporate communications.

For over five years, Cliff was employed at CBS as Financial Manager for the CBS Television Stations Division and CBS Records Division, as Editor, Standards and Practices for the CBS Television Network, and as a Writer/Story Editor for WCBS-TV/New York.

Cliff began his career with four years in the advertising industry, first as Senior Media Planner at Lintas Advertising for such clients as Citicorp, Johnson & Johnson, Bayer, Tropicana and Mennen. He later moved to Bates Advertising, serving as Account Executive on the Panasonic Consumer Electronics business.

Cliff earned a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. He completed an MBA in Finance and Marketing at Columbia Business School in New York where he earned the Distinguished Service Award upon graduation.
