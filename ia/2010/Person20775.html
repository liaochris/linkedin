Bart Cheever’s Summary

An accomplished project management executive, web and mobile startup entrepreneur, with 15 years experience creating innovative advertising, mobile applications, videogames, television and market research for worldwide brands including VH1, MTV, Old Navy, IBM, Yahoo!, Sony, Segasoft, Microsoft and Calvin Klein.

• Vice President of Production at Millions Of Us, one of Omnicom's digital agencies specializing in social media, gaming and virtual worlds. Nominated for Webby Award for viral video campaign for Fox.

• Successful entrepreneur who founded mobile and online startup: closed first round funding from Disney, oversaw product development and rollout of flagship product throughout Europe.

• A proven Online and TV producer and product manager including development of televison pilots, websites, user generated content applications, TV commercials.

• A highly effective market researcher focusing on next generation technology for top brands like General Motors.

• Personally invited by San Francisco Mayor Gavin Newsom to help organize his Digital Media Advisory Committee, promoting visual effects, animation, videogame and interactive advertising industries throughout the Bay Area.

• Leading edge adapter of technology creating one of the first ever sites to show videos on the web in 1994.

• Chosen by Wired Magazine as “one of 25 people reinventing entertainment.” Named one of "ten coolest people in America" by Faith Popcorn.

Bart Cheever’s Specialties:

Interactive Advertising, Production, Project Management and Strategy, Web and Mobile Application Development, Market Research, Entrepreneurship
