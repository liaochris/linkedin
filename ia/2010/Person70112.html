Frederick J. Esposito, Jr., CLM’s Summary

Esposito is an Executive-Level/COO with 20+ years' experience driving strategy, business growth and productivity within legal environments. He has implemented practice management initiatives and has been a key driver in transforming business strategies and operational procedures to measurably improve law firm revenues and profitability. Esposito, a strategic and critical thinker, works closely with law firm principals to analyze financial performance, create controls to reduce costs, increase billings and improve collections. He has created professional development programs addressing business needs including strategic, business and succession planning, financial and risk management, best practices and providing business training for Associates.

Prior to his leadership roles in law firm management, Esposito established his best practices for administrative and financial accountability in the public accounting and health-care industries. Having achieved the status of Certified Legal Manager (CLM) in 2006, he served as a board member and on executive committees of both the international and the Philadelphia and Albany Chapters of the ALA (Association of Legal Administrators). In 2008, Esposito was appointed the ALA Northeast Regional Communications Officer. He is an Associate Member of the American Bar Association and a member of the Law Practice Management and Business Law Sections, and has served on the Articles Review Board of Law Practice Magazine. Esposito served as editor of the ALA Philadelphia Chapter’s Newsletter and served on the Philadelphia Chapter Board as Secretary, President-Elect, President and Past-President. Esposito facilitated ALA Certified Legal Manager (CLM) Idea Exchanges and as of May 2010, is a member of the ALA CLM Certification Committee. Esposito also served on the ALA 2006 Regional Nominating Committee and provided ad hoc editorial and consulting services for the ALA Legal Management Encyclopedia during its early stages of development.

Frederick J. Esposito, Jr., CLM’s Specialties:

Esposito has worked closely with law firms on strategic planning, practice group profitability, mergers and acquisition assessments, record retention and social media policies, business development, risk management and best practices. He is a frequent speaker and author of articles on topics ranging from law office management, finance, collections, profitability models and improvement programs, strategic planning, merger integration, law firm culture and information technology.
