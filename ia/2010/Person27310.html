Brant Skogrand, APR, MBC’s Summary

As an accredited public relations professional with an advanced degree, Brant is passionate about PR and the business results it can deliver for organizations. He is committed to advancing the public relations profession through his leadership roles in Minnesota PRSA. Brant is adept at working with employees at all levels of an organization and with aspects of PR ranging from financial communications to employee communications to social media. Media relations and writing are his core strengths.

Brant Skogrand, APR, MBC’s Specialties:

Public relations, writing, media relations, financial communications, employee communications, social media, speech writing
