Annabel Hodges’s Summary

I am currently heading up the Search and Analytics division within an integrated website development and online editorial company.

With a number of years' SEO and PPC campaign management experience, I am GAP qualified and proficient in advanced Google Analytics implementation.

My daily role includes creating and implementing 360° integrated search marketing strategies, both English and multilingual. This will always include a full analytics implementation.

I pride myself on strong organisational, project management and client facing skills combined with a true love of analytics, and well-planned integrated search marketing strategies that make a real difference.
