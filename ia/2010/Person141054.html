Mena Yousef’s Summary

I am an Internet pioneer with over six years of experience in technology and emerging markets. At my previous position, I was a Software Sales Executive at Cvent, an online event management and web survey software company. During my time at Cvent, I enjoyed close relationships with organizations such as Pricegrabber.com, Verizon Business, and Amadeus. I was an early member and top producer on Cvent's web survey team. Prior to joining Cvent, I was a Business Development Associate at Fulcra Worldwide (formally, Iraqex and Lincoln Group), a strategic communications and government contracting firm. While at Fulcra, I came in on the ground floor to help Fulcra's communications and outreach campaigns in support of the U.S. political and military efforts in Iraq. I have a Bachelor of Science in Public Administration from George Mason University.

I am an expert in consultative selling and translating technical solutions into compelling business solutions. Adept presenter with talent for articulating, developing and delivering message to increase customer satisfaction and raise skill sets of team members. Proficient in Windows OS and MS Office. Familiar with Adobe Acrobat, Photoshop, search engine optimization (SEO), and search engine marketing (SEM). Expert in social media, and other web 2.0 applications. Some say my innovative ideas and strong business acumen help clients succeed.
