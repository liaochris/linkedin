Mohamed Zohair’s Summary

Mohamed Zohair is an engineer in Network technologies including Network Management and security. He has worked on several research problems in the security domain including network vulnerability analysis and Authentication techniques.

He received his bachelors degree in Computers and Systems Dept. from Faculty of Engineering- Al-Azhar University -Cairo in 2004 with excellent with honor degree, and his Network Diploma degree in Information Technology Institute ITI in 2007.

He worked in some business places –through these companies- like Sheraton Cairo Hotel, Conrad Cairo Hotel, JW Marriott Hotel, Saudi Telecoms Company STC, Innmaa Bank and SABIC.
