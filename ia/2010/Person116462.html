Kieran Manzi’s Summary

I have the basic skills to help any company communicate with the world.

I became interested in music, journalism and communications as a vocation during recent years and I have developed the media and computer skills that should jump start my career in communications.

I am quick to learn new computer programs as most of my skills are self taught. I am self taught in basic HTML programming language. I have worked with podcasts and have experience in both traditional announcing & working in a traditional studio setting.

I also have experience in digital & traditional art, I am very involved in social media and different forms of online communication. I hope to find employment in the Communication, Community, or Marketing fields.

I am ready to learn more programs and tasks as needed for any position in addition to my well rounded experience and skills.
