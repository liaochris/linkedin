Stephanie Lamanna’s Summary

ARGENTUS
FOR EVERY LINK IN THE CHAIN

You buy a pack of breath mints at the drugstore. Simple enough product, right? Hardly. The tin came from Beijing. The mints from New Jersey. Many more borders were crossed before tin and mints meet. Hundreds of negotiations and transactions. There were dozens of raw material and product distributors in the mix. Manufacturers. Procurement Managers. Third Party Logistics Specialists. Supply Chain Managers. The list goes on and on.

There's nothing simple about anything our clients do. But it takes a lot of talent and experience to make it look that way. Whether it’s Financial Services or Retail, Consumer Packaged Goods or Pharmaceuticals, we know where the talent is and we are extremely well networked to find the best career opportunities.

Our highly trained recruitment team specializes in top talent for Supply Chain, Strategic Sourcing, Procurement, Operations, Planning & Business Transformation. Additionally we have a special expertise within the Retail Sector which includes Retail Operations, Merchandising & Senior Management.

We deliver the people who keep organizations’ businesses moving. With a focus in Canada and the US our expertise is particularly attractive to busy business leaders who wish to be handled directly by individuals with senior level expertise who don't waste their time.

"When Argentus first opened its doors, Adele turned the key. Having previously worked with Bronwen, she was right on board with the same passion, strategy and results philosophy. True to Argentus’ specialized model, Adele’s expertise lies within Supply Chain, Strategic Planning, Operations, Strategic Sourcing/Procurement and Shared Services. She’s really plugged in. She knows the business inside and out – recruiting & account managing for major Consumer Packaged Goods companies, Pharma's, Communications giant and more. So when you work with her, you’re guaranteed to get the straight goods – which are nothing short of the best."
