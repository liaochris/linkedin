Theodore Cohen’s Summary

Ted Cohen is a Senior Partner of Spolin Silverman & Cohen, LLP. Mr. Cohen heads the Firm's Investment Management Group and co-heads its Business and Transactional Department. Mr. Cohen specializes in representing investment advisors, domestic and off-shore hedge funds and other private investment vehicles in connection with business and regulatory matters. Mr. Cohen is also experienced in representing investment advisors to structured finance vehicles including CDOs and CLOs. Mr. Cohen represents clients in various corporate and securities matters, corporate and venture financings, mergers and acquisitions and general commercial matters. His experience also includes advising Boards of Directors on various issues, including discharging their fiduciary duties in regard to a variety of issues and transactions. Mr. Cohen has extensive experience managing complex litigation and has successfully litigated against several Fortune 1000 companies in financial services related matters involving complex financial instruments and relationships. Mr. Cohen’s primary practice areas are investment management, financial services, private equity, securities, mergers and acquisitions and litigation.
