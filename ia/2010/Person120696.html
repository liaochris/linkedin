Lakshman Pillai’s Summary

Have 21 years of experience in WorkplaceTransformation (WT), Knowledge Management (KM) and information technology as an expert consultant, visionary leader, evangelist and entrepreneur. Founded Lpcube - a Workplace Transformation company - in 2001 with a single mission of building a complete end-to-end solution to change the way people think, learn and perform for delivering personal & organizational excellence.

Am the chief-architect of Lpcube GreatWork that includes highly integrated products such as Knowledge Management (TrueKM), Smart Collaboration, Enterprise Innovation, Talent Management, Process Optimization and Change Management.

Currently leading a team of talented and highly energized people to make our integrated platform the default choice for building a smart workplace. To successfully implement GreatWork platform and best practices, I have also developed the consulting methodologies.

I have provided consulting to organizations in various industries such as IT, insurance, BPO, telecom and semiconductor. I have helped them discover the performance gap at the fastest time possible and suggested the right approach to eliminate the inefficiency patterns at work. I have delivered thought-provoking speaches in various conferences about our unique approach for effective workplace transformation & knowledge management.

In IT, I have gained experience in project management, product development, product management, consulting, training and support.

Am a strong believer of system thinking for building high-performance workforce towards improving organizational excellence and operational efficiency. Am passionate about building global product company, leading people and transforming the workplace for the 21st century.
