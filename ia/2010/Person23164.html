Bryan Gebhardt’s Summary

Accomplished technical manager with proven track record for building and leading engineering and QA teams in fast paced, high growth start ups and medium sized companies. Demonstrated leadership skills with an ability to manage full time resources, contractors and outsourced vendors for the timely delivery of consumer applications in the fast paced mobile phone and web software industry all while maintaining reliability and scalability of the end-to-end systems. Developed mobile ringtones product that generated over 20% of the company’s revenue within three months of its deployment. Led a team in the development and deployment of a highly available server for broadcasting interactive content to over 10 million cable and satellite set top boxes. Formulated patented technology for synchronizing interactive and audio/video content. Successfully directed widely distributed development projects across multiple time zones using several outsourced vendors. Have shipped over 15 major product releases in the past eight years – products that have generated a combined $14 million in revenues.
