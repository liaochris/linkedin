Gary Baney’s Summary

Experienced at building teams of onshore/offshore developers for companies looking to increase software quality, reduce development cycle time, and control resource scalability.

Veteran project manager with experience in every level of PMO function: PMO leadership, project management process definition, tools and software implementation/configuration, usage, and training. Expert at helping companies maximize the benefits derived from PMO operation.

Experienced at initiating, negotiating, and closing strategic partnerships/contracts with organizations such as IBM, NGIT, HP, and Hitachi.

Expert at building effective teams and processes to manage technologies such as IT architecture, infrastructure, middleware, and client applications. Adept at defining optimized roles and responsibities for data center engineering, development and operations which are tuned to achieve organizational objectives.

Expert in system mangement controls (change, problem, and service levels) and effective and efficient production readiness determination.

Effective presenter of new technologies to industry analysts and prospective investors. Savvy at knowing not only what to say but how to say it. Able to create high levels of enthusiasm and buy-in around new technologies in a very short amount of time.

Adept at presenting complex topics to audiences in classrooms/seminars/forums around the world.

Experienced technical and business professor in undergraduate and graduate programs. Tenures include Judson College (Elgin, IL), the Keller Graduate School of Management (Chicago, IL) and Case Western Reserve University’s Weatherhead School of Management (Cleveland, OH)..

My company, Boundless Flight, Inc. lives by four rules:

1. We will never work for ... or with ... an a. h. again!
2. If you aren't having fun, please quit.
3. Always take care of your own first.
4. We may not always agree, but we will always work together agreeably.
