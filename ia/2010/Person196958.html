Sue Cartledge’s Summary

Half a lifetime in journalism from training on a regional daily newspaper to editing health management publications, and contributing articles and editing skills to industry and consumer magazines on health, environment, agriculture, hospitality and fashion.

Through my business, CONTEXTualise Editorial, I provide SEO web articles to a range of commercial clients, and contribute regular articles to to Hospital& Aged Care (http://www.yaffa.com.au/mags/hahmag.htm) and The Veterinarian (http://www.theveterinarian.com.au/), and online at Suite101.com (http://www.suite101.com) and Bukisa (http://www.bukisa.com)

I provide editorial support to several businesses and publications, including flooring and furniture suppliers and, hospitality publications.

Editorial positions held in recent years include:

Editor, Hospital&healthcare (now Hospital & Aged Care), Australasia's largest publication for the health and aged care industries for five years.

Relief editor, The Veterinarian, major monthly industry publication for veterinary surgeons

Contract Sub-editor, Key Media, Sydney business and financial publishers, for 18 months.

Contract sub-editor, The Irish Echo, Sydney community newspaper, for 18 months.

Sue Cartledge’s Specialties:

SEO articles for websites and email newsletters; print articles;

Interviewing experts on complex topics;

Reporting their findings in articles suitable to target readership: industry-specific or general public;

Editing clients' copy for clarity, conciseness and accuracy

Areas of expertise include:
health - mainstream and complementary; nutrition and fitness; the environment; science and engineering; agriculture and veterinary science; gardening
