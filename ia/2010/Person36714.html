  • The Power Writing Coach, Editor, Author & Founder of Writetastic Solutions

    Writetastic Solutions

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    October 2005 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    •Coaching Consultation:
    Listen and respond to your questions and help you discover ways to approach, or improve what you’ve written based on your concerns, which I will gladly clarify. Consultations usually run from 30-minutes to 1-hour at a time.

    •Evaluation and critique:
    Thoroughly read through the entire document noting any weaknesses. Prepare a summary outlining those weaknesses and suggest necessary revisions to correct and tighten your document.

    •Coaching:
    I coach you to create a clear strategy on approach. I will steer you through this sometimes frustrating, writer’s block path, as well as guide you on how to format and prepare your document for submission to agents and publishers.

    •Copyediting:
    Check standardize grammar, spelling, format, punctuation and other mechanics of style. Check for consistency of mechanics and consistency of facts. It may include marking head levels and approximating placement of art if necessary.

    •Editing - Structural:
    Focus on logical organization, rhythm and flow of content to render your document clear, concise and legible. Ensure your arguments make sense, your plot develops in a compelling fashion and your information is accurately conveyed.

    •Editing - Stylistic:
    Clarify meaning, eliminate unnecessary jargon, redundant usage, repetitiveness, polish your distinct style and adjust dialogue to suit your characters, for instance.

    •Proofreading:
    Proof design elements for accuracy and consistency, including headers, level heads, page numbering, word breaks, end-of-line breaks, page breaks, cross references, and appropriate placement of tables, artwork and line-by-line comparison with an original layout.
