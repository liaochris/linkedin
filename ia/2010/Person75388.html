Gillian Kelly’s Summary

Gillian Kelly is an international award winning resume writer and Principal of Career Edge, a premium international resume house.

Recognised for her unique ability to showcase and articulate client's value and career highlights, Jill's work has been featured in multiple books and software publications, and this year saw her selected as the winner of one of the 2007 Toast of the Resume Industry Awards. She is regularly sought by major newpapsers and respected online sites as a contributor to expertise on a variety of career topics.

Jill has written for clients across the world, both independently and for major writing houses. Her expertise has been sourced for inclusion on international panels for Career Directors International, including the "Resume Writer Certification Committee" and the "International Committee".
