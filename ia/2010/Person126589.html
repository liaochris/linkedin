Steve Litwin’s Summary

Steve Litwin is a results-driven executive with extensive business experience with companies ranging from publicly-traded to web-based start-ups.

Steve has proven experience with early stage companies from concept to profitability. Recently focusing on web-based companies and call centers, Steve has filled key roles and fueled growth at LeadPoint and Banctel. Prior to this, he was the top producing senior loan officer at Flagstar Bank-Troy South.

Earning a finance degree at the University of Michigan-Dearborn, Steve has a strong background in strategy, operations, analytics, process improvement, sales and marketing, business development and team building.
