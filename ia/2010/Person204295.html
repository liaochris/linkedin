Timothy Stasek, SPHR’s Summary

I am a Human Resources professional with 10 years of extensive HR generalist experience focusing on workforce planning, talent management and training and development. I have been recognized by my employers and by members of the community for developing and implementing innovative staffing and recruitment programs. I believe in consistently assessing the present and future talent of the organization in order to design training programs that will create the next generation of effective managers. I have been highly successful in partnering with all areas of the organization to achieve a cohesive business unit through the use of my exceptional interpersonal and written communication skills.

My work history includes over 17 years experience in the retail environment where I focused on the merchandising, operations, customer service and human resources functions at such large retailers as Wal-Mart, Target and Home Depot. This large amount of both external and internal customer service experience, recognized by levels as high as the CEO of Home Depot, has given me the interpersonal skills necessary to be an effective human resources professional.

My greatest passion is training and development; it is this long-term investment in an organization's employees that gives it a competitive edge. I have actively searched for deficiencies in knowledge, both technical and managerial, with my business partners. Once training shortfalls have been assessed, I have created and implemented programs attended by individuals ranging from hourly employees to multi-unit district managers.

In the future, I would like to use my experience and knowledge to further develop an organization's internal talent, which will lead to an increase in its success, by addressing the skills necessary for individuals to achieve their career goals. Through the use of innovative recruitment, training and career coaching programs, I want to prove myself a capable and vital business partner.
