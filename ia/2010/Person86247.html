Ivar Akkermans PMP’s Summary

I am working since 1996. As a Project Manager I focus on realizing project deliverables from which stakeholders will use the benefits. I have experience on successfully managing projects in several areas such as networking technology (LAN/WAN), IP Telephony, E-Identity, server migrations, service management and web content management. I also have experience as a bid manager for large bids (>12M USD) and interim management.

I have a broad view on ICT Management from my education in Master ICT Management and my experience from several assignments. I am therefore familiar with amongst others business cases, financials models, organizational structures, outsourcing etc.

I am a goal driven project manager and perform more then well under pressure.
I have done several project management courses, amongst others a professional project management study on a higher education level and Prince2 practitioner.
Due to the combination of my experience and education it is possible for me to fulfill several roles such as consultant, project manager in both technical and non-technical environments and (interim) manager.
