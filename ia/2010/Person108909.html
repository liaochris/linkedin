Jurgen Lust’s Summary

I am a 34 year old creative and enthusiastic IT professional based in Erpe-Mere, Belgium. My main focus is on designing and building powerful and user-friendly enterprise and web applications using Agile and Lean methodologies.

Software development has been my passion for many years. It provides me with an environment where communication, creativity and innovation are key and where I am constantly challenged to think outside the box.

I have worked on numerous software development projects, including simple web applications, large desktop clients, content management installations, enterprise service buses and full-blown EJB enterprise applications. With almost 10 years of experience, I am able to translate complex user requirements into software that is both maintainable and usable. Or, to quote one of my former colleagues: “Jurgen is the man you need when you want to get things done perfectly with no fuss.”

Jurgen Lust’s Specialties:

Scrum, Agile, Enterprise Java development (Spring, Hibernate, JavaServer Faces, J2EE, JEE 5, ESB, Groovy, Grails), Oracle, CMS, UML, Web development, Taking project teams to new heights.
