Barbara Saidel’s Summary

I am an independent consultant focusing on using technology to improve business process, quality and client service. Having spent most of my adult life as a volunteer for not-for-profit organizations, I decided in early 2008 that I would rather focus my professional efforts on using skills developed in the corporate arena to help manage or consult with not-for-profit organizations and ngos.

My most recent experience has been as CIO of Russell Reynolds Associates, an international leader in the executive search and assessment business. I also have significant prior experience in financial and operational leadership roles in law firms, investment banks, retail organizations and in middle market financial and operational consulting.

Barbara Saidel’s Specialties:

Information strategy, knowledge management, operations and financial leadership, creative problem solving, blending people and projects for organizational success. CPA NYS.
