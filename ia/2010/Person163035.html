Peter Pappas’s Summary

I started in 1982 as a CPA in Miami, Florida. I worked in the tax departments of several (then) Big Eight accounting firms before going to law school in 1986 and graduating in 1989.

I now have offices in Orlando and Tampa and my firm is a full service tax and immigration law firm. Our tax division specializes in helping people who are having problems with the IRS and/or state taxing authorities.

We also are a full service immigration law firm handling cases from simple marriage petitions to complex asylum, removal and deportation matters.
