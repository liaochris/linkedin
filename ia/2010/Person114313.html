Kendell Renee Kelly’s Summary

As Assistant General Counsel of Ocean Tomo, LLC, Kendell Renee Kelly was primarily responsible for supporting the firm’s IP Private Sales, Auctions and Corporate Finance businesses. Prior to joining Ocean Tomo, Ms. Kelly practiced law in the Chicago, IL offices of Greenberg Traurig, LLP and Sonnenschein Nath & Rosenthal LLP.

Ms. Kelly’s has practiced in all areas of intellectual property law, including patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets and unfair competition. She has been involved with prosecution before the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office and litigation, licensing, validity and infringement analysis. She has also prosecuted numerous patents in the electrical and mechanical areas, including semiconductor fabrication, kitchen appliances, Internet and other emerging technologies.

Ms. Kelly’s entertainment law experience includes brand development, preparing and negotiating endorsement agreements, master licenses, recording agreements and work-for-hire agreements. She also has experience in preparing contest and sweepstakes disclaimers and rules, and preparing operating agreements for small film companies.

Prior to law school, Ms. Kelly worked at Parallax, Inc. in Atlanta, Georgia, where she oversaw and drafted the Operation & Maintenance procedures for several hazardous sites, including the remediation, by pump and treat methods, of a site contaminated by a petroleum spill. She was also responsible for EPA checklists, and writing storm water protection and operations plans.

Ms. Kelly received a B.S. in General Science from Spelman College, a B.S. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology, and a J.D. from the Georgetown University Law Center. She is admitted to Illinois and U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Illinois bars.
