Thilo Semmelbauer’s Summary

Executive with proven track record building and driving growth in online and offline consumer businesses.

Currently leading North America Consumer Business for TheLadders.com.

From 2000 to 2004, Co-Founder and President of WeightWatchers.com, which grew to $80 million revenue and 15% EBIT in four years to become the leader in online weight loss ($200 million and 30%+ operating income in 2008). Ran Weight Watchers North America business from 2004-2006 and served as Global Chief Operating Officer from 2006-2008, overseeing $1.5B P&L.

Prior to Weight Watchers, was Principal and Consultant at The Boston Consulting Group, with a primary focus on the Consumer, Technology and Internet practices. Also managed a number of engineering and operations functions at Motorola, Inc.

Received MBA and Masters of Engineering degrees from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology / Sloan School and a B.A. from Dartmouth College in Computer Science and Engineering.

Serves as Director and Chair of Finance Committee for Action Against Hunger.
