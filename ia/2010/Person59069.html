Doug Roark’s Summary

Douglas Roark -Director of Recruiting at Paladin Consulting, Inc. a 15-year veteran in recruiting and management leadership in the IT staffing and project solutions markets. Mr. Roark is known for achieving success in the face of challenge and has built a solid reputation in the field of IT recruiting. Douglas has placed Paladin talent of various types in numerous Fortune 500 companies in multiple vertical markets across the U.S. During his 9+ years at Paladin he served in several capacities, most recently as a Lead Recruiter, where Mr. Roark provided leadership to one of Paladin’s diversified recruiting teams supporting multiple market verticals and several national accounts, Mr. Roark has achieved many Paladin achievement awards including Ace and the prestigious Star Award. He is recognized for his knowledge and expertise across the spectrum of recruiting and customer service processes.

Doug is a Board member for the Social Media group at DFWTRN www.dfwtrn.org
