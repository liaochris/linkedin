Marti McGinnis - www.martimcginnis.com’s Summary

Currently I'm creating and building the world's most effective network for equine advocacy at www.EquineConnection.org. This is serving several goals: addressing a need from a personal point of passion and allowing me to learn, test drive and harness the powers newly inherent throughout all the New Media. Last decade was all about Social Networking - next decade - Adding the Game Layer!

My immediate professional goals are to expand my New Media project roster. My extended goals are to continue to build on my work with socially and environmentally responsive and responsible organizations and help them determine and address their own promotional goals using my own FUN (Focused, Unique and Natural) Marketing methods.

My experience and joy is in discovering, uncovering and interpreting the messaging needs of people and designing tools to maximize available resources to get those messages out stylishly and, most of all, effectively.

Thanks to the internet my clients can be anywhere - we can communicate as freely as if in the same building! I get to work with amazingly talented people from San Diego to Chicago, Philadelphia and beyond.

My philosophy: "As you believe - so it becomes.".
