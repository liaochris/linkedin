Stacey Inglis’s Summary

For over twenty years, I have built a career in Advertising, Marketing and Public Relations. Early on in my career, many of the positions I held were concentrated in specifically one of the three. This allowed me to really dig in and get to know that particular discipline. My most recent career positions afforded me the opportunity to combine all three of those disciplines.

Out of the For-Profit industries I have explored; soft drink, automotive, radio, housewares/smallwares and snack foods, I really could not choose a favorite. Each industry has offered me professional and personal growth, challenges and invaluable experiences. My experience has been in predominately entrepreneurial companies, the last one a publicly held company. The entrepreneurial environment suites me best, as I like to be involved in all levels, at creating and building and am very hands on by nature. However, I am comfortable in corporate environments as well.
One of my greatest strengths is that I thrive in chaos and prevail over the undertow of serious multi-tasking! One of my former bosses once told me in a review, that I was "one of the best band leaders he'd ever worked with in his career."

Looking forward to working with you!
Stacey
