Martine Parry’s Summary

Clients include: Activision, GEC Marconi, Barclays Bank, NatWest, Tiga - the games developers trade assoc., BNFL, City Banks, ITI Techmedia, Tangentix .. more to add

I am a highly active, pragmatic, creative and focused business professional who has worked across the digital industries now for 24+ years (games/ MMOs, IT infrastructure, CGI, visualisation, intelligent systems/ AI, pattern recognition, computer vision, online and bespoke billing systems, performance support systems, training & communications).

I have been involved in major market development: product launch, winning, scoping and delivering on many £multimillion projects .. and have been personally responsible for £8+ million in sales of software tools and services, whilst I have been responsible for generating £20m+ in market growth.

I have under-taken major market research projects in the MMO market, 3d model compression, virtual goods, intelligent systems, web 2.0 +, serious games .. and many others.

I have produced successful key conferences attracting top-tier speakers whilst making profit and building year on year.

I have mentored a number of start-ups.

I have an excellent network of funding organisations, VCs and angel investors.

I have an excellent network of professionals across games, media, public sector and corporate enterprise organisations.

I have an algorithm-based IP technical background, including novel technologies underpinning integrated systems at medium - high levels of the OSI comms model and so am well-accomplished in delivering complex value propositions from infrastructure integration through to impact on users and change management.

I produce conferences and networking events where people share ideas, learn and do business. I provide due diligence, introduction to investors and funding channels and can advise about best practice in channel marketing & sales and project management. I also provide advice on marketing, sales, pre-sales & bid-writing
