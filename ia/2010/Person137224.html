Matthew Silverman’s Summary

Matt Silverman is a creative professional with a hand in numerous project-arts, including creative and professional writing, video production and editing, web design, pop music composition, studio recording, and live performance. He is a published writer (featured on Mashable.com and Newsday) and the creator of camerashymedia.com, a video production website that provides artistic editing, compositing, and filming services for families, companies, schools, and special events.
