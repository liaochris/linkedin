Ed Stillman’s Summary

30 plus years working in Fortune 100 Company. Masters in Management with a focus on small to medium size private (for profit) companies. Coaching focus is on assisting members in becoming better leaders, making better decisions and ultimately growing their revenue and profit. My clients are focusing on creating a better balance between business commitments and personal relationships.

My personal goal is to assemble two peer groups in Austin Texas with 16 members in each, meeting on a monthly basic to sharpen their saws and challenge their decisions. One-to-one coaching and access to thousands of white papers via the internet and Vistage International's Gateway complete my service.

Ed Stillman’s Specialties:

Business executive development, advising on most small to medium size company issues. M&A, HR, Sales, Mktg, Branding, Time Mgnt
