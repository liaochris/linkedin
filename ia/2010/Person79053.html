Guy Jutras’s Summary

Feb 2010: I just delivered my latest product, a capillary thermal pre-conditioner for use ahead of a gas chromatograph. Can I help you with you project?

I have over 20 years of experience in developing electronic products. In some cases, it's been a small circuit to improve an existing system, in other cases it's been a complete new product for a startup company.

I offer a free consultation to anyone who has a question about electronic development. I'm always interested in learning more about what companies in the area are doing, so this a good way to make contacts. I've also met some of my best customers this way because they get a chance to see what I'm about without any obligation on their part.

Guy Jutras’s Specialties:

At one time or another, I've been involved with every phase of bringing something to life, from the initial concept, coming up with technical specifications that make sense, analog & digital design, software and firmware, PCB layout, industrial design, enclosure design, BOMs & purchasing, manufacturing, documentation and manuals. I also have practical experience with the business side of things, having been involved with some high-tech startups.
