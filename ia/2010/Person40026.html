Christy Barksdale’s Summary

As a graduate of Ohio University's E.W. Scripps School of Journalism, I have a passion for effective and thoughtful communication.

I joined Cleveland-based PR 20/20, an inbound marketing agency and publications relations firm, as a consultant in January 2010.

Previously, I was an account manager for Penton Custom Solutions, a division of Penton Media. Penton Media is the largest independent B2B publishing company; Penton Custom Solutions specializes in creating and delivering strategic communications and content marketing programs for customers. As an account manager, I am solely responsible for managing all aspects of execution for content marketing initiatives. Prior to my role as account manager, I was an account coordinator, acting as the "go to" for the account managers in order to execute client programs.

Undergraduate experience includes a position as the SoBe energy drink representative for Ohio University (through RedPeg Marketing), where I developed and implemented marketing objectives to incorporate SoBe energy drinks into students' college experiences. I was also an editorial intern for West Penn Allegheny Health System in Pittsburgh, where I served copywriter, editor, proofreader and research roles for internal communications vehicles, as well as developed external media promotions.

Content marketing, project management and execution, budget development and management, marketing strategy, writing and copy editing, hard-working and forward-thinking, energetic attitude, eager to learn.
