Robert Wagner’s Experience

  • Director, Worldwide Corporate Industry Analyst Relations

    Xerox Corporation

    (Public Company; Printing industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    As director of worldwide corporate industry analyst relations at Xerox, Bob leads the corporation's strategy and global engagement with key industry analyst firms, builds the Xerox brand through interactions with industry influencers from international analyst firms, and serves as the company's chief spokesperson to the worldwide industry analyst community. He was appointed to this highly visible, leadership role at Xerox in January 2009.

  • Vice President, Creative Services Business and Premier Partners Global Network

    Xerox Corp.

    (Public Company; XRX; Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 1999 — January 2009 (9 years 8 months)

    As vice president of Xerox's Creative Services Business, Bob served as company's digital printing evangelist for agency and design services professionals. In this role, the former Young & Rubicam executive exposed tens of thousands of designers and print production pros to the power and potential of digital print as a medium for doing great work for their clients.

    Wagner also led the Xerox Premier Partners Global Network of 800 vanguard, Xerox-equipped graphic communications providers who offer their digital printing and fulfillment services to marketers, designers and other savvy print buyers at www.xerox.com/xpp. Global membership grew 33 percent under his leadership of the program.

    In January 2008, Wagner was named by Graphic Design USA magazine as one of its “People to Watch in 2008.” www.gdusa.com/issue_2008/01_jan/feature/p53.php

    Wagner is the author of numerous articles for leading graphic communications industry publications. He created the popular Xerox primer, "A Formal Investigation Into The Urban Legends of Digital Color Printing." www.xerox.com/creative/urbanlegends

    He also is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and events.

  • Adjunct Faculty

    Rochester Institute of Technology

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    March 2005 — May 2008 (3 years 3 months)

    Wagner is an adjunct faculty member at the Rochester (N.Y.) Institute of Technology (RIT), where he teaches a course in multimedia strategies at the School of Print Media. Wagner holds an MBA in marketing from RIT. He is on hiatus at RIT.

  • Prior Corporate Communications Assignments

    Xerox Corporation

    (Public Company; XRX; Information Technology and Services industry)

    April 1991 — May 1999 (8 years 2 months)

    Prior to his current corporate communications role at Xerox, Wagner ran integrated marketing communications and corporate human resources communications functions for the company. He led the public relations campaign to announce the landmark Xerox DocuTech Publishing Series, a system that many industry observers have credited with igniting the digital print-on-demand revolution.

  • VP / Account Director

    Young & Rubicam

    (Public Company; WPP; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1985 — April 1991 (5 years 11 months)

    Wagner began as an account executive and steadily was promoted to a vice president and account director of the Xerox account. In fact, he was so successful leading his focused account, Xerox, he was invited to join the company.
