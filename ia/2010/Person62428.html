Edwin Hincapie’s Summary

Motivated, dedicated, and results-driven professional with experience in General and Hispanic marketing, account management, consumer promotions and experiential marketing. Acknowledged as a goal-oriented leader capable of identifying and capitalizing on market opportunities to provide strategic solutions to accelerate expansion, increase revenues, and maximize profits. Experience in managing national General and Hispanic market product, retail, sports and entertainment programs.

Bio
Whether it’s an A-list concert event by a top recording artist or street team distribution of a morning breakfast drink, Edwin Hincapie will mount and deliver events that are equally engaging to consumers and surpass client expectations.

Edwin provides leadership to enrich the engagement experience for clients and their consumers, developing and executing sports, entertainment, retail and consumer promotions and event marketing.

Having worked with such clients/Brands as Sprint, Verizon, Kraft, Diageo (Johnnie Walker, Buchanan's, Ketel One), Nissan, Coors Light, Bostitch/Home Depot, Caribbean Tourism Organization and Tecate provides Edwin with a well rounded point of view and a variety of experiences.

Edwin has successfully developed sponsorship initiatives, brand partnerships, product launches, consumer promotions, retail/POS programs and national sports and entertainment tours/programs and digital/social media initiatives.

Edwin has managed budgets of up to $20 million and has consistently worked to grow the business of the agency he serves and its clients. He attributes his success to his mentors and former account directors and department heads.

Edwin has a bachelor of science in international marketing and finance from Fordham University, Bronx, New York.
