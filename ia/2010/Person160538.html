Paul Bennett’s Summary

I have two jobs. I write articles for National Geographic, National Geographic Adventure, Smithsonian, and Architectural Record. I also co-founded and manage Context Travel (www.contexttravel.com), a network of 120+ scholars and specialists who lead "walking seminars" of major world cities. We have branches in Paris, London, New York, Rome, Florence, Venice, Naples, and Istanbul. Travel + Leisure magazine has named us one of the top tour operators in Europe.
