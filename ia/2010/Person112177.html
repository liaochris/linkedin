Kathy Colborn’s Summary

I bring 15+ years of highly developed networking, persistence, intuition and integrity to the table; whereby, I am able to effectively connect people together to make great things happen. I'm extremely cognizant of my network, as well as my network's network (a trait I believe separates me in the executive recruiting field,) and I do everything I can to ensure the stability and synergy of these relationships moving forward.

I joined The Bowdoin Group because of their reputation, professionalism and values, which are directly in line with mine above. In the end, it's about the ongoing relationship, but it really just boils down to doing a good job the first time; putting the right people together at the right time, being respectful, and making sure they'd want to work with you again, or refer you, given the chance.

I cannot say enough about what I do - I absolutely love my job.
