John Sotomayor’s Summary

I am an action-oriented, results-driven and creative journalist, copy-editor and manager as president of my own media business. My career highlights span interviewing celebrities including Donald Trump and Mark Burnett, producer (2004) to traveling the world on assignment to cover the Parliamentary elections in Islamabad, Pakistan (Feb. 2007) and the World Expo in Shanghai, China (Oct. 2010).

I started in the legal field having attended law school in Washington DC, and worked for top tier NYC law firms first as a paralegal then as the supersivor of the litigation paralegal department for 10 years.

I made the switch to journalism in 2004 working for ETV as an on-line commentator then editor. As editor, I reviewed, edited, and published articles submitted by freelance writers from across the nation. I also courted and managed industry contacts such as producers, network executives, and reality show contestants from all TV networks in New York City: ABC, CBS, and NBC.

Since then, I worked in various forms of print media including daily and weekly newspapers, local and national magazines, and virtual print as a daily/weekly commentator. I wrote and developed conceptual design of feature, advertorials, personal profiles, editorials, weekly columns and news reports.

I have written and published in Spanish for Voz Latina, El Reportero Latino, and Conexion Latina newspapers.

I received my Bachelor's degree from the University of Rochester in Economics and Political Science with a Certificate of Management from the Simon School of Business concentration on Finance/ Marketing.

This diverse background in business, law and media allowed me to become a seasoned professional with outstanding project planning, execution, monitoring and resource balancing skills with the ability to support multiple simultaneous projects.

I have a proven ability to deliver substantial revenue, productivity and quality improvements through well-managed, on-time projects.
