Clive Gott’s Summary

I have been in the professional speaking industry exactly ten years on 10th June 2009. And despite what we are told about the recession I am enjoying my best first quarter of any year since I started.

My material is for the most part experiential in that I challenge myself both physically and in my business to achieve greater things for myself. This gives me the credibility and authenticity (something that is missing in a greater part of my industry) to speak to my audiences about going on when things seem tough or even impossible.
I have been fortunate enough to speak in some of the most amazing places on this planet of ours including India, Pakistan, The USA, The UAE and most of Europe. At a recent conference I was subject to immediate feedback from my audience by way of remote pads which each of the 250 delegates had been given. Three percent did not find me inspirational, five percent could not decide whether I was or not and 92 percent said that they felt motivated and inspired after listening to me speak for an hour.

Having reached ten years as a professional speaker my goals are presently under review. I can share one major goal with you though. On my 55th birthday on 11th August 2013 I will be in a position physically, emotionally, spiritually and financially to make any decision I chose to make regarding my future.

Clive Gott’s Specialties:

I am a humourist, that means that I am funny. I can Inspire and motivate an audience and still leave them entertained and amused.

I stand by my reputation to deliver whatever it is that I have promised to deliver. I am almost unique in that I never send an invoice until after I have spoken, not even for expenses. That way if you don't think I provided value for money you need not pay me. In ten years I have received every invoice I have ever issued.
