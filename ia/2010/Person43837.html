Courtney Spencer’s Summary

Having graduated from Bentley University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and a minor in Political Science, I have been active in Social Media for over the past 3 years. I am currently the Marketing Associate at Black Duck Software!

For the past 6 months I have also been a constant news contributor to the Boston Examiner on various aspects of Social Media. My involvement with the Boston Examiner over the past 6 months has enhanced not only my social media skills, but as well as my fascination with all elements of news reporting and information dissemination.

I have also worked as a researcher for the social networking, non-profit website, SocialVibe. At SocialVibe, I worked to concept beta test products and Facebook application for a new social media platform that enables consumers to promote, distribute and support brands and charities via social networks such as Facebook, Myspace, Twitter and various Blogs.

I have completed a Marketing Internship with the non-profit Boston Ballet, maintaining and updating their website, designing new logos and flyers and organization of advertising and promotion of various annual Ballet events. I also worked as a public relations intern for the New York based music firm, Sophist Productions. At Sophist, I conducted marketing research as well as produced press release statements and authored music review articles for their website.
