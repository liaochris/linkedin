Alberto D'Ottavi’s Summary

I work at the intersections of technology, Internet, journalism and media, actually with a focus on Web 2.0 and Social Media. So I can say I'm pretty aware of what's happening in the near future of the digital society

I tell the stories I learn from there as journalist, blogger, author, public speaker and teacher

I analyze the models of how things work digitally, and I transfer these as research and strategic consultancy. I help established companies to move in the digital innovation space, and new initiatives to grow

I also helped foster the idea of a new and open entrepreneurship as a member of First Generation Network, an association of first generation entrepreneurs

I'm now mentoring some new web initiatives
