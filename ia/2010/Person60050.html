Nick Poulios’s Summary

Over 15 years of increasing responsibility and achievements in the pharmaceutical industry in the areas of health outcomes research, pricing and reimbursement strategy, patient/payer access and public policy.

Founder, VP and Head of Global Pricing and Reimbursement, Patient Access/Public Policy for Elan Pharmaceuticals specializing in MS, acute chronic pain and Alzheimer’s therapies. Responsible for a team of 17 field and office based professionals.

Founder of Medical Outcomes Research and Economics (MORE) for Baxter Bioscience responsible for strategy development and directing health outcomes research and pricing & reimbursement with emphasis on blood products, biosurgery, renal and vaccines businesses, worldwide; developed healthcare management tools, benchmark-efficiency measurement tools and pharmacy intervention models for health systems to improve health care efficiency.
