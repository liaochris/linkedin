Shanna Trenholm’s Summary

Writer, visionary, and idea-girl. Do you need a dose of inspiration for your small business of nonprofit? Allow me to provide a fresh perspective.

I offer consultation and training in social media, branding, and other communication platforms.

I'm passionate about a variety of topics and work in diverse arenas--just ask. Books, ebooks, web copy, collateral copy, magazine and feature articles--If it needs to be written, I can write it!

In addition to general writing, I specialize in grant writing, research, board and staff training; with experience in the arts/culture, education, literacy, health care, sustainability, and more!

Yoga Alliance Certified [RYT 200] yoga instructor. Upcoming yoga and writing retreats--stay tuned!
