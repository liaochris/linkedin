Tarin Gessert’s Summary

I have professional experience in a variety of fields, and I'm hoping to expand! I work in the publication industry with The Wake student magazine, published bi-weekly. I run the overall production. I also have experience in Marketing and Communications from my job as a Marketing Assistant at the Learning Abroad Center, where I've learned a lot about work flow.

Beyond design, I have experience working with children, which has made me a very responsible person. I started off as a Playground Leader for the Parks and Recreation Dept. in Eau Claire, WI, and moved on to working as a Student Childcare Worker at the U of MN. Summer 2009 I worked as a Summer Camp Counselor at the U. With all of these jobs, it was my responsibility to not only provide a safe environment for the youth, but also to be creative and come up with engaging activities to keep them active.

I have also been volunteering with Big Brothers, Big Sisters for over 3 years now. Their mission is to help children reach their potential through professionally supported, one-to-one relationships with mentors. On these same lines, I am a volunteer with AIGA's Design Ignites Change program as a mentor to a high school student interested in design. We have spent that last 6 weeks or so designing a billboard around the idea of tolerance.

All of these experiences have taught me many things. I've learned extreme responsibility, time management, problem solving, creative thinking, and communication skills.

But now, I'm hoping to expand my experience with a graphic design internship. I want to work in a firm with designers and be creative, and collaborate with my colleagues.
