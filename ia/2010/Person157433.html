Olivier Lorrain’s Summary

I'm a consultant in human factors engineering contributing to designing more usable software / Websites and other complex systems to achieve higher human performance standards.

When we talk about usable or ergonomic interfaces/systems, it's mostly about human performance. From a business stand point, improving human performance will result in lowering costs (fewer organizational accidents and errors) and by increasing profits while using these usable interfaces/systems.

I'll be glad to help you during your development process.

Olivier Lorrain’s Specialties:

Interactive designs, usability strategies, usability testings, heuristic evaluations, card sorting, field observations, workload assessment, task analysis, safety culture assessment, ebusiness
