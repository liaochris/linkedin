Sylvie Renault’s Summary

Art Director, Web Specialist, Creative Problem Solver & Multimedia Designer (Web, BTv, Software & Digital Terrestrial Television Applications), I've work like a Creative Director at Touch srl, Valter Casini & Partners creative lab.
I’m graduated in “Graphic & Illustration for Advertising” at “Istituto Europeo di Design”.

My work it's my passion and I've transform it into a form of expression, eclecticism, originality, creativity and research.

I move easily from painting to publishing industry to ICT and I'm specializing in innovative cross-discipline solutions.

I started on publishing at headings like “Il Mucchio Selvaggio”, Rock & Politic magazine, and others like “Satellite”, “Dvd Digital Magazine” and “Dimore”.
I’ve been working for a long time in ICT world, learning the technological secrets of my profession.
I love multidisciplinary design like events & catering’s organization (Jazz Festival - Lago Maggiore), gadget design for animals rights non-profit association “Associazione Volontari Canile di Porta Portese”, publication & graphic design for the information web site “God-a” Goccia D’acqua and for the humanitarian demonstration “Diritto al Futuro!” (Future Rights!) featuring the painter Simona Albini and the Jazz Brasilian musician Iramar Amaral.
I collaborate with “Cadeau Cultural Association”, "Merlino Viaggi" and Sitcom Media srl.
I've took part of the Food Design 5 (Salone del gusto - Lingotto - Turin),from 10th of october until 28th, with “Play4Food” an experimental performance art's installation.
This is the project site: http://www.play4food.eu
This is the virtual Performance/Web Community: http://www.play4food.it

You can find all my new projects on the site http://www.play4.org

Or you can see all my old work on my blog/portfolio http://www.artmultimediadesign.com
