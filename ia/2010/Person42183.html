Jason Ellis’s Summary

Growing up in a family that has had a real estate brokerage firm since before he was born has helped Jason understand the TRUE value of a wise real estate decision. Jason has been involved in almost every aspect of real estate. After graduating from college, Jason went into real estate appraising (after all, he had a built in referral source). Several years later Jason moved to South Florida, where as a licensed real estate and mortgage broker he worked with everyone from investors to people looking for their first home.

In the fall of 1998 an opportunity opened up in Myrtle Beach. After weighing all of his options and investigating the real estate market in Myrtle Beach, Jason made the decision to move. “This has turned out to be one of the best choices I have ever made”, says Jason. “The market here is flourishing and has an unbelievable amount of room for growth.”

It is these experiences that has made Jason’s knowledge of real estate an invaluable service for his clients.
