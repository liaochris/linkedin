Ryan D. Brown’s Summary

Ryan D. Brown is the President and Founder of Lending Group, a correspondent lender and mortgage brokerage company headquartered in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida.

This year, Lending Group celebrates its 5th year anniversary. This celebration and continued success of the company is driven by Ryan and his team of professional mortgage planners delivering exceptional customer service while providing high level lending strategies to clients, financial advisors and realtors.

As a Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist, Ryan has extensive financial knowledge and expertise regarding the tax and financial planning implications of various mortgage and real estate investment strategies. Ryan has a proven track record of adding significant value in the mortgage planning process by integrating a client's mortgage, debt and home equity strategy into their overall financial plan.

Ryan is actively involved in supporting and encouraging entrepreneurship across a spectrum of industries. Ryan helped establish the Lighthouse Entrepreneurs Network, which is a membership-based group designed to facilitate the exchange of ideas by bringing together successful entrepreneurs, emerging entrepreneurs and other professionals interested in the advancement of entrepreneurship. Additionally, Ryan is on the advisory board of business development for Flight 36, a ministry designed to make an impact in the lives of fatherless boys, single moms and the Kingdom of God.
