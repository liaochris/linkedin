Lee Uber’s Summary

I am a Los Angeles-based voiceover talent in commercial, promo, and narration fields. Contact me for a copy of my demo, or visit my website at LeeUberVO.com.

Also, I'm a broadcast technician specializing in Master Control operations at the Disney/ABC Cable Television Group in Los Angeles, CA (home of the Disney Channel, ABC Family, Disney XD, and SoapNet...If you're watching any of our channels, I'm the one making sure it's getting to your TV!). I have more than 8 years experience in broadcasting, including nearly a year and a half at the cable network origination level as an MCO for the Discovery Networks and several years at ABC, CBS, and PBS network affiliate stations in Kentucky and California.

I am also a licensed and certified advertising sales broker for the RingPlus phone service, providing free long distance and international calls which are supported by ringback tone advertising. For more information, and to advertise your business with RingPlus, contact me!
