Bren Letson’s Summary

My goal is to successfully conclude "Ideal" transactions. By "Ideal", I seek deals where the seller receives full price based on the true value of the business. The buyer is willing to pay full price because, in the Ideal Transaction, the buyer has a clear vision of how the business can be taken to a higher level. Thus, the buyer is buying growth. I believe that this approach creates satisfied sellers and buyers in the short term and, more importantly, repeat customers in the long term. My practice is, therefore, about creating relationships with buyers and sellers that extend beyond any given transaction. If we are successful helping a buyer purchase a business and they successfully grow that business, then when it comes time to sell, the choice of broker is obvious. If we are successful in getting a seller full value and if that seller decides to buy another business, again the choice of broker is obvious. For me, that would be ideal.

I founded The Letson Agency, Nashville’s VR Business Brokers office in June of 2002. The office has been recognized as one of the top VR offices nationally. Before affiliating myself with VR, I spent 20 years as Technical Director with Northrup Grumman directing multi-million dollar systems integration projects. Prior to that, I founded, built and sold two software services firms. I am a graduate of Georgia State University, with a BA in mathematics and economics.
