Jeff Clark’s Summary

Jeff Clark, CEO, co-founded TrackCost Corporation in April of 2008 and has served as CEO since inception. Jeff is also Managing Partner of CMG and Speculate, LLC a sales and marketing consultancy. Since 1989, Mr. Clark has held a number of senior sales and marketing positions for consulting and technology companies in the areas of research, direct marketing, CRM, wireless messaging and location services, and software applications. At Market Probe, he was responsible for an average annual growth rate of 200% for five years and at TeleCommunication Systems for $10M in incremental growth. Clients have included: Chase Bank, Sprint, Telus, Sabre, EDS/GM, P&G, HP, Hallmark, AT&T, S&P, Mastercard, Fidelity Investments, IBM, Wachovia, J.I. Case, and Black & Decker. In addition, he has held a number of leadership positions with trade associations and community service organizations including President-elect of the American Marketing Association, Executive Committee of Milwaukee First in Quality/Metropolitan Milwaukee Association of Commerce, Board of the Wisconsin Quality Network, Chair of the After-Purchase Quality and Bank Industry Groups of the American Society for Quality, and Executive Director of LoveOurLambs, a Christian non-profit organization focused on helping single-parent families. Jeff has frequently spoken at national conferences. Mr. Clark received a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) in Marketing and MIS from the University of Wisconsin.

Jeff Clark’s Specialties:

Assessing organization’s readiness to change, Valuing accounts (current/potential revenue & profitability), Formalizing & integrating the Voice of the Customer (VoC), Facilitating strategic planning sessions, Leveraging technology to optimize processes, Developing superior designs for (CRM) systems, Establishing integrated selling models, Establishing SAM structures, Validating & modeling metrics in strategic scorecards, Wireless-enablement of applications & field staff, etc.
