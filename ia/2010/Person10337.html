Amy Radin’s Summary

Amy is a results-oriented marketing and digital executive and general manager with a successful track record developed through significant consumer business roles at Citigroup and American Express, leading change and leveraging company assets, technology capabilities and direct-to-consumer marketing analytic, communications and engagement tools to drive revenue growth and P&L efficiency. She has held both global and US-oriented assignments.

Amy applies over 20 years of direct marketing, digital, innovation and people management expertise to drive development and execution of channel, product and segment programs that meet customer needs with demonstrated ROI. She is a "nuts and bolts" visionary, team leader and partner, advancing and adapting business models to capitalize on trends to sustain and enhance results.

Amy actively gives back to the community through her service on the board of Healthcare Chaplaincy, a leading NYC not-for-profit, as a fellowship sponsor for community service-based internships in partnership with the NYU Stern Graduate School of Business, and as an active alumnae of Wesleyan University.

She has been recognized by US Banker, BusinessWeek, NYU Stern and Wesleyan for her corporate, leadership and community service achievements and contributions.

Amy Radin’s Specialties:

Direct-to-consumer and digital marketing, and customer relationship management; emerging channel strategy, analytics, and adoption; business model, segment and product innovation; change management. Industry focii include consumer financial services and media, and deep expertise in payments (both network and issuer),
