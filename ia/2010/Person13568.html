Anita Carney’s Summary

I am a national executive search professional with 6 years direct hire sales and recruiting experience. I work in the financial sector, Life Sciences and Engineering, and I work with both technical and business development roles. Clients I represent have local, regional and international positions.

For my clients, I work diligently to understand the criteria that apply to their needs. I will customize my search to focus on what is most important to them in a candidate. With my candidates, I ask for an overview of the kinds of positions they are qualified for, and ask them to think about what a company needs to look like to be a good fit for their goals. This can include thinking about a company's structure, market position, culture and size. Together we develop a profile that I screen against my client needs. Both client and candidate can be assured that I work confidentially on their behalf until that time that both parties have interest in one another.
