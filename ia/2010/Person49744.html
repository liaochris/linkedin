Dave Minella’s Summary

A media relations specialist, Dave has launched products, promoted services and conducted effective national and international business-to-business, consumer and media outreach, securing coverage and maintaining relationships with major news networks, consumer and trade media and blogs in a host of vertical markets including DIY, food/restaurant, industrial design & fabrication, motorsports and consumer goods. He has placed clients in publications such as The Wall Street Journal, The Early Show and The New York Times. His clients' products and projects have been named a Hottest Tool by Better Homes & Gardens magazine and a Top Business Idea by Springwise.

He has touched many aspects of public relations including corporate and employee communications, community relations and event management, and he has gained experience in many other marketing disciplines such as social media management, copywriting, graphic design and media planning. In more than eight years as a PR professional, Dave has represented 100kGarages.com, Eaton Corporation, Georgia Pacific, Highmark Blue Cross Blue Shield, Lowes Home Improvement, McDonald's and Popeyes restaurants, Point Park University, PPG, The Senior Olympics, ShopBot Tools and Whirlpool Corporation. He has developed and implemented marketing plans and managed sponsorships with NASCAR, ARCA and other motorsports organizations.

Prior to beginning his communications career, Dave served eight years in the United States Army and Army Reserve as a UH-60 helicopter gunner, infantry squad leader, weapons specialist, tactical analyst and communications specialist. He served his active-duty and reserve tours with the 82nd Airborne Division based at Fort Bragg, NC.

Dave earned his bachelor of arts in communications studies and history from the University of North Carolina in Wilmington.
