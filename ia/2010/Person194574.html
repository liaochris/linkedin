Stephen Hall FIRP’s Summary

Stephen is a professional Managing Director, Sales Director and Management Consultant with over twenty years Executive Search, Sales, SAP and Recruitment experience, who is dedicated to the highest professional standards. He adds significant value to employer and client companies alike. His strong management and organisational skills have helped in the personal placement of over eight hundred IT and Management Consultancy professionals since commencement in the recruitment business. He is a specialist in the search arena, on an international basis. He has managed and built large teams of recruiters and researchers in both the contract and permanent markets and was previously billing in excess of three hundred and fifty thousand pounds per annum personally, although today, his main function is business management, team building, training and business development. His strong training skills have made significant contribution to team building and development of recruiters over the last 12 years, during his time in senior management. He has built three SAP recruitment businesses from scratch since 1997 with combined sales of over 6 million pounds. He has a strong understanding of business management and corporate finance. He is still hands on and has placed people right up to the present day. He sits on several boards as an executive and non executive Director.mySAP, SAP AM, SAP AbAP , SAP APO, SAP Basis, SAP BBP, SAP BI, SAP BW, SAP CATT, SAP CFM, SAP CO, SAP COPA, SAP COPC, SAP CRM, SAP CS, SAP EBP, SAP EC, SAP EHS, SAP EP, SAP FI, SAP FS, SAP HR, SAP ICM, SAP IM, SAP LE, SAP LO, SAP MM, SAP OIL, SAP PLM, SAP PM, SAP PP, SAP PS, SAP PSM, SAP QM, SAP RE, SAP RM, SAP SD, SAP SEM, SAP SRM, SAP TR, SAP WM, SAP XAP, SAP XI, ECC 6.0.
