Cleaning Agency’s Summary

The Cleaning Agency has, over the past period provided a professional and efficient domestic cleaning service. Supplying the whole of London with quality domestic cleaners who fulfil the high quality of excellence and personalised house cleaning service, that is expected by our clients.

The Cleaning Company provides trustworthy and responsible domestic and carpet cleaners. We ensure that the quality of our cleaning remains at a high standard by constant supervision as well as liaison with our clients. Approximately 95 % of our clients have domestic cleaners who are key holders.

We are a vibrant domestic cleaning company and are proud of our exemplary achievement in a very competitive market place. Our Cleaning Agency London was founded less then ten years ago with the intention of creating and developing a highly successful Cleaning Services in London UK. We provide domestic and house cleaners for all London areas.
