Anne Kiely’s Summary

I'm a Connector. I can connect your business with your customers - past, present and future. Do you want more customers for your business? Public Relations - both online and offline - can help you to get your story out to potential clients or customers.

Offline work woild include press releases, photo shoots, sponsorship deals, newsletters, articles for publication, event management (e.g. launches, business anniversary celebrations), business networking, loyalty schemes and lots more!

Online PR is about social media including LinkedIn, Facebook business pages, Small Business Can, IGO People but also about blogs (your own or others), online directories - and it's a growing list.

My clients are featured on my blog and Facebook business page.

Anne Kiely’s Specialties:

Targeted promotion of SMEs, including social media promotion, networking, press releases, copywriting, proof-reading, media relations, newsletters and e-zines.
