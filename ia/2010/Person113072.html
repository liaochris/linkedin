Katie Hicks’s Summary

Bilingual environmental scientist focusing on statewide environmental justice issues. Specializing in water justice, ecology, environmental chemistry and resource interpretation, with interest in environmental education or interpretation for public lands, open spaces, and interpretive centers in the Southern Appalachians.

Katie Hicks’s Specialties:

Scientific and technical writing, water quality and polluter accountability, environmental education and resource interpretation, ecology of the Southern Appalachians
