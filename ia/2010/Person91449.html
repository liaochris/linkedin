Jay Feitlinger’s Summary

I spend an inordinate (some would say insane) amount of time talking, listening and learning about current and future opportunities in the interactive space. Over the past 15 years in traditional and online marketing I have been passionate about leveraging online marketing and social media to help businesses (especially those in eCommerce, Restaurant, Retail and Real Estate) connect with their consumers.

Outside of work I can be found camping, trying out a new restaurant and sharing that experience with Yelp, or spending time on the playground with my daughters, Lexi (5) and Ella (3). My wife, Rachel, is a WAHM (Work At Home Mom) with her online baby business, Little Butterfly Kiss (http://www.littlebutterflykiss.com). When an available baby sitter can be found (we're looking at you, Nana Judy), we take time off from our hectic lives by traveling, enjoying wine and discussing our next business idea. Our scary average so far is starting a new business every 15 months so we keep our CPA quite busy.

Those that know me say I am really good at coming up with ideas that solve the problem at hand and I love getting my hands dirty by figuring out how to make something work. I often feel like a juggler, both at work and at home, and am known for working hard and playing even harder.

I am also known for being a class clown, which is a scary line to walk for me, as I am deathly afraid of clowns. I love all the latest techie gadgets and am "crazy about social media and online marketing."
