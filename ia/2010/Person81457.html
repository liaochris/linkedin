Hemang Lavana’s Summary

Lead Architect of Cisco's central test automation infrastructure group that designs and develops tools for internal use by over 4,000+ Cisco engineers. Over 10yrs of systems integration and engineering experience in a variety of leadership roles including product development, project management and cross-team collaboration.

Proven track record of designing high performance, robust and scalable products with focus on customer requirements to deliver projects from concept through deployment. Solid architecture and design experience for developing modular, extensible, asynchronous, distributed and collaborative software for workflow automation.

Established and led cross functional teams locally and offshore as well as partnered with 3rd party vendors to deliver on tight, deployment driven schedules for multiple projects. Frequently sought after by peers for design consultation and code reviews as well as assistance on solving complex and hard to debug issues.

Key Achievements and Contributions
* Led the architecture and systems integration efforts for successful design and deployment of Wave, a test automation IDE. Wave was collaboratively developed by 4 geographically dispersed teams and saw an adoption growth to over 1000 users in 6 months.
* Led the architecture, development and deployment of TRADe, a test failure analysis tool, with a team of 9 engineers. TRADe had an adoption growth to over 2000 users in 1.5yrs.
* Mentored team members to take on technical leadership roles to manage and lead projects independently, thereby allowing me to take on multiple projects.
* Established development process guidelines to improve team efficiency along with product quality. Instrumental in growing teams and diversifying projects into multiple new areas.
