Patric Fransko’s Summary

As the lead executive at National Glass Service Group, LLC (NGSG), I am responsible for all functions related to the direction, leadership and operation of the company. NGSG, LLC is comprised of three distinct companies. Service Group Distributing (SGD), Zola Distributing and NGSG. As part of a larger holding company's shared services model (12 individuals companies total), I am responsible for growing the NGSG, LLC family of companies while adhering to a budget set forth by myself and the senior executives from the other 9 companies.
The three companies in the NGSG, LLC family are all related to the window film business. SGD is the exclusive west coast distributor for 3M brand window films in the states of Hawaii, California, Washington and Oregon. Zola Distributing is the premier supplier of window film, vinyl graphic and paint protection film installation tools in the United States with distribution points on both the east and west coast. NGSG is a specification and project management company for window film related products and installations to large retail chains throughout the United States.
