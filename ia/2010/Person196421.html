Bruce Olans’s Summary

Store in a Box offers quick, reliable, excellent quality, and customized service unlike their competition. This is also a benefit for franchisees joining a franchise system, who will no longer need to work on store build outs themselves and they are able to work on promoting their new store openings. When a franchisor offers the competitive advantage by partnering with Store in a Box to their franchisees, it is a sales tool to differentiate themselves from their competition. Also another competitive advantage is that the royalties start flowing back to the franchisor faster because the retail locations are being open faster. It creates a very positive experience for the new franchisees as they are welcomed to the franchise system, not having to deal with the new store build outs.

Store in a Box, Inc. looks to partner with brands that have at least 10 to 25 locations that have been open 3-5 years and have developed a profitable business model. Store in a Box’s clients range from major retail and franchise brands around the country to innovative start-ups looking to expand. Store in a Box works with clients as a protector of their brand and can build out retail stores in a timely and competitively priced manner.

Bruce Olans’s Specialties:

To offer his clients quicker, better and more organized services and to make his own build-out specialty business stronger, Olans pulled all the services into one store in a box package – providing a one-stop-shop solution to all of his clients. Olans recently decided to partner with three successful companies; an architectural company, a general contracting company, a millwork company, and his own sales and marketing company to avoid the big headache of getting a retail store up and running.
