Kate Nelson’s Summary

In my three years at Electrolux, I have been involved in two successful brand launches, with the launch of the Electrolux brand in the United States in 2008, as well as the re-launch of Frigidaire kitchen and laundry appliances in 2009-2010.

Achievements:
• Successfully supported and executed over 100 research studies that led to the award winning launch of the Electrolux brand of kitchen and laundry appliances in the United States in 2008.
• Currently support all research studies surrounding the re-introduction of the Frigidaire brand of kitchen and laundry appliances to Lowes, Sears, Best Buy, and over 10,000 independent appliance dealers.
• Awarded "Outstanding New Project Award for 2008” for my work in the design and ongoing implementation of the Electrolux Return on Marketing Investment Quarterly Report.

Responsibilities:
• Support all new concept and product testing for the Electrolux ICON, Electrolux, Frigidaire, White Westinghouse, and Kenmore brands of appliances
• Manage all questionnaire design and development
• Implement product optimization support for all product lines
• Conduct all analysis related to our Return on Marketing Investment Quarterly Report
• Execute all packaging and design research
• Support all print/animatics/ and finished Ad Testing for the Electrolux and Frigidaire media campaigns
• Execute all point of purchase and display research
• Lead the execution of our retail audit and mystery shop program
• Plan and manage the Consumer Innovation Process program to our internal stakeholders
• Conduct usability and user interface research
• Assist in the development and execution of our retail ad tracking program
• Manage the Electrolux and Frigidaire customer satisfaction study
• Support the ongoing development of our consumer segmentation analysis in the U.S market, as well as for our global segmentation project
• Identify micro and macro trends in the North American market
