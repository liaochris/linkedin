  • Manager, Corporate Communications

    Lutheran Hour Ministries

    (Non-Profit; 501-1000 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Lutheran Hour Ministries is an outreach ministry organization founded by the International Lutheran Laymen's League, the 89-year-old grassroots auxiliary of the Lutheran Church—Missouri Synod. The organization has its roots in The Lutheran Hour, an evangelistic Christian radio program that first aired in 1930. The Lutheran Hour can still be heard on more than 800 stations across the U.S. and Canada, earning it the distinction of being the longest-running Christian radio program in the world. LHM international ministry centers share the Gospel in more than 40 countries. In the United States, LHM partners with congregations of the Lutheran Church—Missouri Synod in planning and conducting outreach efforts in their own communities.
