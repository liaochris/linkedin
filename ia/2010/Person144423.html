Michelle Nitz-Weiss’s Summary

With more than 15 years experience in executive level roles in the nonprofit and healthcare sectors, Michelle strengthens non-profit organizations through strategic planning, project implementation, event strategy and interim management. Michelle enjoys working with organizations to help them more effectively achieve their mission and to maximize their impact in our community.
