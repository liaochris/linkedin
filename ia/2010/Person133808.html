Mark Gilbert, CPA/PFS’s Summary

My entire professional career has been spent in finance and accounting. Since 1997 I have been providing comprehensive and focused financial planning, including investment management services, to individuals, corporate retirement plans, foundations and endowments. Prior to that time, I served as the treasurer and controller of two international professional services firms based in Chicago, IL.
