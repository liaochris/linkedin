Walter Huang’s Summary

Walter is the co-founder and Managing Director of Heracles Investment Corp. He also serves as a Board Director and Senior VP for a Chinese wind energy company publicly traded on TSX Venture Exchange.

Walter led the whole process from the "Red-Chip" reorganization, auditing, executives recruiting, to a private placement raising about US$ 25 million, and finally being listed on TSX-V for the Chinese wind energy company.

Prior to founding Heracles, he was the co-founder and Executive VP of a private equity investment firm based in Newport Beach, CA. During his 6 years tenure, he led the firm's business in China. He originated quality deal flow, conducted due diligence, valuated investments, structured deals, and assisted portfolio companies' growth.

Walter has years experience in investing in such industries as Internet, telecoms, media, consumer products, renewable energy and CDM related projects. He led the efforts of a consortium consisting of five US and Chinese companies to build the largest theme park in Beijing with an investment of over a billion dollar.

Prior to being a PE investor and merchant banker, he worked in several U.S. and Chinese companies including State Farm Insurance Cos and Morningstar, and also served as a consultant for Walgreens, Newell Rubbermaid and The Wm. Wrigley Jr. Co.

As a frequent public speaker, he has given speeches at a variety of conferences hosted by the Univ of Chicago Booth School of Business, Fordham Univ, Tsinghua Univ, Wuhan Univ, the United States of America-China Chamber of Commerce, and Chinese government agencies. He also served as a member of Illinois Asian American Advisory Council for Senate President. He is the council member of Tianjin Overseas Exchanges Association.

He graduated from Fudan Univ, Shanghai with a B.S. in Electronic Engineering, and performed graduate study at the Univ of Cincinnati for 3 years.

Walter Huang’s Specialties:

• Extensive knowledge & expertise of taking Chinese companies public on US or Canadian stock markets
