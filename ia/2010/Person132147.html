Peg Mulligan’s Summary

I am a Content Developer, who has worked in Boston’s high technology sector for about ten years, supporting technical communication, and more recently, content marketing requirements. I am currently working on a technical writing contract. I also write newsletters on search engine marketing at MarketingProfs.

I have a Masters in Technical and Professional Writing from Northeastern University, with a concentration in Writing for the Computer Industry. Additional coursework includes Human Factors, Usability Testing, and User Interface Design from the User Experience Design Certificate program, offered by Bentley University.

Recently, I completed Inbound Marketing Certification through HubSpot’s Inbound Marketing University, with a focus on blogging, social media, lead conversion, lead nurturing, and closed-loop analysis.

My experience includes writing from scratch, or updating in maintenance mode, a wide variety of technical and customer documents, including Administration Guides, Installation Guides, User Guides (including Online Help), Release Notes, Web Page Content, Product Descriptions, White Papers, Style Guides, and Online Marketing Newsletters.

As Lead Writer or the lone Technical Writer, I have frequently participated on Program Management teams, developing documentation plans, providing scheduling estimates, reporting status, identifying dependencies, and escalating any potential risks to the cross-functional team.

My blog at http://pegmulligan.com (Content for a Convergent World) explores topics in technical and professional communication. I am also active on Twitter (@pegmulligan).

For more information about my consulting services or permanent job availability, please contact me at peg.mulligan@gmail.com.
