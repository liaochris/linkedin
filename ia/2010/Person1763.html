Abram Bergen’s Summary

Freelance writer and book critic with particular interest in gender and sexuality issues, hybridity and identity politics, secular ethics, and environmentally-friendly technologies and lifestyles. Available to write articles, reviews of literary fiction, non-fiction, poetry and theatre, author interviews, and ghostwriting.

Have published articles and reviews at Blogcritics Magazine and been syndicated to online newspapers across North America associated with advance.net and Boston.com.
