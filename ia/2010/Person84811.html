Tina Lowe’s Summary

SalesPartners is a business and sales growth company located in Atlanta, GA. Leveraging our extensive experience and business/sales knowledge, we are part of the SalesPartners Worldwide team headed by corporate trainer, Blair Singer. We provide business growth programs, consulting, seminars and training to businesses of all sizes and in all industries specializing in increasing sales inside of an organization. Our services are designed to help your company reach its full potential through personal interaction, expert advice, innovative solutions, and a hands-on approach. We then back up our work with a performance guarantee. We believe in business you should pay for results, not opinion.
