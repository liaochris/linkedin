Arthur Z. Schwartz’s Summary

30 year Union and employee side labor and civil rights lawyer. Continues to represent NYC area labor unions, principally in the public sector, including the Transport Workers Union of Greater New York. In March 2009 became General Counsel to ACORN and its affiliated 501(c)(3)s, the ACORN Institute and the American Institute for Social Justice. In 2009 founded Advocates for Justice, a non-profit legal foundation to work with community groups, both to provide support and to do organizing related litigation.
In 2008 launched political strategy and non-profit consulting group called Progressive Stategies Group. Also helped launch NY Renewable Energy LLC, a new company looking to bring waste to energy technology to NYC, with focus on creating energy out of materials not in the food stream. Active in NYC Democratic Party politics: Greenwich Village Male District Leader 1995-2005; Democratic State Committeeman for Lower West Side of Manhattan 2006 - present; Member NYC Field Team for Barack Obama, elected delegate to 2008 Democratic Presidential Convention, pledged to Barack Obama; community activist-20 years community organizer for parks and playgrounds, Chair Community Board 2 Manhattan Waterfront Committee; Chair or Vice Chair of Hudson River Park Trust Advisory Council since 1999
