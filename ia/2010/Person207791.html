Travis Drouin’s Summary

Leveraging years of experience in public accounting and venture capital, I provide assurance and financial consulting services to clients across a wide spectrum of industries, particularly angel and venture-backed emerging technology companies. I am able to execute at MFA on the promise of superior service and expertise by surrounding myself with some of the best and brightest professionals in New England. We're confident in our dominant position in the market, but it is our clients that consistently tell us that MFA - Moody, Famiglietti & Andronico is the ideal alternative to the Big 4 accounting firms. Whether you and your company have complex financial reporting needs, domestic or international tax questions, or need support and advice with private company valuation matters, my team at MFA and I have the solutions for you.
