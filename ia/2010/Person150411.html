Mario Vittone’s Summary

Mario Vittone has nineteen years of combined military service in the U.S. Navy and Coast Guard. His writing has appeared in Yachting Magazine, Reader's Digest, SaltWater Sportsman, Lifelines, and On-Scene Magazine. He has lectured extensively to business leaders, educators, and the military on team motivation, performance, mission focus, and generational diversity.
