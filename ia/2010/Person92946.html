Jeff Basler’s Experience

  • Partner

    JB Funding

    (Real Estate industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    JB Funding is a progressive boutique real estate firm. We have three defined product lines. We provide private finance to experienced real estate investors. This includes purchase and aquisition funds up to 100% of cost. Our second line is turn key real estate investments for single family homes. We purchase, finance, rehab and lease out each unit on behalf of our client for little to nothing out of pocket. Our third offering is owner financed homes for the working class. In times when bank or mortgage finance is difficult to obtain, JB Funding sells and carries the loan for credit worthy borrowers who are otherwise unable to afford steep down payments. We provide an excellent solution to a growing problem in the world of traditional mortgage qualification. Our mission is to find a way to do a deal.
