Maggie Berry’s Summary

I run womenintechnology.co.uk – we’re an online job board, recruitment and networking forum for women working in the technology profession in the UK. I’ve been involved since womenintechnology's inception in the autumn of 2004 and I manage all aspects of the website and the networking activities womenintechnology organises.
My background is in technology recruitment within the financial services – I spent four years as a recruiter with McGregor Boyall Associates. Prior to this I worked for NatWest as a Graduate Banking Manager, providing financial advice to final year university students and graduates. I graduated from the University of East Anglia in 1997.

Maggie Berry’s Specialties:

"women working in technology" and gender issues in IT, recruitment (direct and via agencies), running events, networking, public speaking, and training courses.

    womenintechnology.co.uk

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    March 2005 — Present (5 years 7 months)

    I manage womenintechnology.co.uk, an on-line job board and networking group for women working in the IT profession in the UK. We launched the website in March 2005 and womenintechnology has subsequently developed into a high profile job board and networking forum organisations can use to attract more female talent. We've built up an active network of 6,000 technology professionals (backed up by a larger network of nearly 10,000 female technologists) who are very committed to promoting women in IT in both the public and private sectors.
    We organise regular networking events which we believe play a vital part in attracting (and retaining) women in the IT profession. These range from formal debates with guest speakers and interactive audience discussion to informal drinks evenings where people can get together in a non-work environment.
