Susan Rock’s Summary

I have been an Account Service Representative in Commercial Sales for nine years. I spent the first eight with Liberty Mutual, and then transitioned to USI as part of a corporate transaction. I am skilled at customer service and relationship building, which I believe are skills that are transferable to any role. While at Liberty Mutual, I was named Division Service Representative of the year three times (2002, 2007 and 2008) and won a quarterly contest among my peers numerous times. These contests are based on metrics such as account retention, billing and policy production, and also services provided. I have been recognized by several customers as having provided superior service for their company. I have also assisted in providing training to new sales and service reps. As a Service Rep in a direct sales environment, I worked closely with other departments such as underwriting, auditing, accounting, claims and loss prevention. This prepared me for building relationships with multiple carriers, which is necessary in my current broker environment. I am always looking for opportunities to utilize these skills, and possibly transition to an underwriting capacity, an area I am very interested in.
