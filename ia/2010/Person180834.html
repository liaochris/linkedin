Sue Salvesen’s Summary

STRe Solutions' services include executive search, professional recruitment and consultants within the G&A area including finance, accounting, IT, HR, and legal—from CFO's to all professional members of the team.

I founded STRe to provide the type of staffing services I desired when I was a CFO. We employ seasoned accounting, finance, IT, HR and legal operating executives to evaluate the candidates. The result is less time spent by the hiring manager to find the right talent.

We also provide consulting resources for engagements such as Sarbanes-Oxley, financial systems designs, IT project consultants, contract recruiters and other interim resources.

I'm interested in connecting with people who are looking for talent, who are talented and to connect with others that I can help through my network.
