15 years of experience in corporate & collegiate recruiting. Nationally-recognized expert on using new media (blog, blogging, social networking sites, deep web recruiting) to source candidates.

Currently running US Talent Acquisition (10,000 employees in NAFTA) for a global corporation with NAFTA HQ located north of Boston. Osram Sylvania Inc. is one of the largest lighting companies in the world, and is a multinational, heavily matrixed organization.
