Sabrina Caluori’s Summary

I am an avid storyteller who has found a home in digital entertainment strategy. What I love about the web, is that it’s a logical extension to on-air plot development. It’s a brilliant tool for extending the conversation in an environment that encourages exploration. While at Deep Focus, I developed successful digital marketing strategies, award-winning websites and ad creative for clients such as HBO, Picturehouse, Court TV, Miramax, Comedy Central and TBS.

Over the course of my experience I have done everything from creative direction in both print and interactive, online media planning and buying, project management, production and publicity. I thrive on highly strategic but slick creative, emerging technologies and the hard work of my staff.
