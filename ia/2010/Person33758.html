Ben Farber’s Summary

Ben Farber brings over 5 years' executive recruiting experience to his position as Vice President at Bristol Associates. Ben specializes in Casino Gaming and has successfully completed many search assignments in Finance, Hotel, Food & Beverage, Marketing, MIS, Security, Human Resources and Casino Operations..

Established in 1967, Bristol Associates is a tightly focused, results-oriented boutique search firm that prides itself on recommending the most highly qualified candidates in extremely specialized areas: gaming, hospitality, direct marketing, tourism, health care and food industries. And because we're small by design, we do it with a personal touch.

Highly focused personal service. Depth of experience and professional knowledge. Stability. Responsiveness. Honesty. Integrity. Ethical standards. Follow-through. A 30-year tradition of these critical qualities - all of which you need in an executive search firm - is what sets us apart from other recruiting organizations.

Examples of completed searches: President-COO, Vice President of Marketing, Director of Food & Beverage, Chief Financial Officer, General Manager, President, Vice President of Human Resources, Corporate Director of Operations, Director of Information Technology, Controller, Director of Slot Operations, Director of Finance, Chief Operations Officer, Chief Executive Officer, Director of Facilities, Vice President Direct Marketing and Marketing Technologies, Corporate Controller, Corporate Director of Player Development, Casino Controller, Director of Hotel Operations, Director of Casino Marketing, Financial Controller, Internal Audit Manager, Director of Financial Planning and Analysis, Director of Property Operations, Cage Manager, Director of Surveillance, Vice President, Relationship Marketing

Away from the desk, Ben is an avid baseball player and enjoys playing for the Royals in the Pacific Coast Baseball League and the Biscuits in the Los Angeles Baseball League..
