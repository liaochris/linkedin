Dr. Alex Ressos’s Summary

Legal recruitment consultants facilitate the recruitment of lawyers. My specialty are the top US/UK law firms and corporate & finance related disciplines, including banking & finance, capital markets, corporate/M&A and funds/asset management.

I am a qualified lawyer (now non-practising) holding a PhD in capital markets laws. Previously I worked as a corporate/M&A lawyer with Clifford Chance, as a legal counsel to a Government of Dubai entity in the financial services sector and as an investment funds lawyer with a leading offshore law firm and an international law firm in Singapore. I have published extensively and have been a frequent speaker at international conferences on financial law topics.
