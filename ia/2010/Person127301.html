Lon Adams’s Summary

I have proven myself in both tough-minded entrepreneurial environments and Fortune 100 companies. In positions at Fortune 100 companies, I have delivered highly profitable results as Vice President, Strategy and heading an E-Business Product Management group. I have years of management experience and have been responsible for geographically dispersed teams, budgeting, staffing and successfully delivering results based on stakeholder requirements. As the former CEO of a venture capital funded software company, I gained international and additional experience managing key / executive relationships and developing and implementing management routines while maintaining operating rhythms.

I am a hands-on manager who knows and uses project management methodologies such as strategy and vision creation, scheduling (work break-down structures) and resource planning, budgeting, scope and change management, requirements development and management, and iterative / test-driven development. I readily use and leverage tools such as various project management software packages, Excel, SharePoint and Visio.

I have an MBA from a top school and am completing a masters degree in Computer Information Systems with a concentrations in security and IT project management. I am certified as a Project Management Professional (PMP) by the Project Management Institute (PMI) and have Six Sigma Green Belt and Lean certifications.
