Greg Murphy | CEO Gapnote | Young Entrepreneur for Good’s Summary

Gapnote is a website that allows you to document your real life. Past, present and future.

It's a brand new way to chronicle and view your life as it unfolds, moment by moment, am to pm, public and private.

Via Gapnotes amazing, interactive application you can chronicle, record and communicate in a totally unique and personal way. Its transformative applications will create a sense of magic in your life.

Gapnote is not Facebook, because we don't do just surface, we do so much more.

Gapnote respects your privacy: we are introducing a superior privacy platform called "Privacy 1.2.3".

We believe your media (social and private) should be about freedom not walls.
The freedom to be private, to create, to live life on your terms.

Gapnote will give you brand new ways to communicate with applications like "Videomate", "You*log*y" and "Secret Chest".

Gapnote is not about being visible to thousands of friends (and friends of friends), but rather a more intimate number whom you want to get to communicate and share more with.

Gapnote is where you document your real life with exciting, interactive technology and subtle but amazing design.

Gapnote is beautiful from the inside out, because you are.

We are about to make your world more friendly, lovey, dovey, giddy and well social.

  • What Does Gapnote Mean?

    Gapnote Inc

    (Internet industry)

    August 2010 — Present (3 months)

    The gap represents life: all that happens between your first day and your last.
    The notes are the connections to family, friends, and loved ones.

    Gapnote is your truth. It represents the magic and fluidity of your life and of your identity. It captures your evolution and your journey.

    Gapnote is your life’s story and the narrator is you.

    Gapnote allows you to chronicle, communicate and record the moments and chapters of your life, past, present and future.

    Gapnote is where real life is both sought and revealed, where intentions are declared and where experiences are shared and won.

    Gapnote is about living and loving life without walls.

    Gapnote is a manifestation of consciousness. Its what you see, feel, and hear.

    A Gapnote is what you capture, acknowledge, create, share, embody, deliver and love.

    Any Gapnote you create is a moment in time, a whisper of passion, a secret unfolded, a mission accomplished, an experience shared, an expression of joy, a dream set to work, its communication set free.

    Gapnote is the color of a live lived to its greatest potential.

    The Gapnotes you create form part of your story, all that you are and all that you can be. They are the beauty in the stillness, the fuel that powers the purpose, the declarations of friendship, the expressions of gratitude and joy.

    A Gapnote is what makes your head tilt back with laughter and joy. Its when your singing in the rain. Its the freedom to simply be yourself and at the same time harness the most important relationships in your life.

    Gapnote is about acknowledgement of our total life not just the now.

    We like to think that if Oprah and Steve Jobs were to sit down over coffee and dream up a new social network that would inspire, value and respect its followers, allow them express life in a perfect way, it would be called Gapnote
