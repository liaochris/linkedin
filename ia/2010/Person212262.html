Vinnie Apicella’s Summary

Vinnie Apicella's professional experience is extensive and diverse. Vinnie is a professional writer, editor and proofreader educated through the writing program at Columbia University.

He specializes in web content, online marketing, business development, and management. His writing career began producing finance and investing articles for Forbes. He has also spent several years as a freelance writer producing published articles within the travel, financial and music industries.

He researched and wrote “Discover China,” a guidebook for foreigners about visiting, doing business and living in China. It is currently for sale on various online publications.

Vinnie earned a Web Master certificate, which served him to create his online business, Vinnie's World. Based on his experience as a successful international traveler and real estate investor, his company provides travel content, international real estate sales, business services, and custom tours in developing markets such as China and Nicaragua. He seeks to expand his investment interests and business services in additional markets and add an international trade platform and property database to his company site.

Vinnie is ambitious and entrepreneurial, with plans to create a new online business that offers professional editorial and translation services for U.S. and international clients with a focus on the Chinese market. He has traveled extensively throughout Europe, Central America and Asia, where he gained valuable life experience and formed key professional relationships to help advance his future business interests.

Future goals include pursuing an MBA; publishing his new book in China; developing new business activity; promoting Nicaragua tourism and selling beach property; pursuing new investment opportunities; the ability to work remotely from his laptop. Vinnie can be reached through Vinnie's World: www.vinniesworld.com
