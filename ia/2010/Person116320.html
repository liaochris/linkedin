Khan Ozol PhD’s Summary

An experienced recruiter and sales professional with a scientific background.

I specialize in recruiting accountants and scientists. Throughout the Atlantic Canada market I focus primarily on the recruitment of accounting professionals. In the USA, I recruit for both Scientists and Accountants. My main focus for the USA market is San Diego and Hawaii.

I work with and represent a variety of clients in these markets from large national firms down to small business owners and organizations of varying size in between. However, my network continues to grow and extends vastly across many states, provinces and countries. The common thread between all my clients is that they take pride in their people and realize that they are their most valuable asset.

In addition, I provide parallel job search coaching to my network by blogging with articles and podcasts. Individuals who want 1-on-1 coaching from me can find out about my services on my website or calling me. For those who are interested, I am able to provide insight to the condition of the local market regarding better opportunities, or benchmarking your current situation against what your peers may be experiencing at competing firms.

My education consists of an early foundation in academics with a BSc Hons Biology, PhD in Neuroscience and short term research as a Postdoc.

  • Job Search Coaching | Insights from an Accountant / Accounting Recruiter & Scientist Recruiter |

    ResumesToJobs.com

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    October 2009 — Present (5 months)

    Website and blog dedicated to providing the job seeker with valuable information. As a recruiter; either accountant recruiting or scientist recruiting; the process requires adding value to clients first. The recruitment business model does not leave room for helping a large share of candidates during their job search process. Therefore this website and blog was created to provide an information tool for candidates in the middle of their job search.

    ResumesToJobs.com partners with career coaches, resume writers, and subject matter experts to provide valuable and up to date information for the job seeker through articles and podcasts. It is based on the pay it forward concept which also helps me to stay up to date on current best practices communicated by thought leaders and subject matter experts.

    Through this platform I provide personal 1-on-1 coaching for those seeking extra help to optimize the job search process by leveraging all available tools to reduce the time spent engaged in the search.

    Coaching services offered include:
    -Resume review and referrals to specialized resume writers within my network.
    -How to leverage social media during the job search - coaching for LinkedIn, Twitter and Facebook.
    -Outside of the box job search strategies and techniques for those seriously engaged in a job search.

    The coaching services I offer are parallel to my recruiting focus: Accountant / Accounting & Finance recruiter and Scientist recruiter for Biotech / Pharmaceutical companies.
