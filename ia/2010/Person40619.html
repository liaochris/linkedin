Cindy Myers, Ph.D.’s Summary

Cindy Myers is an organizational development professional, with specialties in public welfare, nonprofit administration, collaborative methodologies. She is currently the Chief Executive Officer for Marin Services for Women, a treatment and recovery center for women recovering from addiction. MSW is known as one of the few treatment centers in the nation in which addiction and trauma recovery are treated integratively and at which children may be in residence with their mothers and benefit from child development services geared toward children of alcoholics/addicts.

Cindy Myers, Ph.D.’s Interests:

I am interested in public policy as it pertains to social welfare and justice. My background is in clinical psych. and organization development, with research emphasis on public discourse. I am equally interested in public welfare service systems (i.e., mental health, economic aid, children's welfare, criminal justice, education, etc.) and how they interpret public policy, how they do/do not integrate their activities to maximize leverage and accessibility of public resources and the organizational issues that arise within such systems. I am a practitioner or yoga, a golfer, a team bocce player; I love jazz, classical, country and 60's music. I live and work in the San Francisco bay area.
