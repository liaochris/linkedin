Greg Combs’s Summary

Greg is a technologist who has tackled digital media technology, business development and project management. He is a seasoned veteran from his experience at small companies and a long tenure at Hewlett-Packard (including DEC & Compaq) for nearly 24 years retiring as the Chief Architect for the Worldwide Broadband & Media group in the Communications, Media & Entertainment (CME) division. Greg is also a retired USAF Lt. Colonel with 28 years combined Active and Reserve duty where he served in a variety of technology leadership positions.

Goal: Seeking projects where my knowledge and passion for technology enables the business model and makes a difference within the company.

Greg Combs’s Specialties:

- Experienced with system architecture/design, defining customer requirements, and the successful management of pilot projects and application architectures
- Skilled project manager, mentor, and trainer in the areas of technical project management and system architecture
- Holds a FCC Commercial General Radiotelephone License
