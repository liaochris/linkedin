    I am the main developer of an SaaS all-inclusive service management application for the snow plowing and landscaping industries called NEDISO.   • Senior Software Architect

    Rentar Logic Inc (fka Fittipaldi Logistics)

    (Public Company; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    July 2003 — July 2009 (6 years 1 month)

    Rentar Logic is the software arm of Rentar Environmental Solutions. I was the Senior software architect, engineer and administrator of information systems - providing lead design, development, and support to a web-based commercial vehicle telematics (CVT) system providing integrated solutions for commercial fleet management. Components include: a transportation exchange software module that acts as a capacity planner, a brokerage, and tracks shipments and the carriers moving them in real-time; a GPS-based telematics software module that provides real-time and historical vehicle tracking/reporting, Google maps, wireless 2-way communications, dispatch, exception alerts, vehicle navigation, engine diagnostics (J-bus or OBDII), DOT Hours of Service reporting, fuel transaction monitoring, stolen vehicle location, vehicle maintenance reporting, and productivity reporting by tracking of fuel consumption, idle time and driving habits. This Oracle-based application has lead to increased productivity and greatly reduced costs (thousands per vehicle per year), and reduced pollutants by lowering fuel consumption. This helps the customer to be "green" by lowering the carbon footprint and increase its carbon credits based on the Kyoto Protocol.
