Roger Simpson KISS Janitorial’s Summary

FOUNDED IN 1991, KISS INC IS A FULL SERVICE COMMERICAL JANITORIAL SERVICE. KISS INC IS A HUB CERTIFIED WOMAN-OWNED BUSINESS. WE SPECIALIZE IN THE CLEANING OF COMMERICAL OFFICE BUILLDINGS, SHOPPING CENTERS, INDUSTRIAL FACILITIES, HEALTH CARE FACILITIES, EDUCATIONAL FACILITIES, HI-TECH FACILITIES (CLEANROOMS) AND AIRPORT FACILITIES. WE SERVE THE GREATER AUSTIN AREA. GREEN CLEAN INSTITUE CERTIFIED IN GREEN CLEANING. WE HAVE TWO GREEN CERTIFIED CONSULTANTS ON STAFF THAT DOES INDOOR AIR QUALITY TESTING.
