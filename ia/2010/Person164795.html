Phillip Bell’s Summary

An accomplished finance leader with a proven track record of instilling integrity in the finance function, designing and implementing a strong control environment, and effectively managing the finance functions of multiple domestic and foreign locations. With a focus on eliminating non-value add activities, I've utilized system automation and process mapping to streamline processes in order to drive cost out of the organizations I support. Employing a disciplined yet flexible work ethic, I've led organizations as small as 2 and as large as 30 employees. Concurrent with day-to-day responsibilities, I have served as the project manager leading numerous global teams focusing on ERP implementations, integration of acquired businesses, closure of manufacturing plants, implementing Sarbanes-Oxley 404 guidelines, SOX compliance, and re-engineering financial processes.
