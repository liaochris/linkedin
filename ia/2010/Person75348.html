Gill Arnold’s Summary

I am a digital recruiter with over thirteen years experience of growing and managing successful and profitable recruitment businesses, from my time as a manager of Recruit Media’s pioneering digital business to my current role as a Director of Futureheads Recruitment Ltd. My practice is based on delivering a highly professional, ethical service to our customers and I am fortunate enough to have worked with most of my core clients and candidates for over ten years. I focus predominantly on our public and voluntary sector client needs and in 2009 launched a networking forum for the 3rd sector.

I am an experienced line manager and love hiring, training and nurturing people to help them achieve personal and business objectives. With a keen eye for detail I enjoy establishing and honing processes and workflows to maximise quality and profitability and have a strong track record of project managing large-scale tenders and frameworks, distilling complex requirements into simple, effective solutions.

Gill Arnold’s Specialties:

Freelance, contract, permanent recruitment of digital specialists including user experience professionals, digital content managers, project managers, producers, account managers, creatives, & front end developers. Individual staff, freelancers, temps, contractors and project teams. Consultancy, requirements analysis, proposal & bid writing, budgeting, project management. Specialist in public sector recruitment including central and local government, NGOs, charities, professional associations.
