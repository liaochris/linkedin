Sachin Agarwal’s Summary

Sachin Agarwal is currently the Director of Marketing at Blueleaf.com, a "one-stop shop" for all of your personal finance and investment accounts. At Blueleaf, Sachin is charged with designing and implementing a full marketing campaign for Blueleaf, including promotion/seeding strategy, content creation, search engine optimization, the implementation of game and viral mechanics, and overseeing SEM and paid acquisition.

He is also the Founder, President & CEO of Dawdle.com, an online marketplace for new and used video games, systems, and accessories.

Most recently, Sachin was the Operations Lead at oneforty, the Twitter App Store. At oneforty, he was responsible for all aspects of the company's day-to-day operations, including marketplace management, incentive structures, business development, finance, and technical marketing.

Prior to founding Dawdle, Sachin was a part of the investment team at Ascension Health Ventures, a healthcare venture capital firm that invests in healthcare services, IT, and medical devices.

Before AHV, he was an investment banker at Jefferies Broadview with specific industry expertise in digital media, healthcare IT, and open source software.

Sachin has also held positions at Optima Fund Management, Cohen & Steers Capital Management, and Time Inc. New Media.
