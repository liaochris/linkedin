Austin Parker’s Summary

I am a computer science researcher with experience in the areas of computational decision making and knowledge representation (particularly probabilistic spatial temporal knowledge). I am interested in creating tools to allow for more effective data-driven decision making, focusing on scalability and theoretical soundness and using data derived from GPS and other sorts of position information.
