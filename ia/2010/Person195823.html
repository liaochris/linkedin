Steven Toole’s Summary

Toole has nearly 20 years of leadership experience in B2B, B2G and B2C marketing. Throughout his career, Toole has held executive level marketing positions with numerous emerging technology firms marketing to the public and private sectors, including immixGroup, INPUT, iCode (renamed as Everest), Precise Software and W. Quinn (both Symantec acquisitions). He has worked in other marketing capacities with Axent Technologies (also acquired by Symantec) and several marketing communications/ad agency firms.

Steven Toole’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing, government sales and marketing, marketing communications, public relations, demand creation, lead generation, marketing automation, company positioning and differentiation, Internet marketing, sales enablement, public speaking, business writing, sales presentation developpment, marketing infrastructure development (Salesforce.com campaigns, email marketing through Marketo, Vtrenz/Silverpop/Eloqua), and social media marketing via Twitter, Facebook, Flikr, Slideshare, etc.
