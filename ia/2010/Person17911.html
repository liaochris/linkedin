Asma Asrar Qureshi’s Summary

Strong interpersonal skills, commitment and dedication for work, working for eight plus years in different capacities such as Content Writing, Technical Writing, Project Management and Coordination and Human Resource Management.

Asma has keen interest in Research, Management and Business education and keeps on participating in different forums for professional and personal growth. She is very focused about goals in life and consistently struggling to achieve them; a very independent person who has pursued all her professional education on her own. Asma has been employed by International Islamic University, Islamabad for last three years. She is on study leave to pursue PhD in Management Sciences / Service Quality from Loughborough University, UK. She is the only Pakistani student to be offered a fully funded scholarship from the University. Before doing PhD she completed MS/M.Phil Engineering Management from Center for Advanced Studies in Engineering (CASE), an MBA in Human Resource Management from COMSATS Institute of Information Technology, Islamabad and BS Software Engineering from Fatima Jinnah Women University, Rawalpindi.

Asma has attended various trainings in visions, HR and HR productivity and has training people in HR field at International Islamic University . Her keen interest in research has led her into writing research papers. She has managed to write research papers on Impact of Quality of Service Delivery on Business Education ( Published in Sweden), Non-Financial Performance Measures of Financial Institutions ( Published in Malaysia), Leadership and Role of Emotional Intelligence (Published in Pakistan), Recruitment, Selection Issues and Challenges in Public Sector Organizations (Published in Pakistan). Apart from research and teaching Asma has been involved in consultancy work, freelance content writing and volunteer work with different organizations.
