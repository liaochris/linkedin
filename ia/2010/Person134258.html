Mark Morgan’s Summary

Budd Van Lines is the premier, award winning, independent van line, serving Corporate America with Executive Relocation since 1975 through our unique Team-Pack-and-Team-Haul delivery System. Budd Van Lines consistently delivers Corporate America's Best Move. We assign an entire team to handle each move from start to finish nationwide providing greater consistency, reliability and employee satisfaction. Budd Van Lines enjoys an unrivaled customer service reputation as the #1 quality household goods carrier in the corporate employee relocation industry.

The Budd Difference - Signature Standards are "A Commitment to Excellence". These are the practices we perform that truly distinguish us from the competition and make us the "Mover of Choice" for corporate relocations.

Our Signature Standards are unsurpassed in the industry including: a single Budd team will service each family, on time service to the day and hour, fully uniformed back ground-checked moving professionals, modern and immaculate equipment, courteous and friendly driver teams, fully prepped and protected residences and properly padded furniture in each residence. These Signature Standards mean each Budd driver team will pack and unpack every shipment, following strict procedures for loading and unloading, unpacking, setting up and clearing each area of debris, and guaranteed accuracy and timeliness of our paperwork and invoices.

I service Pennsylvania, Maryland, West Virginia, Delaware, Virginia, and Kentucky. Major metropolitan areas include Pittsburgh, Philadelphia, Washington DC, Richmond and Louisville.

I have 22 years in the Relocation Industry. I have an extensive background in domestic and international relocation. I have worked mainly on corporate, government, and executive relocations providing account managnement as well as client development. I want to provide solutions to my clients and be a resource for their needs.

Mark Morgan’s Specialties:
