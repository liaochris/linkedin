Marcelo Di Franco’s Summary

For the past nineteen years Marcelo has been working with some of the most important agencies in Latin America and the US, both in the Creative and the Strategic Planning arenas.

Marcelo began his career as a Creative Director and over time, as he focused in the strategic arena, he became a different CD, one that played a key role with his clients, helping them to unveil powerful positioning strategies, discover market opportunities, define and understand targets, or create engaging strategic platforms.

After many years playing this creative-strategic role, Marcelo was promoted to Planning & Creative Director, his first official Planning role.

Since then, Marcelo has brought his Creative and Multicultural backgrounds to the teams and agencies he worked with, creating insightful and inspiring strategies for mayor clients in a variety of markets, disciplines and categories; including Multicultural and Digital.
