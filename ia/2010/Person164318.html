Kevin Robb’s Summary

Since March 1993 I have been fortunate to have worked in a wide variety of industries accumulating & host of specialised skills. Having successfully undertaken a number of unique design & manufacturing tasks over the years, such as the design & manufacture of the aluminium VW Polo Racing Radiator for Volkswagen Motor Sport South Africa, a wide variety of innovative component designs for the automotive industry, a host of critical aircraft components, Optical lens surfacing machines, pie & pastry machine innovations, furniture designs & much more.
Being artistic by nature, a semi professional photographer doing my own film & print processing since the young age of 10 as well as a love for engineering has developed a keen 3rd eye for design. These skills today I believe have been instrumental in my proficient abilities as an inventor/designer & manufacturer.

Kevin Robb’s Specialties:

Problem solving, process creation, invention, innovation, mechanical design, precision machining & fabrication, specialised welding. Being highly efficient in the welding of Aluminium's, Magnesium, Titanium, Stainless Steels, Coppers, Cast Irons, & all special steels from material thickness's of 0,35mm & upwards. Extensive metallurgical knowledge & experience over more than 17 years provides us with the ability to accurately manipulate the metals & their properties which we work with.
