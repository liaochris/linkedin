Michelle L. Vaughn’s Summary

I am currently a small animal relief veterinarian serving the Twin Cities and surrounding areas. I am licensed in Minnesota but am in the process of obtaining a license to practice in Wisconsin as well. I have been doing relief work for one year and really enjoy the variety of patients, clients, and staff that I have worked with. Prior to relief work, I worked full-time in practice for 13 years at three different veterinary hospitals. Ultimately, I am also interested in a permanent full-time or part-time position with a progressive practice in the Twin Cities.
