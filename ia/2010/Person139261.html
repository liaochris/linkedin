Martin C. McGrath’s Summary

* Twenty years in the U.S. Army with over 12 of those spent in the Special Operations World as a Special Forces Engineer and Intelligence Sergeant with experience in security operations, International relations, training small to large groups of personnel in various Special Operations courses, demolitions training, personal defense measures and plans and operations.
* Extensive experience working in remote and austere locations to include Macedonia, Bosnia, Former Russian countries to include Slovakia, Moldova and Czech Republic, as well as Africa and Iraq.
* One tour to Iraq as part of Operation Desert Storm to include Kuwait and Saudi Arabia as a part of both Operation Desert Shield and Operation Provide Comfort. 1991.
* Three tours to Iraq as part of Operation Iraqi Freedom. 2005, 2006 and 2008.
* Diverse background working within small unit teams to Inter-agency Brigade level staff.
* Experienced instructor working with Foreign Nation's Military as well as U.S. Army and U.S. Navy units.
* Lead instructor for several classes of Surveillance and Counter Surveillance, Interviewing and other investigation techniques.
