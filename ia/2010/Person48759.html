Daria Petrova’s Summary

Marketing professional, passionate about the results of my job. I believe that good marketing should be win-win for both the vendor and the customer. My objective is not just to sell a product, but offer people a solution to their problem and make them happy. I have extensive international marketing experience in B2C and B2B spheres. After having developed a successful brand strategy and improved promotion tactics for two companies in the offline world, I am now specializing in online communications and social media in general, as well as Web 2.0 and Enterprise 2.0 technologies in particular.

After joining Wrike, Inc. I became a Project Management 2.0 evangelist. Project Management 2.0 is a new approach to managing projects that leverages collective intelligence of your whole team. It proves to work better for many projects in the present creative economy, where your requirements and budgets are flexible and your people's creative potential is your biggest asset. If you're interested in Project Management 2.0 I'll be happy to answer your questions and help you get familiar with this new trend.
