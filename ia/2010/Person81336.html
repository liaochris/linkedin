Helen M. Banks’s Summary

PROFILE

Proactive Real Estate, Facilities, Finance Executive & Consultant with extensive experience in retail; office; industrial, (flex space, operations/call centers, logistics and manufacturing); campus real estate, for private and publicly held companies. National and international background, with substantial experience in special use properties including aerospace/defense, banks, IT, entertainment, hospitality as well as all types of healthcare and life science facilities including campuses, MOB’s, surgery centers, laboratories, and CCRC’s.



CAREER HIGHLIGHTS

 Identifying and implementing write-down opportunity, resulting in over $37.5MM in cost savings, over a 5 state territory, for a publicly traded financial services company.

 Lead Facilitator on a major office relocation/consolidation from suburbs to CBD, resulting in $2.3MM savings.

 Achieved within 9 months a $7.9MM cost savings/value add while reducing personnel costs by 50% through outsourcing for a major manufacturer, involving approximately 4MM square feet of distribution/logistics space across North America.

 Project Manager on a 12- week assignment to coordinate and implement $140 MM retail sealed bid offering (84 properties across Southwestern US) and successful sale for Goldman Sachs & the RTC.

 Lead Consultant & Regional Senior Transaction Manager for retail bank branch and ATM expansion in 3 states handling approximately 70 assignments per annum.

 Community & Industry leader - Who's Who of Corporate Real Estate- 2003 -2009
