Marcus Frank’s Summary

I have been designing and managing digital user experiences that engage audiences to interact with brands, messages and information in meaningful and actionable ways since the early days of interactive digital media.

I haven't looked back since those early days and have had the good fortune to work with, and help, many great brands to make the most of interactive communications and digital media – CDC, DDE EuroMax US, DHL, Discovery Communications, Dupont, FTC, Gannett, Lane Bryant, MCI, Merck, NCI, NIH, Orthobiotech, Smithsonian, TeleTV and Xerox just to name a few.

My user experience client engagements have encompassed social marketing, public health communications, B2C, B2B, online marketing, e-learning and education, interactive PR, interactive TV, viral and word of mouth marketing.

As audience media habits and behaviors continue to evolve, and communications channels fragment and converge, I relish every opportunity to help audiences and brands connect and interact.
