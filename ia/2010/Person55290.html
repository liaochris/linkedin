Desiree H. Young’s Summary

I'm an engineer turned small business strategist. I've used my experience in problem solving, troubleshooting and building things from the ground up to create a community of people that wish to affect the world with their message.

My personal mission is to empower people, worldwide, to improve their quality of life AND work through entrepreneurship.

Presently, I'm realizing this purpose through my company VentureWalk, where I guide owners, managers and leaders through a 5 step process (5 C's) to become the best marketers and managers of their messages. This approach includes strategic pieces of the growth equation: visibility, conversion, and loyalty.

I've used this process on the front lines of the Gulf Coast to rebuild and revitalize the small business and non-profit communities. In the journey we've assisted over 27 (last I counted) types of businesses from micro-finance institutions to food manufacturers, schools, online businesses, consulting firms and everything in between.

Through our help, clients have garnered close to $1 million (again, last count) in funding to fuel their growth along with international distribution deals, sales records, national publicity and an overall sense of control and accomplishment in their ventures.

I'm really proud of the clients I've served and the progress we've made.

The new objective is to help locals go global and empower the international community of small business owners to work their way out of poverty via the miracle of entrepreneurship.

I am starting in Haiti, an area that's long overdue for rebuilding and reinvention.
