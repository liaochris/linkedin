Stuart Draper’s Summary

I have been in the retail automotive industry for the last 20 years. During this time I have been involved in many fun and rewarding experiences. I started in the automotive business as a part time salesperson. Over the years I was able to progress and work my way to owning a Dealership and becoming a Dealer Principal. I have worked in the retail sales, service and parts as well as online automotive sales across the country. I was active in the EPIC program from Chrysler (This was the electric minivan program), Supported Dodge Truck Rodeo, Supported Dodge Nascar at Sears Point, Achieved Top Five Nationally for Global Electric Motorcars, Been a three time winner to Chrysler Charger Club, Eight Years for Chrysler’s Five Star Certification Award, Two time winner in Nissan’s Presidents Award, And been a Gold Certified Chrysler Professional. Worked with the following manufactures: Nissan, Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, GEM, Isuzu, Mitsubishi, Lexus, Toyota, Buick, Pontiac, GMC Truck, Lincoln, Mercury, BMW, Mercedes Benz, Ferrari, Lamborghini, Acura, Honda, Audi, VW, Porsche, Rolls Royce, and Chevrolet. I have a real passion for the automobile and enjoy meeting people that share the same passion. My goals are to be successful in the industry and provide world class service to our customers. Currently we are working to expand the reach with Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge and GEM in the commercial market. These brands offer some real opportunity and we are working on spreading the word and building this business. Transportation needs are always specific and there are often many questions and frustrations. My goal is to work through these challenges and create lifelong relationships.
