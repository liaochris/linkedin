Curt Buthman’s Summary

I’m a versatile, dynamic & creative mkt/adv professional who started in the proverbial “mail room” at a small recruitment advertising agency in Dallas.

Now, in Social Media as a Certified Strategist, Not a Guru, Not an Expert, but a hard working person of interest. I also do Web Promotions and Direct Response with over 20 yrs experience in Strategy & Creative.

Was sent from Dallas to NYC to open a new Division at BJK&E. (Cowboy boots and all!) Later changed to NP sales at the Gannett Westchester NP, in charge of the Department Store advertising.

Then got Creative and became Promotion Dir. at Mastandrea Studios, a DM creative shop. I started our own creative group, with clients like; American Express, AOL, Chase, Consumer Reports, Columbia House, Disney, International Masters Publishers, Reader’s Digest, Rolling Stone, Time Inc. and Time Life and Many more.

There, overseeing 28 employees, in management, I handled all aspects of client service including; acquisition, retention, billing, creative, copywriting, marketing, strategy, list recommendation and client presentations.

In 2004 What Box? creative was formed as a full-service Direct Marketing & Web Promotions agency.

We just launched our Social Media Div. which specializes in getting Companies and Individuals set up and trained. It blends the lines between Direct and Social Media Marketing. Social Media is one-to-one marketing in “Real Time” which has tremendous applications.

My goal is to help traditional marketers see the value in Social Media and be able to demonstrate a positive ROI. It will be a challenge, but nothing any harder than we have faced before.

See ya Soon!
