Michele Bergh’s Summary

15 years in Parks and Recreation, beginning in programming and working into a management position.

Founded Infinite Light Wellness Center LLC in 2003 to begin a private practice and teach classes in the areas of holistic health and wellness.

Lodestone Networking Group founded in 2007. In 2009, Lodestone Networking Group expanded into Lodestone Dynamics LLC.

Certifications include Holistic Life Coach, Hypnotherapist, Stress Management Specialist, Yoga Instructor, Reiki Master, ThetaHealing Practitioners, The One Command Practitioner and Energy Healer.

Additional training in many integrated therapies. Currently in school receiving formal education in Interactive Media and Graphic Design.

Services currently offered include ThetaHealing™, The One Command, Aura and Chakra Photography and Consultations, Business Mentoring and Networking for conscious entrepreneurs, graphic design and website design.

Education opportunities offered include workshops on a variety of Integrated Therapies as well as personal and professional growth opportunities for conscious entrepreneurs.

Trained Facilitator for Creating Healing Experiences that Work and has co-created the 6 week program Freedom from Chronic Pain with Ann Dragsten, Holistic Health Practitioner.

Published articles through Essential Wellness, the Edge Life, Second Opinion and Whispers of Spirit. She has been a guest speaker for Minnesota Recreation and Park Association, Women in Leisure Services, Minnesota Seniors Association, Whispers of Spirit, Living Well Northwoods, BodyLab USA, Healthy Life Expo, Elk River Chamber of Commerce and Sherburne County Social Services.

Released a CD in January 2009, entitled Heart’s Desire, containing meditations for manifesting and published her first book, Inspiring Success, in March 2009.

For more information, please visit www.lodestonedynamics.com.
