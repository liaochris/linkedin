Diane McHutchison, PMP, SSBB’s Summary

I am the Project Manager or Analysis Manager that you need. I have the analysis and project management expertise that will meet your needs and give you a Manager who will hit the ground running. I have strong facilitation skills, and have been an instructor for many years, both in the corporate and the volunteer sectors. I have dealt with many different audiences, from CEOs to the shop floor. Recognizing the importance of improving processes, especially in these economic times, I also hold my Six Sigma Black Belt.

In my current position, I am the Senior Project Manager overseeing the implementation of a customizable COTS program into multiple speciality divisions of a major hospital. These divisions range from a very small practices consisting of a few physicians and therapists, to large and busy surgical divisions.

I have experience in health care, pharmaceutical and medical device industries as both a contractor and as an employee. Obviously, rigid quality controls and best practices are no stranger to me. I have supported a development lifecycle process, and contributed to its refinement. My first love is requirements analysis, and those skills make me a better manager, since nearly anything we do requires the critical thinking skills of a good analyst.

Additionally, I am an experienced small business owner, having been CEO/COO of an S-Corporation selling equipment and supplies to emergency responders. After selling that business, but needing a use for free time, I started designing and selling hand crafted jewelry. I am not content to be idle.

MBA. PMP. SSBB.
