Colbi Stone’s Summary

Colbi offers over 3 years of successful experience in social media, traditional public relations, event coordinating, marketing and advertising. She is proficient in industry leading social media monitoring tools and analytics and is educated and trained in Associated Press style writing and editing.

As an ACE at Voce Communications, Colbi brings her enthusiasm and talent to a variety of accounts that often cross the line between “traditional” and social media programs. She has cut her teeth working with clients such as Sony PlayStation, Fujitsu, Ooma, Intel, Actuate and Webtrends.

Prior to joining Voce, Colbi dabbled in event coordinating as a publicist to an author and as an intern for the Scottish Rite Center organization in Portland, Oregon where she planned, executed and hosted several events, including the annual trade show for the International Special Events Society.

Colbi developed her passion for public relations during her four years at the University of Idaho, where she studied public relations and communications, and held office as public relations director for a university organization. While studying at UI, Colbi worked for Western Land Company, a land development and real estate firm, as a marketing coordinator. She executed marketing tactics for development projects through advertising in local publications and held on-site promotional events.
