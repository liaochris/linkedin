Edward Lee Rapp’s Summary

Edward L. Rapp is one of the interactive and new media industry’s pioneers. He is a highly accomplished executive with extensive hands-on-background in media, production, operations, process management, corporate management, and outside management consulting. Mr. Rapp has a track record of achievements across several industry sectors and various levels of government, domestic and international. C-level and senior management executives and government higher-ups have worked directly with him on issues pertaining to operations, media content, online interactive technologies, and digital distribution.

Management responsibilities experience includes production, distribution, operations, strategic planning, project management, web development including content, extranet design and concept visualizations, research, testing, vendor relations, client relations, budgets, studio architectural design, scheduling for corporate and government clients. He brings a high level of expertise developing and running a process.

Mr. Rapp has created commercial new media solutions for advertising, training, education, presentations, entertainment, and the United States Federal Government, using audio, digital, online, and interactive technologies, for B2B and B2C applications. Additionally, he has created architectural designs for film studios and web interface designs as well. He has built cohesive talented teams, which consistently support all other corporate units.
