Noah Brockman, MBA’s Summary

I’m a Business Advisor, Coach, and Instructor working with entrepreneurs, managers, and executives to build strong companies and support them to clear any limiting beliefs that might get in the way.

•As a BUSINESS ADVISOR I assist companies to develop management strategies that align with their short-term goals and long-term vision through business planning, forecasting, implementation, and loan package development. Whether the objectives are growth, financing, or strengthening business infrastructure, I help companies get on track.

•As a BUSINESS COACH, I work with entrepreneurs, managers, and executives to identify and clear the mental, emotional, and energetic blocks that interfere with manifesting their businesses long-term vision and short-term goals. Regardless of the business strategy, I believe that once the conscious and unconscious beliefs are congruent with the business goals - the pathway to success presents itself.

•As a part-time faculty INSTRUCTOR at PCC’s Small Business Development Center, I use adult learning theory to support entrepreneurs in a creative exploration of business management topics and helps them apply their discoveries to their own businesses.

Energetic Business Coaching™ is the facilitation process I use to assist business professionals to IDENTIFY and CLEAR the “road blocks” and limiting beliefs that interfere with achieving their business goals. This powerful coaching model works quickly where logic, behavior change strategies, and affirmations often fall short for integrating lasting change.

I enjoy working with professionals who are invested in making changes to improve their professional and business experience through a conscious process. How committed are you to change your work-life experience on a scale of 1-10? If your commitment to change is a seven or higher, I invite you to drop me a line and we can see if it makes sense to work together.
