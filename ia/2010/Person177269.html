Ron Graham’s Summary

I’m an experienced and reliable entrepreneur coach, with a knack for picking winners!

Here’s what I bring to the table:

- several business plans and e-books completed
- eight years experience teaching college writing and three teaching high school math and computer literacy
- 15 years experience as an engineer in the aerospace and robotics industries
- an online social networker before the term “social networking” was even coined
- currently studying and applying mediation, sales and coaching technique

I am a semi-LION. I will connect to anyone who asks, provided they don't simply send the canned LinkedIn request. And I won't ask everyone I meet myself, only those whom I think would be interested. So, not a full LION. :-)

I am the Editor of the Engineer's Companion (http://www.designnotes.com/companion/), for more than 13 years one of the most respected engineering reference Web sites in the world. For over four years, I have maintained a blog that supplies clarity for young entrepreneurs. That blog is being extended into a podcast.

I work out of three locations: Akron, OH; Trenton, NJ; Saskatchewan, CA. I may be near you soon!
