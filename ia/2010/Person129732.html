Mark Addington’s Summary

Represents management in all types of employment matters involving discrimination law, disability law, employment contracts, employee benefits, union/management, minimum and overtime wages, sexual harassment, affirmative action, OSHA/safety, policy development, and regulatory compliance.

My practice focuses on Labor & Employment Law, Restrictive Covenants (Non-competition, Non-Solicitation, Confidentiality), Wage & Hour, Privacy, Technology, Business Contracts, ADR (Mediation, Arbitration, MedArb)
