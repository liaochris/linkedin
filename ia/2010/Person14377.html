Anne Zieger’s Summary

I am a poised, polished publishing and marketing professional with nearly two decades of communications experience, largely focused primarily in the healthcare and IT space.

My company, nextHospital, creates healthcare-related content of all kinds, including content used to promote corporate interests. We create any type of content vehicle needed to fulfill corporate strategy, including newsletters, Web sites, magazines and social media pages/discussions.

Our content helps companies demonstrate thought leadership and increase bonding with existing/potential customers. Customers spend seven to eight times more time with content like ours than corporate self-promotions, making it much more likely that leads turn into relationships.
