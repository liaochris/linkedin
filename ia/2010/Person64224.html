elyse katz’s Summary

I am a academy award-winning documentary producer and have been a producer, line producer and project manager in independent films, television, documentaries, web and promotional content. My work includes the award winning documentary The Last Days, Sundance entrants, Trudell, Johns and Going All The Way and socially conscious films Bellyfruit and The Fire Within. I am currently producing an Internet hybrid and broadcast documentary series Discovering Women that celebrates the achievements of women around the world and those women who are making significant contributions. I am also producing the independent film Timber about the true story of Judi Bari and Darryl Cherney and their work saving the California Redwoods and a documentary entitled Claiborne Pell: A Passion For Education; which is an intimate portrait of the legacy, politics and contributions of R.I. Senator Claiborne Pell and his impact to the educational landscape. I just recently produced the One Night Only performance of Barbra Streisand Live at the Village Vanguard in NYC on September 26, 2009 and am currently working on the DVD release. I also just completed with Powershot Productions a series of client commercials for City National Bank.
