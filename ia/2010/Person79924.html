Etienne Hardre, CPA’s Experience

  • Senior Manager

    Next Exit Advisors

    (Privately Held; Accounting industry)

    August 2010 — Present (4 months)

    At Next Exit Advisors, we help entrepreneurs to mid-sized businesses use financial data to model and plan for the future. We strongly believe that business owners deserve the highest quality advice and technical expertise for their accounting needs and that the numbers of a business have a critical role in the strategic planning process.

    Next Exit Advisors' core philosophy is that entrepreneurs build the most value in their business when they begin with the end in mind. We want to be personally involved in helping you grow your business. To that end, we offer a host of services designed to allow our clients to focus more on strategically growing their business and less on worrying about the details.

    Our outsourced (part-time) CFO services encompass all of these issues by enabling business owners to leverage the forward-looking expertise of a CFO at the right price for their situation. Instead of hiring a full-time employee for hundreds of thousands of dollars per year to only utilize a portion of their time, our clients can choose the level of service they require on a monthly basis. As their business grows, we have experts available to overcome every financial challenge.

    We also offer tax, bookkeeping, and other accounting services to businesses and individuals. We look forward to being your next CPA!
