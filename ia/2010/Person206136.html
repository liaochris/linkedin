Tom Van Horn’s Summary

As FinFolio's Senior Vice President of Operations, Mr. Van Horn directs the FinFolio technical support and professional service teams. He is responsible for staffing the department, managing FinFolio software releases, and providing support to FinFolio users.

Tom was the Operations Manager and Chief Compliance Officer for Alexander Capital Management Group, a large Denver-based investment advisor subsidiary owned by CoBiz Financial, Inc. (COBZ). Oversight included back-office operations, trading, client service, IT systems and the day to day operations and regulatory compliance of the firm.

Prior to Alexander Capital, Tom was responsible for systems evaluation, selection, integration and implementation for trust, investment, mortgage and retail banking entities of a large Arizona-based financial services company. Tom also founded the InData Independent Users Group.
