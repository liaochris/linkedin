Paul Reino’s Summary

Has been involved in technical recruiting since 1989 as a founder of accurIT STAFFING corpororation and co-founder of Alta Technical Services, The primary focus of both organizations has been the placement of fulltime and contract technology professionals. Target clients cross the gamut from defense contractors to commercial, medical devices to communications products. The typical client is an engineering manager or VP of Engineering and the typical candidates is a hardware/software engineer; engineering, marketing, and product managers and technical sales engineers. Most, but not all clients, are located from New York to Washington, DC.
