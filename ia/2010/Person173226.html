Rick Olmos’s Summary

Rick Olmos has worked for in the public relations and marketing field for over ten years. Prior to joining E3 Alliance, Rick served as Information Officer for Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi, overseeing all design and public relations for the College of Education. During his tenure, Rick spearheaded a complete redesign of the public face of the College of Education, including print, web and identities. In addition, Rick assisted in the creation of the University Preparatory High School in association with Flour Bluff ISD, which was profiled in the Corpus Christi Caller Times as a unique and effective program to accelerate the transition from secondary education to university coursework. His awards include two Addys while working for the Art Museum of South Texas and "Friend of the Arts” award from his volunteer work at Wynn Seale Academy of Fine Arts.

Rick’s involvement in education stretches back almost 15 years. He has taught ESL courses, using theatre and performance skills to teach literacy, mathematics and art. In an effort to give back to the educational community that encouraged his life, Olmos served as a longtime volunteer for Corpus Christi ISD, as an advisor for the Theatre Department of Wynn Seale Academy as well as a UIL one-act play festival judge for the region.

Another aspect of Rick Olmos’ career falls into the Film & Television industry. Rick has been working in the casting side of the Film/TV industry for over 7 years and been in front of the camera for many years as well. Working in films such as Selena, Pearl Harbor and the TV show Friday Night Lights, Rick’s casting experience has helped him in his my career avenues.

Rick holds BA in Communications in the fields of Technical Theatre and Public Relations from Corpus Christi State University.
