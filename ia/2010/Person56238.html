Diane Sinnott’s Summary

I am a Creative Director/copywriter with broad advertising experience at top-tier ad agencies - working in all mediums. Strategically savvy, conceptually strong - with proven leadership and problem solving skills. I have worked as a creative director on Sprint and United Vintners producing websites, print, broadcast and experiential.

I also have international experience on Chanel, British Rail in London for Saatchi and Pepsi for BBDO. I have done a plethora of pitches for numerous agencies. Focused and fluid in the work environment. Enthusiastic and dedicated to every project.

Some days you'll find me concocting social media ideas, the next a trade show for a bunch of scientists. I learn something new every day.

Besides advertising I have an expertise in art. Old and Modern Masters, works on paper.
