Shannon Caldwell’s Summary

Executive Director of Offshore and Remote Sercvices for NuPhysicia LLC which provides improved health care services - delivering doctor visits anytime, anywhere.

NuPhysicia is the only healthcare provider that brings doctors to the patient - no matter where they are; work, home, even remote parts of the globe.

From December 2006 to November 2008 I was the Corporate HSE Advisor for Noble Drilling with primary oversight for Eastern Hemisphere Operations. In my position I provided direction and support for four international divisions and two major shipyard projects with seven new build drilling rigs in Dalian, China and Singapore.

Prior to working for Noble Drilling I was employed as a HSE / Paramedic for KBR, a division of Halliburton, supporting the United States military at a remote location near Baghdad, Iraq.

The bulk of my experience has been in emergency medical response and Training. I am a Nationally Registered Paramedic and have worked as a Paramedic in the air and on the ground.

My first HSE position was as a Safety Officer offshore in the Gulf of Mexico. I have served on offshore drilling rigs, production platforms, and dive support vessels in this role.

I served in the United States Air Force first as a Transportation Specialist before cross training to become a medic. I was on active duty for a total of six years serving in various parts of the world as a flight medic.
