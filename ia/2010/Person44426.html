Creative Designs Creative Care’s Summary

On September 19, 2010, “Creative Designs – Creative Care” opened its doors. The business is owned and operated by three women – Cheryle Hammons, Jennie Spring and Maryjane Gaspar. Their mission is to “Design” top quality, hand-crafted items – apparel, jewelry and gifts – which bring Cancer and Alzheimer’s awareness to others. In addition, they offer “Care” services – transportation, massages, meals, etc. – to those going through cancer treatments, the elderly and caregivers.

Their Designs are hand-crafted by local Michigan artists. A generous portion of their sales are donated to Cancer and Alzheimer’s programs. They work in conjunction with the American Cancer Society – Making Strides Against Breast Cancer and The Relay For Life; the Detroit Komen “Race for the Cure”; Young Survival Coalition and the Alzheimer’s Association – Greater Michigan Chapter. Cancer, caregiving and fund raising are not new to these women. Cheryle, a 26-year cervical cancer survivor, was a caregiver for her Mom during her terminal illness and currently cares for her 82-year old uncle who has early stage dementia. Maryjane, an 11-year breast cancer survivor, cared for her Dad and Mother-in-law during their battles with lung cancer and assists her sister, Jennie, in the care of their Mom. Jennie is a co-survivor to her husband, a 6-year colon cancer survivor, and to her sister, Maryjane, supporting them during their surgeries and treatments. Along with Maryjane, she cared for her Dad during his struggle. Currently, she and her husband care for her Mom, who has been diagnosed with Alzheimer’s.

In addition, Creative Designs – Creative Care, participates in other fund raising activities by hosting events at their store, from which 25% of sales during the event is donated. Three women working together to bring awareness and services to those battling these diseases. . .please stop by, shop and help to make a difference. The coffee is always on.

Creative Designs Creative Care’s Specialties:

We believe that fundraising begins at the community level, which is why we are passionate about donating a generous portion of our retail proceeds towards Cancer & Alzheimer's awareness. We also love to hold fundraising events and / or get involved in other's fundraising efforts, so that more people have more information. The more we know, the better we live.
