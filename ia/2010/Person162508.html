    About Studio Square:

    After undergoing a $20+ million renovation, Studio Square is the premiere event facility in the New York metropolitan area. Only minutes from midtown in the historic section of Long Island City, Studio Square enjoys unobstructed, panoramic views of the Manhattan skyline. Comprised of five unique event spaces ranging from 10,000 sq. ft. to 18,000+ sq. ft., Studio Square is the perfect backdrop for any type of corporate function from opulent parties and promotions, fundraisers, conferences, expos and new product launches to casual company barbecues.

    Our crown jewel is an 8,000+ sq. ft. open-air roof deck with a breathtaking, panoramic view of Manhattan.

    With the design flexibility of our facility and experience of our staff, Studio Square can accommodate events from 50 to over 5,000 guests with the same quality, service and attention to detail.
