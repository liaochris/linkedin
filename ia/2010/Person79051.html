Guy Horesh’s Summary

Mr. Guy Horesh is a senior managment consultant and an enterprenuer with special expertise in the online financial industry (Banking, ACH, Transaction, e Commerce, e Banking)telecommunication markets (Data Communication, Cellular) experienced in selling services or products to mobile telecom operators (Celtel International, Vodafone, Orange, TeliaSonera and more). Guy has many contacts within the global corporate world from large banks to mega operators and has helped companies in value creation by formulating the right strategy at the right time associated with tactial busienss development and sales activities.

Guy brings vast business experience, helping Motorola promote ERP (Oracle Applications), Billing and Provisioning solutions to Mobile operators (GSM, CDMA and Tetra/Dimetra) in Europe, US, Latin America and Africa, prior working with Motorola Guy was consulting and assisting the promotion of GSM telephony and managment systems in African and Eastern European markets for companies such as Comverse, prior to that Guy has held the position of Vice President of Sales and Marketing for Infonet Israel Ltd.
(NYSE: IN), a leading provider of global communications services & solutions for multinationals which has been integrated into British Telecom under:"Infonet BT". Aside from managing the sales team, he directly managed select accounts, including Indigo-HP, Tecnomatix, Amdocs, M Systems, Eci Telecom, Netvision, Internet Gold etc. In his 7 years with the company he was also involved in the establishment of Infonet Israel. Prior to that, Guy was Israel Country Manager for GlobalOne (Equant), a consortium of France Telecom, Deutsche Telecom and Sprint, which provides global data/telecom solutions to multinationals (www.equant.com). Guy is a Lawyer and holds a Kellogg Executive MBA.
