Marc Matthews’s Summary

Marc H. Matthews, President of Corporate Sport Incentives, has over twenty five years experience in the sales incentive, group travel, and special events marketplace. Corporate Sport Incentives specializes in creating and fulfilling customized tiered sales incentive programs based around individual sports and vacation travel packages that are designed to help push product down through distribution networks. Corporate Sport Incentives is unique in that these are risk-free incentive programs - there are no up-front costs to set up one of their programs - and the Client only pays for the specific certificate awarded if and when one of their distributors or sales reps hits an award tier predetermined by the client. If no program participants hit a performance tier, there is no cost to the Client. In this fashion, Corporate Sport Incentives strives to be viewed more as a strategic partner than as a potential vendor in that if a program does not produce the desired results for the Client, Corporate Sport does not charge a fee. ROI is not an issue because there is no up-front investment and, in essence, the entire program is funded by a small percentage of the incremental lift created by the incentive program. The sports and vacation travel certificate awards are extremely flexible: the recipient chooses WHAT dream experience he wants, WHERE he wants it and WHEN. In addition to the shear trophy value associated with these dream experiences, they also have an extremely high perceived value far greater than any cash award.

Mr. Matthews is a Founding Member and Past President of the National Association of Ticket Brokers. He graduated in 1987 from the University of Maryland College of Business with a Degree in Marketing and a Degree in Finance and holds two Associate Degrees in Architecture and Civil Engineering from Northern Virginia Community College.

Marc Matthews’s Specialties:

1) Incentive Sports and Vacation Travel Certificate Programs, 2) Planning, Development, and Management of tiered sales incentive programs, 3) Special Event / Group Travel program development and execution, 4) Special Event ticket procurement, 5) Hotel procurement, 6) Expertise in all facets of Group and Major event travel, 7) UNSWERVING DEDICATION TO DELIVERING SUCCESSFUL PROGRAMS THAT EXCEED OUR CLIENT'S EXPECTATIONS.
