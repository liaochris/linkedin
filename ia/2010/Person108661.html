Julie Schwietert Collazo’s Summary

My academic and professional background has prepared me with the knowledge and experience to deliver projects in a variety of areas.

I graduated with a BA (English and Women's Studies) from Emory University and a Masters of Social Work from New York University. I am currently completing a PhD in Spanish Literature at The Center for Advanced Studies of Puerto Rico and the Caribbean. I am also a registered creative arts therapist specializing in the use of writing in psychotherapy.

I worked five years as a psychotherapist in social service and in private practice. In social service settings I innovated new service programs and served as the assistant director of a major metropolitan agency. I also developed my own business in psychotherapy and consulting.

At present, I own a business that allows me to explore and utilize all of my interests-- including writing, editing, researching, translating (English/Spanish)-- in a variety of areas, ranging from the arts, travel, food, and sociocultural issues.
