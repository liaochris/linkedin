Ronen Hamatian’s Summary

Ronen is a master marketer with a proven sales track record who has extensive affiliate marketing experience. He manages strategic relationships with Millionaire Network's affiliated partners, directs day-to-day operations, oversees marketing, and is personally responsible for developing and maintaining advertiser relationships.

A perfectionist and goal oriented, Ronen works around the clock to create a profitable environment for advertising and publishing partners so both are completely satisfied with the end results. Leveraging Millionaire Network's internal success, volume, and high quality, Ronen makes a point of teaming up with the right agencies to deliver the right offers, best payouts, and solid terms. He is helping to create a leading network that every seven figure affiliate would want to be a part of.

To Azure, Ronen brought over 8 years of sales and marketing experience to the team. He has an extensive background of both online and offline marketing and advertising. At Azure Media, Ronen lead the mobile marketing strategy team and accomplished turning technology, relationships and data into large profits.

Prior, at PartnerWeekly, Ronen has successfully managed email, search, mobile and display advertisers and affiliates and brought a huge spike in revenues and profits to the company in the online specialty finance spectrum.

Prior to PartnerWeekly, Ronen was the Regional Sales Manager for Bay Capital Corp, a Sub prime wholesale bank, responsible for all employees, sales and operations of the west region.

Serving in the Israeli army for 3 years as a combat officer prior to his professional experience has possessed him with excellent work ethic, winning attitude and management and communication skills.

His goal is to strengthen his skills thru continual practice and study of marketing disciplines.
