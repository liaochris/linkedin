David Kirkpatrick’s Summary

Do you need freelance writing – SEO website content, ghosted articles, ghost blogging, marcomm or other collateral?

I am a professional writer with a wealth of experience providing content for both business and media. My background includes press releases, newsletter content, ghosted material, SEO website content and full-length business nonfiction.

My client list includes USA Today, the American City Business Journals family, Office.com, C-Level Media, Highbury House Communications and Oxford Intelligence, among others.

A recent project involved developing and creating all the content for the "Second Home" section of the GMAC Home Services website. That work (approx. 65 articles) can be found beginning at this page:

http://www.gmacrealestate.com/second-homes/second-home.cfm

One value-added service I provide is an informal news/marketing distribution channel. I have a general interest blog (http://davidkirkpatrick.wordpress.com/) that is distributed via Newstex's "Blogs on Demand" (http://www.newstex.com/products/blog_index.php).

Through that premium service my posts are syndicated to LexisNexis, EBSCO, Thompson Financial & Business, ProQuest, NewsBank, CanWest MediaWorks, Amazon Kindle, Cengage Gale and others.
