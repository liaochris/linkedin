Garth O'Brien’s Summary

I am an experienced Operations Manager, SEO, SEM and Social Media Manager with a proven ability to develop and implement integral strategies that support operations and business objectives. I have years of experience in search, online marketing, operational management, market research and customer relations.

Since 2002, I have managed SEO, SEM and online marketing opportunities for various B2B and B2C websites. Portal optimization has realized 50% to 100%+ lift in traffic and greatly boosting number of keyword phrases performing in the top ten search engine results placement. I develop and drive the product roadmaps for implementation of social media solutions such as Twitter, YouTube and Facebook. I champion corporate blogs and introduce new content-rich supporting websites and also manage online advertising campaigns for lead generation and community growth.

While at GMI, I managed the off-shore CS team, developed initiatives to better recruit, engage and retain a global online community of 6 million members and continually developed ways to improve the user experience. I was responsible for approving $8 million annually of incentive payments. I also managed a global affiliate program and owned delivery of internal administration tools.

I am recognized as an operational expert in online communities, delivering needs while optimizing opportunities for new revenue. This includes developing and integrating tools to facilitate community recruitment and engagement. I also am a capable facilitator of cross-functional collaboration motivating various stakeholders to resolve blockers and to deliver release quality products.
