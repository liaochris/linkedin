Eric Horstman’s Summary

Sports Image, Inc. (www.sportsimageinc.com)

Sports Image helps schools and grassroot organizations in the U.S. and Canada to obtain new equipment and revenue for their programs for free through corporate sponsorships. Over $600,000 in cash and $7,500,000 in equipment has been donated to 1000+ schools and organizations around the country.

Flyin' to the Hoop National High School Basketball Invitational (www.flyintothehoop.com)

A national high school basketball invitational in Dayton, OH. over MLK weekend every January showcasing the best teams and talent in the nation. The USA Today has named our event as one of the Top Ten High School Basketball Events in the nation. Over 150+ participants have gone on to play Division I College ball and 11 have been drafted into the NBA. Each year the event brings over $1M into the city of Dayton.
