Bob Bolen’s Summary

Bob Bolen has 22+ years in the investments industry as a securities analyst, portfolio manager and financial planner. I founded Bolen Dodson & Associates, a fee only, independent registered investment advisory firm, in 2003 to help clients live inspired lives, free from worry about their money matters. We do this through life centered financial planning and investment management services, offered on a fiduciary basis.

I primarily work with couples, age 55-65, business owners and execs, and women in transition.
