Jérémie Montessuis’s Summary

I'm a Creative Director with over 15 years experience in Graphic Design, Web Design, Advertising & Branding in Paris, France and Phnom Penh, Cambodia. I would best sum up my expertise as a Senior Digital Creative with a broad Strategic, Conceptual, User Experience and Art Direction background.

I have extensive experience in pitching new clients and winning them, creating and maintaining relationships with co-workers and clients, and mentoring my staff to feel inspired and challenged by their work.

Being a hands-on Digital Designer/Creative Director, immersed in new technology and social trends allows me to stay on top of what I can do and what can be done. My biggest professional accomplishment by far though has been mentoring, inspiring and pushing people to think as big and far as they can to break boundaries challenge execution and think outside the box.

Brands and Companies I have worked for: Telecom Malaysia, ANZ Royal Bank, Wing, KFC, Yeos, Société Générale, Online, Asahi, LG, Acleda Bank, ABA Bank, Intercontinental Hotel, Amanjaya Hotel, Knai Bang Chatt Resort, First Cambodia, SCA (Cambodia Airports), Cambodia Brewery Limited, Beyond Interiors, Mag Alliance, G21 Magazine, Touchstone Magazine, SuperBricks, Le Preview - Workshop Salons.

NGOS: Heritage Watch, USAID, UNAIDS, Arbitration Council Foundation, European Union, UNDP, PSI, AMK, WSP, ILO, Khmer Rouge Trials, Worldvision, Craft Network.

I have been a guest speaker on Creativity and Innovation at the Royal University of Law and Economics of Phnom Penh.
I'm also a keen Photographer, some of my photos have been published in Geo Magazine (Germany) and exhibited in "The beat goes on" at the National Museums, Liverpool, UK.

I'm looking to produce great forward thinking Digital Creative work in tandem with new emerging Media opportunities.
