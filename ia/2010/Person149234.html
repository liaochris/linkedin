Michele Porfilio’s Summary

Michele Porfilio is a Strategic Sourcing Leader at Crowe Horwath LLP and has worked with Crowe since 2005. Michele develops and delivers programs and strategies designed to enhance the firms’ overall recruiting efforts. She creates and manages internal programs to drive greater awareness of hiring needs thru strategic programs. She is responsible for building and opening new sourcing channels for the company’s recruiting department and utilizes technology to drive increased candidate identification and hiring. Michele implements strategies for Research, Sourcing, Employee Referral Programs, Vendor Management, Employment Branding Initiatives and Alumni recruiting and serves as a critical member of the recruiting leadership team within the firm.

Michele has been a leader in advocating the applications of technology in recruiting, utilizing metrics to improve performance, and developing creative solutions to identify talent. Michele is also experienced in interview training to management, staff and job seekers and has served as a career coach to many candidates in her career.

Michele has great passion for recruiting and sourcing and is a master in networking. She has over 15 years of experience in recruiting, providing strategic and tactical recruiting services to organizations. Prior to joining Crowe, Michele owned her own Recruiting Consulting firm, Staffmatch LLP. Michele’s background includes working with firms such as Amoco, Sunbeam, Sterling Staffing, Whitman Hart, Diversified Computer Consultants and Jefferson Wells.

Michele serves as a mentor and model to members of the recruiting community. Michele currently sits on the board of the Technical Recruiters Network and Illinois Recruiters Association.

Michele also has great passion for the community and charitable organizations. When she can find the time, she volunteers for activities to foster awareness and generate financial donations to causes such as Autism Society of Illinois.
