George McGowan III’s Summary

I have over 15 years of experience in consumer brand marketing with broad-based experience in retail, e-commerce, sporting goods, consumer packaged goods, beverage and sports and entertainment. I have also been responsible for leading brand advertising and consumer promotion agencies combined with brand management positions at leading consumer product manufacturers. I am knowledgeable in brand strategy and positioning, retail, channel marketing, retail brand planning, and tactical implementation, promotional and consumer lifestyle marketing and have extensive experience in contributing to top-line and bottom-line growth of small, mid-size and Fortune 500 companies.

I was most recently the President/Founder of Sundog Trading Company, a multi-channel retail concept brand operating e-commerce and inline channels. Sundog Trading carved out a proprietary niche in the highly competitive casual apparel and footwear retail segment with a distinct market position and operated at break-even through the recent economic downturn through strong leadership, clearly defined business strategy and attention to execution on a daily basis. In short, I made the necessary decisions based on the situation and then managed the business in the direction it needed to go in.

I actively accept consulting opportunities and welcome any interested parties.
