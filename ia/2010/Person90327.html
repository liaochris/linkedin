  • Sales Manager/Investment Specialist

    United Capital Group, LLC

    (Real Estate industry)

    December 2008 — January 2010 (1 year 2 months)

    United Capital Group (“UCG”) is a top tier firm specializing in bulk acquisition and disposition of residential properties purchased directly from several sources including real estate clearing houses and banks. The bulk purchases (“Tapes” or “Packages”) consist of 50 to 500 properties per Package, and through UCG each property is usually purchased for between 10 to 25 cents on the dollar. As a result of our long standing relationship with the major national real estate clearing houses and banks, UCG has the rare opportunity to be one of the first firms to review the Packages prior to them being disseminated to various investment groups. In short, we get to review and acquire the “pick of the crop” which translates to greater profits for our investors. Though it is crucial to acquire the properties for the least amount per dollar, it is just as important to acquire quality properties which can be quickly liquidated or held for a long term profit, depending on the investor’s appetite.

    During the past 2 ½ years UCG has brokered or purchased many Packages of residential properties. Each Package consisted of between 100 to 400 properties. In the last 3 months, UCG has moved nearly 700 homes for a combined total value of over $45 million. Each 100 home Package has an assessed value of $6-9 million with purchase prices equaling approximately $1.25- $1.5 million. All Tapes that UCG has been involved in from the start to finish (acquisition to disposition) have returned a profit. Most Packages are liquidated in one of a variety of methods within 120 days or less. Pre-tax net profits have historically averaged between 10 to 25% per Package some actually being much higher, which translates to up to 100% profit per annum with the acquisition of at least four Packages.
