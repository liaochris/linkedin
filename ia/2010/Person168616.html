Ramon J. Thomas’s Summary

Given my experience with the technical side of the Internet as well as my interest in how businesses use the Internet make me a valuable asset. My niche developed is understanding the psychology of technology. I have a network of contacts within the Internet, mobile industries which enable me to rapidly expand strategic partnerships & leverage market opportunities..

My use of Internet began in 1993 pre-dating the Web, and migrating to Social Media after 2003. Since that time I have worked with renown business researcher & technology author, Arthur Goldstuck, as well as Tony Roocroft, a leading expert on Google Adsense, Adwords in Africa.

While teaching Blogging & Social Networking workshops over the years, I have participated in forums like SA Online User Group (SAOUG), 27Dinner movement, ISASA conferences, government initiatives like the Lawyers for Human Rights focus group “Protecting Minors from Harmful Content via Mobile Phones”, as well as regular interaction with MXit, Childline, the Film & Publication Board.

My research into the Online Dating and Internet Cafe industries has positioned me as Online Behaviour Expert. This has led to the publication of ebooks "Parent's Guide to MXit" & “LinkedIn Lessons.” As a commentator I've been a frequent guest on CNBC Africa, 3Talk, Carte Blanche, Special Assignment; 702, SAFM, RSG; Business Day, Financial Mail, ITWeb and many more.

I currently hold a BSc in Computer Science, a Post Graduate Diploma in Information Management; and I am working towards a Masters in Information Science from University of Johannesburg . My first book, “The Psychology of Technology – Beyond Positive & Negative” will be published in April 2010. This book will bring a wider understanding to technology reducing complexity, removing confusion, and avoiding conflict in organisations.
