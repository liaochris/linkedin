Paul Knight’s Summary

I am a Senior Researcher at BlueGlue, I have had 4 years experience in the staffing industry since graduating in Sport and Physical Education at UWIC in Cardiff.

My field of specialisation is Web Development and Web Application Development, Analysis, Architecture and Project Management. I am always interested in hearing from individuals that come from XHTML, CSS, Javascript, Perl, Java, PHP, or any other IT development backgrounds. I am also working heavily with a range of support and consultancy roles within the IT business sector. To contact me directly please either Inmail an invite to connect with supporting information or email me direct at paul.knight@blueglue.co.uk

BlueGlue’s clients have outsourced all their staffing needs to ourselves (we work as an extension of their HR depts) and so our understanding of their culture and skill set’s required is that much more accurate than that of the generic contingency agency. This enables a much better candidate experience through the recruitment process whether the outcome is positive or negative.

Blueglue is the only RPO (recruitment process outsourcer) servicing high growth technology companies in the UK. Through a combination of Research and our Recruitment portal the time and pain associated with finding that next position is removed. For potential candidates that are looking at working for some of the most cutting edge innovative businesses then please do see a full listing of all our live jobs at www.blueglue.eu
