Johan Reinhoudt’s Summary

Clinical Development Performance, Leadership and Innovation Capability Architecture Development Consultant | Executive Business Advisor | Professional Speaker.

Johan is a practical business visionary with deep industry knowledge through more than two decades of experience in the healthcare and life sciences industries with demonstrated success in executive leadership roles. He has lived and worked in the Netherlands, France, Germany and the United States.

In his current role as President and Principal Consultant of Collaborative Primacy Life Sciences Consulting (CPLS Consulting), Johan collaborates with organizations around the world. He primarily assists life sciences industry leaders to advance their unique personal and organizational potential, inspiring new circumstances and success.

Throughout his career, Johan has provided pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies with significant cost savings and performance improvement through operational synergies and leadership development.

Johan has led global corporate initiatives ranging from re-engineering functions and interfaces to full restructuring and the outsourcing of business activities. Johan has had large (9-digit) budget and P&L responsibilities and has operated successfully in start-up and turn-around environments.

Carrying a pragmatic and flexible global worldview in all his activities, Johan is an organizational, leadership and team builder with the right sense of urgency, business and bio-pharmaceutical ethics.

A graduate of the University of Leiden, the Netherlands (B.Sc. in Anesthesiology), Johan is certified in General Management by the INSEAD-CEDEP business school of Fontainebleau, France. He is also certified in Executive Coaching (CMC) by the Behavioral Coaching Institute.

He is a member of the Thomson Reuters© Insight Community Experts, the Gerson Lehrman Group Consulting Management Platform™ and the 2010 Chief Learning Officer Business Intelligence Board.
