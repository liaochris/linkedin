Paul Fremder’s Summary

Source Associates, LLC is a specialty staffing
and consulting firm providing placement services on an interim, project and permanent basis in the accounting, finance and financial services fields. Our clients include Fortune 500, middle market, and emerging growth companies as well as Investment Banks, Private Equity and Venture Capital firms, across diverse industries. Our Managing Partners are CPA’s and bring an unparalleled blend of business and staffing industry experience.

I've had a rewarding career in the executive search business. I began my career with Lanier Worldwide, Inc. (Ricoh) where I recruited and trained a seasoned sales force while managing a multi-million dollar district in New York City. Prior to joining Source Associates LLC, I was a Senior Sales Executive for niche software companies where I was able to attract and retain Fortune 500 clients and mid sized organizations. I have partnered with and established myself amongst a strong network of senior level executives. I received a Bachelors degree from Rutgers University and reside in northern New Jersey with my wife and daughter.

Paul Fremder’s Specialties:

Senior level relationship building, strategic partnerships, exclusive and retainer agreements, negotiations, client centric, new business growth, social media and business networking.
