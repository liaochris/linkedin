Kathi Guiney -SPHR, GPHR’s Summary

Kathi Guiney SPHR, GPHR, President of YES! Your Human Resources Solution, is an experienced human resources executive with a unique and successful track record in delivering significant and sustainable improvements in business through people and operations management. She is recognized for her high level of customer service, can-do attitude and ability to form partnerships with executive teams, and business founders to achieve short and long-range business objectives that contribute to company productivity and profitability. Kathi’s background includes executive level human resource positions at both publicly traded and privately held companies in a variety of industries and revenue sizes. This experience includes service driven companies, manufacturing, entertainment and high technology. She is known as an effective change advocate in periods of rapid expansion including start-up and pre-IPO stages and the integration challenges and opportunities created by mergers and acquisitions. Additional areas of expertise include talent acquisition, project management, organization design and employee communication. Kathi’s key to successful contribution is to effectively utilize her 22 years of HR experience with her ability to have a strategic overview of the company’s business goals while being able as required, to be a tactical “hands-on” manager and project leader. YES! clients say it is the best of both worlds. Kathi has a passion for partnering with emerging growth companies to maximize the impact that best practice HR can have on achieving success.
