Micheline Bourque’s Summary

Professional Focus Group Moderator and Marketing consultant having served major companies for over 13 years. Bilingual and versatile, Micheline has worked on a variety of mandates that have suited the needs of marketing executives and department heads. She has been increasingly involved in Web 2.0 strategy initiatives. As a Focus Group moderator, she has partnered with several major Canadian marketing research firms as well as serving her own clients. An avid web ethusiast, she launched her first blog and has been moderating focus groups on line. She hopes to expand her work on the web both as a strategy consultant and a researcher.
