Christina Benz Meinberg’s Summary

As a teacher, researcher and management consultant in the area of sustainable enterprise, Christina both manages and develops profitable, environmentally friendly and socially sustainable business strategies. She has a decade of public and private sector experience working as environmental strategist and corporate responsibility consultant, business anlyst, writer and market research manager.

With a client history spanning from start-up to Fortune 100 companies, Christina provides sustainability advisory services throughout the operational management cycle, from strategy development and redesign to implementation, reporting and auditing.  Depending upon a corporation’s size and ultimate goals, sustainability strategy and implementation can include many different tools and methodologies such as benchmarking, management interviews, carbon footprinting, training workshops, risk assessment, sustainability reporting and auditing, and stakeholder mapping or engagement.

For more information on Christina’s qualifications and typical consulting services provided – or for a look at past consulting case studies, please visit www.tblstrategies.com.
