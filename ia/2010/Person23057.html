Beverly Harvey’s Summary

Beverly Harvey has been coaching senior managers and C-level executives in job search, career transition and career marketing strategies for 18 years.

Founder of HarveyCareers.com, Beverly is passionate about identifying her clients’ unique talents, crystallizing their brand, articulating their value proposition, and creating dynamic marketing materials and job search strategies to achieve a successful landing. She personally works with each client in all aspects of their career transition.
