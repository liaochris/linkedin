Victor Lund’s Summary

Victor Lund has a foundation of professional experience in Venture Capital, Business Development and Strategic Development which led to owning and operating a variety of companies across multiple industries.

In 1998, Victor Lund sold his share of a consumer products company to a Venture Capital firm which led to an Associate position at King Lee and Associates - a California VC firm. At King Lee and Associates, Lund was able to broaden his expertise in building businesses, and gained experience by holding executive level positions in VC funded businesses (many thanks to King Lee and Henry Hernandez).

In 2002, Lund and his partner Marilyn Wilson joined forces with a trusted friend, Mike Audet to form the WAV Group. WAV Group consults with Multiple Listing Service providers, technology companies that serve MLS, provides strategic planning for all types of corporations, is a leading research and analyst firm in the real estate industry, and venture investor.

Lund is a leading industry speaker on technology in real estate, having spoken or moderated at NAR, NAR Midyear, CAR, CMLS, RIS Meida, REAL Trends, Luxury Real Estate and Inman Connect.
