Paul Formichelli’s Summary

Paul is a commercial real estate broker and consultant and has lead a broad range of office and industrial real estate transactions on behalf of corporations in the New York City area and nationally, negotiating lease transactions totaling in excess of 5,000,000 square feet. He has a strong background in detailed market research and financial analysis and has expert witness experience.

His strategic, asset and transaction management experience consistently assists clients in meeting real estate portfolio and transaction-specific performance goals to better align real estate with core business objectives. This often results in assisting clients to reduce their occupancy costs significantly.
