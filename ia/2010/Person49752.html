Dave Morin’s Experience

  • Senior Platform Manager

    Facebook

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    October 2006 — February 2010 (3 years 5 months)

    Dave was the Senior Platform Manager at Facebook. He was responsible for the company’s platform strategy and further expanding the developer community.

    Dave was a co-inventor of Facebook Connect which was created to make the internet a more social place. Facebook Connect is now used by over 80,000 websites and applications to empower over 60 million users to connect and share with their friends across the Web, the iPhone, and many other devices and consoles.

    Early at Facebook, Dave was a co-inventor of the revolutionary Facebook Platform, the world’s first social platform. Since launch, the Facebook Platform developer community has has grown to over 1 million developers in 180 countries. Over 350,000 active applications have been created and have been installed over 5 billion times by over 350 million people worldwide.

  • Advisor

    Rupture

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    October 2006 — May 2008 (1 year 8 months)

    At Rupture, Dave joined as the first Advisor to the company. In this role, he helped to form the initial vision and product strategy alongside Founders Shawn Fanning and Jonathan Baudanza, helping to spark investment from legendary angel investor Ron Conway and attract interest from the gaming and investment communities. In early 2008, Rupture was acquired by Electronic Arts.

  • Manager, Creation and Collaboration

    Apple

    (Computer Hardware industry)

    March 2006 — October 2006 (8 months)

    Led product and marketing strategy for creation and collaboration technologies. Including iLife, iWork, iTunes U, and Mac OS X Leopard. Worked to turn Mac OS X into the most advanced web development platform on earth by integrating technologies such as Ruby on Rails, Mongrel, and other key technologies and frameworks. Also had dreams of one day bringing the social graph to Mac OS X...

  • Manager, Higher Education Marketing

    Apple

    (Public Company; AAPL; Computer Hardware industry)

    August 2004 — March 2006 (1 year 8 months)

    Led higher education marketing strategy and execution worldwide. Pioneered social media marketing strategy by becoming Facebook's first key business partner. Built worldwide marketing programs including a network of over 800 student marketing representatives.

  • Founder

    DM Design Studios

    (Internet industry)

    2000 — 2004 (4 years )

    Founded DM Design Studios in college dorm room, a design centric internet software company focused on building great user experiences, great web applications, and great communities on the internet.
