Jason Nunes’s Summary

Talented usability and user experience design specialist with well over a decade of interactive design experience in software (Mac and PC), web and ria design, and mobile and device design.

Creative manager, with experience building, mentoring, and managing user experience teams.

Client facing expert, with experience drafting proposals, and leading client meetings and presentations.

Broadcast and film production and post-production expert with over ten years of experience.

Extensive experience in all phases of user-centered design – Exploratory, Generative, and Evaluative – leading, coordinating, and conducting usability activities; designing and evaluating user interfaces, and managing projects.
Outstanding analytical and problem-solving skills.
