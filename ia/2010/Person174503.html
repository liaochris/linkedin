Robert Broadhurst’s Summary

Robert is a software Functional Test Analyst (manual testing) who has been in the industry for 30 years both as Analyst and a Programmer and for the last 20 years performing both Functional and Systems Testing on mainframes, client/server PC's and Web site applications. Robert is experienced in testing a wide variety of systems across numerous platforms and with his extensive experience as both a tester and as a programmer across numerous platforms he has a very good understanding of development and required test environments. He has recently passed the ISEB Intermediate certificate in Software Testing.
