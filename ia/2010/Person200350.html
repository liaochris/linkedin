Tom Chorske’s Summary

I'm focused on advising small to mid-sized businesses (owners, management, drivers/producers) on insurance matters and strategic business benefits. We're advocates for our clients and want to be their source of information. I believe we can contribute to improved profitability, retaining talented/key employees, and protecting their business with a variety of strategies. My goal is to help people feel a greater sense of control by working with them to understand, plan, and take action.

Another passion of mine is to help my friends and clients beyond the insurance and benefits realm and develop their business objectives (networking/referring) as well as charitable interests.

Following a 10 year NHL hockey career, I've spent my "life after hockey" understanding and working with small to medium size businesses. My professional life is now spent focused on helping businesses and higher income earners protect their assets/balance sheets.
