Stephan ten Kate’s Summary

In short, I get enthusiastic about the social aspects of knowledge sharing, especially with regard to computer-mediated interactions between people. This focus has driven me in all my prior activities, and will continually do this in the future.

As a son of an entrepreneur, from the beginning my interests laid in the diverse processes that exist within organizations. As such, I used my first study (Business Studies) to get an general and interdisciplinary understanding of organizational life.

During my master I undertook a research within the national police information department on change management, did an internship at IBM Netherlands focusing on social media and recruitment and completed my master thesis about trust within social networking sites. During these activities, I became aware that my passion laid within the area of knowledge management and its underlying social processes: social interactions.

The subsequent master (Information & Knowledge Management) provided me the in-depth knowledge and academical skills that I was looking for (including a scientific publication and research projects for Reaal insurances and the national institute for public health and environment (RIVM)). As for now, I am finishing my master thesis about social influences within online consumer communities and I am exploring the job market in order to choose a future job I love - that way I don't have to work for the rest of my life ;-)

Stephan ten Kate’s Interests:
