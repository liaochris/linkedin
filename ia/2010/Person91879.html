Jonathan Beutler’s Summary

I am the President of RealTrust Corp. (www.realtrustco.com), and was previously the Director of Communications. RealTrust is a new company that provides a unique and innovative solution to the mortgage crisis.

I enjoy traveling and studying Mandarin Chinese. Also, I am working on a forthcoming book about Brazil's rise in the global economy. Also, I am the cofounder of and a contributor to the blog "Monster Countries" about geopolitics, diplomacy, and trade.

I have worked and studied in Europe, South America, Asia, as well as throughout the United States.

I have experience working in government agencies, the private sector, non-profits, and higher education. I have work experience as a public affairs consultant, assisting companies and organizations connect with government and other public organizations.

I received my masters degree from the UCLA School of Public Affairs (Department of Public Policy) and my BA from BYU.

I also spend time in various community projects:
- Member, Board of Directors, Chinese Chamber of Commerce
- Member, Board of Directors, Los Angeles Maritime Museum
- Chair, International Trade Roundtable committee, Torrance Chamber of Commerce
- Member, Exxon Mobil Community Advisory Board
- Member, Global Initiatives/International Trade Committee, Los Angeles Area Chamber of Commerce
- Torrance Young Professionals Group

I am a participant in the Los Angeles World Affairs Council, the L.A. Area Chamber of Commerce, the Southern California Public Affairs Council, the International Visitors Council of Los Angeles, the American Translators Association, Foreign Policy Association, United Nations Association of America, etc.

I am really interested in becoming involved in projects anywhere in the world.

Jonathan Beutler’s Specialties:

I speak English, Portuguese, and Spanish fluently, and have working knowledge of German and French (written). Additionally, I have studied Arabic, (Mandarin) Chinese, Latin, Greek, Japanese, and Russian.

I am very experienced in grassroots politics, political risk advisory, consulting, editing, event planning, social media, anlaysis, research, localization, multilingual translation, econometrics analytical software, and policy analysis.
