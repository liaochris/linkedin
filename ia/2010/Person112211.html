Kathy Helmetag’s Summary

I'm a research tribologist with extensive formulation experience - I call my area of expertise chemical tribology, and I'm one of less than 100 people world-wide who list this new field as their focus. Test method development for metalworking processes is one of my specialties, and I can bench model many complex processes using lab-scale equipment and computational methods. My advanced chemistry education makes bridging the gap between application and formulation less of a hassle while I work on making pressworking of metal a science instead of an art. My publications have focused on testing and formulation and I can often be found and industry conferences presenting new work or teaching in my subject. The human side of innovation and idea management is one of my favorite coaching topics

That's the formal stuff. For fun, I cover the auto industry for CarDomain.com, have two Volkswagens under various stages of customization and performance modification, and am active in Scouting as a den leader and Troop staff member. For fun work, I customize other people's cars through Bolt.

Kathy Helmetag’s Specialties:

Tribology, metalworking lubrication, crankcase lubrication, test method development, public speaking, technical writing, multi-level technical communication, product line management, innovation, project portfolio management
