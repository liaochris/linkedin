Alan Radding’s Summary

As a writer and ghostwriter, I help business executives put their ideas into words that appear as published books, articles, white papers, blogs, website content, and more. The ideas are those of the executive and only the name of the executive appears on the work. My job as the ghostwriter is to fashion those ideas into a coherent piece and present the ideas in the most compelling way. In addition, I work as a freelance journalist and as an independent media content developer creating content for books, publications, websites, webinars, presentations, video, and web 2.0 opportunities. As the Research Director of IndependentAssessment, I design, develop, and execute research and analysis projects for a wide range of clients, including IBM and HP.
