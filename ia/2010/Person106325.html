Joshua E. Zabec’s Summary

My father Michael Zabec writer of "Ten Steps to High Definition Selling" introduced me to the sales game at the early age of 15 and I have been doing it ever since, canvassing and telemarketing since I was 14.

Since it is professional experience I can say net business of $1,000,000+ is a beautiful thing in a reccession or whatever you call it, and thats while managing an office of 10. Windows and doors (no additions, kitchens, baths, etc.)

I have been around the home improvement industry all my life specializing in replacement, energy effective, and higher end products.

Currently I am pursing a life long dream of motivational/sales speaking, I am an adrenaline junky who would love to stand in front of thousands and have that feeling in my stomach only to open my mouth and talk to ahhh them all, I dream of this and have done it numerous times in my head its call "THE SECRET". I say dream but I should'nt say that because a dream is only a dream if you don't pursue it, and I am so we will call it my calling.

I have spoken for 5 various organizations from 10 people to 85 business owners and must say it is the greatest feeling, my father is a motivational speaker and thats what has let me dip my feet in the water. I think about the USA everyday waiting for my turn.

This economy is one of the best once you know how to stomp around in it, and in my 7 year selling career I have seen a vast change in the marketplace. As with anything you must be able to adapt and once you do you will realize its really not as bad as everyone makes it. Email me we can talk more about this.

My goal is to write my own book based on sales by the age of 23, Feb 8th is my birthday so I have a lot of time.

Close rate is nearing 80%...

Being financially stable enough to support a family without having to work, Always loving my wife who helps me get to where I'm going every single day and for putting up with me for 16 years now I'm not allowed to drink caffeine.
