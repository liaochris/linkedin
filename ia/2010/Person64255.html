William J. Brown’s Summary

■ Innovative, forward-thinking senior interactive commerce, marketing, and operations executive with 19 years of results-driven accomplishment.

■ Career includes leadership roles for FORTUNE 50, mid-cap and start-up companies, in global financial services, hospitality & travel, consumer electronics, and mobile services. Consulted to private equity and institutional investment funds. Taught "internet strategy" and "innovation" on graduate school faculty in the MBA program at University of Miami School and the Executive MBA program at Florida Atlantic University.

■ Led global functions ranging to 170+ FTE across multiple locations and as many as 13 teams, plus substantial partner relationships involving people & technology.

■ Led digital branding, acquisition marketing and retention programs across several companies; undertaken groundbreaking work in behavioral targeting, segmentation-based profiling, and re-marketing during all phases of the relationship cycle to drive rich consumer engagement and conversion in high-volume e-commerce environments.

■ Industry-leading accomplishments in driving "voice of the customer" success through web-based self-service, live agent-supported servicing channels, and social media response.

■ Highly analytical and metrics-driven approach to planning, managing and executing online business initiatives - yet also instinctive and flexible. Speaker, panelist, moderator at conferences in United States, Canada, and Europe. Quoted in industry & retail publications along with vendor case studies. Over 25 publication credits on range of digital business topics.
