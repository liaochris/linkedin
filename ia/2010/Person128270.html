Lisa Penelton’s Summary

Lisa Penelton is a data strategist who develops pragmatic marketing and policy solutions. She is committed to transforming rigorous methodology into results that are measurable, actionable and understandable. Her work philosophy integrates communications with focused analysis because she believes that empirical work is not done well unless it is fully integrated into strategy and translated into tactics.

Penelton served in data strategy roles with Razorfish, United Way, Draft (now DraftFCB), GreyDirect, Ameritech (now AT&T), Claritas and others. She has a long history of customer and market-level analysis with a career focused on comparative methodology and program optimization. Lisa prepared for her marketing career as an academic researcher at the University of Chicago, Northwestern University, and the University of Illinois.

Penelton holds a master’s degree from the Irving B. Harris School at the University of Chicago and a bachelor’s degree from the University of Illinois.
