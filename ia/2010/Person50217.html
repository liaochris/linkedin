David Bobzien’s Summary

I build websites. A general statement, but the most accurate given my range of competencies in internet communications. I developed my first website in 1995 as a graduate assistant working on a project for a federal agency. Since then, I've produced/developed/designed websites for non-profits, small and large businesses and university programs. Helping people and organizations communicate on the Internet to their fullest potential is my passion.

I serve as a Nevada Assemblyman, representing District 24 (Old Northwest Reno, Northeast Reno and parts of the North Valleys.) Please visit: www.davidbobzien.com

David Bobzien’s Specialties:

Web project management, communications strategy, information architecture, usability research, accessibility, training, (X)HTML/CSS, ASP/PHP, open source CMS implementations. Extensive experience with standards-based web design, Dreamweaver templates and large-scale Macromedia Contribute deployments.

  • Campus Webmaster

    University of Nevada, Reno

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry)

    May 2000 — July 2007 (7 years 3 months)

    I served as the University of Nevada, Reno's first professional managing their web presence. I built a team of one web designer and several student developers that produced websites for departments and programs and managed the main university website.

    Projects I managed involved multi-disciplinary teams (photographers, writers, strategists) building sites from 10 pages to 1,000. Sites were designed for various content management systems (Contribute, WordPress, in-house developed, etc.) for content teams of 2 to 30 to communicate effectively on the web. My responsibilities ranged from coding and design to strategy, usability, and information architecture.

    I directed and delivered training and support to faculty, staff and students to maximize the campus community's contribution to the university's web presence. I oversaw and/or consulted on outsourced development projects, providing "translation" services to ensure that clients' business goals were achieved by vendors.
