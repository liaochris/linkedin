Rachel Sweet’s Summary

My primary business is SweetSkills which provides business development services such as training, event management, public speaking and project management centred around business development activities.

I have over 17 years of experience in business sectors such as retail, hospitality, tourism, financial services, care and the public sector.

I have worked for such companies as Virgin, Business Link, The Somerset Chamber of Commerce & Industry, Superdrug, Western Provident Association (WPA) as well as some smaller retail and tourism organisations over the years.

I have delivered customer service and business development training and advice to several businesses mainly around the South West of England.
