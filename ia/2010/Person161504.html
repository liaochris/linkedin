Paul Travis CMC’s Summary

I literally wrote the book on interim management for revenue (co-authored) which was just adopted in the curriculum of a U.K. university course and mentioned in "42 Rules for Driving Success with Books".

I'm pleased to say "Leadership on Demand: How Smart CEO's Tap Interim Management to Drive Revenue" is getting positive reviews! (Is http://www.leadership-on-demand.com on your LinkedIn reading list?)

Throughout decades in high tech, marketing, business development and consulting, I've learned there are really only 3 ways to increase revenue:
* increase the total number of customers,
* increase the frequency of customer visits, and/or
* increase the purchase per visit.

I enjoy "helicoptering" between the strategic and tactical levels of projects, and have developed a focus on "marketing intelligence" [the nexus between a company's product/service, its brand promise, its customer constituencies, and actual patronage].

My industry sweet spots are:
* software/internet,
* food/beverage, and
* manufacturing organizations.

E-mail me to schedule a time to discuss your situation or challenge. I'm always looking for an opportunity to beat my standing record -- increasing revenues 1,000% in 9 months (leading to a $77 million acquisition).

And if I can't help, I'll be happy to make a referral.
