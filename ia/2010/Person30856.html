Heather Burns’s Summary

Heather Burns is a Senior Executive Search Consultant with Sanford Rose Associates - San Francisco, an executive search firm filling mid to senior-level positions within the medical devices/diagnostics, biotechnology, and pharmaceutical segments of the worldwide life sciences industry. Founded in 1959, the Sanford Rose Associates ("SRA") organization serves the needs of corporate and institutional clients from over sixty worldwide offices.

Heather joined Sanford Rose Associates in early 2007, and has since been involved in the placement of executives across many functional areas, including Product Development, Clinical Research, Medical Directorship, and Quality and Regulatory Affairs. Previously, she held the position of Senior Recruiter with a NE Ohio Executive Search firm, providing recruitment services for top Midwest Manufacturing and Healthcare clients. In addition, she was formally a Clinical Research Recruiter with a Connecticut based firm focused specifically on technical and scientific professionals for major clients such as Pfizer, Abbott Labs, Ethicon Endo-Surgery and Procter & Gamble. Heather holds a BS in Public Health Education from the University of Toledo.
