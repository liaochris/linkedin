Tao Garcia’s Summary

After an early career as an award-winning art director in the advertising industry, I turned towards producing – running the New York office for international production agency, Nexus Productions. There I managed multimillion dollar campaigns for companies such as CITGO Petroleum and Diageo Brands (Johnnie Walker, Jose Cuervo, and J&B).

Always having a passion for movies, I decided to switch my focus to narrative filmmaking. In the beginning, I was eager to get my hands dirty and did not shy away from any position. I have held a role in every department in the film industry, particularly distinguishing in Production, Animation and Art Direction.

Notable projects include: J.T Petty’s “Blood Red Earth” released by Lionsgate Entertainment, international festival hit – “The River of Copsa Mica,” and the neo-noir thriller “What Comes Around”.

In this past year alone, I Produced and Designed two independent features, both with Leonard Zelig and Javier Perez-Karam – “SubHysteria” and “Bloodbath Test”, which will be released on the international market in 2010, as well as continuing to develop animated content for a wide array of clients.
