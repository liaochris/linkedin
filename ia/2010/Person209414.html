Ulrica Hung’s Summary

Highly accomplished and results-driven Quality Assurance Manager and Engineer, with a verifiable track record of managing and testing complex applications deployed to on-premise and SaaS environments. Extensive experience leading both onshore and offshore test teams. Successfully balanced competing priorities while managing multiple parallel release streams in Agile SCRUM development environments. Ability in translating product requirements to test plans, perform gap and risk analysis, optimize resources to increase productivity and reduce costs. Detail-oriented and organized leader, with strong technical, collaboration, and problem solving skills to consistently deliver projects on time and within budget.
Key Accomplishments:
- Successfully led and completed the largest worldwide beta program in the company's history with over 50 customers and partners around the world
- Recruited and managed a new automation team to automate the acceptance test suite using QTP resulted in a reduction of 8 person-days of manual testing per build turnover
- Lead the first company-wide application building contest to promote the launching of the company’s new Web 2.0 product. As a result, the Sales, Marketing, and Support groups were more equipped to sell and support this new product
