Alicia Benjamin’s Summary

Imagine this: potato chip crumbs stuck to thighs, a voice-simulated remote control and a collection of Kevin Smith films. Sound like paradise? I didn't think so.

To me, paradise is imagination and wordsmith play. It’s provoking ideas, sharing accomplishments and surrounding myself with extraordinary talent. To me, success doesn’t come from thinking outside the box; iIt comes from standing in its ashes.
