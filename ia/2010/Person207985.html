Trevan Van Buren’s Summary

Currently working as EMI Music’s first Director of Digital Operations for Latin America. I began my career at EMI in 2005, when I helped launched EMI’s digital music campaign in Latin America. As a member of the Global Supply Chain team, I train all labels in the region on digital production and manage the supply chain process for all digital download, video, mobile and streaming partners in the Region. Prior to my operations role, I served as EMI's first Latin America Director of Digital Development and managed a regional video monetization program and a regional sales reporting implementation and cleanup project.
Before joining EMI, I worked in the financial information technology field in the US and abroad (Mexico/Central America) and
earned my degree from Boston College with a degree in Economics and Latin American studies. I am fluent in English, Spanish and Portuguese, and in my free time I play string bass and Brazilian drums in a New York based hip-hop band, Turnstylz.
