Scott Michaels "LION" scott.michaels@gmail.com’s Summary

Scott Michaels, Founder & President
Scott has the day-to-day, tactical executive leadership role as President of ROCKETSTIX Energy. Overall, he executes on the strategy and long-term plans for the company. Prior to creating ROCKETSTIX, Scott developed and managed XShots Energy, a precursor to our current company.

Scott’s innovative spirit prompted him to market one division of his former company, SWG Enterprises, Inc., to over 130 countries in less than 12 months using SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and SEM (Search Engine Marketing) techniques he created. When the company was acquired he had over 200,000 subscribers.

Scott has 20 years experience as a sales engineer and executive, working with large corporations such as eBay, Verizon, SprintPCS and JetBlue Airways. Scott specializes in using cost-effective eMarketing strategies to not only market, but also track and analyze company’s sales and marketing efforts.

Scott's background in SaaS (Software-As-A-Service) technology allowed him to take FranklinCovey’s PlanPlus Online to the global marketplace in less than eight months, resulting in a 3,233% increase in profit. This effort helped attract several large investors, including Donald L. Lucas, who has funded such companies as Oracle, Adobe, Macromedia and over 50 other technology-related companies.

Scott was educated in sales and business management at Weber State University, where he received a bachelor's degree in Technical Sales with emphasis in computing and Japanese.
