Krisstina Wise’s Summary

I own an Austin boutique real estate firm that is nationally recognized as a leader in the industry. My firm has won many awards over the past year for our innovation and early adoption of high-technologies, real estate social marketing & media practices, social networking expertise, building of search engine optimization & website strategies, blogging and other new media approaches for serving our customers superior to our competitors. The GoodLife Team culture and reputation is fast-paced, innovative and highly knowledgeable and professional. In an industry that has been devastated by subprime meltdown & recession with lack of invention and change, my business, in contrast, is thriving and growing.

Bio:
An accomplished business woman in the domain of residential real estate, Krisstina's “uncommon” real estate practice is ranked in the top 1% nationwide. Her success comes as a result of advanced adoption of innovative technologies, an entrepreneurial spirit, and a far-reaching commitment to service – all uncommon for the industry. Sprinting beyond the antiquated systems common in the tradition-bound world of real estate, Krisstina’s real estate business is a leader in marketing systems and internet lead generation through social media platforms. The GoodLife Team brand is recognized locally and nationally as a technology-based firm with a rich commitment to take care of the concerns of their clients.

In addition, Krisstina is a national real estate speaker and trainer and has founded two real estate technology start-ups.

Krisstina is busy raising 2 teenagers with her husband Garry. In her past she has been known for running marathons--today running and yoga is for staying fit and healthy.

In 2008, as Chairman of the Board of Directors for Planet Cancer--a non-profit that helps young adults with cancer--her leadership was instrumental in increasing the non-profit's event revenue by over 500%.
