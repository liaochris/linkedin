Shannon Bullard Sweetser’s Summary

I'm a passionate writer, community manager, marketer, and beer-lover who is currently looking for my next big job opportunity. I've had the extreme fortune of working at three captivating and diverse start-up environments: Hubspot, Zeer, and Smart Destinations.

During my brief contracted stint at HubSpot, I had the opportunity to experience the HubSpot Inbound Marketing methodology firsthand. After acing my Inbound Marketing training, I went to work creating remarkable marketing content for the HubSpot Inbound Marketing blog, an Ad Age Power 150 blog with over 25,000 blog subscribers.

As Community Manager of Zeer, I enthusiastically facilitated lead all customer retention and acquisition initiatives, organized and ran low-cost focus groups and usability sessions, developed our social media & search engine optimization strategy, managed our performed QA and bug testing, drove business and engagement through social media, wrote and updated creative copy for newsletters, Zeer.com and brochures, and helped deliver exceptional customer service for all Zeer members.

Prior to my work at Zeer, I worked at Smart Destinations, a web-based travel and tourism company, where I was responsible for marketing & SEO research as well as leveraging, creating and maintaining online relationships with potential (and current) customers via blogs, forums, and online communities.

Hailing from the South and educated in the North, I graduated magna cum laude with a B.F.A. in Writing, Literature, and Publishing and minor in Marketing Communications from Emerson College.
