Monet Colbert’s Summary

Licensed Cosmetologist /Makeup Artist Since 1996

Monet is not only an entrepreneur with a great track record of success but undoubtedly she is a caring mother and wife.

As an accomplished athlete, writer, model, and actress. Monet is by far one of the most respected leaders in the fitness industry as a makeup artist today. She has inspired both women and men from all walks of life nationally and internationally. Her passion to give her all and commitment to follow through demonstrates that she is a multifaceted force to be reckoned with.

Revolutionizing the beauty industry with her cutting – edge cosmetics line and vision for the future guarantees that Shades of Monet® is well on its way to becoming a household name!
