Phil Chipouras’s Summary

Phil Chipouras
Career Coach / Consultant
_____________________________ . . . __________________________________

Phil Chipouras was a Career Coach/Consultant in the Denver office of Lee Hecht Harrison, a large outplacement organization, for the past eight years. He is presently an independent consultant working with people who need career transition help. He has more than 35 years of human resource development experience working with executives, employees and teams from various industries including: telecommunications, financial services, e-learning, food distribution, manufacturing and government. Phil frequently presents to groups throughout Colorado about career transitions and job trends. He communicates with a vast network of professional contacts. Phil received the LHH 2008 President's Award for Excellence as a Job Market Consultant.

Phil’s expertise is in assisting individuals and organizations to effectively align their interests, goals and leadership competencies to meet their customers’ requirements and sustain a competitive advantage. A motivational career coach, Phil supports individuals through a career transition and encourages both growth and learning. Phil also consults with organizations in the design and implementation of simple and effective methods for strategic planning and management improvement.

Phil holds an M.Ed in Adult Learning from Colorado State University and is certified in the use of various career coaching tools and methodologies.
