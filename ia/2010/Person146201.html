Michael King’s Summary

Over 12 years experience working in broadcast operations for TV and radio within the BBC and commercial radio sector. Predominantly working in network management and broadcast operations overseeing the technical and editorial day-to-day running of national radio & digital TV networks and interactive services. Devising and delivering technological strategies through the development of business and broadcast solutions. Integrating new and existing multimedia technological infrastructures and adapting workflows to fully realise the outcomes and benefits to the business, formulating and delivering the necessary training and support as required.

Michael King’s Specialties:

Talent management, coaching and mentoring, radio production, project management, studio design, broadcast training
