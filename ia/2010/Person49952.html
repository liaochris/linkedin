Dave Weinberg’s Summary

Creative, Design and Marketing Services - Across Media. Dave Weinberg helps businesses develop purpose-driven integrated communications for online and print-based media.

Formerly Senior Art Director with L.L.Bean in Freeport, Maine, Dave was instrumental in advancing the design and usability of llbean.com along with creative, design and marketing synergies across-channel.

He is a founding board member and President of the Maine chapter of AIGA, the professional association for design. He currently serves as adjunct faculty at University of Southern Maine where he teaches Graphic Communications and Cross-Media Publishing.

Dave is the creator of cellphoneSketchpad™ - mobile journal. cyber comic. digital rorschach. An accomplished Illustrator, he holds a BFA from the School of Visual Arts in NYC.

Dave continues to explore a broad range of creative forms, both visual and performance-based. Over the years, designer, writer, art/creative director, stand-up comic, improvisational actor, cartoonist, banana-wild blueberry pancake maker.

Dave is a proud and adoring Dad who also enjoys a really good nap!

Dave Weinberg’s Specialties:

Cross-Media Creative Services - Creative Direction, Graphic Design, Branding and Identity Across Media Channels, User Interface and Interaction Design, cellphoneSketchpad
