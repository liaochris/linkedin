Mark Jackson’s Summary

Mark Jackson fondly known as “MJ” has become the go to life coach for people who want to achieve more and create balance in their lives.

He began as a young man with the desire to transform his life of poverty into entrepreneurial success and now others go to him in order to accomplish the same.

MJ loves seeing people like you discover the secret which is already inside of them.

When not traveling in the US and abroad, sharing his dynamic message, MJ is usually at home working on his many national and international business ventures. Main passions: faith, family, golf and real estate.
