Hans Neubert’s Summary

Multidisciplinary creative with 16 years experience in positions ranging from Executive Creative Director, Managing Director, to Partner and Vice President of Global Design.

Current and past clients include: Altoids, American Express, Apollo Theater, Bacardi, Ben & Jerry’s, Citibank, Coca-Cola, Columbia Sportswear, Dow Jones, Goldman Sachs, HBO, Hitachi, HHMI, IBM, Johnnie Walker, McKinsey, Kraft, Mattel, Microsoft, MTV, Morgan Stanley, Nickelodeon, Nikon, Playboy, Qualcomm, Slim Jim, Sony, Staples, Steelcase, Texaco, Time Warner, Tishman Speyer, United Technologies.
