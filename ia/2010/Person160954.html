Paul Isakson’s Summary

Others have called me a globally recognized leader in strategic thinking for the modern era of marketing.

I prefer to say that I'm a strategist and creative thinker driven to help brands and people find better ways forward.

I create strategies and ideas that deliver meaningful experiences between people and the things they use, buy, see and do.

I believe that the future of marketing is not about saying and doing things to people—it's about doing things with and for them.

I'm always looking for interesting opportunities to do just that.
