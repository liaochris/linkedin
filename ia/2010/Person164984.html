  • Staff Geologist

    PWT

    (Environmental Services industry)

    November 1999 — December 2000 (1 year 2 months)

    Contract work for USGS Yucca Mt. Branch
    Provided technical hydrogeologic support to the former USGS Yucca Mt. Branch by assisting with the characterization of the Yucca Mt. Project for the planned future use as a high level nuclear waste repository.
    Helped to design, plan and implement Yucca Mt. tunnel infiltration and percolation studies. Conducted experiments in the Tunnel using tension infiltrometers situated on fractures, faults and solid rock faces; conducted data gathering using Campbell scientific data loggers, pressure transducers, neutron logging and bromine tracer tests. Worked with Lawrence Livermore Lab personnel to set up Telemetry for remote monitoring of infiltration experiment data from the Tunnel to Livermore, CA. Coordinated with DOE personnel for Tunnel access.
    Conducted laboratory simulations using crushed Yucca Mt. Tuff at a designated USGS lab located on DOE's Losee Road Facility. Obtained soil data properties from crushed Yucca Mt. Tuff such as bulk density, permeability and porosity. Conducted column tests with Yucca Mt. crushed Tuff to observe and calculate wetting front and model breakthrough curves. Attended DOE radiological training for dosimeter use. Attended meetings at the Summerlin Offices and conducted, data analyses and QA/QC of data collected in the Tunnel.
