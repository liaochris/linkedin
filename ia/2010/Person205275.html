Todd Basche’s Summary

As CEO of WordLock we are bringing new innovative consumer products to market. We design, develop and distribute products that are fun and make life easier for the consumer.

Strong technical leader with a proven track record of delivering complex products to market on-time and on quality. Experienced in building and scaling successfull, profitable businesses and development teams in consumer products, enterprise software and hardware companies. Able to synthesize customer requirements into a product vision and motivate the teams to develop and deliver those products.

Todd Basche’s Specialties:

Inventing new products and creating whole new categories. Innovating in consumer products both low technology and high technology.
Experience in Consumer software applications as well as Enterprise Software. Electronic Distribution and E-Commerce centered around PC based games. Content distribution Software for Music Labels, MPAA studios and Game developers. (ESD and ECD) Excellent track record creating award winning hardware and software products in both the consumer and the enterprise market.
