Troy Jensen’s Summary

Troy Jensen currently serves as Chief Executive Officer of SolVentus, Inc., a uniquely focused revenue consultancy, providing CleanTech firms with a variety of consultative services, including complete funding and revenue modeling, sales and marketing services, and synergistic cooperative partnership opportunities. In addition, SolVentus has a rapidly-expanding residential and commercial solar sales division, providing partner solar installation firms with a steady stream of closed and financed install projects. SolVentus works with clients involved in many sectors of the CleanTech Industry, including Solar and Wind Energy, Electric Vehicle OEMs and Charging Infrastructure, Smart Grid, Biofuel, Biowaste, Clean Coal, among others.

Troy has expertise from the ground up, working onsite at at small residential solar installs, to helping broker large commercial and residential Power Purchasing Agreements (PPAs). He has spent his career in marketing, sales, finance, technology startup fundraising and monetization modeling throughout the GreenTech, Digital, Mobile and Technology sectors. His strong background and focus on high-margin gross revenue acceleration has led to the generation of over $470 million in revenue over a 13-year career. Troy has led hundreds of sophisticated digital sales and marketing campaigns and technology offerings for many Fortune 500 clients. He has also founded, co-founded, consulted and led funding efforts for several successful technology startups.

When not consumed by all things CleanTech, Troy's passion is in the air - he has his private pilots license and loves to find any excuse to get "wheels-up." Other interests include traveling, reading, golf, gadgets and emerging technology, and is a avid Dolphins, Yankees and Lakers fan.
