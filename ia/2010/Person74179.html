Geoff Selvidge’s Summary

I love to tryout new ideas believing that 'If you always do what you have always done, you will always get what you have always got!'

I have spent 30 years selling, marketing and managing customer expectation in the field of high value consumer products and B2B products. I have service industry experience too.
