Joe Budelis’s Summary

Specializing in T1, Ethernet, DS3, MPLS, Point-to-Point (PtP), OC3, and OC12 bandwidth and IP service, but consulting at all levels down to home office Telecommunications requirements, I find the best Telecommunications service to satisfy an organization’s needs: the most appropriate product from the best Telecom carrier. We have a patented system that offers real time price quotes for T1, DS3, PRI and T1 data and voice services and we are first to offer you a real time tool for determining if Fast Ethernet or GIGE is available at your location(s). Our automated MPLS tool determines which carriers can provide the services you require at your locations and then requests quotes from these carriers.

Once you submit your information and obtain price quotes, I analyze your needs and assist you in evaluating Carriers and their plans so that you can make an informed decision. Because of our volume, providers give us their best prices, compete against one another for your business, and they pay us a commission. On our T1 and higher services, we include a Low Price Guarantee! Go to http://T1Guy.com or http://www.shopforocx.com to obtain a real time quote and to initiate a relationship for complimentary consulting service.
