Edward Vertigo’s Summary

I am a Freelance Quality Assurance Video Game Tester and Video Game Consultant who aspires to be a Video Game Producer, Developer, Designer, and/or Marketer. At the moment, I do have a job that I'm hoping will become a long and fruitful one, but I have to wait and see for now.

I have experience in both working with teams and working solo for various companies, onsite and offsite (most of it being black box testing). Since July of 2008, I have helped to improve projects such as single player games, co-op games, MMO games, handheld games, PC games, and console games, but am usually not allowed to name specific game or company details due to legally binding Non-Disclosure Agreements.

Testing for bugs, checking game balance and features, finding and reporting any and all deviations from the correct design and functionality (no matter how small) are all implemented. Thanks to my written and spoken communication skills, I am able to help improve the quality of the game while demonstrating my talent for observation, patience, group interaction, and overall tenacity.

I am a current member of several gaming groups, including the IGDA: International Game Developers Association. Though I have strong skills in Testing, Art, Design, Production, and Marketing, I have almost no skills when it comes to Programming. Writing code (such as C++) is not within my range of skills.
