Travis Neal’s Summary

I am passionate about connecting people to ideas and fascinated by how we all perceive, respond to and share information – I am always watching, analyzing and producing new ways to connect people with ideas. Employers consistently attribute my exceptional project management skills to my unique blend of analytical, technical and creative experience.

As a manager and consultant, I have developed technical projects, communication strategies and hundreds of media products for organizations including Macworld, Waste Management and the Defense Intelligence Agency.

• As an Intelligence Analyst in the U.S. Army, I analyzed data, clearly explained what it indicated and made actionable recommendations. In addition, I developed effective production systems that capitalized on technology and the skills of individual team members.

• At IDG, a multi-billion dollar IT company, I developed multimedia presentations for the company’s senior executives (CEO, President, ...), provided onsite technical management for events (London, Barcelona, ...) and coordinated the resources for our six-person team. I produced presentations for key customers (Apple, Microsoft, ...), materials for product launches (magazines, expos, ...), and media for expositions (Macworld, PC World Expo, ...).

• As a consultant I have built over 350 new media products, developed brand communication strategies for 5 organizations, been the Lead User Interface Designer for 8 projects and built the communication architecture, visual design and the underlying code for over 20 web sites.

Currently, I am seeking a management position in the San Francisco Bay Area. What makes this an interesting challenge is that my skills cross a number of different disciplines and often apply to multiple positions (e.g., program management, message strategy, new media). If combining several roles would be of particular value to your organization, I welcome a no-pressure discussion to explore the possibilities.
