Neil Fairbrother’s Summary

pod3.tv is the UK's award winning and fastest growing independent webTV company. Think Channel 4, but download - commercial TV for iTunes on PC, Mac,Video iPod, iPhone and TV.

pod3.tv's in-podcast advertising provides a great way for companies to get their messages between the ears of, and in front of the eyes of, the download generation - people that consume web content away from the internet - where banner ads can't reach.
