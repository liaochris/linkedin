Zhihao Yang’s Summary

Dr. Zhihao Yang is a founder and currently the CTO of NanoMas Technologies, Inc. He had held various senior principal positions with several enterprises, including Eastman Kodak Company and Sinus Rhythm Technologies, Inc. Dr. Yang is an inventor of over 20 issued US patents and an author of numerous technical publications and presentations internationally.

Zhihao Yang’s Specialties:

A technology entrepreneur who brings technology to market.

Technical expertise includes nanomaterials, nanofabrication, microelectronics, MEMS, electronics materials, solar cells and clean energy, microchip packaging, printed electronics and displays, organic semiconductors, coating & printing technologies, and medical devices...
