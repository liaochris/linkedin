Bill Stokes’s Summary

Bill Stokes is an executive search consultant, managing client development and leading executive-level searches. Practice areas are Public Affairs (including government relations, lobbying, and advocacy) and Public Relations (including new media, marketing communications, and investor relations). Sectors covered include policy intelligence, clean tech, clean energy, defense, government contracting, technology and telecommunications sectors. Clients include law firms, associations and non-profits, corporate representation offices, government and public relations firms, foundations and grant-making institutions.

Concurrently, Bill serves Chairman of the Washington Network Group (WNG), an association of professionals representing private sector firms, multilateral institutions, government agencies, the Congress, foreign embassies, and the media.

Previously, Bill was Managing Director of EndeavorConnnect LLC, where he advised technology and new media companies on market entry strategies. Bill is a seasoned non-profit manager, serving at the American Institute for Contemporary German Studies (AICGS), the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS), and the National Council for International Trade Development (NCITD).

He holds a bachelor's degree in History and Political Science from the College of William & Mary in Virginia and went on to attend George Washington University's Elliott School of International Affairs.

Past affiliations include the Institute of Entrepreneurship at the Harry F. Byrd, Jr. School of Business, Shenandoah University (advisory board member); the World Affairs Council of Washington, D.C. (board member); the International Business Exchange Council (IBEC) of the Fairfax County Chamber of Commerce (founding member); First Tuesday Washington, D.C. (city leader); the German American Business Association (board member); and the Phillips Exeter Alumni Association of Washington, D.C. (president).
