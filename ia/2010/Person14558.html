Ann Oliveri’s Summary

Ann Oliveri is a marketing coach and brand strategist with 30 years experience helping entrepreneurial leaders transform and sustain mission-driven organizations by:

 Aligning interests through business strategy and integrated marketing communications;

 Synchronizing grassroots and project teams with visioning, branding, and culture; and,

 Creating new platforms for corporate, government, and civic leadership.

The result is a sustainable engine of knowledge and learning, problem-solving and innovation, social change and wealth creation.

Ann Oliveri’s Specialties:

Ann Oliveri builds:

 CAPACITY through leadership, organization, and new business development;

 BRANDS through strategic marketing, communications, and events, integrating local operations worldwide; and,

 COMMUNITIES, uniting leaders in multi-sector collaborations for economic, environmental, and social ROI--results, outcomes, impact.
