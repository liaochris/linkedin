Angela Motola-Donofrio’s Summary

Family-owned and operated with the utmost attention to customer care, Free Spirit Yacht Cruises offers two luxurious private yachts for business or pleasure charters.

We work with meeting and event planners, concierges, trade show planners and individual companies that want a special venue for a corporate meeting, event or party. We also host wedding receptions, birthday and anniversary parties, showers and family reunions.
