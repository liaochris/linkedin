Jian Chen, Ph.D., CFA’s Summary

Experienced financial risk manager/modeler with more than 10 years of risk management and analytics experience with concentration on residential mortgages and MBS. Risk management background includes distressed asset management, credit risk /counterparty risk/operational risk management. Analytics experience includes building loan performance (prepayment/default/severity) models, servicing modeling, loss mitigation modeling, loan modification modeling. Expert SAS programmer with rich experience working with large data sets, and building sophisticated econometric models.
