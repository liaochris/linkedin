Josef Dunne’s Summary

I am an innovative and creative web developer with a broad range of technical abilities.

I have over 10 years commercial experience working as a web developer, for a number of companies, and a varied selection of projects.

I specialise in building websites that are accessible and follow the W3C guidelines. Web standards, Accessibility and Usability is something I believe in.

I have worked on various projects in many areas from education, sport, fashion, entertainment, art, broadcasting, law, property and telecommunications.

I have a passion for clean coded websites and table-less coding (unless it's a data table!) I produce high quality cross-browser websites which meets W3C standards and adhere to the W3C Accessibility guidelines.

I have experience in information architecture, usability and creating detailed wire-frame specifications, site-maps and user-flows for client work.

I have experience in SEO campaigns and have been very successful in raising the awareness of client websites within search engines.

I have experience building websites using the ever popular Wordpress CMS. I understand the system and its quirks, also the benefits it has to offer.

I am now an official MailChimp Expert and can help with your Email Marketing Campaigns as well as customer database segmentation and targeted Emails, I also understand MailChimps advanced features. As well as how to create cross Email client Email templates.

I can adapt to any new working process easily.
