Cat Lake’s Summary

Cat Lake is a freelance Internet Consultant with over ten years experience in building on-line communities and developing web promotion strategies.

Cat has followed the growth of e-mail groups since ONElist, through eGroups, to Yahoo! Groups and PHP browser-based forums.

During that time she has gained experience of how to run on-line communities, both efficiently and effectively, whilst also honing her skills in on-line promotion and publicity.

Cat Lake’s Specialties:

Able to manage a variety of on-line activities including: social media, social networking, on-line communities, web promotion and viral marketing across multi-media platforms.

Skilled in using software and applications such as: Joomla!, Adobe GoLive, Xara Xtreme and Paint Shop Pro.

Particularly experienced with non-profit organisations, community projects, charity campaigns and fund-raising initiatives.
