Patrick Murphy’s Summary

From Bank One to AOL and now my new venture, Brand Thunder, I've been consistently focused on developing channels for product sales and service to innovative product marketing and promotion. These channels include call centers, web sites, kiosks and direct point of sales contact. In addition, I have a true passion for technology and how it can enhance business operations and improve consumer's business interactions.

Brand Thunder was founded in 2007... we are angel backed and rapidly growing. Brand Thunder creates persistent connections between major brands and their online consumers via the Internet browser. Each Brand Thunder Boom! delivers a visual takeover of the browser, an integrated toolbar for content and functionality, plus the multimedia sidebar for videos, photos and more. The end user enjoys a completely custom and more personalized Internet experience.
