Laura Moreno’s Summary

I'm a web designer with 3+ years experience in the create industry and a photographer with 10+ experience. My specialisation is web design, graphic design, and photography.
I worked with big and small projects on web design, also teaching Photoshop and web design in a 'Design for Web' Course at Tower Hamlets College.

I'm excellent team player, enthusiastic, ambitious, solution focused and assertive.
I have a sharp eye for details and a quick respond to changes in a positive, adaptable and flexible manner.
I'm very good with computer, picking up new systems, software and hardware very quickly.

In 2007 after my finish 'Design for Web' Course I've joined 'Eyedea' - a group of like-minded individuals who have gathered together their collective creativity to form an unbeatable one-stop-shop team who can fulfil all your multimedia needs in the fields of web design, animation, illustration and graphic design.

In november 2009 I left my position as Web Designer & IT Manager in Silveprint to set up myself as Web Designer Freelance giving me more time and opportunities to work in the creative industry and with Eyedea team as partnership.
