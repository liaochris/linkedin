Michael Orr’s Summary

I am a veteran of the Software/Data-communications field, with extensive and consistently successful track-record of providing leadership to R&D teams, and exceptional rapport with customers at all levels from engineering to executive management levels.

With deep technical foundation and exceptional (if I do say so myself ..) communication skills, I am frequently the point-man for important/difficult customers, and industry analysts.

I used to be a software engineer and Unix "white-hat hacker" - System Programmer, Compiler writer, etc. Starting 1991 I transitioned from pure R&D to marketing/Product-management and customer-facing roles, and shifted to data-communications (LAN and WAN).

Starting 1999 I served as CTO of the obligatory Internet-era 10-person start up (that survived !) and since 2000 I have been with Radlan, which has gone from 80-person Start up when I joined to be acquired by Marvell Semiconductors for $250M in 2003/2004. I have now relocated to the USA, to a system-architect job at corp HQ (Hey. someone has to know what that bunch of Israeli Engineers actually DO .. ;-)


Hobbies: Reading, and Martial Arts (Mainly Japanese Karate and Aikido). As a pseudo-hobby, I have served as a teacher/lecturer for the Israeli Open University computer-science department, and as instructor in per-subject tech training to practicing engineers

Note: Slight Dyslexia (so Apologies in advance for Typos - spell checker or not, I still manage to have some in almost everything I write ...)
