Sylvain Gélinas’s Summary

Mr.Gelinas possesses 19 years of experience in the telecommunication industry. A unique combination of broadcast and telecom expertise has been acquired through various responsibilities and organizations with a special focus on technology, business development and marketing strategies. Among other realizations, Mr.Gelinas is the first to implement a large scale micro-wave network for electronic news gathering in Canada. He also implemented a North American and Trans Atlantic fiber optic network for occasional broadcasting events such as sports and news and developed a whole suite of products tailored for the broadcast industry.

His past experience gain with large organizations such as Bell Canada and Videotron combined with a bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering gives a solid foundation for his expertise. In 2006, Mr.Gelinas spent 7 months in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, as senior consultant for broadcasting strategies for the second largest telecommunication organization and one the world largest Teleport.

In the last few months, Mr.Gelinas has developed, with collaboration of Ericsson and Tandberg TV, the next generation triple play network and more specifically the IPTV platform of the future.
