Adam Hellebrand, MA Psychology’s Summary

Career Counseling professional who has a passion for the consultative process. My background includes recruiting and counseling across a wide spectrum of industries as well as supporting diverse populations such as high risk adults and students, My range of skills include advising candidates on a number of critical career development issues such as navigating the market, networking skills, resume development, brand development, the interview process, designing effective search strategies, and strategic career planning. A critical thinker with strong active listening and conflict resolution skills who generates dynamic/impactful solutions to problems, and a passionate counselor who develops collaborative relationships in pursuit of long-term goals


To My Valued Candidates:

I always place the needs of you, the candidate, before my own, and consider myself to be both your guide and counsel during any career change. I invest the time to understand your career needs, challenges, motivators, passions, goals, and unique potential, and ultimatley find you the opportunities to reach it.

My collaborative approach means that I keep you engaged throughout the interview process, ensuring that the job you ultimately land gets you one step closer to realizing your career goals because although your jobs may change, your goals do not.
