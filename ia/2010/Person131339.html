Marc Ernst’s Summary

Started working in publishing in a commercial function (Pub magazine) and than worked for several years as an account executive/manager & director in advertsing & corporate communications (TBWA, Publicis, Ogilvy, e.o).

Was also active as a trainer & consultant in internal communication & HRM (Performers Group, Performance Coaching,...).

Than moved again into publishing, but in a editorial function (editor in chief of the Belgian/Dutch HR Magazine).

Set up my own business (HRM.net) in 1998. Sold it and left the firm in February 2006. Launched a new company in october 2006: BizInfo. BizInfo is the publisher of HRMinfo.net, HRMblogs.com, SalesPlaza.net & BizPlaza.eu

Wrote on a free lance basis a lot of articles/contributions about marketing, communication, management en HRM for several publications

Marc Ernst’s Specialties:

Good knowledge of trade journalism and e-publishing in different markets (a.o: HR, marketing and communication): offer and demand: suppliers & corporations/organisations, information needs and busineness opportunities in that field.
