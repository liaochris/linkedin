Neil Schwartzman’s Summary

I am the point of contact to media, such peer groups as iCAUCE (the international wing), and to both elected officials and public servants at all levels of government. I am also the external relations representative to the London Action Plan http://londonactionplan.org the Anti-Spyware Coalition http://antispywarecoalition.org the Antiphishing Working Group http://apwg.org and the Stop Spam Alliance http://stopspamalliance.org/

I’ve been interviewed and quoted by the Washington Post, New York Times, L.A. Times, CNET, the Globe and Mail, the National Post, Reader’s Digest and the Chicago Tribune, and a featured interview on all Canadian national television and radio networks. I appeared in Spam : The Documentary http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0936499/

Neil Schwartzman’s Specialties:

Internet email, spam, and security policy and law issues.
