Kim DeLawder’s Experience

  • President

    Sensible Staging & Redesign, LLC

    (Design industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    Sensible Staging & Redesign provides staging services to realtors and homeowners that have houses on the market. I will come to your location, do a walk-thru and consultation, and then provide a written report on suggestions to best prepare your house to sell. You have a choice of either doing it yourself (DIY) or I will be happy to return to do the staging for you – whatever works best with your budget and time schedule. During staging, I can use furniture and accessories that you already own or I can provide rentals for you at an additional cost.

    Redesigning works the same way, except I will be designing your home for YOU, the homeowner AND I will use your own furnishings to create a fresh and comfortable place to live and entertain.
