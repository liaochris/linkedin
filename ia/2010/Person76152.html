Glenn Wissner’s Experience

  • Senior Producer, Mobile Games

    Sega of America, Inc.

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Entertainment industry)

    November 2003 — June 2007 (3 years 8 months)

    Since 2003, Glenn Wissner has been instrumental in the reforming of SEGA Mobile. In his role as Senior Producer, Glenn was responsible for all product development, marketing and sales activities for this start-up wireless game division of the SEGA Corporation. He managed the content roadmap for original Product Development based on SEGA IP and well as third party product acquisition (licensed product) for BREW and J2ME wireless games. In addition to this, Glenn was responsible for strategic business development like D2C sales and B2B sales channel development and licensing opportunities. Glenn was also the key person in charge of negotiating new carrier agreements for Tier 1 carriers and the new emerging MVNO’s such as Disney Mobile, Boost Mobile and Virgin Mobile. Glenn has also worked with PlayPhone to power the segamobile.com e-commerce solution.

  • Associate Producer
