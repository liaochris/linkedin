Christian Louca’s Summary

My Principles: To be integral, passionate, determined and to enjoy life.

My Background: I worked in the music industry from a young age for companies such as Warner Music and Pinnacle Entertainment, subsequently setting up my own record label and production house which I ran for six years. From there, I went on to set up a small mobile content & marketing agency, Trusted Media, in 2003; signing over 100 record labels to create mobile content for operators such as O2, Vodafone and Orange. I have been in mobile ever since, working at Enpocket and then Nokia Interactive on large accounts such as Trinity Mirror before moving on to head up YOC’s UK division. I am extremely passionate about the mobile medium.

My Current Experience: YOC Group - Leading European Full Service Mobile Marketing Agency.

Clients include: Coca Cola, Barcadi, Johnson & Johnson, Nike, Kraft, Samsung, Mercedes, GM, Volvo, Deutsche Bank, Deutsche Postbank, Group M, Proximity, BD-NTWK, Masterfoods, Nestle, Vittel, Disney, Wikipedia, Accuweather, 123play.com, Teletext, PC Advisor, Macworld (IDG Communications), Pistonheads, Auto Car, Stuff.tv, What Car (Haymarket Consumer Media), Perez Hilton, Digg, DivX, Peperonity, Flirtomatic, Qeep, 3G Hutchinson, The Independent, The Guardian, The Daily Telegraph, The Sun, the Times, Trinity Mirror, New York Post and 20th Century Fox.

We are members of the MMA and IAB.
