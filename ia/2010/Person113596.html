Kelly Flynn’s Summary

The Advanced Learning Institute is an independent conference planning company, targeting the business and government sectors, offering training on a variety of subjects.

My role is to research experts within a variety of fields, recruiting them to share their best practices and lessons learned to our audiences. I assist speakers in helping to form their topic and presentation, ensuring that their case study or workshop is as compelling and educational as possible. I am heavily involved in producing communication conferences, specifically for the U.S. and Canadian governments.

If you have a story to tell, or would like to learn more about speaking opportunities, please let me know!
