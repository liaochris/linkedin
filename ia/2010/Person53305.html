Deb Aungst’s Summary

You want to grow your business, increase revenues and improve your profitability. As a Certified Business Coach, I am committed to helping you identify and execute the strategies to get you there. An assertive and proactive coach, I have the pleasure of doing what I love--serving the business owner by exceeding what they thought possible in achieving success. Coaching is primarily focused on two things: education and execution. As you and your business advance, you learn how to implement and apply customized strategies so that you have greater control of the direction and success of your business...and I will be there every step along the way to ensure your success. I can show you all the ways to build your business as well as equip you with the tools, knowing that at the end of the day it's up to you to make them work. A consultant will do it for you. A coach will show you how to do it for yourself so you can apply the principles in any endeavor you choose now and in the future. Keys to your success will be the 1. clarity of your plan, 2. knowing the numbers that drive your business as you test and measure your results and 3. leveraging with time, team and systems. Are you ready to take the step to create a real commercial, profitable enterprise that works without you?
