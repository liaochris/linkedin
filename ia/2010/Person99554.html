John (Jack) McLane’s Summary

A pharmaceutical leader with over 20 years of progressive and successful experience in all phases of global development with both large and small pharmaceutical companies. Key strengths in leading full development projects leading to successful IND/CTAs, NDAs and EMEA applications, developing clinical and regulatory strategies, and negotiating with regulatory officials. Proven team builder and catalyst, in creating team processes, in exploiting novel methodologies, and in overcoming regulatory barriers. Have developed and implemented full nonclinical, clinical and regulatory development programs for filing new drug and medical device applications (NDA/MAA). As core member of international marketing and life cycle business teams have developed and implemented medical regulatory and marketing strategies. Demonstrated ability to repair or manage difficult programs. Have had critical role in direct development of in- and out-licensing strategies and negotiations including those for orphan indications and medical devices. Experience in formation and initial development of new start-up companies including major contributions to business plans and presentations to venture capitalists for significant fund-raising.
