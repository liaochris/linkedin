Umesh Manathkar’s Summary

Business driven IT executive with proven ability to lead and manage a global Information Technology operation for companies in the semiconductor, electronic components and LED chips & components industries. Hands on business problem solver with attention to details and expertise in developing, communicating and executing a technology vision that is aligned with business strategy & goals. Deep experience in successfully implementing and managing SAP as well as Oracle Business Suite of enterprise applications. Recipient of 2005 Austin CIO of the year award.

Umesh Manathkar’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning, Leadership, Business Alignment, Enterprise Architecture, SAP & Oracle Applications, Budget Administration, Team Development, Board Presentations, Compliance & Audits, Consulting, Project Management, Business Process Automation, ERP (SAP & Oracle), and CRM (SAP), Business Intelligence (Cognos, BW, OBIEE), Data Warehouse (DW), B2B (EDI / RosettaNet), Supply Chain Management (SCM), Advanced Planning System (APS), Applications Development, Data Center Consolidation.
