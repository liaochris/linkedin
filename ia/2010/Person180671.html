Salim AbiEzzi’s Summary

I'm interested in consulting opportunities and will consider full time positions that leverage my technical skills, my corporate experience, and my extensive industry contacts. My services include technology development, project management, business development, technical marketing/evangelism and industry standards/consortia. I've been an independent consultant since February 2004.

I served in an engineering management capacity for 9 years at Microsoft, 6 at Sun Microsystems, and 5 at RPI. I have a direct experience with standards committees starting with the ANSI committee on computer graphics in 1984. Furthermore, I'm the founding chairman of the UPnP Forum in October 1999; I chaired its steering committee for four years, while growing it to a successful international consortium.

I delivered numerous technical presentations in large conferences including WinHEC, PDC, Siggraph, and Eurographics. I've authored nineteen technical papers -- with three award winners, and I'm an inventor of seven issued and three pending patents; for details and links see <http://www.plextal.com>.

I completed a PhD and MS in CS from RPI, and a BS in CS from UMass Lowell, summa cum laude.

I'm especially passionate about technology that improves people's lives through simplicity, elegant design, and ease of use.
