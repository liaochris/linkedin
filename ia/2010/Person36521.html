Chef Sandy Krebs’s Summary

When I started cooking in California it was an exciting time. Alice Waters and Jeremiah Towers, among others, were beating the drum of this new but really very old concept; if it’s not fresh and wonderful, it’s not on the menu. There was an emphasis on using what was at hand. In the diverse population in California with large Hispanic, Asian and Middle Eastern populations what was at hand was a mixture of spices, smells and flavors that set this young chef’s imagination free. Today the world is such a small place. People migrate more quickly and frequently and their food and traditions migrate right along with them. I think these days most cooking is Fusion.
I have held the positions of Assistant Pastry Chef, Sous Chef and Chef de Cuisine in international hotels. In my last two roles as Executive Chef in a boutique setting I’ve lead a small kitchen brigade. I elevated my restaurant to world class, award winning status by creating and implementing exciting menus and planning and overseeing special events. I am expert in several styles of cooking including Fusion, Italian, Mediterranean, Asian, Southwest, and traditional European. I developed skills to motivate and lead a multi-cultural staff and negotiate with purveyors for the freshest and best quality ingredients at the lowest price. I excel at setting, implementing and meeting financial goals. I am highly motivated and result oriented. All of this is underpinned by my deep passion for the culinary arts. At this point in my career I would like to transition back to a corporate setting, one where there is room for me to learn and grow. I see this move not as getting a job, but making a shift where there is plenty of room for growth.
I am fluent in English and am a beginner at Spanish, German and Mandarin. I have traveled extensively in Africa, Asia, Europe, the South Pacific, So. America and the US. I am open to relocating anywhere for the right opportunity. I am unmarried and I travel single status.

Chef Sandy Krebs’s Specialties:

Eclectic Cuisine, Fusion Cuisine, Mediterranean Cuisine, Asain Cuisine, Menu Development and Implementation, Event Planning, Staff Mentoring, Training and Supervision, Cost Control and Budget Planning.
