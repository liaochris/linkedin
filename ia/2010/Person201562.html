Ray Thackeray’s Summary

Grandfather of Strategic Meetings Management technology - created the first SaaS registration system for planners, drove the first air booking integration and the first Spend Management integrated with Corporate Meeting Cards.

Domestic and international technology sales, marketing and business development. Proven strategist and communicator with business development, operating, strategic planning and team building skills. Negotiation and legal including M&A, employment, SaaS, other contracts.

A decade as VP Sales & Marketing working with all levels of management, C-level down and pioneered SaaS sales methods from 1999.

Nimble management. "When things are not working, change the formula." -- product, packaging, pricing, even as far as concept! "When the market is hostile, change the rules." Track record includes transforming a bloated direct product sales organization into an agile, efficient, successful channels team, doubling revenues in six months at greater margin.

Sold Travent to OnVantage/StarCite, transferred assets that already made starcite.com multi-million dollar profit, after considerable partner sales development and market entry work with American Express creating a new Procurement Card for the groups & events spend sector in Corporate Travel.

Founder of Travent Ltd. (see www.travent.com) and CEO 2002-2007. Sold Strategic Meetings Management sourcing and procurement technology.

Lead inventor & co-founder of seeUthere & named on original patent application. Led Search Engine Optimization and marketing (SEM/SEO) and created new viral marketing techniques. Pioneered Social Media features now found in Facebook and others. Supported sourcing of over $30 million in venture capital funding 1998-2002. Accepted offer for $250 million from MP3.com to acquire seeUthere.com, merger failed on day prior to filing during MP3's troubles with record companies and "pooling of interest" violation; MP3.com invested over $18 million in series B and C.
