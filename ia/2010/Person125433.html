Lindsay Jackson Nichols’s Summary

Lindsay is a highly accomplished consultant and business development professional with over 18 years of experience in management consulting and professional service firms. During this time she has worked with closely, privately and publicly held 'start-up' and long-established companies with single and multi-sites both here and abroad. She assists them with process improvement, management systems (ISO) and strategic planning initiatives.

Her strengths and passion are working at all levels within client organizations to develop and implement strong processes and management systems using the ISO 9001 framework. Her programs decrease costs, redundancies and inconsistencies and increase sales, profitability and stakeholder satisfaction and importantly minimizing the cost and burden of ongoing ISO maintenance.

Lindsay welcomes the opportunity to collaborate with other product and service providers interested in assisting clients develop strong, results driven, ISO management systems.

Lindsay Jackson Nichols’s Specialties:

Operational Evaluations (Gap Analysis), Internal Audits, Development of Process Improvement initiatives, Quality, Environmental and Regulatory Systems to meet ISO 9001, ISO 17025, ISO 13485, EU Medical Device Directives, the QSR & CMDR's, AS9100, ISO/TS 16949, ISO 14971, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, ISO 22000, ISO 20000, ISO 27001, SQF, FSC, SFI, PEFC, HACCP, etc. In addition offering customized In-house ISO Training, ISO Internal Audit Management, ISO Process Improvement and ISO Redesign Services.
