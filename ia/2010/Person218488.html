Dr. Zareh Soghomonian’s Summary

Highly qualified technical leader with over 19+ years of international hands-on engineering experience in electrical, electronics, power systems and elecetromagnetics engineering supported by effective technical and engineering management skills.   • Research Engineer

    European Electrical Steels, UK

    (Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    January 1991 — December 1994 (4 years )

    Worked with industrial and academic bodies in the creation and production scale implementation of an automated non-destructive testing system; invented and completely developed industrial process control systems from concept level to implementation in 1994, resulting in US, European and international patents.
