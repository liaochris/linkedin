Sam Jones’s Summary

Right now, I have over 9 years experience working at the highest level in this industry. I've done 3 triple A games and all associated DLC thereof - Perfect Dark Zero, Halo:3, Halo: ODST and now I am working on Halo: Reach.
Since 2005, I have been either a lead or a manager, or currently, both.
I have managed art teams as smalll as 4 FTEs up to a large body of 26, across all levels of seniority. I have also managed and trained contractors internally and externally, even internationally.
Bungie has a very positive atmosphere for encouraging personal growth and I have taken on more and more responsibilty in recent years very comfortably.
I am an art lead, manager, organiser and contribute creatively as a very senior level production artist.
The management aspect invloves leadership, art direction, career development, assigning art tasks, sorting out personnel issues and scheduling, amongst others.
Creatively I am responsible for missions from concept to release and I am the 'go to man' for each mission I am charge of. Ulitmately I am there to make them look good, play well, come in on time, on budget and performant,
Additionally, I lead all artists on each mission I am responsible for in accordance with the wishes of the Art Director and Creative Director.
I am very much focussing on the personal professional growth of myself and others in my organisation here at Bungie - looking for effeciencies, striving to work smarter, and quicker and aiming to contribute as effectively as humanly possible. I am also interested in pushing new technologies and furthering my creativity.

Sam Jones’s Specialties:

Art and design creation, direction and management. Furthermore, 3D modeling in Max, Maya or Lightwave. Shading with all types of state of the art shading models, lighting with various renderers - off the shelf or custom. In terms of management and scheduling, I bring; planning, reviewing, communication on all levels including public speaking, organizing morale events, team leadership, putting out fires, syncing up various disciplines, pushing for and disseminating new tech.
