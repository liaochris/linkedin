Laura Wilkinson’s Summary

*As an athlete, I am the only woman in history to win all three coveted world titles in platform diving: 2000 Olympic Games, 2004 World Cup and 2005 Olympic Games. I have also won 19 US National titles, 2 NCAA titles, nominated for an ESPY and was just inducted into the University of Texas Women's Athletics Hall of Honor.

*As a speaker, I have addressed audiences as small as 25 and as large as 35,000 with many different themes and lessons always woven into my Olympic journey. I speak to both corporate and faith-based organizations.

*As President and Founder of the Laura Wilkinson Foundation, a 501(c)3 non-profit, I have run fundraising events and set up an organization to build a new facility for diving and related sports to help young athletes achieve their dreams and to see the USA re-emerge as the world leader in the sport of diving.

*As a Designer, I have created three websites, redesigned two websites and am currently working on another website and a redesign. I have created various products to sell on those websites. I have also created logos and numerous marketing materials for all of my endeavors.

  • Motivational Speaker

    LauraWilkinson.com

    (Sports industry)

    October 2000 — Present (9 years 7 months)

    Laura Wilkinson has enthralled audiences around the world for years with her perfect 10's and extraordinary gold medal performances. Today she is inspiring people from a different platform.

    Beginning her speaking career following the 2000 Olympic Games, Laura has addressed audiences as small as 25 and as large as 35,000 with many different themes and lessons always woven into her Olympic journey.

    Laura’s passion shines through her speaking as she takes her audience with her on her leap of faith. The lessons Laura has learned throughout the peaks and valleys of her diving career have given her an insightful outlook on life and have taught her how to be successful in and out of the pool.

    Laura is an energetic and animated speaker, sure to captivate and immerse your audience through her intriguing Olympic stories. As your audience is being swept away into the Olympic Games, Laura shows them the impact that these diving experiences have outside of the pool.
