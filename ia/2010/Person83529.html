H Wayne Lambert, Ph.D.’s Summary

My professional goals include: 1) giving the highest level of instruction to medical and dental students in the anatomical sciences; 2) develop my scholarly activities to impact education and clinical anatomy; and 3) impact the way dental students are taught the basic sciences.

H Wayne Lambert, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

My scholarly activities center on: (1) creation of computer-assisted instruction (CAI) applications to engage medical and dental students to learn the basic sciences; (2) the development, review, and assessment of questions to prepare students for their board exams; (3) the study and research of variability within cadaveric specimens to note its impact on clinical anatomy; and (4) the assessment of content, learning, and instructional methodology in current basic science courses and textbooks.
