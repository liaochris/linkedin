JC Melvin’s Summary

JC Melvin works as an expert witness in real estate related litigation and is considered one of the premier experts in Nevada. He has worked on both the plaintiff side and the defendant's sides of cases. He was hired by the United States Agency for International Development and presented "Train the Trainer" programs in Poland, Romania, Russia and the Ukraine. He is a real estate trainer with an energetic passion for life, Melvin presents various real estate training programs throughout the U S including the GRI programs, ABR and WCR (Woman's Council of Realtors).
Melvin has presented at five NAR (National Association of Realtors) conventions and the international builder's conventions. He has also conducted workshops and programs for Coldwell Banker, Better Homes and Garden, Century 21, Prudential, Keller Williams, Chicago title, First American Title, various banks and many others. Want to wake up your audiance....Call JC! Melvin has also written three books on improving your life. One, "I think I smell Garlic, A Recipe for Life" and the second with co-authors Ken Blanchard and Stephen Covey, "Blueprint for Success."
The latest is called "Reinventing Yourself in Today's Economy."

JC Melvin’s Specialties:

Expert witness and consultation work in the area of real estate litigation. Melvin has acted as a consultant to the Nevada Real Estate Division and authored legislative and regulatory changes within Nevada Law and the NRS. Melvin understands the sales world and can wake up your sales force with his presentations.
