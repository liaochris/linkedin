Rand Han’s Summary

In 2006, I built BA360 from nothing into a tight 15-person operation able to service multinational clients. I learned every aspect of the advertising business the ground up, from pitching clients, coming up with great concepts, directing and executing creative, project and client management; the business was fueled by quick thinking, ambition, and luck. My clients included top multinationals like Volkswagen, Porsche, Schindler, Coke, etc.

In Q1 2009 I built littleredbook.cn, now the industry's most popular China-focused advertising blog; after, I created missniuniu.com, now one of China's top fashion blogs. Based on learnings from Littleredbook and Missniuniu, I then created zerosocialmedia.com, a China social media company in Q3 2009 with partner agency Mailman (est.1999).

As of Q4 2009, ZD's first clients are American Airlines and 3 Suisses (Europe's top fashion ecommerce retailer). Since then, I've consulted with top 4A digital, creative and media agencies for clients like Unilever, Tiffany, Hennessy, Starbucks, J&J and others.

Rand Han’s Specialties:

China advertising, marketing and communications. Business & creative strategy and tactical execution. I've deep experience in China social media, building and marketing websites in China, as well as print and outdoor advertising.
