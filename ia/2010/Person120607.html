Laetitia Faure’s Summary

Planning strategies, building strong business relationships, creating and leading innovative projects, proposing new ideas and concepts, and achieving results is my core expertise.

I am a multilingual French citizen with international experience. I obtained a Master’s degree in Mass Media Communications and Languages ‘summa cum laude’ at the Catholic University of Milan, Italy. I continued my education with a second Master’s degree in International Communication Strategies with high honors. My award-winning thesis on « Identity-based communication strategies of Italian brands » was published in two languages in 2007.

Since then I have worked in different international organizations, advising small and medium companies as well as multinationals - with revenues ranging from $1,000,000 to $300,000,000 - about corporate communication, branding, marketing strategies, and business development.
I am currently employed by the French Government to help the French-American Chamber in its business development, corporate communication and trade services.

Living in different cities in Europe, Africa and in the United States has provided me with an extensive understanding of managing business internationally, while interacting with different cultures and working with stakeholders, officials and entrepreneurs from different backgrounds and perspectives. Work in numerous markets (tourism, aerospace, biotechnologies, gaming, IT, environmental technologies, banking, fashion, and import-export) has established my expertise in global business.


My next aspiration is to invest my communications talents and international experience in the development of a leading worldwide company.
