Bob Divney’s Summary

GOAL:
We seek to provide an alternative for independent artists and small labels to promote and market their music in the constantly evolving music marketplace with the ultimate goal of making these projects profitable with smaller ships and sell through of units. Adrenaline Music Group provides the distribution through Alternative Distribution Alliance, (Warner Music’s independent distribution arm), and provides the back end infrastructure of a major label that can operate at a fraction of the cost in terms of overhead and still provide the same services that would be expected at a major label.

Bob Divney’s Specialties:

I have a practical and unique insight in the evolving music industry as it pertains to marketing and promoting artist owned music projects on budgets that create profitability at smaller ship and sell through on units. At Adrenaline Music Group I along with my partners have acquired a wealth of experience and achieved a high level of success in the ‘small end of the music business,’ that free independent artists and small labels from the status quo of the major label system.
