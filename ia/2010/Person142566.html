Michael Cassaro’s Summary

25 years of specialized medical practice.
Management of medical businesses, including both for profit and non-profit entities.

20 years as a public speaker with presentations to colleagues and to the general public.

Candidate for the United States Senate in 2008. I went down in flames! (Think phoenix?)

Radio talk show host since October 2008. The Painless Living show, 11 am to noon Eastern time on WFIA 94.7 FM and 900 AM. Also, streaming live at http://www.painlesslivingradio.com. And syndicated on the Painless Living Network.

Michael Cassaro’s Specialties:

I practice functional medicine with a focus on relieving pain from any source, along with associated symptoms like fatigue and metabolic problems. My specific areas of expertise are minimally invasive surgery, hormone and metabolic stabilization, and overall wellness.

I help people get better, so they don't have to keep coming back!
