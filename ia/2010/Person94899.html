Jennifer Fairfax’s Summary

Jennifer was just named to the 2010 Rising Stars Super Lawyers for Maryland for her experience in family law. Jennifer handles adoptions, surrogacy and donor related matters for her clients including second parent, step parent, relative, domestic and international adoptions for traditional families, LGBT families and single parent families. She is a member of the American Academy of Adoption Attorneys and the American Academy of Assisted Reproduction Technology Attorneys. She practices in all Maryland Courts and the District of Columbia can handle family buidling matters from the uncontested step-parent adoption to a complex matter invovling multiple parties including contested matters.
