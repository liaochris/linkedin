David Millian’s Summary

For nearly seven years , I hold different positions in a Public Health institution. With its 9000 employees, networking was a necessity . As I was mobile, I was able to take regional (metropolitan & overseas) as well as national management responsibilities .

My last appointment in La Réunion gave me the opportunity to manage the major sanitary crisis of Chikungunya with success. I was able to expend the mandate of my duties to include strategic and political affairs.

Having gained this experience, I decided to further my knowledge in influent communication techniques; lobbying; and competitive intelligence by enrolling in the "École de Guerre Économique" of Paris.

Since august 2009 I moved to Canada to find work on high added value projects and became, at the same time, Strategy Advisor for GoodProd, a french start up in entainterment.
