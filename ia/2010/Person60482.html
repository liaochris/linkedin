Debbie T. Lawrence’s Summary

As a senior marketing executive with 25+ years of brand marketing, strategic planning, sales and distribution experience , directing large, high profile projects and global product launches; Debbie is a well-rounded manager with a firm grasp of the financials complemented with solid creative capabilities.
Responsible for overseeing multi-million dollar projects and departmental budgets, Debbie manages big picture objectives with the knowledge and understanding of the necessary details and insight to accomplish goals. She possesses strong leadership and interpersonal skills dealing with every department and division of the company as well as with external business relationships.
A level-headed executive who is able to multi-task and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment with the ability to prioritize and manage several tasks and individuals at once; Debbie is energetic, a passionate leader with entrepreneurial spirit and maintains outstanding ethics and integrity.
Her personal foundation, 1 Million Memories was founded after her parents death in 2002 to support Alzheimer's Research and Education and has a goal of raising $1 Million Dollars, $1 Dollar at a time for scholarship and Caregiver support.

Debbie has been featured as an author on sites like Intent.com, Type A Moms, and others. Her work with national brands such as Clorox, HP, Wendy's, Haagen Daz, Hebrew National, Volkswagen America, Kimberly Clark and more have brought her voice to the forefront of Social Media and Virtual Publicity. An accomplished speaker and Author, she has brought her "Remember The Conversation" to local and national groups of all sizes. Her specialties include creating buzz and communities for companies that target moms through new media. Her latest projects, We Speak Media and Media Kit 360 further blogger interaction with PR firms and center publicity efforts for bloggers.
