John Bayley’s Summary

John Bayley has held various financial positions up to and including CFO in restaurant and retail companies. He has been on the senior management teams of both franchisor and franchisee organizations and has developed an in depth understanding of the issues involved in those relationships. He has also been involved in repositioning, work out, and turnaround situations which included taking an acquired chain from an $8 million operating loss to a $1 million profit in 18 months.
John has worked in both private and public firms with revenues ranging from a $24 million to over $12 billion. John’s responsibilities have included managing accounting organizations ranging in size from five to 60 persons; he has had responsibility for internal and external financial reporting, financial analysis, treasury, business modeling and cash flow forecasting, budgeting, strategic planning, information technology, legal as well as insurance and risk management.

John has managed successful accounting system conversions and point of sale system roll outs. He has experience with outsourcing arrangements for accounting, payroll and benefits administration including the “on boarding” and “off boarding” processes.
Internally, he has worked closely with all levels of operating management to help them define, articulate, and track key performance indicators for improving operating performance. He has also worked with internal and third party financial and administrative support resources to provide a high level of coordinated service to front line operations.
He has worked with external constituencies ranging from private equity investors, to bankers and institution holders of publicly traded shares

John Bayley’s Specialties:
