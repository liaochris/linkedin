Izabela Lundberg’s Summary

Originally from Europe, Izabela Lundberg is currently residing in the United States. Izabela has worked in corporate America as well as in the non-profit sector, sharpening her skills in leadership, business management, real estate, investing, program developing/directing, coaching, sales, marketing, multilingual interpreting and translating, and much more.

She has worked for the past fifteen years with professionals from more than eighty countries world-wide, gaining priceless global perspective. As a result, her professional experience of coaching and speaking/presenting on a wide range of topics, from cross-cultural issues and crisis intervention, to global business management are just of few assets that she possesses and shares with wide range of audiences locally, nationally and internationally.

In addition, Izabela is a creative and passionate artist, poet and a very soon to be published author. She is a compassionate Human Rights Advocate and Global Good Will Ambassador actively working on conflict resolutions in many parts of the world.

Izabela’s unique style is informed by her life experience, which also comes through in her business/motivational presentations, coaching, training, and mentorship.

Being a living example of what Izabela teaches, “To accomplish your own goals you must concentrate on helping others reach their goals,” she is channeling her energies into her new company to focus on marketing, business development and opportunities, as well as personal growth.
