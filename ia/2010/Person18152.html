Reg Athwal is Co-Founder and Chairman of OneTVO - a global TV platform that creates, films, edits, uploads, broadcasts and markets Web TV shows and Info-Videos in fast growing cities. Starting of with Abu Dhabi and Dubai in the UAE, OneTVO has plans to expand to 30 cities by 2016.

Visit: www.onetvo.com

Prior to OneTVO, Reg Athwal founded RAW GROUP (RAW LTD and RAW LLC) in 2002, with offices now in the United Kingdom and United Arab Emirates, with the headquarters based in Dubai. RAW GROUP owns brands Chase Hamilton (www.chasehamilton.com), R2Talent (www.r2talent.com) and BookPill (www.bookpill.com).

Reg Athwal as a professional presenter has inspired millions of people with his inspirational business speeches and transformed the lives and businesses of over 30,000 CEOs and 250,000 managers in 39 countries since 2002.

Reg is a highly rated Livewire Conference Chairman and Moderator for large conferences and in-company sales incentive events in the Gulf Region and India. In addition he opens and closes conferences as a keynote speaker internationally.

Visit www.rawltd.com for indepth information, free articles, speech vidoes, free books, speech menus, photos, testimonials and much more. Also visit www.bookpill.com.

Reg is an elected member of the International Federation for Professional Speakers (IFFPS) and Professional Speakers Association of the United Kingdom (PSA-UK) and President for 2006-2007. Reg is also the Founding Chairman of the Professional Speakers Association of the Middle East (PSA-ME).
