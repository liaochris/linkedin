Jordi Blasco’s Summary

In order to succeed in Corporate Finance, it was very useful being first a business and corporate lawyer and having been educated in business management, auditing and taxation. It's an interesting combination that allows a wide vision of the M&A process and a high degree of efficiency.

This wide multidisciplinary education, combined with a long professional experience in several roles and sectors, definitely helps in making M&A deals get correctly structured and closed.

From the early nineties, I advised a number of M&A deals, either as a lawyer or M&A adviser, a significant part of them involving more than one jurisdiction (international or transnational deals). I currently advise, as a M&A adviser, a number of deals from my own firm, in a variety of sectors.

From 2005 to 2008, I was Capital Partner in Barcelona for ONEtoONE Capital Partners.

My firm belongs to MSI GLOBAL ALLIANCE, an international organization of more than 250 firms with offices in more than 100 countries.

I also serve as a member of several Boards of Directors and/or advisory boards of companies in different sectors (ports, publishing, events, consultancy, HR, leisure).

After being associate professor in several institutions, I'm currently Associate Professor of Finance at EADA Business School. I teach in the Corporate Finance Program, the Master in Finance and the Executive MBA (private equity, company valuation and legal aspects of a M&A deal, M&A at work), apart from in several focused seminars or short courses.

Jordi Blasco’s Specialties:

Corporate finance, Mergers and Acquisitions, financing rounds, venture capital, private equity, divestures, corporate development, legal and corporate advice to top managers, Board of Directors, shareholders and/or investors. Intellectual Property. Arts.
