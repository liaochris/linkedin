Anthony Santarosa’s Summary

Many consulting firms do not understand the nuances of small business. What is good for the corporate world, does not usually play well on a smaller scale. I specialize in dealing with sole proprietorships, family run businesses and smaller corporate entities

With advanced degrees in engineering and a lifetime of business experience, I am ready to take your company to the next level. Having spent the last decade or more creating my own success, I am now available to make your company better than the rest. Experienced in business growth and management as well as developing strategies for business growth in all economic climates.   

Business metrics are all the buzz, but as an engineer we learned the phrase "Garbage in, garbage out". Having the ability to analyze data is only one aspect. Having the ability to determine if the metrics are valid and actually provide worthwhile information is a different story.

My negotiating skills are extensive from both the purchasing side and the sales side. Over the last decade I have negotiated the purchase of several million dollars in hard assets and sold tens of millions of dollars in services to customers large and small.

Providing IT/Web Design/E-Commerce solutions that enable small business to grow and work more efficiently.

I am a problem solver and a leader. In times of chaos, I deliver the calm that is required to move forward.
