Kristin Yantis’s Summary

Malen Yantis Public Relations is is a full service public relations agency specializing in developing creative campaigns for upscale, lifestyle brands mainly found in the travel and tourism industry. The agency strives to provide clients with added value by offering excellence in service, creativity and results. As an independent company, MYPR offers an array of resources, while providing personalized, one-on-one counsel by the senior expert in the agency.

Kristin Yantis’s Specialties:

-Story placement in local, regional and national media outlets
-Writing and development of media materials including press kits
-Web 2.0 distribution mechanisms and strategies
-Media familiarization trips and individual press visits
-Executive media training
-Crisis communications preparation and counsel
-Key influencer outreach
-Industry awards and accolades
-Internal communications, messaging for new employee orientation

  • Director of Public Relations

    The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch

    (Hospitality industry)

    February 2002 — January 2004 (2 years )

    Yantis was the in-house opening director of public relations for The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch prior to joining DH&R. In order to communicate the property’s positioning as a luxury log cabin she developed a number of creative programs and services to tell the resort’s brand story. Her efforts resulted in extensive media placements in regional and national media outlets including being listed on the Conde Nast Traveler “Hot List” of new hotels and the resort landed on the “15 to Watch” list by the editors of Travel + Leisure.

  • Public Relations Manager, Vail

    Vail Resorts

    (Public Company; MTN; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    February 1997 — December 2001 (4 years 11 months)

    Yantis worked for Vail Resorts, Inc. where she oversaw all aspects of the public relations effort for Vail Ski Area. As the spokesperson for the No. 1 ski resort in the United States, Yantis gained tremendous experience handling crisis and dealing with national and international media contacts. She conducted several international public relations missions throughout Germany, the United Kingdom and attended the 1998 Winter Olympics in Nagano, Japan on behalf of a company sponsorship. Her work with the ski area resulted in strong luxury and entertainment media contacts such as The Today Show, EXTRA, Dateline NBC, Robb Report, People, Departures and Town & Country TRAVEL.
