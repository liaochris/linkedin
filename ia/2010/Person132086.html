Maren Finzer’s Summary

Do you stand out in a sea of similar products, or do you mix in with the pack?

What do you offer that nobody else can?

Infused with enthusiasm, I use my passion for creativity, drive for excellence, and forward thinking to position high-achieving professionals to stand out as the only choice in the minds of their target audience.

Individuals and professionals: Workplace longevity is waning, competition is fierce, and credentials alone are no longer enough. You need an edge. That edge is found in your personal brand.

Entrepreneurs: Attract your ideal client, and command a premium, even during economic downturns. Strong brands survive tough times! Align your business around your own values and passions, gain leverage in partnerships, and attract incredible employees.

Companies and Organizations: Your team members are your greatest asset. By inspiring, engaging, and educating executives and emerging leaders, they can deliver on the corporate brand promise in a way that is authentic to them. Employees are happy, engaged and fulfilled, resulting in increased creativity and innovation. Productivity rises and employee retention goes up. In the new world of work, it's not about getting the most from each employee, it's about getting the best.

Maren Finzer’s Specialties:

Personal branding, small business branding, corporate team building, REACH Master Certified Personal Branding Strategist, Inbound Marketing Specialist, building client relationships, brainstorming/idea generation, integrated marketing communications, social networking.
