Lisa Hanna’s Summary

I provide virtual assistant services to small and mid-size businesses. I build long-term relationships with my clients, becoming an integral member of the team and often a key player in driving business growth.

I spent the first part of my career working as the right-hand to senior executives in some of New York's top branding and marketing agencies but in 2004, my husband and I relocated to Georgia and I started my own business.

I have a wide range of skills, but my greatest strength is my ability to build and maintain strong relationships. From my early career in advertising (think 'Madmen'!) to my current work assisting Blue Sky Resumes' clients in making a career move, I have always been able to connect with people. I care about others and it shows in my work.

Lisa Hanna’s Specialties:

Client Relations, event planning, CRM systems, lead management, document preparation, calendar management, workflow management, M&A administration.
