    2000 — 2006 (6 years )

Also, served as Vice President in charge of Underwriting Strategies and Vice President in the Product management organization responsible for securing regulatory approval of the companies life insurance and annuity products. Finally, managed teams responsible for large scale project development efforts for the organization.

  • Vice President

    General American Life Insurance Company

    (Insurance industry)

    1974 — 2000 (26 years )

    General American acquired by MetLife in early 2000. At General American served as Vice President of Individual Operations Division responsible for Life new business including the Underwriting or risk assessment process, medical department, policyholders service, claims, producer contract and compensation, non-qualified plan services, producer and policyholders call centers. This included the Top Flight service center serving our top tier producers. Prior to this position served as Vice President of the Pension and Annuity Business Unit where responsibilities included profit and loss, marketing, product development and customer service for 401(k) and other qualified plans as well as IRA's and non-qualified annuity contracts.
