Muhammad Altaf, PMP’s Summary

I hold more than 10 years of IT experience, focused on business domain of the services.

At NCR (Teradata - Jan 2000 till July 2008), I played a key role in establishing Center of Expertise (CoE) for providing services with in Middle East and Africa. I was involved in pricing, it’s business development plan and final execution to the success. This enabled NCR / Teradata Pakistan to establish their foot hold in the Middle East and bring in more revenues. During this engagement an off-shore working model was established and implemented successful for reducing cost with improved efficiency.

Three years later, another off-shore center with the name of Global Consulting Center (GCC) was established. I was again appointed as key member of the management for devising the business plan identifying key challenges and opportunities for it’s success. The center was established with 35 resources and 2 service areas. Due to our creative strategy and proactive approach, with in 3 years the number of employees became 300+ and service areas increased to 5. Off-shore and on-shore mix provided good savings for the entire corporate and also added up to revenues for the local organization. I also played a vital role in identifying key project management practices in the organization leading to a new vertical of project delivery and PMO setup.

I joined PTML / Ufone in July 2008. After joining Ufone, I realized that operational reporting efficiencies are effecting revenues as well as growth plans. I worked closely with C levels to identify their challenges and suggest&implement innovative solutions by reducing costs, improving response time and depicting true&real time depiction for effective decision making.

I am also managing key organizational projects & initiatives ensuring it’s successful implementation and roll out for business excellence. It includes projects like customer life cycle management, customer cost / profitability analysis, retail management optimization.
