Dilum Bandara’s Summary

Dilum is a 2nd year PhD candidate at Colorado State University (CSU). He is working on Collaborative P2P systems that can aggregate diverse capabilities from peers to to achieve even greater tasks, that can't be achieved by individual peers. His current work is related to resource discovery in Distributed Collaborative Adaptive Sensing (DCAS) systems.
Dilum also completed his Masters at CSU (2007-2008) during which he worked on novel "Virtual Sensor Networks". Prior to that, he worked as a junior faculty at University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, which is the same university that he did undergrads (2000-2004).
Dilum's immediate goal is to go back to academia, after completing his PhD. He is dreaming of starting his own business some day...
