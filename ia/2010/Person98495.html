Jim Klonaris’s Summary

As National Public Speaker Jim is passionate about teaching the principles of Leadership and Effectiveness to both individuals and organizations. His dynamic motivational style includes fun, challenging, and thought provoking ideas.

Jim has had a long standing sales, management, and marketing background with Coca-Cola, one of America’s Fortune 100 organizations. Additionally, he has owned over 13 successful private businesses. Currently working with FranklinCovey, he is described by his clients as Innovative, versatile, and energetic. Jim's clients represent a variety of industries including Retail, Environmental Services, Manufacturing, Food Service, Grocery, Beverage, Real Estate, and Utilities.

In addition to being skilled in prioritizing multiple projects in high-pressure situations, "Jim tends to translate “real life” experiences into practical tools that help us achieve their most strategic priorities".

Jim holds a B.F.A. from the University of Tennessee from the College of Art and Architecture.

He currently lives in Knoxville, Tennessee and enjoys time with his wife Lori and their three children. His passions include educating and empowering young adults in financial planning systems, real estate investing, creating design concepts and consulting with business startups. He also enjoys landscape, interior and commercial design.
