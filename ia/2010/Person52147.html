David Taylor’s Summary

David Taylor has been the State Chief Information Officer and Executive Director for the Agency for Enterprise Information Technology since July of 2008. He reports to the Governor and Cabinet members for the State of Florida. Mr. Taylor oversees the development and facilitation of statutory enterprise services and state data center consolidation.

In 2010, Mr. Taylor was honored to receive the ComputerWorld’s Premier 100 IT Leaders award for his enterprise IT vision for Florida government.

Mr. Taylor was previously the Chief Information Officer of the Florida Department of Health beginning in 2003. This position entailed overall authority for IT services and management for a 17,000 employee organization with 2.9 billion in budget. IT expenditures exceed 60 million dollars per year.
