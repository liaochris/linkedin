Donny B. Lord’s Summary

RECORDING ARTIST: I am the protege of Michael Jackson's eldest brother Jackie Jackson (The Jackson Five). Rated the #5 Most Popular Unsigned Artist on MySpace in 2008! ...Style is a cross between Michael Jackson and Bob Marley. Genres: R&B, Pop, Reggae, Reggaeton. Similar Artist: Justin TImberlake, Sean Kingston, Akon and Usher.

"D.B.L.is a fusion of mainstream contemporary Pop music with Caribbean roots" (Mentor, Jackie Jackson, The Jackson Five)

SINGER/SONGWRITER: Written songs that have been featured on Emmy Award winning television shows "The District", as well as "Cane" on CBS, and featured on promotional campaigns for Motorola and Verizon and on MTV. Songs have reached #2 on the African Reggae music charts. One song "Fire!" placed second place in the Dance category of the International Songwriting Competition and "Addicted" reached the Finalists round of KIIS FM's Big Break Competition. "Brat Girls" music video reached 2 million views on MySpace.


DANCER/PERFORMER: Lead dancer and featured performer who has worked with the legendary Jackson family of music, the Pop singer Brandy, studied briefly with the London City Ballet in the U.K. and the Alvin Ailey Dance Company and was a regular on Don Cornelius' televised music show Soul Train.

TELEVISION/FILM ACTOR: starring/guest starring roles on shows for Paramount, CBS, HBO and Disney. Also done commercials for Pepsi, Cheerios, Iron Kids Bread, with Jennifer Love Hewitt, and FOX Television.


TRAVEL: England, France, Switzerland, Belgium, Papua New Guinea, Australia, The Orient, South America, West Indies, Mexico, Canada,
