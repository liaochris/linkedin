Bob Foster’s Summary

I am a management expert that has applied my experience helping both small and large companies grow revenue and increase market share. My background in both operations and product development has allowed me to fully leverage best practice methods in the areas of sales execution, business process, alliance development and marketing management.

I've served in a variety of key positions for outsourcing and communication companies, including Vice President of Customer Service for ETGC, Senior Director for Vartec Communications, Director of Product Management and Strategic Alliances for ACS, and a number of Product Management and Operational positions for Verizon Communications. I adhere to a mutual business development philosophy that focuses on meeting growth and profitability objectives for both clients and alliance partners. This constant emphasis on the alignment of tactics with the goals of all participating parties helps ensure clients deliver exceptional value to the market place.
