Amanda Serfozo’s Summary

Amanda Serfozo is a political science, social psychology, and cultural studies major at Emory University in Atlanta. Both an Honors student and future J.D., Amanda specializes in global communication culture, new media, branding, and neuromarketing, as well as long-form and investigative reportage.

Previously, Amanda has held editorial internships at Rodale (www.rodale.com), organized a nationally recognized literacy drive in her home state of Pennsylvania, and worked for a major presidential campaign. She has been awarded for her outstanding achievement in reporting and editing, as well as academic scholarship and merit.

Her thoughtful, humorous, and genuine approach to public relations and media strategies has earned her several positions as an assistant and intern - she has worked alongside famous internet personalities, philanthropists, and new media entrepreneurs alike.

Before moving to Atlanta, Amanda lived in New York, and the Philadelphia suburbs.

When she's not shamelessly self-branding herself or others (or speaking in the third person!) Amanda loves reading Malcolm Gladwell novels, running, and perusing Target.

You can contact her via email at amandamserfozo@gmail.com
