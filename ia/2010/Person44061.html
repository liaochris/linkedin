Craig Wilson’s Summary

I have learned and excelled in several different industries over my career. which has taught me the value of building relationships. You never know who you might meet next. I enjoy facilitating connections between other professionals, regardless of any direct benefit.

How you stay memorable with your prospects, clients & business partners will differentiate you from your competition. The proper use of handwritten notes or cards can make all the difference. And you stay most memorable when you show appreciation instead of the usual self-promotion.

SendOutCards will help you receive more referrals, have more prospects choose you, and your customers will love you more than they already do. Appreciation wins out over self-promotion everyday.
