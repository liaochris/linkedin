Adam Peterson’s Summary

My passion lies in design. Whether it be in creating a fine arts piece to hang on the wall, or designing complex machinery and components in AutoCAD, I love to design. Born and raised in the Marketing world, I had a thirst for knowledge outside the family run Advertising business. Thus I branched out and pursued an education in Manufacturing Engineering. I received my diploma in the discipline after a one year period studying Architecture at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I consider myself a lifelong student and try to learn as much as possible from every situation.

I aspire not only to be successful and enjoy a CAREER (not just a job) in some aspect of design, but to be a successful husband and father someday.

Life is what you make of it- ENJOY EVERY DAY!
