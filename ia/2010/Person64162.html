E. L. (Betsy) Smith’s Summary

Leadership is not for the faint hearted and Betsy has served in leadership roles at the local, state and national levels. Her consulting business E. L. Smith Consulting works with people and organizations who realize the benefits of investing in themselves and their employees.

Betsy wrote "Second Blooming for Women: Growing a Life that Matters after Fifty" with Kathleen V. Logan. Many of the concepts in the book - attitude, strengths, vision, values, purpose, and action plans are the same concepts she integrates into her leadership and team building consulting.




Be bold, and might forces will come to your aid.
