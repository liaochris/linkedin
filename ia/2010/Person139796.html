Brian Wolstenholme, PharmD, CGP, FASCP’s Summary

Part of a new team of “Clinical Science Consultants” (CSCs) launched within the Boehringer-Ingelheim sales organization.
Objective: partner with the primary care practitioner community to improve the way specific targeted disease states such as COPD, are managed across healthcare channels. Strong emphasis is placed on encouraging earlier screening, diagnosis, and appropriate treatment of patients with a high risk for COPD.
The CSC team compliments the efforts of the current sales force and managed markets field personnel; and is strategically placed within specific geographies where the greatest impact may be achieved.

I am president of a medication therapy management firm, located in South Florida. I am a Doctor of Pharmacy, Board-Certified Geriatric Pharmacist, Certified Consultant Pharmacist and Fellow of the American Society of Consultant Pharmacists.

My practice was started to spread the word that medication-related problems, if categorized as a disease, would be the 5th-leading cause of death in the United States. Our specialists identify, resolve and prevent medication-related problems by working with patients, their caregivers, physicians and others to optimize the medication regimen for optimal outcomes.

Data shows that our type of practice will save lives, improve our population's health and significantly reduce healthcare expenditures.
