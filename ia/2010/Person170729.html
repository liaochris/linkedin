Reggie Middleton’s Summary

Working from his home office in New York, professional global macro investor Reggie Middleton applies his own investment tools, technology and research team to predict the value of corporations in a variety of global investment cycles. Loyal readers of BoomBustBlog.com praise Middleton’s sharp perspectives and credit him for inspiring “lifestyle changing gains.” Comments one reader, “Nothing in the financial press can compare to what we have found on your blog — nothing.”

Middleton delivers daily posts containing fundamental analysis and investment strategies at no charge to readers. His no-nonsense approach and trusted research methodology has garnered a devoted following of banks, hedge funds, institutional investors and individual investors seeking comprehensive research that consistently produces tangible returns.

Blog members hail Middleton as the “bad ass” of the investment world and the “future of Wall Street.” Herb Greenberg of the Wall Street Journal Weekend Edition and MarketWatch.com says “a scroll through his archives shows good edginess and an impressive record on credit-related issues.”

Visit Reggie Middleton’s BoomBustBlog.com daily to view investment newsletters demonstrating his research team’s use of global macro strategy and proprietary trading technology to predict the boom and bust of major corporations.
