Preeti Gupta’s Summary

Having been associated with the PR industry for close to a decade now, I feel there is still much more to be explored and developed. Opportunity for creativity is endless. An attribute that I have tried to inculcate and nurture and founded my company upon, is “Reliability”. I believe this quality is the finest offering a person or an organization can make to its clients, subordinates and almost anyone one may be dealing with.

Alongway, I trust myself on having gathered good leadership and communication skills, that enabled me to launch my own PR operations.

Like any other professional, I too have passed through the phases of being at the "leaf" of delivery chain to being a "node" in the network, living multiple roles and responsibilities and fulfilling multiple requirements. In my personal opinion, I see creating employment for others as a definite point of satisfaction in life. To be able to further this ambition should be a beautiful sight.
