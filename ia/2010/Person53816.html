Deb Singer’s Summary

Singer Sustainability is focused on Retail Strategy and Business Development for manufacturers and retailers.

We help manufacturers develop, optimize, market and sell natural consumer brands. Helping craft a strong retail strategy and connect you with the right retailers, leading to your brand’s market place success.

On the retail side, Deb Singer creates profitable sustainable housewares lines and merchandising programs for leading retailers that want to leverage consumers’ growing interest in sustainable products. These products pop off the shelf with color and fun, use best practices in sustainability, tap into the real needs of consumers and are at the forefront of up-to-the minute trends.
Deb spent 15 years at Whole Foods, where she played a leadership role in creating the “Whole Living” line of products and was the visionary for Whole Foods Better Bags. (You know: those ubiquitous reusable totes that seemed strange a couple of years ago, that more and more shoppers now use to carry their purchases instead of taking new bags at every store visit.)

Deb is now committed to bringing sustainability to mainstream retailers, and proving that products that are good for the planet can be fun, functional and deliver margins that match or beat mainstream options.

She lives with her family and beloved beagle in New England.
