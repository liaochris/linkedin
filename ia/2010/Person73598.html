Brian Gallagher’s Summary

Brian’s experience is an amalgamation of technical expertise, data strategy, and consulting. With a B.S. in Information Technology and a concentration in both Management and Economics, his education lends to his role as a consultant.

His career began as an applications developer specialising in database applications. He has experience with various platforms including .NET, ASP, PHP, Java, VBA, and more. Brian’s IT studies primarily involved database administration and data modelling with heavy concentration on Oracle platforms. As such, he has also provided DBA and data modelling services to various organisations.

As the lead Analyst at Auragen Communications in Rochester, NY, Brian developed their interactive marketing offering while also the working as a primary analyst on Eastman Kodak’s worldwide CRM initiatives. While he managed relationships between Kodak and their various CRM platforms, he was also responsible for identifying and translating business requirements for the development of Eastman Kodak’s 2006 worldwide CRM platform.

In addition to analytics consultancy, application development, and other experience mentioned above, Brian also has experience in usability engineering, website design/implementation, and search engine optimisation. He has experience across various analytical platforms including but not limited to WebTrends, Google Analytics, Omniture, VisualSciences, and Hitbox.

His experience has been across various global enterprises including Eastman Kodak Company, Mazda Motors Europe, BirdsEye Foods, Russell Investment Firm, Citroën, Procter & Gamble, Barclays Bank, Marks & Spencer and more.
