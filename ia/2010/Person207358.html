Touraj Parang’s Summary

I enjoy building companies and launching products globally. I have a unique approach that is based on over 10 years of experience in Silicon Valley as a product-oriented executive, venture capitalist and corporate attorney.

My particular focus since 2003 has been on social networking and consumer internet services that have the real potential to grow virally worldwide and make a difference in the lives of millions of people. My first company (JAXTR.COM) was a great success in that regard (virally growing to over 10 million registered users in 1 year). My current company (WEBS.COM) is another viral phenomenon, with over 40 million registered users who have used our service to build a website and millions in revenue.
