Kees Recourt’s Summary

After my BSc education in Information Technology, I decided to develop myself in the field of Life Sciences (MSc Utrecht University), and received my PhD at Leiden University in 1991. During my research career in Wageningen, I specialised in the molecular quality of food products and worked for companies such as Hak, Danone, and Nestle. In 1999, I moved towards the commercialisation of technology and was employed at Licentec (shareholders ABN AMRO & NPM capital), a private firm in the Utrecht area. During this position, I also was CEO of an Amsterdam-spin off company developing a bio-artificial liver device. After learning the principles of VC-financing at the NVP, I accepted a position as investment manager at Mibiton in 2004. The Mibiton funds stimulate the development of professional Life Sciences infrastructures (22 million euro invested, loans and equity capital). Currently, representing Mibiton as a board member of BioConnection BV, a large scale pharmaceutical GMP facility. Owner of a consultancy- and management firm: Recourt Life Sciences.

Kees Recourt’s Specialties:

Due diligence and investing in start up companies. Creating value in the Life Sciences chain by (i) by initiating public-private partnerships (ii) being a board member of LS companies and seed funds (NGI and STW) (iii) managing the Masterclass Biobusiness (Niaba/De Baak/Nyenrode).
