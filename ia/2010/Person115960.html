Kevin Sinnott’s Summary

I am dedicated to improving the world one cup at a time. I’ve studied coffee and performing and can present at all levels of interest. I teach consumers who just want a better cup of coffee using their home equipment. I regale senior audiences with a nostalgic trip through coffee history, from its origins in the Arab world, through its role getting American back to work in the Great Depression. I work a crowd, I love AM talk radio, have appeared with TV Superstars John Stossel and Oprah, but am equally at home at a bookstore or public library. I autograph all my DVDs, books and even coffee cups at events. I teach employees at coffee shops who need to become conversant in coffee talk, as well as how to brew coffee. I consult for major coffee companies, and brewing appliance manufacturers who want to discover what really matters in a machine, and for hotel chains desiring to upgrade their coffee. I will also, for an outrageous fee, personally instruct millionaires in the art of brewing their own coffee, for the purpose of showing off for their friends and to preserve their marriages.

Kevin Sinnott’s Specialties:

Presentations, demonstrations, writing, consulting. "I make the best cup of coffee that can be made".
