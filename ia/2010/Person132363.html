Maria Langen’s Summary

Maria Langen is a Communication Specialist experienced in internal and external strategy work, project management, digital channel development and crisis management. Since 1993 she has been working both with big companies and the public sector.

Maria Langen runs Sverredal & Langen AB / the Swedish Institute for Crisis Communication together with Sverre Sverredal.

Latest publications (books in Swedish):
- "Snacka om kaos!" (Piknik, 2009)
- Drömwebben 2.0 (Uppsala Publishing House, 2007)

The overall goal is to always do such a good job that customers and clients recommend Sverredal & Langen.

Maria Langen’s Specialties:

Integrated Internal and External Communication. Crisis Communication; Strategies, plans, workshops, collaboration, channels, etc.Digital Channels; Social Media, Intranet and External Web Communication. Grief handling; Crisis courses. Project and Change Management.
