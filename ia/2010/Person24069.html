Bill Simmon’s Summary

Award winning filmmaker with extensive experience in all aspects of film/video production & post production. Particular expertise in cinematography and editing.

New media evangelist and web 2.0 consultant. Award winning blogger and podcaster. Public speaker on topics including blogging/podcasting/video blogging, web 2.0, the democratization of media and technology, free speech, and the digital revolution.

Media educator with teaching experience at all education levels and ages. Taught college courses in film production and digital editing.

Published magazine writer with an emphasis on technology and film writing.

Bill Simmon’s Specialties:

cinematography, digital editing, writing, editing, teaching, tech evangelism, free speech advocacy, new media consulting
