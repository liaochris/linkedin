Phil Newell’s Summary

Mr. Newell has over 20 years experience in business management, information technology and business process improvement bringing together strong business acumen, international business development, project/program management, deep technology understanding and business consulting to deliver sustainable, goal exceeding business growth.

• Results oriented leader who uses process improvement as a tool to ensure that he consistently exceeds his goals and objectives from both an organizational perspective as well as a financial deliverable and that those goals and objectives are sustainable within the organization.

• Big picture leader who understands the critical link between strategy and execution and will focus on the gaps that are preventing execution form taking place. Mr. Newell has proven results of his ability to drill down to the root cause of issues and using “out-of-the-box” thinking, develop sustainable solutions that can be effectively implemented to improve business performance.

• International business development successes selling enterprise solutions to Fortune 1000 clients. An Engaging individual, comfortable making business presentations to ‘C’ level executive management of any size company in any part of the world. Leadership style focuses on clear communications, goal setting, mentoring and process.

• Publisher of several papers, recognized for their thought leadership. Accomplished speaker at internationally recognized industry conferences where he frequently introduces new concepts to business and technology managers on enhanced strategic alignment of business improvement methods using technology solutions.
