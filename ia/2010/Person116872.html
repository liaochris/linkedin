Kim Fabian’s Summary

Offer communications consulting, training and team building. Specializing in health care communication, employee communication, and team-building programs that take corporate teams out of the office to experience activities like scavenger hunts, golf, cooking, yoga, boot camp, wine tasting and community outreach projects, then applying learnings back to the work environment.

Co-founder of the Mid-Level Alliance, an innovative management training program that brings together key mid-level personnel from organizations throughout the region to build skills and create a community of peers. Participation in the Mid-Level Alliance ensures that organizations are developing future leaders who have the fine-tuned skills necessary to drive visionary strategy, as well as maximize daily performance.
