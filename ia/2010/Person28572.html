Brian Kalish’s Summary

I am a reporter/producer at Government Executive and Nextgov.com, both Atlantic Media publications. I write and blog daily for the websites and write for the monthly magazine.

I was most recently deputy news editor of the Air Force Times, a weekly Gannett paper. Before that I was copyreader, national at Dow Jones Newswires NewsPlus and Investment Banker in New York City. I was promoted to that role from a reporting assistant at the company in Washington DC, filing stories primarily from SEC reports - where I wrote over 1,800 stories. I also have written over 10 stories for the company-owned Wall Street Journal.

I have previous experience in reporting through internships at USA Today-Washington, the WashingtonPost.com, and Fox News Radio, covering politics at all.

I have helped write a $15,000 book for a London-based luxury publisher, and covered my hometown on Long Island, for the East Meadow Herald. I have also covered stories for The Eagle, American University's student-run newspaper. I graduated AU in May 2008.
