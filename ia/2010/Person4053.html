Pat Ahearne’s Summary

I played professional baseball for 16 seasons playing all over the U.S., Australia, Venezuela, Italy, and Taiwan with Major League time with the Detroit Tigers.

The Way of Baseball is a baseball development website for athletes, coaches, and parents. It brings together all the top level training and information I used for a 16 year, injury-free, PED-free, baseball career.

My goal is to continue the work on my website and benefit as many athletes as possible and to continue work in baseball on the development and coaching side professionally.

My photos have been published in New York Newsday, I've designed and photographed print advertisements for retail establishments.

The photography is on display at http://ahearnephoto.smugmug.com

    Rod Dedeaux Research & Baseball Institute, USC

    (Sports industry)

    September 2007 — Present (3 years )

    The Rod Dedeaux Research and Baseball Institute brings together leaders from the medical science community and the sports training community. Through their joint efforts, the RDRBI helps athletes achieve greater core strength, less risk of injury, and overall higher performance. Our belief is that, by teaching not only athletes but also their parents and coaches, we can set the groundwork for a lifetime of athletic success and health. An integrated effort by all involved parties will research, develop, publish and apply objective information and instruction on throwing and striking biomechanics, as well as optimal physical, nutritional and mental/emotional conditioning. Located adjacent to Dedeaux Field on the University of Southern California campus, the 10,000 square foot RDRBI facility uses state-of-the-art technology to determine where an athlete is at greatest risk of injury and what can be done to reduce that risk.
