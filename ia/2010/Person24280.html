Bing Pascual’s Summary

Since 1999, Bing Pascual has been an active programmer using the PHP language. He has been involved with both the engineering and project management sides of custom software development. With inMotion Group, he continues to service accounts ranging from the small business to Fortune 500 companies.

Currently, Mr. Pascual and his partners have devoted their time and resources to incubate inMotion Group’s current project, the inClick Ad Server – A scalable content-sensitive white-labeled advertisement delivery platform designed for the enterprise.

More information on the inClick Ad Server can be found at http://www.inclick.net

Mr. Pascual is also actively involved in the community. In addition to co-founding a successful non-profit animal welfare group, he also assists a number of similar non-profit groups with formulating solid fund-raising strategies, marketing efforts, and business procedures.
