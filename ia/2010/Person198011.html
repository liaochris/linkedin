Susan Seeley Roe’s Summary

Susan Seeley Roe is a Tucson based Graphic Designer and Artist. Susan received her BFA in Graphic Design from the Minneapolis College of Art & Design in 2001.

With several years experience in the design field, Susan has also become an active member in her community. She was a co-founder and President of Ad2 Tucson. Through her affiliation with AD2, Susan was instrumental in creating a public service campaign for the Literacy Volunteers of Tucson. The AD2 project produced about $181,000 in consulting, creative development, production and media placement for the non-profit organization. The awareness campaign resulted in generating approximately 40 volunteers for the organization.

Susan has also served as a board member for the Tucson Advertising Federation and for the Twin City Fine Arts Organization. She is also an active member of AIGA.
