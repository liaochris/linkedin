Dr. Huei Hsia Holloman’s Summary

I have been an academe in higher education since 2002, with a track record in research, teaching, leadership services experiences. I am also a partner at the Noble Leadership Program in the United States. Our program is committed to the ongoing pursuit of the highest human virtues and aspirations. It is about learning how to lead oneself before leading others and how to overcome all the self destructive habits that prevent us from being true leaders. I am also helping my siblings in China, Taiwan and France expand their international businesses.

My prior career experiences included being a journalist, political campaign manager, policy analyst, survey data manager, and fitness instructor.

Research Interests: Demography, labor market, religion, racial/ethnic relations, gender, sexuality, popular culture, business management and inspirational leadership.
