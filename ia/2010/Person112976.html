Kevin Benz’s Summary

With well over twenty years of Technology Management experience, I provide high-value leadership to the construction and day-to-day management of distributed systems. With direct management of numbers from one to one hundred, both on-shore and off, as well as the varying environments that arise in consulting engagements, I have been lucky to participate in a wide range of development projects integrated on nearly every hardware vendor, open and closed source from single to massively deployed global systems.

Although my technical skills predominates a resume with my track-record, primary to each of those engagements underlies the entrepreneurial capabilities required to build a business from concept to completion as well as the day-to-day rounding that comes from leadership of businesses in varying stages of evolution.

Ultimately a leader’s track record is the barometer with which to measure individual accomplishments and I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my skills can be applied within your organization. Additionally, I am pleased to offer references, not only from those individuals to whom I reported, but additionally, I am pleased to offer, on request, references from those who I managed.
