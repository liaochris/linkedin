Carmine Zinni’s Summary

As a designer fascinated by the technological revolution, I look to interpret the visions of others into visual deliverables as a point of orientation for moving forward.

A quick learner, I adapt to any environment where my design and development skills can be leveraged. I know my limitations and understand my strengths, enjoy team camaraderie and the feeling of accomplishment achieved from working toward a common goal. I have a good sense of humor and can take criticism over any design decision because of the firm belief in the iterative process required to achieve a desired outcome.

I have a deep respect for those who overcome obstacles, sacrifice for others, and project a positive attitude with dignity and self-control.
