Roland Wartenberg’s Summary

Roland started his IT career as a Consultant with Digital Equipment from 1991 to 1997.

He has been with SAP since 1997, and from 2000 to 2002, he was a Development Manager with SAP Markets, Palo Alto.

From 2003 to 2005 he has lent his multiple skills to the position of Product Manager for Adaptive Computing with SAP AG, Walldorf.

At the end of 2005 Roland took over responsibilities as evangelist for SAP's Platform Ecosystem at SAP Labs, Palo Alto.

Since January 2007 he is focusing on SAP's strategy around virtualization.

During the last years Roland did over 200 public presentations at different events. Roland holds a Dipl.Ing. in Electrical Engineering.
