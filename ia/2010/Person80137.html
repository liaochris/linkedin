Harry Brelsford’s Summary

I enjoy business and applying technology to business.

Did you know that I have written 17+ books in the SMB technology area as author and/or co-author? These books have an underlying "Trusted Business Advisor" theme for consultants/channel partners implementing SMB technology solutions in the network infrastructure (e.g. Microsoft Small Business Server) and VoIP areas (Microsoft Response Point).

I am the founder of SMB Nation, a publishing and events entity that serves SMB channel partners and consultants - we are a portal that helps our 36,000 customers (known as tribal members) navigate the SMB technology opportunity - in good times and bad.

Our SMB PC magazine, now in its fourth year of telling the SMB channel "story," exceeds 40,000 readers quarterly. Our Web sites (both SMB Nation and Telephonation) attract a high quality SMB channel partner and consultant who are decision-makers for themselves and their real world customers.

We are in the seventh year of our SMB technology events model. Our big fall show (October), now housed in Las Vegas, has over 600+ SMB channel partners, 50+ expert speakers and 100+ sponsors. Our spring show in May in the New York City area has 200+ attendees and is held at a college campus, creating a fantastic academic "buzz."

Philosophy:I also like to think of the following balance:
**Business - compete hard and fair - it's all good!
**Community - my 12+ years of adjunct teaching and my $ giving are how I participate in the community.
**Fun - I actively ski and bike - again - it's all good!

So I hope that you have now gotten to know me better. NExt time you visit my beloved Bainbridge Island just outside Seattle, I'd be happy to buy you a cup of espresso and visit!

PS - our company mascot is our English Springer Spaniel named "Astro"

:)
