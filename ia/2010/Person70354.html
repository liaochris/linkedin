Floris van Steensel’s Summary

Drive & Partners is a specialist recruitment consultancy for the European ERP/SAP Market. Drive & Partners was founded by me in 2008 and over the past years I have built an extensive international network of SAP candidates and clients. Before starting my own company I worked several years for a large international recruitment firm. Drive & Partners is differentiating itself by delivering fast against lower costs than the bigger players in the market. I built my business on honesty and open communication as well as a personal approach.

If you are an IT/SAP manager looking for a party with which you can work on a pleasant, open and honest way and a partner that finds solutions there is no need to look any further.

We are mainly focusing on the SAP market.
