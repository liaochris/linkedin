Janet Fouts’s Summary

Speaking
I speak on a variety of topics around social media, online community and web marketing strategy. Recent conferences include DevLearn, Non Profit Technology Conference, Silicon Valley Business Week, Social Media Boot Camp, SDForum and several others. I also present webinars and online classes and host a BlogTalk Radio show "Gamechanging".

Social media
My chops in “social media” go back to founding ONTHERAIL, one of the first on-line communities for restaurant folk. I was the online community developer for the Exploratorium before I became Director of Web Development there. As a social media coach I help people "get" social media and find a way to build a strategy that works for them. Then I regularly check in with new information, new tools and support them as their social media campaign grows.

At Tatu Digital we work with social media in a variety of ways, from building blogs and helping our clients define their social media presence to social media marketing campaigns.

Social Media Success!
Published in late Nov, 2009 this book give real actionable information you can put to use immediately. Whether you're a novice or pro you'll find something of use here.

Web marketing
It's no longer a case of “build it and they will come” and frankly, unless you're going to spend thousands, nobody can guarantee you top level on the search engines either. So how do you get your message out and increase your traffic from real leads and not bots? We can show you how. Need a Google AdWords or Facebook ad campaign? We can help you develop a strategy, manage the campaign and help you tune your site and ads to get results.

Web development
We carefully choose the delivery method based on the needs of the client, not just because we want to practice the latest greatest methods on your website. Our tools include PHP, Javascript, AJAX, XML, HTML and CSS.
