Renato Taralli’s Summary

A marketer with over 10 years expertise in building interactive experiences in South America and the U.S., Renato has worked on the strategic and account side for a diverse list of multi-national clients such as: Microsoft, Procter&Gamble, Oracle, Whirlpool, Telefonica, Cigna Insurance, Sun Microsystems, Ferrero’s Kinder Egg, Carrefour, Philips etc.

Associated with a passion for user experience, motion picture, Internet and the art of innovation, his foundation was laid for a career in communications, marketing and project management. Based on an interesting mix of life exposures, Renato is driven by a curiosity and deep passion to understand people’s behavior, culture, interaction and way of living. He is always looking ahead, trying to figure out how to impact/influence/connect the way we live our lives and the choices we make.

Besides his professional career, Renato has many passions and interests such as lead the development of Ultimate Frisbee sport everywhere he is located and, as an outdoor enthusiast, hike and mountain bike every time he has a chance.

Renato is authorized to work in Brazil and the U.S. (employment status: U.S. Green Card).
