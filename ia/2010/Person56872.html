Bernhart Associates partnered with the DMA in providing exclusive analysis for the 2009 DMA Employment Outlook Report. Jerry is a sought after speaker and writer and in 2009
authored a widely acclaimed white paper for the International Journal of Mobile Marketing. Jerry Bernhart has been featured twice on the cover of Target Marketing magazine, including December of 2009, predicting key trends shaping digital and direct marketing.

Jerry has been recruiting database marketing talent since 1992 and remains Director of Executive Search for the Database Marketing Institute (Arthur Hughes). Jerry was a contributor to the critically acclaimed Data Mining Cookbook and has been a guest speaker at SAS User Groups and database marketing conferences.

Jerry is also a charter Advisory Board member of Data University.
