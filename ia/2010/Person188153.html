Shawn Elledge’s Summary

I am a direct marketing professional specializing in developing integrated marketing solutions. I have expertise in demand generation applications designed to align sales and marketing departments. These applications will help automate the distribution of marketing messages, making the marketing department more efficient, while providing sales with lead scores and lead nurturing capabilities that make them more effective.
I am also recognized as a thought leader in other direct response channels such as direct mail, email marketing, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, and conversion optimization.
These days I am focused on the continued education of sales and marketing professionals in tier two cities across the U.S. To help me accomplish this initiative I launched an event series called the Integrated Marketing Summit where I invite other thought leaders from around the world to participate in this one day event. We host 15 programs and 2 keynote presentations with the goal of helping companies drive revenue rapidly.
