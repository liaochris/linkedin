Steve Riegel’s Summary

I am a founder and currently Principal at Faction Media, a full-service digitally led communications agency that helps clients compete, evolve and grow in an increasingly complex and fragmented marketplace. Faction provides digital strategy, audience insight, creative services, interactive development, search marketing, performance analytics and marketing optimization to Fortune 1000 companies including some of the largest and most innovative businesses in the World.

In my daily life, I guide the development and management of Faction’s Search programs, and I act as the chief advocate of best practices for all Search related activity across the accounts.

Throughout my career, my ambition and enthusiasm for Search, and all that it encompasses, has been unwavering. My constant goal has been to connect clients directly to their online target audience through market research, analysis, strategy, and design. My approach to Search has been hailed by industry leaders such as Michael Campbell of Dynamic Media as “Best in Class” and “easily in the top 1% of search engine marketers”.

As a Web Search pioneer, I documented the early years of Search in my first guidebook "A Taste of the Net". Following that, I spent several years in market and behavioral research as a Director of Quantitative Operations in firms owned by Susquehanna Broadcasting and COX Communications. I later returned to Search as the convergence of Internet function and marketing vision began and the world of interactive marketing as we now know it took shape.

In 2001, I brought my Web marketing vision and practical experience together to create one of the first true search marketing firms in the country, SharpLink. With SharpLink, I worked “Search magic” in industries as diverse as Manufacturing, Travel, Marketing, Real Estate and Food and Beverage.
