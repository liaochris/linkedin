Steve McAllister’s Summary

My latest book "The McAllister Code" is a fictional approach to socially conscious marketing that puts Sarasota in the limelight of the coming revolution of business management and personal excellence. You can order the ebook at http://www.themcallistercode.com or through Amazon.

To coincide with the release of the book, I am blogging at http://www.inkensoul.com and other social networking sites about the tenets used to create the themes of the book.

I am also a Contributing Editor at http://www.modernhippiemag.com.

It is my goal to further the education of my community and my world about these innovations through the mediums of film and video.
