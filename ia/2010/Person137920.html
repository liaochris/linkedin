Matt Maltese’s Summary

Matt Maltese is a Film Editor who resides in New York City. He discovered his passion for editing and motion design at a very young age.

After graduating from The New York Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Communication Arts, several of his film professors including the college's President, recognized his talent and employed him for their Documentaries, Narrative Films, and Motion Design projects. His school awarded him the "Academy of Television Arts & Sciences Scholarship Award" for being most likely to succeed in the TV and Film industry.

Since then, he has shot and edited over a dozen narrative short films, promotional videos, and edited a feature length documentary, THE ROAD HOME (2008). He says "What I love about most editing is that despite it's invisibility as an art form, it can still affect you deeply on an emotional level." Matt Maltese continues to develop his talents further by doing what he loves most, editing films. His goal is to become a well known professional film editor, and become extremely versatile in post production.
