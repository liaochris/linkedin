Ari Herzog’s Summary

Sought after for cutting through web 2.0 clutter and sharing best practices on corporate change, public policy, and organizational reform, my dual roles as an online media strategist and elected city councilor have led me to influence business and government policy makers as a social media management consultant, workshop instructor, and online writer/editor.

With a bachelor's in sociology and a master's in public administration, I am actively seeking opportunities to leverage my 10 years of experience in information technology, community journalism, and government administration.

While my post-collegiate years were spent as a corporate webmaster and freelance writer, I began a series of senior government management roles in 2004, the same year I began blogging. Fast forward to today and I am passionate about empowering civic engagement to narrow the digital divide.

Since December 2008, I consult, speak, and write about best practices of social media and online marketing to reduce overhead and increase productivity. I do this through a 5-step process of analyzing, recommending, implementing, branding, and monitoring.

To hire me for your next social media or community management project, please contact me today! Email me at ariherzog@gmail.com or call me at 978-558-0008. Thank you.

Ari Herzog’s Experience

  • City Councilor

    Newburyport City Council

    (Government Administration industry)

    January 2010 — Present (1 month)

    Elected on November 3, 2009 to the Newburyport (Massachusetts) City Council in an at-large position. Unlike ward councilors who serve geographic areas, at-large councilors serve constituents across the city. In Newburyport, this includes about 17,000 people.

    My 2-year term commenced January 2010. I am the chairman of the Neighborhoods and City Services Committee, and a member of the Budget and Finance Committee.

    If you have a Facebook account, consider following updates and sharing ideas at http://facebook.com/ari4newburyport

  • Principal

    Ari Herzog & Associates (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Management Consulting industry)

    December 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    With a holistic approach, I provide online media strategies for organizational leaders in three major areas:

    • Consulting: Through a 5-step process of analyzing, recommending, implementing, branding, and monitoring, I consult about the best practices of social media and online marketing to reduce overhead and increase productivity.
