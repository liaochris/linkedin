Robert Davison’s Summary

Seasoned IT professional with over 28 years in the industry, predominately in Software Houses and Consultancy firms.

Majority of experience is in delivering visionary and strategic software solutions to the following sectors: legal, financial, retail&distrubtion and utility sectors.

He is both a businessman and a technologist who knows the true value that well designed systems can add to an organisation.

Robert is passionate about his subject and about ensuring he continues his values of integrity, understanding and innovative business solutions are highly valued.

He has presented at business and technical seminars throughout the world on his vision for business agility, thought leadership and information management solutions.
