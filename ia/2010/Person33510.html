Matt Carter’s Summary

Execution oriented executive with proven track record in bringing talent, leadership and passion to entrepreneurial and consumer-focused organizations. Able to drive business and marketing strategy, build high-performing, metrics-focused teams, and formulate and execute brand positioning, product/pricing, channel, and customer service strategies that drive profitable growth and strategic change. Consistently successful in turnaround and highly challenging situations across diverse industries, spanning start-ups to household names. Spark positive cultural change and instill “team first” mentality.

►Earned widespread media recognition for spearheading rapid transformation of perceived non-threatening wireless player into formidable competitor and fastest growing prepaid wireless company in U.S.

►Crafted/executed business strategy that reversed profit erosion for wireless company’s core business, delivering double-digit earnings increases for 2 consecutive years while capturing first market share gain in core markets in 2 years.

►Championed and drove strategy and vision to take wireless carrier to double-digit unit sale and profit increases plus highest market share gain within footprint among its competitors.

►Achieved successful integration of $660M acquisition for financial services company, applying strategies to increase revenues 27% over and 60 days ahead of plan. Led acquisition that established bank as full-service financial provider.

Recognized as major contributor and leader in the technology industry. Achieved coveted Top 25 rank among power players and one of the Top Rising Stars by Fierce Wireless in 2009. Named by the pretigious American Business Association 'Turnaround Executive of the Year' for 2009. Sought-after corporate board member/advisor and Member of Executive Leadership Council.
