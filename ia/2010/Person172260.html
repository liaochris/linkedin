Richard Guha’s Summary

Please note that I welcome invitations to join their network from people I know.

If you want me to pass on an introduction, I will be happy to do so as long as you also mention what is in it for them and why they should respond.

I have been successfully running, starting and turning around businesses all my career. I have done this on a global scale, having lived in ten countries on four continents, and had worldwide responsibilities Both as a line manager and consultant, I have transformed under-performing, or wrongly positioned businesses around the world into successful ones. I have done this as a line manager in Fortune 100 companies and as an advisor to, or interim manager in, companies large and small. As President of a $ 7 Billion Energy company, I turned it around, and as General Manager of an unprofitable and struggling $20 Million frozen food business, I transformed it so it grew into the $ 3 Billion business it is now. I have multiplied the market value of the last three large companies I have run a provable several times vs. value when I arrived.

The scope of my experience covers multiple industries, from Consumer Packaged Goods to High-tech. My expertise lies in multiple functions, from General Management to Marketing, and I am more than averagely knowledgeable about Finance, R&D, HR, Operations, Sourcing, Logistics, Sales, and IT. Spoken at many large and small conferences, appeared on national and local TV, radio, newspapers and magazines from CNBC to AdAge; the Wall Street Journal to the Boston Globe, and have been published on multiple occasions

I am also Chairman Emeritus of the Marketing Executives Networking Group, the largest non-profit professional group for senior executives in the world, offering a large continuing education program as well as an opportunity for members to connect with each other.
