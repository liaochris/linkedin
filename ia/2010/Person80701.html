Heather Bourne’s Education

  • University of Massachusetts, Amherst

    Ph.D. , Clinical Pyschology , 2004 — 2006

    Doctoral dissertation: "Gender ideology, depression, and marital qiuality in working-class, dual-earner couples across the transition to parenthood". Drawing upon date from the MIH-funded Work and Family Transitions Project, this thesis used dyadic hierarchical regression models (HLM) to explore linkages between new parents' gender-role ideology, depression, and marital quality.
