Bree Long’s Summary

Bree Long, CEO; Urban Consulting, and Residential Listing Specialist:
Ms. Long is a well-respected and growing leader in urban sales and marketing practices, having earned a reputation for excellence through her partnerships with luxury and urban homebuilders throughout southern California. With an extensive background in sales, marketing, and escrow management, Ms. Long has successfully managed & marketed nearly $700 million in new home real estate developments for some of the region’s most high-profile development projects since 2002.

Recently, Ms. Long has shifted the focus of the organization to managing, re-positioning, and selling bank-owned property for such financial institutions as JP Morgan Chase, Washington Mutual, California National Bank, Countrywide Home Loans, and First American National Title. Through her work with REO’s, Ms. Long has fortified her knowledge base of virtually every Los Angeles sub-market and possesses a unique marketing advantage based on her understanding of the subtle nuances of various areas and neighborhoods throughout Los Angeles.

In light of the broad spectrum of her expertise, Ms. Long continues to put the interests of the consumer as her first, foremost, and primary concern. This core value is reflected through her uncompromising attention to the individual objectives of the client in combination with strategic consideration of the broader conditions of a changing market. From first-time homebuyer to multi-unit investor or developer, Ms. Long ensures sales success for each of her clients through focus on cutting edge marketing and on-point positioning that exceeds expectations and keeps her clients consistently singing her praises.
