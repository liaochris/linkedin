Rob Springer’s Summary

Rob has been a TV & video producer for over 22 years & internet marketer for over 13 years. His career started in New York at CBS Sports in 1987, which lead him to the position of Studio Manager at one of NYC top acting schools.

While working behind-the-scenes on NYC-based media projects - and writing for Videomaker Magazine, Rob also performed in on-camera roles that included Saturday Night Live and independent films.

In the early 90's Rob set up an ambitious production facility on the resort island of Hilton Head, SC. Island Vision Video created a wide & diverse client list while snagging several industry awards.

Island Vision had a successful 10 year run and included clients like DreamWorks, VH1, The Discovery Channel, The History Channel, Citicorp, Pfizer, Verizon and other local & regional companies.

In 2002, Rob started a cutting-edge media production department for a large regional communications company.

His production facility creates diverse television and media projects that range from feature films to national ad campaigns, field production for national TV networks, TV shows and more.

Rob has received accolades that include a Silver & Bronze Apple Award from The National Educational Media Network for Production & Cinematography and an Addy Award.

Most recently, in June 2007, Rob scored two Silver and one Bronze Telly Awards for cinematography, videography and production skill.

Rob's own 13-year-old endeavor, tvscreen.com, focuses on diverse media & content centered web properties.

tvscreen.com media's next major project nears completion:"The Insider's Guide to TV Jobs".

It is scheduled for a January 2010 release.
