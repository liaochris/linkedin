Lubna Shaikh’s Summary

For over 25 years, Calian has established close and lasting relationships with our employees and customers alike. As a result, we have realized long-term sustainable growth, and have emerged as a premier supplier of Business and Technology Services to both the government and private sectors.

Calian’s business is to solve and support our customers’ recruiting and workforce management challenges by providing the right person to the right opportunity. Our success in providing these solutions allows us to offer our employees a wealth of attractive opportunities ranging from full time, part-time and casual employee positions to short and long-term consulting work.

We currently employ a workforce of over 2,200 professionals who provide support and expertise to our customers in project management, IT services, healthcare, training and more. The diversity of our customers allows us to offer opportunities in a variety of exciting locations from urban big-city hubs, mid-sized locales to rural and remote locations.

Calian is dedicated to our customers’ success and building lasting relationships with our employees. Headquartered in Kanata, Ontario, Canada, Calian’s Business and Technology Services (BTS) Division has offices in Toronto, Kingston, Calgary, Edmonton, Montreal, Halifax, Oromocto, and Manassas, Virginia, USA.
