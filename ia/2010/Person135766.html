Marty Jorgensen’s Summary

My experience runs the gamut from the late 80s, starting with Forth programming on Macintoshes, to systems, networking, databases, storage, security, Internet infrastructure services (webservers, email, etc). I do not consider myself a programmer by trade, rather I see myself as an integrator, taking pre-built systems, adding code where necessary to mesh them together, constructing large systems greater than the sum of its parts.
Within the past three years, management has interested me, as I feel I have the skills of working with people to bring out the best in them in a team environment, so that is what I currently do. I manage a team of 5 individuals, some local to myself, and some in other parts of the US and Europe.
