Steve Johnsen, MBA’s Summary

Steve Johnsen is a small business growth specialist, business strategist & entrepreneur. Recognized as a hands-on and visionary leader who has led multiple organizations to financial success, Mr. Johnsen has enabled 50%+ growth rates at multiple companies. He leads company growth and success in just about every area, including leadership, strategic planning, turnarounds, change management, operations, finance & accounting, sales & marketing, system design, business development, project management, operations, supply chain management, product development, and branding. Mr. Johnsen often performs as Chief Operating Officer, Chief Financial Officer, Chief Executive Officer and Chief Strategy Officer for his private clients. He has a particular interest in leveraging technology to make business operations successful and more efficient, and has designed and launched four successful enterprise software applications.

Mr. Johnsen is a lifetime business practitioner who now uses his experience to help other companies create success. His company provides mentoring and consulting for business owners, CEOs, and their growing businesses. He is an active volunteer in the University of Entrepreneur and the Angel Capital Summit, providing free mentoring to new business owners and helping small businesses obtain access to sources of capital. He is also a public speaker and non-profit board member.

Mr. Johnsen’s focus is on small companies from the pre-revenue startup stage up through $10 million in revenue. He has re-architected business, developed new products and product lines, created marketing and sales programs, negotiated contracts with Fortune 50 companies, and raised millions of dollars in capital for small business. Mr. Johnsen has broad industry expertise including biotech, pharma, healthcare, education, publishing, software, professional services, e-commerce, and transportation.
