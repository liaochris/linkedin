Kristi Saucerman’s Summary

THE AUCTIONFROGS STORY...
AuctionFrogs.com began simply as a way for me to simplify the process of hosting silent auctions. After 12 years of charity event management experience as KLS Events, I believed there was a better way to conduct a fundraising auction using technology tools to extend the auction period and simplify the process. I believed a web solution could not only make fundraising easier, but increase the amount of money raised for the organization. After nearly 2 years of development, AuctionFrogs.com was launched in Aug of '07.

About Me:
My fundraising efforts began as a volunteer for organizations such as the Junior League of Boise and HSCS School. Joe and I were two of the founding parents of Hidden Springs Charter School, where we are actively involved on the Parent Teacher Association Board, School Board and as a volunteer in the classroom. I also joined the JLB in '98 where I've served as a committee member, Fund Raising Chairperson, and as a member of the Board of Directors.

In 1995, I organized an effort to raise money for children with special needs by creating a contest. I had these children draw Christmas pictures that would be used to create holiday cards with a personalized story of each child's "ability" on the back of the card. The company I created, Through Their Eyes, Inc., became nationally recognized and was personally endorsed by nationally syndicated radio talk show host, Dr. Laura Schlessinger.

I launched KLS Events in 1996. I've worked and continue to work as a Special Events Coordinator organizing large festivals, coordinated local concerts and events, and plan/coordinate numerous other fund raising benefit events throughout the Treasure Valley.
