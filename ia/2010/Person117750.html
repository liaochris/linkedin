Keith Stoller’s Summary

Currently a confidential coach to current and prospective business owners and entrepreneurs. Additionally, a part time grant writer, screenwriter and business consultant.

Since 1995 I have been successfully writing grants for organizations and various entities. I have consulted and worked for companies of diverse industries in the field of human resources and management. I have also been working diligently in the entertainment industry developing intellectual property and producing in various formats.

A professionally trained actor, I have appeared in short films and smaller projects. After being referred by a colleague a few years back to get some training in this industry I found I enjoyed it and this has since become a significant part of my life. I continue to grow and develop as an actor and my efforts here, combined with my strong business background, have provided me with the outlets for many other endeavors including Stoller Creative.

My business background is management consulting specializing in human resources, strategic planning, IT integration, workforce planning, executive recruiting, department audits, assisting with organizational start-up ventures, right-sizing, re-visioning, turnarounds, and team building as well as finding money saving exit strategies.
