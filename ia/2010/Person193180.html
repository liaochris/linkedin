Stacy Lin’s Summary

The experiences and skills I obtained while completing my master degree in Integrated Marketing Communication specializing in Public Relation at Golden Gate University gives me confidence about this position. My skill-set in leveraging online marketing areas including Web 2.0/social media (MySpace, facebook, flicker, twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn, yelp, and Digg), SEO, PPC (Google Adwords), blogs (wordpress) enhances consumers choice on where, when and how to get their news.

Recently, I was working as a marketing intern for Global Service Corps, where I obtained how to grow an organization's audiences. This including monitoring daily traffic through Google Analytics, gaining backlinks through press releases, and empowering a good cause through digital interactive media. Moreover I served as a volunteer for VolunteerMatch, where I helped approve NGO's profiles and volunteer opportunities and maintain database through data entry.

Among my projects, I participated in creating an authentic PR plan for Greenworks Utility Billing and an effective brand audit for ReferQuest. These projects enabled me to initiate planning and organizing activities which reflect appropriate analysis and problem-solving techniques and have resulted in responsible decision-making based on pertinent facts and cause and effect relationships.

Also, I worked as a procurement assistant at HP International Taiwan. While there, I learned negotiation skills and developed the confidence needed to succeed in a competitive environment. My inventory management experience taught me a great deal about e-commerce. Moreover I served as a press coordinator at the External Trade Development Council (TAITRA), where I provided website content with continual news updates, and engaged in event co-ordination, press release writing and media relations for the Computex Taipei trade fair.
