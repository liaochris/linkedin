I also authored coding sheets and criteria based on opposition Request for Production. I established and implemented review protocols for contract attorneys.     I staffed projects with personal network of top-tier contract attorneys. I would participate in litigation strategy meetings, functioning as the primary liaison between review team and associates and partners.
