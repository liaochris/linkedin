Lorie Parch’s Summary

Over 19 years working in media (online & print), my chief specialty is that I know how to communicate with consumers -- and women in particular - to empower them to make better decisions about their lives and especially about their health and wellness.

My strengths are as a strategist (content, community, credibility) and communicator with clients and colleagues, as well as a teacher and mentor.

There's nothing I love better than brainstorming possibilities, especially in this age of lightning-speed growth in health/wellness, online, and new and yet-to-be-realized platforms for reaching people and connecting with one another. I'd particularly love to connect with those exploring Health 2.0, mobile health, social media and reaching women online and across media.
