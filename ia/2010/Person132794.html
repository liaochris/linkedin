Marilyn Karp’s Summary

More than 20 years experience guiding my clients through the process of integrating technology into their business.

The greatest challenge was implementing the main order processing system for a brokerage that represented 25% of the market's volume. The standard for this technology was "guaranteed delivery". You don't tell a trader you lost his order.

At the major financial institution the challenge was implementing a trading system that used what was then the very latest fax technology. There the challenge was negotiating the truce between the business units and IT organization so that the project could be completed, which it was.

I've worked on many projects in many different capacities. I've been a project manager, business analyst, systems analyst, testing manager, trainer and implementation manager.

What I bring to the table is experience and what I leave behind is knowledge on how you get a project done. I am able to look at a project, see what isn't getting done, or needs support and go and do it. Sometimes what is required is the development of a process and then showing the business how the technology will support the process.
