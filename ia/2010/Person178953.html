Russell Roesner’s Summary

Mr. Roesner is an accomplished senior executive with 15 years of progressive Real Estate, Finance, and Investor Relations consulting experience. His focus is real estate debt placement through hedge funds, broker dealers, PE firms, banks, and RIA's for private placements. He is presently focused on providing debt, equity, and product to experienced buyers of distressed real estate nationwide. He is proficient in all aspects of real estate & financial consulting services: business development, investor relations, mortgage origination, mortgage banking and credit facility relationships, private offerings, syndications, & the coaching and mentoring staff.

Experience
Provided product development analysis and applied findings to restructure new wholesale mortgage programs and rewrote two existing Private Placement Debt Offerings for Broker Dealers, RIA’s, and accredited investors. Acquired over $100 million in investor capital. Has originated and funded over $1 billion in real estate debt over his career.

Trained senior management team to package and sell investments through direct marketing programs and speaking engagements at national conferences to registered broker dealers, registered investment advisors, pension plans, private equity firms, insurance companies, and accredited investors. Over 400 new investors and $200,000,000 were obtained through this process.


Implemented and applied project costing analysis for a Real Estate investment firm doing large scale condominium conversions. Researched potential profitable markets, assisted manager in negotiating offers, showed firm how to acquire institutional, mezzanine, and equity debt for purchases. Hired and managed contractors, created budgets, and worked onsite with the project manager.

Advised Manager of a Real Estate debt fund and mortgage broker on fundraising, increasing profitability, and leveraging current relationships with customers and internal departments within the company.
