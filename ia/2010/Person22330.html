  • Executive Vice President

    NMP Media Corp. - Publishers of National Mortgage Professional Magazine

    (Financial Services industry)

    February 2009 — Present (2 years )

    After months of research, we have decided to launch a new monthly resource—National Mortgage Professional Magazine: “Your source for the latest on originations, settlement and servicing”—that will connect the mortgage professional community under one media format. We have assembled an exceptional team of monthly contributors, all with meaningful expertise in their respectful disciplines, to the most up-to-date news, insight and advice for today’s mortgage professional. In addition, the Magazine is defined by elements of editorial interactivity, as we focus on generating an ongoing dialogue of collaboration with our readers through user-generated content and different forms of social networking. With this in mind, we have developed National Mortgage Professional Magazine together with our continuously updated Web site, NationalMortgageProfessionalMagazine.com (to be launched May 2009), to offer our readers the freshest news and analysis 24/7, and the opportunity to have an active voice and reader-generated feedback on the issues impacting you, the mortgage professional.
    The result will keep you better-informed and engaged in the many changes impacting the mortgage profession, no matter what your mortgage discipline, and be better-prepared to build a more responsive, profitable and compliant business model.

    With this new format, we are able to offer powerful low cost print, online and in-person marketing programs to help companies reach mortgage professionals.
