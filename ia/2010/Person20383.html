Bart Heres’s Summary

Bart currently focuses on growing the Career Athletes Community with Corporate Members & Athletic Department / Athletic Development Organization Members.

Bart previously was with Management Recruiters International / MRINetwork for 16 years as a Franchise Owner, a Corporate Office GM, National Supply Chain Practice Leader, and as VP of one of MRI's most successful franchises which was named MRI’s Business Unit of the Year for three consecutive years. Bart received numerous Regional, National, and International awards of recognition and is a member of MRI’s prestigious Hall of Fame. Further, he has earned Certified Senior Account Manager (CSAM) status through professional accomplishment and demonstrated technical competency. Bart is a frequent speaker to Professional and Academic Associations on the topic of Career Development. These groups have included the Warehouse Education Research Council (WERC), Logistics Students from Pennsylvania State University & Georgia Southern University, and the Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals (CSCMP). He is an active member of CSCMP, WERC, IIE, and APICS.

Prior to his career in Executive Search, Bart enjoyed 10 years of success in the area of Logistics and Operations Management. Upon completion of his MBA, he held Operational Leadership positions with a former division of Genuine Parts and with PepsiCo.
