Bob Radock’s Summary

For years, I have had a passion for building and producing exciting and dynamic shows for worldwide audiences.
After thirty extraordinary years with the Walt Disney Company, I am now working independently as a consultant serving in a leadership capacity. I have produced live shows, special events, music festivals, concert series, press events and television shows and additionally designing performance based development programs that train future artists in all disciplines. As a casting director, toured 12 cities over 16 years, hiring 2000 performers and contracted and hosted more than 300 stars and celebrities. As Festival Manager, I contracted and designed residencies for 100+ international companies from 29 countries. As a Program Director, I produced a Symphony Orchestra Series over 13 years, featuring sixty contraced stars. I have extensive resources and assets in the performance and production areas and established relationships with top artists, producers, and presenters. I have served on a number of nationally recognized arts organizations and boards.

Bob Radock’s Specialties:

Extensive experience and resources in events, sales, marketing, public/artist relations, media, publicity, production, industry leaders, talent relations, producers and associations. I have worked with non-profits as a volunteer and served on boards and arts based organizations for years.
