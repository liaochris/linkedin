Jim Walent’s Summary

Jim Walent is responsible to lead and execute the Hotel Division’s Capital Improvement program. This effort is closely coordinated with the Regional Directors and Hotel General Managers to insure that projects are executed in an effective manner. Areas of focus include:

 Standardized room renovation programs
 Technology enhancements

 Product improvement planning and execution including brand conversions

 Cash flow management and accountability
 Supervision of project managers and contractors
 Develop and mange FF&E procurement, logistics management and installation processes
 Building energy efficiency and sustainability initiatives
