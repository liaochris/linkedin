Christopher Dore’s Summary

Dr. Christopher Dore is an archaeologist and a leader in the heritage industry. He has a record of success using scientific data to achieve creative solutions for clients in government, academia, and the private sector who face difficult research and management challenges. Dr. Dore has a rare combination of both scientific and business credentials. He has successfully led micro-businesses, technical teams, corporate divisions, and entire companies by taking business concepts and practices and applying them to heritage management.
