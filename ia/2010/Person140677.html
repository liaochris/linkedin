Melissa Donovan’s Summary

Writing is the single most powerful form of communication and the web is the single most powerful communication tool.

The written word has longevity. It has the capacity to educate, inform, sell, and entertain. Whether destined for internet, print, or digital publication — writing must be clear and compelling. A writer’s job, then, is to produce words that shine.

The web is universally accessible and allows people and businesses to forge connections and build new relationships. For businesses, an effective web presence is essential for success.

I have a passion for helping writers succeed. My services for writers are backed up by a BA in English with a concentration in creative writing and years of experience as a hard-working writer:

* Proofreading and Editing
* Professional Critiques
* Writing Coach

My creative writing blog was recently recognized by Writer's Digest as one of the "101 Best Websites for Writers."

As a web content specialist, I work to help small businesses and independent entrepreneurs succeed online. Combining my writing skills with my internet expertise makes me a natural for helping people build a better online presence.

* Website Design
* Copywriting
* Blog Services

In addition, I can provide you with a detailed website review, offering feedback and recommendations for the design, content, and structure of your site.
