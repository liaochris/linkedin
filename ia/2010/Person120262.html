M. Kyle Craig (kcraig@alacrityis.com)’s Summary

Multi-discipline Marketing Maven

My passion is working on new, high-potential ideas with an energetic team.

I have a natural inclination towards research and analysis. Usually, this makes is easy to create a clear roadmap and quickly get returns during the execution phase of a program.

My interest now lies in marketing and management. All future business success will be built around communities, content, and targeted information. Building a tightly focused business is the most direct way to create success. It's also hard to do.

My tech background is in embedded software, RTOS, and semiconductors. I've managed deployments in other areas, including designs based on PHP, Javascript, and Ruby.

In 2004, I completed a successful turnaround of a small media company.

I write for several blogs on topics including music marketing and influence.

My technical skills include programming languages, architecture, and software protocols. I have programmed on ARM, SH, PowerPC, Sparc, and other controllers.

I have managed software projects ranging from small updates to large-scale, global teams. I have developed backend systems, real time operating systems, file systems, graphic packages, and drivers for microprocessors.

My last position had me working in the mobile web space, which also occupies has large potential for the future. I have followed that space closely in the past few years.

I have attended several marketing programs, including Pragmatic Marketing and Chasm training.

I write blogs, website content, case studies, and white papers.The topics of the papers range from embedded systems, semiconductors, operating systems, Linux, flash memory, FPGAs, and file systems.

My current interests are in social media and community aspect of the web.
