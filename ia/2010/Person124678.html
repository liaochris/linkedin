Liana Ling’s Summary

I am committed to changing people's lives by providing the tools, resources and services they need to be successful. This is mainly accomplished through my two businesses: Sales and Business Coaching with Tiger Quest and as a Certified WSI Internet Marketing Consultant and Social Media Expert.

=============================================
WSI has over 1500 offices in 87 countries and strategic alliances with industry leading technology and Internet services companies such as Google, Yahoo, SEMPO, Oracle, IBM, Microsoft, Constant Contact, and more. As a WSI Consultant, I leverage the capabilities of the largest Internet marketing organization in the world, to the advantage of my clients.

I work with businesses, educational institutions, commercial and non-profit organizations to make a difference – a difference in the way they conduct day-to-day activities, a difference in how they serve their target audiences and a difference in the way they accomplish their goals. I also help them make more money; faster, easier and for the long-term! My goal is to help organizations increase efficiencies, reduce expense and enhance profits utilizing the power of the Internet.

My company offers strategic consulting and services that include:
• Internet Consulting and Marketing
• Web design, development and hosting
• Content Marketing and Search Engine Optimization
• Social media optimization and marketing
• Email marketing and management
• E-learning
• E-commerce
• Online application development
• Educational seminars, training workshops and webinars on Internet marketing and social media

We have rolled out some fantastic Web marketing initiatives and because I’m such a stickler for “test and learn”, we have learnt a lot. Benchmark, Test, Measure, Record, Analyze, Improve – that’s the typical marketing project cycle we follow.

See examples of our work at www.WSIWisdomBook.com
