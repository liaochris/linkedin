Ken Evoy’s Summary

SiteSell.com, through its flagship product, Site Build It! ("SBI!"), enables small business people to build profitable online BUSINESSES. This far surpasses the current state-of-the Web, which can be stated as "put up a site or blog and then figure it out."

SBI! accomplishes unmatched success rates by delivering a focused combination of process and tools that sweeps away the tech barriers that block the less tech-savvy, that eliminates the outrageous hype that misleads the gullible, and that filters the ever-increasing noise that distracts those who constantly chase "what's new."

Basically, my job is to grow SiteSell by empowering SBI! owners (SBIers) to build their own profitable online BUSINESSES. We are profitable and have been growing, only because they succeed at unmatched rates. In a world that once again focuses on "eyeballs" and not P&Ls (just like in the first bubble), that makes us decidedly UNcool.

Yes, we generate profits the old-fashioned way, by selling a product that offers more value than what we charge for it. And our clients, Site Build It! owners (SBIers) are more than satisfied clients. They rave. Google us and see for yourself.
