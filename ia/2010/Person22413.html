Bernard Yin’s Summary

Summer-Fall 09 - Current marketing projects range from film to food and I am also now involved with some "mommy blogger" projects. I just worked on some late season trout treks in the Sierra as well. There were some banner days with pods of trout on the move and happy clients.

Flashback: I began promoting underground music events in the early 90s via dialup bulletin board services. After a stint as a guitarist for Medicine (American Recordings), I was invited by Marc Geiger and Steve Rogers to assist in the early phases of the Ultimate Band List. Soon this agenda evolved into that of Artist Direct where I served as the in-house director of grassroots strategies. This involved hands on execution of various agendas as well as managing a team of 30. Properties ranged from promotions for the Blair Witch Project to The Backstreet Boys. With so many resources under one roof, it was possible to fine-tune and subsequently track increased traffic and sales at Artist Direct's numerous merchandising channels. Under the roof of Buzztone, DNA Studios and others. I have since delighted in various film, television, gaming, product and music campaigns while always continuing my passion for music. The last few years have had me recording for film and TV as well as performing throughout the US and Europe in a variety of bands including the legendary Fuzztones.

Bernard Yin’s Specialties:

A sensitivity to the behavioral nuances of online communities, partnering, rallying and eventually parlaying results, integration of technical components into grassroots campaigns, problem solving, patience, persistence, humor.
