Kevin McDonnell’s Summary

Seasoned marketing and business development executive with nearly fifteen years of management experience. GE trained, versed in Six Sigma, and a master at networking with people. Very business savvy with uncanny ability to quickly create and build relationships. Subject matter expert in multiple areas including; Healthcare, Information Technology, Accounting & Finance, Recruiting, Career Development, Training, Miller Heimann, Competitive Selling, and Researching. Possesses excellent critical thinking skills and extremely gifted when it comes to creativity. "Big idea" person with instinctive ability to formulate business plans and strategies and then execute them. Natural "change agent" skilled at motivating, encouraging, and inspiring others. Excellent creative writer and a marketing guru.

Have personally hired and managed well over 300 people and interviewed over 1,000 professionals.
