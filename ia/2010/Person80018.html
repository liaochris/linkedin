Harman Parmar’s Summary

Harman has 5+ years of professional experience in US financial industry, with in-depth knowledge of corporate banking products. Harman extensively work with his clients for their Working Capital Financings like revolvers, term loans and M&A structuring & funding needs along with their trade & derivative requirements. Work with various divisions of ICICI Bank like private equity, venture capital, securities, treasury etc. to meet corporate requirements.

His current responsible include business development including sourcing, originating, structuring & executing various corporate banking deals for existing & new clients. He is also accountable for credit appraisal notes, financial, industry & market risk analysis of transaction and presents proposal notes to sanctioning credit committees. Harman currently manages portfolio of clients with over several hundreds of million of debt across various industries with key clients in Energy, Power & Transport Sector, IT Technology & Service (BPO) sector. Harman additional responsibility include identifying and underwriting buy-side loan syndication deals in leverage finance with primary focus on senior secured debt. Harman was also part of initial start-up team in 2007 for setting up ICICI Bank Ltd. in New York Branch where he was responsible for development of various product and operational policies.

His previously worked in distressed asset management firm with AUM under $1.0 billion where he handled client portfolio of small to mid size commercial and residential loans.

Harman is highly proficient in managing and coordinating among various functional silos across the organization from credit, risk, legal, compliance to operations. He carries entrepreneurial attitude and has the ability to thrive in a collaborative, growing and changing environment.

Harman Parmar’s Specialties:

Corporate Banking, Working Capital Finance, Revolvers, Term Loan, Trade, M&A Financing & Distressed Asset Management with focus on leverage finance and commercial loans.

Experience in various financial modeling tools including DCF, LBO, accretion/dilution for M&A and cash flow modeling.

Key network of mid to large corporate clients from South Asian region with operations across US. Strong network with syndication desks of various big bulge bracket to boutique investment banks.
