Cory Gabel’s Summary

Cory Gabel is a composer, songwriter, performer and producer living in Connecticut, US. He professionally produces music for dance, theater, film and advertising.

Cory also is the owner of Atticus Interactive, an online marketing agency that specializes in developing websites and digital marketing strategies for artists and businesses of all types.

Cory actively performs as a solo artist (solo pianist, singer/songwriter guitar & vocals) and as a member of various groups and ensembles (bass guitar for The Joy Spring Jazz Quartet, and orchestral percussionist for The Farmington Valley Symphony Orchestra).

Atticus Interactive began a decade ago as a means to promote Cory's music and composing career. Since that time it has grown into a full-service online agency, and Cory oversees all creative and developmental aspects of the work.

AI focuses on leveraging the online environment for the promotion and development of music and artistic projects (many of which Cory is producing the music), as well as working directly with advertising agencies and other clients for HTML, web development, Flash and more.
