Dr. Nathalie Beauchamp’s Specialties:

Dr. Nathalie Beauchamp, B.Sc., D.C. is a Ottawa based chiropractor, a certified personal fitness trainer, a professional natural bodybuilder, a wellness consultant and lecturer and the author of the newly published book—Wellness On The Go: Take the Plunge- It’s Your Life!

Dr. Nathalie Beauchamp’s Experience

  • Founder Roadmap to Wellness

    Roadmap to Wellness

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    September 2010 — Present (2 months)

    Your Roadmap to Wellness is a simple, self-paced, on-line coaching program, that gives participants the tools and strategies to live a life-long journey of better health, energy and vitality. The program not only addresses the traditional physical topics of nutrition and exercise, but it also addresses the other two important dimensions-biochemical and psychological, which are often overlooked in other wellness programs. Your Roadmap to Wellness will explore the benefits of subjects such as living an organic lifestyle, implementing exercise, Nutritional Typing™, beauty product safety, evaluating wellness at home and effective goal setting.
