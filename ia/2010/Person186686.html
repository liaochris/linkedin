Seth Miller’s Summary

A specialist in managing consumer service and product businesses.

Strong Financial, Marketing and Operational background with significant process improvement and turn-around experience.

Specialist in organization transformation and change management, organizational efficiency and process management, and human capital management

A talent for identifying and creatively solving complex problems, designing/restructuring critical processes, and fundamentally transforming the way a company operates or creating new go to market strategies.

Lead and delivered large, complex projects that identified, designed, and implemented creative business and technology solutions in: Performance Improvement, Supply Chain Management, Corporate Strategy, and Financial Management.

By personality, I am an arranger, a connector, futuristic and visionary, a learner and analytic. I love managing all of the variables in complex situations, figuring out how all the pieces fit together, and determining the most productive configuration possible. I also enjoy connecting people and ideas to create win/win situations. I love to learn and am drawn to the process of learning. I like to spend time understanding unrelated businesses or ideas because their study might come in handy later in a seemingly unrelated business.

My past employers have represented such complex multi-unit brands as Blockbuster Video, Casual Corner, LensCrafters, Easy Spirit, Castle Dental, Boston Proper, Charles Keath and Mark, Fore & Strike.

My education includes a BA from Tufts University and an MBA with concentration in Consumer Marketing and Service Management from Harvard Business School.
