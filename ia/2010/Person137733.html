Matt Holden’s Summary

My first experience with game design came at eight years old - armed with a stenographer's pad, I set out to write a sequel to the NES title 'Metal Gear'. Hideo Kojima beat me to it, but the passion never died. Two decades later, I am co-owner of Darkened Sky Studios, and the driving force behind our Slage engine, a Java/XML/Flex-based client/server game platform that borders on being its own scripting language.

I am a passionate, driven individual who believes that a day starts when you get up and ends when the job is done - leading me to have lived some significantly longer than 24 hour days! Having been a hobby game developer for years, I truly can say I'm one of those who "would do it for free" - I have!

Matt Holden’s Specialties:

Java [J2SE] (including many APIs such as AWT/Swing, JUnit, Smack, ROME, etc.)

C / C++ (including GDI, MFC, DirectX, OpenGL, OpenAL, fmod, Criterion RenderWare)

Adobe Flex 3, JavaScript, PHP, HTML/CSS, XML

PlayStation 2 assembly language

SQL (Microsoft SQL Server, PostgreSQL, mySQL, hSQLdb)

Outside of the computer world, I'm nationally certified in retail sales, enjoy writing everything from technical manuals to fantasy novels, and speak proficient Spanish.
