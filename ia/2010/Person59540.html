I’m Dr. Craig L. Ziering a hair transplant surgeon and leading authority in hair transplant surgery. I’m fortunate enough to work with the team at Ziering Medical who are among the best in the field of hair restoration. Hair Restoration is my passion and I have been and continue to be active in the industry. I do this through ongoing education and research in the U.S. and abroad.

I received my Doctorate of Osteopathic Medicine from Nova Southeastern University in Miami, Florida and completed my residency at Ohio University’s Grandview Medical Center, Dayton, Ohio. After completing a Fellowship in Hair Restoration with Medical Hair Restoration in Orlando, I became the Associate Medical Director at MHR for a period of ten years before founding my private practice, Ziering Medical.

In 2003, I was recognized by my peers at the International Society for Hair Restoration Surgeons for my Whorl Hair Classification System and was awarded the prestigious, “Most Original New Idea” at that year’s scientific assembly.

As a recognized expert in the art and science of hair transplant surgery, I’m a frequent guest on television and radio programs, including the Today Show and I was even invited to perform the very first hair transplant procedure on ABC’s hit show, Extreme Makeover.

I’ve worked with entertainment and sports celebrities, executives and individuals across the country and I consider myself privileged to be a part of changing people’s lives, personally and professionally. There is no better reward for myself and my team than to watch individuals regain their confidence as they run their fingers through their new hair.
