    (Public Company; INTC; Semiconductors industry)

    October 1998 — October 2005 (7 years 1 month)

Selected to be a member of a task force created to expedite the conversion of 4 inch wafer production lines to 6 inch. Selected to be a core member of a PROMIS manufacturing execution system (MES) implementation team.
