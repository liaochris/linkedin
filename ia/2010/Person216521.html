William Riley’s Summary

William Riley oversees marketing and international hardlines sales at SGS US Testing Company, a leading provider of consumer product testing, auditing and verification services for U.S. retailers and manufacturers. His responsibilities include business development, marketing management, and management of key global accounts.

Mr. Riley’s work with SGS draws on a diverse global business background that has spanned industries ranging from aluminum to consumer packaged goods. Immediately before joining SGS in 2006, he served as General Manager, International, for The Enesco Group, a producer of fine gifts and accessories based in Itasca, Illinois. There he was responsible for developing global business strategies for all of Enesco’s licensed and internally developed lines as well as establishing retail and distribution relationships with key global customers.

From 2000 to 2003, Mr. Riley served as Director, Global Business Development, at Alcoa Consumer Products with responsibility for driving that company’s $70 million consumer products businesses in over 80 international markets. From 1989 to 1998, before Alcoa merged with Reynolds Metals Company, he held a variety of positions at Reynolds culminating in his appointment as Manager, Asia/Pacific & Middle East. In that capacity he built the company’s business in Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, the UAE and Israel, as well as laying the groundwork for launching Reynolds in the China market.

Mr. Riley holds both Master of Advertising and Bachelor of Journalism degrees from the University of Illinois.
