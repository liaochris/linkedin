Ruth Stokes’s Summary

Reliable and creative freelance writer. Experience writing in both print and online to tight deadlines. Portfolio of published work includes John Brown Media magazines, Think Publishing, River Publishing, Result Customer Communications, Big Issue, The Guardian Guide, Bad Idea, Matador Travel, Foodtripper.com and Buzz magazine. Subbing experience on Haymarket's Horticulture Week, Morrison's Health and Beauty and south west newspaper The Sunday Independent.

Competent in Indesign, Quark Express and Microsoft Office. Teeline Shorthand to 100 words per minute. Knowledgeable in media law. Postgraduate Diploma in Magazine Journalism from Cardiff University.

Ruth Stokes’s Specialties:

Creative copy, efficient turnaround of articles, feature ideas, teamwork, meeting client objectives for customer publishing, travel, business, lifestyle
