Nate Rimmel’s Summary

Nathan "N8Beats" Rimmel is a certified recording engineer, music producer and entertainment business entrepreneur residing in Hollywood, California. Nate Beats has been hired to produce music for major corporations such as Adidas and Sun Micro Systems. He has created an array of urban production for recording artists, including E-40, Bosko, and Hip Hop magazine The Source's "Unsigned Hype". Nate Beats has worked in-studio with multi-platinum producer Narda Michael Walden, Riza of Wu-Tang, engineer Steve Miller and Paul Olkenfold. His past experience includes the American Mechanical Rights Agency and Urbintra Entertainment Media, and has studied under American Idol judge Simon Cowell. Nate has graduated from the Los Angeles Recording School (Record Engineering) as well as the Musicians Institute (Music Business). Currently Nate Beats currently a Board Member for Non-Profit Organization Youth Infusion, Audio/Visual Producer and Production Director at Ockhams Edge, and overseeing his urban production company, Make-Your-Hit Production.
