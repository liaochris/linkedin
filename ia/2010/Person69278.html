Francesco Cetraro’s Summary

I am a fast-learning, dynamic person who enjoys new challenges and I am always looking for new opportunities to put my experience and enthusiasm to good use.

Having worked in different positions for a major European Domain Registrar, I have developed extensive knowledge of the domain industry and the topics of internet governance, and my top fields of expertise are ccTLDs and Digital Brand Management Services.

I recently joined the DotMobi team as Manager of Business Development and Sales, and will be now focusing my efforts on the development of a wider, richer and more accessible Mobile Internet all over the world.

My academic background is in Political Science and International Relations, which together with a sound interest in new technologies, geography and development studies give me a peculiar perspective on how to approach and solve problems.

Furthermore, my strong technical background is an asset that allows me to work efficiently also in a highly technical environment.

I am a strong advocate of open source software as an instrument to overcome digital divide and to allow a more equal distribution of resources among the people of the world. I see my position as an opportunity to contribute to a fairer development of the internet.
