Ray De Lorenzi’s Summary

I am currently the Communications Director at the American Association for Justice, formerly the Association of Trial Lawyers of America and the world's largest trial bar. I have worked directly with high-profile experts and spokespeople, securing media placements in top U.S. media markets. Additionally, I have extensive experience with rapid and crisis communications, transferable to an array of high-pressure situations. I also serve as an on-the-record spokesperson for AAJ, frequently interviewed for print and broadcast outlets. I manage a communications and research staff that works to highlight AAJ's key issues and initiatives to a nationwide audience.
