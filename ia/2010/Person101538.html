John Appler’s Summary

Over 25 years of experience in general management P&L roles at large companies and in sales and marketing roles at start-ups. Track record of success bringing the largest brands and market leaders to start-ups and emerging companies. Helped create many successful exits for investors.

John Appler’s Specialties:

Technology start-up leadership; online sales and business development expertise; internet, wireless, cable and telco industry experience

John Appler’s Experience

  • President

    River Rock LLC

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    River Rock is a California corporation founded in 2001 which provides commercial website development, business development and online advertising sales.

    Specialties include development of social commerce applications and mobile interactive web sites.

    Mobile sites are deployed via patented technology which allows iPhones, Blackberries and over 5,000 different mobile device types to be served from a single platform.

    Experience includes successful provision of online advertising sales for network of entertainment sites with a total audience of 6 million unique visitors generating 320 million monthly page views.

    Advertisers acquired included Proctor & Gamble, 20th Century Fox, Sony Pictures, Lionsgate Entertainment, Real Networks, Overture Films and MTV Networks.

  • VP Sales

    Sendori, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2007 — August 2008 (1 year 7 months)

    Employee number four and first salesperson at Sendori, The Marketplace for Direct Navigation Search, where advertisers bid via programmatic interfaces to have targeted domain visitors redirected to their web sites. Patent-pending technology provides an economical means by which brand advertisers can acquire a massive new audience of web searchers. Over 50,000 advertisers (including 20 of the largest online) and a majority of the worlds largest domain name holding companies utilize the Sendori marketplace.
