Rick Wilkerson’s Summary

Diversified experience in marketing, advertising and media since 1984. Worked at large agencies (Leo Burnett - Chicago and McCann-Erickson -Indianapolis) and owned small agency in Indianapolis. Co-founded groundbreaking weekly web news magazine (Indianapolis Eye). Last position was an 18 month run at Marketing Informatics - Indianapolis, a direct mail marketing company.

Rick Wilkerson’s Specialties:

Experienced account manager and media planner-buyer. Web savvy. Direct marketing strategist. Blogger. Media and web entrepreneur. Old school experience merged with desire and ability to stay current.
