Terry Hanold’s Summary

Experienced software executive and general manager, with over 15 years experience at Amazon.com, Microsoft, and several software start-ups. Has run global businesses, with nine-figure P&Ls, and launched business lines in Asia, Europe, and North America. Specific domain expertise in payments, enterprise software, and consumer applications. Part of executive or management teams of 3 start-ups in the distributed systems/service oriented-architecture and consumer web application spaces.

Terry Hanold’s Specialties:

Background starting new businesses, either with start-ups, or new business units in larger companies (e.g. Amazon, Microsoft). General management focus, with responsibilities for engineering, product management, sales, marketing, etc. Particular interest in international business.

Terry Hanold’s Experience

  • Vice President and General Manager, Payments

    Amazon.com

    (Public Company; AMZN; Internet industry)

    June 2005 — Present (4 years 10 months)

    Currently, I run Amazon's world-wide Credit and Gift Cards businesses, including our consumer and corporate credit programs. Taken as a whole, these businesses are focused on developing new payment methods, functionality, and features with an eye to improving how we relate to our wallets and the ways that we give and receive money. My teams design the features and benefits of the Amazon payment methods; establish partnerships to promote their use and increase their value; and build the customer-facing and back-end systems required to support the business. We are early adopters of Web services and Web 2.0 technologies, and are part of Amazon's Web services-based e-commerce platform.

    Previously, I worked on Amazon's Web services initiative. AMZN is launching new businesses as an on-demand e-commerce and internet technology provider. My team was responsible for building out the technical infrastructure and developer/partner ecosystem around this initiative.
