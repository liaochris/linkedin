M&A + MI + BI + Strategy + SEO + SMO + ORM/PR + eCRM + SDLC + eSWOT = Digital Strategic Services

Most organizations spend ridiculous amounts of time generating and disseminating metrics that are stupidly anecdotal. It's because that's what everyone else does. Why is a weekly dashboard and a monthly, 50-slide deck the standard? How about NOT simply following the herd? How about having your M&A peeps do meaningful, strategic research and leave you with a nice, concise list of 5 things you should probably look into for increasing awareness, engagement, lead generation, sales, conversion, etc? If you can't get strategic intelligence condensed into 5 bullet points on a powerpoint slide - then you're wasting a lot of time and money.

Information without action is simply overhead.
