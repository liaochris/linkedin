Chiropractor Eduard Burt, D.C., MUAC’s Summary

Chiropractor Chiropractic

My focus is to help patients with pain reduction, with the intention of reducing the need for pain medication. Additionally, I work to improve mobility and overall range of motion and to inhance the quality of life of each of my patients. By increasing mobility and reducing pain through chiropractic, physical therapy modalities and massage therapy, I allow the body to reach its maximum potential.

Dr. Burt has extensive training and clinical experience in treating patients with neuromuscular and skeletal conditions. My goal is to tell as many people as possible about chiropractic success stories all over the world via my blog.

For more info please visit my blog www.burtchiropractic.com
