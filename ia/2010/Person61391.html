Eben Thurston - SEO SEM’s Summary

Over 10 years experience in internet and search marketing, including SEO, SEM, PPC (Google, Yahoo, Bing, etc), email marketing, e-commerce, web analytics and lead generation. I have worked for major media companies, startups, and retailers and have consulted for small businesses including commerce and service companies.

In 2009 I founded Spring Marketing Strategies, LLC. Spring is a marketing firm focused on helping small and medium sized business effectively reach targeted consumers on the web and in search engines.

My parents have run a custom construction firm in Manchester VT since 1979. I have many years experience understanding the needs and challenges of small and local service companies.
