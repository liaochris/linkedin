Jo Brown’s Summary

With more than 20 years of experience, I'm happy to be constantly building on my skills and learning new ones. I specialize in content development-- -nearly all phases, across a wide range of media.

My talents include writing and editing for Web and print, contributing to and managing social media initiatives, and producing and developing Web and video content. I have had equal success at both managing teams and contributing individually, and I specialize in working efficiently and effectively with key stakeholders and contingencies within organizations.

I've enjoyed working for companies both tiny and huge, and have successfully lead content teams of all types and sizes.

Jo Brown’s Specialties:

Content development for online media. My current focus is on helping users accomplish their goals on the Web by crafting clear, concise copy and telling compelling stories. I'm currently immersed in social media both as a practitioner and strategist.
