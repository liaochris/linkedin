Jean Touboul’s Summary

Internet marketing and media expert with a track record of successes in cutting edge technologies that changed the face of online marketing and redefined some aspects of digital advertising.

My expertise is in mixing the right amount of on and off-line marketing channels to maximize the performance of a campaign and enhance a customer's experience within any vertical.

I was the key contributor to the success of Fortune 500 companies such as The Walt Disney Co. and Time/Warner as well as online marketing pioneers like ExecFocus.Net and Commission Junction (CJ.COM), the world’s premier affiliate marketing network.

Author of “Plan Your Internet Success”, a step by step interactive e-book designed to guide Web Entrepreneurs succeed on the Internet.

For more information on my skill-set, check out my presentation at http://jtouboul.com/images/J_Touboul_Presentation_09-1.pdf
