Charles Stuart’s Summary

A professional, versatile and solutions focused Supply Chain Management & Procurement Senior Manager with 25+ years experience in lead roles across the whole Supply Chain in multiple categories, in the main within ‘blue chip’ companies. A Prince2 qualified project manager, he has developed & led organisational change within supply chain organisations locally & globally. Has a reputation for delivering improved performance & reduced costs. A systematic, logical & clear thinking achiever possessing excellent interpersonal & analytical skills.

My objective is to utilise these skills and experience to develop to my Interim business focusing on providing procurement and supply chain solutions to clients across both private and public sectors
