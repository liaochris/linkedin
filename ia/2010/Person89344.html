Janette Levey Frisch’s Summary

"Interactive in-house counsel" to companies and clients, both individually and in-house. I am a versatile in-house attorney with 19 years' experience. A resourceful litigator, astute negotiator and innovative problem solver I quickly establish rapport with clients and adversaries, management and employees at all organizational levels, bridging the gap between "Legal" and "everyone else" in the organization, achieving consistently favorable outcomes. I genuinely enjoy helping companies and clients find creative solutions for meeting their goals and challenges while protecting their legal interests. More than that, I strive to be the attorney that they are comfortable approaching with questions and concerns before they become full-fledged problems.

My expertise lies in reducing liability, assessing risk, facilitating implementation of effective cost controls and increasing staff productivity by instituting sound human resources and operational policies.

In my current position as the In-House Corporate Attorney for Joule, Inc., located in Central New Jersey, I provide comprehensive legal represenation and support as sole in-house counsel to a staffing company with five subsidiaries throughout the East Coast. I vigilantly protect the company's interests in a broad range of legal matters, including without limitation contracts, employment law, regulatory compliance, risk management and records management.
