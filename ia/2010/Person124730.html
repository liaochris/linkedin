Liberation Iannillo’s Summary

I am Liberation Iannillo, a website designer and new media consultant based in New York City. For over nine years I have been developing websites which have a strong focus on user experience, search engine optimization, and are supported by additional online marketing efforts such as blogs and social networking strategies.

I work with authors, artists, publicists, and other creative entrepreneurs who want to promote their projects online and attract new creative opportunities. People work with me because they can depend on me. Working one on one with my clients, I create websites that convey their purpose for being in business, providing a strong visual atmosphere without overshadowing their work.
