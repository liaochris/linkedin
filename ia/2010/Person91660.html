My Story

I've been a mortgage professional for close to 9 years, a real estate investor for almost 7 years, and a credit education instructor for the last 5. The last few years were challenging due to the fact I went through foreclosure, bankruptcy, loan modification and some other roller coaster types of events. At the beginning of the year, due to my family having tough economic times, I moved my whole family in with me to help them. So in addition to me and my two dogs, my house now also includes my mother, sister, both nieces and their two dogs. That's right, a full house and I manage 4 dogs. What's great is my house is now always clean, I always have really good leftovers when I get home from work, and it has brought my family and I alot closer!

This journey has taught me many things about life, how fortunate I am to have my health, my family, how to get through these times, and how to teach what I learned to help others.

If you, a family member, friend, neighbor, co-worker, client or anyone else you know who is struggling with their finances, real estate, or other debt or they are about to, don't keep me a secret. Tell them you know someone who has been through it and will give them suggestions and a strategy to get through their journey.
