Scott G Ames, SQA CMMI Agile Process Improvement’s Summary

Effective IT, software quality, and process improvement specialist with extensive background in the full Software Development Life Cycle.

Created the Test Results Analysis Methodology (TRAM) for determining when software is ready for release. It enhances Test Driven Development and Scrum by adding Metrics, Risk Analysis, Estimation, and Management. No project using TRAM has ever had a late release. It decreases time-to-market and development costs while increasing quality and customer satisfaction. The British Standards Institute accepted it as a Best Practice.
