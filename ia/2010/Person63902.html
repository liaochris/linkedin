Ellen DePasquale’s Summary

ef·fi·cient - productive without waste

The Value of Time

How much is your time worth? How well are you spending your time?

Time is precious. We only get to spend it once and it is gone. There is no "going back" in time.

It's time to reclaim YOUR time!

Reseach has shown that most of us are frustrated an average of 43% of the time we spend at our computer. That's wasting your time. But we all know that if you are in business, you need to spend SOME time at your computer. I say it's time you spend less time at your computer, and more time doing the things YOU want to do.

Over the past 17 years (a good piece of time) I have developed a new approach to software training – short goal-based sessions that include 2-3 specific functions that result in just what you need to know to get your job done. If you have ever been to a long, all-day software training session, then you probably experienced "information overload". My short, on-point training sessions are "information focused" so you remember what you learned and can actually use it to increase productivity. Time well spent.

What do you want to be doing with your time?

Attracting new business, strengthening a relationship with a customer, creating a dynamic marketing campaign, or maybe leaving the office on time so you can be home for dinner!

My software training sessions are always live, but not always in person. You can either hire me to sit right next to you, or you can attend one of my many interactive webinars. The choice is yours and the time is now.
