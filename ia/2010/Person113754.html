Kelly Waters (formerly Lovell)’s Summary

Kelly A. Waters
Managing Director

Waters Group Global is an international consultancy generating measurable gains for small and medium sized companies.

Creator of the GAP model of economic growth and business expansion which focuses highly specialized resources to GROW young companies, ATTRACT external investment, and PREPARE companies and communities for the impacts of growth and expansion. Using this model, the Capital Region of New York State attracted more than $10 billion in new business expansion, most recently a $3.5 billion investment by semiconductor manufacturing giant AMD.

Frequent national speaker on the topic of business growth and expansion. Recipient of the American Marketing Association, Capital Region Chapter, Marketer of the Year award and the Albany Chamber of Commerce Woman of Excellence award. Selected as a Woman of Influence by the Capital Region Business Review, a publication of American City Business Journals. Kelly serves on a number of Corporate Boards of Directors and advisory committees.
