James Hardie’s Summary

At James Hardie, we create innovative siding products that increase the beauty, value, safety and durability of your home. Enjoy the low maintenance that comes with James Hardie ColorPlus® Technology® with our proprietary baked-on color, and feel safe knowing that our siding products are resistant to just about anything Mother Nature can throw at them.

-Engineered for Climate™
-Protecting over 4 million homes nationwide
-Backed by a $100MM of research and development
-30 year non-prorated, transferable, limited warranty on lap, shingle, and panel products
-15-year limited finish warranty on James Hardie® Siding with Color Plus® Technology

James Hardie® Siding is Made of Fiber Cement
James Hardie Siding is made from all natural, non-toxic, renewable materials that make for a highly durable alternative to vinyl and wood siding products. James Hardie fiber cement is composed of sand, water, cellulose wood fibers and cement.
