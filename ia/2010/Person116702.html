Kimberly Koehly’s Summary

Kimberly's known for being a driven leader who can rally the troops internally to bring on new clients. She's excited to be the person responsible for going out and finding the right types of new clients to bring on board as EmpowHER continues to grow and is never afraid to offer assistance and is proactive at bringing solutions to clients and prospective clients. She's often referred to as being on-point, direct, persistent and fun to be around (Kimberly pulls off these conflicting nuances flawlessly). She's an athlete, and loves golfing, hiking and generally staying fit. She also loves to travel and has been to Australia, Thailand, Scotland, most of Europe and far too many fantastic places to name in the U.S.

When she's not working or traveling (so, what, like two hours a week?), you can find her at home. She admits she's not great at relaxing, so often times she ends up being highly productive around the house; redecorating, reorganizing or even just catching up on a stack of magazines or a good book. Whatever she's doing, she loves spending as much time as possible with her better half, Chris, and most recently, can't stop talking about her nieces (Josie, 4; Naomi, 1) and nephew (Gabe, 2.5), or her two darling Dachshunds, Brandy and Fancy.
