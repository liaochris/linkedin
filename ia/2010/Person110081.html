Kamal Jain’s Summary

With nearly 20 years of operational experience in both technology and business, I thrive under the pressure of taking an organization and infrastructure from start-up mode to maturity or through times of rapid growth and change.

I also excel at turning around teams and functions which are not meeting business requirements, and working with customers to ensure their success by being their advocate, often participating in the actual delivery of what they want.

My preferred role is developing operational practices and teams to bring order to chaos or develop new areas, both of which make the organization more efficient and nimble. In the end, it's all about "wowing" the customer, not just satisfying them, and as a result customer service and support are also areas in which I am strong.

I am often described as a "player-coach" who is both the lead hands-on technical resource and the manager/mentor for the group.

Kamal Jain’s Specialties:

Bringing business requirements and technology solutions together as appropriate, taking teams to the next level, delighting customers, getting things done on time and under budget, pursuing excellence in all things, and generally thinking outside the box.

I pride myself on being well-versed in many things; not a "Jack of all trades", but rather a "Master of some"; my employers and clients over the years have come to appreciate being able to set me to task on a wide variety of challenges.
