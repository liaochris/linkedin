Gemma Graziani Vesey’s Summary

Way before being "green" was a fashionable trend, my activities reflected a passion for a broad spectrum of environmental topics. Whether redesigning a US government building with eco materials, organizing an event for Al Gore’s global warming documentary, directing a video on alternative energy for IKEA or making editorial choices & social media branding for Babelgum's Nature Channel, I have always been committed to ecology at large.

Architecture, design, visual media, marketing and communication, have all been means to express my focus on Sustainable Development. At a micro scale, making a firm greener while achieving financial benefits and/or strengthening its brand image, is my main goal. At a macro level, shaping human operations into more sustainable activities via effective communication as well as practical solutions is my ultimate objective. Please find a more detailed presentation of my fields of expertise as well as training and education in the various sections below.
