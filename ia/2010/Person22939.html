Betsy Olwine’s Summary

My 16 year career (9 hotels & 13 positions) with Hyatt Hotels & Resorts has given me the experience to proactively sell Hyatt as a company for others looking to create an exciting experience in their daily lives. I started in Housekeeping at the hotel level and ended as Director of Sales before entering the Corporate Office career path. My goal is to network and build relationships with as many people in the hospitality industry as possible. The position I currently hold requires me to hire outside (Hyatt) sales talent to fill our open positions. I also help some of our hotels in a "task force" manner as Director of Sales & Marketing.
