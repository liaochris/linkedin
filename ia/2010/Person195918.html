Steve Patti’s Summary

Spent first eight years of career in corporate roles acquiring a comprehensive understanding of product development, marketing/sales and operations.

Leveraged that experience in 1996 to launch an international marketing firm that I grew to $20 million in revenues by helping global Fortune 500 brands increase sales across industries with a focus on Technology (software, hardware, wireless/telecom) and Financial Services (banking, mortgage, credit cards). Gained critical insights and hands-on marketing experience helping both B2C and B2B brands improve their strategy, execution & metrics to deliver on brand or sales results.

Spun off a software marketing and distribution company dedicated to supporting large OEMs such as Dell in North America & Europe.

Executive leadership experience includes navigating M&A opportunities, managing international business/staff, re-inventing USP in response to threats (competitors, dot-com bust, 9/11, etc.), and delivering superior financial results (double-digit EBITDA, $1MM revenue/employee).

Steve Patti’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy, customer insights, marketing metrics, complex marketing operations, sales / business development, turnarounds, and start-ups.
