Mario Castaneda’s Summary

Mario has held senior management marketing, strategic, sales and business development positions at Fortune 500 and small companies including start-ups. He has participated in and led multiple successful product and service solutions launches in the B2B and consumer spaces.

He also has consulted in marketing planning, implementation and innovation with Fortune 500 clients. His areas of expertise include medical devices, aerospace, information technology, pharmaceutical and retail sectors.

Mario has been a featured speaker at multiple conferences including the Biocom CEO Summit and the Marcus Evans Chief Marketing Officer Summit.

He has written white papers and co-authored an article titled "Building Marketing Excellence" with the President and Chairman of StratX, Jean-Claude Larreche, that appears in "The 2008 Pfeiffer Annual Leadership Development Book" published by Pfeiffer.
