Mary Gilbert’s Summary

An entrepreneurial global marketing, sales, and operations leader with over 20 years experience driving cross-discipline teams including brand advertising, CRM programs, e-channel marketing, events, direct marketing, audience, business and channel development. Mary consistently delivers exceptional results both from a financial as well as a business outcomes perspective. Mary's career has been focused on helping agencies and clients build integrated marketing, database and digital marketing capabilities and has served leading brands such as: AOL, Cisco, 3Com, Microsoft, Sun, Oracle, Wells Fargo, and Mindjet.

Mary was a founder of Miller/Huber Relationship Marketing, a shop that became a West Coast leader in online technology marketing, and a key leader in the exceptional growth of the McCann Worldgroup/MRM San Francisco office.

As Director of Client Services at MRM, Mary helped take the San Francisco office from a project-based creative boutique to a multidimensional integrated agency with unrivaled strategic and analytic capabilities on the West Coast. When Microsoft was a project-based client Mary helped solidify key relationships and drove campaign successes that strengthened the MRM story for the AOR pitch. She was a catalyst for the agency's growth in strategy, data and analysis driving major increases in revenue from this discipline year over year and has led the development of critical tools and processes that have been recognized by Microsoft as best practices.

Most recently, Mary led Mindjet's brand turn-around, building a stable, accountable marketing operation that delivered the best quarter in the history of the company during the recent economic down turn.

Today, Mary makes her visionary, yet practical skills available to a wide variety of businesses in emerging markets such as Clean Tech and NFP businesses. She aims to accelerate global change through harmonious, accountable marketing practices.

Mary Gilbert’s Specialties:

Integrated communications, brand development, Database marketing, digital marketing, executive leadership, emerging technologies, software, financial services, consumer technologies, account management, strategic planning, business development.
