Nico Debackere’s Summary

I've been active in software testing & development well over a decade. In 1996, I started programming in VBA at The Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation and after that in C on a Tuxedo platform at Belgacom. Because of this programming experience, I was very interested in application performance. Since mid 2001 untill end 2009 I worked for ps_testware and was focussed mainly on performance testing and tuning of java (and .net applications) running on iPlanet-eGate/Oracle, Websphere-iSeries/DB2. Particularly (but not only) on the WebSphere part, I have tuning experience due to the 2 years+ at Fortis Bank and a contribution to a tuning excersice at IBM Hursley. Next to that I helped tuning another java app running on iPlanet-eGate configuration at Axa for almost 2 years and was tuning a Siebel 8 config for several weeks recently.

Problem analysis and profiling using HP Diagnostics (mainly HP Probe/Profiler - free up to 5 threads:) and diagnose large and small application performance problems. Application instrumentation.. Many other interesting technical projects: WAN optimisation and SSL acceleration using RiOS - Riverbed Steelheads, securing webservices using certificates, ...

Coaching of junior and medior testers.
Successfully gave Performance Testing & LoadRunner trainings in Belgium and abroad.

I have a LoadRunner CPS (Certified Product Specialist LoadRunner from HP, formerly Mercury Interactive), mainly worked with Web, Citrix_ICA, Web Services, Siebel-Web and Winsock protocol and also have extensive experience (2y) with IBM Rational Robot (VU).

Goals: building and sharing technical expertise, making client happy by analyzing and resolving problems/

.
