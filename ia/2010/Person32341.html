Carl Griffith’s Summary

A thoroughly rounded business professional combining many years of online marketing experience with through-the-line brand communications and design and innovation management.

Highly developed skills in building strong business relationships based on expertise together with excellent interpersonal, written and communication abilities. Very knowledgeable and extremely passionate about Social Media (I organized Singapore’s first ever ‘TweetUp’), emerging online technologies and customer engagement techniques - I have spoken on the subject several times. With Philips I was part of the Consumer Electronics Regional Marketing team charged with online strategy, microsite development, Viral Marketing Campaigns, advertorial initiatives, online performance reporting and SEM strategy. More recently I have worked as a consultant advising companies on Social Media Strategy.

I have managed teams of up to 12 people at various times in my career and I am also a trained Life Coach. I have lived in Singapore for 6 years and am a Singapore Permanent Resident.
