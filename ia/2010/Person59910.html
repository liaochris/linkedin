    Dr. Woodbury's practice felt like a better opportunity and was only 20 minutes from my home in Marin. So in 1999 I purchased Dr. Woodbury's practice.

    After my lease expired, the landlord doubled my rent. My wife Nancy approached building owners in the neighborhood and found 2555 Ocean Avenue, #202, San Francisco.

    2555 Ocean Avenue is a charming, quiet, tranquil space in a two-story medical building with natural stone walls and warm wood paneling.

    I am blessed to be able to practice in a space that promotes health and well-being.
