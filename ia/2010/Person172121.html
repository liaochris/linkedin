Richard Burns’s Summary

Industry expert with unique and vast experience in every aspect of the consumer marketplace. Broad expertise spans multi-national companies, multiple product lines, business turnarounds, startups, and rapid growth. Exceptional ability to position and launch products and technologies into retail with dramatic results.

Classically trained in retail management at Radio Shack (73 franchise stores) and Premium Brand at Sony, progressing to Senior Zone Vice President of a $680M consumer electronics division. Grew Sega from 5% to 55% market share achieving #1 position vs. competitor Nintendo. Built Mattel Media, 3dfx, and Vialta sales organizations from scratch, and grew GT Interactive and Rio into major brand players. Formed C-level contacts at all Top Tier retailers such as Wal-Mart, Target, Best Buy, including distributors, independent reps and brokers for consumer electronics, videogame and PC software, toys, and computer accessories.

Builds and motivates world-class global sales and support organizations (150+ staff). Blends sales and business savvy to balance short-term sales with long-term growth. “Go to” resource and tough decision maker with street-smart sense and superior judgment. Successful in stepping into executive sales roles under intense pressure. Thrives in competitive, adverse, and challenging environments.

Experienced independent retail consultant for new business or turnaround situations with Disney Interactive, Aiwa, M-Audio, BigFoot Networks and BringRR.
