Nikolai Khomenko’s Summary

Coach in Management of Sustainable Innovation for Business, Research, Education and Government organization. Complex cross disciplinary projects development and implementation. Harmonization of large teams that composed by experts in different domain of knowledge and expertise in order to achieve goals of the innovative project.
Author of various OTSM-TRIZ based intellectual techniques dedicated to overcome non typical, complex, interdisciplinary problem situations.
Scientific Director of Educational Program Advanced Master of Innovative Design at INSA Strasbourg Graduate School of Science and Technology, France. The only University based lifelong educational program on Sustainable Innovation, TRIZ and OTSM. Duration of the program 11 weeks and has accreditation of the Conference des Grand Ecoles. 8 weeks out of 11 are dedicated to practical study Classical TRIZ and OTSM. Students who fulfill Final Project successfully can obtain widely recognized diploma of the Conference des Grand Ecoles.
Coach on Sustainable Innovation at European Institute for Energy Research, Germany.
Large Experience in implementation OTSM and Classical TRIZ for Sustainable Innovation at worldwide companies like: Samsung, LG-Electronics, Peugeot-Citroen, EADS, Forecia, Salomon etc.
Domain of projects in the past: Technologies, Business, Management, Research, Education, Economics and Social, System Architect for OTSM-TRIZ based software on Sustainable Innovation.
Mission: developing and implementation practical instruments for Sustainable Innovation.
