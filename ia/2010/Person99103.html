John Johnston’s Summary

As a social media strategist, I am interested in working with forward-thinking companies, organisations and individuals to improve online communications through the use of social software, online communities, and other emerging web and mobile technologies. I also make, speak and write about social media. My own online personal brand is known as 'jjprojects'.

I hold ten years' experience using online social media (long before it was called that). I have been working in the digital marketing field for ten years, starting as an interactive designer and then digital art director, which led into being highly active in new social media. I hold a Master's degree from the University of Sydney, having graduated in 1998.

Over the past three years in particular, I have become a keen advocate for the purposeful use of new social media in Australia. I have become known as early adopter of new social software and web technologies. I love engaging with many different types of people and building strong communities online. I also like meeting people offline. In 2007 I co-organised the first Tweetup (meetup for Twitter users) event in Sydney.

I have extensive experience working with large companies and major brands such as Nokia, Optus, Vodafone, Telstra and Three. I have worked on marketing projects with digital agencies, interactive design studios, multinational teleco companies and NGOs.

I have a particular interest in using my conceptual and strategic skills to benefit projects that involve social and environmental responsibility. During 2009, I headed social media for WWF's highly successful Earth Hour global campaign. Social media results from the event included: the term "Earth Hour" becoming one of the top ten Trending Topics on Twitter for 2009, Earth Hour content becoming a top item on Digg.com, Earth Hour having multiple top viral videos on the web, Earth Hour and WWF online communities numbering 2.5 million fans and friends worldwide.
