Joel Reuter’s Summary

I am a seasoned professional with over 24 years in strategic development, implementation and evaluation of communications, public relations, marketing and branding initiatives. Most of my leadership experience is in a regulated environment, such as the medical devices & diagnostics industries.

I use communications strategy and tools to drive business results. I draw on my extensive experience from publicly traded and private companies, with the common goal of engaging those who can influence an organization’s success.
