Dave Daetz’s Summary

Successful track record of enabling growth and strategic advantage for companies at different stages of evolution and in diverse industries. Excels in both strategy and execution, and getting deals done under intense time pressure. Acquisitions and multi-faceted strategic alliances with public, private, domestic, and international companies. Enlightened negotiator, creatively structuring deals in a variety of forms, including asset purchases, technology/intellectual property licenses, royalty arrangements, OEM contracts, and tender offers. Orchestrates entire strategic transaction process, including: formulating strategy; identifying potential targets; leading cross-functional teams to perform due diligence and effectively plan and execute integration phase; developing key messages and announcement approach; conducting “post-mortems” to refine and enhance the deal process.
Previously, seasoned product marketing and marketing professional in challenging international roles. Managed teams of up to 20+ people, including multiple VP-level direct reports.
