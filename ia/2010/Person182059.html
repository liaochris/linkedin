Sanjay Puri’s Summary

Sanjay Puri is the CEO of Engage Global Inc., a privately held diversified group consisting of a range of businesses that leverage technology solutions to foster efficiency. He is a dynamic leader with a vision and has been successful in launching and building several businesses based on his vision and his unique ability to pull people together and lead them towards developing a solution.

Engage Global’s portfolio of businesses include a cutting-edge IT solutions firm, web-based businesses, a health care firm, and an online branding venture. Since launching the company on a shoestring budget in 1993, Sanjay has steadfastly spearheaded the growth of Engage Global through innovation, top-notch customer service, and profitability.

Sanjay’s interests are not just confined to the technology world. Sanjay is an authority on US-India relations. He is well recognized for his consistent work and his dedication to promoting US-India business relations. Sanjay serves as the founder and Chairman of the US-India Political Action Committee (USINPAC), a bi-partisan political action group representing more than 2.7 million Indian Americans. He is also the founder-CEO of the Alliance for US-India Business (AUSIB), which promotes US-India business relations. AUSIB hosted the first ever Congressional US-India Renewable Energy Conference on Capitol Hill and has led delegations of business and political leaders to India.

His thought-provoking insights have been featured in The New York Times, The Washington Times, on ABC News Nightline, PBS, CNN, NPR, Voice of America and in testimony to the US Congress .

Sanjay has been instrumental in helping mentor, support and launch budding entrepreneurs as he is passionate about helping others succeed. He actively seeks out and mentors budding entrepreneurs to help them incubate and build their businesses.

Sanjay lives in Northern Virginia, just outside Washington, DC.

  • President

    AUSIB (Alliance for US India Business)

    (International Trade and Development industry)

    2005 — Present (5 years )

    An independent business alliance, AUSIB assists companies seeking to do business in the two most dynamic markets in the global economy. It is co-chaired by Dr. Arun Shourie, former Indian Minister for Disinvestments and Information Technology, and Mr. Salman Khurshid, former Minister for External Affairs

    Our Mission:

    * To help companies overcome the often complex regulatory challenges associated with international transactions while identifying opportunities for subsidies and favored tax treatment
    * To work with the Congressional Taskforce on U.S.-India Investment and Trade Relations to advance legislation promoting US-India trade
    * To reach out to States to promote and encourage business opportunities for members
    * To provide valuable match making introductions for companies seeking successful international JV partners
    * To provide advocacy to maximize investment and trade by drawing upon the power of the Indian American community, which is a strong catalyst in the growth of both economies
    * To organize business and government delegations from both countries that open business opportunities for US and Indian companies
    * To provide interactive sessions with the highest levels of business and government in both the US and India

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)
