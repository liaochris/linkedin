'Abigail is an enthuastic consultant with a thirst to match the best candidates with quality clients!'

Founded in 2005, Precision Sourcing offers recruitment service that is focussed on the development market. From day one, Precision Sourcing has never stoped searching for innovative ways to improve our service in all areas of our business.

Abby Van Rooyen heads up the Development practice division at Precision Sourcing and focuses on placing development talent into the IT/Digital industry. Abby’s expertise and in-depth understanding within the development industry is derived from 3 years recruitment experience. Abby has a successful proven track record of working with major Australian businesses.
