Charles Ritchey’s Summary

Charles has over 17 years of experience in the health care industry, with extensive experience in crisis management and financial turnaround for nursing and healthcare care facilities. Charles is currently Founder and President of DeNovo Healthcare Services, a long term care consulting and acquisitions company.

Previously, Charles was the Senior Vice President of Operations for White Oak Senior Care, Inc. in Baltimore, Maryland. In this capacity, he was responsible for crisis management needs in West Virginia.

Prior to that, Charles was Chief Operations Officer, Co-founder, and Principal for Xavier Health Care Services, Inc., a long-term care management and acquisitions company. During that time, the company owned and managed up to 15 facilities, with Charles providing successful crisis management. Xavier was sold in 2007 to White Oak Senior Care, Inc. Charles served in various Administrator roles throughout the country, including nursing homes and rehabilitation centers in Minnesota, Pennsylvania, and Maryland. He began his career in the healthcare industry as Assistant Administrator for Lorien Health Systems in Baltimore, MD.

Charles is a Licensed Nursing Home Administrator in Maryland, West Virginia, Pennsylvania, Delaware, and previously in Minnesota. He is currently an investor and advisor to Innovative Solar Solutions based in Charlotte, North Carolina and a Strategic Limited Partner and Entrepreneur in Residence with the private equity firm, New Markets Venture Partners, in College Park, Maryland. He has served on the board of directors for the Health Facilities Association of Maryland, the Frostburg State University Alumni Association, and was a Political Action Committee intern for the National Restaurant Association. He graduated from Frostburg State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science.
