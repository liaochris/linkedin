Gerald Seegars’s Summary

Licensed Real Estate Broker in Washington, DC and Maryland with substantial experience in working with residential, multifamily and commercial properties.

Experienced in working with asset management, short sales, property renovations, development projects, foreclosures, and providing superior real estate consultation services.

Successfully litigated several real estate suits in favor of clients including a complex case involving five parties revolving around the District of Columbia's notorious Tenant's Right of First Refusal laws.

Assist clients in matters involving probate, tax sales, distressed properties and pre-foreclosure matters.

Successfully mentored several agents in evolving into real estate developers and dynamic sales professionals.

Gerald Seegars’s Specialties:

Investment properties, short sales, asset management, probate property, tax sale property and real estate legal matters.

  • Senior Attorney

    United States Depart of Commerce, Patent and Trademark Office

    (Law Practice industry)

    1993 — 2001 (8 years )

    I worked at the Patent and Trademark Office or PTO as it is often referred to for a number of years. As a Trademark Examining Attorney, I prosecuted trademark applications, wrote numerous Briefs that were reviewed by the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board. Conducted oral arguments before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board and was one of the first ten attorneys to be selected for the pilot program for the Work-at-Home project. I understand that due to the success of the pilot project, more attorneys work at home than in the office at the PTO today.

    Besides the great people I worked with at the PTO, one of the greatest experiences I had there was coordinating the annual Trademark Expo for two years. My Co-Coordinator and I put together a massive two day expo held in downtown Washington whereby hundreds of companies of all sizes and from all regions of the country would come together to promote their goods and services and help the PTO promote the benefits of Trademarks.
