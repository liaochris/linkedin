Dave Sutula’s Summary

It's my job to bring creative, content and technology together - to synchronize the three so that creative builds artful strategies that speak in the client's voice, do justice to their vision and products, utilize the available technology to the limits of possibility the but don't ask too much of the technology at the same time.

I have a hard time with the straight business-y sound of 'VP of Technology' because I feel it is my job to straddle business, technology and art so I followed the lead of a couple of colleagues who have similar positions and share my vision for new media marketing and I call myself a 'technosynchronator'.
