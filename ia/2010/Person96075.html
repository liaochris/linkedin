Jeremy Lichtman’s Summary

My company does two things well:

1. We build large websites (usually working in partnership with graphic designers) on a white label basis (i.e. we're your development team until you recruit your own). Particular areas of expertise include building large, highly scalable sites, using LAMP technology (PHP, MySQL, Apache etc).
