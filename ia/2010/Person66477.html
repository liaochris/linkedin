Eric Tenin’s Interests:

In March 2005 I started a a blog (www.parisdailyphoto.com) which principle is simply to post a picture a day - and a little caption! - of my home town: Paris. Quite surprisingly I must say, PDP became quickly popular and a lot of people from all over the world started writing me asking how they could start a similar blog for their own city. Very quickly, cities such as London and New York followed, and now today you can find over 300 other “City” Daily Photo Blogs" that have their own following of readers and commenters. This gave birth to another project: the City Daily Photo blog portal that can now be found here: www.citydailyphoto.com.
