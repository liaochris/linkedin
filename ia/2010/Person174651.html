Robert E. Lee, MBA,CCIM’s Summary

Lee has a a wide variety, and well rounded track record in advising buyers and sellers (individuals, family offices, and investment companies) for over a decade as a commercial investment broker/advisor and consultant. Lee has developed student housing and invests on his own behalf as well, primarily in the south eastern U.S. markets. SIG Equity Partners, LLC assists clients with the buying, selling, and investment banking of their commercial real estate investments.

Born and raised in Lexington, KY Robert has lived in the south bay area of Los Angeles in Manhattan Beach, CA for over 10 years. Lee is heavily involved with CCIM, serving as the past president of the Southern CA CCIM Chapter President (www.soccalccim.com). In addition to serving on several international committees within CCIM, Lee is also a 2009 graduate of CCIM prestigious Jey W. Levine Leadership Academy in which 12 commercial real estate professionals across the country are choosen to be groomed and developed as future leaders within the CCIM international Institute.
Lee is a graduate of Pepperdine Universities Graziadio School of Business and Management and served as serve as Chair/President of the alumni association (GAN) from 2008- 2009. Lee also volunteers and is actively involved in leadership positions for other professional and non-profit organizations in Southern California including NAIOP’s YPG Organization, Leadership Manhattan Beach’s Board of Directors, as a Leadership mentor and facilitator for the International Sigma Chi Fraternity Alumni Association and a variety of other organizations.

Robert E. Lee, MBA,CCIM’s Specialties:

Sourcing real estate opportunities from a large international network of brokers, agents, and real estate owners to match those opportunities (both distressed and stabilized) with investors based on their unique investment goals.Banks, Developers, and Other Asset Mgrs engage SIG to sale or stabilize REO and Distressed Properties to mitigate losses.. SIG also sources performing and non-performing note opportunities to Private Equity Investors at discounted prices.
