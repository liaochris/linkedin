Noreen Bowden’s Experience

  • Consultant, Irish diaspora engagement projects

    Global Irish Consulting

    (Internet industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Working on a number of web and diaspora-related projects, specialising in web content management, diaspora networking and outreach, and social media strategies.

    I maintain www.globalirish.ie and also cover diaspora-related news on an occasional freelance basis. I have a particular interest in Irish and global diaspora strategies, communications with the Irish abroad, cultural and historical aspects of global Irish communities, and emigrant political participation.

    I am the Irish representative to Europeans Throughout the World (a Brussels-based NGO representing the interests of European citizens who live abroad), and was elected vice-president of that organisation in 2009.

  • Director

    Éan, Ireland's emigrant advice network

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 2006 — January 2009 (2 years 6 months)

    Ean was an organisation focusing on serving the needs of agencies around the world working on behalf of Irish emigrants. My activities included
    - researching and developing positions on issues of interest to the diaspora (e.g. broadcasting, political participation, cultural expression, etc.)
    - developing the organisation's communications strategy and relaunching the website
    - representing the interests of Irish emigrants in the media and at conferences
    - designing educational tools for teaching Irish emigration and diaspora

    I represented Ireland in the Brussels-based Europeans Throughout the World and also at "Europe on the move: the first meeting of Europeans resident outside their country of origin", hosted by the French Foreign Ministry in Paris in 2008.

  • General Manager, Web Content Manager

    Irish Emigrant Publications
