Eric Van Hall’s Summary

Mediaweb Internet Integrators is a full service internet consultancy, web design and hosting agency based in the Netherlands. We like to prove that functional websites can be beautiful and that beautiful websites can be functional. Since 1995 Mediaweb has developed hundreds of websites for numerous clients. We build state of the art websites according to the latest standards in usability, design and technology.

Some past and present Mediaweb customers are NTI, Grapedistrict, Practicomfort, Miss Etam, Promiss, Sneeuwhoogte.nl, Royal Talens, Blofield, Trefpunt Sports & Leisure Marketing (SPA, Breitling, Delta Lloyd, Essent), Dutch Ministry VROM (SEV, SEV Realisatie, ICEB), Dutch Ministry of Economic Affairs, Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Dutch Ministry of the Interior, Vamex, Cordares Advies, Bank ten Cate & Cie, Hokamo BV, Mona, Eurocongres, Dura Vermeer, Valori, Nuon, Brand Bierbrouwerij, Richard Krajicek Foundation, Landelijke Stichting Tegen Zinloos Geweld, NV Gouda Vuurvast, Achmea, Zilveren Kruis, O'Neill Europe and many others.

Visit our portfolio for a more complete overview of our work: www.mediaweb.nl/portfolio.
