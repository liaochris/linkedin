Ineke Kay Read’s Summary

Ineke is one of the leading recruiters in Queensland. Having joined Davidson over four years ago, she brought unique recruitment experience from the building industry. During her time with Davidson, Ineke has cemented her position as the consultant of choice for some of Queensland’s leading property development companies, placing candidates across senior positions and successfully undertaking difficult search assignments nationally. She was promoted into the Practice Leader role and now looks after all senior civil appointments in addition to managing a team of consultants across property, civil, construction, building products and building services.

Prior to joining Davidson, Ineke was involved in recruiting sales, marketing, management and technical staff within the building sector.

Before embracing a career in recruitment, Ineke held sales, marketing and management positions which included running a profit centre and developing business strategies and marketing plans for sales based operations. Ineke has also been involved in training and development, performance assessment and business improvement techniques.

Ineke holds two degrees - a Bachelor of Business in Human Resource Management and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology. These provide Ineke with an excellent insight into people and people management issues. Many of her clients utilise her consulting abilities across other areas of their business as a result.

Ineke is also an active member of the UDIA urban edge committee, and in 2009 was the winner of the Women in Development Outstanding achievement by a woman award for industry support services.
