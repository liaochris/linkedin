Sally Elatta’s Summary

I am simply a transformer. Someone who is really passionate about transforming individuals, teams and organizations to doing what they do better.
I have a unique mix of technical and business background. I am driven by transforming the IT organization into performing and delivering ROI from a business point of view.
Building high performing teams and moving the organization culture towards achieving lean, efficient and effective processes is my true passion.
I have played many roles throughout my career and have had the opportunity to coach and work closely with many teams in different organizations as a consultant. This collective exposure has given me an appreciation for how these roles operate and how they can all fit together to truly deliver value and align with the company's strategic goals.
I value instilling soft skills and leadership qualities in the people I coach. I believe in Servant Leadership as the way to lead change and create a culture of empowered teams, as opposed to Command and Control.
I have coached and trained Executives, Managers, Architects, Project Managers, BAs, Testers, Developers, Business Product Owners and end users on improving the processes and technologies they use.
I evaluate my performance based on results I've delivered, so my client endorsements here mean a lot to me!
