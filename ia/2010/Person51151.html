David Fitzgerald’s Summary

David Fitzgerald is a highly accomplished business professional with over 17 years experience in corporate leadership, business development, sales management, sales education, marketing and public relations.

Prior to forming the Gravity Marketing Group, he spent his career climbing the sales and marketing ranks with organizations such as Best Buy Company, Kare11 TV, Digital River and as President of Subjex Corporation, a publicly traded artificial intelligence software company.

Today his focus is on strategic lead generation, SEO (Search Engine Optimization), SEM, (Search Engine Marketing), SMM (Social Media Marketing) and traditional marketing functions such as branding and corporate identity development.

David has un-paralleled success as a sales and business development leader through an un-compromising and hands on approach by leveraging his knowledge base and accredited business experience. He is a persuasive communicator as well a true team leader while always “keeping his eye on the prize” on behalf of his organization and its represented client’ base.

David’s Wall Street experience has given him incredible insight and exposure to effective and proven business and revenue development techniques in which he applies daily on behalf of his clients.

He is a graduate of the University of Minnesota and a Lifetime resident of Minnesota.

In Summary: David is a creative, results driven professional. His management style is direct and on point which makes him a highly recognized team coach and mentor. His hands on approach ensures proper execution of the business strategy. His strong track record of successful sales strategies and leadership development is soundly spoken through his presence and presentation.
