Beth Blecherman’s Summary

I am a techie turned blogger mom, authority speaker and social media consultant.

I started my career in application development, system auditing, and then Senior Manager, Computer Process Integrity, for Deloitte.  After senior management I decided to take on family management and started blogging as my career 2.0.

I am Co-Founder and Chief Technology Mom (CTM) of the Silicon Valley Moms Group, collaborative mom blogs in cities across the country as well as the first international site: Canada Moms Blog. I am in charge of developing and maintaining the technology, and work with the team to implement social media strategy, blogger outreach and events.

I founded TechMamas.com,  a tech blog with a parenting slant, as a platform to discuss technology and gadgets for families.

I also consult with companies on blogger outreach, outreach to "digital moms" and social media strategies.  I am active in twitter as @TechMama, and regularly speak at conferences on parenting, social media and technology.

Interested in my services as a blogger, speaker or consultant for your company? Please email me at techmamas(at)gmail(dot)com.
