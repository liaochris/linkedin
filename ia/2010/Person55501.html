Devon Dudgeon’s Summary

World-class strategic digital marketing expert and client services professional with over 15 years’ of experience. Seasoned at launching new initiatives for global brands as well as entrepreneurial startups. Proven track record of building strong agency/client relationships and leading cross-functional teams to grow the business.

After launching an industry-first online CRM program for United Airlines, transferred from Chicago to London to run the Hertz Europe account. Subsequently worked on a freelance and contract basis for some of the top agencies in London, including OgilvyOne and Agency.com.

Relocated to San Francisco in 2009 and currently working with clients in the U.S. and the UK.

Devon Dudgeon’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include: digital marketing and strategy, managing complex web builds, content strategy and planning, social networking, marketing communications, direct marketing, loyalty programs, refining agency processes.


Extensive experience with clients in the travel, financial services, automotive and retail industries, working with brands such as American Express, United Airlines, Tesco, Hertz, Infiniti and IKEA.
