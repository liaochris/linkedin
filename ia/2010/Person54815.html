Dennis Widmyer’s Summary

Film Bio:

Dennis Widmyer is an independent filmmaker living in Los Angeles with a wealth of experience and knowledge already gained in his evolving career. Under his company Parallactic Pictures, he directed the feature 'Absence' in 2006, as well as the documentary on bestselling 'Fight Club' author Chuck Palahniuk. From 2007-2010 he focused on short films and promotional spots for film festivals. He is currently gearing up for his next feature film for hopefully a mid 2010 shoot.

See Reel at http://www.parallacticpictures.com.

SEO Bio:

Before phrases like "social media" and acronyms like "SEO" were the talk of the blogosphere, Dennis Widmyer was lighting up the trenches of online marketing and creating his own buzz. In 1999 he created the official site for best-selling cult author Chuck Palahniuk (Fight Club) at www.ChuckPalahniuk.net. The website was a milestone for online marketing and soon went on to become one of the largest and most successful author sites on the net. In 2007, Dennis partnered up with Craig Diamond's design firm, DIAMONDCreative and was soon consulting clients on successful SEO practices. He broadened his job position by managing Ad Words accounts for clients such as IT service firm Prosum and location management services company Malibu Locations. In 2008, he took over a marketing campaign for Dr. Michael Florman, a successful Beverly Hills orthodontist who wanted more patient conversions for Invisalign, a teeth straightening process. Most recently, Dennis returned to form by heading up a social media campaign for bestselling author Norman Ollestad. Today, Dennis continues to manage Google Ad Words & Analytics accounts for clients, while also serving as a Social Media & SEO expert.
