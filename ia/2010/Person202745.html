Terri Holley’s Summary

Currently serve as sole proprietor of a social media and interactive marketing firm focused on helping profit and non-profit organizations intelligently leverage social technologies. I have a high level of fluency in the integration and use of social media and the exceptional ability to expertly communicate its benefits to others.

My accomplishments include assisting startups and established businesses with meeting goals and objectives by employing social media marketing strategies. My clients frequently enjoy press mentions, more exposure online, and increased leads and sales.

My expertise is regularly featured at Women Grow Business, recently recognized by Forbes magazine as one the best marketing and social media blogs written by women. I am frequently invited to speak at national conferences and participate on panels to discuss best practices and trends related to social media marketing. I am also the producer and host of the Social Media Voices Project, a podcast that spotlights the strategies and leadership techniques of nationally recognized authors, speakers, new media strategists.


Proficiencies:

- Assessing business models, organizational goals, overall mission and technical/social assets (Web, mobile, and traditional media)

- Developing and designing social media tactics that align with branding and marketing messages.

- Assisting with content development,editorial calendars and work flow management.

- Setting up, configuring, and customizing evaluation tools to ensure desired performance insights.

- Evaluating social media campaigns and establishing performance data sources and/or methodologies.

- Articulating the impact of social media engagement and identifying opportunities for improvements.

- Developing employee social media governance policies and guidelines.

- Working with internal teams (legal, PR, human resources) to develop communication, marketing and training strategies for company-wide social media programs.
