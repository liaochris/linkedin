Matt Schlossberg’s Summary

Chicago-based writer, editor, and copyeditor with more than 10 years trade publishing experience in various fields, including dental, medical and horticulture. Most of my career has focused on magazines, journals and e-newsletters.

Last year, I decided to move away from magazine publishing and pursue a career in book publishing. I freelance edited a book on dental ergonomics last year. I also joined a medical association with a book publishing division. I am working to build enough experience to become an acquisitions editor at a trade book publishing house.

Matt Schlossberg’s Specialties:

Magazine management, writing, editing, copyediting, inventory/fulfillment management, manuscript acquisition and solicitation, some marketing and publicity.
