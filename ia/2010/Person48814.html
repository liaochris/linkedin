Dario Diament’s Summary

As Online/Digital Markeitng Manager, Dario is responsible for setting and meeting all client business and communication objectives through Digital Media and Emerging Media strategies. He is a forward-thinker focused on developing paid, owned and earned media programs that seeks to reach, attract and engage target audiences and let them amplify the brands message through meaningful experiences.

Dario joined Latin3 as a Senior Performance Marketing specialist, in charge of the SEO programs, and was part of the Marketing team before being involved in Digital Influence as manager.

Prior to joining Latin3, Dario developed a passion on the digital world while working as senior software developer on popular companies of the chemical, gambling and digital consulting industry.

Bits and zeroes is not everything for Dario. Extremely passionate on traveling and photography, he Bunjy Jumped in New Zealand from the second highest jump in the world, participated in a no-tourist bullfight in Sumatra, Indonesia; camped in the desert of Mexico; danced (at least tried) salsa in Colombia and Cuba; was part of an ancient peregrination in Amritsar, India and photographed pagan carnivals in Bolivia; among other experiences that opened his eyes, heart and soul and defined him as a positive persone. [You can see a small bit of his photos here: http://www.flickr.com/photos/dalan/sets/72157594195960869/]

Dario has a degree on Business Management from UCES (Buenos Aires), he is fluent in English and Spanish and is located in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
