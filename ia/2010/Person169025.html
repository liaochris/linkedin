Randy Sosin’s Summary

Randy is a producer in the industry for over 20 years. Reared in Detroit, he spent his formative years as a musician in the Detroit music scene. He procured a record deal for Rhythm Corps with CBS Records in 1986. He left management to become Executive Producer at a production company, The End, in 1992. In 1995 after 100 videos including videos for Pink Floyd, Ice Cube, Mariah Carey, Salt N’ Pepa, and others – Sosin was lured to A&M Records as Video Commissioner. There he guided the visual images of artists Sting, Sheryl Crow, Soundgarden, Bryan Adams, Blues Traveler, The Bee Gees, Monster Magnet etc. Sosin then joined the venerable Interscope Records. There he worked along side renowned creative visionaries Jimmy Iovine. His first video for Interscope was the award-winning “Stan” video from Eminem. Sosin won back to back MTV Video Of The Year awards for “Lady Marmalade” (2002) feat Christina Aguilera, Pink, Lil Kim, Mya and Missy Elliot and “Without Me” (2003) by Eminem. He is responsible in branding visuals for artists: Eminem, Dr Dre, 50 Cent, Marilyn Manson, Audioslave, Keane, Feist, Queens Of The Stone Age, Enrique Iglesias, Daddy Yankee, The Game, Busta Rhymes etc. Sosin created and EP the Showtime TV Series: Interscope Presents The Next Episode. A critical hit with powerful revues in the New York Times and Top 5 Show of the Year rating in Entertainment Weekly. Sosin started his foray into narrative by Producing the Sarah Silverman movie, Jesus Is Magic . He was EP on additional TV shows: Eminems “Making The Ass” and Platinum Weird: Rock Legends for VH-1- featuring Mick Jagger, Elton John, Dave Stewart and Stevie Nicks. He was EP of LIVE! a film starring Eva Mendes. Sosin also produced a movie titled “I Tried” with classic hip hop artists Bone Thugs N Harmony which was just released. He recently started a content creation company called Gang Of Fore with Jeff Suhy and Piero Giramonti.
