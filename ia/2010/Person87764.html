Jim Ardito’s Experience

  • President

    Ardito Creative Enterprises

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    April 1985 — Present (25 years )

    Jim Ardito has been a successful writer for 29 years. He holds a masters degree in advertising from Michigan State and was one of two students selected from the entire country to write for Foote Cone & Belding Advertising Agency in Chicago. After several successful years at FCB writing on national accounts such as Armour Dial, Hallmark, and Kraft, Jim left to become an independent writer.

    He was co-founder and Charter President of the Independent Writers of Chicago, was a partner in the Right Writers and served for 15 years as President of Ardito Creative Enterprises, a full-service creative resource for a broad range of Chicago-based, national, and international clients.

    Operations at ACE were suspended for 5-years while Jim was employed as a senior writer for Purohit Navigation, a healthcare agency with a strong emphasis on pharmaceutical marketing. Now, Jim is back at the helm of Ardito Creative steering it toward bigger and better horizons.

    Jim is one of the area’s most versatile writers with skills in advertising and marketing, website writing and editing, interactive materials, conceptual development, promotion, audio-visual, training, sales force motivation, newsletters, and more. He has appeared on television on three separate occasions, including an appearance as primary guest on Chicago’s “Business Newsmakers.”
