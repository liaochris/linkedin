Genevieve Pecknay’s Summary

I’ve spent my career years in the southeast pursuing new achievements. Networking is a natural talent for me and through the years I’ve come to create a brilliant kaleidoscope of individuals from coast to coast. I greatly enjoy using my skills to connect the dots of life by introducing the desirable demands to those who seek them. I am driven by my curiosity and the challenges of uncovering the next great idea.
My vocation, whether employed or self-employed, has always been infused with creativity and artistic endeavors. Personal stylist, Interior Designer, Graphic Designer, Internet Marketing and Web Designer projects have satisfied my cravings to indulge in visual art. Two of my favorite creative outlets through out my life have been writing and music. Considering these two passions, I have concentrated my career goals within Marketing, Promotions, and Public Relations of the Entertainment Industry during the past eight years.
Multi-tasking and collaberating motivates my days. While creative problem solving gratifies me with an uplifting sense of success. At the end of the day, knowing I played a part in someone’s achievement and not just my own, allows me to look forward with anticipation for the next day’s ventures. Currently I am looking for new employment that matches my skill set and allows me to learn new skills for growth within my career.
