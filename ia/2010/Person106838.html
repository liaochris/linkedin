Jeffrey Shute’s Summary

Jeff Shute has spent 24 yrs in Organizational Behavior and Organizational Development. As an independent consultant for 14yrs, working with leadership at all levels (even spending some time in Saudi Arabia), has been a facilitator of cultural change, leadership development and personal growth. As a Christian, he has also been involved with church growth and leadership development since 1993.

Jeff is a visionary, loves to think outside the box, and is a motivational speaker and solutions provider. Working with people, their systems, and cultures, he desires to continue to make a difference where ever he goes.
