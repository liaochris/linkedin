Linda Salmon’s Summary

I began my career as a criminal prosecutor and did some litigation work in big law firms. About 8 years ago, I moved into business starting as a Sales Operations Manager and eventually moved into a VP of Operations role. My experience is with small and start up companies, which has given me a hands on approach to management. With the right opportunity, however, I'd be interested in a big company.

My strength is executing on strategy by designing and implementing policies and processes within and across a business functions to reduce costs, increase profit, and increase production capacity. I have a strong people management background and enjoy leading and training teams. I've directly managed account management teams, sales processes, project managers, and technical operations.

Linda Salmon’s Specialties:

Executing on strategy to achieve corporate objectives, process improvements, team development and leadership for production optimization; cross-functional change management.
