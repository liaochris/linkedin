Patrick Morin’s Summary

Patrick Morin is the president and COO of BrightHammer, LLC. a venture firm that specializes in strategy management with an emphasis on sales and marketing development. As a J-V partner, BrightHammer, LLC. works with start-ups and work-out (turnaround) situations to stabilize operations and revenue. It is regularly retained by clients in the multifamily, banking & insurance, real estate, and professional services industries to improve their competitive position and revenue growth.
