Dan Kirsch’s Summary

We are the premier provider of technology professionals to businesses throughout Oregon and Southwest Washington State - our recruiters understand your needs and take the time to pre-screen candidates properly in order to find the best fit for your team.

Dan has been in the staffing business for over 6 years and has established an extensive network of both engineers, technical professionals and human resource professionals. Protingent's reputation in Oregon and SW Washington is excellent and has been built by providing consistent professional and ethical service.

Dan is dedicated to staying in constant awareness of legal issues surrounding staffing and has taken numerous training courses to ensure that. He also is a technical geek that is always looking for the next best product.

We welcome the opportunity to compete for your business and are sure that we will exceed your expectations!
