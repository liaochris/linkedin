    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Hospitality industry)

    January 2007 — January 2008 (1 year 1 month)

    Responsible for the Food and Beverage operation and overall management of the 128-luxury suite Sofitel Reef Casino Cairns, reporting directly to the General Manager and the Reef Casino Trust CEO. The Reef Casino Cairns features seven F&B outlets, plus five function rooms capable of catering to up to 1000 delegates. The Reef Casino complex includes a boutique casino with VIP gaming room, 43 tables, 24 touch-bet roulette terminals and 535 slot machines. The restaurant, bar and sports lounge within the casino are operated by the Sofitel F&B department. The complex is the premier hotel and entertainment venue in the Cairns market, and was recently confirmed again as a five-star property by the AAA organization. The complex employs approximately 600 staff.

  • RESIDENT MANAGER

    Sofitel Fiji Resort & Spa

    (Hospitality industry)

    March 2005 — January 2007 (1 year 11 months)

    Fulfilled all pre-opening aspects, and managing post-opening operations, of the 296-room Sofitel Fiji Resort & Spa. Located on the tourism and residential development destination of Denarau Island, Fiji, the resort opened on 17 December 2005. The project was a joint venture between Air Pacific and Colonial Fiji Ltd, and is managed by Accor’s 5-star hotel brand, Sofitel Hotels and Resorts. Features of the resort included three restaurants, three bars, the largest conference facilities in the Fiji market, a gymnasium, and a purpose built spa facility with nine separate treatment rooms. Further, the property was accredited a 5-star rating by the AAA organisation of Australia during my tenure.
