Robert H. Hacker’s Summary

I develop corporate strategies, business plans, financial models and capital raising strategies to enable startup,early stage and middle market companies to grow. I arrange the mezzanine debt, equity and venture capital required through institutions and funds. I have worked in a wide range of industries, but have particular expertise in wireless, telecom, IT, retailing, distribution and business services.

Professional Highlights
• I arranged over $1 billion in debt and equity in private and public markets, including one IPO
• I served as a CFO for 13 years for both public and private companies, for companies from startups to $1 billion in revenue (retail, internet, telecom)
• I managed a company which grew from $40 million to $1 billion in annual revenue in seven years.
• I provided strategy consulting to Japanese Fortune 500 companies for 10 years
• I have 30 years of international business experience principally in Asia and Latin America
• I have built up relationships with financial institutions over 30 years, including venture debt lenders, mezzanine funds, hedge funds, private equity groups, venture capital firms, banks, commercial finance companies and factors.
• I speak Spanish fluently

I use a three-step process for private company clients.
1. Develop an in depth understanding of the client’s strategic objectives, growth strategy and financing issues
2. Analyze operations to determine if there are any constraints to the strategy that may affect capital raising
3. Develop financing alternatives that blend capital required with the lowest cost structure available
