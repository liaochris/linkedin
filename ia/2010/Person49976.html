Dave Yarnold’s Experience

  • CEO

    ServiceMax

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year )

    ServiceMax is a full suite of field service applications built on force.com -- salesforce.com's cloud computing platform trusted by more than 60,000 companies around the world. The ServiceMax Suite includes installed base management, service contracts and entitlements, field service technician dispatch and scheduling, depot repair, spare parts logistics, service partner enablement, and full mobile field service capabilities. Built and delivered as a native force.com application, the ServiceMax Suite provides complete field service functionality that can be implemented quickly.

    With the ServiceMax Suite, organizations around the world drive more service revenue, control costs, and turn field service into a competitive weapon.
