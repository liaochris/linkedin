Antonio Eram’s Summary

Starting with 1996 I am actively involved in the Romanian Internet and Telecom business. My area of expertise is in mobile marketing and payment services, however I am interested in content related services and mobile convergence.

During my job at Vodafone Romania I was involved with the development with hybrid (by then) mobile web services, with the launch of myx.net portal and complementary services.

In 2005 I have launched my own company NETOPIA.

Today, NETOPIA, located in Bucharest and with a branch in Silicon Valley, is Romania's premier mobile marketing and mobile payments provider. We are investing massively in research and development making us a true trend setter.

Our main services are

web2sms.ro - a true, all purpose, self managed platform for mobile campaigns. With more than 4 Milion sms messages sent monthly our services are used by Top Romanian companies.

mobilpay.ro - reinvented and redefined the mobile payment market. Now, with more than 75% market share we are driving the monetization revolution in Romania.

I am actively involved in other projects, such as the first Games on Demand Platform in Eastern Europe, www.mygod.ro or one of the top Call Centers and Alternative Telecom Operator in Romania.
