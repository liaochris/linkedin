Brett Hickey’s Summary

Small and Medium Sized Business Private Equity Fund Manager.

We provide debt and equity capital to growing businesses seeking to build value through internal expansion, R&D and strategic acquisitions.

We typicall invest $500k to $10MM in US-based businesses in a debt or debt with warrant structure. Companies we invest in generally have $2MM to $50MM in annual revenues.

At my former employer, Citigroup/Salomon Smith Barney, I did mergers & acquisitions and capital raising for insurance companies and other financial institutions.

Brett Hickey’s Specialties:

Industry agnostic private equity firm focused on providing debt capital for growth, turnarounds, acquisitions and restructuring.
