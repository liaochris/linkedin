Matt Wright’s Summary

A senior digital creative with over ten years experience, having worked with Key media organisations including Wunderman Interactive, Euro RSCG, Saint Digital (RKCR Y&R), Ogilvy Interactive, Agency.com, Agency Republic, Publicis, Oyster Partners, ITV, Yell.com and Associated New Media

I have permanent bases in the North and South of England (York and London), although I also work with a number of business's from Asia, America and mainland Europe.

I am used to working closely with project managers, copywriters, IAs, senior creatives and account handlers to produce highly creative, innovative solutions for a diverse range of clients including Virgin, Unilever, Enjoy England, Peugeot, Renault, Vodafone, o2, Orange, Channel 4, BT, Dulux, Sainsburys, The Post Office and Microsoft.

With a proven track record in senior creative roles at creative digital agencies and start-ups, I always look to create strong, impactful creative, that's relevant to the brand.

Until very recently I held the position of Creative Director for an exciting and ambitious start-up called Seatwave.com. After securing 3rd round funding of $25 million they looked to me to source, hire, mentor and manage a team of four designers to assist with all areas of the business including online advertising, white label solutions, micro sites and a complete re-design of the core product.

In my previous full time position as VP Global Design for Mobile Streams (Most Dynamic media company - Media Momentum, 2006) I was charged with re-branding the company from a small business start-up to a corporate entity listed on the London Stock Exchange, as well as creatively leading a variety of b2b and b2c design projects for Web, WAP, on-portal and off-portal.
