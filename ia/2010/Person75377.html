    CEO, Setaram Instrumentation
    Since its founding in the 1948, Setaram Instrumentation has established itself as a global supplier of scientific instrumentation solutions in the field of thermal analysis and calorimety. Headquartered in Caluire, France, Setaram's products include: high performance thermal analyzers, calorimeters, gas sorption and high pressure mass spectroscopy R&D scientific instruments for various applications. Setaram's clientele include industries, such as pharmaceutical and life science, advanced materials, energy (nuclear, hydrogen, gas), and process safety, food and biotechnology. In the pharmaceutical R&D area, the Company's systems are used for polymorphism screening, crystallinity studies, solubility determination and kinetics and testing stability as a function of temperature and humidity. Setaram has also developed unique solutions to characterized nuclear waste by non-destructive assay, and process development tools for the chemical, petrochemical and pharmaceutical industries to assess reaction risks. Setaram Instrumentation employs a seasoned, experienced and dedicated staff of over 100 individuals to oversee, manage and facilitate day to day operations and market its products.
