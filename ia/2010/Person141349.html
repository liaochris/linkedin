Meryl Wiernik’s Summary

With 25 yrs experience in the clinical trials industry, I have led cross-functional groups on a global scale and have a proven track record for enhancing profitability by developing strategies to grow the business, improve processes using Lean practices, build a quality culture, and improve client relationships. I have expanded global markets and reduced costs for Fortune 500 companies in the medical, pharmaceutical and biotech industries. My design, implementation and streamlining of processes enhanced efficiencies and improved quality. Developing high level vendor relationships, negotiating contracts and establishing attractive pricing and value-add terms, I increased corporate profitability. By creating actionable metrics, I reduced errors and improved client satisfaction. I am an action-oriented problem solver and intuitive decision maker, with outstanding ability to implement change, analyze situations quickly, and perform against tight timelines.
