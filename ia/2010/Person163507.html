Phara McLachlan’s Summary

Animus Solutions is a management and IT consulting firm helping organizations solve the toughest enterprise issues and reach their peak performance through the most innovative techniques and technology. Specializing in enterprise asset management, IT asset management (ITAM), software asset management (SAM), business process management (BPM), compliance risk management and advisory services, the company offers vendor-agnostic, best-of-breed solutions to fit each client’s needs.

Animus Solutions consultants have experience with the premier automation products for the management of IT, such as HP/Peregrine AssetManager® and ServiceManager®, CA Asset Management®, BMC Remedy®, LANDesk Asset Lifecycle Manager®, and more.

Phara McLachlan’s Specialties:

Area of expertise lies in the Enterprise and IT Asset Management (EAM/ITAM), Software Asset Management (SAM), Business Process Management (BPM) and Advisory Services. Focus on strategic planning, assessments, training and implementation of industry best practice ITIL processes, ITAM processes and COBIT processes. CSAM and CITAM certified, as well as certified in premier automation products for the management of IT.
