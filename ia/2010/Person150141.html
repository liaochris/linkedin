Mike Fox’s Experience

  • Founder

    The Mullingar Group

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 2006 — Present (4 years 1 month)

    The Mullingar Group provides counsel in three marketing communication areas. First, MullingarMatch™ works with marketers or agencies to improve the relationship between marketer and agency, which can involve conducting an agency reivew, repairing the current agency relationship and/or negotiating agency compensation. Second, MullingarMessaging™ helps marketers develop a brand strategy, which their agencies execute; focus here includes new product launches, financial services, home decor and higher education. Third, Mullingar SecondOpinion™ harvests new insights from your existing research (surveys, focus group tapes, et al) so smarter decisions can be made without paying large sums for new research that simply gather dust.

  • Adjunct Professor

    Wake Forest University

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    1998 — Present (12 years )

    For 10 years Mike has taught a New Product Development class to second year MBA students at Wake Forest University's Babcock Graduate School of Management. This background, as well as his nearly 25 years of working with clients such as Nabisco, Sara Lee, Novartis, BellSouth and VF Corp., uniquely qualify him to provide message strategy to marketing departments looking to launch a new product in a cluttered marketplace.
