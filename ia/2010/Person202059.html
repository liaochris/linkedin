Andrew Hays’s Summary

The mission of Hays Firm LLC is to provide individualized services based on the unique facts of each client's situation. To accomplish this goal, the firm establishes close relationships with its clients in order to understand their present financial situation, future goals and the type of legacy they wish to leave behind. By considering each client's global financial picture, the firm is able to specifically tailor an estate plan that most efficiently accomplishes their goals.

Andrew Hays’s Specialties:

Estate planning, wills and trusts, residential and commercial real estate, probate estate administration, business counseling and entity formation.
