Jason Cobine’s Summary

Hi, my name is Jason Cobine and for the last 20 years I have helped hundreds of businesses and charities in London and the home counties get their insurance claims paid. I do this by ensuring policies are set up correctly which means insurance disputes are avoided.

Also helping businesses reduce the time and money they spend business networking. I'm happiest when showing you how to find the right contacts quickly and easily, online and offline.

Find me quickly @jasoncobine or call 07817 252226.

You gain a real idea of what we do in the websites above or the blogs and recommendations below.

The people we help are usually managing directors, business owners and charity executives who wanted to grow their organisation whilst protecting their income and reputation.

Typically, insurance clients already had cover, hoped it will pay out, yet were not 100% certain that it would. We provide reassurance that you have the right cover.

Business Networking clients now find it quick, easy, fun and profitable finding introductions. Typically, they wanted to reduce the time and effort spent networking and get results.

The "living proof" of what we achieved for them is in our recommendations here and on the websites above.

I am happy to introduce you to anyone in my network who may be able to help you. Get in touch and we can work out exactly who they might be.

Connect with us now to see if anything we do might be of benefit to you.

A great connection for me is an accountant, a solicitor, a loss adjuster, a loss assessor, a training company, a chamber of commerce, a franchise group or a business development group.
