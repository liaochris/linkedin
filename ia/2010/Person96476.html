Jerry L Johnson II’s Summary

I am the fourth generation of real estate entrepreneurs in my family and grew up watching my family buy, hold, build and sell investment properties. Our main focus has been on construction and investment in land, single family homes, and small multi- family properties. I grew up studying investments and started my own business at age 13.

As the first in this next generation to take the lead, I have chosen to move the family tradition into multifamily and commercial real estate. I have formed the family business into a true corporation that builds wealth and opportunity.

I have been mentored by Dave Lindahl, Lance Edwards, Gordon Appleby, Mark Pantak, Linda Muscarello, and Jay Gilden locally. I am a member of Lifestyles Unlimited, The Realty Investment Club of Houston, the North Houston Apartment Entrepreneurs, and the Houston Apartment Association.

I have experience managing under-performing apartment complexes with poor rent collection and I have turned them around and increased their value and profitability. I also have several years of experience as a licensed real estate agent and have helped investors and buyers locate and close on residential property. I regularly attend meetings and seminars hosted by Lifestyles Unlimited, O’Connor and Associates, CCIM, CREN, Entrust, the Wealth Club and RICH.

I have a dual degree Finance and Management with over thirty hours in accounting. I have also studied at the Champions School of Real Estate, Houston Association of Realtors, Institute of Real Estate Managers, Houston Community College and the various mentors and real estate professionals listed earlier. I have gained over 15 years of experience in multiple areas of accounting; retail, restaurant and apartment management; and investment analysis. I have audited and participated in the preparation of the financial statements of several fortune five hundred companies including BP Amoco, Conoco Philips, Schlumberge, Aker Maritime, and Eagle Global Logistics.
