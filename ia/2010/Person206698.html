Tonja Walker Davidson’s Summary

I have been on TV for most of my adult life-since 1980. I have been nominated for an Emmy Award and have won numerous awards from my peers and from audience members over the years. I am a singer and recording artist, and I have traveled around the country with various country and cabaret acts, and performed for National Baseball and Soccer teams and numerous variety shows.My one woman shows have recieved very good reviews. I was honored to have been asked to do a song and dance number for the opening of the televised Emmy Awards written by Bill Conte and David Zippel, in 1995. I've been a guest on Oprah as well as other well known shows.
I was married in 1997 and I have a beautiful family with 2 children and 3 step children,to whom I have dedicated a great deal of my time and energy for the past 12 years.
The Derby Stallion is a film that I produced in 2005 that came out in June of 2007 and was the highest grossing Direct to DVD movie in the country that year. The film won the 2007 Best Feature Award from The New York International Film and Video Festival, as well as other prestigious awards in other festivals. I was very fortunate to have Zac Efron, starring in the film with Bill Cobbs, William R.Moses, Crystal Hunt, Rob Pinkston and myself. Zac's success has brought the film much extra attention and for that I am very grateful.
