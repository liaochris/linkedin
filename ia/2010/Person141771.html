Marley Hughes’s Summary

Marley is graduating form the University of Central Florida in the Spring of 2011 with a Bachelors Degree in Finance and a minor in Real Estate.

Marley has made it a goal to complete three dynamic and challenging internships while attending UCF. After receiving many internship offers from well respected companies, Marley has completed an internship with Edward Jones and RedChip Investments; well on his way to completing his original goal. Marley is looking to round out his senior year with a challenging internship in a Real Estate valuation role.

Currently Marley hopes to gain valuable experience while in college to set himself apart from his peers on a pursuit to excellence in the postgraduate world. He foresees himself holding a position in world class financial firm and eventually obtaining his MBA & JD degrees from well respected schools. Other designations Marley plans to pursue are : CFA, a Masters Degree in Finance, Series 7 & 66, CFP, and one day PhD's in Finance and Economics.

Lately, Marley has created and developed an Investing Club at UCF to help current students understand, appreciate, and love the world of investing. He is also the co-founder of EXEC (Elite Xtreme Entrepreneur Collective), based in Orlando.Currently, Marley is the President of the largest Finance club at UCF- The Financial Management Association, as well as, the Public Relations Chairman of the only Real Estate Club at UCF- Real Estate Academic Leaders. .
