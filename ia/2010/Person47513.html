Danielle M. Criona’s Summary

Roll International is a privately held corporation with companies including Teleflora, Fiji Water, Pom Wonderful, Paramount Citrus, Paramount Farms.

Ms. Criona is a native Californian who earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Loyola Marymount University, a Master’s Degree in Science from California State University at Northridge and Juris Doctor degree from Loyola Law School and was admitted to the California Bar 1999. Ms. Criona practices in all areas of Intellectual Property Law, focusing on federal court litigation. She has been extensively involved in all aspects of patent, copyright and trademark infringement litigation, including two patent infringement jury trials and domain name cases under the Anticybersquatting Consumer Protection Act. Ms. Criona's trademark practice focuses on overall brand management including trademark infringement litigation, opposition and cancellation proceedings before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board, trademark prosecution, protection, enforcement worldwide. In her patent practice, Ms. Criona also has extensive experience with patent infringement and validity analyses and opinions, and license drafting and negotiation and prosecution of patent applications. Ms. Criona also practices in the area of copyright law, with extensive experience in copyright protection and infringement litigation.
