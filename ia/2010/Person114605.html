Kenneth Hemler’s Summary

Kenneth Hemler is an associate in Shearman & Sterling LLP's Executive Compensation & Employee Benefits Group in New York, NY.* He has experience in a variety of executive compensation and benefits matters for both public and private companies. Mr. Hemler's practice involves assisting clients with corporate governance and compliance matters, including the preparation of securities filings. He has also advised clients on the design and implementation of incentive programs and nonqualified deferred compensation plans. In addition, Mr. Hemler has experience with the compensation and benefit issues raised in corporate transactions. His practice also includes drafting employment and severance agreements, board resolutions, and employee communications.

Mr. Hemler previously worked in the Mergers & Acquisitions Group, where he gained experience in public and private mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures, as well as general corporate legal matters.

Mr. Hemler's recent experience includes representation of:

-The Dow Chemical Company in a number of transactions, including (i) Dow’s acquisition of Rohm and Haas Corporation, (ii) the sale by Rohm and Haas, Dow’s wholly owned subsidiary, of Morton International, Inc. (valued at $1.675 billion), (iii) Dow’s sale of its Calcium Chloride business; (iv) Dow’s public offering of approximately 130 million shares of its common stock as well as its $6 billion underwritten public offering of debt securities, and (v) Dow's divestiture of its Styron unit to Bain Capital Partners (valued at $1.63 billion).

-A U.S. registered broker-dealer in connection with the structuring of incentive programs and nonqualified deferred compensation arrangements; and

-HLTH Corporation in connection with its merger with WebMD Health Corp. (valued at $1.3 billion).

*Mr. Hemler is currently on secondment to The Dow Chemical Company's Human Resources Legal Group in Midland, MI.
