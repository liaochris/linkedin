Rian Van Der Merwe’s Summary

Creative, analytical and passionate product management and user experience professional with international training and experience in a broad range of areas, including product strategy & execution, requirements gathering, prioritization and scoping, product marketing, usability, ethnography, and surveys. Problem solver and strategic thinker with the added value of these skills being rooted in a solid theoretical foundation.

My career objective is to use my education, skills and experience in a team-based environment to contribute to the user-centered design and enhancement of interactive products. I want to use my analytical skills to have a thorough understanding of user needs, behavior and attitudes; my creative skills to help develop the best possible systems and strategies for design; and my communication skills to build relationships and execute these strategies effectively.

Rian Van Der Merwe’s Specialties:

Product Management // Product Marketing // Product Discovery & Process // User Experience Strategy // User-Centered Design // User Experience Research (surveys, usability testing, RITE studies, ethnography, participatory design, card sorting, personas) // Market Research (surveys, focus groups, segmentation)
