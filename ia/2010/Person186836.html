    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    The Caretinuum Program has been created to assist a carefully selected group of nursing facilities establish subacute and long term health care programs specifically designed to meet the special needs of mentally and developmentally disabled consumers. Limiting participation in the Caretinuum Program assures that the standards of care, treatment, dignity and respect that special needs agencies, consumers and their families have come to expect of community and health care providers will be delivered accordingly. Caretinuum nursing facilities specialize in a new paradigm of coordinated post-hospitalization health care services beneficial to the special needs consumer, hospital, community residences, and the community-based special needs agency.
