Bruce Black’s Summary

Bruce has broad experience in start-ups, acquistions, change management and problem solving. His extensive deal history and ability to create win/win outcomes has consistently benefited all stakeholders. He has proven skills in planning, team building, execution, relationship building and achieving investor returns.

His work today exclusively involves helping successful people sell and buy businesses in Arizona. Selling or buying a business can be one of the most important transactions in anyone’s life. Achieving the best outcome starts with a well defined exit strategy that incorporates important goals, objectives and realistic timelines. Bruce takes those important business and owner issues and facilitates the marketing of the business and completion of a successful sale based on providing all parties with accurate information, clear communication, creative problem solving while working toward a successful sale. Bruce is using his 35 years of extensive business experience, both as an entrepreneur and an executive, to assist business owners in maximizing their company’s value through effectively negotiated and managed sales transactions.

Bruce would welcome introductions to successful business owners of private companies in Arizona with annual revenues of $1 million to $20 million.

Bruce Black’s Specialties:

Starting and growing businesses, acquisitions, mergers, strategic planning, business valuations, negotiating, team building and service cultures.
