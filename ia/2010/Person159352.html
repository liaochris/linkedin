  • President

    Newton Hospitality, Inc. DBA Event Temps

    (Hospitality industry)

    October 1998 — September 2008 (10 years )

    After 15 years in catering I started Event Temps out of my garage. When my wife got tired of having carloads of wait staff and bartenders coming and going, we opened a tiny office at Downtown Crossing in Boston. In 10 years we grew to 5 offices throughout MA and RI providing employment opportunities to almost 2000 banquet wait staff, bartenders, cooks and dishwashers annually. Because of our unique training program, our team of catering and food service professionals proudly provided the best hospitality staffing services to the local affiliates of national and world wide companies such as Sodexo, Aramark, Hilton, Flik, Eurest, Sage, Sheraton, Guckenheimer, Unidine, and Restaurant Associates. We are proud of our service to Harvard, Northeastern, BU, MIT, Bentley, Babson as well as many other colleges and private secondary schools in the area. We were the industry leaders providing trained culinary and banquet staff to a variety of catering and food service operations in the New England Region. We were proud to support a variety of community non-profits such as Rosie’s Place, Greater Boston Food Bank, Community Servings, Jewish Vocational Services and Southern Poverty Law Center. In September of 2009, we sold Event Temps and I am privileged to continue to service my clients and staff as the Vice President and General Manager.
