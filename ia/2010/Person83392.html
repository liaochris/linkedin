Chip Lutz’s Summary

Public Speaker and Trainer on positive leadership, creating positive working environments, and humor in the workplace.

LCDR Chip Lutz, USN(Ret), MSEd, CLL, is the CLO of founder of Covenant Leadership, LLC and has 22 years leadership experience. A retired Navy Officer, he has had two command tours, served as the Director of Security for Naval District Washington, DC during September 11th, and has held various other key leadership positions. He prides himself on keeping it "light" while getting the job done.

A seasoned educator and trainer, he is currently adjunct faculty for two different universities and was selected as Southwestern College’s Affiliate Faculty Newcomer of the Year for 2005. He has taught over 20 different classes in leadership, management, human resource development, and organizational behavior. He has been published in Security Management Magazine and was the 2007 recipient of the Association for Applied and Therapeutic Humor’s essay contest for Humor in the Workplace.

His ability to relate, respond, and deal with employees in a humorous, “real” way is been the key to his success as a leader and educator.

In short, I help people creative positive environments so that they can make life more workable and work more livable!
