Dee Uzoka’s Summary

I have over 12 years Customer Service and Administration experience, as well as over 5 years as an Office Manager/PA. I also have over 11 years experience delivering IT solutions to consumers and small businesses.

I am well versed in the pressures facing small business owners as I am a business owner myself. My aim is to work in partnership with your business, and take your focus away from those tasks that are not core to your business activity. This will allow you to work on your business and not in your business.

If you are a new start-up or an established small business that would like access to Executive Assistant level support, or would like help and advice on how to make your business work more efficiently, give me a call. I am your one-stop resource.
