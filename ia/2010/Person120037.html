Kurt Phelps’s Summary

Kurt is the President and Founder of Global Network Recruiting (GNR), a hi-tech recruitment firm that specializes in contingency, engaged and retained searches for professionals and executives across North America. Since 2000, GNR has worked with organizations ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them access the best and the brightest talent.

As President, Kurt established and implemented GNR’s aggressive recruiting strategies which include cold-call recruiting directly into clients’ competitors and leading organizations in their industries. The firm retains high-performance, hi-tech experts as recruiters who focus their industry knowledge on finding the right talent for our clients. Under Kurt’s direction, GNR has grown to be one of the largest hi-tech recruiting firms in North America.

Kurt graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering.

The GNR Difference

At GNR we provide the necessary support to ensure your hiring success. We understand the importance of finding exactly the right individual with the precise background and skill-sets required.

We do not use job boards or other passive tools for candidate sourcing. Our customized, flexible and proven recruitment process begins by defining your needs and developing a strategy for identifying best-in-class talent. At every step in your search, we will be there to facilitate the process and ensure the best result for both our clients and candidates.
