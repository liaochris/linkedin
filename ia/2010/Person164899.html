Todd Scott’s Summary

I own a chain of Platoon Fitness locations throughout Pennsylvania. We offer a variety of different programs including Personal Memberships, Platoon Golf, Platoon CrossFit, and Platoon Outdoor. We also offer Personal Fitness Training at an 8000 square foot location devoted solely to working out with a personal trainer, and Platoon U, certifying instructors in the Platoon Fitness technique.

As a young man, I ordered a set of Tony Robbins videos after seeing an ad. I started applying his lessons in my own life, as well as coaching my family and friends. I called Tony Robbins’ office and asked if they were hiring, and sent them a (slightly embellished) resume. They flew me out to San Diego and ended up hiring me on a probationary basis. I accepted as many leads as were offered, making upwards of 200 calls a day, and meeting with 10-15 leads a day. I worked with Tony Robbins to expand into the UK, & eventually owned the first Tony Robbins franchise in San Jose, Ca.

Working with Tony enabled me to get hired by Deepak Chopra, which gave me some extra “tools in my toolbox”, including stress management techniques.

My family owns a chain of radio stations called Great Scott Broadcasting and brought me in to work. I started at the bottom and worked my way up to Vice President, but I did everything from sales to hosting shows. My father taught me to be a self starter and to work for what I want. He taught me to always treat other people like I would want to be treated. I got where I am through hard work and a friendly personality.

While working at the radio station, I saw an ad for Platoon Fitness. My wife and I signed up for a class and really enjoyed ourselves. We continued to take classes, eventually purchasing the club. We revamped the membership requirements expanded memberships. I decided to devote most of my energy to Platoon Fitness, expanding throughout PA.

Platoon Fitness is a great fit for me. I am able to help people, and I have a blast doing it.
