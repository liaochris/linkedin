Chris Black’s Summary

Christopher J. Black, CPC is the Sales and Marketing practice leader of Sanford Rose Associate’s Healthcare IT and Services Practice. He is responsible for originating and managing retained searches for Sales and Marketing positions in the Healthcare IT space.

Prior to joining Sanford Rose Associates, Chris was the Director of Recruiting for a regional executive search firm in the Southeast. Before embarking on his career in executive search, Chris spent 11 years in the investment field, as a Listed Equities Trader and in Institutional Sales where he advised several industries, including Healthcare IT companies, on a wide range of capital funding alternatives, including debt issuance and equity offerings.

Chris belongs to many local and national organizations which assist him in fully meeting the needs of the companies he represents. He is most active as a member of Healthcare Information Management and Systems Society (HIMSS) and as the Educational Director for the South Carolina Association of Personnel and Staffing (SCAPS). During his investment career, and as an executive search consultant, Chris has spoken to many large organizations..

Chris holds a B.S. in Business Economics from The State University of New York at Oneonta and he is currently finishing up his M.B.A. at The Citadel. He is also a Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC).
