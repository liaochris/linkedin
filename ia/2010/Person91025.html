Jason Hill’s Summary

Jason has been a project manager, leading a variety of technical and business projects, for the past ten years. He has been at the US Treasury's Bureau of the Public Debt team since February 2006 in the role of Project Manager, working on ERP/Oracle customer implementations as well as leading the development and rollout of the agency's Project Management Office. He is a graduate of West Virginia University - BS in Finance, George Mason University - MBA, and George Washington University - MS in Information Technology. Jason is PMP certified from the Project Management Institute as well has earned a Master's Certificate in IT Project Management.
