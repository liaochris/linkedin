Hugh Byrne’s Summary

SVP with Greener World Media, home of GreenBiz.com, GreenerBuildings, GreenerDesign, and other green business news and information sites. 10-year track record of success and leadership in General Manager and Vice President of Web Operations and Marketing roles for $25-300M media and technology enterprises. Expertise in developing and launching information/data-based lines of business, creating growth while maintaining high standards of quality and operational efficiencies. Direct marketing roots with Fortune-500 enterprises, possessing an entrepreneurial approach that blends ingenuity with analytics, creating strategic solutions that achieve measurable results.

Information products, directories, social media, direct marketing, b2b, SEO, SEM, publishing, green marketing, lead generation, emarketing, green business, multichannel marketing, database marketing, and product development.
