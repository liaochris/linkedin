Mark Malven’s Summary

Extensive experience handling corporate, finance and technology transactional matters for a wide range of emerging and established businesses. Also have many years' experience acting as the outside general counsel and trusted strategic advisor for numerous technology-based businesses.

Recognized expert and speaker on technology transactions, having been one of the primary negotiators for two of the largest IT outsourcing transactions ever undertaken, involving billions of dollars in services. Handled numerous other strategic technology transactions involving development (content and technology), consulting, sponsored university research, manufacturing, licensing, distribution, value-added reseller, private-label, content licensing, site linking, acquisition, and joint venture relationships.

Business and finance law experience includes representing both businesses and investors in enterprise formation, venture capital and other private financing, public financing, acquisitions and divestitures, and strategic alliances.

First-hand experience as an entrepreneur. Served as Vice President of Business Development and General Counsel for a dotcom startup and subsequently founded and managed own law firm. Guided emerging technology and Internet companies through the early stages of business plan creation, multiple rounds of venture and other funding, customer acquisition, joint venture and partnering arrangements.

Served as a product engineer at Chrysler Motors before attending law school and also a licensed patent attorney.

Mark Malven’s Specialties:

High stakes negotiations; technology transactions including licensing, outsourcing, joint ventures; mergers and acquisitions; financing transactions
