Douglas Morrison’s Summary

As the Director of the Platform Operations Group at Affinion Group, I lead an organization tasked with implementation and support of application platforms which enable the Affinion Group's global marketing initiatives. Prior to joining Affinion Group, I focused on the mobile and wireless industry to integrate handheld devices with enterprise applications. As a founding member of Riverbed Technologies, a mobile software application development company, I led development teams to deliver data synchronization applications for enterprise integrations with intermittently connected mobile devices. Riverbed was acquired by Aether Systems in 2000. As a lead member of the Aether Systems Platform Architecture Group, I was responsible for the architecture direction of multiple software product lines.

After leaving Aether Systems in 2002, I joined Dominion Digital as a Senior Consult. At Dominion Digital, I continued to refine my software architecture and development skills. I leveraged my J2EE architecture skills to implement solutions and mentor other developers. I left Dominion Digital and joined Affinion Group in early 2003 to lead the software architecture and web infrastructure efforts. After a year as lead software architect, I assumed a leadership role as the Director of the Enterprise Technology Group . As the Director of Enterprise Technology for Affinion Group, I was responsible for supporting the technology platform which hosts Loyalty Programs tailored to clients in financial, hospitality and automotive industries. After successfully leading the Enterprise Technology Group, I assumed a larger role in at Affinion Group with my current position as the Director of Platform Operations.
