Rachel Bortz’s Summary

I am the owner of Pet Nanny to the Rescue, a vet tech owned and operated pet sitting and service company serving Roswell, Alpharetta, Canton, Cumming, Woodstock, and many of the surrounding areas (Georgia). I spent over 10 years in the veterinary industry and started the business in 2005. Our services include pet sitting, dog walking, pet taxi and transport (US and International), dog training and more. We are licensed, bonded, insured, and members of the Georgia Network for Professional Pet Sitters, holding ourselves to the standards of such professional organizations as PSI, NAPPS and others.

Rachel Bortz’s Specialties:

Veterinary Technician for over ten years; 5 years wildlife rehabilitation experience, 20 years equine experience including elderly and ill equine and livestock.
