Sam Richter’s Summary

Visionary, passionate leader with extensive experience building companies, business units, and brands. Business strategist and planner. Creative thinker with wide-ranging experience in product/service development and bringing programs to market. "Rainmaker” able to identify and secure new business and generate strategic partners. Award-winning traditional, direct, and online marketing executive and practitioner. Nationally renowned speaker and author on Sales Intelligence, online information technologies, personal reputation management, marketing, and leadership development. Creator of the "Know More" sales improvement program.

Recognition includes USA Book News Best Book of the Year, Sales Book of the Year, and an IPPY Award all for the top-selling "Take the Cold Out of Cold Calling" book. Have won numerous regional, national and international awards including Best of Show and Gold awards from advertising/public relations/and online marketing associations; multiple WEBBY Awards for best Web site development; a Gold Award at the International Film Festival; a Retail Vision and a Codie Award—the "Oscars" of the software industry—for Best E-commerce Software. Named to the Business Journal's "Forty Under 40" list. A finalist for Inc. Magazine's Entrepreneur of the Year.
