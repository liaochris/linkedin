Trevor Hammond’s Summary

Trevor Hammond is the President and owner of Makena Financial, a Financial Coaching and Education company designed to Educate, Empower, and Inspire families and individuals to greater Financial Freedom and Safety.

Trevor has developed several powerful and unique personal coaching processes to help clients:
*Improve monthly Cash-Flow
*Eliminate Debt faster and more efficiently
*Buy real estate to build wealth safely and strategically
*Investment Real Estate planning
*Analyze home refinance strategies and options

Trevor's ultimate goal is to help every family transform their relationship with money, and guide them on a journey that makes Financial Independence a reality. This leads to more freedom of both time and money, and a life of greater fulfillment.

*Trevor is also the Founder and CEO of MyFinancialIndependenceCoach.com (MFIC), a national online financial coaching program designed to help families simplify the path to financial independence. MFIC is available to be licensed by Mortgage and Real Estate Professionals, Companies, and Financial Institutions. The Affiliate program allows other businesses and professionals to license MFIC and provide to their clients as a completely turn-key way to change lives, increase value to clients, generate more business, and build residual income. (Sample site: http://www.MyFinancialIndependenceCoach.com/mortgagepro)

For more information regarding personal coaching or licensing MFIC for your clients or employees email trevor@makenafinancial.com.
