Judy Mauer’s Summary

Judy Mauer, founder of Creatively Organized, comes well equipped with an inspired vision as an Organizing, Life & personal development specialist. She brings to her customers a culmination of her innate gifts, 30 years of professional experience, and 47 years of life experience.

As a social services administrator she specialized in designing and overseeing systems of services and managed teams of up to 78 people, over several programs. Beyond her experience in the workforce, she has owned and operated three small businesses and founded a community based non-profit where she developed her Life & Personal Development practice.

Her education includes a Bachelor of Social Work generalist counseling degree, a minor in interpersonal communications and both formal and informal education in business, psychology, sociology, systems theory and cross cultural studies.

Creatively Organizing has been in existence since 2006 and has served hundreds of individuals, families, seniors, and small businesses in assisting them to find order and clarity in their space, minds & visions.

Judy Mauer’s Specialties:

Judy is best known for her ability to connect to her customer's inner environment of self and outer environment of material space working with both to create systems and solutions that match their personality styles and traits. Results are lasting change and transformation, a better ordered space and increased productivity.

She works with anyone including those who experience symptoms of AD/HD, compulsive hoarding, depression, and anxiety, often working in conjunction with their therapists.
