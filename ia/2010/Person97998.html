Jillian Fisher’s Summary

As managing supervisor on the Dell global small and medium business (SMB) account, Jillian leads the development and execution of communications strategies which support global content and messaging, strategic media relations, executive communications, customer relations, corporate reputation, product launches and thought leadership campaigns.

Jillian has more than seven years of public relations experience, ranging from corporate communications, consumer technology to business-to-business applications.

Prior to joining Axicom, Jillian worked at Access Communications where she managed strategic business and consumer communications for flagship accounts including Intuit, PayPal and Shopping.com.

As a result of her media expertise, Jillian has secured high profile media placements in outlets including FORTUNE, Forbes, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, USA Today and BusinessWeek.

Additionally, Jillian has experience in managing corporate speaker's bureau programs and has secured and developed speeches at the FORTUNE Innovation Forum, FORTUNE Brainstorm TECH and the Inc. 500 Conference.

A member of the Silicon Valley PRSA, Jillian has coordinated media participation at annual events.

Jillian holds a bachelor’s degree in journalism, with an emphasis in PR, from California State University, Chico.
