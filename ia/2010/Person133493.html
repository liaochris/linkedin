Mark Stevens’s Summary

I have worked in the Silicon Valley for over 30 years. In that time, I have had a broad range of experience, ranging from over 20 years of corporate and deal practice at one of the Valley's top law firms (Fenwick & West), several years as EVP of business development at a leading internet company (Excite@Home), and stints as a venture capitalist, public and private company board member and, back in the dawn of time, as a software engineer. And my wife says I'm a pretty good cook.

I work with companies and investors in the technology sector, particularly in the internet, new media and software industries. I am always looking for interesting and challenging clients with excellent teams and high ambitions. I really enjoy complex deals, and have run transactions valued at over $25 billion in my career.

Mark Stevens’s Specialties:

Industries: internet, new media, mobile, games, ad networks, e-commerce, software, cloud computing, SaaS, consumer, online video, computers and systems. Practice: venture capital, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), initial public offerings (IPO), strategic partnering, joint ventures, founders stock, private equity, stock options.
