Iridium Capital’s Summary

Iridium Capital, a New York City-based net leased real estate fund management company, was formed in 2009 to hold real property and target long-term consistent returns and appreciation through reliable commercial real estate investment vehicles.
Iridium Capital funds provide the beneficial returns of owning commercial property to investors lacking the necessary capital and skills to individually acquire and manage income-producing investments. By pooling investors' capital and investing across a wide range of commercial properties our funds provide diversification, reduced risk and a hedge against an inflationary environment. Our commitment to our investors and transparency in our business are the cornerstone of our enterprise.

Iridium Capital’s Specialties:

We are a Net Leased Real Estate Fund Management Company focusing our investments in real property, especially NNN.
