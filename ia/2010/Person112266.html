Kathy McAfee’s Summary

Thank you for visiting my LinkedIn page to learn more about me. I look forward to connecting with you and getting to know you.

I work with motivated business executives, high potential corporate talent and ambitious entrepreneurs to help them become the recognized leaders in their field.

Serving as executive presentation coach and motivational speaker, I empower business leaders and technical professionals to project more of their talent, power and influence when they next present themselves and their ideas.

My company, Kmc Brand Innovation, LLC, offers coaching, training and motivational services in the areas of:

1) high engagement presentation skills training;
2) leadership development and executive coaching;
3) professional networking training and relationship building; and
4) motivational speaking for conferences and meetings.

As a certified master practitioner of Neuro Linguistic Programming and certified Blind Spots executive coach, I work with high potential talent and subject matter experts at major companies to improve their communication, interpersonal skills and leadership presence in order to realize their full leadership potential in the organization.

I bring over 25 years of diverse industry experience and knowledge of marketing and branding to help my clients gain greater competitive advantage and career advancement.

I consider myself to be a high energy motivational speaker, a prolific writer and passionate thought-leader. I am driven to motivate people to action to help create positive changes in the world of work.
