Julie Cortés’s Summary

Julie Cortés has run her own professional advertising /marketing copywriting and proofreading business for more than 12 years. From agency to corporate and everything in between, Julie's done it all. Print, collateral, broadcast, outdoor, direct mail, B2B, B2C, Web, interactive ... you name the medium; she'll add the flair.

Now celebrating a BEST IN SHOW in Copywriting win, this firecracker has been recognized nearly 50 times with industry awards, including Omnis/Addys (Ad Club), Fountains (Business Marketing Association), and Freebés (Freelance Exchange). With over 100 clients on her company roster, she's worked with all sorts of industries, including real estate/development, fashion/retail, food service/restaurant, and much, much more.

As the founder of The Freelance Exchange of Kansas City and an exec board member of the Social Media Club of Johnson County, Julie stays active with volunteer work, giving back to the community that's given so much to her.

Julie Cortés’s Specialties:

Highly conceptual, very succinct, and well versed in all mediums and a variety of industries.
