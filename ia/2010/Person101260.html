Joe Whyte’s Summary

I have been working in the search engine marketing and Internet marketing industry for over 7 years. Through the years I have seen many shifts and changes within this industry making me a well balanced and experienced internet marketer.

Defining component cost on products, improving those costs while improving process's and procedures. Documentation. Training staff members and managing groups of people via Project Management software. Building work flows and optimizing them with specific measurable. Evaluating & compiling P&L statements. Creating Terms of Services for products. Business development and vendor selection and negotiation. Helping to solidify and develop business's SWAT and business plans. Build and engineer cross platformed autonomous programs to help automate and add value to an organizations bottom line.
