Rakesh (Rick) Kabra’s Summary

Entrepreneur and technology specialist, experienced in managing software firms and developing innovating products.

I joined Easy Soft in 2006 as Executive Vice President. Established in 1986, Easy Soft provides legal software products to thousands of law firms across the country. Our primary focus is in the Family Law, Legal Billing & Trust Account Management and Real Estate Closings.

Since joining Easy Soft, I have been instrumental in redesigning nearly all of our legal software products with new interface and many value added features. I also deliver seminars and webinars on many different topics related to law office management and latest law changes. My presentations and the company’s products help small & mid-size law firms use technology to operate efficiently and expand their business development.

Do contact me if we have areas of common interests or serious business opportunities, especially related to products/services for law firm..

My goal at Easy Soft is to continue developing our products and services in highly focused vertical market.
