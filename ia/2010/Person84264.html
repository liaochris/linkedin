Ignited LLC’s Summary

Ignited is a marketing innovations agency.

What’s that mean to you? We don’t really know yet. That’s the thing about innovation. It’s unknown. Unprecedented. And it tends to make people a little uncomfortable. But there’s no such thing as playing it safe anymore. You could even argue that safe is the new risky. These days, you have to be agile. Responsive. Willing to experiment, evaluate, optimize and reinvent. Which is why we tend to laugh at what worked last year, welcome what’s ahead, and worship the clients with the courage to say,“Yes.”

Ignited LLC’s Specialties:

If we help a client create ads that promise innovative products, we will make sure every experience the consumer has with the brand is innovative. The media plan will be innovative. The in-store merchandising will be innovative. The packaging, owner's manual and tradeshow booth will be innovative. And if there’s a 1-800 call center, you can expect the hold music will be innovative, too.

Ignited LLC’s Experience

    Ignited

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    July 1999 — Present (11 years 1 month)

    Ignited was launched July 1, 1999 to be a premier provider of marketing services to technology, internet and entertainment related companies. The company was founded by a group of employees who left Activision, Inc., a publicly traded interactive entertainment software company. The first client was Activision, who helped launch the business by providing a commitment of business, equipment and financial support. The company has expanded significantly since then, and now handles a wide range of companies doing business at the nexus of technology and entertainment.
