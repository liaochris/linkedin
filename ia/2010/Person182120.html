Sanjib Subba’s Summary

Seasoned administrator with cross industry experience. Areas of expertise include Educational institute management, Training programs, Capacity Building, Program development and Banking.

Sanjib has designed and delivered several training programs for the Government of Bhutan, Nepal, Vietnam and also NGOs and corporate in Thailand and Afghanistan. The topics included CSR, budgeting, public accounting, educational reform projects, project management, anti corruption etc

Sanjib Subba’s Specialties:

Progressive and proven track record in Banking with expertise in International Trade Finance, Retail loan products, marketing.
