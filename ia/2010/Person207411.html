Tracey (Blanchfield) D'Aviero’s Summary

I am Tracey D’Aviero, a Certified Internet Marketing Virtual Assistant located in Osgoode (Ottawa), Ontario, Canada.

I have worked as an administrative professional for over 20 years. I have spent twelve of those years working out of my own home office, providing virtual services to clients.

I enjoy helping solopreneurs and small business owners perfect the finer points of their business, by taking care of their administrative details so they can concentrate on their clients.

Last year I became certified in the internet marketing industry and now specialize in providing the services that are outlined in my ebook "Why You Need A Certified Internet Marketing Virtual Assistant". You can obtain a copy of it by going to my website, http://www.traceydaviero.com and subscribing to my mailing list.

You will find many areas that I might help you enhance your online presence in the ebook.
