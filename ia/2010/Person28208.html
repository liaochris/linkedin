    After a decade of serving the payments industry with recruiting services, CSH Consulting, Inc. has announced that it will retire the name and officially be known as Impact Payments Recruiting effective April 1, 2010.     Impact Payments Recruiting has placed more than 1600 professionals with processors, ISOs, payment gateways, POS manufacturers, financial institutions and other organizations in the payments and transaction processing business.
