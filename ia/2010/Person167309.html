    ** Our Mission:

    * Comp-U-Dopt provides computers and technology education to enthusiastic, motivated children in under-served communities of Houston, Texas. By fostering educational opportunities for the children we serve, we encourage them to develop increasingly necessary technology skills and to recognize and appreciate community service.

    * I help as often as I can by delivering donations to the organization from various other individuals and companies, donating my time to help refurbish donated computers by: troubleshooting hardware issues, running DBAN on the computers to securely wipe any previous data, and installing Edubuntu: Ubuntu Linux for Education, “Ubuntu” is an ancient African word, meaning “humanity to others” or “I am what I am because of who we all are”. Ubuntu contains all of the applications of commercial software, from word processing, spreadsheet, presentation, browser and email applications. Edubuntu is built on top of the Ubuntu base and has educational programs specially designed for children ages eight to 18.

    * I also have had the honor of helping at the adoption sessions. In these sessions the children learn how to use and care for their computers including connecting the monitor, computer, mouse and keyboard, and how to log in and get started. We also include an overview of the Edubuntu software. Word processing is explored and, depending on the amount of time available, the children are taught how to cut & paste, to use spell check and thesaurus, and to create and save files. The children will have an opportunity to explore various educational games and, if the facility allows for it, they will learn how to connect to the Internet.

    * Also, I help promote and market the company by spreading the word about who they are and what they do in order to help recruit other volunteers who would like to make a difference in the lives of many local children.
