Chris Bradley’s Summary

I have worked in the publishing industry for the past 14 years.

While working in the cookbook fundraising division and speaking with 1000’s of customers I recognized a need for a simple, fast and cost effective publishing company geared towards any writer with any type of publication.I took my near lifetime family involvement with printing and customer knowledge and created Instantpublisher.com in 2000.

In 2008 I recognized a new need for individual books created from digital photos -the new age photo album so to speak - and started FamilyMemories.com.

Chris Bradley’s Specialties:

Self-publishing, recipe books, fundraiser recipe books, digital photo books
