Chris Perry’s Summary

I graduated this past May with my MBA from the Mason School of Business at the College of William and Mary and am now an Assistant Brand Manager on the Lysol team at Reckitt Benckiser in Parsippany, NJ.

THE BRAND & MARKETING GENERATOR

I consider myself a talented, driven, creative Generation Y Brand and Marketing "Generator" with proven ability to bring endless energy and passion to my work, instill motivation in my teams as both leader and contributing member, propose new and creative ideas and solutions and deliver immediate and lasting results in diverse environments and industries under most difficult conditions and/or extreme pressure. It is my goal to become a contributing member of a close-knit team and earn my way into leadership and management roles. For more information, please visit my website at http://www.chrisperry.me.

THE ENTREPRENEUR

I am also an ambitious entrepreneur, having founded a number of unique online career search services and communities, including Career Rocketeer, Launchpad and MBA Highway, to fill underserved niches within the industry.

THE CAREER ROCKETEER & PERSONAL BRANDING EXPERT

Thanks to my entrepreneurial endeavors in the career services industry, I have developed an extensive knowledge of personal branding, career search and development strategies, social media networks and Web 2.0 tools and tactics for identifying opportunities and successfully pursuing them. I have shared my tips and advice during presentations and workshops for both students and business professionals. Some of my topics have included:

Career Search & Development Strategies
LinkedIn & Web 2.0 Job Search Tools
Personal Branding & Presentation
Resume Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

And More...
