Candace Davies’s Summary

I am a Global Career Management Professional and am the President and CEO of A+ Resumes for Teachers and Cando Career Coaching and Resume Writing.

I am dedicated to assisting clients worldwide leverage their strengths, accomplishments, and unique selling points to capture their dream careers. I have both extensive credentials and experience, combined with the passion to ensure my clients get the very best career and job search assistance.

My maxim is “My client’s success is my success.”

I have written thousands of resumes and other job search documents for clients with incomes ranging from $15,000 to $500,000. My expert knowledge of diverse occupations, employment and workplace trends, job search techniques, career assessment, and career planning strategies has been evident in my “top notch” marketing documents and in my clients’ successes.

I am the author of five career-specific e-books: 101 Teacher Interview Questions and Answers; 101 Teacher and Administrator Job Search Tips; 101 Classroom Management Tips; 101 Principal Interview Questions and Answers; 101 Teacher and Administrator Resume and Cover Letter Tips. I have written and published over 200 career-related articles and I have also been selected and interviewed for numerous articles due to my career coaching expertise.

I am actively involved with many career and resume industry organizations ensuring that I remain on top of all the latest trends and developments in my field. I am committed to providing personal and individualized service tailored to each client’s specific needs.

When working with my clients, I ask them important questions to unearth their abilities and strengths in order to differentiate them from the competition. There is no career situation that I have not dealt with: from career transitions, brand new graduates, to large employment gaps, I will tackle any situation to create job-winning career documents.

Candace Davies’s Specialties:

Specialize in the education sector - transforming out of date or ineffective career documents into interview and job winning professional marketing campaigns.

Expertise in: resume writing, cover letters, philosophy of education statements, autobiographies, personal branding and marketing, job search strategies, interview preparation, career coaching, career transitions, LinkedIn profile development, networking, social media.
