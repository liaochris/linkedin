Nate Peterson’s Summary

I am a serial business problem solver primarily through the use of technology. I am currently the Sr. Manager of eCommerce at The Kroger Co. I have worked up through the ranks of development starting with PowerBuilder and graduating to the Java platform performing J2EE development writing JSPs, Servlets, EJBs. I then moved on and gained experience in the Microsoft platform doing Visual Basic, XSL, XSLT, XML, and C# and .NET. During my time as a developer, designer and software architect, I was constantly making heavy use of object oriented analysis and design. I have experience in many relational databases such as SQL Server, Oracle and Sybase (Access doesn't count).

I would love to classify myself as a technology geek, but that just wouldn't be true. I am in to solving business problems and I usually do that through technology -- as opposed to being in to technology and finding business problems to solve with it. With that being said, I am not technology agnostic in terms of the platforms I work with. I am primarily interested in the Internet and the Web related technological platforms.

I love to Golf, Work Out and self-admittedly a bit of sports-a-holic. ESPN is not the only channel I watch but it is one that is on my favorites list. I am also a huge Fantasy Football fan and team owner of several teams. I am generally on the go all the time. Friends and family think I must have ADD, but I believe I have lots of pans in the fire so I need to stay in constant motion to give each their due attention. I liken myself to a child in that I have two gears, full throttle or sleeping.
