Matthew McGowan’s Summary

My background includes 20+ years of national and international sales with companies like Georgia-Pacific, Tenneco, Oakcrest and Indiana Hardwoods. Some career highlights include developing new commercial and industrial markets in North America, Europe and Asia – and achieving at least a 200% increase in sales at three companies.

1. At Georgia-Pacific, for example, I increased sales from $2.6 million to $15.5 million (595%) over a four year period.

2. At Oakcrest in 2002, I improved wholesale business and created approximately $1 million in added revenue in a single year; this more than doubled sales in a secondary market.

3. At Indiana Hardwoods, I negotiated sales contracts valued as high as $2 million. My team and I developed and sustained account growth for five operations with annual global sales of $45 million.
