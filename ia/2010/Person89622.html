Jann Robinson’s Summary

Jann Robinson has been regarded as a "natural" in today's modern food culture. In the Food Service business with her late husband David H. (Dave) Robinson, III since the early 90's, Jann has catered to President George W. Bush’s Press Corps twice, the likes of Beyonce Knowles, The Winans, Michael McDonald, and several other up and coming acting and musical artists. The details of the entertainment industry made opening restaurants and catering operations a cinch.

Jann and her late husband opened their first restaurant in 1998 after years of catering backstage for national artists at street festival, and small television and movie sets. Their first restaurant Dave’s Deli was awarded the title of Top Ten Best Burger Around for their “Big Bama Burger” in 2000. Running a classy operation was not uncommon to the Robinson’s. Their “upscale” sandwich shop with its white table cloths and white napkin rolled cutlery were often ranked as a 3.5 star facility by the local papers.

Upon the major economy shift following the September 11, 2001 incident, in 2002, they called the restaurant business quits for the more financially secure catering business. One of their biggest catering sellers since 1998 has been their Deep-Fried turkeys. They started with just the turkeys, and by 2001, they were catering entire meals for families regionally, turning down online requests from as far away as Arizona. When you do something right, everyone knows about it.

All the change and devastation of losing a friend and spouse of nearly 15 years has not stopped Jann at all. Although she passed on catering Thanksgiving dinner to many families in Birmingham in 2006 just after Dave died, she made up for it in a big way in 2007 by adding awesome new recipes, and using refrigerated trucks as an innovative means of supplying hundreds of families their Thanksgiving feast just as she and Dave had discussed for 2006.

  • Principal

    Backstage Catering Company

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    October 1994 — Present (16 years 1 month)

    Jann Robinson is a dynamo entrepreneur with a big smile and an even bigger laugh. Jann and her late husband, Dave, started the Backstage Catering Company in Birmingham, AL in 1995. Dave worked as a contractor, and Jann a home-maker. But their shared love of food and preparing it just right won out. “Food was our shared passion. We enjoyed every aspect of it, and doing it together,” exclaimed Jann.

    Although it wasn’t the proverbial piece of cake, Backstage Catering was a hit early on. They specialized in catering to entertainers who visited the South, serving their own style of Southern Hospitality.

    Jann’s business model is simple: serve delicious and healthy “down home” southern food, and serve plenty of it! At the same time, Jann knows she has to somehow be the caterer of choice in a down market and Jann truly sell herself. Jann's’ unique presentation is indeed part of her business model. She is tall, beautiful, has great spiky hair (some times with a streak of pink), has an incredibly style in her dress (no one would ever believe she is the mother of six!), and her personality always wins people over. Indeed, Jann is now looking at ways to take her love of cooking to new mediums – television, radio, and demonstrations – any venue where her style and presentation can win the day.

    Fortunately, Jann believes in herself, and is a master networker. Serving in the Junior League of Birmingham and on the board of a local non-profit, and host of a local radio show gives her access to a wide variety of potential customers and word-of-mouth advertisers. Jann has been able to increase and diversify her business model.

    Jann’s cheerful, positive and down-home attitude has amazed her friends as they watched her take the reins of her life firmly in hand after Dave’s death. “Jann is working hard to make Backstage Catering a top of mind business, and to brand herself as well. I think she may be Birmingham’s answer to Paula Deen!” says a friend.
