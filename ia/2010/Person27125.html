Brandon Lewin’s Summary

Ever since I was young I knew I was put here to do something great. I had dreams of being an important person that impacted people’s lives in a positive way. My greatest attributes growing up were my abilities to be charming, outgoing, and being passionate about everything I did. I truly enjoyed helping people achieve their goals. I think that is due to my upbringing and why it led me to being a Social Media Marketing expert.

I am a born entrepreneur. I started my first business when I was in grade school at around 10 years old, shoveling snow for our neighbors. It was not Social Media Marketing, but it taught me the harder and smarter you work the more successful you become. I took that to heart and that is just part of what drives me to have the best Social Media Marketing Service Company. I went through a lot of experiences and opportunities that has led me to my Social Media Marketing Crusade. In my financial service business I decided to dedicate my marketing efforts to three different strategies. One was social media marketing. Now, this was a time before Social Media Marketing was really considered marketing. I used Social Media to find the next networking event. I used social media to talk old friends that led to new clients. I even used social media to talk to new people. Social Media is my passion and has been for a while and will continue to be.

Today, we have successfully developed strategies, implemented them and consulted for over 40 businesses in the Chicago land area. We continue to grow and expand our knowledge. We do warn people, that we are not the typical marketing firm. The warning is made because in our process we are very creative and at times can be bold, enthusiastic, energetic, and yet professional all at the same time. We will take your Social Media Marketing Campaign from nowhere to everywhere in 3.2 seconds.
