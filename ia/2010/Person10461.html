Ana Almeida Melikian, PhD’s Summary

Ana Almeida Melikian obtained her PhD in Psychology from the University of Sunderland, UK. She received her Masters degree in Interventions in Psychotherapy from the University of Salamanca and another Masters degree in Ericksonian Psychotherapy and Hypnosis from the Institute Erickson Madrid, Spain.
Before moving to the US, she was a Lecturer at the ISMAI in Portugal and a Faculty Associate at the University of Salamanca in Spain. At the present time, she is a Visiting Professor at Instituto Erickson Madrid, Spain and she teaches intensive courses and workshops at the DFP of ISPA in Portugal.
She has co-authored several published articles and book chapters about hypnosis and psychotherapy.
She is certified as a Psychotherapist by the Federación Española de Asociaciones de Psicoterapeutas (FEAP) and had an active practice in Spain and Portugal, working mainly with people with anxiety problems.
She founded the Instituto Milton H. Erickson de Portugal, and as one of its directors she has organized many workshops, courses and other events.
Now living in Phoenix (AZ), she is working as a Professional Life Coach. Her training as a coach has been mainly through the Institute for Life Coach Training (ILCT), which is approved by the International Coach Federation (ICF) as an Accredited Coach Training Program (ACTP).
She is fluent in Portuguese, English and Spanish.
