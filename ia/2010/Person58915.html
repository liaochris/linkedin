Doug Holt’s Summary

Doug Holt is the managing CEO of the Conditioning Specialists, running the day to day business operations as well as providing fitness services to clientele. Doug is able to draw upon his experience and his years in the exercise equipment industry to give clients a well rounded offering of services. Doug Holt also publishes SB Fitness Magazine and maintains his status Director of Information Services for the National Board of Fitness Examiners and is an educator for the Wellness Department at Santa Barbara Business College.

Doug is currently working on developing certification courses for health and fitness professionals. He is a regular contributor to many publications and has been featured as an expert in the field of fitness.

Doug's long list of accomplishments and credentials include:

* Featured in Men's Fitness

* Featured in Shape Magazine

* Regular Contributor to Pro-Trainer Online Magazine

* Featured in BodyBuilding.com

* Featured in Fitness Plus Magazine

* International Sports Sciences Association, Masters in Fitness Sciences

* National Strength and Conditioning Association, Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist

* Bachelor's Degree, University of California at Santa Barbara

* Named as a Master Trainer by the University of California at Santa Barbara's Fitness Instructor Department

* Doug is one of the world's only Certified Fitness Equipment Experts (FEX) instructors. The FEX training program was developed by the American Council on Exercise (ACE), the nation's "Consumer Watchdog" in the fitness industry.

* National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM), advance certification as a Corrective Exercise Specialist (CES)

* United States Weightlifting Federation Club Coach

* Certified SPARQ Trainer (Speed, Power, Agility, Reaction, and Quickness)
