Michael Neuendorff’s Summary

I am the owner of The Growth Coach, a business dedicated to helping other small businesses in my community. I combine my varied background and present experience in owning a business with the proven process of The Growth Coach to help propel small business owners and their key managers to new heights of personal and professional success.

I help business owners in several key ways:
1. I help owners to be more strategic in the way they run their businesses. The simplest way to explain it is to say that they will go from being good at what they do to being great at running a business;
2. I help owners with marketing coaching to increase business through vastly improved marketing and promotion of the business;
3. I help owners with public speaking coaching so they can go out and promote their business through speaking;
4. I help owners and their teams to be better salespeople by setting up cross-sell and upsell strategies as well as keep-in-touch and referral programs.

My goal is to build a practice on the mid-peninsula staffed with multiple coaches to enable small businesses to have a potent edge over their competition: The Growth Coach edge.

Michael Neuendorff’s Specialties:

All aspects of marketing, public speaking, sales, business strategy, business planning, goal setting, coaching, training, mentoring and negotiation.
