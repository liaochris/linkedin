Marshall Clark’s Summary

Accomplished and highly-creative digital marketing/strategy executive specializing in cutting-edge search marketing solutions. In my over 12 years in the Search industry I've worked with everyone from start-ups to Fortune 100's and have come to understand the needs of clients of all sizes and levels of sophistication.

Recognized as one of the top SEO/SEM experts and Digital Marketing thought leaders in the nation, having powered online strategies for Intel, Chrysler, BofA, Philips Medical Systems, Procter & Gamble, & Roxio/Adaptec, among others. Additional successes in product development inspired accolades from several industry moguls.

While my deep background is in Search, my current work focuses equally on the increasingly interrelated fields of ad exchanges/dynamically-priced media, multivariate optimization, and real-time analytics.

The intersection of these disciplines with Search is of major interest to me, and is the driving force behind my development of 'Dynamic Path' a novel approach to dynamic media/content optimization. 'Dynamic Path' is currently being considered for implementation on a number of large Organic clients.

Marshall Clark’s Specialties:

Digital Marketing Strategy, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM/PPC), Ad Exchanges/Dynamic Media, Multivariate Testing and Optimization, Website Conversion Optimization, Social Media Strategy, ROI Tracking & Analysis
