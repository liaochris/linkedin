Richard Feindel’s Summary

Richard Feindel is an EMMY award-winning photographer based in Boston, MA. He currently works as the New Media Promotions Producer at WHDH-TV and WLVI-TV, Boston’s NBC and CW affiliates. Previously, he served as a Pre-Production Coordinator, Promotions Coordinator/Editor and Production Assistant at the station.

On-Air Promotions
Richard shoots on-air promotional campaigns for 7NEWS (WHDH-TV/WLVI-TV) in Boston, MA. In May 2009, he was honored with an EMMY award for Outstanding Short Form Photography. He has also edited television commercials for the United Nations and The History Channel.

Website Design
Richard designed the new WHDH.com, Boston’s premiere local news and video destination. The site sports a clean design, easy-to-use video player and heavy social media integration. As a result, page views increased over 41% and uniques increased over 38%.

Social Media
Richard developed 7NEWS on Facebook, one of the largest local television news Facebook communities in the nation. Viewers can interact with 7NEWS, submitting comments and video messages to be featured on-air and online. Over 90% of the community’s members are within the highly valued 18-49 age demographic. Since WHDH.com launched Richard’s redesign, Facebook referrals have increased 500%.

Television News
Richard’s work has been recognized by the New England Associated Press/RTNDA for excellence in both Breaking News and Feature Videography. He has been honored with multiple awards from the Society of Professional Journalists, including the National Mark of Excellence Award for TV Sports Photography. His work has also been featured on CNN and CNN.com.
