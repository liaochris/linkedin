David Wolf’s Summary

David Wolf is President and Chief Executive Officer of Wolf Group Asia (WGA), a Beijing-based corporate strategic communications advisory firm. David has a broad range of strategic counsel, corporate communications, government relations, and market-entry management experience throughout Greater China and Asia, in both advisory and enterprise management capacities. His advisory work has focused on corporate positioning, government relations, market entry, issues management, scenario planning, executive communications, and strategic advice on business in China at the CEO and Regional MD level.

In addition, David is frequently called upon by regional and global media as a commentator on the technology industry and government in China, and is a regular contributor to publications like Media magazine and AdAge China. He also serves on the Editorial Advisory Board of China Economic Quarterly, as well as the advisory boards of several media and technology startups.

David's clients reflect WGA's focus on the innovative and creative industries, one which sees the continuity of interests among firms engaged in technology development, online services, new media, software, mobile devices, manufacturing, content, retail, and services.
