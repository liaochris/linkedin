Elwyn Fraser’s Summary

From a technical standpoint, I’ve gained a stronger passion for technology by interweaving my spirituality with scientific progress. Scientific progress has incalculably improved the livelihood of humanity, despite the many misuses of science over the centuries. I continue to use my technical skills to be an integral part of the positive force in technology and scientific progress.
