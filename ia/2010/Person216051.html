David Winter’s Summary

Winter Solutions is a consulting firm providing customized solutions to Eastern Massachusetts homes and businesses since 1986. We are a small independent group that is focused upon providing the high levels of service and value that larger organizations are unable to achieve. We concentrate on understanding your individual needs and strive to be your single point of contact for all things technical. We stand behind our work.

Reap the benefits of a permanent IT staff without the expense of hiring. Winter Solutions has provided consulting services to small businesses and home based professionals since 1986. Through our established set of best practices, Winter Solutions can help you increase the reliability, security, and performance of your computing environment, understand how to better utilize technology to address your business needs, and improve employee productivity.

Are you planning a home renovation/construction project? Do you want to future proof your home? Are you ready to turn your TV room into a next generation home theater? Winter Solutions will work with you from the concept to implementation: smart home design, whole house music, custom home theater, wired and wireless networking, and more. We work with your design/build team throughout the project and are there as a resource for you long after the dust has settled.
