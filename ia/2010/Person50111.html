David Todd’s Summary

My leadership experience is broad, and includes the areas of Engineering, Inventory Control, Purchasing, Tool Room / Maintenance, Production, Quality Control, Safety, and Project/Program management. These roles have been in metal forming, welding, machining, finishing, including electro-plating and painting as well as assembly and testing in Six Sigma and Lean Manufacturing environments. I have career experience in supplier and customer quality, new product launches, TS16949 and QS9000. Trained and practiced in problem solving techniques such as 5Y, 8D, 5P (Honda), DMAIC, PDCA, SMED and FMEA.

My career focus has been on cost savings and process improvements achieved through program development and implementation, design enhancements, team building and culture change. This has included the implementation of Total Productive Maintenance, Lean Tools, and Six Sigma methodology. I also have experience with Allen Bradley or Rockwell programmable logic controllers (PLC’s).

Improvements have consisted of the modification of existing processes, and design enhancements with responsibility from concept through approval to implementation. Project scope has ranged from single machines to the value stream map for entire departments and facilities. I have also played major roles in the building and start-up of new facilities.
