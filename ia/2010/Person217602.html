Yoel Ben-Avraham’s Summary

Over my years as an IT Professional I've had the opportunity to explore every role in the Software Development process for the Personal Computer platform. In parallel I have engaged in a variety of training activities that run the gamut from End-User training development & delivery, to technical & occupational training in Software Development and culminating in "soft-skills" acquisition for leaders and managers in the technical environment. More recently I've had the opportunity to apply my extensive technical knowledge toward the challenges of marketing and advertising on-line, as well as other facets of managing on-line businesses and virtual teams.
