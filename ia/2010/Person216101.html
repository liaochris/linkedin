wispeny ellis’s Summary

Wispeny has a passion for sports and has developed an extensive knowledge of a variety of sports from recreation to competitive sports. She was fortunate to work as an HR manager to learn the strategic side of the business but her passion lies with sports. Wispeny has worked with all levels of athletes from youth to college to adult and enjoys training and mentoring individuals when possible.

While attending college at Texas A&M University, Wispeny was involved in recreational sports, a member of the Brazos Officials Association, and assisted the City's Parks and Recreation Department with their youth and adult sport leagues. She officiated a variety of sports, hired and trained staff, supervised events and learned the administrative side of hosting sporting events. She gained experience in risk management, facility operations, training and development staff, and hosting.

Wispeny spent 8 1/2 years as an NCAA Compliance Officer at three different Division I Universities. She was responsible for educating coaches, athletes, and school administrators on the NCAA rules. Over the years she has developed her public speaking abilities and fine tuned her leadership skills. She was successful in improving processes which reduced potential violations and increased work efficiency.

Wispeny spent 2 years as a Human Resources Manager for a growing research company with an operating budget of $21M. Not only did Wispeny handle HR issues, she also filled the roles of a Payroll Specialist, OHSA Compliance Officer, and Front Office Manager. She was instrumental in improving communication and implementing cost saving initiatives.

Wispeny holds a M.S in Kinesiology with a specialization in Sports Administration from University of North Texas and an B.S. in Kinesiology with a specialization in Sports Management from Texas A&M University. She has also completed the Texas A&M University Leadership Institute Program.
