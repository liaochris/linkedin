Christopher M. Turoski’s Summary

► Lead IP Lawyer of Cargill Animal Nutrition Division of Cargill, Incorporated
► Vice President of CAN Technologies, Inc. a subsidiary of Cargill, Incorporated

Chris Turoski leads a worldwide Team of lawyers and professionals to manage the intellectual assets of the Animal Nutrition Division of Cargill. The Team builds value through strategic asset management, licensing, and dispute resolution.

Cargill is an international producer and marketer of food, agricultural, financial and industrial products and services. Founded in 1865, the privately held company employs 138,000 people in 67 countries. In fiscal year 2009, Cargill reported net earnings of $3.33 billion on sales and other revenues of $116.6 billion.

The Cargill Animal Nutrition Division provides customized animal productivity solutions to commercial producers across the Americas, Europe, and Asia.
