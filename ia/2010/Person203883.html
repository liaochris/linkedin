Tim Heard’s Summary

My job is to get to know bright, interesting people. Period. If I do that long enough with enough people, I'm able to start connecting the dots betweeen employers who need problem-solvers and creative minds, and the corresponding people who can come on board and add value to the companies. Connecting with people isn't about making a fast buck or a quick placement. It's about getting to know people, understanding them, and helping them meet their goals.

I'm an unusual kind of open networker. I will connect with most people, assuming that they don't seem to be sleazy or unethical, provided that it seems to make sense to do so. I don't just go around connecting to everyone under the sun for the purpose of adding to my list of contacts.

If I can help you in any way, even if it's unrelated to recruiting, please don't hesitate to ask. I'll do my best to be of service, or try to suggest someone to you who can help.

*** Please note that I hesitate to contact or accept contact requests from other recruiters who operate in niches similar to my own. I don't want to feel that they might be sourcing from my list of contacts, and I wouldn't want another recruiter to worry that I might do the same. ***
