Skip Battle’s Summary

I'm a member of the board of directors at several companies including Advent Software, Expedia, LinkedIn, Netflix, OpenTable and Workday. I'm also a senior fellow at the Aspen Institute. Prior to serving on the boards of several technology companies, I served with Andersen Consulting in various roles, including worldwide managing partner, market development, until my retirement from Andersen Consulting. I served as a director of PeopleSoft until its acquisition by Oracle. I am also the former chief executive officer and executive chairman of Ask Jeeves.

Skip Battle’s Specialties:

Business consulting, investor, member of the boards of directors of several technology companies, market development, etc.
