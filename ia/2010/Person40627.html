Cindy Pain’s Summary

Ultra creative with a huge dose of common sense. I help people get up to bat. I change perspectives. I help you manage your career as well as you do your vacations.

Inspiration to the artist called entrepreneur. With over 20 years corporate experience driving business and profits, I utilize that experience to help my clients get clarity around their role as an entrepreneur and guide them in structuring and marketing their organizations.

As a career coach I help create a road map for clients to pro-actively define their career path, market their personal brand, and successfully manage their search.

Recognized as a dynamic public speaker, seminar leader, and facilitator with both domestic and international appearances.

I am a Linkedin Evangelist. If you are looking for a speaker on how to optimize the use of Linkedin and manage your brand feel free to connect and we can chat.
