Melissa Cardin’s Summary

I have experience as a case manager, in which I meet with families for intakes into a homeless program, and then follow up with those families accepted into the program on a weekly basis. I also develop a financial plan with the residents of the program by reviewing their spending every week and helping them develop a budget. I assist families in creating goals to develop self sufficiency.
I make referrals as needed by each family, allowing me to collaborate with many different agencies in the area. These referrals have often included in-home parenting help, counseling, and other resources that families can access after moving out of the program.
I also have some experiences maintaining statistics of referrals to the program, as well as statistics of families served by the program. With the information gathered through the year, I am able to compile several different types of graphs to show the trends for that year.

I have done some freelance writing work, as well, which has allowed me to continue to learn and develop new connections with people from around New Hampshire.
