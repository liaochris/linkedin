Salene Kraemer’s Summary

Prior to founding the MAZURKRAEMER firm, I worked for over a decade at law firms both large and small, on complex, multi-million dollar matters in major metropolitan areas. I can provide the superior technical skills and experience of a large firm lawyer and attentive, cost-conscious client service.

I have extensive experience in commercial bankruptcy matters, representing nationally prominent debtors, major unsecured creditors, official unsecured creditors’ committees, special interest committees, and trustees in various aspects of Ch. 11 and Ch. 7 corporate bankruptcy cases in courts all over the country.

After I earned a certified turnaround professional designate designation (CTP-D) from the Turnaround Management Association, I joined The Meridian Group in 2008 as a financial consultant and legal advisor. Meridian is a premier investment banking and turnaround management consulting firm.

In 2010, I formed the MAZURKRAEMER firm. MAZURKRAEMER is an entrepreneurial business law firm in tune with how technology has changed the legal and business landscape of our time. With optional billing models and lower overhead, it also provides attentive, cost-conscious legal advice in this dramatically changed economy, without compromising technical skill or experience.

From incorporation to closure or sale, MAZURKRAEMER offers counseling to entrepreneurs and small to mid-sized businesses at every stage of the business cycle. With a clean and simple, “in plain English” approach to drafting and negotiating, Mazur Kraemer renders legal advice that is business minded and results driven, focused on adding value to the client, resolving the problem at hand and catapulting your business to the next level. It is a law firm that is not a necessary evil, but a trusted business advisor in all facets of a client’s business.

Salene Kraemer’s Specialties:

business planning and valuation, contract drafting and negotiation, Chapter 7 and 11 commercial reorganizations, claims dispute resolution, employee issues, prosecuting and defending avoidance actions (particularly preferential and fraudulent transfer litigation), e-commerce, real estate.
