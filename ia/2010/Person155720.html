Patrick Infante’s Summary

I established NJ Boat SafeLy in response to New Jersey mandating all boat and personal water craft (PWC) operators obtain a boating safety certificate. Our goal is provide this necessary and life saving training in a fun, relaxed and informative setting.

NJ Boat SafeLy holds courses throughout New Jesey most often in Flemington, Hillsborough, Manville, Westfield and Whitehouse. We also hold private courses for groups of at least nine. See our upcoming schedule plus much more at www.njboatsafeLy.com
