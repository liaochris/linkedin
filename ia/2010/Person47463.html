Danielle (Elle) Favreau’s Summary

I have been working in web design and development since late 1997. I started with WYSIWYG development and within a couple of years was doing full custom development and hand coding. In 2000 I started a small business providing my services to other local small businesses. In 2003 I moved to the US and put my business on hold for a short time while getting acclimated. In 2006 I began working at Magellan Health Services, Inc. and found that my position there and the skill-set I was acquiring would be complemented by my personal pursuits and vice versa and I renewed my business, adding several additional companies to my portfolio in 2007, and ultimately incorporating in 2008 to form a cohesive company structure.
