Aaron Riney’s Summary

Who I Am

Sure - some have referred to me as art director, senior art director, web director and director of creative services over the years. But at my core - I’m a designer. Fonts, color, kerning and white space are why I chose my profession.

Fortunately for me, I have had the opportunity to work with others like myself at agencies that encourage creativity through collaboration. I have served some of the most recognizable brands in the world like Walmart, Sam’s Club, Procter & Gamble, Unilever, Kellogg’s, Comcast, Cox Communications, Knology, The World Wildlife Foundation and many more.

As team leader, I have encouraged others to respect clean, thoughtful and compelling design, the importance of brand equity and the advantage of tactical thinking. Others, thankfully, have shown me a thing or two along the way and I look forward to learning something new every day.
I have explored both traditional and interactive advertising thoroughly and love both equally.

---

What I’m Looking For

I am looking for a long-term opportunity to work with a forward thinking, creative group in the Tampa Bay area. The ideal position would encourage me to be part conceptualist, part strategist, part mentor, part apprentice and include a healthy dose of production.

I thrive in an environment that requires creativity across a broad range of client and project types as I am not limited to either print, or interactive.

In addition - an agency that appreciates the power of social media will find me ideally suited.
