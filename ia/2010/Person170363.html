Rebecca Markarian, APR’s Summary

With more than 12 years of public relations and communications experience, I've taken my career online with a new focus on communication using new media - Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and others. Particularly I enjoy working with companies that are interested in genuinely deepening their relationships with their various audiences and making all of their marketing programs work more comprehensively together.

My background includes extensive work on luxury and high-end brands such as St. John Knits, DTS Digital Entertainment, Jaguar, Land Rover, James Hardie Building Products and the Resort at Squaw Creek in Lake Tahoe. I've also worked on other automotive and motorsports brands including Suzuki and Isuzu and touched on various consumer brands - Old Spice, Ivory, Direct TV, Toshiba Business Solutions and others.

The overarching professional goal I have set is to continue to challenge myself by applying new learning and technology to enhance my communications abilities for my clients.
