Paul Pang’s Summary

Paul Pang is the CEO of Adnet Communications. He is a Marketing Professional with over 30 years of marketing experience in the Asia Pacific region. He has a MBA in Strategic Marketing from the University of Hull, UK and is a Qualified Google Advertising Professional as well as a certified Advanced Search Engine Marketing Professional. He also holds an Advanced Certificate in Training and Assessment (ACTA).

He has worked in various industries such as the Electronics, Communications, Education and Information Technology industries and has held strategic executive positions such as Sales & Marketing Director, Regional Business Development Manager, General Manager and Managing Director.

Some of the companies he has worked for include Microsoft, Lotus Corporation, Digital Equipment Corporation, Computervision, ICL, International Aeradio, Teledyne Inc. and TMC Educational Group.
