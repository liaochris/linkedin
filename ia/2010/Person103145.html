john simley’s Summary

John brings 20 years of experience in corporate communications, marketing, economic analysis and cross-functional project management. A strong communications strategist and tactician, he also possesses substantial financial background and experience with brand management, real estate, manufacturing processes and statistical quality control. John earned his Bachelor’s degree in Economics and Asian Studies and holds a Master’s Degree in International Commercial Policy from the University of Chicago. He speaks Chinese, with additional capability in German and Spanish.

At Walmart, he has served the Finance, Legal and Information Systems Divisions, as well as Labor Relations, Employment Practices, Tax and Compliance. He is respected for his command of subjects, methodical approach and ability to foster productive relationships with colleagues, business partners and news media.

In previous experience at U.S. Cellular, John pioneered innovative media strategies to position the company for growth in a crowded industry. During five years at Home Depot, he was a specialist in business development, including real estate, environmental programs and marketing communications, in addition to national news crisis management. Earlier in his career, John's responsibilities included Investor Relations functions at Stone Container and Zenith Electronics and economic analysis at The Ameritech Corporation.

John is also an author of several reference book titles in business and economics, including The International Directory of Company Histories and The Directory of Consumer Brands (St. James Press) and The Encyclopedia of Business (Gale).
