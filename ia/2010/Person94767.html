Jennifer Brooks Morris’s Summary

Interior Textile Colorist, Stylist, Editor, Designer, Merchandiser, and Marketer with over 30 years of experience at the Wholesale, Converting/Importing, Mill and Retail levels. A Textile Stylist who utilizes the combination of both Creative and Sales/Marketing disciplines to provide Clients with the best products: those that are beautiful, desirable, and that Sell. "Creativity that Inspires and Captivates, Colors that fuel Imaginations, Merchandising and Marketing that drive Sales".

Jennifer Brooks Morris’s Specialties:

Interior Fabrics and Trimmings: jacquards, dobbies, multicolored textures, solids, embroideried fabrics of all weights and fiber contents, velvets, prints, sheers and trimmings.
Target Industries: Interior Textile Converters, Importers, Mills, Wholesalers, Retailers and Party/Event Fabic Companies.
