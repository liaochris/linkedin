Moshe Kaplan’s Summary

Mr Kaplan is an expert in design, development, infrastructure and security of high load software projects. He specializes in forming technological solutions best fitting business requirements and market needs in a changing world based on Agile methodologies.
Mr Kaplan has over 10 years of experience in providing software solutions to the academic, corporate and defense sectors and he is known to be a cloud evangelist.
He Previously served as a department head in a top IT unit in the IDF and was responsible to several cutting edge projects. Mr Kaplan holds M.Sc in Engineering and B.Sc in Physics and Computer Science, all of from Tel Aviv University.
Moshe is a seasoned lecturer in the fields of software system project management as well as cloud technologies.

Some of the technologies I feel comfort with:
Sharding, Scaling out, Performance tuning and boosting, Software Engineering: UML, Multi threading, N-Tier, DB (ERD), Client/Server, Networking
