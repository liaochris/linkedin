Mike Leeds’s Summary

Increasing YOUR sales results is my specialty!

You will benefit from over 29 years of coaching, sales, and sales management experience. Mike has a track record of consistently exceeding sales goals, improving team results and improving individual results – from sales rookies to sales veterans. The measurement of sales is essentially a black & white process - you're either selling more, or you're not. Mike’s clients get results!

The world's greatest athletes use coaches to enhance their performance - so can you. Let's make achieving your sales goals a reality!

Mike's defintion of sales: "Helping people by solving problems and fulfilling needs." Sales is a consultative process, and if you do it right - you will have customers for life.

Author of the new book "Sales Tips in Under 140 Characters". (Click on the website link above for more info.)

Prior to launching his own business in 2002, Mike's direct sales experience ranges from a small family-owned business to some of the largest corporations in the USA.

Mike Leeds’s Specialties:

Expertise and Mastery of sales, and the sales process covers all all industries. My corporate experience ranges from the industrial supply industry to the high-tech & telecommunication industry. My client industry list is even more diverse, and can be viewed on my website.
