Chris Werben’s Summary

I enjoy helping people be more successful in their endeavors through the thoughtful use of innovative technology. I believe that constant and effective communication throughout an organization will bring out the best in people which will result in the greatest results for any organization. By extension, increased communication and interaction in a community will bring forth the best from that community. This dynamic flow of information can be used to attract new members to the community, foster a trust relationship within the community and stimulate product and service improvements that can lead to increased sales for businesses and fundraising for non-profits.

  • Co-President

  • Senior Vice President

    Banc of America Specialist, Inc.

    (Public Company; BAC; Banking industry)

    December 2004 — February 2007 (2 years 3 months)

    Established a new unit within the company to design and build an algorithmic trading system to provide automated market maker functionality within the context of NYSE Euronext's move to an electronic automated trading platform for listed equities.

  • Board Member

    Project Reach Youth

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    2000 — 2007 (7 years )

    Served as technology consultant on the Board of Directors after establishing a web presence for this Brooklyn, NY community based organization. This non-profit served low income youth and families by providing education, training and counseling services. These include a family literacy program for pre-K children, younger siblings and parents, citizenship classes, ESOL (English for Speakers of Other Languages) classes, counseling, parenting, home visits, after school learning centers for elementary school children, an after school conflict resolution program, and an after school program for junior high school immigrant youth. Also served as co-chair of the Events committee. (Merged with Lutheran Community Health Centers)

  • Managing Director

    NYSE

    (Public Company; NYX; Financial Services industry)

    November 1997 — December 2004 (7 years 2 months)

    Product manager for the NYSE's core trading platform used for the execution of 99% of all equity trades conducted on the trading floor. Managed implementation of enhancements including introduction of automatic execution of trades, publication of depth of book market data, and several usability enhancements through user-centered design that facilitated ever-increasing order and trade volumes. Introduced the first web-based application on the trading floor which provided proprietary trade data for specialist firms to monitor their trading activity, risk exposure and P&L in real-time on a stock, trader and firm level.

  • Manager

    SIAC
