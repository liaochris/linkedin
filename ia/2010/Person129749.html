Madeleine Boerma’s Summary

Approach:
Integral approach based on easy & ready to use model: Power of Inspiration, Creation, Sensation & Realisation© --> check out my website for more information!

Vision:
Over the years, I have become a firm believer in the simple fact that if you want to achieve true and lasting professional transformations, personal transformation is a necessary means to this end. This means that traditional management based on facts & figures no longer suffices and a new kind of leaders is required. Leaders who understands that successful transformations include both high quality process, project and result management as well as honest dialogue, authentic and respectful relations between people.

Why me?
"We have to be the change we want to see in this world" said Ghandi. The combination of my firm belief in this on top of my professional and personal experiences!

Madeleine Boerma’s Specialties:

Authentic communication, dialogue, teambuilding and 15 years of experience in process management & project management. Areas: customer care, billing, IT change, problem & incident management, sales, service delivery, service assurance, service development processes, project management
