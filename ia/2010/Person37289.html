  • Developer

    Les Films des Tournelles

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    July 1997 — August 1997 (2 months)

    Developed the web site for the film “ La Patinoire ” (The Ice Rink) by Jean-Philippe Toussaint and Les Films des Tournelles (production) in collaboration with FranceNet sponsored by Miko.

    The objective was to provide the public with a vision of a real-time filming. The site included the presentation of the film crew and actors (CV included), real-time broadcasting of shooting images through several cameras placed on the film set. The end of the day also brought the visitors a video of the shooting carried out by the film set photographer. Prepared under Avid Videoshop then broadcasted with RealMedia technology.

    The web site was a great success with many press and TV relay (Canal+, M6 …). A poster contest at the international level was also organized.

    Awards: Yahoo Net Events ! (cool site to visit), NB News Editor's Choice, and one of three finalist for the best audiovisual communication award.
