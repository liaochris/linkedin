Craig Tadlock’s Summary

A highly motivated and effective technology leader with 10+ years of experience helping companies succeed by building and growing quality software products and teams. In-depth experience in a range of disciplines including software architecture, team management and leadership, business development, project management and operations in a variety of company sizes and structures. Industry experience includes traditional and online advertising, media, entertainment, e-commerce and fulfillment systems, events management, geospatial, mobile and online services. Focus is on delivering high-quality products quickly to market which provide significant revenue and competitive advantage to the business while building efficient and agile teams and processes while operating in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.

• Started and grew a successful UX and technology consulting agency to 100+ consultants.

• Hands-on experience with all major technology stacks; .NET/SQL, LAMP and Java/Oracle.

• Cultivated and maintain a network of top-performing individuals and partners across the US West coast.
