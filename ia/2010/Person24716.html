Blair Richwood is BR@ScriptNotes.com’s Summary

Drawing on nearly 20 years of A-list experience in MPTV, I personally provide confidential literary development for Writers, Producers, Directors, Creative Executives, and Corporate Content Providers for all motion picture, television, cable and alternate screen outlets on an as-needed, no-strings-attached basis.

My Development-by-the-Hour services include premise positioning, pitch coaching, outline drafting, treatment writing... through story evaluation, script editing, table reads, talent and marketing hits lists... through dialogue polish, punch-up in production and wild lines in post -- to creating a premise and story series for your Corporate Client's Product or Service -- confidentially.

Blair Richwood is BR@ScriptNotes.com’s Specialties:

As one of the few consultants who's been on studio releases from concept -- release, I bring hands-on MPTV/stage experience to my clients with support in:
CREATIVE: script edits/dialogue polish/detailed notes; help execute studio notes; produce table readings; etc.
BUSINESS: sell sheets, marketing tools; etc.
PRODUCTIVITY: keep teams on schedule; coach & deliver pitches;
workshops in how to do notes; maintain script logs/admin for creatives; etc.
