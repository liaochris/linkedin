Morgan Blake’s Summary

MBA candidate for the class of 2011 at MIT Sloan School of Management. Prior to business school I helped grow Boston-based SDG Management Intelligence, the first U.S. office of the European Business Intelligence consulting firm SDG Group, where I became the youngest Partner in SDG Group. In 2009, I was named Managing Director of SDG Management Intelligence and led the team to its most profitable period in firm history. I'm now enrolled in the Entrepreneurship and Innovation Track at MIT Sloan, where I hold leadership positions in IdeaStorm, the MIT Innovation Club and the MIT VC Conference committee. I pursue a range of interests in areas such as high-tech and online media, and maintain a passion for graphic and web design. I hold a BS in Business Administration from Babson College, and remain a Member of the Board at SDG Management Intelligence.

Morgan Blake’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurship, consulting, graphic and web design.
