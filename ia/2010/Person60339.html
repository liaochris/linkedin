Douglas S. Diggle, MBA’s Summary

Mr. Diggle joined Lufthansa Systems the world’s leading IT service provider for the airline and aviation industry to further develop client relations. With his comprehensive background leading business operations, forecasting and strategic planning from various cruise lines he will promote the growing new business portfolio in the Americas for the Lufthansa Group.

Lufthansa Systems grew out of the IT department of Lufthansa, despite the company's origins it does not only provide services in the aviation sector, but all kinds of IT services from cruise line mega yacht network infrastructure to application development, implementation, consulting, passenger services, cargo handling, flight operations, reliable 24-hour operation, aircraft maintenance and repair. Before joining Lufthansa, Douglas developed software applications for the United States Government.

His educational background consists of four college degrees including a Bachelor in Business Finance and a Master of International Business Administration. He is an integral part of our global client support and sales team located from Frankfurt, Hamburg and Downtown Miami.

Douglas brings management and senior management experience for large scale technology integration projects including six sigma process improvement, cost reduction, ratings improvement, revenue enhancement, intercultural marketing communications as well as management of technical professionals, vendors and ship yard contractors.

Douglas offers exceptional track record of successfully completing projects and is well versed in various cultural customs and extensive world travel.

His dedicated work ethic, multi-tasking, and bottom line cost savings helps him leverage new information technology solutions while controlling and reducing expenses. He has a strong reputation for innovation as an agent of change and received the Lufthansa Best of Sales award.
