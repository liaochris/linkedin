Dave King’s Summary

For the nearly 20 years, Dave has captivated audiences from coast to coast in virtually every performance medium available – especially television. He is a dynamic, funny, articulate and charismatic African American male with a loyal following.

As an actor, Dave appeared in several hit television programs including The Cosby Show, Law and Order, Kate and Allie, Hack and Party Down. He also had roles on All My Children, as heart throb Mike Gordon, and Another World, as the tough-talking chief of detectives, Billy Cooper. In addition, Dave also appeared in dozens of commercials and did well over a hundred voice-overs.

Dave began his career as a Host/Anchor, working for HBO Entertainment News. He followed that with a stint as the host of Nine Broadcast Plaza on WWOR in New York. For nearly 14 years, Dave was one of the top Program Hosts on QVC, Dave interviewed superstars in the world of music, television, motion pictures, and sports – a list that includes Diana Ross, Lionel Ritchie, Suze Orman, Shania Twain, Donald Trump, Neil Diamond, Larry King, Vanessa Williams and Joan Rivers.

Most recently, Dave has taken his hosting expertise to ShopNBC, the #1 premiere lifestyle electronic retailer in the world. He also can be seen around the country as Dr. Cool Tech introducing America to the latest and coolest consumer electronics items available.

All tolled, Dave has logged more than 10000 hours of live television. Live - no cue cards, no teleprompter, no second takes. That’s more than Jay Leno and David Letterman combined!

Dave holds a Bachelor of Science degree from Penn State University, where he played Free Safety for the legendary Joe Paterno. He has three children, Dylan, his older son, Devin his younger son, and Demi, his daughter.

In addition to his many broadcast duties, Dave works extensively with The NFL and is a member of the Board of Directors for The Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.

Dave King’s Specialties:

Television and live show Host/Interviewer, voiceover artist, and entertainment professional. Talk shows, morning shows, award show Master of Ceremonies, live home shopping. Coach for CEOs and Senior Management in public and motivational speaking, sales meeting presentations and on-camera. Coaching television and media personalities, inventors and guest hosts appearing on HSN and QVC. Successfully marketing products on HSN and QVC.
