Bob Cherry’s Summary

I have over 25 years of corporate experience from startup to Fortune 100 corporations leading the way for quality improvement through a variety of methods. Utilizing this experience with my music company and news network, I have focused on using Business Intelligence methods in Music News and Promotion.

Quality goals are focused on the cost of quality (or the lack of quality) and how a quality program can improve the return on investment for the enterprise. Improving customer perception through quality service, and measuring service level agreements to insure forward progress of all objectives are being met are all related. By using Business Intelligence tools, I am able to quickly filter the noise and establish meaningful trend analysis to aid in future objectives.

Business Intelligence (BI) isn't a tool -- it is a stategy. A strategy that is business and not IT driven. BI turns data and information to "knowledge." By using KPIs and data from a variety of sources, the BI Tools enable the business to access not just information but, to actually access the knowledge based on that information. Current and immediate changes in market trends become available but, more importantly, usable in the business decision framework.

The market won't wait on you to do the work the hard way. To be competitive, you need the edge that BI can give you. Business goals and objectives drive BI and IT implements them. The database is too complex and requires complicated tools to use effectively. BI is the filter that allows you to see through the noise so you can get to the real information enabling quality decisions in a timely manner.

BI is the enabler to achieve quality and a competitive edge. Feel free to ask me about BI opportunities you may be considering.
