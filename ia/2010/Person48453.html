Dan Rust’s Summary

As Senior Director of Sales Effectiveness for WellCare Health Plans, my role is to partner with associates at every level of the business, from frontline salespeople to the C-suite, to identify opportunities for driving sales growth.

I avoid one-size-fits-all approaches and work with each market to define and execute a sales enhancement plan that may include new sales strategies, innovative value propositions, sales training, performance management or sales leadership development.

In addition I have a broad range of resources in my consulting "tool kit" which are utilyzed when needed: strategic sales planning process, sales forecasting, "dashboards" to monitor results, online skill assessments, e-learning/m-learning courseware, sales coaching and leadership training, and a network of trusted and respected OD colleagues whose expertise I leverage when needed.

As a Sales Effectiveness Consultant for Frontline Learning (since 1991) I have worked with hundreds of organizations and have a proven track record of strategic and tactical success.

Here are just a few of the training programs I have authored:

• Sell Up in a Down Economy
• REAL Communication™
• REAL Selling™
• Professional Selling SkillMap™
• Customer Service SkillMap™

Deep expertise in consultative selling methods to drive business growth in complex and highly competitive markets.

Successful design and execution of blended sales training, integrating assessments, workshops, field coaching, e-learning, web casts, podcasting, audio CDs, video and other media.

World class sales trainer and coach with exceptional presentation and facilitation skills.
