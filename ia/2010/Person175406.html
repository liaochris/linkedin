Robert R Yamashita’s Summary

Co-Founder/Managing Director of a strategic advisory and investment services firm focused on the new media ecosystem, with 15 years as a technology/business executive at Media & Entertainment (M&E) and Consumer Electronics (CE) companies.

Performance-driven, visionary and collaborative leader of teams that first guided DivX into CE (key to its $625M IPO in 2006); brought world’s first network DVD player to market (Digital5/Zoran, 2002); helped generate over $300M of new revenue for Sony (1998-2000); and introduced a new video format for C-Cube (1996-97).

Deep practical knowledge of digital media business and technology, gained through over 15 years of experience in roles, including consulting, product line management, and marketing.
