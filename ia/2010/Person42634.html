Coleen Bryan’s Summary

Coleen worked in Outreach and Admissions for over five years as an Outreach Coordinator for the University of California, Santa Barbara and the University of California, Berkeley. She has been responsible for providing admissions advice to parents, students, teachers, and organizations throughout northern and southern California for the Early Academic Outreach Program (EAOP). Additionally, she has taught test preparation academies, college application workshops, and college preparation seminars for schools in Orange County and the San Francisco Bay Area. Her involvement in outreach allowed her to gain valuable knowledge and insight into the admissions requirements of the University of California and other competitive colleges.

Coleen has had extensive training in editing college applications, analyzing college essays for admission, securing financial aid for students, conducting college preparation workshops, and providing individualized admissions consulting. Additionally, she maintains her college admissions expertise by attending college fairs, participating in professional development opportunities, and touring colleges. She has been a college admissions consultant in Orange County for over 5 years.

Coleen Bryan is a member of several national professional counseling organizations, including the Western Association for College Admission Counseling (WACAC) and the Higher Education Consultants Association (HECA). As a community member she has served on the PTA and as a site-council representative in Ladera Ranch to increase her involvement in education community in Orange County.

Coleen Bryan values higher education and looks forward to sharing her years of expertise in college admissions counseling in Orange County with the clients of College Materials to help them attain their educational goals.
