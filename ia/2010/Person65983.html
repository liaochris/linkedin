Eric J. Gall’s Summary

BUSINESS INTERMEDIARY SERVICES

FBX guides sellers through the sales process. All services are confidential.

- Prepare your business for sale.
- Establish the value of your business.
- List and market your business on the appropriate forums.
- Qualify potential buyers.
- Get your business to closing and complete the sale.

FBX helps buyers find and acquire a business.

- Professional searches.
- Qualify large number of sellers.
- Identify and evaluate financing alternatives.
- Get the business acquired.

SMALL & MID-MARKET BUSINESS CONSULTING

FBX consulting helps you prepare your business to achieve top dollar at time of sale. Benefits may include:

- Improved financial documentation clarity.
- Increased revenues through business specific marketing programs.
- Reduced costs through more efficient sourcing.
- Reduced tax burden.
- Access to quick and easy sources of capital.
- Reduced debt service.
- Revenue from sales of idle assets.
- Reduced inventory holding costs.
- Time savings from maintenance program for due diligence materials.
- Well documented Confidential Memorandum to market the sale of the business.
