Pek Pongpaet’s Summary

PEK PONGPAET is an Internet entrepreneur whose approach strikes a harmonic blend of technology and philosophy. He is a Lead Interactive Developer at Roundarch and a Senior Martial Arts Instructor at Chicago Wushu. Pek is an avid blogger, a highly sought-after speaker in Web 2.0 technologies and a huge proponent of Social Media in general. Pek has positioned himself as a true agent of change for progressive companies wishing to innovate, virally market, and build engaging and unforgettable experiences on the Internet.

Pek is an elite provider of riching Internet marketing applications and an aficionado of the interactive experience whose astonishing versatility in online media provides measureable and sustainable value to his professional clientele. He is a deep thinker in emerging technologies and user interfaces and boasts an impressive online portfolio encompassing graphic design, video, motion capture, gaming, connectivity and real-time streaming. Having served on both the creative and development sides, Pek draws his inspiration from multiple sources (e.g., flash animation, industrial design, gesture-based computing and augmented reality) and is at the forefront of advancing applications that enhance website processes.

Pek is also an accomplished and dedicated martial artist who trains others, has competed internationally, and has judged participants in several competitions. His passion for Chinese martial arts stems from childhood and finds its corollary in every aspect of his life. He read the Wuxia novels, watched the television shows, and studied the moves of the great Kung Fu masters. His dedication and commitment led to motion capture roles in the Mortal Kombat video game franchise, television exposure, and an opportunity to participate in Theatricks, a one-of-a-kind live martial arts theater show. Pek continues to be actively involved in the martial arts community, primarily in a teaching capacity.
