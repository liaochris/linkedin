Paula Kvarnberg’s Summary

Paula is a seasoned executive recruiter and human resources consultant with 20 years of progressive experience. She progressed from an Account Executive to an Executive Director overseeing 30 executive recruiters and regional managers. In the first 10 years of executive search some of her accolades include 7 company records for highest personal production, numerous “Producer of the Month” awards, “Largest Producer of Repeat Business”, and numerous “Top Recruiter of the Year” awards.

In 1997 Paula moved to the corporate side of employment with OutSource International. After many promotions she became the Director of Human Resources reporting to the President/CEO/Chairman of the Board. There she oversaw Employment, Employee Relations, Training, Compensation, and Benefits. Some of her accomplishments included reducing turnover by 22%, reducing EEOC claims by 12%, reducing cost per hire by 10%, reducing outside recruiting costs by over $250K annually, creating an employee referral program reducing cost per hire by 15%, leading the development process for behavioral psychological assessments and performing job analysis on corporate positions saving over $20K SG&A, and developing a training curriculum and certification training program for all positions.

In 2001 Paula founded Search America, LLC to serve clients with higher level executive and professional search needs. Search America, LLC serves clients in a broad spectrum of industries with emerging growth companies and recruits across many functional lines. Its search process best serves clients' needs, allowing it to fully understand the uniqueness of every client and the requirements of every individual search assignment. Search America not only strives to find the most ideal candidates for its client companies but to find the most ideal people for their corporate cultures.

Paula is AIRS Certified in Advanced Internet Recruiting Strategies and SHL Certified to perform Job Analysis and Assessment Analysis.
