Sanjeev Himachali, GPHR, Certified MBTI’s Summary

I am having more than ten years of experience in various facets of HR. I have experience of working in different types of industries (Financial Services; IT Enabled [BPO’s and Call Centers]; and Software Development) and cultures within and outside India.
