STEVE LEWIS’s Summary

STEVEN H. LEWIS is the President of Lewis Floor & Home, a tightly-held, family-owned and operated business that has been serving the flooring and home decorating needs of individuals and businesses in the Chicago area since 1954. Steve provides leadership, stability, and strategic direction to the firm and is widely recognized as an authority in the flooring and home furnishings arenas.

Steve was raised in the flooring business. One might say it’s in his blood. His entrepreneurial instincts were kindled at the age of 10, when he begun to accompany his father to the showroom on weekends. After earning his B.S. in Accounting in 1979, and his J.D. in 1982, Steve took a position as a corporate and tax attorney. In 1984, he eschewed the legal career in favor of the family business, an already established player in the retail carpet marketplace. Steve revamped the business model, set his sights high, and guided the firm to prominence in full service flooring in the highly competitive Chicago market. Ultimately, the firm expanded to a second location and into other aspects of home decoration (kitchen, bath, furnishings, etc.) and, in 2008, became Lewis Floor & Home.

Steve’s approach is highly consultative and customer-focused. He sets the bar high and asks his staff to do the same. “We try to take the fear and anxiety out of the process and instill confidence in our customers,” says Steve. “We set realistic expectations and do our best to exceed them.”

Steve is an active voice in his trade whose tireless work has affected change. In 2001, he was recognized by the Chicago Floor Covering Association for his efforts in revising the legislation in the State of Illinois regarding the Sales & Use Tax on floor coverings. Lewis Floor & Home is also a leader in green flooring and home décor products.

Steve is civic-minded, community-involved, and a passionate philanthropist. He currently serves on the Board of Directors for the Cancer Wellness Center.
