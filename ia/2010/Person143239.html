Michael Ornst’s Summary

Utilizing the comprehensive Integral Framework I assist individuals and organizations in developing an inspiring vision, based in human potential and market understanding, resulting in a clear, concise, and evocative roadmap.

Out of the vision I find the story that speaks directly to the curiosity and listening of the audience moving them to truly embrace a larger vision that they can make their own and want to share.

What is the story, how is it different and how do we ensure it is meaningful and consequential to the audience?

My specialty is using mixed-methods research to find a wide range of disciplines, and fields of study, particularly the emerging fields of human developmental study, to deeply understand all the dimensions of the client, vision and audience. The result is a comprehensive and effective ideation of which story elements and media arts, from traditional to transmedia storytelling, are required to build a consistent, succinct, sustainable and impacting media production: Visual experiences that conjure motivation and inspiration in people everywhere .
