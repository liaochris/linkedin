Daniel Csoka’s Summary

Daniel Csoka is a visionary entrepreneur and experienced CEO with deep knowledge and expertise in wireless technology and banking systems. He has successfully developed complex patent-pending international ecosystems of mobile network operators, financial institutions, electronics transactions processors, money transfer operators, retailers, merchants and POS networks. He has provided a broad range of marketing, strategic and industry oriented consulting services to mobile operators, equipment vendors and content providers worldwide in the wireless industry. He is proficient at identifying new opportunities and is well-practiced at bringing those visions to market. He consistently cultivates, nurtures, maintains, converts and monetizes relationships into measurable results. He is adept at managing, encouraging and leading people as a coach and mentor. His firm grasp of financials combined with acute business acumen consistently adds value to the bottom line.

Csoka is a recognized wireless expert and frequent industry speaker and has chaired, moderated or spoken at numerous wireless conferences and forums worldwide. He has been quoted on various issues related to wireless communications in many industry related business publications including, among others, Fierce Wireless, RCR Wireless, Wireless Week, Wireless Review, the Chicago Tribune, The Prepaid Press, Billing & OSS World, Payments News and Business Week.

Csoka graduated from Northwestern University and is a Rhodes Scholar Finalist and National Merit Finalist. In his spare time he enjoys an active career as a concert violinist appearing as the concertmaster of various orchestras including the Detroit Civic Symphony Orchestra. Csoka has performed throughout North America and Europe for such luminaries as President Bush, President Reagan, and Pope John Paul II.
