Omar Christidis’s Summary

An entrepreneur with a BA and MBA from Yale University and a background in consulting (Booz & Co) and finance (private wealth management - family office).

Co-Founder and Director of Development of the Yale Arab Alumni Association (YAAA), a community of over 200 Yale alumni and affiliates with an active interest in the Arab World. Managed the fundraising for YAAA's first conference in October 2009, which featured sponsorships from Qatar Foundation, Hariri Foundation, CCC, Booz & Co, McKinsey & Co and others.

Currently Vice President of the family-owned business, IBAG, which has 15 years experience in corporate events management. Passionate about internet and working on organizing ArabNet, the premier international conference for the Arab web industry.
