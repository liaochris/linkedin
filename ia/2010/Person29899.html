Bruce Newman’s Summary

I enjoy helping companies achieve their goals. Developing creating solutions – thinking out of the box – and achieving results is my passion. In the process, I improve their productivity and net income.

For 11 years, The Productivity Institute has been providing companies with outstanding consultants who have the experience, knowledge and communication skills to meet a company’s specific consulting requirements. Many people claim to be experts but in actuality, few are truly outstanding. By locating and recognizing those truly outstanding consultants, I supply our clients with experts who produce exceptional results.

Since early 2008, I have been actively involved with social media and have helped numerous companies develop effective social media strategies, policies and brands. It’s a blast to develop a successful strategy that can greatly reduce a company’s marketing costs while producing far superior results to their more traditional campaigns.

Our new course: SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY – includes a 50 minute streaming video on key social policy considerations, a complete social media policy template and a real world example of social media gone wrong. Every company needs a social media policy.

Two additional new services, SOCIAL MEDIA STARTER PACK and MAINTENANCE & MANAGEMENT have also been very well received.

All are available from our website.

In December 2008, I started a content-is-king newsletter that went to 200 friends. It has received many accolades and grown rapidly to over 8,700 opt-in subscribers. Everyone who joins my LinkedIn network will automatically receive it. (You can opt out at any time). The current newsletter can be accessed from our website. I’m always looking for writers so if you would like to become one – and you write well, please contact me.

My PC Mirror is a really cool and unique product that I designed and had built. I have sold tens of thousands of these mirrors to companies of all sizes.
