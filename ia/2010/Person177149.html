Ron Burgess’s Summary

I am a Chief Marketing Officer for hire. I work for smaller companies with under 300 employees; companies of this size rarely have the budget to hire executive level marketing managers. It is a perfect reason to use a consultant as a part-time executive.

I work directly with the president or owner and the top financial person to develop strategic plans for their market niche. I have a full team to execute those plans.

I have been fortunate to work for clients a very long time. My average relationship is 7 ½ years, the longest. . .19 years.

With two and a half decades as a consultant, and well over 30 years as a manager, I’ve worked with and studied hundreds of small businesses and guided most to the top three in their market niche.
