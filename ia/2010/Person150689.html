Kris de Leon’s Summary

My overall purpose is to help small business owners and entrepreneurs strengthen their businesses, which would help revive the economy and create new job opportunities. I am doing this through two main avenues: Internet Marketing and Leadership Development.

I consult businesses on how to properly use Internet marketing not only to drive high volumes of traffic to their website, but also increase conversions rates and customer retention. This is key to long term business success. These strategies can include a combination of:

Free - Blogs, Articles, Videos, Social Media (Facebook, Twitter, Linked In, YouTube, etc.)

Paid - Pay-per-Click (PPC) through Google, Facebook, MySpace, etc.

In addition, I coach entrepreneurs on developing a winning mindset, focusing on leadership development, vision creation, goal setting, and ongoing personal development. I then coach them how to develop their personal brand and attract customers, prospects and opportunities to them.

By combining both a strong mindset, with the proper Internet marketing skills, businesses strand a stronger chance of thriving in this economy.

On the side, I'm involved in network marketing and affiliate marketing. I also enjoy philanthropic activities, such as raising funds for a chosen charity and volunteering.
