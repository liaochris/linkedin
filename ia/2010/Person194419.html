Steve Thornton’s Summary

Located in Austin, Texas. My primary responsibility at Spansion has been in the Fab25 Photolithography module where I lead a team of 25-35 highly technically trained individuals in the manufacture of flash memory semiconductors using I-line and Deep Ultraviolet photolithography manufacturing methods.

Often, I am temporarily diverted away from that role to lead strategic projects to improve manufacturing quality, efficiency, productivity and yield. Currently leading the World Class Line Yield effort in Fab 25 Litho.

-5 time Author of Merit - SPIE, IEEE, Sematech, Future Fab International, Interface.
-7 time Spotlight Award winner.
-3 time Employee of the Month.
-Guest Lecturer on Semiconductor Processing at The University of Texas -
Schools of Electrical, Computer, and Chemical Engineering
