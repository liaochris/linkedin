Cristina Fossati’s Summary

Life and Health Medical Underwriting We offer A Myriad of Pre-Qualification Medical Underwriting Reviews

MediSummary has carefully reviewed the feedback of our clients and have created a new joint service with ISC Services, a leading Life Expectancy provider, licensed in Florida. The service will provide:

CLIENT WILL RECEIVE 4 SEPARATE REVIEWS
1.Full review of the medicals, completed by MediSummary
2.APS SUMMARY, completed by MediSummary, (That can be used by LE provider of your choice)
3.ISC Informal LE- Used for prequalification of case
4.MediSummary Underwriting Assessment- This review will provide feedback on the cases noting any case recommendation on missing records, exams and lack of updated specialty records to name a few

Roger Tafoya of ISC states, "ISC will have an underwriter review the information on the APS summary provided by MediSummary to ensure co-morbidities are handled correctly and set the foundation for a consistent LE if a standard LE is ordered later".

Cristina Fossati of MediSummary said, "I'm excited to provide a much needed service that our industry is lacking. I believe certain products such as the automated LE was headed in the right direction however nothing can compare to an actual review of the medicals. What may be severe to a broker may not be considered severe to an underwriter. Our goal is to save our clients money on LE’s and provide them and their client with realistic expectations with an accurate preliminary LE".

This is a huge milestone for our industry! This new combined service with two leading life settlement service providers will reduce significant cost as well as set client expectations for our clients and consumers!

Cristina Fossati’s Specialties:
