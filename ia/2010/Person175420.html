Rob Farrow’s Summary

Consumer branding and marketing specialist, with over 20 years of experience building, designing and implementing marketing programs. Experienced in working with and in all types of media including, interactive, traditional, viral and social. My specialty has been developing consumer engagement programs that position brands with their key consumers through a combination of events and a blend of interactive and traditional media. Strong technical background with an emphasis on UI, design and the integration of social platforms.

Rob Farrow’s Specialties:

Developing and implementing cost effective, multifaceted marketing strategies with a focus on launching new brands and creating highly engaging consumer programs for existing and mature brands.
