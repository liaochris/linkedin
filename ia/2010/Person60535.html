Duane Lee’s Summary

I started Top Notch back in 1997 in Canada, and expanded into Panama 4 years ago. Currently we represent hotel/resort developments in over 20 countries as well as commercial/mining opportunities. We have a diverse network of people assisting and working with us. Our primary focus is on working in Latin America in catching up with the large amount of business and leisure travelers, where there is a lack of hotels and resorts. Panama is seeing an increase in traffic and we have numerous beach and city hotels, condo/hotel, resort, marina, golf course, etc... developments in or ready to begin construction. In addition I now have founded IHM Group with my partner Cameron and we are working on acquiring properties to develop in Panama and Australia.
