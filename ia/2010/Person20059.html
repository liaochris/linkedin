Kristen McElveen’s Summary

Currently, I recently opened my own private practice, Bare Medicine, in Springvale, ME. I share a building with an acupuncturist, a few massage therapists, a colon hydrotherapist and hopefully an osteopath in the months to come. This integrative setting has always been a goal of mine as well as relocating to New England! I'm very excited for this new journey!

In Fall of 2009, I ended my second-year residency at the National College of Natural Medicine, supervising teaching shifts at our various clinics around Portland, OR. I had also worked several private shifts at our primary clinic, Natural Health Center in downtown Portland. I provided a general practice, including but not limited to women's health, IV therapy, energetic medicine (homeopathy, flower essences, etc), nutrition and weight loss and bodywork.

Check out my website for current schedules and descriptions of treatments!
