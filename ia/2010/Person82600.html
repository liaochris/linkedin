Holly Thomas’s Summary

Public speaker, writer, and instructor on public speaking, financial planning, behavioral finance, and economics.

Although she is a Certified Financial Planner and holds a Master’s degree in Economics, Holly does not touch her clients’ money or derive income from their investment channels.

Her firm works primarily on a retainer basis to identify short- and long-term goals, then match those goals with strategy, and review the results on an ongoing basis.

Once a strategy has been defined, Holly coordinates a client’s professionals: attorneys, bankers, investment managers, insurance agents, HR departments, charitable giving officers, and other links in the chain of her clients’ financial structure, recommending highly qualified and respected individuals and firms where appropriate. Holly receives no third-party compensation for referrals, and does not pay referral fees to other professionals.

In summary, Holly acts as her client’s Personal CFO, assisting each client in developing a big picture view of both personal and business finances.
