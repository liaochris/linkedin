Jenny Coupe’s Summary

A process-driven and results-oriented marketing professional with a history of launching successful buyer-centric (educating v. selling) demand generation programs. With over 20 years in the Silicon Valley and 16 years specifically in B2B Marketing, my background includes a successful and proven track record at large established companies as well as several start-ups.

My philosophy: The marketing team's goal isn't to push a product down the prospect's throat, but instead to keep wafting about in the prospect's line of vision through a wide variety of media (webinars/webcasts, social media, white papers, content sponsorship/syndication, press mentions, direct mail, search, email, events/seminars, etc.) until a prospect finally raises their hand, asking for help with an immediate particular need.

Jenny Coupe’s Specialties:

Outbound marketing champion: online/web marketing, demand creation, lead generation/nurturing, lead lifecycle management, search engine optimization and marketing, social media marketing, web production and management, direct marketing, event management, public/analyst relations, partner/channel/reseller marketing, and branding/positioning/messaging. Vendors of choice include: Marketo, Eloqua, Salesforce, Apogee Search, Omniture, Demandbase, Salesify, Enquiro, TechTarget, ON24, Trackle, etc;
