Rachel R. Reagan’s Summary

Born and raised in Kansas City, Missouri Rachel Reagan’s interest in fitness began in childhood. Her greatest influence was her father and accompanying him to International Fitness Center (now Bally Total Fitness) and early morning workouts with her mother doing the 20 Minute Workout and It Figures programs on television.
Being a devoted wife and mother, here in the Park Hill School District she is a highly motivated fitness enthusiast. Soon after moving to the northland in the late 90’s she began her career as a group fitness instructor with Bally Total Fitness then brought her experience to several fitness facilities here in the northland. Rachel is a certified exercise instructor through Aerobics and Fitness Association of America. She also holds certifications in the nations most popular exercise formats Pilates, Turbokick ®, Yoga, and Zumba®.
She enjoys sharing what she knows and doing what she can to help others “find what moves you”.
