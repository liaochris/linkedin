  • Co-Founder

    FadMashion.com

    (Online Media industry)

    2005 — Present (5 years )

    STAY TUNED FOR REDESIGN w/ BUSINESS MODEL Q3/Q4 2010

    FUNDING OVERVIEW
    Currently we are voluntarily self-funded. We have respectfully declined funding offers from multiple angels in preference of a strategic individual investment specifically from the worlds of fashion and social media. Each founding member contributes a valuable service: programming, design, business development, member development, and we work remotely; zero overhead. We feel investors should add practical value beyond funding through a network of relevant contacts; we are niche and the appropriate investor's offering should reflect our aspirations. Relaunch will include revenue model with earnings expected to begin Q4 2010 - Q1 2011. Please enquire privately for further information.

    ***

    FadMashion (www.fadmashion.com) "Emerging Fashion Network", beta launched 2009, is a professional industry network for emerging designers, retailers, and media; and a global style resource for fashion consumers. As of Jan 2010 our community includes 400 global emerging fashion designer portfolios and 4000 original designs to browse, as well as retailers, media and enthusiasts.

    Our immediate goal is to achieve critical mass as a powerful hybrid B2B/B2C network for global emerging fashion. Our long term goal is to enable a media conglomerate to tap into social media and emerging global fashion.

    We enjoy partnership with nymag.com - one of the most trafficked fashion related sites online with 2.2M monthly uniques - which features FadMashion designers in a bi-weekly recurring spotlight section called "Talent Scout". We are currently exploring additional partnerships and revenue models.
