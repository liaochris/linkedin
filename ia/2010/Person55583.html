De Yarrison’s Summary

Leadership and Personal Coach, Team Builder, Facilitator of Courageous Conversations, and Certified ChangeWorks Practitioner with broad experience in many aspects of organizational development. Work directly with business owners and executives desiring sustainable change within their organizations. Focus on developing transformational leaders and positive cultures with high levels of employee engagement. In addition to my work with business owners and leaders I am passionate about partnering with individuals seeking growth or change in their personal lives. I find it especially fulfilling to coach/guide people interested in simplifying their lives and reducing their impact on the planet (environmentally speaking).

De Yarrison’s Specialties:

Coaching leaders as they embrace change, drive business results, and seek personal fulfillment. Coaching individuals as they seek growth and positive change in their lives. Bringing people together and facilitating courageous conversations. Building cohesive, trusting teams. Passion for and wide knowledge of sustainable communities, including healthy food, simplicity, and reducing our environmental footprint.
