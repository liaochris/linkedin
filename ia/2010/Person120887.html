Lance Polikov’s Summary

I am an accomplished recruiter with a progressive career and proven track record. I help companies hire top talent and I help impact players find better career opportunities.

I work hard to understand the objectives and goals of both clients and candidates to successfully match long-term relationships. I believe in creating a win-win opportunity for both and I will represent both interests fairly. My recruiting philosophy is based on honesty and integrity with a strong commitment and persistence to successfully match solutions.

My specialty has been recruiting in the Commercial Property & Casualty Insurance industry. Some of the positions I work on include, but are not limited to, Branch Management, Vice President, AVP, Underwriting Manager, Underwriter, Production Underwriter, Loss Control Representative, Risk Management, Wholesale Broker, Account Executive, Account Manager, Claim Adjuster, Premium Audit, Insurance Sales / Producer, Insurance Accounting / Accountant, Employee Benefits, Operations Manager, Customer Service Representatives (CSR's), Marketing Representative, Territory Manager, E&O / D&O Professional Liability, Medical Malpractice, Product Manager, Staff Attorney.

I would like the opportunity to speak with you to determine whether I can help you with a staffing need or if I can help you personally take your career to the next level. Please feel free to contact me 770-642-1230 or send an email to lpolikov@cjwalshgroup.com. I work on an extremely confidential basis.
