Ron Nash’s Summary

Executive Search consulting company The Nash Group was started in 1997. It was the brainchild of our illustrious founder, Ron Nash. Since then our company has grown its clients and service offerings to suit the needs of today’s economic environment.

April 1, 2008 was the launch of the Dream Job Online Coaching System. This online coaching system focuses on college grads, job seekers, career transition professionals and executives who are looking for help in defining their career path and creating actionable strategies that work! The best thing is that you can use it from the comfort of your own home – 24/7.

The Dream Job Online Coaching System utilizes Ron Nash’s book, “How to Find Your Dream Job, Even in a Recession” as the work book for this system.

A real story:

“I’m currently in transition looking for a new position and wasn’t having much success over the summer getting interviews. I saw a blog from Ron Nash regarding coaching so I sent Ron an e-mail.

Within a few hours I had a response from Ron asking what he could do to help. The next morning a headhunter called with an interview! I called Ron and he immediately went to work helping me prep for the interview. He gave me a homework assignment to review specific sections in his “Dream Job Online Coaching System.”

It was great stuff and I was sold on Ron! Ron gave me great insight into the interview process and tips on the best way to position myself. On Saturday evening Ron was on the phone with me until 11:30 PM!

We exchanged e-mails on Sunday and on Monday we did a mock interview. It was great and I was well prepared for the interview! Ron also pushed me to pursue some of my personal goals! Thank you Ron.” Donna Wesley, MBA 2009

BTW – Donna Found her Dream Job in Feb 2010!
We invite you to go to www.TheFriendZone.Tv where you can learn more about how we can help you! And remember, “If you’re not a Friend, You’re just another job seeker”.
