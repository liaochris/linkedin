Ronald W Stevens’s Summary

Personal Profile for Ron Stevens

Currently Co-founder/CEO of Mingleverse Labs Inc.
Past President/CEO investor of Mixpo Portfolio Broadcasting

Ron is an American/Canadian entrepreneur and angel investor from Silicon Valley and resides in Vancouver and San Francisco.

Educated in commerce and electrical engineering technology, Ron specializes only in ICT early stage start ups and has been fortunate to be either an engineer, executive or founder of seven Multi-Media software, internet communications and semiconductor start up companies, many achieving great success.

Ron was an early part of LSI Logic Canada, S3 Inc, Arithmos, iCompression and digital 5 Inc.

Ron has in depth industry knowledge with extensive global business development experience having worked and lived in USA, Canada, Europe and Asia

In Canada:
Ron is the co-founder of and current CEO of multiple award winning Mingleverse Laboratories Inc. He also started Mixpo where he served as its President & CEO and board member until end of 2007. Previously, Ron was an EIR at BC’s Yaletown Ventures and an LP in YVP II fund, along with 3 other angel investments in BC based companies. Ron is an Active USA/Canada IT evangelist as well as past chairman of New Media BC Fusion Forum former OACET member.

In USA:
Ron was an early part of 4 great start ups and the former winner of Tri-state CEO and Company of the year for digital 5 Inc. and a member of the (NACD) National Association of Directors (http://www.nacdonline.org)

Ronald W Stevens’s Specialties:

Experienced start- up entrepreneur from $0 to $50M with emphasis on engineering,sales, executive management and board level experience. Focus on consumer facing digital media software, hardware and communications.
