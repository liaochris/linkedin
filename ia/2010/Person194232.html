Stephan Whelan’s Summary

Stephan is an experienced Engagement Manager, Information Management, and Web Entrepreneur who has worked in Business Process Management (BPM), Enterprise Content Management (ECM) and Web Content Management (WCM) fields as well as a pioneer in the Online Publishing, Media and Social Networking who has helped to develop and grow Professional Services team and project methodology as well as implementing a Partner Channel from scratch.

He is experienced in engagement managing strategic and complex Enterprise Software implementations for clients ranging from Small Corporate to large Public Sector and Government such as Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), Rentokil Initial, Alliance & Leicester, Norton Rose, ICI Paints and Symbian.

Stephan also has a key strength in account and relationship management with clients and partners that has resulted in regular sell-on consulting and product with established clients, as well as developing new client relationships and subsequent generation of new client opportunities.

In addition Stephan has founded one of the largest and longest running websites dedicated to the art of diving - www.deeperblue.com. With a large, interactive community of writers and members, the site is regarded as one of the pioneering places to visit if you want information on FreeDiving, Scuba Diving, Spearfishing or Technical Diving. In 2004 an education arm to the site was launched teaching world class FreeDiving courses to students across Europe.
