Joe Lazcano’s Summary

I am an accomplished and proven sales executive who has achieved success in the areas of recruiting, management, personal production, product marketing, public speaking, consulting, negotiation and sales training in several industries.

I am best known professionally for pioneering a concept in the securities industry: In Service Distributions, a method of prospecting for clients that is still being used to train new financial advisers in across the country. This asset gathering system was instrumental in gaining over 4000 clients in 35 states. From 1996 until 2000, I recruited and led a team of 12 financial advisers and 4 sales assistants that produced $3.5 million in revenue per year.

Business travels have taken me to every major city in the U.S. as well as areas in Europe and Mexico; also I have been a keynote speaker at several national conventions.

▪ Recruited, established/trained national sales forces in different industries.
▪ Consistently met high personal production and goal setting standards.
▪ Bilingual: fluent in Spanish.

Recently, through my consulting practice, I’ve had the opportunity to successfully recruit Sales and Management personnel for Fortune 500 companies, as well as small to medium sized businesses in sevaral industries.
