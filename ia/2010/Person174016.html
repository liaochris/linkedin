Ron Smithson’s Summary

We are interested in educating the public on how they can take control of their financial lives, and become part of a "Debt Free America"!
Let me give you a quick example:
Most people refinance or purchase a new home every 5 – 7 years.
If you refinanced your home loan for $200,000 @ 6% for 360 months, your payment would be $1,199 monthly.
Now, if you refinanced or purchased a home every five years; the first 5 years of each loan, you would pay back approximately $58,000 in interest. At the end of the 30 year period, you would have paid back over $348,000 in total interest, and still not own your home, but ALL that money would have been paid out in interest to some Lender. Why not learn how to put some of that money in your OWN pocket?
This example is only concentrating on mortgage debt; we want to show you how to Take Control Of All Your Debt and be “DEBT FREE" in the quickest way possible.
