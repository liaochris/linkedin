Menno Mafait’s Summary

Thinknowlogy is an Artificial Intelligent language system.

• Artificial Intelligence by Design
• Designed to be intelligent
• The only way to create intelligent systems

Not by evolution: Artificial Intelligence is mostly based on the evolution theory: When a system has reached a certain degree of complexity, the power of evolution will create order out of chaos.

However, scientists are unable to locate this magical power in living organisms. It also fails to appear in artificial systems. (The reason: Only micro-evolution is scientifically proven, but not the evolution theory as a whole.)

Such an approach is absolutely unacceptable in other technical disciplines of science.

But by design: All artificial systems are designed, by definition. So, "designing" is a proven method for creating artificial systems.

Each design also needs a fundamental basis: Grammar is the foundation of natural language and contains hidden logic. This logic can be used to create intelligence.

However, traditional systems for Natural Language Processing systems are based on keywords and only have a grammatical top layer. These systems have no fundamental basis. That is why research in this field got stuck, for example at ambiguity.

Only Thinknowlogy is based on grammar and is capable of utilizing the hidden logic in natural language. It is therefore the only computer program in the world that is capable of "understanding" natural language, albeit very limited. It will take a lot of time and effort to develop the grammar of Thinknowlogy to a higher level.

Thinknowlogy will prove that intelligence only will emerge in an artificial (language) system when it is designed with the purpose to be intelligent, in the same way that the universe, physics and life were designed by God with a purpose, namely to serve His glory.
