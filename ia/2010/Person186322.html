Stephanie Daily’s Summary

What is Ghost Tweeting, and why is it good for your company?

You may have heard of this Twitter thing. As a Business Owner you: created an account, gotten on the site, and posted a couple of things on there to promote yourself and your business….and Nothing Happened. So you think to yourself, this is a time waster, and just like that poof, your Twitter Presence disappears.

Here’s where I come in. I can do your “Tweeting” for you. I’m the behind the scenes chick with fairy dust!

I will manage and grow your Twitter Account for you! I will promote your business with links to your blogs, newsletters, and events for you. I will engage your current customers, and get potential clients excited to do business with you. All while you are concentrating on what is most important – Growing Your Business.

I will sit down with you and determine what your goals for Twitter are. What do you want me to say about your and your business? What is important for someone to know about your business? What separates you from the other guys? Once that is determined, I can get started.
