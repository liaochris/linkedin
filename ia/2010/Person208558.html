Troy Tribe’s Summary

Troy Tribe has more than 17 years of industry experience as an Entrepreneur and Key Executive.

Prior to joining True North Academy, Troy was co-founder of Solera Networks and was instrumental in positioning the company for acquisition by The Canopy Group.

Additionally, Troy was part of the founding team of Assentive Corporation, he served as Vice President of Worldwide Sales. While at Assentive he was instrumental in raising $35 Million in funding with Sun Microsystems, Intel, SAIC, TL Ventures and J.W. Seligman.

At Assentive, his teams managed relationships with:

• John Deere International
• Mercury Marine
• General Motors
• Mitsubishi
• General Electric
• And Many Other Corporations

He also held positions at Viewpoint Digital (VWPT), as Director of Sales and Business Development where he was part of the senior team that positioned the company for acquisition by Computer Associates. While at Viewpoint Digital, he managed relationships with:

• Disney
• Industrial Light & Magic
• General Motors
• Pixar, and
• DreamWorks

While at Viewpoint, Mr. Tribe worked on featured films including:

• Shrek
• Batman
• Armageddon
• Titanic
• Air Force One
• ANTZ, and
• Mission Impossible

Prior to Viewpoint Digital, he worked in Silicon Valley for Xerox PARC (Semaphore, Inc), A Xerox Technology Venture (XTV) Company as Western Regional Director of Sales and Key Account Manager.
