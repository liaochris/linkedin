Steven Kincade’s Summary

Executive Profile
Proven Fortune 250 high technology hardware and software sales and strategic marketing leader with 23 years of VP leadership experience driving solution sales, marketing and operations. Steve's passion is developing people, profitability and competitive market share capture via applied technology solutions. Steve builds and leads high performing organizations and channel partners through collaboration, creative thinking and passionate hands on leadership.

• Developed an “Execution For Competitive Advantage” sales strategy that helps focus organizations on key tactical goals that drives revenue growth and increased profitability.
