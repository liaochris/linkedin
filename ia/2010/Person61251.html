Eric Alenius’s Summary

I have built, designed, serviced, and upgraded nearly every form of both semiconductor and data storage high vacuum process equipment for the past twenty years. I have covered the globe accomplishing this task. I was an expatriate for four years in Penang, Malaysia, where I started the regional Veeco office from the ground up and became the regional customer service manager for all of SE Asia. Afterwards, I spent eight years in Silicon Valley California servicing nearly every data storage manufacturer there. I have currently been to 38 countries, most of whom numerous times providing the best quality of service for my customers.

Eric Alenius’s Specialties:

I specialize in IBE, IBD, RIE, ICP,DLC/FCA, and Hiden SIMS/EPD systems. I have excellent troubleshooting and mechanical skills on nearly all high vacuum process equipment. I prefer working overseas and adapt to foreign cultures extremely well. Quite possibly my strongest area of expertise is in communication, both verbal and written. I excel in jobs that require much travel, independent and accurate decision making, and little to no direction and/or support from the factory.
