Vickie Brockelman, Realtor,GRI, EPO 500’s Summary

During the last 3 decades, Brockelman has been a real estate sales professional, an associate broker and a broker/owner of a major real estate franchise group in the Pocono Mountains area. As a broker/owner, she strategically managed unprecedented growth turning the franchise group she owned into the largest independently owned and operated real estate company in Monroe County. Today, she is broker/owner of Commonwealth Real Estate Your Way, LLC., a firm serving the Pocono Mountains region committed to applying the Platinum Rule of treating customers the way they wish to be treated in all transactions and providing customized solutions for customers.
