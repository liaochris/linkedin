PJ Rivera’s Summary

Aurelio “P.J.” Rivera is an Organizational Development Specialist and Human Capital Program Manager at the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS). Currently, Mr. Rivera manages a diverse training and organizational development portfolio for one DHHS's Operating Divisions. Mr. Rivera has provided coaching, training, and consulting for organizations including the U.S. Air Force, U.S. Department of Energy, Federal Aviation Administration, Indian Health Service, and several small non-profit organizations. Mr. Rivera has significant experience serving as a Program Specialist with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, where he helped lead a large-scale organizational transformation initiative that led to measurable improvements in employee engagement and organizational culture. Mr. Rivera’s experience includes program and contract management, policy development, staff supervision, and human capital and knowledge management consulting. He has helped design and implement organizational learning solutions, large-scale training workshops, diversity initiatives, leadership assessment programs, communication programs, and a variety of other organizational development and strategic human capital strategies.
