Eric Morse’s Summary

My professional passion in life is sales. It is a privilege to be part of what I consider to be one of the most important professions. In sales, we get the opportunity to create value, revenue, opportunity, growth and change for our selves and our clients. I love the hunt, I love the win, and I love the competition of sales. In my opinion, a great salesperson is really a "change agent."

With my company, Sales Result Inc., our passion is seeing our clients succeed, scale, and defeat their competition. We accomplish this by building, fixing, optimizing and growing multi-channel sales teams with vertical expertise in the high tech, financial services, light manufacturing, and wholesale sectors.

Sales Result Inc. provides end-to-end sales consulting services. Unlike other sales consultants, our solution is customized to Your Culture, Your Clients, Your way. We feel it's not in our best interests to force our clients to comply with some canned, pre-packaged solution. This also enables us to have great flexibility to address our clients business pains. If they need something as simple as call scripts or as detailed as an automated sales process with corresponding sales tools we've got the bases covered.
