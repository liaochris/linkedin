Brian Graves’s Summary

An environmental scientist with several years experience in GIS Information Systems (GIS), environmental analysis, project permitting, graphic design and photography. On a daily basis, I use GIS and related technology to analyze, develop, and maintain geographic data for environmental impact assessment, project alternatives analyses, and routing studies for a variety of development projects. My technical skills range from expertise in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and ESRI ArcGIS to a more intermediate usage of AutoCAD and 3Ds Max.

I am also a [slightly] published photographer with extensive knowledge of photographic techniques and state and federal archival photo documentation requirements. I use my photographic and design skills along with my software knowledge to evaluate the aesthetic impact of wind turbines, energy facilities, and other structures on the prevailing landscape in accordance with local and state permitting guidelines. I also have a broad range of experience in the environmental aspects of EFSB, MEPA, and regional permit applications for both private and public sector clients.

My earlier work experience centered on customer service and sales operations for internet companies. I served as an operations manager with responsibilities ranging from streamlining business processes and growing key account revenue to vendor relationship management and the supervision of sales support teams.
