Mary Gibbs’s Summary

Mary Gibbs is a career and job search coach whose clients range from senior level professionals to women who are ready to return to the workplace to recent college graduates looking for their first professional position. She has years of experience in all areas of career coaching including assessment, resume development, interviewing skills, networking strategies and salary negotiation.

Prior to starting her own consulting practice, Mary was a Vice President of one of the most highly reputable recruitment firms in Boston specializing in Human Resources search and placement. As a result of working in a high growth search firm for 10 years, Mary brings first- hand knowledge of understanding what clients look for in their candidates. As part of the placement process being in tuned to what managers in every industry look for in a qualified candidate, Mary has interviewed and coached hundreds of candidates throughout her years at Gatti and Associates on what competencies they needed to land a job while always keeping in mind their long term career goals. Mary’s business as an Executive Recruiter allowed her to understand corporate cultures and what it took to be successful to work in these varied environments. Mary is an expert on how to interview, write resumes and assess a strong “fit” for a company.
For the past 17 years, Mary has placed and interviewed well over 1000 candidates from entry level to Vice President in major companies such as Microsoft, IDG, Baybanks, Medtronics, EMC, Pegasystems, Comcast, Mass Financial Services, BankBoston, Avid Technology, Harvard Pilgrim Health Plan, Massachusetts General Hospital, Converse, Sunlife Financial Corporation, Intranet, Lotus, Citizen’s Bank to name a few. Her cross industry experience makes her an expert in understanding what different companies look for in a candidate.
