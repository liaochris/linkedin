Robert OBrien’s Summary

My goal is to advise / coach small to mid-size business owners to implement a strategic approach to improve the success of their business in three key areas: Team - improve processes, management, motivation, and accountability; Money - transform financial management and measures required to a day to day basis and establish supporting metrics; and Time - while instituting a better management handle on their businesses, the owners can find more time for themselves and their families to enjoy life. My money back guarantee is to provide agreed value to their business within the first month of every engagement.

Proven results in identifying and building solutions that generate a rapid return on investment, improve customer service, and provide the positioning needed for growing revenues with existing and new clients. Development of diverse product strategies, staff management, and sales responsibilities, with extensive background in operations,client service, and consulting. Client successes were achieved both internally through re-engineering initiatives and externally through sales to commercial clients. Identification and development of strong business relationships with key strategic partners in offering integrated value to clients evidenced throughout my career. Maintaining currency with regulatory requirements in a rapidly changing industry environment has been an ongoing success factor. Frequent speaker at conferences and industry events.
