antoinette raynes’s Summary

Antoinette Raynes is a dynamic, experienced small business owner adept in the use of social media. She is extremely active in her community and volunteers in a variety of organizations throughout Phoenix, Arizona. She volunteers with SCORE, a national nonprofit association of retired executives who are dedicated to helping small businesses succeed. During her time with SCORE, she has set up different social media for the Phoenix SCORE office and has also trained other volunteers in the use of social media. She currently maintains Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and NING for the Phoenix Chapter.

There are many people and businesses with great ideas and potential, and Raynes Partnerships is focused on assisting small business owners maximize their potential by collaborating with a focused, accountable and passion-driven partner.
