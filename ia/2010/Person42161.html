HsuanHua Chang, PCC’s Summary

I am a Professional Certifed Coach; certified by International Coach Federation and also a Certified Leadership Coach, certified by Georgetown University.

I help small business owners and leaders to grow their business and feel satisfied with their achievements. Brand and Vision are defined. Business plans/goals are set and action plans made to fulfill their vision.

Emotional intelligence, leadership development, body/mind alignment and work/life balance are addressed in coaching process.

Being an immigrant, a parent of 3 children and a daughter of two aging parents, I've learned to believe in abundance, clear communication, learning in action, loving what is, and trusting the process.
