Dr. Andrea Heikkinen’s Summary

I aim to inspire people to become active in their health by balancing their mind, body and spirit through chiropractic adjustments, nutrition, exercise and positive thinking.
I believe that a healthy lifestyle starts in the beginning with early childhood. This is why I have studied an additional 120 hours of coursework specific to chiropractic pediatrics and prenatal care. Ensuring that the mother is subluxation free allows the child to have a healthy start in life. Children are so precious and deserve to have their spines checked for subluxations too. I am a chiropractor because I want to show people how wonderful their lives can be when they are subluxation free and living well.

Dr. Andrea Heikkinen’s Specialties:

Pediatric Chiropractic, Prenatal Chiropractic, Webster Technique, Activator Technique, BioEnergetic Synchronization Technique, Cranial Sacral, SOT, Decompression Traction Therapy, and Nutritional Consultation.
