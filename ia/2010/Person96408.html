Jerron Smith’s Summary

I have spent the last 17 years as both a designer and design educator. I have worked in nearly every facet of the print, web and video production fields, producing graphics and animation for magazine, broadcast and broadband distribution.
Currently, I wear many hats; I am of course working as a freelance creative profesional, I work with Aquent Graphics Institute on a contract basis as an instructor and consultant dealing with Print, Web and Video technologies and am also an adjunct instructor in the Communication Arts dept. at NYIT.
Prior to working with AGI, I was the Associate to the Dean of Design and Digital Media at the Katharine Gibbs School in NYC. In this position I was responsible for managing the day to day operations of the ASA degree granting programs; Visual Communications, Digital Media, and Digital Film making.
