Anthony Fisher’s Summary

Since 1996, Anthony Fisher, M.Ed. has worked with a wide variety of individuals and businesses who are seeking the motivation, methods, and materials to represent themselves in ways that will make them stand out from the crowd and set them apart from their competition. These experiences have served to increase and inform his passion to assist entrepreneurs, professionals, and those in leadership roles in defining and in some cases refining a personal brand that will present them to the world with true distinctiveness.

As a Personal Brand Coach, Speaker, Author, Graphic/Web Designer, Presentation Designer and Communication Specialists, Anthony Fisher has maximized his unique gifts and is passionately excited to share what he knows about Personal Branding and identifying your individual gifts to make room for your potential in order to fulfill your destiny.businesses.
