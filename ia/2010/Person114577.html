Kenneth Cameron’s Summary

Kenneth serves as Chief Executive Officer of Terroir Worldwide. The Terroir Group of Companies include: Terroir2You, Terroir Publishing, Terroir Development and Terroir Restaurant & Winery Marketing.

Every other month Terroir2You club members enjoy the finest local wines, meads and complimentary foods shipped right to their door. Each of the 5 current T2Y clubs – PNW (Oregon, Washington, Idaho and Montana), Colorado, Texas, New England and Hawai’i – offer members the chance to enjoy the very best their region has to offer.

Terroir Publishing publishes lifestyle magazines including regional editions of its wine, spirits and craft brews magazine – Terroir. Terroir is provided to our Terroir2You members as part of their bi-monthly shipment and is also available by subscription, through our local partner wineries and at our private tasting parties. 5 additional regional clubs and editions of Terroir will be available by end of year 2010.

Terroir Development is a firm that specializes in “green” land development – including a stable of micro-wineries and themed restaurants.

Terroir Restaurant & Winery Marketing is one of the trusted leaders in providing restaurant and winery specific products, services and marketing programs for the industry.

Kenneth's core expertise includes being a proven business development professional who has initiated, built and strengthened relationships at all levels within complex client organizations. He is a person who obsesses on ensuring that his clients obtain optimal results for the product and service solutions they provide. Who has met and exceeded aggressive objectives for profitable revenue and a person who clients, customers, colleagues and competitors regard as intelligent, perceptive, creative, resilient, empathic, versatile and tenacious and courageous.
