Roshmond Patten’s Summary

I planted my professional roots in the late 90s as a music journalist. Eventually, I became Associate Editor at a major music magazine. Along the way, I formed a formidable alliance with a lower Manhattan guerilla marketing boutique. They saw tremendous potential for me as a copywriter and sponsored my certification at the School of Visual Arts, giving my raw talent a much needed professional polish. After a decade of churning out copy work while developing my own social media marketing chops, I started my own online community. This community was the embodiment of all the things I in which I excel: creative writing and journalism, giving brands a voice, organizing and managing communities, and putting innovative ideas into action. Throw a little social media strategy, PR and market research experience in the pot, and there’s my formula. Yes, it's complicated, but it all ends up being a beautifully cohesive skill set that is powerful and truly unique.
