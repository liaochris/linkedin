Mark Gordon’s Summary

I am the founder of American Voice Mail / Segway Communications (www.americanvoicemail.com) (www.segwaycommunications.com). I built this business to allow small and medium business to compete and win, by giving them more communications tools than they even knew existed, with no equipment to buy, fix or outgrow. We now provide local phone service in 46 countries, where business owners can get their phone numbers from us for free. Calls can be answered with an Auto Attendant that transfers callers to the person or department they choose, no matter where or how diverse they are located. Even if all calls transfer to you and your name is the only name in the Dial by Name Directory, no one will know, but you will sound like a "Fortune 500" company, instead of a Mom-n-Pop operation. Fax to email is included for free. After being in the voice mail business for over 25 years, we've learned that voice mail doesn't work. Yes, it will answer your phone and take a message, but nobody calls your business to leave a message. Customers call to talk with someone and our systems will find and connect callers to our customers and their staff.

My business is successful because we provide value. There isn't one customer who does not receive more value than they pay for. But that's easy, with packages that start with a local telephone number, voice mail and fax to email for only $8.95mo. That's less than the price of a turkey sandwich, and you can't run a business from a turkey sandwich.
