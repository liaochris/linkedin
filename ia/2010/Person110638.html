Karen Kincy’s Summary

As both a published novelist and an online intern for a New York literary agency, I can offer professional writing and editorial skills, keen attention to detail, and a passion for publishing. I am represented by Sara Crowe at Harvey Klinger, and have a three-book deal with Flux.

My passions include writing, linguistics, and digital photography. I have worked as a graphic designer for my college, and I currently design and maintain my author’s website, www.karenkincy.com, in order to better market my novels and my author's persona.

I graduated this June from The Evergreen State College with a primary emphasis of study in Literature and Writing (93 credits) and a secondary emphasis of study in Linguistics (52 credits).
