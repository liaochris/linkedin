Fred Zuill’s Summary

Fred Zuill has been a Consultant to innovative businesses and creative individuals for over 20 years. He serves his clients with a keen focus on their need to quickly realize viable customer-centric solutions, to implement and support those solutions and then to evangelize among customers, investors and the press.

Fred’s hard-fought collection of broad experiences feed his ability to serve clients so effectively. His long list of successful experiences that have cultivated his broad expertise includes Software Developer, Businessman, Experience Designer, Marketing Support, Salesman, Instructor and Photographer.

As a Consultant, Fred quickly and thoroughly becomes an expert in his client’s business realities, workflows and techniques. He then effectively applies uncanny insights for user and customer behaviors and system needs. With broad technical and “soft” skills, Fred rapidly prototypes and architects meaningful solutions. Finally he brings quality to the product and credibility to his client by publicly serving as customer champion and product evangelist.

Through early 2008, Fred served for over 5 1/2 years with Hewlett Packard as HP's high-end technical interface to VIPs, the press and its "Creative Influencers" in the realm of creative digital photo printing. While at HP, Fred also invented the popular and well-reviewed HP Pro Print plug-in for Adobe Photoshop. In 2008 Fred and his partners won HP's competitive, company-wide business innovation award (including "angel" funding for business incubation) for designing a meaningful new business for HP in the consumer photo realm, for conceiving the associated inventions and for successfully presenting the plan to the internal investors.

Over 20 years, Fred has used his insights to serve clients in many industries including Photography, Sign-making, Flight Test, 911 Dispatch Systems, Financial Planning, Medical Office Automation, Manufacturing, Construction and more.
