Lawrence Taylor-Duncan, MCSE’s Summary

Experienced IT Consultant and Security Analyst with numerous related IT certifications. I undertake computer forensic analysis for clients in the legal community using EnCase Forensic software and provide expert witness testimony in court cases. I am also an active chapter member of InfraGard - an FBI sponsored association for professionals in the InfoSec sector.

In my role as an IT consultant I support a wide range of clients in the healthcare, legal, manufacturing, automotive and aerospace/defense sectors to name but a few. My company's emphasis is on Information Assurance with a focus on network security, threat mitigation, disaster recovery & business continuity, network design and system administration. We support our clients 24x7.
