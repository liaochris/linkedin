Miry Whitehill’s Summary

I'm the Lead Account Executive for Feed Company, the leader for seeding videos on the Web for major brand advertisers in the U.S. I liaison between our Seeding Strategy and Social Marketing teams and our clients- agencies, brands, and channel partners- to coordinate the video seeding campaigns. We employ innovative marketing tactics to get branded videos exposed to popular blogs, video sites, and social networks.

I started my career in Marketing straight out of college as the Marketing Specialist at a high tech company in K'far Saba, Israel. I helped brainstorm and research gaps in the North American and Canadian marketplaces. I also wrote technical specs for new product development.

In my spare time, I did pro-bono consulting for the Israel Animal Guard in Relationship Marketing Strategies and Supporter and Fan Involvement.

I moved on to Keta Keta, a Tel Aviv-based Viral Video Agency, as the Media & Seeding Manager. I developed seeding strategies for viral video campaigns targeting niche online audiences for clients like El Al, LG, and P&G. I also spearheaded Corporate Event Sponsorship and Partnerships with industry suppliers.

After an exciting three years in Israel, I set off on the 6000 mile journey to Los Angeles to change the world with Feed Company. We're almost there. I love what I do.
