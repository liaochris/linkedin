Bill Reaugh’s Summary

8 years of experience in the Renewable Energy, with particular emphasis on solar electricity and solar hot water for commercial and utility scale projects. Historically I have been involved with codes, standards, regulation, financing, and other technical areas. I am in the process of obtaining an MBA so that in combination with my technical background can become an international leader in this industry. With Draker Laboratories I am responsible for the Western Region, which includes AK, AZ, CA, CO, HI, ID, OR, NM, NV, MT, UT, WA, and WY.

Bill Reaugh’s Specialties:

Monitoring requirements for residential, commercial, and utility scale solar electric systems, National Electrical Code as it applies to PV (solar electric) systems, inverter technologies and topologies, solar power production modeling, financial modeling of PV systems, CalFire guidelines for residential and commercial buildings, PV installations, data collection and presentation for distributed generation sources
