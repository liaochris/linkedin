Greg Robertson’s Summary

For over 25 years, I have been creating smart, concept-driven print, broadcast, and web ads, as well as names, branding strategies, packaging, social media tactics and more for brands and companies of every size and most industries.

National consumer brand clients have included AT&T • BF Goodrich • Bic • Birds' Eye • Blue Cross Blue Shield • Cadillac • Coca-Cola • Dannon • Evian • GE • Gentleman Jack • Gorton's • Labatt • Lipton • Logitech • Michelin • Norwegian Cruise Lines • Pepsi • Pizza Hut • Taco Bell • and more.

Advantage: I translate genuine understanding into results-driven creative work for my clients. Understanding the real-world demands on a business principal. Understanding how to turn client offerings into market desire. And understanding how to fuse short-term results with long-term brand growth in every marketing message.

My firm specializes not in one industry or discipline, but in clients who actively want to improve the direction of their brand(s) using smarter creative to advance their cause. Moving the dial is always important, but moving the marketplace to want your brand there is essential.

Greg Robertson’s Specialties:

Work Types: Radio, TV, Print, Web, Social Media, Branding, Design, Direct Mail, and more.

Related: Have interviewed, written for/about, and produced public figures, Including Tracey Ullman, Alan Rachins, Jonathan Katz, Shaquille O'Neal, John Wooden, Norm Abram, and others.

Vertical Markets: Consumer Goods, Health Care, Senior Markets, Medical Products, Banking & Finance, Insurance, Non-Profits, Retail, Professional Services, AEC, Communications, Real Estate, Entertainment.
