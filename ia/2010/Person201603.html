Thane Ryland’s Summary

Specialising in the media and technology sectors, Thane Ryland has ten years international experience in media analytics, market intelligence and secondary research.

He is currently working with a leading international Internet monitoring and analysis firm. Paris-based Synthesio is undergoing considerable growth and has a strong social media client base in health, hospitality, financial services and consumer goods.

Previously, he headed up CARMA's Research Practice, overseeing researchers working on a portfolio of blue chip clients including SAP, adidas, Shell, Microsoft and TomTom.

In his spare time, Thane has conducted media and marketing research consultancies for Ofcom, BBC, Nokia, Microsoft, IKEA, Europe's largest airline, the UK government, various digital and social media agencies, and one of the UK's leading digital publishers. Thane also served as a trendspotter for an American retailer from 2006 - 2009. Prior to moving to the UK, Thane served in marketing and communications roles for brands such as Patagonia, Lands' End and The Nature Conservancy.

Thane holds an MSc degree (Distinction) in Media and Communication from the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE), followed by media research at the LSE on the international social and economic significance of British food-based entertainment. He has presented his research at various international media conferences.
