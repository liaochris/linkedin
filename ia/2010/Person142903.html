Michael Kennedy’s Summary

Mike Kennedy is the Director of E-Commerce Solutions for Mandrien Consulting Group.

Mike joins Mandrien after working at the intersection of business and technology in the business process management industry. In addition to managing many successful lead generating online marketing campaigns for Fortune 100 clients, he added value by streamlining internal marketing and content management processes, wrote a handbook of internal web marketing operations and helped to establish innovative best practices which in turn led to greater ROI for clients.

Mike managed content for an industry-leading web portal, helping to significantly grow its successful article contributor program and monthly topical e-newsletters. He was part of the web team that oversaw a 400% community membership growth. In addition, he helped launch, market and manage a new line of e-business products, leveraging robust e-commerce strategies and helping to establish the company’s leadership position in the online training marketplace.

Mr. Kennedy has a Master's Degree in E-Business and Information Systems from Newcastle University in the UK and a Bachelor’s Degree from the University of Maine at Machias.
