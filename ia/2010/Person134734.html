Mark Troncone, MBA, PMP®’s Summary

I am a PMP® certified Project Manager and a certified IT Business Analyst experienced working within Marketing, Membership, E-Commerce, Consumer Product and Pharmaceutical industries. I use my key skills in communication, analyzing, and presenting alternative solutions to deliver the project initiative.

The value I bring to your organization is my ability to effectively gather and create Business Requirements Documentation in order to ensure only relative functionality is implemented. I can then manage the project team to partner with the Stakeholders, Subject Matter Experts, and IT resources to achieve goals that entail system upgrades, software installations, and application enhancements.

The result for your organization is a project completed within budget and deadline goals, delivering scope objectives, which produce a quality functioning system with minimal defects and low maintenance costs. This in turn, allows key executives to use the application to make strategic business decisions that will have a positive Return on Investment and give the firm a competitive edge in the marketplace.

I build relationships, motivate teams, manage conflict, and resolve issues to achieve collaborative results with users. I am skilled in IT Liaison duties between the business community and IT teams.
