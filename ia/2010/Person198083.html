Susan Whiteside’s Summary

Ms. Whiteside brings 20 years of leadership and marketing experience in a variety of fields to her consulting practice, including almost 10 years with networking leader, Cisco Systems. Prior to Cisco, she held various positions focused on the customer experience and technology management with Bay Networks, Pratt and Whitney, and AT&T. In parallel as well as throughout her career, Ms. Whiteside had engaged with significant non-profit organizations, philanthropy efforts, and research in technology, research in education and ongoing next-generations of academic learning tools.

In 1999, as a marketing manager responsible for growing a then very small e-commerce presence, she managed a global cross-functional team to design, implement, test, and launch the “Cisco Networking Products Marketplace” website and succeeded in taking customer orders placed online from 30% to 80%. While at Bay Networks, Ms Whiteside built relationships with customers and account teams to ensure that Bay Networks was providing comprehensive, effective, and specific support services to key Enterprise and Service Provider LAN and WAN accounts including facilitating post-sales support, analysis of delivery needs and root-cause failures and recommending improvements.

Prior to venturing into the high technology market, Ms Whiteside served as a customer support engineer for a commercial engine maker, Pratt & Whitney. In this capacity she managed 11 classified and unclassified LANs encompassing all phases of customer, product, and life cycle support. She established production, field installation and support processes for installed systems, improving quality and reducing business cost structures. Ms Whiteside also served as a product engineer for telecom giant, AT&T, designing and deploying a series of backbone infrastructure systems.
