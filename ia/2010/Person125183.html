Linda Ferguson’s Summary

Since 1995 my company, New Paradigm Alliance, has offered programs for High Performance that address Leadership Development, Organization Development, Group Dynamics, Emotional Intelligence, Meeting and Retreat Facilitation, and Coaching.

For over 8 years I was a professor of business management teaching undergraduate and MBA classes in Organizational Behavior, Business Ethics, Leadership, Training and Development, Entrepreneurship, Motivation, and Marketing.

I've written a book called "Path for Greatness: Spirituality at Work" (Trafford, 2001). Along with other pioneers in the Spirituality at Work movement, I was asked to contribute a chapter in an edited book "The Workplace and Spirituality" (Skylight Paths, 2009). I also was asked by the publisher of Bella Magazine to write a Coaching column on Work-Life Balance. I recently completed my second book "Staying Grounded in Shifting Sand".

For sample workshop topics, blog postings, and video clips go to: www.lindajferguson.com
