Alexander Ross’s Summary

Globus is a leading executive search business that operates within the security sector. With over fifteen years industry experience we work with some of the largest security companies in the world. The Globus brand spans the globe and 60% of the work we undertake is in continental Europe.

As a business owner and consultant I work with some of the biggest names within the security sector. I have been involved in sourcing many strategic positions throughout Europe & USA, finding roles for some of the key people within the industry. Due to this success I’m regarded as a preferred & retained supplier to a number of top global security companies.

Our methodology ensures a discreet, transparent process for both candidate and client throughout the whole recruitment phase. 67% of our business is from long standing clients that rely on us to provide excellence in process and outcome, we complete all of our retained assignments.

The one thing I have learnt is that every clients requirements are different as they all have different demands on their businesses and that recruitment solutions need to be flexible and bespoke for each situation.

I recognise just how critical it is to be able to identify the right people that will allow you to achieve your businesses goals.
