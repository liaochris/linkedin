Deborah Edwards-Onoro’s Summary

I'm an experienced front-end web developer and user experience professional with knowledge of web standards, usability, information architecture, accessibility, search engine optimization and email marketing.

Through my freelance business Lireo Designs, I provide consulting and services to businesses looking to build their first website, enhance an existing website, and market their website.

I've worked on websites as small as three pages to websites with thousands of pages. Custom themes for content management systems are my specialty.

As a member of multidisciplinary teams across multiple locations on different continents, I've worked on the development and release of applications in three languages. I appreciate and recognize the importance of good communication, both verbal and written.

I'm experienced in writing project plans, task analysis, use cases, content inventory, user guides and quality assurance.

Always interested in consulting, new website design, and website re-design.

Deborah Edwards-Onoro’s Specialties:

User-centered design, site architecture, competitive analysis, usability reports, and accessibility reviews.

Experienced in XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Dreamweaver, Photoshop and third party mailing list management applications.


Advanced knowledge of content management systems (WordPress, Frog CMS, Textpattern).

Expert knowledge in Adobe Acrobat and creating usable and accessible PDFs.
