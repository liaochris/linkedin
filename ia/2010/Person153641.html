Nicholas Bianchi’s Summary

Before taking a position at Burson-Marsteller as a digital media strategist, Nicholas served as the multimedia/web manager for the Division of Enrollment Management at the University of Miami since 2008. Throughout this time he worked to align university enrollment objectives with social media strategies, and was responsible for the development and creation of digital communication vehicles. His experience with the university as well as his previous internship with Worlds End Production's in London, have allowed him to become well versed in all aspects of digital media content production and management; including photo, video, and web.

Nicholas holds an undergraduate degree in video-film production and a Master's in Public Relations from the University of Miami. Nicholas was raised in Hawaii and has lived in Miami for the past four years. When he is not working he loves to travel and create short-films about his adventures. Two of his favorite places are Istanbul, Turkey and Esterillos Este, Costa Rica, both of which he has visited frequently in the past two years.
