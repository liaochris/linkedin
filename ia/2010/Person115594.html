Kevin J. Li’s Summary

Since earning my Master’s in Mechanical Engineering at San Jose State, I have spent the past 14 years designing, analyzing and sustaining mechanical parts and systems in a variety of fields, including semiconductor equipment, automation, nanotechnology and bioscience.

A few years ago, I made the jump to consulting. At Precision Instrumentation Design (PID), I supported our clients in both large and small projects. Additionally I served as vice-president of the company, helping to manage day-to-day operations and build our infrastructure.

Now, I am an independent consultant.
