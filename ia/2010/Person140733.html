Melissa Karlin’s Summary

A work in progress (hopefully more progress & less work)

So, I’m high-energy, with a keen intuition and relationship skills. But what do I think that means (and what I hope you, Prospective Employer, will think)?

I get people. I really do think it’s that simple. I get people, I know what they want and I like to figure out all the ways I can get them what they want. Over my years of experience, I’ve found that steers me towards Public Relations. I genuinely believe that Public Relations is relationship building. With the press, with the public, with your internal customers. It’s about providing a link between what a client/employer hopes to achieve with what is possible.

I’ve found that the past year has definitely been my year of Social Media. I’ve become a very heavy user in my personal life. The people I’ve met along the way have really become my support system. Now, I’m investigating and learning about how to use Social Media in a business environment. How can I use the same relationship building architecture professionally?

I received a Graduate Certificate from the University of Chicago with a focus on Public Relations & Social Media in June and I can’t wait to see what the future looks like.
