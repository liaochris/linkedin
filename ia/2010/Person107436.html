Jeffrey M. Tilton CFE CICA’s Summary

Certified Fraud Examiner, CFE - Sarbanes-Oxley, SOX, Consultant

Assist clients in the implementation of effective fraud risk management programs that include a set of programs and controls to prevent and detect fraud supported by a strong process on how to respond should fraud or abuse be detected.

A quality fraud program must be supported by an effective “tone at the top” from senior management team. The tone at the top is extremely important to the overall controls system as it lends weight from the top down to the key message from senior management that this type of behavior will not be tolerated. Tone at the top is not something to just articulate, but it must also be demonstrated through management behavior and actions as being part of the organizational culture.

A quality Sarbanes-Oxley SOX program will produce improvements in a company's financial reporting and operations and help to lessen the chance of a qualified auditor attestation. If a public company files a year end 10-K with a qualified auditor attestation it can impact negatively on the company's reputation, shareholders and lenders.

Our Sarbanes-Oxley SOX program utiltizes a top down approach that relies heavily on SEC and PCAOB standards, COSO and COBIT frameworks, financial reporting risk assessments, entity level controls and sophisticated models and has dramatically reduced costs by utilizing a best practices approach to corporate compliance, internal control and monitoring.
