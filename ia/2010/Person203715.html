Tim Bull’s Summary

Co-founder of BinaryPlex, we are focussed on developing software to deliver on the promise of Web 2.0 with an automated expertise discovery engine called HiveMind which will unearth the hidden talents in your organisation.

I have always worked in the fields related to Emerging Technologies, in particular adapting these to meet the requirements of large enterprise businesses and have delivered on this with 15 years experience working for PwC.

I'm passionate and always learning about Social Software, Web 2.0 technologies and their application to business and large enterprise, crowd sourcing and anything else that has potential to be a disruptive technology for business innovation.
