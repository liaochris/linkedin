Wendy Perkins’s Summary

All modes of marketing, experienced with WOM, Guerrilla, outdoor, OOH, street, print, online, search, SEO, etc. Light HTML knowledge, adserving systems, experience with ATLAS DMT, Doubleclick, Mediaplex, 24/7 Real Connect. A Media planner, with ability to chose most cost effective and appropriate media channels for the buy. Ability to work with large marketing budgets within tight deadlines. Creatively inclined, able to make creative suggestions, improvements to copy, layout, color execution, etc. to effectively drive performance, results, branding for client or company. Mathematically proficient with strong Excel and PowerPoint skills, to complete analysis on campaign performance, reporting on results and best steps towards optimization.
