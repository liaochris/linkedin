Dave Dias’s Summary

For 22 years I have specialized in business and sales strategy to build long term client value. I have the privilege of leading an amazing technical team and am an owner of this dynamic, privately held, business insurance organization which is licensed in 50 states!

With diversified interests, I serve as a Founding Principle of Trinitas Partners based in the Silicon Valley. I am on the Board of Directors of MPowerGiving, www.mpowergiving.com, which is assisting non profit organizations raise money using texting technology!!!!

I have written a book titled SALES Ethos, Building Long Term Client Value which is available on Amazon. The vision and goal of SALES Ethos is to elevate the words "sales professional" to a prestigious and respected vocation.

I have recently founded Premier Legacy Films which captures the essence of one's legacy with the sights and sounds that make up their life. Premier Legacy Films produces heirloom quality documentaries that can be viewed for generations.

I also have the privilege of serving on the Board of Trustees of Azusa Pacific University and am the co-founder of the Time Out Conference.
