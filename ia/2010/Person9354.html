Anthony Medeiros’s Summary

With 12 years of online and digital solutions for Fortune 500 companies at Razorfish, Anthony has a proven record of increased business performance and successful online strategies with his solutions and experience vision. He has led his teams in adopting agile design practices, successful content management strategies, and deployment of rapid user research and testing methods. His work addresses the needs of social engagement and influence, distributed content models, multi-channel experiences, data visualization, market exchanges and commerce, educational and support intranets, partner extranets, and enterprise content management. His solutions are highly informed by user and competitive research, persona and scenario development, iterative testing and design, and performance analytics and measurement. Prior to his tenure with Razorfish, Anthony was the Senior Design Lead for Creative Development Services at Cendant Corporation, managing the experience development for their digital properties.
