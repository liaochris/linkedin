Craig Nosler, CRS, ABR’s Summary

Residential Realtor / Associate Broker - Over 20 years in the Real Estate and Banking industry combined focused in King County and South Snohomish County (Renton north to Everett), including Seattle-proper.

My mission is to provide exceptional services as a Realtor to Buyers, Sellers and Investors based on a long-term, service and referral-based working relationship, and to bring you success whether buying or selling. I believe firmly in representation with strict adherence to fiduciary duty and the highest standards of care. Whatever the need of my clients, I will find/work the solution - or find a great agent if the need is outside my area of expertise.

A native of the Greater Seattle area, I have extensive knowledge of the region and all it has to offer. I have an extensive relocation background, partnering with relocation companies to assist clients working for companies in the bio-tech industry, as well as employees of Microsoft, Boeing, Starbucks, Costco, Real Networks, SanMar and many other Northwest companies.
