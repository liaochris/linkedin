Twila Kaye’s Summary

Twila is President/CEO of Twila Kaye, a professional group of companies - Chicago Gift Basket Company, Twila Kaye Designs & the recently acquired One Great Gift Dropship. She focuses on custom designed gift baskets for all occasions, corporate gifts & promotional items, as well as exclusive gift design and consulting services for nationally branded companies such as Willa's Shortbread, Mount of Olives Treasures Tea Company, Tea Spree, Bridge Brand Chocolates/San Francisco Chocolate Company, and Wells Fargo.

In 2007 Twila expanded her leadership capacities as Founder of PROs (Professional Referral Organization of America). In 2010 she was appointed as Advisory Board Member of The Gift Basket Association (GBA) and NWI Chapter President of Women's Networking Alliance (WNA), a unique national networking organization for women business owners. Twila is also the 2010 recipient of the Northwest Indiana Entreprenuerial Excellence Women In Business Champion Award.

Twila's success continues to rise as Co-Author of a brand new collaborative book being released November, 2010, Fundamentals - Basic Concepts for your Gift Basket Business and Contributing Writer for Trends & Tips Magazine, a national Gift Basket & Retail Industry trade publication.

When Twila is not busy starting a new company or helping aspiring entreprenuers and business professionals grow their success, she enjoys spending time with her husband and two grown children. She loves to travel and entertain.
