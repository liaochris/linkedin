Traci Bradstreet; bradstreetsearch@comcast.net’s Summary

Traci is a Recruiting and HR Leader who has tailored innovative people strategy programs to support organization growth and evolution. She has held the top HR position for companies like CTC Communications, Logica-CMG, Woodard & Curran and Tessera Enterprise Solutions. She has also held major field leadership roles with companies like Sybase and Avnet.

Her passion for talent search led her to form Bradstreet Search Associates, dedicated to strategic talent acquisition for technology companies. She delivers people solutions that work!

She invites your contact if you are seeking your next career assignment or if you require assistance in bringing top caliber talent to your organization!
