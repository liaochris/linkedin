Dwayne Burns’s Summary

Dwayne Burns has worked in the technology sector in Atlanta for well over 10 years which has allowed him to work with a divergent set of technologies.

While very early in his life working with non tech related companies he always gravitated towards the technical and it was then the love of technology was culminated.

What began as a job building computers for IBM quickly became a long and fruitful career in technology. Following the assignment at IBM he partnered with Bethco\Inacom and worked on various projects with fortune 500 companies like Suntrust and Nextel as well as smaller companies and non profit organizations like The Boys and the Girls Club.

Upon completion of the work with Bethco\Inacom he partnered with a small internet startup called EyeWonder and supported a host of executives and developers as they grew the company.

In 2002 Dwayne Burns left EyeWonder to pursue more entrepreneurial activities and began doing some consulting work with various departments (O.I.A., Deans Office,Etc...) within Morehouse College.

In 2003 He accepted a position as a Network Manager with a privately held company in Lithia Springs (NIOXIN Research Laboratories). In September 2008 Procter & Gamble announced the purchase of NIOXIN Research Laboratories and Dwayne Burns remains at the Llithia Springs location through this transition.
