Mike Smith, PMP’s Summary

Independent consultant for project/program management with extensive experience in managing large complex projects to successful completion as well as mentoring/training clients in project management discipline and process. Diverse industry experience including information technology, medical device manufacturing, military support, travel distribution, telecom, and environmental regulatory compliance.

Mike holds the Project Management Institute's Project Management Professional (PMP) certification, and currently works with several firms on a contract basis including: S2insight, Cyintech, The Solutionz Group, and 361 Consulting. He is always open to new and interesting projects and clients.
