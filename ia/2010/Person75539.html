Gina Palermo’s Summary

http://www.mypromoonline.com

Pro Motion Online, Inc. is dedicated to helping you with your promotions, video, photography, newsletters or any other communication. We specialize in taking the project from the beginning creative concept to creating all of the assets needed and then posting or publishing to the web, print, photography, video, DVD, or any other medium or a combination of several assets to communicate a clear message to your customer. If the customer is internal to your organization or external we can help you with customized assets.

Gina Palermo’s Specialties:

Creative development and graphic design, web design & development, project manager, photographer, videographer, animator, print advertisements, short promotional movies that combine video and motion animation. Creative planning as well as development from the beginning to the end of a project.

Technology used: All Adobe Creative Suite Master Collection products. Apple products used, Final Cut Pro Studio as well as others. Familiar with HTML, CSS, XML and FTP protocol.
