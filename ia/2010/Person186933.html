Shabnam Rezaei’s Experience

  • Co-Founder & President

    Big Bad Boo Studios

    (Privately Held; Animation industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    Big Bad Boo is a production and distribution company dedicated to teaching children culture through entertainment. With offices in New York and Vancouver, the company’s first animated TV series, Mixed Nutz, debuted on PBS and many other stations worldwide in 2010. The creative team's first direct-to-DVD product, Babak & Friends - A First Norooz, told the story of little Babak who is stuck between his Iranian and American life and featured Emmy Award winner Shohreh Aghdashloo. Big Bad Boo is currently working on 26 X 11 minute episodes of 1001 Nights, bringing to life famous characters like Sinbad and Alibaba and the 40 Thieves. 1001 Nights also has a series of related merchandising such as comic books in the works. In addition, they are launching oznoz.com, a platform for distributing multicultural content for kids.

    At Big Bad Boo, we offer world class animation services to our clients with access to Canada's best animation talent, tax credits as well as the industry top writers, storyboard artists, voice-over actors and production crew. Big Bad Boo provides the full spectrum of pre-production, animation, and post production services and is the largest Toon Boom facility in Western Canada, employing an Emmy Award winning creative team.
    Big Bad Boo was incubated in 2005 as Norooz Productions and after winning the NYU business plan competition in 2005 and over time developed into Big Bad Boo Studios. Titles include Babak & Friends, Mixed Nutz and 1001 Nights.

  • Co-Founder & President

    Oznoz.com

    (Privately Held; Consumer Goods industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    Oznoz.com, is an online distribution platform for multi-lingual and multi-cultural products that teach children about different cultures and languages.

  • Founder & Editor-In-Chief

    PersianMirror.com

    (Online Media industry)

    June 2004 — Present (6 years 6 months)

    PersianMirror is a modern magazine for Persian culture, history, travel, film, art, music, modern writing, and much more. The site offers a free events listing page where you can list any Iranian event. PersianMirror also offers the largest Iranian business directory with more than 10,000 listings free of charge. The site is always looking for contributors to submit their own articles and pictures about Iranian culture. Submissions can be posted online at any time at www.persianmirror.com.

Managing team of 30+ functional consultants, project managers, client service managers. In charge of TCM Training Center, delivering training courses for OPICS, Loan IQ and Summit. Responsible for annual revenues in excess of $10 M. Clients included JPMorgan Chase, Keybank, ING, Leumi and Citibank.
    Global Head of Services Loan IQ
    In charge of service group for Loan IQ clients globally. Loan IQ is software for Commercial Lending, syndications, originations and secondary loan trading markets, boasting over 40 clients including JPMorgan Chase, who is the biggest loans participator and originator in the market. Reporting group consisted of 12 professionals in New York and London, including Product Specialists, Accounting Specialist, and Implementation Managers. Grew team utilization by 50% and doubled professional services revenues to $4 M in one year.

Responsibilities included branding, trade show organization and marketing plan development.
