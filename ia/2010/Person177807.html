Roshni Mahtani’s Summary

Before starting theAsianparent.com, Roshni was a TV producer and journalist for various media companies and publications in Singapore and New York.

She turned entrepreneur at age 24 and by the time she was 26 she had founded 2 successful web businesses (theAsianparent.com and tickledmedia.com) and never had to work a conventional job again.

She now spends her days as a Social Media Consultant to various Start Ups and MNC's around the world. Her Clients include Nestle (Singapore), Abbott Nutritional (Singapore), AXA Insurance (Singapore), Prudential (Singapore), Genius Minds (Malaysia), Property Guru (Singapore), iXiGo.Com (India), Nandos (South Africa) and Attentio S.A (Belgium) to name a few.

She holds a double degree in Mass Communication and Marketing but now specialises in digital media and advertising sales.

Her mantra in life is to "seize the day, as tomorrow is not guaranteed."
