Brad Kempski’s Summary

I've been working in the technology sector since early 2001,specifically in internet technologies, networks, applications and now fixed wireless. I provide leadership in the planning, designing, due diligence and implementations of strategic business objectives in order to successfully reach market quotas. I continue to select, develop and evaluate personnel to ensure the efficient operation & growth of the team. Along with continuing to identify, create and manage business relationships with select companies to develop solutions and/or create complementary products/services. Provide direction to form strategic marketing plans, including customer profiles, targeted programs, and action items. Develop, share and maintain an in-depth knowledge of our sector.

The demand for quality business grade broadband is also growing. Existing wire line technologies such as T1 lines, DSL, Cable and Fiber are in many cases too slow, not available or too costly to deploy in needed areas. Fixed wireless technology has grown dramatically in the past 5 years. Sparkplug has product offerings starting at 1Mb and go as high as 1Gb. This technology provides these data rates through the air without any trenching, permits or delay.

Our wireless broadband services are 100% Independent from the LEC which gives us the ability to install most services quicker. Most services can be installed in as little as a few days.

We also have an agent program. Please let me know if anyone needs more information!
