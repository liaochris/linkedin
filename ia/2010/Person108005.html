Julia Thahar’s Summary

I'm a nor-cal native making the cut in Los Angeles. When it comes to my recruiting career, the devil did not wear Prada, but wore corporate heels instead. I came to Aquent after working for several years as a recruiting coordinator in the finance industry. Needless to say, the challenge and diversity of work offered by that position had about as much pizzazz as a Member's Only jacket. Now I put people in the center of the runway by specializing in the recruitment of amazing talent in the interactive, print, & marketing worlds!

I'm always excited about meeting top of the line design and marketing talent. This stems from my intense love for fashion design (in case you couldn't tell) and I'm always a sucker for a great advertising. I also have a degree in marketing that I have brought to good use here.

The people I’ve brought into the Aquent family have gone on to become model talent in a high gloss community of designers, writers & marketing experts that come from every field: entertainment, healthcare, consumer products, advertising, technical and more. Getting to know these talent and their strengths is what allows me to wow clients. When it comes to finding L.A.’s Next Top Talent, I’m the guru that brings it all together!
