Ranny Levy (1,488)’s Summary

Founder and President of the Coalition for Quality Children's Media, a 19-year-old 501-c-3 nonprofit media advocacy organization and producer of the KIDS FIRST! Film Festival. She is a frequent speaker about children's media and her media reviews are available at Amazon.com, Kidsfirst.org, Grand Magazine, TiVo's KidZone, Smart Televison Alliance, Moving Pictures Magazine, Sports Illustrated for Kids, Toys R Us and Parenthood.com. Sponsors include major entertainment companies such as Fox, Sony, Universal, Warner, Starz, E1 Entertainment.

    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    January 2000 — Present (11 years 1 month)

    The KIDS FIRST! Film Festival showcases outstanding films for and by children, ages 2 through 18. Through collaborations with established film festivals, non-profit film centers, children's and art museums and other similar community organizations, it reaches an audience greater than a half million annually. Eighty percent of the venues screen films year-round, not just once a year at an annual event. The Festival is supported by major media companies such as Paramount, Twentieth Century Fox, Universal Studios, Discovery Kids and Starz. In 2006, more than 10,000 individual screenings of 300+ films took place at 100+ venues nationwide. Films that are screened at the Festival come from major studios 40%, independent producers 40%, and youth 20%.
