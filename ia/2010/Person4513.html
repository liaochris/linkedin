Andrew Barbash, MD’s Summary

I am an active practicing neurologist with a focus on stroke care and a co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of Apractis Solutions"Collaboration Partners". I oversaw the development and operational deployment of the EMR at Kaiser Mid-Atlantic for 7 years and in 2005 we began the collaborative helped form Apractis as a service business focused on supporting clinical workflow needs, and how to leverage services and tools available to enhance efficiency.

I am also one of the founders of the UpDox Network (www.updox.com)
