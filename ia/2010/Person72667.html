Jason and Amber Gardner’s Summary

At Gardner Team Real Estate, we've been successfully bringing homebuyers and sellers together in Sherwood Oregon and throughout the greater Portland Metro area - and at the same time pay it forward with every home sold.

As one of the most recognizable Realtors making a positive difference in the Sherwood Oregon area, Jason and Amber Gardner stand by their core philosophy, “Committed to YOUR Success.” The Gardner Team believes the important things in life start at home. Insuring our clients enjoy their experience in the purchase or sale of that home is of paramount importance.

The level of service and results you can expect from our top-producing real estate team is best described by our clients personal experiences - a collection of video testimonials and letters of recommendation are available on our website at www.TheGardnerTeam.net or by going to a new website we're building just for our raving fans, www.RealEstateStories.net

Working primarily by referral, many look our team as "the" areas preferred real estate experts for those looking to purchase or sell residential homes, luxury estates, land, acreage or wine country properties and new construction real estate in and around Sherwood.

Through consistently setting standards of excellence in our field, a focused commitment to servicing your needs and providing unmatched value at all times, we'll utalize our extensive use of technology, powerful online presence, professional network, strategic planning and sterling reputation for marketing and results to create demand for your home that will result in a stronger sale, in a shorter period of time, with service that will make you smile.

If hiring a professional that delivers a higher level of service and results is important to you - we're your team!
