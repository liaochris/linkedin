Jay Newman’s Summary

Founding partner of LBJ Investment Partners, LLC. LBJ IP is a vibrant new managment consulting group that is helping small and start-up companies "Get Past Go!" Look for our new Book "6 Principles for Winning the Bigger Game"--which we hope to have available in May!

Co-Founded the National Civiity Center, a non-profit organization facilitating dialogues empowering people to make a positive impact in their communities.

Managed a 150 employee Intermediate School District with a $15 million budget. Transitioned from a traditional multiple goal/objective oriented school improvement plan to a targeted, strategic plan, outcomes-based organization; using Peter Senge’s 5 Disciplines, Jim Collins' Good to Great, Stephen Covey’s 7-Habits and the Fish Philosophy as a foundation.

Established a Building Strong Youth Council using the Search Institute’s Asset Survey for developing youth-focused initiatives designed to build assets in youth. This council has representatives from all local agencies, the faith community, the non-profit sector, and local business and industry.

Facilitated the creation of the St. Joseph County Sustainability Council. This non-profit seeks to promote a more sustainable community through adherence to the 3-Ps: People, Planet, and Prosperity. Our specific focus is on promoting resposnible entrepreneurship, nature trail develoment, and educating the public about sustainability issues.

Co-Founded Friends and Family Supporting Our Military a non-profit organization dedicated to helping the families and loved ones of our deployed military personnel. Our mission is: to MENTOR families and friends of Military Personnel by SHARING OUR EXPERIENCES and CONNECTING those families and friends to available RESOURCES. To assist us in this mission we will create greater COMMUNITY AWARENESS. The Keys to this mission are: MENTOR, SHARE, CONNECT, and PUBLICIZE!

Jay Newman’s Specialties:

Facilitator of Dialogues that produce actionable decisions which lead to more effective solutions to exisitng problems while empowering people to move beyond the deficit mentality and on towards abundance thinking.
