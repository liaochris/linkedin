Claire Dunning’s Summary

Chartered designer, chartered marketer and FCIM working in partnership with a broad spectrum of SME's, large corporates, public sector and third sector organisations to help boost their profile and profits. Experienced in delivering a range of marketing products and services such as Branding Workshops, Strategic Overviews and Communication Planning. Always interested in helping getting your message out there in a distinctively different way.

Well versed in representing both the chamber network and Dunning in the media, at formal presentations with professional bodies, key city stakeholders, enterprise organisations and networking events and frequently speak at public and civic engagements.

Claire Dunning’s Specialties:

Corporate communication, branding and identity, strategic marketing, social media, digital design, stakeholder engagement and public speaking.
