Ruchi Srivastava’s Summary

Vosche Nighzon Publication is wholly owned subsidiary of Vosche Nighzon Consultant’s Pvt Ltd, a group which has been in existence since 1995. It has been primarily in the field of human resources and education related vertical since inception. In 2000, it became the first ISO 9002 certified human resources consultants in the country.

The Promoter

Mr Shaji Jose the CMD of this group is a management graduate from Bombay University and professionally has been associated in the past with renowned organizations like Cadbury’s, East West Airlines and Chrysler motors Dubai. He was a visiting faculty with a couple of management colleges for PG courses. Counseling being his forte he apart from consultancy moved on to publish his own broadsheet weekly newspaper “Aspirations Unlimited” in the Mumbai market.

The Product – “Aspirations Unlimited”

It’s a broadsheet, India’s first aspiration family newspaper in the newsprint format catering to the growth of every individual of the family in the areas of Personal Growth, Academic Growth and Professional Growth. It caters to the following target audience:
• Special children
• Teachers
• Parents
• Working executives
• Women
• Students
• Entrepreneurs
• Human Resources

It also has sections dedicated to Health & Wellness, Alternative Therapies like Aromatherapy / Color Therapy / Tarot Cards / Music Therapy / Ayurveda / Crystal Therapy / Art Therapy.

It’s the first product in the family category. An apolitical, no masala newspaper, where the parents will not be embarrassed to read the paper along with their growing children.

Competition:
As of now there is no direct competition in the country as it is a highly specialized focus area. The company is intending to take it national in the second year of operation and then taking it international because the said subject has no boundaries, caste, creed or culture.
