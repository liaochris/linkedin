Jeffrey Peterson’s Summary

Director at Inter123 Corp. which operates and invests in new media companies.

Best known as founder of Quepasa, the first online community for U.S. Hispanics to go public on Nasdaq (June 1999). Raised $150 million, investors included Gloria Estefan, Sony and Telemundo. Post-IPO market capitalization reached $450 million. Ranked #1 most popular Hispanic online community (2000) Again served as CEO of Quepasa from 2002-2006, resulting in another $150 million increase in market capitalization and a sale to billionaire investor Richard Scott. Quepasa today remains among the most widely recognized online brands focused on the Hispanic market segment.
