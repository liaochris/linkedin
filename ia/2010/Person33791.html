Cassandra Marrone’s Summary

I recruit high-performing, proven C-level executives.

My clients are successful, often ground-breaking, companies looking for additional leadership to help them grow, succeed, transform. The industries I work in include:
- Technology
- Consumer web
- Entertainment meets technology
- Renewable energy
- Exchanges & Prediction Markets
- Highly regulated industries such as gaming, financial services, etc.

I work closely with my clients to understand their business, culture, and the mandate of the role they need to fill. I focus on understanding the nuances of the role, and delivering candidates who hit the mark.

I have a 15 year track record of closing searches, often very quickly. My team of researchers and associates is just as experienced.
