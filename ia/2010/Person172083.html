Richard Bernhardt’s Summary

For over twenty-years, I have worked with Silicon Valley firms in leadership, management and executive and strategic advisory roles.

It has been my pleasure to work directly with management teams, CEO's, BOD's and investing entities to enhance their businesses performance, guide them into new or expanded markets and focus on plans for funding and strategic alliances. I assist them in developing and implementing effective operational strategy and internal growth management to be an effective company for both organic building and reaching milestones or liquidity events.

I am a seasoned corporate communications, public and external relations director and have successes in both business development and marketing with direct demonstrable bottom line results.

I have a proven record working with companies to review their functional,directional and internal operations to assure that they are tuned to provide the best results and for investors to understand the dynamics of deal-flow potential.

I have served as an effective counselor and leader and bring a broad range of hands-on skills to any client or business.
