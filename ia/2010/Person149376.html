Mike Remedios, CFP, CIMA’s Summary

I am one of three Certified Financial Planners (CFP's) that form the RKJ Group at Smith Barney. I also carry the CIMA (Certified Investment Management Analyst) designation. This is significant from the standpoint of rarity (only 1% of all advisors) and from the skill set it brings. As a team, we have over 40 years of experience in the investment and financial services industry, and specialize in comprehensive wealth planning. Please see our website for more information:
