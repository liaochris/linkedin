Hans Damen’s Summary

Fleet Vision is a group of leading consultants providing its services to the international market for users and providers of fleet related services.

Our mission is to improve the competitive advantage of our clients by understanding their strategic goals.

Our independent and rational review programmes are all grafted on our performance management system. This ensures unbiased business logic and added value derived from every step we take with our clients.

The overall objective of FleetVision is to help manage and simplify the fleet industry and to transfer the related cost savings to our customers.

FleetVision's typical client is an organization operating on a pan-European level. Often this will be large European and US companies who are looking to consolidate their European fleet operations.
