Dorothy Johnson’s Summary

A creative buzzsaw with an equally healthy appetite for strategy and schedules always looking to collaborate with other passionate ideators to produce industry-leading campaigns.

Creative direction and product management has taken multiple products and services from design through production and initiated brand enhancing transformations that have empowered a range of businesses to look good, create buzz and improve sales.

In addition to producing content for a number of niche sites including content management for Digital Capital Week, Dot is also the author of "Dorothy On The Hill"/@dorthyonthehill, a unexpectedly popular blog and twitter feed devoted to discussing the challenges and excitement of the creative process.
