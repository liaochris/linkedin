Sasha Muradali’s Summary

Sasha Muradali is a 2007 public relations graduate from the University of Florida with a M.A International Administration from the University of Miami (’08).

She is assertive, organized and loves the worldwide web. Within six weeks of starting her own blog, the Little Pink Book, it took off and was nominated for the 2009 PR Blog Awards as the “Best Up & Coming” blog. She was recently named one of the "Top 50 Tweeple to Follow on Twitter" because of her dedication to social media.

Her passion for the field shines through in everything she does. She is loyal and determined. Her goals are to work for an public relations firm enabling her to network and learn in order to grow as a well-rounded, global professional.
