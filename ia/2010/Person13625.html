Anita Sinclair’s Summary

Since 2001, Hagen/Sinclair Research Recruiting has built a national reputation for responsive, resourceful qualitative research recruiting. We are a virtual company of market research professionals with over twenty years’ combined experience in research recruiting and a proven method for delivering top-notch respondents. We listen closely to clients’ objectives and devise a custom recruiting strategy for every project.

Hagen/Sinclair Oakland's SPECIALTY AREAS include:

B2B: TOP EXECUTIVE recruitment from C-Level to management, as well as professional and administrative staff.

B2C: CUSTOMER and POTENTIAL MARKET research recruitment;

INTERACTIVE bulletin boards, REAL-TIME ONLINE group discussions and longer term 'hives' requiring respondent participation over weeks or months;

B2B or B2C phone and face-to-face interviews;

CONSUMER focus groups, bulletin boards, usability studies, hives, product evaluation and ethnography;

LOW INCIDENCE and MULTIPLE MARKET recruiting.

INDUSTRIES: We recruit from ALL conceivable industries. Some of our primary areas include: Education, Finance, Government, Hi-Tech, IT, Insurance, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Physicians, Retail and Small Business.

REGIONS: The continental US, CANADA and the UK.

DATABASE: Internal database with 200K records, constantly updated.

Our philosophy is to build and maintain positive relationships with our clients, staff and research participants in order to provide research recruiting of the highest quality.

If you have a project you wish to discuss at any level, please contact Anita Sinclair at sinclaira@hagensinclair.com or on LinkedIn at http://www.linkedin.com/in/anitasinclair.
