Ben Hunter’s Summary

My name is Ben Hunter aka ‘Sabaoth’ I have been involved in the games industry over the past 9 years when I alpha/beta tested a game known as EVE-ONLINE. Since then I joined CCP’s volunteer division where I ended up administrating two divisions at separate times, I am currently in charge of the Bug Hunter division which aids the QA department.

I have recently just graduated from my Computer Design Degree and will be continuing my education on 3D games Modelling Masters Degree. I have knowledge of various game engines, modelling techniques and texturing packages. My goal is to finish my masters degree and further my artistic ability leading me to a placement in the art department of a games company.

I have a strong passion for all games, MMO’s in particular and have experience in running and leading reasonably sized organisations of people.
