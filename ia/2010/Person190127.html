Misty Funk’s Summary

Hi, I'm the owner of SimpleCityLife.com. I am passionate about internet companies that focus on personal branding and informational marketing. In May of 2010, I took the leap from practicing full-time in my chiropractic clinic to spending most of my time exploring a business model that was a better fit for me. I am super excited about my new life and new possibilities.

Simple City Life, a website designed to help urbanites living among the hustle and bustle infuse their lives with a bit of simplicity and relaxation, began August 2010. Connect with SCL on Facebook, Twitter, or just check out the blog. One this is sure, I hope you enjoy the journey to simplicity as much as I do.

In addition to my new found internet passion, I also have a private practice on Saturdays-only. At Tranquility Chiropractic Studio, I'm "Dr. Funk", and I provide treatment for headaches, myofascial pain, neck pain and back pain. I treat patients using spinal manipulation, myofascial release, stretching, and simple strengthening exercises to help reduce pain, improve range of motion, and restore joint function.
