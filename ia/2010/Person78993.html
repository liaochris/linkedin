    Program in Social Dynamics and Collective Intelligence (SDCI)

    Online electronic communities provide a vast amount of raw data on their user's beliefs, behaviors, and relationships over time. This presents currently unmet challenges in scaling up learning algorithms and on-line learning over large, dynamic networks of agents and massive volumes of data.

    The SRI International Artificial Intelligence Center's Social Dynamics and Collective Intelligence Program drives research and applications in distributed intelligence through the combination of advances in machine learning, social network analysis, and adaptive computing. We're creating software that is socially-aware, learns independently, and helps improve people's lives.
