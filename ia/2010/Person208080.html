Anders Trewe’s Summary

ColdCut COBRA, the Cutting Extinguisher, is specially developed as a rescue and fire-fighting tool and has piercing and cutting abillities for all types of materials. The water jetting technique for cutting is producing a spray and mist which produces the cooling and extinguishing capabilities. This water jetting method has a broad range of applications in rescue works and in fire fighting. Coldcut Systems holds several patents for the alications and techniques mentioned. Our customers range from rescue services to industry, off shore, shipping and military.

Anders is a generalist with experience from systems development, systems dynamics, change management, project management, procurement, process development, QA, mergers & aquisitions, personnel development, education, real estate development, sales and after sales.
