Maja Vujovic 2.0’s Summary

My passion for Web 2.0 is a result of my life-long interest in computer-based communication. Having successfully practiced Communications in the distinct work cultures of Europe, Asia & North America, I am now a professional educator and organizational development consultant in South East Europe, providing Communications training, capacity development & knowledge management services. Offline experiences in strategic communications, crisis management, public relations & business affairs complement my varied online activities.

There are no barriers I cannot breach with carefully crafted communication. Campaigns I created won coverage in news services & trade media worldwide. My pragmatic creativity, along with advanced Crisis Management skills, has allowed me to smoothly handle many high-risk, high-profile situations.

My clients rely on my writing, editing & research skills, in English, Serbian or French. I have spent more than 20 years writing and editing effective articles, speeches, strategies, presentations, reports, scripts, advertising, PR & marketing copy in three continents. I handle rhyme very well and, long ago, enjoyed translating medieval and renaissance drama in verse.

For twenty years, I have worked in front & behind a microphone & camera, as a TV & radio writer, host, reporter and producer. A seasoned presenter, I am at ease in front of audiences and can carry a dialogue with anyone.

Having been fortunate to learn pre-press, database design & website development; I know the production world inside out, in marketing, publishing, broadcasting & film.

In leading complex teams to budgets and deadlines, I rely on planning and motivating, as a committed problem-solver with unusual organizational abilities. My secret strength is in research; I am a natural Knowledge Manager - an "information bank". This ability to classify vast amounts of information has put me in the editor's seat on two encyclopedias to date.
