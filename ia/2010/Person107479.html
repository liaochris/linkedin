Jeffrey T. Pollock’s Summary

Biography – 50 Words
Mr. Pollock is a Senior Director for Oracle Fusion Middleware. He is a data integration thought leader, book author and senior executive for enterprise software companies. Jeff’s passion for next-generation, semantics-aware, and metadata-driven middleware developed while working as a CTO and engineer for Cerebra, Modulant, Ernst & Young and Amgen.

Biography – 150 Words
Mr. Pollock is a thought-leader in the field of data integration and author of “Semantic Web for Dummies” and "Adaptive Information.” Jeff a Senior Director with Oracle Fusion Middleware, responsible for management of Oracle’s data integration product portfolio, Mr. Pollock was formerly an independent systems architect for the Defense Department, Vice President of Technology at Cerebra and Chief Technology Officer of Modulant, developing semantic middleware platforms and inference-driven SOA platforms since 2001. Throughout his career, he has architected, designed, and built information integration solutions for Fortune 500 and US Government clients. Previously, Mr. Pollock was a Principal Engineer with Modem Media and Senior Architect with Ernst & Young’s Center for Technology Enablement. He is a frequent speaker at industry conferences, author for industry journals, member of W3C and OASIS, and formerly an engineering instructor with UC Berkeley’s Extension for object-oriented systems, software development process and enterprise systems architecture.

Jeffrey T. Pollock’s Specialties:

Software Development and Delivery, Semantic Technology, Data Integration, Business Intelligence, Solution Architecture, Software Architecture, Client Services Delivery, Business Development, and Enterprise Middleware/SOA.
