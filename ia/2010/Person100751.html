Joel Howard’s Summary

Licensed Professional Engineer and General Contractor. I currently provide engineering and business development support for a heavy haul transportation and rigging company. Responsibilities include development and design of rigging devices, review of rigging devices for safety and development of lift plan drawings.

Prior, I worked as a consultant in the building industry. Engineering experience includes design of wide variety of structures, from one to five stories. Experience designing with steel, concrete, masonry, and wood. Investigation experience with blast damage structures, structural failures, and corroding/failing structures.

Professional Engineer Lisenses in VA, NC, SC, GA.

My goals are to use past experience to continue development on a professional and personal level.

Joel Howard’s Specialties:

Project management and coordination within contractor and consulting settings. Development and design of rigging devices. Stress Analysis.
