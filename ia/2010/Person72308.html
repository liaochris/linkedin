Gabriel Sasson’s Summary

I am currently an Investment Analyst at General Motors Asset Management where I have experience underwriting real estate direct acquisitions/developments, joint venture partnerships and fund investments. I am directly responsible for supporting the Managing Director and a team of Portfolio Managers in the allocation of a multi-billion dollar real estate equity portfolio on behalf of the General Motors pension fund and other non-affiliated institutional clients.

Specifically, my duties include negotiating deal terms, preparing financial analysis based on due diligence and market research materials and writing executive summary reports for the purpose of presenting investment recommendations to the GMAM Investment Approval Committee. I have experience underwriting office, multifamily, retail, industrial and hospitality equity investments as well as whole loan and securitized debt investments.

In my previous position at the Schonbraun McCann Group, a national real estate finance and consulting firm, I performed property level financial due diligence for various institutional clients, including some of the industry’s most successful REIT’s, hedge funds, and privately-held firms.

Gabriel Sasson’s Specialties:

Investment Analyst with background underwriting institutional real estate debt and equity investments of directly-held assets, partnerships and funds across office, retail, multifamily and industrial property types. Skilled at structuring, evaluating and negotiating deal terms, partnership agreements and management fees. Experienced in the ongoing review and monitoring of investment portfolios, including oversight of external managers. Excellent quantitative and qualitative analysis skills.
