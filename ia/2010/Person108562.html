Julien Hofer’s Experience

  • Managing Director

    Datascope Recruitment Ltd.

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    July 1991 — Present (18 years 8 months)

    I started Datascope in the middle of a recession with a desk in shared offices in Soho Square with no money, no contacts, no recruitment experience and a glimmer of an idea. Not surprisingly I got no-where (except being kicked out because my host didn't pay the rent). I then teamed up with a man called Justin Hill whose enthusiasm for games, coupled with an article in the Financial Times convinced me that here was a gap in the market. So we announced ourselves "The Leading Consultancy For The Games Industry" and no-one challenged us for a few months until Lis Welsh and Answers came along.

    To cut a long story short, the company very slowly grew and with 20 people in the US and UK, we now believe that we are the largest games recruitment consultancy in the world.

    Clients include:

    SCE WWS (Sony), Rockstar, Kuju Studios, Guerrilla Games, Bizarre Creations, Lionhead, Ninja Theory, Crytek UK, Evolution, TT Games / Fusion, Monumental Games, Codemasters, Gusto Games, Mere Mortals, Rocksteady Games, BigBig, Jagex, Media Molecule, RealTime Worlds, BlackRock Studio, Splash Damage, Blitz Games Studios, Geomerics, Eutechnyx, Krome, Team Bondi, Next Level Games, Longtail Studios, Starbreez, Activision, Relentless, Frontier, Zoe Mode, Rebellion, THQ, Juice, and I've run out of breath!

    Locations are as diverse as: London, Amsterdam, Canada, Guildford, Brighton, Leamington, Oxford, Scotland, Dundee, Liverpool, Runcorn, Russia, Australia, Nottingham, Newcastle, Sweden, Kent, Manchester, China, Spain, Germany…
