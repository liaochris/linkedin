Victoria Zelin’s Summary

SEASONED BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT EXECUTIVE selling game-changing consulting services and intangibles to organizational leaders. As a trusted advisor, I’ve turned skeptics into committed clients at companies like Chubb, Ernst & Young, McGraw-Hill, Novartis, Merrill Lynch, PSE&G, Wyndham Worldwide, Wal-Mart, Honeywell, Johnson & Johnson and Pepsi.

I am currently working in Deloitte's Sustainability and Climate Change practice.

As SVP and Sustainability Practice Leader at Hudson Gain, I led the organization's entry into Sustainability, co-authoring a research study on The Role of the Chief Sustainability Officer in Corporations and speaking at conferences and on panels.

My contribution lies in building long term relationships for the benefit of the organization and its clients and constituencies. Keen diagnostic, systems-thinking, communication, innovation and connecting skills allow me to network with senior business leaders to provide strategic guidance and build a compelling business case for investment and change.
