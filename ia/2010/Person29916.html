Bruce Poling’s Summary

I am always looking to learn more and stay on the cutting edge. I believe we should never stop learning regardless what age we are. My journey over the past 25 years .. from Lifeguard to trades assistant, to Tradesman to Business owner back to college (yes continued education after 30 is possible and I did enjoy the process) and eventually to industry consultant, has taken me to the point where I am dedicated to bringing the best possible business management solutions to my clients. Currently, this is in the form of a fantastic ERP solutions that performs extremely well and will be sourced and suited to each client specifically. We work directly with our clients and their employees right through implementation and continue to support them from that point forward with coaching and training.

I also work with struggling companies to help them turn their struggles into success stories.

We have developed an awesome ERP program that we sell, install, integrate and support. I am very excited to finally have found a complete ERP package that we can take to the market with no shortcomings that will transform any company into a collaborative workforce. I would be happy to provide you with a demo!

Personal interaction directly with people within a company is the only way to understand its needs. When we can understand why someone does or does not do something we can then assist them to implement positive change in their routines. My goal as a consultant is not to create a LARGE group of clients, but rather a small group of hugely successfully ones.

Implementing CNC machines and training people on the use of Planit software, (CAD CAM) has taught me that it is usually the core of a company that needs improvement first since real change can only happen at the top of the company. All companies need to communicate effectively and collaboratively.

Having spent many years as a consultant, it is clear every company should use a good ERP solution.
