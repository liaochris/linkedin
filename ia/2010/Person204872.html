T.J. Summers’s Summary

I have dedicated my career to providing unparalleled development solutions to the interactive entertainment industry. My business partner Andy Cheren and I started our journey in 2000 when we formed Digital Artist Management. DAM is the leading full-time talent provider specializing in creative, technical and executive recruiting services in the interactive entertainment industry. We recognized a growing demand in temporary employment in the games industry, so we created and managed a secondary arm of the company, DAM Consultants, which focuses on permanent and temporary/on-site contract staffing services for the video game, VFX and emerging technology industries. In 2008, we formed Production Road which represents a new initiative built on the foundations of Digital Artist Management. Production Road provides clients a full service production studio with professional capabilities in the video game, visual effects, feature film, television, on-line and all other forms of digital entertainment.
