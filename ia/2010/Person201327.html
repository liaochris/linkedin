Terry Moving and Storage’s Summary

At Terry Moving and Storage, the safety and security of your belongings is our highest priority. We have state of the art security systems to protect your belongings, and rugged wooden container crates to keep your stored items safe and secure.

Terry Moving and Storage brings the storage to you.
We can bring the crates to your house or business on our flat-bed truck, and load your items into storage crates on location. We can let you go into your crates to get items as you need them. Just give us a call, and we can get your crates down.

* Accessing your storage is a free service.
* We never charge you fees to get to your items in storage.

Terry Moving and Storage can save you time, money and effort when you use our storage vaults. We bring the vaults to you on our flatbed truck, load them at your house, apartment, office or business, store them in our secure indoor warehouse, and then bring them to your new house or business and unload them for you. We can save you money over a self storage facility if your new home or office is not ready because we only handle your furniture for an onload and offload. We don’t have to load it, unload it into a self storage, then load it back onto the truck and offload it at your new home or office.

Terry Moving and Storage can also cater the storage size to suit your needs. We can load the vaults full, and our professional movers can load a vault much better than most people can load a self-storage unit. You only pay for the vaults you need. We load the vaults full, so your storage costs will go down.

A perfect solution for your home improvement or office renovation needs.

If you are doing major renovations on your house or office, and need to store your furniture and belongings while the work is being done, we can save you a lot of trouble and heartache.
