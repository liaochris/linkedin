Blake Jonas’s Summary

Cascade Enforcement Agency (Cascade) is a Public Safety and Security Company established in 1997. Owner and Chief, Blake Jonas started the company nearly thirteen years ago wanting to make a positive difference in the community around him. Blake’s experience in law enforcement, both private and public, gave him the skills to beat the odds and turn a small business into a thriving diverse enterprise. Cascade offers various security solutions to help its clients protect their assets and wellbeing. Cascade provides alarm monitoring and thirty minutes or less alarm response. Cascade also offers courtesy patrol services as well as on-site agents and virtual monitoring through its 24/7 dispatch operation. Cascade currently operates and is licensed in both Oregon and Washington. Its patrol division covers territory ranging from Hillsboro to Gresham and Vancouver to Wilsonville.
Cascade also offers training to its clients on identity theft protection and CPR/ First Aide /AED. The office space is completely secured with an electronic key card access system and CCTV camera system. Cascade runs on a 24/7 security operation with general office hours being 7 AM to 4 PM Monday through Friday with available weekend hours by appointment only.
Beginning this year Cascade opened a new company; Cascade Systems Integration (CSI), which provides our clients with the installation of alarm systems, CCTV camera systems and fire systems. CSI also has great wireless solutions and mobile camera systems that help cut down on targeted crime areas and assists catching criminals in the act. Opening this new company has enabled Cascade to provide its clients with total peace of mind while meeting all their security needs.
