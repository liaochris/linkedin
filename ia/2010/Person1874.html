Jodi Gregory’s Summary

I am passionate about providing information to help your business make more informed business decisions by providing you with secondary market research (articles, social media postings, white papers), industry analysis, company information and competitive intelligence.

How I help you: I increase your awareness of the activities of your competition so you can compete more effectively in your marketplace. I provide you with the data you need to have a better understanding of a product or service in which you are interested.

With WebSmart Seminars, I teach you how to be more productive searchers when using Google and other search engines and in locating sites in the invisible web.

I am the the founder and President of Access Information Services, Inc. I have been in the business of providing organizations with targeted market and industry analysis, competitor tracking, customized industry monitoring, litigation support and other information to address business needs since 1988. My clients range from small, independently owned companies and law firms to Fortune 50 global corporations. I am a partner in WebSmart Seminars teaching businesses how to search Google and other search engines.

I was selected to share my strategies for starting and building a successful information research service in the book, titled Super Searchers Make It On Their Own published by Information Today. I am a columnist for Cyberskeptic's Guide to Internet Research and recently completed a term as the President of the Association of Independent Information Professionals. (www.aiip.org)
