Leah Chaney’s Summary

Over my career, I have enhanced my marketing and communication skills with some of Austin’s leading technology companies. I was the first full-time employee hired by OtherInbox, Inc., where I helped grow and manage a team of people to drive consumer adoption of our product and to provide top customer service to our existing customers. My responsibilities included marketing, event planning, public relations initiatives, business development, and developing a strategic social media roadmap that I designed and executed to grow brand awareness and word of mouth marketing. As a Senior Community Manager for Bazaarvoice, Inc., I served the needs of our top strategic clients. I spent much of my time analyzing market trends and consumer dynamics and developing creative and innovative strategies that would differentiate our clients' products in the marketplace. I organized and ran speaking engagements on social media techniques, ran live demonstrations and webinars, and coached Fortune 500 companies and C level executives on how to leverage social media campaigns to increase brand awareness and ROI. I have been recognized by local and national organizations for my creative marketing strategies and techniques both online and offline.
