    (Fishery industry)

    June 2005 — October 2005 (5 months)

    - Contracted to NOAA to collect data onboard fishing vessels.

    - Biological sampling methods included taking length measurements and age structures from fish as well as obtaining biometrics, genetic and tissue samples from marine mammals, sea turtles and birds incidentally caught in fishing gear.
    - Completed National Marine Fisheries Service training and qualifications in addition to a fishing vessel safety and coldwater survival-training course.
