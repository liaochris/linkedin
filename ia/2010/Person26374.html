Brad Barbour, CPA’s Summary

I am a CPA that assists clients in recruiting and retaining top accounting and finance professionals on a temporary and permanent basis. I provide this service by taking a systematic approach to recruiting including observing the corporate culture, in depth inquiries and my knowledge of accounting and financial principles. Not only does this approach provide the candidate with the best skill set, but it also helps establish and maintain lasting relationships.

CPA Firms is one industry I have been able to provide exceptional value. Having spent five years in Public Accounting, both as an Auditor and Tax Return Preparer, I intimately know the positions CPA Firms are trying to fill and I know what specific skill set, education and work experience to look for in a top recruit. Couple this with my extensive network of professionals and I am able to provide true, lasting value.

I am constantly looking to network on the web and off to CPAs, Accountants, Finance Professionals, Attorneys, Real Estate Brokers, Human Resource Managers, Recruiters and various other professionals.

Brad Barbour, CPA’s Specialties:

I specialize in recruiting accounting and finance professionals for the follow job titles: CPA; Chief Financial Officer (CFO); Controller; Accounting Manager; Senior Accountant; Staff Accountant; Financial Analyst; Audit Manager; Senior Auditor; Tax Manager; Tax Senior and Internal Auditor.

In addition, I specialize in recruiting accounting and finance professionals in the following industries: Healthcare; Manufacturing; Public Accounting/CPA Firms and Nonprofit.
