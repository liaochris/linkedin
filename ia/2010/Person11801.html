Andrew Harlow’s Summary

I'm an imaginative designer, animator and illustrator with over 6 years of experience working for leading digital and integrated advertising agencies in London. I enjoy being involved in every stage of the creative process. From producing creative concepts and then seeing those through from visualisation to project completion.

I’ve been fortunate to work on a large variety of projects, from digital and integrated advertising campaigns, large scale web design and build projects as well as more bespoke digital content for a variety of clients, such as T-Mobile, Royal Mail, Gordon’s Gin, Orange and Masterfoods.
