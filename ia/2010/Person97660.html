Jimmy Goolsby’s Summary

I am the Senior Recruiter for Stark Talent. Our corporate office is in Austin, TX. From this office I recruit for companies through the USA. While my expertise is in IT recruiting, I am very comfortable and qualified to recruit for the Business/Corporate, Engineering and Energy industries.

I have been recruiting for over 11 years and have thoroughly enjoyed every day. I specialize in finding the ideal or "bang-on" candidates with unique skill sets. I work hard to provide the best service possible to my clients as well as the professionals that I represent.

I excel in working directly with client managers and locating perfect candidates for permanent positions. I have maintained an average of 15 contract placements for the past 8 years and normally place 3 permanent candidates each year
