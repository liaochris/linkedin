Jeff Heusser’s Summary

I am currently a freelance Flame/Inferno compositor doing visual effects for Feature Films and Commercials. A list of feature film credits can be seen at imdb: http://us.imdb.com/name/nm0381899.

I am also a co-founder of the leading industy web site fxguide.com. Fxguide provides news, tips, podcasts, forums for the visual effects community. A sister site fxphd.com is an online film school offering over 30 courses in all areas of visual effects, at an affordable price.

After working in television for many years as a Producer, Director and Executive Producer I found myself spending a good deal of time in post production facilities doing show opens, etc. This was when the business of post production was new so when an opportunity arose to move into it, I jumped at it.

This career has led me from Cincinnati to New York to Chicago and for the last 10+ years, Los Angeles.
