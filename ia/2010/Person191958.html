Mark Smith’s Summary

The Hammerstone Institutional Forum (HIF)

Real-time News Flow for Traders and Investors BY Traders and Investors

What is Hammerstone Institutional Forum?
A private messaging system allowing institutional investors and traders to share news flow, proprietary technical levels of the market, ideas and commentary on a real-time basis.

Why is it important for your trading and investing?
The most valuable function of the forum is the ability for the Trader and Investor to see what is really happening in the market, without all the noise. Rarely does the market or an individual stock make a big move without someone in the forum alerting the other members of the move, usually followed by an explanation.

Who is our Forum for?
Professional Investors and Traders; all subscribers are pre-qualified and pay a monthly fee. This is no chat for day traders; rather, it is a group of successful institutional investors, each identified by his or her full name and firm.

The great thing about the HIF is that everybody gets to know each other and really do their best to help each other out, and that won’t change, no matter how many subscribers we add.

Secure and Compliant
With HIF, you can achieve the highest level of message security required. Internal conversations remain physically stored on a dedicated server that is inaccessible by the outside world, giving a completely internal and secure environment that utilizes a secure Network. For firms that require the utmost level of message security, we have the answer.
