Gail Nichols’s Summary

Gail is co-founder of TheNowMall.com & Willow CSN (now Arise.com) with 25 years experience in sales, marketing & management (financial planning, banking, rapid fulfillment & franchising).

TheNowMall is a patented web-based Local Store Aggregator + Multi-Channel Marketing + Rapid Online Order Fulfillment (ROOF) Network for Time-Sensitive commodities (Groceries, Retail, Restaurant, Pharmacy) proven to raise participating shopping center and store sales 25% & profit 58%.

ROOF is the powerful way for shopping centers & stores to recapture rapidly growing Online sales in-store now going to web-based competitors + gain new sales from the 80% of shoppers who can’t easily travel to stores (seniors, disabled, busy families & offices).

Rapid Online Order Fulfillment (ROOF) is 100% turnkey enabling malls & stores to offer affordable 1-hr professional In-Store Shopping & Delivery from their stores 24/7/365.

ROOF is a revolutionary Multi-Channel Marketing & Distribution system virtually eliminating the 30% returns of online orders delivered in 3-10 days from remote warehouses. ROOF enhances retailers' e-commerce programs with higher profit & improved customer loyalty.

TheNowMall is the “gatekeeper” of this Virtual Retail Network via its Consortium of Shopping Centers, Stores & CyberValet Shoppers.

Google, CitySearch, Peapod & FedEx do only aspects of ROOF; which is the sole entity with all key components: the patented “Holy Grail” of LOCAL Search, Shopping & Rapid Order Fulfillment from stores 24/7/365.

Our “Secret Sauce” includes the technological sophistication of our systems, pricing & marketing programs, making usage simple & rewarding for participants. Implementation is Easy As Plugging In A Toaster.

Earlier, we created Willow CSN (now Arise.com) as the pioneer in virtual off-premise call center staffing with a patented business method & technologies. Founders also created other companies with products used worldwide.

Gail Nichols’s Experience

  • Vice Chair & Co-founder

    TheNowMall.com

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2000 — Present (10 years )

    Gail is a co-founder of TheNowMall.com, a patented Internet-based Local Retail Aggregator + Multi-Channel Marketing + Rapid Online Order Fulfillment (ROOF) Network for Time-Sensitive Commodities (Retail, Grocery, Pharmacy, Restaurant and Fast Food).

    The Network’s proprietary technology and systems have been refined over years of extensive experience and field trials and are proven to raise participating Shopping Center and Store sales by 25% and profits by 58% (or more) from incremental online and in-store orders.

    ROOF service (in-store shopping & delivery) is by professional CyberValet® Shoppers within 1-Hour, 24 / 7 / 365 and FREE Delivery is available. Product prices from participating stores can be lower At-The-Door than In-Store.

  • Co-founder, Executive Vice President

    Willow CSN, Inc. (now Arise.com)

    (Privately Held; Outsourcing/Offshoring industry)

    1990 — 1999 (9 years )

    Willow CSN (now Arise.com) is America’s leading online staffing service for major call centers. TheNowMall and Arise have similar workforce structures through sharing of specialized workers among major corporations using patented Internet-based communication technologies. Willow CSN was purchased in 2005 by Accretive, LLC (chaired by Edgar Bronfman, Jr.) after investments by leading venture capitalists and private investors.

  • Co-founder, Executive Vice President

    Holly Home Delivery Supermarket

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 1988 — May 1989 (1 year 5 months)

    We franchised "Grocerias" in several major Canadian cities (Toronto, London, Montreal, Ottawa) that provided 30-minute grocery delivery. We created proprietary software that routed orders to the Groceria located nearest the customer, wherein the order was assembled, scanned and packed within 5 minutes for delivery usually in 20 minutes. Holly Home Supermarkets was acquired by a major shareholder, thus leaving the founders free to pursue their invention of virtual call center technology and systems under Willow Telecommunications Inc. which became Willow CSN Inc. after being invited by the State of Florida to move its operations to Miami from Toronto.
