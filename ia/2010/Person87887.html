James Cord 5925+ [LION]’s Summary

Jacobson Companies - a Supply Chain Management Provider
What can we do for you?

When it comes to supply chain management, the answer is: Leadership, Innovation and Quality Solutions.

With world-class technology as the backbone of our solutions and service. Integrating systems and providing real-time information to help reduce cost by moving your products to market as efficiently as possible.

Our Can Do culture is rooted in customer service, flexibility and innovation. It’s been that way since the Jacobson organization was launched over 40 years ago. Ours is a rich tradition of redefining service for the supply chain management industry.

Our integrated service offerings include:
•Warehousing & Distribution
•Transportation & Asset Based Solutions
•Logistics & Freight Management Solutions
•Contract Packaging & Manufacturing Services
•Staffing Solutions and Temporary Services

Our customized solutions are designed around your specific needs.

We develop solutions that optimize cost and service. And always asking, how can we serve you better?

This commitment to quality customer service led Jacobson to become the first third-party logistics provider to be certified to ISO Quality Management System standards!

Today, our Quality Management System not only serves as the foundation of our on-going Continuous Improvement in day-to-day procedures, it also serves as the cornerstone of the Jacobson Integrated Solutions (JIS).

This process driven approach to problem-solving helps us exceed our customers' expectations, as we tackle service and cost issues within their supply chains.

When we ask . . . "what can we do for you?". . . we’re not just making conversation. We really want to find more ways to serve you, so you can focus your resources on your mission.
