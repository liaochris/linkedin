Ben Mastboom (Ben@OptivityInt.com)’s Summary

H. Ben Mastboom (1967) moved in 2005 with his family from the Netherlands to the USA. He travels extensively to and from Europe to support individuals and organizations with his valuable knowledge on Sales, Marketing and Business Development. Since the early nineties he has led many projects for Big Enterprises and SME which brought them substantial growth and an increase of their productivity in marketing, sales and business development.

Ben has two decades comprehensive international (Europe) experience in business development under his belt. He has been successful in several managerial and executive positions with global operating companies in B2B and B2C environments such as newspaper companies, health insurance, automobile (AAA) service providers, consumer electronics, wholesale industry & others. He has held and holds board positions in international sales and marketing organizations. Ben currently also holds a teaching position at the University of Arnhem, the Netherlands and is a sought-after professional speaker.

Ben is founder of Optivity Nederland b.v. and Optivity International, Inc. (USA). He studied Petrol- and Gas technology and has a business degree in Commercial Economics.

Ben Mastboom (Ben@OptivityInt.com)’s Specialties:

Helping organizations and individuals to achieve more with less by teaching them how to get, keep and develop more customers, convince and influence others more effectively and increase productivity.

Ben is a specialist in account planning & management techniques, train-the-trainer systems and metric development to optimize and drive customer retention, business development and show ROI.

His knowledge can be tapped as an (Interim) Executive, Consultant, Trainer and Professional Speaker.
