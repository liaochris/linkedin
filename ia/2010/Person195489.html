Steven Kowalski, SPHR’s Summary

My professional experience includes over 20 years in line and staff management positions, with over 15 years in HR management. I am an experienced team leader, coach and mentor. I have been a member of SHRM since 1993 and have been SPHR certified since 1997.

I have extensive generalist experience in the areas of employee relations, benefits, recruiting and staffing, process management, organizational development and training, staff development and mentoring, and performance management.

I consider my strengths to be my leadership, project/process management, staffing, and communications.
