Renee Lloyd’s Summary

Renee practiced in the corporate departments of McDermott, Will & Emery as well as Mintz, Levin, Cohn, Ferris, Glovsky & Popeo, PC; in-house as Senior Corporate Counsel at RSA Security Inc.; Associate Director of Technology Transactions at Harvard's Office of Technology Development; and , most recently, Assistant Director and Fellow of the Cyberlaw clinic at the Berkman Center.

Renee Lloyd’s Specialties:

Extensive experience counseling start-up and mature businesses and institutions on corporate and intellectual property matters including: financing, joint ventures, technology acquisitions and spin-offs and other strategic ventures. Expertise in software and patent licensing, including commercial agreements for product development and distribution and other day-to–day operational business issues.
