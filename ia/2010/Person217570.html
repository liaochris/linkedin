Yusuf Matcheswala’s Summary

A U.S. permanent resident with 10 years experience in semiconductor industry with background in analog design.

SKILLS:
*10 years experience in semiconductor industry.
*Analog circuit design experience.
*Writing technical documentation, technical presentation at design reviews and presenting papers at conferences.
* Worked with technology development, device engineering and design teams for successful tape-outs.
*Expert user of various Cadence tools and Cadence SKILL programming.
*Skilled in configuring and deploying Cadence tools and PDKs for HV and LV ICs and for analog and mixed signal design.
*Skilled in DRC, latchup, antenna, metal density, mask generation algorithms, Extract, LVS, pcells, parasitic extraction, simulation, tapeout support etc.
*Write applications using shell scripts, Cadence SKILL language.
*Understanding of power systems concepts like synchronous buck, shoot-through, dead time, efficiency and impact of component losses on efficiency, stability analysis using bode plots/phase margin/gain margin etc.

PROJECTS:
*Designed an op-amp in 0.35um technology with high stability, ~100dB loop gain, high PSRR, wide supply and temperature ranges (3.0-3.6V, -40C to 150C), and low quiescent current over all process corners, used as a V to I buffer as part of a reference circuit.

*Designed an accurate low dropout current mirror in a 0.35um technology (<1% ratio difference over all process, supply, and temperature conditions). The Sooch current mirror was employed for the purpose .

*Designed a fully differential op-amp using a 0.18u technology with 80dB gain,160MHz unity GBW, >50db CMRR and PSRR, <20mW power dissipation among other specifications. CMFB was used to stabilize common mode operating point. Simulations over process corners and temperature were performed in Cadence Spectre.
