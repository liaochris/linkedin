Laura Morris’s Summary

Laura Morris is a successful marketing communications professional and business leader with experience in traditional, interactive/digital and multi-channel marketing projects. She has led numerous customer focused integrated marketing campaigns to help grow brands & business in categories including healthcare, banking, automotive, hospitality, eductation and insurance. She has launched five major radio station brands, transitioned legendary brands into relevancy, created dozens of major events and partnered with hundreds of different advertisers and products on advertising & promotion projects.

Laura’s work with major agencies and brands like Ford, Budweiser, Houston Texans, Houston Rockets, Houston Livestock Show & Rodeo, Gallery Furniture, and Tom Tynan’s Home Show give her broad perspective, deep consumer behavior insight and versatile marketing prowess. She has deep experience in broadcast media, direct marketing, digital media, social media, journalism, public relations and crisis communications to deliver impact, results and ROI.

Laura is also an accomplished business operating executive with multi-million dollar budget and PL experience.

Morris’s career in journalism, marketing and media management includes being honored with the Lifetime Achievement Award from American Women in Radio & Television and induction into the Texas Radio Hall of Fame. An active community volunteer, Morris currently serves on the boards of the Texas Association of Broadcasters and the BBB Education Foundation and has previously served on the boards of the Houston Museum of Natural Science and the Better Business Bureau of Houston. She is an active member of the American Marketing Association, Houston Advertising Federation, American Women in Radio & TV, Public Relations Society, Sam Houston Council of Boy Scouts of America, the Principal for a Day program in addition to several school PTO’s.

Laura Morris’s Specialties:

Laura's areas of expertise include crafting & executing brand strategy; market & product research; integrated marketing communications & advertising; message management; crisis communications; media & collateral design, production, negotiation & placement; project management & execution; P/L & intellectual property management; legal/SOX compliance.
