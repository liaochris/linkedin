Allen Underdown’s Summary

Integral to any organization is the way it communicates -- not only to its customers but to its vendors, investors, office staff, field and service resources, and community as well. Many times the limiting factor in achieving clear communications is the technology available at any given time, and the expertise to integrate new and emerging technologies for improving communications. Praecom Consulting provides this critical expertise in communications technology.

Over our 35-year history, Praecom Consulting has proven experience in a broad base of communications technologies. At some point, in some capacity, our consultants have worked with every aspect of your communications infrastructure -- from developing a Web presence, to creating the network to deliver the information, to figuring out how to handle customer service calls, all the way to delivering critical or time-sensitive information to people in the field. We have worked with large and small enterprises, health care agencies, and utility and governmental agencies, including public safety.

The services we provide our customers are our experience and our advice, with no spin or underlying motives. We are independent consultants -- we do not sell hardware or software and we do not repair equipment. What we do is help our customers understand their communications needs and identify how to meet those needs. We act on our customer’s behalf when dealing with vendors; this allows our customers to focus on running and growing their organization, while being assured that their communications needs are being met.
Since experience and advice are our only products, we keep abreast of technology, trends, and issues specific to our customers.

Praecom Consulting gives you the knowledge you need to make intelligent and informed decisions, and the experience you need to see those ideas through to fruition.
