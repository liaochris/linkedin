Jan Hambrick’s Summary

Hello! My name is Jan Hambrick and I’m the founder and owner of Elegant Adventures. I graduated cum laude from Colorado State University with a degree in Liberal Arts and a background in Wildlife Biology.

Having developed a severe case of wanderlust at a very young age, I decided to make travel my career. I began working for a small commuter airline and, sadly, that company went out of business shortly thereafter. But by then I had the ‘bug’ and soon began working for a major international air carrier. After twelve years with that company, I decided to open my own travel agency. This led to more opportunities to live, learn and teach travel. In total I have more than 25 years’ experience in the travel industry.

After a trip to Florence, Italy to celebrate my 50th birthday, I recognized the need for an intimate tour company that combines elegance and comfort with unique experiences. After a full day of trekking or bird watching, I truly appreciate the ‘creature comforts’ of good local cuisine and comfortably stylish accommodations. I believe you do, too. That’s why I spend part of each year searching out new programs. I feel very strongly that first hand experience is the best way to determine if an adventure is right for my guests.

Not a tour-type person? Me either! I limit my groups to a maximum of 12 so you'll always feel like you're traveling with friends. Feel free to email me with your questions. I’d love to have you join us on our next Elegant Adventure!
