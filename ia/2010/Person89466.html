Janie Behr’s Interests:

Janie Behr, life coach, is an authority on the psychology of empowerment, and life changes. Janie speaks & writes on topics of interest to “babyboomers” facilitates groups, and does business, family & individual coaching. Janie leads an inspiring life that has included traveling the world, meeting the President of the United States in the Oval Office, and even receiving a blessing by Pope Paul VI. She has raised more than half a million dollars for the Center for Women and Families and has volunteered with Habitat for Humanity. Janie has raised four children and spends her free time seeking out new adventures with her faithful canine companions Sam and Sophie.Janie can be found walking & playing with her dogs, laughing with family & friends & being adventurous in unusual ways. Learning to make choices that create happiness lies within you.
