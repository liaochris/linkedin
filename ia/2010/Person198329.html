Suzanne Whitby’s Summary

My passion is human-to-human communication and helping people make a real connection with one another. That's my over-riding objective both on a professional and personal level.

I do this in 3 different ways through my 3 businesses, all of which are connected because of the communication element.

1. Cross-culturally and inter-culturally: within i-connect, I work with individuals and businesses to raise awareness of cultural differents and help them communicate and work effectively with one another by improving intercultural effectiveness.

2. Helping businesses and entrepreneurs communicate: witin suzanne&co, I work with entrepreneurs, small businesses and growing organisations to help them define key messages, communicate them, identify where they want to be in the future and then help them get there.

3. Visually: withn Brand You, I help individuals and businesses create vibrant brands, websites and graphic design materials that "speak" to their audiences.

I have a wealth of skills that include project management, consulting, training, facilitating, idea generation, graphic design, website design, programming, technical writing, copy editing and much more.

I speak English and German, and a smattering of Spanish, Italian, Afrikaans and Zulu.
