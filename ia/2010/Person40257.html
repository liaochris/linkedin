Chuck Franks, PCC’s Summary

I help business owners and individuals take control of their lives.
Briefly the following are the steps we will work through and I will support you in your process.
1) Clarify
2) Focus
3) Take Action
to create what it is they truly want in their lives. Moving them quickly toward their goals and helping them overcome any obstacles which might get in their way.
My focus is appreciative inquiry coaching in relationships. Your in relationship with everyone in your life, including yourself. Starting with yourself we set your goals and then implement an action plan to use your strengths to create the life you want.
You can have a lot of great idea and intentions but if you don't take action you don't move forward. Feel free to contact me for a free consultation.

Chuck Franks, PCC’s Specialties:

Helping people see fresh new perspectives about the challenges they are facing in their lives. I focus on strength based approach helping people use their strengths to reach their goals.
