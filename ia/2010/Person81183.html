Heidi Wiegand’s Summary

Born and raised in Youngstown, Ohio I went to college and I dropped out. I spent the next five years bouncing from state to state doing various jobs. I went back to college, graduated and launched my corporate career. I spent years in Casino Management and Public Relations and decided to move to Michigan to be closer to family. I worked in the automotive industry only to get laid off. Because of the downturn in the automotive industry, I took a job in Media Advertising as a Corporate Sales Executive. As the Media Industry declined I got laid off again.

Tired of working 60+ hours a week, unrealistic financial goals, layoffs and someone else controlling my families livelihood, I knew it was time for a change and that’s when founded my Online Marketing Consulting company. Since then, I have become a leader in Internet Marketing industry. I provide people with a proven business model for retail, home-based or MLM (Multi-Level Marketing) businesses.

I am currently looking to mentor serious entrepreneurs who are sick and tired of their current financial and/or job situation and are ready to start a Home-Based Internet business or expand their current business. I want people who are willing to learn some new skills and work hard so they can change their lifestyle. If you are serious about change, the freedom you want is waiting for you. There is an entrepreneur in all of us. Unlock yours. I want to help you join the top 3% of people who earn massive income from an Online Business.

Contact me today to get to know me better or follow me on www.Twitter.Com/heidiwiegand or www.facebook.com/heidifwiegand
