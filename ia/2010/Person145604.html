Mike Krongel’s Summary

Michael Krongel has been involved in real estate sales, acquisition, disposition and development for a significant period of time. Starting as a broker in New York State in 1967, Michael has bought and sold recreational land, resorts, multi-family properties, golf courses, single family homes and condo developments.

At present, Mike is focused upon the acquisition and disposition of multi-family properties, development of resort recreational properties and second home development at cold weather resorts. Mike is also looking to acquire a medium sized business for his own account and to generate real estate related consulting opportunites for his present profession.

Mike Krongel’s Specialties:

The special skills that I bring to the table are those required in the acquisition and disposition of assets, transaction negotiation, sourcing of transaction opportunities and bringing owners, developers & investors together to "get projects off the ground".
