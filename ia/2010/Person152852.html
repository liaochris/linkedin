Neil Bergstrom, P.E.’s Summary

Professional Engineer (Petroleum) Colorado #40847. Expert in passive magnetic ranging, MWD and LWD measurements, magnetic surveys including QA/QC, instructor for MWD and DD engineers. Expert in wellbore surveying and anti-collision. Experienced in design and execution of relief wells for blowouts (underground and surface) and P&A, open hole re-entry around fish, fracture-salvage, and drilling precision parallel wells such as SAGD pairs. Member of the ISCWSA (Industry Steering Committe on Wellbore Survey Accuracy), also known as the SPE Wellbore Positioning Technical Section (SPE WPTS). Experienced in design and development of technical software.

Experienced in MWD/LWD and wireline geophysical well logging instrumentation and software theory, design, and interpretation as well as manufacture, training, and operations. Experienced in planning and executing complex directional wellpaths.

Neil Bergstrom, P.E.’s Specialties:

Passive Magnetic Ranging, Borehole Geophysics, Wireline Well Logging, MWD/LWD, Magnetic Navigation, Wellbore Surveying, Directional Drilling, Wellplanning, Intercepts, Blowouts, Plug and Abandon operations, Relief wells, Re-Entry Below fish, wellbore anti-collision, active collision avoidance. Analysis and interpretation of physical measurements. Instrumentation, software, and operational troubleshooting, diagnosis, and solutions. Training and mentoring of junior engineers in these specialties.
