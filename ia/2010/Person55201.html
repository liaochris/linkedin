Desigan Chinniah’s Summary

Desigan Chinniah is an enthusiastic, sociable, self-motivated and highly experienced web consultant, who enjoys creating and innovating web products backed by solid user experience and community engagement.

Dees is currently a product architect at Gumtree.com, part of the eBay Classifieds Group. He has over 6 years of experience with leading web entities, such as Ask Jeeves, Ask.com, Skype, BBC World Service, eBay, Gumtree.com and PayPal.

Dees' experience in design & development has helped to mould a well rounded Product Architect. He is passionate about creating simple yet effective interfaces, employing user-centred design methodologies while working within an agile environment.

Actively involved with the London technology, user experience, start-up and social media scene, as a mentor, organiser and regular attendee.

'cyberdees' on Twitter, London IA Ning, Flickr, Last.fm, Upcoming, Readernaut or visit http://desiganchinniah.com

Core skills:
▪ Product definition and execution - requirements gathering, stakeholder buy-in, product specifications and functionality, working on product roadmap and prioritisation, setting analytic goals, tracking and measurement, managing third-party consultants

▪ Merchandising, marketing and monetisation - user segmentation, cross-site engagement, email communications, brand awareness, revenue streams

▪ User-centred techniques - using personas, design principles, user research, testing, wireframing, iterations, prototyping, A/B testing

▪ Design of user interfaces - crafting effective UIs, functional interaction design flows, design patterns, information architecture models. Proficiency in Fireworks, Photoshop, Omnigraffle

▪ Agile methodologies - SCRUM, user stories, sprint planning, burn down charts, retrospectives, product backlogs

▪ Software development - established front-end engineer, knowledge of back-end development and QA. Proficiency in HTML, XHTML, CSS2. Currently exploring HTML5 and CSS3
