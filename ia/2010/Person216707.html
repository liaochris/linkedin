Will Worsham’s Summary

I graduated from Logan-Rogersville High School in 1989 and attended Drury University until 1993 earning an Bachelors degree in English with Honors and a minor in Speech Communication. While there I was the Station Manager of KDCR 530AM and a member of SIGMA PI.

I attended the University of Missouri School of Law from 1993-1996 and was granted a Juris Doctorate. I passed the bar that summer.

I was hired by Shawn Askinosie to start and run a niche practice in Springfield, MO called The Ticket Center which I did until the fall of 1999 when I joined the Greene County Prosecutor's Office as an Assistant Prosecutor. In September of 2002 I left Greene County and started my own firm where I still practice today.

In 2009 I was selected by the Springfield Business Journal for their "40 under 40" award.

My Practice is mostly comprised of Criminal Defense, Business Law, Real Estate and Collections. I have served as City Attorney for Rogersville and Fordland. Additionally, I serve as the Municipal Judge for the City of Fair Grove, Missouri.

In 2002, I launched the website MissouriTrafficTickets.com that allows clients to submit traffic tickets online to have them kept off the record. Since then it has grown into a multistate practice with "branches" in Kansas, Oklahoma and Illinois.

I have also launched the site EvictionsQuick.com which allows Landlords to quickly and inexpensively file Rent & Possession Lawsuits to get non-paying tenants out of their property as soon as possible.

I have the pleasure of serving as Master of Ceremonies for a number of local events including the American Cancer Society's Relay for Life, the Logan-Rogersville Educational Foundation's Wildcat Gala and the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation's Walk for a Cure.

I am also a backup Public Address Announcer for the Springfield Cardinals at Hammon's Field and currently host LawTalk on KWTO 560AM every Monday at 2pm. I hold the rank of 2nd Degree Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do.
