Arthur Gluzman, CEO-Managing Partner’s Summary

Originally from Michigan, I received a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in Accounting from Michigan State University.

I worked as an auditor for KPMG until I founded Global Recruiters, Inc. in 1998. Throughout my career, I have developed strong relationships with the Managing Partners and Top Executives of Top 25 CPA firms and Top SEC organizations in South Eastern Michigan.

Beginning in the fall of 2009, I started the Global Career Strategies Center. Through it, I teach essential personal marketing and career planning skills to my clients, to help them to achieve their career goals.

Associations with professional and civic organizations are important in developing a network of allies. I am a member of the Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA Detroit Chapter), National Association of Black Accountants (NABA), National Association of Personnel Services (NAPS), Career Directors International (CDI), Farmington Hills Chamber of Commerce as well as various networking organizations.

Arthur Gluzman, CEO-Managing Partner’s Specialties:

Specialties:
