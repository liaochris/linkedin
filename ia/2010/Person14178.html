Anne Kostick’s Summary

Anne joined Dulcinea Media, Inc. in August, 2007. Since 2000, she has also been a partner in Foxpath IND, a digital + print + Web consulting and services company specializing in the transition to and from traditional content publishing and online content management, development, and publishing. Her clients include trade book publishers; technology and financial services Web sites; and arts and cultural institutions. Previously, she held senior positions in licensing, product development, acquisitions, and electronic publishing with major trade book publishers, creating top-selling book series, award-winning children’s books, and innovative print-digital crossover products. She is the author of several books for children, as well as a definitive collection of jokes.
