Chris Brison’s Summary

President and CEO of Superian Consulting, specializing in government project and business management consulting for mid to senior level government agency management. Superian Consulting provides management consulting services in the area of strategic management and systems integration. I have an active Secret clearance, a MBA degree, and certifications in project management (PMP), Six Sigma, Lean Management (business process improvement), and Dept. of State software development life cycle management. I have 12 years of project and business management experience, including over 9 years of leadership experience at a "Big 4" management consulting firm (KPMG) and tier one systems integration company. As a Sr. Manager at BearingPoint, I was able to successfully deliver to a diverse clientèle in numerous federal agencies, including delivering all projects (past and current) on schedule and within budget. This ability to apply both best practices and unique approaches to host of client and management responsibilities has resulted in favorable recommendations from clients that have produced numerous contract award opportunities. I have personally managed over $13M in projects, as well as successfully managed over $10M in proposals and served in a leadership role in the successful development of $780M proposals.
