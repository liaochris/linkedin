Recognized, innovative and results driven marketing executive with over 20 years of experience building and growing products in multiple categories and industries. Successful leader in the development of effective local, regional and national marketing plans across multiple channels for established packaged goods, retail franchises and manufacturing companies. Proven record of generating exceptional sales and profits (EBIDTA).

CAREER ACHIEVEMENTS
• Drove evolution and expansion of a traditional two-step wholesale distributor to a brand-centric marketing and manufacturing company.
• Managed 16 brands, launched 4 new brands, acquired 2 existing brands, resulting in brand sales growth of 35% and $400 million growth in total company sales.
• Developed and managed $20 million annual corporate and brand marketing budget.
• Managed product portfolio of 850 vendors / manufacturers, 16 proprietary brands, and over 100,000 product SKUs.
• Grew vendor count by 55% with a 28% increase in direct margin.
• Eliminated 110 poor or declining performance products, resulting in stronger profit margins and a 15% reduction in warehouse space.

• Created brand repositioning and expansion strategy for Firstgear TPG, a premium line of technical performance apparel, resulting in a 10% increase in brand sales and a 20% increase in stocking dealers.
• Reduced annual media expenses 25% by aggregating all corporate and brand media contacts to leverage volume discounting, delivering significant savings to the bottom line.
