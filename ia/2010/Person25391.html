Bob Gilbert’s Summary

Bob was a key member of the pioneering product team that defined the feature set for Riverbed's groundbreaking Steelhead product line, which ultimately helped launch Riverbed as the WAN optimization market leader.

As Riverbed's chief evangelist, Bob heads up the efforts to facilitate conversation among customers, partners, or anyone interesting in IT infrastructure performance. Bob's effort has resulted in the successful launch of Riverbed's presence in online discussion forums, regional user groups, blogs, podcasts, and social media venues like Facebook and LinkedIn. Visit community.riverbed.com for more info.

A prolific and award-winning speaker, Bob has presented at hundreds of IT related venues around the world, covering topics ranging from successful IT consolidation to optimized disaster recovery.

Bob started his career in the early 90s as a systems engineer at technology giant Fujitsu. After helping to launch Fujitsu into the 3D graphics business, Bob moved on to hold product management and product marketing positions at file caching, storage management, and content delivery start-up companies in silicon valley.

Bob is a San Francisco Bay Area native, a dedicated husband of 17 years and father to three girls. Most of Bob's "free" time is spent with his family.
