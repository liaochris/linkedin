Raymond Uhlir’s Summary

Consumer industry leader with extensive experience in Marketing, Brand Licensing and Brand Management involving some of the world's most valuable brands such as IBM, HP, GE, Motorola and others. Specializing in the definition of brand equity, identification of high value strategic partners, and the successful extension of top brands into desired, new market segments and categories satisfying consumer demand and strategic business objectives. Recognized in the industry for top tier brand/marketing management and licensing skills with a proven track record in the development of strategy, alliances, and innovative business and distribution models for a wide variety of products and services ranging from Consumer Electronics, Semiconductors, and Software to Consumer Packaged Goods, Appliances, Toys, Apparel, and Accessories. Developed market leading brand strategy and marketing initiatives resulting in increased brand value, brand awareness, sales and market share involving multi-Billion dollar global businesses. Passionate about building brands and growing consumer business, and directly responsible for delivering some of the largest and most successful Brand Licensing programs in industry history.
