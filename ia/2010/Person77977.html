Gregory Gardner, LEP’s Summary

I am a Licensed Environmental Professional (LEP) in CT with over 25 years experience in providing geoenvironmnetal services to public and private sector clients. My experience has focused on providing services in the following areas: due diligence services to distressed properties, redevelopment, property transactions, assessment, investigation and remediation of commercial and manufacturing industrial sites, Brownfield sites, petroleum sites and landfills. My experience also includes evaluation of funding resources for redevelopment, assessment of remediation costs under new SEC pronouncements for environmental cleanup disclosure rules, and sustainable development and green remediation consulting.
