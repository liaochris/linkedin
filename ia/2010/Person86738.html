Jack Henry’s Summary

Licensed Professional Engineer in Texas and seasoned technical leader providing expertise in the ERCOT, PJM and CAISO markets supplying:

• Expert working knowledge and application of Extra High Voltage transmission modeling and analysis using a variety of industry tools.
• Extensive analytical and problem solving skills in both long term planning and day-ahead operations of transmission and generation facilities.

Jack Henry’s Specialties:

Transmission planning analysis and design for utility and power production entities using PSSE, MUST, PowerWorld and/or UPLAN. Evaluation of proposed and existing power generation sites and their interconnection impact on exisiting and proposed transmission facilities. Forecasting limitations of transmission facilities.
