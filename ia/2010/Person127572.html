Lori Long’s Summary

I have been blessed to work in some of the most challenging and fast-paced industries with some of the most gifted people in the world. This has done a lot to provide me with the confidence in my own gifts. I love to solve business problems and I really enjoy working with a myriad of people throughout this process--to learn about new perspectives I might not have considered. I believe in delivering world-class service--which doesn't mean doing what you're told necessarily--but maybe, doing what is best for your customer....I believe in creating something that works as well as it looks. And I believe in balance--in life, in work and on the road!
