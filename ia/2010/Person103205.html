  • Performer/Administrator

    The Danglers LLC/ Heavywooden Records

    (Public Company; NWS; Music industry)

    December 1997 — Present (12 years 5 months)

    American Progressive Rock band consisting of electric violin, standup bass, and drums that combine Jazz, Classical, Heavy Metal, Heavywooden, Punk and Fusion stylings. Superior musicianship coupled with intelligent song writing often accompanied by improvisation. Known for their mind blowing live shows. The Danglers are a one of a kind original trio that includes members of Violent Femmes and The Tossers.

    The Danglers are a power trio, of Violin, Contrabass and Drums, an unlikely but extremely versatile combination of instruments whose example were few but very helpful in influencing their direction. The band poured through droves of bebop and 20th Century composition while scrutinizing King Crimson, Mahavishnu Orchestra, Frank Zappa, Jeff Buckley, and Led Zeppelin collectively to influence its direction. No Dangler show sounds the same. The Danglers can play a jazz club as efficiently as they can handle a rock stage. Early in their career the band flooded the Milwaukee music scene playing 3 weekly residencies while playing shows and conferences in the Midwest and East coast. Their first release in 1998 was a hand copied cassette called 'Live at Rory's', a stereo mic recording of an early show at the venue that launched the band. Since, the band has recorded two full length studio albums, 'The Danglers', and 'Fade Out/Fade In'. A live and outake collage, 'Cocktales, reflections of the Milwaukee Night Life'. 2 ep's ,'Faux Kack' and 'We Had Heaven' a split 7" as well as compilation records. All Dangler recordings have been self financed as well as self distributed. The Danglers at present are writing their most concise batch of material to date. Only experience can describe a band that operates without rival. See them live!
