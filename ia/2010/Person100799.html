Joel Gómez Treviño’s Summary

Mexican attorney with multinational experience and education. Legal Manager at British American Tobacco Mexico, Head of the IT Law Practice Group at Arochi, Marroquín & Lindner, S.C. Country Counsel at NCR Mexico. Member of the Board of Advisors of NIC Mexico.

Graduated from: ITESM (LL.B.) and University of Arizona / National Law Center for Inter-American Trade and Commerce (LL.M.) Posgraduate Courses on International Intellectual Property, Leadership in Management and e-Commerce Laws and Regulations (UK & UNCTAD). Diplomas on Commercial Laws of NAFTA Countries (ITESM) and International Commercial Arbitration (ELD & ICC).

Mr. Gomez has delivered more than 90 lectures about Cyberlaw and related topics in academic and professional programs of Mexico, US, Canada, Italy, Asia & Latinamerica. Professor of Law for the past 11 years at ITESM (Monterrey), Universidad Panamericana (Guadalajara), Universidad La Salle (Mexico) and UNAM (Mexico).

Joel is the lawyer that has recovered more domain names in Mexico through negotiation and UDRP/LDRP proceedings: mercury.com.mx, hummer.com.mx, montblanc.com.mx, bancoazteca.com, mazda.com.mx, astonmartin.com.mx, apple.co.cr, tvazteca.tv, jetblue.com.mx, aztecamovil.com, innovasport.com, grupomodelo.com, mtvla.com.mx, tous.com.mx, ncr.com.mx, nafnaf.com.mx, mundojoven.com, ineta.com.mx, comerdel.com.mx, maquinasparacafe.com.mx, normont.com.mx y silc.com.mx.
