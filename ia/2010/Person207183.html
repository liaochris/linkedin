Doug Topken’s Summary

Senior Business Transformation, Customer Experience, and Product Management executive with unique ability to form and bridge Business Strategy to technology enablers; equally at ease and credible in the Boardroom as the Data Center. Multichannel Marketing, Sales, and Service expertise.

Held leadership and change agent roles with FedEx Marketing Transformation, FedEx eCommerce team, IBM eBusiness Transformation, Sharp MIS/IT Transformation, D&B Marketing Solutions launch, and D&B Order to Cash (OTC) transformational project. Presently engaged in newly forming Elsevier eBusiness, OTC and Customer Experience transformation.

25 year career with new product, new business, new channel and change initiatives supported by deep experience in "Big M" Marketing, Customer Segmentation, Customer Experience, Sales & Service Operations, Product Management, eBusiness, eCommerce, & Branding as well as the Operational and Technological architectures and processes that empower people to deliver results.

Known as results driven and able to lead cross-functional teams to drive revenue and improved customer experience. Background of senior roles with market leading Brands and record of successful business venture launches, revenue growth, and Brand equity enhancement (ADP, FedEx, IBM, Sharp, D&B, Venda, Reed Elsevier).

Early eCommerce pioneer and leader of multiple global transformational initiatives (FedEx.com launch and multiple enhancements) through present day (Venda eCommerce SaaS North America & Reed Elsevier Global eCommerce).

Recognized and promoted for success in leadership of diverse teams of development, marketing, sales, finance, service, and fulfillment professionals.
