Michael Martin’s Summary

Qualified freelance business counsellor/trainer with extensive contract experience (14+ years: Business Link, Enterprise Agencies) and private practice commercial, public sector, social enterprise and education sector clients. Facilitated 8000+ client interventions with over 500 clients.

Whether via one-to-one mentoring/coaching/counselling/advisory, consultancy, group training, speaker circuit or written sources, Michael and a team of specialists under Qdosology help people realise their definitions of happiness enabling clients to gain greater clarity about their purpose, meaning and direction. We aid Qdosology clientele to improve decision-making and achieve a greater level of desired time, creative and financial freedom.

Michael Martin’s Specialties:

Comprehensive business start-up, growth cycles, acquisition and legacy planning expertise, aiding transition from self-employment to business ownership. The Qdosology team also support existing ventures, comprising specialists in HR, IT, finance, operations, marketing & sales. Improving business owners self-awareness (enhance outlook/capacity to be more effective); empowering via guidance, financial capability insight and to generate sustainable custom; creating masters not disciples.
