Peter Lisney’s Summary

You could say that business is my hobby. It's what I enjoy hearing about and talking about most. In fact, give me a business problem and I switch instantly into hyper-thought mode to find a solution.

After years of working as a company director I realised that my real enjoyment came from doing the things that helped businesses to expand. So since early 2004 I have been helping a select number of businesses to increase their profits as a business development consultant.

"Peter's involvement has completely changed the way that we look at our veterinary practice. We are definitely more busy and feel very excited about the future". Stuart Bedford, Pearl Veterinary Group, Shrewsbury.

"Since working with Peter Lisney we have doubled our turnover, cut our overheads tremendously and have focussed our advertising to put our 40 year old business back on the map". Tony Vowels, Gibson Refrigeration and Air Conditioning, Horsham.

If you are interested in green marketing you can download my FREE Green Marketing Checklist from www.MidlandsBusinessDevelopment.co.uk. Green marketing is a delicate subject and if done without proper consideration can do a company's image more harm than good. As well helping companies consider some basic green marketing issues, the Checklist also provides web links to advertising rules concerning green claims.

You can read my new digital book on the 8 Keys To Recession Proof Your Business at www.recession-proof.co.uk

I also recently launched a new brand in the West Midlands, which provides business development through web design and development. You can see the site at www.Irun-Telford.co.uk.

I love to solve problems and I am always happy to offer advice. Feel free to drop me a line or become a part of my network.

I am an active member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I am married with 4 children and enjoy surf canoeing, badminton and driving my Smart Roadster Coupe.
