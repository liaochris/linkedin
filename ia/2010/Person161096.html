Paull Young’s Summary

As Director of Digital Engagement and Fundraising for charity: water I am responsible for driving online donations to provide funds for water projects serving the nearly 1 billion people without access to clean water.

Prior to charity: water I served as a Senior Account Director for New York based social media agency Converseon. I had the opportunity to lead major projects and organization-wide social media strategy with brands such as Telstra, Newell Rubbermaid, Graco Children's Products and Showtime's Smithsonian Channel and to have been an integral part of projects that have received awards from Forrester Groundswell, WOMMA, PRSA, the Webbys, PR News and OMMA.

My blog 'Young PR' (now PaullYoung.com) has been ranked by AdAge amongst the world's top 150 marketing blogs and by B&T Magazine as Australia's third best PR blog. My thought leadership extends to regular speaking engagements, media appearances including the Fox Business Channel, Sydney Morning Herald and Christian Science Monitor and my work being featured in several PR/Marketing textbooks.

Paull Young’s Specialties:

Social Media Strategy - online monitoring and measurement, internal consulting (training, policies, infrastructure etc), WOM campaigns, influencer outreach and blogger relations, social media tools (blogging, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, Wikipedia), Conversation Mining - online monitoring and measurement
