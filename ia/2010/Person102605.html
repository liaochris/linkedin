John McFaul’s Summary

A leading light in contemporary Art & Design John McFaul has been instrumental in many successful advertising and marketing campaigns the World over. The last decade has seen his studio successfully transcend definition and become one of the most talked about one-stop design hot-shops around. Never afraid to challenge opinion and definitely one to lead and not follow his studio now have a huge global following.
A founding member and Director of the South Coat Design Forum, a partner and Art Director at Big Daddy; a prestigious collaboratarial company set up to fuse the talents of Vault49 (NY) and McFaul Studio's in 2009 and finally a founding Director at Dropp Creative it is obvious his enthusiasm for the progression of art & design is legendary. Having been instrumental in the writing and launch of a Graphic Design New Media course at the then Surrey Institute back in 2002 he found he was best nurturing and not pushing paper. He's an educator, not an academic...and thats what he was all about. The want to nurture. The want to collaborate. The want to get the best out of individuals. McFaulStudio is the outlet.

Previous clients have included: Audi, Toyota, Levi’s, Nike, brand Jordan, Carhartt-Europe, Havaianas, Orange, Vodafone, Nokia, Sony, Samsung, Toshiba, Microsoft, Virgin Atlantic, British Airways, BBC, The Times, British Telecom, IBM, Cancer Research UK, Red Bull F1, and Lucasfilm.

John McFaul’s Specialties:

Art Direction, Strategic Planning, Full Service Consultancy, Integrated Marketing, Design, Motion and Interaction, Illustration, Branding and Identity.
