Jeremy Biddle’s Summary

I specialise in designing wastewater treatment facilities to deal with complex industrial waste streams or municipal wastewater in the UK and overseas. Projects range from small feasibility studies to multi-million pound municipal upgrades.
My secondary speciality is process health and safety. I am a trained HAZOP facilitator and DSEAR assessor (for potentially explosive atmospheres), and train others in both subjects.
