Jared Bell’s Summary

• Following my Graduation from the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater in 2001, I founded Movin’ & Lubin’ in March of 2003 as a strictly “on-site” oil change service provider for a residential customer base. Since then Movin’ & Lubin’ has established a full size maintenance and repair facility offering a wide range of services and has grown to become Milwaukee’s first Napa Mobile Auto Care Center capable of handling all automotive needs.

• Movin’ & Lubin’ currently maintains a customer base of over 1000 fleet vehicles and a large number of consumers from the private sector servicing the greater Milwaukee area.

• I have filled the role of technician and service writer during my years spent with Movin’ & Lubin’, but my mission as President and Owner is to become the most prominent national franchiser and service provider in the automotive repair shop industry. I plan to accomplish this through the expansion of our innovative Mobile Auto Care system as well as strengthening the foundation of the repair shop facility. I hope to one day expand Movin’ & Lubin’s services out of southeastern Wisconsin and into the national scene in order to provide more people and businesses with trusted, quality automotive service that they can rely on.
