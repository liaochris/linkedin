Belma Michael Johnson’s Summary

Belma Michael Johnson is a content creator for television, books, and new media, with a long history in the traditional media (newspapers, magazines, radio and other print media.)

He is also host of "Designed to Sell" on HGTV and a writer, director, producer and creator of television programs and documentaries.

Belma Michael Johnson's latest book is "Easy Green: The Absolute Easiest Ways to Preserve Your Earth."
