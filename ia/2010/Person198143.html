Susie Rust’s Summary

Versatile, senior contract recruiter with broad experience in high volume recruiting in several industries focusing the last nine years of my fourteen in advertising/interactive/media environments. Worked primarily with many dot.com companies and have successfully ridden the wave. Strong technical background however started out in sales and moved into finance. On an HR level, I have provided system consultation and evaluation and have assisted with the implementations of HRIS and ATS for multiple companies. With regards to recruiting, I practice full life cycle recruiting and handle a minimum of thirty diverse postitions at any give time, including AD's, Technical PM's, Creatives, Producers, Developers as well as upper management including CTO's, Presidents and General Counsel, I have designed an organizational chart and hired an entire start up ( from the ground floor on up) for Paul Allen, Co-Founder of Microsoft, for a spin off from his Interval Research lab and VC, Vulcan Ventures.
I am drawn towards environments that have the desire to be the best and that know that in order to achieve this goal, one must utilize "cutting edge" technology. I need to surround myself with intelligent and forward thinking people-no naysayers. Upper management needs to be aware that in today's market, strong recruiting strategies, a strong company wide HRIS system including the highest quality technology infrastructure are the key to a company's success; their proportionate functionality directly correlate to an effective bottom line. In many companies there is a disconnect between upper management and this important business model- my aim is to be that "connect." Diplomacy as a consultant is my greatest skill, and one that is vital in my role.
