    I also wrote 48 music, film, theatre and book reviews for the arts subsite, RTE.ie/arts. In addition, I worked as a web reporter at the national Festival of World Cultures, completing two festival blogs in 2007 and 2006.

Bill Lehane’s Education

  • Dublin City University

    MA , Intercultural Studies , 2005 — 2007

    Completed MA in Intercultural Studies at DCU with second class honours, including a first class honours for a dissertation entitled 'Interculturalism in Irish Public Service Broadcasting'. This thesis, which has now been published in paperback by Lambert Academic Publishing and is available on Amazon, draws together the views of senior RTÉ staff and prominent migrants about this issue.

    Over the course of the programme, I also conducted interview-based research on the Festival of World Cultures and wrote essays on media theory and Africa, depictions of childhood in European cinema and the riots against disadvantage in France in late 2005.

  • Dublin City University

    BA , Journalism , 1999 — 2003

    Graduated with 2:1 in BA in Journalism at DCU. My dissertation was a feature series on tobacco in Irish society coinciding with the announcement of the workplace smoking ban. Part of the project was published in In Dublin.

I also wrote college news and music articles for The College View and contributed news reports to DCUFM bulletins between 1999 and 2003.
