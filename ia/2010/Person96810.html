Jesse Webb’s Summary

Jesse Webb is a professional trader of stocks, options, futures and currencies. Jesse Webb is the author of Trend Following Stocks and is the developer of the top rated Market Trend Signal™ stock trading system. Jesse Webb operates as the founder and co-owner of Market Harbinger Institute LLC. With a focus on providing individual investors with tools to reach their personal financial targets, Market Harbinger Institute provides instructional resources, proven investing strategies, as well as trading and investing systems for individual investors. Brands of the company include Investor City, Market Trend Signal™, Trends University™ and MuscleStocks™.

Jesse Webb is developer of the Market Trend Signal™ trading system. This robust trading system is designed to assist investors find high probability trades. This systematic method is user friendly and effective for all skill and knowledge levels. Jesse Webb also developed MuscleStocks™, a unique proprietary screening system that identifies qualified powerful stocks, with prime trade entry set ups, and profitable characteristics. Utilizing comprehensive pattern trading , Investor City offers practical guidance on effectively managing investments, maximizing gains while reducing risks, through advanced pattern recognition. In addition, Trends University™ pairs personal instructors with students, constructing a disciplined, systematic approach to becoming a successful stock market investor.

Since 2004, Jesse Webb has mentored more than 2,000 students in investing and trading. Jesse Webb previously served as the founder and Head Trader at Orbis Advisors LLC, overseeing the areas of wealth management and private client asset management. Prior to his position at Orbis, Jesse Webb operated as the Vice President of Product Development and Head Trade Mentor at Vision Trading. In 1998, Jesse Webb graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Finance from the David Eccles School of Business at the University of Utah.
