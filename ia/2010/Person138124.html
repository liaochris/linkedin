Matt Schwandt’s Summary

I spent nearly a decade in Nashville, Tennessee, in pursuit of being both a successful musician as well as a creative business professional. After several years of achieving small successes in these endeavors, I decided to go back to school. I graduated with cum laude honors from the University of St. Thomas School of Law in Minneapolis, MN, in May 2009. I am currently working as an Assistant Attorney General for the State of Minnesota.
