equity staffing group’s Summary

Equity was founded on the belief that the “voice of the customer”, our clients and job seekers, must be acknowledged.

For years the staffing industry has ignored its customer’s voices and concealed margins, bill rates, and labor rates, which has resulted a loss of goodwill and loss of trust for the staffing industry.

Equity has heard your voices; we operate with a fully transparent model because our goal is deliver superior results while operating with fair and “equitable” margins that we are proud to share with our clients and jobseekers.
