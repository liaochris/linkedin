Shaun du Toit, CISSP’s Summary

Vibrant, solutions-driven critical thinker with diverse experience spanning IT operations, architecture and risk management functional areas from hands-on technical to prescriptive oversight type capacities in organizations ranging from FTSE 100 listed companies in the financial services sector to SME’s in the manufacturing and IT industries. Instrumental in bolstering the state of security by using strong analytical skills, good strategic appreciation and vision coupled with effective persuasion. Notable success at developing cost effective, risk-based responses to information security issues. Proven track record of exceeding customer’s expectations and using initiative to meet and resolve challenges. As a catalyst for change, continually strives for quality and continuous improvement thereby enhancing the value proposition of IT to the business.
