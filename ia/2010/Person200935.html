Tera Grinnell’s Summary

A certified human resource professional offering 10 years experience in Human Resources / Labor Relations. A strong HR Generalist foundation is enhanced by a strong work ethic and the desire to drive change to meet business needs. Professional competencies are summarized below:

• Experienced in the design and utilization of Organizational Development processes such as performance management, talent management, succession planning and salary planning
• Proficient in the development and maintenance of various HR documents such as training plans, EEO/AAP plans, ER Action plans, Reduction in Force/ Restructuring plans and numerous other HR policies and procedures
• Ability to apply Change Management principles to drive organizational and cultural changes such as centralizing HR policies and procedures or driving synergy as a result of mergers / acquisitions.
• Skilled in the proper administration of, and compliance with, labor and employment laws including Title VII, ADA, EEO, FMLA and FLSA
• Highly experienced in all facets of labor relations including grievance handling, arbitration, contract negotiations and investigatory interviews
• Extensive knowledge of MIOSHA, worker’s compensation, health & safety and ergonomics
• Seasoned at the use of Employee Engagement methods such as Roundtable Discussions, Sensing Sessions, Skip Level meetings and various committees such as Labor/ Management, Apprenticeship/ EIT, Health & Safety and Ergonomics
