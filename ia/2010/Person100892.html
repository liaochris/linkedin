Joel Ungar’s Summary

Hello, my name is Joel Ungar and since 1984 I’ve helped hundreds of businesses meet their financial assurance needs.

One complaint I often hear is how a business can never get a straight answer on what their professional services will cost. That’s one reason why our Firm don’t bill by the hour – we give you a fixed fee upfront. Phone calls and emails are included in the fee – you don’t need to worry about the “meter running.” As long as you tell us everything we need to know up front, the fee stands.

What makes us different? We're not full-service. We specialize in two specific niches:

• SEC Audits – we are a Top 20 SEC auditor (by number of SEC clients). All of our audits clients are Smaller Reporting Companies. We do a lot of them and get the job done on time. We have clients throughout the United States (most of our SEC clients are not in the Detroit area).

AuditWatch just named us the Best Price Firm for SEC companies with revenues between $1 and $5 million - call us and find out why.

• Franchisors and franchisees – we have over ten years experience in the industry and are members of the International Franchise Association. We know franchising inside and out - we know what should be in your Franchise Disclosure Document and often make suggestions that help you as you grow.

Our fees are fair and reasonable, and our service is outstanding.

If the idea of working with a CPA that tells you the fee up front, has broad experience in SEC audits, franchising, restaurants and other industries appeals to you, please contact me.

Regards,

Joel M. Ungar, CPA, CFE
