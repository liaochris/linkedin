Richard Batchelor’s Summary

Delivery of wide ranging complex changes managing a portfolio of 200 IT, policy, people and operational business process changes.
Engaging, managing and evaluating external contractors & facilitating multiple stakeholder discussions to develop business impact and delivery plans.
Matrix managing across a diverse division of 40+ business processes.
Developing training programs for new and improved business processes
Project managing a geographical relocation of 100 operational staff.
Presenting change developments to a mixed range of staff and stakeholders to gain positive buy in.
Project Management using PRINCE2, AMP & PMP.
Recruiting new staff externally and internally
Appraisal, coaching and workforce development planning.

I have just completed my masters in management specialising in Change Leadership at strategic level. I concentrated on the relationship between change management and personality making best use of Myers Briggs type profiling.

I completed a bachelor’s degree in Mathematics with statistics and operational research to honours level.

I have completed first level project management qualifications and have spent the last 4 - 5 years managing business changes as project manager, project delivery leads and business change manager.

I have also gained counselling and psychotherapy qualifications which have benefited my people manager skills.

I enjoy managing change. I like to link together the people and business change processes to benefit the change. This can then develop into systems, environmental or structural changes. I thoroughly enjoyed the work I did for my masters and would consider developing the work further to PhD level in the future.
