Jeff Woelker’s Summary

I am the Senior Manager, Internet Marketing at Whitney Automotive Group. Whitney Automotive Group encompasses numerous brands including JC Whitney, CarParts.com, StylinTrucks, and All Bike Super Shop. At Whitney Automotive, I help manage our search and social media programs across each of these properties.

I have also done public speaking engagements for the Chicago Arts and Business Counsel, the Chicago Association of Direct Marketers (CADM), and the Business Marketing Association of Chicago.

I am continually interested in the fields of usability, search marketing, mobile marketing, social media, and new and emerging online technologies and applications.
