• Highly strategic, multitasking individual with exceptional organizational and prioritization skills regarding all facets of project management using PMI standards.

• Skilled in detailed research and analysis of complex business logic and regulatory requirements.

• Conduct in-depth client meetings to identify clients’ short and long-term business needs, uncover client objectives, and assess project parameters.

• Managed array of cross-functional Information Technology projects and up to 20 Information Technology, Business, and Vendor resources from differing countries and differing time zones.
• Utilize superior team-building and employee relations skills to create a cooperative environment.
• Talented in identifying ways of leveraging technology to reduce cost and improve services.

• Remarkably capable of handling conflict resolution with efficiency and poise.
