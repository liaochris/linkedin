The ABE Network’s Summary

The Advisor Business Exchange(ABE) online network was created in June 2008 by a group of independent financial advisors. It was recognized that a private and confidential discussion forum on succession planning for the financial industry(financial advisory and insurance practices) was needed.

ABE is not affiliated with any mutual fund, insurance, brokerage or banking companies.

It is a networking forum which promotes effective succession planning for financial advisors, insurance agents and junior professionals within the financial industry considering a position as a financial advisor assistant.

Financial advisors and insurance agents may post anonymous ads within the ABE network on businesses for sale, mentorship programs and assistant position openings. Or express their interest in creating a Purchase Agreement Partnership with a peer to secure a buyer for the business in the event of unforeseen death or critical illness.

Succession Planning related discussions within the ABE network can establish a good business match, and cultivate relationships between members. To build a positive rapport that will ultimately lead to the exchange of real contact information to further discussions offline.

The ABE Network’s Specialties:

Joining the ABE network allows members to:
- Locate a Buy or Sell Business Transaction opportunity(financial advisory/insurance agent practices)
- Locate a Purchase Agreement Partnership business relationship with a peer
- List/ Locate Mentorship opportunities
- List/ Locate Assistant Position openings
