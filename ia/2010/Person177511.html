Ron Smith’s Summary

I have founded, co-founded, owned, have had or currently have an ownership position in fourteen businesses.

I served eleven years on the Florida Construction Industry Licensing Board, and also served on the Florida Governor’s Conference on Small Business. In 1979 I was chosen Free Enterprise Citizen of the Year in Ft. Myers/Lee County, FL. In 1980/81 I graduated from Harvard Business School’s Executive Education Program, Managing Organizational Effectiveness.

I am proud of being one of only 28 people to have been elected to Contracting Business magazine’s HVAC Hall of Fame, and was recognized with Service Roundtable’s Servant Leader award. I am on the Editorial Advisory Board of HVACR Business magazine and am one of their contributing editors, and have authored many articles for trade journals for the past 30 years.

In the past I served on the Board of Directors of Spiro International S.A. in Friborg, Switzerland, Buckeye Ventures, the Advisory Board for North Carolina National Bank and the Advisory Board for Roto-Rooter/Service America. I now serve on the Board of Directors of Service Roundtable, Inc and am one of the founders of the company. I am on the Advisory Board of HVACR Business.

I am the author of the best selling book HVAC Spells Wealth, the HVAC Light Commercial Service Agreements book and the 9 Disc Audio CD Set HVAC Spells Wealth, consults and coaches with contractors and distributors. I also have a Tennessee based farm vegetable produce business, am a real estate affiliate broker, a remodeling contractor, and have a real estate investment and development business.

My civic work includes involvement as area coordinator in a South Florida youth drug abuse program, chairing a huge church construction project in Ft. Myers, FL on both the Planning Committee and the Building Committee and serving on his present church’s Leadership Team. My wife, Betty, and I do church mission work in foreign countries and are very active in our church.

Ron Smith’s Specialties:

Helping companies be more successful. One specialty is a two day on-site consulting visit of a company identifying improvements and implementing them, followed by a report.
