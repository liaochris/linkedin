Edgar Santana’s Summary

A professional with over 8 years of experience in national, state and local politics. Experience includes design and implementation of voter contact and GOTV programs, budget management, constituent affairs and comprehensive computer technology experience. Outstanding management, budgetary and supervisory skills with the ability to coordinate multi-faceted initiatives. Ability to determine, analyze, develop and implement corrective actions to problems. Strong oral and written communication skills with the ability to write reports, brief supervisors and elected officials. A resourceful team player with exceptional organizational/planning skills. Simultaneously managed and monitored multiple tasks; the ability to network and build lasting partnerships on behalf of organizations. Coordinated genuine grassroots politics with creative campaign communications, strategies and tactics.
