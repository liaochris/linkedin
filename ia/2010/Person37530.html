Chris Chilek’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur, product evangelist and technologist with a passion for applying technology to increase the value of the college experience. Founded four successful companies over the last 14 years, and been an industry advocate for the openness and transparency of information within higher education to better the lives of college students.

Capitalized and built Pick-A-Prof into a profitable company that helped over 2 million college students select courses and professors each year. Served as CEO and Chairman and led the successful merger with MyEdu Corporation. Broadened MyEdu’s company’s vision to provide a comprehensive service that empowers students to manage their academic experience from selecting the right college and major through to earning their degree. Leader of product strategy at MyEdu, including product direction, product marketing, and site optimization and a member of the company’s Board of Directors.
