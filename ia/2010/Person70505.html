Betsey Ford Allen’s Summary

I am a Bookkeeping Consultant with twenty years experience and the construction industry is my specialty.

Now, I know your asking what does a bookkeeping consultant do?

Well ......................................

• I can provide bookkeeping services on or off site.

• I can do your QuickBooks setup or do your conversion and teach you to use this software to it’s maximum benefit, as well as answer any questions you or your staff may have if you are already using Quickbooks Pro Software.

• I have outstanding organizational skills and can clean up any bookkeeping nightmare put in front of me and then oversee the bookkeeping to assure it never gets out of hand again.

• I can provide backup bookkeeping services for small businesses that have a “one girl” office by creating cross training. This allows your employee to take vacations, sick time, etc.

• I can implement job cost reporting for the construction industry and other reporting needs that are not currently being met.

• I am available for auditing services for business owners who are suspicious of theft in the office, as well as consulting on control methods.

• All services are customized to fit your needs and can be modified as your needs change.

• I will work hand in hand with your CPA to assure accurate and timely delivery of your financials to him/her for Tax preparation.

My scheduling is flexible to fit your needs and of course your first consultation will be of no charge. My goal is to help all my clients get organized and stay that way, by whatever means necessary.

Organization reduces stress and a happy person is a stress free person. Spend your time making money, not keeping track of it.
