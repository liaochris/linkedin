Chris Cosgrove’s Summary

Chris Cosgrove is founder, and director of vivarium planning, for Cosgrove FDS Inc., a consulting firm offering facility design solutions and consulting services to the lab animal industry.

Mr. Cosgrove is widely-recognized as an expert in the specialized field of animal facility design and construction. His work is based in the knowledge that maintenance of the physiological and psychological well being of the animal is vital to conducting quality animal research. He is highly regarded for his ability to balance animal facility operations and function with architectural and mechanical design to create environments that promote excellence in research. He has created environments for a wide range of species from rodents to non-human primates for clients in the academic, government and private sectors.

Mr. Cosgrove is a faculty member of the Institute for Laboratory Animal Management, a frequent lecturer at international conferences, and a contributor to numerous publications on animal facility design.
