Scott Grogan’s Summary

My professional background includes experience within a Fortune 50 company, mid-sized manufacturing company, and large public accounting experience. Below are some highlights of my experience.

• Financial Analysis: Responsible for the financial evaluation of capital investments along with mergers and acquisitions in my role as Vice President of Finance and also as Controller for a large commercial printing plant.

• Forecasting: Worked as finance liaison with long range planning team. Developed models to analyze the financial impact of long and short-term management strategy. Developed processes to create monthly P&L “latest estimate” with variance explanations to budget and last estimate.

• Cash Management: Responsible for projecting and reporting on cash position for mid-sized manufacturing company.

• Budgeting: At Merrill Lynch I worked with senior management and developed annual budgets for the various management units ($480 million). At Lasky Company I was responsible for the entire budgeting and costing process.

• Financial Reporting: I have been involved in the internal and external financial reporting process at all phases of my career. At Merrill Lynch I developed internal product reporting for the various management units used in profitability analysis and progress reporting presented to management. As Lasky Company Controller I was responsible for the preparation and presentation of financial statements, presenting to creditors, and managing the audit process.

• Accounting Management: Supervision of professional staff (ranging from 2-12 people), oversight of accounts payable, accounts receivable, billing, collections, inventory control and analysis, and compensation.
