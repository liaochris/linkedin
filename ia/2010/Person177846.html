Ross Arntson’s Summary

As an entrepreneur for all my life, I have devoted myself to personal success and that of those around me. I’ve owned several of my own businesses taking them to the top of their field. I have amassed an incredible repertoire of achievement, including working around the world with many charities, all of which are learning experiences I like to share.

“Become the architect of your life and there will be no dream you cannot build.” And in a nutshell, I believe it all comes down to knowing how things work. So with this in mind, I’ve developed my consulting, speaking and seminar business around my love for neuro-science and brain technologies and have been certified by the best trainers in Neuro-Linguistic Programming, Emotion Freedom Technique, Hyno-Therapy and Time Line Therapy. All of this, so I can bring an arsenal of techniques to every speaking engagement, every client session and every person I touch on the street. You can enjoy some very interesting videos and audios on my website at http://innerarchitects.com

Currently, I am very excited about finishing my book “Rebuilt with Purpose, get ready for the ride of your life” which will be the first in the “Rebuilt” series. It’s being published by Morgan James Publishing and due out near the first of the year. Its website is www.rebuiltwithpurpose.com.

I’m also focusing my energies on the protection of the world ecological systems, I have teamed up with a group of incredibly talented inventors, scientists and businessmen to offer a phenomenal product to the cities of this nation and others and totally revamp their water treatment systems and completely eliminate the expulsion of toxins into the environment and create a renewable source for bio-fuels and more (www.biofueldynamics.com).

As an active philanthropist and member of Olive Crest, Habit for Humanity, National Geographic, The World Wildlife Fund, Greenpeace and The Red Cross, I have set my targets to give back and help those around me.
