James McCarthy’s Summary

Prior to working with Windermere I worked with Deloitte & Touché where I performed a variety of consultant services for Fortune 500 companies (companies such as Boeing, Microsoft, Nordstrom, Weyerhaeuser, etc). Before that I worked for Nordstrom in the Corporate Finance Department. I hold a myriad of professional certifications/degrees ranging from a Managerial Economics degree from Central Washington University to professional designations in real estate (ABR, AHS, IMS) and the corporate world (CIA, CISA).

The housing market in the Puget Sound area remains relatively strong – fending off national trends due to a unique combination of employers with very diversified industries. World class aerospace, software, retail, ecommerce, even food industry and timber companies help to insulate our economy from the highs and lows that many other areas are experiencing. Not to mention our natural attractions, which continue to garner national press! With all the turmoil in the financial markets and our economy in general, proper representation has never been more critical.

Whether you are looking to buy, sell, invest, reallocate or even liquidate your real estate portfolio; I have the expertise required to assist you with achieving your goals. I look forward to assisting you in any way I can – please don’t hesitate to call or email me if I can answer any questions you may have.
