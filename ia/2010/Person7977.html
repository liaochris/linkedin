James T. Allen LEED AP’s Summary

James T. (JT) Allen has 23 years of experience in facility operations, maintenance, design, construction and program management. He the Director of Facility Planning for Denver Public Schools. JT leads the short-term and long-term planning efforts for defining, scoping and developing projects, maintaining real property records, publishing District design standards, overseeing facility utilization, while maintaining constant communication with site administrators, scheduling construction activities so that schools remain operational while enhancing the learning environment.
JT honorably retired from the United States Air Force in 2008, after serving his country and reaching the rank of Lieutenant Colonel as a Civil Engineering Officer. His service to country provided him a broad and deep knowledge of organizational leadership, process improvement, facility management and prepared him for current and future challenges.
