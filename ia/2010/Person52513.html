A Vibrant Magazine For and About Vibrant People.
Living Life...Boomer Style Magazine has 45 departments in subjects ranging from Music, Travel, Movies, Entertainment Reviews, Spiritual, Inspire, Health, Memory Lane, and more...

While the magazine is about the incredible lifestyles baby boomers have created, many 20 and 30 somethings enjoy reading the fun filled magazine, which is loaded with fabulous images, humorous articles, thought provoking subjects, and profiles of those who helped define living life on your own terms.
