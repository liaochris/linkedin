Mike Corak mikecorak@gmail.com’s Summary

I am the VP of Strategy and Operations at ethology, a Tallwave company. I enjoy diving into audience research and data, and developing marketing programs that produce meaningful results for the agency's largest clients. Besides a love for all things digital, I enjoy expanding client and agency business alike through smart, creative, and resourceful teamwork and collaboration.

I began my digital career in the PM arena, responsibilities expanding to include oversight of all agency projects (with P&L accountability), direct management of the web development team, and leadership of the agency's account management and organic growth efforts.

Prior to joining Tallwave, I served as the Director of Interactive Services, Strategy at Mighty Interactive, a Division of Off Madison Ave. Notable clients included Nike, Fujitsu, Cars.com, Apartments.com, Arizona Office of Tourism, and Leslie's Swimming Pool Supplies.

Prior, I was employed as Senior Strategic Planner at iCrossing where I was responsible for designing effective interactive programs for Walt Disney Parks and Resorts, Coca-Cola, FedEx, and ConAgra Foods among others. This involved regular interaction with resources ranging from account to C-level on both the client and agency side, and coordination across multiple disciplines and time zones. I also played a prominent role in organic and new business development.

Beyond the day-to-day, I participate in a wide variety of industry groups, and blog and speak on related topics. Recent gigs include:

- AMA Phoenix - Social Branding Panel
- Social Media for Business Arizona 3 (SMAZ - socialmediaaz.org)
- AZIMA July 2010 - Event Moderator
- Online Marketing Summit - Minneapolis (June), Phoenix (May) - Mining Search and Social Data to Improve Content Relevancy
- Email Evolution Conference (EEC) 2010 - Miami - Social Media Workshop and American Email Idol Keynote
- ExactTarget Connections
- AZIMA EVP
