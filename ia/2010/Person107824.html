Judy Hunt Drobinski, MBA, SPHR’s Summary

Operationally oriented Human Resources leader with accounting/finance experience and a passion for understanding business has helped multiple growing companies achieve success. Employee relations, benefits and compensation, performance management, recruiting, on-boarding/off-boarding, and HRIS software system selection, implementation and integration are all areas of expertise.
