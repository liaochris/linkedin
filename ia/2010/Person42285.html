Drove mantra for selling and marketing lifestyle and action sports brands: sell the lifestyle, not the product. Created a content schedule for elwoodclothing.com much like an editorial calendar with regular content updates (2-3 times per week). Gave consumers a greater insight into the brand and its sponsored athletes and created an incentive for the users to return to the site regularly.
