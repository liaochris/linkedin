Chris Wessell’s Summary

I am a partner at Pinnacle Recruiting and HR Solutions, where we provide a value proposition that very few staffing firms in the United States can offer; world-class recruiting capability and true human resource consulting (up to and including full outsourcing of HR) under the same roof. This combination of talent and perspective has allowed us to provide scalable solutions to clients large and small.

I've spent a significant portion of my time in the recruiting business focused on technical roles, but I have also recruited for positions ranging from everyday business needs (accountants, salespeople, etc) to the unusual and/or highly specialized (flavor scientists, wind engineers, etc). In all of my searches, I make every effort to understand the position and how it fits into my customer's organization, the nature of their business and how it works, as well as their company culture, in order to provide them with a true solution for their talent acquisition needs.

I am on the Board of Directors of the Capital Region Human Resources Association, the local chapter of SHRM (over 1000 members). I received my PHR certification from HRCI in January of 2009.

Outside the world of recruiting, I am the president of my fraternity's Alumni Association (UAlbany chapter of Tau Kappa Epsilon), and a member of the Board of Directors of the University at Albany Alumni Association.
