Alan Collins’s Summary

I specialize in helping HR professionals and executives advance their careers to the next level.

My current book: "Unwritten HR Rules: 21 Secrets For Attaining Awesome Career Success in Human Resources" is ranked as the #18 HR best seller on Amazon. It describes career advancement strategies HR professionals and executives can put into action now. Two chapters can be immediately downloaded at UnwrittenHRRules.com.

My new book: "Best Kept HR Secrets" will be released in September, 2010. More information can be found at BestKeptHRSecrets.com.

My featured white paper "HR Recession Guide: 7 Ways To Recession Proof Your HR Career & Avoid Losing Your HR Job." is a must-have whether the economy is good or bad. It is available as a free download at HRRecessionGuide.com.

My scholarship program provides grants annually to deserving to high-potential minority college students. More info can be found at BryanCollinsScholarship.org.

I was formerly VP HR with PepsiCo where I led HR initiatives for the Quaker Oats, Tropicana and Gatorade businesses. With 25 years experience in various HR and executive leadership roles, I've been a featured speaker at the SHRM National Meeting, NAAAHR and various local SHRM chapters throughout the U.S. I've written over 100 articles and white papers on HR including features in HR Executive Magazine, Workforce, HRM Today, Linked:HR, Personal Branding and other nationally-known publications read by HR professionals. You can access many of my articles at our main website at SuccessInHR.com.
