Bill Lublin’s Summary

WILLIAM H. LUBLIN is the CEO of CENTURY 21 Advantage Gold which has been recognized by both Real Trends & Rismedia as one of the largest real estate firms in the U.S. It is currently the only Century 21 firm with offices in both Pennsylvania & New Jersey. He is also CEO of the Social Media Marketing Institute, a Social Media Education provider and home of the CSM designation.

Bill has been recognized for his insights in the use of Social Media and technology for business, being named by Inman News as one of "Real Estate's 50 Most Influential Online", Roost's "50 RE People to follow on Twitter", and other social media lists. His ability to connect real world business experience with the use of social media has made him a popular and in demand speaker at conventions and meetings throughout the nation.An active blogger, he has written for Agentgenius (winner if the 2008 Inman Award for Most Innovative Blog) as well as authoring MovePhilly.blogspot.com, REreflections.com, & C21AGVoices.com and the SMMI blog.

Bill served numerous times as a director of the PA Association of Realtors,President of the NE MLB, President of GPAR, and Professional Standards Chairman of the PA Assoc of REALTORS Bill has been a member of the National Assoc. of REALTORS Professional Standards Committee, and was 2008 chairman. Bill is an NAR trained Professional Standards Instructor, a trained DRS Mediator, and a Certified Mediator Trainer, and the developer of the CSM designation.

As an Expert in the field of Organizational REO he represents several large national lenders in the sale of their real estate including Fannie Mae, Homesteps (Freddie Mac), & others. He has trained & spoken Nationally on Foreclosures & Short Sales, leading the Short Sales Working Group for the National Association of REALTORS.

Bill has received numerous awards from professional real estate organizations, including the honor of being inducted into the REALTORS Hall of Fame.

Bill Lublin’s Specialties:

Real Estate Management, REO disposition, Real Estate Investing, Real Estate Education,Mergers and Acquisitions, Real Estate Consulting, Expert Witness, Real Estate Training, Certified Mediator Trainer, Social Media Speaker Trainer and Consultant, experienced in single and multi-author Blogs, and the use of Web 2.0 marketing strategies.
