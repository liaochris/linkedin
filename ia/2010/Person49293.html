David Townes’s Summary

My goal for the next five years is to start or help start what will become a mid-to-large sized company in the health care, medical device, or health care information technology industry. Toward that end, I am pursuing an MBA at the Carlson School of Management in their part-time program, with a specialization in entrepreneurship. To help accelerate this plan, I will be leaving my clinical practice and taking a full course load starting in June of 2010, so that I can complete my MBA by May of 2011.

I have 4 years of experience working as a health care professional. I have worked with a wide variety of client populations and in a wide variety of treatment settings. My professional training is in clinical social work, and I have a specialty in mental/behavioral health. I also have some unique leadership and group management skills derived from working as a wilderness and mountain guide in Southwest Colorado during my summers in college and graduate school.
