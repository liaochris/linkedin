Nancy Holland nancy@directemployers.org’s Summary

Nancy started her Recruitment/Human Resources career in 1998. She has worked with hundreds of companies as a recruitment consultant over the past several years. Nancy has served as Vice President of Professional Development for HRACI, the local Indianapolis chapter of SHRM as well as Program Chair for the volunteer committee of The Greater Indianapolis Chamber of Commerce. Nancy joined DirectEmployers Association, Inc. in March 2006 as Regional Vice President of Membership Development and Retention. In January 2008 Nancy was promoted to Director Business Development and in November of 2008 was promoted to Director of Marketing, Nancy is presently VP of Marketing within DirectEmployers Association.
