Zachary Kamm’s Summary

In today’s world, maintaining business continuity is of paramount importance. Your back-up media and other special items require protection from dust, heat, and humidity, as well as safekeeping from fire and water damage, theft or loss. That is why off-site storage should become an integral part of your company's business continuity program. Our off-site vault is equipped to handle all of your special requirements including daily, weekly or monthly rotation schedules. This facility is ideally suited for computer back up tapes, media tapes, vital business records, film, source code and original documents. Tapes require special handling techniques for longer life and better data integrity. Feel free to contact me regarding tape and document handling procedures.

Stand-alone, windowless structure
24/7/365 monitoring and access to media on deposit in vault
Perimeter fencing
Card-reader access control
Security cameras document all activity at media vault entry gate and interior
Person-trap upon entrance
Temperature, dust and humidity controls maintain 68 degree temp and 40 percent humidity
Dedicated Vaults available
All items are barcoded
Cases used for transport are industry standard to withstand physical and thermal shock
Items are transported in vehicle providing for minimal variation in humidity and temperature
Tracking system allows for items to be queried and ordered via web
Very complex media rotations can be managed for any disaster recovery program
