Jay Morgan’s Summary

My Approach: Attack the problem. Parse the concepts. Diagram the system. Formulate solutions. Simplify it. Disseminate the idea. Savor the success.

I enjoy applying myself to good challenges, and I want my team to enjoy it too. I drive change by establishing trust, cultivating relationships and proving the value of user-centered design principles.

I studied mental models, comprehension, and decision-making. I use my education to both architect better experiences and to advance group problem-solving strategies that allow us to use our imagination at work.

I have managed IA/UX initiatives and projects for two multi-billion dollar retail corporations since 2004. My track record reflects introducing iterative prototyping, building an enterprise design pattern library, building a corporate usability lab, and building tools for cross-functional teams to apply UCD methods.

Jay Morgan’s Specialties:

Information Architecture. Interaction Design. User Experience Design. Prototyping. User Experience Research. Application Definition. Human-Computer Interaction. Concept modeling. Task analysis. Scenario-based design. Mental models. Interaction Design Patterns and Pattern Libraries.
