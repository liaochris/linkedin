Laura Kolkman’s Summary

Laura Kolkman, RN, MS, the founder and President of Mosaica Partners, brings over two decades of international experience in Health Care and Information Technology. She is a nationally recognized expert in the field of Health Information Exchange. Laura began her professional career as a Registered Nurse. Intrigued by the potential for using information technology to improve healthcare quality, Laura returned to school and earned a Masters Degree in Computer Science. After earning her masters degree Ms. Kolkman held a variety of national and international executive level positions in the pharmaceutical and technology industries, including that of Chief Information Officer for a multi-billion dollar corporation.

As an experienced Registered Nurse with a Masters Degree in Computer Science she brings a unique set of skills that aid in seamlessly blending the two worlds of healthcare and information technology.

Mosaica Partners is a nationally recognized consulting firm that works with states and regions to develop health information exchanges. In addition, Mosaica provides advice and guidance to a wide range of hardware, software and services firms considering entry into the health information arena.

Ms. Kolkman is the immediate past (2008-2009) Chair of the HIMSS Health Information Exchange (HIE) Steering Committee where, among other duties, she was actively involved in preparing a HIMSS briefing paper on the subject of health information for the incoming administration of President Obama. She is frequently quoted in the press about challenges and developments in HIE and has published many articles on subjects related to Health Information Exchange.

Ms. Kolkman is currently writting a book on the formation and operation of health information organizations (HIOs) that will be published in early 2010.
