Trent Richardson’s Summary

Trent Richardson has worked as a business owner and consultant in the financial services industry for over 20 years. In this time, Trent has worked with some of the largest, progressive banks, mutual funds, common remitters, and administrators using market leading browser based administrative, ETL, and BPM tools, including OmniPlus, to strategically improve the operations and reduce costs in very competitive environments.

These engagements included the development and extension of leading web-based business solutions for reporting, trading, compliance, and document management with transactions of over 80 million dollars a day. Trent has used and refined the PMI and Agile methodologies for the successful delivery of these solutions to both domestic and global financial services customers. This proven style brings a broad understanding of the solutions from the detailed technical product development, to advanced OmniScript, to strategic design, and through to actual operational implementation for defined contribution schemes including 401(k), 403(b), 457, and superannuation.

This unique combination of business and technical expertise was shown as Trent implemented and developed SunGard’s ASP operations for over 4 million participants in the U.S. And then localized the solution and implemented it in Australia using AGILE techniques on various programming platforms including COBOL, .Net, and VB. The key to the success was having not only a strong understanding of the core technology, but also on applying forward thinking e-business concepts.

Trent Richardson’s Specialties:

Project management, business process automation, system design for financial services, operations management, solution architecture, Saas and ASP platform design and management, application design, business continuity planning, inbound marketing web architecture, retirement services, 401k compliance, common remitting, data conversions, international application localization
