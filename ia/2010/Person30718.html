Michelle Sterling’s Summary

Senior-level HRD and training consultant, workshop facilitator, instructional designer and speaker. Behavior change with measurable results.

Expertise in innovating and implementing leadership-driven cultural change initiatives. I am currently helping organizations with the "People" aspect of CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility.)

I provide Socially Responsible Leadership training to improve employee commitment and engagement, innovate profitable and sustainable solutions, entrench an ethical decision-making process, engage multiple generations in the workplace, and maximize organizational CSR strategies (strategy design, internal communications, employee volunteer efforts, etc.)

Experience includes training department start-ups, directing learning and organizational development functions through periods of significant organizational change (mergers, rebranding, rapid-growth, privatization), managing large remote teams, and internal communications/branding.

Michelle Sterling’s Specialties:

Leadership development, advanced training Skills (needs assessments, evaluation, train-the-trainer, design, facilitation, blended learning), employee engagement, talent management, internal communications.

Cutting edge content on generational differences, work/life balance solutions, corporate social responsibility.
