Chieu Van’s Summary

Graphic designer with interest in identity and branding, packaging, print and web design. I would also like to expand my knowledge in interactive media and motion graphics. My fine art background is an asset when clear and concise comps or storyboards are needed to convey ideas to clients.

Recently I had experience helping a startup company develop their identity in preparation of entering the trade show circuit. I designed their logo, a website, trade show collateral, executive summary, and some icons for their smart phone based application demo.
