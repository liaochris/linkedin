Joe Rogel’s Summary

I’m an experienced social media strategist and freelance web designer living in Denver, Colorado, with over 12 years of interaction and participation on various types of online communities and social media channels.

I enjoy identifying opportunities for companies and nonprofits in social media spaces and developing custom strategies and campaigns. I help clients achieve the goals of building strong relationships with users and key influencers, increasing brand awareness and recognition, promoting content, increasing traffic and inbound links, coordinating offline events, reputation management, and much more.

I currently work for Gaiam, and my list of clients includes Guidespot and Partners Relief and Development.

Joe Rogel’s Specialties:

I am also skilled in website administration and production, content management systems, graphic design, CSS coding/design, and more. I earned a degree in Public Affairs Journalism from OSU and have seven years of writing and publishing experience as a journalist, copy editor and blogger. I am also a fluent Spanish speaker and writer, and working on learning Portuguese.
