Jared Klein’s Summary

The Work Institute (www.workinstitute.com), a leader in relevant workforce and workplace research, provides decision support intelligence to help companies attract, hire and retain talent, improve performance, mitigate risk and manage human capital expense. Leading organizations utilize The Work Institute’s structured, disciplined research methodology and immediate, on-line reporting system to secure actionable, timely, and mission-critical intelligence.

Services include:
• Certifying Companies as Preferred Employers – Certified Preferred Employer

• Human Capital Scorecards/Dashboard Intelligence Reporting

• Employee Intelligence across the Employment Lifecycle

• New Hire Studies/On-Boarding Studies to increase intent to stay for new employees

• Employee Surveys (baseline and pulse based) with incumbent employees to increase length
of stay and measure additional employer specific objectives

• Special Population Studies based on targeted objectives, such as merger-acquisition, diversity, change, safety, etc.

• Exit Studies with former employees to identity the real reasons for leaving, secure competitive analysis, identify interest in return, and eliminate risk
