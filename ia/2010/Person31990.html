Cara Keithley’s Summary

I direct communications and marketing for a state government agency. I am able to balance creative innovation with the strategic vision needed to achieve measurable results. I specialize in strategic relations, building stronger partnerships for our organization through traditional methods and social media.

I am a passionate advocate for Government 2.0, increasing transparency and efficiency at the local, state, and federal level. I am respected for my leadership in new media, working across Agency lines to make positive changes that help Ohio citizens.

In addition to work in the office, I also consult and speak on social media, Gov 2.0, crisis communications, media relations, and higher education. Specifically, I am interested in how higher education can utilize social media and digital marketing to impact enrollment and brand awareness.

I am an adjunct professor at Franklin University in public relations and organizational communication. I teach media relations and crisis communication, rhetoric and social influence, and popular culture.

Cara Keithley’s Specialties:

Gov 2.0 (especially government 2.0 in Ohio and at the federal level), higher education and the use of social media and digital marketing in higher ed, social media, communications, public relations, strategic relations, marketing, branding, event planning, graphic and web design, contract management, and staff and volunteer management.
