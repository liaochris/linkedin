Glenn M. Penton, Jr.’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur that was proudly born and raised in New Orleans, LA. I'm currently living and working out of Houston, TX.

In 2004, I attended LSU and ultimately received a degree in Finance. I began my career in the Oil & Gas Industry here in Houston. In late 2009, I decided to pivot and start my own project company - LanYap Ventures. So far I've had the opportunity to work in two diverse Business Development roles...working for both a chemical company and a clothing company. I've also been focused on developing my own projects.

I'm personally fascinated with technology and the rapid growth of business surrounding the internet. Accessing the social web and leveraging the power of word-of-mouth will help lead the next wave of innovation (as we're already starting to see).

I truly enjoy connecting and helping others in any possible way I can. So, please feel free to contact me anytime, for any reason at all!
