    What's a Blabbermouth?

    Blabbermouths are your new Marketing Department (or new additions to an existing one). They know how to market you and your product or service best, because they are your loyal followers. That's right, your own consumers are ready and willing to spread marketing messages for you, but they're going to need your help.

    Let's face it, your marketing budget doesn't go as far as it used to. You have less and need it to do more. That's where Blabbermouth comes in. Instead of spending most of your budget on expensive media buys and production costs, we engage your loyalists to spread the message for you. For a fraction of the budget of a traditional marketing campaign, we will work with you to distribute strategic, creative marketing through the most credible sources - your consumers.
