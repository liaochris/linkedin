ROSS FREEMAN’s Summary

As founder of 680 Partners LLC, Ross Freeman has specialized in executive search and recruiting for a wide array of industries over the past decade.

Mr. Freeman works in long term partnership with many top venture capital firms, private equity and LBO funds to help build senior management teams. 680 Partners specializes in recruiting senior executives including: CEOs, COOs, CFOs, CTOs, CIOs and VP of Sales.

Prior to founding 680 Partners LLC, Mr. Freeman was Vice President of Results Staffing's Strategic Technology Practice, where he started a technology group that increased revenue by over 200%. He has also provided strategic consultancy services for companies, including PriceWaterhouseCoopers where he helped build the infrastructure and core technology group.

Mr. Freeman also played a key role in initiating and effecting progressive developments online. As Director of Internet Technology with Adler Consulting Mr. Freeman led the creation of the pioneering e-commerce site "www.Demstore.com".

Mr. Freeman received his BA in Psychology at Hobart College. He did graduate computer studies at the New School for Social Research and obtained certification from Microsoft and Sun Microsystems.
