Mark Pearce’s Summary

Innovative F&B executive with national exposure and broad experience in the operational, financial, and development aspects of restaurants, food service, and retail. Presently working in product & concept development for home meal solutions and multi-regional team training; to build, grow, and improve profitability and customer relations. Always interested in new concepts & trends, and interaction with other industry professionals and investors.

Results-charged career that reflects 30 years of progressive leadership roles as a corporate executive, investor, and owner operator across multiple brands and markets with varying scale and complexity.

Strong business network: national real estate/retail developers, real estate brokers, banking and investment-financing officials (to facilitate expansion), and human resources.

Spearheaded the start-up, turn-around, expansion & renovation of numerous restaurants and F&B venues from conception to launch. Vast exposure to traditional and non-traditional site development. Extensive field management & training experience in retail, single, and multi-unit F&B operations.

Skilled professional chef/pastry chef with sound knowledge of the latest industry landscape, culinary trends, designs, equipment and applications. Extensive experience integrating full service restaurant and bakery operations with specialty F&B retail, catering, and special event venues.

Strong leadership, communication, and analytical skills. Exceptional ability to energize teams and empower people to move beyond their comfort zones to think and act in a new way.

Wrote and trained specialists, managers and team members on Gourmet Recipe Manuals for all corporate and independent Gourmet Stores as a complete resource for Corporate Executives, Field Specialist, Chefs, Managers, and Team Members.
