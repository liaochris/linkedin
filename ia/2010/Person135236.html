    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    The guiding principle behind our signature 12 session (6-month) Coaching Program, for both individuals and groups, is based on the concept of Primary and Secondary Food as developed by Joshua Rosenthal, founder of the Institute for Integrative Nutrition.

    It is a holistic, bio-individual model which combines the consideration of one’s personal, professional, financial, emotional, and spiritual lives (Primary Food), with the unique nutritional choices (Secondary Food) a client needs in order to reach a state of optimal health and wellness.

    Knowing that everyone’s needs are different, a wide range of lifestyle practices and dietary theories—from Atkins to the Zone Diet—are explored to see what combination of recommendations works best.

    The result is that clients engage in a fun, motivating, and highly flexible process that not only meets their unique health and wellness needs, but also creates the lasting change they want.

    Sessions are conducted in person, as well as via phone or video conference.

    The 6-month Individual Coaching Program includes:

    Two 1-hour appointments each month—every other week. Each session will include a review of your progress, a featured health topic discussion, informational handouts, recommendations, and an action plan

    Email contact between sessions

    Downloadable mp3 recording of the counseling call or in-person session

    Resources (e.g., books, DVDs, CDs, recipes, whole food samples, etc.), to incorporate healthy habits in real-time into your life

    Healthy Shopping Supermarket Tour

    Personal Pantry Review
