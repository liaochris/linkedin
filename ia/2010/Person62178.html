Eduardo Ramos Olivera’s Summary

Screenwriter of the screenplay:"Enmienda Estructural" (Copyright Eduardo Ramos Olivera).Year 2009.

Scriptwriter of the radio series: “Venerable Concepción Cabrera de Armida, 1862-1937” released by: Catholic.net Radio and Radio Maria world wide. Chap. 1. Mi Familia (28 min.), Chap. 2. Novia y Esposa (20 min.), Chap. 3. Apóstol de la Cruz (28 min.), Chap. 4. Vida Mística (34 min.), Chap. 5. Crecimiento en las Virtudes (32 min.), Chap. 6. Atracción Irresistible por la Cruz (39 min.), Chap. 7. El Tercer Amo.r (26 min.), Chap 8. Misión de una Mujer en la Iglesia (26 min.). The radio series describes the friendship between Jesus Christ and Concepción Cabrera de Armida. Produced by Apostleship of the Cross,Lima-Peru, Year 2008

Scriptwriter of radio dramas for Family Rosary-Peru. Family Rosary was founded by Servant of God Father Patrick Peyton. The radio dramas have been released on: Catholic.net Radio, Radio Católica Mundial and Radio Maria. General Coordinator of the 6th and 7th Festival of Marian Music, the songs of the music festival are dedicated to venerate Blessed Virgin Mary produced by Family Rosary and the Congregation of Holy Cross. Producer of the 5th and 6th Marian Music CD. Director of the 6th Marian Music DVD. Coordinator of the project: “Consagración del Perú a la Virgen María” (Consecration of Peru to Virgin Mary)produced by Family Rosary and the Peruvian Episcopal Conference. Years 2006 – 2007

Filmography

Screenwriter of documentary films released by EWTN(Eternal Word Television Network). EWTN was founded by Mother Angelica. Documentaries: “El Santo Niño de la Salud”(Holy Infant of Good Health) (Duration: 60 min), “Nuestra Señora de la Evangelización” (Our Lady of Evangelization) (Duration: 60 minutes), “Catedral Basílica de la Asunción de María”(Guadalajara Cathedral)(Duration: 60 minutes). Screenwriter Coordinator of the TV Series: “Testigos de Nuestro Tiempo: Mártires Mexicanos”.(Witness of Our Time: Mexican Martyrs) Years 2005 - 2006
