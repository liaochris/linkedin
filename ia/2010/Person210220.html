Valerie Haboush’s Summary

A marketing communications copywriter since 1984, I've worked with companies of all kinds - from major corporations to mid-size firms and start-ups in various industries - developing clear, concise copy for print and electronic purposes to generate interest, inspire action and get results.

I specialize in business and corporate copywriting, but have also for the last 20 years solidified a niche as a real estate marketing writing specialist in the brokerage world both in the New York City area and nationwide. My work has been recently featured on the cover of The New York Times Real Estate section, and on ABC News' "Nightline" television program.

Earlier in my career I worked in many other facets of the marketing arena, including account management, public relations, sales promotion, advertising and events planning. I therefore have a broad understanding of the importance of maximizing the marketing message through impactful communications.
