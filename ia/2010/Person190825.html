Sloane Scott’s Summary

Change Agent-Digital, Social Media and Community Evangelist

Founded in 1914, George P. Johnson is a global Experience Marketing agency. We provide a full suite of relationship building services and solutions that innovate our clients' marketing programs, motivate their audiences to action and activate their brands.

As part of the Entertainment Marketing Group, I help brands tap into and leverage the power of entertainment to reach and transform new audiences.

Currently, I serve as a maven for our new digital offering, GPJOnLive, helping clients define their digital vision, concepts and solutions. I work cross-functionally to make recommendations on the right technology mix (Web 2.0, Social Media, Mobile, Virtual) to apply to a client's portfolio.

I joined George P. Johnson from echo (a Ticketmaster Company), where I led the full lifecycle design, development and delivery of digital campaigns for large entertainment properties and brands. My specialty included the creation of multiple online communities and the right tactics to nurture and create revenue streams within those communities.

I have a combination of client and agency consultancy (B-B and B-C) in the digital space and have an expert understanding of digital technologies and trends.
