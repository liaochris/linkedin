Gary Daveson’s Summary

I am a committed Human Resources professional with more than 14 years experience as an internal and external consultant in Australia, Japan and England and now in the Middle East. My HR, L&D, IT and project management skills, have been gained within the education, utilities, banking and finance industries in both the public and private sectors.

I have provided consulting, mentoring and coaching support to both individuals and groups, along with facilitating, seminars and training programs in Australia,the UK and now the Middle East.

My relevant qualifications, along with skills developed in various consulting roles enable me to focus on outcomes that build and strengthen individual competence and organisational capability.

I am professional, friendly and approachable, yet with a depth of insight, experience and wisdom, which enables me to deliver a positive impact on business thinking, staff morale and the bottom line.
