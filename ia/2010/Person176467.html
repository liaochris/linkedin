Roel Stavorinus’s Summary

My experience is within the design industry. Projectmanagement, communications and designmanagement, always in design related projects, are the functions I feel comfortable in. I specialize in developing identities. Either commissioned by graphic design studios or by commissioning companies.

My professional goal is to implement (the use of) design in companies in such a way that they benefit from it. I like to work on a very pragmatic and practical level. I like to structure and to make clear, to use that clarity as a starting point and solid basis.
