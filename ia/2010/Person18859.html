Avinash Singh .’s Summary

Avinash has close to two decades of rich experience in the areas of Global Cross Border Collaboration, Software Product Development, Offshore Outsourcing and Professional Services.

He has over the years honed skills in General Management, Sales Leadership, Change Management, Negotiation, Branding and Marketing, Human Relations and People Management as well as managing complex and Global P&Ls.

He has a deep understanding of Viral Marketing, Semantic Search Frameworks, Mobile Applications, Web 2.0 Concepts and their application to socio-behavior patterns of various customer demographics.

Avinash is currently the MD of Excel Ventures, an early stage Incubation Fund which invests in the Technology, Healthcare and Education segment in the Indo-US corridor. He is also the Founder of another start-up company in the Health and Wellness space leveraging Web 2.0/3.0 concepts and frameworks.

Avinash was a founding member of Birlasoft and was responsible for the initial setup of the company.

As Chief Operating Officer, Avinash was responsible for global operations across the USA, Europe, APAC and Middle East markets. He played a pivotal and cogent role in growing the company prior to leaving to start his own venture.

He is a dynamic and passionate leader who led the organization through various transformations to what is today a respected Global Solutions company and is part of the Nasscom Top 20.

He was responsible for acquiring a number of significant customers including GE, Metlife, Monster, Citibank, CSFB, PNC Bank etc.

As the Marketing Leader he crafted and led various branding initiatives which resulted in positioning the organization favorably in the Gartner Magic Quadrant.

He has an entrepreneurial management style and is a strategic thinker with the ability to take a vision from concept/strategy to reality through focused Execution.

He has experience dealing with C Level and Board Executives of Fortune 500 companies globally.
