Meredith Oliver, MIRM, MCSP’s Summary

Meredith Oliver is an Internet sales and marketing expert. She is a professional speaker, consultant and founder of Meredith Communications and the Internet Marketing Hotline.

Meredith Communications is a full service Web marketing company delivering services such as Web site development and Search Engine Optimization. The Internet Marketing Hotline is a one-of-a-kind LIVE support service for small business DIY (Do-It-Yourself) Marketers.

Meredith holds a Masters Degree in Communication Technology from the prestigious Rollins College and a Bachelors of Arts Degree, majoring in Psychology, from the University of Central Florida. During the dot com boom and bust, she worked for Move.com as a Regional Sales Manager. Meredith has both the education and the real world experience to help you WOW your customers online and offline.

While she specializes in real estate audiences, Meredith has spoken to a number of industries including healthcare, education, association, insurance, technology, and temporary staffing. She is a proud member of the National Speakers Association.

Meredith has spoken at a number of tradeshows and conferences including the International Builders Show with numerous appearances in the Super Bowl Sales Rally and the Sales Management Summit. Her convention seminars are consistently standing room only and rated by attendees among the most popular, entertaining and educational seminars offered.

Meredith is a MIRM (Member of the Institute of Residential Marketing) Professional Designation and a MCSP (Master Certified Sales Professional).

She is a Life Director for the Florida Home Builders Sales & Marketing Council, Past Orlando Sales & Marketing Council President and Past Institute of Residential Marketing Trustee.

Meredith Oliver, MIRM, MCSP’s Specialties:

Internet marketing such as website design and development, email marketing, search engine marketing and lead management. Sales training and sales management.
