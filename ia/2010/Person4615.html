Anita Johnson’s Summary

For the past 7 years, I have been a full-time student of, and participant in the industries of Affiliate, Attraction, Internet, Network, Referral and Social Marketing.

My previous employment background encompassed 30 years of experience working for numerous law firms and countless attorneys performing word processing, legal secretarial, help desk, software training and troubleshooting responsibilities.

Self-taught with regard to most popular computer software, and considered an Expert when it comes to helping others understand how to efficiently utilize software applications and resolve issues.

One major accomplishment was the original creation, design and production of a customizable training manual tailored for Word Perfect users transitioning to Microsoft Word and then conducting training classes using said manual.

In mid-July, 2005, with the intent to return to traditional corporate employment, I obtained certification as a Microsoft Office Specialist in Word and Excel. However, I have yet to return to the workforce in favor of running my own online affiliate and network marketing businesses from home.

In early 2007, I started providing online help desk support for a few Internet Marketing professionals.

Currently, I am seeking new clients who are in need of outstanding help desk support in the virtual world.
