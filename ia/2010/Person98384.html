Jim Gilmartin’s Summary

Jim Gilmartin has emerged as one of America’s experts on improving marketing, sales and service to baby boomer and senior customers. In addition to his knowledge of how to secure and retain the rapidly growing older markets, Jim brings to his engagements the experience of more than 20 years of leadership roles in business development, marketing, sales and operations.

An author of hundreds of articles on these rapidly growing and lucrative markets, Jim is also a frequent conference speaker and trainer, providing entertaining and enlightening keynotes and presentations to improve marketing, sales and service to baby boomer and senior customers. His presentations emphasize the best way to get the interest of the single largest customer group with the greatest chance of converting that interest into action ora sale. Jim recently co-authored Market Smart: The Best in Age & Lifestyle Specific Design.

Since 1991, Jim has provided leadership to Wheaton, IL based Coming of Age, Incorporated specializing in helping clients increase market share and profit in baby boomer and senior customer markets. Helping them capture and keep these lucrative markets, the marketing firm provides clients agency and PR servcies, sales and service training, research services, consulting and customer loyalty programs.

Current and past clients include: Humana, Inc, AT&T, Genworth Financial, AARP, BCBS of FL, Levitt & Sons, Nordstrom, Sunterra Resorts, Wyndemere Senior Living, Heartland Steaks, Villa St. Benedict Senior Living, Allianz Life Insurance, Fairfield Resorts International.
