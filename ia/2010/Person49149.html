Darryl Matthews’s Summary

Darryl R. Matthews, Sr.; a highly respected and successful senior association executive, previously served as the Executive Director and Chief Operating Officer of the National Association of Black Accountants, Inc. (NABA) headquartered in Greenbelt, Maryland, as an Independent White House consultant for Advance, marketing director for a developmental staged internet and computer based training company in Norcross, Georgia, and as executive director of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. in Baltimore, Maryland. As a member of the National Public Speakers Bureau, Darryl is in constant demand to speak on a multitude of topics but he is especially focused on the positive portrayal and the economic, social, educational and political development of the African American and other minority communities. He is committed to the uplift of the downtrodden by mentoring to youth.

My immediate goal is to add value, to any organization or company with which I am affiliated, by adopting relevant best practices and the review or development of, and the execution of the strategic plan, defining desired and measurable outcomes and promoting growth through enhanced revenue generation. I am dedicated to developing leaders by mentoring, promoting academic excellence, and brotherhood, while serving as an advocate for and providing service to the youth in underserved, dissaffected, and distressed communities.
