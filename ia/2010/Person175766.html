Rob Kaminoff’s Summary

"Where Online and Traditional Marketing Meet"

Rob Kaminoff has over 20 years of marketing, product management and business development experience in B2C and B2B companies, as well as agency environments.. Initially trained in classical marketing, product management and business development, Rob discovered the power of interactive marketing at its inception in the mid 1990s. While currently building his consulting practice and entertaining full-time opportunities, Rob has worked for Fortune 500 companies, start-ups and mid-size companies.

Over the last 15 years, Rob has integrated online marketing and ecommerce into his bag of tricks, including best practices of website development, product merchandising, SEO, SEM, web display advertising, comparative shopping engines, email marketing, affiliate marketing, landing page optimization, web analytics and testing, as well as social media and social networks. His creative and analytic capabilities enable Rob to consistently improve the effectiveness and ROI of online and offline marketing campaigns.

Rob has adapted the comprehensive (but overly bureaucratic) processes learned in large corporations (e.g., AT&T, Cendant) into more streamlined variants that have enabled him to thrive in companies of different sizes, cultures and industries. He is adept at leveraging market research, competitive, technical and financial analysis to inform market strategy and planning. Rob is then able to translate strategy into actions that grow revenues in three major ways: developing new products and services, enabling new sales channels, and stimulating demand by optimizing the effectiveness and ROI of integrated marketing campaigns.

Going forward, Rob is interested in full-time and part-time engagements that will enable him to remain at the forefront of marketing innovations.

Rob Kaminoff’s Specialties:

Market research, competitive analysis, strategic marketing, direct marketing, product management, business development, branding, advertising, public relations, positioning, collateral development, sales training, trade shows and CRM as well as website development, e-commerce, SEO, SEM, display advertising,comparative shopping engines, web analytics, A/B and multivariate testing, as well as email, affiliate and viral marketing that leverages the latest social media and networking technologies.
