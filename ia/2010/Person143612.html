Michael Surtees’s Summary

Michael Surtees is a multidisciplinary designer, writer and educator. He publishes DesignNotes.info which is about people’s daily experience and observations with design. When not thinking about design he's a Design Director of Daylife.

In his past life he was the President of the Alberta North chapter and for a short period of time the National Vice President of Communication for the Society of Graphic Designers of Canada (GDC).

interaction design, graphic design, ux, design, design thinking, information design, marketing, advertising
