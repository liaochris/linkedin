Amy Gregory’s Summary

I work to get our clients into the media to create a buzz around their brand. Working on pr campaigns in Yorkshire, and up and down the country, our main aim is to increase brand awareness to meet client's objectives. These could be increasing sales, driving footfall to a website, or promoting an event... just for starters!

Raising profiles in the press, online, radio or TV, I want people to know about the power of pr and marketing. Having nurtured relationships with the Yorkshire press over the last few years, my experience spans from public sector work to retail - dealing with industry trade press, consumer mags and daily newspapers on a daily basis.

On the event side of things I've helped organise and manage launch events, pr stunts, parties, and photoshoots, and have been involved in planning and running seminars, workshops and courses in pr and how to use it in your business.... everyone should!

Having worked with one man bands to national organisations, on pr retainers, ad hoc pr projects, and pr consultancy, I'd love to hear what pr stories you're sitting on...
