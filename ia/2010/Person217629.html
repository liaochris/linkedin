Yogish Pai’s Summary

Seasoned Chief Technology Officer (CTO) with the vision to identify the technology trends; to pick the right one that meets the business needs and to provide leadership in bringing teams together to achieve these objectives. Expertise in developing technology vision, transforming organizations, developing strategies and managing large teams. Ability to identify market opportunities, evaluate options, develop strategies and set direction. Driven to improve company performance and financial results by combining a laser-like focus on the end-customers and their needs with the passion to provide excellent service through the development of relationships with their decision makers. Personable, with outstanding proficiency in communication, negotiation and the art of persuasion.
