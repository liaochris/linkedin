Alex Salkever’s Summary

I have an eclectic background. I am a practicing and reformed journalist (I still write a lot and was formerly tech editor at BusinessWeek.com). I spent four years doing research for Hedge Funds. I am currently Director of Marketing / Communications, at Picarro. We make the world's best and easiest to use greenhouse gas tracking and analysis equipment and have a mission to let everyone be a scientist measuring our world. I am also involved in doing research and academic paper writing with Vivek Wadhwa and Duke University on the topic of tracking immigration patterns among Asian tech entrepreneurs and trying to better understand these patterns. Lastly, I run and edit a science and research magazine for the University of Hawaii. Oh, I try to surf when I can
