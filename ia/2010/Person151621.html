Natalia Real’s Summary

I am a native English speaker also fluent in Spanish; I work comfortably in both languages.

I have a BA in literature from New College of Florida, the Honors College of Florida, and have written and copyedited for many publications, including magazines, newspapers, and blogs, in both English and Spanish in print and online.

I have worked with texts about the arts, nutrition, the environment, politics, and culture, among others.

My considerable journalistic experience and literature degree both ensure excellent grammar, spelling, and syntax, as well as attention to detail, in my work.

I am painstaking, thorough, engaged, and have advanced researching skills. I hold high standards for myself and always deliver quality work.

Language is my passion, whether I'm delicately translating ideas into another language, crafting a narrative, or enhancing others' ideas.

Natalia Real’s Specialties:

Copywriting, blogging, journalistic and academic writing, copyediting, proofreading, emarketing, and translating (Spanish-English/English-Spanish).

Topics of expertise: environmental issues, especially ecotourism, ecocide, and green living; women's and animal rights; the international fishing and aquaculture industries; social media; and freelancing.
