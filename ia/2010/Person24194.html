Billy Hineline’s Summary

I have been involved in sales and marketing for more than 20 years. Along the way consulting and handling projects across a number of media platforms including print, radio and internet. Most recently, I have been involved with television production and marketing in local, regional and national markets along with having a great understanding and involvement in the area of social networking.

At this point in my career I am involved with the launching of a new multi media marketing company in Palm Beach County, Florida. The company specializes in the promotion of nightlife, entertainment, restaurants, activities and events in a number of different markets. My involvement has me working hands on with web design and internet marketing, the production and distribution of a full color coffee table quality magazine and the production and marketing of a television magazine style program that will cover that DMA and reaches a 5 county area. In addition to our company having social networking on our own website, I am also personally working on our company’s social media presence here on Linkedin as well as on Facebook, Myspace, Twitter and a number of other social networking sites.

I am the point man for the company on one of our newest projects, our new television program Yachting in Paradise. This program will also be shot here in Southeast, Florida. Although we are only in the early stages of pre-production on the program, it is being well received by the members of the Yachting community. It helps when you live in the Yachting Capital of the World.


I also still do some consulting in the Pocono’s region of North Eastern Pennsylvania. It was there that I was born and raised and started my career.
