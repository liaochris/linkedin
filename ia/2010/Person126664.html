Wei Li’s Summary

Dr. Wei Li is a senior computational linguist with over 20 years of professional experience in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Extraction (IE). Dr. Li's background involves both a solid research track record and substantial industrial software development experiences. In his career, he has designed and built three large-scale NLP systems, all transformed into successful real-life products for machine translation, intelligence discovery and business question answering.

At Netbase (formerly Accelovation), Dr. Li has been focusing on leading the development of information extraction and sentiment analysis technology to power business searches. The product illumin8 ( www.illumin8.com ) launched by Elsvere is a result of this application. It can directly answer technology research questions.

Dr. Li was Principal Investigator (PI) or co-PI at Cymfony on 17 federal grants from the DoD SBIR (AF and Navy) contracts in the area of NLP/IE (Information Extraction). As Vice President for R&D at Cymfony (and its spin-off Janya), he demonstrated his management and leadership skills in a long list of projects he led there. Dr. Li was instrumental in helping develop the tiny 2-staff start-up into a 60+ staff technology company (before the spin-off). Dr. Li's SBIR research efforts led to the development and deployment of the InfoXtract engine and a suite of Cymfony’s Brand Dashboard(TM) applications.

Wei Li received a master's degree in Computational Linguistics from Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, China, and a Ph.D. in Computational Linguistics from Simon Fraser University, Canada.
