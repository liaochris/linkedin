Laura Fine-Morrison’s Summary

Role: An HR Generalist position in an emerging company.

About Laura:

She keeps the big picture in mind, and is always looking for ways to improve the business. She drives towards results, asking: "What would make the biggest difference in helping us meet/exceed our goals?", and "Could we be doing this more efficiently?".

Laura is a creative thinker and problem solver. She is an open and straightforward communicator committed to resolving conflicts. She believes that when employees are given the bigger picture and have background information on management decisions, they are more likely to proactively resolve problems in ways that benefit the business.

An effective liaison, she works to develop others: delegating responsibilities, encouraging people to run with their ideas, and giving them the support and resources necessary to be successful. Laura is collaborative and most enjoys successes that are the result of a team effort.
