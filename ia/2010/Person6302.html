  • VP business development

  • CEO

    Bausch Datacom

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1988 — August 1995 (7 years 8 months)

    CEO for Bausch Datacom Netherlands B.V., a corporation specialized in PC-Connectivity (modems/isdn) and security solutions and (internet) services. New products and marketing concepts were developed and tested from within the Dutch operation and later transferred to the German, Belgium and French subsidiaries where they were adopted to the local markets. Other companies in the Bausch Group included Infoboard, a online services company started in 1990 and Bausch Manufacturing and Engineering a production company started in 1993. More information at http://www.bausch.be

  • CEO Netherlands

    Infoboard

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1986 — January 1995 (9 years 1 month)

    Infoboard was a public BBS system which grew from a hobby to a system with 50.000 subscribers in multiple countries in Europe. In 1989, it was the first online service to offer Internet shell accounts and later it grew to one of the first ISPs in Europe. Infoboard was part of the Bausch Datacom group, and was sold to Euronet/Wannado in 1996.
