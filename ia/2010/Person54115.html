Deika Morrison’s Summary

Several years of experience at the leadership level in the public and private sectors.

Experienced legislator, negotiator, policy developer, strategic advisor, business process re-engineer, researcher, analyst, fund-raiser, grant writer, policy developer and public speaker.

Knowledgeable in general business and finance, as well as socio-economic issues/sustainable development.

Private sector positions include: Consultant, President, Chief Research Officer, and Senior Manager - Business Processing.

Public sector positions include: Deputy Finance Minister, Senator, Chairman of the Anti-Dumping and Subsidies Commission, Director of Union Bank Holding Company (and subsidiaries)/Chairman of the Y2K and IT Sub-Committees, Coordinator of Special Projects and Public/Private Sector Development and Environmental Scientist.

Goal: To leverage skills and experience to assist companies in strategy, operations, research and analysis and financial advisory services.

Deika Morrison’s Specialties:

research, financial analysis, strategic planning and development. business development, privatization, government liaison, grant writing

Successfully obtained funding for promotion of the CCPA from the Environmental Foundation of Jamaica – an independent foundation funded by a debt-swap agreement between the US and Jamaican Governments
