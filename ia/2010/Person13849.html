Anna Florey’s Summary

The simplest way to say it is, we work with business owners and executives who are serious about their businesses and are serious about their marketing -- especially their online marketing.

What we do:

• Online Marketing Strategic Plan: We believe the core of any successful marketing endeavor is a fully-researched, well-thought-out strategic plan. We thoroughly investigate the market, competition, website, unique position, and potential marketing channels. Then we create a customized, actionable online marketing strategic plan.

• Website Optimization: The real strength of your online business is your website. No matter how much traffic you drive to your site, if it doesn’t convert, you’ve wasted your money. Our first goal is to capture as many visitors as possible (conversions), then focus on driving more visitors.

• Traffic Generation: Once your website is a converting machine, then we look to tap into all the people interested in your products and services. We offer premium traffic generation services, and we optimize every program to maximize its ROI.

We offer PPC Management, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Comparison Shopping Engine Management, Email Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, Social Media, etc.

• Web Analytics: Accountable marketing is absolutely essential. We insist that our clients use some form of tracking (e.g., Adwords Conversion Tracking, Google Analytics, Omniture, etc) and we help with data interpretation.

What we’re looking for:

• Clients: Small to medium sized Ecommerce businesses; all sizes and types of lead generation businesses; and other businesses who are serious about their online marketing.

• Channel partners: If you offer a premium marketing service and would like to discuss a strategic partnership, or if your clients might benefit from our services and you want to discuss an affiliate partnership, CONTACT ME.
