Steven Flostrand’s Summary

With over fifteen years of marketing and commercial experience, gained largely in the pharmaceutical industry, I bring a strong international perspective and a solid track-record of achievement to my projects. I have built businesses across Europe in both large and small organisations.

A Canadian-British dual national and a resident of France, I hold an MBA from INSEAD and a Bachelor of Commerce (marketing and international business) from UBC.
