Dana Lewis’s Summary

A consultant and strategist in social media and communications, I also serve as the co-creator and moderator of the weekly #hcsm (healthcare communications and social media) conversation on Twitter.

While I am an undergraduate at The University of Alabama completing two bachelor's degrees in public relations and political science, and a minor in the Computer-Based Honors Program in May 2010, I am currently researching full-time opportunities.

My experiences include: building and managing a three-person social media team at the Crimson White; creating and developing one of the most successful and the longest-running healthcare Twitter chat community; patient education and health education writing; and feature writing and pitching that has led to placements in the New York Times and local and state-wide media coverage.
