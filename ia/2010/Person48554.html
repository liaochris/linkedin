Dante Tremayne’s Summary

Don't know if you want to call it genius or what. I have over the course of my life worked in many different trades and professional fields, and excelled at each. I have worked as a rough, finish, and trim carpenter, a painter, a roofer, a cabinet maker, a plumber, a welder, a handyman, and even fiddled as an auto mechanic. I have been a supervisor for the landscape maintenance division of a Golf and Country Club, the assistant manager of the fulfillment division of a book distributor, fill-in supervisor for a utility locating company, warehouse manager and crew leader for a fixture installation company, and finally made my way into my passion, Marketing and Advertising, which role I have filled for several years in a professional capacity.

In each of the above roles, I have observed, studied, and critiqued the business practices of these fields. That experience has given me the advantage over most every other marketing professional in that I have first hand experience in not only how these businesses function, but how the professionals in these fields work, what their strengths and weaknesses are, what their types of businesses need in order to excel.

My goals are always to see business succeed, to see business professionals learn the proper practice of marketing their business, to make them look good, obtain and retain customers and clients, grow in size and revenue, and maintain those levels of excellence.
