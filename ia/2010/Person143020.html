Michael Lodge’s Summary

I’m a SEO professional with an extensive, varied background writing, editing and managing web content. I create well-crafted, well-researched copy rich in SEO value. Accustomed to working with style guides, writing in different voices, using content management systems and web analytics.

I spent 18 months developing the core product of a web 2.0 start-up company. I run a blog called Buzzchow.com which provides snapshots of what buzzing on the web.

I work freelance with my associate Geoff Simon of Simon Search Marketing. We help businesses optimize their web properties for the web. We offer link-building, blog optimization and development, submission packages for blogs/rss feeds.
