Richard Cunningham’s Summary

I am a veteran of corporate marketing and field marketing operations with business-to-business (b-to-b) companies ranging from start-ups to Global 100. My passion is developing demand generation programs that produce measurable results. Four of my favorite initiatives include:

Search Engine Marketing
For many of its important keyword phrases, this company was not displaying on the first page of organic search engine results nor was its paid search program performing optimally. I directed a project to redesign and expand the SEM program. As a result, targeted search traffic increased, a higher number of these visitors converted to leads, and we generated more qualified leads for the field.

Webinar Event Series
The sales team needed compelling events to which they could drive prospects, and the marketing team wanted to increase the selection of interactive content on the company website. I directed an integrated marketing campaign to produce a series of six live and on-demand webinars. This program drew hundreds of new prospects. On-demand registrants converted to qualified leads at a rate higher than other types of interactive content on the site.

Customer Conferences
This company was looking to take its annual conference to a new level as an exciting customer experience – a “must-attend” event that would influence more sales to current and prospective customers. I led a cross-functional team in the make-over. Three key performance indicators improved significantly: Attendance, customer evaluations, and go-forward revenue from attendees.

Audio Marketing
In order to meet its growth objectives, this commercial real estate company needed to increase brand awareness beyond its traditional base, and generate a steady flow of investment proposals. I moderated and produced a 36-episode podcast series. This campaign exceeded its objectives for the number of podcast downloads, and the volume of incremental investment proposals.
