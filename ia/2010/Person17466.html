Ashley Feddersen’s Summary

Carte d'Or Catering & Events exists to provide you with the perfect event experience from start to finish. Established in 1989, Carte d'Or Catering and Events has created innovative and unique events for weddings and corporate clients all over the Denver area. We pride ourselves in evolving to be a better company each year with new menus and fresh ideas. Whether you are getting married, entertaining guests at home or hosting a corporate event, Carte d'Or will deliver the very best food, staff and overall experience. From the very first phone call to the follow-up after your event, our catering sales staff will be there to coordinate the perfect event for you. To impress your guests with the best, use Carte d'Or Catering & Events.

Carte d'Or Catering & Events features a full line of catering services for the Denver area and beyond. Our diverse experts who make up the divisions of Carte d'Or Catering & Events will make any event perfectly yours.

We love what we do and it shows!
