Sahar Eissa , CTIE’s Summary

Exploring different parts of the world has always been my passion. I traveled extensively in four continents;
Africa, Asia, Europe and North America . I have over over 22 years of experience combined in the airline and travel industry.

Extensive Experiences working with top tour operators and cruise companies 1984 -1989, overseeing and managing incoming department operations for groups and FIT from Europe and the USA.

Moved to California, USA in 1989 and formed Cross Country Tours in 1994 , utilizing my expertise in planning and creating memorable vacations and events customized to meet the needs of a wide spectrum of special interests travelers.

As a mother and a very active member in my community, I understand how important it is to deal with a specialist when you are planning a holiday for 3, or a family reunion for 100. I have taken my children on trips ranging from cruises, to tropical resorts and soft adventure tours. I use my experience and knowledge to address the unique needs of travelers, handling the myriad of details to ensure a smooth and hassle-free vacation.

Bi-Lingual in English and French in addition to my native language Arabic.



Looking forward to networking with colleagues inside and outside the Travel Industry.
