Russ Spittler’s Summary

An experienced manager and attorney, my practice of government relations and trade compliance integrates business, legal, and behavioral science principles. Achieving competitive advantage includes taking the lead on regulatory strategy at both domestic and international levels. The past belongs to the firm that views the law as a mere burden, the future to the firm that decisively acts upon the law as a tool to advance and protect the bottom line through fair competition. From foreign policy interpretaton, to Stage Gate product development, to customer product delivery, law touches every aspect of corporate and compliance management. I translate the law into competitive practice.

Russ Spittler’s Specialties:

US trade compliance: ITAR, EAR, OFAC, FTR, ATF, CBP, FCPA, DTRADE-2, SNAP-R, AES; international law and trade
