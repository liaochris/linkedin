Wim Hooghwinkel - iDTP’s Summary

I’m a publishing and technical communications consultant with focus on XML, FrameMaker and DITA implementations.

Since 1994 have been working as a specialist in supporting activities for creating, translating and publishing multilingual technical documentation. Being a DTP and FrameMaker specialist I managed projects for localization and specialized in projects that included XML or DITA publishing.

I have been assigned projects that include multilingual DTP, FrameMaker or XML publishing and have been contract project coordinator for technical publications. I delivered consultancy and hands on for structuring technical documentation and I have been involved in migrating legacy, unstructured documents to structured FrameMaker format, using Docbook and DITA.

I give FrameMaker training to end users, advanced FrameMaker training to content and template developers and I do DITA quick start training.

I work on site or remote, contract based or by hour.

Template design
Structured templates and application files For XML publications using FrameMaker. Regular non structured templates for use with FrameMaker, InDesign or MSWord.

Hands on and consultancy
How often wouldn't you need help with some particular issue, either technical (languages, fonts), system (FrameMaker, MSOffice) or workflow related (XML, tools) - but you don't want to hire new or additional staff? Consult iDTP and we will advise and help you to locate the right tools, make instructions and get your staff on the right track.
