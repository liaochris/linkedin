Dr Mike Cadogan’s Summary

Emergency physician with a passion for medical informatics and medical education. I saw a need for enhanced medical information distribution in Australia both at a structural and an educational level. HealthEngine strives to be the answer to these problems through the development of HealthEngine.com.au , HealthEngine.net.au and HealthEngine.org.au.
In partnership with Elsevier Australia, I hope to create an educational interface and eLearning portal which will facilitate discriminatory feedback to enhance and further develop logical learning tools.

Dr Mike Cadogan’s Specialties:

Concepts, ideas, vision and a drive and enthusiasm to see the dream to reality. Medical Informatics and using Information Technology to develop education and learning in medicine. Medical author.
