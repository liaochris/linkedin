Harry Reczek’s Summary

Harry Reczek, M.A., CSM, Lean Six Sigma Green Belt is a social psychologist, business consultant, teacher-facilitator and coach utilizing process thinking expertise with brain science to create 'teachable' and tangible problem solving tools and methodologies focused on results.

I specialize in mixing social media strategy with traditional marketing tactics aligning content creation with initiatives for relationship building and conversion. As a business facilitator, I improve the connection between people, business process and technology. Process improvement thinking (Lean 6σ) and agile project management (Scrum) with the sociology of networks perspective are frameworks for $ucce$$. The goal is to become smarter and implement faster. My passion is to improve individual and business social media fluency that achieves results. I am focused as a project facilitator on the synergy of visual thinking for social media. Additionally, as a training facilitator, teacher and coach improving one's LinkedIn presence. I have developed innovative learning strategies for social media and research/analysis (e.g., NodeXL). I’ve realized the value of learning as a competitive edge. Have brought together creative thinking tools, visual thinking, mind mapping and social network diagrams to improve communication thru the power of collaboration + visualization + mapping.

My association with Smart Process Works provides a unique software suite of discovery tools for the desktop. Introduced social network practices to addressed cost containment and talent management issues.

We’ve helped leaders understand and drive value through social media training and networking thinking thru LinkedIn.

Recent work includes advancing technology and management innovation that accelerates customer-engaging behaviors. This is 'Program Management | 2.0'. The results focused on business operations that meet buyer demands for revenue growth.
