Sandy Crnic’s Summary

Owner-operated since 1989, Direct Media Marketing is a highly specialized marketing research telephone call center located in Bethel, CT.

Direct Media Marketing has provided telephone based data collection services to companies that expect accurate and detailed data delivered on time and at a competitive cost.

Whether conducting a customized B2B survey-based lead generation program, or monthly satisfaction or tracking studies, our clients rely on our ability to accomplish challenging studies with an experienced staff of trained interviewers who achieve much higher cooperation rates than industry standards. Our performance based billing policy guarantees you will only pay for completed interviews and qualified leads. With over 2,500 telephone studies fielded, we have the accumulated expertise to ensure truly customized research studies and deliver consistently superior outcomes for our clients.

Contact us for a same day quote on your next telephone study.
