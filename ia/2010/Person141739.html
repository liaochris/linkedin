Megan Morris’s Summary

Morris & Kalm, Inc is a professional home staging company that specializes in the art of temporarily decorating a home with the intent to sell. Homes that are staged often sell in half the amount of time that an empty house would sell. We also offer Redesign for Resale programs which use what the homeowner already owns to stage the home. Since home ownership is most likely the seller's largest investment, staging is a sound marketing tool used in today's buyers market to sell homes faster.

• Dynamic professional with 25 years of experience in decorating, staging, sales, marketing and art
• Last 6 years as CO-Owner of Morris & Kalm, Inc. and Tassel, LLC
