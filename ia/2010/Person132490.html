Mariano Jauco’s Summary

Mission
My mission is to serve and work with individuals and organizations in marginalized communities in a culturally sensitive way. I will provide a method to help develop their individual and organzational capacity to produce a positive change in our global community.

Vision
My vision is to live in a society where all individuals and organizations serve communities for the enhancement and betterment of humankind and our world.

I value:
Faith that works for justice.
The dignity of each person.
The richness that results from diversity and inclusion of all peoples.
The ongoing conversion of persons and structures.
Collaboration and participative leadership.
The life experiences of all people and their unique relationships and responsibilities.

All this I will accomplish through the universe's loving action and grace. I am committed to live by this code of ethics as a professional coach, facilitator and consultant.

Objectives
I aim to coach and educate the vast number of individuals, non-profits and businesses (both small and medium size) on smart, effective and manageable practices and tools to increase their capacity and impact on how they serve the community. In doing so these individuals and agencies will improve their knowledge, skill and capabilities. As a Coach & Consultant, I will provide a method and process for success, prosperity and a healthy life. I expect to develop long lasting client relationships with the people I connect with.
