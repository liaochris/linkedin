Michael-James Pennie’s Summary

“The Web 2.0 evolution has seen a substantial amount of emerging entrepreneurs and serial entrepreneurs taking a new direction in to the new media. For me, online business development is my business, entrepreneurship is a way of life, Web 2.0 is my mantra, and my prime motto is, ‘Do You Zoom?’”

Michael-James co-founded !escunid Online Business Development with Gwyn T. Pritchett in 2003 and since then, they’ve never looked back. It was only a natural progression for Pennie, who came from a strong background in entrepreneurship, growing up living and breathing all aspects of the family business, in addition to having a very strong mentor circle guiding him early on in his career, which initially began in corporate sales.

“In sales I recognized immediately the implications of having a strong web presence and the further emergence of acquiring and doing business online. Starting !escunid was only a natural progression and after our first project was completed and a full success, I knew we had something special…”

Michael-James Pennie accordingly brings a wealth of Web Monetization & Internet Marketing Strategy and all niche aspects pertaining to Public Relations, Social Media, Permission Email, Viral, Buzz, Guerrilla, Affiliate, SEM, and SEO marketing. He also does speaking for corporate and government events, having presented at the 2008 Beijing International Business & Technology Summit in Tiananmen Square, Beijing, China and locally at the Vancouver Enterprise Forum Momentum, has appeared on Get Connected TV of the Business News Network, CityTV’s Breakfast Television, was featured in Business in Vancouver’s Hi-Tech BC Magazine, and on the cover of ‘Make it Business’ Magazine.
