Robert Flynn’s Summary

Manufacturing and technology CEO who spent fifteen years as a senior executive at publicly traded $3 billion London based Cookson Group plc. Owned four businesses including Key-Tech, Inc which he acquired from Cookson Group and subsequently sold it to another publicly traded European company. Started and manages business brokerage firm in RI, MA, and NH specializing in mid-cap and small business transactions.

Has also been very active in Rhode Island manufacturing and economic development issues and wrote and managed the passage of five different pieces of pro-manufacturing legislation.

Former or current member of numerous boards including "Save The Bay" (New England's largest environmental organization), Rhode Island affiliate of America Diabetes, founding member "The Manufacturing Summit", and several New England based manufacturing companies.

Manufacturing, distribution, and business and medical brokerage
