Jaime Almond’s Summary

I am passionate about social media, marketing, technology and communication. I help Manufacturers' and Agency Reps to build relationships and generate new leads using tools such as Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin.

My background is in Technology. Starting my career in 1998 infrastructure support and network administration for a national sporting organization, I quickly transitioned into consulting and project management on large roll out projects with companies such as Hydro One and IBM.

In early 2009, I started Social Media Consulting and Training after studying Internet Marketing since 2005.

Social Media Strategy Consulting and Coaching:

I work with small businesses and organizations to create, implement and optimize Social Media strategies that engage their target audience and have measurable results with existing platforms such as Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin or require custom platforms.

Workshops and Trainings:

I custom create workshops and trainings for organizations on Social Media and I run the workshops for entrepreneurs and small businesses including:

* Facebook for Business
* Network Your Way to More Business Using Twitter
* Website Essentials for Entrepreneurs
* The Key to Getting Found Online
* Social Media for Business

Professional Speaker:

I speak to businesses, networking groups and associations on how social networking with Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn can build business and personal brand.

    Jaime Almond, Social Media Strategist

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Everybody is talking about Social Media, but many Independent Reps and Businesses are confused about how to make it work for them.

    I create strategies and teach businesses how to use Social Media and Social Networking to highlight what they are already doing well and share it with more people.

    When getting started with Social Media, it's important to keep it simple and master doing one thing well at a time. I can help you to do this through consulting, coaching and training.

    I focus specifically on how to optimize Social Media and Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin to find new customers, generate new business, and achieve your goals by building meaningful relationships with your target audience by helping them to know, like and trust you.

    I run workshops, teleseminars and webinars for Manufacturer's Reps and Entrepreneurs that can help anyone from the Internet novice to the seasoned social networker get results in as little as an hour a week.
