Frederic Bien’s Experience

  • Co-Founder, President and VP Media Tech

    BLiNQ Media

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 5 months)

    BLiNQ Media specializes in working with advertisers to target, optimize and analyze their social media advertising, primarily across Facebook, MySpace and LinkedIn, to maximize engagement, conversion, and viral distribution. We have a team of Ph.D.’s, interactive marketing and social media experts that understand how to reach and engage the massive and growing new audience in social media sites. We provide targeting and analysis of ad campaigns at the profile level, while respecting the privacy of individuals.

  • GM

    Ad Buying Network, Inc.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    December 2005 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    AdByNet is an online media buying agency, providing services in strategic media planning, tactical buying, analytical reporting and on-going management. AdByNet specializes in working with direct response advertisers that demand a strong Return On Investment. We measure the success of our work by the performance of our clients' sales, generated directly by the advertising that we place for them in internet search marketing and website display advertising.

    AdByNet also offers individually prepared video ads tailor-made for internet advertising. It can create the videos, then target ad placements online in Google's network: AdSense, as well as Yahoo, MSN, AOL, and a myriad of other websites including social networks, video-sharing sites, and Yellow Pages directories.

    AdByNet also helps clients track and understand the success of their advertising campaigns, and plan and manage their yearly marketing budgets. See www.AdByNet.com for more details.
