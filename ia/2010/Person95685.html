Jenny Vergara’s Summary

I've have spent much of my corporate career focused on driving sales and managing profit for multi-million and even billion dollar retail organizations using my marketing skills.
My new consulting position has allowed my big brand professional skills to be applied to a variety of different businesses and models successfully and with great results for my clients. It has allowed me to expand my thinking about how what I know can assist other businesses while continuing to teach me with new challenges that may be industry specific.
With a solid media buying background and some research and brand building experience coupled with my interest and passion with e-commerce and the internet I have managed to draw from all of these experiences and create a well-rounded marketing career.
From a creative perspective, I also have skills as a copy-writer, food stylist and an eye for design which has also come in handy when budgets demanded that I do most of this creative work with out the help of a team or agency.
Managed multiple vendor relationships and I have much experience setting strategy and establishing and managing a marketing budget.
Managed direct reports and I have also enjoyed working independently.
My desire now is to find interesting work that utilizes my professional marketing skills and expands my personal interests in restaurants, food, wine and travel.
