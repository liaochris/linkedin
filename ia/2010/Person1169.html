Aarron Walter’s Summary

I am the author of Building Findable Web Sites: Web Standards, SEO, and Beyond (http://buildingfindablewebsites.com), and speak regularly at major web design conferences including South By Southwest, An Event Apart, Voices That Matter, and Webmaster Jam Sesion to name a few.

I am the lead user experience designer for The Rocket Science Group - makers of the popular email marketing tool MailChimp.com.

I am also a member of The Web Standards Project (WaSP, http://webstandards.org), and the lead of the Education Task Force Curriculum Project. I am leading an international group of Web/education experts in the development of a curriculum designed to teach budding web professionals best practices that will ensure their success as they enter the Web industry.

Since 1999 I've has been teaching a variety of courses on Interactive Design at colleges in the US including Temple University, the University of Georgia, and The Art Institute of Atlanta. Topics of my courses include findability, nonlinear narrative, perceptual interfacing, history of communication media, user-centered interface design, dynamic web applications, and more (full course listing at http://aarronwalter.com/teaching/). From time to time I also write articles about web development for online publications including A List Apart, Peachpit.com, and SitePoint.com.

I regularly blog, build, and shares things at http://aarronwalter.com.

Aarron Walter’s Specialties:

(X)HTML, CSS, JavaScript, DOM, Ajax, PHP, MySQL, Web Standards Development, Object Oriented Programming, CMS Development, Cross-Browser/Platform Development, Site Performance Optimization Strategies, User Experience Design, Visual Design Principles, Usability & Accessibility, Information Architecture, Findability Strategies
