John T. Grosshans’s Summary

As the world's leading provider of business software, with over $17B in annual revenue, SAP (NYSE:SAP) delivers products and services that help accelerate business innovation for more than 92,000 customers. As Vice President of Strategic Initiatives, John leads critical go to market focus areas within the Premiere Customer Network, Enterprise and Utilities sectors including current customers, new customer acquisition, operations and business planning.

As VP, Global Applications and Software for Avaya, John was responsible for leading Avaya’s growing Global Applications and Software business which drives nearly $1B in annual revenue. John led the global Go To Market effort including strategic market development and expansion, the partner ecosystem, and field operations in all global markets that Avaya serves.

John held the position of SVP, Worldwide Sales, and led the market development and expansion, partnership development and revenue generation for GoRemote (Nasdaq:GRIC). GoRemote was successfully acquired by iPass (Nasdaq:IPAS). After the acquisition, John led all market development and partner initiatives for the Fixed Broadband business at iPass.

John held the position VP, Worldwide Sales and Services, and led all market development, sales, and partner initiatives worldwide for Access360, an enterprise network security software and services company. Access360 was successfully acquired by IBM.

Previous to joining Access360, John held the position of VP, Sales both domestically and internationally with Parametric Technology Corporation, an enterprise product lifecycle management software and services company. During this tenure at PTC, the company grew from $80M in annual revenue to over $1B.
