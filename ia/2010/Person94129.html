Jeff Brown’s Summary

After graduate school I joined Armstrong World Industries in Labor Relations/HR holding positions in Lancaster, PA; Pensacola, FL, Los Angeles, CA and back to Lancaster. After being there a year I was promoted to Manager Training and Development and conducted seminars for Armstrong customers around the country. Next I was named as one of six senior salesmen to a new division of Armstrong, Corporate Markets Sales Organization, based out of Dallas, TX respesenting all Armstrong products and services to all major home builders in the SW. During this time I was named Salesman of the year. In 1975 I was recruited by Milliken and Company and over a 6 year period had several promotions leading to Business Manager Contract.

In 1983 I founded Comprehensive Search. One of our early clients was the American Society of Interior Designers where I did a management turnaround in 1985/6. We have been blessed with partnering clients, loyal associates who are experts in their areas and outstanding results for our clients' projects.

My professional goals are to work with clients to increase the ROI of their human capital uisng the specialities below and to do this through dedicated and highly effective Comprehensive Search associates.

General management, management turnaround, hiring, interviewing, testing, reference checking, compensation research and facilitation, evaluating employee options, consultative selling, career counselling

  • Business Manager

    Milliken & Company

    (Privately Held; Textiles industry)

    June 1975 — April 1983 (7 years 11 months)

    My initial position was Marketing Manager Millitron Contract. By 1980 I had held additional positions leading to Business Manager Contract. At that time Milliken was the first manufacturer to create a seperate contract salesforce versus the traditional retail/contract sales force others had. In three years we tripled sales, created a dedicated dealer network, had a highly trained and accountable sales team and were able to produce margins to justify the significant investments Milliken made in terms of R&D, manufacturiing technology and people. My final involvement prior to leaving to start Comprehensive Search was to facilitate the development of Milliken Hospitality Textiles which consolidated Milliken products/capabilities into a coordinated offering to the hospitality industry.
