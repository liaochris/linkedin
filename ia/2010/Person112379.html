    Vision Magazine

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    January 2010 — Present (2 months)

    Currently working on the launch of a new magazine for tourists and residents in Sharm El Sheikh.

    January 2010 brings the start of a new decade and the launch of a new magazine for Sinai, “Vision Magazine”. The magazine is the innovation of Mr Mohamed El Masry, Chairman of “ The Specialist ” legal firm and Vision Magazine which know known as “ Alpha Group LLC “ .

    Mr. Mohamed established “The Specialist” in September 1999, when he identified a need for efficient administration and legal solutions, particularly for foreigners living in Egypt.
    "The Specialist" was the original organization to offer administration & legal services and that eradicated the problems that are often found when attempting to deal with the Egyptian legal system. Services provided include visas, work permissions , company establishment , marriage legalization , accounting services, property legalization , legal consultations and shipping and customs clearance.
    There is currently a lack of thought-provoking reading in the Sharm El Sheikh area and Vision magazine will fill the gap by providing stimulating reading for deep thinking people living in and visiting the area. The team at Vision aim to keep people up to date with news pieces and also cover their interests which could be travel, culture, history, health etc. Articles will include items of both local and international interest.

    The magazine may be a chance for people to discuss important issues that they need to talk or ask about and they may not have anywhere else to turn. Mr. Mohamed’s vision for the magazine is for it to be read by all family members “by respecting their minds and thoughts, by providing them something interesting and useful to read.”

    The magazine covers and highlights the frustrations such as, the language barrier, lack of sufficient and professional information, deficient customer service and of course the infamous “Egyptian Time”.
