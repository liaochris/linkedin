Mike Roman’s Summary

Mike is a POLISHED BUSINESS EXECUTIVE with a proven history of success including nearly fifteen years of successful leadership experience.

Mike is highly skilled in effective, strategic management of Sales, Operations, Consulting, and Administrative professionals in addition to full operations and profit and loss (P&L) management.

Mike is highly self motivated and possesses a significant level of expertise with employee development, training on the art of consultative and strategic selling and client retention as well as effective branding and market penetration. Special expertise in complex opportunity management, contract development and contract negotiations. Strong communications and organizational skills, project management and training.

Mike demonstrates a unique ability to train employees on how to qualify and build robust relationships with decision makers to define, understand and communicate business objectives and execute results-based solutions with timely, cost-effective and profitable results.

Throughout Mike’s career span, he has worked for three of the largest and most well respected Global IT Resource Management firms in the world.

Mike has enjoyed a significant record of achievement of hiring some of the most talented Sales and Operations professionals and has consistently applied an emphasis on advanced training to develop his employees into becoming top performers who have managed complex projects for clients around the globe.

Mike continues to keep abreast of current technology by attending technical training seminars on an ongoing basis and being an active member of several professional associations.

Mike's top priority is ensuring the complete resource and project delivery process and ensuring that the firm he represents exceeds its client’s, employee's, and shareholder's expectations.
