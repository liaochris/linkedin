Elizca Stemmet’s Summary

Elizca was born in Durbanville, Western Cape and matriculated at Huguenot High School in Wellington. After school she ventured back to Cape Town and received het ND in Public Relations in 2002 at the Cape Technikon. (Now known as Cape Peninsula University of Technology)

During her 10 years + work experience (national and international) she have combined traditional marketing and PR with E-marketing (Online Marketing) successfully, as she believe this is the way of the future for marketing and PR in companies. She has a passion for the internet; especially Social Networking and read and study as much as possible about this industry.

Currently Elizca is working for Naspers/Media24 as a Content Manager for two of their websites. Elizca was one of the first people to have joined the Wiki unit almost two years ago and was instrumental in getting the history sites off the ground.

In her spare time you will find her playing on the internet, in the gym or out walking with her dogs in the Northern Suburbs of Cape Town.
