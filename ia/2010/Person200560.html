Aurora Arlet’s Summary

Industry Innovationist and Trend Setter

Aurora Arlet doesn’t miss a beat. Whether it’s serving on the Technology Section of PRSA Denver, lecturing about social media or connecting with good old fashioned print publications, Arlet is on top of all the industry trends and knows exactly where her clients will be successful.

Arlet found her knack and passion for technology while working for a consulting agency in Scandinavia with international innovators such as Nokia, Skype, Telia Sonera, and Movial.

Using her strong sense of industry trends Arlet has helped to create and execute award-winning campaigns that have secured her clients coverage with U.S. and international publications such as The Wall Street Journal; USA Today; MSNBC; The Guardian; WIRED; TechCrunch; The New York Times; Gigaom, CED, Communications Technology; Broadcasting & Cable; Light Reading; Cable & Satellite International; Fierce; InformationWeek; Baseline and BusinessWeek (and many more).

She received a Bachelors of Science from the University of Colorado, at Boulder with an emphasis in Broadcast and a minor in Sociology and Gender Studies.

Aurora Arlet’s Specialties:

Specialty industries include cable and telecommunications; gaming and entertainment; enterprise software; wireless; security; semiconductors; application development; networking and electronics.
