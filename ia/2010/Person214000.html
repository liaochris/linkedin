Wayne Naylor’s Summary

Executive Summary:

Business Executive with CPA and 20+ years of Financial, Risk Management, Treasury, Accounting, Audit, Safety, Security, SOX and MIS experience with Fortune 500 company and public accounting firms. Developed loss control & safety programs, initiated return to work programs and process to review and manage TPA activities saving $2.5 million+ p.a. workers’ compensation costs. Established global liability insurance program saving $350,000 p.a. in premium costs, later converted to high deductible program saving an additional $500,000 p.a.. Significant treasury, audit and financial experience provides solid base for current SOX business environment.
