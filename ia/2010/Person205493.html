Tod McMahon’s Summary

I have spent the majority of my career working in the corporate library and the strategic planning and business development departments of a Fortune 500 telecommunications firm. For eleven years as the primary library point of contact for all business engineering and organizational development research. The next three years in the strategic planning department where I provided market research and competitive intelligence to the executive-level staff. I was involved in the preparation of strategic plans, company and industry profiles, gap analyses and executive briefings. I managed outside research vendors. One of the most exciting projects during this time was working with a small team of scientists and a program manager that introduced the world’s first operational 5 Watt fuel cell power source used with handheld electronics. For the last three years with this organization, I managed a library with a small staff and a $300K budget. I am now working for a small private company in the import/export field.
