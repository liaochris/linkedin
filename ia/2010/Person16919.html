Art Stiefel’s Summary

I am a Creative Director, Brand Strategist, Marketing Innovator and experienced business leader with a proven background in successful strategic planning, program development, marketing, management and implementation from start-up companies to Fortune 100 corporations. I provide a calm, collaborative, clear direction across teams in diverse, high-growth multi-discipline environments. My breadth of experience spans from pharmaceutical, technology, manufacturing, telecommunication, construction, healthcare, software, hardware, government, fine food, automotive, entertainment,real estate, Ecommerce, and nonprofit industries.

I work just as easily with clients around the corner as well as around the globe. From simple small-space ads to highly complex international branding initiatives my vast experience is something that each and
every one of my clients benefits from.

Art Stiefel’s Specialties:

Branding Consultant and Creative Marketing Guru specializing in brand strategy development, brand positioning, brand identity, brand marks, brand continuity, brand communications, brand analysis, brand ideation and brand innovation. Develop proprietary brand solutions for my clients and then execute them across all relevant communication platforms.
