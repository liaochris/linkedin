Patrick Hearron MBA’s Summary

I've had the chance to prove myself in some of the toughest sales environments, where my work has literally been seen by millions. I left the world of television to work in the world of technology based business to business marketing and sales. In addition to on the job experience I hold a Masters in Business Administration from California Lutheran University focusing on Business Development and Leadership.

I am currently working with Adapp Solutions at HasOffers.com. HasOffers.com is the premiere Affiliate Network optimization software. That gives total control to merchant advertisers and affiliates for their online marketing campaigns.

The Hasoffers.com software allows clients to organize and optimizing online advertising campaigns, privatize SEO tracking URLs, and real time reports. HasOffers.com also supplies merchants with robust set of features, enabling them to manage offers and track performance in real-time. The software also provides the complete tools for building an Affiliate Network. This powerful white label software makes assigning accounts to mangers, creating custom payouts, monitoring campaigns, and communicating with affiliates quick and simple.

If you have any questions about HasOffers.com, and what it can do for your business let me know, I would be happy to help.

I am also the owner of "As Seen On: Advertising". An online Advertising Network focused on promoting exclusive products with television exposure and name recognition through a wide range of publishers and affiliates.
