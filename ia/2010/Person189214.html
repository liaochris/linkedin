Hannah Brazee Gregory’s Summary

Founder of Shoestring Creative Group, the nonprofit's agency.
Nonprofit communications expert.
Creative director for nonprofit campaigns.
Nonprofit branding guru.
Speaker and workshop presenter.

Hannah is a national leading expert in nonprofit communications and a sought-after speaker and workshop presenter. At Shoestring, she is responsible for all facets of agency services, most importantly her role as senior strategist and chief creative director. Hannah is particularly well-known for your nonprofit branding expertise and creative energy. Hannah has been steadily growing "the nonprofit's agency" since 1998.

Shoestring Creative Group is an award-winning, full-service marketing communications agency that exclusively serves nonprofits. As national leaders in nonprofit communications, our group is committed to providing creative, affordable solutions specifically created to meet the needs and challenges of nonprofits. We are one of only a few firms in the country that specializes in serving this unique niche.

Mission + Vision Statement:
To make high-quality, creative and effective marketing communications services accessible and affordable to nonprofit organizations under a sustainable business model.

To increase the capacity of nonprofit organizations and further their missions through marketing communications.

To develop best practices in nonprofit marketing communications and provide learning opportunities for nonprofit organizations.

To raise awareness of the important role nonprofit organizations play in our society and increase public perception/opinion of nonprofit organizations across the country.
