John Berling Hardy’s Summary

My passion for helping individuals and businesses to improve has become a worldwide crusade. With my strong background in crisis management, personal advising and international affairs, I have many years of experience working with a wide array of businesses in numerous cultural settings - either consulting on affairs large and small, or speaking on panels and keynotes.

After years in the financial industry, it wasn’t until the early 1990’s that I began to formulate my ideas. While working as a consultant for a struggling food processing company based in Hungary, the wheels began to spin. When fraud, lies and corruption emerged from nowhere....a hidden game was revealed.

Once back in Toronto, Ontario I began to notice the same patterns I experienced at the plant in Hungary repeating themselves in both professional and personal settings. This wasn’t an isolated incident - this was a worldwide phenomena!

From this discovery, Berling Hardy I developed a leading algorithm behind the seeminlgy chaotic array of events that I had experienced over the years. I turned this to my consulting and have been helping businesses improve year after year.

Now a published author of the highly regarded “The Hidden Game Revealed”, I have developed a range of cutting edge training courses, consulting programs, training seminars and more.

My main passions are helping people to understand how The Hidden Game can change their lives and consulting businesses large and small on how to increase their efficiency and improve their productivity.

I also have a very active blog where there are free downloads - http://www.playingtheplayersblog.com

John Berling Hardy’s Experience

  • President

    The Hidden Game

    (Management Consulting industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    After many years working in the financial industry, in the early 1990’s, I was chosen by a major financial institution to come to the aid of a struggling food processing company, Szeged Paprika and Meat Processing Plant, based in Hungary.

    When I arrived, I was assured that the problems were directly related to the liberalization of Hungary’s centrally controlled economy to a market based economy.

    Although these challenges were real enough, the company concealed another, much darker, reality. I felt that something just wasn’t quite right, and I was correct. The true catalyst of the company’s difficulties was completely concealed from the workers, from the community and even from myself.

    What had caused the company’s difficulties was not the economic shift in Hungary as presented, but rather fraud which enlisted the participation of the senior management of the plant, including the local bank’s management (my employer).

    This was when I began formulating the elements of The Hidden Game.

    Upon returning to Toronto and to my business in carriage trade custom construction, I began to notice the same patterns I experienced in Hungary repeating themselves again and again.

    This wasn’t an isolated incident – this was a worldwide phenomena. The same inconsistencies, the same shifts in power, the same manipulations.

    Gradually I began to see this pattern in all aspects of his life – business and personal and spent much time thinking about what it was that I sensed but could not put his finger on.

    As a result I developed the algorithm behind the seemingly chaotic array of events which I had experienced over the years. I developed the master key to the players and the hidden game, a key that I want to share with the world in order to help people from all nations to take control of their lives and to no longer be manipulated by “the players” of the world.
