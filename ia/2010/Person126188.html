Lisa Marie Patterson’s Summary

Lisa Marie Patterson is highly talented in many areas of design and branding from conceptual brainstorming sessions to innovative finished products. Lisa excels in the areas of brochure design, identity development and marketing collateral for clients in retail, residential, health care, resort, and technology industries.

Lisa approaches each client and project with enthusiasm and creativity. Her superb organizational skills, attention to detail, and excellent grasp of software allow her to seamlessly deliver what both the client and the consumer request. Additionally, her excellence in typography, craft, and customer service continually make vast impressions on her clients.

Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite 3 (Photoshop, lllustrator, InDesign, Acrobat); Microsoft Office Suite; Experience in HTML and Dreamweaver; Excellent written and verbal communication skills; Mac and PC platforms
