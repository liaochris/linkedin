Lars Scofield’s Summary

Corporate transactional attorney with (1) substantial in-house legal experience as corporate counsel a Fortune 1000 public company and general counsel at a privately-held software company and (2) broad mergers and acquisition and securities transactional experience representing a diverse range of clients at a large Atlanta law firm. Currently manage and support all legal needs for two significant Equifax business units.
