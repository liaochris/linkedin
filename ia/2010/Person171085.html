Renco Schoemaker’s Summary

After my master at the Vrij Universiteit I started Inzicht Marktonderzoek with Mark Hoornweg. The past years have been quite amazing in terms of experience, customers, development and turnover. We conduct research for several large companies and provide online panel data for a large number of (inter)national market research companies. In the next year we will launch two new concepts: one related to subsidised research for smaller organisations and the other related to our panel.

Renco Schoemaker’s Specialties:

Advanced market research (CFA, SEM, conjoint), data analysis (SPSS, AMOS, LatentGold), programming (PHP, Joomla!), organisational research (DINAMO, OCAI) and, of course, quantitative online research with our panels in the Netherlands (250.000), Belgium (50.000) and France (150.000).
