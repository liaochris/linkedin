Andrea Derricks’s Summary

Andrea Derricks is an Associate at Goodman&Company, a strategic marketing consulting firm for Fortune 500 and Global 1000 BtoB and BtoC clients. At Goodman, she:

- Contributed to the development of an organizational "communications planning playbook" for a large online payments company as a SME on the company's proprietary project management platform
- Helped analyze and synthesize secondary research and internal stakeholder interviews to provide strategic input for defining an innovative, multi-channel consumer acquisition strategy for an online payments company
- Project managed and contributed strategically to a global strategic web design exploration for a Fortune 100 software company
- Helped develop the strategic framework for a loyalty program for an online payments company
- Managed and contributed strategic input on a BtoC CRM lifecycle project for a Global 500 technology company
- Led internal new product development, which was piloted with an online payments company and a successful non-profit
- Conducted qualitative “outside-in” research in three global markets to identify new product requirements for the largest business software company
- Performed an audit on 40 businesses to inform six innovative continuity strategies for one of the world's largest direct response companies
