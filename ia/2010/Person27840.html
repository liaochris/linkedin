Brett Carneiro’s Summary

As a Digital Media strategist my role is to consult and advise clients on how best to take their brand/products into the digital world. Developing the best up to date digital media plan is crucial for companies (no matter how big or small) to stay current and relevant. In this position I have been trusted to lead, execute and maintain campaigns across various industry fields from fashion to entertainment to food. While serving as a digital leader and manager I also work towards building trusting collaborative relationships with my clients and think this is very important to both of our successes. Demonstrating knowledge of client brands, brands equity, consumer definition and motivations is of the highest priority and trusting the right person with this is crucial for taking your business/brand into the digital world. Not only can I do this, but I really enjoy doing this at all levels.

I am always seeking new opportunities and looking forward to setting new goals, both personally and professionally.
