Terry Del Casale’s Summary

Having the privilege to work with recognized, top-tier IT technology channel partners IBM, Sirius Computer Solutions and Dataskill, I have carved a niche in helping companies drive sales of IBM middleware software products and services to the mid-market and enterprise.
