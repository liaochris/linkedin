Robert Hans’s Summary

Accomplished business consultant with comprehensive knowledge of strategic human resources and best business practices. Seasoned leader with highly developed managerial skills and extensive recruiting expertise. Innovative entrepreneurial professional proficient at start-up business endeavors.

Currently, President of RHHR Associates a consulting company offering Recruitment Solutions | HR Management | Social Media Training to our clients.
