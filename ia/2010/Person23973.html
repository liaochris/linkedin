Bill Peck’s Summary

•After graduating with honors in 1997 from the South East Center of Photographic Studies, Bill began working for Tom Bartlett Studios (later bought by Quebecor World) in Orlando, Florida. Bill was the assistant/photographer for this company.

•Bill made the moved to Florida Imaging in 2001 (later transitioned to Acropolis Inc.) where he was the sole photographer who managed & ran the department. Bill's experience there was outstanding. He gained valuable experience and hones his skills in catalog, architectural and food photography, amongst a long list of many other disciplines and styles.

•During the time of 1998 and 2001, Bill taught photography at the Disney Institute at Walt Disney World in Orlando. He had a rewarding and hands on teaching experience with students and focused on Portrait, Journalism, Nature, and Travel photography.

•In January 2006, Bill became 100% self employed. His goals are to continue to build his client base and keep delivering the highest quality photography to his clients.

Bill Peck’s Specialties:

Bill's specialties are Architectural, Product, People, Events and Lifestyle photography.
