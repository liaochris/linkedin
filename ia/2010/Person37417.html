Chris B Johnson’s Summary

Chris B. Johnson is a full-time real estate professional Specializing in REO Transactions. Currently Chris is an REO Listing agent for GoodmanDean (Wells Fargo), CalHFA, SAM-ResNet, Absolute REO and First American REO.Chris has a strong background in financial planning, securities analysis, and asset allocation. Chris has exceptional abilities as a hands-on, goal oriented professional with over 20 years of progressive experience in all phases of planning, operations and management. His proven skills and experience in both detailed tasks and executive level decision making include the ability to dissect and interpret financial contracts, identify real estate investment opportunities, formulate strategic plans, and implement these strategies in challenging and diverse situations. Over his career, Chris has participated in the purchase, sale and funding of over 100 Million dollars worth of Residential Income, Commercial and Industrial Real Estate. Chris has specialized in loan loss mitigation, short sale negotiation and REO marketing since 2005. Chris has been repeatedly recognized for professional achievement. He has a strong ability to build trust with clients and create long lasting business relationships. As a commitment to excellence in his field, Chris earned membership in:
The National Association of Realtors, The American Society of Chartered Financial Consultants. The California Association of Realtors, International Association of Financial Planning. The Simi – Moorpark Association of Realtors, The American Society of Chartered Life Underwriters. Active Member of Ventura & SouthLand Regional MLS. Practioners Division of the IAFP, National Association of Securities Dealers licenses series 6, 7 and 63.
