Ed Cohen’s Summary

Riding the Tiger: Leading Through Learning in Turbulent Times
by Priscilla Nelson and Ed Cohen (ASTD Press)
www.ridingthetiger.com

When leadership matters most…

Are you prepared to influence change & guide your organization in today’s ever-changing business environment? Riding the Tiger provides a rare opportunity to learn from a catastrophic event that shook the foundation of a thriving global organization. Learn innovative leadership techniques and ideas for fostering change that are essential for everyone in these challenging times.

ED COHEN is a global talent leader with 30 years experience in strategic leadership, influencing change and culture, and talent development. Having conducted business in more than 40 countries, Ed has led companies through periods of significant growth, change and crisis.

He is author of Leadership Without Borders, Successful Strategies from World Class Leaders (John Wiley & Sons). It achieved 21 weeks at #1 (global leadership category) on Amazon.com.

From 2005 to 2009, Ed lived in India working as Senior VP for Mahindra Satyam, a global, technology professional services firm. Under Ed’s leadership, Satyam was only company in Asia to attain the # 1 ranking by American Society for Training & Development (ASTD).

They were also the 1st company in Asia to achieve the Top 10 (#8) in Training Magazine Top 125 ranking.

Prior to Satyam, Ed Cohen was eight years with Booz Allen Hamilton. As strategic leader of their corporate university, Booz Allen become one of the most widely recognized corporate universities. In 2006, they achieved the # 1 ranking on both Training Magazine’s Top 125 list and ASTD’s global list. Booz Allen received more than 30 Excellence in Practice recognitions from ASTD (American Society for Training & Development).

Ed Cohen earned a Master of Science degree in education from Nova Southeastern University and a Bachelor of Science degree in accounting from University of Florida.
