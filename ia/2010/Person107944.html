Juhi Raje Upadhyay’s Summary

I got hooked on to Marketing and Lawyered in the head while designing college projects for the Ad/Marketing subject and later realizing that my ideas had already caught on great with brands internationally. That was the Whoa!! moment that brings me to today, a social media evangelist and traditional media enthusiast.

I started reviewing businesses, their pros/cons, and their marketing strategies while writing reviews for tolmolbol.com, a Pennywise Solutions venture. It gives me an analytical edge and pro feedback experience on cases/researches/strategies, all while helping me make a foolproof plan for my own clients.

Currently working on the online brand presence of Skullcandy India.
