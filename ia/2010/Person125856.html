Lisa B. Marshall’s Summary

By improving face-to-face and online communication skills, my clients improve productivity, build stronger relationships, & achieve success.

I deliver workshops & keynotes as well as organizational consulting & private coaching in: conflict mgt, public speaking, professional networking, interviewing, & social media. Clients include organizations (Univ. of Pennsylvania, Genentech, Johns Hopkins, Harvard Univ., Stanford Univ., New York Academy of Sciences, Roche, Merck, etc.) as well as C-level professionals, clinicians, & researchers.

More at www.lisabmarshall.com

I'm host & creator of a popular communication skills podcast published by Macmillan. The show enjoys approximately 178,000 downloads a month and has been consistently in the iTunes Top 25 Business Podcasts (which is amazing to me since they have >150,000 to choose from)! For over a year the show has been the top rated show in the iTunes "career" category. This fun, friendly, and very practical show covers a wide range of communication skills topics from starting conversations, to effective meetings, to adding humor to talks.

http://publicspeaker.quickanddirtytips.com

For help with interviewing, I wrote "The Public Speaker's Guide to Ace Your Interview" which is an audiobook available from Macmillan Audio on Amazon, Audible, and iTunes. I'm hoping to help my friends (and others) get the jobs they want!

Listen to clips: http://cli.gs/ZZbrm
www.interviewextras.com

Recently, I'm working with Marc A. Wolfe to deliver motivational communication programs. Marc and I have a unique on-stage chemistry that is funny, passionate, and compelling. I'm really enjoying delivering these new programs. Topics include: Authenticity and Integrity, Improvisation, Interviewing & Social Media.

www.marshallwolfe.com

Finally, I like to sing with gusto, talk in Spanish, swim, take pictures, and spend time with my kids!
