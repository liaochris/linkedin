Jan Schraeyen’s Summary

Everything I've done so far, asked for communication skills. Whether it was tele-sales, internship as youthcare worker, service desk, organizing events, hosting a weekly radio show or account manager.

Next to that I've always loved to write on things that interest me, which led to contributing to Kick Snare Magazine, Poppunt Magazine, biographies...

While being constantly involved in music and watching the times change, an appetite for marketing knowledge grew on me.

With Wafflez & Wordz I created my own solution to combine those things and I'm open to all kinds of challenges.

I write mainly in Dutch and English, but also do French to Dutch translations on a daily base.

Goals? A sense of gratification when the job's done and a feeling that it mattered.

Website is in development as I write...

Jan Schraeyen’s Specialties:

As a person: structuring, methodical thinking and working.
As copywriter: looking at the whole concept / strategy, understanding which role the content will play and fine tuning it till it fits its purpose and recipients.
