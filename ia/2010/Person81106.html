Heidi Cuppari’s Summary

Currently I'm working on a few projects, all within the Social Enterprise space:

I4C Campaign - (http://www.i4ccampaign.com) A campaign committed to growing innovative businesses that are dedicated to people and the planet in addition to profits. (Triple Bottom Line).

W1SD0M - (http://www.w1sd0m.net) A marketplace for entrepreneurs, advisors and investors in the sustainable enterprise space.

Team2B - A global community of direct selling entrepreneurs who seek to create social change with a percent of their profits.

Ms. Cuppari has spent her professional career building and growing companies. Recently, she started and ran a consumer products company and grew distribution from zero to six hundred stores in one year. Prior to joining mShopper, Ms. Cuppari directed the fundraising and investor relations department for Acumen Fund, a venture fund which focuses on delivering goods and services to people in developing countries. Her department raised over 8 million dollars in one calendar year. Ms. Cuppari is an alumna of Brown University, which inspired her to make a difference in the world.
