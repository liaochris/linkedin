Frederic Dumeny’s Summary

I'm currently the Vice President of Business Development at Adfonic, a self-service mobile advertising platform.
I'm also the CEO and Founder of Fast>>Forward, a business development consultancy serving the European Mobile Internet market.
Fast>>Forward is a multi-local company, we're europeanizing Mobile Internet services.

Until May 2009, I was the Head of Sales and Business Development in Europe for Motricity, a mobile content solution provider

Since founding my first Internet company more than 15 years ago, I have been involved in every new forms of web innovation.

My interests in the last 10 years led me to the mobile internet market in Europe. I've been launching business, concluding & managing partnerships between content providers, platform suppliers, telcos & medias, building & managing sales teams in the Mobile Internet European market for small fast-moving startups as well as for large international companies

I live in Paris where I'm a co-founder of the Mobile Monday & spend most of my time in airports witnessing and participating in the mobile innovation.
