Judge Matthew J. Byrne’s Summary

An attorney for 18 years, Mr. Byrne has been a partner in the firm of Balsamo, Byrne, Cipriani & Ellsworth since 1994. Mr. Byrne joined the private practice of law after a career in the insurance industry as an examiner for both Liberty Mutual and General Star Management Company (member of the Gen Re Group). Mr. Byrne has also served as an Assistant Rockland County attorney advising various departments of county government, inclusive of representing the county's interests in litigation. He is currently in his fourth term as Justice for the Village of Suffern, New York and is also an Adjunct Professor of Law, Government and Business at Iona, Manhattan, and Dominican Colleges. Judge Byrne is a candidate for NYS Supreme Court Justice for the 9th Judicial District (Orange, Rockland, Westchester, Putnam and Dutchess Counties) on November 2, 2010.

Judge Matthew J. Byrne’s Specialties:

In private practice, Mr. Byrne concentrates in the area of residential and commercial real estate transactions, elder law, estate planning, probate and estate and trust administration, corporate and not-for-profit formation.
