Steven Johns’s Summary

Small and medium sized businesses often have unique, communication requirements that are more demanding than the largest companies. Everyone in a smaller team is vital and may perform many roles. That’s why your business needs a communication system that fits with and adapts to your business and allows you to focus on what’s really important.

At Tpad we specialise in providing cost effective and powerful managed communications solutions for businesses like yours.
From the most basic to the most complex solutions, we start with a fact-finding consultation to understand from you how your business communicates with customers and suppliers. Together we can then agree what the “actual” requirements of your business are.

A logical process then follows through design, proposal, system demonstration, order and system installation all of which is supported by excellent project management and superb after sales care.

Using this logical, tried and tested method, we ensure that your project is delivered painlessly, right first time, and on budget.

We also understand that your business may not require a complete overhaul of your systems, but that you may be seeking extra efficiency, enhancements and economies with your existing solution. We are skilled and experienced in projects of this type ensuring you can maximize your current investment AND benefit from new applications and communication methods that make operational and economic sense.
