Donald J. Reilly’s Summary

Information Technology executive with extensive experience building domestic and international IT operations for technology organizations of all sizes. Experienced Business Executive with full P&L responsibility bridges the communications gap between Technology-based IT operations and Business and Strategic Initiatives.

Specialty: Building business alignment based on strategic use of technology. If you feel your company is not getting competitive advantage in the marketplace from your technology investment, I can help.

Holding a Masters degree in Computer Science from Loyola University of Chicago and an MBA from Northern Illinois University's Executive MBA program, Don has a strong background in technology, project, product and personnel management and specializes in the strategic use of technology for competitive advantage. Don also has strong sales and sales management experience, having held positions as senior manager in sales management in both the national and international arenas.

Don has been involved in projects in technical, product and project management consulting for businesses of all sizes, involving infrastructure design, deployment and management, systems development, security, network and DMZ design, business continuity and disaster recovery planning.
