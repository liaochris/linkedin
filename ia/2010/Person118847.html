Kristan Lehtinen’s Summary

Business law attorney and litigator with substantial experience representing local, national, and international clients, business owners and corporate executives across the business spectrum. Profiled in the 2009 Rising Stars editions of Mountain States Super Lawyers Magazine as one of the mountain states' top business litigators under 40. The firm of Lovaas & Lehtinen, P.C. is client-centered, providing large firm style expertise and skill with small firm responsiveness and service. Currently representing clients located in Italy, Russia, Australia, Ireland, and Canada.

Practice Areas: Business litigation; corporate litigation; commercial litigation; civil litigation; real estate litigation; construction litigation; business tort litigation; business transactions; business contracts; licensing agreements; real estate contracts; asset purchase agreements; entity formation; general corporate counsel representation.

Ms. Lehtinen and the firm routinely represent a diverse client list in litigation matters involving breach of contract disputes, commercial leases, and debt collection.

Ms. Lehtinen is a Member of both the International and Business Law Sections of the ABA including: Business and Corporate Litigation, Business Courts, Corporate Counseling and Litigation, International Litigation, and Partnerships and Alternative Business Entities; and also a Member of of the Clark County Bar and Sin City Chamber of Commerce. Ms. Lehtinen was recently unanimously voted to serve on the University of Las Vegas Nevada Libraries Board of Advisors.

Kristan Lehtinen’s Specialties:

Local and international business practice on every aspect of commercial litigation and transactional matters including preparation and review of licensing agreements, franchising contracts, and general business contracts. Routinely represent clients in matters involving the set-up of foreign corporations and affiliates in the U.S. and maintenance of those entities.
