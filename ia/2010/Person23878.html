Bill Kharay’s Summary

Multi-disciplined Mechanical Engineer with over twenty years design experience of upstream & downstream oil, gas & petrochemical industry projects. Possesses good understanding and knowledge of process engineering and plant design. Specialist in the design and specification of high end HPHT API Turbo & LS compressors, HP Pumps, Turbo generators & Diesel plant, Cryogenic engineering incl. ASU & Utility systems, Offsite LNG tanks and process equipment. Process equipment packages including Gasoline Sweetening, Gas Dehydration, Synthesis plant and static equipment.
Practical individual experienced in equipment manufacture, inspection & testing in shop or field. Highly motivated pro-active and conceptual thinker capable of handling the most complex and demanding project assignments at home and-field office. Computer literate with an excellent understanding of project infrastructure from FEED-PMC-EPC.
