Anthony S. Caponiti’s Summary

Anthony S. Caponiti is a passionate entrepreneur who seeks to combine his life-long love and knowledge of sports with social media and web technology.

As a Partner at Activ8Social, Anthony is dedicated to the online activation of professional athletes, entertainers, teams, leagues, and brands through the development of social media strategies and digitally integrated marketing campaigns.

With the assistance of StarBurbs technology, Activ8Social is able to assist clients in establishing an authentic online identity. StarBurbs is currently under heavy beta development and aiming to launch in 2010.

Prior to his entrepreneurial journey, Anthony worked for two years as a Strategy & Change Consultant for IBM's Global Business Services.

Anthony earned his BBA from Emory University’s Goizueta Business School in Atlanta, GA with concentrations on venture management consulting, business law, and communications.

Before turning to marathons to satisfy his athletic thirst, Anthony ran track & field for the Emory Eagles competing in the grueling 800m race (half mile).

Anthony is also a member of the Global Catalyst Group, an avid student of entrepreneurial leadership, and plans to become active with Toastmasters in 2010.
