Bill Bean’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE
* Facilitating the development of company growth strategies and startup plans that increase the pace of execution and the probability of success in businesses of all sizes
* Facilitating the development of sustainability plans for companies who that want begin to position their organization from the top down for the "triple bottom line" of social, environmental and economic impact.
* Coaching professionals to be more successful and productive more quickly in their businesses, careers and retirement endeavors.
* Teaching the components and mechanics of building a real life business strategy that can be managed to for success.
* Speaking about building strategic plans for existing businesses and green entrepreneurial startups. And, more often today, speaking about reinventing careers in the green economy for displaced mid-career professionals and articulating the true value delivered from past experience and existing skill sets into those new market opportunities.

GOAL
Have Green Planning & Coaching follow the path that we help create for others:
* A positive result for our clients and the community
* A minimal impact on the environment, and
* A sustainable economic position for GP&C so that we can continue to grow, thrive and make a greater difference.

Bill Bean’s Specialties:

Unique ability to ask the right questions and create an open professional atmosphere providing a "safe harbor" where clients feel comfortable thinking out loud about their professional dreams and goals. Then we quickly transform them into a profesional planning format for their existing business, entrepreneurial initiatives, careers or retirement.

Bill Bean’s Experience

  • President and LEED AP

    Green Planning & Coaching

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2006 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Strategic business consultant in the "green" and sustainability space offering strategic business planning, professional coaching, workshops, and speaking engagements.

    Combined 30 years of business and planning experience with Professional Coaching certifications and "Green" credentials as LEED AP and NAHB CGP, "Green Consultant" from the Green Business League, and "Green Auditor" and Master CSO (Chief Sustainability Officer) class graduate from Strategic Sustainability Consulting. Also selected as Adjunct Professor in the emerging Green Building Program at UMass, Amherst.

    OUR MISSION at GP&C is to help clients capture the vision for their "Green" careers and business initiatives and build better plans faster to achieve them.

    FOR BUSINESSES: We facilitate and deliver growth plans and sustainability plans (starting with a "green audit") for existing business and strategic plans and "pitch decks" for entrepreneurial startups. The product of our work creates a clear, consistent vision for an organization and enables client's to deliver a confident, compelling message about their future business that can energize their team and educate and entice board members, bankers, investors or others. Areas of focus include entrepreneurial "pitch decks", formalized business plans in the "green" space and sustainability plans.

    FOR INDIVIDUALS: We provide one-on-one professional career coaching, executive coaching and retirement career coaching. The product of our work is a clearer path for career growth, particularly for mid-level professionals who have lost not only their jobs, but their careers in the recent financial meltdown. Those clients often see the "green" economy as a sector of unique growth, opportunity and hope. We help instill discipline in their thinking and confidence in a tough transition, focused on converting their experience and skill sets into tangible value that they can bring to their new careers.

  • Sales, Sales Manager, Marketing Manager, Division Manager

    Trus Joist Corporation (bought by Weyerhaeuser in Jan 2000)

    (Building Materials industry)

    March 1984 — April 2000 (16 years 2 months)

    Territory Manager (Outside Sales position) in 12 Northeastern states for 7 years ("Rookie Of The Year" 1984, and Salesman Of The Year in 1985 and 1988). Then Sales Manager for the Northeast (highest sales volume and most profitable region in North America for my entire tenure) and New England Territories. Followed by Marketing Manager position building the strategic plan and team to introduce a new Engineered Lumber Material (TimberStrand®) located in Minneapolis MN. In 3 years achieved $10,000,000 in annual sales of this new "green" (more resource efficient) structural lumber material. Sales included 13 different market segments throughout the US and Canada. Promoted in 1995 to Division Manager of the Northeastern US and all of Canada for Residential and Industrial products, a $150,000,000 responsibility.

  • Strategic Planning Analyst, Marketing Manager Specialty Products

    International Paper Company

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    June 1975 — February 1984 (8 years 9 months)

    Strategic Planning Analyst for newly formed Wood Products Group of IP, (a Fortune 500 Company) located at their corporate HQ in Manhattan, NY. Spent 3 years in strategic planning developing a wood products strategy to utilize the second largest timberland resource in North America, extract the greatest value and provide secured access to the wood fiber required to run the core manufacturing businesses of paper and cardboard. Relocated to Mobile AL and then Dallas, TX in 1978 as Marketing Manager for Specialty Wood Products. Built the plans and the teams to successfully introduce new wood products (MSR - Machine Stress Rated lumber and Scaffold Plank).with higher value than commodity lumber products.
