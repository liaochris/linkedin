Brenda Violette’s Summary

VBS Virtual Assist, LLC was founded in 2008 by experienced administrative professional Brenda Violette with the goal of assisting small and medium-sized business owners in growing their businesses to be more effective, more efficient, and ultimately more profitable. The company’s core services include a full-line of online business management services including virtual assistance, Internet marketing, and product development.

Brenda’s thirty-year career as an administrative professional has provided her with a wealth of valuable and diverse real-world business experiences that enable her to provide insightful, grounded, and resourceful strategies to business owners across the United States and abroad.

Under Brenda’s leadership, VBS Virtual Assist, LLC has grown to include an impressive partner network comprised of experienced professionals, including virtual assistants, web marketing consultants, and graphic designers, all respected and accomplished within their specific areas of expertise. Brenda and the entire VBS network share the same steadfast commitment to excellence in customer service, and providing reliable and knowledgeable services that exceed the expectations of each and every client.

Now entering into 3rd year, VBS Virtual Assist continues to grow through the firm’s many satisfied return clients, frequent referrals, and new business owners excited to learn more about how the online business management services provided by VBS Virtual Assist can help them manage and grow their businesses.

For more information on improving your business with online business management services, please visit our website at www.VBSVirtualAssist.com
