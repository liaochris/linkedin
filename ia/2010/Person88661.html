James Voss’s Summary

I've been fascinated with technology and computers ever since my early childhood years, taking things apart and putting them back together. That was my niche, troubleshooting is how I learned. I have an excellent knowledge over a large variety of operating systems including some of the most complex to the simplest. In the past I have been told that I can explain complicated things in non-complicated ways which in this industry is a plus. I am very interested in Technology, Real Estate, and the Stock Market.

Many times I find myself a product in my own environment confused and feeling to have lost all ability to troubleshoot issues but the flame inside never dies and I have never; not once had something technical that I cannot conquer. In my past I have responded to many large-scaled denial of service attacks on small networks and large networks. I have also studied this for years including things like the hacker’s mindset and how it reacts to war.

My goals in the future are to become as successful as I possibly can with the drive and ambition like nobody else while excelling in whatever field I find myself in the middle of. My goals can be as complex as the Tuyuca language, but I like keeping things in perspective and living and learning on a day to day basis while still paving the road ahead.

As of right now I find myself doing consultation work along with small business management. I act as your own personal IT department; I proactively manage the technical side of your business so that you can focus on expanding and building a future for your company. Your technology will work as it should; day-in and day-out. I'll help you budget your services with no more unpredictable and unnecessary IT expenses.
